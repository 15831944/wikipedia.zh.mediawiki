'''蔡厝港公园''' （{{lang-en|Choa Chu Kang Park}}) 是一个位于[[蔡厝港|蔡厝港]]的社区公园，此[[公园|公园]]沿着蔡厝港通道而建，并位于克兰芝快速公路旁。

== 公园设施 ==
此公园面积11[[公顷|公顷]]。设施包括一个游乐场、多用途广场、一个健身角落。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.nparks.gov.sg/gardens-parks-and-nature/parks-and-nature-reserves/choa-chu-kang-park|title=Choa Chu Kang Park|accessdate=2018-06-18|work=National Parks Board|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304064125/https://www.nparks.gov.sg/gardens-parks-and-nature/parks-and-nature-reserves/choa-chu-kang-park|archivedate=2016-03-04|df=}}</ref> 公园里也设有一个康疗公园，于2018年7月，新加坡卫生部长{{link-en| 颜金勇|Gan Kim Yong}}主持了该公园的开幕仪式。此康疗公园开发给民众来接着赏花治疗身心病痛。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.channel8news.sg/news8/singapore/20170919-sg-nparks/3830294.html|title=两家康疗公园开幕 通过绿色环境达到康疗作用 - 8频道新闻及时事节目|newspaper=Channel 8 News|accessdate=2018-06-18|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180707201818/https://www.channel8news.sg/news8/singapore/20170919-sg-nparks/3830294.html|archivedate=2018-07-07|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/new-therapeutic-garden-opens-at-choa-chu-kang-park-the-fourth-around-the-island|title=New Therapeutic Garden opens at Choa Chu Kang Park, the fourth around the island|last=hermesauto|date=2018-07-07|newspaper=The Straits Times|accessdate=2018-07-07|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180707190406/https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/new-therapeutic-garden-opens-at-choa-chu-kang-park-the-fourth-around-the-island|archivedate=2018-07-07|df=}}</ref>

公园也开放给民众种植蔬果，民众可向{{link-en|国家公园局|National Parks Board}}租用个约2.5平方米的花床来当个城市农夫。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.zaobao.com.sg/znews/singapore/story20180117-827536|title=公园局下月再开放五个公园花床|author=|date=2018-01-17|work=|newspaper=早报|accessdate=2018-06-18|language=zh|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180117045904/http://www.zaobao.com.sg/znews/singapore/story20180117-827536|archivedate=2018-01-17|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.channel8news.sg/news8/archives/20180116-sg-gardening/3936742.html|title=国家公园局将在下个月 开放多五个公园耕种园地供租用 - 8频道新闻及时事节目|newspaper=Channel 8 News|accessdate=2018-06-18|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180712045044/https://www.channel8news.sg/news8/archives/20180116-sg-gardening/3936742.html|archivedate=2018-07-12|df=}}</ref>

于2017年，时任教育部长（高等教育及技能）兼国防部第二部长[[王乙康|王乙康]]宣布，将于公园内建设新的战备军协俱乐部。此俱乐部主要是服务住在西部的战备军人，是随着新加坡西部的快速发展（如[[登加|登加]]新镇）而建立。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.zaobao.com.sg/znews/singapore/story20170529-765376|title=新战备军协俱乐部 将落户蔡厝港公园|author=|date=2017-05-29|work=|newspaper=早报|accessdate=2018-06-18|language=zh|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180711121019/https://www.zaobao.com.sg/znews/singapore/story20170529-765376|archivedate=2018-07-11|df=}}</ref>

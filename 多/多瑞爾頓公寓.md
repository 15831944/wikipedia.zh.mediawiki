'''多瑞爾頓公寓'''（The Dorilton）是位於[[美國|美國]][[紐約市|紐約市]][[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]]的一座高級公寓，於1900年開始施工，1902年竣工。多瑞爾頓公寓是一棟[[布雜藝術|布雜藝術]]風格建築，外表裝飾華麗<ref>[http://www.thecityreview.com/uws/bway/dorilton.html The Dorilton: photos and description]</ref> The exterior masonry, decorative terra-cotta work and chimneys and roof were expertly restored in 1998 by the Walter B. Melvin architectural firm.<ref>[http://www.wbmelvin.com/portfolio/dorilton.htm "The Dorilton, 171 West 71st Street "], ''Walter B. Melvin Architects, LLC''. Accessed 10 December 2015.</ref><ref name="nrhpinv_ny">{{cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=5421|title=National Register of Historic Places Registration: The Dorilton|date=August 1982|accessdate=2011-03-25|author=Larry E. Gobrecht|publisher=[[New_York_State_Office_of_Parks,_Recreation_and_Historic_Preservation|New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation]]|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121019140302/http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=5421|archivedate=2012-10-19}} ''See also:'' {{cite web|url=http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=5422|title=Accompanying six photos|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121019140318/http://www.oprhp.state.ny.us/hpimaging/hp_view.asp?GroupView=5422|archivedate=2012-10-19}}</ref>。1974年，多瑞爾頓公寓被列入紐約市地標。1983年，該建築又列入[[國家史跡名錄|國家史跡名錄]]。

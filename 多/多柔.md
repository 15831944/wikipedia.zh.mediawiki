'''多柔'''（本名Dorothee Pesch，{{bd|1964年|6月3日}}，出生于[[德国|德国]][[杜塞尔多夫|杜塞尔多夫]]），常被称作'''Doro Pesch'''或者'''Doro'''，德国[[硬式摇滚|硬式摇滚]]女艺人、歌曲作家，早期因是[[重金属音乐|重金属音乐]]乐队Warlock的主唱而在欧美知名。

==传记==
===早期生活===
多柔对[[摇滚乐|摇滚乐]]的儿时回忆是当她3岁时演唱Little Richard的歌曲"Lucille"。<ref name="eyez" > {{cite web | url = http://www.rockeyez.com/interviews/int-doro.html | title = Interview with Doro Pesch | accessdate = 2012-08-11 | last = Rademacher | first = Brian | date = 18 June 2006 | publisher = Rockeyez.com}}</ref><ref name="fourteeng"> {{cite web | url = http://www.fourteeng.net/interviews/doropesch.html | title = Interview: Doro Pesch - Doro | accessdate = 2012-08-16 | last = Omowale | first = Karma E. | date = 23 July 2007 | publisher = Fourteeng.net | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080514075841/http://www.fourteeng.net/interviews/doropesch.html | archivedate = 2008年5月14日 }}</ref> 在十岁时，当她接触了[[华丽摇滚|华丽摇滚]]的乐队诸如 T. Rex, Sweet 和Slade，<ref name="fourteeng" /> 她学习弹钢琴，开始唱歌。<ref name="Attack" > {{cite journal | title = Warlock Via Inferno | journal = Metal Attack | date = 22 July 1985 | issue = 23 | pages = 28-31| id = | accessdate = 2012-07-08| language = fr}}</ref> 在她十六岁时，从一次接近死亡的[[肺结核|肺结核]]病魔下康复后，她决定把更多时间和精力投身于演唱，与此同时也不放弃[[平面设计|平面设计]]的学习。<ref name="gig"> {{cite web | url = http://www.gigdates.net/story.php?id=564 | title = Interview with Doro Pesch | accessdate = 2012-09-04 | date = 1 October 2002 | publisher = Gigdates.net | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20021229042705/http://www.gigdates.net/story.php?id=564 | archivedate = 2002年12月29日 | deadurl = yes }}</ref> 在1980年，她加入了基于杜塞尔多夫地下摇滚乐队Snakebite。<ref name="fanclub" > {{cite journal | title = Warlock: the story so far | journal = International Warlock Fan Club Info Magazine | date = 1984 | issue = 1| id = | url = http://demolishmag.wordpress.com/category/warlock-the-story-so-far/ | accessdate = 2012-06-26}}</ref> 

===Warlock(1982-1988)===
WARLOCK于1982年组团，则以女主唱多柔为代言人。<ref name="megametal" > {{cite book | last1 = Simmons | first1 = Sylvie | title = Mega Metal Kerrang! | chapter = 'Lock Jaw | volume = 4 | publisher = Spotlight 凭借她过人的演唱表演天赋，使她成为80年代欧洲重金属男性界中的少有女性。<ref name="megametal" /><ref name="allmusic" > {{cite web | url = http://www.allmusic.com/artist/doro-mn0000191159 | title = Doro biography | accessdate = Steel，Warlock并随知名乐队在欧洲各摇滚音乐节上进行现场表演。<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www1.sk-static.com/images/media/img/col6/20100813-023318-518437.jpg | title = Metal Hammer Festival 1985 | accessdate = 2012-06-26 | format = JPG | publisher = Songkick.com}}</ref><ref> {{cite web | url = http://braelun.com/MoR%2086%201.gif | title = Monsters of Rock 1986 | accessdate = 2012-07-01 | format = GIF | publisher = ===在美国的多柔(1989-1990)===
[[File:Doro_pesch_hh_01.jpg|150px]]
1989年发行了专辑''Force Majeure'', 多柔个人名义的第一张专辑。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.allmusic.com/album/force-majeure-r6229/review | title = Doro Force Majeure review | accessdate = 2011-06-24 | last = Henderson | first = Alex | work = [[Allmusic|Allmusic]] | publisher = [[Rovi_Corporation|Rovi Corporation]]}}</ref> 这专辑在欧洲热销，<ref name="GER"> {{cite web|url=http://www.charts.de/suche.asp?search=doro&x=4&y=6&country=de|title=Suche Nach "Doro"|work=Charts.de|publisher=Media Control|language=de|accessdate=November 10, 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140517152615/http://www.charts.de/suche.asp?search=doro&x=4&y=6&country=de|archivedate=2014年5月17日}}</ref><ref name="SWI">{{cite web|url= http://swisscharts.com/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Doro |title=Discography Doro |work=swisscharts.com|accessdate=November 10, 2010}}</ref> 但在美国销售不佳。<ref>{{cite web|url= {{Allmusic|class=artist|id=doro-p4120/charts-awards/billboard-albums|pure_url=yes}}|title=Doro > Charts & Awards > Billboard Albums | work=[[Allmusic|Allmusic]] | publisher=[[Rovi_Corporation|Rovi Corporation]]|accessdate=November 10, 2010}}</ref>

===在欧洲的多柔(1991-1999)===
尽管生活在美国，在英语媒体鲜见多柔，但她在德国还是非常受欢迎，<ref name="allmusic" /> 在1991年"德国摇滚计划"中，她唱了一首慈善歌曲"让爱征服世界"。

1993年8月20日，多柔第一次在[[Wacken_Open_Air|Wacken Open Air]]音乐节上领衔表演。<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.wacken.com/en/woa2013/main-history/history/woa-1993/bands/ | title = Billing 1993 | accessdate = 2012-08-31 | publisher = [[Wacken_Open_Air|Wacken Open Air]]}}</ref>

===重返 (2000-2003)===
[[File:Frame_9.jpg|250px]]

在二十一世纪之初，重金属又被全世界大众所喜爱，多柔开始欧美巡演，<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.cuttingedgerocks.com/MMMD.html | title = Doro Steals the Show! | accessdate = 2012-08-24 | publisher = The Cutting Edge Rock.com}}</ref> 包括Doro乐队的第一次俄罗斯之行，在2001年Wacken Open Air音乐节上和德国[[敲击金属|敲击金属]]Holy Moses乐队演唱了"Too Drunk to Fuck".<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4LH3QS-iBSw | title = Holy Moses & Doro - To Drunk To Fuck (Live @ Wacken 2001) | accessdate = 2012-08-14 | date = 7 September 2007 | publisher = [[YouTube|YouTube]]}}</ref>

===金属乐女皇 (2003-至今)===
至今，多数粉丝，媒体和互联网社区都尊称多柔的绰号为“金属乐女皇”,<ref>{{cite web | url = https://sites.google.com/a/wormwoodchronicles.com/wormwood-chronicles/interviews/doro-2 | title = Doro - The Reign of the Silver Queen | accessdate = 2012-09-01 | last = Olma | first = Greg | publisher = Wormwood Chronicles.com}}</ref> 以此来表达对她在重金属音乐事业上的执着和奉献的由衷敬佩和极力支持。<ref> {{cite web | url = http://sonicshocks.com/DORO-interview.php | title = Interview with DORO | accessdate = 2012-08-19 | last = Massei | first = Cristina | date = 2010 | publisher = Sonic Shocks.com | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20101130101343/http://www.sonicshocks.com/DORO-interview.php | archivedate = 2010-11-30 }}</ref> <ref>{{cite web | url = http://metalkings.com/reviews/doro/doro-eng.htm | title = Doro interview | accessdate = 2012-09-04 | date = November 2002 | publisher = MetalKings.com}}</ref>

[[File:Doro-performing-live-at-Norway-Rock-Festival-2009.jpg|240px]]

在2004年Wacken Open Air,多柔乐队和古典夜晚交响乐乐队同台合作演出,<ref name="wacken2004" > {{cite web | url = http://www.blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=23797 | title = Doro: Special Guest Stars Announced For Wacken Open Air Appearance | accessdate = 2012-08-31 | date = 18 June 2004 | work = [[Blabbermouth.net|Blabbermouth.net]] | publisher = [[Roadrunner_Records|Roadrunner Records]]}}</ref> 这也是在著名德国金属音乐节上第一次盛大的交响乐团表演。她还与[[Iron_Maiden|Iron Maiden]]的Blaze Bayley演唱了歌曲"Fear of the Dark"。<ref>{{cite video | people = Doro| title =[[Classic_Diamonds_–_The_DVD|Classic Diamonds – The DVD]] | url =http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dq-7lrZsu1Q | format =DVD| publisher =[[AFM_Records|AFM Records]] | location =Schwalmstadt, Germany |date = August 2004| accessdate = 2 September 2012 | time =0:54| id =AFM 085-8}}
</ref> 当晚，多柔与Warlock前成员重组演唱了他们的老歌曲。.<ref name="wacken2004" />

多柔乐队在全球各音乐节上表演，包括Wacken Open Air<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.wacken.com/en/woa2013/main-history/history/woa-2009/billing-2009/ | title = Billing Bands 2009 | accessdate = 2012-08-31 | publisher = [[Wacken_Open_Air|Wacken Open Air]]}}</ref> 和2009年Metal Female Voices Fest 7<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.metalfemalevoicesfest.be/img/mfvf7.jpg| title = Metal Female Voices Fest VII | accessdate = 2012-09-03 | format = JPG | publisher = [[Metal_Female_Voices_Fest|Metal Female Voices Fest]]}}</ref> (她与Tarja Turunen同台表演<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=129253 | title = Doro Pesch Performs With Tarja Turunen At Metal Female Voices Fest; Video Available | accessdate = 2012-08-26 | date = 25 October 2009 | work = [[Blabbermouth.net|Blabbermouth.net]] | publisher = [[Roadrunner_Records|Roadrunner Records]]}}</ref>) , 法国的Hellfest French music festival<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=146911 | title = Angel Witch, Sodom, Doro, Mayhem, The Haunted, Others Confirmed For France's Hellfest | accessdate = 2012-08-26 | date = 30 September 2010 | work = [[Blabbermouth.net|Blabbermouth.net]] | publisher = [[Roadrunner_Records|Roadrunner Records]]}}</ref>, 德国的Bang Your Head!!!和英国2010年的Bloodstock Open Air.<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=128988 | title = Doro Confirmed For U.K.'s Bloodstock Festival | accessdate = 2012-08-26 | date = 20 October 2009 | work = [[Blabbermouth.net|Blabbermouth.net]] | publisher = [[Roadrunner_Records|Roadrunner Records]]}}</ref> 

2009年, 为纪念Wacken Open Air音乐节的二十周年庆，多柔创作了Wacken颂歌"We Are the Metalheads" 。2009年七月30日这歌发行单曲，由多柔和Wacken组织者Thomas Jensen前乐队Skyline联合表演.<ref> {{cite web | url = http://www.blabbermouth.net/news.aspx?mode=Article&newsitemID=121633 | title = Doro Pesch Records Wacken Open Air Festival Anthem | accessdate = 2012-08-26 | date = 9 June 2009 | work = [[Blabbermouth.net|Blabbermouth.net]] | publisher = [[Roadrunner_Records|Roadrunner Records]]}}</ref>

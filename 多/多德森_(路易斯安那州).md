'''多德森'''（{{lang-en|'''Dodson'''}}），是[[美国|美国]][[路易斯安那州|路易斯安那州]]下属的一座村镇。<ref name="Leeper2012">{{cite book|author=Clare D’Artois Leeper|title=Louisiana Place Names: Popular, Unusual, and Forgotten Stories of Towns, Cities, Plantations, Bayous, and Even Some Cemeteries|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=HgL-Yi7GIEQC&pg=PA82|accessdate=16 January 2013|date=19 October 2012|publisher=LSU Press|isbn=978-0-8071-4738-2|pages=82–}}</ref><ref name="Project1947">{{cite book|author=Federal Writers' Project|title=Louisiana: A Guide to the State|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=lOSvzYLs3tMC&pg=PA615|accessdate=16 January 2013|year=1947|publisher=US History Publishers|isbn=978-1-60354-017-9|pages=615–}}</ref>根据[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]，该市有人口337人。建置上，属于“village”。<ref name=census>{{cite web | url=http://www.census.gov/prod/www/abs/decennial/ |title = Census of Population and Housing |publisher  = U.S. Census Bureau |accessdate = November 18, 2011}}</ref>

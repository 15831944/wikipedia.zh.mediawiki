'''多花海棠'''（學名：''Malus floribunda''）是[[蘋果屬|蘋果屬]]下的一種植物<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hortiplex.gardenweb.com/plants/nph-ind.cgi?name=Malus&f=d&gl=1&p=1|title=GardenWeb's HortiPlex Plant Database, search results for: ''Malus''|accessdate=September 11, 2009|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100404011219/http://hortiplex.gardenweb.com/plants/nph-ind.cgi?name=Malus&f=d&gl=1&p=1|archivedate=April 4, 2010|df=}}</ref><ref name=GRIN>{{GRIN | accessdate = December 20, 2017}}</ref>，原產於東亞。因在花季盛開粉色或白色小花而得名，結直徑一厘米的紅或黃色果實。它是一種受歡迎的園藝植物，曾獲[[皇家園藝學會|皇家園藝學會]]獎項（Award of Garden Merit）。<ref>{{cite web|title=RHS Plant Selector - ''Malus floribunda''|url=http://apps.rhs.org.uk/plantselector/plant?plantid=1249|accessdate=24 May 2013}}</ref>

它對[[苹果黑星病|苹果黑星病]]和[[白粉病|白粉病]]都有很高的抗性<ref>[http://selectree.calpoly.edu/treedetail.lasso?rid=912 Cal Poly]</ref>，因此有研究者試圖將其“VF基因”引入到[[蘋果|蘋果]]中，並獲得了一定的成功。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.path.ethz.ch/docs/publications/2012/Gessler_2 |title=Vf scab resistance of Malus |access-date=2018-10-09 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141219042539/http://www.path.ethz.ch/docs/publications/2012/Gessler_2 |archive-date=2014-12-19 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>

'''多伦多面包车袭击事件'''（'''Toronto van attack'''）发生在2018年4月23日，当时一辆租用的白色全尺寸面包车在加拿大[[多伦多|多伦多]]北约克市中心商业区的[[央街|央街]]上以高速行驶撞向行人，造成10人死亡，15人受伤。<ref name=Globe0423/>疑似司机，25岁的Alek Minassian在袭击开始26分钟后在犯罪现场南部被捕。<ref name=:0>{{cite news|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/pedestrians-struck-white-van-1.4631564|title=9 dead, 16 injured after van strikes pedestrians in North York|work={{tsl|en|CBC News||CBC News}}|date=2018-04-23|accessdate=2018-04-23}}</ref>这是加拿大历史上最致命的[[車輛衝撞攻擊|車輛衝撞攻擊]]<ref name="nyt2">{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/04/23/world/toronto-van.html|title=Toronto Van Plows Along Sidewalk, Killing 9 in 'Pure Carnage'|last1=Austen|first1=Ian|last2=Stack|first2=Liam|date=2018-04-23|work=[[纽约时报|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2018-04-23}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.france24.com/en/20180424-live-canada-pm-trudeau-address-media-toronto-van-attack|title=Canada's Trudeau says security unchanged after Toronto van attack – France 24|date=2018-04-24|work=France 24|access-date=2018-04-24|language=en-US}}</ref>，也是自1989年以来加拿大最严重的大规模屠杀事件，当时多伦多鲁珀特酒店发生的[[縱火|縱火]]事件造成10人死亡。<ref name="Mass Killings in Canada - Toronto Star">{{cite web|title=A Grim History of Mass Killings in Canada|url=https://www.pressreader.com/canada/toronto-star/20180424/281586651202283|website=PressReader.com|publisher=Toromto Star|accessdate=2018-04-25}}</ref><ref name="MSN">{{cite web|title=A history of violence on Canadian soil|url=https://www.msn.com/en-ca/news/breakingnews/a-history-of-violence-on-canadian-soil/ar-AAwfEVa|website=MSN.com|publisher=Toronto Star|accessdate=2018-04-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180425182901/https://www.msn.com/en-ca/news/breakingnews/a-history-of-violence-on-canadian-soil/ar-AAwfEVa|archive-date=2018-04-25|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

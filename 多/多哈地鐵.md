'''多哈地鐵'''（{{lang-ar|مترو الدوحة}}）是目前在[[卡塔爾|卡塔爾]]首都[[多哈|多哈]]興建中的[[地鐵|地鐵]]系統，預定在2019年通車<ref>{{cite web|title=Doha Metro, Qatar|url=http://www.railway-technology.com/projects/doha-metro|publisher=Railway Technology|accessdate=11 February 2015}}</ref>。合共4條路線，全長300公里，共有100個車站<ref>{{cite web|title=25 of the biggest construction projects in the GCC region|url=http://arabianindustry.com/construction/bahrain/photos/2013/jun/25/in-pictures-25-of-the-biggest-construction-projects-in-the-gcc-region-4353063/#qatar-metrorail-network-36bn-qatar-railways-co-announced-the-award-of-the-bulk-of-the-first-phase|publisher=Arabian Industry|accessdate=11 February 2015}}</ref>。多哈地鐵會成為大卡塔爾鐵路網絡的一個重要部分，並與{{link-en|路薩爾輕軌|Lusail LRT}}與客貨運的國鐵。行駛速度可達100公里/小時，多哈地鐵將成為世界上最快的無人駕駛列車之一<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thepeninsulaqatar.com/article/20/08/2017/First-Doha-Metro-trains-arrive-in-Qatar|title=First Doha Metro trains arrive in Qatar - The Peninsula Qatar|website=thepeninsulaqatar.com|access-date=2017-11-14}}</ref>。

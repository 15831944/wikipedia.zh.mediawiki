'''多薩'''是南部印度的一种饼，用发酵的面糊制成。一般和{{tsl|en|Sambar||Sambar}}同时食用。

==起源==
其确切起源成谜<ref name="Charmine2013">{{cite book |author=Charmaine O' Brien |title=The Penguin Food Guide to India |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=BGhBAgAAQBAJ&pg=PT378 |date=15 December 2013 |publisher=Penguin Books Limited |isbn=978-93-5118-575-8 |pages=378 }}</ref>，历史学家{{tsl|en|P. Thankappan Nair||P. Thankappan Nair}}认为其起源于今日的[[卡纳塔克邦|卡纳塔克邦]]的[[乌杜皮|乌杜皮]]，<ref>{{cite book | title=South Indians in Kolkata |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=swNuAAAAMAAJ&pgis=1| edition=| author=P. Thankappan Nair | year=2004 |publisher=Punthi Pustak |page=320 | isbn=81-86791-50-7}}</ref>而食物历史学家{{tsl|en|K. T. Achaya}}则根据文献，认为其早在1世纪左右的{{tsl|en|Tamilakam|古代泰米尔国家}}便有出现<ref name="KTAchaya">{{cite book | title=The Story of Our Food |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bk9RHRCqZOkC&pg=PA80&dq | author=K. T. Achaya| year= |page=80 |publisher=Universities Press| isbn=81-7371-293-X}}</ref>。

'''多格拉语'''（{{lang|doi|डोगरी}} / {{Nastaliq|ڈوگری }}），巴基斯坦常称为'''[[帕哈里语|帕哈里语]]'''（पहाड़ी / {{nastaliq|پہاڑی}}），是[[印度-雅利安语支|印度-雅利安语支]]的语言，大约有500万人作为母语<ref name="book001">{{cite book|title=Encyclopaedia of Teaching Languages in India, v. 20|first=Sita Ram|last=Sharma|publisher=Anmol Publications|location=|year=1992|isbn=|url=https://books.google.com/?id=wIJhAAAAMAAJ|page=6}}</ref>。多格拉语分布于[[印度|印度]]和[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]，主要地区包括[[查谟-克什米尔邦|查谟-克什米尔邦]]和[[喜马偕尔邦|喜马偕尔邦]]的查谟地区，在北方的[[旁遮普地区|旁遮普地区]]和查谟-克什米尔邦的其他地区也有分布<ref name="book002">{{cite book|title=History and Culture of Himalayan States, v.4|first=Anita K.|last=Billawaria|publisher=Light & Life Publishers|location=|year=1978|isbn=|pages=|url=https://books.google.com/?id=HL21AAAAIAAJ}}</ref>。多格拉语的母语使用者被称为[[多格拉人|多格拉人]]，多格拉语的分布地区被称为杜加尔（Duggar）<ref name="book003">{{cite book|title=An Introduction to Dogri Folk Literature and Pahari Art|first=Lakshmi|last=Narain|publisher=Jammu and Kashmir Academy of Art, Culture and Languages|location=|year=1965|isbn=|pages=|url=https://books.google.com/?id=BARAAAAAIAAJ}}</ref>。多格拉语曾被视为[[旁遮普语|旁遮普语]]的一个方言<ref name="book004">{{cite book|title=The Indo-Aryan Languages|first=Colin P.|last=Masica|publisher=Cambridge University Press|location=|year=1993|isbn=0-521-29944-6|pages=427|url=https://books.google.com/?id=Itp2twGR6tsC}}</ref>。和其他[[印欧语系|印欧语系]]语言不同，多格拉语是[[声调语言|声调语言]]<ref name="book005">{{cite book|title=Studies in Phonetics and Phonology: With Special Reference to Dogri|first=Ved Kumari|last=Ghai|publisher=Ariana Publishing House|location=|year=1991|isbn=81-85347-20-4|pages=|url=https://books.google.com/?id=VAMiAAAAMAAJ|quote=''non-Dogri speakers, also trained phoneticians, tend to hear the difference as one of length only, perceiving the second syllable as stressed''}}</ref>。

多格拉语有数种变体，它们之间的词汇相似度大于80%<ref>{{cite web|last1=Brightbill|first1=Jeremy D.|last2=Turner|first2=Scott B.|title=A Sociolinguistic Survey of the Dogri Language, Jammu and Kashmir|url=http://www.sil.org/system/files/reapdata/13/27/43/132743635529526156448342546486105211675/silesr2007_017.pdf|publisher=SIL International|accessdate=2016-03-11|date=2007}}</ref>。在升格为独立语言之前，多格拉语在印度人口普查中被划为旁遮普语的方言<ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=XKzRN1_zw-IC|title=Social Mobilisation And Modern Society|publisher=}}</ref>，

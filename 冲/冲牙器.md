'''冲牙器'''，又称'''水牙线'''，是一种家用的[[口腔|口腔]]清洁装置。在使用时，冲牙器喷射出[[脉冲|脉冲]]水流，冲击清洁牙缝与[[牙龈|牙龈]]处的[[牙菌斑|牙菌斑]]和食物残渣。使用冲牙器可改善牙龈健康，亦有助于清洁[[牙齒矯正器|矫正器]]和[[植牙|人工牙体]]。1962年，美国牙医傑拉德·莫耶（{{lang-en|Gerald Moyer}}）和工程师約翰·馬丁利（{{lang-en|John Mattingly}}）在[[科罗拉多州|科罗拉多州]]的[[科林斯堡_(科罗拉多州)|科林斯堡]]发明了首台冲牙机<ref>{{cite web |title=About Water Pik, Inc. |url=https://www.waterpik.com/about-us/ |website=www.waterpik.com |accessdate=2018-06-26}}</ref>，这也促成了{{tsl|en|Water_Pik,_Inc.|洁碧公司}}的建立<ref>{{cite web|url=https://alexsoralhealth.com/water-flossers/waterpik-interesting-facts-numbers#waterpik-is-the-oldest|title=Waterpik: interesting facts and numbers - Alex's Oral Health|date=2016-08-23|accessdate=2018-06-26|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180626110822/https://alexsoralhealth.com/water-flossers/waterpik-interesting-facts-numbers/#waterpik-is-the-oldest|archivedate=2018-06-26}}</ref>。

==相關研究==
逾五十項研究表明，沖牙器能改善牙周健康問題，如[[牙齦病|牙齦病]]和[[口臭|口臭]]<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Sharma |first1=Naresh C. |last2=Lyle |first2=Deborah M. |last3=Qaqish |first3=Jimmy G. |last4=Galustians |first4=Jack |last5=Schuller |first5=Reinhard |title=Effect of a dental water jet with orthodontic tip on plaque and bleeding in adolescent patients with fixed orthodontic appliances |journal=American Journal of Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics |date=2008-04 |volume=133 |issue=4 |pages=565–571 |doi=10.1016/j.ajodo.2007.12.008 |accessdate=2018-06-27}}</ref>；亦有助於[[牙冠_(修复体)|人造牙冠]]、人工植體和矯正器的深入清潔<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Jahn |first1=CA |title=The dental water jet: a historical review of the literature. |journal=Journal of dental hygiene : JDH |date=2009 |volume=84 |issue=3 |pages=114-20 |pmid=20579423 |accessdate=2018-06-27}}</ref>。2008年的一項[[元分析|綜合分析]]認為，沖牙器對去除可見牙菌斑沒有幫助，但可能對牙齦健康有益<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Jin |first1=Lijian |title=Is oral irrigation beneficial to gingival health as an adjunct to toothbrushing? |journal=Evidence-Based Dentistry |date=2009-06-24 |volume=10 |issue=2 |pages=40–41 |doi=10.1038/sj.ebd.6400644 |accessdate=2018-06-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |last1=Husseini |first1=A |last2=Slot |first2=DE |last3=Van der Weijden |first3=GA |title=The efficacy of oral irrigation in addition to a toothbrush on plaque and the clinical parameters of periodontal inflammation: a systematic review. |journal=International journal of dental hygiene |date=2008-11 |volume=6 |issue=4 |pages=304-14 |doi=10.1111/j.1601-5037.2008.00343.x |pmid=19138181 |accessdate=2018-06-27}}</ref>。[[南加州大學|南加州大學]]的一項研究表明，以頻率為1200次/分钟，壓強為70[[磅力每平方英寸|psi]]的脈衝水流沖洗三秒，可去除目標區域內99.9%的牙菌膜<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Gorur |first1=A |last2=Lyle |first2=DM |last3=Schaudinn |first3=C |last4=Costerton |first4=JW |title=Biofilm removal with a dental water jet. |journal=Compendium of continuing education in dentistry (Jamesburg, N.J. : 1995) |date=2009-03 |volume=30 Spec No 1 |pages=1-6 |pmid=19385349 |accessdate=2018-06-27}}</ref>。另有{{tsl|ja|東京歯科大学|東京齒科大學}}的一項為期兩周的臨床試驗表明，比起僅用牙刷潔牙，牙刷與沖牙器並用可更好地抑制牙菌斑<ref>{{cite journal |author1=両角祐子 |author2=山下穣 |author3=阿部祐三 |author4=安川俊之 |author5=竹田まゆ |author6=宇野清博 |author7=佐藤聡 |title=脈動ジェット水流式口腔洗浄器具の臨床効果 |journal=日本歯科保存学雑誌 |volume=52 |doi=10.11471/shikahozon.52.219 |publisher=日本口腔外科学会 |location=[[東京都|東京都]][[豐島區|豐島區]] |issn=03872343}}</ref>。

==使用方法==
冲牙器推荐在[[刷牙|刷牙]]后或饭后使用，以下是简要的使用步骤<ref>{{cite web |title=冲牙器怎么用 |url=http://www.lesasa.com/article/19561.html |website=乐莎莎 |accessdate=2018-06-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=What is a Water Flosser (And Should You Use One)? |url=https://www.batchelor-dentistry.com/blog/what-is-a-water-flosser |website=General Dentist Harrisonburg VA - Caitlin Batchelor Dentistry |accessdate=2018-06-27 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170824055724/http://www.batchelor-dentistry.com/blog/what-is-a-water-flosser |archivedate=2017-08-24 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Hydro Floss Oral Irrigator - Instructions for use |url=https://www.just4teeth.com/Hydro-Floss-Oral-Irrigator-Instructions-for-use |website=www.just4teeth.com |accessdate=2018-06-27 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161026180721/http://www.just4teeth.com/Hydro-Floss-Oral-Irrigator-Instructions-for-use |archivedate=2016-10-26 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=How to Use an Oral Irrigator to Stop Bleeding Gums |url=https://www.carrieibbetson.com/2013/08/oral-irrigator-to-stop-bleeding-gums/ |website=Oral Health and Dental Wellness |accessdate=2018-06-27 |date=2013-08-19 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161128071341/http://www.carrieibbetson.com/2013/08/oral-irrigator-to-stop-bleeding-gums/ |archivedate=2016-11-28 }}</ref>：
#使用时，预先将冲牙器的出水强度调至最低，并先向冲牙器内倒入温水，也可再倒入适量[[漱口水|漱口水]]。
#俯身在洗手池上方，使得开启冲牙器后口中的水能够直接流入水池内。
#将喷头对准牙龈线，开启冲牙器开关，调整出水强度，保证舒适。
#沿牙龈线横向移动喷头，且在牙缝处短暂停留。水流应始终与牙保持九十度角。
#冲洗完毕后，关闭冲牙器开关，并将机内多余的水倒出。

==其他用途==
沖牙器雖然設計為潔齒用，但其脈衝噴水器之實質也可利用于其他用途，如去除[[扁桃腺結石|扁桃腺結石]]等<ref>{{cite news |author1=Elizabeth Svoboda |title=In Tonsils, a Problem the Size of a Pea |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2009/09/01/health/01tons.html |accessdate=2018-06-27 |date=2009-08-11 |language=en |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180127103418/http://www.nytimes.com/2009/09/01/health/01tons.html |archivedate=2018-01-27 }}</ref>。

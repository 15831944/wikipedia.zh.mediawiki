'''劳莉·布劳因'''（{{lang-en|Laurie Blouin}}，{{bd|1996年|4月7日||}}）生于魁北克省魁北克市，是一名加拿大女子单板滑雪运动员，她的家乡是斯托纳姆<ref name="olympicca">{{cite web|url=https://olympic.ca/team-canada/laurie-blouin/|title=Laurie Blouin|work=Canadian Olympic Committee|accessdate=2019-02-03|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181129150205/https://olympic.ca/team-canada/laurie-blouin/|archivedate=2018-11-29|deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://ici.radio-canada.ca/sports/1142993/laurie-blouin-snowboard-x-games-planchiste-stoneham|script-title=fr:La planchiste Laurie Blouin invitée aux X Games|publisher=Radio Canada|date=2018-12-20|accessdate=2019-02-03|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190202174509/https://ici.radio-canada.ca/sports/1142993/laurie-blouin-snowboard-x-games-planchiste-stoneham|archivedate=2019-02-02}}</ref>。劳莉在六岁时跟随哥哥开始练习单板滑雪<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nationalpost.com/sports/olympics/canadas-laurie-blouin-announces-herself-for-2018-olympics-with-snowboard-world-championship|title=Canada's Laurie Blouin announces herself for 2018 Olympics with snowboard world championship|publisher=National Post|date=2017-03-11|accessdate=2019-02-03}}</ref>。2012年2月，她在家乡斯托纳姆完成了个人的世界杯分站赛首秀<ref name="olympicca"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/snowboard/athlete-profile-n3037091-laurie-blouin.htm|title=Snowboard {{!}} Athlete Profile: Laurie BLOUIN|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-02-03|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180421151604/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/snowboard/athlete-profile-n3037091-laurie-blouin.htm|archivedate=2018-04-21}}</ref>。

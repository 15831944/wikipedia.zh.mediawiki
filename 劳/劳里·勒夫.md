== 早年生活及教育 ==
勒夫出身于[[萨福克郡|萨福克郡]]斯特拉迪什尔，<ref>{{Cite news|title=Vicar's son arrested over US hacking|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/crime/11745493/Vicars-son-arrested-over-US-hacking.html|date=2015-07-16|accessdate=2019-02-21|issn=0307-1235|language=en|first=Nicola|last=Harley|author=|work=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180620132547/https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/crime/11745493/Vicars-son-arrested-over-US-hacking.html|archive-date=2018-06-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>具有英国和芬兰[[双重国籍|双重国籍]]。<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|title=Lauri Love may be faking mental illness claims lawyer for US|url=https://www.computerweekly.com/news/450299332/Lauri-Love-may-be-faking-mental-illness-claims-lawyer-for-US|work=ComputerWeekly.com|accessdate=2019-02-21|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180728190847/https://www.computerweekly.com/news/450299332/Lauri-Love-may-be-faking-mental-illness-claims-lawyer-for-US|archive-date=2018-07-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>其父亚历山大·勒夫曾是监狱牧师。<ref name=":2">{{Cite news|title=Lauri Love case: Vicar father fears son 'could kill himself'|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-england-suffolk-36649095|date=2016-06-28|accessdate=2019-02-21|language=en|author=|work=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180728135900/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-england-suffolk-36649095|archive-date=2018-07-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>劳里·勒夫从[[預科學校_(英國)|预科学校]]辍学，在火鸡场工作。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.judiciary.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/usa-v-love-judgment-1.pdf|title="The Government of the United States of America Requesting Judicial Authority v Lauri Love Requested Person"|accessdate=|author=Her Majesty's Courts and Tribunals Service|date=2016-09-16|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160917084810/https://www.judiciary.gov.uk/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/usa-v-love-judgment-1.pdf|archive-date=2016-09-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>之后，因其母亲是芬兰籍，他申请了芬兰护照，并在[[芬兰国防军|芬兰国防军]]服役六个月，后来成为[[良心拒服兵役者|良心拒服兵役者]]，服[[民役|民役]]六个月。<ref name=":0" />

勒夫曾申请就读[[英格兰|英格兰]][[诺丁汉大学|诺丁汉大学]]，在第二个学期因身心不适而退学，后又申请就读[[苏格兰|苏格兰]][[格拉斯哥大学|格拉斯哥大学]]，在第二学年同样因健康原因退学。<ref>{{Cite web|title=British activist Lauri Love charged with hacking US army database|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/crime/british-activist-lauri-love-charged-with-hacking-us-army-database-8909484.html|work=The Independent|date=2013-10-28|accessdate=2019-02-21|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180728141300/https://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/crime/british-activist-lauri-love-charged-with-hacking-us-army-database-8909484.html|archive-date=2018-07-28|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Former Glasgow Uni student could face time in US prison after hacking accusation|url=http://www.glasgowlive.co.uk/news/glasgow-news/former-glasgow-university-student-could-11898086|work=glasgowlive|date=2016-09-16|accessdate=2019-02-21|first=Glasgow|last=Live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180728191448/https://www.glasgowlive.co.uk/news/glasgow-news/former-glasgow-university-student-could-11898086|archive-date=2018-07-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>勒夫在此期间曾参与{{link-en|2011年占领海瑟林顿大楼|2011 Hetherington House Occupation|学生抗议活动}}。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Insight: Lauri Love - the making of a hacker|url=https://www.scotsman.com/news/politics/insight-lauri-love-the-making-of-a-hacker-1-3170765|work=www.scotsman.com|accessdate=2019-02-21|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180728191126/https://www.scotsman.com/news/politics/insight-lauri-love-the-making-of-a-hacker-1-3170765|archive-date=2018-07-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>

==== 引渡听证会 ====
劳里·勒夫的父亲亚历山大·勒夫于2016年6月28日和29日在{{Link-en|威斯敏斯特裁判法院|Westminster Magistrate's Court}}举行的的引渡听证会上作证称，劳里被诊断出[[亚斯伯格症候群|亚斯伯格症]]<ref name=":3" />，因此不应被引渡。<ref name=":2" />心理医生{{Link-en|西蒙·拜仁—科恩|Simon Baron-Cohen}}也声称，由于劳里在2012年被诊断出[[精神分裂|精神分裂]]、[[皮膚炎|皮肤炎]]和[[抑郁症|抑郁症]]，所以也不应该被引渡。<ref name=":5">{{Cite news|title=Autistic man accused of computer hacking could kill himself if extradited, court is warned|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2016/06/28/lauri-love/|newspaper=The Telegraph|date=2016-06-28|accessdate=2019-02-21|issn=0307-1235|language=en|first=Martin|last=Evans|author=|work=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190112080545/https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2016/06/28/lauri-love/|archive-date=2019-01-12|dead-url=no}}</ref>

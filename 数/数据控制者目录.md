'''数据控制者目录'''（{{lang-en|'''Register of data controllers'''}}）是[[英国|英国]]依据《[[数据保护法案|1998年数据保护法案]]》设立的[[数据库|数据库]]。目录由信息专员办公室（{{lang-en|Information Commissioner's Office}}）管理，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.opsi.gov.uk/acts/acts1998/ukpga_19980029_en_1|title=Data Protection Act 1998|accessdate=2009-11-18|publisher=[[Office_of_Public_Sector_Information|Office of Public Sector Information]]}}</ref>用于登记数据控制者（数据收集者）的信息以及收集和处理数据的目的。<ref name="inspection">{{Cite web|url=https://ico.org.uk/about-the-ico/what-we-do/register-of-data-controllers/|title=Register of data controllers|accessdate=2015-07-16|publisher=UK [[Information_Commissioner's_Office|Information Commissioner's Office]]|quote=We publish the name and address of these data controllers, as well as a description of the kind of processing they do.}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theadvertisingprotectionagency.co.uk/register-of-data-controllers.php|title=Register of Data Controllers|accessdate=22 March 2012|publisher=The Advertising Protection Agency|quote=The ICO then publishes certain details from the registration data in the register of data controllers which is available to the public for inspection.|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110107040423/http://www.theadvertisingprotectionagency.co.uk/register-of-data-controllers.php|archivedate=2011年1月7日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.businesslink.gov.uk/bdotg/action/detail?itemId=1074415109&type=RESOURCES|title=Notifying the Information Commissioner's Office about personal information|accessdate=22 March 2012|work=[[Business_Link|Business Link]]|quote=The Data Protection Act 1998 requires businesses to give details about the way they process personal information to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) for inclusion in a public register, unless they are exempt. This is called notification.}}</ref>

根据《数据保护法案》，在某些情况下，数据控制者可豁免登记。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/register/|title=Register (notify) under the Data Protection Act|accessdate=2015-07-16|publisher=UK [[Information_Commissioner's_Office|Information Commissioner's Office]]|quote=The Data Protection Act 1998 requires every data controller (eg organisation, sole trader) who is processing personal information to register with the ICO, unless they are exempt.}}</ref>当无法达到豁免要求<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/guide-to-data-protection/exemptions/|title=In brief – are there any exemptions from the Data Protection Act?|accessdate=2015-07-16|publisher=UK [[Information_Commissioner's_Office|Information Commissioner's Office]]|quote=... there are some exemptions from the Act to accommodate special circumstances.}}</ref>时，在开始收集数据前未能通知信息专员办公室（即登记到目录）是一项[[无过错责任|无过错责任]]罪行，可被信息专员办公室刑事检控。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.strath.ac.uk/dataprotection/registerofdatacontrollers/|title=Register of Data Controllers - University of Strathclyde|accessdate=22 March 2012|publisher=[[University_of_Strathclyde|University of Strathclyde]]|quote=Every organisation that processes, i.e. holds and uses, personal information must be registered with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), unless they are exempt.  This registration is a statutory requirement under the Data Protection Act and failure to notify the ICO is a criminal offence.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180313214351/https://www.strath.ac.uk/dataprotection/registerofdatacontrollers/|archive-date=2018-03-13|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

登记加入目录缴纳的费用被直接用于英国信息专员办公室的运作。任何人均可免费查询此目录。

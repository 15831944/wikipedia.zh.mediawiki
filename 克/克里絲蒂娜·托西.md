'''克里絲蒂娜·托西'''（'''{{lang-en|Christina Tosi}}'''，{{bd|1981年|11月9日|catIdx=Tosi, Christina}}）是[[美国|美国]]的一名[[廚師|廚師]]、餐廳老闆和電視名人。她是纽约知名甜品店Milk Bar的老板。<ref>[https://www.politico.com/story/2018/05/30/women-rule-christina-tosi-611589 “The politics of Christina Tosi”]. Politico. 30-05-2018.</ref><ref>[https://www.inc.com/magazine/201804/maria-aspan/christina-tosi-milk-bar-momofuku-bakery.html “How Milk Bar's Christina Tosi Went From Momofuku Employee to Bakery Chain CEO”]. Inc. magazine. March/April 2018.</ref>

克里絲蒂娜·托西亦以她同為主持人的廚藝節目而聞名，在美国她參與的節目有：《[[我要做廚神_(美國版)|廚神當道]]》和《小小廚神》。<ref>[https://www.ctvnews.ca/mobile/entertainment/pastry-chef-christina-tosi-set-to-shake-up-masterchef-judges-panel-1.2382345 “Pastry chef Christina Tosi set to shake up 'MasterChef' judges panel”]. CTV NEWS. 20-05-2015.</ref>

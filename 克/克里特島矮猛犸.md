'''克里特島埃猛獁'''（[[英語|英語]]：'''Cretan dwarf mammoth''' ，[[學名|學名]]：'''''Mammuthus creticus'''''）是一種已經滅絕的[[猛犸象|猛犸象]]，同時它是最小的一種猛犸象。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Extreme insular dwarfism evolved in a mammoth|journal=Proc. R. Soc. B|date=2012-08-22|doi=10.1098/rspb.2012.0671|url=http://rspb.royalsocietypublishing.org/content/279/1741/3193|language=en|volume=279|issue=1741|pages=3193–3200|issn=0962-8452|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Victoria L. Herridge, Adrian M. Lister}}</ref>

根據2006年的[[DNA|DNA]]研究，計劃將本劃歸[[古菱齒象亞屬|古菱齒象亞屬]]的這一物種的學名“''Palaeoloxodon creticus''”改作“''Mammuthus creticus''”(Bate, 1907).<ref>{{cite journal|title=Ancient DNA forces reconsideration of evolutionary history of Mediterranean pygmy elephantids|journal=Biology Letters|date=2006-09-22|doi=10.1098/rsbl.2006.0467|url=http://rsbl.royalsocietypublishing.org/content/2/3/451|language=en|volume=2|issue=3|pages=451–454|issn=1744-9561|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Nikos Poulakakis, Aris Parmakelis, Petros Lymberakis, Moysis Mylonas, Eleftherios Zouros, David S. Reese, Scott Glaberman, Adalgisa Caccone}}</ref> ，而在2002年曾有另外一些學者則計劃將這一物種的全部大型種移至新[[亞種|亞種]]名“''Elephas antiquus creutzburgi''”(Kuss, 1965)之下{{來源請求}}。2007年，因為Poulakakis等人在2006年提出的理論的問題顯示出了一些2006年DNA研究的不足之處，從而導致了一些新的爭論<ref name=Poulakakis2006>{{cite journal | author = Poulakakis N., Parmakelis A., Lymberakis P., Mylonas M., Zouros E., Reese D., Glaberman S., Caccone A. | year = 2006 | title = Ancient DNA forces reconsideration of evolutionary history of Mediterranean pygmy elephantids | url = | journal = Biol. Lett. | volume = 2 | issue = 3| pages = 451–454 | doi = 10.1098/rsbl.2006.0467 | pmid = 17148428 | pmc = 1686204 }}</ref> <ref name=BioLetters>{{cite journal | author = Orlando, L., Pagés, M., Calvignac, S., ''et al.''| title = Does the 43bp sequence from an 800000 year old Cretan dwarf elephantid really rewrite the textbook on mammoths? | journal = [[Biology_Letters|Biology Letters]] | doi = 10.1098/rsbl.2006.0536 | volume = 3 | issue = 1 | date = 2007-02-22 | pages = 57–59}}</ref>。然而這個理論中一些[[形態學|形態學]]證據是可疑的，至少對於將其列入[[猛犸象屬|猛犸象屬]]並沒有太多不利。<!-- http://rspb.royalsocietypublishing.org/content/early/2012/05/04/rspb.2012.0671 -->

'''克林特·傑克森·弗雷澤'''（{{lang-en|Clint Jackson Frazier}}, {{bd|1994年|9月6日|}}），綽號「Red Thunder」，為美國職棒大聯盟的外野手。目前效力於紐約洋基隊。

==職業生涯==
===小聯盟===
弗雷澤在2013年大聯盟選秀以第5順位由印地安人選中。他以簽約金350萬向印地安人簽約<ref name=signs>{{cite web|url=http://www.cleveland.com/tribe/index.ssf/2013/06/cleveland_indians_sign_no1_pic_1.html|title=Cleveland Indians sign No.1 pick Clint Frazier for $3.5 million; will report to Goodyear, Ariz.|first=Paul|last=Hoynes|date=15 June 2013|accessdate=3 August 2016|work=[[The_Plain_Dealer|The Plain Dealer]]}}</ref>。在新人聯盟首場比賽，弗雷澤就擊出一發全壘打和一支三壘安打<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ohio.com/sports/indians/cleveland-indians-notebook-top-pick-clint-frazier-has-dynamite-debut-1.408989 |title=Cleveland Indians notebook: Top pick Clint Frazier has dynamite debut |date=26 June 2013|work=[[Akron_Beacon_Journal|Akron Beacon Journal]]|accessdate=1 July 2017}}</ref>。

2016年，印地安人把弗雷澤、Justus Sheffield、Ben Heller和J. P. Feyereisen交易至洋基隊換來[[安德魯·米勒_(棒球員)|安德魯·米勒]] <ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cleveland.com/tribe/index.ssf/2016/07/cleveland_indians_andrew_mille.html|title=All in? Cleveland Indians acquire Yankees relief ace Andrew Miller for Clint Frazier, other prospects|first=Zack|last=Meisel|date=3 August 2016|work=NJ.com}}</ref>。2016年，弗雷澤在3A繳出2成63的打擊率，16支全壘打，55分打點<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.milb.com/player/index.jsp?player_id=640449#/career/R/hitting/2016/ALL |title=Clint Frazier – New York Yankees |publisher=Milb.com |accessdate=July 1, 2017}}</ref>。2017年，他出賽73場，2成57打擊率，12支全壘打，42分打點<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.fangraphs.com/statss.aspx?playerid=15983&position=OF|title=Clint Frazier » Statistics » Batting {{!}} FanGraphs Baseball|access-date=2017-07-10}}</ref>。
===紐約洋基===
由於洋基傷兵不斷，因此弗雷澤提前於2017年7月1日初登板<ref>{{cite web |url=https://nypost.com/2017/07/01/clint-frazier-latest-promotion-in-wild-week-of-yankees-call-ups/|title=Clint Frazier latest promotion in wild week of Yankees call-ups|first=George A.|last=King III|date=1 July 2017|work=[[New_York_Post|New York Post]]}}</ref>。他首打席就擊出二壘安打，更在下個打席擊出生涯首轟<ref>{{cite web |url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/239723206/clint-frazier-homers-in-first-mlb-game/?topicId=27118122|title=With HR and 2B, Frazier has debut for ages|first=Bryan|last=Hoch|date=2 July 2017|work=MLB.com}}</ref>。7月8日，洋基在9局下以2比3落後釀酒人，而弗雷澤擊出再見3分砲助洋基取得勝利<ref>{{cite web|title=Clint Frazier hits walk-off HR as Yanks win 5-3 over Brewers|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/241196828/clint-frazier-walks-yankees-off-vs-brewers/|publisher=MLB.com|accessdate=July 8, 2017}}</ref>。7月19日，由於[[大衛·羅伯森|大衛·羅伯森]]的回歸，弗雷澤因此從原本的30號改穿77號<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/baseball/yankees/clint-frazier-switches-no-77-jersey-robertson-takes-no-30-article-1.3338306|title=Clint Frazier switches to No. 77 jersey as Robertson takes No. 30|work=NY Daily News|access-date=2017-07-19|language=en}}</ref>。弗雷澤成為繼[[喬·迪馬喬|喬·迪馬喬]]後，第二位在前15場比賽就有9支長打的洋基球員。8月10日，弗雷澤因左斜肌扭傷而進入10天傷兵名單<ref>{{cite web|last1=Hoch|first1=Bryan|title=Clint Frazier goes on DL with oblique strain, Aaron Hicks activated|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/247435542/aaron-hicks-activated-clint-frazier-to-dl/|publisher=MLB|accessdate=August 10, 2017}}</ref>。而他也沒在季後賽亮相。

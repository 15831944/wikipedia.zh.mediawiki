'''克氏症候群'''（Klinefelter's syndrome）或稱'''XXY'''、'''47XXY症候群'''，是一系列由於男性有兩條或兩條以上的[[X染色體|X染色體]]所導致的疾病<ref name=NIH2013Def>{{cite web|title=Klinefelter Syndrome (KS): Overview|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/Pages/default.aspx|website=nichd.nih.gov|publisher={{tsl|en|Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development||Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development}}|accessdate=15 March 2015|date=2013-11-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150318011309/http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/Pages/default.aspx|archive-date=2015-03-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>。該疾病的主要特徵是不孕<ref name=NIH2013Def/>。通常症狀都很輕微，甚至許多患者根本不知道他們患有該病<!-- <ref name=NIH2013Sym/> -->。

有些時候症狀會非常顯著，可能會出現肌肉虛弱、身高較高、[[運動協調|運動協調]]差、體毛稀少、[[外生殖器|外生殖器]]偏小、[[男性女乳症|男性女乳症]]與缺乏性慾<ref name=NIH2013Sym/>。通常在[[青春期|青春期]]時發現克氏症候群的徵狀<ref name=NIH2012Diag>{{cite web|title=How do health care providers diagnose Klinefelter syndrome (KS)?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/conditioninfo/Pages/diagnosed.aspx|publisher={{tsl|en|Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development||Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development}}|accessdate=15 March 2015|date=2012-11-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150317010050/http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/conditioninfo/Pages/diagnosed.aspx|archive-date=2015-03-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>。智力發展通常與一般人無異，但常見有[[閱讀障礙|閱讀障礙]]或是在說話上有困難<!-- <ref name=NIH2013Sym/> -->。若有超過三條X染色體，則患者的症狀會比較嚴重<ref name=NIH2013Sym>{{cite web|title=What are common symptoms of Klinefelter syndrome (KS)?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/conditioninfo/Pages/symptoms.aspx|publisher={{tsl|en|Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development||Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development}}|accessdate=15 March 2015|date=2013-10-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402143647/http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/conditioninfo/Pages/symptoms.aspx|archive-date=2015-04-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

克氏症候群的發病是隨機的<!-- <ref name=NIH2012Epi/> -->。随着孕婦年紀增大，懷有罹患此疾病的胎兒機會也逐渐提高<!-- <ref name=NIH2012Epi/> -->。這個疾病沒有[[遺傳|遺傳性]]，所以不會由患病的父親遺傳給下一代<ref name=NIH2012Epi/>。這個疾病的原發機制是在原先就有Y染色體的前提下有一條以上的多餘的X染色體，患者體內的總[[染色體|染色體]]數目至少為47條甚至更多，無論如何，都較一般46條染色體為多<ref name = Vis2006 >{{cite journal | author = Visootsak J, Graham JM | title = Klinefelter syndrome and other sex chromosomal aneuploidies | journal = Orphanet Journal of Rare Diseases | volume = 1 | pages = 42 | year = 2006 | pmid = 17062147 | pmc = 1634840 | doi = 10.1186/1750-1172-1-42 | last2 = Graham Jr }}</ref>。克氏症候群能夠藉著檢測染色體[[核型|核型]]的[[基因檢測|基因檢測]]來獲得確診<ref name=NIH2012Diag/>。

雖然目前尚沒有辦法可以完全治癒克氏症候群，但有一些醫學治療對改善疾病有不小的幫助<ref name=NIH2012Prog/>。[[物理治療|物理治療]]、[[言語治療|言語治療]]、[[輔導|輔導]]，以及調整教學方法等可能有效<!-- <ref name=NIH2013Tx/> --> 。舉例來說，[[物理治療|物理治療]]、[[言語治療|言語治療]]、[[心理輔導|心理輔導]]以及適當調整教學方法等等都能協助患者。{{le|睪固酮補充療法|Androgen_replacement_therapy}}對於賀爾蒙嚴重不足或是低下的患者或有助益<!-- <ref name=NIH2013Tx/> -->。已經發育生成的乳房組織也可以藉由手術的方法移除<!-- <ref name=NIH2013Tx/> -->。約有一半的男性患者，藉由{{le|人工輔助生殖技術|Assisted_reproductive_technology}}的幫助能有機會擁有自己的小孩，但是，這個是有相當的風險以及所費不貲<ref name=NIH2013Tx>{{cite web|title=What are the treatments for symptoms in Klinefelter syndrome (KS)?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/conditioninfo/Pages/treatments.aspx|publisher={{tsl|en|Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development||Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development}}|accessdate=15 March 2015|date=2013-10-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150315093521/http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/conditioninfo/Pages/treatments.aspx|archive-date=2015-03-15|dead-url=no}}</ref> 。患者得乳癌的機率偏高，但仍低於女性得乳癌的機率<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Brinton|first1=LA|title=Breast cancer risk among patients with Klinefelter syndrome.|journal=Acta paediatrica (Oslo, Norway : 1992)|date=June 2011|volume=100|issue=6|pages=814-8|pmid=21241366}}</ref>。此外，克氏症候群病患本身的平均壽命無異於一般人<ref name=NIH2012Prog>{{cite web|title=Is there a cure for Klinefelter syndrome (KS)?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/conditioninfo/Pages/cure.aspx|publisher={{tsl|en|Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development||Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development}}|accessdate=16 March 2015|date=2012-11-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150317020352/http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/klinefelter/conditioninfo/Pages/cure.aspx|archive-date=2015-03-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

'''克里安西斯'''（英語：Cleanthes，{{bd|前331年||前232年|}}<ref>Adkins,Lesley Adkins,Roy A.（1998）. ''Handbook to Life in Ancient Rome''.Oxford University Press,USA.ISBN 9780195123326.</ref>），[[阿苏斯的法尼亚斯|阿苏斯的法尼亚斯]]之子，[[斯多噶学派|斯多噶学派]][[哲学家|哲学家]]。<ref name="age1">According to [[Apollodorus_of_Athens|Apollodorus]] as quoted by [[Philodemus|Philodemus]], Cleanthes was born in Aristophanes' archonship (331/0 BC) and died in Jason's Archonship (230/29 BC). [[Pseudo-Lucian|Pseudo-Lucian]], [[Valerius_Maximus|Valerius Maximus]], and [[Censorinus|Censorinus]] say that Cleanthes lived to the age of 99 (although [[Diogenes_Laërtius|Diogenes Laërtius]] says he died at the age of 80.)  For more information see Tiziano Dorandi, ''Chapter 2: Chronology'', in Algra et al. (1999) ''The Cambridge History of Hellenistic Philosophy'', page 38. Cambridge. Dorandi prefers an age of 101.</ref><ref name="diog1">Diogenes Laërtius, [http://www.fordham.edu/halsall/ancient/diogeneslaertius-book7-stoics.html ''The Lives and Opinions of Eminent Philosophers'', vii].</ref>在学习哲学前是个拳击手，后在[[雅典|雅典]]师从[[季蒂昂的芝诺|季蒂昂的芝诺]]，并成为[[哲学|哲学]]斯多噶学派的领袖。<ref>Barnes 1988: ''Hellenistic''{{Page needed}}{{ISBN missing}}</ref>

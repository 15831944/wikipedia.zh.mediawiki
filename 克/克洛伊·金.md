'''克洛伊·金'''（{{lang-en|'''Chloe Kim'''}}，{{bd|2000年|4月23日|}}），韩裔美国[[单板滑雪|单板滑雪]]女将。在[[2018年平昌冬奥会|2018年平昌冬奥会]]上，年仅17岁克洛伊赢得单板滑雪U型场地项目的金牌，成为最年轻的单板滑雪奥运冠军<ref>{{cite web |url=https://twitter.com/espn/status/963238855355879424 |title=ESPN Twitter |publisher=Twitter |date=2018-02-12 |accessdate=2018-02-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180221104500/https://twitter.com/espn/status/963238855355879424 |archive-date=2018-02-21 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。此外，她是[[世界极限运动会|世界极限运动会]]四届金牌得主，还是首位在[[冬季青年奥林匹克运动会|冬季青年奥林匹克运动会]]单板滑雪项目夺得两枚金牌的女选手<ref>{{cite web |url=http://xgames.espn.com/xgames/athletes/3088782/chloe-kim |title=Chloe Kim X Games Bio |publisher=ESPN |accessdate=2018-02-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180213091037/http://xgames.espn.com/xgames/athletes/3088782/chloe-kim |archive-date=2018-02-13 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Team USA Youth Olympics">{{cite web|url=http://www.teamusa.org/News/2016/February/14/Led-By-Teen-Sensation-Chloe-Kim-Team-USA-Wins-Three-Snowboarding-Medals-at-Youth-Olympics|title=Led By Teen Sensation Chloe Kim, Team USA Wins Three Snowboarding Medals at Youth Olympics|work=Team USA|date=2016-02-14|accessdate=2018-12-13}}</ref>。

== 早年 ==
金在美国[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[长滩_(加利福尼亚州)|长滩]]出生，在附近的[[托伦斯|托伦斯]]长大<ref name="NBC Yap"/>。父母均来自[[韩国|韩国]]。为了女儿学习滑雪，父亲辞掉工作，专程开车带她上山滑雪。每逢有比赛，父亲都会陪着金去参加。4岁时，金在[[南加利福尼亚州|南加州]]度假胜地{{le|高山滑雪场|Mountain High}}完成对单板滑雪的启蒙，6岁时便随高山滑雪队参加比赛<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://people.com/sports/winter-olympics-2018-chloe-kim-dad-sacrifice/|title=Best Dad Ever? How Chloe Kim's Father Quit His Job to Help Make Her Olympic Dream Come True|work=PEOPLE.com|access-date=2018-04-02|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180402101644/http://people.com/sports/winter-olympics-2018-chloe-kim-dad-sacrifice/|archive-date=2018-04-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>。8到10岁时，她在[[瑞士|瑞士]][[瓦莱州|瓦莱州]]受训，之后回到加州在{{le|猛犸山滑雪场|Mammoth Mountain Ski Area}}训练。2013年，金加入美国国家单板滑雪队<ref>{{cite web|url=https://usskiandsnowboard.org/athletes/chloe-kim |title=Chloe Kim Bio |publisher=US Ski and Snowboarding Team |accessdate=2018-02-12}}</ref>。

== 生涯 ==
金由于年龄不符标准无法参加[[2014年索契冬奥会|2014年索契冬奥会]]，但她仍有资格参加同年的[[世界极限运动会|冬季世界极限运动会]]，她在该赛事中赢得超级U型场项目银牌，仅次于{{le|凯莉·克拉克|Kelly Clark}}。次年，克洛伊击败克莱克，赢得U型场金牌<ref>{{cite web|author=Scott Willough |url=http://www.denverpost.com/olympics/ci_24994810/kelly-clark-wins-superpipe-and-chloe-kim-13 |title=Kelly Clark wins superpipe, and Chloe Kim, 13, earns silver |publisher=The Denver Post Twitter|work=Twitter}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/early-lead/wp/2016/01/31/kelly-sildaru-13-becomes-youngest-athlete-ever-to-win-winter-x-games-gold/|title=Kelly Sildaru, 13, becomes youngest athlete ever to win Winter X Games gold|author=Oliver Macklin|date=2016-01-31|work=Washington Post}}</ref>。在2016年的极限运动会上，她成了首位在一届极限运动会上连夺两枚金牌的16岁以下选手<ref>{{cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/espnw/sports/article/14752956/2016-year-firsts-x-games-snowboarder-chloe-kim|title=2016 A Year Of Firsts For X Games Snowboarder Chloe Kim|work=espnW}}</ref><ref name="charleslam">{{cite web|url=http://www.nbcnews.com/news/asian-america/chloe-kim-becomes-first-woman-land-two-1080-spins-wins-n513911|title=Chloe Kim Becomes First Woman to Land Two 1080 Spins, name="charleslam"/><ref name="NBC Yap">{{cite news|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/news/asian-america/16-year-old-snowboarding-champion-chloe-kim-just-normal-teenager-n575411|title=16-Year-Old [[2016年冬季青年奧林匹克運動會|2016年冬季青年奧林匹克運動會]]上，克洛伊被选为美国代表队开幕式旗手，成为首位在冬奥会或青奥会上担任美国国家队旗手的单板滑雪运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.teamusa.org/News/2016/February/11/Chloe-Kim-Selected-As-Team-USA-Flag-Bearer-For-Lillehammer-2016-Winter-Youth-Olympic-Games|title=Chloe Kim Selected As Team USA Flag Bearer For Lillehammer 2016 Winter Youth Olympic Games|work=Team USA}}</ref>。冬青奥会期间，她在女子U型场地决赛中以96.50创下当时青奥会的高分纪录，并成为首位赢得青奥会单板滑雪金牌的美国女选手<ref name="Team USA Youth Olympics" />。后来她获{{le|年度卓越运动员大奖|ESPY Award}}最佳突破运动员奖提名<ref>{{cite web|title=List of 2016 ESPYS Nominees|url=http://espnmediazone.com/us/list-2016-espys-nominees/|website=ESPN MediaZone|accessdate=2016-07-18}}</ref>。

[[2018年平昌冬奥会|2018年平昌冬奥会]]是金首次参加冬奥会。在教练瑞奇·鲍威尔（{{lang|en|Ricky Bower}}）的指导下，她在女子U型场地决赛中夺得金牌<ref>[http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/more-sports/chloe-kim-american-phenom-wins-gold-women-halfpipe-finals-article-1.3816677 Chloe Kim, 17-year-old American snowboarding phenom, wins gold at women's halfpipe finals, Arielle Gold takes bronze] {{Wayback|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/more-sports/chloe-kim-american-phenom-wins-gold-women-halfpipe-finals-article-1.3816677 |date=20181213094212 }}; New York Daily News, February 12, 2018</ref>。她的首轮得分93.75领先第二名8.5分，次轮得分接近满分100，为98.25分，领先第二的中国选手[[刘佳宇|刘佳宇]]将近10分<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.olympic.org/pyeongchang-2018/results/en/snowboard/results-ladies-halfpipe-fnl-000300-.htm|title=Snowboard {{!}} Results Ladies' Halfpipe Final Run 3 - Pyeongchang 2018 Olympic Winter Games|website=www.olympic.org|language=en|access-date=2018-03-30}}</ref>。她也是在奥运会上实现1080翻转两周的最年轻女选手<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://edition.cnn.com/2018/02/13/sport/chloe-kim-snowboard-winter-olympics-intl/index.html|title=Chloe Kim: US teenager makes history at Winter Olympics|last=Lewis|first=Aimee|work=CNN|access-date=2018-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180330212308/https://edition.cnn.com/2018/02/13/sport/chloe-kim-snowboard-winter-olympics-intl/index.html|archive-date=2018-03-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>。17岁夺取奥运金牌的她，也打破了克莱克18岁夺冠的纪录<ref>{{cite news|url=http://time.com/5151999/chloe-kim-gold-olympics-2018/|title=Chloe Kim Wins Snowboard Halfpipe Gold in High Style|website=Time|language=en|access-date=2018-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180328170626/http://time.com/5151999/chloe-kim-gold-olympics-2018/|archive-date=2018-03-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

== 私生活 ==
金是[[韩裔美国人|韩裔美国人]]，属于第二代美国人。父母都是韩国来的移民。她的许多亲人还在韩国。她的奶奶曾亲自去平昌奥运赛场看她比赛<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cbssports.com/olympics/news/how-chloe-kim-became-the-next-shaun-white-of-the-winter-olympics-7-things-to-know/|title=How Chloe Kim became the next Shaun White of the Olympics: 7 things to know|work=CBSSports.com|access-date=2018-04-02|language=en}}</ref>。金擅长多国语言，[[韩语|韩语]]、[[法语|法语]]和[[英语|英语]]流利<ref>{{cite web |author=Shawn Smith |url=http://www.nbcolympics.com/news/who-is-chloe-kim-olympic-snowboarding-halfpipe-team-usa |title=Who is Chloe Kim? |publisher=NBC |accessdate=2018-02-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180302161530/http://www.nbcolympics.com/news/who-is-chloe-kim-olympic-snowboarding-halfpipe-team-usa |archive-date=2018-03-02 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。夺取奥运金牌后，金登上《[[体育画报|体育画报]]》封面<ref>{{cite web |title=Photos: Chloe Kim's Sports Illustrated cover outtakes |url=https://www.si.com/olympic-snowboarding/photo/2018/02/20/usa-snowboarding-gold-medalist-chloe-kim |website=SI.com |accessdate=2018-12-13 |language=en |date=2018-02-20 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181213094141/https://www.si.com/olympic-snowboarding/photo/2018/02/20/usa-snowboarding-gold-medalist-chloe-kim |archive-date=2018-12-13 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Rosenberg |first1=Michael |title=Young Olympians set to star longer than ever before |url=https://www.si.com/olympics/2018/02/19/2018-winter-olympics-chloe-kim-nathan-chen-vincent-zhou |website=Sports Illustrated |accessdate=2018-12-13 |language=en |date=2018-02-19}}</ref>，并为[[家乐氏|家乐氏]][[玉米片|玉米片]]代言，[[芭比|芭比]]以她的形象打造了玩偶产品<ref name="bendbulletin1">{{Cite news|url=http://www.bendbulletin.com/sports/6078003-151/after-winning-halfpipe-gold-american-snowboarder-puts-fame|title=After winning halfpipe gold, American snowboarder puts fame, fun in perspective|last=Writ|first=By EDDIE PELLS AP National|date=2018-03-12|work=The Bulletin|access-date=2018-04-02|language=en-US}}</ref>。网友们在金夺冠后涌入她的[[社交媒体|社交媒体]]帐号互动，她因此成为网络红人<ref name="bendbulletin1"/>。此外，金被[[普林斯顿大学|普林斯顿大学]]破格录取为2022届学生。在2018年年度卓越运动员大奖上，她收获最佳女运动员、最佳女奥运运动员和最佳体育运动员三项大奖<ref>{{cite web |title=ESPYS 2018 Winners: The Complete List |url=https://www.eonline.com/news/949722/espys-2018-winners-the-complete-list |website=E! News |first=Aiello |last=Mckenna |date=2018-07-18 |accessdate=2018-07-23}}</ref>。

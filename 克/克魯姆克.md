'''克魯姆克'''（{{lang-en|Krumkake}}、{{lang-no|ˈkrʉmˌkɑːkə}}）是由麵粉、黄油、雞蛋、食糖和鮮奶油製成的曲奇，並以類似[[意大利|意大利]]裝飾在{{link-en|鐵板 (烹調器具)|Griddle|鐵板}}的[[法蘭酥|法蘭酥]]、[[西西里飲食|西西里飲食]]或傳統用於烘烤圓形蛋糕的[[窩夫烘烤模|窩夫烘烤模]]<ref>Aase Strømstad: ''Mat på norsk. Tradisjonsrike retter fra hele landet'', Aschehoug forlag 1985, side 98, {{ISBN|82-03-11408-3}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Nordic Traditions In The Shadow Of Bonner Mountain|publisher=MTPR|date=2016-12-23|author=RACHEL CRAMER|url=http://mtpr.org/post/nordic-traditions-shadow-bonner-mountain|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170626002711/http://mtpr.org/post/nordic-traditions-shadow-bonner-mountain|archivedate=2017-06-26|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。

[[克里夫蘭布朗|克里夫蘭布朗]]是[[克里夫蘭|克里夫蘭]]的職業美式足球隊。布朗為[[國家美式足球聯盟|國家美式足球聯盟]](NFL)[[美國美式足球聯會|美國美式足球聯會]](AFC)的北區成員之一。

自從1950年加盟NFL開始，布朗隊總共擁有57名不同的先發[[四分衛|四分衛]]。[[職業美式足球名人堂|職業美式足球名人堂]]成員[[奧托·格雷厄姆|奧托·格雷厄姆]]是布朗的第一個先發四分衛，帶領球隊在聯盟的頭六個賽季拿到三次冠軍。布朗自1999年缺席三個賽季後重新加盟以來，就因為四分衛的人選太過無用而備受注目。<ref name=Nadkarni>{{cite news|last1=Nadkarni|first1=Rohan|title=Timeline: Browns quarterbacks since 1999|url=https://www.si.com/nfl/cleveland-browns-quarterbacks-timeline|accessdate=October 17, 2016|work=Sports Illustrated|date=March 24, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Wilder|first1=Charlotte|title=The Browns' quarterback woes are about to reach a historic new low|url=http://ftw.usatoday.com/2016/09/cleveland-browns-woes-rg3-multiple-starting-quarterbacks-15-years|accessdate=October 17, 2016|work=USA Today|date=September 13, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Knox|first1=Luke|title=Browns' QB Carousel|url=http://www.espn.com/espn/feature/story/_/page/hotread-160908/robert-griffin-iii-24-browns-qbs-him|publisher=ESPN|accessdate=October 17, 2016}}</ref>自1999年至2018年賽季的第4週，布朗就用了31名不同的先發四分衛。[[蒂姆·庫奇|蒂姆·庫奇]]在1999年選秀第一輪被布朗選中，他也是目前布朗唯一一位在單一賽季16場比賽中全數先發的四分衛。<ref name=Nadkarni/>布朗已經連續17個賽季有一位以上的先發四分衛。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Kerr-Dineen|first1=Luke|title=16 depressing stats about the Browns' never-ending quarterback crisis|url=http://ftw.usatoday.com/2016/10/cleveland-browns-quarterback-crisis-injury-whitehurst-mccown-browns-kessler|accessdate=October 17, 2016|work=USA Today|date=October 9, 2016}}</ref>

==Most 先發==

quarterbacks who have started for the Cleveland Browns from the team's first NFL season of 1950 to present. A player is credited with a win if he started the game and the team won that game, no matter if the player was injured or permanently removed after the first play from scrimmage. Only regular season games are included. The Browns' [[All-America_Football_Conference|All-America Football Conference]] games from 1946 to 1949 are not included.

(Note: the Browns did not field a team during the years 1996–98.)

Sorted by number of starts. Members of the [[Pro_Football_Hall_of_Fame|Pro Football Hall of Fame]] in italics and most recent starter in '''bold'''.

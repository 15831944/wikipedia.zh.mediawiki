'''克雷伯·曼東沙·費侯'''（{{lang-pt|Kleber Mendonça Filho}}）是巴西電影導演、編劇、製片與影評人。<ref>{{cite web|title=Kleber Mendonça Filho|url=http://www.filmeb.com.br/quem-e-quem/diretor/kleber-mendonca-filho|website=Filme B|accessdate=25 August 2015|language=pt}}</ref>

== 生平 ==
克雷伯·曼東沙·費侯自新聞系畢業後，他先擔任影評與記者。<ref>{{cite web|title=Revista Continente|last1=Mendonça Filho|first1=Kleber|url=http://www.revistacontinente.com.br/|website=Revista Continente|accessdate=25 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Cinetica|last1=Mendonça Filho|first1=Kleber|url=http://revistacinetica.com.br/home/|website=Revista Cinetica|accessdate=25 August 2015}}</ref>1990年代，他開始嘗試執導紀錄片、劇情片與攝像作品，他於2000年代從錄像轉投入數位與35釐米影片的拍攝，拍攝了許多短片。

2013年，他完成第一部劇情長片《{{link-pt|鄰居的狗吠聲|O Som ao Redor}}》，於[[鹿特丹影展|鹿特丹影展]]首映後便獲得許多影評讚賞，[[紐約時報|紐約時報]]影評人A. O. Scott將其列入他2012年十大佳片名單；本片也代表巴西角逐奧斯卡最佳外語片，但未獲提名。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2013/film/news/brazil-sends-neighboring-sounds-to-oscars-1200654580/|title=Brazil Sends ‘Neighboring Sounds’ to Oscars|accessdate=September 20, 2013|work=Variety}}</ref><ref name="Brazil">{{cite web|url=http://www.globalpost.com/dispatch/news/afp/130920/brazil-picks-o-som-ao-redor-oscars-entry |title=Brazil picks "O Som ao Redor" as Oscars entry |accessdate=2013-09-21 |work=Global Post |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130922134124/http://www.globalpost.com/dispatch/news/afp/130920/brazil-picks-o-som-ao-redor-oscars-entry |archivedate=2013-09-22 }}</ref> 

2016年，克雷伯·曼東沙·費侯執導的第二部長片《{{link-en|水瓶女人心|Aquarius (film)}}》獲選為[[第69屆坎城影展|第69屆坎城影展]]正式競賽片。<ref name="IW">{{cite web |url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/cannes-live-stream-2016-film-festival-lineup-list |title=2016 Cannes Film Festival Announces Lineup |accessdate=14 April 2016 |work=IndieWire}}</ref><ref name="VarCannes">{{cite web |url=http://variety.com/2016/film/festivals/cannes-film-festival-official-selection-lineup-2016-1201753269/ |title=Cannes 2016: Film Festival Unveils Official Selection Lineup |accessdate=14 April 2016 |work=Variety}}</ref>

<!-- Definition and medical uses -->
'''克林黴素'''（[[國際非專利藥品名稱|國際非專利藥品名稱]]：Clindamycin <span class="audiolink">[[WP:IPA|/klɪndəˈmaɪsɨn/]]</span>）是一種[[抗生素|抗生素]]，<ref name=AHFS2015>{{cite web|title=Clindamycin Hydrochloride|url=http://www.drugs.com/monograph/clindamycin-hydrochloride.html|publisher=The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|accessdate=Sep 4, 2015}}</ref>可治療[[中耳炎|中耳炎]]、骨骼或[[化膿性關節炎|關節發炎]]、[[骨盆腔發炎感染|骨盆腔發炎感染]]、[[鏈球菌咽炎|鏈球菌咽炎]]、[[肺炎|肺炎]]、[[心內膜|心內膜]]炎等多種[[感染|感染]]；<ref name=AHFS2015/>有時也會用於治療[[耐甲氧西林金黄色葡萄球菌|耐甲氧西林金黄色葡萄球菌]]（MRSA）造成的感染。<ref name=Daum2007>{{cite journal |author=Daum RS |title=Clinical practice. Skin and soft-tissue infections caused by methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus |journal=N Engl J Med |volume=357 |issue=4 |pages=380–90 |year=2007 |pmid=17652653 |doi=10.1056/NEJMcp070747}}</ref>克林黴素也被使用於[[青春痘|青春痘]]的治療；還可以和[[奎寧|奎寧]]合併使用，治療[[瘧疾|瘧疾]]。<!-- <ref name=AHFS2015/><ref name=Ley2006/> -->克林黴素可以口服、[[靜脈注射|靜脈注射]]，也可以以藥膏形式擦在皮膚、陰道。<ref name=AHFS2015/><ref name=Ley2006>{{cite book|last1=Leyden|first1=James J.|title=Hidradenitis suppurativa|date=2006|publisher=Springer|location=Berlin|isbn=9783540331018|page=152|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=hpKFsXwcKlgC&pg=PA152}}</ref>

<!-- Side effects and mechanism -->
克林黴素最常見的副作用有噁心、嘔吐、紅疹和注射部位的疼痛。<ref name=AHFS2015/>克林黴素會使偽膜性腸炎的風險提高四倍之多；<ref name=Thomas>{{cite journal |vauthors=Thomas C, Stevenson M, Riley TV |title=Antibiotics and hospital-acquired Clostridium difficile-associated diarrhoea: a systematic review |journal=J Antimicrob Chemother |volume=51 |issue=6 |pages=1339–50 |year=2003 |pmid=12746372 |doi=10.1093/jac/dkg254 | url = http://jac.oxfordjournals.org/content/51/6/1339.full.pdf }}</ref>若有這方面的考量可改用其他抗生素。<!-- <ref name=AHFS2015/> -->克林黴素可以安全用於孕婦。<!-- <ref name=AHFS2015/> --> 克林黴素屬於[[林可酰胺类抗生素|林可酰胺类抗生素]]，作用機轉為抑制細菌合成蛋白質。<ref name=AHFS2015/>

<!-- History, society and culture -->
克林黴素於1967年被首次合成。<ref>{{cite book|title=Neonatal Formulary: Drug Use in Pregnancy and the First Year of Life|date=2014|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=9781118819517|page=162|edition=7|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=EORvBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA162}}</ref>它被列入[[世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单|世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单]]中，是基層[[衛生系統|衛生系統]]需要的最重要的藥物之一。<ref>{{cite web|title=WHO Model List of Essential Medicines|url=http://apps.who.int/iris/bitstream/10665/93142/1/EML_18_eng.pdf?ua=1|work=World Health Organization|accessdate=22 April 2014|date=October 2013}}</ref>它也被製成許多種廉價的[[通用名药物|通用名药物]]。<ref name=Ric2015>{{cite book|last1=Hamilton|first1=Richart|title=Tarascon Pocket Pharmacopoeia 2015 Deluxe Lab-Coat Edition|date=2015|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|isbn=9781284057560|page=108}}</ref>其批發價約為每片0.06至0.12美元；<ref name=ERC2014>{{cite web|title=Clindamycin|url=http://erc.msh.org/dmpguide/resultsdetail.cfm?language=english&code=CLI300T&s_year=2014&year=2014&str=300%20mg&desc=Clindamycin&pack=new&frm=TAB-CAP&rte=PO&class_code2=06%2E2%2E2%2E&supplement=&class_name=%2806%2E2%2E2%2E%29Other%20antibacterials%3Cbr%3E|website=International Drug Price Indicator Guide|accessdate=6 September 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160107115031/http://erc.msh.org/dmpguide/resultsdetail.cfm?language=english&code=CLI300T&s_year=2014&year=2014&str=300%20mg&desc=Clindamycin&pack=new&frm=TAB-CAP&rte=PO&class_code2=06%2E2%2E2%2E&supplement=&class_name=%2806%2E2%2E2%2E%29Other%20antibacterials%3Cbr%3E|archivedate=2016年1月7日}}</ref>在美國其零售價約為每劑量2.7美元。<ref name=AHFS2015/>

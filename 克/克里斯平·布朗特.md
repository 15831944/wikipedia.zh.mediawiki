'''克里斯平·布朗特'''（Crispin Blunt，{{bd|1960年|7月15日}}）是一位[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]政治人物，他的黨籍是[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]。自1997年開始，他擔任[[賴蓋特_(英國國會選區)|賴蓋特]]選區選出的英國下議院議員<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-surrey-24993541 | work=BBC News | title=Local Conservatives support Reigate's Crispin Blunt | date=18 November 2013}}</ref>。他在2010年出櫃並和妻子離婚<ref>Victoria was devastated.[http://conservativehome.blogs.com/thetorydiary/2010/08/crispin-blunt-decides-to-come-to-terms-with-his-homosexuality-and-separates-from-his-wife.html Crispin Blunt decides "to come to terms with his homosexuality" and separates from his wife], ''[[ConservativeHome|ConservativeHome]]'', 27 August 2010.</ref><ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-11115182 Conservative minister Crispin Blunt reveals he is gay], ''[[BBC_News_Online|BBC News Online]]'', 27 August 2010.</ref>。

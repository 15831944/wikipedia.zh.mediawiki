'''克里斯托弗·乔丹·多尔纳'''（{{lang|en|'''Christopher Jordan Dorner'''}}，{{bd|1979年|6月4日|2013年|2月12日|catIdx=Christopher}}）<ref>{{cite web|author=Johnson, Andy |url=http://www.ctvnews.ca/world/who-was-christopher-dorner-and-why-was-he-so-angry-1.1155046 |title=Who was Christopher Dorner and why was he so angry? |work=[[CTV_News_Channel_(Canada)|CTV]] |accessdate=February 13, 2013|date=February 13, 2013}}</ref>是一名前[[洛杉矶警察局|洛杉矶警察局]]警官、前[[美国海军|美国海军]]预备役隊員。他是[[2013年美國南加州連續槍擊案|2013年美國南加州連續槍擊案]]的主要嫌疑犯，该案件涉及2月3日至12日对于警察的一系列枪击并导致4人（其中2名警察）身亡，另有4名警察受伤。他成为了洛杉矶警察局历史上最大规模抓捕行动之一的抓捕对象，<ref name=Tomlinson>{{cite web|last=Tomlinson|first=Simon|title=Killer ex-cop who left three dead in LA shooting spree sends CNN's Anderson Cooper a bullet-riddled coin and manifesto declaring vendetta against police department that fired him |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2274956/Christopher-Dorner-left-3-dead-sent-Anderson-Cooper-bullet-riddled-coin-vendetta-LAPD.html#axzz2KSdr9614|publisher=Daily Mail UK|accessdate=February 10, 2013|coauthors=Tim Perone, Michael Zennie|date=February 8, 2013}}</ref>这次抓捕行动共涉及美国4个州以及邻国[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=Dorner manhunt stretches from L.A. to Mexico and beyond|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/lanow/2013/02/dorner-manhunt-los-angeles-mexico.html?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+lanowblog+(L.A.+Now)|work=latimes.com|publisher=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=13 February 2013|date=12 February 2013}}</ref>

在枪击案发生期间，多尔纳同母亲居住于加州[[拉帕尔马_(加利福尼亚州)|拉帕尔马]]。多尔纳无子女，法庭记录显示他和妻子在2007年离婚。<ref name='pe'>{{cite news | first = | last = | title = Dorner Manhunt: Career woes, perceived racism fuel ex-cop's anger| date = February 7, 2013 | url = http://www.pe.com/local-news/local-news-headlines/20130207-dorner-manhunt-career-woes-perceived-racism-fuel-ex-cops-anger.ece| work = [[The_Press-Enterprise|The Press-Enterprise]] | accessdate = February 7, 2013}}</ref>

2月11日，地区检察官决定以对一名警官的谋杀罪以及对3名警官的谋杀未遂罪起诉多尔纳。<ref name="usatoday">{{cite web|title=Dorner charged with murder, attempted murder of cops|url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/nation/2013/02/11/los-angeles-murder-charges-dorner/1910643/|publisher=USA Today|accessdate=February 11, 2013}}</ref>次日，据信是多尔纳者又卷入了一起枪击案，导致一名警官死亡，而枪击者本人在其藏身的小屋被火烧尽后，警方在屋內找到一具遺體，經確認遺體即為多尔纳。<ref name=dorner>{{cite web|title=BREAKING: Dorner Surrounded After Big Bear Shootout Kills Officer|url=http://www.sandiego6.com/news/local/BREAKING-Dorner-Shootout-with-Police-in-Big-Bear-Injures-Officers-190918541.html?m=y&smobile=y|publisher=San Diego 6|accessdate=12 February 2013}}</ref>

== 早年 ==

多尔纳生于1979年的[[纽约|纽约]]，成长于加州[[洛杉矶县|洛杉矶县]]。他在诺沃克基督教学校（Norwalk Christian School）完成了1到7年级的学业。在他公开发布的宣言当中，多尔纳表示由于自己是学校里唯一的[[非洲裔美国人|非洲裔美国人]]，他和同学们之间发生诸多种族问题，而他成长的环境也少有黑人。他常常因为其他学生的种族歧视语言而发生打斗，并因此受到责罚。他先后就读于拉帕尔马的约翰·F·肯尼迪高中、[[赛普里斯_(加利福尼亚州)|赛普里斯]]的赛普里斯高中，并在1997年毕业。2001年，他从[[南犹他大学|南犹他大学]]毕业，主修[[政治学|政治学]]，辅修[[心理学|心理学]]。他的大学已确认多尔纳在校期间曾打过至少两年橄榄球。<ref name='laist'>{{cite news| first = | last = | title = Christopher Dorner's Manifesto, In Full (Content Graphic and Disturbing) – UPDATED| date = February 7, 2013| url = http://laist.com/2013/02/07/christopher_dorners_manifesto_in_fu.php| work = [[LAist|LAist]]| accessdate = February 7, 2013| archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20130227045151/http://laist.com/2013/02/07/christopher_dorners_manifesto_in_fu.php| archive-date = 2013-02-27| dead-url = yes}}</ref><ref> {{cite news | title =Anaheim Union HS officials issue statement on Chris Dorner; 'No Danger To Students' cited | date = February 7, 2013 | url = http://www.loscerritosnews.net/2013/02/07/anaheim-union-hs-officials-issue-statement-on-chris-dorner-no-danger-to-students-cited/ | publisher = loscerritosnews.net | accessdate = February 9, 2013}}</ref>身为[[跑鋒|跑鋒]]的多尔纳在1999年赛季共出战6场，在10次进攻中推进了36码。<ref>{{cite web|title=Southern Utah Univ Overall Individual Statistics|url=http://www.suu.edu/Athletics/Football/$TEAM.HTM|date=November 20, 1999|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20000118165406/http://www.suu.edu/Athletics/Football/$TEAM.HTM|archivedate=2000-01-18|publisher=Southern Utah University|access-date=2013-02-15|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

== 海军预备役 ==

多尔纳为前海军预备役[[上尉_(海軍)|上尉]]（O-3）。他于2002年应征入伍，负责指挥[[内华达州|内华达州]]法伦海军航空基地的一只安保小分队，2004年6月23日至2006年2月28日期间被编入近岸水下机动作战小分队（Mobile Inshore Undersea Warfare Unit）。2006年11月3日至次年4月23日，他随海岸河岸作战2大队（Coastal Riverine Group Two）被派驻到[[巴林|巴林]]。<ref>McGregor, Ellen ''U.S. Navy Releases Records of Triple Shooting Suspect Christopher Dorner'' ABC 10 News  02/07/2013 </ref>多尔纳于2013年2月1日正式从海军预备役光荣退役。

2002年，多尔纳及其同学拾到一个装有接近8千美元现金的包裹，这是属于位于[[俄克拉何马州|俄克拉何马州]]伊尼德的伊尼德朝鲜闵恩教堂的。他们将包裹上交警察。在问到动机时，多尔纳曾表示：“这事关正直。”“军队强调人的正直，包裹里有上千美元，如果人们乐于将其捐给教堂，那么对于他们来说这一定意义重大。”他还提到自己的母亲教导他做人要诚实、正直。<ref>{{cite news | title = Vance students turn in lost church money | date = Enid News & Eagle on Nov 5, 2002 | url = http://enidnews.com/localnews/x964898713/ENE-Archives-Vance-students-turn-in-lost-church-money | publisher = Enid News & Eagle | accessdate = February 11, 2013}}</ref>

== 就职洛城警署 ==
2005年，多尔纳加入洛杉矶警察局，并与次年完成警官学校的训练。<ref name = "LATimes_2013-02-07_b">{{cite news
  | title       = Police say ex-cop was bent on exacting revenge
  | url         = http://www.latimes.com/news/local/la-me-dorner-profile-20130207,0,3985225,full.story
  | publisher   = [[洛杉矶时报|Los Angeles Times]]
  | date        = February 7, 2013
  | accessdate  = February 8, 2013
}}</ref>2008年9月4日，他因为提交了一份举报同僚作为的报告而被警署开除。他在报告中指称警官特丽莎·伊万斯（Teresa Evans）不当使用暴力，而警方在调查后表示该指控不实。多尔纳指控伊万斯警官在嫌疑犯克里斯托弗·盖特勒（Christopher Gettler）双手被铐，躺在地上后依然用脚踢其面部。内部调查委员会在调查后认为多尔纳在其报告中说了谎，尽管受害者父亲表示确有其事。<ref>{{cite news | title = Massive manhunt for fired LAPD officer Christopher Dorner leads to San Bernardino Mountains | date = February 7, 2013 | url = http://www.currentargus.com/ci_22538891/massive-manhunt-fired-lapd-officer-suspected-2-killings | publisher = Carlsbad Current-Argus | accessdate = February 9, 2013 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20130212073030/http://www.currentargus.com/ci_22538891/massive-manhunt-fired-lapd-officer-suspected-2-killings | archive-date = 2013-02-12 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>对于涉事的伊万斯警官（现已成为警司）并没有任何惩处行动。在2013年2月初于网上发布的[[宣言|宣言]]中，多尔纳认为自己被革职完全是警署的报复行为，并发誓他报告的施暴行为确实存在，自己计划发动无特定对象暴力行为也是因为此事。

2013年2月9日，洛城警署宣布其将重新审查导致多尔纳被开除的纪律处分流程。<ref> {{cite news | title =LAPD manhunt: Chief Charlie Beck says will reopen case that fired Christopher Dorner| date = February 9, 2013 | url = http://www.scpr.org/blogs/news/2013/02/09/12489/lapd-manhunt-day-3-dorner-search-continues-he-can-/ | publisher = Southern California Public Radio | accessdate = February 9, 2013}}</ref>

== 宣言和枪击案 ==
{{main|2013年美國南加州連續槍擊案}}
在开始枪击案并逃避警方追捕之前，多尔纳据称在网上发布了一则宣言，其中详细述说了自己的历史、动机和计划。<ref>[https://sites.google.com/site/christopherjdorner/home Christopher Dorner Manifesto]</ref>

2013年2月11日，地区检察官决定以对一名警官的谋杀罪以及对三名警官的谋杀未遂罪起诉多尔纳。<ref name="usatoday" />

2013年2月12日，在位于加州大熊湖南的一间住宅，两名清洁女工发现了藏身其中克里斯托弗·多尔纳，多尔纳将她们捆绑起来后，偷盗一辆汽车逃逸。其中的一名妇女成功挣脱并在太平洋时间下午12：20向警方报告。<ref>[http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/lanow/2013/02/christopher-dorner-maids-stumbled-on-suspect-setting-off-chase.html Dorner manhunt: Maids stumbled on suspect, were tied up, then called 911] [[洛杉矶时报|Los Angeles Times]], February 13, 2013</ref>

下午12：45（大平洋时间）来自{{link-en|加州渔业和野生动物局|California Department of Fish and Wildlife}}的看守人发现多尔纳正沿着{{link-en|加利福尼亚州际38号公路|California State Route 38}}行驶到与格拉斯路（Glass Road）交口。多尔纳向一辆有该局标志的车开枪，据报一名看守人开枪还击。<ref name=LAT20130212>{{cite news|last=Cart|first=Julie|title=Dorner manhunt: Fish and Wildlife officers make the big break|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/lanow/2013/02/dorner-manhunt-fish-and-wildlife-officers-make-the-big-break.html|accessdate=February 13, 2013|newspaper=Los Angeles Times|date=February 12, 2013|author2=Stevens, Matt}}</ref><ref>[http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/lanow/2013/02/dorner-fish-and-wildlife-warden-who-fired-on-suspect-was-ex-marine.html Dorner manhunt: Wildlife warden who fired on suspect was ex-Marine; LA Times; February 13, 2013.]</ref>

多尔纳很快被[[圣贝纳迪诺县|圣贝纳迪诺县]]的治安副官围困在安格勒斯奥克斯（Angelus Oaks）东北部的山区地带。期间双方多次交火，两名副官受伤，一名伤势过重死亡。下午4:20（太平洋时间），多尔纳藏身的小屋燃起大火，<ref>[http://www.nbcnews.com/id/50784619/ns/local_news-orange_county_ca/ MANHUNT: Deputy dies in gunfire; cabin burns; NBC News; February 13, 2013.]</ref>其中堆积的弹药开始爆炸。<ref>[http://www.foxnews.com/us/2013/02/12/fugitive-ex-cop-exchanges-fire-with-authorities-ap-source-says/ Fugitive ex-cop believed dead, as cabin stronghold goes up in flames; FoxNews; February 12, 2013.]</ref>

2月13日清晨，圣贝纳迪诺县治安官办公室表示，在小屋的灰烬中发现了烧焦的人体遗骸。<ref>[http://news.blogs.cnn.com/2013/02/12/police-checking-reports-that-ex-lapd-officer-dorner-sighted/ Police: Body found in cabin in hunt for Dorner; CNN News; February 12, 2013.]</ref>在小屋的残骸中，发现了一个钱包，其中有克里斯托弗·多尔纳的加州驾照<ref>{{cite web |title=Dorner's Licence Found In Burned Cabin |url=http://hosted.ap.org/dynamic/stories/U/US_LAPD_REVENGE_KILLINGS?SITE=AP&SECTION=HOME&TEMPLATE=DEFAULT |publisher=AP |accessdate=13 February 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130216053521/http://hosted.ap.org/dynamic/stories/U/US_LAPD_REVENGE_KILLINGS?SITE=AP&SECTION=HOME&TEMPLATE=DEFAULT |archive-date=2013-02-16 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>但是据报道，早在1周前，2月7日，[[圣迭戈|圣迭戈]]警方就已经找到了多尔纳的钱包。<ref>[http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/lanow/2013/02/dorner-manhunt-ex-cop-may-got-help.html Dorner manhunt: Ex-cop may have had help, court records show - latimes.com<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>2月14日，圣贝纳迪诺县治安官办公室表示，通过牙医记录对比，在小屋内发现的尸体已确定为多尔纳。<ref name="death">[http://losangeles.cbslocal.com/2013/02/14/officials-remains-found-in-burned-out-cabin-are-that-of-christopher-dorner/ Officials: Remains Found In Burned-Out Cabin Are That Of Christopher Dorner]</ref>

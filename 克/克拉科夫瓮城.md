'''克拉科夫瓮城''' ({{lang-pl|barbakan krakowski}})是一座曾经与城墙及[[圣福里安门|圣福里安门]]相连的[[瓮城|瓮城]]，控制进入[[克拉科夫老城|克拉科夫老城]]的入口。<ref name="PW">[http://cityguide.krakow.pl/zabytki_en.html Verbia - Guided tours of Krakow] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080531065849/http://cityguide.krakow.pl/zabytki_en.html |date=2008-05-31 }}</ref>，建于1498年，是克拉科夫城墙残留的少量遗迹之一<ref name="MFA">Ministry of Foreign Affairs 2002-2009, [http://www.poland.gov.pl/The,Sites,on,the,UNESCO,List,407.html The Sites on the UNESCO List.] Krakow, at [http://www.poland.gov.pl Poland.gov.pl] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081029120855/http://www.poland.gov.pl/ |date=2008-10-29 }}</ref><ref name="JP">Jane Perlez, [http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9F0CE0DA1538F93BA25754C0A965958260&partner=rssnyt&emc=rss Cracow Emerges From the Shadows] in the [[New_York_Times|New York Times]], July 18, 1993.</ref> 现在是一处旅游景点，举办各种展览<ref name="M-K">Mieczyslaw Kasprzyk, [https://web.archive.org/web/20041210150206/http://www.kasprzyk.demon.co.uk/www/Krakow1.html The Walls, Barbakan and the Florianska] in "Krakow" from the [[Internet_Archive|Internet Archive]]</ref>。

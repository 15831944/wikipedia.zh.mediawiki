'''克熱舒夫修道院'''（Krzeszów Abbey），舊稱格魯索修道院（{{lang-pl|Klasztor w Krzeszowie}}，{{lang-de|Kloster Grüssau}}），是位於[[波蘭|波蘭]][[下西里西亞省|下西里西亞省]]的一座修道院。這座修道院始建於1242年，最初是一座[[本篤會|本篤會]]修道院<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=bU09GwAACAAJ&dq=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&client=firefox-a]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=AU9pAAAAMAAJ&q=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&dq=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&lr=%20&client=firefox-a&pgis=1]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=0SppAAAAMAAJ&q=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&dq=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&lr=%20&client=firefox-a&pgis=1]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=_akAAAAAMAAJ&q=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&dq=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&lr=%20&client=firefox-a&pgis=1]</ref> or '''Grüssau-Wimpfen'''.<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=sgoUAAAAIAAJ&q=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&dq=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&client=firefox-a&pgis=1]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=NQgUAAAAIAAJ&q=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&dq=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&client=firefox-a&pgis=1]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=MWSyAAAAIAAJ&q=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&dq=gr%C3%BCssau+wimpfen&lr=%20&client=firefox-a&pgis=1]</ref>。現在該建築是波蘭國家歷史古蹟之一。

'''克雷格·比爾科'''（'''Craig Bierko'''，{{bd|1964年|8月18日}}）是[[美國|美國]]的一位[[演員|演員]]和[[歌手|歌手]]。比爾科出生在紐約州<ref name="filmr">[http://www.filmreference.com/film/8/Craig-Bierko.html Craig Bierko Film Reference biography]</ref><ref>http://www.nytimes.com/1964/08/23/births.html?_r=0</ref>，他的母親是一位改信天主教的猶太人，比爾科非常自豪他的猶太遺產<ref name="Bierko">{{cite news|last=Lipton|first=Brian Scott|title=MODERN MAN|publisher=TheaterMania|date=2004-12-02|url=http://www.theatermania.com/content/news.cfm/story/5396|accessdate=2006-12-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070210092411/http://www.theatermania.com/content/news.cfm/story/5396|archivedate=2007年2月10日|df=}}<br>"My mother is Jewish, though she converted to Catholicism," Bierko says, "so we ended up being Christmas tree Jews. I grew up in Westchester with a lot of Jewish friends, and I feel very connected to that part of my heritage. When I read the play, it was the first time I'd seen that part of myself addressed."<br></ref> His surname is Polish.<ref>https://twitter.com/MrCraigBierko/status/337473163321569280</ref>。他曾就讀於波士頓大學<ref>[http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1989-10-23/features/8901240682_1_chicago-international-film-festival-movie-cycles Acting Cycle]</ref><ref>[http://www.nytimes.com/movies/person/6122/Craig-Bierko/biography Craig Bierko biography]</ref>。

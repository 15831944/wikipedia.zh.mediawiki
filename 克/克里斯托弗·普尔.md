'''克里斯托弗·普爾'''（Christopher Poole，{{bd|1988年}}）<ref name="Chris Poole part 1/3- ROFLCON 2012 – Solo Panel">[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O5adlMZFVEA Chris Poole part 1/3- ROFLCON 2012 – Solo Panel], Christopher Poole - "I'm 24 years old" [[YouTube|YouTube]] site, May 10, 2012</ref>，美國企業家，[[4chan|4chan]]與{{link-en|Canvas Networks|Canvas Networks}}的創建者。他在2003年10月1日以「moot」為[[化名|化名]]開設4chan。2016年，他開始為[[Google|Google]]工作。

== 影響和活動 ==
2008年，巴西[[環球電視網_(巴西)|環球電視網]]的萊奧波爾多·戈多伊（Leopoldo Godoy）將普爾創立的4chan形容為「西方[[網路文化|網路文化]]的[[原爆點|原爆點]]」<ref>{{cite news |url=http://g1.globo.com/Sites/Especiais/Noticias/0,,MUL930089-16307,00-CONHECA+AS+DEZ+MODINHAS+QUE+MAIS+BOMBARAM+NA+INTERNET+EM.html |last=Godoy |first=Leopoldo |title=Conheça as dez modinhas que mais bombaram na internet em 2008 |language=pt |publisher=G1 |date=December 21, 2008 |accessdate=2008-12-21}}</ref>。

在2009年4月《時代雜誌》主持的網上選舉中，普尔當選为2009年時代百大人物第一名<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.time.com/time/arts/article/0,8599,1894028,00.html |title=The World's Most Influential Person Is... |publisher=[[時代_(雜誌)|時代]] |date=2009-04-27 |accessdate=2009-09-02|language=en}}</ref>。不过，主办者很快發現選舉中有惡搞行为，即4chan用戶用自動投票軟件把普尔推到了第一名<ref name="PCMag-moot">{{cite web | url = http://www.pcmag.com/article2/0,2817,2345987,00.asp | title = 4Chan Followers Hack Time's 'Influential' Poll | last = Heater | first = Brian | publisher = {{link-en|PC Magazine|PC Magazine}} | date = 2009-04-27 | accessdate = 2009-04-27 |language=en}}</ref><ref name="TechCrunch-moot">{{cite news | url = http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2009/04/21/AR2009042101864.html | last = Schonfeld | first = Erick | title = 4Chan Takes Over The Time 100 | publisher = [[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]] | date = 2009-04-21 | accessdate = 2009-04-27 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://musicmachinery.com/2009/04/27/moot-wins-time-inc-loses/ |title=moot wins, Time Inc. loses « Music Machinery |publisher=Musicmachinery.com |date=2009-04-27 |accessdate=2009-09-02|language=en}}</ref>。除此之外，前21名的第一字母还可以组合成一句話：「[[大理石蛋糕|大理石蛋糕]]。還有，[[The_Game_(遊戲)|The Game]]」（mARBLECAKE. ALSO, THE GAME），这正是4chan上两句流行語的组合<ref>{{cite web|author=Reddit Top Links|url=http://www.buzzfeed.com/reddit/also-the-work-of-4chan-pic|title=Marble Cake Also the Game [PIC]|publisher=[[BuzzFeed|BuzzFeed]]|date=|accessdate=2009-09-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090715084148/http://www.buzzfeed.com/reddit/also-the-work-of-4chan-pic|archive-date=2009-07-15|language=en|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。2009年9月12日，普尔在[[奧地利|奧地利]][[維也納|維也納]]的Paraflows座談會發表為何以「模因工廠」而聞名的演讲，并在演讲中將此歸功於4chan的無名發表系統<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.paraflows.at/index.php?id=131 |title=Paraflows 09, Program for Saturday, Sep 12 2009 |publisher=Paraflows.at |date= |accessdate=2010-05-18|language=de}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Herwig, Jana|url=http://digiom.wordpress.com/2010/04/06/moot-on-4chan-and-why-it-works-as-a-meme-factory/|title=Partial transcript: Moot on 4chan and why it works as a meme factory|website=Digiom Blog|date=2010-04-06|accessdate=2010-04-07|language=en}}</ref>。

2010年2月10日，普尔在[[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[長灘_(加利福尼亞州)|長灘]]參加了[[TED大会|TED2010會議]]并发表演说<ref>{{cite web|url=http://conferences.ted.com/TED2010/program/ |title=TED2010 Program |publisher=[[TED大会|TED大会]] |date= |accessdate=2012-02-06|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Fisher, Ken|url=http://arstechnica.com/staff/palatine/2010/02/4chans-moot-takes-pro-anonymity-to-ted-2010.ars|title=4chan's moot takes pro-anonymity to TED 2010|website=[[Ars_Technica|Ars Technica]]|date=2010-02-11|accessdate=2010-02-12|language=en}}</ref>，他在演讲中提到了现今保有固定的网络身份与在[[Facebook|Facebook]]和[[Twitter|Twitter]]等站点上分享自己的个人信息的风潮，并阐述了[[匿名|匿名]]發言對维护互聯網隱私和言論自由的重要性<ref>{{cite news|url=http://scitech.blogs.cnn.com/2010/02/12/4chan-founder-anonymous-speech-is-endangered/ |title=4chan founder: Anonymous speech is 'endangered' « SciTechBlog |publisher=[[CNN|CNN]] |date=2010-02-12 |accessdate=2010-02-12|language=en}}</ref>。巴西報章《{{link-en|聖保羅州報|O Estado de S. Paulo}}》的弗雷德·萊亞爾（Fred Leal）称：「克里斯多福·普尔参与TED2010會議的事件表明非同尋常的情况的發生……4chan的规则跟所有的網絡公約相反：它处于[[Google|Google]]、社交網站和[[博客|博客]]的對立面。」<ref>{{cite news | author = Leal, Fred | title = Feio, sujo e surreal | work = O Estado de S. Paulo | date = 2010-04-19 | page = L1 | url = http://blogs.estadao.com.br/link/feio-sujo-e-surreal/ | accessdate = 2010-04-25 | archiveurl = http://www.webcitation.org/5pF2y1JjE | archivedate = 2010-04-25|language=pt}}</ref>同年，普爾據說籌募了62.5萬美元開設了一個名叫{{link-en|Canvas Networks|Canvas Networks}}的網站<ref name=Cha>{{Cite news |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2010/08/09/AR2010080906102.html?hpid=topnews |title=4chan users seize Internet's power for mass disruptions |work=Washington Post |last=Cha |first=Ariana Eunjung |date=August 10, 2010 |accessdate=August 10, 2010}}</ref><ref name=TechRev /> 。該網站於2011年1月31日開設<ref name=Jeffries>{{Cite news |url=http://www.observer.com/2011/tech/creator-4chan-comes-more-mature-canvas |title=From the Creator of 4chan Comes the More Mature Canvas |first=Adrianne |last=Jeffries |journal=The New York Observer |date=January 31, 2011 |accessdate=February 7, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110204192120/http://www.observer.com/2011/tech/creator-4chan-comes-more-mature-canvas |archivedate=February 4, 2011 }}</ref>。2010年4月，普尔在{{link-en|美国诉大卫·克內尔案|United States of America v. David Kernell}}審判中作為政府證人提供证词。<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/technology/news/7938394/Sarah-Palin-hacker-trial-provides-lolz-courtesy-of-4chan-founder.html | location=倫敦| work=[[每日电讯报|每日电讯报]] | title=Sarah Palin hacker trial provides 'lolz' courtesy of 4chan founder | first=Alastair | last=Jamieson | date=2010-08-11|language=en}}</ref>作為證人，普尔向[[檢察官|檢察官]]解釋了4chan上所使用的術語，包括「OP」（楼主，也即「Original Poster/原发文者」的缩写）和「[[潛水_(網路用語)|潜水员]]」（lurker），也向法院解釋他給[[联邦调查局|美國聯邦調查局]]的數據包括哪些內容，以及怎麼用這些資料確定網站用戶的身份<ref name=transcript>{{cite web |url=http://i.cdn.turner.com/dr/teg/tsg/release/sites/default/files/assets/poole-testimony.pdf |title=Transcript of Chris Poole before the Honorable Thomas W. Phillips on April 22, 2010 |publisher=美国诉大卫·克內尔案，[[美國地方法院|美國地方法院]][[田納西州|田納西州]]東區北科 |format=PDF |date=2010-04-22 |accessdate=2011-10-29 |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100820204109/http://i.cdn.turner.com/dr/teg/tsg/release/sites/default/files/assets/poole-testimony.pdf |archivedate=2010年8月20日 }}</ref>。在同年的一個採訪中，普爾談論了他對多重身份的重視，包括匿名，其與線上企業的合併和如Facebook等許多其他社交網站上存在的現實身份相反<ref name=TechRev>{{cite news |url=http://www.technologyreview.com/web/25997/ |work=Technology Review |title=Radical Opacity |first=Julian |last=Dibbell |date=2010-09-10}}</ref>。

2012年11月，普爾向一間名叫Moot. It的互聯網[[初創企業|創業公司]]發出一封[[停止並終止|停終信函]]<ref name=Betabeat>{{cite news |url=http://betabeat.com/2012/11/4chan-founder-moot-sends-cease-desist-letter-to-startup-moot-it/ |work=Betabeat |title=4Chan Founder Moot Sends Cease & Desist Letter to Startup Moot.It |first=Jessica |last=Roy |date=November 19, 2012}}</ref><ref name="Tripfags.com">{{cite news |url=http://tripfags.com/2012/11/moot-launches-lawsuit-against-startup.html |work=Tripfags.com |title=Moot Launches $10M Lawsuit Against Startup |first=Travis |last=Westphall |date=November 28, 2012 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131210015456/http://tripfags.com/2012/11/moot-launches-lawsuit-against-startup.html |archivedate=2013-12-10 }}</ref>。2014年1月，他宣佈其創建的Canvas Network正式停業，它的DrawQuest功能亦會停止運作<ref name=close>{{Cite web|title=A very important note from Team Canvas |url=http://blog.canv.as/post/74628762928/a-very-important-note-from-team-canvas |publisher=Canvas Blog |accessdate=January 28, 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140128155118/http://blog.canv.as/post/74628762928/a-very-important-note-from-team-canvas |archivedate=January 28, 2014 }}</ref><ref name=BI>{{Cite news |title=A Classy Way to Admit Your Startup is Dead |url=http://www.businessinsider.com/4chan-founder-chris-poole-drawquest-done-2014-1 |publisher=Business Insider |first=Jillian |last=D'Onfro |date=January 21, 2014 |accessdate=January 28, 2014}}</ref><ref name=verge>{{Cite news |title=4chan founder Chris Poole is shutting down Canvas and DrawQuest for iOS |url=https://www.theverge.com/2014/1/21/5331626/4chan-founder-chris-poole-shutting-down-canvas-drawquest |publisher=The Verge |first=Chris |last=Welch |date=January 21, 2014 |accessdate=January 28, 2014}}</ref>。

2015年1月21日，普爾公佈他將辭去其4chan管理員的職務<ref>{{Cite web |title=THE NEXT CHAPTER by moot – 1/21/15 @ 11:00AM EST |url=https://www.4chan.org/news?all#118 |publisher=4chan Blog |accessdate=January 21, 2015}}</ref>。兩天後，他最後一次以「moot」的名義在/qa/（Q&A版）上主持問答環節和在YouTube上[[影音串流|直播串流]]。這標誌他正式從當了11年半的4chan管理員和持有者的位置「退休」<ref>{{Cite web |title=moot's final 4chan Q&A by 4chan – 1/23/15 @ 2:00PM EST |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XYUKJBZuUig |publisher=4chan |accessdate=January 25, 2015}}</ref>。之後他開始把4chan的控制權轉移給三位匿名的4chan版主，同時為網站尋找買家<ref>{{cite news |last1=Kushner |first1=David |title=4chan's Overlord Christopher Poole Reveals Why He Walked Away |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/culture/features/4chans-overlord-christopher-poole-reveals-why-he-walked-away-20150313 |accessdate=June 20, 2015 |work=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]] | date=March 13, 2015}}</ref>。同年9月21日，普爾宣佈創立[[2channel|2channel]]的日本企業家[[西村博之|西村博之]]將接手擔任4chan的所有者<ref>{{cite web|last1=moot|title=Full Circle|url=http://www.4chan.org/news?all#120|website=4chan|accessdate=21 September 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://bits.blogs.nytimes.com/2015/09/21/4chan-sells-to-japanese-web-culture-pioneer-2channel/?_r=0|title=4chan Message Board Sold to Founder of 2Channel, a Japanese Web Culture Pioneer|last=Issac|first=Mike|publisher=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=September 21, 2015|accessdate=September 21, 2015}}</ref>。2016年3月7日，普爾宣佈他已經被Google聘用，但未披露其職位<ref>{{cite web|last1=Poole|first1=Christopher|title=My next chapter|url=http://chrishateswriting.com/post/140641275808/my-next-chapter|website=Chris Hates Writing|accessdate=7 March 2016}}</ref>。

== 身份 ==
[[File:Internet_Superheroes_cropped.png|thumb]]（中）和{{link-en|萊恩·諾斯|Ryan North}}在2008年的{{link-en|ROFLCon|ROFLCon}}上同台接受-{訪問}-]]
剛開始普尔一直把自己的真实世界身份保密，只使用網名作标识，直到在2008年7月9日《[[華爾街日報|華爾街日報]]》才透露真名<ref name="wsj">{{cite news |url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB121564928060441097 |title=Modest Web Site Is Behind a Bevy of Memes |publisher=Wall Street Journal |first=Jamin |last=Brophy-Warren |date=2008-07-09 |accessdate=2008-07-24}}</ref>。同日，《[[時代雜誌|時代雜誌]]》的{{link-en|萊夫·葛羅斯曼|Lev Grossman}}把普尔描述为在網上內容協作的演變中的「一個重要人物」<ref name="time">{{Cite news| last = Grossman| first = Lev| author-link = | title = The Master of Memes| newspaper = [[時代_(雜誌)|時代]]| place = United States | volume = 172| issue = 3| pages = 50–51| date = 2008-07-09| url = http://www.time.com/time/business/article/0,8599,1821435,00.html| accessdate = 2008-07-24|language=en}}</ref>，但在當時《[[華盛頓郵報|華盛頓郵報]]》一篇文章则称“一切（只是）一场大骗局，一个意图引人注目的报道。这只是你们想从4chan的创建者得到的（说法）而已。”，暗示「克里斯托弗·普尔」的身份也有可能是伪造的。<ref name="WP-17Feb09">{{cite news|last=Hesse|first=Monica|title=A Virtual Unknown; Meet 'Moot,' the Secretive Internet Celeb Who Still Lives With Mom|work=[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]]|pages=23–24|date=2009-02-17|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2009/02/16/AR2009021601565.html|accessdate=2009-04-16|language=en}}</ref>在2009年3月，普尔入圍[[時代百大人物|時代百大人物]]<ref name="Time1000nom">{{cite news|url=http://www.time.com/time/specials/packages/article/0,28804,1883644_1883653_1885481,00.html |title=moot - The 2009 TIME 100 Finalists |publisher=[[時代_(雜誌)|時代]] |date= 2009-03-19|accessdate=2009-09-02|language=en}}</ref>。

在接受《華爾街日報》和《時代雜誌》採訪之前，克里斯托弗·普尔一直刻意把自己的真实身份跟4chan相隔离，在《時代雜誌》採訪中，他也表示「我本人的私生活与我在網上的活动泾渭分明……就如中間有堵[[防火牆|防火牆]]般。」<ref name="time" />克里斯托弗·普尔當時也在[[耶魯大學|耶魯大學]]和[[麻省理工學院|麻省理工學院]]的會議上發過言<ref name="time" />，其中2008年一篇《[[觀察家報|觀察家報]]》的文章里描述：「他是你們根本沒有聽說過的最有影響的網絡實業家」<ref name="ObserverGoogle">{{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/technology/2008/jul/20/internet.google|title=The 20-year-old at heart of web's most anarchic and influential site|publisher=[[觀察家報|觀察家報]]|author=David Smith|date=2008-07-20|accessdate=2008-07-20 | location=倫敦|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Cohen|first=Stefanie|title=Grosses and ’Nets|work=[[紐約郵報|紐約郵報]]|page=25|date=2009-02-22|url=http://www.nypost.com/seven/02212009/postopinion/opedcolumnists/the_dark_heart_of_the_internet_156297.htm|accessdate=2009-04-16|language=en}}</ref>。

在2009年2月，《華盛頓郵報》报道称克里斯托弗·普尔在[[弗吉尼亞聯邦大學|弗吉尼亞聯邦大學]]里上了几学期后就退學了，并在退学后一直与母親同住，欠下了2萬美元的運行費用債務以佔有4chan。<ref name="WP-17Feb09"/>。

'''克里斯滕·希尼玛'''（{{lang-en|Kyrsten Sinema}}，{{bd|1976年|7月12日<ref>{{cite news|title=Phoenix Arizona Election Questionnaire for Congress, KYRSTEN SINEMA|url=http://www.azcentral.com/news/politics/2012questionnaires/results.php?id=sinemak|accessdate=29 April 2015|publisher=The Arizona Republic}}</ref>}}），美国政治人物，[[民主党_(美国)|民主党]]籍，现为代表[[亚利桑那州|亚利桑那州]]的联邦[[美国參议院|參议员]]。

== 生平 ==
希尼玛出生于[[亚利桑那州|亚利桑那州]][[图森_(亚利桑那州)|图森]]。她曾先后获得过[[杨百翰大学|杨百翰大学]]学士学位、[[亚利桑那州立大学|亚利桑那州立大学]]社会工作硕士学位，2004年获亚利桑那州立大学法学院[[法律博士|法律博士]]学位。后来，她又于2012年在亚利桑那州立大学获司法研究博士学位。<ref name="NationalJournal">{{cite web|last=Skelton |first=Alissa |title=Arizona, 9th House District: Kyrsten Sinema |date=November 1, 2012 |work=[[National_Journal|National Journal]] |url=http://www.nationaljournal.com/congress-legacy/arizona-9th-house-district-20121101 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121122040754/http://nationaljournal.com/congress-legacy/arizona-9th-house-district-20121101 |archive-date=November 22, 2012 |dead-url=yes |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Project Vote Smart: Rep. Kyrsten Sinema|url=http://votesmart.org/bio.php?can_id=28338|accessdate=June 1, 2008}}</ref>

希尼玛于2002年作为独立人士首次参选[[亚利桑那州众议院|亚利桑那州众议员]]但没有成功。<ref name="Winger">{{cite news|url=http://www.ballot-access.org/2012/11/13/kyrsten-sinema-newly-elected-arizona-congresswoman-was-once-a-green-party-nominee-for-arizona-legislature/|title=Kyrsten Sinema, Newly-Elected Arizona Congresswoman, Was Once a Green Party Nominee for Arizona Legislature|last=Winger|first=Richard|date=November 13, 2012|publisher=[[Ballot_Access_News|Ballot Access News]]|accessdate=November 21, 2012}}</ref>2004年，她作为民主党籍候选人成功当选亚利桑那州众议员。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ourcampaigns.com/RaceDetail.html?RaceID=735328 |title=AZ State House 15 - D Primary Race - Sep 07, 2004 |publisher=Our Campaigns |date= |accessdate=September 1, 2012}}</ref>2010年，又当选[[亚利桑那州参议院|亚利桑那州参议员]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ourcampaigns.com/RaceDetail.html?RaceID=512927 |title=AZ State Senate 15 Race - Nov 02, 2010 |publisher=Our Campaigns |date= |accessdate=September 1, 2012}}</ref>

2012年，希尼玛成功当选代表[[亚利桑那州第九国会选区|亚利桑那州第九国会选区]]的联邦众议员<ref>{{cite news|title=General Election Results|url=http://results.enr.clarityelections.com/AZ/42050/113200/Web01/en/summary.html|accessdate=November 24, 2012|newspaper=Arizona Secretary of State's Office}}</ref>，并连任至今。她在国会中属于[[中间派|中间派]]，加入了由保守派民主党人组成的[[蓝狗联盟|蓝狗联盟]]。

希尼玛还是首位公开[[双性恋|双性恋]]的国会议员。<ref name=success>{{cite news |url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/lifestyle/style/kyrsten-sinema-a-success-story-like-nobody-elses/2013/01/02/d31fadaa-5382-11e2-a613-ec8d394535c6_story.html|title=Kyrsten Sinema: A success story like nobody else's|newspaper=The Washington Post|location=Phoenix, Arizona|first=Manuel|last=Roig-Franzia|date=January 2, 2013|accessdate=January 8, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.npr.org/blogs/itsallpolitics/2013/01/08/168362011/sinema-first-openly-bisexual-member-of-congress-represents-changing-arizona|title=Sinema, First Openly Bisexual Member Of Congress, Represents 'Changing Arizona'|first=Peter|last=O'Dowd|date=January 1, 2013|accessdate=January 8, 2013 |publisher=NPR}}</ref>

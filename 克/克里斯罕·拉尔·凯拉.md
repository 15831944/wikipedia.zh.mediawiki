'''克里斯罕·拉尔·凯拉'''（{{lang-en|'''Krishan Lal Kaila'''}}，1932－2003）是一位[[印度|印度]][[地震学|地震学家]]<ref name="KailaKaila2012">{{cite book|author1=M. M. Kaila|author2=Rakhi Kaila|title=Molecular Imaging of the Brain: Using Multi-Quantum Coherence and Diagnostics of Brain Disorders|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=06cuTEHF6jcC&pg=PR25|date=2012-09-20|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=978-3-642-30301-2|pages=25–|language=en|access-date=2018-11-18}}</ref>。其探究领域主要为深震探测，并因此曾获印度国家最高科学技术奖（即{{link-en|SSB科学技术奖|Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize for Science and Technology}}）。

== 履历 ==
克里斯罕·拉尔·凯拉于1932年9月7日出生于[[英属印度|英属印度]][[拉合尔|拉合尔]]（今属[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]）<ref name="KailaKaila2010">{{cite book|author1=Madan M Kaila|author2=Rakhi Kaila|title=Quantum Magnetic Resonance Imaging Diagnostics of Human Brain Disorders|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=oqmFI88W8rQC&pg=PR9|date=2010-06-21|publisher=Elsevier|isbn=978-0-12-384712-6|pages=9–|language=en|access-date=2018-11-18}}</ref>。他以深震探测（DSS）为研究对象而闻名<ref name="Brief Profile of the Awardee">{{Cite web |url=http://ssbprize.gov.in/content/Detail.aspx?AID=492 |title=Brief Profile of the Awardee |date=2016 |publisher=Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize|language=en|access-date=2018-11-18}}</ref>。他是印度深震探测的先驱之一，其研究覆盖了[[丘德达帕|丘德达帕]]、{{link-en|达尔瓦克地块|Dharwar Craton}}和[[德干暗色岩|德干暗色岩]]的构造区域以及[[古吉拉特|古吉拉特]]和[[喜马拉雅|喜马拉雅]]的[[沉积盆地|沉积盆地]]<ref name="Handbook of Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize Winners">{{cite web | url=http://www.csirhrdg.res.in/ssb.pdf | title=Handbook of Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize Winners | publisher=Council of Scientific and Industrial Research | year=1999 |access-date=2018-11-18 |language=en}}</ref>。2003年，于[[印度|印度]][[新德里|新德里]]逝世，享年71岁<ref name="KailaKaila2010" />。

== 荣誉 ==
1976年，克里斯罕·拉尔·凯拉获{{link-en|印度地球物理联合会|Indian Geophysical Union}}颁发的克里斯南奖章<ref name="Krishnan Medal">{{Cite web |url=http://www.igu.in/awards.htm |title=Krishnan Medal |date=2016 |publisher={{link-en|印度地球物理联合会|Indian Geophysical Union}} |language=en |access-date=2018-11-18 }}</ref>。次年，获{{link-en|科学与工业研究委员会|Council of Scientific and Industrial Research}}颁发的{{link-en|SSB科学技术奖|Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize for Science and Technology}}<ref name="View Bhatnagar Awardees">{{cite web | url=http://ssbprize.gov.in/Content/AwardeeList.aspx | title=View Bhatnagar Awardees | publisher=Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize | date=2016}}</ref>。1994年，获印度地球物理联合会颁发的十年贡献奖（{{lang-en|Decennial Award}}）<ref name="Decennial Award">{{Cite web |url=http://www.igu.in/awards.htm#DECENNIAL%20AWARD%20(Instituted%20in%201975) |title=Decennial Award |date=2016 |publisher={{link-en|印度地球物理联合会|Indian Geophysical Union}} |language=en |access-date=2018-11-18}}</ref>。

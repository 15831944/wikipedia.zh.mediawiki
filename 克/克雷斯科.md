'''克雷斯科'''（Cresco）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[霍华德县_(爱阿华州)|霍华德县]]的城市和县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=2011-06-07 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531210815/http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=May 31, 2011 }}</ref><ref name="Profile for Cresco, Iowa, IA">{{cite web|url= http://www.epodunk.com/cgi-bin/genInfo.php?locIndex=7258|title= Profile for Cresco, Iowa, IA|publisher= ePodunk |accessdate= September 25, 2012}}</ref>2010年美国人口普查时人口为3868人，比2000年人口普查的3905人少。<ref name="IADC">{{cite web|url=http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |title=Data from the 2010 Census |publisher=State Data Center of Iowa |accessdate=March 22, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718201045/http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |archivedate=July 18, 2011 }}</ref>

==历史==
在1866年，当铁路扩张到该地区时，克雷斯科开始规划。<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=n4QUAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA416#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=History of Chickasaw and Howard Counties, Iowa, Volume 1 | publisher=S. J. Clarke Publishing Company | author=Fairbairn, Robert Herd | year=1919 | pages=416}}</ref> 克雷斯科是一个拉丁语短语，意思是“我成长”。<ref name="Profile for Cresco, Iowa, IA"/> 克雷斯科于1868年6月6日建市。<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.city-data.com/city/Cresco-Iowa.html|title= Cresco, Iowa|publisher= City-Data.com |accessdate= September 25, 2012}}</ref>

==地理==
克雷斯科的经度和纬度坐标为43.374491，-92.115121。<ref name="GR1">{{cite web|url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/gazette.html|publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2011-04-23|date=2011-02-12|title=US Gazetteer files: 2010, 2000, and 1990}}</ref>

据[[美国人口调查局|美国人口调查局]]统计，该市总面积3.35平方英里（8.68平方公里），全部是陆地。<ref name="Gazetteer files">{{cite web |title=US Gazetteer files 2010 |url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] |accessdate=2012-05-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/64vfLAeJ2?url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt |archivedate=2012年1月24日 }}</ref>

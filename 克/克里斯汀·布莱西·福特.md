== 早年 ==
福特在[[华盛顿特区|华盛顿特区]]郊区长大<ref name=Testimony27Sep2018/>。父母是注册共和党人，2018年9月{{le|布雷特·卡瓦诺最高法院提名争议|Brett Kavanaugh Supreme Court nomination|最高法院提名听证会}}时，两人分别是80岁和83岁。有两个兄弟<ref>{{cite news |work=The Washington Post |date=2018-09-27 |first=Jessica |last=Contrera |first2=Ian |last2=Shapira |title=Christine Blasey Ford's family has been nearly silent amid outpouring of support |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/local/christine-blasey-fords-own-family-has-been-nearly-silent-amid-outpouring-of-support/2018/09/26/49a3f4a6-c0d6-11e8-be77-516336a26305_story.html |access-date=2018-10-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181010192830/https://www.washingtonpost.com/local/christine-blasey-fords-own-family-has-been-nearly-silent-amid-outpouring-of-support/2018/09/26/49a3f4a6-c0d6-11e8-be77-516336a26305_story.html |archive-date=2018-10-10 |dead-url=no |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |work=The Washington Post |date=2018-09-27 |first=Jessica |last=Contrera |first2=Ian |last2=Shapira |first3=Emma |last3=Brown |first4=Steve |last4=Hendrix |title=Kavanaugh accuser Christine Blasey Ford moved 3,000 miles to reinvent her life. 1978年到1984年<ref name=Testimony27Sep2018/>，她就读于马里兰州贝塞斯达私立全女[[预科学校|预科学校]]{{le|霍尔顿-阿姆斯学校|Holton-Arms School}}<ref name=NYT26Sep2018/>。在地方跳水队时，她曾和跳水运动员[[格雷格·洛加尼斯|格雷格·洛加尼斯]]前往[[白宫|白宫]]讨论[[联合抵制1980年夏季奥运会|联合抵制1980年夏季奥运会]]的事宜<ref name=Sulek>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mercurynews.com/2018/09/17/metoo-spurred-christine-blasey-ford-to-open-up-about-alleged-attack-year-before-kavanaugh-nomination-friends-say/|work=The Mercury News|title=Christine Blasey Ford feared an avalanche of attacks if she went public about Kavanaugh, friends say|date=2018-09-17|access-date=2018-09-18|first=Julia Prodis|last=Sulek|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180918054758/https://www.mercurynews.com/2018/09/17/metoo-spurred-christine-blasey-ford-to-open-up-about-alleged-attack-year-before-kavanaugh-nomination-friends-say/|archive-date=2018-09-18|dead-url=no|language=en}}</ref>。

她于1988年在[[北卡罗来纳大学教堂山分校|北卡罗来纳大学教堂山分校]]<ref name=Testimony27Sep2018/>凭本科学位毕业<ref name=NYT26Sep2018/>，1991年获[[佩珀代因大学|佩珀代因大学]][[临床心理学|临床心理学]]硕士学位<ref name=NYT26Sep2018/>，1996年获[[南加州大学|南加州大学]][[教育心理学|教育心理学]]博士学位<ref name=NYT26Sep2018/>。1995年发表题为《衡量幼儿应对人际冲突的措施》（{{lang|en|''Measuring Young Children's Coping Responses to Interpersonal Conflict''}}）的论文<ref name="Palo Alto Catalog">{{Cite web|url=https://www.paloaltou.edu/sites/default/files/pau_2015_2016_catalog_10_27_15_final.pdf|title=Palo Alto University 2015-16 Catalog|publisher=Palo Alto University|page=190|year=2016|access-date=2018-09-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180917105155/https://www.paloaltou.edu/sites/default/files/pau_2015_2016_catalog_10_27_15_final.pdf|archive-date=2018-09-17|dead-url=no|language=en}}</ref>。2009年，获{{le|斯坦福大学医学院|Stanford University School of Medicine}}<ref name=NYT26Sep2018/>[[流行病学|流行病学]]硕士学位，主攻[[生物统计学|生物统计学]]<ref name=Alene>{{Cite news|url=http://www.latimes.com/local/lanow/la-me-ln-christine-blasey-ford-20180918-story.html|access-date=2018-09-18|date=2018-09-18|work=Los Angeles Times|first=Alene|last=Tchekmedyian|title=Who is Christine Blasey Ford, the woman who accused Supreme Court nominee Brett Kavanaugh of sexual assault?|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180918140022/http://www.latimes.com/local/lanow/la-me-ln-christine-blasey-ford-20180918-story.html|archive-date=2018-09-18|dead-url=no|language=en}}</ref>。

== 生涯 ==
福特自1988年起在[[斯坦福大学|斯坦福大学]]教书<ref name=Schallhorn/>。到2018年9月，她在[[帕罗奥图大学|帕罗奥图大学]]教授研究设计和教育临床心理学<ref name=Weise/>。此外，她还以帕洛奥图大学[[联盟|联盟]]组织成员的身份，参加斯坦福大学医学院的教育计划<ref name=Weise/><ref name=Schallhorn/><ref name=Anderson>{{Cite news|quote=She has been widely published in her field.|url=https://www.npr.org/2018/09/17/648803684/who-is-christine-blasey-ford-the-woman-accusing-brett-kavanaugh-of-sexual-assaul|work=National Public Radio|title=Who Is Christine Blasey Ford, The Woman Accusing Brett Kavanaugh Of Sexual Assault?|date=2018-09-17|access-date=2018-09-18|first=Meg|last=Anderson|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180917225134/https://www.npr.org/2018/09/17/648803684/who-is-christine-blasey-ford-the-woman-accusing-brett-kavanaugh-of-sexual-assaul|archive-date=2018-09-17|dead-url=no|language=en}}</ref>。透过名叫太平洋心理学研究生院的联盟组织，福特教授[[心理统计学|心理统计学]]，研究方法和统计<ref name=Sulek/><ref name=Anderson/>。她还为多家药企提供咨询服务<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2018/09/18/political-cornflakes-who/|access-date=2018-09-18|date=2018-09-18|title=Who is Christine Blasey Ford? We're just beginning to find out|work=The Salt Lake Tribune|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180918231018/https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2018/09/18/political-cornflakes-who/|archive-date=2018-09-18|dead-url=no|language=en}}</ref>。福特还是{{le|Corcept製藥夠公司|Corcept Therapeutics}}生物统计学部门的主任，和[[美国食品药品监督管理局|美国食品药品监督管理局]]统计学家有合作关系<ref name="Palo Alto Catalog"/>。福特发表了众多有关她研究领域的著作<ref name=WaPo16Sep2018/><ref name=Anderson/><ref name=whattowatchfor>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cbsnews.com/news/what-to-watch-for-during-brett-kavanaugh-christine-blasey-ford-hearing/|first=Ed|last=O'Keefe|title=What to watch for during Brett Kavanaugh, Christine Blasey Ford hearing|date=2018-09-27|work=CBS News|access-date=2018-09-28|quote=She is a widely-published professor at Palo Alto University, teaching clinical psychology to graduate students.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180928044331/https://www.cbsnews.com/news/what-to-watch-for-during-brett-kavanaugh-christine-blasey-ford-hearing/|archive-date=2018-09-28|dead-url=no|language=en}}</ref>。

曾和福特一起写过书和多篇文章的斯坦福生物统计学荣誉退休教授{{le|海伦娜·楚姆拉·克雷默|Helena Chmura Kraemer}}概括称福特“专们为研究项目设置统计模型，确保计划得出精确的结论”<ref name=Mercury>{{Cite news|first=Casey|last=Tolan|url=https://www.mercurynews.com/2018/09/16/kavanaugh-accuser-palo-alto-professor-christine-blasey-ford-lauded-as-truth-teller/|title=Kavanaugh accuser lauded as 'truth teller'; senator says nomination may see delay|date=2018-09-16|work=The Mercury News|access-date=2018-09-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180917114530/https://www.mercurynews.com/2018/09/16/kavanaugh-accuser-palo-alto-professor-christine-blasey-ford-lauded-as-truth-teller/|archive-date=2018-09-17|dead-url=no|language=en}}</ref>。福特写过几本心理学议题书籍，其中一本关于沮丧<ref name=Dangelo/>。她在学术期刊发表的其他研究课题文章还包括[[儿童虐待|儿童虐待]]和[[九一一恐怖袭击|九一一恐怖袭击]]<ref name=Schallhorn>{{Cite news|url=http://www.foxnews.com/politics/2018/09/17/who-is-christine-blasey-ford-professor-who-accused-brett-kavanaugh-sexual-misconduct.html|work=Fox News|title=Who is Christine Blasey Ford, the professor who accused Brett Kavanaugh of sexual misconduct?|date=2018-09-17|access-date=2018-09-18|first=Kaitlyn|last=Schallhorn|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180917210843/http://www.foxnews.com/politics/2018/09/17/who-is-christine-blasey-ford-professor-who-accused-brett-kavanaugh-sexual-misconduct.html|archive-date=2018-09-17|dead-url=no|language=en}}</ref><ref name=Dangelo/>。2015年，她与他人创作题为《多少个对象？分析统计对研究的功效》（{{lang|en|''How Many Subjects?

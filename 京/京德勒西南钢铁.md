'''京德勒西南钢铁有限公司'''（{{BSE|500228}}, {{NSE|JSWSTEEL}}）是印度最大的钢铁公司之一，年产能超过1800万吨。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jsw.in/groups/about-groups |title=JSW Steel has become the second largest steel producer in the country after state-owned Steel Authority of India (SAIL)  |publisher=economictimes.com |date= |accessdate=2013-06-03}}</ref> 是孟买的JSW Group的子公司，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.jsw.in/contact/contact.shtml |title=Get in touch with JSW Group Companies |publisher=Jsw.in |date= |accessdate=2010-12-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101219172302/http://jsw.in/contact/contact.shtml |archivedate=2010-12-19 }}</ref> 集团经营范围包括钢铁、能源、基础设施、水泥、风投、运动等。<ref name="autogenerated1">{{cite web|url=http://www.jsw.in/groups/about-groups |title=JSW Group: One of the fastest growing business house |publisher=Jsw.in |date= |accessdate=2010-12-23}}</ref> 

== 历史 ==
1982年Jindal Group并购了Piramal Steel Limited，这是一家位于[[马哈拉施特拉邦|马哈拉施特拉邦]][[塔拉普尔|塔拉普尔]]的小型钢铁厂，并更名为Jindal Iron and Steel Company（JISCO）。<ref name="Jsw.in">{{cite web |url=http://www.jsw.in/companies/company_JSWSteel.shtml |title=JSW Steel Ltd. is one among the largest Indian Steel Companies in India today |publisher=Jsw.in |date= |accessdate=2010-12-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101219170719/http://jsw.in/companies/company_JSWSteel.shtml |archivedate=2010-12-19 }}</ref>

1994年建立京德勒维贾亚纳加尔钢铁有限公司（Jindal Vijayanagar Steel Ltd.，縮寫 JVSL），位于[[卡纳塔克邦|卡纳塔克邦]]{{tsl|en|Toranagallu|托拉納加魯}}。

2005年, JISCO与JVSL合并为JSW Steel Ltd.。

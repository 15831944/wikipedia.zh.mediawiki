== 早期生活 ==
奈特出生於[[伊利诺伊州|伊利諾伊州]]的[[罗克福德|罗克福德]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.rrstar.com/news/20170505/rockford-native-appointed-state-department-spokeswoman|title=Rockford native appointed State Department spokeswoman|last=Braun|first=Georgette|date=June 6, 2017|work=[[Rockford_Register_Star|Rockford Register Star]]}}</ref>。其父彼得·奈特曾經是保險業界的行政人員。奈特有三名兄弟：賈斯汀、喬納森和約瑟夫<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.chicagotribune.com/news/ct-xpm-2007-08-24-0708231132-story.html|title=Death Notice: Peter W. Nauert|date=August 24, 2007|work=[[The_Chicago_Tribune|The Chicago Tribune]]}}</ref>。

奈特在[[罗克福德|罗克福德]]上日校，再到[[麻薩諸塞州|麻省]][[栗树山_(马萨诸塞州)|栗树山]]的{{le|松莊園學院|Pine Manor College}}，之後到[[亞利桑那州立大學|亞利桑那州立大學]]上學。After landing an internship hosting a country music video program in Washington, DC, she stayed there to finish school，最終在{{le|弗農山神學院暨學院|Mount Vernon Seminary and College}}畢業並獲得[[文学士|文學士]]的[[通信|通信]]學位。之後在[[哥伦比亚大学|哥倫比亞大學]]獲得[[新闻学|新聞學]][[文學碩士|文學碩士]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/archive/lifestyle/2000/05/25/the-new-face-of-the-talking-head/148e12dc-1db7-492f-bba3-acee335bce1e/|title=The New Face Of the Talking Head|last=Farhi|first=Paul|date=May 25, 2000|work=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]]}}</ref>。

== 職業生涯 ==

=== 記者 ===
In 1996, Nauert was a reporter for the syndicated weekly business program ''First Business''. She worked for [[福斯新聞頻道|Fox News]] from 1998 to 2005, first as a contributor for three years and then as a correspondent for four years. During her time as a correspondent, she regularly contributed to ''The Big Story''.<ref name="facts">{{Cite news|url=https://heavy.com/news/2017/03/heather-nauert-husband-fox-friends-hosts-state-department-donald-trump-family-net-worth-instagram-age/|title=Heather Nauert: 5 Fast Facts You Need to Know|date=March 6, 2017|work=[[Heavy.com|Heavy.com]]}}</ref>

From 2005 to 2007, Nauert held positions at several news organizations, including [[ABC新聞|ABC News]] as a general assignment correspondent, where she contributed to ''[[ABC世界新聞|ABC World News Tonight]]'', ''[[早安美國|Good Morning America]]'', and ''[[夜線|Nightline]]''. While at ABC, she was nominated for an [[艾美奖|Emmy Award]] for her work on the special series ''13 Around the World''.

In 2007, Nauert returned to Fox News as co-host with John Gibson of the weekday edition of ''The Big Story''.

Nauert also co-anchored the newscasts ''Good Day Early Call'' and ''Good Day New York Wake Up'' with co-anchor Steve Lacy weekday mornings for [[福斯廣播公司|Fox Broadcasting Company]] owned-and-operated station WNYW in [[纽约|New York City]]. In October 2012, Nauert left ''Good Day Wake Up'' and became a [[新聞主播|news presenter]] for ''Fox & Friends''.<ref name="swap">{{Cite news|url=https://www.adweek.com/digital/heather-nauert-and-juliet-huddy-swap-roles-with-fox/|title=Heather Nauert and Juliet Huddy Swap Roles at Fox|last=Barmash|first=Jerry|date=October 17, 2012|work=[[AdWeek|AdWeek]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bustle.com/p/trumps-admin-appoints-heather-nauert-a-former-fox-friends-host-as-acting-under-secretary-8494591|title=Trump's Admin Appoints Heather Nauert, A Former 'Fox & Friends' Host, As Acting Under Secretary|last=KASANA|first=MEHREEN|date=March 13, 2018|work=[[Bustle_(magazine)|Bustle]]}}</ref>

=== 顧問 ===
Nauert has also worked as a [[政府|government]] affairs consultant on issues including [[醫療保險|health insurance]], [[美国社会安全保险|Social Security]] and taxes。奈特亦是[[美国外交关系协会|美国外交关系协会]]會員。

=== 美國國務院 ===
[[File:Heather_Nauert_Regional_Media_Day.png|缩略图]]。]]
[[File:Secretary_Pompeo_Participates_in_a_Bilateral_Meeting_With_Foreign_Minister_Wang_Yi_(28921695728).jpg|缩略图]]與中國國務委員[[王毅|王毅]]舉行會面。攝於2018年6月14日，中國北京。]]
On April 24, 2017, the [[美国国务院|United States Department of State]] announced that Nauert would be the new State Department spokesperson, her first role in government.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/former-fox-anchor-heather-nauert-is-new-state-department-spokeswoman-1493053087|title=Former Fox Anchor Heather Nauert Is New State Department Spokeswoman|last=Schwartz|first=Felicia|date=April 24, 2017|work=[[The_Wall_Street_Journal|The Wall Street Journal]]}}</ref> She held her first [[記者會|press briefing]] in that role five weeks later, on June 6, 2017.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.adweek.com/tvnewser/heather-nauert-gives-first-state-department-press-briefing/331540|title=Heather Nauert Gives First Briefing as State Department Spokesperson|last=Katz|first=A.J.|date=June 6, 2017|work=[[AdWeek|AdWeek]]}}</ref> Following the dismissal of Steve Goldstein on March 13, 2018, Nauert was named acting Under Secretary of State for Public Diplomacy and Public Affairs.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.newsweek.com/who-heather-nauert-former-fox-friends-host-trump-just-appointed-top-state-844923|title=The Trump administration has appointed former Fox News host Heather Nauert to a top State Department job|author=Da Silva, Chantal|last=|date=March 14, 2018|work=[[Newsweek|Newsweek]]|accessdate=}}
[[Category:含有冗余参数的引用的页面|Category:含有冗余参数的引用的页面]]</ref>

In April 2018, Nauert voiced support for [[沙特领导的干预也门行动|Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen]]. She also condemned "Iran’s malign influence" in [[也门|Yemen]].<ref>"[https://www.financialexpress.com/defence/trump-administration-condemns-houthi-missile-attack-on-riyadh-says-supports-saudi-arabias-right-to-defend-borders/1130159/ Trump Administration condemns Houthi missile attack on Riyadh, says supports Saudi Arabia’s right to defend borders]". ''The Financial Express.'' April 12, 2018.</ref> In May 2018, Nauert said in response to the [[2018年加沙邊境抗議事件|Gaza border protests]]: "We oppose actions against Israel at the International Criminal Court (...) because it does not help the cause for peace."<ref>"[https://www.jpost.com/Arab-Israeli-Conflict/US-opposes-PA-going-to-ICC-as-counterproductive-counter-to-peace-558126 U.S. opposes PA going to ICC as counterproductive, counter to peace]". ''The Jerusalem Post.'' May 23, 2018.</ref>

In July 2018, Nauert stirred up controversy when citing the [[诺曼底战役|Invasion of Normandy]] as evidence of a strong relationship between the United States and [[德国|Germany]] on the anniversary of the D Day invasion.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.businessinsider.com/heather-nauert-state-department-fox-news-2018-6|title=How Heather Nauert's unlikely rise at the State Department has made her a top contender for White House press secretary|date=August 6, 2018|work=[[Business_Insider|Business Insider]]}}</ref>

In August 2018, [[加拿大|Canada]] called for the immediate release of Saudi [[人权|human rights]] activist Raif Badawi and his sister, Samar Badawi. In response to Canada's criticism, Saudi Arabia expelled the Ambassador of Canada and froze trade with Canada, leading to a decline in Canada–Saudi Arabia relations.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thestar.com/news/canada/2018/08/05/saudi-arabia-to-expel-canadian-ambassador-and-freeze-trade-in-human-rights-dispute.html|title=Saudi Arabia expels Canadian ambassador, freezes trade in human rights dispute|author=Jon Gambrell|last=|date=5 August 2018|work=|publisher=[[Toronto_Star|Toronto Star]]|accessdate=|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]}}
[[Category:含有冗余参数的引用的页面|Category:含有冗余参数的引用的页面]]</ref> Nauert said: "It is up for the Government of Saudi Arabia and the Canadians to work this out. Both sides need to diplomatically resolve this together. We cannot do it for them."<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2018/aug/11/canada-saudi-arabia-support-us|title=‘We don’t have a single friend’: Canada’s Saudi spat reveals country is alone|date=August 11, 2018|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]}}</ref>

On November 1, 2018, it was reported that President Trump offered Nauert the position of [[美国常驻联合国代表列表|United States Ambassador to the United Nations]] to replace the outgoing officeholder, [[妮基·黑利|Nikki Haley]]. Sources reported that Trump previously had former presidential advisor Dina Powell on the top of his list of people under consideration for the position before she withdrew her name.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://thehill.com/homenews/administration/414348-heather-nauert-offered-un-ambassador-job-reports|title=Heather Nauert offered UN ambassador job: reports|last=Samuels|first=Brett|date=November 1, 2018|work=[[The_Hill_(newspaper)|The Hill]]}}</ref>

== 個人生活 ==
奈特的丈夫為[[摩根士丹利|摩根士丹利]]私人信貸和股權執行董事史葛·諾比，諾比曾經在國家獸醫協會、[[瑞银集团|瑞銀集團]]、[[高盛|高盛]]和[[嘉吉公司|嘉古公司]]工作。二人育有兩個兒子。

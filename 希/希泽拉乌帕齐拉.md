'''希泽拉乌帕齐拉'''（{{lang-en|Hizla Upazila}}，{{lang-bn|হিজলা}}）是[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]][[巴里萨尔专区|巴里萨尔专区]][[巴里萨尔县|巴里萨尔县]]的一个乌帕齐拉<ref name=Banglapedia>{{cite book 
|author=KM Saiful Islam
|chapter=Hizla Upazila
|chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=Hizla_Upazila 
|title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |editor=Sirajul Islam and Ahmed A. Jamal |publisher=[[Asiatic_Society_of_Bangladesh|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh]] |year=2012 |edition=Second}}</ref>。

根据{{le|1991年孟加拉国人口普查|1991 Bangladesh census}}，希泽拉乌帕齐拉拥有人口166,265人，男性52.76%，女性47.24%。超过18岁以上人口为75,315。当地识字率为27.7%<ref name="census">{{cite web |  accessdate = November 10, 2006 |  url = http://www.bangladeshgov.org/mop/ndb/arpc91_v1/tables04.htm | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20050327072826/http://www.bangladeshgov.org/mop/ndb/arpc91_v1/tables04.htm | archivedate = 2005-03-27 | title = Population Census Wing, BBS.}}</ref>。

'''希望螈目'''（學名：Elpistostegalia）為生活於[[泥盆紀|泥盆紀]]晚期（約 385 - 374 百萬年前）[[肉鰭魚總綱|肉鰭魚總綱]]下的一[[目_(生物)|目]]<ref>[http://taxonomicon.taxonomy.nl/TaxonName.aspx?id=987575&tree=0.1 Elpistostegidae on the Taxonomicon]</ref>，屬於較為進階的[[四足形類|四足形類]]，親緣關係上比{{link-en|骨鱗魚目|Osteolepiformes}}更為與[[四足類|四足類]]接近。

== 古生態學 ==
全球氧含量的上升使得大型掠食性魚類得以演化，並成為淺海沿岸與[[淡水沼澤|淡水沼澤]]地區的頂級掠食者<ref name=Dahl>{{cite journal |vauthors=Dahl TW, Hammarlund EU, Anbar AD |title=Devonian rise in atmospheric oxygen correlated to the radiations of terrestrial plants and large predatory fish |journal=Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. U.S.A. |volume=107 |issue=42 |pages=17911–5 |date=October 2010 |pmid=20884852 |pmc=2964239 |doi=10.1073/pnas.1011287107 |bibcode = 2010PNAS..10717911D |display-authors=etal}}</ref>。其中最成功的一群就屬希望螈目的物種，然而這些物種在這樣的環境中仍然會面臨週期性缺氧的問題<ref>{{cite journal|last=Lewis Jr. |first=William M. |title=Morphological Adaptations of Cyprinodontoids for Inhabiting Oxygen Deficient Waters |journal=Copeia |date=1 June 1970 |volume=2 |issue=2 |pages=319–326 |url=http://ciresweb.colorado.edu/limnology/pubs/pdfs/Pub001.pdf |jstor=1441653 |doi=10.2307/1441653 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110813153431/http://ciresweb.colorado.edu/limnology/pubs/pdfs/Pub001.pdf |archivedate=2011-08-13 |df= }}</ref>。在現代[[優養化|優養化]]的湖以及沼澤中，[[肺魚|肺魚]]與[[鯰魚|鯰魚]]得以透過呼吸空氣來解決水中缺氧的困境<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1111/j.1502-3931.1990.tb01357.x |author=Long, J.A. |title=Heterochrony and the origin of tetrapods |journal=Lethaia |volume=23 |issue=2 |pages=157–166 |year=1990 }}</ref><ref name="Armbruster1998">{{cite journal|url=http://www.auburn.edu/academic/science_math/res_area/loricariid/fish_key/Air.pdf|title=Modifications of the Digestive Tract for Holding Air in Loricariid and Scoloplacid Catfishes|journal=[[Copeia|Copeia]]|volume=1998|year=1998|issue=3|pages=663–675|accessdate=25 June 2009|doi=10.2307/1447796 |jstor=1447796|last1=Armbruster|first1=Jonathan W.}}</ref>。

希望螈目有許多特徵得以幫助演化成更為適應陸地生活的四足類，其中最重要的一項就是游泳的主要推進來源由尾鰭轉移至胸鰭與腹鰭，另外也逐漸用肺取代鰓來做為主要的呼吸器官<ref name=Gordon&Long>{{cite journal |doi=10.1086/425183 |author1=Gordon, M.S. |author2=Long, J.A. |title=The Greatest Step In Vertebrate History: A Paleobiological Review of the Fish-Tetrapod Transition |journal=Physiological and Biochemical Zoology |volume=77 |issue=5 |pages=700–719 |year=2004 |url=http://usf.usfca.edu/fac_staff/dever/tetrapod_review.pdf |pmid=15547790}}</ref>，這些特徵多半是為了適應生活於內陸的水生生態系<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1111/j.1096-3642.1998.tb02526.x |author=Ahlberg, P.E. |title=Postcranial stem tetrapod remains from the Devonian of Scat Craig, Morayshire, Scotland |journal=[[Zoological_Journal_of_the_Linnean_Society|Zoological Journal of the Linnean Society]] |volume=122 |issue=1–2 |pages=99–141 |year=1998 }}</ref>。

希望螈目於約 375 百萬年前的{{link-en|法门阶|Famennian}}（相當於[[泥盆紀|泥盆紀]]晚期）演化出[[四足類|四足類]]，其他非四足類的希望螈目物種卻在約於 380 百萬年前的{{link-en|弗拉斯階|Frasnian}}之後快速走向絕種。現存希望螈目下的物種僅存有四足類一演化支<ref name=Trackways>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1038/nature08623 | title = Tetrapod trackways from the early Middle Devonian period of Poland | year = 2010 | last1 = Niedźwiedzki | first1 = Grzegorz | last2 = Szrek | first2 = Piotr | last3 = Narkiewicz | first3 = Katarzyna | last4 = Narkiewicz | first4 = Marek | last5 = Ahlberg | first5 = Per E. | journal = Nature | volume = 463 | issue = 7277 | pages = 43–8 | pmid = 20054388 |bibcode = 2010Natur.463...43N }}</ref>。

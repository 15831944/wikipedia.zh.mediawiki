{{Infobox lake|name=希望谷水庫|image=|caption=|image_bathymetry=|caption_bathymetry=|location=[[南澳大利亞|南澳大利亞]]|coords={{coord|34|51|S|138|41|E|region:AU-SA_type:waterbody_source:GNS-enwiki|display=inline,title}}|lake_type=[[水庫|水庫]]|inflow=|outflow=|catchment=|basin_countries={{Aus}}|length=|width=|area={{convert|0.52|km2|abbr=on}}|depth=|max-depth=|volume={{convert|2.84|GL|e6cuft|abbr=on}}}}'''希望谷水库'''是位于南澳大利亚希望谷的一座容量为2.84千兆升（6.2亿英制或7.5亿美制加仑）的水库。该水库位于南澳大利亚州[[阿德莱德|阿德莱德]]市郊。<ref name="SA Water">{{cite web|url=http://www.sawater.com.au/SAWater/Education/OurWaterSystems/Water+Storage+(Reservoirs).htm|title=Water Storage (Reservoirs)|accessdate=12 January 2010|date=10 November 2009|publisher=SA Water|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100124153923/http://www.sawater.com.au/SAWater/Education/OurWaterSystems/Water%2BStorage%2B%28Reservoirs%29.htm|archivedate=24 January 2010|deadurl=yes|df=dmy-all}}</ref>

此水库完工于1873年，为阿德莱德当时所建的第二座水库；也是在南澳大利亚州内使用至今的最早期水库之一。<ref name="Parliament">{{cite web|url=http://www.parliament.sa.gov.au/NR/rdonlyres/1ECB664D-6F21-4F2E-927B-472BFE03B700/3030/finalreport.pdf|title=Hope Valley Reservoir rehabilitation project (final report)|accessdate=12 January 2010|author=Public Works Committee|date=August 2000|publisher=Parliament of South Australia|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070904115244/http://www.parliament.sa.gov.au/NR/rdonlyres/1ECB664D-6F21-4F2E-927B-472BFE03B700/3030/finalreport.pdf|archivedate=2007-09-04}}</ref> 它占地52公顷（128英亩） ，并保有950米（3,117英尺）长的粘土质屏障。[[River_Torrens|托伦斯河]]的水通过隧道和水渠流入该水库。[[Kangaroo_Creek_Reservoir|袋鼠溪]]和[[Millbrook_Reservoir|米尔布鲁克]]水库的水也流经该河流。

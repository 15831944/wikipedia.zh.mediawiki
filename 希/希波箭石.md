'''希波箭石'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Hibolites'''''}}）是生存在晚[[侏羅紀|侏羅紀]]至中[[白堊紀|白堊紀]][[海洋|海洋]]中的一[[屬|屬]][[箭石|箭石]]。<ref>{{cite journal | last = Sepkoski | first = Jack | title =  A compendium of fossil marine animal genera (Cephalopoda entry) | journal = Bulletins of American Paleontology | volume = 364 |page=560 | year = 2002 | url = http://strata.geology.wisc.edu/jack/showgenera.php?taxon=231&rank=class | accessdate = 2009-04-10 }}</ref>牠們的護甲為細長[[矛|矛]]頭狀，膨大處位於中央偏後，自此處向後逐漸變尖。閉錐內朝向口一側的最外二個隔壁有穿孔，可能為其體管的所在位置，但向後延伸不明。<ref name="Hibolites">{{Cite web |url = http://digimuse.nmns.edu.tw/Demo_2011/showMetadata.aspx?ObjectId=0900000180274779&TypeKind=suMeta&Type=invertebrates&Part=2-2&Domain=gg&Field=fi&Language=CHI |title =  典藏標本~~ 希波箭石 Hibolites |language = en |accessdate = 2018-06-14 }}</ref>其[[化石|化石]]分布於[[歐洲|歐洲]]、[[阿爾及利亞|阿爾及利亞]]、[[馬達加斯加|馬達加斯加]]、[[伊朗|伊朗]]和[[莫三比克|莫三比克]]等地。<ref name="Fossilworks">{{Cite web |url = http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=15868 |title =  Fossilworks: Hibolites |language = en |accessdate = 2018-06-14 }}</ref>

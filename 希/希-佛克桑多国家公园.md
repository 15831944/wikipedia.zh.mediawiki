'''希-佛克桑多国家公园'''（{{lang-en|Shey Phoksundo National Park}}）是目前最大且唯一的跨[[喜马拉雅|喜马拉雅]]地区的[[国家公园|国家公园]]。它位于[[尼泊尔|尼泊尔]]，覆盖面积约3,555平方千米（1,373平方英里），跨越尼泊尔西北部的两个行政区[[德尔帕|德尔帕]]和{{link-en|穆古|Mugu District}}。<ref name="nbrb07">Bhuju, U.R., Shakya, P.R., Basnet, T.B., Shrestha, S. (2007) ''Nepal Biodiversity Resource Book. Protected Areas, Ramsar Sites, and World Heritage Sites.'' International Centre for Integrated Mountain Development, Ministry of Environment, Science and Technology, in cooperation with United Nations Environment Programme, Regional Office for Asia and the Pacific.

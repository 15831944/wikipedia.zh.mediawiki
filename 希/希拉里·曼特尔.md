'''希拉里·玛丽·曼特尔'''女爵士，[[英帝國女爵級司令勳章|DBE]]（{{lang-en|Dame '''Hilary Mary Mantel'''}}，{{bd|1952年|7月6日|catIdx=Mantel, Dame Hilary}}），婚前姓汤普森，[[英国|英国]][[小说家|小说家]]、散文家和评论家。她的工作，包括个人回忆录和[[历史小说|历史小说]]，曾参与好几个重要文学奖项的竞逐。

==生平==
在2009年，她以小说《[[狼厅|狼厅]]》获得[[布克奖|布克奖]]。她最新出版的书，托马斯·克伦威尔三部曲的第二部《[[血季|血季]]》，获得2012年的[[布克奖|布克奖]]。她是继[[库切|库切]]、[[彼得·凯里|彼得·凯里]]和[[法雷尔|法雷尔]]之后的又一个两度获该奖的作家<ref>{{cite web|author=Clark, Nick |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/books/news/booker-prize-2012-hilary-mantel-could-become-first-british-writer-to-win-the-literary-prize-twice-after-bring-up-the-bodies-makes-shortlist-8125426.html |title=Booker Prize 2012: Hilary Mantel could become first British writer to win the literary prize twice after Bring up the Bodies makes shortlist - News - Books |publisher=The Independent |date=11 September 2012 |accessdate=17 October 2012}}</ref><ref name="Hilary Mantel’s ‘Wolf Hall’ Wins U.K. Man Booker, 50,000 Pounds">{{cite news| url=http://www.bloomberg.com/apps/news?pid=20601088&sid=a4993nQqaUFw | work=Bloomberg | title=Hilary Mantel's ‘Wolf Hall' Wins U.K. Man Booker, 50,000 Pounds | authors= Pressley, James and Anderson, Hephzibah|date=6 October 2009|accessdate = 14 May 2012}}</ref>。

'''希拉里臺階'''（{{lang-en|Hillary Step}}）是（或曾是）位於[[珠穆朗瑪峰|珠穆朗瑪峰]]峰頂周圍{{convert|12|m|0}}處一塊幾近垂直的岩石山壁，海拔大約{{convert|8790|m|0}}<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=qJjIBFOMsQsC&pg=PT111&dq=hillary+step&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiDg9q06fzTAhVD2yYKHYU7C9IQ6AEILTAC#v=onepage&q=hillary%20step&f=false|title=ON TOP OF WORLD: My Everest Adventure|last=Vajpai|first=Arjun|date=10 November 2010|publisher=Penguin UK|isbn=9788184753042|language=en}}</ref>。此臺階位於東南側的山脊，處於[[珠穆朗瑪南峰|珠穆朗瑪南峰]]與珠穆朗瑪峰頂的半途上，也是從東南側登頂的路線中最後一個真正的挑戰<ref name="auto2">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=pDYJDgAAQBAJ&pg=PT289&dq=hillary+step&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiDg9q06fzTAhVD2yYKHYU7C9IQ6AEIOTAE#v=onepage&q=hillary%20step&f=false|title=Many Everests: An Inspiring Journey of Transforming Dreams Into Reality|last=Kumar|first=Ravindra|date=14 February 2017|publisher=Bloomsbury Publishing|isbn=9789386141347|language=en}}</ref>。希拉里臺階的命名源自於[[艾德蒙·希拉里|艾德蒙·希拉里]]，他與[[丹增諾蓋|丹增諾蓋]]為首對登上珠穆朗瑪峰頂的人類。

'''希維婭·邦戈·翁丁巴'''（{{lang-en|'''Sylvia Bongo Ondimba'''}}，{{bd|1963年|3月11日|catIdx=Bongo Ondimba, Sylvia}}），[[加蓬|加蓬]][[总统|总统]][[阿里·邦戈·翁丁巴|阿里·邦戈·翁丁巴]]的夫人。<ref>[http://time.com/4318534/first-lady-of-gabon-education/ “First Lady of Gabon: Education Should Create Leaders Who Love the Environment”]. TIME. 06-05-2016.</ref>兩人在1989年結為夫妻。他们育有四名子女。

希維婭出生于法国巴黎，大约两岁时随父母先后移居[[杜阿拉|杜阿拉]]和突尼西亚。1974年，希維婭一家搬到加蓬。<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/8238860.stm “Ali Bongo: Like father, like son?”]. BBC. 04-09-2009.</ref>她的父亲是一名保险公司的总裁，母亲是[[哈吉·奥马尔·邦戈|哈吉·奥马尔·邦戈]]的秘书。<ref>[http://www.africanews.com/2017/06/29/ali-bongo-s-father-in-law-attacked-at-paris-cafe-by-gabonese-men/ “Ali Bongo's father-in-law attacked at Paris cafe by Gabonese men”]. Africa News. 29-06-2017.</ref>

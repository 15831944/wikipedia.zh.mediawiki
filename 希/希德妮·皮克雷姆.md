'''希德妮·皮克雷姆'''（{{lang-en|Sydney Pickrem}}，{{bd|1997年|5月21日||}}）生于美国佛罗里达州奥德马尔，是一名加拿大女子游泳运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sportsnet.ca/olympics/sydney-pickrem-earns-second-rio-swim-canadian-trials/|title=Sydney Pickrem earns a second Rio swim at Canadian trials|website=sportsnet.ca|date=2016-04-08|accessdate=2019-01-15}}</ref>。皮克雷姆拥有加拿大和美国双重国籍<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tampabay.com/sports/swimming/east-lake-swim-alum-sydney-pickrem-realizes-olympic-dream/2285805|title=East Lake swim alum Sydney Pickrem realizes Olympic dream|publisher=Tampa Bay Times|date=2016-07-18|accessdate=2019-01-15}}</ref>，她原本仅拥有美国国籍，14岁时教练发现她的父母都来自加拿大，她也因此获得加拿大国籍，并开始代表加拿大参加国际比赛<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thebatt.com/sports/sydney-pickrem-looks-toward-tokyo/article_323b19d0-f3d1-11e6-85ab-ef7bcf80e739.html|title=Sydney Pickrem looks toward Tokyo 2020|publisher=The Battalion|date=2017-02-15|accessdate=2019-01-15}}</ref>。2015年，她进入德克萨斯州农工大学就读，并代表该校校队参加美国国内的比赛<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.collegeswimming.com/news/2017/feb/16/texas-am-women-florida-men-push-pace-during-day-3-of-southeastern-conference-championships/|title=Texas A&M Women, Florida Men Push Pace During Day 3 of Southeastern Conference Championships|publisher=College Swimming|date=2017-02-16|accessdate=2019-01-15}}</ref>。

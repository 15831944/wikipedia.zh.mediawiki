'''希薇亞·荷克絲'''（{{lang-nl|Sylvia Hoeks}}，{{IPA-nl|ɦuks|lang}}；{{bd|1983年|6月1日}}）是一名荷蘭女演員和前模特兒，她最出名是在《[[銀翼殺手2049|銀翼殺手2049]]》中飾演Luv。<ref>{{Cite web |url = https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/movies/2017/10/06/meet-blade-runner-2049-breakout-sylvia-hoeks-who-plays-killer-replicant-luv/725429001/ |title = Who is the killer replicant Luv? Meet 'Blade Runner 2049' breakout Sylvia Hoeks |author = Bryan Alexander |date = 2017-10-08 |publisher = USA TODAY |language = en}}</ref>

== 早年 ==
荷克絲在[[荷蘭|荷蘭]][[北布拉邦省|北布拉邦省]]的{{link-en|馬爾海澤|Maarheeze}}長大<ref>{{Cite web |url = https://www.vanityfair.com/style/2017/08/sylvia-hoeks-is-ready-for-her-american-debut |title = Sylvia Hoeks Is Ready for Her American Debut |author = Krista Smith |publisher = Vanity Fair |language = en |accessdate = 2018-11-25}}</ref>。她會說荷蘭語、德語、法語和英語<ref>{{Cite web |url = https://variety.com/2011/film/markets-festivals/berlinale-shooting-stars-2011-1118030764/ |title = Berlinale: Shooting stars 2011 |author = Shane Danielsen |date = 2011-01-31 |publisher = Variety |language = en}}</ref>。她在高中畢業後，她加入了{{link-en|馬斯特里赫特戲劇藝術學院|Maastricht Academy of Dramatic Arts}}<ref>{{Cite web |url = https://variety.com/gallery/10-europeans-to-watch-in-2017/#!5/sylvia-hoeks |title = 10 Europeans to Watch in 2017 |publisher = Variety |language = en |accessdate = 2018-11-25}}</ref>。

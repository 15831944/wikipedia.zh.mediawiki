'''恰米強克捲'''（{{lang-en|Chimichanga}}）是[[墨西哥卷饼|墨西哥卷饼]]的一种，流行于美国西南部及墨西哥西北部。通常在玉米餅粉中夾多種[[原材料|原材料]]，包括[[稻|稻米]]、[[乾酪|乾酪]]、[[豆类|豆类]]、{{tsl|en|machaca}}（幹肉）、carne {{tsl|en|adobada}}（醃肉）、{{tsl|en|carne seca}}（幹牛肉）、雞塊，再將其折叠裝入矩形包装內，最後進行[[炸|炸製]]而成。可搭配[[萨尔萨酱|萨尔萨酱]]、[[鳄梨酱|鳄梨酱]]、[[酸奶油|酸奶油]]或奶酪食用。

==起源==
關於其起源尚無定論<ref name=sunset>{{cite news |last=Trulsson |first=Nora Burba |title=Chimichanga Mysteries: The Origin of Tucson's Deep-fried Masterpiece Is an Enigma Wrapped in a Tortilla |magazine={{tsl|en|Sunset (magazine)||Sunset}} |date=October 1999 |url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-55884798.html |issn=0039-5404 |via=HighBeam Research |subscription=yes |accessdate=2009-03-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150328134637/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-55884798.html |archive-date=2015-03-28 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref name=post>{{cite news |last=Henderson |first=John |title=We All Win as Chimichanga War Rages on |newspaper={{tsl|en|The Denver Post||The Denver Post}} |department=Food & Dining section |date=2007-01-24 |url=http://www.denverpost.com/food/ci_5064265 |accessdate=2009-03-19 }}</ref><ref name=phoenixnewtimes>{{cite news |url=http://www.phoenixnewtimes.com/2007-11-22/news/chimi-eat-world/full/ |title=Chimi Eat World: Arizona's deepest-fried mystery is smothered in cheese, guacamole and sour cream |first=Michele |last=Laudig |newspaper={{tsl|en|Phoenix New Times||Phoenix New Times}} |date=2007-11-22}}</ref><ref name=nytimes>{{cite news |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2011/11/16/us/arizonans-vie-to-claim-cross-cultural-fried-food.html |title=Arizonans Vie to Claim Cross-Cultural Fried Food |newspaper=[[纽约时报|The New York Times]] |date=2011-11-15 |first=Marc |last=Lacey}}</ref>。

有說法認為<ref>[http://www.azcentral.com/home/food/mexico/articles/0427chimichange27-CR.html Matteo Marra, "Tales of the chimichanga's origin"] {{Dead link|date=March 2014|fix-attempted=yes}}</ref>，1922年，亞利桑那州El Charro餐廳創始人莫妮卡·弗林一次無意將玉米卷餅放入油炸鍋裡，本準備吐出[[西班牙语|西班牙语]][[髒話|髒話]]「chi……」，又旋即改口為「chimichanga」（{{lang-es|thingamajig}}，意為「無名氏」），是為其來源<ref>{{cite web |first=Linda |last=Stradley |origyear=May 18, 2015 |date=April 27, 2017 |title=Chimichanga History and Recipe |url=http://whatscookingamerica.net/History/Chimichanga.htm |publisher=What's Cooking America (blog)}}</ref>。

Macayo's Mexican Kitchen創辦者伍迪·约翰逊則宣稱自己在1946年首創了恰米強克捲。當時他在原餐廳Woody's El Nido試著將墨西哥卷餅放入油炸鍋之中炸製，結果大受歡迎。故在1952年創辦Macayo's之時，將其作為招牌美食之一<ref name=post/>。

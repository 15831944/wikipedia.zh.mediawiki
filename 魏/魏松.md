'''魏松'''（{{bd|1954年|11月||}}），中国[[男高音|男高音]][[歌唱家|歌唱家]]、[[歌剧|歌剧]][[演员|演员]]，中国国家一级演员。大学就读于[[上海音乐学院|上海音乐学院]]声乐系，其间师从[[周小燕|周小燕]]、王维德等音乐教授。<ref name="nyt">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2010/03/05/arts/05iht-madame.html?_r=0|title=China's First Lady of Opera|author=Robert Turnbull|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=March 4, 2010|access-date=2019-03-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190102094829/https://www.nytimes.com/2010/03/05/arts/05iht-madame.html?_r=0|archive-date=2019-01-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>在歌唱事业方面，他还与[[戴玉强|戴玉强]]、[[莫華倫|莫華倫]]齐名，并称“中国三大男高音”。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/2012olympics/2012-07/26/content_15622001.htm |title='China's Three Tenors' salute London's Olympics |access-date=2019-03-16 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181024035615/http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/2012olympics/2012-07/26/content_15622001.htm |archive-date=2018-10-24 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>[http://www.scotchina.org/news/262-chinas-three-tenors-reviewed China's Three Tenors reviewed] {{Wayback|url=http://www.scotchina.org/news/262-chinas-three-tenors-reviewed |date=20131217092043 }} "China's Three Tenors performed with the Royal Scottish National Orchestra at the Usher Hall in Edinburgh on Friday, 3 August 2012."</ref>

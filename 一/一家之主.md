《'''一家之主'''》（{{lang-en|''Man with a Plan''}}）是一部由{{link-en|傑基和傑夫·菲爾戈|Jackie and Jeff Filgo}}開創，於2016年10月24日在[[CBS_(電視網)|CBS]]開播的[[美国|美国]][[喜剧|喜剧]]电视连续剧。<ref name="Deadline">{{Cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2016/05/training-day-bull-macgyver-the-great-indoors-matt-leblanc-jason-katims-cbs-series-1201755376/|title='Training Day', 'Bull', 'MacGyver', 'The Great Indoors', Matt LeBlanc Comedy & Jason Katims Drama Picked Up By CBS|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=May 13, 2016|accessdate=June 22, 2016}}</ref><ref name="CBS Fall Premiere Dates">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2016/06/cbs-fall-premiere-dates-2016-1201776569/|title=CBS Sets Fall 2016 Premiere Dates, Slates JonBenet Ramsey Limited Series|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=June 21, 2016|accessdate=June 21, 2016}}</ref>

2017年3月23日，本劇獲CBS第2季續訂, 于2017年11月13日首播。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/03/cbs-early-renewals-macgyver-superior-donuts-life-in-pieces-hawaii-five-0-blue-bloods-1202049276/|title=CBS Renews 5 Freshman & 11 Returning Series, Including ‘MacGyver’, ‘Superior Donuts’, ‘Life In Pieces’ & ‘Hawaii Five-0’|website=[[Deadline_Hollywood|Deadline Hollywood]]|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=March 23, 2017|accessdate=March 23, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2017/11/01/me-myself-i-cancelled-cbs-season-1-bobby-moynihan/|title=Me, Myself & I Essentially Cancelled at CBS; Man With a Plan Called Off Bench|website=TVLine|first=Michael|last=Ausiello|date=November 1, 2017|accessdate=November 1, 2017}}</ref>

2018年5月12日，本劇獲CBS第3季續訂, 计划于2019年2月4日首映。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2018/05/man-with-a-plan-renewed-season-3-cbs-kevin-can-wait-superior-donuts-1202383579/|title='Man With A Plan' Renewed For Season 3 By CBS, No Word Yet On 'Kevin Can Wait'|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=May 12, 2018|accessdate=May 12, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2018/11/28/cbs-announces-winter-scheduling-plans-for-new-and-returning-comedies-996000/20181128cbs01/|title=CBS Announces Winter Scheduling Plans for New and Returning Comedies|website=The Futon Critic|date=November 28, 2018|accessdate=November 28, 2018}}</ref>

==製作過程==
=== 前期發展 ===
2016年2月3日，製作方正式收到製作試播集，並由 [[James_Burrows|James Burrows]] 指導試播集。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2016/tv/news/matt-leblanc-cbs-comedy-im-not-your-friend-pilot-1201700541/|title=Matt LeBlanc to Star In CBS Comedy Pilot with Series Commitment|last=Prudom|first=Laura|work=TVLine|date=February 8, 2016|accessdate=February 8, 2016}}</ref> 編劇由[[Jackie_and_Jeff_Filgo|Jackie and Jeff Filgo]]撰寫，並由[[Michael_Rotenberg|Michael Rotenberg]] 及 Troy Zien 擔任執行製作。製作團隊包含 Double Double Bonus Entertainment, [[CBS_Television_Studios|CBS Television Studios]]，以及[[3_Arts_Entertainment|3 Arts Entertainment]]。2016年5月12日，CBS 宣布正式名稱為「一家之主」(Man with a Plan)，並宣布將製作一季。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2016/05/training-day-bull-macgyver-the-great-indoors-matt-leblanc-jason-katims-cbs-series-1201755376/|title=‘Training Day’, ‘Bull’, ‘MacGyver’, ‘The Great Indoors’, Matt LeBlanc Comedy & Jason Katims Drama Picked Up By CBS|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline|date=May 13, 2016|accessdate=May 13, 2016}}</ref> 當天稍晚，CBS 宣布將於 2016 年的秋季於黃金時段，每週一 晚間 8:30 播出。

2017年3月23日，CBS 預定第二季<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/03/cbs-early-renewals-macgyver-superior-donuts-life-in-pieces-hawaii-five-0-blue-bloods-1202049276/|title=CBS Renews 5 Freshman & 11 Returning Series, Including ‘MacGyver’, ‘Superior Donuts’, ‘Life In Pieces’ & ‘Hawaii Five-0’|website=[[Deadline_Hollywood|Deadline Hollywood]]|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=March 23, 2017|accessdate=March 23, 2017}}</ref> ，並於 2017年11月13日首播。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2017/11/01/me-myself-i-cancelled-cbs-season-1-bobby-moynihan/|title=Me, Myself & I Essentially Cancelled at CBS; Man With a Plan Called Off Bench|website=TVLine|first=Michael|last=Ausiello|date=November 1, 2017|accessdate=November 1, 2017}}</ref> 2017年11月27日，CBS 宣布為第二季多預定 8 集，讓第二季的總集數增加到 21 集。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2017/11/27/man-with-a-plan-superior-donuts-season-2-full-orders-cbs/|title=Man With a Plan, Superior Donuts Get Full-Season Orders at CBS|last=Gelman|first=Vlada|publisher=[[TVLine|TVLine]]|date=November 27, 2017|accessdate=November 27, 2017}}</ref> 2018年5月12日，CBS 宣布預定第三季。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2018/05/man-with-a-plan-renewed-season-3-cbs-kevin-can-wait-superior-donuts-1202383579/|title='Man With A Plan' Renewed For Season 3 By CBS, No Word Yet On 'Kevin Can Wait'|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=May 12, 2018|accessdate=May 12, 2018}}</ref>

===選角===
主要角色先從 2016年2月，先選出[[麥特·勒布郎|麥特·勒布郎]]，一直到3月選出[[Matt_Cook_(actor)|Matt Cook]] 以及 Grace Kaufman。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2016/03/matt-cook-cbs-matt-leblanc-jeff-filgos-jackie-filgos-cbs-1201714943/|title=Matt Cook Joins CBS’ LeBlanc Filgos Project|last=Petski|first=Denise|work=Deadline|date=March 4, 2016|accessdate=March 4, 2016}}</ref> 2016年5月13日，報導指出原先[[Jenna_Fischer|Jenna Fischer]]將飾演女主角，與麥特·勒布郎演對手戲，因為退出劇組，將重選角色。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2016/05/matt-leblanc-cbs-comedy-jenna-fischer-exits-recasting-man-with-a-plan-1201755657/|title=Matt LeBlanc CBS Series 'Man With A Plan': Jenna Fischer Exits In Recasting|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline|date=May 13, 2016|accessdate=May 13, 2016}}</ref> 8月1日，宣布 [[Liza_Snyder|Liza Snyder]] 將飾演女主角。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvseriesfinale.com/tv-show/man-plan-liza-snyder-joins-matt-leblanc-sitcom/|title=Man with a Plan: Liza Snyder Joins Matt LeBlanc Sitcom - canceled TV shows - TV Series Finale|date=1 August 2016|publisher=|accessdate=2 November 2016}}</ref> 2016年8月2日，報導指出[[Kevin_Nealon|Kevin Nealon]]將參與飾演主要角色之一。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2016/08/man-with-a-plan-kevin-nealon-matt-leblanc-cbs-sitcom-series-regular-1201797323/|title=‘Man With A Plan’: Kevin Nealon Joins Matt LeBlanc CBS Sitcom|date=2 August 2016|publisher=|accessdate=2 August 2016}}</ref> 2018年9月4日，報導指出[[Kali_Rocha|Kali Rocha]] 將在第三季為常駐角色。<ref>{{cite web|url=hhttps://deadline.com/2018/09/man-with-a-plan-kali-rocha-promoted-series-regular-season-3-matt-leblanc-1202456933/|title='Man With A Plan': Kali Rocha Upped To Series Regular For Season 3|date=September 4, 2018|last=Petski|first=Denise|work=Deadline Hollywood|accessdate=September 10, 2018}}</ref>

== 回響 ==
「一家之主」(Man with a Plan) 收到不少負面評論。在[[匯總媒體|匯總媒體]]和[[爛番茄|爛番茄]]等網站，28位評論的平均得分為 21%，來自。平均得分為 3.5/10。這些網站的普遍共識為「一家之主的問題在於,缺乏想像力的劇情導致其表現不佳。」<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/tv/man_with_a_plan/s01/ |title= Man With a Plan: Season 1 |publisher=Rotten Tomatoes |accessdate=February 2, 2017}}</ref> 在[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]網站，則收到普通的成績，25位評論者的平均成績為 36 (滿分100)。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metacritic.com/tv/man-with-a-plan |title= Man With a Plan - Season 1 reviews|publisher=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]|accessdate=February 2, 2017}}</ref>

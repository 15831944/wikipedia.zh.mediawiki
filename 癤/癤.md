'''癤'''（boil、furuncle）是[[毛囊炎|毛囊炎]]的一種，其常見起因為[[金黃色葡萄球菌|金黃色葡萄球菌]]感染，可導致[[人類皮膚|皮膚]]上出現一片由[[膿|膿]]和死亡组织累積形成之有痛感的腫塊。<ref name=medline>{{MedlinePlusEncyclopedia|001474|Furuncle}}</ref>腫脹的癤腫基本上為充滿膿液的結節。<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.healthguidance.org/entry/11040/1/Causes-and-Cures-of-Skin-Boils.html|title= Causes and Cures of Skin |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |date=  |website= Healthguidance.org |publisher= |accessdate= 2014-07-26}}</ref>單獨的癤連結在一起被稱為[[癰|癰]]。<ref name=medline2>{{MedlinePlusEncyclopedia|000825|Carbuncle}}</ref>
人類大部分感染癤是由凝固酶陽性金黃色葡萄球菌[[分型|分型]]引起的，這種細菌可以產生{{tsl|en|coagulase|凝固酶|凝固酶}}，從而使血液凝結。復發性癤還與遺傳因素相關。<ref name=ElGilany09>{{cite journal |vauthors=El-Gilany AH, Fathy H |title=Risk factors of recurrent furunculosis |journal=Dermatol Online J |volume=15 |issue=1 |pages=16 |date=January 2009 |pmid=19281721 |url=http://dermatology.cdlib.org/1501/letters/furunculosis/elgilany.html}}</ref>此外，[[免疫缺陷|免疫缺陷]]因素（如[[艾滋病|艾滋病]]、[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]、[[營養不良|營養不良]]或[[酗酒|酗酒]]）亦可導致癤腫的發生。<ref name=Demo2012>{{cite journal|last1=Demos|first1=M|last2=McLeod|first2=MP|last3=Nouri|first3=K|title=Recurrent furunculosis: a review of the literature.|journal=The British Journal of Dermatology|date=Oct 2012|volume=167|issue=4|pages=725–32|pmid=22803835|doi=10.1111/j.1365-2133.2012.11151.x}}</ref>

不進行處理，癤亦可能會清除。但更常見的是切開癤，並對其進行引流。為防止進一步感染，這一過程不應該在家中自行完成。此外，[[抗细菌药|抗细菌药]]療法亦推薦用作大型或反復發作的癤治療，如果癤生長在敏感部位，如腹股溝、乳房、腋下、鼻孔周圍或鼻腔內、耳內，亦推薦使用該療法。<ref name=medline/><ref name=Bolognia>{{cite book |vauthors=Blume JE, Levine EG, Heymann WR |chapter=Bacterial diseases |veditors=Bolognia JL, Jorizzo JL, Rapini RP |title=Dermatology |publisher=Mosby |year=2003 |isbn=0-323-02409-2 |page=1126 }}</ref><ref name=habif>{{cite book |author=Habif, TP |chapter=Furuncles and carbuncles |title=Clinical Dermatology: A Color Guide to Diagnosis and Therapy |publisher=Mosby |location=Philadelphia PA |year=2004 |edition=4th }}</ref><ref name=wolf>{{cite book |author=Wolf K |chapter=Section 22. Bacterial infections involving the skin |title=Fitzpatrick's Color Atlas & Synopsis of Clinical Dermatology |publisher=McGraw-Hill |year=2005 |edition=5th |display-authors=etal}}</ref>為防止藥效失效，抗细菌药使用時長不應該超過1個月，並在兩次使用期間間隔兩個月以上。<ref>[http://www.mayoclinic.com/health/boils-and-carbuncles/DS00466/DSECTION=symptoms Mayo Clinic]  {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120815234857/http://www.mayoclinic.com/health/boils-and-carbuncles/DS00466/DSECTION%3Dsymptoms |date=2012-08-15  }}</ref>

== 概述 ==
在英国，任何一部电影都要提交给[[英國電影分級委員會|英國電影分級委員會]]分级，而据电影制片人查利·琳所说，此过程成本平均约为1000英镑，初出茅庐的电影制作人难以支付得起。<ref>[http://www.telegraph.co.uk/film/movie-news/paint-drying-bbfc-certificate/ The Telegraph: Why the BBFC watched paint dry for 10 Hours: filmmaker Charlie Lyne explains]</ref>查利·琳遂以此片表示抗议。为使本片尽可能的长，查利·琳曾创立了一个[[Kickstarter|Kickstarter]]页面来募集资金，从686名赞助者处共筹集了超过5,900英镑。电影最终分级结果为4岁及以上的观众可观看，<ref>[https://qz.com/604112/heres-how-uk-film-censors-rated-a-10-hour-film-of-paint-drying/ Quartz: Here’s how UK film censors rated a 10-hour film of paint drying]</ref><ref>[http://www.independent.co.uk/voices/charlie-lynes-10-hour-plus-film-of-paint-drying-to-the-british-film-board-made-hard-work-of-film-a6844316.html Independent: Charlie Lyne's 10-hour-plus film of paint drying to the British film board made hard work of film censorship]</ref>于2016年1月26日发行。<ref name="Independent">{{Cite web|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/films/news/bbfc-declares-paint-drying-film-a-u-after-sitting-through-all-607-minutes-a6834671.html|title=BBFC rates Paint Drying film 'U', after sitting through all 607 minutes|accessdate=23 June 2018|last=Loughrey|first=Clarisse|publisher=Independent Print Limited}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://mentalfloss.com/article/71591/why-filmmaker-shot-14-hour-movie-about-paint-drying|title=Why This Filmmaker Shot a 14-Hour Movie About Paint Drying|accessdate=23 June 2018|last=Green|first=Anna}}</ref>

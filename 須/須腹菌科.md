'''須腹菌科'''（[[學名|學名]]：Rhizopogonaceae）是[[牛肝菌目|牛肝菌目]]的一個[[科_(生物)|科]]<ref name=Kirk2007>{{cite book |vauthors=Cannon PF, Kirk PM |title=Fungal Families of the World |publisher=CABI |location=Wallingford |year=2007 |pages=313–14 |isbn=0-85199-827-5}}</ref>，於1928年由瑞士植物學家{{link-en|Ernst Albert Gäumann|Ernst Albert Gäumann}}與美國真菌學家{{link-en|卡羅爾·威廉·多奇|Carroll William Dodge}}定義、描述<ref name=Gaumann1928>{{cite book |title=Comparative Morphology of Fungi |vauthors=Gäumann EA, Dodge CW |year=1928 |publisher=McGraw-Hill Book Company |location=New York |url=https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/item/26604#488 |accessdate=2010-03-23 |page=468 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120920014133/http://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/item/26604#488#488 |archive-date=2012-09-20 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。本科下包含{{link-en|須腹菌屬|Rhizopogon}}與''[[Rhopalogaster|Rhopalogaster]]''等兩個[[屬_(生物)|屬]]，共約150種物種<ref name=Kirk2008>{{cite book |vauthors=Kirk PM, Cannon PF, Minter DW, Stalpers JA |title=Dictionary of the Fungi |edition=10th |publisher=CABI |location=Wallingford |year=2008|page=599|isbn=978-0-85199-826-8}}</ref>，其中''Rhopalogaster''為[[單型|單型]]屬，只包含一種物種<ref>KirK PM, pp. 601</ref>。有另一屬''{{link-en|Fevansia|Fevansia}}''傳統上被認為屬於須腹菌科，但已有研究基於分子證據將其改歸入[[紅菇目|紅菇目]]的{{link-en|地花菌科|Albatrellaceae}}<ref name="Smith 2013">{{cite journal |vauthors=Smith ME, Schell KJ, Castellano MA, Trappe MJ, Trappe JM |title=The enigmatic truffle ''Fevansia aurantiaca'' is an ectomycorrhizal member of the ''Albatrellus'' lineage |journal=Mycorrhiza |year=2013 |volume=23 |pages=663–8 |doi=10.1007/s00572-013-0502-2 |url=http://fes.forestry.oregonstate.edu/sites/fes.forestry.oregonstate.edu/files/PDFs/2013%20Smith%20Fevansia.pdf |format=PDF |author= |access-date=2018-10-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304032056/http://fes.forestry.oregonstate.edu/sites/fes.forestry.oregonstate.edu/files/PDFs/2013%20Smith%20Fevansia.pdf# |archive-date=2016-03-04 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

[[分子種系發生學|分子種系發生學]]的研究也顯示[[牛肝菌目|牛肝菌目]]中，須腹菌科與[[鉚釘菇科|鉚釘菇科]]、[[乳牛肝菌科|乳牛肝菌科]]、[[截軸包菌科|截軸包菌科]]（Truncocolumellacae）等類群的親緣關係較為接近，有研究將本科與這三科共同歸屬於乳牛肝菌亞目（Suillineae）<ref name=Hibbett>{{cite journal|title=Molecular systematics and biological diversification of Boletales|author=Binder M, Hibbett DS|publisher=''Mycologia''|year=2006|volume=98|issue=6|page=971-81|url=https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/af91/2cfca2201ff313c8c0f463bfcda58498c7fa.pdf|journal=|access-date=2018-10-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181015003101/https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/af91/2cfca2201ff313c8c0f463bfcda58498c7fa.pdf#|archive-date=2018-10-15|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

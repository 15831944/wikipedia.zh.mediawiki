'''倭犰狳科'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|en|Chlamyphoridae}}）是[[有甲目|有甲目]]下的一科哺乳類動物。傳統分類上，[[雕齒獸亞科|雕齒獸亞科]]是與現存犰狳不同的主幹分支，但有根據形態學上的研究指出它們可能與[[犰狳科|犰狳科]]是一個[[並系群|並系群]]。<ref>Simpson, G. G. (1945). The principles of classification and a classification of mammals. Bull. Amer. Museum Nat. History., 85.</ref><ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Grassé | first1 = P. P. | year = 1955 | title = Ordre des édentés | url = | journal = Traité de zoologie | volume = 17 | issue = 2| pages = 1182–1246 }}</ref><ref>Engelmann, G. F. (1985). The phylogeny of the Xenarthra. The evolution and ecology of armadillos, sloths, and vermilinguas. Smithsonian Institution Press, Washington, DC, 51-64.</ref><ref>Wible, J. R. (2006). 6 The Phylogeny of Living and Extinct Armadillos (Mammalia, Xenarthra, Cingulata): A Craniodental Analysis. Amniote Paleobiology: Perspectives on the Evolution of Mammals, Birds, and Reptiles: University of Chicago Press, Chicago, IL, 153-198.</ref> 

在2016年，根據[[星尾獸|星尾獸]]的[[mtDNA|mtDNA]]的分析，發現是與現存的犰狳共同包含在[[倭犰狳亞科|倭犰狳亞科]]及[[三帶犰狳亞科|三帶犰狳亞科]]的旁系群內。<ref name="Delsuc2016">{{cite journal|last1=Delsuc|first1= F.|last2= Gibb|first2=G. F.|last10=MacPhee|first10=R. D. E.|last11= Poinar|first11=H. N.|title=The phylogenetic affinities of the extinct glyptodonts|journal=Current Biology|volume= 26|issue=4|date= 2016-02-22|pages=R155–R156|doi=10.1016/j.cub.2016.01.039}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author1=Gillian C. Gibb |author2=Fabien L. Condamine |author3=Melanie Kuch |author4=Jacob Enk |author5=Nadia Moraes-Barros |author6=Mariella Superina |author7=Hendrik N.

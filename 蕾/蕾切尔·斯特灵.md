'''蕾切尔·斯特灵'''（{{lang-en|'''Rachael Stirling'''}}，{{bd|1977年|5月30日|||catIdx= S斯特灵}}）出生于[[英国|英国]][[英格兰|英格兰]][[伦敦市|伦敦市]]，为英国女演员。<ref name="BBC">{{citation |title=Drama Faces - Rachael Stirling |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/drama/faces/rachael_stirling.shtml |publisher=[[BBC|BBC]] |date=January 2006 |accessdate=2011-06-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110610080109/http://www.bbc.co.uk/drama/faces/rachael_stirling.shtml |archivedate=2011-06-10 }}.</ref> 

== 生平 ==
1977年，斯特灵出生于[[英国|英国]][[英格兰|英格兰]][[伦敦市|伦敦市]]，她母亲是英国著名女演员[[戴安娜·瑞格|戴安娜·瑞格]]，父亲是男演员阿切尔·斯特灵。<ref name="Evening Standard">{{citation |first=Viv |last=Groskop |date=2010-02-17 |url=http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/theatre/article-23806855-rachael-stirling-is-a-rising-stage-star-and-shes-in-love-with-her-ass.do |accessdate=2011-06-12 |title=Rachael Stirling is a rising stage star – and she's in love with her ass |publisher=''[[London_Evening_Standard|London Evening Standard]]'' |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605071255/http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/theatre/article-23806855-rachael-stirling-is-a-rising-stage-star-and-shes-in-love-with-her-ass.do |archivedate=2011-06-05 }}.</ref> 
1982年，她的父母结婚，1990年，两人离婚。<ref name="Diana Rigg Story">{{cite web |first=Nigel |last=Farndale |url=http://commanderbond.net/components/quicknews/index.php?action=item&item=47868 |title=Diana Rigg: Her Story |work=[[Telegraph.co.uk|Telegraph.co.uk]] |accessdate=2011-06-12 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080803115936/http://commanderbond.net/components/quicknews/index.php?action=item&item=47868 |archivedate=2008-08-03 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>  
她父亲来自苏格兰和她的同名城市斯特灵。<ref name="BBC" />
她毕业于[[爱丁堡大学|爱丁堡大学]]。<ref name="BBC" />

''parodiiis'' due to a mutation in the isoeugenol synthasegene| journal = The Plant Journal| volume = 58| issue = 6| pages = 961–9| year = 2009| last1 = Koeduka | first1 = T. | last2 = Orlova | first2 = I. | last3 = Baiga | first3 = T. J. | last4 = Noel | first4 = J. P. | last5 = Dudareva | first5 = N. | last6 = Pichersky | first6 = E. | pmc=2860387}}</ref>，現在可以由該屬的任何植物雜交而成<ref name=GRIN>{{cite web |url=http://www.ars-grin.gov/cgi-bin/npgs/html/taxon.pl?462920 |title=USDA GRIN Taxonomy |accessdate=17 April 2014 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>，它是一種常見的園藝植物。<ref name="MBG Petunia">{{cite web | url=http://www.missouribotanicalgarden.org/PlantFinder/PlantFinderDetails.aspx?kempercode=a601 | title=Petunia (group) | publisher=Missouri Botanical Garden | accessdate=12 September 2015}}</ref>

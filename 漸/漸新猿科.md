'''漸新猿科'''（Oligopithecidae）是[[始新世|始新世]]末期（約3700萬年前）生活在[[埃及|埃及]]的一[[科|科]][[靈長目|靈長目]]。根據其[[臼齒|臼齒]]及齒尖的構造，相信牠們是吃[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]的。<ref>{{cite journal en|author=Kay, R. F.; Ross, C.; Williams, B.|title=Anthropoid Origins|journal=Science|date=1997-02-07|volume=275|issue=5301|pages=797-804|doi=10.1126/science.275.5301.797}}</ref>在[[新世界猴|新世界猴]]分裂出來前，牠們才與[[舊世界猴|舊世界猴]]及[[猿|猿]]分支的。<ref>{{cite web | author =  Haaramo, M | title =  Mikko's Phylogeny Archive - Anthropoidea - monkeys and apes | url =  http://www.fmnh.helsinki.fi/users/haaramo/metazoa/Deuterostoma/Chordata/Synapsida/Eutheria/Primates/anthropoidea.htm#Oligopithecidae | accessdate =  2007-11-12 | deadurl =  yes | archiveurl =  https://web.archive.org/web/20071223080304/http://www.fmnh.helsinki.fi/users/haaramo/Metazoa/Deuterostoma/Chordata/Synapsida/Eutheria/Primates/anthropoidea.htm#Oligopithecidae | archivedate =  2007-12-23 }}</ref>

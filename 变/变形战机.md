{{Vgname|变形战机|ja=テグザー|en=Thexder}}是一款[[街机|街机]]风格[[跑动射击游戏|跑动射击游戏]]，由[[Game_Arts|Game Arts]]出品，最初于1985年在NEC-PC 8801平台出品，随后在1980和1990年代移植到[[红白机|红白机]]等大量平台。游戏之跑动射击游戏一大里程碑，为《[[魂斗罗|魂斗罗]]》和《[[合金弹头|合金弹头]]》等作品提供了设计路线<ref name="IGN-Retro">{{cite web|author = Travis Fahs|url = http://retro.ign.com/articles/861/861550p2.html|title = The Leif Ericson Awards – Retro Feature at IGN|publisher = Retro.ign.com|date = |accessdate = 2011-09-06}}</ref>。游戏售出100万，是当时最畅销的电脑游戏<ref name="thexder2_cover">{{citation|title = Back Cover|work = [[Fire_Hawk:_Thexder_The_Second_Contact|Fire Hawk: Thexder The Second Contact]]|publisher = [[Game_Arts|Game Arts]] & [[Sierra_On-Line|Sierra On-Line]]|year = 1990|url = http://pics.mobygames.com/images/covers/large/1790158784.jpg|accessdate = 2012-02-10}}</ref>。

==背景==
生命之树－欢乐之光犹太教堂（{{lang|en|Tree of Life – Or L'Simcha Congregation}}）是[[犹太教保守派|犹太教保守派]]会堂<ref name="tolols"/>。会堂位于[[宾夕法尼亚州|宾夕法尼亚州]][[匹兹堡|匹兹堡]]{{le|松鼠山|Squirrel Hill (Pittsburgh)}}区，[[卡内基·梅隆大学|卡内基·梅隆大学]]以东1英里（1.6公里），{{le|匹兹堡市中心|Downtown Pittsburgh}}以东5英里（8公里），自称“传统、进步、平等的犹太教堂”<ref name="tolols">{{cite web|url=https://www.tolols.org/|title=Tree of Life * Or L'Simcha Congregation {{!}} Pittsburgh Synagogue|last=|first=|date=|website=Tree of Life * Or L'Simcha Congregation|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181027164856/http://www.tolols.org/|archive-date=2018-10-27|dead-url=no|access-date=2018-10-27}}</ref><ref name="NBC_News"/>。松鼠山是美国最大的犹太人聚居社区，历史上曾是匹兹堡犹太社区腹地，全市26%的犹太人口都住在该地区<ref name="WaPo">{{cite news |last1=Paul |first1=Deanna |last2=Selk |first2=Avi |last3=Wang |first3=Amy B. |date=2018-10-27 |title=Pittsburgh synagogue shooting leaves multiple people dead and wounded |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/nation/2018/10/27/pittsburgh-police-responding-active-shooting-squirrel-hill-area/?noredirect=on&utm_term=.0129904080e4 |work=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]] |accessdate=2018-10-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Smith |first=Peter |date=2018-02-20 |title=Report: Pittsburgh Jewish community growing, spreading out |url=http://www.post-gazette.com/local/city/2018/02/20/study-Pittsburgh-Jewish-community-growing-spreading-out/stories/201802200058 |work=[[Pittsburgh_Post-Gazette|Pittsburgh Post-Gazette]] |accessdate=2018-10-27}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=Boxer|first=Matthew|title=The 2017 Greater Pittsburgh Jewish Community Study|url=https://www.brandeis.edu/ssri/communitystudies/pittsburghreport.html|volume=|pages=1-3|year=2018|orig-year=2017|publisher=Cohen Center for Modern Jewish Studies|access-date=2018-10-28|last2=Brookner|first2=Matthew|last3=Aronson|first3=Janet|last4=Saxe|first4=Leonard}}</ref>。

生命之树于1864年在匹兹堡市中心成立，最初是[[犹太教正统派|犹太教正统派]]教堂。2010年欢乐之光犹太教堂并入后，现在的教堂正式成立<ref>{{cite web |title=About Us |url=https://www.tolols.org/about-us |website=Tree of Life * Or L'Simcha |accessdate=2018-10-27 |date=2018-10-27}}</ref>。如今的现代犹太教堂建筑，位于威尔金斯大道（{{lang|en|Wilkins Avenue}}）和阴凉大道（{{lang|en|Shady Avenue}}）交汇处，建于1953年。部分空间由[[犹太教重建派|犹太教重建派]]多哈达什（{{lang|en|Dor Hadash}}）和保守派新光（{{lang|en|New Light}}）租用<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=weL9M46TcU8C&pg=PA318 |title=The American Synagogue: A Historical Dictionary and Sourcebook|first1=Kerry M. |last1=Olitzky|first2=Marc Lee |last2=Raphael|year=1996|publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group|isbn=9780313288562|page=318}}</ref><ref name="Haaretz">{{cite news |last1=Tibon |first1=Amir |last2=Landau |first2=Noa |last3=Kaplan Sommer |first3=Allison |last4=Maltz |first4=Judy |date=2018-10-27 |title=Eight Killed in Pittsburgh Synagogue Shooting; Gunman Yelled 'All Jews Must Die' |url=https://www.haaretz.com/us-news/man-reportedly-opens-fire-near-a-pittsburgh-synagoue-1.6595493 |work=[[Haaretz|Haaretz]] |accessdate=2018-10-27}}</ref><ref name="PG">{{cite news|url=https://www.post-gazette.com/local/city/2018/10/27/Police-responding-to-incident-in-Squirrel-Hill/stories/201810270069|title='Multiple casualties,' some fatalities in Squirrel Hill synagogue mass shooting|last=Mamula|first=Kris|date=2018-10-27|work=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|accessdate=2018-10-27|last2=Goldstein|first2=Andrew|last3=Ward|first3=Paula|last4=Navratil|first4=Liz|last5=Bradbury|first5=Shelly}}</ref>。主圣所可容纳1250人<ref>{{cite news|url=https://apnews.com/23b04dc5e5af4129b544ab50cbba3dd6|title=Suspect in custody in Pittsburgh synagogue attack|last=Scolforo|first=Mark|date=2018-10-27|work=|accessdate=2018-10-27|agency=Associated Press|last2=Gillispie|first2=Mark}}</ref>。

尽管松鼠山治安案件少，没有种族紧张的迹象，当地还是于1986年发生了[[反犹太主义|反犹太主义]][[仇恨犯罪|仇恨犯罪]]事件，导致当地的{{le|尼尔·罗森布鲁姆遇害案|Murder of Neal Rosenblum|拉比学生尼尔·罗森布鲁姆被谋杀}}<ref name="FarzanCommunity">{{cite news |last1=Farzan |first1=Antonia Noori |title=Before synagogue mass shooting, a 1986 murder shook Pittsburgh’s Jewish community |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/nation/2018/10/29/before-synagogue-mass-shooting-murder-rabbinical-student-shook-pittsburghs-jewish-community/?utm_term=.1bec09435773 |accessdate=2018-10-29 |publisher=The Washington Post |date=2018-10-29}}</ref>。

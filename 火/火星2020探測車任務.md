'''火星2020探測車任務'''（Mars 2020 rover mission），是[[美國國家航空暨太空總署|美國國家航空暨太空總署]]（NASA）一個正在研議中的[[火星|火星]][[探測車|探測車]]任務概念，可能的發射時間在2020年。<ref name="NASA-Mars2020" />
<ref name="AP-20130709">{{cite news |last=Chang |first=Alicia |title=Panel: Next Mars rover should gather rocks, soil |url=http://apnews.excite.com/article/20130709/DA7EA0K83.html |date=9 July 2013 |work=[[AP_News|AP News]] |accessdate=12 July 2013 }}</ref><ref name='Cowing'>{{cite web | url = http://spaceref.com/mars/science-definition-team-for-the-2020-mars-rover.html | title = Science Definition Team for the 2020 Mars Rover | accessdate = 21 December 2012 | first = Keith Cowing | date = 21 December 2012 | work = NASA | publisher = Science Ref}}</ref>

NASA在2012年12月4日於舊金山舉辦的[[美國地球物理聯盟|美國地球物理聯盟]]宣布火星2020探測車任務。<ref name="CNET Harwood first">{{cite news|title=NASA announces plans for new $1.5 billion Mars rover|url=http://news.cnet.com/8301-11386_3-57557184-76/nasa-announces-plans-for-new-$1.5-billion-mars-rover/|accessdate=5 December 2012|newspaper=CNET|date=4 December 2012|first=William|last=Harwood|quote=Using spare parts and mission plans developed for NASA's Curiosity Mars rover, the space agency says it can build and launch a new rover in 2020 and stay within current budget guidelines.}}</ref>探測車的設計將源自於[[好奇號|好奇號]]，不過會搭載不同的科學酬載。<ref name="BBC-20131204">{{cite news |last=Amos |first=Jonathan |title=Nasa to send new rover to Mars in 2020 |date = 4 December 2012 |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-20603689 |work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] |accessdate=5 December 2012 }}</ref>
NASA正在評估科學酬載的提案。<ref name="NASA-20140121">{{cite web |last1=Webster |first1=Guy |last2=Brown |first2=Dwayne |title=NASA Receives Mars 2020 Rover Instrument Proposals for Evaluation |url=http://www.jpl.nasa.gov/news/news.php?release=2014-017 |date=21 January 2014 |work=[[NASA|NASA]] |accessdate=21 January 2014 }}</ref>

==設計==
一個原本作為好奇號備用裝備的[[放射性同位素熱電機|放射性同位素熱電機]]將會驅動這任務探測車的動力。<ref name="CNET Harwood first" /><ref name="NBC Boyle first">{{cite news|title=NASA plans 2020 Mars rover remake|url=http://cosmiclog.nbcnews.com/_news/2012/12/04/15678579-nasa-plans-2020-mars-rover-remake|accessdate=5 December 2012|newspaper=Cosmic Log|date=4 December 2012|first=Alan|last=Boyle|agency=NBC News}}</ref>

根據{{link-en|航空太空公司|The Aerospace Corporation}}預估，這個任務將耗資15±2億美元範圍內。相較之下，好奇號耗資25億美元。<ref name="CNET Harwood first" />
NASA一直到宣布任務的當天還在計算任務的預估成本。<ref name="ISJ AP first">{{cite news|title=Mars redux: NASA to launch Curiosity-like rover|url=http://www.idahostatejournal.com/news/national/article_d6d9d945-c1b5-5695-a40a-492bded1a6dd.html|accessdate=5 December 2012|newspaper=Idaho State Journal|date=4 December 2012|agency=Associated Press}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

NASA的科學副署長[[約翰·格倫斯菲爾德|約翰·格倫斯菲爾德]]說，由於當初製造好奇號剩餘的備用零件都還在，所以才能在NASA微薄的預算裡推動這個任務。好奇號的工程團隊也將協助火星2020探測車的設計。

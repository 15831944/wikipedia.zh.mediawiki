== 效果 ==
此挑战完成后通常会导致二级[[灼傷|灼傷]]。<ref name="local2"/><ref name="huff">{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/07/30/fire-challenge-video_n_5634043.html |title=What The Hell Is A 'Fire Challenge,' And How Could It Not Go Horribly Wrong? |last=Jauregui |first=Andres |publisher=Huffingtonpost.com |date=2014-07-30 |accessdate=2014-08-03}}</ref>有一个来自[[肯塔基州|肯塔基州]]的青少年参加挑战，挑战后表示挑战后的疼痛"无法忍受"。<ref>{{cite web|date=August 1, 2014|first=Heather|last=Mills |url=http://kdvr.com/2014/08/01/colorado-fire-officials-issue-warning-about-fire-challenge/ |title=Colorado fire officials issue warning about ‘fire challenge’ |publisher=kdvr.com |accessdate=2014-08-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nbcmiami.com/news/local/Fla-Kid-Suffers-2nd--3rd-Degree-Burns-from-Fire-Challenge-269481181.html |title=Fla.

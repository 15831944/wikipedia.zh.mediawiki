[[美国地质调查局|美国地质调查局]]将[[火星|火星]]表面划分为30个四边形区域（quadrangle）。每个区域都以当地特征来命名，<ref name="mapping mars">{{cite book| title=Mapping Mars: Science, Imagination, and the Birth of a World| first=Oliver| last=Morton| publisher=Picador USA| location=New York| year=2002| isbn=0-312-24551-3| pages=98}}</ref><ref>[http://ralphaeschliman.com/id30.htm Online Atlas of Mars<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>同时冠以“MC”（Mars Chart的简写，意为“火星图”）的前缀进行编号。<ref>[http://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/catalog/PIA03467 Catalog Page for PIA03467<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>

'''火腿节'''（{{lang-en|'''Hamfest'''}}）是[[业余无线电|业余无线电]]爱好者的盛会，<ref>''Hello World: A Life in Ham Radio''. Danny Gregory, [[Paul_Sahre|Paul Sahre]], 2003, Princeton Architectural Press, {{ISBN|1-56898-281-X}}</ref>火腿节的主要元素包括商品电台展销、[[跳蚤市场|跳蚤市场]]以及其他业余无线电活动。<ref>"Ham" is the slang word often used to refer to amateur radio operators who are tested and licensed by world governments to operate their personal two-way radio sets and do non-commercial (amateur) communication with their fellow hobbyists around the globe.</ref><ref>[http://www.southgatearc.org/rallies/index.htm Amateur Radio Rallies and Hamfests] {{Wayback|url=http://www.southgatearc.org/rallies/index.htm |date=20180903091123 }} by the Southgate Amateur Radio Club</ref>历史上第一次火腿节于1924年在[[美国|美国]]举办。<ref>{{cite journal |last=Schnell 1MO |first=F.H. |title=Midwest Division Hamfest |journal=QST |issue=Oct., 1924 |pages=40–41}} (first reference to "hamfest" in QST)</ref>

{{tsl|en|Dayton Hamvention|代顿火腿节}}通常被认为是世界上规模最大的火腿节。<ref>http://www.hamvention.org/ {{Wayback|url=http://www.hamvention.org/ |date=20180810185444 }} Dayton Hamvention</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Live webcast of world’s largest amateur radio gathering|url=http://www.portcars.org/wp/?p=5382|website=portcars.org|publisher=PCARS|accessdate=16 May 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160604022746/http://www.portcars.org/wp/?p=5382|archive-date=2016-06-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>

==活动==
火腿节是由业余无线电爱好者为互相交流和推广爱好而组织的集会。一次火腿节的时长从数小时到数天不等，但也有一些年度大型火腿节会持续一周时间。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.arrl.org/hamfests-and-conventions-calendar |title=Hamfests and Conventions Calendar |accessdate=2018-09-08 |author=ARRL |language=en |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180821160151/http://www.arrl.org/hamfests-and-conventions-calendar |archive-date=2018-08-21 |dead-url=no }}</ref>大多数火腿节以[[跳蚤市场|跳蚤市场]]为特色，在跳蚤市场上，爱好者们可以买卖电台和其他周边设备。<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.kb6nu.com/hamfest-stories/ |title=Hamfest stories |accessdate=2018-09-08 |author=Daniel M. Romanchik, KB6NU |date=2018-03-18 |work=KB6NU's Ham Radio Blog |language=en-US |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180908164927/https://www.kb6nu.com/hamfest-stories/ |archive-date=2018-09-08 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.vk3bq.com/2013/10/21/what-is-a-hamfest-a-formal-junk-swap/ |title=What is a Hamfest - "A Formal Junk Swap?"  |accessdate=2018-09-08 |author=Andrew L. Scott, VK3BQ |date=2013-10-21 |language=en-US}}</ref>一些厂商也在火腿节上设置展示厅，展示最新设备。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://hamfest.org/DealersList.php |title=The 2018 Dealer List |accessdate=2018-09-08 |author=Huntsville Hamfest |date=2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180830234316/http://hamfest.org/DealersList.php |archive-date=2018-08-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref>火腿节上的活动也包括业余无线电协会的聚会、业余无线电相关的技术讲座和[[业余无线电#许可|执照]]考试讲解等。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.hamfest.org/Activities.php |title=Huntsville Hamfest Activities/Details |accessdate=2018-09-08 |author=Huntsville Hamfest |date=2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180830225946/http://hamfest.org/Activities.php |archive-date=2018-08-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://n0ssc.com/posts/722-things-to-do-at-hamvention-2018 |title=Things to Do at Hamvention 2018 |accessdate=2018-09-08 |author=Sterling S. Coffey, N0SSC |date=2018-05-08 |work=NØSSC's Ham Radio Blog |language=en-US |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180908165318/http://n0ssc.com/posts/722-things-to-do-at-hamvention-2018 |archive-date=2018-09-08 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

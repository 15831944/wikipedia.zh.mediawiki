'''莊淑玲'''（'''Nurul Huda Ch' Su Lin'''，{{bd|1972年|7月31日}}<ref name="Nurul biography">{{cite web| language = en| publisher = Sports Reference LLC| title = Nurul Huda Ch' Su Lin| url = http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ch/nurul-huda-ch-su-lin-1.html| date = | accessdate = 2012-08-11| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121026131507/http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/ch/nurul-huda-ch-su-lin-1.html| archivedate = 2012-10-26}}</ref>）是前[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]女子游泳運動員，擅長自由泳。是已故新加坡第四任總統[[黃金輝|黃金輝]]的外孫女。從1985年開始連續五年榮獲馬來西亞全國最佳女運動員。<ref name = "奧運名人堂" />1987年，他成為馬來西亞首位獲得[[國際奧委會|國際奧委會]]獎項（“體育與學習”）的選手。<ref name="ioctrophy">{{cite web| language = en| publisher = Olympic Council of Malaysia| title = Gallery of IOC Trophies Winner| url = http://www.olympic.org.my/web/sportpeople/iocTrophies/trophyWinners.htm| date = | accessdate = 2012-08-15| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120815053821/http://www.olympic.org.my/web/sportpeople/iocTrophies/trophyWinners.htm| archivedate = 2012-08-15}}</ref>2004年，她獲選進入馬來西亞奧運會名人堂。<ref name = "奧運名人堂">{{cite web | language = en | publisher = Olympic Council of Malaysia| title = OCM Sports Museum and Hall of Fame - Nurul Huda Abdullah Ch’Su-Lin| url = http://www.olympic.org.my/museum/hof/ind/nha.htm| date = 2009| accessdate = 2012-08-11 }}</ref>

==生涯==
莊淑玲11歲就代表馬來西亞參加[[東南亞運動會|東南亞運動會]]。1989年吉隆坡東南亞運動會，她與[[王觀成|王觀成]]領銜的馬來西亞游泳隊成功獲得史上最佳的11金6银3铜，從而讓馬來西亞首度荣膺游泳全场冠军。<ref name="malaysia swimmer1">{{cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 南洋體育 | title = 长风破浪·《大马泳队游出东南亚》系列1:实力仅能扬威东运 大马飞鱼力争上游 | url = http://www.nanyang.com/node/409330?tid=500 | date = 2011-12-29 | accessdate = 2012-08-11 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304123457/http://www.nanyang.com/node/409330?tid=500 | archivedate = 2016-03-04 }}</ref>她總共參加三屆東南運動會，並累積了22面金牌。<ref name="malaysia swimmer2">{{cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 南洋體育 | title = 长风破浪《大马泳队游出东南亚》系列2：淑玲观成俊道露锋芒 亚运会摘牌入史册 | url = http://www.nanyang.com/node/409573?tid=500 | date = 2011-12-29 | accessdate = 2012-08-11 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304202209/http://www.nanyang.com/node/409573?tid=500 | archivedate = 2016-03-04 }}</ref>她也是馬來西亞首位能在[[亞洲運動會|亞洲運動會]]上獲得獎牌的游泳選手。由於她需要專注學業，所以便在十八歲之齡退休了。

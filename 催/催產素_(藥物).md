<!-- 定義及醫療用途 -->
'''催產素'''（Oxytocin），其藥物名稱為Pitocin，也有其他名稱，是由人工合成的[[催产素|催产素]]<ref name=AHFS2015/><ref>{{cite book | title = The Oxford Handbook of Prosocial Behavior | date = 2015 | publisher = Oxford University Press | isbn = 978-0-19-539981-3 | page = 354 | url = https://books.google.ca/books?id=EfzOBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA354 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20170801001244/https://books.google.ca/books?id=EfzOBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA354 | archivedate = 2017-08-01 | df =  }}</ref>。其醫療用途包括使[[子宮收縮|子宮收縮]]（[[引產|引產]]）、催生、以及[[產後出血|產後出血]]的止血<ref name=AHFS2015/>。若用在這些用途上，會用[[肌肉注射|肌肉注射]]或[[靜脈注射|靜脈注射]]方式給藥<ref name=AHFS2015>{{cite web | title = Oxytocin | url = https://www.drugs.com/monograph/oxytocin.html | publisher = The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists | access-date = 1 June 2015 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150520014256/http://www.drugs.com/monograph/oxytocin.html | archivedate = 20 May 2015 | df =  }}</ref>。

<!-- 副作用 -->
催產素作為醫療用途時，會引發強烈的子宮收縮，為胎兒健康帶來風險<ref name=AHFS2015/>，懷孕女性常見的[[副作用|副作用]]為想吐、[[心跳過緩|心跳過緩]]<ref name=AHFS2015/>，嚴重的副作用有因{{le|劑量 (生物化學)|Dose (biochemistry)|劑量}}過重導致{{le|子宮破裂|Uterine rupture}}以及[[水中毒|水中毒]]<ref name=AHFS2015/>，對此藥{{le|過敏反應|Drug allergy}}可能為[[過敏性休克|過敏性休克]]<ref name=AHFS2015/>。

<!-- 社會及文化 -->
催產素發現於1952年<ref>{{cite book |last1=Hurlemann |first1=Rene |last2=Grinevich |first2=Valery |title=Behavioral Pharmacology of Neuropeptides: Oxytocin |date=2018 |publisher=Springer |isbn=9783319637396 |page=37 |url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=lIRjDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA37 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1113/jphysiol.1906.sp001148 |pmid=16992821 |pmc=1465771 |title=On some physiological actions of ergot |journal=The Journal of Physiology |volume=34 |issue=3 |pages=163–206 |year=1906 |last1=Dale |first1=H. H }}</ref>，是[[醫療系統|醫療系統]]內最有效且安全的必需藥物清單之一，名列[[世界衛生組織基本藥物標準清單|世界衛生組織基本藥物標準清單]]中<ref name=WHO19th>{{cite web|title=WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (19th List)|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|work=World Health Organization|accessdate=8 December 2016|date=April 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161213052708/http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|archivedate=13 December 2016|df=}}</ref>；2014 年，在[[發展中國家|發展中國家]]的批發價約在一劑 0.1 美金到 0.56 美元之譜<ref>{{cite web | title = Oxytocin | url = http://mshpriceguide.org/en/single-drug-information/?DMFId=580&searchYear=2014 | website = International Drug Price Indicator Guide | access-date = 20 December 2015 }}</ref>。

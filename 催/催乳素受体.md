'''催乳素受体'''（{{lang-en|prolactin receptor}}）由染色体5p13-14区段的基因编码，作为一种{{le|细胞表面受体|Cell surface receptor|跨膜受体}}与[[催乳素|催乳素]]相互作用。

它包括一个胞外区以结合催乳素，一个跨膜区，以及一个胞内区域。

该受体属于[[细胞因子受体|细胞因子受体]]<ref>Prolactin: The multi-faceted potentiator of mammary growth and function.  Trott JF, Schennink A, Petrie WK, Manjarin R, Vanklompenberg MK, Hovey RC.  J Anim Sci. 2011 Dec 28.</ref>，其[[第二信使|第二信使]]信号通过[[JAK-STAT信号通路|JAK-STAT信号通路]]、JAK-RUSH信号通路<ref>Prolactin-induced Jak2 phosphorylation of RUSH: a key element in Jak/RUSH signaling.  Helmer RA, Panchoo M, Dertien JS, Bhakta SM, Hewetson A, Chilton BS.  Mol Cell Endocrinol. 2010 Aug 30;325(1-2):143-9.</ref><ref>Prolactin induces Jak2 phosphorylation of RUSHY195.  Helmer RA, Dertien JS, Chilton BS.  Mol Cell Endocrinol. 2011 May 16;338(1-2):79-83.</ref>、Ras-Raf-MAPK和PI-3K<ref>The role of prolactin in mammary carcinoma.  Clevenger CV, Furth PA, Hankinson SE, Schuler LA.  Endocr Rev. 2003 Feb;24(1):1-27.</ref>途径级联放大。

不同组织的催乳素受体可能会有所变化<ref>Impact of prolactin receptor isoforms on reproduction.  Binart N, Bachelot A, Bouilly J.  Trends Endocrinol Metab. 2010 Jun;21(6):362-8.</ref>。

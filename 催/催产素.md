'''催產素'''（{{lang-en|Oxytocin}}，又称'''缩宫素'''，简称为'''OT'''）是一種[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]][[激素|激素]]，是[[肽类激素|肽类激素]]也是{{le|神經肽|Neuropeptide}}，一般是由[[下丘脑|下丘脑]]產生，由{{le|垂體後葉|Posterior pituitary}}分泌<ref>{{cite book | title = Gray's Anatomy: The Anatomical Basis of Clinical Practice | date = 2015 | publisher = Elsevier Health Sciences | isbn = 978-0-7020-6851-5 | page = 358 | edition = 41 | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=b7FVCgAAQBAJ&pg=PA358 }}</ref>。催产素可以在[[大腦|大腦]][[下視丘|下視丘]]「室旁核」與「{{link-en|視上核|supraoptic nucleus}}」[[神經元|神經元]]所自然分泌，經下視丘腦下垂體路徑神經纖維送到{{le|垂體後葉|posterior pituitary gland}}分泌。催产素在社會連結、[[有性生殖|有性生殖]]、在[[分娩|分娩]]中及分娩後都相當的重要<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Yang|first1=HP|last2=Wang|first2=L|last3=Han|first3=L|last4=Wang|first4=SC|title=Nonsocial functions of hypothalamic oxytocin.|journal=ISRN neuroscience|date=2013|volume=2013|pages=179272|pmid=24967304}}</ref>。催產素在雌性[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]生產時大量釋放，擴張[[子宮頸|子宮頸]]和收縮[[子宮|子宮]]，促進[[分娩|分娩]]，分娩後催产素也會刺激[[乳頭|乳頭]]，促進[[乳汁|乳汁]]產生<ref name=Chi2012>{{cite book|last1=Chiras|first1=Daniel D.|title=Human biology|date=2012|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|location=Sudbury, MA|isbn=9780763783457|page=262|edition=7th ed.|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=mqlY1n8Ez1oC&pg=PA262}}</ref>，有助於生產、{{le|母嬰連結|maternal bonding}}及[[哺乳|哺乳]]<ref name=Chi2012/><ref>{{cite book|title=Human Evolutionary Biology|date=2010|publisher=Cambridge University Press|isbn=9781139789004|page=282|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=3NRf_8gwmO8C&pg=PA282}}</ref>。催产素是1906年由[[亨利·哈利特·戴尔|亨利·哈利特·戴尔]]發現的<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1113/jphysiol.1906.sp001148 |pmid=16992821 |pmc=1465771 |title=On some physiological actions of ergot |journal=The Journal of Physiology |volume=34 |issue=3 |pages=163–206 |year=1906 |last1=Dale |first1=H. H }}</ref>，在1952年確定其分子結構<ref>{{cite book | last1 = Corey | first1 = E.J. | name-list-format = vanc | title = Molecules and Medicine | date = 2012 | publisher = John Wiley & Sons | isbn = 978-1-118-36173-3 | chapter-url = https://books.google.com/books?id=jz2GN6DYoOoC&lpg=RA1-PT108&pg=RA1-PT110 | chapter = Oxytocin }}</ref>。[[催產素_(藥物)|做為藥物的催產素]]可以幫助生產<ref name="IUPHAR Oxytocin summary">{{cite web | title=Oxytocin: Summary | url=http://www.guidetopharmacology.org/GRAC/LigandDisplayForward?tab=summary&ligandId=2174 | website=IUPHAR/BPS guide to pharmacology | publisher=International Union of Basic and Clinical Pharmacology | accessdate=14 December 2016 | quote = Endogenous oxytocin is a hormone and neuropeptide, which plays a role in social bonding, sexual reproduction and is required during and after childbirth. Purified oxytocin is used clinically.}}</ref><ref name=AHFS2015>{{cite web|title=Oxytocin|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/oxytocin.html|publisher=The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists | access-date  = 1 June 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite book | title = The Oxford Handbook of Prosocial Behavior | date = 2015 | publisher = Oxford University Press | isbn = 978-0-19-539981-3 | page = 354 | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=EfzOBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA354 }}</ref>。

==簡介==
催產素英文單詞Oxytocin是來自希臘ὼκυτοκίνη，ōkytokínē，意思是“快誕生”，其子宮收縮性質分別由英國藥理學家[[亨利·哈利特·戴爾|亨利·哈利特·戴爾]]在1906年發現的<ref name="pmid16992821">{{cite journal | author = Dale HH | title = On some physiological actions of ergot | journal = J. Physiol. (Lond.) | volume = 34 | issue = 3 | pages = 163–206 | date = 1906-05 | pmid = 16992821 | pmc = 1465771 | doi =  }}</ref>。催奶性能由奧特（Ott）和斯科特（Scott）於1910年<ref>Ott I, Scott JC. The Action of Infundibulum upon Mammary Secretion. Proc Soc Exp Biol. (1910) p.8:48–49.</ref>和謝弗（Schafer）和Mackenzie於1911年描述<ref>Schafer EA, Mackenzie K. ''The action of animal extracts on milk secretion''. Proceedings of the Royal Society of London Series B-Containing Papers of a Biological Character. (1911) p.84:16–22.</ref>。

1953年初科學家[[文森特·迪维尼奥|文森特·迪维尼奥]]的團隊和Tuppy成功測定排列出催產素的九個[[氨基酸|氨基酸]]序列<ref name="pmid13129273">{{cite journal | author = du Vigneaud V, Ressler C, Trippett S | title = The sequence of amino acids in oxytocin, with a proposal for the structure of oxytocin | journal = J. Biol. Chem. | volume = 205 | issue = 2 | pages = 949–57 | date = 1953-12 | pmid = 13129273 | doi =  }}</ref>。後在1953年科學家[[文森特·迪维尼奥|文森特·迪维尼奥]]的團隊合成了可應用到為[[婦女|婦女]][[催生|催生]]的[[人工合成催產素|人工合成催產素]]。1955年，[[文森特·迪维尼奥|迪维尼奥]]因此獲得[[諾貝爾化學奬|諾貝爾化學奬]]<ref>{{Cite journal| author = du Vigneaud V, Ressler C, Swan JM, Roberts CW, Katsoyannis PG, Gordon S  | title = The synthesis of an octapeptide amide with the hormonal activity of oxytocin | journal = J. Am. Chem. Soc. | volume = 75 | issue = 19 | pages = 4879–80 | year = 1953 | pmid =  | doi = 10.1021/ja01115a553 | url = http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ja01115a553 }}</ref><ref name=Ressler_1954>{{cite journal | author = du Vigneaud V, Ressler C, Swan JM, Roberts CW, Katsoyannis PG | title = The synthesis of oxytocin | journal = J. Am. Chem. Soc. | date = 1954-06 | volume = 76 | issue = 12 | pages = 3115–3121 | doi = 10.1021/ja01641a004}}</ref>。催產素是第一個被測序和合成的{{link-en|神经肽|neuropeptide}}。文森特·迪维尼奥1954年出版指出這個合成是第一個神经肽的合成<ref>THE SYNTHESIS OF OXYTOCIN.  Vincent du Vigneaud, Charlotte Ressler, John M. Swan, Carleton W. Roberts, Panayotis G. Katsoyannis  1954  J. Am. Chem. Soc., 1954, 76（12）,pp 3115–3121</ref>。

==結構==
[[File:Oxytocin-neurophysin.png|thumb]]
催产素是由九个[[氨基酸|氨基酸]]组成的[[多肽|多肽]]构成的，[[人類|人類]]與大多數[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]的催產素的化學結構如下：
<div style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: red">[[半胱氨酸|半胱氨酸]]─[[酪氨酸|酪氨酸]]─[[異亮氨酸|異亮氨酸]]─[[穀氨醯胺|穀氨醯胺]]-[[天冬醯胺|天冬醯胺]]-[[半胱氨酸|半胱氨酸]]-[[脯氨酸|脯氨酸]]-[[亮氨酸|亮氨酸]]-[[甘氨酸|甘氨酸]]─ [[氨基|NH<sub>2</sub>]]</span></div>

催產素的結構和[[抗利尿激素|抗利尿激素]]（[[半胱氨酸|半胱氨酸]]─[[酪氨酸|酪氨酸]]─[[苯丙氨酸|苯丙氨酸]]─[[穀氨醯胺|穀氨醯胺]]-[[天冬氨酸|天冬氨酸]]-[[半胱氨酸|半胱氨酸]]-[[脯氨酸|脯氨酸]]-[[精氨酸|精氨酸]]-[[甘氨酸|甘氨酸]]─ NH<sub>2</sub>）非常相似。催產素和抗利尿激素由[[文森特·迪维尼奥|文森特·迪维尼奥]]於1953年分離出和[[化学合成|合成]]，使他獲得1955年諾貝爾化學獎，從人類[[腦下垂體|腦下垂體]]釋放的激素中，目前只有發現这两種激素是會在一段距離作用。

==功能==
最近的研究已經開始探討催產素對於各種行為的影響，包括[[性高潮|性高潮]]、社會認同、夫妻或情侶間的{{link-en|成對結合|pair bonding}}、[[焦慮|焦慮]]，和產婦的行為等<ref name="pmid19482229">{{Cite journal|author=Lee HJ, Macbeth AH, Pagani JH, Young WS |title=Oxytocin: the Great Facilitator of Life |journal=Progress in Neurobiology |volume=88 |issue=2 |pages=127–51 |date=2009-06 |pmid=19482229 |doi=10.1016/j.pneurobio.2009.04.001 |pmc=2689929}}</ref>。出於這個原因，它有時被稱為“愛情激素”。催產素有助於減輕壓力及緩解疼痛<ref name="康健">{{cite journal |author=梁煙純 |date=2005-10 |title=用雙手安撫肌肉的情緒 |journal=康健雜誌 |volume=83期 |url=http://www.commonhealth.com.tw/article/article.action?id=5016721 |accessdate= 2012-04-12 |quote= }}</ref>。無法分泌催產素，與無法感到同情，[[反社會|反社會]]，[[精神病|精神病]]，[[自戀|自戀]]及一般的[[控制狂|操控個性]]有關<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,1992405,00.html |title=Thanks, Mom! |author=O'Callaghan, Tiffany |date=7 June 2010 |work=Time Magazine |publisher=Time, Inc. |accessdate=2010-06-08}}</ref>。

===催乳===
刺激[[乳頭|乳頭]]，促進乳汁產生，有助[[母乳餵養|母乳餵養]]。

===催產===
收縮[[子宮|子宮]]，促進[[分娩|分娩]]。

===社交行為和傷口癒合===
催產素也被認為是調節降低某些[[細胞因子|細胞因子]]的炎症。因此，積極的社會交往增加催產素的釋放，可能加速傷口癒合。

===夫妻關係===
催產素喚起知足的心情，減少焦慮，增加平靜的感覺和圍繞配偶的安全感<ref name="Meyer2007">{{Cite journal|first=Dixie |last=Meyer |year=2007 |title=Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors and Their Effects on Relationship Satisfaction |journal=The Family Journal |volume=15 |issue=4 |pages=392–397 |doi=10.1177/1066480707305470}}</ref>。許多研究已經證明了催產素與人類結合，增加信任，減少恐懼的相關性。一項研究證實，血漿催產素水平和成人浪漫的依戀之間有一個正相關關係<ref name="Marazziti2006">{{Cite journal|author=Marazziti D |title=A relationship between oxytocin and anxiety of romantic attachment |journal=Clinical Practice and Epidemiology in Mental Health |volume=2 |issue= |pages=28 |year=2006 |pmid=17034623 |pmc=1621060 |doi=10.1186/1745-0179-2-28|author-separator=,|author2=Dell'Osso B|author3=Baroni S|display-authors=3|last4=Mungai|first4=Francesco|last5=Catena|first5=Mario|last6=Rucci|first6=Paola|last7=Albanese|first7=Francesco|last8=Giannaccini|first8=Gino|last9=Betti|first9=Laura}}</ref>。這表明，催產素可能抑制與行為控制，恐懼，[[焦慮|焦慮]]相關的[[大腦|大腦]]區域，從而使[[性高潮|性高潮]]出現。

[[德國|德國]][[波昂大學|波昂大學]]的研究發現，催產素可協助已婚或擁有「承諾關係」的男性保持忠貞，讓他們與異性誘惑對象保持距離。波昂大學教授赫勒曼說：「催產素可增加信任……過去研究顯示[[田鼠|田鼠]]在催產素影響下會表現忠貞，我們首度證實催產素對人的效果雷同。」<ref>{{cite news

'''硫酸三(乙二胺)合镍(II)'''是镍(II)的[[乙二胺|乙二胺]][[配合物|配合物]]，化学式为[Ni(en)<sub>3</sub>]SO<sub>4</sub>，其中en为乙二胺的简写。该配合物由[Ni(en)<sub>3</sub>]<sup>2+</sup>和SO<sub>4</sub><sup>2−</sup>离子构成。<ref>CN Caughlan, M-ul-Haque, K Emerson. Crystal and molecular structure of tris(ethylenediamine)nickel(II) sulfate, Ni(NH<sub>2</sub>CH<sub>2</sub>CH<sub>2</sub>NH<sub>2</sub>)<sub>3</sub>SO<sub>4</sub>. Inorganic Chemistry, 1970, 9 (11): 2421-2424</ref>
==制备==
该配合物由[[硫酸镍|硫酸镍]]和[[乙二胺|乙二胺]]反应得到。
==化学性质==
在氦气中，该配合物的分解分两个阶段，第一阶段生成[Ni(en)]SO<sub>4</sub>，并释出乙二胺，第二阶段分解生成Ni<sub>3</sub>S<sub>2</sub>和Ni等物质。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007%2Fs10973-011-1726-z|issue=1|doi=10.1007/s10973-011-1726-z|pages=267–275|title=Thermal behaviour of nickel(II) sulphate, nitrate and halide complexes containing ammine and ethylenediamine as ligands|volume=106|journal=Journal of Thermal Analysis and Calorimetry|date=2011-06-25|issn=1388-6150|language=en|accessdate=2018-11-05|author=K. S. Rejitha, Suresh Mathew}}</ref>而在空气中的分解则会直接得到[[硫酸镍|NiSO<sub>4</sub>]]，并最终生成[[氧化镍|NiO]]。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007%2Fs10973-010-0823-8|issue=3|doi=10.1007/s10973-010-0823-8|pages=931–939|title=Thermoanalytical investigations of tris(ethylenediamine)nickel(II) oxalate and sulphate complexes|volume=102|journal=Journal of Thermal Analysis and Calorimetry|date=2010-05-07|issn=1388-6150|language=en|accessdate=2018-11-05|author=K. S. Rejitha, Suresh Mathew}}</ref>

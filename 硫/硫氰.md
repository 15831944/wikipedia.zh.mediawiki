'''硫氰'''化學式為(SCN)<sub>2</sub>，是一种黄色液体，在常温下可以分解，是一种[[拟卤素|拟卤素]]。

硫氰的[[空間對稱群|空間對稱群]]為C<sub>2</sub>，其結構可表示為NCS-SCN<ref>{{cite journal | first = James | last = Jensen | year = 2005 | title = Vibrational frequencies and structural determination of thiocyanogen | journal = Journal of Molecular Structure: THEOCHEM | volume = 714 | issue = 2–3 | pages = 137–141 | doi = 10.1016/j.theochem.2004.09.046   }}</ref>。硫氰的氧化能力比[[溴|溴]]要大，硫氰會和水反應；

(SCN)<sub>2</sub>  +  H<sub>2</sub>O   →   HSCN  +  HSCNO

硫氰最早是由[[硫氰酸银|硫氰酸银]]与[[碘|碘]]在[[乙醚|乙醚]]中制得，但由於碘的弱氧化力，此反應最後會達到平衡，產率不佳。硫氰也可以由[[硫氰酸鉛|硫氰酸鉛]]和Br<sub>2</sub>反应产生，而硫氰酸鉛可以由硝酸鉛和[[硫氰酸鈉|硫氰酸鈉]]混合而成。可以用無水的硫氰酸鉛和溴在[[冰醋酸|冰醋酸]]中反應<ref>{{cite journal | last = Gardner | first = William Howlett | coauthors = Weinberger, Harold | year = 1939 | title = Inorganic Syntheses | journal = [[Inorganic_Syntheses|Inorganic Syntheses]] | volume = 1 | pages = 84–86 | doi = 10.1002/9780470132326.ch29 | chapter = Thiocyanogen Solution | series = Inorganic Syntheses | isbn = 978-0-470-13232-6}}</ref>。另一種方法是將溶於[[二氯甲烷|二氯甲烷]]中的溴滴入懸浮在0℃二氯甲烷的硫氰酸鉛中，之後再用氬氣保護，會生成硫氰的溶液，但製備的硫氰需立刻使用<ref name=Block>{{cite journal | last = Block | first = E | coauthors = Birringer, M; DeOrazio, R;  Fabian, J; Glass, RS; Guo, C; He, C; Lorance, E; Qian, Q; Schroeder, TB; Shan, Z; Thiruvazhi, M; Wilson, GC; Zhang, Z | year = 2000 | title = Synthesis, Properties, Oxidation, and Electrochemistry of 1,2-Dichalcogenins | journal = J. Amer. Chem. Soc. | volume = 122 | pages = 5052–5064 | doi = 10.1021/ja994134s}}</ref>。

:Pb(SCN)<sub>2</sub>  +  Br<sub>2</sub>   →   (SCN)<sub>2</sub>  +  PbBr<sub>2</sub>

硫氰會和烯類產生加成反應，生成有1,2-二(硫氰)結構的化合物。硫氰也會和二茂钛杂环戊二烯（titanacyclopentadiene）反應，最後生成1,2-二噻烯（1,2-dithiins）。[[硒氰|硒氰]]化學式為(SeCN)<sub>2</sub>，可以由[[硒氰酸银|硒氰酸银]]和碘在0度的[[四氢呋喃|四氢呋喃]]中反應制得<ref>{{cite journal | last = Meinke | first = PT | coauthors = Krafft, GA; Guram, A | year = 1988 | title = Synthesis of selenocyanates via cyanoselenation of organocopper reagents | journal = J. Org. Chem. | volume = 53 | pages = 3632-3634 | doi = 10.1021/jo00250a047}}</ref>，其反應和硫氰類似<ref name=Block/>。另外，硫氰能与硝酸反应，产生氢氰酸，一氧化氮，硫酸等；或被彻底氧化成CO2,NO2,SO3等气体。

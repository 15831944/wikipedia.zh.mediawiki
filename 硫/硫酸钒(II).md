'''硫酸钒(II)'''是二价[[钒|钒]]的[[硫酸盐|硫酸盐]]，化学式为VSO<sub>4</sub>。

==化学性质==
硫酸钒(II)的六水合物在干燥环境下比七水合物更加稳定。它可以形成(NH<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>[V(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>6</sub>](SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>等配合物。<ref>Cotton F A, Falvello L R, Murillo C A, et al. Neutron and X-ray Structural Characterization of the Hexaaquavanadium(II) Compound VSO4.cntdot.7H2O[J]. Inorganic Chemistry, 1994, 33(33):5391-5395.</ref>

硫酸钒(II)和[[碳酸钠|碳酸钠]]在高压[[二氧化碳|CO<sub>2</sub>]]下反应，生成碳酸钒(II)（VCO<sub>3</sub>）。<ref>Ehrhardt, H.; Schober, H. C.; Seidel, H. High-​pressure syntheses of carbonates. III. Synthesis of carbonates of divalent metals by decomposition of their oxalates under high carbon dioxide pressure{{de}}. ''Zeitschrift fuer Anorganische und Allgemeine Chemie'', 1980. 465: 83-91,</ref>

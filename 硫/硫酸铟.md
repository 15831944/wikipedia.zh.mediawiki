'''硫酸铟'''是[[铟|铟]]的[[硫酸盐|硫酸盐]]，化学式为In<sub>2</sub>(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>3</sub>。它是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，由[[铟|铟]]、[[氧化铟|氧化铟]]或其碳酸盐和[[硫酸|硫酸]]反应得到，制备过程中，硫酸需要过量，否则会生成难溶的碱式盐。<ref name=hes63/>硫酸铟可以以[[无水物|无水物]]、五水合物<ref name=Perret74>Perret, R; Tudo, J; Jolibois, B; Couchot, P (July 1974). "Préparation et caractérisation cristallographique de quelques sulfates doubles d'indium(III) et de thallium(III), MI3MIII (SO4)3 (MI = Na, K, Rb et Cs)". Journal of the Less Common Metals (in French). 37 (1): 9–12. doi:10.1016/0022-5088(74)90003-4.</ref>或九水合物的形式存在。硫酸铟可用于制备铟或其它含铟的化合物。硫酸铟可以形成[[酸式盐|酸式盐]]、[[碱式盐|碱式盐]]或[[复盐|复盐]]铟矾（indium alum）。

==性质==
在水溶液中，铟离子可以和水、硫酸根形成配合物，如InSO<sub>4</sub>·5H<sub>2</sub>O<sup>+</sup>和In(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>·4H<sub>2</sub>O<sup>−</sup>。<ref name=cam81>{{cite journal|last1=Caminiti|first1=R.|last2=Paschina|first2=G.|title=An X-ray diffraction study of the structure of the aqua indium(III) ion in indium sulphate solution|journal=Chemical Physics Letters|date=September 1981|volume=82|issue=3|pages=487–491|doi=10.1016/0009-2614(81)85425-5|bibcode=1981CPL....82..487C}}</ref><ref name=cw>{{cite book|last1=Cotton|first1=F. Albert|last2=Wilkinson|first2=Geoffrey|title=Advanced Inorganic Chemistry|date=1966|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|page=438}}</ref>铟和硫酸根的配合物是少见的，硫酸根对其的效应可以通过[[拉曼光谱|拉曼光谱]]展示。<ref name=hes63>{{cite journal|last1=Hester|first1=Ronald E.|last2=Plane|first2=Robert A.|last3=Walrafen|first3=George E.|title=Raman Spectra of Aqueous Solutions of Indium Sulfate, Nitrate, and Perchlorate|journal=The Journal of Chemical Physics|date=1963|volume=38|issue=1|pages=249|doi=10.1063/1.1733470|bibcode=1963JChPh..38..249H}}</ref>硫酸根配合物的配位比随着温度的升高而增加，这表明硫酸根的形成的反应是吸热反应。配位比也随着溶液浓度的增加而增加。<ref name=Rud004>Rudolph, Wolfram W.; Fischer, Dieter; Tomney, Madelaine R.; Pye, Cory C. (2004). [https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Wolfram_Rudolph/publication/250465251_Indium%28III%29_Hydration_in_Aqueous_Solutions_of_Perchlorate_Nitrate_and_Sulfate._Raman_and_Infrared_Spectroscopic_Studies_and_ab-initio_Molecular_Orbital_Calculations_of_Indium%28III%29Water_Clusters/links/00b7d51eeb18a4f8d9000000.pdf "Indium(iii) hydration in aqueous solutions of perchlorate, nitrate and sulfate. Raman and infrared spectroscopic studies and ab-initio molecular orbital calculations of indium(iii)-water clusters"] (PDF). Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics. 6 (22): 5145. Bibcode:2004PCCP....6.5145R. doi:10.1039/b407419j. Retrieved 31 May 2015.</ref>硫酸根配合物以每秒10,000,000的速率和水快速交换，因此，核磁共振不能辨别配位的铟离子和非配位的铟离子。<ref name=Rud004/>硫酸铟溶液显酸性，0.14 mol/L的溶液的[[pH|pH]]为1.85。当pH上升至3.4时，会生成沉淀。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Busev|first1=A.I.|title=The Analytical Chemistry of Indium|date=2013-10-22|publisher=Elsevier|page=30|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=pVf9BAAAQBAJ&pg=PA5|isbn=9781483149554}}</ref>

===化学反应===
加热至{{convert|710|K|C}}以上时，硫酸铟会放出[[三氧化硫|三氧化硫]]蒸汽，生成[[氧化铟|氧化铟]]：<ref name=Zhou999>{{cite journal|last1=Zhou|first1=Huijuan|last2=Cai|first2=Weiping|last3=Zhang|first3=Lide|title=Synthesis and structure of indium oxide nanoparticles dispersed within pores of mesoporous silica|journal=Materials Research Bulletin|date=April 1999|volume=34|issue=6|pages=845–849|doi=10.1016/S0025-5408(99)00080-X}}</ref>

In<sub>2</sub>(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>3</sub> → In<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + 3 SO<sub>3</sub>

将碱加入硫酸铟溶液，会生成碱式盐沉淀。如[[氢氧化钾|氢氧化钾]]加入到溶液中，生成In<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub>.SO<sub>3</sub>·nH<sub>2</sub>O或KIn<sub>3</sub>(OH)<sub>6</sub>(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>，生成物和pH有关。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Grimes|first1=S. M.|title=Chapter 4. Al, Ga, In, Tl|journal=Annual Reports on the Progress of Chemistry, Section A|date=1984|volume=81|page=90|doi=10.1039/IC9848100075|url=http://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/1984/ic/ic9848100075|accessdate=2015-06-02}}</ref>[[焦磷酸钠|焦磷酸钠]]与之反应，形成粘稠的焦磷酸铟（In<sub>4</sub>(P<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>)<sub>3</sub>·3H<sub>2</sub>O）沉淀。和[[高碘酸钾|高碘酸钾]]的反应可以得到碱式高碘酸铟（2 InO<sub>5</sub>·In(OH)<sub>3</sub>·6 H<sub>2</sub>O）沉淀。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Busev|first1=A.I.|title=The Analytical Chemistry of Indium|date=2013-10-22|publisher=Elsevier|pages=67–68|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=pVf9BAAAQBAJ&pg=PA67|isbn=9781483149554}}</ref>和[[草酸|草酸]]的反应，生成草酸铟沉淀（In<sub>2</sub>(C<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub>)<sub>3</sub>·10 H<sub>2</sub>O）；碱金属草酸盐与之反应，生成配合物MIn(C<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>·3 H<sub>2</sub>O（其中M=Na、K、NH<sub>4</sub>）的沉淀。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Busev|first1=A.I.|title=The Analytical Chemistry of Indium|date=2013-10-22|publisher=Elsevier|pages=111–112|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=pVf9BAAAQBAJ&pg=PA111|isbn=9781483149554}}</ref>

==相关化合物==
===酸式盐===
铟可以形成酸式盐HIn(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>·4H<sub>2</sub>O，可以结晶为正交晶系的晶体，晶胞参数a=9.997 Å，b=5477 Å，c=18.44 Å。其密度为2.50 cm<sup>−3</sup>。HIn(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>可以通过蒸发硫酸铟的40%[[硫酸|硫酸]]溶液得到，<ref name=Tudo79>{{cite journal|last1=Tudo|first1=J.|last2=Jolibois|first2=B.|last3=Laplace|first3=G.|last4=Nowogrocki|first4=G.|last5=Abraham|first5=F.|title=Structure cristalline du sulfate acide d'indium(III) hydraté|journal=Acta Crystallographica Section B|date=1979-07-15|volume=35|issue=7|pages=1580–1583|doi=10.1107/s0567740879007172|language=fr}}</ref>或者冷却硫酸铟的60%硫酸溶液得到。<ref name=Voropaeva007>{{cite journal|last1=Voropaeva|first1=E. Yu.|last2=Stenina|first2=I. A.|last3=Yaroslavtsev|first3=A. B.|title=Proton conduction in indium hydrogensulfate and hydrous zirconia composites|journal=Russian Journal of Inorganic Chemistry|date=January 2007|volume=52|issue=1|pages=1–6|doi=10.1134/S0036023607010019}}</ref>

===碱式盐===
向硫酸铟溶液中加入水或[[乙醇|乙醇]]，可以生成碱式盐，晶体在数周后生成。<ref name=Johanss61>{{cite journal|last1=Johansson|first1=Georg|title=The Crystal Structure of InOHSO4(H2O)2|journal=Acta Chemica Scandinavica|date=1961|volume=15|issue=7|pages=1437–1453|url=http://actachemscand.dk/pdf/acta_vol_15_p1437-1453.pdf|accessdate=2015-05-31|doi=10.3891/acta.chem.scand.15-1437}}</ref>InOHSO<sub>4</sub>·(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>2</sub>属单斜晶系，晶胞参数为a=6.06 Å，b=7.89 Å，c=12.66 Å，β=107.5°，晶胞体积为577.6 Å<sup>3</sup>。<ref name=Johanss61/>另一种碱式盐InOHSO<sub>4</sub>为三方晶系，于160 °C加热硫酸铟的溶液，或在密封管内放置一星期得到。<ref name=Johanss62>{{cite journal|last1=Johansson|first1=Georg|title=The Crystal Structure of FeOHSO4 and InOHSO4|journal=Acta Chemica Scandinavica|date=1962|volume=16|issue=5|pages=1234–1244|url=http://actachemscand.org/pdf/acta_vol_16_p1234-1244.pdf|accessdate=2015-05-31|doi=10.3891/acta.chem.scand.16-1234}}</ref>

===硫酸铟(I)===
一价的硫酸铟(I)（In<sub>2</sub>SO<sub>4</sub>）可以通过固相加热[[铟|铟]]和硫酸铟(III)的混合物得到，<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Dmitriev|first1=V.S.|last2=Malinov|first2=S.A.|last3=Dubovitskaya|first3=L.G.|last4=Smirnov|first4=V.A.|title=Vzaimodejstvie metallicheskogo indiya s sul'fatom indiya(3)|journal=Zhurnal Neorganicheskoj Khimii|date=September 1986|volume=31|issue=9|pages=2372–2377|trans_title=Metallic indium interaction with indium(3) sulfate|language=ru|issn=0044-457X}}</ref>溶于水或硫酸时，In<sup>+</sup>与其反应，放出[[氢气|氢气]]。<ref name=Kozin>{{cite journal|last1=Kozin|first1=L.F.|last2=Egorova|first2=A.G.|title=Sul'fat odnovalentnogo indiya, ego sintez i svojstva|journal=Zhurnal Obshchej Khimii|date=May 1982|volume=52|issue=5|pages=1020–1024|trans_title=Monovalent indium sulfate, its synthesis and properties|language=ru|issn=0044-460X}}</ref>混合价态硫酸盐In<sup>I</sup>In<sup>III</sup>(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>也能通过加热铟和硫酸铟(III)得到。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Downs|first1=A. J.|title=Chemistry of Aluminium, Gallium, Indium, and Thallium|date=1993-05-31|publisher=Springer|page=211|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=v-04Kn758yIC&pg=PA211}}</ref>

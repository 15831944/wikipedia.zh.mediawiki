'''半待擊狀態'''（Half-Cock）是指槍械的擊鎚處於未完全拉低的狀態。許多槍械，尤其是較舊的型號，在擊鎚裡面都會有一個缺口容許用戶實現半待擊狀態，當擊鎚被鎖定在這個位置的時候槍枝會無法開火，也不會令安裝於擊鎚裡面的[[擊針|擊針]]點燃子彈的[[底火|底火]]。半待擊狀態的作用在於令用戶能夠安全的裝填子彈，並作為一種槍械安全機制<ref>{{cite web|title=Glossary|url=http://www.saami.org/Glossary/display.cfm?letter=H|publisher=[[SAAMI|SAAMI]]|accessdate=11 June 2016|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160609172306/http://www.saami.org/glossary/display.cfm?letter=H|archivedate=2016年6月9日}}</ref><ref name="Voth">{{cite web|last1=Voth|first1=Al|title=Dropping the Hammer|url=http://www.biggamehunt.net/articles/dropping-hammer|website=BigGameHunt.net|publisher=Carbon Media Group|accessdate=11 June 2016|date=11 January 2010|quote=Any discussion about hammer guns and in what condition they are safe to carry invariably turns to the half-cock or safety notch.}}</ref>。

== 早期例子 ==
[[燧發槍|燧發槍]]在擊發前需要把擊鎚處於半待擊狀態，以令其能夠點燃[[火藥池|火藥池]]。一些早期的[[轉輪手槍|轉輪手槍]]，（如：[[柯爾特1851海軍型轉輪手槍|柯爾特1851海軍型]]和[[柯爾特單動式陸軍轉輪手槍|柯爾特單動式陸軍]]等）都需在裝填子彈前把擊鎚處於半待擊狀態，目的是防止[[弹巢|弹巢]]轉動<ref name="Hacker">{{cite news|last1=Hacker|first1=Rick|title=Single-Action Secrets|url=http://www.handgunsmag.com/reviews/featured_handguns_single_091907/|accessdate=11 June 2016|work=Handguns|publisher=[[InterMedia_Outdoor_Holdings|Outdoor Sportsman Group]]|date=24 September 2010}}</ref>。一般在使用這一類槍械時通常都不會把6發子彈全數裝填，而是裝填5發子彈以留下一個空膛室，然後把擊鎚處於半待擊狀態，這樣做便能夠防止意外走火。<ref name="Campbell">{{cite news|last1=Campbell|first1=Dave|title=How to Load a Single-Action Revolver|url=https://www.americanrifleman.org/articles/2011/4/22/how-to-load-a-single-action-revolver/|accessdate=12 June 2016|work=[[American_Rifleman|American Rifleman]]|date=22 April 2011|quote=This technique—load one, skip one, load the remaining chambers and come to full cock, then lower the hammer on an empty chamber—will work for any single-action revolver, regardless if it is a five-, six- or eight- or nine-shot cylinder.}}</ref>儘管如此，一些用戶仍然會選擇把6發子彈全數裝填，尤其是在危機四伏和無法預測的環境下。此時半待擊狀態便成為一種不可或缺的安全機制。

同樣的，一些早期型[[手動槍機|槓桿式]][[步槍|步槍]]（如：溫徹斯特1894型）也設有一個半待擊位置以作為一種安全機制，這樣可防止在膛室裝有一發子彈的時候發生走火。這些步槍的半待擊狀態曾經被不少獵人使用，不過現在已很少使用。<ref name="Missouri">{{cite web|title=Half-Cock (Hammer) Safety|url=https://www.hunter-ed.com/missouri/studyGuide/Half-Cock-Hammer-Safety/20102501_700079103/|website=Missouri Hunter Ed Course|publisher=Kalkomey Enterprises|accessdate=12 June 2016}}</ref>這類槍械的半待擊狀態不能在裝填子彈時發揮作用。<ref name="ShootingUK">{{cite news|title=Reviving the trend for hammerguns|url=http://www.shootinguk.co.uk/features/reviving-the-trend-for-hammerguns-7921|accessdate=11 June 2016|work=[[Shooting_Times|Shooting Times]]|publisher=[[Time_Inc._UK|Time Inc. UK]]|date=28 June 2007|quote=The lock of a hammergun has three positions: cocked, half-cocked and fired or hammers down.}}</ref>

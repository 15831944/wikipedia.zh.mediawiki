'''半开放性开局'''({{lang|en|Semi-Open Game}})是国际象棋中一种开局，又名“'''半封闭性开局'''”、“'''单王前兵开局'''”，即白方走出 1.e4 后，黑方不走 1. ... e5 的开局。

==著名防禦法==
<!--The most popular Black defense to 1.e4 is the [[Sicilian_Defence|Sicilian]], but the [[French_Defence|French]] and the [[Caro-Kann_Defense|Caro-Kann]] are also very popular. The [[Pirc_Defense|Pirc]] and the [[Modern_Defense|Modern]] are also commonly seen, while the [[Alekhine's_Defence|Alekhine]] and the [[Scandinavian_Defense|Scandinavian]] have made occasional appearances in [[World_Chess_Championship|World Chess Championship]] games. The [[Nimzowitsch_Defense|Nimzowitsch]] is playable but rare, as is [[Owen's_Defense|Owen's Defense]].  The [[Borg_Defence|Borg Defense]] and the [[St._George_Defence|St. George Defense]] are oddities, although [[Tony_Miles|Tony Miles]] once used St. George's Defense to defeat then World Champion [[Anatoly_Karpov|Anatoly Karpov]].

The Sicilian and French Defenses lead to unbalanced positions that can offer exciting play with both sides having chances to win. The Caro-Kann Defense is solid as Black intends to use his c-pawn to support his center (1.e4 c6 2.d4 d5). Alekhine's, the Pirc and the Modern are [[hypermodernism_(chess)|hypermodern]] openings in which Black tempts White to build a large center with the goal of attacking it with pieces.-->

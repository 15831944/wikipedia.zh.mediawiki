'''碘化钯'''是一种无机化合物，化学式为{{化學式|鈀|碘|2}}。碘化钯曾用于钯的沉淀法定量分析。它和[[氯化钯|氯化钯]]或[[溴化钯|溴化钯]]不同，仅微溶于过量的[[碘化物|碘化物]]中。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1021/ac50128a015 | title = Determination of Palladium by Means of Potassium Iodide | year = 1938 | last1 = Beamish | first1 = F. E. | last2 = Dale | first2 = J. | journal = Industrial & Engineering Chemistry Analytical Edition | volume = 10 | issue = 12 | pages = 697}}</ref>它可由[[四氯合钯(II)酸钾|四氯合钯(II)酸钾]]和[[碘化钾|碘化钾]]反应得到：
: K<sub>2</sub>PdCl<sub>4</sub> + 2 KI → PdI<sub>2</sub>↓ + 4 KCl

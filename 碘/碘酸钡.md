'''碘酸钡'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Ba(IO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub>，它是一种白色固体。

== 制备==
碘酸钡可以[[碘|碘]]和[[氢氧化钡|氢氧化钡]]的反应得到，也能通过[[氯酸钡|氯酸钡]]与[[碘酸钾|碘酸钾]]的作用产生。<ref>{{cite book |last=Mellor |first= |date=1973 |title=Melorova moderna neorganska hemija |url= |location=Belgrade |publisher=[[Naučna_knjiga|Naučna knjiga]] |isbn= |accessdate= }}</ref>

== 性质 ==
碘酸钡在{{convert|580|C|K}}以下是稳定的，在此之上会发生Rammelsberg反应：<ref>{{cite book |last=H. Stern |first=Kurt |date=2000  |title=High temperature properties and thermal decomposition of inorganic salts |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2BpMo7HpXzIC&pg=PA241&lpg=PA241&dq=Ba(IO)3&source=bl&ots=Ab3a__mK82&sig=pVKEm4VqV-jQik1aDGIBlu3sy8Y&hl=en&ei=4VuRSu2EJ4Ge_gayq42vAg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=4#v=onepage&q=Ba(IO)3&f=false |location= |publisher=Taylor & Francis |isbn=0849302560 |accessdate= 11-1-2013}}</ref>
:Ba(IO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub> → Ba<sub>5</sub>(IO<sub>6</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + 9 O<sub>2</sub> + 4 I<sub>2</sub>

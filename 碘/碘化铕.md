'''碘化铕'''是一种[[铕|铕]]的[[碘化物|碘化物]]，化学式为EuI<sub>3</sub>。它可以由[[氧化铕|氧化铕]]和[[氢碘酸|氢碘酸]]反应，并从溶液中析出EuI<sub>3</sub>·9H<sub>2</sub>O。<ref>Emel'yanov, V. I.; Kuznetsova, L. I.; Abramova, L. V.; Ezhov, A. I. Systems Eu2O3-​HI-​H2O and EuI3-​HI-​H2O at 25°C{{ru}}. ''Zhurnal Neorganicheskoi Khimii'', 1997. 42(8): 1394-1396</ref>试图将九水合物用碘化铵处理，只会得到[[碘化亚铕|碘化亚铕]]（EuI<sub>2</sub>）。<ref>Jenden, Charles M.; Lyle, Samuel J. A Moessbauer spectroscopic study of the iodides of europium. ''Journal of the Chemical Society, Dalton Transactions: Inorganic Chemistry (1972-1999)'', 1982. 12: 2409-2414.</ref>碘和铕的反应生成EuI<sub>2</sub>和少量的EuI<sub>3</sub>，EuI<sub>3</sub>在580°C生成的同时发生分解。<ref>Molodkin, A. K.; Tupolev, V. S.; Karagodina, A. M.; Dudareva, A. G.; Krokhina, A. G. Thermal study of the iodination of europium{{ru}}. ''Zhurnal Neorganicheskoi Khimii'', 1983. 28 (11): 2977-2979</ref>

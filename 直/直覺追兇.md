* [[亞倫·甘明|亞倫·甘明]]飾演 as Dr. Dylan Reinhart, an author, psychology professor, and former CIA paramilitary officer, who joins the NYPD to solve bizarre cases.
* {{tsl|en|Bojana Novakovic||Bojana Novakovic}} as Elizabeth "Lizzie" Needham, an [[纽约市警察局|NYPD]] detective and Dylan's partner. Her partner and fiancé was killed in the line of duty a year prior, and she has refused to have a partner since.

* {{tsl|en|Naveen Andrews||Naveen Andrews}} as Julian Cousins, a contact of Dylan's from his days at the CIA.
* [[莎朗·莉爾|莎朗·莉爾]] as Jasmine Gooden, Lizzie's lieutenant and friend.

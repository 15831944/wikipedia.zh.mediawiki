'''直布羅陀大學'''（{{lang-en|University of Gibraltar}}）是位於[[直布羅陀|直布羅陀]]的一所大學，於2015年9月正式開辦，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.euroweeklynews.com/3.0.15/news/on-euro-weekly-news/gibraltar-news/130113-the-university-of-gibraltar-to-open-in-september|title=The University of Gibraltar to open in September|author=Steve Walsh|work=euroweeklynews.com}}</ref>這是境內第一所大學，<ref>http://www.panorama.gi/localnews/headlines.php?action=view_article&article=11997&offset=0</ref>其建築成本大約一千萬[[英鎊|英鎊]]，開設包括商業、旅遊與酒店、健康研究與體育科學等在內的四個專業。
<ref>原文網址：https://read01.com/NkOONn.html。{{cite web|url=http://www.surinenglish.com/20141016/news/gibraltar/gibraltar-million-university-plans-201410161748.html|title=Gibraltar's £10 million university plans are formally launched|work=surinenglish.com}}</ref>

直布羅陀大學主要以{{link-en|塞舌爾大學|University of Seychelles}}作為藍本，在2014年塞舌爾大學更與直布羅陀大學展開了一項教育方面的合作，使兩校學生增加了互相交流的機會。<ref>[http://www.seychellesnewsagency.com/articles/718/University+of+Seychelles+seals+partnership+with+Gibraltar+for+exchanges University of Seychelles seals partnership with Gibraltar for exchanges], Seychelles News Agency, 13 June 2014, retrieved 7 September 2014</ref>

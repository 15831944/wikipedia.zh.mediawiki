'''法蘭西斯科·米格爾·林多爾'''（{{lang-en|Francisco Miguel Lindor}}, {{bd|1993年|11月14日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟[[克里夫蘭|克里夫蘭]][[印地安人|印地安人]]的[[游擊手|游擊手]]。身高180公分，體重86公斤。

2016年，他入選明星賽，還拿下金手套獎。成為史上首位拿下金手套的[[波多黎各|波多黎各]]籍游擊手。

[[2011年美國職棒大聯盟選秀|2011年美國職棒大聯盟選秀]]，[[克里夫蘭印地安人隊|克里夫蘭印地安人隊]]在第1輪第8順位選進了林多爾。2013年，他被評為印地安人隊的頭號新秀。
==職業生涯==
===克里夫蘭印地安人===
====2015年====
6月14日，林多爾登上大聯盟。<ref>{{cite web|title=Indians Promote INF Lindor From Columbus; Place Swisher on 15-day D.L.|url=http://indians.mlb.com/news/article/130548290/indians-promote-inf-lindor-from-columbus-place-swisher-on-15-day-dl|accessdate=June 14, 2015}}</ref>他擔任代打並擊出大聯盟首安。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sports.yahoo.com/blogs/mlb-big-league-stew/francisco-lindor-shows-sense-of-humor-after-first-major-league-hit-005731941.html|title=Francisco Lindor shows sense of humor after first major-league hit|date=June 15, 2015|work=Yahoo Sports|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.si.com/mlb/2015/06/14/cleveland-indians-francisco-lindor-first-hit-video|title=Video: Cleveland Indians' Francisco Lindor first career hit - MLB - SI.com|author=Leon Halip/Getty Images|work=SI.com|accessdate=July 1, 2015}}</ref>他在9月打擊率為3成62，並獲選美聯單月最佳新秀。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.com/news/article/153383578/francisco-lindor-named-the-american-league-rookie-of-the-month-for-september|title=Francisco Lindor Named the American League Rookie of the month for September|publisher=|accessdate=June 5, 2016}}</ref>該季他打擊率3成13，12轟51分打點。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cleveland.com/tribe/index.ssf/2015/10/jose_ramirez_and_the_outdated.html|title=Francisco Lindor's value, Trevor Bauer's drone and Carlos Santana's numbers: Zack Meisel's musings|publisher=|accessdate=June 5, 2016}}</ref>他在美聯年度最佳新秀票選中排行第二，僅次於[[卡洛斯·柯瑞亞|卡洛斯·柯瑞亞]]。<ref>{{cite web |last=Miller |first=Doug |url=http://mlb.com/news/article/157430944/bbwaa-rookies-of-the-year-unveiled |title=Bryant cruises to NL ROY; Correa wins in AL |publisher=MLB.com |date=November 16, 2015 |accessdate=November 16, 2015}}</ref>
====2016年：世界大賽====
該季他打擊率為3成01，15轟78分打點，19次盜壘成功。<ref>[https://www.fangraphs.com/leaders.aspx?pos=all&stats=bat&lg=all&qual=y&type=0&season=2016&month=0&season1=2016&ind=0&team=0&rost=0&age=0&filter=%20&players=0&sort=17,d Major League Leaderboards » 2016 » Batters » Standard Statistics | FanGraphs Baseball<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.com/player/596019/francisco-lindor|title=Francisco Lindor's Stats |publisher=|accessdate=November 5, 2016}}</ref>他擊敗了Jose Iglesias和Andrelton Simmons拿下金手套獎。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.com/news/article/207322712/gold-glove-award-finalists/|title=Gold Glove Award finalists revealed|publisher=|accessdate=November 5, 2016}}</ref>該年印地安人闖入季後賽，林多爾擊出16支安打，他在世界大賽擊出6支安打。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cleveland19.com/story/33517089/francisco-lindor-just-set-an-mlb-record|title=Francisco Lindor just set an MLB record|publisher=|accessdate=November 5, 2016}}</ref>該年他還拿下白金金手套獎，<ref>https://pbs.twimg.com/media/C2pQvzbWIAIgzPe?format=jpg&name=large</ref><ref name=gg>{{cite news|url=http://mlb.com/news/article/208399730/gold-glove-winners-announced/|title=Gold Glove winners unveiled on ESPN|date=November 8, 2016|accessdate=November 8, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.rawlings.com/category-landing-pages/gold-glove-platinum-page.html|title=Rawlings Platinum Glove Award™ Fan Vote|date=November 13, 2016|accessdate=November 13, 2016}}</ref>在美聯[[MVP|MVP]]票選中排名第9。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/awards/awards_2016.shtml#all_AL_MVP_voting |title=2016 awards voting |work=Baseball-Reference.com |accessdate=March 11, 2018}}</ref>
====2017年====
4月5日，他擊出生涯首支滿貫砲。<ref>{{Cite newspaper |last=Hoynes |first=Paul |url=http://www.cleveland.com/tribe/index.ssf/2017/04/cleveland_indians_texas_ranger_45.html |title=Francisco Lindor atones for error with game-winning slam as Cleveland Indians sweep Texas with 9-6 win |work=Cleveland Plain Dealer |date=April 5, 2017 |accessdate=April 6, 2017 |language=en-US}}</ref>7月22日，他擊出生涯首支再見轟。<ref>{{cite newspaper |last=Lewis |first=Ryan |url=https://www.ohio.com/akron/writers/ryan-lewis/francisco-lindor-hits-walk-off-home-run-indians-top-blue-jays-2-1-in-10-innings |title=Francisco Lindor hits walk-off home run, Indians top Blue Jays 2−1 in 10 innings |work=[[Akron_Beacon_Journal|Akron Beacon Journal]] |date=July 22, 2017 |accessdate=October 15, 2017}}</ref>

[[2017年美國聯盟分區賽|2017年美國聯盟分區賽]]，林多爾在第二場面對洋基隊擊出滿貫砲，成為史上第3位達成此成就的游擊手。<ref>{{Cite news |last1=Bastian |first1=Jordan |last2=Hoch |first2=Bryan |url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/20171006257663558/yan-gomes-hit-in-13th-sends-tribe-past-yanks/?game_pk=526478 |title=Lucky 13th: Gomes' hit delivers walk-off win |work=MLB.com |date=October 6, 2017 |access-date=October 6, 2017 |language=en-US}}</ref>

該年他打擊率為2成73，33轟89分打點。<ref>{{cite news |author=Baseball America Press Release |url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/majors/afterthought-foundation-winner/#cGRtl3TBYItVZXDT.97 |title=From afterthought to foundation of a winner |work=Baseball America |date=October 5, 2017 |accessdate=October 28, 2017}}</ref>他拿下首座銀棒獎，<ref name=usa110917>{{cite newspaper |author=USA Today Sports |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/mlb/2017/11/09/silver-slugger-awards-mlb/849087001/ |title=Jose Altuve, Nolan Arenado among repeat Silver Slugger Award winners |work=USA Today |date=November 9, 2017 |accessdate=November 10, 2017}}</ref>在美聯MVP票選排名第5。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/awards/awards_2017.shtml#all_AL_MVP_voting |title=2017 awards voting |work=Baseball-Reference.com |accessdate=March 11, 2018}}</ref>
====2018年====
5月初，林多爾連續2週拿下美聯單週最佳球員。5月12日和6月1日，他單場雙響砲且擊出2支二壘安打，成為史上第4位達成2次單場雙響和單場2支二壘打的球員。<ref>{{cite news |last=Axisa |first=Mike |url=https://www.cbssports.com/mlb/news/francisco-lindors-historic-game-saves-indians-from-an-embarrassing-loss/ |title=Francisco Lindor's historic game saves Indians from an embarrassing loss: The Indians blew an 8-0 lead before Lindor came to a rescue |work=CBS Sports |date=June 1, 2018 |accessdate=June 2, 2018}}</ref>

7月10日，林多爾在明星賽前達成80得分。打破1996年Edgar Martinez締造的79分，成為波多黎各籍選手中，在明星賽前得分最多的球員。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.primerahora.com/deportes/beisbol/nota/lindorrompemarcadecarrerasengrandesligas-1292000/|title=Lindor rompe marca de carreras en Grandes Ligas|publisher=}}</ref>他連續3年入選明星賽。該年他打擊率為2成77。<ref>[https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/AL/2018-batting-leaders.shtml 2018 American League Batting Leaders | Baseball-Reference.com<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>

他在美聯分區賽打擊率為3成64，不過印地安人最終遭到[[太空人隊|太空人隊]]橫掃出局。
==國際賽==
[[2017年世界棒球經典賽|2017年世界棒球經典賽]]，林多爾代表波多黎各隊出賽。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/215455022/puerto-ricos-world-baseball-classic-roster/|title=Young stars join Beltran, Yadi for Puerto Rico|first=Chad|last=Thornburg|work=MLB.com|date=February 8, 2017|accessdate=March 14, 2017}}</ref>他在D組獲選為MVP。他在第一輪比賽中，打擊率4成55，5安2轟。<ref>{{cite newspaper |last=Fuentes |first=Christian |url=https://www.metro.pr/pr/noticias/2017/03/14/francisco-lindor-mvp-grupo-d.html |title=Francisco Lindor es el MVP del Grupo D: El boricua recibió el honor de parte del Clásico Mundial |work=[[Metro_International|Metro]] |date=March 14, 2017 |accessdate=January 21, 2018 |language=es}}</ref>波多黎各隊一路闖到決賽，不過最終輸給美國隊，拿下亞軍。不過林多爾成功獲選為經典賽明星隊成員。<ref>{{cite news |last=Perry |first=Dayn |url=http://www.cbssports.com/mlb/news/world-baseball-classic-previous-champs-results-medal-count-mvps-all-wbc-teams/ |title=World Baseball Classic: Previous champs, results, medal count, MVPs, All-WBC teams |work=CBS Sports |date=March 23, 2017 |accessdate=January 21, 2018}}</ref>

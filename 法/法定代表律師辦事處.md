'''法定代表律師辦事處'''是根據《法定代表律師條例》建立，獨立於[[香港|香港]][[法律援助署|法律援助署]]轄下的其他單位，<ref name="HallLibraries1996">{{cite book|author1=GK Hall|author2=New York Public Library. Research Libraries|author3=New York Public Library Staff|coauthors=Thomson Gale|title=Bibliographic Guide to Government Publications: foreign|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=eRnhAAAAMAAJ|accessdate=2 February 2013|year=1996|publisher=G. K. Hall.}}</ref>負責代表未成年（未滿18歲）及無行為能力者的有關利益。<ref name="Hong Kong">{{cite book|title=Hong Kong|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Zy0fAQAAMAAJ|accessdate=2 February 2013|year=2007|publisher=Government Press}}</ref>法定代表律師由[[法律援助署|法律援助署署長]][[鄺寶昌|鄺寶昌]]太平紳士兼任，助理法定代表律師為[[陳妙娟|陳妙娟]]。

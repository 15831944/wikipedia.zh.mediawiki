'''法式城堡'''（{{lang-fr|Château}} {{IPA-fr|ʃɑto}}）是法國[[莊園|莊園]]領主的宅邸，或是[[绅士|绅士]]、[[貴族|貴族]]的住所，不一定擁有防禦設施，并非真正意义上的城堡<ref name="Dictionary.com">{{cite web | url=http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/chateau | title=Dictionary.com | accessdate=13 April 2014}}</ref>。許多法式城堡都位在[[卢瓦尔河流域|卢瓦尔河流域]]，例如[[昂布瓦斯城堡|昂布瓦斯城堡]]、[[香波尔城堡|香波尔城堡]]和[[舍农索城堡|舍农索城堡]]等等，形成了[[盧瓦爾河谷城堡群|盧瓦爾河谷城堡群]]。

==意义==
如同英国的[[英國鄉村別墅|stately home]]一样，Château是一种家族权力的象征。其所有者一般是皇室、贵族，但一些上层士绅也可以建造Château。因此质量也千差万别<ref name="Giedion1982">{{cite book |last=Giedion |first=Sigfried |title=Space, Time and Architecture: The Growth of a New Tradition |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ZHZnmKxkGMwC&pg=PA134 |year=1982 |publisher=Harvard University Press |isbn=978-0-674-83040-0 |page=134}}</ref><ref name="Anderson2007">{{cite book |last=Anderson |first=James Maxwell |title=Daily Life During the French Revolution |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=XAjhcHjfUisC&pg=PA2 |date=1 January 2007 |publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group |isbn=978-0-313-33683-6 |page=2}}</ref><ref name="Richardson2008">{{cite book |last=Richardson |first=Glenn |title=The Contending Kingdoms: France and England, 1420-1700 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kFSqKelemSMC&pg=PA64 |date=1 January 2008 |publisher=Ashgate Publishing, Ltd. |isbn=978-0-7546-5789-7 |page=64}}</ref>。其前身是古罗马时代的贵族别墅。每个Château都有着自己的领地（terres）。<ref name="JordiSommer2007">{{cite book |last1=Jordi |first1=Nathalie |last2=Sommer |first2=Lauren |last3=Sussman |first3=Anna |title=MTV France |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=g_qzLLQvdcgC&pg=PA146 |date=5 June 2007 |publisher=John Wiley & Sons |isbn=978-0-7645-8770-2 |page=146}}</ref>
[[Image:Chateaufrontenac-quebec-canada-rs.jpg|thumb]]的酒店[[芳堤娜城堡|芳堤娜城堡]]（Château Frontenac）]]
[[卢浮宫|卢浮宫]]（设防）和[[卢森堡宫|卢森堡宫]]原本是Château，但后来在慢慢被城市包围，人们也渐渐将它当做“宫”而不是Château来看待。Château的本意在法国以外的地方也产生了变化，如加拿大人通常用它来指加拿大铁路发展黄金时期建造的一大批五星级城堡式酒店。<ref name="HempsteadCuthbert2013">{{cite book |last1=Hempstead |first1=Andrew |last2=Cuthbert |first2=Pamela |last3=Aykroyd |first3=Lucas |title=Frommer's Far & Wide: A Weekly Guide to Canada's Best Travel Experiences |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=GA9Ong8K2bkC&pg=PT149 |date=12 November 2013 |publisher=John Wiley & Sons |isbn=978-1-118-10169-8 |page=149}}</ref>

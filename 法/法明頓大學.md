== 學校概述 ==
[[File:University_of_Farmington_logo2.png|thumb]]
法明頓大學的總部位於[[密西根州|密西根州]][[法明頓山_(密西根州)|法明頓山]]，在西北公路（{{lang|en|Northwestern Highway}}）往北過因克斯特路（{{lang|en|Inkster Road}}）後一個辦公大樓的地下室。本校網站宣稱「提供學生世界上獨特的教育經驗」，並列出學術課程與相關細節。網站上並宣稱本校由{{link-en|密西根州許可和監管事務部|Michigan Department of Licensing and Regulatory Affairs}}與{{link-en|美國職業學校和學院認證委員會|Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges}}（ACCSC）認證，並顯然得到{{link-en|外國學生和訪問學者計劃|Student and Exchange Visitor Program}}授權以招收外籍學生<ref name = "Antonia" /><ref name = "UFFactoids">{{cite web | title = Factoids | url = https://universityoffarmington.edu/factoids | publisher = The University of Farmington | dead-url = yes | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20190131064227/https://universityoffarmington.edu/factoids | archive-date = 2019-01-31}}</ref>。[[印度時報|印度時報]]表示想申請此學校的學生無法從學校網站分辨學校真偽<ref name = "TimesIndia190204">{{cite news | title = US fake university racket: Students had no way to check Farmington's authenticity | publisher = [[印度時報|The Times of India]] | url = https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/articleshow/67823186.cms | date = 2019-02-04 | accessdate = 2019-02-13 | language = en}}</ref>。

本校沒有教師或實際課程<ref name="NYT_31Jan" />，每年-{zh-hant:大學部;zh-hans:本科}-學費為8,500美元，明顯比其他美國大學低<ref name="thehindu" /><ref name = "PTInews">{{Cite news | title = Indians studying in a fraudulent university arrested | agency = [[印度報業托拉斯|Press Trust of India]] | work = Business Standard India | url = https://www.business-standard.com/article/pti-stories/119013100096_1.html | date = 2019-01-31 | accessdate = 2019-02-13 | language = en}}</ref>。

== 反應 ==
在同大樓上班的麥特·弗里德曼（{{lang|en|Matt Friedman}}）表示，法明頓大學沒有教室，他也沒看過有人進出大學的總部<ref name = "Detroit" />。其他在同大樓上班的人懷疑檢察官「學生蓄意騙取簽證」的說法，並堅稱他們曾看到背著背包的學生詢問有關此大學的資訊，並焦急地尋找學校的行程或管理人員<ref name = "TimesIndia190204" />。

執業於[[亞特蘭大|亞特蘭大]]的移民律師拉維·孟南（{{lang|en|Ravi Mannam}}）批評這次執法行動有誤導性，並譴責政府用非常有問題且令人不安的方法使外國學生進入此大學，因為有些學生懷著將在合法學校修習的憧憬<ref name = "NYT_31Jan" /><ref name = "BBCNews" />。其他印度媒體亦有類似的掛念<ref name = "Firstpost" /><ref name="NewIndianExp190202">{{cite news | first = Sadaf | last = Aman | title = 'Students were handcuffed like criminals & taken away' | publisher = The New Indian Express | url = http://www.newindianexpress.com/states/telangana/2019/feb/02/students-were-handcuffed-like-criminals--taken-away-1933116.html | date = 2019-02-02 | accessdate = 2019-02-13 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20190208120740/http://www.newindianexpress.com/states/telangana/2019/feb/02/students-were-handcuffed-like-criminals--taken-away-1933116.html | archive-date = 2019-02-08 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref name = "TimesIndia190203">{{cite news | first = Sudipta | last = Sengupta | title = Indian body fumes at DHS 'bait & catch' move against students | publisher = The Times of India | url = https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/articleshow/67812346.cms | date = 2019-02-03 | accessdate = 2019-02-13 | language = en}}</ref>。

印度政府發出[[外交照會|外交照會]]，要求立即釋放被逮捕的學生，且不要將他們驅逐出境<ref name = "Hindustan190203">{{cite news | title = India issues demarche to US on detention of students: Officials from the Indian embassy and consulates in the US visited several detention centres and about 30 students have so far been contacted by consular officers | work = Hindustan Times | location = [[新德里|New Delhi]] | url = https://www.hindustantimes.com/india-news/story-1ql6ruNRSJpV9BhZYPxnFM.html| date = 2019-02-03 | accessdate = 2019-02-13 | language = en}}</ref>。在印度領事館的介入下部份學生被釋放，領事館並為此事件設立[[熱線|救助電話]]<ref name="NDTV190202">{{cite news | title = Indian Embassy Opens Hotline For 129 Students Detained In US Visa Scam | publisher = NDTV | url = https://www.ndtv.com/indians-abroad/indian-embassy-opens-hotline-for-129-students-detained-in-us-visa-scam-1987146 | date = 2019-02-02 | accessdate = 2019-02-13 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20190208024719/https://www.ndtv.com/indians-abroad/indian-embassy-opens-hotline-for-129-students-detained-in-us-visa-scam-1987146 | archive-date = 2019-02-08 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

'''法蒂瑪·薩納·謝赫'''（{{lang-hi|फ़ातिमा सना शेख}}，{{Lang-en|Fatima Sana Shaikh}}，{{bd|1992年|1月11日|catIdx=S}}）<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/pics-dangal-girl-fatima-sana-shaikh-brings-in-her-birthday-with-bestie-sanya-malhotra/articleshow/56469634.cms “Pics: 'Dangal' girl Fatima Sana Shaikh brings in her birthday with bestie Sanya Malhotra”]. Times of India. 29-01-2017.</ref>是[[印度|印度]]女演員，主要出現在[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]電影。

她至今最為人所知的角色是在電影《[[我和我的冠軍女兒|我和我的冠軍女兒]]》 （Dangal）中扮演[[阿米爾·罕|阿米爾·罕]]的角力選手女兒。該電影在印度及海外（尤其是中國）均大受歡迎。<ref>[https://tribune.com.pk/story/1405343/dangal-back-square-one-fatima-sana-shaikh/ "After 'Dangal', I was back to square one: Fatima Sana Shaikh"], The Express Tribune. 09-05-2017.</ref>

==著名事件==
===採訪埃及總統===
2012年6月，法斯通訊社發布一則{{link-en|埃及總統|President of Egypt}}[[穆罕默德·穆尔西|穆罕默德·穆尔西]]的專訪，其中指穆爾西告訴法斯社他希望重建埃及與伊朗的關係，且意欲重新審視{{tsl|en|Egypt–Israel Peace Treaty|埃及-以色列和約|與以色列的和約}}<ref name=":0">{{cite news |last=Paraszczuk |first=Joanna |date=2012-06-25 |url=http://www.jpost.com/MiddleEast/Article.aspx?id=275186 |title=Morsy Denies Iranian Interview on Peace 'Review' |newspaper=[[The_Jerusalem_Post|The Jerusalem Post]] |accessdate=2013-08-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120923193909/http://www.jpost.com/MiddleEast/Article.aspx?id=275186# |archive-date=2012-09-23 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。隨後穆爾西駁斥了這項說法<ref name=":0"/>，法斯社則公佈了一段據稱是專訪的錄音，對此[[阿拉伯衛星電視台|阿拉伯衛星電視台]]引述不具名專家的評論，表示此錄音並非穆爾西的聲音<ref name=":0"/>。

===轉載洋蔥報的虛構新聞===
2012年9月，法斯通訊社轉載了美國小報[[洋蔥報|洋蔥報]]關於一項「民意調查」的新聞，該報導指絕大多數美國鄉村的白人寧願在接下來的總統大選中投票給時任[[伊朗總統|伊朗總統]]的[[马哈茂德·艾哈迈迪内贾德|马哈茂德·艾哈迈迪内贾德]]，也不願投給[[美國總統|美國總統]][[巴拉克·歐巴馬|巴拉克·歐巴馬]]<ref name="Martinez">{{cite news|last=Martinez |first=Michael |date=2013-09-29 |url=http://articles.cnn.com/2012-09-28/world/world_iran-news-agency-duped_1_onion-story-fars-news-agency-gallup-poll |title=Iran's News Agency Portrays Satirical Onion Story as Its Own |work=[[CNN|CNN]] |accessdate=2013-08-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121004193407/http://articles.cnn.com/2012-09-28/world/world_iran-news-agency-duped_1_onion-story-fars-news-agency-gallup-poll |archivedate=2012-10-04 }}</ref><ref name="McGreal">{{cite news|authorlink=Chris McGreal|last=McGreal|first=Chris|date=2012-09-28|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/us-news-blog/2012/sep/28/iran-fars-reports-onion-article-ahmadinejad?newsfeed=true|title=Iranian News Agency Fars Reports Onion Article on Ahmadinejad as Actual News –  Satirical Newspaper's Claim That Americans Would Rather Hang Out with Ahmadinejad over Obama Taken Seriously in Tehran|newspaper=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|accessdate=2013-08-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180924033322/https://www.theguardian.com/world/us-news-blog/2012/sep/28/iran-fars-reports-onion-article-ahmadinejad?newsfeed=true#|archive-date=2018-09-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>。法斯社接近一字不漏地轉載原報導，包括原報導「引述」一位虛構[[西維吉尼亞州|西維吉尼亞州]]居民的評論，指他寧願與艾哈迈迪内贾德去看棒球，因為後者「認真地看待國防，且不會如歐巴馬一般，讓一群[[同性戀|同性戀]]的抗議者指導他如何治理國家」<ref name="Martinez" /><ref name="McGreal" />。

法斯通訊社後來承認了此錯誤，但仍聲稱多數美國人應該都喜歡美國政治體制以外的人，勝過歐巴馬等檯面上的政治人物<ref>{{cite news|author=Philip Caulfield|date=2012-10-01|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/news/world/iran-news-agency-apologizes-running-onion-article-article-1.1172205|title=Iran Fars news agency apologizes for running Onion article saying Americans prefer Ahmadinejad to Obama|newspaper=Daily News|location=New York|access-date=2018-11-13|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180924033318/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/world/iran-news-agency-apologizes-running-onion-article-article-1.1172205#|archive-date=2018-09-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

===時光機報導===
2013年4月，法斯通訊社報導稱一名伊朗科學家成功發明了[[時光機|時光機]]，使人們得以預見未來，數日後該報導即被撤下，取而代之的是一則伊朗官員否認有時光機被登記發明的報導<ref>{{cite news|last=Satherley |first=Dan|url=http://www.3news.co.nz/Iran-denies-having-time-machine/tabid/417/articleID/294743/Default.aspx|work=[[3_News|3 News]]|title= Iran Denies Having Time Machine|date=2013-04-18|accessdate=2013-08-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=Vahdat, Ahmed|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/middleeast/iran/9985757/Iranian-scientist-claims-to-have-invented-time-machine.html|work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]]|title=Iranian Scientist Claims to Have Invented 'Time Machine' –  An Iranian Businessman Claims to Have Mastered Time with a Machine That Allows Users to Fast Forward up to Eight Years into the Future|date=2013-04-10|accessdate=2013-08-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181018121449/https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/middleeast/iran/9985757/Iranian-scientist-claims-to-have-invented-time-machine.html#|archive-date=2018-10-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

'''法拉第波'''是在氣液分界面上，由於流體[[不穩定性|不穩定性]]（法拉第不穩定性）產生的一種[[非線性|非線性]][[駐波|駐波]]，當其振盪頻率超過臨界值時，氣液分界面就會不穩定，可用於液基微小物體聚集器，法拉第波得名自[[麥可·法拉第|麥可·法拉第]]。法拉第最早是在1831年在[[皇家學會|皇家學會]]《[[自然科学会报|自然科学会报]]》的一篇文章中的附錄提到法拉第波<ref>Faraday, M. (1831) "On a peculiar class of acoustical figures; and on certain forms assumed by a group of particles upon vibrating elastic surfaces", ''Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society (London)'', vol. 121, pages 299–318.  "Faraday waves" are discussed in an appendix to the article, [https://books.google.com/books?id=RgOc86j3VTwC&pg=PA335&lpg=PA335&source=bl&ots=2tJKmzwP3j&sig=HZfgsPbHFffKkZdzy_QZu8w3v8s&hl=en&ei=d1UWTPHvB8O78gaR4PSwCg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CBUQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&f=false "On the forms and states assumed by fluids in contact with vibrating elastic surfaces"].  This entire article is also available on-line (albeit without illustrations) at [http://www.ebooksread.com/authors-eng/michael-faraday/experimental-researches-in-chemistry-and-physics-hci/page-31-experimental-researches-in-chemistry-and-physics-hci.shtml "Electronic Library"].</ref><ref>Others who investigated "Faraday waves" include:  '''(1)'''  Ludwig Matthiessen (1868) [https://books.google.com/books?id=jQs4AAAAMAAJ&pg=PA107&lpg=PA107&source=bl&ots=eac7KB3ZIo&sig=VQJ-cz4g5haOkpNugwPNMMURzuM&hl=en&ei=82IWTKeoB8H78Abek6ieDA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CBQQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false "Akustische Versuche, die kleinsten Transversalwellen der Flüssigkeiten betreffend" (Acoustic experiments concerning the smallest transverse waves of liquids)], ''Annalen der Physik'', vol. 134, pages 107–117 ; '''(2)'''  Ludwig Matthiessen (1870) [https://books.google.com/books?id=82_kAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA375&lpg=PA375&source=bl&ots=iuyi1V7XpL&sig=AFlaE433VRg-I60K1xyWx734PS4&hl=en&ei=qF8WTLTqN4L58AbpnuirCg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CBwQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q&f=false "Über die Transversalschwingungen tönender tropfbarer und elastischer Flüssigkeiten" (On the transverse vibrations of ringing low-viscosity and elastic liquids)], ''Annalen der Physik'', vol. 141, pages 375–393 ; '''(3)'''  John William Strutt (Lord Rayleigh) (1883), [https://books.google.com/books?id=GFMbAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA50&lpg=PA50&source=bl&ots=qQRs8BFx9D&sig=wKvFicJrCCL4k-q_0ScjoP7pW-I&hl=en&ei=NmIWTLCdD4P88AaOtOinCg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CBIQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&f=false "On the crispations of fluid resting upon a vibrating support,"] ''Philosophical Magazine'', vol. 16, pages 50–58.</ref>。

若將一層液體放置在垂直振蕩的活塞頂部，則會出現以一半驅動頻率振盪的駐波模式，可以給出某些不穩定性的標準<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Patterning of particulate films using Faraday waves|author=Wright, P.H.; Saylor, J.R. (September 2003)|url=http://www.ces.clemson.edu/~jsaylor/paperPdfs/rsi.v74.n09.pdf|journal=Review of Scientific Instruments. 74 (9): 4063–4070|issue=|doi=|others=|year=|volume=|page=|pmid=}}</ref>。 這涉及[[參數共振|參數共振]]的問題。駐波可能以條紋、緊密堆積的六邊形、甚至正方形或準週期的型式出現。通常在發出鈴鐺聲般的葡萄酒酒杯裡面的葡萄酒表面可以觀察到細條紋的法拉第波。 法拉第波也能解釋了[[磬_(法器)|磬]] 裡的“噴泉”現象。

法拉第波波長可以用來類比量子力學中的[[物質波|德布羅伊波]]波長<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tcm.phy.cam.ac.uk/~mdt26/tti_talks/deBB_10/bush_tti2010.pdf|title=Quantum mechanics writ large|accessdate=|author=John W. M. Bush Department of Mathematics, MIT|date=|publisher=}}</ref>。

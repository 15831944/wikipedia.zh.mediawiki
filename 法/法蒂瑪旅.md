'''法蒂瑪旅'''（{{lang-ar|لواء الفاطميون}}）是[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]中一個在2014年建立的親政府[[民兵|民兵]]組織，由[[伊朗政府|伊朗政府]]招募的[[阿富汗什葉派伊斯蘭教|阿富汗什葉派穆斯林]]組成，協助[[敘利亞政府|敘利亞政府]]對抗反政府武裝。法蒂瑪旅由伊朗[[伊斯兰革命卫队|伊斯兰革命卫队]]訓練及提供資金和裝備，並由伊朗軍官指揮作戰。<ref name="irantrained">{{cite web|url=http://www.washingtoninstitute.org/policy-analysis/view/irans-afghan-shiite-fighters-in-syria|title=Iran's Afghan Shiite Fighters in Syria|publisher=|accessdate=2015-05-09}}</ref>據報法蒂瑪旅在2016年6月的兵力有12,000－14,000人。<ref name="tasnim">{{Cite web |url=https://www.tasnimnews.com/fa/news/1395/03/29/1107833/%D8%AA%D8%B4%DA%A9%DB%8C%D9%84%D8%A7%D8%AA-%D9%81%D8%B9%D9%84%DB%8C-%D9%81%D8%A7%D8%B7%D9%85%DB%8C%D9%88%D9%86-%D8%A7%D8%A8%D8%AA%D8%AF%D8%A7-%DB%8C%DA%A9-%D9%87%DB%8C%D8%A6%D8%AA-%D8%AE%D8%A7%D9%86%DA%AF%DB%8C-%D8%A8%D9%88%D8%AF-%D8%AA%D8%B9%D8%AF%D8%A7%D8%AF%DB%8C-%D8%A7%D8%B2-%D8%A7%D9%81%D8%BA%D8%A7%D9%86-%D9%87%D8%A7-%D8%A7%D8%B2-%D8%A7%D8%B1%D9%88%D9%BE%D8%A7-%D8%A8%D9%87-%D9%81%D8%A7%D8%B7%D9%85%DB%8C%D9%88%D9%86-%D8%A2%D9%85%D8%AF%D9%86%D8%AF-%D9%88-%D8%B4%D9%87%DB%8C%D8%AF-%D8%B4%D8%AF%D9%86%D8%AF |script-title=fa:تشکیلات فعلی فاطمیون ابتدا یک هیئت خانگی بود/ تعدادی از افغان‌ها از اروپا به فاطمیون آمدند و شهید شدند}}</ref>

法蒂瑪旅的成員主要從伊朗的阿富汗難民以及阿富汗的[[哈扎拉族|哈扎拉人]]中招募。<ref name="arrests">{{cite web|url=https://now.mmedia.me/lb/en/NewsReports/567310-afghanistan-arrests-iran-official-for-recruiting-shiite-fighters|title=Afghanistan arrests Iran official for recruiting Shiite fighters|date=2016-08-29|publisher=NOW|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160830172136/https://now.mmedia.me/lb/en/NewsReports/567310-afghanistan-arrests-iran-official-for-recruiting-shiite-fighters|archivedate=2016-08-30}}</ref>應募者獲承諾給予伊朗公民權及500美元月薪。<ref name="irantrained"/><ref name=mmedia>{{cite web|url=https://now.mmedia.me/lb/en/reportsfeatures/564354-afghans-in-syria-ayatollahs-soldiers-serving-assad|title=Afghans in Syria: Ayatollah’s soldiers serving Assad|date=2014-11-05|publisher=|accessdate=2016-05-06|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160624043957/https://now.mmedia.me/lb/en/reportsfeatures/564354-afghans-in-syria-ayatollahs-soldiers-serving-assad|archivedate=2016-06-24}}</ref>他們在伊朗接受兩星期的基本軍事訓練後，以包機送到敘利亞參與戰鬥。<ref>Fariba Sahraei. [http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-36035095 "Syria war: The Afghans sent by Iran to fight for Assad"]. BBC News. 15 April 2016.</ref>

在敘利亞政府收復[[幼发拉底河|幼发拉底河]]以西的大部分失地後，2019年美國之音報導已有數以千計的法蒂瑪旅成員離開敘利亞。阿富汗官員擔心這些有戰鬥經驗的人返回阿富汗後或會繼續聽令於伊朗，威脅阿富汗安全，要求伊朗勿把他們送回阿富汗。<ref name=voa201904>{{Cite news |url=https://www.voanews.com/a/returning-from-syria-iranian-backed-afghan-fighters-could-pose-threat/4867715.html |title=Returning from Syria, Iranian-Backed Afghan Fighters Could Pose Threat |website=VOA |date=2019-04-08}}</ref>

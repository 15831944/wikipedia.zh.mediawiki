'''法罕·阿克塔爾'''（{{lang-en|'''Farhan Akhtar'''}}，{{bd|1974年|1月9日|catIdx=Akhtar, Farhan}}）是一名[[印度|印度]]男演員、製片人和导演。他的父亲Javed Akhtar是印度知名诗人、作曲家和編劇。<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/lucknow-central-actor-farhan-akhtar-on-his-dreams-father-javed-akhtar-and-the-worrying-trend-in-bollywood-diana-penty-4842983/ “Lucknow Central actor Farhan Akhtar on his dreams, father Javed Akhtar and the worrying trend in Bollywood”]. The Indian Express. 14-09-2017.</ref>阿克塔爾最為人所知的电影有《[[人生不再重来|人生不再重来]]》、《Bhaag Milkha Bhaag》、《Dil Dhadakne Do》、《Wazir》等。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/abhay-deol-ready-for-zindagi-sequel-but-zoya-akhtar-has-to-have-a-story/articleshow/63478795.cms “Abhay Deol : Ready for 'Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara' sequel but Zoya Akhtar has to have a story”]. The Times of India. 27-03-2018.</ref><ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/amitabh-bachchan-finds-it-tough-to-work-with-farhan-akhtar/ “Amitabh Bachchan finds it ‘tough’ to work with Farhan Akhtar”].

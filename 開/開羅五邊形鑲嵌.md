在[[幾何學|幾何學]]中，'''開羅五邊形鑲嵌'''是一種[[平面|平面]]鑲嵌，其為半正鑲嵌[[扭稜正方形鑲嵌|扭稜正方形鑲嵌]]的[[對偶多面體|對偶鑲嵌]]<ref>{{MathWorld | urlname=DualTessellation | title=Dual tessellation}}</ref>，密鋪於歐氏平面，其名為「開羅」是因為這種幾何圖形經常在埃及開羅的街道上出現<ref>{{citation|title=Charming proofs: a journey into elegant mathematics|volume=42|series=Dolciani mathematical expositions|first1=Claudi|last1=Alsina|first2=Roger B.|last2=Nelsen|publisher=Mathematical Association of America|year=2010|isbn=978-0-88385-348-1|page=164|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=mIT5-BN_L0oC&pg=PA164}}.</ref><ref>{{citation|title=Transformation Geometry: An Introduction to Symmetry|series=Undergraduate Texts in Mathematics|first=George Edward|last=Martin|publisher=Springer|year=1982|isbn=978-0-387-90636-2|page=119|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=KW4EwONsQJgC&pg=PA119}}.</ref>，是15種已知的等面五邊形鑲嵌之一。

它也被稱為'''麥克馬洪網格'''（MacMahon's net）<ref>{{citation|title=Plane nets in crystal chemistry|first1=M.|last1=O'Keeffe|first2=B. G.|last2=Hyde|journal=Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society of London. Series A, Mathematical and Physical Sciences|volume=295|issue=1417|year=1980|pages=553–618|jstor=36648|doi=10.1098/rsta.1980.0150}}.</ref>，出於珀西亞歷山大麥克馬洪1921年出版的《New Mathematical Pastimes》<ref>{{citation|title=New Mathematical Pastimes|first=Major P. A.|last=Macmahon|publisher=University Press|year=1921}}.</ref>。

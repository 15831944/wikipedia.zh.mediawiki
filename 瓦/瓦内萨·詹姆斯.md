'''瓦内萨·詹姆斯'''（{{lang|en|Vanessa James}}，{{bd|1987年|9月27日||}}）生于加拿大安大略省士嘉堡，是一名法国女子花样滑冰运动员，主攻双人滑<ref name=ISU-1112>{{cite web |url= http://www.isuresults.com/bios/isufs00013733.htm |title= Vanessa JAMES / Morgan CIPRES: 2011/2012 |publisher= International Skating Union |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20120419142806/http://www.isuresults.com/bios/isufs00013733.htm |archivedate= 2012-04-19 |deadurl= unfit |accessdate=2019-02-04 }}</ref>。她童年时居住于[[百慕大|百慕大]]，直至10岁时随父母移居至美国[[弗吉尼亚州|弗吉尼亚州]]<ref name=RG091224>{{cite news |url= http://www.royalgazette.com/article/20091224/SPORT/312249952 |title= Former MSA student set for Winter Games |first= Kyle |last= Hunter |publisher= The Royal Gazette |date= 2009-12-24 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131230010123/http://www.royalgazette.com/article/20091224/SPORT/312249952 |archivedate= 2013-12-30 |deadurl= no }}</ref>。后来她又移居至法国[[巴黎|巴黎]]<ref name=NYT100127>{{cite news |url= https://www.nytimes.com/2010/01/28/sports/olympics/28pairsside.html |title= From Online to the Olympics, Pairs Skaters Click |first= Christopher |last= Clarey |work= The New York Times |date= 2010-01-27 |accessdate=2010-01-27 }}</ref>。2009年12月，她正式获得法国国籍<ref name=AFP091223>{{cite news |url= https://www.google.com/hostednews/afp/article/ALeqM5jcvuQ2c4nMlvcpvP3J7s6HSXZDFA |script-title=fr: Patinage artistique : Vanessa James naturalisée française pour les JO 2010 |work= [[Agence_France-Presse|Agence France-Presse]] |publisher= Google News |date= 2009-12-23 |accessdate=2010-01-27 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20100127055128/https://www.google.com/hostednews/afp/article/ALeqM5jcvuQ2c4nMlvcpvP3J7s6HSXZDFA |archivedate= 2010-01-27 |deadurl= no }}</ref>。瓦内萨的双胞胎姐妹Melyssa同样也从事花样滑冰运动<ref name=RG091224/>。

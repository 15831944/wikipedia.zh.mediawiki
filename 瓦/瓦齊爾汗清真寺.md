'''瓦齊爾汗清真寺'''（{{lang-ur|{{nq|مسجد وزیر خان}}}}）是一座位於[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]][[旁遮普省|旁遮普省]]省會[[拉合爾|拉合爾]]市的17世紀清真寺。清真寺在[[莫卧儿帝国|莫臥兒]]皇帝[[沙贾汉|沙贾汉]]在位期間連同包括{{link-en|夏希土耳其浴場|Shahi Hammam}}等建築一併建造。清真寺在1634年開始動工建造，並在1641年完工。<ref>{{cite news|title=Conservation of the Wazir Khan Mosque Lahore: Preliminary Report on Condition and Risk Assessment|url=http://archnet.org/system/publications/contents/6585/original/DPC3347.pdf?1384798179|accessdate=2016-08-25|agency=Aga Khan Development Network|date=2012|quote=The Wazir Khan Mosque was built in 1634-35 AD (1044-45 AH), by Hakim 'Ali ud din* a governor of the Punjab in the early part of the reign of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160827000354/http://archnet.org/system/publications/contents/6585/original/DPC3347.pdf?1384798179|archive-date=2016-08-27|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

瓦齊爾汗清真寺被認為是莫臥兒風格清真寺中裝飾最豪華的一座<ref name=Masson>{{cite book|last1=Masson|first1=Vadim Mikhaĭlovich|title=History of Civilizations of Central Asia: Development in contrast : from the sixteenth to the mid-nineteenth century|date=2003|publisher=UNESCO|isbn=9789231038761}}</ref>，也著名於其錯綜複雜的{{link-en|釉陶|Faience}}藝術「kashi-kari」（{{lang|ur|{{nq|کاشی کاری}}}}），以及建築內部的精細的莫臥兒時代[[濕壁畫|濕壁畫]]。在{{link-en|阿迦汗文化信託|Aga Khan Trust for Culture}}和{{link-en|旁遮普省政府|Government of Punjab, Pakistan}}的領導<ref>{{cite news|title=Walled city of Lahore conservation|url=http://www.akdn.org/where-we-work/south-asia/pakistan/cultural-development/walled-city-lahore-conservation|accessdate=2016-08-25|quote=The Walled city of Lahore is famous for several historic monuments including the Lahore Fort – a World Heritage site, the Badshahi and Wazir Khan mosques. Close to 2,000 buildings within the Walled city display a range of architectural features that mark Lahore’s centuries old cultural landscape. A majority of these buildings and the mohallas (local neighbourhoods) in which they are situated form a unique heritage footprint. The work consequently carried out by the Aga Khan Trust for Culture (AKTC) and the Aga Khan Historic Cities Programme (AKHCP) was initiated under a 2007 public-private partnership framework agreement with the Government of Punjab.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160826235341/http://www.akdn.org/where-we-work/south-asia/pakistan/cultural-development/walled-city-lahore-conservation|archive-date=2016-08-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>，以及[[德国|德国]]、[[挪威|挪威]]和[[美国|美国]]政府的協助之下<ref>{{cite news|last1=Muzaffar|first1=Zareen|title=The Walled City of Lahore: Protecting Heritage and History|url=http://thediplomat.com/2016/02/the-walled-city-of-lahore-protecting-heritage-and-history/|accessdate=2016-08-25|agency=The Diplomat|date=2016-02-08|quote=The Walled City of Lahore program was put into effect in partnership with the Aga Khan Trust for Culture. AKTC supports the Walled City Authority in all technical matters in terms of restoration and conservation work being carried out. Other donors include the World Bank, Royal Norwegian Government, USAID, and the German Embassy.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160829133126/http://thediplomat.com/2016/02/the-walled-city-of-lahore-protecting-heritage-and-history/|archive-date=2016-08-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>，清真寺在2009年起進行了大規模翻新。

== 地理位置 ==
[[File:Revised_photo_Interior_of_Wazir_Khan_Mosque.jpg|thumb]]
清真寺位於{{link-en|拉合爾城牆|Walled City of Lahore}}南部一條稱為「Shahi Guzargah」的道路的附近，而該道路是以前莫臥兒貴族前往[[拉合尔堡|拉合尔堡]]皇宮的必經之路<ref name="archnet.org">{{cite web|title=History and Background in Conservation of the Wazir Khan Mosque Lahore: Preliminary Report on Condition and Risk Assessment.|url=http://archnet.org/publications/6586|website=Aga Khan Historic Cities Programme|publisher=Aga Khan Cultural Services - Pakistan|accessdate=2016-08-25|date=2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160827011121/http://archnet.org/publications/6586|archive-date=2016-08-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>。清真寺位於[[德里门_(拉合尔)|德里門]]（即清真寺的{{tsl|en|Dargah|陵園}}的所在地以西約260米）<ref name="archnet.org"/>。清真寺附近是{{link-en|瓦齊爾汗集市廣場|Wazir Khan Chowk}}和[[奇塔门|奇塔门]]。

==建造背景==
[[File:Tomb_of_Syed_Muhammad_Ishaq,_within_Wazir_Khan_Mosque,_Lahore.jpg|thumb]]
[[File:Shop_outside_the_praying_area_of_mosque..jpg|thumb]]
清真寺是莫臥兒皇室庭園總設計師{{link-en|瓦齊爾汗 (拉合爾)|Wazir Khan (Lahore)|瓦齊爾汗}}之作<ref name="WKM">Asher, p.225</ref><ref>{{cite book|first=Shelomo Dov|last=Goitein|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=cmn8U8uNtFAC&pg=PA170&dq=wazir+arabic+word&hl=nl&sa=X&ved=0CCAQ6AEwAGoVChMI28upua-LxgIVZivbCh0RbQDU#v=onepage&q=wazir%20arabic%20word&f=false|title=Studies in Islamic History and Institutions|publisher=BRILL|year=2010|isbn=9004179313|page=170}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Masjid Vazir K̲h̲ān|url=http://archnet.org/sites/1750|website=Archnet|accessdate=2016-08-25|quote=The mosque was founded by Hakim Ilmud Din Ansari, a distinguished physician from Chiniot who received the Ministerial title of 'Wazir Khan' under the reign of Shah Jahan, and was later promoted to the position of Viceroy of Punjab.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160827011117/http://archnet.org/sites/1750|archive-date=2016-08-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>。瓦齊爾汗受任為{{link-en|旁遮普省 (英属印度)|Punjab Province (British India)|旁遮普省}}總督後，被委託在拉合爾當地興建若干紀念建築<ref name="WKM"/>。瓦齊爾汗在奇塔门附近一帶擁有不少物業，並在1634年受委託興建瓦齊爾汗清真寺，以包圍備受崇敬的蘇非聖人{{link-ur|塞義德·穆罕默德·以撒·格茲魯尼|اسحاقگازرونی سہروردی لاہوری}}（{{lang|ur|{{nq|اسحاق گازرونی سہروردی لاہوری}}}}，又稱米倫·伯德舍赫）的陵墓<ref name="Wazir">Westcoat, p.160</ref>；該陵墓現時位於清真寺內的庭院裏<ref name="WKM"/>。清真寺原址在清真寺動工興建以前是一個老舊的聖陵<ref>{{cite news|title=Conservation of the Wazir Khan Mosque Lahore: Preliminary Report on Condition and Risk Assessment|url=http://archnet.org/system/publications/contents/6585/original/DPC3347.pdf?1384798179|accessdate=2016-08-25|agency=Aga Khan Development Network|date=2012|quote=It was built on the remains of an old Sufi complex and the Sufi grave sites associated with it.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160827000354/http://archnet.org/system/publications/contents/6585/original/DPC3347.pdf?1384798179|archive-date=2016-08-27|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

清真寺內部由鞣合了莫臥兒和旁遮普本地傳統裝飾風格的濕壁畫點綴，而外部則由波斯風格的精細釉陶藝術「kashi-kari」（{{lang|ur|{{nq|کاشی کاری}}}}）大幅裝飾<ref name="Dumbarton Oaks">{{cite book|last1=Wescoat|first1=James|title=Mughal Gardens: Sources, Places, Representations, and Prospects|date=1996|publisher=Dumbarton Oaks|pages=160}}</ref>。瓦齊爾汗清真寺取代了先前的{{link-en|別姬夏希清真寺|Begum Shahi Mosque}}成為拉合爾在[[主麻日|主麻日]]的主清真寺<ref name="WK">Gharipour, p.87</ref>。

瓦齊爾汗清真寺是的一個較大規模的建築群（包括一個傳統上由書法店和書店進駐的商店區）的一部分，而集市廣場則位於清真寺正門的前方。<ref name="CURRENT PROJECTS">{{cite web|title=CURRENT PROJECTS|url=http://www.akdn.org/where-we-work/south-asia/pakistan/cultural-development/walled-city-lahore-conservation|publisher=Aga Khan Development Network|accessdate=2016-08-25|quote=The complex included the mosque itself, the chowk (an urban introductory space), a row of hujras (shops) integrated in the entrance system meant specifically for calligraphers and bookbinders, and additional shops on the eastern and northern facades built into the body of the monument.CURRENT PROJECTS|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160826235341/http://www.akdn.org/where-we-work/south-asia/pakistan/cultural-development/walled-city-lahore-conservation|archive-date=2016-08-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>

瓦齊爾汗清真寺市集是南亞首個為特定目的而建造的四軸市集：其中兩軸對準了清真寺的入口，而另外兩軸則組成了「書法家市集」。<ref name="auto">{{cite web|last1=Salman|first1=Muhammad|title=DOCUMENTATION AND CONSERVATION OF WAZIR KHAN MOSQUE, LAHORE, PAKISTAN|url=http://cipa.icomos.org/fileadmin/template/doc/PRAGUE/129.pdf|website=Aga Khan Cultural Service|publisher=Aga Khan Cultural Service|accessdate=2016-08-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151123185847/http://cipa.icomos.org/fileadmin/template/doc/PRAGUE/129.pdf|archive-date=2015-11-23|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

除了由一系列商店所組成的「書法家市集」以外，清真寺方面也出租了寺內的一些地方給在寺內北方和東北方[[立面|立面]]一帶經營生意的商人；清真寺方面也同時營運鄰近的夏希土耳其浴場<ref name="CURRENT PROJECTS"/><ref>{{cite web|title=History and Background in Conservation of the Wazir Khan Mosque Lahore: Preliminary Report on Condition and Risk Assessment.|url=http://archnet.org/publications/6586|website=Aga Khan Historic Cities Programme|publisher=Aga Khan Cultural Services - Pakistan|accessdate=2016-08-25|date=2012|quote=The spectacular monumental ensemble of the Wazir Khan Mosque in the Walled City of Lahore was built in 1634 during the reign of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. Its endowment then comprised the congregational mosque, an elaborate forecourt, a serai, a hammam, a bazaar, and a special bazaar for calligraphers and bookbinders.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160827011121/http://archnet.org/publications/6586|archive-date=2016-08-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>。由那些來源得到的收入被當成維持清真寺運作的[[瓦合甫|瓦合甫]]（獻金）<ref name="Qasmi">{{cite book|first=A. H.|last=Qasmi|title=International encyclopaedia of Islam|publisher=Gyan Publishing House|isbn=9788182053205|page=269}}</ref>。

==歷史==
瓦齊爾汗清真寺在莫臥兒皇帝沙賈漢的統治下於1634年或1635年開始動工，並在約七年內完工。1880年代後期，[[魯德亞德·吉卜林|魯德亞德·吉卜林]]之父{{link-en|約翰·洛克伍德·吉卜林|John Lockwood Kipling}}曾在《印度藝術期刊》（{{lang|en|Journal of Indian Art}}）撰寫過一篇有關清真寺以及其裝飾的文章<ref>{{cite web|url=https://umedia.lib.umn.edu/taxonomy/term/799|title=Media Archive|website=umedia.lib.umn.edu|access-date=2019-03-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170804115333/https://umedia.lib.umn.edu/taxonomy/term/799|archive-date=2017-08-04|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=Journal of Indian Art|url=https://umedia.lib.umn.edu/node/483154|journal=Journal of Indian Art (1886 and 1887)|location=London|publisher=W. Griggs and Sons|volume=1, 2|author=|access-date=2019-03-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180905141052/https://umedia.lib.umn.edu/node/483154|archive-date=2018-09-05|dead-url=no}}</ref>。英國學者{{link-en|弗雷德里克·亨利·安德魯斯|Fred Henry Andrews}}在1903年曾經提及過瓦齊爾汗清真寺曾經陷入失修狀態<ref name="Reading Masjid Wazir Khan2">{{cite web|url=http://islamic-arts.org/2011/reading-masjid-wazir-khan/|title=Reading Masjid Wazir Khan|last1=Mumtaz|first1=Kamil Khan|website=Islamic Arts|accessdate=2016-08-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160622160943/http://islamic-arts.org/2011/reading-masjid-wazir-khan/|archive-date=2016-06-22|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

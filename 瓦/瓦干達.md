'''瓦干達'''（{{lang-en|Wakanda}}）是個位於[[撒哈拉以南非洲|撒哈拉以南非洲]]的虛構國家，由[[漫威漫畫|漫威漫畫]]的[[史丹·李|史丹·李]]和[[積克·高比|積克·高比]]創建，最早出現在于1966年7月出版的《{{link-en|神奇4俠 (漫畫)|Fantastic Four (comic book)|神奇4俠}}》第52集<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://goodcomics.comicbookresources.com/2010/09/18/a-year-of-cool-comics-day-262/|title=A Year of Cool Comics – Day 262|accessdate=2010-09-29|date=2010-09-19|last=Cronin|first=Brian|publisher=[[Comic_Book_Resources|Comic Book Resources]] CSBG Archive}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/07/23/books/black-panther-marvel-comics-roxane-gay-ta-nehisi-coates-Wakanda.html|title=Marvel’s World of Wakanda Will Spotlight Women, on the Page and Behind It|author=GEORGE GENE GUSTINES|date=2016-07-22|newspaper=NYT|accessdate=2016-07-26}}</ref>。在漫畫中，瓦干達因受益於豐富的[[汎金屬|汎金屬]]儲藏量而發展成了一個科技發達的國家。

== 名称 ==
關於瓦干達名稱的由來有幾種理論。這名字可能來自一個同名的的[[苏族|苏族]]神，或者來自[[埃德加·赖斯·巴勒斯|埃德加·赖斯·巴勒斯]]筆下小說“The Man-Eater”的虛構非洲部落Wakandas<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/acts-of-faith/wp/2018/03/07/the-surprising-religious-backstory-of-black-panthers-Wakanda |title=The surprising religious backstory of ‘Black Panther’s’ Wakanda |access-date=2018-11-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180828172205/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/acts-of-faith/wp/2018/03/07/the-surprising-religious-backstory-of-black-panthers-wakanda/ |archive-date=2018-08-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，又或是源自肯尼亞部落[[坎巴族|坎巴族]]（{{lang-en|Wakamba}}）<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.refworld.org/cgi-bin/texis/vtx/rwmain?page=search&docid=3ae6ad3a0&skip=0&query=kamba |title=Kenya: The Kamba tribe, including its traditions and beliefs; the religion practised; and whether female genital mutilation is practised |access-date=2018-11-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181116041344/http://www.refworld.org/cgi-bin/texis/vtx/rwmain?page=search&docid=3ae6ad3a0&skip=0&query=kamba |archive-date=2018-11-16 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>[https://cinema.everyeye.it/articoli/speciale-black-panther-storia-cultura-geografia-religioni-del-Wakanda-37429.html Black Panther: storia, cultura, geografia e religioni del Wakanda] {{Wayback|url=https://cinema.everyeye.it/articoli/speciale-black-panther-storia-cultura-geografia-religioni-del-Wakanda-37429.html |date=20180922211733 }},</ref>，又或者來自“kanda”這個字（即[[刚果语|刚果语]]中的“家庭”）<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://sunubuzzsn.com/international/black-panther-decouvrez-vraie-signification-Wakanda/ |title=Black Panther – Découvrez la vraie signification de Wakanda |access-date=2018-11-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180922211502/https://sunubuzzsn.com/international/black-panther-decouvrez-vraie-signification-Wakanda/ |archive-date=2018-09-22 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

== 位置 ==
[[File:Lake_Turkana_vicinity.jpg|缩略图]]
瓦干達位於[[東非|東非]]，但其確切位置在不同故事中有所不同：一些版本（例如在《{{link-en|神奇4俠 (漫畫)|Fantastic Four (comic book)|神奇4俠}}》第三季第21集）將瓦干達置於[[坦桑尼亞|坦桑尼亞]]北部，也有一些版本（例如在Marvel Atlas#2中<ref name="marvelatlas2">Michael Hoskin, {{Comic book reference||Writer=Michael Hoskin, [[Anthony_Flamini|Anthony Flamini]], Eric J. Moreels & Stuart Vandal|Story=|Title=Marvel Atlas|Issue=2|Date=May 2008|Publisher=[[Marvel_Comics|Marvel Comics]]}}</ref>）將瓦干達置於[[圖爾卡納湖|圖爾卡納湖]]的北端，與[[肯尼亞|肯尼亞]]，[[埃塞俄比亞|埃塞俄比亞]]，[[烏干達|烏干達]]和[[南蘇丹|南蘇丹]]接壤（周圍有像Azania，Canaan和Narobia之類的虛構國家）。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://origins.osu.edu/connecting-history/searching-Wakanda-african-roots-black-panther-story |title=Searching for Wakanda: The African Roots of the Black Panther Story |access-date=2018-11-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180922211715/http://origins.osu.edu/connecting-history/searching-Wakanda-african-roots-black-panther-story |archive-date=2018-09-22 |dead-url=no }}</ref>值得注意的是此範圍在現實世界中是個{{link-en|埃勒米三角區|Ilemi Triangle|存在主權爭議的地區}}，而申索方正是上文提到的四國<ref name ="express">{{Cite web |url=https://www.express.co.uk/entertainment/films/918843/Black-Panther-Wakanda-location-Africa-Avengers-Infinity-War |title=Black Panther: Where is Wakanda supposed to be located in Africa? |publisher=[[每日快報|每日快報]]}}</ref>。[[漫威電影宇宙|漫威電影宇宙]]使用后一种版本。<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.theatlantic.com/notes/2016/04/the-world-of-Wakanda/479466/ |title=Conceptualizing the Black Panther |access-date=2018-11-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181107110923/https://www.theatlantic.com/notes/2016/04/the-world-of-wakanda/479466/ |archive-date=2018-11-07 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

==== 漫威电影宇宙 ====
*在[[漫威电影宇宙|漫威电影宇宙]]中，瓦干達首次出現在《[[鋼鐵俠2|鋼鐵俠2]]》中的一幅地圖上<ref name ="express"></ref>，並在《[[復仇者聯盟2：奧創紀元|復仇者聯盟2：奧創紀元]]》作為[[汎金屬|汎金屬]]的來源首次被正式提及。至於瓦干達的景象就要到《[[美国队长3|美国队长3]]》的[[片尾彩蛋|片尾彩蛋]]中[[美國隊長|美國隊長]]與[[巴奇_(漫威)|巴基]]一起到瓦干達避難時才首次被正式描繪。
*在電影《[[黑豹_(電影)|黑豹]]》中，瓦干達對電影的劇情推進佔重要角色。此電影亦進一步描述了其歷史和文化，例如瓦干達中振金的由來、五大部落、對外關係政策等等。
*瓦干達在同年上映的續集《[[復仇者聯盟3：無限之戰|復仇者聯盟3：無限之戰]]》亦佔重要角色。片末[[復仇者_(漫畫)|復仇者]]跟[[星際異攻隊_(2008年)|銀河守護隊]]聯合對抗魁隆軍隊的情節就是發生在瓦干達。

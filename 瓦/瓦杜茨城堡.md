'''瓦杜茨城堡'''（{{lang-de|'''Schloß Vaduz'''}}）是[[列支敦士登親王|列支敦士登親王]]的官方居住地<ref name=o>{{cite web|url=http://www.gowealthy.com/gowealthy/wcms/en/home/articles/travel/sightseeing/Vaduz-Castle-Liechtenstein-JPhKgKrA1x.html |title=Sightseeing : Vaduz Castle- Liechtenstein |publisher=Gowealthy.com |date= |accessdate=2012-06-20}}</ref> 。城堡位于[[列支敦士登|列支敦士登]]首都[[瓦杜茨|瓦杜茨]]附近的山顶上，首都也由此城堡得名<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.virtualtourist.com/travel/Europe/Liechtenstein/Vaduz/Things_To_Do-Vaduz-MISC-BR-1.html |title=Fun Things To Do in Vaduz - Attractions & Must See |publisher=VirtualTourist |date= |accessdate=2012-06-20}}</ref>。

==历史==

==今日==
城堡在1905年至1920年间经过一次大修缮，此后于20年代初在[[约翰二世_(列支敦士登)|约翰二世]]主持下又经历一次修缮，并于30年代初由[[弗朗茨·约瑟夫二世_(列支敦士登)|弗朗茨·约瑟夫二世]]主持扩建。自1938年起，该城堡便是列支敦士登大公家庭的主要居住地。大公家庭现今仍居住于城堡中，故城堡不对外开放<ref>{{cite web|url=http://merlinandrebecca.blogspot.com/2011/08/castle-hunting-schloss-valduz.html |title=Article about Vaduz Castle and Schalun castle (includes images of both) |date=2011-08-18 |accessdate=2013-04-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tourismus.li/en/navpage-CultureLI-HistoricalSightsLI-142905.html |title=Liechtenstein - Vaduz Castle |publisher=Tourismus.li |date= |accessdate=2012-06-20}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ruinenland.de/burgen-luxemburg.htm |title=Photo gallery of Liechtenstein and Luxembourg castles, including photos of Vaduz Castle |date=2011-08-18 |accessdate=2013-04-14}}</ref>。

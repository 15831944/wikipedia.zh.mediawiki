'''瓦希達·雷曼'''（{{Lang-en|Waheeda Rehman}}，{{bd|1936年|5月14日|catIdx=Rehman, Waheeda}}）是印度[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]國寶級女演員，自1950年代開始從影。著名作品包括 ''[[C.I.D._(1956_film)|C.I.D.]]'' (1956) and 5 [[Guru_Dutt|Guru Dutt]] classics - ''[[Pyaasa|Pyaasa]]'' (1957), ''12 O'Clock'' (1958), ''[[Kaagaz_Ke_Phool|Kaagaz Ke Phool]]'' (1959), ''[[Sahib_Bibi_Aur_Ghulam|Sahib Bibi Aur Ghulam]]'', ''[[Chaudhvin_Ka_Chand|Chaudhvin Ka Chand]]''(1961). Her other notable works include ''[[Solva_Saal|Solva Saal]]'' (1958), ''[[Baat_Ek_Raat_Ki|Baat Ek Raat Ki]]'' (1962), ''[[Kohra|Kohra]]'' (1964), ''[[Guide_(film)|Guide]]'' (1965), ''[[Teesri_Kasam|Teesri Kasam]]'', ''[[Mujhe_Jeene_Do|Mujhe Jeene Do]]'' (1966), ''[[Neel_Kamal|Neel Kamal]]'' and ''[[Khamoshi|Khamoshi]]'' (1969)。

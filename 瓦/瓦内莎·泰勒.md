'''瓦内莎·泰勒'''（{{lang-en|Vanessa Taylor}}）是一位美国[[制片人|电视制片人]]、[[编剧|编剧]]。

==职业生涯==
Her television credits include ''[[权力的游戏_(电视剧)|权力的游戏]]、[[Gideon's_Crossing|Gideon's Crossing]], [[Alias_(TV_series)|Alias]]'' (hired by [[J.J._Abrams|J.J. Abrams]]), ''[[Everwood|Everwood]]'' (hired by [[Greg_Berlanti|Greg Berlanti]]), ''[[Tell_Me_You_Love_Me|Tell Me You Love Me]]'', and ''[[Cupid_(TV_series)|Cupid]]''. She also co-created ''[[Jack_&_Bobby|Jack & Bobby]]''.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0961827/</ref>

泰勒创作了2012年的电影[[希望温泉|希望温泉]]，由[[大衛·法蘭科|大衛·法蘭科]]执导，[[梅麗·史翠普|梅麗·史翠普]]、[[湯米·李·瓊斯|湯米·李·瓊斯]]和[[史提夫·卡爾|史提夫·卡爾]]主演，被誉为“婚姻治疗师尝试帮助有31年婚姻的夫妇重燃一个没有爱的关系”<ref>http://www.deadline.com/2011/.../tommy-lee-jones-for-great-hope-springs/</ref>。

当前为电影[[分歧者·異類叛逃|分歧者·異類叛逃]]的编剧之一。

==提名/获奖==
She was nominated for a Primetime Emmy (Outstanding Drama Series) in 2012, and a WGA Award--TV (Drama Series) in 2013, both for ''[[Game_of_Thrones|Game of Thrones]]''.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0961827/awards</ref> She was also nominated for a WIN Outstanding Writer Award (Outstanding Film or Show Written by a Woman) in 2012 for ''[[Hope_Springs_(2012_film)|Hope Springs]]''.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0961827/bio#trivia</ref>

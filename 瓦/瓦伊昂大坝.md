'''瓦伊昂大壩'''（{{lang-it|Diga del Vajont}}）是位於意大利[[威尼斯|威尼斯]]北部[[多克山|多克山]]中的一個已廢棄的水壩。這座水壩1957年始建，完工於1959年，高{{convert|262|m|ft}}，是[[世界高坝列表|世界上最高的大壩]]之一。<ref>{{cite web
|url=http://temi.repubblica.it/corrierealpi-diga-del-vajont-1963-2013-il-cinquantenario/la-costruzione-della-diga/

1963年10月9日發生了大規模的山體滑坡。五千萬立方米的水體從水壩上漫出，導致附近村莊和城市約兩千名居民喪生。大壩本身則完好，至今仍保留在原地<ref name="Stephens1976">{{cite book|author=John Hall Stephens|title=The Guinness book of structures: bridges, towers, tunnels, dams|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ZyRSAAAAMAAJ|year=1976|publisher=Guinness Superlatives}}</ref><ref name="GilliMangan2012">{{cite book|author1=Éric Gilli|author2=Christian Mangan|author3=Jacques Mudry|title=Hydrogeology: Objectives, Methods, Applications|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SwPest4lwi8C&pg=PA301|date=14 September 2012|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=978-1-57808-794-5|pages=301–}}</ref>
。

==事故==
[[File:Disastro_Vajont.jpg|thumb]]
[[File:Vajont1963usarmy.jpg|thumb]]]]
[[File:Longarone_Pirago.jpg|thumb]]
1962年建築方的工程師就提出水壩蓄水後可能會導致山體滑坡，但未引起重視。1963年夏蓄水，并開始發生小規模山體滑坡。運營方（[[Enel|Enel]]）在9月決定降低大壩水位，但為時已晚。1963年10月9日下午10時39分，發生了2.6億立方米的[[山體滑坡|山體大滑坡]]，山體以{{convert|110|kph|mph}}的速度傾瀉入水庫中，用時45秒。水庫中的五千萬立方米的水漫出水壩，造成{{convert|250|m|ft}}高的巨浪<ref name="Vaj2">[http://tellnet.jp/pdf/italy.pdf The Vajont Dam Disaster] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080227120506/http://tellnet.jp/pdf/italy.pdf |date=27 February 2008 }}, TeLL-Net Kick-Off Assembly, 2006, retrieved January 2008.</ref>。

洪水衝垮了包括[[隆加羅內|隆加羅內]]在內的多個村莊，造成約兩千人喪生<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.lastampa.it/redazione/cmsSezioni/cronache/200810articoli/37192girata.asp |title=Vajont, il muro d'acqua che ha ucciso Longarone |publisher=Lastampa.It |date=2008-10-09 |accessdate=2012-10-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120930084101/http://www1.lastampa.it/redazione/cmsSezioni/cronache/200810articoli/37192girata.asp |archivedate=30 September 2012  }}</ref>。

===事後===
事後災難倖存者在[[塔利亞門托河|塔利亞門托河]]平原上組建了新鎮[[瓦伊昂|瓦伊昂]]，[[隆加羅內|隆加羅內]]則重建。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-24464867|title=Night of the 'tsunami'|first=Mark|last=Duff|date=10 October 2013|publisher=|via=www.bbc.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://globalnews.ca/news/892928/2000-people-11-family-members-died-in-1963-italian-earthquake/|title=2000 people, 11 family members, died in 1963 Italian rockslide|publisher=}}</ref>

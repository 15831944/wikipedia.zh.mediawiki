== 背景 ==
1841年，文莱帝国授予[[砂拉越王国|砂拉越王国]]独立的地位，自1888年，其被英国保护。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://www.ijern.com/journal/January-2014/46.pdf|title=Borneo Art Education - BAE|author=Aloysius Yapp|journal=International Journal of Education and Research|date=January 2014|volume=II|issue=1|pages=2|ISSN=2201-6333|format=[[PDF|PDF]]}}</ref> 然而，在那个时候，砂拉越并未获得“完全独立”<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/opinion/khairie-hisyam-aliman/article/no-sarawak-did-not-gain-independence-in-1841|title=No, Sarawak did not gain independence in 1841|author=Khairie Hisyam Aliman|publisher=The Malay Mail|date=13 September 2014|accessdate=15 September 2014|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140915014609/http://www.themalaymailonline.com/opinion/khairie-hisyam-aliman/article/no-sarawak-did-not-gain-independence-in-1841|archivedate=2014年9月15日|deadurl=no}}</ref> [[第二次世界大战|第二次世界大战]]结束后，其领土被英国军事管制区接管，且在1946年成为了英殖民地。<ref>{{cite book|author1=Patricia Pui Huen Lim|author2=Diana Wong|title=War and Memory in Malaysia and Singapore|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=ADkiembZcLYC&pg=PA124|year=2000|publisher=Institute of Southeast Asian Studies|isbn=978-981-230-037-9|pages=124–}}</ref> 领土转移给殖民政府引起了砂拉越人民的不满，人民期望能够获得独立，开展了反割让运动（Anti-cession movement of Sarawak）1949年，英国第二任驻砂总督被马来年轻老师罗斯利多比（Rosli Dhobi）暗杀<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/magazine-17299633|title=The stabbed governor of Sarawak|author=Mike Thompson|publisher=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|date=12 March 2012|accessdate=30 May 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theborneopost.com/2013/09/22/farewell-to-the-crown-prince/|title=Farewell to the Crown Prince|author=Antonia Chiam|publisher=The Borneo Post|date=22 September 2013|accessdate=30 May 2014}}</ref>。总督继任者为安东尼安东尼·阿贝尔（Anthony Abell），是科博德委员会（Cobbold Commission）的成员之一。

砂拉越是于1963年7月22日获得“自治权”的<ref name="discovery.nationalarchives.gov.uk"/>，且以砂拉越将会在同年9月16日共组马来西亚为前提<ref>{{cite book|author=Frans Welman|title=Borneo Trilogy Sarawak: Volume 2|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=A-H-O8pxUnMC&pg=PA134|publisher=Booksmango|isbn=978-616-245-089-1|pages=134–}}</ref> 沙拉越自治前的最后一名英人总督是Alexander Waddell，<ref>'Sarawakiana' 26 August 2008 "The last British governor of Sarawak'</ref> ，而第一名砂拉越人[[首席部长|首席部长]]由英殖民政府任命，为Stephen Kalong Ningkan<ref>{{cite book|title=Asia Yearbook|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=IdZHAAAAYAAJ|year=1964|publisher=Far Eastern economic review}}</ref>。

'''朱巴'''（{{bo|z=སྤྲུལ་པ་|w=sprulpa}}，{{lang-en|Tulpa}}）是[[神秘主义|神秘主义]]中的一个概念，用以指代通过精神力量所创造的一个物体的超自然现象。<ref name="Campbell">{{cite book|last1=Campbell|first1=Eileen|last2=Brennan|first2=J.H|last3=Holt-Underwood|first3=Fran|title=Body, Mind & Spirit: A Dictionary of New Age Ideas, People, Places, and Terms|date=1994|publisher=C.E. Tuttle Company|location=Boston|isbn=080483010X|edition=Revised|section=Thoughtform}}</ref>这一概念由20世纪的西方通神论者提出，该词在藏语中本意为“散发”或“显现”。<ref name="Mikles">{{cite journal|last1=Mikles|first1=Natasha L.|last2=Laycock|first2=Joseph P.|title=Tracking the Tulpa: Exploring the "Tibetan" Origins of a Contemporary Paranormal Idea|journal=Nova Religio: The Journal of Alternative and Emergent Religions|date=6 August 2015|volume=19|issue=1|pages=87–97|doi=10.1525/nr.2015.19.1.87}}</ref>

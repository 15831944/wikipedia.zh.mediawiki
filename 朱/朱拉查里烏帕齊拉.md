'''朱拉查里乌帕齐拉'''（{{lang-bn|জুরাইছড়ি}}，{{lang-en|Juraichhari Upazila}}）是[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]][[蘭加馬蒂縣|蘭加馬蒂縣]]的一个[[乌帕齐拉|乌帕齐拉]]，位於[[吉大港专区|吉大港专区]]的[[蘭加馬蒂縣|蘭加馬蒂縣]]。<ref name=Banglapedia>{{citation |author=Sayed Abdullah Al Mamun Chowdhury |chapter={{{3}}} Upazila |chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title={{{3}}}_Upazila |title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |editor=Sirajul Islam and Ahmed A. Jamal |publisher={{tsl|en|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh||Asiatic Society of Bangladesh}} |year=2012 |edition=Second }}{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>。

==人口==
據{{tsl|en|1991 Bangladesh census|1991年孟加拉國人口普查}}，朱拉查里共有戶數2,412戶，人口11621人。其中男性佔比52.16%，女性佔比47.84%；成年人口6,158人。該地7歲以上人口之平均識字率為23.4%。<ref name="census">{{cite web |  accessdate = November 10, 2006 |  url = http://www.bangladeshgov.org/mop/ndb/arpc91_v1/tables04.htm | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20050327072826/http://www.bangladeshgov.org/mop/ndb/arpc91_v1/tables04.htm | archivedate = 2005-03-27 | title = Population Census Wing, BBS.}}</ref>

'''朱迪斯·巴特勒'''（{{lang-en|'''Judith Butler'''}}，{{bd|1956年|2月24日|catIdx=B}}），美國[[後結構主義|後結構主義]]學者，其研究領域有[[女性主義|女性主義]]、[[酷兒理論|酷兒理論]]、[[政治哲學|政治哲學]]以及[[倫理學|倫理學]]<ref name=ownskafka>{{cite web | url=http://www.lrb.co.uk/v33/n05/judith-butler/who-owns-kafka| title=Who Owns Kafka?| author=Judith Butler  | work=London Review of Books | date= 2011-03-03 | accessdate=2011-02-27}}</ref>。目前任教於[[加州大学伯克利分校|加州大学伯克利分校]][[修辞学|修辞学]]與[[比较文学|比较文学]]系。巴特勒在1984年从耶鲁大学获得她的哲学博士学位，后开始从事后结构主义和西方女权主义理论的研究。她被认为是“在现代政治理论中最有影响力的声音之一”<ref>Derek Wai Ming Barker, "Judith Butler's Postmodern Antigone," in ''Tragedy and Citizenship: Conflict, Reconciliation, and Democracy from Haemon to Hegel'', p. 119, [[SUNY_Press|SUNY Press]], 2009, ISBN 0791476294</ref>和今天“最有影响力的[[女性主义|女性主义]]理论家之一”。<ref>Helmi Järviluoma, Pirkko Moisala, Anni Vilkko, "Performing and negotiating gender", ''Gender and Qualitative Methods'', p. 14, 2003, [[SAGE_Publications|SAGE Publications]], ISBN 9780761965855</ref>

她自己是[[犹太人|犹太人]]，最近的工作主要集中在犹太哲学，探索国家暴力的前后和与对犹太复国主义者的批评。<ref name=berkbio>{{cite web | url=http://rhetoric.berkeley.edu/faculty_bios/judith_butler.html|title=U.C. Berkeley Biography| work=U.C. Berkeley | accessdate=2010-03-01}}</ref><ref>Butler, Judith. "The Charge of Anti-Semitism: Jews, Israel, and the Risks of Public Critique. ''Wrestling with Zionism: Progressive Jewish-American Responses to the Israeli-Palestinian Conflict''. Ed. Tony Kushner and Alisa Solonmon. New York: Grove, 2003. pp. 249–265.</ref><ref>{{cite news | url=http://mondoweiss.net/2012/08/judith-butler-responds-to-attack-i-affirm-a-judaism-that-is-not-associated-with-state-violence.html | title=Judith Butler responds to attack: ‘I affirm a Judaism that is not associated with state violence’ | first=Judith | last=Butler | newspaper=[[Mondoweiss|Mondoweiss]] | date=2012-08-27}}</ref>

在政治上，她是“犹太和平之声”顾问委员会的成员<ref name="Mondoweiss27"/>和[[抵制、撤资、制裁|“抵制，撤资和制裁”]][[以色列|以色列]]的支持者。<ref name=taught>{{cite web| url=http://haaretz.com/hasen/spages/1152017.html| title=Judith Butler: As a Jew, I was taught it was ethically imperative to speak up| author=Udi Aloni| work=Haaretz| date=2010-02-24| accessdate=2010-02-24| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100227081749/http://www.haaretz.com/hasen/spages/1152017.html| archivedate=2010年2月27日}}</ref>她认同“犹太教与[[國家恐怖主義|國家恐怖主義]]是无关的”<ref name="Mondoweiss27">{{cite web | url=http://mondoweiss.net/2012/08/judith-butler-responds-to-attack-i-affirm-a-judaism-that-is-not-associated-with-state-violence.html| title= Judith Butler responds to attack: ‘I affirm a Judaism that is not associated with state violence’| author=Judith Butler | newspaper=[[Mondoweiss|Mondoweiss]] | date= 2012-08-27 | accessdate= 2012-08-27}}</ref>，并强调，以色列并不代表所有的犹太人。<ref name="Derstandard15">{{cite web | url=http://derstandard.at/1347492636246/US-Philosophin-Butler-Israel-vertritt-mich-nicht| title= US-Philosophin Butler: Israel vertritt mich nicht | author= | newspaper=[[Der_Standard|Der Standard]] | date= 2012-09-15 | accessdate= 2012-09-15}}</ref>

她曾於美國佔領華爾街運動中，公開演講。

==个人生活==
巴特勒目前与她的伴侣Wendy Brown一起生活，Wendy Brown是一位政治学家。巴特勒为反犹太复国主义犹太人和以色列政治的批评者。<ref>{{cite web|title=Judith Butler|quote=Butler identifies as an anti-Zionist Jew and is a critic of Israeli politics54.|publisher=Oxford Reference Online Premium}}</ref>

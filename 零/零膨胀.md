'''零膨胀模型'''（{{lang-en|Zero-inflated models}}）是人们在社会科学、自然中的计数资料的实际研究中，观察事件发生数中含有大量的零值。例如保险索赔次数，索赔数为0的概率很高，否则保险公司就面临破产风险。这种数据数资料中的零值过多，超出了Poisson分布等一般离散分布的预测能力。零膨胀这个概念首先是由 Lambert在1992年的论文“Zero-Inflated Poisson Regression, with an Application to Defects in Manufacturing”中提出<ref>{{cite journal | title = Zero-Inflated Poisson Regression, with an Application to Defects in Manufacturing | first =Diane | last = Lambert | journal = Technometrics | year = 1992 | volume = 34 | issue = 1 | pages = 1-14 | url =http://www.jstor.org/stable/1269547}}</ref>.。

1994年, Greene根据Lambert的方法提出了零膨胀负二项模型 (ZINB)<ref>{{Cite journal |last=Greene | first=William H. | title=Some Accounting for Excess Zeros and Sample Selection in Poisson and Negative Binomial Regression Models |journal=Working Paper EC-94-10: Department of Economics, New York University |year=1994|url=http://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=1293115}}</ref>。 2000年, Daniel根据Lambert的方法提出了零膨胀二项模型 (ZIB)<ref>{{cite journal | title = Zero-Inflated Poisson and Binomial Regression with Random Effects: A Case Study | first =Daniel | last = B. Hall  | journal =Biometrics| year = 2000 | volume = 56 | issue = 4| pages = 1030-1039| url =http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.0006-341X.2000.01030.x/abstract}}</ref>。

'''疯人院影业'''（{{Lang-en|'''The Asylum'''}}）是[[美国|美国]]一家独立电影制作与发行公司，其主要关注于低成本、[[录影带首映|录影带首映]]类电影领域。疯人院影业的大部分作品都是“[[恶搞|恶搞]]”[[北美主要电影公司|巨头电影公司]]的大片，他们会在自己作品的标题和剧本上[[戏仿|戏仿]]时下大片。这类影片也被媒体称之为“{{Link-en|恶搞影片|Mockbuster}}”。<ref name="Potts">{{cite news |first=Rolf |last=Potts |title=''The New B Movie'' |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2007/10/07/magazine/07wwln-essay-t.html?_r=2&ref=magazine&oref=slogin |work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=2007-10-07|accessdate=2009-02-06}}</ref><ref name="Chicago Tribune">{{cite news |first=Christopher |last=Borrelli |title=''Bizarro Blockbusters'' |url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2009-07-03/entertainment/0907010536_1_asylum-parallel-universe-dreamworks |work=[[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]] |date=2009-07-03|accessdate=2010-10-12}}</ref><ref name="NPR">{{cite news |title=''Movie 'Mockbusters' Put Snakes on Trains''|url=https://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=16986812 |publisher=[[National_Public_Radio|National Public Radio]] |date=2007-12-08 |accessdate=2009-02-06}}</ref><ref name="Adult Swim">{{cite web |url=http://www.adultswim.com/blog/interviews/how-to-make-a-mockbuster.html |title=How to Make a Mockbuster (In Five Easy Steps) |author=Solomon, Dan |publisher=[[Adult_Swim|Adult Swim]] |date=2011-08-23|accessdate=2011-08-24}}</ref><ref name="Somma">{{cite web |url=http://the-artifice.com/what-the-asylum-is-all-about/ |title=Masters of the Mockbuster:What The Asylum Is All About |author=Somma, Brandon |publisher=''The Artifice'' |date=2013-01-04 |accessdate=2013-01-05}}</ref> 疯人院影业的电影主要交由{{Link-en|回声桥家庭娱乐|Echo Bridge Home Entertainment}}和GT传媒负责发行；不过在2015年之前，{{Link-en|赛点数码|Cinedigm}}也负责发行该公司影片。疯人院影业的作品中以“[[風飛鯊系列電影|鲨卷风系列]]”最为知名。截止2009年到2010年财年，疯人院影业还从未在其投资的影片上亏损。

'''戰神遊行'''（{{Lang-bn|মঙ্গল শোভাযাত্রা}}）是孟加拉的一個在[[孟加拉新年|孟加拉新年]]第一天舉行的大規模遊行<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/features/magazine/dramatic-dawn/article3360271.ece|title=Dramatic dawn|last=Habib|first=Haroon|newspaper=The Hindu|access-date=30 November 2016}}</ref>。這個遊行最初由孟加拉達卡大學藝術學院的師生所籌備的一個慶典。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://bdnews24.com/bangladesh/2015/04/14/mangal-shobhajatra-for-removing-evil|title='Mangal Shobhajatra' for removing evil|newspaper=bdnews24.com|access-date=30 November 2016}}</ref> 這個遊行是孟加拉人長久以來促進團結的{{tsl|en|Secularism in Bangladesh||習俗}}<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.fr/2016/11/30/lunesco-inscrit-la-biere-belge-au-patrimoine-culturel-immateriel-de-lhumanite/|title=La bière belge inscrite au "patrimoine culturel immatériel" de l'humanité par l'Unesco|website=Le Huffington Post|access-date=30 November 2016}}</ref> 它在2016年時被聯合國教科文組織登錄為[[非物质文化遗产|非物質文化遺產]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.unesco.org/culture/ich/en/RL/mangal-shobhajatra-on-pahela-baishakh-01091|title=UNESCO recognises Mangal Shobhajatra as cultural heritage|date=30 November 2016|newspaper=UNESCO official site|access-date=30 November 2016}}</ref> ，分類為具有代表性的人類非物質文化遺產。<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/country/unesco-recognises-mangal-shobhajatra-cultural-heritage-1322926|title=UNESCO recognises Mangal Shobhajatra as cultural heritage|date=30 November 2016|newspaper=The Daily Star|access-date=30 November 2016}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=http://en.prothom-alo.com/entertainment/news/131041/Mangal-Shobhajatra-listed-as-UNESCO-heritage|title=UNESCO lists Mangal Shobhajatra as cultural heritage|newspaper=Prothom Alo|accessdate=28 February 2017|archivedate=1 December 2016|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161201013259/http://en.prothom-alo.com/entertainment/news/131041/Mangal-Shobhajatra-listed-as-UNESCO-heritage}}</ref> 

== 語源學 ==

孟加拉語的''Mangal Shobhajatra'' 意思是 "祈福的遊行".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nationmultimedia.com/news/aec/asean_plus/30301254|title=Mangal Shobhajatra included in Unesco cultural heritage list – The Nation|newspaper=The Nation|access-date=1 December 2016}}</ref>

== 歷史 ==
[[File:Mangal_Shobhajatra_in_Dhaka.jpg|thumb]]像出现在戰神遊行中]]
第一次的遊行舉辦在1989年,<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2016/12/01/503974771/unesco-deems-belgiums-beer-culture-a-treasure-of-humanity|title=UNESCO Deems Belgium's Beer Culture A Treasure of Humanity|newspaper=NPR.org|access-date=4 January 2017}}</ref> 當時獨裁統治者[[艾尔沙德|艾爾沙德]]是這個國家的總統。他通過一場兵不血刃的{{tsl|en|1982 Bangladesh coup d'état||政變}}當上總統。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/op-ed/the-rocky-road-democracy-1325515|title=The Rocky Road of Democracy|date=6 December 2016|newspaper=The Daily Star|access-date=4 January 2017}}</ref>

在那時，孟加拉在軍政府的統治下遭受{{tsl|en|Floods in Bangladesh||水災}}侵襲。 於是在達卡發生了一場{{tsl|en|1990 Mass Uprising in Bangladesh||大規模起義}}，許多人在那時犧牲了，包括{{tsl|en|Noor Hossain||諾·霍珊}}。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/perspective/dissent-and-the-price-freedom-1312171|title=Dissent and the price of freedom|date=10 November 2016|newspaper=The Daily Star|language=en|access-date=22 January 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://en.prothom-alo.com/bangladesh/news/85565/Shaheed-Noor-Hossain-Day-observed|title=Shaheed Noor Hossain Day observed|newspaper=Prothom Alo|access-date=22 January 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171020221216/http://en.prothom-alo.com/bangladesh/news/85565/Shaheed-Noor-Hossain-Day-observed|archive-date=2017-10-20|dead-url=yes}}</ref> [[達卡大學|達卡大學]]藝術學院的學生決定透過在{{tsl|en|Pohela Boishakh||孟加拉新年}}時舉辦戰神遊行來表達對政府的不滿 。<ref name=":2">{{Cite news |author=Diplomatic Correspondent |date=1 December 2016 |title=Unesco lists Mangal Shobhajatra as cultural heritage |url=http://www.thedailystar.net/frontpage/unesco-cultural-heritage-list-adds-mangal-shobhajatra-1323331 |newspaper={{tsl|en|The Daily Star (Bangladesh)||The Daily Star}} |location=Dhaka, Bangladesh |access-date=24 January 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://en.prothom-alo.com/bangladesh/news/131111/Pohela-Boishakh%E2%80%99s-rally-gets-UNESCO%E2%80%99s-recognition|title=Pohela Boishakh's rally gets UNESCO's recognition|newspaper=Prothom Alo|access-date=1 December 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170409034056/http://en.prothom-alo.com/bangladesh/news/131111/Pohela-Boishakh%E2%80%99s-rally-gets-UNESCO%E2%80%99s-recognition|archive-date=2017-04-09|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name="Business News 24 BD">{{Cite web|url=http://businessnews24bd.com/mangal-shobhajatra-gets-recognition-as-cultural-heritage/|title=Mangal Shobhajatra gets recognition as cultural heritage {{!}} Business News 24 BD|website=businessnews24bd.com|access-date=1 December 2016}}</ref>

== 相關 ==
每年都有上千人戴著鳥，魚，動物等等的面具參加這場遊行。這個遊行真正的意涵是代表團結，和平，驅趕邪惡並為社會帶來進步<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":0" /> 它被視爲孟加拉{{tsl|en|Secularism in Bangladesh||長久的統一}}的表現，不論階級，信仰，性別，年齡都能夠團結起來。<ref name=":3" />

== 聯合國教科文組織的認證 ==
2014年{{tsl|en|Bangla Academy||孟加拉研究院}}編寫了一份{{tsl|en|Ministry of Cultural Affairs||孟加拉文化部}}附和的提名文件並聽從聯合國教科文組織的認證。<ref name=":2" /> 2016年11月30日，戰神遊行在[[联合国教育、科学及文化组织|聯合國教科文組織]]在'''[[亚的斯亚贝巴|阿迪斯阿貝巴]]'''舉行的的第11次會議上通過跨政府組織保護非物質文化遺產公約，認證為非物質文化遺產。<ref name="Business News 24 BD" /><ref name=":2"/>

'''福泰制药公司'''（Vertex Pharmaceuticals, Inc.）是一家总部位于美国[[波士顿|波士顿]]的制药公司，1989年由[[约书亚·博格|约书亚·博格]]<ref name="Globe">{{cite news |title=Rearranging the Corporate DNA |first=Robert |last=Weisman |url=http://www.boston.com/business/healthcare/articles/2011/04/24/can_vertex_transform_itself_into_a_money_making_enterprise/ |newspaper=[[The_Boston_Globe|Boston Sunday Globe]] |date=24 April 2011 |accessdate=25 June 2012 |page=G1}}</ref>和[[凯文·J·金塞拉|凯文·J·金塞拉]]创立<ref name=molecule-book>{{cite book |last=Werth |first=Barry |authorlink=Barry Werth |date=1994 |title=The Billion-Dollar Molecule: One Company's Quest for the Perfect Drug|location=New York |publisher=Simon & Schuster |isbn=0671510576 |oclc=32047662}} {{Google books|Pw4_sKSwizYC|The Billion-Dollar Molecule}}.</ref>。该公司主要研究和生产各类治療絕症的[[小分子藥物|小分子藥物]]，著名产品包括治疗囊性纤维化的[[伊伐卡托|伊伐卡托]]以及抗丙肝药[[特拉匹韦|特拉匹韦]]。

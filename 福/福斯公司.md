}}'''福斯公司'''（'''Fox Corporation'''／'''Fox Corp.'''），由[[迪士尼收購福斯|迪士尼收购21世纪福斯]]资产后，收购协议以外的部分组成。<ref name="FSN">{{Cite news|author=Littleton|first=Cynthia|title=Fox Confirms It Won’t Bid on Disney’s Regional Sports Networks|url=https://variety.com/2019/biz/news/fox-disney-regional-sports-networks-bid-1203105421/|work=Variety|date=January 11, 2019|accessdate=January 11, 2019}}</ref><ref name="Fox Notes">{{Cite web|url=https://www.21cf.com/news/21st-century-fox/2019/21st-century-fox-announces-pricing-of-senior-notes-by-fox-corporation/|title=21st Century Fox Announces Pricing Of Senior Notes By Fox Corporation|date=January 15, 2019|accessdate=February 1, 2019}}</ref> 该公司于2019年3月19日开始股票交易。

福斯公司是21世纪福斯公司的合法继承者（[[新闻集团_(1979－2013年)|新闻集团]]的再继承者）主要经营电视广播、[[新聞媒體|新闻]]和体育直播。其姐妹公司[[新闻集团|新闻集团]]，持有默多克的报纸股份以及澳大利亚的其他媒体资产（均由他和他的家族通过家族信托持有，各持有39%的股权）。 默多克为联合执行董事长，他的儿子[[拉克蘭·梅鐸|拉克兰·默多克]]是董事长兼首席执行官。

福斯的资产包括[[福斯廣播公司|福斯广播公司]]、{{le|福斯电视台集团|Fox Television Stations|福斯电视台}}、[[福斯新聞頻道|福斯新闻]]、[[福斯財經網|福斯財經網]]以及本土运营的[[FOX體育_(美國)|福斯体育]]等。

== 历史 ==

=== 形成 ===
{{main|迪士尼收購21世紀福斯}}
2017年12月14日，[[华特迪士尼公司|迪士尼公司]]宣布有意收購21世纪福斯的影視娱乐、有线娱乐和直播卫星部门。而其余的部分，将整合為一个"新的福斯"，持有的资产包括福斯的广播电视网络、[[福斯新聞頻道|新闻频道]]以及[[FOX體育_(美國)|福斯体育]]。而福斯区域體育网络包含在出售資產中。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2013/jun/28/entertainment/la-et-ct-news-corp-21st-century-fox-20130628|title=News Corp. divides into two companies; 21st Century Fox is born|author=James|first=Meg|date=June 28, 2013|work=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=February 8, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://variety.com/2017/biz/news/disney-fox-merger-deal-52-4-billion-merger-1202631242/|title=Disney to Buy 21st Century Fox Assets for $52.4 Billion in Historic Hollywood Merger|author=Steinberg|first=Cynthia|author2=Littleton|first2=Brian|date=December 14, 2017|work=Variety|accessdate=February 8, 2018}}</ref>2018年5月确认由[[拉克蘭·梅鐸|拉克兰·默多克]]管理新的福斯公司。<ref name="Lachlan">{{Cite web|url=http://money.cnn.com/2018/05/16/media/lachlan-murdoch-new-fox-ceo/index.html|title=Lachlan Murdoch, not James, will lead New Fox company|author=Disis|first=Jill|work=CNN Money|date=May 16, 2018|accessdate=May 17, 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180517012745/http://money.cnn.com/2018/05/16/media/lachlan-murdoch-new-fox-ceo/index.html|archivedate=May 17, 2018}}</ref>

併購將面临反垄断审查，由于[[美国联邦通信委员会|美国联邦通信委员会]]（FCC）的複數無線電視網所有權限制規則（Dual Network Rule）禁止顶级广播网络之间的合并，迪士尼在法律上无法同时拥有ABC和福斯网络。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/thr-esq/fcc-reviewing-rule-prohibiting-mergers-big-four-broadcast-networks-1168629|title=FCC Reviewing Rule Prohibiting Mergers Among Big Four Broadcast Networks|work=The Hollywood Reporter|language=en|accessdate=2019-03-19}}</ref> [[司法部|司法部]]还下令，迪士尼在90天内關閉福斯区域体育网络。司法部還认为，迪士尼拥有的[[ESPN|ESPN]]（80%）與福斯区域网络结合将使迪士尼在有线体育市场占据主导地位。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2018/biz/news/disney-21st-century-fox-justice-department-approval-1202859241/|title=Justice Department Approves Disney’s Acquisition of 21st Century Fox With Divestiture of Regional Sports Networks|author=Littleton|first=Cynthia|date=June 27, 2018|work=Variety|accessdate=June 27, 2018}}</ref><ref name="cnbc">{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2018/06/27/us-set-to-give-antitrust-approval-for-disney-fox-deal.html|title=Disney wins US antitrust approval to buy Fox assets|work=CNBC|date=June 27, 2018|accessdate=June 27, 2018}}</ref>

2018年中旬，[[康卡斯特|康卡斯特]]對迪士尼計劃收購的資產以及[[天空公司|天空公司]]（21世纪福斯控股的公司）发起竞标。 2018年7月，福斯同意迪士尼將收購價增至713亿美元，以抵御康卡斯特的反竞标。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2018/06/disney-outbids-comcast-in-escalating-bidding-war-for-21st-century-fox/|title=Sorry, Comcast: Fox and Disney sign new merger deal, rejecting Comcast bid|work=Ars Technica|accessdate=20 June 2018|language=en-us}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/20/business/dealbook/disney-comcast-21st-century-fox.html|title=Disney Ups Its Bid for 21st Century Fox to $71.3 Billion|work=The New York Times|accessdate=20 June 2018|language=en}}</ref> 英国监管机构下令對[[天空公司|天空公司]]進行盲拍，被康卡斯特購得。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/media/2018/sep/26/rupert-murdochs-sky-reign-to-end-as-fox-sells-all-shares-to-comcast|title=Rupert Murdoch's Sky reign to end as Fox sells all shares to Comcast|author=Waterson|first=Jim|date=26 September 2018|work=The Guardian|language=en|accessdate=27 September 2018}}</ref>

2018年10月10日，据报道正在筹备即将完成的出售，整合為"新福斯"将在2019年1月1日實現。<ref name="New Fox Date">{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2018/biz/news/disney-fox-deal-will-be-ready-to-close-jan-1-says-peter-rice-exclusive-1202976061|title=Fox Will Be Ready to Close Disney Deal Jan. 1, Says Peter Rice (EXCLUSIVE)|author=Holloway|first=Daniel|work=Variety|date=October 10, 2018|accessdate=November 21, 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181011133308/https://variety.com/2018/biz/news/disney-fox-deal-will-be-ready-to-close-jan-1-says-peter-rice-exclusive-1202976061/|archivedate=October 11, 2018}}</ref> 2018年11月14日透露，新独立的公司将保持原来的"福斯"名称。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2018/tv/news/21st-century-fox-new-name-disney-1203028619/|title=Official Name of New Fox Is, Uh, Fox – and Other Takeaways From Annual Stockholders Meeting|author=Donnelly|first=Matt|work=Variety|date=November 14, 2018|accessdate=November 15, 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181114233118/https://variety.com/2018/tv/news/21st-century-fox-new-name-disney-1203028619/|archivedate=November 14, 2018}}</ref>

2019年1月7日，福斯公司的登记表已提交[[美国证券交易委员会|美国证券交易委员会]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/01/21st-century-fox-files-registration-statement-sec-new-fox-1202530940/|title=21st Century Fox Files Registration Statement With SEC To Form 'New' Fox|author=Chmielewski|first=Dawn|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=January 7, 2019|accessdate=January 7, 2019}}</ref><ref name="US">{{Cite web|url=https://www.21cf.com/news/21st-century-fox/2019/21st-century-fox-announces-filing-of-registration-statement-on-form-10-for-fox-corporation/|title=21st Century Fox Announces Filing of Registration Statement on Form 10 for Fox Corporation|work=21st Century Fox|date=January 8, 2019|accessdate=January 8, 2019}}</ref>

2019年1月11日，福斯的证券文件中表示没有计划竞标其以前的区域体育网络。<ref name="FSN">{{Cite news|author=Littleton|first=Cynthia|title=Fox Confirms It Won’t Bid on Disney’s Regional Sports Networks|url=https://variety.com/2019/biz/news/fox-disney-regional-sports-networks-bid-1203105421/|work=Variety|date=January 11, 2019|accessdate=January 11, 2019}}</ref>

2019年3月12日，迪士尼宣佈，交易将在2019年3月20日完成。<ref name="spinoffdate-variety">{{Cite web|author=Littleton|first=Cynthia|title=Disney Sets March 20 Closing Date for 21st Century Fox Acquisition|url=https://variety.com/2019/biz/news/disney-march-20-close-21st-century-fox-acquisition-1203161135/|work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|accessdate=March 15, 2019|language=en|date=March 12, 2019}}</ref> 在2019年3月19日，福斯公司正式在[[标准普尔500指数|标普500]]交易，替换21世纪福斯。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.marketwatch.com/story/fox-corp-to-replace-twenty-first-century-fox-on-sp-500-2019-03-14|title=Fox Corp. to replace Twenty-First Century Fox on S&P 500|author=Chang|first=Sue|work=MarketWatch|language=en-US|accessdate=2019-03-19}}</ref> 共和党政治家及前[[美國眾議院議長|议长]][[保羅·萊恩|保罗·瑞恩]]加入福斯董事会。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://variety.com/2019/biz/news/fox-corporation-debut-disney-murdoch-1203165925/|title=Fox Corporation Emerges as Standalone Entity, Paul Ryan Joins Board|author=Steinberg|first=Cynthia Littleton,Brian|date=2019-03-18|work=Variety|language=en|accessdate=2019-03-19}}</ref>

'''福伊·戴维·科勒'''（{{lang-en|'''Foy David Kohler}}'''；{{bd|1909年|2月15日|1990年|12月23日|}}），是[[美国|美国]]外交官，曾進入美國國務院駐外事務處並在加拿大[[温莎_(安大略省)|溫莎]]、南斯拉夫[[贝尔格莱德|贝尔格莱德]]、羅馬尼亞[[布加勒斯特|布加勒斯特]]任職。1935年在[[北卡羅來納州|北卡羅來納州]][[格林斯伯勒_(北卡罗来纳州)|格林斯伯勒]]結婚<ref name="obit">Kohart, Georgia, [http://pauldingcountylibrary.org/ambassadorkohler.htm Foy David Kohler Obituary] Defiance Ohio Crescent-News January 28, 2001</ref>，此後又任職於希臘[[雅典|雅典]]、埃及[[开罗|开罗]]、[[越南|越南]]以及[[玻利維亞|玻利維亞]]<ref name="usem">[http://moscow.usembassy.gov/links/print_ambassadors.php U.S. Ministers and Ambassadors to Russia] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071006000000/http://moscow.usembassy.gov/links/print_ambassadors.php |date=October 6, 2007 }}, American Embassy, Moscow</ref>。[[古巴导弹危机|古巴导弹危机]]期间担任美国驻苏联大使<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.encyclopedia.com/humanities/encyclopedias-almanacs-transcripts-and-maps/kohler-foy-david|title=Kohler, Foy David - Encyclopedia.com|website=www.encyclopedia.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://history.state.gov/departmenthistory/people/kohler-foy-david|title=Foy David Kohler - People - Department History - Office of the Historian|website=history.state.gov}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1990/12/26/obituaries/foy-d-kohler-envoy-to-moscow-during-cuba-crisis-is-dead-at-82.html|title=Foy D. Kohler, Envoy to Moscow During Cuba Crisis, Is Dead at 82|first=Wolfgang|last=Saxon|date=1990-12-26|publisher=|via=NYTimes.com}}</ref>。

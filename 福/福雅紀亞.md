'''福雅紀亞'''（[[英語|英語]]：Fuagea Isle）是一個位於[[吐瓦魯|吐瓦魯]]首都[[富納富提|富納富提]]的一座[[珊瑚礁|珊瑚礁]][[島嶼|島嶼]]。<ref name="TIFun">{{cite book |last1=  |first1= |authorlink1= |title=Map of Funafuti |url= http://www.tuvaluislands.com/maps/m-funafuti1.htm|format= |accessdate=  |year= |publisher=Tuvaluislands.com|location= |language= |isbn=|oclc= |doi= |id= |page=|pages=|chapter= |chapterurl= |quote= |ref= |bibcode= }}</ref><ref name="ANC">{{cite book |last1=  |first1= |authorlink1= |title= Admiralty Nautical Chart 2983 Tuvalu - Funafuti atoll|url= |format= |accessdate=  |year= |publisher= United Kingdom Hydrographic Office (UKHO)|location= |language= |isbn=|oclc= |doi= |id= |page=|pages=|chapter= |chapterurl= |quote= |ref= |bibcode= }}</ref><ref name="PDN">{{cite book|last1=Lal|first1=Andrick|authorlink1=|title=South Pacific Sea Level & Climate Monitoring Project - Funafuti atoll|url=http://www.pacificdisaster.net/oip/FinalReport/Annex/3_Survey%20LDP/Survey_Diagrams_JPACE-TV.pdf|format=|accessdate=|year=|publisher=SPC Applied Geoscience and Technology Division (SOPAC Division of SPC)|location=|language=|isbn=|oclc=|doi=|id=|page=70|pages=|chapter=|chapterurl=|quote=|ref=|bibcode=|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140203011855/http://www.pacificdisaster.net/oip/FinalReport/Annex/3_Survey%20LDP/Survey_Diagrams_JPACE-TV.pdf|archivedate=2014-02-03}}</ref>福雅紀亞在1996年成為自然保護區，目的是保護該地區的動物及植物生態。<ref>[http://www.tuvaluislands.com/islands/funafuti_kogatapu.html Kogatabu Funafuti Conservation Area]</ref>

'''福斯'''（{{lang-en|Foss}}）是[[19世紀|19世紀]][[英國|英國]][[詩人|詩人]][[愛德華·利爾|愛德華·利爾]]飼養的一隻[[虎斑貓|虎斑貓]]，因出現在利爾的許多插圖中而廣為知名<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.britishlibrary.cn/zh-hk/works/book-of-nonsense/|title=愛德華·利爾的《荒誕書》|author=安德鲁·迪克森|work=大英圖書館|accessdate=2018-05-02|language=zh|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170929231750/http://www.britishlibrary.cn/zh-hk/works/book-of-nonsense/|archivedate=2017-09-29}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://alstewart.com/publicfiles/HISTORY_elear.htm|title=Edward Lear|accessdate=2018-05-02|work=alstewart.com|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170817101304/http://alstewart.com/publicfiles/HISTORY_elear.htm|archivedate=2017-08-17}}</ref><ref name=":2" />。

1887年11月，17歲的福斯去世，利爾將牠埋在[[義大利|義大利]][[聖雷莫|聖雷莫]]一座[[花園|花園]]內，並為其設立了墓碑及撰寫[[墓誌銘|墓誌銘]]<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/books/9261071/Edward-Lear-was-the-master-of-glorious-nonsense.html|title=Edward Lear was the master of glorious nonsense|author=|last=Massie|first=Allan|date=2012-05-12|work=|accessdate=2018-05-02|language=en-GB|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170617101400/http://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/books/9261071/Edward-Lear-was-the-master-of-glorious-nonsense.html|archivedate=2017-06-17}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/voices/book-review-oh-bibbles-oh-pips-this-will-set-you-purring-the-literary-companion-to-dogs-christopher-1505969.html|title=BOOK REVIEW / Oh Bibbles, oh Pips, this will set you purring: The|date=1993-11-22|newspaper=The Independent|accessdate=2018-05-30|language=en-GB}}</ref>。僅兩個月後，利爾本人亦於1888年1月逝世<ref name=":1" />。

== 插圖 ==
利爾為福斯畫了不少插圖<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com.tw/books?id=H663SrkVVP8C&q=edward+lear+cat+foss&dq=edward+lear+cat+foss&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjLvoyo6ebaAhWBU7wKHRT4A0U4FBDoAQhSMAc|title=The Illustrated Cat|last=Howard Loxton, Tom Howard|publisher=Chartwell Books|year=1994|isbn=0785801790|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180502214030/https://books.google.com.tw/books?id=H663SrkVVP8C&q=edward+lear+cat+foss&dq=edward+lear+cat+foss&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjLvoyo6ebaAhWBU7wKHRT4A0U4FBDoAQhSMAc|archivedate=2018-05-02}}</ref>；同時福斯也是利爾的荒誕詩作品《[[荒誕書|荒誕書]]》中一些[[貓|貓]]角色的靈感來源，例如《[[貓頭鷹與貓咪|貓頭鷹與貓咪]]》中的貓<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com.tw/books?id=fXoMBgAAQBAJ&pg=PA160&dq=edward+lear+cat+foss&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiw2YaC5ubaAhVKo5QKHfhPCzsQ6AEIPDAD#v=onepage&q=edward%20lear%20cat%20foss&f=false|title=The Cat's Out of the Bag: Truth and lies about cats|last=Max Cryer|publisher=Exisle Publishing|year=2015|isbn=1775592073|location=|pages=160|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180502214029/https://books.google.com.tw/books?id=fXoMBgAAQBAJ&pg=PA160&dq=edward+lear+cat+foss&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiw2YaC5ubaAhVKo5QKHfhPCzsQ6AEIPDAD#v=onepage&q=edward%20lear%20cat%20foss&f=false|archivedate=2018-05-02}}</ref>。在《荒誕書》自序中利爾亦提及了福斯：「他有很多朋友，有俗人也有[[牧師|牧師]]；他的寵物貓名叫老福斯」<ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite book|title=荒誕書|last=愛德華·利爾|publisher=人民文學出版社|year=2004年7月|isbn=7-02-004661-4|location=北京|pages=2}}</ref>。

《[[紐約時報|紐約時報]]》的一篇評論認為利爾畫得最好的漫畫就是有關福斯的插畫<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1995/05/21/books/a-life-of-nonsense.html|title=A Life of Nonsense|author=|last=Parker|first=Peter|date=1995-05-21|work=The New York Times|accessdate=2018-05-30|language=en}}</ref>。

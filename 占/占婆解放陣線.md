'''占婆解放陣線'''（FLC，{{lang-fr|Front pour la libération du Champa}}）是一個已不存在的[[占族|占婆]][[民族主義|民族主義]]組織，活躍於[[越南|越南]][[西原地區|西原地區]]。<ref>{{cite book |last1=Minahan |first1=James B. |title=Encyclopedia of Stateless Nations: Ethnic and National Groups around the World, 2nd Edition: Ethnic and National Groups around the World |date=2016 |publisher=ABC-CLIO |isbn=9781610699549 |page=504 |url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=pGClDAAAQBAJ |accessdate=5 July 2018 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |last1=Schliesinger |first1=Joachim |title=Ethnic Groups of Cambodia Vol 3: Profile of Austro-Thai and Sinitic-Speaking Peoples |date=2015 |publisher=Booksmango |isbn=9781633232402 |page=19 |url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=ayUjBgAAQBAJ |accessdate=5 July 2018 |language=en}}</ref><ref name="Fealy2006">{{cite book|author=Greg Fealy|title=Voices of Islam in Southeast Asia: a contemporary sourcebook|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=mhLgAAAAMAAJ&q=Sihanouk+FULRO+Front&dq=Sihanouk+FULRO+Front&hl=en&sa=X&ei=TLPiU6zhIc7nsASps4C4CQ&ved=0CCIQ6AEwAzge|date=31 May 2006|publisher=Institute of Southeast Asian Studies|isbn=978-981-230-368-4|page=34}}</ref>這個組織於1962年成立，後來於1964年與[[高原解放陣線|高原解放陣線]]（FLHP）、[[下高棉解放陣線|下高棉解放陣線]]（FLKK）合併，組成[[被壓制民族鬥爭統一戰線|被壓制民族鬥爭統一戰線]]（FULRO）。<ref name="Petersen1994">{{cite book|author=Barry Petersen|title=Tiger Men: A Young Australian Soldier Among the Rhade Montagnard of Vietnam|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SXVuAAAAMAAJ|year=1994|publisher=White Orchid Press|isbn=978-974-89212-5-9|page=85}}</ref>

==历史==
[[File:Gijeong-ri_Flag.jpg|thumb]]重达{{Convert|270|kg|lb|0|abbr=on}}，照耀机井洞上空，临近[[板門店|板門店]]。]]

朝鲜政府称机井洞是拥有200家庭的[[集体农场|集体农场]]，拥有幼儿园和中小学网、医院。<ref>''A Sightseeng Guide to Korea'' by Pang hwon Ju & Hwang Bong Hyok, Foreign Languages Publishing House, Pyongyang, DPRK. 1991</ref>韩国方面则称机井洞是1950年建设的无人居住的宣传村庄，用来诱使韩国人投靠，村子中居住的是[[朝鲜人民军|朝鲜人民军]]，负责维护边境上众多的军事设施碉堡和地下掩体。<ref name=tran/><ref name="tour"/><ref name="potts">Potts, Rolf. [http://www.salon.com/1999/02/03/feature_115/ Korea's No-Man's-Land]. [[Salon_(magazine)|Salon]], February 3, 1999</ref><ref>Sullivan, Kevin. [http://www.stat.ualberta.ca/people/schmu/panmunjom.htm Borderline Absurdity: A Fun-Filled Tour of the Korean DMZ]. [[Washington_Post|Washington Post]] Foreign Service, January 11, 1998.</ref>
===旗杆===
韩国政府在臺城洞建设了一个{{Convert|98.4|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}高的旗杆，上面有一面重{{Convert|130|kg|lb|0|adj=on}}的[[韩国国旗|韩国国旗]]。之後，朝鲜政府建设了更高的板门店旗杆，高达{{Convert|160|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}，上面的朝鲜国旗重{{Convert|270|kg|lb|0|abbr=on}}。旗杆距离边境{{Convert|1.2|km|mi|1|abbr=on}}(37°56'30.24"N, 126°40'48.07"E)，于是韩国方面称之为战争旗杆。当时是世界第二高的旗杆，仅次于[[阿塞拜疆|阿塞拜疆]][[巴库|巴库]]国家旗杆广场的{{Convert|162|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}<ref name=potts/><ref>{{cite news |url=http://edition.cnn.com/2002/WORLD/asiapcf/east/02/19/koreas.dmz/ |title=Korea's DMZ: 'Scariest place on Earth' |accessdate=2007-10-23 | date=2002-02-20}}</ref><ref>{{ko icon}} {{cite web|url=http://economy.ohmynews.com/articleview/article_blogview.asp?at_code=370439|title=개성에 '구멍탄' 5만장 배달했습니다|work=economy.ohmynews.com|accessdate=2006-12-06}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。2001年，這两个旗杆都被[[塔吉克斯坦|塔吉克斯坦]]高达{{Convert|165|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}的[[杜尚别|杜尚别]]旗杆超越。

'''沃伦·麦卡洛克'''（November 16, 1898 – September 24, 1969）是美国[[神经科学家|神经科学家]]和[[控制论|控制论]]学者。

== 著作==
麦卡洛克的主要著作有：<ref>His papers now reside in the manuscripts collection of the [[American_Philosophical_Society|American Philosophical Society]].</ref>
* 1965, ''Embodiments of Mind''. MIT Press, Cambridge, MA.
* 1993, ''The complete works of Warren S. McCulloch''. Intersystems Publications: Salinas, CA.

论文：
* 1943, "A Logical Calculus of the Ideas Immanent in Nervous Activity". With: [[Walter_Pitts|Walter Pitts]]. In: ''Bulletin of Mathematical Biophysics'' Vol 5, pp 115–133.
* 1945, "A Heterarchy of Values Determined by the Topology of Nervous Nets". In: ''Bulletin of Mathematical Biophysics'', 7, 1945, 89–93.
* 1959, [https://web.archive.org/web/20110928024235/http://jerome.lettvin.info/lettvin/Jerome/WhatTheFrogsEyeTellsTheFrogsBrain.pdf "What The Frog's Eye Tells The Frog's Brain"]. With [[Jerome_Lettvin|Jerome Lettvin]], [[Humberto_Maturana|H.R. Maturana]] and W.H. Pitts [It is widely known{{Weasel-word|date=April 2013}} that the actual authors of this work were only Lettvin and Maturana.] In: ''Proc. of the I. R. E.'' Vol 47 (11).
* 1969, [http://www.cyberneticians.com/materials/mcculloch-recollections.pdf "Recollections of the Many Sources of Cybernetics"], published in: ASC FORUM Volume VI, Number 2 -Summer 1974.
* 1945, [https://web.archive.org/web/20080509162455/http://www.chilit.org/Published%20Papers/48.%20McCulloch%20--%20Word.PDF "One Word After Another"].
* 1959, [https://web.archive.org/web/20080509163314/http://www.chilit.org/Published%20Papers/56.%20McCulloch%20--%20Delusion.PDF "The Past of a Delusion"].
* 1959, [https://web.archive.org/web/20080509183513/http://www.chilit.org/Published%20Papers/66.%20McCulloch%20--%20Natural%20Fit.PDF "The Natural Fit"].

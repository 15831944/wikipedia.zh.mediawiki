'''迈肯·卡斯佩尔森·法拉'''（{{lang-no|Maiken Caspersen Falla}}，{{bd|1990年|8月13日||}}）来自阿克什胡斯郡费特，是一名挪威女子越野滑雪运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.indre.no/sport/langrenn/maiken-caspersen-falla/spurtslo-sprintesset-fra-fet/s/5-25-47967|script-title=no:Spurtslo sprintesset fra Fet|publisher=Indre|date=2016-12-02|accessdate=2019-01-27}}</ref>。她的双胞胎兄弟Marius同样也是越野滑雪选手<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.aftenposten.no/100Sport/vintersport/langrenn/Tvillingbroren-om-gulljenta---Hun-sliter-med-selvtilliten-231083b.html|script-title=no:Tvillingbroren om gulljenta: - Hun sliter med selvtilliten|publisher=Aftenposten|date=2017-02-23|accessdate=2019-01-27}}</ref>。她在早年时由于训练过多而患上[[哮喘|哮喘]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.expressen.se/sport/langdskidor/caspersen-falla-jag-ar-beroende-av-den/|script-title=sv:Caspersen Falla: "Jag är beroende av den"|publisher=Expressen|date=2016-07-31|accessdate=2019-01-27}}</ref>。2008年，她在[[芬兰|芬兰]][[库萨莫|库萨莫]]完成个人的世界杯分站赛首秀<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/cross-country-skiing/athlete-profile-n3026476-maiken-caspersen-falla.htm|title=Cross-Country Skiing {{!}} Athlete Profile: Maiken Caspersen FALLA|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-27|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180207123036/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/cross-country-skiing/athlete-profile-n3026476-maiken-caspersen-falla.htm|archivedate=2018-04-20}}</ref>。

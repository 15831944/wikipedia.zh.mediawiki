{{Vgname|邪恶老鼠：老鼠的复仇|en=Bad Rats: The Rats' Revenge}}是[[Invent4_Entertainment|Invent4 Entertainment]]于2009年7月20日在[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]]平台发布的一款益智类[[电脑游戏|电脑游戏]]。2010年5月，[[Strategy_First|Strategy First]]购买了该游戏的版权。其续作{{Vgname-nb|邪恶老鼠秀|en=Bad Rats Show}}于2016年7月20日发布，距离本作恰好7年。

== 评价 ==

[[File:Bad_Rats_gameplay_example.png|thumb]]
<!-- non free image [[File:Bad_Rats_gameplay_example.jpg|thumb]] -->

[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]上并没有任何评论家评论该游戏。<ref name="Metacritic">{{cite web | url = http://www.metacritic.com/game/pc/bad-rats-the-rats-revenge/critic-reviews | title = Bad Rats: The Rats Revenge Critic Reviews for PC | website = [[Metacritic|Metacritic]] | publisher = [[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive]] | accessdate = 24 July 2016}}</ref>[[Kotaku|Kotaku]]则认为该游戏“简直烂到要死”（putridly awkward），是款“几乎人人喊打的游戏”（nearly universally reviled）。<ref name="Kotaku">{{cite web | url = http://steamed.kotaku.com/six-years-later-bad-rats-is-still-known-as-steams-shit-1705908382 | title = Six Years Later, ''Bad Rats'' Is Still Known As Steam's Shittiest Game | last = Grayson | first = Nathan | date = 20 May 2015 | website = [[Kotaku|Kotaku]] | publisher = [[Gawker_Media|Gawker Media]] | accessdate = 24 July 2016}}</ref>然而2009年该游戏登陆[[Steam|Steam]]时，由于当时Steam平台对游戏质量审核较为严格，导致游戏受到了特殊程度的关注。直到2015年游戏在社区仍有活跃的玩家。

该游戏收获的用户评价多为正面，或许开启了对低劣游戏给予讽刺性好评的趋向，同时它也成了恶搞其他玩家时经常赠送的礼物。<ref name="Kotaku" />[[Rock,_Paper,_Shotgun|Rock, Paper, Shotgun]]称之为“游戏界内的奇葩”（the gaming equivalent of neckties with kooky prints or fart-scented soap）。<ref name="rps">{{cite web | url = https://www.rockpapershotgun.com/2016/07/20/bad-rats-2-released/ | title = Send In The Clowns: Bad Rats 2 Released | last = O'Connor | first = Alice | date = 20 July 2016 | website = [[Rock,_Paper,_Shotgun|Rock, Paper, Shotgun]] | accessdate = 24 July 2016}}</ref>

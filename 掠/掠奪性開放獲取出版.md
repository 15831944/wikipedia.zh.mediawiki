'''掠奪性開放獲取出版'''（predatory open access publishing）是[[学术出版|学术出版]]的用語，是指剝削性[[開放獲取|開放獲取]]出版的業務模式，是指期刊對作者收取[[版面费|版面费]]，但不提供一般[[學術期刊|學術期刊]]的編輯及出版服務（可能是開放獲取，也可能不是）。Beall's List是{{le|Jeffrey Beall|Jeffrey Beall}}在2017年1月之前定期更新的報告，列出掠奪性出版物分類的準則，並且列出符合該準則的出版商及獨立期刊<ref name=CHE2012>{{cite web |last=Elliott |first=Carl |title=On Predatory Publishers: a Q&A With Jeffrey Beall |website=Brainstorm |publisher=[[The_Chronicle_of_Higher_Education|The Chronicle of Higher Education]] |date=2012-06-05 |url=http://chronicle.com/blogs/brainstorm/on-predatory-publishers-a-qa-with-jeffrey-beall/47667}}</ref>。不過Jeffrey Beall自2017年1月起已不再發布Beall's List<ref>{{cite web |last=Silver |first = Andrew |title=Controversial website that lists ‘predatory’ publishers shuts down |website = Nature.com |publisher = Nature Publishing |date=January 18, 2017 |url=http://www.nature.com/news/controversial-website-that-lists-predatory-publishers-shuts-down-1.21328}}</ref>。<!--(see below for more details; the list  had 1155 inclusions as of 31 December 2016).-->有說法指出，[[開發中國家|開發中國家]]的新進學者有成為這類出版模式受害者的風險<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1002/nur.21640|title=Predatory Publishing: What Authors Need to Know|journal=Research in Nursing & Health|volume=38|pages=1–3|year=2015|last1=Kearney|first1=Margaret H.}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1002/asi.23265|title=Who publishes in "predatory" journals?|journal=Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology|pages=1406–1417|year=2014|last1=Xia|first1=Jingfeng|last2=Harmon|first2=Jennifer L.|last3=Connolly|first3=Kevin G.|last4=Donnelly|first4=Ryan M.|last5=Anderson|first5=Mary R.|last6=Howard|first6=Heather A.|volume=66|issue=7}}</ref>。

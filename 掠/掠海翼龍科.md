'''掠海翼龍科'''（Thalassodromidae）是[[翼龍目|翼龍目]][[翼手龍亞目|翼手龍亞目]][[神龍翼龍超科|神龍翼龍超科]]的一科，生存於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期的[[巴西|巴西]]，目前僅包含兩個屬：[[掠海翼龍|掠海翼龍]]、[[妖精翼龍|妖精翼龍]]<ref name=KC07>{{cite journal |last=Kellner |first=A.W.A. |coauthors=and Campos, D.A. |year=2007 |title=Short note on the ingroup relationships of the Tapejaridae (Pterosauria, Pterodactyloidea |journal=Boletim do Museu Nacional |volume=75 |pages=1–14 }}</ref>。

==分類==
古生物學界對於掠海翼龍科的分類位置，目前仍有爭議，目前分成兩派。[[呂君昌|呂君昌]]等人認為這些物種較接近[[神龍翼龍科|神龍翼龍科]]，而離[[古神翼龍科|古神翼龍科]]較遠<ref name=luetal2008>Lü, J., Unwin, D.M., Xu, L., and Zhang, X. (2008). "A new azhdarchoid pterosaur from the Lower Cretaceous of China and its implications for pterosaur phylogeny and evolution." ''Naturwissenschaften'',</ref>；並將他們建立為獨立的掠海翼龍科、或者是[[妖精翼龍科|妖精翼龍科]]<ref name="martilletal2007">Martill, D.M., Bechly, G., and Heads, S.W. (2007). "Appendix: species list for the Crato Formation." In: Martill, D.M., Bechly, G., and Loveridge, R.F. (eds.), 2007. ''The Crato Fossil Beds of Brazil: Window into an Ancient World.'' Cambridge University Press, Cambridge. Pp. 582–607.</ref>但掠海翼龍科的命名時間較早<ref name=KC07/>。另一派分類法則認為牠們是古神翼龍科的近親，並將相關物種歸類於古神翼龍科的一個亞科，掠海翼龍亞科（Thalassodrominae）<ref name=pinheiroetal2011>Pinheiro, F.L., Fortier, D.C., Schultz, C.L., De Andrade, J.A.F.G. and Bantim, R.A.M. (in press). "New information on ''Tupandactylus imperator'', with comments on the relationships of Tapejaridae (Pterosauria)." ''Acta Palaeontologica Polonica'', in press, available online 03 Jan 2011. {{doi|10.4202/app.2010.0057}}</ref>。

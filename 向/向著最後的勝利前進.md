《'''向著最後的勝利前進'''》（{{lang-ko|최후의 승리를 향하여 앞으로}}）是一首歌頌[[朝鮮|朝鮮]]最高領導人[[金正恩|金正恩]]的歌曲，由[[尹斗根|尹斗根]]（윤두근）作曲，[[金文革|金文革]]作詞，自2012年7月以來推出宣傳。<ref name="The Atlantic 5 July 2012">{{cite news|last=Sim|first=Shuan|title=Kim Jong Un's Official New Theme Song, Translated|url=http://www.theatlantic.com/international/archive/2012/07/kim-jong-uns-official-new-theme-song-translated/259475/|accessdate=7 July 2012|newspaper=[[The_Atlantic|The Atlantic]]|date=5 July 2012}}</ref>

歌詞內容摘自金正恩在[[金日成|金日成]]誕辰100週年[[閱兵式|閱兵式]]上的講話，但金正恩本人的名字未被提及。<ref name="The Guardian (Branigan) 6 July 2012">{{cite news|last=Branigan|first=Tania|title=North Korea's Kim Jong-un gets new official theme song|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2012/jul/06/north-korea-kim-jong-un-song|accessdate=7 July 2012|newspaper=The Guardian|date=6 July 2012}}</ref>“[[強盛大國|強盛大國]]”的觀念仍被強化，但不再以“大國”而是以“國家”稱之。<ref name="The Atlantic 5 July 2012" />

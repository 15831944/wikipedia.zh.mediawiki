'''葛列戈里·保羅·柏德'''（{{lang-en|Gregory Paul Bird}}, {{bd|1992年|11月9日}}），為美國職棒大聯盟紐約洋基的一壘手。於2015年8月13日初登板。

==職業生涯==
===小聯盟===
2011年大聯盟選秀，柏德於第5輪以捕手身分被紐約洋基選中。<ref>{{cite web|author=Ryan CaseyThe Denver Post |url=http://www.denverpost.com/ci_18222725 |title=Grandview's Greg Bird drafted by Yankees in fifth round |work=The Denver Post |accessdate=November 16, 2013}}</ref>後來轉戰一壘手。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.postandcourier.com/article/20130626/PC20/130629452/charleston-riverdogs-first-baseman-greg-bird-x2018-mature-beyond-his-years-x2019 |title=Charleston RiverDogs first baseman Greg Bird ‘mature beyond his years’ |publisher=Postandcourier.com |date=June 26, 2013 |accessdate=November 16, 2013}}</ref>

2011年，柏德在新人聯盟繳出打擊三圍.337/.450/.494的成績。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.newsday.com/sports/baseball/yankees/yankees-farmhand-greg-bird-has-command-of-the-strike-zone-1.5910319 |title=Yankees farmhand Greg Bird has command of the strike zone |work=Newsday |date=August 17, 2013 |accessdate=November 16, 2013}}</ref>2013年，柏德在1A的打擊三圍為.288/.428/.511，並擊出20支全壘打，獲得107次保送。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://newyork.yankees.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20140321&content_id=69835294&vkey=pr_nyy&c_id=nyy|title=Yankees name INF Greg Bird and RHP Shane Greene winners of the 2013 Kevin Lawn 'Player of the Year' and "Pitcher of the Year" Awards|work=New York Yankees|accessdate=November 15, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2014/03/yankees_spring_training_greg_bird_shane_greene_2013_minor_league_players_of_the_year.html|title=Yankees spring training: Greg Bird, Shane Greene 2013 minor league players of the year|work=The Star-Ledger|accessdate=November 15, 2014}}</ref>

2014年，柏德在高階1A的打擊率為2成77，擊出7支全壘打，32分打點，並升上了2A。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.trentonian.com/sports/20140805/thunder-hoping-bird-can-produce-same-spark-as-departed-obrien|title=Thunder hoping Bird can produce same spark as departed OBrien|work=The Trentonian|date=August 5, 2014|accessdate=November 15, 2014}}</ref>2A球季結束後他升上秋季聯盟。柏德在亞利桑那秋季聯盟明星賽擊出了450英尺的全壘打。該季他打出6支全壘打領先整個秋季聯盟，最終獲選MVP。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.yankees.mlb.com/news/article/101542126/yankees-greg-bird-named-arizona-fall-league-mvp|title=Yankees' Bird named Arizona Fall League MVP|work=New York Yankees|accessdate=November 15, 2014}}</ref>

===紐約洋基===
====2015年:菜鳥年====
2015年8月13日，洋基宣布將柏德升上大聯盟，做為[[馬克·塔克薛拉|馬克·塔克薛拉]]的候補。<ref name="callup">{{cite web|url=https://nypost.com/2015/08/13/yankees-to-call-up-top-prospect-greg-bird/|title=Yankees to call up top prospect Greg Bird|work=New York Post|accessdate=August 13, 2015}}</ref>當晚，柏德即在大聯盟亮相，但是5個打數無安打。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.northjersey.com/sports/baseball/yankees/yankees-notes-greg-bird-makes-big-league-debut-1.1392476|title=Yankees notes: Greg Bird makes big-league debut|publisher=NorthJersey.com|accessdate=August 18, 2015}}</ref>8月15日，柏德面對藍鳥隊後援投手LaTroy Hawkins擊出大聯盟首安。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://blogs.denverpost.com/rockies/2015/08/15/watch-greg-bird-former-grandview-star-gets-1st-mlb-hit/20737/|title=WATCH: Greg Bird, former Grandview star, gets 1st MLB hit|work=On the Rox|accessdate=August 18, 2015}}</ref>8月17日，[[馬克·塔克薛拉|馬克·塔克薛拉]]因觸身球造成腿部受傷而該季提前報銷，柏德便成為洋基先發一壘手。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Martin|first1=Daniel|title=Mark Teixeira done for 2015 after MRI reveals leg fracture.|url=https://nypost.com/2015/09/11/mark-teixeira-done-for-year-after-mri-reveals-leg-fracture/|work=New York Post|accessdate=September 11, 2015}}</ref>

8月19日，面對雙城隊投手[[厄文·桑塔那|厄文·桑塔那]]，柏德擊出大聯盟首轟。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nypost.com/2015/08/20/why-greg-birds-coming-out-party-is-no-surprise-to-yankees/|title=Homers not only reason Yankees drooling over Bird’s potential|work=[[New_York_Post|New York Post]]|first=Kevin|last= Kernan|date=August 20, 2015|accessdate=August 20, 2015}}</ref>9月22日，柏德在10局上面對藍鳥隊擊出致勝3分砲。該季他打擊率2成61，3成43上壘率。該年46場比賽157個打席，柏德擊出11支全壘打，31分打點。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fangraphs.com/statss.aspx?playerid=14131&position=1B|title=Gregory Bird » Statistics » Batting {{!}} FanGraphs Baseball|website=www.fangraphs.com|access-date=April 1, 2017}}</ref>

====2016年====
2015-16的休賽季，柏德右肩膀受傷，後檢驗出來為肩盂脣撕裂，整季報銷。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Miller|first1=Randy|title=19 shares Yankees' Greg Bird has 'awesome' time in Fall League opener, but still not 100 percent|url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2016/10/yankees_greg_bird_loves_playing_arizona_fall_leagu.html|website=The Star-Ledger|accessdate=October 31, 2016}}</ref>

====2017年====
春訓時，柏德展現他傷癒後的打擊水準，擊出8支全壘打，和[[布萊斯·哈波|布萊斯·哈波]]並列第一。在表演賽中，柏德在[[太陽信託球場|太陽信託球場]]擊出首轟。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.cbssports.com/mlb/news/watch-suntrust-parks-first-home-runs-come-from-greg-bird-freddie-freeman/|title=WATCH: SunTrust Park's first home runs come from Greg Bird, Freddie Freeman|publisher=CBSSports.com|access-date=April 1, 2017|language=en}}</ref>

5月2日，柏德因為右腳踝擦傷休息10天。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Macklin|first1=Oliver|title=Greg Bird goes on DL|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/227805990/yankees-place-greg-bird-on-disabled-list/|publisher=Major League Baseball|accessdate=May 2, 2017}}</ref>7月，柏德右腳三角骨受傷，因此手術切除骨頭。<ref>http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2017/07/18/greg-bird-yankees-ankle-surgery</ref>8月26日，柏德從60天傷兵名單回歸。例行賽結束，他擊出8支全壘打，打擊率2成53。

[[2017年美國聯盟分區賽|2017年美國聯盟分區賽]]第三戰，柏德面對印地安人最強牛棚投手[[安德魯·米勒_(棒球員)|安德魯·米勒]]擊出超前陽春砲，這也僅僅是米勒三年來被左打者打的第2轟而已，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cbssports.com/mlb/news/indians-yankees-alds-game-3-the-greg-bird-home-run-that-was-the-difference/|title=Indians-Yankees ALDS Game 3: The Greg Bird home run that was the difference|work=CBSSports.com|access-date=2017-12-26|language=en}}</ref>最終洋基1比0贏球。[[2017年美國聯盟冠軍賽|2017年美國聯盟冠軍賽]]最終戰，柏德擊出飛球出局，洋基無緣世界大賽。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/10/21/sports/baseball/world-series-astros-yankees.html|title=Astros Move On to World Series as the Yankees Run Out of Gas|last=Witz|first=Billy|date=2017-10-21|work=The New York Times|access-date=2017-12-26|language=en-US|issn=0362-4331}}</ref>該年季後賽，柏德的打擊三圍為.244/.426/.512，敲出3支全壘打，6分打點。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/player/gamelog/_/id/32695/greg-bird|title=Greg Bird|website=ESPN.com|access-date=2017-12-26}}</ref>

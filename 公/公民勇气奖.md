'''公民勇气奖'''（{{lang-en|'''Civil Courage Prize'''}}）2000年由纽约特雷恩基金会建立的人权奖，奖给那些“冒着极大的个人危险坚决抵抗黑暗势力”的个人或组织。<ref name=CCP>{{cite web |url=http://www.civilcourageprize.org/index.htm |title=Civil Courage Prize |date=2012 |publisher=Civil Courage Prize |archivedate=2012-08-11 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/69qPLl2L5?url=http://www.civilcourageprize.org/index.htm |accessdate=August 10, 2012 |dead-url=no }}</ref> 公民勇气奖不是为了给人排名而是让世人关注具有良知的英雄，<ref name=CCP /> 它的灵感来源自[[苏联|苏联]][[持不同政见者|持不同政见者]][[亚历山大·索尔仁尼琴|亚历山大·索尔仁尼琴]]。<ref name=FN>{{cite web |url=http://www.foxnews.com/story/0,2933,455108,00.html |title=Egyptian Playwright Awarded Prize for Fight for Peace |date=November 20, 2008 |publisher=Fox News |archivedate=2012-08-11 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/69qYRcI9D?url=http://www.foxnews.com/story/0,2933,455108,00.html |accessdate=August 11, 2012 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>

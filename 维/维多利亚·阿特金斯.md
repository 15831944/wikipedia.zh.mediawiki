'''维多利亚·玛丽·阿特金斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Victoria Mary Atkins'''}} {{Post-nominals|country=GBR|size=100%|MP}}，{{bd|1976年|3月22日}}）<ref name="telegraph.co.uk">{{cite news | last = Jamieson | first = Sophie | url = http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/general-election-2015/11568431/Parliaments-future-female-front-bench-stars.html | title = Female MPs: Parliament’s future front bench stars | date = 29 April 2015 | work = [[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]] | location = London | publisher = [[Telegraph_Media_Group|Telegraph Media Group]] | accessdate = 25 May 2015 }}</ref>是一位英国政治人物，[[保守党|保守党]]党员。2015年起一直是代表[[劳斯和合恩塞_(英国国会选区)|劳斯和合恩塞选区]]的下院议员。

== 早年经历和教育 ==
阿特金斯是前保守党政府国家遗产部{{le|体育政务次官|Minister for Sport and Civil Society|体育副大臣}}和[[歐洲議會議員|欧洲议会议员]]{{le|罗伯特·阿特金斯 (政治人物)|Robert Atkins (politician)|罗伯特·阿特金斯}}的女儿。阿特金斯在位于[[兰开夏郡|兰开夏郡]][[布莱克浦|布莱克浦]]的独立学校--{{le|阿诺德学校|Arnold School}}受教育，该校为男女同校，其后在剑桥大学基督圣体学院学习[[英国法系|法律]]。后又在[[中殿律师学院|中殿律师学院]]学习，1998年被{{le|接纳为律师的仪式|Call to the bar|授予律师资格}}。<ref name=teleg>[http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/general-election-2015/11568431/Parliaments-future-female-front-bench-stars.html Profile], telegraph.co.uk; accessed 10 May 2015.</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://thebardirectory.co.uk/legalhub/app/init/signon?redirect=%252Flegalhub%252Fapp%252Fdocument%253Frs%253D%2526vr%253D%2526src%253Dri%2526docguid%253DI0368bd108bcb11dcaa9aaaaa0e13eb96%2526suppsrguid%253Di0ad69f8e000001553c04468a7519c4f0%2526spos%253D3%2526epos%253D3%2526td%253D841%2526ctype%253DThe%252520Bar%252520Directory%2526bctocguid%253DI07d24b008bc611dc8bbd8c0e07b9dd39%2526bchistory%253D5%253B6%253B%2526ststate%253DS%253BS%253BS%2526linktype%253Dref%2526querylink%253Dtrue%2526toc-search%253Dtrue&vr=&vr=1.0&wluk-usertype=&wluk-usertype=&rs=&rs=BOL1.0 |title=Victoria Mary Atkins | Barrister | Barrister & Expert Witness Directory | Legal Hub |publisher=Thebardirectory.co.uk |date= |accessdate=3 April 2017}}</ref>

== 早年政治事业 ==
成为国会议员前，阿特金斯一直在伦敦作为[[大律师|大律师]]执业，业务领域是反欺诈。<ref name=teleg/><ref>Simmons, Richard, [http://l2b.thelawyer.com/home/insight/city-solicitors-a-22-year-old-ukip-law-grad-and-a-barrister-standing-for-the-whigs-the-legal-professions-mp-hopefuls/3033862.article Meet the lawyers standing for Parliament], ''Lawyer 2B'', 10 April 2015</ref>

2010年她曾入围保守党[[安全选区|安全选区]][[索尔兹伯里_(英国国会选区)|索尔兹伯里]]的党内候选人名单。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=BVWqCQAAQBAJ&pg=PT176#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=The Politicos Guide to the New House of Commons 2015: Profiles of the New MPs and Analysis of the 2015 General Election Results|first=Tim|last=Carr|date=18 May 2015|publisher=Biteback Publishing|accessdate=18 June 2017|via=Google Books}}</ref>{{le|2012年英格兰和威尔士警察和犯罪专员选举|England and Wales police and crime commissioner elections, 2012|2012年}}曾出马参选{{le|格洛斯特郡警察|Gloucestershire Constabulary|格洛斯特郡警察和罪案专员}}，但失利。<ref>{{cite news | title = Police and Crime Commissioner for Gloucestershire election results | url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-19478233 | date = 16 November 2012 | work = [[BBC_News|BBC News]] | location = London | publisher = BBC | accessdate = 25 May 2015 }}</ref>

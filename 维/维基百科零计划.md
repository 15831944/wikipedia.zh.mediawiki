'''维基百科零计划'''（{{lang-en|Wikipedia Zero}}）是[[维基媒体基金会|维基媒体基金会]]一个项目，通过与移动运营商合作为用户提供在[[智能手机|智能手机]]上免除流量费用访问[[维基百科|维基百科]]的服务，特别是在[[发展中国家|发展中国家]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.technobuffalo.com/2013/02/22/wikipedia-zero-sms/|title=Wikipedia Zero Wants to Bring Wikipedia to Mobile Users Without a Data Plan|first=Brandon|last=Russell|accessdate=2013-04-08|date=2013-02-22|work=''TechnoBuffalo''}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.knightfoundation.org/blogs/knightblog/2013/2/22/getting-wikipedia-people-who-need-it-most/|title=Getting Wikipedia to the people who need it most|date=2013-02-22|publisher=Knight Foundation|accessdate=2013-04-08|first=Kul Takanao|last=Wadhwa|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130704041945/http://www.knightfoundation.org/blogs/knightblog/2013/2/22/getting-wikipedia-people-who-need-it-most/|archivedate=2013-07-04}}</ref>。该计划在2012年提出<ref>{{Cite journal|url=http://www.popularmechanics.com/how-to/blog/sxsw-wikipedia-for-non-smartphones-is-brilliant-here-s-why-15189767|title=SXSW: Wikipedia for Non-Smartphones Is Brilliant. Here's Why|accessdate=2013-04-08|date=2013-03-08|first=Erik|last=Sofge|work=[[Popular_Mechanics|Popular Mechanics]]}}</ref>，2013年3月该计划赢得了赢得[[西南偏南|西南偏南]]的“互动”奖<ref>{{cite news |url = http://www.austinchronicle.com/blogs/sxsw/2013-03-12/sxsw-interactive-awards-announced/ |title=SXSW Interactive Awards Announced|first=Monica|last=Riese|accessdate=2013-04-08|date=2013-03-12|work=[[The_Austin_Chronicle|The Austin Chronicle]]|location=Austin, Texas|issn=1074-0740|publisher=Austin Chronicle Corp.}}</ref>。该计划的目的是减少访问自由数据库的成本。不过此计划多年来也受到了违背[[网络中立|网络中立]]原则的批评<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />。2018年2月，维基媒体基金会宣布结束此倡议，将改为采取新的合作方式<ref name=":2" />。

{{tsl|en|Facebook Zero}}计划据称灵感来自维基百科零计划<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dw.de/wikipedia-zero-free-data-if-you-can-afford-it/a-17304321|title = Wikipedia Zero: free data if you can afford it|last = Dillon|first = Conon|date = 2013-12-18|accessdate = 2014-01-15}}</ref>。

==发展==
维基百科零计划最早于2012年5月于[[马来西亚|马来西亚]]推出<ref>{{cite web|url=http://blog.wikimedia.org/2012/05/26/wikipedia-zero-launches-in-malaysia-with-digi/ |title=Wikipedia Zero launches in Malaysia with Digi — Wikimedia blog |publisher=Blog.wikimedia.org |date=2012-05-26 |accessdate=2013-06-27}}</ref>。2012年10月，DTAC和沙特电信公司分别在[[泰国|泰国]]与[[沙特阿拉伯|沙特阿拉伯]]推出该服务。2013年5月，Mobilink在[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]推出该服务<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.nation.com.pk/pakistan-news-newspaper-daily-english-online/business/05-Jun-2013/mobilink-brings-wikipedia-zero-to-pakistan |title=Mobilink brings Wikipedia Zero to Pakistan |publisher=Nation.com.pk |date= |accessdate=2013-06-27 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130622082157/http://www.nation.com.pk/pakistan-news-newspaper-daily-english-online/business/05-Jun-2013/mobilink-brings-wikipedia-zero-to-pakistan |archivedate=2013-06-22 }}</ref>。2013年6月，Dialog Axiata在[[斯里兰卡|斯里兰卡]]推出该服务<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.dialog.lk/personal/mobile/features-and-vas/information-services/wikipedia-free/ |title=Dialog brings Wikipedia Zero to Sri Lanka |publisher=www.dialog.lk |date= |accessdate=2013-06-24 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130715111202/http://www.dialog.lk/personal/mobile/features-and-vas/information-services/wikipedia-free/ |archivedate=2013-07-15 }}</ref>。Aircel集团在[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]]、马来西亚、[[柬埔寨|柬埔寨]]、斯里兰卡和[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]]等国推出了该服务，其中在印度尼西亚、柬埔寨、孟加拉国为首次<ref>{{cite web |url =

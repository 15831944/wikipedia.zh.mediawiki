'''维生素缺乏症'''（英文：{{lang|en|'''Avitaminosis'''}}）是由[[维生素|维生素]]缺乏或者代谢转化（例如[[色氨酸|色氨酸]]转[[烟酸|烟酸]]过程）缺损所引起的一系列慢性或长期的[[疾病|疾病]]，这类疾病根据与其有关的维生素的字母进行定名<ref>{{cite book |title=Vitamins in Animal and Human Nutrition |author=Lee Russell McDowell |publisher=Wiley-Blackwell |edition=2 ed. |year=2000 |isbn=0-8138-2630-6}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |title=Human-milk intoxication due to B1 avitaminosis |author=Lydia Fehily |journal=British Medical Journal |pages=509- |year=1944 |url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2286425/pdf/brmedj03910-0006.pdf |pmc=2286425 |pmid=20785731 |volume=2 |issue=4374}}</ref>。

与此相反的是[[维生素过多症|维生素过多症]]，这类疾病主要是体内摄入过量脂溶性维生素造成的<ref>{{cite journal | url=http://www.jbc.org/content/239/4/1266.full.pdf | title=Effects of Hypervitaminosis A and D on Skeletal Metabolism | author=Clark I, Smith MR | journal=The Journal of Biological Chemistry |date=1964年4月}}</ref>。

== 类型 ==
维生素缺乏症包含了一些几种类型：
* [[维生素A缺乏症|维生素A缺乏症]]：造成[[干眼病|干眼病]]和[[夜盲症|夜盲症]]<ref name="WHO Vitamin A deficiency - Micronutrient deficiencies">{{cite web |url=http://www.who.int/nutrition/topics/vad/en/ |title=WHO Vitamin A deficiency - Micronutrient deficiencies |accessdate=2008-03-03 |format= |work=}}</ref><ref name="isbn92-5-103818-X">{{cite book |author=Latham, Michael E. |title=Human Nutrition in the Developing World (Fao Food and Nutrition Paper) |publisher=Food & Agriculture Organization of the United |location= |year= 1997|pages= |isbn=92-5-103818-X |oclc= |doi=}}</ref><ref name="isbn92-4-154478-3">{{cite book |author=Sommer, Alfred |title=Vitamin a Deficiency and Its Consequences: A Field Guide to Detection and Control |publisher=World Health Organization |location=Geneva |year= 1995|pages= |isbn=92-4-154478-3 |oclc= |doi=}}</ref><ref name="A world fit for children">{{cite web |url=http://www.unicef.org/worldfitforchildren/files/A-RES-S27-2E.pdf |title=A world fit for children |accessdate=2008-03-03 |format= |work=}}</ref>
* [[硫胺素缺乏症|硫胺素（维生素B<sub>1</sub>）缺乏症]]：造成[[脚气病|脚气病]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmedhealth/PMH0001379/ | title=Beriberi - PubMed Health | publisher= National Center for Biotechnology Information| accessdate=2012-08-26}}</ref>
* [[烟酸缺乏症|烟酸（维生素B<sub>3</sub>）缺乏症]]：造成[[糙皮病|糙皮病]]
* [[维生素B12缺乏症|维生素B<sub>12</sub>缺乏症]]：造成[[巨幼红细胞性贫血|巨幼红细胞性贫血]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/000574.htm | title=Anemia - B12 deficiency: MedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia | publisher=MedlinePlus | accessdate=2012-08-26}}</ref>和[[脊髓亚急性联合变性|脊髓亚急性联合变性]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/000723.htm | title=Subacute combined degeneration: MedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia | publisher=MedlinePlus | accessdate=2012-08-26}}</ref>
* [[维生素C缺乏症|维生素C缺乏症]]：造成[[坏血病|坏血病]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/002404.htm | title=Vitamin C: MedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia | publisher=MedlinePlus | accessdate=2012-08-26}}</ref>
* [[维生素D缺乏症|维生素D缺乏症]]：造成[[佝偻病|佝偻病]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmedhealth/PMH0001384/ | title=Rickets - PubMed Health | publisher=National Center for Biotechnology Information | accessdate=2012-08-26}}</ref>
* [[维生素K缺乏症|维生素K缺乏症]]：造成[[凝血|凝血]]障碍<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/002407.htm | title=Vitamin K: MedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia | publisher=MedlinePlus | accessdate=2012-08-26}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.umm.edu/altmed/articles/vitamin-k-000343.htm | title=Vitamin K | publisher=University of Maryland Medical Center | accessdate=2012-08-26}}</ref>

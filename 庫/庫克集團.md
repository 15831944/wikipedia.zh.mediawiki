'''庫克集團股份有限公司'''係一家總部位於美國[[印第安納州|印第安納州]][[布盧明頓|布盧明頓]]的私人控股公司。其主要從事醫療器械製造。該公司在[[福布斯|福布斯]]「2008年美國最大的私營公司」排行榜中排第324位<ref name="forbes-privates08">{{cite web | url = http://www.forbes.com/lists/2008/21/privates08_Cook-Group_6V9R.html | title = #324 Cook Group | date = 2008-11-03 | work = [[Forbes.com|Forbes.com]]: America's Largest Private Companies | publisher = Forbes.com LLC | location = [[New_York_City|New York City]] | accessdate = 2009-10-19 }}</ref>。它有三個主要部門：庫克醫療公司、聯合製造、及聯營公司。所有這三個部門都是由若干附屬公司所組成<ref name="cook-profile">{{cite web | url = http://www.cookgroup.com/profile/ | title = Corporate Profile | publisher = Cook Group Incorporated | location = Bloomington, Indiana | date = 2009-10-01 | accessdate = 2009-10-19 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110609001637/http://www.cookgroup.com/profile/ | archivedate = 2011-06-09 }}</ref>。

該公司的母公司，庫克股份有限公司於1963年在[[布盧明頓|布盧明頓]]成立<ref name="cook-history">{{cite web | url = http://www.cookgroup.com/history/index.html | title = History & Innovators | publisher = Cook Group Incorporated | location = Bloomington, Indiana | date = 2009-10-01 | accessdate = 2009-10-19 }}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。上述公司最初經營[[導管|導管]]，[[針頭|針頭]]和線導向器等器械。如今，該公司的產品已銷往135個國家.<ref>{{cite web|title=Clinical Divisions at a Glance|url=https://www.cookmedical.com/divisions|publisher=Cook Medical|accessdate=12 February 2014}}</ref> 。

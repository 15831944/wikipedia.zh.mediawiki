'''庫佛氏細胞'''（{{lang-en|Kupffer cell、Browicz-Kupffer cell、stellate macrophages}}，亦称为'''肝巨噬细胞'''）是位於[[肝臟|肝臟]]中的特殊[[巨噬細胞|巨噬細胞]]，是[[单核吞噬细胞系统|单核吞噬细胞系统]]（mononuclear phagocyte system）的一部分。

==歷史==
庫佛氏細胞最早於1876年由[[波羅的海德意志人|波羅的海德意志]]解剖學家Karl Wilhelm von Kupffer無意中發現<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Haubrich | first1 = WS. | author-name-separator= | year = 2004 | title = Kupffer of Kupffer cells | url = | journal = Gastroenterology | volume = 127 | issue = 1| page = 16 | pmid = 15236167 }}</ref>。他在以[[印度墨|印度墨]]染兔肝時發現星狀的細胞，誤以為是[[肝臟星狀細胞|肝臟星狀細胞]]，並認定是肝血管[[內皮|內皮]]的一部分，直到1898年，波蘭科學家Tadeusz Browicz才重新確認它們屬於[[巨噬細胞|巨噬細胞]]<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Szymańska | first1 = R | last2 = Schmidt-Pospuła | first2 = M | title = Studies of liver's reticuloendothelial cells by Tadeusz Browicz and Karl Kupffer. A historical outline | journal = Archiwum historii medycyny | volume = 42 | issue = 3 | pages = 331–6 | year = 1979 | pmid = 386989  | author-name-separator= }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Stachura | first1 = J | last2 = Gałazka | first2 = K | title = History and current status of Polish gastroenterological pathology. | journal = Journal of physiology and pharmacology : an official journal of the Polish Physiological Society | volume = 54 Suppl 3 | pages = 183–92 | year = 2003 | pmid = 15075472  | author-name-separator= }}</ref>。

==發育==
庫佛氏細胞的發育來自[[骨髓|骨髓]]，由[[前單核球|前單核球]]（promonocytes）、[[原單核球|原單核球]]（Monoblast）、[[單核球|單核球]]發育而來。血液中的單核球再進一步在肝竇壁發育成庫佛氏細胞<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Naito | first1 = M | last2 = Hasegawa | first2 = G | last3 = Takahashi | first3 = K | title = Development, differentiation, and maturation of Kupffer cells. | journal = Microscopy Research and Technique | volume = 39 | issue = 4 | pages = 350–64 | year = 1997 | pmid = 9407545  | author-name-separator= | doi = 10.1002/(SICI)1097-0029(19971115)39:4<350::AID-JEMT5>3.0.CO;2-L }}</ref>。

==功能==
[[紅血球|紅血球]]在肝中被庫佛氏細胞吞噬後遭到裂解，[[血紅素|血紅素]]分子也被分解，球蛋白將被分解成[[胺基酸|胺基酸]]而回收，含有鐵的[[血基質|血基質]]會進一步被分解釋出鐵離子與[[膽紅素|膽紅素]]，鐵離子被回收，膽紅素則在[[肝細胞|肝細胞]]（Hepatocyte）中與[[葡糖醛酸|葡糖醛酸]]結合，並由[[膽汁|膽汁]]釋出。

Helmy等人在庫佛氏細胞表面發現了一種稱為[[CRIg|CRIg]]的受體（Complement receptor of the immunoglobulin family），缺乏CRIg的老鼠之庫佛氏細胞無法正常清除循環系統中被[[補體|補體]]結合的病原。CRIg在人類與老鼠中都在[[先天免疫|先天免疫]]扮演著重要的角色<ref>{{cite journal | author = Helmy K, Katschke K, Gorgani N, Kljavin N, Elliott J, Diehl L, Scales S, Ghilardi N, van Lookeren Campagne M | title = CRIg: a macrophage complement receptor required for phagocytosis of circulating pathogens | journal = Cell | volume = 124 | issue = 5 | pages = 915–27 | year = 2006 | pmid = 16530040 | doi = 10.1016/j.cell.2005.12.039}}</ref>。

==在酒精性肝病變中的角色==
庫佛氏細胞的活化在早期的{{link-en|酒精性肝病變|Alcoholic liver disease}}扮演著重要角色。庫佛氏細胞的{{link-en|TLR4|TLR4}}受體與{{link-en|CD14|CD14}}受體被活化，促進許多造成發炎的[[細胞因子|細胞因子]][[轉譯|轉譯]]（如[[腫瘤壞死因子α|腫瘤壞死因子α]]）及[[超氧化物|超氧化物]]的製造。腫瘤壞死因子α可造成[[肝臟星狀細胞|肝臟星狀細胞]]大量合成[[膠原蛋白|膠原蛋白]]，造成肝臟的[[纖維化|纖維化]]，肝的纖維化最終造成[[肝硬化|肝硬化]]並失去功能<ref>{{cite journal|author = Michael D. Wheeler, Ph.D.  | title = Endotoxin and Kupffer Cell Activation in Alcoholic Liver Disease. | journal = National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism of the NIH | year = 2004 }}</ref>。

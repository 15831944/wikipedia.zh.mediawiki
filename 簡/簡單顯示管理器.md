'''简单显示管理器'''（{{lang-en|Simple Desktop Display Manager}}，縮寫爲SDDM）是为[[X_Window系统|X Window系统]]和[[Wayland|Wayland]]提供的一个[[X显示管理器|显示管理器]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://github.com/sddm/sddm/wiki/0.12.0-Release-Announcement|title=0.12.0-Release-Announcement}}</ref>SDDM使用[[C++11|C++11]]编写，并可使用[[QML|QML]]编写主题。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=MTI3ODc|title=SDDM: A Lightweight QML-Based Display Manager|accessdate=2014-03-05|date=2013-01-19}}</ref>

SDDM是[[自由及开放源代码软件|自由及开放源代码软件]]，依据[[GNU通用公共许可证|GNU通用公共许可证]]的条款发布。

== 相關事件 ==
2013年，[[Fedora|Fedora KDE]]成员决定在Fedora 21中将SDDM用于默认的显示管理器。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rdieter.blogspot.com/2013/11/sddm-change-pushed-back-to-fedora-21.html|title=Rex's Blog|author=Rex Dieter|date=2013-11-26}}</ref>

SDDM也被用於[https://liri.io Hawaii]的默認圖形登錄界面。<ref name="display_managers_finale">{{cite web |url=http://blog.davidedmundson.co.uk/blog/display_managers_finale |title=Display Managers In Plasma 5 |date=2014-11-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150827124914/http://blog.davidedmundson.co.uk/blog/display_managers_finale |archivedate=2015-08-27 |df= }}</ref>

[[KDE|KDE]]在[[KDE_Plasma_5|Plasma 5]]中选择SDDM作为[[KDM|KDE显示管理器]]的替代。<ref name="display_managers_finale"/><ref name="Phoronix 2014-11-03">{{cite news|last=Larabel|first=Michael|author-link=Michael Larabel|date=2014-11-03|title=SDDM Is The Recommended Display Manager Of KDE Plasma 5|url=https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=MTgyOTU|newspaper=[[Phoronix|Phoronix]]|accessdate=2014-11-03}}</ref>

[[LXQt|LXQt]]开发组建议使用SDDM作为默认显示管理器。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sourceforge.net/p/lxde/mailman/message/31580081/|title=[Lxde-list] CALL FOR TESTERS: LXQt now available for testing|date=2013-10-30|publisher=SourceForge|quote=... For a display manager, SDDM is recommended ...|last1=Leclanche|first1=Jerome|website=sourceforge.net|access-date=2015-08-08}}</ref>

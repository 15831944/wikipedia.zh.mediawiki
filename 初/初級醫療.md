'''初級醫療'''（Primary care）也稱為'''基礎醫療'''，是{{le|醫療人員|health care provider}}日常提供的[[医疗卫生|医疗]]。在[[醫療系統|醫療系統]]中，初級醫療提供者多半也是第一者接觸[[病人|病人]]的人，並且提供後續主要的醫療護理，並且在病患有需要時，協調其他專科醫師的醫療<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.euro.who.int/en/what-we-do/health-topics/Health-systems/primary-health-care/main-terms-used|title=WHO/Europe -  Main terminology|work=who.int}}</ref><ref name="Cookie">{{cite web |last1=Thomas-MacLean |first1=R |last2=Tarlier |first2=D |last3=Fortin |first3=M |last4=Ackroyd-Stolarz |first4=S |last5=Stewart |first5=M |url=http://www.uwo.ca/fammed/csfm/tutor-phc/documentation/trainingpapers/TUTOR_Definitio_%20of_primar_%20health_care.pdf |title=No cookie-cutter response: conceptualizing primary health care |publisher=[[University_of_Western_Ontario|University of Western Ontario]] |date=2008 |accessdate=18 November 2014}}</ref><ref>World Health Organization. [http://www.wpro.who.int/nr/rdonlyres/45b45060-a38e-496f-b2c1-bd2dc6c04c52/0/44definitionofterms2009.pdf ''Definition of Terms.''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110303183810/http://www.wpro.who.int/NR/rdonlyres/45B45060-A38E-496F-B2C1-BD2DC6C04C52/0/44Definitionofterms2009.pdf |date=2011-03-03 }} Accessed 24 June 2011.</ref>。會提供初級醫療的人包括{{le|初級醫療醫師|primary care physician}}（[[家庭醫學科醫師|家庭醫學科醫師]]或是普通科醫生）、[[执业护士|执业护士]]（{{le|成人老年病學執業護士|adult-gerontology nurse practitioner}}、{{le|家庭醫學執業護士|family nurse practitioner}}、{{le|小兒科執業護士|pediatric nurse practitioner}}）或是{{le|醫師助理|physician assistant}}。有些地區也會有其他的人提供初級醫療，例如{{le|註冊護士|registered nurse}}、[[藥劑師|藥劑師]]<!--a [[clinical_officer|clinical officer]] (as in parts of Africa), or a [[Ayurveda|Ayurvedic]] or other traditional medicine professional (as in parts of Asia).-->。病患有時也會依照其病況[[轉診|轉介]]，接受二級醫療（secondary care）或是三級醫療（tertiary care）。

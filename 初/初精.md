'''初精'''是指[[青春期|青春期]]男性的[[睪丸|睪丸]]初次分泌[[精子|精子]]<ref name="a">''Adolescence and Emerging Adulthood: A Cultural Approach'', Jeffrey Jensen Arnett,  N.J.: Prentice-Hall, 3rd edition, 2006. ISBN 9780131950719</ref>，或是第一次[[射精|射精]]，和女性的[[初潮|初潮]]類似，都是生殖器官發育中會出現的情形<ref>''Adolescence'', E. Atwater, Englewood Cliffs, N.J.: Prentice-Hall, 3rd edition, 1992. ISBN 9780130074690</ref><ref name="c">The onset of sperm production in pubertal boys. Relationship to gonadotropin excretion, H. E. Kulin, M. A. Frontera, L. M. Demers, M. J. Bartholomew, and T. A. Lloyd, ''American Journal of Diseases of Children'' '''143''' (1989), pp. 190–193. {{pubmed|2492750}} {{doi|10.1001/archpedi.1989.02150140080024}}</ref>。青春期男性對於初精會有不同的反應，從懼怕到興奮甚至欣慰都有<ref name="a" />。

初精表示男性要開始進入[[性成熟|性成熟]]的階段，發生在第二性徵正要開始發育的時候<ref name="d">Onset of the release of spermatozoa (spermarche) in boys in relation to age, testicular growth, pubic hair, and height, C. T. Nielsen et al., ''Journal of Clinical Endocrinology & Metabolism'' '''62''' (#3, March 1986), pp. 532–535.{{pubmed|3944237}} {{doi|10.1210/jcem-62-3-532}}</ref>。初精的年齡不容易判斷，不過研究者試著在不同的族群中採集男孩的過夜尿液，檢驗當中是否有[[精子|精子]]<ref name="c" />。根據不同的研究資料，初精約在10歲到13歲之間<ref name="d" />。

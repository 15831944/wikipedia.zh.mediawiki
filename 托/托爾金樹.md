== 歷史 ==
托爾金樹被認為是1799年由[[植物學|植物學]]家{{le|約翰·西爾托普|John Sibthorp}}所收集的種子種下的<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.oxfordtoday.ox.ac.uk/news/2014-08-15-farewell-tolkiens-tree|title=Farewell, Tolkien's Tree|accessdate=2018-03-18|date=2014-08-15|publisher=Oxford Today|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170720121551/http://www.oxfordtoday.ox.ac.uk/news/2014-08-15-farewell-tolkiens-tree|archivedate=2017-07-20}}</ref>。

1925年至1959年間，托爾金於牛津大學[[彭布羅克學院|彭布羅克學院]]和[[牛津大學默頓學院|默頓學院]]擔任語言學教授，當時的他非常熱愛這棵樹，時常坐在該樹下或與之合影，甚至托爾金生前的最後一張照片就是跟這棵樹合影的<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":2" /><ref name=":3" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lonelyplanet.com/news/2018/02/23/jrr-tolkien-exhibition-bodleian-libraries/|title=Tolkien works exploring mythical romance will go on display in Oxford|accessdate=2018-03-18|last=Planet|first=Lonely|work=www.lonelyplanet.com|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180321131338/https://www.lonelyplanet.com/news/2018/02/23/jrr-tolkien-exhibition-bodleian-libraries/|archivedate=2018-03-21}}</ref>。後來托爾金樹被認為是《魔戒》小說裡奇幻生物「[[樹人|樹人]]」的靈感來源<ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.oxfordmail.co.uk/news/11376864.The_Ent_is_nigh_for_Tolkien_tree/|title=The Ent is nigh for Tolkien tree|accessdate=2018-03-18|work=Oxford Mail|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180321131338/http://www.oxfordmail.co.uk/news/11376864.The_Ent_is_nigh_for_Tolkien_tree/|archivedate=2018-03-21}}</ref>，具有紀念價值，一直以來許多《魔戒》小說讀者都會去[[牛津|牛津]]參觀托爾金樹<ref name=":1" />。

== 被砍除 ==
2014年7月26日，已有215年樹齡的托爾金樹的兩根樹枝墜落，砸毀了一面牆<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />，植物園園長艾莉森·佛斯特判斷可能是天氣乾燥的緣故導致樹枝倒塌<ref name=":3" />。雖然該起事件並無造成人員傷亡，但牛津大學與牛津市議會作出決定要將托爾金樹砍除<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.tolkiensociety.org/2014/07/tolkiens-favourite-tree-to-be-cut-down/|title=Tolkien’s favourite tree to be cut down|date=2014-07-30|newspaper=The Tolkien Society|accessdate=2018-03-18|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180308143158/https://www.tolkiensociety.org/2014/07/tolkiens-favourite-tree-to-be-cut-down/|archivedate=2018-03-08}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://middleearthnews.com/2014/07/30/tolkiens-favourite-tree-to-be-cut-down/|title=Tolkien’s favourite tree to be cut down|accessdate=2018-04-19|date=2014-07-30|publisher=Middle-earth News}}</ref>，這個決定引起了世界各地《魔戒》小說讀者的激烈抗議，甚至有人還聲稱植物園一方是「[[薩魯曼|薩魯曼]]」（《魔戒》裡破壞森林的[[巫師_(中土大陸)|巫師]]）<ref name=":2" />。然而托爾金樹還是在同年8月份被砍除<ref name=":4" />。

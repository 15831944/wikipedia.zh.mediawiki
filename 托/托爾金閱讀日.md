'''托爾金閱讀日'''（{{lang-en|Tolkien Reading Day}}）是由{{link-en|托爾金協會|The Tolkien Society}}在2003年3月25日開始發起的年度活動，旨在鼓勵民眾閱讀[[J·R·R·托爾金|J·R·R·托爾金]]的文學作品。選在3月25日的原因是為了紀念《[[魔戒|魔戒]]》小說中黑暗魔君[[索倫_(魔戒)|索倫]]敗亡的日子<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.tolkiensociety.org/society/events/reading-day/|title=Tolkien Reading Day|date=2016-10-30|newspaper=The Tolkien Society|accessdate=2018-02-15|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.theonering.net/torwp/2015/03/26/97605-tolkien-reading-day-never-too-late/|title=Tolkien Reading Day (Never too late!)|date=2015-03-26|newspaper=TheOneRing.net|accessdate=2018-02-15|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.theonering.net/torwp/2014/03/04/87373-tolkien-reading-day-draws-near-come-out-and-read/|title=Tolkien Reading Day draws near – come out and read|date=2014-03-04|newspaper=TheOneRing.net|accessdate=2018-02-15|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://hk.on.cc/hk/bkn/cnt/lifestyle/20180325/bkn-20180325080037345-0325_00982_001.html|title=今日係……托爾金閱讀日|date=2018-03-25|newspaper=on.cc東網|accessdate=2018-07-06|language=zh-hk}}</ref>。

這個活動當初是受到了[[紐約州|紐約州]][[锡拉丘兹_(纽约州)|雪城]]當地[[報紙|報紙]]《{{link-en|雪城郵報|The Post-Standard}}》專欄作家[[肖恩·克斯特|肖恩·克斯特]]（Sean Kirst）的建議而設立的<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.syracuse.com/kirst/index.ssf/2006/03/the_international_tolkien_read.html|title=The International Tolkien Reading Day: Looking back on how it started, in Syracuse|newspaper=syracuse.com|accessdate=2018-02-15|language=en-US}}</ref>。

'''托莱多'''（Toledo）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[泰马县_(爱阿华州)|泰马县]]的城市，是泰马县的县城，<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx|accessdate=2011-06-07|title=Find a County|publisher=National Association of Counties}}</ref> 2010年人口普查时人口为2341人。

==历史==
托莱多成立于1853年，作为泰马县的县城。<ref name="history">{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=44IUAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA201#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=History of Tama County, Iowa: Its Cities, Towns and Villages | publisher=Toledo Times Office | author=Chapman, Samuel D. | year=1879 | pages=201-202}}</ref> 它以俄亥俄州[[托莱多_(俄亥俄州)|托莱多]]命名。<ref>{{cite book|author=Chicago and North Western Railway Company|title=A History of the Origin of the Place Names Connected with the Chicago & North Western and Chicago, St. Paul, Minneapolis & Omaha Railways|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OspBAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA131|year=1908|page=131}}</ref> 托莱多于1866年建市。<ref name="history" />

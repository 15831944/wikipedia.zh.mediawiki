'''攝龜屬'''（'''''Cyclemys'''''），或稱'''齒緣攝龜屬'''、'''亞洲葉龜'''，是[[地龜科|地龜科]]下的一個[[屬|屬]]。該屬的[[龜|龜]]都是淡水龜，常見於[[南亞|南亞]]及[[東南亞|東南亞]]地區，現有7[[種|種]]。

==命名==
攝龜屬位於[[地龜科|地龜科]]下的[[地龜亞科|地龜亞科]]中，是由[[英國|英國]][[動物學家|動物學家]][[托馬斯·貝爾_(動物學家)|貝爾]]在1834年首次描述的。<ref name="Gray">{{cite book|last =Gray|first =John Edward |title =Catalogue of Shield Reptiles in the Collection of the British Museum|publisher =Order of the Trustees, British Museum (Natural History). |year =1855|isbn =|url=http://www.archive.org/details/catalogueofshiel01brit}}</ref><ref name="zootaxa">{{cite journal|last1=Fritz|first1=Uwe|last2=Nowak-Kemp|first2=Malgosia|year=2010|title=Chelonian type specimens at the Oxford University Museum|journal=Zootaxa|volume=1|issue=19|publisher=Magnolia Press|url=http://www.iucn-tftsg.org/wp-content/uploads/file/Articles/Nowak-Kemp_and_Fritz_2010.pdf|accessdate=March 27, 2011 }}</ref> 攝龜屬及其近緣屬（[[閉殼龜屬|閉殼龜屬]]、[[鋸緣攝龜屬|鋸緣攝龜屬]]和[[果龜屬|果龜屬]]）被認為都有同樣的[[東方龜屬|東方龜屬]]祖先。<ref name="honda">{{cite journal|last1=Honda|first1=Masanao|last2=Yasukawa|first2=Yuichirou|last3=Hirayama|first3=Ren| last4=Ota|first4=Hidetoshi|year=2002|title=Phylogenetic Relationships of the Asian Box Turtles of the Genus ''Cuora'' sensu lato (Reptilia: Bataguridae) inferred from Mitochondrial DNA Sequences|journal=Zoological Science|volume=19|pages=1305–1312|publisher=Zoological Society of Japan|url=http://www.tulips.tsukuba.ac.jp/dspace/bitstream/2241/104116/1/ZS_19-11.pdf|accessdate= March 28, 2011|doi=10.2108/zsj.19.1305|pmid=12499674|issue=11}}</ref><ref name="zhang">{{cite journal|last1=Zhang|first1=Yanyun|last2=Nie|first2=Liuwang|last3=Huang|first3=Yuqing|last4=Pu|first4=Youquang|last5=Zhang|first5=Li|year=2009|title=The Mitochondrial DNA Control Region Comparison Studies of Four Hinged Turtles and its Phylogentic Significance of the Genus  ''Cuora''  Sensu Lato (Testudinata: Geoemydidae)|journal=Genes and Genomics|volume=31|issue=5|pages=349–359|publisher=The Genetics Society of Korea|url=http://www.springerlink.com/content/E1M3072916577321/fulltext.pdf|accessdate=March 29, 2011|doi=10.1007/BF03191253 }}</ref><ref name="bramble"/>
[[File:Cycle_denta_100207-0641_ipb.jpg|thumb]]，這是一隻成年的[[齒緣攝龜|齒緣攝龜]]]]

因為各個[[種|種]]之間太過相似，攝龜屬的[[分類學|分類]]和[[種系發生|種系發生]]曾經一時糾纏不清。<ref name="stuart">{{cite journal|last1=Fritz|first1=Uwe|year=2008|title=Historical DNA from museum type specimens clarifies diversity of Asian leaf turtles (''Cyclemys'')|journal=Biological Journal of the Linnean Society|volume=94|issue=1|publisher=The Linnean Society of London|accessdate= March 27, 2011|doi=10.1111/j.1095-8312.2008.00966.x|last2=Fritz|first2=UWE|pages=131–141}}</ref> 很久之後才得出該屬在[[形態學|形態學]]上只有[[齒緣攝龜|齒緣攝龜]]和[[歐氏攝龜|歐氏攝龜]]這兩個可分辨的類型。<ref name="div"/><ref name="iucn">Asian Turtle Trade Working Group 2000. ''[http://www.iucnredlist.org/apps/redlist/details/39551/0 Cyclemys dentata]''. In: IUCN 2010. [http://www.iucnredlist.org IUCN Red List of Threatened Species]. Version 2010.4. Retrieved March 25, 2011.</ref> 再後來才將該屬擴充到有7個種，不過這之中仍有爭議，譬如[[白舌攝龜|白舌攝龜]]、[[黑橋攝龜|黑橋攝龜]]和[[美麗攝龜|美麗攝龜]]的名稱合法性還存在爭議。<ref name="div"/><ref name="hartner">{{cite journal|last1=Artner|first1=H.|year=2008|title=The World's Extant Turtle Species|journal=Emys|volume=15|issue=3|publisher=[http://www.chelonia2002.com Chelonia 2002 - Turtle Center]|url=http://www.chelonia2002.com/ownimages/artnerchecklist.pdf|accessdate=March 27, 2011|author=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110912072018/http://www.chelonia2002.com/ownimages/artnerchecklist.pdf|archive-date=2011-09-12|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name="repatripons"/>

根據[[線粒體DNA|線粒體DNA測序]]，之前確定的條頸攝龜（''Cyclemys tcheponensis''）和緬甸攝龜（''Cyclemys shanensis''）都已被合並到歐氏攝龜之下。而其原來所描述的形態學差異並不足以將之劃分為兩個不同的種。<ref name="div"/> 其他一些舊種也做了移動調整。

攝龜屬歸類的區分主要取決於其[[胸甲|胸甲]]的顏色：美麗攝龜、黑橋攝龜、齒緣攝龜的胸甲都是黃色的，而白舌攝龜、[[灰舌攝龜|灰舌攝龜]]、[[印度攝龜|印度攝龜]]、歐氏攝龜則是黑的。<ref name="div"/>

攝龜屬的屬名''Cyclemys''來自於[[希臘語|希臘語]]κύκλος（意為“圓”或者“週期”，指的是它們的龜殼）和εμύς（意為“淡水龜”）。<ref name="tabaka">{{cite web|url=http://www.chelonia.org/Articles/Cyclmeyscare.htm|title=Genus: Cyclemys (Asian Leaf Turtles)|last1=Tabaka|first1=Chris|last2=Senneke|first2=Darrell|date=January 28, 2003|publisher=World Chelonian Trust|accessdate=March 27, 2011}}</ref> [[英語|英語]]中常稱'''Asian leaf turtles'''（亞洲葉龜）或'''leaf turtles'''（葉龜），原因就是它們的殼外表與樹葉相似。不過[[地龜屬|地龜屬]]、[[擬水龜科|擬水龜科]]的[[安南龜|安南龜]]及其他龜類也有稱葉龜的。<ref name="tabaka"/><ref name="franklin"/>

== 描述==
[[File:Cyclemys_carapace_and_plastron.png|right]][[盾板|盾板]]（左上）和[[胸甲|胸甲]]（右上）的圖案，下方則是其腹盾板（只能用於區分成年龜）]]
攝龜屬的龜可以依靠其[[龜殼|殼]]的圓與否來區分，其殼的顏色也有深綠、淺褐和橄欖等諸多色彩。齒緣狀的腹側[[盾板|盾板]]常見於接近成年的攝龜身上，成年龜只有身體後半部份的盾板才有齒緣。<ref name="eol"/>

成年攝龜的前後兩部份胸甲會彌合在一起，但不像其親緣[[閉殼龜屬|閉殼龜屬]]那樣，<ref name="namerica">{{cite book|last =Dodd|first =C. Kenneth |title =North American Box Turtles: A Natural History|publisher =University of Oklahoma Press|year =2002|series=Animal Natural History|page=14|isbn =978-0-8061-3501-4|url=http://books.google.com/?id=Vgpz0d16GpEC&dq=cyclemys+plastral+hinge}}</ref> 攝龜的胸甲彌合並不完全。<ref name="bramble">{{cite journal|last1=Bramble|first1=Dennis M.|year=1974|title=Emydid Shell Kinesis: Biomechanics and Evolution|journal=Copeia|volume=1974|issue=3|publisher=American Society of Ichthyologists and Herpetologists|accessdate= March 29, 2011|jstor=1442685}}</ref><ref name="petcare">{{cite web|url=http://petcaretips.net/asian-leaf-turtle.html|title=Asian Leaf, Yellow-headed Temple, Striped-necked Leaf, & Borneo Black Leaf Turtle|publisher=Free Pet Care Tips Newsletter|accessdate=March 29, 2011}}</ref> 這一構造可能有助於成年雌性龜產卵。<ref name="franklin"/> 這一屬最明顯的特徵之一也就是彌合不完全導致的腹側生出附加的小型三角盾板。<ref name="gemeli">{{cite journal|last1=Praschag|first1=Peter|last2=Hundsdörfer|first2=Anna K.|last3=Fritz|first3=Uwe|year=2009|title=Further specimens and phylogenetic position of the recently described leaf turtle species ''Cyclemys gemeli'' (Testudines: Geoemydidae)|journal=Zootaxa|volume=29|issue=37|publisher=Magnolia Press|issn=1175-5334|url=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2009/f/zt02008p037.pdf|accessdate=March 29, 2011}}</ref>

攝龜屬屬於[[曲頸龜亞目|曲頸龜亞目]]，可以將脖頸直接縮回殼內。有些種類的頭部有紅或橙色條紋，有些則沒有，其胸甲有從中心向四周輻射出的細線。<ref name="eol"/> 其足有蹼，適於兩栖，攝龜可以長到{{convert|25|cm|in|}}長。<ref name="tabaka"/>

攝龜屬的龜在7至12歲時性成熟，雄性要早於雌性。<ref name="eol"/><ref name="coleman">{{cite web|url=http://www.ukchelonia.info/paulsarticles/leafs.htm|title=The Asian Leaf Turtle (Cyclemys dentata)|author=Paul Coleman|year=1995|publisher=UKchelonia.info|accessdate=March 29, 2011}}</ref> [[兩性異形|兩性異形]]在攝龜身上體現得十分明顯。<ref name="div"/><ref name="jensen">{{cite journal|first1=Jensen|last1=Karen A.|first2=Das|last2=Indraneil|year=2006|title=Biological Observations on the Asian Soft-Shell Turtle in Sarawak, Malaysian Borneo, with notes on the Biology and Conservation of other Non-marine Turtles|journal=Testudo|volume=6|issue=3|publisher=British Chelonia Group|url=http://www.britishcheloniagroup.org.uk/testudo/v6/v6n3jensen.htm|accessdate=March 30, 2011|author=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120321214900/http://www.britishcheloniagroup.org.uk/testudo/v6/v6n3jensen.htm|archive-date=2012-03-21|dead-url=yes}}</ref> 雌性通常一次會產下10至15枚卵。<ref name="franklin"/>

一隻人工飼養的齒緣攝龜曾活了將近15年，<ref name="lifespan">{{cite web|url=http://genomics.senescence.info/species/entry.php?species=Cyclemys_dentata|title=AnAge entry for Cyclemys dentata|publisher=AnAge database at the Human Ageing Genomic Resources.|accessdate=March 28, 2011}}</ref> 但是有更加長壽的記錄，因此其實際情況不能確定。<ref name="eolage">{{cite web|url=http://www.eol.org/pages/795210?text_id=6706261|title=''Cyclemys dentata'' Gray 1831, Lifespan, longevity, and ageing |author=Joao Pedro de Magalhaes |year=2010|publisher=Encyclopedia of Life|accessdate=March 28, 2011}}</ref>

==分佈與習性==
[[File:Leaf_turtle_Cyclemys_species.jpg|left]][[阿魯那恰爾邦|阿魯那恰爾邦]][[東卡門縣|東卡門縣]]一家虎類保護區內長流河中的攝龜]]

攝龜屬下各種龜的具體分佈尚不明確，不過其總體常見於[[南亞|南亞]]及[[東南亞|東南亞]]國家。<ref name="div"/>

成年的攝龜大多數時間都生活在陸地上，出沒於池塘、溪流和其他山地叢林的慢流的淺水附近。<ref name="tabaka"/><ref name="franklin">{{cite book|last =Franklin|first =Carl J.|title =Turtles: An Extraordinary Natural History 245 Million Years in the Making|publisher =Voyageur Press|year =2007|isbn =978-0-7603-2981-8|url=http://books.google.com/?id=rBbuYFjiTNMC&pg=PA109&lpg=PA109&dq=cyclemys+elevation+wild#v=onepage&q&f=false}}</ref> 它們常見於低海拔地區，不過即使[[海拔|海拔]]{{convert|1000|m|ft}}以上的地區也有其蹤跡。<ref name="nepal">{{cite journal|last1=Rui|first1=Kalu Ram|year=2004|title=Ecological Distribution Of Cyclemys Oldhamii(Gray 1863) From Nepal|journal=Our Nature|volume=2|issue=7|publisher=Nature Conservation and Health Care Council, Nepal|url=http://nepjol.info/index.php/ON/article/viewFile/317/309|accessdate=2013-03-16}}</ref>

攝龜屬的龜是[[雜食動物|雜食動物]]，<ref name="kirk">{{cite web|url=http://www.unc.edu/~dtkirkpa/stuff/oversemi.html|title=An Overview of Common Semi-Aquatic Turtles|author=David T. Kirkpatrick|year=1996|publisher=[http://www.unc.edu/ University of North Carolina]|accessdate=March 28, 2011}}</ref> 接近成年的個體更偏愛[[食肉動物|肉食]]，而又因其偏愛的多是水生生物，故而幼年個體更常見於水中。<ref name="tabaka"/>

===黃腹種===
*[[黑橋攝龜|黑橋攝龜]]（''Cyclemys atripons'' <small>Iverson & McCold, 1997</small>）：背殼淺紅褐色、橢圓至瘦長、可能有輻射狀黑色圖案。胸甲大多都是黃色的，可能有輻射狀黑色圖案。頭部有斑點，咽部呈黃色。脖頸有條紋。其通用名“黑橋”指的是其胸甲和背殼連接處有黑色條紋或純黑色的彎曲部份（“橋”）。<ref name="div"/><ref name="franklin"/><ref name="cambodia"/> 出現於[[柬埔寨|柬埔寨]]、[[泰國|泰國]]南部和[[越南|越南]]。<ref name="repatripons">{{cite web|url=http://reptile-database.reptarium.cz/species.php?genus=Cyclemys&species=atripons|title=Cyclemys atripons Iverson & McCord, 1997|last1= Uetz |first1=Peter|last2=Hallermann|first2=Jakob|last3= Hosek|first3=Jiri|date=|publisher=The Reptile Database|accessdate=March 27, 2011}}</ref>

*[[齒緣攝龜|齒緣攝龜]]（''Cyclemys dentata'' <small>Gray, 1831</small>）：背殼深褐色、可能有輻射狀黑色圖案。胸甲大部份是黃色的、可能有輻射狀黑色圖案。頭部有斑點，咽部有深黃色條紋。脖頸有條紋。“橋”部呈黃色，可能有輻射狀黑色圖案。<ref name="div"/> 在寵物市場上，齒緣攝龜是最受歡迎的一種攝龜。發現於[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]]、<ref name="reza">{{cite journal|last1=Ali Reza|first1=A.H.M.|year=2004|title=Natural Resources Management in Bangladesh: Linking National Priority to Global Perspective|journal=Tigerpaper|volume=31|issue=2|url=http://wildreza.com/publicaitons_files/Reza%2004_NR%20Mgnt%20in%20Bd.pdf|accessdate=March 27, 2011|author=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120321200918/http://wildreza.com/publicaitons_files/Reza%2004_NR%20Mgnt%20in%20Bd.pdf|archive-date=2012-03-21|dead-url=yes}}</ref> [[汶萊|汶萊]]、[[印度尼西亞|印度尼西亞]]、[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]、[[菲律賓|菲律賓]]、<ref name="phils">{{cite journal|last1=Diesmos|first1=Arvin C.|last2=Rogelio V.|first2=Sison|year=2008|title=Status and Distribution of Nonmarine Turtles of the Philippines|journal=Chelonian Conservation and Biology|volume=7|issue=2|publisher=Chelonian Research Foundation|doi=10.2744/CCB-0672.1 |url=http://www.bioone.org/doi/abs/10.2744/CCB-0672.1?journalCode=ccab|accessdate=|last3=Alcala|first3=Angel C.|last4=Sison|first4=Rogelio V.|pages=157–177 }}</ref>[[新加坡|新加坡]]、[[印度|印度]]北部和[[泰國|泰國]]。<ref name="CDG">{{cite web|url=http://reptile-database.reptarium.cz/species.php?genus=Cyclemys&species=dentata|title=Cyclemys dentata (Gray, 1831)</small>|last1= Uetz |first1=Peter|last2=Hallermann|first2=Jakob|last3= Hosek|first3=Jiri|date=|publisher=The Reptile Database|accessdate=March 27, 2011}}</ref>

*[[美麗攝龜|美麗攝龜]]（''Cyclemys pulchristiata'' <small>Fritz, Gaulke, and Lehr 1997</small>）：背殼淺紅褐色、橢圓至瘦長、可能有輻射狀黑色圖案。胸甲大多都是黃色的，可能有短粗的線條。頭部有斑點，咽部呈黃色。脖頸有條紋。“橋”部呈黃色，有黑色條紋，也有呈純黑色的個體。與黑橋攝龜極為相似，需要用基因採樣才能具體確定。<ref name="div"/><ref name="cambodia"/> 發現於柬埔寨和越南。<ref name="div"/><ref name="cambodia">{{cite journal|last1=Durkin|first1=Louise|last2=Handschuh|first2=Markus|last3=Sovannak|first3=Keo|last4=Ward|first4=Lizzy|last5=Hulse|first5= Nikki|last6=Mould|first6=Alistair|year=2010|title=Discovery of a hitherto unknown breeding population of the Asian leaf turtle ''Cyclemys aff. atripons'' in Phnom Kulen National Park, northwestern Cambodia|journal=Cambodian Journal of Natural History |issue=1|publisher=Centre for Biodiversity Conservation, Phnom Penh|url=http://www.accb-cambodia.org/en/Discovery%20of%20Cyclemys%20in%20PKNP_ACCB%20-%20Durkin%20et%20al.pdf|accessdate=2013-03-16}}</ref>

===黑腹種===
*[[白舌攝龜|白舌攝龜]]（''Cyclemys enigmatica'' <small>Fritz, Guicking, Auer, Sommer, Wink & Hundsdörfer, 2008</small>）：背殼深黑色或稍微發紅、橢圓形，成年體沒有圖案。胸甲深褐色或黑色，可能有密集的黑色輻射狀圖案。頭部可能是日曬色或者微發紅的褐色，咽部和脖頸都是深色的。“橋”部深褐色或黑色。<ref name="div"/> 其[[同域|分佈區]]與齒緣攝龜重疊。<ref name="hoog">{{cite journal|last1=Hoogmoed|first1=M.S.|last2=Gassó Miracle|first2=M.E.|last3=van den Hoek Ostende|first3=L.W.|year=2002|title=Type specimens of recent and fossil Testudines and Crocodylia in the collections of the Netherlands Centre for Biodiversity Naturalis, Leiden, the Netherlands|journal=Zool. Med. Leiden|volume=84|issue=8|pages=|publisher=Netherlands Centre for Biodiversity Naturalis|issn=0024-0672|url=http://dpc.uba.uva.nl/cgi/t/text/get-pdf?idno=m8401a08;c=zoomed|accessdate= March 28, 2011}}</ref><ref name="repenigmatica">{{cite web|url=http://reptile-database.reptarium.cz/species.php?genus=Cyclemys&species=enigmatica|title=Cyclemys enigmatica Fritz, Guicking, Auer, Sommer, Wink & Hundsdörfer, 2009|last1= Uetz |first1=Peter|last2=Hallermann|first2=Jakob|last3= Hosek|first3=Jiri|date=|publisher=The Reptile Database|accessdate=March 27, 2011}}</ref>

*[[灰舌攝龜|灰舌攝龜]]（''Cyclemys fusca'' <small>Fritz, Guicking, Auer, Sommer, Wink & Hundsdörfer, 2008</small>）：背殼深黑色、橢圓形，成年體沒有圖案。胸甲深褐色或黑色，可能有密集的黑色輻射狀圖案。頭部淺黃綠色，咽部和脖頸都是深色的。“橋”部深褐色或黑色。<ref name="div"/><ref name="gemeli"/> 發現於緬甸，也可能存在於附近的印度和孟加拉國地區。<ref name="repfusca">{{cite web|url=http://reptile-database.reptarium.cz/species.php?genus=Cyclemys&species=fusca|title=Cyclemys fusca Fritz, Guicking, Auer, Sommer, Wink & Hundsdörfer, 2009|last1= Uetz |first1=Peter|last2=Hallermann|first2=Jakob|last3= Hosek|first3=Jiri|date=|publisher=The Reptile Database|accessdate=March 27, 2011}}</ref>

*[[印度攝龜|印度攝龜]]（''Cyclemys gemeli'' <small>Fritz, Guicking, Auer, Sommer, Wink & Hundsdörfer, 2008</small>）：成年體背殼沒有圖案、瘦長並可能兩邊平行。胸甲沒有圖案，呈深褐色。頭部黑色，咽部和頸部都是深色的。“橋”部也是深褐色的。<ref name="div"/><ref name="gemeli"/>發現於孟加拉國和印度部份地區。<ref name="repgemeli">{{cite web|url=http://reptile-database.reptarium.cz/species.php?genus=Cyclemys&species=gemeli|title=Cyclemys gemeli Fritz, Guicking, Auer, Sommer, Wink & Hundsdörfer, 2009|last1= Uetz |first1=Peter|last2=Hallermann|first2=Jakob|last3= Hosek|first3=Jiri|date=|publisher=The Reptile Database|accessdate=March 27, 2011}}</ref>

*[[歐氏攝龜|歐氏攝龜]]（''Cyclemys oldhamii'' <small>Gray, 1863</small>）：背殼深褐色、矩形、成年體沒有團。胸甲成深褐色或黑色，可能有密集的黑色輻射狀圖案。頭部有斑點，咽部深色。頸部可能有條紋。“橋”部呈黑色或褐色。<ref name="div"/> 發現於柬埔寨、<ref name="cambodia"/> 印度尼西亞、[[老撾|老撾]]、緬甸、馬來西亞部份地區、泰國、越南、印度部份地區和[[尼泊爾|尼泊爾]]。<ref name="nepal"/><ref name="repoldhamii">{{cite web|url=http://reptile-database.reptarium.cz/species.php?genus=Cyclemys&species=oldhami|title=Cyclemys oldhami Gray, 1863|last1= Uetz |first1=Peter|last2=Hallermann|first2=Jakob|last3= Hosek|first3=Jiri|date=|publisher=The Reptile Database|accessdate=March 27, 2011}}</ref>

==保護現狀==
攝龜屬的龜常備捕捉作為[[寵物|寵物]]、食物或用作[[傳統醫學|傳統醫藥]]材料。<ref name="namkading">{{cite web|url=http://namkading.org/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=6:leaf-turtle&catid=11:turtles&Itemid=32|title=Leaf Turtle|publisher=Nam Kading Research & Training Centre|accessdate=March 27, 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110904093116/http://www.namkading.org/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=6:leaf-turtle&catid=11:turtles&Itemid=32|archive-date=2011-09-04|dead-url=yes}}</ref> 2007年5月，[[中華人民共和國|中華人民共和國]][[廣東省|廣東省]][[陽江市|陽江市]]查處了藏有超過3000隻攝龜以及閉殼龜的條板箱。<ref name="UNBeijing">{{cite book|author=United Nations Environment Programme|title =Beijing 2008 Olympic games: an environmental review|publisher =UNEP Division of Communications and Public Information|series =|year =2007|isbn =978-92-807-2888-0|url=http://books.google.com/?id=p0O6gU4_aa0C&dq=cyclemys}}</ref>

因為命名混亂和區分不易，[[IUCN|IUCN]]的評定工作也不易展開。<ref name="iucn"/> 1991年，IUCN給出的評價是需要一定的保護。<ref name="conserv">{{cite book|author=IUCN/SSC Tortoise and Freshwater Turtle Specialist Group|title =Tortoises and freshwater turtles: an action plan for their conservation|publisher =IUCN/WWF|year =1989|url=http://books.google.com/?id=bxxWx4qkHYAC&dq=cyclemys |isbn =2-88032-974-4}}</ref> 現在該屬龜類的評級工作仍未完成。<ref name="eol">{{Cite book|url=http://www.edulifedesks.org/class/964/taxon-page/1064|title=Cyclemys dentata (Gray, 1831)|editor=Dr. Kenneth Angielczyk |last1=Melstrom |first1=Keegan|date=November 17, 2009|publisher=Encyclopedia of Life|accessdate=March 27, 2011|postscript=}}</ref><ref name="nepal"/>

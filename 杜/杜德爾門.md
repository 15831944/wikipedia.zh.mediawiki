'''杜德爾門'''（Durdle Door）是位於[[英國|英國]][[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[多塞特郡|多塞特郡]]西盧沃斯附近的自然形成的[[石灰岩|石灰岩]]拱門<ref name="west">West, I.W., 2003. "[http://www.soton.ac.uk/~imw/durdle.htm Durdle Door; Geology of the Dorset Coast]". [[Southampton_University|Southampton University]], UK. Version H.07.09.03.</ref>，是侏羅紀海岸的一部分。該拱門由一個家庭所有<ref name=telegraph>{{cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/middleeast/unitedarabemirates/6022473/UAE-hotel-draws-condemnation-over-use-of-Durdle-Door-image-on-website.html|title=UAE hotel draws condemnation over use of Durdle Door image on website|author=Aislinn Simpson|date=13 August 2009|accessdate=8 August 2013|location=London|work=The Daily Telegraph}}</ref>，但對公眾開放。

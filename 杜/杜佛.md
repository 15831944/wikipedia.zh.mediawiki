'''杜弗''' （{{lang-en|Dover}}，{{lang-ta|டோவெர்}}）是[[女皇镇|女皇镇]]新镇的分区，位置在女皇镇新镇的西北部。 区内的主要设施包括[[住宅|住宅]]，学府等。

==名字由来==
杜弗所处的位置曾是[[英国|英国]]的军事基地，因此这地带的路名往往与英国有关（多数与[[肯特郡|肯特郡]]息息相连）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mindef.gov.sg/oms/imindef/resourcelibrary/cyberpioneer/topics/articles/features/2010/dec10_fs2.html#.Wx-9OYozbNM|title=Goodbye, Ayer Rajah Camp|accessdate=2018-06-12|work=www.mindef.gov.sg|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180712044156/https://www.mindef.gov.sg/oms/imindef/resourcelibrary/cyberpioneer/topics/articles/features/2010/dec10_fs2.html#.Wx-9OYozbNM|archivedate=2018-07-12|df=}}</ref>[[多佛尔|多佛尔]]是肯特郡的一个郡，名字在肯特郡语中代表着水而其灵感来之附近的[[英吉利海峡|英吉利海峡]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cornishdictionary.org.uk/english-wordz?populate=water|title=English Words - cornish dictionary / gerlyver kernewek|website=www.cornishdictionary.org.uk|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171030004240/http://www.cornishdictionary.org.uk/english-wordz?populate=water|archivedate=2017-10-30|df=}}</ref>新加坡的杜弗区因此以英国的多佛尔郡而命名。<ref>Victor R Savage, Brenda S A Yeoh (2003), ''Toponymics - A Study of Singapore Street Names'', Eastern Universities Press, {{ISBN|981-210-205-1}}</ref>

'''杜瓦米什河'''（{{lang-en|Duwamish River}}）是美国[[华盛顿州|华盛顿州]][[西雅图|西雅图]]的一条长约{{Convert|12|mi|km}}的河流，也可以视为[[格林河_(杜瓦米什河支流)|格林河]]的下游部分，其人工疏浚的河口一段也被称为'''杜瓦米什水道'''（Duwamish Waterway）<ref name = Boe>Boeing-History of the Duwamish Waterway; http://www.boeing.com/principles/environment/duwamish/history.page retvd 12 13 15</ref>。名称来源于当地的土著部落{{le|杜瓦米什人|Duwamish people}}<ref name=Duw>Duwamish Tribe-culture and history; http://www.duwamishtribe.org/culture.html {{Wayback|url=http://www.duwamishtribe.org/culture.html |date=20180120031607 }} retvd 12 13 15</ref>。跨河的[[斯波坎街大橋|斯波坎街大橋]]是世界上第一座，也是唯一一座液压操作的[[混凝土|混凝土]]双葉{{le|平轉橋|Swing bridge}}<ref name="Holstine and Hobbs 2005">{{Cite book|title=Spanning Washington: Historic Highway Bridges of the Evergreen State|last=Holstine|first=Craig|last2=Hobbs, Richard|publisher=Washington State University Press|year=2005|isbn=978-0-87422-281-4}}</ref>。

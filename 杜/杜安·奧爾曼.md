'''杜安·奧爾曼'''({{lang-en|'''Duane Allman'''}})又譯為'''杜恩·歐曼，'''全名為'''霍華德·杜安·奧爾曼'''({{lang-en|'''Howard Duane Allman'''}})是美國著名的[[藍調|藍調]][[吉他手|吉他手]]、[[歌手|歌手]]和{{link-en|錄音工作室音樂家|Session musicians}}，也是[[歐曼兄弟樂團|歐曼兄弟樂團]]的共同創始團員和領導人物。1971年他24歲時，在一場[[摩托車|摩托車]]車禍事故中去世。

歐曼兄弟樂團於1969年在[[傑克遜維爾_(佛羅里達州)|佛羅里達州傑克遜維爾]]成立。<ref name="Poe2008">{{cite book|author=Randy Poe|title=Skydog: The Duane Allman Story|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=lcwXAAAAQBAJ&pg=PT225|date=1 August 2008|publisher=Backbeat Books|isbn=978-1-61713-487-6|page=225}}</ref><ref name="Jennings2013">{{cite book|author=Stephen Taylor and Matthew Jennings|title=Macon|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=hZAbAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA123|year=2013|publisher=Arcadia Publishing|isbn=978-1-4671-1115-7|page=123}}</ref><ref name="Hall2014">{{cite book|author=Mitchell K. Hall|title=The Emergence of Rock and Roll: Music and the Rise of American Youth Culture|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=gA2LAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA157|date=9 May 2014|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-135-05358-1|page=157}}</ref><ref name="LuhrssenLarson2017">{{cite book|author1=David Luhrssen|author2=Michael Larson|title=Encyclopedia of Classic Rock|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=phsIDgAAQBAJ&pg=PA3|date=24 February 2017|publisher=ABC-CLIO|isbn=978-1-4408-3514-8|page=3}}</ref> 該樂團在1970年代早期取得巨大的演出成功，歐曼兄弟樂團最美好的記憶是，杜安·奧爾曼當時為樂團雖然短暫卻有影響力的演奏，尤其是他傑出的[[滑音管吉他|幻燈片吉他]]彈奏和有創作質量的[[即興演奏|即興演奏]]技巧。<ref name="In memory1">{{cite web|url=http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/15427375/|title=In memory of Duane Allman 35 years after his death, Skydog still among rock's very best guitarists|last=Ventre|first=Michael |date=October 30, 2006 |work=MSNBC|publisher= 2009 msnbc.com|access-date=2009-09-02}}</ref>在[[滾石雜誌|滾石雜誌]]2003年選拔的有史以來最偉大一百名吉他手中，奧爾曼被列名第二名，僅次於[[吉米·亨德里克斯|吉米·亨德里克斯]]。在2011年滾石雜誌重新選拔，[[滾石雜誌一百大吉他手|史上一百大吉他手]]奧爾曼則被列在第9名。<ref name="rsmagazine">{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/5937559/the_100_greatest_guitarists_of_all_time/ |title=Rolling Stone: The 100 Greatest Guitarists of All Time |access-date=2006-11-26 |publisher=RealNetworks, Inc |year=2006 |author=Rolling Stone |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061125054919/http://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/5937559/the_100_greatest_guitarists_of_all_time/ |archivedate=November 25, 2006 |deadurl=yes |df=mdy }}</ref> 他彈奏吉他技巧的音色，是使用[[Gibson_Les_Paul|Gibson Les Paul]]電吉他和2個50瓦特低音[[:en:Marshall_amplification|馬歇爾音箱]])，他被''[[:en:Guitar_Player|吉他手雜誌]]''選為有史以來最偉大的一個。<ref name="tones">{{cite journal

他在歐曼兄弟樂團組團之前和在樂團期間，同時是一位很受歡迎的錄音工作室彈奏吉他的樂師，杜安·奧爾曼與King Curtis、Aretha Franklin、Wilson Pickett和Herbie Mann等明星都一起合作錄製過唱片。他還參與[[艾瑞克·克萊普頓|艾瑞克·克萊普頓]]成立的[[德雷克和多米諾樂團|德雷克和多米諾樂團]]的錄音演奏，正式成為樂團成員，共同完成了1970年的專輯《[[萊拉和其它情歌|萊拉和其它情歌]]》。

杜安·奧爾曼的吉他技巧，除了個人優異彈奏的素質，及彈奏吉他的強度和動力，並且能夠在錄製音樂時，充分配合其他樂手，而發揮整體現場演出或錄音的良好效果。<ref>His brother, Gregg Allman, said that "it would be real accurate to say that Duane was the father of the band, He had a lot to do with the spontaneity of the whole thing. He was like the mother ship. Somehow he had this real magic about him that would lock us all in, and we'd all take off. He really had that quality about him. Those were very happy days." Jas Obrecht, "Duane Allman remembered", ''Guitar Player'', October 1981, Vol. 15, no 10.</ref>在當時他仍然屢屢被人稱呼和提起他的綽號“Skydog”，因為大家認為他的吉他技藝高過任何一般人。<ref>This may be a reference to his signature guitar sound and tone. Many consider "Skydog" a variant of the nickname "Skyman" given to him by Wilson Pickett during the recording of Pickett's cover of the Beatles' "Hey Jude." Jim Dickinson was quoted in Keith Richards' autobiography ''Life'' as saying he was given the name because he was high much of the time.</ref>

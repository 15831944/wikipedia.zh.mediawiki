'''雙縮氨基硫脲'''（Bisthiosemicarbazone）是[[氨基脲|氨基脲]]和[[二酮|二酮]]之間的[[脱去反应|脫去反應]]之產物。它的結構式為H<sub>2</sub>NHC(=S)NN=C(R<sub>1</sub>)-R<sub>2</sub>-C(R<sub>3</sub>)=NNHC(=S)NH<sub>2</sub>。雙縮氨基硫脲中有一個縮胺脲的骨架，其中酮基的氧原子由硫原子代替。本品可與細胞中的銅及鐵結合，故而有抗病毒、抗瘧疾，及抗癌的特性<ref>{{cite journal|title=In Vitro and in Vivo Anticancer Activity of Copper Bis(thiosemicarbazone) Complexes|first1=Duraippandi|last2=Shinde|first2=Sridevi Vijay|date=14 February 2013|journal=Journal of Medicinal Chemistry|issue=3|doi=10.1021/jm300938r|volume=56|pages=722–734|pmid=23320568|last3=Somasundaram|first3=Kumaravel|last4=Samuelson|first4=Ashoka G.|last1=Palanimuthu}}</ref>。它也可能可以作爲遞送[[放射性同位素|放射性同位素]]的配體，因其對於缺氧組織具有選擇性，特別是心臟及大腦<ref>{{cite journal|title=Copper bis(thiosemicarbazone) complexes as hypoxia imaging agents: structure-activity relationships|first1=Jason L.|last2=Lewis|first2=Jason S.|date=20 April 2014|journal=JBIC Journal of Biological Inorganic Chemistry|issue=3|doi=10.1007/s007750100291|volume=7|pages=249–259|last3=Mullen|first3=Gregory E.|last4=Welch|first4=Michael J.|last5=Blower|first5=Philip J.|last1=Dearling}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=Studies on the Mechanism of Hypoxic Selectivity in Copper Bis(Thiosemicarbazone) Radiopharmaceuticals|first1=Richard I.|last2=Blower|first2=Philip J.|date=March 2002|journal=Journal of Medicinal Chemistry|issue=7|doi=10.1021/jm0104217|volume=45|pages=1420–1431|last3=Dilworth|first3=Jonathan R.|last4=Reynolds|first4=Christopher A.|last5=Zheng|first5=Yifan|last6=Mullen|first6=Gregory E. D.|last1=Maurer}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=An Unusual Dimeric Structure of a Cu(I) Bis(thiosemicarbazone) Complex:  Implications for the Mechanism of Hypoxic Selectivity of the Cu(II) Derivatives|first1=Andrew R.|last2=Dilworth|first2=Jonathan R.|date=May 2002|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|issue=19|doi=10.1021/ja012668z|volume=124|pages=5270–5271|last3=Donnelly|first3=Paul S.|last4=Labisbal|first4=Elena|last5=Sousa|first5=Antonio|last1=Cowley}}</ref>。雙縮氨基硫脲鋅原子螯合後，也可作爲光學[[顯微鏡學|顯微鏡]]中的熒光劑。[[File:Bisthiosemicarbazone-2D.png|居中]]]]

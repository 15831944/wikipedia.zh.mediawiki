'''雙點工作室'''（{{lang-en|Two Point Studios}}），是一家電玩[[遊戲開發|遊戲開發]]公司，由班·翰墨（Ben Hymers）、{{link-en|馬克·韋伯利|Mark Webley}}及{{link-en|蓋瑞·卡爾|Gary Carr (video game developer)}}於2016年7月26日創立<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://en-gb.facebook.com/pg/twopointstudios/about/?ref=page_internal|title=Two Point Studios|accessdate=2017-10-21}}</ref><ref name="GIBPub">{{Cite web|url=http://www.gamesindustry.biz/articles/2017-05-30-sega-signs-publishing-deal-with-former-lionhead-leaders|title=Sega signs publishing deal with former Lionhead leaders|accessdate=2017-10-21|date=2017-05-30|last=Christopher Dring}}</ref><ref>{{cite magazine|magazine=[[Edge_(magazine)|Edge]]|publisher=[[Future_plc|Future plc]]|issue=317|date=April 2018|pages=46,47|ISSN=1350-1593|title=Two Point Hospital|department=Hype|location=Bath}}</ref>。卡爾與韋伯利為《[[杏林也瘋狂|杏林也瘋狂]]》、《[[善與惡|善與惡]]》以及[[牛蛙製作|牛蛙製作]]與[[獅頭工作室|獅頭工作室]]聯合製作的{{link-en|神鬼寓言系列|Fable (video game series)}}的開發者之一， 雙點工作室的其他製作人員皆來自牛蛙製作、獅頭工作室以及{{link-en|Mucky Foot Productions|Mucky Foot Productions}}<ref name="GINPub">{{Cite web|url=http://www.gameindustry.com/news-industry-happenings/sega-europe-partners-two-point-studios/|title=SEGA Europe Partners with Two Point Studios|accessdate=2017-10-21|date=2017-05-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://gamingenthusiast.net/2017/05/31/sega-signs-publisher-two-point-studios/|title=SEGA Signs on as Publisher for Two Point Studios|accessdate=2017-10-21|date=2017-05-31|last=A.K Rahming|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171021163527/http://gamingenthusiast.net/2017/05/31/sega-signs-publisher-two-point-studios/#|archive-date=2017-10-21|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://venturebeat.com/2017/05/30/sega-europes-partnership-with-two-point-studios-is-another-sign-of-its-pc-growth/|title=Sega Europe’s partnership with Two Point Studios is another sign of its PC growth|accessdate=2017-10-21|date=2017-05-30|last=Stephanie Chan}}</ref>。

== 歷史 ==
2017年5月，雙點工作室宣布與[[世嘉|世嘉]]簽署了一個[[模擬遊戲|模擬遊戲]]開發的合作關係。該內容為世嘉計劃尋找並支持有才華的開發人員並將他們的專業知識帶入遊戲市場的一部分<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.develop-online.net/news/sega-to-publish-former-lionhead-outfit-two-point-studios/0233143|title=Sega to publish former Lionhead outfit Two Point Studios|accessdate=2017-10-21|date=2017-05-31|last=Sean Cleaver}}</ref>。世嘉在歐洲的發行商的高級副總約約翰·克拉克（John Clark）表示，雙點工作室的願景與世嘉的加盟條件一樣<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.pcgamer.com/we-dont-feel-anything-is-off-the-table-says-sega-on-growing-its-pc-catalogue/|title='We don't feel anything is off the table' says Sega on growing its PC catalogue|accessdate=2017-10-21|date=2017-06-29|last=Samuel Roberts}}</ref>。

雙點工作室於2018年1月發表了他們的第一款遊戲雙點醫院，該作完美的繼承了杏林也瘋狂的精神，該作已經於2018年8月30日發行<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.polygon.com/2018/1/16/16894874/two-point-hospital-theme-hospital-spiritual-successor-sega-sim|title=Theme Hospital creators working on spiritual successor with Sega|accessdate=2018-01-16|date=2018-01-16|last=McWhertor|first=Michael|work=[[Polygon_(website)|Polygon]]}}</ref> 。受到之前牛蛙製作幾款遊戲的啟發，他們也計畫將此作視為後續其他幾款模擬遊戲中的先鋒，使每一個作品相互分享彼此的世界觀<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.pcgamer.com/two-point-hospital-is-a-new-management-sim-from-the-creators-of-theme-hospital/|title=Two Point Hospital is a new management sim from the creators of Theme Hospital|accessdate=2018-01-16|date=2018-01-16|last=Donnelly|first=Joe|work=[[PC_Gamer|PC Gamer]]}}</ref>。

'''洛克希德馬丁核融合反應爐'''（Lockheed Martin Compact Fusion Reactor）是[[洛克希德馬丁|洛克希德馬丁]]公司[[臭鼬工廠|臭鼬工廠]]查爾斯·查斯（Chase Chase）領導的團隊開發的核融合計畫。這個計畫於2013年2月7日首次在Google Solve for X論壇上發布<ref>{{citation|title=FuseNet: The European Fusion Education Network|url=http://www.fusenet.eu/node/400|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130506035703/http://www.fusenet.eu/node/400|deadurl=no|archivedate=2013-05-06}}</ref>
。

這個計畫在不到一年的時間內建造和測試小型核融合反應爐，在五年內完成原型。原型將產生100百萬瓦電力，尺寸為7×10英尺（2.1×3.0米），可以安裝在大型卡車上，約為當前反應爐原型尺寸的十分之一<ref name=":2" />。

==歷史==
洛克希德馬丁於2010年開始核融合反應爐計畫<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url = http://www.theengineer.co.uk/news/news-analysis/new-details-on-compact-fusion-reveal-scale-of-challenge/1019393.article|title = New details on compact fusion reveal scale of challenge|last = Nathan|first = Stuart|date = 22 October 2014|work = The Engineer|accessdate = March 2015}}</ref>。

2014年10月，洛克希德·馬丁公司宣布將嘗試開發一種小型核融合反應爐，可以安裝在大型卡車上，產生100百萬瓦電力，足以為8萬人居住城鎮提供電力<ref>{{cite magazine |work=[[Aviation_Week_&_Space_Technology|Aviation Week & Space Technology]] |date=20 October 2014 |title=Fusion Frontier |first1=Guy |last1=Norris}}</ref>。

小型核融合反應爐（CFR）的首席設計師和技術小組負責人是湯瑪斯·馬怪爾（Thomas McGuire）<ref name="aw20141020">{{cite news |last1=Hedden |first1=Carole |title=Meet The Leader Of Skunk Works’ Compact Fusion Reactor Team |url=http://aviationweek.com/technology/meet-leader-skunk-works-compact-fusion-reactor-team |accessdate=2014-11-24 |work=[[Aviation_Week_&_Space_Technology|Aviation Week & Space Technology]] |date=2014-10-20}}</ref>。湯瑪斯·馬怪爾研究核融合作為太空推進能源，幫助美國太空總署改善[[火星|火星]]旅行時間<ref name="guyReac">{{citation |last1=Norris |first1=Guy |url=http://aviationweek.com/technology/skunk-works-reveals-compact-fusion-reactor-details |title=Skunk Works Reveals Compact Fusion Reactor Details |work=[[Aviation_Week_&_Space_Technology|Aviation Week & Space Technology]] |date=15 October 2014 |accessdate=18 October 2014 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141017164725/http://aviationweek.com/technology/skunk-works-reveals-compact-fusion-reactor-details |archivedate=2014-10-17}}</ref><ref name="guyHope">{{citation |last1=Norris |first1=Guy |url=http://aviationweek.com/blog/high-hopes-can-compact-fusion-unlock-new-power-space-and-air-transport |title=High Hopes – Can Compact Fusion Unlock New Power For Space And Air Transport? |work=[[Aviation_Week_&_Space_Technology|Aviation Week & Space Technology]] |date=14 October 2014 |archivedate=18 October 2014 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141018065452/http://aviationweek.com/blog/high-hopes-can-compact-fusion-unlock-new-power-space-and-air-transport |deadurl=no}}</ref><ref name="hedLead">{{citation |last1=Hedden |first1=Carole |url=http://aviationweek.com/technology/meet-leader-skunk-works-compact-fusion-reactor-team |title=The Leader Of Skunk Works’ Compact Fusion Reactor Team |work=[[Aviation_Week_&_Space_Technology|Aviation Week & Space Technology]] |date=20 October 2014 |archivedate=18 October 2014 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141018153950/http://aviationweek.com/technology/meet-leader-skunk-works-compact-fusion-reactor-team |deadurl=no}}</ref>。

2016年5月，Rob Weiss宣布洛克希德馬丁繼續支持該計畫，並將增加對計畫的投資<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=http://nextbigfuture.com/2016/05/lockheed-portable-fusion-proejct-still.html|title=Lockheed Portable Fusion project still making progress|first=Brian|last=Wang|date=May 3, 2016|website=[[Next_Big_Future|Next Big Future]]|access-date=2016-07-27}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.defensenews.com/story/defense/innovation/2016/05/03/lockheed-nuclear-fusion-generator-investment/83870398/|title=Lockheed Still Supporting Portable Nuclear Generator|first= Aaron |last=Mehta|date=May 3, 2016|access-date=2016-07-27}}</ref>。

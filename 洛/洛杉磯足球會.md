'''洛杉磯足球會'''是一個位於[[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]擁有擴張專營權的足球俱樂部。該隊計畫於2018年加入[[美國職業足球大聯盟|美國職業足球大聯盟]]<ref>{{cite press release |last=Bartlett |first=Blair |date=September 15, 2015 |title=Los Angeles' newest Major League Soccer team announces official club name and launches collaborative effort with supporters to identity club colors with social media campaign |url=https://cdn.lafc.com/uploads/2015/09/LAFC-Club-Name-Announcement-and-Colors-Press-Release-9.15.pdf |format=PDF |publisher=Los Angeles Football Club |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150916010044/https://cdn.lafc.com/uploads/2015/09/LAFC-Club-Name-Announcement-and-Colors-Press-Release-9.15.pdf |archivedate=2015年9月16日 |accessdate=September 15, 2015 }}</ref><ref name="latimes">{{cite news|url=http://www.latimes.com/sports/soccer/la-sp-la-soccer-stadium-20150518-story.html|title= Expansion L.A. soccer team plans new stadium on Sports Arena site|date=May 17, 2015|accessdate=May 18, 2015|newspaper=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]}}</ref>。該隊於2014年10月宣布<ref name="chivas shutdown">{{cite news|url=http://www.espnfc.com/chivas-usa/story/2112968/mls-shuts-down-chivas-usanew-clubownership-to-return-in-2017|title=MLS shuts down Chivas USA; new club, ownership to return in 2017|first=Jeff|last=Carlisle|publisher=ESPN FC|date=October 27, 2014|accessdate=October 28, 2014}}</ref>。

所有權組織由風險投資家阮寶黃、企業家[[彼得·古柏|彼得·古柏]]和前[[NBA|NBA]]高級管理人員 Tom Penn領導<ref>{{cite news | title = MLS officials to announce details of new franchise replacing Chivas USA | url = http://www.dailynews.com/sports/20141030/mls-officials-to-announce-details-of-new-franchise-replacing-chivas-usa | work = The Los Angeles Times | date = October 30, 2014 | accessdate = October 30, 2014}}</ref>。所有權組織還包括商人[[魯本·古納納林甘|魯本·古納納林甘]]和[[陳志遠_(商人)|陳志遠]]<ref name="LAFC inside story">{{cite web|url=http://www.si.com/soccer/planet-futbol/2014/11/07/henry-nguyen-tom-penn-mls-lafc-los-angeles-football-club|title=How did LAFC come to be? These businessmen are behind MLS' newest team - SI.com|author=Grant Wahl|work=SI.com|accessdate=September 16, 2015}}</ref>。該俱樂部還有21名投資者包括[[魔術強森|魔術強森]]、[[諾馬·賈西亞帕拉|諾馬·賈西亞帕拉]]、[[米婭·哈姆|米婭·哈姆]]、[[查德·赫利|查德·赫利]]、[[托尼·羅賓斯|托尼·羅賓斯]]和[[威爾·法洛|威爾·法洛]]<ref name="LAFC inside story"/><ref>{{cite news|url=http://prosoccertalk.nbcsports.com/2014/10/30/los-angeles-officially-given-new-mls-team-magic-johnson-vincent-tan-among-investors/|title=Los Angeles officially given new MLS team; Magic Johnson, Vincent Tan among investors|first=Nicholas|last=Mendola|work=Pro Soccer Talk|publisher=NBCSports.com |date =2014-10-30 | accessdate =2014-10-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.si.com/planet-futbol/2016/01/07/will-ferrell-lafc-part-owner-announcement-press-conference-introduction|title=Will Ferrell announced as LAFC part owner|date=2016-01-07|work=Sports Illustrated}}</ref>。

《'''洛拉·范瑟丝'''》（{{lang-en|Lola Versus}}，是2012年[[美国|美国]][[导演|导演]][[达里尔·文|达里尔·文]]执导的电影，[[剧本|剧本]]是文和他的合作伙伴合著的。由[[比尔·普尔曼|比尔·普尔曼]]、[[格里塔·葛韦格|格里塔·葛韦格]]、[[乔尔·吉纳曼|乔尔·吉纳曼]]、[[佐伊·李斯特·琼斯|佐伊·李斯特·琼斯]]和[[黛布拉·温格|黛布拉·温格]]主演<ref>[http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/06/07/lola-versus-filmmakers-zoe-lister-jones-daryl-wein-romantic-comedies_n_1579285.html  'Lola Versus' Filmmakers, Zoe Lister-Jones And Daryl Wein, Rap About Romantic Comedies, By Emma Gray, The Huffington Post, June 7, 2012]</ref><ref>[https://www.nytimes.com/2012/06/14/fashion/daryl-wein-and-zoe-lister-jones-partners-in-life-and-in-movies.html?_r=3&pagewanted=all In Life and Art, It’s All About Us, By David Amsden, The New York Times, June 13, 2012]</ref><ref>[http://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2012/06/daryl-wein-zoe-lister-jones-independent-versus-stu.html Daryl Wein & Zoe Lister Jones: Independent ''Versus'' Studio, By Caitlin Colford, Paste Magazine, June 16, 2012]</ref>。

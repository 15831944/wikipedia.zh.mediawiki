'''乔希·佩克'''（{{lang-en|Josh Peck}}，{{bd|1986年|11月10日|||catIdx=P佩}}）是美国导演、演员，1990年代他就作为一个童星出现在影视剧中。

== 生平 ==
1986年，佩克出生在[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]][[曼哈顿|曼哈顿]]，他曾在专业表演学校读书。他的父母并没有结婚，在他出生后，他从未见过自己的亲生父亲。佩克的母亲芭芭拉是一个[[犹太人|犹太人]]，他一直和母亲、外婆生活。<ref name="ref1">{{cite news|last=Keck|first=William|coauthors=|title=Josh Peck lightens up|pages=|work=[[USA_Today|USA Today]]|date=2004-08-25|url=http://www.usatoday.com/life/people/2004-08-25-peck_x.htm|accessdate=2008-01-25}}</ref><ref name="ref2">{{cite news|last= |first= |coauthors= |title=INTERVIEW: Josh Peck Talks Ice Age: The Meltdown |pages= |publisher=MovieWeb |date=2006-03-29 |url=http://www.movieweb.com/news/11/11811.php |accessdate=2008-01-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080113163909/http://www.movieweb.com/news/11/11811.php |archivedate=2008-01-13 }}</ref> 
童年时，佩克患有[[哮喘病|哮喘病]]，他只能呆在屋子里看情景剧。<ref>{{cite news|last=Syme |first=Rachel |url=http://www.nypost.com/pagesixmag/issues/20080629/Whos+Former+Teen+Star+Josh+Peck+Mans |title=Who's That: Former Teen Star Josh Peck Mans Up |work=[[New_York_Post|New York Post]] |date=2008-06-27 |accessdate=2010-08-04 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120106233840/http://www.nypost.com/pagesixmag/issues/20080629/Whos%2BFormer%2BTeen%2BStar%2BJosh%2BPeck%2BMans |archivedate=2012-01-06 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Murray|first=Rebecca|url=http://movies.about.com/od/meancreek/a/meansm081704_3.htm|title=Scott Mechlowicz, Trevor Morgan and Josh Peck Interview|work=[[About.com|About.com]]|quote = |date=|accessdate=2010-08-04}}</ref><ref name="ref3">{{cite news|last=Douglas |first=Edward |coauthors= |title=Josh Peck on The Wackness |pages= |publisher=ComingSoon.net |date=2008-01-25 |url=http://www.comingsoon.net/blog/2008/01/josh_peck_on_the_wackness.php |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080710062858/http://www.comingsoon.net/blog/2008/01/josh_peck_on_the_wackness.php |archivedate=2008-07-10 |accessdate=2008-01-25 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>
8岁时，他受到别人的鼓励，参加单口喜剧演出，后来他在纽约市参加儿童音乐剧，9岁时，他出演青年剧。

'''乔治敦'''（{{lang|en|Georgetown}}）是[[美国|美国]][[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[布朗县_(俄亥俄州)|布朗县]]的一个村，是布朗县的县城<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=2011-06-07 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531210815/http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=2011-05-31 |df= }}</ref>。2010年人口普查时人口为4331人。乔治敦是[[尤利西斯·格兰特|尤利西斯·格兰特]]的童年故乡。

==历史==
乔治敦在1819年被规划。<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=udUyAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA385#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=The History of Brown County, Ohio: Containing a History of the County, Its Townships, Towns, Churches, Schools, Etc. | publisher=Higginson Book Company | year=1883  | pages=385}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-bnYAQAAQBAJ&lpg=PP1&pg=PA13#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=120 Waterfall Hikes Around Cincinnati Ohio | publisher=Lulu.com | accessdate=27 November 2013 | author=Karle, Tina | pages=13}}</ref>村庄的名字来自肯塔基州的[[乔治敦_(肯塔基州)|乔治敦]]。<ref>{{cite book|last=Overman|first=William Daniel|title=Ohio Town Names|url=https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015015361465;view=1up;seq=66|year=1958|publisher=Atlantic Press|location=Akron, OH|page=50}}</ref> 一个叫乔治敦的邮局自1821年开始运作。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?state=OH |title=Post offices |publisher=Jim Forte Postal History |accessdate=11 June 2016 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151013181747/http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?state=OH |archivedate=2015年10月13日 |df= }}</ref>

'''莎麗·海明斯'''（'''Sally Hemings'''，{{bd|1773年||1835年}}）是一位非裔美國[[奴隸|奴隸]]，自從1802年的一篇報導出現以後，關於[[托马斯·杰斐逊|托马斯·杰斐逊]]是否為莎麗·海明斯數名子女的父親之問題一直倍受爭議。

根據傑佛遜的記載，她一共有5名子女：
* Harriet Hemings (I) (October 5, 1795 - December 7, 1797) <ref>http://www.whosyomama.com/gabroaddrick3/26807.htm</ref>
* Beverly Hemings ''(possibly born William Beverly Hemings)'' (April 1, 1798 - after 1873) <ref>http://www.whosyomama.com/gabroaddrick3/26808.htm</ref>
* Harriet Hemings (II) (May 22, 1801 - after 1863) <ref>http://www.whosyomama.com/gabroaddrick3/26809.htm</ref>
* [[Madison_Hemings|Madison Hemings]] ''(possibly born James Madison Hemings)'' (January 19, 1805 - 1877) <ref>http://www.whosyomama.com/gabroaddrick3/26810.htm</ref>
* Eston Hemings ''(possibly born Thomas Eston Hemings)'' (May 21, 1808 - 1856) <ref>http://www.whosyomama.com/gabroaddrick3/26811.htm</ref>

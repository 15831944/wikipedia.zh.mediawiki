'''莎莉卡'''（{{lang-en|'''Sarika'''}}，{{bd|1960年|12月5日|catIdx=Sarika}}）是[[印度|印度]]女演员和服装设计师。2005年，莎莉卡在電影《Parzania》中的出色表现榮獲[[印度國家電影獎|印度國家電影獎]]最佳女演員獎。<ref>[https://www.thehindu.com/features/cinema/Doing-it-her-way-Sarika/article16894359.ece “Doing it her way- Sarika”]. The Hindu. 26-11-2009.</ref><ref>[https://www.hindustantimes.com/entertainment/girl-interrupted/story-MZ5FNHd6wjEeWYqGWWe49J.html “Girl Interrupted”]. Hindustan Times. 14-07-2016.</ref>

她的前任丈夫是泰米爾納德邦的巨星[[卡茂爾·哈桑|卡茂爾·哈桑]]。<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/regional/kamal-haasan-gautami-breakup-celebrity-relationship-history-3732439/ “From Gautami to Sarika: The women in Kamal Haasan’s life”]. The Indian Express. 01-11-2016.</ref>两人共育有兩名女兒，分別是演员和导演。<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/regional/sarika-proud-of-daughters-shruti-aksharas-work/ “Sarika proud of daughters Shruti, Akshara’s work”]. The Indian Express. 10-01-2016.</ref>

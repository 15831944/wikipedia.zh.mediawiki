The Hindu. 3 August, 2003.</ref>。莎拉的祖父曾擔任印度板球代表隊的隊長、祖母[[莎米拉·泰戈|莎米拉·泰戈]]則是一名來自[[加尔各答|加尔各答]]名門的知名[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]女演員。她的父親[[賽伊夫·阿里·罕|賽伊夫·阿里·罕]]、母亲[[安姆麗塔·辛格|安姆麗塔·辛格]]及继母[[卡琳娜·卡浦爾|卡琳娜·卡浦爾]]均是寶萊塢演员。<ref>[https://www.timesnownews.com/entertainment/news/bollywood-news/article/kedarnath-success-has-made-my-parents-proud-sara-ali-khan/328331 “Kedarnath success has made my parents proud Sara Ali Khan”]. Times Now News. 09-12-2018.</ref><ref>[https://www.indiatoday.in/movies/story/sara-ali-khan-my-father-never-said-kareena-was-my-second-mother-1391518-2018-11-19#close-overlay “Sara Ali Khan: My father never said Kareena was my second mother

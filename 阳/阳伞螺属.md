'''阳伞螺属'''（学名：''{{lang|la|Onustus}}''）是[[缀壳螺科|缀壳螺科]]的一個[[屬_(生物)|屬]]{{r|shell_469}}。

==物種==
{{as of|2018|5|8}}，[[WoRMS|WoRMS]]本屬物種計有：
*Species ''[[Onustus_aquitanus|Onustus aquitanus]]'' {{small|Simone & Cunha, 2012}}
*Species ''[[Onustus_caribaeus|Onustus caribaeus]]'' {{small|(Petit de la Saussaye, 1857)}}
* [[阳伞螺|阳伞螺]] ''[[Onustus_exutus|Onustus exutus]]'' {{small|(Reeve, 1842)}}
* [[印度缀壳螺|印度缀壳螺]] ''[[Onustus_indicus|Onustus indicus]]'' {{small|(Gmelin, 1791)}}
*Species ''[[Onustus_longleyi|Onustus longleyi]]'' {{small|(Bartsch, 1931)}}
;異名
*Species Onustus flindersi Cotton & Godfrey, 1938 accepted as Xenophora flindersi (Cotton & Godfrey, 1938) (original combination)
*Species Onustus javanicus Gray, 1857 accepted as Xenophora solarioides (Reeve, 1845) (synonym)
*Species Onustus peronianus Iredale, 1929 accepted as Xenophora peroniana (Iredale, 1929) (original combination)
*Species Onustus prognatus Finlay, 1926 † accepted as Xenophora prognata (Finlay, 1926) †

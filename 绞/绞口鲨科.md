'''絞口鯊科'''（學名''Ginglymostomatidae''）又稱'''鏽鬚鮫科'''，是[[板鰓亞綱|板鰓亞綱]][[鬚鯊目|鬚鯊目]]的其中一科。

==分類==
'''絞口鯊科'''下分為3個屬，如下：
*[[絞口鯊屬|絞口鯊屬]](''Ginglymostoma'') <small>[[約翰內斯·彼得·繆勒|J. P. Müller]] & [[Friedrich_Gustav_Jakob_Henle|Henle]], 1837</small>
**[[鉸口鯊|鉸口鯊]](''Ginglymostoma cirratum'') <small>[[Pierre_Joseph_Bonnaterre|Bonnaterre]], 1788</small>：俗名护士鲨
**[[太平洋護士鯊|太平洋護士鯊]](''Ginglymostoma unami'') <small>[[Luis_Fernando_Del-Moral-Flores|Del-Moral-Flores]], [[Emmanuel_Ramírez-Antonio|Ramírez-Antonio]], [[Arturo_Angulo_Sibaja|Angulo]] & [[Gerardo_Pérez-Ponce_de_León|Pérez-Ponce de León]], 2015</small> <ref name=Del-Moral-Flores2015>{{cite journal | authors = Moral-Flores, L.F.D., Ramírez-Antonio, E., Angulo, A. & Pérez-Ponce de León, G. | year = 2015 | title = ''Ginglymostoma unami'' sp. nov. (Chondrichthyes: Orectolobiformes: Ginglymostomatidae): a new species of nurse shark from the Tropical Eastern Pacific | journal = Revista Mexicana de Biodiversidad | volume = 86 | pages = 48–58 | url = https://www.researchgate.net/publication/274270842_Ginglymostoma_unami_sp._nov._%28Chondrichthyes_Orectolobiformes_Ginglymostomatidae%29_una_especie_nueva_de_tiburn_gata_del_Pacfico_oriental_tropical | doi=10.7550/rmb.46192}}</ref>
*[[光鱗鯊屬|光鱗鯊屬]](''Nebrius'') <small>[[Eduard_Rüppell|Rüppell]], 1837</small>
**[[鏽鬚鮫|鏽鬚鮫]](''Nebrius ferrugineus'') <small>[[勒內-普里梅韋勒·萊松|Lesson]], 1831</small>：又稱光鱗鯊。
*[[護士鯊屬|護士鯊屬]](''Pseudoginglymostoma'') <small>[[Guido_Dingerkus|Dingerkus]], 1986</small>
**[[短尾護士鯊|短尾護士鯊]](''Pseudoginglymostoma brevicaudatum'') <small>[[阿爾伯特·甘瑟|Günther]], 1867</small>：俗名短尾鉸口鯊、短尾光鱗鯊

'''扎卡里·泰勒·“扎克”·多诺霍'''（{{lang-en|Zachary Tyler "Zach" Donohue}}，{{bd|1991年|1月8日||}}）生于康乃狄克州哈特福，是一名美国男子花样滑冰运动员，主攻冰舞<ref name=ISU-1718/>。他的家乡在[[麦迪逊_(康乃狄克州)|麦迪逊]]<ref name=USABio>{{cite web |url= https://www.teamusa.org/us-figure-skating/athletes/Zach-Donohue |title= Zach Donohue |work=United States Olympic Committee |accessdate=2019-03-27 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190127094624/https://www.teamusa.org/us-figure-skating/athletes/Zach-Donohue |archivedate=2019-01-27 }}</ref>。他在10岁时开始练习滑冰<ref name="nbc">{{cite web|url=https://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/sports/Zach-Donohue-Madison-Hubbell-Pyeongchang-Olympics-Figure-Skating-471304164.html|title=Connecticut's Zach Donohue, Partner Madison Hubbell Look for Ice Dance Medal in Pyeongchang|publisher=NBC New York|date=2018-01-26|accessdate=2019-03-27|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190327082637/https://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/sports/Zach-Donohue-Madison-Hubbell-Pyeongchang-Olympics-Figure-Skating-471304164.html|archivedate=2019-03-27}}</ref>。他在2011年开始和[[麦迪逊·胡贝尔|麦迪逊·胡贝尔]]搭档，在此之前，他曾和四个不同的女选手搭档<ref name=USFS110512>{{cite news |url= http://www.usfsa.org/Story.asp?id=46152&type=media |title= Madison Hubbell and Keiffer Hubbell Announce End of Partnership |publisher= U.S. Figure Skating |date= 2011-05-12 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20120324071503/http://www.usfsa.org/Story.asp?id=46152&type=media |archivedate= 2012-03-24 |deadurl= unfit }}</ref><ref name="nbc"/>。

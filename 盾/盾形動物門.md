=== 側水母綱（Dipleurozoa） ===
{{TransH}}
The dipleurozoan body is subradial, divided by isomers entirely (e.g., ''[[Dickinsonia|Dickinsonia]]'' and ''{{le|Phyllozoon|Phyllozoon}}''). ''Dickinsonia'' juveniles show undivided anterior areas but these regions were reduced in the course of ontogeny, and in the adult stages ''Dickinsonia''-like proarticulates changed so radically that they became almost indistinguishable from isomers.<ref name=Ivantsov2004/><ref name=Ivantsov2007/><ref name=Ivantsov2002>{{cite journal

=== Cephalozoa ===
其身體前端沒有異構體節，所以呈現不完整的分割。例如：{{le|約吉亞蟲|Yorgia}}、''{{le|Praecambridium|Praecambridium}}''、''{{le|Andiva|Andiva}}''、''[[Archaeaspinus|Archaeaspinus]]''、''[[Ivovicia|Ivovicia]]''、[[斯普里格蠕蟲|斯普里格蠕蟲]]、''{{le|Marywadea|Marywadea}}''和''[[Cyanorus|Cyanorus]]''等。<ref name=Ivantsov2001/><ref name=Ivantsov2004/><ref name=Ivantsov2004Prato/><ref name=Ivantsov2007>{{cite journal
 | author=Ivantsov, A. Yu.
 
 | title = Small Vendian transversely Articulated fossils

 | volume = 41
 | issue = 2

}}</ref>{{TransH}}
 Some cephalozoans from the [[科_(生物)|family]] [[Yorgiidae|Yorgiidae]] demonstrate pronounced asymmetry of left and right parts of the body. For instance, ''Yorgia''’s initial right isomer is the only one which spreads far towards the left side of the body. ''Archaeaspinus'' has an unpaired anterior lobe confined by the furrow to the left side only.<ref name=Ivantsov2001/><ref name=Ivantsov1999>{{cite journal

=== 其他[[地位未定|地位未定]]物種 ===
在''{{le|Onega stepanovi|Onega stepanovi}}''和''{{le|Tamga hamulifera|Tamga (genus)}}''中，所有的異構體節都被外圍的不分割區域包圍。''Onega stepanovi''的異構體節彼此相互接觸，形成筏狀區域，至於''Tamga hamulifera''的異構體節則彼此分離且不接觸。在''{{le|Lossinia|Lossinia}}''中，中心未分割的區域沒有明顯可見的異構體節，而是將來自未分割區域外圍的葉狀異構體節作為“橫向[[關節|關節]]”發出。<ref name=Ivantsov2007/>

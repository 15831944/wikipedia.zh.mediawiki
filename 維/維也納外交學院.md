'''維也納外交學院''' ('''DA'''; 德語為 ''Diplomatische Akademie Wien'')是一所位於[[奧地利|奧地利]][[維也納|維也納]]的專業研究生院，為學生提供專業的[[國際事務|國際事務]][[政治科學|政治科學]]、法律、語言、語言和經濟<ref name=DAWwebsite>{{cite web |title=Diplomatic Academy of Vienna |url=http://www.da-vienna.ac.at/diplomatic_academy_of_vienna |work=www.da-vienna.ac.at |publisher=Diplomatic Academy |accessdate=15 February 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120215152340/http://www.da-vienna.ac.at/diplomatic_academy_of_vienna |archivedate=15 February 2012 |df= }}</ref>學校另外也擁有[[法語|法語]]名稱''École des Hautes Études Internationales de Vienne''。

The school confers [[Master's_degree|Master's degree]]s and [[postgraduate|postgraduate]] diplomas upon its graduates. It is the only member of the [[Association_of_Professional_Schools_of_International_Affairs|Association of Professional Schools of International Affairs]] in Austria.<ref name=APSIAProfile>{{cite web|title=Diplomatic Academy of Vienna Profile |url=http://www.apsia.org/apsia/members/members.php?institutionID=125 |work=APSIA website |publisher=Association of Professional Schools of International Affairs |accessdate=15 February 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111215064951/http://www.apsia.org/apsia/members/members.php?institutionID=125 |archivedate=15 December 2011 |df= }}</ref> The Diplomatic Academy's current director is Ambassador [[Emil_Brix|Emil Brix]] (b. 1956 in Vienna), an Austrian diplomat and historian.<ref name=DAteam>{{cite web|title=The DA welcomes its new director, Emil Brix |url=https://www.da-vienna.ac.at/en/The-Academy/News-and-Media/MoreInformation/Id/119/The-DA-welcomes-its-new-director-Amb-Emil-Brix |publisher=Diplomatic Academy of Vienna |accessdate=26 October 2017}}</ref>

==歷史==
學院一開始由[[玛丽亚·特蕾西亚|玛丽亚·特蕾西亚]]在1754年成立，名為"東方學院"，為培訓[[哈布斯堡王朝|哈布斯堡王朝]]的外交官而立。The school was renamed multiple times and reorganized over the centuries, and it eventually gained independent public institution status in 1996.<ref name=Pfusterschmid>{{cite book|last=Pfusterschmid-Hardtenstein|first=Heinrich|title=A Short History of the Diplomatic Academy of Vienna|year=2008|publisher=Diplomatic Academy of Vienna|location=Vienna|isbn=978-3-902021-57-1|pages=58|url=http://www.da-vienna.ac.at/jart/prj3/diplomatische_akademie/data/uploads/geschichteHistory/ShortHistoryDA.pdf}}</ref> Given its roots, the Diplomatic Academy claims to be the oldest school of its kind, one that is dedicated to professional foreign affairs training.<ref name=DAhistory>{{cite web |title=The History of the Diplomatic Academy of Vienna |url=http://www.da-vienna.ac.at/diplomatic_academy_of_vienna/history |publisher=Diplomatic Academy of Vienna |accessdate=15 February 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111228130517/http://www.da-vienna.ac.at/diplomatic_academy_of_vienna/history |archivedate=28 December 2011 |df= }}</ref>

==學術==
學院只提供研究生學位。學生可以選擇修讀兩年制的高階國際研究碩士(MAIS)或環境科技與國際事務碩士(MSc ETIA)學位。MAIS課程與[[維也納大學|維也納大學]]合作提供，MSc ETIA課程則與[[維也納工業大學|維也納工業大學]]合作提供。 A one-year "Diploma Programme" is also available to postgraduates.<ref name=APSIAProfile/>

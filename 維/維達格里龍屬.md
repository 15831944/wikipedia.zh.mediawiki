'''維達格里龍屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Vitakridrinda''）是[[獸腳亞目|獸腳亞目]][[阿貝力龍科|阿貝力龍科]][[恐龍|恐龍]]的一屬，生存於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]晚期的[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]。

[[巴基斯坦地質測量局|巴基斯坦地質測量局]]的一群古生物學家，在[[俾路支省|俾路支省]]發現許多恐龍化石，屬於Pab組的維達格里段（Vitakri Member），地質年代相當於[[馬斯垂克階|馬斯垂克階]]<ref name=dawn>{{cite web |url=http://www.dawn.com/weekly/science/archive/060325/science5.htm |title=Country’s first dinosaur fossils |accessdate=2007-11-09 |last=Rana |first=A.N. |date=2006-03-25 |publisher=DAWN}}</ref><ref name=MSM06>{{cite journal |last=Malkani |first=M.S. |year=2006 |title=Biodiversity of saurischian dinosaurs from the Latest Cretaceous Park of Pakistan |journal=Journal of Applied and Emerging Sciences |volume=1 |issue=3 |pages=108–140 |url=http://www.gsp.gov.pk/Malkani%202006b.pdf |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5hjMtRzjs?url=http://www.gsp.gov.pk/Malkani%202006b.pdf |archivedate=2009-06-22 }}</ref>。

[[正模標本|正模標本]]包含：腦殼、一顆牙齒、兩個[[股骨|股骨]]、以及一個部分口鼻部。除此之外，一些脊椎也可能屬於維達格里龍<ref name=MSM06vita>{{cite journal |last=Malkani |first=M.S. |year=2006 |title=First rostrum of carnivorous ''Vitakridrinda'' (Abelisaurid theropod dinosaur) found from the Latest Cretaceous dinosaur beds (Vitakri) Member of Pab Formation, Alam Kali Kakor Locality of Viakri area, Darkham District, Balochistan, Pakistan |journal=Sindh University Y Research Journal (Science Series) |volume=38 |issue=3 |pages=7–26}}</ref><ref name="DinoData">{{cite web| last =| first =| authorlink =| coauthors =| title =Vitakridrinda sulaimani | work =| publisher =DinoData.org| year =2007| url =http://www.dinodata.org/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=9667&Itemid=67| format =| doi =| accessdate=2007-11-08}}</ref>。

'''維爾納·科赫'''（{{lang-de|Werner Koch}}，{{bd|1961年|7月11日}}），德國[[自由軟體|自由軟體]][[軟體設計師|設計師]]<ref name="wayner-new-york-times">{{cite news | url = http://partners.nytimes.com/library/tech/99/11/cyber/articles/19encrypt.html | date = 1999-11-19 | accessdate = 2015-02-06 | first = Peter | last = Wayner | newspaper = [[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] | title = Germany Awards Grant for Encryption}}</ref> ，[[GnuPG|GnuPG]]的主要作者<ref name="angwin-propublica">{{cite web | url = https://www.propublica.org/article/the-worlds-email-encryption-software-relies-on-one-guy-who-is-going-broke | date = 2015-02-05 | accessdate = 2015-02-06 | first = Julia | last = Angwin | publisher = [[ProPublica|ProPublica]] | title = The World’s Email Encryption Software Relies on One Guy, Who is Going Broke | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150206005618/http://www.propublica.org/article/the-worlds-email-encryption-software-relies-on-one-guy-who-is-going-broke | archive-date = 2015-02-06 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，同時亦是{{link-en|歐洲自由軟體基金會|Free Software Foundation Europe}}的辦公室主任和德國分部的副部長。2015年，他因開發GnuPG的工作而獲頒{{link-en|FSF自由軟體獎|Award for the Advancement of Free Software|FSF自由軟體促進獎}}<ref name=fsf2015>[https://www.fsf.org/news/library-freedom-project-and-werner-koch-are-2015-free-software-awards-winners Library Freedom Project and Werner Koch are 2015 Free Software Awards winners] {{Wayback|url=https://www.fsf.org/news/library-freedom-project-and-werner-koch-are-2015-free-software-awards-winners |date=20160320004349 }} FSF</ref>。

記者和安全技術人員非常依賴科赫創建的GnuPG，洩露[[美國國家安全局|美國國家安全局]]（NSA）[[棱鏡計劃|棱鏡計劃]]的[[愛德華·斯諾登|愛德華·斯諾登]]亦用它來逃避監控<ref name="goodin-ars">{{cite web | url = https://arstechnica.com/security/2015/02/once-starving-gnupg-crypto-project-gets-a-windfall-but-can-it-be-saved/ | date = 2015-02-06 | accessdate = 2015-07-28 | first = Dan | last = Goodin | publisher = [[Condé_Nast|Condé Nast]] | website = [[Ars_Technica|Ars Technica]] | title = Once-starving GnuPG crypto project gets a windfall. Now comes the hard part | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150715070510/http://arstechnica.com/security/2015/02/once-starving-gnupg-crypto-project-gets-a-windfall-but-can-it-be-saved/ | archive-date = 2015-07-15 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

== 生平和工作 ==
科赫在1961年7月11日出生於[[西德|西德]]的[[杜塞多夫|杜塞多夫]]，現時居於鄰近杜塞多夫的[[埃尔克拉特|埃尔克拉特]]。他於1997年開始編寫GnuPG，其靈感啟發自[[理查德·斯托曼|理查德·斯托曼]]的演講<ref name="angwin-propublica" />。最終科赫於1999年釋出GnuPG的第一個版本，它之後也成為大多數流行的電子郵件加密程序的基礎，包括GPGTools、[[Enigmail|Enigmail]]和[[Gpg4win|Gpg4win]]<ref name="angwin-propublica" />。

在1999年，科赫憑著他在{{link-en|德國Unix用戶組|German Unix User Group}}的董事會職位<ref name="angwin-propublica" />獲得[[德國聯邦經濟事務和能源部|德國聯邦經濟事務和能源部]]318,000[[德國馬克|德國馬克]]（約170,000美元）的資助，以使GnuPG與Microsoft Windows兼容<ref name="wayner-new-york-times" />。2005年，科赫收到一份支持[[S/MIME|S/MIME]]開發工作的德國政府合同。

雖然GnuPG一直很受歡迎，但科赫卻長期陷於經濟困難，自2001年開始每年的淨收入就只有大約25,000美元<ref name="angwin-propublica" />，因此他考慮放棄該項目並從事更好的牟利編程工作<ref name="goodin-ars" />。但科赫注意到斯諾登事件發生後NSA對GnuPG進行監視，因此他選擇繼續運營<ref name="goodin-ars" />。在2014年，他舉辦了資助活動，並在其中獲得了137,000美元的公眾捐款<ref name="angwin-propublica" />，而[[Facebook|Facebook]]和[[Stripe|Stripe]]亦各自承諾每年向GnuPG捐獻五萬美元<ref name="angwin-propublica" /><ref name="thomson-the-register" />。2015年，科赫還得到[[Linux基金會|Linux基金會]]的{{link-en|核心基礎設施倡導者|Core Infrastructure Initiative}}一次性撥款60,000美元<ref name="thomson-the-register">{{cite news | url = https://www.theregister.co.uk/2015/02/05/gnupg_funding/ | date = 2015-02-05 | accessdate = 2015-02-06 | first = Iain | last = Thomson | newspaper = The Register | title = Internet lobs $$$s at dev of crucial GPG tool after he runs short of cash | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150206040021/http://www.theregister.co.uk/2015/02/05/gnupg_funding/ | archive-date = 2015-02-06 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.linuxfoundation.org/news-media/blogs/browse/2015/02/answering-call-werner-koch-s-everywhere | date = 2015-02-08 | accessdate = 2015-07-28 | first = Jim | last = Zemlin | publisher = [[Linux基金會|Linux Foundation]] | title = Answering the Call for Werner Koch’s Everywhere | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150906203340/http://www.linuxfoundation.org/news-media/blogs/browse/2015/02/answering-call-werner-koch-s-everywhere | archive-date = 2015-09-06 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>。

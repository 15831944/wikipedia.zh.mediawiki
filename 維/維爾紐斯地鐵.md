'''維爾紐斯地鐵'''（{{lang-lt|Vilniaus metropolitenas}}）是[[立陶宛|立陶宛]]首都[[維爾紐斯|維爾紐斯]]的一個提議的地鐵系統。維爾紐斯地鐵包括三條路線<ref>{{cite web|title=Vilnius is Becoming a Bridge Between Eastern and Western Europe|publisher=[[Vilnius_city_municipality|Vilnius city municipality]]|url=http://www.vilnius.lt/newvilniusweb/index.php/116/?itemID=34902|year=2002|accessdate=2008-10-04|quote=A new and modern means of transportation to be introduced into Vilnius City in the near future is considered now. That is the fast tramway, the routes of which shall connect the main city districts with the centre and workplaces.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Metro šalininkai siūlo Vilniui tris linijas ir tiltą|publisher=[[delfi.lt|delfi.lt]]|url=http://www.delfi.lt/archive/article.php?id=13628217|year=2007|accessdate=2008-10-04|language=lt}}</ref>，連接維爾紐斯人口最多和最繁忙的城區，修建目的是減輕交通堵塞<ref>{{cite web|title=Tramvajus ar metropolitenas Vilniaus urbanistinėje struktū roje|publisher=[[Gediminas_Technical_University|Gediminas Technical University]]|url=http://www.tpa.vgtu.lt/upload/urban_zur/59-70_juskevicius%20et%20al_tramvajus%202008-32-2.pdf|format=PDF|year=2008|accessdate=2008-10-04|language=lt|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110722153115/http://www.tpa.vgtu.lt/upload/urban_zur/59-70_juskevicius%20et%20al_tramvajus%202008-32-2.pdf|archivedate=2011-07-22|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Riga and Vilnius tackle traffic|publisher=[[The_Baltic_Times|The Baltic Times]]|url=http://www.baltictimes.com/news/articles/19007/|year=2007|accessdate=2008-10-04}}</ref>。維爾紐斯地鐵計劃在公眾中引發廣泛討論，其高昂的成本和可能破壞舊城區歷史建築均是引發反對的原因。該計劃雖然得到立陶宛政府接受，但被立陶宛總統[[達利婭·格里包斯凱特|達利婭·格里包斯凱特]]否決<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.vz.lt/archive/article/2014/7/25/prezidente-vetavo-metropoliteno-istatyma|title=Prezidentė vetavo Metropoliteno įstatymą|last=Aukštuolytė|first=Stasys Gudavičius, Rima|work=vz.lt|access-date=2018-08-02|language=lt-LT}}</ref>。

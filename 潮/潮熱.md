'''潮热'''（英語：hot flash）多发于女性[[更年期|更年期]]时，因為體內的[[雌二醇|雌二醇]]減少而引起{{tsl|en|Flushing (physiology)|皮膚發紅}}的一种生理症狀。
<!--'''Hot flashes''' (also known as '''hot flushes''') are a form of {{tsl|en|Flushing (physiology)|臉紅、皮膚發紅（因尷尬、發熱等原因）}} due to reduced levels of [[雌二醇|estradiol]].-->
導致潮熱的因素可能不只一種，然而常見的因素是：於[[更年期|更年期]]時，體內的[[荷爾蒙|荷爾蒙]]下降而引起。
<!--Hot flashes are a symptom which may have several other causes, but which is often caused by the changing [[激素|hormone]] levels that are characteristic of [[更年期|menopause]].--> 
遇到潮熱的人大多會感受到燥熱、多汗和心跳快速等，而且每次潮熱大概會持續2~30分鐘不等。
<!--They are typically experienced as a feeling of intense heat with sweating and rapid heartbeat, and may typically last from 2 to 30 minutes for each occurrence. -->

==症狀和徵兆 <!--Signs and symptoms--> ==
遇到潮熱的人（常見於處在[[更年期|更年期]]和[[近更年期|近更年期]]<!--perimenopause-->的人）大多會感受到燥熱、[[汗液|多汗]]和[[心跳|心跳]]快速等。每次潮熱大概會持續2~30分鐘不等，且兩個潮熱的間隔<u>可以</u>趨近零秒。

<!--Hot flashes, a common symptom of [[更年期|menopause]] and 【[[更年期|perimenopause]] 錯誤的重定向】, are typically experienced as a feeling of intense heat with [[汗液|sweating]] and rapid [[心率|heartbeat]], and may typically last from two to thirty minutes for each occurrence, ending just as rapidly as they began. -->

嚴重的潮熱可能導致晚上睡眠品質變差，進而引起[[失眠|失眠]]、影響[[心情|心情]]、[[專注力|專注力]]、和其他生理問題。
<!--Severe hot flashes can make it difficult to get a full night's sleep (often characterized as [[失眠|insomnia]]), which in turn can affect mood, impair concentration, and cause other physical problems. -->
出現在深夜的潮熱也被稱為「夜裡盜汗（ "night sweats"）」。這是因為[[雌激素|雌激素]]在體內的濃度通常在深夜時分最低。有些女性可能遇到「夜裡盜汗（ "night sweats"）」但其他時候卻無潮熱發生的狀況。
<!--When hot flashes occur at night, they are called "night sweats". As [[雌激素|estrogen]] is typically lowest at night, some women get night sweats without having any hot flashes during the daytime.--><ref>{{cite web | author = University of Glasgow | authorlink = University of Glasgow | title = Doctors seek the key to understanding hot flushes | work = University News (Archive of news) | publisher = University of Glasgow   | date = 24 October 2007 | url = http://www.gla.ac.uk/news/archiveofnews/2007/october/headline_50212_en.html | accessdate = 19 April 2013}}</ref>
===年輕女性 <!--Young women--> ===
如果年輕女性遇到潮熱，則可能是[[腦下垂體|腦下垂體]]出問題的徵兆，應該盡速至[[神經科|神經科]]求診。<!--If hot flashes occur at other times in a young woman's menstrual cycle, then it might be a symptom of a problem with her [[腦下垂體|pituitary gland]]; seeing a doctor is highly recommended. -->
如果是手術關係導致提前停經，則潮熱的強度可能會高於自然停經的女性且持續到自然停經的年紀。
<!--In younger women who are surgically menopausal, hot flashes are generally more intense than in older women, and they may last until natural age at menopause.--><ref name="Menopause on About.com">{{cite web | last = About.com | title = Menopause | publisher = About.com | date = n.d. | url = http://adam.about.net/reports/Menopause.htm | accessdate = 19 April 2013 }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

===男性 <!--Men--> ===
男性出現潮紅的原因可能有很多。潮紅對男性來說可能是低[[testosterone|睪丸素]]的徵兆。
<!-- Hot flashes in men could have various causes. It can be a sign of low testosterone.--><ref>{{cite web | last = Bunyavanich | first = Supinda | title = Low Testosterone Could Kill You | work = ABC News | publisher = ABC News Internet Ventures | date = 6 June 2007 | url = http://abcnews.go.com/Health/ActiveAging/story?id=3247773#.UXKwlcWhhCI | accessdate = 20 April 2013}}</ref> 
另一個可能的徵兆則是：{{tsl|en|andropause||andropause}}、或「男性更年期」。<!--Another is {{tsl|en|andropause||andropause}}, or "male menopause".--><ref name="livestrong.com">{{cite web | last = Holley | first = Casey L. | title = 5 Things You Need to Know About the Causes of Hot Flashes in Men | work = Livestrong.com | publisher = Demand Media Inc. | date = 11 August 2011 | url = http://www.livestrong.com/article/9770-need-causes-hot-flashes-men/ | accessdate = 20 April 2013}}</ref> 
罹患[[前列腺癌|前列腺癌]]或[[睾丸癌|睾丸癌]]的男性也可能遇到潮熱。在那些正在以[[抗雄激素|抗雄激素]]治療的人來說，潮熱出現的機率也會大增，因為這會讓睪丸素的濃度降到與睪丸被閹割後睪丸素水平附近。<!--Men with [[前列腺癌|prostate cancer]] or [[睾丸癌|testicular cancer]] can also have hot flashes, especially those who are undergoing hormone therapy with [[抗雄激素|antiandrogens]], also known as androgen antagonists, which reduce testosterone to castrate levels.--><ref>{{cite web | title = What to Expect During Therapy | work = Lupron Depot | publisher = Abbott Laboratories | url =http://www.lupronadvancedprostatecancer.com/about-lupron-depot/what-to-expect-during-therapy.cfm | accessdate = 20 April 2013}}</ref> 
例如[[太監|太監]]等曾被[[閹割|閹割]]的人也較有可能遇到潮熱。
<!--Men who are [[閹割|castrated]] can also get hot flashes.--><ref>{{cite web | title = Hot Flashes In Men -- Mayo Clinic Researchers Describe A Treatment | work = ScienceDaily   | publisher = Science Daily LLC | date = 19 October 2004 | url = http://sciencedaily.com/releases/2004/10/041019085808.htm | accessdate = 20 April 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | last = Engstrom | first = Christine A. | last2 = Kasper | first2 = Christine E. | title = Physiology and Endocrinology of Hot Flashes in Prostate Cancer | journal = American Journal of Men's Health | volume = 1 | issue = 1 | pages = 8–17 | publisher = Sage Publications | date = March 2007 | url = http://jmh.sagepub.com/content/1/1/8.abstract | doi = 10.1177/1557988306294162 | accessdate = 20 April 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | last = Anitei | first = Stefan | title = Men Can Experience Hot Flashes, Just Like Women in Menopause | work = Softpedia | publisher = Softpedia   | date = 16 April 2007 | url = http://news.softpedia.com/news/Men-Can-Experience-Hot-Flashes-Like-Menopause-Women-52030.shtml | accessdate = 20 April 2013}}</ref>
當然還有其他病症（ailment not aliment）可能導致男性潮紅。[[飲食|飲食]]也有可能在潮紅中扮演一個腳色。<!--There are also other ailments and even dietary changes which can cause it.--><ref name="livestrong.com"/> 

===種類 <!--Types--> ===

==中醫觀點==
{{medref}}
'''潮热'''为[[中医学|中医学]]病症，发热起伏如潮水之涨退有时。
本症有虚实之分：实者为阳盛所致，如热病过程中，有日晡潮热、谵语、腹满而硬、脉沉实有力等症，治宜泄热、通腑为主；虚者为阴虚所致，如疲劳，有两颧发赤、手足心热、盗汗等，治宜滋阴清热。

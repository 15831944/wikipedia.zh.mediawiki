'''梅拉妮·马尔加里斯'''（{{lang-en|Melanie Margalis}}，{{bd|1991年|12月30日||}}）生于佛罗里达州克利尔沃特，是一名美国女子游泳运动员，主攻混合泳。她的哥哥[[罗伯特·马尔加里斯|罗伯特·马尔加里斯]]和姐姐斯蒂芬妮·马尔加里斯同样都是游泳选手<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.swimmingworldmagazine.com/news/6-things-you-dont-know-about-olympic-rookie-melanie-margalis/|title=6 Things You Don't Know About Olympic Rookie Melanie Margalis|publisher=Swimming World Magazine|date=2016-07-25|accessdate=2019-01-15}}</ref>，她也正是在10岁时跟随着哥哥的脚步开始练习游泳的<ref>{{cite web|url=http://northeastjournal.org/st-petersburg-swimming-in-olympic-gold/|title=St. Petersburg Swimming in Olympic Gold|publisher=Northeast Journal|date=2016-09-09|accessdate=2019-01-15}}</ref>。她在乡村高中（{{lang|en|Countryside High School}}）毕业<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tampabay.com/sports/olympics/michael-phelps-makes-fifth-olympics-countryside-highs-melanie-margalis/2283656|title=Michael Phelps makes fifth Olympics; Countryside High's Melanie Margalis makes first|publisher=Tampa Bay Times|date=2016-06-30|accessdate=2019-01-15}}</ref>，大学时就读于乔治亚大学，期间她代表该校校队参加国内比赛<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.redandblack.com/sports/georgia-women-s-swim-and-dive-picks-up-sixth-national/article_3547cdda-b298-11e3-bb2a-001a4bcf6878.html|title=Georgia women's swim and dive picks up sixth national championship|last=Eggers|first=Nikki|date=2014-03-23|publisher=Morris Communications|work=Athens Banner-Herald|accessdate=2014-03-26}}</ref>。

'''梅根·杜哈梅尔'''（{{lang-en|Meagan Duhamel}}，{{bd|1985年|12月8日||}}）生于安大略省大萨德伯里，是一名加拿大女子花样滑冰运动员，主攻双人滑。杜哈梅尔在沃尔登长大<ref>{{cite web |url=http://olympic.ca/team-canada/meagan-duhamel/ |title=Meagan DUHAMEL |work= Canadian Olympic Committee |accessdate=2015-02-20|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181129145200/http://olympic.ca/team-canada/meagan-duhamel/|archivedate=2018-11-29}}</ref>。她三岁时开始练习滑冰<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/figure-skating/athlete-profile-n3022231-meagan-duhamel.htm|title=Figure Skating {{!}} Athlete Profile: Meagan DUHAMEL|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-20|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180223014735/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/figure-skating/athlete-profile-n3022231-meagan-duhamel.htm|archivedate=2018-02-23}}</ref>。14岁时，她移居至[[巴里_(安大略省)|巴里]]，在那里接受更加专业的训练<ref name=TCP130204>{{cite news |url= http://www.680news.com/2013/02/04/different-paths-brought-duhamel-radford-to-the-canadian-pairs-podium/ |title= Different paths brought Duhamel, Radford to the Canadian pairs podium |first= Lori |last= Ewing |work= The Canadian Press |publisher= 680 News |date= 2013-02-04 |accessdate=2019-01-20 }}</ref>。

杜哈梅尔在2008年某一天在机场看到一本关于[[素食主义|素食主义]]的书后，便于12月决定成为一名素食主义者<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/sports/olympics/winter/figureskating/meagan-duhamel-s-vegan-diet-helps-her-train-focus-recover-1.3516294|title=Meagan Duhamel's vegan diet helps her train, focus, recover|publisher=CBC|date=2016-04-01|accessdate=2019-01-20|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161223044315/http://www.cbc.ca/sports/olympics/winter/figureskating/meagan-duhamel-s-vegan-diet-helps-her-train-focus-recover-1.3516294|archivedate=2016-12-23}}</ref>。2014年7月，她和花样滑冰教练[[Bruno_Marcotte|Bruno Marcotte]]订婚<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.oasport.it/2014/10/meagan-duhamel-and-eric-radford-figure-skating-stars-were-training-quadruple-salchow-we-want-to-win/|title=Meagan Duhamel and Eric Radford, figure skating stars: "We're training quadruple salchow. We want to win"|publisher=OA Sport|date=2014-10-18|accessdate=2019-01-20}}</ref>，两人于2015年6月在[[百慕大|百慕大]]正式结婚<ref>{{cite web |last1=Brannen |first1=Sarah S. |title=The Inside Edge: Pairs skaters say 'I do' in droves |url=http://www.icenetwork.com/news/2015/06/08/129290114 |website=IceNetwork.com |date=2015-06-08 |access-date=2019-01-20 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150621080909/http://www.icenetwork.com/news/2015/06/08/129290114 |archive-date=2015-06-21 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。

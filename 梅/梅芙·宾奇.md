'''梅芙·宾奇·斯内尔'''（'''Maeve Binchy Snell'''，{{bd|1939年|5月28日|2012年|7月30日|catIdx=B}}<ref name="dudgeon">Born 1939 as per biography, ''Maeve Binchy'' by Piers Dudgeon, Thomas Dunne Books  2013; {{ISBN|978-1-250-04714-4}} (hardcover), pp. 4, 280, 302; {{ISBN|978-1-4668-4750-7}} (ebook)</ref>）经常被称为'''梅芙·宾奇'''（'''Maeve Binchy'''），是一位[[爱尔兰人|爱尔兰]]小说家，剧作家，专栏作家和演说家，以她对爱尔兰小镇生活和人物幽默的描写，对人性的兴趣，以及她经常巧妙惊奇的结局而闻名。<ref name="Guardian">{{cite news|url=http://books.guardian.co.uk/authors/author/0,,-21,00.html|title=Maeve Binchy|work=Guardian Unlimited Books|date=2008-07-22}}</ref><ref name="EB">{{cite web|url=http://www.britannica.com/eb/article-9095218/Maeve-Binchy|title=Maeve Binchy|work=Encyclopædia Britannica}}</ref>她的小说被翻译成37种语言，在全世界销售了超过4千万册。2012年7月30日梅芙·宾奇去世，享年73岁。她被称为爱尔兰最受喜爱和最知名的作家之一。<ref name=bbc_death/><ref name=irish_times_death>{{cite news|first=Patsy|last=McGarry|url=http://www.irishtimes.com/newspaper/frontpage/2012/0731/1224321158054.html|title=Maeve Binchy, best-loved writer of her generation, dies aged 72|newspaper=The Irish Times|date=2012-07-31|accessdate=2012-07-31}}</ref><ref name=rte_death>{{cite news|url=http://www.rte.ie/news/2012/0730/writer-maeve-binchy-dies-aged-72.html |title=Writer Maeve Binchy dies aged 72 |work=RTÉ News |date=2012-07-30 |accessdate=2012-07-30 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120801041515/http://www.rte.ie/news/2012/0730/writer-maeve-binchy-dies-aged-72.html |archivedate= 2012-08-01 }}</ref><ref name=journal_death>{{cite news|url=http://www.thejournal.ie/beloved-irish-writer-maeve-binchy-has-died-aged-72-538914-Jul2012|title=Beloved Irish writer Maeve Binchy has died; Sad news this evening as the death of Ireland's most well-known novelist has passed away after a short illness|work=The Journal|date=2012-07-30|accessdate=2012-07-30}}</ref>

她的作品在美国市场经常入选[[紐約時報暢銷書榜|紐約時報暢銷書榜]]和{{tsl|en|Oprah's Book Club||奥普拉的读书俱乐部}}。<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/arts/story/2012/07/31/obit-maeve-binchy.html|title=Maeve Binchy, bestselling Irish author, dies|work=CBC News|date=2012-07-31|accessdate=2012-07-31}}</ref>她以“完全没有恶意”<ref>{{cite news|first=Roy|last=Greenslade|url=https://www.theguardian.com/media/greenslade/2012/jul/31/maevebinchy-irish-times|title=Maeve Binchy, a journalist whose head was full of stories|work=[[衛報|The Guardian]]|date=2012-07-31|accessdate=2012-07-31}}</ref>和对其他作家的慷慨而闻名，她在2000年{{tsl|en|World Book Day (UK and Ireland)||英国和爱尔兰的世界图书日}}民意调查中获得第三名，领先于[[简·奥斯汀|简·奥斯汀]]，[[查尔斯·狄更斯|查尔斯·狄更斯]]和[[斯蒂芬·金|斯蒂芬·金]]。<ref name=bbc_death>{{cite news|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-19057922|title=Author Maeve Binchy dies aged 72|work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|date=2012-07-31|accessdate=2012-07-31}}</ref><ref name=award_relief_anxious>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/entertainment/1297873.stm|title=Award relief for 'anxious' Binchy|work=BBC News|date=2001-04-27|accessdate=2001-04-27}}</ref>

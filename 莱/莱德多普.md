'''莱德多普'''（{{lang-nl|Leiderdorp}}）(人口: 26,182于2004年)是[[荷兰|荷兰]]西部[[南荷兰省|南荷兰省]]的城镇和基层政权，靠近[[莱顿|莱顿]]

== 历史与地理 ==
<!--
The municipality covers an area of 12.29 km² (4.75 mile²) of which 0.56 km² (0.22 mile²) is water. Leiderdorp has now become a suburb of the city of [[Leiden|Leiden]], although the [[Oude_Rijn|Oude Rijn]] river and the [[Zijl|Zijl]] river separates the two. The [[HSL-Zuid|HSL-Zuid]] [[high-speed_rail|high-speed rail]] line between [[Amsterdam|Amsterdam]] and [[Brussels|Brussels]] is currently being built under Leiderdorp.

It is one of the oldest towns in Zuid Holland province and was a base for the Spanish army in the [[Eighty_Years'_War|Eighty Years' War]].

'''莱列克区'''（{{lang-ky|Лейлек району}}）是[[吉尔吉斯斯坦|吉尔吉斯斯坦]]西南州份[[巴特肯州|巴特肯州]]的一个[[区_(苏联)|区]]。东面紧邻[[巴特肯区|巴特肯区]]，西面和北面与[[塔吉克斯坦|塔吉克斯坦]]接壤。该区面积为{{convert|4653|km2}}，2009年人口为116,861人。<ref name=stat>{{cite web|url=http://212.42.101.100:8088/nacstat/sites/default/files/%D0%91%D0%B0%D1%82%D0%BA%D0%B5%D0%BD%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B0%D1%8F%20%D0%BE%D0%B1%D0%BB%D0%B0%D1%81%D1%82%D1%8C.pdf |title=2009 population census of the Kyrgyz Republic: Batken Region |accessdate=2013-05-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110810173103/http://212.42.101.100:8088/nacstat/sites/default/files/%D0%91%D0%B0%D1%82%D0%BA%D0%B5%D0%BD%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B0%D1%8F%20%D0%BE%D0%B1%D0%BB%D0%B0%D1%81%D1%82%D1%8C.pdf |archivedate=10 August 2011 |df= }}</ref>首府位于[[伊斯法納|伊斯法納]]。<ref>[http://enrin.grida.no/htmls/kyrghiz/soe2/english/map/map3.htm Enrin.grida.no] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090802180521/http://enrin.grida.no/htmls/kyrghiz/soe2/english/map/map3.htm |date=2009-08-02 }}</ref>

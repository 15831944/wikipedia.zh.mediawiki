'''萊絲莉·曼恩'''（{{lang-en|Leslie Mann}}，{{bd|1972年|3月26日}}<ref>{{cite news|title=Leslie Mann|url=http://www.maxim.com/todays-girl/leslie-mann|accessdate=April 19, 2013|newspaper=Maxim|date=July 30, 2009|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130416013246/http://www.maxim.com/todays-girl/leslie-mann|archivedate=四月 16, 2013}}</ref>），美國女演員暨喜劇演員。

2012年，《[[ELLE|ELLE]]》雜誌封她為「好萊塢喜劇天后」（Hollywood's queen of comedy）<ref name="ellemagazine">{{cite web|last=Combe|first=Rachael|title=Leslie Mann On Being Hollywood's Reigning Funny Girl |url=http://www.elle.com/pop-culture/celebrities/leslie-mann-movies-interview|accessdate=November 11, 2012|date=September 17, 2012}}</ref>。

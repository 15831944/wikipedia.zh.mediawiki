'''屋簷'''，是[[屋頂|屋頂]]邊凸出引[[雨水|雨水]]滴落地面的上[[蓋|蓋]]<ref name=EB1911-1>{{cite book|last1=Chisholm|first1=Hugh|title=Encyclopædia Britannica|date=1911|page=840|edition=Eleventh|url=https://archive.org/details/encyclopaediabrit08chisrich|archiveurl=https://archive.org/details/encyclopaediabrit08chisrich|archivedate=2010-03-17|quote=in architecture, the projecting edge of a sloping roof, which overhangs the face of the wall so as to throw off the water.}}</ref>，也能起到调节[[日光|日光]]射入的作用<ref name=EAVESRISK>{{cite book|title=External moisture – a guide to using the risk matrix|year=2005|publisher=Department of Building and Housing New Zealand|pages=8|url=http://www.dbh.govt.nz/UserFiles/File/Publications/WHRS/pdf/e2-riskmatrix.pdf}}</ref>。英语中的屋檐一词“{{lang|en|eaves}}”源自[[古英语|古英语]]的“efes”一词，意指边缘<ref>Eaves. def. 1a. ''Oxford English Dictionary'' Second Edition on CD-ROM (v. 4.0) © Oxford University Press 2009</ref>。

中国相对[[降水量|多雨]]的南方地区屋檐一般比相对少雨的北方地区长，使流下的雨水远离墙体，达到减少雨水对墙体的侵蚀、增加墙体的寿命的目的<ref name="王玉2015">{{cite book|author=王玉|title=中國旅遊文化|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=a48kCwAAQBAJ&pg=PA48|date=1 February 2015|publisher=元華文創|isbn=978-986-393-096-9|page=48}}</ref>。

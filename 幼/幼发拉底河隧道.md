'''幼发拉底河隧道'''是在[[美索不达米亚|美索不达米亚]]時期建造在[[幼发拉底河|幼发拉底河]]下，用於連接[[巴比伦|巴比伦]]兩邊的城市。據說隧道長{{Convert|929|m|mi||adj=}}<ref name="Berlow">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=I4t4CAAAQBAJ&pg=PA54|title=Reference Guide to Famous Engineering Landmarks of the World: Bridges, Tunnels, Dams, Roads and Other Structures|last=Berlow|first=Lawrence|date=2015-04-22|publisher=Routledge|p=54}}</ref>。考古学家認為它建造於公元前2180－2160年<ref name="Brown">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1990/12/02/world/tunnel-drilling-old-as-babylon-now-becomes-safer.html|title=Tunnel Drilling, Old as Babylon, Now Becomes Safer|last=Browne|first=Malcomn W.|date=1990-12-02|journal=New York Times}}</ref>。在公元1824年马克布鲁内尔建立[[泰晤士隧道|泰晤士河隧道]]前，沒有人試圖建造新的[[行人隧道|地下隧道]]<ref name="Berlow" />。

== 特点 ==
據說，建設工程是從幼發拉底河上的一座臨時大壩開始，採用[[明挖回填式隧道|明挖回填法]]建造<ref name="Berlow" />。

隧道{{Convert|12|ft|m}}高，{{Convert|15|ft|m}}宽，推測是用来連接主要的神廟與對岸的皇宮，供行人和马车通行。隧道用磚塊堆砌，並用[[柏油|沥青]]黏接以防水<ref>{{cite web|url=http://penelope.uchicago.edu/Thayer/e/roman/texts/diodorus_siculus/2a*.html|title=Library of History: Book II, Paragraph 9|author=Diodorus Siculus}}</ref>。

'''中線旁橋腦網狀系統'''（{{lang|en|'''P'''aramedian '''P'''ontine '''R'''eticular '''F'''ormation}}），簡稱'''PPRF'''或'''外旋旁核'''（Paraabducens nucleus）屬於[[橋腦|橋腦]][[網狀結構|網狀結構]]的一部分。PPRF與{{tsl|en|Eye movement (sensory)||眼睛動作}}的調控有關，特別負責水平眼動，與{{tsl|en|saccade||快速眼動}}。

== Input, Output, and Function ==
The PPRF is located [[人体解剖学方位|anterior]] and lateral to the {{tsl|en|medial longitudinal fasciculus||medial longitudinal fasciculus}} (MLF). It receives input from the {{tsl|en|superior colliculus||superior colliculus}} via the predorsal bundle and from the {{tsl|en|Brodmann area 8||frontal eye fields}} via {{tsl|en|frontopontine fibers||frontopontine fibers}}. The rostral PPRF probably coordinates vertical saccades; the caudal PPRF may be the generator of horizontal saccades. In particular, activity of the excitatory burst [[神經元|neuron]]s (EBNs) in the PPRF generates the "pulse" movement that initiates a saccade. In the case of horizontal saccades the "pulse" information is conveyed via [[轴突|axon]]al fibers to the {{tsl|en|abducens nucleus||abducens nucleus}}, initiating lateral {{tsl|en|Eye movement (sensory)||eye movements}}. The [[角速度|angular velocity]] of the eye during horizontal saccade ranges from 100 to 700 [[度_(角)|degree]]s per second. Larger saccades have faster pulses; the PPRF is involved in this determination.{{ref|Brazis}}

==受損影響==
單側PPRF{{tsl|en|lesion||病灶}}會造成下列問題：{{ref|Zee}}
*眼球往病灶處水平轉動的功能喪失，不論受損時眼球的位置為何。
*視野偏向受損對側。
*眼球向受損對側[[眼球震颤|震颤]]。

雙側受損則會導致{{tsl|en|ophthalmoparesis||眼球無法水平移動}}，且垂直轉動也會變慢。

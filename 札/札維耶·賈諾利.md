'''札維耶·賈諾利'''（{{lang-fr|Xavier Giannoli}})是[[法國|法國]]電影導演。<ref>[http://www.culture.gouv.fr/culture/artsetlettres/janvier2010.html French Ministry of Culture]</ref>

== 生平 ==
札維耶·賈諾利曾以《{{link-fr|我當歌手的日子|Quand j'étais chanteur}}》(2006)與《{{link-fr|夢想高速公路|À l'origine (film)}}》(2009)兩度進入[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]正式競賽單元<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.urbancinefile.com.au/home/view.asp?a=11818&s=Features |title=CANNES 2006 – PREVIEW |accessdate=26 May 2017}}</ref><ref name="festival-cannes-InCompetition">{{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/2009/inCompetition.html |title=Festival de Cannes: In Competition |accessdate=13 May 2009 |work=festival-cannes.com |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091003184949/http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/2009/inCompetition.html |archivedate=3 October 2009}}</ref>；也以《{{link-fr|超級巨星 (2012年電影)|Superstar (film, 2012)|超級巨星}}》(2012)與《{{link-fr|巴黎走音天后|Marguerite (film)}}》(2015)兩度獲選為[[威尼斯影展|威尼斯影展]]正式競賽片。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.labiennale.org/en/cinema/festival/lineup/off-sel/venezia69/|title=Venezia 69|work=labiennale|accessdate=26 July 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120728223053/http://www.labiennale.org/en/cinema/festival/lineup/off-sel/venezia69|archivedate=2012年7月28日}}</ref>

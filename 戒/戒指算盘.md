'''戒指算盘'''是一种戒指，其正面缀有一个小型[[中式算盤|中式算盤]]，可用于简单计算。由于算珠尺寸过小，常常需要使用[[发簪|发簪]]等工具进行操作使用。该工具在[[清朝|清朝]]时期发明，被誉为世界上首个“[[可穿戴设备|可穿戴设备]]”。

==外观与使用==
戒指的[[算盘|算盘]]部分长{{convert|12|mm}}，宽{{convert|7|mm}}（一说长{{convert|10|mm}}，宽{{convert|5|mm}}<ref name="chinaculture" />），算珠直径小于{{convert|1|mm}}。<ref name="ifeng" />算盘通体纯银打造，分为两档，上面一档有两颗算珠，下面一档有五颗算珠，每颗算珠都能上下移动，没有滑涩感。<ref name="ifeng" /><ref>{{cite web|author1=江志伟|title=算盘世界拾珍|url=http://zsbwg.nantong.gov.cn/art/2015/3/6/art_45680_1835814.html|website=zsbwg.nantong.gov.cn|publisher=中国珠算博物馆|accessdate=2017-07-08|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20170708220104/http://zsbwg.nantong.gov.cn/art/2015/3/6/art_45680_1835814.html|archivedate=2017-07-08|language=zh-cn|deadurl=yes}}</ref>

由于尺寸很小，戒指算盘无法直接用手进行操作，但其主要使用者——中国古代妇女在使用时只需取下她们头上的[[发簪|发簪]]即可拨动算珠进行计算工作，<ref name="chinaculture">{{cite web|title=The Story of the Chinese Abacus|url=http://www.chinaculture.org/classics/2010-04/20/content_383263_4.htm|website=www.chinaculture.org|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20160221192226/http://www.chinaculture.org/classics/2010-04/20/content_383263_4.htm|archivedate=2016-02-21|language=en|deadurl=yes}}</ref>商人在交易时也可拿针对着戒指算盘进行简单的计算。<ref name="dailymail" />

==发明==
二五珠算盘在[[明朝|明朝]]发明，而戒指算盘诞生于[[清朝|清朝]]，其历史可追溯至17世纪，<ref name="ifeng">{{cite web|title=中国清朝的可穿戴设备戒指算盘：长仅1.2厘米|url=http://tech.ifeng.com/discovery/front/detail_2014_03/24/35069096_0.shtml|website=tech.ifeng.com|accessdate=2017-07-08|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20170708210907/http://tech.ifeng.com/discovery/front/detail_2014_03/24/35069096_0.shtml|archivedate=2017-07-08|language=zh-cn|deadurl=yes}}</ref>但原发明者已不可考。<ref name="dailymail">{{cite web|title=300-year-old abacus ring uncovered from the Qing Dynasty in China|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sciencetech/article-2584437/Is-wearable-computer-300-year-old-Chinese-abacus-ring-used-Qing-Dynasty-help-traders.html|website=Mail Online|accessdate=2017-07-08|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20170708211717/http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sciencetech/article-2584437/Is-wearable-computer-300-year-old-Chinese-abacus-ring-used-Qing-Dynasty-help-traders.html|archivedate=2017-07-08|language=en|deadurl=yes}}</ref>这种戒指最开始造出来时是一种辟邪用的装饰物。<ref>{{cite web|title=戒指算盘与“巨无霸”算盘 - 中国农历网|url=http://www.zgnongli.com/zhongguolishiwuqiannian/zhongwaishoucangyiwen/5817.html|website=www.zgnongli.com|accessdate=2017-07-08|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20170708214131/http://www.zgnongli.com/zhongguolishiwuqiannian/zhongwaishoucangyiwen/5817.html|archivedate=2017-07-08|language=zh-cn|deadurl=yes}}</ref>尽管这种戒指除了计算之外无法完成任何其他复杂任务，<ref>{{cite web|title=Wearable Computing|url=https://www.interaction-design.org/literature/book/the-encyclopedia-of-human-computer-interaction-2nd-ed/wearable-computing|website=The Interaction Design Foundation|accessdate=2017-07-08|language=en|quote=Such devices have existed for centuries, but do not successfully embody Humanistic Intelligence.

'''牙齒膿腫'''（英文：dentoalveolar abscess）又稱為'''牙根膿腫'''、或'''牙槽膿腫'''，是指與牙齒相關的[[膿|膿]]包。最常見的牙齒膿腫類型為牙根尖周圍膿腫（periapical abscess），其次是{{tsl|en|periodontal abscess||牙周膿腫}}。
牙齒膿腫通常起源於由[[蛀牙|蛀牙]]、{{tsl|en|dental trauma|broken teeth|斷裂的牙齒}} 及/或 [[牙周炎|牙周發炎]]（牙周病）導致之柔軟壞死的[[牙髓|牙髓]]內的細菌感染累積。 
失敗的[[根管治疗术|根管治疗术]]（root canal treatment），也可能導致牙齒膿腫。

雖然{{tsl|en|odontogenic infection||齒源性感染}}比較泛指那種已經擴散到受感染的牙齒（causative tooth）周遭的細菌感染，然而牙齒膿腫仍歸納於於齒源性感染之中。

==治療==
治療牙齒膿腫最重要的就是減少和消除組織的[[發炎|發炎]]。因此牙醫師透過手術將牙齒中的[[膿包|膿包]]排出後，使用[[抗生素|抗生素]]消炎是治療牙齦膿腫的方法之一。<ref>{{cite web | title = Dental abscess - Management | work = Clinical Knowledge Summaries | url = http://www.cks.nhs.uk/dental_abscess/management/detailed_answers/waiting_to_see_a_dental_practitioner/recommended_antibiotic |access-date=2012-03-01 |deadurl=yes |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20111227174027/http://www.cks.nhs.uk/dental_abscess/management/detailed_answers/waiting_to_see_a_dental_practitioner/recommended_antibiotic |archive-date=27 December 2011 |df=dmy-all }}</ref>
對於已經廣泛擴散的急性牙齦膿腫，會建議醫生不要開立過多的抗生素處方，以防出現[[抗藥性|抗藥性]]<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Dar-Odeh NS, Abu-Hammad OA, Al-Omiri MK, Khraisat AS, Shehabi AA | title = Antibiotic prescribing practices by dentists: a review | journal = Therapeutics and Clinical Risk Management | volume = 6 | pages = 301–6 | date = July 2010 | pmid = 20668712 | pmc = 2909496 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Oberoi SS, Dhingra C, Sharma G, Sardana D | title = Antibiotics in dental practice: how justified are we | journal = International Dental Journal | volume = 65 | issue = 1 | pages = 4–10 | date = February 2015 | pmid = 25510967 | doi = 10.1111/idj.12146 }}</ref>,。2014年[[Cochrane|考科籃協作組織]]的文獻回顧結果認為，投予急性牙齦膿腫患者抗生素，可能有效的治療急性牙齦膿腫，但尚須更多的研究才足以確立此結論。<ref name="pmid24967571">{{cite journal | vauthors = Cope A, Francis N, Wood F, Mann MK, Chestnutt IG | title = Systemic antibiotics for symptomatic apical periodontitis and acute apical abscess in adults | journal = The Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews | volume = | issue = 6 | pages = CD010136 | date = June 2014 | pmid = 24967571 | doi = 10.1002/14651858.CD010136.pub2 }}</ref>

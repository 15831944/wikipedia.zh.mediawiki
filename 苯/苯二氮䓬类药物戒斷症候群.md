'''苯二氮䓬类药物戒斷症候群'''（{{lang-en|Benzodiazepine withdrawal syndrome}}）是指服用[[苯二氮䓬类药物|苯二氮䓬类药物]]（可能因医疗原因或[[娛樂性藥物|娱乐性原因]]用药）并产生[[苯二氮䓬类药物依赖性|生理依赖性]]后，减少用量或停药时产生的[[藥物戒斷|藥物戒斷]]症状。觅药行为或按处方服药均可能导致生理依赖性与药物成瘾（或二者之一）以及因此产生的戒断症状。某些症状可能持续数年。苯二氮䓬类药物戒斷症候群的显著特征症状包括：[[睡眠障碍|睡眠障碍]]、易怒、紧张[[焦虑|焦虑]]、[[恐慌發作|恐慌發作]]、手颤、发抖、出汗、难以集中精神、意识混乱和认知障碍、记忆减退、恶心干呕、体重下降、[[心悸|心悸]]、头痛、肌肉疼痛僵硬、知觉变化、[[幻觉|幻觉]]、[[癲癇發作|癲癇發作]]、[[思覺失調|思覺失調]]<ref name=Petursson>{{cite journal |doi=10.1111/j.1360-0443.1994.tb03743.x |title=The benzodiazepine withdrawal syndrome |year=1994 |last1=Petursson |first1=H. |journal=Addiction |volume=89 |issue=11 |pages=1455–9 |pmid=7841856}}</ref>以及自杀<ref name=pdatheagtcau>{{Cite book | last1 = Colvin | first1 = Rod | title = Overcoming Prescription Drug Addiction: A Guide to Coping and Understanding |url=https://books.google.com/?id=lalhQgAACAAJ |edition=3 | date = 26 August 2008 | publisher = Addicus Books | location = United States of America | isbn  = 978-1-886039-88-9 | pages = 74–76 | quote ={{lang|en|I have treated ten thousand patients for alcohol and drug problems and have detoxed approximately 1,500 patients for benzodiazepines – the detox for the benzodiazepines is one of the hardest detoxes we do. It can take an extremely long time, about half the length of time they have been addicted – the ongoing relentless withdrawals can be so incapacitating it can cause total destruction to one’s life – marriages break up, businesses are lost, bankruptcy, hospitalization, and of course suicide is probably the most single serious side effect.}} }}</ref><ref name="pmid28257172">{{cite journal |vauthors=Dodds TJ |title=Prescribed Benzodiazepines and Suicide Risk: A Review of the Literature |journal=Primary Care Companion for CNS Disorders |volume=19 |issue=2 |pages= |year=2017 |pmid=28257172 |doi=10.4088/PCC.16r02037 }}</ref>。此外，这些症状可能时好时坏，每日或每周的严重程度不一，而不是持续稳定好转。<ref name=Ashton>{{cite web|title=Chapter III: Benzodiazepine withdrawal symptoms, acute & protracted|url=http://www.benzo.org.uk/manual/bzcha03.htm|publisher=Institute of Neuroscience, Newcastle University|accessdate=29 April 2013|author=C. Heather Ashton DM|quote=Benzodiazepines : How they work and how to withdraw}}</ref>

苯二氮䓬类戒断症状可能很严重，症状复杂且常常长期持续。<ref name="ashman">{{cite web | author= Professor Heather Ashton | year= 2002 | url= http://benzo.org.uk/manual/index.htm | title= Benzodiazepines: How They Work and How to Withdraw }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |pmid=8105385 |year=1993 |last1=O'Connor |first1=RD |title=Benzodiazepine dependence--a treatment perspective and an advocacy for control |volume=131 |pages=266–9 |journal=NIDA research monograph}}</ref>长期使用苯二氮䓬类（即每日用药，持续至少三个月）<ref name = "Voshaar">{{cite journal |doi=10.1192/bjp.189.3.213 |title=Strategies for discontinuing long-term benzodiazepine use: Meta-analysis |year=2006 |last1=Voshaar |first1=R. C. O. |journal=British Journal of Psychiatry |volume=189 |issue=3 |pages=213–20 |pmid=16946355 |last2=Couvée |first2=JE |last3=Van Balkom |first3=AJ |last4=Mulder |first4=PG |last5=Zitman |first5=FG}}</ref>可能有更高的药物依赖风险<ref name=Nutt>{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/0165-6147(86)90420-7 |title=Benzodiazepine dependence in the clinic: Reason for anxiety? |year=1986 |last1=Nutt |first1=David |journal=Trends in Pharmacological Sciences |volume=7 |pages=457–60}}</ref>，亦可能造成用量增加、药效降低、增加事故和摔倒的概率（尤其是老年人），<ref name="deGier2011">{{cite journal |doi=10.1093/fampra/cmq113|pmid=21193495 |title=Discontinuation of long-term benzodiazepine use: 10-year follow-up |year=2010 |last1=De Gier |first1=N. |last2=Gorgels |first2=W. |last3=Lucassen |first3=P. |last4=Oude Voshaar |first4=R. |last5=Mulder |first5=J. |last6=Zitman |first6=F. |journal=Family Practice |volume=28 |issue=3 |pages=253–9}}</ref>还可能与认知、<ref name="Authier-">{{cite journal |doi=10.2515/therapie/2009051 |title=Second Meeting of the French CEIP (Centres d'Évaluation et d'Information sur la Pharmacodépendance). Part II: Benzodiazepine Withdrawal |year=2009 |last1=Authier |first1=Nicolas |last2=Boucher |first2=Alexandra |last3=Lamaison |first3=Dominique |last4=Llorca |first4=Pierre-Michel |last5=Descotes |first5=Jacques |last6=Eschalier |first6=Alain |journal=Thérapie |volume=64 |issue=6 |pages=365–70 |pmid=20025839}}</ref>神经系统和智力损伤有关<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1055/s-0028-1100831 |title=Benzodiazepin-Abhängigkeit: Ursachen und Behandlungsmöglichkeiten |language=de |trans-title=Benzodiazepine Dependence: Causalities and Treatment Options |year=2008 |last1=Heberlein |first1=A. |last2=Bleich |first2=S. |last3=Kornhuber |first3=J. |last4=Hillemacher |first4=T. |journal=Fortschritte der Neurologie · Psychiatrie  |volume=77 |pages=7–15 |pmid=19101875 |issue=1}}</ref>。因此不宜长期使用苯二氮䓬类药物。<ref name=Nutt/>将苯二氮䓬类药物作为短效安眠药使用，在入睡时可能有效，但可能因戒断反应而导致后半段睡眠质量不佳。<ref name="sleep_medicine_a04">{{Cite book | last1 = Lee-chiong | first1 = Teofilo | title = Sleep Medicine: Essentials and Review | date = 24 April 2008 | publisher = Oxford University Press, USA | url = https://books.google.com/?id=s1F_DEbRNMcC&pg=PT468 | isbn  = 0-19-530659-7 | page = 468 }}</ref>尽管如此，对于长期服用苯二氮䓬类药物者，不应违背其意愿强迫停药。<ref name="ashman"/>

苯二氮䓬类戒断可能有重症反应，甚至产生癫痫发作等危及生命的症状，<ref>{{cite book |last1=Evans |first1=Katie |last2=Sullivan |first2=Michael J. |title=Dual Diagnosis: Counseling the Mentally Ill Substance Abuser |edition=2nd |year=2001 |publisher=Guilford Press |isbn=978-1-57230-446-8 |chapter=Withdrawal and Medical Issues |chapterurl=https://books.google.com/books?id=lvUzR0obihEC&pg=PA52 |pages=52–3}}</ref>尤其是突然戒断或减量过快的大剂量或长期用药者。<ref name="ashman"/>然而逐步减量或相对用量较少的短期用药者亦可能出现重症戒断反应，<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=2891684 |year=1987 |last1=Lader |first1=M |title=Long-term anxiolytic therapy: The issue of drug withdrawal |volume=48 Suppl |pages=12–6 |journal=The Journal of Clinical Psychiatry}}</ref>在动物模型中甚至曾有单次大剂量用药后戒断出现重症反应。<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=3098961 |year=1986 |last1=Boisse |first1=NR |last2=Periana |first2=RM |last3=Guarino |first3=JJ |last4=Kruger |first4=HS |last5=Samoriski |first5=GM |title=Pharmacologic characterization of acute chlordiazepoxide dependence in the rat |volume=239 |issue=3 |pages=775–83 |journal=Journal of Pharmacology and Experimental Therapeutics}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |pmid=3092067 |year=1986 |last1=Boisse |first1=NR |last2=Periana |first2=RM |last3=Guarino |first3=JJ |last4=Kruger |first4=HS |title=Acute chlordiazepoxide dependence in the rat: Comparisons to chronic |volume=67 |pages=197–201 |journal=NIDA research monograph}}</ref>少数人会出现{{tsl|en|post-acute-withdrawal syndrome|遷延性戒斷症狀}}，症状可能在戒断后数月甚至数年内持续，维持在亚急性状态。缓慢而逐步的减少药物用量能使遷延性戒斷症狀的出现概率尽可能降低。<ref name="pwsfb">{{cite web| author= Professor Heather Ashton| year= 2004| url=http://www.benzo.org.uk/pws04.htm| title= Protracted Withdrawal Symptoms From Benzodiazepines | publisher= Comprehensive Handbook of Drug & Alcohol Addiction}}</ref>

长期使用苯二氮䓬类药物会导致神经适应，降低药物效果，造成[[藥物耐受性|耐受]]和[[身体依赖|依赖]]。<ref name="Allison-2003">{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/S0163-7258(03)00029-9 |title=Neuroadaptive processes in GABAergic and glutamatergic systems in benzodiazepine dependence |year=2003 |last1=Allison |first1=C |last2=Pratt |first2=J.A |journal=Pharmacology & Therapeutics |volume=98 |issue=2 |pages=171–95 |pmid=12725868}}</ref>即使是持续服用治疗剂量期间，长期使用苯二氮䓬类药物亦可能引起类似戒断反应的症状，尤其是在服药间隔期。<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=2889722 |year=1987 |last1=Herman |first1=JB |last2=Brotman |first2=AW |last3=Rosenbaum |first3=JF |title=Rebound anxiety in panic disorder patients treated with shorter-acting benzodiazepines |volume=48 Suppl |pages=22–8 |journal=The Journal of Clinical Psychiatry}}</ref>停药或减少用量后，用药者即会出现药物戒断症状，直至其生理适应恢复。<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=14767398 |year=2003 |last1=Allgulander |first1=C |last2=Bandelow |first2=B |last3=Hollander |first3=E |last4=Montgomery |first4=SA |last5=Nutt |first5=DJ |last6=Okasha |first6=A |last7=Pollack |first7=MH |last8=Stein |first8=DJ |last9=Swinson |first9=RP |last10=World Council Of |first10=Anxiety |title=WCA recommendations for the long-term treatment of generalized anxiety disorder |volume=8 |issue=8 Suppl 1 |pages=53–61 |journal=CNS Spectrums}}</ref>这些{{tsl|en|Rebound effect|反跳作用|反跳症状}}可能与最初服药前的病症相同，亦可能是戒斷症候群的一部分。<ref name = "Salzman">{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/0022-3956(93)90021-S |title=Benzodiazepine treatment of panic and agoraphobic symptoms: Use, dependence, toxicity, abuse |year=1993 |last1=Salzman |first1=Carl |journal=Journal of Psychiatric Research |volume=27 |pages=97–110 |pmid=7908335}}</ref>在重症病例中，戒断反应可能会恶化，或与严重的精神疾病相似，如[[狂躁|狂躁]]、[[精神分裂症|精神分裂症]]，尤其在大剂量用药者中还会出现[[癲癇重積狀態|癲癇重積狀態]]。<ref name="gabbards">{{Cite book | last1 = Gabbard | first1 = Glen O. | title = Gabbard's Treatments of Psychiatric Disorders, Fourth Edition (Treatments of Psychiatric Disorders) | date = 15 May 2007 | publisher = American Psychiatric Publishing | url=https://books.google.com/?id=-T2aEqfwLW4C&pg=PA209 | isbn  = 1-58562-216-8 | pages = 209–211 }}</ref>若不能正确诊断出戒斷症候群，可能会误诊为服药前的疾病复发而需要继续用药，导致戒断失败、重新开始用药，而重新用药往往比之前的剂量更大。<ref name="gabbards"/>

提高戒断成功率的方法包括：提升对戒断反应的意识，针对用药者的戒断反应严重程度而制定个人化的逐步戒断方法，以及增加其他替代方法辅助，如使用药者安心、参加苯二氮䓬类药物戒断互助小组。这些方法都能有效提高戒断的成功率。<ref name="tbws1989">{{cite journal |pmid=2576073 |year=1989 |last1=Onyett |first1=SR |title=The benzodiazepine withdrawal syndrome and its management |volume=39 |issue=321 |pages=160–3 |pmc=1711840 |journal=The Journal of the Royal College of General Practitioners}}</ref><ref name="pmid1675688">{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/0740-5472(91)90023-4 |title=Protracted withdrawal syndromes from benzodiazepines |year=1991 |last1=Ashton |first1=Heather |journal=Journal of Substance Abuse Treatment |volume=8 |pages=19–28 |pmid=1675688 |issue=1–2}}</ref>

==症状和体征==
{{multiple image|align=right|direction=horizontal|image1=Diazepam2mgand5mgtablets.JPG|caption1=[[地西泮|地西泮]]，其2毫克与5毫克片剂常用于治疗苯二氮䓬类戒断反应<ref name="pmid28328330"/>|image2=Chlordiazepoxidetabletsgeneric.JPG|caption2={{tsl|en|Chlordiazepoxide|氯二氮平}}，其5毫克胶囊剂有时可作为地西泮的替代药物，用于治疗苯二氮䓬类戒断反应。氯二氮平与地西泮都有较长的[[生物半衰期|消除半衰期]]和长效[[活性代謝產物|活性代謝產物]]}}

苯二氮䓬类、[[巴比妥类药物|巴比妥类]]等镇静安眠药物或酒精成瘾者在停用后会产生严重的戒断反应症状。《临床成人精神医学手册》显示这些戒断症状比[[鸦片类药物|鸦片类药物]]的戒断症状更严重、危害更大。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Lindsay|first1=S. J. E.|title=The Handbook of Clinical Adult Psychology|date=1994|publisher=Psychology Press|isbn=9780415072168|pages=363|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=WSaVLNBapdcC&pg=PA363}}</ref>使用者在停药时一般甚少获得相关建议和支持。<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/j.pharma.2009.07.001 |title=Benzodiazepine dependence: Focus on withdrawal syndrome |year=2009 |last1=Authier |first1=N. |last2=Balayssac |first2=D. |last3=Sautereau |first3=M. |last4=Zangarelli |first4=A. |last5=Courty |first5=P. |last6=Somogyi |first6=A.A. |last7=Vennat |first7=B. |last8=Llorca |first8=P.-M. |last9=Eschalier |first9=A.  |journal=Annales pharmaceutiques françaises |volume=67 |issue=6 |pages=408–13 |pmid=19900604}}</ref>一些戒断症状与用药治疗之前的病症相同，<ref name = "Salzman"/>可能症状严重或长期持续。[[生物半衰期|半衰期]]较长的苯二氮䓬类药物可能在停用长达三周之后才会出现戒断反应，而半衰期较短的药物则较早出现戒断反应，一般在停用24-48小时后即会出现。<ref name=CSM2007>{{cite web |url = http://www.bnf.org/bnf/bnf/58/3139.htm |title = Hypnotics and anxiolytics |accessdate = 17 September 2007 |author = Committee on Safety of Medicines |year = 2007 |publisher = British National Formulary }}{{registration required}}</ref>不同剂量用药者的戒断症状种类可能没有重大差异，但大剂量用药者的症状严重程度往往更高。<ref name = murphy>{{cite journal |doi=10.1192/bjp.158.4.511 |title=A double-blind comparison of the effects of gradual withdrawal of lorazepam, diazepam and bromazepam in benzodiazepine dependence |year=1991 |last1=Murphy |first1=S.

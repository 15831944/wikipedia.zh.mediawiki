'''苯基溴化镁'''是一种[[格氏试剂|格氏试剂]]，化学式为C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>MgBr。它可以在有机合成反应中提供[[苯基|苯基]][[合成子|合成子]]。它在实验室可以通过[[溴苯|溴苯]]和[[镁|镁]]屑的反应得到，反应中需要加入少量的[[碘|碘]]来活化镁，以引发反应。<ref name = robertson>{{cite web | publisher = [[MiraCosta_College|MiraCosta College]] | author = Robertson, D. L. | date = 2007-01-03 | title = Grignard Synthesis: Synthesis of Benzoic Acid and of Triphenylmethanol | accessdate = 2008-01-25 | url = http://www.miracosta.edu/home/dlr/211exp2.htm}}</ref>

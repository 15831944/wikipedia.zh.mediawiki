<!-- 定義及醫療用途 -->
'''苯海拉明'''（{{lang-en|'''Diphenhydramine'''}}）是一種[[抗組胺藥|抗組胺藥]]，主要用於治療[[過敏|過敏症]]<ref name=AHSP2016/>，也可以用於治療[[失眠|失眠]]、[[感冒|感冒]]症狀、[[帕金森氏症|帕金森氏症]]震顫和[[噁心|噁心]]<ref name=AHSP2016/>。給藥方式是口服、[[靜脈注射|靜脈注射]]及[[肌肉注射|肌肉注射]]<ref name=AHSP2016/>。最大效果通常在給藥後約兩小時，效果可持續長達七小時<ref name=AHSP2016>{{cite web|title=Diphenhydramine Hydrochloride|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/diphenhydramine-hydrochloride.html|website=Drugs.com|publisher=American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|accessdate=28 September 2016|date=6 September 2016|deadurl=no|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160915002404/https://www.drugs.com/monograph/diphenhydramine-hydrochloride.html|archivedate=15 September 2016}}</ref>。

<!-- 副作用及機制 -->
苯海拉明常見的副作用包括嗜睡、方向感不佳以及反胃<ref name=AHSP2016/>，不建議給嬰兒服用<ref name=AHSP2016/>。目前還不清楚在懷孕時使用的風險，不過不建議在[[哺乳|哺乳]]時服用此藥物<ref>{{cite web|title=Diphenhydramine Pregnancy and Breastfeeding Warnings|url=https://www.drugs.com/pregnancy/diphenhydramine.html|website=Drugs.com|accessdate=28 September 2016|deadurl=no|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161002114958/https://www.drugs.com/pregnancy/diphenhydramine.html|archivedate=2 October 2016}}</ref>。苯海拉明是第一代的{{le|H1拮抗劑|H1 antihistamine}}，會阻斷一些組織胺的作用<ref name=AHSP2016/>。苯海拉明也是[[抗膽鹼劑|抗膽鹼劑]]<ref>{{cite book|last1=Ayd|first1=Frank J.|title=Lexicon of Psychiatry, Neurology, and the Neurosciences|date=2000|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=978-0-7817-2468-5 | page = 332 | url = https://books.google.ca/books?id=ea_QVG2BFy8C&pg=PA332  | deadurl = no | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20170908214041/https://books.google.ca/books?id=ea_QVG2BFy8C&pg=PA332 | archive-date = 8 September 2017}}</ref>。

<!-- 歷史及社會 -->
苯海拉明最早是由{{le|George Rieveschl|George Rieveschl}}製備，在1946年開始販售<ref>{{cite book|last1=Dörwald|first1=Florencio Zaragoza|title=Lead Optimization for Medicinal Chemists: Pharmacokinetic Properties of Functional Groups and Organic Compounds |date = 2013 | publisher = John Wiley & Sons | isbn = 978-3-527-64565-7 | page = 225 | url = https://books.google.ca/books?id=YTeY9ZEfNccC&pg=PA225 | deadurl = no | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161002085621/https://books.google.ca/books?id=YTeY9ZEfNccC&pg=PA225|archivedate=2 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Benadryl|url=http://ohiohistorycentral.org/w/Benadryl?rec=2591|website=Ohio History Central|accessdate=28 September 2016|deadurl=no|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161017192649/http://ohiohistorycentral.org/w/Benadryl?rec=2591|archivedate=17 October 2016}}</ref>。目前是[[通用名药物|通用名药物]]<ref name=AHSP2016/>。在開發中國家的藥價是每劑美金0.01元<ref>{{cite web|title=Diphenhydramine|url=http://mshpriceguide.org/en/single-drug-information/?DMFId=273&searchYear=2014|website=International Drug Price Indicator Guide|accessdate=28 September 2016}}</ref>。在美國，每個月的藥費小於美金25元<ref name=Ric2015>{{cite book|last1=Hamilton|first1=Richart|title=Tarascon Pocket Pharmacopoeia 2015 Deluxe Lab-Coat Edition|date=2015|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|isbn=978-1-284-05756-0|page=240}}</ref>。苯海拉明的商品名有Benadryl，也有其他的名稱<ref name=AHSP2016/>。

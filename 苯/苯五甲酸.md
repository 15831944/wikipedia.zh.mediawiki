'''苯五甲酸'''是一种[[有机化合物|有机化合物]]，化学式为C<sub>11</sub>H<sub>6</sub>O<sub>10</sub>。它是无色的[[单斜晶系|单斜]]晶体，可由[[高锰酸钾|高锰酸钾]]在室温下氧化[[五甲苯|五甲苯]]制备得到。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Structure of benzenepentacarboxylic acid|journal=Acta Crystallographica Section C: Crystal Structure Communications|volume=46|pages=2399–2401|date=1990-12-15|doi=10.1107/S0108270190003316|issn=0108-2701|language=en|issue=12|url=http://scripts.iucr.org/cgi-bin/paper?S0108270190003316|accessdate=2019-01-20|author=F. Gómez-Beltrán, S. García-Granda, C. Barrio}}</ref>它和[[4,4'-联吡啶|4,4'-联吡啶]]可以形成共晶<ref>{{cite journal|title=Hydrated and anhydrous molecular complexes of benzenepentacarboxylic acid and 4,4′-bipyridine|volume=1050|pages=216–221|date=2013-10-24|issn=0022-2860|doi=10.1016/j.molstruc.2013.07.016|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0022286013006212|journal=Journal of Molecular Structure|accessdate=2019-01-20|author=V. Nagarajan, Manishkumar R. Shimpi, V. R. Pedireddi}}</ref>，或者在其存在下与过渡金属化合物构建[[金属有机框架材料|金属有机框架材料]]<ref>{{cite journal|title=Synthesis and structural evaluation of five coordination complexes of benzenepentacarboxylic acid with aza-donor ligands|volume=1114|pages=38–47|date=2016-06-15|issn=0022-2860|doi=10.1016/j.molstruc.2016.02.010|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0022286016301041|journal=Journal of Molecular Structure|accessdate=2019-01-20|author=Manishkumar R.

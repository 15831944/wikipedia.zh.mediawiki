'''多米尼克·韋恩·伍茲'''（{{lang|en|Dominic Wynn Woods}}<ref name=complex-cosign>{{cite web|last1=Drake|first1=David|title=The Recipe: Sage The Gemini On Hitmaking Without a Cosign|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2013/11/sage-the-gemini-interview|work=Complex|publisher=Complex Media, Inc.|accessdate=2016-01-24| archivedate= 2016-02-03 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160203063128/http://www.complex.com/music/2013/11/sage-the-gemini-interview| deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Artist Spotlight: Get To Know Sage The Gemini|url=http://www.republicrecords.com/artist-spotlight-sage-the-gemini/|publisher=Republic Records|accessdate=2016-01-24|archivedate=2016-01-30|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160130164838/http://www.republicrecords.com/artist-spotlight-sage-the-gemini/|deadurl=yes}}</ref>，{{bd|1992年|6月20日}}<ref name=allmusic>{{cite web|last1=Jeffries|first1=David|title=Sage the Gemini Biography|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/sage-the-gemini-mn0003036335/biography |work=AllMusic.com |publisher=All Media Network, LLC |accessdate=2016-01-24}}</ref>），藝名'''賢者雙子座'''（{{lang|en|'''Sage the Gemini'''}}），是一位[[美國|美國]][[饒舌歌手|饒舌歌手]]、詞曲作家及音樂製作人。他是[[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]]地下嘻哈團體{{le|The HBK Gang|The HBK Gang}}的成員。他因為2013年與{{le|Iamsu!|Iamsu!}}合作製作的歌曲〈{{le|Gas Pedal|Gas Pedal}}〉而知名。

== 早年生活 ==
賢者雙子座出生於[[美國|美國]][[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[舊金山|舊金山]]，6歲前居住在{{le|獵人角 (加利福尼亞州)|Hunters Point, San Francisco|獵人角}}，之後因為社區的犯罪問題而搬遷至[[費爾菲爾德_(加利福尼亞州)|費爾菲爾德]]。<ref name=complex-cosign /><ref name="eastbayexpress"/>12歲那年，他與他的兄弟做一些修剪草坪、清理車庫的零工，賺到一些小錢後，在費爾菲爾德的戈登樂器行（{{lang|en|Gordon's Music and Sound}}）購入一支[[麥克風#電容式麥克風|電容式麥克風]]及{{le|Behringer|Behringer}}混音器，開始與他的兄弟錄製音樂。<ref name=complex-cosign />他所製作的首支歌曲名為〈Made In China〉。<ref name="eastbayexpress">{{cite web|last=McIntire |first=George |url=http://www.eastbayexpress.com/oakland/sage-the-geminis-career-slams-the-gas-pedal/Content?oid=3750785 |title=Sage the Gemini's Career Slams the 'Gas Pedal' |publisher=East Bay Express |date= |accessdate=2014-03-28}}</ref>14歲時，他為了提升自己的嘻哈水準，從學校退學以全神貫注在音樂上，並與他的堂表兄弟組成一個音樂團體Tree Boys。<ref name="eastbayexpress"/>伍茲以他的眼睛顏色與[[星座|星座]]取藝名為賢者雙子座。<ref name="republicrecords1">{{cite web|url=http://www.republicrecords.com/artists/sage-the-gemini/ |title=Artist Profile: Sage The Gemini|publisher=Republic Records |accessdate=2014-03-28| archivedate= 2015-07-09 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150709062614/http://www.republicrecords.com/artists/sage-the-gemini/ | deadurl=no}}</ref><ref name="hiphopdx1">{{cite web|last=Ju |first=Shirley
|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/interviews/id.2336/title.sage-the-gemini-recalls-meeting-hbk-gang-his-early-influences |title=Sage The Gemini Recalls Meeting HBK Gang and His Early Influences | Rappers Talk Hip Hop Beef and Old School Hip Hop |publisher=HipHop DX |date= 2014-03-27 |accessdate=2014-03-28| archivedate=2015-01-15 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150115060226/http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/interviews/id.2336/title.sage-the-gemini-recalls-meeting-hbk-gang-his-early-influences | deadurl=no}}</ref>

== 音樂生涯 ==
2008年，賢者雙子座在[[MySpace|MySpace]]發布他的首支單曲〈You Should Know〉。這首歌很快地成為一首熱門單曲，獲得超過300萬次的點擊率，引起全球廣泛關注。隨後，他加入Black Money音樂集團，與饒舌歌手{{le|Iamsu!|Iamsu!}}結識，又找來幾位朋友組成地下嘻哈團體{{le|The HBK Gang|The HBK Gang}}。<ref name="republicrecords1" /><ref name="hiphopdx1" />

2013年3月，賢者雙子座推出兩支單曲：找來{{le|Iamsu!|Iamsu!}}合作的〈{{le|Gas Pedal|Gas Pedal}}〉曾登上[[告示牌百強單曲榜|《告示牌》百強單曲榜]]第29名及{{le|熱門節奏藍調/嘻哈單曲榜|Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs}}第6名，是賢者雙子座的首支四十強單曲。<ref name="sagebillboard">{{cite web |url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/2549565/sage-the-gemini/chart |title=Sage The Gemini – Chart history |publisher=Billbo-{}-ard |accessdate=2017-07-08}}</ref>收錄這首歌曲的[[迷你專輯|迷你專輯]]《Gas Pedal》登上{{le|節奏藍調/嘻哈專輯榜|R&B/Hip-Hop Albums}}第29名；另一首同為賢者雙子座製作的歌曲〈{{le|Red Nose|Red Nose}}〉亦登上百強單曲榜第54名，及熱門節奏藍調/嘻哈單曲榜第12名。<ref name="sagebillboard"/>〈Red Nose〉及〈Gas Pedal〉皆獲[[美國唱片業協會|美國唱片業協會]]認證為[[音樂唱片銷售認證|白金單曲]]。<ref>{{cite web|last=Baker |first=Soren |url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.26930/title.sage-the-gemini-s-gas-pedal-goes-platinum-red-nose-goes-gold |title=Sage the Gemini's "Gas Pedal" Goes Platinum and "Red Nose" Goes Gold | Get The Latest Hip Hop News, Rap News and Hip Hop Album Sales |publisher=HipHop DX |date=2014-01-07 |accessdate=2014-03-28}}</ref>

2013年8月，他與{{le|共和唱片|Republic Records|環球共和唱片}}簽約。<ref name="sign-republic">{{cite web |url=http://www.thizzler.com/blog/2013/8/22/sage-the-gemini-signs-to-universal-republic.html |title=Sage The Gemini signs to Universal Republic |work=Thizzler.com |publisher=Thizzler On The Roof |date=2013-08-22 |accessdate=2014-01-16}}</ref>2014年3月25日，賢者雙子座發行他的首張[[錄音室專輯|錄音室專輯]]《{{le|Remember Me (賢者雙子座專輯)|Remember Me (Sage the Gemini album)|Remember Me}}》。<ref name="xxlmag1">{{cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2013/12/sage-gemini-kool-john-party-jumping-college-drop/ |title=Sage The Gemini and Kool John Have The Party Jumping In "College Drop" - XXL |publisher=Xxlmag.com |date=2013-12-23 |accessdate=2014-01-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://news.radio.com/2014/02/13/interview-sage-the-gemini-on-his-new-album-working-with-justin-bieber/ |title=Interview: Sage the Gemini On His New Album and Working With Justin Bieber |publisher=News.radio.com |date=2014-02-13 |accessdate=2014-03-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140222230016/http://news.radio.com/2014/02/13/interview-sage-the-gemini-on-his-new-album-working-with-justin-bieber/ |archivedate=2014年2月22日 |df= }}</ref>2014年10月21日，[[佛羅·里達|佛羅·里達]]推出與賢者雙子座合作的單曲〈{{le|G.D.F.R.

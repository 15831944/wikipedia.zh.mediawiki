'''威廉·蒂莫西·'提姆'·奥布莱恩''' ({{lang-en|William Timothy "Tim" O'Brien}}，{{bd|1946年|10月1日|catIdx=O'Brien, Tim}})，美国著名小说家。提姆主要因撰写与[[越南战争|越南战争]]有关的战争小说而知名。提姆曾在越南战争中服役。其最知名的作品《[[士兵的重负|士兵的重负]]》（一本半自传小说）就是受其在越战中的经历启发而来。<ref>[http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=125128156 NPR Interview on Book]</ref><ref>Michiko Kakutani review of The Yellow Birds which cites [[The_Things_They_Carried|The Things They Carried]] as Vietnam Classic [http://www.nytimes.com/2012/09/07/books/the-yellow-birds-by-kevin-powers.html]</ref><ref>[http://www.wnyc.org/shows/shorts/2010/mar/21/ WNYC Story] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130116151335/http://www.wnyc.org/shows/shorts/2010/mar/21/ |date=2013-01-16 }}</ref>

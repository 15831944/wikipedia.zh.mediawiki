G. |title= Genesis, Rogers Rangers: The First Green Berets, The Corps & The Revivals, April 6, 1758-December 24, 1783 (A heritage classic) |year= 2007 |publisher= Heritage Books |isbn= 0788415751| page= 3 }}</ref><ref>{{cite book |author= Reid S. |title= Quebec 1759: The Battle That Won Canada (Campaign) |year= 2003 |publisher= Osprey Publishing |isbn= 1855326051 |page= 9 }}</ref>），	指[[法國-印第安人戰爭|法國-印第安人戰爭]]期間，1759年7月26日及27日一場輕微的戰術對抗，地點在鐘琴砲台（Fort Carillon，後改稱 Fort Ticonderoga，即提康德羅加砲台<ref>{{cite book |author= Nester W.

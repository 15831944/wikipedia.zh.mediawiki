'''刘作虎'''（{{bd|1975年|5月5日|catIdx=Liu刘}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://cn.ceibs.edu/node/6259|title=刘作虎简介|accessdate=2017-05-27|publisher=CEIBS}}</ref><ref>{{Sinaweibo post|user=1110411735|id=F1DiMCafy|title=刘作虎生日|accessdate=2017-05-27|name=刘作虎}}</ref>）是一名中国企业家，[[智能手机|智能手机]]制造商[[一加|一加]]的创始人兼首席执行官<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.qualcomm.com/news/snapdragon/2014/05/16/qa-pete-lau-oneplus-launch-one|title=Q&A with Pete Lau from OnePlus on the launch of the One|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=2014-05-16|publisher=Qualcomm.com}}</ref>。1998年大学毕业后，刘作虎入职[[OPPO|OPPO]]。工作十余年后，他于2013年11月辞职，次月创立一加。

== 生涯 ==

=== OPPO ===
1998年[[浙江大学|浙江大学]]毕业后，刘作虎入职[[OPPO|OPPO]]，任硬件工程师<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://tech.sina.com.cn/i/2013-12-17/02199011715.shtml|title=手机创业机会何在？一加刘作虎：不够美|accessdate=2017-05-26|author=孟鸿|date=2013-12-17|publisher=新浪科技}}</ref>。2008年，刘出任OPPO[[蓝光|蓝光]]事业部总经理。他曾砸了一个蓝光播放器的[[主板|主板]]，表达他对电路设计的失望。任职期间，刘作虎以注重细节在科技圈闻名<ref name="engadget1">{{cite web|url=http://www.engadget.com/2014/04/23/oneplus-one-launch-ceo-pete-lau-interview/|title=Meet the One, OnePlus' $299 Nexus killer|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=2014-04-23|last=Lai|first=Richard|publisher=Engadget.com}}</ref>。随后，他成为市场营销总监，最终被任命为副总经理。他将[[CyanogenMod|CyanogenMod]]（一款基于[[Android|Android]]的操作系统）引入[[OPPO_N1|OPPO N1]]智能手机<ref name="theverge1">{{cite web|url=http://www.theverge.com/2013/12/16/5217794/oppo-ex-vice-president-pete-lau-launches-oneplus|title=From Oppo to OnePlus: a new company wants to build the next great smartphone|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=|last=Wenz|first=John|publisher=The Verge}}</ref>。在公司工作十多年后，刘作虎于2013年11月辞职<ref>{{cite web|url=https://plus.google.com/+PeteLau/posts/LYbr2ws6RjM|title=Today is my last day at OPPO.|accessdate=2015-01-21|author=Pete Lau|date=2013-11-17|publisher=Plus.google.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://technode.com/2014/08/12/techcrunch-beijing-pete-lau-chinese-smartphone-company-oneplus-went-global-8-months/|title=[TechCrunch Beijing] Pete Lau: How Chinese Smartphone Company OnePlus Went Global in just 8 Months|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=|last=Soo|first=Zen|publisher=TechNode}}</ref>。

===一加===
2013年12月，以打造“受全球用户尊敬的品牌”为目标，刘作虎成立了[[一加|一加]]。最初，一加只有6名员工<ref>{{cite web|url=http://technode.com/2014/11/30/interview-ceo-oneplus-pete-lau-petes-product-philosophy-behind-oneplus-one/|title=Interview with OnePlus CEO Pete Lau: OnePlus One Product Philosophy|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=|last=Yoo|first=Eva|publisher=TechNode}}</ref>。降低成本是公司早期的优先事务，刘作虎参照[[Google_Nexus|Nexus]]系列和OPPO的市场模式，将他公司的第一个产品放到网上销售。他选择Cyanogenmod作为设备的操作系统，进一步扩大与Cyanogen公司的史蒂夫·孔迪克（Steve Kondik）的联系，后者在刘作虎于OPPO任职期间就已结识<ref name="engadget1" /><ref name="theverge1" />。

由于一加没有生产公司，刘作虎在他的前公司OPPO的工厂中生产设备<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.engadget.com/2013/12/16/oppo-pete-lau-oneplus-smartphone/|title=Ex-Oppo exec reveals new company OnePlus, plans to make 'the perfect smartphone'|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=2013-12-16|last=Moon|first=Mariella|publisher=Engadget.com}}</ref>。一加手机1于2014年4月正式公布，2014年6月开启在线订购<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gsmarena.com/oneplus_one-6327.php|title=OnePlus One - Full phone specifications|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=|publisher=Gsmarena.com}}</ref>。手机得到了科技界的积极评价，其规格、性能、设计和积极的成本控制受到好评<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gsmarena.com/oneplus_one-review-1101.php |title=OnePlus One review: When opportunity strikes |publisher=GSMArena.com |date=2014-07-09 |accessdate=2015-01-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Brad Molen |url=http://www.engadget.com/products/oneplus/one/ |title=OnePlus One review |publisher=Engadget |date= |accessdate=2015-01-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Mundydocument |first=Jon |url=http://www.in.techradar.com/reviews/phones/mobile-phones/OnePlus-One/articleshow/38463270.cms |title=OnePlus One | Techradar India |publisher=In.techradar.com |date=2014-05-29 |accessdate=2015-01-21}}</ref>。手机对细节的关注得到了几位技术专家的赞扬，这可归因于刘作虎性格的一方面<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/bytes/one-plus-one-simple-math-46508|title=Hands On Review | One Plus One: Simple Math|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=|publisher=Thedailystar.net|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150121074233/http://thedailystar.net/bytes/one-plus-one-simple-math-46508|archivedate=2015-01-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/magazines/panache/et-review-oneplus-one-a-revelation-at-rs-21999/articleshow/45445412.cms|title=ET Review: OnePlus One a revelation at Rs 21,999 - The Economic Times|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=2014-12-10|last=Raj|first=Hitesh|publisher=Economictimes.indiatimes.com}}</ref>。由于供应有限，手机最初只能通过邀请系统购买<ref>Sawers, Paul. [http://venturebeat.com/2015/11/14/oneplus-founder-talks-nfc-invite-system-never-settle/ "OnePlus founder talks NFC, meeting demand, and what ‘never settle’ really means"], ''14 November 2015. Accessed 10 December 2015.''</ref>。

科技媒体广泛讨论此手机的成本。16 GB版本的手机成本为299美元，而64 GB版本则为349美元，这一价值几乎是当时其他类似规格的旗舰手机的一半。刘作虎认为成本低是由营销成本不足、在线营销策略和利润率低几个原因造成的。科技网站Tech Radar认为对新公司来说此策略足够勇敢和冒险<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.in.techradar.com/news/phone-and-communications/mobile-phones/Revealed-the-reason-the-OnePlus-One-is-so-cheap/articleshow/38482039.cms|title=Revealed: the reason the OnePlus One is so cheap | Techradar India|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=2014-05-14|last=Beavisdocument|first=Gareth|publisher=In.techradar.com}}</ref>。另一个科技网站Phone Arena评论道，“如果一加可以在没有电视广告的情况下成功销售其智能手机，那么它就能做出一些大厂永远做不到的事情。”<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.phonearena.com/news/OnePlus-CEO-Lau-Were-selling-the-OnePlus-One-at-cost_id56131|title=OnePlus CEO Lau: We're selling the OnePlus One at cost|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=|publisher=Phonearena.com}}</ref>截至2014年12月，一加已售出近100万部手机<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gsmarena.com/nearly_a_million_units_of_oneplus_one_sold_last_year-news-10722.php|title=Nearly a million units of OnePlus One sold last year - GSMArena.com news|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=|publisher=Gsmarena.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.forbes.com/sites/ewanspence/2015/01/12/one-plus-one-sells-one-million-smartphones/|title=How $300 Of Advertising Sold One Million Smartphones|accessdate=2015-01-21|date=2015-01-12|publisher=Forbes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pcworld.com/article/2866332/chinas-oneplus-targets-flagship-phones-from-big-players.html|title=China's OnePlus targets flagship phones from big players|accessdate=2015-01-21|author=Michael Kan|date=|publisher=PCWorld}}</ref>。

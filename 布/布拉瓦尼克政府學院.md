'''布拉瓦尼克政府學院'''（'''Blavatnik School of Government'''）是附屬於[[牛津大學|牛津大學]]的[[公共政策學院|公共政策學院]]，創建於[[2010年|2010年]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.ox.ac.uk/media/news_stories/2010/100920.html | title=Blavatnik School of Government launched | publisher=[[University_of_Oxford|University of Oxford]] | location=UK | date=20 September 2010 | accessdate=20 February 2011 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101230094145/http://www.ox.ac.uk/media/news_stories/2010/100920.html | archivedate=30 December 2010 | df= }}</ref><ref>[https://www.telegraph.co.uk/education/educationnews/8009832/Oxford-University-to-open-school-of-government.html Oxford University to open school of government] The Telegraph, 20 September 2010; Retrieved 20 February 2011</ref><ref>[https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-oxfordshire-11370396 School of Government launched at Oxford University] BBC News, 20 September 2010; Retrieved 20 February 2011</ref>，第一批學生則在[[2012年|2012年]]入學。學院由[[李奧納德·布拉瓦尼克|李奧納德·布拉瓦尼克]]捐款7500萬英鎊、[[牛津大學|牛津大學]]出資2600萬英鎊興建<ref>{{cite news| url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/10342862 | title=Oil tycoon's £75m gift for Oxford University school | work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] | publisher=[[BBC|BBC]] | location=UK | date=17 June 2010 | accessdate=20 February 2011 }}</ref>。

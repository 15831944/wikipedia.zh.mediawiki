'''布氏珍龜 '''（[[學名|學名]]： ''Homopus boulengeri''）<ref>F. Slavens and K. Slavens, ''Reptiles and Amphibians in Captivity – Breeding, Longevity and Inventory'', 1991.</ref> 是[[珍龜屬|珍龜屬]]下的一個[[種|種]]。原生於[[南非乾旱臺地高原|南非乾旱臺地高原]]。已受到棲息地破壞的威脅。<ref name=Obst>J. Obst, K. Richter, and U. Jacob, ''The Completely Illustrated Atlas of Reptiles and Amphibians for the Terrarium'', T.F.H. press, 1988.</ref>

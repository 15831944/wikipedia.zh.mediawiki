'''布雷西亞地鐵'''（{{lang-it|Metropolitana di Brescia}}）<ref>{{cite web|title=LA METROPOLITANA DI BRESCIA|url=http://www.bresciamobilita.it/utenti/metrobs/la-metropolitana-a-brescia|publisher=Brescia Mobilità|accessdate=2 August 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131013090300/http://www.bresciamobilita.it/utenti/metrobs/la-metropolitana-a-brescia|archivedate=2013年10月13日}}</ref>是[[義大利|義大利]]城市[[布雷西亞|布雷西亞]]的大眾交通運輸系統。現在布雷西亞地鐵有一條線路，長{{convert|13.7|km}}，有17個車站<ref name="mappe">{{cite web |url=http://www.bresciamobilita.it/public/resources/brescia%20mobilita/utenti/metrobs/linea-completa.pdf |title=Mappa della linea metropolitana |publisher=Brescia Mobilitá |language=it |trans_title= |date= |accessdate=2013-11-07}}</ref>。布雷西亞地鐵的建設計劃開始於1980年代，開工於2004年，竣工通車於2013年<ref name="opening">{{cite web |url=http://www.bresciamobilita.it/news/parte-la-metro-2-marzo-2013 |title=Parte la metro! 2 marzo 2013 |publisher=Brescia Mobilitá |language=it |trans_title=The Metro goes! 2 March 2013 |date=5 February 2013 |deadurl=yes |accessdate=2013-10-11 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131012211621/http://www.bresciamobilita.it/news/parte-la-metro-2-marzo-2013 |archivedate=2013年10月12日 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cityrailways.it/news/la-metro-di-brescia-apre-sabato-2-marzo.html |title=La metro di Brescia apre sabato 2 marzo |website=CityRailways.it |language=it |trans_title=The Brescia Metro opens 2 March |date=5 February 2013 |deadurl=yes |accessdate=15 February 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131012193235/http://www.cityrailways.it/news/la-metro-di-brescia-apre-sabato-2-marzo.html |archive-date=2013-10-12 }}</ref>。

'''布鲁克林·戴可儿'''（{{lang-en|'''Brooklyn Danielle Decker'''}}，{{bd|1987年|4月12日|||catIdx= D戴可儿}}）出生于[[美国|美国]][[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[蒙哥馬利縣_(俄亥俄州)|蒙哥马利县]]，美国模特儿和女演员。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.heftig-magazin.de/2010/12/09/v2/ |title=V2 | heft|ig magazin |publisher=Heftig-magazin.de |date=October 17, 2008 |accessdate=September 23, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://lucire.com/2007/0112ll0.shtml |title=Brooklyn in New York |publisher=Lucire |date=January 12, 2007 |accessdate=January 13, 2007}}</ref>

== 生平 ==
1987年，戴可儿出生于[[美国|美国]][[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[蒙哥馬利縣_(俄亥俄州)|蒙哥马利县]]，她的母亲特萨是一个[[护士|护士]]，她的父亲斯蒂芬·戴克是一个[[推销员|推销员]]，戴可儿有一个弟弟，乔丹，比她年轻3岁。<ref>{{cite web|author=By Théoden Janes |url=http://www.charlotteobserver.com/2010/02/10/1236286/brooklyn-deckers-biggest-splash.html |title=Brooklyn Decker's biggest splash | CharlotteObserver.com & The Charlotte Observer Newspaper |publisher=Charlotteobserver.com |date=February 10, 2010 |accessdate=October 24, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://cincytv.blogspot.com/2006_02_01_archive.html |title=Television: February 2006 |publisher=Cincytv.blogspot.com |accessdate=September 23, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.middletownjournal.com/hp/content/oh/story/news/local/2006/12/30/mj123006mccrabbtop10.html |title=Dayton, Ohio, news and information |publisher=MiddletownJournal |accessdate=September 23, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110927235342/http://www.middletownjournal.com/hp/content/oh/story/news/local/2006/12/30/mj123006mccrabbtop10.html |archivedate=2011年9月27日 }}</ref> 
起初，戴可儿一家搬迁到了俄亥俄州的米德尔顿，后来有搬迁到[[北卡罗来纳州|北卡罗来纳州]]夏洛特。<ref name=Askmen>{{cite web |url=http://www.askmen.com/celebs/women/models_300/380_brooklyn_decker.html |title=profile |publisher=Askmen.com |date=August 3, 1987 |accessdate=February 17, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110216084438/http://www.askmen.com/celebs/women/models_300/380_brooklyn_decker.html |archivedate=2011年2月16日 }}</ref>
戴可儿在夏洛特的一个购物中心被[[星探|星探]]发掘，开始了自己的模特生涯。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.babyrazzi.com/2011/01/19/brooklyn-deckers-dad-saved-her-from-eating-disorder/ |title=Brooklyn Decker's Dad Saved Her From Eating Disorder |publisher=BabyRazzi |accessdate=October 24, 2011}}</ref>

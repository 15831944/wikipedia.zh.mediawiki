'''布蘭登·布魯默'''出生於1986年8月8日，現居香港。布蘭登是一位美國企業家、行政人員及投资者，亦是開發分散式賬本[https://eos.io EOS.IO]的[[科技公司|科技公司]][https://block.one/ Block One]的首席執行官。<ref name="pamelaambler">{{Cite web|author=Ambler|first=Pamela|work=[[Forbes|Forbes]]|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/pamelaambler/2018/02/07/brendan-blumer-block-eos-crypto-cryptocurrency/|title=Ethereum Challenger Brendan Blumer Breaks Biggest Token Sale Record With EOS|date=7 February 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref>

== 早期生活 ==
布蘭登出生及成長於[[錫達拉皮茲|錫達拉皮茲]]。<ref name="paulvigna">{{Cite web|author=Vigna|first=Paul|work=[[Wall_Street_Journal|Wall Street Journal]]|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/chasing-the-next-bitcoin-investors-shell-out-700-million-for-coins-with-no-purpose-1513602000|title=Chasing the Next Bitcoin, Investors Shell Out $700 Million for Coins With 'No Purpose'|date=18 December 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref> 布蘭登15歲時開發了一個用以销售[[大型多人在线角色扮演游戏|多人在线游戏]]上的[[虛擬經濟|虚拟资产]]的網站Gamecliff（通常稱為''GaMeCliff）<ref name="katharinecarlon">{{Cite web|author=Carlon|first=Katharine|work=Corridor Business|url=https://mixergy.com/interviews/brendan-blumer-accounts-ne-interview/|title=CR native is building the future, block by block|date=9 January 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref>''，網站上展示了包括[[魔兽世界|魔兽世界]]及[[无尽的任务|無盡的任務]]等遊戲的角色﹑武器和房屋等。<ref name="pamelaambler">{{Cite web|author=Ambler|first=Pamela|work=[[Forbes|Forbes]]|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/pamelaambler/2018/02/07/brendan-blumer-block-eos-crypto-cryptocurrency/|title=Ethereum Challenger Brendan Blumer Breaks Biggest Token Sale Record With EOS|date=7 February 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref>

== 职业生涯 ==
2005年，布蘭登的網站Gamecliff被[[IGE|IGE]]收購。<ref name="dianabruk">{{Cite web|author=Bruk|first=Diana|work=[[Best_Life|Best Life]]|url=https://bestlifeonline.com/10-cryptocurrency-millionaires-40/|title=Here Are the 10 Wealthiest Cryptomillionaires Under 40|date=7 February 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref> [[IGE|IGE]]亦轉移其總部至[[香港|香港]]以領導Gamecliff的營運。<ref name="pamelaambler">{{Cite web|author=Ambler|first=Pamela|work=[[Forbes|Forbes]]|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/pamelaambler/2018/02/07/brendan-blumer-block-eos-crypto-cryptocurrency/|title=Ethereum Challenger Brendan Blumer Breaks Biggest Token Sale Record With EOS|date=7 February 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref>

2007年，布蘭登成立Accounts.net，該公司專門销售[[大型多人在线角色扮演游戏|多人在线游戏]]內的角色。<ref name="andrewwarner">{{Cite web|author=Warner|first=Andrew|work=Mixergy|url=https://mixergy.com/interviews/brendan-blumer-accounts-ne-interview/|title=At 15, He Sold Game Avatars. Today, He's Selling Asia|date=9 August 2011|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref>

2010年，布蘭登創立了一個為亞洲 [[地產代理|房地产经纪]]而設的[[Data_sharing|数据共享]]平台[https://www.okay.com/ Okay.com]，<ref name="pamelaambler">{{Cite web|author=Ambler|first=Pamela|work=[[Forbes|Forbes]]|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/pamelaambler/2018/02/07/brendan-blumer-block-eos-crypto-cryptocurrency/|title=Ethereum Challenger Brendan Blumer Breaks Biggest Token Sale Record With EOS|date=7 February 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref> 该公司後來與房地產公司[[Asia_Pacific_Properties|Asia Pacific Properties]]合併。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.asiapacificproperties.com/corporate-news/archive-news/26/312/asia-pacific-properties-acquired-a-leading-real-estate-technology-company.html|title=Asia Pacific Properties Acquired a Leading Real Estate Technology Company|accessdate=|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref>

2013年，布蘭登成立了一家致力於[[印度|印度]]房地產上市的創業公司ii5。<ref name="paulvigna">{{Cite web|author=Vigna|first=Paul|work=[[Wall_Street_Journal|Wall Street Journal]]|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/chasing-the-next-bitcoin-investors-shell-out-700-million-for-coins-with-no-purpose-1513602000|title=Chasing the Next Bitcoin, Investors Shell Out $700 Million for Coins With 'No Purpose'|date=18 December 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref>

2016年，布蘭登認識了[[程序员|程序員]][[丹尼尔·拉里默|丹尼尔'''·'''拉里默]]。

2017年3月，布蘭登與[[丹尼尔·拉里默|丹尼尔'''·'''拉里默]]共同成立了專注從事高性[[区块链|区块链]]技术的[[开源软件|开放源码软件]]出版公司Block.One。<ref name="paulvigna">{{Cite web|author=Vigna|first=Paul|work=[[Wall_Street_Journal|Wall Street Journal]]|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/chasing-the-next-bitcoin-investors-shell-out-700-million-for-coins-with-no-purpose-1513602000|title=Chasing the Next Bitcoin, Investors Shell Out $700 Million for Coins With 'No Purpose'|date=18 December 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref>同年5月，布蘭登與[[丹尼尔·拉里默|丹尼尔]]宣布他們的第一個項目[[EOS.IO|EOS.IO]]<ref name="danlarimer">{{Cite web|author=Larimer|first=Dan|work=YouTube|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MUZWZj1pu94|title=EOS Consensus Presentation May 2017|date=5 June 2017|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref> 。外界批評Block.One為期一年的EOS[[首次代币发行|首次代幣發行]]所幕集的大量資金是不必要的。<ref name="paulvigna">{{Cite web|author=Vigna|first=Paul|work=[[Wall_Street_Journal|Wall Street Journal]]|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/chasing-the-next-bitcoin-investors-shell-out-700-million-for-coins-with-no-purpose-1513602000|title=Chasing the Next Bitcoin, Investors Shell Out $700 Million for Coins With 'No Purpose'|date=18 December 2018|accessdate=24 May 2018}}</ref>

2018年2月，他被''福布斯''評定為"虛擬貨幣上最有錢的人"。<ref name="forbes">{{Cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/richest-in-cryptocurrency/|title=The Richest People In Cryptocurrency|date=6 February 2018|work=Forbes|accessdate=20 July 2018}}</ref>

2019年2月，布蘭登獲邀出席慕尼黑安全會議，發表區塊鏈如何改變金融、政府、及安全政策的演說。

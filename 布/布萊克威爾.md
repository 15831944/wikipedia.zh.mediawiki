'''布萊克威爾英國'''（Blackwell UK），又稱布萊克威爾集團，是[[英國|英國]]的一家學術書籍銷售商，創業於1879年，擁有約1,000名僱員<ref name="Staff ownership">{{cite news|last=Finch|first=Julia|title=Blackwell book chain owner plans to hand firm over to staff|url=https://www.theguardian.com/business/2010/sep/08/blackwells-bookstores-staff-ownership-switch|accessdate=27 January 2012|newspaper=The Guardian|date=8 September 2010}}</ref><ref name="library jobs cut">{{cite news|last=Campbell|first=Lisa|title=Blackwell cuts 19 from library supply business|url=http://www.thebookseller.com/news/blackwell-cuts-19-library-supply-business.html|accessdate=27 January 2012|newspaper=The Bookseller|date=10 May 2011}}</ref>。2012年，布萊克威爾聯合其他多家零售商開發了自己的電子書籍。

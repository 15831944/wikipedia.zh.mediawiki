'''布希拉·伊姆蘭''' （{{lang-en|Bushra Imran}}，{{bd|1978年|8月16日|catIdx=Imran, Bushra}}），[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]第一夫人，总理[[伊姆蘭·汗|伊姆蘭·汗]]的现任妻子。<ref>[https://images.dawn.com/news/1180700 “First Lady Bushra Imran's veil is none of your business”]. Dawn. 18-08-2018.</ref>她亦是一名宗教靈修導師（[[穆里希德|穆里希德]]）。<ref>[https://www.khaleejtimes.com/international/pakistan/what-imran-khans-wife-bushra-maneka-said-about-his-victory-in-pakistan “What Imran Khan's wife Bushra Maneka said about his victory in Pakistan”]. Khaleej Times. 28-07-2018.</ref>

她來自於旁遮普省一個保守的家族。在2017年离婚前，她名字叫布希拉·玛内卡。首任丈夫Khawar Maneka是一名高级海关官员，两人育有三子二女。<ref>[https://dailytimes.com.pk/178023/whos-khawar-farid-maneka-bushra-took-divorce/ “Who’s Khawar Farid Maneka and why Bushra took divorce from him”]. Pakistan Daily Times. 18-01-2018.</ref>

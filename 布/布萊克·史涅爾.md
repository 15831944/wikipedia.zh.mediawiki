'''布萊克·艾許頓·史涅爾'''（{{lang-en|Blake Ashton Snell}}, {{bd|1992年|12月4日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟坦帕灣光芒隊的投手。2018年拿下生涯首座賽揚獎。

==職業生涯==
===坦帕灣光芒===
====2016年====
2016年4月23日，史涅爾於洋基球場初登板。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.si.com/mlb/2016/04/22/tampa-bay-rays-call-blake-snell|title=Rays will call up top prospect Blake Snell|last=|first=|date=April 22, 2016|work=Sports Illustrated|access-date=May 13, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180619062750/https://www.si.com/mlb/2016/04/22/tampa-bay-rays-call-blake-snell|archive-date=2018-06-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>首局就因為暴投而失分，不過他初登板主投5局6K僅失一分，無關勝敗。整季他6勝8敗，防禦率3.54，89局投球送出98K。
====2017年====
看到史涅爾在2016年的表現，球隊給予他非常高的期望。然而，該年的史涅爾前8場先發都投不滿6局，甚至平均4局的用球量就超過100球。前8場先發投下來，他的防禦率高達4成71。因此他在5月13日被下放到3A磨練。6月28日，他回歸大聯盟，並且在7月23日後，投出5勝1敗，防禦率3.31的好表現。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.foxsports.com/florida/video/978027075633|title=Blake Snell called up from Triple-A, will start for Rays on Wednesday {{!}} FOX Sports|work=FOX Sports|access-date=2017-07-19|language=en-US|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170729093309/http://www.foxsports.com/florida/video/978027075633|archive-date=2017-07-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>
====2018年：賽揚獎====
2018年開季，史涅爾被球隊安排為2號先發，排在[[克理斯·亞契|克理斯·亞契]]之後。6月3日，他在客場對決西雅圖水手時，開局連續投出7次三振，追平了美聯紀錄。

明星賽開打前，史涅爾投出12勝4敗，2.09的防禦率為美聯最低，但是他卻沒有入選明星賽，這也引發了不少球員、教練與粉絲們的抗議。<ref>{{cite web|title=Blake Snell Snub Highlights Issue With MLB All-Star Selection System|url=https://www.si.com/mlb/video/2018/07/09/black-snell-mlb-all-star-snub-selection-process-broken|website=SI.com|accessdate=July 13, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180714080832/https://www.si.com/mlb/video/2018/07/09/black-snell-mlb-all-star-snub-selection-process-broken|archive-date=2018-07-14|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Chris Archer on Blake Snell's snub from All-Star roster: 'Something like that can't happen'|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/24045379/chris-archer-says-blake-snell-snub-all-star-roster-joke-calls-voting-players-do-due-diligence|website=ESPN.com|accessdate=July 13, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180714081539/http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/24045379/chris-archer-says-blake-snell-snub-all-star-roster-joke-calls-voting-players-do-due-diligence|archive-date=2018-07-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>後來[[柯瑞·克魯伯|柯瑞·克魯伯]]因為受傷退出明星賽，史涅爾因而遞補，生涯首度入選明星賽。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/baseball/ny-sports-snell-rays-all-star-20180713-story.html|title=Rays starter Blake Snell added to All-Star roster after initial snub - NY Daily News|last=Becker|first=Jake|work=nydailynews.com|access-date=2018-07-14|language=en-US|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180714051145/http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/baseball/ny-sports-snell-rays-all-star-20180713-story.html|archive-date=2018-07-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>

7月23日，史涅爾因肩膀疲勞而進入傷兵名單，<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.tampabay.com/blogs/rays/2018/07/23/rays-place-blake-snell-on-disabled-list-with-shoulder-fatigue/|title=Rays place Blake Snell on disabled list with shoulder fatigue|date=2018-07-23|access-date=2018-07-24|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180724004903/http://www.tampabay.com/blogs/rays/2018/07/23/rays-place-blake-snell-on-disabled-list-with-shoulder-fatigue/|archive-date=2018-07-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>8月4日回歸球場。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.tampabay.com/blogs/rays/2018/08/03/rays-journal-pham-on-10-day-dl-snell-back-on-the-mound/|title=Rays Journal: Pham on 10-day DL, Snell back on the mound|date=2018-08-03|access-date=2018-08-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180804092237/http://www.tampabay.com/blogs/rays/2018/08/03/rays-journal-pham-on-10-day-dl-snell-back-on-the-mound/|archive-date=2018-08-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>8月21日，他達成了連續13場主場先發的責失分都在一分以下的紀錄。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://twitter.com/stevecarney/status/1032074767963176960?s=21|title=Steve Carney on Twitter|work=Twitter|access-date=2018-08-22|language=en}}</ref>他8月投出4勝0敗，防禦率1.08，獲選美聯單月最佳投手。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/cole-hamels-blake-snell-are-pitchers-of-month/c-293287818|title=Hamels, Snell named August Pitchers of Month|date=2018-09-04|access-date=2018-09-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180905122101/https://www.mlb.com/news/cole-hamels-blake-snell-are-pitchers-of-month/c-293287818|archive-date=2018-09-05|dead-url=no}}</ref>

9月18日，史涅爾拿下單季20勝，成為繼2012年的[[大衛·普萊斯|大衛·普萊斯]]後，第一位拿下單季20勝的光芒投手。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mlb.com/rays/news/blake-snell-earns-20th-win-to-lead-majors/c-295138300|title=Milestone night for Snell: 20th win, 200th K|date=2018-09-18|access-date=2018-09-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180919102814/https://www.mlb.com/rays/news/blake-snell-earns-20th-win-to-lead-majors/c-295138300|archive-date=2018-09-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>9月23日，他拿下第21勝，成為光芒隊史勝投王。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/blake-snell-earns-rays-club-record-21-wins/c-295749522|title=Snell picks up franchise-record 21st win of year|date=2018-09-23|access-date=2018-09-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180923224225/https://www.mlb.com/news/blake-snell-earns-rays-club-record-21-wins/c-295749522|archive-date=2018-09-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>整個9月，他投出5勝0敗，防禦率1.26，再度蟬聯美聯單月最佳投手。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/blake-snell-german-marquez-win-sept-awards/c-296786604|title=Snell, Marquez earn Pitcher of Month honors|date=2018-10-01|access-date=2018-10-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181001220509/https://www.mlb.com/news/blake-snell-german-marquez-win-sept-awards/c-296786604|archive-date=2018-10-01|dead-url=no}}</ref>

整季他拿下21勝為美聯最高，1.89的防禦率為美聯最低。他31場先發有21場先發無失分，其中27場責失分都在2分以下。

11月14日，史涅爾以169積分打敗154積分的[[賈斯丁·韋蘭德|賈斯丁·韋蘭德]]，在賽揚獎票選中勝出。他也成為繼2012年的[[大衛·普萊斯|大衛·普萊斯]]後，第一位得到賽揚獎殊榮的光芒投手。

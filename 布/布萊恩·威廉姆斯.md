'''布萊恩·道格拉斯·威廉斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Brian Douglas Williams'''}}<ref name=tvg>{{cite web| url= http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/brian-williams/bio/198488| title=Brian Williams| publisher= [[TV_Guide|TVGuide.com]]|accessdate=March 22, 2015}}</ref>，{{bd|1959年|5月5日}}）是一位[[美國|美國]]記者和主持人，他從2004年至2015年主持[[NBC夜間新聞|NBC夜間新聞]]，年薪約1,300萬美元。在2007年，他被[[時代雜誌|時代雜誌]]選為這年[[2007年时代百大人物|世界上最有影響力的100個人]]<ref>[http://www.time.com/time/specials/2007/time100/article/0,28804,1595326_1595332_1616821,00.html Brian Williams - The TIME 100 - TIME]</ref>。在2010年，他被一個著名的媒體觀察家評為「21世紀的[[沃爾特·克朗凱特|沃爾特·克朗凱特]]」<ref name="marketwatch.com">[http://www.marketwatch.com/story/brian-williams-21st-century-cronkite-2010-06-02 Jon Friedman, "Brian Williams: a Cronkite for the 21st century"], MarketWatch, June 2, 2010.</ref>。

==早年生活==
布萊恩生於美國[[新澤西州|新澤西州]]一個[[愛爾蘭人|愛爾蘭裔]]家庭<ref name=tvg /><ref name="NY Post">{{Cite news|last=Albiniak|first=Page|url=http://nypost.com/1999/11/30/questions-for-brian-williams/|title=Questions for Brian Williams|work=[[New_York_Post|New York Post]]|quote =I come from a loud Irish-Catholic family.|date=November 1, 2009|accessdate=October 7, 2010}}</ref>，父親戈登·路易斯·威廉姆斯（{{lang|en|Gordon Lewis Williams}}）是美國全國零售商協會（{{lang|en|National Retail Merchants Association}}）的行政副總裁<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.nytimes.com/1986/06/08/style/brian-williams-weds-jane-stoddard-tv-producer.html | work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] | title=Brian Williams Weds Jane Stoddard, TV Producer | date=June 8, 1986}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bates.edu/commencement/annual/y2005/honorands/brian-williams/address-by-brian-williams/|title=Address by Brian Williams — Commencement 2015 - Bates College|work=bates.edu|accessdate=February 6, 2015}}</ref>，母親桃樂茜·梅（{{lang|en|Dorothy May}}）是一名業餘演員<ref name="NY Post"/>，他在家中排行最小，對上有3個兄長<ref>{{cite web|last=Mullen |first=Shannon |url=http://old.seattletimes.com/html/television/2002144924_brianwilliams10.html |title=Television: Brian Williams is living his dream as "Nightly News" anchor |publisher=The Seattle Times |date=2005-01-10 |accessdate=October 4, 2013}}</ref>。他幼時在[[紐約州|紐約州]]生活過10年，之後隨家人遷回新澤西州，並於當地入讀初中<ref>Strauss, Robert. [http://www.nytimes.com/2002/10/27/nyregion/in-person-the-life-of-brian-annotated.html "IN PERSON; The Life Of Brian, Annotated"], ''The New York Times'', October 27, 2002. Retrieved June 13, 2011: "Mr. Williams grew up in Mom-apple-pie-and-TV-trays style in Middletown, Monmouth County, a town of true middle class.... Mr. Williams, who was in junior high when the family moved there from Elmira, N.Y., was an average student who had his eyes on fast cars, fun summer jobs and hanging out at the local fire station, where he became a volunteer firefighter."</ref>。

高中期間，曾經當過3年志願[[消防員|消防員]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nopactalent.com/speakers/Brian-Williams/1043|title=Brian Williams|publisher=NOPAC Talent|accessdate=October 14, 2007|quote=Graduated from Mater Dei, a Roman Catholic High School in New Monmouth, NJ.| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090202134158/http://www.nopactalent.com/celebrity/Brian-Williams-agent-booking-appearances.php| archivedate=February 2, 2009}}</ref>，亦在學校報章當過編輯<ref>{{cite news|url=http://time.com/3700177/see-brian-williams-through-the-years/|title=See Brian Williams Through The Years|work=TIME.com|accessdate=February 24, 2015}}</ref>，曾經在一次足球比賽中受傷，撞歪了鼻<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1995/12/10/opinion/liberties-send-in-the-clones.html|title=Liberties - Send in the Clones - NYTimes.com|date=December 10, 1995|work=nytimes.com|accessdate=February 22, 2015}}</ref>。

中學畢業後，入讀{{link-en|布魯克代爾社區學院|Brookdale Community College}}，之後轉到[[美國天主教大學|美國天主教大學]]及[[喬治華盛頓大學|喬治華盛頓大學]]繼續學業<ref>{{cite news|url=http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9905E1DA153CF934A15753C1A9649C8B63|title=IN PERSON; The Life Of Brian, Annotated|last=Strauss|first=Robert|date=October 27, 2002|publisher=''The New York Times''|accessdate=October 14, 2007}}</ref>。1970年代末輟學，為[[吉米·卡特|吉米·卡特]]政府做實習生，他對此抱憾終生<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.grads.tulane.edu/transcript_brianwilliams.html|title=Remarks by Brian Williams. Tulane University Commencement|date=May 19, 2007}}</ref>。

==醜聞==
2015年2月，布萊恩被爆出其講述多次的傳奇經歷，在2003年[[伊拉克戰爭|伊拉克戰爭]]中乘直升機被擊落後緊急迫降的故事，全部是虛構，已經有媒體取得關鍵證據後2月7日只好出來承認並道歉，連帶也有人開始懷疑其風災中報導大量浮屍等聳動性風格的過往報導，也許全部是添油加醋的杜撰。此事之後NBC將其暫時停職。<ref>[http://fund.cnyes.com/news.aspx?choose=newscontent&sn=20150209100904552378711 採訪經歷造假 NBC《夜間新聞》資深主播威廉斯停職]</ref>2015年6月18日，NBC宣布NBC晚间新闻平日主播改由原周末主播[[萊斯特·霍爾特|萊斯特·霍爾特]]担任，但布莱恩仍留任NBC新闻主播，目前擔任[[MSNBC|MSNBC]]频道的新聞主播.

'''布朗克斯動物園'''（Bronx Zoo）是位於美國[[紐約市|紐約市]][[布朗克斯公園|布朗克斯公園]]中、橫跨[[布朗克斯河|布朗克斯河]]的一個動物園，它是美國面積最大的動物園之一，達{{convert|265|acre|ha}}。它是[[國際野生生物保護學會|國際野生生物保護學會]]的總部所在地，[[动物园与水族馆协会|动物园与水族馆协会]]成員。<ref name="nycparks">{{cite web |url=http://www.nycgovparks.org/parks/brooklynbotanicgarden/history |title=Brooklyn Botanic Garden History |website=NYC Parks |accessdate=April 28, 2013}}</ref>

== 歷史 ==

[[File:William_Hornaday.jpg|thumb]]正在餵一隻[[大旋角羚|大旋角羚]]，1920年]]
1895年，以[[麥迪遜·格蘭特|麥迪遜·格蘭特]]為首的一群布恩和克罗克特俱樂部（Boone and Crockett Club，1887年由[[西奧多·羅斯福|西奧多·羅斯福]]建立的一個狩獵俱樂部）組建了紐約動物學會（New York Zoological Society，今[[國際野生生物保護學會|國際野生生物保護學會]]），并打算再成立一個動物園以促進動物學的研究<ref>{{cite book|title=Annual Report of the New York Zoological Society|date=1896|publisher=New York Zoological Society|location=New York|url=https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/item/66903#page/9/mode/1up|accessdate=29 August 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |last=Grinnell |first=George |title=Brief History of the Boone and Crockett Club |date=1910 |publisher=Forest and Stream Publishing Company |location=New York, New York |page=7}}</ref>。

動物園本身（Bronx Zoological Park<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://timesmachine.nytimes.com/timesmachine/1903/11/27/105067396.pdf |title=New Antelope house |work=nytimes.com |publisher=New York Times |date=November 27, 1903 |accessdate=February 28, 2011}} "The antelope house at the Bronx Zoological Park was opened to the public yesterday."</ref>或Bronx Zoological Gardens<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://timesmachine.nytimes.com/timesmachine/1911/05/24/105027894.pdf |title=Taft Enjoys Trip To The Bronx Zoo |work=nytimes.com |publisher=New York Times |date=May 24, 1911 |accessdate=February 28, 2011}} "President Taft paid a two-hour visit to the Bronx Zoological Gardens yesterday afternoon, as the guest of the New York Zoological Society."</ref>）在1899年11月8日對外開放，共有843隻動物。首任園長為動物學家[[威廉·坦普爾·霍那代|威廉·坦普爾·霍那代]]，他在這個位子上一呆就是三十年。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.nytimes.com/1937/03/07/archives/dr-w-t-hornaday-dies-in-stamford-noted-naturalist-82-was-the-first.html |title=Dr. W. T. Hornaday Dies In Stamford |work=nytimes.com |publisher=New York Times |date=March 7, 1937 |accessdate=May 31, 2010}}</ref>

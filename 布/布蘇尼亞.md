'''布蘇尼亞'''（{{lang-en|Busunya}}）為位在[[迦納|迦納]][[布朗阿哈福地區|布朗阿哈福地區]]的城鎮，2010年人口普查中該城鎮有5,424人<ref name=statsghana/>。該城鎮的學校以布蘇尼亞中學（Busunya High School）較知名<ref name=Scho>{{cite web|title=Educational Institutions|url=http://www.centralregion.gov.gh/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=34&Itemid=57|publisher=www.centralregion.gov.gh|accessdate=12 August 2011}}</ref><ref name=MORESCHO>{{cite web|title=References » Schools/Colleges|url=http://www.modernghana.com/GhanaHome/regions/schools.asp?menu_id=6&sub_menu_id=310&gender=&s=a|publisher=www.modernghana.com|accessdate=12 August 2011}}</ref>，屬二階段制學校<ref name=allschool>{{cite web|title=List of Secondary Schools in Ghana|url=http://www.ghanaschoolsnet.com/page/list-of-senior-secondary|publisher=www.ghanaschoolsnet.com/|accessdate=12 August 2011}}</ref>。

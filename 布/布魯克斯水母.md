'''布魯克斯水母'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Brooksella alternata'''''}}）是一種已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[海綿|海綿]]，生存於中[[寒武紀|寒武紀]]的[[海洋|海洋]]中。其[[化石|化石]]於[[美國|美國]][[阿拉巴馬州|阿拉巴馬州]]和[[喬治亞州|喬治亞州]]的{{Le|Conasauga|Conasauga Formation}}組中被發現。<ref name=ciampaglio>{{cite journal|last1=Ciampaglio|first1=Charles N.|last2=Babcock|first2=Loren E.|last3=Wellman|first3=Carrie L.|last4=York|first4=Angela R.|last5=Brunswick|first5=Holly K.|title=Phylogenetic affinities and taphonomy of Brooksella from the Cambrian of Georgia and Alabama, USA|journal=[[Palaeoworld|Palaeoworld]]|date=August 2006|volume=15|issue=3-4|pages=256–265|doi=10.1016/j.palwor.2006.10.002}}</ref> 牠們的化石呈[[葉|葉]]狀，通常具有6個或更多的裂片。

== 分類歷史 ==
布魯克斯水母在1896年被{{Le|Charles Doolittle Walcott|Charles Doolittle Walcott|d=Walcott}}首次描述<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Fossil jelly fishes from the Middle Cambrian terrane|url=http://agris.fao.org/agris-search/search.do?recordID=AV2012047782|last=D.|first=Walcott, Charles|date=1896|journal=AGRIS: International Information System for the Agricultural Science and Technology}}</ref>，並將其獨立一科[[布魯克斯水母科|布魯克斯水母科]]（Brooksellidae），將其劃入[[刺絲胞動物門|刺絲胞動物門]][[缽水母綱|缽水母綱]]<ref>{{Cite book|title=Fossil medusae. Vol. 30.|last=Walcott, Charles Doolittle.|first=|publisher=US Government Printing Office|year=1898|isbn=|location=|pages=}}</ref>。後來[[Caster,_K.E|卡斯特]]（Caster, K.E）將其劃為一獨立新綱──[[原水母綱|原水母綱]]<ref>{{Cite book|title=A new jellyfish (Kirklandia texana Caster) from the Lower Cretaceous of Texas.|last=Caster, Kenneth Edward.|first=|publisher=Palaeontological research institution|year=1945|isbn=|location=|pages=}}</ref>。後來更進一步的研究認為布魯克斯水母應當屬於[[多孔動物門|多孔動物門]][[六放海绵綱|六放海绵綱]]的成員，故基本上[[原水母綱|原水母綱]]已經廢棄不用<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Phylogenetic affinities and taphonomy of Brooksella from the Cambrian of Georgia and Alabama, USA|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1871174X06000345|last=Ciampaglio|first=Charles N.|last2=Babcock|first2=Loren E.|date=2006-08-01|journal=Palaeoworld|issue=3|doi=10.1016/j.palwor.2006.10.002|series=The Fourth International Symposium on the Cambrian System|volume=15|pages=256–265|last3=Wellman|first3=Carrie L.|last4=York|first4=Angela R.|last5=Brunswick|first5=Holly K.}}</ref>。

'''草原古馬屬'''（'''''Merychippus'''''）是一屬已經滅絕的三趾[[馬科|馬科]]生物，生活於[[中新世|中新世]]的北美洲地區。<ref>[http://paleodb.org/cgi-bin/bridge.pl?action=checkTaxonInfo&taxon_no=43009&is_real_user=1 Paleobiology Database: ''Merychippus'' basic info]</ref>

==命名==
[[File:Merychippus_insignis_life_restoration.jpg|thumb]]
草原古馬屬由約瑟夫·萊迪命名於1856年，模式種為''Merychippus insignis''<ref>R. L. Carroll. 1988. Vertebrate Paleontology and Evolution. W. H. Freeman and Company, New York 1-698</ref><ref>B. J. MacFadden. 1998. Equidae. In C. M. Janis, K. M. Scott, and L. L. Jacobs (eds.), Evolution of Tertiary Mammals of North America 1:537-559</ref><ref>V. M. Bravo-Cuevas and I. Ferrusquía-Villafranca. 2006. ''Merychippus'' (Mammalia, Perissodactyla, Equidae) from the Middle Miocene of state of Oaxaca, southeastern Mexico. Géobios 39:771-784</ref>。

==描述==
[[File:Anchitherium.jpg|upright]]
草原古馬是一種群居的馬，高約{{convert|89|cm|in}}<ref name=EoDP/>，在當時算是較高的馬。它的中趾在奔跑時不會接觸到地面，牙齒和[[副马属|副马属]]生物很像。<ref name=EoDP>{{cite book |editor=Palmer, D.|year=1999 |title= The Marshall Illustrated Encyclopedia of Dinosaurs and Prehistoric Animals|publisher= Marshall Editions|location=London|pages= 256–257|isbn= 1-84028-152-9}}</ref>

門多薩等人測量了四具草原古馬化石，其預估體重為：<ref>M. Mendoza, C. M. Janis, and P. Palmqvist. 2006. Estimating the body mass of extinct ungulates: a study on the use of multiple regression. Journal of Zoology 270(1):90-101</ref>

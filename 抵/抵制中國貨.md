'''抵制中國貨'''（{{lang-en|Boycott Chinese products}}），或稱'''抵制中國製造'''，是[[經濟|經濟]][[抵制|抵制]]的一種，目標為由[[中華人民共和國|中華人民共和國]]所持有或及生產的商品。一般認為抵制中國貨的原因是認為中國貨質量低劣，以及領土爭端，和難以接受部分社會模式（例如一年一度的[[玉林荔枝狗肉節|玉林荔枝狗肉節]]）<ref>{{cite web | url = http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/lucknow/City-cleric-gives-call-to-boycott-Chinese-products/articleshow/44636071.cms | title = Lucknow cleric gives call to boycott Chinese products  | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 19, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref><ref name="sft-canada.org">{{cite web| url =http://www.sft-canada.org/campaigns/economic/boycott-made-in-china/| title =Boycott Made in China : SFT Canada – Students for a Free Tibet Canada| author =| date =| accessdate =October 19, 2014| publisher =| language =| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20141016013014/http://www.sft-canada.org/campaigns/economic/boycott-made-in-china/| archivedate =2014年10月16日| df =}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://opinion.inquirer.net/32863/the-call-to-boycott-chinese-products | title = The call to boycott Chinese products | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 19, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref>。

[[印度|印度]]、[[菲律賓|菲律賓]]和[[越南|越南]]以及中國的[[分離主義|分離主義]]組織曾經發起抵制[[中國貨|中國貨]]的運動。普遍認為完全抵制中國貨是困難的，因為該國產品在全世界廣泛銷售和使用，而且有很多外資企業在中國內地設立工廠或者委託中國內地的工廠來生產商品，質量亦算上乘<ref>{{cite web | url = http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/chennai/Netizens-clamour-for-boycott-of-Made-in-China-products/articleshow/19888903.cms | title = Netizens clamour for boycott of ‘Made in China’ products  | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 20, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref>。

==起因==
中國是全世界人口最多的大國，並且是面積第三大的國家，有很長的邊境線並與數個國家相鄰。<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/ch.html | title = The World Factbook | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 22, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref>歷史上，中國與其鄰國發生過許多次衝突。<ref name="xiaobing">Xiaobing Li (2012). ''China at War: An Encyclopedia''. Publisher ABC-CLIO. ISBN 1598844156. Preface XV.</ref>一些中國皇帝試圖通過侵略戰爭的手段將其帝國擴張到[[中亞|中亞]]。這些衝突導致中亞鄰國的人反對中國，並發起抵制中國貨的運動以表達不滿。

1949年，[[中國共產黨|中國共產黨]]奪取中國的控制權，並宣佈獨立自主。<ref>Mikhail Iosifovich Sladkovskiĭ (1966). ''History of Economic Relations Between Russia and China''. Publisher Transaction Publishers. ISBN 1412825199. Page 236.</ref>從後，中國領導人以經濟發展為第一優先。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.japantimes.co.jp/opinion/2008/11/11/commentary/china-japan-can-help-by-helping-themselves/#.VEbqR_msWvM | title = China, Japan can help by helping themselves | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 22, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref>中國商家常常根據市場預期來調整產品的生產，因此中國產品質量可能並不高，<ref>Pula, Gabor& Santabárbara, Daniel (March 2011). [http://www.ecb.europa.eu/pub/pdf/scpwps/ecbwp1310.pdf Is China climbing up the quality ladder? Estimating cross country differences in product quality using Eurostat's COMEXT trade database]. WORKING PAPER SERIES.</ref>而消費者選擇的是其低廉的價格。

質量不高的中國產品，其使用壽命也是不長，但是中國商家普遍認為，如果使用壽命過長，反而會對他們非常不利，因為使用良久都不替換，會影響商家的生意。

[[人口過剩|人口過剩]]也是製造質量低劣和假貨（造假）的一個間接原因。許多公司無法找到足夠的所需原材料以製造消費者所需的產品，反而用低價材料生產假貨。另一些公司模仿[[蘋果公司|蘋果公司]]、[[凱悅酒店|凱悅酒店]]以及[[星巴克|星巴克]]這類國際知名公司的產品，<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.theguardian.com/world/2012/oct/17/china-fake-apple-shops-romney | title = China's fake Apple shops point to impatience for the newest products | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 24, 2014 | publisher = the Guardian | language = }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.forbes.com/sites/michaelzakkour/2014/04/30/copycat-china-still-a-problem-for-brands-chinas-future-just-ask-apple-hyatt-starbucks/ | title = Copycat China Still A Problem For Brands & China's Future: Just Ask Apple, Hyatt & Starbucks | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 24, 2014 | publisher = Forbes | language = }}</ref>或是既模仿其知名商標又模仿整個商店。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/money/industries/technology/2011-07-31-China-counterfeiting-fake-Western-goods-stores_n.htm | title = China takes knock-offs to a new level, copying entire stores - USATODAY.com | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 28, 2014 | publisher = USATODAY.COM | language = }}</ref>

[[2008年中國奶製品污染事件|2008年中國奶製品污染事件]]被認為是中國缺乏[[食品安全|食品安全]]的一個信號，影響到了成千上萬的人，許多中國父母不再信任中國奶製品。<ref>[http://www.eeo.com.cn/ens/2013/0613/245231.shtml Why it’s Still So Hard to Find Safe Baby Formula] By Qi Yue (启越) [[The_Economic_Observer|The Economic Observer Online]] 2013-06-13 17:44</ref>

==抵制活動==
==={{IND}}===
由於2014年中印邊境衝突的關係，[[印度|印度]]和[[西藏流亡政府|西藏流亡政府]]聯合發起抵制中國貨的運動<ref>{{cite web | url = http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/Indo-Tibetan-meet-proposes-boycott-of-Chinese-goods/articleshow/22914680.cms | title = Indo | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 20, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://zeenews.india.com/news/uttar-pradesh/boycott-chinese-products-says-prominent-cleric-in-eid-sermon_1480643.html | title = Boycott Chinese products, says prominent cleric in Eid sermon | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 20, 2014 | publisher = Zee News | language = }}</ref>。印度「{{link-en|民族義勇團|Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh}}」領袖{{link-en|莫漢·巴格瓦特|Mohan Bhagwat}}宣佈：「我們談論關於自立和抗衡中國。新政府似乎是抗衡它了。但是如果我們不抵制購買中國貨，政府何以從中汲取力量呢？」<ref>{{cite web | url = http://indiatoday.intoday.in/story/mohan-bhagwat-says-ban-chinese-goods-rss-chief-dd-telecast/1/394013.html | title =  Boycott Chinese goods, says RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat in DD telecast : India, News  | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 20, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref>。一些印度商店因爲兩國之間的衝突而開始抵制中國貨<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.savetibet.org/shops-in-india-join-chinese-goods-boycott-campaign/ | title = Shops in India join Chinese goods boycott campaign  | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 20, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref>。

==={{PHL}}===
在菲律賓，有不同的團體發起全國性的抵制「中國製造」運動。<ref>{{cite web | url = https://ph.news.yahoo.com/filipino-vietnamese-americans-call-boycott-made-china-products-091003080.html | title = Filipino, Vietnamese Americans call for boycott of 'Made in China' products | author = News Desk in Washington/Philippine Daily Inquirer | date =  | accessdate = October 19, 2014 | publisher = Yahoo News Philippines | language =  | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141019183920/https://ph.news.yahoo.com/filipino-vietnamese-americans-call-boycott-made-china-products-091003080.html | archivedate = 2014年10月19日 }}</ref>由於2012年[[菲律賓|菲律賓]]與中國因[[黃岩島主權問題|黃岩島主權問題]]而發生衝突，[[阿爾拜省|阿爾拜省]]省長{{link-en|喬伊·薩爾斯達|Joey Salceda}}支持菲律賓人抵制中國貨。<ref>{{cite web| url =http://news.asiaone.com/News/Latest+News/Asia/Story/A1Story20110613-283760.html| title =Philippine governor calls for boycott of 'Made in China' products| author =| date =| accessdate =October 19, 2014| publisher =| language =| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20141021160008/http://news.asiaone.com/News/Latest%2BNews/Asia/Story/A1Story20110613-283760.html| archivedate =2014年10月21日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://globalnation.inquirer.net/37179/albay-gov-renews-call-for-boycott-of-china-products/ | title = Albay gov renews call for boycott of China  products | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 20, 2014 | publisher =  | language = }}</ref>

==={{VNM}}===
由於[[南中國海|南中國海]]主權爭議，越南與中國的關係緊張，尤其是[[海洋石油981|海洋石油981]]鑽井平臺的設立觸發了越南抵制中國貨的運動。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://learningenglish.voanews.com/content/vietnamese-boycott-chinese-products/1972975.html | title = Vietnamese Boycott Chinese Products | author =  | date =  | accessdate = October 19, 2014 | publisher = VOA | language = }}</ref>在[[2014年越南排華暴動|2014年越南排華暴動]]中，越南人通過抵制中國貨、使用越南貨來表達他們在南中國海爭端上的愛國主義精神。

==={{KOR}}===
2017年因[[韓國部署薩德反導彈系統事件|韓國部署薩德反導彈系統事件]]引起争端，中国掀起抵制韩货运动，[[韩国|韩国]]社会反弹，出现抵制中國貨呼声。

'''莉安·伍德{{link-en|威爾斯國民議會議員列表|Members of the National Assembly for Wales|議員}}'''（{{lang-en|'''Leanne Wood AM'''}}，{{bd|1971年|12月3日|||catIdx=W}}），是[[威爾斯人|威爾斯]]的[[政治家|政治家]]、[[威尔士党|威尔士党]]黨魁。

==早年生活==
1971年12月3日，本內特在[[威爾斯|威爾斯]]{{link-en|朗達 (威爾斯)|Rhondda|朗達}}{{link-en|立文尼比亞|Llwynypia}}出生<ref name="democracy live">{{cite news |title=Your representatives; Leanne Wood|url= http://news.bbc.co.uk/democracylive/hi/representatives/profiles/33490.stm | year=2011 |accessdate=23 July 2011 |publisher=BBC |work=[[BBC_Online|BBC News Democracy Live]] website }}</ref><ref name="Debrett's">{{cite web |url=http://www.debretts.com/people/biographies/browse/w/23617/Leanne+WOOD.aspx |title=Ms Leanne Wood, AM's biography |year=2011 |publisher=[[Debrett's|Debrett's]] |accessdate=25 July 2011 |work=[[Debrett's|Debrett's]] website |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120911094136/http://www.debretts.com/people/biographies/browse/w/23617/Leanne+WOOD.aspx |archivedate=2012年9月11日 }}</ref>，是傑夫和艾薇·伍德（John and Joy Bennett）的女兒<ref name=":0">[http://www.ukwhoswho.com/view/article/oupww/whoswho/U4000309 "Wood, Leanne"], ''Who's Who 2014'', A & C Black, an imprint of Bloomsbury Publishing plc, 2014; online edn, Oxford University Press, 2014 ; online edn, Nov 2014. Retrieved 9 May 2015 (subscription required).</ref>。她在{{link-en|潘尼埃|Penygraig}}成長，至今仍住在附近的村落。她曾就讀於[[東尼潘迪|東尼潘迪]]綜合學校（現東尼潘迪社區學院），並在{{link-en|格拉摩根大學|University of Glamorgan}}（今[[南威爾斯大學|南威爾斯大學]]）完成大學課程<ref name=Withers>{{cite news|author=Matt Withers |url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/news/wales-news/2012/03/17/she-leads-one-of-wales-biggest-parties-but-who-really-is-leanne-wood-91466-30556182/#ixzz2GLJTCzWo |title=She leads one of Wales' biggest parties... but who is Leanne Wood? |date=12 March 2012 |accessdate=28 December 2012 |publisher=[[Media_Wales|Media Wales Ltd]] |newspaper=[[Western_Mail_(Wales)|Western Mail]] |location=Cardiff}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=Rob Jones |url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/news/south-wales-news/rhondda/2007/01/11/people-and-places-that-helped-shape-the-tonypandy-we-know-today-91466-18459885/ |title=People and places that helped shape the Tonypandy we know today |accessdate=23 July 2011 |date=11 January 2007 |publisher=[[Media_Wales|Media Wales Ltd]] |newspaper=[[Rhondda_Leader|Rhondda Leader]] |location=Pontypridd}}</ref>。

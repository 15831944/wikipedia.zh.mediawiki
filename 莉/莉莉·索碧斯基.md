==個人生活==
索碧斯基過去在法國生活時，從她的父親那學會了一口流利的法語<ref name=Weeks>Janet Weeks. ''[[TV_Guide|TV Guide]]''; 1999-05-15; Pages 38 – 42</ref><ref name="france">{{cite web | title = Leelee Sobieski.com | work = Leeleesobieski.com – In Person Interview | url = http://www.leeleesobieski.com/articles/eric/eric2.html | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20061024144729/http://www.leeleesobieski.com/articles/eric/eric2.html | dead-url = yes | archive-date = 2006-10-24 | accessdate = 2006-08-23 }}</ref>。2009年1月，索碧斯基開始與時裝設計師亞當·坎摩爾（Adam Kimmel）交往，他是美國房地產開發商{{le|馬丁·坎摩爾|Martin Kimmel}}的兒子和美國賽船手兼設計師{{le|唐納德·阿羅諾|Donald Aronow}}的孫子。兩人於2009年5月28日訂婚，並於2009年6月23日對外公開；當時，索碧斯基在出席《[[頭號公敵|頭號公敵]]》（2009年）首映會時被粉絲發現戴著訂婚戒指。該夫妻於2009年7月17日正式宣布他們的訂婚<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.accesshollywood.com/leelee-sobieski-engaged-to-designer_article_20715 | title = Leelee Sobieski Engaged To Designer | publisher = [[Access_Hollywood|Access Hollywood]] | accessdate = 2010-11-22 | date = 2009-07-17 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100822141909/http://www.accesshollywood.com/leelee-sobieski-engaged-to-designer_article_20715 | archivedate = 2010-08-22 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.starpulse.com/news/index.php/2009/07/17/leelee_sobieski_engaged_ |title=Leelee Sobieski Engaged? |publisher=Starpulse.com |date=2009-07-17 |deadurl=no |accessdate=2012-12-16 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121008192259/http://www.starpulse.com/news/index.php/2009/07/17/leelee_sobieski_engaged_ |archivedate=2012-10-08 }}</ref><ref>{{cite magazine |url=http://www.usmagazine.com/news/leelee-sobieski-is-engaged-2009167 |title=Leelee Sobieski is Engaged |work=[[Us_Weekly|Us Weekly]] |date=2009-07-17 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090720073801/http://www.usmagazine.com/news/leelee-sobieski-is-engaged-2009167 |archivedate=2009-07-20 }}</ref>。

他們的女兒露易絲安娜·雷（Louisanna Ray）出生於2009年12月，而兒子馬丁（Martin）則出生於2014年8月<ref>{{cite magazine | url = http://celebritybabies.people.com/2009/12/18/leelee-sobieski-welcomes-daughter-louisanna-ray/ | title = Leelee Sobieski Welcomes Daughter Louisanna Ray | work = [[People_(magazine)|People]] | accessdate = 2013-02-10 | date = 2009-12-18 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130118110109/http://celebritybabies.people.com/2009/12/18/leelee-sobieski-welcomes-daughter-louisanna-ray/ | archivedate = 2013-01-18 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Leelee Sobieski Gives Birth, Welcomes Second Child With Husband Adam Kimmel|url=https://celebrity.yahoo.com/news/leelee-sobieski-gives-birth-welcomes-second-child-husband-212848962-us-weekly.html|accessdate=2015-01-12|publisher=Yahoo|date=2014-11-10|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402150705/https://celebrity.yahoo.com/news/leelee-sobieski-gives-birth-welcomes-second-child-husband-212848962-us-weekly.html|archivedate=2015-04-02}}</ref>。

2012年，索碧斯基證實「我不再拍片」。索碧斯基在受訪時透露，她只想專心照顧孩子，且拍戲也容易和他人產生複雜的關係<ref>{{cite magazine |first=Rachel Paula |last=Abrahamson |url=http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-moms/news/find-out-why-leelee-sobieski-left-hollywood-w203330 |title=Find Out Why 'Never Been Kissed' Actress Leelee Sobieski Left Hollywood |work=[[Us_Weekly|Us Weekly]] |date=2016-04-19 |accessdate=2016-04-30 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160501102754/http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-moms/news/find-out-why-leelee-sobieski-left-hollywood-w203330 |archivedate=2016-05-01 }}</ref>。

'''莉查·查達'''（{{lang-en|'''Richa Chadda'''}}，{{bd|1986年|12月18日|catIdx=Chadda, Richa}}）是[[印度|印度]]电影女演员，主要出現在[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]電影。2008年拍摄电影《Oye Lucky! Lucky Oye!》出道。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Gangs-of-Wasseypur-got-me-respect-as-an-actress-Richa-Chadda/articleshow/19967602.cms “'Gangs of Wasseypur' got me respect as an actress: Richa Chadda”]. The Times of India. 10-05-2013.</ref>2013年凭电影《Gangs of Wasseypur – Part 1》中的出色表现获得[[印度電影觀眾獎|印度電影觀眾獎]]的影评人最佳好女主角。<ref>[https://www.filmfare.com/news/bollywood/richa-chadha-complete-a-decade-in-hindi-cinema-28370.html “Richa Chadha completes a decade in Hindi cinema”]. Filmfare. 20-05-2018.</ref>她之后較成功的電影包括：《Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela》和《Masaan》等。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Richa-Chaddas-Masaan-gets-standing-ovation-at-Cannes/articleshow/47356013.cms “Richa Chadda's 'Masaan' gets standing ovation at Cannes”]. The Times of India. 20-05-2015.</ref>

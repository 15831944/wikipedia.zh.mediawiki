'''莉莉-蘿絲·美樂蒂·戴普'''（{{lang-en|'''Lily-Rose Melody Depp'''}}，{{bd|1999年|5月27日|catidx=Stone}}）是一位[[法國|法國]]女[[模特兒|模特兒]]和[[演員|演員]]。父親為[[美國|美國]]演員[[強尼·戴普|強尼·戴普]]，母親為法國[[歌手|歌手]][[凡妮莎·帕拉迪絲|凡妮莎·帕拉迪絲]]。莉莉-蘿絲亦參與了帕拉迪絲[[專輯|專輯]]《{{le|喜悅 (凡妮莎·帕拉迪絲專輯)|Bliss (Vanessa Paradis album)|喜悅}}》錄製。

== 早期生活 ==
1999年5月27日，莉莉-蘿絲·美樂蒂·戴普生於[[法國|法國]][[巴黎|巴黎]]<ref name=Blitz>{{cite book|last1=Blitz|first1=Michael|title=Johnny Depp: A Biography|date=2008|publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group|isbn=9780313343001|page=71}}</ref>。母親帕拉迪絲來自法國[[聖莫代福塞|聖莫代福塞]]，帕拉迪絲的雙親為室內設計師昂德列與珂琳·帕拉迪絲（{{lang|fr|André}} & {{lang|fr|Corinne Paradis}}）。
2012年強尼·戴普與凡妮莎·帕拉迪絲分手後，莉莉-蘿絲於固定時間來返[[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]與[[巴黎|巴黎]]和父親或母親同居<ref name=jointcustody>{{cite news|last1=Peters|first1=Sam|title=Johnny Depp and me: Vanessa Paradis on acting, gigging and life after a break up|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/profiles/johnny-depp-and-me-vanessa-paradis-on-acting-gigging-and-life-after-a-break-up-9422069.html|accessdate=10 June 2015|publisher=Independent|date=May 24, 2014}}</ref>。

莉莉-蘿絲有一位弟弟約翰·「傑克」·克里斯多福·戴普三世（{{lang|en|John "Jack" Christopher Depp III}}）<ref name=babyboy1>{{cite news|title=Baby boy for Depp and Paradis|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/entertainment/1938099.stm|accessdate=9 June 2015|publisher=BBC|date=Apr 18, 2002}}</ref>。莉莉-蘿絲是法國演員{{le|愛莉森·帕拉迪絲|Alysson Paradis}}的[[姨甥女|姨甥女]]<ref name=wedding>{{cite news|last1=Bailey|first1=Leanne|title=Johnny Depp has got married to Amber Heard|url=http://www.glamourmagazine.co.uk/news/celebrity/2014/01/15/are-amber-heard-and-johnny-depp-engaged|accessdate=9 June 2015|publisher=Glamour Magazine UK|date=Feb 5, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160703151211/http://www.glamourmagazine.co.uk/news/celebrity/2014/01/15/are-amber-heard-and-johnny-depp-engaged|archive-date=2016-07-03|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name=aunt>{{cite news|title=Vanessa Paradis brings sister as date to movie premiere|url=http://pagesix.com/2012/01/24/vanessa-paradis-brings-sister-as-date-to-movie-premiere/|accessdate=9 June 2015|publisher=Page Six|date=Jan 24, 2012}}</ref>。莉莉-蘿絲會說流利的[[法語|法語]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.people.com/article/yoga-hosers-lily-rose-depp-johnny-depp|title=Yoga Hosers: See Lily-Rose Depp, Johnny Depp's Daughter, in First Look : People.com|work=PEOPLE.com|accessdate=22 October 2015}}</ref>。

== 職業生涯 ==
=== 模特兒 ===
2015年4月，莉莉-蘿絲出現在澳洲版《{{le|Oyster Magazine|Oyster (magazine)|Oyster}}》雜誌內頁<ref name=Oystermag>{{cite news|title=Introducing: Lily-Rose Depp, Shot By Dana Boulos|url=http://www.oystermag.com/introducing-lily-rose-depp-shot-by-dana-boulos|accessdate=30 May 2015|publisher=Oyster|date=Apr 23, 2015}}</ref>。

2015年7月，莉莉-蘿絲以[[香奈兒|香奈兒]]模特兒的身份初次露面。她出現在[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]][[饒舌歌手|饒舌歌手]]{{le|瑞吉·史諾|Rejjie Snow}}[[音樂錄影帶|音樂錄影帶]]《All Around the World》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.msn.com/en-us/music/news/johnny-depps-daughter-lily-rose-depp-makes-her-music-video-debut-watch/ar-AAcO8w1|title=Johnny Depp's Daughter Lily-Rose Depp Makes Her Music Video Debut: Watch|work=msn.com|accessdate=22 October 2015}}</ref>。

2015年8月，莉莉-蘿絲參與「{{lang|en|Self Evident Project}}」攝影計畫，此計畫為拍攝一萬名生活在[[美國|美國]]並承認自己非100%[[異性戀|異性戀]]的人<ref>{{cite web|url=https://instagram.com/p/6s4QOauIih/|title=Instagram|work=Instagram|accessdate=22 October 2015}}</ref>。

=== 演員 ===
莉莉-蘿絲出演第一個角色是驚悚電影《{{le|人型海象|Tusk (2014 film)}}》裡的女店員，該片由[[凱文·史密斯|凱文·史密斯]]執導，史密斯同時也是莉莉-蘿絲好友[[哈莉·奎茵·史密斯|哈莉·奎茵·史密斯]]的父親。之後莉莉-蘿絲與哈莉主演《人型海象》的番外篇《[[瑜珈魯蛇|瑜珈魯蛇]]》，該片於2016年9月上映。
2015年6月，莉莉-蘿絲確定會出演《瑜珈魯蛇》續集《{{lang|en|Moose Jaws}}》<ref>{{cite web|last1=Smith|first1=Kevin|title=I love this so much. This is from the end credits of #YogaHosers. Get ready to face a monster moose, @harleyquinnnn_ & @lilyrose_depp!|url=https://facebook.com/YesThatKevinSmith/photos/a.77596326929.88348.6985046929/10152814889886930/?type=1&source=48&_ft_=top_level_post_id.10152814889886930|website=Facebook|accessdate=12 June 2015}}</ref>。

2015年，莉莉-蘿絲出演法國[[導演|導演]]{{le|蕾貝卡·斯洛托維斯基|Rebecca Zlotowski}}第三部自編自導作品《{{le|通靈美人|Planetarium (film)}}》，此片在法國巴黎拍攝，背景設定在1930年代，敘述一對擁有與鬼魂[[通靈|通靈]]的姊妹<ref>{{cite web|last1=Keslassy|first1=Elsa|title=Cannes: Natalie Portman to Star in French Ghost Story ‘Planetarium’ (EXCLUSIVE)|url=http://variety.com/2015/film/markets-festivals/natalie-portman-lily-rose-depp-planetarium-1201493381/|accessdate=3 October 2015}}</ref>。同年莉莉-蘿絲將主演{{le|史黛芬妮·迪·朱斯托|Stéphanie Di Giusto}}執導[[現代舞|現代舞]]創始人[[伊莎朵拉·鄧肯|伊莎朵拉·鄧肯]]傳記電影《{{le|狂舞摯愛|The Dancer (2016 film)}}》<ref>{{cite web|last1=Keslassy|first1=Elsa|title=Lily-Rose Depp to Star as Isadora Duncan in ‘The Dancer’|url=http://variety.com/2015/film/global/lily-rose-depp-to-star-as-isadora-duncan-in-the-dancer-1201601820/|accessdate=3 October 2015}}</ref>。

== 私人生活 ==
從莉莉-蘿絲出生以來，她一直是媒體追逐的對象，媒體一直注意她的生活，包括生日派對、出席社交圈活動和她的時尚衣著<ref name=birthdays>{{cite news|last1=Reed|first1=Sam|title=COOL TEEN LILY-ROSE DEPP CELEBRATES "SOUR SIXTEEN"|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/lily-rose-depp-sour-sixteen-798842|accessdate=6 June 2015|publisher=Hollywood Reporter|date=May 29, 2015}}</ref><ref name=birthday2>{{cite news|last1=Donnelly|first1=Erin|title=Lily-Rose Depp Had A "Sour Sixteen," Is Coolest Teen Ever|url=http://www.refinery29.com/2015/05/88289/lily-rose-depp-sour-sixteen-birthday-party|accessdate=Jun 6, 2015|publisher=Refinery 29|date=May 29, 2015}}</ref><ref name=fashionshow1>{{cite news|last1=Thistlewaite|first1=Felicity|title=A chip off the old block: Johnny Depp's daughter Lily-Rose attends fashion show|url=http://www.express.co.uk/news/showbiz/567639/Johnny-Depp-daughter-Lily-Rose-fashion-show|accessdate=6 June 2015|publisher=Express|date=Apr 1, 2015}}</ref><ref name=fashion1>{{cite news|last1=Ledbetter|first1=Carly|title=9 Reasons Why Lily-Rose Depp Is Fashion's Latest 'It' Girl|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2015/04/04/lily-rose-depp-style_n_6992746.html|accessdate=6 June 2015|publisher=Huffington Post|date=Apr 4, 2015}}</ref>。

2015年8月，莉莉-蘿絲參與[[藝術家|藝術家]]{{le|歐伊·蒂利特·萊特|iO Tillett Wright}}的「{{lang|en|Self Evident Project}}」攝影計畫，外界普遍認為莉莉-蘿絲透過攝影計畫承認她非100%異性戀<ref name=sexuality>{{cite news|last1=McDermott|first1=Maeve|title=Lily-Rose Depp came out via this inspiring Instagram post|url=http://entertainthis.usatoday.com/2015/08/24/lily-rose-depp-sexually-fluid-instagram/|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Entertain This|date=Aug 24, 2015}}</ref><ref name=sexuality2>{{cite news|last1=Williams|first1=Joe|title=Johnny Depp’s daughter comes out|url=https://www.pinknews.co.uk/2015/08/24/lily-rose-depp-comes-out/|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Pink News|date=Aug 24, 2015}}</ref>。

莉莉-蘿絲目前與演員[[提摩西·夏勒梅|提摩西·夏勒梅]]交往。

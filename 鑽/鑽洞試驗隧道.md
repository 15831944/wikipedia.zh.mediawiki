隧道的大部份路段均位於{{le|霍桑市際機場|Hawthorne Municipal Airport (California)}}的機場範圍外部，有一個非常短的部分位於机场围栏的角落處，但並非位處跑道上<ref name="shadbehr-201708222">{{cite report|url=http://hawthorne-ca.granicus.com/MetaViewer.php?view_id=&event_id=465&meta_id=32264|title=Agenda Item No. 6|publisher=City of Hawthorne City Council|series=Agenda Bill|date=22 August 2017|first=Arnold|last=Shadbehr|first2=Russ|last2=Miyahira|page=42|quote=strip of land {{convert|15|feet|m|sigfig=2}} wide … beginning of a non-tangent curve concave south westerly and having a radius of {{convert|530.00|feet|m|sigfig=4}} … {{convert|13.56|feet|m|sigfig=1}} southerly of the 120th Street centerline … thence tangent to said curve … parallel with and distant southerly a distance of {{convert|7721.21|feet|m|sigfig=3}} to a point of terminus}}</ref>。
隧道起點Dominguez雨水道和克伦肖大道之間的SpaceX停車場下方，穿過120西街，一條大直路和微轉彎後抵達終點。隧道的中間處計劃连接一個小型汽車升降機<ref name="shadbehr-201809112">{{cite report|url=http://hawthorne-ca.granicus.com/MetaViewer.php?view_id=2&clip_id=541&meta_id=35798|title=Agenda Item No. 9|publisher=City of Hawthorne City Council|series=Agenda Bill|date=11 September 2018|first=Arnold|last=Shadbehr|first2=Brian|last2=James|author3=Planning & Community Development|format=Planning report}}</ref>。

== 时间线 ==
2016年12月17日， [[伊隆·马斯克|伊隆·马斯克]] 表示對塞車感到沮丧，因而著手興建隧道<ref>{{Cite tweet|author-link=Elon Musk|user=elonmusk|number=810108760010043392|date=17 December 2016|title=Traffic is driving me nuts. Am going to build a tunnel boring machine and just start digging...}}</ref>。2017年4月前，鑽洞公司获得一个二手的隧道掘进机，並把機器運送至[[霍桑_(加利福尼亚州)|霍桑]]，并塗上公司的標誌<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://uk.businessinsider.com/elon-musk-launches-boring-company-to-dig-tunnels-2017-4|title=We just got our first glimpse of Elon Musk's new tunnel company|author=Muoio|first=Danielle|date=27 April 2017|work=Business News}}</ref>。{{TransH}}On 31 January 2018 the Boring Company acquired the land around a family house at 3834 West 119th Place for $0.5 million.<ref>{{cite report|url=https://portal.assessor.lacounty.gov/parceldetail/4048011032|title=Parcel detail: 4048-011-032|publisher=Los Angeles County|work=Assessor Portal|quote=3834 {{abbr|W 119TH PL|West 119th Place}} Hawthorne {{abbr|CA|California}} 90250-3226 ... == 運作 ==
2018年12月18日，鑽洞試驗隧道正式開始運作，所使用的車輛類似[[導向巴士|導向巴士]]。車隊中的[[特斯拉X型|特斯拉X型]]车最高時速為65公里。

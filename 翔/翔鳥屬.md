'''翔鳥屬'''（[[屬|屬]]名：{{lang|la|''Xiangornis''}}）為一種生活於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期的[[反鳥亞綱|反鳥類]]，化石發現於[[中國|中國]][[遼寧|遼寧]]的[[朝陽市|朝陽市]][[大平房鎮|大平房鎮]]，屬於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期的[[九佛堂組|九佛堂組]]地層<ref name = Hu>{{ cite journal | author = Hu, D.; Li, L.; Hou, L.; Xu, X. | title = "A New Enantiornithine Bird from the Lower Cretaceous of Western Liaoning, China" | journal = Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology | volume = 32 | issue = 3 | pages = 639–645 | year = 2012 | url = https://www.researchgate.net/publication/213771927_A_New_Enantiornithine_Bird_from_the_Lower_Cretaceous_of_Western_Liaoning_China }}</ref>。

== 物種 ==
* [[阿嘉翩弄蝶|阿嘉翩弄蝶]] ''Pyrrhopygopsis agaricon'' <small>Druce, 1908</small>
* ''Pyrrhopygopsis quispica'' <small>(Plötz, 1886)</small>
* [[羅穆翩弄蝶|羅穆翩弄蝶]] ''Pyrrhopygopsis romula'' <small>(Druce, 1875)</small>
* [[翩弄蝶|翩弄蝶]] ''Pyrrhopygopsis socrates'' <small>(Ménétries, 1855)</small>

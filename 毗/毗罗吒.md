'''毗罗吒'''（{{lang-sa|विराट}}，[[IAST|IAST]]：{{IAST|Virāṭa}}），印度古代史诗《[[摩诃婆罗多|摩诃婆罗多]]》人物。他是{{tsl|en|Matsya Kingdom|摩蹉国}}国王，娶{{tsl|en|Sudeshna|妙施}}为妻，两人育有四子，其中女儿{{tsl|en|Uttarā (Mahabharata)|至上公主}}嫁与阿周那之子{{tsl|en|Abhimanyu|激昂}}为妻，激昂之子{{tsl|en|Parikshit|继绝}}在[[俱卢之战|俱卢之战]]后封为国王<ref>{{cite book|last= Dowson|first= John|title=A Classical Dictionary of Hindu Mythology and Religion, Geography, History, and Literature|url=https://archive.org/stream/aclassicaldictio00dowsuoft#page/n45/mode/2up|year=1888|publisher=Trubner & Co., London|}}</ref>。在流放的第十三年，[[般度族|般度族]]五子中的长兄[[坚战|坚战]]接受[[阿周那|阿周那]]的建议，带领兄弟来到摩蹉国并在此乔装打扮住下一年<ref>{{cite book|last=Menon|first=[translated by] Ramesh|title=The Mahabharata : a modern rendering|volume=1|year=2006|publisher=iUniverse, Inc.|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=iOsat19ryloC|isbn=9780595401871|page=602}}</ref>。

俱卢之战前，般度族第五子[[偕天|偕天]]希望毗罗吒任主帅，但坚战提名{{tsl|en|Dhrishtadyumna|猛光}}<ref>{{cite book|last=Menon|first=[translated by] Ramesh|title=The Mahabharata : a modern rendering|volume=2|year=2006|publisher=iUniverse, Inc.|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=5GgCZqN_hAgC|isbn=9780595401888|page=87}}</ref>。战争期间，他代表般度族一方作战，最终被敌将[[德罗纳|德罗纳]]击败<ref>{{cite book|title=The Mahabharata of Krishna-Dwaipayana Vyasa|ISBN=9781465526373|url=https://books.google.com.ph/books?id=ygA2240G74kC}}</ref>。

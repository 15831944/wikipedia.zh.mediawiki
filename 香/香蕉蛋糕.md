'''香蕉蛋糕'''（{{lang-en|Banana cake}}）是一個以香蕉和典型成分為主的蛋糕。它可以以各種方式準備，如[[千層糕|千層糕]]、[[鬆餅|鬆餅]]和蛋糕等；在[[中国菜|中国菜]]、[[印尼菜|印尼菜]]和[[越南菜|越南菜]]中發現“蒸香蕉蛋糕”<ref name="Tropp 1982">{{cite book | last=Tropp | first=B. | title=The Modern Art of Chinese Cooking | publisher=Morrow | series=Cookbook Library | year=1982 | isbn=978-0-688-00566-5 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=y5ssAAAAYAAJ&q=steamed+%22Banana+cake%22 | page=518}}</ref><ref name="Long 2015">{{cite book | last=Long | first=L.M. | title=Ethnic American Food Today: A Cultural Encyclopedia | publisher=Rowman & Littlefield Publishers | series=Ethnic American Food Today | year=2015 | isbn=978-1-4422-2731-6 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=DBzYCQAAQBAJ&pg=PA294 | page=294}}</ref><ref name="Le 2014">{{cite book | last=Le | first=H. | title=Vietnamese Food with Helen's Recipes: | publisher=Createspace Independent Pub | year=2014 | isbn=978-1-5005-2971-0 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=1_AaBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA158 | page=158–161}}</ref>。

== 概述 ==
香蕉蛋糕以香蕉和典型的蛋糕成分為主，如[[麵粉|麵粉]]、[[糖霜_(餐飲)|糖霜]]、[[雞蛋|雞蛋]]、[[黄油|黄油]]、[[人造奶油|人造奶油]]、[[植物油|植物油]]和[[碳酸氢钠|小蘇打粉]]等<ref name="Greenspan Richardson 2006">{{cite book | last=Greenspan | first=D. | last2=Richardson | first2=A. | title=Baking: From My Home to Yours | publisher=Houghton Mifflin | year=2006 | isbn=978-0-618-44336-9 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=oCji-fn5qEUC&pg=PA204 | page=204}}</ref><ref name="Reisman 2002">{{cite book | last=Reisman | first=R. | title=The Complete Idiot's Guide to Light Desserts | publisher=Alpha Books | series=Complete Idiot's Guide to | year=2002 | Recipes | publisher=JustZen | year=2013 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Xw1zAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA18 | page=18}}</ref><ref name="Paré 2002">{{cite book | last=Paré | first=J. | title=The Rookie Cook | publisher=Company's Coming Publishing, Limited | series=Company's Coming | year=2002 | isbn=978-1-895455-92-2 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=HOv-Gzpl3vIC&pg=PA146 | page=146}}</ref>。香蕉可以使用食品加工機做成{{link-en|原漿|Purée}}或用[[攪拌機|攪拌機]]混合成麵糊或者切片香蕉裝飾<ref name="Greenspan Richardson 2006"/><ref name="Reisman 2002"/>，也能使用咖啡色或過度成熟的香蕉；味道可與巧克力、[[胡桃|核桃]]和[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]][[坚果|坚果]]組合<ref name="Reisman 2002"/><ref name="Moll 1993">{{cite journal | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ZwcAAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA22 | title=A 香蕉蛋糕可加入糖霜或{{link-en|釉面 (烹飪技術)|Glaze (cooking technique)}}製備，也可烘烤或使用[[蒸籠|蒸籠]]蒸煮；也可包含如[[波本威士忌|波本威士忌]]的酒精飲料<ref name="Reisman 2002"/><ref name="Mahunnop 2013"/><ref name="Brass Brass Ryan 2015"/>；可用於[[千層蛋糕|千層蛋糕]]準備，其[[面糊|面糊]]可以用來準備香蕉[[玛芬|玛芬]]和蛋糕<ref name="Reisman 2002"/><ref name="Paré 2002"/><ref name="Brass Brass Ryan 2015"/><ref name="Koch Legato 2012">{{cite book | last=Koch | first=M. | last2=Legato | first2=S. | title=Eat More of what You Love | publisher=Running Press | year=2012 | isbn=978-0-7624-4589-9 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=CpfD2ahsNkcC&pg=PA322 | page=322}}</ref>。

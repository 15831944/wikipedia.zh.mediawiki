'''香柑内酯'''（'''5-甲氧补骨脂素'''）是一种存在于[[香柠檬|香柠檬]][[精油|精油]]与其他[[柑橘属|柑橘属]]植物精油，<ref>{{cite journal | title = HPLC profile of citrus essential oils from different species and geographic origin | author = Calvarano I., Calvarano M., Gionfriddo F., Bovalo F., Postorino E. | journal = Essenze Derivati agrumari | volume = 65 | pages = 488–502 | year = 1995}}</ref> 以及[[葡萄柚汁|葡萄柚汁]]的[[呋喃香豆素_（furanocoumarin）|呋喃香豆素 （furanocoumarin）]]，属[[补骨脂素|补骨脂素]]的衍生物。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Sakamaki N., Nakazato M., Matsumoto H., Hagino K., Hirata K., Ushiyama H. | title = Contents of furanocoumarins in grapefruit juice and health foods | journal = Journal of the Food Hygienic Society of Japan | year = 2008 | volume = 49 | issue = 4 | pages = 326–331 | doi = 10.3358/shokueishi.49.326 | pmid = 18787320}}</ref> 它是香柠檬精油中引起[[光敏性|光敏性]]（光毒性）的活性物质。<ref>{{cite journal | title = Elimination of Furocoumarins in Bergamot Peel Oil | author = Francesco Gionfriddo, Enrico Postorino and Giuseppe Calabrò | journal = Perfumer & Flavorist | volume = 29 | year = 2004}}</ref> 目前市面上的[[香水|香水]]采用的是不含香柑内酯的香柠檬精油或人工合成的类似香味物质。

医学上香柑内酯与长波紫外线（UVA）一同用于[[银屑病|银屑病]]的[[長波紫外線光化治療|長波紫外線光化治療]]。<ref>{{cite journal | last=HONIGSMANN | first=HERBERT | last2=JASCHKE | first2=ERNA | last3=GSCHNAIT | first3=FRITZ | last4=BRENNER | first4=WILHELM | last5=FRITSCH | first5=PETER | last6=WOLFF | first6=KLAUS | title=5-Methoxypsoralen (Bergapten) in photochemotherapy of psoriasis | journal=British Journal of Dermatology | publisher=Wiley-Blackwell | volume=101 | issue=4 | year=1979 | issn=0007-0963 | doi=10.1111/j.1365-2133.1979.tb00014.x | pages=369–378}}</ref><ref>{{cite pmid|3279089}}</ref>

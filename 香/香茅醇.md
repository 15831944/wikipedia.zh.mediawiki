'''香茅醇'''（'''Citronellol'''）是一种天然有机化合物，存在于多种植物精油以及一些水果中，属于[[类萜|类萜]]。其两种[[对映异构体|对映异构体]]在自然界中均有分布：R-(+)-香茅醇, 存在于[[香茅|香茅]]油 (50%)；而S-(−)-香茅醇存在于[[蔷薇属|蔷薇属]]植物精油中(18–55%)，以及[[天竺葵属|天竺葵属]]植物等。<ref>{{cite book | author = Lawless, J. | title = The Illustrated Encyclopedia of Essential Oils | isbn = 1-85230-661-0}}</ref>

==制备==
香茅醇可由[[香叶醇|香叶醇]]或[[橙花醇|橙花醇]]部分氢化合成。<ref>{{cite book | author = Robert H. Morris | chapter = Ruthenium and Osmium | title = The Handbook of Homogeneous Hydrogenation | editor = J. G. de Vries and C. J. Elsevier | year = 2007 | publisher = WILEY-VCH | location = Weinheim | isbn = 978-3-527-31161-3}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1016/0957-4166(95)00015-H | title = Catalytic Synthesis of (R) and (S) citronellol by homogeneous hydrogenation over amidophosphinephosphinite and diaminodiphosphine rhodium complexes | year = 1995 | last1 = Ait Ali | first1 = M | last2 = Allaoud | first2 = S | last3 = Karim | first3 = A | last4 = Roucoux | first4 = A | last5 = Mortreux | first5 = A | journal = Tetrahedron: Asymmetry | volume = 6 | issue = 2 | pages = 369}}</ref>

==应用==
香茅醇用于香水和驱虫剂，<ref>{{cite journal | author = Taylor WG, Schreck CE. | title = Chiral-phase capillary gas chromatography and mosquito repellent activity of some oxazolidine derivatives of (+)- and (−)-citronellol | journal = J Pharm Sci. | year = 1985 | volume = 74 | issue = 5 | pages = 534–539 | doi = 10.1002/jps.2600740508 | pmid = 2862274}}</ref>农业害螨引诱剂。<ref name = usepa>{{cite web|url=https://www3.epa.gov/pesticides/chem_search/reg_actions/registration/fs_PC-167004_22-Apr-04.pdf|title=
Citronellol (167004) Fact Sheet|work=epa.gov|accessdate=12 May 2017}}</ref>香茅醇在短距离内是一种很好的驱蚊剂，但保护性能随距离增加大大降低。<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1016/j.actatropica.2013.03.011 | title = Reduction of mosquito biting-pressure: Spatial repellents or mosquito traps? A field comparison of seven commercially available products in Israel | year = 2013 | last1 = Revay | first1 = Edita E. | last2 = Kline | first2 = Daniel L. | last3 = Xue | first3 = Rui-De | last4 = Qualls | first4 = Whitney A. | last5 = Bernier | first5 = Ulrich R. | last6 = Kravchenko | first6 = Vasiliy D. | last7 = Ghattas | first7 = Nina | last8 = Pstygo | first8 = Irina | last9 = Müller | first9 = Günter C. | journal = Acta Tropica | volume = 127 | pages = 63–8 | pmid = 23545129 | issue = 1 }}</ref>与[[环糊精|环糊精]]结合能增强驱蚊效用，有效时间提升至1.5小时。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1007/s10847-011-9985-7 | title = Investigation of inclusion complexes of citronella oil, citronellal and citronellol with β-cyclodextrin for mosquito repellent | year = 2011 | last1 = Songkro | first1 = Sarunyoo | last2 = Hayook | first2 = Narissara | last3 = Jaisawang | first3 = Jittarat | last4 = Maneenuan | first4 = Duangkhae | last5 = Chuchome | first5 = Thitima | last6 = Kaewnopparat | first6 = Nattha | journal = Journal of Inclusion Phenomena and Macrocyclic Chemistry | volume = 72 | issue = 3–4 | pages = 339}}</ref>

香茅醇是生产{{en-link|玫瑰醚|rose oxide}}的原料。<ref>{{cite journal | url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/op900076g | title=“Dark” Singlet Oxygenation of β-Citronellol: A Key Step in the Manufacture of Rose Oxide | journal=Organic Process Research & Development}}</ref>

==安全性==
美国食品药品监督管理局(FDA)将香茅醇归入“一般认为安全”(GRAS)之列，<ref name = usepa/>在食物中仅限作为香料用途添加。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ifraorg.org/en-us/standards_restricted/s3/p3 |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2012-07-19 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120106222844/http://www.ifraorg.org/en-us/standards_restricted/s3/p3 |archivedate=2012-01-06 }}</ref>有报道称一些人群可能对香茅醇敏感，但其作为食品添加剂时的剂量是否足以使人过敏则尚不清楚。<ref>[http://www.leffingwell.com/Cropwatch%20Claims%20Victory%20Over%2026%20Allergens.pdf Cropwatch Report April 2008]</ref><ref>[http://www2.mst.dk/common/Udgivramme/Frame.asp?pg=http://www2.mst.dk/udgiv/publications/2006/87-7052-278-2/html/kap07_eng.htm Survey and health assessment of chemical substances in massage oils] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927205013/http://www2.mst.dk/common/Udgivramme/Frame.asp?pg=http%3A%2F%2Fwww2.mst.dk%2Fudgiv%2Fpublications%2F2006%2F87-7052-278-2%2Fhtml%2Fkap07_eng.htm |date=2007-09-27 }}</ref>

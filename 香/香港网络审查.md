'''香港網路審查'''指[[香港特別行政區政府|香港特別行政區政府]]對[[網際網路|網際網路]]進行的官方[[互联网审查|審查]]行為。根據[[香港人權法|香港人權法]]，香港居民擁有自由言論與結社的權利<ref name="HKBOR">[http://www.hkhrm.org.hk/english/law/eng_boro1.html Hong Kong Bill of Rights], 8 June 1991, Hong Kong Human Rights Monitor, accessed 30 June 2012</ref>，而且香港政府通常不會進行類似[[中華人民共和國網絡審查|中國大陸網路審查]]的政治審查活動，[[網絡服務供應商|網絡服務供應商]]亦沒有建立諸如防火牆的阻擋設施。法律許可香港政府進行的網路審查包括[[電腦犯罪|電腦犯罪]]、[[色情|色情]]與[[侵犯版權|侵犯版權]]的材料<ref>[http://www.legislation.gov.hk/eng/home.htm?SearchTerm=Criminal%20Jurisdiction%20Ordinance%20%28Amendment%20of%20Section%202%282%29%29%20Order%202002 "Section 161: Access to computer with criminal or dishonest intent"], Chapter 200 Crimes Ordinance, Bilingual Laws Information System, Department of Justice, Hong Kong, 1997, accessed 30 June 2012</ref><ref>[http://www.legislation.gov.hk/blis_pdf.nsf/6799165D2FEE3FA94825755E0033E532/A1A8E9EBA159FF2C482575EE007998AB?OpenDocument&bt=0 "Chapter 390: Control of Obscene and Indecent Articles Ordinance"], enacted in 1987, amended in 1997, 2000, 2003, 2012, Bilingual Laws Information System, Department of Justice, Hong Kong, accessed 30 June 2012.</ref><ref>[http://www.legco.gov.hk/yr10-11/english/bills/brief/b35_brf.pdf "Legislative Council Brief for the Copyright (Amendment) Bill 2011"], Commerce, Industry and Tourism Branch, Commerce and Economic Development Bureau, Hong Kong, 2 June 2011, accessed 30 June 2012</ref>。然而亦有社會活動人士指控香港政府出於政治動機而監控其網路活動<ref name="HK-CROHRP-2011">[http://www.state.gov/j/drl/rls/hrrpt/2011/eap/187483.htm "Hong Kong"], ''2011 Country Reports on Human Rights Practices'', Bureau of Democracy, Human rights, and Labor, U.S. Department of State, 24 May 2012</ref>。

== 網路審查事件 ==

=== 陳冠希事件 ===
{{See also|陳冠希裸照事件}}

2008年，陳冠希私人電腦中內容被竊，其中所含的香港知名藝人不雅照在網路上被廣為傳播，因此事[[香港警察|香港警察]]抓捕了十名被指控傳播色情材料的嫌疑人<ref>[http://www.thestandard.com.hk/news_detail.asp?pp_cat=12&art_id=61125&sid=17431562&con_type=3 "Eight now held in internet sex probe"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080205040310/http://www.thestandard.com.hk/news_detail.asp?pp_cat=12&art_id=61125&sid=17431562&con_type=3 |date=2008-02-05 }}, Damon Pang, Bonnie Chen and Diana Lee, ''The Standard'', 4 February 2008</ref>。承認最初竊取陳冠希電腦內容的電腦修理師以電腦犯罪之罪名被判刑八年半<ref>[http://www.smh.com.au/news/technology/articles/2009/05/13/1241894033965.html "Tech jailed for stealing sex-with-starlet photos"], ''Sydney Morning Herald'', 13 May 2009</ref>。此後，香港警方陸續會見超過200名香港網站負責人，要求其配合警方刪除陳冠希事件中洩漏的照片，又以此事件為由從香港本土網站處獲取了超過30名照片傳播者的[[IP位址|IP位址]]<ref>[http://www.shanghaidaily.com/editor/article.asp?id=545#4 "Alleged HK celebrity sex photos create a stir"], Lydia Chen, ''Shanghai Daily'', 31 January 2008</ref>。這一事件使得人權活動人士指責香港警方違反了基本的公民[[私隱權|私隱權]]與言論自由的保障，且其選擇性執法忽略了一般公民在網路上的權利<ref>[http://www.nytimes.com/2008/02/13/business/worldbusiness/13internet.html?_r=2&em&ex=1203051600&en=5e5d33c170b9b534&ei=5087%0A&oref=slogin "Internet Sex Video Case Stirs Free-Speech Issues in Hong Kong"], Keith Bradsher, ''New York Times'', 13 February 2008</ref>。

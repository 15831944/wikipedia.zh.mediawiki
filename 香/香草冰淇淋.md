[[香莢蘭|香草]]（Vanilla）是[[冰淇淋|冰淇淋]]常見的口味之一，在北美及歐洲更常見<ref name="Berger 2007">{{cite book|editor-last=Berger|editor-first=R.G. |title=Flavours and fragrances chemistry, bioprocessing and sustainability |year=2007|publisher=Springer |location=Berlin|isbn=3540493395|page=459|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ax1OvyH8jGoC&pg=PA459}}</ref>。香草冰淇淋作法和其他口味的冰淇淋類似，是由奶油、糖、香草混合後在含有冰及鹽的容器內冷卻而成<ref name="Smith 2007">{{cite book|last1=Smith|first1=Andrew|title=The Oxford Companion to American Food and Drink|date=2007|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=0195307968|page=314|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=AoWlCmNDA3QC&pg=PA314&dq=origins+of+vanilla+ice+cream&hl=en&sa=X&ei=Oe9SVPfOMpHgoASrlIDIAQ&ved=0CEQQ6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=origins%20of%20vanilla%20ice%20cream&f=false|accessdate=2014-11-12}}</ref>。香草冰淇淋使用的香草類類隨地區而不同。在北美及歐洲會喜歡有烟薰風味的，在[[爱尔兰岛|爱尔兰岛]]則是喜歡類似[[茴芹|茴芹]]味道的<ref name="Berger 2007" />。為了讓冰淇淋有一致的滑順口感，混合物在[[凝固|凝固]]過程需要偶爾攪拌，之後再放回冰鹽容器中繼續凝固<ref name="Smith 2007" />。依照《Iced: 180 Very Cool Concoctions》所述，許多人認為香草冰淇淋是冰淇淋的「{{link-en|預設效果|default effect|預設}}」或「一般」口味<ref>{{cite book |first1=Katri |last1=Hilden |first2=Tim|last2=Robinson|first4=Emma |last4=Hutchinson |last3=Currie|title=Iced: 180 Very Cool Concoctions|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=02YG2aTL89MC |accessdate=2013-04-04|year=2006|publisher=Murdoch Books |isbn=978-1-74045-818-4 |first3=Lee|page=15 |quote=''Vanilla has become a synonymous with 'plain'- perhaps most vanilla ice cream is flavoured with fake vanilla extract''}}</ref>。

'''任仕達控股'''（Randstad Holding NV），通常稱為任仕達，是荷蘭的一家跨國人力資源諮詢公司，總部位於[[迪門|迪門]]<ref>{{cite web |url = http://markets.ft.com/research/Markets/Tearsheets/Business-profile?s=RAND:AEX |title = Randstad Holdings, RAND:AEX company performance |work = Financial Times |accessdate = 26 Apr 2013}}</ref>。任仕達是世界僅次於[[德科集團|德科集團]]的第二大人力資源服務公司<ref>{{cite web |url = http://markets.ft.com/research/Markets/Tearsheets/Business-profile?s=RAND:AEX |title = Randstad Holdings, RAND:AEX company performance |work = Financial Times |accessdate = 26 Apr 2013}}</ref>。

==历史==
創建於1960年，現在在39個國家開展有業務<ref name="randstad1">{{cite web|url=http://www.randstad.com/about-us/worldwide-operations |title=Locations - Randstad | Randstad Holding |publisher=Randstad |date= |accessdate=2015-10-11}}</ref>。任仕達是[[阿姆斯特丹證券交易所|阿姆斯特丹證券交易所]]的上市公司之一。

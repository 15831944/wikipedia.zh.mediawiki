==生平==
任娜瑛出生在辽宁[[沈阳|沈阳]]，初中毕业于[[沈阳市第七中学|沈阳市第七中学]]，高中就读于[[沈阳市第二中学|沈阳市第二中学]]文科“宏志班”。毕业后考入[[清华大学|清华大学]]英语语言与文学专业，<ref name=":1" />在校期间她关注于[[性别研究|性别研究]]和[[酷儿理论|酷儿理论]]，并导致她成为LGBT组织，比如北京LGBT中心的志愿者，<ref name=":0" /> 2015年成为首批获得[[罗德奖学金|罗德奖学金]]的四名中国学生之一。<ref>[http://mt.sohu.com/20151209/n430423906.shtml 沈阳女孩任娜瑛获“全球最难申请的奖学金”]，搜狐新闻。</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://blogs.wsj.com/chinarealtime/2015/12/14/an-lgbt-activist-becomes-one-of-chinas-first-rhodes-scholars/|title=An LGBT Activist Becomes One of China’s First Rhodes Scholars|last=Chen|first=Te-Ping|website=WSJ|access-date=2016-06-16}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.globaltimes.cn/content/958305.shtml|title=The cream of the crop - Global Times|website=www.globaltimes.cn|access-date=2016-06-16}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk/news/rhodes-scholarships-awarded-in-china|title=Rhodes Scholarships awarded in China in historic launch|website=The Rhodes Scholarships|access-date=2016-06-16|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160821211745/http://www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk/news/rhodes-scholarships-awarded-in-china|archivedate=2016-08-21}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://spectrum.suntimes.com/news/10/155/8701/lgbt-activist-china-wins-rhodes-scholarship-ren-naying-oxford|title=LGBT advocate wins one of China's first Rhodes Scholarships|date=2015-12-14|website=Spectrum|language=en-US|access-date=2016-06-16|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160131224728/http://spectrum.suntimes.com/news/10/155/8701/lgbt-activist-china-wins-rhodes-scholarship-ren-naying-oxford|archivedate=2016-01-31}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.shanghaidaily.com/national/Rhodes-names-first-Chinese-scholars/shdaily.shtml|title=Rhodes names first Chinese scholars|website=www.shanghaidaily.com|access-date=2016-06-16}}</ref>

她是[[非政府组织|非政府组织]][[中国青年同伴网络|中国青年同伴网络]]（China LGBT+ Youth Network）的联合创始人，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.scmp.com/lifestyle/article/1918555/chinas-first-rhodes-scholars-journalist-lgbt-activist-and-lawyer-fighting|title=China’s first Rhodes Scholars: the journalist, LGBT activist and lawyer fighting for social justice|website=South China Morning Post|access-date=2016-06-16}}</ref> 该组织倡议提高 "大学社区建筑的LGBT权利"，<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk/rhodes-scholars-elect-class-of-2016/naying-ren|title=Naying Ren|website=The Rhodes Scholarships|access-date=2016-06-16|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160821223425/http://www.rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk/rhodes-scholars-elect-class-of-2016/naying-ren|archivedate=2016-08-21}}</ref>目前居住在[[北京|北京]]。<ref name=":1" />

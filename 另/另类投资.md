'''另类投资'''（{{lang-en|Alternative investment}}）是指在除[[股票|股票]]、[[债券|债券]]和[[现金|现金]]之外的投资。另类投资包含范围较为广泛，包括[[贵金属|贵金属]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/investor/2012/04/09/five-alternative-investments-to-protect-your-wealth-from-inflation/|title=Five Alternative Investments To Protect Your Wealth From Inflation|first=Intelligent|last=Investing|publisher=|accessdate=7 January 2017}}</ref>、艺术品<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Coslor|first=Erica|last2=Spaenjers|first2=Christophe|title=Organizational and epistemic change: The growth of the art investment field|url=https://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.aos.2016.09.003|journal=Accounting, Organizations and Society|volume=55|pages=48–62|doi=10.1016/j.aos.2016.09.003}}</ref>、古玩、硬币、邮票<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/finance/personalfinance/investing/3144943/First-class-returns-for-alternative-investments.html|title=First class returns for alternative investments|first=By John|last=Greenwood|publisher=|accessdate=7 January 2017}}</ref>等有形资产，和[[房地产|房地产]]、大宗[[商品|商品]]、[[私人股权投资|私人股权]]、不良债券等金融资产。

其中在房地产、森林和航运上的资产投资实际上是一种传统的保值增值手段，但仍被习惯性地称为“另类”投资。

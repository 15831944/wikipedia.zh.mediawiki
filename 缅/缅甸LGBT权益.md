'''女同性恋，男同性恋，双性恋和跨性别（[[LGBT|LGBT]]）权益'''在[[缅甸|缅甸]]受官方迫害和歧视，LGBT居民面临其他公民没有经历过的法律和社会挑战。同性性行为是[[犯罪|犯罪]]，国家刑法第377条将[[同性戀|同性戀]]性行为（无论双方是否自愿是否私下完成）的刑罚定为从10年监禁到终身监禁不等。跨性别者受到警察的骚扰和性侵犯，他们的性别变更不被国家承认。<ref name="nickerson">{{cite news |last1=Nickerson |first1=James |title=Myanmar's abused, intimidated LGBT people long for acceptance in new era |url=https://uk.reuters.com/article/uk-myanmar-gay/myanmars-abused-intimidated-lgbt-people-long-for-acceptance-in-new-era-idUKKBN13B00R |accessdate=2018-10-09 |publisher=Reuters UK |date=2016-11-16}}</ref>在[[国家和平与发展委员会|国家和平与发展委员会]]的长期军事独裁统治期间，很难获得关于[[LGBT|LGBT]][[缅甸|缅甸]]公民的法律或社会地位的准确信息。然而，随着正在进行的[[缅甸民主改革|政治改革]]，媒体和公民自由的改善使LGBT人群在该国获得了更多的关注和支持。<ref name="mizzima-2015">{{cite news |title=Gay subject of documentary warns of continuing rights violations in Myanmar |url=http://www.mizzima.com/lifestyle-news/gay-subject-documentary-warns-continuing-rights-violations-myanmar |accessdate=2018-10-09 |work=Mizzima |date=2015-06-18 |language=en}}</ref><ref name="McFetridge">{{cite news |last1=McFetridge |first1=Matthew |title=The Outlook for LGBT Rights in Myanmar |url=https://thediplomat.com/2014/09/the-outlook-for-lgbt-rights-in-myanmar/ |accessdate=2018-10-09 |work=The Diplomat |date=2014-09-05}}</ref><ref name="ferrie">{{cite news |last1=Ferrie |first1=Jared |title=LGBT festival opens in Myanmar after first public launch party |url=https://finance.yahoo.com/news/lgbt-festival-opens-myanmar-first-110526884.html |accessdate=2018-10-09 |work=Yahoo! Finance |agency=Reuters |date=2018-02-01}}</ref>尽管[[翁山蘇姬|翁山蘇姬]]的[[全國民主聯盟|全國民主聯盟]]在[[2015年緬甸議會選舉|2015年緬甸議會選舉]]的竞选胜利中承诺改善人权，但反LGBT法律并未发生变化。<ref name="nickerson" />

== 关于同性性行为的法律 ==
《刑法典》第377条禁止同性性行为和[[鸡奸|鸡奸]]。除了罚款外，规定的处罚是终身监禁十年，尽管法律并未严格执行。<ref name="MM-Times-2017">{{cite news |last1=Myint |first1=Lae Phyu Pyar Myo |last2=Htwe |first2=Nyein Ei Ei |title=Prejudice and progress: a snapshot of LGBT rights in Myanmar |url=https://www.mmtimes.com/lifestyle/26228-prejudice-and-progress-a-snapshot-of-lgbt-rights-in-myanmar.html |accessdate=2018-10-09 |work=The Myanmar Times |date=2017-06-01 |language=en}}</ref>2001年，流亡组织[[全缅学生民主阵线|全缅学生民主阵线]]称投票通过废除该法律。这被视为缅甸劳工权利委员会的一次胜利，尽管考虑到普遍存在的反对变革的政治气氛，这种改变被认为不太可能发生。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.glapn.org/sodomylaws/world/burma/burma.htm |title=Sodomylaws.Org |publisher=Sodomylaws.Org |date= |accessdate=2011-01-20}}</ref>根据“警察法”第35（c）条的规定，LGBT人群也成为“影子法”的目标，该法允许警察拘留他们认为在日落之后有可疑行为的任何人。<ref name="nickerson" /><ref name="McFetridge" /><ref name="MM-Times-2017" />

== 同性伴侣关系 ==
缅甸不承认[[同性婚姻|同性婚姻]]和[[民事結合|民事結合]]。<ref name="hawley-2014">{{cite news |last1=Hawley |first1=Samantha |title=Myanmar couple in 'first public gay wedding ceremony' |url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2014-03-04/an-myanmar-couple-in-27first-public-gay-wedding-ceremony27/5296432 |accessdate=2018-10-09 |work=ABC News |publisher=Australian Broadcasting Corporation |date=2014-03-04 |language=en-AU}}</ref>2014年，一对缅甸同性伴侣在共同生活了10年之后举行了一场非正式的婚礼，引起了媒体的广泛关注。<ref name="hawley-2014" /><ref name="McFetridge" />这也引发了社会保守派的强烈反对，他们质疑为什么反同性恋法律没有针对他们强制执行。<ref name="hiv-aids-2014">{{cite news |title=Burma's homosexuality law 'undermining HIV and Aids fight' |url=https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/2014/mar/21/burma-homosexuality-law-hiv-aids |accessdate=2018-10-09 |work=The Guardian |date=2014-03-21 |language=en}}</ref>

== 跨性别权益 ==
缅甸不允许人们改变出生时登记的性别。<ref name="tg-trans-2016">{{cite news |last1=England |first1=Charlotte |title=Myanmar's transgender people not just chasing rainbows in fight for equality |url=https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/2016/feb/02/myanmar-transgender-people-lgbt-colors-rainbow-equality |accessdate=2018-10-09 |work=The Guardian |date=2016-02-02 |language=en}}</ref>缅甸跨性别者常受到警察的强奸，虐待或勒索，<ref name="tg-trans-2016" />并且警察经常使用“警察法”第35（c）节中的“影子法”对跨性别者进行攻击。<ref name="MM-Times-2017" />一般来说，跨性别缅甸者只有三种“值得尊敬的”的职业可选择：美容师，时装设计师或nat kadaw（“精神妻子”或灵媒）。<ref name="baker2017">{{cite news |last1=Baker |first1=Nick |title=How Myanmar's Paranormal Spirit Wives Escape LGBTQ Persecution |url=https://www.vice.com/en_us/article/43axew/how-myanmars-paranormal-spirit-wives-escape-lgbtq-persecution |accessdate=2018-10-11 |work=Vice |date=2017-10-23 |language=en-us}}</ref> LGBT人士选择成为一个nat kadaw，可以得到尊重和崇拜，否则他们将被缅甸社会否认。<ref name="baker2017" />

== 生存现状 ==

===公众态度===
在军事政权期间，没有LGBT政治或社会组织存在。缅甸关于人类性行为的社会习俗被描述为“非常保守”。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.gng.org/currents/myanmar/mya_hiv.html |title=CURRENTS: HIV/AIDS in Myanmar | global nomads group |publisher=Gng.org |date=2005-07-02 |accessdate=2011-01-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060821223009/http://www.gng.org/currents/myanmar/mya_hiv.html |archivedate=2006-08-21  }}</ref>男同性恋者会受到侮辱，如果他们是艾滋病病毒携带者/艾滋病人，那程度就更加严重。<ref name="McFetridge" />在当地的佛教传统中，那些生来就是LGBT的人被视为因前世犯下的罪而受到惩罚。<ref name="gay-mandalay">{{cite news |title=It Ain't Great Being Gay in Mandalay |url=https://www.vice.com/da/article/qbmp3w/lgbt-in-burma |accessdate=2018-10-11 |work=Vice |date=2014-01-30 |language=da}}</ref><ref name="nyo-me-2018">{{cite news |last1=Me |first1=Nyo |title=Not very gay |url=https://www.mmtimes.com/news/not-very-gay.html |accessdate=2018-10-11 |work=The Myanmar Times |publisher=mspiral creative agency |date=2018-05-17 |language=en}}</ref>历史上，官方对同性恋的恐惧心理、有限的公众意识和缺乏社区角色榜样相结合，使得LGBT在缅甸社会中变得隐形。<ref name="mosbergen">{{cite news |last1=Mosbergen |first1=Dominique |title=Gay People In Myanmar Can't Live Openly. Here's Why |url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com.au/entry/lgbt-myanmar_us_561633eee4b0e66ad4c67fea |accessdate=2018-10-09 |work=Huffington Post |date=2015-10-18 |language=en-AU}}</ref>

===LGBT运动===
Aung Myo Min是一名公开的同性恋男子，是[[全缅学生民主阵线|全缅学生民主阵线]] (ABSDF)的成员。2015年，他谈到了自己的[[出柜|出柜]]过程和[[同性戀恐懼|同性戀恐懼]]的存在，即使是民主反对派也不例外。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mizzima.com/archives/news-in-2005/news-in-feb/20-February%2005-25.htm |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2007-10-12  |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20050305102116/http://www.mizzima.com/archives/news-in-2005/news-in-feb/20-February%2005-25.htm |archivedate= 2005-03-05 }}</ref>

===媒体===
尽管LGBT人群被定罪，但现在他们在缅甸变得可见，特别是在[[缅甸民主改革|政治改革]]之后。<ref>{{cite news|title=Gay people in Burma start to challenge culture of repression|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2012/may/13/gay-people-burma-challenge-repression|publisher=Guardian | location=London|first=Esmer|last=Golluoglu|date=2012-05-13}}</ref>男同性恋和女同性恋伴侣在[[仰光|仰光]]和[[曼德勒|曼德勒]]等主要城市自由同居，但他们在法律上不允许结婚。媒体自由度的提高也使记者能够报道同性恋社区。<ref>{{cite web|title=Woman raised as a man in Birma|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sNJkbevpZ4M|website=Youtube.com|accessdate=2013-04-20}}</ref>同性伴侣也可以在没有任何法律迫害的大城市举行礼仪婚礼。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://waiphyomyint.wordpress.com/2012/09/13/myanmar-gay-marriage/|title=Myanmar Gay Marriage|date=2012-09-13|website=Waiphyomyint.wordpress.com|accessdate=2018-01-16}}</ref>

2003年FocusAsia (Star TV) 播出了关于nat kadaws的故事。《柬埔寨、老挝、缅甸和越南乌托邦指南》提到“在缅甸的神圣节日中传递灵魂的[[跨性別|跨性別]]萨满。”<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.prweb.com/releases/2005/11/prweb304444.htm |title=A Revealing Glimpse at Gay Life in Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar and Vietnam |publisher=Prweb.com |date= |accessdate=2011-01-20}}</ref>  

2016年，{{tsl|en|The Gemini (2016 film)||''The Gemini''}}成为缅甸第一部LGBT电影。这部电影公开抨击缅甸同性恋法律。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/movies/la-et-mn-capsule-gemini-review-20160920-snap-story.html|title=Convoluted plot and tech issues undo gay romantic thriller 'The Gemini'|website=Latimes.com|accessdate=2018-01-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.irrawaddy.com/burma/burmese-director-explores-same-sex-relationships-in-new-film.html|title=Burmese Director Explores Same-Sex Relationships in New Film|date=2016-01-28|website=Irrawaddy.com|accessdate=2018-01-16}}</ref>.

===活动===
2012年，缅甸还在全国几个城市庆祝了它的第一个同性恋骄傲日，以纪念[[國際不再恐同日|國際不再恐同日]]。<ref>{{cite news|title=A pride with no parade for Burma's first gay festival|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-asia-18106018|publisher=BBC | date=2012-05-17}}</ref>2018年，官员们批准了一个公众骄傲活动。近6000人参加了此次活动，比之前的时间有所增加。<ref>https://www.gaystarnews.com/article/thousands-turn-celebrate-myanmars-first-legal-pride-party/#gs.fWX5Hrg</ref>第二天这个数字进一步上升到10,000。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dvb.no/news/lgbt-festival-opens-burma-first-public-launch-party/79549|title=LGBT festival opens in Burma after first public launch party- DVB Multimedia Group|date=2018-02-01|publisher=}}</ref>

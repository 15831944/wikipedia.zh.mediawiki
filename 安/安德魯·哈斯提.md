'''安德魯·哈斯提'''（Andrew Hastie，{{bd|1982年|9月30日}}）是一位[[澳洲|澳洲]]政治人物，他的黨籍是[[澳洲自由黨|澳洲自由黨]]。自2015年開始，他是[[坎寧_(澳大利亞國會選區)|坎寧]]選區選出的[[澳大利亚众议院|澳大利亚众议院]]的議員。他曾在澳洲軍隊工作<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2015-09-19/andrew-hastie-wins-canning-by-election/6789334 | publisher=ABC News| title=Canning by-election: Liberal candidate Andrew Hastie wins by-election | date=19 September 2015 | accessdate=20 September 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=https://au.news.yahoo.com/thewest/wa/a/29575981/live-blog-andrew-hastie-claims-canning-in-byelection/ | publisher=The West Australian| title=Andrew Hastie claims Canning in by election | date=19 September 2015 | accessdate=20 September 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.smh.com.au/federal-politics/political-news/canning-byelection-liberal-candidate-andrew-hastie-set-for-win-over-matt-keogh-20150919-gjqjv1.html/ | publisher=Sydney Morning Herald| title=Canning byelection: Liberal candidate Andrew Hastie wins as Matt Keogh concedes | date=20 September 2015 | accessdate=20 September 2015}}</ref>。

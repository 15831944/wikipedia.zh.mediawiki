Randall |title=The Good, the Bad, and the Mad: Some Weird People in American History |year=1999 |publisher=Barnes & Noble Publishing, Inc. |location=New York |isbn=978-0-7607-6600-2 |page=18}}</ref>|1914年|<ref name="online-literature.com">[http://www.online-literature.com/bierce/]</ref>
|catIdx=Bierce}}）是一位[[美国|美国]][[记者|记者]]、[[短篇小说|短篇小说]]、[[传说|传说]]与讽刺小说[[作家|作家]]。著名作品有短篇小说《[[鹰溪桥上|鹰溪桥上]]》（{{lang|en|An Occurrence at Owl Creek Bridge}}）和讽刺小说《[[魔鬼辞典|魔鬼辞典]]》（{{lang|en|The Devil's Dictionary}}）等。

==名言==
{{cquote|爱国主义：名词，一堆随时可以被任何野心家所点燃，去照亮他的名字的易燃垃圾。在[[塞缪尔·约翰逊|约翰逊博士]]著名的字典中，爱国主义被定义为无赖最后的避难所。带着对这位开明但有缺陷的词典编纂家应有的敬意，我斗胆认为爱国主义应该是无赖的第一个避难所。（Patriotism, n. Combustible rubbish ready to the torch of any one ambitious to illuminate his name. In Dr. Johnson's famous dictionary patriotism is defined as the last resort of a scoundrel. With all due respect to an enlightened but inferior lexicographer I beg to submit it is the first.）|安布罗斯·格溫莱特·比尔斯，1911|《魔鬼辞典》}}

{{Vgname2|安迪·钱伯斯|en=Andy Chambers}}是英国[[作家|作家]]和游戏设计师，因为在曾[[Games_Workshop|Games Workshop]]工作而知名。曾参与制作[[战锤40000|战锤40000]]的规则手册和原始资料，并为该游戏撰写了复式科幻小说。<ref>Williams, Ian [http://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2015/04/warhammer-this-is-the-way-the-world-ends.html Warhammer: This is the Way the World Ends] ''Paste Magazine''. July 25, 3025</ref><ref>Contreras, Paulmichael [http://www.playstationlifestyle.net/2011/07/29/orks-and-humans-clash-in-the-latest-warhammer-40k-space-marine-trailer/ Orks and Humans Clash in the Latest Warhammer 40K: Space Marine Trailer] ''PlayStation Lifestyle''. July 25, 2015</ref>

2003年，安迪·钱伯斯加入Mongoose Publishing并成为星河战队桌面游戏的首席设计师。<ref name="Mongoose1">{{cite web | title =Andy Chambers Joins Starship Troopers! | work =Mongoose Publishing - News | publisher =Mongoose Publishing | date =2003 | url =http://www.mongoosepublishing.com/news/news_item.php?pkid_news=35 | accessdate =2007-02-23 | archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20070322034128/http://www.mongoosepublishing.com/news/news_item.php?pkid_news=35 | archivedate =2007年3月22日 | deadurl =yes }}</ref>

2012年5月14日，参与的由梦幻飞行游戏发布的《Dust Warfare》的制作。<ref name="FFG Announces Dust Warfare">{{cite web | title =Fantasy Flight Games announces Dust Warfare| work =Fantasy Flight Games| publisher =Fantasy Flight Games | date =2011-07-28| url =http://www.fantasyflightgames.com/edge_news.asp?eidn=2512}}</ref><ref name="FFG Release">{{cite web | title =Fantasy Flight Games announces release of Dust Warfare| work =Fantasy Flight Games| publisher =Fantasy Flight Games | date =2012-05-14| url =http://www.fantasyflightgames.com/edge_news.asp?eidn=3282}}</ref>

目前安迪·钱伯斯成为[[暴雪娱乐|暴雪娱乐]]的首席作家，并参与《[[星际争霸II：自由之翼|星际争霸II：自由之翼]]》的工作。<ref name="Blizz">{{cite web | title =Blizzard Outlines Starcraft II Gameplay | work =Gamespot | publisher =CNet Networks Entertainment | date =2007-05-19 | url =http://au.gamespot.com/pc/strategy/starcraft2/news.html?sid=6171178&mode=all | accessdate =2007-05-29 | deadurl =yes | archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20070929091632/http://au.gamespot.com/pc/strategy/starcraft2/news.html?sid=6171178&mode=all | archivedate =2007-09-29 }}</ref>

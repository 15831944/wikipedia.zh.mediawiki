'''安·E·邓伍迪'''（Ann Elizabeth Dunwoody，{{bd|1953年|1月14日||}}）<ref>http://www.publicbackgroundchecks.com/SearchResponse.aspx?view=NM&fn=ann&mn=elizabeth&ln=dunwoody&city=&state=&zip=&dob=19530114&age=</ref><ref>http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com/~battle/celeb/dunwoody.htm</ref> 是一位前[[美国陆军|美国陆军]]女性四星上將，于2012年8月7日正式退役<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.amc.army.mil/amc/news.html#releases |title=U.S. Army Materiel Command |deadurl=yes |accessdate=August 17, 2012 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140606205512/http://www.amc.army.mil/amc/news.html#releases |archivedate=2014年6月6日 }}</ref>。2008年6月17日至2008年11月13日担任美国陆军装备司令部副司令官。2008年7月23日升任司令<ref name=SenateConfirmation>{{cite web|url=http://www.senate.gov/pagelayout/legislative/one_item_and_teasers/noms_confn.htm |title=Nominations Confirmed (Non-Civilian)|date=October 2, 2008 |publisher=United States Senate}}</ref>。2008年11月14日，她成为首位美国女性四星上将<ref name="four-star">{{cite news | last = Tyson | first = Ann Scott | title = Army Promotes Its First Female Four-Star General | work = Washington Post | date = November 15, 2008 | url = http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2008/11/14/AR2008111400259.html?hpid=sec-nation | accessdate =November 15, 2008}} Prior to Dunwoody's promotion to general (O-10), the highest-ranking woman in the history of the uniformed services was [[Patricia_Ann_Tracey|Patricia Ann Tracey]], who, as a [[vice_admiral_(United_States)|vice admiral]] in the [[United_States_Navy|United States Navy]], wore three stars and retired in 2004; [[United_States_Marine_Corps|United States Marine Corps]] [[lieutenant_general_(United_States)|lieutenant general]] [[Carol_Mutter|Carol Mutter]] received promotion in the same year (1996) as Tracey but retired earlier (in 1999).</ref>。

==教育==
* 1975年，获得[[紐約州立大學|紐約州立大學]]Cortland分校 物理教育学士
* 1976年，美国陆军军需部隊（Quartermaster Corps）军官基础课程与基础空降学院（Basic Course and Basic Airborne School）
* Quartermaster Officers Advanced Course
*[[美国陆军指挥参谋学院|美国陆军指挥参谋学院]]（Command and General Staff College）
* 1988年，获得[[佛罗里达理工学院|佛罗里达理工学院]]后勤管理研究生学位
* 1995年，获得[[美國三軍工業學院|美國三軍工業學院]]（Industrial College of the Armed Forces）国家资源战略（National Resource Strategy）研究生学位
* United States Army Jumpmaster Course graduate.

===其他荣誉===
* 1998 Recipient of the Military Distinguished Order of Saint Martin (Army Quartermaster Corps).
* 2001 Distinguished Alumna for Cortland State SUNY.
* 2002 Inducted as a Distinguished Member of the Quartermaster Regiment.
* 2004 Recipient of the National Defense Transportation Association’s DoD Distinguished Service Award.
* 2007 Recipient of Military Order of the World Wars (MOWW) Distinguished Service Award.
* 2008 First female four-star general in the United States Armed Services.
* 2012 Inducted into the Quartermaster Hall of Fame
* 2012 Recipient Ancient Order of Saint Martin (Army Quartermaster Corps)

'''安哥拉护照'''是[[安哥拉共和国|安哥拉共和国]]签发给起公民的国际旅行证件，由[[罗安达|罗安达]]的移民和外国事务署签发<ref> {{cite web|url=http://www.unhcr.org/refworld/country,,IRBC,,AGO,456d621e2,3f7d4d4e31,0.html |title=Angola: Procedure for acquiring a passport; documents required; whether an agent may receive the passport on behalf of the applicant; processing time; whether a person under the age of 16 can obtain a passport in their own name and if so, whether they require parental or guardian consent; Canadian missions that issue visitor's visas to applicants from Angola |accessdate=2009-10-28 |date=2003-03-06 |publisher=[[联合国难民署|联合国难民署]] Refworld |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121009135153/http://www.unhcr.org/refworld/country%2C%2CIRBC%2C%2CAGO%2C456d621e2%2C3f7d4d4e31%2C0.html |archivedate=2012-10-09 }}</ref><ref> {{cite web|url=http://www.angolaconsulate-tx.org/us/consulate/consular-activity/travel-documents.html |title=TRAVEL DOCUMENTS |accessdate=2009-10-28 |publisher=Republic of Angola Consulate General in Houston |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090831080311/http://www.angolaconsulate-tx.org/us/consulate/consular-activity/travel-documents.html |archivedate=2009-08-31 }}</ref>。
截止2017年1月1日，安哥拉公民可持护照免签或落地签进入45个国家或地区，排名第90位<ref>{{cite web|title=Global Ranking - Visa Restriction Index 2017|url=https://henleyglobal.com/files/download/hvri/HP_Visa_Restrictions_Index_170301.pdf|publisher=Henley & Partners|accessdate=14 March 2017}}</ref>。

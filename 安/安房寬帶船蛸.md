'''安房寬帶船蛸'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Obinautilus awaensis''）為一種[[滅絕|滅絕種]][[章魚|章魚]]。本種於1983年發表描述，基於自[[上新世|上新世]][[Senhata組|Senhata組]][[日本|日本]][[房總半島|房總半島]]出土的化石材料上。<ref>Tomida, S. (1983). Two new fossil ''Argonauta'' and firstly discovered ''Aturia coxi'' Miller from the late Tertiary of Boso Peninsula, Japan. ''Muzunami-shi Fossil Museum Bulletin'' '''10''': 107-116.</ref>

本[[分類單元|分類單元]]的原初描述劃為[[船蛸屬|船蛸屬]]的種類之一，然而此後就移動到[[寬帶船蛸屬|寬帶船蛸屬]]。<ref>Martill, D.M. & M.J. Barker (2006). [http://www.blackwell-synergy.com/doi/abs/10.1111/j.1475-4983.2006.00578.x?journalCode=pala A paper nautilus (Octopoda, ''Argonauta'') from the Miocene Pakhna Formation of Cyprus.] ''Palaeontology'' '''49''' (5): 1035-1041.</ref>

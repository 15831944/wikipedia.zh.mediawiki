'''安德魯·巴爾'''（{{lang-en|Andrew Barr}}；{{bd|1973年}}）是一位[[澳洲|澳洲]]政治家，所屬於[[澳洲工黨|澳洲工黨]]。他是現任[[澳洲首都領地|澳洲首都領地]]首席部長.<ref name="abcnews_chief_minister">{{cite news|url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2014-12-11/andrew-barr-elected-act-chief-minister/5959984|title=Andrew Barr elected ACT Chief Minister, seventh in history|work=[[ABC_News_(Australia)|ABC News]]|location=Australia|date=11 December 2014|accessdate=11 December 2014}}</ref>。安德魯·巴爾在2014年出櫃，是澳洲自2006年以來首個出櫃的國會議員<ref name="abcnews_chief_minister"/><ref>{{cite news|title=Andrew pops the question|first=Jorian|last=Gardner|newspaper=CityNews|edition=Canberra|volume=15|number=40|page=3|date=15–21 October 2009|url=|quotation=''Openly gay Minister Andrew Barr will enter into a civil union with partner Anthony Toms next month, just as the ACT Legislative assembly attempts another run at putting forward same-sex partnerships legislation similar to that recently passed in Tasmania.''|accessdate=}}</ref>。

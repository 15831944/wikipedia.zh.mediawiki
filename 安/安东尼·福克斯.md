'''安东尼·福克斯'''（{{lang-en|Anthony Renard Foxx}}，{{bd|1971年|4月30日||}}）是一位非洲裔美国政治人物，律师。

==生平==
1971年出生在美国[[夏洛特_(北卡罗来纳州)|夏洛特 (北卡罗来纳州)]]，1996年获得[[纽约大学法学院|纽约大学法学院]]法律学位。毕业后在多家律所工作过，之后担任位于[[辛辛那提|辛辛那提]][[美国联邦第六巡回上诉法院|美国联邦第六巡回上诉法院]]法官[[纳撒尼尔·R·琼斯|纳撒尼尔·R·琼斯]]专员，为[[美国司法部|美国司法部]]和[[美国众议院|美国众议院]]工作过。2009年到2013年担任[[夏洛特_(北卡罗来纳州)|夏洛特 (北卡罗来纳州)]]市长。<ref name=elected>{{cite news |title=Foxx elected Charlotte's mayor |author=Morrill, Jim |author2=Lyttle, Steve |date=2009-11-03 |accessdate=2009-11-03 |url=http://www.charlotteobserver.com/breaking/story/1035240.html |publisher=[[Charlotte_Observer|Charlotte Observer]]}} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://charlotte.bizjournals.com/charlotte/stories/2009/11/09/story4.html |title=Pat McCrory: Seven terms and not (quite) done yet |publisher=[[Charlotte_Business_Journal|Charlotte Business Journal]] |author=Spanberg, Erik |date=2009-11-06 |accessdate=2009-11-11}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Charlotte Mayor-Elect Anthony Foxx Sits Down For Exclusive Interview With Channel 9 |url=http://www.wsoctv.com/politics/21528474/detail.html |date=2009-11-05 |accessdate=2009-11-09 |publisher=[[WSOC-TV|WSOC-TV]] |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110726114949/http://www.wsoctv.com/politics/21528474/detail.html |archivedate=2011-07-26 |df= }}</ref>成为该市最年轻市长。<ref>[http://results.enr.clarityelections.com/NC/Mecklenburg/11010/18880/en/summary.html State Board of Elections - Nov. 3 Mecklenburg election results]</ref>以及第二个[[非洲裔美国人|非洲裔美国人]]市长。<ref>[http://www.bizjournals.com/charlotte/blog/going_green/2013/04/charlotte-mayor-foxx-family-wont-run.html "Charlotte Mayor Anthony Foxx cites family in announcement he won’t run for third term" (Charlotte Business Journal article)]</ref>2013年到2017年担任[[美国运输部长|美国运输部长]]。<ref>{{cite news |first=Peter |last=Baker |title=Charlotte Mayor Is Chosen as Transportation Chief |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2013/04/30/us/charlotte-mayor-is-nominated-for-transportation-secretary.html |location = New York City |work=The York Times |date=29 April 2013 }}</ref><ref>[http://purl.fdlp.gov/GPO/gpo44667 Nomination of Mayor Anthony Foxx to be Secretary of the U.S. Department of Transportation: Hearing before the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation, United States Senate, One Hundred Thirteenth Congress, First Session, May 22, 2013.]</ref>

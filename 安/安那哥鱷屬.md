'''安那哥鱷屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Energosuchus''）是[[勞氏鱷科|勞氏鱷科]]的一屬，生存於[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]晚期的[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]。

化石發現於[[烏拉爾山脈|烏拉爾山脈]]北部的Karyomayol組的上層、Synya組的下層<ref>Novikov, I. V. (1994). Continental Triassic biostratigraphy of the Timan–North Urals region using tetrapod fauna. ''Trudy Paleontologicheskogo Instituta'' '''261''':1–139.</ref><ref>Tverdokhlebov, V. P., Tverdokhlebova, G. I., Surkov, M. V. and Benton, M. J. (2003). Tetrapod localities from the Triassic of the SE of European Russia. ''Earth-Science Reviews'' '''60''':1–66.</ref>，以及[[巴什科爾托斯坦|巴什科爾托斯坦]]的Bukobay組地層<ref>Gower, D. J. and Sennikov, A. G. (2000). Early archosaurs from Russia. In M. J. Benton, E. N. Kurochkin, M. A. Shishkin, D. M. Unwin (eds.), ''The Age of Dinosaurs in Russia and Mongolia''. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge. pp. 140-159</ref>。這些地層的年代都是三疊紀中期的[[拉丁尼階|拉丁尼階]]。

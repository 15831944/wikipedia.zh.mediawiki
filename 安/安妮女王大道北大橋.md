'''安妮女王大道北大橋'''（{{lang-en|North Queen Anne Drive Bridge}}）是一座跨越[[西雅圖|西雅图]]狼溪的[[拱桥|拱桥]]，橋長73米（238英尺），连接{{tsl|en|Queen Anne, Seattle|安妮女王區}}與承載[[99号华盛顿州州道|99号华盛顿州州道]]的[[喬治·華盛頓紀念大橋|喬治·華盛頓紀念大橋]]。大橋的前身是一座木頭橋。大橋橋拱异常地高，但只使用了少量的支撐物；基於大橋独特的建築风格，大橋在1981年12月28日被指定為西雅圖地標。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.historylink.org/index.cfm?DisplayPage=output.cfm&file_id=3209|title=Seattle Landmarks: Queen Anne Drive Bridge (1936)|accessdate=April 4, 2011|date=April 17, 2001|last=Wilma|first=David|work=HistoryLink.org Online Encyclopedia of Washington State History}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=National Trust Guide, Seattle: America's Guide for Architecture and History Travelers|last=Crowley|first=Walt|last2=Dorpat|first2=Paul|publisher=Preservation Press, J. Wiley & Sons|year=1998|isbn=978-0-471-18044-9|page=251}}</ref>

在{{tsl|en|2001 Nisqually earthquake|2001年尼斯阔利地震}}，大橋的伸縮縫遭受破裂和剥落<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Nisqually, Washington, Earthquake of February 28, 2001: Lifeline Performance|last=McDonough|first=Peter W.|publisher=American Society of Civil Engineers|year=2002|isbn=978-0-7844-0615-1|pages=76}}</ref>。大桥已经被改装以使其更能防震<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.seattlepi.com/default/article/Steel-truss-bridges-get-emergency-look-1245626.php|title=Steel-truss bridges get emergency look|last=Lange|first=Larry|date=August 2, 2007|newspaper=Seattle Post-Intelligencer|accessdate=April 4, 2011}}</ref>。

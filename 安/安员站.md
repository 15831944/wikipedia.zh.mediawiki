'''安员站'''（{{vie|qn=Ga Yên Viên|hn=Ga安員|f=y}}）是[[越南|越南]]的一座[[火车站|火车站]]<ref>[http://www.vr.com.vn/ban-do-duong-sat-viet-nam.html?page=9 越南铁路站名一览] [2018-05-25].</ref>，位于[[河内市|河内市]][[嘉林县|嘉林县]]，距河内站11公里<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://giotaugiave.vr.com.vn/giotau/diaphuong.aspx?option=2&tuyen=3 |title=Giờ tàu tuyến Hà Nội - Đồng Đăng |access-date=2018-05-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180524222151/http://giotaugiave.vr.com.vn/giotau/diaphuong.aspx?option=2&tuyen=3 |archive-date=2018-05-24 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。铺设有[[标准轨|标准轨]]及[[米轨|米轨]]共行的[[混合轨|混合轨]]<ref>[http://www.vr.com.vn/hientaihoatdong.html Hạ tầng cơ sở Đường sắt]  {{Wayback|url=http://www.vr.com.vn/hientaihoatdong.html |date=20111229063244 }}</ref>。每天设有DD5/DD6次列车通往河内及同登。站南1千米处即[[安员大桥|安员大桥]]。

'''安托瓦内特·德容'''（{{lang-nl|Antoinette de Jong}}，{{IPA-nl|ɑntʋaːˈnɛtə də jɔŋ}}，{{bd|1995年|4月6日||}}）生于弗里斯兰省德弗里斯科马伦罗特姆，是一名荷兰女子速度滑冰运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.schaatsen.nl/nieuws/archief/2013/1/profiel-het-plan-van-antoinette-de-jong/|website=schaatsen.nl|date=2013-01-08|accessdate=2019-01-29|script-title=nl:Profiel: Het plan van Antoinette de Jong}}</ref>。安托瓦内特在六岁时开始练习滑冰<ref name="pyeongchang2018">{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/speed-skating/athlete-profile-n3036034-antoinette-de-jong.htm|title=Speed Skating {{!}} Athlete Profile: Antoinette DE JONG|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-29|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180421163050/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/speed-skating/athlete-profile-n3036034-antoinette-de-jong.htm|archivedate=2018-04-21}}</ref>。她在童年时期也有练习马术，还在全国比赛上获得过冠军<ref name="pyeongchang2018"/>。然而随着年龄的增长，她意识到自己不可能身兼两个项目，于是她在15岁时决定放弃马术<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.isu.org/speed-skating/speed-skating-news/interviews/11878-antoinette-de-jong-horses-or-blades?templateParam=15|title=Antoinette de Jong: horses or blades?|work=ISU|date=2018-01-17|accessdate=2019-01-29|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180228030603/https://www.isu.org/speed-skating/speed-skating-news/interviews/11878-antoinette-de-jong-horses-or-blades?templateParam=15|archivedate=2018-02-28}}</ref>。安托瓦内特的妹妹Michelle同样也是速度滑冰选手<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.schaatsen.nl/nieuws/2018/maart/dilemma-s-met-michelle-de-jong-ben-af-en-toe-best-een-gekkie/|script-title=nl:Dilemma's met Michelle de Jong: 'Ben af en toe best een gekkie'|work=schaatsen.nl|date=2018-03-22|accessdate=2019-01-29|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20190129133432/https://www.schaatsen.nl/nieuws/2018/maart/dilemma-s-met-michelle-de-jong-ben-af-en-toe-best-een-gekkie/|archivedate=2019-01-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

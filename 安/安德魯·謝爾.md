'''安德魯·謝爾'''（{{lang|en|Andrew Scheer}}，{{bd|1979年|5月20日}}），[[加拿大|加拿大]]政治人物，現任[[加拿大保守黨|聯邦保守黨]]黨魁和[[加拿大官方反對黨領袖|官方反對黨領袖]]，2004年起擔任[[加拿大眾議院|眾議院]]利載拿—卡佩爾選區（{{lang|en|Regina—Qu'Appelle}}）議員。他於2011年-15年間擔任[[加拿大國會下議院議長|眾議院議長]]<ref name=Andrew-Scheer>{{cite web|url=http://www.parl.gc.ca/Parliamentarians/en/members/Andrew-Scheer(25454)|title=Profile|publisher=Parliament of Canada|date=July 17, 2015}}</ref><ref name="Speaker of the House of Commons">{{cite web|url=http://www.parl.gc.ca/About/House/Speaker/bio-e.html|title=Speaker of the House of Commons|publisher=Parliament of Canada|date=July 17, 2015}}</ref>，就任時年僅32歲，為眾議院史上最年輕的議長<ref name="CBC-youngest-speaker">{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/story/2011/06/02/pol-speaker-election.html|title=MPs elect youngest Speaker|last=Fitzpatrick|first=Meagan|publisher=CBC News|date=June 2, 2011|accessdate=May 27, 2017}}</ref>。他於2016年9月宣布角逐聯邦保守黨黨魁<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/andrew-scheer-conservative-leadership-race-1.3782244|title=Ex-Commons Speaker Scheer declares candidacy for Conservative leadership|accessdate=September 28, 2016}}</ref>，並於2017年5月舉行的黨魁選舉中勝出<ref name="cbcleadwinner">{{cite news|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/conservative-leadership-race-winner-1.4134575|title=Andrew Scheer elected new Conservative leader|last=Harris|first=Kathleen|publisher=CBC News|date=May 27, 2017|accessdate=May 27, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.intvoting.com/cpcresults/index.html?lang=en|title=Results - CPC Leadership|website=Intvoting.com|publisher=Dominion Voting|date=May 27, 2017|accessdate=May 27, 2017}}{{dead link|date=2018年5月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。

==早年及個人生活==
謝爾生於[[安大略省|安大略省]][[渥太華|渥太華]]，母親瑪莉為一名護士，父親-{zh-hans:詹姆斯;zh-hk:占士;zh-tw:詹姆斯;}-則為一名圖書管理員和校對員，並為[[天主教渥太華總教區|天主教渥太華總教區]]的一名[[執事|執事]] <ref>{{cite web|url=http://bcc.rcav.org/canadian/796-deborah-gyapong|title=The B.C. Catholic Paper - New Speaker of the House has never hidden his faith|author=Brent Mattson|work=rcav.org|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141106021827/http://bcc.rcav.org/canadian/796-deborah-gyapong|archivedate=2014-11-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.ca/2017/05/25/andrew-scheer-tory-leadership-race-2017_n_16807094.html|title=Andrew Scheer, 'Consensus Candidate,' Hopes The Nice Guy Finishes First In Tory Leadership|last=Raj|first=Althia|publisher=The Huffington Post|date=May 25, 2017|accessdate=May 28, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.legacy.com/obituaries/ottawacitizen/obituary.aspx?pid=184451234|title=Mary Scheer Obituary|publisher=Ottawa Citizen|website=Legacy.com|date=March 11, 2017|accessdate=May 28, 2017}}</ref>。謝爾中學畢業後到[[渥太華大學|渥太華大學]]修讀歷史和政治學，期間曾於[[加拿大國會下議院議長|眾議院議長辦公室]]見習<ref name="About">{{cite web|url=http://www.andrewmp.ca/p/about.html|title=About Andrew Scheer, MP (Regina-Qu'Appelle)|accessdate=2017-07-07|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170625001046/http://www.andrewmp.ca/p/about.html|archivedate=2017-06-25}}</ref><ref name="RLP170529">{{cite news|url=http://leaderpost.com/news/politics/reginas-andrew-scheer-waiter-insurance-broker-and-now-federal-opposition-leader|title=Regina's Andrew Scheer: waiter, insurance broker, and now federal Opposition Leader|last=Pacholik|first=Barb|publisher=Regina Leader-Post|date=2017-05-29|accessdate=2017-07-07}}</ref>。他結識了來自[[薩克其萬省|薩克其萬省]][[利載拿|利載拿]]的吉爾·賴恩（{{lang|en|Jill Ryan}}）後於2002年遷至該市，於[[里贾纳大學|里贾纳大學]]繼續學業並取得文學士學位。他一度在保險公司工作<ref name="About"/>，其後出任眾議院利載拿—林士頓—湖泊中心選區（{{lang|en|Regina—Lumsden—Lake Centre}}）議員拉利·史賓沙（{{lang|en|Larry Spencer}}）的選區辦公室秘書<ref name="RLP170529"/>。

他於2003年與吉爾結婚<ref name="RLP170529"/>，兩人共育有五名子女<ref name="About"/>。

==政治生涯==
謝爾於[[2004年加拿大聯邦大選|2004年加拿大聯邦大選]]首度代表[[加拿大保守黨|保守黨]]出戰利載拿—卡佩爾選區，並以861票之差壓倒參求連任的[[加拿大新民主黨|新民主黨]]候選人羅恩·奈斯特倫（{{lang|en|Lorne Nystrom}}）<ref>[http://www.elections.ca/scripts/OVR2004/default.html Elections Canada, Official Voting Results], June 28, 2004.</ref>，首次晉身[[加拿大眾議院|眾議院]]。兩人於2006年聯邦大選再度交鋒，謝爾以2740之差再次獲勝<ref>[http://www.elections.ca/scripts/OVR2006/default.html Elections Canada, Official Voting Results], January 23, 2006.</ref>。謝爾並於該屆眾議院獲任為全體委員會助理副主席<ref name="Parlinfo">{{cite web|url=https://lop.parl.ca/ParlInfo/Files/Parliamentarian.aspx?Item=6519cfba-0080-419d-baa9-8ae6e56d032f&Language=E&Section=ALL|title=SCHEER, Andrew, B.A.|publisher=加拿大國會|accessdate=2017-07-08}}</ref>。

他於[[2008年加拿大聯邦大選|2008年聯邦大選]]順利連任，其後獲任為眾議院副議長和全體委員會主席<ref>[http://www2.parl.gc.ca/HousePublications/Publication.aspx?Language=E&Mode=1&Parl=40&Ses=1&DocId=3611885 Journal of the House of Commons of Canada], November 21, 2008.</ref>。

保守黨於[[2011年加拿大聯邦大選|2011年聯邦大選]]贏取多數政府地位，而連任眾議員的謝爾則被視為[[加拿大國會下議院議長|眾議院議長]]熱門人選<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/politics/ottawa-notebook/health-care-talks-with-provinces-should-top-harpers-list-poll-finds/article2044202/|title=Health Care Talks with Provinces Should Top Harper’s List, Poll Finds|quote=Others on that list [of candidates for Speaker] – Saskatchewan Tory MP and perceived frontrunner Andrew Scheer [...]|work=The Globe and Mail|date=June 2, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nationalpost.com/news/aims+second+female+speaker+history/4820527/story.html|title=NDP MP Aims to Be Second Female Speaker in History|work=National Post|date=May 21, 2011|quote=It's widely speculated, however, that a Conservative is going to get the position, and Andrew Scheer, who has served as Assistant Deputy Speaker and Deputy Speaker for more than five years, is considered the frontrunner.}}{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。同年6月2日，謝爾經過六輪投票後當選眾議院議長，成為眾議院歷來最年輕的議長<ref name=CBC-youngest-speaker/>以及首位代表沙省選區的議長<ref name="RLP170529"/>。

加拿大政府於2014年3月因[[2014年克里米亞危機|克里米亞併入俄羅斯聯邦]]而向俄國實施制裁和禁足令，俄羅斯政府遂於同月宣佈禁止包括謝爾在内的13名加拿大人入境<ref name="CBC140324">{{cite news |url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/russian-sanctions-against-canadians-a-badge-of-honour-1.2584137 |title=Russian sanctions against Canadians a 'badge of honour' |first=Susana |last=Mas |date=2014-03-24 |publisher=CBC News |accessdate=2016-03-08 }}</ref><ref name="明報140325">{{cite news |url=http://www.mingpaocanada.com/Tor/htm/News/20140325/tdd1.htm?m=0 |title=俄羅斯反擊 禁13加國政客入境 |publisher=《[[明報|明報]]》加東版 |date=2014-03-25 |accessdate=2016-03-08 }}</ref>。

謝爾於[[2015年加拿大聯邦大選|2015年聯邦大選]]連任眾議員，但保守黨則於該屆大選落敗<ref>[http://www.elections.ca/scripts/OVR2004/default.html  Elections Canada, Official Voting Results], October 19, 2015.</ref>，黨魁[[史蒂芬·哈珀|哈珀]]遂宣布辭職。謝爾隨後獲保守黨署理黨魁[[羅娜·安布羅斯|羅娜·安布羅斯]]委任為眾議院反對黨領袖<ref name="Parlinfo"/>，但於2016年9月13日辭去該職，以便他準備競逐保守黨黨魁職務<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/conservative-leadership-tuesday-1.3759864|title=With MacKay out, Scheer steps down as House leader to explore Tory leadership run|last=McGregor|first=Janyce|publisher=CBC News|date=September 13, 2016|accessdate=September 22, 2016}}</ref>。他於同月28日正式宣布角逐黨魁<ref name=":0" />，並表示他已獲保守黨黨團中的32人支持<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.nationalpost.com/news/canada/canadian-politics/andrew-scheer-announces-support-of-20-members-of-conservative-caucus-as-he-makes-leadership-bid-official|title=Andrew Scheer announces support of 20 members of Conservative caucus as he makes leadership bid official|last=Fekete|first=Jason|work=National Post|date=September 28, 2016|accessdate=September 28, 2016}}</ref>。在2017年5月27日舉行的保守黨黨魁選舉，謝爾和卞聶爾（{{lang|fr|Maxime Bernier}}）在第13輪投票交鋒，最終謝爾以50.95%得票當選黨魁<ref name="cbcleadwinner"/><ref name="星島170528">{{cite news |url=http://toronto.singtao.ca/1777127/2017-05-28/post-%E9%81%B8%E5%89%8D%E5%A4%A7%E7%86%B1%E5%8D%9E%E8%81%B6%E7%88%BE%E9%80%A3%E8%B4%8F12%E8%BC%AA%E5%B0%BE%E8%BC%AA%E5%BE%AE%E8%BC%B8-38%E6%AD%B2%E3%80%8C%E7%BF%BB%E7%89%88%E5%93%88%E7%8F%80%E3%80%8D/?variant=zh-hk |title=選前大熱卞聶爾連贏12輪尾輪微輸 38歲「翻版哈珀」 爆冷當選保守黨魁 |publisher=《[[星島日報|星島日報]]》加東版 |date=2017-05-28 |accessdate=2017-07-08 }}</ref>。

==政治立場==
謝爾在社會議題上傾向保守，因此被視為藍色托里派（{{lang|en|Blue Tory}}）<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ottawasun.com/2017/05/21/as-conservative-leadership-race-wraps-the-partys-in-good-hands|title=As Conservative leadership race wraps, the party's in good hands|last=Krayden|first=David|publisher=Ottawa Sun|date=May 21, 2017|accessdate=May 28, 2017}}</ref>。然而，為了拓闊其受眾層面，他於競逐保守黨黨魁期間低調處理該些社會議題；他強烈主張的立場包括取消[[碳稅|碳排放徵稅]]和加重打擊罪案<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ctvnews.ca/politics/andrew-scheer-s-conservative-leadership-plans-moving-beyond-debate-club-1.3233739|title=Andrew Scheer's Conservative leadership plans: moving beyond 'debate club'|last=Payton|first=Laura|publisher=CTV News|date=January 10, 2017|accessdate=May 27, 2017}}</ref>。

*'''墮胎'''：謝爾反對墮胎<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.campaignlifecoalition.com/index.php?id=259&p=Federal_Voting_Records|title=MP Andrew Scheer|author=Campaign Life Coalition|quote=Scheer has an impeccable voting record on life & family issues during his long career as a federal MP.|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170528134819/http://www.campaignlifecoalition.com/index.php?id=259&p=Federal_Voting_Records|archivedate=May 2, 2017}}</ref>，但表示他尊重保守黨目前有關墮胎的政綱<ref name=macleans>{{cite web|url=http://www.macleans.ca/politics/ottawa/andrew-scheers-path-to-leadership-of-the-conservative-party/|title=Andrew Scheer's path to leadership of the Conservative Party - Macleans.ca|date=27 May 2017|publisher=}}</ref>。該政綱為：「保守黨政府不會支持任何規管墮胎的法規」{{noteTag|name=abortion|1=原文為："A Conservative government will not support any legislation to regulate abortion"}}，但該黨容許黨員在表決相關議題時自由投票<ref name=macleans/>。

*'''家庭事務'''：謝爾承諾新生嬰兒的家長領取由就業保險金系統所提供的生育和育兒津貼時將豁免徵收聯邦入息税，並提高該些家長收取津貼時可從職場領取的入息上限<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/scheer-maternity-paternity-benefits-tax-free-1.3970637/|title=Andrew Scheer proposes tax-free maternity and parental benefits|last=McGregor|first=Janyce|publisher=CBC News|date=February 7, 2017|accessdate=May 27, 2017}}</ref>。

*'''財政'''：謝爾承諾豁免徵收家居暖氣的[[加拿大銷售稅|聯邦貨勞稅或統一銷售稅]]、減低向企業徵稅、以及向私校和家校學童的家長提供稅務優惠<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.torontosun.com/2017/05/30/five-things-you-should-know-about-andrew-scheers-policy-positions|title='Five things to know about Andrew Scheer's policy positions|author= Andy Blatchford and Mia Rabson|publisher=THE CANADIAN PRESS|date=May 30, 2017}}</ref>。

*'''言論自由'''：謝爾承諾取消向「不在校園内培育言論自由文化」的大專院校提供聯邦政府撥款<ref name="Global170527"/>。

*'''大麻合法化'''：謝爾並不支持大麻合法化，但亦表示[[加拿大自由黨|自由黨]]政府通過大麻合法化議案後，他不會重開此項議題<ref>{{cite news|url=http://globalnews.ca/news/3377312/i-think-i-have-the-best-chance-of-winning-says-tory-leadership-hopeful-andrew-scheer/|title='I think I have the best chance of winning' says Tory leadership hopeful Andrew Scheer|last=Scotti|first=Monique|publisher=Global News|date=April 16, 2017|accessdate=May 27, 2017}}</ref>。

*'''安樂死'''：謝爾於2016年在眾議院表決C-14號法案時投反對票；該法案容許精神上有能力且處於持續和不能忍受的痛苦中的成年人，在死亡是“合理預見”的情況下由醫學從業員協助自殺<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/canada-physician-assisted-death-law-1.3535193|title=Doctor-assisted dying bill restricted to adults facing 'foreseeable' death|publisher=CBC News|archivedate=May 30, 2017|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170530013742/http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/canada-physician-assisted-death-law-1.3535193}}</ref><ref name="WhereStand"/>。

*'''難民'''：謝爾傾向優先協助最弱勢的難民，如在中東因從伊斯蘭教改信基督教而面臨逼害的教徒<ref name="Global170527">{{cite web|url=http://globalnews.ca/news/3483406/who-is-andrew-scheer/|title=Who is Andrew Scheer?|first=Monique|last=Scotti|publisher=Global News|date=2017-05-27|accessdate=2017-07-09}}</ref>。

*'''同性婚姻'''：謝爾於2004年聯邦大選競選期間表明反對[[加拿大同性婚姻|同性婚姻]]<ref name="Interim">{{cite news|url=http://www.theinterim.com/issues/marriage-family/saskatchewan-tory-mp-andrew-scheer-values-family/|title=Saskatchewan Tory MP Andrew Scheer values family|last=Tuns|first=Paul|publisher=The Interim|date=October 23, 2009|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170528135837/http://www.theinterim.com/issues/marriage-family/saskatchewan-tory-mp-andrew-scheer-values-family/|archivedate=May 28, 2017}}</ref>；而眾議院於2005年通過同性婚姻後，他於2006年投票支持一項有關重開婚姻定義議題的動議<ref name="WhereStand">{{cite news|url=http://www.ctvnews.ca/politics/where-14-conservative-leadership-candidates-stand-on-social-issues-1.3267451|title=Where 14 Conservative leadership candidates stand on social issues|last=Dehaas|first=Josh|publisher=CTV News|date=February 1, 2017|accessdate=May 28, 2017}}</ref>。謝爾後來表示他不會就此議題重啓辯論<ref name=macleans />。

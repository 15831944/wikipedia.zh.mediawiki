'''安娜罗萨·莱里'''（{{lang-it|'''Annarosa Leri'''}}），[[意大利|意大利]][[干细胞|干细胞]]学者，[[哈佛大学|哈佛大学]]副教授。她和[[皮耶罗·安韦萨|皮耶罗·安韦萨]]教授在[[波士顿|波士顿]]的[[布莱根妇女医院|布莱根妇女医院]]从事医学研究。<ref name="Science:Targets of misconduct probe launch a legal counterattack">{{cite web|url=http://science.sciencemag.org/content/347/6217/13|title=Targets of misconduct probe launch a legal counterattack|accessdate=26 April 2017|date=2 January 2015|publisher=Science|last1=Servick|first1=Kelly}}</ref>她和安韦萨等人于2003年提出使用[[心脏干细胞|心脏干细胞]]修复受损[[心肌细胞|心肌细胞]]，但后来被证实是一场彻头彻尾的学术骗局。<ref name="New Scientist: Heart may be home to its own stem cells">{{cite web|url=https://www.newscientist.com/article/dn9239-heart-may-be-home-to-its-own-stem-cells.html#.VUsnaiGqqko|title=Heart may be home to its own stem cells|accessdate=7 May 2015|date=29 May 2006|publisher=New Scientist|last1=Gosline|first1=Anna}}</ref>

'''安锡湖'''（{{lang-fr|Lac d'Annecy}}）法国[[上萨瓦省|上萨瓦省]]近阿尔卑斯山一个淡水湖<ref name=sediment>Jean-Daniel Stanley and Thomas F. Jorstad, ''Direct Sediment Dispersal from Mountain to Shore, with Bypassing via Three Human-Modified Channel Systems to Lake Annecy, SE France'' (2004) Vol 20 (4) Journal of Coastal Research pp 958 - 969 [https://www.jstor.org/stable/4299357 JStor].</ref>{{rp|958}}，是法国第三大湖泊。因1960年起执行严格的环保条例，法国人称安锡湖为“欧洲最干净的湖泊”<ref>[http://no.france.fr/en/discover/lake-annecy-3 Lake Annecy - the purest lake in Europe] 法国旅游局</ref>。

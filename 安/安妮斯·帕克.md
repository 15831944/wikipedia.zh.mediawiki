'''安妮斯·达内特·帕克'''（{{lang-en|'''Annise Danette Parker'''}}，{{bd|1956年|5月17日|catIdx=P}}）是一位[[美国|美国]]政治人物，2010年到2016年担任[[休斯敦|休斯敦]]市长。<ref>{{cite web|title=Mayor's Office|url=http://www.houstontx.gov/mayor/index.html|publisher=City of Houston, TX.|accessdate=2011-12-15}}</ref>

帕克是休斯敦的第二位女性市长，是美国的主要城市中第一个公开的[[同性恋|同性恋]]市长。<ref>{{cite news| url=http://online.wsj.com/article/SB126057851102188215.html | work=The Wall Street Journal | title=Houston Election May Prove Historic | first=Miguel | last=Bustillo | date=2009-12-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Olson |first=Bradley |title=Annise Parker elected Houston's next mayor |work=Houston Chronicle |date=2009-12-13 |url=http://www.chron.com/disp/story.mpl/metropolitan/6767658.html |accessdate=2009-12-13 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091217014228/http://www.chron.com/disp/story.mpl/metropolitan/6767658.html |archivedate=十二月 17, 2009 |deadurl=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Houston Is Largest City to Elect Openly Gay Mayor |work=New York Times |date=2009-12-12 |author=James C. McKinley Jr |accessdate=2009-12-13 |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2009/12/13/us/politics/13houston.html |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091214121725/http://www.nytimes.com/2009/12/13/us/politics/13houston.html |archivedate=十二月 14, 2009 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

== 个人生活和教育 ==
帕克出生于休斯顿，她的母亲是一个簿记员，她的父亲曾在[[红十字会|红十字会]]工作。 1971年，帕克15岁时，她们一家随美军搬到[[德国|德国]][[曼海姆|曼海姆]]两年。在德国时，她主动在红十字青年服务组织工作。<ref name="About Annise">{{cite web

帕克开始在[[萊斯大學|萊斯大學]]学习，1974年获得奖学金。她做好几份工作以支付房租。<ref name="About Annise"/>1978年毕业，她获得人类学，心理学和社会学学士学位。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.media.rice.edu/media/NewsBot.asp?MODE=VIEW&ID=13478|title=Rice alumna Annise Parker elected Houston mayor|last=Brotzen|first=Franz|date=2009-12-13|work=Rice University|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100106072306/http://www.media.rice.edu/media/NewsBot.asp?MODE=VIEW&ID=13478|archivedate=2010年1月6日}}</ref>

成为一个民选官员之前，帕克曾经作为一个软件分析师在石油和天然气行业工作逾20年<ref name="Annise Parker, Houston's Gay Mayor">{{cite news|url=http://www.time.com/time/nation/article/0,8599,1947648,00.html |work=Time |title=Annise Parker, Houston's Gay Mayor |date=2009-12-15 |accessdate=2010-04-25 |first=Randy |last=James |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100327053854/http://www.time.com/time/nation/article/0%2C8599%2C1947648%2C00.html |archivedate=三月 27, 2010 |deadurl=no }}</ref>，其中18年在莫斯巴赫尔能源（Mosbacher Energy）工作。

帕克和她的伴侣Kathy Hubbard自1990年以来一直生活在一起。<ref name="About Annise" />2014年1月16日，帕克和Hubbard在加州[[棕榈泉_(加利福尼亚州)|棕榈泉]]结婚<ref name="HousChronMorris01172014">{{cite news |last=Morris |first=Mike |date=2014-01-16 |title=Mayor Parker marries longtime partner |url=http://www.chron.com/news/houston-texas/houston/article/Mayor-Parker-marries-longtime-partner-5150872.php |newspaper=[[Houston_Chronicle|Houston Chronicle]] |location=[[Houston,_Texas|Houston, Texas]] |publisher=[[Hearst_Corporation|Hearst Newspapers LLC]] |accessdate=2014-01-17}}</ref><ref name="TurnerHousChron12272013">{{cite news |last=Turner |first=Allan |date=2013-12-27 |title=Source: Parker, partner plan January wedding |url=http://www.chron.com/default/article/Source-Parker-partner-plan-January-wedding-5096719.php |newspaper=[[Houston_Chronicle|Houston Chronicle]] |location=[[Houston,_Texas|Houston, Texas]] |publisher=[[Hearst_Corporation|Hearst Corporation]] |accessdate=2013-12-28 |quote=Mayor Annise Parker and her partner of 23 years, Houston tax preparer Kathy Hubbard, are planning to marry, a source close to the couple said Friday.}}</ref>。她们有两个养女（Daniela Parker和Marquitta Parker）和一个养子（Jovan Tyler）。<ref name="HousChronMorris01172014"/><ref name="TurnerHousChron12272013"/><ref name="About Annise" /><ref name="houstontx.gov">{{cite web | title = Office of the City Controller | accessdate =2009-03-09 | url = http://www.houstontx.gov/controllercurrent.html}} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}}</ref>

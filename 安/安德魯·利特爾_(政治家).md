'''安德魯·利特爾'''（Andrew James Little，{{bd|1965年|5月7日}}）是紐西蘭的一位政治人物。自2014年11月18日開始到2017年8月1日，他擔任[[紐西蘭勞動黨|紐西蘭勞動黨]]的黨魁。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.parliament.nz/en-NZ/MPP/MPs/MPs/3/7/6/50MP175491-Little-Andrew.htm | title=Andrew Little | publisher=[[New_Zealand_Parliament|New Zealand Parliament]] | date=6 March 2013 | accessdate=15 February 2015 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130306085725/http://www.parliament.nz/en-NZ/MPP/MPs/MPs/3/7/6/50MP175491-Little-Andrew.htm | archivedate=2013年3月6日 | deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title = Audrey Young: Little smashed it - literally|url = http://www.nzherald.co.nz/opinion/news/article.cfm?c_id=466&objectid=11542022|newspaper = New Zealand Herald|date = 2015-11-08|access-date = 2015-11-08|issn = 1170-0777|language = |last = Young|first = Audrey}}</ref>。他在大學期間主修法律<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.students.org.nz/history | title=New Zealand Union of Students' Associations - General History | publisher=NZUSA | accessdate=15 February 2015}}</ref>。

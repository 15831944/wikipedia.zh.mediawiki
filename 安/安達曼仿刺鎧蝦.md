'''安達曼仿刺鎧蝦'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Munidopsis andamanica'''''}}）是棲息在深海的一種[[鎧甲蝦|鎧甲蝦]]<ref name=MacGilchrist1905>{{Cite journal en|author=MacGilchrist, A.C.|year=1905|title=Natural history notes from the R.I.M.S. “Investigator,” Capt. T.H. Heming, R.N. (retired), commanding. - Series III, No. 6. An account of the new and some of the rarer decapod Crustacea obtained during the surveying seasons 1901-1904|journal=Ann. Mag. Nat. Hist., ser. 7|volume=15|pages=233-268}}</ref>，舊屬[[鎧甲蝦科|鎧甲蝦科]]，今屬[[仿刺鎧蝦科|仿刺鎧蝦科]]。

==食性==
安達曼仿刺鎧蝦只吃深海中的木材，包括樹木、[[葉子|葉子]]及[[椰子|椰子]]等，甚至是沉在深海的船舶。<ref name=Hoyoux2009>{{Cite journal en|author=Caroline Hoyoux, Magali Zbinden, Sarah Samadi, Françoise Gaill and Philippe Compère|year=2009|title=Wood-based diet and gut microflora of a galatheid crab associated with Pacific deep-sea wood falls|journal=Marine Biology|volume=156|issue=12|pages=2421-39|doi=10.1007/s00227-009-1266-2|url=http://www.springerlink.com/content/454434m34555807n/}}</ref>牠們的[[牙齒|牙齒]]及食道都有吃木的適應性：牙齒有特別的鋸齒可以撕開木材；食道內有[[細菌|細菌]]及[[真菌|真菌]][[消化|消化]]木材碎片。<ref name=Hoyoux2009/>

'''芒种''' ({{lang-en|Grain in Ear}}, {{lang-ko|망종}})是一部2005年上映的电影，由中国朝鲜族导演[[张律|张律]]执导。<ref>{{cite web|title=Grain in Ear (2006)|url=http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/films/index/filmsView.jsp?movieCd=20060062|website=Korean Film Biz Zone|accessdate=20 May 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Hartzell|first1=Adam|title=Grain in Ear|url=http://koreanfilm.org/kfilm06.html#graininear|website=Koreanfilm.org|accessdate=20 May 2015}}</ref>片名取自中国传统[[二十四节气|二十四节气]]之一。<ref>{{cite web|title=The Twenty-four Solar Terms |url=http://www.xinghui.com/english/other/Twenty-four.htm |website=Beijing Star-Light Translation Center |accessdate=20 May 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150305020632/http://www.xinghui.com/english/other/Twenty-four.htm |archivedate=5 March 2015 |df= }}</ref>影片拍摄于[[北京|北京]]近郊，距离市中心40多分钟车程的一个小型工业乡镇。<ref name=Yu>{{cite web|last1=Yu|first1=Gu|title=Grain in Ear|url=http://www.schemamag.ca/viff2005/2005/10/grain_in_ear.html|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120223215032/http://www.schemamag.ca/viff2005/2005/10/grain_in_ear.html|website=Schema Magazine|date=11 October 2005|archivedate=23 February 2012}}</ref>

==剧情==
崔顺姬是一位朝鲜族单身母亲，她远离故土靠卖[[朝鲜泡菜|朝鲜泡菜]]维持生计。她生命中曾经出现三个背叛过她的男人，当她儿子车祸不幸去世，她决定实施报复。

==荣誉==
导演[[张律|张律]]凭借此片斩获第10届[[釜山国际电影节|釜山国际电影节]][[新浪潮奖|新浪潮奖]]。

'''暮蟬屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{sname|Tanna}}），也稱'''螗蝉属'''，隸屬[[蟬|蟬]]科，遍佈於[[臺灣|臺灣]]（中、低海拔）<ref name="陳振祥2007-p129"/>和[[東南亞|東南亞]]等地<ref>{{lang|en|Metcalf, Z.P. 1963. General catalogue of the Homoptera. Fascicle VIII. Cicadoidea. Part 1. Cicadidae. Section I Tibiceninae: i-vii, 1-585. – North Carolina State College, Raleigh, North Carolina.}}{{en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|author=Lee, Young June, and Hayashi, Masami|year=2004|title=Taxonomic review of Cicadidae (Hemiptera, Auchenorrhyncha) from Taiwan, part 3. Dundubiini (two other genera of Cicadina), Moganiini, and Huechysini with a new genus and two new species|journal=Journal of Asia-Pacific Entomology|volume=7|issue=1|pages=45–72|doi=10.1016/S1226-8615(08)60200-9}}</ref>，中型蟬，公蟲多於傍晚、黎明時分及陰天時鳴叫，因而稱為「暮蟬」。<ref name="陳振祥2007-p129"/>

'''行星际运输系统''' ('''Interplanetary Transport System；ITS''')<ref name=ars20160918/>，之前被称作'''火星殖民运输器''' ('''Mars Colonial Transporter；MCT''')，是[[SpaceX|SpaceX]]的私人出资研发计划，以设计和建造一个用于火星殖民的太空飞行系统为目的。项目包括可重复使用[[运载火箭|运载火箭]]和[[太空飞船|太空飞船]]、地球上用于快速火箭发射与重新发射的基础设施、[[近地轨道|近地轨道]]推进剂加注系统、地外科技，最终用来实现[[火星殖民|火星殖民]]。这项技术也被设计成有能力支持以太阳系中其他地方为目的地的[[太空探索|太空探索任务]]，包括[[木星|木星]]和[[土星|土星]]的卫星<ref name="NYT-20160927">{{cite news |last=Chang|first=Kenneth |title=Elon Musk’s Plan: Get Humans to Mars, and Beyond |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2016/09/28/science/elon-musk-spacex-mars-exploration.html |date=27 September 2016 |work=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |accessdate=27 September 2016 }}</ref>。

SpaceX在2014年之前就开始了他们[[猛禽火箭发动机|猛禽火箭发动机]]的研发，到了2016年9月，SpaceX发布的遠程计划中<ref>{{cite news |url=http://abcnews.go.com/Technology/elon-musk-expected-detail-spacexs-mars-ambitions/story?id=42361304 |title=Elon Musk Expected to Detail SpaceX's Mars Ambitions |work=[[ABC_News|ABC News]] |date=2016-09-27 |accessdate=2016-09-27 |quote=''[Musk is] hoping for the first mission to Mars to take place around 2025.'' }}</ref>，将先使用[[猎鹰重型运载火箭|猎鹰重型运载火箭]]发射两艘基于[[龙飞船|龙飞船]]的火星探测器，为今后星際探索任务作准备<ref name=gw20160128>
{{cite news |last=Boyle|first=Alan |url=http://www.geekwire.com/2016/spacexs-elon-musk-wants-to-go-into-space-by-2021-and-start-sending-people-to-mars-by-2025/ |title=SpaceX’s Elon Musk wants to go into space by 2021 and start Mars missions by 2025 |work=GeekWire |date=2016-01-27 |accessdate=2016-01-29 }}</ref>；按照計畫，首次前往火星的ITS货运飞行不早于2022年，2024年將进行第一次载人ITS飞行<ref name=wapo20160613/> 。

==历史==
早在2007年，伊隆·马斯克就表示他个人的目标就是最终能让人类得以探索并殖民[[火星|火星]]<ref name=wired20070522>{{cite news|last=Hoffman|first=Carl|title=Elon Musk Is Betting His Fortune on a Mission Beyond Earth's Orbit|url=http://www.wired.com/science/space/magazine/15-06/ff_space_musk?currentPage=all| accessdate=2014-03-14| newspaper=Wired Magazine| date=2007-05-22}}</ref><ref name=mw20110422>{{cite news| title=Elon Musk: I'll Put a Man on Mars in 10 Years| url=http://www.marketwatch.com/video/asset/elon-musk-ill-put-a-man-on-mars-in-10-years-2011-04-22/CCF1FC62-BB0D-4561-938C-DF0DEFAD15BA#!CCF1FC62-BB0D-4561-938C-DF0DEFAD15BA| newspaper=Market Watch| date=2011-04-22| publisher=The Wall Street Journal| location=New York| accessdate=2011-12-01| archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/63bvaGo3E?url=http://www.marketwatch.com/video/asset/elon-musk-ill-put-a-man-on-mars-in-10-years-2011-04-22/CCF1FC62-BB0D-4561-938C-DF0DEFAD15BA#!CCF1FC62-BB0D-4561-938C-DF0DEFAD15BA| deadurl=no| archivedate=2011-12-01}}</ref>

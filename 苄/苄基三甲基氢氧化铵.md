'''苄基三甲基氢氧化铵'''又称'''Triton B'''，是一种[[季铵盐|季铵碱]]。它常以[[水|水]]溶液或[[甲醇|甲醇]]溶液的形式存在，颜色为无色至浅黄色。<ref>Mary Ellen Bos "Benzyltrimethylammonium Hydroxide" in Encyclopedia of Reagents for Organic Synthesis, 2001 John Wiley & Sons.  {{DOI|10.1002/047084289X.rb079}}</ref>由于水解产生[[三甲胺|三甲胺]]，市售品会有特征的鱼腥味。

==应用==
苄基三甲基氢氧化铵可以用作[[相转移催化剂|相转移催化剂]]。<ref name=Ullmann>Marc Halpern "Phase-Transfer Catalysis" in Ullmann's Encyclopedia of Industrial Chemistry 2002, Wiley-VCH, Weinheim. {{DOI|10.1002/14356007.a19_293}}</ref>

它可用于[[羟醛缩合|羟醛缩合]]反应和碱催化[[失水反应|失水反应]]。它也可在[[Horner–Wadsworth–Emmons反应|Horner–Wadsworth–Emmons反应]]的Ando's Z-selective变体用作碱。<ref>Chaturvedi, D., & Ray, S. (2006). Triton b catalyzed, efficient, one-pot synthesis of carbamate esters from alcoholic tosylates. Monatshefte fuer Chemie, 137. Retrieved from http://www.springerlink.com/content/m48703268m3qw323/fulltext.pdf {{doi|10.1007/s00706-005-0452-2}}</ref>

'''弓箭手悖论'''（Archer's paradox），是指射箭时，尽管箭刚搭上弦时指向目标的侧面，看起来好像会射偏。但射出的箭还是会沿着拉满弓时箭头指示的方向前进，进而命中目标。箭射出时箭身的弯曲可以解释这一悖论。

弓箭手悖论一词最早由E.J. Rendtroff在1913年提出<ref>{{cite journal|title=The Toxophilist’s Paradox|author=|date=8 February 1913|journal=[[Forest_and_Stream|Forest and Stream]]}}</ref>，当时的理解的原因是箭被射出时箭身弯曲偏离弓（第一次被Clarence Hickman拍摄到）<ref>{{cite journal|title=Exterior and Interior Ballistics of Bows and Arrows - Review|last=Rheingans|first=W. R.|date=March–April 1936|journal=[[Archery_Review|Archery Review]]|pages=236 ff}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=Spine and Arrow Design|first1=W. R.|last2=Nagler|first2=F.|date=June–August 1937|journal=[[American_Bowman_Review|American Bowman Review]]|pages=226–232|last1=Rheingans}}</ref>，并在朝目标飞行过程中不断左右来回弯曲<ref>{{cite journal|title=Arrow behaviour in the lateral plane during and immediately following the power stroke of a recurve archery bow|last=Park|first=James L.|date=8 September 2013|journal=[[Proceedings_of_the_Institution_of_Mechanical_Engineers,_Part_P:_Journal_of_Sports_Engineering_and_Technology|Proceedings of the Institution of Mechanical Engineers, Part P: Journal of Sports Engineering and Technology]]|issue=3|doi=10.1177/1754337112464844|origyear=9 November 2012|volume=227|pages=172–183}}</ref>。这种动态的弯曲过程常常被错误的用来指代“弓箭手悖论”，给那些只熟悉现代的中心撒放弓的人造成误解。因为中心撒放弓的箭身总是指向目标，在射击过程中并未真正出现这种看似矛盾的现象。

== 细节 ==
[[箭|箭]]必须具有足够的[[撓度|挠度]]（或称[[剛度|刚性]]或“动态挠度"），才能在瞄准时偏离弓身、但射出后回到正确的飞行路线上<ref>{{cite journal|title=High-speed video analysis of arrow behaviour during the power stroke of a recurve archery bow|last=Park|first=James L.|date=June 2013|journal=[[Proceedings_of_the_Institution_of_Mechanical_Engineers,_Part_P:_Journal_of_Sports_Engineering_and_Technology|Proceedings of the Institution of Mechanical Engineers, Part P: Journal of Sports Engineering and Technology]]|issue=2|doi=10.1177/1754337112446406|origyear=1 June 2012|volume=227|pages=128–136}}</ref>。如果箭的挠度不正确，箭与弓接触的接触情况不确定，从而箭飞出后的受力也无法预测导致准头下降<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.texasarchery.org/Documents/ArchersParadox/Archersparadox.htm|title=Archer's Paradox|accessdate=13 February 2013|date=24 June 2001|last=Carmichael|first=A. Ron|publisher=Texas State Archery Association|website=texasarchery.org|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131020213548/http://www.texasarchery.org/Documents/ArchersParadox/Archersparadox.htm|archivedate=2013年10月20日}}</ref>。另外，如果弓箭选手选用挠度各不相同的几只箭，射出时箭身会有不同的弯曲，因此会命中不同的位置。因此，专业弓箭手不仅要根据弓尽量选用挠度在合理范围内的箭，也要注意一组箭的挠度要高度一致。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://begaarchers.com.au/?page_id=2656|title=The Archer's Paradox|accessdate=13 February 2013|date=February 2013|publisher=Bega Valley Traditional Archers}}</ref>这些要求可以在静态挠度测试仪的帮助下实现。

== 弓的选择与挠度 ==
拉力较弱的弓需要配[[撓度|挠度]]较低的箭（挠度是箭身的[[剛度|刚性]]）<ref name="Jim">{{cite web|url=http://www.texasarchery.org/Documents/ArcheryLore/SpineTester/Spine_Tester.html|title=Jim Hill's Spine Tester|accessdate=13 February 2013|publisher=Texas State Archery Association|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131020212920/http://www.texasarchery.org/Documents/ArcheryLore/SpineTester/Spine_Tester.html|archivedate=20 October 2013|website=texasarchery.org|deadurl=yes|df=dmy}}</ref>。拉力较弱的弓在加速箭时造成箭身的变形较小（参考欧拉[[挫曲|挫曲]]），因此箭在射出前必须较容易被弯曲。相反，拉力大的弓需要挠度较大的箭，因为箭被加速时产生的弯曲效果更加显著<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huntersfriend.com/carbon_arrows/hunting_arrows_selection_guide_chapter_3.htm|title=Carbon Arrow University|accessdate=13 February 2013|year=2011|publisher=Hunter's Friend LLC}}</ref>。如果箭的挠度太大（箭身较硬），箭身较难被弯曲。因此撒放后随着弦靠近弓，箭头会被迫射向目标的旁边。反之，如果箭的挠度太小（箭身较软）变形太大，加速时会射向目标的另一边。极端情况下，箭身可能在撒放之前折断，造成安全隐患。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Controlling Dynamic Arrow == 校准 ==
动态挠度很大程度上取决于箭杆长度，箭头重量，与静态挠度。静态挠度是静态条件下箭杆中点的刚性<ref>{{cite web|url=http://yeoldedelphbowmen.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Arrow-Spine-Information.pdf|title=Arrow Spine Information|accessdate=13 February 2013|date=December 2012|publisher=yeoldedelphbowmen.com|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131020215448/http://yeoldedelphbowmen.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Arrow-Spine-Information.pdf|archivedate=20 October 2013|deadurl=yes|df=dmy}}</ref>。射箭贸易协会（ATA）（原称为射箭制造商和招商局组织（AMO））静态挠度测试方法是在26英寸（0.66米）箭杆的中点处悬挂2磅（0.91千克）的重物<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.outlab.it/doc/amostd.pdf|title=AMO Standards (1987)|accessdate=13 February 2013|year=1987|publisher=Archery Manufacturers and Merchants Organization}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://peteward.com/AMOStandards.pdf|title=AMO Standards (2001)|accessdate=13 February 2013|year=2001|publisher=Archery Manufacturers and Merchants Organization}}</ref>。美国检测与材料协会（ASTM）F2031-05（《箭杆静态挠度（刚度）测量的标准检测方法》）为在28英寸（0.71米）箭杆的中点处悬挂1.94磅（0.88千克）的重量<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.astm.org/Standards/F2031.htm|title=ASTM F2031 - 05(2010) Standard Test Method for Measurement of Arrow Shaft Static Spine (Stiffness)|accessdate=13 February 2013|year=2010|publisher=American Society for Testing and Materials}}</ref>。 （过时的）英国国家射箭协会（GNAS）系统使用1.5磅（0.68千克）的重量并将箭身两端（两支点位于箭头之后和扣弦处之前）支起。由于两支点间距离不固定， GNAS不能直接转换为ATA或ASTM，现已废弃不再使用。

挠度的主要测量单位是千分之一英寸的偏转（500的偏转等于0.500英寸）。有时也通过将26除以英寸的偏转，将此数值转换为磅。 （26除以0.500等于挠度为52磅）<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Traditional Bowyer's Bible|last=Cosgrove|first=Gabriela|publisher=The Lyons Press|year=1994|isbn=1-58574-087-X|volume=Volume Three|location=Guilford|page=228|chapter=Wooden Arrows}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=The Traditional Bowyer's Bible|last=Cosgrove|first=Gabriela|publisher=The Lyons Press|year=1994|isbn=1-58574-087-X|volume=Volume Three|location=Guilford|page=228|chapter=Wooden Arrows}}</ref>

目前有許多研究正在探討Psychobiotics對[[腸-腦軸線|腸-腦軸線]]、[[肠神经系统|肠神经系统]]及口腔微生物<ref name="pmid23963608">{{cite journal|vauthors=Noble JM, Scarmeas N, Papapanou PN | title=Poor oral health as a chronic, potentially modifiable dementia risk factor: review of the literature. | journal=Curr Neurol Neurosci Rep | year= 2013 | volume= 13 | issue= 10 | pages= 384 | pmid=23963608 | doi=10.1007/s11910-013-0384-x | pmc= | url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/eutils/elink.fcgi?dbfrom=pubmed&tool=sumsearch.org/cite&retmode=ref&cmd=prlinks&id=23963608  }}</ref>的影響。目前的研究認為Psychobiotics可以調整{{le|神經免疫學|Neuroimmunology|神經免疫}}、{{le|神經炎症|Neuroinflammation}}、{{le|神經激素|neurohormone}}及{{le|氣體傳導分子|gasotransmitter}}的系統<ref name="pmid27389418">{{cite journal| author=Oleskin AV, Shenderov BA| title=Neuromodulatory effects and targets of the SCFAs and gasotransmitters produced by the human symbiotic microbiota. | journal=Microb Ecol Health Dis | year= 2016 | volume= 27 | issue=  | pages= 30971 | pmid=27389418 | doi=10.3402/mehd.v27.30971 | pmc=4937721 | url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/eutils/elink.fcgi?dbfrom=pubmed&tool=sumsearch.org/cite&retmode=ref&cmd=prlinks&id=27389418  }} </ref>。其他可能造成影響的機制有[[下丘脑-垂体-肾上腺轴|下丘脑-垂体-肾上腺轴]]、[[迷走神经|迷走神经]]、{{le|小膠質細胞|microglia}}、[[髓磷脂|髓鞘形成]]及神經元的[[基因表現|基因表現]]<ref name="pmid27045844">{{cite journal|author1=Hoban AE |author2=Stilling RM |author3=Ryan FJ |author4=Shanahan F |author5=Dinan TG |author6=Claesson MJ | title=Regulation of prefrontal cortex myelination by the microbiota. | journal=Transl Psychiatry | year= 2016 | volume= 6 | issue=  | pages= e774 | pmid=27045844 | doi=10.1038/tp.2016.42 | pmc= 4872400 |display-authors=etal}}</ref>，目前研究也發現Psychobiotics對[[情感障礙|情感障礙]]可能有幫助，但相關機制的研究還不太足夠<ref name="Cryan2014">{{cite book|author1=Mark Lyte |author2=John F. Cryan|title=Microbial Endocrinology|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=5Gn1AwAAQBAJ&pg=PA390|date=6 July 2014|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-1-4939-0897-4|pages=390–}}</ref>。

有一篇2016年發表，針對市售益生菌種的臨床前實驗及小規模人體實驗的[[系統綜述|系統綜述]]，發現在測試的菌種中，{{le|双歧杆菌|Bifidobacterium}}及[[乳桿菌屬|乳桿菌屬]]（{{le|长双歧杆菌|B. longum}}、{{le|短双歧杆菌|B. breve}}、{{le|嬰兒雙歧杆菌|B. infantis}}、{{le|瑞士乳桿菌|L. helveticus}}、{{le|酪乳酸桿菌|Lactobacillus rhamnosusm}}、{{le|植物乳桿菌|Lactobacillus plantarum}}及[[乾酪乳酸桿菌|乾酪乳酸桿菌]]）最有潛力可以用在特定{{le|中樞神經系統疾病|Central nervous system disease}}上<ref name="CNS SystRev 2016">{{cite journal | vauthors = Wang H, Lee IS, Braun C, Enck P | title = Effect of probiotics on central nervous system functions in animals and humans - a systematic review | journal = J. Neurogastroenterol Motil. | volume = | issue = | pages = | date = July 2016 | pmid = 27413138 | doi = 10.5056/jnm16018|quote=We reviewed the effect of probiotics on the central nervous system in randomized controlled trials in animals and humans, and analyzed the possibility of translating animal models to human studies because few human studies have been conducted to date.

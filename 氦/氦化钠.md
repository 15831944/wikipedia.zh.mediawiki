'''氦化钠'''是一种[[钠|钠]]的[[稀有气体化合物|稀有气体化合物]]，[[化学式|化学式]]为Na<sub>2</sub>He，其结构可表示为(Na<sup>+</sup>)<sub>2</sub>He(2e<sup>−</sup>)，于2016年由中国化学家制得。

== 制备 ==
氦化钠由[[钠|钠]]与[[氦|氦]]在高温与极高压（>113GPa）下合成。<ref name="dong">{{cite journal|title=Stable Compound of Helium and Sodium at High Pressure|first1=Xiao|last2=Oganov|first2=Artem R.|date=25 April 2014|journal=Nature Chemistry|issue=5|doi=10.1038/nchem.2716|volume=9|pages=440|arxiv=1309.3827|bibcode=2017NatCh...9..440D|pmid=28430195|last1=Dong}}</ref><ref name="dong2">{{cite journal|title=A stable compound of helium and sodium at high pressure|first1=Xiao|last2=Oganov|first2=Artem R.|date=6 February 2017|journal=Nature Chemistry|issue=5|doi=10.1038/nchem.2716|volume=9|pages=440|bibcode=2017NatCh...9..440D|pmid=28430195|last3=Goncharov|first3=Alexander F.|last4=Stavrou|first4=Elissaios|last5=Lobanov|first5=Sergey|last6=Saleh|first6=Gabriele|last7=Qian|first7=Guang-Rui|last8=Zhu|first8=Qiang|last9=Gatti|first9=Carlo|last1=Dong|last10=Deringer|first10=Volker L.|last11=Dronskowski|first11=Richard|last12=Zhou|first12=Xiang-Feng|last13=Prakapenka|first13=Vitali B.|last14=Konôpková|first14=Zuzana|last15=Popov|first15=Ivan A.|last16=Boldyrev|first16=Alexander I.|last17=Wang|first17=Hui-Tian}}</ref>

== 性质 ==
Na<sub>2</sub>He在接近1,500[[开尔文|K]]的高温下熔化，远高于[[钠|钠]]的熔点。

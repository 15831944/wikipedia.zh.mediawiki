'''焦尔塔加河'''（{{lang-bn| জলঢাকা নদী}}），原名迪丘河（{{lang|en|Dichu river}}）<ref>{{citation |last1=Sharma |first1=Suresh Kant |last2=Sharma |first2=Usha |title=Discovery of North-East India: Geography, History, Culture, Religion, Politics, Sociology, Science, Education and Economy. Assam. Volume three |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0PpMFndsXKQC&pg=PA141 |year=2005 |publisher=Mittal Publications |isbn=978-81-8324-037-6 |pages=141}}</ref>，是[[南亚|南亚]]的一条国际河流。河流全长{{convert|192|km}}，发源于喜马拉雅山脉东部的[[锡金邦|锡金邦]]，流经[[不丹|不丹]]与[[印度|印度]]数个邦，在[[拉尔莫尼哈德县|拉尔莫尼哈德县]]注入{{tsl|en|Dharla River||多尔拉河}}。该河流流经数个国家边界，只有一小段位于孟加拉国<ref>{{cite book

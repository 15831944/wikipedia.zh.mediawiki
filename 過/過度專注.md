'''過度專注'''（{{lang-en|Hyperfocus}}）指的是一種重度的[[注意力|注意力]]或{{tsl|en|creative visualization|創意視覺化}}，旨在將個人的[[consciousness|精神意識]]投注在一個科目、主題或任務。在一些有過度專注傾向的人身上，其過度專注的科目或主題可能以[[白日夢|白日夢]]、[[概念|概念]]、[[虛構作品|幻想]]、[[想象|想象]]和其他{{tsl|en|object of the mind||腦海中的事物}}呈現。過於專注在某個或某些事情可能會耽誤、無法兼顧應該做或重要的事情。

過度專注可能與[[心流理論|心流理論]]的概念有關<ref name="webb">{{citation |first1=James T. |last1=Webb |first2=Edward R. |last2=Amend |first3=Nadia E. |last3=Webb |first4=Jean |last4=Goerss |first5=Paul |last5=Beljan |first6=F. In gifted children without ADD/ADHD, this rapt and productive attention state is described by Csikszentmihalyi (1990) as ‘flow.’ … What has been coined ‘hyperfocus’ in persons with [[ADHD|ADD]]/[[ADHD|ADHD]] seems to be a less medical-sounding description of perseveration. Thus the apparent ability to concentrate in certain limited situations does not exclude the diagnosis of ADD/ADHD. }}</ref>。

'''芭芭拉·查理·喬丹''' （{{lang-en|Barbara Charline Jordan}}，{{bd|1936年|2月21日|1996年|1月17日}}）是一名[[美國|美國]]律師、教育家<ref name=":1">{{Cite book|title = Encyclopedia of African American History 1896 to the Present: From the Age of Segregation to the Twenty-First Century|last = Finkelman|first = Paul|publisher = Oxford University Press|year = 2009|isbn = 978-0-19-516779-5|location = New York|pages = 59–61}}</ref>、政治家與[[非裔美國人民權運動|非裔美國人民權運動]]領袖。作爲[[民主黨_(美國)|民主黨人]]，她是[[美國重建時期|美國重建時期]]之後首位入選{{le|德克薩斯州參議院|Texas Senate}}的非裔美國人，以及首位入選[[美國衆議院|衆議院]]的[[美國南部|美國南部]]非裔女性<ref name="Clines">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1996/01/18/us/barbara-jordan-dies-at-59-her-voice-stirred-the-nation.html|title=Barbara Jordan Dies at 59; Her Voice Stirred the Nation|last=Clines|first=Francis X.|newspaper=The New York Times|issn=0362-4331|access-date=2016-10-22}}</ref>。 芭芭拉·喬丹以她在{{le|美國衆議院司法委員會|United States House Committee on the Judiciary}}「{{le|理查德·尼克松彈劾程序|impeachment process against Richard Nixon}}」開場陳述期間所表現的極佳口才以及在[[民主黨全國代表大會|民主黨全國代表大會]]上作爲首個美國非裔女性進行演説的事跡最爲聞名<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://history.house.gov/People/Detail?id=16031|title=JORDAN, Barbara Charline {{!}} US House of Representatives: History, Art & Archives|website=history.house.gov|access-date=2016-10-22}}</ref>。

喬丹曾經被授予過包括[[總統自由勳章|總統自由勳章]]在内多項榮譽。1978年至1980年間，她也曾是[[皮博迪獎|皮博迪獎]]評審委員會的成員之一<ref>[http://www.peabodyawards.com/stories/story/george-foster-peabody-awards-board-members The Peabody Awards - George Foster Peabody Awards Board Members<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>。1996年逝世後，芭芭拉·喬丹成爲了首位被安葬於{{le|德克薩斯州立公墓|Texas State Cemetery}}的美國非裔女性<ref name=HumanitiesTX>{{cite web|title=Barbara Jordan|url=http://www.humanitiestexas.org/programs/tx-originals/list/barbara-jordan|work=Humanities Texas |accessdate=18 February 2016|quote=[...] When she died, in 1996, her burial in the Texas State Cemetery marked yet another first: she was the first black woman interred there. [...]}}</ref><ref>Wilson, Scott. ''Resting Places: The Burial Sites of More Than 14,000 Famous Persons'', 3d ed.: 2 (Kindle Location 24267). McFarland & Company, Inc., Publishers.

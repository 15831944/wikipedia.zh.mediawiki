'''芭比塔'''（{{lang-en|Babita}}，{{bd|1948年|4月20日|catIdx=B}}）<ref>[https://www.vogue.in/content/kareena-kapoor-khan-and-saif-ali-khan-at-babitas-70th-birthday/ “Kareena Kapoor Khan and Saif Ali Khan at Babita Kapoor’s 70th birthday”]. Vogue India. 20-04-2017.</ref>是一位[[印度|印度]][[宝莱坞|宝莱坞]]女演员。她是印度演员、导演[[拉兹·卡普尔|拉兹·卡普尔]]的媳婦，演员[[蘭迪·卡浦爾|蘭迪·卡浦爾]]的妻子。<ref>[https://www.hindustantimes.com/bollywood/why-should-i-want-to-divorce-babita-asks-randhir-kapoor-about-his-estranged-wife/story-MihMxQqEBfZGyIDIpa9iGN.html “‘Why should I want to divorce Babita?’ asks Randhir Kapoor about his estranged wife”]. The Hindustan Times. 22-04-2017.</ref>她的两个女兒[[卡麗詩瑪·卡浦爾|卡麗詩瑪]]和[[卡琳娜·卡浦爾|卡琳娜]]均是宝莱坞知名女星。<ref>[https://mumbaimirror.indiatimes.com/entertainment/bollywood/kareena-and-karisma-kapoor-host-a-birthday-bash-for-mom-babitas/articleshow/58290108.cms “KAREENA AND KARISMA KAPOOR HOST A BIRTHDAY BASH FOR MOM BABITA'S”]. Mumbai Mirror. 21-04-2017.</ref>她的父親Hari Shivdasani是一名[[信德族|信德族]]演員，母亲Barbara則是英国人。

自1966年出道以來，芭比塔共拍攝了19部電影。1971年結婚後在1973年息影。

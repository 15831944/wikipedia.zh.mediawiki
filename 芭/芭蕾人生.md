《'''芭蕾人生'''》（{{lang-en|''Breaking Pointe''}}，又译：'''脚尖上的世界'''、'''决战芭蕾之巅'''），是一部[[美国|美国]]真人秀。记录盐湖城芭蕾舞学校生活的<ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/index.php?op=ViewArticle&articleId=5068&blogId=1 星期四：其他剧集剧情简介（2012年5月31日）]</ref>，本片于2012年5月31日在[[CW电视网|CW电视网]]播出<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2012/04/09/the-cw-announces-premiere-dates-for-summer-reality-series-430114/20120409cw01/|title=The CW Announces Premiere Dates for Summer Reality Series|date=April 9, 2012|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=May 3, 2012}}</ref>。

同年7月底CW主席[[马克·佩德维兹|马克·佩德维兹]]表示由有可能续订本节目<ref>[http://www.deadline.com/2012/07/cws-mark-pedowitz-on-selection-battle-royale-development-plans-digital-route-with-comedy-shorter-drama-orders-tca/ CW’s Mark Pedowitz On ‘Selection’, ‘Battle Royale’ Development Plans, Digital Route With Comedy, Shorter Drama Orders: TCA]</ref>，最终在2012年9月26日[[CW电视网|CW电视网]]宣布续订本节目第二季<ref>[http://blog.cwtv.com/2012/09/26/the-cw-orders-new-seasons-of-“oh-sit”-and-“breaking-pointe”/ THE CW ORDERS NEW SEASONS OF “OH SIT!” AND “BREAKING POINTE”] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130521234706/http://blog.cwtv.com/2012/09/26/the-cw-orders-new-seasons-of-%E2%80%9Coh-sit%E2%80%9D-and-%E2%80%9Cbreaking-pointe%E2%80%9D/ |date=2013-05-21 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/09/26/oh-sit-and-breaking-pointe-renewed-by-the-cw-for-new-season/150344/|title='Oh Sit!' and 'Breaking Pointe' Renewed by The CW for New Seasons|publisher=TV By the Numbers|accessdate=September 26, 2012}}</ref>。第二季将于2013年7月29日开播<ref>[http://thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/04/01/the-cw-announces-premiere-dates-for-new-summer-2013-schedule-634114/20130401cw01/ The CW Announces Premiere Dates for New Summer 2013 Schedule]</ref><ref>[http://blog.cwtv.com/2013/04/01/the-cw-announces-premiere-dates-for-new-summer-2013-schedule/ THE CW ANNOUNCES PREMIERE DATES FOR NEW SUMMER 2013 SCHEDULE] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130408030221/http://blog.cwtv.com/2013/04/01/the-cw-announces-premiere-dates-for-new-summer-2013-schedule/ |date=2013-04-08 }}</ref>。

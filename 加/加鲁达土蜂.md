'''加鲁达土蜂'''（學名：''{{lang|la |Megalara garuda}}''）<ref name="NatGeo">{{cite news |last=Mosher |first=Dave|url=http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2012/03/120327-new-species-wasps-king-bugs-indonesia-animals-science/|title=Bizarre "King of Wasps" Found in Indonesia |publisher=National Geographic|date=28 March 2012|accessdate=19 July 2016}}</ref>屬於[[方頭泥蜂科|方頭泥蜂科]]（[[Crabronidae|Crabronidae]]），是'''''[[Megalara|Megalara]]''屬'''之下的唯一物種<ref name="UCDavis">{{cite web|url=http://entomology.ucdavis.edu/news/warriorwaspnewspecies.html |title=UC Davis Entomologist Discovers New Species of Wasp: Gigantic Wasp with Long, Powerful Jaws |date=19 August 2011 |publisher=[[UC_Davis|UC Davis]] Department of Entomology |access-date=24 August 2011 |deadurl=yes |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110912054322/http://entomology.ucdavis.edu/news/warriorwaspnewspecies.html |archive-date=12 September 2011 }}</ref>。
本物種現時已知只見於[[印尼|印尼]][[蘇拉威西島|蘇拉威西島]]東南部的[[梅公加山脉|梅公加山脉]]。

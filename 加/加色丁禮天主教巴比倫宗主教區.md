'''加色丁禮天主教巴比倫宗主教區'''（{{lang-la|Patriarchatus Babylonensis Chaldaeorum}}）是[[伊拉克|伊拉克]]一個[[東儀天主教|東儀天主教]]（[[加色丁禮天主教會|加色丁禮]]）[[宗主教|宗主教區]]，下轄耶路撒冷和約旦兩個直轄區。

宗主教區成立於1553年4月20日，當時[[東方亞述教會|東方亞述教會]]的[[巴比倫牧首列表|巴比倫牧首]][[西默盎八世·若望·蘇拉卡|西默盎八世·若望·蘇拉卡]]與羅馬教宗共融，加色丁禮天主教會於焉成立。

雖然宗主教區名義上位於[[巴比倫|巴比倫]]，但座堂及宗主教府實際位於首都[[巴格達|巴格達]]。現任宗主教為[[類斯·辣法厄爾一世·薩科|類斯·辣法厄爾一世·薩科]][[樞機|樞機]]<ref>{{cite press release |title=DALLE CHIESE ORIENTALI, 01.02.2013 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2013/02/01/0066/00181.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2018-12-22 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181222141529/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2013/02/01/0066/00181.html |archivedate=2018-12-22 |language=it |date=2013-02-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Pope Francis announces names of fourteen new cardinals |url=https://www.vaticannews.va/en/pope/news/2018-05/pope-francis-new-cardinals-angelus.html |accessdate=2018-12-22 |publisher=Vatican News |date=2018-05-20 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181222142153/https://www.vaticannews.va/en/pope/news/2018-05/pope-francis-new-cardinals-angelus.html |archivedate=2018-12-22 |language=en}}</ref>，輔理主教為{{link-en|羅勃·傑爾吉斯|Robert Jarjis}}<ref>{{cite press release |title=Resignations and Appointments, 22.12.2018 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2018/12/22/181222c.html |publisher=Holy See Press Office |accessdate=2018-12-22 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181222133137/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2018/12/22/181222c.html |archivedate=2018-12-22 |language=en |date=2018-12-22}}</ref>。

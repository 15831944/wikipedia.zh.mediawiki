'''加密貨幣交易所'''是一種業務，允許客户將[[加密貨幣|加密貨幣]]與其他資產進行交易，例如傳統的[[法定貨幣|法定货币]]或其他加密貨幣。加密貨幣交易所可以是[[做市商|市場莊家]]，將買賣價差作為服務的交易佣金；或者作為純粹收取佣金的配對平台。

== 概念 ==
加密貨幣交易所可以是實體業務或者線上業務。實體交易所接受傳統的支付方式以交換加密貨幣，線上交易所則接受電子支付方式以交換加密貨幣。<ref name="secp">{{Cite journal|title=Draft Report on Money Laundering and Terrorist Financing through New Payment Methods|url=http://www.secp.gov.pk/IACCD/pub_iaccd/AML/MONEY%20LAUNDERING%20&%20TERRORIST%20FINANCING%20VULNERABILITIES%20OF%20COMMERCIAL%20WEBSITES%20AND%20INTERNET%20PAYMENT%20SYSTEMS.pdf|last=Working Group on Typologies|date=18 October 2010|publisher=[[打擊清洗黑錢財務行動特別組織|打擊清洗黑錢財務行動特別組織]]|location=Paris|format=PDF|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140211235106/http://www.secp.gov.pk/IACCD/pub_iaccd/AML/MONEY%20LAUNDERING%20%26%20TERRORIST%20FINANCING%20VULNERABILITIES%20OF%20COMMERCIAL%20WEBSITES%20AND%20INTERNET%20PAYMENT%20SYSTEMS.pdf|archivedate=11 February 2014|deadurl=yes}}</ref>通常加密貨幣交易所於西方國家以外的地區營運以避免監管與被起訴，但它們會持有多個國家的銀行戶口以處理不同國家貨種，包括西方國家。<ref name="ndic2008">{{Cite web|title=Substantiation – Money laundering in digital currencies (Unclassified)|work=Money Laundering in Digital Currencies|date=June 2008|publisher=National Drug Intelligence Center, US Department of Justice|url=http://www.justice.gov/archive/ndic/pubs28/28675/sub.htm|accessdate=11 January 2014}}</ref><ref name="sood2013">{{Cite news|author=Sood|first=Aditya K|author2=Enbody|first2=Richard J|last3=Bansal|first3=Rohit|title=Cybercrime: Dissecting the State of Underground Enterprise|year=2013|work=IEEE Internet Computing|publisher=IEEE Computer Society|pages=60–68|issue=1|doi=10.1109/MIC.2012.61}}</ref> 交易所可能接受信用卡付款﹑電匯或其他形式的支付以交換加密貨幣。至2018年，許多發達的司法管轄區對加密貨幣交易所的監管仍未明朗。

交易所可以將加密貨幣傳送至用戶的個人[[密码货币钱包|加密貨幣钱包]]，亦有些交易所會將加密貨幣餘額轉換至匿名的預付卡，用戶可以於全球的[[自動櫃員機|自動櫃員機]]提取貨幣。<ref name="ndic2008">{{Cite web|title=Substantiation – Money laundering in digital currencies (Unclassified)|work=Money Laundering in Digital Currencies|date=June 2008|publisher=National Drug Intelligence Center, US Department of Justice|url=http://www.justice.gov/archive/ndic/pubs28/28675/sub.htm|accessdate=11 January 2014}}</ref><ref name="sood2013">{{Cite news|author=Sood|first=Aditya K|author2=Enbody|first2=Richard J|last3=Bansal|first3=Rohit|title=Cybercrime: Dissecting the State of Underground Enterprise|year=2013|work=IEEE Internet Computing|publisher=IEEE Computer Society|pages=60–68|issue=1|doi=10.1109/MIC.2012.61}}</ref> 有部份的加密貨幣是由現實商品支持的，比如黃金。<ref name="ByrnesMunro2013">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=cVLUdo4JQv4C&pg=PP2802|title=Money Laundering, Asset Forfeiture and Recovery and Compliance – A Global Guide|last=Byrnes|first=William H.|last2=Munro|first2=Robert J.|date=2 October 2013|publisher=LexisNexis|isbn=978-0-327-17084-6|page=2802}} (Page number assigned by Google Books.)</ref>

加密貨幣的創造者很多時與為該貨幣提供交易所之間並沒有關係。<ref name="sood2013">{{Cite news|author=Sood|first=Aditya K|author2=Enbody|first2=Richard J|last3=Bansal|first3=Rohit|title=Cybercrime: Dissecting the State of Underground Enterprise|year=2013|work=IEEE Internet Computing|publisher=IEEE Computer Society|pages=60–68|issue=1|doi=10.1109/MIC.2012.61}}</ref> 在一類系統中，加密貨幣供應商為它們的客戶持有及管理帳戶，但不會向發行加密貨幣給客戶。<ref name="secp">{{Cite journal|title=Draft Report on Money Laundering and Terrorist Financing through New Payment Methods|url=http://www.secp.gov.pk/IACCD/pub_iaccd/AML/MONEY%20LAUNDERING%20&%20TERRORIST%20FINANCING%20VULNERABILITIES%20OF%20COMMERCIAL%20WEBSITES%20AND%20INTERNET%20PAYMENT%20SYSTEMS.pdf|last=Working Group on Typologies|date=18 October 2010|publisher=[[打擊清洗黑錢財務行動特別組織|打擊清洗黑錢財務行動特別組織]]|location=Paris|format=PDF|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140211235106/http://www.secp.gov.pk/IACCD/pub_iaccd/AML/MONEY%20LAUNDERING%20%26%20TERRORIST%20FINANCING%20VULNERABILITIES%20OF%20COMMERCIAL%20WEBSITES%20AND%20INTERNET%20PAYMENT%20SYSTEMS.pdf|archivedate=11 February 2014|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref name="Hesterman2013">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=pX5uOpcXkdgC&pg=PA218|title=The Terrorist-Criminal Nexus: An Alliance of International Drug Cartels, Organized Crime, and Terror Groups|last=Hesterman|first=Jennifer L|date=17 April 2013|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=978-1-4665-5761-1|page=218}}</ref>客戶自行於加密貨幣交易所買入或賣出貨幣，並於加密貨幣供應商提供的帳戶進行存取。<ref name="Hesterman2013" />部份交易所為加密貨幣供應商的子公司，但大多數交易所為獨立的合法企業。<ref name="secp" /> 供應商帳戶中的資金可以是真實或者虛構的貨幣。<ref name="Hesterman2013" />

=== 分散式交易所 ===
{{tsl|en|Decentralized exchange||分散交易所}} （例如[[比特股|比特股]]、Etherdelta﹑IDEX﹑HADAX等）不會將用戶的資金儲存於交易所，取而代之的是[[點對點|點對點]]加密貨幣交易。分散式交易所並不存在傳統交易所的{{tsl|en|Cryptocurrency and security||安全问题}}，但截至2018年中期，它們的交易數量仍然十分低。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/why-cryptocurrency-exchange-hacks-keep-happening-1531656000|title=Cryptocurrency Exchanges Are Getting Hacked Because It’s Easy|author=Russolillo|first=Steven|date=2018-07-16|work=Wall Street Journal|accessdate=2018-09-11|author2=Jeong|first2=Eun-Young|language=en-US|issn=0099-9660}}</ref>

== 法規 ==
至2016年，有幾間於[[歐盟|歐盟]]營運的加密貨幣交易所取得{{tsl|en|Payment Services Directive||支付服务指令}}和{{tsl|en|E-Money Directive||欧盟电子钱指令}}發出的牌照。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://arstechnica.com/information-technology/2016/04/bitcoin-firm-circle-electronic-money-licence-uk-barclays/|title=Bitcoin firm bags first electronic money licence in the UK|work=arstechnica.com|date=4 July 2016}}</ref>這類牌照尚未經司法測試以証明是否足以監管加密貨幣交易所的運作。歐洲理事會和歐洲議會宣布，它們將針對加密貨幣交易平台而頒布更嚴格的法規。

2018年，[[美国证券交易委员会|美国证券交易委员会]]堅持認為「如果一个平台提供數字資產証券及以[[1933年證券法|聯邦證券法]]定義的交易所形式運作，那麼該平台必須在[[美國證券交易委員會|美國證券交易委員會]]註冊為國家證券交易所或申請豁免」。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sec.gov/news/public-statement/enforcement-tm-statement-potentially-unlawful-online-platforms-trading|title=Statement on Potentially Unlawful Online Platforms for Trading Digital Assets|work=U.S. Secured and exchange commission|date=7 March 2018}}</ref> 商品期货交易委员会現已批准加密貨幣衍生商品的公開交易。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2018/03/07/the-sec-made-it-clearer-that-securities-laws-apply-to-cryptocurrencies.html|title=The SEC just made it clearer that securities laws apply to most cryptocurrencies and exchanges trading them|author=Cheng|first=Evelyn|date=2018-03-07|work=CNBC|accessdate=2018-09-03}}</ref>

亞洲國家當中，日本規定加密貨幣交易所必須從{{tsl|en|Financial Services Authority||金融服务管理局}}取得特別許可才能營運。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2018/03/23/japanese-regulator-warns-major-cryptocurrency-exchange-for-operating-without-a-license-bitcoin-falls.html|title=Japanese regulator warns big cryptocurrency exchange for operating without a license, bitcoin falls|author=Cheng|first=Evelyn|date=2018-03-23|work=CNBC|accessdate=2018-09-02}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://bitcoinmagazine.com/articles/how-japan-creating-template-cryptocurrency-regulation/|title=How Japan Is Creating a Template for Cryptocurrency Regulation|author=Castor|first=Amy|work=Bitcoin Magazine|language=en|accessdate=2018-09-02}}</ref> 中國和韓國政府对加密貨幣持反对态度<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/sarahsu/2018/01/15/chinas-shutdown-of-bitcoin-miners-isnt-just-about-electricity/#41b7ee23369b|title=China's Shutdown Of Bitcoin Miners Isn't Just About Electricity|author=Hsu|first=Sara|work=Forbes|accessdate=2018-09-03|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.yicaiglobal.com/news/china%E2%80%99s-regulators-freeze-multiple-bitcoin-otc-accounts-latest-crackdown-cryptocurrency|title=China’s Regulators Freeze Multiple Bitcoin OTC Accounts in Latest Crackdown on Cryptocurrency|work=Yicai Global|language=en|accessdate=2018-09-03}}</ref> 2017年9月，中國政府宣布禁止及將打擊[[首次代币发行|首次代幣發行]]（ICO）活動，其後南韓亦宣布禁止，俄羅斯也開始打擊ICO。<ref>{{Cite web|title=信報財經月刊 -- 中國禁ICO 精英轉戰日本|url=http://monthly.hkej.com/monthly/article/id/1721286/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%E7%A6%81ICO+%E3%80%80+%E7%B2%BE%E8%8B%B1%E8%BD%89%E6%88%B0%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC|work=信報財經月刊|accessdate=2019-02-01}}</ref>中國法律頒布後，一些加密貨幣馬上修改條款，禁止中國公民參與其ICO。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://eos.io/documents/block.one%20-%20EOS%20Token%20Purchase%20Agreement%20-%20September%204,%202017.pdf|title=EOS TOKEN PURCHASE AGREEMENT|accessdate=|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref>

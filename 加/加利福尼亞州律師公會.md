'''加利福尼亞州律師公會'''（State Bar of California、加州律師公會）為美國[[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]]的官方律師執業證照認證規範機構，<ref>The State Bar of California,"Our Mission: What We Do"[http://www.calbar.ca.gov/About-Us/Our-Mission attorney licensing agency],The State Bar of California,2018.</ref>屬於{{le|美國律師公會入會審核|Admission to the bar in the United States|強制性|}}的[[美國州律師公會|州律師公會]]。加州律師公會負責辦理[[律師|律師]]執業證照申核、調查律師專業上不端行為的投訴，以及規定適當的律師守則。加州律師公會直接對[[加利福尼亞州最高法院|加利福尼亞州最高法院]]負責。所有律師執業證照的核可、與{{le|取消律師執業資格|Disbarment|律師執業資格的取消}}，均根據加州律師公會的建議而發布，然後由加州最高法院循例批准。<ref>See California Business and Professions Code sections [http://www.calbar.ca.gov/state/calbar/calbar_generic.jsp?cid=10159&id=1269#6064 6064] (empowering State Bar to certify admission of applicants) and [http://www.calbar.ca.gov/state/calbar/calbar_generic.jsp?cid=10159&id=1274#6078 6078] (empowering State Bar to recommend disbarment, suspension, or reproval).</ref><ref>''In re Rose'', [http://online.ceb.com/CalCases/C4/22C4t430.htm 22 Cal. 4th 430] (2000) ("[t]he State Bar may make only recommendations to this court, which undertakes an independent determination whether the attorney should be disciplined as recommended").</ref><ref>See also ''Hallinan v. Committee of Bar Examiners'', [http://online.ceb.com/CalCases/C2/65C2d447.htm 65 Cal. 2d 447] (1966) (Supreme Court may override State Bar's finding of lack of good moral character and force State Bar to certify applicant for admission) and ''Pineda v. State Bar'', [http://online.ceb.com/CalCases/C3/49C3d753.htm 49 Cal. 3d 753] (1989) (Supreme Court may override State Bar's recommendation of punishment and prescribe more severe punishment). Notably, [[Terence_Hallinan|Terence Hallinan]] went on to become San Francisco's [[district_attorney|district attorney]], while Lynn Pineda was [http://members.calbar.ca.gov/search/member_detail.aspx?x=62411 disbarred by the Supreme Court of California on September 14, 1990].</ref>

加州律師公會於1927年7月29日依法成立，當時州律師公會的法案即刻生效。<ref name=Genesis>"Introductory: The Genesis and Development of the State Bar", in the ''Proceedings of the First Annual Meeting of the State Bar of California'' (San Francisco: The Recorder Printing and Publishing Co., 1929), xvi.</ref>{{rp|xiii–xix}}而且加州律師公會是美國最大的公會，截至2015年2月，共有253,306名活動的會員，其中183,763人處於活躍狀態。<ref name="demographics" />公會的總部位於[[舊金山|舊金山]]，在[[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]及[[沙加緬度_(加利福尼亞州)|沙加緬度]]均設有分部。

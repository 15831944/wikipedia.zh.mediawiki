==早年生活==
维纳查克生于[[苏联|苏联]]，1978年移民美国，当时苏联已经签署[[限制战略武器谈判|限制战略武器谈判]]条约，该条约以美国小麦为交换条件允许{{Tsl|en|Soviet Jews|苏联犹太人}}离开苏联。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1981/02/22/opinion/what-price-a-soviet-jew.html|title=What Price a Soviet Jew?|date=1981-02-22|<!--newspaper=The New York Times|-->issn=0362-4331|access-date=2016-12-09|work=[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]|language=en}}</ref>加里一家九口居住在[[皇后区|纽约皇后区]]的一间卧室客厅厨房连体的小公寓。<ref>{{Citation|last=Vaynerchuk|first=Gary|title=SXSW Keynote 2016|date=2016-03-19|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h67-oxooaoM&feature=youtu.be&t=18m51s|accessdate=2016-12-09|language=en}}</ref>之后, 维纳查克一家迁居到[[爱迪生_(新泽西州)|新泽西州的爱迪生镇]]，在那里，维纳查克创办了柠檬汁专卖站连锁店，并通过在周末交易{{Tsl|en|Baseball card|棒球卡}}挣得了成千上万美元的收入。<ref name = inc>{{cite web | url = http://www.inc.com/candice-galek/gary-vaynerchuk-reveals-the-skill-that-made-him-millions-and-that-anyone-can-lea.html | title =  Gary Vaynerchuk Reveals the Skill That Made Him Millions (and That Anyone Can Learn)| publisher = Inc.杂志 | accessdate = 2017-02-20|language=en}}</ref><ref>Friend,tad.[http://www.newyorker.com/talk/2010/06/07/100607ta_talk_friend "V-va-va-voom!"], ''[[纽约客|纽约客]]''，2010年6月7日。查阅日期2013年1月31日。 "他摇了摇头。'我出生在苏联，来这里时我们很穷' — 新泽西州的爱迪生镇 — '所以当很多人都很感兴趣时让我觉得非常不可思议。'"</ref>14岁那年，他开始参与自家的葡萄酒零售生意。1998年，维纳查克从[[牛顿_(马萨诸塞州)|麻州牛顿市]]的{{Tsl|en|Mount Ida College|伊达山学院}}本科毕业，获得学士学位。<ref name="New York Times">{{cite news|last=Asimov | first=Eric |title= Pop goes the critic | url=https://www.nytimes.com/2009/09/09/dining/09pour.html?_r=1|date=2009-09-08 | work=[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]}}</ref>

== 事业 ==
=== Wine Library葡萄酒庄 ===
1999年大学毕业以后，维纳查克开始接管父亲在{{Tsl|en|Springfield Township, Union County, New Jersey|新泽西斯普林菲尔德}}的葡萄酒庄的日常运作，把店名Shopper's Discount Liquors改为Wine Library，并且推出在线销售。2006年又推出名为Wine Library TV的葡萄酒[[网络广播|网播]]节目，每日一期。<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url = https://www.nytimes.com/2008/06/22/nyregion/nyregionspecial2/22winenj.html|date = 2008-06-22|accessdate =2016-10-10 |title = At Wine Superstores,Tastings Are Just the Start| website = |publisher = |last = |first = |language=en}}</ref> 

通过[[电子商务|电子商务]]，电子邮件行銷和定价的天才组合，截至2005年，维纳查克已经将业务从3百万美元增至6千万。2011年8月，维纳查克宣布从Wine Library功成身退，转而把精力集中于创建VaynerMedia（维纳查克兄弟于2009年合力创办的{{Tsl|en|Digital agency|數位代理}}）。<ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.fastcompany.com/1005871/selling-wine-web-20-way|title = SELLING WINE THE WEB 2.0 WAY|date = 2008-09-16|accessdate = |website = KERMIT PATTISON|publisher = |last = |first = |language=en}}</ref>

=== VaynerMedia ===
2009年，维纳查克兄弟加里和AJ合办了VaynerMedia，一个以社交媒体为重点的數位机构。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url = https://www.nytimes.com/2013/11/03/technology/riding-the-hashtag-in-social-media-marketing.html|title = Riding the Hashtag in Social Media Marketing|date = 2013-11-02|accessdate = |website = |publisher = [[纽约时报|纽约时报]]|last = |first = |language=en}}</ref>VaynerMedia向[[通用电气|通用电气]]、[[安海斯-布希|安海斯-布希]]、[[亿滋国际|亿滋国际]]和[[百事公司|百事公司]]等《财富》500强企业提供社交媒体及战略服务。<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url = http://adage.com/article/special-report-agency-alist-2015/ad-age-2015-agency-a-list-standouts-grey-180la-akqa/296732/|title = Ad Age's 2015 Agency A-List Standouts: Grey, 180LA, AKQA and More|date = |access-date=2016-12-09 |website = |publisher = |last = |first = |language=en}}</ref> 2015年，VaynerMedia跻身于《{{Tsl|en|Advertising Age|AdAge}}》一线机构名单。<ref name=":1"/>2016年，共有600名员工的VaynerMedia总收入达一亿美元。<ref name = cnbc>{{cite web | url = http://www.cnbc.com/2016/03/29/cnbcs-follow-the-leader-uncovers-the-secrets-to-entrepreneurial-success-beginning-wednesday-april-6-at-10pm-etpt.html | title = CNBC's 'Follow the Leader' Uncovers the Secrets to Entrepreneurial Success | publisher = CNBC | accessdate = 2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>VaynerMedia还与[[Vimeo|Vimeo]]合作，以數位内容为立足点为各大品牌及电影制作人牵线搭桥。<ref name = adage>{{cite web | url = http://adage.com/article/agency-news/vimeo-vaynermedia-create-exclusive-content-partnership/306956/ | title = Vimeo and VaynerMedia Create Exclusive Content Partnership| publisher = AdAge | accessdate =2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>

=== The Gallery ===

2017年据《[[华尔街日报|华尔街日报]]》报道，维纳查克成立了The Gallery，该公司旗下不仅有维纳查克和RSE Ventures收购的PureWow，还包括其他媒体和创意产业。<ref name = businessinsider/><ref name = wsj2>{{cite web | url = https://www.wsj.com/articles/gary-vaynerchuk-acquires-womens-publisher-purewow-1483531201 | title = Gary Vaynerchuk Acquires Women’s Publisher PureWow| publisher = 华尔街日报 | accessdate =2017-03-09|language=en}}</ref>{{Tsl|en|PureWow}}总裁Ryan Harwood担任The Gallery总裁。數位代理公司VaynerMedia的姐妹公司Marketing Dive如此描述PureWow加盟VaynerMedia：“强力结合后，其内部团队和资源使得提高视频方面的能力成为可能。”<ref name = marketingdive>{{cite web | url =http://www.marketingdive.com/news/agency-entrepreneur-gary-vaynerchuk-buys-womens-lifestyle-publisher/433340/ | title = Agency entrepreneur Gary Vaynerchuk buys women's lifestyle publisher | publisher = Marketing Dive | accessdate =2017-03-09|language=en}}</ref>

==投资==

2017年维纳查克曾以[[天使投资者|天使投资者]]的身份进行个人投资，投资对象包括女性刊物{{Tsl|en|PureWow}}，也是[[优步|优步]]、[[脸书|脸书]]、[[推特|推特]]、Venmo及数十家创业公司的投资人。<ref name = inc2>{{cite web |  url = http://www.inc.com/gary-vaynerchuk/vaynerchuk-gary-playbook-angel-investments.html | title = How Gary Vaynerchuk Chooses Angel Investments| publisher = Inc.杂志| accessdate =2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>

=== VaynerRSE ===
从[[Tumblr|Tumblr]]和Buddy Media退出以后，维纳查克与RSE Ventures的{{Tsl|en|Matt Higgins (businessman)|Matt Higgins}}创建了2500万美元的投资基金VaynerRSE，并得到[[迈阿密海豚|Miami Dolphins]]老板Stephen Ross的鼎力支持。该基金致力于消费者技术，除了进行传统的[[天使投资|天使投资]]以外，还具有[[企业孵化器|企业孵化器]]的功能。<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://venturebeat.com/2014/02/14/heres-what-gary-vaynerchuck-is-really-up-to-with-that-new-25m-fund-exclusive/|title = Here's what Gary Vaynerchuck is really up to with that new $25M fund (exclusive)|date =2014-02-14|accessdate = |website = Venture Beat|publisher = |last = |first = |language=en}}</ref>

=== BRaVe Ventures ===
2014年，维纳查克与社交电视企业家Jesse Redniss、David Beck联手创立了BRaVe Ventures。<ref name = wsj3>{{cite web | url = http://blogs.wsj.com/venturecapital/2014/09/24/the-daily-startup-digital-media-veterans-launch-brave-ventures/ | title = The Daily Startup: Digital Media Veterans Launch BRaVe Ventures| publisher =华尔街日报| accessdate =2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>该公司就新兴技术和资金为电视网络出谋划策，并孵化新兴的多屏和[[社交网络|社交网络]]创业公司和技术。<ref name = adweek>{{cite web | url = http://www.adweek.com/lostremote/jesse-redniss-david-beck-and-gary-vaynerchuk-form-brave-ventures/47564 | title = Jesse Redniss, David Beck, and Gary Vaynerchuk Form BRaVe Ventures| publisher = AdWeek | accessdate =2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref> 2016年11月，据《[[综艺_(杂志)|综艺]]》杂志报道，[[特纳广播公司|特纳广播公司]]聘请BRaVe Ventures为其旗舰品牌[[TBS|TBS]]及[[TNT|TNT]]的业务和策略发展献计献策。<ref name = variety>{{cite web | url = http://variety.com/2016/tv/news/turner-brave-jesse-redniss-david-beck-tbs-tnt-1201919457/ | title = Turner Taps Consultancy to Build Ventures Around TBS, TNT| publisher = Variety | accessdate =2017-03-08|language=en}}</ref>

=== VaynerSports ===
2016年，维纳查克对体育机构Symmetry进行投资，成立了VaynerSports，提供全方位运动员代理服务。2017年，VaynerSports与包括Jalen Reeves Maybin和Jon Toth在内的美国职业橄榄球大联盟（NFL）签约球员订立了合同。<ref>{{cite news| url= http://blog.jobsinsocialmedia.com/2016/06/14/vaynersports/ | title=Gary Vaynerchuk Creates VaynerSports to Go After the Sports Agency Market with Social Media|work=jobsinsocialmedia|date=2016-06-14|accessdate=2016-06-22|language=en}}</ref><ref name = bizjournals>{{cite web | url = http://www.bizjournals.com/newyork/news/2017/01/25/vaynersports-gets-off-to-quick-start-with-nfl.html | title = VaynerSports gets off to quick start with NFL draft prospects| publisher = The Business Journals | accessdate = 2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>

=== 《''DailyVee''》 ===
《''DailyVee''》是YouTube上的每日视频纪录片系列，纪录了维纳查克作为父亲、商人和公司总裁的生活花絮。从2015年起，维纳查克开始在VaynerMedia播放录制的真人秀，录制内容包括访谈、投资者会议以及策略谋划会。<ref name =huffingtonpost>{{cite web | url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/kelsey-humphreys/gary-vaynerchuk-entrepren_b_10633756.html | title = Gary Vaynerchuk:"Entrepreneurs,You're Doing It Wrong" | publisher = [[赫芬顿邮报|赫芬顿邮报]] | accessdate =2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>在该纪录片系列里，维纳查克运用各种社会媒体策略（特别是通过 [[Snapchat|Snapchat]]）来展示社会媒体营销之道。<ref name = inc3>{{cite web | url = http://www.inc.com/candice-galek/gary-vaynerchuk-reveals-the-skill-that-made-him-millions-and-that-anyone-can-lea.html | title = Gary Vaynerchuk reveals the skill that made him millions and that anyone can learn | publisher = Inc.杂志| accessdate =2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>

=== 《''The #AskGaryVee Show''》 ===
2014年，维纳查克及其个人内容制作团队在[[YouTube|YouTube]]上推出《''The #AskGaryVee Show''》。在节目中，维纳查克从推特和[[Instagram|Instagram]]中搜罗各种问题，然后以其独有的即兴发挥的方式一一作答。<ref name = forbes2>{{cite web | url = https://www.forbes.com/sites/jimkeenan/2015/07/22/askgaryvee-changing-the-mentoring-game-for-young-entrepreneurs/ | title = #askgaryvee Changing the Mentoring Game for Young Entrepreneurs | publisher = [[福布斯|福布斯]]杂志 | accessdate =2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>这些问题通常与创业、家庭和生意有关，由制作团队预先筛选，但维纳查克本人直到节目开拍才知道这些具体问题。在《''The #AskGaryVee Show''》的启发下，维纳查克完成了他的第四本书《AskGaryVee: 一位创业家对领导力、社会媒体和自我意识的解读》。<ref name = entrepreneur2>{{cite web |url = https://www.entrepreneur.com/article/249052 | title = Why Gary Vaynerchuk's '#AskGaryVee Show' Is Marketing Gold| publisher = Entrepreneur杂志| accessdate =2017-02-23|language=en}}</ref>

=== 《''The Thank You Economy''》 ===
2011年，维纳查克的第二本书《''The Thank You Economy''》在《纽约时报》的咨询类精装畅销书中名列第二，该书讨论了促成商家与客户之间成功关系的各类数字及软性因素。<ref>''[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]''  [https://www.nytimes.com/books/best-sellers/2011/03/27/hardcover-advice/ The New York Times Best Sellers]</ref>

=== 《''Jab, Jab, Jab, Right-Hook''》 ===
2013年，维纳查克通过哈珀商业出版了他的第三本书《''Jab, Jab, Jab, Right Hook: How to Tell Your Story in a Noisy Social World''》。通过讲述主要社交媒体平台中种种推广活动与策略的成与败，这本书演示了商家应该使用或避免的社交媒体行銷手段和策略。《''Jab, Jab, Jab, Right-Hook''》首次出版就荣登《华尔街日报》的商务丛书排行榜首，并在《纽约时报》的咨询类精装畅销书中名列第四。<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/books/best-sellers/2016/03/27/advice-how-to-and-miscellaneous/?_r=0|title=NYT Best Sellers|last=|first=|date=2013-12-15|website=|publisher=|accessdate=|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url = https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB10001424052702303497804579237991296864348|title = Best-Selling Books Week Ended Dec. 1|date = |accessdate = |website =www.wsj.com|publisher =  [[华尔街日报|华尔街日报]]|last = |first = |language=en}}</ref>

=== 《''AskGaryVee: One Entrepreneur's Take on Leadership, Social Media, and Self-Awareness''》 ===
2016年3月，维纳查克通过[[哈珀柯林斯|哈珀柯林斯]]出版社的哈珀商业出版了他的第四本书《''AskGaryVee: One Entrepreneur's Take on Leadership, Social Media, and Self-Awareness''》。以维纳查克的YouTube系列秀《''#AskGaryVee''》为创作原型，维纳查克在这本小说中收录了其YouTube系列秀中的问答隽语，范围涵盖自我意识、为人父母以及创业江湖。《''#AskGaryVee''》成为维纳查克的第四本《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》畅销书。<ref name=":3" />

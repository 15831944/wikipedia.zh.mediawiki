'''加依勒区'''（{{lang-ky|Жайыл району}}，{{lang-en|Jayyl District}}）是[[吉尔吉斯斯坦|吉尔吉斯斯坦]]北部州份[[楚河州|楚河州]]的一个[[区_(苏联)|区]]。该区原名加里宁区，1993年改为现名。该区面积为{{convert|3453|km2}}，2009年常住人口为92,645人。<ref name=stat>{{cite web|url=http://212.42.101.100:8088/nacstat/sites/default/files/%D0%A7%D1%83%D0%B9%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B0%D1%8F%20%D0%BE%D0%B1%D0%BB%D0%B0%D1%81%D1%82%D1%8C.pdf |title=2009 population census of the Kyrgyz Republic: Chuy Region |accessdate=2010-03-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110810174007/http://212.42.101.100:8088/nacstat/sites/default/files/%D0%A7%D1%83%D0%B9%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B0%D1%8F%20%D0%BE%D0%B1%D0%BB%D0%B0%D1%81%D1%82%D1%8C.pdf |archivedate=10 August 2011 |df= }}</ref>治所位于[[卡拉巴勒塔|卡拉巴勒塔]]。

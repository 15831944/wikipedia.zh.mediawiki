'''加拿大南极研究所'''（{{lang-en|Antarctic Institute of Canada}}）是由前[[南极|南极]]研究员[[奧斯丁·馬當|奥斯丁·马当]]在1985年成立的一家非盈利的慈善机构<ref name="multiple">[http://corporate.britannica.com/antarctic-institute-of-canada/ "Antarctic Institute of Canada"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160729151514/http://corporate.britannica.com/antarctic-institute-of-canada/ |date=2016-07-29 }}. Encyclopædia Britannica, Inc. Corporate Site. Retrieved 27 July 2016.</ref>。研究所曾经用以游说[[加拿大政府|加拿大联邦政府]]增加在南极的研究；如今它也支持“学术研究和学术写作”<ref name="multiple"></ref>。研究所同时为[[金色流星出版社|金色流星出版社]]出版的书提供研究经费。这些书包括《A Description of the Western Isles of Scotland》<ref>[[奧斯丁·馬當|Mardon, Austin]]. [https://books.google.ca/books?id=ce8cAgAAQBAJ&lpg=PP5&dq=antarctic%20institute%20of%20canada&pg=PP5#v=onepage&q=antarctic%20institute%20of%20canada&f=false "A Description of the Western Isles of Scotland"]. Google Books. Golden Meteorite Press. ISBN 9781897472088. Retrieved 27 July 2016.</ref>与《A Description of the Western Isles of Scotland》<ref>Chenard, Marcel. [https://books.google.ca/books?id=bRz9AQAAQBAJ&pg=PP3&dq=antarctic+institute+of+canada&hl=en&sa=X&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=antarctic%20institute%20of%20canada&f=false "Dark Age Defenders"]. Google Books. Golden Meteorite Press. ISBN 9781897480069. Retrieved 27 July 2016.</ref>。

== 历史 ==
研究所由奥斯丁·马当于1985年在[[阿尔伯塔|阿尔伯塔]]省[[萊斯布里奇|莱斯布里奇]]市成立，现在于[[埃德蒙顿|埃德蒙顿]]市运作<ref>[http://www.antarcticinstituteofcanada.ca/page5contactus.htm "Contact Us"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160710124059/http://www.antarcticinstituteofcanada.ca/page5contactus.htm |date=2016-07-10 }}. Antarctic Institute of Canada. Retrieved 27 July 2016.</ref>。自成立以来，研究所增加了多种研究范围，包括历史、地理和加拿大政治等<ref name="multiple"></ref>。

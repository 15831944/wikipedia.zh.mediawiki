'''加拿大鵝'''（[[英語|英語]]：Canada Goose）是一家[[加拿大|加拿大]]高端冬季服裝製造商。

==历史==
由山姆·提克創建於1957年，得名自[[五大湖|五大湖]]地區的特色物種[[加拿大雁|加拿大雁]]。主要產品有各類[[夾克|夾克]]、[[派克大衣|派克大衣]]、背心、帽子、手套等。因夾克領子使用了[[郊狼|郊狼]]皮毛<ref name=MRketplace>{{Cite news|last=LaRochelle|first=Jillian|title=When hell freezes over|work=MRketplace|date=7 November 2012|url=http://www.mrketplace.com/44932/when-hell-freezes-over/|accessdate=19 November 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121109115618/http://www.mrketplace.com/44932/when-hell-freezes-over/|archivedate=2012年11月9日}}</ref>，曾引起[[動物權利|動物權利]]保護者的抗議。<ref name=PETAcoyotes>{{cite web |url=http://www.peta.org/features/coyotes-killed-for-canada-goose | title=When You Look at These Pictures, You’ll Return Your Canada Goose Jacket |access-date=16 January 2018 |author=[[People_for_the_Ethical_Treatment_of_Animals|People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals]] |year=2018 |quote=Wild coyotes are caught in steel leg clamps, head-crushing traps, body-gripping traps, or neck snares. Often, they’re mothers who are desperate to get back to their starving pups. Once caught, these animals can spend days suffering. Some—especially the mothers—even attempt to chew off their own limbs to escape. Their slow deaths can be the result of blood loss, shock, dehydration, frostbite, gangrene, or attacks by predators.}}</ref>填充衣服所用的[[絨羽|絨羽]]多進購自加拿大農村地區的[[胡特爾派|胡特爾派]]農民。<ref name=iain>{{cite news|author1=Iain Marlow|author2=Sean Silcoff|author3=Susan Krashinsky|title=Canada Goose sells a majority stake – with a made-in-Canada guarantee |url= https://www.theglobeandmail.com/report-on-business/canada-goose-sells-majority-stake-to-us-private-equity-firm/article15848715/ |work=The Globe and Mail|date=December 10, 2013}}</ref>

2013年12月，贝恩资本以2.5亿美元收购了Canada Goose70%股权，公司现任CEO Dani Reiss保留了少数股权。

根据Canada Goose的招股说明书，在2017年3月IPO中，贝恩资本出售了10%股权，同时继续保留Canada Goose的控股权。这10%的股权帮贝恩换回来了2.5亿美金。

'''加州之王'''({{lang-en|King of California}}) 是一部2007年上映的美国喜剧电影，是美国导演Mike Cahill首次执导的电影。<ref>{{cite news|last=Goldstein|first=Gregg|title="King of California" rules at Sundance|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/2007/01/26/film-sundance-king-dc-idUSN2626468820070127|accessdate=29 March 2011|newspaper=[[Reuters|Reuters]]|date=26 January 2007|quote=It marks the debut effort of writer-director Mike Cahill.}}</ref> 电影于2007年1月24日在[[圣丹斯电影节|圣丹斯电影节]]上放映。<ref>{{cite web|title=King of California|url=http://history.sundance.org/films/3730|work=history.sundance.org|publisher=[[Sundance_Institute|Sundance Institute]]|accessdate=29 March 2011|quote=With an energy that both disarms and delights, Cahill transforms the stereotypical image of the California eccentric and the even-more-familiar tale of pursuing your dreams into a dramatically powerful and imaginative fable that explores both the difficulties of a father/daughter relationship and the excesses of people and societies run amok.}}</ref>2007年9月14日在北美地区[[有限上映|有限上映]]。

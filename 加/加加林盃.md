'''加加林盃'''是[[大陸冰球聯盟|大陸冰球聯盟]]季後賽所設計的冠軍獎盃，該名紀念[[尤里·阿列克谢耶维奇·加加林|尤里·阿列克谢耶维奇·加加林]]。<ref name="New Kontinental Hockey League takes shape, sets rules">{{cite news|title=New Kontinental Hockey League takes shape, sets rules|url=http://www.eurohockey.net/news/story.html?id=20080313111756newkontinentalhockeyleaguetakesshapesetsrules|author =Seren Rosso, Alessandro |date =2008-03-13|accessdate =2008-07-09}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.flashscore.com/team/ska-st-petersburg/zVJwe4ER/|title=SKA St. Petersburg live scores, results, fixtures, Metallurg Magnitogorsk v SKA St. Petersburg live {{!}} Hockey, Russia|website=www.flashscore.com|language=en|access-date=2017-04-15}}</ref>這個獎盃據說是以加加林的名字命名的，因為第一屆KHL賽季的最後一場比賽將在4月12日舉行，該日是[[载人空间飞行国际日|加加林紀念日]]。

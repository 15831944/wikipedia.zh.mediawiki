'''加州大学体育学会'''（{{lang-en|'''California Collegiate Athletic Association'''}}；简称'''CCAA'''）是[[全美大学体育学会|全美大学体育学会]]下属的一个校际体育团体。<ref>{{cite web|title=NCAA Division II WebPages |url=http://www.siue.edu/ATHLETIC/d2/conf.html |work=[[Southern_Illinois_University_Edwardsville|Southern Illinois University Edwardsville]] |accessdate=2008-09-12 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080609013459/http://www.siue.edu/ATHLETIC/d2/conf.html |archivedate=2008-06-09 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>
目前，其成员都是[[公立大学|公立大学]]，除[[加利福尼亞大学圣地牙哥分校|加利福尼亚大学圣地亚哥分校]]是[[加利福尼亚州立大学|加利福尼亚州立大学]]系统的成员之外。<ref>{{cite web| title= Quick Facts| url= http://www.goccaa.org/Sports/ccaa/2006/quickfacts.asp?nl=1&tab=ccaa| work= CCAA| accessdate= 2008-09-12| deadurl= yes| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20081019134827/http://www.goccaa.org/Sports/ccaa/2006/quickfacts.asp?nl=1&tab=ccaa| archivedate= 2008-10-19}}</ref>

它始建于1938年12月，1939年开始在运作。<ref>{{cite web| title= Commissioner's Office| url= http://www.goccaa.org/Sports/ccaa/2006/adminhq.asp?nl=4&tab=ccaa| work= CCAA| accessdate= 2008-09-12| deadurl= yes| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20081019134822/http://www.goccaa.org/Sports/ccaa/2006/adminhq.asp?nl=4&tab=ccaa| archivedate= 2008-10-19}}</ref>

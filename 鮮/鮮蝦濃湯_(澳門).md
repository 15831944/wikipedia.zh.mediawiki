==歷史==
鮮蝦濃湯的根源來自位於[[馬來亞半島|馬來亞半島]]的[[馬六甲|馬六甲]]<ref name="MOTourismZH"/>，並被認為是從[[馬來西亞飲食|馬來西亞菜]][[叻沙|叻沙]]演變出來的<ref name="FatRiceBook">{{cite book|last1=Conlon|first1=Abraham|last2=Lo|first2=Adrienne|last3=Amano|first3=Hugh|title=The Adventures of Fat Rice: Recipes from the Chicago Restaurant Inspired by Macau|date=2016|publisher=Ten Speed Press|isbn=978-1-60774-896-0|page=92|edition=第一版|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=mt0iDQAAQBAJ&pg=PA92#v=onepage&q&f=false|language=[[英文|英文]]|accessdate=2018-03-07}}</ref>。

鮮蝦濃湯的葡文名 (Lacassá) 於[[澳門土生葡語|澳門土生葡語]]裡有[[米粉|米粉]]的意思<ref>{{cite web|title=Macanese language|url=http://macaneselibrary.org/uiFiles/Articles/Misc/RemediosGeorgePatua.htm|website=Macaneselibrary.org|accessdate=2018-03-07}}</ref>，而此道湯亦含有米粉<ref name="FatRiceBook"/><ref>{{cite web|last1=Sheridan|first1=Margaret|title=Original Fusion|url=https://www.saveur.com/article/Travels/Original-Fusion|website=Saveur|accessdate=2018-03-07|date=2001-07-10|language=[[英文|英文]]}}</ref>。

==食用==
由於鮮蝦濃湯裡不含紅肉，而傳統上[[天主教|天主教]]教徒必須在[[平安夜|平安夜]]守齋，所以鮮蝦濃湯往往成為[[澳門土生葡人|澳門土生葡人]]平安夜餐桌上出現的的一道菜<ref name="ShesCookin">{{cite web|title=The Unique Cuisine of Macau|url=https://shescookin.com/the-unique-cuisine-of-macau/|website=She's Cookin'|accessdate=2018-03-07|language=[[英文|英文]]}}</ref>。

傳統上，鮮蝦濃湯亦會在土生葡人的[[肥茶|肥茶]] (Cha Gordo) 中出現<ref>{{cite web|last1=Cummings-Yeates|first1=Rosalind|title=Feasting on Fat Tea in Macau|url=https://www.travelpulse.com/opinions/blog/feasting-on-fat-tea-in-macau.html|website=Travel Pulse|accessdate=2018-03-07|date=2015-08-18|language=[[英文|英文]]}}</ref>。

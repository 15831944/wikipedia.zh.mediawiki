《'''蝙蝠侠'''》（{{lang-en|Batman}}）是一部正在连载的美国系列漫画，以[[DC漫画|DC漫画]]超级英雄[[蝙蝠侠|蝙蝠侠]]为主角。 该角色首次出现在《[[侦探漫画|侦探漫画]]》第27期（1939年5月）。 蝙蝠侠是如此的受欢迎，以至于DC决定为他开启一个独立的连载系列，并于1940年春季开始出版。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://archive.org/stream/catalogofcopyrig352lib#page/142/mode/2up/search/Batman|title=The Catalog of Copyright Entries 1940 Periodicals Jan-Dec New Series Vol 35 Pt 2|publisher=[[United_States_Copyright_Office|United States Copyright Office]]|year=1940|location=Washington, D.C.|page=142}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.comics.org/issue/724/|title=''Batman'' #1 (Spring 1940)|publisher=Grand Comics Database}}</ref>它是第一次宣传是在1940年4月初，就在他的新伙伴神奇小子罗宾出场一个月后。

《蝙蝠侠》漫画最初是作为季刊发行的，但后来在20世纪50年代末成为双月刊，之后又成为月刊，此后一直如此。2011年9月，新52重启改变了DC漫画宇宙的连续性。在这个新的时间线上《蝙蝠侠》重新推出了新的第一期。

2016年,DC漫画开始的第二次期号重开，新系列标题为[[DC宇宙：重生|DC重生]]。2016年8月开始的第3辑蝙蝠侠为半月刊连载。

== 出版的历史 ==

=== 黄金时代 ===
1939年5月，蝙蝠侠首次出现在《侦探漫画》第27期中。1940年春，《蝙蝠侠》第1期发行并在蝙蝠侠的故事中引入了新角色，其中最着名的是猫女和蝙蝠侠的宿敌：小丑。<ref>{{Cite book|title=DC Comics Year By Year A Visual Chronicle|last=Wallace|first=Daniel|last2=Dolan|first2=Hannah, ed.|date=2010|publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]]|isbn=978-0-7566-6742-9|location=London, United Kingdom|page=31|chapter=1940s|quote=The first issue of Batman's self-titled comic written by Bill Finger and drawn by Bob Kane, represented a milestone in more ways than one.  With Robin now a partner to the Caped Crusader, villains needed to rise to the challenge, and this issue introduced two future legends: the Joker and Catwoman.}}</ref>并在第16期介绍了阿尔弗雷德,韦恩家的管家。<ref>Wallace "1940s" in Dolan, p. 44: "Batman and Robin got some help in their crusade against crime with the arrival of butler Alfred in a thirteen-page back-up story by writer Don Cameron and artist Bob Kane."</ref>

编辑惠特尼·埃尔斯沃思在1941年将《蝙蝠侠》的故事分配给了画师迪克·肖普。<ref name="Archives223">{{Cite book|title=Batman Archives, Vol. 3|last=Desris|first=Joe|date=1994|publisher=DC Comics|isbn=1-56389-099-2|location=New York, New York|page=223}}</ref>由于预料到鲍勃·凯恩将被征召参加第二次世界大战，DC预存了肖普的工作以防止延误。肖普第一次在《蝙蝠侠》上的工作是蝙蝠侠第18期的封面。<ref>Verified by Sprang at [http://www.comics.org/details.lasso?id=3105 ''Batman'' #18 (Aug.-Sept. 1943)] and [http://www.comics.org/details.lasso?id=3462 ''Detective Comics'' #84 (Feb. 1944)] at the Grand Comics Database</ref>肖普第一个原创的蝙蝠侠故事工作,出现在蝙蝠侠第19期。他画了封面和前三个故事,并为第四个故事做了铅笔稿由诺姆·法伦描线。<ref>Verified by Sprang at [http://www.comics.org/details.lasso?id=3231 ''Batman'' #19 (Oct.-Nov. 1943)] at the Grand Comics Database</ref>像所有那时蝙蝠侠作家,肖普只能作为凯恩的”影子画师“。

在这个时代首次亮相的恶棍包括第49期（1948年10月）的疯帽匠和第63期（1951年2月）的杀手蛾。<ref>Wallace "1940s" in Dolan, p. 59: "Inspired by Lewis Carroll's ''Alice in Wonderland'', the Mad Hatter joined the other costumed freaks of Gotham City on his debut in October's ''Batman'' #49"</ref><ref>[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_C._Irvine Irvine, Alex] "1950s" in Dolan, p. 66: "''Batman'' #63 kicked off with the origin story of a new Batman villain: the Killer Moth."</ref>1953年，谢尔登·莫多夫成为另一个主要的蝙蝠侠”影子画师“，他与温·莫蒂默和迪克·肖普一起，在凯恩的监督下按照凯恩的风格，创作出了一些署名为鲍勃·凯恩的故事<ref name="ae59">{{Cite journal|title=Maybe I Was Just Loyal Longtime ''Batman'' artist Sheldon Moldoff talks about Bob Kane and other phenomena|last=Morris|first=Brian K.|date=June 2006|publisher=[[TwoMorrows_Publishing|TwoMorrows Publishing]]|issue=59|location=Raleigh, North Carolina|volume=3|pages=14–23}}</ref>1955年6月在第92期中比尔·芬格和莫多夫引入了蝙蝠猎犬艾斯。<ref>Irvine "1950s" in Dolan, p. 77: "Once Superman had a dog, Batman got one too, in "Ace, the Bat-Hound!" In the story by writer Bill Finger and artist Sheldon Moldoff, Batman and Robin found a German Shepherd called Ace."</ref>

=== 白银时代 ===
漫画迷和历史学家都知道蝙蝠侠在白银时代曾大量涉足科幻故事。<ref>Irvine "1950s" in Dolan, p. 88: "Throughout 1958 Batman encountered aliens from different planets and dimensions."</ref>此时期新加入的角色包括冷冻先生和最初代的蝙蝠女孩——贝蒂凯恩。<ref>Irvine "1950s" in Dolan, p. 92: "The Dynamic Duo battled the frosty foe Mr. Zero in a story written by Dave Wood and with art by Sheldon Moldoff in ''Batman'' #121...The 1960s ''Batman'' TV series, starring [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adam_West Adam West], included the character of Mr. Zero but renamed him Mr. Freeze. Later comic book incarnations of the ice-cold villain would adopt the new name."</ref><ref>McAvennie, Michael "1960s" in Dolan, p. 102: "Young Betty Kane assumed the costumed identity of Bat-Girl in this tale by writer Bill Finger and artist Sheldon Moldoff."</ref>

1964年，尤利乌斯·施瓦茨被任命为《蝙蝠侠》系列负责人。他抛弃了许多搞笑角色如蝙蝠女侠、蝙蝠猎犬、蝙蝠小子等。由《侦探漫画》第327期（1964年五月）开始，漫画封面标示了“新面貌”。<ref>McAvennie "1960s" in Dolan, p. 110: "The Dark Knight received a much-needed face lift from new Batman editor Julius Schwartz, writer John Broome, and artist Carmine Infantino. === 1980年代 ===
马福·沃夫曼曾在第331期(1981年1月)短暂地写过《蝙蝠侠》，并参与创作了闪电人。<ref>Manning, Matthew K. "1980s" in Dolan, p. 201: "Jason Todd first appeared in a circus scene in the pages of Batman #357, written by Gerry Conway and illustrated by Don Newton."</ref>之后托德在第368期(1984年2月)中担任罗宾。<ref>Manning "1980s" in Dolan, p. 207: "Jason Todd was proving himself as Batman's new partner in his war on crime...Dick [Grayson] then graciously passed the mantle of Robin to Jason, who eagerly adopted it."</ref><ref>{{Cite comic|Writer=[[Doug_Moench|Moench, Doug]]|writer=[[Doug_Moench|Moench, Doug]]|Penciller=[[Don_Newton|Newton, Don]]|penciller=[[Don_Newton|Newton, Don]]|Inker=[[Alfredo_Alcala|Alcala, Alfredo]]|inker=[[Alfredo_Alcala|Alcala, Alfredo]]|Story=A Revenge of Rainbows|story=A Revenge of Rainbows|Title=Batman|title=Batman|Issue=368|issue=368|Date=February 1984|date=February 1984}}</ref>编剧道格·莫许在第360期开始接手创作，他和画师汤姆·曼德雷克在《蝙蝠侠》第386期((1985年8月)中创造了黑面具这个角色。<ref name="Moench-GCD">{{Cite web|url=http://www.comics.org/search/advanced/process/?target=sequence&method=icontains&logic=False&keywords=&order1=series&order2=date&order3=&start_date=&end_date=&title=&feature=&job_number=&pages=&script=Doug+Moench&pencils=&inks=&colors=&letters=&story_editing=&genre=&characters=&synopsis=&reprint_notes=&story_reprinted=None&notes=&pub_name=DC&pub_notes=&brand=&brand_notes=&indicia_publisher=&is_surrogate=None&ind_pub_notes=&series=Batman&series_notes=&tracking_notes=&issue_count=&issues=&volume=&issue_title=&variant_name=&issue_date=&indicia_frequency=&price=&issue_pages=&format=&issue_editing=&isbn=&barcode=&issue_notes=&issue_reprinted=None&is_indexed=None|title=Doug Moench's run on ''Batman''|publisher=Grand Comics Database}}</ref><ref>Manning "1980s" in Dougall (2014), p. 145: "When Gerry Conway parted ways with the Caped Crusader, a new regular writer was needed for both titles. 在《无限地球危机》后DC漫画的连续性被改变了。旧角色有机会被重新介绍。虽然《蝙蝠侠》系列没有重启，但在蝙蝠侠404到407期连载的“第一年”故事线中法兰克·米勒与画家大卫·马索伽利合作为蝙蝠侠这个人物的起源作重新的设定。故事《蝙蝠侠:第一年》因其对蝙蝠侠起源的真实演绎以及对从未读过蝙蝠侠系列的新读者的易于理解而广受好评。<ref name="Number404">{{Cite comic|Writer=[[Frank_Miller_(comics)|Miller, Frank]]|writer=[[Frank_Miller_(comics)|Miller, Frank]]|Penciller=[[David_Mazzucchelli|Mazzucchelli, David]]|penciller=[[David_Mazzucchelli|Mazzucchelli, David]]|Inker=Mazzucchelli, David|inker=Mazzucchelli, David|Story=Chapter 1: Who I Am and How I Came to Be Batman: Year One|story=Chapter 1: Who I Am and How I Came to Be Batman: Year One|Title=Batman|title=Batman|Issue=404|issue=404|Date=February 1987|date=February 1987}}</ref> IGN漫画将“第一年”排在了前25位最伟大的蝙蝠侠漫画小说之首，称“在此之前或之后，没有任何一本书能如此完美地捕捉到戈登和蝙蝠侠的现实主义、勇气和人性。”<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://comics.ign.com/articles/624/624619p1.html|title=The 25 Greatest Batman Graphic Novels|accessdate=June 10, 2012|date=October 25, 2011|last=Goldstein|first=Hilary|publisher=[[IGN|IGN]]|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/68JaHVtUD?url=http://comics.ign.com/articles/624/624619p1.html|archivedate=June 10, 2012}}</ref> 著名的漫画作家格雷格·卢卡, 杰夫·勒布和朱迪·维尼克都把“第一年”作为他们最喜欢的故事。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/batman-creators-share-favorites-100610.html|title=''Batman'' #700 Bat-Creators Share Some Favorite Bat-Things|accessdate=June 10, 2012|date=June 10, 2010|last=Rogers|first=Vaneta|publisher=[[Newsarama|Newsarama]]|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/68Jak0wGK?url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/batman-creators-share-favorites-100610.html|archivedate=June 10, 2012}}</ref> 在“第一年”之后，编剧马克斯·艾伦·柯林斯和画师克里斯·华纳为杰森·托德创作了一个新的起源。<ref>Manning "1980s" in Dolan, p. 229: "No longer happy with Jason Todd's copycat origin of the original Robin, editor Denny O'Neil used the Earth-changing ''Crisis on Infinite Earths'' maxiseries...to retroactively alter the continuity of Robin's origin as well. === 1990年代 ===
部分受到1989年蒂姆•伯顿执导的电影《蝙蝠侠》的影响，20世纪90年代的漫画作品基调更为阴暗。蒂姆·德雷克版的罗宾在艾伦·格兰特和诺姆·布里福格尔创作的第457期(1990年12月)中得到了尼尔·亚当斯设计的新服装。<ref>Manning "1990s" in Dolan, p. 247: "In this tale by writer Alan Grant and artist Norm Breyfogle, Robin finally got a new uniform...When DC editorial made the decision to modify the classic costume of the iconic Boy Wonder, they called upon several artists to put their own spin on it. It was legendary artist Neal Adams who delivered the winning concept. "</ref>20世纪90年代，蝙蝠侠系列的主要作者是格兰特、道格·莫许和查克·狄克逊。莫许和狄克逊策划了骑士陨落的联动事件，让蝙蝠侠的背部被反派贝恩打断。<ref>Manning "1990s" in Dolan, p. 259: 'Knightfall' was a nineteen-part crossover event that passed through the pages of ''Batman'', by writer Doug Moench...[and] ''Detective Comics'' written by Chuck Dixon."</ref>而名为死亡天使的尚·保罗·范雷在布鲁斯·韦恩复健期间受托穿上蝙蝠装代替成为新的蝙蝠侠。但他过于嗜血暴力,韦恩复原后不得不强行收回了蝙蝠装。<ref name="Number510">{{Cite comic|Writer=[[Doug_Moench|Moench, Doug]]|writer=[[Doug_Moench|Moench, Doug]]|Penciller=[[Mike_Manley_(artist)|Manley, Mike]]|penciller=[[Mike_Manley_(artist)|Manley, Mike]]|Inker=[[Josef_Rubinstein|Rubinstein, Joe]]|inker=[[Josef_Rubinstein|Rubinstein, Joe]]|Story=Part Seven: Return of the Bat Knights End|story=Part Seven: Return of the Bat Knights End|Title=Batman|title=Batman|Issue=510|issue=510|Date=August 1994|date=August 1994}}</ref>莫许和画师凯利·琼斯在蝙蝠侠第535期（1996年10月）中共同创造了食人魔。<ref>Manning "1990s" in Dougall (2014), p. 224: "Writer Doug Moench and artist Kelley Jones introduced a pair of new villains into Batman's world with the Ogre and the Ape."</ref>

1999年开始了一个长达一年横跨所有蝙蝠侠相关漫画期刊的故事线《无主之地》，讲述受地震袭击后的哥谭市的故事。<ref>Manning "1990s" in Dolan, p. 287: "Numbering eighty-five comics officially labeled as part of the crossover...'No Man's Land' created a Gotham City never seen before."</ref>2000年，作家格雷格·卢卡将此故事改编成小说出版。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Batman: No Man's Land|last=Rucka|first=Greg|authorlink=Greg Rucka|date=2000|publisher=[[Pocket_Books|Pocket Books]]|isbn=0671038281|location=New York, New York|pages=448}}</ref>

=== 2000年代 ===

==== 2000 - 2003 ====
在“无主之地”结束后，格雷格·卢卡去负责《侦探漫画》，《蝙蝠侠》由编剧拉里·哈马和画师斯科特·麦克丹尼尔负责创作了七期。在第582期，艾德·布鲁贝克成为了《蝙蝠侠》的编剧<ref>Manning "2000s" in Dougall (2014), p. 252: "Ed Brubaker became a regular Batman scribe with this issue, partnering with artist Scott McDaniel."</ref> ，并开始了一个描写更真实的犯罪故事的趋势，其中包括一些更接地气的反派角色，如企鹅，新反派蔡司，以及死射。<ref name="Number582">{{Cite comic|Writer=[[Ed_Brubaker|Brubaker, Ed]]|writer=[[Ed_Brubaker|Brubaker, Ed]]|Penciller=[[Scott_McDaniel|McDaniel, Scott]]|penciller=[[Scott_McDaniel|McDaniel, Scott]]|Inker=[[Karl_Story|Story, Karl]]|inker=[[Karl_Story|Story, Karl]]|Story=Fearless Part 1|story=Fearless Part 1|Title=Batman|title=Batman|Issue=582|issue=582|Date=October 2000|date=October 2000}}</ref> 布鲁贝克为了写一个短篇故事“警察倒下”而中断了他蝙蝠侠连载的工作，这个短篇故事描述了戈登局长在执行任务时被枪击，最终从哥谭警察部队退役。在布鲁贝克回来之前，作家布莱恩·k·沃恩写了一份三期的短故事，重点关注了蝙蝠侠的伪装的犯罪角色火柴马龙。之后由布鲁贝克和鲁卡策划的联动事件名为“布鲁斯韦恩：凶手？”  布鲁斯·韦恩因谋杀女友而遭陷害，差点抛弃平民身份。

==== 2003 - 2006 ====
2002年末，第608期开始编剧杰夫·洛布与画家吉姆·李合作为期12期的“缄默”故事线；此故事成为自蝙蝠侠第500期后首次登上钻石漫画发行公司销售冠军的蝙蝠侠漫画。吉姆·李的线条扎实，配合宏大的场景和精美的电脑上色，画面效果极致奢华。<ref>Cowsill, Alan "2000s" in Dolan, p. 307: "The 'Hush' story arc [begun] in ''Batman'' #608 was artist Jim Lee's first major work since he joined DC...Written by Jeph Loeb, 'Hush' brought profound changes to the life of the Dark Knight."</ref>引入故事同名的新角色,以及重新定义了谜语人,治愈了哈维·登特,并对杰森·托德之死的相关事件提出了质疑。眩晕漫画《100发子弹》的创作团队在《蝙蝠侠》中开始《破碎之城》的六期故事。<ref name="Number620-625">{{Cite comic|Writer=[[Brian_Azzarello|Azzarello, Brian]]|writer=[[Brian_Azzarello|Azzarello, Brian]]|Penciller=[[Eduardo_Risso|Risso, Eduardo]]|penciller=[[Eduardo_Risso|Risso, Eduardo]]|Inker=Risso, Eduardo|inker=Risso, Eduardo|Title=Batman|title=Batman|Issue=620–625|issue=620–625|Date=December 2003 – May 2004|date=December 2003 – May 2004}}</ref><ref>Manning "2000s" in Dougall (2014), p. 269: "Editor Bob Schreck gave two more big name creators a shot at the Batman when he hired writer Brian Azzarello and artist Eduardo Risso for a six-issue noir thriller."</ref>编剧贾德·温尼克成为该系列的作者，并在一个题为“头罩之下”的故事中表明说，杰森·托德早已复活，并以红头罩为幌子下成为了哥谭的反英雄。<ref name="Annual25">{{Cite comic|Writer=[[Judd_Winick|Winick, Judd]]|writer=[[Judd_Winick|Winick, Judd]]|Penciller=[[Shane_Davis|Davis, Shane]]|penciller=[[Shane_Davis|Davis, Shane]]|Inker=[[Mark_Morales_(comics)|Morales, Mark]]|inker=[[Mark_Morales_(comics)|Morales, Mark]]|Story=Deadalus and Icarus The Return of Jason Todd|story=Deadalus and Icarus The Return of Jason Todd|Title=Batman Annual|title=Batman Annual|Issue=25|issue=25|Date=May 2006|date=May 2006}}</ref>

==== 2006 - 2009 ====

之后,主要的蝙蝠侠系列连载中断，直到短篇《披风争夺战》后迪克·格雷森成为新任蝙蝠侠,布鲁斯·韦恩从DC宇宙中消失。<ref name="BFTC3">{{Cite comic|Writer=[[Tony_Daniel|Daniel, Tony]]|writer=[[Tony_Daniel|Daniel, Tony]]|Penciller=Daniel, Tony|penciller=Daniel, Tony|Inker=[[Sandu_Florea|Florea, Sandu]]|inker=[[Sandu_Florea|Florea, Sandu]]|Story=Last Man Standing|story=Last Man Standing|Title=[[Batman:_Battle_for_the_Cowl|Batman: Battle for the Cowl]]|title=[[Batman:_Battle_for_the_Cowl|Batman: Battle for the Cowl]]|Issue=3|issue=3|Date=July 2009|date=July 2009}}</ref>格兰特莫里森一直参与写作蝙蝠侠,但是是在一个新的系列题为《蝙蝠侠和罗宾》,在此时期格雷森成为蝙蝠侠而达米安·韦恩担任新的罗宾。作家贾德·温尼克写了格雷森的第一个蝙蝠侠身份的故事，然后把编剧和画作责任交给了托尼·丹尼尔。<ref name="Issue687">{{Cite comic|Writer=Winick, Judd|writer=Winick, Judd|Penciller=[[Ed_Benes|Benes, Ed]]|penciller=[[Ed_Benes|Benes, Ed]]|Inker=[[Rob_Hunter|Hunter, Rob]]|inker=[[Rob_Hunter|Hunter, Rob]]|Story=A Battle Within|story=A Battle Within|Title=Batman|title=Batman|Issue=687|issue=687|Date=August 2009|date=August 2009}}</ref><ref name="Issue692">{{Cite comic|Writer=Daniel, Tony|writer=Daniel, Tony|Penciller=Daniel, Tony|penciller=Daniel, Tony|Inker=Florea, Florea|inker=Florea, Florea|Story=Life After Death Part 1: The Awakening|story=Life After Death Part 1: The Awakening|Title=Batman|title=Batman|Issue=692|issue=692|Date=Late December 2009|date=Late December 2009}}</ref>

=== 2010年代 ===
丹尼尔担任这个系列的主要编剧，直到第699期。随着《蝙蝠侠》第700期(2010年8月)的出版，《蝙蝠侠》达到了一个新的里程碑，格兰特·莫里森回归，并与丹尼尔、弗兰克·奎利特、安迪·库伯特和大卫·芬奇组成的画师团队合作。几个单独的故事联系在一起来说明蝙蝠侠的遗产是无穷无尽的，并且将会延续到最遥远的时间。<ref>Cowsill "2000s" in Dolan, p. 342: "Written by Grant Morrison with art by Tony S. Daniel, Andy Kubert, Frank Quitely, [David] Finch, and Richard Friend, this milestone issue of ''Batman'' featured an all-star roster of talent."</ref>莫里森直到第702期继续担任该剧的编剧，同时还在撰写《蝙蝠侠与罗宾》系列和一部短连载《布鲁斯·韦恩归途》。托尼·丹尼尔在第704期恢复了编剧和画师的职责。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/tony-daniel-batman-100819.html|title=Tony Daniel Returns to Writing with November's ''Batman''|accessdate=June 10, 2012|date=August 19, 2010|last=Rogers|first=Vaneta|publisher=Newsarama|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/68JbMIMK5?url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/tony-daniel-batman-100819.html|archivedate=June 10, 2012}}</ref>在布鲁斯·韦恩回归后，迪克·格雷森仍然担任了蝙蝠侠一年，同时也是《蝙蝠侠》、《蝙蝠侠与罗宾》和《侦探漫画》的主角。布鲁斯·韦恩则在两部新刊中担任蝙蝠侠：《蝙蝠群英会》和《蝙蝠侠:黑暗骑士》。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/batmen-coming-november-100830.html|title=Bruce Wayne and Dick Grayson Are Batman This Fall|accessdate=June 10, 2012|date=August 30, 2010|last=Doran|first=Michael|publisher=Newsarama|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/68JbfXgss?url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/batmen-coming-november-100830.html|archivedate=June 10, 2012}}</ref>

2011年6月1日，在闪点事件重启了DC宇宙之后，DC漫画宣布在DC宇宙中发行的所有系列要么被取消，要么被重新启动，发行新的第1期。蝙蝠侠也不例外，新系列的第一期于2011年9月21日发行。

==== 新52 ====
2011年9月，大事件闪点之后DC漫画宇宙重启。《蝙蝠侠》由斯科特·斯奈德担任编剧，画师则是DC新挖来的格雷格·卡普罗。<ref>Manning "2010s" in Dougall (2014), p. 320: "Scott Snyder was paired with superstar artist Greg Capullo for this new series."</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.comicsalliance.com/2011/06/06/new-dcu-batman-detective-batgirl|title=Batman Relaunch: New #1s for ''Batgirl'', ''Batman'', ''Detective'', ''Catwoman'', ''Birds of Prey''|accessdate=April 21, 2012|date=June 6, 2011|last=Khouri|first=Andy|publisher=[[ComicsAlliance|ComicsAlliance]]|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/676WaWjTf?url=http://www.comicsalliance.com/2011/06/06/new-dcu-batman-detective-batgirl|archivedate=April 22, 2012}}</ref><ref name="snyderlatimes">{{Cite news|url=http://herocomplex.latimes.com/2011/09/20/batman-and-swamp-thing-scott-snyders-dark-plans-for-dc/|title=''Batman'' and ''Swamp Thing'': Scott Snyder’s dark plans for DC|last=Walecka|first=Travis|date=September 20, 2011|work=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=April 1, 2012|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/66auoo0BD?url=http://herocomplex.latimes.com/2011/09/20/batman-and-swamp-thing-scott-snyders-dark-plans-for-dc/|archivedate=April 1, 2012}}</ref>重启后蝙蝠侠年龄回到了32岁，蝙蝠侠的历史被精简后压缩所在7年之内。除了斯蒂芬妮·布朗之外，所有曾担任过罗宾的角色都被保留仍然为蝙蝠侠效力。布鲁斯·韦恩作为唯一的蝙蝠侠出现，而迪克·格雷森也回到了夜翼的身份，出现在他自己的系列。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/sdcc-2011-dc-justice-league-110722.html|title=SDCC 2011: DC Comics ''Justice League'' Panel Live!|accessdate=June 10, 2012|date=July 22, 2011|last=Kistler|first=Alan|publisher=Newsarama|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/68Jc31h5K?url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/sdcc-2011-dc-justice-league-110722.html|archivedate=June 10, 2012}}</ref>

首个故事“猫头鹰法庭”，<ref name="Vol2Number1">{{Cite comic|Writer=[[Scott_Snyder|Snyder, Scott]]|writer=[[Scott_Snyder|Snyder, Scott]]|Penciller=[[Greg_Capullo|Capullo, Greg]]|penciller=[[Greg_Capullo|Capullo, Greg]]|Inker=[[Jonathan_Glapion|Glapion, Jonathan]]|inker=[[Jonathan_Glapion|Glapion, Jonathan]]|Story=Knife Trick|story=Knife Trick|Title=Batman|title=Batman|Volume=2|volume=2|Issue=1|issue=1|Date=November 2011|date=November 2011}}</ref>讲述了蝙蝠侠面对一个控制哥谭市数个世纪的秘密结社—猫头鹰法庭的故事。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/scott-snyder-batman-night-of-owls-120111.html|title=Snyder Spills Even More on Night of Owls, Batman's Future|last=Rogers|first=Vaneta|date=January 11, 2012|publisher=[[Newsarama|Newsarama]]|accessdate=June 3, 2012|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/688eWSJ99?url=http://www.newsarama.com/comics/scott-snyder-batman-night-of-owls-120111.html|archivedate=June 3, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.comicbookresources.com/?page=user_review&id=4852|title=Review ''Batman'' #10|accessdate=July 13, 2012|date=June 14, 2012|last=Zawisza|first=Doug|publisher=Comic Book Resources|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/698fdGqvK?url=http://www.comicbookresources.com/?page=user_review&id=4852|archivedate=July 14, 2012}}</ref>第二个故事线是“灭族之灾”，小丑认为蝙蝠侠因为蝙蝠家族的羁绊变得弱小了，于是回到高谭市同时攻击了蝙蝠家族的每一个成员。之后斯奈德回顾了蝙蝠侠的初始用“零年”重写了蝙蝠侠诞生的故事。之后的“终局”则延续了“灭族之灾”的故事，小丑终于对蝙蝠侠彻底失望，他决意终结两人间失去乐趣的关系，于是小丑历史上最为庞大的计划开始了。在“终局”之后蝙蝠侠与小丑双双重伤陷于地下，回归后的布鲁斯失忆了，但哥谭不能没有蝙蝠侠，于是戈登局长成为了代班蝙蝠侠。在蝙蝠侠连载的最后布鲁斯归来重新成为蝙蝠侠，而整个新52也已经进入尾声。

==== DC 重生 ====

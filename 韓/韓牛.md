'''韓牛'''(한우)，是[[歐洲|歐洲]][[牛|牛]]與[[韓國|韓國]]牛的[[混血|混血]]種。這種[[牛肉|牛肉]]在韓國最受歡迎，儘管價格高昂，但[[朝鲜|朝鲜]]人喜歡它們的程度多於便宜的[[美國|美國]]牛肉。<ref>''Early postmortem processing conditions on meat quality of Hanwoo (Korean Native Cattle) beef during storage.'', B.C. Kim, M.S. Rhee, Y.C. Ryu, J.Y. Imm, and K.C. Koh, ''Asian-Aust. J. Anim. Sci.'', Vol. 14(7), 2001, pp 1763-1768.</ref>這個問題使美韓兩國多次執拗，因為前者品質更好，更適合作[[韓國烤肉|韓國烤肉]]。韓牛在韩国[[飲食文化|飲食文化]]中有著重要地位。韓牛在韓國也是一種在節日、[[假日|假日]]贈送親朋好友的禮品。<ref name="Hur">[http://www.springerlink.com/content/357q780585504910/ A Comparison of the Meat Qualities from the Hanwoo (Korean Native Cattle) and Holstein Steer], Sun Jin Hur, Gu Boo Park and Seon Tea Joo, ''Food and Bioprocess Technology'', Springer New York, ISSN 1935-5130, Issue Volume 1, Number 2 / June, 2008, pp 196-200</ref> 

[[韓國|韓國]][[江原道|江原道]][[橫城郡|橫城郡]]是韓牛最大的產地。<ref>[http://www.donghaetour.org/cn/travel/read.asp?key_cate=172&seq=3078&key_region=5 橫城]{{dead link|date=2018年5月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

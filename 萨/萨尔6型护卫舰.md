'''萨尔6型护卫舰'''是[[以色列海军|以色列海军]]2015年5月订购的四艘护卫舰型号。

==概述==
原型基于德国的[[布伦瑞克级护卫舰|布伦瑞克级护卫舰]]，但做了一些设计修改。<ref>[http://www.defensenews.com/story/defense/naval/navy/2016/08/04/israel-submarine-ship-corvette-dolphin-germany/88061450 "Israeli Navy Marks Milestones at German Shipyards"], by Barbara Opall-Rome, August 4, 2016</ref> 

所有四艘都由{{tsl|en|German Naval Yards Holdings|德国海军造船厂}}与[[蒂森克虜伯海洋系統|蒂森克虜伯海洋系統]]联合制造。首艘计划2019年入役。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Azulai|first1=Yuval|title=Israel signs €430m deal for German patrol vessels|url=http://www.globes.co.il/en/article-israel-signs-430m-deal-for-german-patrol-vessels-1001035494|accessdate=14 May 2015|work=Globes|date=11 May 2015}}</ref>单价约4.3亿欧元。<ref name="i24news">{{cite web|url=http://www.i24news.tv/en/news/israel/diplomacy-defense/95544-151213-israel-to-arm-warships-with-iron-dome-system-to-protect-gas-reserves-at-sea|title=Israel to arm warships with Iron Dome system to protect gas reserves at sea|date=13 December 2015|work=i24news|accessdate=1 May 2016}}</ref>以色列政府承担三分之二造价，德国政府支付三分之一造价。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Zitun|first1=Yoav|title=Major deal: Israel to purchase four patrol ships from Germany to defend gas rigs|url=http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4656029,00.html|accessdate=14 May 2015|work=ynet|date=11 May 2015}}</ref> 

这些护卫舰将保护东地中海的天然气田。<ref>Yaniv Kubovich, [https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/1.832507 "Israel Assesses Hezbollah Can Now Strike Its Offshore Gas Platforms"], [[Haaretz|Haaretz]], 4 January 2018</ref>

排水量2,000吨，长90m。装备一座[[奧托梅萊拉76公厘艦炮|奧托梅萊拉76公厘艦炮]]、两套[[颱風武器站|颱風武器站]]、4X4[[闪电-8导弹|闪电-8导弹]][[舰对空导弹|舰对空导弹]]射程70km、4X10垂直发射[[鐵穹防禦系統|C-Dome]]点防御系统射程20km、4X4鱼叉[[反舰导弹|反舰导弹]]反射管、S波段{{tsl|en|EL/M-2248 MF-STAR}}[[電子掃描陣列雷達|電子掃描陣列雷達]]（前三后一）、两套324 mm鱼雷发射管。一架[[SH-60海鷹直升機|SH-60海鷹直升機]]。<ref>[http://www.navyrecognition.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=3029 Design of Future TKMS Built Saar 6 MEKO A100 Corvettes for Israeli Navy Unveiled] - Navyrecognition.com, 23 August 2015</ref>

'''萨希瓦尔燃煤电站'''，是位于巴基斯坦旁遮普省的一个[[火力發電廠|火力发电设施]]。由中国华能山东如意（巴基斯坦）能源有限公司建设运营。装机容量为1320兆瓦。于2017年7月3日全面投产<ref name=july>{{cite news|title=Second unit of Sahiwal power plant being inaugurated today|url=http://nation.com.pk/business/03-Jul-2017/second-unit-of-sahiwal-power-plant-being-inaugurated-today|accessdate=6 July 2017|publisher=The Nation|date=3 July 2017}}</ref>。

== 历史沿革 ==
2014年3月，为帮助缓解巴基斯坦的能源短缺，旁遮普省政府决定建设两座660兆瓦的发电厂，并开始对外投标<ref>{{cite news|title=PM performs ground breaking of Punjab's first coal power plant|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/1109535|accessdate=17 December 2015|publisher=Dawn|date=30 May 2014}}</ref>。2014年5月，萨希瓦尔燃煤电厂项目由中国企业得标。2015年4月，萨希瓦尔燃煤电厂项目被列入中巴经济走廊规划中<ref>{{cite web|last1=Liu|first1=Youliang|title=APPLICATION FOR A GENERATION LICENSE OF HUANENG SHANDONG RUYI (PAKISTAN) ENERGY (PRIVATE) LIMITED FOR ITS 2 X 660 MW IMPORTED COAL FIRED POWER PROJECT AT SAHIWAL, PAKISTAN|url=http://www.nepra.org.pk/Licences/Licence%20Application/2015/Generation%20License%20App%20of%20Hunaneg%20Shdong%20RUYI.PDF|publisher=NEPRA|accessdate=17 December 2015|page=90|date=9 March 2015}}</ref>。

萨希瓦尔燃煤电厂是由[[中国华能集团|中国华能集团]]和{{tsl|en|Shandong Ruyi|山东如意集团}}组成的联合财团建造的，华能集团将拥有51%的股份，山东如意将持有49%的股份<ref>{{cite web|last1=Liu|first1=Youliang|title=APPLICATION FOR A GENERATION LICENSE OF HUANENG SHANDONG RUYI (PAKISTAN) ENERGY (PRIVATE) LIMITED FOR ITS 2 X 660 MW IMPORTED COAL FIRED POWER PROJECT AT SAHIWAL, PAKISTAN|url=http://www.nepra.org.pk/Licences/Licence%20Application/2015/Generation%20License%20App%20of%20Hunaneg%20Shdong%20RUYI.PDF|publisher=NEPRA|accessdate=17 December 2015|page=235|date=9 March 2015}}</ref>。巴基斯坦政府将以8.3601美分/千瓦时的电价从萨希瓦尔燃煤电厂购买电力。该项目建立在[[BOT模式|BOT模式]]基础上，其中工厂的所有权将在运营30年后转让给旁遮普省政府<ref>{{cite news|title=Sahiwal coal power project: power to people|url=https://www.thenews.com.pk/print/106311-Sahiwal-coal-power-project-power-to-people|accessdate=1 April 2017|agency=The News|date=19 March 2016}}</ref>。

== 场地设备 ==
萨希瓦尔燃煤电厂位于巴基斯坦旁遮普省萨希瓦尔和奥卡拉坎顿之间，萨希瓦尔燃煤电厂位于连接这两个城镇的道路的北部。该发电厂是巴基斯坦第一座[[超臨界鍋爐|超临界燃煤电站]]，第一期工程由两座660兆瓦发电机组组成，总发电量为1320兆瓦<ref>{{cite news|title=Sahiwal coal power project will be functional by 2017: CM |url= http://www.dailytimes.com.pk/punjab/30-Sep-2015/sahiwal-coal-power-project-will-be-functional-by-2017-cm |accessdate=17 December 2015 |publisher=Daily Times |date=30 September 2015}}</ref>。未来的第二期工程将包括两个1000兆瓦的发电机组<ref>{{cite web |last1=Liu |first1=Youliang |title=APPLICATION FOR A GENERATION LICENSE OF HUANENG SHANDONG RUYI (PAKISTAN) ENERGY (PRIVATE) LIMITED FOR ITS 2 X 660 MW IMPORTED COAL FIRED POWER PROJECT AT SAHIWAL, PAKISTAN |url=http://www.nepra.org.pk/Licences/Licence%20Application/2015/Generation%20License%20App%20of%20Hunaneg%20Shdong%20RUYI.PDF|publisher=NEPRA |accessdate=17 December 2015 |page=109 |date=9 March 2015}}</ref>。
该项目占地690公顷，土地由旁遮普政府免费提供<ref>{{cite web|last1=Liu|first1=Youliang|title=APPLICATION FOR A GENERATION LICENSE OF HUANENG SHANDONG RUYI (PAKISTAN) ENERGY (PRIVATE) LIMITED FOR ITS 2 X 660 MW IMPORTED COAL FIRED POWER PROJECT AT SAHIWAL, PAKISTAN|url=http://www.nepra.org.pk/Licences/Licence%20Application/2015/Generation%20License%20App%20of%20Hunaneg%20Shdong%20RUYI.PDF|publisher=NEPRA|accessdate=17 December 2015|page=236|date=9 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Liu|first1=Youliang|title=APPLICATION FOR A GENERATION LICENSE OF HUANENG SHANDONG RUYI (PAKISTAN) ENERGY (PRIVATE) LIMITED FOR ITS 2 X 660 MW IMPORTED COAL FIRED POWER PROJECT AT SAHIWAL, PAKISTAN|url=http://www.nepra.org.pk/Licences/Licence%20Application/2015/Generation%20License%20App%20of%20Hunaneg%20Shdong%20RUYI.PDF|publisher=NEPRA|accessdate=17 December 2015|page=236|date=9 March 2015}}</ref>。该项目包括建造一条从优素福瓦拉村（Yusuf Wala）到该厂厂址的铁路专用线<ref>{{cite web|last1=Liu|first1=Youliang|title=APPLICATION FOR A GENERATION LICENSE OF HUANENG SHANDONG RUYI (PAKISTAN) ENERGY (PRIVATE) LIMITED FOR ITS 2 X 660 MW IMPORTED COAL FIRED POWER PROJECT AT SAHIWAL, PAKISTAN|url=http://www.nepra.org.pk/Licences/Licence%20Application/2015/Generation%20License%20App%20of%20Hunaneg%20Shdong%20RUYI.PDF|publisher=NEPRA|accessdate=17 December 2015|page=117|date=9 March 2015}}</ref>。

每个发电机组由一座锅炉、汽轮机和发电机组成，由次烟煤提供燃料，电厂所需燃料将在一个铁路专用车站卸载<ref>{{cite web|last1=Liu|first1=Youliang|title=APPLICATION FOR A GENERATION LICENSE OF HUANENG SHANDONG RUYI (PAKISTAN) ENERGY (PRIVATE) LIMITED FOR ITS 2 X 660 MW IMPORTED COAL FIRED POWER PROJECT AT SAHIWAL, PAKISTAN|url=http://www.nepra.org.pk/Licences/Licence%20Application/2015/Generation%20License%20App%20of%20Hunaneg%20Shdong%20RUYI.PDF|publisher=NEPRA|accessdate=17 December 2015|page=117|date=9 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Liu|first1=Youliang|title=APPLICATION FOR A GENERATION LICENSE OF HUANENG SHANDONG RUYI (PAKISTAN) ENERGY (PRIVATE) LIMITED FOR ITS 2 X 660 MW IMPORTED COAL FIRED POWER PROJECT AT SAHIWAL, PAKISTAN|url=http://www.nepra.org.pk/Licences/Licence%20Application/2015/Generation%20License%20App%20of%20Hunaneg%20Shdong%20RUYI.PDF|publisher=NEPRA|accessdate=17 December 2015|page=111|date=9 March 2015}}</ref>。这些电厂总发电量达1320兆瓦，总效率为42.11％，使用温度高达580摄氏度的超临界蒸汽发生器<ref>{{cite news|last1=Abrar|first1=Mian|title=Sahiwal Coal Power Project set to become first CPEC project to be completed|url=http://www.pakistantoday.com.pk/2016/05/07/business/sahiwal-coal-power-project-set-to-become-first-cpec-project-to-be-completed/|accessdate=8 May 2016|agency=Pakistan Today|date=7 May 2016}}</ref>。

萨希瓦尔燃煤电厂设置空气质量监测系统、烟气脱硫系统和[[静电除尘|静电除尘]]设备，以便减少来自该工厂的灰尘和硫排放<ref>{{cite news|last1=Abrar|first1=Mian|title=Sahiwal Coal Power Project set to become first CPEC project to be completed|url=http://www.pakistantoday.com.pk/2016/05/07/business/sahiwal-coal-power-project-set-to-become-first-cpec-project-to-be-completed/|accessdate=8 May 2016|agency=Pakistan Today|date=7 May 2016}}</ref>。该燃煤电厂需要60000立方米的水来运转，水是从巴里多布河下游抽取的。该工厂所需的水泥、沙子、木材等建筑材料在巴基斯坦本国采购<ref>{{cite news|last1=Abrar|first1=Mian|title=Sahiwal Coal Power Project set to become first CPEC project to be completed|url=http://www.pakistantoday.com.pk/2016/05/07/business/sahiwal-coal-power-project-set-to-become-first-cpec-project-to-be-completed/|accessdate=8 May 2016|agency=Pakistan Today|date=7 May 2016}}</ref>。

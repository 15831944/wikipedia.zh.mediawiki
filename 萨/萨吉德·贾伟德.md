'''薩吉德·賈偉德'''（{{lang-en|Sajid Javid}} {{post-nominals|country=GBR|PC|MP}}，{{bd|1969年|12月5日|||catIdx=J}}）；是[[英国保守黨|英国保守黨]]政治人物，前[[德意志银行|德意志银行]]董事总经理，现任[[英國下議院|英國下議院]][[布羅姆斯格羅夫_(英國國會選區)|布羅姆斯格羅夫議員]]、[[英国内政大臣|英国内政大臣]]，是为英国首位亚裔内政大臣。其内政大臣任内施政与其前任[[安珀·路德|安珀·路德]]相比较为软化。

賈偉德曾在2016年至2018年间任[[英国内阁|英国内阁]]{{le|社區及地方政府大臣|Secretary of State for Communities and Local Government}}、<ref>{{cite news |title=Coalition government and Labour reshuffle – the full lists|first=Paul|last=Owen|url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2013/oct/07/coalition-government-reshuffle-the-full-list |newspaper=The Guardian|date=7 October 2013|accessdate=7 October 2013}}</ref>2015至2016年任{{le|商业创新及技能大臣|Secretary of State for Business, Innovation and Skills}}、2014年至2015年擔任{{le|文化傳媒及體育大臣|Secretary of State for Culture, Media and Sport}}、2014年擔任{{link-en|婦女及平等事務部長|Minister for Women and Equalities|平等事務部長}}、2013年至2014年出任{{le|財政部金融秘書|Financial Secretary to the Treasury}}和2012年到2013年出任{{le|財政部經濟秘書|Economic Secretary to the Treasury}}。<ref>{{cite news |title=Coalition government reshuffle |first=Paul|last=Owen|url=http://www.theguardian.com/politics/2013/oct/07/coalition-government-reshuffle-the-full-list |newspaper=The Guardian |date=7 October 2013 |accessdate=7 October 2013}}</ref>

== 早年生活 ==

贾伟德出生在[[兰开夏郡|兰开夏郡]][[罗奇代尔|罗奇代尔]]的[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]裔家庭，兄弟五人。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.newstatesman.com/politics/2014/04/sajid-javids-father-would-never-have-made-it-camerons-britain|title=Sajid Javid's father would never have made it into Cameron's Britain|first=George|last=Eaton|work=New Statesman|date=14 April 2014|accessdate=22 November 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://new.spectator.co.uk/2013/01/im-still-a-thatcherite/|title=Interview with Sajid Javid, the bus driver's son who may end up leading the Tories|first=James|last=Forsyth|work=The Spectator|date=26 January 2013|accessdate=22 November 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151123033754/http://new.spectator.co.uk/2013/01/im-still-a-thatcherite/|archivedate=23 十一月 2015}}</ref>其父为巴士司机。后来全家从兰开夏郡搬到英国西南[[布里斯托尔|布里斯托尔]]的斯台普顿路，因其父母在那里开了家商店，一家人住在商店上层两居室的公寓里。<ref name=":1">{{Cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-43947008 |title=Who is Sajid Javid, the new home secretary? |date=30 April 2018 |work=''BBC News''|accessdate=30 April 2018}}</ref>这家商店地处当地最声名狼藉的地区，贾伟德曾开玩笑说小时候看到妓女就站在店外面。<ref name=javid-shop-anacdote>{{Cite news |url=https://www.thetimes.co.uk/article/sajid-javids-rise-caps-triumph-of-five-brothers-b09z3mf3d |title=Sajid Javid’s rise caps triumph of five brothers |date=6 May 2018 |work=''The Times''|accessdate=2 August 2018}}</ref>在[[撒切尔夫人内阁|撒切尔政府]]施行私有化政策之后，青少年时期的贾伟德对[[金融市场|金融市场]]产生了兴趣。十四岁时，他从银行借了500英镑投资[[股票|股票]]，还成为英国《[[金融时报|金融时报]]》的忠实读者。<ref name=":1" />

1981年至1986年，贾伟德在布里斯托尔附近的国立综合学院Downend School受教育，在2014年发表的演讲中，他承认自己在学校时“很顽皮，对观看《{{le|格兰其山|Grange Hill}}》比做作业更感兴趣”。<ref name=":1" />1986年入读{{le|菲尔顿技术学院|South Gloucestershire and Stroud College}}，1988年至1991年在[[埃克塞特大学|埃克塞特大学]]学习经济学和政治学，并成为保守党党员。<ref>[http://www.sajidjavid.com/text.aspx?id=78 Sajid Javid Biography] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715225824/http://www.sajidjavid.com/text.aspx?id=78 |date=2011-07-15 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.sajidjavid.com/upload/The%20Village.pdf Profile of Sajid Javid] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715225748/http://www.sajidjavid.com/upload/The%20Village.pdf |date=2011-07-15 }}</ref><ref>"[http://www.bristolpost.co.uk/news/bristol-news/11-bristol-celebrities-go-school-17024 Where did these 11 Bristol celebrities go to school?]",''Bristol Post'', 2 April 2017 (Accessed 4th April 2017)</ref>

20岁时，贾伟德第一次出席{{le|保守党年会|Conservative Party (UK) Conference}}，并且反对{{le|撒切尔政府|Premiership of Margaret Thatcher}}加入[[欧洲汇率机制|欧洲汇率机制]]（ERM）的决定，称之为“致命的错误”。<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=http://www.standard.co.uk/news/politics/treasury-minister-sajid-javid-dont-slam-the-city-it-represents-some-of-the-best-of-capitalism-8134667.html|title=Treasury minister Sajid Javid: Don’t slam the City, it represents some of the best of capitalism|work=The Evening Standard|accessdate=28 January 2015}}</ref>

大学毕业后贾伟德立即[[纽约市|纽约市]]加入[[纽约市|纽约市]]的[[大通银行|大通曼哈顿银行]]，主要在南美洲工作。1993年他作为兼职幕僚帮助[[鲁迪·朱利安尼|鲁迪·朱利安尼]]{{le|1993年纽约市市长选举|New York City mayoral election, 1993|竞选纽约市市长}}。<ref name=javid-usa-guardian>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2014/apr/11/sajid-javid-a-wonderful-life-banker-culture-minister| title= Sajid Javid's Wonderful Life – from investment banker to culture minister|date=11 April 2014|work=The Guardian|accessdate=17 August 2018}}</ref>25岁，{{efn|通常说法是25岁，但保守党官网则称24岁。<ref name="Wall Street">{{cite web |last=Herrmann |first=Joshi |title=What Thatcherite union buster Sajid Javid learned on Wall Street |url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2015/jul/15/sajid-javid-what-thatcherite-union-buster-learned-from-wall-street |website=The Guardian |accessdate=31 July 2018  |date=15 July 2015}}</ref>其他来源<ref>{{cite web |title=Sajid Javid - profile: the working class Conservative taking over from |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/politics/sajid-javid-profile-the-working-class-conservative-taking-over-from-maria-miller-as-culture-9248545.html |website=The Independent |accessdate=31 July 2018}}</ref><ref name= javid-telegraph-2015int>{{cite web |last1=Brown |first1=Mick |title=Sajid Javid: 'Plenty of British Muslims would agree we can no longer be held back by political correctness' |url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/politics/11509291/Sajid-Javid-Plenty-of-British-Muslims-would-agree-we-can-no-longer-be-held-back-by-political-correctness.html |accessdate=31 July 2018 |date=4 April 2015}}</ref>.}}他成为该银行历史上最年轻的副总裁。{{r|"Wall Street"}}<ref>{{cite web |last1=Nelson |first1=Fraser |title=Sajid Javid: the man who thinks big |url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/politics/conservative/10757640/Sajid-Javid-the-man-who-thinks-big.html |accessdate=31 July 2018 |date=11 April 2014}}</ref>{{efn|A 2012 article says he was actually vice-chairman.<ref name="Dale1012">Iain Dale [https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/politics/conservative/9589957/Iain-Dales-Top-100-most-influential-figures-from-the-Right-2012.html "Iain Dale's Top 100 most influential figures from the Right 2012"], ''The Daily Telegraph'', 7 October 2012</ref>}}1997年他回到伦敦工作，2000年加入[[德意志银行|德意志银行]]担任[[董事|董事]]。2004年，成为德意志银行的[[首席执行官|董事总经理]]，一年后担任其全球新兴市场结构负责人。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.db.com/medien/en/content/press_releases_2005_2697.htm|title=Deutsche Bank appoints Sajid Javid as Global Head of Emerging Markets Structuring|date=27 May 2005|work=db.com|accessdate=28 January 2015}}</ref>

2007年，贾伟德前往[[新加坡|新加坡]]任德意志银行亚洲区信贷交易、股票可转换债券、商品和私募股权业务负责人，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.db.com/medien/en/content/press_releases_2006_3217.htm|title=Deutsche Bank appoints Sajid Javid Head of Global Credit Trading, Asia|date=11 October 2006|work=db.com|accessdate=28 January 2015}}</ref>并兼任德意志银行国际有限公司董事会成员。2009年离开德意志银行进军[[政治|政坛]]。他在德意志银行的年收入到离职时约为300万英镑。<ref>{{Cite news |last1 = Donaldson |first1 = Kitty |last2 = Hutton |first2 = Robert |date = 9 April 2014 |title = U.K. Treasury's Javid Moves to Culture After Miller Quits |url = https://www.bloomberg.com/news/2014-04-09/u-k-treasury-s-javid-moves-to-culture-after-miller-quits.html |publisher = [[Bloomberg_L.P.|Bloomberg L.P.]] |accessdate = 9 April 2014 }}</ref>《伦敦标准晚报》称他从政后收入减少了98%。<ref name=guardiansjrand>{{cite news |last=Elgot|first=Jessica|date=30 April 2018|title=Who is Sajid Javid, the UK's new home secretary?|url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2018/apr/30/for-sajid-javid-the-hostile-environment-is-political-and-personal|newspaper=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|accessdate=6 June 2018}}</ref>

贾伟德是伦敦Early Years基金会的[[受托人|受托人]]，曾任Normand Croft社区{{le|学校校长|School governor}}，并率领了考察非洲最高山[[乞力马扎罗山|乞力马扎罗山]]的登山队，表示他对公益组织“{{le|帮助老年人|Help the Aged}}”的支持。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sajidjavid.com/text.aspx?id=60 |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2011-02-15 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715225443/http://www.sajidjavid.com/text.aspx?id=60 |archivedate=2011-07-15 }}</ref>

== 国会议员 ==
=== 成为保守党候选人 ===
2009年5月28日，时任[[布羅姆斯格羅夫_(英國國會選區)|布羅姆斯格羅夫選區]]国会议员、前影子文化大臣{{le|茱莉·柯克布賴德|Julie Kirkbride}}因卷入{{le|国会开支丑闻|United Kingdom parliamentary expenses scandal}}而宣布放弃在[[2010年英国大选|下次大选]]时连任。在她试图撤销辞呈未果后，其辞呈于当年12月被确认。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/news/politics/article6962201.ece |title=Julie Kirkbride to stand down at election after expenses scandal |date=19 December 2009 |last=Jagger |first=Suzy |publisher=The Times |accessdate=8 May 2010 |location=London}}</ref>

[[File:From_left_to_right_Stuart_Popham_and_Sajid_Javid_MP.jpg|thumb]]
2010年2月6日，布羅姆斯格羅夫選區的保守党协会就继任人进行了选拔，贾伟德以超过70%的选票的支持，被宣布为2010年大选的保守党国会议员候选人。在2010年5月6日举行的大选中，贾伟德以22,558张票赢得议席，多数票为11,308张。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk_politics/election_2010/england/8663175.stm |title=Tory wins health candidate Dr Taylor's Wyre Forest seat |date=7 May 2010 |publisher=BBC |accessdate=8 May 2010}}</ref>该选区的保守党候选人在[[2005年英国大选|上一次大选]]时获得的多数票为10,080张，<ref>{{cite news| url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/shared/vote2005/html/102.stm | work=BBC News | title=BBC NEWS – Election 2005 – Bromsgrove}}</ref>相比之下，他的前任获得的票数为24,387张，占全部选票的51.0%，而贾伟德只占43.7%。

=== 所受赞誉 ===

=== 商务大臣 ===
[[2015年英国大选|2015年]]，保守党完全赢得大选，贾伟德在[[卡梅伦第二政府内阁|新政府]]中担任{{le|商业创新及技能大臣|Secretary of State for Business, Innovation and Skills}}。他当时被描述为“内阁最强劲的右派”，一个“真正的撒切尔主义者”。<ref>{{Cite news|title = Conservatives: the party of business?|url = http://www.ft.com/cms/s/0/70295a84-c4f4-11e5-b3b1-7b2481276e45.html|newspaper = Financial Times|date = 3 February 2016|access-date = 8 February 2016|issn = |first = Jim|last = Pickard}}</ref>

贾维德被任命为商务大臣后表示，在保守党新政府领导下罢工的法律将会发生“重大变化”，影响基本公共服务的罢工将需要政府计划中符合资格的工会40％的成员的支持。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/business-32702585|title=Sajid Javid: Significant changes to strike law|accessdate=12 May 2015 | work=BBC News|date=12 May 2015}}</ref>

贾维德是英国留欧的支持者，尽管他把自己描述为“不想与欧洲更近一步”的欧洲人。<ref>{{cite news |last=Javid |first=Sajid |date=14 May 2016 |title=The only thing leaving the EU guarantees is a lost decade for British business |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2016/05/14/sajid-javid-the-only-thing-leaving-the-eu-guarantees-is-a-lost-d/|newspaper=Daily Telegraph |location=London |access-date=26 July 2016 }}</ref>

2017年2月，法庭透露，贾伟德不顾高级公务员的意见，不断向沙特阿拉伯颁发武器出口许可证，尽管沙特阿拉伯被指控领导[[沙特领导的干预也门行动|干预也门的行动]]是战争罪行。出口管制组织负责人爱德华·贝尔（Edward Bell）在2016年2月发出的的电子邮件是作为对英国对沙特阿拉伯销售武器的司法审查的一部分而被宣读的。电子邮件说：“说实话，我对[贾伟德]非常直率和诚实，我的直觉告诉我，我们应该暂停[对沙特阿拉伯的武器出口]”。在后来的一封电子邮件中，他说：“[贾伟德]决定不要在昨天晚上作出决定，现在已经与[首相]提出了这个问题。”<ref>https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/feb/07/official-advised-uk-minister-to-suspend-saudi-weapons-exports-court-hears</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
贾伟德和妻子劳拉与他们的四个孩子住在一起。他以前曾说过他的家庭传统信仰是伊斯兰教，但他本人并没有任何宗教信仰，虽然他认为“我们应该认识到基督教是我们国家的宗教”。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://villageonline.co.uk/village/news/fullstory/politics_in_the_pulpit |title=Politics in the pulpit |work=villageonline.co.uk|accessdate=28 January 2015}}</ref>

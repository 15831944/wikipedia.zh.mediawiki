穆罕默德·萨迪克·桑吉拉尼（{{lang-ur|{{Nastaliq|صادق سنجرانی}}}}，{{bd|1978年|4月14日||}}）是[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]政治家，现任[[巴基斯坦議會|巴基斯坦参议院]]第八届主席。2018年3月12日，他宣誓就任巴基斯坦参议院议员和主席。他是巴基斯坦参议院第一位来自[[俾路支省|俾路支省]]的主席，也是有史以来最年轻的主席。

== 早期生活和教育 ==
桑吉拉尼于1978年4月14日<ref name="dawn/12march2018">{{Cite news|author=Shahid|first=Saleem|title=Sadiq Sanjrani: a little-known politician from Chagai|url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1394762|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=[[Dawn_(newspaper)|DAWN]]|date=12 March 2018}}</ref>出生于俾路支省{{link-en|诺贡迪|Nok Kundi}}。<ref name="geo/12march2018">{{Cite news|title=Who is Senator Sadiq Sanjrani?|url=https://www.geo.tv/latest/185906-who-is-senator-elect-sadiq-sanjrani?5aa6142660b52|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=Geo|date=12 March 2018}}</ref><ref name="tribune/12march2018">{{Cite news|title=Opposition’s Sadiq Sanjrani elected 8th chairman of Senate|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/1657562/1-senate-oath-taking-ceremony-underway/|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=The Express Tribune|date=12 March 2018}}</ref>

他在诺贡迪接受了早期教育，然后搬到[[伊斯兰堡|伊斯兰堡]]，在那里获得了[[硕士|硕士]]学位。<ref name="dawn/12march2018">{{Cite news|author=Shahid|first=Saleem|title=Sadiq Sanjrani: a little-known politician from Chagai|url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1394762|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=[[Dawn_(newspaper)|DAWN]]|date=12 March 2018}}</ref>又有报道说他是在{{link-en|俾路支大学|University of Balochistan}}获得了文学硕士学位。<ref name="geo/12march2018">{{Cite news|title=Who is Senator Sadiq Sanjrani?|url=https://www.geo.tv/latest/185906-who-is-senator-elect-sadiq-sanjrani?5aa6142660b52|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=Geo|date=12 March 2018}}</ref>

== 政治生涯 ==
1998年，桑贾尼作为时任总理[[納瓦茲·謝里夫|納瓦茲·謝里夫]]团队的协调员开始了他的政治生涯，他在那一直工作到[[1999年巴基斯坦政變|1999年巴基斯坦政變]]。<ref name="geo/12march2018">{{Cite news|title=Who is Senator Sadiq Sanjrani?|url=https://www.geo.tv/latest/185906-who-is-senator-elect-sadiq-sanjrani?5aa6142660b52|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=Geo|date=12 March 2018}}</ref><ref name="dawn/12march2018">{{Cite news|author=Shahid|first=Saleem|title=Sadiq Sanjrani: a little-known politician from Chagai|url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1394762|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=[[Dawn_(newspaper)|DAWN]]|date=12 March 2018}}</ref>

2008年，他被任命为[[优素福·拉扎·吉拉尼|优素福·拉扎·吉拉尼]]总理秘书处投诉办公室的负责人，在那任职了五年。<ref name="geo/12march2018">{{Cite news|title=Who is Senator Sadiq Sanjrani?|url=https://www.geo.tv/latest/185906-who-is-senator-elect-sadiq-sanjrani?5aa6142660b52|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=Geo|date=12 March 2018}}</ref>

在{{link-en|萨努拉·汗·泽里|Sanaullah Khan Zehri}}担任俾路支省首席部长期间，桑贾尼仍然是他的特别助理。<ref name="dawn/12march2018">{{Cite news|author=Shahid|first=Saleem|title=Sadiq Sanjrani: a little-known politician from Chagai|url=https://www.dawn.com/news/1394762|accessdate=12 March 2018|work=[[Dawn_(newspaper)|DAWN]]|date=12 March 2018}}</ref>

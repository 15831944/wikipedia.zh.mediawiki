'''萨勒瓦·艾伊德·纳赛尔'''（{{lang-en|Salwa Eid Naser}}，{{bd|1998年|5月23日||}}）是一名巴林女子田径运动员，主攻[[400米赛跑|400米赛跑]]项目。她生于尼日利亚，在尼日利亚居住期间，她使用“{{lang|en|Ebelechukwu Agbapuonwu}}”这一名字<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.makingofchamps.com/2017/08/10/nigerian-born-salwa-eid-naser-wins-world-400m-silver-bahrain/|title=Nigerian-born Salwa Eid Naser wins World 400m Silver for Bahrain!|publisher=Making of Champions|date=2017-08-10|accessdate=2018-10-03}}</ref>。她的父亲是巴林人，母亲是尼日利亚人<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.iaaf.org/news/feature/salwa-eid-naser-400m|title=Naser takes a tip from George Michael and gets 400m gold in Cali|publisher=[[国际田径联合会|IAAF]]|date=2015-07-21|accessdate=2018-10-03}}</ref>。2014年，她获得巴林国籍，开始代表巴林比赛。同时她也将其名字改为现名，并开始信奉[[伊斯兰教|伊斯兰教]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://dailytimes.ng/ebele-agbapuonwu-becomes-salwa-naser-nigerian-athletes-dump-nigeria-droves/|title=Ebele Agbapuonwu becomes Salwa Naser – Nigerian athletes dump Nigeria in droves|date=2017-08-11|accessdate=2018-10-03|publisher=Daily Times}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vanguardngr.com/2017/10/salwa-eid-naser-tale-two-countries-yemi-olus/|title=Salwa Eid Naser and a Tale of Two Countries, By Yemi Olus|date=2017-10-28|accessdate=2018-10-03|publisher=Vanguard}}</ref>。纳赛尔对自己的新国籍很满意，在和她同样来自尼日利亚的归化巴林选手[[Kemi_Adekoya|Kemi Adekoya]]表达其对原来的国家的田径协会的不满的时候，纳赛尔称自己对于尼日利亚田径协会没什么意见<ref>{{cite web|url=https://stargist.com/news/salwa-naser-is-glad-she-chose-bahrain-ahead-of-nigeria/|title=Here's Why This Athlete Is Pleased She Dumped Nigeria For Bahrain|publisher=Stargist|date=2017-08-11|accessdate=2018-10-03}}</ref>。

'''韦莱镇'''（{{lang-my|ဝက်လက်မြို့နယ်}}）是[[缅甸|缅甸]][[实皆省|实皆省]][[瑞波县|瑞波县]]的一个镇區，位于{{link-en|亩河|Mu River}}和[[伊洛瓦底江|伊洛瓦底江]]之间的平原。

== 历史 ==
{{link-en|翰林城|Hanlin, Burma}}是[[骠国|骠国]]时期的古城之一，位于韦莱镇和翰林村附近。这座城市因火灾被毁<ref>http://www.mrtv3.net.mm/newpaper/2711newsn.pdf{{dead link|date=2018年5月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} Page 9 Col 1 [2017-11-18] {{en}}.</ref>。它建立于公元1世纪左右，直至七、八世纪左右，它仍然是骠国最大的和最重要的城市<ref>{{cite web|title=Ancient Burmese kingdom awarded World Heritage status |url=http://www.dvb.no/dvb-video/ancient-burmese-kingdom-awarded-world-heritage-status-myanmar-pyu/41755 |publisher=dvb |accessdate=2017-11-18 |language=英语}}</ref>。2014年6月，它成为位于[[缅甸|缅甸]]的首批[[世界遗产|世界遗产]]中的遗址之一<ref>{{cite web|title=Myanmar’s first site inscribed to World Heritage List |url=http://whc.unesco.org/en/news/1158 |publisher=[[联合国教育、科学及文化组织|UNESCO]] |accessdate=2017-11-18 |language=英语}}</ref>。

== 地理 ==
韦莱镇位于[[伊洛瓦底江|伊洛瓦底江]]东岸的{{link-en|亩河|Mu River}}和伊洛瓦底江之间的平原<ref name="MIMU001">[http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs6/MIMU001_A3_SD%20&%20Township%20Overview.pdf "Myanmar States/Divisions & Townships Overview Map"] Myanmar Information Management Unit (MIMU) [2017-11-18] {{en}}.</ref>。韦莱镇北部毗邻[[瑞波镇|瑞波镇]]，南部毗邻{{link-en|实皆镇|Sagaing Township}}，西部毗邻[[阿亚道镇|阿亚道镇]]<ref name="MIMU001"/>。

== 下辖村庄 ==
韦莱镇下辖超过七十个村庄。其中规模较大的村庄有安禅塔（{{lang|en|Aungchantha}}）<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/aungchantha/ "Aungchantha Map"] Maplandia</ref>、常米朵（{{lang|en|Chaungmido}}）<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/chaungmido/ "Chaungmido Map"] Maplandia</ref>、{{link-en|哈林唐博|Halin Taungbo}}<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/halin-taungbo/ "Halin Taungbo Map"] Maplandia</ref>、{{link-en|赫拉答夫|Hladaw}}<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/hladaw/ "Hladaw Map"] Maplandia</ref>、姆格宜（{{lang|en|Mugyi}}）<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/mugyi/ "Mugyi Map"] Maplandia</ref> 、穆因朵（{{lang|en|Myindaw}}）<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/myindaw/ "Myindaw Map"] Maplandia</ref>、 撒贝朵（{{lang|en|Sabedaw}}）<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/sabedaw/ "Sabedaw Map"]、Maplandia</ref>、{{link-en|萨当一村|Sadaung I, Wetlet Township}}<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/sadaung-22-26-0-n-95-50-0-e/ "Sadaung Map"] Maplandia</ref>、{{link-en|萨当二村|Sadaung II, Wetlet Township}}和{{link-en|耶瓦达|Ywatha, Wetlet Township}}<ref>[http://www.maplandia.com/burma/sagaing/wetlet/ywatha/ "Ywatha Map"] Maplandia</ref>。

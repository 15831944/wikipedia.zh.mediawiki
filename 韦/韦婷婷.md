
'''韦婷婷'''是一位中国的女权主义者、同性恋权利活动者<ref>{{Cite news|author=Ar|first=Zurairi|title=Malaysian transgender activist crowned ‘hero’ at regional LGBT awards|url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/malaysian-transgender-activist-crowned-hero-at-regional-lgbt-awards|accessdate=2015-11-02|date=2015-04-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151025151244/http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/malaysian-transgender-activist-crowned-hero-at-regional-lgbt-awards|archive-date=2015-10-25|dead-url=no}}</ref>和[[婦女權利|女性权利]]活动人士。

== 早期生活和职业生涯 ==
成长于中国南方农村，就读于[[武汉大学|武汉大学]]。根据《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》文章，她在大學二年級那年参与了一次「[[阴道独白|阴道独白]]」的演出，以挑起一些男同學的憤怒。她后来成为北京性健康教育研究会的项目经理。这期间她协助组织了在[[长城|长城]]上的年度艾滋徒步活动，出席了在印度和韩国的妇女会议，并且开始收集为有关中国[[双性恋者|双性恋者]]的纪录片材料<ref name="Jacobs">{{Cite news|author=Jacobs|first=Andrew|title=Taking Feminist Battle to China’s Streets, and Landing in Jail|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2015/04/06/world/asia/chinese-womens-rights-activists-fall-afoul-of-officials.html?_r=1|accessdate=2015-11-02|work=The New York Times|date=2015-04-05}}</ref>。她还担任北京一家LGBT权益组织“纪安德”的-{zh-tw:專案;zh-hk:計劃;zh-hans:项目}-主管<ref>{{Cite web|title=China: Arbitrary arrest and detention of nine women’s and LGBT rights defenders|url=https://www.fidh.org/en/region/asia/china/china-arbitrary-arrest-and-detention-of-nine-women-s-and-lgbt-rights|publisher=[[International_Federation_for_Human_Rights|International Federation for Human Rights]]|accessdate=2015-11-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304095626/https://www.fidh.org/en/region/asia/china/china-arbitrary-arrest-and-detention-of-nine-women-s-and-lgbt-rights|archive-date=2016-03-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

== 行动 ==
2012年情人节，韦婷婷和李婷婷参与了一个在北京的反家庭暴力抗议行動<ref name="Jacobs">{{Cite news|author=Jacobs|first=Andrew|title=Taking Feminist Battle to China’s Streets, and Landing in Jail|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2015/04/06/world/asia/chinese-womens-rights-activists-fall-afoul-of-officials.html?_r=1|accessdate=2015-11-02|work=The New York Times|date=2015-04-05}}</ref>。

'''萘锂'''是一种[[有机化合物|有机化合物]]，化学式为LiC<sub>10</sub>H<sub>8</sub>。[[锂|锂]]在苯中与[[萘|萘]]反应可以得到萘锂。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://link.springer.com/10.1007/s00289-016-1715-2|issue=2|doi=10.1007/s00289-016-1715-2|pages=307–323|title=Lithium naphthalenides in non-polar or in low-polarity media: some insights regarding their use as initiators in anionic polymerizations|volume=74|journal=Polymer Bulletin|date=2016-06-14|issn=0170-0839|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-23|author=Mario D. Ninago, María Loreta Sena Marani, Verónica A. González, Angel J. Satti, Claudia Sarmoria, Marcelo A. Villar, Enrique M. Vallés, Andrés E. Ciolino}}</ref>萘锂可以用作[[还原剂|还原剂]]，可溶于[[乙醚|乙醚]]、[[苯|苯]]和[[四氢呋喃|四氢呋喃]]。但它与四氢呋喃或质子溶剂在加热时反应。<ref>Short, Kevin M.; Wei, Alexander; Padungros, Panuwat. Lithium naphthalenide. ''e-EROS Encyclopedia of Reagents for Organic Synthesis'', 2014: 1-6. ISBN 978-0-470-84289-8</ref>

'''萘氮芥'''（{{lang-en|'''Chlornaphazine'''}}）是一种[[氮芥类|氮芥类]][[化疗|化疗]]药物，属于[[2-萘胺|2-萘胺]]的衍生物。它在20世纪50年代被研制出来，并用于治疗{{link-en|红细胞增多症|polycythemia}}和[[霍奇金氏病|霍奇金氏病]]。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1111/j.0954-6820.1954.tb11446.x | author = Videbaek, A.; Kaae, S. | title =  2-Naphthylbis(chloroethyl)amine in the treatment of malignant diseases, particularly Hodgkin's disease | journal = Acta Medica Scandinavica  | year = 1954 | volume = 149 | pages = 361–368 | pmid = 13180246 | issue = 5}}</ref> 但是，在接受萘氮芥治疗的病人中[[膀胱癌|膀胱癌]]的高发生率使得它不再被使用。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Benedicte Laursen | journal = Br Med J. | year = 1970 | volume = 3 | issue = 5724 | pages = 684–685 | title = Cancer of the Bladder in Patients Treated with Chlornaphazine | pmc = 1701774 | doi = 10.1136/bmj.3.5724.684 | pmid = 5470116}}</ref> 

[[国际癌症研究机构|国际癌症研究机构]]（IARC）将萘氮芥列为人类致癌物。<ref>[http://www.inchem.org/documents/iarc/suppl7/chlornaphazine.html N,N-Bis(2-Chloroethyl)-2-Naphthylamine (Chlornaphazine)], 国际癌症研究机构</ref>

'''兀兰体育场'''（{{lang-en|Woodlands Stadium}}），位于新加坡[[兀兰|兀兰]]规划区内，于1989年开放。球场可以容纳4050名观众。<ref name="Women Soccerway 2012"/>于1996年至2014年，兀兰体育场是[[新加坡职业足球联赛|新加坡职业足球联赛]][[兀蘭威靈頓足球會|兀蘭威靈頓足球會]]的主场。<ref name="TODAYonline 2016">{{cite web | title=New beginnings for Woodlands Wellington | website=TODAYonline | date=2016-09-09 | url=https://www.todayonline.com/sports/football/new-beginnings-woodlands-wellington | access-date=2018-07-22 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161114174907/http://www.todayonline.com/sports/football/new-beginnings-woodlands-wellington | archivedate=2016-11-14 | deadurl=no }}</ref>[[勇士足球會|勇士足球會]]也于2014-2016年间暂时使用该体育场作为主场（原本的主场[[蔡厝港体育场|蔡厝港体育场]]正在进行翻新）。<ref name="AsiaOne 2015">{{cite web | title=S.League: Uniformed giants move North | website=AsiaOne | date=2015-01-08 | url=http://www.asiaone.com/news/sports/sleague-uniformed-giants-move-north | accessdate=2018-07-22 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171023154917/http://www.asiaone.com/news/sports/sleague-uniformed-giants-move-north | archivedate=2017-10-23 | deadurl=no }}</ref>

==体育场设施==
球场拥有一个可供[[新加坡职业足球联赛|新加坡职业足球联赛]]举办赛事的草皮。体育场也有也有一条8赛道的[[400米|400米]]塑胶跑步道。<ref name="ActiveSG"/>体育场傍边还有游泳池，健身室，网球场等设施。<ref name="Building & Construction Authority 2013">{{cite web | title=Our Friendly Built Environment Portal | website=Building & Construction Authority | date=2013-04-02 | url=http://www.bca.gov.sg/friendlybuilding/FindBuilding/Building.aspx?Id=3858 | accessdate=2018-07-22 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180722051052/http://www.bca.gov.sg/friendlybuilding/FindBuilding/Building.aspx?Id=3858 | archivedate=2018-07-22 | deadurl=no }}</ref><ref name="ActiveSG1">{{cite web | title=Woodlands Sports Centre | website=ActiveSG | url=https://www.myactivesg.com/facilities/woodlands-sports-centre | accessdate=2018-07-22 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170714184528/https://www.myactivesg.com/facilities/woodlands-sports-centre | archivedate=2017-07-14 | deadurl=no }}</ref>

==体育场附近==
[[File:Woodlands_Stadium_OSM_Capture.png|thumb]]
体育场附近有一个邻里中心马西岭商场。主要的购物群是附近的居民。<ref name="BeritaHarian 2018">{{cite web | title=Pegerai, pelanggan lama jumpa semula di Marsiling Mall, Berita Setempat | website=BeritaHarian | date=2018-01-22 | url=http://www.beritaharian.sg/setempat/pegerai-pelanggan-lama-jumpa-semula-di-marsiling-mall | language=ms | accessdate=2018-07-22 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180722051053/https://www.beritaharian.sg/setempat/pegerai-pelanggan-lama-jumpa-semula-di-marsiling-mall | archivedate=2018-07-22 | deadurl=no }}</ref>附近其他设施包括兀兰镇公园（东部）<ref name="National Parks Board">{{cite web | title=Woodlands Town Park East | website=National Parks Board | url=https://www.nparks.gov.sg/gardens-parks-and-nature/parks-and-nature-reserves/woodlands-town-park-east | language=en | accessdate=2018-07-22 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151009022405/https://www.nparks.gov.sg/gardens-parks-and-nature/parks-and-nature-reserves/woodlands-town-park-east | archivedate=2015-10-09 | deadurl=no }}</ref>以及未来的兀兰警局。<ref name="TODAYonline 2015">{{cite web | title=Plans unveiled for new police division HQ in Woodlands | website=TODAYonline | date=2015-11-23 | url=https://www.todayonline.com/singapore/plans-unveiled-new-police-division-hq-woodlands | access-date=2018-07-22  | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171225094353/http://www.todayonline.com/singapore/plans-unveiled-new-police-division-hq-woodlands | archivedate=2017-12-25 | deadurl=no }}</ref>附近也建有一所[[人民行动党|人民行动党]]设立的大型托儿所。于2016年开幕，该大型托儿所的一项重点计划就是给予育儿机会接触体育活动。因此校方运用体育场的设施来进行体育课程。<ref name="TODAYonline 20151">{{cite web | title=PCF to open mega childcare centre in Woodlands next year | website=TODAYonline | date=2015-11-01 | url=https://www.todayonline.com/singapore/pcf-mega-childcare-centre-open-woodlands-2016  | accessdate=2018-07-22 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160401180626/http://www.todayonline.com/singapore/pcf-mega-childcare-centre-open-woodlands-2016 | archivedate=2016-04-01 | deadurl=no }}</ref>

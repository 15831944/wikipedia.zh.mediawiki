'''罩螺科'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''Tryblidiidae'''}}），是[[软体动物门|软体动物门]][[单板纲|单板纲]][[罩螺目|罩螺目]][[罩螺超科|罩螺超科]]的一個已滅絕的古生物科。

== 屬 ==
罩螺科（Tryblidiidae <small>von Zittel, 1899</small>）包括以下品種：
* [[罩螺屬|罩螺屬]] ''Tryblidium'' <small>[[Gustaf_Lindström|Lindström]], 1880</small>，從[[奧陶紀|奧陶紀]]到[[志留紀|志留紀]]<ref name="Dall">[[W._H._Dall|Dall W. H.]] 1913. ''Gastropods''. In: [[Charles_Rochester_Eastman|Eastman C. R.]] ''Text Book of Paleontology edited by C. R. Eastman adapted from German of K. A. von Zittel, 2nd edition revised and enlarged by the editor in collaboration with the following named specialists: R.S. Bassler, W. H. Dall, [[Charles_Doolittle_Walcott|C. D. Walcott]].'' [http://www.archive.org/details/textbookofpaleon01zittrich Volume 1]. MacMillan & Co., London. xii + 839 pp. [http://www.archive.org/stream/textbookofpaleon01zittrich#page/521/mode/1up Page 521].</ref>。
* ''[[Drahomira|Drahomira]]'' <small>Perner, 1903</small>
** ''[[Drahomira_barrandei|Drahomira barrandei]]''
** ''[[Drahomira_glaseri|Drahomira glaseri]]''
** ''[[Drahomira_kriziana|Drahomira kriziana]]''

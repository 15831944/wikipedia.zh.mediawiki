'''冈多法勒斯一世'''为巴基斯坦西部[[印度-帕提亞王國|印度-帕提亞王國]]的建立者<ref name="Bivar_2003">{{citation |last=Bivar |first=A. D. H. |chapter=Gondophares |title=Encyclopaedia Iranica |volume=11.2 |year=2003 |location=Costa Mesa |publisher=Mazda |chapter-url=http://www.iranicaonline.org/articles/gondophares}}</ref>。根据现代研究，其统治时间应该为公元前20年至前十年<ref>See main [[印度-帕提亞王國|Indo-Parthian]] page for references to Robert Senior's modern chronology</ref>。但{{tsl|en|Bopearachchi||Bopearachchi}}认为应当是公元21年至40年<ref>{{tsl|en|Osmund Bopearachchi||Osmund Bopearachchi}}, 2007, [https://www.academia.edu/22821570/Some_observations_on_the_chronology_of_the_early_Kushans Some observations on the chronology of the early Kushans]</ref>。

==统治==
通常所给的都是后一个日期。根据巴基斯坦[[马尔丹|马尔丹]]附近的[[塔克特依巴依|塔克特依巴依]]佛院一块由冈多法勒斯铭刻的石头记述，他于公元20年即位<ref>A. D. H. Bivar, "The History of Eastern Iran", in Ehsan Yarshater (ed.), ''The Cambridge History of Iran,'' Vol.3 (1), ''The Seleucid, Parthian and Sasanian Periods,'' London, Cambridge University Press, 1983, p.197.</ref>。

冈多法勒斯自[[印度-塞西亞王國|印度-塞西亞王國]]的{{tsl|en|Azes|阿泽斯}}手中夺取了[[喀布尔|喀布尔]]谷、[[旁遮普地区|旁遮普地区]]及[[信德省|信德省]]。事实上，还有许多附庸国倒向了冈多法勒斯。其王国疆域虽广阔，但是只维系在一个松散的框架内，在其死后即开始瓦解。王国都城为犍陀罗城市[[塔克西拉|塔克西拉]]<ref>B. N. Puri, “The Sakas and Indo-Parthians”, in A.H. Dani, V. M. Masson, Janos Harmatta, C. E. Boaworth, ''History of Civilizations of Central Asia,'' Motilal Banarsidass Publ., 2003, Chapter 8, p.196</ref>。塔克西拉位于旁遮普，今日伊斯兰堡以西。

恩斯特·埃米尔·赫兹菲尔德认为，冈多法勒斯代表着{{tsl|en|House of Suren|苏伦家族}}<ref>Ernst Herzfeld, ''Archaeological History of Iran,'' London, Oxford University Press for the British Academy, 1935, p.63;  name="Bivar_2003"/><sup> ''cf.'' </sup> name="Bivar_1983_51">{{citation |title=Cambridge History of Iran |volume=3.1 |year=1983 |publisher=Cambridge UP |location=London |editor-last=Yarshater |editor-first=Ehsan |chapter=The Political History of Iran under the Arsacids |last=Bivar |first=A. D. H. |pages=51<!--21–100-->}}</ref>.

==圣经人物“Gaspar”==
“冈多法勒斯”（Gondophares）翻译作亚美尼亚语为“Gastaphar”，再转换为西方语言则为“Gaspar[d]”，他可能就是基督传说中在耶稣出生时前去探望的[[東方三博士|東方三博士]]之一——“Caspar”<ref>Alfred von Gutschmid, Die Königsnamen in den apokryphen Apostelgeschichten, in the ''Rheinisches Museum für Philologie'' (1864), XIX, 161-183, nb p.162; Mario Bussagli, "L'art du Gandhara", p.207</ref>。

==与使徒多馬及阿波罗尼乌斯的关系==
[[File:Gondophares_receives_a_letter_from_St,_Thomas.jpg|thumb]]
在伪经《{{tsl|en|Acts of Thomas|托马斯行记}}》中提及一位叫做“Gudnaphar”的国王，许多学者都将其与冈多法勒斯一世联系在一起，因为并无确定其他王国有同名的国王<ref>Richard N. Frye, “The Fall of the Graeco-Bactrians: Sakas and Indo-Parthians”, in Sigfried J. de Laet, ''History of Humanity,'' London, New York and Paris, Routledge and Unesco, Volume III, 1996, Joachim Herrmann and Erik Zürcher (eds.), ''From the Seventh Century BC to the Seventh Century AD,'' p.455.</ref>。A. D. H. Bivar则提及塔克特依巴依所记录冈多法勒斯在位的时间与[[多馬_(使徒)|使徒多马]]所记录的时间相同（公元30年）<ref>W. Wright (transl.), ''The Apocryphal Acts of Thomas,'' Leiden, Brill, 1962, p.146; cited in A. D. H. Bivar, "The History of Eastern Iran", in Ehsan Yarshater (ed.), ''The Cambridge History of Iran,'' Vol.3 (1), ''The Seleucid, Parthian and Sasanian Periods,'' London, Cambridge University Press, 1983, p.197.</ref><ref>India and the Apostle Thomas, A. E. Medlycott, fully reproduced with illustrations (including the coins of Gondaphares) in the Indian Church History Classics ed. George Menachery, Ollur, 1998</ref>。

古希腊哲学家[[提亚纳的阿波罗尼乌斯|阿波罗尼乌斯]]提及了其在43至44年拜访塔克西拉的情景，并提到冈多法勒斯有一个希腊名字——Phraotes<ref>Puri, “The Sakas and Indo-Parthians”, p.197.</ref>。

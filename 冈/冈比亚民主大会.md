'''冈比亚民主大会'''是冈比亚的一个政党，由前[[爱国再建同盟|爱国再建同盟]][[冈比亚国民议会|国民议会]]成员[[曼马·坎德|曼马·坎德]]领导。它成立于2016年，是{{le|2017年冈比亚议会选举|2017 Gambian parliamentary election|2017年议会选举}}的常任候选人。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://fatunetwork.net/2763-2/ |title=Mamma Kandeh's Gambia Democratic Congress Finally Joins the Fray |date=9 April 2016 |work=Fatu Network}}</ref>成立后，坎德环游了冈比亚，吸引了“庞大”的人群。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://sidisanneh.blogspot.com/2016/07/gambia-democratic-congress-gdc-new-kid.html |title=Gambia Democratic Congress (GDC) : The new kid on the block |date=15 July 2016 |author=Sidi Sanneh |authorlink=Sidi Moro Sanneh}}</ref>最初的副领袖法鲁·西拉在后来与坎德发生争执后离开了党。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.whatson-gambia.com/news/headline-news/1472-what-s-wrong-with-mamma-kandeh-s-gdc |title=What's wrong with Mamma Kandeh's GDC? |date=13 July 2016 |work=What's On Gambia}}</ref>

'''雷氏不連續面'''（'''Lehmann discontinuity'''）是指在地球的[[地核|地核]]中地震波[[P波|P波]]和[[S波|S波]]突然加速的區域，該區域一般在5155±30公里深處，該不連續面由丹麥女性[[地震學|地震學]]家[[英格·雷曼|英格·雷曼]]發現並以其姓氏命名<ref name=Lowrie>該區域厚度約220公里，參見

{{cite book |title=Fundamentals of geophysics |author=William Lowrie |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=7vR2RJSIGVoC&pg=PA158 |page=158 |isbn=0-521-46728-4 |year=1997 |publisher=Cambridge University Press}}

</ref>。

==概要==
雷氏不連續面在大陸地殼下存在，但在海洋地殼的大多數區域以下不存在<ref name=Stixrude>

{{cite journal |url=http://supercronopio.es.ucl.ac.uk/~crlb/RESEARCH/PAPERS/StixrudeCLB05a.pdf |quote=The first possible explanation is that the Lehmann is not a global feature...the Lehmann is more prevalent under continents and may be absent under all or most of the oceans. |title=Mineralogy and elasticity of the oceanic upper mantle: Origin of the low-velocity zone |author=Lars Stixrude and Carolina Lithgow-Bertolloni |journal=J Geophys. Res. |volume=110 |doi=10.1029/2004JB002965 |year=2005 |page=B03204 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110830060832/http://supercronopio.es.ucl.ac.uk/~crlb/RESEARCH/PAPERS/StixrudeCLB05a.pdf |archivedate=2011-08-30 }}

</ref>，並且在全球性的平均研究中不容易出現。關於地震波速度增加的現象已有數個解釋提出，例如它的下限和有彈性的[[軟流圈|軟流圈]]相連，[[相變|相變]]造成震波速度變化<ref name= Condie>

{{cite book |title=Plate tectonics and crustal evolution |author=Kent C. Condie |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=HZrA6OQzsvgC&pg=PA123 |page=123 |isbn=0-7506-3386-7 |publisher=Butterworth-Heinemann |year=1997 |edition=4rth}}

</ref>；而目前認為最可能的是S波在深度變化時的[[各向异性|非均向性]] <ref name=Savage>

{{cite book |title=Vertical coupling and decoupling in the lithosphere; ''Volume 227 of special publications'' |page=14 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=SBs3o5uWeicC&pg=PA14 |author=MK Savage, KM Fischer CE Hall |chapter=Strain modelling, seismic anisotropy and coupling at strike-slip boundaries... |editor=John Gocott |isbn=1-86239-159-9 |publisher=Geological Society |year=2004}}
  
</ref>。相關細節請參見[[唐戸俊一郎|唐戸俊一郎]]著作<ref name=Karato>
{{cite book |title=Deformation of earth materials: an introduction to the rheology of solid earth |author=Shunʼichirō Karato |url=http://books.google.ru/books/about/Deformation_of_earth_materials.html?id=rZC7osgnyyYC&redir_esc=y |page=318 |isbn=0-521-84404-5 |year=2008 |publisher=Cambridge University Press}}
</ref>。

'''實驗哲學'''（Experimental Philosophy）是一種新的[[哲學|哲學]]流派，旨在結合實證研究與傳統的哲學研究方法。<ref>Lackman, Jon. [http://www.slate.com/id/2137223/ The X-Philes Philosophy meets the real world], ''Slate'', March 2, 2006.</ref><ref>Appiah, Anthony. [http://www.nytimes.com/2007/12/09/magazine/09wwln-idealab-t.html?_r=1 The New New Philosophy], ''[[New_York_Times|New York Times]]'', December 9, 2007.</ref><ref>Appiah, Anthony. [http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=17815241 The 'Next Big Thing' in Ideas], ''[[National_Public_Radio|National Public Radio]]'', January 3, 2008.</ref><ref name="Against Intuition">Shea, Christopher. [http://chronicle.com/free/v55/i06/06b00901.htm Against Intuition], ''[[Chronicle_of_Higher_Education|Chronicle of Higher Education]]'', October 3, 2008.</ref><ref name="Philosophy's great experiment">Edmonds, David and Warburton, Nigel. [http://www.prospectmagazine.co.uk/2009/03/philosophysgreatexperiment/ Philosophy's great experiment] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090930153840/http://www.prospectmagazine.co.uk/2009/03/philosophysgreatexperiment/ |date=2009-09-30 }}, [[Prospect_(magazine)|''Prospect'']], March 1, 2009</ref> 實驗哲學家利用[[認知科學|認知科學]]的方法來了解普通人對於一些基礎哲學問題的想法。<ref>[http://www.experimentalphilosophy.org/ExperimentalPhilosophy.html The Experimental Philosophy Page] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150405015929/http://www.experimentalphilosophy.org/ExperimentalPhilosophy.html |date=2015-04-05 }}.</ref><ref>Prinz, J. [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CS4DdLikfPk Experimental Philosophy], ''YouTube'' September 17, 2007.</ref><ref>Knobe, Joshua. [http://pantheon.yale.edu/~jk762/ExperimentalPhilosophy.pdf What is Experimental Philosophy?] ''[[The_Philosophers'_Magazine|The Philosophers' Magazine]]'' (28) 2004.</ref><ref>Knobe, Joshua and Nichols, Shaun. [http://pantheon.yale.edu/~jk762/manifesto.pdf An Experimental Philosophy Manifesto], in Knobe & Nichols (eds.) [http://www.amazon.com/Experimental-Philosophy-Joshua-Knobe/dp/0195323262 ''Experimental Philosophy''] 2008.</ref>

實驗哲學家認為，經由瞭解哲學直覺（philosophical intuitions）的心智背景，他們的實驗結果可以間接影響哲學研究。<ref>Knobe, Joshua and Nichols, Shaun. [http://pantheon.yale.edu/~jk762/manifesto.pdf An Experimental Philosophy Manifesto], in Knobe & Nichols (eds.) [http://www.amazon.com/Experimental-Philosophy-Joshua-Knobe/dp/0195323262 ''Experimental Philosophy''], § 2.1. 2008.</ref> 這對於實驗結果的依靠被廣泛的認為是反對於一種只依靠於先驗証明的哲學研究方法，有時被稱為"扶手椅"哲学（"armchair" philosophy）。<ref name="Against Intuition"/><ref name="Philosophy's great experiment"/> 哲學家對於實驗哲學的影響有不同的認見，而也有一些哲學家提出對於實驗哲學的批評。<ref>Kauppinen, A. (2007). [http://www.helsinki.fi/~amkauppi/phil/The_Rise_and_Fall_of_Experimental_Philosophy.pdf The Rise and Fall of Experimental Philosophy.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110805040203/http://www.helsinki.fi/~amkauppi/phil/The_Rise_and_Fall_of_Experimental_Philosophy.pdf |date=2011-08-05 }} Philosophical Explorations 10 (2), pp. 95–118.</ref><ref>Ludwig, K. (2007). [http://www.clas.ufl.edu/users/ludwig/mws2007ludwig.pdf The Epistemology of Thought Experiments: First vs. Third Person Approaches] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120310050716/http://www.clas.ufl.edu/users/ludwig/mws2007ludwig.pdf |date=2012-03-10 }}. Midwest Studies in Philosophy. 31:128-159.</ref><ref>Cullen, S. (forthcoming). [http://discursively.org/x-phi/survey-driven-romanticism.pdf Survey-Driven Romanticism.] European Review of Philosophy, 9.</ref>

最早的實驗哲學是專注於關於跨文化差異（cross-cultural difference）<ref>Weinberg, J., Nichols, S., & Stich, S. (2001). [http://ruccs.rutgers.edu/ArchiveFolder/Research%20Group/Publications/NEI/NEIPT.html Normativity and Epistemic Intuitions.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110803080118/http://ruccs.rutgers.edu/ArchiveFolder/Research%20Group/Publications/NEI/NEIPT.html |date=2011-08-03 }} ''Philosophical Topics'' 29, pp. 429–460.</ref><ref>Machery, E., Mallon, R., Nichols, S., & Stich, S. (2004). [http://www.rci.rutgers.edu/~stich/Publications/Papers/SemanticIntuitions.pdf Semantics, Cross-Cultural Style]. ''Cognition'' 92, pp. B1-B12.</ref> ，[[自由意志|自由意志]] <ref>Nahmias,E., Morris, S., Nadelhoffer, T. & Turner, J. [http://www2.gsu.edu/~phlean/papers/Surveying_Freedom.pdf Surveying Freedom: Folk Intuitions about Free Will and Moral Responsibility] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091122173645/http://www2.gsu.edu/~phlean/papers/Surveying_Freedom.pdf |date=2009-11-22 }}. ''Philosophical Psychology'' (18) 2005 p.563</ref>，與[[行動理論|行動理論]] <ref>Knobe, J. (2003a). [http://pantheon.yale.edu/~jk762/Side-Effect.pdf Intentional action and side effects in ordinary language,] ''Analysis'' 63, pp. 190–193.</ref><ref>Shaun Nichols & Joseph Ulatowski (2007). [http://www3.interscience.wiley.com/journal/117998150/abstract?CRETRY=1&SRETRY=0 Intuitions and Individual Differences: The Knobe Effect Revisited.]{{Dead link|date=2019年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} Mind & Language 22 (4):346–365.</ref> 的問題。現在，實驗哲學已被擴大到新的領域。

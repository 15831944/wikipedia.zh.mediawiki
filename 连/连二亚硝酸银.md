'''连二亚硝酸银'''是一种[[离子化合物|离子化合物]]，化学式为{{chem|Ag|2|N|2|O|2}}，或表示为({{chem|Ag|+}})<sub>2</sub>[ON=NO]<sup>2−</sup>。它是难溶于水和有机溶剂（如DMF、DMSO）的亮黄色固体。<ref name="tram">Trambaklal Mohanlal Oza, Rajnikant Hariprasad Thaker (1955), "The Thermal Decomposition of Silver Hyponitrite". Journal of the American Chemical society, volume 77, issue 19, pages 4976–4980. {{doi|10.1021/ja01624a007}}</ref><ref name="mend">G. David Mendenhall (1974), "Convenient synthesis of silver hyponitrite". Journal of the American Chemical society, volume 96, issue 15, page 5000. {{doi|10.1021/ja00822a054}}</ref><ref name = "Wiberg&Holleman">{{cite book|first1=Egon |last1=Wiberg |first2=Arnold Frederick |last2=Holleman|date=2001 |title=Inorganic Chemistry |publisher=Elsevier |ISBN=0-12-352651-5}}</ref>

==制备==
该化合物最早于1848被报道。<ref name="phil">(1848), "On the formation of hyponitrite of silver". Philosophical Magazine Series 3, XIII. Intelligence and miscellaneous articles, volume 33 (1848), issue 219, page 75. {{doi|10.1080/14786444808646049}}</ref>

它可由[[连二亚硝酸钠|连二亚硝酸钠]]和[[硝酸银|硝酸银]]在水溶液中反应得到：<ref name="mend"/>
: Na<sub>2</sub>N<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub> + 2AgNO<sub>3</sub> → Ag<sub>2</sub>N<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub> + 2NaNO<sub>3</sub>
过量的硝酸银会生成棕色或黑色沉淀。<ref name="tram"/><ref name="mend"/>

[[钠汞齐|钠汞齐]]还原[[硝酸银|硝酸银]]也能得到连二亚硝酸银。<ref name="masa">Masatsugu Sekiguchi, Michio Kobayashi, Hiroshi Minato (1974), "Reactions between Acyl Halides and Silver Hyponitrite". Bulletin of the Chemical Society of Japan, volume 45, issue 9, pages 2932-2934. {{doi|10.1246/bcsj.45.2932}}</ref>
: 2 AgNO<sub>3</sub> + 2 NaNO<sub>2</sub> + 4 Na[Hg] + 2 H<sub>2</sub>O → Ag<sub>2</sub>N<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub> + 2 NaNO<sub>3</sub> + 4 NaOH + 4 Hg<ref>无机化学丛书 第四卷 氮 磷 砷分族. 科学出版社. pp 122-123. 5.5 连二亚硝酸</ref>

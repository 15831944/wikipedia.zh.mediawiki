'''连二亚硫酸'''是一种硫的含氧酸，化学式为H<sub>2</sub>S<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub>。它的游离态不稳定，<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1021/jp972658d | title = Structures and Energies of Various Isomers of Dithionous Acid, H2S2O4, and of Its Anion HS2O4- 1 | year = 1998 | last1 = Drozdova | first1 = Yana | last2 = Steudel | first2 = Ralf | last3 = Hertwig | first3 = Roland H. | last4 = Koch | first4 = Wolfram | last5 = Steiger | first5 = Thomas | journal = The Journal of Physical Chemistry A | volume = 102 | issue = 6 | pages = 990}}</ref>但是它的[[盐|盐]]能够稳定存在。

最初化学家通过[[量子化学从头计算|量子化学从头计算]]得出''C''<sub>2</sub>对称性的结构HOS(=O)-S(=O)OH是分子式为{{chem|H|2|S|2|O|4}}的化合物中最稳定的。<ref>Drozdova, Y.; Steudel, R.; Hertwig, R. H.; Koch, W.; Steiger, T.  ''J. Phys. Chem. A'' '''1998''', ''102'', 990-996. DOI: [http://dx.doi.org/10.1021/jp972658d 10.1021/jp972658d]</ref>这是由于该分子中存在分子间氢键。现在已经知道连二亚硫酸自发分解为SO<sub>2</sub>和S(OH)<sub>2</sub>。

它的盐当中[[连二亚硫酸钠|连二亚硫酸钠]]用途较为广泛。

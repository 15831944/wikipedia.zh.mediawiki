在人類解剖學中，'''翼腭窝'''（{{lang|en|pterygopalatine fossa}}，或{{lang|en|sphenopalatine fossa}}）為人類顱骨中一個窩狀構造。翼腭窝共有一對，為[[颞下窝|颞下窝]]内侧的一個圓錐狀凹窩。該窩位於[[上颌骨|上颌骨]]後方<ref>Illustrated Anatomy of the Head and Neck, Fehrenbach and Herring, Elsevier, 2012, page 69</ref>。翼顎窩位於{{le|翼上頜裂|pterygomaxillary fissure}}內側，通往{{le|蝶顎孔|sphenopalatine foramen}}，可通向鼻腔、口腔、顳下窩、眼眶，及咽，另有8個孔通向中顱窩<ref>{{Cite journal
| last       = Osborn | first = Anne |date=March 1979
| title      = Radiology of the Pterygoid Plates and Pterygopalatine Fossa
| journal    = AJR | volume = 132 | issue = 3 | pages = 389–394
| url        = http://www.ajronline.org/content/132/3/389.full.pdf
| accessdate = December 2012 | pmid = 106641 | doi=10.2214/ajr.132.3.389}}</ref>。

翼腭窝向外通颞下窝，向前藉[[眶下裂|眶下裂]]通[[眶面|眶面]]，向内藉腭骨于蝶骨围成的[[蝶腭孔|蝶腭孔]]通[[鼻腔|鼻腔]]，向后藉[[圆孔|圆孔]]通[[颅中窝|颅中窝]]，藉[[翼管|翼管]]通[[颅底|颅底]]外面，向下移行至[[腭大管|腭大管]]，继而经[[腭大孔|腭大孔]]通[[口腔|口腔]]。

==解剖==
===邊界===
下列為翼腭窝的邊界：
* 前界：[[顳下窩|顳下窩]]內上部分。
* 後界：{{le|翼突|pterygoid process}}根部，必毗鄰{{le|蝶骨大翼|greater wing}}前緣。
* 內界：{{le|顎骨垂直板|perpendicular plate of palatine bone}}及{{le|顎骨蝶骨突|sphenoidal process|蝶骨突}}
* 外界：{{le|翼上頜裂|pterygomaxillary fissure}}
* 下界：part of the floor is formed by the [[pyramidal_process|pyramidal process]] of the palatine bone.

==功能==
翼腭窝內包含
* the [[pterygopalatine_ganglion|pterygopalatine ganglion]] suspended by nerve roots from the [[maxillary_nerve|maxillary nerve]]
* the terminal third of the [[maxillary_artery|maxillary artery]]
* the maxillary nerve (CN V<sub>2</sub>, the second division of the trigeminal nerve), with which is the [[nerve_of_the_pterygoid_canal|nerve of the pterygoid canal]], a combination of the [[greater_petrosal_nerve|greater petrosal nerve]] (preganglionic [[parasympathetic|parasympathetic]]) and the [[deep_petrosal_nerve|deep petrosal nerve]] (postganglionic [[Sympathetic_nervous_system|sympathetic]]).  To obtain block anesthesia of the entire second division of the trigeminal nerve, an intraoral injection can be administered into this area.

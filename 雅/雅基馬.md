'''亚基马''' （{{lang en|'''Yakima'''}}）是[[美國|美國]][[華盛頓州|華盛頓州]][[雅基馬縣|雅基馬縣]]縣治，位於該州中南部。2000年人口為71,845人，都會區人口為229,094人。<ref>{{cite web | title = State and City Quickfacts | publisher = U.S. Census Bureau | url = http://quickfacts.census.gov/qfd/states/53/5380010.html | accessdate = 2006年11月13日 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20060922183410/http://quickfacts.census.gov/qfd/states/53/5380010.html | archivedate = 2006年9月22日 }}</ref>這個數字於2010年上升至243,231。<ref>[http://factfinder2.census.gov/ 2010 census factfinder2]</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.census.gov/newsroom/releases/archives/2010_census/cb11-cn45.html | title = U.S. Census Bureau Delivers Washington's 2010 Census Population Totals, Including First Look at Race and Hispanic Origin Data for Legislative Redistricting| format = [[Microsoft_Excel|.xls]] | publisher = U.S. Census Bureau | date = 2011-02-23 | accessdate = 2011-03-26}}</ref>其所在的雅基馬谷地以生產[[蘋果|蘋果]]和[[啤酒花|啤酒花]]馳名，而美國75%啤酒花皆於亚基马一帶生產。<ref name=AHM>{{cite web | title = American Hop Museum | url = http://www.americanhopmuseum.org/history.htm | accessdate = 2009-03-05 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://archive.is/20130112143057/http://www.americanhopmuseum.org/history.htm | archivedate = 2013-01-12 }}</ref>亚基马都會區名為「大亚基马」（Greater Yakima）。<ref>{{cite web | title = State and City Quickfacts | publisher = U.S. Census Bureau | url = http://quickfacts.census.gov/qfd/states/53/5380010.html | accessdate = 2006-11-13 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20060922183410/http://quickfacts.census.gov/qfd/states/53/5380010.html | archivedate = 2006-09-22 }}</ref>

城名來自[[雅基馬族|雅基馬族]]。城東南、南部為[[雅基馬印第安人保留地|雅基馬印第安人保留地]]。<ref>{{cite web | title = City of Yakima History | publisher = City of Yakima | url = http://www.ci.yakima.wa.us/tour/history/ | accessdate = 2006-12-28}}</ref>

==地理==
根據[[美國2000年人口普查|2000年人口普查]]，本城面積為{{convert|27.69|sqmi|sqkm|2}}，其中{{convert|27.18|sqmi|sqkm|2}}為陸地，{{convert|0.51|sqmi|sqkm|2}}為水域。<ref name ="Gazetteer files">{{cite web|title=US Gazetteer files 2010|url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt|publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2012-12-19}}</ref>

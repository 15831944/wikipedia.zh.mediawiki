'''雅加達國際馬術公園'''（{{lang-id|'''Pacuan Kuda Pulomas'''}}）是一座位於[[印尼|印尼]][[雅加達|雅加達]][[普洛馬斯|普洛馬斯]]的[[馬術|馬術]]場館。翻新後，馬術場於2018年8月2日落成<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thejakartapost.com/news/2018/08/02/equestrian-center-for-asian-games-inaugurated.html|title=Equestrian center for Asian Games inaugurated|newspaper=The Jakarta Post|accessdate=2018-08-02}}</ref>。它為[[2018年亞洲運動會|2018年亞洲運動會]]比賽場館之一<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.beritasatu.com/jakarta/448378-equestrian-park-pulomas-ditargetkan-rampung-november-2017.html|title=Equestrian Park Pulomas Ditargetkan Rampung November 2017|newspaper=Berita Satu|accessdate=2018-06-05}}</ref>。場館為四層樓建築，佔地面積35公頃，有1500個座位，兩層樓156個馬廄，運動員宿舍，動物醫院和培訓場所<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thejakartapost.com/news/2017/10/14/pulo-mas-equestrian-facility-to-be-completed-next-month.html|title=Pulo Mas equestrian facility to be completed next month|newspaper=The Jakarta Post|accessdate=2018-06-05}}</ref>。

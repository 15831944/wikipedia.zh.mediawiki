'''亚尼礼·库玛·特亚基'''（{{lang-en|Anil Kr Tyagi}}，{{bd|1951年|4月2日}}）是印度植物生物学家，出生于印度[[怀拉菲罗兹布尔|怀拉菲罗兹布尔]]。为德里{{tsl|en|Indraprastha University|古鲁大学}}[[校監|校監]]<ref>{{cite news|title=Delhi Univ professor selected as Indraprastha varsity VC|url=http://www.business-standard.com/article/pti-stories/delhi-univ-professor-selected-as-indraprastha-varsity-vc-114043000212_1.html|accessdate=2014-06-06|publisher=Business Standard}}</ref>。在此之前，他曾担任UGC-SAP项目协调员以及[[德里大学|德里大学]]南校区[[生物化学|生物化学]]系主任<ref>{{cite news|title=2 scientists honoured with Vigyan Gaurav Samman|url=http://lite.epaper.timesofindia.com/getpage.aspx?articles=yes&pageid=2&max=true&articleid=Ar00201&sectid=1edid=&edlabel=TOIL&mydateHid=30-03-2010&pubname=Times+of+India+-+Lucknow+-+Times+City&title=2+scientists+honoured+with+Vigyan+Gaurav+Samman&edname=&publabel=TOI|accessdate=2014-06-11|newspaper=Times of India|date=2010-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141108115600/http://lite.epaper.timesofindia.com/getpage.aspx?articles=yes&pageid=2&max=true&articleid=Ar00201&sectid=1edid=&edlabel=TOIL&mydateHid=30-03-2010&pubname=Times+of+India+-+Lucknow+-+Times+City&title=2+scientists+honoured+with+Vigyan+Gaurav+Samman&edname=&publabel=TOI#|archive-date=2014-11-08|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，并于2004年至2006年担任印度生物化学家协会副主席<ref>{{cite web|title=Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi (India)|url=http://ipu.ac.in/vc/vc_ipu.htm|website=ipu.ac.in|publisher=Indraprastha University|accessdate=2014-06-06}}</ref>。

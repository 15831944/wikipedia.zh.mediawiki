'''荷兰锅'''是一种带盖的厚壁煮锅，通常用[[铸铁|铸铁]]做成，但也可用铝、瓷等其他材料制作。它源自英国而非荷兰，是欧洲国家较常见的一种煮锅。

==历史==
17世纪时英格兰的家用炊具多是[[黄铜|黄铜]]做成的，但当时黄铜无论是从荷兰进口（价格略低）还是本地生产都很昂贵<ref>Rosen, William, ''The Most Powerful Idea in the World: A Story of Steam, Industry, and Invention'' (Chicago, Illinois:  University of Chicago Press, 2010),[https://books.google.com/books?id=4wycmAWSWSMC&pg=PA145#v=onepage&q&f=false  p. 145.]</ref>。1704年布里斯托尔黄铜公司（Brass Works Company of Bristol）的[[亚伯拉罕·达比|亚伯拉罕·达比]]前往荷兰学习黄铜铸造法<ref>(Rosen, 2010), p. 145.</ref>，发现荷兰人用的铸模是砂制而不是泥制的，而这有利于铸成质量更好的器皿<ref>Ashton, Thomas Southcliffe, ''Iron and Steel in the Industrial Revolution'' (Manchester, England:  Manchester University Press, 1924), [https://archive.org/stream/ironsteelinindus00asht#page/26/mode/2up pp. 26–27.]</ref>。1706年他开始在布里斯托尔用新方法铸造炊具，随后又意识到使用铸铁的东西更便宜，进过反复试验之后终于做出铸铁的炊具来<ref>Burwood, Stephen, "Abraham Darby" in:  Magill, Frank N., ed., ''The 17th and 18th Centuries: Dictionary of World Biography'', vol. 4 (Ipswich, Massachusetts:  Salem Press, Inc., 1999), pp. 396–398 ; [https://books.google.com/books?id=HaHdAAAAQBAJ&&pg=PA396#v=onepage&q&f=false see p. 396.]</ref>。1707年他将用砂模铸铁的方法申请了专利<ref>Darby, Abraham, [https://books.google.com/books?id=JGxyAEvuhOgC&pg=PA46#v=onepage&q&f=false "Casting iron bellied pots in sand only,"] British patent no. 380 (issued:  1707), in: Woodcroft, Bennet, ed., ''Appendix to Reference Index of Patents of Invention, Containing Abstracts from such of the Early Patents …''   (London, England:  Great Seal Patent Office, 1855), p. 46.</ref>，因为这方法本身是来自荷兰的，所以做出来的锅又叫“荷兰锅”（Dutch oven）。根据历史记录，这个英语词最早在1710年就已经出现<ref>''Dutch Ovens Chronicled'' 3–4</ref><ref>[https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Dutch%20oven  韦氏词典]认为最早出现时间是1769年</ref>。

[[File:Braadpan.jpg|thumb]]
1891年荷兰炊具制造商BK发明的“braadpan”（又叫sudderpan）与荷兰锅外观几乎一样，但这实际是一种搪瓷的铁制炒锅<ref>{{nl icon}} [http://www.bk.nl/over_bk.php?nim_id=19&sub_nim_id=33&command=display_sub_content A History of BK (De geschiedenis van BK)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131002220742/http://www.bk.nl/over_bk.php?nim_id=19&sub_nim_id=33&command=display_sub_content |date=2013-10-02 }}, Retrieved 15 September 2013</ref>。

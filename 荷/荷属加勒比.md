'''荷属加勒比'''（Dutch Caribbean）是[[荷兰王国|荷兰王国]]位于[[加勒比地区|加勒比地区]]的岛屿领地的总称。<ref>{{cite web|title=Visa for the Dutch Caribbean|url=http://unitedkingdom.nlembassy.org/passports-visas--consular/visas/visa-for-the-dutch-caribbean.html|publisher=Netherlands Embassy in the United Kingdom|accessdate=7 May 2013|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140119043938/http://unitedkingdom.nlembassy.org/passports-visas--consular/visas/visa-for-the-dutch-caribbean.html|archivedate=2014-01-19|deadurl=yes}}</ref>它包括[[阿鲁巴|阿鲁巴]]、[[库拉索|库拉索]]、[[荷属圣马丁|荷属圣马丁]]以及[[荷兰加勒比区|荷兰加勒比区]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rijksdienstcn.com/Index.php?set_language=EN|title=Rijksdienst Carbische Nederland (Rijksdienst Dutch Caribbean)|accessdate=26 July 2012|work=Government of the Netherlands|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120704215850/http://www.rijksdienstcn.com/Index.php?set_language=EN|archivedate=2012年7月4日}}</ref>

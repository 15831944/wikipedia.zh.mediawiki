'''荷西·安立奎·拉米瑞茲'''（{{lang-en|José Enrique Ramírez}}, {{bd|1992年|9月17日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟的三壘手。現效力於克里夫蘭印地安人隊。2009年11月26日，他與印地安人簽約。2013年登上大聯盟。

2017年與2018年，拉米瑞茲都入選明星賽且拿下銀棒獎。2017年，他成為大聯盟第19位達成單季56支二壘安打的球員。
==職業生涯==
===克里夫蘭印地安人===
加盟印地安人前，拉米瑞茲在多明尼加打球。<ref>{{cite web|author=DPL |url=http://dplbaseball.com/dpl-alumni-jose-ramirez-making-himself-noticed-in-cleveland/ |title=Jose Ramirez becomes the First DPL Alumni in the Show - Dominican Prospect League |publisher=Dplbaseball.com |date= |accessdate=October 10, 2017}}</ref>2009年，17歲的拉米瑞茲被印地安人的球探在[[博卡奇卡|博卡奇卡]]相中，隨後與球隊簽約。<ref name=ohio>{{cite web|first=Stephanie |last=Storm |url=http://www.ohio.com/sports/aeros/indians-second-base-prospect-jose-ramirez-proves-worth-for-aeros-1.395130 |title=Indians second base prospect Jose Ramirez proves worth for Aeros - Aeros |website=Ohio.com |date= |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>

2011年，拉米瑞茲在新人聯盟出賽，打擊率3成25。2012年升上1A。2013年升上2A。
====2013年====
9月1日，拉米瑞茲登上大聯盟。當時他擔任代跑，並靠著Mike Aviles的再見滿貫砲回來得分。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/recap?gameId=330901106 |title=Mike Aviles' grand slam lifts Indians by Miguel Cabrera-less Tigers |website=Espn.go.com |date=September 1, 2013 |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>9月9日，他擊出大聯盟首安。<ref name="baseball-reference1">{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/boxes/CLE/CLE201309090.shtml |title=September 9, 2013 Kansas City Royals at Cleveland Indians Box Score and Play by Play |website=Baseball-Reference.com |date=September 9, 2013 |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>

2013年，印地安人隊繼2007後再度闖進季後賽。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/players/gl.cgi?id=ramirjo01&t=b&year=2013 |title=Jose Ramirez 2013 Batting Gamelogs |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date= |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Bill Lucey |url=http://www.newspaperalum.com/2013/09/remembering-2007-the-cleveland-indians-last-postseason-appearance.html |title=Remembering 2007, the Cleveland Indians Last Postseason Appearance |website=NewspaperAlum |date=September 30, 2013 |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>不過在外卡戰不敵光芒隊。
====2014年====
2014年開季，拉米瑞茲先待在小聯盟，不過表現不錯。<ref>{{cite web|last=Lukehart |first=Jason |url=http://www.letsgotribe.com/2014/5/1/5673036/indians-rumors-jose-ramirez-called-up-jason-kipnis-dl |title=The Indians are calling up 2B prospect Jose Ramirez on Friday - Let's Go Tribe |website=Letsgotribe.com |date=May 1, 2014 |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>他在5月1日升上大聯盟，當時他頂替受傷的[[傑森·基普尼斯|傑森·基普尼斯]]。基普尼斯回歸後，拉米瑞茲再度被下放。不過他並沒有在小聯盟待太久，他在7月23日重返大聯盟。<ref>{{cite web|author=Marla Ridenour |url=http://www.ohio.com/sports/indians/indians-shortstop-jose-ramirez-comes-of-age-against-yankees-1.512681 |title=Indians shortstop Jose Ramirez comes of age against Yankees - Indians |publisher=Ohio |date= |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>

7月31日，球隊將游擊手Asdrúbal Cabrera交易到國民隊，拉米瑞茲成為主力游擊手。<ref name="cleveland1">{{cite web|url=http://www.cleveland.com/tribe/index.ssf/2014/08/post_382.html |title=Jose Ramirez makes big splash in Big Apple for Cleveland Indians |website=Cleveland.com |date= |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>8月9日，拉米瑞茲擊出大聯盟首轟。該季他打擊率2成62，62安10盜。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://cleveland.indians.mlb.com/stats/sortable.jsp?c_id=cle#elem=%5Bobject+Object%5D&tab_level=child&click_text=Sortable+Player+hitting&game_type='R'&season=2014&season_type=ANY&league_code='MLB'&sectionType=sp&statType=hitting&page=1&ts=1468523436296&playerType=ALL&sportCode='mlb'&split=&team_id=114&active_sw=&position=&page_type=SortablePlayer&sortOrder='desc'&sortColumn=avg&results=&perPage=50&timeframe=&last_x_days=&extended=0 |title=Sortable Player Stats | Cleveland Indians |website=Cleveland.indians.mlb.com |date=January 20, 2016 |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>
====2015年====
2015年，拉米瑞茲首度進入開幕戰先發名單。然而他上半季的表現並不理想，球隊也在美聯中區墊底。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/player/608070/jose-ramirez |title=Jose Ramirez Stats, Fantasy & News | MLB.com |website=M.mlb.com |date= |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>到了下半季，拉米瑞茲漸漸找回手感。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fangraphs.com/statsd.aspx?playerid=13510&position=SS&type=&gds=&gde=&season=2015 |title=Jose Ramirez » Game Logs » 2016 » Batting | FanGraphs Baseball |website=Fangraphs.com |date= |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>該年他打擊率2成80。他在9月擊出4轟成為他個人單月全壘打紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fangraphs.com/leaders.aspx?pos=all&stats=bat&lg=all&qual=0&type=8&season=2015&month=9&season1=2015&ind=0&team=5&rost=0&age=0&filter=&players=0 |title=Cleveland Indians Leaderboards » 2015 » Batters » Dashboard | FanGraphs Baseball |website=Fangraphs.com |date= |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>
====2016年====
經過了去年的小低潮，拉米瑞茲在這年打擊爆發。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://cleveland.indians.mlb.com/stats/sortable.jsp?c_id=cle#elem=%5Bobject+Object%5D&tab_level=child&click_text=Sortable+Player+hitting&game_type='R'&season=2016&season_type=ANY&league_code='MLB'&sectionType=sp&statType=hitting&page=1&ts=1468523628614 |title=Sortable Player Stats | Cleveland Indians |website=Cleveland.indians.mlb.com |date=January 20, 2016 |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>他也在該年守了四個位置：二壘、三壘、游擊和左外野。當時左外野手[[麥可·布蘭特利|麥可·布蘭特利]]因傷提前報銷，拉米瑞茲補上了他的位置。

6月17日到7月1日，印地安人打出了一波14連勝，追平隊史紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|author=Cliff Corcoran |url=https://www.si.com/mlb/2016/07/02/indians-streak-ends-blue-jays-rajai-davis-cycle |title=Indians' win streak ends amid controversial replay call |website=SI.com |date=July 2, 2016 |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/teams/CLE/2016-schedule-scores.shtml |title=2016 Cleveland Indians : Schedule and Results |website=Baseball-reference.com |accessdate=December 23, 2016}}</ref>在這14場比賽中，拉米瑞茲的打擊率為2成98，9分打點。該年印地安人也一路闖進世界大賽，在世界大賽第5場，拉米瑞茲擊出了他在世界大賽的首轟。不過印地安人最後連輸3場遭到小熊隊逆轉，無緣拿下冠軍。
====2017年====
3月28日，拉米瑞茲與球隊簽下5年2600萬美元的合約。<ref>{{cite news  |author=Indians Press Release |title=Indians sign José Ramĺrez to a long-term contract |url=http://m.indians.mlb.com/news/article/221235966/indians-sign-jose-ramlrez-to-a-long-term-contract/ |work=Indians.MLB.com |date=March 28, 2017 |accessdate=March 28, 2017}}</ref>在6月，他締造了連續9場敲出2支安打以上的紀錄。<ref name=beck101217>{{cite news |last=Beck |first=Jason |url=http://m.indians.mlb.com/news/article/258321780/indians-early-exit-shouldnt-overshadow-season/ |title=Indians' exit shouldn't overshadow the journey: Plenty of great memories—102 wins, 22 in a row, historic pitching |work=Indians.MLB.com |date=October 12, 2017 |accessdate=October 14, 2017}}</ref>6月18日，他被選為美聯單週最佳球員。那一週，他的打擊率為5成16，3轟7分打點。<ref name=roto062117>{{cite news |author=Roto Wire Staff |url=https://www.cbssports.com/fantasy/baseball/news/indians-jose-ramirez-named-al-player-of-the-week/ |title=Indians' Jose Ramirez: Named AL Player of the Week |work=[[CBSSports.com|CBSSports.com]] |date=June 21, 2017 |accessdate=October 15, 2017}}</ref>7月9日，二壘手[[傑森·基普尼斯|傑森·基普尼斯]]受傷，<ref>{{cite newspaper |agency=Associated Press |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/mlb/2017/08/23/indians-jason-kipnis-on-dl-again-with-hamstring-injury/104889120/ |title=Indians' Jason Kipnis on disabled list again with hamstring injury |work=USA Today |date=August 23, 2017 |accessdate=October 14, 2017}}</ref>球隊讓拉米瑞茲在剩餘球季擔任二壘手一職。該年他也首度入選明星賽。<ref>{{cite news |author=Indians Press Release |title=Five (5) Indians earn American League All-Star nods |url=http://m.indians.mlb.com/news/article/240179478/five-5-indians-earn-american-league-all-star-nods/ |work=Indians.MLB.com |date=July 2, 2017 |accessdate=July 2, 2017}}</ref>

8月24日到9月15日，印地安人締造了22連勝，打破了由2002年「魔球軍團」運動家保持的20連勝美聯紀錄。這也是大聯盟史上第2長的連勝紀錄。拉米瑞茲在這段期間，打擊率4成23，上壘率4成62，長打率9成44。<ref>{{cite news |last=Castrovince |first=Anthony |url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/254555046/awards-for-indians-winning-streak/ |title=Let's honor The Streak with some awards |work=MLB.com |date=September 16, 2017 |accessdate=September 16, 2017}}</ref>9月5日，他再度入選美聯單週最佳球員。<ref name=staff090517>{{cite newspaper |author=Staff Report |url=http://www.news-herald.com/sports/20170905/jose-ramirez-named-al-player-of-the-week |title=Jose Ramirez named AL Player of the Week |work=[[The_News-Herald_(Ohio)|The News-Herald]] |date=September 5, 2017 |accessdate=October 15, 2017}}</ref>

該年他出賽152場，擊出56支二壘安打，打擊率3成18，29轟83分打點。在擊出25轟以上的球員中，他保有最少三振的紀錄，僅69次。<ref>{{cite news |last=Kelly |first=Matt |url=https://www.mlb.com/news/picking-best-two-strike-hitter-in-2017/c-264338104 |title=Who was 2017's best two-strike hitter?  Judge, Rendon and Votto all put up compelling cases |work=MLB.com |date=January 9, 2018 |accessdate=January 11, 2018}}</ref>

此外，他也拿下銀棒獎。<ref name=usa110917>{{cite newspaper |author=USA Today Sports |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/mlb/2017/11/09/silver-slugger-awards-mlb/849087001/ |title=Jose Altuve, Nolan Arenado among repeat Silver Slugger Award winners |work=USA Today |date=November 9, 2017 |accessdate=November 10, 2017}}</ref>在美聯MVP票選中，他拿下第三名，僅次於[[荷西·奧圖維|荷西·奧圖維]]及[[亞倫·賈吉|亞倫·賈吉]]。<ref>{{cite magazine |last=Kolur |first=Nihal |url=https://www.si.com/mlb/2017/11/16/jose-altuve-giancarlo-stanton-american-league-national-league-mvp |title=Jose Altuve, Giancarlo Stanton named MVP Award winners |work=Sports Illustrated |date=November 16, 2017 |accessdate=November 19, 2017}}</ref>
====2018年====
5月29日，他擊出單季第17轟。在5月尚未結束前就擊出17轟讓他追平隊史紀錄。<ref>{{cite news |last=Schoenfield |first=David |url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/23654723/mlb-real-not-jose-ramirez-keeps-pushing-ceiling-higher |title=Real or not? Jose Ramirez keeps pushing his ceiling higher and higher |work=ESPN.com |date=May 29, 2018 |accessdate=June 7, 2018}}</ref>後來他與隊友Francisco Lindor在同一月擊出至少10轟，繼2002年的[[吉姆·湯米|吉姆·湯米]]和Karim García後再度追平隊史紀錄。<ref>{{cite newspaper |last=Noga |first=Joe |url=https://www.cleveland.com/tribe/index.ssf/2018/06/francisco_lindor_jose_ramirez_1.html |title=Francisco Lindor, Jose Ramirez already making strong cases for All-Star selections |work=The Plain Dealer |date=June 1, 2018 |accessdate=June 1, 2018}}</ref>

上半季他打擊率2成92，24轟59分打點，19盜，他再度入選明星賽。<ref>[https://www.mlb.com/news/jose-ramirez-one-of-five-indians-in-asg/c-284829158 Jose Ramirez one of five Indians in ASG | MLB.com<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>整季他打擊率為2成70，上壘率3成87，長打率5成52。<ref>[https://www.fangraphs.com/leaders.aspx?pos=all&stats=bat&lg=all&qual=y&type=1&season=2018&month=0&season1=2018&ind=0&team=0&rost=0&age=0&filter=%20&players=0&sort=6,d Major League Leaderboards » 2018 » Batters » Advanced Statistics | FanGraphs Baseball<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>

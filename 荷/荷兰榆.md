'''荷蘭榆'''（'''''Ulmus × hollandica'''''）是分佈于歐洲的[[無毛榆|無毛榆]]（''Ulmus glabra''）和[[欧洲光叶榆|欧洲光叶榆]]（''Ulmus minor''）自然雜交產生一種[[榆|榆]]，後來經過人工栽培其分佈範圍又進一步擴大。<ref name="Richens"/><ref name=Bean>Bean, W. J. (1981). ''Trees and shrubs hardy in Great Britain'', 7th edition. Murray, London</ref><ref name=Elwes,a>Elwes, H. J. & Henry, A. (1913). ''The Trees of Great Britain & Ireland.'' Vol. VII. 1848–1929. Private publication, Edinburgh.</ref>

其形態介於無毛榆和歐洲光葉榆之間<ref name=Collin>Collin, E. (2001). Elm. In Teissier du Cros (Ed.) (2001) ''Forest Genetic Resources Management and Conservation. France as a case study''. Ministry of Agriculture and Fisheries, Bureau of Genetic Resources. INRA DIC. France.</ref>，其[[F1品種|F1品種]]繁殖能力很強，之後又產生了許多其他的變種<ref name=Richens>Richens, R. H. (1983), ''Elm''</ref>。

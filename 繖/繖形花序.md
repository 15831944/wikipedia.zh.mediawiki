'''繖形花序'''（{{lang|en|Umbel}}）為一種[[花序|花序]]，屬於無限花序（Indeterminate inflorescence）<ref name= Beentje/><ref>{{cite book |author1=Walters, D.R. |author2=Keil, D.J. |year=1975  |title=Vascular Plant Taxonomy |publisher=Kendall/Hunt Publishing Company |location=Dubuque  }}</ref>，由從一點上發出的許多[[花梗|花梗]]組成，其形狀有如傘骨。此一稱呼可追溯至1590年，來自[[拉丁文|拉丁文]]的''umbella''，意指遮陽用的傘<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?term=umbel# |title=umbel etymology |access-date=2018-11-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170703052927/http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?term=umbel# |archive-date=2017-07-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref> 。繖形花序中，花梗末端有可能再分支出數個次級花梗，而自成一獨立的繖形結構（umbellules, umbellets）<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=1T9GAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA186&lpg=PA186&dq=compound+umbel+umbellules&source=bl&ots=bH2UYkMzBI&sig=UMEr46V3fwE6QsS0Kq6xDPqvZd4&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjOhMvRsMneAhX0IjQIHSzfBsUQ6AEwDXoECAIQAQ#v=onepage&q=compound%20umbel%20umbellules&f=false|page=186|title=The Elements of Botany|author=Adrien de Jussieu, James Hewetson Wilson|publisher=John Van Voorst}}</ref>，此種花序稱為'''複繖形花序'''（compound umbels）<ref name= Beentje>{{cite book |author1=Beentje, H. |author2=Williamson, J. |year=2010  |title=The Kew Plant Glossary: an Illustrated Dictionary of Plant Terms |publisher=Kew Publishing |location=Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew  }}</ref>。

複繖形花序是[[繖形科|繖形科]]植物（包括[[香芹|香芹]]、[[香菜|香菜]]、[[胡蘿蔔|胡蘿蔔]]等）的特徵<ref>{{cite web|url=http://web.bvu.edu/faculty/hampton/taxonomy/Umbelliferae/Umbelliferae.htm|title=Umbelliferae: The Parsley Family|publisher=Buena Vista University}}</ref>，另外[[五加科|五加科]]的[[常春藤屬|常春藤屬]]、[[楤木屬|楤木屬]]、[[八角金盤屬|八角金盤屬]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ucmp.berkeley.edu/anthophyta/asterids/apiales/apialesmm.html|title=Apiales: Morphology|publisher=University of California Museum of Paleontology|access-date=2018-11-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180408204746/http://www.ucmp.berkeley.edu/anthophyta/asterids/apiales/apialesmm.html#|archive-date=2018-04-08|dead-url=no}}</ref>，以及[[石蒜科|石蒜科]]的[[蔥屬|蔥屬]]等類群的花序亦為繖形花序<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Protandry and sequence of flower opening in the onion (Allium cepa L.)|year=1978|publisher=''New Phytologist''|volume=81|issue=2|page=419-428|doi=10.1111/j.1469-8137.1978.tb02647.x}}</ref>。

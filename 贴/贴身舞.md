'''贴身舞'''<ref>参见汉译：http://www.dzwww.com/rollnews/news/200912/t20091211_5232999.htm {{Wayback|url=http://www.dzwww.com/rollnews/news/200912/t20091211_5232999.htm |date=20100426133104 }}</ref><ref>参见汉译：http://airj1990.blog.163.com/{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>是[[:en:partner_dance|舞伴舞]]的一种，即两个或多个舞伴以互相[[摩擦|摩擦]]对方身体的方式跳舞。做为一种新近出现的[[现代舞|现代舞]]，贴身舞最早在[[夜总会|夜总会]]风靡，后来更流行于[[美国|美国]]和[[加拿大|加拿大]]的年轻[[学生|学生]]之间<ref>[http://articles.nydailynews.com/2010-02-16/entertainment/27056442_1_freak-dancing-msnbc-com-charles-salter 'Dance Like Grandma's Watching': High schools nationwide crack down on freak dancing, grinding]{{dead link|date=2018年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} ''[[:en:New_York_Daily_News|纽约每日新闻]]'', retrieved on February 17th, 2010.</ref>。由于不赞同有[[性暗示|性暗示]]<ref>[http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/35339424/ns/health-kids_and_parenting/ 'When teens grind, schools freak: Schools use Burt Bacharach, contracts to halt sexy dancing'].</ref>[的贴身舞，有些地方以“容易受伤”为名试图禁止贴身舞<ref>[http://bangordailynews.com/2010/01/12/news/bangor/a-grinding-halt-for-bangor-high-school-dances/ "Agrinding halt for Bangor High School dances"]</ref>。 Dancing which consists mainly of grinding has been referred to as ''freak dancing'' or ''freaking''.<ref name="WashPost">{{cite news|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/ac2/wp-dyn?pagename=article&node=education/educationweek&contentId=A63657-2001Feb27|title="Freak Dancing" Craze Generates Friction, Fears|work=Washington Post|date=2001-02-27|author=Catherine Gewertz|publisher=The Washington Post Company}}</ref> 

贴身舞的前体是从20世纪80年代开始流行的社交舞蹈“跳跃舞”（"The Bump"）。其他作为贴身舞前体的元素还包括1987年的电影''[[Dirty_Dancing|Dirty Dancing]]''. The [[Lambada|Lambada]] was a [[dance_craze|dance craze]] that featured grinding actions, as seen, for example, in the films ''[[The_Forbidden_Dance|The Forbidden Dance]]'' and ''[[Lambada_(film)|Lambada]]''. [[Perreo|Perreo]] is a [[Reggaeton|Reggaeton]] style of grinding.

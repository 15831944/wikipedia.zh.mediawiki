'''胡安·古铁雷斯·莫雷诺'''（{{lang-es|'''Juan Gutiérrez Moreno'''}}，{{bd|1976年|7月23日|catidx=Juanito}}），通常稱'''华尼托'''（{{lang-es|'''Juanito'''}}），前[[西班牙|西班牙]]足球運動員，擔任[[中堅|中堅]]。

A player of physical display, he is also noted for his excellent aerial ability. He spent the bulk of his career with [[Real_Betis|Betis]], appearing in more than 300 official games and winning one [[Copa_del_Rey|Copa del Rey]].

With the [[Spain_national_football_team|Spanish national team]], Juanito appeared in one [[FIFA_World_Cup|World Cup]] and two [[UEFA_European_Championship|European Championships]], contributing to the conquest of [[UEFA_Euro_2008|Euro 2008]].

==國家隊生涯==
Juanito made his international debut for [[Spain_national_football_team|Spain]] on 21 August 2002 against [[Hungary_national_football_team|Hungary]], in a [[Testimonial_match|testimonial]] for [[Ferenc_Puskás|Ferenc Puskás]]. His first goal for the national side came against [[Côte_d'Ivoire_national_football_team|Côte d'Ivoire]] in a 3–2 win.

Juanito represented Spain at [[UEFA_Euro_2004|UEFA Euro 2004]] and [[UEFA_Euro_2008|Euro 2008]] (playing in the 2–1 win over [[Greece_national_football_team|Greece]] in the latter tournament<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/football/euro_2008/7363473.stm|title=Russia 2–0 Sweden & Greece 1–2 Spain|publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]|date=18 June 2008|accessdate=19 December 2012}}</ref>) and the [[2006_FIFA_World_Cup|2006 FIFA World Cup]], scoring with his head in the 1–0 triumph over [[Saudi_Arabia_national_football_team|Saudi Arabia]].<ref>[http://www.uefa.com/competitions/worldcup/fixturesresults/round=2259/match=82405/report=rp.html Juanito maintains Spain momentum] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080329115657/http://www.uefa.com/competitions/worldcup/fixturesresults/round%3D2259/match%3D82405/report%3Drp.html |date=2008-03-29 }}; UEFA.com, 23 June 2006</ref> That goal was the first ever scored by a Betis player in the competition.

In the [[2010_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_(UEFA)|2010 World Cup qualifier]] against [[Estonia_national_football_team|Estonia]] on 11 October 2008, he netted through another header in a 3–0 away success.<ref>[http://www.uefa.com/competitions/worldcup/fixturesresults/round=15218/match=301806/report=rp.html Spain battle to beat Estonia] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090414085715/http://www.uefa.com/competitions/worldcup/fixturesresults/round%3D15218/match%3D301806/report%3Drp.html |date=2009-04-14 }}; UEFA.com, 11 October 2008</ref> He was overlooked however for the finals in South Africa, as the national side emerged victorious.

'''土器，'''又称'''瓦器'''，是一种低级的粗[[陶器|陶器]]'''，'''有的施釉、有的不施釉<ref>ASTM C242 – 15. </ref> ，通常低于1200°C烧制{{notetag|[[瓷器|瓷]]器、[[骨瓷|骨瓷]]和炻[[炻器|器]]的烧制温度都要高于土器。}}<ref>[http://www.getty.edu/vow/AATFullDisplay?find=earthenware&logic=AND&note=%20&english=N&prev_page=1&subjectid=300140803 Getty AAT, "Earthenware"]</ref> 。

土器的概念涵盖所有原始陶器、兵马俑、建筑砖，以及17世纪前的欧洲陶器，我们今天使用大部分的餐具也属于细土器。<ref name="billington">Dora Billington, ''The Technique of Pottery'', London: B.T.Batsford, 1962</ref> 最早的窑可以追溯到公元前29,000–25 000年。<ref>"Modern Ceramic Engineering: Properties, Processing And Use In Design." </ref><ref>"On the Origins of Pottery." </ref> 中国在新石器文化期开始使用窑来烧制土器。

土器烧制时没有玻璃化<ref name="EU">Combined Nomenclature of the [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union European Union] Published by the EC Commission in Luxembourg, 1987</ref> ，质地软。

== 特点 ==
土器比[[白瓷|白瓷]]、[[骨瓷|骨瓷]]的[[塑性變形|可塑性]]更好<ref>An industry term for ceramics including tableware and sanitary ware</ref>。<ref name="Ryan">Whitewares: Testing and Quality Control. </ref><ref>Pottery Science: Materials, Process And Products. </ref>

土器的吸水率约5-8％，<ref>Ceramics Glaze Technology. </ref> 强度比骨瓷、白瓷、炻器低。

橙色或红色的土器通常[[铁氧化物|氧化铁]]含量高，被广泛用于花盆、瓷砖、工艺品和炉子。

== 生产 ==
现代土器一般采用25%[[高嶺石|的高岭土]]、25%的球土、35％的[[石英|石英]]和15%的[[长石|长石]]。<ref>Dictionary of Ceramics, 3rd edition. </ref>

现代土器陶可能是[[比斯克瓷|比斯克瓷]]<ref name="rich">{{Cite book|title=The Materials and Methods of Sculpture|last=Rich, Jack C.|publisher=Courier Dover Publications|year=1988|isbn=9780486257426|pages=49}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ceramicartsdaily.org/firing-techniques/electric-kiln-firing/ten-basics-of-firing-electric-kilns/|title=Ceramic Arts Daily – Ten Basics of Firing Electric Kilns|accessdate=16 April 2012|year=2012|work=ceramicartsdaily.org}}</ref> ，烧制温度在1000至1150 °C之间，并施釉<ref>{{Cite book|title=Ceramics an Illustrated Primer|last=Norton|first=F.H.|publisher=Hanover House|year=1960|pages=74–79}}</ref><ref>Frank and Janet Hamer, ''The Potter's Dictionary of Materials and Techniques''</ref> 。

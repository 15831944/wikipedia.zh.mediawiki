'''御夫座SU'''（'''SU Aurigae'''）是一顆位於金牛座－御夫座複合體內的[[金牛T星|金牛T星]]，距離地球約496光年，[[恆星光譜|光譜類型]] G2 III<ref name="Chakraborty">Chakraborty, A. and Ge, J., ''[http://iopscience.iop.org/1538-3881/127/5/2898/fulltext/203315.text.html Unveiling Su Aurigae in the near Infrared: New high spatial resolution results using Adaptive Optics]'', The Astronomical Journal, 127:2898-2903, May 2004.</ref>。

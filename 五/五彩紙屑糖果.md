'''五彩紙屑糖果'''（{{lang-en|Confetti candy}}）是一種用煮熟的糖和[[玉米糖漿|玉米糖漿]]製成的糖果食品，其形成片狀，冷卻後破碎成塊。它具有堅硬和脆性質地；五彩紙屑糖果有時在烹飪後灑在頂上，而成型過程完成後可增加眼睛的吸引力<ref name="FL">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=t6t454MPEqgC&pg=PA32 |title=Family Living: Simply Delicious Party Snacks - Leisure Arts |publisher=|date= |pages=32–33|accessdate=2014-03-25}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Ou0abdBiLPUC&pg=PA71 |title=Celebrating Candy - Leisure Arts |publisher= |date= |page=71|accessdate=2014-03-25}}</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=AA3v2f6X1L8C&pg=PA259 Centuries of Success: Lessons from the World's Most Enduring Family Businesses - William T. O'Hara]. p. 259.  Retrieved on 2014-03-25.</ref>。

五彩紙屑糖果，也被稱為紙屑[[巧克力米|巧克力米]]，它也是一種扁平或盤狀的糖果，而這些類似於{{link-en|糖丸|Nonpareils}}的圓形是以盤狀形式準備，它通常用於裝飾糖果和其他糖果<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=movq4Bw5PX0C&pg=PA122 |title=Making Great Candy: A Sweet Selection of Fun and Easy Recipes - Laura Dover Doran |publisher= |date= |pages=122–123 |accessdate=2014-03-25}}</ref><ref name="google2">[https://books.google.com/books?id=SGb0oUTT7GYC&pg=PT22 Sweets on a Stick: More Than 150 Kid-Friendly Recipes for Cakes, Candies ... - Linda Vandermeer]. (2011-11-18). Retrieved on 2014-03-25.</ref>。

'''五氟化氯'''（Chlorine pentafluoride）化學式为ClF<sub>5</sub>，是[[氟|氟]]和[[氯|氯]]的化合物，首次合成於1963年。<ref>{{cite journal
| author = Smith D. F.
| title = Chlorine Pentafluoride
| journal = [[Science_(journal)|Science]]
| year = 1963
| volume = 141
| issue = 3585
| pages = 1039–1040
| doi = 10.1126/science.141.3585.1039
| pmid = 17739492}}</ref>

它具有C<sub>4v</sub>对称性的[[四方锥|四方锥]]结构已经被高清晰度的<sup>19</sup>F[[核磁共振|核磁共振]]所证实。<ref name=Pilipovich>{{cite journal
| author = Pilipovich, D., Maya, W., Lawton, E.A., Bauer, H.F., Sheehan, D. F., Ogimachi, N. N., Wilson, R. D., Gunderloy, F. C., Bedwell, V. E.
| title = Chlorine pentafluoride. Preparation and Properties
| journal = [[Inorganic_Chemistry|Inorganic Chemistry]]
| year = 1967
| volume = 6
| issue = 10
| pages = 1918
| doi = 10.1021/ic50056a036}}</ref>

== 制备 ==

最初，一种常用于合成这种[[超价分子|超价分子]]的方法是[[三氟化氯|ClF<sub>3</sub>]]与[[氟|F<sub>2</sub>]]在高温高压下反应。 此外，金属氟化物即MClF<sub>4</sub>(例如KClF<sub>4</sub>, RbClF<sub>4</sub>, CsClF<sub>4</sub>)与F<sub>2</sub>的反应产生ClF<sub>5</sub>和相应的金属[[氟化物|氟化物]]MF。{{ref_label|Pilipovich|1|}} 1981年, 研究人员发现[[二氟化镍|NiF<sub>2</sub>]]是一个很好的制备ClF<sub>5</sub>的[[催化剂|催化剂]]。<ref>{{cite journal
| author = Šmalc, A., Žemva, B., Slivnik, J., and Lutar K.
| title = On the Synthesis of Chlorine Pentafluoride
| journal = Journal of Fluorine Chemistry
| year = 1981
| volume = 17
| pages = 381–383
| doi = 10.1016/S0022-1139(00)81783-2}}</ref>

== 反應 ==

五氟化氯与[[水|水]]剧烈反應产生[[高氯酸氟|FClO<sub>4</sub>]]和[[氟化氢|HF]]<ref name="patnaik2007">{{cite book
| title = A comprehensive guide to the hazardous properties of chemical substances
| author = Pradyot Patnaik
| edition = 3rd
| publisher = Wiley-Interscience
| year = 2007
| isbn = 0471714585
| pages = 478–479
}}</ref> 。另外，它也是一個強大的[[氟化劑|氟化劑]]。虽然不与除了[[碳|碳]]的[[第二周期|第二周期]][[非金属|非金属]]反应，但它在室温下就能很容易地与第三周期和第四周期的非金属（除了[[氯|氯]]）反应。<ref name=Pilipovich />

'''喉囊腫'''（laryngeal cysts）是指[[囊腫|囊腫]]發生在[[喉|喉]]部、或更頻繁使用的[[聲門|聲門]]上之位置，諸如「[[會厭谷|會厭谷]]囊腫(epiglottic vallecula cyst)」發生在[[會厭|會厭]]旁{{le|谷 (解剖學)|Vallecula|谷}}裡的現象。<ref name="pmid11139891">{{cite journal|last1=Borkó|first1=R|last2=Szûcs|first2=S|title=[Congenital laryngeal cysts].|journal=HNO|date=November 2000|volume=48|issue=11|pages=843–5|pmid=11139891|language=de}}</ref>通常喉囊腫不會延伸到[[甲狀軟骨|甲狀軟骨]]。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Burgess|first1=LP|last2=Yim|first2=DW|title=Laryngeal cyst of the thyroid cartilage.|journal=Archives of otolaryngology (Chicago, Ill. : 1960)|date=December 1985|volume=111|issue=12|pages=826|pmid=4062656|doi=10.1001/archotol.1985.00800140070014}}</ref>喉囊腫可能是[[先天性障碍|先天性]]的存在<ref name="Khashu2006">{{cite journal|last1=Khashu|first1=M|last2=Osiovich|first2=H|last3=Kozak|first3=F|last4=Pelligra|first4=G|last5=Hirsh|first5=D|last6=Smyth|first6=J|title=Congenital epiglottic cyst presenting with severe airway obstruction at birth|journal=Journal of Perinatology|date=January 2006|volume=26|issue=1|pages=71–72|doi=10.1038/sj.jp.7211414}}</ref>，或則最終可能是由於[[退化|退化]]原因而發展出的。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Newman|first1=BH|last2=Taxy|first2=JB|last3=Laker|first3=HI|title=Laryngeal cysts in adults: a clinicopathologic study of 20 cases.|journal=American journal of clinical pathology|date=June 1984|volume=81|issue=6|pages=715–20|pmid=6731351}}</ref>喉囊腫經常干擾著{{le|發聲態|phonation}}。

'''殲敵者級'''（[[梵語|梵語]]：'''अरिहंत'''，[[英語|英語]]：'''Arihant'''，在[[梵語|梵語]]意思中指「征服者」）是[[印度|印度]]的[[核動力|核動力]][[弹道导弹潜艇|弹道导弹潜艇]]，也是印度首度自主研發的[[核子動力潛艇|核子動力潛艇]]。首艦「殲敵者號」於2009年下水，並在2014年12月開始測試，2016年服役<ref name="indiatimes54907081" /><ref name="theecotimes">{{cite news|first=S|last=Anandan|url=http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/defence/indias-first-nuclear-submarine-ins-arihant-ready-for-operations-passes-deep-sea-tests/articleshow/51098650.cms|title=Second nuclear submarine headed for year-end launch|newspaper=[[The_Economic_Times|The Economic Times]]|date=23 February 2016|access-date=17 March 2016}}</ref>。

殲敵者級使印度成為除了[[联合国安全理事会常任理事国|联合国安全理事会常任理事国]]的五個成員國之外首次有他國能獨自研發和建造核潛艇的國家，被外界視為印度加入常任理事國的重要籌碼。<ref name=bbc2>{{cite news|last=Marcus|first=Jonathan|title=Indian-built Arihant nuclear submarine activated|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-asia-india-23648310|access-date=12 October 2013|work=[[BBC|BBC]]|date=10 August 2013}}</ref>

== 歷史 ==
在[[1971年印巴战争|1971年印巴战争]]期間，時任美國總統[[理查德·尼克松|理查德·尼克松]]派出命名為{{link-en|第74特遣部隊|Task Force 74}}的[[航空母艦戰鬥群|航空母艦戰鬥群]]，由核動力航空母艦[[企業號航空母艦_(CVN-65)|企業號航空母艦]]作為母艦領導戰鬥群進入[[孟加拉灣|孟加拉灣]]，企圖恐嚇印度。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://in.rbth.com/articles/2011/12/20/1971_war_how_russia_sank_nixons_gunboat_diplomacy_14041|title=1971 War: How Russia sank Nixon’s gunboat diplomacy|website=in.rbth.com|access-date=2016-04-29}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/news/us-fleet-in-bay-of-bengal-a-game-of-deception|title=US Fleet in Bay of Bengal: A game of deception|date=2013-12-15|website=The Daily Star|access-date=2016-04-29}}</ref>對此，[[蘇聯|蘇聯]]派出核導彈潛艇符拉迪沃斯托克號跟蹤美軍在孟加拉灣的行動。<ref>{{cite news|first=Rakesh|last=Krishnan Simha|url=http://indrus.in/articles/2011/12/20/1971_war_how_russia_sank_nixons_gunboat_diplomacy_14041.html|title=US-Soviet Actions in 1971 Indo-Pakistani War |publisher=Indrus.in |date=20 December 2011|access-date=22 April 2013|work=indrus.in}}</ref>是次美蘇之間的行動向時任印度總理[[英迪拉·甘地|英迪拉·甘地]]展示了核武器和[[弹道导弹潜艇|弹道导弹潜艇]]的重要性<ref name=idr-arihant>{{cite news|url= http://www.indiandefencereview.com/2010/02/arihant-the-annihilator.html|title=Arihant: the annihilator|work=Indian Defence Review |date=25 October 2010|access-date=8 January 2012}}</ref>，使印度在繼1974年[[微笑的佛陀|微笑的佛陀]]的核試驗後，便開始了核動力推進系統的可行性研究（第932項目）。<ref>{{cite news|first=Premvir|last=Das|url=http://www.business-standard.com/article/opinion/premvir-das-ins-arihant-a-watershed-moment-109073000008_1.html|title=Project 932|newspaper=[[Business_Standard|Business Standard]]|date=30 July 2009|access-date=24 April 2013}}</ref>

在1998年，時任印度國防部長{{link-en|喬治·費爾南德斯|George Fernandes}}證實了印度海軍已經開始了核動力潛艇的初步設計工作。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://fas.org/nuke/guide/india/sub/ssn/index.html|title=India's SNS Project Report|publisher=Fas.org|access-date=24 April 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.indianexpress.com/res/web/pIe/ie/daily/19980519/13950884.html|title=George defends position on China|publisher=[[Indian_Express|Indian Express]]|date=19 May 1998|access-date=24 February 2011}}</ref>雖然在1998年{{link-en|波卡蘭-II|Pokhran-II}}核試驗後印度曾承諾[[不首先使用|不首先使用]]核武器，但該項目是以完成印度[[核三位一体|核三位一体]]戰略目標而發展。<ref name=gs-atv>{{cite web|first=John|last=Pike|url=http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/world/india/atv.htm|title=Advanced Technology Vessel (ATV)|publisher=Globalsecurity.org|access-date=24 January 2011|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20101226032637/http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/world/india/atv.htm| archive-date= 26 December 2010}}</ref><ref name=dna-induction>{{cite news|url= http://www.dnaindia.com/india/report_first-indigenous-nuclear-sub-is-inducted-into-the-navy_1277218 |title=First indigenous nuclear sub is inducted into the navy |newspaper=DNA|date=26 July 2009|access-date=24 January 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url= http://www.rediff.com/news/2001/feb/16josy4.htm|title=India's nuclear sub still a distant dream|work=[[Rediff|Rediff]]|date=16 February 2001|access-date=24 January 2011}}</ref>

2006年9月22日<ref name=idr-arihant/>，由{{link-en|巴巴原子能研究中心|Bhabha Atomic Research Centre}}在{{link-en|英迪拉·甘地原子能研究中心|Indira Gandhi Centre for Atomic Research}}設計和製造的壓水反應堆原型正式運作<ref name=hindu-indiandesign>{{cite news|url= http://www.hindu.com/2009/08/16/stories/2009081655260900.htm|title=INS Arihant is an Indian design: Anil Kakodkar|newspaper=[[The_Hindu|The Hindu]]|date=16 August 2009|access-date=17 March 2016|location=Chennai, India}}</ref>，並在三年內進行測試工作。後來該原型測試的成功使印度得以進行量產化反應堆以用在殲敵者號的身上。<ref name="pwr_1">{{cite news|url= http://www.hindu.com/2009/08/03/stories/2009080353810100.htm|title=PWR building shows indigenous capability|first=T.S.|last=Subramanian |date=2 August 2009|newspaper=[[The_Hindu|The Hindu]]|access-date=17 March 2016|location=Chennai, India}}</ref><ref name="pwr_2">{{cite news|url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/StoryPage/StoryPage.aspx?sectionName=HomePage&id=5721403d-8f66-43e2-92c4-4f3148032565&Headline=Arihant+propulsion+reactor+unveiled|title=Arihant propulsion reactor unveiled|first=M.R.|last=Venkatesh|date=2 August 2009|access-date=17 March 2016|newspaper=[[Hindustan_Times|Hindustan Times]]}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

2009年7月26日，殲敵者級首號艦[[殲敵者號核潛艇|殲敵者號核潛艇]]正式下水。2016年2月23日，有報導指出，殲敵者號已被確認經成功通過所有測試，在技術上能夠隨時準備入役。<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|title=INS Arihant not to feature at International Fleet Review|url=http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/defence/ins-arihant-not-to-feature-at-international-fleet-review-indian-navy-chief/articleshow/50865629.cms|newspaper=[[The_Economic_Times|The Economic Times]]|date=5 February 2016|access-date=5 February 2016}}</ref><ref name="Arihant_ready">{{Cite news|title = India's first nuclear submarine INS Arihant ready or operations, passes deep sea tests|url = http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/defence/indias-first-nuclear-submarine-ins-arihant-ready-for-operations-passes-deep-sea-tests/articleshow/51098650.cms|newspaper=[[The_Economic_Times|The Economic Times]]|date=23 February 2016|access-date=23 February 2016}}</ref>

== 設計 ==
殲敵者級核子動力潛艇是源於印度的「先進技術艦艇」（ATV）項目<ref name="domain-b.com">{{cite web|url= http://www.domain-b.com/defence/general/20090716_indian_indigenous.html |title=Indian indigenous nuclear sub to be unveiled on 26 July: report|publisher=domain-b.com|date=16 July 2009|access-date=24 January 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url= http://in.reuters.com/article/companyNews/idINISL35153320090212|title=India nuclear sub project near completion|work=[[Reuters|Reuters]]|date=12 February 2009|access-date=24 January 2011}}</ref><ref name="launch1">{{cite news|url= http://news.in.msn.com/national/article.aspx?cp-documentid=3097464|title=PM to launch indigenous nuke submarine by month-end|date=16 July 2009|work=[[MSN|MSN]]|access-date=19 July 2009}}</ref><ref name="launch2">{{cite news|url= http://www.hindu.com/2009/07/19/stories/2009071958490500.htm |title= Indigenous nuclear submarine goes on trial|newspaper=[[The_Hindu|The Hindu]]|access-date=19 July 2009 |location=Chennai, India|date=19 July 2009}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|first=Hari|last=Sud|url=http://www.upiasia.com/Security/2009/08/14/indias_nuclear_submarine_and_the_indian_ocean/7676/|title=India's nuclear submarine and the Indian Ocean |publisher=upiasia.com |date=14 August 2009|access-date=24 January 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://blogs.reuters.com/india/2009/07/31/indias-nuclear-submarine-dream-still-miles-to-go/|work=[[Reuters|Reuters]]| title=India's nuclear submarine dream, still miles to go|date=31 July 2009|access-date=24 January 2011}}</ref>，是印度首次設計和建造的核潛艇。<ref name=it-finaltest>{{cite news|url= http://indiatoday.intoday.in/story/Final+test+of+K-15+ballistic+missile+on+Tuesday/1/5002.html|title=Final test of K-15 ballistic missile on Tuesday|date=25 February 2008|access-date=24 January 2012}}</ref>該級潛艇長112米（367英尺），橫樑長11米（36英尺），排水量6,000噸（5900長噸；6600短噸），吃水10米（33英尺），潛水深度300米（980英尺）。該級潛艇是由一個83兆瓦（111,000馬力）的[[压水反应堆|压水反应堆]]推動，在浮出水面時能夠以12至15節（22-28公里/小時）的速度航行，在潛航時能夠以最大24節（44公里/小時）的速度潛航。<ref name="navaltech"/>

該潛艇的駝峰上設有四枚發射管，能夠攜帶多達12枚的[[K-15海洋导弹|K-15海洋导弹]]或4枚{{link-en|K-4導彈|K-4 (SLBM)}}。<ref name=it-undersea>{{cite news|url= http://indiatoday.intoday.in/story/The+secret+undersea+weapon/1/3659.html|title=The secret undersea weapon|work=[[India_Today|India Today]]|date=17 January 2008|access-date=8 January 2012}}</ref><ref name=it-secretk>{{cite news|url=http://indiatoday.intoday.in/site/story/the-secret-k-missile-family/1/120488.html|work=[[India_Today|India Today]]|title=The secret 'K' missile family|date=20 November 2010|access-date=8 January 2012}}</ref>殲敵者的整體設計類於[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]的[[971型核潜艇|971型核潜艇]]<ref name="navaltech">{{cite web|url= http://www.naval-technology.com/projects/arihant-class/|title=SSBN Arihant Class Submarine, India|publisher=naval-technology.com|access-date=22 April 2013}}</ref>，而印度海軍中有一艘核潛艇是租自該型號。<ref name=autogenerated1>{{cite web|url=http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/world/india/atv.htm |title=Arihant – Advanced Technology Vessel (ATV)|publisher=Global Security|access-date=8 January 2012}}</ref><ref name=it-setsail>{{cite news|url=http://indiatoday.intoday.in/story/leased-russian-n-submarine-to-set-sail-for-india-this-month-end/1/164449.html|title=Leased Russian n-submarine to set sail for India this month end|date=15 December 2011|access-date=8 January 2012}}</ref>

'''銀鋸眶鯻'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Bidyanus bidyanus''、{{lang-en|Silver Perch}}），又稱'''銀鱸'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://terms.naer.edu.tw/detail/1685507/|title=銀鱸|publisher=[[國家教育研究院|國家教育研究院]]|work=雙語詞彙、學術名詞暨辭書資訊網}}</ref>，為[[鱸形目|鱸形目]][[鱸亞目|鱸亞目]][[鯻科|鯻科]]的一種魚類，是[[澳洲|澳洲]][[墨累—大令盆地|墨累—大令盆地]]底中層水域的[[特有種|特有種]]<ref name="Conservation Advice">{{cite web|year=2013|url=http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/threatened/species/pubs/76155-conservation-advice.pdf|title=Australian Government Conservation Advice for Silver Perch|website=Australian Government - Department of the Environment and Energy|access-date=2018-10-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170317145648/http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/threatened/species/pubs/76155-conservation-advice.pdf#|archive-date=2017-03-17|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="Major Mitchell">{{cite book|url=http://gutenberg.net.au/ebooks/e00035.html|title=Three Expeditions into the Interior of Eastern Australia; with Descriptions of the Recently Explored Region of Australia Felix, and of the Present Colony of New South Wales|first=Thomas|last=Mitchell|publisher=T. and W. Boone|location=London|year=1838|edition=2nd|access-date=2018-10-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180905033329/http://gutenberg.net.au/ebooks/e00035.html#|archive-date=2018-09-05|dead-url=no}}</ref>，被[[IUCN|IUCN]]列為[[易危物種|易危物種]]<ref name=iucn/>。

==外形==
銀鋸眶鯻體延長而側扁，高度中等，頭小、眼小，吻尖，背部褐色至深灰色，體側為銀灰色，腹部銀白色，背鰭及尾鰭黑色，臀鰭白色，體長多為30-40公分，體重約1.5公斤，但亦有體長超過60公分、體重接近8公斤的紀錄，是鯻科體型最大的魚類。<ref name="NSW Recovery Plan">{{cite web|year=2006|url=http://www.dpi.nsw.gov.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0007/636388/NSW-Silver-Perch-Recovery-Plan.pdf|title=Silver Perch (Bidyanus bidyanus) NSW Recovery Plan|website=NSW Department of Primary Industries|accessdate=7 September 2016}}</ref><ref name="Mallen-Cooper & Stuart">{{cite journal|first1=Martin|last1=Mallen-Cooper|first2=Ivor G.|last2=Stuart|year=2003|url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/rra.714/abstract|title=Age, growth and non-flood recruitment of two potamodromous fishes in a large semi-arid/temperate river system|journal=River Research and Applications|volume=19|issue=7|accessdate=7 September 2016|doi=10.1002/rra.714|author=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170324212037/http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/rra.714/abstract#|archive-date=2017-03-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>

==分類==
銀鋸眶鯻於1832年由澳洲探險家{{tsl|en|Thomas Mitchell (explorer)|湯瑪斯·米謝爾 (探險家)|湯瑪斯·米謝爾}}探索{{tsl|en|Barwon River (New South Wales)|巴望河 (澳洲)|巴望河}}時紀錄發表，其學名來自當地原住民對這種魚的稱呼「Bidyan」<ref name="Major Mitchell"/>。銀鋸眶鯻的英文俗名雖為「{{lang|en|Silver Perch}}」，但其屬於[[鯻科|鯻科]]，並非{{link-en|鱸屬|Perca}}（''Perca''）的成員。

==生態==
成魚棲息在水流快速的溪流、水庫及湖泊，成群活動，屬雜食性，以水生[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]、[[軟體動物|軟體動物]]、[[蠕蟲|蠕蟲]]、[[藻類|藻類]]等為食<ref name="Conservation Advice" /><ref name="Clunie & Koehn">{{cite report|first1=Pam|last1=Clunie|first2=John D.|last2=Koehn|year=2001|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/287841352_Silver_Perch_A_Resource_Document|title=Silver Perch: A Resource Document|publisher=Murray-Darling Basin Commission:Canberra|accessdate=7 September 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181030090903/https://www.researchgate.net/publication/287841352_Silver_Perch_A_Resource_Document#|archive-date=2018-10-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>，會進行季性洄游，繁殖期為11月至隔年1月，成魚於黃昏或夜間在接近表面的水域交配，雌魚可一次產50萬顆漂浮卵，卵在水流不明顯時會沉入水中。水溫攝氏26-27度時，其卵於24-36小時後孵化，不過其卵能孵化的溫度範圍較廣，有記錄顯示在攝氏20度以下的低溫仍可孵化，但所需時間較久<ref name="Conservation Advice"/>。

過去認為銀鋸眶鯻和澳洲東南部其他原生種魚類一樣相當長壽，此特徵可以確保其成魚在當地嚴峻的環境中，一生至少可以經歷一次特別適合產卵的季節（通常是每10-20年發生一次、造成當地特別潮濕的強烈[[反聖嬰現象|反聖嬰現象]]）<ref name="Conservation Advice" /><ref name="NSW Recovery Plan" /><ref name="Clunie & Koehn" />，曾有27歲的個體被紀錄<ref name="Mallen-Cooper & Stuart" />。不過2017年的一項研究顯示銀鋸眶鯻族群中，只有少部分個體能存活超過7年，因此若連續幾年的氣候都不適合繁殖，其族群就會面臨嚴重危機<ref name="MDBA News Feed 2017">{{cite news|url=https://getinvolved.mdba.gov.au/Nativefishforum2017/news_feed/research-prompts-new-approach-to-supporting-silver-perch|title=Research prompts new approach to supporting silver perch|accessdate=2018-10-30|publisher=Murray-Darling Basin Authority|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180712083953/https://getinvolved.mdba.gov.au/Nativefishforum2017/news_feed/research-prompts-new-approach-to-supporting-silver-perch#|archive-date=2018-07-12|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==經濟利用==
[[File:Bidyanus_bidyanus_2014-09-19.JPG|thumb]]的{{link-en|國家動物園和水族館|National Zoo & Aquarium}}]]
可做為食用魚及遊釣魚，在[[澳洲|澳洲]]將銀鋸眶鯻做為養殖魚的農場逐漸增加<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fishbase.de/summary/12232|title=''Bidyanus bidyanus'' (Mitchell, 1838)|publisher=[[Fishbase|Fishbase]]|access-date=2018-10-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181030090928/https://www.fishbase.de/summary/12232#|archive-date=2018-10-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==保育==
銀鋸眶鯻於1970年代前廣泛分布於[[墨累河|墨累河]]、[[達令河|達令河]]流域，然而之後由於不明的原因，野外的族群大量消失，目前僅[[墨累河|墨累河]]的族群數量較多，雖然人工養殖的數量及範圍增加，然其對於該物種的保育的成效並不佳。經研究其數量減少的原因可能是因爲[[水壩|水壩]]的興建，導致魚群無法進行洄游，又其魚卵須沉澱在含氧量高的沉積物上，然水壩的調節水量功能，致使魚卵因沉積物掩埋、缺氧而死亡<ref name="Conservation Advice" /><ref name="Clunie & Koehn" />。另外，[[外來種|外來種]]魚類（如[[鯉魚|鯉魚]]）的入侵和傳染疾病（如EHN病毒）亦為導致銀鋸眶鯻數量減少的原因<ref name="Weirs 1">{{cite web|year=2013|url=http://www.finterest.com.au/fish-movement-and-migration/impacts-of-weirs-on-downstream-passage-of-native-fish-in-the-murray-darling-basin/|title=Impacts of weirs on downstream passage of native fish in the Murray-Darling Basin|website=Finterest|accessdate=7 September 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181029191641/http://www.finterest.com.au/fish-movement-and-migration/impacts-of-weirs-on-downstream-passage-of-native-fish-in-the-murray-darling-basin/#|archive-date=2018-10-29|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="Weirs 2">{{cite web|first1=CA |last1=Boys |first2=L |last2=Baumgartner |first3=W |last3=Robinson |first4=C |last4=Lay |year=2010 |url=http://www.mdba.gov.au/system/files/NFS-2010-fish-forum-abstracts_Final.pdf/ |title=Protecting migrating fish at in-stream structures: downstream mortality at weirs and screening water diversions |website=Murray Darling Basin Authority |accessdate=7 September 2016 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120715081236/http://www.mdba.gov.au/system/files/NFS-2010-fish-forum-abstracts_Final.pdf |archivedate=15 July 2012 |df= }}</ref>。自2000年起，墨累河的許多[[堰|壩堰]]建立了[[魚道|魚道]]，使當地原生魚類得以洄游，加上{{link-en|環境流量|environmental flow}}的管理，使銀鋸眶鯻的野外族群開始復甦<ref name="Conservation Advice" />。

'''弗列德瑞克·查爾斯·弗里曼'''（{{lang-en|Frederick Charles Freeman}}, {{bd|1989年|9月12日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟亞特蘭大勇士隊的一壘手。他2010年升上大聯盟，3度入選明星賽。
==職業生涯==
[[2007年美國職棒大聯盟選秀|2007年美國職棒大聯盟選秀]]，亞特蘭大勇士隊在第2輪78順位選進弗里曼。他與球隊簽下409,500美元的合約。2009年，他成為隊上第5號新秀。
===亞特蘭大勇士===
====2010年====
2010年9月1日，弗里曼進入勇士隊的擴編名單，同日升上大聯盟。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|title=Freeman thrust right into Braves' lineup|url=http://m.braves.mlb.com/news/article/14177328/|accessdate=October 11, 2015|publisher=MLB.com|date=September 1, 2010}}</ref>9月5日，他擊出大聯盟首安。9月21日，他面對賽揚強投[[洛伊·哈勒戴|洛伊·哈勒戴]]擊出大聯盟首轟。<ref>{{cite news|title=Scott Cousins delivers walk-off win with 1st career hit after teammate is hit in on-deck circle|url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/recap?gameId=300905128|accessdate=October 11, 2015|agency=Associated Press|publisher=ESPN.com|date=September 5, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Whicker|first1=Mark|title=El Modena's Freeman gets 1st homer - off Halladay|url=http://www.ocregister.com/angels/strong-420881-braves-atlanta.html|accessdate=October 11, 2015|work=Orange County Register|date=September 22, 2010}}</ref>該季他出賽20場，打擊率1成67。2010年勇士闖進季後賽，不過弗里曼沒有上場，勇士該年在國聯分區賽以1勝3敗遭到巨人隊淘汰。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/playoffs/2010/news/story?id=5651892 |title=Derek Lowe to start Game 1 for Braves |publisher=ESPN.com |date=October 5, 2010 |accessdate=November 15, 2011}}</ref>
====2011年====
2011年球季，弗里曼成為球隊的先發一壘手。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/story/_/id/6803748/atlanta-braves-rookie-freddie-freeman-showing-belongs-bigs |title=Braves have keeper in Freddie Freeman |publisher=ESPN.com |date=July 27, 2011 |accessdate=November 15, 2011}}</ref>7月4日，弗里曼達成生涯首次單場雙響。<ref>{{cite news|author=Mark Bowman|url=http://atlanta.braves.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20100831&content_id=14141378&vkey=news_atl&fext=.jsp&c_id=atl |title=Prospect Freeman gets called to The Show |publisher=MLB.com |date= |accessdate=September 2, 2010}}</ref>7月18日，弗里曼達成單季50分打點，成為繼1954年的[[漢克·阿倫|漢克·阿倫]]後，再度達成此成就的勇士選手。<ref>{{cite web |author=David O&#39;Brien |url=http://blogs.ajc.com/atlanta-braves-blog/2011/07/19/freeman-gets-50-rbis-as-quickly-as-rook-named-hank/ |title=Freeman gets 50 RBIs as quickly as rook named Hank |publisher=Blogs.ajc.com |date=July 19, 2011 |accessdate=November 15, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110920151555/http://blogs.ajc.com/atlanta-braves-blog/2011/07/19/freeman-gets-50-rbis-as-quickly-as-rook-named-hank/ |archivedate=September 20, 2011 |df= }}</ref>7月，他拿下國聯單月最佳新秀。該月他出賽27場，打擊率3成62，擊出38安。

8月，他與隊友[[丹·阿格拉|丹·阿格拉]]一度達成連20場擊出安打的紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20110806&content_id=22840388&vkey=news_atl&c_id=atl |title=Uggla, Freeman both push hit streaks forward |publisher=MLB.com |date= |accessdate=November 15, 2011}}</ref>弗里曼最終在8月7日中止該紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/recap?gameId=310807121 |title=Dan Uggla's hit streak at 28 as Braves edge injury-stricken Mets |publisher=ESPN.com |date=August 7, 2011 |accessdate=November 15, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://atlanta.braves.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20110808&content_id=22933344&notebook_id=22933354&vkey=notebook_atl&c_id=atl |title=Streak ends, but Freeman's focus on winning |publisher=MLB.com |date= |accessdate=November 15, 2011}}</ref>該季他打擊率2成82，21轟76分打點。

該年的國聯年度最佳新秀，弗里曼票選第二，輸給隊友[[克雷格·金布雷爾|克雷格·金布雷爾]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://bbwaa.com/2011/11/2011-rookies |title=Official site of the Baseball Writers' Assn. of America |publisher=BBWAA.com |date= |accessdate=November 15, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120707075121/http://bbwaa.com/2011/11/2011-rookies |archivedate=July 7, 2012 |df= }}</ref>
====2012年====
該季他出賽149場，打擊率2成59，23轟94分打點。
====2013年====
[[2013年美國職棒大聯盟全明星賽|2013年美國職棒大聯盟全明星賽]]，弗里曼在最終票選贏得了1970萬的選票。但他卻在明星賽開打前3天傷到拇指而缺席。該季他打擊率3成19，23轟109分打點。
====2014年====
2014年2月4日，弗里曼與球隊簽下了8年1億3500萬美元的合約。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/story/_/id/10403089/freddie-freeman-atlanta-braves-agree-contract-extension | title=Braves extend Freddie Freeman | publisher=ESPN.com | date=February 4, 2014 | accessdate=February 4, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article/atl/atlanta-braves-sign-freddie-freeman-jason-heyward-130-million-for-first-baseman?ymd=20140204&content_id=67434036&vkey=news_atl | title=Freeman, Heyward sign; $130M for first baseman? | publisher=MLB.com | date=February 4, 2014 | accessdate=February 4, 2014 | author=Bowman, Mark}}</ref>該年他第2次入選明星賽。該季他162場全數出賽，打擊率2成88，18轟78分打點。
====2015年====
6月17日，他因為右手腕受傷進入傷兵名單，中斷了他個人連續234場出賽的紀錄。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|title=Braves being cautious with Freeman|url=http://m.braves.mlb.com/news/article/131690202|accessdate=January 31, 2016|date=June 19, 2015}}</ref>該季他打擊率2成76，18轟66分打點。<ref>{{cite news|title=Freddie Freeman says right wrist is finally pain-free|url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/story/_/id/14680790/freddie-freeman-atlanta-braves-says-ailing-right-wrist-pain-free|accessdate=January 31, 2016|agency=Associated Press|publisher=ESPN.com|date=January 30, 2016}}</ref>
====2016年====
2016年，勇士隊宣布重建，並把許多球員交易出去，<ref>{{cite news|last1=Petriello|first1=Mike|title=Is it time for Braves to trade Freeman?|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/159300696/atlanta-braves-should-trade-freddie-freeman/|accessdate=September 30, 2016|publisher=MLB.com|date=December 12, 2015}}</ref>但唯獨留下弗里曼。<ref>{{cite news|title=Braves GM would trade his right arm before Freddie Freeman|url=http://www.foxsports.com/mlb/story/atlanta-braves-freddie-freeman-trade-rumors-john-coppolella-right-arm-111815|accessdate=September 30, 2016|publisher=Fox Sports|date=November 18, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Stark|first1=Jayson|authorlink1=Jayson Stark|title=Freddie Freeman, the last Brave standing, is 'here to stay'|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/page/springtraining_freddiefreeman/freddie-freeman-last-brave-standing-here-stay|accessdate=September 30, 2016|publisher=ESPN.com|date=March 11, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|title=Coppolella can't see Braves trading Teheran|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/185975990/john-coppolella-wont-trade-julio-teheran/|accessdate=September 30, 2016|publisher=MLB.com|date=June 24, 2016}}</ref>弗里曼也成為隊上的老大哥，帶領著一群年輕人往前邁進。6月15日，弗里曼達成生涯首次完全打擊。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Sachs|first1=Aimee|title=Freddie takes a ride on the Free-cycle|url=http://m.braves.mlb.com/news/article/204162268/braves-freddie-freemans-hit-streak-snapped/|accessdate=September 30, 2016|publisher=MLB.com|date=June 16, 2016}}</ref>他也在該球季首度達成單季30轟。9月29日，他中止了個人連續30場安打紀錄。該季他打擊率3成02，34轟91分打點。<ref>[https://www.fangraphs.com/leaders.aspx?pos=all&stats=bat&lg=all&qual=y&type=2&season=2016&month=0&season1=2016&ind=0&team=0&rost=0&age=0&filter=%20&players=0&sort=5,d Major League Leaderboards » 2016 » Batters » Batted Ball Statistics | FanGraphs Baseball<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>
====2017年====
該年他代表加拿大隊參加經典賽。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|title=Braves sending 10 to World Baseball Classic|url=http://m.braves.mlb.com/news/article/215481558/braves-freeman-to-represent-club-at-classic/|accessdate=February 9, 2017|publisher=MLB.com|date=February 8, 2017}}</ref>該年也是勇士隊新主場[[太陽信託公園|太陽信託公園]]的首度啟用年。弗里曼在新球場的首場比賽，面對大都會就猛打賞。

5月17日，他被藍鳥隊投手Aaron Loup觸身球砸中退場。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|title=Freddie Freeman out 10 weeks with broken wrist|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/231011420/freddie-freeman-out-10-weeks-with-broken-wrist/|publisher=MLB|accessdate=May 18, 2017}}</ref>在他受傷復原期間，代替他一壘手位置的Matt Adams表現不錯。<ref>{{cite news|title=Freddie Freeman plans to return from DL as third baseman|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/19700484/freddie-freeman-plans-return-dl-atlanta-braves-third-baseman|accessdate=June 22, 2017|publisher=ESPN.com|date=June 21, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|title=Freeman serious about returning at third base|url=http://m.braves.mlb.com/news/article/237660364/braves-freddie-freeman-could-play-third-base/|accessdate=June 22, 2017|publisher=MLB.com|date=June 21, 2017}}</ref>因此7月4日，弗里曼以先發三壘手身分重返亞特蘭大。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|title=Freeman back from DL, makes move to 3B|url=http://m.braves.mlb.com/news/article/240410488/braves-freddie-freeman-activated-from-dl/|accessdate=July 5, 2017|publisher=MLB.com|date=July 4, 2017}}</ref>7月6日，他擊出生涯1000安。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|last2=Collier|first2=Jamal|title=Braves deliver after long night, beat Gio, Nats|url=http://m.braves.mlb.com/news/article/240939728/braves-beat-nationals-after-3-hour-plus-delay/|accessdate=July 7, 2017|publisher=MLB.com|date=July 8, 2017}}</ref>而勇士隊總教練史尼克也隨後將弗里曼調回一壘守備。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Sachs|first1=Aimee|title=Freeman returns to first base for good|url=http://m.braves.mlb.com/news/article/245827670/braves-freddie-freeman-returns-to-first-base/|accessdate=August 2, 2017|publisher=MLB.com|date=August 2, 2017}}</ref>

該季他打擊率3成07，28轟71分打點。
====2018年====
2017年10月，弗里曼去動了雷射手術，<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bowman|first1=Mark|title=Eyes on the prize: Freeman back after Lasik|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/freddie-freeman-on-lasik-surgery-left-wrist/c-266572888|accessdate=February 17, 2018|publisher=MLB.com|date=February 16, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=O'Brien|first1=David|title=Freeman can see clearly now, and the (wrist) pain is gone|url=http://www.myajc.com/sports/baseball/freeman-can-see-clearly-now-and-the-wrist-pain-gone/MoIcnMdAVBRupJGHMsx7kJ/|accessdate=February 17, 2018|work=Atlanta Journal Constitution|date=February 16, 2018}}</ref>改善他的視力問題。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Odum|first1=Charles|title=Vision problems continue for Braves' Freeman|url=http://savannahnow.com/sports/2012-05-28/vision-problems-continue-braves-freeman|accessdate=February 17, 2018|work=Savannah Morning News|agency=Associated Press|date=May 28, 2012}}</ref>該年他第三度入選明星賽，<ref>{{cite news |last1=Burns |first1=Gabriel |title=How Braves fared in 2018 All-Star game |url=https://www.ajc.com/sports/baseball/how-braves-fared-2018-mlb-all-star-game/xSoOwmdLpBW1CG5S1yW2EJ/ |accessdate=November 5, 2018 |work=Atlanta Journal Constitution |date=July 18, 2018}}</ref>他還參加了全壘打大賽。<ref>{{cite news |last1=Burns |first1=Gabriel |title=Freddie Freeman falls to Bryce Harper in home run derby |url=https://www.ajc.com/sports/baseball/freddie-freeman-falls-bryce-harper-home-run-derby/XXBwRuJktRlF3sS3RzuATL/ |accessdate=November 5, 2018 |work=Atlanta Journal Constitution |date=July 16, 2018}}</ref>

該季他擊出191支安打，國聯第一，<ref name="ajc2018seasonrecap">{{cite news |title=VOTE: Who was the Braves’ MVP this season? |url=https://www.ajc.com/sports/baseball/vote-who-was-the-braves-mvp-this-season/j7dPCBOgcLkbkCzvJlvVeL/ |accessdate=November 5, 2018 |work=Atlanta Journal Constitution |date=October 16, 2018}}</ref>在大聯盟僅次於Whit Merrifield。<ref>{{cite news |last1=Hadley |first1=Greg |title=South Carolina great Whit Merrifield claims the MLB hit crown for 2018 |url=https://www.thestate.com/sports/college/university-of-south-carolina/usc-baseball/article219287250.html |accessdate=November 5, 2018 |work=The State |date=September 30, 2018}}</ref>他打擊率3成09，23轟98分打點。ref name="ajc2018seasonrecap"/><ref>{{cite news |last1=Perez |first1=Braulio |title=Freddie Freeman Finishes Regular Season as NL's Hits Leader |url=https://www.msn.com/en-us/sports/mlb/freddie-freeman-finishes-regular-season-as-nls-hits-leader/ar-BBNLXP2 |accessdate=November 5, 2018 |publisher=MSN |date=October 1, 2018}}</ref>他與隊友Ender Inciarte及[[尼克·馬卡克斯|尼克·馬卡克斯]]一同入選金手套獎，這是勇士隊史上首次三名外野手都獲得金手套獎。<ref>{{cite news |last1=Dillard |first1=Zach |title=Braves capture franchise record 3 Gold Gloves for 2018 season |url=https://www.foxsports.com/south/story/braves-2018-gold-gloves-inciarte-markakis-freeman-110418 |accessdate=November 5, 2018 |publisher=Fox Sports South |date=November 4, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Burns |first1=Gabriel |title=Braves earn franchise-best three Gold Gloves |url=https://www.ajc.com/sports/baseball/braves-earn-franchise-best-three-gold-glovers/nRbpjeby9t012S8cTOSdnN/ |accessdate=November 5, 2018 |work=Atlanta Journal Constitution |date=November 4, 2018}}</ref>此外，弗里曼也贏得威爾森年度防守獎。<ref>{{cite news |last1=Thornburg |first1=Chad |title=Winners of Wilson defensive honors unveiled |url=https://www.mlb.com/braves/news/2018-defensive-player-of-year-awards-announced/c-300375608 |accessdate=November 8, 2018 |publisher=MLB.com |date=November 7, 2018}}</ref>

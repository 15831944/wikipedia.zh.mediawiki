[[弗兰德|弗兰德]]的[[贝居安会院|贝居安会院]]（{{lang-en|Flemish Béguinages}}）自1998年起被列入[[UNESCO|UNESCO]] [[世界遗产名录|世界遗产名录]]。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://whc.unesco.org/pg.cfm?cid=31&id_site=855 | title = Flemish Béguinages | publisher = Unesco | work = List of World Heritage }}</ref>

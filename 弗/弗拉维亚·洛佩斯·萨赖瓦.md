'''弗拉维亚·洛佩斯·萨赖瓦'''（{{lang-pt|Flávia Lopes Saraiva}}，{{bd|1999年|9月30日||}}）生于里约热内卢，是一名巴西女子竞技体操运动员。萨赖瓦7岁时开始练习体操<ref>{{cite web|url=https://oglobo.globo.com/esportes/flavia-saraiva-talento-da-ginastica-brasileira-vai-do-pe-de-goiaba-ao-podio-16091072|script-title=pt:Flávia Saraiva, talento da ginástica brasileira, vai do pé de goiaba ao pódio|publisher=Grupo Globo|date=2015-05-08|accessdate=2018-11-04}}</ref>。她17岁时身高仅有1米33，这一身高远小于其他女子体操选手<ref>{{cite web|url=https://qz.com/756000/the-curious-case-of-the-incredible-shrinking-olympic-gymnast/|title=The curious case of the incredible shrinking Olympic gymnast|publisher=Quartz|date=2016-08-12|accessdate=2018-11-04}}</ref>。2014年，她顶替受伤的队友丽贝卡·安德雷德参加南京青奥，获得1枚金牌和2枚银牌<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.elle.com/culture/news/a38345/flavia-saraiva-brazilian-gymnast/|title=Fall in Love With Flavia Saraiva, Brazil's Breakout Star Gymnast|publisher=Elle|date=2016-08-07|accessdate=2018-11-04}}</ref>。2015年，她入选成年国家队，开始参加成年级别赛事<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.esportealternativo.com.br/ginastica-rio-2016/28029-flavia-saraiva-disputa-sua-primeira-final-olimpica-na-trave|script-title=pt:Flávia Saraiva disputa sua primeira final olímpica na trave|publisher={{lang|pt|eSporte Alternativo}}|date=2016-08-14|accessdate=2018-11-04}}</ref>。

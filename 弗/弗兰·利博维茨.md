'''弗兰·利波维兹'''（{{lang-en|'''Fran Lebowitz'''}}，{{bd|1950年|10月27日}}）是一位美国作家、<ref name="PR">{{Cite journal|title=Fran Lebowitz, A Humorist at Work|author=George Plimpton|url=http://www.theparisreview.org/interviews/1931/a-humorist-at-work-fran-lebowitz|authorlink=George Plimpton|last2=James Linville|date=Summer 1993}}</ref>演讲家、<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2010/11/18/fashion/18scene.html|title=Infallibility Has Its Upside|date=November 17, 2010|last=Detrick|first=Ben|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://archive.boston.com/lifestyle/blogs/bostonspirit/2012/10/fran_lebowitiz_is_coming_to_to.html|title=Fran Lebowitiz is coming to town to tell it like it is|last=King|first=Loren|date=October 6, 2012|work=Boston.com|accessdate=2016-10-15}}</ref>业余演员。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://variety.com/2017/dirt/real-estalker/fran-lebowitz-new-york-city-condo-1201986942/|title=Fran Lebowitz Buys $3.1 Million, One-Bedroom Condo in New York City|author=Mark David|date=February 13, 2017|work=Variety}}</ref>她最为人熟知的是她通过对于[[纽约|纽约]]大都会生活的察觉、洞悉和描述<ref name="NYM">{{Cite web|url=http://nymag.com/daily/entertainment/2010/11/fran_lebowitz_interview_transc.html|title=The Vulture Transcript: Fran Lebowitz on Sarah Palin, Keith Richards, Her Side Career as a Law & Order Judge, and Much More|date=November 23, 2010|last=Bennett|first=Bruce|work=[[New_York_(magazine)|New York]]}}</ref>，映射出对于整个美国社会生活方式具有讽刺幽默意味的评论。有一些评论家称之她为「当代的{{link-en|多萝西·帕克|Dorothy Parker}}」。<ref name="NYT">{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1981/08/23/books/the-sour-cream-sensibility.html|title=The sour cream sensibility|date=August 23, 1981|last=Collins|first=Glenn|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]}}</ref>

==生平==
1950年出生，长大于纽泽西州的[[莫里斯敦_(新澤西州)|莫里斯敦]]。<ref name="nytimes-lunch">{{Cite news|url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F30810FC3C540C738DDDA10894DC494D81|title=At Lunch With: Fran Lebowitz; Words Are Easy, Books Are Not|last=Morris|first=Bob|date=August 10, 1994|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]}}{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>利波维兹描述认同她的“犹太人身份来自于其种族，文化或任何其他相关的含义。但与其所有的宗教含义无关。”<ref>[http://njjewishnews.com/article/29683/the-nj-thats-in-me-doesnt-exist-anymore "'The NJ that's in me doesn't exist anymore'"] by Johanna Ginsberg, ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Jersey_Jewish_News New Jersey Jewish News]'', January 27, 2016</ref>从7岁起，她就已经是一位无神论者。<ref>{{cite video | url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MXPLuzRq6-Y&t=18s | title=Fran Lebowitz Interview on Charlie Rose | publisher=YouTube.com | date=February 15, 2017 |access-date=July 17, 2017}}</ref>

在被高中开除但依然拿到[[普通教育發展證書|普通教育发展证书]]之后，利波维兹做了一些不太平常的小工作，最终加入了[[安迪·沃荷|安迪·沃荷]]的工厂，并作为他主办的杂志《[[訪問_(雜誌)|访问]]》的一名撰稿人<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.dailycal.org/2012/11/07/fran-lebowitz-goes-road-tripping/|title=Fran Lebowitz goes road-tripping|date=November 7, 2012|last=Coughlan|first=Thomas|work=[[The_Daily_Californian|The Daily Californian]]}}</ref>。在那里，她写了一篇称为《我们的海滨》的文稿<ref name="Lebowitz">{{Cite news|url=https://www.theparisreview.org/miscellaneous/1931/a-humorist-at-work-fran-lebowitz|title=A Humorist at Work|last=Lebowitz|first=Fran|work=The Paris Review|accessdate=2017-10-29|language=en}}</ref>。之后她也在杂志《淑女》（Mademoiselle）工作过一段时间。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/elysabeth-alfano/fran-lebowitz-harris-theater_b_1916499.html|title=Quintessential New Yorker and Social Commentator, Fran Lebowitz, Comes to Chicago|date=September 28, 2012|last=Alfano|first=Elysabeth|work=[[The_Huffington_Post|The Huffington Post]]}}</ref>她的前2部作品《都市生活》（Metropolitan Life，1978年）和《社会探究》（Social Studies，1981年）均收录在论文集《弗然·利波维兹读者》（The Fran Lebowitz Reader，1994年）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.slantmagazine.com/house/2011/02/fran-lebowitz-in-public-speaking/|title=Fran Lebowitz in Public Speaking|date=February 21, 2011|last=Callahan|first=Dan|work=[[Slant_Magazine|Slant Magazine]]}}</ref>

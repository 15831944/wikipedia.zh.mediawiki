'''綿蟹總科'''（{{lang|la|''Dromioidea''}}）是[[十足目|十足目]][[短尾下目|短尾下目]]（[[螃蟹|螃蟹]]）的[[綿蟹派|綿蟹派]]之下三個總科的其中一個，其成員主要在[[馬達加斯加|馬達加斯加]]發現。綿蟹總科生物最早期的化石是在[[达宁阶|达宁阶]]的岩層發現<ref>{{cite journal |title=A reconstruction of an evolutionary scenario for the Brachyura (Decapoda) in the context of the Cretaceous–Tertiary boundary |author=Andreas Brösing |year=2008 |journal=[[Crustaceana|Crustaceana]] |volume=81 |issue=3 |pages=271–287 |url=http://decapoda.nhm.org/pdfs/30458/30458.pdf |doi=10.1163/156854008783564091}}</ref>。

==分類==
根據Grave et al.，本總科包括下列三個科<ref name="Grave" />：
*[[綿蟹科|綿蟹科]] ''Dromiidae'' <small>De Haan, 1833</small>
*[[貝綿蟹科|貝綿蟹科]] ''Dynomenidae'' <small>Ortmann, 1892</small>
*[[Etyiidae|Etyiidae]]：化石物種。

[[ITIS|ITIS]]根據 Bowmen (1982)<ref name=bowman>{{cite book |chapter=Chapter 1: Classification of the Recent Crustacea |last1=Bowman |first1=Thomas E.|last2=Lawrence |first2=G. Abele |serieseditor1=Lawrence G. Abele, ed. |serieseditor2=Dorothy E. Bliss, ed.-in-chief	|year=1982 |title=The Biology of Crustacea, vol. 1: Systematics, the Fossil Record, and Biogeography  |pages=1-27 |publisher=Academic Press |location=New York, New York, USA |isbn=0-12-106401-8 }}</ref>，不包括[[Etyiidae|Etyiidae]]，但還包括以下兩個科<ref name=itis />：
* Family [[Dakoticancridae|Dakoticancridae]]：在 Grave ''et al.'' (2009) 升格成為一個總科<ref name="Grave" />。　
* Family [[Prosopidae|Prosopidae]] <small>[[:en:Hermann_von_Meyer|von Meyer]], 1860</small><ref name=bowman />：在 Grave ''et al.'' (2009) 歸入[[人面綿蟹總科|人面綿蟹總科]]<ref name="Grave" />。

另外，在 Grave et al. (2008) 提及的[[Tymolidae|Tymolidae]]<ref name="Grave2008">{{cite journal |last1=Ng |first1=P.K.L. |last2=Guinot |first2=D. |last3=Davie |first3=P.J.F. |year=2008 |title=Systema Brachyurorum: Part I. An annotated checklist of the extant Brachyuran crabs of the world. |journal=The Raffles Bulletin of Zoology |volume=17 |pages=1-286 |language=en }}</ref>，WoRMS亦不承認，認為只是[[圓關公蟹派|圓關公蟹科]]（[[Cyclodorippidae|Cyclodorippidae]] <small>Ortmann, 1892</small>）的[[同種異名|同種異名]]<ref name=WoRMS_246497>{{cite WoRMS |title=Tymolidae |id=246497 |last1=Vandepitte |first1=Leen |last2=Vanden Berghe |first2=Edward |date=2008-06-12 |accessdate=2015-09-23 }}</ref>。

'''藤加查爾島'''（{{lang-bn|টেঙ্গার চর}}、{{lang-en|Thengar Char、Bhasan Char}}），也譯作'''藤加沙爾島'''、'''藤加查岛'''、'''巴贊查爾島'''，是[[孟加拉灣|孟加拉灣]]北部的一座島嶼，距離海岸37英里<ref name="cnnpokharethousandsof">{{cite news|last1=Pokhare|first1=Sugam|last2=Westcott|first2=Ben|title=Thousands of Rohingya refugees may be sent to remote island|url=http://edition.cnn.com/2017/01/31/asia/myanmar-bangladesh-thengar-char/|accessdate=February 25, 2017|work=CNN|date=January 31, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170225210058/http://edition.cnn.com/2017/01/31/asia/myanmar-bangladesh-thengar-char/#|archive-date=2017-02-25|dead-url=no}}</ref>，屬[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]][[吉大港專區|吉大港專區]][[諾阿卡利縣|諾阿卡利縣]][[哈蒂亞烏帕齊拉|哈蒂亞烏帕齊拉]]，面積約300平方公里<ref name="foreignaffairsthetroublewiththengarchar">{{cite news|last1=Kullab|first1=Samya|title=The Trouble With Thengar Char|url=https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/bangladesh/2017-02-23/trouble-thengar-char|accessdate=2018-11-16|work=Foreign Affairs|date=2017-2-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170223201706/https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/bangladesh/2017-02-23/trouble-thengar-char#|archive-date=2017-02-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>，位於[[梅克納河|梅克納河]]河口，[[哈提亞島|哈提亞島]]東邊約30公里處<ref name=bbc2017/>。

藤加查爾島於2006年由梅克納河沖刷的[[淤泥|淤泥]]形成，多數地圖都未標出其位置。島上目前無人居住，且缺乏道路、堤防等建設<ref name=bbc2017>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-38799586|title=Rohingya refugees in Bangladesh face relocation to island|publisher=BBC|date=2017-1-30|access-date=2018-11-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180614180508/https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-38799586#|archive-date=2018-06-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>，6月至9月的雨季島上多數地方均被水淹沒（該島被描述為“只能在冬季進入，是海盜的避風港”）<ref name="foreignaffairsthetroublewiththengarchar"/>。2017年，孟加拉政府計劃將[[羅興亞人|羅興亞難民]]安置在藤加查爾島，而在該島上展開建設<ref name=Paul/><ref name=bd/>。

==安置難民==
2015年6月，孟加拉政府提出要將[[羅興亞人|羅興亞難民]]安置在藤加查爾島上<ref name="theguardianunconcernatbangladesh">{{cite news|title=UN concern at Bangladesh plan to move thousands of Rohingya to flooded island|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/jun/14/un-concern-at-bangladesh-plan-to-move-thousands-of-rohingya-to-flooded-island|accessdate=February 25, 2017|work=The Guardian|date=June 14, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170225123029/https://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/jun/14/un-concern-at-bangladesh-plan-to-move-thousands-of-rohingya-to-flooded-island#|archive-date=2017-02-25|dead-url=no}}</ref>，[[聯合國難民署|聯合國難民署]]認為此提案為在實行上相當困難<ref name="theguardianunconcernatbangladesh" />。但2017年1月26日，孟加拉政府仍發布了決定將羅興亞難民遷至該島的命令<ref name="cnnpokharethousandsof" /><ref name="foreignaffairsthetroublewiththengarchar" /><ref name="theguardianplantomovetoremote">{{cite news|last1=Rahman|first1=Shaikh Azizur|title=Plan to move Rohingya to remote island prompts fears of human catastrophe|url=https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/2017/feb/02/bangladesh-government-plan-move-rohingya-remote-island-human-catastrophe|accessdate=February 25, 2017|work=The Guardian|date=February 2, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170225213934/https://www.theguardian.com/global-development/2017/feb/02/bangladesh-government-plan-move-rohingya-remote-island-human-catastrophe#|archive-date=2017-02-25|dead-url=no}}</ref>，非政府組織[[人權觀察|人權觀察]]指這項決定是一起正在發生的人道危機與災難<ref name="foreignaffairsthetroublewiththengarchar" />。

[[孟加拉總理|孟加拉總理]][[謝赫·哈西娜|謝赫·哈西娜]]表示此計畫是為了紓解[[科克斯巴扎爾縣|科克斯巴扎爾縣]]難民營人滿為患的狀況，而做出的暫時性安排<ref name=Paul>{{cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-myanmar-rohingya-island/floating-island-new-home-for-rohingya-refugees-emerges-in-bay-of-bengal-idUSKCN1G603T|title=Floating Island: New home for Rohingya refugees emerges in Bay of Bengal|publisher=Reuters|author=Ruma Paul, Clare Baldwin, Andrew R.C. Marshall|date=2018-2-21|access-date=2018-11-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181024090203/https://www.reuters.com/article/us-myanmar-rohingya-island/floating-island-new-home-for-rohingya-refugees-emerges-in-bay-of-bengal-idUSKCN1G603T#|archive-date=2018-10-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>。孟加拉正與英國、中國的承包商合作，在藤加查爾島建築房屋與暴雨的避難所以供將來的難民使用<ref name=Paul/><ref name=bd>{{cite web|url=https://bdnews24.com/bangladesh/2018/09/24/hasina-will-open-bhasan-char-island-project-to-house-100000-rohingyas-on-oct-4|title=Hasina will open Bhasan Char island project to house 100,000 Rohingyas on Oct 4|publisher=bdnews|date=2018-9-24|access-date=2018-11-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180925120447/https://bdnews24.com/bangladesh/2018/09/24/hasina-will-open-bhasan-char-island-project-to-house-100000-rohingyas-on-oct-4#|archive-date=2018-09-25|dead-url=no}}</ref>。該國外交部長{{link-en|阿布·哈桑·马哈茂德·阿里|Abul Hassan Mahmood Ali}}表示政府將在該島建築學校、醫院、清真寺等，且在建設完成後才會將難民轉移至島上<ref>{{cite web|url=https://bdnews24.com/bangladesh/2017/02/05/foreign-diplomats-back-bangladesh-s-plan-to-relocate-myanmar-rohingyas-to-thengar-char|title=Foreign diplomats back Bangladesh's plan to relocate Myanmar Rohingyas to Thengar Char|publisher=bdnews|date=2017-2-5|access-date=2018-11-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170211041839/http://bdnews24.com/bangladesh/2017/02/05/foreign-diplomats-back-bangladesh-s-plan-to-relocate-myanmar-rohingyas-to-thengar-char#|archive-date=2017-02-11|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

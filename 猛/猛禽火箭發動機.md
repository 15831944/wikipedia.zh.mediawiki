'''猛禽'''（{{lang-en|Raptor}}）是[[SpaceX|SpaceX]]正在研发的一款低温<ref name="youtube.com">{{Cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hJD0MMP4nkM |title=存档副本 |access-date=2016-10-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150906075127/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hJD0MMP4nkM |archive-date=2015-09-06 |dead-url=no }}</ref>[[甲烷|甲烷]]燃料[[火箭发动机|火箭发动机]]。该发动机被计划用于高性能的[[行星際運輸系統|行星際運輸系統]]第一節與第二節。猛禽火箭發動機采用液态甲烷<ref>http://spaceref.com/news/viewsr.html?pid=47400</ref>与[[液氧|液氧]]作为推进剂<ref name=fg20120316>{{cite news |last=Rosenberg |first=Zach |title=SpaceX readies upgraded engines |url=http://www.flightglobal.com/news/articles/spacex-readies-upgraded-engines-369600/ |accessdate=2012-03-17 |newspaper=Flightglobal |date=2012-03-16 |quote=''SpaceX is in the midst of a variety of ambitious engine programmes, including the Merlin 2, a significant modification of the Merlin 1 series, and the Raptor upper stage engine. Details of both projects are tightly held.'' |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6MXM7eRIa?url=http://www.flightglobal.com/news/articles/spacex-readies-upgraded-engines-369600/ |archive-date=2014-01-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，而非猎鹰9号运载火箭所使用的煤油/液態氧。更早的猛禽火箭發動機设计中计划采用液態氢作为燃料，而不是甲烷<ref>{{cite conference|last=Markusic|first=Tom|title=SpaceX Propulsion|pages=12–15|date=2010-07-28|accessdate=2015-10-28|url=http://images.spaceref.com/news/2010/SpaceX_Propulsion.pdf|conference=46th AIAA/ASME/SAE/ASEE Joint Propulsion Conference|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160730033502/http://images.spaceref.com/news/2010/SpaceX_Propulsion.pdf|archive-date=2016-07-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

猛禽火箭發動機将有着数倍于[[默林火箭发动机#默林真空1D|默林真空1D]]发动机的推力。
更广义上的猛禽火箭發動機概念是一款高可重用性的甲烷分级燃烧发动机，用于SpaceX的下一代用于火星探索与殖民的运载火箭<ref name=sn20131025>{{cite news |last=Leone |first=Dan |title=SpaceX Could Begin Testing Methane-fueled Engine at Stennis Next Year |url=http://www.spacenews.com/article/launch-report/37859spacex-could-begin-testing-methane-fueled-engine-at-stennis-next-year |accessdate=2013-10-26 |newspaper=Space News |date=2013-10-25 |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6MXKoSexY?url=http://www.spacenews.com/article/launch-report/37859spacex-could-begin-testing-methane-fueled-engine-at-stennis-next-year |archive-date=2014-01-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref> 。根据[[伊隆·馬斯克|伊隆·馬斯克]]的说法，这个设计将能够达到完全复用的目标，并能让太空飞行的开销降低两个数量级<ref name="youtube.com"/>。

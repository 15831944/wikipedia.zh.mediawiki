'''麦芽糖酶'''（{{lang-en|Maltase}}，{{EC number|3.2.1.20}}）是一种发现于人[[小肠|小肠]]刷状边缘的酶，催化[[雙醣|雙醣]][[麥芽糖|麥芽糖]]水解为单糖[[葡萄糖|葡萄糖]]<ref name="urlMaltase – Definition from the Merriam-Webster Online Dictionary">{{cite web |url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Maltase |title=Maltase – Definition from the Merriam-Webster Online Dictionary |format= |work= |accessdate=2009-04-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Bruni CB, Sica V, Auricchio F, Covelli I | title = Further kinetic and structural characterization of the lysosomal alpha-D-glucoside glucohydrolase from cattle liver | journal = Biochimica et Biophysica Acta | volume = 212 | issue = 3 | pages = 470–7 | date = September 1970 | pmid = 5466143 | doi = 10.1016/0005-2744(70)90253-6 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Flanagan PR, Forstner GG | title = Purification of rat intestinal maltase/glucoamylase and its anomalous dissociation either by heat or by low pH | journal = The Biochemical Journal | volume = 173 | issue = 2 | pages = 553–63 | date = August 1978 | pmid = 29602 | pmc = 1185809 | doi=10.1042/bj1730553}}</ref><ref>{{cite book | chapter = Other glucosidases | title = The Enzymes |author1 = Larner, J. |date = 1960 |volume = 4 |pages = 369–378 |editor1 = Boyer, P.D. |editor2 =Lardy, H. |editor3 =Myrbäck, K. |edition  = 2nd |publisher = Academic Press |location = New York }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Sivakami S, Radhakrishnan AN | title = Purification of rabbit intestinal glucoamylase by affinity chromatography on Sephadex G-200 | journal = Indian Journal of Biochemistry & Biophysics | volume = 10 | issue = 4 | pages = 283–4 | date = December 1973 | pmid = 4792946 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Sørensen SH, Norén O, Sjöström H, Danielsen EM | title = Amphiphilic pig intestinal microvillus maltase/glucoamylase. Structure and specificity | journal = European Journal of Biochemistry | volume = 126 | issue = 3 | pages = 559–68 | date = September 1982 | pmid = 6814909 | doi = 10.1111/j.1432-1033.1982.tb06817.x }}</ref>。这种酶也发现存在于植物、细菌、酵母中。

[[吸血蝠亞科|吸血蝠亞科]]是已知惟一的一支不生成麦芽糖酶的[[脊椎动物|脊椎动物]]<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Schondube JE, Herrera-M LG, Martínez del Rio C | title = Diet and the evolution of digestion and renal function in phyllostomid bats | journal = Zoology | volume = 104 | issue = 1 | pages = 59–73 | date = 2001 | pmid = 16351819 | doi = 10.1078/0944-2006-00007 | url = http://www.uwyo.edu/cmdelrio/site/publications_files/bats,%20isotopes,%20and%20kidneys.pdf }}</ref>。

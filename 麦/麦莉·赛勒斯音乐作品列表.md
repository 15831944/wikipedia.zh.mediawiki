1|Singles=28<!-- As of "Younger Now"-->}}美国歌手[[麦莉·赛勒斯|麦莉·赛勒斯]]发行了五张[[录音室专辑|录音室专辑]]、一张[[现场专辑|现场专辑]]、一张[[迷你专辑|迷你专辑]]、28支单曲和7支推广单曲。麦莉以[[迪士尼频道|迪士尼频道]]电视连续剧《[[汉娜·蒙塔娜|汉娜·蒙塔娜]]》为主角而闻名。截至2014年，麦莉及以角色“汉娜·蒙塔娜”发行的专辑销售总量为1,590万<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/6077135/miley-cyrus-bangerz-hits-1-million-in-sales|title=Miley Cyrus' 'Bangerz' hits 1 Million In Sales|accessdate=December 14, 2014|date=May 1, 2014|last=Caulfield|first=Keith}}</ref>。{{TransH}}In June 2007, the series' second soundtrack and Cyrus' debut studio album were jointly released by [[Walt_Disney_Records|Walt Disney Records]] and [[Hollywood_Records|Hollywood Records]] as the [[double_album|double album]] ''[[Hannah_Montana_2:_Meet_Miley_Cyrus|Hannah Montana 2: Meet Miley Cyrus]]''. The project reached number one in the United States. Its latter disc included the single "[[See_You_Again_(Miley_Cyrus_song)|See You Again]]", which became Cyrus' first track to impact the top-ten in the United States, Australia, and Canada. On her debut live album ''[[Best_of_Both_Worlds_Concert_(soundtrack)|Best of Both Worlds Concert]]'', released in March 2008, Cyrus performed seven songs as herself and seven tracks as her title character [[Hannah_Montana_(character)|Hannah Montana]].

The singer's second studio album ''[[Breakout_(Miley_Cyrus_album)|Breakout]]'' was released in July 2008, topping the United States, Australian, and Canadian charts. Its lead single "[[7_Things|7 Things]]" reached the top 10 in the United States, Australia, and Norway. She recorded two tracks as herself for the [[Hannah_Montana:_The_Movie_(soundtrack)|soundtrack]] for the feature film ''[[Hannah_Montana:_The_Movie|Hannah Montana: The Movie]]'', which came out in March 2009. The soundtrack's lead single "[[The_Climb_(Miley_Cyrus_song)|The Climb]]" peaked within the top five in the United States, Australia, Canada, Ireland, and Norway. Cyrus' first extended play ''[[The_Time_of_Our_Lives_(EP)|The Time of Our Lives]]'' followed in August 2009, and its track "[[Party_in_the_U.S.A.|Party in the U.S.A.]]" made the top five in the United States, Canada, Ireland, and New Zealand. She released her third studio album ''[[Can't_Be_Tamed|Can't Be Tamed]]'' in June 2010, peaking within the top five in the United States, Australia, Austria, Canada, Germany, Ireland, Italy, and New Zealand. While the [[Can't_Be_Tamed|title track]] reached the top 10 in the United States, Canada, Ireland, and New Zealand, the album was less commercially successful than her past efforts.

Cyrus' fourth studio album ''[[Bangerz|Bangerz]]'' was released through [[RCA_Records|RCA Records]] in October 2013, topping the United States, Australia, Canada, Ireland, Norway, and the United Kingdom charts. Its songs "[[We_Can't_Stop|We Can't Stop]]" and "[[Wrecking_Ball_(Miley_Cyrus_song)|Wrecking Ball]]" both peaked at number one in the United Kingdom while former also topped the charts in New Zealand and the latter went number one in the United States and Canada. Her fifth album, ''[[Miley_Cyrus_&_Her_Dead_Petz|Miley Cyrus & Her Dead Petz]]'', was released via streaming in August 2015 and did not chart. She will release her sixth album, ''[[Younger_Now|Younger Now]]'', in September 2017. Its lead single "[[Malibu_(Miley_Cyrus_song)|Malibu]]" reached the top five in Australia, Canada, Norway, and New Zealand.{{TransF}}

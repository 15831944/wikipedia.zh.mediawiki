'''麦迪逊·L·胡贝尔'''（{{lang-en|Madison L. Hubbell}}，{{bd|1991年|2月24日||}}）生于密歇根州兰辛，是一名美国女子花样滑冰运动员，主攻冰舞<ref name=ISU-1718/>。她的哥哥凯费尔同样是一名花样滑冰选手，两人在2001年至2011年间搭档参赛<ref name=ST070218>{{cite news |url= http://www.skatetoday.com/2007/02/18/duos-dazzling-dance-debut/ |title= Duos Dazzling Dance Debut |first= Barry |last= Mittan |work= SkateToday |date=2007-02-18 |accessdate=2019-03-27 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180113183904/http://www.skatetoday.com/2007/02/18/duos-dazzling-dance-debut/ |archivedate=2018-01-13}}</ref><ref name=USFS110512>{{cite news |url= http://www.usfsa.org/Story.asp?id=46152&type=media |title= Madison Hubbell and Keiffer Hubbell Announce End of Partnership |publisher= U.S. Figure Skating |date= 2011-05-12 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20120324071503/http://www.usfsa.org/Story.asp?id=46152&type=media |archivedate= 2012-03-24 |deadurl= unfit }}</ref>。之后她开始和[[扎卡里·多诺霍|扎卡里·多诺霍]]搭档<ref name=USFS110512/>。2014年，她开始和西班牙冰舞选手[[Adrián_Díaz|Adrián Díaz]]交往，两人在2018年4月正式宣布订婚<ref name=TUSA180413>{{cite news |url= https://www.teamusa.org/News/2018/April/13/Madison-Hubbell-Announces-Engagement-To-Spanish-Ice-Dancer-Adrian-Diaz |title= Madison Hubbell Announces Engagement To Spanish Ice Dancer Adrian Diaz |first= Brandon |last= Penny |work= United States Olympic Committee |date= 2018-04-13 |accessdate=2019-03-27}}</ref>。

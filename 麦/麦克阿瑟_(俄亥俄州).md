'''麦克阿瑟'''（{{lang|en|McArthur}}）是[[美国|美国]][[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[文顿县_(俄亥俄州)|文顿县]]的一个村，是文顿县的县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=June 7, 2011 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531210815/http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=May 31, 2011 }}</ref>  2010年人口普查时人口为1,701人。

==历史==
麦克阿瑟在1815年被规划建设。<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=3elPAQAAIAAJ&pg=PA1215#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=History of Hocking Valley, Ohio | publisher=Inter-State Publishing Co. | year=1883 | pages=1215-1216}}</ref>它以1812年战争中的军队将军邓肯·麦克阿瑟命名。<ref>{{cite book|last=Gannett|first=Henry|title=The Origin of Certain Place Names in the United States|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9V1IAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA193|year=1905|publisher=U.S. Government Printing Office|page=193}}</ref>

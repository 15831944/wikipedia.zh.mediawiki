'''万民中央教会'''是一个[[新教|新教]]教会，1982年由[[李载禄|李载禄]]在[[韩国|韩国]]建立<ref>{{citation|title=When Doors Open in No. Korea, So. Korean Christians 'Willing to be Martyred'|periodical=Crosswalk.com (Religion Today)|date=2008-04-08|accessdate=2008-04-08|url=http://www.crosswalk.com/news/religiontoday/11572695/}}</ref>。传教士罗伯特·约翰逊比较了美国和韩国教会。他说起韩国人对基督教的热诚令人印象深刻，称他们整夜祈祷，然后去上班，甚至出席周三和周五夜间的聚会，然后在周日，他们整天留在教堂里。<ref>{{citation|title=CP World Report: Anders Breivik, 'The Voice' Bible, Syria, North Korea|periodical=World Report, The Christian Post in partnership with Crossroads, WEA |date=2012-04-17|accessdate=2012-04-17|url=http://www.christianpost.com/news/cp-world-report-anders-breivik-the-voice-bible-syria-north-korea-73367/}}</ref>2004年，李建立了全球性的基督教电视网络，向世界各地传播福音，于2005年10月推出<ref>{{citation|title=24 hour missionary satellite broadcaster, GCN|periodical=Deulsori Times|date=2005-09-26 |accessdate=2005-09-26 |url=http://www.deulsoritimes.co.kr/?var=news_view&page=1&code=101&no=15277&b_no=&keyword=%C0%CC%C0%E7%B7%CF}}</ref>。万民中央教会拥有的教会数量居韩国首位<ref name="The Korea Herald">{{citation|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/national/Detail.jsp?newsMLId=20081011000006|periodical=The Korea Herald|title=Manmin church celebrates anniversary
|date=2010-04-04|accessdate=2010-04-04}}</ref>李声称有120,000名信徒遍布世界各地的9,000个支教会<ref name="Christian Daily News">{{citation|url=http://www.icdn.tv/s05_1.htm?mo。de=read&read_no=12245&now_page=284&menu=|periodical=Christian Daily News|title=4th GCN Anniversary and celebration performance|date=2009-11-05|accessdate=2009-11-05}}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name="Korea Times">{{citation|url=http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/art/2011/03/135_82267.html|periodical=Korea Times|date=2011-03-01|accessdate=2011-03-01|title=Lee's books featured in Israel book fair}}</ref>。

该教会已扩展到美国、日本、南美洲及非洲，信徒呈爆炸式增长<ref>{{citation|title=Korean culture embroidered Baltic sea with Korean traditional fan dance·costumes·Taekwondo |periodical=Wolgan Joongang (Monthly Joongang Magazine) |date=2011-02-03 |accessdate=2011-02-03 |url=http://live.joinsmsn.com/news/article/article.asp?total_id=5010062 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110713114620/http://live.joinsmsn.com/news/article/article.asp?total_id=5010062 |archivedate=2011-07-13 }}</ref>。

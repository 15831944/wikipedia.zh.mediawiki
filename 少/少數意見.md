《'''少數意見'''》（{{kor|k='''소수의견'''}}；{{lang-en|'''''Minority Opinion'''''}}），是2015年6月上映的一部韓國[[劇情片|劇情片]]，由[[金成宰|金成宰]]執導，[[尹啟相|尹啟相]]、[[柳海真|柳海真]]及[[金玉彬|金玉彬]]主演<ref>{{cite web|last1=Oh|first1=Mi-jung|title=Yoon Kye Sang's ''Minority Opinion'' to Start Shooting on March 21|url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/en/article/31842/minority-opinion-starring-yoon-kye-sang-yu-hae-jin-and-kim-ok-bin-to-start-shooting-soon|website=enewsWorld|accessdate=2017-08-26|date=2013-03-20}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Lee|first1=Sun-min|title=Yoon Kye-sang is back in the movies|url=http://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/article.aspx?aid=2968934|website=Korea JoongAng Daily|accessdate=2017-08-26|date=2013-03-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Ahn|first1=Woorim|title=Who Respects ''Minority Opinion''?|url=http://www.bntnews.co.uk/app/news.php?nid=21927|website=BNTNews|accessdate=2017-08-26|date=2015-06-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Kim|first1=Yeon-ji|title=Yoon still 'craves' acting|url=http://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/Article.aspx?aid=3005981|website=Korea JoongAng Daily|accessdate=2017-08-26|date=2015-06-30}}</ref>。本電影改編自[[孫雅藍|孫雅藍]]的同名小說《少數意見》，該小說以2009年於韓國[[首爾|首爾]]發生的「[[龍山區_(首爾)|龍山]]悲劇」為藍本，事緣受該區[[市區更新|市區更新]]影響而被逼搬遷的近40户家庭，因不滿政府沒有給予他們足夠的補償而與警方發生衝突，事件最後導致5名租戶及一名警察不幸身亡。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Heo|first1=Hwan-ju|script-title=ko:철거민이 경찰을 죽였다...법정에서 진실이 밝혀질까?|url=http://www.pressian.com/news/article.html?no=100516|website=Pressian|language=ko|accessdate=2017-08-26|date=2010-05-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Yongsan disaster revisited|url=https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/opinon/2013/01/202_114439.html|website=The Korea Times|accessdate=2017-08-26|date=2012-07-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Lee|first1=Seong-hee|title=4 Years Since the Yongsan Disaster: "They Killed My Family, Yet It's Still a Vacant Lot. == 上映 ==
本電影的拍攝期早於2013年6月3日完結，但卻因電影中的政治內容，直至2015年才得到發行商的協助使其順利上映<ref>{{cite web|last1=Lee|first1=Eun-ah|title=Yoon Kye-sang, Kim Ok-vin's New Film Cranks Up|url=http://www.tenasia.com/archives/59174|website=TenAsia|date=2013-06-10|accessdate=2017-08-26|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140227163803/http://www.tenasia.com/archives/59174|archivedate=2014-02-27}}</ref>，本電影最終在2015年6月24日在韓國上映<ref>{{cite web|last1=Won|first1=Ho-jung|title=Herald Review: Ugly side of justice rears head in ''Minority Opinion''|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20150630001054|website=The Korea Herald|accessdate=2017-08-26|date=2015-06-30}}</ref>

<!-- Definition and symptoms -->
'''阻生智齿'''（'''阻生第三臼齿'''）是[[智齿|智齿]]受其他牙齿影响而未能完全{{tsl|en|tooth eruption|牙萌出}}的{{tsl|en|Tooth impaction|阻生齿}}。阻生智齿可能会造成{{tsl|en|pericoronitis|冠周炎}}或损坏相邻牙。通常考虑第三臼齿的萌出角度、牙根发育阶段、阻生深度、萌出可用空间以及第三臼齿的尺寸。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.oralhealthgroup.com/features/third-molar-surgery-a-review-of-current-controversies-in-prophylactic-removal-of-wisdom-teeth/|title=Third Molar Surgery: A Review Of Current Controversies In Prophylactic Removal Of Wisdom Teeth - Oral Health Group|newspaper=Oral Health Group|language=en-US|access-date=2016-11-21}}</ref>

<!-- Cause and screening-->
智齿变为阻生的原因是牙尺寸与下颌尺寸不匹配。阻生智齿的分类按照阻生方向、相比较于邻牙咬合面的深度、覆盖在[[牙冠|牙冠]]上的[[牙龈|牙龈]]或牙槽骨的量。<ref>Hupp, James R., et. al.  ''Contemporary Maxillofacial Surgery, 6E'', Elsevier-Mosby, 2014. ISBN 978-0-323-09177-0</ref><ref name="Gintaras">{{cite journal |title=Mandibular Third Molar Impaction: Review of Literature and a Proposal of a Classification (review)|vauthors=Juodzbalys G, Daugela P |journal=J Oral Maxillofac Res |date=Apr–Jun 2013 |volume=4 |issue=2 |pages=e1 |doi=10.5037/jomr.2013.4201 |pmid=24422029 |pmc=3886113}}</ref>也可按是否有临床症状分类。<ref name="DodsonClinics">{{cite journal |title=The management of the asymptomatic, disease-free wisdom tooth: removal versus retention. (review) |author=Dodson TB |journal=Atlas Oral Maxillofac Surg Clin North Am |date=Sep 2012 |volume=20 |issue=2 |pages=169–76 |doi=10.1016/j.cxom.2012.06.005 |pmid=23021394}}</ref>通常在青春期后期邻牙出现阻生症状时才发现智齿问题。可用{{tsl|en|panoramic radiograph|口腔全景发射线检查}}筛选。

<!-- Treatment -->
阻生智齿感染可用抗生素、[[牙結石清除術|牙結石清除術]]（洗牙）、{{tsl|en|gingivectomy|龈切除术}}做初步治疗。但通常不会奏效并发生其它并发症。最常用办法是拔除智齿。其风险与[[拔牙术|拔牙]]的难度成正比。如果有高风险的{{tsl|en|inferior alveolar nerve|下牙槽神经}}，可能仅拔除智齿的牙冠，故意保留智齿的牙根，这称为{{tsl|en|coronectomy|智齿冠切术}}。其风险是残留牙根的顽固性感染。

==分类==
[[File:Impacted_wisdom_teeth_summary.ogv|thumb]]
牙齿可分类为发育牙、{{tsl|en|Tooth eruption|萌出牙}}、埋伏牙（不受其它牙影响但是无法萌出）、{{tsl|en|Tooth impaction|阻生牙}}。

智齿[[牙齿发育|发育]]于14岁至25岁，16岁时已经有50%的牙根形成。25岁时有95%的人智齿全部萌出。但是智齿的移动在25岁以后仍会继续。<ref name="Petersons2nd">{{cite book |title=Peterson's Principles of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery |edition=2nd |publisher=PMPH-USA |last1=Peterson |first1=Larry J. |last2=Miloro |first2=Michael |year=2004 |isbn=978-1-55009-234-9}}</ref>{{rp|140}}
 
临床统计结果，11%的阻生智齿发病并有临床症状，0.6%有症状但未发病，51%无症状但发病，37%无症状且未发病。<ref name="DodsonClinics" />

阻生智齿按照其方向（最常见的是向前倾斜，称“近中”，阻生深度、是否感染发炎来描述。<ref name="Petersons2nd"/>{{rp|143–144}} 其中年龄是最相关于拔除智齿的难度与复杂度<ref name="Pogrel2012">{{cite journal |title=What Is the Effect of Timing of Removal on the Incidence and Severity of Complications (review) |author=Pogrel MA |journal=J Oral Maxillofac Surg |year=2012 |volume=70 |issue=Suppl 1 |pages=37–40}}</ref>而不是阻生方向.<ref name="Bali2013">{{cite journal |title=Is Pederson Index a True Predictive Difficulty Index for Impacted Mandibular Third Molar Surgery? A Meta-analysis. |vauthors=Bali A, Bali D, Sharma A, Verma G |journal=J Oral Maxillofac Surg |date=Sep 2013 |volume=12 |issue=3 |pages=359–364 |doi=10.1007/s12663-012-0435-x}}</ref>

Preparation of the copper(II)​-​glycine complex. ''Hiroshima Daigaku Suichikusangakubu Kiyo'', 1978. 17 (1): 35-41.</ref>氧化铜和甘氨酸的反应也能得到产物。<ref name=Rafiqi>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0379677913001185|doi=10.1016/j.synthmet.2013.03.009|pages=32–38|title=Doping polyaniline with copper bisglycinate[Cu(gly)2]—Synthesis, characterization and thermal study|volume=171|journal=Synthetic Metals|date=2013-05|issn=0379-6779|accessdate=2018-08-15|author=Ferooze Ahmad Rafiqi, Mohammad Sideeq Rather, Kowsar Majid}}</ref>

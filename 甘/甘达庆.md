'''甘达庆''' ({{lang-en|Derek Tai-Ching Kan}})是一位美国华人政治人物，[[美国运输部|美国运输部]]副部长。

==生平==
毕业于[[南加州大学|南加州大学]]，[[斯坦福商学研究生院|斯坦福商学研究生院]]和[[伦敦政治经济学院|伦敦政治经济学院]]，毕业后相继在[[贝恩策略顾问|贝恩策略顾问]]，[[艾略特管理集团|艾略特管理集团]]和[[来福车|来福车]]任职。2015年被时任美国总统[[贝拉克·奥巴马|贝拉克·奥巴马]]提名为[[美铁|美铁]]董事会成员。<ref>{{cite news |last1=Primack |first1=Dan |title=Amtrak's Newest Board Member Works at Lyft|url=http://fortune.com/2016/01/21/amtrak-board-member-lyft/|accessdate=May 18, 2017 |publisher=Fortune|date=January 21, 2016}}</ref>2017年4月被时任美国总统[[唐納·川普|唐納·川普]]提拔为[[美国运输部|美国运输部]]副部长。<ref>{{cite news|last1=della Cava|first1=Marco|title=Trump nominates Lyft exec to join Transportation Department|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/tech/talkingtech/2017/04/06/lyft-exec-may-join-trumps-dot/100154370/|accessdate=May 18, 2017 |publisher=USA Today|date=April 6, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Dickey|first1=Megan Rose|title=Trump plans to nominate Lyft’s Derek Kan to transportation department |url=https://techcrunch.com/2017/04/06/trump-reportedly-plans-to-nominate-lyfts-derek-kan-to-transportation-department/|accessdate=May 18, 2017 |publisher=TechCrunch|date=April 6, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=President Donald J. Trump Announces Intent to Nominate Derek Kan to the Department of Transportation|url=https://www.whitehouse.gov/the-press-office/2017/04/06/president-donald-j-trump-announces-intent-nominate-derek-kan-department|accessdate=May 18, 2017|publisher=The White House|date=April 6, 2017}}</ref>他同时也是[[美国行政管理和预算局|美国行政管理和预算局]]成员。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Zanona|first1=Melanie|title=Trump taps Lyft manager for No. 3 spot at Transportation Department |url=http://thehill.com/policy/transportation/327778-trump-taps-lyft-exec-for-no-3-spot-at-transportation-department|accessdate=May 18, 2017 |publisher=The Hill |date=April 7, 2017}}</ref>

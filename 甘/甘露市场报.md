《'''甘露市场报'''》（{{lang-bn|অমৃতবাজার পত্রিকা}}）是南亚历史最悠久的[[日报|日报]]之一，也是孟加拉国历史最悠久的日报。最初以[[东部天城文|东部天城文]]出版，后来演变为英语格式，在[[加尔各答|加尔各答]]和[[克塔克|克塔克]]、[[蘭契|蘭契]]和[[安拉阿巴德|安拉阿巴德]]等地出版。<ref name="agent">{{Cite news|url=http://www.tribuneindia.com/2003/20030102/nation.htm#11|title=Amrita Bazar Patrika may be relaunched|last=Gupta|first=Subhrangshu|date=2 January 2003|work=[[The_Tribune_(Chandigarh)|The Tribune]]|accessdate=2006-12-28}}</ref><ref>[https://rni.nic.in/ Registrar of Newspapers for India] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150313012936/https://rni.nic.in/|date=13 March 2015}}</ref>这份报纸在出版123年后于1991年停刊，2006年在[[达卡|达卡]]重新推出，现在再次以[[孟加拉语|孟加拉语]]出版。<ref name="ABCIND">{{Cite news|url=https://www.indiatoday.in/magazine/society-the-arts/media/story/19910715-debts-kill-123-year-old-english-daily-amrita-bazar-patrika-815485-1991-07-15|title=Debts kill 123-year-old English daily Amrita Bazar Patrika|last=Banerjee|first=Ruben|date=15 July 1991|publisher=India Today|accessdate=24 September 2018}}</ref>

《甘露市場報》創刊於1868年2月20日，由西西尔·高希（Sisir Ghosh）和莫蒂·拉尔·高希（Moti Lal Ghosh）发起。西西尔·高希和莫蒂·拉尔·高希开创了《甘露市场报》作为周报的先河。它首先由没有受过正规大学教育的莫蒂·拉尔·高希编辑。它已经建立了自己的读者群，成为班加利（Bengalee）的竞争对手。<ref>''Political Agitators in India, A Confidential Report'', pp. 15, Available in Digitized form on Archives.org, contributed by Library of University of Toronto, Digitized for Microsoft Corporation by Internet Archive in 2007, provided by University of Toronto, accessed on 8 June 2009 and link at https://archive.org/details/politicalagitato00slsnuoft</ref>

'''甘達亞特'''是印度[[奧里薩邦|奧里薩邦]]一個種姓<ref>{{cite book |title=Politics and Social Change |first=Frederick George |last=Bailey |publisher=University of California Press |year=1970 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=VNHRWtDWsRIC&pg=PA132 |page=132 |quote="...the large Chasa and Khandayat cultivating castes of the Orissa plains"}}</ref>，意为“劍之師”，“{{lang|hi|खंडा}}”（Khanda）是一种剑，“{{lang|hi|आयत}}”（ayata）意为“控制”<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://odisha.gov.in/e-magazine/Orissareview/2014/Dec/engpdf/48-50.pdf |title=Martial Art Tradition of Odisha |date=December 2014 |accessdate=2015-12-16 |website=OdishaReview |publisher=Govt. of Odisha |last=Mohapatra |first=Dr. Hemanta Kumar |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151222123049/http://odisha.gov.in/e-magazine/Orissareview/2014/Dec/engpdf/48-50.pdf |archivedate=2015-12-22 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.iakoweb.com/pakhada.html|title=Paika Akhada|last=Indian Association of Kickboxing Organisations|date=2013-02-09}}</ref>。

他們宣稱是[[剎帝利|剎帝利]]但一般是相對富裕的農民，而一些人則是收租者，村長和地主。學者們認為，與剎帝利的聯繫可能是他們社區的一種主張，其中一些人利用他們的軍事歷史來提高自己的社會地位並使他們對其他人的控制合法化<ref>{{cite book |title=Situating Social History: Orissa, 1800-1997 |first=Biswamoy |last=Pati |publisher=Orient Blackswan |year=2001 |isbn=978-8-12502-007-3 |page=35 |url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=uJtnQN3KueEC&pg=PA35}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |title=India's Princely States: People, Princes and Colonialism |editor1-first=Waltraud |editor1-last=Ernst |editor2-first=Biswamoy |editor2-last=Pati |publisher=Routledge |year=2007 |isbn=978-1-13411-988-2 |chapter=The Order of Legitimacy: Princely Orissa |pages=87, 89 |first=Biswamoy |last=Pati |url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=9eKbW3ukh9oC&pg=PA87}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |title=Community, Self and Identity |editor-first=Bhabagrahi |editor1-last=Misra |editor2-first=James |editor2-last=Preston |publisher=Walter de Gruyter |year=1978 |isbn=978-3-110802-65-8 |url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=jgDTCVW51WkC&pg=PA22 |page=22 |chapter=Gods, Kings, and the Caste System in India |first=L. K. |last=Mahapatra}}</ref>。

他們原先是信仰傳統宗教但在信仰印度教後被授予此種姓，地位也上升了。

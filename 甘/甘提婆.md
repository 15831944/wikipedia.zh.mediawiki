'''甘提婆'''（{{Lang-sa|गाण्डीव}}；[[IAST|IAST]]：{{IAST|Gāṇḍīva}}），又译'''甘狄拨'''，古印度史诗《[[摩诃婆罗多|摩诃婆罗多]]》中英雄[[阿周那|阿周那]]所持之弓<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=HkUQYvw2HGkC|title=Vyasa's Mahabharatam|author=Bharadvaja Sarma, Vyāsa, Bharadvaja Sarma|publisher=Academic Publishers|page=844}}</ref>。阿周那掌控此弓，克敌制胜，得名“甘提婆之持有者”（{{lang|sa|गाण्डीवधारी}}，{{IAST|Gāṇḍīvadhārī}}）。<ref> {{Cite book | author = Ganga Ram Garg | title = Encyclopaedia of the Hindu World | pages = 618 | ISBN = 9788170223733 | accessdate = 2018-06-25 | url = https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=0U2QRpDv2KMC | language = en }} </ref>

== 传说 ==
《火烧甘味林篇》中记载，火神[[阿耆尼|阿耆尼]]欲吞噬{{le|甘味林|Khandava Forest}}，恢复昔日的力量与荣光，便向奎师那和阿周那这两位英雄寻求帮助，其中阿周那是有史以来最为伟大的射手。阿周那向阿耆尼寻求一件可与其武技、力量相称的趁手武器，最终阿耆尼为其提供了一架旗帜上饰有神猴[[哈奴曼|哈奴曼]]图案的战车、阿周那梦寐以求的武器甘提婆，以及两个箭支取之不尽的箭袋。<ref name="Mahabharata">{{cite book |title=The Mahabharata of Krishna-Dwaipayana Vyasa: Adi parva. Sabha parva |date=1883 |publisher=Bharata Press |url=https://books.google.com.bz/books?id=Ym_NAQAACAAJ&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_atb#v=onepage&q=Gandiva&f=false |language=en}}</ref><ref name="Vyasa's Mahabharatam">{{cite book |title=Vyasa's Mahabharatam |date=2008 |publisher=Academic Publishers |isbn=9788189781682 |url=https://books.google.co.in/books?redir_esc=y&id=HkUQYvw2HGkC&q=Gandiva#v=snippet&q=Gandiva&f=false |language=en}}</ref>

[[俱卢之战|俱卢之战]]中，甘提婆大放异彩，阿周那亦因此立于不败之地。许多伟大的战士和神灵皆成为阿周那的弓下亡魂，如{{le|信度王国|Sindhu Kingdom}}国王{{le|胜车|Jayadratha}}<ref>{{Cite web |url = http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m07/m07142.htm |title = The Mahabharata, Book 7: Drona Parva: Jayadratha-Vadha Parva: Section CXLV |language = en |accessdate = 2018-06-25 }}</ref>、太阳神[[苏利耶|苏利耶]]之子[[迦尔纳|迦尔纳]]。<ref>{{Cite web |url = http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m08/m08094.htm |title = The Mahabharata, Book 8: Karna Parva: Section 94 |language = en |accessdate = 2018-06-25 }}</ref>

据《摩诃婆罗多》中《升天篇》所载，青铜时代（即德伐波罗时）将要结束时，[[奎师那|奎师那]]之灵识离开肉身，回归天国。此时的阿周那无法挽弓射箭，亦无法记住关键的咒语以召唤神器。在[[般度族|般度五子]]前往天国之途中，阿周那邂逅阿耆尼，遵其旨意将弓与箭袋扔入水中，归还给水神[[伐楼拿|伐楼拿]]。<ref name="Vyasa's Mahabharatam" />

== 特征 ==
该弓周身装饰华美，光彩溢目；其威力相当于10万把普通弓弩，坚不可摧，诸天神与众[[乾闼婆|乾闼婆]]皆敬拜之<ref name="Mahabharata" />。持弓者通过辛劳努力得来的自信与骄傲皆满溢其中。<ref>{{cite book |last1=Srivastava |first1=Diwaker Ikshit |title=Decoding the Metaphor Mahabharata |date=2017 |publisher=Leadstart Publishing PvtLtd |isbn=9789352010004 |url=https://books.google.co.in/books?id=ZmxCDwAAQBAJ&pg=PT165&source=gbs_toc_r&cad=4#v=onepage&q&f=false |language=en}}</ref>

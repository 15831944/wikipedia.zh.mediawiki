'''齒股蠅屬'''（學名：''{{lang|la|Hydrotaea}}''）是[[家蠅科|家蠅科]]之下一個[[雙翅目|雙翅目]][[昆蟲|昆蟲]]的[[属|属]]{{r|TaiBIF}}。雖然幾乎全球都可見到本屬物種的踪影，但在較溫暖的氣候更常見。在夏季，牠們時常會在[[糞便|糞便]]上出現，在[[家畜|家畜]]的排遺附近很常見。本屬已知物種有超過130種，當中最常見的為{{tsl|en|Hydrotaea aenescens|}}{{r|Skidmore88}}。

如同其他同科的屬，本屬物種無論在經濟方面或公共衛生方面都具有[[#司法科学的重要性|司法科学]]領域的重要性。無論是在[[人類|人類]][[医学|医学]]或[[兽医学|兽医学]]這兩方面，本屬物種均被多個權威機構認定為最重要的昆蟲{{r|Bay88}}。
已證明部分本屬物種會將某些疾病的病原體攜帶並傳播給溫血動物，包括人類<ref name="Loeschcke04"/>。研究人員繼續監測這些疾病的[[载体_(生物学)|傳播媒介，]]因為它們與小母牛的乳房發生的[[夏季乳房炎|夏季乳房炎]]（summer mastitis）的傳播有關<ref name="Chirico97"/>。

== Description ==
<!-- [[Larva|Larva]] -->[[幼体|幼体]]e of this genus are often <!-- [[Polymorphism_(biology)|dimorphic]] -->[[多态性_(生物学)|多态性 (生物学)]] obligate <!-- [[carnivore|carnivore]] -->[[肉食動物|肉食動物]]s. The second and third {{tsl|en|instar|}}s are predators, and the first is also carnivorous in some species. Some species have <!-- [[cannibalism_(zoology)|cannibalistic]] -->[[同类相食|同类相食]] final instars. The smallest larvae are under 6 mm<ref name="Adrienne09">{{Cite book| last = Brundage| first = A.| title = Muscidae and Other Families| location = Texas A&M University Forensic Entomology Lecture| publisher= Texas A&M University| date = March 2, 2009 }}</ref> and the largest are up to 16 mm.<ref name="Sukontason">{{cite journal | last = Sukontason | first = K. L.| title = Ultrastructure of spiracles of ''Musca domestica'' and ''Hydrotaea chalcogaster'' (Diptera: Muscidae)  | journal = Parasitol Res | volume = 100 | issue = 1 | pages = 19–23  | publisher = Springer-Verlag 2006 | date = 20 April 2006 | doi = 10.1007/s00436-006-0221-0 | pmid = 16763837|display-authors=etal}}</ref> The abdomen has ventral welts and large <!-- [[spiracle|spiracle]] -->[[氣門|氣門]]s with {{tsl|en|Sinuosity|}} slits.<ref name="Skidmore88"/>

Adults commonly feed on the <!-- [[blood|blood]] -->[[血液|血液]] of <!-- [[mammal|mammal]] -->[[哺乳动物|哺乳动物]]s. They are able to reopen wounds that are almost completely healed. Mouthpart <!-- [[morphology_(biology)|morphology]] -->[[形態學_(生物學)|形態學 (生物學)]] in the genus ranges from sponging mouthparts to mouths with rasping teeth. Many species have enlarged prestomal teeth. The {{tsl|en|metathorax|}} spiracle is covered in long, thick {{tsl|en|seta|}}e.<ref name="Sukontason"/> The average male ''Hydrotaea'' is 6.5-8.5 mm and the average female is 5.75-7.5 mm. They are very light brown to bluish black with large, red eyes and plumose <!-- [[Antenna_(biology)|antennae]] -->[[觸角|觸角]].

==分佈及棲息地==
本屬物種大多数分布在[[北半球|北半球]]{{r|zgmjswxjkzzz}}[[古北界|古北界]]的[[欧洲|欧洲]]、[[亚洲|亚洲]]和[[北美洲|北美洲]]比較和暖的地區{{r|Skidmore88}}，在[[非洲|非洲]]南部、[[南美洲|南美洲]]和[[澳洲区|澳洲区]]亦有少数种分布{{r|zgmjswxjkzzz|Skidmore88}}。這些齒股蠅通常都在[[市区|市区]]環境棲息，會被[[发酵|发酵]]了的東西吸引，常見於[[植物|植物]]、[[糞便|糞便]]及[[分解作用|分解中的]][[腐肉|腐肉]]，主要是死去超過十日的屍體{{r|Skidmore88}}。

==Life cycle==
The <!-- [[Biological_life_cycle|life cycle]] -->[[生物生命週期|生物生命週期]] of ''Hydrotaea rostrata'' has been studied in the field of <!-- [[forensic_entomology|forensic entomology]] -->[[法醫昆蟲學|法醫昆蟲學]]. Mature flies seek out decay, preferably in material that has been dead for an extended amount of time. Masses of <!-- [[maggot|maggot]] -->[[蛆|蛆]]s, which are often characteristic of other species of flies, are not typical of ''H. rostrata''. The larvae of this species progress through three stages, or {{tsl|en|instar|}}s, which are then followed by the formation of a <!-- [[pupa|pupa]] -->[[蛹|蛹]]. The adult fly emerges from the pupa. The developmental process from egg to adult takes between 518.4 and 1555.6 hours, according to one study in Australia. Development is more rapid in warmer temperatures of {{convert|19|-|30|C|F}}. In cooler temperatures of {{convert|12|-|18|C|F}}, development may take up to 64.5 days.<ref name="Dadour">{{cite journal

==Associated pathogens==
Research has incriminated ''Hydrotaea irritans'' as a vector of summer {{tsl|en|mastitis|}}, a <!-- [[bovine|bovine]] -->[[牛亚科|牛亚科]] infection that can be caused by multiple suspected <!-- [[bacteria|bacteria]] -->[[细菌|细菌]].<ref name="Chirico97">{{cite journal
  | last = Chirico
  | first = J.| title = Summer mastitis experimentally induced by ''Hydrotaea irritans'' exposed to bacteria
  | journal = Medical and Veterinary Entomology
  | volume = 11
  | pages = 187–192
  | year = 1997
  | doi = 10.1111/j.1365-2915.1997.tb00312.x
  | pmid = 9226651
  | issue = 2|display-authors=etal}}
</ref> The research allowed flies inoculated with select bacteria from a sample representing summer mastitis to come in contact with the {{tsl|en|udder|}}s of <!-- [[cattle|cattle]] -->[[家牛|家牛]]. The bacteria appeared in some of the cows and on the bodies of just over a third of the doctored flies. The evidence indicates that ''H. irritans'' transmits the bacterial species ''<!-- [[Staphylococcus_aureus|Staphylococcus aureus]] -->[[金黃色葡萄球菌|金黃色葡萄球菌]]'', ''{{tsl|en|Streptococcus dysgalactiae|}}'', ''{{tsl|en|Streptococcus uberis|}}'', ''{{tsl|en|Streptococcus agalactiae|}}'', ''{{tsl|en|Arcanobacterium pyogenes|}}'', ''<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[Peptostreptococcus_indolicus|Peptostreptococcus indolicus]]'', and ''{{tsl|en|Fusobacterium necrophorum|}}''.<ref name="Chirico97"/>

''H. irritans'' attacks many other warm-blooded animals, including humans.<ref name="Loeschcke04"/>

==司法科学的重要性==
本屬物種在世界各地[[司法科学|司法科学]]個案方面有其重要性，因為這些物種的存在及數量顯示了[[尸体现象|尸体现象]]（[[post-mortem_interval|post-mortem interval]]，PMI）發生的時間{{r|Forensic}}。這些物種會在屍體開始[[分解作用|分解作用]]之後四至五個月棲息於屍體之內{{r|Adrienne09}}。牠們顯示了屍體未有被火化，而且在較早期被[[麗蠅|麗蠅]]所棲息的期間已完結{{r|Sukontason01}}。 ''Hydrotaea'' larvae may feed on each other and on other flies, such as blow-flies.<ref name="Adrienne09"/>

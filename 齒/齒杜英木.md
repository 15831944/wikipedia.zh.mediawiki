'''齒杜英木'''（[[學名|學名]]：''{{lang|la|Elaeocarpus dentatus}}''）是一種[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]][[特有種|特有的]][[低地|低地]]植物。其[[毛利|毛利]]名稱有多個，包括 ''hīnau''、''hangehange''、''pōkākā''及 ''whīnau''。

齒杜英木屬於杜英科杜英屬，於紐西蘭[[北島|北島]]及[[南島|南島]]均有分佈，但並未出現於[[斯圖爾特島|斯圖爾特島]]上。<ref>{{cite web|title=Hīnau, Elaeocarpus - Faculty of Science - The University of Science|url=http://web.auckland.ac.nz/uoa/science/about/departments/sbs/newzealandplants/maoriuses/food/trees-and-shrubs/hinau-elaeocarpus.cfm|publisher=The University of Auckland|accessdate=2011-12-25|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130208184336/http://web.auckland.ac.nz/uoa/science/about/departments/sbs/newzealandplants/maoriuses/food/trees-and-shrubs/hinau-elaeocarpus.cfm|archivedate=2013-02-08}}</ref>葉片為深綠，葉緣齒狀。葉的底部有小坑。<ref>{{cite web|title=Elaeocarpaceae 2 (Wineberry family) - Faculty of Science, University of Auckland|url=http://web.auckland.ac.nz/uoa/science/about/departments/sbs/newzealandplants/seed-plants-flowering/find-by-north-&-south-islands/elaeocarpaceae-2-wineberry-family.cfm|accessdate=2011-12-25|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120716161849/http://web.auckland.ac.nz/uoa/science/about/departments/sbs/newzealandplants/seed-plants-flowering/find-by-north-&-south-islands/elaeocarpaceae-2-wineberry-family.cfm|archivedate=2012-07-16}}</ref>於春天長出成簇的小白花，並於夏未結出肉汁豐富的果子。<ref>{{cite web|title=Elaeocarpus dentatus Hinau|url=http://www.bushmansfriend.co.nz/elaeocarpus-dentatus-hinau-xidc17760.html|publisher=bushmansfriend.co.nz|accessdate=2011-12-25}}</ref>

首次的科學紀錄由博物學家[[约瑟夫·班克斯|约瑟夫·班克斯]]及[[丹尼尔·索兰德|丹尼尔·索兰德]]於1769年11月5日所紀錄。<ref>http://collections.tepapa.govt.nz/objectdetails.aspx?oid=328198</ref>

'''双子宫畸形'''（{{lang-en|Uterus didelphys, uterus didelphis}}），是一种罕见的[[妇科|妇科]]缺陷，指患者有两个完全分离的[[子宫|子宫]]，各自有独立的宫体、[[宫颈|宫颈]]，附有各自的[[输卵管|输卵管]]、[[卵巢|卵巢]]、[[圆韧带|圆韧带]]、[[阔韧带|阔韧带]]等，是双侧[[副中肾管|副中肾管]]未融合的结果<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Grimbizis |first1=G. F. |last2=Camus |first2=M |last3=Tarlatzis |first3=BC |last4=Bontis |first4=JN |last5=Devroey |first5=P |title=Clinical implications of uterine malformations and hysteroscopic treatment results |journal=Human Reproduction Update |volume=7 |issue=2 |pages=161–74 |year=2001 |pmid=11284660 |doi=10.1093/humupd/7.2.161}}</ref><ref name=heinonen>{{cite journal |last1=Heinonen |first1=P.K. |title=Uterus didelphys: a report of 26 cases |journal=European Journal of Obstetrics & Gynecology and Reproductive Biology |volume=17 |issue=5 |pages=345–50 |year=1984 |pmid=6479426 |doi=10.1016/0028-2243(84)90113-8}}</ref>。

临床表现为[[月经|月经]]持续时间长，有时有[[痛经|痛经]]。可有性生活不适。[[窥阴器|窥阴器]]检查可发现有两个[[阴道|阴道]]、两个宫颈，有时可触及两个子宫。[[超声波|超声波]]检查中可以看到横切宫颈有一椭圆形回声大于正常，内部有两条并列短光索，提示存在两个宫颈，宫体处有两个椭圆形宫体切面，回声相似、并列存在，呈现特异性的眼镜征。必要时用探针可探到两个宫腔<ref name=woelfer>{{cite journal |last1=Woelfer |first1=B |title=Reproductive outcomes in women with congenital uterine anomalies detected by three-dimensional ultrasound screening |journal=Obstetrics & Gynecology |volume=98 |pages=1099–103 |year=2001 |doi=10.1016/S0029-7844(01)01599-X |issue=6 |pmid=11755560 |last2=Salim |first2=R |last3=Banerjee |first3=S |last4=Elson |first4=J |last5=Regan |first5=L |last6=Jurkovic |first6=D}}</ref>。

双侧子宫均有受孕机会，但受孕常反复发生在同一侧子宫内，偶有双侧子宫异期受孕，两胎儿相差数天，发生率为百万分之一。因供血不足，孕早期易流产，孕可因体晚期易发生[[妊娠高血压综合征|妊娠高血压综合征]]<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Madureira |first1=A. J. |last2=Mariz |first2=C. M. |last3=Bernardes |first3=J. C. |last4=Ramos |first4=I. M. |title=Case 94: Uterus Didelphys with Obstructing Hemivaginal Septum and Ipsilateral Renal Agenesis |journal=Radiology |volume=239 |issue=2 |pages=602–6 |year=2006 |pmid=16641359 |doi=10.1148/radiol.2392031187}}</ref>。胎儿宫内发育迟缓、臀位。孕中期妊娠位改变发生[[子宫扭转|子宫扭转]]，而至[[胎盘|胎盘]]早剥，子宫破裂<ref>{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/health/6199363.stm |title= Triplets for woman with two wombs |accessdate=2007-06-27 |work=BBC News | date=December 21, 2006}}</ref>。

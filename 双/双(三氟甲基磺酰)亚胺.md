'''双(三氟甲基磺酰)亚胺'''是一种[[有机化合物|有机化合物]]，化学式为C<sub>2</sub>HF<sub>6</sub>NO<sub>4</sub>S<sub>2</sub>。由于分子中存在两个强烈吸电子的三氟甲基，双(三氟甲基磺酰)亚胺是一种[[超酸|超酸]]。<ref name=Zhao />它可用作一些有机反应的催化剂。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Atropisomerism of α,β-Unsaturated Amidines: Stereoselective Synthesis by Catalytic Cascade Reaction and Optical Resolution|journal=Chemistry – A European Journal|volume=15|issue=29|pages=7026–7030|doi=10.1002/chem.200901103|url=https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/chem.200901103|date=2009|issn=1521-3765|accessdate=2019-01-19|author=Naoya Shindoh, Yoshiji Takemoto, Kiyosei Takasu}}</ref>

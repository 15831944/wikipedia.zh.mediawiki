'''双(三氟甲基磺酰)氨基锂'''又称'''双(三氟甲烷磺酰)亚胺锂'''，是一种弱配位阴离子的锂盐，化学式为LiN(CF<sub>3</sub>SO<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。<ref name=cu>{{cite journal|title=Copper(II) bis((trifluoromethyl) sulfonyl) amide. A novel Lewis acid catalyst in Diels-Alder reactions of cyclopentadiene with methyl vinyl ketone|volume=36|issue=1|pages=81–84|date=1997-04-25|issn=0920-5861|doi=10.1016/S0920-5861(96)00199-X|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S092058619600199X|journal=Catalysis Today|accessdate=2019-01-19|author=Jin Nie, Hiroshi Kobayashi, Takaaki Sonoda}}</ref>它可用作复合聚合物电解质材料。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Composite polymer electrolytes based on poly(ethylene oxide), hyperbranched polymer, BaTiO3 and LiN(CF3SO2)2|volume=156|issue=3|pages=393–399|date=2003-01-01|issn=0167-2738|doi=10.1016/S0167-2738(02)00682-3|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0167273802006823|journal=Solid State Ionics|accessdate=2019-01-19|author=Takahito Itoh, Yosiaki Ichikawa, Takahiro Uno, Masataka Kubo, Osamu Yamamoto}}</ref>

==制备==
该化合物可由[[双(三氟甲基磺酰)亚胺|双(三氟甲基磺酰)亚胺]]和[[氢氧化锂|氢氧化锂]]或[[碳酸锂|碳酸锂]]在水溶液中反应得到，无水物通过110 °C真空干燥获得：<ref name=cu />
: LiOH + HNTf<sub>2</sub> → LiNTf<sub>2</sub> + H<sub>2</sub>O

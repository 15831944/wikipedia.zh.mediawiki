'''双环铂'''是一种[[化疗药物|化疗药物]]，用于治疗包括[[非小细胞肺癌|非小细胞肺癌]]和[[前列腺癌|前列腺癌]]在内的多种癌症。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=D.|first1=Zhao|last2=Y.|first2=Zhang|last3=C.|first3=Xu|last4=C.|first4=Dong|last5=H.|first5=Lin|last6=L.|first6=Zhang|last7=C.|first7=Li|last8=S.|first8=Ren|last9=X.|first9=Wang|last10=S.|first10=Yang|last11=D.|first11=Han|last12=X.|first12=Chen|title=Pharmacokinetics, Tissue Distribution, and Plasma Protein Binding Study of Platinum Originating from Dicycloplatin, a Novel Antitumor Supramolecule, in Rats and Dogs by ICP-MS|journal=Biological Trace Element Research|date=February 2012|volume=148|page=203–208|doi=10.1007/s12011-012-9364-2}}</ref>

双环铂治疗过程中观察到的副作用包括[[恶心|恶心]]、[[呕吐|呕吐]]、[[血小板减少|血小板减少]]、[[嗜中性白血球低下|嗜中性白血球低下]]、[[贫血|贫血]]、[[疲劳|疲劳]]、[[厌食|厌食]]、[[肝酶升高|肝酶升高]]和脱发等。这些药物是铂基抗肿瘤药物的一种，它的作用方式是使线粒体功能障碍，导致细胞死亡。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=G.Q.|first1=Li|last2=X.G.|first2=Chen|last3=X.P. |first3=Wu|last4=J.D.|first4=Xie|last5=Y.J.|first5=Liang|last6=X.Q.|first6=Zhao|last7=W.Q|first7=Chen|last8=L.W.|first8=Fu|title=Effect of Dicycloplatin, a Novel Platinum Chemotherapeutical Drug, on Inhibiting Cell Growth and Inducing Cell Apoptosis|journal=PLOS ONE|date=November 2012|volume=7|issue=11|doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0048994}}</ref>.

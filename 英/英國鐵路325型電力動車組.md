'''英鐵325型電力動車組'''（{{lang-en|British Rail Class 325}}）是一款在1995─1996年間由[[ABB|ABB]]在英國[[德比_(英格蘭)|德比]]製造的郵政列車，主要負責為[[皇家郵政|皇家郵政]]運送郵件。

325型列車的車身及動力設備皆與[[英鐵319型電力動車組|319型列車]]相同，但325型列車採用了Adtranz「{{link-en|聯網者列車|Networker (train)|聯網者}}」列車的頭幅<ref name="AllTime">{{cite web|url=http://www.railway-centre.com/uploads/7/2/2/3/7223531/all_time_emu_classes.pdf|title=The All Time Guide to EMU Classifications|date=2010-05-??|accessdate=2015-11-30|language=en|page=2}}</ref>。此外，325型亦裝配了兼容[[鐵路機車|機車]]的[[鐵路連接器|連接器]]，可直接由機車牽引行走沒有電氣化的鐵路而毋需加掛{{link-en|緩衝車|Barrier vehicle}}<ref name="DB">{{cite web|url=http://www.docbrown.info/docspics/ArchiveSteam/lococlass325.htm|title=Class 325 Electric Multiple Units ABB DERBY|first1=Phil|last1=Brown|work=Doc Brown's Travel Pictures|accessdate=2015-11-30|language=en}}</ref><ref name="SR">{{cite web|url=http://www.scot-rail.co.uk/page/class+325|title=Class 325|publisher=scot-rail.co.uk|accessdate=2015-11-30|language=en}}</ref>。

ABB最初建造了16列325型列車，但編號為325010的列車因損毀而於2012年被拆卸<ref name="RM">{{cite journal|url=http://www.ncl.ac.uk/newrail/assets/docs/RailwaymagazineJune2012001.pdf|title=Class 325 scrapping: Conversion costs too high|journal=The Railway Magazine|page=82|issn=1466-3562|accessdate=2015-11-30|language=en|date=2012年6月|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304125605/http://www.ncl.ac.uk/newrail/assets/docs/RailwaymagazineJune2012001.pdf|archivedate=2016-03-04}}</ref>。與[[皇家郵政|皇家郵政]]簽訂郵件運輸合約的[[德鐵信可鐵路|德鐵信可鐵路]]現時負責管理餘下的15列325型動車組，合約期至2018年止<ref name="DBS1">{{cite news|url=https://www.rail.dbschenker.co.uk/rail-uk-en/News_Media/news/9235040/retains_Royal_Mail_contract.html?start=10|title=DB Schenker Rail UK retains Royal Mail contract|publisher=德鐵信可鐵路|date=2015-04-17|accessdate=2015-11-30|language=en|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305032408/https://www.rail.dbschenker.co.uk/rail-uk-en/News_Media/news/9235040/retains_Royal_Mail_contract.html?start=10|archivedate=2016-03-05}}</ref>。

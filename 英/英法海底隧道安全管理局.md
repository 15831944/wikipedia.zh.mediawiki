'''英法海底隧道安全管理局（CTSA）'''是一個負責[[英法海底隧道|英法海底隧道]]安全的國際監管機構。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=The Channel Tunnel Safety Authority|last=Eisner|journal=Safety Science|issue=1|doi=10.1016/S0925-7535(99)00030-2|volume=36|pages=1–18}}</ref>

CTSA设立了[[坎特伯雷條約|坎特伯雷條約]]。它向政府委員會提供安全事宜的諮詢意見，並確保[[海峽隧道|海峽隧道]]的安全規則符合現行的安全法規。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rail-reg.gov.uk/server/show/nav.1233|title=Channel Tunnel Intergovernmental Commission (IGC) and Safety Authority (CTSA)|accessdate=14 August 2011|date=21 July 2011|publisher=[[Office_of_Rail_Regulation|Office of Rail Regulation]]}}</ref>

CTSA有五个来自[[法国|法国]]的成员及五个来自[[英国|英国]]的成员；领导每年会进行交替。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hse.gov.uk/press/2005/ctsa0501.htm|title=Channel Tunnel Safety Authority annual report 2004/2005|accessdate=14 August 2011|date=14 October 2005|publisher=[[Health_and_Safety_Executive|Health and Safety Executive]]|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120331012528/http://www.hse.gov.uk/press/2005/ctsa0501.htm|archivedate=31 March 2012}}</ref>

安全规则改变后将允许[[歐洲之星|欧洲之星]]使用德国制造的[[西门子Velaro|西门子Velaro]]列车、法國政府代表駁回了此建议，并以其他车辆替代。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.railjournal.com/newsflash/eurostar-train-procurement-row-heads-for-court.html|title=Eurostar train procurement row heads for court|accessdate=14 August 2011|date=22 October 2010|work=International Railway Journal|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101029014834/http://www.railjournal.com/newsflash/eurostar-train-procurement-row-heads-for-court.html|archivedate=29 October 2010}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/2010-10-21/france-s-top-channel-tunnel-safety-adviser-loses-job-ft-reports.html|title=French Tunnel Adviser Loses Job After Dispute Over German Trains, FT Says|accessdate=14 August 2011|date=21 October 2010|publisher=Bloomberg}}</ref>

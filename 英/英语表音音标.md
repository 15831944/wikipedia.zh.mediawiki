'''英语表音音标'''（{{lang-en|English Phonotypic Alphabet}}）是[[艾萨克·皮特曼|艾萨克·皮特曼爵士]]和[[亚历山大·约翰·埃利斯|亚历山大·约翰·埃利斯]]创造的一种[[英语|英语]][[音标|音标]]，被作为[[英语拼写改革|英语拼写改革]]的一部分。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com.ph/books?id=ospMAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA831&lpg=PA831&dq=henry+ellis+english+phonotypic+alphabet&source=bl&ots=9dGqroh_3w&sig=Q5yXMkjSEz7DzgScix1XsjdS1NQ&hl=en&sa=X&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=henry%20ellis%20english%20phonotypic%20alphabet&f=false|title=The World's Writing Systems|last=Daniels|first=Peter T.|date=1996|publisher=Oxford University Press|year=|isbn=9780195079937|location=|pages=831|language=en}}</ref> 它从来没有获得广泛接受，但也被纳入了现代国际注音字母系统中。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com.ph/books?id=y3KdxBqjg5cC&redir_esc=y|title=The Blackwell Encyclopedia of Writing Systems|last=Coulmas|first=Florian|date=1999-03-12|publisher=Wiley|year=|isbn=9780631214816|location=|pages=|language=en|chapter=English Phonotypic Alphabet}}</ref>

英语表音音标最初在1845年6月公布。<ref>''Completion of the Phonotypic Alphabet'', The Phonotypic Journal, 5 Nelson Place, Bath, Phonographic Institution, vol. 4, no 42, June 1845, p. 105-106</ref> 之后公布其扩展字母表，包括[[德语|德语]]、[[阿拉伯语|阿拉伯语]]、 [[西班牙语|西班牙语]]、[[法语|法语]]、[[威尔士语|威尔士语]]、[[意大利语|意大利语]]、[[葡萄牙语|葡萄牙语]]等语言。<ref>''Extension of the Phonotypic Alphabet'', The Phonotypic Journal, 5 Nelson Place, Bath, Phonographic Institution, vol. 4, no 43, June 1845, p. 121–123</ref>

'''英孚英语水平指数'''（或称：'''英语熟练度指标报告'''；{{lang-en|'''EF English Proficiency Index'''}}；英文缩写：'''EF EPI'''）是一个由[[英孚教育|英孚教育]]所发布的年度报告。<ref name="Edweek">[http://blogs.edweek.org/edweek/learning-the-language/2015/11/a_global_look_at_english-langu.html Study Offers Snapshot of Global English-Language-Learner Trends]. Education Week. Retrieved on 2017-01-17.</ref><ref name="EF SET">[https://www.efset.org/research/epi/ The EF SET powers the EF EPI]. EF SET. Retrieved on 2017-10-05.</ref>英孚英语水平指数是根据全球各地成人的平均[[英语|英语]]水平对全球每个参与国家的英语水平和掌握能力进行排名。<ref name="economist1">[https://www.economist.com/blogs/johnson/2011/04/english English: Who speaks English?]. The Economist. Retrieved on 2011-05-29.</ref>

第一版《英语熟练度指标报告》于2011年发布，<ref name="ef.com">[http://www.ef.com/about-us/highlights/2017/ef-epi/ The EF EPI]. ef.com. Retrieved on 2017-10-05.</ref>目前最新版本为第七版，于2017年11月发布。<ref>[https://www.cnbc.com/2016/11/14/singapores-english-skills-continue-to-improve-as-shanghai-beats-hong-kong.html Singapore's English skills continue to improve, as Shanghai beats Hong Kong]. CNBC. Retrieved on 2016-11-16</ref>

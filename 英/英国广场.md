'''英国广场'''（Britanski trg）是[[克罗地亚|克罗地亚]]首都[[萨格勒布|萨格勒布]]的一个城市广场<ref>{{cite web |author=Shaun Madison |url=http://www.mbendi.com/a_sndmsg/place_view.asp?pid=4253505 |title=Britanski trg, Croatia |publisher=Mbendi.com |date= |accessdate=2010-02-25 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。它是少数仍设有露天农贸市场的广场之一，出售直接来自农场的新鲜水果、蔬菜和其他食物<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vegguide.org/entry/7457 |title=Britanski Trg Market |publisher=VegGuide.Org |date= |accessdate=2010-02-25}}</ref>。英国广场位于城市主广场[[耶拉其恰廣場|耶拉其恰廣場]]以西几个街区。它也是一个交通枢纽，是多条公共汽车线路的终点站，而1路、6路和11路电车在其南侧停靠。<ref>{{cite web|title=Regular Tram Services|url=http://www.zet.hr/UserDocsImages/Prilozi/PDF/dnevna%20tram%20mre%C5%BEa.pdf|publisher=Zagrebački električni tramvaj|accessdate=2015-09-23|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150428234847/http://www.zet.hr/UserDocsImages/Prilozi/PDF/dnevna%20tram%20mre%C5%BEa.pdf|archivedate=2015年4月28日|df=}}</ref> 该广场本身便利进入萨格勒布的几个精英高档社区，如Pantovčak和Zelengaj。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.karte.hr/lokacije/britanski-trg |title=Britanski trg |publisher=karte.hr |date= |accessdate=2010-02-25}}</ref>露天市场每天从清晨到中午举行，每周日在广场举行古董展。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.absolute-croatia.com/shop/zagreb/britanski_trg |title=Britanski Trg (British Square) | Absolute World - Croatia |publisher=Absolute-croatia.com |date= |accessdate=2010-02-25 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091123030315/http://www.absolute-croatia.com/shop/zagreb/britanski_trg |archive-date=2009-11-23 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.inyourpocket.com/croatia/zagreb/shopping/markets/farmers-market-britanski-trg-3749v |title=Markets Farmer's Market - Britanski trg - Shopping in Zagreb - In Your Pocket city guide - essential travel guides to cities in Croatia |publisher=Inyourpocket.com |date= |accessdate=2010-02-25 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

'''英仙-飞马座纤维状结构'''是一个包含[[英仙-双鱼超星系团|英仙-双鱼超星系团]]，跨越约10亿[[光年|光年]]的[[大尺度纤维状结构|大尺度纤维状结构]]。当下，它被认为是宇宙中[[已知最大的结构|已知最大的结构]]之一。<ref>{{cite web|title=Superclusters - Large Scale Structures|url=http://science.jrank.org/pages/6614/Superclusters-Large-scale-structures.html|publisher=science.jrank.org|accessdate=2017-05-25}}</ref><ref name="note 1" group="note">The reference cited claims the Perseus-Pisces Filament as the largest known structure in the universe. However, various reports cite the [[Hercules-Corona_Borealis_Great_Wall|Hercules-Corona Borealis Great Wall]] as the largest at {{convert|10000000000|ly|pc}} across.</ref>这个纤维状结构毗邻[[双鱼-鲸鱼座超星系团复合体|双鱼-鲸鱼座超星系团复合体]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=Astronomy & Cosmology - Large Scale Structure of the Universe|url=http://www.whillyard.com/science-pages/filaments-walls.html|publisher=whillyard.com|accessdate=2017-05-25}}</ref><ref>'Astrophysical Journal', Part 1 ({{ISSN|0004-637X}}), vol. 299, Dec. 1, 1985, p. 5-14. "A possible 300 megaparsec filament of clusters of galaxies in Perseus-Pegasus" ''12/1985'' {{bibcode|1985ApJ...299....5B}}</ref>

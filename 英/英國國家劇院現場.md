'''英國國家劇院現場'''（{{lang-en|National Theatre Live}}，簡稱{{lang|en|NT Live}}）是一個由[[英國|英國]][[皇家國家劇院|皇家國家劇院]]主辦的項目。它通過[[衛星|衛星]]向英國及世界其他地區的電影院和藝術場館轉播[[舞台劇|舞台劇]]實況（[[實況轉播|實況轉播]]與重播兼有），劇目主要由該劇院出品，也包括少數其他劇院的作品。

== 概況 ==
{{quote|我是六十年代在[[曼徹斯特|曼徹斯特]]長大的。如果我當時就能在家附近的影院看到[[劳伦斯·奥利维尔|奧立維耶]]{{efn|指[[劳伦斯·奥利维尔|劳伦斯·奥利维尔]]（1907–1989），英國著名演員、導演，國家劇院的創辦者和首任藝術總監（1963–1973）。}}的國家劇院的話，那我一定會沒完沒了地去看的。|{{link-en|尼古拉斯·希特納|Nicholas Hytner}}，[[皇家國家劇院|皇家國家劇院]]藝術總監（2003–2015）<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.nationaltheatre.org.uk/45470/national-theatre-live/what-is-it-how-does-it-work.html | title = National Theatre Live: What is it? How does it work? | work = National Theatre Live | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20101229061938/https://www.nationaltheatre.org.uk/45470/national-theatre-live/what-is-it-how-does-it-work.html | archivedate =2010-12-29 | deadurl = yes | accessdate = 2018-02-24}}</ref>}}

2006年，[[美國|美國]][[大都會歌劇院|大都會歌劇院]]開始在影院對歌劇表演進行實況轉播；英國國家劇院受其啟發，在2009年推出了「英國國家劇院現場」，成為了全球首家提供實況轉播的舞台劇院<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/theatre-dance/features/no-mans-land-patrick-stewart-ian-mckellen-national-theatre-live-harold-pinter-a7469726.html | title = Live theatre on the big screen: The fear that streaming plays in cinemas would cannibalise theatre sales has largely been disproven | work = The Independent | accessdate = 2018-06-13 | date = 2016-12-12 | first = Matilda | last = Battersby | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180623064128/https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/theatre-dance/features/no-mans-land-patrick-stewart-ian-mckellen-national-theatre-live-harold-pinter-a7469726.html | archivedate = 2018-06-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | first1 = Hasan | last1 = Bakhshi | first2 = David | last2 = Throsby | year = 2014 | title = Digital complements or substitutes? A quasi-field experiment from the Royal National Theatre | url = https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007%2Fs10824-013-9201-2 | journal = Journal of Cultural Economics | volume = 38 | issue = 1 | pages = 1-8 | doi = 10.1007/s10824-013-9201-2 | accessdate = 2018-06-13}}</ref>。該項目於2009年6月開始了首季放映，轉播的首部作品是[[法國|法國]]舞台劇《{{link-en|費德爾 (話劇)|Phèdre|費德爾}}》；國家劇院出品的這一版本由[[海倫·米蘭|海倫·米蘭]]主演，全世界19個國家的280間影院（包括英國的73間）對其進行了實時轉播或錄影重播，共有約五萬名觀眾在影院觀看了這齣舞台劇<ref name=phedre>{{cite web | url = http://www.nationaltheatre.org.uk/52918/productions/previous-production-phdre.html | title = Previous production: Phèdre | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20101229203701/https://www.nationaltheatre.org.uk/52918/productions/previous-production-phdre.html | work = National Theatre Live | archivedate = 2010-12-29 | deadurl = yes | accessdate = 2018-02-24}}</ref><ref name=phedreGuardian>{{cite web | url = https://www.theguardian.com/stage/2009/jun/26/national-theatre-live-phedre | title = National Theatre Live: Phèdre | first = Michael | last = Billington | work = The Guardian | accessdate = 2018-06-13 | date = 2009-06-26 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180623061151/https://www.theguardian.com/stage/2009/jun/26/national-theatre-live-phedre | archivedate = 2018-06-23}}</ref><ref name=faq/>。截至2017年2月，轉播英國國家劇院現場劇目的場館超過2500間，覆蓋60個國家<ref name=faq/>。

英國國家劇院現場的絕大多數表演場次設於倫敦當地時間的星期四晚上（見下節列表）。通常情況下，英國和[[歐洲|歐洲]]其他地區（除[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]外）的放映場地對各場演出均進行實況轉播；[[美國|美國]]和[[加拿大|加拿大]]的多數場地也在表演當日進行放映，但會按照時差推後幾小時；而[[北美洲|北美洲]]、[[南美洲|南美洲]]、[[亞洲|亞洲]]、[[非洲|非洲]]、[[大洋洲|大洋洲]]的其他放映場地則在表演日幾天或幾週後才進行放映<ref name=faq>{{cite web | url = https://www.nationaltheatre.org.uk/about-the-national-theatre/press/nt-live-press/nt-live-faqs | title = NT Live Press FAQs | work = National Theatre | accessdate = 2018-06-13 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180623061151/https://www.nationaltheatre.org.uk/about-the-national-theatre/press/nt-live-press/nt-live-faqs | archivedate = 2018-06-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.occc.edu/news/2015/national-theatre-live.html | title = OCCC partners with National Theatre Live to bring world-class productions to OKC | work = Oklahoma City Community College | accessdate = 2018-06-13 | date = 2015-09-01 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180614044650/http://www.occc.edu/news/2015/national-theatre-live.html | archivedate = 2018-06-14}}</ref>。實況轉播與延時重播所放映的版本完全相同，國家劇院不在重播時對轉播錄影進行任何刪減<ref name=faq/>。此外，個別受歡迎的劇目會被安排在影院重映<ref name=faq/><ref name=telegraph>{{cite web | url = https://www.telegraph.co.uk/theatre/national-theatre-live/everything-you-need-to-know/ | title = National Theatre Live: everything you need to know | work = The Telegraph | accessdate = 2018-06-13 | date = 2016-08-31 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180623110650/https://www.telegraph.co.uk/theatre/national-theatre-live/everything-you-need-to-know/ | archivedate = 2018-06-23}}</ref>。

據國家劇院統計，截至2017年2月，觀看過英國國家劇院現場放映的觀眾接近650萬人<ref name=faq/>。由[[班奈狄克·康柏拜區|班奈狄克·康柏拜區]]主演的2015年版《[[哈姆雷特|哈姆雷特]]》是該項目中迄今為止觀看人數最多的劇目，觀看實況轉播（包括同日內的延時轉播）的觀眾人數超過22萬5千人<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.playbill.com/article/benedict-cumberbatchs-hamlet-screening-breaks-national-theatre-record-com-367691 | title = Benedict Cumberbatch's Hamlet Screening Breaks National Theatre Record | work = Playbill | accessdate = 2018-06-13 | date = 2015-10-16 | first1 = Robert | last1 = Viagas | first2 = Mark | last2 = Shenton | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20180614021414/http://www.playbill.com/article/benedict-cumberbatchs-hamlet-screening-breaks-national-theatre-record-com-367691 | archivedate = 2018-06-14}}</ref>，計入延時重播和影院重映後的總觀眾人數則達到80萬人<ref>{{cite web | url = http://ntlive.nationaltheatre.org.uk/productions/ntlout27-hamlet | title = Hamlet | work = National Theatre Live | accessdate = 2019-03-24}}</ref>。

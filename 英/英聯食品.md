'''英聯食品'''<ref name=官方>{{cite web|url=http://www.abf.cn/|title=上海英联食品饮料有限公司|accessdate=2014-05-15|publisher=英聯食品}}</ref>（或稱'''英國聯合食品'''，{{lang-en|'''Associated British Foods plc'''}}）是一家[[英國|英國]][[跨國公司|跨國]][[食品加工|食品加工]]和[[零售|零售]]公司，總部位於[[倫敦|倫敦]]。它是世界第二大的糖和[[麵包酵母|麵包酵母]]生產商<ref name=introduction>{{cite web|url=http://www.abf.co.uk/grpintroduction.aspx|title=Introduction|accessdate=26 September 2010|publisher=Associated British Foods plc|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100922082158/http://www.abf.co.uk/grpintroduction.aspx|archivedate=22 September 2010|deadurl=no}}</ref>，也是世界主要食品生產商之一，其下的品牌包括[[阿華田|阿華田]]、[[Ryvita|Ryvita]]、[[喬丹斯_(公司)|喬丹斯]]和[[唐寧茶|唐寧茶]]<ref name=introduction/>。其下的子公司[[普利馬克|普利馬克]]在西歐有200多家服裝店<ref name=prelims/><ref name=introduction/>。

英聯食品54.5%的股份由[[威丁頓投資|威丁頓投資]]持有<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.abf.co.uk/uploads/abf-annual-report-2011.pdf |title=Annual Report 2011 |author=Associated British Foods |accessdate=28 July 2012}}</ref>，而後者的大多數股份屬於[[加菲爾德·韋斯頓基金|加菲爾德·韋斯頓基金]]，該基金的持有人是英聯食品創始人的[[韋斯頓家族|韋斯頓家族]]。

== 歷史==
1935年英聯食品由加拿大人[[W·加菲爾德·韋斯頓|W·加菲爾德·韋斯頓]]成立，最初的名稱是食品投資有限公司（Food Investments Limited），一個月后改名為聯合麵包有限公司（Allied Bakeries Limited）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bakersfederation.org.uk/memberprofile.aspx?id=1 |title=Bakers Federation |publisher=Bakers Federation |date= |accessdate=15 May 2010 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100407033318/http://www.bakersfederation.org.uk/memberprofile.aspx?id=1 |archivedate=7 April 2010 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。1960年改名為英聯食品（Associated British Foods）。1963年收購英國英國連鎖超市[[Fine_Fare|Fine Fare]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/obituaries/sir-alistair-grant-728740.html |title=Obituary: Sir Alistair Grant |work=The Independent |location=UK |date= 24 January 2001|accessdate=15 May 2010  | first=Berry | last=Ritchie}}</ref>。1978年創始人去世，公司交由其子[[加里·韋斯頓|加里·韋斯頓]]，公司在北美的部分則由最年輕的兒子[[蓋倫·韋斯頓|蓋倫·韋斯頓]]管理。

1986年英聯食品將Fine Fare賣掉，1991年收購[[英國糖業|英國糖業]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/obituaries/garry-weston-729753.html |title=Obituary: Garry Weston |work=The Independent |location=UK |date= 16 February 2002|accessdate=15 May 2010 }}</ref>。1997年將北愛爾蘭和愛爾蘭的零售產業賣給了[[Tesco|Tesco]]<ref>{{cite news |title=ABF's £1.5bn cash mountain provides investors with food for thought |first=Derek |last=Pain |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/business/stock-market-week-abfs-pounds-15bn-cash-mountain-provides-investors-with-food-for-thought-1267175.html |work=The Independent | location=UK |date=14 April 1997 |accessdate=28 July 2012}}</ref>。

2000年將旗下的波頓餅乾（Burton's Biscuits）賣給了HM Capital Partners<ref>{{Cite news|last=Wootliff |first=Benjamin |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/money/main.jhtml?xml=/money/2000/10/31/cnabf31.xml |title=Wagon Wheels roll west as ABF sells Burton's |work=The Daily Telegraph |location=UK |date=31 October 2000 |accessdate=15 May 2010 }}</ref>。2002年從[[聯合利華|聯合利華]]處收購了數家食品和服裝品牌<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.achfood.com/overview.cfm |title=ACH Foods Company Overview |publisher=achfood.com |date= |accessdate=7 December 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131215000103/http://www.achfood.com/overview.cfm |archivedate=15 December 2013 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/ach-food-companies-inc-buys-unilevers-mazola-corn-oil-and-associated-brands-77078937.html |title=ACH Food Companies, Inc. Buys Unilever's Mazola Corn Oil and Associated Brands |publisher=prnewswire.com |date=23 April 2002 |accessdate=7 December 2013}}</ref>。2004年從[[伯恩斯菲利普|伯恩斯菲利普]]處收購香料和酵母生產產業<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.secinfo.com/dsvr4.18f8.htm |title=Burns Philp & Co Ltd |publisher=Secinfo.com |date= |accessdate=15 May 2010}}</ref>。2007年買下[[Patak's|Patak's]]<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/business/6700173.stm "AB Foods buys curry firm Patak's"] ''BBC News Online''  Tuesday, 29 May 2007</ref>。

2011年3月26的倫敦示威中成為抗議組織UK Uncut反[[避稅及逃稅|避稅及逃稅]]的目標<ref name="UK Uncut press release">[http://www.ukuncut.org.uk/blog/press-release-uk-uncut-occupy-tax-dodgers-fortnum-and-mason UK Uncut Occupy Tax Dodgers Fortnum and Mason] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110329141844/http://www.ukuncut.org.uk/blog/press-release-uk-uncut-occupy-tax-dodgers-fortnum-and-mason |date=2011-03-29 }}. 26 Mar 2011.</ref>。根據該抗議組織的調查，英聯食品和旗下公司通過無息貸款每年逃稅達970萬英鎊<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.taxresearch.org.uk/Documents/abfeh.pdf |title=ABF European Holdings & Co SNC accounts 2008 |format=PDF |date= |accessdate=2013-02-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.taxresearch.org.uk/Blog/2011/03/28/this-is-why-uk-uncut-picked-on-fortnums/ |title=Tax Research UK article |publisher=Taxresearch.org.uk |date=2011-03-28 |accessdate=2013-02-16}}</ref>。而2013年2月英聯食品對此的回應是聲稱並未有任何“非法和不道德的”逃稅行為<ref>{{Citation

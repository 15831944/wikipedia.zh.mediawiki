'''英國循道會'''（'''Methodist Church of Great Britain'''）是英國的[[循道宗|循道宗]]教會，也是全世界所有循道會的母教<ref>{{cite book|last=Jr|first=Charles Yrigoyen|title=T&T Clark Companion to Methodism|date=25 September 2014|publisher=A&C Black|language=en |isbn=9780567290779|page=73|quote=British Methodism therefore holds an inescapable chronological priority in the history of world Methodism and it has also often been accorded a courteous priority of esteem, being regard still as the 'mother church' by Methodists from many parts of the globe. The story of the origins and development of Methodism in what is now the United Kingdom and the Republic of Ireland, therefore, is the story, first, of an eighteenth-century movement which gave birth to the whole Methodist enterprise and then of a nineteenth-century church whose influence reached out across the world through the missionary endeavors of the various British Connexions within and beyond the British Empire.}}</ref>。1738年，它由[[英国人|英国人]][[约翰·卫斯理|约翰·卫斯理]]（1703年－1791年）和其弟[[查理·卫斯理|查理·卫斯理]]于[[伦敦|伦敦]]创立，現在信仰者超20万人<ref name="mission">{{cite web|title=Methodism in Numbers – Statistics at a Glance|url=http://www.methodist.org.uk/media/1771003/Methodism_in_Numbers_2015.pdf|website=methodist.org.uk|publisher=The Methodist Conference|accessdate=23 December 2015|date=July 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151223190349/http://www.methodist.org.uk/media/1771003/Methodism_in_Numbers_2015.pdf|archivedate=2015年12月23日}}</ref>。英國循道會的最高組織是每年由會長舉行的循道會議會（Methodist Conference）。

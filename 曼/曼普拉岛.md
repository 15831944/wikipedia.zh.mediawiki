'''曼普拉岛'''（{{lang-bn|মনপুরা দ্বীপ}}、{{lang-en|Manpura Island}}）是[[孟加拉灣|孟加拉灣]]北部的一座島嶼，位於[[梅克納河|梅克納河]]河口<ref name=solar>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/backpage/solar-hope-manpura-1383478|title=Solar hope for Manpura|date=2017-03-30|work=The Daily Star|access-date=2018-11-15|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170401022255/http://www.thedailystar.net/backpage/solar-hope-manpura-1383478#|archive-date=2017-04-01|dead-url=no}}</ref>，面積為373平方公里，屬於[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]][[巴里薩爾專區|巴里薩爾專區]][[波拉縣|波拉縣]][[曼布拉烏帕齊拉|曼布拉烏帕齊拉]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/news-detail-11878|title=1.4cr coastal people still out of shelter coverage|date=2007-11-16|work=The Daily Star|access-date=2017-04-18|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170418165156/http://www.thedailystar.net/news-detail-11878#|archive-date=2017-04-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>。此區域主要的島嶼除了本島外，尚有[[波拉島|波拉島]]與[[哈提亞島|哈提亞島]]，三座島皆為人口稠密的島嶼<ref>{{cite web|last=Amin|first=Murtaza|title=Hatia Islands (island cluster, Bangladesh)|url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/256873/Hatia-Islands|publisher=Britannica Online Encyclopedia|accessdate=5 December 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140311213955/http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/256873/Hatia-Islands#|archive-date=2014-03-11|dead-url=no}}</ref>。2017年曼普拉島的人口約1,25,000人，其中大多數以捕魚為業<ref name=solar/>。

截至2017年，曼普拉島未與孟加拉的國家電網連接，只能以有限的發電機組供電，一天僅供電6小時，且範圍相當有限，孟加拉政府計劃於本島架設[[太陽能板|太陽能板]]以解決供電問題<ref name=solar/>。

==文化==
2009年的孟加拉電影《{{link-en|曼普拉|Monpura}}》即於本島取景，其中男主角為島上的漁夫，女主角則是另一名漁夫之女<ref>{{cite web|publisher=''The Daily Star''|date=2009-3-27|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/magazine/2009/03/04/film.htm|title=Film Review a Heartwarming Film, written by Jackie Kabir|accessdate=2018-11-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180918083029/https://www.thedailystar.net/magazine/2009/03/04/film.htm#|archive-date=2018-09-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==災難==
曼普拉島地勢平坦，最高點海拔僅3公尺，且排水設施不良而會發生季節性的淹水<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://journal.library.du.ac.bd/index.php?journal=dujbs&page=article&op=view&path%5B%5D=542|title=
Loss and gain of land of Manpura island of Bhola district: An integrated approach using remote sensing and GIS|author=Md. Shahjahan Ali, Md. Fazlul Haque, Shah Md. Mizanur Rahman, Kazi Farhed Iqubal, Ms Nazma, Ashfaque Ahmed|volume=22|issue=1|year=2013|publisher=Dhaka University Journal of Biological Sciences}}</ref>。[[1970年波拉氣旋|1970年波拉氣旋]]中，曼普拉島因沿岸均無堤防，全島遭到淹沒，造成毀滅性的災情，當時島上約22000名居民中，有高達約16500人罹難<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/nov/18/bhola-cyclone-manpura-east-pakistan-bangladesh-1970|title=From the archive, 18 November 1970: Fight for life as aid reaches stricken isles|author=Howard Whitten|publisher=The Guardian|date=2014-11-18|access-date=2018-11-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160722055035/https://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/nov/18/bhola-cyclone-manpura-east-pakistan-bangladesh-1970#|archive-date=2016-07-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>（也有來源指為島上約30000名居民中，有約25000人罹難<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.londoni.co/index.php/25-history-of-bangladesh/1970-bhola-cyclone/313-bhola-cyclone-1970-east-pakistan-cyclone-history-of-bangladesh|title=Build up to the Great Bhola Cyclone|publisher=Londoni Worldwide Limited}}</ref>）。

2005年，曼普拉島曾受到[[海盜|海盜]]襲擊<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://archive.thedailystar.net/2005/01/08/d50108070968.htm|title=Pirates loot 5 trawlers on Meghna estuary|last=|first=|date=|website=archive.thedailystar.net|publisher=The Daily Star|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170418163651/http://archive.thedailystar.net/2005/01/08/d50108070968.htm#|archive-date=2017-04-18|dead-url=no|access-date=2017-04-18}}</ref>。

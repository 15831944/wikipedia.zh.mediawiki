'''曼迪·吉爾'''（{{lang-en|Mandip Kaur Gill}}，{{bd|1988年|1月5日}}）<ref>{{cite web|last1=McEwan|first1=Cameron|title=Doctor Who series 11: Everything you need to know about new companion Mandip Gill|url=http://www.digitalspy.com/tv/doctor-who/news/a841267/doctor-who-mandip-gill-new-companion-hollyoaks-age-casualty/|accessdate={{Start date|2018|6|25}}|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://search.findmypast.co.uk/results/world-records/england-and-wales-births-1837-2006?firstname=mandip&lastname=gill&eventyear=1988&eventyear_offset=0|title=findmypast.co.uk|website=search.findmypast.co.uk|accessdate={{Start date|2018|6|25}}|language=en}}</ref>是一位[[英格兰|英格兰]]女演员。她的第一個熒幕角色是在[[第四台_(英國)|英國第四台]]的[[肥皂劇|肥皂劇]][[聖橡鎮少年|聖橡鎮少年]]中飾演菲比·麥昆。2015年離開該劇后，先後出演電視劇{{le|布穀鳥 (電視劇)|Cuckoo (TV series)|布穀鳥}}、{{le|醫生們 (電視劇)|Doctors (BBC TV series)|醫生們}}、{{le|善緣醫院|Cuckoo (TV series)}}和[[急诊室_(电视剧)|急診室]]。2017年10月，[[英國廣播公司|英國廣播公司]]宣佈曼迪·吉爾將在2018年飾演[[異世奇人|-{zh-cn:神秘博士; zh-hk:異世奇人; zh-tw:超時空奇俠;}-]]中新加入的角色，[[博士_(異世奇人)|博士]]的同伴亞斯米妮·罕。

== 早期經歷 ==
曼迪·吉爾於2009年在[[中央兰开夏大学|中央兰开夏大学]]取得表演專業[[文學士|學士]]學位。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uclan.ac.uk/about_us/case_studies/acting_graduates.php|title=BA (Hons) Acting Graduates|publisher=[[中央兰开夏大学|中央兰开夏大学]]|accessdate={{Start date|2018|6|25}}|language=en}}</ref>

== 職業生涯 ==
Gill appeared in several theatre productions, before she secured her first television role as [[Phoebe_Jackson|Phoebe Jackson]] (later McQueen) in the [[Channel_4|Channel 4]] soap opera ''[[Hollyoaks|Hollyoaks]]''.<ref name="Amrit">{{cite web|url=http://punjab2000.com/exclusive-interview-with-hollyoaks-phoebe-mcqueen-mandip-gill/|title=Exclusive Interview with Hollyoak's Phoebe McQueen – Mandip Gill|author=Amrit|year=2014|publisher=Punjab2000.com|accessdate=6 August 2017}}</ref><ref name="Newcomer">{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.com/soaps/hollyoaks/news/a359566/hollyoaks-newcomer-teases-phoebe-role-squatting-plot/|title='Hollyoaks' newcomer teases Phoebe role, squatting plot|last=Kilkelly|first=Daniel|date=11 January 2012|work=[[Digital_Spy|Digital Spy]]|accessdate=6 August 2017}}</ref> Gill had attended a number of auditions for the soap in the past, and she was unsure if the character of Phoebe was right for her, but after a number of callbacks, Gill was given the role.<ref name="Amrit"/> Phoebe was introduced as a homeless teenager and a friend for established character [[George_Smith_(Hollyoaks)|George Smith]] (Steven Roberts).<ref name="Newcomer"/> Gill chose to leave the show in 2015 and Phoebe was killed off as part of the long-running "Gloved Hand Killer" storyline.<ref name="Kilkelly">{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.com/soaps/doctors/interviews/a793384/mandip-gill-reveals-all-the-gossip-on-her-doctors-role-and-why-she-quit-hollyoaks/|title=Mandip Gill reveals all the gossip on her Doctors role and why she quit Hollyoaks|last=Kilkelly|first=Daniel|date=9 May 2016|work=[[Digital_Spy|Digital Spy]]|accessdate=6 August 2017}}</ref> The following year, Gill had a guest role in the BBC sitcom series ''[[Cuckoo_(TV_series)|Cuckoo]]'',<ref name="Kilkelly"/> before appearing in ''[[Doctors_(2000_TV_series)|Doctors]]'' for a five episode arc. Gill played Shazia Amin, a pregnant homeless woman.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.com/soaps/doctors/news/a792447/hollyoaks-star-mandip-gill-lands-gritty-new-guest-role-in-doctors/|title=Hollyoaks star Mandip Gill lands gritty new guest role in Doctors|last=Kilkelly|first=Daniel|date=29 April 2016|work=[[Digital_Spy|Digital Spy]]|accessdate=6 August 2017}}</ref> In 2017, Gill appeared in ''[[The_Good_Karma_Hospital|The Good Karma Hospital]]'',<ref name="James"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.radiotimes.com/tv-programme/e/fjsyxf/the-good-karma-hospital--series-1-episode-2/|title=The Good Karma Hospital Series 1 – Episode 2|last=Seale|first=Jack|work=[[廣播時報|廣播時報]]|accessdate=6 August 2017}}</ref> and an episode of ''[[Casualty_(TV_series)|Casualty]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b08tg9nc|title=Swift Vengeance Waits|publisher=[[BBC|BBC]]|accessdate=6 August 2017}}</ref> She also stars in [[Kay_Mellor|Kay Mellor]]'s BBC drama ''[[Love,_Lies_and_Records|Love, Lies and Records]]'' as Talia, a junior registrar.<ref name="James">{{cite journal|last=James|first=Alison|date=12–25 August 2017|title=The Ex Factor|journal=Soaplife|issue=413|page=45}}</ref>

In October 2017, the BBC announced that Gill had been cast as a [[Companion_(Doctor_Who)|companion]] in the [[Doctor_Who_(series_11)|eleventh series]] of ''[[Doctor_Who|Doctor Who]]'', and will appear alongside [[Jodie_Whittaker|Jodie Whittaker]] as the [[Thirteenth_Doctor|Thirteenth Doctor]] in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.radiotimes.com/news/2017-10-22/doctor-who-bradley-walsh-tosin-cole-mandip-gill-and-sharon-d-clarke-join-jodie-whittaker-as-regular-cast-members/|title=Doctor Who: Bradley Walsh, Tosin Cole, Mandip Gill and Sharon D Clarke join Jodie Whittaker as regular cast members|work=[[廣播時報|廣播時報]]|date=22 October 2017|accessdate=22 October 2017}}</ref>

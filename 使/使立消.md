'''使立消'''（{{lang-en|Strepsils}}）是一系列用於緩解口腔和咽喉感染引起的不適的[[喉片|喉片]]。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Weckmann G, Hauptmann-Voß A, Baumeister SE, Klötzer C, Chenot JF |title=Efficacy of AMC/DCBA lozenges for sore throat: A systematic review and meta-analysis |journal=Int J Clin Pract |date=2017 |volume=71 |issue=10 |pages=1742-1241 |pmid=28869700 |doi=10.1111/ijcp.13002}}</ref><ref>NHS [http://www.nhs.uk/medicine-guides/pages/MedicineOverview.aspx?medicine=Strepsils Strepsils at NHS Choices] Page accessed June 23, 2015  {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140817212841/http://www.nhs.uk/medicine-guides/pages/MedicineOverview.aspx?medicine=Strepsils |date=August 17, 2014 }}</ref>此藥物個是溫和的[[殺菌劑|殺菌劑]]。適應症為消滅口腔細菌及[[咽喉炎|咽喉炎]]。該品牌最初於1950年以[[漱口水|漱口水]]推出。<ref>Reckitt Benckiser Group plc. Page accessed 28 October 2017 [https://www.rb.com/brands/strepsils/ Strepsils - Our Health Brands]</ref>

使立消是[[英國|英國]][[利潔時|利潔時]]（Reckitt Benckiser）生產的一系列[[喉片|喉糖]]，是[[利潔時|利潔時]]於2006年收購的[[Boots|Boots Healthcare International]]品牌之一。

使立消中兩種[[原料药|活性成份]]為{{Link-en|Amylmetacresol|Amylmetacresol|戊间甲酚}}（amylmetacresol）<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.medicines.org.uk/emc/medicine/23391|title=Strepsils - Summary of Product Characteristics (SPC) - (eMC)|last=|first=|date=|website=Datapharm UK|language=en|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-02-20}}</ref>和{{Link-en|2,4-dichlorobenzyl alcohol|2,4-Dichlorobenzyl_alcoholl|2,4-二氯苯乙醇}}（2,4-dichlorobenzyl alcohol），其他有些配方還含有[[抗壞血酸|抗壞血酸]]（維生素C）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.medicines.org.uk/emc/medicine/23331|title=Strepsils Orange with Vitamin C 100mg - Summary of Product Characteristics (SPC) - (eMC)|last=|first=|date=|website=Datapharm UK|language=en|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-02-20}}</ref>非活性成分包括[[薄荷醇|薄荷醇]]，[[酒石酸|酒石酸]]和[[丙二醇|丙二醇]]。<ref name=":0" /> 

建議劑量為成人每兩至三小時一顆 <ref>{{cite journal |author=Weckmann G, Hauptmann-Voß A, Baumeister SE, Klötzer C, Chenot JF |title=Efficacy of AMC/DCBA lozenges for sore throat: A systematic review and meta-analysis |journal=Int J Clin Pract |date=2017 |volume=71 |issue=10 |pages=1742-1241 |pmid=28869700 |doi=10.1111/ijcp.13002}}</ref>。

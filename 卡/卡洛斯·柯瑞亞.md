'''卡洛斯·哈維爾·柯瑞亞·歐本海默'''（{{lang-en|Carlos Javier Correa Oppenheimer}}, {{bd|1994年|9月22日|}}），為波多黎各職業棒球員。2012年成為選秀狀元，目前擔任休士頓太空人隊上的游擊手。

2017年，他替波多黎各打經典賽，最後波多黎各拿下第二名。太空人也在該年拿下世界大賽冠軍。

==棒球生涯==
===休士頓太空人===
====2015====
6月8日，柯瑞亞升上大聯盟，面對白襪強投[[克里斯·塞爾|克里斯·塞爾]]，他敲出了生涯首安。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/story/_/id/13033395/houston-astros-promote-top-prospect-carlos-correa|title=Carlos Correa, No. 1 pick in 2012 draft, to join Astros on Monday|work=espn.com|date=June 8, 2015|accessdate=June 8, 2015}}</ref>隔天，他面對Zach Duke擊出生涯首轟且完成生涯首盜。<ref>{{cite web|last1=McTaggart|first1=Brian|title=Correa hits first home run, steals a base.|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/129603030/carlos-correa-knocks-first-homer-has-first-sb|website=mlb.mlb.com|publisher=MLB.com|accessdate=June 8, 2015}}</ref>他9場出賽就盜壘3次，為史上第二快，僅次於[[瑞奇·韓德森|瑞奇·韓德森]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/131492118/astros-carlos-correa-sprints-to-record-books-with-3-steals|title=Correa sprints to record books with 3 steals|work=Major League Baseball|accessdate=July 6, 2015}}</ref>柯瑞亞在10場出賽就敲出14安，創下了太空人隊史紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/131353702/astros-ride-carlos-correa-power-surge-to-win-over-rockies|title=Correa ignites power surge as Astros top Rox|work=Major League Baseball|accessdate=July 6, 2015}}</ref>他在15場出賽就擊出20安，也是隊史紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxsports.com/west/story/luis-valbuena-carlos-correa-power-houston-astros-to-13-3-rout-of-los-angeles-angels-062315|title=Luis Valbuena, Carlos Correa power Houston Astros to 13-3 rout of Los Angeles Angels|author=Fox Sports|work=FOX Sports|accessdate=July 6, 2015}}</ref>他在6月獲選美聯單月最佳新秀。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxsports.com/mlb/story/red-hot-june-nets-astros-correa-rookie-of-the-month-honors-070315|title=Red-hot June nets Astros' Correa Rookie of the Month honors|work=FOX Sports|date=July 3, 2015|accessdate=July 6, 2015}}</ref>

7月5日，柯瑞亞成為1914年來首位在初登板開始25個打席就有5場猛打賞表現的球員。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mobile.twitter.com/brianmctaggart/status/617830579371905025|title=Twitter|work=twitter.com|accessdate=August 4, 2015}}</ref>

8月1日，柯瑞亞生涯首次單場雙響，他在46場比賽敲出12轟，為太空人隊史最快紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://astros.mlb.com/news/2015/08/02/140546868/astros-carlos-correa-posts-first-2-hr-game|title=Correa continues ascent with first 2-HR effort|author=Chandler Rome|publisher=[[MLB.com|MLB.com]]|date=August 2, 2015|accessdate=August 2, 2015}}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>

10月12日，柯瑞亞成為最年輕在季後賽開轟的游擊手。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mweb.cbssports.com/mlb/eye-on-baseball/25336899/carlos-correa-becomes-youngest-shortstop-to-ever-homer-in-playoffs|title=Carlos Correa becomes youngest shortstop to ever homer in playoffs|author=Matt Snyder|publisher=CBS Sports|date=October 12, 2015|accessdate=October 12, 2015}}</ref>

11月16日，柯瑞亞獲選為美聯年度最佳新秀。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.elnuevodia.com/deportes/beisbol/nota/carloscorreaansiosoantelaesperadelpremio-2127431/|title=Carlos Correa es el Novato del Año de la Liga Americana|work=El Nuevo Dia|language=es|accessdate=December 21, 2015}}</ref>成為太空人1991年的Jeff Bagwell後第一人。
====2016====
柯瑞亞成為太空人史上最年輕在開幕戰開轟的選手。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlb.com/astros/news/carlos-correa-does-it-all-in-astros-win/c-170646696|title=Young upstart: Correa revs Astros in lid-lifter|first=Brian|last=McTaggart|publisher=[[MLB.com|MLB.com]]|date=April 5, 2016|accessdate=April 8, 2018}}</ref>不過他因為肩膀受傷，因此缺席9月的賽季。<ref>https://www.si.com/mlb/2016/09/08/houston-astros-carlos-correa-shoulder-injury</ref>該季他打擊率2成74，20轟，96分打點。
====2017====
5月，柯瑞亞成為單月美聯最佳球員，他在5月25到29號連續5場擊出單場雙安以上，合計14安。

7月18日，他因為左手拇指撕裂傷而缺席6到8週。<ref>{{cite news |last1=McTaggart |first1=Brian |title=Carlos Correa out 6–8 weeks with torn thumb ligament |url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/242740676/astros-place-carlos-correa-on-disabled-list/ |work=MLB.com |date=July 18, 2017 |accessdate=July 18, 2017}}</ref>

10月6日，柯瑞亞在美聯分區賽第2戰面對紅襪擊出全壘打。2天後又擊出全壘打。10月14日，他擊出第三轟且打出再見二壘安助太空人打敗洋基。最後太空人進入世界大賽與道奇對決。<ref>{{cite web |last=Wells |first=Adam |url=http://bleacherreport.com/articles/2739991-jose-altuve-astros-advance-to-world-series-with-alcs-game-7-win-vs-yankees |title=Jose Altuve, Astros advance to World Series with ALCS Game 7 win vs. Yankees |work=Bleacher Report |date=October 21, 2017 |accessdate=October 26, 2017}}</ref>第2戰，柯瑞亞和隊友[[荷西·奧圖維|荷西·奧圖維]]及[[喬治·史普林格|喬治·史普林格]]皆在延長賽開轟，道奇隊的Charlie Culberson和Yasiel Puig也開轟，締造世界大賽延長賽最多全壘打紀錄。<ref>{{cite news |last=Justice |first=Richard |url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/259714382/astros-dodgers-author-a-classic-fall-classic/ |title=Astros, Dodgers author a classic Fall Classic: Houston evens World Series at 1−1 in Game 2 thriller filled with wild moments |work=MLB.com |date=October 26, 2017 |accessdate=October 28, 2017}}</ref>

第5戰，柯瑞亞5支3且擊出全壘打，太空人最後以13比12打敗道奇。最終太空人4勝3敗贏得世界大賽冠軍。<ref>{{cite news  |last=Doolittle |first=Bradford |url=http://www.espn.com/blog/sweetspot/post/_/id/84360/houston-strongest-after-more-than-a-half-century-astros-win-first-world-series-title |title=Houston Strongest! After more than a half century, Astros first World Series title |work=ESPN.com |date=November 2, 2017 |accessdate=November 2, 2017}}</ref>
==國際賽==
2017經典賽，柯瑞亞接受了波多黎各的徵召，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/puerto-ricos-world-baseball-classic-roster/c-215455022|title=Young stars join Beltran, Yadi for Puerto Rico|first=Chad|last=Thornburg|work=MLB.com|date=February 8, 2017|accessdate=March 14, 2017}}</ref>與Francisco Lindor和[[哈維爾·巴耶茲|哈維爾·巴耶茲]]成為隊友，他成為波多黎各的三壘手。<ref>https://www.mlb.com/news/carlos-correa-to-play-3b-for-team-puerto-rico/c-218380198</ref>在此系列賽，柯瑞亞打擊率3成33，3支全壘打，9分打點，<ref>https://www.mlb.com/news/2017-wbc-mvp-and-all-tournament-team-announced/c-220520420</ref>並在準決賽擊出全壘打。<ref>https://www.mlb.com/news/carlos-correa-homers-in-wbc-2017-semifinal/c-220186322</ref>

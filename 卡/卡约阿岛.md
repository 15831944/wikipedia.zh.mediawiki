'''卡约阿岛'''（Kayoa、Kaioa）是马鲁古群岛的一座火山岛，也叫'''Pulau Urimatiti'''，靠近[[哈马黑拉岛|哈马黑拉岛]]，位于[[马基安岛|马基安岛]]南部14.5公里，行政上由[[北马鲁古省|北马鲁古省]][[南哈马黑拉县|南哈马黑拉县]]管辖。主岛长16公里，岛上地形以山地为主<ref>{{cite book | url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=gXAPAQAAIAAJ&q=kayoa&dq=kayoa&hl=en&sa=X&ei=PYtNUbK3HoHL0QWTsIG4Ag&ved=0CF0Q6AEwCQ | title=Publications of the United States Hydrographic Office | publisher=Hydrographic Office | author=Anon | year=1916 | pages=91}}</ref>。[[蒂加拉鲁火山|蒂加拉鲁火山]]位于岛西部，卡约阿岛与周围岛屿不同，主要由[[沉积岩|沉积岩]]构成而不是[[火山岩|火山岩]]<ref name=Peregrine>{{cite encyclopedia | title=Uattamdi | encyclopedia=Encyclopedia of Prehistory | publisher=Springer |editor1-last=Peregrine|editor1-first=Peter Neal|editor2-last=Ember|editor2-first=Melvin | year=2001 | volume=3: East Asia and Oceania|isbn=0306462575}}</ref>。居民主使用[[印尼语|印尼语]]、{{le|西马基安语|West Makian language|西马基安语}}和{{le|塔巴语|Taba language|塔巴语}}<ref name=Watuseke>{{cite journal | jstor=30027583 . | title=LinguisticsWest Makian, a Language of the North-Halmahéra Group of the West-Irian Phylum | author=Watuseke, F.S. | journal=Anthropological Linguistics |date=September 1976 | volume=18 | issue=6 | pages=274–285}}</ref>（也叫东马基安语）<ref>{{cite journal | url=http://journals.cambridge.org/action/displayAbstract;jsessionid=70527C4EB32E1B488E160C53520439D0.journals?fromPage=online&aid=1799824 | title=Phonetic Representation Illustrations of the IPA: Taba |author1=Bowden, John |author2=Hajek, John | journal=Journal of the International Phonetic Association |date=June 1996 | volume=26 | issue=1 | pages=55–57 | doi=10.1017/S0025100300005326}}</ref>。

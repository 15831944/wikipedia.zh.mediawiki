'''卡拉·海登''' （{{lang-en|Dr. Carla Diane Hayden}}，{{bd|1952年|8月10日}}）<ref name=HistoryMakers-Hayden-2010>{{cite news|title=Carla Hayden|url=http://www.thehistorymakers.com/biography/carla-hayden-41|work=The HistoryMakers|date=16 July 2010}}</ref><ref name=HistoryMakers-EAD-FindingAid-2010>{{cite news|title=Finding Aid to The HistoryMakers® Video Oral History with Carla Hayden|url=http://thmdrupal.azureedge.net/sites/production/files/A2010_082_Hayden_Carla_EAD.pdf|work=The HistoryMakers|date=16 July 2010|format=Finding aid}}</ref> 是一位美国图书馆学家和第十四任[[美国国会图书馆|美国国会图书馆]]馆长。<ref name=AmericanLibraries-MeetCarlaHayden-2016>{{cite news|last1=Landgraf|first1=Greg|title=Meet Carla Hayden: America's Librarian|url=https://americanlibrariesmagazine.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/1116-AL-web.pdf|work=[[American_Libraries|American Libraries]]|date=November 2016}}</ref><ref name=NYTimes-QandA-2017>{{cite news|last1=Cox|first1=Ana Marie|title=Carla Hayden Thinks Libraries Are a Key to 卡拉·海登是第一位女性和非洲裔[[美国国会图书馆|美国国会图书馆]]馆长，也是该职位第一位被推选的有色人种候选人。<ref name=BaltimoreSun-LibrarianOfCongress-2016>{{cite news|last1=Brown|first1=Matthew Hay|title=Carla Hayden, longtime Enoch Pratt CEO, to be sworn in as 14th librarian of Congress on Sept. 14|url=http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/maryland/bs-md-carla-hayden-swearing-in-20160902-story.html|work=[[The_Baltimore_Sun|The Baltimore Sun]]|date=6 她也是过去六十年来第一位图书馆学专业出身而推选出来的会长。<ref name=ALA-Hayden-LibrarianOfCongress-2016>{{cite news|last1=Hines|first1=Shawnda|title=Dr. Carla Hayden to serve as the next Librarian of Congress|url=http://www.ala.org/news/press-releases/2016/07/dr-carla-hayden-serve-next-librarian-congress|work=[[American_Library_Association|American Library Association]]|date=16 July 2016|format=Press release}}</ref>

==生平==
1952年出生在美国[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]][[塔拉哈西|塔拉哈西]]，1973年获得[[罗斯福大学|罗斯福大学]]政治科学和非洲历史学士学位，1977年获得[[芝加哥大学|芝加哥大学]][[图书馆学|图书馆学]]硕士学位，继续攻读1987年获得该校博士学位。1993年到2016年担任[[巴尔的摩|巴尔的摩]]一家图书馆馆长。2003年到2004年还担任[[美国图书馆协会|美国图书馆协会]]会长。<ref name=ALA-PastPresidents-2011>{{cite news|title=ALA's Past Presidents|url=http://www.ala.org/ala/aboutala/governance/officers/past/|work=[[American_Library_Association|American Library President|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=LMMDAAAAMBAJ&q=hayden#v=onepage&q=carla%20hayden&f=false|work=[[Jet_(magazine)|Jet]]|publisher=Johnson Publishing Company|date=27 May 2002|language=en}}</ref>在她担任会长期间，是反对最新通过的《[[美国爱国者法案|美国爱国者法案]]》的[[美国图书馆协会|美国图书馆协会]]主要发言人。<ref name=ALA-PatriotAct-2006>{{cite news|last1=DiFulvio|first1=Frank|title=Public Statement by American Library

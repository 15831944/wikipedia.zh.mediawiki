'''卡伦·斯科特森'''（{{lang-en|Callum Scotson}}，{{bd|1996年|8月10日||}}）生于南澳大利亚州高勒，是一名澳大利亚男子自行车运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bmc-switzerland.com/us-en/experience/the-feedzone/get_to_know_callum_scotson/|title=Get to Know: Callum Scotson|publisher=BMC Switzerland|date=2017-04-10|accessdate=2019-03-16|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20190316062452/https://www.bmc-switzerland.com/us-en/experience/the-feedzone/get_to_know_callum_scotson/|archivedate=2019-03-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>。他的哥哥{{link-en|米尔斯·斯科特森|Miles Scotson}}同样也是一名自行车运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ridemedia.com.au/interviews/interview-talking-cycling-with-callum-scotson/|title=Interview: Talking cycling with Callum Scotson|publisher=Ride Media|date=2018-02-21|accessdate=2019-03-06}}</ref>，两人都曾就读于三一学院高勒分校<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.trinity.sa.edu.au/78-news-events/sports-news/509-scotson-world-champion |title=Scotson World Champion |publisher=Trinity College |accessdate=2016-04-16 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160428092321/http://www.trinity.sa.edu.au/78-news-events/sports-news/509-scotson-world-champion|archivedate=2016-04-28}}</ref>。两人在童年时期曾和同样是国家队成员的[[Jack_Bobridge|Jack Bobridge]]成为邻居<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.abc.net.au/news/2016-06-22/adelaides-scotson-brothers-chase-olympic-dream/7532890|title=Adelaide's Scotson brothers chase Rio Olympic cycling dream with former neighbour Jack Bobridge|publisher=ABC News|date=2016-06-22|accessdate=2019-03-16}}</ref>。

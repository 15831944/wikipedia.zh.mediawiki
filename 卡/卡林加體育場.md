'''卡林加體育場'''是一座位於[[印度|印度]][[奧里薩邦|奧里薩邦]][[布巴內什瓦爾|布巴內什瓦爾]]的綜合體育場。1987年由Biju Patnaik奠基石，它有田徑、足球、曲棍球、籃球、網球、桌球和游泳設施。<ref name=indoor>{{cite news|last=Mallick|first=Lelin Kumar|title=Stadium boost to indoor sports|url=http://www.hindu.com/2010/11/30/stories/2010113055280200.htm|accessdate=4 January 2013|newspaper=The Telegraph|date=19 January 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Pradhan|first=Ashoke|title=Permanent floodlights for Kalinga stadium on anvil|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2012-06-10/bhubaneswar/32155132_1_floodlights-stadium-halogen-bulbs|accessdate=4 January 2013|newspaper=Times of India|date=10 June 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Pradhan|first=Ashoke|title=Bhubaneswar needs to do more to imbibe sports culture|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2012-08-12/bhubaneswar/33167161_1_sports-talents-dilip-tirkey-sports-personality|accessdate=4 January 2013|newspaper=Times of India|date=12 August 2012}}</ref>
體育場的其他設施還包括8道的合成田徑跑道、一座體育旅館、一座體育館和印度首個新建的奧運標準粉色和藍色曲棍球場<ref>{{cite web|title=Sports Infrastructure in Odisha|url=http://www.orissa.gov.in/sports&youthservices/dsys/infrastructure.htm|publisher=Government of Odisha|accessdate=8 February 2013}}</ref>。2014年男子曲棍球冠軍盃、[[2017年亞洲田徑錦標賽|2017年亞洲田徑錦標賽]]和[[2018年世界盃男子曲棍球賽|2018年世界盃男子曲棍球賽]]都於此舉行。

'''卡波埃塔'''是[[南蘇丹|南蘇丹]]東南部的小鎮，由[[納莫魯揚州|納莫魯揚州]]負責管轄，位於首都[[朱巴|朱巴]]以東約275公里<ref>[http://distancecalculator.globefeed.com/Sudan_Distance_Result.asp?fromplace=Juba%20%28%29%20(Airport)%20&toplace=Kapoeta%20%28Sharq%20al%20Istiwa%27iyah%29&fromlat=4.872006&tolat=4.7716667&fromlng=31.601117&tolng=33.5902778 Map Showing Kapoeta And Juba With Distance Marker]</ref>，海拔高度677米<ref>[http://www.airportsearch.org/HSKP/Kapoeta-Airport.html  Elevation of 2,220 Feet Above Sea Level] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120317130006/http://www.airportsearch.org/HSKP/Kapoeta-Airport.html |date=2012-03-17 }}</ref>，鎮上有機場設施，2008年8月人口約7,000。

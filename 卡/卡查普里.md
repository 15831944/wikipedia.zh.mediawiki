'''卡查普里'''（{{lang-ka|ხაჭაპური}}）是[[格鲁吉亚飲食|格鲁吉亚飲食]]中的一種烤餅，亦是當地最普遍的主食。卡查普里的做法是在發酵過的的麵餅上加入[[乾酪|乳酪]]和[[雞蛋|雞蛋]]等食材<ref>{{cite book|last=Goldstein|first=Darra|title=The Georgian feast: the vibrant culture and savory food of the Republic of Georgia|publisher=University of California Press|location=1999|pages=136–139|isbn=0-520-21929-5|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=3PM_FnWgPBAC&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_v2_summary_r&cad=0#v=onepage&q&f=false}}</ref>。據2009年的一份調查，88%的格鲁吉亚人喜歡卡查普里超過披薩。卡查普里在男性和老人中人氣更高<ref>{{cite news|last1=Kakabadze|first1=David|title=Georgians Still Prefer Khachapuri To Pizza|url=http://www.rferl.org/content/Georgians_Still_Prefer_Khachapuri_To_Pizza__/1366699.html|agency=[[Radio_Free_Europe/Radio_Liberty|Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty]]|date=5 January 2009|quote=At the same time, 97 percent of khachapuri lovers are older than 40, while 28 percent of young people prefer pizza. Pizza appears to be more popular among women than among men.}}</ref>。卡查普里作為格鲁吉亚的主食，其價格被用來監控格鲁吉亚的通貨膨脹情況<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.investor.ge/issues/2010_2/02.htm|title=Tracking monetary policy one big mac - and one khachapuri - at a time|last=Svensson|first=Therese|date=April–May 2010|work=Investor.ge|publisher=American Chamber of Commerce|accessdate=29 April 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111007010149/http://www.investor.ge/issues/2010_2/02.htm|archivedate=7 十月 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.iset-pi.ge/index.php?article_id=715 |title=Khachapuri Index Project|publisher=International School of Economics at Tbilisi State University|accessdate=29 April 2010}}</ref>。

'''卡塔爾博物館管理局'''（{{lang-ar|هيئة متاحف قطر}}）是一個位於[[卡塔爾|卡塔爾]]首都[[多哈|多哈]]的[[博物館|博物館]]協會，由前卡塔爾國[[埃米爾|埃米爾]][[哈邁德·本·哈利法·阿勒薩尼|哈邁德·本·哈利法·阿勒薩尼]]創立於2005年。其目標是聯合卡塔爾所有博物館組建一個可以提升博物館收藏、保護等能力的有效系統。<ref>[http://www.gulf-times.com/site/topics/article.asp?cu_no=2&item_no=240140&version=1&template_id=36&parent_id=16 Archaeological discovery in western Qatar sheds new light on early man] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120106184441/http://www.gulf-times.com/site/topics/article.asp?cu_no=2&item_no=240140&version=1&template_id=36&parent_id=16 |date=2012-01-06 }} in the [[Gulf_Times|Gulf Times]], 7 September 2008</ref>

現任主席是[[谢赫·阿尔·玛雅沙·本·哈马德·本·哈利法·阿勒萨尼|谢赫·阿尔·玛雅沙·本·哈马德·本·哈利法·阿勒萨尼]]<ref>[http://www.artsqatar.com/index.php/news-happenings/news-events/item/170-invitation-to-enter-the-first-international-curate-award-competition Invitation to enter the first International Curate Award competition]</ref>，在她任內該管理局的活動範圍更廣，通過收購《[[你何時結婚？|你何時結婚？]]》等畫作使得多哈成為了世界現代藝術品的集中地之一。<ref>[http://www.theartnewspaper.com/articles/Qatar-revealed-as-the-world-s-biggest-contemporary-art-buyer/24185 Qatar revealed as the world’s biggest contemporary art buyer]</ref>

2011年，該管理局領導了卡塔爾爭取[[UNESCO|UNESCO]][[世界遺產委員會|世界遺產委員會]]候選人資格的行動，<ref>[http://www.gulf-times.com/site/topics/article.asp?cu_no=2&item_no=470557&version=1&template_id=36&parent_id=16 Qatar wins seat to join Unesco heritage panel] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111117203317/http://gulf-times.com/site/topics/article.asp?cu_no=2&item_no=470557&version=1&template_id=36&parent_id=16 |date=2011-11-17 }} in the [[Gulf_Times|Gulf Times]], 16 November 2011</ref> 在為[[祖巴拉|祖巴拉]]申遺之後，<ref>[http://www.unesco.org/new/en/media-services/single-view/news/qatar_and_fiji_get_their_first_world_heritage_sites_as_world_heritage_committee_makes_six_additions_to_unesco_list/ Qatar and Fiji get their first World Heritage sites as World Heritage Committee makes six additions to UNESCO List], 22 June 2013</ref> 該管理局也開始負責保護這個卡塔爾的第一個世界遺產。<ref>[http://dohanews.co/photos-qatars-history-on-show-as-al-zubarah-opened-to-public/ Qatar’s history on show as Al Zubarah opened to public]</ref> 

除去博物館相關的領域外，該管理局還組織過[[多哈翠貝卡電影節|多哈翠貝卡電影節]]等其他文化事業，<ref>[http://business.highbeam.com/1758/article-1G1-189647139/tribeca-film-festival-and-qatar-museums-authority-launch Tribeca Film Festival and Qatar Museums Authority To Launch 'Tribeca Film Festival Doha' in November 2009] {{Wayback|url=http://business.highbeam.com/1758/article-1G1-189647139/tribeca-film-festival-and-qatar-museums-authority-launch |date=20140517122027 }}, 24 November 2008</ref> 它也是[[世界電影基金會|世界電影基金會]]和[[村上隆|村上隆]]在[[凡爾賽宮|凡爾賽宮]]、<ref>[http://www.chateauversailles.fr/resources/pdf/en/presse/dp-murakami-en.pdf Takashi Murakami at the Château de Versailles]</ref><ref>[http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/gallery/2010/sep/10/takashi-murakami-palace-versailles Takashi Murakami at the Palace of Versailles]</ref> [[達米恩·赫斯特|達米恩·赫斯特]]在[[泰特現代藝術館|泰特現代藝術館]]展出的贊助者。<ref>[http://www.chinanewswire.com/pr/2007060621562255 Qatar Airways And Qatar Museum Authority Support World Cinema Foundation At Cannes International Film Festival] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120114133550/http://www.chinanewswire.com/pr/2007060621562255 |date=2012-01-14 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.qma.com.qa/en/news/news-archive/470-qma-sponsored-damien-hirst-show-at-tate-modern QM sponsored Damien Hirst show at Tate Modern] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131011030133/http://www.qma.com.qa/en/news/news-archive/470-qma-sponsored-damien-hirst-show-at-tate-modern |date=2013-10-11 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.collectortribune.com/2012/03/12/damien-hirst-at-tate-modern-sponsored-by-the-qatar-museums-authority/ Damien Hirst at Tate Modern sponsored by the Qatar Museums Authority]</ref>

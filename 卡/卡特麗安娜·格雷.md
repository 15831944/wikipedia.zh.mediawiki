'''卡特麗安娜·格雷'''（{{lang-en|Catriona Gray}}，{{bd|1994年|1月6日|catIdx=Gray, Catriona}}），[[菲律賓|菲律賓]]模特儿、歌手、演员兼選美冠軍。出生于[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]，格雷的父亲是苏格兰裔澳洲人，母亲則是来自菲律賓[[阿尔拜省|阿尔拜省]]。<ref>[https://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-6502841/amp/Australian-Filipino-singer-wins-2018-Miss-Universe-pageant.html “Just who is Catriona Gray? Meet the Australian-Filipino singer and model who has just won Miss Universe 2018”]. Daily Mail. 17-12-2018.</ref>她拥有澳大利亚和菲律賓雙重國籍。<ref>[http://www.missworldphilippines.com/portfolio/catriona-elisa-magnayon-gray/ “Catriona Elisa Magnayon Gray”]. Miss World Philippines.</ref>

2018年12月16日，她在[[泰国|泰国]][[曼谷|曼谷]]举办的第67届[[环球小姐|环球小姐]]总决赛中代表菲律賓摘得桂冠。<ref>[https://www.rt.com/news/446618-miss-universe-philippines-catriona-gray/amp/ “Philippines’ Catriona Gray is crowned Miss Universe 2018 (PHOTOS, VIDEOS)”] RT. 17-12-2018.</ref><ref>[https://www.dailystar.co.uk/news/world-news/748553/miss-universe-2018-catriona-gray-miss-philippines-thailand-bangkok-world-news/amp “Meet Miss Universe 2018 winner Catriona Gray”]. Daily Star. 17-12-2018.</ref>她也是第四位夺得环球小姐总冠军的菲律賓选手。


'''卡曼达蕾雅酒'''（{{lang-en|Commandaria}}，{{lang-el|Κουμανδαρία}})）是一种琥珀色甜酒，产于[[塞浦路斯|塞浦路斯]][[特罗多斯山|特罗多斯山]]（Troödos mountains）山麓的卡曼达蕾雅地区。卡曼达蕾雅酒是由晒干的Xynisteri和Mavro品种葡萄酿制的。虽然是[[加强葡萄酒|加强葡萄酒]]，但是在“加强”前初酿的酒液酒精浓度就已经高达15％左右。它是[[塞浦路斯|塞浦路斯]]历史悠久的葡萄酒风格之代表，其历史可以追溯到公元前800年，毫无疑问，它也是现今仍在生产的有名字的葡萄酒中，历史最悠久的，其名“卡曼达蕾雅”可以追溯到12世纪[[十字军东征|十字军东征]]时期。

== 历史 ==
卡曼达蕾雅酒有着悠久的历史，据说可追溯到古希腊时期，当时它是在节日中一种流行的饮料。[[塞浦路斯|塞浦路斯]]的干葡萄酒最早被人所知是在公元前800年，由希腊诗人[[赫西俄德|赫西俄德]]（Hesiod）在诗中称其为''Cypriot Manna''，意思是塞浦路斯的甘露。<ref>{{cite book | title = The Quality of Foods and Beverages: Chemistry and Technology | others = ed. George E. Inglett & George Charalambous | publisher = Academic Press Inc. | year = 1981 | isbn = 0-12-169101-2 | author = ed. by George Charalambous ...}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | first=Michele | last=Kambas | coauthors= | title=Cypriots thought to be first Mediterranean winemakers | date=2005-05-21 | publisher=Reuters | url=http://www.ekathimerini.com/4dcgi/news/content.asp?aid=56560 | work=Kathimerini | pages= | accessdate=2007-04-03 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071120005508/http://www.ekathimerini.com/4dcgi/news/content.asp?aid=56560 | archivedate=2007-11-20 }} </ref>

12世纪，[[十字军东征|十字军东征]]期间，卡曼达蕾雅酒出现在国王[[理查一世|理查一世]]与[[纳瓦拉的贝伦加丽亚|纳瓦拉的贝伦加丽亚]]在[[利马索尔|利马索尔]]的婚礼上。婚礼上，[[理查一世|理查一世]]当众称赞卡曼达蕾雅酒为“国王的葡萄酒，以及葡萄酒之王”（the wine of kings and the king of wines）<ref name='Reuters 1'>{{cite news | first=Sarah | last=Ktisti | coauthors= |authorlink= | title=Ancient Cypriot wine enters vintage major league | date=Aug 11, 2009 | publisher=[[Reuters|Reuters]] | url =http://www.reuters.com/article/lifestyleMolt/idUSTRE57A2PA20090811 | work = | pages = | accessdate = 2009-08-12 }}</ref>。12世纪末，[[理查一世|理查一世]]与将岛卖给了[[圣殿骑士团|圣殿骑士团]]，[[圣殿骑士团|圣殿骑士团]]又把它卖给了[[吕西尼昂的居伊|吕西尼昂的居伊]]，但在[[利马索尔|利马索尔]]附近的科洛西（Kolossi）保留了一块封建种植园，这块种植园被称作“La Grande Commanderie”。“Commanderie”的意思是军事总部，而Grande（大）则是为了区分岛上两个较小的指挥所，一个靠近[[帕福斯|帕福斯]]，另一个在[[凯里尼亚|凯里尼亚]]附近。这一区域在[[圣殿骑士团|圣殿骑士团]]（后来的[[医院骑士团|医院骑士团]]）控制下成为著名的卡曼达蕾地区（Commandaria）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.google.co.uk/books/pdf/The_Edinburgh_encyclopaedia__conducted_b.pdf?vid=0Gc3bIjIGir1lkJQ&id=7QmtyiHKwLYC&output=pdf&sig=65H5mJ5pkgtTqXBGO-CTVtZMfYk |title=The Edinburgh encyclopaedia (Volume VII, page 538) |last=Brewster |first=D. |year=1830 |format=PDF |publisher=William Blackwood }}</ref>。骑士们开始生产大量的葡萄酒，出口到欧洲的王室宫廷，并提供给前去圣地朝圣的朝圣者。这些葡萄酒也以当地的地名为名。因此，毫无疑问，卡曼达蕾雅酒也是现今仍在生产的有名字的的葡萄酒中，历史最悠久的<ref>{{cite news | first=David | last=Levin | coauthors= | title=The Wines of Aphrodite's Isle | publisher=Wine Communications Group | url =http://www.winebusiness.com/html/PrinterVersion.cfm?dataId=3582 | work =Wine Business Monthly | pages = | accessdate = 2007-04-02 |date=November 2000}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | first=Jill | last=Campbell Mackay | coauthors= | title=On the wine trail with Jill Campbell Mackay | date= March 5, 2006 | publisher= | url =http://www.cyprus-mail.com/living/wine-trail-jill-campbell-mackay-0 | work =[[Cyprus_Mail|Cyprus Mail]] | pages = | accessdate = 2007-04-02 }} {{Dead link|date=April 2012|bot=H3llBot}}</ref>。

虽然今天，卡曼达蕾雅酒仍以“卡曼达蕾雅”这个名字生产和销售，但过去岁月中它曾有过好几个类似名称和拼写方式。1863年，''Thomas George Shaw''在他的著作《葡萄酒 —— 葡萄藤和酒窖》（Wine, the vine, and the cellar）中将这种酒写称为“Commanderi”<ref>{{cite news | first=Thomas George | last=Shaw | coauthors= | title=Wine, the vine, and the cellar | year=1863 | publisher=Longman | url =http://www.google.co.uk/books?vid=OCLC08949299&id=soIBAAAAQAAJ&pg=RA6-PA314&lpg=RA6-PA314&dq=cyprus+wine&as_brr=1#PPP14,M1 | work = | pages =315 | accessdate = 2007-02-20 }}</ref>；在1833年Cyrus Redding在他的著作《当代葡萄酒的历史与特色》（A history and description of modern wines）中，称之为“Commandery”<ref>{{cite news | first=Cyrus | last=Redding | coauthors= | title=A history and description of modern wines | year=1833 | publisher=Whittaker Treacher & Arnot | url =http://www.google.co.uk/books?vid=OCLC06498122&id=S8QqcnF48cgC&pg=RA12-PA265&lpg=RA12-PA265&dq=cyprus+wine+Cyrus+Redding&as_brr=1#PRA12-PA263,M1 | work = | pages =263 | accessdate = 2007-02-20 }}</ref>；而在1879年，Samuel Baker称之为“Commanderia”。<ref name="gutenberg.org">{{cite book | author = Samuel W. Baker | title = Cyprus, as I Saw it in 1879 | page = 120 | year = 1879 | publisher = Project Gutenberg (Etext edition, 2003) | url = http://www.gutenberg.org/etext/3656}}</ref>

传说在13世纪法国的[[腓力二世_(法兰西)|菲利普·奥古斯（腓力二世）]]都举行了有史以来第一次品酒大赛。这一事件被称为“葡萄酒战役（{{lang-fr|La Bataille des Vins}}），收录在1224年Henry d'Andeli著名的法国诗集中<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fr.wikisource.org/wiki/La_Bataille_des_vins |title=La Bataille des Vins |accessdate=2007-04-03 |last=Henri |first=d'Andeli |coauthors=(verse 15) |year=1224 |language=fr }}</ref>。这场竞赛收集了全[[法国|法国]]乃至全[[欧洲|欧洲]]的葡萄酒，最终由来自[[塞浦路斯|塞浦路斯]]的一支葡萄酒夺魁，相信这支葡萄酒就是卡曼达蕾雅酒<ref>{{cite web | title = Wine, the Last 1,000 Years | work = [[h2g2|h2g2]] | publisher = BBC | date = May 21, 2002 | url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/dna/h2g2/A734915}}</ref>。1307年，在镇压了[[圣殿骑士团|圣殿骑士团]]后，卡曼达蕾地区也归于[[腓力四世_(法兰西)|（腓力四世）]]统治。

根据另一种传说，[[奥斯曼帝国|奥斯曼帝国]][[苏丹_(称谓)|苏丹]][[塞利姆二世|塞利姆二世]]曾经占领该岛，目的只是为了得到卡曼达蕾雅酒。用于酿造这种酒的葡萄也被引进到[[葡萄牙|葡萄牙]]，最终酿出了著名的[[波特酒|波特酒]]。<ref>{{cite news | first=Victorino | last=Matus | coauthors= | title=Selling Cyprus | date=2002-04-24 | publisher=News Corporation | url =http://www.weeklystandard.com/Content/Public/Articles/000/000/001/167sdyzx.asp | work =The Daily Standard | pages = | accessdate = 2007-08-10 }}</ref>

== 生产 ==
[[File:Mavro.jpg|thumb]]
[[Image:Xynisteri.jpg|thumb]]

卡曼达蕾雅酒完全是由两种不同类型的[[塞浦路斯|塞浦路斯]]本地葡萄酿造的——Xynisteri和Mavro<ref>{{cite news | first = Stephen | last = Meuse | title = Winemaking in transition | date = June 6, 2010 | url = http://www.boston.com/travel/getaways/europe/articles/2010/06/06/winemaking_in_transition/?page=2 | work = [[The_Boston_Globe|The Boston Globe]] | pages = 2 | accessdate = 2010-06-07}}</ref>。葡萄被留在藤上熟透，使其糖分达到所需要的水平（相当高的[[葡萄汁重量|葡萄汁重量]]（Must weight)：葡萄中糖分含量标准）然后采摘。最特别的是，Xynisteri葡萄在12°Bé（[[波美度|波美度]]Baumé Scale，测量葡萄汁浓度的单位）采摘，而Mavro葡萄在15-16°Bé采摘。接下来葡萄会放在太阳下曝晒，蒸发水分进一步增加其糖分含量。当[[葡萄汁重量|葡萄汁重量]]达到19-23°Bé时，通过挤压榨汁<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.loel.com.cy/wine/liqueur_wine/commandaria_alasia.php |title=Commandaria Production Details |accessdate=2007-01-27 |work=LOEL website |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070307071217/http://www.loel.com.cy/wine/liqueur_wine/commandaria_alasia.php |archivedate=2007-03-07 }} </ref>。发酵在一个水池中进行，当酒精浓度达到15%左右时发酵过程自然结束。卡曼达蕾地区的14个指定的村庄按上述方法酿造酒液。根据法规，卡曼达蕾雅酒必须在橡木桶中陈酿（aging）至少4年，陈酿过程可以在卡曼达蕾以外地区进行，但必须在塞浦路斯境内，以受到严格的控制和法律监管<ref name='Council Regulation (EC) No 1493/1999 Annex VI Part D (1)(b)'> {{cite web |url=http://ec.europa.eu/agriculture/markets/wine/studies/wine.pdf |title=Negotiation results on Wine (page 6 negotiation position No 29) |accessdate=2007-03-30 |format=PDF |publisher=European Commission |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070114222638/http://ec.europa.eu/agriculture/markets/wine/studies/wine.pdf |archivedate=2007-01-14 }}</ref>。

一旦发酵已完成，酒精浓度最低也在10％（实际上通常大大超出这个量），通过添加95%的葡萄酒精（葡萄汁制作的酒精）或大约70%的蒸馏酒，卡曼达蕾雅酒的酒精浓度还会大大增加。然而，“加强”后葡萄酒实际最终酒精含量不超过20％，而其潜在酒精含量总和（包括其糖分含量）必须至少是22.5％<ref>{{cite encyclopedia |editor=[[Jancis_Robinson|Jancis Robinson]] | encyclopedia=[[Oxford_Companion_to_Wine|Oxford Companion to Wine]] |edition= Third |title=Commandaria |year=2006 |publisher=Oxford University Press |location=Oxford |isbn=0-19-860990-6 |pages=190 }}</ref>。因此，卡曼达蕾雅酒虽然作为[[加强葡萄酒|加强葡萄酒]]，但其“加强”过程其实不是必需的。

这种生产方法的起源不得而知。公元前7世纪，[[赫西俄德|赫西俄德]]在他的诗歌《[[工作與時日|工作与时日]]》中写道：
{{cquote|Forget not next the ripen'd Grapes to lay, Ten Nights in Air, nor take them in by Day; Five more remember, e're the Wine is made, To let them ly, to mellow in the Shade; And in the sixth briskly yourself employ, To cask the Gift of Bacchus, Sire of Joy."<ref>{{cite web |url=http://animalrightshistory.org/animal-rights-timeline/animal-rights-h/hesiod/works-and-days-2.htm |title=Works and Days, Book 2 (verse 301-15) |accessdate=2007-06-20 |last=[[Hesiod|Hesiod]] |coauthors=Translated from the Greek by Mr. Cooke |date=MDCCXXVIII |publisher=N. Blandford |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110130214825/http://animalrightshistory.org/animal-rights-timeline/animal-rights-h/hesiod/works-and-days-2.htm |archivedate=2011-01-30 }} </ref>}}

1879年Samuel Baker在他的作品中描述道：
 {{cquote|...the commanderia grapes are collected and spread upon the flat mud-plastered roofs of the native houses, and are exposed for several days, until they show symptoms of shriveling in the skin, and the stalks have partially dried: they are then pressed……"}}

有趣的是，他声称，演变出这种生产方法更多是因为必须如此而不是因为人为选择：
{{cquote|"It has been imagined by some travellers that the grapes are purposely dried before pressing; on the other hand, I have been assured by the inhabitants that their only reason for heaping and exposing their crop upon the house-tops is the danger of leaving it to ripen in the vineyard. None of the plots are fenced, and before the grapes are sufficiently ripe for pressing they are stolen in large quantities, or destroyed by cattle, goats, mules, and every stray animal that is attracted to the fields…."<ref>{{cite book |author=Samuel White Baker, |title=Cyprus As I Saw It in 1879 |publisher= |location= |year= |isbn=1-84637-912-1 |oclc= |doi=}}</ref>}}

大型葡萄酒生产商（KEO，ETKO，LOEL和SODAP）和卡曼达蕾指定地区的小制造者都生产卡曼达蕾雅酒。

从Samuel Baker1879年的著作《我眼中的塞浦路斯》（Cyprus - How I saw it）的数据统计中可以发现，在19世纪后期，塞浦路斯每年卡曼达蕾雅酒产量的约30万okes（一种奥斯曼质量单位），相当于约385,000公升（这只是完税产品的生产量）。这其中有180,103 okes从[[利马索尔|利马索尔]]港出口，大多数去出口奥地利（155,000 okes值£2,075）<ref name="gutenberg.org"/>。

“塞浦路斯葡萄产品委员会”公布的官方数字表明，生产量有一种普遍增加的趋势。 大多数卡曼达蕾雅酒仍然主要用于出口<ref>{{cite news | first=Yiannis | last=Ioannou | coauthors= | title=Επιστροφή στην κουμανδαρία (Return to Commandaria) | date=2006-05-08 | publisher= | url=http://www.simerini.com/nqcontent.cfm?a_id=250845 | work=[[Simerini|Simerini]] | pages= | accessdate=2007-06-20 | language=el | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928133640/http://www.simerini.com/nqcontent.cfm?a_id=250845 | archivedate=2007-09-28 }}</ref>。

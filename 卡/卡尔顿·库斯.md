'''卡尔顿·库斯'''（{{lang-en|Arthur Carlton Cuse}}）<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0WYrAQAAMAAJ&q=%22Mr.+and+Mrs.+Arthur+Cuse;+a+boy,+Arthur+Carlton:+on+March+22.%22&dq=%22Mr.+and+Mrs.+Arthur+Cuse;+a+boy,+Arthur+Carlton:+on+March+22.%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=q5qSUdzQD8q1rgHCqYFo&ved=0CCUQ6AEwAA|title=Amistad: Magazine of American Society of Mexico|date=1958-01-01|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thecrimson.com/article/2006/6/5/carlton-cuse-a-carlton-cuse-81/|title=Carlton Cuse {{!}} News {{!}} The Harvard Crimson|website=www.thecrimson.com|language=en|access-date=2017-02-01}}</ref>（{{bd|1959年|3月22日|}}） 是一位美国电视编剧和制作人，最为知名的作品是美国电视连续剧''[[迷失|迷失]]''。

==生平==
1959年出生在[[墨西哥城|墨西哥城]]，2010年被''[[时代_(杂志)|时代 (杂志)]]''选为[[时代百大人物|时代百大人物]] 。<ref name = TIME>{{cite news |last =Poniewozik |first=James |title=Carlton Cuse and Damon Lindelof |work=The 2010 TIME 100—Artists |publisher=TIME |date=April 29, 2010 |url=http://content.time.com/time/specials/packages/article/0,28804,1984685_1984940_1985533,00.html |accessdate=March 28, 2014}}</ref> 卡尔顿·库斯被认为是[[跨媒体叙事|跨媒体叙事]]的领军人物，也因为他的合作成果被业内同行尊敬。<ref>http://variety.com/2015/tv/news/carlton-cuse-lost-the-strain-1201626731/</ref><ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/technology-11733566 Fox TV joins US networks to block Google TV ] By Maggie Shiels (2010).

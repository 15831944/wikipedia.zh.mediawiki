'''卡森莊園'''（Carson Mansion）是位於[[美國|美國]][[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[尤里卡_(加利福尼亞州)|尤里卡]]舊城的一處大型維多利亞風格建築<ref name = "CMIT">{{cite book|first=Benjamin |last= Sacks|title=Carson Mansion and Ingomar Theatre: Cultural adventures in California|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=3Ng7AQAAIAAJ|year=1979|publisher=Valley Publishers|isbn=978-0-913548-64-6}}</ref>{{rp|33}}。該建築被認為是美國規模最大的[[維多利亞式房屋|維多利亞式]]建築<ref name="LOC">{{cite web  | last = | title =Carson House, Eureka, Humboldt, CA  | work =Historic American Building Surveys, Engineering Records, Landscape Surveys Prints and Photographs Online Catalog   | publisher = Library of Congress  | date = 2013 | url =http://www.loc.gov/pictures/item/CA0174/  | accessdate = 21 September 2011 }}</ref>。自1950年開始，卡森莊園是一家私人俱樂部<ref name="LOC" /> ，房子和庭園並未對外開放。<ref name="NCJ2003">{{cite news   | last =Sanderson  | first =Helen  |author2=Keith Easthouse   | title =The Eureka icon that's off limits: Tourists feel misled by promotion of Carson mansion  | publisher = North Coast Journal  | date =August 7, 2003  | url =http://www.northcoastjournal.com/080703/news0807.html#anchor698077  | accessdate = October 24, 2013 }}</ref>。

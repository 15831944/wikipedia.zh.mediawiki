'''卡罗琳·朱莉娅·戴恩内奇'''（{{lang-en|'''Caroline Julia Dinenage'''}}；<ref>{{London Gazette |issue=59418 |page=8742 |date=13 May 2010}}</ref>{{bd|1971年|10月28日}}）是一位英国[[政治人物|政治人物]]，保守党党员，现任[[英国下议院|英国下议院]][[戈斯波特_(英国国会选区)|戈斯波特选区]]议员、{{le|英国卫生部|Department of Health and Social Care (United Kingdom)|卫生部}}卫生和社会关怀副大臣。

曾任{{le|工作和养老金部|Department for Work and Pensions}}家庭扶植住房和儿童保育政务次官，<ref>[https://www.gov.uk/government/people/caroline-dinenage UK Government Official website]</ref>[[英国教育部|教育部]]女性平权和青年政务次官兼司法部政务次官，<ref name="gov.uk">https://www.gov.uk/government/people/caroline-dinenage</ref>教育大臣[[妮基·摩根|妮基·摩根]]{{le|国会私人秘书|Parliamentary Private Secretary}}等职。

== 早年经历 ==
戴恩内奇生于1971年10月28日，<ref>{{cite news|url= http://news.bbc.co.uk/democracylive/hi/representatives/profiles/35430.stm|title=Caroline Dinenage MP|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=25 July 2010}}</ref>其父{{le|弗雷德·戴恩内奇|Fred Dinenage}}为电视节目主持人。她大部分时间都住在{{le|汉普郡南部|South Hampshire}}。<ref name="Caroline Dinenage">{{cite web|title=Caroline Dinenage profile|url=http://www.conservatives.com/People/Members_of_Parliament/Dinenage_Caroline.aspx|publisher=The Conservative Party|accessdate=11 May 2010}}</ref>

她中学就读于{{le|滑铁卢维尔|Waterlooville}}{{le|奥克兰天主教学校|Oaklands Catholic School}}，现任国际发展大臣[[佩妮·莫当特|佩妮·莫当特]]也曾读于该校。后入[[斯旺西大学|斯旺西大学]]学习政治学和英文。<ref>{{cite web|title=Caroline Dinenage, Conservative, Gosport|url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/politics/election_2010/gosport/candidates/7984329.Caroline_Dinenage__Conservative__Gosport|work=Daily Echo|accessdate=11 May 2010}}</ref>

戴恩内奇曾任戴恩内奇斯有限公司的董事兼公司秘书，该司为根据企业形象设计[[特许经营|特许经营]]合同供应和经销[[企业识别|企业形象]]产品的私人有限公司。<ref>[https://publications.parliament.uk/pa/cm/cmregmem/140224/dinenage_caroline.htm Register of Members' Financial Interests, as at 24 February 2014]</ref>2010年她宣布将自己持有的公司股份减少四分之一，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://caroline4gosport.co.uk/about-caroline/declaration-of-interests/ |title=Caroline Dinenage MP | About Caroline |publisher=Caroline4gosport.co.uk |date= |accessdate=9 May 2015}}</ref>但其所持有股权仍有15%，<ref>Companies House Records, Dinenages Ltd; Registered No: 03852824; Access date: 5 March 2014</ref>2015年她转让了剩余股权。<ref>[https://www.theyworkforyou.com/mp/24873/caroline_dinenage/gosport]</ref>

戴恩内奇的政治事业始于在[[温彻斯特|温彻斯特]]，她任该市议会议员五年，2003年辞职。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/archive/2003/04/08/Hampshire+Archive/5594660.City_council_nominations/ |title=Archive | Archive of stories uploaded to the Daily Echo website |publisher=Daily Echo |date=8 April 2003 |accessdate=9 May 2015}}</ref>她在[[2005年英国大选|2005年大选]]中成为[[南朴茨茅斯_(英国国会选区)|南朴茨茅斯选区]]保守党候选人，以33.9％的选票名列第二未能当选。<ref name="Portsmouth South">{{cite web |title=Portsmouth South |url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/constituency/1230/portsmouth-south |work=The Guardian |accessdate=11 May 2010}}</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
戴恩内奇前夫Carlos Garetta为[[英国皇家海军|英国皇家海军]]军官，育有两个孩子。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mirror.co.uk/news/uk-news/caroline-dinenage-mark-lancaster-cameron-2011834|title=Caroline Dinenage dumps husband to date Tory MP Mark Lancaster|author=Matthew Drake|work=[[Daily_Mirror|Daily Mirror]]|date=30 June 2013|accessdate=5 March 2014}}</ref>{{better source|date=September 2017}}2014年2月，她与保守党同事[[马克·兰开斯特|马克·兰开斯特]]结婚。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.portsmouth.co.uk/news/gosport-mp-caroline-dinenage-weds-fellow-politician-at-house-of-commons-chapel-1-5881270|title=Gosport MP Caroline Dinenage weds fellow politician at House of Commons chapel|publisher=Portsmouth News|date=17 February 2014|accessdate=5 March 2014}}</ref>她是[[朴茨茅斯足球俱乐部|朴茨茅斯足球俱乐部]]终身支持者。<ref name=BBC14156085>{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-14156085 |title=MP Caroline Dinenage on duck islands, dads and the Krays |first=Justin |last=Parkinson |publisher=BBC News |date=27 July 2011 |accessdate=27 July 2011}}</ref>

'''卡塔琳娜·特羅伊特勒'''(Katharina Treutler，[[埃尔福特|埃尔福特]])是一家德国[[钢琴家|钢琴家]]<ref>{{cite web|author1=Birgit Kummer|title=Erfurter Pianistin Katharina Treutler ist international gefragt|url=http://erfurt.thueringer-allgemeine.de/web/erfurt/startseite/detail/-/specific/Erfurter-Pianistin-Katharina-Treutler-ist-international-gefragt-1926520330|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=de|date=2014-01-11}}</ref>
== 生平 ==
卡塔琳娜·特羅伊特勒出生于埃尔福特市，在欧洲亚洲及美国多次举办过个人演奏会<ref>{{cite web|title=Katharina Treutler, piano|url=http://www.sandiegosymphony.org/about-the-sdso/artistic/guest-artists/member/katharina-treutler-piano/|website=San Diego Symphony|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=September 14|url=https://www.studioniculescu.com/programm/2014/september-14/|website=studioniculescu|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=de|date=2014-09-26|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170730110850/https://www.studioniculescu.com/programm/2014/september-14/|archivedate=2017-07-30}}</ref>。与之合作演出过的乐团有伦敦交响乐团，旧金山交响乐团<ref>{{cite web|last1=writer|first1=A. Los Angeles based|last2=Anime|first2=She Has Been a Long Time Fan of|title=Final Symphony arrives in US this summer!|url=http://nerdreactor.com/2016/05/09/final-symphony-arrives-in-us-this-summer/|website=Nerd Reactor|accessdate=2017-06-27|date=2016-05-09}}</ref>，皇家斯德哥尔摩爱乐乐团，东京爱乐乐团等<ref>{{cite web|title=Weltklassik am Klavier|url=http://www.weltklassik.de/index.php?page=kuenstler&id=5|website=www.weltklassik.de|accessdate=2017-06-27}}</ref>。

此外，在阿姆斯特丹音乐厅<ref>{{cite web|title=Final symphony|url=https://www.concertgebouw.nl/concerten/final-symphony/07-05-2016|website=Het Concertgebouw|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=nl}}</ref>，东京文化会馆<ref>{{cite web|title=Final Symphony to dazzle Tokyo|url=http://www.gameconcerts.com/en/neues/ansicht/article/final-symphony-auch-in-tokio/|website=Game Concerts|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=en}}</ref>，旧金山路易斯戴维斯交响乐音乐厅举办过演奏会<ref>{{cite web|title=PressReader.com - Connecting People Through News|url=https://www.pressreader.com/usa/san-francisco-chronicle/20160724/284541587455009|website=pressreader|accessdate=2017-06-27}}</ref>。

她曾就学于汉诺威<ref>{{cite web|title=Katharina Treutler|url=https://www.hellostage.com/Katharina-Treutler-2/home|website=HELLO STAGE|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=en}}</ref>，东京，巴黎，马德里，弗莱堡，师承格茨克<ref>{{cite web|title=Klavierkonzert mit Meisterpianistin Katharina Treutler|url=https://allevents.in/neustadt%20an%20der%20orla/klavierkonzert-mit-meisterpianistin-katharina-treutler/882709781841894|website=allevents.in|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=en}}</ref>、[[雅克·魯維耶|雅克·魯維耶]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Association Jeunes Talents|url=http://www.jeunes-talents.org/musiciens/468/Katharina%20-Treutler|website=www.jeunes-talents.org|accessdate=2017-06-27}}</ref>、{{tsl|en|Dmitri_Bashkirov|德米特里·巴什基羅夫|巴斯基洛夫}}<ref name="Biography {{!}} Katharina Treutler">{{cite web|title=Biography {{!}} Katharina Treutler|url=http://www.katharinatreutler.com/en/biohraphy/|website=www.katharinatreutler.com|accessdate=2017-06-27}}</ref>、埃里克.李.萨基等<ref>{{cite web|title=Katharina Treutler spielt in Altena|url=http://lennespiegel.de/katharina-treutler-spielt-in-altena/|website=lennespiegel.de|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=de}}</ref>。并多次获得国际钢琴大赛奖项。<ref>{{cite web|title=Pianistin Katharina Treutler spielt Fantastisches • Westfalen erleben|url=http://westfalium.de/2015/10/02/pianistin-katharina-treutler-spielt-fantastisches/|website=Westfalen erleben|accessdate=2017-06-27|date=2015-10-02}}</ref>

2016年起任教于莱比锡 菲利克斯 门德尔松 巴托尔迪 音乐与戏剧学院。<ref name="Biography {{!}} Katharina Treutler"/><ref>{{cite web|title=Personen & Kontakt {{!}} Hochschule für Musik und Theater »Felix Mendelssohn Bartholdy« Leipzig|url=http://www.hmt-leipzig.de/de/home/personen-und-kontakt?id=2608&|website=www.hmt-leipzig.de|accessdate=2017-06-27|language=de}}</ref>

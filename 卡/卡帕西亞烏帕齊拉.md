'''卡帕西亞'''（{{lang-bn|কাপাসিয়া}}，{{lang|en|Kapasia}}）是[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]]的一個[[烏帕齊拉|烏帕齊拉]]，位於[[達卡專區|達卡專區]]的[[加濟布爾縣|加濟布爾縣]]。<ref name=Banglapedia>{{cite book |last=Islam |first=Md Nazrul |year=2012 |chapter=Kapasia Upazila |chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=Kapasia_Upazila |editor1-last=Islam |editor1-first=Sirajul |editor1-link=Sirajul Islam |editor2-last=Jamal |editor2-first=Ahmed A. |title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |edition=Second |publisher={{tsl|en|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh||Asiatic Society of Bangladesh}}}}</ref>

==人口==
據{{tsl|en|1991 Bangladesh census|1991年孟加拉國人口普查}}，卡帕西亞烏帕齊拉共有人口303710人，其中男性佔比50.83%，女性49.17%；成年人口154204人。<ref name="census">{{cite web |  accessdate = November 10, 2006 |  url = http://www.bangladeshgov.org/mop/ndb/arpc91_v1/tables04.htm | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20050327072826/http://www.bangladeshgov.org/mop/ndb/arpc91_v1/tables04.htm | archivedate = 2005-03-27 | title = Population Census Wing, BBS.}}</ref>

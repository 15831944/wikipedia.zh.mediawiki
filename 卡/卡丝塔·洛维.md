'''卡丝塔·洛维'''（{{lang-en|Karsta Lowe}}，{{bd|1993年|2月2日||}}）生于加利福尼亚州圣地亚哥，是一名美国女子排球运动员，同时也是美国国家女子排球队队员。大学期间，她曾代表[[加州大学洛杉矶分校|加州大学洛杉矶分校]]参加排球比赛<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espn.com/espnw/news-commentary/article/11930309/ucla-karsta-lowe-blossoming-fiery-new-attitude|title=UCLA's Karsta Lowe Blossoming With Fiery New Attitude|publisher=[[ESPN|ESPN]]|date=2014-11-26|accessdate=2017-11-10}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Karsta Lowe Accepts AVCA First Team All-America Award|url = http://www.uclabruins.com/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=30500&ATCLID=209820820|website = uclabruins.com|access-date = 2016-02-19|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160301160532/http://www.uclabruins.com/ViewArticle.dbml?DB_OEM_ID=30500&ATCLID=209820820|archivedate = 2016-03-01}}</ref>。2015年，她首次代表国家队参加世界女排大奖赛，最终获得冠军，她也荣获此次比赛最有价值球员奖<ref>{{cite web|url=http://olympics.nbcsports.com/2015/07/27/usa-womens-volleyball-wins-grand-prix-olympics-brazil/|title=U.S. women dominate en route to FIVB World Grand Prix title|publisher=NBC Sports|date=2015-07-27|accessdate=2017-11-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fivb.com/en/about/news/usas-karsta-lowe-earns-world-grand-prix?id=55326|title=USA's Karsta Lowe earns World Grand Prix MVP in first FIVB Tournament|publisher=[[国际排球联合会|FIVB]]|date=2015-07-26|accessdate=2017-11-10}}</ref>。之后她入选2015年女子排球世界杯和2016年奥运女子排球美国队阵容。最终美国队在这两场比赛中均获得铜牌<ref>{{cite web|url=http://worldcup.2015.women.fivb.com/en/news/usa-continue-to-lead-womens-world-ranking?id=57062|title=USA continue to lead women's World Ranking after World Cup|publisher=FIVB|date=2015-09-10|accessdate=2017-11-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nbcolympics.com/news/usa-womens-volleyball-take-home-bronze|title=U.S. women's volleyball team takes home bronze|publisher=NBC Olympics|date=2016-08-21|accessdate=2017-11-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170507225605/http://www.nbcolympics.com/news/usa-womens-volleyball-take-home-bronze|archive-date=2017-05-07|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

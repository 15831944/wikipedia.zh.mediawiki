'''贝儿卡莉丝·马莲妮丝·艾尔曼萨尔'''（{{lang-en|Belcalis Marlenis Almánzar}}，{{bd|1992年|10月11日}}），艺名'''卡迪·B'''（{{lang|en|Cardi B}}），是一名出生于美国[[纽约市|纽约市]][[布朗克斯|布朗克斯]]的女性[[饒舌歌手|饒舌歌手]]。卡迪·B的知名度最初是透过社交网络上关于她曾做过[[脱衣舞娘|脱衣舞娘]]的传言打响<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/latin/8463929/-i-like-it-hitting-no-1-means-to-latin-music-billboard-chart-cardi-b-j-balvin-bad-bunny |title=The Times Have Changed: What 'I Like It' Hitting No. 1 Means to Latin Music |last=Cobo |first=Leila |date=July 3, 2018 |work=[[Billboard_(magazine)|Billboard]] |accessdate=July 6, 2018}}</ref>。她是[[Instagram|Instagram]]上的[[网络红人|网络红人]]。从2015年到2017年，她一直出演[[VH1|VH1]]的真人秀节目“Love&Hip-Hop: New York”。同期，她还推出过两张混音带<ref name="bb">{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/lifestyle/7768025/cardi-b-fashion-designer-rejection |title=Cardi B Opens Up About Fashion Designers Rejecting Her |work=Billboard |date=April 22, 2017 |accessdate=July 17, 2017}}</ref><ref name="fader1">{{cite web|author=Kameir, Rawiwa |url=http://www.thefader.com/2017/06/22/cardi-b-cover-story-interview |title=Cardi B Did It Her Way |publisher=The Fader |date=June 22, 2017 |accessdate=July 17, 2017}}</ref><ref name="complex1">{{cite web|author=Natalia Mantini |url=http://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2015/12/cardi-b-interview-pictures |title=Cardi B on 'Love & Hip Hop,' Haters and Hoeism |website=Complex.com |date= |accessdate=July 17, 2017}}</ref>。

自2017年与[[大西洋唱片|大西洋唱片]]签约以来，她的“Bodak Yellow”和“I Like It”两首单曲曾排上[[告示牌百强单曲榜|告示牌百强单曲榜]]首位。前一首令之成為自1998年的[[劳伦·希尔|劳伦·希尔]]以來，第二位憑藉單曲成為榜首的女性[[饒舌歌手|饒舌歌手]]，加上第二隻上頭位，更成為首位有多隻頭位上榜歌曲的女性[[饒舌歌手|饒舌歌手]]<ref name="I like it">{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8463605/cardi-b-i-like-it-hot-100-number-one-j-balvin-bad-bunny|title=Cardi B Becomes First Female Rapper With Two Billboard Hot 100 No. 1s, as 'I Like It', With Bad Bunny & J Balvin, Follows 'Bodak Yellow' to the Top|last=Trust|first=Gary|date=July 2, 2018|work=Billboard|accessdate=July 2, 2018}}</ref>。

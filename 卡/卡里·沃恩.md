'''卡里·沃恩'''（{{lang en|'''Cary Vaughan'''}}）是一位[[美國|美國]][[英語|英語]]講師和記者，並因[[黎巴嫩|黎巴嫩]]的[[特里·安德森|特里·安德森]]人質事件而聞名。

==生平==
卡里·沃恩是[[貝魯特美國大學|貝魯特美國大學]]的一位講師，以及[[美聯社|美聯社]]的一位兼職記者。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/boston/access/59293927.html?FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&type=current&date=Dec+04%2C+1991&author=Brian+McGrory%2C+Globe+Staff&pub=Boston+Globe+(pre-1997+Fulltext)&desc=Anderson's+fame+was+his+misfortune&pqatl=google |title=Anderson's fame was his misfortune |author=McGrory, Brian |date=4 December 1991 |work=Boston Globe |accessdate=3 June 2011}}</ref>沃恩和丈夫比爾·弗利在特里·安德森被綁架前早已經是朋友。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=CMwmAAAAIBAJ&sjid=EwIGAAAAIBAJ&pg=6542,5372101&dq=cary+vaughan+beirut&hl=en |title=Reach Out For Peace; It's What We All Must Do |author=Anderson, Bonnie M. |date=18 March 1988 |work=Miami Herald |accessdate=3 June 2011}}</ref> 在安德森遭真主黨武裝分子綁架後，沃恩和弗利便努力拯救他。對於他們的努力，[[保護記者委員會|保護記者委員會]]於是在1991年頒予他們[[国际新闻自由奖|国际新闻自由奖]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cpj.org/attacks96/frontmatter/ipfa.html |title=Journalists Receive 1996 Press Freedom Awards |author= |year=1996 |work= |publisher=Committee to Protect Journalists |accessdate=28 May 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=aMk_AAAAIBAJ&sjid=tFcMAAAAIBAJ&pg=6903,5972698&dq=cary+vaughan+beirut&hl=en |title=2 Journalists are honored for championing free press |agency=Associated Press |date=30 October 1991 |work=The Robesonian |accessdate=3 June 2011}}</ref>

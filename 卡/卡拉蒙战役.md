'''卡拉蒙战役'''（英文：'''Battle of Qalamoun'''）是[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]中的一次战役。该战役开始于2013年11月15日敘利亞政府军对位于[[卡拉蒙|卡拉蒙]]卡拉镇（Qara）的空袭行动。这场战役的目的是政府军试图切断反对派的[[大马士革|大马士革]]与[[黎巴嫩|黎巴嫩]]之间的[[补给线|补给线]]。<ref name="fierce">{{cite web|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/middleeast/2013/11/fierce-syria-clashes-spark-exodus-lebanon-2013111712321697322.html |title=Fierce Syria clashes spark exodus to Lebanon |publisher=Al Jazeera }}</ref>这一重要地区已经被反对派武装用作发动针对大马士革周边地区行动的重要后方基地。而政府军方面需要该地区附近的[[高速公路|高速公路]]连接大马士革和[[霍姆斯省|霍姆斯省]]中部地区，并且在该地区有很多武器仓库。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hurriyetdailynews.com/syria-air-force-bombards-town-near-lebanon-monitor.aspx?pageID=238&nID=58038&NewsCatID=352 |title=Syria air force bombards town near Lebanon: monitor |work=Hürriyet Daily News }}</ref>

==攻占卡拉镇==
在11月15日至17日间，在政府军发出警告表示将进攻该地区的反对派武装之后，1200－1700个家庭（约占卡拉镇90%）逃离该镇前往黎巴嫩小镇Arsal<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Lebanon-News/2013/Nov-16/238049-syrian-families-cross-into-lebanon-from-qalamoun.ashx#axzz2kuran5Dc |title=Thousands of Syrians from Qalamoun flee to Lebanon |work=The Daily Star|location=Lebanon }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Blast near Damascus kills 31 troops: monitor |url=http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Middle-East/2013/Nov-17/238118-31-troops-killed-in-blast-near-damascus-activists.ashx#axzz2k3r8IiK5|work=The Daily Star |date=17 November 2013 |accessdate= 17 November 2013}}</ref>。作为这场进攻的准备，数以千计的黎巴嫩真主党武装分子前往卡拉蒙地区边境对面的黎巴嫩一侧并驻扎下来。<ref name="launches"/>

11月15日，政府军发动了一场针对该地区的攻势，<ref>{{cite web|agency=Associated Press |url=http://www.mininggazette.com/page/content.detail/id/409877/Syrian-army-launches-offensive-near-Lebanon-border.html?isap=1&nav=5016 |title=Syrian army launches offensive near Lebanon border |publisher=Mininggazette.com |date=16 November 2013}}</ref>并且第二天多次空袭了反对派控制的该镇。<ref>[http://english.ahram.org.eg/NewsContent/2/8/86676/World/Region/Syria-air-force-bombards-town-near-Lebanon.aspx Syria air force bombards town near Lebanon]</ref>战斗也在奈卜克镇和Rima镇附近展开。<ref>{{cite web|author=Ryan Lucas, Associated Press |url=http://www.philly.com/philly/news/nation_world/20131117_Assad_gaining_ground_in_Syrian_civil_war.html |title=Assad gaining ground in Syrian civil war |work=Philadelphia Daily News }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|agency=Reuters |url=http://www.voanews.com/content/reu-syrian-army-fights-for-road-needed-to-remove-chemical-weapons/1791527.html |title=Syrian Army Fights for Road Needed to Remove Chemical Weapons |publisher=Voice of America }}</ref> <ref name="launches">{{cite web|url=http://www.thonline.com/news/national_world/article_831c0a3c-c749-5de1-bd53-138e8c590ece.html |title=Syrian army launches offensive near Lebanon border |publisher=Thonline.com }}</ref>该地区的反对派武装被动员起来抵抗政府军的进攻。<ref>{{cite news|title=Syrian army fights for road needed to remove chemical weapons |url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/11/16/us-syria-crisis-road-idUSBRE9AF06920131116?feedType=RSS&dlvrit=992637 |agency=Reuters |date=16 November 2013 |accessdate= 16 November 2013}}</ref>

11月17日，政府军向卡拉镇周边的山区推进，<ref>{{cite web|agency=Associated Press |url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/international/world/syrias-qalamoun-offensive-forces-thousands-to-flee-to-lebanon/article5361017.ece |title=Syria's Qalamoun offensive forces thousands to flee to Lebanon |work=The Hindu }}</ref>并试图进攻该镇，<ref name="fierce"/>使该镇遭遇了更多的空袭和炮击。<ref>{{cite web|author=Liz Sly |url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/world/syria-fighting-in-qalamoun-triggers-new-exodus-of-refugees-to-lebanon/2013/11/17/f08641cc-4fb4-11e3-9ee6-2580086d8254_story.html |title=Syria fighting in Qalamoun triggers new exodus of refugees to Lebanon |work=The Washington Post }}</ref><ref>[http://www.globalpost.com/dispatch/news/thomson-reuters/131117/syrian-army-besieges-town-strategic-qalamoun-mountains Syrian army besieges town in strategic Qalamoun mountains] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141029054917/http://www.globalpost.com/dispatch/news/thomson-reuters/131117/syrian-army-besieges-town-strategic-qalamoun-mountains |date=2014-10-29 }}</ref>直至当天下午，政府军仍未能攻入该镇。<ref>[http://byonlineweb.com/2013/11/17/syria-air-force-bombards-town-near-lebanon-monitor/ Syria air force bombards town near Lebanon: monitor] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141021123214/http://byonlineweb.com/2013/11/17/syria-air-force-bombards-town-near-lebanon-monitor/ |date=2014-10-21 }}</ref>

冲突使得大马士革和霍姆斯之间的高速公路中断通车。

11月18日，政府军继续攻势。根据亲政府的al-Watan日报报道，政府军攻占了卡拉镇的关键地区。<ref>{{cite news|title=Syrian troops recapture key positions in northern Damascus' town |url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/english/world/2013-11/18/c_132897876.htm |agency=Xinhua News Agency |date=18 November 2013 |accessdate= 18 November 2013}}</ref>叙利亚空军还多次空袭了卡拉蒙山区和耶卜鲁德山区。<ref>[http://documents.sy/news.php?id=9294&lang=en Damascus Countryside] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141025144037/http://documents.sy/news.php?id=9294&lang=en |date=2014-10-25 }}</ref>一名逃往黎巴嫩的当地居民描述了对卡拉镇的攻击“卡拉镇完了”。<ref>{{cite web|last=Yacoub |first=Khaled |url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/11/18/us-syria-crisis-idUSBRE9AH0NU20131118 |title=Syria rebel chieftain killed; Assad forces bomb besieged town |agency=Reuters }}</ref>

== 反对派进攻代尔阿提耶 ==
11月20日，在奈卜克和代尔阿提耶发生了多起针对政府军的自杀式袭击。在奈卜克郊外，一起汽车炸弹袭击了一处政府军检查站，还有一颗汽车炸弹在安全部队总部附近被引爆。在另一起袭击事件中，两名沙特人试图袭击代尔阿提耶的一座医院，但被政府军士兵阻止。<ref>[http://abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/syrian-troops-capture-key-town-lebanon-border-20934291 Suicide Bombs Hit Syria Troops North of Damascus]</ref>在针对检查站的袭击事件中，7名士兵丧生，5人受伤。而在针对医院的袭击中多名政府军士兵丧生。在沙特袭击者引爆了身上的炸弹后，5名反对派武装分子进入医院试图捣毁医疗设备并绑架一名受伤的政府军军官以及Ikhbariya al-Suriya电视台的工作人员。但是他们被政府军士兵击退，那名军官以及电视台工作人员安然无恙。在代尔阿提耶周边有8名反对派武装分子被击毙。<ref>[http://english.ahram.org.eg/NewsContent/2/8/87055/World/Region/Suicide-bombers-hit-Syria-troops-near-Lebanon-Moni.aspx Suicide bombers hit Syria troops near Lebanon: Monitor, SANA]</ref><ref>[http://syriahr.com/index.php?option=com_news&nid=12088&Itemid=2&task=displaynews#.Ut6tyxA1jcs Regular forces regain control of the monastery gift]</ref>

11月22日，在[[伊拉克和黎凡特伊斯兰国|伊拉克和黎凡特伊斯兰国]]和[[努斯拉阵线|努斯拉阵线]]的恐怖分子领导下，反对派武装夺取了代尔阿提耶的一个较大的基督教徒聚居城镇。只有Bassel医院和一座小山还在政府军的控制下。<ref name="keytown">[http://www.globalpost.com/dispatch/news/afp/131122/syria-rebels-take-key-town-army Syria rebels take key town from army] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150214003711/http://www.globalpost.com/dispatch/news/afp/131122/syria-rebels-take-key-town-army |date=2015-02-14 }}</ref>

11月25日，政府军开始在代尔阿提耶附近部署部队，<ref name=press>[http://www.yourmiddleeast.com/news/syria-rebels-press-advance-around-damascus-and-aleppo_19701 Syria rebels press advance around Damascus and Aleppo]</ref>准备发动反攻夺回该镇。<ref>[http://www.panarmenian.net/eng/news/173339/ Assad forces recapture Christian town]</ref>第二天，叙空军空袭了奈卜克，导致7人丧生，其中包括3名儿童。同时，叙利亚卫生部长Saad al-Nayef指责反对派在代尔阿提耶进行屠杀，杀害了5名医生、5名护士和2名救护车驾驶员。<ref>[http://www.google.com/hostednews/afp/article/ALeqM5jqEsqFe51OCJpUIlQP3POWfgRalw?docId=a9ca61b7-5c29-456c-bcf9-5b3ce8d31aff&hl=en Syria opposition, regime reject compromise ahead of talks]</ref>反对派活动人士承认在当地主要医院的冲突中有5名医生、4名护士丧生。<ref>[http://www.miamiherald.com/2013/11/28/3784783/syrian-troops-capture-western.html Syrian troops capture town near Lebanon border]</ref>

11月27日，在卡拉蒙地区，有4名真主党武装分子在战斗中被击毙，其中一人是黎巴嫩看守农业部长（同时也是真主党的高级官员）的侄子。<ref>[http://www.yalibnan.com/2013/11/28/nephew-of-a-top-hezbollah-official-killed-in-syria/ Nephew of a top Hezbollah official killed in Syria]</ref>在当天，在政府军控制的马卢拉爆发了战斗。

11月28日，政府军重新夺回代尔阿提耶，<ref name="western">[http://www.adn.com/2013/11/28/3201911/syrian-troops-capture-western.html Syrian troops capture western town] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131209162135/http://www.adn.com/2013/11/28/3201911/syrian-troops-capture-western.html |date=2013-12-09 }}</ref>而在周边郊区战斗仍在持续。<ref name="retake">[http://www.google.com/hostednews/afp/article/ALeqM5gHxSAHeg07q811lwZJSe76HfFljA?docId=808ac4a9-842a-4b72-82b7-6e6453c74851&hl=en=en Syrian forces 'retake Deir Attiyeh']</ref>

== 政府军在奈卜克的进展以及马卢拉地区的战斗 ==
在政府军夺回代尔阿提耶的同一天，政府军进入了[[奈卜克|奈卜克]]，<ref name="dailystar">[http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Middle-East/2013/Nov-28/239226-syria-army-retakes-deir-attiyeh-town-near-damascus-tv.ashx#axzz2lw9JjOka Syria army retakes Deir Attiyeh town near Damascus: TV]</ref>战斗在该镇周边地区打响。<ref>[http://triblive.com/usworld/world/5163955-74/syrian-fighting-government#axzz2lzvvyUjU Syrian troops capture key town]</ref>来自军方的消息说如果攻占该镇，政府军就只剩下[[耶卜鲁德|耶卜鲁德]]（Yaburd）和其他几个村落没有控制。如果这些地方也被政府军攻占，政府军将完全封锁住叙黎边境。<ref name="dailystar"/>

11月29日，政府军对那些组织他们推进的反对派据点进行炮击。<ref>[http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Middle-East/2013/Nov-29/239363-syria-army-presses-advance-near-damascus.ashx#axzz2lzqzan13 Syria army presses advance near Damascus]</ref>叙政府军还在他们攻占的奈卜克西部地区拘留了十余人。<ref>[http://supportkurds.org/news/friday-29-november-2013/ Friday 29 November 2013]</ref>

在29日夜间，反对派开始进攻马卢拉，<ref name="Christian">[http://www.sunherald.com/2013/11/30/5156326/shelling-of-market-in-northern.html Syrian rebels enter Christian village near capital] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203030349/http://www.sunherald.com/2013/11/30/5156326/shelling-of-market-in-northern.html |date=2013-12-03 }}</ref>试图夺取该地并切断政府军连接大马士革和奈卜克的补给线。<ref name="historic">[http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Middle-East/2013/Nov-30/239482-syrian-rebels-re-enter-historic-town-maaloula-activists.ashx#axzz2lzqzan13 Syrian rebels re-enter historic town of Maaloula: activists]</ref> <ref name="Christian"/>在9月份，反对派进攻过马卢拉，但被政府军击退。<ref name="Christian"/>在第二天最初的几个小时，反对派武装攻入该镇并夺取了马卢拉西部的Mar Takla修道院。同时，在奈卜克战斗仍在继续，该地遭到多次空袭。叙空军还空袭了耶卜鲁德。<ref name="historic"/>

12月1日，在马卢拉，战斗集中在该镇的舊城區。<ref>[http://www.utsandiego.com/news/2013/dec/01/activists-death-toll-up-to-26-in-syrian-strike/ Activists: New Syrian strike near Aleppo kills 11]</ref>同时，一名自杀式袭击者袭击了一处临近奈卜克的大马士革-霍姆斯高速公路上的检查站，5名政府军丧生。截至当时，根据叙利亚安全部队的消息，政府军已经夺取了奈卜克60%的地区。<ref name="al-bab">[http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Middle-East/2013/Dec-01/239514-new-strikes-kill-seven-in-syrias-aleppo-province-activists.ashx#axzz2lzqzan13 20 killed in second day of air raids on Syria's Al-Bab]</ref>

12月2日，反对派将填满炸药的轮胎从悬崖顶上抛进镇内驻扎的政府军，随后推进至了马卢拉镇中心。<ref>[http://www.naharnet.com/stories/en/108126-syria-rebels-advance-in-maalula-send-tire-bombs-burtling-down-on-army Syria Rebels Advance in Maalula, Send 'Tire Bombs' Burtling Down on Army]</ref> 此时，反对派已经攻占了马卢拉镇较老的部分。<ref>[http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Middle-East/2013/Dec-02/239655-syria-rebels-send-tyre-bombs-hurtling-down-on-army-source.ashx#axzz2lGvTgDRI Islamists take Syrian Christian town, monastery: State media]</ref><ref>[http://www.nytimes.com/reuters/2013/12/02/world/middleeast/02reuters-syria-crisis-maloula.html?partner=rss&emc=rss&_r=0 Islamists Take Syrian Christian Town, Monastery: State Media]</ref>政府军和亲政府民兵一整天都在作战以试图重新夺回该地区。圣战者抓了多名修道院内的修女，<ref>[http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/12/02/us-syria-crisis-maloula-idUSBRE9B10LD20131202 Islamists take Syrian Christian town, monastery: state media]</ref>根据梵蒂冈的发言人的说法，12名叙利亚和黎巴嫩的东正教修女被强行带往耶卜鲁德。在当天夜里，反对派武装完全控制了马卢拉，<ref name="rebelsretake">[http://www.dailystar.com.lb/News/Middle-East/2013/Dec-03/239723-rebels-retake-christian-town-of-maaloula.ashx#axzz2lzqzan13 Rebels retake Christian town of Maaloula]</ref>而镇外双方仍在交战。<ref>[http://supportkurds.org/news/monday-2-december-2013/ Monday 2 December 2013]</ref>在夺取该镇之后，三名基督徒因为拒绝皈依伊斯兰教被反对派处决。

12月3日，政府军向马卢拉方向增派援兵，试图和仍在该镇郊外的政府军恢复联系，并试图重新夺回该镇。<ref>[http://www.iol.co.za/news/world/deadly-suicide-bomb-hits-damascus-1.1616187#.Up3rtsRDufU Deadly suicide bomb hits Damascus]</ref>

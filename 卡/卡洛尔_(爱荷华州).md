'''卡洛尔'''（Carroll）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[卡洛尔县_(爱阿华州)|卡洛尔县]]的城市和县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=2011-06-07 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531210815/http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=2011-05-31 |df= }}</ref>[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]中的人口为10103人，比2000年人口普查中的10106人少了3人。<ref name=USCB>{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |title=Population & Housing Occupancy Status 2010 |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] American FactFinder |accessdate=2011-08-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721034521/http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |archivedate=2011-07-21 |df= }}</ref><ref name=IADC>{{cite web|url=http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |title=Data from the 2010 Census |publisher=State Data Center of Iowa |accessdate=2011-08-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718201045/http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |archivedate=2011-07-18 |df= }}</center></ref>

==历史==
卡洛尔在1867年被规划。<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bH8HtIwXzdcC&pg=PA503#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=History of Western Iowa, Its Settlement and Growth | publisher=Western Publishing Company | year=1882 | pages=503}}</ref>它的名字来自卡洛尔县，以纪念[[马里兰州|马里兰州]]的[[查尔斯·卡罗尔|查尔斯·卡罗尔]]（Charles Carroll）为名。<ref>{{cite book|author=Chicago and North Western Railway Company|title=A History of the Origin of the Place Names Connected with the Chicago & North Western and Chicago, St. Paul, Minneapolis & Omaha Railways|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OspBAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA52|year=1908|page=52}}</ref> 他是《[[独立宣言|独立宣言]]》签署人中唯一的[[天主教会|天主教]]徒。

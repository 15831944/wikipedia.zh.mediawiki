'''Pepe the Frog'''，音译'''佩佩蛙'''、青蛙佩佩（{{lang|en|Frog Pepe}}）等。它源自Matt Furie所绘制的一幅[[漫畫|漫畫]]，自2008年起於[[Myspace|Myspace]]、[[蓋亞在線|蓋亞在線]]和[[4chan|4chan]]上流行，2015年為[[Tumblr|Tumblr]]最受歡迎的[[網路爆紅|網路爆紅圖片]]之一。<ref name=ithome>{{cite web|author1=外事儿|title=表情包界最知名青蛙，年仅12岁死于“种族歧视”|url=http://www.ithome.com/html/it/308685.htm|website=IT之家|accessdate=2017-05-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170608152425/https://www.ithome.com/html/it/308685.htm|archivedate=2017-06-08}}</ref>

Pepe一般被刻畫为一隻有青蛙頭部但人形身體的生物<ref name="4chan's Pepe">{{Cite web|url = http://www.dailydot.com/lol/4chan-pepe-the-frog-renaissance/|title = 4chan's Pepe the Frog is bigger than ever—and his creator feels good, man|date = April 12, 2015|accessdate = January 4, 2016|website = The Daily Dot|last = Khan|first = Imad}}</ref>。該迷因的含义和用途亦隨時間改變，擁有許多變體，包括Feels Good Man（感觉不错蛙）<ref>{{cite web|title=青蛙是如何风靡互联网的？|url=https://www.jiemian.com/article/720062.html|website=界面新闻|accessdate=2017-05-13|date=2016-06-29|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170526153101/http://www.jiemian.com/article/720062.html|archivedate=2017年5月26日|df=}}</ref>、Sad frog（傷心青蛙、悲伤蛙）、Feels frog（感情青蛙）和"You will never..." frog（「你永遠不會⋯⋯」青蛙）等。<ref name="4chan's Frog">{{Cite web|url = http://motherboard.vice.com/read/4chans-frog-meme-went-mainstream-so-they-tried-to-kill-it|title = 4chan's Frog Meme Went Mainstream, So They Tried to Kill It|date = April 9, 2015|accessdate = January 4, 2016|website = Motherboard|last = Kiberd|first = Roisin}}</ref>

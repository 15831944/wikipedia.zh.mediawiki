'''佩頓·基柴爾'''（Maxie Patton Kizzire，{{bd|1986年|3月3日}}）是一位美國職業[[高爾夫球|高爾夫球]]手，現在參加[[PGA巡迴賽|PGA巡迴賽]]的賽事。基柴爾出生在亞拉巴馬州，在[[奧本大學|奧本大學]]就讀時就加入了學校的高爾夫隊<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.auburntigers.com/sports/m-golf/mtt/patton_kizzire_212596.html |title=Patton Kizzire Bio |publisher=Auburn Tigers |accessdate=August 3, 2015}}</ref>。2015年，他獲得Web.com巡迴賽的獎金王<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/golf/2015/08/02/patton-kizzire-wins-web-com-tours-utah-championship/31039937/ |title=Patton Kizzire wins Web.com Tour's Utah Championship |newspaper=USA Today |agency=Associated Press |date=August 2, 2015}}</ref>，並因此獲得2015-16賽季PGA巡迴賽的參賽權<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pgatour.com/players/player.32757.patton-kizzire.html/season |title=Patton Kizzire – Season |publisher=PGA Tour |accessdate=August 2, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pgatour.com/webcom/news/2015/11/18/patton-kizzire-named-2015-player-of-the-year.html |title=Kizzire named Web.com Tour Player of the Year |publisher=PGA Tour |date=November 18, 2015}}</ref>。

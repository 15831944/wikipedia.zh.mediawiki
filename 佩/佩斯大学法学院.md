'''佩斯大学法学院'''（Pace University School of Law）是一所自1976年起在[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]][[白原市|白原市]]创设的法学院，附属于[[佩斯大学|佩斯大学]]。
该学院在《[[美国新闻与世界报道|美国新闻与世界报道]]》的[[美国法学院排名|法学院排名]]里被评为第117名。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://grad-schools.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-graduate-schools/top-law-schools/pace-university-03111 |title=Pace University | Best Law School | US News |publisher=Grad-schools.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com |date=2011-08-01 |accessdate=2012-06-27}}</ref> 2012年3月，该刊物亦将该学院评为“全美十大毕业生负债最多的法学院”之一，该院每个学生平均负债13.9007万美元。<ref>http://www.usnews.com/education/best-graduate-schools/the-short-list-grad-school/articles/2012/03/22/10-law-schools-that-lead-to-the-most-debt</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Lytle |first=Ryan |url=http://www.usnews.com/education/best-graduate-schools/the-short-list-grad-school/articles/2012/03/22/10-law-schools-that-lead-to-the-most-debt |title=10 Law Schools That Lead to the Most Debt - US News and World Report |publisher=Usnews.com |date=2012-03-22 |accessdate=2012-06-27}}</ref>

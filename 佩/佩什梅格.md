'''佩什梅格'''（[[中部库尔德语|库尔德语]]：{{lang|ckb|{{big|پێشمەرگە}}}} ''{{lang|ku|Pêşmerge}}''，意为“面对死亡的人”），又译为“（库尔德）'''自由斗士'''”<ref>“自由斗士”是中文媒体的称呼，按照其字面意思，“佩什梅格”类似于中文语境中的“敢死队”，因此译为“库尔德敢死队”较为贴切。</ref>，是[[伊拉克库尔德斯坦|伊拉克库尔德斯坦]]自治区的[[武装力量|军队]]。<ref>来自[[库尔德语|库尔德语]] ''{{lang|ku|pêş}}'' ({{lang|ckb|{{big|پێش}}}}) “面对”和 ''{{lang|ku|merg}}'' {{lang|ckb|{{big|مەرگ}}}}“死亡”。</ref>佩什梅格的正式领导者是[[伊拉克库尔德斯坦总统|伊拉克库尔德斯坦总统]]。这支武装力量本身在很大程度上被划分为两部分，分别由[[库尔德斯坦民主党|库尔德斯坦民主党]]和[[库尔德斯坦爱国联盟|库尔德斯坦爱国联盟]]领导，虽然这两个政党都宣誓效忠[[库尔德地区政府|库尔德地区政府]]。目前，相关部门正在协调以期将整个部队集结于佩什梅格事务部的领导下。<ref name="ReferenceA">{{cite web |first=Mahmoud |last=Nawzad |title=Sources: Barzani Orders Peshmerga Forces Reformed, United |url=http://rudaw.net/english/kurdistan/250820142 |work=[[Rudaw_Media_Network|Rudaw Media Network]] |date=25 August 2014 |accessdate=13 February 2015}}</ref>佩什梅格部队的职责是保卫库尔德地区的国土、人民和机构。<ref>{{cite web |title=Summary of the most important tasks of the Ministry of Peshmerga |url=http://mope.krg.org/about-e.php?z=8&l=3 |work=Ministry of Peshmerga |date=12 November 2012 |accessdate=13 February 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150112042513/http://mope.krg.org/about-e.php?z=8&l=3 |archivedate=2015年1月12日 }}</ref>

因为[[伊拉克宪法|伊拉克宪法]]規定[[伊拉克陆军|伊拉克陆军]]不得进入[[伊拉克库尔德斯坦|伊拉克库尔德斯坦]]，<ref>{{cite web |title=Iraqi PM criticizes Kurdish region for barring army from Syrian border area |url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/english/world/2012-07/28/c_131745233.htm |work=[[Xinhua_News_Agency|Xinhua News Agency]] |date=28 July 2012 |accessdate=13 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Information about Kurdistan |url=http://heevie.org/aboutkurdistan |work=Kurdistan Development Organization |date=2014 |accessdate=13 February 2015}}</ref>所以佩什梅格以及其它的库尔德安全附属机构负责库尔德地区的安全事务。<ref>{{cite web |first=Jay |last=Newton-Small |title=Destination Kurdistan: Is This Autonomous Iraqi Region a Budding Tourist Hot Spot? |url=http://world.time.com/2012/12/31/destination-kurdistan-is-this-autonomous-iraqi-region-a-budding-tourist-hotspot/ |work=[[Time_(magazine)|Time]] |date=31 December 2012 |accessdate=13 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |first=Heath |last=Druzin |title=Rare terrorist attack in peaceful Kurdish region of Iraq kills 6 |url=http://www.stripes.com/news/middle-east/rare-terrorist-attack-in-peaceful-kurdish-region-of-iraq-kills-6-1.244095 |work=[[Stars_and_Stripes_(newspaper)|Stars and Stripes]] |date=29 September 2013 |accessdate=13 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |first=Jenna |last=Krajeski |title=The Iraq War Was a Good Idea, If You Ask the Kurds |url=http://www.theatlantic.com/international/archive/2013/03/the-iraq-war-was-a-good-idea-if-you-ask-the-kurds/274196/ |work=[[The_Atlantic|The Atlantic]] |date=20 March 2013 |accessdate=14 February 2015}}</ref>这些附属机构包括Asayish（官方情报机构）、''Parastin u Zanyarî''（协助情报机构）和Zeravani（宪兵队）。

在2003年[[伊拉克战争|伊拉克战争]]期间，据说佩什梅格在抓捕[[萨达姆·侯赛因|萨达姆·侯赛因]]的任务中发挥了关键作用。<ref>{{cite web |first=Manish |last=Rai |title=Kurdish Peshmerga Can Be A Game Changer In Iraq And Syria |url=http://www.khaama.com/kurdish-peshmerga-can-be-a-game-changer-in-iraq-and-syria-6802 |work=[[Khaama_Press|Khaama Press]] |date=6 October 2014 |accessdate=14 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Operation Red Dawn's eight-month hunt |url=http://www.smh.com.au/articles/2003/12/15/1071336860245.html |work=[[The_Sydney_Morning_Herald|The Sydney Morning Herald]] |date=15 December 2003 |accessdate=14 February 2015}}</ref>2004年，库尔德反恐怖主义力量抓获了[[基地组织|基地组织]]核心人物[[哈桑·古尔|哈桑·古尔]]，哈桑·古尔透露了[[本·拉登|本·拉登]]信使的身份，最终导致[[奥萨马·本·拉登之死|本·拉登的死亡]]。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://theweek.com/article/index/243389/how-the-cia-really-caught-bin-ladens-trail#axzz34IzUbxqb |last=Ambinder |first=Marc |title=How the CIA really caught Bin Laden's trail |newspaper=[[The_Week|The Week]] |date=29 April 2013 |accessdate=14 February 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |first=Arom |last=Roston |title=Cloak and Drone: The Strange Saga of an Al Qaeda Triple Agent |url=http://www.vocativ.com/usa/nat-sec/cloak-drone-strange-saga-al-qaeda-triple-agent/ |work=[[Vocativ|Vocativ]] |date=9 January 2014 |accessdate=14 February 2015}}</ref>

在2014年8月[[伊斯蘭國|伊斯蘭國]]对伊拉克库尔德斯坦发动大规模[[2014年8月伊拉克北部攻势|进攻]]后，佩什梅格和来自邻国的其他库尔德武装力量在伊拉克和叙利亚发动了针对ISIS的战争。

==历史==
[[File:Barzani1.jpg|110px]]
库尔德渴望独立的尚武传统已存在了几千年，早期的库尔德战士抗击各个波斯帝国、奥斯曼帝国和大英帝国。<ref name="main history source">{{cite web |first=Michael G. |last=Lortz |title=Willing to Face Death: A History of Kurdish Military Forces – the ''Peshmerga'' – from the Ottoman Empire to Present-Day Iraq |url=http://diginole.lib.fsu.edu/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?article=3203&context=etd |format=PDF |work=MA Thesis |publisher=[[Florida_State_University|Florida State University]] |date=28 October 2005 |accessdate=14 February 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150727174333/http://diginole.lib.fsu.edu/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?article=3203&context=etd |archivedate=2015年7月27日 }}</ref>而“佩什梅格”这个词是由已故的库尔德作家[[易卜拉欣·艾哈迈德|易卜拉欣·艾哈迈德]]于二十世纪中叶创造的。<ref>{{cite web |first=Allegra |last=Stratton |title=Hero of the people |url=http://www.newstatesman.com/node/153517 |work=[[New_Statesman|New Statesman]] |date=26 June 2006 |accessdate=14 February 2015}}</ref>历史上，“佩什梅格”只是作为游击队组织存在，但是在自行宣称的[[马哈巴德共和国|马哈巴德共和国]]（1946年–1947年）时期，“佩什梅格”成为了由[[穆斯塔法·巴尔扎尼|穆斯塔法·巴尔扎尼]]领导的国家军队。<ref name="first mahabad source">{{cite web |first=Mufid |last=Abdulla |title=Mahabad – the first independent Kurdish republic |url=http://kurdistantribune.com/2011/mahabad-first-independent-kurdish-republic/ |work=The Kurdistan Tribune |date=12 June 2011 |accessdate=14 February 2015}}</ref><ref name="ma barzani"/>在共和国垮台以及国家元首[[卡齐·穆罕默德|卡齐·穆罕默德]]被处决后，“佩什梅格”重新分裂成为若干游击队组织，在20世纪余下时期里继续对抗着[[伊朗|伊朗]]和[[伊拉克政府|伊拉克政府]]。<ref>{{cite book |first=Susan |last=Meiselas |title=Kurdistan: In the Shadow of History |edition=2nd |publisher=[[University_of_Chicago_Press|University of Chicago Press]] |date=2008 |isbn=978-0-226-51928-9}}</ref>

在伊拉克，大部分的“佩什梅格”士兵由[[库尔德民主党|库尔德民主党]]的[[穆斯塔法·巴尔扎尼|穆斯塔法·巴尔扎尼]]领导。<ref name="ma barzani">{{cite web |title=President |url=http://www.krgspain.org/government/profiles/english-president/ |work=[[Kurdistan_Regional_Government|Kurdistan Regional Government Representation in Spain]] |date=2015 |accessdate=14 February 2015}}</ref>1975年，“佩什梅格”在[[第二次伊库战争|第二次伊库战争]]中战败。同年，库尔德民主党的高层人物[[贾拉勒·塔拉巴尼|贾拉勒·塔拉巴尼]]排除阻力建立了[[库尔德斯坦爱国联盟|库尔德斯坦爱国联盟]]。这次事件引起了库尔德民主党的不满，两派的冲突也造成了“佩什梅格”和库尔德社会的分隔。

穆斯塔法·巴尔扎尼死后，他的儿子马苏德·巴尔扎尼继承了他的领导职位。<ref name="ma barzani" />随着库尔德民主党和库尔德斯坦爱国联盟的矛盾加剧，大部分的“佩什梅格”既要保卫各自政党控制的领地又要对抗伊拉克军队的入侵。在[[两伊战争|两伊战争]]时期，伊拉克库尔德斯坦陷入了两大库尔德政党之间的[[伊拉克库尔德斯坦内战|内战]]状态下，各自领导的“佩什梅格”部队被用来打击对方。当巴尔扎尼和塔拉巴尼签署了和平条约后，内战于1998年9月正式结束。<ref>{{cite web |first=Frman |last=Abdulrahman |title=never ending mystery: what really happened to kurdish civil war missing  |url=http://www.niqash.org/articles/?id=3000 |work=niqash |date=23 February 2012 |accessdate=22 February 2015}}</ref>在该协议中，双方同意分享财政收入和权力，拒绝将伊拉克北部交给[[土耳其库尔德工人党|土耳其库尔德工人党]]使用，也不允许伊拉克部队进入库尔德地区。双方共有约5000名库尔德人在內戰中被殺，而更多的人则是被驱逐到了对方的领地里。<ref>{{cite web |first=Charles |last=McDermid |title=New force emerges in Kirkuk  |url=http://www.atimes.com/atimes/Middle_East/LB20Ak02.html |work=[[Asia_Times_Online|Asia Times Online]] |date=20 February 2010 |accessdate=22 February 2015}}</ref>多年后，气氛仍高度紧张，但双方走向对方，在2003年，两党都参与了作为[[伊拉克战争|伊拉克战争]]一部分的[[推翻萨达姆政权|推翻萨达姆政权]]的战斗中。他们保持着和平的状态，形成了现在的伊拉克库尔德斯坦。不像其他的民兵部队，佩什梅格从来没有受到伊拉克法律的禁止。<ref name=WhoPeshmerga>[http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-28738975 Profile: Who are the Peshmerga?] BBC News. Retrieved 19 December 2014.</ref>

2015年，佩什梅格士兵第一次接受了由外国教练——[[坚决行动联合特遣队|坚决行动联合特遣队]]传授的城市作战和军事情报的训练。<ref>{{cite news|title=EXCLUSIVE: Coalition helps Peshmerga muscle up on urban warfare|url=http://rudaw.net/english/kurdistan/160420155|accessdate=17 April 2015|work=Rudaw|date=16 April 2015}}</ref>

==构成==
[[File:Pershmega_near_Syria_-_June_23,_2014.jpg|thumbnail]]
佩什梅格武装力量的确切规模未知，估计的人数从80000人到250000人不等。<ref>{{cite web |first=Peter |last=Beaumont |title=How effective is Isis compared with the Iraqi army and Kurdish peshmerga? |url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/jun/12/how-battle-ready-isis-iraqi-army-peshmerga |work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] |date=12 June 2014 |accessdate=22 February 2015}}</ref>这些部队被分成36个[[旅|旅]]，分别被库尔德民主党、库尔德斯坦爱国联盟和佩什梅格事务部控制，且几乎没有相互协调。<ref>{{cite web |first=Fazel |last=Hawramy |title=THE DESASTROUS COMMAND-NON-COMMAND-STRUCTURE |url=http://www.mesop.de/mesop-the-desastrous-command-non-command-structure/ |work=MESOP |date=14 January 2015 |accessdate=22 February 2015}}</ref>

Security Forces of Kurdistan Regional Government, Us Army War College. 2009, page. 3.</ref>2014年6月[[ISIS|ISIS]]入侵伊拉克，伊拉克军队撤退后，库尔德地区政府（KRG）填补了空白并控制了几乎所有有争议的地区。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://reliefweb.int/report/iraq/jihadist-drive-allows-iraq-kurds-take-disputed-areas|title=Jihadist drive allows Iraq Kurds to take disputed areas|work=ReliefWeb|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>这些地区后来也被划分为两党佩什梅格各自的控制区。

由于没有一个统一的指挥部可以统领全军，佩什梅格各部只能依靠政治上的忠诚而追随各自的领导团体。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://lebanonwire.com/1409MLN/14091314STR.asp |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-01-13 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150113050615/http://lebanonwire.com/1409MLN/14091314STR.asp |archivedate=2015-01-13 }}</ref>不过，佩什梅格的领导团体一直在试图减少党派偏见，禁止在战场上使用党派旗帜。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.shafaaq.com/index.php/politics/10849-barzani-prevents-lifting-partisan-flags-in-fighting-fronts-except-for-kurdistan-flag|title=Barzani prevents lifting partisan flags in fighting fronts except for Kurdistan flag|publisher=|accessdate=20 March 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150112051404/http://english.shafaaq.com/index.php/politics/10849-barzani-prevents-lifting-partisan-flags-in-fighting-fronts-except-for-kurdistan-flag|archivedate=2015年1月12日}}</ref>一项将整个部队统一到地区政府单一指挥的政治改革也正在进行中。<ref name="ReferenceA"/>截止到2015年1月，36个旅中已有12个纳入库尔德地区政府的控制之下，剩余70%的兵力依然由两党把控。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2015/01/iraq-kurdish-peshmerga-division-islamic-state.html#|title=Kurdish peshmerga divisions hamper war effort - Al-Monitor: the Pulse of the Middle East|work=Al-Monitor|accessdate=20 March 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150922034757/http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2015/01/iraq-kurdish-peshmerga-division-islamic-state.html|archive-date=2015-09-22|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

由于资金有限而军队规模庞大，库尔德民主党一直计划将军事素质较低而人员众多的部队缩减成规模更小但效率更高且训练有素的部队。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.dk/books?id=S6oP3Y9ZM3sC&pg=PA129&lpg=PA129&dq=15%27th+and+16%27th+division+peshmerga&source=bl&ots=c0IHY4AnSX&sig=UWDppeBU7nS4a3Lqe9dMc_7Aekk&hl=da&sa=X&ei=uxWzVMWRCurNygOq94CABw&ved=0CCwQ6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=15'th%20and%2016'th%20division%20peshmerga&f=false|title=Withdrawal from Iraq|publisher=|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ekurd.net/mismas/articles/misc2011/1/state4537.htm|title=Iraqi Kurdistan Armed Forces, Peshmerga, to Lose 130,000 Soldiers|publisher=|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref> 因此，在2009年，库尔德民主党和巴格达商议将部分佩什梅格士兵并入[[伊拉克陆军|伊拉克陆军]]，成立第15和第16师。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.dk/books?id=KgEo2q76J_0C&pg=PA309&lpg=PA309&dq=Peshmerga%09Projected+Residual+Force&source=bl&ots=MPM_oAI2pT&sig=wccwwoaiVcyLp3PkIhEi-WHmMvE&hl=da&sa=X&ei=MRKzVLrTIIjjywPA9IKQCg&ved=0CCQQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=Peshmerga%09Projected%20Residual%20Force&f=false|title=Iraq and the United States|publisher=|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref><ref>Chapman, Dennis. Security Forces of Kurdistan Regional Government, Us Army War College. 2009, page. 112.</ref>然而，埃尔比勒和巴格达之间由于争议地区关系持续紧张，转移计划被搁置。据报道，一些已经被转移的佩什梅格士兵被再次抛弃，有指控说，前佩什梅格士兵保持了对库尔德民主党的忠诚而不效忠于伊拉克军队指挥系统。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hurriyetdailynews.com/1000-kurdish-soldiers-desert-from-iraqi-army.aspx?pageID=238&nID=48701&|title=1,000 Kurdish soldiers desert from Iraqi army - MIDEAST|publisher=|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.dk/books?id=LzFaaWCuhdQC&pg=PA425&lpg=PA425&dq=peshmerga+iraqi+army+allegations&source=bl&ots=fDbx1jl-Oi&sig=rsdbbgjg7wAZJC29g9GMcViKk6o&hl=da&sa=X&ei=1RyzVLidL8n_ywO6-YHIBg&ved=0CD8Q6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=peshmerga%20iraqi%20army%20allegations&f=false|title=Iraq's Insurgency and the Road to Civil Conflict|publisher=|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>

虽然大部分的佩什梅格士兵是[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]，但也有一些属于[[基督教|基督教]]和[[雅兹迪教派|雅兹迪教派]]的士兵。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2014/12/21/us-mideast-crisis-iraq-sinjar-idUSKBN0JZ0QO20141221|title=Iraqi Kurds, Yazidis fight Islamic State for strategic town of Sinjar|work=Reuters|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://rudaw.net/english/kurdistan/070120151|title=Rudaw|work=Rudaw|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>

虽然军队几乎由男性组成，但自成立以来也一直存在着少数的女性士兵，目前有600名[[女兵|女兵]]在列。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/islamic-state/11216064/Meet-the-Kurdish-women-fighting-the-Islamic-State.html|title=Meet the Kurdish women fighting the Islamic State|date=8 November 2014|work=Telegraph.co.uk|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>库尔德民主党的佩什梅格女兵不被准许进入前线，并主要集中在物流和管理岗位。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rudaw.net/english/kurdistan/28092014|title=No Frontline Deployment for Female Kurdish Troops|work=Rudaw|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>而库尔德斯坦爱国联盟的佩什梅格女兵被部署到前线，积极对抗着ISIS。<ref>[http://rudaw.net/english/kurdistan/220620151 KRG halts recruiting of female Peshmerga]</ref><ref>[http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2014/08/iraq-kurdistan-female-regiment-peshmerga-fight-is.html Meet the female peshmerga forces fighting IS] {{Wayback|url=http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2014/08/iraq-kurdistan-female-regiment-peshmerga-fight-is.html |date=20150716054104 }}</ref>

截至2015年1月，佩什梅格武装依旧被以下三个实体分开：佩什梅格事务部、库尔德民主党和库尔德爱国联盟。

==装备==

由于库尔德地区并非独立的国家，所以佩什梅格的军火库是有限的。库尔德斯坦地方政府和伊拉克政府之间一直存在纠纷，巴格达担心库尔德人的独立倾向，便不再向伊拉克库尔德斯坦地区输送武器。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxnews.com/world/2015/01/03/iraq-peshmerga-desperate-for-us-arms-in-fight-against-isis/|title=Iraq's Peshmerga desperate for US arms in fight against ISIS|author=Hollie McKay|work=Fox News|accessdate=20 March 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924190810/http://www.foxnews.com/world/2015/01/03/iraq-peshmerga-desperate-for-us-arms-in-fight-against-isis/|archive-date=2015-09-24|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dw.de/arms-for-kurdish-peshmerga-to-affect-military-balance/a-17853077|title=Arms for Kurdish peshmerga to affect military balance|work=DW.DE|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>部队主要依赖于2003年美国入侵伊拉克时所缴获的伊拉克陆军的旧武器，那时佩什梅格武装很活跃。在此之前，一些武器也是在[[1991年伊拉克起义|1991年伊拉克起义]]期间缴获的。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2013/06/iraq-asks-krg-for-pre-saddam-weapons.html#|title=Iraqi Defense Ministry Asks KRG To Return Saddam-Era Weapons - Al-Monitor: the Pulse of the Middle East|work=Al-Monitor|accessdate=20 March 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150115214454/http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2013/06/iraq-asks-krg-for-pre-saddam-weapons.html|archivedate=2015年1月15日|df=}}</ref>在2014年6月ISIS发起进攻后，新伊拉克陆军撤退之际，佩什梅格武装据称再次设法得到了一些伊拉克陆军遗留的武器。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thenational.ae/world/middle-east/as-isil-retreats-iraqi-kurds-gain-new-ammunition#page1|title=As ISIL retreats, Iraqi Kurds gain new ammunition|author=Hugh Naylor|publisher=|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>自2014年8月以来，佩什梅格武装还缴获了一些来自ISIS的武器。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.nationalpost.com/2014/10/03/kurdish-forces-captured-a-isis-base-after-a-two-day-siege-but-the-isis-fighters-inside-somehow-slipped-away/|title=Kurdish forces captured an ISIS base after a two-day siege — but the ISIS fighters inside somehow slipped away|author=Richard Spencer, The Telegraph|date=3 October 2014|work=National Post|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>

2014年8月ISIS进攻后，多个政府决定使用诸如轻型武器、夜视仪和弹药的轻武器装备来装备佩什梅格部队。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/aug/14/uk-britain-arms-supply-kurdish-forces-iraq-isis|title=UK prepares to supply arms directly to Kurdish forces fighting Isis|author=Nicholas Watt|work=the Guardian|accessdate=23 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://ekurd.net/mismas/articles/misc2014/8/state8392.htm|title=Seven western states join US to arm Iraqi Kurdistan: Pentagon|publisher=|accessdate=23 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/aug/11/us-arm-peshmerga-iraq-kurdistan-isis|title=US to directly arm Kurdish peshmerga forces in bid to thwart Isis offensive|author=Spencer Ackerman|work=the Guardian|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>但库尔德官员和佩什梅格都强调，他们没有得到足够的装备。他们同时强调，巴格达甚至阻止轻型武器流入库尔德地区，武器需要直接发往库尔德地区政府而不是通过巴格达。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.washingtontimes.com/news/2014/nov/21/iraqi-kurds-us-we-need-heavy-weapons-defeat-islami/|title=Iraqi Kurds to U.S.: We need heavy weapons to defeat Islamic State|work=The Washingtion Times|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/world/national-security/iraqs-kurds-appeal-for-new-us-arms-to-combat-islamic-state/2014/11/12/d2a0fe28-6aa6-11e4-9fb4-a622dae742a2_story.html|title=Iraq’s Kurds appeal for new U.S. arms to combat Islamic State|work=Washington Post|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://hotair.com/archives/2014/09/10/kurds-to-us-give-us-the-heavy-weapons-we-need-to-fight-isis/|title=Kurds to US: Give us the heavy weapons we need to fight ISIS|work=HotAir.com|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2014/11/19/us-mideast-crisis-barzani-idUSKCN0J30ON20141119|title=Iraqi Kurds say West not providing enough arms to defeat Islamic State|work=Reuters|accessdate=20 March 2015}}</ref>

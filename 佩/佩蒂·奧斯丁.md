'''佩蒂·奧斯丁'''（Patti Austin）是[[美國|美國]][[節奏布魯斯|節奏布魯斯]]和[[爵士樂|爵士樂]][[歌手|歌手]]，生於1950年8月10日，其中她的世界名曲《Say You Love Me》被[[葉德嫻|葉德嫻]]和原田知世改編成粵語、日語版本<ref>Some sources still list Austin's birth year as 1948. She gave this date in the early years of her career, in order to avoid child work regulations. "I lied about my age and I kicked it up two years because in those days it was a problem to work at that age. [...] I think women are very foolish when they say they're younger than they are anyway."

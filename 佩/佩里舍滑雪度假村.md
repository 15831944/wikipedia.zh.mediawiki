'''佩里舍滑雪度假村'''（{{lang-en|Perisher Ski Resort}}）位於[[澳洲|澳洲]][[大雪山|大雪山]]，佔地{{convert|12|km2}}，基礎海拔為{{convert|1720|m}}，頂峰為{{convert|2054|m}}，是[[南半球|南半球]]最大的[[滑雪場|滑雪場]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.visitnsw.com/destinations/snowy-mountains/kosciuszko-national-park/perisher-valley|title=Perisher Valley|publisher=Destination NSW|accessdate=17 June 2013}}</ref>。度假村由{{link-en|雪道高山鐵路|Skitube Alpine Railway}}連接，是澳洲唯一的地下[[齒軌鐵路|齒軌鐵路]]系統<ref>{{citation | url = http://www.fido.org.au/2020/papers/David%20Jehan.pdf | title = Rails through the wilderness | author = David Jehan | publisher = Rail Technical Society of Australia | accessdate = 1 March 2015 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150924012420/http://www.fido.org.au/2020/papers/David%20Jehan.pdf | archive-date = 2015-09-24 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>。2015年3月30日，被美資{{link-en|韋爾度假村|Vail Resorts}}以1.77億澳元收購。

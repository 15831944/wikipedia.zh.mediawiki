'''悟真會'''（Self-Realization Fellowship，又譯'''自明友誼會'''）是一個國際性瑜伽組識，旨在向世界推廣瑜伽的思想。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.britannica.com/topic/Self-Realization-Fellowship|title=Self-Realization Fellowship {{!}} spiritual society|accessdate=2018-12-23|work=Encyclopedia Britannica|language=en}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite book|title=Autobiography of a Yogi|last=Yogananda|first=Paramhansa|publisher=Self-Realization Fellowship|year=1998|isbn=978-0-87612-079-8|location=Los Angeles, CA|pages=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=瑜伽之龍（上、下冊）|last=尤迦南達|first=|last2=李|first2=匡正|publisher=心靈文化|year=1989|isbn=|location=板橋市|pages=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=一個瑜伽行者的自傳|last=尤迦南達|first=|last2=劉|first2=粹倫|publisher=紅桌文化|year=2017|isbn=9789869280570|location=台北市|pages=}}</ref>它由著名[[瑜伽士|瑜伽士]][[帕拉宏撒·尤迦南達|尤迦南達]]在一九二零年於美國創立，至今已發展至世界各地，全球擁有超過五百個地方分會。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://members.yogananda-srf.org/CenterSearch/SearchMap.aspx|title=Find Nearest SRF Locations|accessdate=2018-12-23|work=members.yogananda-srf.org}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=Journey to Self-Realization - Collected Talks and Essays|last=Yogananda|first=Paramahansa|publisher=Self-Realization Fellowship|year=2000|isbn=9780876122563|location=|pages=}}</ref>不過，目前在華語地區中，只有香港一個地點。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://members.yogananda-srf.org/CenterSearch/SRFGroupDetailsTemplate.aspx?grpid=CTR010253|title=Hong Kong Meditation Circle|accessdate=2018-12-23|work=members.yogananda-srf.org}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.srfhk.com/|title=Self Realization Fellowship, Hong Kong|accessdate=2018-12-23|work=www.srfhk.com}}</ref>悟真會的宗旨，是向全世界宣揚瑜伽的知識，消除各種文化與宗教之間的分歧，務求以科學的方法使人打破自我意識的界限，親身獲得感受上帝的經驗。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=The ‘world of yoga’: The production and reproduction of therapeutic landscapes|url=https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S027795360700113X|date=2007-07-01|journal=Social Science & Medicine|issue=1|doi=10.1016/j.socscimed.2007.02.050|volume=65|pages=112–124|language=en|issn=0277-9536}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=Hinduism, gurus, and globalization|url=https://brill.com/abstract/book/edcoll/9789047422716/Bej.9789004154070.i-608_025.xml|last=Saha|first=Shandip|date=2007-01-01|journal=Religion, Globalization, and Culture|pages=485–502|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.yogananda-srf.org/uploadedFiles/meditation/HomeStudy/UOP_2014.pdf|title=Undreamed-of Possibilities|accessdate=2018-12-25|author=Self-Realization Fellowship|date=1997|work=www.yogananda-srf.org|publisher=Self-Realization Fellowship}}</ref>

'''坦佩雷主教座堂'''（{{lang-fi|Tampereen tuomiokirkko}}, {{lang-sv|Tammerfors domkyrka}}）是[[芬兰|芬兰]][[坦佩雷|坦佩雷]]的一座[[信义宗|信义宗]]教堂，坦佩雷教区的主教座堂。该建筑由{{tsl|fi|Lars Sonck|拉尔斯·松克}}设计，建于1902-1907年。<ref>[http://evl.fi/EVLen.nsf/Documents/61F63B4363AC14E4C22572BF00385766?openDocument&lang=EN Tampere Cathedral | Evl.fi - Evangelical Lutheran Church of Finland] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718055711/http://evl.fi/EVLen.nsf/Documents/61F63B4363AC14E4C22572BF00385766?openDocument&lang=EN |date=2011-07-18 }}</ref>

该堂以[[壁画|壁画]]著称，由[[象征主义|象征主义]]画家[[胡戈·辛贝里|胡戈·辛贝里]]绘于1905-1906年<ref>[http://www.finland-tourism.com/web/guest/finland-guide/what-to-see/attractions/detail/-/article-detail/10123/87106 VisitFinland - Detail - The Official Travel and Tourism Portal of Finnish Tourist Board] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303235418/http://www.finland-tourism.com/web/guest/finland-guide/what-to-see/attractions/detail/-/article-detail/10123/87106 |date=2016-03-03 }}</ref>。这些绘画在当时饱受批评<ref>[http://www.uta.fi/FAST/FIN/REL/ev-cathe.html The History, Art and Architecture of Tampere Cathedral] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070901093700/http://www.uta.fi/FAST/FIN/REL/ev-cathe.html |date=2007-09-01 }}</ref>。尤其是天花板顶部画了一条蛇，通常解释为罪的象征。

{{vgname|究极喧哗|en=Extreme Paintbrawl}}是1998年发行于[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]]的[[漆弹|漆弹]]电子游戏。游戏被视为史上最烂电子游戏，[[GameSpot|GameSpot]]打出1.7/10分<ref name="gs">{{cite web|title=Extreme Paintbrawl Review|url=http://www.gamespot.com/reviews/extreme-paintbrawl-review/1900-2535875|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111028005534/http://www.gamespot.com/pc/action/extremepaintbrawl/review.html |archivedate=2011-10-28 |publisher=[[GameSpot|GameSpot]]|accessdate=26 August 2011}}</ref>，[[IGN|IGN]]打出0.7/10分<ref name="ign-paintbrawl">{{cite web|url=http://pc.ign.com/articles/160/160455p1.html |title=IGN: Extreme PaintBrawl Review: Extreme?!? Yeah, like my mom at her modern dance class!!! |work=IGN.com |accessdate=2013-03-11}}</ref>，《[[PC_World|PC World]]》则只给出6/100分。其1999年、2000年和2002年发行的三款续作亦皆获得负面评价。

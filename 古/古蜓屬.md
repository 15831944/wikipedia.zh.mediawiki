'''古蜓屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Petalura'''''}}）是[[古蜓科|古蜓科]]下的一[[屬|屬]]，是一類體型非常巨大的[[蜻蜓|蜻蜓]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://biodiversity.org.au/afd/taxa/Petalura|title=Genus ''Petalura'' Leach, 1815|last=|first=|date=2012|website=[[Australian_Faunal_Directory|Australian Faunal Directory]]|publisher=[[Australian_Biological_Resources_Study|Australian Biological Resources Study]]|access-date=20 March 2017}}</ref>，其中生活在[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]的''[[Petalura_ingentissima|Petalura ingentissima]]''是現存最大的蜻蜓，體長超過{{convert|100|mm}}，翼展超過{{convert|160|mm}}。牠們的身體既長又纖細，有四支寬闊且充滿脈徑的[[翅膀|翅膀]]。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=76 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

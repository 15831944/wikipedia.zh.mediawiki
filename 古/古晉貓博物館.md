== 概況 ==
[[File:666_-_Kuching_Cat-Museum.JPG|缩略图]]
古晉貓博物館現坐落於古晉北区市政局總部大樓的底層<ref name=":0" />。博物館免費向公眾開放，但若拍照錄影則須收取額外費用<ref name=":1" />。

在館內共藏有兩千多種和貓有關的藏品<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.atlasobscura.com/places/kuching-cat-museum|title=Kuching Cat Museum|accessdate=2018-04-05|work=Atlas Obscura|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170914012043/http://www.atlasobscura.com/places/kuching-cat-museum|archivedate=2017-09-14}}</ref>，內容豐富，類型涵蓋[[標本|標本]]、圖畫、照片、[[藝術品|藝術品]]、書籍、[[玩具|玩具]]、海報等，甚至包括[[埃及|埃及]]的貓[[木乃伊|木乃伊]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://sarawak.attractionsinmalaysia.com/Cat-Musuem.php|title=Cat Museum, Kuching|accessdate=2018-04-05|publisher=Sarawak Tourist & Travel Guide {{!}} Malaysia|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170913013143/http://sarawak.attractionsinmalaysia.com/Cat-Musuem.php|archivedate=2017-09-13}}</ref><ref name=":2" />。每年還會舉辦世界性的貓展<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://people.com.cn/BIG5/paper68/17056/1496878.html|title=馬來西亞有個貓城|accessdate=2018-04-05|author=孟青|date=2006-03-09|work=人民網|publisher=環球時報|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180406013803/http://people.com.cn/BIG5/paper68/17056/1496878.html|archivedate=2018-04-06}}</ref>。

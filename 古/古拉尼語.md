'''古拉尼語'''（Gora dialect、Goranski、Našinski（字面意思是"我們的語言"））是由位於[[科索沃|科索沃]]、[[阿尔巴尼亚|阿尔巴尼亚]]以及[[馬其頓共和國|馬其頓共和國]]之間的邊界地區[[古拉尼人|古拉尼人]]所說的[[南斯拉夫语支|南斯拉夫语支]]的變種語言。 它是[[托爾拉庫方言|托爾拉庫方言]]語群的一部分。<ref name="Browne">Browne, Wayles (2002): Serbo-Croat. In: Bernard Comrie, Greville G. Corbett (eds.), ''The Slavonic Languages''. London: Taylor & Francis. [https://books.google.com/books?id=F4yvzLnMw3cC&pg=PA306&dq=The+Slavonic+Languages,+Bernard+Comrie,+Greville+G+Corbett&hl=de&source=gbs_toc_r&cad=0_0]. p. 383</ref>托爾拉庫方言語群也是東西方[[南斯拉夫语支|南斯拉夫语支]]之間的過渡語言。<ref name="ConcEnc">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/?id=F2SRqDzB50wC&pg=PA129 |title=Concise encyclopedia of languages of the world |first1=Keith |last1=Brown |first2=Sarah |last2=Ogilvie |publisher=Elsevier |year=2008 |isbn=0-08-087774-5 |page=120|accessdate=2013-03-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=itlREOzLnM0C&pg=PA17 |title=Papers from the 6-th International Conference on Historical Linguistics, Current issues in linguistic theory |first1=Jacek |last1=Fisiak |publisher= Benjamins Publishing |year=1985 |isbn= 9027235287 |pages=17 |chapter=Henrik Birnbaum: Divergence and convergence in linguistic evolution  |accessdate=2013-03-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=xGZedef70zAC&pg=PA620 |title=The handbook of language contact, Blackwell handbooks in Linguistics |first1=Raymond |last1=Hickey |publisher=John Wiley & Sons |year=2010 |isbn=140517580X |page=620 |accessdate=2013-03-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=F2SRqDzB50wC&printsec=frontcover&dq=balkan+linguistic+union+torlak&source=bl&ots=SPmCuaEIU9&sig=KebR2wB0wapdCQOJQqmDPD_uwTs&hl=bg#v=snippet&q=balkan%20sprachbund&f=false |title=Concise Encyclopedia of Languages of the World |first1=Keith |last1=Brown |first2=Sarah |last2=Ogilvie |publisher=Elsevier |year=2009 |isbn=0080877745 |pages=119-120 |accessdate=2013-03-24}}</ref>

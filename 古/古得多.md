'''古得多'''（{{lang-en|Goutte d'Or}}，{{IPA-fr|ɡut dɔʁ}}，又譯作'''黃金滴'''）是一處位於[[法國|法國]][[巴黎|巴黎]][[巴黎十八區|十八區]]的街區。

此處以Le marché Dejean市場而聞名<ref name="Dyer">Dyer, Ervin. "[http://www.post-gazette.com/travel/20021027paris1027trp2.asp Little Africa in Paris is a continent within a city]." ''[[Pittsburgh_Post-Gazette|Pittsburgh Post-Gazette]]''. Sunday 27 October 2002. Retrieved on 22 February 2010.</ref>，而Pôle Santé健康中心位於此<ref>"[http://www.paris.fr/portail/english/Portal.lut?page_id=8144&document_type_id=5&document_id=33678&portlet_id=18794 Health and social welfare: services in Paris]." City of Paris. Retrieved on 16 May 2010.</ref>。該街區有著大批[[阿拉伯|阿拉伯]]和[[北非|北非]]移民。

「Goutte d'Or」一名是指位於[[蒙馬特|蒙馬特]]東部和巴黎十八區{{link-en|巴黎小教堂街區|Quartier de La Chapelle|小教堂街區}}以西的地帶。

2016年1月，此處發生了{{link-en|2016年1月巴黎警察局襲擊|January 2016 Paris police station attack|恐怖主義攻擊}}<ref name=Landauro>{{cite news|last1=Landauro|first1=Inti|title=French Police Treating Knife Attack at Paris Police Station as Act of Terrorism|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/french-police-shoot-man-who-tried-to-enter-paris-police-station-1452169931|accessdate=2016-01-09|publisher=[[Wall_Street_Journal|Wall Street Journal]]|date=2016-01-08}}</ref><ref name=Breeden>{{cite news|last1=Breeden|first1=Aurelein|title=Man With Fake Explosives Killed in Paris on Charlie Hebdo Anniversary|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/01/08/world/europe/paris-attacker-knife-police-station.html?_r=0|accessdate=2016-01-09|publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]]|date=2016-01-07}}</ref>。

'''古粟特字母'''（{{lang-en|Old Sogdian}}）是一個位於[[Unicode字符平面映射#第一輔助平面|第一輔助平面]]的[[Unicode區段|Unicode區段]]，收錄了一群彼此相關、且非草書體的字母。此類字母曾在西元3至5世紀。<ref>{{cite book | title = The Unicode Standard, Version 11.0 | chapter = Chapter 14: South and Central Asia-III, Ancient Scripts | publisher = Unicode, Inc | date = June 2018 | location = Mountain View, CA | url = https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode11.0.0/ch14.pdf | isbn = 978-1-936213-19-1 }}</ref>

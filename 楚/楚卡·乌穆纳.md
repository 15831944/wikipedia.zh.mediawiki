'''楚卡·哈里森·乌穆纳'''（{{lang-en|'''Chuka Harrison Umunna'''}}） {{post-nominals|country=GBR|MP}}（{{bd|1978年|10月17日}}）是一位英国獨立政治人物，前[[工黨_(英國)|工黨]]籍議員。他于[[2010年英国大选|2010年]]当选斯特里汉姆选区国会议员，2011-2015年任影子商务大臣<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.timeshighereducation.co.uk/story.asp?sectioncode=26&storycode=417726&c=1 |title=Labour appoints fresh face to replace John Denham |work=Times Higher Education |date=7 October 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.debretts.com/people/debretts-500/politics/chuka-umunna|title=Debrett's 500|work=debretts.com|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924155134/http://www.debretts.com/people/debretts-500/politics/chuka-umunna|archivedate=2015-09-24}}</ref><ref name="independent1">{{cite news |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/politics/labour-mp-chuka-umunna-apologises-for-comments-on-elite-social-network-saying-londons-nightclubs-are-full-of-trash-8561809.html |title=Labour MP Chuka Umunna apologises for comments on elite social network saying London's nightclubs are 'full of trash' |work=The Independent |date=5 April 2013 |location=London}}</ref><ref name="twitlonger.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.twitlonger.com/show/n_1snes8j|title=Statement by Chuka Umunna MP on leaving the Shadow Cabinet by mutual agreement|date=13 September 2015|accessdate=13 September 2015}}</ref>。2019年2月18日與另外6位工黨議員集體退黨，創立中間派政治組織「[[獨立小組_(英國)|獨立小組]]」<ref>{{cite news |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=18-02-2019 |title=Seven MPs leave Labour Party in protest at Jeremy Corbyn's leadership |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-politics-47278902 |language= en|publisher=[[BBC|BBC]] |access-date=22-02-2019}}</ref>。

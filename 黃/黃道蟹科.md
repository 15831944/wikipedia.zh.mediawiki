'''黃道蟹科'''（[[學名|學名]]Cancridae）是[[蟹|蟹]]的一[[科|科]]。它們由6個現存的[[屬|屬]]<ref name="Ng"/>及11個只有[[化石|化石]]的屬組成。<ref name="Grave">{{cite journal en|journal=Raffles Bulletin of Zoology|year=2009|volume=Suppl. 21|pages=1–109|title=A classification of living and fossil genera of decapod crustaceans|author=Sammy De Grave, N. Dean Pentcheff, Shane T. Ahyong ''et al.''|url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s21/s21rbz1-109.pdf}}</ref>這些屬詳列如下：

*Cancrinae
** ''Anatolikos''
** ''Anisospinos''
** [[黃道蟹屬|黃道蟹屬]]（''Cancer''）
** ''Ceronnectes''
** ''Cyclocancer''
** ''Glebocarcinus''
** ''Metacarcinus''
** ''Microdium''
** ''Notocarcinus''
** ''Platepistoma''
** ''Romaleon''
** ''Santeecarcinus''
** ''Sarahcarcinus''
* Lobocarcininae
** ''Lobocarcinus''
** ''Miocyclus''
** ''Tasadia''

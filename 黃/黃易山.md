'''黃易山'''（{{lang-en|'''Yishan Wong'''}}）是一名[[美籍華人|美籍華人]]，[[Reddit|Reddit]]前任CEO<ref>{{cite web|last=Wong|first=Yishan|title=New reddit CEO reporting for duty|url=http://blog.reddit.com/2012/03/new-reddit-ceo-reporting-for-duty.html|publisher=[[reddit|reddit]]|accessdate=2012-03-28|date=2012-03-08}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://techcrunch.com/2010/03/24/longtime-facebook-engineer-yishan-wong-departs/|title=Longtime Facebook Engineer Yishan Wong Departs|date=2012-03-08|publisher=[[TechCrunch|TechCrunch]]}}</ref>，他和別人在[[山景城_(加利福尼亞州)|山景城]]共同創辦了Sunfire Offices<ref>{{cite web|url=http://techcrunch.com/2010/08/28/sunfire-offices/|title=Ex-Googler and Ex-Facebooker Start Invite-Only Workspace Sunfire Offices|publisher=[[TechCrunch|TechCrunch]]|date=2010-08-28}}</ref>，并擔任[[Quora|Quora]]的顧問。亦曾是[[Paypal|Paypal]]的高級工程經理，後來到[[Facebook|Facebook]]做工程項目主管。自2011年4月，開始給《[[福布斯|福布斯]]》擔任特約編輯<ref>{{cite web|title=Yishan Wong - SNIPPETS FROM SILICON VALLEY|url=http://blogs.forbes.com/people/yishanwong/|publisher=[[福布斯|Forbes Magazine]]|accessdate=28 March 2012|date=April 2011}}</ref>。

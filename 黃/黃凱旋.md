'''黃凱旋'''（{{lang-en|Benedict Wong}}，{{bd|1971年|7月3日}}）是一位[[英國籍華裔|華裔英國人]]男演員，其知名角色包括有[[Netflix|Netflix]]自製影集《[[马可·波罗_(电视剧)|馬可波羅]]》（2014）中的[[忽必烈|忽必烈]]大汗<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/tv-and-radio/2014/dec/13/benedict-wong-marco-polo-netflix |title=Benedict Wong: 'Television is reflecting an era that has passed, it's wrong' |last=Aroesti  |first=Rachel |newspaper=The Guardian |date=2014-12-13 |accessdate=2016-08-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/tv/features/the-chinese-game-of-thrones-marco-polo-sets-sail-for-netflix-9890764.html |title=The 'Chinese Game of Thrones': Marco Polo sets sail for Netflix |first=Sarah |last=Hughes |newspaper=The Independent |date=2014-11-29 |accessdate=2016-08-10}}</ref>、科幻電影《[[絕地救援|絕地救援]]》（2015年）中的布魯斯·吳（Bruce Ng）、科幻電影《[[普羅米修斯_(電影)|普羅米修斯]]》（2012年）中的太空船駕駛員<ref>{{cite web |first=Cameron |last=K. |url=http://www.denofgeek.com/movies/18758/actor-benedict-wong-talks-about-prometheus |title=Actor Benedict Wong talks about Prometheus |website=[[Den_of_Geek|Den of Geek]] |publisher=[[Dennis_Publishing|Dennis Publishing]] |date=2012-02-20 |accessdate=2014-07-13}}</ref>。他除了電影與電視作品外，也參加[[舞台劇|舞台劇]]演出。他於2016年上映的《[[奇異博士|奇異博士]]》<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/martian-actor-nabs-key-doctor-858155 |title='The Martian' Actor Nabs Key 'Doctor Strange' Role |first=Borys |last=Kit |newspaper=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |date=2016-01-21 |accessdate=2016-08-10}}</ref>電影中飾演「[[王_(漫威漫畫)|王]]」一角。

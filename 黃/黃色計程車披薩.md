'''黃色計程車披薩'''（{{lang-en|Yellow Cab Pizza}}）是菲律賓一家主要以販售披薩的連鎖店，2001年由埃里克·普諾、亨利·李和阿爾伯特·譚成立<ref>{{cite web|url=https://ph.she.yahoo.com/photos/8-food-brands-that-are-surprisingly-pinoy-in-origin-1375581264-slideshow/yellow-cab-pizza-photo-1375581863023.html|title=8 food brands that are surprisingly Pinoy in origin|date=2013-08-04|accessdate=2014-08-05|publisher=Yahoo SHE|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130807040458/http://ph.she.yahoo.com/photos/8-food-brands-that-are-surprisingly-pinoy-in-origin-1375581264-slideshow/yellow-cab-pizza-photo-1375581863023.html|archive-date=2013-08-07|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

== 擴展分店 ==
2017年8月11日，黃色計程車披薩透過員工努力和董事長承諾下，在{{link-en|斯里巴加萬港|Kiulap}}開設分店<ref>{{cite news|title=Yellow Cab Pizza Co opens doors to second branch|publisher=Borneo Bulletin|date=2018-01-19|author=James Kon|url=https://borneobulletin.com.bn/yellow-cab-pizza-co-opens-doors-to-second-branch/|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180223023953/https://borneobulletin.com.bn/yellow-cab-pizza-co-opens-doors-to-second-branch/|archivedate=2018-02-23|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。

'''突破摄星'''（{{lang-en|Breakthrough Starshot}}）是由[[突破倡議|突破计划]]提出的[[太空探索|太空探索]]项目，旨在研发名为“星片”（{{lang|en|StarChip}}）的[[光帆|光帆]]飞行器<ref name="CD-20160412">{{cite web |last=Gilster |first=Paul |title=Breakthrough Starshot: Mission to Alpha Centauri |url=http://www.centauri-dreams.org/?p=35402 |date=12 April 2016 |work=Centauri Dreams |accessdate=14 April 2016 }}</ref>，以期能以五分之一[[光速|光速]]（每秒6万千米）、经过约20年的航行时间抵达[[半人马座α星|半人马座α星]]，并在到达后再经过约4年的时间向地球传回信息。<ref>[http://newsrepublica.com/2016/04/13/mark-zuckerberg-joined-stephen-hawking-create-tiny-interstellar-spaceships/ Mark Zuckerberg joined Stephen Hawking to create tiny interstellar spaceships] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160423080737/http://newsrepublica.com/2016/04/13/mark-zuckerberg-joined-stephen-hawking-create-tiny-interstellar-spaceships/ |date=2016-04-23 }}</ref><ref name="NYT-20160412-db">{{cite news |last=Overbye |first=Dennis |authorlink=Dennis Overbye |title=Reaching for the Stars, Across 4.37 Light-Years; A Visionary Project Aims for Alpha Centauri, a Star 4.37 Light-Years Away |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2016/04/13/science/alpha-centauri-breakthrough-starshot-yuri-milner-stephen-hawking.html |date=12 April 2016 |work=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |accessdate=12 April 2016 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last= Stone |first=Maddie |url=http://gizmodo.com/a-russian-billionaire-and-stephen-hawking-want-to-build-1770467186 |title=Stephen Hawking and a Russian Billionaire Want to Build an Interstellar Starship |work=Gizmodo |date=April 12, 2016 |accessdate=12 April 2016 }}</ref><ref name="Starshot home">{{cite web |url=http://breakthroughinitiatives.org/Initiative/3 |title=Breakthrough Starshot |work=Breakthrough Initiatives |date=12 April 2016 |accessdate=12 April 2016 }}</ref>

物理学家[[史蒂芬·霍金|史蒂芬·霍金]]与投资人[[尤里·米尔纳|尤里·米尔纳]]于2016年4月12日在[[纽约|纽约]]共同宣布了该项目正式启动。项目的初期投资为1亿美元。米尔纳预计整个项目最终耗资可达50亿至100亿美元。<ref name="NYT-20160412-db"/>

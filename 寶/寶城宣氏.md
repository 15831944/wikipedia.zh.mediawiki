'''寶城宣氏'''（{{lang-ko|보성 선씨}}）是一個[[朝鮮族|朝鮮族]][[氏族|氏族]]，[[本貫_(朝鮮半島)|本貫]]於[[全羅南道|全羅南道]][[寶城郡|寶城郡]]。2000年左右，寶城宣氏約有34842名成員。該氏族的始祖為[[高麗王朝|高麗王朝]]時代歸化的[[宣允祉|宣允祉]]。他曾擔任中國[[明朝|明朝]]的學士。他以擊敗[[全羅道|全羅道]]沿海一帶的[[倭寇|倭寇]]建功並定居。在高麗滅亡後，放棄功勳留居寶城。<ref>{{Cite book|author=|date=|title=Academy of Korean Studies 보성선씨 宝城宣氏|publisher=[[Academy_of_Korean_Studies|Academy of Korean Studies]]|url=http://terms.naver.com/entry.nhn?docId=291138&cid=42835&categoryId=42835&mobile}}{{Dead link|date=2018年5月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|author={{ill|JIN Guanglin|ja|金光林 (歴史学者)}}|date=2014|title=A Comparison of the Korean and Japanese Approaches to Foreign Family Names|publisher=Journal of Cultural Interaction in East Asia Vol.5 [[Society_for_Cultural_Interaction_in_East_Asia|Society for Cultural Interaction in East Asia]]|url=http://www.sciea.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/03_JIN.pdf |isbn=}}p20</ref><ref>{{Cite book|author=|date=|title=선윤지(宣允祉)|publisher=[[Academy_of_Korean_Studies|Academy of Korean Studies]]|url=http://people.aks.ac.kr/front/tabCon/ppl/pplView.aks?pplId=PPL_5COc_A9999_1_0021091}}</ref>

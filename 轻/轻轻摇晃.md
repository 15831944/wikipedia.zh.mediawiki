《'''轻轻摇晃'''》({{lang-en|Lilting}}）是一部2014年英国剧情片，由短片作品《春》入选2011年柏林电影节和[[圣丹斯电影节|圣丹斯电影节]]的柬埔寨英裔[[许泰丰|许泰丰]]（Hong Khaou）导演<ref>{{cite web|url=http://microwave.filmlondon.org.uk/get_inspired/lilting/cast_and_crew/|title=Microwave – Get Inspired – Lilting – Cast and Crew – Crew – Writer/Director – Hong Khaou|publisher=}}</ref>，多米尼克·布坎南制片<ref>{{cite web|url=http://microwave.filmlondon.org.uk/get_inspired/lilting/cast_and_crew/crew/producer_dominic_buchanan|title=Microwave – Get Inspired – Lilting – Cast and Crew – Crew – Producer – Dominic Buchanan|publisher=}}</ref>。影片于2014年1月16日圣丹斯电影节首日全球首映<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sundance.org/festival/release/2014-festival-us-world-and-next-films-announced/|title=Sundance Institute Announces Films in U.S. and World Competitions, NEXT  for 2014 Sundance Film Festival – Sundance Film Festival|publisher=|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131205162021/http://www.sundance.org/festival/release/2014-festival-us-world-and-next-films-announced/|archivedate=2013-12-05}}</ref>，赢得世界电影戏剧大赛摄影奖<ref>{{cite web |title=Sundance: ‘Whiplash’ & ‘Rich Hill’ Win Grand Jury Awards; Dramatic Directing Goes To Cutter Hodierne For ‘Fishing Without Nets’|url=http://www.deadline.com/2014/01/sundance-film-festival-winners-2014-awards-list/|accessdate=January 26, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title='Whiplash' Owns the 2014 Sundance Film Festival Awards Netting Two Top Prizes|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/the-2014-sundance-film-festival-winners-as-they-come-in|accessdate=January 26, 2014}}</ref>。2014年8月8日影片于英国上映<ref>{{cite web |title=Lilting UK release date|url=http://www.filmdates.co.uk/films/6746-lilting/|accessdate=August 18, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Lilting BBC UK|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/bbcfilms/film/lilting|accessdate=August 18, 2014}}</ref>，2014年9月26日于美国上映<ref>{{cite web |title=Lilting|url=https://www.yahoo.com/movies/film/lilting|accessdate=August 18, 2014}}</ref>

== 制作 ==
剧本原题《轻快的过往》（Lilting the Past），在2011年最佳未制作英国电影剧本榜单Brit List中夺得第三名<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118045861|title=‘Call Up’ tops Brit List|work=Variety}}</ref>。

2012年，影片是Microwave三部开绿灯的作品中的一部<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118054312?refCatId=1061|title=Microwave heats up three pics|work=Variety}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://unitedagents.co.uk/node/5328|title=Hong Khaou's LILTING announced among new Microwave projects – United Agents|publisher=}}</ref>。剧组随后公布了三名主演<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.chinatownartsspace.com/files/casting_for_lilting.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2012-11-29 |0=2012-11-13 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121113150536/http://www.chinatownartsspace.com/files/casting_for_lilting.pdf |archivedate=2012-11-13 }}</ref>，随后由[[郑佩佩|郑佩佩]]和[[Andrew_Leung|Andrew Leung]]填补。

2012年11月开始摄制。导演许泰丰称电影受到了[[王家卫|王家卫]]《[[花样年华|花样年华]]》的视觉启发<ref>{{cite web|url=http://microwave.filmlondon.org.uk/get_inspired/lilting/directors_vision/|title=Microwave – Get Inspired – Lilting – Directors Vision|publisher=}}</ref>。制片过程中，Microwave安排迈克尔·温特伯顿辅导编剧兼导演许泰丰，布坎南由《[[伦敦到布莱顿|伦敦到布莱顿]]》、《[[污垢|污垢]]》和《[[献给爱妻的歌|献给爱妻的歌]]》制片人[[肯·马歇尔|肯·马歇尔]]执导<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.screendaily.com/news/production/microwaves-lilting-starring-ben-whishaw-underway-in-london/5049666.article?blocktitle=Latest-news&contentID=1846|title=Microwave’s Lilting, starring Ben Whishaw, underway in London|publisher=}}</ref>。预算跟所有Microwave导演一样是12万英镑。影片是Microwave计划中的第一部双语电影<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.standard.co.uk/news/celebritynews/ben-whishaw-goes-from-skyfall-q-to-budget-film-8399271.html|title=Ben Whishaw goes from Skyfall Q to budget film|work=The Evening Standard}}</ref>。

== 评价 ==
《轻轻摇晃》获得评论家的积极评价。影评聚合网站[[烂番茄|烂番茄]]基于24条积极评价，打出96%的“新鲜度”，评级得分6.9（满分10分）<ref>{{cite web |title=Lilting (2014)|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/lilting/|work=[[Rotten_Tomatoes|Rotten Tomatoes]]|publisher=Flixster|accessdate=August 19, 2014}}</ref>。

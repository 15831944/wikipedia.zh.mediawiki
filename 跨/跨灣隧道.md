'''跨灣隧道'''（{{lang-en|Transbay Tube}}），也稱'''海湾隧道'''，是[[美國|美國]][[舊金山灣區捷運系統|舊金山灣區捷運系統]]的一部分，位于[[加州|加州]][[舊金山灣|舊金山灣]]下方，西端銜接舊金山市中心內的[[市場街地鐵|市場街地鐵隧道]]，以東則銜接至[[伯克利_(加利福尼亞州)|柏克萊]]、[[奥克兰_(加利福尼亚州)|奧克蘭]]（屋崙）等東灣地區。隧道长{{convert|3.6|mi|km}}，如果从隧道边最近的车站计算，长度共计{{convert|6|mi|km|0}}。隧道在地表以下最深达到{{convert|135|ft|m}}。

隧道的部件在地面组装，再由船只运至施工现场，然后沉入海底并固定（将管壁用沙砾和海床固定）。这种[[沉管式隧道|沉管式隧道]]不同于在岩层中破土前进的[[鑽挖式隧道|鑽挖式隧道]]。

这条隧道是湾区地铁初期计划的最后一部分。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=PX4cAAAAIBAJ&sjid=21EEAAAAIBAJ&pg=7080%2C1462789|title=San Francisco gets its space age underwater trains|author=Strand, Robert|date=14 September 1974|newspaper=The Dispatch|accessdate=20 August 2016|agency=UPI}}</ref>除[[里奇蒙－費利蒙線|里奇蒙－費利蒙線]]外的所有地铁线都通过这里，所以此隧道是湾区地铁里最繁忙的区间，在高峰期每小时会有2.8万人次通过<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bayareaeconomy.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/BACEI_TransbayCrossing_Feb2016b.pdf|title=The Case for a Second Transbay Transit Crossing|date=February 2016|publisher=Bay Area Council Economic Institute|page=7|access-date=2016-03-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160319065212/http://www.bayareaeconomy.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/BACEI_TransbayCrossing_Feb2016b.pdf|archive-date=2016-03-19|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，最短[[班距|班距]]只有2分半。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/opinion/article/2nd-Transbay-Tube-needed-to-help-keep-BART-on-5737004.php|title=2nd Transbay Tube needed to help keep BART on track|last=Mallett|first=Zakhary|date=September 7, 2014|work=San Francisco Chronicle}}</ref> 地铁在隧道里可达到最高时速{{convert|80|mi/h|}}, 是均速{{convert|36|mi/h}} 的两倍多。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=3VEaAAAAIBAJ&sjid=ZycEAAAAIBAJ&pg=2542%2C102474|title=BART: It's not the system it set out to be|author=Minton, Torri|date=17 September 1984|newspaper=Spokane Chronicle|accessdate=20 August 2016|agency=AP|quote=Hitting speeds close to 80 mph only in the 3.6-mile tube under the bay, the trains average 36 mph for safety reasons, [BART spokesman Sy] Mouber said.}}</ref>

== 构想与建设 ==

=== 早期构想 ===
最早的跨旧金山湾海底隧道的概念是由旧金山的“怪人”[[诺顿一世|诺顿一世]]在1872年5月12日[[诺顿一世#下旨建桥|提出]]的。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://cdnc.ucr.edu/cgi-bin/cdnc?a=d&d=PA18720615.2.4|title=Proclamation|author=Norton I|date=June 15, 1872|author-link=Emperor Norton|newspaper=The Pacific Appeal|via=California Digital Newspaper Collection|page=1|quote=Believing Oakland Point to be the proper and only point of communication from this side of the Bay to San Francisco, we, Norton I, Dei gratia Emperor of the United State and Protector of Mexico, do hereby command the cities of Oakland and San Francisco to make an appropriation for paying the expense of a survey to determine the practicability of a tunnel under water; and if found practicable, that said tunnel be forthwith built for a railroad communication. Norton I. Given at Brooklyn the 12th day of May, 1812.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.emperorsbridge.org/bridge/proclamations/|title=Bridge Proclamations|accessdate=23 July 2017|date=2016|last=Lumea|first=John|website=The Emperor's Bridge Campaign|deadurl=bot: unknown|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170723210329/http://www.emperorsbridge.org/bridge/proclamations/|archivedate=2017年7月23日|df=}}</ref> 他在同年9月17日再次“下达”了这个“旨意”，还威胁要以“抗旨”为由“逮捕”奥克兰和旧金山两市的市长。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://cdnc.ucr.edu/cgi-bin/cdnc?a=d&d=PA18720921.2.7|title=Proclamation|author=Norton I|date=September 21, 1872|author-link=Emperor Norton|newspaper=The Pacific Appeal|via=California Digital Newspaper Collection|page=1|quote=Whereas, we issued our decree, ordering the citizens of San Francisco and Oakland to appropriate funds for the survey of a suspension bridge from Oakland Point via Goat Island; also for a tunnel; and to ascertain which is the best project; and whereas, the said citizens have hitherto neglected to notice our said decree; and whereas, we are determined our authority shall be fully respected; now, therefore, we do hereby command the arrest, by the army, of both the Boards of City Fathers, if they persist in neglecting our decrees. Given under our royal hand and seal, at San Francisco, this 17th day of September, 1872. NORTON 1.}}</ref>

这一概念被正式地提出是在1920年10月，由{{Translink|en|George Washington Goethals|戈瑟尔斯}}[[少将|少将]]，[[巴拿马运河|巴拿马运河]]的建造者提出。他的计划中考虑到了抗震的方面，提及了把隧道建在在湾区海底的海泥中，与今天的隧道几乎吻合。他预计的成本是{{US$|50000000|1921|round=-5}}<ref>{{cite journal|title=San Francisco Bay Bridge Project Revived by New Plans|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kIEcAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA16#v=onepage&q&f=false|date=7 July 1921|journal=Engineering News-Record|accessdate=8 September 2016|issue=1|volume=87|pages=16–17|quote=Howe & Peters, consulting engineers of San Francisco, have been working for nearly two years as Pacific Coast representatives of George W. [...] The gradient would be kept below 3 per cent so freight could be handled easily. Vipond Davies和{{Tsl|en|Ralph Modjeski}}又提出了隧道加桥梁的方案，这个方案和改良后的{{Translink|en|Southern Crossing (California)||南部通道}}（从旧金山的使命岩和{{Tsl|en|Potrero Point|波卓洛角}}，向东至[[阿拉米達_(加利福尼亞州)|阿拉米達]]）很接近。两人同时指出了戈瑟尔斯将军的计划中，长距离公铁两用隧道潜在的通风问题，也暗示了仅通行[[电气化铁路|电气化铁路]]隧道的可行性。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Features of San Francisco Bay Bridge Report|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kIEcAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA268#v=onepage&q&f=false|date=18 August 1921|journal=Engineering News-Record|accessdate=8 September 2016|issue=7|volume=87|pages=268–269|quote=Any high bridge between [[Yerba_Buena_Island|Yerba Buena Island]] and San Francisco would naturally land on [[Telegraph_Hill,_San_Francisco|Telegragh Hill]] ''[sic]''. 戴维斯和莫杰斯基的计划在1921年10月加入了其他12种跨湾通道方案，其中不少也是电气铁路方案。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Thirteen Projects Submitted for San Francisco Bay Bridge|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kIEcAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA739#v=onepage&q&f=false|date=3 November 1921|journal=Engineering News-Record|accessdate=8 September 2016|issue=18|volume=87|page=739}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=xlE4hwWVqqwC&pg=PA178#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=The San Francisco Bay Area: A Metropolis in Perspective|author=Scott, Mel|date=1985|publisher=University of California Press|accessdate=8 September 2016|isbn=0-520-05510-1|edition=Second|location=Berkeley, California|page=178|chapter=ELEVEN: Seeds of Metropolitan Regionalism}}</ref>

=== 建造过程 ===

1979年1月17日约18点，一辆开往旧金山方向的7节列车（编号117）在隧道内发生电气火灾<ref name="MTTSS">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bLjUbrsqpMYC&pg=PA42#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Making Transportation Tunnels Safe and Secure|author=<!--Staff writer(s); one killed|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=18 January 1979|newspaper=Eugene Register-Guard|accessdate=17 August 2016|agency=AP}}</ref> ——因为吸入浓烟，在救火时不幸殉职。 车上的40名乘客和2名地铁工作人员随后被另一方向的列车救起<ref name="NTSB-RAR7905">{{cite report|url=http://www.ntsb.gov/investigations/AccidentReports/Reports/RAR7905.pdf|title=Railroad Accident Report: Bay Area Rapid Transit District fire on train No. 117 and evacuation of passengers while in the Transbay Tube|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line-->|number=NTSB-RAR-79-5|publisher=National Transportation Safety Board|date=19 July 1979|accessdate=17 August 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=UbIzAAAAIBAJ&sjid=ZzIHAAAAIBAJ&pg=3487%2C4325795|title=Fire shuts down BART 'tube'|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=19 January 1979|newspaper=Lodi News-Sentinel|accessdate=17 August 2016|agency=UPI}}</ref> 。此次灭火行动中低效的通讯和协调，促使[[美国消防协会|美国消防协会]](NFPA)增加运输行业的标准(NFPA 130, ''固定线路运输和乘客轨道交通系统标准''<!-- 原文：Standard for Fixed Guideway Transit and Passenger Rail Systems -->)<ref name="MTTSS" />。

当天早些时候，开往旧金山的363号列车在16：30，因为不明烟雾，在隧道内紧急停车。在排除故障时，驾驶员发现6节和8节车厢的防出轨条脱落，9节车厢启动了制动。随后驾驶员清理了防脱轨器的电路，解除了9节车厢的制动，并重新启动了列车。列车最后在戴利城站退出服务，作进一步检修。之后的列车都改为手动驾驶，不过很快便恢复了自动控制。其中有驾驶员报告看见363号列车遗留的零件，但是轨道没有被阻挡，依然能使用。<ref name="NTSB-RAR79053">{{cite report|url=http://www.ntsb.gov/investigations/AccidentReports/Reports/RAR7905.pdf|title=Railroad Accident Report: Bay Area Rapid Transit District fire on train No. 117 and evacuation of passengers while in the Transbay Tube|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line-->|number=NTSB-RAR-79-5|publisher=National Transportation Safety Board|date=19 July 1979|accessdate=17 August 2016}}</ref>

火灾是由117号列车第5节和6节的集电靴引起的，他们撞到了前面列车落下的零件，发生了短路并起火。列车在18：06，刚刚进入隧道后停车，驾驶员报告称，列车内出现浓烟，他无法看清隧道。控制中心随后切断第三轨供电，但不到1分钟后又恢复，希望驾驶员能断开起火的车厢，然而未能成功。排气扇在08分打开，随后第三轨在15分再次断电。列车上有一名地铁管理局的高层，协助了乘客离开事发车厢。

奥克兰市消防局接到奥克兰西站的报警后，派出了2名地铁警察和9名消防员，乘坐900号列车前往现场。900号列车最后在事发车厢{{convert|200|ft}}报告了起火车厢猛烈的火势和浓烟。在接近火场时，1名警察和7名消防员进入了逃生走廊，而其他人则因为浓烟被迫回到900号列车上。

另一边，载着千余名乘客的111号列车被扣在了旧金山一侧的[[內河碼頭站|內河碼頭站]]。在18：21，111号列车沿着东行隧道行驶，并接近起火车厢的位置。当所有117号列车的乘客都上车后，消防员搜索了起火列车，并在18：59向指挥部报了平安。虽然一些烟雾飘了过来，111号列车还是成功启动，随后疾驰至奥克兰西站，送乘客前往医院。同时更多消防员从奥克兰一侧进入隧道，赶赴火场。<ref name="NTSB-RAR79054">{{cite report|url=http://www.ntsb.gov/investigations/AccidentReports/Reports/RAR7905.pdf|title=Railroad Accident Report: Bay Area Rapid Transit District fire on train No. 117 and evacuation of passengers while in the Transbay Tube|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line-->|number=NTSB-RAR-79-5|publisher=National Transportation Safety Board|date=19 July 1979|accessdate=17 August 2016}}</ref>但由于门被锁住，加上现场浓烟滚滚，消防员没能进入东行隧道来避难。<ref name="MTTSS3">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bLjUbrsqpMYC&pg=PA42#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Making Transportation Tunnels Safe and Secure|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=2006|publisher=Transportation Research Board|accessdate=17 August 2016|isbn=978-0-309-09871-7|location=Washington, DC|pages=42–44|chapter=3: Case Studies}}</ref>

111号列车经过的气流带倒了一些消防员，他们排成一列，开始在浓烟中，向东寻找出路。过程中，他们的防毒面具渐渐失灵，其中小队长威廉·埃利奥特无法再正常呼吸，并向队员求救。消防员再撤离火场时，又有一辆列车开来接应他们，并立即送他们会奥克兰西站，前往最近的医院。埃利奥特由于吸入过量浓烟和氰化物，不幸殉职。<ref name="NTSB-RAR79055">{{cite report|url=http://www.ntsb.gov/investigations/AccidentReports/Reports/RAR7905.pdf|title=Railroad Accident Report: Bay Area Rapid Transit District fire on train No. 117 and evacuation of passengers while in the Transbay Tube|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line-->|number=NTSB-RAR-79-5|publisher=National Transportation Safety Board|date=19 July 1979|accessdate=17 August 2016}}</ref>

火虽然没有被完全扑灭，但是还是在22：45得到了控制。次日18点，奥克兰的消防员又再次前往地铁的车辆段，阻止已经面目全非<!-- gutted
extremely bad -->的车厢里火势的扩大。<ref name="MTTSS2">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bLjUbrsqpMYC&pg=PA42#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Making Transportation Tunnels Safe and Secure|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=2006|publisher=Transportation Research Board|accessdate=17 August 2016|isbn=978-0-309-09871-7|location=Washington, DC|pages=42–44|chapter=3: Case Studies}}</ref> 湾区地铁在因隧道停运造成的{{US$|1000000|1979|round=-4}}亏损基础上，还花费了{{US$|1100000|1979|round=-4}}维修隧道和加强安全措施。<ref name="Lodi79">{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=ZLIzAAAAIBAJ&sjid=ZzIHAAAAIBAJ&pg=1089%2C1856502|title=BART cancels request to reopen bay tube|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=12 February 1979|newspaper=Lodi News-Sentinel|accessdate=20 August 2016|agency=UPI}}</ref>

随后，在同年的二月，湾区地铁管理局向旧金山和奥克兰消防局的局长提交了新的疏散方案，<ref name="Lodi792">{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=ZLIzAAAAIBAJ&sjid=ZzIHAAAAIBAJ&pg=1089%2C1856502|title=BART cancels request to reopen bay tube|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=12 February 1979|newspaper=Lodi News-Sentinel|accessdate=20 August 2016|agency=UPI}}</ref> 但是由于公共设施委员会主任理查德·葛拉威尔认为，“海底隧道的乘客应该意识到，地铁方面现在重开隧道，是不能提供有保障的服务的”<!-- 原文："the patrons of BART who utilize its services should be fully aware that the instant order [to reopen service] does not in any way provide a guarantee of safe service." -->，隧道在4月才恢复通行。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=0hVWAAAAIBAJ&sjid=_eEDAAAAIBAJ&pg=4436%2C1433318|title=BART resumes tube service for first time since fatal fire|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=5 April 1979|newspaper=Eugene Register-Guard|accessdate=17 August 2016|agency=AP}}</ref> 两市消防局也批评湾区地铁方面没有让消防队充分掌握紧急情况的进展。<ref name="MTTSS4">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bLjUbrsqpMYC&pg=PA42#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Making Transportation Tunnels Safe and Secure|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=2006|publisher=Transportation Research Board|accessdate=17 August 2016|isbn=978-0-309-09871-7|location=Washington, DC|pages=42–44|chapter=3: Case Studies}}</ref>

=== 地震 ===
作为一项预防措施，地铁的应急预案里要求，地铁在遭遇地震时应及时停车，而在海底隧道和伯克利丘隧道里的列车则应尽快前往最近的车站。随后线路会被检查，在确认安全后再恢复运营。<ref>{{cite press release|url=http://www.bart.gov/news/articles/2015/news20151014|title=BART to participate in statewide earthquake drill Thursday|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=14 October 2015|publisher=San Francisco Bay Area Rapid Transit District|accessdate=7 September 2016}}</ref>迄今最大的[[1989年洛馬普里塔地震|1989年洛马普里塔地震]]发生时，一辆正在隧道内的列车被下令停车，虽然驾驶员没有感受到震动。<ref name="Loma+252">{{cite web|url=http://www.bart.gov/marketing/25-years-after-Loma-Prieta-quake|title=Behind the Scenes of BART's Role as Lifeline for the Bay Area|accessdate=7 September 2016|author=Jordan, Melissa|date=2014|publisher=San Francisco Bay Area Rapid Transit District|quote=Donna "Lulu" Wilkinson, an experienced train operator, was barreling through the Transbay Tube at 80 miles per hour in the cab of a 10-car train when the quake hit.<br/>"I didn't even feel it," she recalled. === 行人闯入 ===
在2012年10月<ref>{{cite news|url=http://sanfrancisco.cbslocal.com/2012/10/15/man-walking-in-transbay-tube-prompted-temporary-bart-shutdown/|title=Man Walking In Transbay Tube Prompts Temporary BART Shutdown|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=15 October 2012|publisher=CBS SFBayArea|accessdate=17 August 2016}}</ref>和2013年8月<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.mercurynews.com/breaking-news/ci_23841648/bart-experiencing-delay-due-police-activity|title=BART trains back on track after man found walking in Transbay Tube|author=Alund, Natalie Neysa|date=11 August 2013|newspaper=Oakland Tribune|accessdate=18 August 2016}}</ref>，都发生过行人从[[內河碼頭站|內河碼頭站]]闯入隧道，逼停地铁服务的事故。在2016年12月底，又有一位行人以相同的方式闯入，并在隧道内逗留了一个小时。为了保障地铁的服务，警方一边搜寻他的下落，一边要求地铁以手动模式，低速通过隧道。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/crime/article/Man-who-sparked-BART-delays-by-running-into-10827018.php|title=Man who sparked BART delays by running into Transbay Tube IDd|author=Bodley, Michael|date=30 December 2016|newspaper=San Francisco Chronicle|accessdate=13 April 2017}}</ref>

=== 设备故障 ===
由于设备开始老化，列车曾几次被滞留在隧道内。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/Stuck-BART-train-causes-major-delays-10839769.php|title=BART systemwide meltdown after train gets stuck in West Oakland|date=6 January 2017|author2=Veklerov, Kimberly|newspaper=San Francisco Chronicle|accessdate=13 April 2017|author3=Ravani, Sarah|author1=Cabanatuan, Michael}}</ref>

* 2010年3月，一辆列车的车厢突然脱钩，导致列车被迫停下。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/BART-train-splits-in-two-in-Transbay-Tube-3195858.php|title=BART train splits in two in Transbay Tube|author=Lee, Henry K.|date=17 March 2010|newspaper=San Francisco Chronicle|accessdate=13 April 2017}}</ref> 
* 2014年9月，两辆维护车辆在隧道内相撞，并损坏了部分轨道，导致地铁只能使用另一侧的轨道。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/BART-reopens-Transbay-Tube-track-4565168.php|title=BART reopens Transbay Tube track|date=3 September 2014|author2=Huet, Ellen|newspaper=San Francisco Chronicle|accessdate=13 April 2017|author3=Lee, Henry K.|author1=Kane, Will}}</ref> 
* 2015年1月，一辆列车的制动意外启动，使列车在隧道内停下。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/BART-delays-after-train-gets-stuck-in-Transbay-6000555.php|title=BART delays again after train gets stuck in Transbay Tube|author=Williams, Kale|date=7 January 2015|newspaper=San Francisco Chronicle|accessdate=13 April 2017}}</ref> 
* 2016年12月，一辆列车出现故障，在隧道内抛锚。之后列车只能以手动模式，减速通行<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/Train-stuck-in-Transbay-Tube-causes-major-BART-10808541.php|title=Train stuck in Transbay Tube causes major BART delays|author=Veklerov, Kimberly|date=20 December 2016|newspaper=San Francisco Chronicle|accessdate=13 April 2017}}</ref> 
* 2017年4月，又有一辆列车的制度意外启动，使列车停下。

=== 噪音 ===
[[旧金山纪事报|旧金山纪事报]]在2010年做了一份调查，指出海底隧道是全地铁系统里噪音最大的部分，达到了100[[分貝|分貝]]，和一台电钻不相上下。<ref name=":02">{{Cite web|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/Noise-on-BART-How-bad-is-it-and-is-it-harmful-3175757.php|title=Noise on BART: How bad is it and is it harmful?|date=2010-09-07|last=Cabanatuan|first=Michael|website=SFGate|access-date=2016-04-22}}</ref> 噪音又因为四周的水泥墙壁，和隧道内弯曲的轨道，而非常尖锐，被形容为“女鬼的声音、尖叫的猫头鹰和《[[異世奇人|异世奇人]]》中失控的[[TARDIS|时光机]]”。<ref name=":02" /> 2015年，地铁方面更换了{{Convert|6500|feet}}、润滑了{{Convert|3|mi}}的轨道，有效减少了噪音，也得到了乘客的好评。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bart.gov/news/articles/2015/news20150813|title=Riders notice a quieter ride following first of two tube shutdowns|date=2015-08-13|website=www.bart.gov|access-date=2016-04-22}}</ref>

=== 海运影响 ===
穿行在湾区的船只，在下锚的时候，可能会损坏[[陰極防蝕|阴极保护]]层的[[阳离子|阳离子]]。由于阳极是从围绕着隧道的壕沟中突出来的，他们更容易受到损坏。虽然海运部门要求了船只在经过隧道附近时不能下锚，但地铁方面还是会定期检查阳离子层的情况。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://archives.sfexaminer.com/sanfrancisco/bart-working-to-protect-transbay-tube-from-elements-ships/Content?oid=2337152|title=BART working to protect Transbay Tube from elements, ships|author=Reisman, Will|date=12 May 2013|newspaper=San Francisco Examiner|accessdate=9 September 2016}}</ref>

2014年1月31日，由于一艘货船在早上8：45在隧道附近下锚。基于船只的位置，[[美国海岸警卫队|海岸警卫队]]在11：55通知了地铁管理局，认为锚离隧道非常近，隧道随后被关闭20分钟，以检查潜在的损坏。在检查过程中，两列隧道内的列车也被迫停驶，列车服务也晚点了15-20分钟。由于没有问题，隧道在12：15重新开放，并于13：00恢复正常运营。<ref name="MercuryAnchor22">{{cite news|url=http://www.mercurynews.com/2014/01/31/bart-no-damage-after-container-ships-anchor-drops-near-transbay-tube/|title=BART: No damage after container ship's anchor drops near Transbay Tube|date=31 January 2014|author2=Alund, Natalie Neysa|newspaper=The Mercury News|accessdate=9 September 2016|location=San Jose, California|author1=Bender, Kristin J.}}</ref>码头的引航员<!-- harbor pilot
引航员 -->随后记录了锚的准确位置，在隧道西南方向{{convert|1200|ft}}处。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/bayarea/article/BART-tube-reopened-after-anchor-scare-5193816.php|title=BART tube reopened after anchor scare|date=1 February 2014|author2=Ho, Vivian|newspaper=San Francisco Chronicle|accessdate=9 September 2016|author1=Williams, Kale}}</ref> 

2017年4月，一艘为地铁方面做保护层维护的[[起重機船|起重机船]]''复仇者''遭在夜间遇了一场冬季风暴，随后反转并沉没，船体最后停在了隧道的外壁上，万幸没有影响隧道的通行，潜水员也在次日止住了船上泄露的柴油。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfexaminer.com/close-eye-kept-sunken-barge-atop-barts-transbay-tube/|title=Close eye being kept on sunken barge atop BART's Transbay Tube|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=11 April 2017|newspaper=San Francisco Examiner|accessdate=13 April 2017|agency=Bay City News}}</ref>

== 未来发展 ==
为庆祝创建50周年，湾区捷运在2007年宣布了未来50年的计划。由于隧道的运力在2030年将达到饱和，管理局构想了在旧金山湾下方建造4个隧道，于现有的隧道平行，并在南部建造[[跨湾换乘枢纽|跨湾换乘枢纽]]（已初步完工），连接[[加州火車|加州火车]]和计划中的[[加利福尼亞高速鐵路|加州高铁]]系统。计划新建的四个隧道中，两个将提供给地铁，另外两个提供给常规/高速铁路使用，最终将有六个隧道。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?file=/c/a/2007/06/22/MNGJQQJVSD1.DTL|title=BART's New Vision: More, Bigger, Faster|last=Cabanatuan|first=Michael|date=2007-06-22|publisher=[[San_Francisco_Chronicle|San Francisco Chronicle]]|pages=A-1|accessdate=2008-04-17}}</ref>

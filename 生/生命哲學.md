'''生命哲學'''<ref>{{cite journal|author=郭官义|title=〈什么是生命哲学〉|journal=《世界哲学》|year=1985|volume=4|page=79|url=http://www.cqvip.com/qk/80458a/198504/1002296954.html|accessdate=2017-10-23}}</ref>（{{lang-de|Lebensphilosophie}}，在英文當中譯爲「{{lang|en|Life-philosophy}}」 ，有時與[[人生哲學|人生哲學]]同樣寫作「{{lang|en|Philosophy of life}}」）是哲學當中將[[生命的意義|生命意義]]、價值和目的作爲哲學之中心而强調的一個流派<ref name=routledge>{{cite book|first=Jason |last=Gaiger |chapter=Lebensphilosophie |title={{le|羅德里奇哲學大百科|Routledge Encyclopedia of Philosophy|Routledge Encyclopedia of Philosophy}} |editor1-first=Edward |editor1-last=Craig |publisher=Routledge |year=1998}}</ref>。

==綜述==
生命哲學的概念最早由[[阿圖爾·叔本華|阿圖爾·叔本華]]、[[索倫·奧貝·克爾凱郭爾|索倫·奧貝·克爾凱郭爾]]、[[弗里德里希·尼采|弗里德里希·尼采]]等學者的作品啓發，作爲對[[實證主義|實證主義]]的崛起和後康德主義當中的理論焦點之回應誕生於19世紀[[德國|德國]]<ref name=routledge/><ref name="Friedman">{{le|邁克爾·弗里德曼 (哲學家)|Michael Friedman (philosopher)|Michael Friedman}}, ''A Parting of the Ways: Carnap, Cassirer, and Heidegger'', Open Court, 2013.</ref>。生命哲學運動與[[生命冲力|生命冲力]]的{{le|主觀 (哲學)|Subject (Philosophy)|主觀}}哲學體系存在間接關聯，後者由[[亨利·柏格森|亨利·柏格森]]提出、著重於{{le|直接經驗|Direct experience}}<ref name=britannica>{{cite web|quote=In Germany the corresponding school [to [[vitalism|vitalism]]], known as ''Lebensphilosophie'' (“philosophy of life”), began to take on aspects of a political ideology in the years immediately preceding World War I. The work of Hans Driesch and Ludwig Klages, for example, openly condemned the superficial intellectualism of Western civilization. In associating “reason” with the shortcomings of “civilization” and “the West,” Lebensphilosophie spurred many German thinkers to reject intellection in favour of the irrational forces of blood and life. In the words of Herbert Schnädelbach, at this point “philosophy of life tendentiously abolished the traditional difference between nature and culture and thus facilitated the success of the general biologism in the theory of culture, which culminated in National Socialist racism.” |title=Continental philosophy |first=Richard |last=Wolin |work=[[Encyclopædia_Britannica|Encyclopædia Britannica]] |url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/1308494/continental-philosophy |accessdate=April 24, 2011}}</ref>。

20世紀的生命哲學對[[社會規範|社會規範]]與[[風俗|風俗]]相當重視。{{le|以色列裔美國人|Israeli Americans|以色列裔美國}}歷史學家尼特贊·萊博維奇（{{lang|en|Nitzan Lebovic}}）認爲生命哲學顯現了生命概念之合集與1920年代德國教育制度所迎來的「生命學説」或「生命科學」（{{lang|en|Lebenskunde}}）概念間的緊密聯係——後者支撐了當時生物學家普遍懷有之廣泛意義上的哲學觀。在其書中，萊博維奇對[[弗里德里希·尼采的哲學思想|後尼采式哲學]]從[[斯特凡·喬治|斯特凡·喬治]]{{le|喬治學派|George-Kreis|學派}}的激進美學到納粹及{{le|生命政治|Biopolitics}}修辭的演變過程進行了追溯<ref>Nitzan Lebovic, ''The Philosophy of Life and Death Ludwig Klages and the Rise of a Nazi Biopolitics'', Palgrave Macmillan, 2013.</ref>。

「生命」本身作爲整體的存在是生命哲學的核心，且衹能通過由内而外的方式去理解。生命哲學運動可以說是對[[康德義務主義|康德義務主義]]之抽象哲學或實證主義之科學[[還原論|還原論]]的反對。

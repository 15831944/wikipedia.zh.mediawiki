'''費莉西蒂·史莫克'''（{{lang-en|Felicity Smoak}}）是美國[[DC漫畫|DC漫畫]]的角色。首次出現於《The Fury of Firestorm》# 23 (1984年5月)，由{{link-en|格里·康維|Gerry Conway}}及{{link-en|拉菲爾·卡亞娜|Rafael Kayanan}}創作<ref>{{cite web|url= http://comicsalliance.com/tags/felicity-smoak/|title= Gerry Conway Starts Blog Aimed At Fair Compensation For DC Character Creators|first= Matt D.|last= Wilson|date= July 1, 2013|publisher= [[Townsquare_Media|ComicsAlliance]]|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20150906085511/http://comicsalliance.com/gerry-conway-blog-dc-comics-character-fair-compensation-fans/|archivedate= September 6, 2015|deadurl= yes|quote= Gerry Conway, the writer who co-created the character with artist Rafael Kayanan in a 1984 issue of ''Firestorm''.|df= }}</ref> 。她最初是一家電腦軟體公司的經理，反對魯莽的超級英雄[[火風暴_(漫畫)|火風暴]]（Firestorm）。最終成為愛德華·雷蒙德（Edward Raymond）的第二任妻子，並成為羅尼·雷蒙德（Ronnie Raymond）的繼母。<ref name="Sept2012ScreenRant">{{cite web|url=http://screenrant.com/arrow-cw-dc-comics-characters-cameos-guest-stars/|title=11 Confirmed DC Comic Characters Appearing in CW’s ‘Arrow’|last=Stoute|first=Scott|publisher=ScreenRant|date=September 13, 2012|accessdate=March 9, 2014}}</ref>

角色於2012年美國電視劇《[[綠箭俠_(電視劇)|綠箭俠]]》重新改編，於2016年好萊記者民意調查顯示，這個版本的角色被列入了50位喜愛女性角色的第15位<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/lists/50-best-female-characters-entertainment-industry-survey-results-951483/item/juno-juno-50-favorite-female-characters-951484|title=Hollywood's 50 Favorite Female Characters|author=THR Staff|publisher="[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]"|date=December 9, 2016|accessdate=November 30, 2017}}</ref>。[[新52|新52]]中的《[[綠箭俠|綠箭俠]]》(vol. 5) #35也引入相關角色<ref name="Jul2014DailyNews">{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/tv/producers-cw-show-arrow-tapped-write-comic-book-adventures-super-hero-article-1.1854524|title=Producers for CW show 'Arrow' tapped to write super hero's comic book adventures|last=Sacks|first=Ethan|work=New York Daily News|date=July 4, 2014|accessdate=July 4, 2014}}</ref> 。

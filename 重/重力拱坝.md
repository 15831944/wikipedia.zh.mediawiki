'''重力拱坝'''是一类大坝。在水平面为[[拱坝|拱坝]]，向上游迎水面凸出，把水压通过坝体承压传导给左右坝肩基岩。<ref>{{cite book| author=Luis E. Plaza| year=1998| month=| url=| title=Dam Safety: Proceedings of the International Symposium on New Trends| publisher=Taylor and Francis| page= 425| isbn = 90-5410-974-2}}<!--| accessdate=2007-12-29--></ref>重力拱坝比[[重力坝|重力坝]]更薄。<ref>{{cite web | author= | year= | month= | url=http://www.usbr.gov/library/glossary/#archgravitydam | title=Arch Gravity dam | publisher=U.S. Bureau of Reclamation | accessdate=2007-12-27
| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20071225164200/http://www.usbr.gov/library/glossary/| archivedate= 25 December 2007 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>坝体承受的荷载大部分借[[悬臂梁|悬臂梁]]的作用传给坝基；小部分借拱的作用传给两岸。<ref>{{cite web| author=| year=| month=| url=https://www.pbs.org/wgbh/buildingbig/glossary_head.html| title=Glossary onf Engineering Words - Gravity dam| publisher=pbs.org| accessdate=2007-12-28

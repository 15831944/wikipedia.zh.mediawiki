'''[[東加|東加]]'''自1984年起開始參與奧林匹克運動會，此後每年[[夏季奥林匹克运动会|夏季奧運會]]皆有參與。1996年，{{tsl|en|Paea Wolfgramm||Paea Wolfgramm}}於[[1996年夏季奥林匹克运动会|亞特蘭大奧運會]]的拳擊項目上奪得超重量級銀牌，該國成為贏得奧運獎牌的國家中，面積最小的一個。

東加業餘運動協會（Tonga Amateur Sports Association，TASA）曾表示希望能派出一名選手參加[[2010年冬季奥林匹克运动会|2010年冬奧]]的[[無舵雪橇|無舵雪橇]]項目。於是再2008年12月派出{{tsl|en|Fuahea Semi|富赫·塞米}}及Taniela Tufunga等兩名男性運動員至德國受訓，希望能擇一參賽<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20110527150103/http://www.matangitonga.to/article/sports/other/tonga_luge_171108.shtml "Wanted: strong, athletic, Tongan, looking for an icy challenge"], ''{{tsl|en|Matangi Tonga||Matangi Tonga}}'', November 17, 2008</ref><ref>[http://www.smh.com.au/news/sport/even-cooler-runnings-as-tonga-take-up-luge/2008/11/20/1226770644126.html "Even cooler runnings as Tonga take up luge"], ''Sydney Morning Herald'', November 21, 2008</ref><ref>[http://www.matangitonga.to/scripts/artman/exec/view.cgi?archive=9&num=5451.shtml "Two Tongans ready for an icy challenge"]{{dead link|date=April 2017|bot=InternetArchiveBot|fix-attempted=yes}}, ''Matangi Tonga'', October, 2009</ref>。後來塞米取得了代表權，並在德國贊助者的支持之下，以「布魯諾·巴納尼」（Bruno Banani）為名參賽<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20100101215932/http://www.spasifikmag.com/hpsportleisure01/11dec09tongascoolrunnings/ "Feel the rhythm, feel the rhyme, get on up, its Luging time?"], ''Spasifik Magazine'', December 11, 2009</ref><ref>[https://www.theguardian.com/media/2012/jan/31/tongan-luger-banani-marketing-hoax?newsfeed=true "Tongan luger Bruno Banani exposed as a German marketing hoax"], ''The Guardian'', 31 January 2012</ref><ref>[http://en.olympic.cn/news/sports_news/2009-11-27/1929235.html "Tonga's first luger Bruno Banani earned 41st place at Nations Cup in Calgary"], official website of the Chinese Olympic Committee, November 27, 2009</ref><ref>[http://www.spiegel.de/spiegel/vorab/0,1518,811941,00.html "Wie Fuahea Semi zum Rodler Bruno Banani wurde"], ''[[明镜|Der Spiegel]]'', 29 January 2012</ref>。然在資格賽中遭到淘汰，該國未能參與2010年冬奧會<ref>[http://www.radioaustralianews.net.au/stories/201002/2807009.htm?desktop "Tongan athlete narrowly misses out on Winter Olympics"], Australian Broadcasting Corporation, February 1, 2010</ref>。2014年，巴納尼捲土重來，成功參與[[2014年冬季奥林匹克运动会|索契冬奧]]，成為首位參與冬奧會的東加選手<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20140209165840/http://results.nbcolympics.com/luge/event/men/phase=lgm010102/index.html "Men's Luge results, 2014 Olympic Games", February 8, 2014]</ref>。

'''奥斯卡·穆诺兹''' （{{lang-es|'''Oscar Muñoz'''}}，{{bd|1959年|1月|}}）是一位美国企业家。2015年9月8日被任命为美国[[联合航空|联合航空]]的董事长和首席执行官。<ref name="united-20150908">{{cite web | url=http://ir.united.com/phoenix.zhtml?c=83680&p=irol-newsArticle&ID=2086209 | title=United Airlines Names Oscar Munoz Chief Executive Officer | date=September 8, 2015 | access-date=September 8, 2015 | publisher=United Airlines | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151016050925/http://ir.united.com/phoenix.zhtml?c=83680&p=irol-newsArticle&ID=2086209 | archivedate=2015年10月16日 }}</ref> 2010年奥斯卡·穆诺兹是母公司[[联合大陆控股公司|联合大陆控股公司]]的董事会成员，是由[[联合航空|联合航空]]和[[美国大陆航空|美国大陆航空]]合并而成。<ref name="lazare2015">{{Cite news|url = http://www.bizjournals.com/chicago/news/2015/09/08/united-airlines-ceo-jeff-smisek-abruptly-resigns.html|title = United Airlines CEO Jeff Smisek abruptly resigns, as Oscar Muñoz steps into the post|last = Lazare|first = Lewis|date = September 8, 2015|work = [[Chic._Bus._J.|Chicago Business Journal]]|access-date = September 9, 2015}}</ref> 2004年奥斯卡·穆诺兹是[[美国联合航空|美国联合航空]]的董事会成员。<ref name="lazare2015" />

2017年3月奥斯卡·穆诺兹被英国杂志[[PRWeek|PRWeek]]授予「2017年度传播者」称号。<ref name="auto">{{cite web |url=http://time.com/4733467/united-airlines-ceo-oscar-munoz-award/ |title=People Are Not Happy the United Airlines CEO Was Recently Named 'Communicator of the Year' |last=Rhodan |first=Maya |date=April 10, 2017 |website=TIME |publisher=Time Inc. |access-date=April 12, 2017}}</ref> 在2017年[[联合航空3411号班机事件|联合航空3411号班机事件]]之后，其发布的不当言论饱受社会各界广泛指责。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-us-canada-39580112|title=United Airlines boss Oscar Munoz will not resign|date=April 12, 2017|work=BBC News|access-date=April 12, 2017|language=en-GB}}</ref>

== 生平 ==
1959年1月奥斯卡·穆诺兹出生在[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]]的一个墨西哥裔家族，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://beta.companieshouse.gov.uk/officers/M-hPQ-i6dENmjqw_XpqqqY9qkj0/appointments |title=Oscar MUNOZ - Personal Appointments (free information from Companies House) |publisher=Beta.companieshouse.gov.uk |date=September 8, 2015 |accessdate=April 12, 2017}}</ref> 他是家中九个孩子的老大，也是家族中第一位大学毕业生。他本科在[[南加州大学|南加州大学]]，MBA在[[佩珀代因大學|佩珀代因大學]]。<ref name=ForbesBio2015>{{cite web |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/susanadams/2015/09/09/who-is-oscar-munoz-united-continentals-new-ceo/#18b3c6f727e2|title=Who Is Oscar Munoz, United Continental's New CEO?|date = September 15, 2015 |work=Forbes |accessdate=April 13, 2017}}</ref>

他曾为[[可口可乐公司|可口可乐公司]]和[[百事公司|百事公司]]工作过。<ref name="united-20150908" />2001年到2003年担任2012年担任[[AT&T|AT&T]]的首席财务官。<ref>{{Cite news
|url = http://www.bizjournals.com/chicago/news/2015/09/09/heres-why-oscar-munoz-made-the-surprising-move.html|title = Here's why Oscar Munoz made the surprising move from CSX to United Airlines|last = Werley|first = Jensen|date = September 9, 2015|work = [[Chic._Bus._J.|Chicago Business Journal]]|access-date = September 9, 2015}}</ref>[[CSX_Corporation|CSX Corporation]]的首席运营官，2015年担任其董事长。<ref name="lazare2015" /><ref>{{cite web|title=CSX Corp Executive Profile - Oscar Munoz|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=5478847&privcapId=257948|website=Bloomberg|accessdate=April 12, 2017}}</ref>

== 健康 ==
奥斯卡·穆诺兹成为[[联合航空|联合航空]]首席執行官一个月后曾經[[心脏病|心脏病]]发<ref name="WSJ">{{cite news |url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/united-continental-ceo-oscar-munoz-suffers-heart-attack-1445015488 |title=United Continental CEO Oscar Munoz Suffers Heart Attack |date=October 16, 2015 |work=[[Wall_Street_Journal|Wall Street Journal]] |access-date=October 16, 2015}}</ref> 。他的病情并不能马上知晓，在芝加哥的医院休养一段时间后有好转。<ref name="BNO">{{cite news |url=http://bnonews.com/news/index.php/news/2190 |title=United Airlines CEO Oscar Munoz suffers heart attack, rushed to hospital |date=October 16, 2015 |work=[[BNO_News|BNO News]] |access-date=October 16, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151117023805/http://bnonews.com/news/index.php/news/2190 |archive-date=2015-11-17 |dead-url=yes }}</ref> 2016年1月5日，他接受了心脏移植手术，两个月后返回工作。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://fortune.com/united-airlines-ceo-oscar-munoz |title=How United’s Oscar Munoz Bounced Back After a Heart Transplant |last=Truglia |first=Saverio |date=November 18, 2016 |website=Fortune |publisher=Time Inc. |access-date=April 12, 2017}}</ref>

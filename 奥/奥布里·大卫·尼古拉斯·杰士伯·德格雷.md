'''奥布里·大卫·尼古拉斯·杰士伯·德格雷'''（{{lang-en|Aubrey David Nicholas Jasper de Grey}}，{{bd|1963年|4月20日}}<ref>{{cite book |title=Future of Intelligent and Extelligent Health Environment, volume 118 |publisher=IOS Press |editor=Bushko, Renata G. |year=2005 |page=328 |isbn=1-58603-571-1}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=de Grey|first1=A.|last2=Jacobsen|first2=S.D.|title=Dr. Aubrey de Grey: SENS Research Foundation, Chief Science Officer and Co-founder; Rejuvenation Research, Editor-in-Chief|journal=In-Sight|date=8 June 2014|issue=5.A|pages=29–33|url=http://in-sightjournal.com/2014/06/08/dr-aubrey-de-grey-rejuvenation-research-editor-in-chief-methuselah-foundation-co-founder-sens-foundation-co-founder/}}</ref>），是一位英国作家兼[[老年学家|老年学家]]，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://calendar.northeastern.edu/event/biology_colloquia_series_7288#.VTkHrbduvMI|title=Fall 2014 Biology Distinguished Lecturer  – Aubrey de Grey, Ph.D. of the Sens Research Foundation|work=Northeastern University|accessdate=23 April 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://socgen.ucla.edu/events/live-to-120-plus-utopia-or-dystopia/|title=Live to 120 Plus—Utopia or Dystopia? – The UCLA Institute for Society and Genetics|work=ucla.edu|accessdate=23 April 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151222165748/http://socgen.ucla.edu/events/live-to-120-plus-utopia-or-dystopia/|archive-date=2015-12-22|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://video.mit.edu/watch/regenerative-medicine-against-aging-dr-aubrey-de-grey-part-1-mit-club-of-northern-california-8397/|title=Regenerative Medicine Against Aging - Dr. Aubrey de Grey - Part 1 - MIT Club of Northern California|work=MIT Video|accessdate=23 April 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://reuters.com/article/idUSTRE7632ID20110704?irpc=932|title=Who wants to live forever?

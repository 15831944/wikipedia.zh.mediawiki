自2018年3月2日起，美国城市[[德克萨斯州|德克萨斯州]][[奧斯汀|奥斯汀]]市及周边发生共计五起连环[[郵包炸彈|包裹炸弹]]爆炸事件，造成两人死亡，五人受伤。已知的最后爆炸事件发生在3月20日。在3月21日与警方的对峙中，犯罪嫌疑人引爆车内的爆炸装置[[自殺|自杀]]身亡，并造成一名警官负伤。该名犯罪嫌疑人后来被确认为是居住在德州普弗拉热维尔的23岁男子马克·安东尼·康迪特（Mark Anthony Conditt）。<ref name="time_Aust">{{Cite web|url=http://time.com/5207936/austin-texas-bombing-suspect-dead/|title=Austin Serial Bombing Suspect Identified as Mark Anthony Conditt|accessdate=2018-03-22|date=2018-03-21|last=Ducharme|first=Jamie|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180322022353/http://time.com/5207936/austin-texas-bombing-suspect-dead/|archivedate=2018-03-22}}</ref><ref name="npr._Mark">{{Cite web|url=https://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2018/03/21/595604376/mark-anthony-conditt-what-we-know-about-the-austin-bombing-suspect|title=Mark Anthony Conditt: What We Know About The Austin Bomber|date=2018-03-21|last=Chappell|first=Bill|website=NPR.org|access-date=2018-03-22|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180321232309/https://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2018/03/21/595604376/mark-anthony-conditt-what-we-know-about-the-austin-bombing-suspect|archivedate=2018-03-21}}</ref>

== 爆炸事件 ==
2018年3月2日，39岁的安东尼·斯蒂芬·豪斯（Anthony Stephan House）在家中被杀，死因为包裹炸弹。<ref name="abcvictims">{{Cite web|url=http://abcnews.go.com/US/victims-package-bomb-blasts-include-father-rising-star/story?id=53738532|title=Victims of package bomb blasts include father, rising star student|accessdate=2018-03-20|date=2018-03-14|last=Keneally|first=Meghan|publisher=ABC News|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180320030020/http://abcnews.go.com/US/victims-package-bomb-blasts-include-father-rising-star/story?id=53738532|archivedate=2018-03-20}}</ref>

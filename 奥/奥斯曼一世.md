'''奥斯曼一世'''或稱'''奥斯曼加齊'''（[[奥斯曼土耳其语|奥斯曼土耳其语]]：عثمان غازى，[[土耳其语|土耳其语]]：Osmān Ghāzi ; {{bd|?||1323/1324年|catIdx=O}}）<ref name=birth>{{Cite book |last=Kermeli |first=Eugenia  |editor-last=Ágoston |editor-first=Gábor |editor2=Bruce Masters |title=Encyclopedia of the Ottoman Empire |chapter=Osman I |date=2009 |page=444 |quote=Reliable information regarding Osman is scarce. His birth date is unknown and his symbolic significance as the father of the dynasty has encouraged the development of mythic tales regarding the ruler’s life and origins, however, historians agree that before 1300, Osman was simply one among a number of Turkoman tribal leaders operating in the Sakarya region. }}</ref><ref name=death>{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |page=16 |quote=By the time of Osman's death (1323 or 1324)...}}</ref>，他領導[[奥斯曼土耳其人|奥斯曼土耳其人]]部族開創了[[奧斯曼王朝|奧斯曼王朝]]。新生的[[奧斯曼帝國|奧斯曼帝國]]國名由來即是以奠基者奧斯曼一世的名字所命名（剛建立的奧斯曼帝國當時被稱為「[[奧斯曼帝國|奧斯曼侯國]]」）。儘管在奧斯曼的統治時期，初生的奧斯曼侯國只是位於[[安那托利亞|安那托利亞]]地區的一個小國家，但在他去世後的幾個世紀裡，奧斯曼侯國逐步演變成了一個橫跨[[歐洲|歐]][[亞洲|亞]][[非洲|非]]三洲的強大帝國<ref>The Ottoman Empire, 1700-1999, Donald Quataert, page 4, 2005</ref>。奧斯曼帝國國祚直到[[第一次世界大戰|第一次世界大戰]]結束後的1923年才宣告終結。

由於奧斯曼統治時期沒有任何字面資料流傳至今，導致有關他生涯事蹟的歷史資料極為缺乏<ref>{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |page=xii |quote=There is still not one authentic written document known from the time of ʿO<u>s</u>mān, and there are not many from the fourteenth century altogether. }}</ref>。奧斯曼帝國在他去世一百多年後，也就是直到十五世紀才有文字正式記錄奧斯曼的生平<ref>{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |page=93}}</ref>。因此，歷史學者要從講述他豐功偉業的許多故事中區分史實與神話極具挑戰性<ref>{{Cite book |last=Finkel |first=Caroline |title=Osman's Dream: The Story of the Ottoman Empire, 1300-1923 |page=6 |publisher=Basic Books |date=2005 |quote=Modern historians attempt to sift historical fact from the myths contained in the later stories in which the Ottoman chroniclers accounted for the origins of the dynasty |isbn=978-0-465-02396-7}}</ref>。甚至有史學家將奧斯曼一世的生平描述為奧斯曼帝國歷史中的「黑洞」時期，直言後人要釐清這段帝國早年歷史是不可能的<ref>{{Cite book |last=Imber |first=Colin |editor=Elizabeth Zachariadou |title=The Ottoman Emirate (1300-1389) |page=75 |date=1991 |publisher=Crete University Press |place=Rethymnon |quote=Almost all the traditional tales about Osman Gazi are fictitious. The best thing a modern historian can do is to admit frankly that the earliest history of the Ottomans is a black hole. Any attempt to fill this hole will result simply in more fables. }}</ref>。

根據奧斯曼帝國日後的傳統觀點，奧斯曼一世的先祖是來自[[中亞|中亞]]地區[[卡耶|卡耶]]部落的[[烏古斯人|烏古斯突厥人]]，由於[[蒙古帝国|蒙古帝国]]發動的[[蒙古征戰|征服戰役]]而被迫西遷至安納托利亞一帶，以躲避[[蒙古族|蒙古人]]的戰火<ref name=kayi/>。1299年，部落首領奧斯曼宣布在[[比提尼亞|比提尼亞]]地區建立起自己獨立的小國家，雖然在當時僅是十三世紀下半葉出現的眾多[[安納托利亞侯國|安納托利亞侯國]]之一。不過由於位處歐亞交界的邊緣地帶，因此特別易於對衰落中的[[拜占庭帝国|拜占庭帝国]]發動攻擊，蠶食拜占庭已搖搖欲墜的領土，奧斯曼的後代終將追隨他的腳步，完成征服整個拜占庭帝國的事業。

==奧斯曼的名字==

部分學者認為奧斯曼早年的名字帶有土耳其風格，名為阿圖曼或者是阿杜曼，後來才改為相近的[[阿拉伯语人名列表|阿拉伯式名字]]，根據和奧斯曼生活在同個時代的[[希臘人|希臘裔]]歷史學家{{link-en|喬治·帕奇梅雷斯|George Pachymeres}}的記載，奧斯曼的名字被拼寫為阿圖曼（Ατουμάνor）與阿杜曼（Ατμάν），而[[希臘語|希臘語]]在書寫「奧斯曼」人名時通常寫作Οσμάν，此外早期的奧斯曼家族，如奧斯曼的父親與叔叔皆未採用阿拉伯人名，顯示奧斯曼極有可能在他人生的中後期，將原本的名字改為更具威望的[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]人名<ref>{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |page=124 }}</ref>。

==奧斯曼帝國的起源==
{{Details|奧斯曼帝國的崛起}}
[[File:I_Osman.jpg|thumb]]]] 

奧斯曼確切的出生日期尚有爭議，由於可靠史料的稀缺加上奧斯曼人在後來的幾個世紀中不斷為他的事蹟大力吹捧，使奧斯曼的生平被掩蓋於神話的迷霧之中，對他的真實身分和早年生活事實上卻知之甚少<ref name=birth/><ref>{{Cite book |last=Finkel |first=Caroline |title=Osman's Dream: The Story of the Ottoman Empire, 1300-1923 |page=12 |publisher=Basic Books |date=2005 |quote=Beyond the likelihood that the first Ottoman sultan was a historical figure, a Turcoman Muslim marcher-lord of the Byzantine frontier in north-west Anatolia whose father may have been called Ertuğrul, there is little other biographical information about Osman. }}</ref>。依據十六世紀奧斯曼歷史學家凱末爾·帕夏扎德（Kemal paşazade）所寫的日期，奧斯曼最有可能出生於十三世紀中葉的1254年到1255年之間<ref>{{cite book |last=Murphey |first=Rhoads |title=Exploring Ottoman Sovereignty: Tradition, Image, and Practice in the Ottoman Imperial Household, 1400-1800 |publisher=Continuum |place=London |year=2008 |isbn=978-1-84725-220-3 |pages=24}}</ref>。而奧斯曼人的崛起過程，根據奧斯曼帝國的官方說法，為了逃離蒙古大軍西征的鐵蹄，奧斯曼的父親[[埃尔图鲁尔加齐|埃尔图鲁尔加齐]]帶領[[突厥人|突厥人]][[卡耶|卡耶]]部落從[[突厥斯坦|突厥斯坦]]往西遷移進入安納托利亞。隨後埃爾圖魯爾向當地統治者[[魯姆蘇丹國|魯姆蘇丹國]]（[[塞尔柱帝国|塞尔柱帝国]]的分支政權）的[[苏丹_(称谓)|蘇丹]]宣示效忠，並獲得魯姆蘇丹授予他鄰近[[拜占庭帝國|拜占庭帝國]]邊界的[[瑟於特|瑟於特]]小鎮作為封地，且有權在夏季前往瑟於特西南方的高地放牧牲畜<ref name=Shaw-13>Stanford Shaw, ''[https://books.google.com/books?id=E9-YfgVZDBkC&pg=PA13 History of the Ottoman Empire and Modern Turkey]'' (Cambridge: University Press, 1976), vol. 1 {{isbn|9780521291637}}, p. 13</ref>。然而，埃爾圖魯爾和[[塞爾柱王朝|塞爾柱王朝]]間的關係很大程度上是由一個世紀後的奧斯曼宮廷御用史學家所編篡的歷史，因此奧斯曼人的真正起源與出身背景仍然模糊不清<ref>{{New Cambridge History of Islam |last=Fleet |first=Kate |volume=2 |chapter=The rise of the Ottomans |pages=313 |quote=The origins of the Ottomans are obscure. According to legend, largely invented later as part of the process of legitimising Ottoman rule and providing the Ottomans with a suitably august past, it was the Saljuq ruler ʿAlāʾ al-Dīn who bestowed rule on the Ottomans.}}</ref>。

當奧斯曼的父親埃爾圖魯爾大約於 1280年去世之後，奧斯曼繼承了[[貝伊|貝伊]]（相當於首領或酋長之意）的頭銜與地位<ref name=Shaw-13/>。現今對奧斯曼的早年生涯一無所知，只約略知曉他控制了瑟於特周圍的土地，並以瑟於特為基地展開針對[[拜占庭帝國|拜占庭帝國]]的軍事行動。奧斯曼生平第一個有確切紀錄的事件是發生於1301年或1302年的{{link-en|巴菲烏斯戰役|Battle of Bapheus}}，當時奧斯曼率領部隊在戰鬥中擊敗了一支拜占庭的軍隊<ref>{{Cite book|last=Imber |first=Colin |title=The Ottoman Empire, 1300-1650: The Structure of Power |edition=2 |publisher=Palgrave Macmillan |place=New York |date=2009 |page=8 }}
* {{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |page=129 |quote=Of [military undertakings] we know nothing with certainty until the Battle of Bapheus, Osman's triumphant confrontation with a Byzantine force in 1301 (or 1302), which is the first datable incident in his life.}}</ref>。

奧斯曼似乎為了避免與更強大的突厥鄰居發生衝突，而決定實行以攻擊拜占庭人所控制的領土為擴張策略<ref name=Shaw-13/>，奧斯曼首先將領地從原本掌握的[[埃斯基谢希尔|埃斯基谢希尔]]一帶，貧瘠的[[弗里吉亚|北佛里幾亞]]地區擴張到更肥沃的[[比提尼亞|比提尼亞]]平原；根據學者史丹佛·肖爾（Stanford Shaw）的研究，奧斯曼特別針對當地拜占庭貴族所擁有的土地展開征服行動，「其中一些貴族在戰鬥中被擊敗，其他領主則經由以金錢購買城鎮以及政治聯姻等方式和平地被吸收到奧斯曼人的國土中<ref name=Shaw-14>Shaw, ''Ottoman Empire'', p. 14</ref>。」

關於奧斯曼一世年輕時的英勇事蹟與冒險故事是奧斯曼帝國作家歷來最喜愛的創作主題，特別是他在公平競爭下追求[[玛尔可敦|玛尔可敦]]芳心的愛情故事。這則有關奧斯曼的傳奇軼聞在後來的奧斯曼帝國作家筆下，成為如夢似幻般的浪漫史詩。

==奧斯曼之夢==
{{main|奧斯曼之夢}}

奧斯曼與一位名叫{{link-en|謝赫·艾德巴利|Sheikh Edebali}}的當地宗教領袖間有著密切的關係，日後的奧斯曼歷史學者與作家們間廣泛流傳著一則有關兩者的神話故事，故事描述年輕的奧斯曼某次投宿於謝赫·艾德巴利家中時所作的一場夢境<ref>{{Cite book |last=Kermeli |first=Eugenia  |editor-last=Ágoston |editor-first=Gábor |editor2=Bruce Masters |title=Encyclopedia of the Ottoman Empire |chapter=Osman I |date=2009 |page=445 |quote=Apart from these chronicles, there are later sources that begin to establish Osman as a mythic figure. From the 16th century onward a number of dynastic myths are used by Ottoman and Western authors, endowing the founder of the dynasty with more exalted origins. Among these is recounted the famous “dream of Osman” which is supposed to have taken place while he was a guest in the house of a sheikh, Edebali. [...] This highly symbolic narrative should be understood, however, as an example of eschatological mythology required by the subsequent success of the Ottoman emirate to surround the founder of the dynasty with supernatural vision, providential success, and an illustrious genealogy. }}
*{{Cite journal|last=Imber |first=Colin |title=The Ottoman Dynastic Myth |date=1987 |journal=Turcica |pages=7–27 |volume=19 |quote=The attraction of Aşıkpasazade's story was not only that it furnished an episode proving that God had bestowed rulership on the Ottomans, but also that it provided, side by side with the physical descent from Oguz Khan, a spiritual descent. [...] Hence the physical union of Osman with a saint's daughter gave the dynasty a spiritual legitimacy and became, after the 1480s, an integral feature of dynastic mythology.}}
</ref>。這篇史詩故事出現在十五世紀晚期由{{link-en|阿沙克·帕夏札德|Aşıkpaşazade}}編篡的史書中，內容如下：

他（指奧斯曼）看到一輪明月從聖者（指謝赫·艾德巴利）的胸前升起，緩緩沉入自己的胸膛之中。隨即一棵大樹從奧斯曼的肚臍中長出，大樹的樹蔭覆蓋了整個世界。陰影之下有著高聳的山嶺，條條溪水自各山山腳下湧出。有人自淙淙溪水中取水而飲，有人逕取泉水澆灌花草，亦有巧匠引水噴泉。奧斯曼醒來後將夢中所見告訴聖者，聖者回答：「吾子奧斯曼，恭喜你！神已將帝國的寶座賜予給你以及你的後代子孫，我的女兒瑪爾也將成為你的妻子<ref>{{Cite book |last=Finkel |first=Caroline |title=Osman's Dream: The Story of the Ottoman Empire, 1300-1923 |publisher=Basic Books |year=2005 |page=2}}, citing {{Cite book|last=Lindner |first=Rudi P. |title=Nomads and Ottomans in Medieval Anatolia |publisher=Indiana University Press |place=Bloomington |date=1983 |isbn=0-933070-12-8 |page=37}}</ref>。」

這場夢境成為奧斯曼帝國的一則重要奠基神話，奧斯曼之夢不但賦予奧斯曼王室握有神所允諾統治全世界的權力，同時也美化了早年帝國創立者奧斯曼的功績與成就<ref>{{Cite book |last=Finkel |first=Caroline |title=Osman's Dream: The Story of the Ottoman Empire, 1300-1923 |page=2 |quote=First communicated in this form in the later fifteenth century, a century and a half after Osman's death in about 1323, this dream became one of the most resilient founding myths of the empire. |publisher=Basic Books |isbn=978-0-465-02396-7}}</ref>。除此之外，奧斯曼之夢也可視為神聖的契約：就像真主承諾賜給奧斯曼及其後代王權與榮耀一樣，也暗喻著奧斯曼王室亦有責任為其臣民提供繁榮昌盛的義務<ref>{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |pages=132–3 }}</ref>。

==征服與統治==
[[File:Osman_I_area_map.PNG|thumb]]

據學者史丹佛·肖爾的考證，隨著魯姆蘇丹國衰弱與分崩離析，奧斯曼也趁著安納托利亞地區陷入各方勢力割據的機會，趁勢佔領埃斯基謝希爾與卡拉賈希薩爾（Karacahisar）兩座堡壘。不久後，奧斯曼攻下人口稠密的城鎮[[耶尼謝希爾|耶尼謝希爾]]，並將都城遷移至新占領的耶尼謝希爾<ref name=Shaw-14/>。

1302年，當奧斯曼在[[伊兹尼克|尼西亞]]（現稱伊兹尼克）一帶擊潰拜占庭的部隊後，他的力量開始威脅到拜占庭帝國位於安那托利亞領土的安全<ref name="Steven Runciman pg 32">[[Steven_Runciman|Steven Runciman]], ''The Fall of Constantinople 1453'' (Cambridge: University Press, 1969) p. 32</ref>。

隨著奧斯曼的勢力越來越強大，拜占庭人紛紛逃離原本居住的安納托利亞村莊。雖然拜占庭的領主們試圖遏制奧斯曼人的擴張，但他們所做的微弱抵抗最終仍無法逃離遭到併吞的命運。在接下來的日子裡，奧斯曼一世主要朝兩個方向擴展他的勢力範圍，其一是向北沿著[[萨卡里亚河|萨卡里亚河]]併吞沿途的村莊與土地，另一目標則是往西南方[[马尔马拉海|馬摩拉海]]的海岸線推進，1308年，奧斯曼的軍隊還參與了[[艾登王朝|艾登侯國]]征服[[以弗所|以弗所]]的戰役，以弗所是拜占庭帝國在[[爱琴海|爱琴海]]東部沿岸所掌握的最後一個根據地，該城淪陷後拜占庭在亞洲地區的勢力更加衰弱<ref name="Steven Runciman pg 32"/>。

奧斯曼生涯的最後一場戰役，是在1317年展開攻打重鎮[[布尔萨|布尔萨]]的軍事行動，經過嚴酷而漫長的圍城，拜占庭人在1326年放棄抵抗，宣布投降<ref>Runciman, ''The Fall of Constantinople'', p. 33</ref>。雖然奧斯曼因為身染重病而沒有親身參與這次的戰鬥，但對於奧斯曼帝國來說，成功[[布爾薩圍城戰|征服布爾薩]]對帝國的影響深遠，因為這座城市不僅是拜占庭帝國在安那托利亞地區的重要據點，除此之外，奧斯曼的兒子[[奥尔汗一世|奥尔汗一世]]在攻下這座城市後，更決定將帝國的首都遷移到布爾薩，並將他已逝的父親奧斯曼隆重地安葬於布爾薩城中的[[清真寺|清真寺]]內。

==奧斯曼之劍==
{{main|奧斯曼之劍}}

奧斯曼之劍是在奧斯曼帝國[[奥斯曼帝国苏丹列表|歷任蘇丹]]於加冕儀式中所需使用的一把重要的[[尚方寶劍|國家之劍]] <ref>[[Frederick_William_Hasluck|Frederick William Hasluck]], [First published 1929], "XLVI. The Girding of the Sultan", in [[Margaret_Hasluck|Margaret Hasluck]], ''Christianity and Islam Under the Sultans II'', pp. 604–622. {{ISBN|978-1-4067-5887-0}}</ref>。授劍的傳統源自奧斯曼的岳父謝赫·艾德巴利幫他佩帶上這把伊斯蘭之劍後，往後的蘇丹皆延續這項授劍的即位儀式<ref>Frank R. C. Bagley, ''The Last Great Muslim Empires'' (Leiden: Brill, 1969), p. 2 {{ISBN|978-90-04-02104-4}}</ref>。佩戴奧斯曼之劍是極為重要的登基典禮，大多於新任蘇丹繼承皇位的兩週內舉行。新蘇丹首先會從首都[[伊斯坦堡|伊斯坦堡]]的[[托卡比皇宮|托普卡匹皇宮]]出發，並經由水路橫越[[金角湾|金角湾]]抵達[[艾郁普蘇丹清真寺|艾郁普蘇丹清真寺]]舉行加冕儀式。奧斯曼之劍也是蘇丹的信物，這把劍象徵著：新任的奧斯曼帝國蘇丹亦是一名手持戰劍的戰士。奧斯曼之劍在登基典禮中由一名來自[[梅夫拉維教團|梅夫拉維教團]]的[[德爾維希|托缽僧]] - [[科尼亞_(土耳其)|科尼亞]]的[[谢里夫|谢里夫]]（Sharif of Konya）負責佩戴在新蘇丹的身上，科尼亞的[[蘇非主義|蘇菲派]]教徒保有這項幫新任蘇丹授劍的特權直至奧斯曼帝國滅亡<ref>{{cite news |date=1876-09-18 |title=Girding on the Sword of Osman |newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |page=2 |issn=0362-4331 |url=https://timesmachine.nytimes.com/timesmachine/1876/09/18/94655470.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]] |accessdate=2009-04-19}}</ref>。

==家庭==
[[File:Türbe_of_Osman_I,_Bursa.jpg|thumb]]

由於記錄奧斯曼生平的史料極為缺乏，導致現今對奧斯曼的家庭關係所知甚少。十五世紀時，部分奧斯曼帝國的作家聲稱奧斯曼家族是來自[[烏古斯人|烏古斯人]]卡耶部落的後裔，這項說法後來成為奧斯曼官方認可的奧斯曼王室族譜，並廣受{{link-en|土耳其民族主義|Turkish nationalism}}者與歷史學家{{link-en|穆罕默德·福阿德·科普魯律|Mehmet Fuat Köprülü}}的支持<ref>
{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |pages=10, 37 }}</ref>。然而，現存最早的奧斯曼家譜中被未出現可追溯至卡耶部族的相關記載。因此，許多研究奧斯曼帝國歷史的學者皆認為奧斯曼家族來自卡耶部落的說法是日後所捏造的族譜，目的是為了鞏固[[奧斯曼皇室|奧斯曼皇室]]的正統性，讓奧斯曼王朝統治者得以宣稱比其他同位於安納托利亞的突厥競爭對手擁有更高貴的血統<ref name=kayi>
{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |page=122 |quote=That they hailed from the Kayı branch of the Oğuz confederacy seems to be a creative "rediscovery" in the genealogical concoction of the fifteenth century. It is missing not only in Ahmedi but also, and more importantly, in the Yahşi Fakih-Aşıkpaşazade narrative, which gives its own version of an elaborate genealogical family tree going back to Noah. If there was a particularly significant claim to Kayı lineage, it is hard to imagine that Yahşi Fakih would not have heard of it. }} 
* {{Cite book |last=Lowry |first=Heath |title=The Nature of the Early Ottoman State |publisher=SUNY Press |date=2003 |page=78 |isbn=0-7914-5636-6 |quote=Based on these charters, all of which were drawn up between 1324 and 1360 (almost one hundred fifty years prior to the emergence of the Ottoman dynastic myth identifying them as members of the Kayı branch of the Oguz federation of Turkish tribes), we may posit that...}}
* {{Cite book |last=Lindner |first=Rudi Paul |title=Nomads and Ottomans in Medieval Anatolia |publisher=Indiana University Press |date=1983 |page=10 |quote=In fact, no matter how one were to try, the sources simply do not allow the recovery of a family tree linking the antecedents of Osman to the Kayı of the Oğuz tribe. }}</ref>。

由於奧斯曼帝國早年的歷史資料往往有多種不同說法，讓歷史學界很難從奧斯曼人傳述的奧斯曼一世諸多事績中，區分出哪些是史實而那些部分則僅是神話傳說<ref name=Kafadar105>{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |page=105 }}
*{{Cite book |last=Finkel |first=Caroline |title=Osman's Dream: The Story of the Ottoman Empire, 1300-1923 |page=6 |publisher=Basic Books |quote=Modern historians attempt to sift historical fact from the myths contained in the later stories in which the Ottoman chroniclers accounted for the origins of the dynasty |isbn=978-0-465-02396-7}}</ref>。根據其中一則軼聞，奧斯曼有一位名叫唐達爾（Dündar）的叔叔，奧斯曼曾在早年擔任首領時與他的叔叔發生過爭吵。當時奧斯曼希望攻打信仰基督教的[[比萊吉克|比萊吉克]]當地領主，但是唐達爾卻反對姪子的決定，唐達爾認為他們已有夠多的敵人需要應付，不必再另外與基督徒結仇。奧斯曼將唐達爾的異議視為對自己首領權威的挑戰，因此用弓箭射殺了自己的叔叔<ref>{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |page=105 }}</ref>。這場事件並未被記錄於日後眾多的奧斯曼史書中，假如這則故事為真，意味著奧斯曼殺害他叔叔的事情可能被刻意地掩蓋，以避免奧斯曼帝國的創始者背負謀殺家庭成員的污名。這則故事也可能象徵著奧斯曼人與他們基督徒鄰居關係產生重大變化，奧斯曼人從過往與基督徒和平相處的政策，開始轉為更加積極的征服與佔領手段<ref>{{Cite book |last=Kafadar |first=Cemal |title=Between Two Worlds: The Construction of the Ottoman State |date=1995 |pages=107–8 }}</ref>。

库克曾在青少年时期学过芭蕾舞与体操。8岁起，她便在家乡的课后戏剧活动{{Internal link helper/en|奥尔德姆戏剧研习会|Oldham Theatre Workshop}}中参与表演。在很长的一段时间内，她都只在研习会中作为群众演员出演，直到17岁时，她才在{{Internal link helper/en|奥尔德姆预科学校|Oldham Sixth Form College}}出品的《[[西城故事|西区故事]]》中出演了女主角玛利亚。而这之后，她才第一次也是最后一次在奥尔德姆戏剧研习会改编的《[[灰姑娘|灰姑娘]]》音乐剧版本中担任了主角。<ref name="interview" /><ref name="chronicle" />

在14岁时，库克在[[曼彻斯特|曼彻斯特]]结识了她的第一位经纪人，并在此后接拍了一系列宣传广告。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://voices.suntimes.com/arts-entertainment/the-daily-sizzle/the-quiet-ones-star-olivia-cooke-i-have-no-dignity-anymore-after-this-movie/#.U2e_FnrD8m8|title='The Quiet Ones' star Olivia Cooke: 'I have no dignity anymore after this movie.'|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=|date=2014-04-23|last=Pearlman|first=Cindy|publisher=|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141218065158/http://voices.suntimes.com/arts-entertainment/the-daily-sizzle/the-quiet-ones-star-olivia-cooke-i-have-no-dignity-anymore-after-this-movie/#.U2e_FnrD8m8|archivedate=2014-12-18|website=[[芝加哥太阳报|Chicago Sun-Times]]|deadurl=yes}}</ref>2012年，她出现在[[1世代|单向乐队]]的“Autumn Term”巡演音乐录影带中，饰演一名骑在[[哈利·斯泰爾斯|哈里·斯泰尔斯]]肩上的学生。<ref name="teenvogue">{{cite web|url=http://www.teenvogue.com/entertainment/movies/2014-03/olivia-cooke/?slide=1|title=Meet Olivia Cooke, the Scary-Good Actress Set to Take Hollywood by Storm|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=|date=|last=Rotunno|first=Anthony|publisher=|language=en|website=Teen Vogue|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141205021523/http://www.teenvogue.com/entertainment/movies/2014-03/olivia-cooke/?slide=1|archivedate=2014-12-05}}</ref>在参加这个角色的试镜时，库克通过表演吐談而吸引了制作人的注意。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.toofab.com/2014/04/25/olivia-cooke-bates-motel-the-quiet-ones-one-direction/|title='The Quiet Ones' Star Talks Ouiji Boards, 'Bates Motel' & Riding Harry Styles!|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=|date=2014-04-25|last=Particelli|first=Brian|publisher=|language=en|website=TooFab|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170902133800/http://toofab.com/2014/04/25/olivia-cooke-bates-motel-the-quiet-ones-one-direction/|archivedate=2017-09-02}}</ref>虽然库克的经纪人以她已经开始演艺工作为由，对她去戏剧学院进修多加劝阻，然而库克还是执意申请[[皇家戏剧艺术学院|皇家戏剧艺术学院]]。最终她进入到了最后一轮面试，然而最终并未被录取。<ref name="interview" />

== 职业生涯 ==

=== 2012年-2013年：演艺生涯的开始 ===
在奥尔德姆戏剧研习会表演后，在库克经纪公司隔壁工作的选角导演比佛利·基奥主动为她争取了多个电视剧角色。库克在2012年[[英国广播公司|BBC]]出品的两集迷你剧《{{Internal link helper/en|黑暗救赎|Blackout (TV series)}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://chrisecclestonbulletin.blogspot.com/2011/05/fuse.html?m=1|title='Blackout'|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=|date=|publisher=Art Thou Beguil'd Now? 2012年，在拍摄完《死寂亡灵》后，库克结识了她在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的经纪人。<ref name="collider" />在阅读了[[A+E電視網|A&E电视台]]出品的《[[驚魂記|惊魂记]]》前传电视剧《[[貝茲旅館|贝茨旅馆]]》的角色设定后，她向电视台寄送了试镜艾玛·德寇蒂一角的录影带。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://zap2it.com/blogs/bates_motels_olivia_cooke_im_not_sexy_so_ill_go_for_emma-2013-04|title='Bates Motel's' Olivia Cooke: 'I'm not sexy, so I'll go for Emma'|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=|date=2013-04-08|last=O'Hare|first=Kate|publisher=Zap2It|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150623061311/http://www.zap2it.com/blogs/bates_motels_olivia_cooke_im_not_sexy_so_ill_go_for_emma-2013-04|archivedate=2015-06-23}}</ref>三周后，库克得到了艾玛一角，这也是她飾演的第一个美国人角色。<ref name="collider">{{cite web|url=http://www.collider.com/nicola-peltz-olivia-cooke-bates-motel-interview/|title=Nicola Peltz and Olivia Cooke Talk BATES MOTEL, Their Characters, Their Familiarity with PSYCHO, and More|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=|date=|last=Radish|first=Christina|publisher=Collider.com|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908063947/http://collider.com/nicola-peltz-olivia-cooke-bates-motel-interview/|archivedate=2017-09-08}}</ref>最初制作人将艾玛设定为[[曼彻斯特|曼彻斯特人]]，来弥补库克的口音问题，库克对此十分沮丧。但后来在精通美式口音，同为英国演员的[[費迪·夏摩亞|弗雷迪·海默]]的帮助下，库克成功克服了口音问题，并从此常被人误认做美国演员。<ref name="interview" /><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UqBFjXN9-mY|title=Bates Motel: Olivia Cooke Is Not American|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=|date=2014-05-07|publisher=A&E, YouTube|language=en|access-date=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160309154814/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UqBFjXN9-mY|archivedate=2016-03-09}}</ref>库克也在艾玛虚构的[[博客|博客]]上发布了数段剧集相关短片。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.aetv.com/bates-motel/emmas-blog/|title=Emma's Blog: My Life in Fast-Forward|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=|date=|publisher=AETV.com|language=en|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170128091835/http://www.aetv.com/bates-motel/emmas-blog/|archivedate=2017-01-28}}</ref>

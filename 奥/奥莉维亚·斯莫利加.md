'''奥莉维亚·斯莫利加'''（{{lang-en|Olivia Smoliga}}，{{bd|1994年|10月12日||}}）生于伊利诺伊州格伦维尤，是一名美国女子游泳运动员，主攻仰泳。她的父母1991年从[[波兰|波兰]]移民至美国<ref>{{cite web|url=http://chronicleillinois.com/news/cook-county-news/hard-work-pays-off-olympics-qualifier-smoliga/|title=Hard work pays off for Olympics qualifier Smoliga|publisher=Chronicle Media|date=2016-07-19|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>。奥莉维亚于2013年毕业于南格伦布鲁克高中，同年就读佐治亚大学<ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2013-06-15/sports/ct-spt-0616-prep-athlete-of-year-20130615_1_preps-plus-athlete-olivia-smoliga-georgia-bound-smoliga|title=Tribune/WGN-Ch. 9 2012-13 Athlete of the Year {{!}} Glenbrook South's Olivia Smoliga|publisher=Chicago Tribune|date=2013-06-15|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>。她有一个比她小五岁的弟弟马修，从事曲棍球项目<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/highschool/ct-spt-1211-prep-athlete-of-month-smoliga-20111210-story.html|title=Tribune/WGN-Ch. 9 Preps Plus Athlete of the Month {{!}} Glenbrook South's Olivia Smoliga: High water marks|publisher=Chicago Tribune|date=2011-12-10|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>，马修和她毕业于同一所高中。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.chicagotribune.com/suburbs/glenview/news/ct-nhs-niles-olympic-party-tl-0811-20160805-story.html|title=Pep rally for Glenbrook South grad in Olympics about showing 'support and love'|publisher=Chicago Tribune|date=2016-08-05|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>

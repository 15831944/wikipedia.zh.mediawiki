'''[[澳大拉西亞|澳大拉西亞]]'''是在[[1908年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1908年]]和[[1912年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1912年夏季奥林匹克运动会]]中由[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]和[[紐西蘭自治領|紐西蘭自治領]]的联合组队。[[第一次世界大战|第一次世界大战]]之后，[[1920年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1920年]]开始，两个国家开始独立参赛。

== List of medalists ==
The Australasia team won a total of twelve medals in the two Olympiads in which they competed, mostly in swimming. One New Zealander won a medal in 1908 ([[Harry_Kerr_(racewalker)|Harry Kerr]] a bronze in athletics<ref>{{cite web |title=Harry Kerr |url=http://www.olympic.org.nz/athletes/harry-kerr/ |publisher=[[New_Zealand_Olympic_Committee|New Zealand Olympic Committee]] |accessdate=20 August 2016}}</ref>), and two New Zealanders ([[Malcolm_Champion|Malcolm Champion]] a gold in swimming,<ref>{{cite web |title=Malcolm Champion |url=http://www.olympic.org.nz/athletes/malcolm-champion/ |publisher=[[New_Zealand_Olympic_Committee|New Zealand Olympic Committee]] |accessdate=20 August 2016}}</ref> [[Anthony_Wilding|Anthony Wilding]] a bronze in tennis<ref>{{cite web |title=Anthony Wilding |url=http://www.olympic.org.nz/athletes/anthony-wilding/ |publisher=[[New_Zealand_Olympic_Committee|New Zealand Olympic Committee]] |accessdate=20 August 2016}}</ref>) won medals in 1912; all other medalists for Australasia were Australians.

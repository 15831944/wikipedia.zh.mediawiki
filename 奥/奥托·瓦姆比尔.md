'''奥托·-{zh-hans:弗雷德里克;zh-tw:佛烈德瑞克;zh-hant:弗雷德里克;zh-hk:費特烈;}-·瓦姆比尔'''（{{lang-en|Otto Frederick Warmbier}}, {{bd|1994年|12月12日|2017年|6月19日|catIdx=W}}）是一位美国[[弗吉尼亚大学|弗吉尼亚大学]]大学三年级在校学生。2016年1月，他随团访问[[朝鲜|朝鲜]]，因被指控窃取[[羊角岛国际饭店|羊角岛国际饭店]]大堂的政治宣传标语被判劳教15年。此后，美国一直利用外交手段寻求瓦姆比尔获释<ref name=hotelvideo /><ref name=NYT /><ref name="WaPo_death"/>。

入狱后两个月，瓦姆比尔患上严重的{{le|神经障碍|Neurological disorder}}，被囚约18个月后于2017年6月获释。朝鲜当局指[[肉毒杆菌中毒|肉毒杆菌中毒]]和[[安眠药|安眠药]]造成瓦姆比尔昏迷，但美国医师并未发现肉毒杆菌的迹象<ref name=ABC>{{cite web|url=http://abcnews.go.com/Politics/north-korea-releases-jailed-american-student/story?id=48005214 |title=Coma-stricken student released from North Korea arrives back in US |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170620151237/http://abcnews.go.com/Politics/north-korea-releases-jailed-american-student/story?id=48005214 |archivedate=2017-06-20 |website=ABC News |date=2017-06-12}}</ref>。6月13日，瓦姆比尔抵达俄亥俄州[[辛辛那提|辛辛那提]]，被火速送往{{le|辛辛那提大学医疗中心|University of Cincinnati Academic Health Center}}进行评估和治疗。

2017年6月19日，回国第六天，瓦姆比尔逝世<ref name="wapo61917">{{cite news|last=Svrluga|first=Susan|title=Otto Warmbier dies days after release from North Korean detainment|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/grade-point/wp/2017/06/19/otto-warmbier-dies-days-after-release-from-north-korean-detainmen|access-date=2017-06-19|work=The Washington Post|date=2017-06-19}}{{subscription required}}</ref>。美国官员认为他的死亡是由于朝鲜方面的不人道行为造成的，并对这一行为进行了强烈的谴责<ref name=death>{{cite web|url=http://www.washingtonexaminer.com/john-mccain-otto-warmbier-murdered-by-the-kim-jong-un-regime/article/2626448 |title=John McCain: Otto Warmbier 'murdered by the Kim Jong-un regime |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170620014107/http://www.washingtonexaminer.com/john-mccain-otto-warmbier-murdered-by-the-kim-jong-un-regime/article/2626448 |archivedate=2017-06-20 |website=Washington Examiner |date=2017-06-19}}</ref>。他也是自1996年来朝鲜扣押的16位美国公民的其中一位，截至2017年10月仍有三人在押<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/06/14/world/asia/north-korea-otto-warmbier-.html |title=Otto Warmbier Got an Extra Dose of Brutality From North Korea. The Mystery Is Why |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170620112933/https://www.nytimes.com/2017/06/14/world/asia/north-korea-otto-warmbier-.html |archivedate=2017-06-20 |website=The New York Times |date=2017-06-14}}</ref>。

医疗团队表示，大脑扫描结果显示，瓦姆比尔脑组织大面积受损，与[[循环系统|心肺]]问题一道导致{{le|脑组织缺氧|Cerebral hypoxia}}。医生并不知道心脏骤停的原因，推测可能是由呼吸停止引起的，而医院的神经功能重症监护专家表示没有肉毒杆菌中毒的证据。他们表示医生没有从身体上找到虐待或酷刑的证据，扫描瓦姆比尔脖颈和头部结果，表明大脑外部损伤是正常的<ref name="nbc61517" /><ref name="BBC08028">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-40308028 |title=Otto Warmbier: How did North Korea holiday end in jail, and a coma? |publisher=BBC News |date=2017-06-18 |access-date=2017-06-22 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621015901/http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-40308028 |archivedate=2017-06-21 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Berlinger |first1=Joshua |title=Ex-North Korea detainee Otto Warmbier has 'severe neurological injury' |url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/06/15/politics/otto-warmbier-north-korea/index.html |access-date=2017-06-22 |publisher=CNN |date=2017-06-15 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622060227/http://www.cnn.com/2017/06/15/politics/otto-warmbier-north-korea/index.html |archivedate=2017-06-22 }}</ref><ref name="wapo61517">{{cite news|last1=Svrluga |first1=Susan |title=Otto Warmbier has extensive loss of brain tissue, no obvious signs of trauma, doctors say |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/grade-point/wp/2017/06/15/otto-warmbier-has-extensive-loss-of-brain-tissue-no-obvious-signs-of-trauma-doctors-say/ |access-date=2017-06-15 |work=The Washington Post |date=2017-06-15 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170616043429/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/grade-point/wp/2017/06/15/otto-warmbier-has-extensive-loss-of-brain-tissue-no-obvious-signs-of-trauma-doctors-say/ |archivedate=2017-06-16 }}{{subscription required}}</ref>。

6月15日，瓦姆比尔父亲举行新闻发布会。会上，有记者问到神经损伤是否由袭击引起，他没有回答，表示会交给医生来推断。他表示，他们完全不相信朝鲜的说明<ref name="pressconference">{{cite news|title=Parents of American student released from North Korea hold news conference |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pp5qQVoPsOE |access-date=2017-06-20 |publisher=YouTube |date=2017-06-15 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170619232912/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pp5qQVoPsOE |archivedate=2017-06-19 }}</ref>，并对朝鲜对待儿子的态度表示愤慨：“但凡文明的国家都不会隐瞒他的状况，不让他享受一流的医疗服务<ref name="nbc61517" />。”

朝鲜自称是“谣传”的“最大受害者”，表示他们对他的待遇是“人道的”。发言人补充道：“尽管我们完全没有理由可怜一位敌国的罪犯，但鉴于他的健康状况恶化，我们基于人道主义向他提供医疗护理，直到他回到美国<ref>{{cite web|last1=News|first1=ABC|title=North Korea says it's 'biggest victim' in US student's death|url=http://abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/north-korea-denies-torturing-american-detainee-otto-warmbier-48229663|website=ABC News|accessdate=2017-06-23|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170623132733/http://abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/north-korea-denies-torturing-american-detainee-otto-warmbier-48229663|archivedate=2017-06-23|date=2017-06-23}}</ref>。”

==去世==
{{quote box||align=right|width=25em|bgcolor = LightCyan|quote=无数的男人和女人们死在朝鲜的受伤，但唯独是奥托·瓦姆比尔案例最为触动美国人的心灵。|source= 美国驻联合国大使[[妮基·黑利|妮基·黑利]]<ref name=death />}}

2017年6月19日下午2点20分，瓦姆比尔在医院逝世，得年22岁<ref name="wapo61917" /><ref>{{cite news|first=Sheryl Gay |last=Stolberg |title=Otto Warmbier, American Student Released From North Korea, Dies |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/06/19/us/otto-warmbier-north-korea-dies.html |newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=2017-06-19 |access-date=2017-06-22 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170619213420/https://www.nytimes.com/2017/06/19/us/otto-warmbier-north-korea-dies.html |archivedate=2017-06-19 }}</ref>。他的家人发表声明表达悲恸，同时感谢医院工作人员的付出<ref name="WaPo_death">{{cite news|last1=Svrluga |first1=Susan |title=Otto Warmbier dies days after release from North Korean detainment |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/grade-point/wp/2017/06/19/otto-warmbier-dies-days-after-release-from-north-korean-detainment/ |access-date=2017-06-19 |work=The Washington Post |date=2017-06-19 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170619225726/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/grade-point/wp/2017/06/19/otto-warmbier-dies-days-after-release-from-north-korean-detainment/ |archivedate=2017-06-19 }}{{subscription required}}</ref>。总统特朗普也发表声明：“对于一对父母来说，没有比失去一位正值人生黄金时期的孩子更让人伤心，我们和奥托的家人、朋友以及所有爱他的人同在，一起祈祷<ref name="wapo61917" /><ref>{{cite news|last1=Lockie |first1=Alex |title=Trump responds to 'tragic' death of Otto Warmbier, saying North Korea is a 'brutal regime' and 'we'll be able to handle it' |url=http://www.businessinsider.com/trump-statement-otto-warmbier-2017-6 |access-date=2017-06-19 |work=Business Insider |date=2017-06-19 |language=en |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170620001757/http://www.businessinsider.com/trump-statement-otto-warmbier-2017-6 |archivedate=2017-06-20 }}</ref>。”

出于对瓦姆比尔家人的敬重，尸检没有进行，只进行了一次尸体外部检查<ref name="autopsy">{{cite news|last1=Grinberg |first1=Emanuella |title=Otto Warmbier's family declines autopsy |url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/06/20/health/otto-warmbier-autopsy/index.html |accessdate=2017-06-21 |publisher=CNN |date=2017-06-20 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621014101/http://www.cnn.com/2017/06/20/health/otto-warmbier-autopsy/index.html |archivedate=2017-06-21 }}</ref>。医生推测，死因可能是[[血栓|血栓]]、[[肺炎|肺炎]]、[[败血症|败血症]]或[[肾功能衰竭|肾功能衰竭]]。如果瓦姆比尔是因为肉毒杆菌中毒瘫痪的话，安眠药可能导致瓦姆比尔停止呼吸<ref>{{cite news|last1=Fox |first1=Maggie |title=What killed Otto Warmbier? |url=http://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/what-killed-otto-warmbier-maybe-infection-or-blood-clot-n774421 |accessdate=2017-06-21 |publisher=NBC News |date=2017-06-20 |language=en |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621020523/http://www.nbcnews.com/health/health-news/what-killed-otto-warmbier-maybe-infection-or-blood-clot-n774421 |archivedate=2017-06-21 }}</ref>。虽然没有找到肉毒杆菌中毒的证据，但考虑到瓦姆比尔回到美国前在朝鲜待的时间很长，不能排除肉毒杆菌毒素<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/grade-point/wp/2017/06/20/what-happened-to-otto-warmbier-when-the-unthinkable-is-unknowable/ |title=What happened to Otto Warmbier? When the unthinkable is unknowable |website=Washington Post |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621191658/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/grade-point/wp/2017/06/20/what-happened-to-otto-warmbier-when-the-unthinkable-is-unknowable/ |archivedate=2017-06-21 }}</ref>。

他去世后，组织旅行的青年先锋旅行社宣布不再接受美国公民旅行<ref>{{cite web|title=The tour company that took Otto Warmbier to North Korea halts trips for Americans|url=http://www.abc2news.com/news/national/the-tour-company-that-took-otto-warmbier-to-north-korea-halts-trips-for-americans|website=ABC News|date=2017-06-20}}</ref>。美方官员指出，国务院在网站上强烈警告美国人不要去朝鲜<ref>{{cite web|last1=Stolberg|first1=Sheryl Gay|script-title=en:Otto Warmbier Was ‘Brutalized and Terrorized’ in North Korea, Father Says|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/06/15/us/otto-warmbier-north-korea-ohio-student-home.html?_r=0|website=The New York Times|accessdate=2017-07-19|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170619025557/https://www.nytimes.com/2017/06/15/us/otto-warmbier-north-korea-ohio-student-home.html?_r=0|archivedate=2017-07-19|date=2017-06-15}}</ref>。2017年7月，据报道美国政府即将禁止美国公民前往朝鲜<ref>{{cite web|title=US to ban its citizens' travel to North Korea: US official|url=http://www.cnbc.com/2017/07/21/us-to-ban-its-citizens-travel-to-north-korea-us-official.html|website=CNBC|date=2017-07-21}}</ref>。

瓦姆比尔的葬礼于6月22日在怀俄明高中举行，2500多人前往吊唁。他葬于俄亥俄州{{le|格伦代尔 (俄亥俄州)|Glendale, Ohio|格伦代尔}}的橡树山公墓（{{lang|en|Oak Hill Cemetery}}）<ref>{{cite web|title=Thousands pay tribute to Otto Warmbier at Ohio funeral|url=http://www.nbcnews.com/news/north-korea/funeral-held-otto-warmbier-former-north-korean-prisoner-ohio-alma-n775421|website=NBC News|accessdate=2017-06-22|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622173609/http://www.nbcnews.com/news/north-korea/funeral-held-otto-warmbier-former-north-korean-prisoner-ohio-alma-n775421|archivedate=2017-06-22|date=2017-06-22}}</ref>。学生们沿着高中到公墓的三公里游行路线，给每一棵树和每一根柱子绑上丝带<ref>{{cite news|last1=Chiles |first1=Richard |title=Wyoming adorned with Otto Warmbier tributes ahead of burial |url=http://www.wlwt.com/article/wyoming-adorns-city-with-otto-warmbier-tributes-ahead-of-burial/10203510 |accessdate=2017-06-29 |publisher=WLWT |date=2017-06-21 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622180636/http://www.wlwt.com/article/wyoming-adorns-city-with-otto-warmbier-tributes-ahead-of-burial/10203510 |archivedate=2017-06-22 }}</ref>。

==影响==

2017年10月24日，[[美国众议院|美国众议院]]通过一项以瓦姆比尔命名的法案《奥托·瓦姆比尔朝鲜核制裁法案》（Otto Warmbier North Korea Nuclear Sanction Act），加強對朝鲜的制裁，向任何与朝鲜有贸易或投资合作的個人或實體實施制裁<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.voachinese.com/a/news-house-passes-warmbier-nk-sanction-act-20171025/4086341.html |title=美众院通过瓦姆比尔法案 扩大对朝金融制裁 |publisher=美國之音 |date=2017-10-26}}</ref>。

2017年11月21日，时任美国总统的唐纳德·特朗普在内阁会议前向媒体表示<ref>{{cite web|title=特朗普总统在内阁会议前的讲话|url=https://china.usembassy-china.org.cn/zh/remarks-by-president-trump-before-cabinet-meeting/|publisher=美国驻华大使馆和领事馆|accessdate=2017-11-21}}</ref>，美国将把朝鲜认定为一个支持恐怖主义的国家。在讲话中他表示，“我们今天在采取这项行动时，心系奥托·瓦姆比尔（Otto Warmbier）这位优秀的青年，以及受到北韩镇压的残酷迫害的数不胜数的其他人士”。

國際奧委會會為所有所屬成員國編配的3個英文字母的國家或地區代碼，每個編碼只會代表一個國家或地區，由於歷史和政治等原因，有部分國家或地區的成員會變更國家名稱及編碼，相关国家的奖牌数并不累加，一对一的变更除外（如[[荷兰|荷兰]]的國家奧委會編碼由HOL改为NED，或是如锡兰改名为[[斯里兰卡|斯里兰卡]]）。

以斜體表示的國家實體為已不存在。國家或地區奧委會的總數並沒有與他們的前任和繼任者的總數。

== 奧委會獎牌榜 ==
以下列表統計出'''历届[[奥林匹克运动会|奥林匹克运动会]]總奖牌榜，包括1896年至2018年历届[[夏季奥林匹克运动会|夏季奥林匹克运动会]]和[[冬季奥林匹克运动会|冬季奥林匹克运动会]]頒發的所有獎牌'''。本表不包括[[1906年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1906年夏季奥林匹克运动会]]，此届奥运会不被[[国际奥委会|国际奥委会]]承认为正式奥运会。另外獎牌榜只計入奧運會正式比賽項目頒發的獎牌，奧運會表演項目不會被計算入獎牌榜內。

列表排列順序根據國家英文名稱順序依次列出，會列出151個國家或地區在夏季及冬季奧運會曾經獲得的獎牌，包括金牌，銀牌及銅牌，列表不包括[[遭剥夺奥运会奖牌列表|賽後被取消]]的獎牌。獎牌榜的變動包含2015年因為禁藥醜聞所造成，會顯示於本表。獎牌數據截止於2017年12月22日以及[[2018年冬季奧林匹克運動會|2018年冬季奧運]]的獎牌變動，[[2006年冬季奧林匹克運動會|2006年冬季奧林匹克運動會]]以前（含）所獲得的獎牌幾乎不會再變動。

''斜體''為已不存在的代表團。

The sum total of gold, silver and bronze medals are not equal for the following reasons:
* Some combat sports (such as [[Boxing_at_the_Summer_Olympics|boxing]], [[Judo_at_the_Summer_Olympics|judo]], [[Taekwondo_at_the_Summer_Olympics|taekwondo]] and [[Wrestling_at_the_Summer_Olympics|wrestling]]) award two bronze medals per competition.
* Some tied performances have resulted in multiple medals of the same colour being awarded for an event. If this tie is for gold or silver, there will be a consequent absence of a silver or bronze medal for that event.
* Not all medals which have been revoked have been re-awarded.
* In a handful of early events, such as [[Cricket_at_the_1900_Summer_Olympics|Cricket at the 1900 Summer Olympics]], there were fewer than three entrants eligible to receive medals.

They competed together during the [[Australasia_at_the_1908_Summer_Olympics|1908]] and [[Australasia_at_the_1912_Summer_Olympics|1912]] Games.
* {{note label|BOH|BOH|BOH}} → {{note label|TCH|TCH|TCH}} → {{note label|CZE|CZE|CZE}}, {{note label|SVK|SVK|SVK}} Prior to the foundation of ''[[捷克斯洛伐克|Czechoslovakia]]'' (TCH) after [[第一次世界大战|World War I]], athletes from ''[[波希米亚|Bohemia]]'' (BOH) (now part of the present-day Czech Republic) competed in 1900, 1908, and 1912 Games. ''[[捷克斯洛伐克|Czechoslovakia]]'' competed in 1920–1992, from 1994 represented by successor NOCs of [[捷克|Czech Republic]] (CZE) and [[斯洛伐克|Slovakia]] (SVK).
* {{note label|BWI|BWI|BWI}} → {{note label|BAR|BAR|BAR}}, {{note label|JAM|JAM|JAM}}, {{note label|TRI|TRI|TRI}} Athletes from [[巴巴多斯|Barbados]] (BAR), [[牙买加|Jamaica]] (JAM), and [[千里達及托巴哥|Trinidad and Tobago]] (TRI) competed as the ''[[西印度群島聯邦|British West Indies]]'' (BWI) at the [[British_West_Indies_at_the_1960_Summer_Olympics|1960 Games]]. The ''[[西印度群島聯邦|West Indies Federation]]'' only existed as a nation from 1958–1962, so the constituent nations once again competed independently in 1964.
* {{note label|SAA|SAA|SAA}}, {{note label|EUA|EUA|EUA}}, {{note label|GDR|GDR|GDR}}, {{note label|FRG|FRG|FRG}} → {{note label|GER|GER|GER}} [[德国|Germany]] (GER) competed 1896–1952 and from 1992. Due to the [[partition_of_Germany|partition of Germany]] after [[第二次世界大战|World War II]], Germany was represented by two teams at the 1952 Games — [[德国|Germany]] (GER) and the ''[[薩爾保護領|Saar]]'' (SAA). The Saar was reintegrated back into the Federal Republic of Germany on 1 January 1957, and Saar athletes then competed for Germany. ''[[東德|East Germany]]'' did not contribute athletes to the 1952 team, as the National Olympic Committee for the German Democratic Republic was only granted "provisional" recognition by the IOC in 1955. For the Games of 1956–1964, Germany participated as a ''[[奥林匹克运动会德国联合代表团|Unified Team of Germany]]'' (EUA), representing the National Olympic Committees of both ''[[西德|West Germany]]'' and ''[[東德|East Germany]]''. Retrospectively, the IOC uses the country code EUA for this team. After the NOC for the German Democratic Republic was granted full recognition by the IOC in 1968, ''[[東德|East Germany]]'' (GDR) and ''[[西德|West Germany]]'' (FRG) competed as two independent teams from the 1968 to the 1988 Games.
* {{note label|MAL|MAL|MAL}}, {{note label|NBO|NBO|NBO}} → {{note label|MAS|MAS|MAS}} Athletes from ''[[马来亚联合邦|Malaya]]'' (MAL) and ''[[北婆羅洲直轄殖民地|North Borneo]]'' (NBO) competed as independent teams at the 1956 Games and Malaya also competed at the 1960 Games, prior to the formation of the [[马来西亚|Federation of Malaysia]] (MAS) in 1963.
* {{note label|AHO|AHO|AHO}} The ''[[荷属安的列斯|Netherlands Antilles]]'' (AHO), an autonomous country within the [[荷兰|Netherlands]] (NED), competed 1952–2008. The [[Netherlands_Antilles_Olympic_Committee|Netherlands Antilles Olympic Committee]]'s membership in the IOC was withdrawn in 2011 because of the [[荷属安的列斯解体|dissolution of the Netherlands Antilles]].
* {{note label|RU1|RU1|RU1}} → {{note label|URS|URS|URS}} → {{note label|EUN|EUN|EUN}} → {{note label|RUS|RUS|RUS}} The ''[[俄罗斯帝国|Russian Empire]]'' (RU1) participated at the 1900, 1908 and 1912 Games prior to [[第一次世界大战|World War I]]. ''[[苏联|Soviet Union]]'' (URS) competed 1952–1988. ''[[奥林匹克运动会独联体代表团|Unified Team]]'' (EUN) was the name used for team of several [[獨立國家聯合體|Commonwealth of Independent States]] nations that competed together at the 1992 Games after the breakup of the ''[[苏联|Soviet Union]]''. After the dissolution of the ''[[苏联|Soviet Union]]'', the [[俄罗斯|Russian Federation]] (RUS) and other post-Soviet republics has participated since 1994.

* {{note label|SRB|SRB|SRB}}, {{note label|YUG|YUG|YUG}} → {{note label|IOP|IOP|IOP}}, {{note label|SCG|SCG|SCG}} [[塞尔维亚|Serbia]] (SRB, 1912, 2006-current) first participated at the Olympic Games in [[1912年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1912]]. ''[[南斯拉夫|Yugoslavia]]'' (YUG, 1920-1992 Winter) competed as ''[[南斯拉夫王國|Kingdom of Yugoslavia]]'' (officially the ''Kingdom of Serbs, Croats and Slovenes'' until 1929) in five Games (1920–1936) before the [[第二次世界大战|Second World War]]. They later competed as ''[[南斯拉夫社会主义联邦共和国|Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia]]'' for the Games (1948–1992 Winter). This ended with the [[南斯拉夫解體|Breakup of Yugoslavia]]. Some ex-Yugoslavian nations ([[克罗地亚|Croatia]], [[斯洛文尼亚|Slovenia]], [[波斯尼亚和黑塞哥维那|Bosnia and Herzegovina]]) have competed as independent teams since 1992. Individual athletes from the ''[[塞爾維亞與蒙特內哥羅|Federal Republic of Yugoslavia]] (Serbia and Montenegro)'' competed as ''[[1992年夏季奧林匹克運動會獨立參賽者|Independent Olympic Participants]]'' (IOP) at the [[1992年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1992 Summer Games]] because of sanctions placed by the [[联合国|United Nations]]. Athletes representing [[馬其頓共和國|Macedonia]] likewise competed as independent olympic participants because their NOC had not yet been formed. Since 1996 [[馬其頓共和國|Macedonia]] has competed as independent team. The combined team ''[[塞爾維亞與蒙特內哥羅|Serbia and Montenegro]]'' (SCG, 1996-2006) consisted of the [[塞爾維亞共和國_(1992年-2006年)|Republic of Serbia]] and the [[蒙特內哥羅共和國_(1992–2006)|Republic of Montenegro]]. They competed as ''[[塞爾維亞與蒙特內哥羅|Federal Republic of Yugoslavia]]'' from (1996–2002) and then changed to ''[[塞爾維亞與蒙特內哥羅|State Union of Serbia and Montenegro]]'' (2004–2006). After 96 years [[塞尔维亚|Serbia]] returned to the Olympics as an independent team at the [[2008年夏季奥林匹克运动会|2008]] Games. [[蒙特內哥羅|Montenegro]] first participated as an independent team in [[2008年夏季奥林匹克运动会|2008]].
* {{note label|YAR|YAR|YAR}}, {{note label|YMD|YMD|YMD}} → {{note label|YEM|YEM|YEM}} Prior to [[也門統一|Yemeni unification]] in 1990, ''[[阿拉伯也门共和国|North Yemen]]'' participated as the ''Yemen Arab Republic'' (YAR) in 1984 and 1988, and ''[[也门民主人民共和国|South Yemen]]'' participated as the ''Yemen Democratic Republic'' (YMD) at the 1988 Games. After forming united republic [[也门|Yemen]] (YEM) has participated since 1992.
* {{note label|ROC|ROC|ROC}} → {{note label|CHN|CHN|CHN}}, {{note label|TPE|TPE|TPE}} The ''[[中華民國|Republic of China]]'' was designated as ''China'' at the 1932, 1936, and 1948 Games and was representing all of China (including [[中華民國|Taiwan]]). After the [[国共内战|Chinese Civil War]] the [[中华人民共和国|People's Republic of China]] (CHN) and [[中華臺北|Chinese Taipei]] (TPE) have participated as separate teams.
* {{note label|ZZX|ZZX|ZZX}} Special code used by the [[国际奥林匹克委员会|IOC]] to refer to medals won by athletes of multiple nations competing together, which was common in early Games (1896–1904). These medals are not included in the respective totals for each nation represented by individual mixed team athletes.
* {{note label|IOA|IOA|IOA}} Special code used by the [[国际奥林匹克委员会|IOC]] for athletes whose NOC is not recognized by IOC. At the 2000 Summer Olympics four athletes from [[东帝汶|East Timor]] competed as ''[[Individual_Olympic_Athletes_at_the_2000_Summer_Olympics|Individual Olympic Athletes]]'' (IOA). At the 2012 Summer Olympics three athletes from former [[荷属安的列斯|Netherlands Antilles]] and one athlete from [[南蘇丹|South Sudan]] competed as ''[[2012年夏季奥林匹克运动会独立参赛者|Independent Olympic Athletes]]'' (IOA). At the 2016 Summer Olympics nine athletes from [[科威特|Kuwait]] competed as ''[[2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會獨立參賽者|Independent Olympic Athletes]]'' (IOA). Kuwait Olympic Committee had been suspended by the International Olympic Committee (IOC) for the second time in five years due to governmental interference.

The IOC does not recognize the participation of [[意大利|Italy]] at the [[1904年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1904 Summer Olympics]].

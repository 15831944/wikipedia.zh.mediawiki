'''奥尼昂塔'''（{{lang en|'''Oneonta'''}}），是[[美国|美国]][[阿拉巴马州|阿拉巴马州]]下属的一座城市，[[布朗特县_(亚拉巴马州)|布朗特县]]的縣治所在。{{GR|6}}面积约为15.17平方英里（约合 39.28平方公里）。根据[[2000年美国人口普查|2000年人口普查]]，该市有人口5,576人。{{GR|2}}<ref name=2000census>{{Cite web| title = Fact Sheet- Oneonta city, Alabama  | work = American Fact Finder  | publisher = United States Census Bureau| url = http://factfinder.census.gov/servlet/SAFFFacts?_event=Search&geo_id=01000US&_geoContext=&_street=&_county=oneonta&_cityTown=oneonta&_state=04000US01&_zip=&_lang=en&_sse=on&ActiveGeoDiv=geoSelect&_useEV=&pctxt=fph&pgsl=010&_submenuId=factsheet_1&ds_name=ACS_2008_3YR_SAFF&_ci_nbr=null&qr_name=null&reg=null%3Anull&_keyword=&_industry=&show_2003_tab=&redirect=Y | accessdate =7 January 2010 }}</ref>根据[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年人口普查]]，该市有人口6,567人，人口密度为433.01/平方英里（约合167.18/平方公里）。<ref name=census>{{cite web |url=http://www.census.gov/prod/www/abs/decennial/ |title =  Census of Population and Housing |publisher = U.S. Census Bureau |accessdate = November 18, 2011}} </ref><ref>[http://factfinder2.census.gov/ 2010 census factfinder2]</ref>廊橋節是在奥尼昂塔舉辦。<ref>{{Cite web| title = 26th Annual Blount County Covered Bridge Festival| work = Blount-Oneonta Chamber of Commerce| url = http://www.blountoneontachamber.org/covered-bridge-festival| accessdate = 7 January 2010| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20101216171947/http://www.blountoneontachamber.org/covered-bridge-festival| archivedate = 2010年12月16日| df = }}</ref>

'''学院奖最佳服装设计奖'''（{{lang-en|'''Academy Award for Best Costume Design'''}}）是[[电影艺术与科学学院|电影艺术与科学学院]]颁发一个专门针对电影中优秀服装设计成就的年度奖项<ref>{{Cite web | url =http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/rule01.html | publisher =AMPAS | title =Rule One: Awards Definitions | deadurl =yes | archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20110821040412/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/rule01.html | archivedate =2011-08-21 }}</ref>。这一奖项首次颁发是在1949年初举办，针对1948年电影进行奖励的[[第21届学院奖颁奖典礼|第21届学院奖颁奖典礼]]上<ref>{{Cite web| url = http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/about/history.html | publisher = AMPAS | title = History of the Academy Awards}}</ref>。

起初时，服装设计奖也与当时的[[学院奖最佳摄影奖|摄影]]、[[学院奖最佳艺术指导奖|艺术指导]]两个奖一样，是按黑白片和彩色片分开颁奖的<ref>{{Cite web| url = http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/21st.html  | publisher =电影艺术与科学学院| title = The 21st Academy Awards (1949)}}</ref>。但到了1958年3月26日举行的[[第30届学院奖颁奖典礼|第30届学院奖颁奖典礼]]上，这三个奖项都统一为一个奖<ref>{{Cite web| url =http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/30th.html | publisher =AMPAS| title = The 30th Academy Awards (1958)}}</ref>。不过这一做法仅持续了两届，到了[[第32届学院奖颁奖典礼|第32届学院奖颁奖典礼]]又一次分开成了两个奖<ref name=awards1959>{{cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/32nd-winners.html |title=The 32nd Academy Awards (1960) Nominees and Winners |publisher=AMPAS |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140406222747/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/32nd-winners.html |archivedate=2014-04-06 }}</ref>并一直保持至[[第39届学院奖颁奖典礼|第39届]]时为止。从[[第40届学院奖颁奖典礼|第40届]]开始，三个奖项再也没有按黑白片和彩色片为开颁奖过<ref>{{Cite web| url =http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/40th.html | publisher =AMPAS| title = The 40th Academy Awards (1968)}}</ref>。有意思的是，自第40届学院奖开始至今，服装设计奖仅有两次颁给了时代背景为当代的电影，其余获奖影片均为历史题材（古装片）<ref name=cdglist>{{cite web|url=http://www.costumedesignersguild.com/press-room/motion-picture-academy-awards-best-costume-design.pdf |title=Motion Picture Academy Awards - Costume Design |publisher=Costume Designers Guild |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110112082146/http://www.costumedesignersguild.com/press-room/motion-picture-academy-awards-best-costume-design.pdf |archivedate=2011-01-12 }}</ref>。

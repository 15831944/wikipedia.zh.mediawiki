'''外團體同質性效應'''（{{lang-en|out-group homogeneity effect}}）是指人們常持有「[[外團體|外團體]]都具有相同性質、而內團體性質较多元化」的一種[[認知偏誤|認知偏誤]]。<ref name="Quattrone & Jones (1980)">{{cite journal|last1=Quattrone|first1=George A.|last2=Jones|first2=Edward E.|title=The perception of variability within in-groups and out-groups: Implications for the law of small numbers.|journal=Journal of Personality and Social Psychology|volume=38|issue=1|year=1980|pages=141–152|doi=10.1037/0022-3514.38.1.141}}</ref>這一效應能在廣泛的社會群體（如政治立場、種族、年齡、性別）中發現。<ref name=Rubin2004>Rubin, M., Hewstone, M., Crisp, R. J., Voci, A., & Richards, Z. (2004). Gender out-group homogeneity: The roles of differential familiarity, gender differences, and group size. In V. Yzerbyt, C. M. Judd, & O. Corneille (Eds.), The psychology of group perception: Perceived variability, entitativity, and essentialism (pp. 203-220). New York: Psychology Press. [https://books.google.com/books?id=IA9Qg-fDga8C&pg=PA153&dq=rubin+gender+out-group+homogeneity&hl=en&sa=X&ei=ZQQxT7-AAo6ZiQen14j3BA&ved=0CDMQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=rubin%20gender%20out-group%20homogeneity&f=false [View<nowiki>]</nowiki>]</ref>

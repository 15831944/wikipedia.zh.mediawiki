'''外担菌纲'''（[[学名|学名]]：Exobasidiomycetes）是[[担子菌门|担子菌门]][[黑粉菌亚门|黑粉菌亚门]]下的一个纲。该纲[[真菌|真菌]]与一些植物的[[菌瘿|菌瘿]]有关。这一纲共包含六个目，分布为实球黑粉菌目（Doassansiales）、叶黑粉菌目（Entylomatales）、外担菌目（Exobasidiales）、Georgefischeriales、Microstromatales以及腥黑粉菌目（Tilletiales）。<ref>{{cite journal|author=Hibbett DS, Binder M, Bischoff JF, ''et al''. |year=2007 |title=A higher level phylogenetic classification of the Fungi |journal=Mycological Research |volume=111 |issue=5 |pages=509–47 |url=http://www.clarku.edu/faculty/dhibbett/AFTOL/documents/AFTOL%20class%20mss%2023,%2024/AFTOL%20CLASS%20MS%20resub.pdf |doi=10.3852/08-177 |pmid=19202837 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090326135053/http://www.clarku.edu/faculty/dhibbett/AFTOL/documents/AFTOL%20class%20mss%2023%2C%2024/AFTOL%20CLASS%20MS%20resub.pdf |archivedate=2009-03-26 }}</ref>

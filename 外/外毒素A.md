'''外毒素A'''（{{lang-en|exotoxin A}}）也稱'''绿脓杆菌外毒素'''、'''假单胞菌外毒素'''（Pseudomonas exotoxin），是由[[綠膿桿菌|綠膿桿菌]]（学名：铜绿假单胞菌，''Pseudomonas aeruginosa'')产生的[[外毒素|外毒素]]<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Structure-function analysis of water-soluble inhibitors of the catalytic domain of exotoxin A from Pseudomonas aeruginosa|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15458385|last=Yates|first=Susan P.|last2=Taylor|first2=Patricia L.|date=2005-02-01|journal=The Biochemical Journal|issue=Pt 3|doi=10.1042/BJ20041480|volume=385|pages=667–675|issn=1470-8728|pmid=15458385|last3=Jørgensen|first3=René|last4=Ferraris|first4=Dana|last5=Zhang|first5=Jie|last6=Andersen|first6=Gregers R.|last7=Merrill|first7=A. Rod}}</ref>。它的功能是将[[NAD+|NAD+]]上的[[二磷酸腺苷核糖|二磷酸腺苷核糖]]（ADP-ribose）转移到目标蛋白质上，这一过程称为[[ADP-核糖基化|ADP-核糖基化]][[翻译后修饰|修饰]]。它的致病机制是通过将[[真核延伸因子2|真核延伸因子2]]（EF2）的{{le|白喉酰胺|Diphthamide}}残基[[ADP-核糖基化|ADP-核糖基化]]来抑制EF2<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Elucidation of eukaryotic elongation factor-2 contact sites within the catalytic domain of Pseudomonas aeruginosa exotoxin A|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/14733615|last=Yates|first=Susan P.|last2=Merrill|first2=Allan R.|date=2004-05-01|journal=The Biochemical Journal|issue=Pt 3|doi=10.1042/BJ20031731|volume=379|pages=563–572|issn=1470-8728|pmid=14733615}}</ref>，从而导致多肽的延长停止（毒素的机制类似于[[白喉毒素|白喉毒素]]）。目前该现象已被用于[[乙肝|乙肝]]<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Pseudomonas exotoxin antisense RNA selectively kills hepatitis B virus infected cells|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18473403|last=Hafkemeyer|first=Peter|last2=Brinkmann|first2=Ulrich|date=2008-05-14|journal=World Journal of Gastroenterology|issue=18|doi=10.3748/wjg.14.2810|volume=14|pages=2810–2817|issn=1007-9327|pmid=18473403|last3=Brinkmann|first3=Elizabeth|last4=Pastan|first4=Ira|last5=Blum|first5=Hubert-E.|last6=Baumert|first6=Thomas-F.}}</ref>和癌症<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Engineering toxin-resistant therapeutic stem cells to treat brain tumors|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/25346520|last=Stuckey|first=Daniel W.|last2=Hingtgen|first2=Shawn D.|date=2015-2|journal=Stem Cells (Dayton, Ohio)|issue=2|doi=10.1002/stem.1874|volume=33|pages=589–600|issn=1549-4918|pmid=25346520|last3=Karakas|first3=Nihal|last4=Rich|first4=Benjamin E.|last5=Shah|first5=Khalid}}</ref>的治疗研究。

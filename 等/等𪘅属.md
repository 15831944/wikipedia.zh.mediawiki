'''單{{僻字|𪘅|左虫右齒}}屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Isophanes'''''}}<ref name=mhn />），是[[啮虫总目|啮虫目]]之下的一個屬。


== 分類及物種 ==
舊屬[[真齧蟲亞目|真齧蟲亞目]]（Eupsocida）<ref name=ztflh_16425>{{ztflh |id=16425 |title=真啮虫亚目}}</ref>，今屬[[啮虫总目|啮虫目]]（Psocodea）[[啮虫亚目|啮虫亚目]]（Psocomorpha）。
依據[[GBIF|GBIF]]等𪘅属有物種12種<ref>{{GBIF |id=4396203|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes Banks, 1937 }}</ref>，當中有四個物種有在中國出現<ref name=cnki>{{cite journal |url=http://www.cnki.com.cn/Article/CJFDTotal-GZKX199302011.htm |title=等𪘅属及中国四新种记述(𪘅目:单𪘅科)|author=[[李法圣|李法圣]] |language=zh-hans|accessdate=2014-12-16|date=1993-02 |journal=《[[贵州科学|贵州科学]]》|volume=1993年|issue=02期 }}</ref>。這12個物種分別為：
* [[安哥拉等𪘅|安哥拉等𪘅]]''Isophanes angolensis'' <small>Badonnel, 1955</small>：於[[安哥拉|安哥拉]]發現。<ref name=cnki /><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396205|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes angolensis Badonnel, 1955 }}</ref>
* ''Isophanes capeneri'' <small>Smithers, 1956</small>：於[[南非|南非]]發現。<ref name=cnki /><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396204|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes capeneri Smithers, 1956 }}</ref>
* [[圓印等𪘅|圓印等𪘅]] ''Isophanes cyclostigma '' <small>Li, 1993</small>：於[[南非|南非]]發現。<ref name=cnki /><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396212|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes cyclostigma Li, 1993 }}</ref>
* [[惑等嚙蟲|惑等嚙蟲]] ''Isophanes decipiens'' <small>Banks, 1937</small>：模式種<ref name=cnki />，有在台灣發現<ref name=TaiBIF_348643>{{TaiBIF |id=348643 |taxon=Isophanes decipiens Banks, 1937 |zh=惑等嚙蟲 |accessdate=2015-01-28 }}</ref><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396206|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes decipiens Banks, 1937 }}</ref>
* [[酆都等𪘅|酆都等𪘅]] ''Isophanes fengduensis'' <small>Li, 1997</small>：<ref>{{GBIF |id=4396206|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes fengduensis Li, 1997 }}</ref>
* ''Isophanes luteus'' <small>Li, 1992</small><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396208|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes luteus Li, 1992 }}</ref>
* [[東方等𪘅|東方等𪘅]] ''Isophanes orientalis'' <small>Garcia Aldrete, 1999</small><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396213|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes orientalis Garcia Aldrete, 1999 }}</ref>
* [[尖印等𪘅|尖印等𪘅]] ''Isophanes oxyostigma '' <small>Li, 1993</small><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396207|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes oxyostigma Li, 1993 }}</ref>
* ''Isophanes palliatus '' <small>(Hagen, 1859)</small>：舊稱''Psocus palliatus''。<ref name=cnki /><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396210|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes palliatus (Hagen, 1859)}}</ref>
* [[塊印等𪘅|塊印等𪘅]] ''Isophanes platystigma '' <small>Li, 1997</small>：<ref>{{GBIF |id=4396212|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes platystigma Li, 1997 }}</ref>
* [[中國等𪘅|中國等𪘅]] ''Isophanes sinensis '' <small>New, 1991</small><ref>{{GBIF |id=4396211|accessdate=2015-01-28 |title=Isophanes sinensis New, 1991 }}</ref>

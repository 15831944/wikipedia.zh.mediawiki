《'''沙丘魔堡'''》（{{lang-en|''Dune''}}）是一部2020年[[美國|美國]][[科幻電影|科幻電影]]，由[[丹尼·維勒納夫|丹尼·維勒納夫]]執導，{{link-en|艾瑞克·羅斯|Eric Roth}}、{{link-en|喬·斯派茨|Jon Spaihts}}和導演本人共同編寫劇本，劇情改編自[[法蘭克·赫伯特|法蘭克·赫伯特]]的1965年英文同名[[科幻小說|科幻小說]]《{{link-en|沙丘 (小說)|Dune (novel)|沙丘}}》。

== 製作 ==
2016年11月，[[傳奇影業|傳奇影業]]取得《{{link-en|沙丘 (小說)|Dune (novel)|沙丘}}》的電影和電視製作權<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2016/11/dune-frank-herbert-sci-fi-novel-legendary-rights-acquisition-1201858221/ |title=Legendary Acquires Frank Herbert's Classic Sci-Fi Novel ''Dune'' For Film And TV |last=Busch |first=Anita |website=Deadline Hollywood |date=2016-11- 21 |accessdate=2019-02-18 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2016/film/news/legendary-dune-frank-herbert-1201923648/ |title=Legendary Lands Rights to Classic Sci-Fi Novel ''Dune''|last=Kroll |first=Justin |work=Variety |date=2016-11-21 |accessdate=2019-02-18 |language=en}}</ref>。12月，據報導[[丹尼·維勒納夫|丹尼·維勒納夫]]正洽談執導此計畫<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2016/film/news/denis-villeneuve-dune-reboot-legendary-pictures-1201929280/ |title=''Blade Runner 2049'' Helmer Denis Villeneuve Eyed to Direct ''Dune'' Reboot |last=Kroll |first=Justin |work=Variety |date=2016-12-21 |accessdate=2019-02-18 |language=en}}</ref>，隔年2月證實由他執導<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bleedingcool.com/2017/02/01/official-dune-reboot-legendary-pictures-director/ |title=It's Official: The ''Dune'' Reboot Has Found Its Director |last=Watters |first=Bill |work=Bleeding Cool |date=2017-02-01 |accessdate=2019-02-18 |language=en}}</ref>。2018年3月，維勒納夫表示他的目標是將原著小說分成兩部電影拍攝<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.slashfilm.com/denis-villeneuve-dune-remake-two-films/ |title=At Least Two ''Dune'' Films Are Being Planned by Denis Villeneuve |last=Bui |first=Hoai-Tran |website=/Film |date=2018-03-09 |accessdate=2019-02-18 |language=en}}</ref>，並於5月宣布電影劇本的初稿已完成<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.slashfilm.com/dune-first-draft-denis-villeneuve/ |title=The First Draft of Denis Villeneuve's ''Dune'' is Done, Pre-Production Begins 'Soon' |last=Bui |first=Hoai-Tran |website=/Film |date=2018-05-11 |accessdate=2019-02-18 |language=en}}</ref>。

== 發行 ==
影片由[[華納兄弟|華納兄弟]]發行，預計於2020年11月20日在美國上映<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/02/warner-bros-dates-dune-for-november-2020-more-1202558747/ |title=Warner Bros. Dates ''Dune'' For November 2020 & More |website=Deadline Hollywood |first=Anthony |last=D'Alessandro |date=2019-02-15 |accessdate=2019-02-18 |language=en}}</ref>。

'''沙怪龍屬'''（[[屬|屬]]名：{{lang|la|''Seitaad''}}）又名'''賽泰龍'''，是一種原始的[[蜥腳類|蜥腳類]][[恐龍|恐龍]]，屬於[[蜥腳形亞目|蜥腳形亞目]]的[[蜥腳形類|蜥腳形類]]，生存於[[侏羅紀|侏羅紀]]早期的[[北美洲|北美洲]]，化石發現於[[美國|美國]][[猶他州|猶他州]]東南部的[[聖胡安縣_(猶他州)|聖胡安]]地區<ref name=Seitaad>{{Cite journal|author=Joseph J. W. Sertich and Mark A. Loewen |year=2010 |title=A New Basal Sauropodomorph Dinosaur from the Lower Jurassic Navajo Sandstone of Southern Utah |url= http://www.plosone.org/article/info%3Adoi%2F10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0009789 |journal=PLoS ONE |volume=5 |issue=3 |pages=e9789 |doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0009789 |pmid= 20352090|pmc=2844413|editor1-last=Laudet|editor1-first=Vincent }}</ref>，是繼[[近蜥龍|近蜥龍]]後第二種在北美洲發現的原始蜥腳類恐龍<ref name=Sarahsaurus>{{Cite journal|author=Timothy B. Rowe, Hans-Dieter Sues and Robert R. Reisz |year=2011 |title=Dispersal and diversity in the earliest North American sauropodomorph dinosaurs, with a description of a new taxon |url= http://rspb.royalsocietypublishing.org/content/278/1708/1044.full |journal=Proceedings of the Royal Society B: Biological Sciences |volume=278 |issue=1708 |pages=1044–1053 |doi=10.1098/rspb.2010.1867 |pmid=20926438 |pmc=3049036}}</ref>。

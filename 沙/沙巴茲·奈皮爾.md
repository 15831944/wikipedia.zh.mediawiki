'''沙巴茲·波茲·內皮爾'''（{{lang-en|'''Shabazz Bozie Napier'''}}，{{bd|1991年|7月14日}}），波多黎各裔美國職業[[籃球|籃球]]運動員，目前效力於[[NBA|NBA]][[布鲁克林篮网|布鲁克林篮网]]。他在[[2014年NBA選秀|2014年NBA選秀]]第24順位被[[夏洛特黃蜂|夏洛特黃蜂]]選中，隨後被交易至[[迈阿密热火|迈阿密热火]]。內皮爾大學時期效力於{{tsl|en|Connecticut Huskies men's basketball|康乃狄克大學哈士奇男子籃球隊|康乃狄克大學哈士奇隊}}，曾在{{tsl|en|2011 NCAA Men's Division I Basketball Tournament|2011年NCAA一级男子篮球锦标赛|2011}}及[[2014年NCAA一级男子篮球锦标赛|2014年]]贏得兩座全國錦標賽冠軍。{{tsl|en|2010–11 Connecticut Huskies men's basketball team|2010－11年康乃狄克大學哈士奇男子籃球隊|2010–11年NCAA錦標賽}}賽季期間，內皮爾擔任哈士奇隊的核心人物並入選[[大東聯盟|大東聯盟]]最佳新秀陣容。<ref name=DBL>{{cite web |url=http://espn.go.com/ncb/recap?gameId=313240041 |title=Shabazz Napier's triple-double helps UConn rally to beat Coppin State |date=2011-11-21 |website=espn.go.com |publisher=[[ESPN|ESPN]] |accessdate=2018-03-14 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160306103822/http://espn.go.com/ncb/recap?gameId=313240041 |archivedate=2016-03-06 }}</ref>接著在大三那年的NCAA賽季，入選大東聯盟最佳陣容第一隊。<ref name="bigeast">{{cite web|last1=Duffy|first1=Kevin|title=Napier named All-Big East First Team, Calhoun on All-Rookie Team - UConn Basketball|url=http://blog.ctnews.com/uconnbasketball/2013/03/10/napier-named-all-big-east-first-team-calhoun-on-all-rookie-team/|website=Connecticut News|publisher=Connecticut Post|accessdate=2014-06-29}}</ref>

2013－2014年賽季，內皮爾入選[[美国竞技联盟|美国竞技联盟]]最佳陣容第一隊，並獲選為首屆{{tsl|en|American Athletic Conference Men's Basketball Player of the Year|美國競技聯盟年度最佳男子籃球運動員|美國競技聯盟年度最佳球員}}。<ref name=lifts>{{Cite web|url=http://scores.espn.go.com/ncb/recap?gameId=400496843|title=Shabazz Napier's triple-double lifts No. 19 UConn over Yale|work=ESPN.com|date=2013-11-11|accessdate=2014-06-27|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140722124009/http://scores.espn.go.com/ncb/recap?gameId=400496843|archivedate=2014-07-22}}</ref>{{tsl|en|2013–14 Connecticut Huskies men's basketball team|2013－14年康乃狄克大學哈士奇男子籃球隊|2014年}}，他再度成為康乃狄克大學四年內二度贏得NCAA聯賽冠軍的關鍵人物，並獲選為[[NCAA籃球聯賽最傑出球員|年度最傑出球員]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=UConn's Napier named most outstanding player of Final Four|url=http://www.foxnews.com/sports/2014/04/08/uconn-napier-named-most-outstanding-player-final-four/|website=Fox News|publisher=Associated Press|accessdate=2014-06-29|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140716044954/http://www.foxnews.com/sports/2014/04/08/uconn-napier-named-most-outstanding-player-final-four/|archivedate=2014-07-16}}</ref>

==早年生活==
內皮爾生於[[美國|美國]][[麻薩諸塞州|麻薩諸塞州]]{{tsl|en|Roxbury, Boston|羅克斯伯里 (波士頓)|羅克斯伯里}}， 母親卡門·貝拉斯克斯（{{lang|es|Carmen Velasquez}}）為[[波多黎各|波多黎各]]人，家中還有姊姊蒂塔娜·貝拉斯克斯（{{lang|en|Titana Velasquez}}）及哥哥蒂米·巴羅斯（{{lang|en|Timmie Barrows}}）。他在波士頓的[[傳教山_(波士頓)|傳教山]]計畫中長大，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sportsonearth.com/article/67611648/how-shabazz-napier-has-become-connecticut-huskies-star|title=THE PATH TO STARDOM|work=SportsOnEarth.com|date=2014-02-11|accessdate=2014-06-27|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141008210451/http://www.sportsonearth.com/article/67611648/how-shabazz-napier-has-become-connecticut-huskies-star|archivedate=2014-10-08}}</ref>在此成長的過程中開始愛上籃球。<ref name="uconnprofile">{{cite web|url=http://www.uconnhuskies.com/sports/m-baskbl/mtt/shabazz_napier_714048.html|title=Shabazz Napier Bio|work=UConnHuskies.com|accessdate=2014-06-27|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6OlOkLtvL?url=http://www.uconnhuskies.com/sports/m-baskbl/mtt/shabazz_napier_714048.html|archivedate=2014-04-11}}</ref>

==高中生涯==
起初，內皮爾於麻薩諸塞州[[查爾斯頓_(波士頓)|查爾斯頓]]的{{tsl|en|Charlestown High School|查爾斯頓高中}}就讀兩年，之後便轉學至[[格罗顿_(马萨诸塞州)|格罗顿]]的{{tsl|en|Lawrence Academy at Groton|勞倫斯學院 (格羅頓)|勞倫斯學院}}並重讀高二。2010年，高三的內皮爾在{{tsl|en|New England Preparatory School Athletic Council|新英格蘭預科學校體育理事會|新英格蘭預科學校}}C級聯賽冠軍賽中取得23分、8次抄截，帶領勞倫斯學院擊敗{{tsl|en|St. Mark's School (Massachusetts)|聖馬可中學 (麻薩諸塞州)|聖馬可中學}}贏得冠軍，創下29勝0敗未嘗敗績的賽季，內皮爾也在賽後獲選為該賽季的最有價值球員。<ref>{{cite news |title=Highly touted point guard Shabazz Napier commits to UConn |first=Gavin |last=Keefe |date=2010-04-03 |newspaper={{tsl|en|The Day (New London)|The Day (新倫敦)|The Day}} |publisher=Gary Farrugia }}</ref>

內皮爾被[[Rivals.com|Rivals.com]]評為4星新生，在2010年的控球後衛中名列第25，全國總排名位居第98名。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://n.rivals.com/content/prospects/2010/shabazz-napier-58165 |title=Shabazz Napier Recruiting Profile |website=rivals.com |accessdate=2018-03-14 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180410112352/https://n.rivals.com/content/prospects/2010/shabazz-napier-58165 |archivedate=2018-04-10 }}</ref>

2014年1月，查爾斯頓高中將內皮爾的球衣退役。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.nhregister.com/sports/20140130/shabazz-napier-going-back-to-where-it-all-started |title=Shabazz Napier going back to where it all started |date=2014-01-30 |work=New Haven Register |publisher=[[赫茲國際集團|Hearst Communications]] |accessdate=2018-03-14 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161225193309/http://www.nhregister.com/sports/20140130/shabazz-napier-going-back-to-where-it-all-started |archivedate=2016-12-25 }}</ref>

==大學生涯==

===大一賽季===

從預科學校畢業後，內皮爾選擇進入由{{tsl|en|Jim Calhoun|吉姆·卡爾霍恩}}教練執教的[[康乃狄克大學|康乃狄克大學]]就讀。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Schwartz|first1=Alex|title=BLOG.NORTHSTARBBALL.COM: Shabazz Napier Picks UConn, Attempting to Reclassify|url=http://blog.northstarbball.com/2010/04/03/shabazz-napier-picks-uconn-attempting-to-reclassify.aspx|website=Northstar Basketball Blog|accessdate=2014-06-29|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140726135105/http://blog.northstarbball.com/2010/04/03/shabazz-napier-picks-uconn-attempting-to-reclassify.aspx|archivedate=2014-07-26}}</ref>2010－11年賽季，大一的內皮爾擔任[[肯巴·沃克|肯巴·沃克]]的替補，板凳上場41場並取得場均7.8分、3次助攻及1.8次抄截的成績。<ref name="basketref">{{cite web |title=Shabazz Napier Stats |url=https://www.basketball-reference.com/players/n/napiesh01.html |website=Basketball-Reference.com |publisher=Basketball-Reference |accessdate=2018-03-23 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180328202108/https://www.basketball-reference.com/players/n/napiesh01.html |archivedate=2018-03-28 }}</ref>例行賽季結束後，內皮爾與隊友[[杰里米·兰姆|杰里米·兰姆]]一同入選[[大東聯盟|大東聯盟]]最佳新秀陣容。<ref>{{cite web|title=Three Huskies Honored By Big East << CBS Connecticut|url=http://connecticut.cbslocal.com/2011/03/06/three-huskies-honored-by-big-east/|website=CBS Connecticut|publisher=CBS|date=2011-03-06|accessdate=2014-06-28|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140724235253/http://connecticut.cbslocal.com/2011/03/06/three-huskies-honored-by-big-east/|archivedate=2014-07-24}}</ref>

內皮爾在[[NCAA一级男子篮球锦标赛|四強賽]]對陣肯塔基大學時投進兩顆關鍵罰球，幫助球隊晉級總決賽。內皮爾在本季為求隊做出相當的貢獻，致使康乃狄克大學以32勝9負（聯盟賽9勝9負）的戰績結束本賽季，取得2011年大東聯盟冠軍及全國總冠軍。<ref name="uconnprofile"/>

===大三賽季===
2012－13年賽季，內皮爾與隊友{{tsl|en|Ryan Boatright|萊恩·博特賴特}}擔任球隊後衛搭檔，兩人平均每場共取得33分及9次助攻，傑出的表現被視為當時全國的最佳後衛組合之一。<ref>{{cite web |title=Ryan Boatright helps UConn field a super heroic backcourt |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/ncaab/2014/04/04/college-basketball-tournament-final-four-ryan-boatright-shabazz-napier-uconn/7305321/ |first=Nicole |last=Auerbach |website=USA TODAY Sports |date=2014-04-04 |accessdate=2018-03-26 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140406001133/http://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/ncaab/2014/04/04/college-basketball-tournament-final-four-ryan-boatright-shabazz-napier-uconn/7305321/ |archivedate=2014-04-06 }}</ref>內皮爾在對上[[帝博大學|帝博大學]]的比賽中，取得他生涯的第1000分得分。<ref name="uconnprofile"/>內皮爾於延長賽的表現也相當卓越，在共計45分鐘的延長賽內取得55分，幫助球隊在7場進入延長賽的比賽中取得5場勝利。<ref name="returnsenior">{{cite web |title=Shabazz Napier Will Return For Senior Year |url=http://www.uconnhuskies.com/sports/m-baskbl/spec-rel/042613aab.html |website=UConnhuskies.com |accessdate=2014-06-29 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140726023700/http://www.uconnhuskies.com/sports/m-baskbl/spec-rel/042613aab.html |archivedate=2014-07-26 }}</ref>內皮爾本季場均17.1分、4.6次助攻、4.4個籃板及2.0次抄截。<ref name="returnsenior"/>

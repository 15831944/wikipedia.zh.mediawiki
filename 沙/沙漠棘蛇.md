'''沙漠棘蛇'''（学名：''Acanthophis pyrrhus''；英语：desert death adder）是[[棘蛇属|棘蛇属]]下的一种剧毒蛇类，分布于澳大利亚西部沙漠地区。体长可达{{cvt|70|cm}}，呈红褐色，头部扁平，呈三角形<ref name ="Southwest">{{cite book |author1=Browne-Cooper, R. |author2=Bush, B. |author3=Maryan, B. |author4=Robinson, D. |title= Reptiles and Frogs in the Bush: Southwestern Australia|year= 2007|month= |publisher= [[University_of_Western_Australia|University of Western Australia]] Press|isbn= 978-1-920694-74-6 |pages=  243, 244|chapter= |chapterurl= |quote=}}</ref>。

虽然人类被其咬伤后死亡率高达50%，但由于分布偏远、数量不多以及抗毒血清的发明，很少有被其咬伤致死的案例。<ref name="AVRU">{{cite web|url=http://www.avru.org/general/general_desadder.html|title=Desert Death Adder (Acanthophis pyrrhus)|work=Venomous Animals|publisher=Australian Venom Research Unit (AVRU)|accessdate=2008-10-13}}</ref><ref name="Toxinology">{{cite web|url=http://www.toxinology.com/generic_static_files/cslavh_antivenom_deathader.html|title=CSL Death Adder Antivenom|work=Toxinology Department: CSL Antivenom Handbook|publisher=Women's & Children's Hospital, Adelaide, Australia|accessdate=2008-10-13}}</ref>

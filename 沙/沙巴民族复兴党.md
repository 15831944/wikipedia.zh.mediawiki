'''沙巴民族复兴党<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/chinese/malaysia/article/20161023-party-warisan-change-chinese-name|title=传承等于船沉？沙菲益政党正名沙巴复兴党|date=2016-10-23|publisher=}}</ref>'''（{{lang-ms|Parti Warisan Sabah}}，简称WARISAN或民兴党），是马来西亚[[沙巴|沙巴]]的執政黨<ref>{{cite web|url=http://malaysiandigest.com/news/637519-shafie-s-party-name-logo-approved-to-be-called-parti-warisan-sabah.html|title=Shafie's Party Name, Logo Approved, To Be Called 'Parti Warisan Sabah'|work=New Straits Times|publisher=Malaysian Digest|date=17 October 2016|accessdate=17 October 2016}}</ref>，也是聯邦執政黨[[希望聯盟|希望聯盟]]的合作政黨。该党由达勒雷京和前巫统副主席[[沙菲益阿达|沙菲益阿达]]于2016年10月17日成立<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.thesundaily.my/node/401778|title=Parti Warisan Sabah is new name of Shafie-led party|work=Bernama|publisher=The Sun|date=17 October 2016|accessdate=17 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/1577970|title=沙菲益接管政党·易名沙传承党|date=2016-10-18|publisher=}}</ref>。新党取代2013年成立时所取名的沙巴传承发展党（Parti Pembangunan Warisan Sabah）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailyexpress.com.my/news.cfm?NewsID=113464|title=Shafie's Parti Warisan Sabah name change okayed|publisher=Daily Express|date=18 October 2016|accessdate=18 October 2016}}</ref>。

该党也在[[2018年马来西亚大选|2018年马来西亚大选]]和沙巴[[希望联盟|希望联盟]]的[[民主行动党|民主行动党]]和[[人民公正党|人民公正党]]合作，但只在沙巴州参选，随着希望联盟执政而成为联邦内阁名单的成员之一<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/418074|title=Warisan seals electoral pact with DAP and PKR|publisher=Malaysiakini|date=2 April 2018|accessdate=2 April 2018}} {{subscription required}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/nation/2018/04/352101/warisan-will-work-only-pakatan-harapan-ge14|title=Warisan will work only with Pakatan Harapan for GE14|author=Suzianah Jiffar|publisher=New Straits Times|date=2 April 2018|accessdate=2 April 2018}}</ref>。

'''沙普里島'''（{{lang-bn|শাহপরী দ্বীপ}} <small>''Sahapari Dwip''</small>、{{lang-my|ရှင္မျဖဴကၽြန္း}}、{{lang-en|Shapuree、Shahpori、Shah Parir<ref name=Banglapedia/>、Shapura<ref>{{citation |authors=Tucker, S.C. |year=2009 |title=A Global Chronology of Conflict: From the Ancient World to the Modern Middle East [6 volumes]: From the Ancient World to the Modern Middle East |publisher=ABC-CLIO |isbn=9781851096725 |url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=h5_tSnygvbIC |page=1142 |access-date=2018-11-16 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181004101842/https://books.google.ca/books?id=h5_tSnygvbIC# |archive-date=2018-10-04 |dead-url=no }}</ref>、Shalpuri、Shinmabyu Island}}）是[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]]的一座島嶼，位於孟加拉與[[緬甸|緬甸]]的界河[[納夫河|納夫河]]的河口，行政區劃屬於[[吉大港專區|吉大港專區]][[科克斯巴扎爾縣|科克斯巴扎爾縣]][[代格納夫烏帕齊拉|代格納夫烏帕齊拉]]，為其半島的延伸<ref name=Banglapedia>{{cite book |last=Mainuddin |first=Md |year=2012 |chapter=Teknaf Upazila |chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=Teknaf_Upazila |editor1-last=Islam |editor1-first=Sirajul |editor1-link=Sirajul Islam |editor2-last=Jamal |editor2-first=Ahmed A. |title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |edition=Second |publisher=[[Asiatic_Society_of_Bangladesh|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh]]}}</ref>。1823年，英國以緬甸佔據沙普里島為[[戰爭藉口|戰爭藉口]]對其開戰，發動[[第一次英緬戰爭|第一次英緬戰爭]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www1.somerset.gov.uk/archives/sli/1burmese.htm|title=The First Burmese War 1824-1826|publisher=Somerset County Council|work=Somerset Archives and Local Studies|access-date=2018-11-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160217102053/http://www1.somerset.gov.uk/archives/sli/1burmese.htm#|archive-date=2016-02-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>，並佔領了該島<ref name=dw-60>{{cite book | title=The Making of Burma| author=Dorothy Woodman| year=1962 | page=60 | edition=1 | publisher=The Cresset Press| location=London}}</ref>。

沙普里島是從緬甸乘船而來的[[羅興亞人|羅興亞難民]]進入孟加拉的上岸地點之一<ref>{{cite journal|title=Border guards push back 500|author=Dwaipayan Barua|journal=The Daily Star|date=12 June 2012|url=http://www.thedailystar.net/newDesign/print_news.php?nid=237990}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://narinjara.com/bangladesh-pushes-back-31-myanmar-citizens/|title=Bangladesh pushes back 31 Myanmar citizens|date=2017-8-21|publisher=Narinjara News|access-date=2018-11-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180407053911/http://narinjara.com/bangladesh-pushes-back-31-myanmar-citizens/#|archive-date=2018-04-07|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

'''沙希杜尔·阿拉姆'''（{{lang-bn|শহিদুল আলম}}，{{lang-en|Shahidul Alam}}，{{bd|1955年}}），是一位[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]]摄影师、社交活动家。他是德里克的创始人。

==生平 ==
阿拉姆在伦敦学习化学，并在[[伦敦大学|伦敦大学]]获授哲学博士学位。1989年，他建立了[[德里克图片图书馆|德里克图片图书馆]]。1998年，他成立了南亚摄影学院<ref>{{cite news |title=Chobi-Mela |publisher=Asia Pulse |agency=United News of Bangladesh |date=6 December 2004 |quote="Festival director for Chobi Mela Shahidul Alam presided."}}</ref>。他亦是世界新闻摄影大赛评审团的首位亚洲主席<ref>{{citation |url=http://universes-in-universe.org/eng/nafas/articles/2009/shahidul_alam |author=Fariha Karim|date=April 2009|title=Shahidul Alam |publisher=Nafas Art Magazine |accessdate=31 March 2015}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://photographemarocain.com/shahidul-alam-le-photographe-qui-derange/|title=Bangladesh. Shahidul Alam, Le photographe qui dérange {{!}} Photographe Marocain|accessdate=2018-08-30|work=photographemarocain.com|language=fr}}</ref>。

2015年，他举办的、由秘鲁学者{{le|豪尔赫·比利亚科尔塔|Jorge Villacorta}}策划的展览“Crossfire”广受好评，但后被警方关闭，导致全国范围内爆发了抗议活动<ref>{{cite news| url=http://lens.blogs.nytimes.com/2010/03/16/showcase-137/ | work=The New York Times | first=David | last=Gonzalez |title=Where Death Squads Struck in Bangladesh | date=16 March 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.demotix.com/news/283513/crossfire-exhibition-sparks-angry-police-reaction |title=’Crossfire’ exhibition sparks angry police reaction |work=Demotix |date=22 March 2010 |author=Bayazid Akter |accessdate=31 March 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150102040943/http://www.demotix.com/news/283513/crossfire-exhibition-sparks-angry-police-reaction |archivedate=2015年1月2日 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{citation|url=http://www.shahidulnews.com/drik-photo-power |author=Satish Sharma |date=n.d. |title=Drik: Photo power |accessdate=31 March 2015}}</ref><ref name=Representing>{{citation |title=Representing ‘Crossfire’: politics, art and photography |url=http://www.shahidulnews.com/representing-‘crossfire’-politics-art-and-photography |author=Rahnuma Ahmed |date=n.d. |accessdate=31 March 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150831233702/http://www.shahidulnews.com/representing-%E2%80%98crossfire%E2%80%99-politics-art-and-photography |archive-date=2015-08-31 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。在德里克的律师向政府发出通知后，警方移走了路障。该展览馆在3月31日重新开放，供公众参观<ref name=Representing/>。

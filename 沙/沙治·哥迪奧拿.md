'''沙治哥迪奧拿'''（'''Sergio "Sergi" Guardiola Navarro'''，{{bd|1991年|5月29日||}}）是[[西班牙|西班牙]]的職業足球運動員，司職[[中場|中場]]，現效力於[[西甲|西甲]]球隊[[皇家巴利亞多利德足球俱樂部|華拉度列]]。

== 參賽歷史 ==
2012年8月1日加盟[[翁蒂年特足球俱乐部|翁蒂年特]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ontinyentcf.es/index.php/noticies/1er-equip/85-sergio-guardiola-i-chupi-fitxen-per-lontinyent|title=Sergio Guardiola i Chupi fitxen per l'Ontinyent|trans_title=Sergio Guardiola and Chupi sign for Ontinyent|publisher=Ontinyent CF|language=ca|date=1 August 2012|accessdate=19 June 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160913203036/http://www.ontinyentcf.es/index.php/noticies/1er-equip/85-sergio-guardiola-i-chupi-fitxen-per-lontinyent|archivedate=2016年9月13日}}</ref>2013年1月12日加盟[[赫塔費足球俱樂部|基達菲B隊]] <ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.lasegundab.es/blog/2013/01/12/sergi-guardiola-traspasado-al-getafe-5938.html|title=Sergi Guardiola traspasado al Getafe|trans_title=Sergi Guardiola transferred to Getafe|publisher=La Segunda B|language=es|date=12 January 2013|accessdate=19 June 2015}}</ref>

2015年12月28日，哥迪奧拿簽約[[巴塞罗那足球俱乐部|巴塞隆納]]的[[巴塞隆拿足球會B隊|B隊]]1年半。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sport.es/es/noticias/barca/barcelona-anuncia-tres-refuerzos-para-gerard-lopez-4782102|title=El Barcelona anuncia tres refuerzos para Gerard López|trans_title=Barcelona announces three signings for Gerard López|newspaper=[[每日體育報|Sport]]|location=Barcelona|language=es|date=28 December 2015|accessdate=28 December 2015}}</ref>但是，幾小時後被發現2013年曾在[[Twitter|Twitter]]發表對[[加泰隆尼亞|加泰隆尼亞]]反感的言論而被解約。（[[皇馬|皇馬]]萬歲，X你X的[[加泰隆尼亞|加泰隆尼亞]]，hala madrid puta catalunya）還說「我不會把[[梅西|梅西]]放在自己的球隊中，他在場上散步，影響了整支球隊的比賽」。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sport.es/es/noticias/barca/guardiola-mas-madridista-anticatalan-4782152|title=Sergi Guardiola desata la polémica en las redes por sus tuits|trans_title=Sergi Guardiola unleashes social media controversy due to his tweets|newspaper=Sport|location=Barcelona|language=es|date=28 December 2015|accessdate=28 December 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/35191837|title=Barcelona's Sergi Guardiola sacked after offensive tweets|publisher=[[BBC體育|BBC Sport]]|date=28 December 2015|accessdate=29 December 2015}}</ref> 
他聲稱，他的朋友只是作笑話，並沒有注意到他們已發表。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/dec/29/sergi-guardiola-barcelona-b-cancel-contract-offensive-tweets|title=Barcelona release new signing Sergi Guardiola due to offensive tweets|newspaper=[[衛報|The Guardian]]|location=London|date=28 December 2015|accessdate=29 December 2015}}</ref>

Guardiola was promoted to [[格拉纳达足球俱乐部|Granada CF]]'s first team by new manager [[帕科·赫梅斯|Paco Jémez]] in July 2016, appearing regularly during the pre-season and also being an unused substitute in the opening game of the [[2016–17賽季西班牙足球甲級聯賽|new season]] against [[比利亞雷阿爾足球俱樂部|Villarreal CF]]. However, after the arrival of new signings, he was deemed suplus to requirements and was loaned to [[澳大利亞職業足球聯賽|A-League]] club [[阿德莱德联足球俱乐部|Adelaide United FC]] on 13 September 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.adelaidenow.com.au/sport/football/a-league/adelaide-united-fill-aleague-import-quota-with-signing-of-spanish-striker-sergi-guardiola/news-story/fe0eaac0e720c793c4beeb77d583637d|title=Adelaide United fill A-League import quota with signing of Spanish striker Sergi Guardiola|newspaper=[[廣告人報_(阿德萊德)|The Advertiser]]|date=13 September 2016|accessdate=13 September 2016}}</ref>

On 27 January 2017, Guardiola moved to [[皇家穆爾西亞足球俱樂部|Real Murcia]] also in a temporary deal.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mundodeportivo.com/futbol/20170127/413763359720/el-murcia-logra-del-granada-la-cesion-de-sergi-guardiola.html|title=El Murcia logra del Granada la cesión de Sergi Guardiola|trans_title=Murcia get loan of Sergi Guardiola from Granada|newspaper=Mundo Deportivo|language=es|date=27 January 2017|accessdate=28 January 2017}}</ref>

'''沙姆解放組織'''（{{Lang-ar|هيئة تحرير الشام}}）是[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]中一個活躍的武裝團體，於2017年1月28日由恐怖組織[[征服沙姆陣線|征服沙姆陣線]]與其它一些反政府武裝團體合併而成。<ref name="al-Qaeda latest incarnation">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-38934206|title=Tahrir al-Sham: Al-Qaeda's latest incarnation in Syria|first=B. B. C.|last=Monitoring|date=2017-02-12 |publisher=BBC}}</ref>
沙姆解放組織在成立初期有31,000人兵力，其中約18,000人來自[[征服沙姆陣線|征服沙姆陣線]]。<ref name="surfacing">{{cite web|url=http://english.aawsat.com/2017/01/article55366551/syria-surfacing-haiat-tahrir-al-sham-threatens-truce|script-title=en:Syria: Surfacing of ‘Hai’at Tahrir al-Sham’ Threatens Truce|first=Nazeer|last=Rida|date=2017-01-30|publisher=Asharq Al-Awsat|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170215122504/http://english.aawsat.com/2017/01/article55366551/syria-surfacing-haiat-tahrir-al-sham-threatens-truce|archivedate=2017年2月15日|df=}}</ref>其後有更多團體加入沙姆解放組織，兵力增加至50,000人。<ref name="joins">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/islamist-rebel-group-joins-al-qaeda-franchise-syria/|title=Islamist rebel group joins Al-Qaeda franchise in Syria|first=Chris|last=Tomson|accessdate=2017-05-21 |publisher=Al-Masdar News|date=2017-05-21}}</ref>一些評論員認為沙姆解放組織是現時敘利亞境內單一最大的反巴沙爾政府武裝團體。<ref name="FSA group joins HTS">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/fsa-group-joins-al-qaeda-coalition-making-it-the-largest-militant-group-in-syria/|title=FSA-affiliated group joins Al-Qaeda coalition; making it the largest militant group in Syria|first=Paul|last=Antonopoulos|date=2017-03-21 |publisher=Al-Masdar News}}</ref>

原征服沙姆陣線頭目[[阿布·穆罕默德·約拉尼|阿布·穆罕默德·約拉尼]]擔任沙姆解放組織的軍事領導人。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.longwarjournal.org/archives/2017/02/hayat-tahrir-al-sham-leader-calls-for-unity-in-syrian-insurgency.php|script-title=en:Hay’at Tahrir al Sham leader calls for ‘unity’ in Syrian insurgency|publisher=Long War Journal|date=2017-02-10}}</ref>雖然沙姆解放組織與[[征服沙姆陣線|征服沙姆陣線]]關係密切，但至今[[美國國務院|美國國務院]]尚未把沙姆解放組織列為恐怖組織。<ref name="CBC">[http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/terror-list-omission-1.4114621 Syria's al-Qaeda affiliate escapes from Canada's terror list]. ''CBC News'', 2017-05-14.</ref>

[[伊朗|伊朗]]政府宣稱[[卡塔尔|卡塔尔]]和[[沙特阿拉伯|沙特阿拉伯]]支援沙姆解放組織。<ref>"[http://en.farsnews.com/newstext.aspx?nn=13951121000712 Confrontation between Turkey, Saudi Arabia Root Cause of Tensions among Terrorist Groups in Syria]". ''Fars News Agency''. 2017-02-09.</ref>

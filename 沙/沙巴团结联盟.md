'''沙巴团结联盟'''（{{Lang-ms|Gabungan Bersatu Sabah}}）是于[[2018年马来西亚大选|2018年马来西亚大选]][[国民阵线|国民阵线]]败选后，由[[百林吉丁岸|百林吉丁岸]]领导沙巴州数个反对党组合而成的一个政治联盟。新组织的联盟成员包括[[沙巴团结党|沙巴团结党]]（PBS）、 [[沙巴立新党|沙巴立新党]]（STAR）和预留给国阵的其他成员，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nst.com.my/news/politics/2018/05/368753/sabah-bn-coalition-be-disbanded-pave-way-gabungan-bersatu|title=Sabah BN coalition to be disbanded to pave way for Gabungan Bersatu|author=Kristy Inus|publisher=New Straits Times|date=12 May 2018|accessdate=13 May 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://sg.news.yahoo.com/sabah-bn-quit-coalition-platform-100524590.html|title=Sabah BN to quit coalition for new platform|author=Julia Chan|work=The Malay Mail|publisher=Yahoo! News Singapore|date=12 May 2018|accessdate=13 May 2018}}</ref>并期望由[[慕沙阿曼|慕沙阿曼]]在沙巴州领导的巫统成员能加入沙巴团结党。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2018/05/12/pbs-leaves-bn-musa-aman-to-join-pbs/|title=PBS leaves BN, Musa Aman to leave Umno and join PBS|author1=Stephanie Lee|author2=Fatimah Zainal|publisher=The Star|date=12 May 2018|accessdate=13 May 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2018/05/12/sabah-umno-members-expected-to-quit-and-join-pbs-to-be-more-local/|title=Sabah Umno members expected to quit and join PBS to be more 'local'|author1=Muguntan Vanar|author2=Philip Golingai|author3=Stephanie Lee|author4=Fatimah Zainal|author5=Natasha Joibi|publisher=The Star|date=12 May 2018|accessdate=13 May 2018}}</ref>但时至今日，沙巴州国阵成员仍然未确认加入沙巴团结党。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailyexpress.com.my/news.cfm?NewsID=125121|title=Gabungan Sabah to be spearheaded by PBS: Max|publisher=Daily Express|date=13 June 2018|accessdate=13 June 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180613131537/http://www.dailyexpress.com.my/news.cfm?NewsID=125121|archivedate=13 June 2018|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailyexpress.com.my/news.cfm?NewsID=125108|title=Gabungan Sabah awaits Musa's nod|publisher=Daily Express|date=13 June 2018|accessdate=14 June 2018|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180614061634/http://www.dailyexpress.com.my/news.cfm?NewsID=125108|archivedate=14 June 2018|deadurl=yes}}</ref>

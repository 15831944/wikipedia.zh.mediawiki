'''沙棘'''（學名：'''''Hippophae rhamnoides'''''）是[[沙棘屬|沙棘屬]]的一種帶有棘刺的落葉灌木，原產於亞洲和歐洲的溫帶、寒溫帶地區<ref name="Li" />。沙棘可防風固沙，其果實可以食用或用來製作飲料，此外沙棘果在美容和藥用上也有價值。

== 形態 ==
[[File:Seabuckthorn_berries,_Nubra_valley,_Ladakh.jpg|thumb]]

作為一種落葉灌木，沙棘的高度在二米至四米之間<ref name="Li" />，枝幹粗糙、外皮呈黑色<ref name="Li" />，葉披針形<ref name="Synge">{{cite book|last1=Synge|first1=PM|title=Dictionary of gardening: A practical and scientific encyclopaedia of horticulture|date=1974|publisher=Clarendon Press|location=Oxford|edition=2|isbn=978-0198691068}}</ref>。在種下三年後開花<ref name="Foldesi">{{cite journal|last1=Bernáth|first1=J.|last2=Földesi|first2=D.|title=Sea Buckthorn (Hippophae rhamnoides L.): A Promising New Medicinal and Food Crop|journal=Journal of Herbs, Spices & Medicinal Plants|date=1992|volume=1|issue=1–2|pages=27–35|doi=10.1300/J044v01n01_04}}</ref>，雌雄異株，通過風來傳播種子<ref name="Li">{{cite journal|last1=Li|first1=TSC|last2=Schroeder|first2=WR|title=Sea Buckthorn (Hippophae rhamnoides L.): A Multipurpose Plant|journal=HortTechnology|date=1996|volume=6|issue=4|pages=370–380|url=http://horttech.ashspublications.org/content/6/4/370.full.pdf}}</ref>。沙棘的根系十分發達，其根與[[弗兰克氏菌属|弗兰克氏菌]]共生<ref name="Lu">{{cite journal|last1=Lu|first1=R|title=Sea buckthorn: A multipurpose plant species for fragile mountains|journal=ICIMOD Occasional Paper No. 20|date=1992|location=International Centre for Integrated Mountain Development, Katmandu, Nepal|url=http://lib.icimod.org/record/24761/files/SEBUCKTHORN%20A%20Multipurpose%20plant%20species%20for%20fraglle%20mountains.pdf}} Index page from publisher is [http://lib.icimod.org/record/24761 here].</ref>。

單個果實重量在270至480毫克之間，富含維生素C、E、[[維生素B12|B12]]<ref name="Stobdan">{{cite journal|last1=Stobdan|first1=T|last2=Chaurasia|first2=OP|last3=Korekar|first3=G|last4=Mundra|first4=S|last5=Ali|first5=Z|last6=Yadav|first6=A|last7=Singh|first7=SB|title=Attributes of Seabuckthorn (Hippophae rhamnoides L.) to Meet Nutritional Requirements in High Altitude|journal=Defence Science Journal|date=2010|volume=60|issue=2|pages=226–230|doi=10.14429/dsj.60.344|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/232289197_Attributes_of_Seabuckthorn_Hippophae_rhamnoides_L_to_Meet_Nutritional_Requirements_in_High_Altitude}}</ref>和類胡蘿蔔素、類黃酮和脂肪酸<ref name="Bal">{{cite journal|last1=Bal|first1=LM|last2=Meda|first2=V|last3=Naik|first3=SN|last4=Satya|first4=S|title=Sea buckthorn berries: A potential source of valuable nutrients for nutraceuticals and cosmoceuticals|journal=Food Research International|date=2011|volume=44|issue=7|pages=1718–1727|doi=10.1016/j.foodres.2011.03.002}}</ref>。

'''沙赫巴兹'''（{{lang-fa|شاه‌باز}}）是波斯神话中的一只神鸟。外表是[[雕|雕]]，体型略大于[[鹰|鹰]]或[[隼|隼]]。在波斯前伊斯兰时代，沙赫巴兹是波斯的国家象征、百鸟之王。<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Persian Night: Iran under the Khomeinist Revolution|last=Taheri|first=Amir|publisher=Encounter Books|year=2010|isbn=9781594035524|location=|pages=1970}}</ref>据希腊史家[[色诺芬|色诺芬]]的叙述，这只鹰被置于[[阿契美尼德王朝|阿契美尼德王朝]]皇家纹章之上，脚踩一支木杆或长枪。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5HulCgAAQBAJ&pg=PT152&dq=Achaemenid+eagle&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiahLTWwIXfAhWMEbwKHcvsBmIQ6AEIQjAD#v=onepage&q=Achaemenid%20eagle&f=false|title=Persian Art|last=Lukonin|first=Vladimir|publisher=Parkstone International|year=2015|isbn=9781783107964|location=|pages=}}</ref>作为波斯王权的象征，它在波斯历史和传说中有着重要的地位。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=8q-oAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA121&dq=Persian+eagle&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwj94KTNwoXfAhUHOrwKHVWhCbsQ6AEIKTAA#v=onepage&q=Persian%20eagle&f=false|title=Focusing Biblical Studies: The Crucial Nature of the Persian and Hellenistic Periods: Essays in Honor of Douglas A. Knight|last=|first=|publisher=Bloomsbury Publishing USA|year=2012|isbn=9780567369079|editor-last=Berquist|editor-first=Jon L.|location=|pages=121}}</ref>英国探险家[[理查德·弗朗西斯·伯顿|理查德·弗朗西斯·伯顿]]推测沙赫巴兹应该是一只[[蒼鷹|苍鹰]]<ref name="Burton">{{cite book|title=Falconry in the valley of the Indus|last=[[Richard_Francis_Burton|Burton]]|first=Sir Richard Francis|year=1852}}</ref>

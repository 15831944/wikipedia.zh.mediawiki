'''沙拉工坊'''（{{lang-en|Saladworks}}）是一家主要販售[[沙律|沙律]]、[[麵包|麵包]]、湯和[[三明治|三明治]]的加盟連鎖餐館<ref>{{cite web | title=Central Delaware Chamber of Commerce joins Saladworks for ribbon cutting | website=Dover Post | date=2017-11-16 | url=http://www.doverpost.com/news/20171116/central-delaware-chamber-of-commerce-joins-saladworks-for-ribbon-cutting | accessdate=2017-12-10}}</ref>。沙拉工坊被歸類為快速休閒餐館，並將其菜單作為其他[[快餐|快餐]]的健康替代品銷售<ref>{{cite book | title=Prepared Foods | publisher=Gorman Publishing Company | year=2008 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=rwEKAQAAMAAJ | accessdate=2017-12-10 | page=61}}</ref>。

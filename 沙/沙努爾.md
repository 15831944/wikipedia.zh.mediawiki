'''沙努爾'''（印尼文：Pantai Sanur）是[[巴厘岛|巴厘岛]]東南邊[[丹帕沙|丹帕沙]]市延伸的海灘，距[[伍拉·賴國際機場|伍拉·賴國際機場]]車程約30分鐘，當地已發展出小的村落<!--which has grown into a little town in its own right.-->。沙努爾海岸中，從Matahari Terbit海灘到Mertasari海灘之間5.1公里的區域，已在2008年被收回<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.thejakartapost.com/bali-daily/2013-04-03/unclear-beach-rules-causing-disarray.html |title=Unclear beach rules causing disarray |last=Winarti |first=Agnes |work=雅加达邮报 |date=April 3, 2013 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130406042248/http://www.thejakartapost.com/bali-daily/2013-04-03/unclear-beach-rules-causing-disarray.html |archivedate=April 6, 2013 |dead-url=yes |accessdate=June 24, 2016}}</ref>。

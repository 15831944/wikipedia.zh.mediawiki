'''肥胖症'''（{{lang|en|Obesity}}）是指體脂肪累積過多而對健康造成負面影響的身體狀態，可能導致壽命減短及各種健康問題<ref name="WHO 2000">{{cite book|author=World Health Organization (WHO) |title=Technical report series 894: Obesity: Preventing and managing the global epidemic. |location=Geneva |publisher=World Health Organization |year=2000 |url=http://www.who.int/nutrition/publications/obesity/WHO_TRS_894/en/ |format=PDF |isbn=92-4-120894-5}}</ref>{{rp|9}}。肥胖的標準常使用[[身體質量指數|身體質量指數]]（{{lang|en|BMI}}）來衡量，即以體重（公斤）除以身高（公尺）的平方<ref name="WHO 2000"/>{{rp|6}}<ref name=HaslamJames/> 。西方人認為BMI大於{{val|30|ul=kg/m<sup>2</sup>}} 即為肥胖，介於25到{{val|30|ul=kg/m<sup>2</sup>}}間則為過重；一些东亚国家採用更严格的标准，例如中華民國[[行政院衛生署|行政院衛生署]]（今[[衛生福利部|衛生福利部]]）於2002年4月公布成人的BMI≧27 即為肥胖，24≦{{lang|en|BMI}}＜27 則為過重<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://health99.hpa.gov.tw/OnlinkHealth/Onlink_BMI.aspx|title = BMI 測試|accessdate = 2015-11-19|author = |date = |publisher = 衛生福利部國民健康署}}</ref>。但幼兒並不適合用成人的{{lang|en|BMI}}標準來評量<ref>{{cite book|author=Peter G. Kopelman, Ian D. Caterson, Michael J. Stock, William H. Dietz |title=Clinical obesity in adults and children: In Adults and Children |publisher=Blackwell Publishing |location= |year=2005 |pages=12–28 |isbn=1-4051-1672-2}}</ref>。

肥胖會增加[[心血管疾病|心血管疾病]]、[[2型糖尿病|第二型糖尿病]]、[[睡眠呼吸中止症|睡眠呼吸中止症]]、某些[[癌症|癌症]]、[[退化性關節炎|退化性關節炎]]及其他疾病的發生機會<ref name=HaslamJames/>。而造成肥胖的主因常包括[[熱量|熱量]]攝取過多、欠缺[[運動|運動]]及體質問題等，其他如基因缺陷、內分泌異常、藥物影響及精神疾病也可能造成肥胖。有種說法認為「肥胖的人由於[[代謝|代謝]]慢，因此即使吃得不多也會越來越胖」，但目前的科學證據傾向不支持此種論點，因為肥胖的人必須花更多能量維持較重的體重，所以他們的代謝率反而高於常人<ref>{{cite book|author=Kushner, Robert |title=Treatment of the Obese Patient (Contemporary Endocrinology) |publisher=Humana Press |location=Totowa, NJ |year=2007 |page=158 |isbn=1-59745-400-1 |url=https://books.google.com/?id=vWjK5etS7PMC |doi= |accessdate=2009-04-05}}</ref><ref name=Anes2000>{{cite journal | author = Adams JP, Murphy PG | title = Obesity in anaesthesia and intensive care | journal = Br J Anaesth | volume = 85 | issue = 1 | pages = 91–108 | date = 2000-07 | pmid = 10927998 | doi = 10.1093/bja/85.1.91 | url = http://bja.oxfordjournals.org/cgi/content/full/85/1/91 }}</ref>。

肥胖的主要治療方式有[[節食|飲食計畫]]和[[體能鍛煉|運動]]。患者在日常飲食中必須避免高熱量（高油高糖）食物並增加高[[膳食纖維|纖]]食物，若良好的飲食控制無法有效減重，則可以考慮搭配{{le|抗肥胖藥物|Anti-obesity medication}}來減低食慾和抑制脂肪吸收。如果飲食、運動、甚至搭配藥物都不見效，用來減少胃容積的[[胃內水球置放術|胃內水球置放術]]可能會有幫助，以[[手術|手術]]來減少胃容積或腸道長度也能直接降低食量並減少營養素的吸收<ref>NICE 2006 p.10–11</ref><ref name=balloon2008>{{cite journal | author = Imaz I, Martínez-Cervell C, García-Alvarez EE, Sendra-Gutiérrez JM, González-Enríquez J | title = Safety and effectiveness of the intragastric balloon for obesity. A meta-analysis | journal = Obes Surg | volume = 18 | issue = 7 | pages = 841–6 | date = 2008-07 | pmid = 18459025 | doi = 10.1007/s11695-007-9331-8 }}</ref>。

肥胖是一種很常見的{{le|可預防死因|Preventable causes of death}}<ref name=WHO2015>{{cite web|title=Obesity and overweight  Fact sheet N°311|url=http://www.who.int/mediacentre/factsheets/fs311/en/|website=WHO|accessdate=2 February 2016|date=January 2015}}</ref>，也是21世紀最重要的[[公共衛生|公共衛生]]問題之一<ref name=Dibaise2013>{{cite journal|author=Dibaise JK, Foxx-Orenstein AE|title=Role of the gastroenterologist in managing obesity|journal=Expert Review of Gastroenterology & Hepatology|volume=7|issue=5|pages=439-51|date=2013-07|pmid=23899283|doi=10.1586/17474124.2013.811061|type=Review}}</ref>。目前成人與[[兒童|兒童]]的肥胖盛行率都在上升，且女性較男性更常發生<ref name=WHO2015 />。2014年，全球有6億名成人（13%）和4200萬名五歲以下的孩童有肥胖問題<ref name=WHO2015 />。歷史上，常視肥胖為財富與多產的象徵，部分國家現今仍保有這樣的看法；然而在現代社會中（尤其是[[西方世界|西方國家]]），肥胖已經受到汙名化<ref name=HaslamJames/><ref name=Woodhouse/>。2013年，[[美國醫學會|美國醫學會]]將肥胖定義為一種疾病<ref name=NYTimes20130618>{{cite news |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/06/19/business/ama-recognizes-obesity-as-a-disease.html?_r=0 |title=A.M.A. Recognizes Obesity as a Disease |last=Pollack |first=Andrew |date=2013-06-18 |newspaper=New York Times |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6Hav05TK0?url=http://www.nytimes.com/2013/06/19/business/ama-recognizes-obesity-as-a-disease.html?_r=1 |archivedate=2013-06-23 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hhnmag.com/hhnmag/HHNDaily/HHNDailyDisplay.dhtml?id=5870001020 |title=The Facts About Obesity |last1=Weinstock |first1=Matthew |date=2013-06-21 |website=H&HN |publisher=American Hospital Association |accessdate=2013-06-24}}</ref>。

==定義==
[[File:Obesity_&_BMI.png|thumb]]
[[File:Obesity6.JPG|thumb]]
肥胖指的是體脂肪過度堆積而對健康造成負面影響的身體狀態<ref name="WHO 2000"/>{{rp|6}}。肥胖最初的定義以[[身體質量指數|身體質量指數]]（{{lang|en|Body Mass Index, BMI}}）為指標，之後也加入了其他標準，例如評估[[腰臀比|腰臀比]]或其他心血管病症風險因子<ref>{{cite journal | author = Sweeting HN | title = Measurement and Definitions of Obesity In Childhood and Adolescence: A field guide for the uninitiated | journal = Nutr J | volume = 6 | issue = 1 | page = 32 | year = 2007 | pmid = 17963490 | pmc = 2164947 | doi = 10.1186/1475-2891-6-32 | url = http://www.nutritionj.com/content/6/1/32 }}</ref><ref name="nhlbi"/>{{rp|14}}。BMI與[[體脂肪率|體脂肪率]]和體脂肪總量都密切相關<ref>{{cite journal | author = Gray DS, Fujioka K | title = Use of relative weight and Body Mass Index for the determination of adiposity | journal = J Clin Epidemiol | volume = 44 | issue = 6 | pages = 545–50 | year = 1991 | pmid = 2037859 | doi = 10.1016/0895-4356(91)90218-X }}</ref>。兒童的健康體重因性別與年齡而有不同的標準，此標準需透過與同年齡性別的其他孩子（常模）比較來決定。舉例而言，兒童與青少年的肥胖定義為BMI高於95%的同儕（95[[百分位數|百分位]]以上）<ref name="cdc.gov">{{cite web|url=http://www.cdc.gov/nccdphp/dnpa/healthyweight/assessing/bmi/childrens_BMI/about_childrens_BMI.htm |title=Healthy Weight: Assessing Your Weight: BMI: About BMI for Children and Teens |publisher=Center for disease control and prevention |accessdate=2009-04-06}}</ref>。用於決定兒童與青少年肥胖與否的常模資料來自1963年至1994年，因此並沒有反映近年平均體重的上升<ref name="Flegal KM, Ogden CL, Wei R, Kuczmarski RL, Johnson CL 2001 1086–93">{{cite journal | author = Flegal KM, Ogden CL, Wei R, Kuczmarski RL, Johnson CL | title = Prevalence of overweight in US children: comparison of US growth charts from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention with other reference values for body mass index | journal = Am. J. Clin. Nutr. | volume = 73 | issue = 6 | pages = 1086–93 | date = 2001-06 | pmid = 11382664 | doi =  | url = http://www.ajcn.org/cgi/content/full/73/6/1086 }}</ref>。

===身體質量指數（BMI）===
BMI定義為體重（公斤）除以身高的平方（公尺）：
:<math>\mathrm{BMI}= \frac{m}{h^2}</math>
式子中的m是體重，h是身高。BMI通常以公斤/平方公尺為單位，若以英制單位（磅與吋）計算，則應該再乘以703（公斤/平方公尺）/（磅/平方吋）。

最常用的肥胖標準由[[世界衛生組織|世界衛生組織]]於1997年所制訂，並於2000年發表，詳細標準列於左方表格<ref name="WHO 2000"/>{{rp|9}}。

有些組織則對世界衛生組織的肥胖標準做了一些修正，比方說外科文獻上通常將「第三級肥胖」再細分如下，但細分的標準目前還沒有定論<ref name=morbid2007>{{cite journal | author = Sturm R | title = Increases in morbid obesity in the USA: 2000–2005 | journal = Public Health | volume = 121 | issue = 7 | pages = 492–6 | date = 2007-07 | pmid = 17399752 | pmc = 2864630 | doi = 10.1016/j.puhe.2007.01.006 }}</ref>。
* BMI大於35或40者為嚴重肥胖。
* BMI大於35並患有肥胖相關健康問題者，或是BMI介於40-44.9公斤/平方公尺者為病態肥胖。
* BMI大於45或50者為極度肥胖。

由於亞洲人相對於[[高加索人種|高加索人種]]而言，在較低的BMI就可能出現健康問題，因此部分國家和地区據此修正國內的肥胖定義。日本將BMI大於25公斤/平方公尺定義為肥胖<ref>{{cite journal | author = Kanazawa M, Yoshiike N, Osaka T, Numba Y, Zimmet P, Inoue S | title = Criteria and classification of obesity in Japan and Asia-Oceania | journal = Asia Pac J Clin Nutr | volume = 11 Suppl 8 | issue =  | pages = S732–S737 | date = 2002-12 | pmid = 12534701 | doi = 10.1046/j.1440-6047.11.s8.19.x }}</ref>；中國為大於28公斤/平方公尺<ref>{{cite journal | author = Bei-Fan Z | title = Predictive values of body mass index and waist circumference for risk factors of certain related diseases in Chinese adults: study on optimal cut-off points of body mass index and waist circumference in Chinese adults | journal = Asia Pac J Clin Nutr | volume = 11 Suppl 8 | issue =  | pages = S685–93 | date = 2002-12 | pmid = 12534691 | doi = 10.1046/j.1440-6047.11.s8.9.x }}</ref>；台灣則為大於27公斤/平方公尺<ref>{{cite web|url=http://obesity.hpa.gov.tw/TC/downloadList.aspx?cid=82 |title=國民健康署肥胖防治網成人健康體重對照表 |publisher=Obesity.hpa.gov.tw |date=2015-12-17 |accessdate=2016-01-21}}</ref>。

===其他判斷肥胖的方式===
[[腰臀比|腰臀比]]也是一種用來判斷肥胖的標準，根據世衛組織的定義，男性大於 0.9m 或女性大於 0.85m 即屬肥胖<ref name=WHO2008>{{cite web|url=http://whqlibdoc.who.int/publications/2011/9789241501491_eng.pdf|title=Waist Circumference and Waist-Hip Ratio  |work=World Health Organization |accessdate=2016-09-03}}</ref>。<BR>有研究指出，腰臀比能比BMI更準確地預測心血管疾病風險<ref name="MørkedalRomundstad2011">{{cite journal |vauthors=Mørkedal B, Romundstad PR, Vatten LJ | title = Informativeness of indices of blood pressure, obesity and serum lipids in relation to ischaemic heart disease mortality: the HUNT-II study | journal = European Journal of Epidemiology | volume = 26 | issue = 6 | pages = 457–461 | year = 2011 | pmid = 21461943 | pmc = 3115050 | doi = 10.1007/s10654-011-9572-7 | issn = 0393-2990 }}</ref>；且相較於腰圍或BMI，腰臀比和老人（大於75歲）的死亡率關係更為密切<ref>{{cite journal | author = Price GM, Uauy R, Breeze E, Bulpitt CJ, Fletcher AE | title = Weight, shape, and mortality risk in older persons: elevated waist-hip ratio, not high body mass index, is associated with a greater risk of death | journal = Am. J. Clin. Nutr. | volume = 84 | issue = 2 | pages = 449–60 | date = 2006-08 | pmid = 16895897 | laysummary = http://www.medicalnewstoday.com/medicalnews.php?newsid=49218 }}</ref>。

[[File:FatCT2008.JPG|thumb]]。右侧是一名肥胖症患者的腹部CT，请注意其皮下脂肪厚度为36mm。]]
[[體脂肪率|體脂肪率]]則是脂肪含量占总体重的百分比，一般认为男性体脂大於25%或女性大於33%即為肥胖<ref name=emedicine>
{{cite web |url=http://www.emedicine.com/med/TOPIC1653.HTM |author=Schwarz, Steven| title=Obesity |accessdate=2008-09-30 |work= |publisher=emedicine |date=2007-11-01}}</ref>。體脂肪率可通过以下公式用BMI的数值进行计算：

:体脂<math>\% = 1.2 \times BMI + 0.23 \times</math>年龄<math> - 5.4 - 10.8 \times</math>性别

其中男性性别取值为1，女性取0。该公式考虑到在相等BMI的情形下，男性体脂含量比后者低約10%；且即使體重不變，體脂肪率也會隨著年齡增长。<ref name=Kopelman2005 />

== 肥胖症对健康的影响 ==

體重過重與許多疾病的發生相關，包括[[心血管疾病|心血管疾病]]、[[第二型糖尿病|第二型糖尿病]]、[[睡眠呼吸中止症|睡眠呼吸中止症]]、某些[[癌|癌]]症、[[退化性關節炎|退化性關節炎]]<ref name=HaslamJames/>、以及[[氣喘|氣喘]]<ref name=HaslamJames/><ref name=Poulain/>，由於上述疾病的緣故，研究也顯示肥胖者的預期餘命較短<ref name=HaslamJames/>。

=== 致病性 ===
肥胖會使許多生理與心理疾病的風險上升，疾病與肥胖的共存關係展現在[[代謝症候群|代謝症候群]]上<ref name=HaslamJames/>，代謝症候群包括[[第二型糖尿病|第二型糖尿病]]、[[高血壓|高血壓]]、血中膽固醇過高、血中三酸甘油酯過高等<ref>{{cite journal | author = Grundy SM | title = Obesity, metabolic syndrome, and cardiovascular disease | journal = J. Clin. Endocrinol. Metab. | volume = 89 | issue = 6 | pages = 2595–600 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15181029 | doi = 10.1210/jc.2004-0372 }}</ref>。

肥胖的併發症有可能是直接由肥胖造成，或者是與肥胖有共同的原因：比如不健康的飲食或是少動的生活型態（[[坐式生活型態|坐式生活型態]]）。肥胖與不同疾病的關聯性強弱不一，如[[第二型糖尿病|第二型糖尿病]]和肥胖高度相關，64%男性糖尿病患與77%的女性糖尿病患都有體脂肪過度堆積的現象。<ref name = "Kopelman2005"/>{{rp|9}}。

肥胖對健康的影響可分為兩大類：第一是體脂肪量增加造成的影響（包括[[退化性關節炎|退化性關節炎]]、[[睡眠呼吸中止症|睡眠呼吸中止症]]、社交汙名化等）；第二是體內脂肪細胞增加造成的影響（包括[[第二型糖尿病|第二型糖尿病]]、[[癌|癌症]]、[[心血管疾病|心血管疾病]]、非酒精性脂肪肝等）<ref name=HaslamJames/><ref name=Bray2004>{{cite journal | author = Bray GA | title = Medical consequences of obesity | journal = J. Clin. Endocrinol. Metab. | volume = 89 | issue = 6 | pages = 2583–9 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15181027 | doi = 10.1210/jc.2004-0535 }}</ref>。體脂肪的增加會改變人體對[[胰島素|胰島素]]的反應，造成[[胰島素抵抗|胰島素阻抗]]；高體脂也會使身體較容易產生發炎反應<ref>{{cite journal | author = Shoelson SE, Herrero L, Naaz A | title = Obesity, inflammation, and insulin resistance | journal = Gastroenterology | volume = 132 | issue = 6 | pages = 2169–80 | date = 2007-05 | pmid = 17498510 | doi = 10.1053/j.gastro.2007.03.059 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Shoelson SE, Lee J, Goldfine AB | title = Inflammation and insulin resistance | journal = J. Clin. Invest. | volume = 116 | issue = 7 | pages = 1793–801 | date = 2006-07 | pmid = 16823477 | pmc = 1483173 | doi = 10.1172/JCI29069 | url = http://www.jci.org/articles/view/29069 }}</ref> ，並且更容易形成[[血栓|血栓]]<ref name=Bray2004/><ref>{{cite journal | author = Dentali F, Squizzato A, Ageno W | title = The metabolic syndrome as a risk factor for venous and arterial thrombosis | journal = Semin. ===生存矛盾===
雖然有大量證據支持一般人的肥胖會對健康造成負面影響，但在部分族群中BMI的上升卻似乎對健康有幫助。這個現象稱為「肥胖生存矛盾」<ref name=Schmidt2007>{{cite journal | author = Schmidt DS, Salahudeen AK | title = Obesity-survival paradox-still a controversy? | journal = Semin Dial | volume = 20 | issue = 6 | pages = 486–92 | year = 2007 | pmid = 17991192 | doi = 10.1111/j.1525-139X.2007.00349.x | type = Review }}</ref>，1999年時，此現象首次在過重與肥胖的[[血液透析|血液透析]]患者身上發現<ref name=Schmidt2007/>，稍後也在[[心衰竭|心衰竭]]與[[周邊血管疾病|周邊血管疾病]]的患者身上發現<ref name=paradox2003>{{cite journal | author = U.S. Preventive Services Task Force | title = Behavioral counseling in primary care to promote a healthy diet: recommendations and rationale | journal = Am Fam Physician | volume = 67 | issue = 12 | pages = 2573–6 | date = 2003-06 | pmid = 12825847 | doi = | type = Review }}</ref>。

對於患有[[心衰竭|心衰竭]]的人而言，BMI界於30.0-34.9間的人死亡率比正常體重者來得低，心衰竭患者在病情惡化時伴隨的體重減輕或許能解釋此一現象<ref>{{cite journal | author = Habbu A, Lakkis NM, Dokainish H | title = The obesity paradox: Fact or fiction? | journal = Am. J. Cardiol. | volume = 98 | issue = 7 | pages = 944–8 | date = 2006-10 | pmid = 16996880 | doi = 10.1016/j.amjcard.2006.04.039 | type = Review }}</ref>，其他的心臟疾病也能發現類似的情形。一級肥胖的心臟病患者在未來發生其他心臟問題的機會，並不會比體重正常的心臟病患者來得高；但對於肥胖程度更高的患者來說，未來發生其他心血管問題的機會就會開始上升<ref>{{cite journal | author = Romero-Corral A, Montori VM, Somers VK, Korinek J, Thomas RJ, Allison TG, Mookadam F, Lopez-Jimenez F | title = Association of bodyweight with total mortality and with cardiovascular events in coronary artery disease: A systematic review of cohort studies | journal = Lancet | volume = 368 | issue = 9536 | pages = 666–78 | year = 2006 | pmid = 16920472 | doi = 10.1016/S0140-6736(06)69251-9 | type = Review }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Oreopoulos A, Padwal R, Kalantar-Zadeh K, Fonarow GC, Norris CM, McAlister FA | title = Body mass index and mortality in heart failure: A meta-analysis | journal = Am. Heart J. | volume = 156 | issue = 1 | pages = 13–22 | date = 2008-07 | pmid = 18585492 | doi = 10.1016/j.ahj.2008.02.014 | url = | type = Meta-analysis, Review }}</ref>。即使對接受[[心血管繞道手術|心血管繞道手術]]的患者而言，肥胖與過重的患者死亡率相對於一般人也沒有上升<ref>{{cite journal | author = Oreopoulos A, Padwal R, Norris CM, Mullen JC, Pretorius V, Kalantar-Zadeh K | title = Effect of obesity on short- and long-term mortality postcoronary revascularization: A meta-analysis | journal = Obesity (Silver Spring) | volume = 16 | issue = 2 | pages = 442–50 | date = 2008-02 | pmid = 18239657 | doi = 10.1038/oby.2007.36 | type = Meta-analysis }}</ref>。研究指出，肥胖與過重的患者預後較預期良好是由於它們在心臟病發作後會得到較積極的治療<ref>{{cite journal | author = Diercks DB, Roe MT, Mulgund J, Pollack CV, Kirk JD, Gibler WB, Ohman EM, Smith SC, Boden WE, Peterson ED | title = The obesity paradox in non-ST-segment elevation acute coronary syndromes: Results from the Can Rapid risk stratification of Unstable angina patients Suppress ADverse outcomes with Early implementation of the American College of Cardiology/American Heart Association Guidelines Quality Improvement Initiative | journal = Am Heart J | volume = 152 | issue = 1 | pages = 140–8 | date = 2006-07 | pmid = 16824844 | doi = 10.1016/j.ahj.2005.09.024 | type = Research Support }}</ref>，也有研究指出若[[周邊血管疾病|周邊血管疾病]]患者同時又患有[[慢性阻塞性肺病|慢性阻塞性肺病]]，那肥胖對預後的改善就不復存在<ref name=paradox2003/>。

== 肥胖症的病因 ==
大多数的学者认为过多的能量摄入和坐式生活方式是导致肥胖症的主要原因。<ref>{{cite book | author=Sara Bleich, David Cutler, Christopher Murray, Alyce Adams | title=Working paper 12954: Why is the developed world obese? | publisher=National Bureau of Economic Research | date=2007-03 | url=http://www.nber.org/papers/w12954}}</ref>少数情况下，食物过量摄入与遗传、生理和心理疾病有关，<ref name=Pool2001>{{cite book |author=Pool, Robert |title=Fat: Fighting the Obesity Epidemic |publisher=Oxford University Press |location=Oxford, UK |year=2001 |pages= |isbn=0-19-511853-7}}</ref>当然也有部分原因來自文化差异和工業化大量生产。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Nestle M, Jacobson MF |title=Halting the obesity epidemic: A public health policy approach |journal=Public Health Rep |volume=115 |issue=1 |pages=12–24 |year=2000 |pmid=10968581 |pmc=1308552 |doi= 10.1093/phr/115.1.12|url=}}</ref><ref name=James2008/>

就個人層面來說，[[食物熱量|熱量]]攝取過多與[[體能鍛煉|運動]]不足可以解釋大多數肥胖者的發胖<ref name="CADG2006">{{cite journal|title=2006 Canadian clinical practice guidelines on the management and prevention of obesity in adults and children summary|author=Lau DC, Douketis JD, Morrison KM, Hramiak IM, Sharma AM, Ur E|url=http://www.cmaj.ca/cgi/content/full/176/8/S1|date=2007-04|journal=CMAJ|issue=8|doi=10.1503/cmaj.061409|volume=176|pages=S1–13|type=Practice Guideline, Review|pmc=1839777|pmid=17420481}}</ref>，但仍有一小部分肥胖者是基因、醫療、或精神疾病所造成的<ref>{{cite journal | author = Bleich S, Cutler D, Murray C, Adams A | title = Why is the developed world obese? | journal = Annu Rev Public Health | volume = 29 | pages = 273–95 | year = 2008 | pmid = 18173389 | doi = 10.1146/annurev.publhealth.29.020907.090954 | type = Research Support }}</ref>。另一方面，社群中整體肥胖率的上升則歸因於食物取得變得容易、食物變得更為可口<ref>{{cite journal | author = Drewnowski A, Specter SE | title = Poverty and obesity: the role of energy density and energy costs | journal = Am. J. Clin. Nutr. | volume = 79 | issue = 1 | pages = 6–16 | date = 2004-01 | pmid = 14684391 | doi =  | url = http://www.ajcn.org/cgi/content/full/79/1/6 | type = Review }}</ref>、以及車輛與機械化生產的普及<ref>{{cite journal | author = Nestle M, Jacobson MF | title = Halting the obesity epidemic: a public health policy approach | journal = Public Health Rep | volume = 115 | issue = 1 | pages = 12–24 | year = 2000 | pmid = 10968581 | pmc = 1308552 | doi = 10.1093/phr/115.1.12 | url = | type = Research Support }}</ref><ref name=James2008>{{cite journal | author = James WP | title = The fundamental drivers of the obesity epidemic | journal = Obes Rev | volume = 9 | issue = Suppl 1 | pages = 6–13 | date = 2008-03 | pmid = 18307693 | doi = 10.1111/j.1467-789X.2007.00432.x | type = Review }}</ref>。

2006年发表的一篇综述总结了10种导致肥胖症[[发病率|发病率]]和[[患病率|患病率]]增高的其他原因：
# [[睡眠|睡眠]]不足
# [[激素样活性物质|内分泌干扰物]]（能影响[[脂肪酸代谢|脂肪酸代谢]]的環境汙染物）
# 生活環境的溫度變化率下降
# 吸菸率下降（吸菸會抑制食慾）
# 造成體重上升的藥物使用率增加（例如第二代[[抗精神病藥|抗精神病藥]]）
# 人口組成的改變（平均體重較高的年齡層或種族佔的比例增加）
# 懷孕年齡的延後（可能造成兒童更容易肥胖）
# 促使發胖的[[表徵遺傳學|表徵遺傳]]因子隨世代累積
# BMI高的人在[[自然选择|自然选择]]过程中具有一定的优势
# [[选择性配对|选择性配对]]，相同体重等级的人容易相互认识（這會使肥胖與消瘦的人數都上升，整體的體重變異數也會上升）<ref name="pmid16801930">{{cite journal |author=Keith SW, Redden DT, Katzmarzyk PT, ''et al.'' |title=Putative contributors to the secular increase in obesity: Exploring the roads less traveled |journal=Int J Obes (Lond) |volume=30 |issue=11 |pages=1585–94 |year=2006 |pmid=16801930 |doi=10.1038/sj.ijo.0803326 |url=http://www.nature.com/ijo/journal/v30/n11/full/0803326a.html}}</ref>。

雖然有許多證據支持這些原因在肥胖發生過程中扮演的角色，但目前仍缺乏決定性的證據斷言它們和肥胖症的關聯，而作者也認為這些原因對肥胖的影響可能也小於上段所談的其他因素<ref name="pmid16801930"/>。

|url=http://www.cdc.gov/nccdphp/dnpa/nutrition/nutrition_for_everyone |accessdate=2008-07-09}}</ref>，但飲食過量與飲食內容不健康的問題卻未因此改善<ref>{{cite journal | author = Marantz PR, Bird ED, Alderman MH | title = A }}</ref>，多出來的[[碳水化合物|碳水化合物]]主要來自含糖飲料和[[馬鈴薯片|馬鈴薯片]]<ref>{{cite journal|title=Changes in Diet and Lifestyle and Long-Term Weight Gain in Women and Men|author=Mozaffarian D, Hao T, Rimm EB, Willett WC, 歐美的[[農業政策|農業政策]]與[[绿色革命|技術]]使糧食價格下滑。美國基於{{le|美國農業法案|United States farm bill|農業法案}}對玉米、大豆、小麥、以及米補助，造成加工食品在價格上低於蔬果<ref>{{cite news|author=Pollan, Michael |title=You Are What You Grow |work=New York Times |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2007/04/22/magazine/22wwlnlede.t.html?ex=1186027200&en=bbe0f6a2c10e3b3c&ei=5070 |date=2007-04-22 |accessdate=2007-07-30}}</ref>。近年來國家開始制定{{le|熱量標示法令|Calorie count laws|熱量標示}}與[[營養標籤|食品成份標示]]的相關法令，希望能使人們選擇較為健康的食物，並提升對自身熱量攝取的關心。

相對於正常體重者，肥胖者傾向於將自己的食量說得較低<ref>{{cite book|author=Peter G. Kopelman, Ian D. Caterson, Michael J. Stock, William H. === 坐式生活型態 ===

[[坐式生活型態|坐式生活型態]]在肥胖症的发病过程中起重要作用<ref name=Kopelman2005/><ref name = "Kopelman2005"/>{{rp|10}}。肥胖症患者的活动量较体重正常的人少，例如在加拿大，[[生活型態|生活方式]]为坐式的男性27%患有肥胖症，但正常活动量的男性仅有19.6%患有肥胖症<ref name=Tjepkema2005>{{cite book | author=Tjepkema M | chapter=Measured Obesity–Adult obesity in Canada: Measured height and weight | title=Nutrition: Findings from the Canadian Community Health Survey | publisher=Statistics Canada | date=2005-07-06 | location=Ottawa, Ontario | url=http://www.statcan.gc.ca/pub/82-620-m/2005001/article/adults-adultes/8060-eng.htm}}</ref>。

世界各地都可看到工作體力需求減少的現象<ref name=WHO2009>{{cite web|url=http://www.who.int/dietphysicalactivity/publications/facts/obesity/en/ |title=WHO: Obesity and overweight |work=World Health Organization |accessdate=2009-01-10 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081218104805/http://www.who.int/dietphysicalactivity/publications/facts/obesity/en/ |archivedate=2008-12-18}}</ref><ref |work=[[World_Health_Organization|World Health Organization]] |accessdate=2009-02-22}}</ref><ref name=Ness2006>{{cite journal | author = Ness-Abramof R, Apovian CM | title = Diet modification for treatment and prevention of obesity | journal = Endocrine | volume = 29 | issue = 1 | pages = 5–9 | date = 2006-02 | pmid = 16622287 | doi = 10.1385/ENDO:29:1:135 | url = | type = Review }}</ref>，至少有30%的人生活中的運動量不足<ref name=WHOExercise/>。這主要是由於在移動時搭乘交通工具的機會增加，且協助在家中省力的新技術也增加<ref name=WHO2009/><ref name=WHOExercise/><ref name=Ness2006/>。中国有一项研究表明，[[都市化|城市化]]导致每日能量支出减少300-400kcal，以车代步则使能量支出减少量又增加200kcal。<ref name=James2008/>。運動量下降的現象在兒童身上也能看到，主因是走路的機會變少，且體育課的量也下降<ref>{{cite journal | author = Salmon J, Timperio A | author = Borodulin K, Laatikainen T, Juolevi A, Jousilahti P | title = Thirty-year trends of physical activity in relation to age, calendar time and birth cohort in Finnish adults | journal = Eur J }}</ref>卻得到相反的結論，至於在美國的研究則發現美國人在娛樂活動中消耗的體力並沒有明顯變化<ref>{{cite journal | author = Brownson RC, Boehmer TK, Luke DA | title = Declining rates of physical activity in the United States: what are the }}</ref>。

不論在兒童或成人，看電視的時間與肥胖的風險都有關聯<ref>{{cite journal | author = Gortmaker SL, Must A, Sobol AM, Peterson K, Colditz GA, Dietz WH | title = Television viewing as a cause of increasing obesity among children in the United States, 1986–1990 | journal = Arch Pediatr Adolesc Med | volume = 150 | issue = 4 | pages = 356–62 | date = 1996-04 | pmid = 8634729 | doi = 10.1001/archpedi.1996.02170290022003 | type = Review }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Vioque J, Torres A, Quiles J | title = Time spent watching television, sleep duration and obesity in adults living in Valencia, Spain | journal = Int. Disord. | volume = 24 | issue = 12 | pages = 1683–8 | date = 2000-12 | pmid = 11126224 | doi = 10.1038/sj.ijo.0801434 | url = | type = Research Support }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Tucker LA, Bagwell M | title = Television viewing and obesity in adult females | journal = Am J Public Health | volume = 81 | issue = 7 | pages = 908–11 | date = 1991-07 | pmid = 2053671 | pmc = 1405200 | doi = 10.2105/AJPH.81.7.908 | url = http://www.ajph.org/cgi/reprint/81/7/908 | format = PDF }}</ref>。一篇評論性文章指出，在針對兒童肥胖的73個研究中，有63個（86%）都發現兒童和媒體的接觸頻率與兒童的肥胖率會同步上升，且上升的程度與看電視的時間成正相關<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ipsdweb.ipsd.org/uploads/IPPC/CSM%20Media%20Health%20Report.pdf |title=Media + Child and Adolescent Health: A Systematic Review |publisher=Common Sense Media |year=2008 |format=PDF |work=Ezekiel J. === 遗传因素 ===
<!--{{Main|Genetics of obesity}}-->
[[File:La_monstrua_desnuda_(1680),_de_Juan_Carreño_de_Miranda..jpg|thumb]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.esst.org/newsletter2000.htm |title=Case Study: Cataplexy and SOREMPs Without Excessive Daytime Sleepiness in Prader Willi Syndrome. Is This the Beginning of Narcolepsy in a Five Year Old? |author=Mary Jones |publisher=European Society of Sleep Technologists |accessdate=2009-04-06}}</ref>]]
如同許多疾病，肥胖是基因與環境因子互動所產生的結果。控制[[食慾|食慾]]與[[代謝|代謝]]的[[基因|基因]]決定了人們在食物不虞匱乏時是否會有肥胖症狀。至2006年為止，已認定人類基因體上有超過41個基因在特定情形下會造成肥胖<ref>{{cite journal | author = Poirier P, Giles TD, Bray GA, Hong Y, Stern JS, Pi-Sunyer FX, Eckel RH | title = Obesity and cardiovascular disease: pathophysiology, evaluation, and effect of weight loss | journal = Arterioscler. Thromb. Vasc. Biol. | volume = 26 | issue = 5 | pages = 968–76 | date = 2006-05 | pmid = 16627822 | doi = 10.1161/01.ATV.0000216787.85457.f3 | type = Review }}</ref>。擁有兩套{{le|肥胖基因|FTO gene}}（脂肪與肥胖關聯基因）的人比常人平均重了3-4公斤，患上肥胖的機率是沒有該[[等位基因|等位基因]]者的1.67倍<ref>{{cite journal | author = Loos RJ, Bouchard C | title = FTO: the first gene contributing to common forms of human obesity | journal = Obes Rev | volume = 9 | issue = 3 | pages = 246–50 | date = 2008-05 | pmid = 18373508 | doi = 10.1111/j.1467-789X.2008.00481.x | url = | type = Review }}</ref>。因遺傳造成的BMI差異會隨族群而不同，差異可以6%至85%<ref>{{cite journal | author = Yang W, Kelly T, He J | title = Genetic epidemiology of obesity | journal = Epidemiol Rev | volume = 29 | issue = | pages = 49–61 | year = 2007 | pmid = 17566051 | doi = 10.1093/epirev/mxm004 | type = Review }}</ref>。

肥胖是幾個症候群的主要特徵，比如說[[普瑞德威利症候群|普瑞德威利症候群]]（俗稱小胖威利症候群）、[[巴德-畢德氏症候群|巴德-畢德氏症候群]]、[[科恩綜合症|科恩症候群]]、以及{{le|MOMO症候群|MOMO syndrome}}；因此有時會用「非症候群相關的肥胖」這個名詞來排除這些疾病造成的肥胖<ref name="pmid19506576">{{cite journal | author = Walley AJ, Asher JE, Froguel P | title = The genetic contribution to non-syndromic human obesity | journal = Nature Reviews Genetics | volume = 10 | issue = 7 | pages = 431–42 | date =  2009-06 | pmid = 19506576 | doi = 10.1038/nrg2594 | url = | type = Review }}</ref>。患有早發性嚴重肥胖的人（其定義為在十歲前就發生肥胖，且BMI較平均高出3個標準差以上）有7%帶有[[單核苷酸多態性|核苷酸的單點突變]]（SNP）<ref>{{cite journal | author = Farooqi S, O'Rahilly S | title = Genetics of obesity in humans | journal = Endocr. Rev. | volume = 27 | issue = 7 | pages = 710–18 | date = 2006-12 | pmid = 17122358 | doi = 10.1210/er.2006-0040 | url = http://edrv.endojournals.org/cgi/content/full/27/7/710 | type = Review }}</ref>。

具有FTO基因的人若定期進行中等强度体力活动（相当于3-4小时的快步行走）时，罹患肥胖的機率就會下降<ref>{{cite journal | author=Rampersaud E, Mitchell BD, Pollin TI ''et al.'' | title=Physical activity and the association of common FTO gene variants with body mass index and obesity | journal=Arch Intern Med | year=2008 | volume=16 | pages=1791–97 | doi=10.1001/archinte.168.16.1791 | pmid=18779467}}</ref>。有一个研究表明肥胖父母的后代有80%也是肥胖症患者，而体重正常父母的后代只有10%是肥胖症患者。<ref name=Kopelman2005>{{cite book|author=Peter G. Kopelman, Ian D. Caterson,  Michael J. Stock,  William H. Dietz|title=Clinical obesity in adults and children: In Adults and Children|publisher=Blackwell Publishing|location= |year=2005|pages=493 |isbn=140-511672-2|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=u7RvldSr5M0C}}</ref><ref name=Kolata2007/>不同的人處在同樣的環境時，也會因為他們的基因差異而有不同的肥胖風險<ref>{{cite journal | author = Walley, Andrew J., Asher, Julian E., Froguel, Philippe | title = The genetic contribution to non-syndromic human obesity. | journal = Nat Rev Genet. | volume = 10 | issue = 7 | pages = 431–42 | date = 2009-07 | pmid = 19506576 | doi = 10.1038/nrg2594 | url = http://www.nature.com/nrg/journal/v10/n7/full/nrg2594.html | type = Review |quote=However, it is also clear that genetics greatly influences this situation, giving individuals in the same 'obesogenic' environment significantly different risks of becoming obese. }}</ref>。

{{le|节约基因理论|thrifty gene hypothesis}}假设在相同的环境条件下，某些特定的种族或人群比较容易发生肥胖。他们能充分吸收食物中的能量并以脂肪的形式储存，以适应环境的改变（例如[[飢荒|飢荒]]）及自然选择的压力。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Chakravarthy MV, Booth FW |title=Eating, exercise, and "thrifty" genotypes: Connecting the dots toward an evolutionary understanding of modern chronic diseases |journal=J. Appl. Physiol. |volume=96 |issue=1 |pages=3–10 |year=2004 |pmid=14660491 |doi=10.1152/japplphysiol.00757.2003}}</ref>，但在食物供應穩定的環境下，這種儲存脂肪的能力卻會變成一種適應障礙<ref>{{cite journal | author = Chakravarthy MV, Booth FW | title = Eating, exercise, and "thrifty" genotypes: Connecting the dots toward an evolutionary understanding of modern chronic diseases | journal = J. Appl. Physiol. | volume = 96 | issue = 1 | pages = 3–10 | year = 2004 | pmid = 14660491 | doi = 10.1152/japplphysiol.00757.2003 | type = Review }}</ref>。有學者認為就是为什么一些在沙漠中生活的{{le|Pima印地安人|Pima people}}在西方生活方式下會发生严重肥胖<ref name=Pool2001/>。節儉基因假說受到不少批評，另外也有許多其他基於演化的假說提出（例如{{le|基因漂移假說|drifty gene hypothesis}}與{{le|節儉表型理論|thrifty phenotype}}<ref>{{cite journal | author = Wells JC | title = Thrift: A guide to thrifty genes, thrifty phenotypes and thrifty norms | journal = International Journal of Obesity | volume = 33 | issue = 12 | pages = 1331–1338 | year = 2009 | pmid = 19752875 | pmc =  | doi = 10.1038/ijo.2009.175 | type = Review }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Wells JC | title = The thrifty phenotype: An adaptation in growth or metabolism? | journal = American Journal of Human Biology | volume = 23 | issue = 1 | pages = 65–75 | year = 2011 | pmid = 21082685 | pmc =  | doi = 10.1002/ajhb.21100 | type = Review }}</ref>。

=== 生理和心理疾病的影响 ===
某些生理或心理疾病以及治疗它们的药物會增加患者罹患肥胖症的風險。生理疾病包括上述的罕见遗传病，以及一些先天或後天疾病，比如[[甲状腺功能低下|甲状腺功能低下]]、[[库兴氏综合症|库欣综合征]]、{{le|生长激素缺乏症|Growth hormone deficiency}}等<ref>{{cite journal |author=Rosén T, Bosaeus I, Tölli J, Lindstedt G, Bengtsson BA |title=Increased body fat mass and decreased extracellular fluid volume in adults with growth hormone deficiency |journal=Clin. Endocrinol. (Oxf) |volume=38 |issue=1 |pages=63–71 |year=1993 |pmid=8435887| doi = 10.1111/j.1365-2265.1993.tb00974.x}}</ref>，另外也包括了[[进食障碍|进食障碍]]（如[[过胖暴食症|暴食障碍]]和{{le|夜间进食综合征|night eating syndrome}}）<ref name=HaslamJames/>。即使肥胖與部分的精神疾病相關，但肥胖不被視為精神疾病，因此并未将其列入[[精神障碍诊断与统计手册|精神障碍诊断与统计手册]]<ref name=Kolata2007>{{cite book |author=Kolata,Gina |title=Rethinking thin: The new science of weight loss - and the myths and realities of dieting |publisher=Picador |location= |year=2007 |pages= |isbn=0-312-42785-9}}</ref>。

某些药物可以导致体重增加和体质构成改变，這些藥物包括[[胰岛素|胰岛素]]、[[硫酰脲|硫酰脲]]、{{le|噻唑烷二酮|Thiazolidinedione}}、{{le|非典型抗精神病药|Atypical antipsychotic}}、[[抗抑郁药|抗抑郁药]]、[[类固醇|糖皮质激素]]、某些{{le|抗癫痫药|Anticonvulsant}}（比如[[苯妥英钠|苯妥英钠]]、[[丙戊酸钠|丙戊酸钠]]）、{{le|苯噻啶|Pizotifen}}以及某些剂型的{{le|激素类避孕药|Hormonal contraception}}<ref name=HaslamJames/>。

===社會因子===
<!--{{Main|Social determinants of obesity}}-->
[[File:Yamai_no_Soshi_-_Obesity.JPG|thumb]]
雖然基因對肥胖的影響很重要，但它無法解釋為何部分國家（甚至全球）的肥胖率正在大幅上升<ref>{{cite journal | author = Yach D, Stuckler D, Brownell KD | title = Epidemiologic and economic consequences of the global epidemics of obesity and diabetes | journal = Nat. Med. | volume = 12 | issue = 1 | pages = 62–6 | date = 2006-01 | pmid = 16397571 | doi = 10.1038/nm0106-62 | url =  }}</ref>。雖然對個人來說，熱量攝取高於消耗會帶來肥胖；但在社會層面上，造成熱量攝取與消耗消長的真正成因仍在爭論中，並有許多理論試圖找出原因，但多數人相信真正的原因是這許多因素的總和 。
[[社會階級|社會階級]]與BMI的關聯性強弱在世界各地有所不同。1989年的評論性文章指出[[已開發國家|已開發國家]]中社會地位較高的女性較不易有肥胖問題，但不同社會地位的男性的肥胖率則沒有顯著差異；在[[發展中國家|發展中國家]]，社會地位較高的女性、男性、以及兒童都有較高的肥胖率<ref>{{cite journal | author = Sobal J, Stunkard AJ | title = Socioeconomic status and obesity: A review of the literature | journal = Psychol Bull | volume = 105 | issue = 2 | pages = 260–75 | date = 1989-03 | pmid = 2648443 | doi = 10.1037/0033-2909.105.2.260 | type = Review }}</ref>。該評論文章在2007年更新，發現肥胖與社會地位的關係雖然不變，但關聯性減弱，並認為關連性的減弱是受到全球化的影響<ref name=McLaren2007>{{cite journal | author = McLaren L | title = Socioeconomic status and obesity | journal = Epidemiol Rev | volume = 29 | issue =  | pages = 29–48 | year = 2007 | pmid = 17478442 | doi = 10.1093/epirev/mxm001 | type = Review }}</ref>。在已開發國家中，[[收入不平等|收入越不平均]]的地區，成人肥胖率與青少年過重率越高，這個現象在美國也能觀察到：在收入不平等的州，即使是較高社會地位的人也容易有肥胖問題<ref name="spirit">{{cite book|title=The Spirit Level: Why More Equal Societies Almost Always Do Better |last1=Wilkinson |first1=Richard  |last2=Pickett |first2=Kate |publisher=Allen Lane |location=London |isbn=978-1-84614-039-6 |year=2009 |pages=91–101}}</ref>。

BMI與社會地位的關係有許多解釋。有些人認為在已開發國家中，富人能消費營養較為均衡的食物，且維持纖細體態的社會壓力較大，維持[[體適能|體適能]]的機會與期待也都比較高；在[[未開發國家|未開發國家]]，認為負擔食物開銷的能力、高勞動帶來的高熱量消耗、以及對較胖體態的文化偏好都能解釋肥胖率與社會地位的關連<ref name=McLaren2007/>。對體重的態度也在肥胖的發生中佔一定角色，在朋友、兄弟姊妹、以及配偶的身上都能觀察到他們之間的BMI變化有關聯性<ref>{{cite journal | author = Christakis NA, Fowler JH | title = The Spread of Obesity in a Large Social Network over 32 Years | journal = New England Journal of Medicine | volume = 357 | issue = 4 | pages = 370–379 | year = 2007 | pmid = 17652652 | doi = 10.1056/NEJMsa066082 | type = Research Support }}</ref>。精神壓力及社會地位低似乎也會增加肥胖率<ref name="spirit" /><ref>{{cite journal | author = Björntorp P | title = Do stress reactions cause abdominal obesity and comorbidities? | journal = Obesity Reviews | volume = 2 | issue = 2 | pages = 73–86 | year = 2001 | pmid = 12119665 | doi = 10.1046/j.1467-789x.2001.00027.x }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Goodman E, Adler NE, Daniels SR, Morrison JA, Slap GB, Dolan LM | title = Impact of objective and subjective social status on obesity in a biracial cohort of adolescents | journal = Obesity Reviews | volume = 11 | issue = 8 | pages = 1018–26 | year = 2003 | pmid = 12917508 | doi = 10.1038/oby.2003.140 | type = Research Support }}</ref>。

吸菸對個人的體重有明顯影響。男性戒菸者十年內平均會增重4.4公斤（9.7磅），女性戒菸者則會增重5公斤（11.0磅）<ref>{{cite journal | author = Flegal KM, Troiano RP, Pamuk ER, Kuczmarski RJ, Campbell SM | title = The influence of smoking cessation on the prevalence of overweight in the United States | journal = N. Engl. J. Med. | volume = 333 | issue = 18 | pages = 1165–70 | date = 1995-11 | pmid = 7565970 | doi = 10.1056/NEJM199511023331801 | url = http://content.nejm.org/cgi/content/full/333/18/1165 }}</ref>，但整體而言吸菸率的變化對整體的肥胖率並沒有明顯影響<ref>{{cite journal | author = Chiolero A, Faeh D, Paccaud F, Cornuz J | title = Consequences of smoking for body weight, body fat distribution, and insulin resistance | journal = Am. J. Clin. Nutr. | volume = 87 | issue = 4 | pages = 801–9 | date = 2008-04-01 | pmid = 18400700 | url = http://www.ajcn.org/cgi/content/full/87/4/801 | type = Review }}</ref>。

在美國，生子的數量與肥胖風險也有關。每生一個孩子，女性的肥胖風險就上升7%，男性則會上升4%<ref>{{cite journal | author = Weng HH, Bastian LA, Taylor DH, Moser BK, Ostbye T | title = Number of children associated with obesity in middle-aged women and men: results from the health and retirement study | journal = J Women's Health (Larchmt) | volume = 13 | issue = 1 | pages = 85–91 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15006281 | doi = 10.1089/154099904322836492 | type = Comparative Study }}</ref>。這個現象可能是因為在西方國家，家庭撫養的孩子越多，家長的運動量就越少<ref>{{cite journal | author = Bellows-Riecken KH, Rhodes RE | title = A birth of inactivity? A review of physical activity and parenthood | journal = Prev Med | volume = 46 | issue = 2 | pages = 99–110 | date = 2008-02 | pmid = 17919713 | doi = 10.1016/j.ypmed.2007.08.003 | type = Review }}</ref>。

在發展中國家，[[都市化|都市化]]在肥胖率上升的過程中扮演一定的角色。在中國，平均肥胖率只有不到5%，但在某些都市肥胖率卻高達20%以上<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.who.int/dietphysicalactivity/media/en/gsfs_obesity.pdf |title=Obesity and Overweight |format=PDF |publisher=[[World_Health_Organization|World Health Organization]] |accessdate=2009-02-22}}</ref>。早年營養不良和發展中國家的肥胖率上升也有一定的關聯<ref name="DC2001">{{cite journal | author = Caballero B | title = Introduction. Symposium: Obesity in developing countries: biological and ecological factors | journal = J. Nutr. | volume = 131 | issue = 3 | pages = 866S–870S | date = 2001-03 | pmid = 11238776 | doi =  | url = http://jn.nutrition.org/cgi/content/full/131/3/866S | type = Review }}</ref>，營養不良時期造成的內分泌變化可能會誘使身體在有食物時傾向堆積脂肪<ref name="DC2001" />。

[[流行病學|流行病學]]資料指出{{le|認知障礙|Cognitive deficit}}與肥胖風險相關<ref name="Smith2011">{{cite journal | author = Smith E, Hay P, Campbell L, Trollor JN | title = A review of the association between obesity and cognitive function across the lifespan: implications for novel approaches to prevention and treatment | journal = Obesity Reviews | volume = 12 | pages = 740–755 | year = 2011 | pmid = 21991597 | doi = 10.1111/j.1467-789X.2011.00920.x | url = http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1467-789X.2011.00920.x/abstract | issue=9|type=Review}}</ref>，但究竟是認知障礙造成肥胖，或者是反過來，則尚不明朗。

===感染性因素===
<!--{{See also|Infectobesity}}-->
感染性因素如何影響代謝的相關研究才剛起步。目前已經知道正常體重者與肥胖者身上的{{le|腸道菌叢|Gut flora}}組成是不同的，證據顯示腸道菌叢可以影響代謝能力。正常體重者與肥胖者有明顯的代謝能力差異，因此推測從腸道中吸收熱量的能力可能會影響肥胖的發生，但目前還不確定這些差異是否會直接造成肥胖，或者這些其實是肥胖造成的結果<ref>{{cite journal | author = DiBaise JK, Zhang H, Crowell MD, Krajmalnik-Brown R, Decker GA, Rittmann BE | title = Gut microbiota and its possible relationship with obesity | journal = Mayo Clinic proceedings. Mayo Clinic | volume = 83 | issue = 4 | pages = 460–9 | date = 2008-04 | pmid = 18380992 | doi = 10.4065/83.4.460 | type = Review }}</ref>。

在人類與其他數種動物身上也能觀察到[[病毒|病毒]]與肥胖的關聯，但還不清楚這與肥胖率的上升是否有關<ref>{{cite journal | author = Falagas ME, Kompoti M | title = Obesity and infection | journal = Lancet Infect Dis | volume = 6 | issue = 7 | pages = 438–46 | date = 2006-07 | pmid = 16790384 | doi = 10.1016/S1473-3099(06)70523-0 | url = | type = Review }}</ref>。

==病理生理學==
[[File:Fatmouse.jpg|thumb]]，因此體型明顯和右側的正常老鼠不同]]

肥胖的發生與維持有許多種可能的[[病理生理學|病理生理]]機轉<ref name="flier">{{cite journal | author = Flier JS | title = Obesity wars: Molecular progress confronts an expanding epidemic | journal = Cell | volume = 116 | issue = 2 | pages = 337–50 | year = 2004 | pmid = 14744442 | doi = 10.1016/S0092-8674(03)01081-X | type = Review }}</ref>，但這個研究領域一直乏人問津，在1994年時，弗萊德曼（{{lang|en|J. M. Friedman}}）的研究團隊才發現[[瘦蛋白|瘦素]]基因<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Zhang|first1=Y|last2=Proenca|first2=R|last3=Maffei|first3=M|last4=Barone|first4=M|last5=Leopold|first5=L|last6=Friedman|first6=JM|title=Positional cloning of the mouse obese gene and its human homologue.|journal=Nature|date=1994-12-01|volume=372|issue=6505|pages=425–32|doi=10.1038/372425a0|pmid=7984236|type=Research Support}}</ref>。弗萊德曼的研究團隊認為[[瘦蛋白|瘦素]]是影響飽足感的因子。實驗中發現{{link-en|胖胖鼠|ob/ob mouse}}（一種意外發現，具有過度食慾並且極端肥胖的實驗用小鼠）帶有突變[[瘦蛋白|瘦素]]基因，因此展現肥胖的表現型，這個結果開啟以[[瘦蛋白|瘦素]]來治療人類肥胖的可能性，但不久後卡羅（{{lang|en|J. F. Caro}}）的研究團隊就發現無法在肥胖的人身上找到任何瘦素基因的突變；另一方面，在肥胖者身上的[[瘦蛋白|瘦素]]基因表現量上升，反而指出了肥胖者身上可能有瘦素抵抗現象<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Considine|first1=RV|last2=Considine|first2=EL|last3=Williams|first3=CJ|last4=Nyce|first4=MR|last5=Magosin|first5=SA|last6=Bauer|first6=TL|last7=Rosato|first7=EL|last8=Colberg|first8=J|last9=Caro|first9=JF <!--exactly 9 authors--> |title=Evidence against either a premature stop codon or the absence of obese gene mRNA in human obesity.|journal=The Journal of Clinical Investigation|date=1995-06|volume=95|issue=6|pages=2986–8|pmid=7769141|doi=10.1172/jci118007|pmc=295988|type=Research Support}}</ref>。在發現瘦素後，研究者陸續發現許多其他影響[[食慾|食慾]]、進食、[[脂肪組織|脂肪儲存]]、[[胰島素抗性|胰島素抗性]]的激素，舉例來說[[饑餓素|饑餓素]]、[[胰島素|胰島素]]、[[食慾素|食慾素]]、{{link-en|雙酪胺酸肽3-36|peptide YY}}、[[膽囊收縮素|膽囊收縮素]]、[[脂聯素|脂聯素]]等因子都是學者研究的對象。{{le|脂激素|Adipokine}}是由脂肪組織釋放出的訊息因子，它們的作用會影響許多肥胖相關的疾病。

一般認為[[瘦蛋白|瘦素]]與[[饑餓素|饑餓素]]在食慾的影響上扮演互補的角色。饑餓素由胃製造，負責調節短期的食慾變化，比方說在胃排空時進食並在胃裝滿時停止；而瘦素則是由脂肪組織製造，反映人體脂肪儲藏的總量，並調節長期食慾變化，比如說在脂肪儲藏量低時增加食量，並在儲藏量高時減少食量。雖然使用瘦素對一小部分瘦素缺乏的肥胖者有效，但目前認為絕大多數的肥胖者都有瘦素抗性，在他們的身體中也都已經有高濃度的瘦素<ref>{{cite journal | author = Hamann A, Matthaei S | title = Regulation of energy balance by leptin | journal = Exp. Clin. Endocrinol. Diabetes | volume = 104 | issue = 4 | pages = 293–300 | year = 1996 | pmid = 8886745 | doi = 10.1055/s-0029-1211457 | type = Review }}</ref>。瘦素抗性可以解釋為何使用瘦素無法有效減少多數肥胖者的食量<ref name="flier"/>。

[[File:Leptin.png|thumb]]的分子模型]]
[[瘦蛋白|瘦素]]與[[饑餓素|饑餓素]]雖然由周邊組織製造和分泌，它們仍然要透過影響[[中樞神經系統|中樞神經系統]]才可以發揮調節食慾的功能。更精確的說，它們以及其他食慾相關激素會作用在[[下視丘|下視丘]]，這是腦中負責管理進食與能量消耗的中心。下視丘中有幾個迴路在調節食慾中扮演重要角色。{{link-en|黑皮質素|melanocortin}}路徑是目前了解最透徹的食慾訊息傳遞分子<ref name="flier"/>，它從下視丘的[[弓狀核|弓狀核]]分泌至{{link-en|下視丘外核|lateral hypothalamus}}（LH）與{{link-en|下視丘腹中核|ventromedial nucleus}}（VMH），這兩個核分別是腦中的進食中樞與飽足中樞<ref>{{cite book|author=Boulpaep, Emile L.; Boron, Walter F. |title=Medical physiology: A cellular and molecular approach |publisher=Saunders |location=Philadelphia |year=2003 |page=1227 |isbn=0-7216-3256-4}}</ref>。

[[弓狀核|弓狀核]]內含有兩群[[神經元|神經元]]<ref name="flier"/>：第一群同時表現{{link-en|神經肽Y|neuropeptide Y}}（NPY）與{{link-en|刺豚鼠關聯肽|agouti-related peptide}}（AgRP），它們會刺激{{link-en|下視丘外核|lateral hypothalamus}}並抑制{{link-en|下視丘腹中核|ventromedial nucleus}}；第二群則表現[[鴉片黑皮質素原|鴉片黑皮質素原]]（POMC）與{{le|古柯鹼-安非他命關聯轉錄因子|cocaine- and amphetamine-regulated transcript}}（CART），並會刺激{{link-en|下視丘腹中核|ventromedial nucleus}}及抑制下視丘外核。因此表現NPY / AgRP的神經元會促進進食並抑制飽足感；而表現POMC / CART的神經元則相反地會抑制進食並激發飽足感。在[[弓狀核|弓狀核]]中的兩組神經元都受[[瘦蛋白|瘦素]]調控：瘦素會抑制表現NPY / AgRP的神經元並刺激表現POMC / CART的神經元。因此如果瘦素相關的訊息傳導不足（可能由於瘦素不足或是瘦素抵抗），就會造成食慾提升而過度進食，這也能解釋某些基因疾病與後天狀況造成的肥胖<ref name="flier"/>。

目前的主流觀點認為，對抗肥胖症需有一套完整的對策。因此，肥胖政策與行動框架（Obesity Policy Action (OPA) framework）將對策區分為：上游、中游、與下游策略。上游策略主要試圖改變社會；中游策略則試圖改變個人行為以預防肥胖；下游策略則是治療已經肥胖的人口<ref>{{cite journal|title=Obesity Policy Action framework and analysis grids for a comprehensive policy approach to reducing obesity|author=Sacks G, Swinburn B, Lawrence M|date=2009-01|journal=Obes Rev|issue=1|doi=10.1111/j.1467-789X.2008.00524.X|volume=10|pages=76–86|pmid=18761640}}</ref>。

=== 饮食控制 ===
控制饮食的方法一般分为如下四类：低脂饮食，低碳水化合物饮食，低热量饮食以及极低热量饮食。<ref name=Strychar/>一份針對六份隨機控制試驗所做出的[[後設分析|後設分析]]發現主要的[[節食|節食]]方法（低熱量、低碳水化合物及低脂）在減肥效果上沒有任何差異，都能順利減少二至四公斤。<ref name=Strychar/>

* 低脂饮食：主要是减少食物中脂肪的含量，但不减少摄入食物的总量。该方法是{{le|美国国家胆固醇教育计划|National Cholesterol Education Program}}中的第1步和第2步。16个试验（持续时间2-12个月不等）的 [[後設分析|後設分析]]结果显示，低脂饮食能使体重减少3.2公斤。<ref name=Strychar/>

* [[低碳水化合物饮食|低碳水化合物饮食]]：指增加食物中脂肪或脂肪和蛋白质的比例，比如[[食肉减肥法|Atkins减肥法]]和{{le|高蛋白质饮食法|High-protein diet}}。这种方法非常流行，但腎功能略差就不適用（許多人腎功能稍差，但他們沒有任何症狀），且此方法也不为美国心脏协会所推荐。据研究，该方法造成体重下降的原理是使饱足感增加，导致能量摄入减少<!--。有些研究指出低碳水化合物饮食并未发现有不良反应--><ref>{{cite journal |author=Bravata DM, Sanders L, Huang J, ''et al.'' |title=Efficacy and safety of low-carbohydrate diets: A systematic review |journal=JAMA |volume=289 |issue=14 |pages=1837–50 |date=2003-04 |pmid=12684364 |doi=10.1001/jama.289.14.1837}}</ref>。

* 低[[升糖指數|升糖指數]]、低[[升糖負荷|升糖負荷]]飲食：此方法的理論基礎在於血液中[[胰島素|胰島素]]過高與波動會造成食量上升與脂肪堆積，因此認為減少糖分攝取能避免肥胖症發生<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Glycemic index and obesity|url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12081852|last=Brand-Miller|first=Janette C.|last2=Holt|first2=Susanna H. A.|date=2002-07-01|journal=The American Journal of Clinical Nutrition|issue=1|volume=76|pages=281S–5S|issn=0002-9165|pmid=12081852|last3=Pawlak|first3=Dorota B.|last4=McMillan|first4=Joanna}}</ref>。

* 低热量饮食：指每天只摄入800-1500kcal热量，形成能量负平衡，每周可使体重下降0.5公斤。{{le|美國衛生研究院|The National Institutes of Health}}回顾分析了34个[[随机对照试验|随机对照试验]]，发现该方法能在3-12个月内减少8%的体重。<ref name=Strychar/>

* 极低热量饮食：指每日只摄入400-800kcal热量，严格限制蛋白质、脂肪和碳水化合物的摄入，使個體处于长期饥饿状态，每周能使体重下降1.5-2.5公斤。但是會引起[[瘦素|瘦素]]减少、[[痛风|痛风]]风险增加、和[[电解质平衡紊乱|电解质平衡紊乱]]等不良反应，因此不受广泛推荐。尝试该方法的患者需要在医生的指导下进行，并严格预防并发症的发生。<ref name=Strychar/>

===藥物治療===
市面已有三種減肥藥已有證據可以長期使用，分別是[[奧利司他|奧利司他]]（羅氏鮮）、{{link-en|洛卡色林|lorcaserin}}（Belviq）以及{{le|芬他命與托吡酯複方|Phentermine/topiramate}}（Qsymia）<ref name=Yan2014>{{cite journal | author = Yanovski SZ, Yanovski JA | title = Long-term drug treatment for obesity: a systematic and clinical review. | journal = JAMA: the Journal of the American Medical Association | volume = 311 | issue = 1 | pages = 74–86 | date = 2014-01-01 | pmid = 24231879 | doi = 10.1001/jama.2013.281361 | pmc=3928674 |  type = Review }}</ref>。[[奧利司他|奧利司他]]能少量減輕體重，平均約為在一到四年間減少2.9公斤<ref name=Orli07>{{cite journal | author = Rucker D, Padwal R, Li SK, Curioni C, Lau DC | title = Long term pharmacotherapy for obesity and overweight: updated meta-analysis | journal = BMJ | volume = 335 | issue = 7631 | pages = 1194–99 | year = 2007 | pmid = 18006966 | pmc = 2128668 | doi = 10.1136/bmj.39385.413113.25 | url = http://www.bmj.com/cgi/content/full/335/7631/1194 | type = Meta-analysis }}</ref>，但有相當高的機會產生腸胃副作用<ref name=Orli07/>，它對腎臟的影響也受到質疑<ref>{{cite web|last=Wood |first=Shelley |title=Diet Drug Orlistat Linked to Kidney, Pancreas Injuries |url=http://www.medscape.com/viewarticle/740855?src=mp&spon=30 |work=Medscape |publisher=Medscape News |accessdate=2011-04-26}}</ref>。另外兩種藥物則只在美國上市<ref name=EMA2013>{{cite journal | author = Wolfe SM | title = When EMA and FDA decisions conflict: differences in patients or in regulation? | journal = BMJ (Clinical research ed.) | volume = 347 | pages = f5140 | date = 2013-08-21 | pmid = 23970394 | doi = 10.1136/bmj.f5140 }}</ref>。洛卡色林平均能在一年內比安慰劑多減少3.1公斤的體重（3%體重）<ref>{{cite journal | author = Bays HE | title = Lorcaserin: drug profile and illustrative model of the regulatory challenges of weight-loss drug development | journal = Expert review of cardiovascular therapy | volume = 9 | issue = 3 | pages = 265–77 | date = 2011-03 | pmid = 21438803 | doi = 10.1586/erc.10.22 | type = Review }}</ref>，但可能會引發心臟瓣膜的問題<ref name=EMA2013/>。芬他命與托吡酯同時使用也具有某種程度的減重效果<ref>{{cite journal | author = Bays HE, Gadde KM | title = Phentermine/topiramate for weight reduction and treatment of adverse metabolic consequences in obesity | journal = Drugs Today | volume = 47 | issue = 12 | pages = 903–14 | date = 2011-12 | pmid = 22348915 | doi = 10.1358/dot.2011.47.12.1718738 | type = Review }}</ref>，但使用這兩種藥物同樣與心臟問題有關聯<ref name=EMA2013/>。現下還沒有資料能闡明這些減重藥物對肥胖的長期併發症（比方說心血管疾病與死亡）的影響<ref name=Yan2014/>。

===減肥手術===
對肥胖最有效的治療是[[減肥手術|減肥手術]]<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Colquitt|first1=JL|last2=Pickett|first2=K|last3=Loveman|first3=E|last4=Frampton|first4=GK|title=Surgery for weight loss in adults|journal=The Cochrane database of systematic reviews|date=2014-08-08|volume=8|pages=CD003641|pmid=25105982|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD003641.pub4 | type=Meta-analysis, Review}}</ref>。嚴重肥胖者接受手術後能夠長期減輕體重、改善肥胖相關問題<ref name=Chang2013>{{cite journal | author = Chang SH, Stoll CR, Song J, Varela JE, Eagon CJ, Colditz GA | title = The effectiveness and risks of bariatric surgery: an updated systematic review and meta-analysis, 2003-2012 | journal = JAMA Surgery | volume = 149 | issue = 3 | pages = 275–87 | year = 2014 | pmid = 24352617 | doi = 10.1001/jamasurg.2013.3654 | type = Meta-analysis, Review }}</ref>、並降低死亡率。一項研究指出手術能在十年間減輕患者14%-25%的體重（視手術方法而定），且相對於一般減重方法，還能減低29%的死亡率<ref>{{cite journal | author = Sjöström L, Narbro K, Sjöström CD, Karason K, Larsson B, Wedel H, Lystig T, Sullivan M, Bouchard C, Carlsson B, Bengtsson C, Dahlgren S, Gummesson A, Jacobson P, Karlsson J, Lindroos AK, Lönroth H, Näslund I, Olbers T, Stenlöf K, Torgerson J, Agren G, Carlsson LM | title = Effects of bariatric surgery on mortality in Swedish obese subjects | journal = N. Engl. J. Med. | volume = 357 | issue = 8 | pages = 741–52 | date = 2007-08 | pmid = 17715408 | doi = 10.1056/NEJMoa066254 | type = Research Support }}</ref> 。不過手術併發症的發生率為17%，並且有7%的病人需要接受第二次手術<ref name=Chang2013/>。因為手術的花費與風險，研究者們正在尋找侵入性較低卻同樣有效的治療方法，比如說在胃中置入佔空間的物體（氣球）來減少胃容量<ref>{{cite web|last=Weintraub |first=Karen |title=New allies in war on weight |url=http://www.bostonglobe.com/business/2014/06/29/novel-ways-lose-weight-include-stomach-gel-electrical-impulses/LLzowmiW9d8yZsXXiHSOWN/story.html |work=The Boston Globe |publisher=The Boston Globe |accessdate=2014-06-30}}</ref>。

==歷史==
===英文字源===
肥胖的英文「obesity」來自拉丁語的「{{Lang|la|obesitas}}」，指的是體型大、肥、或渾圓。「Esus」則是「edere」（進食）的過去分詞，並加上前綴修飾字「ob-」（過度地）<ref name=etymol>{{cite web|url=http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?term=obesity |title=Online Etymology Dictionary: Obesity |work=Douglas Harper |accessdate=2008-12-31}}</ref>。據[[牛津辭典|牛津辭典]]記載，蘭德爾·科特格雷夫（Randle Cotgrave）在1611年首次使用肥胖這個單詞<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.oed.com/ |title=Obesity, n |work=Oxford English Dictionary 2008 |accessdate=2009-03-21}}</ref>。

===歷史上對肥胖的態度===
[[File:Charles_Mellin_(attributed)_-_Portrait_of_a_Gentleman_-_Google_Art_Project.jpg|thumb]]及[[文藝復興|文藝復興]]時肥胖是富有的象徵，在上流社會中比較常見，1645年的繪畫<ref name=Zach2003>{{cite journal|author=Zachary Bloomgarden |title=Prevention of Obesity and Diabetes |journal=Diabetes Care |volume=26 |pages=3172–3178 |year=2003 |pmid=14578257 |doi=10.2337/diacare.26.11.3172 |url=http://care.diabetesjournals.org/content/26/11/3172.full |issue=11 | type=Review}}</ref>]]
[[File:Venus_von_Willendorf_01.jpg|thumb]]雕刻]]

{{le|古希臘醫學|Ancient Greek medicine}}和古埃及的文獻都將肥胖視為一種疾病<ref name=Haslam2007>{{cite journal | author = Haslam D | title = Obesity: a medical history | journal = Obes Rev | volume = 8 Suppl 1 | issue =  | pages = 31–6 | date = 2007-03 | pmid = 17316298 | doi = 10.1111/j.1467-789X.2007.00314.x | url = | type=Review }}</ref>。[[希波克拉底|希波克拉底]]這麼寫道：「肥胖本身不是一種病，但它是其他疾病的前兆。」公元前六世紀的印度外科醫師{{le|蘇許魯塔|Sushruta}}認為肥胖與[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]與[[心血管疾病|心血管疾病]]有關<ref name=Dwivedi&Dwivedi07/>，並建議以運動來治療肥胖以及過重體位<ref name=Dwivedi&Dwivedi07>{{cite web|url=http://medind.nic.in/iae/t07/i4/iaet07i4p243.pdf |format=PDF |title=History of Medicine: Sushruta – the Clinician – Teacher par Excellence |accessdate=2008-09-19 |work=Dwivedi, Girish & Dwivedi, Shridhar |publisher= |year=2007}}</ref>。

古印度的[[波斯匿王|波斯匿王]]曾因為發胖而苦，要求[[佛陀|佛陀]]教他[[減肥|減肥]]的方法。[[佛|佛]]要他[[節食|節食]]、節制[[睡眠|睡眠]]、多[[運動|運動]]、多[[思考|思考]]，保持謙遜的態度。波斯匿王不久就減肥成功。<ref name="http://www.cbeta.org/result/normal/T02/0099_042.htm">[http://www.cbeta.org/result/normal/T02/0099_042.htm 《雜阿含經‧卷第四十二‧第一一五〇經》] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150219014249/http://www.cbeta.org/result/normal/T02/0099_042.htm |date=2015-02-19 }}，中華電子佛典協會。</ref><ref name="http://www.cbeta.org/result/normal/T02/0100_004.htm">[http://www.cbeta.org/result/normal/T02/0100_004.htm 《別譯雜阿含經‧卷第四‧第七十三經》] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131001000714/http://www.cbeta.org/result/normal/T02/0100_004.htm |date=2013-10-01 }}，中華電子佛典協會。</ref>

在歷史長河中，人類大部分的時間都在食物缺乏中求生存<ref>{{cite book|author=Theodore Mazzone; Giamila Fantuzzi |title=Adipose Tissue And Adipokines in Health And Disease (Nutrition and Health) |publisher=Humana Press |location=Totowa, NJ |year=2006 |page=222 |isbn=1-58829-721-7 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref>，因此視肥胖為財富與繁榮的象徵。在[[中世紀|中世紀]]與[[文藝復興|文藝復興]]時代的歐洲高官身上<ref name=Zach2003/>以及古代東亞文明的權貴身上都經常可以見到肥胖的情形<ref>{{cite book|author=Keller, Kathleen |title=Encyclopedia of Obesity |publisher=Sage Publications, Inc |location=Thousand Oaks, Calif |year=2008 |isbn=1-4129-5238-7 |oclc= |url=https://books.google.com/?id=aRp2rJrEqZsC}}</ref>{{rp|49}}。隨著[[工業革命|工業革命]]的發生，人們開始認為國家的軍事經濟力量立基於軍人與工人的體態與力量<ref name=Caballero/>，人們的平均[[身體質量指數|身體質量指數]]（BMI）開始從今日視為體重過輕的程度提升至正常範圍<ref name=Caballero/>。從十九世紀開始，[[已開發國家|已開發國家]]人民的平均身高與體重就已經開始上升；到了二十世紀，身高已經達到基因潛力的上限，於是體重的成長就超過身高而開始產生肥胖<ref name=Caballero/>。1950年代，已開發國家的兒童死亡率因財富的累積而下降，但心臟疾病與腎臟疾病的發生率則隨著體重一起上升<ref name=Caballero/><ref>{{cite journal | author = Breslow L | title = Public Health Aspects of Weight Control | journal = Am J Public Health Nations Health | volume = 42 | issue = 9 | pages = 1116–20 | date = 1952-09 | pmid = 12976585 | pmc = 1526346 | doi = 10.2105/AJPH.42.9.1116 }}</ref>。在此同時，保險業發覺了體重與預期壽命間的關聯性，於是調漲肥胖者的保費<ref name=HaslamJames/>。

歷史上的許多文化都認為肥胖源自於人格上的瑕疵。在[[古希腊喜剧|希臘喜劇]]中，肥胖的角色通常是貪吃的人，也是嘲弄的對象。[[基督教|基督教]]信仰中認為食物會讓人犯下[[七宗罪|七宗罪]]的{{le|怠惰 (原罪)|Sloth (deadly sin)|怠惰}}與[[性欲|色慾]]兩項原罪<ref name=Woodhouse/>。在當代西方文化中，肥胖經常視為不受歡迎的同義詞，也與許多負面刻板印象相關。不論年齡，肥胖的人都可能面對社交上的汙名化，並可能遭受[[霸凌|霸凌]]或同儕排擠，肥胖於是成為歧視的原因<ref name=Bias2001>{{cite journal | author = Puhl R, Brownell KD | title = Bias, discrimination, and obesity | journal = Obes. Res. | volume = 9 | issue = 12 | pages = 788–805 | date = 2001-12 | pmid = 11743063 | doi = 10.1038/oby.2001.108 | type = Review }}</ref>。

西方社會並不認為「健康體重」與「理想體重」是同一件事，而且對這兩件事的認識在二十世紀中開始發生變化。「理想體重」的標準自1920年代開始下降：自1922年至1999年間，美國小姐的身高上升了2%，但體重卻下降了12%<ref>{{cite journal | author = Rubinstein S, Caballero B | title = Is Miss America an undernourished role model? | journal = JAMA | volume = 283 | issue = 12 | page = 1569 | year = 2000 | pmid = 10735392 | doi = 10.1001/jama.283.12.1569 | type = Letter }}</ref>；但在此同時，人們對「健康體重」的標準則反其道而行：2007年的英國人對於過重的標準，相對於1999年時寬鬆得多<ref name=John2008>{{cite journal | author = Johnson F, Cooke L, Croker H, Wardle J | title = Changing perceptions of weight in Great Britain: comparison of two population surveys | journal = BMJ | volume = 337 | issue =  | pages = a494 | year = 2008 | pmid = 18617488 | pmc = 2500200 | doi = 10.1136/bmj.a494 | url = http://www.bmj.com/cgi/content/full/337/jul10_1/a494 |type = Comparative Study }}</ref>。這些變化被認為是因為人們的體脂不斷上升，因此人們開始認為過剩的體脂是一種正常的表現<ref name=John2008/>。

在[[非洲|非洲]]許多地區，仍視肥胖為富裕與健康的象徵，尤其在[[人類免疫缺陷病毒|HIV]]疫情發生後，這樣的看法變得越來越常見<ref name=HaslamJames/>。

===藝術===
最早的人體雕塑創作於二萬到三萬五千年前，主要都是描繪肥胖的女體。有些人認為[[維納斯|維納斯]]塑像的表現方式象徵的是多產，但也有些人認為這單純是表現創作年代的人們的肥胖程度<ref name=Woodhouse/>。在希臘與羅馬的藝術創作中，幾乎見不到肥胖的人物，這或許與他們文化對於適中的追求有關，而希臘羅馬傳統也由多數的基督教歐洲文化所繼承，在他們的創作中，只有低社經地位的角色才會是肥胖的<ref name=Woodhouse/>。

自[[文藝復興|文藝復興]]時代開始，部分上層階級開始炫耀自己的體胖，在英王[[亨利八世|亨利八世]]與{{link-en|亞歷山卓·達·布羅|Alessandro dal Borro}}的畫像中就能看到這樣的趨勢<ref name=Woodhouse>{{cite journal | author = Woodhouse R | title = Obesity in art: A brief overview | journal = Front Horm Res | volume = 36 | issue =  | pages = 271–86 | year = 2008 | pmid = 18230908 | doi = 10.1159/000115370 | url = https://books.google.com/?id=nXRU4Ea1aMkC&pg=PA271&lpg=PA271 | isbn = 978-3-8055-8429-6 | series = Frontiers of Hormone Research }}</ref>。知名畫家[[彼得·保羅·魯本斯|魯本斯]]經常描繪[[魯本斯式|魯本斯式]]的女性全身像，雖然表現上也強調多產，但在他畫中的女性仍然維持沙漏狀的身形<ref name=Fumento>{{cite book|author=Fumento, Michael |title=The Fat of the Land: Our Health Crisis and How Overweight Americans Can Help Themselves |publisher=Penguin (Non-Classics) |year=1997 |page=126 |isbn=0-14-026144-3}}</ref>。到了十九世紀，整個西方世界對於肥胖的看法開始改變，在肥胖作為財富與地位的象徵許多世紀以後，纖瘦取而代之成為新的追求目標<ref name=Woodhouse/>。

== 社會文化 ==
=== 經濟衝擊 ===
肥胖不僅對健康產生影響，也帶來許多其他面向的問題，比方說就業困難<ref name="Puhl R. p.29">Puhl R., Henderson K., and Brownell K. 2005 p.29</ref>。有研究指出，肥胖程度會與個人的薪資高低呈現負相關<ref>{{cite journal | author = Johansson E, Böckerman P, Kiiskinen U, Heliövaara M | title = Obesity and labour market success in Finland: The difference between having a high BMI and being fat | journal = Economics and Human Biology | volume = 7 | issue = 1 | pages = 36–45 | year = 2009 | pmid = 19249259 | doi = 10.1016/j.ehb.2009.01.008 }}</ref>。肥胖的影響廣及社會中的各個層面，下至個人、公司行號，上達政府。2005年美國醫療支出中有20.6%與肥胖相關，估計為1902億美元<ref name=medical-costs>{{cite journal | author = Cawley J, Meyerhoefer C | title = The medical care costs of obesity: An instrumental variables approach | journal = Journal of Health Economics | volume = 31 | issue = 1 | pages = 219–230 | date = 2012-01 | pmid = 22094013 | doi = 10.1016/j.jhealeco.2011.10.003 | accessdate = 2012-08-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Finkelstein EA, Fiebelkorn IA, Wang G |title=National medical spending attributable to overweight and obesity: How much, and who's paying |journal=Health Affairs |volume=Online |issue=May |pages= |date=2003-01|url=http://content.healthaffairs.org/cgi/content/full/hlthaff.w3.219v1/DC1 |doi=10.1377/hlthaff.w3.219}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cdc.gov/nccdphp/dnpa/obesity/economic_consequences.htm |title=Obesity and overweight: Economic consequences |publisher=Centers for Disease Control and Prevention |date=2007-05-22 |accessdate=2007-09-05}}</ref>；加拿大在1997年時，肥胖佔據了2.4%的醫療支出，達到20億加幣<ref name="CADG2006"/>；澳洲2005年有關肥胖的醫療支出高達210億澳幣，過重與肥胖的澳洲人從政府領取了356億澳幣的各式補助<ref name="MJA2009">{{cite journal | author = Colagiuri S, Lee CM, Colagiuri R, Magliano D, Shaw JE, Zimmet PZ, Caterson ID | title = The cost of overweight and obesity in Australia | journal = The Medical Journal of Australia | volume = 192 | issue = 5 | pages = 260–4 | year = 2010 | pmid = 20201759 | doi = | url = http://www.mja.com.au/public/issues/192_05_010310/col10841_fm.html | type = Comparative Study }}</ref>。在美國，每年花在食物上的開銷估計則有四百到一千億美元<ref>{{cite news|last=Cummings |first=Laura |title=The diet business: Banking on failure |publisher=BBC News |date=2003-02-05 |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/business/2725943.stm |accessdate=2009-02-25}}</ref>。

目前已有預防肥胖的方案，希望可以降低和肥胖有關疾病的醫療成本。不過人若活的越久，其醫療的費用本來就會提高。因此研究者的結論是減少肥胖有助於提昇公眾的健康，但不太可能減少整體的醫療費用<ref>{{cite journal | author = van Baal PH, Polder JJ, de Wit GA, Hoogenveen RT, Feenstra TL, Boshuizen HC, Engelfriet PM, Brouwer WB | title = Lifetime Medical Costs of Obesity: Prevention No Cure for Increasing Health Expenditure | journal = PLoS Med. | volume = 5 | issue = 2 | pages = e29 | date = 2008-02 | pmid = 18254654 | pmc = 2225430 | doi = 10.1371/journal.pmed.0050029 | url = http://www.plosmedicine.org/article/info:doi/10.1371/journal.pmed.0050029 | type = Comparative Study }}</ref>。

[[File:Wide_Chair.jpg|thumb]]
肥胖可能帶來社交上的汙名化與就業困難<ref name="Puhl R. p.29"/>。比起一般體重的人，肥胖的員工較常缺席與請病假，因此對於雇主來說會造成人事成本上升與生產力下降<ref>{{cite journal | author = Neovius K, Johansson K, Kark M, Neovius M | title = Obesity status and sick leave: a systematic review | journal = Obes Rev | volume = 10 | issue = 1 | pages = 17–27 | date = 2009-01 | pmid = 18778315 | doi = 10.1111/j.1467-789X.2008.00521.x | url = | type = Review }}</ref>。對於杜克大學雇員進行的研究顯示，[[身體質量指數|身體質量指數]]（BMI）超過40公斤/平方公尺的雇員申請{{link-en|勞工災害補償保險|Workers compensation}}的頻率是常人（BMI介於18.5至24.9公斤/平方公尺）的兩倍，無法工作的日數則比常人多了十二倍。對於BMI大於40的人而言，最常見到的傷害發生在下肢、掌腕、背部，這些傷害通常源自跌倒與攀爬<ref>{{cite journal | author = Ostbye T, Dement JM, Krause KM | title = Obesity and workers' compensation: Results from the Duke Health and Safety Surveillance System | journal = Arch. Intern. Med. | volume = 167 | issue = 8 | pages = 766–73 | year = 2007 | pmid = 17452538 | doi = 10.1001/archinte.167.8.766 | type = Research Support }}</ref>。阿拉巴馬州雇員保險委員會通過一項爭議性的方案，當中規定過重的雇員若不接受減重方案以改善健康，每月就需多付25美元的健康保險費，這個方案在2010年一月開始施行，主要針對BMI超過35公斤/平方公尺且在一年後仍無法改善自身健康狀況的雇員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.webmd.com/diet/news/20080825/alabama-obesity-penalty-stirs-debate |title=Alabama "Obesity Penalty" Stirs Debate |work=Don Fernandez |accessdate=2009-04-05}}</ref>。

部分研究顯示肥胖的人在就職與升遷都比常人困難<ref name=Bias2001/>，即使在同樣工作內容的職位上，肥胖的人的薪資也相對較低（肥胖的女性的薪資較平均低6%；肥胖的男性則低3%）<ref>Puhl R., Henderson K., and Brownell K. 2005 p.30</ref>。

肥胖對部分行業（包括航空、醫療、以及飲食）會產生其他附加影響。舉例來說，因為肥胖率的上升，航空公司必須付出更高的燃料費，並面臨加大座位的壓力<ref>{{cite web|author=Lisa DiCarlo |url=http://www.forbes.com/2002/10/24/cx_ld_1024obese.html |title=Why Airlines Can't Cut The Fat |work=Forbes.com |date=2002-10-24 |accessdate=2008-07-23}}</ref>。光是在2000年，肥胖旅客就讓航空公司多花了2.75億美元<ref>{{cite journal | author = Dannenberg AL, Burton DC, Jackson RJ | title = Economic and environmental costs of obesity: The impact on airlines | journal = American journal of preventive medicine | volume = 27 | issue = 3 | page = 264 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15450642 | doi = 10.1016/j.amepre.2004.06.004 | type = Letter }}</ref>。醫療業必須為了肥胖的患者準備特別的設備，比方說升降梯或者是{{link-en|大型救護車|bariatric ambulance}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://abcnews.go.com/Health/Diet/obese-health-care-bariatric-ambulances/story?id=7981746 |title=Who Should Pay for Obese Health Care? |author=Lauren Cox |publisher=ABC News |date=2009-07-02 |accessdate=2012-08-06}}</ref>。餐飲業則面臨他們提供的飲食造成肥胖的訴訟，進而將訴訟成本轉嫁到價格上<ref name=Govtrack>{{cite web|url=http://www.govtrack.us/congress/bill.xpd?bill=h109-554 |title=109th U.S. Congress (2005–2006) H.R. 554: 109th U.S. Congress (2005–2006) H.R. 554: Personal Responsibility in Food Consumption Act of 2005 |publisher=GovTrack.us |accessdate=2008-07-24}}</ref>。2005年美國國會研議立法禁止人民向餐飲業因「造成肥胖」提出訴訟，但並沒有通過<ref name=Govtrack/>。

[[美國醫學會|美國醫學會]]在2013年將肥胖列入慢性病<ref name=NYTimes20130618/>，希望藉此鼓勵健康保險業者將更多針對肥胖的治療、諮詢、及手術列入給付，如果保險業者願意投資，減肥藥物或基因療法的發展成本門檻也會降低<ref name=WashPost20130620/>。但由於美國醫學會的分類方式並沒有法律上的強制力，保險業者仍然有拒絕給付肥胖治療與處置的權利<ref name=WashPost20130620>{{cite web |url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/innovations/wp/2013/06/20/a-changing-battlefield-in-the-fight-against-fat/?wpisrc=nl_tech_b |title=A changing battlefield in the fight against fat |last=Basulto |first=Dominic |date=2013-06-20 |website=The Washington Post |publisher= |accessdate=2013-06-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/6HWwftXgI?url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/innovations/wp/2013/06/20/a-changing-battlefield-in-the-fight-against-fat/?wpisrc=nl_tech_b |archivedate=2013-06-21 }} ()</ref>。

2014年，[[欧洲法院|歐洲法院]]認定病態肥胖為殘障，指出若雇員的肥胖狀況使其無法「和其他雇員以平等的立足點，有效且全職的參與工作」，這樣的肥胖就應該視為殘障，而因為肥胖解雇員工也將視同歧視行為<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.reuters.com/article/2014/12/18/eu-courts-obesity-idUSL1N0U20PE20141218|title = Obesity can be deemed a disability at work - EU court|date = 2014-12-18|accessdate = 2014-12-18|website = Reuters|publisher = }}</ref>。

===體型與認同===

肥胖認同運動主要的目的是減少對過重與肥胖者的歧視<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.size-acceptance.org/mission.html |title=ISAA Mission Statement |work=International Size Acceptance Association |accessdate=2009-02-17}}</ref>，有些認同運動者甚至試圖主張肥胖與身體健康並沒有負相關性<ref name=Pulver2007>{{cite book|author=Pulver, Adam |title=An Imperfect Fit: Obesity, Public Health, and Disability Anti-Discrimination Law |publisher=Social Science Electronic Publishing |location= |year=2007 |pages= |isbn= |oclc= |doi= |url=http://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=1316106 |accessdate=2009-01-13}}</ref>。主張認同肥胖的團體在二十世紀後半開始增加<ref>{{cite journal | author = Neumark-Sztainer D | title = The weight dilemma: a range of philosophical perspectives | journal = Int. J. Obes. Relat. Metab. Disord. | volume = 23 Suppl 2 | issue =  | pages = S31–7 | date = 1999-03 | pmid = 10340803 | doi = 10.1038/sj.ijo.0800857 | url = | type = Review }}</ref>，{{link-en|國家肥胖認同推廣協會|National Association to Advance Fat Acceptance}}成立於1969年，主要的活動範圍在美國，定位為倡議消除體型歧視的人權團體<ref>{{cite web|author=National Association to Advance Fat Acceptance |url=http://www.naafaonline.com/dev2/ |title=We come in all sizes |publisher=NAAFA |year=2008 |accessdate=2008-07-29}}</ref>。{{link-en|國際體型認同協會|International Size Acceptance Association}}則是創立於1997年的[[非政府組織|非政府組織]]，它的宗旨是推動體型認同並消除體重歧視<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.size-acceptance.org/ |title=International Size Acceptance Association – ISAA |work=International Size Acceptance Association |accessdate=2009-01-13}}</ref>，活動範圍廣及全球。這些倡議團體主張肥胖應列入[[美國殘障者法案|美國殘障者法案]]，但立法當局則認為肥胖帶來的公共衛生成本超過將肥胖列入這部反歧視法案帶來的好處<ref name="Pulver2007" />。

==兒童肥胖==
對兒童來說，健康的身體質量指數（BMI）範圍會因年齡和性別而有所不同。兒童與青少年的{{le|兒童肥胖|Childhood obesity|肥胖標準}}是BMI超過95[[百分位数|百分位]]<ref name="cdc.gov"/>，然而該參考常模來自於1963年至1994年間的研究資料，因此並未計入近年來肥胖率的上升<ref name="Flegal KM, Ogden CL, Wei R, Kuczmarski RL, Johnson CL 2001 1086–93"/>。兒童的肥胖在二十一世紀已經成為一種流行病，不論是在[[發展中國家|發展中國家]]或是[[已開發國家|已開發國家]]，兒童肥胖率都不斷上升。在加拿大，兒童肥胖率自1980年代的11%爬升到1990年代的30%，在此同期，巴西的兒童肥胖率則自4%上升到14%<ref name=flynn2006/>。

如同成人的肥胖，兒童肥胖也受許多不同的因素影響。飲食習慣改變與運動量下降被認為是近年來兒童肥胖率上升的主要推手<ref>{{cite journal | author = Dollman J, Norton K, Norton L | title = Evidence for secular trends in children's physical activity behaviour | journal = Br J Sports Med | volume = 39 | issue = 12 | pages = 892–7; discussion 897 | date = 2005-12 | pmid = 16306494 | pmc = 1725088 | doi = 10.1136/bjsm.2004.016675 | url = | type = Review }}</ref>。由於兒童肥胖常持續到成人，並與許多慢性病息息相關，肥胖兒童經常需要篩檢是否患有[[高血壓|高血壓]]、[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]、[[高血脂|高血脂]]、以及[[脂肪肝|脂肪肝]]<ref name=CADG2006/>。兒童肥胖的治療主要是改變生活型態以及行為治療，但嘗試增加兒童的運動量的效果並不明顯<ref name="pmid23044984">{{cite journal | author = Metcalf B, Henley W, Wilkin T | title = Effectiveness of intervention on physical activity of children: systematic review and meta-analysis of controlled trials with objectively measured outcomes (EarlyBird 54) | journal = BMJ (Clinical Research Ed.) | volume = 345 | issue = | pages = e5888 | year = 2012 | pmid = 23044984 | doi = 10.1136/bmj.e5888 | type=Review, Meta-analysis }}</ref>。目前美國[[食品藥物管理署|食品藥物管理署]]並未核准任何藥物用於兒童肥胖<ref name=flynn2006>{{cite journal | author = Flynn MA, McNeil DA, Maloff B, Mutasingwa D, Wu M, Ford C, Tough SC | title = Reducing obesity and related chronic disease risk in children and youth: a synthesis of evidence with 'best practice' recommendations | journal = Obes Rev | volume = 7 Suppl 1 | issue =  | pages = 7–66 | date = 2006-02 | pmid = 16371076 | doi = 10.1111/j.1467-789X.2006.00242.x | type = Review }}</ref>。

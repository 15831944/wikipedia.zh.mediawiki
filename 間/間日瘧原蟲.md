'''間日瘧原蟲'''（'''''Plasmodium vivax'''''）是一種[[瘧原蟲|瘧原蟲]]，可引發間日瘧<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = White NJ | title = Plasmodium knowlesi: the fifth human malaria parasite | journal = Clinical Infectious Diseases | volume = 46 | issue = 2 | pages = 172–3 | date = January 2008 | pmid = 18171246 | doi = 10.1086/524889 }}</ref>。它引發的症狀雖不如[[惡性瘧原蟲|惡性瘧原蟲]]那麼劇烈，但也會導致感染者死亡，直接死因一般是[[脾肿大|脾肿大]]<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Baird JK | title = Neglect of Plasmodium vivax malaria | journal = Trends in Parasitology | volume = 23 | issue = 11 | pages = 533–9 | date = November 2007 | pmid = 17933585 | doi = 10.1016/j.pt.2007.08.011 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Anstey NM, Douglas NM, Poespoprodjo JR, Price RN | title = Plasmodium vivax: clinical spectrum, risk factors and pathogenesis | journal = Advances in Parasitology | volume = 80 | pages = 151–201 | year = 2012 | pmid = 23199488 | doi = 10.1016/b978-0-12-397900-1.00003-7 | isbn = 9780123979001 | series = Advances in Parasitology }}</ref>。它僅由雌性[[瘧蚊|瘧蚊]]攜帶<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Crompton PD, Moebius J, Portugal S, Waisberg M, Hart G, Garver LS, Miller LH, Barillas-Mury C, Pierce SK | title = Malaria immunity in man and mosquito: insights into unsolved mysteries of a deadly infectious disease | journal = Annual Review of Immunology | volume = 32 | issue = 1 | pages = 157–87 | date = 2014 | pmid = 24655294 | doi = 10.1146/annurev-immunol-032713-120220 | pmc=4075043}}</ref>。每次發作間隔時間為48小時，與[[卵形瘧|卵形瘧]]相同<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cdc.gov.tw/professional/info.aspx?treeid=4c19a0252bbef869&nowtreeid=4dc827595f55c334&tid=057581BDCA39C82E|title=瘧疾|publisher=衛生福利部疾病管控署|date=2018-04-16}}</ref>。

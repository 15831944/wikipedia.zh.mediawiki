'''間歇性斷食'''（{{lang-en|Intermittent fasting}}，缩写：IF）是種間歇性的進食方式通稱，包括在一個期間內進行斷食與進食循環。間歇性斷食可能有助於體重減輕，產生相當於長期限制卡路里的效果。<ref name="ANRENUPATTERSON2017">{{cite journal|title=Metabolic Effects of Intermittent Fasting|first1=Ruth E.|last2=Sears|first2=Dorothy D.|date=2017-08-21|journal=Annual Review of Nutrition|issue=1|doi=10.1146/annurev-nutr-071816-064634|volume=37|pages=371–393|last1=Patterson}}</ref><ref name="Davis2015">{{cite journal|title=Intermittent energy restriction and weight loss: a systematic review|first1=C S|last2=Clarke|first2=R E|date=2015-11-25|journal=European Journal of Clinical Nutrition|publisher=Nature|issue=3|doi=10.1038/ejcn.2015.195|volume=70|pages=292–299|last3=Coulter|first3=S N|last4=Rounsefell|first4=K N|last5=Walker|first5=R E|last6=Rauch|first6=C E|last7=Huggins|first7=C E|last8=Ryan|first8=L|last1=Davis}}</ref><ref name="Johnstone2014">{{cite journal|title=Fasting for weight loss: an effective strategy or latest dieting trend?|first1=A|date=2014-12-26|journal=International Journal of Obesity|publisher=Nature|issue=5|doi=10.1038/ijo.2014.214|volume=39|pages=727–733|last1=Johnstone}}</ref><ref name="Tinsley2015">{{cite journal|title=Effects of intermittent fasting on body composition and clinical health markers in humans|first1=Grant M.|last2=La Bounty|first2=Paul M.|date=October 2015|journal=Nutrition Reviews|issue=10|doi=10.1093/nutrit/nuv041|volume=73|pages=661–674|last1=Tinsley}}</ref>

== 實踐與變化 ==
間歇性斷食可能是減少熱量攝取，減輕體重，減少體脂肪量及改善胰島素敏感性的可行策略。<ref name="ANRENUPATTERSON2017" /><ref name="Johnstone2014" /><ref name="Tinsley2015" /><ref>{{Cite journal|title=Intermittent Fasting and Human Metabolic Health|first1=R. E|last2=Laughlin|first2=G. A|journal=Journal of the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics|issue=8|doi=10.1016/j.jand.2015.02.018|year=2015|volume=115|pages=1203–1212|language=en|issn=2212-2672|pmc=4516560|pmid=25857868|last3=Lacroix|first3=A. Z|last4=Hartman|first4=S. J|last5=Natarajan|first5=L|last6=Senger|first6=C. M|last7=Martínez|first7=M. E|last8=Villaseñor|first8=A|last9=Sears|first9=D. D|author1=Patterson|last10=Marinac|first10=C. R|last11=Gallo|first11=L. C}}</ref><ref name="mattson17">{{cite journal|title=Impact of intermittent fasting on health and disease processes|first1=M. P|last2=Longo|first2=V. D|journal=Ageing Research Reviews|doi=10.1016/j.arr.2016.10.005|year=2017|volume=39|pages=46–58|pmc=5411330|pmid=27810402|last3=Harvie|first3=M|author1=Mattson}}</ref> 間歇性斷食方案可分為兩種：全天斷食法和限時進食法。

* 全天斷食法包括定期斷食一整天。最嚴格的形式是隔日斷食（ADF），也就是 24 小時斷食，然後接著進入 24 小時非斷食期，不斷循環。<ref name="Varady2011">{{cite journal|title=Intermittent versus daily calorie restriction: Which diet regimen is more effective for weight loss?|first1=K. A|journal=Obesity Reviews|issue=7|doi=10.1111/j.1467-789X.2011.00873.x|year=2011|volume=12|pages=e593–601|pmid=21410865|last1=Varady}}</ref> 改良版隔日斷食（ADMF）和 5：2 飲食 - 後者定義為每週五天不斷食，每週兩天進行斷食或改良的斷食 - 兩者都允許在斷食日消耗大約500-600卡路里。<ref name="ANRENUPATTERSON2017" /><ref name="Johnstone2014" /><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/howto/guide/what-52-diet|title=What is the 5:2 diet?|author=Fisher, Roxanne|date=2016-06-01|publisher=BBC GoodFood, Worldwide}}</ref><ref name="fleming">{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/wordofmouth/2015/jan/27/fasting-facts-is-the-52-diet-too-good-to-be-true|title=Fasting facts: is the 5:2 diet too good to be true?|first1=Amy|date=2015-01-27|newspaper=The Guardian|accessdate=2018-01-18|last1=Fleming}}</ref>
* 限時進食法 (TRF)  意指每天只在有限的幾小時內進食。<ref name="pmid24739093">{{cite journal|title=Time-restricted feeding and risk of metabolic disease: A review of human and animal studies|first1=Jeff|last2=Hoddy|first2=Kristin K|journal=Nutrition Reviews|issue=5|doi=10.1111/nure.12104|year=2014|volume=72|pages=308–18|pmid=24739093|last3=Jambazian|first3=Pera|last4=Varady|first4=Krista A|last1=Rothschild}}</ref>  最常見的限時進食法 (TRF) 包括每天斷食16小時，只准在其餘的 8 小時內吃東西，通常每天在相同的時間內進食。<ref name="pmid27737674">{{cite journal|title=Effects of eight weeks of time-restricted feeding (16/8) on basal metabolism, maximal strength, body composition, inflammation, and cardiovascular risk factors in resistance-trained males|first1=Tatiana|last2=Tinsley|first2=Grant|journal=Journal of Translational Medicine|issue=1|doi=10.1186/s12967-016-1044-0|year=2016|volume=14|pages=290|pmc=5064803|pmid=27737674|last3=Bianco|first3=Antonino|last4=Marcolin|first4=Giuseppe|last5=Pacelli|first5=Quirico Francesco|last6=Battaglia|first6=Giuseppe|last7=Palma|first7=Antonio|last8=Gentil|first8=Paulo|last9=Neri|first9=Marco|last1=Moro|last10=Paoli|first10=Antonio}}</ref> 較為彈性的做法是12小時的斷食配合12小時的飲食，或更嚴格形式是每天只吃一餐，這將涉及每天約23小時的斷食<ref>{{cite journal|title=A controlled trial of reduced meal frequency without caloric restriction in healthy, normal-weight, middle-aged adults|url=http://ajcn.nutrition.org/content/85/4/981.full.pdf|first1=KS|last2=Baer|first2=DJ|journal=The American Journal of Clinical Nutrition|issue=4|year=2007|volume=85|pages=981–8|pmc=2645638|pmid=17413096|last3=Spears|first3=K|last4=Paul|first4=DR|last5=Harris|first5=GK|last6=Rumpler|first6=WV|last7=Strycula|first7=P|last8=Najjar|first8=SS|last9=Ferrucci|first9=L|last1=Stote|last10=Ingram|first10=D. K.|last11=Longo|first11=D. L.|last12=Mattson|first12=M. P.}}</ref>

斷食期間可以輔助食用的東西因人而異，建議視健康情況調整。有些人主張只能喝水，有些人認為也能喝茶或咖啡（不加糖、奶）或添加[[甜味剂|甘味劑]]的零卡路里飲料，因為斷食可能會增加脫水的風險。<ref name="CNN20180730">{{cite web|url=https://edition.cnn.com/2018/03/23/health/intermittent-fasting-food-drayer/index.html|title=How to succeed at intermittent fasting|accessdate=2018-07-30|date=2018-03-23|last1=CNN|first1=Lisa Drayer|website=CNN}}</ref>  斷食形式的變化型包括改良性斷食，如改良版隔日斷食 (ADMF)，意指在斷食期間還是會攝入有限的熱量（正常值的20％），而不是完全不攝入任何熱量，以改善斷食者的耐受性和情緒。.<ref name="ANRENUPATTERSON2017" /><ref name="Johnstone2014" /> 間歇性斷食與週期性斷食（持續兩天或更多天）的持續時間不同（最多48小時).<ref name="ANRENUPATTERSON2017" /><ref>{{cite journal|title=Impact of intermittent fasting on health and disease processes|first1=Mark P.|last2=Longo|first2=Valter D.|date=October 2017|journal=Ageing Research Reviews|doi=10.1016/j.arr.2016.10.005|volume=39|pages=46–58|last3=Harvie|first3=Michelle|last1=Mattson}}</ref>

== 初步研究 ==
2018 年一份對肥胖者進行間歇性斷食實驗的文獻回顧表明，減少每週一至六天的卡路里攝入量，持續長達至少 12 週以上，平均可協助受試者減輕 7 公斤的體重。<ref name="harris">{{cite journal|title=Intermittent fasting interventions for treatment of overweight and obesity in adults|url=https://www.fisiologiadelejercicio.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/Intermittent-fasting-interventions-for-treatment-of.pdf|first1=Leanne|last2=Hamilton|first2=Sharon|date=February 2018|journal=JBI Database of Systematic Reviews and Implementation Reports|issue=2|doi=10.11124/JBISRIR-2016-003248|volume=16|pages=507–547|last3=Azevedo|first3=Liane B.|last4=Olajide|first4=Joan|last5=De Brún|first5=Caroline|last6=Waller|first6=Gillian|last7=Whittaker|first7=Vicki|last8=Sharp|first8=Tracey|last9=Lean|first9=Mike|last1=Harris|last10=Hankey|first10=Catherine|last11=Ells|first11=Louisa}}</ref> 研究認為間歇性斷食法的減肥功效，與肥胖者在短期內連續斷食減肥，是同樣有效。<ref name="harris" /> 但 2014 年的文獻回顧也警告，間歇性斷食尚未在兒童，老年人或體重不足者的情況下進行全面研究，因此可能對這些人群有害。<ref name="pmid24440038">{{cite journal|title=Fasting: Molecular Mechanisms and Clinical Applications|first1=Valter D|last2=Mattson|first2=Mark P|journal=Cell Metabolism|issue=2|doi=10.1016/j.cmet.2013.12.008|year=2014|volume=19|pages=181–92|pmc=3946160|pmid=24440038|last1=Longo}}</ref>

在其他初步研究中，隔日斷食法 (ADF) 對體重減輕是有效的，其有效程度與卡路里限制相去不遠，<ref name="Davis2015" /><ref name="Johnstone2014" /> 肥胖者和體重正常者都觀察到了體重減輕的情況。<ref name="Tinsley2015" /> 且初步證據表明，幾種心血管問題和代謝指標（如體脂， [[低密度脂蛋白|低密度脂蛋白 LDL]]， [[三酸甘油酯|三酸甘油酯]]，胰島素敏感性，血壓等）也得到改善。<ref name="mattson17" /><ref name="Anton2018">{{cite journal|title=Flipping the Metabolic Switch: Understanding and Applying the Health Benefits of Fasting|first1=Stephen D.|last2=Moehl|first2=Keelin|date=February 2018|journal=Obesity|issue=2|doi=10.1002/oby.22065|volume=26|pages=254–268|pmc=5783752|pmid=9086496|last3=Donahoo|first3=William T.|last4=Marosi|first4=Krisztina|last5=Lee|first5=Stephanie A.|last6=Mainous|first6=Arch G.|last7=Leeuwenburgh|first7=Christiaan|last8=Mattson|first8=Mark P.|last1=Anton}}</ref><ref name="Almeneessier2018">{{cite journal|title=Intermittent Fasting, Insufficient Sleep, and Circadian Rhythm: Interaction and Effects on the Cardiometabolic System|first1=Aljohara S.|last2=Pandi-Perumal|first2=Seithikurippu R.|date=2018-07-19|journal=Current Sleep Medicine Reports|doi=10.1007/s40675-018-0124-5|last3=BaHammam|first3=Ahmed S.|last1=Almeneessier}}</ref> 且隔日斷食 (ADF)，也就是每隔 24 小時進行一次斷食的方法，並未影響除去脂肪的淨體重。<ref name="Davis2015" /><ref name="Johnstone2014" /><ref name="Tinsley2015" />

間歇性斷食可能減少[[快速動眼期|睡眠中的快速動眼期]]，<ref name="ANRENUPATTERSON2017" /><ref name="Almeneessier2018" /> 但其他對大腦功能的影響仍未確定。<ref name="pmid24440038" /><ref>{{cite journal|title=Effects of Intermittent Fasting, Caloric Restriction, and Ramadan Intermittent Fasting on Cognitive Performance at Rest and During Exercise in Adults|first1=Anissa|last2=Roelands|first2=Bart|date=2015-10-05|journal=Sports Medicine|issue=1|doi=10.1007/s40279-015-0408-6|volume=46|pages=35–47|pmid=26438184|last3=Meeusen|first3=Romain|last4=Chamari|first4=Karim|last1=Cherif}}</ref> 沒有任何證據證明斷食可以預防或治療癌症。<ref name="NACRe">{{cite web|url=https://www6.inra.fr/nacre/Le-reseau-NACRe/Outils-pour-professionnels/Rapport-NACRe-jeune-regimes-restrictifs-cancer-2017|title=Réseau NACRe - Réseau National Alimentation Cancer Recherche - Rapport NACRe jeûne regimes restrictifs cancer 2017|accessdate=2018-09-19|date=November 2017|language=fr|website=www6.inra.fr}}</ref>

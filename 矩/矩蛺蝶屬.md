'''矩蛺蝶屬'''（'''Lilac Beauty'''，學名：'''''Salamis'''''）是[[蛺蝶亞科|蛺蝶亞科]][[眼蛺蝶族|眼蛺蝶族]]中的一個[[屬|屬]]。物種分佈於[[埃塞俄比亞界|埃塞俄比亞界]]<ref>Brower, Andrew V. Z. 2010. Salamis Boisduval 1833. the Lilac Beauties. Version 21 July 2010 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Salamis/70321/2010.07.21 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/</ref>。曾被視作此屬[[異名|異名]]或下級種團的''[[Protogoniomorpha|Protogoniomorpha]]''已成為獨立一屬<ref>Wahlberg N, Brower AVZ, and Nylin S. 2005. Phylogenetic relationships of tribes and genera in the subfamily Nymphalinae (Lepidoptera: Nymphalidae) based on three gene sequences. Biol. J. Linn. Soc. 86: 227-251.</ref>。

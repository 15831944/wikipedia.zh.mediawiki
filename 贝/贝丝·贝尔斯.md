'''贝丝·贝尔斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Beth Behrs'''}}，{{bd|1985年|12月26日|||catIdx= B贝}}）出生于[[美国|美国]][[宾夕法尼亚州|宾夕法尼亚州]][[兰开斯特_(宾夕法尼亚州)|兰开斯特]]，美国女演员，因出演《美国派：索爱天书》而知名全美。<ref name="cbspage">{{Cite web |title=2 Broke Girls Cast: Beth Behrs |url=http://www.cbs.com/shows/2_broke_girls/cast/61709/?pg=1 |publisher=[[CBS|CBS]] |accessdate=2011-10-05 }}</ref>

== 生平 ==
1985年，贝尔斯出生于[[美国|美国]][[宾夕法尼亚州|宾夕法尼亚州]][[兰开斯特_(宾夕法尼亚州)|兰开斯特]]，父亲大卫·贝丝的，是一个[[学校|学校]]的[[校长|校长]]，她是家中的长女，母亲莫林·贝丝，是一个一年级的[[小学|小学]][[教师|教师]]。<ref name="adams">{{Cite news |first=Marv |last=Adams |date=2011-06-05 |title=A star was born (here) |publisher=[[Lancaster_Online|Lancaster Online]] |url=http://lancasteronline.com/article/local/400585_A-star-was-born--here-.html |accessdate=2011-09-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131214160731/http://lancasteronline.com/article/local/400585_A-star-was-born--here-.html |archivedate=2013-12-14 }}</ref><ref name="behrs"/> 她有个小她六岁的妹妹.<ref name="loughlin">{{Cite news |first=Sue |last=Loughlin |date=2007-10-26 |title=The Woods installs first male president |newspaper=[[Tribune-Star|Tribune-Star]] |url=http://tribstar.com/local/x1155716620/The-Woods-installs-first-male-president |accessdate=2011-09-16 }}</ref>
1989年，贝尔斯家搬迁到[[弗吉尼亚州|弗吉尼亚州]][[林奇堡_(弗吉尼亚州)|林奇堡市]]，并且在那里长大。<ref name="behrs">{{Cite news |first=David |last=Behrs |date=2011-06-12 |title=Letters to the editor - June 12, 2011 |publisher=Lancaster Online |url=http://lancasteronline.com/article/local/404015_Letters-to-the-editor---June-12--2011.html |accessdate=2011-09-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120609114000/http://lancasteronline.com/article/local/404015_Letters-to-the-editor---June-12--2011.html |archivedate=六月 9, 2012 }}</ref>
4岁时，贝尔斯开始学习表演，[[足球|足球]]运动伴随她的成长。<ref name="werts">{{Cite news |first=Diane |last=Werts |date=2011-09-09 |title=Behrs bears up to be a 'Broke' girl |magazine=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118042117 |accessdate=2011-09-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120313174609/http://www.variety.com/article/VR1118042117 |archivedate=2012-03-13 }}</ref> 
15岁时，贝尔斯家搬迁到在[[旧金山|旧金山]]海湾地区的[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[马林县|马林县]]。<ref name="crocker">{{Cite news |first=Amy |last=Crocker |date=2009-12-30 |title=SFist Interviews: Beth Behrs, a Direct-to-DVD American Pie Sequel Actress from Marin |publisher=[[SFist|SFist]] |url=http://sfist.com/2009/12/30/sfist_interviews_a_direct-to-dvd_am.php |accessdate=2011-09-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100102101620/http://sfist.com/2009/12/30/sfist_interviews_a_direct-to-dvd_am.php |archivedate=2010-01-02 }}</ref>
2001年，贝尔斯在Tamalpais High School读高中。<ref name="larson">{{Cite news |first=Vicki |last=Larson |date=2011-09-20 |title=Marin's Beth Behr is 1 lucky girl for role in '2 Broke Girls' |newspaper=[[Marin_Independent_Journal|Marin Independent Journal]] |url=http://www.mercurynews.com/tv/ci_18929386 |accessdate=2011-09-21 }}</ref>
2004年，贝尔斯搬迁到加利福尼亚[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]。

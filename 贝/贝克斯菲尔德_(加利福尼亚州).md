'''贝克斯菲尔德'''（{{lang en|'''Bakersfield'''}}），美国华裔普遍简称'''贝克菲尔'''位于[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]]中南部，是[[克恩县|克恩县]]的县治所在。{{GR|6}}根據[[美國2010年人口普查|2010年人口普查]]，本城共有人口347,483人。<ref>[http://factfinder2.census.gov/ 2010 census factfinder2]</ref><ref>[http://www.dof.ca.gov/research/demographic/state_census_data_center/census_2010/documents/2010Census_Pop_Rankers.xls Top Population Rank: Cities 2000 and 2010] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721033303/http://www.dof.ca.gov/research/demographic/state_census_data_center/census_2010/documents/2010Census_Pop_Rankers.xls |date=2011-07-21 }}. California Department of Finance. Retrieved March 14, 2012.</ref>本城於1869年成立<ref name=Historic>{{Cite book |author= Mildred Brooke Hoover, Douglas E. Kyle |title= Historic Spots in California|publisher=[[史丹福大學出版社|Stanford University Press]] |isbn=978-0-8047-1734-2 |year=1990 |url= http://books.google.com/books?id=p2WrAAAAIAAJ&printsec=frontcover&lr=&source=gbs_book_other_versions_r&cad=1_1#PPA128,M1|page=128}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bakersfieldmagazine.net/arts/kern-history/456-kern-county-shapers |title=Kern County Shapers |publisher=Bakersfield Magazine |first=Gordon F. |last=Lull |accessdate=2013-01-17 |quote=Baker ...was admitted to the Ohio bar at age 19 and later appointed a colonel in his home state's militia. |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121230192356/http://www.bakersfieldmagazine.net/arts/kern-history/456-kern-county-shapers |archivedate=2012-12-30 }}</ref>，是美國第二大不直接連接到一條[[州際公路|州際公路]]的城市。<ref>[http://www.bakersfieldchamber.org/images/documents/8137_3659.PDF Community Profile] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140630071628/http://www.bakersfieldchamber.org/images/documents/8137_3659.PDF |date=2014-06-30 }}. Greater Bakersfield Chamber of Commerce. Page 4. Retrieved March 14, 2012.</ref>農業、石油、加工和製造業是城市主要的經濟。<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.archive.org/stream/up5californiawater20178calirich/up5californiawater20178calirich_djvu.txt |title=Full text of "California water"|work=CONTROLLING THE KERN RIVER Problems|publisher= archive.org| accessdate=2009-04-07}}</ref>贝克斯菲尔德是加州第9大城市，美國第51大城市。

==地理==
根據[[美國2000年人口普查|2000年人口普查]]，本城面積為{{convert|143.6|sqmi|sqkm|2}}，其中{{convert|142.2|sqmi|sqkm|2}}為陸地，{{convert|1.4|sqmi|sqkm|2}}為水域。<ref name ="Gazetteer files">{{cite web|title=US Gazetteer files 2010|url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2013-01-25}}</ref>本城延伸至[[紅杉國家森林|紅杉國家森林]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bakersfieldhelpwanted.org/ |title=Bakersfield Geography |work=source |accessdate=2010 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101011234406/http://www.bakersfieldhelpwanted.org/ |archivedate=2010年10月11日 |deadurl=yes }}</ref> 

===氣候===
贝克斯菲尔德擁有[[沙漠氣候|沙漠氣候]]<ref>Peel, M. C., Finlayson, B. L., and McMahon, T. A.: {{tsl|en|Media:Americas Köppen Map original colors.png||Updated world map of the Köppen-Geiger climate classification}}, Hydrol. Earth Syst. Sci., 11, 1633–1644, 200 7.</ref>，其夏季漫長、炎熱、乾燥，冬季短促、涼爽、濕潤。贝克斯菲尔德是美國晴天最多的城市之一。<ref>Ozborn, Liz. [http://www.currentresults.com/Weather-Extremes/US/sunniest.php Sunniest Places in United States].

'''恋人岛礁'''（{{lang-en|Lovers' Isle}}）是位于[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[槟城州|槟城州]][[槟岛|槟岛]]北部海岸旁的一座[[岛礁|岛礁]]。

==历史==
恋人岛礁处于[[乔治市|乔治市]][[峇都丁宜|峇都丁宜]]的海岸外。16世纪，第一批来至欧洲的航海员就是首先登入这个无人居住的岛屿，然后才陆续前往槟城其他地带。<ref name="Penang Island">{{cite web | title=Batu Ferringhi - A Favorite Place For Beach Lovers | website=Penang Island | url=http://www.penang-vacations.com/batu-ferringhi.html | access-date=2018-08-30 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180201121816/http://www.penang-vacations.com/batu-ferringhi.html | archive-date=2018-02-01 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Wijnen">{{cite web | last=Wijnen | first=Ben van | title=Batu Ferringhi | website=Tourisme Maleisië | url=https://www.malaysiasite.nl/batuferringhieng.htm | access-date=2018-08-30 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180208160520/http://www.malaysiasite.nl/batuferringhieng.htm | archive-date=2018-02-08 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Borneo , Malaysia, Sarawak Daily News 2015"/>

==名称由来==
小岛曾经被命名为''Batu Ferringhi''。[[马来语|马来语]]“Batu Ferringhi”指的是葡萄牙人的石岛。[[马来语|马来语]]的“Ferringhi”一词是来自[[阿拉伯语|阿拉伯语]]的，指的是[[法蘭克人|法蘭克人]]。法蘭克人指的是葡萄牙人的后裔。今日[[乔治市|乔治市]]的热门景点[[峇都丁宜|峇都丁宜]]就是以这个岛屿的原名命名的。小岛后来改名为恋人岛礁。新的名称来自当地的一个传说，当时有一对不同种族的恋人由于无法结婚而在这个岛礁自杀。如今，不少恋人经常在低潮的时候到访此岛礁，认为会有好姻缘。<ref name="Borneo , Malaysia, Sarawak Daily News 2015">{{cite web | title=The isle where love is cast in stone – BorneoPost Online - Largest English Daily In Borneo | website=Borneo , Malaysia, Sarawak Daily News | date=2015-05-31 | url=http://www.theborneopost.com/2015/05/31/the-isle-where-love-is-cast-in-stone/ | access-date=2018-08-30 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180801072230/http://www.theborneopost.com/2015/05/31/the-isle-where-love-is-cast-in-stone/ | archive-date=2018-08-01 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Boulos 2015">{{cite web | last=Boulos | first=Nick | title=Penang: great food, golden beaches and urban style | website=The Independent | date=2015-08-24 | url=http://www.independent.co.uk/travel/asia/penang-a-colonial-melting-pot-with-great-food-golden-beaches-and-urban-style-10469368.html | access-date=2018-08-30 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170530083202/http://www.independent.co.uk/travel/asia/penang-a-colonial-melting-pot-with-great-food-golden-beaches-and-urban-style-10469368.html | archive-date=2017-05-30 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

'''非洲白鷺花'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Hydnora africana''）是一種寄生於[[大戟科|大戟科]]根部的植物，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://botany.org/Parasitic_Plants/Hydnora_africana.php|title=Botanical Society of America: ''Hydnora africana''}}</ref>，並取其營養維生<ref name=crazy>{{cite web|last1=Campbell|first1=Dana|url=http://eol.org/pages/5514361/overview |title=Jackal Food (''Hydnora africana'')|work=Encyclopedia of Life|accessdate=28 October 2014}}</ref>，分布於[[非洲|非洲]]南部。全株無[[葉綠素|葉綠素]]，長年只在泥中生長，只有散發含硫化物，氣味如糞的肉質花會在暴雨後露出地面，以吸引[[糞金龜|糞金龜]]和[[葬甲科|葬甲科]][[甲蟲|甲蟲]]到花中<ref>{{cite book |editor1-last=Waser |editor1-first=Nickoloas Merritt |editor2-first=Jeff |editor2-last=Ollerton |chapter=Table 6.1: Angiosperms with rewardless flowers |chapterurl=https://books.google.com/books?id=Fbl5c9fUxTIC&pg=PA126 |title=Plant-Pollinator Interactions: From Specialization to Generalization |year=2006 |publisher=University of Chicago Press |isbn=978-0-226-87400-5 |pages=126–9}}</ref>。花內設有「陷阱」去暫時囚禁甲蟲，以增加為其傳播花粉的機會<ref>{{cite web|title=BSA Parasitic Plant Pages- ''Hydnora africana'' |url=http://www.botany.org/parasitic_plants/Hydnora_africana.php |publisher=Botanical Society of America |accessdate=9 June 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130604124214/http://www.botany.org/parasitic_plants/Hydnora_africana.php |archivedate=4 June 2013 |df= }}</ref>。果實長於土內，需要兩年成熟，果內藏約二萬籽，可食用，味道、口感如[[馬鈴薯|薯]]，亦可提煉成魚網的[[收斂劑|收斂劑]]。

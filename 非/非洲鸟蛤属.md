==分類==
根據[[WoRMS|WoRMS]]，本屬只有三個物種，尚有兩個過往被認為是獨立物種的，現時都認為只是其他物種的同名<ref name="WoRMS_137733" />。這三個物種如下<ref name="WoRMS_137733" />：
* †[[心形非洲鸟蛤|心形非洲鸟蛤]] ''Afrocardium carditaeforme'' <small>({{tsl|en|Lovell Augustus Reeve|洛弗爾·奧古斯都·里夫|Reeve}},1845)</small> ：化石物種<ref>{{cite web |url=http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=100047 |title= †Afrocardium carditaeforme Reeve 1845 (cockle)  |publisher=Fossilworks |accessdate=2014-12-16 |language=en }}</ref>。
* ''Afrocardium exochum'' == [[齿纹非洲鸟蛤|齿纹非洲鸟蛤]] ''Afrocardium infantile'' <small>(Nomura et Zinbo,1934)</small><ref name="WoRMS_137733">{{cite WoRMS | id = 137733 | author = Serge Gofas | year  = 2013 | title = ''Afrocardium'' <small>Tomlin, 1931</small> }}</ref>
* [[爱巴非洲鸟蛤|爱巴非洲鸟蛤]] ''Afrocardium richardi''
* ''Afrocardium shepstonense'':模式種。

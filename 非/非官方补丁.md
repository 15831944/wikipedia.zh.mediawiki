'''非官方补丁'''（unofficial patch）是由[[虛擬社群|用户社区]]而非原始[[软件设计师|开发者]]为一款软件创作的{{tsl|en|Non-commercial|非商业}}[[修補程式|修補程式]]。与普通的官方补丁一样，非官方补丁一般也是为修复[[软件缺陷|软件缺陷]]或劣势制作，而通常不会改变软件的预期用法，这不同于第三方的软件适配（例如[[遊戲模組|遊戲模組]]或{{tsl|en|Software cracking|软件破解|破解补丁}}）。

非官方补丁是一种通常经过万维网传播的[[用户生成内容|用户生成内容]]，可以被视作[[Web_2.0|Web 2.0]]与{{tsl|en|remix culture|重组文化}}的一部分。

== 动机 ==
创建非官方补丁的一个常见原因是缺少软件原作者或提供者的{{tsl|en|Technical support|技术支持}}。一些潜在的原因是：
* 软件产品已达预定的[[產品壽命結束|产品寿命]]并/或被其他产品取代（[[计划报废|计划报废]]）<ref name="obsolescence">{{Cite web|url=http://www.theinquirer.net/inquirer/news/1001739/open-source-weapon-planned-obsolescence|title=Open Source, the only weapon against "planned obsolescence"|accessdate=2012-01-15|date=2007-03-28|last=Cassia|first=Fernando|publisher=theinquirer.net}}</ref>
* 软件最初是为另一个不同的环境设计，并且可以被改进/优化（[[移植_(軟體)|移植]]）
* 开发者已经停产（[[老软件|老软件]]）<ref name="losthistoryss">{{Cite web|url=http://www.g4tv.com/thefeed/blog/post/713030/the-lost-history-of-system-shock|title=The Lost History of System Shock|accessdate=2012-05-30|author=g4tv staff|date=2011-05-30|publisher=g4tv.com|quote=''Looking Glass Studios closed in 2000, a year after System Shock 2's release, and the copyright to the series went into the hands of an insurance company. That left EA with only the System Shock name, but no actual development rights.''}}</ref>
* 官方支持所要求的经济成本过高（例如对小市场的本地化）
* 对紧迫问题的快速解决方案（例如[[计算机安全隐患|安全漏洞]]），如果官方响应过缓<ref name="security">{{Cite web|url=http://www.eweek.com/c/a/Security/Another-Unofficial-WMF-Patch-Released/|title=IT Security & Network Security News - Another Unofficial WMF Patch Released|accessdate=2012-01-07|date=2006-01-04|last=Naraine|first=Ryan|publisher=eweek.com|quote=''Security vendor ESET, makers of the NOD32 anti-virus program, on Wednesday shipped an interim patch for the bug, almost a week before Microsoft Corp. is scheduled to release a properly tested security update.''}}</ref><ref name="zdnet2006">{{Cite web|url=http://www.zdnet.co.uk/news/security-management/2006/01/03/firms-urged-to-use-unauthorised-windows-patch-39245447/|title=Firms urged to use unauthorised Windows patch|accessdate=2012-01-29|date=2006-01-06|last=Espiner|first=Tom|publisher=[[ZDNet|ZDNet]].co.uk}}</ref><ref name="cnn2006">{{Cite web|url=http://money.cnn.com/2006/01/03/technology/windows_virusthreat/index.htm?cnn=yes|title=Huge virus threat rocks Microsoft|accessdate=2012-01-29|author=CNN Staff|date=2006-01-03|publisher=[[CNN|CNN]]money|quote=''Because of the severity of the threat, the SANS Institute, a computer security group, has released a patch for the vulnerability until Microsoft's fix is available next week.''}}</ref>
* 官方开发人员无法解决此问题

== 类型 ==
在[[电脑游戏|电脑游戏]]领域中，非官方补丁最为常见，它们有时也被称为爱好者补丁（Fan patches），尤其是如果为一人独自完成。社区中多名作者共同完成则一般称为社区补丁（Community Patch）。这些补丁着眼典型程序流程中的关键错误，进行崩溃和兼容性方面的修复，例如适配新版[[操作系统|操作系统]]、增加分辨率等。

虽然非官方补丁常见于PC平台，但在主机游戏中也有它们的存在，例如仿真社区中的交流。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.romhacking.net/?page=hacks&genre=&platform=&game=&category=4&perpage=50&title=&author=&hacksearch=Go|title=Hacks - Bugfix|accessdate=2014-08-13|publisher=romhacking.net}}</ref>

; 翻译
另一种非官方补丁是{{tsl|en|Fan translation (video gaming)|爱好者翻译}}（中国称汉化补丁）。爱好者将{{tsl|en|Japanese role-playing game|日本角色扮演游戏}}翻译为英语很是常见，因为这些游戏通常不会为西方市场进行[[本地化|本地化]]。<ref name="ClydeMandelin">{{Cite web|url=http://www.gamasutra.com/view/feature/3891/you_say_tomato_a_pro_on_.php|title=You Say Tomato: A Pro on Fan-Translating Nintendo's Mother 3|accessdate=2011-04-03|date=2008-12-26|last=Parkin|first=Simon|publisher=[[Gamasutra|gamasutra.com]]}}</ref><ref name="retrogamer">{{Cite journal|title=Japanese ROM Translation|url=http://www.hardcoregaming101.net/Fantranslation/Romhacking.htm|last=Szczepaniak|first=John|date=June 2006|journal=Retro Gamer|volume=25|pages=102–105}} [https://web.archive.org/web/20110718200406/http://img145.imageshack.us/img145/6211/tra17fu.jpg][https://web.archive.org/web/20110716024241/http://img128.imageshack.us/img128/1900/tra26ba.jpg][https://web.archive.org/web/20110718200414/http://img145.imageshack.us/img145/4573/tra37ws.jpg][https://web.archive.org/web/20110718200429/http://img145.imageshack.us/img145/7259/tra42bl.jpg]</ref>

还有一种非官方补丁是补丁集，是将官方的零散补丁汇总整合，以便安装使用。

== 方法 ==
最常见的情况是没有软件的[[源代码|源代码]]和原开发工具。因此，可能只得在[[執行期|執行期]]以[[逆向工程|逆向工程]]和[[调试|调试]]方法分析缺陷软件的二进制文件，找到问题所在后再设法进行修复。有时解决问题只需修改[[配置文件|配置文件]]或[[注册表|注册表]]，而有时则需对二进制文件或[[可执行文件|可执行文件]]进行修改才能解决文件。如果有[[软件开发工具包|软件开发工具包]]（例如Mod系统）可用，修复补丁的制作可能会更为容易，否则就需要自己使用工具（例如[[Nullsoft腳本安裝系統|NSIS]]、[[Inno_Setup|Innosetup]]）打包给用户使用的修补程序。

== 法律 ==
虽然直接涉及非官方补丁的法庭案件不常见，但历史上有过类似的案件<ref>[http://games.qq.com/a/20041008/000023.htm 《生与死》裸体补丁诉讼案告终[多图]]</ref>。{{transH}}''{{tsl|en|Galoob v. Nintendo}}'' found that it was not [[盗版|盗版]] by a user to apply an unauthorized [[修補程式|patch]] to a system (while the scope was very specific to the {{tsl|en|Game Genie}}). On the other hand, the case {{tsl|en|Micro Star v. FormGen Inc.}} found that user-generated maps were [[衍生作品|衍生作品]]s of the original game.  In ''{{tsl|en|Sega v. Accolade}}'', the [[美国联邦第九巡回上诉法院|美国联邦第九巡回上诉法院]] held that making copies in the course of [[逆向工程|逆向工程]] is a fair use, when it is the only way to get access to the "ideas and functional elements" in the copyrighted code, and when "there is a legitimate reason for seeking such access". According to [[美国版权法|美国版权法]] 17 U.S. Code § 117, the owner of a copy of a program can modify it as necessary for "Maintenance or Repair",<ref>https://www.law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/17/117</ref> without permission from the copyright holder; an argumentation also raised by {{tsl|en|Daniel J. Bernstein}} professor at the [[伊利諾大學芝加哥分校|伊利諾大學芝加哥分校]].<ref>[http://cr.yp.to/softwarelaw.html softwarelaw] on cr.yp.to</ref> Similar user rights are given also according to European copyright laws. The question if and which copyright holder unauthorized changes of lawfully obtained software are [[合理使用|fair]] and beneficial, is a long-standing debate among academic scholars.<ref>[https://www.law.berkeley.edu/php-programs/faculty/facultyPubsPDF.php?facID=346&pubID=157 MODIFYING COPYRIGHTED SOFTWARE: ADJUSTING COPYRIGHT DOCTRINE TO ACCOMMODATE A TECHNOLOGY] by Pamela Samuelson, Professor of Law, {{tsl|en|University of Pittsburgh School of Law}} (1988)</ref> An article of Helbraun law firm remarks in the context of fan translations that redistributing complete games with adaptions most likely does not fall under fair use, but in patch form it might fall under fair use, while this was never tested in court.<ref>[https://www.helbraunlaw.com/reviewing-the-questionable-legality-of-fan-made-translations-of.html Reviewing the Questionable Legality of Fan-Made Translations of Video Games] on helbraunlaw.com (accessed May 07, 2017)</ref>{{transF}}

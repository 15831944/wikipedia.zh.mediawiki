'''非洲紫檀'''（'''''Pterocarpus soyauxii'''''）是[[紫檀屬|紫檀屬]]的一種喬木，分佈于非洲中西部<ref name=ildis>International Legume Database & Information Service: [http://www.ildis.org/LegumeWeb?version~10.01&LegumeWeb&tno~1178&genus~Pterocarpus&species~soyauxii ''Pterocarpus soyauxii'']</ref><ref name=grin>{{GRIN | accessdate = 16 January 2018}}</ref>。其高度在27–34米之間，樹幹直徑可達一米，樹皮呈灰紅色<ref name=wafc>World Agroforestry Centre: [http://www.worldagroforestrycentre.org/Sea/Products/AFDbases/AF/asp/SpeciesInfo.asp?SpID=17977 ''Pterocarpus soyauxii'']</ref>。其葉子富含維生素C，可食用<ref name=wafc/>，一些地區的人會將其用作草藥老治療皮膚寄生蟲和真菌感染<ref name=wafc/>。其木材密度為0.79 g/cm³，是一種貴重的材料<ref name=cirad>CIRAD Forestry Department: [http://tropix.cirad.fr/africa/padouk.pdf Padouk (pdf file)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071028013351/http://tropix.cirad.fr/africa/padouk.pdf |date=2007-10-28 }}</ref>，可用來製作家具。而又因其耐用而共振效果好，所以多用在樂器製作中<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rogerwilliamsguitars.com/tonewood_options.htm |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2009-11-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090221094207/http://www.rogerwilliamsguitars.com/tonewood_options.htm |archivedate=2009-02-21 |df= }}</ref>。部分人群對其木屑過敏，可能會引發皮膚炎<ref>Kiec-Swierczynska, M., Krecisz, B., Swierczynska-Machura, D., Palczynski, C. (2004). Occupational allergic contact dermatitis caused by padauk wood (''Pterocarpus soyauxii'' Taub.). ''Contact Dermatitis'' 50 (6): 384-385.</ref>。

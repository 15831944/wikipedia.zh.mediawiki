'''非洲轉型運動'''（{{lang-af|Afrikaanse Transformasie Beweging}}，{{lang-en|African Transformation Movement}}；簡稱為ATM）是[[南非|南非]]的一個[[政黨|政黨]]。其由領導人兼黨主席{{le|維優爾威特·祖恩古拉|Vuyolwethu Zungula}}領導<ref>https://www.iol.co.za/news/politics/we-are-people-driven-says-new-political-party-atm-17470665 | We are people driven, says new political party ATM | IOL News</ref><ref>https://www.timeslive.co.za/politics/2019-01-09-mzwanele-manyi-announced-as-policy-chief-of-pro-zuma-party-atm/ Mzwanele Manyi announced as policy chief of pro-Zuma party, ATM</ref><ref>https://ewn.co.za/2019/01/09/mzwanele-manyi-explains-why-he-joins-african-transformation-movement/amp | Mzwanele Manyi: This is why I've joined the ATM</ref><ref>http://www.sabcnews.com/sabcnews/new-political-party-officially-launched-in-the-eastern-cape/ New political party officially launched in the Eastern Cape - SABC News</ref>。

== 政策 == 
在2019年1月，該黨宣布如果當選，將恢復[[死刑|死刑]]以及廢除公立學校的低合格分數<ref name=":0" />。

非洲轉型運動的政策與戰略主管姆扎瓦內尼·曼依進一步指出，無論憲法是否同意，該黨都計劃恢復死刑。曼依聲稱，這是因為南非被犯罪者濫用，以致成為逃避法律的“避風港”<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=https://www.timeslive.co.za/politics/2019-01-11-from-the-death-penalty-to-scrapping-low-pass-marks--manyis-ambitions-for-the-atm/|title=From the death penalty to scrapping low pass marks: Manyi's ambitions for the ATM|website=TimesLIVE}}</ref>。

'''多哈玫瑰聖母堂'''（{{lang-ar| كنيسة سيدة الوردية}}）是[[卡塔爾|卡塔爾]]首都[[多哈|多哈]]的一所[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]教堂，位於阿布哈穆爾。<ref name="GT"/>是自7世紀伊斯蘭教傳入以來第一家興建的基督教教堂。<ref>{{cite news |url = http://www.cbc.ca/news/reportsfromabroad/middleeast/2008/03/faith_in_the_desert_qatars_fir.html |title = Faith in the desert: Qatar's first official Catholic church |first = Nahlah | last = Ayed | authorlink = Nahlah Ayed |work = CBC News | date = 2008-03-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.csmonitor.com/World/Middle-East/2009/0209/p01s01-wome.html | title = For the first time, Christians in Qatar worship in church |first = Caryle | last = Murphy | work = [[The_Christian_Science_Monitor|The Christian Science Monitor]] | date = 2009-02-09 }}</ref>

聖堂造價約2,000萬美元，所在土地由時任卡塔爾[[埃米爾|埃米爾]]的[[哈邁德·本·哈利法·阿勒薩尼|哈邁德·本·哈利法·阿勒薩尼]]捐出。<ref name="al-jazeera">{{cite web | title = Qatar opens first church, quietly | work = Al Jazeera English | url = http://www.aljazeera.com/news/middleeast/2008/03/2008525173738882540.html | first = Shabina S. | last = Khatri | date = 2008-06-20}}</ref>由於當地實施[[伊斯蘭法律|伊斯蘭法律]]，聖堂外部沒有十字架、尖塔或鐘。<ref name="FOX">{{cite news |first=Sonia |last=Verma |url=http://www.foxnews.com/story/0,2933,338014,00.html |work=Fox News |title=First Catholic Church Opens in Qatar, Sparking Fear of Backlash Against Christians |date=14 March 2008 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080316000915/http://www.foxnews.com/story/0%2C2933%2C338014%2C00.html |archivedate=March 16, 2008 |df= }}</ref>

聖堂於2008年3月14日舉出獻堂禮，由[[伊萬·迪亞斯|伊萬·迪亞斯]]樞機主持，卡塔爾副總理[[阿卜杜拉·本·哈馬德·阿提亞|阿卜杜拉·本·哈馬德·阿提亞]]、聖座駐海灣大使[[保祿·哈希姆|保祿·哈希姆]]總主教、[[天主教南阿拉伯宗座代牧區|天主教南阿拉伯宗座代牧區]]主座代牧保祿·欣德、前聖座駐海灣大使若瑟·安德里亞總主教，以及卡塔爾多名官員都有主席。<ref name="usa-today">{{cite news | author = [[Associated_Press|Associated Press]] | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/news/world/2008-03-15-1061984524_x.htm | work = [[USA_Today|USA Today]] | title = First Christian church opens in Qatar | date = 15 March 2008}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.catholicnewsagency.com/news/first_catholic_church_in_arabic_country_to_be_dedicated_to_mary/ | work = [[Catholic_News_Agency|Catholic News Agency]] | title = First Catholic Church in Arabic country to be dedicated to Mary | date = 29 February 2008}}</ref>

聖堂是[[天主教北阿拉伯宗座代牧區|天主教北阿拉伯宗座代牧區]]的一部分，服務當地200,000名天主教徒，<ref name="GT"/>大部分是來自菲律賓、印度、非洲、南美洲、歐洲和黎巴嫩的移工。<ref name="GT"/><ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.avona.org/vicariate/vicariate_reorg.htm |title = Re-Organisation of the Vicariates of the Arabian Peninsula | publisher = [[Apostolic_Vicariate_of_Northern_Arabia|Apostolic Vicariate of Northern Arabia]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.upi.com/Top_News/2008/03/14/First-church-in-Qatar-has-low-profile/UPI-33611205528488/ |title = First church in Qatar has low profile |date = 2008-03-14 | publisher = [[United_Press_International|United Press International]]}}</ref>

'''含孕激素宫内节育器'''（{{lang-en|Intrauterine device with progestogen}}），商品名为'''曼月乐'''（{{lang|en|Mirena}}）等，是一种可以释放[[左炔诺孕酮|左炔诺孕酮]]的[[宫内节育器|宫内节育器]]，其用途包括[[避孕|避孕]]、缓解[[月经过多|月经过多]]、预防{{le|雌激素替代疗法|Hormone replacement therapy}}可能产生的[[子宫内膜增生|子宫内膜增生]]等。<ref name=BNF69>{{cite book|title=British national formulary: BNF 69|date=2015|publisher=British Medical Association|isbn=9780857111562|page=556|edition=69th}}</ref>这是最为有效的避孕方式之一，其年避孕失败率仅为0.2%左右。<ref name=Trus2011>{{cite book|last=Trussell|first=James|year=2011|chapter=Contraceptive efficacy|editor1-last=Hatcher|editor1-first=Robert A.|editor2-last=Trussell|editor2-first=James|editor3-last=Nelson|editor3-first=Anita L.|editor4-last=Cates|editor4-first=Willard Jr.|editor5-last= Kowal|editor5-first=Deborah|editor6-last=Policar|editor6-first=Michael S. (eds.)|title=Contraceptive technology|edition=20th revised|location=New York|publisher=Ardent Media|isbn=978-1-59708-004-0|issn = 0091-9721|oclc=781956734|pages=779–863}} Table 26–1 = <span class="plainlinks">[http://www.contraceptivetechnology.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/CTFailureTable.pdf Table 3–2 Percentage of women experiencing an unintended pregnancy during the first year of typical use and the first year of perfect use of contraception, and the percentage continuing use at the end of the first year. United States.]</span></ref>该装置经由手术放入[[子宫|子宫]]中，有效期为三至五年。<ref name=MTM2017>{{cite web|title=Levonorgestrel intrauterine system medical facts from Drugs.com|url=https://www.drugs.com/mtm/levonorgestrel-intrauterine-system.html|publisher=www.drugs.com|accessdate=1 January 2017}}</ref>取出之后，[[生育能力|生育能力]]将很快得以恢复。<ref name=BNF69/>

其不良反应包括月经失调、良性[[卵巢囊肿|卵巢囊肿]]、[[骨盆|骨盆]]疼痛、抑郁等。<ref name=BNF69/>个别情况下可能出现{{le|子宫穿孔|Uterine perforation}}。<ref name=BNF69/>[[妊娠|妊娠]]时不推荐放入该装置，而[[哺乳期|哺乳期]]时则能安全使用。<ref name=BNF69/>

含孕激素宫内节育器是一种{{le|长效可逆避孕方法|Long-acting reversible contraception}}。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Wipf|first1=Joyce|title=Women's Health, An Issue of Medical Clinics of North America,|date=2015|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences |isbn=9780323376082 |page=507 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=fJzuCQAAQBAJ&pg=PA507}}</ref>该装置通过孕激素以稠化[[宫颈|宫颈]][[黏液|黏液]]并让子宫内膜变薄，从而阴止[[精子|精子]]通过宫颈并影响[[受精卵|受精卵]]着床，以此起到避孕的作用。<ref name=BNF69/>

含左炔诺孕酮的宫内节育器最初于1990年在芬兰批准上市，2000年起开始在美国上市。<ref name=Brad2008>{{cite book|last1=Bradley|first1=Linda D. |last2=Falcone |first2=Tommaso|title=Hysteroscopy: Office Evaluation and Management of the Uterine Cavity|date=2008|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences |isbn=0323041019 |page=171 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9gbcQ0xoUqoC&pg=PA171}}</ref>其被列入[[世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单|世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单]]，属于最有效、最安全的药物之一。<ref name=WHO19th>{{cite web|title=WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (19th List)|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|work=World Health Organization|accessdate=8 December 2016|date=April 2015}}</ref>其在[[发展中国家|发展中国家]]的批发成本约为122.46美元。<ref name=ERC2014>{{cite web|title=IUD (Levonorgestrel)|url=http://erc.msh.org/dmpguide/resultsdetail.cfm?language=english&code=IUDLV&s_year=2014&year=2014&str=52%20mg&desc=IUD%20%28Levonorgestrel%29&pack=new&frm=IUD&rte=VAG&class_code2=18%2E3%2E3%2E&supplement=&class_name=%2818%2E3%2E3%2E%29Intrauterine%20devices%3Cbr%3E|publisher=International Drug Price Indicator Guide|accessdate=8 December 2016}}{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>在英国，[[国民保健署_(英国)|国民保健署]]购买该装置的价格约为88英镑。<ref name=BNF69/>而在美国的售价则约为844美元。<ref>{{cite book |last1=Zieman |first1=Mimi |title=Managing Contraception 2012–2014|publisher=Managing Contraception|isbn=9781467536400|chapter=Chapter 27|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SdUDdQtGMGoC&pg=PT378}}</ref>已有120余个国家批准了含左炔诺孕酮的宫内节育器，并为超过1000万名女性所使用。<ref name=Brad2008/><ref>{{cite book|last1=Schäfer-Korting|first1=Monika|title=Drug Delivery|date=2010|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=9783642004773|page=290|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=uYseqDzV3s4C&pg=PA290}}</ref>

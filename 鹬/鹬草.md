'''鹬草'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Phalaris arundinacea'''''}}），又稱'''虉草'''，为[[禾本科|禾本科]][[鹬草属|鹬草属]]下的一种多年生草本植物，廣泛分佈于歐亞、北非和北美地區，在很多地方是一種入侵植物<ref name=kim>Kim, K. D., et al. (2006). [http://depts.washington.edu/waipc/docs/Phalaris%20arundinacea.pdf Controlling ''Phalaris arundinacea'' (reed canarygrass) with live willow stakes: A density-dependent response.] ''Ecological Engineering'' 26 219-27.</ref><ref name=lav>Lavergne, S. and J. Molofsky. (2004). [http://php53test.uvm.edu/~plantbio/molofsky.ReedCanaryGrass.pdf Reed canary grass (''Phalaris arundinacea'') as a biological model in the study of plant invasions.] ''Critical Reviews in Plant Sciences'' 23(5) 415-29.</ref>，生長在水邊<ref name=grin>{{GRIN | accessdate = 2017-12-15}}</ref>。

其高度可達兩米<ref name=fs>Waggy, Melissa, A. 2010. ''[http://www.fs.fed.us/database/feis/plants/graminoid/phaaru/all.html Phalaris arundinacea]''. In: Fire Effects Information System. U.S. Department of Agriculture, Forest Service, Rocky Mountain Research Station, Fire Sciences Laboratory.</ref>，[[圆锥花序|圆锥花序]]高30釐米，葉子通常純綠，偶有雜色<ref name=fs/><ref name=china>[http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=2&taxon_id=200025869 ''Phalaris arundinacea''.] Flora of China.</ref>，具粗壯的[[根状茎|根状茎]]<ref name=fs/>，種子傳播速度極快，因此難以根除<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=aEzkrNYyI-4C&pg=PA395&dq=%22Reed+canary+grass%22+invasive&hl=en&ei=_a_STdbKJ8Xn0QGEsdiEDA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=8&ved=0CHgQ6AEwBw#v=onepage&q=%22Reed%20canary%20grass%22%20invasive&f=false Ecology of freshwater and estuarine wetlands] By Darold P. Batzer, Rebecca R. Sharitz</ref>。

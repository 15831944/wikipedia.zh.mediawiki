'''熱巧克力'''（{{lang-en|'''Hot chocolate'''}}）是一种将[[可可粉|可可粉]]或融化后的[[巧克力|巧克力]]与熱牛奶或水混合制成的[[饮料|热饮]]，通常配以[[食糖|食糖]]等甜味剂，并在顶层铺上[[鲜奶油|鲜奶油]]。融化后的巧克力制作的热巧克力有时也被称为饮用巧克力（{{lang|en|drinking chocolate}}），其拥有更低的甜度和更醇厚的口感。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Chocolate: history, culture, and heritage |last1=Grivetti |first1=Louis E. |first2=Howard-Yana |author2=Howard-Yana Shapiro |year=2009 |publisher=John Wiley and Sons |isbn=978-0-470-12165-8 |page=345 }}</ref>

最早的巧克力饮料由[[玛雅文明|玛雅人]]于2500至3000年前发明，它也曾是公元15世纪[[阿兹特克|阿兹特克]]文化的重要组成部分，阿兹特克人将其称为“xocōlātl”。<ref name="dailymail">{{cite news |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-493335/Chocolate-invented-3-100-years-ago-Aztecs--trying-make-beer.html |work=Daily Mail |location=London |title=Chocolate invented 3,100 years ago by the Aztecs - but they were trying to make beer |date=2007-11-13 |access-date=2011-07-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111010015531/http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-493335/Chocolate-invented-3-100-years-ago-Aztecs--trying-make-beer.html |archive-date=2011-10-10 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Trivedi">{{cite web|last1=Trivedi|first1=Bijal|title=Ancient Chocolate Found in Maya "Teapot"|url=http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2002/07/0717_020717_TVchocolate.html|publisher=National Geographic|accessdate=2017-07-15|date=2012-07-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170630094718/http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2002/07/0717_020717_TVchocolate.html|archive-date=2017-06-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>在发现[[新大陆|新大陆]]后，这种饮料由墨西哥引进至欧洲并流行开来，自那时起它经历了多次变化。19世紀，熱巧克力甚至被用於治療肝病、胃病等疾病。

2010年代，在世界各地，人们皆饮用热巧克力，这种饮料也产生了多种变种，例如[[拉丁美洲|拉丁美洲]]五香的''chocolate para mesa''、[[義大利|義大利]]醇厚的''cioccolata calda''以及[[西班牙|西班牙]]浓稠的''chocolate a la taza served''等。成品热巧克力可以在各种商店中购买，如[[食堂|食堂]]、[[快餐店|快餐店]]和[[咖啡店|咖啡店]]。而在家中自制热巧克力，只需将预先混合的热巧克力粉加入热水或热牛奶中即可，这种粉末在[[杂货店|杂货店]]和[[网络购物|互联网线上零售商]]有售。

== 歷史 ==
=== 起源 ===
{{Wiktionary|en:chocolate}}
{{main|{{link-en|巧克力历史|History of Chocolate}}}}
通常认为巧克力（{{lang-en|chocolate}}）这一名称来源于[[纳瓦特尔语|纳瓦特尔语]]“xocōlātl”（[[国际音标|国际音标]]：{{IPA|/ʃo.ko.latɬ/}}）一词，其中“xococ”解作“苦”，“atl”解作“水”，而“x”不规则变化为“ch”。<ref name="etymonline">{{cite web|publisher= Online Etymological Dictionary|url= http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?search=Chocolate&searchmode=none|title= Chocolate|access-date= 2007-10-15|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20090203064027/http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?search=Chocolate&searchmode=none|archive-date= 2009-02-03|dead-url= no}}</ref>

[[File:Chocolatepot.jpg|thumb|upright|right|
银质巧克力壶，法国，1779。<ref>{{cite web|publisher= [[维多利亚和阿尔伯特博物馆|Victoria and Albert Museum]]|url= http://www.vam.ac.uk/content/articles/s/silverware-design-themes/|title= Silver Chocolate Pot|work= Metalwork|accessdate= 2007-08-18|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20110526075837/http://www.vam.ac.uk/content/articles/s/silverware-design-themes/|archive-date= 2011-05-26|dead-url= no}}</ref>藏于英国伦敦[[维多利亚和阿尔伯特博物馆|维多利亚和阿尔伯特博物馆]]。]]
考古学家发现，玛雅人最早于公元前500年开始食用[[巧克力|巧克力]]，甚至有推断表明，巧克力在玛雅人之前已经存在。<ref name="Trivedi" />玛雅人将[[可可豆|可可豆]]研磨成酱，并与水、{{tsl|en|cornmeal|玉米面粉|-{zh-cn:玉米面粉;zh-tw:玉米麵粉;zh-hk:粟米麪粉;}-}}、[[辣椒_(果实)|辣椒]]以及其他原料混合，<ref name="Riches" />随后将这种混合物在杯子和壶之间来回倾倒，直至形成厚实的泡沫，制成这种巧克力冷饮。<ref name="Trivedi" />玛雅人的各种社会阶层都可以享用巧克力，但富人会用陪葬品中一种“大壶嘴容器”来饮用。<ref name="Trivedi" /><ref>{{cite web|publisher= The Field Museum|url= http://www.fieldmuseum.org/Chocolate/about.html|title= All About Chocolate|accessdate= 2007-08-18|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20071019074947/http://www.fieldmuseum.org/chocolate/about.html|archive-date= 2007-10-19|dead-url= no}}</ref>在[[危地马拉|危地马拉]]里奥阿苏尔一座[[玛雅文明|玛雅]]古典时期前期（公元460至480年）的墓葬遗址中，发现容器表面存在着代表可可的[[玛雅文字|玛雅文字]]（或浮雕），同时它们内部含有巧克力饮料的残渣。<ref name="Riches">{{Cite book|last=Burleigh|first=Robert|title=Chocolate: Riches from the Rainforest|publisher=Harry N. Abrams, Ins., Publishers|year=2002|isbn=0-8109-5734-5}}</ref><ref name="M&M's">{{Cite book|last=Earley|first=Diane|title=The Official M&M's History of Chocolate|publisher=Charlesbridge Publishing|year=2001|isbn=1-57091-448-6}}</ref>

由于[[食糖|糖]]尚未传播至美洲，<ref name="Riches" />故最初xocōlātl指代一种{{tsl|en|Acquired taste|后天味觉}}。后来西班牙人所称的xocōlātl则是一种基于巧克力、添加[[香荚兰|香荚兰]]等香料的冷饮。<ref>Hickling, William (1838). ''History of the Conquest of Mexico''.《墨西哥征服史》 {{ISBN|0-375-75803-8}}.</ref><ref name="Rediscover">{{cite web|url=https://whatscookingamerica.net/Beverage/HotChocolate-History.htm|last=Stradley|first=Linda|title=Rediscover True Hot Chocolate - History of Hot Chocolate|accessdate=2008-06-27|publisher=What's Cooking America|year=2004|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141227211827/http://whatscookingamerica.net/Beverage/HotChocolate-History.htm|archive-date=2014-12-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>与现代的热巧克力相反，这种饮料味辣且苦。<ref name="dailymail" /><ref name="Riches" />至于何时、由谁将xocōlātl变为热饮，各种来源的说法彼此冲突。<ref name="Riches" /><ref name="Rediscover" />16世纪晚期曾居住于[[秘鲁|秘鲁]]及[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]的[[西班牙帝国|西班牙帝国]][[耶稣会|耶稣会]]会士[[何塞·德·阿科斯塔|何塞·德·阿科斯塔]]描述道：

<blockquote>对那些不习惯喝这种饮料的人来说，它很令人讨厌，有一种味道很不好的浮渣或泡沫。但这种饮料却很受印度人重视，他们用它招待途径印度的贵族。西班牙人无论男女都渴望拥有巧克力，这在西班牙已司空见惯。他们表示，他们制作各种种类的巧克力，热的、冷的、温的，在其中放入许多“辣椒”；他们将它制作成酱，并说这种酱对胃好、可以治疗粘膜炎。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.exploratorium.edu/exploring/exploring_chocolate/choc_3.html|title=The Sweet Lure of Chocolate|accessdate=2008-07-18|publisher=Exploratorium|first=Jim|last=Spadaccini|year=2008|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080916073721/http://www.exploratorium.edu/exploring/exploring_chocolate/choc_3.html|archive-date=2008-09-16|dead-url=no}}</ref></blockquote>

在中美洲，许多饮料都用可可豆制成，并加入香荚兰等香料增添风味。<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|title=Conquests of Chocolate|last=Norton|first=Marcy|publisher=OAH Magazine of History|year=2004|isbn=|location=|pages=}}</ref>这种做法是对[[阿兹特克|阿兹特克]]致敬。阿兹特克或墨西卡曾要求被征服的人民提供巧克力，[[门多萨手抄本|门多萨手抄本]]中列出了他们所要求的杯具、瓜果、可可豆等物品。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Codex Mendoza|last=Berdan|first=Frances|publisher=Univ. of California Press|year=1992|isbn=|location=Berkeley|pages=}}</ref>可可甚至成为了中美洲的一种通货。<ref name=":0" />阿兹特克人仅有上流阶层能使用巧克力，低下阶层的平民若饮用巧克力，则会被视为一种凶兆。<ref name=":0" />1502年，[[克里斯托弗·哥伦布#第四次航行（1502～1504）|哥伦布第四次航行]]时，欧洲人首次有接触巧克力的记录。<ref name=":0" />

=== 引进欧洲 ===

1528年，[[科爾蒂斯|科爾蒂斯]]击败蒙特苏马的军队并迫使阿兹特克贵族进贡财宝后，帶著可可豆和製造巧克力飲料的设备返回[[西班牙|西班牙]]。<ref name="Cortes">{{cite web|url=http://www.cadbury.co.uk/EN/CTB2003/about_chocolate/history_chocolate/cortes.htm|title=Don Cortes|publisher=Cadbury Trebor Bassett|accessdate=2008-06-27|year=2008 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20071021055732/http://www.cadbury.co.uk/EN/CTB2003/about_chocolate/history_chocolate/cortes.htm |archivedate = 2007-10-21}}</ref>此时，巧克力仍然只用于制作玛雅人发明的苦味饮料。<ref name="Riches" />甜味的热巧克力和[[巧克力棒|巧克力棒]]仍未被发明。巧克力被引进欧洲后渐渐受到欢迎。[[查理五世_(神圣罗马帝国)|查理五世]]的宫廷很快接受了它，令其成为西班牙上层社会的流行饮料。此外，可可还被西班牙王室当作[[嫁妆|嫁妆]]带到欧洲其他地方。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://btcocoa.com/index.php?page=articles-detail|title=History of Hot Chocolate|accessdate=2008-06-26|publisher=Pelangie Studio Design|year=2008|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110812222003/http://btcocoa.com/index.php?page=articles-detail|archive-date=2011-08-12|dead-url=no}}</ref>但当时可可豆只在南美洲种植，因此巧克力在欧洲依然十分昂贵。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://chocolate.org.uk/timeline/index.html|last=Pearce|first=David|title=Cacao and Chocolate Timeline|accessdate=2008-06-27|publisher=David Pearce|year=2008|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20050219115253/http://chocolate.org.uk/timeline/index.html|archivedate=2005-02-19}}</ref>

17世纪，甜的热巧克力诞生了，变为欧洲贵族的一种奢侈品。<ref name="Green">{{cite news|last1=Green|first1=Matthew|title=How the decadence and depravity of London's 18th century elite was fuelled by hot chocolate|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/travel/destinations/europe/united-kingdom/england/london/articles/surprising-history-of-london-chocolate-houses/|work=The Daily Telegraph|accessdate=2017-07-15|date=2017-03-11|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170714140753/http://www.telegraph.co.uk/travel/destinations/europe/united-kingdom/england/london/articles/surprising-history-of-london-chocolate-houses/|archive-date=2017-07-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>甚至在1657年，第一间巧克力屋（类似于现代咖啡店的一种商店）<ref name="Riches" />开业时，巧克力仍然十分昂贵，每磅价值50至75[[便士|便士]]（约等于2016年的45至65英镑）。<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.cadbury.co.uk/EN/CTB2003/about_chocolate/history_chocolate/chocolate_houses.htm |title=Chocolate Houses|accessdate=2008-06-27|publisher=Cadbury Trebor Bassett|year=2008 |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20071028025113/http://www.cadbury.co.uk/EN/CTB2003/about_chocolate/history_chocolate/chocolate_houses.htm |archivedate = 2007-10-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/monetary-policy/inflation|title=Inflation|accessdate=2017-12-13|publisher=Bank of England|year=2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171213011045/https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/monetary-policy/inflation|archive-date=2017-12-13|dead-url=no}}</ref>这时，热巧克力通常需添加香料以增添风味，例如一种较出名的做法是向热巧克力中“加入新鲜[[茉莉花|茉莉花]]、[[琥珀|琥珀]]、[[麝香|麝香]]、[[香荚兰|香荚兰]]以及[[龙涎香|龙涎香]]”。<ref name="Green" />17世纪末，[[伦敦皇家内科医学院|伦敦皇家内科医学院]]院长[[汉斯·斯隆|汉斯·斯隆]]访问了[[牙买加|牙买加]]。他在那里品尝了巧克力并认为它“令人作呕”，但他随后发现巧克力与[[牛奶|牛奶]]混合后会变得比较可口，于是将配方带回了英国，从此欧洲就有了牛奶巧克力。<ref name="Sloane">{{cite web|url=http://www.nhm.ac.uk/research-curation/scientific-resources/collections/botanical-collections/sloane-herbarium/about-sloane/index.html|title=About Sir Hans Sloane|publisher=The Natural History Museum|accessdate=2015-10-03|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151004203423/http://www.nhm.ac.uk/research-curation/scientific-resources/collections/botanical-collections/sloane-herbarium/about-sloane/index.html|archive-date=2015-10-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>巧克力饮料的贵族血统为[[可可树|可可树]]带来它的学名——''Theobroma cacao''，意为“上帝的饮料”。<ref name="Trivedi" /><ref name="Green" />

西班牙人使用{{tsl|es|Jícara (vasija)|瓷质茶盏}}替代了原住民使用的空心葫芦。<ref name=":0" />此外，为了创造一种属于自己的新式巧克力，他们还改进了原有配方，添加[[肉桂|肉桂]]、[[黑胡椒|黑胡椒]]、[[茴芹|茴芹]]、[[芝麻|芝麻]]等香料，试图重现那些无法轻易获得的原始香料的风味。黑胡椒用来代替辣椒、{{Link-es|糙葉胡椒|Piper amalago}}（''mecaxochitl''），肉桂用来代替{{Link-en|垂花舟瓣花|Cymbopetalum penduliflorum}}（''orejuelas''），糖则代替蜂蜜。<ref name=":0" />

1828年，荷兰化学家{{tsl|en|Coenraad Johannes van Houten|昆拉德·约翰内斯}}发明了世界上第一台可可粉机。<ref name="Riches" /><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.middleboro.k12.ma.us/Middleboro/CHOC/Chocolate.htm|title=Chocolate History|accessdate=2008-06-26|publisher=Middleborough Public Schools|year=2008|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080702220326/http://www.middleboro.k12.ma.us/Middleboro/CHOC/Chocolate.htm|archivedate=2008-07-02}}</ref>它将可可豆中的[[可可脂|可可脂]]挤压分离，留下的便是纯可可粉。<ref name="Riches" />这种粉末与现代即食可可粉类似，更容易和温牛奶或水混合。因此，同一时间也诞生了另一个重要的发明——固态巧克力，使用可可粉和少量可可脂便能生产出[[巧克力棒|巧克力棒]]。1847年，第一根巧克力棒诞生。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Klein|first1=Christopher|title=The Sweet History of Chocolate|url=http://www.history.com/news/hungry-history/the-sweet-history-of-chocolate|publisher=The History Channel|accessdate=2017-07-15|date=2014-02-13|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140308003218/http://www.history.com/news/hungry-history/the-sweet-history-of-chocolate|archive-date=2014-03-08|dead-url=no}}</ref>“[[巧克力|巧克力]]”成了后来流行的固态巧克力的简称，而液态的巧克力饮料就产生了“热巧克力”一词加以区别。<ref name="etymonline" />

== 与热可可的区别 ==
“热可可”和“热巧克力”之间存在区别。“热可可”使用去除[[可可脂|可可脂]]的磨制可可豆制成，<ref name="Term">{{cite web|url=http://www.statssheet.com/articles/article61849.html|title=Why Hot Cocoa Is Healthier Than Hot Chocolate|last=Craddock|first=Darren|accessdate=2008-06-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090122111445/http://www.statssheet.com/articles/article61849.html|archive-date=2009-01-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>而“热巧克力”可直接由巧克力棒制成，巧克力棒中则包含可可、糖以及可可脂。<ref name="Rediscover" /><ref name="Term" />因此，两者的主要区别在于可可脂。去除可可脂的热可可脂肪含量显著比热巧克力低，同时仍保留巧克力中所有的[[抗氧化剂|抗氧化剂]]。<ref name="daily">{{cite web|url=https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2003/11/031106051159.htm|title=Hot Cocoa Tops Red Wine And Tea In Antioxidants; May Be Healthier Choice|publisher=Science Daily|date=2003-11-06|accessdate=2008-06-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080702022232/http://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2003/11/031106051159.htm|archive-date=2008-07-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>

制作热巧克力可以将黑巧克力或牛奶巧克力切成小块加入牛奶中，并添加食糖。而美式即食热可可粉通常包含奶粉等原料，所以无需添加牛奶亦可制作。<ref name="Nibble">{{cite web| last = The Nibble| title = Some Like It Hot: Hot Chocolate & Hot Cocoa Mixes: An Overview| publisher = Lifestyle Direct, Inc.| date = 2005–2008| url = http://www.thenibble.com/reviews/main/beverages/cocoas/hot-chocolate-overview.asp| accessdate = 2008-07-15| archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20080917050557/http://www.thenibble.com/reviews/main/beverages/cocoas/hot-chocolate-overview.asp| archive-date = 2008-09-17| dead-url = no}}</ref>在英国，“热巧克力”是一种甜的巧克力饮料，液体为热牛奶或水，而粉末为巧克力、糖和奶粉。“可可”则是一种类似的饮料，但仅包含热牛奶和可可粉，可以添加食糖增甜，也可不添加糖直接饮用。<ref name="Nibble"/>

== 各地做法 ==
今天，人们认为热巧克力、热可可等饮料是{{tsl|en|Comfort food|安慰食物}}，世界各地皆饮用这些饮料。欧洲的热巧克力相对更浓郁，而美国的速食热巧克力则较稀。热巧克力不是一种[[茶|茶]]，但[[尼日利亚|尼日利亚]]人认为早晨饮用的饮料都是“茶”，因此将热巧克力归为“茶”。<ref>{{cite web|author=Kperogi, Farooq|url=http://www.dailytrust.com.ng/sunday/index.php/politics-of-grammar/15779-qa|title=Q and A on the grammar of food, usage and Nigerian English|publisher=''{{tsl|en|Media Trust|Daily Trust}}''|date=2014-01-26|accessdate=2017-02-23|quote=We call everything we drink in the morning “tea” even if it’s not.[...]“Daddy, why do Nigerians call hot cocoa ‘tea’?” she asked me.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170223030623/http://www.dailytrust.com.ng/sunday/index.php/politics-of-grammar/15779-qa|archive-date=2017-02-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>许多地区皆有独特的配料，例如棉花糖、奶油和芝士。<ref name="Thicker" /><ref name="European" /><ref name="Nikkitha Bakshani" />

=== 欧洲 ===
[[File:chocolademelk.jpg|thumb]]
[[File:Chocolate_with_churros.jpg|thumb]]]]

在欧洲大陆（尤其是西班牙和意大利），由于使用了[[玉米淀粉|-{zh-cn:玉米淀粉;zh-tw:玉米澱粉;zh-hk:粟米澱粉;}-]]等增稠的材料，热巧克力通常十分浓厚。<ref name="European">{{cite web|last1=McGavin|first1=Jennifer|title=Kakao oder Heisse Schokolade|url=https://www.thespruce.com/kakao-oder-heisse-schokolade-1446917|publisher=the spruce|accessdate=2017-07-09|date=2017-04-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170930090912/https://www.thespruce.com/kakao-oder-heisse-schokolade-1446917|archive-date=2017-09-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>意大利的''cioccolata calda''便是欧洲一种粘稠的热巧克力。<ref>{{cite book|author={{tsl|en|Eric Lanlard|Eric Lanlard}}|title=Totally Chocolate: 60 deliciously seductive recipes|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=qFFOBAAAQBAJ|year=2014|publisher={{tsl|en|Mitchell Beazley|Mitchell Beazley}}|page=170}}</ref>

在西班牙，熱巧克力和[[西班牙油条|西班牙油条]]是工人的傳統[[早餐|早餐]]。這種西班牙式的熱巧克力很濃，有熱巧克力[[布丁|布丁]]的黏稠度。<ref name="NatGeo">{{cite web|last1=Hall|first1=Christopher|title=Where to Find the Best Hot Chocolate and Churros in Spain|url=http://travel.nationalgeographic.com/travel/international-foods/hot-chocolate-churros-spain/|publisher=National Geographic Traveler|accessdate=2017-07-09|date=November–December 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170327105649/http://travel.nationalgeographic.com/travel/international-foods/hot-chocolate-churros-spain|archive-date=2017-03-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>热巧克力在荷兰也是十分流行的饮料，其名为''warme chocolademelk''，人们通常在家中或咖啡店中饮用热巧克力。法国的热巧克力常常在早餐时间供应，有时巧克力中会浸入涂抹了-{zh-hans:黄油;zh-hk:牛油;zh-tw:奶油;}-、果酱、蜂蜜或[[能多益|能多益]]的面包条。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Poncelin|first1=Pamela|title=Culinary Ambassadors: Breakfast in France|url=http://www.seriouseats.com/2010/09/culinary-ambassadors-breakfast-in-france.html|publisher=Serious Eats|accessdate=2017-07-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170709021811/http://www.seriouseats.com/2010/09/culinary-ambassadors-breakfast-in-france.html|archive-date=2017-07-09|dead-url=no}}</ref>

在德国，使用融化的巧克力制作的热巧克力（''Heiße Schokolade Wiener Art''）与使用粉末制作的热巧克力（''Trinkschokolade''）是不同的。<ref name="European" />热巧克力上常铺有鲜奶油。<ref name="European" />

欧洲还存在更多热巧克力的变种。若客人于比利时的咖啡馆中点一杯''warme chocolade''或''chocolat chaud''，热牛奶和苦巧克力片将分别呈上，由客人自行调配。<ref name="Nibble" />[[维也纳|维也纳]]式变种''Heiße Schokolade Wiener Art''则使用蛋黄增稠。<ref name="European" />

=== 北美洲 ===
[[File:Hot_chocolate.jpg|thumb]]]]
美国和加拿大流行的热巧克力制作简单，将包含可可粉、糖、奶粉的混合粉末加入热水或牛奶中即可。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/3883796/|title=Hot chocolate like you've never tasted before|last=Rosengarten|first=David|date=2004-01-06|publisher=TODAY|accessdate=2009-12-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040806184941/http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/3883796/|archive-date=2004-08-06|dead-url=no}}</ref>这种热巧克力的变种较稀且甜，可搭配[[棉花糖_(颗粒状)|棉花糖]]、[[淡奶油|淡奶油]]或一块固态巧克力。<ref name="Thicker">{{cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/printedition/life/20061208/dd_chocolate08.art.htm|title=Warming up to real hot chocolate |last=Billet|first=Felisa|date=2009-10-23|publisher=USA TODAY|accessdate=2009-12-20}}</ref>17世纪，荷兰人将热巧克力带到北美洲，但1775年起殖民者才开始销售热巧克力。<ref name="Illustrated">{{Cite book|last=Morton|first=Marcia|author2=Frederic Morton|title=Chocolate, An Illustrated History|publisher=Crown Publishers, Inc.|location=New York|year=1986}}</ref>传统上，美国人和加拿大人会将热巧克力与寒冷、冬季以及甜品联系在一起。<ref>{{cite web|last=Sciscenti|first=Mark J.|url=https://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2015/feb/07/why-we-drink-hot-chocolate-in-the-winter|title=Why We Drink Hot Chocolate in the Winter|publisher=The Guardian|date=2015-02-07|accessdate=2015-02-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150218033535/http://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2015/feb/07/why-we-drink-hot-chocolate-in-the-winter|archive-date=2015-02-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>

热巧克力还是墨西哥流行的国民饮料，其包含牛奶巧克力、[[肉桂|肉桂]]、糖以及[[香荚兰|香荚兰]]。<ref name="Moncayo">{{cite web|last1=Moncayo|first1=Jennifer|title=Mexican Chocolate: A Short History & Recipe|url=http://thelatinkitchen.com/how/ingredients/a/mexican-chocolate-short-history-recipe|publisher=The Latin Kitchen|accessdate=2017-07-15|date=2013-01-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170821074508/http://thelatinkitchen.com/how/ingredients/a/mexican-chocolate-short-history-recipe|archive-date=2017-08-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>这种热巧克力一般以圆形或六边形片剂售卖，<ref name="Moncayo" />能溶于热牛奶、水或奶油，混合后会产生一层-{奶油色}-的泡沫。1942年，一篇《[[芝加哥论坛报|芝加哥论坛报]]》的文章描述道，饮用墨西哥肉桂热巧克力时常常搭配各式墨西哥甜味油酥糕点，如{{tsl|en|Pan dulce|甜面包}}和西班牙油条。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Johnson|first1=Dorothy|title=Hot Chocolate is Historical Mexican Drink|url=http://archives.chicagotribune.com/1942/12/06/page/206/article/hot-chocolate-is-historical-mexican-drink|accessdate=2017-07-15|publisher=Chicago Tribune|date=1942-12-06}}</ref>

=== 南美洲 ===
哥伦比亚的热巧克力使用特质工具{{tsl|en|Chocolatera|Chocolatera}}以及{{tsl|en|Molinillo (whisk)|Molinillo}}来制作。哥伦比亚人通常在早餐时享用热巧克力，并搭配面包以及新鲜柔软的农场芝士。<ref name="Nikkitha Bakshani">{{cite web|last1=Bakshani|first1=Nikkitha|title=Colombian Hot Chocolate: Just Add Cheese|url=https://www.thedailymeal.com/travel/columbian-hot-chocolate-just-add-cheese|publisher=The Daily Meal|accessdate=2017-07-09|date=2015-03-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170628082806/https://www.thedailymeal.com/travel/columbian-hot-chocolate-just-add-cheese|archive-date=2017-06-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>类似地，[[厄瓜多尔|厄瓜多尔]]的热巧克力也同样在饮料顶端放有芝士。<ref name="latin">{{cite web|title=Hot Chocolate in Latin America|url=http://www.vivatravelguides.com/live-list/latin/hot-chocolate-in-latin-america/|publisher=Viva Travel Guides|accessdate=2017-07-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170930170445/http://www.vivatravelguides.com/live-list/latin/hot-chocolate-in-latin-america/|archive-date=2017-09-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>

在[[秘鲁|秘鲁]]，[[圣诞节|圣诞节]]时南半球的夏季已经开始，但人们依然会在早餐时间饮用热巧克力，同时食用[[意大利面包|意大利面包]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=The curious Peruvian tradition of drinking hot chocolate and paneton in summer|url=https://rischmoller.wordpress.com/2012/11/30/the-curious-peruvian-tradition-of-drinking-hot-chocolate-and-paneton-in-summer/|publisher=Rischmoller Real Estate|accessdate=2017-07-09|date=2012-11-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170930131152/https://rischmoller.wordpress.com/2012/11/30/the-curious-peruvian-tradition-of-drinking-hot-chocolate-and-paneton-in-summer/|archive-date=2017-09-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>另外，许多秘鲁人会将巧克力酱加入饮料中。<ref name="latin" />[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]的''submarino''也是一种巧克力饮料，将巧克力棒和糖加入沸腾的牛奶中制成。<ref name="latin" />

虽然热巧克力通常作为饮料饮用，但其也可能对健康有正面影响。2003年，[[康奈尔大学|康奈尔大学]]研究发现可可含有大量[[抗氧化剂|抗氧化剂]]，或能帮助抵御癌症。<ref name="anti">{{cite web|url=http://www.news.cornell.edu/releases/Nov03/HotCocoa-Lee.bpf.html|date=2003-12-03|last=Friedlander Jr.|first=Blaine P.|title=Ahhhh! Better than red wine or green tea, cocoa froths with cancer-preventing compounds, Cornell food scientists say|accessdate=2008-07-05|publisher=Cornell News|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080621065430/http://www.news.cornell.edu/releases/Nov03/HotCocoa-Lee.bpf.html|archive-date=2008-06-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>16世纪至19世纪，热巧克力除了充当饮料外也用作药物。<ref name="Rediscover" />

探险家弗朗西斯科·埃尔南德斯（{{lang|en|Francisco Hernández}}）记录道，巧克力饮料能用于治疗发热和肝病。<ref name="Rediscover"/>另一位探险家圣地亚哥·德·巴尔韦尔德·特里希思（{{lang|en|Santiago de Valverde Turices}}）认为，饮用大量热巧克力有助治疗肺部疾病，饮用少量则能治疗胃病。<ref name="Rediscover"/>17世纪，巧克力被引进法国时，报道称其可用于“对抗愤怒和坏脾气”，这种功效可能与巧克力中的[[苯乙胺|苯乙胺]]成分有关。<ref name="Illustrated"/>

巧克力最初流行时受到天主教教堂的抵制。<ref name=":1">{{Cite book|title=Tastes of paradise: a social history of spices, stimulants, and intoxicants|last=Schivelbusch|first=Wolfgang|publisher=Vintage Books|year=1993|isbn=|location=New York|pages=}}</ref>其为液体饮料，不能用于破斋。<ref name=":1" />有人认为，[[咖啡|咖啡]]是对身体好但伤害心神的饮料，巧克力则可以滋润人的潜能，所以可以饮用巧克力来恢复咖啡造成的伤害。<ref name=":1" />

另一方面，由于一些热巧克力饮品含有大量糖<ref name="Health">{{cite web|url=http://www.annamariavolpi.com/chocolate01.html|title=Can Chocolate Benefit Your Health?|last=Bonneville|first=Marguerite|accessdate=2008-06-27|publisher=Open Kitchen|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120720072240/http://www.annamariavolpi.com/chocolate01.html|archivedate=2012-07-20}}</ref>、氢化食用油和脂肪<ref name="White">{{cite web|url=http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/woman/health/575585/Health-Trans-fats-Hot-drinks.html|title=Killer fats hidden in hot drinks|last=White|first=Ellie|date=2007-12-13|publisher=News Group Newspapers Ltd|accessdate=2009-08-11|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090827013121/http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/woman/health/575585/Health-Trans-fats-Hot-drinks.html|archive-date=2009-08-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>，它也可能对健康造成负面影响。

某些产品广告声称睡前饮用热巧克力有助于睡眠。<ref>Whittard of Chelsea - Whittard of Chelsea Plc - Hot Chocolate {{cite web |url=http://www.whittard.co.uk/ProductDetails.aspx?pid=214148&cid=hc4010&language=en-GB |title=Luxury Hot Chocolate |accessdate=2007-10-15 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071101110548/http://www.whittard.co.uk/ProductDetails.aspx?pid=214148&cid=hc4010&language=en-GB |archivedate=2007-11-01 }}</ref>但也有人认为，巧克力含有[[咖啡因|咖啡因]]，因而不利于睡眠。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://seniorliving.about.com/od/sleep/a/sleep_tips.htm |title=How to Get a Good Night’s Sleep |accessdate=2007-10-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071013130709/http://seniorliving.about.com/od/sleep/a/sleep_tips.htm |archive-date=2007-10-13 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

=== 正面影响 ===
[[File:Hot_chocolate_antioxidants_graph.jpg|thumb]]、[[绿茶|绿茶]]和可可中的抗氧化剂含量]]
研究表明饮用热巧克力对健康有正面影响。康奈尔大学一项研究显示，热巧克力比红酒、绿茶含有更多的抗氧化剂，能降低心脏疾病的风险。<ref name="daily"/>研究人员发现，一份可可中含有611毫克等量[[没食子酸|没食子酸]]（GAE）和564毫克等量[[儿茶素|儿茶素]]（ECE），而红酒中含有340毫克GAE、163毫克ECE，绿茶则含有165毫克GAE、47毫克ECE。<ref name="anti" />康奈尔大学教授、主导这项研究的研究员{{lang|en|Chang Yong Lee}}表示，巧克力饮料加热时会释放大量抗氧化剂。<ref name="daily" />

制作热巧克力所使用的可可豆含有[[黄酮类化合物|黄酮类化合物]]，其对动脉血管健康有益。一项由[[玛氏食品|玛氏食品]]支持、[[国立卫生研究院_(美国)|美国国立卫生研究院]]进行的研究显示，饮用富含[[黄烷醇|黄烷醇]]的可可饮料后血液流动改善。<ref name="Times">{{cite web |url=https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9E01E0D8173DF934A25751C0A9629C8B63 |title=Beyond Delicious, Chocolate May Help Pump Up Your Heart |last=Olson |first=Elizabeth |date=2004-02-17 |publisher=New York Times |accessdate=2008-07-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090122134540/http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9E01E0D8173DF934A25751C0A9629C8B63 |archive-date=2009-01-22 |dead-url=no }}</ref>研究中，实验对象（27名年龄介于18至72岁之间的成人）每天饮用含有900毫克黄烷醇的可可饮料，持续五天，最终发现“血液流动以及血管附近[[内皮细胞|内皮细胞]]的功能显著改善”。<ref name="Times" />

[[布莱根妇女医院|布莱根妇女医院]]及[[哈佛医学院|哈佛医学院]]教授诺曼·K·霍伦伯格（{{lang|en|Norman K. Hollenberg}}）主导的进一步研究发现，黄烷醇有助血管舒张、防止血小板聚集于血管壁。<ref name="Times" />热巧克力中的黄烷醇可以抵御[[自由基|自由基]]对血管壁的伤害，<ref name="Clinic">{{cite web |url=http://my.clevelandclinic.org/heart/prevention/nutrition/chocolate.aspx |title=Your Heart and Vascular Health |date=1995–2008 |publisher=The Cleveland Clinic |accessdate=2008-07-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080717081502/http://my.clevelandclinic.org/heart/prevention/nutrition/chocolate.aspx |archive-date=2008-07-17 |dead-url=no }}</ref>还被认为可以平衡[[类花生酸|类花生酸]]的水平，对心血管健康有正面影响。<ref name="Clinic" />

=== 负面影响 ===
热巧克力也可能对健康有一些负面影响。对于不同种类的热巧克力，其影响类型和程度也不同。<ref name="cocoa vs choc">{{cite web|url=http://bestfriendscocoa.com/cocoa_vs_choc.html|title=Hot Cocoa vs. Hot Chocolate|accessdate=2008-06-29|last=Guttman|first=Linda|author2=Uri Guttman|publisher=Best Friends Cocoa|year=2008|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080531100109/http://www.bestfriendscocoa.com/cocoa_vs_choc.html|archive-date=2008-05-31|dead-url=yes}}</ref>使用牛奶制成的热巧克力含有牛奶中的糖类。即食可可粉则通常添加额外的食糖。<ref name="Riches" />一些品牌还含有氢化食用油和脂肪，其中最常见的是椰子油衍生物。<ref name="White"/>这些人工食用油可能提高血液内[[低密度脂蛋白|低密度脂蛋白]]的含量，同时降低[[高密度脂蛋白|高密度脂蛋白]]的含量，进而提高心脏病发生的风险。<ref name="White"/><ref name=ATPIII2003>{{Cite book | author = National Institutes of Health | title = Third Report of the Expert Panel on Detection, Evaluation, and Treatment of High Blood Cholesterol in Adults (Adult Treatment Panel III) Full Report | url = http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/guidelines/cholesterol/atp3_rpt.htm | year = 2002 | publisher = NIH publication | pages = IV-1-3 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20050524010904/http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/guidelines/cholesterol/atp3_rpt.htm | archivedate = 2005-05-24 }}</ref>

== 製作 ==
=== 開發 ===
2018年7月8日，[[亞馬遜影片|亞馬遜]]宣佈直接預訂第一季，共有9集<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2018/04/utopia-series-amazon-studios-gillian-flynn-auk-format-1202367487/|title=Amazon Orders 'Utopia' Drama Series From Gillian Flynn Based On UK Format|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=2018-4-19|accessdate=2018-8-14|language=en-US|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline]]}}</ref>。電視劇改編自{{le|烏托邦 (英國電視劇)|Utopia (UK TV series)|英國同名劇集}}，[[吉莉安·弗琳|吉莉安·弗琳]]擔任劇集主理人，並執筆編劇<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/utopia-remake-ordered-series-as-amazon-inks-showrunner-gillian-flynn-deal-1103988|title='Utopia' Remake Ordered to Series as Amazon Inks Showrunner Gillian Flynn to Overall Deal|publisher=[[好萊塢報道|好萊塢報道]]|first=Lesley |last=Goldberg|date=2018-4-19|accessdate=2018-9-23|language=en-US}}</ref>。該劇最初是與[[HBO|HBO]]接洽并計劃由[[大卫·芬奇|大卫·芬奇]]執導，終因金融糾紛未果<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/utopia-remake-gone-girls-david-679813|title='Utopia' Remake From 'Gone Girl's' David Fincher, Gillian Flynn Gets HBO Series Order|publisher=[[好萊塢報道|好萊塢報道]]|first=Lesley |last=Goldberg|date=2014-2-12|accessdate=2018-9-23|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2015/07/david-fincher-pilot-utopia-jeopardy-hbo-1201488110/|title=David Fincher HBO Pilot 'Utopia' In Jeopardy Over Budget Issues|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=2015-7-30|accessdate=2018-8-14|language=en-US|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline]]}}</ref>。

2018年6月，[[好萊塢報道|好萊塢報道]]稱本劇將在2018年秋季開拍<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/features/amy-adams-hbo-sharp-objects-female-antihero-tv-1121553|title=Amy Adams Goes Dark: 'Sharp Objects' and a Female Antihero for a Troubled Time |publisher=[[好萊塢報道|好萊塢報道]]|first=Lacey |last=Rose |date=2018-6-20|accessdate=2018-9-23|language=en-US}}</ref>。8月8日，有消息稱本劇將包括9集，由三位導演分別執導其中3集<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://collider.com/gillian-flynn-interview-sharp-objects-hbo|title=Gillian Flynn on 'Sharp Objects', Amy Adams, and Writing for TV|last=Radish|first=Christina|date=2018-08-06|work=Collider|access-date=2018-08-22|language=en-US}}</ref>。

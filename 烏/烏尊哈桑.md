'''烏尊哈桑'''（1423年－1478年1月6日）（{{lang-en|Uzun Hasan}}；{{lang-az|اوزون حسن , Uzun Həsən}}；{{lang-tr|Uzun Hasan}}）是[[白羊王朝|白羊王朝]]的第九任統治者（1453年－1478年）。白羊王朝實際創建人{{link-en|卡拉·奥斯曼|Qara Osman}}的曾孫。

烏尊哈桑出生於[[迪亞巴克爾|迪亞巴克爾]]，他信奉[[遜尼派|遜尼派]]教義，通曉《[[古蘭經|古蘭經]]》，熟悉突厥語、波斯語和阿拉伯語。烏尊哈桑曾娶[[特拉比松帝國|特拉比松帝國]]的科穆寧王朝君主[[約翰四世_(特拉比松帝國)|約翰四世·梅加斯·科穆寧]]的女兒狄奧多拉·梅加斯·科穆寧娜為妻。

[[File:Map_Aq_Qoyunlu_1478-en.png|thumb]]

1453年，烏尊哈桑繼承為白羊王朝統治者，在他領導下，白羊王朝開始強大。1467年10月30日<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com.pk/books?id=vZI3AAAAIAAJ&dq=jahan+shah+defeated+by+uzun+hasan&source=gbs_navlinks_s|title=A History of Persian Literature Under Tartar Dominion (A.D, 1265–1502)|author=Edward Granville Browne|year=2009|publisher=Cambridge: The University press Publication |location=|isbn=|page=89|accessdate=2013-02-04}}</ref>（或11月11日<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com.pk/books?id=fY01Tc2SZVEC&dq=jahan+shah+defeated+by+uzun+hasan&source=gbs_navlinks_s|title=The Cambridge History of Iran, Volume 6|author=Peter Jackson, Lawrence Lockhart|year=1986|publisher=Cambridge University Press|location=|isbn=9780521200943|pages=1120|accessdate=2013-02-04}}</ref>），烏尊哈桑帶領白羊土庫曼軍隊在[[賓格爾省|賓格爾省]]附近擊敗[[黑羊王朝|黑羊王朝]]的統治者{{link-en|傑漢·沙|Jahan Shah}}<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com.pk/books?id=WjQfo3a1eVMC&pg=PA907&lpg=PA907&dq=sanjaq+chapakchur&source=bl&ots=YsN0b-QlVs&sig=kPL9xCLMsweIvNKGAjRCMSFlEhA&hl=en&sa=X&ei=HRMbUcqWNceSswbdhYHYCQ&ved=0CCwQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=sanjaq%20chapakchur&f=false|title=Conflict and Conquest in the Islamic World: A Historical Encyclopedia, Volume 1|author=Alexander Mikaberidze|year=2011|publisher=ABC-CLIO|location=|isbn=9781598843361|page=907|accessdate=2013-02-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com.pk/books?id=fY01Tc2SZVEC&pg=PA173&lpg=PA173&dq=sanjaq+chapakchur&source=bl&ots=YeNRZ1QmFy&sig=_m7w0xTbuoVkMyYS90SdBAMVcv4&hl=en&sa=X&ei=HRMbUcqWNceSswbdhYHYCQ&ved=0CC4Q6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=sanjaq%20chapakchur&f=false|title=The Cambridge History of Iran, Volume 6|author=Peter Jackson, Lawrence Lockhart|year=1986|publisher=Cambridge University Press|location=|isbn=9780521200943|page=173|accessdate=2013-02-13}}</ref>，並於翌年將黑羊王朝滅亡。1468年，他又擊敗[[帖木兒帝國|帖木兒帝國]]的[[卜撒因|卜撒因]]，並佔據[[亞美尼亞|亞美尼亞]]、[[阿塞拜疆|阿塞拜疆]]、伊拉克北部和伊朗中西部，把國土伸展至[[巴格達|巴格達]]以及波斯灣沿岸，並進一步將疆域伸展至伊朗甚至到達[[大呼罗珊|呼羅珊]]地區，亦因此遷都[[大不里士|大不里士]]。<ref>Stevens, John. ''The history of Persia. Containing, the lives and memorable actions of its kings from the first erecting of that monarchy to this time; an exact Description of all its Dominions; a curious Account of India, China, Tartary, Kerman, Arabia, Nixabur, and the Islands of Ceylon and Timor; as also of all Cities occasionally mention'd, as Schiras, Samarkand, Bukhara, &c. Manners and Customs of those People, Persian Worshippers of Fire; Plants, Beasts, Product, and Trade. With many instructive and pleasant digressions, being remarkable Stories or Passages, occasionally occurring, as Strange Burials; Burning of the Dead; Liquors of several Countries; Hunting; Fishing; Practice of Physick; famous Physicians in the East; Actions of Tamerlan, &c. To which is added, an abridgment of the lives of the kings of Harmuz, or Ormuz. The Persian history written in Arabick, by Mirkond, a famous Eastern Author that of Ormuz, by Torunxa, King of that Island, both of them translated into Spanish, by Antony Teixeira, who liv'd several Years in Persia and India; and now render'd into English.''</ref>。

在西面的戰線，烏尊哈桑與[[特拉比松帝國|特拉比松帝國]]及[[威尼斯共和國|威尼斯共和國]]結盟以對抗[[鄂圖曼帝國|鄂圖曼帝國]]的[[穆罕默德二世_(鄂圖曼帝國)|穆罕默德二世]]。但是於1461年，烏尊哈桑未能阻穆罕默德二世滅亡與他有姻親關係的特拉比松帝國。更於1473年，烏尊哈桑在{{link-en|泰爾詹戰役|Battle of Otlukbeli}}中以輕騎兵為主的傳統土庫曼軍隊在僅僅一天時間內就被鄂圖曼帝國軍隊以火槍和[[加農炮|加農炮]]摧毀<ref>{{cite book | last = Babinger | first = Franz | authorlink = Franz Babinger | others = ed. by William C. Hickman, trans. by Ralph Manheim  | title = Mehmed the Conqueror and his Time | series = Bollingen Series XCVI | publisher = Princeton University Press | year = 1978 | pages = 314–315 | isbn = 0-691-09900-6 }}</ref>。

1478年，烏尊哈桑去世，其子哈里勒繼位為蘇丹。烏尊哈桑死後，繼承人在爭權內訌，終於在19年後的1497年在傳位五任蘇丹後國家分裂為二，再被[[薩非王朝|薩非王朝]]的創建者[[伊斯瑪儀一世|伊斯瑪儀一世]]於1501年在[[阿塞拜疆|阿塞拜疆]]起兵打敗他們，1502年並攻克白羊王朝首都大不里士，定都於此地。1508年，伊斯瑪儀一世將白羊王朝覆滅，為薩非王朝所取代。

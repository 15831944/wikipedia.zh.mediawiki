'''烏來青黴'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Penicillium ulaiense''）是[[青黴屬|青黴屬]]的一種真菌，為[[植物病理學|植物病原菌]]，於1987年由台灣真菌學家謝慧美、蘇鴻基與[[曾顯雄|曾顯雄]]描述發表<ref name=Hsieh>{{cite journal|author = Hsieh H. M., Su H. J. and Tzean S. S.| year = 1987 | title = The genus ''Penicillium in Taiwan''. I. Two new taxa of synnematous Penicillium. Transactions of the mycological society of republic of China | journal = Transactions of the mycological society of republic of China | volume = 2 | issue = 2 | pages = 157–168 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.airitilibrary.com/Publication/alDetailedMesh?docid=a0000200-198712-2-2-157-168-a|title=台灣青黴菌屬之調查Ⅰ.兩種束絲型青黴菌之世界新種|author=謝慧美、蘇鴻基、曾顯雄|journal = Transactions of the mycological society of republic of China | volume = 2 | issue = 2 | pages = 157–168}}</ref>。烏來青黴可感染柑橘的果實，特別容易在{{link-en|水果包裝廠|Packing house}}中造成感染，除了台灣以外，已於[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]、[[美國|美國]]、[[歐洲|歐洲]]、[[澳洲|澳洲]]、[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]]、[[埃及|埃及]]<ref>{{cite journal |author1=K. Youssef |author2=Y. Ahmed |author3=A. Ligorio |author4=A.M. D’Onghia |author5=F. Nigro |author6=A. Ippolito | year = 2010 | title = First report of ''Penicillium ulaiense'' as a postharvest pathogen of orange fruit in Egypt | journal = Plant Pathology | volume = 59 | issue = 6 |page = 1174 | doi=10.1111/j.1365-3059.2010.02337.x}}</ref>與[[南非|南非]]等世界各地發現。本種有時會被誤認為[[指狀青黴|指狀青黴]]或[[義大利青黴|義大利青黴]]，可形成高1-7毫米的{{link-en|分生孢子束|Synnema}}，[[分生孢子|孢子]]顏色為藍灰色<ref>{{cite journal|author = Holmes G. J., Eckert J. W. and Pitt J. I. | year = 1993 | title = A new postharvest disease of citrus in California caused by ''Penicillium ulaiense'' | journal = Plant disease | volume = 77 | page = 537 | doi = 10.1094/PD-77-0537D}}</ref>。

'''烏拉圭鱷屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Uruguaysuchus''）是種已滅絕[[鱷形超目|鱷形超目]]，生存於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]晚期的[[烏拉圭|烏拉圭]]。烏拉圭鱷是[[獅鼻鱷|獅鼻鱷]]與[[馬拉威鱷|馬拉威鱷]]的近親<ref name=AT04>{{cite journal |last=Turner |first=Alan H. |year=2004 |title=Crocodyliform biogeography during the Cretaceous: evidence of Gondwanan vicariance from biogeographical analysis |journal=Proceedings of the Royal Society B (Biological Sciences) |volume=271 |issue=1552 |pages=2003–2009 |doi=10.1098/rspb.2004.2840 |url=http://journals.royalsociety.org/content/w439xq941burk9qh/fulltext.pdf |format=pdf}}</ref>。烏拉圭鱷是種中小型動物，身長被估計約1.2公尺<ref>Li, Jinling(1985), ''A revision of Edentosuchus tienshanensis young from the Tugulu Group of Xinjiang Autonomous Region''.</ref>。

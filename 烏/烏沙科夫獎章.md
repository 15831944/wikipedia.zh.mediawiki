'''烏沙科夫獎章'''（{{lang-ru|Медаль Ушакова}}），是前[[蘇聯|蘇聯]]和現在的[[俄羅斯聯邦|俄羅斯聯邦]]一項軍事[[奖章|奖章]]，以[[俄羅斯帝國|俄羅斯帝國]]海军上将[[費奧多爾·費奧多羅維奇·烏沙科夫|費奧多爾·費奧多羅維奇·烏沙科夫]]命名。

== 歷史 ==
烏沙科夫獎章由[[蘇聯最高蘇維埃|蘇聯最高蘇維埃]]在1944年3月設立<ref name="sovietdecree1944">{{cite web  | authorlink = Russian Government | title = Decree of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR of  March 3, 1944 | publisher = Wikisource | date=2011-09-28 |url = http://ru.wikisource.org/wiki/Указ_Президиума_ВС_СССР_от_3.03.1944_об_учреждении_военных_орденов:_ордена_Ушакова_I_и_II_степени_и_ордена_Нахимова_I_и_II_степени| language = ru | accessdate =2012-02-12 }}</ref>；[[蘇聯解體|蘇聯解體]]後，根據1994年3月2日的[[總統令|總統令]]<ref name="decree442">{{cite web|authorlink=Russian Government |title=Decree of the President of the Russian Federation of March 2, 1994 No 442 |publisher=Commission under the President of the Russian Federation on state awards |date=1999-12-15 |url=http://award.adm.gov.ru/doc/u442.htm |language=ru |accessdate=2012-02-15 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120205195909/http://award.adm.gov.ru/doc/u442.htm |archivedate=二月 5, 2012 }}</ref>，此獎章及其設計繼續在俄羅斯聯邦使用，頒發章程曾分別在1999年1月6日和2010年9月7日根據總統令進行修訂<ref name="decree1099">{{cite web  | authorlink = Russian Government | title = Decree of the President of the Russian Federation of September 7, 2010 No 1099 | publisher = Russian Gazette | date = 2010-09-07 | url = http://www.rg.ru/2010/09/15/nagrady-dok.html | language = ru | accessdate =2012-02-15}}</ref><ref name="decree19">{{cite web|authorlink=Russian Government |title=Decree of the President of the Russian Federation of January 6, 1999 No 19 |publisher=Commission under the President of the Russian Federation on state awards |date=1999-12-15 |url=http://award.adm.gov.ru/doc/u19.htm |language=ru |accessdate=2012-02-15 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120205195833/http://award.adm.gov.ru/doc/u19.htm |archivedate=二月 5, 2012 }}</ref> 。

== 頒發 ==
烏沙科夫獎章是頒發予在戰時和平時在[[蘇聯海軍劇院|蘇聯海軍劇院]]、保護蘇聯的海上邊界、危險的軍事任務中顯示勇氣和勇敢的[[蘇聯海軍|蘇聯海軍]][[水手|水手]]、[[士兵|士兵]]、[[士官|士官]]和[[軍官|軍官]]。由1944年到1991年蘇聯解體，烏沙科夫獎章一共被頒發過估計14,000至16,000次<ref name="ruwiki article">{{cite web | title = Медаль Ушакова – Medal of Ushakov | publisher = Russian Wikipedia | url = http://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%9C%D0%B5%D0%B4%D0%B0%D0%BB%D1%8C_%D0%A3%D1%88%D0%B0%D0%BA%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%B0 | language = ru | accessdate =2012-02-15}}</ref>。 

== 獎章外貌 ==
烏沙科夫獎章是一塊直徑為36mm的圓形銀牌，邊緣凸起；獎章正面中心有烏沙科夫的浮雕半身像，上方有一行字“海軍上將★烏沙科夫”（俄語：АДМИРАЛ★УШАКОВ），獎章背面則有一個錨<ref name="statute">{{cite web | authorlink = Russian Government | title = Statute of the Medal of Ushakov | publisher = Commission under the President of the Russian Federation on state awards | date = 2010-09-07 | url = http://award.gov.ru/ushakov_medal.html| language = ru | accessdate =2012-02-15}}</ref>。烏沙科夫獎章的綬帶為一[[五邊形|五邊形]]，中間有一條銀色的金屬鏈<ref name="statute"/>。

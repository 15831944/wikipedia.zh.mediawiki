'''乌布'''为印度尼西亚[[巴厘岛|巴厘岛]][[吉安雅县|吉安雅县]]的一座城镇。作为一处艺术和文化中心，其旅游业得到长足发展<ref>{{cite news |title=Ubud, Bali |url=http://www.smh.com.au/ftimages/2005/03/02/1109700517692.html |publisher=Sydney Morning Herald|date=2005-03-02 |accessdate=2013-03-06}}</ref>。

乌布人口数量为30,000。对于游客而言，城镇与周围的13个村落混杂难辨<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x=1283240716&lng=de&des=wg&geo=293357526&srt=npan&col=adhoq&msz=1500&men=gpro&lng=en | title = World Gazetteer: Ubud | accessdate = 2010-08-31 | work = World Gazetteer | archiveurl = https://archive.today/20130105082410/http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x=1283240716&lng=de&des=wg&geo=293357526&srt=npan&col=adhoq&msz=1500&men=gpro&lng=en | archivedate = 2013-01-05 | deadurl = yes }}</ref>。此区域外环绕着小农场、稻田及茂密的森林。

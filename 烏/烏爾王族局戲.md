'''烏爾王族局戲'''（Royal Game of Ur），又可名'''廿格戲'''（20 Squares）<ref name=BBC>{{cite news |author= |coauthors= |title=Assyrian guardian figure |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/history/ancient/cultures/mesopotamia_gallery_09.shtml |quote=Bored guards have scratched a gaming board on the plinth. The game is a version of the 'Game of Twenty Squares', which was played at Ur in southern Iraq in about 2,600 BC. |work=[[BBC|BBC]] |date= |accessdate=2010-09-10 }}</ref>，是[[蘇美|蘇美]]文明的[[圖版遊戲|圖版遊戲]]，最早的考古實物是約西元前2600年的[[烏爾|烏爾]]王族古墓，是非常古老的[[擲賽遊戲|擲賽遊戲]]。詳細規則並不明，推測與古埃及的[[塞尼特|塞尼特]]相似<ref>[http://www.luckydog.pwp.blueyonder.co.uk/games/ur/ The Game of Ur] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20050509045707/http://www.luckydog.pwp.blueyonder.co.uk/games/ur/ |date=2005-05-09 }}</ref>。

[[Image:Royal_game_of_Ur,at_the_British_Museum.jpg|thumb]]

==棋具==

*棋盤通常為二十格的方格棋盤，有些有記號。
*每方七枚棋子，以顏色區分敵我。
*擲具為數根長斫。

==變體==
在烏爾出土的變體是每方五枚棋子，有暴風鳥、渡鴉、雞、鷹、家燕這五種棋子，依采名移動不同種棋子<ref>[http://mysteriouswritings.com/sacred-shapes-and-the-royal-game-of-ur/ Sacred Shapes and the Royal Game of Ur]</ref>。

'''長嘴鳥'''（[[學名|學名]]''Longirostravis''）是生存於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期的一類[[反鳥亞綱|反鳥亞綱]][[鳥類|鳥類]]。它們是在[[中國|中國]][[遼寧|遼寧]]的[[義縣組|義縣組]]發現。<ref name="Houetal.2004">{{Cite journal|author=Lianhai Hou, LuisM. Chiappe, Fucheng Zhang and Cheng-Ming Chuong|year=2004|title=New Early Cretaceous fossil from China documents a novel trophic specialization for Mesozoic birds|url=http://www.springerlink.com/content/t21k01e8vuvfb84g/|journal=Naturwissenschaften|volume=91|issue=1|pages=22–5|doi=10.1007/s00114-003-0489-1}}</ref>它們與[[長翼鳥|長翼鳥]]及[[抓握鳥|抓握鳥]]屬於[[長翼鳥科|長翼鳥科]]的[[分支|分支]]。<ref name="O'connoretal.2009">{{Cite journal|author=Jingmai K. O'Connor, Xuri Wang, Luis M. Chiappe, Chunling Gao, Qingjin Meng, Xiaodong Cheng, and Jinyuan Liu|year=2009|url=http://www.bioone.org/doi/abs/10.1671/039.029.0121|title=Phylogenetic Support for a Specialized Clade of Cretaceous Enantiornithine Birds with Information from a New Species|journal=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology|volume=29|issue=1|pages=188–204|doi=10.1671/039.029.0121}}</ref>

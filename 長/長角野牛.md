'''長角野牛'''（學名：'''''Bison latifrons'''''），或稱作'''寬額野牛'''，是一種已[[絕種|絕種]]的[[美洲野牛屬|美洲野牛屬]]，生活於 200,000 年前[[更新世|更新世]]時期的北美洲，大約於 21,000 至 30,000 年前[[末次冰盛期|末次冰盛期]]開始時滅絕。

==描述==
[[File:Bison_latifrons_skull.jpg|thumb]]所展示的長角野牛頭骨]] 
長角野牛的肩高可達 {{convert|2.5|m|ft|sp=us}} ，體重則可達 {{convert|2000|kg|lb|sp=us}} 。體型與生存於[[非洲|非洲]]的{{link-en|Pelorovis}}匹敵，而體重則超越現存的[[長頸鹿|長頸鹿]]。長角野牛的[[角_(動物)|角]]兩角角尖相距可達{{convert|213|cm|in|sp=us}}，而現存[[美洲野牛|美洲野牛]]的兩角角尖相距僅有{{convert|66|cm|in|sp=us}}<ref name=Kurten1980>{{cite book|last=Kurten|first=B|last2=Anderson|first2=E|title=Pleistocene mammals of North America|edition=1st|chapter=Order Artiodactyla|pages=295–339|publisher=Columbia University Press|location=New York|year=1980|isbn=0-231-03733-3|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=f8pviaxPIKEC&pg=PA337&lpg=PA337&dq=%22long+horn-cores,+which+have+a+maximum+span+of+213+cm%22#v=onepage&q=%22long%20horn-cores%2C%20which%20have%20a%20maximum%20span%20of%20213%20cm%22&f=false}}</ref>。而身體尺寸也明顯超越現存所有的[[牛|牛]]，包括目前僅存的兩種野牛 － [[美洲野牛|美洲野牛]]與[[歐洲野牛|歐洲野牛]]。根據長角野牛與美洲野牛肢骨長度的比較，推算出長角野牛可能比美洲野牛還重上25%–50%<ref name=Hoganson>{{cite journal|last=Hoganson|first=JW|title=Occurrence of the Giant Ice Age Bison, Bison latifrons, in North Dakota|journal=NDGS Newsletter|volume=29|issue=2|pages=1–3|year=2002|issn=0889-3594|url=https://www.dmr.nd.gov/ndfossil/education/pdf/bison.pdf}}</ref>。事實上，長角野牛可能是所有已知種類中體型最大的牛<ref name=about>{{cite web|url=http://dinosaurs.about.com/od/mesozoicmammals/p/Bison-Latifrons.htm|title=Bison Latifrons - Characteristics, Behavior and Habitat of Bison Latifrons, the Giant Bison|publisher=Dinosaurs.about.com|date=2010-12-18|accessdate=2012-05-13}}</ref>。

==演化==
長角野牛被認為演化自生活於中部北美大陸的[[西伯利亞野牛|西伯利亞野牛]]族群，這些野牛在 240,000 至 220,000 年前透過[[白令陸橋|白令陸橋]]從[[西伯利亞|西伯利亞]]遷徙至[[北美洲|北美洲]]<ref name="Belletal.2004">{{cite book|author=Bell CJ|year=2004|chapter=The Blancan, Irvingtonian, and Rancholabrean mammal ages|title=Late Cretaceous and Cenozoic Mammals of North America: Biostratigraphy and Geochronology|editor=Woodburne, M.O.|pages=232–314|location=New York|publisher=Columbia University Press|isbn=0-231-13040-6}}</ref><ref name=Scott2008>{{cite book|vauthors=Scott E, Cox SM |year=2008|chapter=Late Pleistocene distribution of ''Bison'' (Mammalia; Artiodactyla) in the Mojave Desert of Southern California and Nevada|title=Geology and Vertebrate Paleontology of Western and Southern North America|veditors=Wang X, Barnes LG |pages=359–82|location=Los Angeles|publisher=Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County}}</ref><ref name=Sandersetal2009>{{cite book|author1=Sanders AE |author2=Weems RE |author3=Albright III LB |year=2009|chapter=Formalization of the mid-Pleistocene "Ten Mile Hill beds" in South Carolina with evidence for placement of the Irvingtonian–Rancholabrean boundary|title=Papers on Geology, Vertebrate Paleontology, and Biostratigraphy in Honor of Michael O. Woodburne|editor=Albright III LB|pages=369–75|location=Flagstaff|publisher=Museum of Northern Arizona}}</ref>。長角野牛屬於在[[第四紀滅絕事件|第四紀滅絕事件]]中絕種的北美洲[[巨型動物群|巨型動物群]]之一，大約絕種於 21,000 至 30,000 年前（大約在[[末次冰期#威斯康辛冰期|威斯康辛冰期]]）<ref name=Kurten1980/>。長角野牛絕種後的區位由體型較小的[[古風野牛|古風野牛]]取代，並在約 10,000 年前演化成體型更小的[[美洲野牛|美洲野牛]]<ref name="Wilsonetal.2008">{{cite journal|author1=Wilson, M.C. |author2=L.V. Hills, B. Shapiro  |last-author-amp=yes |title=Late Pleistocene northward-dispersing ''Bison antiquus'' from the Bighill Creek Formation, Gallelli Gravel Pit, Alberta, Canada, and the fate of ''Bison occidentalis''|journal=Canadian Journal of Earth Sciences|volume=45|issue=7|pages=827–59|year=2008|doi=10.1139/E08-027}}</ref>。

==行為與棲息地==
長角野牛為[[草食動物|草食動物]]，在[[北美大平原|北美大平原]]與[[林地|林地]]上行小群體[[放牧|放牧]]生活。長角野牛喜歡較溫暖的氣候，在現今[[加州|加州]]的[[聖地牙哥|聖地牙哥]]有化石紀錄出土<ref>{{cite news|title=Ice-Age Bison Fossil Found in San Diego|url=http://www.nbcsandiego.com/news/local/Ice-Age-Bison-Fossil-Found-in-San-Diego-207284941.html|accessdate=16 May 2013|newspaper=NBC 7 San Diego|date=3 April 2013}}</ref>。雄性巨大的長角被認為是用來同性之間性展示或是用來抵禦當時的大型[[掠食者|掠食者]]（包括[[斯劍虎|斯劍虎]]<ref name=Carbone2009>{{cite journal|last=Carbone|first=C|last2=Maddox|first2=T|last3=Funston|first3=PJ|last4=Mills|first4=MG|last5=Grether|first5=GF|last6=Van Valkenburgh|first6=B|year=2009|title=Parallels between playbacks and Pleistocene tar seeps suggest sociality in an extinct sabretooth cat, Smilodon|journal=Biology Letters|volume=5|issue=1|pages=81–5|doi=10.1098/rsbl.2008.0526|pmid=18957359|pmc=2657756|url=}}</ref>以及[[巨型短面熊|巨型短面熊]]<ref name=Cassiliano1999>{{cite journal|author=Cassiliano ML|title=Biostratigraphy of Blancan and Irvingtonian mammals in the Fish Creek-Vallecito Creek section, southern California, and a review of the Blancan-Irvingtonian boundary|journal=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology|volume=19|issue=1|pages=169–86|year=1999|doi=10.1080/02724634.1999.10011131|pmc=|pmid=|url=http://www.mendeley.com/research/biostratigraphy-blancan-irvingtonian-mammals-fish-creekvallecito-creek-section-southern-california-review-blancanirvingtonian-boundary/}}</ref>）用。在2014年，{{link-en|國立人類學暨歷史研究學院|Instituto Nacional de Antropología e Historia}}在墨西哥南部[[格雷羅州|格雷羅州]]的[[奇爾潘辛戈|奇爾潘辛戈]]發現長角野牛的遺骸<ref name="INAH">{{cite web|title=Descubren en Guerrero fósiles de bisonte del Pleistoceno Tardío|url=http://www.inah.gob.mx/es/boletines/1990-descubren-en-guerrero-fosiles-de-bisonte-del-pleistoceno-tardio|website=www.inah.gob.mx|accessdate=29 July 2017|language=es-es}}</ref>。

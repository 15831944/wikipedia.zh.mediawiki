'''長鎖龍類'''（Leptocleidia）是[[蛇頸龍亞目|蛇頸龍亞目]]的一個[[演化支|演化支]]<ref name=KB10>{{cite journal |last=Ketchum |first=H.F. |coauthors=and Benson, R.B.J. |year=2010 |title=Global interrelationships of Plesiosauria (Reptilia, Sauropterygia) and the pivotal role of taxon sampling in determining the outcome of phylogenetic analyses |journal=Biological Reviews |volume=85 |issue=2 |pages=361–392 |doi=10.1111/j.1469-185X.2009.00107.x |pmid=20002391}}</ref>，最初在2007年被命名為Leptocleidoidea亞科<ref name=DR08>{{cite journal |last=Druckenmiller |first=P.S. |coauthors=and Russel, A.P. |year=2007 |title=A phylogeny of Plesiosauria (Sauropterygia) and its bearing on the systematic status of ''Leptocleidus'' Andrews, 1922 |url=http://www.bio.ucalgary.ca/contact/faculty/pdf/russell/321.pdf |journal=Zootaxa |volume=1863 |pages=1–120 |isbn=978-1-86977-262-8 |issn=1175-5334 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111004103333/http://www.bio.ucalgary.ca/contact/faculty/pdf/russell/321.pdf |archivedate=2011-10-04 }}</ref>，事後在2010年被重新命名為Leptocleidia，以避免分類位階上的衝突。目前範圍包含[[長鎖龍|長鎖龍]]、[[雙臼椎龍|雙臼椎龍]]的最近共同祖先，與其最近共同祖先的所有後代<ref name=KB10/>。

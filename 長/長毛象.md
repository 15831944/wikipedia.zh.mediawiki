'''長毛象'''(Mammut Sports Group)是瑞士跨國[[登山|登山]]和健行用品公司，總部位於瑞士[[塞翁|塞翁]]。公司由Kaspar Tanner在1862年於[[丁蒂孔|丁蒂孔]]成立。截至2017年，長毛象屬於Conzzeta AG。除此之外Raichle（登山健行鞋），Ajungilak（睡袋）和Toko（滑雪蠟）屬於長毛象<ref>{{citation|title=The first ispo china in Shanghai was a great success|publisher=skipressworld.com|date=21 March 2005|url=http://www.skipressworld.com/eu/en/daily_news/2005/03/the_first_ispo_china_in_shanghai_was_a_great_success.html?cat=Finance|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927080015/http://www.skipressworld.com/eu/en/daily_news/2005/03/the_first_ispo_china_in_shanghai_was_a_great_success.html?cat=Finance|archivedate=27 September 2007|df=}}</ref>。

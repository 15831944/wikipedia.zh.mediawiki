'''-{zh-hans:佩涅罗珀·玛丽·“彭妮”·莫當特;zh-tw:潘妮洛碧·瑪莉·「佩妮」·摩丹特;zh-hk:莫佩琳}-'''<ref>{{London Gazette |issue=59418 |date=13 May 2010 |page=8744}}</ref>（{{lang-en|Penelope Mary -{"Penny" Mordaunt}-}} {{Post-nominals|country=GBR|size=100%|MP}}，{{bd|1973年|3月4日}}），英國保守黨政治人物，2010年起當選下議院北樸茨茅夫選區議員，2017年11月9日出任{{le|国际发展大臣|Secretary of State for International Development}}。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-41923007|title=Priti Patel quits over Israel meetings row|date=8 November 2017|work=BBC News|access-date=8 November 2017|language=en-GB}}</ref>

莫当特生于德文郡的海滨城镇[[托基|托基]]，大学就读于雷丁大学，学习哲学。进入国会前，她曾在私营、公共和慈善部门从事过商务和沟通工作，包括担任{{le|肯辛顿和切尔西自治市议会|Kensington and Chelsea London Borough Council}}的{{le|通信主管|Director of communications}}。她目前是下院女性议员中唯一的海军预备役军官。

2014年她被时任首相卡梅伦提拔为{{le|社区和地方政府部政务次官|Department for Communities and Local Government}}。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/politics/10969676/Pen-portraits-of-the-10-Conservative-women-ministers-who-were-promoted-in-the-reshuffle.html|title=Pen portraits of the 10 Conservative women ministers who were promoted in the reshuffle|work=[[每日电讯报|每日电讯报]]|date=15 July 2014|accessdate=15 July 2014|author=Christopher Hope}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/housing-network/2014/jul/15/brandon-lewis-housing-planning-minister-reshuffle|title=Brandon Lewis promoted to housing and planning minister in reshuffle|work=The Guardian|date=15 July 2014|accessdate=15 July 2014|author=Liam Kelly}}</ref>2015年升任[[英國國防部|国防部]]{{le|武装力量副大臣|Minister of State for the Armed Forces}}，成为第一位担任这一职务的女性。<ref>{{cite news|title=Penny Mordaunt first woman to become Armed Forces Minister|url=https://twitter.com/David_Cameron/status/597790191621951488|date=11 May 2015}}</ref> 

2016年特蕾莎·梅成为首相后，莫当特调任新升格的{{le|工作和养老金部|Department for Work and Pensions|残障人士副大臣}}。2017年接替因私会以色列官员而辞职的[[普丽蒂·帕特尔|普丽蒂·帕特尔]]任国际发展大臣。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-41931063|work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|title=Penny Mordaunt replaces Priti Patel in cabinet reshuffle|date=9 November 2017}}</ref>

== 早年生活 ==
莫当特生于军人家庭，父亲曾服役于{{le|英国陆军伞兵团|Parachute Regiment (United Kingdom)}}，退役后改做教师。<ref name=DMail2541461>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-2541461/Why-Penny-Mordaunt-MP-flaunting-wibbly-bits-Splash.html#ixzz2rWLlW4mZ|title=Why our sexiest MP is on Splash! tonight|author=Jane Fryer|publisher=[[Daily_Mail|Daily Mail]]|date=17 January 2014|accessdate=26 January 2014}}</ref>她的名字来源于一艘皇家海军{{le|利安得级护卫舰|Leander-class frigate}}{{le|“珀涅罗珀”号(F127)|HMS Penelope (F127)}}。<ref name=DMail2541461/> 

莫当特有一个哥哥詹姆斯和一个孪生弟弟爱德华。<ref name="profile">[http://www.conservatives.com/People/Prospective_Parliamentary_Candidates/Mordaunt_Penny.aspx "Penny Mordaunt profile"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100430133318/http://www.conservatives.com/People/Prospective_Parliamentary_Candidates/Mordaunt_Penny.aspx |date=2010-04-30 }}, conservatives.com; accessed 8 May 2015.</ref>她是第一位工党籍[[財政大臣_(英國)|财政大臣]]{{le|菲利普·斯诺登|Philip Snowden, 1st Viscount Snowden}}的后裔，也是名演员[[安吉拉·兰斯伯里|安吉拉·兰斯伯里]]的远房表亲。<ref>Matthew Bell [https://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/diary/the-feral-beast-cecilia-pins-her-hopes-on-the-pope-8547065.html ''The Feral Beast:'' "Cecilia pins her hopes on the Pope"], independent.co.uk, 24 March 2013</ref>

莫当特在位于[[漢普郡|汉普郡]]{{le|沃特鲁伟尔|Waterlooville}}的{{le|奥克兰兹天主教学校|Oaklands Catholic School}}接受教育，后转入Victoryland戏剧学校研习戏剧文学。<ref>[http://www.conservatives.com/OurTeam/Members_of_Parliament/Mordaunt_Penny.aspx Profile], conservatives.com; accessed 8 May 2015.</ref>

莫当特15岁时，母亲因[[乳腺癌|乳腺癌]]逝世，她肩负起照顾离开学校的弟弟爱德华的职责。<ref name=DMail2541461/>第二年，其父确诊罹患癌症，后康复。<ref name=DMail2541461/>她通过在朴茨茅斯的魔术表演组织“{{le|魔法阵 (魔术)|The Magic Circle|魔法阵}}”兼职{{le|魔术师助理|Magician's assistant}}来赚取高中学费。<ref name=DMail2541461/>

上大学之间的那个间隔年，莫当特前往罗马尼亚，在当地医院和孤儿院工作，当时正值[[1989年羅馬尼亞革命|1989年革命]]后，莫当特将自己对政治的兴趣归功于那段经历。<ref>[http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/news/article-9713092-details/Howard's+harem/article.do "Howard's harem"]{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}, thisislondon.co.uk; accessed 8 May 2015.</ref>1995年她以文学士学位在雷丁大学毕业，成为其家庭中第一位接受过大学教育者。<ref name="profile"/>

'''孙雪玲''' （英文：'''Sun Xueling''') 出生于1979年7月10日，<ref name="introduction">{{cite news|last1=Au-Yong|first1=Rachel|title=A Singaporean born and bred|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/a-singaporean-born-and-bred|work=The Straits Times|date=23 August 2015|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180112160452/http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/a-singaporean-born-and-bred|archivedate=2018年1月12日|df=}}</ref> 是[[新加坡|新加坡]]的[[商人|商人]]与[[政治家|政治家]]。她是执政的[[人民行动党|人民行动党]] (PAP) 的一名[[国会议员|国会议员]]（{{link-en|白沙-榜鹅集选区|Pasir_Ris-Punggol_Group_Representation_Constituency}} ）。

== 教育 ==
她所就读的学府包括[[新加坡国立大学|新加坡国立大学]]（荣获[[经济学|经济学]][[学士|学士]]）以及[[倫敦政治經濟學院|倫敦政治經濟學院]]（荣获[[国际|国际]][[政治学|政治]][[经济学|经济学]][[硕士|硕士]]）。<ref name="introduction"/><ref name="GE2015campaign">{{cite news|last1=Lim|first1=Jessica|title=GE2015: On the campaign trail with PAP's Sun Xueling|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/ge2015-on-the-campaign-trail-with-paps-sun-xueling|accessdate=4 April 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160425134235/http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/ge2015-on-the-campaign-trail-with-paps-sun-xueling|archivedate=2016年4月25日|df=}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=https://www.pap.org.sg/upload/item/2015Candidate/Sun%20Xueling_CV_Statement_v.pdf|title=Sun Xue Ling|publisher=PAP|accessdate=29 May 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160701073251/https://www.pap.org.sg/upload/item/2015Candidate/Sun%20Xueling_CV_Statement_v.pdf|archivedate=2016年7月1日|df=}}</ref>

== 事业史 ==
于2003年加入[[星展銀行|星展銀行]]任助理副总裁。<ref name=":0" /> 2007-2014年间出任[[德意志银行|德意志银行]]与[[麥格理銀行|麥格理銀行]][[中国|中国]]与[[香港|香港]]区高级副总裁。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.yan.sg/ceoxunxunlingchurentontshangzhonggou/|title=这才是真正的美女总裁：孙雪玲出任通商中国CEO -|date=2015-11-02|newspaper=新加坡眼|accessdate=2018-06-16|language=zh-CN|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180616204313/http://www.yan.sg/ceoxunxunlingchurentontshangzhonggou/|archivedate=2018-06-16|df=}}</ref>

于获选后，改为担任通商中国总裁。<ref>{{cite news|title=Business as usual for CEO and mum of toddler|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/business-as-usual-for-ceo-and-mum-of-toddler|accessdate=4 April 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160425125920/http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/business-as-usual-for-ceo-and-mum-of-toddler|archivedate=2016年4月25日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Asia Briefs: ESM Goh in China for economic forum|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/asia/east-asia/asia-briefs-esm-goh-in-china-for-economic-forum|accessdate=4 April 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160405191207/http://www.straitstimes.com/asia/east-asia/asia-briefs-esm-goh-in-china-for-economic-forum|archivedate=2016年4月5日|df=}}</ref> 

== 政治生涯 ==
当选前是一名基层领袖，服务于[[女皇镇|女皇镇]]的波纳维斯达区。<ref name="introduction" /> 她曾在国会提及关于妇女权力等课题, 并且也担任人民行动党妇女组副秘书。<ref name="GE2015campaign" /><ref>{{cite news|title=Parliament: Move to extend maintenance payments to some men a 'calibrated' measure|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/parliament-move-to-extend-maintenance-payments-to-some-men-a-calibrated-measure|accessdate=4 April 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160310181849/http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/parliament-move-to-extend-maintenance-payments-to-some-men-a-calibrated-measure|archivedate=2016年3月10日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Women MPs to help gather female views on key issues|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/women-mps-to-help-gather-female-views-on-key-issues|accessdate=4 April 2016|publisher=Straits Times|date=8 Mar 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160425122136/http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/women-mps-to-help-gather-female-views-on-key-issues|archivedate=2016年4月25日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Teng|first1=Amelia|title=PAP's Women Wing to start discussions on women's perspectives and aspirations|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/paps-women-wing-to-start-discussions-on-womens-perspectives-and-aspirations|accessdate=4 April 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160425131433/http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/paps-women-wing-to-start-discussions-on-womens-perspectives-and-aspirations|archivedate=2016年4月25日|df=}}</ref>

于2015年9月11日获选{{link-en|白沙-榜鹅集选区|Pasir_Ris-Punggol_Group_Representation_Constituency}}（榜鹅西）议员。<ref>{{cite news|title=7 Things Spotted On The Campaign Trail, Sept 2 edition|url=http://news.asiaone.com/news/general-election/7-things-spotted-campaign-trail-sept-2-edition|accessdate=4 April 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160423211536/http://news.asiaone.com/news/general-election/7-things-spotted-campaign-trail-sept-2-edition|archivedate=2016年4月23日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Pasir Ris-Punggol GRC: Temasek exec attends her 'first formal event'|url=http://news.asiaone.com/news/singapore/pasir-ris-punggol-grc-temasek-exec-attends-her-first-formal-event|publisher=Asiaone|date=2 August 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160423225800/http://news.asiaone.com/news/singapore/pasir-ris-punggol-grc-temasek-exec-attends-her-first-formal-event|archivedate=2016年4月23日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Toh|first1=Yong Chuan|title=New measures riding on SkillsFuture credits to be introduced|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/new-measures-riding-on-skillsfuture-credits-to-be-introduced|accessdate=4 April 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160404064753/http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/new-measures-riding-on-skillsfuture-credits-to-be-introduced|archivedate=2016年4月4日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Six months on the ground: New MPs on their political journey so far|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/six-months-on-the-ground-new-mps-on-their-political-journey-so-far|accessdate=4 April 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160319084919/http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/six-months-on-the-ground-new-mps-on-their-political-journey-so-far|archivedate=2016年3月19日|df=}}</ref> 

于2018年5月出任内政部兼国家发展部高级政务次长。 <ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.parliament.gov.sg/mps/list-of-current-mps/mp/details/sun-xueling|title=MP - Parliament Of Singapore|website=www.parliament.gov.sg|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180712044745/https://www.parliament.gov.sg/mps/list-of-current-mps/mp/details/sun-xueling|archivedate=2018-07-12|df=}}</ref>

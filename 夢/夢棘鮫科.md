'''夢棘鮫科'''又名'''睡鯊科'''，是[[板鰓亞綱|板鰓亞綱]][[角鯊目|角鯊目]]的其中一科。

==分類==
'''夢棘鮫科'''下分為6個屬17種，如下：

* [[荊鯊屬|荊鯊屬]] ''Centroscymnus'' <small>[[José_Vicente_Barbosa_du_Bocage|Barbosa du Bocage]] & [[Félix_António_de_Brito_Capello|Brito Capello]], 1864</small>
** [[腔鱗荊鯊|腔鱗荊鯊]] ''Centroscymnus coelolepis'' <small>Barbosa du Bocage & Brito Capello, 1864</small>
** [[歐氏荊鯊|歐氏荊鯊]] ''Centroscymnus owstonii'' <small>[[Samuel_Garman|Garman]], 1906</small>
* [[絨鮫屬|絨鮫屬]] ''Centroselachus'' <small>Garman, 1913</small>
** [[長吻荊鯊|長吻荊鯊]] ''Centroselachus crepidater'' <small>Barbosa du Bocage & Brito Capello, 1864</small>
* [[擬鎧鯊屬|擬鎧鯊屬]] ''Scymnodalatias'' <small>[[Jack_Garrick|Garrick]], 1956</small>
** [[白尾擬鎧鯊|白尾擬鎧鯊]] ''Scymnodalatias albicauda'' <small>[[Toru_Taniuchi|Taniuchi]] & Garrick, 1986</small>
** [[加氏擬鎧鯊|加氏擬鎧鯊]] ''Scymnodalatias garricki'' <small>Kukuyev & Konovalenko, 1988</small>
** [[寡齒擬鎧鯊|寡齒擬鎧鯊]] ''Scymnodalatias oligodon'' <small>Kukuyev & Konovalenko, 1988</small>
** [[希氏擬鎧鯊|希氏擬鎧鯊]] ''Scymnodalatias sherwoodi'' <small>[[Gilbert_Edward_Archey|Archey]], 1921</small>
* [[異鱗鯊屬|異鱗鯊屬]] ''Scymnodon'' <small>Barbosa du Bocage & Brito Capello, 1864</small>
** [[石原異鱗鯊|石原異鱗鯊]] ''Scymnodon ichiharai'' <small>[[Kazunari_Yano|Yano]] & [[Shigeho_"Sho"_Tanaka|S. Tanaka (II)]], 1984</small>
** [[大棘異鱗鯊|大棘異鱗鯊]] ''Scymnodon macracanthus'' <small>[[Charles_Tate_Regan|Regan]], 1906</small>
** [[普氏異鱗鯊|普氏異鱗鯊]] ''Scymnodon plunketi'' <small>[[Edgar_Ravenswood_Waite|Waite]], 1910</small>
** [[尖齒異鱗鯊|尖齒異鱗鯊]] ''Scymnodon ringens'' <small>Barbosa du Bocage & Brito Capello, 1864</small>
* [[睡鯊屬|睡鯊屬]] ''Somniosus'' <small>[[Charles_Alexandre_Lesueur|Lesueur]], 1818</small>
** [[南極睡鯊|南極睡鯊]] ''Somniosus antarcticus'' <small>[[Gilbert_Percy_Whitley|Whitley]], 1939</small>
** [[長肌睡鯊|長肌睡鯊]] ''Somniosus longus'' <small>Tanaka, 1912</small>
** [[小頭睡鯊|小頭睡鯊]] ''Somniosus microcephalus'' <small>[[Marcus_Elieser_Bloch|Bloch]] & [[約翰·哥特洛布·施耐德|J. G. Schneider]], 1801</small>：又稱大西洋睡鯊、格陵蘭鯊。
** [[太平洋睡鯊|太平洋睡鯊]] ''Somniosus pacificus'' <small>[[Henry_Bryant_Bigelow|Bigelow]] & [[William_Charles_Schroeder|Schroeder]], 1944</small>
** [[小鰭睡鯊|小鰭睡鯊]] ''Somniosus rostratus'' <small>[[Antoine_Risso|Risso]], 1827</small>
* [[鱗睡鯊屬|鱗睡鯊屬]] ''Zameus'' <small>[[David_Starr_Jordan|D. S. Jordan]] & [[Henry_Weed_Fowler|Fowler]], 1903</small>
** [[鱗睡鯊|鱗睡鯊]] ''Zameus squamulosus'' <small>[[Albert_Günther|Günther]], 1877</small>

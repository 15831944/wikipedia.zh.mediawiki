'''擬星螺屬'''（學名：''{{lang|la|Bolma}}''）是一個包括有中型到大型[[海螺|海螺]]的一個[[屬_(生物)|屬]]{{r|WoRMS_138603}}，其物種都有[[鈣質|鈣質]]的{{link-en|口蓋|Operculum (gastropod)}}，皆為[[蠑螺科|蠑螺科]]的[[海洋生物|海洋]][[腹足纲|腹足纲]][[软体动物|软体动物]]{{r|WoRMS_138603}}。

==化石紀錄==
本屬物種已知從[[始新世|始新世]]開始就已經出現到現在（範圍：3390萬年前到現在）。這個物種的化石螺殻曾於以下地區發現<ref>{{fossilworks |id=8610 |title=Bolma Risso 1826 (snail) |access-date=2018-03-11}}</ref>：
* [[地中海|地中海]]的：[[奧地利|奧地利]]、[[塞浦路斯|塞浦路斯]]、[[法國|法國]]、[[希臘|希臘]]、[[意大利|意大利]]、[[摩洛哥|摩洛哥]]、[[斯洛伐克|斯洛伐克]]及[[西班牙|西班牙]]
* [[太平洋|太平洋]]的：[[日本|日本]]、[[瓦努阿图|瓦努阿图]]、[[關島|關島]]、[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]]、[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]及[[湯加|湯加]]。
* [[印度洋|印度洋]]的：[[南非|南非]]

==型態特徵==
本物種的[[舌齒|舌齒]]的中央齒have no cusps. They have a narrow long basal plate, which is produced above the body of the tooth. The latter is wide, oval, and not reflected above. Its lower margin is not well-defined in my specimens which are, however, not stained. The laterals are of the usual form and bear cusps.

The imperforate shell has a turbinate shape. The spire is conic with [[Whorl_(mollusc)|whorl]]s rounded at the periphery.  The upper whorls are spiny. The base of the shell is convex. The [[Operculum_(gastropod)|operculum]] is nearly round with an excentric nucleus. The outside of the shell is polished, concave in the middle, with a convexity or rib upon the center of the spiral.<ref>[https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/item/77914 G.W. Tryon (1888), Manual of Conchology X; Academy of Natural Sciences, Philadelphia]</ref>

==分佈==
This marine genus has a wide distribution. It occurs in the [[Red_Sea|Red Sea]], the Eastern Indian Ocean, the West Pacific, the [[South_China_Sea|South China Sea]], the [[East_China_Sea|East China Sea]], and off [[South_Africa|South Africa]], Indo-China, the [[Philippines|Philippines]], [[Japan|Japan]], [[Indonesia|Indonesia]], [[New_Caledonia|New Caledonia]], [[New_Zealand|New Zealand]] and [[Australia|Australia]] (New South Wales, Northern Territory, Queensland).

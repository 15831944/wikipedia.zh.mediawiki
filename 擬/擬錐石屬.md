'''擬錐石屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Paraconularia''}}）是[[缽水母綱|缽水母綱]]{{tsl|en|Conulariida|錐石目}}下已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的一[[屬|屬]]，生存於[[志留紀|志留紀]]至[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]。<ref name="Fossilworks: Paraconularia">{{Cite web |url = http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=4587 |title = Fossilworks: Paraconularia |language = en |accessdate = 2017-11-10 }}</ref>本屬的物種生活在[[海洋|海洋]]中，利用其小底盤附著在[[海床|海床]]上。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=44 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
殼有四個面，呈[[圓錐|圓錐]]狀，其切面為[[菱形|菱形]]。四個面都都朝上拱起，間距著很密的線，四面相交的四個角是窄小的[[V|V]]形裂片。殼本身很薄，由[[磷酸鹽|磷酸鹽]]和[[蛋白質|蛋白質]]等構成。<ref name="Fossils"></ref>

'''擬蟹體鱟'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Paracarcinosoma'''''}}）是生存於[[志留紀|志留紀]]的一[[屬|屬]][[海蠍|海蠍]]，其[[化石|化石]]分布於[[亞洲|亞洲]]及[[歐洲|歐洲]]等地。<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Tetlie | first1 = O. E. | title = Distribution and dispersal history of Eurypterida (Chelicerata) | doi = 10.1016/j.palaeo.2007.05.011 | journal = Palaeogeography, Palaeoclimatology, Palaeoecology | volume = 252 | issue = 3–4 | pages = 557–574| year = 2007 | pmid =  | pmc = }}</ref>牠們的身體、似[[方形|方形]]的[[頭|頭]]甲以及六對附肢均被[[幾丁質|幾丁質]]的[[外骨骼|外骨骼]]包裹住。其餘十二節體節和末端的尾刺均與頭相連接。頭甲下六對附肢的第一對長有[[螯|螯]]，第二至五對用於行走，第六對呈[[鰭|鰭]]狀。<ref>{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=73 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

'''代爾祖爾之圍'''是[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]對敘利亞[[代尔祖尔省|代尔祖尔省]]首府[[代尔祖尔|代尔祖尔]]的長期圍攻戰。伊斯蘭國在2014年擊敗省內其它反對派武裝後，到2014年7月已幾乎控制整個代爾祖爾省，只剩下代爾祖爾市仍由敘利亞政府軍控制。
2017年9月5日，敘利亞武裝部隊打破恐怖組織伊斯蘭國對於東北部城市代爾祖爾長達3年的封鎖。
<ref>{{cite web|url=http://af.reuters.com/article/worldNews/idAFKBN0FJ1I020140714|title=Islamic State expels rivals from Syria's Deir al-Zor – activists|work=Reuters|date=2014-07-14}}</ref>

==2014年==
2014年9月，伊斯蘭國數次進攻代爾祖爾空軍基地，皆被政府軍擊退。9月15日，政府軍炸斷連接[[幼发拉底河|幼发拉底河]]兩岸的最後一條橋樑Siasiyya橋。<ref name="stronghold">{{cite web|url=https://southfront.org/the-stronghold-of-deir-ezzor-all-what-you-need-to-know-about-the-battle-against-isis-in-eastern-syria/|title=The Stronghold of Deir Ezzor: All What You Need To Know About The Battle Against ISIS In Eastern Syria|date=2016-12-01 |publisher=}}</ref>

2014年10月下旬，政府軍第104空降旅進攻Sakr島，奪回該島的9成地方，只有北部小部分仍由伊斯蘭國控制。超過120名武裝分子在島上陣亡。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.almasdarnews.com/article/175-isis-fighters-killed-deir-ezzor-saa-advances-sakr-island/|title=Over 175 ISIS Fighters Killed in Deir Ezzor; SAA Advances at Sakr Island|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=2014-10-25 |publisher=|accessdate=2017-03-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-suffers-heavy-casualties-deir-ezzor-syrian-army-destroys-2-tunnels/|title=ISIS Suffers Heavy Casualties in Deir Ezzor; Syrian Army Destroys 2 Tunnels|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=2014-10-31 |publisher=|accessdate=2017-03-05}}</ref>

2014年12月，伊斯蘭國朝代爾祖爾空軍基地方向再次發動進攻，3日戰鬥造成68－200名武裝分子及43－51名守軍士兵陣亡。<ref name="mount">{{cite web|url=http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=7693|title=Regime forces use Chlorine gas to stop ISIS advances in Der-Ezzor military airport |publisher=SOHR |date=2014-12-06 |accessdate=2014-12-06}}</ref><ref name="almasdarnews.com">{{cite web|title=Deir Ezzor: ISIS Suffers Significant Losses Near the Military Airport|url=http://www.almasdarnews.com/article/deir-ezzor-isis-suffers-significant-losses-near-military-airport/|website=Al-Masdar|accessdate=2014-12-06}}</ref><ref name="51killed">{{cite web|url=http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=7696|title=111 killed in 3 days of violent clashes between regime forces and ISIS in Der-Ezzor |publisher=SOHR |date=2014-12-06 |accessdate=2014-12-06}}</ref>

==2015年==
2015年1月，伊斯蘭國封鎖所有連接代爾祖爾市與外界的道路，並切斷所有連接政府控制區的光纖線和電纜線。<ref name=pdf>{{cite web|url=https://jfldz.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/Siege-of-Deir-Ezzor.pdf|title=420 days of siege laid on Deir Ezzor: The De Facto Reality of the Besieged Neighborhoods with Numbers|work=Justice for Life Observatory in Deir Ezzor|date=February 2016|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171010120315/https://jfldz.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/Siege-of-Deir-Ezzor.pdf|archivedate=2017-10-10}}</ref>

2015年5月初，伊斯蘭國攻佔Sakr島。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://carnegie-mec.org/diwan/60312|title=After Palmyra: Military and Economic Targets of the Islamic State|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.agathocledesyracuse.com/archives/366|title=Agathocle de Syracuse - Syria areas of control (1 June 2015)|website=www.agathocledesyracuse.com}}</ref>

2015年11月上旬，伊斯蘭國對空軍基地的進攻再度被擊退，58名武裝分子陣亡。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sputniknews.com/middleeast/201511091029793024-syrian-troops-kill-60-isil/|title=Syrian Troops Kill Almost 60 Islamists in Failed ISIL Attack on Airbase|first=|last=Sputnik|website=sputniknews.com |date=2015-11-09}}</ref>

==2016年==
{{Seealso|2016年美國聯軍空襲敘利亞政府軍事件}}
[[File:Deir_ez-Zor_clashes_(January_2016).svg|thumb]]
2016年1月16日，伊斯蘭國再次進攻代爾祖爾。當日有6名自殺炸彈客參與進攻。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-idUSKCN0UU0TM|title=Islamic State kills dozens in Syria's Deir al-Zor city|date=17 January 2016|publisher=|via=Reuters}}</ref>經過數日激戰後，伊斯蘭國攻佔Baghiliya和Ayyash社區。<ref>{{cite web|title = LiveLeak.com - ISIS offensive has been officially repelled in Deir Ezzor|url = http://www.liveleak.com/view?i=58a_1452991570|access-date = 23 January 2016}}</ref>「敘利亞人權觀察」（SOHR）宣稱伊斯蘭國綁架了約400名來自親政府戰士家庭的平民。伊斯蘭國部隊又攻佔了Baghiliya南面和西面的區域。<ref name=":2">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/01/19/world/middleeast/isis-islamic-state-eastern-syria.html|title=ISIS Captures Government-Held Ground in Eastern Syria|date=19 January 2016|work=The New York Times}}</ref>SOHR宣稱約440人在伊斯蘭國的5日攻勢中喪生，包括200名政府軍士兵、110名伊斯蘭國成員和127名平民。喪生的伊斯蘭國成員包括30名自殺炸彈客。85名平民和48名士兵被伊斯蘭國處決，42名平民死於政府軍空襲和炮擊。<ref name="shr">{{cite web|url=http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=43075|title=About 440 killed and executed during the past 5 days in Der-Ezzor|accessdate=21 January 2016}}</ref><ref name="cnn">{{cite news|title = Deir Ezzor: Hundreds may be dead after ISIS abductions - CNN.com|url = http://www.cnn.com/2016/01/18/middleeast/syria-deir-ezzor-isis/index.html|website = CNN|access-date = 19 January 2016}}</ref>

2016年9月17日，美國領導的聯軍空襲位於代爾祖爾機場西南面的薩爾達山（Jabal Therdeh）的敘政府軍據點，造成超過60名敘利亞軍人死亡，超過100人受傷。伊斯蘭國隨即攻佔遇襲的薩爾達山。

==2017年==
{{Seealso|代爾祖爾之戰 (2017年)}}
[[File:Deir_ez-Zor_clashes_(january_2017).svg|thumb]]
2017年1月16日，伊斯蘭國攻佔聯繫代爾祖爾機場與敘政府軍主要控制區的道路，把敘軍在代爾祖爾的控制區分割成東西兩部分。<ref name=telegraph>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2017/01/16/syrian-rebels-agree-attend-russia-brokered-peace-talks/ |title=Islamic State launches major offensive to take Syrian city |publisher=The Telegraph|author=Josie Ensor|date=2017-01-16 |accessdate=2017-01-16}}</ref><ref name=gains>{{cite web|url=http://www.wsj.com/articles/islamic-state-gains-in-syria-province-1484676322 |title=Islamic State Gains in Remote Syria Outpost |first1=Noam|last1=Raydan|first2=Nour|last2=Alakraa|date=2017-01-17 |publisher=|via=Wall Street Journal}}</ref>

2017年6月6日至8日，俄軍空襲準備進攻代爾祖爾的伊斯蘭國部隊，炸死約180名武裝分子。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/world/2017-06/18/c_1121162551.htm |title=俄军挫败“伊斯兰国”进攻叙东部城市代尔祖尔的图谋 |publisher=新華網 |date=2017-06-18}}</ref>

2017年7月中，敘政府軍在敘利亞中部發起[[敘利亞中部戰役|攻勢]]，陸續肅清伊斯蘭國在代爾祖爾市以西的多個據點，為代爾祖爾市解圍掃除障礙。

2017年8月21日，俄羅斯國防部公佈俄軍早前針對一支開往代爾祖爾市的伊斯蘭國車隊進行空襲，俄方宣稱在行動中殲滅200多名武裝分子。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.chinanews.com/gj/2017/08-21/8309940.shtml |title=俄空天部队在叙利亚歼灭200多名极端分子 |publisher=中新網 |date=2017-08-21}}</ref>

由[[老虎部隊|老虎部隊]]帶頭的敘政府軍沿[[蘇赫奈|蘇赫奈]]－代爾祖爾（M20）公路向代爾祖爾進攻，8月31日攻佔Haribshah鎮，距離代爾祖爾市不到50公里。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-syrian-forces-within-50-km-deir-ezzor-city/ |title=BREAKING: Syrian forces within 50 km of Deir Ezzor city |date=2017-08-31}}</ref>到9月3日，解圍部隊距離代爾祖爾市只有約10公里。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-deir-alzor/syrian-army-allies-race-to-relieve-deir-al-zor-idUSKCN1BE0W4 |title=Syrian army, allies race to relieve Deir al-Zor |publisher=Reuters |date=2017-09-03}}</ref>9月5日，解圍部隊推進至長期被圍困的市郊第137旅基地。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-41162683 |title=Syria war: Army 'breaks IS siege of Deir al-Zour' |work=BBC News |date=2017-09-05}}</ref>9月9日，東面的代爾祖爾機場解圍。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria/syrian-army-allies-reach-airbase-besieged-by-islamic-state-in-eastern-syria-commander-idUSKCN1BK0IL |title=Syrian army, allies reach airbase besieged by Islamic State in eastern Syria: commander |publisher=Reuters |date=2017-09-09}}</ref>

==對平民的影響==
伊斯蘭國切斷對代爾祖爾市的供電。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://time.com/3916518/isis-syria-photographs/|title=Besieged by ISIS: Photographs From Inside the Syrian City of Deir ez-Zor|work=Time|author=Conal Urquhart|date=11 June 2015}}</ref>在被圍困的平民當中，營養不良現象普遍。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.middleeasteye.net/news/under-russian-syria-and-coalition-bombs-life-deir-ezzour-goes-bad-worse-1457841367|title=Syria's forgotten war: The silent slaughter of Deir Ezzor|work=Middle East Eye|date=18 February 2016}}</ref>自2016年4月起，聯合國的[[世界糧食計劃署|世界糧食計劃署]]在代爾祖爾市進行逾百次空投行動。至2017年1月，仍有超過10萬名平民留在被圍困的地區。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.un.org/apps/news/story.asp?NewsID=55987|title=Syria: Heavy fighting forces UN food relief agency to suspend airdrops in Deir ez-Zor|work=[[United_Nations|United Nations]]|date=17 January 2017}}</ref><ref name="threaten 120,000">{{cite web|url=https://www.rt.com/news/374011-deir-ezzor-offensive-airbase/|title=ISIS cut off Deir ez-Zor airbase, threaten 120,000 civilians – RT reporter|publisher=|accessdate=5 March 2017}}</ref>

'''议会大厦'''（Parliament House）位于[[苏格兰|苏格兰]][[爱丁堡|爱丁堡]][[爱丁堡老城|爱丁堡老城]]的[[议会广场_(爱丁堡)|议会广场]]，[[皇家一英里|皇家一英里]]旁，曾经是苏格兰议会所在地（1639-1707），现在是苏格兰最高法院，毗邻[[圣吉尔斯大教堂|圣吉尔斯大教堂]]。

议会大厦最古老的部分是议会厅（Parliament Hall），是不列颠群岛最古老的议会建筑。<ref>[http://www.rps.ac.uk/ Records of the Scottish Parliament, St Andrews University. Under ''Locations of Parliament''-accessed 26 August 2013]</ref>建于1639年，由詹姆斯·Murray设计。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://canmore.rcahms.gov.uk/en/site/52512/details/edinburgh+11+parliament+square+parliament+hall/ | title = Edinburgh, 11 Parliament Square, Parliament Hall | accessdate = 2012-10-28}}</ref>屋顶使用了[[斯堪的纳维亚|斯堪的纳维亚]]橡木。新[[苏格兰议会大楼|苏格兰议会大楼]]的屋顶延续了这一传统。<ref name="parlsite">{{cite web |url=http://www.scottish.parliament.uk/nmcentre/events/holyroodOpening/h-p-hall.htm |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2010-07-18 |0=2010-09-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100925135440/http://www.scottish.parliament.uk/nmcentre/events/holyroodOpening/h-p-hall.htm |archivedate=2010-09-25 }}</ref>

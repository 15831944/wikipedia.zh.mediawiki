'''超級食肉動物'''為進食食物中有 70% 為[[肉類|肉類]]的[[動物|動物]]，剩下部分則可能包含[[真菌|真菌]]、[[水果|水果]]或其他植物<ref>{{cite journal |author=Van Valkenburgh, B. |year=1988 |title=Trophic diversity in past and present guilds of large predatory mammals |journal=[[Paleobiology_(journal)|Paleobiology]] |volume=14 |pages=155–73}}</ref><ref name=HollidaySteppan>{{cite journal |author1=Holliday, J.A |author2=Steppan, S.J. |year=2004 |title=Evolution of hypercarnivory: the effect of specialization on morphological and taxonomic diversity |journal=[[Paleobiology_(journal)|Paleobiology]] |volume=30|pages=108–128 |doi=10.1666/0094-8373(2004)030<0108:EOHTEO>2.0.CO;2 |issn=0094-8373}}</ref>。一些現存的例子包括：[[鱷魚|鱷魚]]、[[短吻鱷|短吻鱷]]、[[貓頭鷹|貓頭鷹]]、[[伯勞|伯勞]]、[[鵰|鵰]]、[[貓科|貓科]]、多數野生[[犬科|犬科]]、[[海豚|海豚]]、[[虎鯨|虎鯨]]、[[蛇|蛇]]、[[蜘蛛|蜘蛛]]、[[蠍子|蠍子]]、[[螳螂|螳螂]]、[[旗魚|旗魚]]、[[石斑魚|石斑魚]]、[[鯊魚|鯊魚]]等。

[[食肉形類|食肉形類]]（包括食肉目及[[細齒獸超科|細齒獸超科]]，但不包含[[肉齒目|肉齒目]]）、較早期的肉齒目與{{link-en|白堊獸目|Cimolesta}}中的許多已絕種哺乳動物為超級食肉動物。最早的超級食肉哺乳動物為 ''{{link-en|Cimolestes}}''，出現於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]晚期至[[古近紀|古近紀]]早期的北美洲。多數[[獸腳亞目|獸腳亞目]][[恐龍|恐龍]]如[[暴龍|暴龍]]、[[異特龍|異特龍]]等也均為超級食肉動物。

即使親緣相近的物種，兩者依然可以有差異甚大的食性。例如：[[北極熊|北極熊]]與[[棕熊|棕熊]]約於 150,000 年前分家<ref name="Lindqvist">{{Cite journal |title=Complete mitochondrial genome of a Pleistocene jawbone unveils the origin of polar bear |first=Charlotte |last=Lindqvist |first2=Stephan C. |last2=Schuster |first3=Yazhou |last3=Sun |first4=Sandra L. |last4=Talbot |first5=Ji |last5=Qi |first6=Aakrosh |last6=Ratan |first7=Lynn P. |last7=Tomsho |first8=Lindsay |last8=Kasson |first9=Eve |last10=Aars |first10=J. |last11=Miller |first11=W. |last12=Ingolfsson |first12=O. |last13=Bachmann |first13=L. |last14=Wiig |first14=O. |last9=Zeyl |journal=[[Proceedings_of_the_National_Academy_of_Sciences|PNAS]] |volume=107 |issue=11 |pages=5053–5057 |doi=10.1073/pnas.0914266107 |year=2010 |pmid=20194737 |pmc=2841953 |bibcode = 2010PNAS..107.5053L |display-authors=8 }}</ref>，然而北極熊屬於超級食肉動物，進食食物中有 90% 為肉類；而棕熊僅有不到 10% 為肉類<ref>{{cite book|author=Stephen Herrero|year=1985|title=Bear Attacks, their causes and avoidance|page= 156}}</ref><ref>{{cite episode | title = Arctic Bears | url =https://www.pbs.org/wnet/nature/arcticbears/index.html | series = PBS Nature | serieslink = Nature (TV series) | airdate = 17 February 2008}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hww.ca/hww2.asp?id=90&cid=8 |work=Hinterland Who's Who |title=Grizzly |accessdate=March 4, 2010 |postscript={{inconsistent citations}} |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110103075335/http://www.hww.ca/hww2.asp?id=90&cid=8 |archivedate=January 3, 2011 |df= }}</ref>。

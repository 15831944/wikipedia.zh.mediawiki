'''超敏性'''（{{lang|en|ultrasensitivity}}）在[[分子生物学|分子生物学]]中是指某一体系对刺激的敏感性高，能产生强于[[米氏动力学|米氏动力学]]理论值的反应。具有超敏性的反应体系在刺激强度低时反应强度也低，但刺激强度超过某一阈值后，反应强度会迅速升高<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=21562426 |year=2011 |last1=Greenwald |first1=EC |last2=Saucerman |first2=JJ |title=Bigger, Better, Faster: Principles and Models of AKAP Anchoring Protein Signaling |volume=58 |issue=5 |pages=462–9 |doi=10.1097/FJC.0b013e31822001e3 |pmc=3173587 |journal=Journal of cardiovascular pharmacology}}</ref>。

超敏性反应体系的反应强度可以通过以下式子（[[希尔方程_(生物化学)|希尔方程]]）计算，确定了希尔係數(n)就能对一个具超敏性的反应体系进行定量：
:<math chem display="inline">\ce{Response} = {Stimulus^n \over (\ce{EC50}^n+Stimulus^n)}</math>

超敏性是[[细胞|细胞]]从一个[[细胞周期|细胞周期]]进入另一个细胞周期的分子机制之一<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=15907212 |year=2005 |last1=Mutalik |first1=VK |last2=Venkatesh |first2=KV |title=Quantification of the glycogen cascade system: The ultrasensitive responses of liver glycogen synthase and muscle phosphorylase are due to distinctive regulatory designs |volume=2 |pages=19 |doi=10.1186/1742-4682-2-19 |pmc=1180476 |journal=Theoretical biology & medical modelling}}</ref><ref name="Ferrell">{{cite journal |pmid=9868363 |year=1998 |last1=Ferrell Jr |first1=JE |title=How regulated protein translocation can produce switch-like responses |volume=23 |issue=12 |pages=461–5 |journal=Trends in Biochemical Sciences |doi=10.1016/S0968-0004(98)01316-4}}</ref>。

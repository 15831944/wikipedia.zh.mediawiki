'''学院哥特式建筑'''（Collegiate Gothic）是[[哥特复兴式建筑|哥特复兴式建筑]]的一种，吸取了英格兰的[[都铎式建筑|都铎式建筑]]和[[哥特式建筑|哥特式建筑]]的特色。它在19世纪末至20世纪初流行于[[美国|美国]]和[[加拿大|加拿大]]的大学和学院中，因而得名。21世纪初仍有许多大学建筑采用这种风格。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/arts/la-ca-cm-building-type-hogwarts-effect-20171221-story.html|title=College campuses are constructing buildings that look like they're straight out of Harry Potter's world|last=Hawthorne|first=Christopher|website=latimes.com|access-date=2018-03-21}}</ref>

==历史==
美国的[[凯尼恩学院|凯尼恩学院]]在1829年首次采用哥特复兴风格来建造校内的建筑<ref>Rev. Norman Nash designed the building. Architect [[Charles_Bullfinch|Charles Bullfinch]] was asked to review the plans, and designed the steeple. Marjorie Warvelle Harbaugh, "Charles Bullfinch," ''The First Forty Years of Washington DC Architecture'', (Lulu, 2013), p. 362.[https://books.google.com/books?id=A6myBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA362&lpg=PA362&dq=kenyon+college+charles+bulfinch&source=bl&ots=RZYCeSCXa0&sig=gxu1brlvjn0Op1-MLCvM57kKO0I&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0CC0Q6AEwAmoVChMI1cmL0pS1xwIVgjw-Ch1NPAWX#v=onepage&q=kenyon%20college%20charles%20bulfinch&f=false]</ref>。随着[[亚历山大·杰克逊·戴维斯|亚历山大·杰克逊·戴维斯]]为[[纽约大学|纽约大学]]建造的大学堂（University Hall，1833–37）等其他学校建筑的出现<ref>Daniel Coit Gilman, "The Library of Yale College," ''The University Quarterly'' (October 1860), p. 9.[http://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=miun.arc6825.0001.001;view=1up;seq=1]</ref><ref>Kenneth A. Breisch, ''Henry Hobson Richardson and the Small Public Library in America'', (MIT Press, 1997), p. 60.</ref>，哥特复兴式建筑开始大量出现于美国的大學校園中。1880年代开始，[[布林莫尔学院|布林莫尔学院]]<ref>{{cite web |title=Collegiate Gothic |url=http://www.brynmawr.edu/library/exhibits/thomas/gothic.html |publisher=Bryn Mawr Library}}</ref>、[[宾夕法尼亚大学|宾夕法尼亚大学]]、[[普林斯顿大学|普林斯顿大学]]等许多大学也开始建造这类建筑。

“Collegiate Gothic”一词由亚历山大·杰克逊·戴维斯在1853年提出<ref>{{cite book|last1=Truettner|first1=Julia M.|title=Aspirations for Excellence: Alexander Jackson Davis and the First Campus Plan for the University of Michigan, 1838|date=31 December 2002|publisher=University of Michigan Press|page=49|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Hod9Lrg7FW8C&pg=PA49&lpg=PA49|accessdate=16 March 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Golovin|first1=Anne Castrodale|title=Bridgeport's Gothic Ornament The Harral-Wheeler House|url=https://repository.si.edu/bitstream/handle/10088/2417/SSHT-0018_Hi_res.pdf?sequence=1&isAllowed=y|website=Smithsonian Institution|publisher=Smithsonian Institution Press|accessdate=16 March 2018}}</ref>，不过直到1890年代开始这个词才开始流行起来。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Marter, ed.|first1=Joan M.|last2=Regain|first2=Melissa|title=The Grove Encyclopedia of American Art, Volume 1|date=2011|publisher=Oxford University Press|page=362|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=sPGdBxzaWj0C&pg=RA1-PA362|accessdate=16 March 2018}}</ref>

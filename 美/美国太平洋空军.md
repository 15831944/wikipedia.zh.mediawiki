'''太平洋空军'''（'''Pacific Air Forces'''，簡稱'''PACAF'''） 是[[美国空军|美国空军]]的一个[[美国空军一级司令部列表|一级司令部]]，同时担负[[美国印太司令部|美国印太司令部]]的空军职能，总部位于[[夏威夷|夏威夷]][[珍珠港-希卡姆联合基地|珍珠港-希卡姆联合基地]]，是美国空军两个海外一级司令部之一，另一个是[[美國駐歐洲空軍|美國駐歐洲空軍]]，是历史非常悠久的司令部，迄今已有60多年，并曾多次参战。

太平洋空军的辖区是整个亚太地区，东起美国西海岸，西至非洲东海岸和阿拉伯半岛，覆盖约半个地球，包括[[日本|日本]]、[[韩国|韩国]]、[[夏威夷|夏威夷]]、[[阿拉斯加|阿拉斯加]]和[[关岛|关岛]]等约44个国家和地区，辖区内生活着20亿民众。太平洋空军拥有约45,000名空军人员，共有4支[[编号航空队|编号航空队]]、9个主[[美国空军基地|基地]]和近375架各式战机。

== 历史 ==

=== Far East Air Forces ===
:''不要与{{tsl|en|Far East Air Force (United States)}}混淆。这是1941年10月28日-1942年2月5日驻菲律宾与[[荷属东印度|荷属东印度]]的美国陆军航空兵部队''

1944年6月15日，[[美国第十三航空队|美国第十三航空队]]编入了[[西南太平洋战区|西南太平洋战区]]。{{tsl|en|George Kenney}}中将在他的原[[美国第五航空队|第五航空队]]司令部基础上创建了远东空军（Far East Air Forces，FEAF），<ref>http://www.af.mil/AboutUs/Biographies/Display/tabid/225/Article/104770/general-george-churchill-kenney.aspx</ref>，同时前进指挥所（Advanced Echelon）成为第五航空队的新司令部。{{sfn|Griffith|1998|pp=174–175}}1944年8月3日，远东空军正式在澳大利亚[[布里斯班|布里斯班]]开始战斗值班。1944年10月，澳大利亚空军以{{tsl|en|Australian First Tactical Air Force|澳大利亚第一战术航空队}}名义编入远东空军。{{sfn|Stephens|2001|pp=168–170}} 随着麦克阿瑟掌握太平洋地区全部美军的指挥权，1945年7月14日[[美国第十三航空队|美国第十三航空队]]编入了远东空军。{{sfn|Kenney|1949|pp=537–538}}远东空军隶属于{{tsl|en|U.S. Army Forces Far East|远东美军}}。<ref>Globalsecurity.org, [http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/agency/usaf/pacaf.htm PACAF History Fact Sheet]</ref>

二战结束时，远东空军部属于东至夏威夷，西至印度，北至日本，南至澳大利亚的广阔战区的数百条岛屿跑道与中国、缅甸等机场。为适应和平时期重新部署，1945年12月6日远东空军改称'''美国陆军太平洋航空司令部'''（Pacific Air Command, United States Army，PACUSA）。部署调整为：
* 第五航空队：日本东京。负责占领[[日本本土|日本本土]]与[[朝鲜半岛|朝鲜半岛]]南部。
* 第七航空队：夏威夷[[珍珠港-希卡姆联合基地|希卡姆机场]]，部属于中太平洋岛屿。
* [[美国第8航空军|第八航空队]]：[[冲绳县|冲绳县]][[嘉手纳空军基地|嘉手纳空军基地]]，负责[[琉球群岛|琉球群岛]]与[[琉璜岛|琉璜岛]]。
* 第十三航空队：菲律宾[[克拉克空军基地|克拉克空军基地]]。负责菲律宾、[[新几内亚|新几内亚]]、[[所罗门群岛|所罗门群岛]]。
* [[美国第20航空军|第二十航空队]]：关岛{{tsl|en|Harmon Air Force Base|Harmon空军基地}}。负责西太平洋地区战略威慑.
太平洋空军管理并指挥战区内所有的陆军航空兵。<ref>[http://www.history.army.mil/documents/8-5/8-5.htm Chronology of the Occupation]</ref><ref>[http://ibiblio.org/hyperwar/AAF/VII/AAF-VII-17.html Army Air Forces in World War II Vol. VII: Services Around the World CHAPTER 17 REDEPLOYMENT AND DEMOBILIZATION]</ref><ref>[http://www.afhra.af.mil/organizationalrecords/numberedairforces.asp USAF Historical Research Agency] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081024232733/http://www.afhra.af.mil/organizationalrecords/numberedairforces.asp |date=2008-10-24 }}</ref>

1945年11月，{{tsl|en|509th Composite Group|第509混成航空大队}}离开了提尼安岛{{tsl|en|North Field (Tinian)|北机场}}，部署到新墨西哥{{tsl|en|Walker AFB|罗斯威尔陆军航空兵机场}}，把原子弹投送轰炸能力由远东转移到美国本土。不久，1946年6月7日第八航空队转隶新成立的{{tsl|en|Strategic Air Command|美国战略空军司令部}}。

1946-1950年间，远东空军的任务是占领日本、对抗弱小的苏联远东航空力量、在[[太平洋试验场|西太平洋试验场]]进行大量原子弹爆炸试验。[[美国陆军航空兵|陆航]]意欲证明核武及战略轰炸将是第三次世界大战的制胜武器，只有空军远程轰炸机能作有效原子打击；而[[美国海军|海军]]则欲阻止陆航垄断核武投射权力，并引证水面军舰能够有效抵御核爆。 
[[File:Far_East_Air_Forces_-_Emblem_1954.png|thumb]]
随着美国空军作为一支独立[[军种|军种]]的创建，1947年1月1日太平洋陆航改称为远东空军（Far East Air Forces，FEAF）。同时，夏威夷的第7航空队撤销。<ref>[http://www.afhra.af.mil/factsheets/factsheet.asp?id=12354 PACAF page, AFHRA] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081024193207/http://www.afhra.af.mil/factsheets/factsheet.asp?id=12354 |date=2008-10-24 }}</ref>

=== 冷战===
[[File:Dafb-c-124-f-104-1958.jpg|thumb]]在加州准备装载[[F-104|F-104]]运往台湾, 1958年.]]
朝鲜停战后，远东空军在日本与韩国的力量逐渐衰减。1955年3月1日，第20航空队撤销。<ref name="multiple6"/>

1954年7月1日，在夏威夷成立太平洋空军（Pacific Air Force，PACAF）。隶属于远东空军。1956年7月1日改称太平洋空军/远东空军后方（Pacific Air Force/FEAF(Rear)）。1957年7月1日，远东空军正式撤到夏威夷改称太平洋空军（Pacific Air Forces，PACAF）。<ref name="multiple6"/>

五十年代中期的两次[[台湾海峡危机|台湾海峡危机]]牵累了太平洋空军极大精力。1955年解放军[[一江山岛战役|三军联合作战夺取一江山岛]]后，美国远东海军与太平洋空军出动重兵掩护[[大陈岛战役|大陈岛撤退]]；同时美国为了维护台湾海峡军事平衡，通过了[[福爾摩沙決議案|福尔摩萨决议案]]，许诺在解放军进攻[[马祖岛|马祖岛]]或[[金门岛|金门岛]]时，将用原子弹轰炸中国大陆主要城市。[[炮击金门|1958年台海危机]]高潮中，太平洋空军出动[[F-104|F-104C]]进驻[[台中清泉岗机场|清泉岗空军基地]]以确保台湾海峡上空的制空权。<ref>[http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/ops/quemoy_matsu-2.htm Second Taiwan Strait Crisis Quemoy and Matsu Islands]</ref>[[1960年美国总统大选|1960年美国总统大选]]中，是否应该确保[[马祖岛|马祖岛]]或[[金门岛|金门岛]]不被共军夺取而不惜为此与中国、苏联展开同归于尽的核大战成为激烈交锋的政治议题。共和党总统竞选人[[理查德·尼克松|理查德·尼克松]]指控[[John_F._Kennedy|John F. Kennedy]]不愿承诺为了这两个亚洲大陆沿岸几公里内的国民党人（Nationalist）占据的前沿小岛在被共产党进攻时使用美国的核武器。<ref>[http://www-cgi.cnn.com/ALLPOLITICS/1996/debates/history/1960/ 1960 Presidential Debates @ CNN.com] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080913151716/http://www-cgi.cnn.com/ALLPOLITICS/1996/debates/history/1960/ |date=2008-09-13 }}</ref>为此，肯尼迪在就职演说中宣布要为冷战前沿的盟友们承担无底线的义务：“我们将付出任何代价，忍受任何重负，应付任何艰辛，支持任何朋友，反对任何敌人，以确保自由的存在与实现。这是我们矢志不移的事...”，带领美国义无反顾地走向了[[越南战争|越南战争]]。

1960年，太平洋空军报纸了一支备战威慑力量，共计35个中队，部署在6个国家的10个空军基地。<ref name="multiple6"/>

=== 越南战争 ===
{{See also|United States Air Force in South Vietnam|在泰国的太平洋空军}}

[[File:F-4E_of_the_421st_Tactical_Fighter_Squadron_at_DaNang.jpg|thumb]]的第421战术战斗机中队的F-4E]]
[[File:F-105G-63-8319-561tfs.jpg|thumb]]
[[File:F-100F_352TFS_35TFW_PhanRang_1971.jpg|thumb]]的第352战术战斗机中队的F-100F-20-NA352d]]

1960年代初期，随着肯尼迪发动在[[南越|南越]]的[[特种战争|特种战争]]，太平洋空军开始调整部署与编制，并大力武装[[越南共和国|西贡政权]]空军。1964年[[东京湾事件|东京湾事件]]，美国借此正式派遣陆海军全面参加南越战事，并对北越的城市与交通线展开地毯式战略轰炸。{{tsl|en|Tactical Air Command|战术空军司令部}}指挥战斗机中队进驻南越的[[岘港|岘港]]、[[藩朗-塔占市|藩朗]]、泰国的[[呵叻|呵叻]]、[[北榄坡|北榄坡]]等空军基地。<ref name="multiple7">Futrell, Robert F. with the assistance of Blumenson, Martin (1991) The United States Air Force in Southeast Asia: The Advisory Years to 1965, Office Of Air Force History, United States Air Forceriority in Korea. Annapolis: U.S. Naval Institute Press, 2005. ISBN 1-59114-933-9.</ref>

美国从本土调集大量战术空军部属于菲律宾、日本、台湾、韩国，以轮战方式进驻南越与泰国前沿部署。<ref name="multiple7"/>

1962年，太平洋空军组建了{{tsl|en|2d Air Division|第二航空师}}，作为在南越的空军指挥机构。随着战事不断扩大深化，1966年4月1日组建了[[美国第七航空队|美国第七航空队]]负责南越的空军指挥任务。

1968年越战高潮时，太平洋空军的部队如下：<ref name="multiple8">Schlight, John (1996) A War Too Long: The History of the USAF in Southeast Asia, 1961–1975, Office Of Air Force History, United States Air Force</ref>

* 日本(第五航空队)
* 南韩 (第五航空队)
* 菲律宾 (第13航空队)
* 台湾 (第13航空队)
* 南越 (第7航空队)
* 泰国(第7/13航空队)
 
1970年，尼克松总统开始搞越南战争{{tsl|en|Vietnamization|越南化}}，即出钱出枪让越南人打越南人，美国不再秉持肯尼迪“付出任何代价，忍受任何重负，应付任何艰辛，支持任何朋友，反对任何敌人”的无限无条件支持任何盟友的立场。太平洋空军在越南的参战规模日益收缩。1970年，在泰国成立了第7/13航空队联合司令部（joint Seventh/Thirteenth Air Force headquarters ），直接对太平洋空军报告。一直到1976年从东南亚撤出为止。<ref name="multiple8"/><ref>Glasser, Jeffrey D. (1998). The Secret Vietnam War: The United States Air Force in Thailand, 1961–1975. McFarland & Company. ISBN 0-7864-0084-6.</ref>
1973年8月15日空袭柬埔寨，是太平洋空军在越南战争的最后一个篇章。

1976年太平洋空军在泰国的军事基地全部移交给泰军。1979年，由于中美建交，太平洋空军被迫撤出在台湾[[台中清泉岗机场|清泉岗空军基地]]的核轰炸与战术空军部队。<ref name="multiple8"/>
=== 冷战后期===
<!--
[[File:51fwosan.jpg|thumb]]
The post-Vietnam era found the command focusing on improving its readiness and PACAF's organizational structure saw a marked period of rapid and extensive changes. Inactivated at the end of the Vietnam War in 1975, [[Seventh_Air_Force|Seventh Air Force]] was reactivated at [[Osan_Air_Base|Osan Air Base]], South Korea in 1986 to take over [[Fifth_Air_Force|Fifth Air Force]] activities in South Korea.  Also in 1986, the [[Goldwater-Nichols_Act|Goldwater-Nichols Act]] reworked the overall command structure of the United States military.  With the creation of [[Unified_Combatant_Command|Unified Combatant Command]]s (UCC) organized either on a geographical basis (known as "Area of Responsibility", AOR) or on a functional basis, Pacific Air Forces became a part of the [[United_States_Pacific_Command|United States Pacific Command]] (USPACOM).

[[Andersen_AFB|Andersen AFB]] in Guam was reassigned from [[Strategic_Air_Command|Strategic Air Command]] ([[Strategic_Air_Command|SAC]]) to PACAF in 1989, and [[Eleventh_Air_Force|Eleventh Air Force]] became a part of the command in late 1990. In the late 1980s and early 1990s, civil unrest in the Philippines and negotiations with the extant government of the [[Republic_of_the_Philippines|Republic of the Philippines]] for the lease of [[Clark_Air_Base|Clark Air Base]], along with other U.S. military installations in the Philippines, had reached an impasse.  However, following the volcanic eruption of [[Mount_Pinatubo|Mount Pinatubo]], the resultant damage to Clark AB, and with a post-[[Cold_War|Cold War]] desire by the U.S. Government to reduce defense spending, Clark AB was closed and [[Thirteenth_Air_Force|Thirteenth Air Force]] relocated in 1991.<ref name="multiple6"/>

In 1992, changes took place in force structure within PACAF as the command assumed control of theater-based tactical airlift wings, theater C-130 aircraft and crews, and associated theater C-130 support following the disestablishment of [[Military_Airlift_Command|Military Airlift Command]] ([[Military_Airlift_Command|MAC]]). PACAF also gained control of all operational support aircraft and all aeromedical airlift assets in the Pacific previously under the cognizance of MAC.<ref name="multiple6"/>  With the concurrent disestablishment of [[Strategic_Air_Command|Strategic Air Command]] ([[Strategic_Air_Command|SAC]]) and [[Tactical_Air_Command|Tactical Air Command]] ([[Tactical_Air_Command|TAC]]) the same year, PACAF also assumed responsibility for all active KC-135 aerial refueling aircraft based in Hawaii and Japan, "gaining command" responsibility for all Air National Guard KC-135 aircraft in Hawaii and Alaska, and all E-3 AWACS aircraft in Japan and Alaska.

Throughout its history PACAF has played a vital role in world events. In addition to its key combat role in World War II, Korea and Vietnam, PACAF units fought in [[Desert_Storm|Desert Storm]] in 1991 and continued to deploy to [[Saudi_Arabia|Saudi Arabia]], Turkey and [[Italy|Italy]] for peacekeeping operations such as [[Operation_Southern_Watch|Operation Southern Watch]] and [[Operation_Northern_Watch|Operation Northern Watch]]. PACAF provided its expertise, aircraft, personnel and equipment to facilitate the new Expeditionary Air Force, especially as it applied to successful airbridge operations spanning the vast Pacific Ocean. Following the 11 September 2001 terrorist attacks on the United States, PACAF again demonstrated its intrepid spirit through its units deployed in support of [[Operation_Noble_Eagle|Operation Noble Eagle]], [[Operation_Enduring_Freedom|Operation Enduring Freedom]] and, in 2003, [[Operation_Iraqi_Freedom|Operation Iraqi Freedom]].<ref name="multiple6"/>

Since 1944, the command has also participated in more than 140 humanitarian operations within its area of responsibility and beyond. In these operations PACAF people quickly and efficiently airlifted food, medicine and other supplies to areas devastated by storms, floods, earthquakes, volcanoes and other natural disasters.<ref name="multiple6"/>

As PACAF entered the second decade of the 21st century, expanding theater challenges and simultaneous resource constraints have forced continuing innovation and adjustments by PACAF in order to meet mission requirements.  Previously assigned four numbered air forces, PACAF downscoped to three numbered air forces in September 2012, inactivating [[13th_Air_Force|13th Air Force]] and merging its functions into PACAF.  Base consolidations and infrastructure limitations have also required the Air Force and PACAF to developed increased capability while striving to remain within budgetary resource constraints.  One such example has been the evaluation of alternate runway(s)/divert field(s) in the Marianas since late 2011 as a backup to Andersen AFB on Guam, a process that remains on-going as of 2014.<ref>Kelman, Brett. [http://www.airforcetimes.com/news/2011/10/gannett-air-force-guam-runway-western-pacific-101111/ "AF seeks backup runway in western Pacific."] ''Pacific Daily News'', 11 October 2011.</ref><ref>http://www.mvariety.com/cnmi/cnmi-news/local/71785-air-force-still-mulls-saipan-tinian-alternatives</ref>

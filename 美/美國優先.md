'''美国優先'''（{{lang-en|'''America First'''}}）指的是[[美国|美国]]的一类[[外交政策|外交政策]]，强调{{link-en|美國民族主義|American nationalism|美国的民族主义}}和{{link-en|不干涉主義|Non-interventionism}}，这是[[唐纳德·特朗普|唐纳德·特朗普]][[美国总统|总统]]的现行政策。<ref name="WH.gov">{{cite web|title=America First Foreign Policy|url=https://www.whitehouse.gov/america-first-foreign-policy|website=Whitehouse.gov|publisher=The White House|accessdate=January 26, 2017}}</ref><ref name="NPRAF">{{cite news|last1=Shapiro|first1=Ari|title=As Trump Adopts 'America First' Policy, China's Global Role Could Change|url=https://www.npr.org/2017/01/23/511267259/as-trump-adopts-america-first-policy-chinas-global-role-could-change|accessdate=January 26, 2017|agency=National Public Radio|date=January 23, 2017}}</ref><ref name="economist.com">{{cite news|title=The New Nationalism|url=https://www.economist.com/news/leaders/21710249-his-call-put-america-first-donald-trump-latest-recruit-dangerous|accessdate=January 26, 2017|publisher=The Economist|date=November 19, 2016}}</ref><ref name="BBCAF">{{cite news|title=Trump details 'America First' foreign plan|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/election-us-2016-36152947|accessdate=January 26, 2017|agency=BBC World Service|date=April 28, 2016}}</ref>

==历史==
美国第一次有时候被一些[[共和黨_(美國)|共和党]]人当作其纲领，尤其是[[帕特·布坎南|帕特·布坎南]]，他赞扬反二战的{{link-en|美国優先委員會|America First Committee}}，并说“该组织的成就是巨大的”。<ref>{{cite journal|author=Pat Buchanan|title=The Resurrection of 'America First!'|url=http://www.theamericancause.org/patamericafirst.htm|publisher=The American Cause|date=October 13, 2004|accessdate=2008-02-03|postscript={{inconsistent citations}}}}</ref>布坎南呼吁“我们应当实行一个美国優先的政策”，并将其与美国優先委员会进行了对比。<ref>Michael Cox and Martin Durham, "The Politics of Anger: The Extreme Right in the United States" (p. 287), in Paul Hainsworth, ed., ''The Politics of the Extreme Right: From the Margins to the Mainstream'', London/New York: Pinter, 2000, {{ISBN|1855674599}}</ref>

这个口号被一些批评人士拿来与美国優先委员会进行比较；<ref>{{cite news|last1=Elving|first1=Ron|title=Trump Vows Policy Vision Of 'America First,' Recalling Phrase's Controversial Past|url=https://www.npr.org/2017/01/21/510877650/trump-vows-policy-vision-of-america-first-recalling-phrases-controversial-past|accessdate=January 27, 2017|agency=NPR|date=January 21, 2017}}</ref>然而，特朗普否认自己是孤立主义者，并且说：“我喜欢这个表述。”<ref>{{cite news|last1=Thomas|first1=Louisa|title=America First, for Charles Lindbergh and Donald Trump|url=http://www.newyorker.com/news/news-desk/america-first-for-charles-lindbergh-and-donald-trump|accessdate=February 2, 2017|work=The New Yorker|date=July 24, 2016}}</ref>。一些学者（如{{link-en|德博拉·達什·摩爾|Deborah Dash Moore}}），评论员（如{{link-en|比爾·克里斯托爾|Bill Kristol}}）和犹太组织（包括[[反誹謗聯盟|反誹謗聯盟]]和{{link-en|猶太人公共事務委員會|Jewish Council for Public Affairs}}）批评特朗普使用该口号，因为它与[[本土主义|本土主义]]和[[反犹主义|反犹主义]]有着历史联系。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Thomas|first1=Louisa|title=America First, for Charles Lindbergh and Donald Trump|url=http://www.newyorker.com/news/news-desk/america-first-for-charles-lindbergh-and-donald-trump|accessdate=February 2, 2017|work=The New Yorker|date=July 24, 2016}}<br/>{{cite news|last1=Calamur|first1=Krishnadev|title=A Short History of 'America First'|url=https://www.theatlantic.com/politics/archive/2017/01/trump-america-first/514037/|accessdate=January 29, 2017|work=The Atlantic|date=January 21, 2017}}<br/>{{cite news|last1=Nathan-Kazis|first1=Josh|title=Trump’s ‘America First’ Leaves Jewish Groups Hesitant|url=http://forward.com/news/national/360712/america-first-comes-to-the-white-house-as-jews-warn-of-anti-semtitic-echoes/|accessdate=January 29, 2017|work=The Forward|date=January 20, 2017}}</ref>

相比于其他[[发达国家|发达国家]]，'''美国的贫困问题'''相对严重，贫困率也较高。

美国政府{{link-en|贫困线 (美国)|Poverty thresholds (United States Census Bureau)|对相对贫困的定义}}是“缺乏主流社会拥有的生活物资，无法享受主流社会应有的服务”<ref name="Schwartz">{{cite book |last=Schwartz |first=J. E. |year=2005 |title=Freedom Reclaimed: Rediscovering the American Vision |location=Baltimore |publisher=Johns Hopkins University Press |isbn=0-8018-7981-7 }}</ref>。2015年的调查数据表明，美国13.5%的人口是贫困的<ref name="2009 Poverty Rate Reuters">"Basic Statistics - Talk Poverty". ''Talk Poverty''. Retrieved October 27, 2015.</ref>。但也有观点认为大约三分之一的美国人（大约一亿人）生活在贫困线下，或生活水平仅略高于贫困线<ref>{{Cite book|title=Routledge Handbook of Poverty in the United States|publisher=Routledge|year=2015|isbn=978 0 41 567344 0|editor-last=Haymes|editor-first=Stephen N.|location=London and New York|pages=7|editor-last2=de Haymes|editor-first2=María V.|editor-last3=Miller|editor-first3=Reuben J.|display-editors=1}}</ref>。同时有观点指出，大约一半的美国人相对贫困或属于低收入群体<ref>[http://www.cbsnews.com/news/census-data-half-of-us-poor-or-low-income/ Census data: Half of U.S. poor or low income]. ''[[CBS_News|CBS News]],'' December 15, 2011. Retrieved February 10, 2014.</ref>。[[赤贫|赤贫]]人口方面，2011年，美国有150万个日均开支少于两[[美元|美元]]的赤贫家庭，较1996年翻了一番，其中包括280万名孩童<ref>[http://npc.umich.edu/publications/policy_briefs/brief28/policybrief28.pdf "Extreme Poverty in the United States, 1996 to 2011"] ''National Poverty Center'', February 2012</ref>。2016年，[[国际货币基金组织|国际货币基金组织]]（IMF）警告称，高贫困率是美国亟需解决的问题<ref>[https://www.bbc.com/news/business-36599316 IMF warns the US over high poverty]. BBC, June 22, 2016.</ref>。

20世纪30年代，美国的相对贫困人口开始多于富裕人口。2009年，美国的贫困率已回到20世纪60年代美国总统[[林登·约翰逊|林登·约翰逊]]发起的“{{link-en|向贫困宣战|War on Poverty}}”运动开始前的水平<ref>{{cite web |title=U.S. Health in International Perspective |url= http://www.nap.edu/openbook.php?record_id=13497&page=171 |publisher=National Research Council and Institute of Medicine |accessdate= 2014-02-11 |author1=Woolf, Steven |author2=Aaron, Laudon |pages=171–172}}</ref>。美国的儿童贫困率近年日趋严重。美国的儿童贫困率在2011年达到高峰，大约有167万名孩童缺乏足够的食物，相比2007年的数据上升了35%。根据一份[[联合国儿童基金会|联合国儿童基金会]]2012年的报告，美国的儿童贫困率为23.1%，在发达国家中第二高，仅低于[[罗马尼亚|罗马尼亚]]<ref name=WalkerBBC>{{cite news|last=Walker|first=Duncan|title=The children going hungry in America|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/magazine-21636723|accessdate=2013-03-13|newspaper=BBC News|date=2013-03-06}}</ref><ref name=UNICEF2012>{{cite web |author1=UNICEF |title=Measuring child poverty - New league tables of child poverty in the world's rich countries |url=https://www.unicef-irc.org/publications/pdf/rc10_eng.pdf |publisher=UNICEF |accessdate=2018-10-11}}</ref>。

美国城市[[郊区|郊区]]的贫困率低于市中心和[[农村|农村地区]]<ref>{{cite web | last = Savage | first = Sarah | title = Child Poverty High in Rural America | url=http://www.carseyinstitute.unh.edu/publications/FS_RuralChildPoverty_08.pdf | accessdate = 2008-08-26}}</ref><ref>[http://newswise.com/articles/view/543788/ Child Poverty High in Rural America] Newswise, Retrieved on August 26, 2008.</ref>。大部分的美国人在25至75岁之间会有一段时间生活在贫困线以下<ref name="Hacker">{{cite journal |last=Hacker |first=J. S. |year=2006 |title=The Great Risk Shift: The New Insecurity and the Decline of the American Dream |location=New York |publisher=Oxford University Press |isbn=0-19-533534-1 }}</ref>。美国相对贫困率最低的州是[[新罕布什尔州|新罕布什尔州]]、[[佛蒙特州|佛蒙特州]]、[[明尼苏达州|明尼苏达州]]和[[内布拉斯加州|内布拉斯加州]]<ref name="SPM2016">{{cite web|author1=Renwick, Trudi|author2=Fox, Liana|title=The Supplemental Poverty Measure: 2015|url=https://census.gov/library/publications/2016/demo/p60-258.html |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|date=2016-09-13|quote=Number and Percentage of People in Poverty by State Using 3-Year Average Over 2013, 2014, and 2015}}</ref>。

[[美国联邦政府|美国联邦政府]]有两个主要的贫困标准，一个是由[[美国人口调查局|美国人口调查局]]制定的“贫困线”（{{lang|en|poverty thresholds}}），主要用于估算贫困人口，并对其[[种族|种族]]、性别、年龄进行统计<ref>{{Cite web|url = https://www.census.gov/hhes/www/poverty/about/overview/measure.html|title = How the Census Bureau Measures Poverty|date = |accessdate = 2015-09-16|website = census.gov|publisher = [[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100711082047/http://www.census.gov/hhes/www/poverty/about/overview/measure.html|archivedate = 2010-07-11}}</ref>；一个是由[[美国卫生及公共服务部|美国卫生及公共服务部]]制定的“贫困指南”（{{lang|en|poverty guidelines}}），主要用于测评相关人士或家庭是否符合领取救济金的标准<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://aspe.hhs.gov/2015-poverty-guidelines|title = 2015 POVERTY GUIDELINES|date = |accessdate = 2015-09-03|website = hhs.gov|publisher = United States Department of Health and Human Services}}</ref>。美国经济学家{{link-en|莫莉·奥珊斯基|Mollie_Orshansky}}对美国贫困标准的制定做出了巨大贡献。她认为应该基于人必须的饮食开销以及家庭的人口来制定贫困标准<ref>Mollie Orshansky. [http://www.ssa.gov/policy/docs/ssb/v26n7/v26n7p3.pdf Children of the Poor]. ''Social Security Bulletin'', Vol. 26, No. 7, July 1963, pp. 3-13.</ref><ref>Mollie Orshansky. [http://www.ssa.gov/policy/docs/ssb/v28n1/v28n1p3.pdf Counting the Poor:  Another Look at the Poverty Profile]. ''Social Security Bulletin'', Vol. 28, No. 1, January 1965, pp. 3-29</ref>。目前，如果一个家庭的收入少于基本食品开销7.8倍（过去的标准是3倍{{NoteTag|因食品开销随时代发展在家庭总支出中占比下降，因此这个标准从3倍上调到了7.8倍。}}），那么就可以认为这个家庭生活在贫困线以下<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.brookings.edu/testimonies/why-the-united-states-needs-an-improved-measure-of-poverty/|title=Why the United States Needs an Improved Measure of Poverty|last=Blank|first=Rebecca M.|date=2008-07-17|work=Brookings|access-date=2018-08-31|language=en-US}}</ref>。

==具体情况==
在美国，[[黑人|黑人]]的贫困率高于[[白人|白人]]。此外，[[西班牙裔|西班牙裔]]美国人贫困率相对较高。2014年的调查显示，10.1%的贫困人口是非西裔白人、12%的贫困人口是[[亚裔美国人|亚裔]]，西裔、[[非洲裔美国人|黑人]]、[[美洲原住民|原住民]]贫困人口占总贫困人口的大头，分别占总贫困人口的23.6%、26.2% 、28.3%<ref name=pov10>"[https://www.census.gov/content/dam/Census/library/publications/2015/demo/p60-252.pdf Income and Poverty in the United States: 2014]". U.S. Census Bureau</ref>。贫困率也与家庭组成有关。根据2007年的统计数据，[[单亲家庭|单亲家庭]]的贫困率最高，为26.6%，其次是单身者，为19.1%，而完整家庭的贫困率相对较低，仅有5.8%<ref name=pov02>U.S. Census Bureau. Current Population Survey. ''Persons  in Families by Family Structure, Age, and Sex, Iterated by Income-to-Poverty Ratio and Race: 2007: [http://pubdb3.census.gov/macro/032008/pov/new02_100_01.htm Below 100% of Poverty – All Races] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080930073740/http://pubdb3.census.gov/macro/032008/pov/new02_100_01.htm |date=2008-09-30 }}.''</ref>。调查数据表明，超过75%的贫困家庭的主要收入来自家中的女性<ref>{{cite book|last=Dáil|first=Paula vW.|title=Women and Poverty in 21st Century America|year=2012|publisher=McFarland|location=NC, USA|isbn=978-0-7864-4903-3|pages=27|url=http://www.mcfarlandpub.com/book-2.php?id=978-0-7864-4903-3}}</ref>。美国[[美国监禁制度|贫困人口的监禁率]]远高于其他发达国家<ref name=Yellen_Ineq14>{{Cite web|url=https://www.federalreserve.gov/newsevents/speech/yellen20141017a.htm|title=Perspectives on Inequality and Opportunity from the Survey of Consumer Finances|website=Board of Governors of Federal Reserve System|accessdate=2016-12-26}}</ref>。

美国的儿童贫困问题和长者贫困问题较为突出。根据2010年[[经济合作与发展组织|经济合作与发展组织]]（OECD）的调查报告，美国的儿童贫困率是20%，而长者贫困率是23%<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.oecd.org/dataoecd/47/2/41528678.pdf|format=PDF|title=OECD (2008), Growing Unequal? : Income Distribution and Poverty in OECD Countries : COUNTRY NOTE: UNITED STATES|website=Oecd.org|accessdate=2017-10-14}}</ref>。相当比例的儿童面临饥饿的威胁<ref>{{cite news | title=3.5M Kids Under 5 On Verge Of Going Hungry <br />Study: 11 Percent Of U.S. Households Lack Food For Healthy Lifestyle | url=http://www.cbsnews.com/stories/2009/05/07/health/main4998190.shtml | format="SHTML | work=Health | publisher=CBS NEWS | date=2009-05-07 | accessdate=2009-05-08}}</ref>。2011年，美国的儿童贫困率达到历史最高位，约有167万名孩童无法获得充足的食物，相比2007年的数据上升了35%。[[联合国儿童基金会|联合国儿童基金会]]（UNICEF）发表于2012年的报告指出，美国的儿童贫困率为23.1%，在[[发达国家|发达国家]]中仅比[[罗马尼亚|罗马尼亚]]低<ref name=WalkerBBC>{{cite news|last=Walker|first=Duncan|title=The children going hungry in America|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/magazine-21636723|accessdate=2013-03-13|newspaper=BBC News|date=2013-03-06}}</ref><ref name=UNICEF2012>{{cite web |author1=UNICEF |title=Measuring child poverty - New league tables of child poverty in the world's rich countries |url=https://www.unicef-irc.org/publications/pdf/rc10_eng.pdf |publisher=UNICEF |accessdate=2018-10-11}}</ref>。研究表明贫困人口也更难获得更好的教育，来自贫困家庭的孩童教育发展明显受到限制。另一方面，许多美国贫困人口教育水平较低。低学历被认为是造成贫困的重要原因之一<ref name="EngleBlack2008">{{cite journal|last1=Engle|first1=Patrice L.|last2=Black|first2=Maureen M.|title=The Effect of Poverty on Child Development and Educational Outcomes|journal=Annals of the New York Academy of Sciences|volume=1136|issue=1|year=2008|pages=243–256|issn=00778923|doi=10.1196/annals.1425.023}}</ref><ref name=Western2010>Western, B. & Pettit, B., (2010). Incarceration and social inequality. Daedalus, 139(3), 8-19</ref>。

不过，也有观点认为，美国的贫困问题被夸大了。保守派的[[美国传统基金会|美国传统基金会]]在2011年称，美国的贫困人口大都能获得充足的饮食、服装，以及医疗服务，亦有可供居住的住所<ref>Jonathan Rothwell (November 8, 2011). Retrieved November 18, 2014.</ref>。2005年的统计表明，78.3%的贫困家庭有空调<ref>{{cite web |url=https://templatelab.com/what-is-poverty/ |title=Air Conditioning, Cable TV, and an Xbox: What is Poverty in the United States Today? |last1=Rector |first1=Robert |last2=Rachel |first2=Sheffield|date=2011-07-18 |work=The Heritage Foundation |accessdate=2011-07-27}}</ref><ref name="Timbro, E.U. vs U.S.A">{{cite web|url=http://www.timbro.se/bokhandel/pdf/9175665646.pdf|format=PDF|title=E.U. vs U.S.A|author=Timbro|accessdate=2007-11-10|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161115141716/http://www.timbro.se/bokhandel/pdf/9175665646.pdf|archivedate=2016-11-15}}</ref>。[[史迪芬·平克|史迪芬·平克]]在一篇发表于《[[华尔街日报|华尔街日报]]》的文章中提出了他对贫困的新定义。按他的标准，美国2018年的贫困率应为3%，比1988年的11%要低<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/the-enlightenment-is-working-1518191343 |title=The Enlightenment is Working |author1=Steven Pinker |authorlink=Steven Pinker |date=2018-02-09 |publisher=Wall Street Journal | accessdate=2018-02-11}}</ref>。

==造成贫困的原因==
[[File:Homeless_Please_Help_(7497165720).jpg|缩略图]]
在美国，收入分配不均是造成贫困人口的重要原因之一<ref>Adams, R. H., (2004). Economic growth, inequality, and poverty: Estimating the growth elasticity of poverty, World Development 32(12), p. 1989-2014.</ref>。此外，学历偏低的人群中贫困率更高，高教育水平人群的收入水平相对较高，有相对低的贫困率<ref name=Western2010/><ref name="U. S. Census: Income, Expenditures, Poverty and Wealth, Table 698">{{cite web|url=https://www.census.gov/prod/2009pubs/10statab/income.pdf|format=PDF|title= U. S. Census: Income, Expenditures, Poverty and Wealth|accessdate=2010-03-20}}</ref>。失业者更容易处于贫困之中。根据2007年的统计数据，21.5%的失业者是贫困人口，而仅有2.5%的全职工作者是贫困人口<ref name="U. S. Census: Income, Expenditures, Poverty and Wealth, Table 698"/>。有研究表明美国的[[社会保障|社会保障制度]]确实促进了贫困率的下降，如果没有现行的社会保障制度，贫困率将会更高<ref>Drum, Kevin (December 9, 2013). [https://www.motherjones.com/kevin-drum/2013/12/new-study-says-poverty-rate-hasnt-budged-40-years New Study Says Poverty Rate Hasn't Budged For 40 Years]. ''Mother Jones.'' Retrieved February 15, 2014.</ref><ref>Zachary A. Goldfarb (December 9, 2013). [https://www.washingtonpost.com/business/economy/study-us-poverty-rate-decreased-over-past-half-century-thanks-to-safety-net-programs/2013/12/09/9322c834-60f3-11e3-94ad-004fefa61ee6_story.html Study: U.S. poverty rate decreased over past half-century thanks to safety-net programs]. ''[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]].'' Retrieved January 20, 2015.</ref>。但尽管如此，美国的社会保障制度却是发达国家中最脆弱的。美国低程度的社会保障开销是贫困率相对较高的原因之一<ref>{{cite journal |last1= Kenworthy |first1= L. |year= 1999 |title= Do Social-Welfare Policies Reduce Poverty? A Cross-National Assessment| journal =  Social Forces|volume = 77|issue=3|pages= 1119–1139 |doi=10.1093/sf/77.3.1119}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Bradley, D., E. Huber, S. Moller, F. Nielsen, and J. D. Stephens |year=2003 | title = Determinants of Relative Poverty in Advanced Capitalist Democracies| journal = American Sociological Review | volume = 68 | issue = 1| pages = 22–51 | doi = 10.2307/3088901 }}</ref>。

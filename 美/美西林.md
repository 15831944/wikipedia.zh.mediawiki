'''美西林'''（[[国际非专利药品名称|INN]]：Mecillinam；[[United_States_Adopted_Name|USAN]]：Amdinocillin）是一種广谱半合成[[青霉素|青霉素]]<ref name=Neu>{{cite journal |author=Neu HC |title=Amdinocillin: a novel penicillin. Antibacterial activity, pharmacology and clinical use |journal=Pharmacotherapy |volume=5 |issue=1 |pages=1–10 |year=1985 |pmid=3885172 |doi= 10.1002/j.1875-9114.1985.tb04448.x|url=}}</ref>，對[[革兰氏阴性菌|革兰氏阴性菌]]作用强，也可用來治療[[傷寒|傷寒]]和[[副伤寒|副伤寒]]<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Clarke PD, Geddes AM, McGhie D, Wall JC |title=Mecillinam: a new antibiotic for enteric fever |journal=[[BMJ|Br Med J]] |volume=2 |issue=6026 |pages=14–5 |date=July 1976 |pmid=820402 |pmc=1687648 |doi= 10.1136/bmj.2.6026.14|url=}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Geddes AM, Clarke PD |title=The treatment of enteric fever with mecillinam |doi=  10.1093/jac/3.suppl_b.101|journal=J Antimicrob Chemother |volume=3 Suppl B |issue= |pages=101–2 |date=July 1977 |pmid=408321|url=}}</ref>。其口服利用率很差，[[匹美西林|匹美西林]]則是針對此缺陷開發出來的。<ref name=POC-IT>{{cite web |url=http://prod.hopkins-abxguide.org/antibiotics/antibacterial/pcn_others/amdinocillin__mecillinam_.html |title=Amdinocillin (Mecillinam) |vauthors=Pham P, Bartlett JG |date=August 28, 2008 |work=Point-of-Care Information Technology ABX Guide |publisher=[[Johns_Hopkins_University|Johns Hopkins University]]}} Retrieved on August 31, 2008. Freely available with registration.</ref>

這種藥物是荷蘭製藥公司“Leo Pharmaceutical Products”（今[[利奥制药|利奥制药]]）研發出來的，最初代號為FL 1060，1972年面世。<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Lund F, Tybring L |title=6β-amidinopenicillanic acids—a new group of antibiotics |journal=Nature New Biology |volume=236 |issue=66 |pages=135–7 |date=April 1972 |pmid=4402006 |doi=10.1038/236135c0}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Tybring L, Melchior NH |title=Mecillinam (FL 1060), a 6β-Amidinopenicillanic Acid Derivative: Bactericidal Action and Synergy In Vitro |journal=[[Antimicrobial_Agents_and_Chemotherapy|Antimicrob Agents Chemother]] |volume=8 |issue=3 |pages=271–6 |date=September 1975 |pmid=170856 |pmc=429305 |doi= 10.1128/aac.8.3.271|url=http://aac.asm.org/cgi/pmidlookup?view=long&pmid=170856}}</ref>

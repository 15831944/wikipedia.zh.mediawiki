'''美國維珍鐵路'''（{{lang-en|Virgin Trains USA}}），前稱'''光明綫'''（{{lang-en|Brightline}}）是[[美國|美國]][[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]]一條{{tsl|en|higher-speed rail|準高速鐵路}}系統。此系統由“All Aboard Florida”發展，“All Aboard Florida”是{{tsl|en|Florida East Coast Industries|佛羅里達州東岸工業}}（{{lang-en|Florida East Coast Industries}}，縮寫FECI）的全資子公司。<ref name=FECFacts1>{{cite web|url=https://www.fra.dot.gov/Page/P0672|title=All Aboard Florida - Miami to Orlando Passenger Rail Service|website=Federal Railroad Authority|accessdate=17 February 2015}}</ref>2018年11月，[[维珍集团|维珍集团]]入股光明綫，同时该系统将更名为'''美国维珍铁路'''<ref>{{cite web |last1=Branson |first1=Richard |title=Introducing Virgin Trains USA |url=https://www.virgin.com/richard-branson/introducing-virgin-trains-usa |website=Virgin |accessdate=16 November 2018 |language=en |date=16 November 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.railwaygazette.com/news/single-view/view/virgin-trains-usa-formed-by-virgin-group-and-brightline.html |title=Virgin Trains USA formed by Virgin Group and Brightline|publisher=Railway Gazette International|date=2018-11-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=维珍航程公司将在迈阿密港口建新邮轮码头|url=http://www.epochtimes.com/gb/18/12/1/n10885754.htm|publisher=大纪元|date=2018-12-02}}</ref>。

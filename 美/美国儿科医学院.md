'''美國小兒科工會'''（{{lang-en|'''American College of Pediatricians'''}}，简称{{lang-en|'''ACPeds'''}}）是由約五百名提倡{{tsl|en|social conservatism in the United States|socially conservative|保守勢力}}復興的[[小兒科|小兒科醫-{zh-tw:師; zh-cn:生}-]]所組成的工会。<ref>{{cite|title=The Trouble with Amicus Facts|last=Larsen|first=Allison Orr|year=2014|journal=Virginia Law Review|volume=100|issue=8|page=1762|url=http://www.virginialawreview.org/sites/virginialawreview.org/files/Larsen_Book.pdf}}</ref> 

该會創立於2002年，創始者都來自[[美國小兒科學會|美國小兒科學會]]，因為無法接受[[美國小兒科學會|美國小兒科學會]]支持[[LGBT收養|LGBT收養]]的缘故，因此決定脫離原學會，轉而創立该學院，以示抗議。<ref name="Pinto">{{cite news|last=Pinto |first=Nick |title=University of Minnesota professor's research hijacked |url=http://www.citypages.com/news/university-of-minnesota-professors-research-hijacked-6725473 |accessdate=2010-11-17 |work={{tsl|en|City Pages||City Pages}} |date=2010-05-26 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101117131203/http://www.citypages.com/2010-05-26/news/university-of-minnesota-professor-s-research-hijacked |archivedate=2010-11-17 |deadurl=no }}</ref><ref name="Kranish">{{cite news | first = Michael | last = Kranish | title = Beliefs drive research agenda of new think tanks | url = http://www.boston.com/news/nation/washington/articles/2005/07/31/beliefs_drive_research_agenda_of_new_think_tanks | work = Boston Globe | date = 2005-07-31 | accessdate = 2007-10-21}}</ref> 

截至2016年為止，此學院的會員人數約為500人。<ref name="Beale">{{cite news|last=Beale |first=Stephen |title=Lawsuits Challenge Federal 'Transgender Mandate' |url=http://www.ncregister.com/daily-news/lawsuits-challenge-federal-transgender-mandate|accessdate=2016-11-14 |work=NC Register |date=2016-11-11}}</ref>

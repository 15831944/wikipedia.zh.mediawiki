'''美源伯根公司'''（{{lang-en|AmerisourceBergen Corporation}}，{{nyse|ABC}}）是一家美国药品[[批發|批發]]公司，由美源健康和伯根布伦斯维格于2001年合并而成<ref name="AmerisourceBergen-Aug-2001-8-K">{{cite web |url=http://edgar.secdatabase.com/607/94787101500695/filing-main.htm |title=AmerisourceBergen, Form 8-K, Current Report, Filing Date Aug 30, 2001 |publisher=secdatabase.com |accessdate=2013-01-07 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130526105446/http://edgar.secdatabase.com/607/94787101500695/filing-main.htm |archivedate=2013-05-26 }}</ref>。公司提供药物销售和相关服务，旨在降低成本并改善患者的治疗效果，分销一系列商标和仿制[[藥品|藥品]]，[[非处方药|非处方药]]（OTC），保健产品和家庭保健用品和设备到遍布[[美国|美国]]各地的各种医疗保健提供商，包括紧急护理[[醫院|醫院]]和卫生系统，独立和连锁零售[[药学|药店]]，邮购设施，医生，诊所和其他备用场所设施以及熟练的护理和辅助生活中心。还为长期护理，工人补偿和特殊药物患者提供药品和药房服务。

美源伯根是药品分销领域的市场领导者，销售和分销的所有药品占全美总量约20％，并在2017年[[财富美国500强|财富美国500强]]名单中排名第11位，年收入超过1000亿美元。<ref name=f5002011>{{cite web|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170611090322/http://fortune.com/fortune500/amerisourcebergen|archivedate=2017-06-11|url=http://fortune.com/fortune500/amerisourcebergen/|title=AmerisourceBergen - Fortune 500 2017|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|work=[[财富美国500强|Fortune 500]] 2015|publisher=[[财富_(杂志)|Fortune]]|accessdate=2016-09-25|deadurl=no}}</ref>就收入而言（2012年为794.9亿美元），该公司是[[宾夕法尼亚州|宾夕法尼亚州]]最大的公司。<ref>AmerisourceBergen: The biggest Pennsylvania company you never heard of, by David Sell, Philadelphia Inquirer, July 15, 2012</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bizjournals.com/philadelphia/news/2013/05/08/seven-area-companies-made-fortune-500.html |title=Eight area companies make Fortune 500 |newspaper={{tsl|en|Philadelphia Business Journal||Philadelphia Business Journal}} |date=2013-05-28 |accessdate=2013-06-24 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130805065432/http://www.bizjournals.com/philadelphia/news/2013/05/08/seven-area-companies-made-fortune-500.html |archivedate=2013-08-05 }}</ref>

==历史==
美源伯根成立于2001年，由美源健康和伯根布伦斯维格而成。<ref>{{cite web|title=AmerisourceBergen Added to S&P 500 index|url=http://www.amerisourcebergen.com/investor/phoenix.zhtml?c=61181&p=irol-newsArticle&ID=203110|accessdate=2017-07-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322190042/http://amerisourcebergen.com/investor/phoenix.zhtml?c=61181&p=irol-newsArticle&ID=203110|archivedate=2016-03-22}}</ref>美源健康和伯根布伦斯维格都是成功的药物分销商，服务于类似的市场，但覆盖地区不同。两家公司合并后，增强了全国影响力，特别是对于独立社区药房，区域零售连锁药店，医院，医生办公室，诊所和其他替代护理机构。

美源伯根在美国拥有26个药品分销中心，在加拿大有9个分销中心，在美国有4个专业分销中心，在美国和英国拥有超过100万平方英尺的包装生产厂。由于专业快递公司世界速递的加入，美源伯根在全球增设了150多家办事处。

美源伯根因为分销过量止痛药给[[西維吉尼亞州|西維吉尼亞州]]导致[[鴉片類藥物氾濫|鴉片類藥物氾濫]]而被州政府起诉。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/health-science/lawsuits-filed-against-drug-distributors-in-west-virginia/2017/03/09/f9e3165e-0501-11e7-b1e9-a05d3c21f7cf_story.html|title=Opioid distributors sued by West Virginia counties hit by drug crisis|last=Higham|first=Scott|date=2017-03-09|work=Washington Post|access-date=2018-02-13|last2=Bernstein|first2=Lenny|language=en-US|issn=0190-8286|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180210131241/https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/health-science/lawsuits-filed-against-drug-distributors-in-west-virginia/2017/03/09/f9e3165e-0501-11e7-b1e9-a05d3c21f7cf_story.html|archivedate=2018-02-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB10001424052702304058404577492853204473294|title=West Virginia Sues Drug Distributors in Pill-Abuse Fight|author=Jon Kamp|date=2012-06-27|work=WSJ|accessdate=2016-01-23|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160226135858/http://www.wsj.com/articles/SB10001424052702304058404577492853204473294|archivedate=2016-02-26}}</ref><ref name="ReferenceB">{{cite web|url=http://www.wvgazette.com/article/20150517/GZ01/150519456/1419|title=Drug firms shipped 40M pain pills a year to WV|work=Charleston Gazette-Mail|accessdate=2016-01-23}}</ref>

2018年2月12日，据报道，[[Walgreens_Boots_Alliance|沃尔格林联合博姿集团]]正在考虑收购该公司。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://fortune.com/2018/02/13/walgreens-amerisourcebergen-buyout-talks/|title=What Is AmerisourceBergen and Why Does Walgreens Want to Buy It?|work=Fortune|access-date=2018-02-13|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180214073411/http://fortune.com/2018/02/13/walgreens-amerisourcebergen-buyout-talks/|archivedate=2018-02-14}}</ref>

==企业结构==
美源伯根旗下四个主要单位：美源伯根药物公司（ABDC），美源伯根专业集团（ABSG），美源伯根咨询服务（ABCS）和世界速递。2016年，[[Walgreens_Boots_Alliance|沃尔格林联合博姿集团]]宣布它将行使购买2270万股美源伯根股票的选择权，从而控制该公司15％的股份。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.dailyherald.com/article/20160318/business/160318688/|agency=Associated Press|access-date=2016-09-25|date=2016-03-18|title=Walgreens Boots buys bigger stake in AmerisourceBergen|first1=George|last1=Leeclaire|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160915155912/http://www.dailyherald.com/article/20160318/business/160318688/|archivedate=2016-09-15}}</ref>

==好邻居药房==
{{tsl|en|Good Neighbor Pharmacy||好邻居药房}}是由3,400多家独立拥有和经营的药店组成的美国零售商合作网络。它与美源伯根建立了业务关系，美源伯根赞助该网络并拥有“Good Neighbor Pharmacy（好邻居药房）”这个名称。好邻居药房是拉斯维加斯举办的年度贸易展览会“Thought Spot”的赞助商。

===ThoughtSpot===
ThoughtSpot是7月在拉斯维加斯举行的为期四天的贸易展，由好邻居药房承办，目标客户限于社区药店和独立药店。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://goodneighborpharmacyevents.com/events.php|website=AmerisourceBergen|title=Events|accessdate=2016-05-11|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160525214839/http://goodneighborpharmacyevents.com/events.php|archivedate=2016-05-25}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.prweb.com/releases/retail-management-rms/thoughtspot/prweb12822943.htm|title=Retail Management Solutions to Attend The AmerisourceBergen ThoughtSpot 2015 Conference and Trade Show|work=PRWeb|access-date=2017-03-24|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170415200628/http://www.prweb.com/releases/retail-management-rms/thoughtspot/prweb12822943.htm|archivedate=2017-04-15}}</ref>参展商遍及整个医疗行业，包括商标药，仿制[[藥品|药]]和[[非处方药|非处方药]]生产商。

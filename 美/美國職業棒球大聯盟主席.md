'''美國職業棒球大聯盟主席'''（{{lang-en|Commissioner of Baseball}}，又稱'''美國職業棒球大聯盟執行長'''，簡稱'''大聯盟主席'''、'''大聯盟執行長'''）為[[美國職業棒球大聯盟|美國職業棒球大聯盟]]與[[美國職業棒球小聯盟|美國職業棒球小聯盟]]的行政首長<ref>Frick, Ford. [https://books.google.com/books?id=crnl1c9JXGAC&pg=PA129&dq=%22Commissioner+of+Baseball%22%2B%22organized+baseball%22&hl=en&ei=u0gbTqyXL8O30AGh7e2WBQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CDEQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=%22Commissioner%20of%20Baseball%22%2B%22organized%20baseball%22&f=false "Statement of Ford Frick – Commissioner of Baseball"]. In Sullivan, Dean A. (2002). ''Late Innings: A Documentary History of Baseball, 1945–1972'', pp. 129–131. University of Nebraska Press. {{ISBN|0-8032-9285-6}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = MLB Executives | publisher = Major League Baseball | year = 2007 | url = http://mlb.mlb.com/mlb/official_info/about_mlb/executives.jsp | accessdate = 2007-06-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web  | title = MLB Executives  | publisher = Major League Baseball  | year = 2007  | url = http://mlb.mlb.com/mlb/official_info/about_mlb/executives.jsp  | accessdate = 2007-06-30}}</ref>。在大聯盟主席的領導之下，其辦公室職責為雇用與維持裁判組織，以及在[[市場營銷|市場營銷]]、勞工與{{le|美國職業棒球大聯盟與電視|Major League Baseball television contracts|電視合約}}當中進行談判。大聯盟主席是由其所有球隊持有者投票產生。目前的大聯盟主席為{{le|羅伯·曼佛瑞|Rob Manfred}}，於2015年1月25日就任。

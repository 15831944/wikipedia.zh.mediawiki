==组织==
===陆军===
{{Main|乌克兰陆军}}

乌克兰[[陆军|陆军]]是乌克兰武装部隊的地面作战[[军种|军种]]。源自1991年12月从[[苏联陆军|苏联陆军]]继承而来的三大军区的部队：[[基辅军区|基辅军区]]、[[喀尔巴阡军区|喀尔巴阡军区]]、[[敖德萨军区|敖德萨军区]]。

===空军===
{{Main|乌克兰空军}}

乌克兰[[空军|空军]]是乌克兰武装部隊的一部分<ref name="Europe">{{Citation |publisher = TANDF online | url = http://www.tandfonline.com/doi/pdf/10.1080/04597222.2011.559835 |title = Military Balance in Europe 2011 |date = March 7, 2011}}</ref>。乌克兰空军指挥部和总部位于[[文尼察|文尼察]]。当1991年[[苏联解体|苏联解体]]后，大量空军[[飞机|飞机]]离开了乌克兰。此后乌克兰空军便[[裁军|裁军]]并升级装备，进行[[现代化|现代化]]改建。但尽管尽了一些努力，其主要装备还是即将淘汰的老式苏製空军飞机。目前该军有36300名军人和187架飞机在役<ref name="AFM">{{cite journal |last=Trendafilovski |first=Vladimir |date=March 2006 |title= Ukrainian Reforms |journal=Air Forces Monthly |issue= 216 |pages= 32–39}}</ref><ref>[[Air_Forces_Monthly|Air Forces Monthly]], December 2007 issue, p. 64.</ref>，但只有大约八十架有起飞作战能力。<ref>http://en.ria.ru/military_news/20140404/189043839/Ukraine-Puts-Mothballed-Mig-29-Fighter-Jets-Back-in-Service.html</ref>且所有的[[洲际弹道导弹|洲际弹道导弹]]和[[战略轰炸机|战略轰炸机]]都已停用（其中一些交付给[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]]）<ref>{{Citation |title = The conventional imbalance & debate on Russian non strategic nuclear weapons |url = http://www.europeanleadershipnetwork.org/the-conventional-imbalance-and-debate-on-russian-non-strategic-nuclear-weapons_462.html |publisher = The European leadership network |deadurl = yes |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141006091727/http://www.europeanleadershipnetwork.org/the-conventional-imbalance-and-debate-on-russian-non-strategic-nuclear-weapons_462.html |archivedate = 2014-10-06 }}</ref>。

'''乌克兰空军'''（{{lang-uk|Повітряні Сили України}}，{{lang-en|Ukrainian Air Force}}）是[[烏克蘭武裝部隊|烏克蘭武裝部隊]]中的[[空軍|空軍]]。<ref name="Europe">{{Citation | publisher = TANDF online | url = http://www.tandfonline.com/doi/pdf/10.1080/04597222.2011.559835 | title = Military Balance in Europe 2011 | date = March 7, 2011}}</ref>[[乌克兰|乌克兰]][[空军|空军]]指挥总部位于[[文尼察|文尼察]]。当1991年[[苏联解体|苏联解体]]后，大量空军[[飞机|飞机]]离开了乌克兰。此后乌克兰空军便[[裁军|裁军]]并升级装备，进行[[现代化|现代化]]改建。但尽管尽了一些努力，其主要装备还是即将淘汰的老式的苏製空军飞机。目前该國空軍有36300名军人以及187架飞机在服役<ref name="AFM">{{cite journal |last=Trendafilovski |first=Vladimir |date=March 2006 |title= Ukrainian Reforms |journal=Air Forces Monthly |issue= 216 |pages= 32–39}}</ref><ref>[[Air_Forces_Monthly|Air Forces Monthly]], December 2007 issue, p. 64.</ref>，但是只有大约八十架有起飞的作战能力。<ref>http://en.ria.ru/military_news/20140404/189043839/Ukraine-Puts-Mothballed-Mig-29-Fighter-Jets-Back-in-Service.html</ref>而且所有的[[洲际弹道导弹|洲际弹道导弹]]以及[[战略轰炸机|战略轰炸机]]都已經停用（其中一些交付给[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]]）。<ref>{{Citation | title = The conventional imbalance & debate on Russian non strategic nuclear weapons | url = http://www.europeanleadershipnetwork.org/the-conventional-imbalance-and-debate-on-russian-non-strategic-nuclear-weapons_462.html | publisher = The European leadership network | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141006091727/http://www.europeanleadershipnetwork.org/the-conventional-imbalance-and-debate-on-russian-non-strategic-nuclear-weapons_462.html | archivedate = 2014-10-06 }}</ref>

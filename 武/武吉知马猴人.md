'''武吉知马猴人'''（英语：{{lang|en|Bukit Timah Monkey Man}},通常简写为'''BTM'''或'''BTMM''',是一种生存于[[新加坡|新加坡]][[武吉知马|武吉知马]]的森林地区的[[神秘生物|神秘生物]]。<ref>{{cite web | title = Listing of Cryptic Primates | publisher = iLovePrimates | url = http://iloveprimates.com/2010/03/listing-of-cryptic-primates/ | date = 2 March 2010 | accessdate = 2010-07-04 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100605083512/http://iloveprimates.com/2010/03/listing-of-cryptic-primates/ | archivedate = 2010年6月5日 }}</ref><ref name="Branson-Trent" /> 这种生物被认为是森林中的原始[[人类|人类]]或者是[[灵长目|灵长目]]动物, 还有人认为是神仙；<ref name="Branson-Trent">{{Cite book | last = Branson-Trent | first = Gregory | title = Creatures Of Myth And Legend: Fairies, Vampires, Werewolves, Dragons And More | publisher = CreateSpace | date = 17 June 2009 | pages = 325 | isbn = 978-1-4486-0731-0 }}</ref> 关于它的存在性历来都有很大的争论。关于武吉知马猴人的资料是稀少而分散的，被广泛认为这是民间传说的产物。[[神秘动物学家|神秘动物学家]]Karl Shuker在他2007年出版的书籍 ''Extraordinary Animals Revisited''中定义了这种生物的长度。<ref>{{Cite book | last = Shuker | first = Karl | authorlink = Karl Shuker | coauthors = Jonathan Downes, Janet Bord | title = Extraordinary Animals Revisited | publisher = CFZ Press | location = Bideford | year = 2007 | pages = 189 | isbn = 1-905723-17-2, 9781905723171 }}</ref>

《[[嗜血法医|嗜血法医]]》第八季于2011年11月18日被[[Showtime电视网|Showtime]]连同第七季一起续订<ref>[http://news.mtime.com/2011/11/20/1475833.html 与第一杀手不见不散《嗜血法医》获两季续订]</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/11/18/dexter-renewed-for-7th-8th-seasons-by-showtime/111173/|title='Dexter' Renewed For 7th & 8th Seasons By Showtime|work=TV by the Numbers|last=Gorman|first=Bill|date=November 18, 2011|accessdate=November 19, 2011}}</ref>，2012年5月执行制片人Sara Colleton表示第八季将是最后一季<ref name="TVGuide">{{cite web | url=http://www.tvguide.com/News/Dexter-Season7-Spoilers-Sara-Colleton-1049195.aspx/ | title=''Dexter'' Season 8 will be its last | publisher=[[TVGuide|TVGuide]] | date=June 26, 2012}}</ref>，并表示一些故事将穿插到第八季<ref>{{cite web|url=http://screenrant.com/dexter-series-finale-season-8-benk-142024/ |title=''Dexter'' Finale Story Arc Begins This Year; Series to End with Season 8 |publisher=Screen Rant |date=December 2, 2011 |accessdate=June 16, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://screenrant.com/dexter-season-7-spoilers-deb-aco-144333/all/1/ |title=''Dexter'' Season 7 Details Revealed |publisher=Screen Rant |date=December 20, 2011 |accessdate=June 16, 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120701010432/http://screenrant.com/dexter-season-7-spoilers-deb-aco-144333/all/1/ |archivedate=2012年7月1日 }}</ref>。2013年1月12日Showtime宣布第八季将在夏季播出，于6月30日起在星期天9pm播出并前导新剧RAY DONOVAN<ref>[http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/01/12/showtime-2013-original-series-premiere-slate-31511/20130112showtime01/ Showtime(R) 2013 Original Series Premiere Slate]</ref>；由于上季的优异表现，Showtime在2013年1月12日的[[电视评论协会|电视评论协会冬季媒体见面会]]表示第八季是否为最后一季还没确定<ref>[http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/01/12/showtime-at-tca-episodes-wont-be-back-until-early-2014-948515/10159/ Showtime at TCA: "Episodes" Won't Be Back Until Early 2014]</ref><ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2013/01/13/newsletter-2013-01-13-tca-winter-cbs-showtime/ 1月13日简讯：电视评论协会冬季媒体见面会专题报道（Showtime、CBS）]</ref>。而在3月6日[[Showtime电视网|Showtime]]的母公司CBS的CEO表示第八季将为最后一季<ref>[http://www.latinospost.com/articles/13753/20130306/dexter-season-8-news-next-will-last-cbs-chief.htm 'Dexter' Season 8 News: Next Season Will Be Its Last, Says CBS Chief]</ref><ref>[http://news.mtime.com/2013/03/07/1508072.html CBS高层透露《嗜血法医》第八季后将告别荧屏]</ref>，最终Showtime电视网于4月19日确定第八季是最后一季<ref name="finalseason">{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2013/04/its-official-dexters-eighth-season-will-be-its-last/|title=It's Official: Dexter's Eighth Season Will Be Its Last|work=Deadline|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|date=April 18, 2013|accessdate=April 19, 2013}}</ref>。

'''肯尼迪悲剧'''（'''Kennedy tragedies'''或'''Kennedy Curse'''），是指美国[[肯尼迪家族|肯尼迪家族]]的一系列悲剧事件。<ref name=WP>{{cite web|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-srv/national/longterm/jfkjr/timeline.htm|title=Kennedy Family Tragedies|publisher=''[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]]''|year=1999|accessdate=2009-08-26}}</ref><ref name=boston>{{cite web|url=http://www.boston.com/news/packages/jfkjr/mcgrory.htm|title=Family overshadowed by a litany of tragedy|publisher=''[[波士顿环球报|波士顿环球报]]''|author=McGrory, Brian|date=1999-07-18|accessdate=2009-08-26}}</ref>

由于这些事件与肯尼迪家族有高度关联，因而在民间有“肯尼迪诅咒”的说法。这种[[都市传说|都市传说]]后来被各种媒体提及。<ref name="WeeklyWorldNews1">{{cite web | url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=CfMDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA12&lpg=PA12&dq=joseph+kennedy+500+jew+visa+Chaim+Gutnick&source=bl&ots=4nNucfvA7e&sig=APxliJ6B-cSXTOqMecdcLAVwRao&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ei=gyucUaKFOPCZiAeuwIFA&ved=0CD8Q6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=joseph%20kennedy%20500%20jew%20visa%20Chaim%20Gutnick&f=false | title=How to lift a family curse | publisher=Weekly World News | accessdate=2013-05-26}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://news.ifeng.com/world/detail_2012_05/18/14625891_0.shtml | title=肯尼迪侄媳上吊自杀 美媒称“肯尼迪诅咒”再发威 | publisher=中国日报网 | work=凤凰网 | accessdate=2012-05-18}}</ref>

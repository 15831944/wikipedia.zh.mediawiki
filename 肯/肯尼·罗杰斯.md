'''肯尼·羅傑斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Kenneth Ray Rogers'''}}，{{bd|1938年|8月21日}}），美國知名歌手、詞曲作者、演員、唱片製作人、企業家，亦是[[鄉村音樂名人堂|鄉村音樂名人堂]]成員<ref name=cmt1304>{{cite web|title=Country Hall of Fame Elects Kenny Rogers, Bobby Bare, Jack Clement|url=http://www.cmt.com/news/country-music/1705310/country-hall-of-fame-elects-kenny-rogers-bobby-bare-jack-clement.jhtml|work=CMT News|publisher=cmt.com|accessdate=April 13, 2013|author=CMT.com Staff|date=April 10, 2013}}</ref>，累計唱片銷量超過1億張，是其中一個[[畅销音乐艺人列表|世界最暢銷的藝人]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.dailytownsman.com/breaking_news/262386041.html|title=Kenny Rogers touches down in Cranbrook this week|publisher=[[Cranbrook_Daily_Townsman|Cranbrook Daily Townsman]]|date=June 9, 2014|last=Coulter|first=Barry|accessdate=June 11, 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140714145334/http://www.dailytownsman.com/breaking_news/262386041.html|archivedate=July 14, 2014|df=mdy-all}}</ref>。

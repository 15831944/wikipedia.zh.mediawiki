'''亿康先达'''（{{lang-de|'''Egon Zehnder'''}}）是一家瑞士管理咨询和猎头公司。

==历史==
1953年创建于瑞士[[苏黎世|苏黎世]]，目前在40个国家地区拥有68个办事处，有超过450位咨询师和2600位员工。<ref name="WSJ2014October19GuWei">{{cite news|last1=Gu|first1=Wei|title=Executive Talent Search Focuses on Adaptive Leadership; Egon Zehnder Chairman Says Today's Workers Shun the Authoritative Approach|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/boss-talk-asia-executive-talent-search-focuses-on-adaptive-leadership-1413751711|accessdate=3 January 2017|agency=Dow Jones & Company Inc|publisher=Wall Street Journal|date=19 October 2014|location=New York, N.Y., United States|language=en}}</ref><ref name="TheTimesOfIndia2014June23SinghNamrata">{{cite news|last1=Singh|first1=Namrata|title=Staffing Companies Join Search for Women Leaders|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/india-business/Staffing-companies-join-search-for-women-leaders/articleshow/37050971.cms|accessdate=5 December 2016|agency=TNN|issue=|publisher=The Times of India|date=23 June 2014|location=India|language=en}}</ref>

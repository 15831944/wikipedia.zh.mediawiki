'''扁钉螺属'''（学名：''{{lang|la|Clenchiella}}''）是{{tsl|fr|Clenchiellidae|扁钉螺科}}一個[[腹足纲|腹足纲]][[软体动物|软体动物]]的[[属|属]]。本屬的[[模式種|模式種]]為''[[Clenchiella_victoriae|Clenchiella victoriae]]''{{r|WoRMS_722761}}；舊屬[[中腹足目|中腹足目]][[钉螺科|钉螺科]]{{r|shell_3491}}，今屬[[玉黍螺類|玉黍螺類]]支序{{r|IUCN 2012}}。

==物種==
{{as of|2017|9|28}}，[[WoRMS|WoRMS]]紀錄本屬包括物種如下{{r|WoRMS_722761}}：
* ''[[Clenchiella_bicingulata|Clenchiella bicingulata]]'' {{small|Ponder, Fukuda & Hallan, 2014}}<ref name=Ponder/>
* ''[[Clenchiella_iriomotensis|Clenchiella iriomotensis]]'' {{small|Ponder, Fukuda & Hallan, 2014}}<ref name=Ponder/>
* ''[[Clenchiella_minutissima|Clenchiella minutissima]]'' {{small|(Wattebled, 1884)}}
* ''[[Clenchiella_varicosa|Clenchiella varicosa]]'' {{small|Ponder, Fukuda & Hallan, 2014}}<ref name=Ponder/>
* ''[[Clenchiella_victoriae|Clenchiella victoriae]]'' {{small|Abbott, 1948}}
;異名
* [[微小扁钉螺|微小扁钉螺]] ''[[Clenchiella_microscopica|Clenchiella microscopica]]''{{r|shell_3491}}
* ''[[Clenchiella_papuensis|Clenchiella papuensis]]'' {{small|van Benthem Jutting, 1963}}：今作''[[Clenchiella_minutissima|Clenchiella minutissima]]'' {{small|(Wattebled, 1884)}}
* ''[[Clenchiella_sentaniensis|Clenchiella sentaniensis]]'' {{small|van Benthem Jutting, 1963}}：今作''[[Coleglabra_sentaniensis|Coleglabra sentaniensis]]'' {{small|(van Benthem Jutting, 1963)}}（原來組合）

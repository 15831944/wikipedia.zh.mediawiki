'''社会正义战士'''（{{lang-en|Social justice warrior}}，通常简称为{{lang-en|'''SJW'''}}）是一个[[贬义|贬义]]词语，指支持[[女性主義|女性主義]]<ref name=WashingtonPost /><ref name="Eric Johnson 2016"/>、[[民权|民权]]<ref name=WashingtonPost />、[[多元文化主義|多元文化主義]]<ref name=WashingtonPost />和[[身份認同政治|身份認同政治]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/billy-corgan-compares-social-justice-warriors-to-cults-maoists-kkk-20160419 |title=Billy Corgan Compares 'Social Justice Warriors' to Cults, Maoists, KKK |work=Rolling Stone |date=2016-04-19|author=Jon Blistein|language=en}}</ref>等[[左派|左派]][[进步主义|进步主义]]观点的个人。<ref name=WashingtonPost /> 指责一个人为社会正义战士带有指责其寻求主观验证而非任何深层次的信念的意味<ref name=heronbelford/>，并且借助参与虚伪的社会正义讨论或[[行动主义|行动主义]]以提高个人声誉<ref name=ViceAllegra />。在[[漢語|漢語]]地區也存有相近詞語，例如台灣的「[[正義魔人|正義魔人]]」、香港的「[[鍵盤戰士|鍵盤戰士]]」、以及中國大陸的「[[鍵盤俠|鍵盤俠]]」。

该词汇作为一个中性或正面的术语起源于20世纪后期，指投身[[社会正义|社会正义]]运动的人们<ref name=WashingtonPost />。2011年，这一词汇第一次出现在[[Twitter|Twitter]]上时，含义从最初的正面转变为压倒性的负面<ref name=WashingtonPost />。在[[玩家门|玩家门争议]]中，愈來愈多的人將該詞作為負面詞彙來使用，並特別使用在針對那些支持[[社会自由主义|社会自由主义]]、文化包容性、女性主義以及其他[[政治正确|政治正确]]觀點的激進左派人士。<ref name="parreno" /><ref name="technologytell" /><ref name="breiner" />

現在此术语已进入[[流行文化|流行文化]]，例如2014年发行的[[戲仿|戲仿]][[角色扮演游戏|角色扮演游戏]]“社会正义战士”。<ref name="parreno" /><ref name="technologytell" /><ref name="breiner" />

== 起源 ==
{{details|社会正义}}
1824年时“社会正义”是指社会层面的公正。<ref>{{citation|work=[[牛津英語詞典|牛津英語詞典]]|date=September 2005|subscription=yes|title=social justice|url=http://oed.com/search?searchType=dictionary&q=social+justice|edition=3rd|author=[[Oxford_University_Press|Oxford University Press]]}}</ref> Abby Ohlheiser 在华盛顿邮报中写到“社会正义战士”在过去曾被用作颂扬性的短语，并提供了一个可以追溯到1991年的例子。<ref name=WashingtonPost>{{citation|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/the-intersect/wp/2015/10/07/why-social-justice-warrior-a-gamergate-insult-is-now-a-dictionary-entry|title=Why 'social justice warrior,' a Gamergate insult, is now a dictionary entry |author=Abby Ohlheiser|date=2015-10-07|work=[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]] |accessdate=2018-06-07|language=en|dead-url=no|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180606183617/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/the-intersect/wp/2015/10/07/why-social-justice-warrior-a-gamergate-insult-is-now-a-dictionary-entry/|archive-date=2018-06-06}}</ref> 她引用牛津大学出版社的美国字典负责人马丁·凯瑟琳说过的话：“所有我见过的直到最近的用例都是在恭维某人”。<ref name=WashingtonPost/> 华盛顿邮报认为，从20世纪90年代到21世纪该短语一直被以正面的态度来使用。<ref name=WashingtonPost /> 在2015年10月这篇文章出版时，马丁说对于该短语“词典编纂者没有进行对其最早引用的完整研究”。<ref name=WashingtonPost /> [[音樂電視網|音樂電視網]]的 Kristina Marusic 指出，早在该词被以消极方式使用前，“社会正义战士”被用来指[[圣雄甘地|圣雄甘地]]和[[马丁·路德·金|马丁·路德·金]]来描述他们为实现社会正义作出的努力。<ref name="marusic">{{citation|first=Kristina|last=Marusic |url=http://www.mtv.com/news/2087859/trolls-sjw-laci-green/|publisher=MTV|title=Sorry Trolls, You're Not Going to Win the Battle Against Social Justice Warriors|date=2015-02-23 |archivedate=2016-03-18|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160318121441/http://www.mtv.com/news/2087859/trolls-sjw-laci-green/|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 贬义使用 ==
根據凯瑟琳·马丁的說法，该短语的涵义从2011年左右从基本正面转向压倒性的负面，当时它在[[Twitter|Twitter]]上首次被用作一种侮辱性詞彙。<ref name=WashingtonPost /> 同年[[城市词典|城市词典]]中也出现该词汇。<ref name=WashingtonPost /> 该词在[[玩家门|玩家门争议]]中，變得以負面的涵義來使用為主，<ref name="sarahjeong">{{citation|first=Sarah|last=Jeong|author-link=Sarah Jeong|year=2015|title=The Internet of Garbage|publisher=[[Forbes|Forbes Media]]}}</ref> 玩家门的支持者喜欢用该词来描述其观点的反对者。<ref name=WashingtonPost /> 在[[互联网|互联网]]和[[电玩游戏文化|电玩游戏文化]]中，该短语与玩家门争议和更宽泛的文化战争后果具有广泛的联系，其中包括2015年影响了[[雨果奖|雨果奖]]的悲伤小狗运动。<ref name="Eric Johnson 2016">{{citation|language=en|quote=A Social Justice Warrior, or SJW, is any person, female or male, who argues online for political correctness or feminism. 'Social justice' may sound like a good thing to many of our readers, but the people who use this term only use it pejoratively.|url=https://www.recode.net/2014/10/10/11631790/understanding-the-jargon-of-gamergate|title=Understanding the Jargon of Gamergate|date=2014-10-10|work=Recode |last=Johnson |first=Eric |archivedate=2016-01-02|dead-url=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160102031743/http://recode.net/2014/10/10/understanding-the-jargon-of-gamergate/}}</ref><ref name=ViceAllegra>{{citation|title=Meet the Female Gamer Mascot Born of Anti-Feminist Internet Drama|date=2014-08-28|last=Ringo|first=Allegra|work=[[Vice_(magazine)|Vice]]|url=https://www.vice.com/en_us/article/exmwzp/meet-the-female-gamer-mascot-created-by-anti-feminists-828|accessdate=March 22, 2016|language=en|archivedate=2016-01-14|dead-url=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160114083321/http://www.vice.com/read/meet-the-female-gamer-mascot-created-by-anti-feminists-828|quote=In other words, SJWs don't hold strong principles, but they pretend to. The problem is, that's not a real category of people. It's simply a way to dismiss anyone who brings up social justice—and often those people are feminists. It's awfully convenient to have a term at the ready to dismiss women who bring up sexism, as in, 'You don't really care. As an SJW, you're just taking up this cause to make yourself look good!'}}</ref><ref>{{citation|first=Donald|last=Clarke|accessdate=March 22, 2016|archivedate=January 2, 2016|dead-url=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160102031743/http://www.irishtimes.com/news/technology/game-reviews/are-gamers-misogynistic-some-certainly-are-1.1968159|work=[[The_Irish_Times|The Irish Times]]|url=http://www.irishtimes.com/news/technology/game-reviews/are-gamers-misogynistic-some-certainly-are-1.1968159|date=October 18, 2014|title=Gamers Misogynistic? Some Certainly Are|quote=The term "social justice warrior" GamerGate: A Closer Look At The Controversy Sweeping Video Games (surely a good thing) has been used pejoratively to describe those writers who choose to examine the social and political subtexts of contemporary video games}}</ref><ref name="WaPoGamergate">{{citation|first=Caitlin|last=Dewey|work=[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]]|title=The Only Guide to Gamergate You Will Ever Need to Read|date=October 14, 2014|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/news/the-intersect/wp/2014/10/14/the-only-guide-to-gamergate-you-will-ever-need-to-read/|quote=...'SJW,' for social justice warrior—a kind of shorthand insult for liberals and progressives.|accessdate=April 22, 2015}}</ref><ref name=SlateWaldman>{{citation|url=http://www.slate.com/blogs/browbeat/2015/04/08/_2015_hugo_awards_how_the_sad_and_rabid_puppies_took_over_the_sci_fi_nominations.html|title=2015 Hugo Awards: How the sad and rabid puppies took over the sci-fi nominations|first=Katy |last=Waldman|date=April 8, 2015|work=[[Slate_(magazine)|Slate]]|accessdate=March 22, 2016|archivedate=January 14, 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160114234926/http://www.slate.com/blogs/browbeat/2015/04/08/_2015_hugo_awards_how_the_sad_and_rabid_puppies_took_over_the_sci_fi_nominations.html}}</ref> 该词的贬义用法流行于网站[[Reddit|Reddit]]和[[4chan|4chan]]。<ref name="maxhill">{{citation|last=Hill|first=Max|date=November 17, 2014|url=http://www.the-peak.ca/2014/11/in-defence-of-social-justice-warriors/ |title=In defence of 'social justice warriors' |work=The Peak |accessdate=March 22, 2016 |dead-url=no|archivedate=March 17, 2016|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160317005411/http://www.the-peak.ca/2014/11/in-defence-of-social-justice-warriors/}}</ref>

使用该词被描述为试图贬低成为SJW的人的动机，暗示其动机是“主观验证而非出于任何深层的信念。”<ref name="heronbelford">{{cite journal |last1=Heron|first1=Michael James|first2=Pauline |last2=Belford|first3=Ayse|last3=Goker|title=Sexism in the circuitry: female participation in male-dominated popular computer culture |journal=ACM SIGCAS Computers and Society |year=2014 |volume=44 |issue=4 |pages=18–29 |doi=10.1145/2695577.2695582 <!--|accessdate=March 22, 2016-->}}</ref>

负面含义特别针对那些[[进步主义|进步主义]]、文化包容性或[[女性主義|女性主義]]观点的信奉者。<ref name=WashingtonPost /><ref name="Eric Johnson 2016"/>根據''[[Vice_(magazine)|Vice]]'' 杂志的說法，称某人是社會正義戰士的指責暗示了他虛偽地參與社會正義的論述或行動以提高自己的個人聲望。<ref name=ViceAllegra /> Vice 杂志观察到：“这个术语可以非常方便地用来反驳提到性别歧视的女性。”<ref name=ViceAllegra /> 该杂志这样评价该术语的使用：“问题是，那不是真正存在的一类人。它只是一种反驳任何提到社会正义的人的方法——而且那些人经常是女性主義者。”<ref name=ViceAllegra /> David French认为，社会正义战士们的目标被[[基督教右派|基督教保守主义者]]所反对。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nationalreview.com/2015/11/religious-zealots-campus-social-justice-warriors/|title=The Ferocious Religious Faith of the Campus Social-Justice Warrior|website=National Review|language=en|date=2015-11-23|access-date=2018-06-07|author=David French|dead-url=no|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180606182943/https://www.nationalreview.com/2015/11/religious-zealots-campus-social-justice-warriors/|archive-date=2018-06-06}}</ref>

{{quote box |width=25em | bgcolor=#c6dbf7 |align=right |halig=left | salign=right|quote=“‘社会正义战士’，即认为女性主義者非理性、道貌岸然、抱有偏见、自我夸耀的刻板印象。”|source= ''新文学史''<ref name="selisker" />}}
该术语通常被网上讨论的参与者用于对女性主義的批评。<ref name="selisker">{{citation|issn=0028-6087|oclc=1296558|doi=10.1353/nlh.2015.0024|last=Selisker|first=Scott|title=The Bechdel Test and the Social Form of Character Networks|publisher=[[Johns_Hopkins_University_Press|Johns Hopkins University Press]]|journal=[[New_Literary_History|New Literary History]] |volume=46|issue=3|year=2015|pages=505–523}}</ref> 《新文学史》期刊中的一篇文章描述了他们的网上行为模式：“他们往往将对所见事物的个人批评当作一种类型：‘社会正义战士’，即认为女性主義者非理性、道貌岸然、抱有偏见、自我夸耀的刻板印象。”<ref name="selisker" />

在2015年8月，贬义词“社会正义战士”和另外几个词语被加入牛津词典。<ref name=WashingtonPost/><ref name="canyouusethat">{{cite web |url=http://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2015/08/27/435232388/can-you-use-that-in-a-sentence-dictionary-adds-new-words |publisher=[[全国公共广播电台|美国全国公共广播电台]] |title=Can You Use That In A Sentence? Dictionary Adds New Words |date=August 27, 2015 |first=Laura |last=Wagner |accessdate=March 22, 2016 |archivedate=March 20, 2016|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160320210735/http://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2015/08/27/435232388/can-you-use-that-in-a-sentence-dictionary-adds-new-words|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="timeoxford">{{citation|url=http://time.com/4010748/oxford-dictionary-update-2015/ |work=Time |title=Oxford Dictionaries Adds 'Fat-Shame,' 'Butthurt' and 'Redditor' |first=Katy |last=Steinmetz |date=August 26, 2015 |accessdate=March 22, 2016 |archivedate=January 20, 2016|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160120185422/http://time.com/4010748/oxford-dictionary-update-2015/|dead-url=no}}</ref> 在对该词词源的讨论中，马丁概述了该词和“政治正确”一词贬义使用的相似性，认为相似性在于都是用来诋毁，他指出“已知的（进步主义政治）的正统观念激起了那些认为自己言论受到管制的人的反弹。”<ref name=WashingtonPost/>

== 在流行文化中 ==
2014年5月，这一概念被纳入一个[[戲仿|戲仿]][[角色扮演游戏|角色扮演游戏]]社会正义战士。<ref name="parreno">{{citation|first=Ryan|last=Parreno |url=http://gameranx.com/updates/id/24055/article/social-justice-warriors-now-have-their-own-rpg/ |title=Social Justice Warriors Now Have Their Own RPG |date=2014-09-09|language=en|work=Gameranx|archivedate=2015-01-03|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150103021359/http://www.gameranx.com/updates/id/24055/article/social-justice-warriors-now-have-their-own-rpg|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="technologytell">{{citation|url=http://www.technologytell.com/gaming/144852/social-justice-warrior-review-meh/ |title=Social Justice Warrior Review: Meh |first=Elf |last=Princess |language=en|work=TechnologyTell |date=2015-03-30|archivedate=2016-01-03|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160103130249/http://www.technologytell.com/gaming/144852/social-justice-warrior-review-meh/|dead-url=yes}}</ref>社会正义战士游戏由 Nonadecimal Creative 开发，包括参与网路辩论对抗作出[[种族主义|种族主义]]或其他挑衅性评论的网路[[白目|山怪]]，对抗方式是选择不同的反应，如“‘用你的逻辑驳倒他们的言论，反复发送他们的信息招来他人攻击，或进行人身攻击。”<ref name="technologytell"  /> 用户可以选择角色类别；游戏过程涉及用户理智和声誉的变化。<ref name="technologytell" /> 这个游戏2015年2月在计算机平台[[Steam|Steam]]上发售。<ref name="breiner">{{citation|first=Andrew|last=Breiner |url=https://thinkprogress.org/dont-feed-the-trolls-fight-them-9511d9def046/|title=Don't Feed The Trolls, Fight Them|date=2015-03-13|work=[[ThinkProgress|ThinkProgress]]|language=en|archive-date=2018-06-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180606185946/https://thinkprogress.org/dont-feed-the-trolls-fight-them-9511d9def046/|dead-url=no}}</ref> 

游戏创作者埃里克·福特解释说，这个游戏的目的是培养[[批判性思维|批判性思维]]，没有“意在表明种族主义、性别歧视或其他攻击性的评论不应该出现在网上。我们的目标是鼓励批判性思维，考虑对于现实世界中的社会正义战士而言这件事可以如何更有效和低成本地完成。”<ref name="breiner" /> 他评论道：“一旦你走上纠正网路上陌生人的每个不正确言论的道路，唯一的必然结果会是，你的耐心被挫折所耗尽，你的声誉被山怪的诽谤或自己的行为毁掉，或者你会因厌恶而放弃。”<ref name="breiner" />

女星凯特琳·巴洛描述她在2016年美国喜剧《教师》中的角色为社会正义战士。<ref name="ninametz" /> 巴洛解释说：“我扮演Cecilia Cannon，她是超级脆弱的嬉皮社会正义战士，总是试图拯救这个世界，无论人们关心与否。而且她总是将她的左翼观点推广给她的学生们。”<ref name="ninametz">{{citation|first=Nina|last=Metz |url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/tv/ct-katydids-teachers-tv-land-ent-0127-20160126-column.html|work=[[芝加哥論壇報|芝加哥論壇報]]|date=January 26, 2016|title=Stars of twisted new sitcom 'Teachers' bring laughs home from school |accessdate=March 22, 2016 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160312113127/http://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/tv/ct-katydids-teachers-tv-land-ent-0127-20160126-column.html|archivedate=March 12, 2016|dead-url=no}}</ref>

[[荷里活報道|荷里活報道]]的记者莱斯利·戈德堡和凯特·斯坦霍普在2016年3月提到女星 Isabella Gomez 在[[Netflix|Netflix]]翻拍的 One Day at a Time 中扮演艾琳娜，一个自我认同为社会正义战士的角色。<ref name="hollywoodreporter" /> 戈德堡和斯坦霍普写道：“一个骄傲的书呆子、理想主义者和社会正义战士，艾琳娜固执己见，不害怕说出自己的想法。”<ref name="hollywoodreporter">{{citation|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/netflixs-one-day-at-a-876502 |work=[[好莱坞报道|好莱坞报道]]|first1=Lesley|last1=Goldberg|first2=Kate|last2=Stanhope|date=March 17, 2016|title=Netflix's 'One Day at a Time' Remake Adds 'Matador' Actress |accessdate=March 22, 2016 |dead-url=no|archivedate=March 22, 2016|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322010955/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/netflixs-one-day-at-a-876502}}</ref>

[[艾利·羅斯|艾利·羅斯]]在推广他的电影[[食人煉獄|食人煉獄]]时说：“我想写一部关于现代行动主义的电影。我看到很多人想要关注和帮忙，但总的来说我觉得人们真的不希望使自己的生活不便。我看到很多人只是在社交媒体上作出反应。这些社会正义战士。‘这是错的，这是错的，这是错的。’而他们只是发推特和转推。他们实际上没有做任何事。或者你看到有人涉足他们真的不是非常了解而又为之疯狂的事。我要拍一部像那样的人们的电影。”<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/herocomplex/la-et-hc-eli-roth-green-inferno-sjw-20150709-story.html|title=Eli Roth's 'Green inferno' devours the Internet's 'social justice warriors'|first=Meredith|last=Woerner|date=July 9, 2015|work=[[洛杉磯時報|洛杉磯時報]]}}</ref>

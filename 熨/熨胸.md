Retrieved 2011-04-02.</ref>，一般由女孩母亲施行，目的是防止[[性骚扰|性骚扰]]、[[性侵犯|性侵犯]]<ref name=Millions/>、[[早孕|早孕]]<ref name=IPS>{{cite news | url=http://www.ipsnews.net/2006/06/rights-cameroon-an-unwelcome-gift-of-god/ | title=An Unwelcome 'Gift of God' | publisher=[[Inter_Press_Service|IPS News]] |author=Sylvestre Tetchiada |date=13 June 2006 |accessdate=2008-01-02}}</ref>或推后结婚等<ref name=BBC/><ref name=IPS/>。该习俗主要流行于西非的[[喀麦隆|喀麦隆]]，当地男人认为女性[[乳房发育|乳房发育]]是[[性成熟|性成熟]]的标志<ref name=BBC/>。

'''伞花铁心木'''（[[學名|學名]]：''{{lang|la|Metrosideros umbellata}}''），又名'''南方鐵心木'''，是[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]][[特有種|特有的]]十一種[[鐵心木屬|鐵心木]]的其中一種。能長至15[[米_(單位)|米]]高及樹幹可達1米闊。<ref>{{cite web |title=''Metrosideros umbellata''|work=Flora of New Zealand |url= http://floraseries.landcareresearch.co.nz/pages/Taxon.aspx?id=_42b6cbd7-e582-4f4d-ba21-7d69070266dd&fileName=Flora%201.xml|accessdate=2007-06-09 }}</ref>與其同屬物種一樣，能長出鮮艷的紅花；但與其近似種[[粗狀鐵心木|粗狀鐵心木]]不同，此種甚少以[[附生植物|附生植物]]形式生長。

==描述==
傘花鐵心木的花一般呈鮮紅色，也有白花及黃花，雄蕊約2厘米長。花期通常在12月至2月期間，取決於當地的環境條件。葉長由3到6厘米長不等，前緣尖銳。木質堅硬緻密。

樹皮是粗糙的和呈片狀，並為如''[[Astelia|Astelia]]''物種和[[Freycinetia_banksii|Freycinetia banksii]]（Kiekie）等附生植物提供一個理想的分層結構作其根部。傘花鐵心木是南島西海岸線花蜜的主要來源。 [[卡卡|卡卡]] [[tui|TUI]]和[[新西蘭鐘聲鳥|鈴鳥]]就因其豐富的花蜜而時常造訪。

==分佈==
[[Image:Southern-Rata-Auckland-Islands.jpg|thumb]]50度奧克蘭群島上的傘花鐵心木，也是此種所能生長的最南的極限]]
多在降雨量大而清爽的地區生長，是南島西岸重要的原生鐵心木花蜜的重要來源。<ref>*{{cite web |title=''Metrosideros umbellata''|work=New Zealand Plant Conservation Network |url= http://www.nzpcn.org.nz/flora_details.asp?ID=978|accessdate=2010-10-02 }}</ref>
此種是最廣泛分佈的新西蘭鐵心木品種。從[[北島_(紐西蘭)|北島_(紐西蘭)]]的南緯36度以南開始，在南島西部變得常見，但較少在東部出現。在[[斯圖爾特島|斯圖爾特島]]（47ºS）上分普遍，並在[[奧克蘭群島|奧克蘭群島]]在那裡達到其50ºS的南限。

==保育==
Although Southern rātā is not regarded as threatened, it is rather uncommon in the North Island, and in certain areas it is threatened by possum browse. In the Tararua range, Southern rātā populations appear to have been replaced by a population consisting of hybrids between Northern and Southern rātā.

[[Project_Crimson|Project Crimson]] is a charitable trust that promotes conservation of rata as well as the related [[pohutukawa|pohutukawa]].

==種植==
Southern rātā is a beautiful specimen tree, but growth can be slow unless it is grown in ideal conditions with moist soil. It is easily grown from fresh seed. While it is possible to grow the tree from softwood or semi-hardwood cuttings, these often prove reluctant to strike roots. It is ideal for coastal environments because it has a good resistance to wind and salt.<ref name=GCNursery>{{cite web|url=http://www.gcnursery.co.uk/gondwana.html |title=Gondwanan Plants What Are They and Why Grow Them in Britain? |publisher=Garden Cottage Nursery |accessdate=2009-06-18 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090429071738/http://www.gcnursery.co.uk/gondwana.html |archivedate=2009-04-29 }}</ref>

'''''律政巨人（Goliath）''''' (先前劇名為''審判'') 是美國[[Amazon_Studios|Amazon Studios]]專為網路電視所製播的一部法庭劇集。此劇從2015年12月1日開拍第1季的8集<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://deadline.com/2015/12/trial-series-amazon-david-e-kelley-billy-bob-thornton-1201647570/|title=Amazon Confirms David E. Kelley Drama Series ‘Trial’ Starring Billy Bob Thornton |last=Andreeva |first=Nellie|date=October 15, 2015 |publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]] |access-date=October 15, 2016}}</ref> ，並於2016年10月13日在[[Amazon_Video|Amazon Video]]上推出<ref>{{cite web|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|url=http://deadline.com/2015/12/trial-series-amazon-david-e-kelley-billy-bob-thornton-1201647570/|title=Amazon Sets David E. Kelley Drama 'Trial' Starring Billy Bob Thornton|publisher=Deadline.com|date=December 1, 2015|accessdate=April 20, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Schwartz|first1=Ryan|title=Transparent Season 3, High Castle Season 2, Woody Allen Comedy and Others Get Amazon Premiere Dates|url=http://tvline.com/2016/08/07/transparent-season-3-premiere-date-woody-allen-comedy-amazon/|date=August 7, 2016|work=TVLine}}</ref> 。2017年2月15日Amazon宣布將籌拍第2季<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/02/goliath-renewed-season-2-clyde-phillips-showrunner-1201912585/|title=‘Goliath’ Renewed For Season 2 With Clyde Phillips As New Showrunner|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|publisher=Deadline|date=February 15, 2017|accessdate=February 16, 2017}}</ref>。

==劇情簡介==
*[[Billy_Bob_Thornton|Billy Bob Thornton]] 飾演Billy McBride，一位與Donald Cooperman共同創立了Cooperman McBride法律事務所並將其經營的非常成功，但現在卻一貧如洗的律師。Billy有才華且有個人魅力，但在他運用法律專業為一位謀殺嫌疑犯辯護並使其脫身，但該名嫌疑犯卻殺害了全家人後，他被逐出事務所並開始酗酒，棲身在 [[Santa_Monica_Pier|Santa Monica Pier]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|url=http://deadline.com/2015/07/billy-bob-thornton-trial-david-e-kelley-amazon-pilot-1201484439/|title=Billy Bob Thornton To Star In David E. Kelley's Amazon Drama 'Trial'|publisher=Deadline.com|date=July 23, 2015 |accessdate=April 20, 2016}}</ref>（地名）的小旅館裡。
*[[William_Hurt|William Hurt]] 飾演Donald Cooperman，Billy的合夥人，共同創立了Cooperman McBride事務所。他的臉部曾遭火灼留下疤痕，因此鮮少露面，甚至不常離開辦公室，事務所的絕大部分員工都未曾親眼見過他，他則透過監視器來監控會議及開庭情形。他甚至在員工不知情下在整間事務所裝置竊聽器，以便介入事務所內發生的每件事情。他對Billy懷有極深的敵意<ref>{{cite web|first=Elizabeth|last=Wagmeister |authorlink=Elizabeth Wagmeister |url=http://variety.com/2015/tv/news/william-hurt-cast-trial-amazon-series-1201574249/|title='Trial': William Hurt Joins Cast of Amazon Legal Drama from David E. Kelley |work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |date=August 24, 2015 |accessdate=April 20, 2016}}</ref>。

==評價及獎項==
此劇集在[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]網站上獲得普遍的好評，得分為65分（總分100）<ref>{{cite web|title=''Goliath'' - Season 1 Reviews|url=http://www.metacritic.com/tv/goliath|work=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]|publisher=[[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive]]|accessdate=January 9, 2017}}</ref> 。[[Rotten_Tomatoes|Rotten Tomatoes]]網站上35位觀眾中有77%願意推薦，獲得了「Certified Fresh」的評價<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/tv/goliath/s01/|title=''Goliath'': Season 1|work=[[Rotten_Tomatoes|Rotten Tomatoes]]|publisher=[[Fandango_(company)|Fandango]]|accessdate=January 9, 2017}}</ref>。

在[[第74屆金球獎|第74屆金球獎]]上，[[Billy_Bob_Thornton|Billy Bob Thornton]]贏得了[[金球獎電視劇集最佳演員獎|金球獎電視劇集最佳演員獎]]<ref>{{citeweb|url=http://variety.com/2017/tv/news/billy-bob-thornton-wins-golden-globe-for-best-actor-in-drama-tv-series-goliath-1201953795/|title=Billy Bob Thornton Wins Golden Globe for Best Actor in Drama TV Series for ‘Goliath’|first=Elizabeth |last=Wagmeister |work=Variety|date=January 9, 2017 |accessdate=January 9, 2017}}</ref>。

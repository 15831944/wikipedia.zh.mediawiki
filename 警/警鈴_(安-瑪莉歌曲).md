== 創作背景 ==
{{Cquote|"Alarm" is about somebody I was dating and I guess I just always had a suspicion that he was doing something naughty. I’m a bit of an investigator and I found out my inkling was right! The thing is, he left someone to be with me and I always thought he could do it again. I love this song so much and I hope you love it too!|安-瑪莉<ref>{{cite web|url= https://genius.com/Anne-marie-alarm-lyrics|title= 安-瑪莉 - 警鈴 - 歌詞|work= [[AllMusic|AllMusic]]|language= en}}</ref>}}

歌曲是關於她懷疑她的男朋友和其他人在一起，然而她的猜想是正確的。

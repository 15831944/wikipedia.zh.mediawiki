'''里奥·霍华德'''（{{lang-en|'''Leo Howard'''}}，{{bd|1997年|7月13日|||catIdx= H霍华德}}）出生于[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[奥兰治县_(加利福尼亚州)|奥兰治县]][[纽波特比奇_(加利福尼亚州)|纽波特比奇]]，为美国演员，模特，武术家，也是美国著名的童星。

== 生平 ==
1997年，霍华德出生于[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[奥兰治县_(加利福尼亚州)|奥兰治县]][[纽波特比奇_(加利福尼亚州)|纽波特比奇]]，母亲兰德伊，父亲{{tsl|en|Todd Howard|托德·霍华德|-{zh-cn:托德;zh-tw:陶德;}-·侯活}}，<ref name="DisneyXDMedianet">{{cite web|url=http://www.disneyxdmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=3136029&type=howard|title=Leo Howard – "Jack"|accessdate=June 13, 2011|work=DisneyXDMedianet.com|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120407131544/http://www.disneyxdmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=3136029&type=howard|archivedate=四月 7, 2012}}</ref><ref name="North County Times">{{cite web|url=http://www.nctimes.com/news/local/columnists/inperson/article_0b896da7-2d2a-5fab-98aa-6fc2de23f79e.html|title=Fallbrook native is accomplished actor, martial artist|date=June 3, 2011|work=North County Times|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120911102037/http://www.nctimes.com/news/local/columnists/inperson/article_0b896da7-2d2a-5fab-98aa-6fc2de23f79e.html|archivedate=九月 11, 2012}}</ref><ref name="OC Register">{{cite web|url=http://articles.ocregister.com/2011-06-10/news/29651126_1_martial-arts-shorin-ryu-cute-kid|title=Disney series is a kick for young karate expert|date=June 10, 2011|work=OC Register|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110703131546/http://articles.ocregister.com/2011-06-10/news/29651126_1_martial-arts-shorin-ryu-cute-kid|archivedate=七月 3, 2011}}</ref><ref name="SideswipePro.com">{{cite web|url=http://sideswipepro.com/news/|title=Disney XD's 'My Life' with Leo Howard|date=July 19, 2011|work=SideswipePro.com|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110907045256/http://sideswipepro.com/news/|archivedate=九月 7, 2011}}</ref> 
他的双亲都是养殖狗和大型牛头犬的农场主商人，他们善于养殖斗牛犬。<ref name="ToddHoward.net">{{cite web|url=http://toddhoward.net/2-uncategorised/1-todd-howard-big-bulldog|title=Todd Howard – The Big Bulldog|date=February 16, 2011|work=ToddHoward.net|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111111141601/http://toddhoward.net/2-uncategorised/1-todd-howard-big-bulldog|archivedate=十一月 11, 2011}}</ref><ref name="BigBulldogs.com">{{cite web|url=http://bigbulldogs.com/|title=Top Quality English and French Bulldogs|accessdate=October 12, 2011|work=BigBulldogs.com}}</ref>

'''里舍·巴塔尼'''（{{lang-hi|ऋषि भुटानी}}，{{bd|1980年|12月18日}}）是一位印度演员，常出演[[宝莱坞|宝莱坞]]电影<ref>{{cite web | url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Rishi-Bhutani-is-learning-on-the-job/articleshow/5651238.cms | title=Rishi Bhutani is learning on the job | publisher=[[印度时报|Times of India]] | work={{tsl|en|The Times Group||The Times Group}} | date=7 March 2010 | accessdate=26 March 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.thehindu.com/features/cinema/showbitz/article531763.ece | title=Showbitz | publisher=[[印度报|The Hindu]] | work={{tsl|en|The Hindu Group||The Hindu Group}} | date=25 July 2010 | accessdate=26 March 2016 | author=Harshikha, Udasi}}</ref>。他凭借出演惊悚电影《{{tsl|en|Bolo Raam|博洛拉姆}}》获得认可，得到电影评论家{{tsl|en|Taran Adarsh|塔兰·阿达什}}褒扬<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.sify.com/movies/i-bolo-raam-i-is-a-compelling-flick-review-bollywood-pclxy5cbhfgii.html | title=Bolo Raam is a compelling flick | publisher={{tsl|en|Sify||Sify}} | work=Sify desk | date=2009 | accessdate=26 March 2016 | author=Taran, Adarsh}}</ref>，并获得2009年{{tsl|en|Lions Gold Awards}}奖项提名<ref>{{cite web | url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Rishi-Looking-for-a-long-steady-inning/articleshow/5455584.cms | title=Rishi: Looking for a long steady inning | publisher=[[印度时报|Times of India]] | work={{tsl|en|The Times Group||The Times Group}} | date=28 January 2010 | accessdate=26 March 2016}}</ref>。2012年，其出演的短片《LOC... A Playground》于因帕尔国际电影节上获奖<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.filmfestivals.com/blog/imphalfilmfestival/awarded_films_at_the_first_imphal_international_short_film_festival_2012 | title=Awarded Films at the First Imphal International Short Film Festival 2012 | publisher=FilmFestivals.com | date=13 May 2012 | accessdate=26 March 2016}}</ref>。2015年传记电影《{{tsl|en|Jai Jawaan Jai Kisaan (film)|赞扬士兵，赞扬农民 (2015年电影)|赞扬士兵，赞扬农民}}》中，他饰演了[[贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁|贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://archive.indianexpress.com/news/prem-chopra-to-play-pt.-govind-vallabh-pant-in-jai-jawan-jai-kisan/1107505/ | title=Prem Chopra to play Pt. Govind Vallabh Pant in Jai Jawan Jai Kisan | publisher=[[印度快報|The Indian Express]] | work={{tsl|en|Indian Express Limited||Indian Express Limited}} | date=25 April 2013 | accessdate=26 March 2016 | author=Namita, Nivas}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Jai-Jawaan-Jai-Kisaan-now-in-Allahabad/articleshow/20368178.cms | title='Jai Jawaan Jai Kisaan' now in Allahabad | publisher=[[印度时报|Times of India]] | work={{tsl|en|The Times Group||The Times Group}} | date=31 May 2013 | accessdate=28 March 2016 | author=Dasgupta, Piyali}}</ref>。

2015年，巴塔尼被提名为福布斯印度名人百强<ref>{{cite web | url=http://forbesindia.com/article/special/forbes-india-celebrity-100-nominees-list-for-2015/41475/1 | title=Forbes India Celebrity 100 Nominees List for 2015 | publisher=[[福布斯|Forbes]] (India) | date=4 November 2015 | accessdate=26 March 2016}}</ref>。此外，他还在2012年法国维泰勒国际电影节上斩获奖项<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.navhindtimes.in/nagpurs-french-connection/ | title=Nagpur’s French connection | publisher={{tsl|en|The Navhind Times||The Navhind Times}} | work=NT desk | date=24 November 2014 | accessdate=28 March 2016}}</ref>。

ChievoVerona|language=it|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141104195432/http://www.chievoverona.it/it/news/ufficiale-meggiorini-al-chievoverona|archivedate=4 十一月 2014}}</ref>。他也曾代表意大利U19和U20國家隊參賽<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.figc.it/nazionali/DettaglioConvocato?codiceConvocato=2841&squadra=11|title=Convocazioni e presenze in campo|work=figc.it|accessdate=20 May 2014|language=it|publisher=FIGC}}</ref>。他也曾效力於[[國際米蘭|國際米蘭]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.inter.it/aas/news/reader?N=22792&L=it|title= MERCATO: RINNOVATE 15 COMPROPRIETÀ |trans_title=FIFTEEN CO-OWNERSHIP AGREEMENTS RENEWED|date=23 June 2004|accessdate=4 July 2010|work=FC Internazionale Milano|publisher=[http://www.inter.it www.inter.it]|language=it}}</ref>和[[都靈足球俱樂部|都靈足球俱樂部]]<ref>http://au.soccerway.com/players/riccardo-meggiorini/58418/</ref>等球隊。

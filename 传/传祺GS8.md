'''传祺GS8'''是[[广汽集团|广汽集团]]旗下的一款全尺寸[[SUV|SUV]]。GS8在2016年[[北京车展|北京车展]]首次亮相，是传祺旗下尺寸最大的车型。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://carnewschina.com/2016/04/29/guangzhou-auto-trumpchi-gs8-suv-debuts-on-the-beijing-auto-show/|title=Guangzhou Auto Trumpchi GS8 SUV debuts on the Beijing Auto Show}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://carnewschina.com/2016/07/20/guangzhou-auto-trumpchi-gs8-suv-is-ready-for-the-chinese-car-market/|title=Guangzhou Auto Trumpchi GS8 SUV Is Ready For The Chinese Car Market}}</ref>根据传祺GS8所公布的动力系统，其搭载了2.0升[[渦輪增壓|渦輪增壓]]四缸引擎,搭配六缸[[自動變速器|自动变速箱]]。指导价为163800-259800元。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://carnewschina.com/2016/10/27/guangzhou-auto-trumpchi-gs8-hits-the-chinese-car-market/|title=Guangzhou Auto Trumpchi GS8 Hits The Chinese Car Market}}</ref>

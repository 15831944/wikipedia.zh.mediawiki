<!-- Definition and medical uses -->
[[睪酮|睪酮]]是一種[[藥品|藥品]]，也是自然常見的[[類固醇激素|類固醇激素]]。<ref name=AHFS2016/>醫藥上用來治療男性[[性腺功能低下症|性腺功能低下症]]以及特定類型的[[乳癌|乳癌]]，<!-- <ref name=AHFS2016/> -->也用以增強[[體能鍛煉|運動能力]]。<ref name=AHFS2016/>目前尚不清楚在[[更年期#.E7.94.B7.E6.80.A7.E6.9B.B4.E5.B9.B4.E6.9C.9F|男性更年期]]使用睪酮會帶來好處或是壞處。<ref name=FDA2015>{{cite web |author=Staff |title=Testosterone Products: Drug Safety Communication – FDA Cautions About Using Testosterone Products for Low Testosterone Due to Aging; Requires Labeling Change to Inform of Possible Increased Risk of Heart Attack And Stroke |url=http://www.fda.gov/Safety/MedWatch/SafetyInformation/SafetyAlertsforHumanMedicalProducts/ucm436280.htm |date=3 March 2015 |work=[[FDA|FDA]] |accessdate=5 March 2015 }}</ref>睪酮可以於皮膚塗抹、注射入肌肉或是放在臉頰上使用。<ref name=AHFS2016/>

<!-- Side effects -->
常見的副作用包括[[痤疮|痤疮]]、腫脹和[[男性乳房发育症|男性的乳房脹大]]，<!-- <ref name=AHFS2016/> -->嚴重的副作用可能包含{{le|肝毒性|Hepatotoxicity}}、[[心血管疾病|心血管疾病]]和行為上的轉變，<!-- <ref name=AHFS2016/> -->女性與幼兒暴露在睪酮可能引發[[男性化|男性化]]，<!-- <ref name=AHFS2016/> -->那些患有[[前列腺癌|前列腺癌]]不推薦使用此藥物，<!-- <ref name=AHFS2016/> -->[[妊娠|妊娠]]或[[母乳哺育|哺乳]]期間可能會造成危害。<ref name=AHFS2016/>

<!-- Physiological effects -->
睪酮主要由[[雄性|雄性]]的[[睪丸|睪丸]]所製造，<!-- <ref name=Pur2014/> -->少部分在[[腎上腺|腎上腺]]和[[雌性|雌性]]的[[卵巢|卵巢]]。<ref name=Pur2014>{{cite book|last1=Puri|first1=Dinesh|title=Textbook of Medical Biochemistry|date=2014|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9788131238059|page=631|edition=3|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=NijbAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA631|language=en}}</ref>它是雄性主要的[[性类固醇|性类固醇]]並且在[[男性生殖系統|男性生殖系統]]的發育中扮演重要的角色，<!-- <ref name=Hef2010/> -->在成年男性中它促使[[第二性徵|第二性徵]]包括[[肌肉|肌肉]]和體毛的增長。<ref name=Hef2010>{{cite book|last1=Heffner|first1=Linda J.|last2=Schust|first2=Danny J.|title=The Reproductive System at a Glance|date=2010|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=9781405194525|page=17|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=_2yd2b8ybNoC&pg=PA17|language=en}}</ref>普遍來說成年男性的睪酮濃度會高於女性五倍。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Burtis|first1=Carl A.|last2=Ashwood|first2=Edward R.|last3=Bruns|first3=David E.|title=Tietz Textbook of Clinical Chemistry and Molecular Diagnostics|date=2012|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=1455759422|page=1975|edition=5|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=BBLRUI4aHhkC&pg=PA1975|language=en}}</ref>

<!-- History, society and culture -->
睪酮於 1935 年被分離出來，<ref>{{cite book|last1=N. Taylor|first1=William|title=Anabolic Steroids and the Athlete, 2d ed.|date=2002|publisher=McFarland|isbn=9780786411283|page=180|edition=2|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=OGcQ0Tp2AFcC&pg=PA180|language=en}}</ref>在美國的使用率於 2001 年至 2011 年間成長為三倍。<ref name=Des2016>{{cite journal|last1=Desroches|first1=B|last2=Kohn|first2=TP|last3=Welliver|first3=C|last4=Pastuszak|first4=AW|title=Testosterone therapy in the new era of Food and Drug Administration oversight.|journal=Translational andrology and urology|date=April 2016|volume=5|issue=2|pages=207-12|pmid=27141448}}</ref>它被列在[[世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单|世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单]]中，一個在基本醫療制度中所需最重要的藥物清單。<ref name=WHO2015E>{{cite web |url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML2015_8-May-15.pdf |title=19th WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (April 2015) |date=April 2015 |accessdate=May 10, 2015 |publisher=WHO }}</ref>它是一種[[學名藥|學名藥]]，<ref name=AHFS2016>{{cite web|title=Testosterone|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/testosterone.html|website=Drugs.com|publisher=American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|accessdate=3 September 2016|date=December 4, 2015}}</ref>它的價格會根據使用的方法所決定。<ref name=Ric2015>{{cite book|last1=Hamilton|first1=Richart|title=Tarascon Pocket Pharmacopoeia 2015 Deluxe Lab-Coat Edition|date=2015|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|isbn=9781284057560|page=197}}</ref>

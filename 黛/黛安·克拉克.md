'''黛安·克拉克'''（{{lang-en|'''Diane Cluck'''}}），是一位美国[[创作歌手|创作歌手]]，她将自己的音乐称作“直觉民谣”（intuitive folk）<ref name="endoftheroad">[http://www.endoftheroadfestival.com/artists/diane-cluck-with-anders-griffen/ End Of The Road Festival 2010 biography] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100322185840/http://www.endoftheroadfestival.com/artists/diane-cluck-with-anders-griffen/ |date=2010-03-22 }}</ref><ref name="facebook.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.facebook.com/DianeCluck?sk=info |title=Diane Cluck Musician/Band Page (Facebook) |accessdate=2012-02-21}}</ref>。黛安现居于[[弗吉尼亚州|弗吉尼亚州]]<ref name="facebook.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.facebook.com/DianeCluck?sk=info |title=Diane Cluck Musician/Band Page (Facebook) |accessdate=2012-02-21}}</ref>。

==早年经历==
黛安在[[兰开斯特_(宾夕法尼亚州)|宾夕法尼亚州的兰开斯特]]长大。她从7岁开始学习钢琴，后来在宾夕法尼亚音乐学院（Pennsylvania Academy of Music）接受古典音乐训练<ref name="Perazzetti">{{cite web |url=http://nervousnero.com/am/0104/dcluck.html |accessdate=2012-02-21 |title=Diane Cluck:  The Art Of Personal Philosophy |last=Perazzetti |first=Michael |date=March 2001 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120609013104/http://nervousnero.com/am/0104/dcluck.html |archivedate=2012-06-09 |df= }}</ref><ref name="Keba">{{cite web |url=http://www.hookandlinemag.com/2011/12/diane-cluck.html |accessdate=2012-02-21 |title=Diane Cluck|last=Keba |date=December 24, 2011}}</ref>。

==风格==
《{{le|乡村之声|The Village Voice|乡村之声}}》的肖恩·博斯勒（Shawn Bosler）写道：“她很可能是最精致、最优雅的新民谣歌手之一。一位才华横溢又极具个人特色的吉他手，一位机智诙谐的作词人，一位声音充满力量且想象力丰富的作曲家；她那幽暗而内省的曲调让人很难不被打动，有点像一个更世俗版的[[凯特·布什|凯特·布什]]。<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.villagevoice.com/2009-05-13/voice-choices/jeffrey-lewis-the-junkyard-diane-cluck/|title = Jeffrey Lewis & the Junkyard+Diane Cluck|accessdate = 2010-03-29|last = Bosler|first = Shawn|date = May 20, 2009|work = preview|publisher = [[Village_Voice|Village Voice]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://dianecluck.info/quotes/|title=- Diane Cluck's Official Website|work=Diane Cluck's Official Website}}</ref>”

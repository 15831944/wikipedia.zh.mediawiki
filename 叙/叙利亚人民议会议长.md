'''叙利亚人民议会议长'''（{{lang-ar|رئيس مجلس الشعب}}）是[[叙利亚人民议会|叙利亚人民议会]]的领袖<ref>{{cite book | title =Article 55 of the 2012 Constitution of the Syrian Arab Republic |chapter = Article 55 of the 2012 Constitution of the Syrian Arab Republic | publisher = [[Government_of_Syria|Government of the Syrian Arab Republic]]  | accessdate = }}</ref><ref name="guards">{{cite book | title = Article 73 of the 2012 Constitution of the Syrian Arab Republic | chapter = Article 73 of the 2012 Constitution of the Syrian Arab Republic | publisher =[[Government_of_Syria|Government of the Syrian Arab Republic]]  | accessdate = }}</ref>。现任人民议会议长是[[汉穆达·萨巴格|汉穆达·萨巴格]]。

人民议会每四个历年选举一次。新当选的人民委员会第一次会议负责选举该委员会的议长。人民议会每年至少举行三次会议，议长有权召开理事会特别会议。人民议会的警卫属于议长的权力范围。在主席任期届满前60天，议长要求举行新的选举。所有总统候选人都必须由议长亲自批准。如果只有一名候选人可以接受，议长应推迟选举。选举结果计算完毕后将公布选举结果<ref>{{cite book | title = Article 86 of the 2012 Constitution of the Syrian Arab Republic | chapter = Article 86 of the 2012 Constitution of the Syrian Arab Republic | publisher = [[Government_of_Syria|Government of the Syrian Arab Republic]]  | accessdate = }}</ref>。

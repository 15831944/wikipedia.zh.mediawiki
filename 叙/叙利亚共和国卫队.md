'''叙利亚共和国卫队'''是由2万多人组成的[[装甲部队|装甲部队]]，负责保卫首都[[大马士革|大马士革]]的安全。是叙利亚唯一允许在首都活动的军事力量<ref name=MEIB2>{{cite journal|last=MEIB|title=Syria's Praetorian Guards: A Primer|journal=Middle East Intelligence Bulletin|volume=2|issue=7|url=http://www.meforum.org/meib/articles/0008_s2.htm|accessdate=20 July 2011|date=August 2000|date=2000}}</ref>。

== 历史 ==
共和国卫队成立之初是一支由1万人组成的装甲部队，创建于当时反叙利亚的[[巴勒斯坦|巴勒斯坦]]武装组织袭击叙利亚的1976年。目的在于保护叙利亚政府官员免遭外部威胁<ref>{{cite book|last=Paul|first=James|title=Human rights in Syria|year=1990|publisher=Human Rights Watch|pages=50|url= http://books.google.com/books?id=N-xjxWYWnlwC&pg=PA50&dq=syrian+%22republican+guard%22&hl=en&ei=wvImTt_FLY31sga0vOyPCQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=8&ved=0CEsQ6AEwBw#v=onepage&q=%22republican%20guard%22&f=false}}</ref>。为了使卫队士兵绝对忠诚于叙利亚当局，卫队的指挥官可获得来自于[[代尔祖尔|代尔祖尔]]的[[石油|石油]]收益，因此这部分开支占据了国家预算支出的很大部分<ref>{{cite book|last=Batatu| first=Hanna|title=Syria's peasantry, the descendants of its lesser rural notables, and their politics|year=1999|publisher=Princeton Univ. Press|location=Princeton, NJ|page=237|isbn=0691002541|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Mbr-ZfU_uCoC&pg=PA237&dq=syrian+%22republican+guard%22&hl=en&ei=wvImTt_FLY31sga0vOyPCQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&ved=0CDIQ6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=syrian%20%22republican%20guard%22&f=false}}</ref>。

许多[[阿萨德家族|阿萨德家族]]的成员都曾在卫队中服役。现任总统[[巴沙尔·阿萨德|巴沙尔·阿萨德]]曾是卫队中的一名[[上校|上校]]，并指挥着其中的一个[[旅|旅]]。他弟弟[[马赫尔·阿萨德|马赫尔·阿萨德]]也曾经担任卫队的上校，现在统率着卫队<ref>{{cite paper|last=Bar |first=Shmuel |url=http://www.herzliyaconference.org/_Uploads/2590Bashars.pdf |title=Bashar’s Syria: The Regime and its Strategic Worldview |date=2006 |pages=379, 384 |accessdate=2011-06-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110723214138/http://www.herzliyaconference.org/_Uploads/2590Bashars.pdf |archivedate=2011-07-23 }}</ref>。

蕨类植物（Pteridophyta）是[[维管植物|维管植物]]排除了[[种子植物|种子植物]]所形成的并系群，曾长期被视为植物界中的一个单系类群，现因为蕨类植物不构成单系群，而被拆分为[[石松門|石松門]]和[[链束植物|链束植物]]。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Phylogenetic Relationships of Extant Ferns Based on Evidence from Morphology and rbcL Sequences|url=http://www.jstor.org/stable/1547810|last=Pryer|first=Kathleen M.|last2=Smith|first2=Alan R.|date=1995|journal=American Fern Journal|issue=4|doi=10.2307/1547810|volume=85|pages=205–282|last3=Skog|first3=Judith E.}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=Phylogeny and evolution of ferns (monilophytes) with a focus on the early leptosporangiate divergences|url=http://doi.wiley.com/10.3732/ajb.91.10.1582|last=Pryer|first=K. M.|last2=Schuettpelz|first2=E.|date=2004-10-01|journal=American Journal of Botany|issue=10|doi=10.3732/ajb.91.10.1582|volume=91|pages=1582–1598|language=en|issn=0002-9122|last3=Wolf|first3=P. G.|last4=Schneider|first4=H.|last5=Smith|first5=A. R.|last6=Cranfill|first6=R.}}</ref>

蕨类植物特征为不开花，通过[[孢子|孢子]]繁殖。大约有1万多种，主要分布于中低海拔的热带亚热带地区<ref>{{cite journal |last1=CHRISTENHUSZ |first1=MAARTEN J.M. |last2=BYNG |first2=JAMES W. |title=The number of known plants species in the world and its annual increase |journal=Phytotaxa |date=2016-05-20 |volume=261 |issue=3 |pages=201 |doi=10.11646/phytotaxa.261.3.1}}</ref>。

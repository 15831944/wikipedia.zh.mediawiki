'''额日西莫足球俱乐部'''（{{lang-mn|Эрчим}}，“能源”）是一支位于[[蒙古|蒙古]]首都[[乌兰巴托|乌兰巴托]]的足球俱乐部，是[[蒙古國家超級足球聯賽|蒙古國家超級足球聯賽]]参赛球队，自1996年职业联赛创建以来，已七次问鼎联赛冠军。<ref name="RSSSF2">[http://www.rsssf.com/tablesm/mongchamp.html Mogolia - List of Champions] at RSSSF</ref><ref name="MFF" /> 球队主场设在乌兰巴托国家体育场，2011年11月由亞足聯批准，蒙古加入[[2012年亞足聯主席盃|2012年亞足聯主席盃]]，球队以蒙古超级杯冠军的身份，代表蒙古首次在亚洲赛事中亮相。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.the-afc.com/en/news-centre/inside-afc/676-afc-news/35878-mongolia-eyes-pres-cup-2012|title=Mongolia eyes Pres Cup 2012|work=The-AFC.com|publisher=Asian Football Confederation|date=29 July 2011|accessdate=3 August 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120912080543/http://www.the-afc.com/en/news-centre/inside-afc/676-afc-news/35878-mongolia-eyes-pres-cup-2012|archivedate=12 九月 2012}}</ref><ref name="afc1128" /><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.the-afc.com/en/component/content/article/937-presidents-cup-2012/37347-presidents-cup-place-for-mongolia|title=President's Cup place for Mongolia|work=The-AFC.com|publisher=Asian Football Confederation|date=1 December 2011|accessdate=1 December 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120912180949/http://www.the-afc.com/en/component/content/article/937-presidents-cup-2012/37347-presidents-cup-place-for-mongolia|archivedate=12 九月 2012}}</ref><ref name="afc1128">{{cite web |title=Decision by Competitions Committee & Executive Committee for AFC Club Competitions |url=http://www.the-afc.com/en/resources/regulations-a-guidelines/competition-regulations/2012/336-decision-by-competitions-committee-a-executive-committee-for-afc-club-competitions/download |work=The-AFC.com |publisher=Asian Football Confederation |format=PDF |accessdate=25 January 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180717012947/http://www.the-afc.com/en/resources/regulations-a-guidelines/competition-regulations/2012/336-decision-by-competitions-committee-a-executive-committee-for-afc-club-competitions/download |archive-date=2018-07-17 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>

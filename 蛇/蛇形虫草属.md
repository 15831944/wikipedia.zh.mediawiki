'''蛇形蟲草屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Ophiocordyceps''）是{{link-en|蛇形蟲草科|Ophiocordycipitaceae}}的一個[[屬|屬]]。於1931年由英國真菌學家{{link-en|湯姆·佩奇|Tom Petch}}描述發表。本屬包含大約140種寄生於[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]的物種<ref name=Kirk2008>{{cite book |vauthors=Kirk PM, Cannon PF, Minter DW, Stalpers JA |title=Dictionary of the Fungi |edition=10th |publisher=CABI |location=Wallingford, UK |year=2008 |page=483 |isbn=978-0-85199-826-8}}</ref><ref name=Petch1931>{{cite journal |author=Petch T. |title=Notes on entomogenous fungi |journal=Transactions of the British Mycological Society |year=1931 |volume=16 |issue=1 |pages=55–75 |url=http://www.cybertruffle.org.uk/cyberliber/59351/0016/001/0055.htm |doi=10.1016/S0007-1536(31)80006-3}}</ref><ref name=Sung2007>{{cite journal |vauthors=((Sung G-H)), Hywel-Jones NL, ((Sung J-M)), Luangsa-ard JJ, Shrestha B, Spatafora JW |year=2007 |title=Phylogenetic classification of ''Cordyceps'' and the clavicipitaceous fungi |journal=Studies in Mycology |volume=57 |pages=5–59 |doi=10.3114/sim.2007.57.01 |pmid=18490993 |pmc=2104736}}</ref><ref name="urlMycoBank: Ophiocordyceps">{{cite web |title=''Ophiocordyceps'' Petch 1931 |url=http://www.mycobank.org/MycoTaxo.aspx?Link=T&Rec=3598 |work=MycoBank |publisher=International Mycological Association |accessdate=2011-07-19}}</ref>。

蛇形蟲草屬許多物種重要的特徵以其寄生於[[螞蟻|螞蟻]]而聞名。它能夠入侵螞蟻聚集在體內[[肌动蛋白丝|肌肉纖維]]周圍，共同影響宿主的行為，感染經過一段時間後，真菌在宿主體內大量生長，其中它為了能在更佳的生存環境下繁殖，常透過改變寄主螞蟻的行為，最後殺死螞蟻並從螞蟻頭部生長其[[子實體_(真菌)|子實體]]並釋放孢子。<ref>{{Cite journal|url=http://www.plosone.org/article/info%3Adoi%2F10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0017024|author=H. C. Evans|author2=Simon L. Elliot|author3=David P. Hughes|year=2011|title=Hidden Diversity Behind the Zombie-Ant Fungus Ophiocordyceps unilateralis: Four New Species Described from Carpenter Ants in Minas Gerais, Brazil|journal= PLoS ONE |volume= 6|issue=3 |pages=e17024|accessdate=2013-05-01|doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0017024|pmid=21399679|pmc=3047535}}</ref>

'''蛇足石杉'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Huperzia serrata'''''}}）为[[石杉科|石杉科]][[石杉属|石杉属]]下的一个种。其含有的{{tsl|en|Hyperzine A|石杉堿甲}}这种[[生物碱|生物碱]]可以抑制[[乙酰胆碱酯酶|乙酰胆碱酯酶]]活性，<ref>Xing Zu Zhu, Xiao-Yu Li, Jin Li.,"Recent pharmacological studies on natural products in China".,''European Journal of Pharmacology'' 500 (2004) 221 – 23 </ref>作为一种号称可[[益智药|益智]]的[[非处方药|非处方]][[补剂|补剂]]售卖。<ref name="Talbott2012">{{cite book|first=SM|last=Talbott|work=A Guide to Understanding Dietary Supplements| title=Huperzine A (HupA)|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9ZZrW_j9XrcC&pg=PA304|date= 2012|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-136-80570-7|pages=304–}}</ref>

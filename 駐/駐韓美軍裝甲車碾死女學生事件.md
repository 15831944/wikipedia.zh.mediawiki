'''駐韓美軍裝甲車碾死女學生事件'''發生於2002年6月13日的[[韓國|韓國]][[京畿道|京畿道]][[楊州郡|楊州郡]]（今[[楊州市|楊州市]]）。該事件中，[[駐韓美軍|駐韓美軍]][[美國陸軍第2步兵師|第2步兵師]]的一個營在進行訓練時，所屬的一輛推進橋[[裝甲車|裝甲車]]於公路上撞死了兩名當地的女中學生。後來這場事故成了韓國國內大規模[[反美|反美聲浪]]的導火線<ref name='oh0127'>{{Cite news|title=미선이 事件에 憤怒하면 정치 편향?|url=http://www.ohmynews.com/nws_web/view/at_pg.aspx?CNTN_CD=A0001309595|publisher=오마이뉴스|著者=|쪽=|date=2010-01-27|確認日字=}}</ref>。

== 事故發生 ==
2002年6月13日星期四，辛孝純（{{lang|ko|신효순}}）和沈美善（{{lang|ko|심미선}}）兩名女學生前往鄰村參加同學的慶生派對<ref>[http://www.nytimes.com/2002/12/08/international/asia/08KORE.html Howard W. French and Don Kirk, "American Policies and Presence Under Fire in South Korea," ''New York Times''], December 8, 2002</ref><ref name=do>{{Cite news|url=http://news.donga.com/3/all/20020718/7843682/1|date=2002-07-18 |title=생일파티길 두 女中生 軌道車량에 慘變 顚末|publisher=동아일보|date=2012-05-02|author=송평인 記者}}</ref>。同日，隸屬於[[駐韓美軍|駐韓美軍]][[美國第八軍團|第八軍團]][[美國陸軍第2步兵師|第2步兵師]]44工兵營的工程車隊準備前往[[漢城|漢城]]（今[[首爾|首爾]]）都會區北方19公里處的一座靶場參與[[軍事演習|軍事演習]]<ref>[http://www.iht.com/articles/2002/07/31/kor_ed3__1.php Don Kirk, "Road Accident Galvanizes the Country: Deaths in Korea Ignite Anti-American Passion," ''International Herald Tribune''], July 31, 2002.</ref>。當車隊中一輛重達{{convert|57|ST|t}}的裝甲車行經兩名女學生行走的鄉間窄路時，於上午10時左右從後方撞上了行走於右側路肩的兩人，她們當場被輾斃<ref name="news.163.com">[http://news.163.com/07/0115/01/34RG1QF10001126S.html 资料：美军装甲车轧死2名韩国女学生事件始末_网易新闻中心]</ref>。

== 事後處理 ==
7月5日，由於事故的發生，及韓美{{Link-en|地位協定|Status of Forces Agreement}}（{{lang|en|SOFA}}）中對於美軍人員在韓犯行由美國軍法處置的規定，以{{Link-en|統一軍法典|Uniform Code of Military Justice}}（{{lang|en|UCMJ}}）中的[[過失致死|過失致死]]罪起訴了當時操縱裝甲車的兩名美軍，駕駛兵馬克·沃克中士（{{lang|en|Sgt. Mark Walker}}）和管制兵費爾南多·尼諾中士（{{lang|en|Sgt. Fernando Nino}}），理由是「未注意車輛之安全操作」<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/2097137.stm "US Soldiers Charged for Korean Deaths," ''BBC News: World Edition''], July 5, 2002</ref>。

然而在7月10日，[[大韓民國法務部|大韓民國法務部]]為了能以韓國法律在民事法庭中起訴兩名肇事美兵，而提出了請駐韓美軍司令部將該案裁判權移交給韓國司法系統審判的請求書。因為根據韓美地位協定，駐韓美軍司令官在規定上能於斟酌後，將原本由美軍軍法處理的人員移交給韓方辦理。
{{TransH}}
Citing concerns about setting a [[precedent|precedent]] in terms of allowing civilian proceedings against U.S. military personnel, then Judge Advocate of the USFK, Colonel Kent Myers, said the U.S. command would not do so, noting that the U.S. Army had waived jurisdiction only once before in a case in which the act committed was intentional and not accidental. In a statement issued by the USFK, Col. Myers noted that Walker and Nino were clearly performing assigned duties in an official capacity and were therefore subject to the UCMJ under the U.S.-ROK SOFA.<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/2178156.stm "US Refuses Korean Justice for Soldiers," ''BBC News: World Edition''], August 7, 2002</ref>

Although they refused the request of the South Korean Justice Ministry, American officials did, however, invite more than 30 media representatives, representatives from the South Korean Justice Ministry, the South Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade, and from other South Korean non-governmental organizations (NGOs) to observe the trials. In addition, rooms with closed circuit television (CCTV) coverage were provided to accommodate the increased level of interest. Moreover, families of the victims were invited to attend and, to protect their privacy, were offered the use of a separate CCTV-equipped room staffed with an interpreter and military lawyer to explain the processes involved.<ref>[http://seoul.usembassy.gov/pi111902.html "US Leaders Emphasize Openness," News Release of the US Embassy Seoul] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080506124205/http://seoul.usembassy.gov/pi111902.html |date=2008-05-06 }}, November 19, 2002.</ref> In addition to these actions, public statements made by U.S. officials stressed the fair and impartial nature of the U.S. military legal process.

During the proceedings, lawyers for Sgt. Nino contended that he attempted to alert Sgt. Walker to the presence of the two girls on the periphery of the road. Reports differ as to whether Sgt. Walker did not hear the order due to a defective communications device or because he had altered the frequency of his radio in order to communicate with others in the convoy.

Sergeants Nino and Walker were subsequently found not guilty of "negligent homicide" in verdicts issued independently by two separate panels on Wednesday, November 20 and Friday, November 22, 2002. While demonstrators questioned the [[legitimacy_(law)|legitimacy]] and [[objectivity_(philosophy)|objectivity]] of the U.S. military court and its [[verdict|verdict]], the South Korean Justice Ministry voiced dissatisfaction with the verdict, but respect for the process as employed.<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/2497947.stm "Korean Anger as US Soldiers Cleared," ''BBC News: World Edition''], November 22, 2002</ref>
美軍當局은7月3日 運轉兵과 管制병을 [[過失致死罪|過失致死罪]]로 美[[軍事法院|軍事法院]]에 起訴하는 한편, 羅布트 駐韓 美軍 司令官의 赦過를 전했다. 그와 별도로 [[大韓民國檢察|大韓民國檢察]]도 關聯 美軍에 대해 自體 調査를 벌이기로 했다. 이는 유족들이 6月28日 車輛運轉兵과 管制병, 美軍第二師團長等美軍責任者六名을 業務上過失致死嫌疑로 議政府支廳에 告訴하고, 미측의 裁判權抛棄를 要請한 데 따른 것이다. 하지만 美軍 惻隱 身邊 威脅을 理由로 檢察의 召喚調査에 응하지 않았다. [[法務部|法務部]]는 7月10日, 事相 처음으로 미측에 裁判權抛棄要請書를 보냈다. 그러나 8月7日美軍 當局은 “동 事故가 [[公務|公務]] 중에 일어난 事故이기에 裁判權이 美國에 있으며, 이제껏 美國이 1차적 裁判權을 抛棄한 前例가 없다.”라는 理由를 들어 裁判權抛棄를 拒否했다.

以後 11月18日부터11月23日까지 東豆川 [[캠프_케이시|캠프 케이시]] 내 美軍事法庭에서 열린 [[軍事裁判|軍事裁判]]에서 陪審員단은 起訴된 美軍 2名 모두에게 公務를 行下던 中 發生한 過失事故임을 根據로 無罪(not guilty) 評決을 내렸다. 그 후 美軍은 無罪 評決이 있은 지 5日 萬人11月27日 死罪 聲明을 發表하였다.

한편 美國의 無誠意한 態度에 對韓 反美示威가 繼續 이어지면서 美國의 高位 管理들이 直諫接敵으로 赦罪하였고 詔旨 w 俯視 大統領도 間接的 赦罪를 하였다. 하지만 間接赦罪에 對韓 反美示威가 이어지면서 金大中 大統領과 電話筒화를 通解 다시 赦罪하였다.

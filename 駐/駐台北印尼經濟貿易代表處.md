'''駐台北印尼經濟貿易代表處'''（{{lang-id|Kantor Dagang dan Ekonomi Indonesia di Taipei}}）是[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]]在[[中華民國|中華民國]]境內的最高辦事處，業務上與[[實質大使館|實質大使館]]相當。 

本處成立于1970年，當初名稱為印度尼西亚貿易協會（KADIN）。<ref name="KDEI">{{Cite web |url=http://www2.kdei-taipei.org/index.php/2014-08-11-03-58-09/sejarah-singkat |title=Sejarah KDEI Taipei |access-date=2014-11-25 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141009085953/http://www2.kdei-taipei.org/index.php/2014-08-11-03-58-09/sejarah-singkat |archive-date=2014-10-09 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref>[https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=8mvN1Dc_jtAC&lpg=PA270&dq=%22Belgian%20Trade%20Association%22%20taipei&pg=PA270#v=onepage&q=%22Belgian%20Trade%20Association%22%20taipei&f=false ''Taiwan Business: The Portable Encyclopedia for Doing Business with Taiwan''], Christine Genzberger, World Trade Press, 1994, page 270</ref>在1995年改為現名。<ref name="Enduring Dynamics">[https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=wFxxAAAAMAAJ&dq=Pacific+Economic+and+Cultural+Center+in+Manila+renamed+Taipei+Economic+and+Cultural+Office+1989&focus=searchwithinvolume&q=%22Taipei+Economic+and+Cultural+Office%22 ''Ensuring Interests: Dynamics of China-Taiwan Relations and Southeast Asia''], Khai Leong Ho, Guozhong He, Institute of China Studies, University of Malaya, 2006, page 25</ref>台灣方面在[[印尼|印尼]]的對應機構為[[雅加達|雅加達]]市內的{{link-en|駐印尼台北經濟貿易代表處|Taipei Economic and Trade Office, Jakarta, Indonesia}}。<ref>[http://www.roc-taiwan.org/ID Taipei Economic and Trade Office, Jakarta, Indonesia]</ref>

'''月活跃用户数'''（英文：Monthly active users，简称：'''MAU'''）也称作'''“月活”'''。是一种用来衡量的[[在线游戏|在线游戏]]、[[社交网络服务|社交网络服务]]和[[移动应用程序|移动应用程序]]活跃度的方法或标准，也可以用来判断上述服务的成功率。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://joshuaopinion.com/2011/01/monthly-active-users-mau-definition-for-facebook/|title=The Facebook}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.quora.com/Internet-Terms-Jargon/What-is-the-definition-of-Monthly-Active-Users-for-a-web-app|title="Internet Terms & Jargon: What is the definition of "Monthly Active Users" for a web app?"}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://acronyms.thefreedictionary.com/Monthly+Active+Users|title=Free Internet Dictionary by Farlex: MAU}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.insidefacebook.com/2009/01/12/application-insights-how-exactly-is-mau-calculated/|title=Application Insights: How Exactly is MAU Calculated?}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thenextweb.com/socialmedia/2010/09/01/facebook-amends-its-monthly-active-user-algorithm/|title=Facebook Amends Its Monthly Active User Algorithm}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://allfacebook.com/breaking-facebook-changes-reporting-to-monthly-active-users_b1386|title=Breaking: Facebook Changes Reporting to Monthly Active Users}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://marketingland.com/charts-facebooks-5-billion-ipo-by-the-numbers-5090|title=Facebook’s $5 Billion IPO, By The Numbers}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://allfacebook.com/active-users-pages_b40725|title=How Facebook Calculates Active Users For Pages}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kumulos.com/2016/11/22/mau-vs-dau-measure-mobile-app-retention/|title=How to Measure Mobile App Retention}}</ref>通常情况下，这个指标是通过在特定的测量期间内（例如在之前的30天内）统计唯一用户的数量来进行测量的。

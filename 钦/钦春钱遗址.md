'''钦春钱遗址'''（Tzintzuntzan）是[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]古国[[塔拉斯卡王国|塔拉斯卡王国]]的都城遗址，城名意为“蜂鸟之地”<ref name="vive">{{cite web |url=http://culturainah.org/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=256&Itemid=157 |title=Tzintzuntzan |publisher=INAH |location=Mexico |language=es |accessdate=2009-12-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150706182045/http://culturainah.org/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=256&Itemid=157# |archive-date=2015-07-06 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="enc">Carrasco 2001, p. 279.</ref>。古城位于[[帕茨夸罗湖|帕茨夸罗湖]]北岸，后者也是塔拉斯卡文化的发源地<ref name="adkins">{{cite web |url=http://faculty.smu.edu/rkemper/anth_3311/anth_3311_adkins_tarascan_paper.htm |title=Mesoamerican Anomaly? The Pre-Conquest Tarascan State |first=Julie |last=Adkins |publisher=Southern Methodist University |location=Dallas, TX |accessdate=2009-12-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091219132626/http://faculty.smu.edu/rkemper/anth_3311/anth_3311_adkins_tarascan_paper.htm |archivedate=2009-12-19 }}</ref>。古城大部分在西班牙征服期间被毁<ref name="oficio">{{cite web |url=http://eloficiodehistoriar.com.mx/2008/11/06/las-yacatas-de-tzintzuntzan/ |title=Tzintzuntzan, "lugar de colibríes" |publisher=El oficio de historiar |location=Mexico |language=es |trans-title=Tzintzuntzan, place of hummingbirds |accessdate=2009-11-25 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100717033928/http://eloficiodehistoriar.com.mx/2008/11/06/las-yacatas-de-tzintzuntzan/# |archive-date=2010-07-17 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。遗址到20世纪30年代才被发掘<ref name="adkins"/>。遗址最大的建筑是亚卡塔（yácata）金字塔，如今遗址的古建筑仍然用于当地的节日庆典<ref name="alba">{{cite news |title= Las Yácatas de Tzintzuntzan, sede del Festival Cultural de Fin de Año |first=Erick |last=Alba |url=http://www.lajornadamichoacan.com.mx/2008/12/26/index.php?section=cultura&article=013n1cul |newspaper=La Jornada de Michoacán |location=Morelia, Mexico |date=2008-12-26 |accessdate=2009-12-02 |language=es |trans-title=The yacatas of Tzintzuntzan, site of the Cultural Festival of the End of the Year }}</ref>。

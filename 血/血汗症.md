'''血汗症'''（Hematidrosis）也稱為'''血汗'''（blood sweat）是[[汗液|流汗]]時汗液中會帶有[[血液|血液]]的罕見疾病<ref name="Rare">{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1016/j.amjoto.2013.09.006| pmid = 24315735| title = Blood otorrhea: blood stained sweaty ear discharges: hematohidrosis; four case series (2001-2013).| journal = American Journal of Otolaryngology| volume = 35| issue = 2| pages = 271–3| year = 2014| last1 = Tshifularo | first1 = M. }}</ref>。血汗症的原文源自[[古希臘語|古希臘語]] ''haîma/haímatos''（αἷμα/αἵματος，意思是血）及''hīdrṓs''（ἱδρώς，意思是汗）。

== 症狀及體徵 ==
血汗一般會從前額、指甲、[[肚脐|肚脐]]等皮膚滲出。而且血液從{{link-en|粘膜皮膚區|Mucocutaneous zone}}滲出，會造成[[流鼻血|流鼻血]]、帶血的眼淚、或類似[[月經|月經]]的異常陰道分泌物<ref name="Holo">{{Cite journal | pmid = 8982961| year = 1996| author1 = Holoubek| first1 = J. E.| title = Blood, sweat and fear. "A classification of hematidrosis"| journal = Journal of Medicine| volume = 27| issue = 3–4| pages = 115–33| last2 = Holoubek| first2 = A. B.}}</ref>。發作時會伴隨[[頭痛|頭痛]]、[[腹痛|腹痛]]<!--and are usually self-limiting.-->有時分泌的液體很稀，感覺上只是帶有血的液體而已，有時分泌的液體濃椆，感覺上就是出血<ref name="India">{{Cite journal | doi = 10.4103/0019-5154.119964| pmid = 24249903| title = A curious case of sweating blood| journal = Indian Journal of Dermatology| volume = 58| issue = 6| pages = 478–80| year = 2013| last1 = Biswas | first1 = S. | last2 = Surana | first2 = T. | last3 = De | first3 = A. | last4 = Nag | first4 = F. | pmc=3827523}}</ref>。

雖然血汗症失血情形不嚴重，不過也會使得皮膚變軟且變脆弱。

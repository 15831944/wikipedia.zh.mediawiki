Res. | volume = 87 | issue = 5 | pages = E1–9 | year = 2000 | pmid = 10969042 | doi = | issn = | url= http://circres.ahajournals.org/cgi/content/abstract/87/5/e1 }}</ref>或[[血管紧张素#血管紧张素II|血管紧张素II]]转化为[[血管紧张素#血管紧张素-（1-7）|血管紧张素-（1-7）]]<ref name="pmid17049503">{{cite journal en| author = Keidar S, Kaplan M, Gamliel-Lazarovich A | title = ACE2 of the heart: From angiotensin I to angiotensin (1-7) | journal = Cardiovasc Res. | volume = 73 | issue = 3 | pages = 463-9 | year = 2007 | pmid = 17049503 | doi = | issn = | url=http://cardiovascres.oxfordjournals.org/cgi/content/full/73/3/463 }}</ref>的[[外肽酶|外肽酶]]。此外，ACE2还是[[SARS|SARS]][[冠状病毒|冠状病毒]]（[[:en:severe_acute_respiratory_syndrome|severe acute respiratory syndrome]] (SARS)-CoV ）和人CoV-NL63冠状病毒的[[受体|受体]]<ref name="pmid16339739">{{cite journal en| author = Weiss SR, Navas-Martin S | title = Angiotensin-converting enzyme 2--a new cardiac regulator | journal = Microbiol Mol Biol Rev. | volume = 69 | issue = 4 | pages = 635-64 | year = 2005 | pmid = 16339739 | doi =  | issn = }}</ref>。

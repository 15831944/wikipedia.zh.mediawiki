'''俄羅斯劇院'''（{{lang-ru|Русский театр Эстонии}}，{{lang-et|Vene teater}}）是位於[[愛沙尼亞|愛沙尼亞]]首都[[塔林|塔林]]的一個俄語劇院。劇院的建築興建於1926年，是一座[[裝飾風藝術|裝飾風藝術]]風格建築。<ref name=draamaa>{{cite web|url=http://www.draamamaa.ee/theatres/russian-drama-theatre/|title=Russian Drama Theatre of Estonia|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=|website=|publisher=draamamaa|access-date=1 November 2016|quote=|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161103220538/http://www.draamamaa.ee/theatres/russian-drama-theatre/|archivedate=2016年11月3日}}</ref><ref name=teateree>{{cite web |url= http://www.teater.ee/eng/theatre_in_estonia/theatres/Russian_Drama_Theatre.theatre_id-12|title= Russian Drama Theatre|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |date= |website= |publisher= Eesti Teatri Agentuur|access-date= 1 November 2016|quote=}}</ref><ref name=visitee>{{cite web |url= http://www.visitestonia.com/en/russian-theatre-of-estonia|title= Russian Theatre of Estonia|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |date= |website= |publisher= Visit Estonia|access-date= 1 November 2016|quote=}}</ref>

'''俄羅斯國家女子足球隊'''是[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]]國際[[女子足球|女子足球]]隊伍。 隊伍由[[俄羅斯足球總會|俄羅斯足球總會]]管理並隸屬於[[歐洲足球協會聯盟|UEFA]]。[[Vera_Pauw|Vera Pauw]]取代 [[Igor_Shalimov|Igor Shalimov]]自2011年4月成為教練。

俄羅斯參加過兩次[[國際足協女子世界盃|世界盃]], [[1999年國際足協女子世界盃|1999]], [[2003年國際足協女子世界盃|2003]]和4次[[欧洲女子足球锦标赛|欧洲女子足球锦标赛]]， [[UEFA_Women's_Euro_1995|1995]], [[UEFA_Women's_Euro_1997|1997]], [[UEFA_Women's_Euro_2001|2001]], [[UEFA_Women's_Euro_2009|2009]].

和[[俄羅斯國家足球隊|男子隊伍]]一樣，俄羅斯女子隊都直接繼承[[CIS_women_national_football_team|獨立國協]]和[[USSR_women's_national_football_team|蘇聯]]女子國家隊。

==歷史==
<!---
===Beginning===
The USSR (who became the Commonwealth of Independent States during the campaign) reached the 1993 UEFA European Women's Championship quarter-finals at their only attempt and Russia were to match that two years later, with both teams losing to Germany over two legs. In 1997, they qualified directly for the final tournament but once there were defeated by Sweden, France – who they had beaten in the preliminaries – and Spain. However, they were among six European sides to qualify for the 1999 FIFA Women's World Cup, thanks to two 2–1 play-off wins against Finland, and comfortable victories over Japan and Canada earned them a quarter-final, where they lost to eventual runners-up China.

===After the turn of the 21st century===
They cruised unbeaten into the 2001 continental finals but managed only a point against England in the group stage. Russia's fine qualifying run then continued in the 2003 World Cup and they again reached the quarter-finals before a 7–1 loss to Germany. That preceded something of a decline in fortunes as Finland avenged their 1999 reverse by beating Russia in the play-offs for UEFA WOMEN'S EURO 2005, before Russia had the misfortune to draw Germany in 2007 World Cup qualifying.

===當前===
Renewed hope soon began to come from the younger generation, however, with a young member of the 2003 squad, [[Elena_Danilova|Elena Danilova]], inspiring victory in the 2005 UEFA European Women's Under-19 Championship, their first post-Soviet national team title at any level. Although the striker has suffered injury problems, many of her colleagues have graduated to the senior squad, with Russia eventually reaching the [[UEFA_Women's_Euro_2009|2009 finals]] with a dramatic away-goals play-off success against Scotland. At the final tournament, Russia were drawn against [[瑞典国家女子足球队|Sweden]], [[意大利国家女子足球队|Italy]] and [[英格蘭女子足球代表隊|England]] in Group C. The team was unable to get past the group stage and finished last as they lost all the three matches, scoring 2 and conceding 8.

In the [[2011_FIFA_Women's_World_Cup_qualification_(UEFA)|2011 FIFA Women's World Cup Qualifiers]], Russia were drawn in Group 6 with [[Switzerland_women's_national_football_team|Switzerland]], [[Republic_of_Ireland_Women's_National_Football_Team|Republic of Ireland]], [[Israel_women's_national_football_team|Israel]] and [[Kazakhstan_women's_national_football_team|Kazakhstan]], where Russia was eliminated at the group stage as they ended the stage behind Switzerland.

==球隊衣服==
俄羅斯的主場衣服包括金色襪子，栗色短褲和栗色襯衫。她們的客場衣服是白色的球衣和淺藍色短褲。

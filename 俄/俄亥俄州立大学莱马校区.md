'''俄亥俄州立大学莱马校区'''是[[俄亥俄州立大学|俄亥俄州立大学]]设在[[莱马_(俄亥俄州)|莱马]]的校区。校区占地{{convert|565|acre|km2|sing=on}}，北距[[托莱多_(俄亥俄州)|托莱多]]{{convert|80|mi|km}}。该校区提供140门课程和12个本科文理学士学位，其中有十个学位为四年制项目，另两个项目为专升本项目。<ref name="majors">{{cite web | title = Academic Programs | url = http://lima.osu.edu/admissions/bachelors.php | accessdate = 2012-08-29 | publisher = The Ohio State University at Lima | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121114101221/http://lima.osu.edu/admissions/bachelors.php | archivedate = 2012-11-14 }}</ref> 超过170个专业的学生可以在莱马校区开始自己的学业，并在哥伦布主校区毕业。莱马校区有超过20个学生社团，另有12个校内体育俱乐部。<ref name="ClubsOrgs">{{cite web | title = Student Life: Student Organizations and Clubs | url = http://lima.osu.edu/students/studentclubs.php | accessdate = 2012-08-29 | publisher = The Ohio State University at Lima | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120828054019/http://lima.osu.edu/students/studentClubs.php | archivedate = 2012-08-28 }}</ref><ref name="Intramurals">{{cite web | title = Athletics: Barons Intramural Sports | url = http://lima.osu.edu/athletics/intramurals.php | accessdate = 2012-08-29 | publisher = The Ohio State University at Lima | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120909113628/http://lima.osu.edu/athletics/intramurals.php | archivedate = 2012-09-09 }}</ref>

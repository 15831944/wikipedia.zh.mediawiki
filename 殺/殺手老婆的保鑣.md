== 製作 ==
2018年5月，據報導[[萊恩·雷諾斯|萊恩·雷諾斯]]、[[山繆·傑克森|山繆·傑克森]]和[[莎瑪·海耶克|莎瑪·海耶克]]正為回歸《殺手保鑣》續集《The Hitman's Wife's Bodyguard》進行早期洽談，並計劃在同年晚些時候開始拍攝。[[獅門影業|獅門影業]]正為美國的發行權進行洽談，[[派屈克·休斯|派屈克·休斯]]亦為回歸執導電影一事進行洽談<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2018/05/hitmans-bodyguard-sequel-lionsgate-ryan-reynolds-samuel-l-jackson-salma-hayek-1202383900/|title=Lionsgate Circling Sequel To ‘Hitman’s Bodyguard’ With Ryan Reynolds, Samuel L. Jackson In Talks|work=[[Deadline_Hollywood|Deadline Hollywood]]|date=2018-05-07|accessdate=2018-11-02|author=Andreas Wiseman}}</ref>。2018年11月，獅門影業從Millennium Films手中收購了美國發行權，而馬修·歐托爾和李斯·韋爾登將通過Millennium和Campbell Grobman Films製作電影。休斯確認回歸執導電影，布蘭登·墨菲和菲爾·墨菲撰寫劇本<ref name="Nov2018THR">{{cite news |last1=Roxborough |first1=Scott |title=AFM: Lionsgate to Release 'The Hitman's Bodyguard' Sequel Stateside (Exclusive) |url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/lionsgate-release-hitmans-bodyguard-sequel-stateside-1156835 |accessdate=2018-11-02 |work=The Hollywood Reporter |date=2018-11-01 |language=en}}</ref>。雷諾斯、傑克森和海耶克正式簽約回歸續集<ref>{{cite news |last1=McNary |first1=Dave |title=Ryan Reynolds, Samuel L. Jackson to Return for ‘Hitman’s Bodyguard’ Sequel |url=https://variety.com/2018/film/markets-festivals/ryan-reynolds-samuel-jackson-returning-hitmans-bodyguard-sequel-1203017031/ |accessdate=2018-11-06 |work=Variety |date=2018-11-01}}</ref>。2019年3月，[[法蘭克·葛里洛|法蘭克·葛里洛]]加盟電影<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2019/film/news/hitmans-bodyguard-sequel-frank-grillo-1203153306/|title=Frank Grillo Joins Ryan Reynolds in ‘Hitman’s Bodyguard’ Sequel (EXCLUSIVE)|website=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|first=Justin|last=Kroll|date=2019-03-01|accessdate=2019-03-01}}</ref>。

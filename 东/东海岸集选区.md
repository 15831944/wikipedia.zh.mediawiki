'''东海岸集选区'''（{{lang-en|East Coast Group Representation Constituency}}：{{lang-ms|Kawasan Undi Perwakilan Berkumpulan East Coast}}：{{lang-ta|ஈஸ்ட் கோஸ்ட் குழுத்தொகுதி}}）是一个位于[[新加坡|新加坡]]{{tsl|en|East Region, Singapore||东部}}的四人[[集选区|集选区]]。该集选区于1997年创立，涵盖了东海岸、{{tsl|en|Bedok|勿洛}}、[[樟宜|樟宜]]－[[四美_(新加坡)|四美]]、{{tsl|en|Siglap|实乞纳}}、[[淡滨尼|淡滨尼]]、{{tsl|en|Pasir Ris|巴西立}}、[[菜市_(新加坡)|菜市]]、[[新加坡樟宜机场|樟宜机场]]和几个离岸[[島嶼|島嶼]]如[[德光島|德光島]]以及[[烏敏島|烏敏島]]。

自选区成立以来，一直由执政的[[新加坡人民行动党|人民行动党]]胜出。人民行动党也同时管辖该集选区的市镇会。<ref name="Meet Your Mps"/>

==历史==
东海岸集选区于1997年创立，并有6名成员<ref name="singapore-elections.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/1997/east-coast-grc.html|title=Singapore Parliamentary General Election 1997 > East Coast GRC|website=www.singapore-elections.com|access-date=2018-09-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322125140/http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/1997/east-coast-grc.html|archive-date=2016-03-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。东海岸集选区是由勿洛集选区和友诺士集选区合并而成。{{tsl|en|Joo Chiat Single Member Constituency|如切单选区}}随后在2001年从该集选区转移至{{tsl|en|Marine Parade GRC|马林百列集选区}}里<ref name="Singapore Elections12">{{cite web | title=Singapore Parliamentary General Election 1997 > East Coast GRC | website=Singapore Elections | url=http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/1997/east-coast-grc.html | access-date=2018-08-20 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322125140/http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/1997/east-coast-grc.html | archive-date=2016-03-22 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。2006年时，大部分{{tsl|en|Kaki Bukit, Singapore|加基武吉 (新加坡)|加基武吉}}社区和60%[[菜市_(新加坡)|菜市]]社区从东海岸集选区转移至{{tsl|en|Marine Parade GRC|马林百列集选区}}，而[[周亨曾|周亨曾]]、{{tsl|en|Tan Soo Khoon|陈树群}}和{{tsl|en|Lee Yock Suan|李玉全}}也在当年退选，以及2011年时退选的[[贾古玛|贾古玛]]和{{tsl|en|Abdullah Tarmugi|阿都拉·塔姆基}}。这使东海岸集选区从6名代表减少至5名<ref name="singapore-elections.com1">{{cite web|url=http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2011/east-coast-grc.html|title=Singapore Parliamentary General Election 2011 > East Coast GRC|website=www.singapore-elections.com|access-date=2018-09-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322140739/http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2011/east-coast-grc.html|archive-date=2016-03-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。2015年边界调整时[[实龙岗岛|实龙岗岛]]被转移至巴西立－榜鹅集选区，而[[凤山单选区|凤山]]则独立成单选区，使东海岸集选区只剩下4名代表<ref name="Singapore Elections32">{{cite web | title=Singapore Parliamentary General Election 2015 > Fengshan | website=Singapore Elections | url=http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2015/fengshan.html | access-date=2018-08-20 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322190909/http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2015/fengshan.html | archive-date=2016-03-22 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。

[[人民行动党|人民行动党]]在{{tsl|en|Singapore general election, 1997||1997年}}和{{tsl|en|Singapore general election, 2001||2001年}}大选皆不战而胜<ref name="singapore-elections.com"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2001/east-coast-grc.html|title=Singapore Parliamentary General Election 2001 > East Coast GRC|website=www.singapore-elections.com|access-date=2018-09-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322190817/http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2001/east-coast-grc.html|archive-date=2016-03-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>，[[2006年新加坡大選|2006年大选]]则有[[工人黨_(新加坡)|工人党]]前来竞选<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2006/east-coast-grc.html|title=Singapore Parliamentary General Election 2006 > East Coast GRC|website=www.singapore-elections.com|access-date=2018-09-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160319035155/http://singapore-elections.com/general-election/2006/east-coast-grc.html|archive-date=2016-03-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>。在2011年和2015年，人行党和工人党获得的选票相差不大，然而皆由人行党继续胜出，而败选的工人党候选人也获得以非选区议员身份进入国会<ref name="singapore-elections.com1"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2015/east-coast-grc.html|title=Singapore Parliamentary General Election 2015 > East Coast GRC|website=www.singapore-elections.com|access-date=2018-09-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160322124449/http://www.singapore-elections.com/general-election/2015/east-coast-grc.html|archive-date=2016-03-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

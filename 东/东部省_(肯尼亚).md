'''东部省'''（[[英文|英文]]：'''Eastern Province'''）是[[肯尼亚|肯尼亚]][[肯尼亚行政区划|八省]]之一，首府[[恩布|恩布]]（英文：Embu）.

== 行政区划 ==
东部省分为26个县：
# Chalbi District
# [[恩布县|恩布县]] Embu District
# Garbatula District
# Igembe District
# Imenti North District
# Imenti South District
# [[伊西奧洛縣|伊西奧洛縣]] Isiolo District
# Kangundo District
# Kibwezi District
# Kitui District
# Kyuso District
# Laisamis District
# Machakos District
# Makueni District
# [[馬薩比特縣|馬薩比特縣]] Marsabit District
# Mbeere District
# Mbooni District
# Meru Central District
# Meru South District (Nithi District)
# Moyale District
# Mutomo District (Kitui South District)
# Mwala District
# Mwingi District
# Tharaka District
# Tigania District
# Yatta District

'''原生质球'''（{{lang|en|Spheroplast}}）是指[[细菌|细菌]]、[[酵母|酵母]]，或[[真菌|真菌]][[细胞|细胞]]失去部分[[细胞壁|细胞壁]]后，其余部分在[[表面张力|表面张力]]作用下形成的球状体。原生质球对渗透压敏感，置于低[[渗透压|渗透压]]溶液中，原生质球将会破裂<ref name="spheroplast">{{cite book |author1=Miller-Keane |title=Encyclopedia and Dictionary of Medicine, Nursing, and Allied Health |date=2003 |edition=Seventh Edition|section=spheroplast|ISBN=978-1455726240}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author1=J. M. GEBICKI |author2=A. M. JAMES |title=The Preparation and Properties of Spheroplasts of Aerobacter aerogenes |journal=J. gen. Microbiol. |date=1960 |issue=23 |pages=9-18 |url=https://www.microbiologyresearch.org/docserver/fulltext/micro/23/1/mic-23-1-9.pdf?expires=1545928094&id=id&accname=guest&checksum=CF7ABA102830FD33190AB538D4B52AFD |accessdate=2018-12-27}}</ref>。

原生质球可用于[[抗生素|抗生素]]的筛选中。如果某一种细菌经药物处理后，可观察到原生质球的形成，就能推定这种药物能抑制该菌的细胞壁合成<ref>Silver, L.L. Rational approaches to antibiotic discovery: pre-genomic directed and phenotypic screening, 2.4.2 Screens for spheroplast formation. In: Thomas Dougherty, Michael J. Pucci, Antibiotic Discovery and Development. Chap. 2, p. 46.</ref>。

'''原杉藻'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Prototaxites''}}）為一屬陸生的[[真菌|真菌]]，生存於[[志留紀|志留紀]]晚期至[[泥盆紀|泥盆紀]]晚期（4億3千萬年前 - 3億6千萬年前），其[[子實體_(真菌)|子實體]]形成類似[[樹幹|樹幹]]的結構，直徑達{{convert|1|m|ft|0}}，高度則可達{{convert|8.8|m|ft|0}}<ref name=Boyce2007>{{Cite journal |author1=Boyce, K.C. |author2=Hotton, C.L. |author3=Fogel, M.L. |author4=Cody, G.D. |author5=Hazen, R.M. |author6=Knoll, A.H. |author7=Hueber, F.M. |title=Devonian landscape heterogeneity recorded by a giant fungus |journal=[[Geology_(journal)|Geology]] |volume=35 |issue=5 |pages=399–402 |date=May 2007 |url=http://geology.geoscienceworld.org/cgi/reprint/35/5/399.pdf |format=PDF |doi=10.1130/G23384A.1 |bibcode=2007Geo....35..399B |author= |access-date=2019-03-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110928121333/http://geology.geoscienceworld.org/cgi/reprint/35/5/399.pdf |archive-date=2011-09-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，由直徑{{convert|50|um}}的管束交織而成，為當時陸地上最大的陸生生物。

傳統上很難將原杉藻歸類至現存的分類系統中，目前的觀點認為它們屬於真菌<ref name=Boyce2007/>，也有研究認為它們為[[藻類|藻類]]的[[共生|共生體]]（即[[地衣|地衣]]），且其組織中的管束細胞與[[球囊菌門|球囊菌門]]的十分相似<ref name="Retallack">{{cite journal |author1=Retallack G.J. |author2=Landing, E. |lastauthoramp=yes |year= 2014|title=Affinities and architecture of Devonian trunks of ''Prototaxites'' loganii|journal=Mycologia|volume=106|pages=1143–1156 |doi=10.3852/13-390 |pmid=24990121}}</ref>。而對於原杉藻與其他真菌類群的關係亦有多種觀點，除了認為其與球囊菌門接近者外，也有觀點認為其屬於[[擔子菌門|擔子菌門]]、[[毛黴亞門|毛黴門]]或[[子囊菌門|子囊菌門]]<ref name=Honegger2017>{{cite journal|title=Fertile Prototaxites taiti: a basal ascomycete with inoperculate, polysporous asci lacking croziers|author=Rosmarie Honegger, Dianne Edwards, Lindsey Axe, Christine Strullu-Derrien|journal=''Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society B''|volume=373|issue=1739|doi=10.1098/rstb.2017.0146|year=2017}}</ref>。

== 形態學 ==
[[Image:Prototaxites_apex.jpg|thumb]]
原杉藻[[化石|化石]]主幹的直徑達{{convert|1|m|ft|0}}，高度可達{{convert|8.8|m|ft|0}}<ref name=Boyce2007>{{Cite journal |author1=Boyce, K.C. |author2=Hotton, C.L. |author3=Fogel, M.L. |author4=Cody, G.D. |author5=Hazen, R.M. |author6=Knoll, A.H. |author7=Hueber, F.M. |title=Devonian landscape heterogeneity recorded by a giant fungus |journal=[[Geology_(journal)|Geology]] |volume=35 |issue=5 |pages=399–402 |date=May 2007 |url=http://geology.geoscienceworld.org/cgi/reprint/35/5/399.pdf |format=PDF |doi=10.1130/G23384A.1 |bibcode=2007Geo....35..399B |author= |access-date=2019-03-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110928121333/http://geology.geoscienceworld.org/cgi/reprint/35/5/399.pdf |archive-date=2011-09-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，為當時陸地上最大的陸生生物。它們主幹的基部較粗且具有分枝，可能具有類似根部的構造，但仍然未有化石紀錄<ref name=Selosse2002>{{cite journal | author = Selosse, M.A. | year = 2002 | title = ''Prototaxites'': A 400 Myr Old Giant Fossil, A Saprophytic Holobasidiomycete, Or A Lichen? | journal = Mycological Research | volume = 106 | issue = 6 | pages = 641–644 | doi = 10.1017/S0953756202226313}}</ref>。可能為原杉藻根部構造的鑄型印痕化石在泥盆紀地層年代十分常見<ref name=Hillier2008>{{cite journal | title=Sedimentological evidence for rooting structures in the Early Devonian Anglo–Welsh Basin (UK), with speculation on their producers | year = 2008 | doi = 10.1016/j.palaeo.2008.01.038 |author1=Hillier, R |author2=Edwards, D |author3=Morrissey, L. B. | journal= Palaeogeography, Palaeoclimatology, Palaeoecology | volume=270 | pages=366 | issue=3–4 }}</ref>。主枝幹具有環狀生長輪，這些生長輪中偶爾會嵌入一些植物殘骸<ref name=Hueber2001>{{cite journal | author = Hueber, F.M. | year = 2001 | title = Rotted wood-alga-fungus: the history and life of ''Prototaxites'' Dawson 1859 | journal = Review of Palaeobotany and Palynology | volume = 116 | issue = 1 | pages = 123–158 | doi = 10.1016/S0034-6667(01)00058-6}}</ref>，顯示原杉藻可能透過不斷包覆外層來進行成長。它們的主枝幹可能為子實體，透過[[菌絲體|菌絲體]]供給營養。微觀上，原杉藻的枝幹由兩種管束（可能為[[菌絲|菌絲]]）交織而成：骨架管束直徑約{{convert|20|to|50|um}}，內壁厚度約{{convert|2|to|6|um}}；另一種增生管束則較細，直徑約{{convert|5|to|10|um}}，且具有許多交叉的分枝，增生管束內具有{{tsl|en|septa|隔膜 (真菌)|隔膜}}，且隔膜上具有小孔隙，這項特徵只有在現存的真菌與[[紅藻|紅藻]]上發現<ref name=Schmid1976>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1126/science.191.4224.287 | pmid = 17832148 | author = Schmid, Rudolf | journal = [[Science_(journal)|Science]] | volume = 191 | issue = 4224 | pages = 287–288 | year = 1976 | title = Septal pores in ''Prototaxites'', an enigmatic Devonian plant|bibcode = 1976Sci...191..287S }}</ref>。

志留紀晚期疑似為植物的化石{{link-en|織絲體|Nematothallus}}同樣有發現類似的管束結構，顯示織絲體可能其實為原杉藻上的葉狀組織，然而這項理論仍然處在假說階段，缺乏相關支持證據<ref name=Jonker1979>{{cite journal | author = Jonker, F.P. | year = 1979 | title = ''Prototaxites'' in the Lower Devonian | journal = Palaeontographica, B | pages = 39–56}}</ref>。

== 研究歷史 ==
[[File:Reconstruction_of_Prototaxites_loganii.tif|thumb]]
[[Image:Prototaxites_Dawson1888.PNG|150px]]
原杉藻的化石最早發現於1843年<ref>A fossil specimen collected by [[Charles_Darwin|Charles Darwin]]'s friend [[Joseph_Dalton_Hooker|Joseph Dalton Hooker]], was mislaid for 163 years at the [[British_Geological_Survey|British Geological Survey]] offices in London ([http://www.csmonitor.com/Science/2012/0117/Scientists-find-lost-Darwin-fossils-in-gloomy-corner-of-British-Geological-Survey "Scientists find lost Darwin fossils in gloomy corner of British Geological Survey", ''Christian Science Monitor'', 17 January 2012] {{Wayback|url=http://www.csmonitor.com/Science/2012/0117/Scientists-find-lost-Darwin-fossils-in-gloomy-corner-of-British-Geological-Survey |date=20190326064804 }}; identifying Hooker as "John Hooker").</ref>，然而一直到了14年後才由[[加拿大|加拿大]]地質學家{{link-en|約翰·威廉·道森|John William Dawson}}開始研究原杉藻的化石並進行發表，最初道森認為這個樣本屬於已經部分腐爛的[[松柏門|松柏門]]植物組織，並且上面附生有正在分解組織的真菌<ref name=Hueber2001/>。1872年，蘇格蘭植物學家{{link-en|威廉·卡魯瑟斯|William Carruthers (botanist)}}質疑這項理論，並認為原杉藻的學名''Prototaxites''（意為「第一個紫杉」<ref>The "Taxinaea" ([[Taxaceae|Taxaceae]]) are the grouping of conifers to which Dawson drew analogy</ref>）應該要更名為''Nematophycus''（意思為「絲狀的藻類」<ref>{{Cite book|title=The American Palaeozoic Fossils: A Catalogue of the Genera and Species, with Names of Authors, Dates, Places of Publication, Groups of Rocks in which Found, and the Etymology and Signification of the Words, and an Introduction Devoted to the Stratigraphical Geology of the Palaeozoic Rocks|last=Almond Miller|first=Samuel|publisher=author|year=1877|isbn=|location=|pages=35}}</ref>）<ref name=Hueber2001/>，不過這項行為已嚴重違反了生物的命名規約，因此無法實行<ref>{{Citation | author1=Seward, A. C. (Albert Charles) | title=Fossil plants : a text-book for students of botany and geology | publication-date=1898 | publisher=University Press ; New York : Macmillan | page=192 | url=http://trove.nla.gov.au/work/3568018 | accessdate=13 February 2016 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180801124810/https://trove.nla.gov.au/work/3568018 | archive-date=2018-08-01 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。道森雖然不斷堅持自己的理論，但是原杉藻的結構在微觀上顯示他的理論是站不住腳的，在這之後道森也不斷提議將原杉藻改名（改成 ''Nematophyton''，意思為「絲狀的植物」），意圖掩蓋自己當年將原杉藻誤認為是一種樹木的事實<ref name=Hueber2001/>，但是基於[[國際藻類、真菌、植物命名法規|國際藻類、真菌、植物命名法規]]，原杉藻的學名已確立並且無法更改，縱使意思與事實相違背，此學名仍然沿用至今。

雖然有許多證據顯示原杉藻生活於陸地上<ref>{{Cite book|title=Plant Life Through the Ages: A Geological and Botanical Retrospect Cambridge Library Collection - Earth Science|last=Seward|first=A. C.|publisher=Cambridge University Press|year=2010|isbn=9781108016001|location=|pages=119}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=The Geological History of Plants|last=William Dawson|first=Sir John|publisher=Library of Alexandria|year=2016|isbn=9781465606853|location=|pages=}}</ref>，卡魯瑟斯當時認為它們其實為一種海生的[[藻類|藻類]]，1919年，英格蘭植物學家{{link-en|阿圖·哈里·查爾赫|Arthur Harry Church}}則指出他不應排除其為真菌的可能性。由於缺乏任何可與現存生物進行比較研究的組織特徵，各種理論都難以獲得支持<ref name=Hueber2001/>，因此原杉藻的分類地位的謎團遲遲未解。直到2001年，經過20年的研究，[[美國國立自然史博物館|美國國立自然史博物館]]的Francis Hueber發表了一篇論文，提出證據表明原杉藻實屬一種真菌<ref name=Hueber2001/>。

這個理論最初受到了懷疑，但在之後出現了更多證據來支持這項理論<ref>Debra Lindsay (2005) Prototaxites Dawson, 1859 or Nematophycus Carruthers, 1872: Geologists V. Botanists in the Formative Period of the Science of Paleobotany. Earth Sciences History: 2005, Vol. 24, No. 1, pp. 35-61.</ref>。2007年，由包括Hueber與[[芝加哥大學|芝加哥大學]]的{{link-en|C·凱文·博伊斯|C. Kevin Boyce}}所組成的研究團隊針對原杉藻化石進行了同位素分析<ref name=Boyce2007 />，結果亦支持原杉藻屬於真菌，因為原杉藻的化石中含有多種[[碳的同位素|碳的同位素]]，而行[[光合作用|光合作用]]的植物與藻類則僅會有少數幾種特定的碳同位素，這代表原杉藻的生存仰賴多種而非單一的碳來源，為[[異營生物|異營生物]]（植物等[[自營生物|自營生物]]多半僅有單一來源，也就是透過光合作用取得），和現代的真菌一樣以吸收基質中的各種有機物維生<ref name=Boyce2007/>。原杉藻可能有十分龐大的地下菌絲網路來吸收足夠的營養支持其生長，亦可能具有{{link-en|菌絲索|Mycelial cord}}而有能力進行長距離的營養輸送<ref>{{cite journal | author = Hillier RD, Edwards D, Morrissey LB | year = 2008 | title = Sedimentological evidence for rooting structures in the Early Devonian Anglo-Welsh Basin (UK), with speculation on their producers | url = | journal = Palaeogeography, Palaeoclimatology, Palaeoecology | volume = 270 | issue = | pages = 366–380 | doi=10.1016/j.palaeo.2008.01.038}}</ref>。

關於原杉藻與各類群真菌的關係也有許多不同理論。Hueber認為原杉藻的化石中有[[子實層|子實層]]與原始[[擔子|擔子]]的痕跡，而認為其屬於[[擔子菌門|擔子菌門]]<ref name=Hueber2001/>。也有觀點認為其應歸屬於[[球囊菌門|球囊菌門]]或[[毛黴亞門|毛黴門]]<Ref>{{cite journal|title=Affinities and architecture of Devonian trunks of Prototaxites loganii|author=G. J. Retallack, Ed Landing|journal=''[[Mycologia|Mycologia]]''|volume=106|issue=6|year=2014|doi=10.3852/13-390}}</ref>。2017年，有研究再次檢視原杉藻化石的構造，認為有[[子囊|子囊]]與{{link-en|側絲|paraphyses}}的痕跡，而主張其應歸屬於[[子囊菌門|子囊菌門]]<ref name=Honegger2017/>。

有其他研究認為原杉藻可能是層層堆疊的[[地錢門|地錢]]<ref name="Graham2010"/>，但這項理論被認為可能性不大<ref name="Taylor2010">{{Cite journal | last1 = Taylor | first1 = T. N. | last2 = Taylor | first2 = E. L. | last3 = Decombeix | first3 = A. -L. | last4 = Schwendemann | first4 = A. | last5 = Serbet | first5 = R. | last6 = Escapa | first6 = I. | last7 = Krings | first7 = M. | title = The enigmatic Devonian fossil Prototaxites is not a rolled-up liverwort mat: Comment on the paper by Graham et al. (AJB 97: 268-275) | doi = 10.3732/ajb.1000047 | journal = American Journal of Botany | volume = 97 | issue = 7 | pages = 1074 | year = 2010 | pmid =  21616859| pmc = }}</ref>。亦有其他研究主張原杉藻是是類似於[[紅豆杉屬|紅豆杉屬]]的維管束植物、類似[[海帶目|海帶目]]的藻類與具有類似真菌管狀組織的[[地錢門|地錢門]]植物<ref name=Graham2010>{{cite journal | title=Structural, physiological, and stable carbon isotopic evidence that the enigmatic Paleozoic fossil ''Prototaxites'' formed from rolled liverwort mats  | year = 2010 | doi = 10.3732/ajb.0900322 | author=Graham, LE, Cook, ME, Hanson, DT, Pigg, KB and Graham, JM | journal=[[American_Journal_of_Botany|American Journal of Botany]] |volume=97 | pages=268–275 | pmid=21622387 | issue=2}}</ref>。

與本屬相似的''{{link-en|Nematasketum}}''屬之化石組織中同樣也有類似的管束構造，代表它們可能也是真菌<ref name="Edwards2012">{{Cite journal | last1 = Edwards | first1 = D. | last2 = Axe | first2 = L. | doi = 10.1111/j.1095-8339.2011.01195.x | title = Evidence for a fungal affinity for ''Nematasketum'', a close ally of ''Prototaxites''| journal = Botanical Journal of the Linnean Society | volume = 168 |issue=1| pages = 1–18| year = 2012 | pmid =  | pmc = }}</ref>。

== 古生態學 ==
原杉藻可能為當時最高的陸生生物，當時最高的植物為[[頂囊蕨|頂囊蕨]]，高度僅有{{convert|1|m|ft|0}}，其他陸地生物包括一些小型的[[無脊椎動物|無脊椎動物]]。[[維管束|維管束]]樹木及灌木的興起後原杉藻便[[滅絕|滅絕]]了<ref name=Selosse2002 />。原杉藻高聳的結構可能可用來散播[[孢子|孢子]]，或用以競爭光線<ref name=Selosse2002/>，[[芝加哥大學|芝加哥大學]]研究團隊則將原杉藻復原為無分枝的柱狀組織<ref>[http://www-news.uchicago.edu/releases/07/070423.fungus.shtml Prehistoric mystery organism verified as giant fungus] {{Wayback|url=http://www-news.uchicago.edu/releases/07/070423.fungus.shtml |date=20181126130225 }} Press release from [[University_of_Chicago|University of Chicago]], April 23, 2007.</ref>。化石中發現與藻類相關的生物分子代表它們的表面可能被[[共生|共生]]或[[寄生|寄生]]性的藻類所覆蓋，也可能其本身就是藻類<ref name=Selosse2002/><ref name=Niklas1976>{{cite journal| author = Niklas, K.J. | year = 1976| title = Chemical Examinations of Some Non-Vascular Paleozoic Plants| journal = Brittonia | volume = 28 | issue = 1| pages = 113–137| doi = 10.2307/2805564| jstor=2805564}}</ref><ref name=Niklas1980>{{cite journal| author = Niklas, K.J. |author2=Pratt, L.M.  | year = 1980| title = Evidence for Lignin-Like Constituents in Early Silurian (Llandoverian) Plant Fossils| journal = Science | volume = 209 | issue = 4454| pages = 396–7 | doi = 10.1126/science.209.4454.396| pmid = 17747811 | bibcode=1980Sci...209..396N}}</ref>。

曾經有發現過入侵維管束植物的原杉藻菌絲化石<ref name=Hueber2001/>，也曾有發現有動物居住於原杉藻其中的證據：動物挖掘造成的空洞遍布於原杉藻枝幹內，有理論認為這些動物的寄生可能導致原杉藻的滅絕<ref name=Hueber2001/>。然而在泥盆紀早期至晚期均有節肢動物在原杉藻枝幹內鑽孔的化石痕跡發現，這代表原杉藻的族群有能力承受這些動物所帶來的傷害達數百萬年<ref name=Labandeira2007>{{cite journal| author = Labandeira, C.| year = 2007| title = The origin of herbivory on land: Initial patterns of plant tissue consumption by arthropods| journal = Insect Science| volume = 14| issue = 4| pages = 259–275| doi = 10.1111/j.1744-7917.2007.00152.x}}</ref>。之後植物才開始演化出木質化莖，這些動物也漸轉移到植物上鑽孔<ref name=Labandeira2007/>。

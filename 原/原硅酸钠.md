'''原硅酸钠'''（Sodium orthosilicate）是一种无机化合物，化学式为{{Chem|Na|4|SiO|4}}。它是{{le|原硅酸钠|Sodium silicate|钠的硅酸盐}}之一，是{{le|正硅酸盐|Orthosilicate|原硅酸盐}}，形式上是不稳定的[[原硅酸|原硅酸]]（{{Chem|H|4|SiO|4}}）的[[盐_(化学)|盐]]。<ref name="baur">Werner H. Baur, Erich Halwax, and Horst Völlenkle (1986): "Comparison of the crystal structures of sodium orthosilicate, {[chem|Na|4|SiO|4, and sodium orthogermanate, {{Chem|Na|4|GeO|4}}". ''Monatshefte für Chemie'', volume 117, issue 6–7, pages 793–797. {{Doi|10.1007/BF00810070}}</ref> <ref name="barker">M,. G. Barker, P. G.Gadd (1981): "The preparation and crystal structure of sodium orthosilicate, {{Chem|Na|4|SiO|4}}." ''Journal of Chemical Research'', London] Chemical Society, volume 9, pages S:274 (synopse), M:3446-3466 (main).</ref> <ref name="sche">J. F. Schairer and N. L. Bowen (1956): "The system {{Chem|Na|2|O}}—{{Chem|Al|2|O|3}}—{{Chem|SiO|2}}". ''American Journal of Science'', volume 254, issue 3, pages 129-195 {{Doi|10.2475/ajs.254.3.129}}</ref>

== 用途 ==
原硅酸钠被认为是[[油田|油田]][[水驱|水驱]]中的[[表面张力|界面张力]]降低添加剂，用于提高[[油|油]]的提取率。在实验室环境中，发现它对于某些类型的油比[[氢氧化钠|氢氧化钠]]更有效。 <ref name="camp">Thomas C. Campbell (11977): "A Comparison Of Sodium Orthosilicate And Sodium Hydroxide For Alkaline Waterflooding." Society of Petroleum engineers, SPE California Regional Meeting 13–15 April 1977, Bakersfield; document IDSPE-6514-MS
{{Doi|10.2118/6514-MS}}</ref> 

原硅酸钠已发现可形成稳定的[[铁|铁]]和[[钢|钢]]的表面[[Ferrate|高铁酸盐]]薄膜作为[[腐蚀|抗腐蚀]]处理。<ref name="harri"> John R. Harrison（1954）：“用高铁酸盐溶液处理金属的方法”。转让给[https://patents.google.com/patent/US2850415A/en EIdu] Pont de Nemours的美国专利[https://patents.google.com/patent/US2850415A/en US2850415A] </ref> 

== 自然矿存 ==
在自然界中未发现原硅酸钠。然而，最近在[[科拉半岛|科拉半岛]]发现了化学上相关的矿物[[chesnokovite|chesnokovite]]，二钠盐[{{Chem|Na|+}}]<sub>2</sub>[{{Chem|SiO|2|(OH)|2|2-}}]·8{{Chem|H|2|O}}。<ref name="chuk">I. V. Pekov, N. V. Chukanov, A. E. Zadov, N. V. Zubkova, and D. Yu. Pushcharovsky (2007): "Chesnokovite, {{Chem|Na|2}}[{{Chem|SiO|2|(OH)|2}}] · 8{{Chem|H|2|O}}, the first natural sodium orthosilicate from the Lovozero alkaline pluton, Kola Peninsula: Description and crystal structure of a new mineral species", ''Geology of Ore Deposits'', volume 49, issue 8, pages 727–738. {{Doi|10.1134/S1075701507080077}}</ref>

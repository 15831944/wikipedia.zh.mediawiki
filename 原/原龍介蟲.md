'''原龍介蟲'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Proliserpula''}}），又名'''後蛇蟲'''，是[[龍介蟲科|龍介蟲科]]下一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[環節動物|環節動物]]，生存於[[晚白堊世|晚白堊世]]的深[[水|水]]區域。<ref name = "COL">{{cite web |url= http://www.catalogueoflife.org/annual-checklist/2011/search/all/key/proliserpula/match/1|title= Species 2000 & ITIS Catalogue of Life: 2011 Annual Checklist.|author= Bisby F.A., Roskov Y.R., Orrell T.M., Nicolson D., Paglinawan L.E., Bailly N., Kirk P.M., Bourgoin T., Baillargeon G., Ouvrard D. (red.)|year= 2011|publisher= Species 2000: Reading, UK.|accessdate= 24 september 2012}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
原龍介蟲的體管總是附著在堅硬的基底上，以不規則的螺旋狀盤卷，沿[[中線|中線]]有條狹窄的[[背|背]]。其孔眼是[[圓|圓]]的，孔眼的側面顯示出生長線。<ref>{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=40 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

'''原初生命體'''（{{lang-en|protocell 或 protobiont}}）被认为有可能是[[原核细胞|原核细胞]]的祖先，是由膜或膜样的结构包围非生物生产的[[有机分子|有机分子]]的集合体。<ref name="Chen 2010">{{cite journal |title=From Self-Assembled Vesicles to Protocells |journal=Cold Spring Harb Perspect Biol. |date=July 2010 |first=Irene A. |last=Chen |first2=Peter |last2=Walde |volume=2 |issue=7 |pmc=2890201 |doi=10.1101/cshperspect.a002170 |url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2890201/pdf/cshperspect-ORI-a002170.pdf |format=PDF |pmid=20519344}}</ref>其表现出一些与生命相关的属性，如简单的[[繁殖|繁殖]]，[[代谢|代谢]]与兴奋性。有人认为，它们是在地球上[[生命起源|生命起源]]的一个关键步骤。现已有实验证明，它们可以在与早期地球上的环境条件相似的情况下被自发形成，形成[[脂质体|脂质体]]和微球 。<ref name="Exploring">{{cite web |url=http://exploringorigins.org/protocells.html |title=Exploring Life's Origins - Protocells |author=National Science Foundation |year=2013 |accessdate=2014-03-18 }}</ref><ref name="Chen 2006">{{cite journal |title=The Emergence of Cells During the Origin of Life |journal=Science |date=8 December 2006 |first=Irene A. |last=Chen |volume=314 |issue=5805 |pages=1558-1559 |doi=10.1126/science.1137541 |url=http://www.sciencemag.org/content/314/5805/1558.full }}</ref><ref name="Discover 2004">{{cite journal |title=What Came Before DNA? |journal=Discover Magazine |date=26 June 2004 |first=Carl |last=Zimmer |pages=1-5 |url=http://discovermagazine.com/2004/jun/cover }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Rasmussen |first=Steen |url=http://astrobiology.com/2014/10/scientists-create-possible-precursor-to-life.html |title=Scientists Create Possible Precursor to Life |work=A Letters Journal Exploring the Frontiers of Physics. Volume 107, Number 2, July 2014. |publisher=Astrobiology Web |date=2 July 2014 |accessdate=2014-10-24 }}</ref>它有类似的细胞中发现的[[磷脂双分子层|磷脂双分子层]]的膜结构 。

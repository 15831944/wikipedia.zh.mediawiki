'''工农斗争协会'''（{{lang-en|Mazdoor Kisan Sangram Samiti}}，缩写为MKSS；{{lang-hi|मजदूर किसान संग्राम समिति}}）是印度[[比哈尔邦|比哈尔邦]]的群众组织。MKSS由Vinayan博士、Arvind Ji和其他群众领导人于1981年创立。 MKSS的以下主要由达利特组成。<ref>Menon, Nivedita, and Aditya Nigam. ''[https://books.google.com/books?id=vujXq2dA6YAC&pg=PA120 Power and Contestation: India Since 1989]''. London [etc.]: Zed Books, 2007. p. 120</ref>

MKSS从比哈尔的贾雅普拉卡什·纳拉扬的反紧急状态斗争中涌现出来。Vinayan博士和Arvind Ji博士是JP运动的领导人，受Mahamaya Prasad Sinha的影响。他们成立了MKSS，为土地改革和最低工资而奋斗。[[杰哈纳巴德|杰哈纳巴德]]和[[加雅县|加雅县]]为运动的震中。印度共产党（马列）团结组织（从1982年起，[[印度共产党（马列）党团结|印度共产党（马列）党团结]]）与MKSS一致。印度共产党（马列）党团结作为MKSS的武装小队，面对地主的私人军队（senas）。<ref name="i2">Nedumpara, Jose J. ''[https://books.google.com/books?id=pINgUv_hxcYC&pg=PA117 Political Economy and Class Contradictions: A Study]''. New Delhi: Anmol Publications, 2004. pp. 116-117</ref>

1984年，MKSS书记克里希纳·辛格（Krishna Singh）被地主军队杀死。<ref>Thakor, Harsh. ''[http://www.antiimperialista.org/en/node/1989 Bihar]''</ref> 1986年4月，警察在阿瓦尔的一个MKSS集会上开火，造成23名贫苦农民死亡。<ref name="arwalmassacre">{{cite news|author=Farzand Ahmed|title=Arwal massacre: Bihar CM Bindeshwari Dubey justify police action|accessdate=13 June 2003|publisher=India Today|date=31 May 1986 | url = https://www.indiatoday.in/magazine/indiascope/story/19860531-arwal-massacre-bihar-cm-bindeshwari-dubey-justify-police-action-800894-1986-05-31}}</ref> 继阿尔瓦尔大屠杀之后，MKSS被禁止。<ref name="mkssbanned">{{cite news|author=Farzand Ahmed|title=CM Bindeshwari Dubey's decision to ban MKSS raises doubts about his grip on Bihar|accessdate=13 June 2003|publisher=India Today|date=15 September 1986 | url = https://www.indiatoday.in/magazine/indiascope/story/19860915-cm-bindeshwari-dubey-decision-to-ban-mkss-raises-doubts-about-his-grip-on-bihar-801232-1986-09-15}}</ref>

1987年6月，MKSS被分成两部分。Vinayan博士变得越来越敌视印共（马列）党团结，理由是该党进行的杀戮破坏了MKSS的形象。一个MKSS派系仍然忠于Vinayan博士，另一个由Arvind Ji领导，保留与印共（马列）党团结的联系。Vinayan博士派系重建为Jan Mukti Andolan。<ref name="i2"/> 印共（马列）党团结重组农民阵线为Mazdoor Kisan Sangrami Parishad。然而，名字'Sangram Samiti'或简称'Sangram'仍然留在比哈尔邦的日常对话中。<ref>Kumar, Rabindra. ''[https://books.google.com/books?id=VPaK3uQwgkIC&pg=PA59 Impact of Rural Development on Scheduled Castes]''. New Delhi, India: Anmol Publ, 2002. p. 59</ref>

== 道路運輸 ==
[[File:Andhra_Pradesh_NH_5_Highway_India.jpg|缩略图]]
[[內洛爾|內洛爾]]的道路總長度為1189.95公里。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://centralapp.cdma.ap.gov.in:8080/CDMAAPTaxesInfo/RoadDetails.jsp|title=Details of Roads in each ULB of Andhra Pradesh|accessdate=2017-11-02|website=Municipal Administration and Urban Development Department|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160801101300/http://centralapp.cdma.ap.gov.in:8080/CDMAAPTaxesInfo/RoadDetails.jsp|archivedate=2016-08-01}}</ref> 內洛爾市政府于2017年建议修建更多外环路及用于減少交通擠塞的内部道路。并建議構建共長72公里，寬200英尺的外环路。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thehansindia.com/posts/index/Andhra-Pradesh/2017-07-06/Nellore-Urban-Development-Authority-to-start-fresh-work-on-Outer-Ring-Road/310500|title=Nellore Urban Development Authority to start fresh work on Outer Ring Road|accessdate=2017-11-02}}</ref>國道16號，亞洲高速公路45和金四邊形环绕并链接城市。

內洛爾城市道路与{{tsl|en|National Highway (India)|印度國道}}连接，在城市旁边繞過的[[國道5號_(印度)|國道5號]]是{{tsl|en|Asian Highway 45|亞洲公路45号线}}和{{tsl|en|Golden Quadrilateral|金四邊形}}的一部分。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.newindianexpress.com/states/andhra_pradesh/NHAI-Under-Pressure-to-Lay-Another-Bypass-Road/2014/08/25/article2397230.ece|title=NHAI Under Pressure to Lay Another Bypass Road|date=2017-11-02|work=The New Indian Express|accessdate=2016-06-04}}</ref>通過[[國道5號_(印度)|國道5號]]连接至[[維傑亞瓦達|維傑亞瓦達]]（280公里），[[蒂鲁帕蒂|蒂魯帕蒂]]（124公里）和[[清奈|清奈]]（175公里），透过亞洲公路45号线连接至[[海得拉巴_(印度)|海得拉巴]]（454公里），[[國道4號_(印度)|國道4號]]则连接至邦加羅爾。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://apit.ap.gov.in/district-profiles/District%20Profile%20-%20Sri%20Potti%20Sri%20Ramulu%20Nellore%20District.pdf|title=District Profile-Nellore district|accessdate=2017-11-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160414234515/http://apit.ap.gov.in/district-profiles/District%20Profile%20-%20Sri%20Potti%20Sri%20Ramulu%20Nellore%20District.pdf|archive-date=2016-04-14|dead-url=yes}}</ref>共有一条國道，兩條國道繞道及五條安得拉邦高速公路途径该城市。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/andhra-pradesh/sc-order-liquor-licensees-face-problems-in-nellore/article19200122.ece|title=SC order: liquor licensees face problems in Nellore|accessdate=2017-11-02}}</ref>

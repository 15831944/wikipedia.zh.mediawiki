在[[幾何學|幾何學]]中，'''內側菱形三十面體'''，又稱'''小星形三十面體'''<ref>Coxeter, H. S. M. Regular Polytopes, 3rd ed. New York: Dover, 1973. ISBN 978-0486614809 p. 102-103</ref><ref>{{mathworld | urlname = MedialRhombicTriacontahedron| title = Medial Rhombic Triacontahedron}} （[http://mathworld.wolfram.com/MedialRhombicTriacontahedron.html 原始]內容[https://web.archive.org/web/20160801064119/http://mathworld.wolfram.com/MedialRhombicTriacontahedron.html 存檔]於2016-09-01）.</ref>是一種菱形三十面體的星形多面體<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.software3d.com/MedialRTC.php | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304101204/http://www.software3d.com/MedialRTC.php | archivedate =2016-03-04|title = Medial Rhombic Triacontahedron | publisher = software3d.com}}</ref>，由30個全等且互相相交的菱形組成。

[[均勻多面體|均勻多面體]][[十二合十二面体|十二合十二面体]]的[[對偶多面體|對偶多面體]]。

== 性質 ==
內側菱形三十面體由30個面、60條邊和24個頂點組成，其30個面皆由菱形組成。內側菱形三十面體有兩種頂角，一種由菱形的銳角組成，頂點圖為五邊形；另一種由菱形的鈍角組成，頂點圖為五角星，其中，頂點圖為五角星的頂點藏在圖形內部<ref>Wenninger, M. J. Dual Models. Cambridge, England: Cambridge University Press, pp. 41 and 46, 1983. ISBN 978-0521245241</ref>。

=== 頂點座標 ===
對偶邊長為1的內側菱形三十面體的頂點座標為<ref name="dmccooeydata">{{Cite web|url=http://dmccooey.com/polyhedra/MedialRhombicTriacontahedron.txt|title=Data of Medial Rhombic Triacontahedron | publisher = dmccooey.com | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160901025243/http://dmccooey.com/polyhedra/MedialRhombicTriacontahedron.txt | archivedate =2016-09-01}}</ref>：
:<math>(\pm \frac{3}{4},   0,   \pm \frac{3(1 + \sqrt{5})}{8})</math>
:<math>(  \pm \frac{3(1 + \sqrt{5})}{8}, \pm \frac{3}{4},   0)</math>
:<math>(  0,   \pm \frac{3(1 + \sqrt{5})}{8}, \pm \frac{3}{4})</math>
:<math>(  \pm \frac{3(\sqrt{5} - 1)}{8},   0, \pm \frac{3}{4})</math>
:<math>(\pm \frac{3}{4},   \pm \frac{3(\sqrt{5} - 1)}{8},   0)</math>
:<math>(  0, \pm \frac{3}{4},   \pm \frac{3(\sqrt{5} - 1)}{8})</math>

=== 二面角 ===
大六角二十四面體僅有一種二面角，為兩個菱形的棱之交角，其值為負二分之一的反餘弦值<ref>{{cite web|url = http://dmccooey.com/polyhedra/MedialRhombicTriacontahedron.html|title=Versi-Regular Polyhedra: Medial Rhombic Triacontahedron |publisher=dmccooey.com | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160324082424/http://dmccooey.com/polyhedra/MedialRhombicTriacontahedron.html | archivedate =2016-03-24}}</ref>：
:<math>\cos^{-1} (-\frac{1}{2}) = \frac{2\pi}{3} \approx 2.0943 = 120 ^{\circ}</math>

== 相關多面體 ==

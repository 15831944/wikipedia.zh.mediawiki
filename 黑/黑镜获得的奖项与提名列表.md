《'''[[黑镜|黑镜]]'''》（{{lang-en|''Black Mirror''}}）是一部由[[查理·布鲁克|查理·布鲁克]]创作的英国科幻独立单元电视剧。该剧首播于2011年12月4日，前两季及特别集《白色圣诞节》在英国[[第四台_(英國)|第四台]]播出，之后两季在美国流媒体平台[[网飞|网飞]]上播出。该剧前四季共19集，另外一部互动电影《[[黑镜：潘达斯奈基|潘达斯奈基]]》也已上映，第五季现已开拍<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ew.com/tv/2018/03/05/black-mirror-season-5/|title=''Black Mirror'' renewed for season 5 by Netflix|accessdate=4 August 2018|author=Hibberd|date=5 March 2018|first=James|work=[[Entertainment_Weekly|Entertainment Weekly]]|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180620232427/https://ew.com/tv/2018/03/05/black-mirror-season-5/|archivedate=20 June 2018}}</ref>。受电视剧《[[迷离境界|迷离境界]]》的启发，《黑镜》的每一集都是独立的，探讨了科技及其副作用的共同主题<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2011/dec/01/charlie-brooker-dark-side-gadget-addiction-black-mirror|title=Charlie Brooker: the dark side of our gadget addiction|date=1 December 2011|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|accessdate=4 August 2018|location=London}}</ref>。

《黑镜》获得了评论界的广泛好评，其获得了79项提名，其中21项获得奖。最受欢迎的剧集是获得四项[[艾美奖|艾美奖]]的《{{Le|卡利斯特号|USS Callister}}》和获得两项艾美奖的《[[圣朱尼皮罗|圣朱尼皮罗]]》。由于演员很少出现在超过一集的剧集中，因此只有编剧查理·布鲁克、制片设计师乔尔·柯林斯、编辑肯尼·克拉克和迈克尔·马鲁萨斯因本片获得了两个奖项。该剧获得了[[英国电影学院奖|英国电影学院奖]]14提2中，艾美奖13提5中。

== 英国电影学院奖 ==
[[英国电影学院奖|英国电影学院奖]]（{{Lang|en|BAFTA}}）是1947年于创立英国电影学院名下以表彰英国电影产业的杰出贡献的奖项In 1958, they merged with the Guild of Television Producers and Directors, and began to give awards to British television programmes.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bafta.org/heritage/history|title=History and heritage of BAFTA|publisher=[[British_Academy_of_Film_and_Television_Arts|British Academy of Film and Television Arts]]|accessdate=3 August 2018}}</ref> In 2000, television awards were split into the [[British_Academy_Television_Awards|British Academy Television Awards]], given for work in production, and the [[British_Academy_Television_Craft_Awards|British Academy Television Craft Awards]], presented for technical achievements.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bafta.org/television/craft-awards/craft-awards-ten-years-of-talent|title=Craft Awards: Ten Years of Talent|publisher=[[British_Academy_of_Film_and_Television_Arts|British Academy of Film and Television Arts]]|date=7 April 2009|accessdate=3 August 2018}}</ref>

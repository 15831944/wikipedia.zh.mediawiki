'''黑遊蛇'''又名'''黑遊蛇'''、'''縊縮游蛇'''、'''縊縮遊蛇'''（學名：'''''Coluber constrictor'''''）是[[游蛇科|游蛇科]]'''游蛇屬'''下的唯一一種，總共11個亞種。

== 亞種 ==
[[File:Coluber_constrictorPCCP20030612-1115B.jpg|thumb]]
* [[北部黑游蛇|北部黑游蛇]]（''Coluber constrictor constrictor''） <small>[[Carl_Linnaeus|Linnaeus]], 1758</small> - northern black racer
* ''Coluber constrictor anthicus'' <small>([[Edward_Drinker_Cope|Cope]], 1862)</small> - buttermilk racer
* ''Coluber constrictor etheridgei'' <small>[[:fr:Larry_David_Wilson|Wilson]], 1970</small> - tan racer
* ''Coluber constrictor flaviventris'' <small>[[Thomas_Say|Say]], 1823</small> - eastern yellow-bellied racer
* ''Coluber constrictor foxii'' <small>([[Spencer_Fullerton_Baird|Baird]] & [[Charles_Frédéric_Girard|Girard]], 1853)</small> - blue racer
* ''Coluber constrictor helvigularis'' <small>[[Walter_Auffenberg|Auffenberg]], 1955</small> - brown-chinned racer
* ''Coluber constrictor latrunculus'' <small>Wilson, 1970</small> - black-masked racer
* ''Coluber constrictor oaxaca'' <small>([[Giorgio_Jan|Jan]], 1863)</small> - Mexican racer
* ''Coluber constrictor paludicola'' <small>Auffenberg & [[Lewis_Hall_Babbitt|Babbitt]], 1955</small> - Everglades racer
* ''Coluber constrictor priapus'' <small>[[Emmett_Reid_Dunn|Dunn]] & [[G._C._Wood|Wood]], 1939</small> - southern black racer
* ''Coluber constrictor mormon'' <small>(Baird & Girard, 1852)</small> - western yellow-bellied racer<ref>[http://dsp-psd.pwgsc.gc.ca/Collection/CW69-14-413-2005E.pdf Eastern and Western Yellow-bellied Racers], COSEWIC Assessment and Update Status Report</ref>

== 分布 ==
遍布[[美國|美國]]各地，位於[[洛磯山脈|洛磯山脈]]以東，還有位於[[加拿大|加拿大]]北部，南部則是[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]、[[瓜地馬拉|瓜地馬拉]]、[[貝里斯|貝里斯]]。

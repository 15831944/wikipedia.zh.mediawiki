'''黑素皮质素'''（{{lang-en|melanocortin}}），也称'''促黑激素'''、'''促黑色素細胞激素'''、'''黑細胞促素'''等，是一类[[肽类激素|肽类激素]]，其中包含[[促腎上腺皮質激素|促腎上腺皮質激素]]（ACTH）和各类[[黑色素细胞刺激素|黑色素细胞刺激素]]（MSH）。<ref name="Gantz_2003">{{cite journal | vauthors = Gantz I, Fong TM | title = The melanocortin system | journal = American Journal of Physiology. Endocrinology and Metabolism | volume = 284 | issue = 3 | pages = E468–74 | date = March 2003 | pmid = 12556347 | doi = 10.1152/ajpendo.00434.2002 | url = http://ajpendo.physiology.org/cgi/content/full/284/3/E468 }}</ref>这类激素由[[前腦啡黑細胞促素皮促素|前腦啡黑細胞促素皮促素]]（POMC）在[[腦下垂體|腦下垂體]]产生<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Raffin-Sanson ML, de Keyzer Y, Bertagna X | title = Proopiomelanocortin, a polypeptide precursor with multiple functions: from physiology to pathological conditions | journal = European Journal of Endocrinology | volume = 149 | issue = 2 | pages = 79–90 | date = August 2003 | pmid = 12887283 | doi = 10.1530/eje.0.1490079 }}</ref>，通过结合并激活{{tsl|en|melanocortin receptor|黑素皮质素受体|}}（MCR）产生作用。<ref name="Gantz_2003" />{{tsl|en|melanocortin receptor|黑素皮质素受体|}}（MCR）共有5种，且均为[[G蛋白偶联受体|G蛋白偶联受体]]：在大脑中仅表达MC3R与MC4R两种受体，二者配体为α-MSH，而{{tsl|en|Agouti-related peptide|刺鼠肽基因相关蛋白|}}（AgRP）是其内源性拮抗剂。<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Yoon YR, Baik JH | title = Melanocortin 4 Receptor and Dopamine D2 Receptor Expression in Brain Areas Involved in Food Intake | language = en | journal = Endocrinology and Metabolism | volume = 30 | issue = 4 | pages = 576–83 | date = December 2015 | pmid = 26790386 | pmc = 4722414 | doi = 10.3803/enm.2015.30.4.576 }}</ref> 

== 功能 ==
黑素皮质素系统在哺乳动物体内与[[瘦蛋白|瘦蛋白]]等竞争，共同调节了摄食活动。<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Zhou Y, Rui L | title = Leptin signaling and leptin resistance | journal = Frontiers of Medicine | volume = 7 | issue = 2 | pages = 207–22 | date = June 2013 | pmid = 23580174 | pmc = 4069066 | doi = 10.1007/s11684-013-0263-5 }}</ref>目前已知的释放黑素皮质素的神经仅有弓状核，位于下丘脑。弓状核其中有POMC神经细胞和AgRP神经细胞等神经元<ref name="Klok_2007" />。当POMC神经释放α-MSH，食欲减退；而当AgRP神经细胞释放{{tsl|en|Agouti-related peptide|刺鼠肽基因相关蛋白|AgRP}}，食欲则增强。

[[瘦蛋白|瘦蛋白]]是一种负责调控饱腹感的蛋白，{{tsl|en|Ghrelin|食欲刺激素|}}则是一种负责刺激食欲的激素，二者共同调节了大脑内黑素皮质素系统的上游通路。<ref name="Klok_2007">{{cite journal | vauthors = Klok MD, Jakobsdottir S, Drent ML | title = The role of leptin and ghrelin in the regulation of food intake and body weight in humans: a review | journal = Obesity Reviews | volume = 8 | issue = 1 | pages = 21–34 | date = January 2007 | pmid = 17212793 | doi = 10.1111/j.1467-789x.2006.00270.x }}</ref>这两种激素同会参与黑素皮质素在内的激素调节，而瘦蛋白-黑素皮质素之间在大脑内的平衡如果失去，则会引起早发的肥胖在内的各类新陈代谢类疾病，还将抑制免疫功能。<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Nunziata A, Borck G, Funcke JB, Kohlsdorf K, Brandt S, Hinney A, Moepps B, Gierschik P, Debatin KM, Fischer-Posovszky P, Wabitsch M | title = Estimated prevalence of potentially damaging variants in the leptin gene | journal = Molecular and Cellular Pediatrics | volume = 4 | issue = 1 | pages = 10 | date = November 2017 | pmid = 29101506 | pmc = 5670095 | doi = 10.1186/s40348-017-0074-x }}</ref>

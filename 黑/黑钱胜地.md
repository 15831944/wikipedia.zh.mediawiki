《'''黑钱胜地'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Ozark'''''}}）是一部由{{link-en|比尔·迪比克|Bill Dubuque}}创作的[[电视剧|电视剧]]。<ref>[http://www.inquisitr.com/2846525/new-netflix-series-called-ozark-starring-jason-bateman-slated-to-debut-in-2017/ New Netflix Series 'Ozark' Starring Jason Bateman Slated to Debut in 2017]</ref><ref>[http://deadline.com/2016/02/netflix-jason-bateman-ozark-tv-series-mrc-1201709182/ Netflix Picks Up Jason Bateman’s Drama Series ‘Ozark’ From MRC]</ref>[[杰森·贝特曼|杰森·贝特曼]]主演，并导演了半数剧集。<ref>[http://www.slashfilm.com/jason-bateman-netflix-show-ozarks/ Jason Bateman’s New Series Goes To Netflix, Will Be Dark Dangerous Drama]</ref>本剧主要讲述了贝特曼饰演的理财师马蒂·伯德（Marty Byrde）与[[劳拉·琳尼|劳拉·琳尼]]饰演的其妻子温迪。<ref>[http://deadline.com/2016/07/laura-linney-star-jason-bateman-netflix-drama-series-ozark-1201783401/ Laura Linney To Star In Jason Bateman’s Netflix Drama Series ‘Ozark’]</ref>他们一家突然从芝加哥郊区搬到奥沙克湖的度假胜地，<ref>[http://variety.com/2016/tv/news/ozark-cast-netflix-julia-garner-jason-bateman-laura-linney-1201900108/ Netflix Drama ‘Ozark’ Adds Julia Garner Opposite Jason Bateman and Laura Linney]</ref>并必须还清墨西哥毒枭的债。第一季于2017年7月21日在[[Netflix|Netflix]]首播。<ref>[https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/tv/news/netflix-ozark-tv-series-new-drug-trade-drama-jason-bateman-laura-linney-mexico-a7755401.html Ozark trailer: Exclusive look at Netflix's gritty, dark new drug trade drama]</ref>

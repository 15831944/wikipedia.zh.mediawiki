'''照济河'''（{{lang-my|ဇော်ဂျီမြစ်}}），一译'''藻基河'''，是缅甸东部的一条河。 该河流经[[掸邦|掸邦]]山脉丘陵地带，<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://mandalar.net/gallery/2007/11/29/zawgyi-river-and-foothills-of-shan-mountain-range/ |title=Mandalar.net |accessdate=2008-01-16 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080205182315/http://mandalar.net/gallery/2007/11/29/zawgyi-river-and-foothills-of-shan-mountain-range/ |archivedate=2008-02-05 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>是[[米界河|米界河]]的支流，在[[皎克西|皎克西]]以北20公里的良宾达汇流。<ref name="NF47-09">[http://www.lib.utexas.edu/maps/ams/burma/txu-oclc-6924198-nf47-9.jpg NF 47-09 "Mandalay, Burma"] U.S. Army Map Service topographic map 1:250,000, Series U-542, first printing December 1959</ref>

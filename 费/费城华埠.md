'''费城华埠'''（Chinatown）是[[费城|费城]]的[[唐人街|唐人街]]。 費城華埠發展會(Philadelphia Chinatown Development Corporation, PCDC)支持费城华埠。

19世纪粤移民创造洗衣店和饭店。<ref name="Wallace3">Wallace, David J. "Near Philadelphia's Chinatown, 51 New Homes." ''[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]''. 1998年3月8日. [http://www.nytimes.com/1998/03/08/realestate/near-philadelphia-s-chinatown-51-new-homes.html?pagewanted=3&src=pm 3].</ref> 1968年費城華埠發展會开设. 因为費城華埠發展會开设了, 所以社会团体组长和公司组长有打更权。<!--Original English sentence says "The Philadelphia Chinatown Development Corporation was formed in 1968.  This gave community and business leaders more say in matters of local development.--><ref>"[http://www.chinatown-pcdc.org/about_history.htm History] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090414060707/http://www.chinatown-pcdc.org/about_history.htm |date=2009-04-14 }}." 费城华埠.</ref>

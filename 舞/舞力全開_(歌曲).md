== 作詞與靈感 ==
《舞力全開》起初由卡卡與[[阿肯|阿肯]]與歌曲的製作人[[RedOne|RedOne]]合作作詞。在雜誌《{{tsl|en|Heat (magazine)|Heat (雜誌)|Heat}}》的採訪中，卡卡解釋這首歌的作詞靈感。她說:「當時我身陷酒醉。我花了10分鐘與RedOne一起作詞，這是我第一次在好萊塢錄音室裡工作。感覺頗新鮮的，待在這樣一個裝有巨大揚聲器的巨大房間中。」<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.dailytelegraph.com.au/entertainment/lady-gagas-poker-face-about-bisexuality-just-dance-written-in-10-minutes/story-e6frewyr-1225699697459|title=Lady GaGa's Poker Face about bisexuality, Just Dance written in 10 minutes|last=Reporter|first=Daily|date=April 17, 2009|work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph_(Sydney)|The Daily Telegraph]]|accessdate=April 28, 2009|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091013112831/http://www.dailytelegraph.com.au/entertainment/lady-gagas-poker-face-about-bisexuality-just-dance-written-in-10-minutes/story-e6frewyr-1225699697459|archivedate=October 13, 2009}}</ref> 卡卡於2008年1月為《舞力全開》作詞，她表示這作品「投入了大量心血，而最初，有一大群人認為這不會成功。」<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/artists/lady-gaga/news/91020-lady-gaga-interview|title=Lady GaGa Interview|last=Madsen|first=Ben|date=January 15, 2009|publisher=MTV|=|accessdate=April 28, 2009|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090428095033/http://www.mtv.co.uk/artists/lady-gaga/news/91020-lady-gaga-interview|archivedate=April 28, 2009}}</ref> 之後卡卡表示：

<blockquote>
「這首歌拯救了我。我過去曾深陷在紐約黑暗的一面中，我曾如此鬱悶並整天泡在酒吧中。我坐上飛機前往洛杉磯去製作我的音樂並一次性的試著寫出一首足以改變我人生的歌，而我做到了。從此我再也沒有回去，我放下了我的男友，我的公寓套房。不再回頭。而我的母親為我親身打理了這些事。」<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2009/sep/06/lady-gaga-popandrock|title=Soundtrack of my life: Lady Gaga|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|publisher=[[Guardian_News_and_Media|Guardian News and Media]]|last=Thomson|first=Graeme|date=September 6, 2009|accessdate=May 6, 2010|location=London|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130922011757/http://www.theguardian.com/music/2009/sep/06/lady-gaga-popandrock|archivedate=September 22, 2013|df=mdy-all}}</ref>
</blockquote>

在{{tsl|en|Contactmusic.com}}的採訪中，卡卡解釋《舞力全開》是首快樂的歌曲並用於鼓勵歷經人生低潮: 例如失去工作或房子等等的人。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.contactmusic.com/news.nsf/story/gaga-i-wrote-hit-in-10-minutes_1100865|title=I wrote hit in 10 minutes|first=Contactmusic|last=Press|date=April 17, 2009|publisher=[[Contactmusic.com|Contactmusic.com]]|accessdate=April 28, 2009|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110606082915/http://www.contactmusic.com/news.nsf/story/gaga-i-wrote-hit-in-10-minutes_1100865|archivedate=June 6, 2011}}</ref> 她曾進一步與{{tsl|en|Artistdirect}}解釋她希望以《舞力全開》寫出一首美麗的歌。當被問到為何《舞力全開》如此出名時，卡卡表示她認為「每個人都在尋找著能夠完全表達我們靈魂和心中的喜悅並享受美好時光的歌曲，而這首歌就是其中之一。這首歌感覺真的很棒，而當你聽下去時，它也會使你的內心也感到雀躍。它就是這麼簡單。我認為這首歌能夠直達心房並不是什麼難事。因為它是以心靈為主題的歌曲。」<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.artistdirect.com/nad/news/article/0,,4931544,00.html|title=Interview: Lady Gaga|date=December 30, 2009|accessdate=April 27, 2009|publisher=[[Artistdirect.com|Artistdirect.com]]|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090202083110/http://artistdirect.com/nad/news/article/0%2C%2C4931544%2C00.html|archivedate=February 2, 2009}}</ref> 2010年3月，《舞力全開》與《[[撲克臉_(女神卡卡歌曲)|撲克臉]]》、《{{tsl|en|Monster (Lady Gaga song)|魔女 (女神卡卡歌曲)|魔女}}》、《[[羅曼死|羅曼死]]》作為節奏電子系列遊戲《{{tsl|en|Rock Band|搖滾樂隊}}》的[[下載包|下載包]]「Lady Gaga Pack 01」。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rockband.com/news/lady-gaga-eric-cartman-dlc|title=Lady GAGA Makes Her Debut On Rock Band With A Hot Four Pack!|publisher=RockBand.com|date=March 10, 2010|accessdate=March 11, 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100311205152/http://www.rockband.com/news/lady-gaga-eric-cartman-dlc|archivedate=March 11, 2010|df=}}</ref>

==創作與構思==

《舞力全開》是一首有節奏感的[[合成器流行|合成器流行]]和[[流行舞曲|流行舞曲]]類歌曲。歌曲結合了帶有輕快[[節拍|節拍]]的合成樂，並在提升了[[電音|電音]]部份的同時加入了當代[[節奏藍調|節奏藍調]]的鼓舞性節奏。<ref name="guardian"/> 歌曲製作時音調以[[升C小調|升C小調]]為主並加入了每分119[[速度_(音樂)|拍]]的適度舞蹈節奏。卡卡的聲域跨度於最低G{{music|sharp}}<sub>3</sub>至最高C{{music|sharp}}<sub>5</sub>。<ref name="sheet">{{cite web|url=http://www.musicnotes.com/sheetmusic/mtdFPE.asp?ppn=MN0070084|title=Digital Sheet Music: Lady Gaga 'Just Dance'|publisher=Music Notes.com. [[Alfred_Publishing|Alfred Publishing]]|accessdate=April 24, 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151105205409/http://www.amazon.com/dp/B001MRMED6|archivedate=November 5, 2015}}</ref> 歌曲在卡卡在合成樂帶起的輕快節奏下喊出「RedOne」時開始。《舞力全開》有著 C{{music|sharp}}m–E–B–F{{music|sharp}}m的基本{{tsl|en|Chord progression|和弦進程}}。<ref name="sheet"/> 在歌曲中，奧多尼斯以跟卡卡相同的聲域演唱，<ref name="sheet"/> 接著是卡卡帶有節奏藍調風格的橋段，<ref name="guardian"/> 之後，在奧多尼斯擔任和聲並提供行進節奏時重複唱出副歌。並在字詞「dance」的回音下結束。<ref name="sheet"/>

就歌詞上，《舞力全開》以歌詞「舞池裡發生了什麼? / 我愛這旋律，寶貝，但我已經無法直走」<ref group="注"> 原文：「What's going on on the floor? / I love this record, baby but I can't see straight anymor」</ref> 帶出不完全的詼諧視角。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.avclub.com/articles/lady-gaga-the-fame,6744/|title=Lady Gaga:The Fame|last=Koski|first=Genevieve|date=November 10, 2008|work=[[The_A.V._Club|The A.V. Club]]|publisher=Onion LLC|accessdate=April 28, 2009|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090916224422/http://www.avclub.com/articles/lady-gaga-the-fame%2C6744/|archivedate=September 16, 2009}}</ref> 歌詞整體描述在派對中完全的[[酒精中毒|酒精中毒]]。<ref name="songfacts">{{cite web|url=http://www.songfacts.com/detail.php?id=12724|title=Just Dance by Lady Gaga Songfacts|publisher=Songfacts.com|accessdate=May 3, 2009|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090204062537/http://www.songfacts.com/detail.php?id=12724|archivedate=February 4, 2009}}</ref> 歌曲開頭的歌詞「A RedOne」時常被[[空耳#Mondegreen|誤聽]]為「[[紅酒|red wine]]」但這段時是影射到歌曲製作人RedOne。<ref name="tribunemagazine">{{cite news|url=http://www.tribune.ie/magazine/article/2009/may/17/gaga-by-name/|title=Gaga by name...|last=Mullally|first=Una|date=May 17, 2009|work=[[Tribune_(magazine)|The Express Tribune]]|accessdate=May 24, 2009|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160307111753/http://tribune.ie/magazine/article/2009/may/17/gaga-by-name|archivedate=March 7, 2016|df=}}</ref>

==評論反響==
Matthew Chisling of [[AllMusic|AllMusic]] described the song as "a powerhouse of dance waves and infectiously produced beats".<ref name="allmusic">{{cite web|url={{Allmusic|class=album|id=r1451926|pure_url=yes}}|title=The Fame [Bonus Track] – Lady Gaga | AllMusic|last=Chisling|first=Matthew|date=September 9, 2008|publisher=AllMusic|accessdate=April 28, 2009}}</ref>{{TransH}}
 [[Alexis_Petridis|Alexis Petridis]] of ''[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]'' called it a "beguilingly compulsive tale of pulling a drug-induced whitey, with a combination of clipped marching beats, sawing electronics and mild R&B flavor that bears a vague resemblance to [[Nelly_Furtado|Nelly Furtado]]'s '[[Maneater_(Nelly_Furtado_song)|Maneater]]'".<ref name="guardian">{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2009/jan/09/lady-gaga-the-fame|title=Lady Gaga: The Fame|last=Petridis|first=Alex|date=January 9, 2009|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|publisher=[[Guardian_News_and_Media|Guardian News and Media]]|accessdate=April 28, 2009|location=London|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130922145027/http://www.theguardian.com/music/2009/jan/09/lady-gaga-the-fame|archivedate=September 22, 2013}}</ref> Ben Norman of [[About.com|About.com]] said that the song "opens the album like a valkyrie leading the charge [...] riding triumphant ahead of her army. If you don't know this song, use your browser. I won't waste time explaining what it sounds like." However he also noted that the song is not groundbreaking and compared it to the music of [[Rihanna|Rihanna]], [[Chris_Brown|Chris Brown]] and [[The_Pussycat_Dolls|The Pussycat Dolls]].<ref name="about1">{{cite web|url=http://dancemusic.about.com/od/reviews/fr/LadyGagaTheFame.htm|title=Lady Gaga – 'The Fame'|last=Norman|first=Ben|date=October 2, 2008|publisher=[[About.com|About.com]]|accessdate=April 28, 2009|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090502215806/http://dancemusic.about.com/od/reviews/fr/LadyGagaTheFame.htm|archivedate=May 2, 2009}}</ref> Bill Lamb from About.com called the song tame but catchy enough for Gaga to be noticed by mainstream. He also added that "'Just Dance' has strong energy and features Lady GaGa's striking voice, but it ultimately adds up to rather bland dance-pop."

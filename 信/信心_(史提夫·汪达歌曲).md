《'''信心'''》是美国歌手兼作曲家[[史提夫·汪达|史提夫·汪达]]的歌曲，[[爱莉安娜·格兰德|爱莉安娜·格兰德]]助陣演唱。
歌曲为2016年[[歡樂好聲音|歡樂好聲音]]音乐动画电影原声带的主打单曲。该曲由[[瑞恩·泰德|瑞恩·泰德]]、[[班尼·布兰科|班尼·布兰科]]和{{tsl|en|Francis Farewell Starlite}}撰词。单曲在2016年11月4日发行。<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/hear-stevie-wonder-ariana-grandes-upbeat-duet-on-faith-w448584 | title=Hear Stevie Wonder, Ariana Grande's Upbeat Duet on New Song 'Faith' | work=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]] | date=November 4, 2016 | accessdate=November 13, 2016 | author=Reed, Ryan}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://pitchfork.com/news/68840-listen-to-stevie-wonders-new-song-with-ariana-grande-faith/ | title=Listen to Stevie Wonder’s New Song With Ariana Grande, "Faith" | work=[[Pitchfork_(website)|Pitchfork]] | date=November 4, 2016 | accessdate=November 13, 2016 | author=Strauss, Matthew}}</ref>

==背景==
这首歌曲是{{tsl|en|Sing: Original Motion Picture Soundtrack|歡樂好聲音：電影原聲帶}}中的主打单曲，由美国创作歌手史提夫·汪达主唱，和美国歌手爱莉安娜·格兰德客串。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7565569/stevie-wonder-ariana-grandes-faith | title=Stevie Wonder and Ariana Grande's 'Faith' Collaboration Has Arrived: Listen | work=[[Billboard_(magazine)|Billboard]] | date=November 4, 2016 | accessdate=November 13, 2016}}</ref>

==评价==
这首歌被提名为[[第74屆金球獎|第74屆金球獎]][[金球獎最佳原創歌曲|最佳原創歌曲]]。<ref name=goldenglobe>{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/golden-globe-nominations-2017-full-list-nominees-955132 |title=Golden Globes 2017: The Complete List of Nominations|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|date=December 12, 2016|accessdate=March 12, 2017}}</ref>

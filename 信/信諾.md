'''信诺'''（Cigna）是一家美国医疗保健公司，提供各类保险和其他一些医疗服务。该公司旗下有多家分公司负责不同业务，如信諾環球人壽保險及信諾環球保險等<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cigna.com/|title=Health Insurance Options and More from Cigna|website=Cigna.com}}</ref>。2018年它列在[[财富美国500强|财富美国500强]]中的第73位<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://fortune.com/fortune500/list/|title=Fortune 500 Companies 2018: Who Made the List|website=Fortune.com|language=en-US|access-date=2018-11-10}}</ref>。

==历史==

该公司在1982年由Connecticut General Life Insurance Company（CG）和INA Corporation（[[北美洲保险公司|北美洲保险公司]]的母公司）合并形成。而北美洲保险公司的历史则可以追溯至1792年。CG成立于1865年。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.Cigna.com/aboutus/company-history|title=Cigna Milestones|work=Cigna, a Global Health Insurance and Health Service Company|accessdate=24 July 2015}}</ref>

2015年6月美国保险公司[[Anthem_Inc.|Anthem Inc.]]打算以470亿美元的现金和股票收购信诺<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2015/06/21/business/dealbook/anthem-makes-47-billion-offer-for-rival-cigna.html?_r=0|title=Anthem Makes $47 Billion Offer for Rival Cigna|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=20 June 2015}}</ref>，但次年7月21日美国司法部发出反垄断禁令<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2016-07-21/anthem-cigna-sued-by-u-s-seeking-to-block-insurer-merger|title=Anthem, Aetna Sued by U.S. Seeking to Block Insurer Mergers |website=Bloomberg.com}}</ref>，最终使得该并购案流产<ref>{{cite news| url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/federal-judge-blocks-anthems-planned-acquisition-of-cigna-1486599640 | title=Federal Judge Blocks Anthem’s Planned Acquisition of Cigna | newspaper=[[The_Wall_Street_Journal|The Wall Street Journal]] | date=February 9, 2017 | accessdate=February 10, 2017}}</ref>。

2018年3月7日宣布将以670亿美元的价格收购[[快捷藥方|快捷藥方]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2018/03/07/health-insurer-cigna-is-close-to-buying-express-scripts.html|title=Health insurer Cigna to buy Express Scripts in $67 billion deal|work=CNBC|date=March 7, 2018|accessdate=March 8, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fiercehealthcare.com/payer/cigna-express-scripts-merger-pharmacy-benefit-management|title=Cigna to buy Express Scripts in $67B deal|last=Minemyer|first=Paige|work=FierceHealthcare|date=March 8, 2018|accessdate=March 8, 2018}}</ref>，同年8月24日此交易被双方股东批准<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.newsmax.com/finance/companies/cigna-express-scripts-shareholders/2018/08/24/id/878640/|title=Cigna Shareholders Approve $67 Billion Express Scripts Acquisition|work=Newsmax Finance|date=August 24, 2018|accessdate=August 31, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://hamodia.com/2018/08/26/cigna-express-scripts-shareholders-approve-67-billion-deal/|title=Cigna, Express Scripts Shareholders Approve $67 Billion Deal|work=Hamodia|date=August 26, 2018|accessdate=August 31, 2018}}</ref>。交易在2018年12月20日以540亿美元的价格成交<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-express-scripts-m-a-cigna/cigna-closes-54-billion-purchase-of-express-scripts-idUSKCN1OJ2AO|title=Cigna closes $54 billion purchase of Express Scripts|last=Humer|first=Caroline|date=December 20, 2018|website=Reuters|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=January 4, 2019}}</ref>。

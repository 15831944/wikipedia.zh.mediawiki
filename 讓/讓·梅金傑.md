'''讓·多米尼克·安東尼·梅金傑'''（Jean Dominique Antony Metzinger {{IPA-fr|mɛtsɛ̃ʒe|lang}}；{{bd|1883年|6月24日|1956年|11月3日}}）是一位法國藝術家、藝術評論家和作家。他與[[阿尔伯特·格列兹|阿尔伯特·格列兹]]一起寫下了[[立体派|立体派]]早期重要著作《[[Du_"Cubisme"|Du "Cubisme"]]》<ref>André Salmon, ''La Jeune Peinture française, Histoire anecdotique du cubisme'', (''Anecdotal History of Cubism''), Paris, Albert Messein, 1912, Collection des Trente</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=UZ4Gu7a7V9UC&pg=PA213&lpg=PA213&dq=%22discern+and+that+which+pre-exists+within+us%22] André Salmon, ''Anecdotal History of Cubism'', quoted in Herschel Browning Chipp ''et al'', ''Theories of Modern Art: A Source Book by Artists and Critics'', University of California Press, 1968, {{ISBN|0-520-01450-2}}. p. 205</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=BngwOaFvEecC&q=metzinger#v=snippet&q=metzinger&f=false André Salmon on French Modern Art, by André Salmon, Cambridge University Press, Nov 14, 2005] {{ISBN|0-521-85658-2}}</ref><ref name="Apollinaire">[https://books.google.com/books/about/The_Cubist_Painters.html?id=qYATQ3Rw6qgC&redir_esc=y Guillaume Apollinaire, ''The Cubist Painters'' (''Les Peintres cubistes, Méditations Esthétiques'', 1913), translation and accompanying commentary by Peter F. Read, University of California Press, 1 oct. 2004]</ref>。他的早期作品（1900-04年）受[[新印象派|新印象派]]畫家[[修拉|修拉]]和[[亨利-埃德蒙·克羅斯|亨利-埃德蒙·克羅斯]]的影響，後來間開始轉向[[野獸派|野獸派]]和立體派。

自1908年開始梅金傑開始接觸後來所謂的立體派風格，并開始發表立體派相關著作<ref name="Metzinger 1910">Jean Metzinger, October–November 1910, "Note sur la peinture" Pan: 60</ref>。第一次世界大戰期間他發展出了立體派的分支[[水晶立體派|水晶立體派]]，強調畫家本身視角而不是現實本身的重要性。<ref name="MoMA">[https://www.moma.org/collection/details.php?theme_id=10068&section_id=T020545 Christopher Green, ''Late Cubism'', MoMA, Grove Art Online, Oxford University Press, 2009]</ref>

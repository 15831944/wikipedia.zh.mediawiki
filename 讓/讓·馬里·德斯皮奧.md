'''讓·馬里·德斯皮奧'''（{{lang-fr|Jean Marie Despiau}}，{{bd|？||1824年||}}），[[法國|法國]][[醫生|醫生]]，<ref>[http://www.historicvietnam.com/les-colonies-francaises-1889-part-1/ The Conquest and Settlement of Cochinchina in “Les Colonies Francaises,” 1889]</ref>後來成為[[越南|越南]][[阮朝|阮朝]]的一名[[士大夫|士大夫]]。<ref>[[Trần_Trọng_Kim|Trần Trọng Kim]], ''[[Việt_Nam_sử_lược|Việt Nam sử lược]]'', [[:s:vi:Việt_Nam_sử_lược/Quyển_II/Cận_kim_thời_đại/Chương_I|Quyển 2, Cận kim thời đại, Chương 1]]</ref><ref>[[Phan_Khôi|Phan Khôi]], ''[[:s:vi:Bác_cái_thuyết_"Nước_Pháp_giúp_nước_Nam_về_hồi_cuối_thế_kỷ_XVIII"|Bác cái thuyết "Nước Pháp giúp nước Nam về hồi cuối thế kỷ XVIII"]]''</ref>

出生在[[吉倫特省|吉倫特省]]的{{link-en|巴扎斯|Bazas}}。1795年，自[[葡屬澳門|澳門]]來到[[交趾支那|交趾支那]]。<ref>Wynn Wilcox, [https://books.google.com/books?id=EvqKqpSCpaEC&pg=PA61&lpg=PA61&dq=Jean+Marie+Despiau&source=bl&ots=Skf_1j6O-Y&sig=v-D2nFAL6bnRVAYZpvo12wOvUJY&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwiO2_6i7pfeAhViNH0KHbILC3QQ6AEwA3oECAgQAQ#v=onepage&q=Jean%20Marie%20Despiau&f=false ''Vietnam and the West: New Approaches'']</ref>他成為了[[阮福映|阮福映]]（後來的嘉隆帝）的[[私人醫生|私人醫生]]。<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=Mw1msV1J534C&pg=PA103&lpg=PA103&dq=Desperles+Nguyen+Anh&source=bl&ots=rzokBZAMiK&sig=Kn-HwGr1otoY6j2QdlcdbjhEcJU&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwizpO3g8JjeAhUKi1QKHb6IAj8Q6AEwDXoECAUQAQ#v=onepage&q=Despiaux%20Nguyen%20Anh&f=false ''Abrégé de l'histoire d'Annam'']</ref>{{rp|105}}後來在1802年，他成為阮朝的[[御醫|御醫]]直至逝世。<ref name=yale>[http://www.yale.edu/seas/ThompsonM.htm The Nguyen Mission for Smallpox Vaccine: Promising Leads from the Portuguese Archives] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130201012325/http://www.yale.edu/seas/ThompsonM.htm |date=2013-02-01 }}</ref>

德斯皮奧受到嘉隆帝的信任。<ref name=social/>1802年，嘉隆帝接受他的建議，組織了對原住民的醫療援助，並在越南全境建立省級醫師，對得病者、年長者、無法治癒者、體弱者和貧困者進行治療。<ref>[https://leprosyhistory.org/geographical_region/country/vietnam Indo China under the French]</ref>他被描述為一個對政治一點也不感興趣的人。在[[明命帝|明命帝]]繼承皇位後，他繼續受到新皇帝的喜愛。<ref name=social>C. Michele Thompson, [https://books.google.com/books?id=TUXzCQAAQBAJ&q=Jean+Marie+Despiau#v=snippet&q=Jean%20Marie%20Despiau&f=false ''Vietnamese Traditional Medicine: A Social History'']</ref>1820年，他被派往澳門尋求[[人痘接種法|人痘接種法]]。<ref name=yale/>

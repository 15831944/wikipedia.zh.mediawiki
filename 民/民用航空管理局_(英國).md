'''民用航空管理局'''（'''Civil Aviation Authority'''，縮寫：'''CAA''') 是英國的[[民航局|民航局]]，由[[英國運輸部|英國運輸部]]管轄。總部位於倫敦[[霍本|霍本]]，<ref>"[http://www.caa.co.uk/docs/33/CAA_Map_London.pdf London Head Office]." Civil Aviation Authority. Retrieved on 9 September 2010.</ref>安全管理部門則在[[克勞利|克勞利]]的[[蓋特威克機場|蓋特威克機場]]運作。<ref>"[http://www.caa.co.uk/docs/33/CAA_Bus_Service.pdf Bus Services to CAA Safety Regulation Group, Aviation House]." Civil Aviation Authority. Retrieved on 9 September 2010. "Aviation House South Area Gatwick Airport RH6 0YR"</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url = https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/civil-aviation-authority|title = Civil Aviation Authority - GOV.UK|date = |accessdate = October 20, 2014|website = [[GOV.UK|GOV.UK]]|publisher = HM Government|last = |first = }}</ref>

該機構依照1971年民航局法案（Civil Aviation Act 1971）建立於1972年，負責國內大部分的[[航空|航空]]領域事務，另外一部分職能由[[欧洲航空安全局|欧洲航空安全局]]行使。<ref>[http://www.flightglobal.com/pdfarchive/view/1969/1969%20-%200839.html ''The Edwards Report — Principal recommendations'', Air Transport, Flight International, 8 May 1969, p. 745]</ref>

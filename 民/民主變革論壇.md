'''民主變革論壇'''是[[烏干達|烏干達]]的最大反對黨，成立於2004年12月14日，由多個反對黨派合併而成。該黨稱其宗旨是建設一個繁榮、和平、團結的烏干達。報導表明，在烏干達，民主變革論壇的成員常常遭受到[[全國抵抗運動|烏干達抵運]]當局的騷擾<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.refworld.org/docid/4e43d2ba2.html|title=Uganda: The Forum for Democratic Change (FDC); its structure, platform, leadership and executive members; treatment of its members by authorities|publisher=Canada: Immigration and Refugee Board of Canada|date=2010-06-02|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171103060423/http://www.refworld.org/docid/4e43d2ba2.html|archivedate=2017-11-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://fdc.ug/mission/|title=Mission|publisher=FDC}}</ref>。

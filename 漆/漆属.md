'''漆属'''（[[学名|学名]]：''{{lang|la|Toxicodendron}}''）是[[漆树科|漆树科]]下一属。漆属物种形态包括[[乔木|乔木]]、[[灌木|灌木]]、[[攀缘植物|木质藤本]]。本属物种皆产生[[漆酚|漆酚]]，可导致强烈[[过敏|过敏]]反应。本属属名''{{lang|la|Toxicodendron}}''来自于[[希腊语|希腊语]]，由[[wikt:τοξικός|τοξικός]]（''toxikos''，有毒的）与[[wikt:δένδρον|δένδρον]]（''dendron''树）组成。<ref>{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NJ6PyhVuecwC |title=The Names of Plants |first=D. |last=Gledhill |edition=4 |publisher=Cambridge University Press |isbn=978-0-521-86645-3 |year=2008 |page=382}}</ref>

本属是[[盐肤木属|盐肤木]]复合种的成员，曾被单独分为一属，或划为盐肤木属的亚属。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://etd.lsu.edu/docs/available/etd-04152004-101232/ | title=Molecular Systematics of the Cashew Family (Anacardiaceae) (PhD dissertation at Louisiana State University) | author=Pell, Susan Katherine | date=18 February 2004 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/66Bwn7GfO?url=http://etd.lsu.edu/docs/available/etd-04152004-101232/ | archivedate=15 March 2012 | df= }}, page 89</ref>有证据显示本属应作为[[单系群|单系群]]独立存在，然而有人指出本属与盐肤木属属于复合种，其关系需要进一步研究。<ref>{{cite journal|doi=10.1111/j.1759-6831.2009.00045.x | volume=47 | issue=5 | title=Phylogenetic analysis ofToxicodendron(Anacardiaceae) and its biogeographic implications on the evolution of north temperate and tropical intercontinental disjunctions | journal=Journal of Systematics and Evolution | pages=416–430 | author=Ze-Long NIE| year=2009 }}</ref>

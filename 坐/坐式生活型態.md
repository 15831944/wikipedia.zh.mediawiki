'''坐式生活型態'''是指只有很少或没有[[运动|体能活动]]的社交性[[生活型態|生活型態]]。一个以坐式生活型態生活的人常常由於[[阅读|阅读]]、[[社交|社交]]联谊、观看[[电视|电视]]、玩[[电子游戏|电子游戏]]或操作[[手机|手机]]或[[电脑|电脑]]等原因，在一天中大部份时间[[坐|坐]]着或[[躺卧|躺]]着。坐式生活型態是很多{{le|可预防性死亡|Preventable_causes_of_death}}的因素之一。''屏幕时间''是指一个人在观看电视、电脑、[[平板电脑|平板电脑]]等电子屏幕花费的时间。过多的屏幕时间会引起[[健康|健康]]的负面影响<ref>{{cite web|author=Amy E. Mark, M.Sc. and Ian Janssen, Ph.D. |doi=10.1093/pubmed/fdn022 |title=Relationship between screen time and metabolic syndrome in adolescents |publisher=Jpubhealth.oxfordjournals.org |date=2008-03-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ambulatorypediatrics.org/article/S1530-1567(05)60054-8/abstract |title=Elsevier |publisher=Ambulatorypediatrics.org |date= |accessdate=2013-11-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jpeds.com/article/S0022-3476(08)00176-5/abstract |title=Elsevier |publisher=Jpeds.com |date= |accessdate=2013-11-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Olds | first1 = T. | last2 = Ridley | first2 = K. | last3 = Dollman | first3 = J. | doi = 10.1111/j.1467-842X.2006.tb00106.x | title = Screenieboppers and extreme screenies: The place of screen time in the time budgets of 10–13 year-old Australian children | journal = Australian and New Zealand Journal of Public Health | volume = 30 | issue = 2 | pages = 137–142 | year = 2006 | pmid = 16681334| pmc = }}</ref>。

'''觉醒和激动人心艺术阵线'''（Chetna Natya Manch，缩写为CNM）<ref name="Dilip">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=HxQDBAAAQBAJ&pg=PT199&output=text#c_top|title=Indians in a Globalizing World|last=Hiro|first=Dilip|publisher=Harper Collins (India)|isbn=978-93-5136-267-8|page=199}}</ref> 是[[印度共产党（毛主义）|印度共产党（毛主义）]]的“文工团”。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/indepth/inpictures/2014/10/pictures-india-maoist-heartlan-2014105104030403635.html|title=In Pictures: India's Maoist heartland|work=[[半岛电视台|半岛电视台]]|accessdate=28 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=IZ83V3JLXFMC&pg=PA122&output=text#c_top|title=Hello, Bastar : The Untold Story of India's Maoist Movement|last=Pandita|first=Rahul|publisher=Westland (Tranquebar Press)|year=2011|isbn=978-93-80658-34-6|location=Chennai|page=122|oclc=754482226}}</ref><ref name="AR">{{cite web|url=http://www.outlookindia.com/article.aspx?264738-0|title=Walking with the Comrades|accessdate=28 October 2014|date=29 March 2010|last=Roy|first=Arundhati|publisher=[[Outlook_(magazine)|Outlook]]|author-link=Arundhati Roy}}</ref> 由来自[[安得拉邦|安得拉邦]]的Leng领导，<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bMXB6sGPk2kC&q=Comrade+Leng#v=snippet&q=Comrade%20Leng&f=false|title=Broken Republic|last=Roy|first=Arundhati|publisher=[[企鹅出版集团|企鹅出版集团]]|year=2013|isbn=978-81-8475-484-1}}</ref> 拥有超过一万名成员。<ref name="Gautam">{{cite book|title=Days and Nights in the Heartland of Rebellion|last=Navlakha|first=Gautam|publisher=Penguin Books|year=2012|isbn=978-81-8475-654-8|chapter=A Hurried and Much Too Short a Meeting|chapterurl=https://books.google.com/books?id=tw_Mo1dF1WgC&pg=PT125&output=text#c_top|author-link=Gautam Navlakha}}</ref>

== 背景和活动 ==
{{Quote box|width=15em|align=right|quote="Gearing the fallows,<br />Ploughing the fields,<br />With our sweat as streams,<br />We harvested the crops.<br />Whose was the grain?<br />Whose was the gruel?"|salign=right|source=— Cherabandaraju's song, oftentimes performed by the CNM<ref name="Dilip"/>}}

CNM是[[印度共产党（马列）人民战争|印度共产党（马列）人民战争]]的“宣传单位”<ref name="Satanama">{{cite book|title=Jangalnama : Inside the Maoist Guerrilla Zone|author=Satanāma|publisher=[[企鹅出版集团|企鹅出版集团]]|others=Translated from Punjabi by Vishav Bharti|year=2010|origyear=2003|isbn=978-0-14-341445-2|location=New Delhi|page=85|chapter=Walking through the jungle|chapterurl=https://books.google.com/books?id=OKDcCDlEZ0EC&pg=PA85&output=text#c_top|oclc=634661617}}</ref>和“文化部门”，在村里“举办舞蹈、戏剧、诗歌及音乐工作室”，“鼓励青少年加入人民战争集团”。<ref name="Dilip"/> 据他们说，他们是一个“文化团队”，他们“不战斗”，只“唱歌”。 他们还专注于文学和[[造型艺术|造型艺术]]。他们自己筹集音乐磁带，还有一个“移动编辑单位”。<ref name="Gautam"/>

CNM在他们的演讲中吸引了“大量人群”。<ref>{{cite web |title=Bastar, Maoism and Salwa Judum |url=http://indiandemocracy08.berkeley.edu/docs/Sundar-BastarMaoismAndSalwa%20.pdf |last=Sundar |first=Nandini |publisher=[[加利福尼亞大學柏克萊分校|加利福尼亞大學柏克萊分校]] |format=PDF |year=2006 |accessdate=28 October 2014}}</ref>

== 出版物 ==
1994年8月，CNM开始以[[孟加拉语|孟加拉语]]、 [[冈德语|冈德语]]、[[印地语|印地语]]、[[马拉地语|马拉地语]]和[[泰卢固语|泰卢固语]]出版双月刊《Jhankar》，至今仍在出版。<ref name="Gautam"/>

== 反对 ==
印度政府信息和广播部（ministry of Information and Broadcasting）“激活自己的文化部门”以“对抗”CNM，他们认为，这是“通过歌曲和文化节目煽动部族反抗印度政权。”<ref>{{cite book |title=India's National Security: Annual Review 2009 |chapter=Internal Security Developments |chapterurl=https://books.google.com/books?id=x-esAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA95&output=text#c_top |first=Satish |last=Kumar |publisher=Routledge |page=95 |year=2009 |isbn=978-1-136-70491-8}}</ref>

2013年8月16日，[[恰蒂斯加尔邦政府|恰蒂斯加尔邦政府]]根据《恰蒂斯加尔邦特别公共安全法》（Chhattisgarh Special Public Security Act）以其疑似参与[[恰蒂斯加尔邦|恰蒂斯加尔邦]][[红色走廊|红色走廊]]地区的“叛乱活动”为由取缔了CNM，最近，该禁令被延长至2015年8月30日。<ref>{{cite news |title=Chhattisgarh govt extends ban on CNM |url=http://news.webindia123.com/news/Articles/India/20140901/2451185.html |publisher=Web India 123 |location=Raipur |date=1 September 2014|accessdate=30 October 2014}}</ref>

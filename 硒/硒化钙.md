'''硒化钙'''是[[硒|硒]]和[[钙|钙]]以等化学计量比形成的化合物，化学式为CaSe。<ref>https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/compound/102107</ref>

== 制备及性质 ==
硒化钙可以由[[硒化氢|硒化氢]]和金属[[钙|钙]]在[[液氨|液氨]]中反应得到。<ref>Kohle, J.; Petzel, T. The preparation of calcium selenide, strontium selenide, barium selenide, and europium(II) selenide by the reaction of the metals with hydrogen selenide in liquid ammonia{{de}}. ''Zeitschrift fuer Anorganische und Allgemeine Chemie'', 1977. Vol 437: 193-196.</ref>
: Ca + H<sub>2</sub>Se → CaSe + H<sub>2</sub>

硒化钙和硒化铟在真空中高温反应，生成CaIn<sub>2</sub>Se<sub>4</sub>。<ref>Yagubov, N. I.; Guliev, T. N.; Rustamov, P. G.; Chiragov, M. I.; Safarov, V. G. Preparation and study of the properties of calcium indium selenide, CaIn<sub>2</sub>Se<sub>4</sub>{{ru}}. ''Zhurnal Neorganicheskoi Khimii'', 1988. 33 (6): 1387-1389.</ref>
: CaSe + In<sub>2</sub>Se<sub>3</sub> → CaIn<sub>2</sub>Se<sub>4</sub>

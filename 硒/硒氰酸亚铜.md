'''硒氰酸亚铜'''是一种无机化合物，化学式为CuSeCN，难溶于水，[[溶度积|溶度积]]为1.82×10<sup>-10</sup>（20℃）。<ref name=AM>A M Golub, Viktor V Skopenko. Metal Selenocyanates and Their Properties. Russian Chemical Reviews, 1965. 34 (12)</ref>CuSeCN存在α、β、γ、γ'、δ、ε等相。<ref>Tsetseris L. Two-dimensional copper thio- and seleno-cyanates.[J]. Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics, 2016, 18(11):7837-7840.</ref>
==制备==
电化学法是以[[铜|铜]]为[[阳极|阳极]]电解[[硒氰酸钾|硒氰酸钾]]溶液，可以得到硒氰酸亚铜晶体：<ref>Paul A. Kilmartin, Graham A. Wright. [http://www.publish.csiro.au/ch/C96110 Copper Selenocyanate Formation on the Copper Anode]. Australian Journal of Chemistry, 1997. 50(4) 321 - 328. doi: 10.1071/C96110</ref>
: Cu + SeCN<sup>−</sup> − ''e''<sup>−</sup> → CuSeCN

化学法是利用了[[氯化亚铜|氯化亚铜]]和[[硒氰酸钾|硒氰酸钾]]的反应，反应物在丙酮中混合，加入少量水析出沉淀。<ref name=AM />
==性质==
硒氰酸亚铜可以吸收氢气，形成CuSeCNH<sub>2</sub>。<ref>Tsetseris L. Two-dimensional cyanates: stabilization through hydrogenation[J]. Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics, 2016, 18(21):14662.</ref>

==化学性质==
硒酸镝受热分解，先生成[[亚硒酸盐|亚硒酸盐]]，最后得到氧化物。<ref>Belik, N. P.; Serebrennikov, V. V. Thermal stability of rare earth selenates{{ru}}. ''Tr. Tomsk. Univ.'', 1971. 204. pp 285-8</ref>

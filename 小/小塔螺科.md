'''小塔螺科'''{{r|qdhys}}（學名：{{lang|la|Pyramidellidae}}），亦作'''尖塔螺科'''或簡單只叫'''[[塔螺科|塔螺科]]'''，是[[軟體動物門|軟體動物門]][[腹足綱|腹足綱]]下的一個[[科_(生物)|科]]。舊屬[[腸紐目|腸紐目]]（Heterostropha，又譯[[異旋目|異旋目]]）{{r|qdhys}}，成員主要是營[[寄生|寄生]]生活的小型[[海螺|海螺]]。

小塔螺科的物種分佈於整個世界，有超過六千個不同的物種，合組成超過350個屬與亞屬{{r|Odostomiinae}}。由於本科物種皆屬於[[微小貝|微小貝]]，相關研究少，物種間{{link-en|支序親緣關係|phylogenetic}}並不十分清楚，大眾對於各物種的歸屬也沒有共識。舉例說：單單是 Schander (1999) 就列出了超過300個屬級以上的分類單元命名，並將這些分類單元整理{{r|Schander etal 2011}}。由於近年本科在世界各地各個屬的分類未再有可一般化的修訂，這個科內各個分類單元仍然保持是[[多系群|多系群]]，也就是說這些分類的混亂還有一段時間。然而，從整個科來看仍然是個[[單系群|單系群]]{{r|Dinapoli 2010}}。

==分類==
本科物種主要分為11個亞科（[[Winston_Ponder|Ponder]] & [[David_R._Lindberg|Lindberg]] 1997）<ref name="Ponder 1997"/>。An alternative interpretation is that the family Pyramidellidae is but one of six families within the superfamily [[Pyramidelloidea|Pyramidelloidea]] ([[Christoffer_Schander|Schander]], van Aartsen & Corgan 1999).<ref name="Schander1999"/> 
當中有不少罕見或不常紀錄的物種。

本科之下的亞科在不同年代刊行的分類學文獻都有所不同，當中最為普遍的分類是[[布歇特和洛克羅伊的腹足類分類_(2005年)|2005年的布歇特和洛克羅伊的腹足類分類]]{{r|Bouchet 2005}}。以下詳列自1997年以來多個分類間的差異。

==分佈==
本物種有世界性分佈，但當中不少物種都只在相當細小範圍內分佈。在[[佛得角|佛得角]]的各物種均為當地的[[特有種|特有種]]<ref name="Aartsen">{{cite book |url=http://www.repository.naturalis.nl/document/44074 |first1=J.J. |last1=van Aartsen |first2=E. |last2=Gittenberger |first3=J. |last3=Goud |title=Pyramidellidae (Mollusca, Gastropoda, Heterobranchia) collected during the Dutch CANCAP and MAURITANIA expeditions in the south-eastern part of the North Atlantic Ocean (part 2) |language=en }}</ref>。

==型態描述==
The length of the slender, elongated (turreted or conical) shells varies between 0.5 mm and 3.5 cm, but most species in the family have shells which are smaller than 13 mm.

The texture of these shells is smooth or [[sculpture_(mollusc)|sculptured]] in various forms such as ribs and spirals. Their color is mostly white, cream or yellowish, sometimes with red or brown lines.

The [[gastropod_shell|shell]] of these snails has a blunt, [[heterostrophic|heterostrophic]] (i.e. whorls appear to be coiled in the opposite direction to those of the teleoconch) [[protoconch|protoconch]], which is often pointed sideways or wrapped up. The [[teleoconch|teleoconch]] is [[gastropod_shell#Chirality_in_gastropods|dextrally coiled]], but the [[larva|larva]]l shells are [[gastropod_shell#Chirality_in_gastropods|sinistral]]. This results in a sinistrally coiled protoconch.

The [[Columella_(mollusc)|columella]] has usually one, but sometimes several, spiral [[plait_(gastropod)|folds]]. The [[aperture_(mollusc)|aperture]] is closed by an [[Operculum_(gastropod)|operculum]].

The [[Operculum_(gastropod)|operculum]] is ovoid and paucispiral, with the apex anterior, a thread-like arcuate ridge on the proximal side, the inner margin notched in harmony with the plaits of the pillar when prominent.

They do not have a [[radula|radula]]. Instead their long [[proboscis|proboscis]] is used to pierce the skin of its prey and suck up its fluids and soft tissues. The eyes on the grooved tentacles are situated  toward the base of the tentacles. These tentacles have a concave surface. Between the head and the propodium (the foremost division of the foot), a lobed process called the [[mentum|mentum]] (= thin projection) is visible. This mentum is slightly indented in midline.

==語源==
本科的學名源於[[金字塔|金字塔]]的英語''pyramid''，而這亦是這些物種普遍的外型{{r|Tenney et al 1875}}。
準確點說：這些物種都呈[[正圓錐體|正圓錐體]]的模樣{{r|Step 1901}}，又或是正多邊形的[[錐體|錐體]]{{r|Robbins 1907}}。

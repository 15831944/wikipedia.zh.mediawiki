== 現場翻唱歌曲 ==
以下是小飛俠現場翻唱表演的歌曲

* "[[American_Idiot|American Idiot]]" ([[Green_Day|Green Day]]) - <small>在Wonderland Tour上表演，當表演《5 Colours in Her Hair》時演奏</small>
* "Black Or White" ([[Michael_Jackson|Michael Jackson]]) - <small>在[[Radio:ACTIVE_Tour|Radio:ACTIVE Tour]]和[[The_Nokia_Green_Room|The Nokia Green Room]]上表演.</small>
* "Born To Run" ([[Bruce_Springsteen|Bruce Springsteen]]) - <small>在[[BBC_Radio_1|BBC Radio 1]]表演.</small>
* "The Boys Are Back In Town" ([[Thin_Lizzy|Thin Lizzy]]) - <small>在[[The_Prince's_Trust|The Prince's Trust]]演唱會上表演.</small>
* "Ghostbusters Theme" ([[Ray_Parker_Jr|Ray Parker Jr]]) - <small>在[[Motion_in_the_Ocean|Motion in the Ocean]] Tour上表演.</small>
* "Happy Christmas (War Is Over)" ([[John_Lennon|John Lennon]]) - <small>在[[BBC_Radio_1|BBC Radio 1]]表演.</small>
* "Honky Tonk Women" ([[The_Rolling_Stones|The Rolling Stones]])
* "I Want To Hold Your Hand" ([[The_Beatles|The Beatles]]) - <small>在[[Children_In_Need|Children In Need]]表演.</small>
* "I Gotta Feeling" ([[Black_Eyed_Peas|Black Eyed Peas]]) - <small>在European Tour 2009上表演.</small>
* "Let It Snow" - <small>在[[Popworld|Popworld]] Christmas Concert上表演.</small>
* "Little Saint Nick" ([[The_Beach_Boys|The Beach Boys]]) - <small>在the [[EastEnders|EastEnders]] Christmas Party表演.</small>
* "On My Own" ([[The_Used|The Used]])</small>
* "Pass Out" ([[Tinie_Tempah|Tinie Tempah]]) <small>在[[Above_the_Noise_Tour|Above the Noise Tour]]表演</small>
* "The Promise" ([[Girls_Aloud|Girls Aloud]]) - <small>在"[[Up_Close..._And_This_Time,_It's_Personal|Up Close... And This Time, It's Personal]]表演" tour.</small>
* "Rocks" ([[Primal_Scream|Primal Scream]]) <small>與[[Charlotte_Church|Charlotte Church]]在[[The_Charlotte_Church_Show|The Charlotte Church Show]]表演.</small>
* "YMCA" ([[Village_People|Village People]]) <small>在the Motion in the Ocean Tour表演.</small>
* "Dynamite" ([[Taio_Cruz|Taio Cruz]]) <small>在[[Radio_1_Live_Lounge|Radio 1 Live Lounge]]以及''Before the Noise'' tour表演.</small>

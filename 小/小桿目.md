'''小桿目'''（學名：{{lang|la|Rhabditida}}）是[[色矛綱|色矛綱]][[色矛亞綱|色矛亞綱]]的一個目。

==分類==
{{as of|2018|3|7}}，本目包括以下五個分類單元{{r|WoRMS_2143|DeLey 2004}}：
*Suborder [[Myolaimina|Myolaimina]] {{small|Inglis, 1983}}
** 
***[[Myolaimoidea|Myolaimoidea]] {{small|Andrassy, 1958}}
* [[小桿亞目|小桿亞目]] [[Rhabditina|Rhabditina]]{{r|WoRMS_458814}}
** Infraorder [[Bunonematomorpha|Bunonematomorpha]]
***Superfamily [[Bunonematoidea|Bunonematoidea]] {{small|Micoletzky, 1922}}
** Infraorder [[Diplogasteromorpha|Diplogasteromorpha]]
** Infraorder [[Rhabditomorpha|Rhabditomorpha]]
* [[旋尾亞目|旋尾亞目]] [[Spirurina|Spirurina]]：包括下列五個下目{{r|WoRMS_22833}}<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Nematodes: The Worm and Its Relatives|url=http://journals.plos.org/plosbiology/article?id=10.1371/journal.pbio.1001050|last=Blaxter|first=Mark|date=2011-04-19|journal=PLOS Biology|issue=4|doi=10.1371/journal.pbio.1001050|volume=9|pages=e1001050|issn=1545-7885}}</ref>：
** [[蛔形下目|蛔形下目]] Ascaridomorpha
***Superfamily Ascaridoidea Baird, 1853
***Superfamily Cosmocercoidea Travassos, 1925
***Superfamily Heterakoidea Railliet & Henry, 1912
***Superfamily Seuratoidea Railliet, 1906
***Superfamily Subuluroidea Yorke & Maplestone, 1926
** {{le|旋尾下目|Spiruromopha}} Spiruromopha：即原來在旋尾目的分類，例如：[[絲蟲科|絲蟲科]]或[[蟠尾絲蟲科|蟠尾絲蟲科]]
** {{le|Gnathostomatomorpha}}
** {{le|尖尾下目|Oxyuridomorpha}} Oxyuridomorpha
*** {{le|尖尾總科|Oxyuroidea}} Oxyuroidea {{small|Cobbold, 1864}}
*** {{le|Thelastomatoidea}} {{small|Travassos, 1929}}
** {{le|里崗下目|Rhigonematomorpha}} Rhigonematomorpha

'''小行星4429'''（4429 Chinmoy）是一颗绕[[太阳|太阳]]运转的[[小行星|小行星]]，为主小行星带小行星。该小行星于1978年9月12日发现。小行星得名孟加拉作家，詩人和哲學家斯里蘭卡Chinmoy（1931-2007). <ref>Springer: [http://books.google.de/books?id=KWrB1jPCa8AC&pg=PA381&lpg=PA381&dq=Named+in+honor+of+Sri+Chinmoy,+Bengali+poet,+artist+and+philosopher,+preacher+of+peace,+who+travels+the+world,+inspiring+peace-loving+peoples+with+his+music,+poetry+and+works+of+art.%20&source=bl&ots=BGKOaPL8WD&sig=mktBQymnI7cNzZmAI_m5HZQEKu8&hl=de&sa=X&ei=vJZ2UfOfIsrHtAbtl4DwCg&ved=0CDkQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=Named%20in%20honor%20of%20Sri%20Chinmoy%2C%20Bengali%20poet%2C%20artist%20and%20philosopher%2C%20preacher%20of%20peace%2C%20who%20travels%20the%20world%2C%20inspiring%20peace-loving%20peoples%20with%20his%20music%2C%20poetry%20and%20works%20of%20art.%20&f=false  Dictonary of Minor Planets]</ref>

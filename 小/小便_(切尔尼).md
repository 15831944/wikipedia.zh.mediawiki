'''小便''' （{{lang-cs|'''Čůrající postavy'''}}；英语：Piss）是捷克艺术家大卫·切尔尼在2004年创作的户外雕塑和喷泉，位于捷克[[布拉格|布拉格]][[布拉格小城|布拉格小城]][[卡夫卡博物馆|卡夫卡博物馆]]外<ref name="Strangest">{{cite news|last1=von Kayser|first1=Ada|title=Top 10 strangest statues in Prague|url=https://www.praguepost.com/166-expats-in-cz/38034-top-10-strangest-statues-in-prague|accessdate=2018-02-06|work=The Prague Post|date=29 March 2014}}</ref><ref name=idnes09>{{cite news|title=David Černý věnuje politikům fontánu z penisů|language=cs|url=https://kultura.zpravy.idnes.cz/david-cerny-venuje-politikum-fontanu-z-penisu-f4x-/vytvarne-umeni.aspx?c=A091014_185002_vytvarneum_tt|accessdate=2018-06|work=Mladá fronta DNES|date=009-10-14}}</ref>。

喷泉的青铜底座设计成捷克共和国的形状。喷泉中站立着两个电动控制的男性青铜雕塑，彼此相对，高2.1米，正在小便<ref>{{cite news|title=Čůrající postavy|language=cs|url=http://www.prague.eu/cs/objekt/mista/1867/curajici-postavy|accessdate=2018-02-06|work=Prague.eu|date=n.d.}}</ref>。游客可以向其发送[[短信|短信]]，指挥雕像会将短信内容写在水中<ref name=Strangest/><ref name="Pickering">{{cite news|last1=Pickering|first1=Mark|title=A David Cerny sculpture walk in Prague|url=https://www.theguardian.com/travel/2012/aug/10/david-cerny-sculpture-walk-prague-city-break|accessdate=2018-02-06|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|date=10 August 2012}}</ref><ref name="Conde">{{cite news|last1=Holmes|first1=Natalie|title=You Haven't Seen Prague Until You've Seen These Sculptures|url=https://www.cntraveler.com/stories/2014-02-18/david-cerny-prague-sculptures|accessdate=6 February 2018|work=Condé Nast Traveler|date=2014-02-18}}</ref>。

《布拉格邮报》将该作品列为“布拉格十大最奇怪的雕像”之一，称其“既有争议又有趣”<ref name=Strangest/>。

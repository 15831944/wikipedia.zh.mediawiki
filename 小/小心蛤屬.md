'''小心蛤屬'''（學名：{{lang|la| ''Carditella''}}）是[[小心蛤亞科|小心蛤亞科]] （Carditellinae）的[[屬_(生物)|屬]]{{r|Xu 2012}}。本屬原屬[[心蛤科|心蛤科]]{{r|Xu 2012|Bouchet 2005}}，2012年與小心蛤亞科一起改屬{{le|Condylocardiidae科|Condylocardiidae}}{{r|COL|ITIS_80789 |WoRMS_196904}}。

==型態描述==
本屬物種外殼的型態與[[算盤蛤屬|算盤蛤屬]]（''[[Cardita|Cardita]]''）物種的型態相似。鉸齒在左殼有兩顆主齒，右殼只有一顆；側齒則兩邊均有兩顆。one nearly marginal on the one side, the other on the opposite side being well within the outer edge, with a groove between it and the margin for the reception of the submarginal tooth of the other valve. External ligament small, yet distinct. Internal cartilage minute, placed immediately beneath the apex of the valves. Pallial line simple.

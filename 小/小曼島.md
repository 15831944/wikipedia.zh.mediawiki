'''小曼島'''（{{lang-en|Calf of Man}}，{{lang-gv|Yn Cholloo}}）是[[愛爾蘭海|愛爾蘭海]]中的一個島嶼，面積{{convert|618|acre|ha|adj=on}}，位於[[曼島|曼島]]西南海岸<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.manxnationalheritage.im/attractions/the-sound-and-calf-of-man/|title=Places to visit: Calf of Man|publisher=Manx National Heritage|accessdate=25 May 2016|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160619030823/http://www.manxnationalheritage.im/attractions/the-sound-and-calf-of-man/|archivedate=2016年6月19日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/isleofman/content/articles/2005/10/07/calf_of_man_feature.shtml|title=Calf of Man explored (archived)|publisher=BBC|accessdate=25 May 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.manxnationalheritage.im/explore-the-island/places-to-visit/coast-countryside/|title=Coast & countryside|publisher=Manx National Heritage|accessdate=25 May 2016|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160518050537/http://www.manxnationalheritage.im/explore-the-island/places-to-visit/coast-countryside/|archivedate=2016年5月18日}}</ref>。小曼島和曼島之間只隔有一條很窄的水道，島上只有兩個季節性居民。島上最高處位於西部，海拔128米（415英尺）。

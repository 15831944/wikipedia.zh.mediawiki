'''小火蟻'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Wasmannia auropunctata'''''}}，{{lang-en|'''electric ant'''}}、{{lang|en|'''little fire ant'''}}）是一種小型的[[切葉蟻|家蟻亞科]]物種，原産於中南美洲，現已擴張到北美洲、[[以色列|以色列]]、<ref>{{cite journal|last=Vonshak|first=Merav|title=The little fire ant ''Wasmannia auropunctata'': a new invasive species in the Middle East and its impact on the local arthropod fauna
|coauthors=T. Dayan, A. Ionescu-Hirsch, A. Freidberg, A. Hefetz|journal=Biological Invasions|year=2010|volume=12|pages=1825–1837|url=http://www.springerlink.com/content/n57t18648k7m5540/}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Foucaud|first=Julien|coauthors=et al|title=Worldwide invasion by the little fire ant: routes of introduction and eco-evolutionary pathways|journal=Evolutionary Applications|year=2010|volume=3|pages=363–374}}</ref> 和一些[[太平洋島嶼|太平洋島嶼]]（[[加拉帕戈斯群島|加拉帕戈斯群島]]、[[夏威夷|夏威夷]]、[[新喀里多尼亞|新喀里多尼亞]]和[[所羅門群島|所羅門群島]]），此外東北澳大利亞的[[凱恩斯_(昆士蘭州)|凱恩]]也發現了其蹤跡。<ref name="QldDPI01">[http://www.dpi.qld.gov.au/cps/rde/dpi/hs.xsl/4790_6453_ENA_HTML.htm Electric ant (''Wasmannia auropunctata'') webpage] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090214085035/http://dpi.qld.gov.au/cps/rde/dpi/hs.xsl/4790_6453_ENA_HTML.htm |date=2009-02-14 }} Accessed 7 March 2009</ref><ref name="Globaldbase01">[http://www.issg.org/database/species/search.asp?st=100ss&fr=1&str Global Invasive Species Database - No. 100 - ''Wasmannia auropunctata'']</ref>

其英文名稱“electric ant”來自其咬傷產生如[[觸電|觸電]]的強烈痛感，<ref name="QldWarning01">[http://www.mitchell-river.com.au/news-and-issues/27888_electric_ant_web.pdf Queensland Government Electric Ant: Warning] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110218112416/http://mitchell-river.com.au/news-and-issues/27888_electric_ant_web.pdf |date=2011-02-18 }} Accessed 7 March 2009</ref> 它也是[[世界百大外來入侵種|世界百大外來入侵種]]之一。小火蟻體長約{{convert|1.5|mm}}，通體姜色。雌雄兩種都可以[[無性繁殖|無性繁殖]]，是世界上首個被發現兩性都可以無性繁殖的動物。<ref name= Fournier2005>{{cite journal |last1=Fournier |first1=Denis |last2=Estoup |first2=Arnaud |last3=Orivel |first3=Jérôme |last4=Foucaud |first4=Julien |last5=Jourdan |first5=Hervé |last6=Breton |first6=Julien Le |last7=Keller |first7=Laurent |title=Clonal reproduction by males and females in the little fire ant |journal=Nature |volume=435 |issue=7046 |pages=1230–4 |year=2005 |pmid=15988525 |doi=10.1038/nature03705}}</ref><ref name=Fournier2005/><ref name=Pearcy2004>{{cite journal |last1=Pearcy |first1=M. |last2=Aron |first2=S |last3=Doums |first3=C |last4=Keller |first4=L |title=Conditional Use of Sex and Parthenogenesis for Worker and Queen Production in Ants |journal=Science |volume=306 |issue=5702 |pages=1780–3 |year=2004 |pmid=15576621 |doi=10.1126/science.1105453}}</ref>

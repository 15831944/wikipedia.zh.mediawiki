'''小斋'''在[[基督宗教|基督宗教]]中是一种虔修方式，指在周五（[[东正教|东正教]]还包括周三）进行禁食或不吃（某些）肉类的做法，要求教徒守小斋的宗派包括[[天主教|天主教]]、东正教，以及[[新教|新教]]中的[[圣公宗|圣公宗]]以及[[循道宗|循道宗]]<ref name=OrInf>[http://www.orthodoxinfo.com/praxis/exo_fasting.aspx Concerning Fasting on Wednesday and Friday] {{Wayback|url=http://www.orthodoxinfo.com/praxis/exo_fasting.aspx |date=20180620151351 }}. ''Orthodox Christian Information Center''. Accessed 2010-10-08.</ref><ref name="Crowther1815">{{cite book|last=Crowther|first=Jonathan|title=A Portraiture of Methodism: Or, The History of the Wesleyan Methodists|year=1815|publisher=T. Blanshard|language=en|pages=251, 257}}</ref><ref name=Beard>{{cite journal|title=The spiritual discipline of fasting|last=Beard|first=Steve|date=2012-01-30|publisher=United Methodist Church|newspaper=Good News Magazine }}</ref> 。

{{le|亞歷山大教宗伯多祿|Pope Peter I of Alexandria}}称在周五守小斋是为了纪念[[耶稣|耶稣]]在[[圣周五|圣周五]]的殉难<ref name=OrInf/>。

==内容==
罗马天主教要求教徒在周五禁食热血肉类，但可以食用[[鱼|鱼]][[虾|虾]]等水族<ref>{{cite book|title=中山大学史学集刊|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=CpYdAQAAMAAJ|publisher=广东人民出版社|page=135}}</ref>。东正教要求教徒除在除[[圣诞节|圣诞节]]后12天、[[复活节|复活节]]以及之后一周、[[聖三一主日|聖三一主日]]以及之后一周外的每一个周三、周五守小斋，不能食用或饮用热血肉类、鱼（但可以食用[[贝类|贝类]]）、蛋奶制品、[[橄榄油|橄榄油]]（但可以食用[[油橄榄|橄榄]]），以及[[酒精饮料|酒精饮料]]<ref name="MullinCheskin2014">{{cite book|author1=Gerard E. Mullin|author2=Lawrence J. Cheskin|author3=Laura E. Matarese|title=Integrative Weight Management: A Guide for Clinicians|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=oK68BAAAQBAJ&pg=PA454|year=2014|publisher=Springer Science & Business|isbn=978-1-4939-0548-5|page=454}}</ref><ref name="KittlerSucher2011">{{cite book|author1=Pamela Goyan Kittler|author2=Kathryn P. Sucher|author3=Marcia Nelms|title=Food and Culture|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=R06H7WabJuMC&pg=PA88|date=2011-08-22|publisher=Cengage Learning|isbn=0-538-73497-3|page=88}}</ref>。

新教中的[[循道宗|循道宗]]也有小斋的教规。循道宗创始人[[约翰·卫斯理|约翰·卫斯理]]认为寻求[[圣秩圣事|圣秩圣事]]的教徒应同时在周三（因为犹大在周三[[犹大之吻|背叛耶稣]]）和周五禁食<ref name=Beard>{{cite journal|title=The spiritual discipline of fasting|last=Beard|first=Steve|date=2012-01-30|publisher=United Methodist Church|newspaper=Good News Magazine }}</ref>。根据1744年的一项指令，循道宗信徒应在全年的周五禁食肉类<ref name="Crowther1815"/><ref name="McKnight2010">{{cite book |last1=McKnight |first1=Scot |title=Fasting: The Ancient Practices |date=2010 |publisher=Thomas Nelson |isbn=9781418576134 |pages=88 |language=en|quote=John Wesley, in his ''Journal'', wrote on Friday, August 17, 1739, that "many of our society met, as we had appointed, at one in the afternoon and agreed that all members of our society should obey the Church to which we belong by observing 'all Fridays in the year' as 'days of fasting and abstinence.'}}</ref>。循道宗[[传教士|传教士]]{{le|菲比·帕尔默|Phoebe Palmer}}亦认同这一主张。在19世纪的圣洁运动中，循道宗将守小斋正式纳入教规<ref name="Synan1997">{{cite book|last=Synan|first=Vinson|title=The Holiness-Pentecostal Tradition: Charismatic Movements in the Twentieth Century|date=1997-08-25|publisher=Wm. B. Eerdmans Publishing|language=en|isbn=9780802841032|page=24}}</ref><ref name="Smith2008">{{cite journal|last=Smith|first=Larry D.|date=September 2008|title=Progressive Sanctification|journal=God's Revivalist and Bible Advocate|publisher=God's Bible School and College|volume=120|issue=6|url=https://www.gbs.edu/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/0809_gods_revivalist.pdf|language=en|author=|access-date=2019-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170710192855/https://www.gbs.edu/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/0809_gods_revivalist.pdf|archive-date=2017-07-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。循道宗鼓励教徒在周五禁食，尤其是大斋期内的周五<ref name=Beard/>。[[卫理宗|卫理宗]]也有周五禁食肉类的教规，但很难判断这一教规的实行情况如何<ref>[http://justus.anglican.org/resources/bcp/1928/Calendar&Tables_1928.pdf Tables and Rules for the Movable and Immovable Feasts,Together with the Days of Fasting and Abstinence, through the Whole Year] {{Wayback|url=http://justus.anglican.org/resources/bcp/1928/Calendar%26Tables_1928.pdf |date=20180205035035 }}, p. 3 of 6. ''The 1928 U.S. Book of Common Prayer''. Accessed 2009-04-09.</ref>。

==影响==
[[File:McDonald%27s_Filet-O-Fish_sandwich_(1).jpg|thumb]]]]
天主教为守小斋，在周五-{只}-食用鱼肉而不食用其他肉类，曾在历史上促进[[渔业|渔业]]的发展<ref name="谢觉民1991">{{cite book|author=谢觉民|title=人文地理学|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Bz8EAAAAMAAJ|year=1991|publisher=中国友谊出版公司|page=87}}</ref>。在英国的[[宗教改革运动|宗教改革运动]]中，新教徒为表示自己与天主教徒的区别而选择不在周五食用鱼肉，一度出现新教徒不吃鱼肉的现象，不吃鱼肉也一度成为忠于英国国教圣公宗的象征。英国谚语“{{lang|en|He eats no fish and plays the game}}”（直译意为“他不吃鱼而且玩游戏”）意为“他忠诚而守规矩”<ref>{{cite book|title=二十世纪现代汉语语法论文精选|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=m6oPAAAAYAAJ|year=2005|publisher=商务印书馆|isbn=978-7-100-04142-3|page=26}}</ref>。但在1563年英国女王[[伊丽莎白一世|伊丽莎白一世]]在位时期，为促进渔业的发展、使肉类的价格得到控制，法律又规定周三、周五和周六必须吃鱼而不能吃其他肉类（但[[小牛肉|小牛肉]]、野味、禽肉不在限制之列）<ref>{{cite web |author1=Liza Picard |title=Food in Elizabethan England |url=https://www.bl.uk/shakespeare/articles/food-in-elizabethan-england# |website=British Library |accessdate=2019-03-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170701000518/http://www.bl.uk/shakespeare/articles/food-in-elizabethan-england |archive-date=2017-07-01 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Shakespeare: The strange way people looked at food in the 16th Century |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/magazine-36072989 |website=BBC |accessdate=2019-03-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170403023655/http://www.bbc.com/news/magazine-36072989 |archive-date=2017-04-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

因天主教徒在周五禁食热血肉类，[[麦当劳|麦当劳]]为应对周五汉堡销量的下滑，在1962年推出只含鱼肉而无其他肉类的产品[[麦香鱼|麦香鱼]]<ref name="Schramm2019">{{cite book|author=Carl J. Schramm|title=Burn the Business Plan: What Great Entrepreneurs Really Do|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eZV5DwAAQBAJ&pg=PA150|date=2019-01-08|publisher=Simon and Schuster|isbn=978-1-4767-9437-2|page=150}}</ref>。

'''小鸮属'''（''{{lang|la|Athene}}''）為[[鴟鴞科|鴟鴞科]]下的一[[属_(生物)|属]] ，現生有4種[[物种|物种]]。本屬物種體型較小、喙部棕色或白色，眼睛黃色，眉白色。成員物種廣布於各大洲，除[[澳大利亚|澳洲]]、[[南极洲|南极洲]]及[[撒哈拉以南非洲|漠南非洲]]之外。

該屬學名「''Athene''」命名自希臘女神[[雅典娜|雅典娜]]（Athena），蓋因該屬的[[縱紋腹小鴞|縱紋腹小鴞]]的形象常出現於雅典娜身邊<ref name=job>{{cite book | last= Jobling | first= James A | year= 2010| title= The Helm Dictionary of Scientific Bird Names | publisher= Christopher Helm | location = London | isbn = 978-1-4081-2501-4 | pages = 58, 274}}</ref>。

The Cretan owl was a flightless or near-flightless form that was more than 50 cm (almost 2 ft) tall. It went [[绝灭|extinct]] soon after the island of Crete became inhabited by humans.

{{tsl|en|Late Miocene||Late Miocene}} (about 11 mya) fossil remains from [[魯道巴尼奧|Rudabánya]] (NE [[匈牙利|Hungary]]) have been tentatively assigned to this genus.<ref>Bernor, R.L.; Kordos, L. & Rook, L. (eds): "[http://rocek.gli.cas.cz/Reprints/Bernor%20et%20al.pdf Recent Advances on Multidisciplinary Research at Rudabánya, Late Miocene (MN9), Hungary: A compendium.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070628021930/http://rocek.gli.cas.cz/Reprints/Bernor%20et%20al.pdf |date=2007-06-28 }}" ''Paleontographica Italiana'' '''89''': 3-36.</ref> Considering the known fossil range of ''Athene'' and the misassignments of many Miocene strigids from Europe, it may be a [[基群|basal]] member of the present genus or not belong here. The supposed species ''"Athene" murivora'' is the name given to subfossil bones of male {{tsl|en|Rodrigues owl||Rodrigues owl}}s.

'''小飛鼠'''（屬名：'''''Petaurillus'''''；英語名：'''Pygmy flying squirrels'''）是[[鼯鼠|鼯鼠]]中的一個屬，包含以下物種：

* [[Lesser_pygmy_flying_squirrel|Lesser pygmy flying squirrel]] ''[[Petaurillus_emiliae|Petaurillus emiliae]]'' Thomas, 1908 
* [[Hose's_pygmy_flying_squirrel|Hose's pygmy flying squirrel]] ''[[Petaurillus_hosei|Petaurillus hosei]]'' (Thomas, 1900) 
* [[Selangor_pygmy_flying_squirrel|Selangor pygmy flying squirrel]] ''[[Petaurillus_kinlochii|Petaurillus kinlochii]]'' (Robinson and Kloss, 1911)

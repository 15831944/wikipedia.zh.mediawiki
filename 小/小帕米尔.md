'''小帕米尔'''（[[Wakhi_language|Wakhi language]]: ''Wuch Pamir''; [[Kyrgyz_language|Kyrgyz]]: ''Kichik Pamir''; {{lang-fa|''Pāmīr-e Kochak''或''Pāmīr-e Khord''}}<ref name=erdkunde>{{Cite journal  | last = Felmy  | first = Sabine  | authorlink =   | author2 = Kreutzmann, Hermann  | title = Wakhan Woluswali in Badakhshan  | journal = Erdkunde  | volume = 58  | issue =   | pages = 97–117  | publisher =   | location =   | year = 2004  | url = http://www.mtnforum.org/oldocs/831.pdf  | issn =   | doi = 10.3112/erdkunde.2004.02.01  | id =   | accessdate = 24 July 2010  }}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name=lp>[http://content.yudu.com/Library/A1nrka/AsiaTravelAFGHANISTA/resources/118.htm Lonely Planet (2007):'  ''Afghanistan'' p.170]</ref>)是[[帕米尔高原|帕米尔高原]]的[[八帕|八帕]]之一，位于[[瓦罕走廊|瓦罕走廊]]最东端的山谷，是[[阿富汗|阿富汗]]最东部的领土。山谷东西向长100 km，南北宽10 km<ref>[http://www.akdn.org/publications/2010_akf_wakhan.pdf Aga Khan Development Network (2010):  ''Wakhan and the Afghan Pamir''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110123124819/http://www.akdn.org/publications/2010_akf_wakhan.pdf |date=2011-01-23 }} p.3</ref>，北部为{{tsl|en|Nicholas Range (Pamir Mountains)|瓦罕岭}}。

{{tsl|en|Chaqmaqtin Lake|卡克马同提尼湖}}（9 km长，2 km宽）位于西端，托克满苏（Tegerman Su）山谷位于东端。发源于小帕米尔卡克马同提尼湖的阿克苏河（[[巴爾坦格河|巴爾坦格河]]源流）及下游的[[穆尔加布河|穆尔加布河]]从卡克马同提尼湖东流穿过整个小帕米尔。湖西侧的Bozai Darya（也称{{tsl|en|Little Pamir River|小帕米尔河}}）向西流15 km与Wakhjir River汇合后成为{{tsl|en|Wakhan River|瓦罕河}}。<ref>Some accounts state that the Bozai Darya also rises from Chaqmaqtin Lake.  See [http://nzdl.sadl.uleth.ca/gsdl/collect/areu/Upload/1710/Kamal_River%20basins%20and%20watersheds2004.pdf Afghanistan Information Management Service: ''River basins and Watersheds of Afghanistan" (2004)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110706211414/http://nzdl.sadl.uleth.ca/gsdl/collect/areu/Upload/1710/Kamal_River%20basins%20and%20watersheds2004.pdf |date=2011-07-06 }}, p.5.  The [http://www.law.fsu.edu/library/collection/LimitsinSeas/IBS026.pdf International Boundary Study of the Afghanistan-USSR Boundary (1983)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140817020033/http://www.law.fsu.edu/library/collection/LimitsinSeas/IBS026.pdf |date=2014-08-17 }} by 美国{{tsl|en"Bureau of Intelligence and Research|情报研究局}}, p.10, calls the lake "a deeper and possibly marshy section within the Aq Su-Little Pamir River drainage divide".</ref>  

小帕米尔是吉尔吉斯人牧民的夏季草场。1979年阿富汗战争开始后，当地约有200土著吉尔吉斯人牧民。<ref>[http://www.juldu.com/Pamir/HERMANN%20KREUTZMANN.pdf Hermann Kreutzmann (2003) ''Ethnic minorities and marginality in the Pamirian Knot'']</ref> 2003年有140户牧民。<ref name=erdkunde/>

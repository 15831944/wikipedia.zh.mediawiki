'''尋屍犬'''（{{lang-en|Cadaver dogs}}）是[[工作犬|工作犬]]的一種，指經特殊訓練、能尋找人類[[屍體|屍體]]的犬隻<ref>{{Cite news|title=Cadaver dogs lead grim search for victims in California fire|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-california-wildfires-dogs/cadaver-dogs-lead-grim-search-for-victims-in-california-fire-idUSKCN1NK022|newspaper=Reuters|date=2018-11-15|accessdate=2019-02-27|language=en|author=}}</ref>。其任務包括尋找命案遇害者的遺體、尋找[[失蹤者|失蹤人士]]以及尋找災難現場的屍體和殘骸<ref>{{Cite web|title=曾在芬兰受过特训．6寻尸犬连连建功|url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/1241935|date=2011-08-19|accessdate=2019-02-27|language=zh-hans|publisher=星洲网}}</ref>。

尋屍犬大多由[[德國牧羊犬|德國牧羊犬]]和[[拉不拉多|拉不拉多]]擔綱。一般而言，要成為尋屍犬的犬隻得在18個月大到2歲時接受訓練<ref>{{Cite news|title=Cadaver dogs: attending camp with the canines trained to smell death|url=https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2015/sep/08/cadaver-dogs-trained-to-smell-death|newspaper=The Guardian|date=2015-09-08|accessdate=2019-02-27|issn=0261-3077|language=en-GB|first=Liz|last=Lucking}}</ref>。

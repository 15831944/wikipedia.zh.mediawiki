'''蝟蝦下目'''（'''Stenopodidea'''）是[[十足目|十足目]]下的一個小下目。該下目的蝦常被與[[真蝦下目|真蝦下目]]或[[枝鳃亚目|枝鳃亚目]]混淆，但實際上這一類蝦梗更接近[[爬行亞目|爬行亞目]]（例如[[龍蝦|龍蝦]]和[[螃蟹|螃蟹]]）。其擴大的第三[[步足|步足]]是典型特徵。<ref name="Carideorum">{{cite journal |author=S. De Grave & C. H. J. M. Fransen |year=2011 |title=Carideorum Catalogus: the Recent species of the dendrobranchiate, stenopodidean, procarididean and caridean shrimps (Crustacea: Decapoda) |journal=[[Zoologische_Mededelingen|Zoologische Mededelingen]] |volume=85 |issue=9 |pages=195–589, figs. 1–59 |isbn=978-90-6519-200-4 |publisher= |url=http://www.zoologischemededelingen.nl/85/nr02/a01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20121220093841/http://www.zoologischemededelingen.nl/85/nr02/a01 |archivedate=2012-12-20 }}</ref> 現該科下有3科12屬71種現存物種。<ref name="Grave">{{cite journal |journal = [[Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |year=2009 |volume = Suppl. 21 |pages=1–109 |title = A classification of living and fossil genera of decapod crustaceans |author=Sammy De Grave, N. Dean Pentcheff, Shane T.

'''鱼香'''是一种用于[[中餐|中餐]]的混合[[调料|调料]]，也用来指烹饪肉菜时所加入的调味汁。传说鱼香起源自[[川菜|川菜]]，但在中餐的其他[[菜系|菜系]]中也极为普遍。<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|title=Land of Plenty: A Treasury of Authentic Sichuan Cooking|last=Dunlop|first=Fuchsia|publisher=|year=2003|isbn=978-0393051773|location=|pages=395}}</ref>尽管名为“鱼香”，但这种调料并不包含[[海鲜|海鲜]]成分，也不被算进海鲜品类中。制作鱼香通常会使用到[[食糖|糖]]、[[醋|醋]]、[[豆瓣酱|豆瓣酱]]、[[酱油|酱油]]和[[泡椒|泡椒]]。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Vr2qnK_QOuAC&pg=PA1174|title=The Cambridge world history of food. 2|last=Kiple|first=Kenneth F.|last2=Ornelas|first2=Kriemhild Coneè|date=2000|publisher=Cambridge University Press|year=|isbn=9780521402156|location=|pages=|language=en}}</ref>

一些菜品的名称含有鱼香两字<ref>{{Cite book|title=Szechuan Chinese Cuisine: Spicy and Delicious Recipes of China|last=Stevens|first=JR|publisher=|year=2017|isbn=|location=|pages=101|asin=B074LK4KZP}}</ref>，例如[[鱼香肉丝|鱼香肉丝]]<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=e5rLVM5SknQC&pg=PT137|title=Mrs. Chiang's Szechwan Cookbook: Szechwan Home Cooking|last=Schrecker|first=Ellen|date=2010-08-30|publisher=askmar publishing|year=|isbn=9781935842101|location=|pages=|language=en|chapter=}}</ref>、[[魚香茄子|鱼香茄子]]<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Q-dlgvTZdtQC&pg=PT189|title=Pei-Mei's Chinese Cookbook|last=Pei-Mei|first=Fu|date=2010-07-26|publisher=askmar publishing|year=|isbn=9781935842040|location=|pages=|language=en}}</ref>和鱼香牛腩。

== 制作鱼香 ==
制作鱼香需要准备切碎的[[泡椒|泡椒]]、[[葱|白葱]]、[[生姜|生姜]]和[[大蒜|大蒜]]。人们大多等量混合这些原料，但也有人喜欢多放泡椒少放-{zh-hans:姜;zh-hant:薑}-蒜。然后，油炸这些食材直到飘出香气。然后，可以加入水、淀粉、糖、醋等做出基本的酱料。<ref name=":0" />

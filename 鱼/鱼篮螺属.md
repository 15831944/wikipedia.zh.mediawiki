'''鱼篮螺属'''（学名：{{lang|la|''Nassaria''}}）{{r|Zhang 2015}}，較舊的文獻亦作
'''鱼兰螺属'''{{r|shelldoc_420090220145}}或'''海因螺屬'''（学名：{{lang|la|''Hindsia''}}）{{r|shell_6083|shell_6084}}，是[[新腹足目|新腹足目]][[峨螺科|峨螺科]]之下一個[[海洋生物|海洋]][[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]]的[[屬_(生物)|屬]]{{r|shell_6083|shell_6084 |WoRMS_205431|WoRMS_491146 |Zhang 2015}}。

==模式種==
魚籃螺屬的模式種的問題有點混亂：Cernohorsky (1981) 及 Fraussen (2006) 假設所謂的「''Nassaria lyrata'' {{small|Link, 1807}}」在Link (1807)列出的物種中應該是指「''Buccinum lyratum'' {{small|Gmelin, 1791}}」，而不是另外一個新的分類元。
Link’s (1807) original reference reads “N. lyrata. L. G. p. 3494. M.C. 4, t. 122, f. 1122, 1123“；而「L.G.」是 Linnaeus, Gmelin 版本的縮寫，「M.C.」則是Martini & Chemnitz的“Conchylien Cabinet”。

Nevertheless, as pointed out by Mörch (1862), MacNeil (1961), and others, the two cited references are inconsistent. Gmelin’s ''Buccinum lyratum'' is introduced on pp. 3494-3495, and currently considered as a synonym of ''[[Boreotrophon_clathratus|Boreotrophon clathratus]]'' <small>(Linnaeus, 1767)</small> whereas the reference to Martini & Chemnitz (1780), vol. 4, t. 122, f. 1122, 1123 matches ''[[Buccinum_album|Buccinum album]]'' <small>Gmelin, 1791</small>, described on page 3495 with reference to the same figures. The vernacular name “Fischreuseschnecken” (a German translation of Nassa snails) used by Link matches “Fischreuse” used by Martini & Chemnitz for f. 1122, 1123, indicating that the latter was the species really intended. This is the rationale for the current interpretation, where ''Nassaria lyrata'' <small>Link, 1807</small> is considered an available name based on Martini & Chemnitz f. 1122, 1123 and objective junior synonym of Buccinum album Gmelin, 1791.  <ref name="WoRMS_491146" />

==分佈==
本屬物種主要分佈於[[印度洋-太平洋海域|印度洋-西太平洋海域]]的[[暖海区|暖海区]]{{r|Zhang 2015}}, 一般栖息於近岸浅水或稍深的[[潮下带|潮下带]]{{r|shell_6083|shell_6084 |shelldoc_420090220135}}海底, 少数种类生活在近1000米深的海底{{r|Zhang 2015}}。
[[中国大陆|中国大陆]]的[[東中國海|東海]]、[[南中國海|南海]]{{r|shell_6083|shell_6084}}近海海域、[[台灣海峽|台灣海峽]]{{r|shell_6083|shelldoc_200135}}以及[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]]{{r|shell_1072}}。

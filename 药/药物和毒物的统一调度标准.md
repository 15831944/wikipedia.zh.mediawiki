'''《药物和毒物的统一调度标准》'''（{{lang-en|'''S'''tandard for the '''U'''niform '''S'''cheduling of '''M'''edicines and '''P'''oisons}}，{{abbr|'''SUSMP'''|Standard for the Uniform Scheduling of Medicines and Poisons}}）是[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]{{tsl|en|Therapeutic Goods Administration|澳大利亚政府药物管理局|政府药物管理局}}出台的{{tsl|en|legislative instrument|法律文书}}。<ref name="austlii-tga-s">{{Cite Legislation AU|Cth|act|tga1989191|Therapeutic Goods Act 1989|52D}}, subsection 4A.</ref>在2010年之前，该文书曾名为{{lang|en|'''S'''tandard for the '''U'''niform '''S'''cheduling of '''D'''rugs and '''P'''oisons}}（{{abbr|'''SUSDP'''|Standard for the Uniform Scheduling of Drugs and Poisons}}）。<ref name="fedleg-poisons-2009">{{Citation|publisher=Department of Health|website=Federal Register of Legislation|title=''Poisons Standard 2009''|url=https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/F2009L03012|date=2009-08-03}}.</ref>{{abbr|SUSMP|药物和毒物的统一调度标准}}将[[药物|药物]]和[[毒物|毒物]]划入不同的调度标准、使用量，并就药物向大众推广的普及程度等问题提出了相应的意见。<ref name="fedleg-poisons-Oct-2016-explanatory">{{Citation|publisher=Department of Health|website=Federal Register of Legislation|title=Explanatory statement: ''Poisons Standard October 2016''|url=https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/F2016L01505/Explanatory%20Statement/Text|date=2016-10-30}}.</ref>

该调度标准基于[[澳大利亚行政区划|澳大利亚本土]]有关法律提出，旨在管控药物。虽然澳大利亚的每个省都有着自己的一套法律，但大部分药物都是按照{{abbr|SUSMP|药物和毒物的统一调度标准}}的标准进行分类的，以达到全国各地藥物管理标准统一。<ref name="tga-states">{{cite web|publisher={{tsl|en|Therapeutic Goods Administration||Therapeutic Goods Administration}}|title=Contacts for State/Territory drugs & poisons units|date=2015-12-07|url=https://www.tga.gov.au/contacts-stateterritory-drugs-poisons-units}}</ref>当前的版本，{{abbr|SUSMP|药物和毒物的统一调度标准}} 16，收录在了《[[2017年2月毒物标准|2017年2月毒物标准]]》里。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/F2017L00057|title=Poisons Standard, February 2017|last=|first=|date=|website=Legislation.gov.au|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=}}</ref>

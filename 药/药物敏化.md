'''药物敏化'''（{{lang-en|drug sensitization}}），也称'''逆耐药性'''（{{lang-en|reverse tolerance}}），是指在[[藥物耐受性|耐药性]]已经确立后，不敏感度的减少。<ref>{{cite journal | pmid = 3437393 | volume=10 | title=Cross reverse tolerance between amphetamine, cocaine and morphine | author=Hijikuro K, Kaneto H | journal=J Pharmacobiodyn | pages=503–5 | doi=10.1248/bpb1978.10.503}}</ref><ref>[http://deep6inc.com/previewcoc16.html Drugs & Death : Profiles of illegal drug abuse. Joseph C. Rupp, M.D., Ph.D.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081218102206/http://deep6inc.com/previewcoc16.html |date=2008-12-18 }}</ref>一般来说，这种现象是由于加药、停药（{{tsl|en|drug holiday|用药假期}}）导致。[[苯丙胺|苯丙胺]]和[[选择性5-羟色胺再摄取抑制剂|选择性5-羟色胺再摄取抑制剂]]（SSRI）等药物都会引发药物敏化的情况。<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Leith N, Kuczenski R | title = Chronic amphetamine: tolerance and reverse tolerance reflect different behavioral actions of the drug. | journal = Pharmacol Biochem Behav | volume = 15 | issue = 3 | pages = 399–404 | year = 1981 | pmid = 7291243 | doi = 10.1016/0091-3057(81)90269-0}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Chaudhry I, Turkanis S, Karler R | title = Characteristics of "reverse tolerance" to amphetamine-induced locomotor stimulation in mice. | journal = Neuropharmacology | volume = 27 | issue = 8 | pages = 777–81 | year = 1988 | pmid = 3216957 | doi = 10.1016/0028-3908(88)90091-3}}</ref>

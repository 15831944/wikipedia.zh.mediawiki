'''剛布理剛'''（{{lang-en|Ghân-buri-Ghân}}）是在[[J·R·R·托爾金|J·R·R·托爾金]]奇幻小說《[[魔戒|魔戒]]》裡登場的角色，[[第三紀元|第三紀元]]期間，他領導著居住在[[督伊頓森林|督伊頓森林]]裡的[[野人|野人]]族群<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.glyphweb.com/arda/g/ghanburighan.html|title=Ghân-buri-Ghân|accessdate=2018-04-12|publisher=阿爾達百科全書|language=en-GB}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.theonering.net/torwp/2013/07/13/75554-six-overlooked-yet-important-characters-from-the-lord-of-the-rings/|title=Six overlooked yet important characters from The Lord of the Rings|date=2013-07-13|newspaper=TheOneRing.net|accessdate=2018-04-12|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>Smith, Mark Eddy (2002). [https://books.google.com/books?id=ridvKJhsXUIC&pg=PA108&dq=Ghan-Buri-Ghan&hl=en&sa=X&ei=xRweVPanGoLIiwLTnIFg&ved=0CDsQ6AEwAw#v=onepage&q=Ghan-Buri-Ghan&f=false ''Tolkien's Ordinary Virtues'']. Intervarsity Press. p. 108. ISBN 0-8308-2312-3.</ref>。在《魔戒》第三部分《[[王者再臨|王者再臨]]》，剛布理剛指引[[洛汗國王|洛汗國王]][[希優頓|希優頓]]和他的軍隊穿越督伊頓森林，讓他們可以順利趕去支援[[米那斯提力斯|米那斯提力斯]]的戰事<ref>Day, David (1991). [https://books.google.com/books?id=Nzh93npJTe4C&pg=PA250&dq=Ghan-Buri-Ghan&hl=en&sa=X&ei=xRweVPanGoLIiwLTnIFg&ved=0CDUQ6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=Ghan-Buri-Ghan&f=false ''Tolkien: The Illustrated Encyclopedia'']. Simon and Schuster. p. 250.</ref>；作為回報，希優頓承諾他的子民往後將與野人族群保持和平，並出兵趕走剛布理剛所痛恨的「哥剛」（即[[半獸人|半獸人]]）<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|title=魔戒三部曲：王者再臨|last=托爾金|publisher=聯經|year=2012年12月|isbn=978-957-08-4102-2|location=臺北}}</ref>。

在整個[[魔戒聖戰|魔戒聖戰]]告終、[[中土大陸|中土]]恢復和平之際，剛[[加冕|加冕]]為[[剛鐸國王|剛鐸國王]]的[[亞拉岡_(魔戒)|亞拉岡]]宣布將督伊頓森林交給剛布理剛與他的族人管理，沒有他們的許可，任何人都不能擅自進入該處<ref name=":0" />。

剛布理剛被認為是當時中土大陸裡尚存在的一種[[史前|史前]]類型的[[人類_(中土大陸)|人類]]。跟他的其他族人一樣，剛布理剛有一張平坦的臉，[[皮膚|皮膚]]和[[眼睛|眼睛]]都非常黑，並且只穿一條草裙<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=PQSpu0lU8K4C&pg=PT123&dq=Ghan-buri-Ghan&hl=en&sa=X&ei=FhU-Uq27C-qpiQLJqIGgDg&ved=0CEEQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=Ghan-buri-Ghan&f=false Chance, Jane, ''Tolkien the Medievalist''], Routledge, 2005, page 100</ref>。他被視為一個好人，是高尚[[野蠻人|野蠻人]]的典型例子<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=FRiViwMylSUC&pg=PA286&dq=%22Gh%C3%A2n-buri-Gh%C3%A2n%22+-inpublisher:icon&lr=%20&ei=B_ygS8S6MoyaMrTdtdQH&cd=14#v=onepage&q=%20&f=false Rutledge, Fleming, ''The Battle for Middle-earth: Tolkien's Divine Design in the Lord of the Rings,''] page 286, William B. Eerdmans Publishing, 2004</ref><ref>Abigail E Ruane，Patrick James .[https://books.google.com.tw/books?id=EUEOHLDka-IC&pg=PA65&lpg=PA65&dq=Ghan+buri+Ghan+lotr&source=bl&ots=JAkVrU5yyD&sig=RqVKyBm8jnJEiSHw-JR_3oJ19pY&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwi0zdbAn7XaAhVDxrwKHe56C-04KBDoAQg0MAI#v=onepage&q=Ghan%20buri%20Ghan%20lotr&f=false ''The International Relations of Middle-earth: Learning from The Lord of the Rings''].University of Michigan Press, 2012.</ref>。他也並不愚蠢，謝絕被他人庇護<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=Wo9asc1i09YC&pg=PA79&dq=ghan-buri-ghan&lr=%20&ei=Mv6gS52JGJTaMcmenNIH&cd=23#v=onepage&q=ghan-buri-ghan&f=false Stanton, Michael L., ''Hobbits, Elves, and Wizards: Exploring the wonders and worlds of J.R.R. Tolkien,''] page 79, Palgrave McMillan, 2001</ref>。

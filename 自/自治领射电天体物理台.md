'''自治领射电天体物理台'''（{{lang-en|'''Dominion Radio Astrophysical Observatory'''}}，缩写为{{lang|en|'''DRAO'''}}）是成立于1960年的一所[[研究|研究]]机构，位于[[加拿大|加拿大]][[不列颠哥伦比亚省|不列颠哥伦比亚省]]Okanagan Falls的西南部。 <ref>{{Cite journal|title=Official Opening of The Dominion Radio Astrophysical Observatory White Lake, Penticton, B.C., June 20, 1960|url=http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1960JRASC..54..269O|last=J.|first=Odgers, G.|date=1960|journal=Journal of the Royal Astronomical Society of Canada|volume=54|pages=|language=en|issn=0035-872X}}</ref>该站点有4台[[射电望远镜|射电望远镜]]：[[干涉測量術|干涉]]射电望远镜，26米单碟[[天线|天线]]，[[太阳|太阳]][[通量|通量]]监测器和[[加拿大氢强度测绘实验|加拿大氢强度测绘实验]]（CHIME），以及支持[[工程学|工程]][[实验室|实验室]]。DRAO由加拿大政府下属的国家研究委员会{{le|赫茨贝格天体物理研究所|Herzberg Institute of Astrophysics}}运营。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ieeeghn.org/wiki/index.php/Milestones:List_of_IEEE_Milestones|title=List of IEEE Milestones|accessdate=4 August 2011|author=|date=|work=IEEE Global History Network|publisher=IEEE}}</ref> 

== 设备 ==

=== 综合孔径望远镜 ===
[[File:DRAO_synthisis.JPG|缩略图]]
综合孔径望远镜由沿着600米东西向基线的7个9米金属网反射器天线组成。这些天线配备408 [[赫兹|MHz]]的单圆[[偏振|偏振]]接收机和1420 MHz的双圆形接收机。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/facilities/hia/radio-astrophysical/synthesis-telescope.html|title=DRAO Synthesis Telescope - Facilities - NRC-CNRC|accessdate=2012-01-17|publisher=National Research Council Canada|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120723014514/http://www.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/facilities/hia/radio-astrophysical/synthesis-telescope.html|archivedate=2012-07-23}}</ref> 

=== John A. Galt望远镜 ===
[[File:DRAO_26m_dish.JPG|缩略图]]
John A. Galt望远镜包含一个25.6米的金属网天线，可在408 MHz和1.5，2.7，4.9，6.6和8.4 GHz等频率进行观测。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/facilities/hia/radio-astrophysical/26m-telescope.html|title=DRAO 26-m Telescope - Facilities - NRC-CNRC|accessdate=2012-01-17|publisher=National Research Council Canada|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120721121203/http://www.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/facilities/hia/radio-astrophysical/26m-telescope.html|archivedate=2012-07-21}}</ref>这台望远镜以前简称为26米望远镜，在2014年举行的特别仪式上更名为John A. Galt望远镜，他是DRAO的第一位员工及前任主任。 <ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.pentictonherald.ca/news/article_1e217f74-42c2-11e4-|title=Special dedication at DRAO|author=Moorhouse|first=John|date=2014-09-22|work=[[Penticton_Herald|Penticton Herald]]|accessdate=2015-07-29|location=[[Penticton,_British_Columbia|Penticton, British Columbia]]}}{{Dead link|date=September 2017|fix-attempted=yes|bot=InternetArchiveBot}}</ref> 

=== 太阳流量监测器 ===
[[File:DRAO_solar_flux.JPG|缩略图]]
该监视器位于不列颠哥伦比亚省彭蒂克顿附近，由两个固体表面碟形天线组成，在波长10.7厘米处同时观测<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/facilities/hia/radio-astrophysical/highlights-of-drao.html|title=Highlights of DRAO - Facilities - NRC-CNRC|accessdate=2012-01-17|publisher=National Research Council Canada|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120720111148/http://www.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/facilities/hia/radio-astrophysical/highlights-of-drao.html|archivedate=2012-07-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=The Penticton Solar Flux Receiver|last=John White and Ken Tapping|date=February 2013|journal=QST|publisher=The [[American_Radio_Relay_League|American Radio Relay League]]|pages=39–45}}</ref> 

=== 加拿大氢强度测绘实验 ===
[[加拿大氢强度测绘实验|加拿大氢强度测绘实验]]（CHIME）是一种射电干涉仪。它由四个半圆柱形反射器天线组成，每个长100 米，宽 20 米。这架望远镜可以测量[[重子声学振荡|重子声学振荡]]，并监测[[快速射电暴|快速射电暴]]。<ref name="2017-09-07_TGAM">[https://beta.theglobeandmail.com/technology/science/chime-telescope-dark-energy/article36183652/ Listening for the universe to chime in], Ivan Semeniuk, [[The_Globe_and_Mail|The Globe and Mail]], 2017-09-07</ref><ref name="2017-09-11_SD">[http://www.spacedaily.com/reports/Canadian_ingenuity_crafts_game_changing_technology_for_CHIME_telescope_999.html Canadian ingenuity crafts game-changing technology for CHIME telescope], [[SpaceDaily|SpaceDaily]], 2017-09-11</ref>

=== 工程 ===
除观测设备外，DRAO还负责接收机和电子设备的设计和开发。其项目包括外部天文台的仪器，如[[甚大天线阵|甚大阵]]和詹姆斯·克拉克·麦克斯韦望远镜 。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/solutions/facilities/drao.html|title=Astronomy Technology Research Facilities at Penticton - Facilities - NRC-CNRC|accessdate=2014-04-02|publisher=National Research Council Canada}}</ref>

'''自殺方式'''是指一個人用以[[自殺|自殺]]的任何手段，全球相對較為普遍的自殺方式包括[[服毒|服毒]]、[[自縊|自縊]]和[[吞槍|吞槍]]<ref name="who 实况报道">{{cite web|title=自杀|url=http://www.who.int/mediacentre/factsheets/fs398/zh/|year=2018|publisher=世卫组织|accessdate=2018-03-20|work=实况报道}}</ref>；其他自殺方式則包括[[自溺|淹溺]]、從高處跳下、電擊、[[窒息|窒息]]、[[藥物過量|服用及注射過量藥物]]等<ref name="Means Matter"/><ref name="Med Sci Law"/><ref name="Suffocation-Smothering"/>。

放血自殺涉及到大量的出血，其會使血液的體積和血壓下降。大量失血通常是[[动脉|動脈]]損傷的结果<ref>{{cite web|title=外伤出血如何急救?|url=http://www.redcross.org.cn/hhzh/zh/hxyw/yjjh/zswd/200806/t20080620_886.html|publisher=中国红十字会|date=2003-02-25|accessdate=2018-03-19}}</ref>。[[頸動脈|頸動脈]]、橈動脈、尺動脈和[[股動脈|股動脈]]皆有可能是自殺者的目标。身體[[血容量減少|血容量減少]]或不足会令人直接致死（血容量下降40%的人不太可能存活<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Gutierrezy|first1=Guillermo|last2=Reines|first2=H David|last3=Wulf-Gutierrez|first3=Marian E|title=Clinical review: Hemorrhagic shock|journal=Crit Care|volume=8|issue=5|pages=373–381|year=2004|pmid=15469601|pmc=1065003|doi=10.1186/cc2851}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Hemorrhagic Shock in Trauma|url=http://boneandspine.com/hemorrhagic-shock-in-trauma/|author=Arun Pal Singh||publisher=bone and spine|accessdate=2018-04-11}}</ref>），因為循環系統中的血液體積会影響其載氧能力，一旦過低便會使體細胞欠缺其運作所必需的氧氣<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Klonsky|first1=E. D.|last2=May|first2=A. M.|title=Impulsivity and Suicide Risk: Review and Clinical Implications|journal=Psychiatric Times|date=2015|volume=32|issue=8|page=13}}</ref>。 

擁有自殺企圖的人可能会先切开一段或數段較淺的傷口，文獻中会稱其为試探性創傷（tentative wounds）或猶豫性創傷（hesitation wounds）。猶豫性創傷通常为幾條平行的非致命傷口<ref>{{cite web|last=Pounder|first=Derrick|title=Lecture Notes in Forensic Medicine|url=http://www.dundee.ac.uk/forensicmedicine/notes/Lecture%20Notes%20in%20Forensic%20Medicine%20Derrick%20Pounder%2048pages.pdf|accessdate=2011-04-16|page=6|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110614015411/http://www.dundee.ac.uk/forensicmedicine/notes/Lecture%20Notes%20in%20Forensic%20Medicine%20Derrick%20Pounder%2048pages.pdf|archivedate=2011-06-14|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。2015年的台湾透過自殺通報關懷系統通報的個案中，以「切穿工具來自殺或自傷」的個案為第二常見，佔全部個案的27.1%<ref>{{cite journal |title=自殺通報關懷系統通報-2015年通報個案統計|journal=自殺防治網通訊|volume=11|issue=1|page=13|url=https://issuu.com/tspc/docs/11-1|date=2016年4月}}</ref>，前幾年的排名不變<ref>{{cite journal |title=自殺通報關懷系統通報-2014年通報個案統計|journal=自殺防治網通訊|url=https://issuu.com/tspcebook/docs/________________________10-1|volume=10|issue=1|pages=7-8|date=2015年3月}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |title=自殺通報關懷系統通報-2013年通報個案統計|journal=自殺防治網通訊|url=https://issuu.com/tspcebook/docs/_____________________9-1|volume=9|issue=1|page=6|date=2014年3月}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |title=自殺通報關懷系統通報-2012年通報個案統計|journal=自殺防治網通訊|url=https://issuu.com/tspcebook/docs/_____________________8-1|volume=8|issue=1|pages=5-6|date=2013年3月}}</ref>。

===割腕===
[[割腕|割腕]]的目的可能只是[[自傷|自傷]]而不是自殺，但若出血量大或不加以控制地繼續，便有机会引起[[心律失常|心律失常]]，繼使当事人陸續地出現血容量不足、[[休克|休克]]、循環衰竭/[[心搏停止|心搏停止]]的情况，最终致死<ref>{{cite web|last=Dutton MD. MBA|first=Richard P.|title=Pathophysiology of Traumatic Shock|url=http://www.itaccs.com/traumacare/archive/2008_vol18_no1/Pathophysiology_of_Traumatic_Shock_Itaccs_vol18no1-4.pdf|work=Web|accessdate=2013-08-23|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101009094747/http://www.itaccs.com/traumacare/archive/2008_vol18_no1/Pathophysiology_of_Traumatic_Shock_Itaccs_vol18no1-4.pdf|archivedate=2010-10-09}}</ref>。在企圖自殺但失敗的情況下，外屈肌的[[肌腱|肌腱]]、控制手部肌肉的尺神經和[[正中神经|正中神经]]皆可能會受損。以上兩种情況都可能使当事人的感覺或運動能力暫時或永久地減弱，此外亦有一定机会導致慢性疼痛<ref>{{cite journal | last = Bukhari | first =AJ |author2=Saleem M |author3=Bhutta AR |author4=Khan AZ |author5=Abid KJ.  | title = Spaghetti wrist: management and outcome | journal = J Coll Physicians Surg Pak. | volume = 14 | issue =10 | pages = 608–11 |year=2004 | pmid = 15456551}}</ref>。

==窒息==
窒息式自殺是指当事人故意抑制呼吸能力或限制呼吸時所吸取的氧氣的行為，目的是令自己[[缺氧|缺氧]][[窒息|窒息]]而死<ref name="Suffocation-Smothering">{{cite web | url =http://www.forensicpathologyonline.com/e-book/asphyxia/suffocation-smothering|title =Suffocation-Smothering|accessdate=2018-03-20|publisher= Forensic Pathology Online |last=Rao|first=D}}</ref>。此一自殺方法有著許多不同的變種，包括以[[自殺用塑料袋|自殺用塑料袋]]蓋著頭部<ref name="PDH">{{cite book |last1=Nitschke |first1=Philip |last2=Stewart |first2=Fiona |title=The Peaceful Pill Handbook |chapter=Hypoxic Death and the Exit bag |publisher=Exit International US Limited |year=2016 |isbn=9780975833919 }}</ref>{{rp|45}}<ref name="Suffocation-Smothering"/>、把自身禁閉於沒有氧氣的封閉空間内<ref>{{cite book|last1=Huether|first1=Sue E|last2=McCance|first2=Kathryn L.|title=Understanding Pathophysiology - E-Book|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=vyGKCwAAQBAJ&pg=PA94|page=94 |publisher=Mosby |date=2015-12-23|accessdate=2018-03-20}}</ref>。

人們不可能透過屏住呼吸自殺，當血液中氧氣濃度過低時，腦部便會使呼吸肌不隨意地反射，令其收縮，繼使當事人恢復呼吸。即使有人能夠克服這種無意識的反應，其也不能長久地控制呼吸<ref>{{cite web|last=Kurzban|first=Robert|title=Why Can’t You Hold Your Breath Until You’re Dead?|url=http://www.epjournal.net/blog/2011/02/why-can%E2%80%99t-you-hold-your-breath-until-you%E2%80%99re-dead/|work=Web.|accessdate=2013-08-23|date=2011-02-07}}</ref>。

因此與閉氣相比，人們透過吸入氣體來自殺的成功機會會較高。意圖自殺者常透過吸入[[不活性氣體|不活性氣體]]和有毒氣體來使自己在短短幾分鐘內窒息致死。不活性氣體的例子有[[氦|氦氣]]、[[氮氣|氮氣]]、[[氬|氬氣]]，有毒氣體則包括[[一氧化碳中毒|一氧化碳]]<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.osha.gov/dts/shib/shib042704.html | title=Deaths Involving the Inadvertent Connection of Air-line Respirators to Inert Gas Supplies }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Goldstein M|title=Carbon monoxide poisoning|journal=Journal of Emergency Nursing|volume=34|issue=6|pages=538–542|year=2008|pmid=19022078|doi=10.1016/j.jen.2007.11.014}}</ref>。澳洲醫生尼奇克（Philip Nitschke）及荷蘭設計師班尼克（Alexander Bannink）所設計，並預計於2019年上傳至網路予人3D打印的自殺膠囊艙Sarco亦是基於此一原理——把氮氣注入艙內，令自殺者於短時間內失去意識，直至死亡<ref name="hp2018">{{cite news |last=Nitschke |first=Philip |url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/sarco-death-philip-nitschke_us_5abbb574e4b03e2a5c7853ca |title=Here’s Why I Invented A ‘Death Machine’ That Lets People Take Their Own Lives |work=Huffington Post|date=2018-04-05 |accessdate=2018-04-06 }}</ref><ref name="sarc3">{{cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/society/2018/apr/15/nitschke-suicide-machine-amsterdam-euthanasia-funeral-fair |title=Nitschke's 'suicide machine' draws crowds at Amsterdam funeral fair |work=The Guardian |date=2018-04-15 |accessdate=2018-04-15 }}</ref><ref name="capsule">{{cite news |last=Overington |first=Caroline |url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/news/health-science/philip-nitschke-invents-suicide-capsule-with-3d-printer/news-story/318561693f3a6eee1e10069df83a3839?nk=8a500b8067a5ef429d365255e635c3d2-1511568834 |title=Philip Nitschke invents suicide capsule with 3D printer |work=The Australian |publisher=News Corporation |date=2017-11-25 |accessdate=2017-11-25 }}</ref>。
===淹溺===
{{Main article|自溺}}
[[File:Street_Girl's_End.jpg|thumb]]]]
自溺是指当事人故意淹沒在水或其他液體中的行為，目的是令自己的大腦不能獲取足夠的氧氣，窒息致死<ref name="WHO 2005">{{citation |title=A new definition of drowning: towards documentation and prevention of a global public health problem |author1=E.F. van Beeck |author2=C.M. Branche |author3=D. Szpilman |author4=J.H. Modell |author5=J.J.L.M. Bierens |publisher=Bulletin of the World Health Organization |volume=83 |year=2005 |publication-date=2005-11-11 |pages=801–880 |url=http://www.who.int/bulletin/volumes/83/11/vanbeeck1105abstract/en/index.html |accessdate=2012-07-19 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121022231346/http://www.who.int/bulletin/volumes/83/11/vanbeeck1105abstract/en/index.html |archivedate=2012-10-22  }}</ref>，其致死率為65–80%<ref name=Yip2012/>。由於身體傾向於令自身獲得足夠的空氣，所以以此方法自殺的人往往会使用較重的物體來限制自己在液體中的浮力及移動能力<ref>{{cite journal |title=Suicide by drowning: A forensic challenge|author=Todt, M., Ast, F., Wolff-Maras, R., Roesler, B., & Germerott, T.|year=2014|journal= Forensic science international|volume=240|pages=e22-24}}</ref>。[[中樞神經系統|中樞神經系統]]亦会在过程中向呼吸肌發出非隨意信號，使人在水中繼續呼吸<ref name="pmid17274316">{{cite journal |last1=Lindholm |first1=P |last2=Lundgren |first2=C. E. |title=Alveolar gas composition before and after maximal breath-holds in competitive divers |journal=Undersea & Hyperbaric Medicine : Journal of the Undersea and Hyperbaric Medical Society, Inc |volume=33 |issue=6 |pages=463–7 |year=2006 |pmid=17274316 |doi= |url=http://archive.rubicon-foundation.org/5053 |accessdate=2010-12-30 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110324130435/http://archive.rubicon-foundation.org/5053 |archivedate= 2011-03-24 }}</ref>
。当事人在过程中常因氧氣水平太低，不能維持腦細胞正常運作而死。这种自殺方式在美国是相对較为罕見的，只佔所有自殺个案的2%以下<ref name='WISQARS'>{{cite web| url = http://webappa.cdc.gov/sasweb/ncipc/leadcaus10.html| title = WISQARS Leading Causes of Death Reports|accessdate = 2009-07-06}}</ref>；其在日本（6–8%）、印度（9–16%）、[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]（10.9%）以及[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]（11%）則是第三常見的自殺方法<ref name=pmid22690187/>。

===自缢===
{{Main article|自缢}}
[[File:Giotto_-_Scrovegni_-_-47-_-_Desperation.jpg|thumb]]所繪的《絕望》（Desperatio），其對自缢進行了描繪]]
自缢是一種常見於[[中國傳統文化|中國傳統文化]]和高收入國家的自殺方式<ref name="Preventing suicide who"/><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/?id=Zi-xoFAPnPMC|title=Comprehensive Textbook of Suicidology|author1=Ronald W. Maris |author2=Alan L. Berman |author3=Morton M. Silverman |author4=Bruce Michael Bongar |isbn=1-57230-541-X|year=2000| publisher=Guildford Press| page=96}}</ref>，一項於2008年發表的综述以[[世界卫生组织|世界卫生组织]]的死亡率數據為依據，發現自缢是56個國家中最常見的自殺方法<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.2471/BLT.07.043489 |title=Methods of suicide: International suicide patterns derived from the WHO mortality database |year=2008 |last1=Ajdacic-Gross |first1=Vladeta |journal=Bulletin of the World Health Organization |volume=86 |issue=9 |pages=726–32 |pmid=18797649 |last2=Weiss |first2=MG |last3=Ring |first3=M |last4=Hepp |first4=U |last5=Bopp |first5=M |last6=Gutzwiller |first6=F |last7=Rössler |first7=W |pmc=2649482}}</ref>，分别佔男女自殺者的53%和39%<ref>{{cite book|title=International Handbook of Suicide Prevention: Research, Policy and PracticeO'Connor|last1=Rory|first1= C|last2=Platt |first2=Stephen |last3=Gordon|first3=Jacki|display-authors=etal|year=2011|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=3fDGLWQtwFkC&pg=PA34|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|page=34}}</ref>。其致死率為60–85%<ref name=Yip2012/>。

中國傳統文化相信此一自殺方式所帶來的憤怒能使自殺者的[[魂魄|魂魄]]繼續留在世上，折磨其所憎恨的生者<ref>{{citation |first=Jonathan H.X. |last=Lee |author2=Kathleen Nadeau |display-authors=1 |ref={{harvid|Lee & al.|2011}} |date=2011 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-0sEJ_0vV1QC |title=Encyclopedia of Asian American Folklore and Folklife |publisher=ABC-CLIO |p=[https://books.google.com/books?id=-0sEJ_0vV1QC&pg=PA11 11] }}.</ref><ref>{{citation |last=Lee |first=Evelyn |date=1997 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=PLXmf749kmAC |title=Working with Asian Americans: A Guide for Clinicians |publisher=Guilford Press |p=[https://books.google.com/books?id=PLXmf749kmAC&o&pg=PA59 59] }}</ref>。以此方式自殺的人常會在自殺時採取象征性的行動，比如選用合適的服裝<ref name=pel>{{citation |editor-last=Perry |editor-first=Elizabeth J. |editor2=Mark Selden |date=2003 |display-editors=0 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=fUKqoXHqDGEC |title=Chinese Society: Change, Conflict and Resistance |location=Abingdon |publisher=[[Routledge|Routledge]] |p=[https://books.google.com/books?id=fUKqoXHqDGEC&pg=PA297 297] |last=Lee |first=Sing |author2=Arthur Kleinman |display-authors=1 |contribution=Suicide as Resistance in Chinese Society <!--|pp=289–311--> |contribution-url=https://books.google.com/books?id=fUKqoXHqDGEC&pg=PA289 |ref={{harvid|Lee & al.|2003}} }}</ref>。[[閩南|閩南]]與[[臺灣民間信仰|臺灣民間信仰]]為了此事還有[[送肉粽|送肉粽]]的習俗。

嘗試自缢的人常会先準備至少一根绳索，其次把其结索/结環，然后把绳索吊起；完成上述步驟后便把结索/结環部分套至脖子自缢<ref name=oed>Oxford English Dictionary, 2nd ed. Hanging as method of execution is unknown, as method of suicide from 1325.</ref>。以此方式自殺的人最終會是勒死還是脖子斷裂則取決於絞索位置等因素。實際的死亡原因则一般取決於當事人落體的距離。

若落體距離很短，当事人便很可能死於勒死——因[[窒息|腦部缺氧]]而死<ref>{{cite web|title=Hanged by the neck until dead!The processes and physiology of judicial hanging.|accessdate=2018-03-20 |url=http://www.capitalpunishmentuk.org/hanging2.html}}</ref>。当事人可能在过程中经歷缺氧、皮膚刺痛、頭暈、視力變弱、[[驚厥|驚厥]]、[[休克|休克]]和急性[[呼吸性酸中毒|呼吸性酸中毒]]等情況。自缢的绳索亦會壓迫[[總頸動脈|總頸動脈]]和/或頸靜脈，引起腦部局部缺血和缺氧的情況，促使當事人死亡。自缢未遂的人的氣管和喉部常有嚴重創傷，其頸動脈、脊柱、損傷以及腦部亦可能受創<ref>{{cite journal|title=Near-hanging injuries: A 10-year experience|url=http://www.researchgate.net/profile/Peter_Rhee/publication/7274994_Near-hanging_injuries_a_10-year_experience/links/0912f505c0616b80a4000000|last1=Salim |first1=Ali|last2=Martin|first2=Matthew|last3= Sangthong|first3=Burapat |last4= Brown|first4=Carlos|last5=Rhee|first5=Peter|last6=Demetriades|first6=Demetrios|journal=Injury|year=2006|volume=37|issue=5|pages=435-9|deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141102045459/http://www.researchgate.net/profile/Peter_Rhee/publication/7274994_Near-hanging_injuries_a_10-year_experience/links/0912f505c0616b80a4000000|archivedate=2014-11-02|pmid=16503334|doi=10.1016/j.injury.2005.12.013 }}</ref>。

若落體距離很長，当事人的頸椎便很可能發生骨折，特別是第二節至第五節頸椎<ref>{{cite book|title=Kentucky Lynchings|author=TC Cottrell|page=3|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=VzFBDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA3&lpg=PA3&dq=cervical+vertebrae+long+drop&source=bl&ots=ztCP1QV-lb&sig=PQTzyCisduSAfPc0nPP7ryP45EM&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjXnOyb8PnZAhWFkZQKHVY-AaQ4ChDoAQhLMAY#v=onepage&q=cervical%20vertebrae%20long%20drop&f=false|date=2017-11-19 |publisher=lulu.com |isbn=9781387380466}}</ref>；而這可能会致癱，甚至致死。若落體距離長至一定水平，自缢便可能使当事人斷頭<ref>Wyatt, ''et al''., [https://books.google.com/books?id=pggVmvFqf2gC&pg=PA107 p. 107].
* For the length, see Miletich and Lindstrom.</ref>。

===頸部受壓===
此一方法的其中一个例子就是以绳索勒着頸部的方式壓迫頸動脈，防止氧氣運至腦部；其最终會使当事人神智不清，甚至死亡。頸部受壓亦跟柔道的某些技巧和[[窒息式性愛|窒息式性愛]]有關<ref>{{cite book|last=Docker|first=Chris|title=Five Last Acts - The Exit Path|year=2013|isbn=9781482594096|chapter=Compression}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Atilgan M|title=A Case of Suicidal Ligature Strangulation by Using a Tourniquet Method|journal=Am J Forensic Med Pathol|year=2010|volume=31|issue=1|pages=85–86|doi=10.1097/paf.0b013e3181c21c24}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Demirci S|author2=Dogan K |author3=Erkol Z |author4=Gunaydin G |title=Suicide by Ligature Strangulation: Three Case Reports|journal=Am J Forensic Med Pathol|year=2009|volume=30|issue=4|pages=369–372|doi=10.1097/paf.0b013e318187e06b}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Maxeiner H|author2=Bockholdt B |title=Homicidal and suicidal ligature strangulation - a comparison of post-mortem findings|journal=Forensic Sci Int|year=2003|volume=137|issue=1|pages=60–66|doi=10.1016/s0379-0738(03)00279-2}}</ref>。

==失溫==
{{Main article|失溫症}}
失溫式自殺<ref>{{cite journal|title=Suicide by Hypothermia: A Report of Two Cases and 23-Year Retrospective Review|last1=Rebecca|first1=Wilcoxon|last2=Lorren|first2=Jackson|last3=M |first3=Baker Andrew|journal=Acad Forensic Pathol|volume=5|issue=3|year=2015|pages=462-75|url=http://www.afpjournal.com/ArticleDetail/Details/361|doi=10.23907/2015.051}}</ref>的過程緩慢，且有著好幾個階段。當中跟失溫症有關的症狀會逐漸加重。起始時可能造成發抖與意識混濁；中度失溫的發抖症狀消失，但精神錯亂的狀況則會加劇；重度失溫則可能引致心搏停止<ref name=NEJM2012>{{cite journal|last1=Brown|first1=DJ|last2=Brugger|first2=H|last3=Boyd|first3=J|last4=Paal|first4=P|title=Accidental hypothermia.|journal=The New England Journal of Medicine|date=2012-11-15|volume=367|issue=20|pages=1930–8|pmid=23150960|doi=10.1056/NEJMra1114208}}</ref>。其最終會使主要器官停止運作，導致腦死亡<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/hypothermia/symptoms-causes/syc-20352682|title=Hypothermia|publisher=MayoClinic|date=2018-01-06|accessdate=2018-03-19}}</ref>。

==电击==
[[觸電|电击式自殺]]是指當事人故意使用可以致死的[[電流|電流]]來自殺的行為<ref name="Med Sci Law">{{cite journal|title=Suicide by electrocution|last1=Fernando|first1=R|last2= Liyanage|first2=S|journal =Med Sci Law|volume =30|issue =3|pages=219-20| pmid =2398798|doi=10.1177/002580249003000309}}</ref>。其會使當事人發生[[心臟節律不整|心臟節律不整]]的情況<ref name="Koumbourlis">{{cite journal|title=Electrical injuries|last=Koumbourlis|first=A.C.|journal =Critical care medicine|volume =30|issue =11|pages=S424-30|url=https://flexiblelearning.auckland.ac.nz/poplprac763/7/5/files/electrical_injuries.pdf| pmid =12528784}}</ref> ，即不同房室的收缩節律不協調；而這有可能使血流方向改變或停滞。此外若電流過大，當事人的身體亦可能会起火燃燒<ref name="Koumbourlis"/>。为此電椅常用於死刑的執行中，{{link-en|內布拉斯加州最高法院|Nebraska Supreme Court}}的法官{{link-en|威廉·M.康諾利|William M. Connolly}}指出：「觸電致死会引起痛楚及痛苦萬分的煎熬……電椅會使犯人的痛楚毫无理由地增加」<ref>{{cite news| url=https://www.nytimes.com/2008/02/09/us/09penalty.html | work=The New York Times | title=Electrocution Is Banned in Last State to Rely on It| first=Adam | last=Liptak | date=2008-02-09 | accessdate=2010-05-24}}</ref>。

==從高處跳下==
{{Main article|跳落}}
從高處跳下是指從相對海拔較高的地方跳落的行為。該些地方包括高樓層酒店、多層停車場、懸崖和橋樑<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sprc.org/sites/default/files/migrate/library/SuicideHotspotsGuidance%20PDF.pdf|title=Guidance on action to be taken at suicide hotspots |publisher=Suicide Prevention Resource Center|accessdate=2018-03-20}}</ref>。此一方法的致死率為35–60%<ref name=Yip2012/>。以此方式自殺未遂的人可能需要面對的後果包括[[脊髓損傷|脊髓損傷]]、骨折、四肢受創<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Rocos|first1=Brett|last2=Chesser|first2=T.J|title=Injuries in jumpers - are there any patterns?|journal=World J Orthop|volume=7|issue=3|pages=182–7|date=2016-03-18|doi=10.5312/wjo.v7.i3.182|pmid=27004166|pmc=4794537}}</ref>。

從高處跳下在美國是最不常見的自殺方法之一（2005年所有被呈报的自殺個案中只有<2%是以從高處墮下的方式進行的）<ref name="WISQARS"/>。其在[[中國大陸|中國大陸]]、[[香港|香港]]和[[新加坡|新加坡]]等擁有大量容易出入的高层建筑物的地區中，则是最常见的自殺方法之一<ref name="Preventing suicide who"/><ref>{{cite book|title=The International Handbook of Suicide Prevention|editor=Rory C. O'Connor；Jane Pirkis|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=ejMWDQAAQBAJ&pg=PT1029&lpg=PT1029|isbn=978-1-118-90327-8|publisher=Wiley-Blackwell|year=2016|edition=2nd}}</ref>。它佔香港2015年所有被呈报的自殺個案55.9%<ref>{{cite web |title=防止學生自殺委員會最終報告 |work= |publisher=立法會教育事務委員會|year=2016 |url=https://www.legco.gov.hk/yr16-17/chinese/panels/ed/papers/ed20161114cb4-79-5-c.pdf |doi= |accessdate=2018-03-23 }}</ref>，且前幾年亦差不多<ref name="csrp">{{cite web |title= Suicide Rate|url =https://csrp.hku.hk/statistics/|publisher=Centre for Suicide Research and Prevention|accessdate=2018-03-23}}</ref>；並佔新加坡所有被呈报的自殺個案的约80%<ref>{{cite journal|author1=Ying-Yeh Chen|author2=Kevin Chien-Chang Wu|display-authors=etal|title=Suicide in Asia: Opportunities and Challenges|journal=Epidemiologic Reviews|date=2012-01-01|volume=34|issue=1|pages=129–44|doi=10.1093/epirev/mxr025|pmid=22158651|url=https://academic.oup.com/epirev/article/34/1/129/498617}}</ref>。

==吞槍==
[[飲彈|吞槍]]又稱[[飲彈|飲彈]]，是一种自殺方法<ref name="Means Matter"/>。當事人一般會以[[近距離平射射程|直接射中目標而不需瞄準的距離]]瞄準太陽穴、口腔內部、下巴和胸部<ref>{{cite book|title=Firearm and Toolmark Examination and Identification|editor=Max Houck|publisher=Academic Press|date=2015-10-22 |isbn=9780128005668|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=rGidBgAAQBAJ&pg=PA107|page=107}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=Colour Atlas of Wounds and Wounding|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=v90sBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA55|author=G.A. Gresham|page=55|year=1986|publisher=Springer Netherlands|isbn=978-94-010-8328-7}}</ref>。吞槍自殺的百分比在世界各地有着明顯差異，並受到該文化對槍支的接受程度及其可獲得性的影響。吞槍自殺佔全部自殺個案的比例从4.5%（一般高收入國家）到46%（美國）不等<ref name="Preventing suicide who">{{cite book|title=Preventing suicide: a global imperative|publisher=World Health Organization|year=2014|url=http://apps.who.int/iris/bitstream/handle/10665/131056/9789241564779_eng.pdf;jsessionid=4420D08FDF4A91846BDD17D8810F53BE?sequence=1|isbn=978-92-4-156477-9|pages=23-5}}</ref>。此一方法的致死率為80-90%<ref name=Yip2012>{{cite journal|last=Yip|first=PS|author2=Caine, E |author3=Yousuf, S |author4=Chang, SS |author5=Wu, KC |author6= Chen, YY |title=Means restriction for suicide prevention|journal=Lancet|date=2012-06-23|volume=379|issue=9834|pages=2393–9|pmid=22726520|doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(12)60521-2}}</ref>。

吞槍自殺未遂的人亦可能需面對一些後果，包括[[慢性疼痛|慢性疼痛]]、認知和運動功能下降、[[硬膜下血肿|硬膜下血肿]]、頭部異物，氣顱和腦脊液漏出。若當事人吞槍自殺未遂而該槍又是瞄準顳骨的話，那麽便有可能併發顳葉膿腫、腦膜炎、失語症、偏盲和偏癱。受過瞄準顳骨的槍傷而又存活的人當中，多達50％發生臉部神經受損的情況<ref name = "TEMPORAL_SHOT">{{cite web | url=http://www.bcm.edu/oto/grand/8593.html | title=Temporal Bone Gunshot Wounds: Evaluation and Management | publisher=Baylor College of Medicine | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080517030957/http://www.bcm.edu/oto/grand/8593.html |archivedate=2008-05-17 | first=Douglas | last=Backous |date=1993-08-05}}</ref>。

槍支的可獲得性與自殺風險之間存有正相關<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Miller|first1=M|last2=Azrael|first2=D|last3=Barber|first3=C|title=Suicide mortality in the United States: the importance of attending to method in understanding population-level disparities in the burden of suicide.|journal=Annual Review of Public Health|date=April 2012|volume=33|pages=393–408|pmid=22224886|doi=10.1146/annurev-publhealth-031811-124636}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Dowd|first1=MD|last2=Sege|first2=RD|author3=Council on Injury, Violence, and Poison Prevention Executive Committee.|title=Firearm-related injuries affecting the pediatric population.|journal=Pediatrics|year=2012|volume=130|issue=5|pages=e1416-23|pmid=23080412|url=http://pediatrics.aappublications.org/content/130/5/e1416.long|doi=10.1542/peds.2012-2481 |publisher=American Academy of Pediatrics}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Westefeld|first1=John S.|last2=Gann|first2=Lianne C.|last3=Lustgarten|first3=Samuel D.|last4=Yeates|first4=Kevin J.|title=Relationships between firearm availability and suicide: The role of psychology.|journal=Professional Psychology: Research and Practice|year=2016|volume=47|issue=4|pages=271–277|doi=10.1037/pro0000089}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Anglemyer|first1=Andrew|last2=Horvath|first2=Tara|last3=Rutherford|first3=George|title=The Accessibility of Firearms and Risk for Suicide and Homicide Victimization Among Household Members|journal=Annals of Internal Medicine|date=2014-01-21|volume=160|issue=2|pages=101–110|doi=10.7326/M13-1301|pmid=24592495}}</ref>，此一关系在美國当中尤其特顯<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Brent|first1=David A.|title=Firearms and Suicide|journal=Annals of the New York Academy of Sciences|date=2006-01-25|volume=932|issue=1|pages=225–240|doi=10.1111/j.1749-6632.2001.tb05808.x}}</ref>；且幾乎肯定該关系不是混雜因子所致的，因為任何導致這種关系的可能混雜因子都必須符合多重難以置信的標準<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Miller|first1=M.|last2=Swanson|first2=S. A.|last3=Azrael|first3=D.|title=Are We Missing Something Pertinent? A Bias Analysis of Unmeasured Confounding in the Firearm-Suicide Literature|journal=Epidemiologic Reviews|date=2016-01-13|pages=mxv011|doi=10.1093/epirev/mxv011}}</ref>。因自身職業而擁槍的人更有可能以吞槍的方式自殺——91.5％自殺的美國警察選擇吞槍自殺<ref name="ebscohost1">{{cite web |url=http://web.a.ebscohost.com/ehost/detail/detail?vid=0&sid=5c745ffe-e0b2-47e9-b784-e5df4bcde1f1%40sessionmgr4007&bdata=JnNpdGU9ZWhvc3QtbGl2ZQ%3d%3d#AN=2016-29858-001&db=pdh |title=Bookmarkable URL intermediate page |publisher=Web.a.ebscohost.com |date= |accessdate=2018-03-18 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。

====日本====
[[File:2013年3月27日_飛び込み上野駅３番線ホーム_午後5時ごろ_2nd.JPG|thumb]]，2013年摄于[[上野站|上野站]]3站台（[[山手線|山手线]]外环）]]
每年日本皆有許多人透过進入路軌範圍自殺。日本人一般视透过火車來自殺为一項社會問題，在[[東京|東京]]等大城市發生时更是如此，因為这类{{tsl|ja|鉄道人身障害事故||人身事故}}會擾亂火車時刻表；如果在早上高峰期出現這種情況，便會使許多上班上学的乘客遲到。然而儘管日本的人壽保險公司共同拒絕向受益人支付款項（其他形式的自殺一般都会支付之），但透过火車來自殺的情況依舊存在。透过[[新幹線|新幹線]]的高速子彈頭列車來自殺的事件極其罕見，因為公眾通常無法進入軌道，且当地《{{tsl|ja|新幹線特例法}}》第三条定明，对此类侵入轨道的行为，最高可处1年监禁或50000日元[[罰金|罰金]]，这比《{{tsl|ja|鉄道営業法|铁道营业法}}》规管下，侵入通常轨道所面临的[[科料|科料]]要重{{noteTag|除此之外，铁路公司也可能向自杀者提出赔偿请求。如果自杀成功后，家属选择放弃[[继承权|继承权]]且获{{tsl|ja|家庭裁判所}}认可，则通常不需承担此类赔偿责任，但同时也不能继承自杀者的遗产。}}<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2000/06/06/world/kunitachi-city-journal-japanese-trains-try-to-shed-a-gruesome-appeal.html|title=Kunitachi City Journal; Japanese Trains Try to Shed a Gruesome Appeal|last=French|first=Howard W.|date=2000-06-06|newspaper=The New York Times|issn=0362-4331|access-date=2017-01-14}}</ref>。

日本的鐵道公司已採取措施去減少相關事件，其中之一便是在主要鐵路的月台和[[平交道|平交道]]安裝藍色LED燈，用以阻嚇意圖自殺者不要進入路軌範圍，有研究指在安裝後意圖進入路軌自殺者降低了8成<ref>{{Cite book |editor=Updesh Kumar|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=qaI7DwAAQBAJ&pg=PA214|title=Handbook of Suicidal Behaviour|publisher=Springer Singapore|isbn=978-981-10-4815-9|page=214|year=2017}}</ref>。

===车祸===
一些人會透過车祸來自殺。此自殺方式所造成的车祸往往只涉及1人和1輛车，「由於不少人選擇以此方式自殺的缘故，所以普遍觀念認為駕车本身就具有一定風險，此外它亦能為個體提供一個機會，使其能在不需面對自身的自殺意圖的情況下，便可傷害及了結自己<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Selzer | first1 = M. L. | last2 = Payne | first2 = C. E. | year = 1992 | title = Automobile accidents, suicide, and unconscious motivation | doi = 10.1176/ajp.119.3.237 | journal = American Journal of Psychiatry | volume = 119 | issue = |pmid=13910542 | pages = 237–40 [239]}}</ref>。」

透過车祸來自殺的真正比例不明；一些有关自杀的研究表明：「因自殺意圖而起的车祸的比例介乎1.6-5％之間<ref>{{cite journal | year = 1977 | title = Suicide by vehicular crash | doi = 10.1176/ajp.134.2.175 | journal = American Journal of Psychiatry | volume = 134 | issue = 2| pages = 175–178 | last1=Schmidt, Jr. | first1= C. W. | last2= Shaffer | first2= J. W. | last3= Zlotowitz | first3= H. I. | last4= Fisher | first4= R. S. |pmid=835740}}</ref>。」一些自殺个案被錯誤標籤為事故，因為必須證明当事人擁有自殺意圖才可把其定性為自殺；「值得注意的是，即使某些事故有着很強的自殺嫌疑也好，只要沒發現当事人留有遺書，那麽也只能將其定性為『事故』<ref name="accident_or_suicide">{{cite journal | pmid = 7676880 |url=http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_m2248/is_n118_v30/ai_17150135/pg_1 | year = 1995 | last1 = Peck | first1 = DL | last2 = Warner | first2 = K | title = Accident or suicide? Single-vehicle car accidents and the intent hypothesis | volume = 30 | issue = 118 | pages = 463–72 | journal = Adolescence }}</ref>。」

一些研究者認為，偽裝成车祸的自殺比以前所估計的还要普遍。一次大規模的澳洲社區調查得出以下結論：「已有計劃自殺的人群當中，14.8％（19.1％的男性；11.8％的女性）計劃以發生『车祸事故』的方式自殺；在所有已嘗試自殺的人中，8.3％（13.3％的未成年男性）曾經嘗試以撞车的方式自殺」<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Murray | first1 = D. | last2 = de Leo | first2 = D. | title = Suicidal behavior by motor vehicle collision | journal = Traffic Inj Prev | volume = 8 | issue = 3 | pages = 244–7 |date=September 2007 | doi = 10.1080/15389580701329351 | pmid = 17710713 }}</ref>。

===服用農藥===
全球總共有2成自殺个案为透过服用[[农药|農藥]]進行之<ref name="who 实况报道"/>。然而此一方法的使用率在不同地區有著很大的差異，從4％（歐洲）到50+％（太平洋地區）不等<ref name="Gunnell D, Eddleston M, Phillips MR, Konradsen F 2007 357">{{cite journal |vauthors=Gunnell D, Eddleston M, Phillips MR, Konradsen F |title=The global distribution of fatal pesticide self-poisoning: Systematic review |journal=BMC Public Health |volume=7|page=357 |year=2007 |pmid=18154668 |pmc=2262093 |doi=10.1186/1471-2458-7-357 |url=}}</ref>，此一方法的致死率為6–75%<ref name=Yip2012/>。故意服用農藥在中國農村女性中十分普遍，且遭視為國內重大社會問題之一<ref>{{cite news | title=Rural China's suicide problem | url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/asia-pacific/6711415.stm | date=2007-06-04 | accessdate=2010-03-20 | work=BBC News | first=Daniel | last=Griffiths}}</ref>。20世紀50年代的芬蘭人常用高度致命的農藥{{link-en|巴拉刈|Parathion}}來自殺。此一化學品遭到限制後，便有其他自殺方法取替之；这使得研究者得出結論，認為限制某些自殺方法幾乎不会对整體自殺率構成影響<ref>{{Cite journal|doi=10.1192/bjp.166.1.35|author1=A Ohberg |author2=J Lonnqvist |author3=S Sarna |author4=E Vuori |author5=A Penttila |title=Trends and availability of suicide methods in Finland. Proposals for restrictive measures|publisher=The British Journal of Psychiatry|pages=35–43|issue=1|volume=166|journal=The British Journal of Psychiatry|url=http://bjp.rcpsych.org/cgi/content/abstract/166/1/35|year=1995|pmid=7894873|postscript=<!--None-->}}</ref>。但自從[[斯里蘭卡|斯里蘭卡]]禁止第1類有害農藥和[[安殺番|安殺番]]後，農藥自殺率和總自殺率皆有所下降<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1126/science.341.6147.738| title = In Rural Asia, Locking Up Poisons to Prevent Suicides| journal = Science| volume = 341| issue = 6147| pages = 738–9| year = 2013| last1 = Hvistendahl | first1 = M.| pmid=23950528}}</ref>。韓國在2012年全面禁用巴拉刈之後，農藥自殺率減少了40%<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.twna.org.tw/TWNA_BACKEND/upload/web/home_00/NewsNew/6454/支持農委會禁用巴拉刈之聯合聲明-1060606.pdf|title=關鍵一刻，扭轉一生：支持農委會禁用巴拉刈|publisher=全國自殺防治中心|year=2017|accessdate=2018-03-23}}</ref>。

===服用或注射過量藥物===
服用或注射[[藥物過量|過量藥物]]是一種自殺的方法<ref name="Means Matter">{{cite web|title=Lethality of Suicide Methods|url=https://www.hsph.harvard.edu/means-matter/means-matter/case-fatality/|accessdate=2018-03-20|publisher=Harvard T.H. 在攝入過量藥物來自殺的個案中，以服用[[镇痛药|镇痛药]]最為常見，因為它是一種非處方藥，意圖自殺者可以相对較容易地獲得之<ref>{{cite journal |last =Brock |first =Anita |author2=Sini Dominy |author3=Clare Griffiths  |date=2003-11-06 |title = Trends in suicide by method in England and Wales, 1979 to 2001 |journal = Health Statistics Quarterly |volume = 20 |pages = 7–18 |issn = 1465-1645 |url = http://www.statistics.gov.uk/CCI/article.asp?ID=1538&Pos=4&ColRank=1&Rank=176 |accessdate = 2007-06-25 }}</ref>。

==安乐死==
{{Main article|安乐死}}
[[安乐死|安乐死]]是指為了減輕痛苦和疾痛而故意結束生命的行為<ref>{{cite web|quotation=Philosopher [[Helga_Kuhse|Helga Kuhse]]: "'Euthanasia' is a compound of two Greek words – eu and thanatos meaning, literally, 'a good death'. A similar definition is offered by Blackburn (1994) with "the action of causing the quick and painless death of a person, or not acting to prevent it when prevention was within the agent's powers."</ref>。

攝入過量藥物往往是實踐者想要安乐死的首選表達方法。死亡權爭取組織「[[解脱国际|解脫國際]]」（Exit International）表示，與使用膠袋、一氧化碳生成器相比，89％的人較想以攝入藥丸的方式去實踐安乐死<ref>{{cite book|author=Philip Nitschke|title=The Peaceful Pill Handbook|publisher=Exit International US |year=2007|isbn=0-9788788-2-5|page=33}}</ref>。

雖然像速可眠（Seconal）和耐波他（Nembutal）般的[[巴比妥类药物|巴比妥類藥物]]長久以來一直是服藥自殺者的一個選擇，但意圖自殺者越來越難取得上述类别的藥物，於是荷蘭死亡權爭取組織WOZZ便提出了幾種用於安樂死的有效替代药物<ref>{{cite book|title=Guide to a Humane Self-Chosen Death|author= Pieter Admiraal|display-authors=etal|publisher=WOZZ Foundation|location=The Netherlands. |isbn=90-78581-01-8}}</ref>。

==儀式性自殺==
===切腹===
{{main article|切腹}}
[[切腹|切腹]]為日本的一種自殺儀式<ref name="Britannica Seppuku"/>，於5世紀-15世紀時最為常見，儘管现代仍存有零星的切腹個案<ref name="Britannica Seppuku"/>——[[三岛由纪夫|三岛由纪夫]]便是一個例子，他在發表關於恢復天皇舊有權力的[[政變|政變]]演說失敗後，切腹自殺<ref>{{cite book|last= Nathan|first=John|title=Mishima: A biography|publisher=Little Brown and Company |location= Boston/Toronto|year=1974}}</ref>。日本武士會為了謝罪、表達憤怒等原因而切腹<ref name="jpninfo"/>。

切腹常會為當事人帶來極大痛苦<ref name="Britannica Seppuku">{{cite web|url=https://www.britannica.com/topic/seppuku|title=Seppuku|publisher=Encyclopaedia Britannica|accessdate=2018-03-21}}</ref>，切腹者会在事前把劍放在面前或布上，並可能會透過寫一篇辞世诗來準備死亡<ref>{{Google books|sBWasQSsb-UC|Samurai Fighting Arts: The Spirit and the Practice|page=48|keywords=shini-shōzoku|text=|plainurl=}}</ref>。然後揭開身穿的和服<ref>{{cite book|last=Seward|first=Jack|year=2012 |publisher= Charles E. Tuttle Co|isbn=978-0804802314|title=Hara-kiri: Japanese Ritual Suicide|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=ThLRAgAAQBAJ&pg=PT40&lpg=PT40}}</ref>，拿起刀，捅進自己腹部，從左切至右，然後把刀向上移；其后演化至[[介錯人|介錯人]]的參與，其會為切腹自殺者斬首，以讓切腹者更快死亡<ref name="jpninfo">{{cite web|title=Seppuku (Harakiri): The Honorable Death of a Samurai|url=http://jpninfo.com/41352|publisher=jpninfo|date=2016-02-03|accessdate=2018-03-21}}</ref>。

===自我獻祭===
[[阿茲特克|阿茲特克]]和瑪雅文化的藝術品描繪了祭司和國王以自我[[斬首|斬首]]的方式來進行[[人祭|獻祭]]的場面<ref>{{cite book|author=Cecelia Klein|chapter=The Ideology of Autosacrifice at the Templo Mayor|title=The Aztec Templo Mayor|pages=293-370|location=Washington, D.C|publisher=Dumbarton Oaks|year=1987|isbn=0-88402-149-1}}</ref><ref name="KremerFlores">{{cite journal|title=The Ritual Suicide of Maya Rulers|author1=Jürgen Kremer |author2=Fausto Uc Flores |journal=Eighth Palenque Round Table|volume=10|year=1993|pages=79–91|publisher=Pre-Columbian Art Research Institute}}</ref>。該些場面一般刻畫成以黑曜石刀或斧頭对着頸側<ref name="KremerFlores"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.famsi.org/research/kerr/articles/xbalanque/index.html|title=The Transformation of Xbalanqué or The Many Faces of God A1|author=Justin Kerr|publisher=Foundation for the Advancement of Mesoamerican Studies}}</ref>。

在印度教中，一名男性奉獻者可能通過自我斬首來把自己獻祭給自身崇拜的[[难近母|难近母]]<ref>{{cite book|author=Miranda Eberle Shaw|title=Buddhist goddesses of India|publisher=Princeton University Press|year=2006|page=416|isbn=0-691-12758-1}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|author=George Cœdès|title=The Indianized states of Southeast Asia|publisher=University of Hawaii Press|year=1968|page=101|isbn=0-8248-0368-X}}</ref>。

==绝食==
{{Main article|餓死|絕食}}
[[絕食|絕食]]於最終可能會致死<ref>{{cite journal|title=Deaths due to hunger strike: post-mortem findings|last1=Altun|first1=Gurcan|last2=Akansu|first2=Bulent|display-authors=etal|journal=Forensic Sci Int|year=2004|volume=146|issue=1|pages=35-8|pmid=15485719|doi=10.1016/j.forsciint.2004.03.022}}</ref>。[[印度教|印度教]]徒和[[耆那教|耆那教]]修士會以絕食來懺悔，並可能視其為令自己更快死亡的一種方法<ref>{{cite book|last=Docker|first= C|chapter=The Art and Science of Fasting|title=Beyond Final Exit|year=1995}}</ref> 。[[卡特里派|卡特里派或阿爾比派]]的信徒也會在接受名為康索雷门图（Consolamentum）的圣礼後禁食，以便在道德無缺陷的情況下死去<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=xAmMNnJlfnoC&pg=PA90&dq=cathars+starvation&hl=en&sa=X&ei=TPQGUeb2NY6R0QWVkICIDA&ved=0CDkQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=starvation&f=false |title=The New Encyclopedia of the Occult - John Michael Greer - Google Books |publisher=Books.google.co.uk |accessdate=2014-02-04}}</ref>。此一自殺方法亦跟政治抗爭有關，相關例子包括[[1981年爱尔兰绝食抗议|1981年爱尔兰绝食抗议]]，其要求改善以戰俘營的方式運作的監獄環境。此一運動造成了10人死亡<ref>{{cite journal |last=O'Keeffe |first=Terence |year=1984 |title=Suicide and Self-Starvation |journal=Philosophy |volume=59 |issue=229 |pages=349–363 |url= |jstor=3750951 |doi=10.1017/S0031819100069941}}</ref>。

儘管[[神经性厌食症|神经性厌食症]]一直遭視為潛意識的自殺<ref>{{cite book|last=Farnsworth|first=D|title=Bombastic Gadflies|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=A-2-5GMBhXsC&pg=PR89&dq=anorexia+subconscious+suicide&hl=en&sa=X&ei=y2h9UeGLConTPKuWgfAI&ved=0CFUQ6AEwBw#v=onepage&q=anorexia%20subconscious%20suicide&f=false}}</ref>，但死亡證不會把其所導致的死亡歸類為自殺。根據英國的《1983年精神健康法》，在當地拒絕按正常需要攝食及飲用飲料者可遭到拘留、治療，甚至強制餵食，不过有些跡象顯示精神衛生專業人員對此類方法的抵制依舊存在<ref>{{cite news|publisher= BBC News|url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/health-22984708|accessdate = 2013-06-24|title = Beth's Story: What is it like to be sectioned?|location= London|first= Lucy|last= Wallis|date=2002-04-19}}</ref>。
==脱水==
脱水自殺的過程可持續數天至數週不等，因此它不像其他方法般能於一時衝動的情況下完成。死於脫水的人常在死亡之前神志不清，並可能會發生[[譫妄|譫妄]]和血钠水平异常增高的情況<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Science, Hospice and Terminal Dehydration|author=Baumrucker, Steven|publisher=American Journal of Hospice and Palliative Medicine|volume=16|date=May–June 1999|issue=3|postscript=<!--None-->}}</ref>。短時間內停止補充水分不會使人發生真正的乾渴，儘管人們常把口乾的感覺稱為「乾渴」。現有廣泛的證據支持「口乾」不等於「乾渴」，比如靜脈輸液不會使因口乾而起的不適感缓解，而潤濕舌頭和嘴唇則能緩解之。伴有「[[水肿|水腫]]」此一症狀的人常花更多时間才能死於脱水，因為他們體內積聚了过多的體液<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.preciouslegacy.com/chap13.html|title=Treatment of Pain and Suffering in the Terminally Ill|author=Lieberson, Alan D.|postscript=<!--None-->}}</ref>。

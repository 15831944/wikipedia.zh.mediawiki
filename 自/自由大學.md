'''自由大學'''（'''Liberty University'''）是位於[[美國|美國]][[弗吉尼亞州|弗吉尼亞州]][[林奇堡_(弗吉尼亞州)|林奇堡]]的一所基督教私立大學，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.liberty.edu/news/index.cfm?PID=18495&MID=56836 |title=Liberty Online now 80,000 strong | Liberty University |publisher=Liberty.edu |date= |accessdate=2015-04-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www2.newsadvance.com/news/2012/may/16/five-years-after-death-jerry-falwell-sr-growth-boo-ar-1917125/ |title=Five years after death of Jerry Falwell Sr., growth booming at Liberty | The News & Advance |publisher=.newsadvance.com |date= |accessdate=2012-07-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120519093716/http://www2.newsadvance.com/news/2012/may/16/five-years-after-death-jerry-falwell-sr-growth-boo-ar-1917125/ |archivedate=2012-05-19 }}</ref> 如果算上其在線課程學生數，它是世界最大的[[福音派|福音派]]大學、美國最大的非營利性私立大學和第七大四年制大學，此外也是弗吉尼亞州最大的大學。<ref>[http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2010/06/02/AR2010060203280.html Liberty University reviewing claims that seminary president lied about his past] [[Washington_Post|Washington Post]], Michelle Boorstein. June 2, 2010</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=McDonald |first=Michael |url=http://www.bloomberg.com/news/2013-02-27/god-a-click-away-as-web-courses-fuel-falwell-s-college.html |title=God a Click Away as Web Courses Fuel Falwell’s College |publisher=Bloomberg |date=2013-02-27 |accessdate=2014-02-08}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Wukman |first=Alex |url=http://www.onlinecolleges.net/2012/06/13/liberty-university-online-enrollment-reaches-80000/ |title=Liberty University Online Enrollment Reaches 80,000 |publisher=Online Colleges |date=2012-06-13 |accessdate=2012-07-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120906051640/http://www.onlinecolleges.net/2012/06/13/liberty-university-online-enrollment-reaches-80000/ |archivedate=2012-09-06 }}</ref>

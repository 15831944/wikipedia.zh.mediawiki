'''轉車里集中營'''是在[[朝鲜民主主义人民共和国|-{zh-hans:朝鲜;zh-hant:北韓}-]]的[[朝鮮民主主義人民共和國集中營|再教育營]]，其正式名稱是'''轉車里第十二號敎化所'''。集中營位於[[咸鏡北道|咸鏡北道]][[會寧市|會寧市]][[茂山里|茂山里]]<ref>[http://nk.joins.com/map/view.asp?idx=i187.htm 회령시(會寧市) HWERYONGSI]</ref>，會寧市和清津市的公路、鐵路中樞<ref name = "hrnk">{{cite web | title= The Hidden Gulag – Satellite imagery (p. 227)| work= The Committee for Human Rights in North Korea | url= http://www.hrnk.org/uploads/pdfs/HRNK_HiddenGulag2_Web_5-18.pdf| accessdate= September 14, 2012}}</ref>，距[[豊山里|豐山里]]2.5公里處。<ref>[http://www.freekorea.us/2009/09/06/revealed-the-first-published-images-of-camp-12-chon­go-ri-north-korea/ One Free Korea: Camp 12 – Chon­gori camp with satellite photographs]</ref>

轉車里集中營關押有大量逃亡中國後被逮捕遣返的[[脫北者|脫北者]]<ref name="english.chosun.com">[http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2009/09/01/2009090100232.html?FORM=ZZNR9 Chosun Ilbo: “N. Korea in brutal crackdown on defectors”, Chosun Ilbo, September 1, 2009]</ref>。理論上經過再教育的囚犯有機會被釋放，但漫長的刑期和高強度勞動致使許多人難以生存。據曾在轉車里服刑的脫北者估計，在他八個月的服刑中約有800人死於苦役和飢餓。<ref>{{cite web | title= The Hidden Gulag – Exposing Crimes against Humanity in North Korea’s Vast Prison System (p. 88)| work= The Committee for Human Rights in North Korea | url= http://www.hrnk.org/uploads/pdfs/HRNK_HiddenGulag2_Web_5-18.pdf| accessdate= September 14, 2012}}</ref>

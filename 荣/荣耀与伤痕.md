《'''荣耀与伤痕'''》（{{lang-en|''Glory and Gore''}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.5music.com.tw/CDList-C.asp?cdno=434485679253|title=蘿兒:天生英雌|work=五大唱片|accessdate=2017-08-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.amazon.cn/%E6%B4%9B%E5%BE%B7-%E7%BA%AF%E7%B2%B9%E5%A5%B3%E8%8B%B1%E9%9B%84/dp/B00HUO8RHM|title=洛德:纯粹女英雄|work=Amazon.cn|accessdate=2017-08-10}}</ref>是[[新西兰|新西兰]]歌手[[洛德_(歌手)|洛德]]演唱的一首歌曲，出自其首张专辑《{{le|天生英雌|Pure Heroine}}》。这首歌曲于2014年3月11日由{{le|岩浆唱片|Lava Records}}和{{le|共和唱片|Republic Records}}正式发行为专辑的第5支单曲。歌曲由洛德和其制作人{{le|乔尔·利特|Joel Little}}共同创作。《荣耀与伤痕》是一首受到{{le|寒潮 (音乐)|chillwave|寒潮}}和[[嘻哈音乐|嘻哈音乐]]所影响的[[流行電音|电子流行]]歌曲。歌词围绕着现代社会着魔于暴力和名人文化。歌曲获得了主流乐评褒贬不一的评价，并分别在美国[[告示牌百强单曲榜|《告示牌》百强单曲榜]]和[[告示牌榜單|摇滚歌曲榜]]获得第68位和第9位的成绩。《荣耀与伤痕》曾作为[[历史频道|历史频道]]电视剧《[[维京传奇|维京传奇]]》第二季广告的插曲。

==乐评==
《The 405》的拉里·达伊（{{lang|en|Larry Day}}）在评价《纯粹女英雄》这张专辑时称《荣耀与伤痕》是「值得作为单曲的歌曲」<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.thefourohfive.com/review/article/lorde-pure-heroine |title=Lorde – Pure Heroine |first=Larry |last=Day |date=2013-10-25 |accessdate=2014-05-11 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140512222339/http://www.thefourohfive.com/review/article/lorde-pure-heroine |archivedate=2014-05-12 }}</ref>。《告示牌》的杰森·利普舒茨（{{lang|en|Jason Lipshutz}}）认为洛德在这首歌副歌记忆点的演唱方式「极具有感染力」<ref name="Lipshutz"/>。《{{le|前音后果|Consequence of Sound}}》的乔恩·哈杜赛克（{{lang|en|Jon Hadusek}}）写道《荣耀与伤痕》实在与《纯粹女英雄》这张专辑里大部分的简约音乐制作大相径庭<ref>{{cite web|last=Hadusek|first=Jon|title=Album Review: Lorde – Pure Heroine|url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2013/09/album-review-lorde-pure-heroine/|publisher=Consequence of Sound|accessdate=2013-09-30|date=2013-09-30|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131003142142/http://consequenceofsound.net/2013/09/album-review-lorde-pure-heroine/|archivedate=2013-10-03}}</ref>。《[[Pitchfork_Media|Pitchfork]]》的林赛·佐拉兹（{{lang|en|Lindsay Zoladz}}）批评这首歌曲将太多内容塞进每一句歌词中<ref>{{cite web |url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/18614-lorde-pure-heroine/ |title=Lorde: Pure Heroine |first=Lindsay |last=Zoladz |publisher=[[Pitchfork_Media|Pitchfork]] |date=2013-10-03 |accessdate=2014-02-28 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140307115506/http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/18614-lorde-pure-heroine/ |archivedate=2014-03-07 }}</ref>。musicOMH的约翰·墨菲（{{lang|en|John Murphy}}）在评价《纯粹女英雄》这张专辑的后半部分时，称「在《荣耀与伤痕》和《清醒》开始播放之后，那些模版（化的歌曲）開始聽起來讓人覺得有些厭倦。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.musicomh.com/reviews/albums/lorde-pure-heroine |title=Lorde – Pure Heroine |first=John |last=Murphy |publisher=musicOMH |date=2013-10-24 |accessdate=2014-05-11 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140408141649/http://www.musicomh.com/reviews/albums/lorde-pure-heroine |archivedate=2014-04-08 }}</ref>」

==商业成绩==
《纯粹女英雄》发行之后，《荣耀与伤痕》于2013年10月7日在[[新西兰官方音乐榜|紐西蘭艺人单曲榜]]最高到达第17位<ref name="Top 20 New Zealand Singles Chart">{{cite web |url=http://nztop40.co.nz/chart/nzsingles?chart=2281 |title=Top 20 New Zealand Singles Chart |date=2013-10-07 |publisher=[[Recorded_Music_NZ|Recorded Music NZ]] |accessdate=2014-02-28 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20140112054623/http://nztop40.co.nz/chart/nzsingles?chart=2281 |archivedate=2014-01-12 }}</ref>。在作为单曲发行之前，歌曲于2014年3月8日以32,000份銷量（几乎是前一星期的双倍）排在[[告示牌百强单曲榜|《告示牌》百强单曲榜]]第88位<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.billboard.com/charts/hot-100?page=8 |title=The Hot 100 |work=Billboard |date=2014-03-08 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140228021556/http://www.billboard.com/charts/hot-100?page=8 |archivedate=2014-02-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Trust|first=Gary|location=New York|date=2014-02-28|url=http://www.billboard.com/biz/articles/news/5922858/chart-moves-keith-urban-drives-cop-car-up-hot-country-songs-cage-the|title=Chart Moves: Keith Urban Drives 'Cop Car' Up Hot Country Songs; Cage The Elephant Scores First Triple A No. 1; Christopher Cross Ends Top 10 Drought|work=Billboard|accessdate=2014-02-28|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20150804030646/http://www.billboard.com/biz/articles/news/5922858/chart-moves-keith-urban-drives-cop-car-up-hot-country-songs-cage-the|archivedate=2015-08-04}}</ref>。次周，《荣耀与伤痕》以47,000份銷量（增加46%）上升至第68位，并成为该周百强单曲榜的「数位贏家」<ref name="Trust">{{cite news |url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/5923189/pharrell-williams-happy-holds-at-no-1-on-hot-100-chart |title=Pharrell's 'Happy' Holds at No. 1 On Hot 100 |first=Gary |last=Trust |location=New York |work=Billboard |date=2014-03-05 |accessdate=2014-03-05 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20150804053454/http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/5923189/pharrell-williams-happy-holds-at-no-1-on-hot-100-chart |archivedate=2015-08-04 }}</ref>。歌曲在美国[[公告牌数字歌曲榜|数字歌曲榜]]最高达到第30位<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/1553855/lorde/chart?f=395 |title=Lorde – Chart history: Digital Songs |work=Billboard |accessdate=2014-05-11 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140517083050/http://www.billboard.com/artist/1553855/lorde/chart?f=395 |archivedate=2014-05-17 }}</ref>，在美国[[告示牌榜單|另类歌曲榜]]最高达到第17位<ref name="USalt">{{cite web |url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/1553855/lorde/chart?f=377 |title=Lorde – Chart history: Alternative Songs |work=Billboard |accessdate=2014-04-29 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140330175544/http://www.billboard.com/artist/1553855/Lorde/chart?f=377 |archivedate=2014-03-30 }}</ref>，并在美国主流[[告示牌榜單|摇滚歌曲榜]]最高达到第9位<ref name="rock">{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/1553855/lorde/chart?f=902|title=Lorde – Chart history: Hot Rock Songs|work=Billboard|accessdate=2014-04-29|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6rI9Advxm?url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/1553855/lorde/chart?f=902|archivedate=2017-06-17}}</ref>。截至2014年4月，《荣耀与伤痕》在美国已獲得307,000數位銷量<ref name="chartbeat"/>。

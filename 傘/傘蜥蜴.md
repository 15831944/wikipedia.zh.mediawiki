'''傘蜥蜴'''（學名：''Chlamydosaurus kingii''），又名'''斗篷蜥'''，是[[飛蜥科|飛蜥科]]'''斗篷蜥属'''的[[單種屬|唯一一種生物]]，分布於[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]西、北部和[[新幾內亞|新幾內亞]]南部。受威脅時頸部的傘狀薄膜會張開、口部張大以嚇走敵人。平常以四足行走，逃跑時用後足生活於乾燥的草原灌木地帶，以[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]和其他小型蜥蜴為食。天敵為[[蛇|蛇]]。

==命名==
傘蜥蜴由[[約翰·愛德華·格雷|約翰·愛德華·格雷]]基於英國皇家海軍美人魚號（HMS Mermaid）的船長[[菲利普·帕克·金|菲利普·帕克·金]]帶回英國的標本正式描述于1825年<ref>{{cite journal|last=Gray|date=1825|title= A synopsis of the genera of reptiles and Amphibia, with a description of some new species|journal=Annals of Philosophy 2|volume=10|pages=193–217 [197]|url=https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/page/2531391}}</ref><ref name="AFD">{{cite web|url=http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/abrs/online-resources/fauna/afd/taxa/Chlamydosaurus_kingii|title=Species ''Chlamydosaurus kingii'' J.E. Gray, 1825|last=Australian Biological Resources Study|date=28 May 2014|work=Australian Faunal Directory|publisher=Department of the Environment, Water, Heritage and the Arts, Australian Government|accessdate=11 May 2015|location=Canberra, Australian Capital Territory|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150518091616/http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/abrs/online-resources/fauna/afd/taxa/Chlamydosaurus_kingii|archive-date=2015-05-18|dead-url=yes|df=}}</ref><ref name=EDR>Beolens, Bo; Watkins, Michael; Grayson, Michael (2011). ''The Eponym Dictionary of Reptiles''. Baltimore: Johns Hopkins University Press. xiii + 296 pp. {{ISBN|978-1-4214-0135-5}}. ("''Chlamydosaurus kingi'' [sic]", p. 141).</ref>，而這個標本本身則是隨船植物學家[[艾倫·坎寧安|艾倫·坎寧安]]在澳大利亞西北部的海灣搜集到的<ref name="Kent1897">{{cite book|last=Kent|first=William Saville|authorlink=William Saville Kent|title=The naturalist in Australia|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=1xMuAQAAIAAJ&pg=PA70|year=1897|publisher=Chapman & Hall, limited|pages=70–73}}</ref>。

屬名來自古希古希臘語“chlamydo”（有斗篷的）和“sauros”（蜥蜴），種加詞就是菲利普·帕克·金的姓<ref name=EDR/>。

==形態==

它是一種較大的飛蜥科生物，體長可達{{convert|85|cm|ft|}}<ref name=length>{{cite web|url=http://animaldiversity.ummz.umich.edu/accounts/Chlamydosaurus_kingii/ |title=''Chlamydosaurus kingii'' |accessdate=2012-09-21 |last=Savage |first=Melissa |work=Animal Diversity Web }}</ref>。平常以四足行走，但也可以用後足奔跑<ref name="LydekkerKirby1897">{{cite book|last1=Lydekker|first1=Richard|last2=Kirby|first2=William Forsell|last3=Woodward|first3=Bernard Barham|title=Natural History|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eO8VdqvUbasC&pg=PA400|year=1897|publisher=D. Appleton and company|page=400}}</ref>。

'''膝龍屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Genusaurus''）意為「膝蓋[[蜥蜴|蜥蜴]]」，是[[下白堊紀|下白堊紀]]的一[[屬|屬]][[恐龍|恐龍]]。膝龍是屬於[[角鼻龍下目|角鼻龍下目]]，並可能是[[食肉牛龍|食肉牛龍]]的近親。膝龍的[[化石|化石]]被發現於[[法國|法國]]。膝龍的身長估計為9.8公尺<ref name="Holtz">{{cite book| last = Holtz| first = Thomas R. Jr.| authorlink = | title = Dinosaurs: The Most Complete, Up-to-Date Encyclopedia for Dinosaur Lovers of All Ages| publisher = Random House| date = 2008| location = | url = http://www.geol.umd.edu/~tholtz/dinoappendix/DinoappendixSummer2008.pdf| doi = | id = | isbn = 0375824197| page = 52, updated appendix}}</ref>，重量約1.5公噸<ref name="Montague">{{cite journal| last = Montague| first = R.| title = Estimates of body size and geological time of origin for 612 dinosaur genera (Saurischia, Ornithischia)| journal = Florida Scientist| volume = 69| issue = 4| pages = 243–257| publisher = | location = | date = 2006| url = http://apt.allenpress.com/perlserv/?request=get-abstract&doi=10.1043%2F0098-4590%282006%29069%5B0243%3AEOBSAG%5D2.0.CO%3B2| doi = | id = | accessdate = 2008-06-09| year = 2006}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。膝龍的生存年代約是[[阿普第階|阿普第階]]，約1億2000萬到1億1000萬年前<ref name="Holtz"/>。

[[模式種|模式種]]是''G. sisteronis''，是在1995年根據部份骨骼而描述、命名的。

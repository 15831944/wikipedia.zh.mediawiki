'''细胞保护作用'''是指化学物质对[[细胞|细胞]]提供保护，使其免受危险物质危害的整个过程。<ref name="urlwww.nlm.nih.gov">{{cite web | url = http://www.nlm.nih.gov/cgi/mesh/2009/MB_cgi?mode=&term=Cytoprotection | title = MeSH Heading: Cytoprotection | author = | authorlink = | coauthors = | date = | format = | work = Medical Subject Headings | publisher = United States National Library of Medicine | pages = | language = | archiveurl = | archivedate = | quote = | accessdate = }}</ref>

例如，[[胃|胃]]的“细胞保护试剂”就是指任何一种通过增加粘膜保护而不减少[[胃酸|胃酸]]的药物<ref name="pmid20227024">{{cite journal | author = Tulassay Z, Herszényi L | title = Gastric mucosal defense and cytoprotection | journal = Best Pract Res Clin Gastroenterol | volume = 24 | issue = 2 | pages = 99–108 | pmid = 20227024 | doi = 10.1016/j.bpg.2010.02.006 | url = | issn = |date=April 2010}}</ref>。胃的保护试剂包括能防止粘膜受损的[[前列腺素|前列腺素]]。非甾体类消炎药就会抑制前列腺素的合成，致使胃粘膜更容易受到伤害<ref name="pmid1516961">{{cite journal | author = Wallace JL | title = Prostaglandins, NSAIDs, and cytoprotection | journal = Gastroenterol. Clin. North Am. | volume = 21 | issue = 3 | pages = 631–41 | pmid = 1516961 | doi = | url = | issn = |date=September 1992}}</ref>。

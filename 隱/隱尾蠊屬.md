'''隱尾蠊屬'''（''Cryptocercus''）是[[網翅總目|網翅總目]]下的一屬及隱尾蠊科下的唯一成員<ref name=":0">Beccaloni, G. & P. Eggleton. (2013). [http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2013/f/zt03703p048.pdf Order Blattodea.] ''In'': Zhang, Z.-Q.(Ed.) Animal Biodiversity: An Outline of Higher-level Classification and Survey of Taxonomic Richness (Addenda 2013). ''Zootaxa'' 3703(1) 46-48.</ref>。此屬為[[亞社會性|亞社會性]]，若蟲需要親代大量的照顧。隱尾蠊屬的腸胃中生有和食木性白蟻相同的木質消化共生菌，此點被視為兩者有著極為接近關係的證據，也就是說白蟻基本上是從社會性蟑螂演化而來<ref name=":1">Daegan Inward, George Beccaloni, and Paul Eggleton (2007) [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2464702/ Death of an order: a comprehensive molecular phylogenetic study confirms that termites are eusocial cockroaches]</ref>。

隱尾蠊屬能在[[北美洲|北美洲]]和[[亚洲|亞洲]]被發現，目前共有12個已知物種<ref>{{Cite journal|title=The genus Cryptocercus in East Asia: distribution and new species (Insecta, Dictyoptera, Blattaria, Polyphagidae).|author=Grandcolas, P., Legendre, F., Park, Y. C., Belles, X., Murienne, J., & Pellens, R.|url=http://sciencepress.mnhn.fr/sites/default/files/articles/pdf/z2005n4a4.pdf|journal=Zoostema-Paris|issue=27|doi=|others=|year=2005|volume=4|page=725|pmid=}}</ref>。

隱尾蠊屬擁有許多和[[白蟻|白蟻]]相同的特徵。[[系统发生学|種系發生學]]研究顯示本屬與白蟻的關係比起其他的蜚蠊還要更加密切<ref name=":2">Djernæs, M., et al. 2012. [http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1365-3113.2011.00598.x/abstract Phylogeny of cockroaches (Insecta, Dictyoptera, Blattodea), with placement of aberrant taxa and exploration of out-group sampling.] ''Systematic Entomology'' 37(1): 65–83. doi: 10.1111/j.1365-3113.2011.00598.x.</ref>，隱尾蠊屬為白蟻領科的姊妹群。

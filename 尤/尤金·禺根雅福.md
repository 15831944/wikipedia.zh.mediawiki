'''尤金·禺根雅福'''（{{lang-nl|Eugene Rhuggenaath}}<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://curacaochronicle.com/politics/high-expectations-for-new-minister-of-economic-development/|title=High Expectations For New Minister Of Economic Development|language=en |publisher=Curaçao Chronicle|date= |accessdate=13 May 2017}}</ref>；{{bd|1970年|2月4日||}}），是[[庫拉索|庫拉索]]政治人物、{{le|安的列斯重組黨|Party for the Restructured Antilles}}黨魁。他曾在{{le|庫拉索內閣|List of cabinets of Curaçao|科伊曼內閣}}出任經濟發展部部長，之後所屬黨在{{le|2017年庫拉索大選|Curaçao general election, 2017}}取得最高票而獲{{le|庫拉索總督|Governor of Curaçao|總督}}{{le|露西爾·喬治-沃特|Lucille George-Wout}}授權和{{le|MAN黨|Partido MAN}}及{{link-nl|全國革新黨|Partido Inovashon Nashonal}}合組政府<ref>{{cite web|url=https://thedailyherald.sx/islands/65864-rhuggenaath-is-now-formateur|title=Rhuggenaath is now ‘formateur’|date=12 May 2017|work=The Daily Herald}}</ref>。

他在2017年5月29日正式就任[[库拉索总理|库拉索总理]]<ref>{{cite web|url= http://curacaochronicle.com/main/new-curacao-government-under-the-leadership-of-rhuggenaath-sworn-in/|title=New Curaçao Government Under The Leadership Of Rhuggenaath Sworn In|date=29 May 2017|work=The Curaçao Chronicle}}</ref>。

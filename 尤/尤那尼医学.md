'''尤那尼医学'''（{{lang-ur|طب یونانی}}，拉丁转写：{{lang|la|''tibb yūnānī''}}<ref>the transcription as ''Unani'' is found in 19th-century English language sources: "the ''Ayurvedic'' and ''Unani'' systems of medicine"
"Madhya Pradesh District Gazetteers: Hoshangabad", ''Gazetteer of India'' 17 (1827), [https://books.google.ch/books?id=hqE8AAAAIAAJ&pg=PA587  p. 587].</ref>）是一种在[[莫卧儿帝国|莫卧儿帝国]]、[[穆斯林征服印度次大陆|南亚穆斯林地区]]以及[[中亚|中亚]]广泛使用的波斯-阿拉伯[[传统医学|传统医学]]。尤那尼一词意为“希腊的”<ref name="Dalrymple1994">{{cite book|author=William Dalrymple|title=City of Djinns: A Year in Delhi|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=PzQWAQAAMAAJ&dq=greek&focus=searchwithinvolume&q=greek|year=1994|publisher=Flamingo|isbn=978-0-00-637595-1|page=269}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Unani Tibb|url=http://broughttolife.sciencemuseum.org.uk/broughttolife/techniques/unanitibb|publisher=Science Museum, London|accessdate=7 October 2017}}</ref>，因为{{le|中世纪伊斯兰世界医学|Medicine in the medieval Islamic world|波斯-阿拉伯医学系统}}建立在古希腊医生[[希波克拉底|希波克拉底]]和[[盖伦|盖伦]]的理论上<ref>Unani Medicine in India: Its Origin and Fundamental Concepts by [[Hakim_Syed_Zillur_Rahman|Hakim Syed Zillur Rahman]], History of Science, Philosophy and Culture in Indian Civilization, Vol. IV Part 2 (Medicine and Life Sciences in India), Ed. B. V. Subbarayappa, Centre for Studies in Civilizations, [[Project_of_History_of_Indian_Science,_Philosophy_and_Culture|Project of History of Indian Science, Philosophy and Culture]], New Delhi, 2001, pp. 298-325</ref>。

尤那尼医学的希腊之源可以从它建立在[[体液学说|四大体液体系]]上可以看出：[[痰|痰]]（Balgham）、血（Dam）、黄[[胆汁|胆汁]]（Ṣafrā'）和黑胆汁（Saudā'），不过它也受到了印度传统医学和[[中医学|中国传统医学]]的影响<ref>{{cite journal |title=The use of Chinese herbal drugs in Islamic medicine |journal=Journal of Integrative Medicine |doi=10.1016/S2095-4964(15)60205-9 |url=http://www.jcimjournal.com/jim/showAbstrPage.aspx?articleID=S2095-4964(15)60205-9}}</ref>。

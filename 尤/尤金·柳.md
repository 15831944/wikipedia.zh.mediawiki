'''尤金·C·柳'''（{{lang-en|'''Eugene Chin Yu'''}}），[[美国|美国]][[商人|商人]]及[[政治人物|政治人物]]，曾三度角逐[[美国众议院|美国国会众议院]]{{link-en|佐治亚州第12国会选区|Georgia's 12th congressional district}}的竞选。

==生平==
尤金·柳生于[[韩国|韩国]]，十几岁时随父母移民[[美国|美国]][[佐治亚州|佐治亚州]]，课余时曾在当地工厂工作补贴家用<ref name="Metrospirit">{{cite web| url=http://metrospirit.com/eugene-yu-running-congress/| title=EUGENE YU IS RUNNING FOR CONGRESS… AGAIN!| publisher=Metrospirit| accessdate=2018-05-26| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180527043800/http://metrospirit.com/eugene-yu-running-congress/| archivedate=2018-05-27}}</ref>。高中毕业后，进入奥古斯塔学院就读，同时在[[奥古斯塔-里奇蒙县_(佐治亚州)|里奇蒙县]]任消防员<ref name="Metrospirit"/>。1977年，柳加入[[美国陆军|美国陆军]]，成为了一名[[宪兵|宪兵]]，1980年退役<ref name="Metrospirit"/>。柳回到里奇蒙县，成为了一名警察，后升至副警长<ref name="Metrospirit"/>。此后，柳下海从商，先是在东南装备公司（{{lang-en|Southeastern Equipment Co.}}）任职，后于1994年创立了自己的企业——大陆军事服务公司（{{lang-en|Continental Military Services Inc.}}）销售军事用品<ref name="Metrospirit"/>。柳还曾在韩国联合协会（{{lang-en|Federation of Korean Associations}}）担任总裁<ref>{{cite web| url=https://www.wabe.org/korean-american-candidate-running-gop-primary-us-senate-seat/| title=Korean-American Candidate Running in GOP Primary for U.S. Senate Seat| publisher=NPR-WABE| accessdate=2018-05-26| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180527043800/https://www.wabe.org/korean-american-candidate-running-gop-primary-us-senate-seat/| archivedate=2018-05-27}}</ref>。目前，柳与妻子和两个孩子生活在[[奥古斯塔_(佐治亚州)|奥古斯塔]]，全家为沃伦浸礼会教堂（{{lang-en|Warren Baptist Church}}）的长期成员<ref>{{cite web| url=https://www.yu4congress.us/meet-eugene| title=MEET EUGENE: An American Story| publisher=Yu4Congress| accessdate=2018-05-26| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180527043800/https://www.yu4congress.us/meet-eugene| archivedate=2018-05-27}}</ref>。

==国会选举==
柳是一名[[共和党_(美国)|共和党]]人，曾于2014、2016、2018年连续三度角逐[[美国众议院|美国国会众议院]]{{link-en|佐治亚州第12国会选区|Georgia's 12th congressional district}}的竞选，但皆在党内初选阶段败给[[里克·W·埃伦|里克·W·埃伦]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://results.enr.clarityelections.com/GA/51345/130487/en/summary.html |title=OFFICIAL RESULTS General Primary/General Nonpartisan/Special Election May 20, 2014 |publisher=Georgia Secretary of State |accessdate=2014-07-23 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140524111724/http://results.enr.clarityelections.com/GA/51345/130487/en/summary.html |archivedate=2014年5月24日 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://results.enr.clarityelections.com/GA/60041/171475/en/summary.html |title=Official Results |publisher=Georgia Secretary of State |accessdate=2016-06-21 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160627150228/http://results.enr.clarityelections.com/GA/60041/171475/en/summary.html |archivedate=2016-06-27 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url=https://www.nytimes.com/elections/results/georgia-house-district-12-primary-election| title=Georgia Primary Election Results: 12th House District| publisher=New York Times| accessdate=2018-05-26| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180527043800/https://www.nytimes.com/elections/results/georgia-house-district-12-primary-election| archivedate=2018-05-27}}</ref>。柳还曾于2014年参选该州的国会参议员，但中途退选<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.wfxg.com/story/24796216/yu-drops-out-senate-race-now-seeks-congress-seat?utm_content=buffer98636&utm_medium=social&utm_source=facebook.com&utm_campaign=buffer| title=Yu drops out of senate race, now seeks congressional seat| publisher=WXFG-Fox54 News Now| accessdate=2018-05-26| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180527043801/http://www.wfxg.com/story/24796216/yu-drops-out-senate-race-now-seeks-congress-seat?utm_content=buffer98636&utm_medium=social&utm_source=facebook.com&utm_campaign=buffer| archivedate=2018-05-27}}</ref>。

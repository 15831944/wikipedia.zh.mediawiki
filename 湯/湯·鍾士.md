== 早年生活 ==
他在英國[[南威爾斯|南威爾斯]]小鎮{{link-en|翠佛瑞|Treforest}}（{{lang|en|Treforest}}）出生，父親老托馬斯·伍德沃德是名煤炭[[礦工|礦工]]，母親娘家姓氏為瓊斯。他的祖父母和外祖母都是[[英格蘭人|英格蘭人]]，外祖父為[[威爾士人|威爾士人]]<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/newstopics/howaboutthat/5490511/Sir-Tom-Jones-is-English.html |title=Sir Tom Jones 'is English' |work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]] |date=10 June 2009|publisher=[[Telegraph_Media_Group|TMG]] |location=London |issn=0307-1235 |oclc=49632006 |accessdate=4 May 2013}}</ref>。他年幼時已經喜愛唱歌，在家庭聚會和婚宴上都會高歌，課餘亦會參加學校的[[合唱團|合唱團]]。他討厭上學，但是在唱歌中找到自信<ref name="DIDiscs TJ">{{cite episode |title=Desert Island Discs with Tom Jones |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b00tx499 |series=Desert Island Discs | serieslink=Desert Island Discs |network=[[BBC|BBC]] |station=[[BBC_Radio_4|Radio 4]] |airdate=2010-09-26}}</ref>。12歲那年曾經染上[[肺結核|肺結核]]，需要臥床兩年方能痊癒，期間他可以做的只有聽歌和畫畫<ref>Lucy Ellis & Bryony Sutherland, ''Tom Jones Close Up'', p. 9-10.</ref>。

1957年，與中學同學梅林達·特倫查德（{{lang|en|Melinda Trenchard}}）奉子成婚，二人當時只得16歲<ref>[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-1298013/Tom-Jones-70-finally-looks-old-53-year-old-son-stops-dyeing-hair.html Tom Jones, 70, finally looks as old as his 53-year-old son as he stops dyeing his hair] – ''Daily Mail''. By Georgina Littlejohn. Published 27 July 2010. Retrieved 12 April 2012.</ref>。為了生計，曾經在手套工廠打工，亦試過從事建築<ref name="Tom Jones NNDB">[http://www.nndb.com/people/347/000025272/ "Tom Jones NNDB"]</ref>。

== 感情生活 ==
他有過多次越軌的記錄<ref>[http://www.walesonline.co.uk/news/wales-news/2009/02/21/tom-jones-i-still-love-linda-91466-22978645/2/ Tom Jones 'I still love Linda'] – WalesOnline.co.uk. Published 21 February 2009. Retrieved 12 April 2012.</ref>，在事業全盛時期，身邊的女伴無數，甚至有說他一年有多達250次[[多人性交|多人性交]]，傳過緋聞的女子包括：[[至上女聲組合|至上女聲組合]]成員{{link-en|瑪麗·威爾遜|Mary Wilson (singer)}}（{{lang|en|Mary Wilson}}）、前世界小姐{{link-en|馬喬裡·華萊士|Marjorie Wallace}}（{{lang|en|Marjorie Wallace}}）、女星{{link-en|卡桑德拉·彼得森|Cassandra Peterson}}（{{lang|en|Cassandra Peterson}}）。他的元配雖然曾經為此沮喪，甚至向湯·鍾斯施襲，但始終對他不離不棄<ref name= "Daily Mail">{{cite news |date=13 September 2006 |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-404951/Tom-Jones-reveals-night-wife-beat-black-blue.html |title=Tom Jones reveals the night my wife beat me black and blue |work=MailOnline |last=Moodie |first=Clemmie |accessdate=16 July 2009}}</ref>。

1987年10月，在美國巡迴表演期間曾經跟模特兒凱瑟琳·博克利（{{lang|en|Katherine Berkery}}）有過一段霧水情緣。凱瑟琳為他誕下麟兒，但經過法庭裁決和[[親子鑑定|親子鑑定]]後，他依然不認兒子。

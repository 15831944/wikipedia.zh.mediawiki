'''湯利·哈斯'''（{{lang-en|Townley Haas}}，{{bd|1996年|12月13日}}）是[[美國|美國]]的一位游泳運動員。他現在就讀於[[德克薩斯大學|德克薩斯大學]]<ref name=UT>{{cite web|url=http://www.texassports.com/roster.aspx?rp_id=6310 |title=Texas Longhorns Athletics - Townley Haas - 2015-16 |publisher=Texassports.com |date= |accessdate=2017-07-27}}</ref>。哈斯出生在[[維吉尼亞州|維吉尼亞州]][[里士滿_(維吉尼亞州)|里士滿]]<ref>{{cite news|last1=GILLIGAN|first1=GREGORY J.|title=Richmonder Townley Haas' family in 'pure pandemonium' after he wins Olympic gold medal|url=http://www.richmond.com/sports/olympics/summer/article_e60a8e4f-02c5-59d8-864d-2545ee3444a1.html|accessdate=10 August 2016|agency=Richmond Times Dispatch|publisher=BH Media Group|date=August 10, 2016|quote="the Henrico County native won his first Olympic gold medal" ... "Haas family house in the Church Run subdivision in western Henrico shortly after the Benedictine College Preparatory graduate received his gold medal"}}</ref>。他代表美國參加了2016年奧運會和2017年世界游泳錦標賽。

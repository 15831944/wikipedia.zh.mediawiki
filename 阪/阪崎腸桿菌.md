'''阪崎腸桿菌'''（學名：{{lang|la|'''''Cronobacter sakazakii'''''}}），舊屬[[腸桿菌屬|腸桿菌屬]]，2007年獲《[[國際系統及演化微生物學期刊|國際系統及演化微生物學期刊]]》（{{lang|en|International Journal of Systematic and Evolutionary Microbiology}}）的確認，現時已連同其他新發現的細菌獨立成為一個新的屬''[[Cronobacter|Cronobacter]]''<ref name="Iversen C, Mullane N, Barbara McCardell, et al. 2008">{{cite journal |author=Iversen C, Mullane N, Barbara McCardell |title=Cronobacter gen. nov., a new genus to accommodate the biogroups of Enterobacter sakazakii, and proposal of Cronobacter sakazakii gen. nov. comb. nov., C. malonaticus sp. nov., C. turicensis sp. nov., C. muytjensii sp. nov., C. dublinensis sp. nov., Cronobacter genomospecies 1, and of three subspecies, C. dublinensis sp. nov. subsp. dublinensis subsp. nov., C. dublinensis sp. nov. subsp. lausannensis subsp. nov., and C. dublinensis sp. nov. subsp. lactaridi subsp. nov |journal=[[國際系統及演化微生物學期刊|IJSEM]] |date=2008-06 |volumn=58 |pages=1442-1447 |doi=10.1099/ijs.0.65577-0|display-authors=etal}}</ref><ref name="Joseph et al.">{{cite journal|title=Cronobactercondimenti sp. nov., isolated from spiced meat, and Cronobacteruniversalis sp. nov., a species designation for Cronobacter sp. genomospecies 1, recovered from a leg infection, water and food ingredients|journal=International Journal of Systematic and Evolutionary Microbiology|volume=62|issue=6|pages=1277–1283|doi=10.1099/ijs.0.032292-0|url=http://ijs.microbiologyresearch.org/content/journal/ijsem/10.1099/ijs.0.032292-0|date=2012|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Susan Joseph, Esin Cetinkaya, Hana Drahovska, Arturo Levican, Maria J. Figueras, Stephen J. Forsythe}}</ref>。牠是一種[[革蘭氏陰性菌|革蘭氏陰性菌]]、[[兼性厭氧生物|兼性厭氧生物]]及呈棒狀的[[細菌|細菌]]，屬於[[腸桿菌科|腸桿菌科]]。
''Cronobacter''屬之下的大多數物種都是成人的[[病原|病原]]，會使宿主免疫受損的機會性感染細菌。雖然偶爾會令幼童感染，但其感染後的死亡率很高（達40–80%）<ref name="MMWR">{{cite journal |title=Enterobacter sakazakii infections associated with the use of powdered infant formula--Tennessee, 2001 |journal=MMWR Morb Mortal Wkly Rep |volume=51 |issue=14 |pages=297–300 |year=2002 |pmid=12002167 |doi= |author1= Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)}} [http://www.cdc.gov/mmwr/preview/mmwrhtml/mm5114a1.htm Free full text]</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author=Lai KK |title=''Enterobacter sakazakii'' infections among neonates, infants, children, and adults. Case reports and a review of the literature |journal=Medicine (Baltimore) |volume=80 |issue=2 |pages=113–22 |year=2001 |pmid=11307587 |doi=10.1097/00005792-200103000-00004}}</ref><ref name="Bowen">{{cite journal |author=Bowen AB, Braden CR |title=Invasive Enterobacter sakazakii disease in infants |journal=Emerging Infect Dis |volume=12 |issue=8 |pages=1185–9 |year=2006 |pmid=16965695 |doi=10.3201/eid1208.051509 |pmc=3291213}}</ref>。

在嬰兒可引起[[菌血症|菌血症]]、[[腦膜炎|腦膜炎]]和[[壞死性小腸結腸炎|壞死性小腸結腸炎]]。有些新生兒受阪崎腸桿菌感染的原因發現與使用受污染的[[嬰幼兒配方奶粉|嬰幼兒配方奶粉]]相關<ref name="MMWR"/><ref name="Bowen"/>，
有些菌種甚至還可以在乾燥的狀態下生存兩年以上<ref name="Caubilla-Barron">{{cite journal |author=Caubilla-Barron J & Forsythe S |title=Dry stress and survival time of ''Enterobacter sakazakii'' and other ''Enterobacteriaceae'' in dehydrated infant formula |journal= Journal Food Protection |volume=13 |pages=467–472 |year=2007}}</ref>。不過，阪崎腸桿菌可被高溫殺滅，所以世衛建議沖泡配方奶粉時應用攝氏70度的熱水來調開，然後再加入冷開水降溫<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.cfs.gov.hk/tc_chi/programme/programme_rafs/programme_rafs_fm_02_04.html |title=? |publisher=香港衞生署 |accessdate=2016-02-05 |language=zh-hant}}</ref>。

然而並非所有個案都與受污染的嬰兒配方奶粉相關。2011年11月，[[高潔絲|高潔絲]][[衛生棉條|衛生棉條]]的多個批次由於受阪崎腸桿菌污染而要收回<ref name=seegerweiss>{{cite web | url=http://www.seegerweiss.com/news/Kotex_Tampons_Recalled_Due_to_Risk_of_Life-Threatening_Infection | title=FDA Recall of Kotex Tampons | publisher=Seeger Weiss LLP | accessdate=2012-02-29 |language=en }}</ref>。

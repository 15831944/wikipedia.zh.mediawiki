'''泰勒河'''（{{lang-en|Taylor River}}）源头位于科罗拉多州的[[埃尔克山_(科罗拉多州)|埃尔克山]][[城堡峰_(科罗拉多州)|城堡峰]]附近<ref>[http://www.peakware.com/peaks.html?pk=1095 Castle Peak]. </ref>，流经[[甘尼森縣_(科羅拉多州)|甘尼森县]]的东北部，靠近[[大陆分水岭|大陆分水岭]]
<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Columbia gazetteer of the world|last=Cohen,|first=Saul Bernard|publisher=[[Columbia_University_Press|Columbia University Press]]|year=1998|isbn=0-231-11040-5}}</ref>。在阿尔蒙特，泰勒河与[[东河_(科罗拉多州)|东河]]汇合形成[[甘尼森河|甘尼森河]]，全长{{Convert|48.2|mi}}。<ref name="NHD">U.S. Geological Survey. </ref>

大部分河段位于[[甘尼森国家森林|甘尼森国家森林]]中,是一个受欢迎的[[飛蠅釣|钓鱼]]<ref>[http://www.wildtroutonthefly.com/currentfishingreports/taylorriver.html Taylor River: trout fly fishing]. </ref><ref>[http://www.anglerguide.com/colorado/taylor.html Fishing the Taylor River] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110707143649/http://www.anglerguide.com/colorado/taylor.html |date=2011-07-07 }}. </ref>和漂流目的地<ref>[http://www.coloradodirectory.com/rafting/taylor.html Taylor River: white water rafting]. </ref>。

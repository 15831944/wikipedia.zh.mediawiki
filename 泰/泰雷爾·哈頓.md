'''泰雷爾·哈頓'''（'''Tyrrell Hatton'''，{{bd|1991年|10月14日}}）是[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]的一位職業高爾夫球運動員，參加高爾夫歐巡賽的賽事。他在2010年以業餘球員身份參加了英國公開賽。2011年，他轉為職業球員<ref name=Woodcote>{{cite web |url=http://www.jamegatour.co.uk/news-and-reports/tournament-reports/302-hattons-first-professional-win.html |title=Hattons First Professional Win |publisher=[[Jamega_Pro_Golf_Tour|Jamega Pro Golf Tour]] |accessdate=7 September 2014}}</ref><ref name=Caversham>{{cite web |url=http://www.jamegatour.co.uk/news-and-reports/tournament-reports/451-caversham-heath-is-hattons-garden.html |title=Caversham Heath  is "Hatton's Garden" |publisher=[[Jamega_Pro_Golf_Tour|Jamega Pro Golf Tour]] |accessdate=7 September 2014}}</ref><ref name=Frilford>{{cite web |url=https://www.europrotour.com/blog/hatton-cards-66-to-win-at-frilford-heath/ |title=Hatton cards 66 to win at Frilford Heath |publisher=[[PGA_EuroPro_Tour|PGA EuroPro Tour]] |accessdate=7 September 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140907191256/https://www.europrotour.com/blog/hatton-cards-66-to-win-at-frilford-heath/ |archivedate=2014年9月7日 }}</ref><ref name=YGTC>{{cite web |url=http://www.europrotour.com/blog/hatton-wins-your-golf-travel-classic/ |title=Hatton wins Your Golf Travel Classic |publisher=[[PGA_EuroPro_Tour|PGA EuroPro Tour]] |accessdate=7 September 2014}}</ref>。2014年，他開始參加歐巡賽的賽事。

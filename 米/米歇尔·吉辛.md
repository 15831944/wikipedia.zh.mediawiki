'''米歇尔·吉辛'''（{{lang-de|Michelle Gisin}}，{{bd|1993年|12月5日||}}）生于格劳宾登州萨梅丹，是一名瑞士女子高山滑雪运动员<ref name="schweizer"/>。米歇尔·吉辛一岁时受父母的影响开始接触滑雪<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/alpine-skiing/athlete-profile-n3034537-michelle-gisin.htm|title=Alpine Skiing {{!}} Athlete Profile: Michelle GISIN|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-09|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180228025621/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/alpine-skiing/athlete-profile-n3034537-michelle-gisin.htm|archivedate=2018-02-28}}</ref>。她的哥哥[[Marc_Gisin|Marc]]和姐姐[[Dominique_Gisin|Dominique]]也都是高山滑雪运动员<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.fis-ski.com/alpine-skiing/news-multimedia/news/article=dominique-gisin-solden-the-goal.html |title=Dominique Gisin: "Sölden is the goal" |date=2012-08-31 |work=[[国际滑雪联合会|Fédération Internationale de Ski]] |accessdate=2013-12-17|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140221221028/https://www.fis-ski.com/alpine-skiing/news-multimedia/news/article=dominique-gisin-solden-the-goal.html|archivedate=2014-02-21}}</ref>。她在2014年开始和意大利高山滑雪[[Luca_De_Aliprandini|Luca De Aliprandini]]交往<ref name=si>{{cite web |url=https://www.schweizer-illustrierte.ch/stars/schweiz/michelle-gisin-facebook-freund-luca-de-aliprandini-liebe |script-title=de:Michelle Gisin und ihr Freund sprechen über ihre Liebe |last=Meier |first= Sarah|date=2018-01-01|publisher={{lang|de|Schweizer Illustrierte}}|access-date=2018-02-25}}</ref>。

'''米利瓦尼利'''（Milli Vanilli）是[[德國|德國]]二人團體，由法博·莫爾文（Fab Morvan）與羅伯·皮拉特斯（Rob Pilatus）組成，其發行的首張專輯《Girl You Know It's True》在全球獲得廣泛成功<ref name="Milli Vanilli">{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/02/27/milli-vanilli_n_4860222.html|work=Huffington Post|date=2014-02-27|title=The Real Voices Behind Milli Vanilli Share Their Side Of The Lip Syncing Scandal}}</ref>。

之後醜聞傳出，證實莫爾文與皮拉特斯沒有參與專輯中任何一首歌曲的演唱，演唱歌曲的歌手另有其人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.latimes.com/1990-11-16/entertainment/ca-4894_1_milli-vanilli|title=It's True: Milli Vanilli Didn't Sing : Pop music: The duo could be stripped of its Grammy after admitting it lip-synced the best-selling 'Girl You Know It's True.'|work=Los Angeles Times|date=1990-11-16|accessdate=2014-12-20}}</ref>。其製作人隨後於1990年11月14日承認，莫爾文與皮拉特斯二人確實沒有演唱過專輯中的歌曲。<ref name="History">{{cite web|url=http://www.history.com/this-day-in-history/milli-vanilli-win-the-best-new-artist-grammy|work=History Channel|accessdate=2014-12-20|title=Milli Vanilli win the Best New Artist Grammy}}</ref>。

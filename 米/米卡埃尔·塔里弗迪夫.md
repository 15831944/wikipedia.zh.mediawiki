'''米卡埃尔·塔里弗迪夫'''（俄语：МикаэлТаривердиев，亚美尼亚语：ՄիքայելԹարիվերդիեւ ; 1931年8月15日- 1996年7月24日）是一位拥有亚美尼亚血统的著名苏联作曲家，曾担任苏联电影摄影师联盟作曲家协会领导。主要作品有《[[春天里的十七个瞬间|春天里的十七个瞬间]]》。

== 作品 ==
*''Night Pastimes'' (film music)
*''Quo vadis?'' ([[Organ_Symphony|Organ Symphony]] '''''Chernobyl''''', 1st [[Organ_Concerto_(Tariverdiev)|Organ Concerto]] '''''Cassandra''''', Two Chorale Preludes) 
*''Moods'' (6 preludes from the cycle ''10 chorale preludes imitating to the old masters'', 3rd Organ concerto, 10 preludes from the cycle ''Moods'' (organ transcription by [[Alexey_Parchine|Alexey Parchine]]) 
*''Remembering Venice'' (film music)
*''[[Seventeen_Moments_of_Spring|Seventeen Moments of Spring]]'' (film music)
*''I Am The Tree'' (monologues on [[Posenyan|Posenyan]], [[Andrey_Voznesensky|Voznesensky]], [[Mikhail_Arkadyevich_Svetlov|Svetlov]], [[Ashkenazy|Ashkenazy]] poetry singing by author) 
*''Instrumentalnye kinokhity'' (''Film Music Hits'')
*Composer's interpretation of Jewish songs
*''Prescience of love'' (''20th Century Madrigals'')
*''Graf Cagliostro'' (opera comique)
*''Mikael Tariverdiev's  Avant-Guard'' (vocal cycles on Japan medieval poetry, [[Bella_Akhmadulina|Bella Akhmadulina]], [[Leonid_Martynov|Leonid Martynov]], [[Semen_Kirsanov|Semen Kirsanov]], [[Mikhail_Malishevsky|Mikhail Malishevsky]])
*''Nostalgy'' - Japan duo ''Hide-Hide'' performing Mikael Tariverdiev's music on classical Japan instruments - [[semisen|semisen]] and [[siakuhkati|siakuhkati]] with orchestra.
*VOX HUMANA. Vocal cycles  to the verses by L/Martinov, B. Akhmadulina, Medieval Japanese poets “Watercolors”, E. Vinokurov, M. Tzvetaeva, “Waiting” to the poem of R/Rojdestvenski. Performers: Zara Dolukhanova (soprano), Nina Lebedeva  (soprano), Maria Lemesheva (soprano), Nina Svetlanova (piano), Mikael Tariverdiev (piano), Orchestra of Boris Pokrovsky Chamber Music Theater  (conductor – Vladimir Agronsky).

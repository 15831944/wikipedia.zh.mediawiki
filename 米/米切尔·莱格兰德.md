'''米切尔·莱格兰德'''（{{lang-fr|'''Michel Legrand'''}}，{{IPA-fr|miʃɛl ʒɑ̃ ləɡʁɑ̃}}，{{bd|1932年|2月24日|2019年|1月26日|catIdx=Legrand, Michel}}），法国[[作曲家|作曲家]]、编曲家、[[指挥家|指挥家]]和爵士乐钢琴师<ref name=hindu>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thehindu.com/entertainment/music/oscar-crowned-french-composer-michel-legrand-dies-at-86/article26098965.ece|title=Oscar-crowned French composer Michel Legrand dies at 86|last=Reuters|date=2019-01-26|work=The Hindu|access-date=2019-01-26|language=en-IN|issn=0971-751X}}</ref>。职业生涯共为200多部影视作品创作配乐和歌曲<ref name=nyt>{{cite news|last=Chinen|first=Nate|title=Music in Review; Michel Legrand|url=https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9F00E7D91331F933A25750C0A9619C8B63&ref=michellegrand|access-date=2011-12-09|newspaper=New York Times|date=2007-03-10}}</ref>，作品包括[[法国新浪潮|法国新浪潮]]导演[[雅克·德米|雅克·德米]]的《{{le|瑟堡的雨伞|The Umbrellas of Cherbourg}}》（1964年）和《{{le|柳媚花娇|The Young Girls of Rochefort}}》（1967年），后者使得他首次获得[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]提名。他最终凭借1968年电影《{{le|龙凤斗智|The Thomas Crown Affair (1968 film)}}》歌曲《{{le|心中的风车|The Windmills of Your Mind}}》赢得个人首个奥斯卡奖<ref name=hallfame>{{cite web|title=Michel Legrand|url=http://www.songwritershalloffame.org/exhibits/C143|publisher=Songwriters Hall of Fame|access-date=2011-12-09|archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/68BDRhBSc?url=http://www.songwritershalloffame.org/exhibits/C143|archive-date=2012-06-04|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

莱格兰德于2019年1月26日在巴黎去世，享年86岁<ref name= france24>{{cite web|url=https://www.france24.com/en/20190126-oscar-winning-french-composer-michel-legrand-dies-86 |title= Oscar-winning French composer Michel Legrand dies at 86|website=france24.com|access-date= 2019-01-26|date= 2019-01-26}}</ref>。去世前，他仍然坚持创作音乐，还计划在春季举行多场音乐会<ref>{{Cite news | url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2019/jan/26/michel-legrand-oscar-winning-composer-dies-aged-86 | title=Michel Legrand, Oscar-winning composer, dies aged 86| newspaper=The Guardian| date=2019-01-26| last1=France-Presse| first1=Agence}}</ref>。

'''米歇·翁福雷'''（Michel Onfray）（1959年1月1日出生）當代法國哲學家，奉行[[感質主義|感質主義]]，[[無神論|無神論]]<ref name='Ireland'>{{cite journal | title = Introductory Note to Onfray | journal = [[New_Politics_(magazine)|New Politics]] | date = Winter 2006 | first = Doug | last = Ireland | authorlink = Doug Ireland | volume = X | issue = 4 | id =  | url = http://ww3.wpunj.edu/newpol/issue40/Ireland40.htm | accessdate = 2010-06-01 }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>和[[無政府主義|無政府主義]]<ref>"He is a self-described hedonist, atheist, libertarian, and left-wing anarchist".[http://www.ainfos.ca/06/dec/ainfos00234.html (en) France, Media, Michel Onfray, A self labeled Anarchist Philosopher]</ref>。他是一個多產的作家，寫過超過50本哲學書<ref name='Irelandquote'>{{cite journal | title = Introductory Note to Onfray | journal = [[New_Politics_(magazine)|New Politics]] | date = Winter 2006 | first = Doug | last = Ireland | authorlink = Doug Ireland | volume = X | issue = 4 | id =  | url = http://ww3.wpunj.edu/newpol/issue40/Ireland40.htm | quote = a gifted and prolific author who, at the age of only 46, has already written 30 books | accessdate = 2010-06-01 }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>[[:fr:Michel_Onfray#Œuvres|Complete list of works on the French Wikipedia page]]</ref>。其思想很大的啟發於法國後現代唯物主義哲學家[[喬治·巴代伊|巴塔耶]]之著作中。

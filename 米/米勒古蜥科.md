'''米勒古蜥科'''（Millerettidae）是種已滅絕[[無孔類|無孔類]][[爬行動物|爬行動物]]，生存於[[二疊紀|二疊紀]]中晚期的[[南非|南非]]<ref name=Parareptilia2>{{cite journal |author=Marcello Ruta, Juan C. Cisneros, Torsten Liebrect, Linda A. Tsuji and Johannes Muller |year=2011 |title=Amniotes through major biological crises: faunal turnover among Parareptiles and the end-Permian mass extinction |url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1475-4983.2011.01051.x/abstract |journal=Palaeontology |volume=54 |issue=5 |pages=1117–1137 |doi=10.1111/j.1475-4983.2011.01051.x }}</ref>。牠們是小型食蟲性動物，外表與生活方式可能類似現代[[蜥蜴|蜥蜴]]。

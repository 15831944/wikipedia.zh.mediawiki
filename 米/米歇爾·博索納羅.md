'''米歇爾·德·保拉·菲尔莫·雷拿度·博索納羅'''（{{lang-pt|Michelle de Paula Firmo Reinaldo Bolsonaro}}，{{bd|1982年|3月22日|catIdx=Bolsonaro, Michelle}}）是[[巴西|巴西]]第一夫人和总统[[雅伊爾·博索納羅|雅伊爾·博索納羅]]的第三任妻子。<ref>[https://www.news.com.au/finance/work/leaders/new-brazilian-presidents-glamorous-wife-steals-the-spotlight/news-story/74802454a2cf7652c988051345ca57c1 “New Brazilian president’s glamorous wife steals the spotlight”]. News.com.au. 03-01-2019.</ref><ref>[https://www.thenational.ae/world/the-americas/watch-jair-bolsonaro-s-wife-michelle-gives-speech-in-sign-language-1.808787 “Watch: Jair Bolsonaro's wife Michelle gives speech in sign language”]. The National. 02-01-2019.</ref>

2007年6月，25岁的米歇爾在進步黨工作時認識了當時52歲的政治家雅伊爾·博索納羅。同年9月，她成为了雅伊爾的秘书。<ref>[https://www.thesun.co.uk/news/7724057/jair-bolsonaros-wife-michelle-congressional-secretary-brazil-president-elect-first-lady/ “Who is Jair Bolsonaro’s wife Michelle? Former congressional secretary married to Brazil’s President-elect”]. The Sun. 12-11-2018.</ref>他们在2013年11月结婚。他們的女兒在2011年出生，而米歇爾本身亦育有1名和前伴侶所生的女兒。

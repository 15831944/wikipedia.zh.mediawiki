'''贺猛'''（{{lang-my|ဟိုမိန်းမြို့}}）<ref>[http://www.themimu.info/sites/themimu.info/files/documents/Tsp_Map_VL_Langkho_Shan_MIMU154v03_28Nov2013_A1.pdf Myanmar Information Management Unit - Langkho Township, Shan State] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150922143149/http://www.themimu.info/sites/themimu.info/files/documents/Tsp_Map_VL_Langkho_Shan_MIMU154v03_28Nov2013_A1.pdf |date=2015-09-22 }}</ref>，又译'''贺蒙'''、'''洪孟'''等，是[[缅甸|缅甸]][[掸邦|掸邦]][[朗科縣|朗科縣]][[朗科镇区|朗科镇区]]下辖的一个村。

==地理==
贺猛位于山区，距离[[莱兰山|莱兰山]]东北约15公里，距离[[泰国|泰国]][[湄宏順府|湄宏順府]]边界以西约5.7公里。<ref name="GE"/>

==历史==
由于该村地处[[薩爾溫江|薩爾溫江]]东边，交通不便，使得[[缅甸国防军|缅甸国防军]]势力难以介入。自1985年以来，众多的掸邦反叛集团将该村作为大本营，<ref name="GS">{{cite web|url=http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/world/para/sua.htm|title=Shan United Army (SUA) - Mong Tai Army (MTA)|work=GlobalSecurity|accessdate=2016-05-26}}</ref>如1996年以前的[[孟泰军|孟泰军]]、[[掸邦民族军|掸邦民族军]]和[[南掸邦军|南掸邦军]]。<ref>[https://democracyforburma.wordpress.com/2013/03/06/burma-myanmar-14-military-trucks-arrived-in-homong-opposite-maehongson-on-3-march/ BURMA MYANMAR: 14 military trucks arrived in Homong, opposite Maehongson, on 3 March]</ref>同时，该村也成为了一个{{le|毒品贩运|drug traffic}}枢纽<ref>[http://www.burmanet.org/news/2006/03/31/shan-herald-agency-for-news-biz-hub-moves-to-mandalay/ Shan Herald Agency for News: Biz hub moves to Mandalay, Fri 31 Mar 2006] {{Wayback|url=http://www.burmanet.org/news/2006/03/31/shan-herald-agency-for-news-biz-hub-moves-to-mandalay/ |date=20160624183252 }}</ref>。

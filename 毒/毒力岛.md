'''毒力岛'''（{{lang-en|Pathogenicity island}}，'''PAIs'''，也译为'''致病岛'''）一词出现于1990年，是指一些[[微生物|微生物]]通过[[基因水平轉移|基因水平轉移]]获得的一类[[基因组岛|基因组岛]]<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Hacker | first1 = J | last2 = Bender | first2 = L | last3 = Ott | first3 = M | last4 = Wingender | first4 = J | last5 = Lund | first5 = B | display-authors = etal   | year = 1990 | title = Deletions of chro- mosomal regions coding for fimbriae and hemolysins occur in vivo and in vitro in various extraintestinal Escherichia coli iso- lates | url = | journal = Microb. Pathog | volume = 8 | issue = | pages = 213–25 }}</ref><ref name="Hacker_2000">{{cite journal | last1 = Hacker | first1 = J | last2 = Kaper | first2 = JB | year = 2000 | title = Pathogenicity islands and the evolution of microbes | url = | journal = Annu Rev Microbiol | volume = 54 | issue = | pages = 641–679 | pmid = 11018140 | doi=10.1146/annurev.micro.54.1.641}}</ref>，通常出现在动物和植物的[[病原体|病原体]]微生物[[基因组|基因组]]里。此外，[[革蘭氏陽性菌|革蘭氏陽性菌]]和[[革兰氏阴性菌|革兰氏阴性菌]]中均发现存在毒力岛。毒力岛可以通过[[质粒|质粒]]、[[噬菌体|噬菌体]]或 [[转座子|转座子]]接合来实现横向基因转移<ref name="Hacker_1997">{{cite journal | last1 = Hacker | first1 = J. | last2 = Blum-Oehler | first2 = G. | last3 = Muhldorfer | first3 = I. | last4 = Tschape | first4 = H. | year = 1997 | title = Pathogenecity islands of virulent bacteria:  structure, function and impact on microbial evolution | url = | journal = Molecular Microbiology | volume = 23 | issue = 6| pages = 1089–1097 | doi=10.1046/j.1365-2958.1997.3101672.x | pmid=9106201}}</ref>。因此，毒力岛能促进微生物的发展进化。

一种细菌可能有不止一个毒力岛。例如，[[沙门氏菌|沙门氏菌]]（''Salmonella''）具有至少五个毒力岛。

[[根瘤菌|根瘤菌]]基因组中的类似结构称为[[共生岛|共生岛]]（symbiosis island）。

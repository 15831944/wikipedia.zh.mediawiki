'''碎片大厦'''（{{lang-en|The Shard}}），又稱'''夏德塔'''、'''摘星塔'''。是位于[[英国|英国]][[伦敦|伦敦]]的伦敦桥站西南侧的[[摩天大楼|摩天大楼]]。高309.6米，为[[英國|英國]]最高建筑物，[[欧洲|欧洲]]第二高建筑物（不計電視發射塔）。耗资15亿英镑（包括收購土地及利息支出），2008年9月开工，2012年7月5日竣工外侧部分，2013年2月1日完工。<ref>[http://www.news24.com/World/News/The-Shard-launched-in-London-amid-debate-20120706 The Shard launched in London amid debate]</ref>

== 结构 ==
碎片大厦由曾担任[[巴黎|巴黎]]的[[蓬皮杜艺术中心|蓬皮杜艺术中心]]设计的[[意大利|意大利]]建筑师[[伦佐·皮亚诺|伦佐·皮亚诺]]负责设计。大厦在经济不景气的时候开幕，开幕之时一个单位都未售出<ref>[http://hk.apple.nextmedia.com/international/art/20120707/16491753 倫敦 The Shard揭幕 歐洲第一高樓僅一租戶]《蘋果日報》2012年7月7日</ref>。

直至2013年2月，《[[泰晤士報|泰晤士報]]》報道碎片大廈25層辦公樓層仍未簽到租客。2013年5月，《每日郵報》報道受到高房租和經濟不景氣影響，大廈72層中僅有六層啟用。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2330926/Almost-year--The-Shard-turns-tallest-white-elephant-world.html|title=Almost empty after a year ... The Shard turns into the tallest white elephant in the world|work=Daily Mail|date=25 May 2013|accessdate=16 June 2013}}</ref>

現時大樓的第31-33層是三家分別名為“Oblix”<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/lifeandstyle/2013/jun/08/restaurants-food-and-drink|title=Restaurant: Oblix, London|work=The Guardian|date=8 June 2013|accessdate=28 June 2013}}</ref>，“胡同”<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thehandbook.com/news/hutong|work=The Handbook|title=Hutong at The Shard|date=6 June 2013|accessdate=25 June 2013}}</ref>和“碎片水”的餐館。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.timeout.com/london/restaurants/aqua-shard|title=Aqua Shard|work=Time Out|date=7 January 2013|accessdate=28 June 2013}}</ref>34-52層則是[[香格里拉酒店|香格里拉酒店]]，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hospitality-interiors.net/news/articles/2013/03/369874252-excitement-builds-shangri-la’s-summer-opening-shard|title=Excitement builds for Shangri-La's summer opening at The Shard|publisher=Hospitality Interiors|date=13 March 2013|accessdate=4 August 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.building.co.uk/news/shard’s-luxury-hotel-hit-by-fit-out-delays/5055868.article|title=Shard's luxury hotel hit by fit-out delays|publisher=Building.co.uk|date=6 June 2013|accessdate=4 August 2013}}</ref>2014年5月6日開張。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/luxury/travel/24017/shangri-la-london-opens-in-the-shard.html|title= Shangri-La London opens in the Shard|publisher=The Daily Telegraph (London)|date=6 February 2014|accessdate=21 April 2014}}</ref> 

截止2014年2月，大樓的其他租客為：[[卡塔爾|卡塔爾]]的[[半島電視臺|半島電視臺]]在大樓中有演播室；<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.standard.co.uk/news/london/al-jazeera-to-open-new-tv-studio-in-the-shard-8713331.html|title=Al Jazeera to open new TV studio in The Shard|work=London Evening Standard|date=17 July 2013|accessdate=31 July 2013}}</ref>律師事務所Mathys & Squire；<ref>{{cite news|last=Joanne|first=Harris|title=IP firm Mathys & Squire becomes first firm to move into Shard|url=http://www.thelawyer.com/analysis/the-lawyer-management/management-news/ip-firm-mathys-and-squire-becomes-first-firm-to-move-into-shard/3017951.article|accessdate=17 April 2014|newspaper=The Lawyer|date=21 March 2014}}</ref>投資銀行Duff & Phelps；能源公司南拐煤氣公司（South Hook Gas）；風險資本公司Foresight Group；以及私立醫療公司HCA集團。大樓的空置率為70%。<ref>{{cite news|last=Kate|first=Allen|title=Shard finds more tenants as Irvine Sellar steps up lettings|url=http://www.ft.com/cms/s/0/9780866a-94aa-11e3-af71-00144feab7de.html#axzz2z9wY4oin|accessdate=17 April 2014|newspaper=Financial Times|date=13 February 2014}}</ref>

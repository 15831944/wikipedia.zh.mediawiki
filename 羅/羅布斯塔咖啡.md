'''羅布斯塔咖啡'''（Robusta coffee）是指由[[中果咖啡|中果咖啡]]果製成的[[咖啡豆|咖啡豆]]。

羅布斯塔咖啡原產地是[[漠南非洲|漠南非洲]]的中部和西部。<ref name=dagoon/>它易於照看，有更高的產量，[[咖啡因|咖啡因]]幾乎是兩倍，有更多[[抗氧化劑|抗氧化劑]]，<ref>{{cite journal | url= http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0308814610008319 | title = Antioxidant activity, polyphenols, caffeine and melanoidins in soluble coffee: The influence of processing conditions and raw material| first1=J. A. | last1=Vignoli | first2=D. G. | last2= Bassoli | first3=M. T. | last3=Benassi | publisher= Elsevier Ltd. | journal=Food Chemistry | volume=124 | issue=3 | year=2011 | pages=863–868 | doi=10.1016/j.foodchem.2010.07.008}}</ref>比[[小果咖啡|小果咖啡]]更不易受感染。<ref name="bbc">{{cite web|last1=Penarredonda|first1=Jose Luis|title=The disease that could change how we drink coffee |url=http://www.bbc.com/future/story/20171106-the-disease-that-could-change-how-we-drink-coffee |date = 6 November 2017 |website=BBC: In depth, Food |accessdate=23 November 2017}}</ref>它在全球咖啡產量中佔了大約40%，與小果咖啡達成平衡。<ref name="CoffeeProduction">{{cite web |title=Coffee: World Markets and Trade |publisher=United States Department of Agriculture - Foreign Agricultural Service |date=June 16, 2017 |url=http://usda.mannlib.cornell.edu/usda/fas/tropprod//2010s/2017/tropprod-06-16-2017.pdf |accessdate=December 8, 2017}}</ref>

羅布斯塔咖啡豆是更加強烈、濃郁的咖啡，擁有與眾不同的、泥土香味，但比小果咖啡更苦，這是由於有更高的[[吡嗪|吡嗪]]含量。

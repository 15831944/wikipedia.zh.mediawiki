'''羅傑'''（{{lang-en|'''Roger'''}}，2006年-2018年12月8日）是一隻澳洲的雄性[[紅大袋鼠|紅大袋鼠]]，牠因為體格壯碩而聞名。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/video/2018/dec/10/remembering-roger-the-ripped-kangaroo-video-obituary|title=Remembering Roger the ripped kangaroo – video obituary|first1=Gary|last1=Marshall|first2=Source: The Kangaroo Sanctuary Alice|last2=Springs|date=10 December 2018|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2018|via=www.theguardian.com}}</ref>

==生平==
羅傑的母親被一輛汽車撞死，當時還是幼袋鼠的羅傑被克里斯·巴恩斯（Chris Barnes）救出<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tmz.com/2018/12/10/roger-the-viral-ripped-kangaroo-dead/|title=Roger the Ripped Kangaroo Dead at 12|website=TMZ|accessdate=10 December 2018}}</ref> Barnes started the Kangaroo Sanctuary<ref>{{cite web |title=The Kangaroo Sanctuary |url=https://kangaroosanctuary.com/ |publisher=The Kangaroo Sanctuary |accessdate=11 December 2018}}</ref>，之後牠和其它袋鼠一起被安置在[[愛麗斯泉|愛麗斯泉]]，巴恩斯表示，羅傑多年來一直是那裏的雄性[[社群首領|社群首領]]。羅傑的身高長到{{convert|6|ft|7|in|m|0}}，體重超過{{convert|200|lb|kg}}，而有些說法則指出牠最重時達{{convert|196|lb|kg}}。

在2015年，一段羅傑擊凹了鐵桶的影片在網路上流傳，這造成羅傑在網路上爆紅<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-australia-46503284|title=RIP Roger the enormous kangaroo|date=10 December 2018|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2018|via=www.bbc.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DFCvPrzj4BU|title=200-pound ripped kangaroo crushes metal|first=|last=CNN|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2018|via=YouTube}}</ref>，2016年，巴恩斯表示羅傑罹患了關節炎<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/news/world/roger-internet-s-favorite-buff-kangaroo-has-died-n945906|title=Roger, the internet's favorite buff kangaroo, has died|website=NBC News|accessdate=10 December 2018}}</ref>。

巴恩斯表示羅傑在2018年12月8日逝世<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.foxnews.com/science/famous-musclebound-kangaroo-named-roger-dead-at-12-report|title=Famous musclebound kangaroo named Roger dead at 12: report|date=10 December 2018|website=The Sun|accessdate=11 December 2018}}</ref>，在聽到羅傑的死訊後，澳大利亞藝人[[娜塔莉·安博莉亚|娜塔莉·安博莉亚]]表示「羅傑是如此地驕傲與堅強，牠總是帶著笑容」，澳大利亞旅遊局則稱牠是「真正的偶像」<ref>{{cite web|url=https://wtvr.com/2018/12/10/roger-the-ripped-kangaroo-and-true-icon-has-died/|title=Roger, the ripped kangaroo and ‘true icon,’ has died|date=10 December 2018|publisher=|accessdate=10 December 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Sutton |first1=Caroline |title=Viral buff kangaroo 'Roger' has died at the age of 12 |url=http://www.kxxv.com/story/39617691/viral-buff-kangaroo-roger-has-died-at-the-age-of-12 |accessdate=11 December 2018 |work=www.kxxv.com |date=10 December 2018 |language=en}}</ref>。根據調查報告，羅傑的死因是年事已高。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bombalatimes.com.au/story/5803903/buff-boxing-kangaroo-roger-dies-of-old-age/|title=Buff boxing kangaroo Roger dies of old age|first=Christine Flatley and Stuart|last=Layt|date=10 December 2018|website=Bombala Times|accessdate=10 December 2018}}</ref>

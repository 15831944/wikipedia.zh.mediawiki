'''羅斯福島橋'''（Roosevelt Island Bridge）是[[紐約市|紐約市]]的橋樑之一，連接[[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]]的[[羅斯福島|羅斯福島]]及[[皇后區|皇后區]]的[[阿斯托里亞_(皇后區)|阿斯托里亞]]兩地，也是前往羅斯福島的主要交通方式。羅斯福島橋於1952年3月17日開始興建，耗資650萬美元，在1955年5月18日通車<ref>{{cite news |title=Welfare Island Gets Own Bridge; $6,500,000 Link With Long Island City Is Opened by Jack and Lundy |newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=May 19, 1955 |page=33 |url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F10617F6385E107B93CBA8178ED85F418585F9 |accessdate=2009-08-15}}</ref>。1973年改為現名<ref name="NYT-2001-01-14">{{cite news |last=Lippincott |first=E.E. |title=Neighborhood Report: Roosevelt Island; ... and a Recommendation to Make Its Only Bridge an Immovable Object |newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=January 14, 2001 |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2001/01/14/nyregion/neighborhood-report-roosevelt-island-recommendation-make-its-only-bridge.html |accessdate=2009-08-15}}</ref>。

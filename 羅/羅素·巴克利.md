'''羅素·巴克利'''（Russell A. Barkley，{{bd|1949年|12月17日}}）是[[臨床心理學|臨床心理師]]，{{tsl|en|Medical University of South Carolina|南卡羅來納州醫學大學}}的精神病學{{tsl|en|clinical professor|臨床教授}}，也是及許多[[注意力不足過動症|注意力不足過動症]]（ADHD）相關書籍的作者<ref>Aliyah Baruchin, [https://www.nytimes.com/ref/health/healthguide/esn-adhd-ess.html "Attention Deficits That May Linger Well Past Childhood"] {{Wayback|url=https://www.nytimes.com/ref/health/healthguide/esn-adhd-ess.html |date=20170216122847 }} ''The New York Times'', March 12, 2008.</ref><ref>[http://consults.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/02/17/a-d-h-d-to-medicate-or-not-to-medicate/ "A.D.H.D. Not Everyone Agrees"] {{Wayback|url=http://www.newsweek.com/2014/02/28/richard-saul-says-adhd-does-not-exist-not-everyone-agrees-245584.html |date=20140413142719 }}, ''Newsweek'', February 25, 2014.</ref><ref>Jessica Yadegaran, [https://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2010/12/31/AR2010123103787.html "How to manage your marriage when your spouse has ADHD"] {{Wayback|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2010/12/31/AR2010123103787.html |date=20171004150956 }}, ''The Washington Post'', January 2, 2011.</ref><ref>[[全国公共广播电台|NPR]], [https://www.npr.org/2013/04/02/176037647/a-focus-on-adults-living-with-chronic-adhd "A Focus On Adults: Living With Chronic ADHD"] {{Wayback|url=https://www.npr.org/2013/04/02/176037647/a-focus-on-adults-living-with-chronic-adhd |date=20180621143657 }}.</ref>。 羅素從1973年開始投身科學研究，並在1977年成為認證的[[心理學家|心理學家]]。他是ADHD專家，他在科學研究生涯中，長期致力於研究注意力不足過動症以及與注意力不足過動症相關的問題，例如：童年時期的對立反抗（Childhood defiance）等<ref>Frontline.

'''羅威·迪恩'''（{{lang-en|Lowell Dean}}，{{bd|1979年|1月17日}}）<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/arts/film/crime-fighting-werewolf-hits-the-big-screens/article19053898/|title=Crime-fighting werewolf hits the big screens|last=Tepper|first=Sean|work=[[The_Globe_and_Mail|The Globe and Mail]]|date=2014-06-08|accessdate=2015-04-02}}</ref>是一名[[加拿大|加拿大]]男導演、製片人、編劇、剪輯師和演員。他較著名的是2014年自編自導的恐怖喜劇片《{{le|魔狼特警|WolfCop}}》，該片於2014年6月在加拿大的{{le|辛尼布萊克娛樂公司|Cineplex Entertainment|辛尼布萊克影院}}上映<ref>{{cite web|title=WolfCop …Unique approach to film's production|first=Calvin|last=Daniel|work=Yorkton This Week|date=2014-05-28|url=http://www.yorktonthisweek.com/article/20140528/YORKTON0501/140529860/-1/YORKTON/wolfcop-8230-unique-approach-to-films-production|accessdate=2019-01-23}}</ref>；其續集《{{le|魔狼特警2|Another WolfCop}}》於2017年推出<ref>{{cite web|work=Bloody Disgusting|first=Patrick|last=Cooper|date=2015-03-26|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/interviews/3335395/interview-wolfcop-director-lowell-dean/|title=Interview: ‘WolfCop’ Director Lowell Dean|accessdate=2019-01-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/interviews/3335366/interview-wolfcop-leo-fafard/|title=Interview: ‘WolfCop’ Himself, Leo Fafard!|first=Patrick|last=Cooper|work=Bloody Disgusting|date=2015-03-09|accessdate=2019-01-23}}</ref>。

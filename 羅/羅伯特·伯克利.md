'''羅伯特·厄爾·伯克利'''（{{lang-en|Robert Earl Buckley}}，{{bd|1981年|5月2日||}}）是一位[[美國|美國]]演員。著名作品有[[全國廣播公司|NBC]]《[[口紅森林|口紅森林]]》和[[CW電視網|The CW]]《[[籃球兄弟|籃球兄弟]]》。2012年，主演了[[美國廣播公司|ABC]]《[[鬼楼契约|鬼楼契约]]》，<ref name=BuckleyMasohn>{{cite web|author= Matt Webb Mitovich|url=http://www.tvline.com/2012/03/mercedes-masohn-robert-buckley-666-park-avenue/|title=Pilot News: The Finder's Mercedes Masöhn, Tree Hill's Robert Buckley Join 666 Park Avenue|publisher={{le|TVLine|TVLine}}|date=March 8, 2012|accessdate=March 11, 2012}}</ref><ref name=MasohnBuckley>{{cite web|author= Catriona Wightman  |url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/ustv/news/a370033/the-finder-mercedes-masohn-tree-hill-star-join-666-park-avenue.html|title='The Finder' Mercedes Masohn, 'Tree Hill' star join '666 Park Avenue'|publisher=[[Digital_Spy|Digital Spy]]|date=March 8, 2012|accessdate=March 11, 2012}}</ref> 現於The CW影集《[[我是殭屍_(電視劇)|我是殭屍]]》擔任主要演員。

== 個人生活 ==
伯克利出生於[[美國|美國]][[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[洛杉磯縣|洛杉磯縣]][[克萊蒙特_(加利福尼亞州)|克萊蒙特]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.alumni.ucsd.edu/s/1170/2column-social.aspx?sid=1170&gid=1&pgid=2991|title=We Are Tritons – Robert Buckley '03|website=[[聖地牙哥加利福尼亞大學|聖地牙哥加利福尼亞大學]]|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 他在[[聖地牙哥加利福尼亞大學|聖地牙哥加利福尼亞大學]]修讀[[經濟學|經濟學]]，並在2003年畢業。<ref>{{cite web|last=Abrams|first=Sean|url=http://www.maxim.com/style/outfitted-robert-buckley|title=Outfitted: Robert Buckley|website=[[美信|美信]]|date=April 23, 2015|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 巴克利在學生時代，曾參加過電視節目《{{le|價格猜猜猜|The Price Is Right}}》的錄影。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://soaps.sheknows.com/fashion-house/news/182/fashion-house-star-robert-buckley-says-that-he-is-t|title=Fashion House Star Robert Buckley Says That He Is The "Nice Guy"|website=Soaps.com|date=September 22, 2006|accessdate=December 26, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.etonline.com/news/2009/02/70599/|title=Robert Buckley: 'Privileged' Gets a New Hunk|website={{le|今夜娛樂|Entertainment Tonight}}|date=February 24, 2009|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090327071144/http://www.etonline.com/news/2009/02/70599/|archivedate=March 27, 2009|accessdate=December 26, 2014}}</ref> 在任職經濟顧問後的一年，他搬回到洛杉磯，開始追尋他的演藝生涯。<ref>{{cite web|last=Schoels|first=Kris|url=http://www.starpulse.com/news/Kris_Schoels/2010/01/18/qaamp_a_with_robert_buckley_new_addition|title=Q&A With Robert Buckley: New Addition To 'One Tree Hill' & Economics Consultant?|website=Starpulse.com|date=January 18, 2009|accessdate=March 11, 2013}}</ref>

巴克利於2016年4月，和共同主演《{{le|404次元|Dimension 404}}》的[[麗婭·米歇爾|麗婭·米歇爾]]交往，<ref>{{cite web|last=Vulpo|first=Mike|url=http://www.eonline.com/uk/news/763840/new-couple-alert-lea-michele-is-dating-izombie-star-robert-buckley|title=New Couple Alert! Lea Michele Is Dating iZombie Star Robert Buckley|website={{le|E!|E!|E! Online}}|date=May 11, 2016|accessdate=June 9, 2016}}</ref> 但這段戀情僅維持了3個月，便在該年7月劃下句點。<ref>{{cite web|last=Webber|first=Stephanie|url=http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/lea-michele-robert-buckley-split-after-whirlwind-romance-w212351|title=Lea Michele, Robert Buckley Split After Whirlwind Romance|website={{le|我們周刊|Us Weekly}}|date=July 8, 2016|accessdate=July 8, 2016}}</ref>

==演藝生涯==
2006年，巴克利以[[MyNetworkTV|MyNetworkTV]]的肥皂劇《{{le|時尚之屋|Fashion House}}》作為他的第一部演藝作品，他在劇中飾演主要角色麥可·鮑爾。<ref>{{cite web|last=De Leon|first=Kris|url=http://www.buddytv.com/articles/fashion-house/what-are-they-up-to-fashion-ho-8207.aspx|title=What Are They Up To? Fashion House's Robert Buckley|website={{le|BuddyTV|BuddyTV}}|date=July 14, 2007|accessdate=May 7, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180317164936/http://www.buddytv.com/articles/fashion-house/what-are-they-up-to-fashion-ho-8207.aspx|archive-date=2018-03-17|dead-url=yes}}</ref> 同時，也演出了他的首部電影《{{le|殺手來電|When a Killer Calls}}》。隨後，他也加入了MyNetworkTV的肥皂劇《{{le|美國女繼承者|American Heiress}}》演員陣容。2007年，巴克利客串了[[CBS_(電視網)|CBS]]《[[靈感應|靈感應]]》，並在[[全國廣播公司|NBC]]喜劇劇情影集《[[口紅森林|口紅森林]]》中擔任常設演員。<ref>{{cite web|last=Sullivan|first=Brian Ford|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/reviews/2007/07/31/the-futons-first-look-lipstick-jungle-nbc-25093/20070731_lipstickjungle/|title=The Futon's First Look: "Lipstick Jungle" (NBC)|website=The Futon Critic|date=July 31, 2007|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref>

2008年，巴克利和{{le|海瑟·拉克理|Heather Locklear}}共同主演{{le|Lifetime|Lifetime (TV network)}}電視電影《{{le|情挑四十|Flirting with Forty}}》。<ref>{{cite web|last=Bierly|first=Mandi|url=http://www.ew.com/article/2008/12/09/flirting-with-f|title='Flirting With Forty' is my new favorite Lifetime movie|website=[[Entertainment_Weekly|Entertainment Weekly]]|date=December 9, 2008|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 隨後，也參演了喜劇恐怖電影《{{le|殺手電影|Killer Movie}}》，該電影於是年4月在[[翠貝卡電影節|翠貝卡電影節]]首映。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tribecafilm.com/filmguide/archive/512ce9101c7d76e046000ef3-killer-movie|title=Killer Movie – TFF 2008|website=[[翠貝卡電影節|翠貝卡電影節]]|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref>

2009年，巴克利客串了[[CW電視網|The CW]]《{{le|調教富家女|Privileged (TV series)}}》。<ref>{{cite web|last=Mitovich|first=Matt|url=http://www.tvguide.com/news/robert-buckley-privileged-1001239/|title=Exclusive: Jungle Boy Toy Has "Write Stuff" for Privileged|website=[[電視指南_(美國雜誌)|電視指南]]|date=January 3, 2009|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 同年6月，巴克利成為了The CW影集《[[籃球兄弟|籃球兄弟]]》的主要演員，<ref>{{cite web|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/robert-buckley-joins-tree-hill-85241|title=Robert Buckley joins 'One Tree Hill'|website=[[荷里活報道|好萊塢報導]]|date=June 10, 2009|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 自第7季開始演出至該劇最終季第9季。<ref>{{cite web|last=Ford|first=Rebecca|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/one-tree-hill-final-season-mark-schwahn-280294|title='One Tree Hill's' Final Season 'Will Be Dark,' Says Creator Mark Schwahn|website=好萊塢報導|date=January 11, 2012|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 2010年2月，巴克利有意爭取演出[[漫威影業|漫威影業]]《[[美國隊長_(電影)|美國隊長]]》的男主角[[美國隊長|史蒂夫·羅傑斯]]一角，但最終由[[克里斯·埃文斯|克里斯·伊凡]]獲得演出機會。<ref>{{cite web|last=Mason|first=Ian|url=http://www.digitalspy.com/movies/news/a204462/robert-buckley-linked-to-captain-america/|title=Robert Buckley linked to 'Captain America'|website=[[數碼間諜|數碼間諜]]|date=February 21, 2010|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref>

2012年，巴克利於[[美國廣播公司|ABC]]《[[鬼樓契約|鬼樓契約]]》中擔任主要角色，但該劇僅播出一季便遭取消。<ref>{{cite web|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/mercedes-masohn-robert-buckley-666-park-avenue-297615|title='The Finder's' Mercedes Masohn, 'One Tree Hill's' Robert Buckley Join ABC's '666 Park Ave' Pilot|website=好萊塢報導|date=March 7, 2012|accessdate=December 26, 2014}}</ref> 2013年，巴克利客串了The CW《[[南國醫戀|南國醫戀]]》；<ref>{{cite web|last=Gelman|first=Vlada|url=http://www.tvline.com/2013/08/22/hart-of-dixie-season-3-cast-robert-buckley-stranger-lemon/|title=Hart of Dixie Casts One Tree Hill's Robert Buckley – Will He Charm a Bluebell Resident?|website=TVLine|date=August 22, 2013|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 隔年，在《[[美眉校探|美眉校探]]》衍生劇《{{le|美眉校探後傳|Play It Again, Dick}}》中擔任主要演員。<ref>{{cite web|last=Holman|first=Jordyn|url=http://www.variety.com/2014/tv/news/veronica-mars-web-series-1201281409/|title='Veronica Mars': Cast, Date Announced for Web Spinoff|website=好萊塢報導|date=August 12, 2014|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 2015年，巴克利於《美眉校探》主創{{le|羅伯·湯瑪斯 (編劇)|Rob Thomas (writer)|羅伯·湯瑪斯}}新劇《[[我是殭屍_(電視劇)|我是殭屍]]》中擔任主要角色梅傑·利利懷特。<ref>{{cite web|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/one-tree-hills-robert-buckley-686924|title='One Tree Hill's' Robert Buckley to Co-Star in CW's 'iZombie'|website=好萊塢報導|date=March 7, 2014|accessdate=May 7, 2016}}</ref> 2016年，巴克利和[[麗婭·米歇爾|麗婭·米歇爾]]與[[喬爾·麥克哈爾|喬爾·麥克哈爾]]主演了[[Hulu|Hulu]][[詩選劇|詩選劇]]《{{le|404次元|Dimension 404}}》。<ref>{{cite web|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/lea-michele-robert-buckley-star-900788|title=Lea Michele, Robert Buckley to Star in Hulu Anthology 'Dimension 404'|website=好萊塢報導|date=June 8, 2016}}</ref>

'''羅斯·尤蘇波夫'''（1984年5月4日－） ，布哈拉猶太人，美国设计师和科技企业家。他是現任HQ Trivia首席創意官和前任[[首席执行官|首席执行官]]<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.crunchbase.com/person/rus-yusupov|title=Rus Yusupov - Co-founder @ Hype {{!}} Crunchbase|website=Crunchbase|language=en|access-date=2018-01-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Clark |first1=Kate |title=HQ Trivia names new CEO and teases upcoming Wheel of Fortune-style game |url=https://techcrunch.com/2018/09/18/hq-trivia-teases-upcoming-wheel-of-fortune-style-mobile-game/ |website=TechCrunch}}</ref>。

== 早期生活和教育 ==
1984年，尤蘇波夫生於[[苏联|蘇聯]][[塔吉克苏维埃社会主义共和国|塔吉克苏维埃社会主义共和国]][[杜尚别|杜尚别]]（現今[[塔吉克斯坦|塔吉克斯坦]][[杜尚别|杜尚别]]）一個布哈拉猶太人家庭。[[东欧剧变|东欧剧变]]後，全家移民[[纽约|纽约]]，尤蘇波夫在紐約成長並在[[視覺藝術學院_(紐約)|視覺藝術學院]]的[[平面设计|平面设计]]畢業。

== 事业 ==

=== 早期职业生涯 ===
尤蘇波夫在數間公司任職數碼產品設計師，包括Razorfish和R/GA，他的作品包括2007年的Hulu.com初版<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2009/11/15/arts/design/15kino.html|title=Paul H-O, Gallery Gadfly, Revives His Act|last=Kino|first=Carol|date=2009-11-11|work=The New York Times|accessdate=2018-02-27|language=en-US|issn=0362-4331}}</ref>。2008年，他創立了總部位於[[纽约|紐約]]的設計和工程工作室「大人物」。「大人物」的客戶包括[[時代雜誌|時代雜誌]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://time.com/|title=TIME Homepage|accessdate=2018-12-18|work=Time|language=en}}</ref>、[[市井词典|市井词典]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.urbandictionary.com/|title=Urban Dictionary, December 18: Lets gain this grain|accessdate=2018-12-18|work=Urban Dictionary|language=en-US}}</ref>、Jetsetter<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.jetsetter.com/|title=Trip Ideas. Destinations. Food + Drink. Hotels. Style.|accessdate=2018-12-18|author=|date=|work=Jetsetter|publisher=|language=en-US}}</ref>、[[華納媒體|時代華納]]和Fortune<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://bighuman.com/work|title=Work - Big Human|accessdate=2018-02-27|language=en}}</ref>。

=== Vine ===
2012年6月，尤蘇波夫與他人聯合創辦Vine，其乃一個循環短影片分享平台<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://allthingsd.com/20121009/twitter-buys-vine-a-video-clip-company-that-never-launched/|title=Twitter Buys Vine, a Video Clip Company That Never Launched|work=AllThingsD|accessdate=2018-01-30|language=en-us}}</ref>。Vine成立後不久的2012年10月，Vine被[[Twitter|推特]]收購，據報收購價為3000萬美元<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2013/jun/20/business/la-fi-vine-20130620|title=Video app Vine's popularity is spreading, six seconds at a time|last=Dave|first=Paresh|date=2013-06-20|work=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=2018-01-30|language=en-US|issn=0458-3035}}</ref>。2013年，Vine一度成為Apple Store的最受歡迎免費應用程式，同時為最多人使用的影片分享應用程式<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://techcrunch.com/2013/03/06/771378/|title=Vine Takes Early Command In The Mobile Video Market Over Viddy, Socialcam And Others Despite Low Adoption|last=Contributor|work=TechCrunch|accessdate=2018-02-27|language=en}}</ref>。2015年尾，Vine不再被推特重視，尤蘇波夫決定離開推特，並發帖文告誡其他人：「不要賣掉你的公司！」<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/rus/status/791681274339622913?lang=en|title=Don’t sell your company!|accessdate=2018-02-27|date=2016-10-27|last=Rus|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://techcrunch.com/2017/10/17/hq-trivia/|title=Vine’s founders are back with HQ, a live trivia game show app|last=Constine|first=Josh|work=TechCrunch|accessdate=2018-02-27|language=en}}</ref>

=== 跨媒體實驗室 ===
離開[[Twitter|推特]]後，尤蘇波夫與他人在2015年聯合創辦了應用程式工作室「跨媒體實驗室」<ref name=":2">{{Cite news|url=https://techcrunch.com/2017/10/17/hq-trivia/|title=Vine’s founders are back with HQ, a live trivia game show app|last=Constine|first=Josh|work=TechCrunch|language=en|access-date=2018-01-30}}</ref>。首個由「跨媒體實驗室」研發的應用程式為「Hype」，其為一個讓用戶能夠將各種多媒體內容共享到直播的平台。

其他由跨媒體實驗室研發的應用程式包括Bounce和HQ Trivia，前者讓用家創作混音視頻，後者則是直播答題應用程式。

=== HQ Trivia ===
被譽為「跨媒體實驗室」最成功應用程式的HQ Trivia在2017年8月推出。平均每個直播有多達70至100萬名觀眾收看<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://nypost.com/2018/01/10/inside-the-latest-millennial-obsession-hq-trivia/|title=Inside the latest millennial obsession: HQ Trivia|date=2018-01-11|work=New York Post|accessdate=2018-01-30|language=en-US}}</ref>。[[App_Store_(iOS)|App Store]]和[[Google_Play|Google Play]]均有此遊戲，其中直播騷的主要觀眾來自北美、澳洲和英國<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theverge.com/2018/1/8/16863034/hq-trivia-app-launches-uk-android-ios|title=HQ trivia app launches in the UK|work=The Verge|accessdate=2018-01-30}}</ref>。
{{TransH}}
The app takes the form of a live game show, played at 9 PM Eastern Time every day, and 3 PM Eastern Time on weekdays. The host of the show asks a series of (usually) twelve multiple choice questions, each with three possible answers. Those players who get the questions correct within the 10 second limit move on, the rest are eliminated. The players that correctly answer the final question split the prize money. This prize is currently $5,000 for most games, but it has been as high as $400,000 on one occasion.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2017/11/20/vines-creators-release-trivia-app-hq-trivia.html|title=Vine's creators want you to win free money with their new trivia app|last=Castillo|first=Michelle|date=2017-11-20|work=CNBC|accessdate=2018-02-27}}</ref>

According to a [https://www.thedailybeast.com/ceo-of-hq-the-hottest-app-going-if-you-run-this-profile-well-fire-our-host 2017 article from The Daily Beast], Yusupov threatened to fire HQ host Scott Rogowsky if the website chose to run a profile on Rogowsky's rise to fame via HQ. Yusupov later claimed the accusations were false.

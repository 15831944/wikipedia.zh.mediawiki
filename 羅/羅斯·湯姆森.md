'''羅斯·湯姆森'''（{{lang-en|Ross Thomson}}；{{bd|1987年|9月21日}}）是[[英國|英國]][[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]的一位政治人物，黨籍是[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]。自2017年開始，他是[[南阿伯丁_(英國國會選區)|南阿伯丁]]選區選出的[[英國眾議院|英國眾議院]]議員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.parliament.uk/biographies/commons/ross-thomson/4599|title=Ross Thomson MP|website=UK Parliament}}</ref>.在當選國會議員之前，他曾是[[蘇格蘭議會|蘇格蘭議會]]議員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-40260099|title=New Tory MPs quit Holyrood for Westminster|date=13 June 2017|publisher=|via=www.bbc.co.uk}}</ref>以及阿伯丁市議會議員<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.scottishconservatives.com/2013/05/candidate-chosen-for-aberdeen-donside-by-election/|title=Candidate chosen for Aberdeen Donside by-election - Scottish Conservatives|date=10 May 2013|website=Scottish Conservatives|language=en-US|access-date=6 May 2016}}</ref><ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=http://stv.tv/news/north/37501-aberdeen-city-council-election-2012-hazleheadashleyqueens-cross/|title=Aberdeen City Council Election 2012: Hazlehead/Ashley/Queen’s Cross|website=STV News|access-date=6 May 2016}}</ref>。湯姆森畢業於[[阿伯丁大學|阿伯丁大學]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://rossthomson.net/content/?page_id=160|title=About Ross {{!}} Councillor Ross Thomson: Scottish Conservatives|website=rossthomson.net|access-date=6 May 2016}}</ref>。

'''阿貝爾·馬可尼恩·特斯法耶'''（{{lang-en|Abel Makkonen Tesfaye}}，{{lang-am|አቤል ተስፋዬ}}, ''{{transl|am|ʾÄbel Täsfaye}}''，1990年2月16日－）， 較廣為人知的藝名為'''威肯'''（{{lang-en|The Weeknd}}{{noteTag|1=音同「The Weekend」。}}），是一位[[加拿大|加拿大]][[歌手|歌手]]，[[詞曲作家|詞曲作家]]和[[唱片製作人|唱片製作人]]。<ref name="sex-drugs">{{cite web|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/features/sex-drugs-and-r-b-inside-the-weeknds-dark-twisted-fantasy-20151021?page=6|title=Page 6 of Sex, Drugs and R&B: Inside The Weeknd's Dark Twisted Fantasy - Rolling Stone|author=|date=|work=Rolling Stone|accessdate=1 November 2015}}</ref>在2010年末，特斯法耶上傳一些歌曲至[[YouTube|YouTube]]並署名「威肯」。他在2011年發布三支內有九首歌的混音帶：《{{le|House of Balloons|House of Balloons}}》、《{{le|Thursday|Thursday (album)}}》、《{{le|Echoes of Silence|Echoes of Silence}}》，這些混音帶廣受好評。<ref name=happy>{{cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/music/house-of-balloons/critic-reviews |title=House of Balloons – The Weeknd |publisher=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]] |accessdate=16 March 2012}}</ref>翌年，他發布了合輯《{{le|Trilogy|Trilogy (The Weeknd album)}}》，它是由混合不同元素的三十首翻唱曲目以及三個額外的歌曲所組成。此專輯是由[[Republic_Records|Republic Records]]及{{le|XO （唱片公司）|XO (record label)|XO}}共同推出。

2013年，他推出他的首張[[錄音室專輯|錄音室專輯]]《{{le|Kiss Land|Kiss Land}}》，主打歌為〈{{le|Kiss Land|Kiss Land (song)}}〉及〈{{le|Live For|Live For}}〉。他的第二張錄音室專輯《[[Beauty_Behind_the_Madness|Beauty Behind the Madness]]》，成為他第一張登上[[告示牌200大專輯榜|告示牌200大專輯榜]]第一名的專輯。其中單曲〈{{le|Earned It|Earned It}}〉登上了[[告示牌百強單曲榜|告示牌百強單曲榜]]前五名，〈{{le|The Hills (歌曲)|The Hills (song)|The Hills}}〉、〈{{le|Can't Feel My Face|Can't Feel My Face}}〉則登上了告示牌百強單曲榜第一名。上述單曲都同時在「告示牌熱門[[節奏藍調|節奏藍調]]歌曲榜」前三名，使得他成為史上第一位囊括「告示牌熱門藍調歌曲榜」前三名的歌手。<ref name=alone>[http://music.cbc.ca/#!/blogs/2015/7/Alone-on-the-podium-The-Weeknd-becomes-first-artist-to-take-over-Billboard-RB-chart "Alone on the podium: The Weeknd becomes first artist to take over Billboard R&B chart"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160301002442/http://music.cbc.ca/ |date=2016-03-01 }}. [[CBC_Music|CBC Music]], 17 July 2015.</ref>他曾經獲得兩座[[葛萊美獎|葛萊美獎]]，以及被[[奧斯卡最佳原創歌曲獎|奧斯卡最佳原創歌曲獎]]提名。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.grammy.com/artist/the-weeknd | title=The Weeknd | publisher=The Recording Academy | accessdate=March 3, 2016}}</ref>2016年9月，威肯宣布將發行新專輯《[[Starboy_(專輯)|Starboy]]》，並釋出2支單曲：〈[[星聞人物_(歌曲)|Starboy]]〉及〈{{le|False Alarm (威肯歌曲)|False Alarm (The Weeknd song)|False Alarm}}〉。<ref name=":0" />

==早年生活==
阿貝爾·馬可尼恩·特斯法耶（Abel Makkonen Tesfaye）<ref name="sex-drugs"/>生於1990年2月16日的[[加拿大|加拿大]][[安大略|安大略]][[士嘉堡|士嘉堡]]。<ref name=allmusic>{{cite web |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/the-weeknd-mn0002674162/biography |title=The Weeknd Biography |first=Andy |last=Kellman |publisher=[[Allmusic|AllMusic.com]] |accessdate=13 October 2015 |archivedate=2015-10-13 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6cFl8uYyO?url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/the-weeknd-mn0002674162/biography |deadurl=no }}</ref>童年亦在該處成長。<ref name="jcaramanica">{{cite news |first=Jon |last=Caramanica |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/08/02/magazine/can-the-weeknd-turn-himself-into-the-biggest-pop-star-in-the-world.html?_r=1 |title=Can The Weeknd Turn Himself Into the Biggest Pop Star in the World? |work=[[The_New_York_Times_Magazine|The New York Times Magazine]] |date=27 July 2015 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6cFlDxFgo?url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/08/02/magazine/can-the-weeknd-turn-himself-into-the-biggest-pop-star-in-the-world.html?_r=0 |archivedate=2015-10-13 |deadurl=no }}</ref>他是位[[獨生子|獨生子]]<ref name="RSinterview">{{cite web|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/features/sex-drugs-and-r-b-inside-the-weeknds-dark-twisted-fantasy-20151021?page=3|title=Sex, Drugs and R&B: Inside The Weeknd's Dark Twisted Fantasy|author=Josh Eeels|work=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]]|publisher=([[Wenner_Media_LLC|Wenner Media LLC]])|date=21 October 2015|accessdate=11 November 2015}}</ref>。他擁有[[衣索比亞|衣索比亞]]血統。父母親馬可尼恩（Makkonen）及山姆拉（Samra）在1980年代從衣索比亞移民至加拿大。<ref name="jcaramanica"/><ref name="RStone">{{cite web|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/features/sex-drugs-and-r-b-inside-the-weeknds-dark-twisted-fantasy-20151021 |title=Sex, Drugs and R&B: Inside The Weeknd's Dark Twisted Fantasy |date=21 October 2015 |access-date=26 October 2015}}</ref><ref name="Mistry">{{cite web |first=Anupa |last=Mistry |url=http://www.torontostandard.com/culture/daily-disc-the-weeknds-echoes-of-silence |title=Daily Disc: The Weeknd's 'Echoes Of Silence' |publisher=Torontostandard.com |accessdate=30 December 2012}}</ref>他的母親曾經做過幾項工作包括護理，餐飲，夜校。<ref name="RSinterview"/>在特斯法耶長大的過程中，他父親就不在身邊了，他的母親時時刻刻在工作，他的祖母照料他大部分的青春歲月。因此，特斯法耶會說一口流利的[[阿姆哈拉語|阿姆哈拉語]]，這是他學會的第一種語言。<ref name="where the e went">{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/1714103/the-weeknd-reddit-ama/|title=The Weeknd Reveals How He Got His Name... And Where The ‘E’ Went|work=MTV News|publisher=MTV|accessdate=15 September 2013}}</ref>他的祖母會帶他去[[衣索比亞正教會|衣索比亞正教會]]服務。<ref name="RSinterview"/>

特斯法耶自我描述了他的青少年时期的电影“没有艾滋病的儿童”("''KIDS'' without the [[AIDS|AIDS]]")。 特斯法说他11岁开始吸食大麻，后来转向更难吃药。 特斯法声称他经常购物以补充他对摇头丸，羟考酮，阿普唑仑，可卡因，裸盖菇素和氯胺酮的使用。<ref name="jcaramanica"/><ref name="auto">{{cite web|last1=Wagstaff, Swanson|first1=Keith, David|title=10 Things You Should Know About the Weeknd|url=https://www.gq.com/story/5-things-you-should-know-about-the-weeknd|website=GQ|accessdate=13 November 2016}}</ref>

特斯法耶說，他採用了藝名The Weeknd是在17歲高中輟學之後，他與他的同儕在外翹家「一個週末（weekend）」。他更改了weekend拼寫方式，以避免與加拿大樂團{{le|The Weekend|The Weekend (Canadian band)}}有商標上的問題。<ref name="jcaramanica"/><ref name="where the e went"/>

==事業生涯==
===2010-11：事業開始及混音帶===


特斯法耶在[[多倫多|多倫多]]遇見了一位製作人傑洛米·羅斯，他有一個「黑暗節奏藍調音樂計畫」的點子名為「The Weeknd」。在羅斯試圖將想法推銷給音樂家{{le|Curtis Santiago|Curtis Santiago}}之後，羅斯為特斯法耶彈奏了一小段曲子，而特斯法耶則即興的哼出了一段[[即興饒舌|饒舌]]，在這之後，他們開始製作一張專輯。他製作了三首歌曲：〈What You Need〉、〈Loft Music〉、以及〈The Morning〉，特斯法耶唱饒舌的部分，但是最後並沒有被羅斯採用。羅斯讓特斯法耶在「這三首歌曲要記功在羅斯上」的條件下保留他製作的歌曲。<ref name="McGuire">{{cite journal|last=McGuire|first=Patrick|date=April 2012|url=http://www.vice.com/read/how-the-producer-of-the-weeknds-breakout-tracks-got-majorly-screwed|title=How the Producer of The Weeknd’s Breakout Tracks Got Majorly Screwed|journal=[[Vice_(magazine)|Vice]]|location=New York|accessdate=3 March 2013}}</ref>然而，特斯法耶在2010年12月上傳了〈What You Need〉、〈Loft Music〉、以及〈The Morning〉至[[YouTube|YouTube]]，並署名「威肯」。<ref name="nytimes">{{cite news| last = Caramanica A-Train| first = Jon| url = http://www.nytimes.com/2011/01/02/arts/music/02playlist.html?_r=4&ref=music| title = Screams That Charmed, and Other Overlooked Highlights| work = [[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]| date = 26 December 2010| accessdate = 29 March 2011}}</ref>儘管他的身份最初是不被知道的。<ref name="pforkwynreview">{{cite web| last = Fitzmaurice| first = Larry| url = http://pitchfork.com/reviews/tracks/12131-what-you-need/| title = The Playlist – The Weeknd – ''What You Need''| publisher= [[Pitchfork_Media|Pitchfork Media]]| date 2011年3月21日，特斯法耶透過他的網站免費地發布一張有九首歌曲的混音帶：《{{le|House of Balloons|House of Balloons}}》。<ref name="mtv">{{cite web| last = Ramirez| first = Erika| url = http://rapfix.mtv.com/2011/03/21/say-hello-to-the-weeknd-drake-co-signs/| title = Say Hello to The Weeknd, Drake Co-Signs| publisher = MTV| date = 21 March 2011| accessdate = 29 March 2011}}</ref>專輯由{{le|Doc McKinney|Doc McKinney}}及{{le|Illangelo|Illangelo}}出品，即使他上述的三首歌曲並沒有歸功於羅斯。<ref name="McGuire"/>《House of Balloons》獲得了一致好評，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.metacritic.com/music/house-of-balloons/the-weeknd|title=House of Balloons Reviews|publisher=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]|accessdate=3 March 2013}}</ref>它被{{le|2011年北極星音樂獎|2011 Polaris Music Prize}}提名為10位入圍提名人之一。<ref name=shortlist>{{cite web|url=http://www.polarismusicprize.ca/2011/ |title=Polaris Music Prize |author= |date= |work=Polaris Music Prize |accessdate=1 November 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140409053023/http://www.polarismusicprize.ca/2011/ |archivedate=9 四月 2014 }}</ref>

===2012-14：《Trilogy》及《Kiss Land》===

2012年4月，威肯在[[科切拉音樂節|科切拉音樂節]]表演，開始了他在[[美國|美國]]的巡迴演唱會。<ref name="tour">{{cite web |url=http://pitchfork.com/news/46157-watch-the-weeknds-entire-coachella-set/?utm_medium=site&utm_source=more-of&utm_name=news |title=Abel Tesfaye makes his U.S. debut |author=Snapes, Laura |date=16 April 2012 |publisher=Pitchfork Media |accessdate=16 March 2012}}</ref>他和他的樂隊造訪各個主要城市，<ref name="tour"/>最後在[[紐約|紐約]]結束了他的巡演。[[滾石_(雜誌)|滾石雜誌]]給予在紐約兩場票售罄的演唱會正面的評價。<ref name="Stones">{{cite journal|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/the-weeknd-mesmerizes-at-bowery-ballroom-20120429 |title=The Weeknd Mesmerizes at Bowery Ballroom |author= Krishnamurthy, Sowmya |journal=Rolling Stone |location=New York|date=29 April 2012 |accessdate=27 May 2012}}</ref>特斯法耶和他的巡迴樂團繼續在[[歐洲|歐洲]]各個主要的[[音樂節|音樂節]]巡演，包括在[[西班牙|西班牙]]和[[葡萄牙|葡萄牙]]的{{le|春天之聲音樂節|Primavera Sound Festival}}、<ref>{{cite web |url=http://prettymuchamazing.com/news/the-weeknd-announces-first-ever-tour |author=Luis Tovar |title=The Weeknd announces first ever tour |publisher=prettymuchamazing.com |accessdate=16 March 2012}}</ref>在[[英國|英國]][[倫敦|倫敦]]的{{le|無線音樂節|Wireless Festival}}、<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wirelessfestival.co.uk/line-up/ |title=Wireless line up 2012 |publisher=wirelessfestival.co.uk |accessdate=16 March 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120316180853/http://www.wirelessfestival.co.uk/line-up/ |archivedate=16 三月 2012 }}</ref>還有在[[法國|法國]][[巴黎|巴黎]]及[[比利時|比利時]][[布魯塞爾|布魯塞爾]]的演唱會。他在英國首站倫敦表演時，在觀眾面前翻唱[[麥可·傑克森|麥可·傑克森]]的歌曲〈[[Dirty_Diana|Dirty Diana]]〉，其中[[凱蒂·佩芮|凱蒂·佩芮]]及[[芙蘿倫絲·威爾希|芙蘿倫絲·威爾希]]也在臺下聆聽。<ref name="June">{{cite journal|url=http://www.nme.com/news/weekend--3/64204 |title=The Weeknd covers Michael Jackson as he plays his debut UK show |journal=[[NME|NME]] |location=London |accessdate=30 December 2012}}</ref>六月時，根據報導，「氣球三部曲」已被下載800萬次，這將在今年晚些時候正式發布。<ref name="June"/>

在2012年9月，威肯簽約[[Republic_Records|Republic Records]]，並且在其旗下創立[[唱片公司|唱片公司]]品牌{{le|XO（唱片公司）|XO (record label)|XO}}。<ref name="Hampp">{{cite journal|last=Hampp|first=Andrew|date=12 November 威肯已經從許多音樂藝術家獲得好評。資深藝人[[娃娃臉_(藝人)|娃娃臉]]正面地評價威肯：「我真的很愛威肯，我愛他的點是，不管誰與和他合作，他們都會將節奏藍調作品與其他的元素融合，這樣的感覺是很棒的。……這就意義而言是很有前途的，我認為其他默默無聞的人將會自告奮勇然後回到音樂圈成為音樂家。我認為這勝過一切，我懷念的是一位音樂家成為過程的一部分。」[[德瑞克_(歌手)|德瑞克]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Drake Says The Weeknd Will Be on 'Take Care'|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-juice/467984/drake-says-the-weeknd-will-be-on-take-care|accessdate=10 August 2011}}</ref>、[[尼克·強納斯|尼克·強納斯]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Nick Jonas Reveals Solo Album Influenced by Prince, The Weeknd, Jhene Aiko|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GeLBpP66xIA|accessdate=26 September 2014}}</ref>、[[托芙蘿|托芙蘿]]<ref>http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/6812867/tove-lo-the-weeknd-second-album</ref>都讚美他的音樂影響力。

==個人生活==

[[貝拉·哈蒂德|貝拉·哈蒂德]]在2015年初開始與威肯約會，這對情侶第一次被看到在一起是該年四月的[[科切拉音樂節|科切拉音樂節]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/style-blog/wp/2015/09/11/kylie-jenner-and-bella-hadid-are-18-their-boyfriends-are-25-why-do-their-relationships-elicit-such-different-reactions/|title=Kylie Jenner and Bella Hadid are 18. Their boyfriends are 25. Why do their relationships elicit such different reactions?|date=11 September 2015|author=Soraya Nadia McDonald|work=Washington Post|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.elle.com/culture/celebrities/a31091/bella-hadid-19th-birthday/|title=Bella Hadid Spends Her Birthday Weekend With The Weeknd|author=Sarah Lindig|work=ELLE|accessdate=17 October 2015}}</ref>哈蒂德在2015年12月主演他的歌曲〈{{le|In the Night|In the Night (The Weeknd song)}}〉之[[音樂錄影帶|音樂錄影帶]]。在2016年2月的葛萊美頒獎典禮上，情侶倆在紅地毯上亮相。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.popsugar.com/celebrity/Weeknd-Bella-Hadid-Grammys-2016-40199219#photo-40199219|title=The Weeknd and Bella Hadid Made Their Red Carpet Debut at the Grammys|last=Sisavat|first=Monica|website=POPSUGAR Celebrity|language=en-US|access-date=2016-06-06}}</ref>2016年11月，有報導聲稱貝拉和威肯分手，但也有消息表示他們依然戀愛中，只是行程有過多的衝突。<ref>{{cite web|last1=TRACY|first1=BRIANNE|title=The Weeknd and Bella Hadid Have Split: 'They Still Have a Great Deal of Love for One Another'|url=http://people.com/music/the-weeknd-bella-hadid-split/|website=People|publisher=Time INC.|date=2016-11-10|accessdate=2017-04-09|language=en}}</ref>2016年年底，威肯被狗仔拍到和[[席琳娜·戈梅茲|席琳娜·戈梅茲]]兩人在餐廳外熊抱擁吻，公開戀情，而貝拉也取消對席琳娜在[[Instagram|Instagram]]的追蹤。2017年年底，威肯和席琳娜·戈梅茲分手，而威肯也取消對席琳娜在[[Instagram|Instagram]]的追蹤。

他在社交媒體上，例如[[Twitter|Twitter]]，將他的名字後方加上「xo」作為字尾。<ref name="Hoby"/>根據Hermione Hoby的敘述，它的意思是{{le|擁抱與親吻|Hugs and kisses|一個親吻與一個擁抱}}的表情符號，<ref name="Hoby">{{cite news|last=Hoby|first=Hermione|date=8 November 2012|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/music/2012/nov/08/the-weeknd-sense-and-sensibility|title=The Weeknd: Sounds and sensibility|newspaper=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|location=London|at=section G2, p. 12|accessdate=6 January 2013}}</ref>[[VH1|VH1]]的Zara Golden說，它用來代替他有使用消遣性毒品[[搖頭丸|搖頭丸]]及{{le|羥考酮|Oxycodone}}的習慣。<ref>{{cite web|last=Golden|first=Zara|date=16 November 2012|url=http://www.vh1.com/music/tuner/2012-11-16/who-is-the-weeknd-5-things-you-should-know|title=Who Is The Weeknd? 5 Things You Should Know|publisher=[[VH1|VH1]]|accessdate=16 August 2013}}</ref>

威肯是[[HBO|HBO]]熱播劇《[[权力的游戏_(电视剧)|冰與火之歌：權力遊戲]]》的粉絲。<ref name="where the e went"/>

===法律問題===

2015年1月，威肯由於毆打拉斯維加斯警察而被逮捕。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Stutz|first1=Colin|title=The Weeknd Arrested For Punching Las Vegas Police Officer|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-juice/6436581/the-weeknd-arrested-for-punching-las-vegas-police-officer|website=Billboard|accessdate=2016-08-08}}</ref>他並沒有為他的行為辯護，並被判處50小時的社區服務。<ref>{{cite web|title=THE WEEKND Pleads No Contest TO PUNCHING A COP|url=http://www.tmz.com/2015/10/22/the-weeknd-no-contest-punching-cop/|website=TMZ|accessdate=2016-08-08}}</ref>

===慈善事業===

2014年榮獲比基拉專業卓越獎時，他決定向[[多倫多大學|多倫多大學]]的伊索比亞傳統語言[[吉茲語|吉茲語]]課程捐贈50,000美元。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/the-weeknd-donates-50000-to-the-university-of-toronto-to-help-start-an-ethiopic-studies-program-a7177606.html|title=The Weeknd donates $ 50, 000 to language class at the University of Toronto|last=Carissimo|first=Justin|date=7 August 2016|website=[[Independent.co.uk|Independent.co.uk]]|accessdate=7 August 2016}}</ref>2015年，他與[[瑞安·西克雷斯特|瑞安·西克雷斯特]]的基金會合作，到[[亞特蘭大|亞特蘭大]]的兒童醫院探望。<ref>{{cite web|title=The Weeknd Does Charity Work Amidst Cheating Rumors + Do-Gooder 2 Chainz Donates Minivan To Family With Brain Damaged Son|author1=Natasha|url=http://theybf.com/2015/12/17/the-weeknd-does-charity-work-amidst-cheating-rumors-do-gooder-2-chainz-donates-minivan-to|website=Young, Black & Fabulous|accessdate=2016-10-31}}</ref>2016年，他捐贈250,000美元予「[[黑人的命也是命|黑人的命也是命]]」組織。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Lockett|first1=Dee|title=The Weeknd Donates $250,000 to the Black Lives Matter Movement|url=http://www.vulture.com/2016/08/weeknd-donates-250k-to-black-lives-matter.html|website=Vulture|publisher=New York Media LLC|date=2016-08-10|accessdate=2017-04-09|language=en}}</ref>

'''威氏蘇鐵屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Williamsonia'''''}}），又名'''威爾遜蘇鐵屬'''、'''威廉姆遜花屬'''，是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[本內蘇鐵目|本內蘇鐵目]]下的一[[屬|屬]]，和現今的[[蘇鐵目|蘇鐵]]類有相似之處。其下的[[物種|物種]]生存於早[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]至晚[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]。它們生長在熱帶的[[樹|樹]][[蕨類|蕨]][[森林|森林]]中。其[[化石|化石]]分布於全世界。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author={{link-en|西里爾·沃克 (古生物學家)|Cyril Walker (palaeontologist)|Walker, Cyril}} | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=300 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref><ref name="pl">{{cite book | title=Prehistoric Life: The Definitive Visual History of Life on Earth | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Palmer, Douglas| year=2009 | location=New York | isbn=978-0-7566-5573-0 | page=230 | edition=1st American|display-authors=etal}}</ref><ref name="firefly">{{cite book | title=Firefly Guide to Fossils | publisher=Firefly Books | year=2003 | isbn=1-55297-812-5 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=vz0xOhakQiQC&pg=PA174&hl=en&sa=X&ei=89wyT8W-KsLX0QGI4InsBw&ved=0CEwQ6AEwAzgK#v=onepage&q&f=false | accessdate=February 8, 2012 | edition=illustrated | page=174}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
威氏蘇鐵的外觀很像[[灌木|灌木]]或小樹，典型的植株高度約為3[[公尺|公尺]]。具有堅實的[[莖|莖]]，莖皮為[[鑽石|鑽石]]形。[[葉|葉]]為[[棕櫚|棕櫚]]葉狀。<ref name="Fossils" />

== 物種<ref name="Fossilworks" /> ==
* ''Williamsonia gigas'' <small>Carruthers 1870</small>
* ''Williamsonia sewardiana'' <small>Sahni 1932</small>

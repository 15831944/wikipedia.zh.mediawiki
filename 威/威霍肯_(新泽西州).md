'''威霍肯''' 是[[美国|美国]][[新泽西州|新泽西州]][[哈德遜縣|哈德逊郡]]的一个城市。 根据[[2010年美國人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]，威霍肯拥有人口12,554人。 

== 名称 ==
威霍肯这个名字一般被认为来自于勒纳佩[[阿尔冈昆语族|阿尔冈昆语族]]。 它可以被被解释为"玉米地"，"海鸥之地","像树的岩石"或"结束"。<ref>[http://www.history.navy.mil/danfs/w4/weehawken-i.htm ''Weehawken''], [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dictionary_of_American_Naval_Fighting_Ships Dictionary of American Naval Fighting Ships]. Accessed June 13, 2007. "A township in Hudson County, N.J., seven miles northeast of Jersy{{sic}} City. The name was originally an Algonquin Indian term and later changed by folk-usage to a pseudo-Dutch form. Its exact meaning is unclear, but variously translated as ''place of gulls,'' ''rocks that look like trees,'' ''maize land,'' ''at the end'' (of the Palisades) and ''field lying along the Hudson.''"</ref><ref>Hutchinson, Viola L. [http://mapmaker.rutgers.edu/356/nj_place_names_origin.pdf#page=32 ''The Origin of New Jersey Place Names''], New Jersey Public Library Commission, May 1945. Accessed October 23, 2015.</ref><ref>[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_Gannett Gannett, Henry]. [https://books.google.com/books?id=9V1IAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA31 ''The Origin of Certain Place Names in the United States''], p. 31. [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Government_Printing_Office United States Government Printing Office], 1905. Accessed October 23, 2015.</ref>

== 历史 ==
威霍肯于1859年3月15日由[[新泽西州议会|新泽西州议会]]成立为市。在1874年一部分被割让给[[霍博肯_(新泽西州)|霍博肯]]。 1879年从西霍博肯吞并了一部分地区。<ref name="Story">Snyder, John P. [http://www.state.nj.us/dep/njgs/enviroed/oldpubs/bulletin67.pdf ''The Story of New Jersey's Civil Boundaries: 1606–1968''], Bureau of Geology and Topography; Trenton, New Jersey; 1969. p. 148. Accessed October 25, 2012.</ref>

== 地理 ==
威霍肯是[[紐約都會區|纽约都会区]]的一部分。 坐落在[[哈德遜河|哈德逊河]]的西岸，与[[曼哈頓|曼哈顿]][[中城|中城]]隔水相望,是[[林肯隧道|林肯隧道]]西端的所在地。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Hudson County New Jersey Street Map|publisher=[[Hagstrom_Map|Hagstrom Map Company, Inc.]]|year=2008|isbn=0-88097-763-9}}</ref> 

根据 [[美国人口调查局|美国人口调查局]]，该市总面积为1.477平方英里(3.826 公里<sup>2</sup>)，包括0.796平方英里(2.063 公里<sup>2</sup>)土地和0.681平方英里(1.764 公里<sup>2</sup>)水面(46.10%).

威霍肯的人口密度大约相当于泽西市，是美国人口密度最高的城市之一。 <ref>Cullen, Deanna. [http://hudsonreporter.com/view/full_story/11366274/article-A-19th-Century-ode-to-Weehawken-Census-shows-town-today-far-from-'hour-of-infancy'-?instance=lead_story_left_column "A 19th Century ode to Weehawken; Census shows town today far from 'hour of infancy'"], ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hudson_Reporter The Hudson Reporter]'', February 13, 2011. Accessed January 14, 2012. "Still, the town's urban population density – 13,948 per square mile – is among the highest in the United States, comparable with that of Jersey City."</ref>

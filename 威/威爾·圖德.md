'''威爾·圖德'''（{{lang-en|'''William James Sibree Tudor'''}}<ref name="fwpl">{{cite web|title=Will Tudor|website=Filmweb.pl|accessdate=27 July 2014|url=http://www.filmweb.pl/person/Will+Tudor-1856649|language=pl}}</ref>，{{bd|1987年|4月11日}}）是[[英國|英國]]的一位演員。他最出名的是他在[[HBO|HBO]]系列遊戲《[[冰與火之歌：權力遊戲|冰與火之歌：權力遊戲]]》中擔任Olyvar的角色，從[[冰與火之歌：權力遊戲_(第三季)|第3季]]到[[冰與火之歌：權力遊戲_(第五季)|第5季]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wetpaint.com/2014-07-21-season-5-spoilers-another-intimate/|title=Game of Thrones Season 5 Spoilers: Another "Intimate" Scene for Loras – But Who With?|website=[[Wetpaint|Wetpaint]]|date=21 July 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Game of Thrones star Will Tudor: "How amazing it must be to be a student at Leicester at the moment"|url=http://www2.le.ac.uk/offices/press/features/features-2016/game-of-thrones-star-will-tudor|website=le.ac.uk|accessdate=30 September 2016}}</ref>。他還出演電視迷你劇《{{link-en|紅色帳篷|The Red Tent}}》<ref>{{cite web|last=Hawkins|first=Kayla|title=Who Plays Joseph in 'The Red Tent'? Will Tudor Will Look Familiar to HBO Fans|url=https://www.bustle.com/articles/52736-who-plays-joseph-in-the-red-tent-will-tudor-will-look-familiar-to-hbo-fans|website=Bustle|accessdate=30 September 2016}}</ref>和[[Channel_4|Channel 4]]/[[AMC_(TV_channel)|AMC]]電視劇《{{link-en|Humans|Humans (TV series)}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.co.uk/tv/news/a620653/humans-channel-4-and-amcs-sci-fi-drama-releases-its-first-image.html?rss|title=Humans: Channel 4 and AMC's sci-fi drama releases its first image|website=[[Digital_Spy|Digital Spy]]|date=10 January 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title='Humans' Star On Life In The Biggest TV Show Of The Year|url=http://www.esquire.co.uk/culture/film/news/a8631/humans-actor-will-tudor-interview/|website=Esquire|accessdate=30 September 2016|date=29 July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Will Tudor on Being Human|url=http://www.theprotagonistmagazine.com/stage-screen-1/will-tudor-on-being-human|website=The Protagonist|accessdate=30 September 2016}}</ref> 。他在[[Freeform|Freeform]]的系列影集《[[闇影獵人_(電視劇)|闇影獵人]]》飾演賽巴斯汀·維拉克/喬納森·摩根斯坦<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://variety.com/2017/tv/news/shadowhunters-will-tudor-game-of-thrones-mortal-instruments-1201970996/|title=‘Shadowhunters’ Casts ‘Game of Thrones’ Actor Will Tudor as Sebastian|last=Levy|first=Dani|date=2017-01-26|work=Variety|access-date=2017-05-08|language=en-US}}</ref>。

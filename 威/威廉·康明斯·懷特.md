'''威廉·康明斯·懷特'''（{{lang-en|William Comings White}}；{{bd|1890年||1965年}}）是一位[[電機工程學|電機工程師]]。<ref>{{cite book |title=The American Engineer |url=https://books.google.com/?id=RNImAAAAMAAJ |accessdate=2008-01-21 |year=1967 |publisher=National Society of Professional Engineers |pages=18}}</ref>他是[[諾貝爾化學獎|諾貝爾化學獎]]得主[[歐文·朗繆爾|歐文·朗繆爾]]的親戚<ref>{{cite book |last=Langmuir |first=Irving |authorlink=Irving Langmuir |title=The Collected Works of Irving Langmuir |url=https://books.google.com/?id=bDgYAAAAIAAJ |accessdate=2008-01-21 |year=1960 |publisher=Pergamon Press |pages=101 }}</ref>，也是朗繆爾在[[通用電器|通用電器]]研發實驗室的研究助理。<ref>{{cite book |last=Birr |first=Kendall |title=Pioneering in Industrial Research: The Story of the General Electric Research Laboratory |url=https://books.google.com/?id=CT9L-aH3GpoC |accessdate=2008-01-21 |year=1957 |publisher=Public Affairs Press |pages=52}}</ref>懷特生於[[布魯克林|布魯克林]]，大部分的人生都在[[斯克內克塔迪_(紐約州)|斯克內克塔迪]]度過，住在{{link-en|通用電器住宅規劃區|General Electric Realty Plot}}羅威爾路（Lowell Road）上的屋子裡。<ref>{{Cite book|title = Who's who in Engineering|url = https://books.google.com/books?id=nD4MAQAAIAAJ|publisher = John W. Leonard Corporation|date = 1922-01-01|language = en|first = John William|last = Leonard|first2 = Winfield Scott|last2 = Downs|first3 = M. M.|last3 = Lewis}}</ref>

懷特的工作內容主要是協助開發品名為Kenotron與Pliotron的二極管與三極管，這些[[真空管|真空管]]會在極端的真空狀態下運作。<ref>{{cite book |title=Vacuum Tubes in Wireless Communication |last=Bucher |first=Elmer E. |year=1919 }}</ref>

懷特於1948年獲[[哥倫比亞大學|哥倫比亞大學]]頒發[[名譽學位|名譽學位]]。<ref>{{cite news |title=COLUMBIA DEGREES ARE GIVEN TO |url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F40C11F63F5F167B93C0A9178DD85F4C8485F9 |work=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |date=1948-06-02 |accessdate=2008-01-21 }}</ref>

懷特的論文都儲藏在{{link-en|聯合學院|Union College}}的霞飛圖書館特別館藏部（Schaffer Library Special Collections Department）。<ref>{{Cite web|title = William Comings White Papers, ca. 1924-1955 {{!}} Lemelson Center for the Study of Invention and Innovation|url = http://www.invention.si.edu/william-comings-white-papers-ca-1924-1955|website = www.invention.si.edu|accessdate = 2015-10-04}}</ref>

'''威海卫旗帜'''是指[[英租威海卫|英租威海卫]]的旗帜。从1903年到1930年，旗帜主要由英国的[[蓝船旗|蓝船旗]]和两支[[鸳鸯|鸳鸯]]组成。

1898年，依据[[订租威海卫专条|订租威海卫专条]]，大清将威海卫租借予英国，租期25年。在最初的几年，驻守[[刘公岛|刘公岛]]大英帝国专员使用[[联合杰克|联合杰克]]和[[中国|中国]][[龙|龙]]为背景的旗帜，没有任何特殊标志作为威海卫标志。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://flagspot.net/flags/cn_gbcol.html |title=Foreign colonies in China |publisher=Flags of the World |date= |accessdate=2017-04-25}}</ref>

1902年，[[威海卫|威海卫]]专员[[骆克|骆克]]致信给[[殖民地部|殖民地部]]，认为使用一种中国标志作为英国海外领土旗帜并不合适，请求换成[[鸳鸯|鸳鸯]]作为本旗帜的标志。<ref name=c />


信件[[英语|英语]]原文如下：
{{cquote|The design of the flag hitherto used by the Commissioner of this Dependency is a dragon on the Union Jack and is in my opinion quite unsuitable. I have therefore to request that the Crown Agents may be instructed to have made for the use of the Commissioner two new flags, the device of the Mandarin Duck being substituted for the Dragon, which is as you are aware the national emblem of China and not appropriate in the case of a British Dependency.<ref name=c>{{cite web|url=https://www.noexperiencenecessarybook.com/X2pGW/the-colours-of-the-fleet-the-flag-institute.html |title=The Colours of the Fleet |publisher=The Flag Institute |accessdate=2017-04-25}}</ref>}}

1903年[[爱德华七世|爱德华七世]]回信，批准[[骆克|骆克]]的建议，将专员旗帜和[[蓝船旗|蓝船旗]]都改变设计。他还同意[[商船旗|商船旗]]也可以使用。<ref name=french>{{cite web|first=Paul |last=French |authorlink=Paul French (author) |url=http://www.chinarhyming.com/2009/04/30/flags-of-british-weihaiwei/ |title=Flags of British Weihaiwei |publisher=China Rhyming |date=2009-04-30 |accessdate=2017-04-25}}</ref>1930年当租期到期后，[[大英帝国|大英帝国]]和[[中华民国_(大陆时期)|中华民国]]的多轮谈判之后，中国收回除刘公岛外的威海卫，置威海卫行政区，直属国民政府行政院。'''威海卫旗帜'''最终在移交仪式上被降下，不再是[[英租威海卫|英租威海卫]]的旗帜。<ref>{{cite web|first=Teresa |last=Poole |url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/perfect-goodbye-hong-kong-dreams-of-gun-salutes-and-grateful-thanks-the-perfect-goodbye-1356485.html |title=perfect goodbye Hong Kong dreams of Gun salutes and grateful thanks . . . the perfect goodbye |publisher=The Independent |date= |accessdate=2017-04-25}}</ref>

[[威爾斯|威爾斯]]是一個新興的旅遊目的地。2002年，有8,078,900名遊客到訪威爾斯的國民信託<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.wales.gov.uk/keypubstatisticsforwales/content/publication/compendia/2003/dws2003/dws2003-ch10/dws2003-t10-36.xls |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060806034403/http://www.wales.gov.uk/keypubstatisticsforwales/content/publication/compendia/2003/dws2003/dws2003-ch10/dws2003-t10-36.xls |archivedate=2006年8月6日 |title=Visitors to tourist attractions |publisher=[[Welsh_Assembly_Government|Welsh Assembly Government]] |accessdate=25 April 2011 |format=[[Microsoft_Excel|Microsoft Excel]] spreadsheet |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。威爾斯的旅遊業產值達到35億英鎊<ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-11351080 Investment in tourism 'declines'] BBC News Wales, 19 September 2010</ref>。2005年，旅遊業為威爾斯提供了超過10萬個就業崗位，佔威爾斯就業人口的超過8%。威爾斯主要的外國遊客來自愛爾蘭共和國、美國和德國，但大多數遊客還是英國國內遊客為主<ref>[http://new.wales.gov.uk/topics/tourism/research1/fast-facts/?lang=en Welsh Assembly Government Fast Facts]{{dead link|date=November 2010}}</ref>。卡迪夫是威爾斯最受歡迎的旅遊目的地<ref>Abby Alford {{cite web | url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/cardiffonline/cardiff-news/2010/10/27/report-highlights-booming-tourist-trade-in-cardiff-91466-27548342/ | title=Report highlights booming tourist trade in Cardiff | accessdate=27 October 2010}}</ref>。

'''威廉氏症候群'''（{{lang-en|Williams–Beuren syndrome, WBS}}），也称为'''鸡尾酒会综合征'''，是一種罕見的遺傳疾患，患者神經發育異常<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://ghr.nlm.nih.gov/condition/williams-syndrome|title=Williams syndrome|accessdate=2017-01-22|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref>，行為舉止異常興奮，語言能力相对一般人好，且不怕陌生人，個性外向<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":0" />。大部分病人都有典型的輕度到中度智能障礙<ref name=":0" />。

患者有着像妖精、[[矮人|矮人]]的特殊面部特徵<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1">{{Cite journal|title=Research Review: Williams syndrome: a critical review of the cognitive, behavioral, and neuroanatomical phenotype|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/18489677|last=Martens|first=Marilee A.|last2=Wilson|first2=Sarah J.|date=2008-06-01|journal=Journal of Child Psychology and Psychiatry, and Allied Disciplines|issue=6|doi=10.1111/j.1469-7610.2008.01887.x|volume=49|pages=576–608|issn=1469-7610|pmid=18489677|last3=Reutens|first3=David C.}}</ref>，且臉部特徵隨年齡增長而更加明顯。

威廉氏症候群是一種遺傳疾患，尤其是因為7號染色體的長臂中27个基因被刪除而發生，此狀況通常是卵子或精子在形成人時的發生的偶發事件；在少數病例中，威廉氏症候群是遺傳自顯性患病的父母；患者的特殊面部特徵常被認為是缺失了特定的基因，通常確診是基於症狀引起懷疑，並透過基因檢測以確認患病。

治療方式包含特殊教育及各種形式的療法，手術僅被用於治療心臟疾病，當患者有高血鈣時才需要改變飲食或施以藥物治療<ref>{{Cite book|url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK1249/|title=GeneReviews(®)|last=Morris|first=Colleen A.|date=1993-01-01|publisher=University of Washington, Seattle|editor-last=Pagon|editor-first=Roberta A.|location=Seattle (WA)|chapter=Williams Syndrome|pmid=20301427}}</ref>。威廉氏症候群的症狀首見於出現紐西蘭心臟學家之口，平均在7500到20000位新生兒裡會出現一位患者，患者的預期壽命較一般人為短，是因為心血管疾病的風險增加之故。

威廉氏症候群最早於1961年由[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]]心臟學家J.C.P. Williams發現<ref>{{Cite journal|title=John C. P. Williams of Williams-Beuren syndrome|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/21107555|last=Lenhoff|first=Howard M.|last2=Teele|first2=Rita L.|date=2011-02-01|journal=Pediatric Radiology|issue=2|doi=10.1007/s00247-010-1909-y|volume=41|pages=267–269|issn=1432-1998|pmid=21107555|last3=Clarkson|first3=Patricia M.|last4=Berdon|first4=Walter E.}}</ref><ref name="dobbs">{{cite news |first=David |last=Dobbs |authorlink= |coauthors= |title=The Gregarious Brain. |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2007/07/08/magazine/08sociability-t.html |quote=If a person suffers the small genetic accident that creates Williams syndrome, he’ll live with not only some fairly conventional cognitive deficits, like trouble with space and numbers, but also a strange set of traits that researchers call the Williams social phenotype or, less formally, the “Williams personality”: a love of company and conversation combined, often awkwardly, with a poor understanding of social dynamics and a lack of social inhibition. |publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |date=July 8, 2007 |accessdate=2007-09-25 }}</ref>，因為[[7號染色體|7號染色體]]長臂約26個基因有所[[刪除_(遺傳學)|缺失]]，發生率介於1/7500與1/20000之間<ref name="Martens">{{cite journal |journal= J Child Psychol Psychiatry |year=2008 |volume=49 |issue=6 |pages=576–608 |title= Research Review: Williams syndrome: a critical review of the cognitive, behavioral, and neuroanatomical phenotype |author= Martens MA, Wilson SJ, Reutens DC |doi=10.1111/j.1469-7610.2008.01887.x |pmid=18489677}}</ref>。

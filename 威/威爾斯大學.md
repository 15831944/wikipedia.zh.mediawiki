'''威爾斯大學'''（[[英語|英語]]：'''University of Wales'''，[[威爾斯語|威爾斯語]]：'''Prifysgol Cymru'''）於1893年創立，是英國大學學位頒發機構之一，2011年廢校，現其功能基本已被[[威爾士三一聖大衛大學|威爾士三一聖大衛大學]]取代。威爾士大學本為類似於[[倫敦大學|倫敦大學]]的聯邦制大學，于1893年根据英国皇家宪章成立，由班戈、卡迪夫和阿伯里斯特威斯三個分校組成。隨著其不斷發展，威爾士的絕大多數大學一度都位於其旗下。

==醜聞與合併==

2010年該大學爆出學術造假醜聞。2011年英國高等教育质量保证署（Quality Assurance Agency for Higher Education）調查發現該大學對旗下組織提供的課程沒有進行過嚴格審查<ref>{{cite news|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-wales-13862552|title=Leighton Andrews: University of Wales 'let Wales down'|date=22 June 2011}}</ref> 。隨後其在倫敦的分校又爆出簽證造價醜聞<ref>{{cite news|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-wales-15157119|title=University of Wales to stop validating other degrees|date=3 October 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-wales-15171830|title=University of Wales degree and visa scam exposed by BBC|date=5 October 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|newspaper=Daily Telegraph|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/education/educationnews/8843200/University-of-Wales-abolished-after-visa-scandal.html|title=University of Wales abolished after visa scandal|author=Julie Henry|date=22 October 2011}}</ref>。[[阿伯里斯特威斯大學|阿伯里斯特威斯大學]]、[[班戈大學|班戈大學]]、[[卡迪夫大學|卡迪夫大學]]、[[斯旺西大學|斯旺西大學]]和[[格拉摩根大学|格拉摩根大学]]（現為[[南威爾士大學|南威爾士大學]]的一部分）等大學的校長對其表示不滿。<ref>{{cite news|work=Wales Online|url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/news/wales-news/bogus-degree-scandal-prompts-calls-1804048|title=Bogus degree scandal prompts calls to wind up University of Wales|date=6 October 2011}}</ref>

2011年10月該大學宣佈正式廢校，併入[[威爾士三一聖大衛大學|威爾士三一聖大衛大學]]<ref name=autogenerated2>[https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15410424 University of Wales effectively abolished in merger] - BBC News, 21 October 2011</ref><ref name=autogenerated3>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15157119 |title=University of Wales to stop validating other degrees - BBC News |publisher=Bbc.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2016-07-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Jenkins |first=Ciaran |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15171830 |title=University of Wales degree and visa scam exposed by BBC - BBC News |publisher=Bbc.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2016-07-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15191954 |title=Scrap University of Wales call by vice-chancellors - BBC News |publisher=Bbc.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2016-07-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Warning not to strip University of Wales assets|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-15689167|accessdate=17 November 2011|newspaper=BBC News|date=11 November 2011}}</ref>，但到2018年為止合併仍未完成<ref>{{cite news|title=Academic brands Wales' university mergers 'disgraceful' |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-south-east-wales-38536974|work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|date=6 January 2017|accessdate=17 June 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wales.ac.uk/Resources/Documents/Merger/Merger-FAQs-January2018.pdf|date=January 2018|accessdate=11 April 2018|publisher=University of Wales|title=University of Wales Merger – Frequently Asked Questions}}</ref>。

'''威斯康辛州蝙蝠人'''（英语：Wisconsin Man-Bat）是指2006年[[美国|美国]][[威斯康辛州|威斯康辛州]]的一次[[神秘生物列表|神秘生物]]目击事件。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://themothman.wikia.com/wiki/Awohali's_Flying_Humanoid_Sighting|title=Awohali's Flying Humanoid Sighting|newspaper=TheMothMan Wikia|accessdate=2018-06-20|language=en}}</ref>2007年1月3日，《Coulee News》一篇标题为“Man Bat Tale Tops 2006 for Weirdness”的新闻报道两名目击者在威斯康辛州目击一名“蝙蝠人”。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://cryptomundo.com/cryptozoo-news/manbat-06/|title=Weird Wisconsin:  Man Bat|last=Cryptids|first=Cryptomundo,|date=2007-01-04|newspaper=Cryptomundo|accessdate=2018-06-20|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://lacrossetribune.com/couleecourier/opinion/random-eruptions-man-bat-tale-tops-for-weirdness/article_3b07f852-2d04-5101-b593-36e2c67f8724.html|title=RANDOM ERUPTIONS: Man Bat tale tops 2006 for weirdness|last=Editor|first=RANDY ERICKSON /|newspaper=La Crosse Tribune|accessdate=2018-06-20|language=en}}</ref>2006年9月26日晚9:30左右，53岁的目击者沃哈利（Wohali）{{NoteTag|Wohali是一名[[切罗基人|切罗基人]]|name="not only"}}与25岁的儿子在威斯康辛州{{link-en|拉克罗斯县|La Crosse County, Wisconsin}}霍尔门（Holmen）布里格斯路（Briggs Road）遭遇一只高6-7英尺（1.8-2.1米）、翼展10-12英尺（3-3.7米）、肋骨突出、皮肤呈灰色的[[蝙蝠|蝙蝠]]状人形神秘生物。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.pinebarrensinstitute.com/post/150399902818/cryptid-profile-the-wisconsin-man-bat-wisconsin|title=Cryptid Profile: The Wisconsin Man-Bat Wisconsin,... - The Pine Barrens Institute|accessdate=2018-06-20|work=The Pine Barrens Institute|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180621070233/http://www.pinebarrensinstitute.com/post/150399902818/cryptid-profile-the-wisconsin-man-bat-wisconsin|archive-date=2018-06-21|dead-url=yes}}</ref>在这次目击后，两名目击者因反胃而呕吐，并且出现生病状况。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.phantomsandmonsters.com/2011/04/lacrosse-wisconsin-man-bat.html|title=The LaCrosse, Wisconsin Man-Bat|accessdate=2018-06-20|work=www.phantomsandmonsters.com}}</ref>在目击发生18天后，作家Linda S. Godfrey前往目击地点调查，发现一只失去后肢与肠子的鹿的遗骸。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://jesswitkins.wordpress.com/2011/05/13/paranormal-wisconsin-the-bat-man-of-briggs-road/|title=Paranormal Wisconsin: The Bat Man of Briggs Road|date=2011-05-14|newspaper=Jess Witkins' Happiness Project|accessdate=2018-06-20|language=en-US}}</ref>

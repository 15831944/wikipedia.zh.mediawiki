'''納華薩甸·薛迪奇'''（{{lang-en|'''Nawazuddin Siddiqui'''}}，{{bd|1974年|5月19日|catIdx=Siddiqui}}）是[[印度|印度]]男演員，主要作品為[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]電影。

薛迪奇較知名的電影有《[[美味情書|美味情書]]》（2013）和《[[小萝莉的猴神大叔|小萝莉的猴神大叔]]》（2015）等等。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Nawazuddin-Siddiqui-open-to-doing-slap-stick-comedy/articleshow/52398165.cms “Nawazuddin Siddiqui open to doing slap-stick comedy”]. The Times of India. 28-01-2017.</ref><ref>[https://www.huffingtonpost.in/2015/07/20/chand-nawab_n_7832174.html “Meet The Real Chand Nawab From 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' -- TV Reporter Who Just Wants To Finish His Damn Piece To Camera”]. Huffington Post India. 20-07-2015.</ref>他曾經因出色的表现而得到[[印度國家電影獎|印度國家電影獎特別評審獎]]及一個[[印度電影觀眾獎|印度電影觀眾獎]]的最佳男配角殊榮。<ref>[http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/before-nawazuddin-siddiqui-became-faisal-khan-of-gangs-of-wasseypur-here-are-10-films-you-might-have-missed-him-4623794/ “Before Nawazuddin Siddiqui became Faisal Khan of Gangs Of Wasseypur, here are 10 films you might have missed him”]. The Indian Express. 22-04-2017.</ref>

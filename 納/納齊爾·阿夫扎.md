'''納齊爾·阿夫扎'''，[[OBE|O.B.E.]]（{{lang-en|Nazir Afzal}}，{{bd|1962年|catIdx=A}}）<ref>[https://www.thesun.co.uk/news/3528212/nazir-afzal-former-cps-prosecutor-rochdale-sex-gang/ “Who is Nazir Afzal? Former CPS top prosecutor who brought prosecutions against the Rochdale grooming gang”]. The Sun. 03-06-2017.</ref>是一名[[英国|英国]][[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]裔律師和檢察官。他的父親是一名來自巴基斯坦的移民。阿夫扎致力於促進女性權利，反對[[强迫婚姻|强迫婚姻]]、[[残割女性生殖器|残割女性生殖器]]和[[名譽殺人|名譽殺人]]。<ref name=nytimes>{{cite web |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2013/09/28/world/europe/a-muslim-prosecutor-in-britain-fighting-forced-marriages-and-honor-crimes.html?pagewanted=all&_r=0 |title=A Muslim Prosecutor in Britain, Fighting Forced Marriages and Honor Crimes |accessdate=30 September 2013}}</ref> 

2011年—2015年間，他是[[英国皇家检控署|英国皇家检控署]][[西北英格蘭|西北英格蘭]]區的檢察總長，是检控署歷史上第一位[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]檢察總長。<ref>{{cite web|title=Nazir Afzal OBE appointed new chief executive - The Association of Police and Crime Commissioners|url=http://www.apccs.police.uk/latest_news/nazir-afzal/|website=The Association of Police and Crime Commissioners|accessdate=3 March 2018}}</ref>

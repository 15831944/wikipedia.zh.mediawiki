'''約翰·羅伯特·李昂尼提'''（{{lang-en|'''John Robert Leonetti'''}}，{{bd|1956年|7月4日}}）是一名[[美國|美國]]男攝影師和導演<ref name="Grant1998">{{cite book|last=Grant|first=Edmond|title=The Motion Picture Guide, 1998 Annual: The Films of 1997|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=nE8OAQAAMAAJ|accessdate=2011-03-06|date=August 1998|publisher=CineBooks|isbn=978-0-933997-41-7|page=288|quote=The look of the film is quite spectacular, though, for a relatively low-budget martial-arts fantasy; perhaps this is because John R. Leonetti, cinematographer of the first KOMBAT, here assumed the task of director.}}</ref>，隸屬於{{le|美國電影攝影師協會|American Society of Cinematographers}}<ref name="Leydon">{{cite news|url=http://www.variety.com/review/VE1117934280/|title=Variety Reviews - I Know Who Killed Me|last=Leydon|first=Joe|date=2007-07-27|work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|accessdate=2011-03-06}}</ref>。他時常與導演[[溫子仁|溫子仁]]合作，在他的電影中擔任攝影師。

'''約翰·麥迪甘'''（John Madigan，{{bd|1966年|7月21日}}）是一位[[澳洲|澳洲]]政治人物<ref name="TheAge22Aug2010">{{cite news |url=http://news.theage.com.au/breaking-news-national/dlp-an-outside-chance-for-senate-20100822-13ako.html |title=DLP an outside chance for Senate |last=Turnbull |first=Jeff |work=[[The_Age|The Age]] |publisher=[[Fairfax_Media|Fairfax Media]] |accessdate=7 September 2010}}</ref>，他是一位獨立參議員。自2011年開始，他是代表[[維多利亞州|維多利亞州]]的[[澳大利亞參議院|澳大利亞參議院]]議員之一<ref name="RedLeatherDay">{{cite news |url=http://www.smh.com.au/federal-election/redleather-day-for-the-dlp-20100911-15629.html |title=Red-leather day for the DLP |work=[[Sydney_Morning_Herald|Sydney Morning Herald]] |date=12 September 2010 |first=Melissa |last=Fyfe |accessdate=16 September 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.theage.com.au/national/family-firsts-steve-fielding-loses-senate-seat-20100916-15ebv.html |title=Family First's Steve Fielding loses Senate seat |first=Megan |last=Levy |work=The Age |date=16 September 2010 |accessdate=17 September 2010 |publisher=[[Fairfax_Media|Fairfax Media]]}}</ref>。他出生在一個天主教家庭。

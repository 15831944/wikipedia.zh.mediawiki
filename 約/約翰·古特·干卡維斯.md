'''約翰·古特·干卡維斯'''（{{lang-fr|Yohan Goutt Goncalves}}；{{bd|1994年|12月20日|}}<ref>[https://www.olympic.org/yohan-goncalves-goutt Sochi 2014 profile]</ref>）是一名[[法國|法國]][[東帝汶|東帝汶]][[高山滑雪|高山滑雪]]運動員，2013年開始參賽<ref>[http://data.fis-ski.com/dynamic/athlete-biography.html?sector=AL&competitorid=187394&type=result GOUTT Yohan Goncalves Biography on FIS]</ref>，他取得參賽資格代表東帝汶參加在[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]][[索契|索契]]舉行的[[2014年冬季奧林匹克運動會|2014年冬季奧林匹克運動會]]。他也成為首位取得冬季奧運參賽資格的東帝汶運動員<ref name="a">{{cite news |last=Trouillard |first=Stéphanie |date=5 January 2014 |title=The skier taking East Timor to the Winter Olympics |url=http://www.france24.com/en/20140105-yohan-goutt-goncalves-skier-taking-east-timor-sochi-winter-olympics/ |newspaper=[[France_24|France 24]] |location= |publisher= |accessdate=6 January 2014}}</ref><ref name="b">{{cite news |last=Jones |first=Ruby |date=7 January 2014 |title=Even cooler runnings as skier from tropical East Timor qualifies for Winter Olympics |url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2014-01-07/east-timor-skier-qualified-winter-olympics-yohan-goutt-goncalves/5188796 |newspaper=[[ABC_Online|ABC Online]] |location= |publisher= |accessdate=7 January 2014}}</ref>。

古特·干卡維斯的父親為法國人，母親為東帝汶人，所以使它能夠到代表母親的母國出賽.<ref>{{cite news |last=Passa |first=Dennis |date=29 November 2013 |title=One man's journey from tropical East Timor to the Winter Olympics |url=http://www.couriermail.com.au/sport/more-sports/one-mans-journey-from-tropical-east-timor-to-the-winter-olympics/story-fnii0hmo-1226771170007 |newspaper=[[The_Courier-Mail|The Courier-Mail]] |location= |publisher= |accessdate=7 January 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Waters |first=Luke |date=26 August 2013 |title=East Timor skier eyes winter Olympics |url=http://www.sbs.com.au/news/article/2013/08/02/east-timor-skier-eyes-winter-olympics |newspaper=[[Special_Broadcasting_Service|Special Broadcasting Service]] |location= |publisher= |accessdate=7 January 2014}}</ref>。

2013年12月29日，在塞爾維亞舉行的一場比賽中約翰·古特·干卡維斯將總分降至140以下，正式取得2014年冬季奧林匹克運動會的參賽資格<ref name="a" />。

約翰·古特·干卡維斯決定放棄參加[[2017年世界高山滑雪錦標賽|2017年世界高山滑雪錦標賽]]，來參加[[2017年亞洲冬季運動會|2017年亞洲冬季運動會]]，使他成為首位代表東帝汶參加該洲際賽事的運動員<ref>{{cite news|title=Winter Asian Games are very topical in tropical East Timor|url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/sport/news/article.cfm?c_id=4&objectid=11805598|accessdate=22 February 2017|work=New Zealand Herald|agency=Associated Press|publisher=NZME. Pub|date=22 February 2017}}{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ocasia.org/News/IndexNewsRM.aspx?WKegervtea3AGEhmpxD0Sw==|title=Lone skier flying the flag for Timor-Leste at Asian Winter Games|website=www.ocasia.org/|publisher=[[Olympic_Council_of_Asia|Olympic Council of Asia]]|date=22 February 2017|access-date=22 February 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170914124751/http://www.ocasia.org/News/IndexNewsRM.aspx?WKegervtea3AGEhmpxD0Sw==|archive-date=2017-09-14|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

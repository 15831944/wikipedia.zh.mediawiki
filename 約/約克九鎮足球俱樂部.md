'''約克九鎮足球俱樂部'''（{{lang-en|'''York 9 Football Club'''}}）是一家位於[[加拿大|加拿大]][[安大略省|安大略省]][[約克區|約克區]]的[[足球|足球]]俱樂部，將於2019年起參加[[加拿大超級足球聯賽|加拿大超級足球聯賽]]賽事<ref name="Join">{{cite web |url=http://york9fc.canpl.ca/article/press-release-launch |title=York 9 Football Club Joins Canadian Premier League |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |date=2018-05-10 |website=York9FC.CanPL.ca |publisher= |access-date=2018-05-15 |quote=}}</ref><ref name="TorStar">{{cite web |url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/soccer/2018/05/10/soccers-fledgling-canadian-premier-league-unveils-its-first-team-the-york-9-football-club.html |title=Soccer’s fledgling Canadian Premier League unveils its first team, the York 9 Football Club |author=Laura Armstrong |date=2018-05-10 |website=TheStar.com |publisher=《[[多倫多星報|多倫多星報]]》 |access-date=2018-05-15 |quote=}}</ref>。俱樂部名稱裡面的「九」象徵組成約克區的九個市鎮<ref name="Join"/>。

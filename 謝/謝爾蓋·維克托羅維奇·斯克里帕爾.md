'''謝爾蓋·維克托罗維奇·斯克里帕爾'''（{{lang-ru|'''Серге́й Ви́кторович Скрипаль'''|'''Sergei Viktorovich Skripal'''}}，{{bd|1951年|6月23日|||catIdx=S}}），前[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]][[俄羅斯聯邦軍|聯邦軍]][[上校|上校]]，曾為[[英国|英国]]當[[间谍|间谍]]。<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-43291394 | date =2018-03-05 | accessdate =2018-03-05 | work=BBC News | title = Who is former Russian intelligence officer Sergei Skripal?}}</ref>

斯克里帕爾在俄罗斯被判定犯有「为英国[[軍情六處|軍情六處]]刺探情报」的罪行，於2006年8月以间谍罪判處入獄13年。他於2010年美國與俄羅斯達成交换被捕间谍協議後獲釋，在英国获得庇护。<ref name=dm01>{{Cite news |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-5463567/Hospital-shuts-E-department-major-incident.html |title=Former Russian 'spy' fights for life in Salisbury hospital |work=Daily Mail Online |date=2018-03-06}}</ref><ref name=voa01>{{Cite news |url=https://www.voachinese.com/a/russia-spy-20180305/4281569.html |title=前俄间谍在英国接触不明物质后病危 |publisher=美國之音 |date=2018-03-06}}</ref>

2018年3月4日，斯克里帕爾和其女兒尤利娅被發現倒在[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[威爾特郡|威爾特郡]][[索爾茲伯里|索爾茲伯里]]一处购物中心的长椅上不省人事，應是遭[[神经毒素|神经毒素]]謀害<ref name="graun">{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2018/mar/07/russian-spy-police-appeal-for-witnesses-as-cobra-meeting-takes-place|title=Sergei Skripal: former Russian spy poisoned with nerve agent, say police|last=Dodd|first=Vikram|last2=Harding|first2=Luke|last3=MacAskill|first3=Ewen|date=8 March 2018|publisher=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|accessdate=8 March 2018}}</ref>，二人被送进[[加护病房|加护病房]]，初時病情危殆，<ref name=dm01/><ref name=voa01/>經治療後已先後甦醒並逐漸康復。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://orientaldaily.on.cc/cnt/china_world/20180408/00180_003.html |title=中毒叛諜 甦醒將被問話 |publisher=東方日報 |date=2018-04-08}}</ref>

== 生平 ==
1972年，谢尔盖·斯克里帕尔从[[加里宁格勒|加里宁格勒]]军事工程学校毕业，获得工伞兵资格<ref name="nacheku">[http://www.bbc.com/russian/features-43294559 "Сергей всегда был начеку": как жил Скрипаль в России и Британии] BBC俄文网, 2018-03-08.</ref>。其后在莫斯科军事工程学院深造，并服役于苏联空降部队。在部队，斯克里帕尔加入[[格魯烏|總參謀部情報總局（格鲁乌）]]，出任駐西班牙[[駐外武官|武官]]<ref name="nacheku" />。联邦安全局和其他消息来源显示，1995年，他在西班牙經英国情报机构人员巴勃罗·米勒（{{lang|en|Pablo Miller}}）招入英国情报部门，化名安东尼奥·阿尔瓦雷斯·德伊达尔戈（{{lang|es|Antonio Alvarez de Hidalgo}}）<ref>[https://www.kommersant.ru/doc/795751 Налоговый полицейский узнал своего вербовщика: Он оказался старым знакомым контрразведчиков ФСБ] [[生意人报|生意人报]], 2007-08-16.</ref><ref>[https://meduza.io/en/feature/2018/03/06/a-hundred-grand-and-hundreds-of-betrayed-agents A hundred grand and hundreds of betrayed agents What was former GRU Colonel Sergey Skripal's treason against Russia?] Meduza, 2018-03-06.</ref>。

回到俄罗斯后，斯克里帕尔在格鲁乌总部工作，曾短暂担任格鲁乌人事處代理處長。1999年，斯克里帕尔以[[上校|上校]]军衔年满退役。

俄罗斯检察官表示，斯克里帕尔于1995年在英国[[军情六处|军情六处]]工作，泄露了国家机密，例如俄罗斯情报特工的身份，退役后同时为[[俄罗斯外交部|俄罗斯外交部]]內務司和英國军情六处效命<ref>[http://www.interfax.ru/world/602643 Лондон пообещал Москве жесткий ответ в случае причастности РФ к инциденту со Скрипалем] Interfax, 2018-03-06.</ref><ref>[https://sputniknews.com/russia/2006080952428496/ Russian convicted spy colonel worked for Britain's MI6] [[俄新社|俄新社]]/[[卫星通讯社|卫星通讯社]], 2006-08-09.</ref><ref>{{cite news |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070312001313/http://www.khaleejtimes.com/DisplayArticleNew.asp?xfile=data%2Ftheworld%2F2006%2FAugust%2Ftheworld_August324.xml&section=theworld |archive-date=2007-03-12 |url=http://www.khaleejtimes.com/DisplayArticleNew.asp?xfile=data/theworld/2006/August/theworld_August324.xml&section=theworld |title=Russian colonel sentenced to 13 years in prison for spying for Britain |date=2006-08-09 |publisher=[[法新社|法新社]] |deadurl=yes }}</ref> 
。

2001年，斯克里帕尔供职于[[莫斯科市政府|莫斯科市政府]]直辖市部<ref name="nacheku" />。

2004年12月，斯克里帕尔在俄罗斯被控“间谍形式严重叛国罪”並遭到逮捕，并被定罪。检察官最初要求判刑15年，但考虑到被告人健康状况欠佳且配合调查，最終判處入狱13年。直到2006年8月判刑，他的控罪和審訊才公之于众<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/europe/4775131.stm|title=Russian colonel jailed for spying|publisher=BBC News|date=2006-08-09|accessdate=2018-03-08}}</ref>。斯克里帕尔的律师提出了上诉<ref>{{cite news|url=https://sputniknews.com/russia/2006081052484523/|title=Colonel convicted of spying for U.K. appeals verdict|agency=RIA Novosti/Sputnik|date=2006-08-10|accessdate=2018-03-08}}</ref>。

2010年7月，斯克里帕尔和另外三名被控犯有间谍罪的俄罗斯公民，作为美国逮捕在美国从事{{le|非法人士计划|Illegals Program|间谍行动}}的俄罗斯人后，美俄间谍互换计划的一部分，获得赦免出狱。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/europe/russia/7877570/Russia-and-US-preparing-spy-swap.html|title=Russia and US preparing spy swap|work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Telegraph]]|date=2010-07-07|accessdate=2018-03-08}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2010/jul/09/russian-spies-swap-us|title=Spy swap: US and Russia hand over agents in full media glare|first1=Tom|last1=Parfitt|first2=Matthew|last2=Weaver|first3=Richard|last3=Norton-Taylor|date=2010-07-09|newspaper=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|accessdate=2018-03-08}}</ref><ref name="guardian-taylor">{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2010/jul/11/british-security-services-debrief-russians-spy-swap|date=2010-07-11|accessdate=2018-03-08|first1=Richard|last1=Norton-Taylor|first2=Tom|last2=Parfitt|first3=Andrew|last3=Clark|newspaper=The Guardian|location=London|title=British security services debrief two Russians freed in spy swap}}</ref>赦免令由时任[[俄罗斯总统|俄罗斯总统]][[德米特里·梅德韦杰夫|德米特里·梅德韦杰夫]]發出<ref name="BBC-43303651">{{cite web|title=Boris Johnson warns Russia over spy collapse|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-43303651|publisher=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|accessdate=2018-03-08|date=2018-03-06}}</ref>。英国政府坚称他的名字纳入互换计划中<ref name="nacheku" />。

2011年，他迁居英国[[威尔特郡|威尔特郡]][[索尔兹伯里|索尔兹伯里]]，在当地买下一间房子<ref>{{cite news|title=Salisbury, scratchcards and sausage: the quiet life of Sergei Skripal|url=https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2018/mar/06/salisbury-scratchcards-and-sausage-the-quiet-life-of-sergei-skripal|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|date=2018-03-06|accessdate=2018-03-08}}</ref>。据英国安全机构称，斯克里帕尔在2010年后的一段时间里继续向英国和其他西方国家的情报机构提供情报。

他的妻子于2012年因[[子宫内膜癌|子宫内膜癌]]扩散去世。女儿2014年回到莫斯科，当销售员。儿子于2017年3月在前往[[圣彼得堡|圣彼得堡]]时突然去世，死因未明<ref>{{cite news|title=Russian spy attack: military convoy arrives at Salisbury hospital |url=https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2018/mar/09/skripals-condition-still-serious-says-amber-rudd-on-salisbury-visit|publisher=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|date=2019-03-09|accessdate=2018-03-09|first1=Vikram|last1=Dodd|first2=Ewen |last2= MacAskill| first3= Steven |last3=Morris}}</ref>，哥哥于毒害事件发生两年前去世<ref name="guardian-morris">{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2018/mar/06/uk-counter-terror-police-investigate-russian-spy-mystery-sergei-skripal|date=2018-03-06|access-date=2018-03-08|first1=Steven|last1=Morris|first2=Luke|last2=Harding|first3=Caroline|last3=Bannock|newspaper=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|location=London|title=Woman in Russian spy mystery identified as Sergei Skripal's daughter|edition=US}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-43297638|date=2018-03-06|accessdate=2018-03-08|publisher=BBC News|title=Russian spy: Sergei Skripal collapsed alongside daughter}}</ref>。妻子和儿子都葬在索尔兹伯里当地的墓园<ref name=BBC43312625>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-43312625|title='More known' about substance in spy case|date=2018-03-07|publisher=BBC News}}</ref>。

== 遭毒害 ==
{{main|英國前俄羅斯間諜毒殺案}}
2018年3月4日，一名路过的医生和护士在索尔兹伯里一处购物中心的长椅上发现斯克里帕尔和他从莫斯科到来的33岁女儿尤利娅不省人事<ref>{{cite news|title=Russian spy: What we know so far|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-43315636|work=BBC News|date=8 March 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2018/03/05/alleged-former-russian-spy-critically-exposure-unknown-substance/|title=Alleged former Russian spy critically ill after exposure to unknown substance in Salisbury|work=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]]|date=5 March 2018|access-date=8 March 2018}}</ref>。医护人员将两人送往{{le|索尔兹伯里区医院|Salisbury District Hospital}}，经诊断两人[[神经毒剂|神经毒剂]]中毒，病情危急<ref name=bbc43359154>{{Cite news |url=http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-43359154 |title=Russian spy: 240 witnesses identified over poisoning |work=BBC News |date=2018-03-10}}</ref>。當地警方其後宣佈此乃一宗「重大事件」，多個部門正在進行調查<ref name=auto>{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-43295134|date=5 March 2018|access-date=8 March 2018|publisher=BBC News|title=Critically ill man 'former Russian spy'}}</ref>。

随后到达现场三名警察受到感染，其中两人受伤轻微，一人伤势严重<ref>{{cite news|title=Russian spy 'attacked with nerve agent'|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-43323847|work=BBC News|date=7 March 2018}}</ref>。案发后，健康官员检查了21名急救人员和民众是否出现症状，但只有三人送院<ref name=itv-20180308>{{cite news |url=http://www.itv.com/news/2018-03-08/nerve-agent-attack-state-russian-spy-daughter/ |title=Up to 21 people treated after nerve agent attack on Russian spy Sergei Skripal |work=ITV News |date=8 March 2018 |access-date=9 March 2018}}</ref><ref name=bbcr4wato-20180309>{{cite AV media |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b09tc4xr |last=Casciani |first=Dominic |publisher=BBC Radio 4 |title=World at One |date=9 March 2018 |access-date=9 March 2018 |time=21m47s |quote=there was some erroneous reporting that there were 21 other people being treated, that is not true, there has only been these three casualties and they are all still in hospital}}</ref>。

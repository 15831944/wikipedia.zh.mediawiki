'''謝爾蓋·庫塞維茲基'''（Serge Koussevitzky）<ref>Koussevitzky's original Russian forename is usually [[transliteration|transliterated]] into English as either '''Sergei''' or '''Sergey'''; however, he himself adopted the French spelling '''[[Serge_(given_name)|Serge]]''', using it in his signature. (See [http://www.koussevitzky.org/ The Koussevitzky Music Foundations official web site]. Retrieved 2009-11-05.) His surname can be transliterated variously as '''Koussevitzky''', '''Koussevitsky''', '''Kussevitzky''', '''Kusevitsky''', or, into Polish, as '''Kusewicki'''; however, he himself chose to use '''Koussevitzky'''.</ref> (Russian: Серге́й Алекса́ндрович Кусеви́цкий; Sergey Aleksandrovich Kusevitsky;1874年7月26日  [ OS 7月14日]   - 1951年6月4日) 是俄羅斯出生的指揮，作曲家和低音提琴家，1924年至1949年長期擔任[[波士頓交響樂團|波士頓交響樂團]][[音樂總監|音樂總監]]。

謝爾蓋·庫塞維茲基出生於一個貧窮的[[猶太人|猶太人]]家庭，位於[[特維爾省|特維爾省]]（今特維爾州）[[上沃洛喬克|上沃洛喬克]]，距離約[[莫斯科|莫斯科]]250公里。他的父母是專業音樂家，教他小提琴，大提琴和鋼琴。他還學會[[小號|小號]]<ref name=NewGrove2>José Bowen, "Koussevitzky [Kusevitsky], Sergey (Aleksandrovich)" in [[Stanley_Sadie|Sadie, Stanley]]; [[John_Tyrrell_(professor_of_music)|John Tyrrell]], eds. (2001). ''[[The_New_Grove_Dictionary_of_Music_and_Musicians|The New Grove Dictionary of Music and Musicians]]'', 2nd edition. New York: Grove's Dictionaries. ISBN 1-56159-239-0.</ref>。

'''鳥胸骨類'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|Ornithothoraces}}），又名'''鳥胸類'''，是一個包含所有[[反鳥亞綱|反鳥亞綱]]和[[今鳥亞綱|今鳥亞綱]]的[[鳥類|鳥類]]分支。

鳥胸骨類的胸部高度進化，令它們具有特強的飛行能力。它們的[[喙突|喙突]]很長，[[胸骨|胸骨]]很大，改良了的肩膀關節盂，半剛硬的背部胸籃。最早已知的鳥胸骨類是1億4000萬年前[[中國|中國]]的[[原羽鳥|原羽鳥]]。<ref name=jinetal2008>{{cite journal|doi=10.1007/s11434-008-0209-5|last1=Jin|first1=F.|last2=Zhang|first2=F.C.|last3=Li|first3=Z.H.|last4=Zhang|first4=J.Y.|last5=Li|first5=C.|last6=Zhou|first6=Z.H.|year=2008|title=On the horizon of ''Protopteryx'' and the early vertebrate fossil assemblages of the Jehol Biota|journal=Chinese Science Bulletin|volume=53|issue=18|pages=2820–7}}</ref>它們是[[伊比利亞鳥|伊比利亞鳥]]<ref name="Chiappe&Calvo1994">{{Cite journal|author=Chiappe, Luis; Calvo, J.O.|year=1994|title=''Nequenornis volans'', a new Late Cretaceous bird (Enantiornithes:Avisauridae) from Patagonia, Argentina|journal=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology|volume=14|pages=230-46}}</ref><ref name="Chiappe1996">{{Cite book|author=Chiappe, Luis|year=1996|chapter=Late Cretaceous birds of southern South America: anatomy and systematics of Enantiornithes and ''Patagopteryx deferrariisi''|pages=pp. 203-244|editor=G. Arratia (ed.)|title=Contributions of Southern South America to Vertebrate Paleontology|publisher=Munchner Geowissenschaftliche Abhandlungen, reihe A, Geologieund Palaontologie 30. Verlag Dr. Friedrich Pfeil, Munich}}</ref>或[[中國鳥|中國鳥]]<ref name="Sereno1998">{{Cite journal|author=Sereno, Paul|year=1998|title=A rationale for phylogenetic definitions, with application to the higher-level taxonomy of Dinosauria|journal=Neues Jahrbuch fur Geologie und Palaontologie|volume=210|pages=41-83}}</ref>及所有現存鳥類的共同祖先。

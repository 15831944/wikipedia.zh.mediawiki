'''錫蘭觀察家報'''為一份已停辦的[[斯里蘭卡|斯里蘭卡]]英文日報，由「錫蘭聯合報章有限公司」出版。其前身'''觀察家與商業廣告報'''於1834年在[[科倫坡|科倫坡]]創刊，1982年停刊<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.revolvy.com/page/The-Ceylon-Observer|title="The Ceylon Observer" on Revolvy.com|accessdate=2018-11-17|last=LLC|first=Revolvy,|work=www.revolvy.com|language=en}}</ref>。

== 历史 ==
《觀察家與商業廣告報》于1834年2月4日由居於科伦坡的英国商人創立<ref name="JDBUC">{{Cite journal|title=Periodicals and Newspapers in Ceylon|url=http://www.dutchburgherunion.org/journals/vol_31_40/JDBU%20Vol%2031%20No%203%20-%201942(1).pdf|date=April 1942|journal=Journal of the Dutch Burgher Union of Ceylon|issue=4|volume=XXXI|pages=137–152|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923220953/http://www.dutchburgherunion.org/journals/vol_31_40/JDBU%20Vol%2031%20No%203%20-%201942(1).pdf|archivedate=2015-09-23|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref name="ACH">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Wo9YWvrWFcIC&printsec=frontcover#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Asian Communication Handbook 2008|last=Karunanayake|first=Nandana|date=2008|publisher=[[Asian_Media_Information_and_Communication_Centre|Asian Media Information and Communication Centre]], Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information, [[Nanyang_Technological_University|Nanyang Technological University]]|isbn=9789814136105|editor-last=Banerjee|editor-first=Indrajit|editor2-last=Logan|editor2-first=Stephen|location=Singapore|pages=446–460|chapter=18: Sri Lanka}}</ref>。首任編輯達利 (E. J. Darley) 兼任報章負責人<ref name="20th">{{Cite book|url=http://www.noolaham.org/wiki/index.php?title=Twentieth_Century_Impressions_of_Ceylon|title=Twentieth Century Impressions of Ceylon|last=Beven|first=Francis|publisher=[[Asian_Educational_Services|Asian Educational Services]]|editor-last=Wright|editor-first=Arnold|pages=301–319|chapter=The Press}}</ref>，他随后任命乔治·温特为编辑。報章每逢星期一和星期四出版，后来改為每日下午出版<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.sundaytimes.lk/060709/funday/OurHeritage.html|title=Ceylon Times' begins publication|date=9 July 2006|work=[[The_Sunday_Times_(Sri_Lanka)|The Sunday Times]]}}</ref>。報章創刊首年就因為發表公開信批評警察局局長而被控告誹謗罪，幸被判無罪<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=Qp7WgKdja2UC&pg=PA45&lpg=PA45&dq=The+Observer+sri+lanka+harassment&source=bl&ots=b6AsxeuT7-&sig=MKXQkCq-ujoWlE7oyBLmdoXv6mo&hl=en&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwiii8rQ19reAhXEgLwKHVndBw0Q6AEwDXoECAgQAQ|title=Resplendent Sites, Discordant Voices: Sri Lankans and International Tourism|last=Crick|first=Malcolm|date=2012-10-12|publisher=Routledge|year=|isbn=9781136645082|location=|pages=|language=en|chapter=Resplendent Sites, Discordant Voices: Sri Lankans and International Tourism}}</ref>。

1835年，[[巴杜勒|巴杜勒]]外科医生克里斯托弗·艾略特 (Christopher Elliott) 成为该报编辑，后来成为该报纸的負責人<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.irishancestors.ie/christopher-elliott-1809-1859/|title=Christopher Elliott (1809–1859) – Irish Genealogical Research Society|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=www.irishancestors.ie|language=en-GB}}</ref>。艾略特將報章改名為《科倫坡觀察家報》<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pressreader.com/sri-lanka/sunday-times-sri-lanka/20120909/282888022874902|title=PressReader.com - Connecting People Through News|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=www.pressreader.com}}</ref>。《科倫坡觀察家報》因多番批评威尔莫特 - 霍顿 (Wilmot-Horton) 總督<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Governor Sir Robert Wilmot Horton and the Reforms of 1833 in Ceylon|url=http://www.jstor.org/stable/2638119|last=Samaraweera|first=Vijaya|date=1972|journal=The Historical Journal|issue=2|volume=15|pages=209–228}}</ref>，最終導致公務員在1837年創立親政府報章《锡兰纪事报》(The Ceylon Chronicle) <ref>

{{Cite news|url=http://www.sundaytimes.lk/060430/funday/2.html|title=Significant events in May: An English newspaper|date=30 April 2006|newspaper=[[The_Sunday_Times_(Sri_Lanka)|The Sunday Times]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.dailynews.lk/2012/05/15/fea22.asp|title=Newspapers and journals in early Sri Lanka|last=Scott|first=Andrew|date=15 May 2012|newspaper=[[Daily_News_(Sri_Lanka)|Daily News]]}}</ref>。《科倫坡觀察家報》支持斯图尔特 - 麦肯齐 (Stewart-Mackenzie) 總督<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.worldgenweb.org/lkawgw/gen1268.html|title=Sri Lanka Anglo-Burgher Family G|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=www.worldgenweb.org}}</ref>，但反对坎贝尔 (Campbell) 和林顿 (Torrington) 政府。1840年，姊妹報章《陸上觀察家報》創刊，原先為月刊，后来改為半月刊，再改為周刊<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://trove.nla.gov.au/version/8607303|title=Colombo overland observer and fortnightly summary of Ceylon|last=|first=|date=1800|publisher=Colombo : A.M. Ferguson|year=|isbn=|location=|pages=|language=en|chapter=Colombo overland observer and fortnightly summary of Ceylon intelligence}}</ref>。

1846年，弗格森 (Alastair Mackenzie Ferguson) 加入了《科伦坡观察報》<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bookdepository.com/Souvenirs-Ceylon-Alastair-MacKenzie-Ferguson/9781293287927|title=Souvenirs of Ceylon : Alastair MacKenzie Ferguson : 9781293287927|accessdate=2018-11-17|last=Depository|first=Book|work=www.bookdepository.com|language=en}}</ref>。1859年，隨著艾略特加入政府民政部門擔任特等官員，弗格森接手並收購報章<ref name="Martyn">{{Cite book|url=http://noolaham.net/project/47/4668/4668.pdf|title=Notes on Jaffna – Chronological, Historical, Biographical|last=Martyn|first=John H.|publisher=[[American_Ceylon_Mission|American Ceylon Mission]] Press|year=1923|isbn=81-206-1670-7|location=[[Tellippalai|Tellippalai]]|pages=205–206}}</ref>。1861年，弗格森的侄子约翰·弗格森加入本報<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.worldgenweb.org/lkawgw/gen1264.html|title=Sri Lanka British Family Genealo|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=www.worldgenweb.org}}</ref>。 1867年，该报改名为《锡兰觀察家報》。约翰·弗格森于1870年成为联合编辑，1875年成为合作伙伴<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.worldgenweb.org/lkawgw/ferguson.html|title=JOHN FERGUSON|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=www.worldgenweb.org}}</ref>。隨著弗格森於1892年去世，约翰·弗格森成为该报主編。其兒子罗纳德·哈顿·弗格森子承父業，成為主編。

1920年，本報被锡兰欧洲协会旗下的一家公司收購<ref name="DFT310514">{{Cite news|url=http://www.ft.lk/2014/05/31/the-observer-is-180-years-old/|title=The 'Observer' is 180 years old|date=31 May 2014|work=[[Daily_FT|Daily FT]]|access-date=2018-11-03|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160624031723/http://www.ft.lk/2014/05/31/the-observer-is-180-years-old/#|archive-date=2016-06-24|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。1923年，域佐穫典拿 (D. R. Wijewardena) 收購本報，并将其添加到他的媒体帝国（后来称为錫蘭聯合報章有限公司，亦稱湖邊小屋）<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nation.lk/2007/05/20/Sup3.pdf|title=Newspaper proliferation and the vanishing tribe|date=20 May 2007|work=[[The_Nation_(Sri_Lanka)|The Nation]]}}</ref>。1928年2月4日，《星期日观察家报》創刊<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.sundayobserver.lk/2008/02/03/spe01.asp|title=Sunday Observer 80th Anniversary: Eighty years in print|last=Chandrarathne|first=Ranga|date=3 February 2008|work=[[Sunday_Observer_(Sri_Lanka)|Sunday Observer]]}}</ref>。

在二十世纪初，《锡兰观察家報》及其姊妹报纸《锡兰日报》积极推動锡兰的宪法改革<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.sundaytimes.lk/980201/plus8.html|title=Those good ole days!|last=Salgado|first=Upali|date=1 February 1998|work=[[The_Sunday_Times_(Sri_Lanka)|The Sunday Times]]}}</ref>。1948年锡兰从英国独立后，湖邊小屋及其竞争对手锡兰時代有限公司主导了國內报业<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.media.gov.lk/institutions/1/associated-newspapers-of-ceylon-ltd|title=Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Ltd|accessdate=2018-11-17|last=gayan|work=www.media.gov.lk|language=en-gb}}</ref>。湖邊小屋被視為[[统一国民党|統一國民黨]]的陣營<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.dailynews.lk/2018/01/03/features/138752/century%E2%80%99s-fruition|title=A century's fruition|accessdate=|author=Daily News|date=|publisher=}}</ref>。1973年7月，由[[斯里兰卡自由党|斯里兰卡自由党]]领导的联合政府将湖邊小屋國有化。湖邊小屋至今仍然是國內最大型的報章公司<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.lakehouse.lk/about/our-history|title=Our History|date=2017-07-23|newspaper=Lake House|accessdate=2018-11-17}}</ref>，旗下的出版刊物均被指「盲目支持政府」，不管哪个政党掌权<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://srilankalaw.lk/Volume-I/associated-newspapers-of-ceylon-ltd-special-provisions-law.html|title=Associated Newspapers of Ceylon Ltd (Special Provisions) Law|accessdate=2018-11-17|author=|date=|last=Super-admin|work=srilankalaw.lk|publisher=|language=en-gb}}</ref>。《錫蘭觀察家報》於1982年2月停辦，姊妹報《星期日觀察家報》則尚在營運，有時亦被稱爲錫蘭觀察家報<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://sundayobserver.lk/|title=Sunday Observer|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=sundayobserver.lk|language=en}}</ref>。

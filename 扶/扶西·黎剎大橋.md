'''扶西·黎剎大橋'''（{{lang-en|Jose Rizal Bridge}}），原名'''第十二大道南大橋'''（{{lang-en|12th Avenue South Bridge}}），又稱'''迪爾伯恩街大橋'''（{{lang-en|Dearborn Street Bridge}}），是[[美國|美國]][[西雅圖|西雅圖]]首座永久性的鋼結構橋樑，建於1911年；[[90號州際公路|90號州際公路]]與第十二大道南從此橋跨越南迪爾伯恩街，並连接{{link-en|西雅圖唐人街與國際區|International District, Seattle, Washington|國際區}}與{{tsl|en|Beacon Hill, Seattle, Washington|烽火山}}<ref name="historylink">{{Cite web|url=http://www.historylink.org/index.cfm?DisplayPage=output.cfm&File_Id=8437|title=Seattle's 12th Avenue South (Dearborn Street) Bridge is built in 1911|accessdate=2015-10-04|date=2007-12-23|last=Long|first=Priscilla|publisher=[[HistoryLink|HistoryLink]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130806053007/http://historylink.org/index.cfm?DisplayPage=output.cfm&file_id=8437|archive-date=2013-08-06|dead-url=no}}</ref>。1974年，為紀念菲律賓國父[[黎剎|黎剎]]醫生，大橋被更改為現名，但西雅圖市民對該官方名字並不太熟悉<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://o.seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/eastsidenews/2003585417_ontheroad23e.html|title=Bridge names: The real monikers|first=Amy|last=Roe|date=2007-02-23|work=[[The_Seattle_Times|The Seattle Times]]|accessdate=2013-06-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180103193414/http://o.seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/eastsidenews/2003585417_ontheroad23e.html|archive-date=2018-01-03|dead-url=no}}</ref>。大桥在1982年以其原名被列於[[國家史蹟名錄|國家史蹟名錄]]上<ref name="historylink">{{Cite web|url=http://www.historylink.org/index.cfm?DisplayPage=output.cfm&File_Id=8437|title=Seattle's 12th Avenue South (Dearborn Street) Bridge is built in 1911|accessdate=2015-10-04|date=2007-12-23|last=Long|first=Priscilla|publisher=[[HistoryLink|HistoryLink]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130806053007/http://historylink.org/index.cfm?DisplayPage=output.cfm&file_id=8437|archive-date=2013-08-06|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="nrhp-weekly">{{Cite web|url=http://www.nps.gov/nr/listings/Weekly_List_FR-March-1-1983-listingyear1982.pdf|title=National Register of Historic Places; 1999年，時任西雅圖市長{{tsl|en|Paul Schell|保罗·谢尔}}計劃替西雅圖市內所有主要地標裝上燈光裝置，以慶祝[[千禧年|千禧年]]的來臨，然而由於缺乏資金和後勤問題，最後只有兩座地標被安裝上燈光裝置，當中包括扶西·黎剎大橋，至今大橋仍然是由泛光灯照明<ref name="historylink"/><ref>{{cite news|url=http://community.seattletimes.nwsource.com/archive/?date=20000106&slug=A20000107010328|title=Queen Anne panel rejects bridge lights|last=Hodson|first=Jeff|date=2000-01-06|work=[[The_Seattle_Times|The Seattle Times]]|accessdate=2009-06-23|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5hmuQ2IIC?url=http://community.seattletimes.nwsource.com/archive/?date=20000106|archivedate=2009-06-25}}</ref>。

2002年，一名西雅圖警方[[舉報人|綫人]]被殺害，其尸首被发现於大橋下一个无家可归者的露宿地點附近<ref>{{cite news|url=http://seattleweekly.com/2007-09-12/news/seattle-s-longest-murder-trial.php|title=Seattle's Longest Murder Trial|author=Rick Anderson|date=2007-09-12|publisher=Seattle Weekly|accessdate=2007-12-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071126144751/http://www.seattleweekly.com/2007-09-12/news/seattle-s-longest-murder-trial.php|archive-date=2007-11-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

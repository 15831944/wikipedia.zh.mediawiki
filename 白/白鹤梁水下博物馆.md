'''白鹤梁水下博物馆''' ({{lang-en|White Crane Ridge Underwater Museum}})<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.meiyatravel.com/Guide/11801.aspx |title=White Crane Ridge Underwater Museum |website=www.meiyatravel.com |publisher=Meiya Travel |accessdate=24 September 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151222085550/http://www.meiyatravel.com/Guide/11801.aspx |archivedate=22 December 2015 |df= }}</ref>是位于重庆市涪陵区[[白鹤梁|白鹤梁]]的一处水下博物馆，也是中国第一座水下博物馆。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.epeanchina.com/en/case/show.aspx?caseid=144115188075855872&id=163 | title=Under-Water Museum of White Crane Ridge Inscription In Chongqing | website=www.epeanchina.com | publisher=Epean China | accessdate=24 September 2014 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url=http://ngm.nationalgeographic.com/2013/03/fuling-china/hessler-text | title=Fuling, China: Return to River Town | first=Peter | last=Hessler | authorlink=Peter Hessler | work=[[国家地理_(杂志)|国家地理 (杂志)]] | date=March 2013 | accessdate=24 September 2014 }}</ref><ref name="chinahush">{{cite web| url=http://www.chinahush.com/2009/05/21/chongqing-the-first-underwater-museum-in-china-has-been-built-and-opened/ | title=Chongqing: The First Underwater Museum in China has Been Built and Opened | website=www.chinahush.com | publisher=Chinahush | date=21 May 2009 | accessdate=23 August 2014 }}</ref>

==历史==
因为修建[[三峡大坝|三峡大坝]]，导致水平面上涨淹没国宝，为保护文物石刻，由[[中国科学院|中国科学院]]武汉岩土力学研究所研究员，[[中国科学院|中国科学院]]院士[[葛修润|葛修润]]提议采用“无压容器”方案修建一座水下博物馆供游客参观，2003年2月13日，白鹤梁原址水下保护工程正式开工。<ref name="chinahush" /> 2009年5月18日正式对外开放。<ref name="unesco">{{cite web|url=http://www.unesco.org/new/en/culture/themes/underwater-cultural-heritage/about-the-heritage/underwater-museums/the-baiheliang-underwater-museum/ | title=The Baiheliang Underwater Museum, Fuling, Chongqing Municipality, China | publisher=[[UNESCO|UNESCO]] | accessdate=23 August 2014 }}</ref><ref>[http://news.sciencenet.cn/htmlnews/2011/1/242450.shtm 探访葛修润院士抢救三峡库区白鹤梁题刻心路历程]，科学网。</ref>

'''白屈氨酸'''（{{lang-en|'''Chelidamic acid'''}}，又名'''白屈菜氨酸'''）是一种分子式为C<sub>7</sub>H<sub>5</sub>NO<sub>5</sub>的[[杂环化合物|杂环]][[氨基酸|氨基酸]]，分子中含有一个[[吡啶|吡啶]]环。

== 合成 ==
第一步是[[丙酮|丙酮]]与[[草酸二乙酯|草酸二乙酯]]在强碱（一般常用[[乙醇钠|乙醇钠]]）的作用下发生[[克莱森缩合|克莱森缩合]]，生成[[白屈菜酸|白屈菜酸]]：<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Howáth | first1 = G. R. | last2 = Rusa | first2 = C. | last3 = Köntös | first3 = Z. N. | last4 = Gerencsér | first4 = J. N. | last5 = Huszthy | first5 = P. T. | title = A new Efficient Method for the Preparation of 2,6-Pyridinedihiethyl Ditosylates from Dimethyl 2,60-Pyridinedicarboxylates | doi = 10.1080/00397919908086011 | journal = Synthetic Communications | volume = 29 | issue = 21 | pages = 3719 | year = 1999 | pmid =  | pmc = }}</ref>
[[File:Chelidonic_Acid_Synthesis.svg|left]]{{Clear|left}}
第二步是白屈菜酸与氨水反应得到白屈氨酸水溶液：<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Haitinger | first1 = L. | last2 = Lieben | first2 = A. | doi = 10.1007/BF01554631 | title = Untersuchungen über Chelidonsäure | journal = Monatshefte für Chemie | volume = 6 | pages = 279 | year = 1885 | language = de| pmid =  | pmc = }}</ref>
[[File:Chelidamic_acid_synthesis.svg|left]]{{Clear|left}}
之后再从水溶液中结晶，得到白屈菜酸的[[水合物|一水合物]]（也是大部分商品白屈菜酸的存在形式）。<ref name="Sigma" />

== 性质 ==
白屈氨酸在常温下一般是淡黄色的粉末，加热后会在熔点（267 °C）分解。<ref name="Sigma">{{Sigma-Aldrich|SIGMA|C8011|Datum=16. März 2011|Name=Chelidamic acid hydrate}}</ref> 它有两种[[酮-烯醇互变异构|酮-烯醇互变异构]]产生的[[互变异构体|互变异构体]]，分别是具有[[芳香性|芳香性]]的[[烯醇|烯醇]]式和不具芳香性的[[酮|酮]]式。它的一水合物则是[[内盐|内盐]]，在[[羟基|羟基]]上带负电，在[[氮|氮]]原子上带正电。<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Hall | first1 = A. K. | last2 = Harrowfield | first2 = J. M. | last3 = Skelton | first3 = B. W. | last4 = White | first4 = A. H. | title = Chelidamic acid monohydrate: The proton complex of a multidentate ligand | doi = 10.1107/S0108270199015620 | journal = Acta Crystallographica Section C Crystal Structure Communications | volume = 56 | issue = 4 | pages = 448 | year = 2000 | pmid =  | pmc = }}</ref>

== 用途 ==
白屈氨酸是一种常用的[[有机合成|有机合成]]中间体，在[[有机化学|有机化学]]、[[医学|医学]]及[[生物化学|生物化学]]研究中用于合成各种杂环化合物，如[[配体|配体]][[二噁唑啉吡啶|二噁唑啉吡啶]]（PyBOX）<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Weissberg | first1 = A. | last2 = Halak | first2 = B. | last3 = Portnoy | first3 = M. | doi = 10.1021/jo050237j | title = The First Solid-Phase Synthesis of Bis(oxazolinyl)pyridine Ligands | journal = The Journal of Organic Chemistry | volume = 70 | issue = 11 | pages = 4556–4559 | year = 2005 | pmid =  15903347| pmc = }}</ref> 和[[三联吡啶|三联吡啶]]（Terpy）<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Adrian | first1 = J. C. | last2 = Hassib | first2 = L. | last3 = De Kimpe | first3 = N. | last4 = Keppens | first4 = M. | title = A new approach to symmetric 2,2′:6′,2″-terpyridines | doi = 10.1016/S0040-4020(98)00005-2 | journal = Tetrahedron | volume = 54 | issue = 11 | pages = 2365 | year = 1998 | pmid =  | pmc = }}</ref> 等。作为中间体，它的吡啶环上的[[羟基|羟基]]极容易被取代，而[[羧基|羧基]]则很适合于碳链的延长。同时它还是一种强螯合配体，能与许多金属离子形成稳定的[[螯合物|螯合物]]，因此常用于各种[[稀土金属|稀土金属]]性质的研究。<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Tse | first1 = M. K. | last2 = Bhor | first2 = S. | last3 = Klawonn | first3 = M. | last4 = Anilkumar | first4 = G. | last5 = Jiao | first5 = H. | last6 = Döbler | first6 = C. | last7 = Spannenberg | first7 = A. | last8 = Mägerlein | first8 = W. | last9 = Hugl | first9 = H. | doi = 10.1002/chem.200501261 | title = Ruthenium-Catalyzed Asymmetric Epoxidation of Olefins Using H2O2, Part I: Synthesis of New Chiral N,N,N-Tridentate Pybox and Pyboxazine Ligands and Their Ruthenium Complexes | journal = Chemistry - A European Journal | volume = 12 | issue = 7 | pages = 1855 | year = 2006 | pmid = | pmc = }}</ref> 不过价格的原因限制了它的进一步使用。

白屈氨酸具有很强的抑制[[谷氨酸脱羧酶|谷氨酸脱羧酶]]的作用。<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1016/0006-2952(85)90207-2 | last1 = Porter | first1 = T. G. | last2 = Martin | first2 = D. L. | title = Chelidonic acid and other conformationally restricted substrate analogues as inhibitors of rat brain glutamate decarboxylase | journal = Biochemical pharmacology | volume = 34 | issue = 23 | pages = 4145–4150 | year = 1985 | pmid = 4062982 }}</ref>

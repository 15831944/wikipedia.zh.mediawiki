'''白山山脉国家森林'''（{{lang-en|White Mountain National Forest，'''WMNF'''）是一个位于美国东北部[[白山山脈_(新罕布夏州)|白山山脈]]的国家森林。它成立于1918年<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foresthistory.org/ASPNET/Policy/WeeksAct/WeeksActForests.aspx|title=Chronology of National Forests Established Under the Weeks Act|accessdate=2011-03-07|publisher=The Forest History Society|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110726061741/http://www.foresthistory.org/ASPNET/Policy/WeeksAct/WeeksActForests.aspx|archivedate=2011-07-26|deadurl=yes}}</ref>，但联邦的土地采购已经于1914年开始<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fs.fed.us/r9/forests/white_mountain/about/history/|title=History of the White Mountain|accessdate=2011-03-07|publisher=White Mountain National Forest}}</ref>。它的总面积为{{Convert|750852|acre|ha|0}}，大多数位于在[[新罕布什尔州|新罕布什尔州]]，一小部分（约占森林的5.65%）位于相邻的[[缅因州|缅因州]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fs.fed.us/land/staff/lar/2007/TABLE_6.htm|title=Table 6 - NFS Acreage by State, Congressional District and County|accessdate=2008-05-19|date=2007-10-07|publisher=U.S. Forest Service}}</ref>。[[阿帕拉契小徑|阿帕拉契小徑]]贯穿白山山脉国家森林。白山山脉国家森林所占土地面积大小的县依次是新罕布什尔州[[格拉夫頓縣|格拉夫頓縣]]、[[库斯县_(新罕布什尔州)|库斯县]]和[[卡洛爾縣_(新罕布什爾州)|卡罗尔县]]， 缅因州[[牛津縣_(緬因州)|牛津縣]]。

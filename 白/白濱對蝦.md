'''白濱對蝦'''（''Litopenaeus setiferus''）是[[濱對蝦屬|濱對蝦屬]]下的一種蝦，生活在北美洲的大西洋沿岸和[[墨西哥灣|墨西哥灣]]中<ref name="Muncy">{{cite book|author=Robert J. Muncy|year=1984|series=Species profiles: life histories and environmental requirements of coastal fishes and invertebrates (South Atlantic)|title=White shrimp|publisher=[[United_States_Fish_and_Wildlife_Service|United States Fish and Wildlife Service]]|id=FWS/OBS-82/11.27|pages=1–19|url=http://www.nwrc.usgs.gov/wdb/pub/species_profiles/82_11-027.pdf|format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]]}}</ref>。

==形態==

白濱對蝦長度可達{{convert|197|mm}}，雌蝦比雄蝦大<ref name="Muncy"/>，觸鬚的長度可達體長的三倍。它通體青白色，體側泛粉，有黑色斑點<ref name="SI">{{cite web|url=https://www.sms.si.edu/irlspec/Penaeu_setife.htm|title=''Litopenaeus setiferus''|publisher=[[Smithsonian_Marine_Station_at_Fort_Pierce|Smithsonian Marine Station at Fort Pierce]]|work=Field Guide to the Indian River Lagoon|accessdate=October 2, 2018}}</ref>。
==生態==
白濱對蝦一般分佈于潮間帶和河口地區的溫水中，低於{{convert|3|C|F}}的水中無法生存，僅在溫度超過{{convert|20|C|F}}时生長<ref name="Muncy"/><ref name="SI"/>。它是一種雜食動物，以海草和生物碎屑為食<ref name="Davis">{{cite web|url=https://studylib.net/doc/8220586/a-generalized-food-web-for-lake-pontchartrain-in-southeas...|title=A Generalized Food Web for Lake Pontchartrain in Southeastern Louisiana|date=July 2009|author=Christopher D. Davis|accessdate=October 2, 2018|publisher=[[Lake_Pontchartrain_Basin_Foundation|Lake Pontchartrain Basin Foundation]]}}</ref>。[[红鼓鱼|红鼓鱼]]和很多海龜為其天敵<ref name="Davis"/>。

==捕撈==
大西洋沿岸的[[美國原住民|美國原住民]]本來就有捕食白濱對蝦的傳統<ref name="Riekerk">{{cite web|url=http://nerrs.noaa.gov/Doc/SiteProfile/ACEBasin/html/resource/commfish/cfshmpfh.htm|title=Commercial Fisheries: Shrimp|publisher=[[National_Oceanic_and_Atmospheric_Administration|National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration]]|work=Characterization of the Ashepoo-Combahee-Edisto (ACE) Basin, South Carolina|accessdate=May 11, 2011|author=G. Riekerk}}</ref>，殖民者到來之後也將其當做一種美食<ref name="Riekerk"/>，美國的商業捕撈出現於1709年之前。<ref name="Muncy"/>

'''苊钠'''是一种有机化合物，可由金属[[钠|钠]]和[[苊|苊]]在[[四氢呋喃|四氢呋喃]]中反应得到。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ic00201a010|issue=7|doi=10.1021/ic00201a010|pages=1010–1018|title=The clusters [Co8S6(SPh)8]4-,5-: preparations, properties, and structural relationship of near-cubic Co8(.mu.4-S)6 cores to the clusters in synthetic pentlandite|volume=24|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|date=1985-03|issn=0020-1669|language=EN|accessdate=2018-07-23|author=G. Christou, K. S. Hagen, J. K. Bashkin, R. H. Holm}}</ref>苊钠是一种还原剂，如可以将六价钼的氢三(3-异丙基吡唑-1-基)硼酸盐配合物还原为五价：<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ja952525a|issue=12|doi=10.1021/ja952525a|pages=2912–2921|title=Complexes Containingcis-[MoVO2]+andcis-[MoVO(OH)]2+Centers|volume=118|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|date=1996-01|issn=0002-7863|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-23|author=Zhiguang Xiao, Robert W. : Na(Acn) + LMo<sup>VI</sup>O<sub>2</sub>(SPh) + PPh<sub>4</sub>Cl → Acn + PPh<sub>4</sub>[LMo<sup>V</sup>O<sub>2</sub>(SPh)] + NaCl

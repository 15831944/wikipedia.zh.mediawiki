'''聖誕節麵包'''（{{lang-nl|Kerststol}}）是一種荷蘭傳統的橢圓形麵包<ref>{{cite web|last = Engelbrecht|first=Karin|title = Kerststol (Dutch Christmas Bread)|publisher=About.com: Dutch food|url=http://dutchfood.about.com/od/glossaryhijk/g/Kerststol.htm|accessdate = 2009-01-21}}</ref><ref>The best kerststol of 2008 was baked by Bakker Nijkamp uit Holten. In 2012 and 2013 Baker Gommans from Sevenum won the title. see {{cite web|title=Bakkerij Holten maakt 's lands beste kerststol|publisher = De Stentor|date=2008-12-12|url=http://www.destentor.nl/regio/deventer/4192956/Bakkerij-Holten-maakt-s-lands-beste-kerststol.ece|accessdate=2009-01-21}}</ref>。基本是由商業酵母麵團，搭配果乾、葡萄乾、桑特醋栗、檸檬和橙、果皮屑、水、牛奶、奶油、食糖、香莢蘭、肉桂和白蘭地組成，更豪華的種類可能包括切碎的[[核桃|核桃]]、[[扁桃|扁桃]]或[[榛子|榛子]]<ref>{{cite web|title = Kerststol of kerstbrood|publisher = Mijn Receptenboek| date=2007-10-13|url=http://www.mijnreceptenboek.nl/recept/speciale_gelegenheden/kerst/kerststol-of-kerstbrood-4190.html|accessdate=2009-01-21}}</ref>。

'''聖座與伊拉克庫爾德斯坦關係'''是指[[聖座|聖座]]與[[伊拉克庫爾德斯坦|伊拉克庫爾德斯坦]]地區之間的雙邊關係，雙方都沒有向對方派駐代表，但雙方的領袖之間曾經進行過交流。

==歷史==
庫爾德斯坦地區總統[[馬蘇德·巴爾扎尼|馬蘇德·巴爾扎尼]]分別在2005年11月、2011年2月和2014年5月在[[梵蒂岡城|梵蒂岡城]]與[[教宗若望·保祿二世|教宗若望·保祿二世]]、[[教宗本篤十六世|教宗本篤十六世]]和[[教宗方濟各|教宗方濟各]]進行正式訪問<ref>{{cite news|title=President|url=http://www.presidency.krd/english/pdisplay.aspx?sm=oYjJbpqKulY=|accessdate=2017-08-09|agency=Kurdistan Region Presidency|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170809191751/http://presidency.krd/english/pdisplay.aspx?sm=oYjJbpqKulY=#|archive-date=2017-08-09|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Pope Benedict commends President Barzani for promoting religious tolerance|url=http://cabinet.gov.krd/a/d.aspx?s=02010100&l=12&r=223&a=38998&s=010000|accessdate=2017-08-09|agency=Kurdistan Regional Government|date=2011-02-24|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170810171320/http://cabinet.gov.krd/a/d.aspx?s=02010100&l=12&r=223&a=38998&s=010000#|archive-date=2017-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=President Barzani Meets Pope Francis at the Vatican|url=http://www.presidency.krd/english/articledisplay.aspx?id=M1myz0RzT44=|accessdate=2017-08-09|agency=Kurdistan Region Presidency|date=2014-05-31}}</ref>。

2015年3月，巴爾扎尼向教宗方濟各討論庫爾德地區的[[人道主義|人道主義]]危機，又請求教宗鼓勵國際社會對庫爾德斯坦地區提供[[人道援助|人道援助]]<ref>{{cite news|title=Iraqi Kurdistan prime minister asks Pope Francis to encourage international community|url=http://www.romereports.com/en/2015/03/02/iraqi-kurdistan-prime-minister-asks-pope-francis-to-encourage-international-community/|accessdate=2017-08-09|agency=Rome Reports|date=2015-03-02}}</ref>。教宗向庫爾德斯坦地區捐款，以援助當地流離失所的[[基督教徒|基督教徒]]<ref>{{cite news|title=Pope makes donation to help displaced Christians in Iraq and Nigeria|url=http://www.catholicherald.co.uk/news/2015/03/30/pope-makes-donation-to-help-displaced-christians-in-iraq-and-nigeria/|accessdate=2017-08-09|agency=Catholic Herald|date=2015-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160417202814/http://www.catholicherald.co.uk/news/2015/03/30/pope-makes-donation-to-help-displaced-christians-in-iraq-and-nigeria/#|archive-date=2016-04-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>。同月，包括[[費爾南多·斐洛尼|費爾南多·斐洛尼]][[樞機|樞機]]在內的[[萬民福音部|萬民福音部]]代表團作為教宗特使訪問庫爾德斯坦地區首府[[埃爾比勒|埃爾比勒]]，是繼2014年8月第二次到當地進行訪問<ref>{{cite news|title=A sweet sign of hope: Pope's envoy returns to Iraq with Easter cake|url=http://www.catholicnewsagency.com/news/a-sweet-sign-of-hope-popes-envoy-returns-to-iraq-with-easter-cake-65935/|accessdate=2017-08-09|agency=Catholic News Agency|date=2015-03-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170423065847/http://www.catholicnewsagency.com/news/a-sweet-sign-of-hope-popes-envoy-returns-to-iraq-with-easter-cake-65935/#|archive-date=2017-04-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。 

2016年，教宗方濟各向埃爾比勒一家天主教慈善機構捐贈了11萬[[美元|美元]]，用於為患有[[慢性疾病|慢性疾病]]的伊拉克難民提供藥品<ref>{{cite news |author1=Inés San Martín |title=Pope donates $110,000 to clinic for Iraqi refugees in Erbil |url=https://cruxnow.com/global-church/2016/06/19/pope-donate-110000-clinic-iraqi-refugees-erbil/ |accessdate=2018-11-23 |agency=Crux |date=2016-06-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160620193951/https://cruxnow.com/global-church/2016/06/19/pope-donate-110000-clinic-iraqi-refugees-erbil/# |archive-date=2016-06-20 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，以及通過國際組織「援助有需要教會」向流離失所的伊拉克基督徒捐款<ref>{{cite news|title=Pope Francis sends vestments, financial aid to Iraqi Christians|url=http://www.catholicnewsagency.com/news/pope-francis-sends-vestments-financial-aid-to-iraqi-christians-42028/|accessdate=2017-08-09|agency=Catholic News Agency|date=2016-05-31|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170222152738/http://www.catholicnewsagency.com/news/pope-francis-sends-vestments-financial-aid-to-iraqi-christians-42028/#|archive-date=2017-02-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

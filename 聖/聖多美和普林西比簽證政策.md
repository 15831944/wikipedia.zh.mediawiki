==落地签==
{{flag|China}}，{{flag|Hong Kong}}，{{flag|Macau}}签发的普通护照持有人可在抵达时获得有效期为15天的签证。<ref>"[https://macauhub.com.mo/2017/05/11/governo-de-sao-tome-e-principe-concede-visto-de-entrada-aos-cidadaos-da-china/ Government of São Tomé and Príncipe grants entry visa to citizens of China]". ''Macau Hub''. 11 May 2017.</ref>

==电子签==
需要签证的国家的国民可以通过eVisaST系统在线获取签证。<ref>"[http://www.smf.st/evisa/index.php#EN-lang eVisa STP]" (Portuguese). ''Serviço de Migração e Fronteiras''.</ref>电子签将在7个工作日内受理。<ref>"[https://web.archive.org/web/20130613082223/http://www.smf.st/faq.html Serviço de Migração e Fronteiras]" (Portuguese). ''Serviço de Migração e Fronteiras''. Archived from [http://www.smf.st/faq.html the original] on 13 June 2013.</ref>

==替代签证==

[[美国|美国]]或[[申根区|申根区]]成员国签发的签证或居留许可持有人可免签入境15天。

==过境免签==
在同一天内有确认前往第三国的航班机票的乘客。乘客必须留在机场的国际过境区，并有下一个目的地所需的文件。

'''聖昆汀主教座堂''' <ref>[http://www.gcatholic.org/churches/europe/3142.htm Cathedral of St. Quentin in Hasselt]</ref>（{{lang-fr| Cathédrale Saint-Quentin de Hasselt}}，{{lang-nl|Sint-Quintinuskathedraal}}），又稱哈瑟爾特主教座堂，是比利時北部城市[[哈瑟爾特|哈瑟爾特]]一座重要的羅馬天主教教堂<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.ve/books?id=6o7pAgAAQBAJ&pg=PT65&dq=Cath%25C3%25A9drale+Saint-Quentin+de+Hasselt&hl=es&sa=X&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=Cath%2525C3%2525A9drale%252520Saint-Quentin%252520de%252520Hasselt&f=false|title=La Promesse d'Almache: Roman régional|last=Dantinne|first=Alain|date=2014-02-18|publisher=Primento|isbn=9782874892646|language=fr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.ve/books?id=v3FHAQAAIAAJ&q=Cath%25C3%25A9drale+Saint-Quentin+de+Hasselt&dq=Cath%25C3%25A9drale+Saint-Quentin+de+Hasselt&hl=es&sa=X&redir_esc=y|title=Liège et Bourgogne|last=Lejeune|first=Jean|last2=wallon|first2=Musée de l'art|date=1968-01-01|publisher=Le Musée|language=fr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.ve/books?id=m0KDA2O4IzMC&pg=PA393&dq=Cath%25C3%25A9drale+Saint-Quentin+de+Hasselt&hl=es&sa=X&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=Cath%2525C3%2525A9drale%252520Saint-Quentin%252520de%252520Hasselt&f=false|title=Belgique|date=2005-01-01|publisher=Lannoo Uitgeverij|isbn=9789020963434|language=fr}}</ref>。1967年，這座教堂升格為主教座堂。教堂於11世紀開始建設，興建耗時兩個世紀。

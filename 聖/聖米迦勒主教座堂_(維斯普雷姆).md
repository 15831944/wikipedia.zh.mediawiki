'''聖米迦勒主教座堂'''<ref>[http://www.gcatholic.org/churches/europe/1140.htm St. Michael’s Cathedral Basilica]</ref>（{{lang-hu|Szent Mihály székesegyház}}），又稱維斯普雷姆主教座堂<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2fIww8GHA2AC|title=Contents and Addresses of Hungarian Archives: With Supplementary Information for Research on German-Speaking Ancestors from Hungary|last=Brandt|first=Edward Reimer|last2=Brandt|first2=Di|date=2009-06-01|publisher=Genealogical Publishing Com|isbn=9780806346076|language=en}}</ref>，是[[匈牙利|匈牙利]]城市[[維斯普雷姆|維斯普雷姆]]的一座[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]的主教座堂<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=urIXKGuEHCEC|title=Nationalism and the Crowd in Liberal Hungary, 1848-1914|last=Freifeld|first=Alice|date=2000-07-17|publisher=Woodrow Wilson Center Press|isbn=9780801864629|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=8uJVS6cBOrgC|title=Queens and Queenship in Medieval Europe: Proceedings of a Conference Held at King's College London, April 1995|last=Duggan|first=Anne|date=1997-01-01|publisher=Boydell Press|isbn=9780851158815|language=en}}</ref>。考古發現證明這裡在1001年之前就已經有一座教堂。1991年，教皇[[若望·保祿二世|若望·保祿二世]]將這座教堂升格為小聖殿。

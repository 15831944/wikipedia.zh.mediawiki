Sometimes it is simply referred to as the ''St. Petersburg Synagogue''.</ref>是歐洲第二大[[猶太會堂|猶太會堂]]<ref>http://www.inyourpocket.com/st-petersburg-en/Grand-Choral-Synagogue-of-St-Petersburg_10264v</ref>。聖彼得堡大合唱猶太會堂興建於1880年至1888年，奉獻於1893年<ref name=history>[http://eng.jewishpetersburg.ru/modules.php?name=Content&pa=showpage&pid=270#ja21 History of the Grand Choral Synagogue of Saint Petersburg.]</ref>。

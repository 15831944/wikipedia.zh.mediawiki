'''聖昆丁州立監獄'''（{{lang-en|San  Quentin State Prison}}）是一座{{le|加利福尼亚州矫正与康复部|California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation}}建立的男子{{le|州立監獄|state prison}}，位於美國加利福尼亞州{{le|聖昆丁|San Quentin, California}}。1852年7月開門，是加州最老的監獄。

此監獄是加利福尼亞唯一男[[死刑犯|死刑犯]]的監獄<ref name=CDCRSQ>{{cite web | author=San Quentin State Prison (SQ) | title=Mission Statement | url=http://www.cdcr.ca.gov/Visitors/Facilities/SQ.html | publisher=California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation | year=2009 | accessdate=2009-08-20 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090806152712/http://www.cdcr.ca.gov/Visitors/Facilities/SQ.html | archivedate=2009-08-06 }} </ref><ref name=FimriteDeathRow>{{cite news | author=Fimrite, Peter | url=http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/c/a/2005/11/20/INGFUFHCFL56.DTL | title=Inside death row. At San Quentin, 647 condemned killers wait to die in the most populous execution antechamber in the United States | work=The San Francisco Chronicle | date=20 November 2005 | accessdate=2009-08-20}}</ref>，有一間毒氣室。但從1996年開始，只通過[[注射死刑|注射死刑]]執行死刑。這個監獄在電影電視上出現；是很多書籍的題材；舉行過音樂會；囚禁過許多惡名昭彰的犯人。

'''聖安東尼教堂'''<ref>[http://www.cicts.org/default.asp?id=388  «Catholic Masses outside Jerusalem»]</ref>（{{lang-he|כנסיית אנטוניוס הקדוש}}）是[[以色列|以色列]][[中央區_(以色列)|中央區]]城市[[特拉維夫|特拉維夫]]南部郊區衛星城[[雅法|雅法]]的一座[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]的[[教堂|教堂]]<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_Gmvypiz49gC|title=Foreign Workers in Israel: Global Perspectives|last=Drori|first=Israel|date=2009-01-21|publisher=SUNY Press|isbn=9780791477090|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5ZJR9R0OaJoC|title=City Guide Tel Aviv|last=Goldman|first=Lisa|date=2008-01-01|publisher=Greenleaf Book Group|isbn=9789659099733|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://catholicchurch-holyland.com/?p=3633|title=The Catholic Church Of The Holy Land » Territorial Parishes Israel|website=catholicchurch-holyland.com|access-date=2016-05-20}}</ref>。這座教堂外觀是哥特復興式建築，竣工於1932年。教堂主要服務菲律賓人外國勞工，提供阿拉伯語和英語的宗教服務。

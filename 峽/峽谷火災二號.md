'''峽谷火災二號'''（Canyon Fire 2）又稱'''峽谷二號火災'''，是發生於[[加州|加州]][[橙縣_(加利福尼亞州)|橘郡]][[安納罕市|安納罕市]]的[[安納罕嶺|安納罕嶺]]。起火時間在太平洋時間早上9:20，起火點約在[[91號加利福尼亞州州道|91號加利福尼亞州州道]]與石膏峽路（Gypsum Canyon Road）交叉口附近，一路延燒穿越{{le|241號加利福尼亞州州道|California State Route 241}}。火勢一發不可收拾，燬損民宅。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://beta.latimes.com/local/lanow/la-me-anaheim-hills-fire-20171009-story.html|title=At least four homes burn as fast-moving brush fire hits Anaheim Hills|last1=Do|first1=Anh|last2=Queally|first2=James|date=October 9, 2017|work=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=9 October 2017}}</ref>[[橘郡|橘郡]][[安那翰_(加利福尼亞州)|安那翰]]與[[塔斯廷|塔斯廷]]約有4,000居民遭強制疏散。<ref name = KTLA1 />

當日中午時分，火場廣達800英畝。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ocregister.com/2017/10/09/brush-fire-burning-25-acres-in-anaheim-hills/|title=Canyon Fire 2 in Anaheim Hills up to 800 acres, forcing evacuations and damaging at least 6 homes|date=October 9, 2017|work=Orange County Register|accessdate=9 October 2017}}</ref> 及至2:30，已達2,000英畝，0%受到控制。<ref name="ABC">{{cite news|url=http://abc7.com/anaheim-hills-fire-explodes-to-2000-acres-burns-homes/2512065/|title=Anaheim Hills fire explodes to 2K acres, burns homes, prompts evacuations|date=October 9,  2017|work=ABC-7 News|accessdate=9 October 2017}}</ref> 及至當地時間10月10日早上六時，已燒燬7,500英畝土地及24幢建築，包含十數棟民宅。此時據報火場已有5%受到控制。<ref name = KTLA1>{{cite news|url=http://ktla.com/2017/10/10/canyon-fire-2-homes-destroyed-as-thousands-remain-evacuated-in-anaheim-orange-and-tustin/|title=Canyon Fire 2: Homes Destroyed as Thousands Remain Evacuated in Anaheim, Orange and Tustin|date=October 10, 2017|work=KTLA|accessdate=10 October 2017}}</ref>

在[[迪斯奈樂園|迪斯奈樂園]]，肉眼可見火焰，且灰燼飄散到附屬旅館，但樂園保持開放。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ocregister.com/2017/10/09/disneyland-guests-can-see-canyon-fire-2-smoke-ash-and-its-making-things-look-really-spooky/|title=Disneyland guests can see Canyon Fire 2 smoke, ash|date=October 9, 2017|work=Orange County Register|accessdate=9 October 2017}}</ref>

該地[[科洛納_(加利福尼亞州)|科羅納]]與[[安那翰_(加利福尼亞州)|安那翰]]交界處曾於當年九月底發生命名為峽谷火災的一場山火，故本次火災命名為峽谷火災二號。之前的火災延燒2,600英畝但未損及民宅。<ref name="ABC" />

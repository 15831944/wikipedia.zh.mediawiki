《'''遺失的樂章2'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Lost Themes II'''''}}）是美國電影導演兼作曲家[[約翰·卡本特|約翰·卡本特]]的第二張專輯，於2016年4月15日發布，並由{{le|Sacred Bones Records|Sacred Bones Records}}唱片公司下發行<ref name="la_weekly_4-11-16">{{cite web|last1=Ohanesian|first1=Liz|title=Horror Filmmaker John Carpenter Is Loving His Second Career as a Musician|url=http://www.laweekly.com/music/horror-filmmaker-john-carpenter-is-loving-his-second-career-as-a-musician-6807287|website=LA Weekly|publisher=Voice Media Group|accessdate=17 October 2016|date=11 April 2016}}</ref>。這張專輯卡本特與兒子科迪·卡本特和他的乾兒子{{le|丹尼爾·戴維森|Daniel Davies (musician)}}一同創作與作曲。<ref name="rs_2-1-16">{{cite web|last1=Grow|first1=Kory|title='Halloween' Filmmaker John Carpenter Announces Album Sequel|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/halloween-filmmaker-john-carpenter-announces-album-sequel-20160201|website=Rolling Stone|publisher=Wenner Media LLC|accessdate=17 October 2016|date=1 February 2016}}</ref>

==參與人員==
*[[約翰·卡本特|約翰·卡本特]]：作曲、表演、監督
*科迪·卡本特：作曲、表演、監督
*{{le|丹尼爾·戴維森|Daniel Davies (musician)}}：作曲、表演、監督
*Jay Shaw：設計
*Kyle Cassidy：攝影

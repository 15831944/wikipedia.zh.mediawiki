'''陰莖異常勃起'''（priapism）是一種不正常[[陰莖|陰莖]][[勃起|勃起]]狀態，在這種狀態下陰莖雖然沒有受到與[[性慾|性慾]]相關的刺激，卻無法自然恢復疲軟狀態。一般與性慾無關的勃起持續四小時即算是異常勃起，這個時間標準得到醫學家的認可，較少以六個小時為標準的劃分方法。一般可分為低流血量和高流血量性陰莖異常勃起這兩種，成因分別是靜脈阻塞和動脈血注入，而80%到90%的病例都屬於前一種。<ref name="scielo.br">PRIAPISM – ETIOLOGY, PATHOPHYSIOLOGY AND MANAGEMENT, C. VAN DER HORST, HENRIK STUEBINGER, CHRISTOPH SEIF, DIETHILD MELCHIOR, F.J. MARTÍNEZ-PORTILLO, K.P. JUENEMANN; http://www.scielo.br/pdf/ibju/v29n5/18554.pdf</ref>

==病因==

其病因至今尚未明晰，但可以肯定的是，它至少與[[神經|神經]]與[[血管|血管]]相關，例如[[鐮刀型紅血球疾病|鐮刀型紅血球疾病]]就被認為是病因之一。此外陰莖異常勃起還可能與[[葡萄糖-6-磷酸脫氫酶缺乏症|葡萄糖-6-磷酸脫氫酶缺乏症]]有關，後者將導致[[煙醯胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸磷酸|煙醯胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸磷酸]]缺乏，繼而形成一氧化氮，而一氧化氮被認為可能是陰莖異常勃起的成因之一。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Finley DS | title = Glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase deficiency associated stuttering priapism: report of a case | journal = [[J_Sex_Med|J Sex Med]] | volume = 5 | issue = 12 | pages = 2963–6 |date=December 2008 | pmid = 18823322 | doi = 10.1111/j.1743-6109.2008.01007.x }}</ref> 而[[腺苷|腺苷]]水平提高會導致血管擴張，繼而誘發陰莖異常勃起。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Mi T, Abbasi S, Zhang H, Uray K, Chunn JL, Xia LW, Molina JG, Weisbrodt NW, Kellems RE, Blackburn MR, Xia Y | title = Excess adenosine in murine penile erectile tissues contributes to priapism via A2B adenosine receptor signaling | journal = [[J._Clin._Invest.|J. Clin. Invest.]] | volume = 118 | issue = 4 | pages = 1491–501 |date=April 2008 | pmid = 18340377 | pmc = 2267015 | doi = 10.1172/JCI33467 | url = http://www.jci.org/articles/view/33467/files/pdf?disposition=attachment | format = PDF | laysummary = http://www.newscientist.com/channel/health/dn13461-relief-in-sight-for-sufferers-of-constant-erections.html | laysource = New Scientist }}</ref>

針對鐮刀型紅血球疾病引起的陰莖異常勃起，高壓氧療法在一些患者身上取得過成效。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Macaluso JN | title = Priapism: Update for the non-urologist | journal = Sexual Medicine Today | volume = 9 | pages = 11–15 | year = 1985 }}</ref> 一些[[狂犬病|狂犬病]]患者也曾出現陰莖異常勃起的症狀。最常見的導致此病的藥物是用於[[海綿竇|海綿竇]]內注射的藥物，如[[罌粟鹼|罌粟鹼]]和[[前列地爾|前列地爾]]。有記錄的可誘發陰莖異常勃起的藥物還有[[降壓藥|降壓藥]]、[[安定藥|安定藥]]、[[抗抑鬱劑|抗抑鬱劑]]（[[曲唑酮|曲唑酮]]）、[[抗驚厥劑|抗驚厥劑]]、[[心境穩定劑|心境穩定劑]]、<ref name="Bansal">{{cite journal | author = Bansal S, Gupta SK | title = Sodium Valproate induced priapism in an adult with bipolar affective disorder | journal = [[Indian_Journal_of_Pharmacology|Indian Journal of Pharmacology]] | volume = 45 | issue = 6 | pages = 629 |date=November 2013 | doi = 10.4103/0253-7613.121383}}</ref> [[抗凝血劑|抗凝血劑]]和娛樂毒品（酒精、海洛因和可卡因）。[[斑蝥|斑蝥]]等一些昆蟲的咬傷也可能會造成陰莖異常勃起。[[PDE-5|PDE-5]]抑製劑是現在常用的陰莖異常勃起預防藥。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Burnett AL, Bivalacqua TJ, Champion HC, Musicki B | title = Long-term oral phosphodiesterase 5 inhibitor therapy alleviates recurrent priapism | journal = [[Urology|Urology]] | volume = 67 | issue = 5 | pages = 1043–8 |date=May 2006 | pmid = 16698365 | doi = 10.1016/j.urology.2005.11.045 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Burnett AL, Bivalacqua TJ, Champion HC, Musicki B | title = Feasibility of the use of phosphodiesterase type 5 inhibitors in a pharmacologic prevention program for recurrent priapism | journal = [[J_Sex_Med|J Sex Med]] | volume = 3 | issue = 6 | pages = 1077–84 |date=November 2006 | pmid = 17100941 | doi = 10.1111/j.1743-6109.2006.00333.x }}</ref>

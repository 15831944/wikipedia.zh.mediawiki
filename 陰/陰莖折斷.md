'''陰莖折斷'''指的是覆蓋[[陰莖海綿體|陰莖海綿體]]的白膜其中之一或兩者全部破裂的情況。病因是[[陰莖|陰莖]]勃起後遭到劇烈的打擊。<ref name="Greenberg's Text-Atlas of Emergency Medicine">{{cite book|title=Greenberg's Text-Atlas of Emergency Medicine|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=tjZttRRH8H8C&pg=PA318|accessdate=15 October 2012|date=22 November 2004|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=978-0-7817-4586-4|page=318}}</ref> 有時也會造成[[尿道|尿道]]阻斷或陰莖背部神經、靜動脈血管受損。<ref name=Haas>{{cite journal |author=Haas CA, Brown SL, Spirnak JP |title=Penile fracture and testicular rupture |journal=World J Urol |volume=17 |issue=2 |pages=101–6 |date=April 1999 |pmid=10367369 |doi= |url=}}</ref>

陰道性交、[[肛交|肛交]]和粗暴的[[手淫|手淫]]是最常見的原因。<ref name="Greenberg's Text-Atlas of Emergency Medicine"/> 陰莖折斷後陰莖會迅速疲軟，并判隨著劇烈疼痛，常常也會出現不同類型的[[血腫|血腫]]。<ref name="Greenberg's Text-Atlas of Emergency Medicine"/>

==治療==

陰莖折斷屬於{{le|醫療應急|Medical emergency}}事故，需要立即進行手術對受損的陰莖進行修復。不進行手術治療可能有10–50%的機率導致[[勃起功能障礙|勃起功能障礙]]、永久性陰莖彎曲、尿道受損和性交疼痛。得到有效治療的患者的併發症率為11%。<ref name="Greenberg's Text-Atlas of Emergency Medicine"/><ref name="PeitzmanRhodes2007">{{cite book|author1=Andrew B. Peitzman|author2=Michael Rhodes|author3=C. William Schwab|coauthors=Donald M Yealy, Timothy C Fabian|title=The trauma manual: trauma and acute care surgery|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=JnTMQOMcYZwC&pg=PA305|accessdate=18 April 2010|date=1 September 2007|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=978-0-7817-6275-5|pages=305–}}</ref>

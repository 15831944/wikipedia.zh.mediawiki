'''陰道避孕環'''（Contraceptive vaginal ring）是一種[[生育控制|生育控制]]的裝置，是置入[[阴道内给药|阴道内]]的環狀裝置，會釋放藥物，達到避孕效果。

陰道避孕環有很多種，其中有一種是{{link-en|炔雌醇/依托孕烯|ethinylestradiol/etonogestrel vaginal ring|炔雌醇/依托孕烯陰道環}}，也稱為NuvaRing（舞悠），是具可撓性的塑膠（[[乙烯/醋酸乙烯酯共聚物|乙烯/醋酸乙烯酯共聚物]]）環，每個NuvaRing會釋放低劑量[[黄体制剂|黄体制剂]]及[[雌激素|雌激素]]達三週的時間。全世界約有150萬名女性在使用<ref>{{cite web |author=Organon |date=2007-03-21 |title=NuvaRing now available for Australian women |url=http://www.organon.com/news/press_releases/2007_03_21_nuvaring_now_available_for_australian_women.asp |accessdate=2007-04-12 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。因為製造商曾隱瞞和NuvaRing可能會增加[[血栓|血栓]]風險的資訊，之前曾有多筆針對製造商的[[訴訟|訴訟]]<ref name=Mencimer>{{cite news|last=Mencimer|first=Stephanie|title=Is NuvaRing Dangerous?|url=https://www.motherjones.com/environment/2009/05/nuvaring-dangerous|accessdate=2013-06-30|newspaper=Mother Jones|year=2009}}</ref><ref name=lawsuit2013>{{cite news|title=New NuvaRing Blood-Clotting Lawsuit Allegations Update: Resource4thePeople Reports Lawsuits Continue to Be Filed and Trial on Track|url=http://www.wrcbtv.com/story/22640289/new-nuvaring-blood-clotting-lawsuit-allegations-update-resource4thepeople-reports-lawsuits-continue-to-be-filed-and-trial-on-track|accessdate=2013-06-30|newspaper=WRCB TV|year=2013|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130630124628/http://www.wrcbtv.com/story/22640289/new-nuvaring-blood-clotting-lawsuit-allegations-update-resource4thepeople-reports-lawsuits-continue-to-be-filed-and-trial-on-track#|archive-date=2013-06-30|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

目前也開發了[[孕酮陰道環|孕酮陰道環]]<ref name=Whit2014/>，由於不會影響[[母乳哺育|母乳哺育]]，可以供正在哺乳的女性使用<ref name=Whit2014>{{cite book|last1=Whitaker|first1=Amy|last2=Gilliam|first2=Melissa|title=Contraception for Adolescent and Young Adult Women|date=2014|publisher=Springer|isbn=9781461465799|page=98|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=vMQkBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA98|language=en}}</ref>。孕酮陰道環的副作用包括有[[阴道分泌物|阴道分泌物]]及排尿時疼痛<ref name=WHO2015Use>{{cite book|title=The selection and use of essential medicines: Twentieth report of the WHO Expert Committee 2015 (including 19th WHO Model List of Essential Medicines and 5th WHO Model List of Essential Medicines for Children).|date=2015|publisher=WHO|isbn=9789240694941|pages=334–335|url=http://apps.who.int/iris/bitstream/10665/189763/1/9789241209946_eng.pdf?ua=1|accessdate=2016-12-08|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161220085929/http://apps.who.int/iris/bitstream/10665/189763/1/9789241209946_eng.pdf?ua=1|archivedate=2016-12-20|df=}}</ref><!--，似乎沒有嚴重的副作用<ref name=WHO2015Use/><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Carr|first1=SL|last2=Gaffield|first2=ME|last3=Dragoman|first3=MV|last4=Phillips|first4=S|title=Safety of the progesterone-releasing vaginal ring (PVR) among lactating women: A systematic review.|journal=Contraception|date=2016-09-01|volume=94|issue=3|pages=253–61|pmid=25869631}}</ref>-->。

==避孕效果==
陰道避孕環會緩慢釋放激素到體內，主要是{{link-en|陰道給藥雌激素|vaginally administered estrogens}}及/或[[孕激素|孕激素]]（一種包括[[孕酮|孕酮]]在內的激素分類），和口服避孕藥的激素相同<ref name=":1">{{Cite journal|last=Angello|first=Anthony|date=2013-01-01|title=Vaginal Contraceptive Ring|url=http://web.b.ebscohost.com/ehost/detail/detail?vid=1&sid=13274cb4-841b-4916-835e-fbd7fc552ec3%40sessionmgr1&bdata=JnNpdGU9ZWhvc3QtbGl2ZQ%3d%3d#AN=36257422&db=hxh|journal=CRS - Adult Health Advisor|volume=|issue=|doi=|pmid=|accessdate=2016-09-16|via=EBSCOhost}}</ref>。激素作用的方式是停止排卵以及使子宮頸黏液變濃，使精子無法和卵子受精<ref name=":1"/>。理論上上述激素在受精卵著床之後也會影響受精卵，不過還沒有相關證據<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Rivera R, Yacobson I, Grimes D |year=1999 |title=The mechanism of action of hormonal contraceptives and intrauterine contraceptive devices |journal=Am J Obstet Gynecol |volume=181 |issue=5 Pt 1 |pages=1263–9 |pmid=10561657 |doi=10.1016/S0002-9378(99)70120-1|quote=The hormonal methods, particularly the low-dose progestin-only products and emergency contraceptive pills, have effects on the endometrium that, theoretically, could affect implantation. However, no scientific evidence indicates that prevention of implantation actually results from the use of these methods.}}</ref>。若使用三週，再移除不使用一週。每個陰道避孕環可以維持一個月避孕的效果。若是連續使用的避孕方式，使用者也可以連續使用四週，不過這種使用方式就不會有[[月經|月經]]<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.plannedparenthood.org/learn/birth-control/birth-control-vaginal-ring-nuvaring|title=NuvaRing Effectiveness {{!}} Birth Control Vaginal Ring|website=www.plannedparenthood.org|access-date=2016-09-16}}</ref>。在額外的這一週內，血清中的激素濃度仍然會在有避孕效果的範圍<ref name="arhp2014">{{Cite web|url=http://www.arhp.org/Publications-and-Resources/Quick-Reference-Guide-for-Clinicians/choosing/Vaginal-Ring|title=Choosing a Birth Control Method|last=Phelps|first=Rachel|date=2014-06-01|website=Association of Reproductive Health Professionals|publisher=Bayer Healthcare Pharmaceuticals, Conceptus, Inc., and Teva Pharmaceuticals|access-date=2016-09-16}}</ref>。

和複合式避孕丸比較，陰道避孕環可以將月經週期調整的比較好，也可以治療[[經血過多|經血過多]]的問題<ref name=":3">{{Cite journal|last=Dahiya|first=P|date=2016-05-20|title=Efficacy of combined hormonal vaginal ring in comparison to combined hormonal pills in heavy menstrual bleeding|journal=Eur J Obstet Gynecol Reprod Biol.|volume=203|doi=10.1016/j.ejogrb.2016.05.009|pmid=27285306|via=PubMed|pages=147–51}}</ref>。不過這二種方式對生育年齡內的婦女而言都是有效的短期治療方式。陰道避孕環會讓正常陰道的分泌物增加<!--、使體重減輕-->、減輕[[经前综合症|经前综合症]]，不過偶爾也會有[[陰道炎|陰道炎]]、一些和裝置相關問題，以及[[白帶_(生理)|白帶]]<ref name=":1" /><ref name="arhp2014" /><ref name=":3" />。陰道避孕環會釋放雌激素，因此可能會增加心臟病、中風及其他嚴重的副作用<ref name=":2" />。有些藥物或是食物補充品，像是抗生素[[利福平|利福平]]、抗真菌劑[[灰黃黴素|灰黃黴素]]、抗癲癇藥、[[貫葉連翹|貫葉連翹]]及[[人類免疫缺陷病毒|HIV]]藥物，會降低陰道避孕環的效果<ref name=":2" />。陰道避孕環無法保護使用者不罹患[[性感染疾病|性感染疾病]]。唯一有此效果的避孕器材是乳膠或是聚氨酯的[[避孕套|避孕套]]<ref name=":1" />。

陰道避孕環若依標準方式使用，其失敗率為0.3%，若經常使用，失敗率為9%<ref name="arhp2014" /><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Trussell|first=J|date=2011|title=Contraceptive failure in the United States|journal=Contraception|volume=83|issue=5|doi=10.1016/j.contraception.2011.01.021|pmid=21477680|via=PubMed|pmc=3638209|pages=397–404}}</ref>。

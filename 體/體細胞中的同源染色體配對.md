'''體細胞中的同源染色體配對'''與早期的[[減數分裂|減數分裂]]配對類似，已在[[雙翅目|雙翅目]]昆蟲<ref name=":0">Metz, Charles W. (1916). "Chromosome studies on the Diptera. II. The paired association of chromosomes in the Diptera, and its significance". ''Journal of Experimental Zoology''. '''21''' (2): 213–279. ISSN [https://www.worldcat.org/issn/0022-104X 0022-104X]. doi:[https://doi.org/10.1002%2Fjez.1400210204 10.1002/jez.1400210204].</ref>（如[[黑腹果蝇|黑腹果蠅]]）與[[酵母|芽殖酵母]]<ref>Burgess, Sean M; Kleckner, Nancy; Weiner, Beth M (1999). "Somatic pairing of homologs in budding yeast: existence and modulation". ''Genes & Development''. '''13''': 1627–1641. ISSN [https://www.worldcat.org/issn/1549-5477 1549-5477]. doi:[https://doi.org/10.1101%2Fgad.13.12.1627 10.1101/gad.13.12.1627].</ref>中觀察到這一現象。在哺乳動物中除了[[幹細胞|幹細胞]]幾乎沒有配對現象的產生，並且幹細胞中的配對是受到生長信號調節，發生在特定部位的。

儘管減數分裂中的配對過程已經受到廣泛地研究，然而體細胞中發生的配對至今沒有很好地被理解，甚至還不清楚它在機制上是否與減數分裂中發生的配對有關。<ref>Copenhaver, Gregory P.; Bosco, Giovanni (2012). [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3359980 "Chromosome Pairing: A Hidden Treasure No More"]. ''PLoS Genetics''. '''8''' (5): e1002737. ISSN [https://www.worldcat.org/issn/1553-7404 1553-7404]. PMC [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3359980 3359980] . PMID [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22654678 22654678]. doi:[https://doi.org/10.1371%2Fjournal.pgen.1002737 10.1371/journal.pgen.1002737].</ref>

== 早期工作 ==
Metz在1916年首次觀察到體細胞中的同源染色體配對<ref name=":0" />，引用了在1907<ref>Stevens, N. M. (1907) The chromosomes of Drosophila ampelophila. Proc. VII Internat. Zool. Cong</ref>與1908年期間[[内蒂·史蒂文斯|內蒂·史蒂文斯]]在細胞系的實驗中關於同源染色體配對的描述<ref name=":1">Stevens, N. M. (1908). "A study of the germ cells of certain diptera, with reference to the heterochromosomes and the phenomena of synapsis". ''Journal of Experimental Zoology''. '''5''' (3): 359–374. ISSN [https://www.worldcat.org/issn/0022-104X 0022-104X]. doi:[https://doi.org/10.1002%2Fjez.1400050304 10.1002/jez.1400050304].</ref>：<blockquote>“在這些昆蟲的生活史中可能發生與減數分裂相聯繫的同源染色體配對。”（p.215）</blockquote>史蒂文斯注意到了這種現象在信息交流與遺傳方面的意義。<ref name=":1" />

== 近年來通過高通量篩選的發現 ==
1998年，確定在黑腹果蠅中同源染色體配對發生在獨立的起始位點。<ref>Apte, Manasi S.; Meller, Victoria H. (2012). "Homologue Pairing in Flies and Mammals: Gene Regulation When Two Are Involved". ''Genetics Research International''. '''2012''': 1–9. ISSN [https://www.worldcat.org/issn/2090-3154 2090-3154]. doi:[https://doi.org/10.1155%2F2012%2F430587 10.1155/2012/430587].</ref><ref>Fung, Jennifer C.; Marshall, Wallace F.; Dernburg, Abby; Agard, David A.; Sedat, John W. (1998). "Homologous Chromosome Pairing inDrosophila melanogasterProceeds through Multiple Independent Initiations". ''The Journal of Cell Biology''. '''141''' (1): 5–20. ISSN [https://www.worldcat.org/issn/0021-9525 0021-9525]. doi:[https://doi.org/10.1083%2Fjcb.141.1.5 10.1083/jcb.141.1.5].</ref>

2002年，第一次採用[[RNAi|RNAi]]篩選（基於DNA [[熒光原位雜交|FISH]]）來鑑別調節黑腹果蠅體細胞配對的基因<ref>Joyce, Eric F.; Williams, Benjamin R.; Xie, Tiao; Wu, C.-ting (2012). [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3349724 "Identification of Genes That Promote or Antagonize Somatic Homolog Pairing Using a High-Throughput FISH–Based Screen"]. ''PLoS Genetics''. '''8''' (5): e1002667. ISSN [https://www.worldcat.org/issn/1553-7404 1553-7404]. PMC [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3349724 3349724] . PMID [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22589731 22589731]. doi:[https://doi.org/10.1371%2Fjournal.pgen.1002667 10.1371/journal.pgen.1002667].</ref>，它們包括40個配對增強子基因和65個“抑制配對”基因，其中許多與人類基因同源。<ref>Copenhaver, Gregory P.; Bosco, Giovanni (2012). [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3359980 "Chromosome Pairing: A Hidden Treasure No More"]. ''PLoS Genetics''. '''8''' (5): e1002737. ISSN [https://www.worldcat.org/issn/1553-7404 1553-7404]. PMC [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3359980 3359980] . PMID [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/22654678 22654678]. doi:[https://doi.org/10.1371%2Fjournal.pgen.1002737 10.1371/journal.pgen.1002737].</ref>

'''财务部'''（{{lang-en|Department of Finance}}）是[[澳大利亚政府|澳大利亚政府]]下属的一个部门，成立于2013年9月18日，主要职能是帮助澳大利亚政府达到支出目标，为政府在预算案等方面提供协助<ref name="AAO Sep 2013">{{cite web|url=http://www.dpmc.gov.au/parliamentary/docs/aao_20130918.pdf |title=Administrative Arrangements Order |date=18 September 2013 |publisher=Commonwealth of Australia |accessdate=27 October 2013 |work=Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet |format=PDF |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131014052331/http://www.dpmc.gov.au/parliamentary/docs/aao_20130918.pdf |archivedate=14 October 2013 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=About the department |url=https://www.finance.gov.au/about-the-department/ |website=Department of Finance |accessdate=2019-02-12}}</ref>。该机构的总部位于[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]首都[[坎培拉|坎培拉]]政府中心区的约翰·戈顿大楼。

澳大利亚财务部的前身是财务及放松监管部（{{lang|en|Department of Finance and Deregulation}}）<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/national-affairs/tony-abbott-puts-broom-through-bureaucracy/story-fn59niix-1226721922686|title=Tony Abbott puts broom through bureaucracy |author=Packham, Ben|date=18 September 2013|newspaper=The Australian|accessdate=24 September 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite press release|title=The Coalition will restore strong, stable and accountable government|url=http://www.pm.gov.au/media/2013-09-18/coalition-will-restore-strong-stable-and-accountable-government|author=Tony Abbott|date=18 September 2013|publisher=Commonwealth of Australia|work=Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet|location=Canberra, Australia|accessdate=20 September 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=Wilson, Lauren|newspaper=The Australian|title=Coalition carves up the public service|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/national-affairs/coalition-carves-up-public-service/story-fn59niix-1226722363408|date=19 September 2013|accessdate=24 September 2013}}</ref>。在1976年-1997年之间，澳大利亚政府也曾设立过财务部<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/opinion/columnists/what-ails-treasury-cannot-be-fixed-with-a-night-of-the-long-knives/story-fn7078da-1226712315822#|title=What ails Treasury cannot be fixed with a night of the long knives|first=Henry|last=Ergas|newspaper=The Australian|date=6 September 2013|publisher=News Corp Australia}}</ref>。

澳大利亚的财务部（Department of Finance)的职责仅限于政府收支。与许多英语国家类似，澳大利亚主管经济政策的部门称为“Treasury”（[[澳大利亞財政部|财政部]]），澳大利亚的财政部大约相当于美国的财政部（Department of the Treasury）和英国的财政部（HM Treasury）。澳大利亚财政部长（Treasurer）职权广泛，而财务部长（Minister for Finance）的职责相对狭小。

==历史上的对应部委==
*财务部（Department of Finance）（1976-1997）
*行政服务部（Department of Administrative Services）（1994-1997）
*财务及行政部（Department of Finance and Administration）（1997-2007）
*财务及放松监管部（Department of Finance and Deregulation）（2007-2013）

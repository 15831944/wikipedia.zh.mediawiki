'''艾芙琳·別列津'''（{{lang-en|Evelyn Berezin}}，{{bd|1925年|4月12日|2018年|12月8日}}），美國電腦程式設計師。她設計了第一套電腦[[文書處理器|文書處理器]]<ref name="McFadden">{{cite news | title = Evelyn Berezin, 93, Dies; Built the First True Word Processor | first = Robert D. | last = McFadden | work = [[紐約時報|紐約時報]] | url = https://www.nytimes.com/2018/12/10/obituaries/evelyn-berezin-dead.html | date = 2018-12-10 | accessdate = 2018-12-17 | language = en-US | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181216164539/https://www.nytimes.com/2018/12/10/obituaries/evelyn-berezin-dead.html | archive-date = 2018-12-16 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news | title = Word processor pioneer dies aged 93 | work = [[BBC新聞|BBC新聞]] | url = https://www.bbc.com/news/technology-46539934 | date = 2018-12-12 | accessdate = 2018-12-17 | language = en-GB | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181217151939/https://www.bbc.com/news/technology-46539934 | archive-date = 2018-12-17 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，被視為[[剪下、複製、貼上|-{zh:复制粘贴; zh-hant:複製貼上; zh-hans:复制粘贴}-]]發明者<ref name="ithome">{{cite web | title = 复制粘贴功能发明者去世，享年93岁 | author = 远洋 | website = [[IT之家|IT之家]] | url = https://www.ithome.com/html/it/400939.htm | date = 2018-12-18 | accessdate = 2018-12-19 | language = zh-cn | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181219182650/https://www.ithome.com/html/it/400939.htm | archive-date = 2018-12-19 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，並協助設計第一套電腦航空訂位系統<ref name = "wayne">{{cite book | title = American Women of Science Since 1900 | first = Tiffany K. | last = Wayne | publisher = ABC-CLIO | year = 2011 | isbn = 9781598841589 | pages = 234 | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=gPGZJ_YuMwgC}}</ref>。

== 早年經歷 ==
別列津出生在[[布朗克斯|布朗克斯]]一個俄羅斯裔[[猶太裔|猶太人]]家庭。她就讀布朗克斯-{zh:克里斯多福; zh-tw:克里斯多福; zh-hk:基斯杜化; zh-hans:克里斯托弗;}-·哥倫布中學（{{lang|en|Christopher Columbus High School}}）<ref name = "McFadden" />，並在1941年1月至[[亨特學院|亨特學院]]攻讀[[經濟學|經濟學]]，而非她所嚮往的[[物理學|物理學]]（當時一般人認為女性較適合讀經濟學）。[[第二次世界大戰|第二次世界大戰]]爆發後，她獲得[[紐約大學|紐約大學]][[史登商學院|-{zh-hant:史登; zh-hans:斯特恩}-商學院]]獎學金，加上在亨特學院和[[紐約大學坦登工程學院|布魯克林理工學院]]的免費課程，讓她得以攻讀物理學。那時她白天在國際印刷公司（{{lang|en|International Printing Company}}）研究部的[[流變學|流變學]]部門擔任全職助理，夜晚到學校上課，並在1946年獲得物理[[理學士|學士]]<ref name = "McFadden" />。

== 事業與貢獻 ==
別列津領受[[美國原子能委員會|美國原子能委員會]]的獎助金在[[紐約大學|紐約大學]]進行學士後研究<ref name="SION">{{cite web | title = About Sion Power | website = Sion Power | url = https://sionpower.com/about/ | accessdate = 2018-12-17 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181217202416/https://sionpower.com/about/ | archive-date = 2018-12-17 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。1951年她進入電子電腦公司（{{lang|en|Electronic Computer Corporation}}）擔任[[邏輯綜合|邏輯設計]]部門主管，並且是該公司唯一一位為設計該公司所研發電腦的邏輯電路的人<ref name = "SION" />。1957年電子電腦公司被安德伍德公司（原[[安德伍德打字機公司|安德伍德打字機公司]]）收購。她在安德伍德公司設計不少架構相似但有用在不同用途的電腦，包含[[美國陸軍|美國陸軍]]用計算射程的系統、雜誌派送系統，和第一部辦公室電腦<ref name="witi">{{cite web | title = Evelyn Berezin | work = WITI Hall of Fame | publisher = 國際科技婦女 | url = http://www.witi.com/center/witimuseum/halloffame/303047/Evelyn-Berezin-Management-Consultant-Brookhaven-Science-Associates/ | accessdate = 2018-12-17 | language = en-US | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181214072617/http://www.witi.com/center/witimuseum/halloffame/303047/Evelyn-Berezin-Management-Consultant-Brookhaven-Science-Associates/ | archive-date = 2018-12-14 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。在安德伍德公司發展停滯的1957年，別列津加入曾是[[西聯匯款|西聯匯款]]部門的{{lang|en|Teleregister}}<ref name="Teleregister">{{Cite web | title = Teleregister Corporation | publisher = Computer History Museum | url = http://www.computerhistory.org/brochures/t-z/teleregister-corporation/ | accessdate = 2018-12-17 | language = en-US | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181217062759/http://www.computerhistory.org/brochures/t-z/teleregister-corporation/ | archive-date = 2018-12-17 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

{{lang|en|Teleregister}}曾使用[[真空管|真空管]]電腦和機電開關為[[美國航空|美國航空]]建立{{lang|en|Reservisor}}訂位系統，該系統是世界上最早的航空公司訂位系統之一<ref name="Reservisor">{{Cite journal | title = The Reservisor automated airline reservation system: combining communications and computing | first = Jon | last = Eklund | journal = IEEE Annals of the History of Computing | url = https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/251855/ | doi = 10.1109/85.251855 | volume = 16 | issue = 1 | pages = 62－69 | date = 1994 | accessdate = 2018-12-17 | language = en-US | quote = Shortly after the beginnings of the computer in the mid-1940s, a machine appeared that was the first in a long line of important commercial systems integrating communications and processing: the Reservisor airline reservation system built by the Teleregister Corporation. | author =  | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181217202443/https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/251855/ | archive-date = 2018-12-17 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。別列津使用新的[[電晶體|-{zh:電晶體; zh-tw:電晶體; zh-cn:晶体管}-]]技術為[[聯合航空|聯合航空]]建立一套電腦化訂位系統，是當時最大的電腦系統之一。該系統連結60座城市，只需1秒便能得到回應<ref name ="witi" />。在{{lang|en|Teleregister}}時，她亦研發第一套電腦化銀行系統<ref name = "Domske">{{cite news | title = Evelyn Berezin, Computer Scientist Behind Groundbreaking Word Processor, Dies At 93 | first = Camila | last = Domonske | publisher = [[全國公共廣播電台|全國公共廣播電台]] | url = https://www.npr.org/2018/12/12/676024428/ | |date = 2018-12-12 | accessdate = 2018-12-17 | language = en-US}}</ref>。

1968年時，別列津構想一套[[文書處理器|文書處理系統]]以簡化祕書的工作。她在1969年設立{{lang|en|Redactron}}公司<ref name = "SION" /><ref>{{cite news | title = Muere Evelyn Berezin, creadora del primer procesador digital de textos | first = Sandro | last = Pozzi | publisher = [[國家報|國家報]] | url = https://elpais.com/tecnologia/2018/12/11/actualidad/1544546267_076535.html | format = html | trans_title = 第一套-{zh:數位; zh-tw:數位; zh-hk: 數碼; zh-cn:数字}-文書處理系統設計者艾芙琳·別列津逝世 | date = 2018-12-12 | accessdate = 2018-12-18 | language = es | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20181212182024/https://elpais.com/tecnologia/2018/12/11/actualidad/1544546267_076535.html | archivedate = 2018-12-12}}</ref>，<!--which became a public company and delivered thousands of systems to customers throughout its international marketing organization.-->主要產品為「資料祕書」（{{lang|en|Data Secretary}}），大小和一臺小型冰箱相若，沒有-{zh:螢幕; zh-tw:螢幕; zh-hk:熒幕; zh-hans:荧幕}-，藉由一臺{{link-en|IBM Selectric打字機|IBM Selectric typewriter}}輸入與印出文字<ref name ="McFadden" />。

1970年代時，雖然市場仍然強勁，但經濟遭遇嚴重的[[通貨膨脹|通貨膨脹]]。高昂的利率使像{{lang|en|Redactron}}等設備以承租為主的公司難以為繼。1976年時{{lang|en|Redactron}}被{{link-en|巴勒斯|Burroughs Corporation}}收購，成為其下的辦公室設備部門。別列津在那任職到1979年<ref name = "wayne" />。1980年，別列津擔任溫室管理公司（{{lang|en|Greenhouse Management Company}}）主席，該公司提供資金支持草創時期的科技公司<ref name ="SION" />。

別列津獲得{{link-en|艾德菲大學|Adelphi University}}和[[東密西根大學|東密西根大學]]授予[[名譽學位|名譽博士]]學位<ref name ="SION" />。她在{{link-en|信諾集團|Cigna}}、標準微系統（{{lang|en|Standard Microsystems}}）、{{link-en|考伯斯|Koppers}}和{{link-en|資料點|Datapoint}}等公司的董事會任職<ref name="CHM">{{cite web | title = Remembering CHM Fellow Evelyn Berezin | author = CHM Editorial Team | website = [[計算機歷史博物館|計算機歷史博物館]] | url = https://www.computerhistory.org/atchm/remembering-chm-fellow-evelyn-berezin/ | date = 2018-12-11 | accessdate = 2018-12-18 | language = en-US | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181214165203/https://www.computerhistory.org/atchm/remembering-chm-fellow-evelyn-berezin/ | archive-date = 2018-12-14 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，亦在[[石溪大學|石溪大學]]的石溪基金會、[[布魯克黑文國家實驗室|布魯克黑文國家實驗室]]和{{link-en|博伊斯·湯普森研究所|Boyce Thompson Institute}}等機構董事會任職<ref name="SION" />。

別列津設立別列津－威倫茨基金（{{lang|en|Berezin-Wilenitz Endowment}}）。依照她的[[遺囑|遺願]]，這筆資金由她的房產價值資助石溪大學在科學領域的系主任或教授，或相關研究經費<ref name = "StonyBrook">{{ cite web | title = Evelyn Berezin and Israel Wilenitz | website = [[石溪大學|石溪大學]] | url = https://stonybrook.giftplans.org/index.php?cID=247 | accessdate = 2018-12-19 | format = html | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20181219093233/https://stonybrook.giftplans.org/index.php?cID=247 | archivedate = 2018-12-19}}</ref>。此外，她和她的先夫伊斯雷·威倫茨（{{lang|en|Israel Wilenitz}}）設立山姆與蘿絲·別列津獎學金（{{lang|en|Sam and Rose Berezin Endowed Scholarship}}）以紀念她的雙親，此獎學金提供計畫攻讀科學、工程或數學學位-{zh:大學生; zh-tw:大學生; zh-hans:本科生}-全額學費。她們亦設伊斯雷·威倫茨基金（{{lang|en|Israel Wilenitz Endowment}}）資助石溪大學語言學系；該系曾授予威倫茨先生[[碩士|碩士學位]]<ref name ="StonyBrook" />。

== 個人生活 ==
別列津和丈夫伊斯雷·威倫茨結縭51年。威倫茨1922年於[[倫敦|倫敦]]出生，2003年2月20日逝世<ref name="wilenitz">{{cite web | title = Paid Notice:Deaths WILENITZ, ISRAEL | url = https://www.nytimes.com/2003/02/23/classified/paid-notice-deaths-wilenitz-israel.html | publisher = 紐約時報 | date = 2003-02-23 | accessdate = 2018-12-17 | language = en-US | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20181210205729/https://www.nytimes.com/2003/02/23/classified/paid-notice-deaths-wilenitz-israel.html | archive-date = 2018-12-10 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，別列津則於2018年12月8日因[[淋巴癌|淋巴癌]]以93歲高齡辭世<ref name = "ithome" /><ref name = "McFadden" />。

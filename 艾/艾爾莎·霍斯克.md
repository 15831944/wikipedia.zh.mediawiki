Harper’s Bazaar. 28-04-2015.</ref><ref>[https://www.thesun.ie/tvandshowbiz/710220/who-is-elsa-hosk-victorias-secret-model-and-former-professional-basketball-player-heres-what-we-know/ “Who is Elsa Hosk?

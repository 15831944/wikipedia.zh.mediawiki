== 早年生活 ==
钟出生在 Privett, [[漢普郡|Hampshire]]，是Gilian和Phillip钟的女儿。他的父亲是中国血统，而她的母亲是英国血统。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/tv-and-radio/2010/oct/03/alexa-chung-mtv-interview|title=Alexa Chung: 'I'm probably not the best role model'|first=Elizabeth|author=Day|publisher=The Guardian|date=2 October 2010|accessdate=29 September 2015}}</ref> 钟在当地Perins学校读中学，在Winchester，Peter Symonds学院读[[預科|六年级]]。她被[[伦敦国王学院|伦敦国王学院]] 录取学习英语，但在参加之前被一家模特公司选中。<ref name="lifeandhealth.guardian.co.uk">{{Cite news|author=Barton|first=Laura|url=https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2008/mar/31/television|title=She's the one|publisher=The Guardian|date=30 March 2008|accessdate=18 July 2014|location=London|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131208235850/http://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2008/mar/31/television|archivedate=8 December 2013|deadurl=no}}</ref>

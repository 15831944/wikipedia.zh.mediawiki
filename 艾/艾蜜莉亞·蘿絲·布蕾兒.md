'''艾蜜莉亞·蘿絲·布蕾兒'''（{{lang-en|Amelia Rose Blaire}}，{{bd|1987年|11月20日|||CatIdx=Y}}），[[美國|美國]][[電視|電視]]與[[電影|電影]][[演員|演員]]。出生於[[紐約|紐約]]。因出演[[HBO|HBO]]影集[[噬血真愛|噬血真愛]]裡的{{link-en|Willa Burrell|Willa Burrell}}以及《[[驚聲尖叫_(電視劇)|驚聲尖叫]]》中的常設角色派佩·蕭出名。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/movies/news/q-a-true-blood-actress-amelia-rose-blaire-on-vamping-it-up-20130730|title=Q&A: 'True Blood' Actress Amelia Rose Blaire on Vamping It Up|author=Sarene Leeds|work=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]]|date=July 30, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.popsugar.com/celebrity/True-Blood-Amelia-Rose-Blaire-Interview-Video-34929316|title=True Blood's Amelia Rose Blaire: I'd Choose Alcide Over Bill|author=Lindsay Miller|work=popsugar.com|date=June 6, 2014}}</ref>

== 個人生活 ==
艾蜜莉亞生於[[紐約市|紐約市]]並在[[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]被撫養長大。<ref name ="The Vault">{{cite web |url=http://www.trueblood-online.com/the-vaults-exclusive-interview-with-willa-burrell-amelia-rose-blaire/ |title=The Vault’s Exclusive Interview with Willa – Amelia Rose Blaire |work=The Vault |first= |last= |date=August 1, 2013 |accessdate=December 18, 2013}}</ref>

2017年6月，布蕾兒在[[推特|推特]]宣布與演員[[布萊恩·德查特|布萊恩·德查特]]訂婚。<ref>{{cite tweet|user=AmeliaRBlaire |author=Amelia Rose Blaire |number=873618207264854016 |date = June 10, 2017 |title=We're getting married!! @BryanDechart proposed in the gardens of Louis XIV's palace @CVersailles an incredible start to our new chapter 💝}}</ref>

'''艾爾河'''（River Aire）是英格蘭[[約克郡|約克郡]]的主要河流，源頭在北約克郡[[马哈姆谷地|马哈姆谷地]]，長{{convert|148|km|mi}}<ref>{{cite web|title=River Aire Factfile|url=http://www.environmentdata.org/fedora/repository/ealit:1270/OBJ/19001000.pdf|website=Environment Data|publisher=Environment Agency|accessdate=15 November 2016|page=2|format=PDF}}</ref>，在東約克郡[[Airmyn|Airmyn]]注入[[烏茲河|烏茲河]]。[[利茲|利茲]]附近的艾爾河被運河化，形成[[艾爾和考爾德通航運河|艾爾和考爾德通航運河]]。艾爾河上游三座水電站，即[[渡輪橋水電站|渡輪橋水電站]]、[[艾格伯勒水電站|艾格伯勒水電站]]和[[德拉克斯水電站|德拉克斯水電站]]<ref>{{Cite web|url =http://lubrafil.nl/phocadownload/Bollfilter/HEAVY%20DUTY%20PROTECTION%20FOR%20FGD%20SYSTEM%20-%20boll_eggborough%20-%206.18.2.pdf|title =Lubrafil|date =|accessdate =|website =|publisher =|last =|first =|deadurl =yes|archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160304082733/http://lubrafil.nl/phocadownload/Bollfilter/HEAVY%20DUTY%20PROTECTION%20FOR%20FGD%20SYSTEM%20-%20boll_eggborough%20-%206.18.2.pdf|archivedate =2016-03-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Ferrybridge C Power Station officially closes after 50 years|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-leeds-35927009|accessdate=15 November 2016|work=BBC News|date=31 March 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Reprieve for threatened Eggborough Power Station|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-york-north-yorkshire-35527220|accessdate=15 November 2016|work=BBC News|date=8 February 2016}}</ref>。

因原來的西約克郡重工業區位于該河流域，水質污染曾是艾爾河的一大問題<ref>{{cite web|title=A brief history of the River Aire|url=http://aireriverstrust.org.uk/a-brief-history-of-the-river-aire/|website=|publisher=Aire Rivers Trust|accessdate=24 November 2015}}</ref>。2007年約克郡水利局花費1.1億英鎊進行清理，使得水質問題得以改善，鱒魚、[[茴魚屬|茴魚]]和雅羅魚的出現更加頻繁，水獺和[[水䶄|水䶄]]等動物也回到了艾爾河流域<ref name=":0">{{cite web|title = Waste Water |url = https://www.yorkshirewater.com/household/services/waste-water#ls7|publisher= Yorkshire Water|accessdate = 21 October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title = River Water Quality |url = https://www.yorkshirewater.com/about-us/what-we-do/investment-environment/river-water-quality|publisher= Yorkshire Water|accessdate = 21 October 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title = Pollution is water under the bridge|url = http://www.keighleynews.co.uk/news/1650812.Pollution_is_water_under_the_bridge/|work= Keighley News|accessdate = 20 October 2015}}</ref>。此外流域内還設置了羅德利自然保護區（Rodley Nature Reserve）等。

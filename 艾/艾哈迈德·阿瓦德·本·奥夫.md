'''艾哈迈德·阿瓦德·本·奥夫'''（{{lang-ar|أحمد عوض بن عوف}}；{{bd|1957年|}}<ref>{{cite web|url=https://wikileaks.org/plusd/cables/08STATE61842_a.html|title=Cable: 08STATE61842_a|work=wikileaks.org|accessdate=28 October 2015}}</ref> ），[[苏丹|苏丹]]将军、政治家，自2019年2月起担任[[苏丹第一副总统|苏丹第一副总统]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.africanews.com/2019/04/11/sudan-protest-hub-africanews-updates/|title=Sudan protest hub: Bashir ousted, military outlines next steps|first=|last=AfricaNews|date=2019-04-11|website=Africanews}}</ref> ，曾任苏丹国防部部长。<ref>https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/world-leaders-1/pdfs/SU.pdf</ref>2019年初，反對時仼總統[[奥马尔·巴希尔|奥马尔·巴希尔]]的示威不斷。他發動[[2019年苏丹政变|2019年苏丹政变]]后，透過國營電視台宣布解散政府，拘捕巴席爾，並担任[[苏丹总统|过渡军事委员会]]主席，即臨時[[国家元首|国家元首]]。但由於示威活動仍未止，他在翌日辭職，由{{le|阿卜杜勒·法塔赫·阿卜杜勒拉赫曼·布尔汉|Abdel Fattah Abdelrahman Burhan}}中將繼仼过渡军事委员会主席。<ref name=euroapril12>https://www.euronews.com/2019/04/12/head-of-sudan-military-council-steps-down-one-day-after-long-time-leader-bashir-toppled-in</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sfgate.com/news/world/article/The-Latest-Sudanese-army-defends-its-ouster-of-13761972.php|title=The Latest: Sudan's post-coup transitional leader steps down|date=2019-04-12|website=SFGate}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dw.com/cda/en/sudans-interim-leader-promises-return-to-civilian-rule/a-48311358|title=Sudan's interim leader promises return to civilian rule - DW - 13.04.2019|first=Deutsche|last=Welle (www.dw.com)|website=DW.COM}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.alarabiya.net/en/News/middle-east/2019/04/12/Sudan-s-new-ruling-military-council-chief-steps-down.html|title=Sudan defense minister steps down as head of transitional military council|website=english.alarabiya.net}}</ref>

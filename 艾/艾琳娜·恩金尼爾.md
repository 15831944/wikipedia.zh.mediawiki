'''艾琳娜·恩金尼爾'''（[[英語|英語]]：'''Aryana Engineer'''；2001年3月6日<ref>[http://aryana-engineer.com/aryana_bio.php "Aryana Engineer Official Site"]. [http://web.archive.org/web/20091022101153/http://aryana-engineer.com/aryana_bio.php Archived] from the original on 22 October 2009. Retrieved 13 November 2009.</ref>─)，她是[[加拿大|加拿大]]的女演員，也是一名童星，她因在電影2009年《[[孤兒怨|孤兒怨]]》中飾演麥絲·柯曼而當紅。

==簡介==
恩金尼爾出生於加拿大溫哥華，她患有聽障，但她對美國式手語和唇語都很流利<ref>[http://www.newswire.ca/en/story/596635/one-inspires-many-a-celebration-of-ability-courage-and-the-human-spirit-at-opening-ceremony-for-the-2010-paralympic-winter-games "One Inspires Many: a celebration of ability, courage and the human spirit at Opening Ceremony for the 2010 Paralympic Winter Games"]. [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VANOC VANOC]. March 12, 2010. Retrieved April 19, 2013.</ref>，也因此賦予本片中的耳聾角色麥絲·柯曼有很棒的真實感，她也喜歡跳舞，尤其是爵士舞和芭蕾舞。

'''艾伦·邁克爾·沙塔内'''（{{lang-en|Allen Michael Chastanet}}），是一名[[聖盧西亞|聖盧西亞]]商人與政治人物，2016年起出任[[圣卢西亚总理|圣卢西亚总理]]<ref name="Observer 7 June">{{Cite news |title=Allen Chastanet sworn in new St Lucia PM |newspaper=Jamaica Observer |date=7 June 2016 |url=http://www.jamaicaobserver.com/news/Allen-Chastanet-sworn-in-new-St-Lucia-PM |accessdate=7 June 2016}}</ref>。他現時也是[[可可棕櫚酒店|可可棕櫚酒店]]的常務董事<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.stluciamirroronline.org/?p=812 |title=Chastanet Returns to Coco Palm |work=The St. Lucia Mirror |date=16 February 2013 |accessdate=29 July 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304102649/http://www.stluciamirroronline.org/?p=812 |archivedate=2016年3月4日 }}</ref>。

==個人生活==
沙塔内是商人邁克爾·沙塔内（Michael Chastanet）的兒子<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://htsstlucia.org/michael-chastanet-on-qualifications-saga/ |title=Michael Chastanet on qualifications saga |publisher=HTS Channel 4 |author=Brown, Jade |date=29 July 2015}}</ref>，後與拉奎爾·杜布雷-沙塔内（Raquel DuBoulay-Chastanet）結婚並育有兩名子女<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.jamaicaobserver.com/magazines/allwoman/24450_Happy-birthday-Digicel- |title=Happy birthday Digicel! |work=Jamaica Observer |date=22 April 2002 |accessdate=6 June 2016 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160625115836/http://www.jamaicaobserver.com/magazines/allwoman/24450_Happy-birthday-Digicel- |archivedate=2016年6月25日 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |title=Political Atheist |newspaper=St. Lucia Star |date=5 August 2013 |url=https://stluciastar.com/political-atheist/ |accessdate=6 June 2016}}</ref><ref name="Observer 7 June" />。

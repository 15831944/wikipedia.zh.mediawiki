'''艾許·安傑'''（{{lang-en|Asher Angel}}，{{bd|2002年|9月6日}}）<ref name="DisneyABCPress-Andi Mack-Bios">{{cite web|url=http://www.disneyabcpress.com/disneychannel/shows/andi-mack/bios/|title=Disney Channel – Andi Mack – Show Bios (Asher Angel)|website=Disney ABC Press|accessdate=2017-11-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171126201004/http://www.disneyabcpress.com/disneychannel/shows/andi-mack/bios/|archivedate=2017-11-26}}</ref>是一名[[美國|美國]]男演員。較著名的是在電視劇《[[安迪麥克|安迪麥克]]》中飾演喬納·貝克（{{lang|en|Jonah Beck}}），以及於2019年[[超級英雄電影|超級英雄電影]]《[[沙贊！_(電影)|沙贊！]]》中飾演少年[[驚奇隊長_(DC漫畫)|比利·貝特森]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=‘Shazam!’ Trailer Debuts at Comic-Con (Watch)|url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/shazam-trailer-comic-con-1202880244/|work=Variety|date=2018-07-21|accessdate=2019-03-26|first=Ellis|last=Clopton}}</ref>。

== 早期生活 ==
艾許·安傑出生於[[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]][[鳳凰城_(亞利桑那州)|鳳凰城]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=Actor Asher Angel enjoys his work on ‘Andi Mack’|url=https://www.parkrecord.com/entertainment/actor-asher-angel-enjoys-his-work-on-andi-mack/|work=ParkRecord.com|date=2017-08-01|accessdate=2019-03-26|first=Scott|last=Iwasaki|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170802030830/https://www.parkrecord.com/entertainment/actor-asher-angel-enjoys-his-work-on-andi-mack/|archivedate=2017-08-02}}</ref>，並住在[[仙谷_(亞利桑那州)|仙谷]]<ref name="Heightline">{{cite web|url=https://heightline.com/asher-angel-biography/|title=Asher Angel Biography, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Family|work=Heightline|accessdate=2018-07-15|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180715094407/https://heightline.com/asher-angel-biography/|archivedate=2018-07-15}}</ref><ref name="ArizonaJewishLife">{{cite web|url=http://azjewishlife.com/asher-angel-following-his-dream-from-desert-stages-to-disney/|title=ASHER ANGEL: FOLLOWING HIS DREAM FROM DESERT STAGES TO DISNEY|work=Arizona Jewish Life|date=2017-03-28|accessdate=2018-07-15|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180106184819/http://azjewishlife.com/asher-angel-following-his-dream-from-desert-stages-to-disney/|archivedate=2018-01-06}}</ref>。他是喬迪（{{lang|en|Jody}}）和可可·安傑（{{lang|en|Coco Angel}}）的大兒子，有著一個弟弟艾維（{{lang|en|Avi}}）和妹妹倫敦（{{lang|en|London}}）<ref name="FamousBirthdays">{{cite web|url=https://www.famousbirthdays.com/people/asher-angel.html|title=Asher Angel - Bio, Facts, Family|work=Famous Birthdays|accessdate=2018-07-15|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170527200954/http://www.famousbirthdays.com/people/asher-angel.html|archivedate=2017-05-27}}</ref>。艾許·安傑有英格蘭、蘇格蘭和[[猶太|猶太]]血統<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Asher Angel – Ethnicity of Celebs {{!}} What Nationality Ancestry Race|url=http://ethnicelebs.com/asher-angel|accessdate=2019-04-11|author=|date=2017-11-6|publisher=Ethnicity of Celebs}}</ref>。艾許·安傑的外祖母有英格蘭血統和蘇格蘭血統<ref name=":0" />。

== 職業生涯 ==
安傑出演的首部電影為2008年的《{{le|喬琳娜 (電影)|Jolene (film)|喬琳娜}}》<ref name="Heightline" />。他作為舞台劇演員開始了演藝生涯<ref>{{Cite web|title=Asher Angel Talks Andi Mack and Airheads|url=https://www.bsckids.com/2017/04/asher-angel-talks-andi-mack/|accessdate=2019-03-29|author=|date=2017-04-01|publisher=BSCKids}}</ref>。安傑7歲時，亞利桑那州的斯科茨代爾沙漠舞台劇院在為音樂劇《{{le|奧利佛 (音樂劇)|Oliver!|奧利佛}}》舉行試鏡，在父母允許的情況下，安傑去試鏡並獲得了一個角色<ref>{{Cite web|title=EXCLUSIVE COVER STORY: Asher Dov Angel|url=http://www.ysbnow.com/en/detail/59ed2721d74440c0747d519b/exclusive-cover-story:-asher-dov-angel|accessdate=2019-03-24|date=2017-10-22|publisher=YSBnow}}</ref>。之後，安傑在斯科茨代爾沙漠舞台劇院演出多部舞台劇，包括《{{le|小美人魚 (音樂劇)|The Little Mermaid (musical)|小美人魚}}》、《{{le|蘇斯博士 (音樂劇)|Seussical|蘇斯博士}}》、《{{le|歡樂滿人間 (音樂劇)|Mary Poppins (musical)|歡樂滿人間}}》、《{{le|魔法黑森林 (音樂劇)|Into the Woods|魔法黑森林}}》等音樂劇<ref name="ysbnow(2017-03-28)">{{cite web|url=http://www.ysbnow.com/en/detail/596d3040d74440124e7d50ce/watch-asher-dov-angel-as-flounder-in-an-on-stage-production-of-the-little-mermaid|title=Watch Asher Dov Angel As Flounder In An On-Stage Production of The Little Mermaid|work=ysbnow|date=2017-03-28|accessdate=2018-07-15|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180715094407/http://www.ysbnow.com/en/detail/596d3040d74440124e7d50ce/watch-asher-dov-angel-as-flounder-in-an-on-stage-production-of-the-little-mermaid|archivedate=2018-07-15}}</ref><ref name="ArizonaJewishLife" />。

12歲時，安傑在[[迪士尼頻道|迪士尼頻道]]電視劇《{{le|安迪麥克|Andi Mack}}》的試鏡中贏得了喬納·貝克（{{lang|en|Jonah Beck}}）一角，且為了便於該劇的拍攝，使他們全家都搬到了[[猶他州|猶他州]]<ref name="ArizonaJewishLife" />。

2017年11月6日，宣布安傑將在[[DC擴展宇宙|DC擴展宇宙]]電影《[[沙贊！_(電影)|沙贊！]]》中與[[柴克·萊威|柴克·萊威]]共同飾演主角[[驚奇隊長_(DC漫畫)|比利·貝特森]]，萊威將飾演他的成年版超級英雄<ref name="Shazam!">{{cite news|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/shazam-asher-angel-star-as-billy-batson-dc-movie-1055189|title='Shazam!' Finds Its Billy Batson with 'Andi Mack' Actor|work=The Hollywood Reporter|date=2017-11-06|accessdate=2017-11-07|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171107070958/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/shazam-asher-angel-star-as-billy-batson-dc-movie-1055189|archivedate=2017-11-07}}</ref><ref name="CosmicBookNews(2017-12-29)">{{cite news|url=https://www.cosmicbooknews.com/watch-asher-angel-say-shazam-black-adam-teased|title=Watch Asher Angel Say "Shazam!"; Black Adam Teased|last=McGloin|first=Matt|work=Cosmic Book News|date=2017-12-29|accessdate=2018-07-15|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180715094407/https://www.cosmicbooknews.com/watch-asher-angel-say-shazam-black-adam-teased|archivedate=2018-07-15}}</ref>。該片定於2019年4月5日在美國上映<ref name="DigitalSpy(2017-09-19)">{{cite news|url=http://www.digitalspy.com/movies/shazam/news/a838482/shazam-movie-2019-release-date-dceu-filming-2018/|title=Superhero movie Shazam! to start filming in early 2018|first1=Bea|last1=Mitchell|work=Digital Spy|publisher=Hearst Magazines UK|location=London, England|date=2017-09-19|accessdate=2017-09-20|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170921174657/http://www.digitalspy.com/movies/shazam/news/a838482/shazam-movie-2019-release-date-dceu-filming-2018/|archivedate=2017-09-21}}</ref><ref name="Instagram(2018-01-29)">{{cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BeipkbDAYpp/?taken-by=ponysmasher|first=David F.|title=Let’s go!⚡️|last=Sandberg|via=[[Instagram|Instagram]]|date=2018-01-29|accessdate=2018-02-05}}</ref>。

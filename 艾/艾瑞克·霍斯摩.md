'''艾瑞克·約翰·霍斯摩'''（{{lang-en|Eric John Hosmer}}, {{bd|1989年|10月24日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟聖地牙哥教士的一壘手。先前曾待過皇家隊。2008年大聯盟選秀，於第一輪第3順位被皇家隊選走。2011年登上大聯盟。2015年拿下世界大賽冠軍。2016年拿下明星賽MVP。2017年球季結束離開皇家隊，加入教士隊。
==職業生涯==
===堪薩斯市皇家===
====2011年====
2011年5月5日，皇家隊老將捕手Jason Kendall整季報銷，因而由霍斯摩遞補其空缺。<ref name="Kendall">{{cite news|url=http://www.kansascity.com/2011/05/05/2852531/royals-call-up-eric-hosmer.html|title=Royals call up Hosmer, send Ka'aihue to Omaha|last=Dutton|first=Bob|date=May 5, 2011|work=Kansas City Star|accessdate=January 19, 2012}}</ref>5月6日，霍斯摩登上大聯盟，代替原一壘手Kila Ka'aihue。<ref name="Bringing">{{cite web|url=http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/news/story?id=6488194|title=Royals bringing up 1B Eric Hosmer|work=ESPN|agency=Associated Press|accessdate=January 19, 2012}}</ref>他吞下2次三振，2次保送，1次盜壘。<ref name="debutgame">{{cite news|url=http://www.kansascity.com/2011/05/06/2855662/promoting-hosmer-could-turn-out.html|title=Promoting Hosmer could turn out costly for KC.|last=Mellinger|first=Sam|date=May 6, 2011|work=Kansas City Star|accessdate=January 19, 2012}}</ref>

5月11日，霍斯摩已成為球隊的第四棒。他面對洋基隊投手[[A·J·柏奈特|A·J·柏奈特]]擊出生涯首轟。他4月的打擊率為2成83。7月16日，他面對雙城隊終結者Matt Capps擊出再見全壘打。<ref name="gamewinner">{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/c/a/2011/07/16/SPVU1KAS9V.DTL|title=Eric Hosmer's homer in 9th lifts Royals over Twins|date=July 16, 2011|work=San Francisco Chronicle|agency=Associated Press|accessdate=January 19, 2012}}</ref>這支全壘打也讓雙城隊在隔天拔除了Capps的終結者位置。<ref name="replaced">{{cite news|url=https://www.usatoday.com/sports/baseball/al/2011-07-16-4054001520_x.htm|title=Nathan replaces Capps as Twins closer|work=USA Today|agency=Associated Press|accessdate=January 19, 2012|date=July 16, 2011}}</ref>他7月則拿下單月最佳新秀。<ref>{{cite web |url= http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20110803&content_id=22715848&notebook_id=22717372&vkey=notebook_kc&c_id=kc|title=Hosmer surprised by Rookie of the Month honor |first1=Dick |first2=Adam | last1=Kaegel |last2=Holt |date=August 4, 2011 |work=[[MLB.com|MLB.com]] |accessdate=August 3, 2011}}</ref>9月20日，他單場擊出5安。<ref name="fivehits">{{cite web|url=http://www.mlive.com/tigers/index.ssf/2011/09/brad_penny_rocked_as_tigers_dr.html|title=Brad Penny rocked as Tigers drop 10–2 decision to Kansas City Royals|last=Mayo|first=David|date=September 20, 2011|work=MLive.com|publisher=Michigan Live LLC.|accessdate=January 19, 2012}}</ref>他在9月再度拿下單月最佳新秀。<ref name="secondROM">{{cite news|url=http://kansascity.royals.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20110929&content_id=25389666&vkey=news_kc&c_id=kc|title=Hosmer improves Rookie of the Year resume|last=Casella|first=Paul|date=September 29, 2011|work=MLB.com|accessdate=May 3, 2012}}</ref>

他在美聯年度最佳新秀排行第三，僅次於光芒隊的Jeremy Hellickson與天使隊的[[馬克·川柏|馬克·川柏]]。他該季打擊率2成93，19轟73分打點。<ref name="Stats">{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/players/h/hosmeer01.shtml|title=Eric Homer|work=Baseball Reference|accessdate=January 19, 2012}}</ref>
====2012年====
2月18日，皇家隊與霍斯摩簽下一年合約。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kshb.com/dpp/sports/baseball/royals/royals-sign-four-more-including-hosmer-and-moustakas|title=Royals sign four more...|publisher=KSHB-TV website|date=February 18, 2012|accessdate=February 18, 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120324003720/http://www.kshb.com/dpp/sports/baseball/royals/royals-sign-four-more-including-hosmer-and-moustakas|archivedate=March 24, 2012|df=mdy-all}}</ref>在春訓期間就打下29分打點，長打率7成14。<ref name="yahooevans">{{cite web|url=https://sports.yahoo.com/blogs/fantasy-roto-arcade/royal-highness-eric-hosmer-realistically-crack-30-homers-202451036.html|title=Royal Highness: Can Eric Hosmer realistically crack 30 homers this year?|last=Evans|first=Brad|date=April 2, 2012|work=Yahoo! Sports|accessdate=April 24, 2012}}</ref>連「皇家先生」[[喬治·布列特|喬治·布列特]]都非常看好他以後的表現。堪薩斯市的報紙甚至譽他為「球隊的新招牌」、「未來的運動明星」等等。<ref name="faceof">{{cite news|url=http://www.kansascity.com/2012/03/31/3527375/hosmer-the-face-of-the-franchise.html|title=Hosmer: The face of the franchise|last=Dodd|first=Rustin|date=March 31, 2012|work=The Kansas City Star|accessdate=April 24, 2012}}</ref><ref name="nextKC">{{cite news|url=http://www.kansascity.com/2012/04/14/3554554/we-may-see-hosmers-best-years.html|title=We may see Hosmer's best years in KC|last=Mellinger|first=Sam|date=April 14, 2012|work=Kansas City Star|accessdate=April 24, 2012}}</ref>然而霍斯摩開季打得並不順，球隊甚至還一度11連敗。<ref name="currentstats">{{cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/player/_/id/30993/eric-hosmer|title=Eric Hosmer|work=ESPN.com|accessdate=April 24, 2012}}</ref>該季他打擊率2成32，14轟60分打點。
====2013年====
該年他拿下生涯首座金手套獎。<ref>[http://kansascity.royals.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20131029&content_id=63500994&vkey=pr_kc&c_id=kc Gordon, Hosmer, Perez win Rawlings Gold Glove Awards | MLB.com<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>該季他打擊率為3成02，17轟79分打點。
====2014年====
7月20日，他被紅襪隊投手[[喬恩·萊斯特|喬恩·萊斯特]]觸身球擊中手部。然而他持續忍痛出賽，最後他在7月31日一次的揮棒中受傷。X光顯示他手指骨碎裂，因此他缺席了4週的比賽。他該季打擊率2成70，9轟58分打點。

該年的外卡戰，霍斯摩5次打擊全數上壘。美聯分區賽第2場，他擊出關鍵2分砲助球隊贏球。他也成為史上第一位在同一年季後賽延長賽擊出三壘安打和全壘打的一壘手。<ref name=EliasSports>{{cite web|title=Elias Says...|url=http://espn.go.com/espn/elias|website=ESPN|publisher=ESPN Internet Ventures|accessdate=October 4, 2014}}</ref>
====2015年====
2月18日，霍斯摩與球隊簽下了2年1390萬美元的合約。<ref>{{cite web|title=1B Eric Hosmer avoids arbitration|url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/story/_/id/12344859/eric-hosmer-kansas-city-royals-agree-2-year-139-million-contract|website=[[ESPN|ESPN]]|accessdate=February 19, 2015}}</ref>該年成為了他的生涯年，打擊率2成97，18轟93分打點。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Eric Hosmer – Kansas City Royals – 2015  Player Profile – Rotoworld.com|url = http://www.rotoworld.com/player/mlb/5384/eric-hosmer|website = www.rotoworld.com|accessdate = 2015-10-26}}</ref>10月23日，美聯冠軍賽第6場，他追平了[[喬治·布列特|喬治·布列特]]的季後賽23分打點隊史紀錄。<ref>[http://www.kansascity.com/sports/mlb/kansas-city-royals/article41314929.html Homework got Lorenzo Cain home from first on single | The Kansas City Star<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>2015年世界大賽第1場，他在延長14局擊出高飛犧牲打，打破了布列特的紀錄。<ref>[https://www.si.com/mlb/2015/10/28/world-series-game-1-royals-mets SI.com]</ref>他在世界大賽第5場第9局跑回追平分，最終皇家打敗大都會，拿下世界大賽冠軍。<ref>[http://www.cbssports.com/mlb/gametracker/boxscore/MLB_20151028_NYM@KC CBS Sports]</ref>他也連3年拿下金手套獎。<ref>{{cite web |last=Miller |first=Doug |url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/156972846/gold-glove-winners-announced |title=Defensive standouts nab Gold Glove Awards |publisher=MLB.com |date=November 10, 2015 |accessdate=November 10, 2015}}</ref>
====2016年====
7月12日，他在明星賽擊出追平轟，<ref>[http://m.mlb.com/news/article/189461578/eric-hosmer-wins-2016-all-star-game-mvp-award/ Eric Hosmer wins 2016 All-Star Game MVP Award | MLB.com<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>他最終也拿下了明星賽MVP。該年他出賽158場，打擊率2成66，25轟為生涯新高，以及104分打點。
====2017年====
該年球季他全數出賽，打擊率3成18為生涯新高，25轟94分打點，上壘率3成85也是生涯新高。球季結束後他成為自由球員。<ref>{{cite news|title=Reports: Eric Hosmer agrees to deal with Padres|date=February 18, 2018|website=ESPN.com|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/22484179/eric-hosmer-agrees-deal-san-diego-padres|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180218164832/http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/22484179/eric-hosmer-agrees-deal-san-diego-padres|archivedate=February 18, 2018|deadurl=no}}</ref>他也拿下了生涯第4座金手套。<ref>https://www.mlb.com/news/alex-gordon-eric-hosmer-win-gold-glove-awards/c-260892242</ref>
===聖地牙哥教士===
2018年2月19日，霍斯摩與教士隊簽下8年1億4400萬美元的天價合約，這是教士隊史上最大的合約。<ref>http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/22506469/eric-hosmer-san-diego-padres-finalize-144-million-8-year-deal</ref>霍斯摩將原先在皇家隊穿的35號改為30號，一方面是因為教士隊的35號已經退休，一方面霍斯摩用此背號紀念他在皇家已逝的前隊友[[尤丹諾·范圖拉|尤丹諾·范圖拉]]。<ref>{{cite news|last=Kramer|first=Daniel|title=Hosmer to honor Yordano, wear No. 30 in SD|date=February 19, 2018|website=MLB.com|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/eric-hosmer-to-wear-no-30-in-honor-of-ventura/c-266758302|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180220114234/https://www.mlb.com/news/eric-hosmer-to-wear-no-30-in-honor-of-ventura/c-266758302|archivedate=February 20, 2018|deadurl=no}}</ref>2018年球季，霍斯摩打擊率為2成53，18轟69分打點。

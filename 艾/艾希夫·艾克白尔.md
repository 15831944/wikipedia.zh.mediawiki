'''艾希夫·艾克白尔'''（{{lang-bn|আসিফ আকবর}}，{{lang|en|Asif Akbar}}，{{bd|1972年|3月25日||}}）<ref name=sun>{{cite news |title=Singer Asif gets back to business |url=http://www.daily-sun.com/details_yes_31-07-2013_%0CSinger-Asif-gets-back-to-business_573_1_7_1_0.html |newspaper=Daily Sun |location=Dhaka |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140106034341/http://www.daily-sun.com/details_yes_31-07-2013_%0CSinger-Asif-gets-back-to-business_573_1_7_1_0.html |archive-date=6 January 2014}}</ref>，又译'''艾时夫·艾克巴尔'''、'''艾希夫·艾克宝尔'''，[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]]男性歌手<ref>{{cite news |author=Shilpi Mahalanobish |date=12 August 2004 |title=Asif Akbar: A ripple in the music industry |url=http://www.thedailystar.net/2004/08/12/d408121402108.htm |work=[[The_Daily_Star_(Bangladesh)|The Daily Star]] |accessdate=26 October 2009 }}</ref>，出生于[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]][[库米拉县|库米拉县]]<ref name=sun/>。他发行过[[独唱|独唱]]、二重唱及混合专辑，曾为孟加拉国电影献唱。他因在孟加拉语电影{{tsl|en|Rani Kuthir Baki Itihash}}中演唱而获得{{tsl|en|Meril Prothom Alo Awards}}一奖项<ref name=fame>{{cite news |author=Syeda Shamin Mortada |date=2 February 2009 |title=Using fame to promote social causes |url=http://www.thedailystar.net/news-detail-73928 |newspaper=The Daily Star |access-date=13 February 2017}}</ref>。

其妻名为Salma Asif Mitu<ref name=bnp>{{Cite web|url=https://bdnews24.com/politics/2011/02/04/asif-quits-bnp|title=Asif quits BNP|website=bdnews24.com|date=2011-02-04|access-date=2018-06-07}}</ref>。此外，他亦是[[孟加拉國民族主義黨|孟加拉國民族主義黨]]中央执行委员会的成员<ref name=bnp/><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thedailystar.net/news-detail-216801|title=Last moment campaign goes peacefully|date=2012-01-03|work=The Daily Star|access-date=2018-06-07|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thedailystar.net/news/al-bnp-central-leaders-knee-deep-in-campaign|title=AL, BNP central leaders knee-deep in campaign|date=2013-06-11|work=The Daily Star|access-date=2018-06-07|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thedailystar.net/news-detail-215936|title=Musclemen, black money big concern|date=2011-12-28|work=The Daily Star|access-date=2018-06-07|language=en}}</ref>。

==争议==
2018年6月，歌手{{tsl|en|Shafiq Tuhin}}指控艾克白尔未经授权出售歌曲复制品，并向警察局提起诉讼<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thedailystar.net/country/popular-singer-asif-akbar-arrested-in-copyright-issues-case-filed-under-ict-act-1587199|title=Singer Asif denied bail, lands in jail|date=2018-06-06|work=The Daily Star|access-date=2018-06-07|language=en}}</ref>。6月6日，艾克白尔于其工作室被捕<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://bdnews24.com/bangladesh/2018/06/06/singer-asif-lands-in-jail-in-a-music-piracy-case|title=Singer Asif lands in jail in a music piracy case|website=bdnews24.com|access-date=2018-06-07}}</ref>。之后，法院驳回了他的还押与保释请求，下令将其送入监狱<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thedailystar.net/backpage/singer-asif-akbar-lands-jail-over-digital-piracy-1587529|title=Singer Asif lands in jail over digital piracy|date=2018-06-07|work=The Daily Star|access-date=2018-06-07|language=en}}</ref>。在监狱关押五天后，艾克白尔被保释<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thedailystar.net/city/singer-asif-secures-bail-1589557|title=Singer Asif secures bail|date=2018-06-11|work=The Daily Star|access-date=2018-06-13|language=en}}</ref>。

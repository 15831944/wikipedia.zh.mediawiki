'''艾氏濱蟹'''（[[學名|學名]]''Carcinus aestuarii''），又名'''艾氏真蟹'''，是原產於[[地中海|地中海]][[海濱帶|海濱帶]]的一種[[蟹|蟹]]。它們非常像[[普通濱蟹|普通濱蟹]]，有時甚至被認為其[[亞種|亞種]]。艾氏濱蟹與普通濱蟹最不同的地方是[[眼睛|眼睛]]間甲殼前端，普通濱蟹的短而有鋸齒，而艾氏濱蟹的較長而圓滑。艾氏濱蟹的[[生殖肢|生殖肢]]筆直，而普通濱蟹的則是彎曲。<ref>{{cite journal en|quote=no|journal=Journal of Shellfish Research|volume=20|issue=3|pages=905–9|year=2001|title=Field identification of the European green crab species: ''Carcinus maenas'' and ''Carcinus aestuarii''|author=S. B. Yamada & L. Hauck|url=http://www.sgnis.org/publicat/papers/yamahauc.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927120719/http://www.sgnis.org/publicat/papers/yamahauc.pdf|archivedate=2007-09-27}}</ref>[[分子生物學|分子生物學]]研究顯示這兩者有明顯的分別，故確認艾氏濱蟹的[[物種|物種]]地位。<ref>{{cite journal en|quote=no|author=Roman, J. & S. R. Palumbi|title=A global invader at home: population structure of the green crab, ''Carcinus maenas'', in Europe|journal=Molecular Ecology|year=2004|volume=13|pages=2891–8|url=http://joeroman.com/images/roman&palumbi.eu.04.pdf|doi=10.1111/j.1365-294X.2004.02255.x}}</ref>

雖然普通濱蟹入侵了[[世界|世界]]各地海岸，但艾氏濱蟹（或可能是艾氏濱蟹與普通濱蟹的[[混種|混種]]）只入侵了[[日本|日本]]的海岸。<ref>{{cite journal en|quote=no|title=Episodic global dispersal in shallow water marine organisms: the case history of the European shore crabs ''Carcinus maenas'' and ''C. aestuarii''|year=2003|author=Carlton, T J & A N Cohen|journal=Journal of Biogeography|volume=30|pages=1809–20|url=http://www.sfei.org/bioinvasions/Reports/2003-GreenCrabDispersal383.pdf|doi=10.1111/j.1365-2699.2003.00962.x|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080829111536/http://www.sfei.org/bioinvasions/Reports/2003-GreenCrabDispersal383.pdf|archivedate=2008-08-29}}</ref>

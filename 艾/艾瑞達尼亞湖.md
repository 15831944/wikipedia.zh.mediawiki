'''艾瑞達尼亞湖'''（'''Eridania Lake'''）是一個理論中曾經存在於[[火星|火星]]表面的古代湖泊，面積約110萬平方公里。該湖泊被認為是[[外流浚道|外流浚道]][[馬丁谷|馬丁谷]]的水源<ref>{{cite journal| journal=[[Science_(journal)|Science]]| volume=296| issue= 5576| pages= 2209–2212| doi=10.1126/science.1071143| title=A Large Paleolake Basin at the Head of Ma'adim Vallis, Mars| last=Rossman | first=P. Irwin III| coauthors= Ted A. Maxwell, Alan D. Howard, Robert A. Craddock, David W. Leverington| url=http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/full/296/5576/2209| pmid=12077414|bibcode = 2002Sci...296.2209I |date=21 June 2002}}</ref>。一般認為艾瑞達尼亞湖在[[諾亞紀|諾亞紀]]晚期逐漸乾涸並分離成數個較小個湖泊<ref>{{cite journal | title=The Geology of Atlantis Basin, Mars, and Its Astrobiological Interest| last=de Pablo| first= M. A.| coauthors=Fairén, A. G.; Márquez, A.| journal=35th [[月球與行星科學研討會|Lunar and Planetary Science Conference]], 15–19 March 2004, League City, Texas| id= abstract no.1223| url=http://www.lpi.usra.edu/meetings/lpsc2004/pdf/1223.pdf|date=3 March 2004}}</ref>。

'''艾安·穆科吉'''（{{lang-en|'''Ayan Mukerji'''}}，{{bd|1983年|8月15日|catIdx=Mukerji, Ayan}}）<ref>[https://wwww.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/photo-features/alia-bhatts-instagram-pictures-you-should-not-miss/Alia-Bhatts-birthday-wish-for-her-soulmate-Ayan-Mukerji/photostory/60073349.cms “Alia Bhatt's Instagram Pictures You Should Not Miss!”]. Times of India. 15-08-2017.</ref>是一名[[印度|印度]]电影导演，主要活跃于[[宝莱坞|宝莱坞]]电影界。他来自宝莱坞有名的穆科吉家族，亲戚包括演员[[卡約兒|卡約兒]]和[[拉妮·穆科吉|拉妮·穆科吉]]等人。<ref>[https://tribune.com.pk/story/546111/i-didnt-get-access-to-key-people-in-the-film-industry-ayan-mukerji/ “I didn’t get access to key people in the film industry: Ayan Mukerji”]. The Express Tribune. 08-05-2013.</ref><ref>[https://www.news18.com/news/india/being-a-mukerji-was-not-like-being-rishi-kapoors-son-ayan-mukerji-607753.html “Being a Mukerji was not like being Rishi Kapoor's son: Ayan Mukerji”]. News18. 07-5-2013.</ref>

他作為导演的出道作品《Wake Up Sid》備受肯定。穆科吉之後在2013年拍摄了《[[那些年我們瘋狂的青春|那些年我們瘋狂的青春]]》，這電影大獲好評並成為宝莱坞当是票房收入最高的电影之一。

'''州际公路桥'''（{{lang-en|Interstate Bridge}}，又称为'''哥伦比亚河州际公路桥'''，{{lang|en| Columbia River Interstate Bridge}}，'''I-5桥''', I-5 Bridge，'''波特兰-温哥华州际公路桥'''，Portland-Vancouver Interstate Bridge，'''温哥华-波特兰大桥'''，Portland-Vancouver Bridge）是[[美国|美国]]的一对几乎垂直升降[[桁架桥|桁架桥]]<ref name="oprd nrhp list">{{cite web | title=Oregon National Register List | publisher=Oregon Parks and Recreation Department | url=http://www.oregon.gov/OPRD/HCD/NATREG/docs/oregon_nr_list.pdf | page=39 | format=PDF | date=June 6, 2011 | accessdate=July 13, 2011}}</ref><ref name = "Wood-Wortman">{{cite book | last= Wood Wortman | first= Sharon |author2=Wortman, Ed | title = The Portland Bridge Book | edition = 3rd | publisher = Urban Adventure Press | year = 2006 | pages = 107–112 | isbn= 0-9787365-1-6 }}</ref>，是[[5號州際公路|5號州際公路]]跨过[[哥倫比亞河|哥倫比亞河]]的通道，连接了[[华盛顿州|华盛顿州]][[溫哥華_(華盛頓州)|温哥华]]和[[俄勒冈州|俄勒冈州]][[波特蘭_(俄勒岡州)|波特蘭]]。旧桥修建于1917年<ref name="formally opened">[http://oregonnews.uoregon.edu/lccn/sn83025138/1917-02-15/ed-1/seq-1/ "Columbia Span Is Formally Opened: Dream of Half Century Is Realized (subheadlines: Traffic Starts With Brilliant Ceremony on Bridge; Thousands Attend Affair)"]. (February 15, 1917). ''[[The_Oregonian|The Morning Oregonian]]'', p. 1.</ref>，新桥修建于1958年<ref name=bottenberg>{{cite book | last= Bottenberg | first= Ray | title = Bridges of Portland | publisher = [[Arcadia_Publishing|Arcadia Publishing]] | year = 2007 | pages = 78, 80| isbn= 978-0-7385-4876-0 }}</ref>，目前都为单向通行。

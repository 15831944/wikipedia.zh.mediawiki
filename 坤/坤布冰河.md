'''坤布冰河'''（英語：Khumbu Glacier）是[[尼泊爾|尼泊爾]][[索盧昆布縣|索盧昆布縣]]的一條高山冰川，發源自世界最高峰[[聖母峰|聖母峰]]與第四高峰[[洛子峰|洛子峰]]之山塊，源流海拔約7600公尺，冰河末端海拔則有約4900公尺,<ref>Nepa Maps (Pvt.Ldt. ), NE517: Everest Base Camp & Gokyo, Kathmandu, Nepal, 2013</ref>，是世界海拔最高的冰河<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.ingentaconnect.com/content/igsoc/agl/2002/00000034/00000001/art00060?crawler=true | title = ASTER measurement of supraglacial lakes in the Mount Everest region of the Himalaya: ''The main Khumbu Glacier is about 17 km long with elevations ranging from 4900m at the terminus to 7600m at the source''....The 7600m to 8000m elevations are also depicted on numerous detailed topographic maps |accessdate = 2008-11-24 }}</ref>。冰河河岸為聖母峰基地營之所在，上游的{{link-en|西冰斗|Western Cwm}}是由基地營通往聖母峰與洛子峰的主要攀登路線之一<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.adventurephotographs.com/nepal/everest_region/general/image.asp?i=022 |title=The Khumbu Glacier |work= |first= |last= |publisher= |accessdate=2010-05-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110707090048/http://www.adventurephotographs.com/nepal/everest_region/general/image.asp?i=022 |archivedate=2011-07-07 }}</ref>。

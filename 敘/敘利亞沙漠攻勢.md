'''敘利亞沙漠攻勢'''是敘利亞政府軍與盟友部隊於2017年5月開始的軍事行動，意圖把政府軍控制區向東擴展至[[伊拉克|伊拉克]]邊境，並為[[代尔祖尔|代尔祖尔]]解圍作準備。<ref name="fronts">{{cite web|url=https://smmsyria.wordpress.com/2017/05/24/saa-opens-six-fronts-in-badiyeh-region-to-face-terrorists/|title=SAA OPENS SIX FRONTS IN BADIYEH REGION TO FACE TERRORISTS|date=24 May 2017|publisher=|accessdate=27 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://southfront.org/syrian-war-report-may-26-2017-syrian-army-liberated-5000km2-isis/|title=Syrian War Report – May 26, 2017: Syrian Army Liberated 5,000km2 From ISIS|date=26 May 2017|accessdate=26 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>

==巴格達－大馬士革公路攻勢==
2017年5月7日，敘政府軍向[[霍姆斯省|霍姆斯省]][[薩拜比埃爾|薩拜比埃爾]]（Al-Sabe' Biyar）地區的[[自由叙利亚军|自由叙利亚军]]發動攻擊。<ref>[http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2017/05/syria-army-moves-area-rebels-evicted-isil-170515043533965.html Syria army 'moves into area' where rebels evicted ISIL], Al-Jazeera, 15 May 2017</ref><ref name="important">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-begins-important-operation-prevent-us-jordanian-troops-entering-damascus/|author=Leith Fadel|publisher=almasdarnews.com|title=Syrian Army begins important operation to prevent US-Jordanian troops from entering Damascus|accessdate=10 May 2017}}</ref>到次日，敘軍已推進約45公里。<ref name="focus">{{cite web|url=http://www.miamiherald.com/news/nation-world/article149433474.html|publisher=miamiherald.com|title=Syrian troops shift focus to IS-held east | Miami Herald|accessdate=10 May 2017}}</ref>

5月14日至15日之間的晚上，包括[[伊拉克|伊拉克]]什葉派民兵在內的親政府增援部隊抵達薩拜比埃爾。<ref name="reinforcements">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-government-iraqi-reinforcements-reach-southeast-damascus-upcoming-offensive/|title=Syrian government, Iraqi reinforcements reach southeast Damascus for upcoming offensive|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=15 May 2017|publisher=}}</ref><ref name="bringing">{{cite news|url=http://en.eldorar.com/node/5677|title=Assad regime bringing in new Iraqi militias|work=El-Dorar Al-Shamia|date=12 May 2017|accessdate=15 May 2017|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170826095055/http://en.eldorar.com/node/5677|archivedate=2017年8月26日}}</ref><ref name="toward">{{cite web|url=http://ca.reuters.com/article/topNews/idCAKCN18B01D-OCATP|title=Syrian army sends reinforcements toward border with Iraq: rebels |first=Suleiman|last=Al-Khalidi|website=Reuters}}</ref>此時，敘軍距離敘伊邊境的坦夫（al-Tanf）口岸約24至100公里。<ref name="special">{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2017/05/15/syrian-troops-advancing-towards-us-british-special-forces/?WT.mc_id=tmg_share_tw|title=Syrian troops advancing towards US and British special forces|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-prepares-seize-jordanian-iraqi-borders/|title=Syrian Army prepares to seize the Jordanian and Iraqi borders|author=Izat Charkatli|work=[[al-Masdar_News|al-Masdar News]]|date=15 May 2017|accessdate=17 May 2017}}</ref>與此同時，美軍和英軍的特種部隊抵達坦夫，<ref name="special"/>協助反對派進攻伊斯蘭國控制區。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://macedoniaonline.eu/content/view/32040/2/|title=MINA Breaking News|website=macedoniaonline.eu}}</ref>

5月18日，[[國防軍_(敘利亞)|國防軍]]與敘軍第5和第7裝甲師在[[苏韦达省|苏韦达省]]東部的反對派控制區推進了35公里。<ref name="setback">{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/us-backed-rebels-suffer-major-setback-syrian-army-troops-capture-much-eastern-sweida/|title=US-backed rebels suffer major setback as Syrian Army troops capture much of eastern Sweida|last=Fadel|first=Leith|work=[[al-Masdar_News|al-Masdar News]]|date=18 May 2017|accessdate=18 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>敘軍繼續向坦夫前進，遭到美國領導的聯軍空襲，事後美國官員表示在空襲前曾警告敘軍停止向有聯軍駐扎的坦夫前進但不獲理會。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.alarabiya.net/en/News/middle-east/2017/05/18/US-led-Coalition-strikes-pro-regime-convoy-in-Syria-near-Jordan.html|title=US-led coalition strikes pro-regime convoy in Syria near Jordan|publisher=}}</ref>美方稱敘軍在空襲中損失數輛車輛，包括一輛坦克<ref name="rare">{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/national-security/us-launches-rare-intentional-strike-on-pro-government-forces-in-syria/2017/05/18/9e4e2830-3bf0-11e7-8854-21f359183e8c_story.html|title=U.S. launches rare intentional strike on pro-government forces in Syria|publisher=}}</ref>及兩輛推土機。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/world/2017/05/18/united-states-official-airstrike-syria/101835198/|title=Military official: U.S. airstrike hits pro-regime forces in Syria|publisher=}}</ref>

==沿約旦邊境的推進==
5月20日，敘政府軍與民兵收復[[苏韦达省|苏韦达省]]東部大片領土。<ref name="chunk"/>次日政府軍攻佔敘利亞沙漠南部的數個據點。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://syriadirect.org/news/pro-regime-forces-advance-toward-base-in-syrian-desert-as-us-warns-%E2%80%98coalition-forces-will-defend-themselves%E2%80%99/|title=Pro-regime forces advance toward base in Syrian desert as US warns: Coalition will 'defend' itself|website=Syria Direct}}</ref>

==打通大馬士革－巴爾米拉公路==
5月23日，敘政府軍對伊斯蘭國開闢了一條100公里長的新戰線。敘軍迅速收復超過1,200平方公里的領土。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-opens-new-front-isis-surprise-offensive/|title=Syrian Army opens new front against ISIS in a surprise offensive|last=Charkatli|first=Izat|date=23 May 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News|accessdate=23 May 2017|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-opens-100-km-front-isis-new-surprise-offensive/|title=Syrian Army opens a 100 km front against ISIS in new surprise offensive|last=Charkatli|first=Izat|date=24 May 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News|accessdate=23 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>5月26日，政府軍成功打通大馬士革至[[巴尔米拉|巴尔米拉]]的公路。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-liberates-roads-damascus-palmyra-video/|title=Syrian Army liberates roads between Damascus, Palmyra: video|last=Fadel|first=Leith|date=2017-05-26 |work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |accessdate=2017-05-26|language=en}}</ref>

==抵達伊拉克邊境==
2017年6月6日，美國空軍攻擊位於美英軍隊在坦夫的訓練區約25英里外的親政府部隊。<ref name="cbstanf">[http://www.cbsnews.com/news/us-led-coalition-strikes-pro-government-forces-in-syria/ U.S.-led coalition strikes pro-government forces in Syria]. CBS News, 2017-06-06.</ref>6月8日，美國空軍再度攻擊在坦夫附近的敘政府軍。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/video-us-backed-rebels-pound-syrian-army-positions-damascus-baghdad-highway/|title=VIDEO: US-backed rebels pound Syrian Army positions on the Damascus-Baghdad highway|date=2017-06-08|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |access-date=2017-06-08|language=en}}</ref>

6月9日，敘軍在坦夫東北約70公里處建立據點，自2015年以來首次控制部分與伊拉克接壤邊境。<ref>[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/afp/article-4589436/Syria-army-reaches-Iraq-border-US-tensions-monitor.html Syria says troops reach Iraq border after US tensions] Daily Mail, 9 June 2017.</ref><ref>[http://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/afp/2017/06/syria-conflict-iraq-us-is-border.html Syria army reaches Iraq border after US tensions: monitor]</ref><ref>[http://sana.sy/en/?p=107808 Syrian Army reaches Syrian-Iraqi borders northeast of al-Tanf, thwarts ISIS attack in Deir Ezzor]</ref>

==向蘇赫奈推進==
6月13日，敘軍收復阿拉克（Arak）及附近的阿拉克氣田。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://southfront.org/arak-town-and-arak-gas-field-liberated-by-government-forces-reports/|title=Arak Town And Arak Gas Field Liberated By Government Forces - Reports|date=2017-06-13|publisher=SouthFront blog|access-date=2017-06-14|language=en}}</ref>同日[[叙利亚共和国卫队|共和國衛隊]]第103旅抵達巴爾米拉增援。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/republican-guard-reinforcements-arrive-palmyra-aid-deir-ezzor-liberation-offensive/|title=Republican Guard reinforcements arrive in Palmyra to aid Deir Ezzor liberation offensive|date=2017-06-14|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News|access-date=2017-06-14|language=en}}</ref>

6月25日，敘軍的Fuad Khaddour-{准}-將在霍姆斯省東部陣亡。6月26日，敘軍進一步迫近[[阿布凱馬勒|阿布凱馬勒]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/deir-ezzor-liberation-within-sight-syrian-army-pounces-isis-held-desert/|title=Deir Ezzor liberation within sight as Syrian Army pounces through ISIS-held desert|author=Chris Tomson|publisher=al-Masdar News|date=26 June 2017|accessdate=29 June 2017}}</ref>7月6日，親政府部隊推進至距離[[蘇赫奈|蘇赫奈]]不到15公里處。

==在蘇韋達省和大馬士革農村省的推進==
2017年7月10日，親政府部隊展開「偉大黎明行動」第二階段。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-syrian-army-kicks-off-2nd-phase-operation-big-dawn-southeast-syria/|title=Breaking: Syrian Army kicks off 2nd phase of Operation Big Dawn in southeast Syria|date=2017-07-10|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News access-date=2017-07-10|language=en}}</ref>政府軍一天內從反對派手上收復3,000平方公里領土。<ref name="blitz">{{Cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/titanic-blitz-offensive-pro-syria-forces-sees-3000-km2-liberated-just-12-hours-map-update/ |title=Titanic blitz offensive by pro-Syria forces sees 3,000 km2 liberated in just 12 hours: Map update |date=2017-07-10}}</ref>

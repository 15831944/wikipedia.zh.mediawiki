'''敘利亞中部戰役'''是[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]其中一場戰役，於2017年7月14日開始，承接先前的[[敘利亞沙漠攻勢|敘利亞沙漠攻勢]]和[[拉卡省南部攻勢|拉卡省南部攻勢]]。敘利亞政府軍的目標是攻佔[[蘇赫奈|蘇赫奈]]，切斷[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]在蘇赫奈以西的控制區與[[代尔祖尔省|代尔祖尔省]]的聯繫，<ref name="11000square">{{cite web|url=http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=69945 |script-title=en:The regime forces are about 70 km away of besieging the “Islamic State” in more than 11000 square km within the provinces of al-Raqqa, Homs, and Hama|publisher=|accessdate=2017-07-26}}</ref><ref name="lebanons">{{cite web|url=http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=69998|title=To besiege an area larger than Lebanon’s area… eastern countryside of Hama and al-Sukhnah Desert witness intensified raids by Russian warplanes as well as regime’s warplanes|publisher=|accessdate=26 July 2017}}</ref>然後向[[代尔祖尔|代尔祖尔]]進軍，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-eyes-deir-ezzor-advance-southeast-raqqa-map/|title=Syrian Army eyes Deir Ezzor as they advance in southeast Raqqa: map|date=24 July 2017|publisher=|accessdate=26 July 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-marching-deir-ezzor-new-village-captured/|title=Syrian Army marching on Deir Ezzor, new village captured|date=24 July 2017|publisher=|accessdate=26 July 2017}}</ref>解除伊斯蘭國對當地的[[代爾祖爾之圍|長期圍困]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.morningstaronline.co.uk/a-c778-Army-pushes-ahead-to-lift-the-siege-on-Deir-Ezzor-city#.WXdJ0YSGNhF|title=Army pushes ahead to lift the siege on Deir Ezzor city|first=People’s Printing Press|last=Society|publisher=|accessdate=26 July 2017|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170807235427/https://www.morningstaronline.co.uk/a-c778-Army-pushes-ahead-to-lift-the-siege-on-Deir-Ezzor-city#.WXdJ0YSGNhF|archivedate=2017年8月7日|df=}}</ref>

==北線進展==
2017年7月14日，敘政府軍在[[拉卡省|拉卡省]]南部向伊斯蘭國進攻，向古城魯薩費（Resafa）以南的地區推進。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/tiger-forces-scythe-isis-lines-raqqa-en-route-sukhnah-map-update/|title=Tiger Forces scythe through ISIS lines in Raqqa en-route to Sukhnah - Map update|date=2017-07-14 |publisher= |accessdate=2017-07-26}}</ref>與此同時，俄軍在拉卡省和[[霍姆斯省|霍姆斯省]]猛烈空襲伊斯蘭國分子。<ref name="widescale">{{cite web |url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/russian-air-force-resumes-wide-scale-campaign-isil-east-syria/ |title=Russian Air Force resumes wide-scale campaign against ISIL in east Syria|date=2017-07-15 |publisher=|accessdate=2017-07-26}}</ref>

7月21日，敘軍推進至魯薩法以東30公里處。<ref name="30kilometers">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-syrian-army-advances-30-kilometers-isis-held-eastern-raqqa/|title=BREAKING: Syrian Army advances 30 kilometers through ISIS-held eastern Raqqa|date=2017-07-21 |publisher=|accessdate=2017-07-26}}</ref>4天後，敘軍距離代爾祖爾省西部邊界只有9公里。<ref name="influence">{{cite web|url=http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=70544|title=Big advancement for the regime forces southeast of Al-Raqqah draws areas of influence and reduces the percentage of control the ISIS to 10% of the province|publisher=|accessdate=2017-07-26}}</ref>敘政府軍在[[敘利亞民主力量|敘利亞民主力量]]控制的[[幼發拉底河|幼發拉底河]]南岸地區以南向東和東北推進，抵達[[拉卡|拉卡]]東南方的幼發拉底河南岸。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-recaptures-villages-en-route-deir-ezzor/|title=Syrian Army recaptures more villages en route to Deir Ezzor|date=2017-07-25|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |accessdate=2017-07-25|language=en}}</ref>

7月27日，北線敘軍從拉卡省攻入代爾祖爾省。<ref>[http://uk.reuters.com/article/uk-mideast-crisis-syria-raqqa-idUKKBN1AC2XZ Syrian army advances east of Raqqa into Deir al-Zor province - monitors]</ref>

7月29日，數十名伊斯蘭國分子在日出時份乘小貨車突襲拉卡省西部的敘軍據點，敘軍敗退後重新集結和反攻，伊斯蘭國分子帶着擄獲的武器彈藥撤退。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/pictures-isis-launches-sudden-shock-attack-army-forces-western-raqqa/ |title=In pictures: ISIS launches sudden shock attack against army forces in western Raqqa|date=2017-07-29}}</ref>同日伊斯蘭國分子在幼發拉底河南岸的-{Ghan Al-‘Ali}-和Shinan兩鎮附近發動反攻，出動超過10輛[[自殺炸彈|自殺炸彈]]車攻擊以[[老虎部隊|老虎部隊]]為主力的敘軍，超過40名敘軍士兵陣亡。敘軍最終奪回所有重要據點。<ref name="heaviest">[https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-suffers-heaviest-losses-latest-push-deir-ezzor/ Syrian Army suffers its heaviest losses in latest push to Deir Ezzor]</ref><ref>[https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/statistical-breakdown-army-losses-recent-southern-raqqa-fighting-isis/ A statistical breakdown of army losses in recent southern Raqqa fighting with ISIS]</ref>

老虎部隊在8月1日繼續進攻，至8月2日已收復幼發拉底河西岸的7個村鎮，伊斯蘭國方面有超過100人傷亡。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.chinanews.com/gj/2017/08-03/8295237.shtml |title=叙政府军在拉卡收复曾由极端组织控制的多个村镇 |publisher=中新網 |date=2017-08-03}}</ref>

==南線進展==
7月16日，伊斯蘭國在位於霍姆斯省與[[代尔祖尔省|代尔祖尔省]]交界的Hamimah地區擊退敘利亞陸軍第5軍和第18裝甲師的進攻。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-suffers-heavy-casualties-failed-offensive-near-deir-ezzor-border/|title=Syrian Army suffers heavy casualties in failed offensive near Deir Ezzor border|date=2017-07-17|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |language=en}}</ref>

7月26日，敘軍攻佔[[蘇赫奈|蘇赫奈]]西南8公里處的最後一些小山，至此敘軍炮火已覆蓋蘇赫奈全鎮。<ref>[https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-syrian-army-completely-dominates-isis-held-town-al-sukhnah-fire-control/ BREAKING: Syrian Army completely dominates ISIS-held town of al-Sukhnah with fire control]</ref><ref name="eyeing">[https://tribune.com.pk/story/1467889/eyeing-deir-al-zor-syrian-army-advances-islamic-state-town/ Eyeing Deir al-Zor, Syrian army advances on Islamic State town]</ref>隨後敘軍開始進攻蘇赫奈。<ref>[https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-syrian-army-troops-storm-al-sukhnah-town-eastern-homs-liberation-imminent/ BREAKING: Syrian Army troops storm al-Sukhnah town in eastern Homs, liberation imminent]</ref>

7月29日，敘軍攻入蘇赫奈西南部。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2017/07/syria-troops-enter-key-isil-held-town-al-sukhna-170728204920508.html|title=Assad troops enter ISIL-held town of al-Sukhna in Homs|date=2017-07-29|work=Al Jazeera|access-date=2017-07-29|language=en}}</ref>8月5日，敘軍攻佔蘇赫奈。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/uk-mideast-crisis-syria-sukhna-idUKKBN1AL0QS |title=Syrian army takes last IS-held town in Homs - monitor |publisher=Reuters |date=2017-08-05}}</ref>

==南北合圍==
2017年8月18日，南北兩路敘軍在{{Link-en|伊特里亞|Ithriya}}以南地區會師，伊斯蘭國在霍姆斯省和哈馬省的一塊有3,000平方公里大的控制區被完全包圍。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/english/2017-08/18/c_136537305.htm |title=Syrian army fully besieges IS areas in eastern countryside of Homs, Hama provinces |publisher=Xinhua |date=2017-08-18}}</ref>

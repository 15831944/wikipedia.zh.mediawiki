'''朗納·德瑞福'''（{{lang_en|'''Ronald Drever'''}}，{{bd|1931年|10月26日|2017年|3月7日}}），[[英國|英國]]實驗物理學者，[[加州理工學院|加州理工學院]]榮譽教授，是[[LIGO|LIGO]]計畫創始人之一。德瑞福是能夠穩定激光的{{le|龐德-德瑞福-霍爾技術|Pound-Drever-Hall technique}}的發明者之一。對於2015年9月人類首次直接探測到引力波的創舉，這技術具有關鍵性作用。<ref name="Telegraph">{{cite web | url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/science/12153127/British-scientist-who-played-key-role-in-gravitational-waves-research-is-suffering-from-dementia.html | title=British scientist who played key role in gravitational waves research is suffering from dementia | publisher=Telegraph Media Group Limited | work=The Telegraph | date=12 February 2016 | accessdate=12 February 2016 | author=Knapton, Sarah}}</ref><ref name="PRL-20160211">{{cite journal |author=Abbott, B.P. |title=Observation of Gravitational Waves from a Binary Black Hole Merger |url=http://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.116.061102 |journal=Phys. Rev. Lett. |volume=116 |pages=061102 |year=2016 |doi=10.1103/PhysRevLett.116.061102 |display-authors=etal}}</ref><ref name="Nature_11Feb16">{{cite journal |title=Einstein's gravitational waves found at last |journal=Nature News|url=http://www.nature.com/news/einstein-s-gravitational-waves-found-at-last-1.19361 |date=11 February 2016 |last=Castelvecchi |first=Davide |last2=Witze |first2=Alexandra |doi=10.1038/nature.2016.19361 |accessdate=11 February 2016 }}</ref>

在[[格拉斯哥大學|格拉斯哥大學]]，德瑞福開始了他的學術生涯，後來，他被聘請至加州理工學院。在那裏，他成立了一個引力波計畫。<ref name="Telegraph">{{cite web | url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/science/12153127/British-scientist-who-played-key-role-in-gravitational-waves-research-is-suffering-from-dementia.html | title=British scientist who played key role in gravitational waves research is suffering from dementia | publisher=Telegraph Media Group Limited | work=The Telegraph | date=12 February 2016 | accessdate=12 February 2016 | author=Knapton, Sarah}}</ref><ref name="NYT">{{cite web | url=http://www.nytimes.com/2016/02/12/science/ligo-gravitational-waves-black-holes-einstein.html?_r=0 | title=Gravitational Waves Detected, Confirming Einstein’s Theory | publisher=The New York Times Company| work=New York Times | date=11 February 2016 | accessdate=12 February 2016 | author=OVERBYE, DENNIS , CORUM, JONATHAN  and DRAKEFORD, JASON}}</ref>

德瑞福最近期的研究涉及到發展一種光學浮置科技，其可以使得實驗儀器與地震隔離。德瑞福退休后因痴呆症而在蘇格蘭的一家療養院休養。<ref name="Telegraph" />德瑞福已于2017年3月7日在苏格兰[[爱丁堡|爱丁堡]]逝去，享年85岁<ref>{{cite web|author=Jonathan Amos|title=Gravitational waves pioneer Ronald Drever dies|work=BBC news|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/science-environment-39212305|accessdate=2017-03-10}}</ref>。

== 榮譽與獎勵 ==
由於德瑞福在科學領域成就非凡，他獲得了很多榮譽與獎勵：
*2002年，他被遴選為[[美國文理科學院|美國文理科學院]]院士。<ref>[http://media.caltech.edu/press_releases/12285 Five Caltech Faculty Members Elected to Membership in the American Academy of Arts and Sciences] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100601181737/http://media.caltech.edu/press_releases/12285 |date=2010-06-01 }}</ref>
*他與[[萊納·魏斯|萊納·魏斯]]共享2007年 {{le|愛因斯坦獎|Einstein Prize (APS)}}。
*由於「探測到[[GW150914|引力波的蹤跡]]，開啟天文學與物理學的新視野」，德瑞福、[[基普·索恩|基普·索恩]]、[[萊納·魏斯|萊納·魏斯]]與1012位團隊同工共同榮獲2016年[[基礎物理學突破獎|基礎物理學突破獎]]。<ref>{{cite web | url =https://breakthroughprize.org/News/32  | title =Special Breakthrough Prize In Fundamental Physics Awarded For Detection Of Gravitational Waves 100 Years After Albert Einstein Predicted Their Existence | date =2 May 2016 | publisher =Breakthrough Prize}}</ref>
*因為「探測到引力波」，德瑞福、索恩、魏斯獲得2016年[[格鲁伯宇宙学奖|格鲁伯宇宙学奖]]。<ref>[http://gruber.yale.edu/cosmology/press/2016-gruber-cosmology-prize-press-release 2016 Gruber Cosmology Prize]</ref>
*由於對「激光干涉儀重力波觀測站LIGO的構思和設計」，德瑞福、索恩、魏斯獲頒2016年邵逸夫獎。<ref>{{cite web
 | url =http://www.shawprize.org/big5/shaw.php?tmp=5&twoid=79&threeid=253&fourid=481
 | title =2016年度「邵逸夫獎」得獎者名單公佈
 | date =31 May 2016
 | website =邵逸夫獎
 | publisher =邵逸夫獎基金會 }}</ref>
*2016年，德瑞福、索恩、魏斯因「直接探測到引力波」，共獲[[科维理天体物理学奖|科维理天体物理学奖]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.kavliprize.org/prizes-and-laureates/prizes/2016-kavli-prize-astrophysics|title=2016 Kavli Prize in Astrophysics {{!}} www.kavliprize.org|website=www.kavliprize.org|access-date=2016-06-02}}</ref>

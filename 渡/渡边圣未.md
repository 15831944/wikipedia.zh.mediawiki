'''渡边圣未'''（{{jpn|j=渡辺 聖未|hg=わたなべ きよみ|rm=Watanabe Kiyomi}}，{{bd|1996年|8月25日||}}）生于宿务省宿务市，是一名日本裔菲律宾女子柔道运动员，主攻63公斤级。她的父亲是日本人，母亲是菲律宾人。她先前居住在宿务，后来随父母移居至其父亲的家乡[[山梨县|山梨县]]。<ref name="autogenerated1">{{cite web|author=Reijet Jarreau Lazaro Pelea, Tridel Technologies Inc. |url=http://www.mb.com.ph/despite-language-barrier-fil-japanese-rises/ |title=Despite language barrier, Fil-Japanese rises | Manila Bulletin | Latest Breaking News | News Philippines |publisher=Mb.com.ph |date=2013-12-21 |accessdate=2014-02-15 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140303053805/http://www.mb.com.ph/despite-language-barrier-fil-japanese-rises/ |archivedate=2014-03-03 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tokyo-np.co.jp/article/sports/list/201510/CK2015100802000230.html|script-title=ja:全日本学生選手権Ｖ セブ島育ちの渡辺　フィリピン代表で五輪目指す|publisher={{lang|ja|東京新聞}}|date=2015-10-08|accessdate=2018-11-23|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151011063448/http://www.tokyo-np.co.jp/article/sports/list/201510/CK2015100802000230.html|archivedate=2015-10-11}}</ref>这之后，她开始练习柔道<ref>{{cite web|last=Cin |first=Stanley |url=http://www.cebu.gov.ph/news/filipino-japanese-judoka-enjoys-competing-for-the-philippines/ |title=Filipino-Japanese judoka enjoys competing for the Philippines |publisher=Cebu Provincial Government |date=2013-12-26 |accessdate=2015-06-20}}</ref>。2014年，她进入[[早稻田大学|早稻田大学]]就读<ref name="mt021316">{{cite news|last1=Noguera|first1=Emil|title=SEA Games champion Watanabe braces for Olympic qualifying events|url=http://www.manilatimes.net/sea-games-champion-watanabe-braces-for-olympic-qualifying-events/244762/|accessdate=2016-02-13|publisher=The Manila Times|date=2016-02-13}}</ref>。

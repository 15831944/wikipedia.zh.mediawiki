'''史蒂芬·杭特'''（{{lang-en|Stephen Hunter}}，{{bd|1968年|10月28日||}}）是一位[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]]的電影與電視演員，於[[威靈頓|威靈頓]]出生<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://middleearthnews.com/2014/10/28/happy-birthday-stephen-hunter/|title=Happy Birthday, Stephen Hunter!|accessdate=2018-03-23|date=2014-10-28|publisher=Middle-earth News|language=en}}</ref>，目前居住於[[澳洲|澳洲]]<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nyfa.edu.au/faculty/sydney-acting.php|title=Sydney Acting Faculty|accessdate=2018-03-23|publisher=New York Film Academy Australia|language=en-US}}</ref>。杭特主要在幾部紐西蘭和澳洲的電視劇裡扮演小角色；他也在《[[哈比人電影系列|哈比人電影系列]]》中飾演[[矮人_(中土大陸)|矮人]][[龐伯|龐伯]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cinemablend.com/new/The-Hobbit-Cast-Announced-Martin-Freeman-Is-Bilbo-21356.html|title=The Hobbit Cast Announced, Martin Freeman Is Bilbo|date=2010-10-21|newspaper=CINEMABLEND|accessdate=2018-03-23}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.theonering.net/torwp/2016/09/08/101724-torn-chats-to-stephen-hunter/|title=TORn chats to Stephen Hunter|date=2016-09-08|newspaper=TheOneRing.net|accessdate=2018-03-23|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://middleearthnews.com/2013/04/24/the-12-days-of-bombur-the-full-stephen-hunter-interview/|title=The 12 Days Of Bombur – The Full Stephen Hunter Interview|accessdate=2018-03-23|date=2013-04-24|publisher=Middle-earth News|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://herocomplex.latimes.com/movies/hobbit-stephen-hunter-on-bomburs-blubber-dwarf-boot-camp/#/0|title=‘Hobbit’: Stephen Hunter on Bombur’s blubber, dwarf boot camp|date=2012-12-10|newspaper=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=2018-03-23|language=en-US}}</ref>，在受訪時他表示《哈比人》是他所參與的第一部大預算電影<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.theonering.net/torwp/2010/10/31/39979-torn-exclusive-an-interview-with-stephen-hunterbombur/|title=TORn Exclusive: an interview with Stephen Hunter/Bombur|date=2010-11-01|newspaper=TheOneRing.net|accessdate=2018-03-23|language=en-US}}</ref>。

'''史蒂芬·福斯特纪念馆'''（Stephen Collins Foster Memorial）位于[[美国|美国]][[宾夕法尼亚州|宾夕法尼亚州]][[匹兹堡|匹兹堡]]Oakland社区福布斯大道，[[匹兹堡大学|匹兹堡大学]]校园内，是一个表演艺术中心和博物馆，纪念民歌作家[[史蒂芬·福斯特|史蒂芬·福斯特]]。

史蒂芬·福斯特纪念馆位于[[申利农场历史区|申利农场]][[美国历史区|历史区]]<ref>{{cite web | url={{NRHP-PA|H050656_01H.pdf}} | title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory-Nomination Form: Schenley Farms Historic District | publisher=Pittsburgh HIstory & Landmarks Foundation | last=Mann | first=Christina | date=February 1982 | accessdate=2009-11-25}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url=http://mac10.umc.pitt.edu/u/FMPro?-db=ustory&-format=d.html&-lay=a&-sortfield=issueid%3a%3aissuedate&-sortorder=max=50&-recid=38544&-find= | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120227134612/http://mac10.umc.pitt.edu/u/FMPro?-db=ustory&-format=d.html&-lay=a&-sortfield=issueid%3A%3Aissuedate&-sortorder=max%3D50&-recid=38544&-find= | dead-url=yes | archive-date=2012-02-27 | title=Hearing set on historic landmark nomination for two Pitt buildings | first=Mike | last=Sajna | newspaper=University Times | date=1997-01-23 | volume=29 | issue=10 | publisher=University of Pittsburgh | location=Pittsburgh, PA | accessdate=2009-11-25 | df= }}</ref>，是一处历史地标<ref name="PHLF list"/><ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.flickr.com/photos/arejay/397259024/in/pool-phlf/ | title=SFosterMem-PHLF on Flickr | publisher=flickr | last=rj-photo | date=2007-02-20 | accessdate=2009-11-25}}</ref>。

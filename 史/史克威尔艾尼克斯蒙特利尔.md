'''史克威爾艾尼克斯蒙特利爾'''（Square Enix Montreal）是由[[史克威尔艾尼克斯|史克威尔艾尼克斯]]子公司[[史克威尔艾尼克斯欧洲|史克威尔艾尼克斯欧洲]]持有，位于加拿大的[[电子游戏|电子游戏]][[电子游戏开发者|开发工作室]]。工作室由[[史克威尔艾尼克斯|史克威尔艾尼克斯]]于2011年建立，由之前負責Square Enix 倫敦工作室的Lee Singleton擔任公司總經理<ref name="SEM">{{cite web |url=http://www.hd.square-enix.com/eng/news/2011/html/f59a62fddbf4cff95811a41d5cde6676.html |title=SQUARE ENIX TO EXPAND FURTHER IN MONTRÉAL |date=November 21, 2011 |publisher=Square Enix |language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180716223806/http://www.hd.square-enix.com/eng/news/2011/html/f59a62fddbf4cff95811a41d5cde6676.html|archivedate=2018-07-16}}</ref>。

== 歷史 ==
史克威爾艾尼克斯蒙特利爾原先是作為[[刺客任務系列|刺客任務]]新作而設立的工作室，預計將與[[IO_Interactive|IO Interactive]]輪流開發[[刺客任務系列|刺客任務]]新作<ref name="SEM" />。但在2014年，遊戲設計師Richard Knight確認工作室已經取消[[刺客任務系列|刺客任務]]新作的開發<ref name="next-gen Hitman title confirmed cancelled videogamer">{{cite web |last1=Scammell|first1=David|url=https://www.videogamer.com/news/square-enix-montreals-next-gen-hitman-title-confirmed-cancelled|title=Square Enix Montreal's next-gen Hitman title confirmed cancelled|date=2014-01-06|publisher=videogamer|accessdate=2018-08-31|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180627034644/https://www.videogamer.com/news/square-enix-montreals-next-gen-hitman-title-confirmed-cancelled |archivedate=2018-06-27}}</ref><ref name="next-gen Hitman title confirmed cancelled neogaf">{{cite web |url=https://www.neogaf.com/threads/square-enix-montreals-next-gen-hitman-title-confirmed-cancelled-io-still-on-hitman.746566/|title=Square Enix Montreal's next-gen Hitman title confirmed cancelled (IO still on Hitman)|date=2014-01-06|accessdate=2018-08-31 |publisher=neogaf|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180831221320/https://www.neogaf.com/threads/square-enix-montreals-next-gen-hitman-title-confirmed-cancelled-io-still-on-hitman.746566/|archivedate=2018-08-31 }}</ref>，工作室未來將專注於移動平台的開發。

2016年5月2日，曾經開發過《光明旅者》的Teddy Dief加入史克威爾艾尼克斯蒙特利爾並擔任創意總監<ref name="Moving to Square Enix Montréal">{{cite web|last1=Dief|first1=Teddy|url=http://www.teddydief.com/blog/2016/5/2/moving-to-square-enix-montral|title=Moving to Square Enix Montréal
|date=2016-05-02|accessdate=2018-08-31|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180831221122/http://www.teddydief.com/blog/2016/5/2/moving-to-square-enix-montral|archivedate=2018-08-31}}</ref>，之後於2018年1月29日離開了史克威爾艾尼克斯蒙特利爾<ref name="Leaving Square Enix Montreal">{{cite web|last1=Dief|first1=Teddy|url=http://www.teddydief.com/blog/2018/1/29/leaving-square-enix-montreal|title=Leaving Square Enix Montreal|date=2018-01-29|accessdate=2018-08-31|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180831221237/http://www.teddydief.com/blog/2018/1/29/leaving-square-enix-montreal|archivedate=2018-08-31}}</ref>。

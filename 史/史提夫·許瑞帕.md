'''史提夫·許瑞帕'''（{{lang-en|Steve Schirripa}}，本名：{{lang|en|Steven Ralph Schirripa}}，{{bd|1958年|8月1日}}）是一名美國演員、監製、作家和配音員。他最出名是在[[HBO|HBO]]電視劇《[[人在江湖_(美國電視劇)|人在江湖]]》中飾演{{link-en|Bobby Baccalieri|Bobby Baccalieri}}<ref>{{Cite web |url = https://wgnradio.com/2017/06/11/actor-steve-schirripa-on-the-sopranos-10th-anniversary-of-series-finale-recurring-role-on-cbs-blue-bloods-new-woody-allen-movie-wonder-wheel/ |title = Actor Steve Schirripa on ‘The Sopranos’ 10th Anniversary of Series Finale, Recurring Role on CBS’ ‘Blue Bloods’, New Woody Allen Movie ‘Wonder Wheel’ |date = 2017-06-11 |publisher = WGN Radio |language = en}}</ref>。他是{{link-en|Investigation Discovery|Investigation Discovery}}頻道兩部電視節目《''{{link-en|Karma's A B*tch|Karma's A B*tch}}''》和《''{{link-en|Nothing Personal (TV series)|Nothing Personal (TV series)|Nothing Personal}}''》的監製和主持。

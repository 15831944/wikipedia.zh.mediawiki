'''刘易斯·斯特朗凯西·史迈士'''（{{lang-en|'''Lewis Strong Casey Smythe'''}}<ref>{{Cite web|title=The Bulletin, Spring 2008 (Vol. 44, No. 2): The Newsletter of the Lexington Theological Seminary |year=2008 |page=4 |url=https://www.lextheo.edu/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/bulletinspring08.pdf}}</ref>，{{Bd|1901年|1月31日|1978年|6月1日}}），[[美国|美国]][[传教士|传教士]]，[[金陵大学|金陵大学]]社会学系教授，[[南京大屠杀|南京大屠杀]]见证者<ref>{{Cite web|title=Lewis S.C. Smythe | publisher=The Nanking Massacre Project: Yale Divinity School, Yale University |url=http://web.library.yale.edu/divinity/nanking/smythe}}</ref><ref name=autogenerated8>{{Cite web|title=Oral History Catalogue: Claremont Graduate University |url=http://web.cgu.edu/oralhistory/china_missionaries_project.htm |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081204104328/http://web.cgu.edu/oralhistory/china_missionaries_project.htm |archivedate=2008-12-04 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=They Were in Nanjing: The Nanjing Massacre Witnessed by American and British Nationals, Vol. 1. | author=Suping Lu |publisher=[[Hong_Kong_University_Press|Hong Kong University Press]] |year=2004 |page=137 |ISBN=9789622096868 }}</ref><ref name=autogenerated3>{{Cite book|title=A Mission Under Duress: The Nanjing Massacre and Post-massacre Social Conditions Documented by American Diplomats |editor=Suping Lu |publisher=University Press of America |year=2010 |page=331 |ISBN=9780761851509 }}</ref>，[[南京安全区|南京安全区]]国际委员会成员和管理委员会秘书<ref name="鼓楼地方志">{{cite book|author1=南京市鼓楼区地方志编纂委员会|title=鼓楼区志|date=2006-03-01|publisher=中华书局|location=北京|isbn=7101046371|pages=1502|edition=第一版|url=http://dfz.njgl.gov.cn/art/2013/4/24/art_40675_2306038.html|accessdate=2017-03-18}}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。
==生平==
史迈士早年生活于[[美国|美国]][[大瀑布城|大瀑布城]]<ref name=autogenerated6>{{Cite book|title=They Were in Nanjing: The Nanjing Massacre Witnessed by American and British Nationals, Vol. 1. | author=Suping Lu |publisher=[[Hong_Kong_University_Press|Hong Kong University Press]] |year=2004 |pages=137- |ISBN=9789622096868 }}</ref>，高中毕业后，在[[牧场|牧场]]工作<ref name=autogenerated5>{{Cite web|title=113 Session-North Carolina Convention of Christian Churches (Disciples of Christ) |date= November 12–14, 1957 |page=21 |url=https://archive.org/details/programsofncdisc01nort}}</ref>。1918年就读于{{lang-en|德雷克大学|Drake University}}，1923年获{{link-en|法学学士学位|Bachelor of Science in Law}}<ref name=autogenerated5 /><ref name=autogenerated6 /><ref name=autogenerated3 />。1927年3月获{{link-en|芝加哥神学院|University of Chicago Divinity School}}实践神学系硕士学位<ref>{{Cite web|title=the University of Chicago Dissertations and Theses: The [University of Chicago] Library |url=http://pi.lib.uchicago.edu/1001/cat/bib/4415439}}</ref>，1928年8月获{{link-en|芝加哥神学院|University of Chicago Divinity School}}基督教神学与伦理系博士学位<ref>{{Cite web|title=the University of Chicago Dissertations and Theses: The [University of Chicago] Library |url=http://pi.lib.uchicago.edu/1001/cat/bib/4261946}}</ref>。他也受到[[芝加哥大学|芝加哥大学]]的[[芝加哥社會學派|芝加哥学派]]教授的影响，尤其是{{link-en|欧内斯特·伯吉斯|Ernest Burgess}}和[[罗伯特·艾兹拉·帕克|罗伯特·艾兹拉·帕克]]<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Editor's Note | author=M. F. Nimkoff |journal=Journal of Marriage and Family Living (now the [[Journal_of_Marriage_and_Family|Journal of Marriage and Family]]) |year=1952 |volume=30 | issue=4 |pages=272–273 }}</ref>。1928年，史迈士被[[印第安纳波利斯|印第安纳波利斯]]基督教联合传教会派往[[南京|南京]]，担任[[金陵大学|金陵大学]]社会学教授<ref name=autogenerated6 />。

1937年[[金陵大学|金陵大学]]西迁，史迈士与[[贝德士|贝德士]]、[[林查理|林查理]]等人组成留守委员会留校看守<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://historymuseum.nju.edu.cn/univerhistory/index_02.asp?column=01|title=南京大学校史|accessdate=2017-05-20|work=historymuseum.nju.edu.cn}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.chinanews.com/cul/2012/01-20/3618556.shtml|title=真实版《金陵十三钗》：费吴生将胶卷缝进大衣出城-中新网|accessdate=2017-05-20|last=chinanews|work=www.chinanews.com}}</ref>。[[南京大屠杀|南京大屠杀]]期间，史迈士担任[[南京安全区|南京安全区]]国际委员会成员和管理委员会秘书<ref name="鼓楼地方志"/><ref name=autogenerated4>{{Cite book|title=Protestant Medical Missionary Experience During the War in China 1937-1945: The Case of Hubei Province |author=Jocelyn Mary Chatterton |publisher=School of Oriental and African Studies, University of London |year=2010 |url=http://eprints.soas.ac.uk/16916/2/Jocelyn.pdf}}</ref><ref name=nankingdocument>{{Cite book|title=Documents on the rape of Nanking |editor=Timothy Brook |publisher=University of Michigan Press: Ann Arbor Paperbacks |year=1999 |ISBN=978-0-472-08662-7 }}</ref>，与[[约翰·拉贝|约翰·拉贝]]一起记录了日军暴行，并与[[日本大使馆|日本大使馆]]交涉<ref name=nankingdocument/>。[[二战|二战]]结束后，前往[[远东国际军事法庭|远东国际军事法庭]]作证。

1951年，在[[中国共产党|中国共产党]]要求下，史迈士被迫离开[[中国|中国]]<ref name=autogenerated1>{{Cite web|title=The Courier-Journal newspaper: Louisville, Kentucky |date= December 4, 1953 |page=51 |url=https://www.newspapers.com/newspage/108476558/}}</ref><ref name=autogenerated7>{{Cite web|title=The Bulletin, Spring 2008 (Vol. 44, No. 2): The Newsletter of the Lexington Theological Seminary |year=2008 |page=5 |url=https://www.lextheo.edu/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/bulletinspring08.pdf}}</ref>。史迈士随后担任几个组织的顾问，撰写有关中国社会变革的文章和书籍<ref name=autogenerated8 />。1952年-1964年，担任{{link-en|列克星敦神学院|Lexington Theological Seminary}}教授<ref>{{Cite book|title=Lexington Theological Seminary, 1865-1965: The College of the Bible Century |editor=Dwight E. Stevenson |year=1964 |pages=301, 331, 344 |publisher=The Bethany Press |ASIN=B000HHNQES}}</ref><ref name=autogenerated5 /><ref>{{Cite journal|title=The Success of Chinese Families as Families | author=Lewis S. C. Smythe |journal=Journal of Marriage and Family Living (now the [[Journal_of_Marriage_and_Family|Journal of Marriage and Family]]) |year=1952 |volume=30 | issue=4 |pages=286–294 }}</ref>。1957年-1958年休假一年，并担任[[菲律宾|菲律宾]]{{link-en|西利曼大学|Silliman University}}校长助理<ref name=autogenerated7 /><ref name=autogenerated2>{{Cite web|title=The Courier-Journal newspaper: Louisville, Kentucky |date= February 8, 1957 |page=22 |url=https://www.newspapers.com/newspage/108808651/}}</ref>，制定了当地的农村发展计划<ref>{{Cite book|title=Lexington Theological Seminary, 1865-1965: The College of the Bible Century |editor=Dwight E. Stevenson |year=1964 |pages=351 |publisher=The Bethany Press |ASIN=B000HHNQES}}</ref>。
==家庭==
史迈士于本科期间认识了玛格丽特·马迪·莉莲·加勒特（{{lang|en|Margaret "Mardie" Lillian Garrett}}）<ref name=autogenerated7 />，两人于1924年结婚<ref name=autogenerated6 />，后来有了两个女儿<ref name=autogenerated9>{{Cite web|title=China News Letter - Letters from Phillips House to friends in the United States | publisher=The Nanking Massacre Project: Yale Divinity School, Yale University |url=http://divinity-adhoc.library.yale.edu/Nanking/Images/NMP0263.pdf}}</ref><ref name=autogenerated4 />：玛格丽特·佩吉安（{{lang|en|Margaret "Peggy" Ann}}，1931年-2016年）和柯·琼（{{lang|en|E. Joan}}，1934年-1963年）<ref name=autogenerated9 /><ref>{{Cite web|title=The Cincinnati Enquirer newspaper: Cincinnati, Ohio |date=January 27, 1945 |page=9 |url=https://www.newspapers.com/newspage/104079797/}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=The Courier-Journal newspaper: Louisville, Kentucky |date= May 15, 1957 |page=17 |url=https://www.newspapers.com/newspage/108138986/}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=The SASA News, March 2016 (No. 79) | author=Shanghai American School Association |year=2016 |pages=2–3 |url=https://www.saschina.org/uploaded/community/Alumni/SASA_20Newsletter_Spring_2016.pdf}}</ref>。玛格丽特出生于[[南京|南京]]的一个美国传教士家庭<ref name=autogenerated6 />，后来取得了{{link-en|拉什大学|Rush University}}[[医学博士|医学博士]]学位<ref name=autogenerated1 />，在华期间担任[[医疗传教士|医疗传教士]]<ref name=autogenerated5 /><ref name=autogenerated2 />。

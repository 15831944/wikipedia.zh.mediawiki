'''史蒂芬·克拉布'''（{{lang-en|'''Stephen Crabb'''}}；{{bd|1973年|1月20日||catIdx=T}}），是威爾斯[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]黨員與自2005年起的[[英國國會|英國國會]][[普里塞里-彭布羅克郡_(英國國會選區)|普里塞里-彭布羅克郡選區]][[國會議員|下議院議員]]，在2014年7月15日，他獲委任為{{le|威爾斯事務大臣|Secretary of State for Wales}}<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-politics-19495871 | work=BBC News | title=MP Stephen Crabb wins Wales Office promotion | date=5 September 2012}}</ref>。他曾是一位[[黨鞭|黨鞭]]和威爾士大臣<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-politics-19495797 | work=BBC News | title=The reshuffle: twist in the tail | date=5 September 2012}}</ref>；BBC描述他曾擁有同時作為黨鞭及大臣的不尋常雙重角色<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-politics-19497621 | work=BBC News | title=The unexpected Wales Office call for Baroness Randerson | date=5 September 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-wales-19499236 | work=BBC News | title=Stephen Crabb MP on his new job in Wales Office | date=6 September 2012}}</ref>。

2016年，[[戴维·卡梅伦|卡梅倫]]辭任首相和保守黨黨魁職位，觸發[[2016年英國保守黨黨魁選舉|保守黨黨魁選舉]]。史蒂芬·克拉布宣布競逐<ref name="CrabbTeam">{{cite web|url=http://www.politico.eu/article/work-and-pensions-secretary-stephen-crabb-declares-he-is-running-to-replace-david-cameron-as-conservative-party-leader/|title=Stephen Crabb declares he is running to replace David Cameron|publisher=[[Politico_Europe|Politico Europe]]|last=McTague|first=Tom|date=28 June 2016}}</ref>，在第一輪投票中排名尾二，決定退選並在決勝局中支持[[文翠珊|文翠珊]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.standard.co.uk/news/politics/conservative-party-leadership-race-stephen-crabb-pulls-out-of-contest-to-leave-threehorse-race-a3288791.html|title=Stephen Crabb pulls out of Tory leadership contest|newspaper=Evening Standard|accessdate=2017-12-04|language=en-GB}}</ref>。

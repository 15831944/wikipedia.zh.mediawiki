'''史特凡·拉森'''（{{lang|sv|Stefan Larsson}}，{{bd| 1974/1975年}}）是[[雷夫·羅倫馬球|雷夫·羅倫馬球]][[執行長|執行長]]。

==早年生活==
拉森在芬蘭[[漢肯經濟學院|漢肯經濟學院]]及瑞典{{le|苑學品大學國際商學院|Jönköping International Business School}}取得[[企業管理碩士|企業管理碩士]]雙聯學位。<ref name=bloomberg>{{cite web|title=Executive Profile* Stefan Larsson|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=222438511&privcapId=274265|website=Bloomberg.com |publisher=[[Bloomberg|Bloomberg]]|accessdate=2015-09-30 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151002021456/http://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=222438511&privcapId=274265 |archivedate=2015-10-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |first=Susan |last=Adams |title=Who Is Stefan Larsson, Ralph Lauren's New CEO? |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/susanadams/2015/09/30/who-is-stefan-larsson-ralph-laurens-new-ceo|website=forbes.com |publisher=[[Forbes|Forbes]] |date=2015-09-30 |accessdate=2017-04-12 }}</ref>

==職業生涯==
最初，拉森任職於瑞典時裝公司[[H&M|H&M]]長達15年，從基層一路升任至全球銷售主管，負責管理2,300間店面，造就每年約170億美元的銷售額。<ref name=NYtimes /><ref name=bloomberg />在他擔任公司高層的期間，年營收從30億美元增長至170億美元，將營業據點從12個拓展至44個國家。此外，他也負責監督店面的設計及施工。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Babcock|first1=Gregory|title=Everything You Need to Know About Ralph Lauren's New CEO, Stefan Larsson|url=http://www.complex.com/style/2015/09/who-is-stefan-larsson-ralph-lauren-new-ceo/|website=complex.com|publisher=Complex Media Inc.|accessdate=2015-09-30}}</ref>

進入雷夫·羅倫馬球前，拉森曾在2012年10月至2015年9月間擔任[[蓋璞|蓋璞]]旗下品牌[[Old_Navy|Old Navy]]的全球總裁。他讓Old Navy成為蓋璞最賺錢的品牌，獲利連續三年成長，營收更勝過[[香蕉共和國|香蕉共和國]]及蓋璞。<ref>{{cite web|last1=La Monica|first1=Paul|title=Gap shares plunge after Old Navy chief leaves for Ralph Lauren|url=http://money.cnn.com/2015/09/30/investing/gap-stock-old-navy-ralph-lauren/|website=money.cnn.com|publisher=[[CNN|CNN]]|accessdate=2015-10-01|date=2015-09-30}}</ref>拉森藉由提供低價流行服飾的策略，並徹底重組品牌的全球供應鏈，提高產品上市速度，讓Old Navy增加近10億美元的收入。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Dziemianowicz|first1=Joe|title=Meet Stefan Larsson, the Old Navy exec who's taking over at Ralph Lauren|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/life-style/fashion/old-navy-executive-stefan-larsson-named-ced-ralph-lauren-article-1.2380472|website=nydailynews.com|publisher=New York Daily News| accessdate=2015-10-01|date=2015-09-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Russell|first1=Michell|title=New CEO likely to focus on Ralph Lauren supply chain|url=http://www.just-style.com/news/new-ceo-likely-to-focus-on-ralph-lauren-supply-chain_id126293.aspx|website=just-style.com|publisher=Just Style| accessdate=2015-10-01 }}</ref> 

2015年11月，拉森接下該公司創辦人[[雷夫·羅倫|雷夫·羅倫]]的執行長職位，羅倫則繼續擔任執行董事長兼創意長。<ref name=NYtimes>{{cite news|last1=Tabuchi|first1=Hiroko|last2=Friedman|first2=Vanessa|title=Ralph Lauren, Creator of Fashion Empire, Is Stepping Down as C.E.O.|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2015/09/30/business/ralph-lauren-creator-of-fashion-empire-steps-down-as-ceo.html|accessdate= 2015-09-30|website=nytimes.com|publisher=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=2015-09-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Bracket|first1=Ed|title=Ralph Lauren leaving CEO post|url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/money/business/2015/09/29/ralph-lauren-stepping-down-ceo/73046654/|website=usatoday.com|publisher=[[USA_Today|USA Today]]|accessdate=2015-09-30}}</ref>2017年2月，雷夫·羅倫馬球宣布拉森將於5月1日辭去不到兩年的執行長職務。<ref>{{cite news |last1= Hoi-Nga Stephanie |first1= Wong |title=Ralph Lauren CEO Leaving After Creative Clash With Founder|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2017-02-02/ralph-lauren-ceo-is-leaving-company-after-clash-with-founder |website= bloomberg.com |publisher= [[Bloomberg_Businessweek|Bloomberg Businessweek]] |date=2017-02-02 |accessdate=2017-04-12}}</ref>

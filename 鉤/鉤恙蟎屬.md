'''鉤恙蟎屬'''（{{lang|la|'''''Leptotrombidium'''''}}；({{IPAc-en|ˌ|l|ɛ|p|t|oʊ|t|r|ɒ|m|ˈ|b|ɪ|d|i|ə|m}}{{refn|{{Dictionary.com|accessdate=2016-01-23|Leptotrombidium}}}}）是[[蛛形綱|蛛形綱]][[蜱蟎亞綱|蜱蟎亞綱]][[蟎形總目|蟎形總目]][[恙蟎目|恙蟎目]][[恙蟎總科|恙蟎總科]][[恙蟎科|恙蟎科]]之下的一個屬，可透過叮咬人類而令人類感染[[恙蟲病|恙蟲病]]（[[恙蟲東方體|恙蟲東方體]]感染，亦作[[叢林斑疹傷寒|叢林斑疹傷寒]]）<ref name="IASR"/>。鉤恙蟎屬的幼虫主要宿主是[[啮齿类|啮齿类]]，但有时也能寄生于人类或其他大型哺乳类动物中。

== Life history ==
The larva is pale orange in colour and feeds on liquified skin tissue, not blood, as their mouth parts (chelicerae) are too short to reach the blood vessels.<ref>
{{cite journal
| title = Distribution of ''Rickettsia tsutsugamushi'' in organs of ''Leptotrombidium (Leptotrombidium) fletcheri'' (Prostigmata: Trombiculidae)
| journal = J Med Entomol
| volume = 12
| issue = 3

| url = http://www.ingentaconnect.com/content/esa/jme/1975/00000012/00000003/art00015
 |vauthors=Roberts LW, Robinson DM, Rapmund G, etal }}</ref> They have 3 pairs of legs. The larvae most commonly target rodents, but will also attach to humans.<ref name="Pham"/> For humans, the bite is painless, but pain commonly develops only after the larva detaches from the skin, leaving a red papule that may then develop into an eschar.<ref>

The larval stage lasts for 1 to 2 weeks. After feeding, the larvae drop to the ground and become nymphs. Nymph is brick-red in colour and has 4 pairs of legs. Nymphal stage lasts for 1 to 3 weeks. Nymphs mature into adults which have 4 pairs of legs, first pair being the largest. They are harmless to humans. In the post larval stage, they are not parasitic and feed on plant materials.<ref>

《'''[[瑞克和莫蒂|瑞克和莫蒂]]'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Rick and Morty'''''}}）是一系列於[[Adult_Swim|Adult Swim]]播出的美國電視科幻[[情景喜劇|情景喜劇]]動畫，該劇於2013年12月2日首播<ref name="TV Guide S1">{{cite web|url=http://www.tvguide.com/tvshows/rick-and-morty/episodes-season-1/617769|title=''Rick and Morty'' Season 1 episodes|publisher=CBS Interactive|work=TV Guide|accessdate=2014-02-26|deadurl=no|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20140226074339/http://www.tvguide.com/tvshows/rick-and-morty/episodes-season-1/617769|archivedate=2014-02-26}}</ref>。

待第二季於2015年7月播出後。2015年8月，Adult Swim續訂了第三季，共10集<ref name="Deadline article 2015-08-12">{{cite web | url= https://deadline.com/2015/08/rick-and-morty-renewed-season-3-adult-swim-dan-harmon-justin-roiland-1201497834/ | title= ‘Rick And Morty’ Renewed For Season 3 By Adult Swim | publisher= Penske Business Media, LLC | work= Deadline | first= Denise | last= Petski | date= 2015-08-12 | accessdate= 2015-08-12 | deadurl= no | archiveurl= http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20161013015418/https://deadline.com/2015/08/rick-and-morty-renewed-season-3-adult-swim-dan-harmon-justin-roiland-1201497834/ | archivedate= 2016-10-13 }}</ref>。第三季的第一集於2017年4月1日首播，其餘的集數於2017年7月30日播出<ref>{{cite web|title='Rick and Morty' Season 3 Gets Premiere Date and Trailer|url=http://ew.com/tv/2017/06/29/rick-and-morty-trailer-premiere-date-season-3/|website=EW.com|accessdate=2017-06-30|date=2017-06-30|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170630052150/http://ew.com/tv/2017/06/29/rick-and-morty-trailer-premiere-date-season-3/|archivedate=2017-06-30}}</ref>。

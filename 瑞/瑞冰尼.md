'''瑞冰尼'''（{{lang-my|ရွှေဖျင်းငယ်}}，{{IPA-my|ʃwèbjɪ́ɴ ŋɛ̀|pron}}）是37尊[[緬甸神靈|緬甸神靈]]之一。他是[[瑞冰祇|瑞冰祇]]的弟弟，{{link-en|波巴梅杜|Popa Medaw}}與{{link-en|裴多|Byatta}}的兒子。瑞冰尼與兄長生前因建造[[佛塔|佛塔]]時玩忽職守，被[[阿奴律陀|阿奴律陀]]處死，後被追認為[[納_(緬甸)|納]]。瑞冰尼的信眾忌食[[豬肉|豬肉]]，因為他的父親{{link-en|裴多|Byatta}}被認為是來自[[印度|印度]]的[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]軍官。<ref>《緬甸史》，戈·埃·哈威著，姚梓良譯，65頁、129頁</ref><ref>Hmannan Yazawin，第1卷，246–250頁</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.irrawaddy.org/archives/42088|title=Myanmar’s Multicultural Spirits|last=Gilbert|first=David|date=15 August 2013|work=The Irrawaddy|accessdate=8 September 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.myanmar.gov.mm/Perspective/persp1997/8-97/nat8-97.htm|title=The Taung Byone Nat Festival|author=Shwe Mann Maung|publisher=''Perspective'' (August 1997)|accessdate=2008-09-11|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20040717075934/http://www.myanmar.gov.mm/Perspective/persp1997/8-97/nat8-97.htm|archivedate=2004-07-17|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Qs2SB4XanBUC&pg=PA158&lpg=PA158&dq=nat+spirits&source=web&ots=4T1_i7nCyX&sig=xtDhIDMENUn3bxxoZRYXjJDYKzI&hl=en&sa=X&oi=book_result&resnum=3&ct=result#PPA158,M1|title=Haunting the Buddha: Indian Popular Religions and the Formation of Buddhism|last=DeCaroli|first=Robert|year=2004|publisher=[[Oxford_University_Press|Oxford University Press]], US|accessdate=2008-09-13 | isbn=978-0-19-516838-9}}</ref>

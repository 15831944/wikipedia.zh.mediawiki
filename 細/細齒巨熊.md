'''細齒巨熊'''（學名：'''''Arctotherium'''''）是已滅絕的[[南美洲|南美洲]][[熊科|熊科]][[短臉熊|短臉熊]]<ref name="Soibelzon">{{Cite journal|last=Soibelzon|first=L. H.|author2= Tonni, E. P.|author3= Bond, M.|title =The fossil record of South American short-faced bears (Ursidae, Tremarctinae)|journal= Journal of South American Earth Sciences|volume= 20|issue = 1–2 |pages= 105–113|date= October 2005| doi = 10.1016/j.jsames.2005.07.005}}</ref>。牠們的祖先在[[更新世|更新世]]晚期時，透過[[巴拿馬地峽|巴拿馬地峽]]自[[北美洲|北美洲]]遷徙至南美洲（稱為「[[南北美洲生物大遷徙|南北美洲生物大遷徙]]」）。目前已知最早的細齒巨熊來自{{link-en|Ensenadan}}地層（約為1千2百萬年前的更新世早至中期）。牠們現存親緣關係最近的近親為[[眼鏡熊|眼鏡熊]]<ref>{{cite journal |author=Kieren J. Mitchell |author2=Sarah C. Bray |author3=Pere Bover |author4=Leopoldo Soibelzon |author5=Blaine W. Schubert |author6=Francisco Prevosti |author7=Alfredo Prieto |author8=Fabiana Martin |author9=Jeremy J. Austin |author10=Alan Cooper |last-author-amp=yes |year=2016 |title=Ancient mitochondrial DNA reveals convergent evolution of giant short-faced bears (Tremarctinae) in North and South America |journal=Biology Letters |volume=12 |issue=4 |pages=20160062 |doi=10.1098/rsbl.2016.0062 |pmid=27095265 |pmc=4881349}}</ref>。

== 描述 ==
細齒巨熊由{{link-en|Hermann Burmeister}}於1879年命名。從[[布宜諾斯艾利斯|布宜諾斯艾利斯]]出土的 ''A. angustidens'' [[肱骨|肱骨]]化石推算，細齒巨熊的體重約在 {{convert|983|至|2042|kg|abbr=on}} 之間，然而這樣的推算被認為不太可能，因此最後保守的估計體重為 {{convert|1588|至|1749|kg|abbr=on}}。細齒巨熊為目前已知體型最大的熊，以及體型最大的陸域[[肉食性|肉食性]]哺乳動物<ref name = "Soibelzon2">{{Cite journal | last = Soibelzon | first = L. H. |author2=Schubert, B. W. | title = The Largest Known Bear, ''Arctotherium angustidens'', from the Early Pleistocene Pampean Region of Argentina: With a Discussion of Size and Diet Trends in Bears | journal = {{link-en|Journal of Paleontology}} | volume = 85 | issue = 1 | pages = 69–75 | publisher = {{link-en|Paleontological Society}} | date = January 2011 | url = http://jpaleontol.geoscienceworld.org/cgi/content/abstract/85/1/69 | doi = 10.1666/10-037.1 | accessdate = 2011-06-01}}</ref><ref name = "Dell'Amore">Dell'Amore, C. (2011): [http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2011/02/110203-biggest-bear-largest-giant-short-faced-animals-science/ Biggest Bear Ever Found], [[National_Geographic_Society|National Geographic]] News, Published February 3, 2011</ref>。體型巨大的原因在於，細齒巨熊必須和許多後來同樣自北美洲遷徙過來的[[食肉目|食肉目]]競爭，包括[[美洲豹|美洲豹]]、[[美洲擬獅|美洲擬獅]]以及[[斯劍虎|斯劍虎]]，細齒巨熊可以利用體型上的優勢掠奪牠們的獵物<ref name = "Soibelzon2"/><ref name = "Dell'Amore"/><ref name = "Hodge">{{Cite web | last = Hodge | first = A.-M. | title = Updated Range of Immensity for Arctotherium: New Record for Largest Known Bear | work = blogs.nature.com | publisher = [[自然出版集團|自然出版集團]] | date = 2011-03-31 | url = http://blogs.nature.com/amch/2011/03/31/south-america-is-currently-home | accessdate = 2011-06-01}}</ref>。這些自北美洲遷徙過來的食肉目成員很快地就取代了原本南美洲掠食者的[[生態位|生態位]]，包括[[後獸下綱|後獸下綱]]的[[袋犬目|袋犬目]]以及[[駭鳥|駭鳥]]。然而根據 ''A. angustidens''（滅絕於中更新世）與 ''A. wingei''（滅絕於早[[全新世|全新世]]）樣本的觀察，隨著時間演變，細齒巨熊的體型有縮小的趨勢，食性也由肉食性逐漸轉變為[[雜食性|雜食性]]<ref name = "Soibelzon2"/>。

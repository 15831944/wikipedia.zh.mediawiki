'''細紋蜆蝶屬'''（學名：'''''Calephelis'''''）是[[蜆蝶亞科|蜆蝶亞科]][[蜆蝶族|蜆蝶族]]裡的一個[[屬|屬]]。共有45個物種，廣泛分佈於南北美洲<ref>Tree of Life Web Project. 2007. Calephelis Grote & Robinson 1869. Lephelisca Barnes & Lindsey 1922 junior objective synonym. Version 05 May 2007 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Calephelis/104634/2007.05.05 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>，是整個亞科中極少數出沒於[[新北界|新北界]]的種類<ref name=e2015>Espeland, M., Hall, J. P., DeVries, P. J., Lees, D. C., Cornwall, M., Hsu, Y. F., ... & Salzman, S. 2015. Ancient Neotropical origin and recent recolonisation: Phylogeny, biogeography and diversification of the Riodinidae (Lepidoptera: Papilionoidea). Molecular phylogenetics and evolution, 93, 296-306. {{en}}</ref>。

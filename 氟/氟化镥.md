'''氟化镥'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，[[化学式|化学式]]为LuF<sub>3</sub>。

==制备==
氟化镥可由[[氧化镥|氧化镥]]和[[氟化氢|氟化氢]]反应，或者[[氯化镥|氯化镥]]和[[氢氟酸|氢氟酸]]反应得到：<ref>Georg Brauer (ed.), In collaboration with Marianne Baudler u. a .: Handbook of Preparative Inorganic Chemistry. 3rd, revised edition. Volume I, Ferdinand Enke, Stuttgart 1975, ISBN 3-432-02328-6 , p. 254.</ref>
: Lu<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + 6 HF → 2 LuF<sub>3</sub> + 3 H<sub>2</sub>O
: LuCl<sub>3</sub> + 3 HF → LuF<sub>3</sub> + 3 HCl

氟化镥可由[[硫化镥|硫化镥]]和[[氢氟酸|氢氟酸]]反应，再将沉淀灼烧得到：<ref>{{cite journal|journal=Journal of Fluorine Chemistry|volume=207|language=en|date=2018-3|pages=77–83|doi=10.1016/j.jfluchem.2017.12.001|url=https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0022113917304426|title=Synthesis and thermal stability of rare earth compounds REF 3 , REF 3 · n H 2 O and (H 3 O)RE 3 F 10 · n H 2 O (RE = Tb − Lu, Y), obtained from sulphide precursors|accessdate=2019-03-26|author=O.V. Andrrev, I.A. Razumkova, A.N. Boiko}}</ref>

: 3 Lu<sub>2</sub>S<sub>3</sub> + 20 HF + (2+2x)H<sub>2</sub>O → 2 (H<sub>3</sub>O)Lu<sub>3</sub>F<sub>10</sub>·xH<sub>2</sub>O↓ + 9 H<sub>2</sub>S↑（x=0.9）
: (H<sub>3</sub>O)Lu<sub>3</sub>F<sub>10</sub> → 3 LuF<sub>3</sub> + HF↑ + H<sub>2</sub>O↑

氧化镥和[[三氟化氮|三氟化氮]]的反应在240°C发生，生成中间产物LuOF，第二步在低于460°C时便发生，生成LuF<sub>3</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|journal=Journal of Fluorine Chemistry|volume=146|language=en|date=2013-2|pages=86–97|doi=10.1016/j.jfluchem.2012.12.013|url=https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0022113913000043|title=Thermal NF3 fluorination/oxidation of cobalt, yttrium, zirconium, and selected lanthanide oxides|accessdate=2019-03-26|author=Randall D. Scheele, Bruce K. McNamara, Andrew M. Casella, Anne E. Kozelisky, Doinita Neiner}}</ref>

'''氟化镧'''是一种高熔点的[[离子化合物|离子化合物]]，化学式为LaF<sub>3</sub>。<ref name="Cotton">{{cite book|author=Cotton, Simon |title=Lanthanide and Actinide Chemistry|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SvAbtU6XvzgC&pg=PA26|date=30 January 2007|publisher=Wiley|isbn=978-0-470-01007-5|pages=25–27}}</ref>其Ksp为7.0×10<sup>-17</sup>。<ref name=Wu>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0167577X08001389|pages=3003–3006|title=Influence of solubility on the particle size of fluorides crystalline precipitated from aqueous solutions|journal=Materials Letters|volume=62|issue=17-18|accessdate=2018-04-12|doi=10.1016/j.matlet.2008.01.095|author=Daxiong Wu, Xijun Wu, Yanfei Lv, Hui Wang}}</ref>

==制备==
氟化镧可由[[氧化镧|氧化镧]]和[[氟化氢铵|氟化氢铵]]反应得到：<ref>scribd.com: [https://web.archive.org/web/20101027102127/http://www.scribd.com/doc/17105402/9439811 http://www.scribd.com/doc/17105402/9439811] ([https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Web-Archivierung#Begriffsbestimmung Memento] vom 27. Oktober 2010 im ''[[互联网档案馆|Internet Archive]]'')</ref>

:<math>\mathrm{ La_2O_3 + 6 \ NH_4HF_2 \ \xrightarrow{300 - 400^{\circ}C} \ 2 \ LaF_3 + 6 \ NH_4F + 3 \ H_2O}</math>
[[硝酸镧|硝酸镧]]和[[氟化铵|氟化铵]]反应，沉淀出氟化镧：<ref name=Wu />
:La(NO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub> + 3 NH<sub>4</sub>F → LaF<sub>3</sub>↓ + 3 NH<sub>4</sub>NO<sub>3</sub>

'''氟化钪'''是一种离子化合物，化学式为{{chem|ScF|3}}。它有着[[三氟化钒|三氟化钒]]（VF<sub>3</sub>）的结构，与[[二氧化铼|二氧化铼]]的ReO<sub>3</sub>结构单元类似，有六配位的金属离子。它微溶于水，在过量氟化物的存在下形成{{chem|ScF|6|3-}}而可溶。<ref name = "Wiberg&Holleman"/>

==制备==
[[氟|氟]]和[[钪|钪]]的反应可以生成ScF<sub>3</sub>。<ref name = "Simon Cotton">S.A.Cotton, Scandium, Yttrium and the Lanthanides: Inorganic and Coordination Chemistry, Encyclopedia of Inorganic Chemistry, 1994, John Wiley & Sons, {{ISBN|0-471-93620-0}}.</ref>[[氟化氢铵|氟化氢铵]]在高温处理{{le|钪钇石|thortveitite}}也能得到氟化钪。<ref name = "Patnaik">Pradyot Patnaik, 2003, ''Handbook of Inorganic Chemicals'', McGraw-Hill Professional, {{ISBN|0-07-049439-8}}.</ref>
: Sc<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + NH<sub>4</sub>HF<sub>2</sub> → 2 ScF<sub>3</sub> + 6 NH<sub>4</sub>F + 3 H<sub>2</sub>O

==性质==
氟化钪有着{{le|负热膨胀效应|negative thermal expansion}}这一不寻常的性质，即它在加热的时候收缩。<ref name="woo">{{cite web|url=http://www.physorg.com/news/2011-11-incredible-material-reveal-scandium-trifluoride.html|title=An incredible shrinking material: Engineers reveal how scandium trifluoride contracts with heat|last=Woo|first=Marcus|date=7 November 2011|publisher=Physorg|accessdate=8 November 2011}}</ref>

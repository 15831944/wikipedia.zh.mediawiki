'''氟硼酸钠'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为NaBF<sub>4</sub>。它是无色或白色正交晶体，易溶于水（108 g/100 mL），在有机溶剂中溶解性较小。<ref>{{Cite journal | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=oWdc2qcb3QsC&pg=PA564&dq=Sodium+tetrafluoroborate&cd=5#v=onepage&q=Sodium%20tetrafluoroborate&f=false | title = Gardner's Commercially Important Chemicals: Synonyms, Trade Names, and Properties | isbn = 9780471735182 | author1 = Milne | first1 = G. W. A | date = 2005-07-11}}</ref>

氟硼酸钠可在焊接或制备[[三氟化硼|三氟化硼]]中当熔体。<ref name="Georg">{{cite book|last=Brauer|first=Georg|title=Handbook of Preparative Inorganic Chemistry Vol. 1, 2nd Ed.|date=1963|publisher=Academic Press|location=Newyork|isbn=978-0121266011|page=220 & 222|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=TLYatwAACAAJ&dq=Handbook+of+Preparative+Inorganic+Chemistry&hl=en&sa=X&ei=IPT2UtTZGMfgoASR1IGgDg&ved=0CC8Q6AEwAQ}}</ref>

==制备==
氟硼酸钠可由[[碳酸钠|碳酸钠]]或[[氢氧化钠|氢氧化钠]]和[[氟硼酸|氟硼酸]]反应得到：<ref name ="concise">{{Cite journal | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=Owuv-c9L_IMC&pg=PA1001&dq=Sodium+tetrafluoroborate&cd=19#v=onepage&q=Sodium%20tetrafluoroborate&f=false | title = Concise Encyclopedia Chemistry | isbn = 9783110114515 | author1 = Eagleson | first1 = Mary | year = 1994}}</ref>
:NaOH + HBF<sub>4</sub> →   NaBF<sub>4</sub>  +  H<sub>2</sub>O
:Na<sub>2</sub>CO<sub>3</sub>  +  2 HBF<sub>4</sub>  →  2 NaBF<sub>4</sub>  +  H<sub>2</sub>O  +  CO<sub>2</sub>

[[碳酸钠|碳酸钠]]、[[硼酸|硼酸]]和[[氢氟酸|氢氟酸]]之间的反应也可制得氟硼酸钠：<ref name="Georg"></ref>

:2H<sub>3</sub>BO<sub>3</sub> + 8 HF + Na<sub>2</sub>CO<sub>3</sub> → 2NaBF<sub>4</sub> + 7H<sub>2</sub>O + CO<sub>2</sub>

==性质==
将氟硼酸钠加热至熔化，它分解为[[氟化钠|氟化钠]]和[[三氟化硼|三氟化硼]]：<ref>{{Cite journal | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=LkIWiNg4FGEC&pg=PA1271&dq=%22Sodium+tetrafluoroborate%22 | title = Hazardous Chemicals Desk Reference | isbn = 9780470334454 | author1 = Richard j. Lewis | first1 = Sr | date = 2008-07-14}}</ref>
:NaBF<sub>4</sub>   →   NaF  +  BF<sub>3</sub>

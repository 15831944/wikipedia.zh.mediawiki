'''氟硼酸镉'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Cd(BF<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。

==制备==
氟硼酸镉可以通过[[氟硼酸|氟硼酸]]的水溶液和[[碳酸镉|碳酸镉]]或[[氧化镉|氧化镉]]反应来制备：<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/substance/12886773 |title=Compound Summary for PubChem CID 12886773 |last= |first= |date=2017 |website=NIH |archive-url= |archive-date= |dead-url= |access-date=}}</ref>
:<chem>{H3OBF4_(aq)} + {CdCO3_(s)} ->  {Cd(BF4)2_(aq)} + {HCO3^-_(aq)} + {H2O_(l)} </chem>
:<chem>{H3OBF4_(aq)} + {CdO_(s)} -> {Cd(BF4)2_(aq)} + {HO^-_(aq)} + {H2O_(l)}</chem>

[[氟硼酸亚硝酰|氟硼酸亚硝酰]]和金属[[镉|镉]]的[[氧化还原反应|氧化还原反应]]也能制得氟硼酸镉：<ref>{{Cite journal |last1=Hathaway |first1=B. J. |last2=Holah |first2=D. G. |last3=Underhill |first3=A. E. |date=1962 |title=The preparation and properties of some bivalent transition-metal tetrafluoroborate–methyl cyanide complexes |url= |journal=Journal of the Chemical Society |volume= |pages=2444–8 |doi=10.1039/JR9620002444}}</ref>
:<chem>{Cd(s)} + {2NOBF4} ->[{ethyl acetate}][{methyl cyanide}] {Cd(BF4)2} + {2NO}</chem>

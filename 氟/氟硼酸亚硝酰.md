'''氟硼酸亚硝酰'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为NOBF<sub>4</sub>，无色固体，在[[有机合成|有机合成]]中可用作[[亚硝化反应|亚硝化试剂]]。<ref>{{ cite web | url = http://www.alfa.com/en/GP100W.pgm?DSSTK=A15806 | title = A15806 Nitrosonium tetrafluoroborate, 98% | work = [[Alfa_Aesar|Alfa Aesar]] website | accessdate = 2010-09-04 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110707111715/http://www.alfa.com/en/GP100W.pgm?DSSTK=A15806 | archivedate = 2011年7月7日 | df =  }}</ref>

NOBF<sub>4</sub>是氟硼酸的亚硝酰盐，由[NO]<sup>+</sup>阳离子和[BF<sub>4</sub>]<sup>−</sup>阴离子构成。

==反应==
氟硼酸亚硝酰可用于制备[M<sup>II</sup>(CH<sub>3</sub>CN)<sub>''x''</sub>][BF<sub>4</sub>]<sub>2</sub>类型的盐，其中M为Cr、Mn、Fe、Co、Ni和Cu。亚硝酰阳离子为氧化剂，反应时被还原为[[一氧化氮|一氧化氮]]：<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1002/0471224502.ch2 | journal = [[Inorg._Synth.|Inorg. Synth.]] | volume = 33 | pages = 75–83 | title = 11. Homoleptic Transition Metal Acetonitrile Cations with Tetrafluoroborate or Trifluoromethanesulfonate Anions |author1=Robert A. Heintz |author2=Jennifer A. Smith |author3=Paul S. Szalay |author4=Amy Weisgerber |author5=And Kim R. Dunbar }}</ref>

: M + NOBF<sub>4</sub> + ''x''CH<sub>3</sub>CN → [M(CH<sub>3</sub>CN)<sub>''x''</sub>](BF<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + NO

它和[[二茂铁|二茂铁]]反应，生成[[氟硼酸二茂铁|氟硼酸二茂铁]]。<ref>{{cite journal | title = Solvent and electrolyte effects on the kinetics of ferrocenium-ferrocene self-exchange. A reevaluation |author1=Roger M. Nielson |author2=George E. McManis |author3=Lance K. Safford |author4=Michael J. Weaver | journal = [[J._Phys._Chem.|J. Phys. Chem.]] | doi = 10.1021/j100342a086 | year = 1989 | volume = 93 | issue = 5 | pages = 2152}}</ref>

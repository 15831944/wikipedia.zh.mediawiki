'''氟氯化钡'''是[[钡|钡]]的混合[[卤化物|卤化物]]，化学式为BaClF。它在自然界中以[[張培善石|張培善石]]的形式存在。<ref>Shimazaki H, Miyawaki R, Yokoyama K, et al. [http://www.schweizerbart.de/content/papers_preview/download/58276 Zhangpeishanite, BaFCl, a new mineral in fluorite from Bayan Obo, Inner Mongolia, China][J]. European Journal of Mineralogy, 2008, 20(6):1141-1144.</ref>

==制备==
氟氯化钡可由[[氯化钡|氯化钡]]和[[氟化铵|氟化铵]]在溶液中沉淀制备。<ref>Wang, Xianglei; Liu, Zhiqiang; Stevens-Kalceff, Marion A.; Riesen, Hans. A facile method for the preparation of Eu2+-​doped nanocrystalline BaFCl. ''Materials Research Bulletin'', 2013. 48 (10): 3691-3694</ref>

在[[幾何學|幾何學]]中，'''十二合十二面體'''是一種[[凹多面體|凹多面體]]，屬於[[星形多面體|星形多面體]]及[[均勻多面體|均勻多面體]]，也可以歸類在非凸均勻多面體，其索引為[[Uniform_polyhedron|U]]<sub>36</sub>, [[Harold_Scott_MacDonald_Coxeter|C]]<sub>45</sub>, [[List_of_Wenninger_polyhedron_models|W]]<sub>73</sub>。

== Wythoff 佈局==
十二合十二面體有四種[[Wythoff_construction|Wythoff construction]]有四個[[Schwarz_triangle|Schwarz triangle]]: '''2 | 5 5/2''', '''2 | 5 5/3''', '''2 | 5/2 5/4''', '''2 | 5/3 5/4'''但代表相同的多面體。同樣地，可以給出四個[[施萊夫利符號|施萊夫利符號]]： t<sub>1</sub>{5/2,5}, t<sub>1</sub>{5/3,5}, t<sub>1</sub>{5/2,5/4}, and t<sub>1</sub>{5/3,5/4}. 也可以有四種{{link-en|考克斯特-迪肯符號|Coxeter-Dynkin digram|考克斯特符號}}表示法: {{CDD|node|5|rat|d2|node_1|5|node}}, {{CDD|node|5|rat|d3|node_1|5|node}}, {{CDD|node|5|rat|d2|node_1|5|rat|d4|node}}, and {{CDD|node|5|rat|d3|node_1|5|rat|d4|node}}.

== Related polyhedra ==

Its [[convex_hull|convex hull]] is the [[icosidodecahedron|icosidodecahedron]]. It also shares its [[edge_arrangement|edge arrangement]] with the [[small_dodecahemicosahedron|small dodecahemicosahedron]] (having the pentagrammic faces in common), and with the [[great_dodecahemicosahedron|great dodecahemicosahedron]] (having the pentagonal faces in common).

This polyhedron can be considered a [[Rectification_(geometry)|rectified]] [[great_dodecahedron|great dodecahedron]]. It is center of a truncation sequence between a [[small_stellated_dodecahedron|small stellated dodecahedron]] and [[great_dodecahedron|great dodecahedron]]:

The [[Truncation_(geometry)|truncated]] small stellated dodecahedron looks like a [[dodecahedron|dodecahedron]] on the surface, but it has 24 faces: 12 [[pentagons|pentagons]] from the truncated vertices and 12 overlapping as (truncated pentagrams). The truncation of the dodecadodecahedron itself is not uniform, but it has a uniform quasitruncation, the [[truncated_dodecadodecahedron|truncated dodecadodecahedron]].

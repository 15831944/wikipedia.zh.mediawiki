'''流行性腦炎'''或'''甲型脑炎'''是一種非典型的[[腦炎|腦炎]]，又稱為'''嗜睡性腦炎'''（但不是由[[采采蠅|采采蠅]]（tsetse fly）傳播的[[非洲人类锥虫病|非洲昏睡症]]）。此病最先由[[神經學|神經學]]家[[Constantin_von_Economo|Constantin von Economo]]于1917年报告。<ref>{{WhoNamedIt|synd|3356}}</ref><ref>K. von Economo. Encepahlitis lethargica. ''Wiener klinische Wochenschrift'', May 10, 1917, 30: 581-585. Die Encephalitis lethargica. Leipzig and Vienna, Franz Deuticke, 1918.</ref> 此病影響大腦，令一些病人不能說話，甚至不能移動。<ref name=Dale_2004>{{cite journal | author = Dale RC, Church AJ, Surtees RA, ''et al.'' | title = Encephalitis lethargica syndrome: 20 new cases and evidence of basal ganglia autoimmunity | journal = Brain | volume = 127 | issue = Pt 1 | pages = 21–33 | year = 2004 | url = http://brain.oxfordjournals.org/cgi/content/full/127/1/21 | pmid = 14570817 | doi = 10.1093/brain/awh008}}</ref> 在1915年到1926年間，<ref>{{DorlandsDict|nine/000952335|Encephalitis lethargica}}</ref> 该疾病曾經發生全球性的大流行；而之後只有零星個案而沒有再爆發。<ref name=Stryker_1925>{{cite journal | author = Stryker Sue B. | title = Encephalitis lethargica: the behavior residuals | journal = Training School Bulletin | volume = 22 | issue = 1925 | pages = 152–7}}</ref><ref name=Reid_2001>{{cite journal | author = Reid AH, McCall S, Henry JM, Taubenberger JK | title = Experimenting on the past: the enigma of von Economo's encephalitis lethargica | journal = J. Neuropathol. Exp. Neurol. | volume = 60 | issue = 7 | pages = 663–70 | year = 2001 | pmid = 11444794 | doi = }}</ref>

==癥狀==
流行性腦炎的癥狀為[[發熱|發高燒]]、[[喉嚨痛|喉嚨痛]]、[[頭痛|頭痛]]、[[重影|重影]]（Double vision）、動作及思想遲緩、日夜巔倒（sleep inversion）, 緊張症（catatonia）和疲勞<ref name=Dale_2004/>。一些急症病人可能進入類似[[昏迷|昏迷]]的狀態。病人的眼也可能出現不正常的運動、<ref name="pmid16200538">{{cite journal |author=Vilensky JA, Goetz CG, Gilman S |title=Movement disorders associated with encephalitis lethargica: a video compilation |journal=Mov. Disord. |volume=21 |issue=1 |pages=1–8 |pmid=16200538 |doi=10.1002/mds.20722|date=January 2006}}</ref>[[柏金遜症|-{zh-hans:巴金森症; zh-hk:柏金遜症; zh-tw:巴金森症;}-候群]]（parkinsonism）、上半身無力、肌肉痛、手震、頸部僵硬和行動變異（如[[思覺失調|思覺失調]]）。

患者可能會在短至一年後患上腦炎後型-{zh-hans:巴金森症; zh-hk:柏金遜症; zh-tw:巴金森症;}-。

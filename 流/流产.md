<!-- Definition and symptoms -->
'''流產'''（{{lang|en|Miscarriage}}），又稱'''自然流產'''（{{lang|en|Spontaneous abortion}}），俗稱'''小產'''。是指[[胚胎|胚胎]]或[[胎兒|胎兒]]發育到{{le|胎兒存活率|Fetal viability|可在子宮外獨立生存}}之前的自然死亡<ref name=John2012/><ref name=NIH2013Def/>有些懷孕20週後的胎兒死亡會稱為[[死產|死產]]<ref name=NIH2013Def>{{cite web|title=What is pregnancy loss/miscarriage?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/pregnancyloss/conditioninfo/Pages/default.aspx|website=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/|accessdate=14 March 2015|date=2013-07-15}}</ref>。常見症狀為無痛{{le|陰道出血|vaginal bleeding}}或伴隨疼痛感，感到[[悲傷|悲傷]]、[[焦慮|焦慮]]、以及[[罪惡感|罪惡感]]<ref name="Pregnancy loss">{{cite journal|last1=Robinson|first1=GE|title=Pregnancy loss.|journal=Best practice & research. Clinical obstetrics & gynaecology|date=January 2014|volume=28|issue=1|pages=169–78|pmid=24047642|doi=10.1016/j.bpobgyn.2013.08.012}}</ref>；組織黏液與血塊也可能從[[陰道|陰道]]流出<ref>{{cite web|title=What are the symptoms of pregnancy loss/miscarriage?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/pregnancyloss/conditioninfo/Pages/symptoms.aspx|website=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/|accessdate=14 March 2015|date=2013-07-15}}</ref>。陰道輕微出血而胎兒繼續成長的，稱'''先兆流產'''<ref>[https://www.fhs.gov.hk/tc_chi/health_info/woman/15681.html 家庭健康服務 - 健康資訊 >> 婦女健康]</ref>或'''作小產'''<ref>[http://orientaldaily.on.cc/cnt/news/20100331/00176_049.html 懷孕初期 勿早下判斷]</ref>（{{lang|en|Threatened abortion}}）。

<!-- Risk factors and diagnosis -->
流產的危險因子包括高齡、流產史、一手與二手菸的環境、[[肥胖|肥胖]]、[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]、使用藥物或酒精等等<ref name=NIH2013Epi/><ref name="Ol2014"/>。孕婦小於35歲的流產風險約為10%，超過40歲則約為45%<ref name=John2012/>；風險約在30歲時開始增加<ref name=NIH2013Epi/>。有80%的流產發生在[[妊娠|妊娠第一期]]<!-- <ref name=John2012/> -->，有一半的潛在因素是{{le|染色體異常|Chromosonal abnormalities}}所致<!-- <ref name=John2012/> -->；會造成類似狀況的其他症狀包括[[異位妊娠|異位妊娠]]和{{le|著床出血|Iimplantation bleeding}}<ref name=John2012/>。診斷流產的方法包括檢查[[子宮頸|子宮頸]]的開合、[[人绒毛膜促性腺激素|人绒毛膜促性腺激素]]的血中濃度和[[超音波|超音波]]<ref>{{cite web|title=How do health care providers diagnose pregnancy loss or miscarriage?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/pregnancyloss/conditioninfo/Pages/diagnosed.aspx|website=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/|accessdate=14 March 2015|date=2013-07-15}}</ref>。

<!-- Prevention and treatment -->
有良好的孕期照護與進行完善的[[產前檢查|產前檢查]]是有可能預防流產的<!-- <ref name=NIH2013Pre/> -->；避免使用藥物及酒精，避免得到感染性疾病，避免接受[[輻射|輻射]]可以避免流產<ref name=NIH2013Pre>{{cite web|title=Is there a cure for pregnancy loss/miscarriage?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/pregnancyloss/conditioninfo/Pages/cure.aspx|website=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/|accessdate=14 March 2015|date=2013-10-21}}</ref>。通常在流產的前七至十四天並無須特別的治療<ref name=Ol2014/><ref name=NIH2013Tx/>。一旦流產的狀況發生，通常無需人為介入就能完成。有時會使用藥物[[米索前列醇|米索前列醇]]或{{tsl|en|vacuum aspiration|真空抽吸術}}幫助移除剩餘的妊娠物<ref name=NIH2013Tx/><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Tunçalp|first1=O|last2=Gülmezoglu|first2=AM|last3=Souza|first3=JP|title=Surgical procedures for evacuating incomplete miscarriage.|journal=The Cochrane database of systematic reviews|date=8 September 2010|issue=9|pages=CD001993|pmid=20824830|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD001993.pub2}}</ref>。[[Rh血型系统|Rh(-)]]血型的婦女可能需要提供{{tsl|en|Rho(D) immune globulin||Rho免疫球蛋白}}<ref name=Ol2014>{{cite journal|last1=Oliver|first1=A|last2=Overton|first2=C|title=Diagnosis and management of miscarriage.|journal=The Practitioner|date=May 2014|volume=258|issue=1771|pages=25–8, 3|pmid=25055407}}</ref>。[[止痛藥|止痛藥]]對流產的疼痛緩解也許有益處<ref name=NIH2013Tx/>。心理支持也有助於負面情緒的控制<ref name="NIH2013Tx">{{cite web|title=What are the treatments for pregnancy loss/miscarriage?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/pregnancyloss/conditioninfo/Pages/treatments.aspx|website=http://www.nichd.nih.gov|accessdate=14 March 2015|date=2013-07-15}}</ref>。

<!-- Epidemiology -->
流產在[[妊娠|妊娠]]早期是最常見的[[併發症|併發症]]<ref name=NICE-2012>{{cite web|last=National Coordinating Centre for Women's and Children's Health (UK)|title=Ectopic Pregnancy and Miscarriage: Diagnosis and Initial Management in Early Pregnancy of Ectopic Pregnancy and Miscarriage|url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmedhealth/PMH0055257/|work=NICE Clinical Guidelines, No. 154|publisher=Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists|accessdate=4 July 2013|date=December 2012}}</ref>。在已知自己懷孕的婦女中，流產率約10-20%，而在所有[[受孕|受孕]]後流產的比率約30-50%<ref name=John2012>{{cite book|title=The Johns Hopkins Manual of Gynecology and Obstetrics|date=2012|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9781451148015|pages=438–439|edition=4|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=4Sg5sXyiBvkC&pg=PA438}}</ref><ref name=NIH2013Epi>{{cite web|title=How many people are affected by or at risk for pregnancy loss or miscarriage?|url=http://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/pregnancyloss/conditioninfo/Pages/risk.aspx|website=http://www.nichd.nih.gov|accessdate=14 March 2015|date=2013-07-15}}</ref>。約有5%的婦女有連續兩次流產的經驗<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Garrido-Gimenez|first1=C|last2=Alijotas-Reig|first2=J|title=Recurrent miscarriage: causes, evaluation and management.|journal=Postgraduate Medical Journal|date=March 2015|volume=91|issue=1073|pages=151–162|pmid=25681385|doi=10.1136/postgradmedj-2014-132672}}</ref>。有些人建議與那些有流產經驗的人在討論時不要使用[[墮胎|墮胎]]這個詞彙，以減少其痛苦<ref>{{cite book|last1=Greaves|first1=Ian|last2=Porter|first2=Keith|last3=Hodgetts|first3=Tim J.|last4=Woollard|first4=Malcolm|title=Emergency Care: A Textbook for Paramedics|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2Z3cMX7owGwC&pg=PA506|date=2005|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|location=London|isbn=0-7020-2586-0|page=506}}</ref>。

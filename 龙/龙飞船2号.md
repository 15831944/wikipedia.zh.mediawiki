'''飛龍2號'''（SpaceX Dragon 2），又譯'''龙飞船2号'''，又称'''载人龙飞船'''、'''龙飞船V2'''，是[[SpaceX|SpaceX]]的第二代[[龙飞船|飛龍號]]。

第一代的外型與目前使用的貨運型相同，而飛龍2型外觀有了較大的改變，將逃逸火箭直接做在艙體側面結構上。它将会是一款经过载人认证宇宙飞船，開發初期曾野心勃勃的想於重返地球時，直接使用反推力火箭進行软着陆，後來因技術問題放棄，改用降落傘減速，落海回收，並以此定案。<ref>https://www.spacex.com/news</ref>

它携带4个侧挂式的推进器仓，每个舱内有2台{{le|SuperDraco|SuperDraco}}发动机，既能作为[[发射逃逸系统|发射逃逸系统]]，也能用于动力着陆。它还有更大的舷窗，从底部伸出的着陆支架，新的飞控电脑、航电系统，重新设计的太阳能板。飞船外形较先前已飞行了数年的龙飞船也有了较大变化。<ref name=sfn20141009>{{cite news|last1=Clark|first1=Stephen|title=NASA clears commercial crew contractors to resume work|url=http://spaceflightnow.com/news/n1410/09cctcap/#.VDgLfBaum5d|accessdate=2014-10-10|work=Spaceflight Now|date=2014-10-09|quote=''a highly-modified second-generation Dragon capsule fitted with myriad upgrades and changes -- including new rocket thrusters, computers, a different outer mold line, and redesigned solar arrays – from the company's Dragon cargo delivery vehicle already flying to the space station.''}}</ref>

2014年5月29日，龙飞船在一次于SpaceX总部进行的媒体活动中揭晓。<ref name=aw20140530>{{cite news |last=Norris|first=Guy |script-title=en:SpaceX Unveils ‘Step Change’ Dragon ‘V2’ |url=http://aviationweek.com/space/spacex-unveils-step-change-dragon-v2|accessdate=2014-05-30 |newspaper=Aviation Week |date=2014-05-30}}</ref><ref name=sdc20140529>{{cite news |last=Kramer|first=Miriam |title=SpaceX Unveils Dragon V2 Spaceship, a Manned Space Taxi for Astronauts — Meet Dragon V2: SpaceX's Manned Space Taxi for Astronaut Trips |url=http://www.space.com/26063-spacex-unveils-dragon-v2-manned-spaceship.html |accessdate=2014-05-30 |newspaper=space.com |date=2014-05-30}}</ref><ref name=nsf20140530>{{cite news |last=Bergin|first=Chris |title=SpaceX lifts the lid on the Dragon V2 crew spacecraft |url=http://www.nasaspaceflight.com/2014/05/spacex-lifts-the-lid-dragon-v2-crew-spacecraft/ |accessdate=2014-05-30 |newspaper=NASAspaceflight.com |date=2014-05-30}}</ref>

龙飞船2号设计用于将宇航员送至太空，因此它与货运版龙飞船有着明显的差别，而货运版龙飞船自2010年起就已投入使用。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://spaceflightnow.com/launch-schedule/ |title=Space Flight Now Launch Schedule |work=SpaceFlightNow |accessdate=2017-05-30}}</ref><ref name=shotwell-crewdragon>{{cite news |last=Foust |first=Jeff |url=http://spacenews.com/spacex-seeks-to-accelerate-falcon-9-production-and-launch-rates-this-year/ |title=SpaceX seeks to accelerate Falcon 9 production and launch rates this year |work=[[SpaceNews_(publication)|SpaceNews]] |date=2016-02-04 |accessdate=2016-03-21 |quote= Shotwell said the company is planning an in-flight abort test of the Crew Dragon spacecraft before the end of this year, where the vehicle uses its thrusters to separate from a Falcon 9 rocket during ascent. That will be followed in 2017 by two demonstration flights to the International Space Station, the first without a crew and the second with astronauts on board, and then the first operational mission. }}</ref>

2015年5月6日，SpaceX完成了发射台上中止发射的测试。<ref name=sfn20150506>{{cite web|last1=Clark|first1=Stephen|title=SpaceX crew capsule completes dramatic abort test|url=http://spaceflightnow.com/2015/05/06/spacex-crew-capsule-completes-dramatic-abort-test/|website=Spaceflight Now|accessdate=2015-05-14|date=2015-05-06}}</ref>

2015年11月24日，SpaceX又完成了龙飞船2号的悬停测试。<ref name=youtube-hovertest>{{cite media |title=Dragon 2 Propulsive Hover Test |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=07Pm8ZY0XJI |publisher=[[SpaceX|SpaceX]] |date= 2016-01-21 |accessdate=2016-01-21}}</ref>

NASA已经与SpaceX签署合同，在{{le|CCDev|Commercial Crew Development}}项目下订购了多达6次前往国际空间站的发射。

2019年3月2日，龙飞船2号进行首次无人发射测试，前往[[国际空间站|国际空间站]]。飞船裏有一个假人模型，用来记录真实宇航员承受的各项数据。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.chinanews.com/gj/2019/03-02/8769476.shtml |title=升空！SpaceX载人龙飞船首次进行无人发射测试 |website=中新網 |date=2019-03-02}}</ref>3月8日，龙飞船2号离开国际空间站回到地球，溅落大西洋<ref>[http://sputniknews.cn/society/201903081027873623/ NASA：“龙-2”飞船脱离国际空间站重返地球]</ref>。

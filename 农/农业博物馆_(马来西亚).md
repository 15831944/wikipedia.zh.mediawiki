'''农业博物馆'''（{{lang-ms|Muzium Pertanian}}），是位于[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[马六甲州|马六甲州]][[野新县|野新县]]的一家博物馆，于1990年10月开放<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mpjasin.gov.my/en/muzium-pertanian;jsessionid=DD82C3CC1A0CEEB2DE52C147B8248D2A |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2015-05-28 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150528033835/http://www.mpjasin.gov.my/en/muzium-pertanian%3Bjsessionid%3DDD82C3CC1A0CEEB2DE52C147B8248D2A |archivedate=2015-05-28 |df= }}</ref>。

==历史==
该博物馆位于野新县中心旧警察局的对面，旧址曾为[[英屬馬來亞|英殖民时期]]的邮政局建筑，后来邮政局搬迁后该处被改造为博物馆对外开放<ref>{{cite web|url=http://asmaliana.com/blog/2008/05/14/jasin-agricultural-museum/|title=Jasin Agricultural Museum|work=My World of Tourism|access-date=2015-05-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150528063415/http://asmaliana.com/blog/2008/05/14/jasin-agricultural-museum/|archive-date=2015-05-28|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mpjasin.gov.my/muzium-pertanian |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2015-07-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141128085255/http://www.mpjasin.gov.my/muzium-pertanian |archivedate=2014-11-28 |df= }}</ref><ref name="sinchew.com.my">{{cite news|url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/904611|title=坐落市中心．民众浑然不知．野新农业博物馆冷清！|date=2012-11-24|publisher=[[星洲网|星洲网]]|access-date=2018-07-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180727111233/http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/904611|archive-date=2018-07-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>。野新县曾盛产锡矿及农业产品，这座博物馆可教育当地的青年人士知晓野新县农业曾经的辉煌时期。该博物馆自建立至今已有20余年的历史。<ref name="cnyes.com">{{cite web|url=http://cnews.cnyes.com/Content/20140825/KIX9TEWXZKZVA.shtml|title=马六甲农业博物馆  认识当地传统农业_新闻_钜亨网|website=cnews.cnyes.com|access-date=2018-07-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180727111234/http://cnews.cnyes.com/Content/20140825/KIX9TEWXZKZVA.shtml|archive-date=2018-07-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>

==展览==
目前，这间博物馆乃由马六甲博物馆机构所管理，馆内楼上及底楼摆设不少农民塑像及农业工具，并可免费参观<ref>{{cite web|url=http://historicalmalacca.com/city/melaka-city/attractions/agriculture-museum/|title=Agriculture Museum|work=historicalmalacca.com|access-date=2015-05-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150528033222/http://historicalmalacca.com/city/melaka-city/attractions/agriculture-museum/|archive-date=2015-05-28|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.worldheritage.com.my/2012/05/museum-jasin-agricultural-museum/|title=Museum: Jasin Agricultural Museum|work=MALAYSIA WORLD HERITAGE TOURS|access-date=2015-07-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304073819/http://www.worldheritage.com.my/2012/05/museum-jasin-agricultural-museum/|archive-date=2016-03-04|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://ww1.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y%3D2006%26dt%3D0926%26pub%3DUtusan_Malaysia%26sec%3DRencana%26pg%3Dre_07.htm |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2015-07-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150712235855/http://ww1.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y=2006&dt=0926&pub=Utusan_Malaysia&sec=Rencana&pg=re_07.htm |archivedate=2015-07-12 |df= }}</ref>。参观的游客也可以透过塑像了解农民收割、取出[[稻米|稻米]]，制作[[椰子|椰糖]]，采割[[油棕|油棕果]]以及收割[[橡胶|橡胶]]的过程<ref name="cnyes.com"/>。其开放时间为每日早上9时至下午5时。<ref name="sinchew.com.my"/>

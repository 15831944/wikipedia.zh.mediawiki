《[[我要做廚神_(美國版)|'''廚神當道''']]》（{{lang-en|MasterChef}}） 是美國的[[真人實境秀|真人實境秀]]節目，第五季於2014年5月26日於[[福斯廣播公司|福斯]]首播。<ref name="Enstarz">{{cite web|title=‘MasterChef’ Season 5 CAST: 30 Competitors Announced Including Students, Stay-At-Home Parents & Petroleum Landman Competing?|url=http://www.enstarz.com/articles/37229/20140430/masterchef-season-5-cast-30-competitors-announced-including-students-stay-at-home-parents-petroleum-landman-competing-video.htm|website=enstarz.com|date=Apr 30, 2014|accessdate=2017-09-24}}</ref>

由[[戈登·拉姆齊|戈登·拉姆齊]]、{{link-en|葛拉漢·艾略特|Graham Elliot}}和{{link-en|喬·巴斯提亞尼許|Joe Bastianich}}擔任評審，{{link-en|喬·巴斯提亞尼許|Joe Bastianich}}於本季結束後轉往[[廚神當道_(義大利版)|廚神當道 (義大利版)]]主持。<ref name="KDrama Stars">{{cite web|title=HOMETRENDING NEWS VIEW ARCHIVES 'MasterChef' Host Joe Bastianich Leaves Show To Focus On Another Reality TV? Christina Tossi Steps In For Restaurateur|url=http://www.kdramastars.com/articles/68554/20150115/masterchef-host-joe-bastianich-leaves-show-focus-another-reality-tv.htm|website=kdramastars.com|date=Jan 15, 2015|accessdate=2017-09-24}}</ref>

本節目首次由2名女性參賽者共同角逐冠亞軍，最後由Courtney Lapresi勝出獲得冠軍。<ref name="DAILY NEWS">{{cite web|title=Courtney Lapresi wins ‘MasterChef’|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/tv/philly-stripper-courtney-lapresi-wins-masterchef-article-1.1940486|website=nydailynews.com|accessdate=2017-09-24|date=September 15, 2014}}</ref>

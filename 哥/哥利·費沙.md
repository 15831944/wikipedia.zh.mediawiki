'''安東尼·佳·哥利·費沙'''（Anthony Guy Corey Fisher，{{bd|1988年|4月8日}}）乃美國大專籃球員。費沙司職得分後衛，就讀於賓夕法尼亞州[[Villanova大學|Villanova大學]]。費沙在高中時效力新澤西伊利沙伯[[St._Patrick高中|St. Patrick高中]] <ref>{{cite web | title = Meet PARADE’s 2007 All-America High School Boys Basketball Team | publisher = Parade Magazine | url =http://www.parade.com/articles/editions/2007/edition_04-01-2007/Basketball | date = March 23, 2007 | accessdate = January 10, 2009 }}</ref>

2010年8月7日，費沙在一場夏季費中狂轟105分<ref name=NYDN>{{Cite web| last = Beekman| first = Daniel| title = Villanova guard Corey Fisher sets street basketball record scoring 105 points in one game in Bronx| publisher = ''[[New_York_Daily_News|New York Daily News]]''| date = August 14, 2010| url = http://www.nydailynews.com/ny_local/bronx/2010/08/14/2010-08-14_its_105point_hoopla_bx_guard_courts_legend_status_after_street_ball_record.html| accessdate = August 14, 2010}}</ref>。有可能刷新街場個人得分紀錄，飛威廉斯曾在1978年轟入100分<ref name=NYDN/>。在紐約州布隆斯屈臣基里臣球場舉行的賽事，費沙的105分助球隊以138:130擊敗對手GymRatsNYC，他更28投三分球中了23球。<ref name=NYDN/><ref name=DOCS>{{Cite web| last = Moll| first = Allen| title = Villanova’s Corey Fisher Drops 105 Points in NYC Summer League Game| publisher = TheHoopDoctors.com| date = August 9, 2010| url = http://thehoopdoctors.com/online2/2010/08/villanovas-corey-fisher-drops-105-points-in-nyc-summer-league-game/| accessdate = August 14, 2010}}</ref>

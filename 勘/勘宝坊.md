'''勘宝坊'''（{{lang-en|Compass One}}）是位于[[新加坡|新加坡]][[盛港|盛港镇]]的购物中心。

==历史==
勘宝坊是综合发展项目的一部分，与[[盛港地铁站|盛港地铁站]]和盛堡公寓（{{lang-en|Compass Heights}}）同时兴建。<ref name="Khaw Boon Wan">{{cite news | author = Khaw Boon Wan| title = The ticket to seamless travel | publisher = ''[[The_Straits_Times|The Straits Times]]'' | date = 26 November 2002| page=}}</ref><ref name="The Straits Times Mar 2001">{{cite news | author = | title = 61 Compass Heights units sold | publisher = ''[[The_Straits_Times|The Straits Times]]'' | date = 3 March 2001| page=S14}}</ref> 建设成本为2.3亿新元。商场于2002年8月正式开幕，并且是[[新加坡地铁东北线|东北线]]边上的第一家大型商场。

2015年10月底，勘宝坊开始为期一年的翻新工程。2016年2月，M&G房地产经过股权收购交易后，正式成为商场的唯一所有者。该公司宣布，商场将于2016年9月重新开幕。<ref name="The Straits Times Jul 2016">{{cite news | url = https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/sengkang-mall-compass-one-to-reopen-in-september-after-11-month-revamp | author = Lee Min Kok | title = Sengkang mall Compass One to reopen in September after 11-month revamp | publisher = ''[[The_Straits_Times|The Straits Times]]'' | date = 28 July 2016 | access-date = 2019-03-14 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20170720063327/http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/sengkang-mall-compass-one-to-reopen-in-september-after-11-month-revamp | archive-date = 2017-07-20 | dead-url = no }}</ref>

2016年9月27日，勘宝坊以全新的设计概念重新开幕。<ref name="The Straits Times sep 2016">{{cite news | url = https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/compass-one-mall-officially-opens-in-sengkang-with-features-to-cater-to-young-families | author = Jalelah Abu Baker | title = Compass One mall officially opens in Sengkang with features to cater to young families | publisher = ''[[The_Straits_Times|The Straits Times]]'' | date = 27 September 2016 | access-date = 2019-03-15 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20170101075514/http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/compass-one-mall-officially-opens-in-sengkang-with-features-to-cater-to-young-families | archive-date = 2017-01-01 | dead-url = no }}</ref> 商场的商店数量已增至208家，其中包括50家新加入的零售商。<ref name="The Straits Times sep 2016"></ref>

==交通链接==
勘宝坊毗邻设有[[盛港地铁站|盛港地铁站]]，盛港巴士转换站以及康埔桦巴士转换站。

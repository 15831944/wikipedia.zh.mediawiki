'''陶德·布萊恩·弗雷澤'''（{{lang-en|Todd Brian Frazier}}, {{bd|1986年|2月12日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟紐約大都會的三壘手。先前曾效力於紅人、白襪與洋基。他在2015年參加全壘打大賽並獲得冠軍。

==職業生涯==
===辛辛那提紅人===
2007年大聯盟選秀，紅人隊以第一輪34順位選進了弗雷澤。<ref>[http://mlb.mlb.com/mlb/events/draft/y2007/drafttracker.jsp MLB.com 2007 Draft Tracker], [[MLB.com|MLB.com]] Draft Tracker. Accessed June 8, 2007.</ref>7月11日，他以指定打擊的身分初登小聯盟。<ref>Rachac, Greg. [http://www.billingsgazette.net/articles/2007/07/12/sports/local/18-mustangs.txt Mustangs shake off recent slump in 9–3 win over Orem] {{Webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20120912211149/http://www.billingsgazette.net/articles/2007/07/12/sports/local/18-mustangs.txt |date=September 12, 2012 }}, ''[[Billings_Gazette|Billings Gazette]]'', July 12, 2007. Accessed July 14, 2007.</ref>

在小聯盟打拚一陣子後，到了2011年，因為紅人隊將[[艾汀森·沃奎茲|艾汀森·沃奎茲]]和Jordan Smith下放3A，弗雷澤因而升上大聯盟。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fox19.com/story/14699766/struggling-volquez-sent-to-minors|title=Struggling Volquez Sent to Minors|last=Danneman|first=Joe|date=May 23, 2011|work=FOX19|accessdate=May 23, 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110927181020/http://www.fox19.com/story/14699766/struggling-volquez-sent-to-minors#|archive-date=September 27, 2011|dead-url=yes}}</ref>5月23日，他以代打身分初登板，不過吞下三振。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nj.com/mets/index.ssf/2011/05/todd_frazier_former_rutgers_st_1.html|title=Todd Frazier, former Rutgers star, makes major-league debut|last=Johnson|first=Brent|date=May 23, 2011|work=The Star-Ledger|accessdate=May 24, 2011}}</ref>隔天他又回到3A，紅人將位置讓給另一名後援投手Carlos Fisher。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hardballtalk.nbcsports.com/2011/05/24/that-was-quick-reds-send-todd-frazier-back-to-triple-a|title=That was quick: Reds send Todd Frazier back to Triple-A|last=Gleeman|first=Aaron|date=May 24, 2011|work=NBC Sports|accessdate=May 24, 2011}}</ref>

同年7月31日，弗雷澤擊出生涯首轟，而且苦主還是巨人王牌[[貝瑞·齊托|貝瑞·齊托]]。2012年5月16日，弗雷澤擊出雙響砲。一週後，他面對勇士隊擊出再見全壘打。

2012年11月5日，弗雷澤首獲球員票選獎 (Player Choice Award)。<ref>{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.foxsportsohio.com/11/06/12/Frazier-named-Players-Choice-Rookie-of-t/landing_blog.html?blockID=815459&feedID=10938 |title=frazier-named-players-choice-rookie-of-the-year | FOX Sports |publisher=Foxsportsohio.com |date=November 6, 2012 |accessdate=July 14, 2015}}</ref>2014年7月6日，他與隊友[[強尼·奎托|強尼·奎托]]、[[阿羅魯迪斯·查普曼|阿羅魯迪斯·查普曼]]、Devin Mesoraco以及Alfredo Simón一同入選明星賽。該年他也參加了全壘打大賽，<ref>{{cite web|author=Kevin Manahan |url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2014/07/njs_todd_frazier_from_a_little_slugger_to_mlbs_home_run_derby.html |title=N.J.'s Todd Frazier: From a little slugger to MLB's Home Run Derby |publisher=NJ.com |date= |accessdate=July 14, 2015}}</ref>並請他的哥哥Charlie做為餵球投手，他一路闖到決賽，最終不敵[[約尼斯·塞佩達斯|約尼斯·塞佩達斯]]，拿下亞軍。<ref name="cincinnati.com">{{cite web|author= |url=http://www.cincinnati.com/story/sports/mlb/reds/2014/07/14/reds-todd-frazier-home-run-derby-cespedes/12660181/ |title=Todd Frazier falls in HR Derby, but family wins |publisher=Cincinnati.com |date=July 15, 2014 |accessdate=July 14, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Todd Frazier Reaches Home Run Derby Finales, Falls to Defending Champion|url=http://www.sportsmedia101.com/cincinnatireds/2014/07/15/todd-frazier-reaches-home-run-derby-finals-falls-to-defending-champion/|access-date=July 21, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140727211602/http://www.sportsmedia101.com/cincinnatireds/2014/07/15/todd-frazier-reaches-home-run-derby-finals-falls-to-defending-champion/#|archive-date=July 27, 2014|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

2015年2月8日，他與紅人簽下了2年1200萬美元的合約。<ref>{{cite web|title=Todd Frazier, Reds agree to deal|url=http://espn.go.com/mlb/story/_/id/12297507/cincinnati-reds-sign-third-baseman-todd-frazier-two-year-12-million-deal|website=[[ESPN|ESPN]]|accessdate=February 9, 2015}}</ref>

2015年7月13日，弗雷澤再度挑戰全壘打大賽。他接連打敗[[普林斯·菲爾德|普林斯·菲爾德]]、[[賈許·唐納森|賈許·唐納森]]和Joc Peterson拿下冠軍。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/136520166/todd-frazier-wins-home-run-derby-at-home|title=Deep goes Frazier! Reds star KOs Derby field|work=MLB.com|date=July 13, 2015|accessdate=July 14, 2015|first=Mark|last=Sheldon}}</ref><ref name="edges">{{cite web | url=http://www.sfgate.com/giants/article/Todd-Frazier-edges-Joc-Pederson-in-Home-Run-Derby-6382976.php | title=Todd Frazier edges Joc Pederson in Home Run Derby | work=[[SF_Gate|SF Gate]] | date=July 13, 2015 | accessdate=July 15, 2015 | author=Shea, John}}</ref>他成為1990年的Ryne Sandberg後，第一位在自己家鄉拿下全壘打大賽冠軍的球員。
===芝加哥白襪===
2015年12月16日，白襪紅人道奇進行三方交易。弗雷澤被交易至白襪隊。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.dodgers.mlb.com/news/article/159795544/reds-trade-todd-frazier-to-white-sox|title=White Sox acquire Frazier in 3-team deal|work=mlb.com|first=Andrew|last=Simon|date=December 16, 2015|accessdate=December 16, 2015}}</ref>

2016年5月11日，他在三壘後方觀眾席做出一個精彩美技，但是也造成他臉部受傷，因此進入傷兵名單。<ref>{{cite web|title=Todd Frazier hurt as White Sox struggle late again in loss to Rangers|url=http://www.csnchicago.com/chicago-white-sox/todd-frazier-hurt-white-sox-struggle-late-again-loss-rangers|publisher=Comcast Sports Chicago|accessdate=May 11, 2016}}</ref>

7月11日，弗雷澤第三度參加全壘打大賽。連三年闖到決賽，不過這回輸給「怪力男」[[賈恩卡洛·斯坦頓|賈恩卡洛·斯坦頓]]無緣衛冕。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nj.com/yankees/index.ssf/2016/07/mlb_home_run_derby_all-star_game.html|title=2016 MLB Home Run Derby: Marlins' Giancarlo Stanton beats N.J.'s Todd Frazier|work=nj.com|date=July 12, 2016|accessdate=July 15, 2016|first=Brendan|last=Kuty}}</ref>
===紐約洋基===
2016年年底結束，白襪進入重建期，先在2016年年底交易[[克里斯·塞爾|克里斯·塞爾]]。2017年7月18日，白襪將弗雷澤、Tommy Kahnle以及[[大衛·羅伯森|大衛·羅伯森]]交易至洋基隊，換來[[泰勒·克里帕德|泰勒·克里帕德]]與三名小聯盟新秀。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://chicago.suntimes.com/sports/amid-trade-rumors-white-sox-todd-frazier-scratched-from-lineup/|title=White Sox trade Robertson, Frazier, Kahnle to Yankees, call up Moncada|work=chicago.suntimes.com|date=July 18, 2017|accessdate=July 18, 2017|first=Daryl|last=Van Schouwen}}</ref>弗雷澤在加入洋基隊之前都是採用21號做為背號，但是洋基球迷不希望21號給Paul O'Neill以外的任何人使用，因此弗雷澤將背號改為29號。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/baseball/yankees/todd-frazier-hopes-talk-paul-o-neill-wearing-no-21-article-1.3339443|title=Todd Frazier hopes to talk to Paul O’Neill about wearing No. 21|work=NY Daily News|access-date=July 19, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://nypost.com/2017/07/21/the-yankees-wont-let-todd-frazier-wear-number-he-wants/|title=The Yankees won’t let Todd Frazier wear number he wants|last=III|first=George A. King|date=July 21, 2017|website=New York Post|access-date=July 23, 2017}}</ref>總計他2017年打擊率2成13，26轟77分打點。洋基隊打入季後賽時，弗雷澤不只會帶動球隊的氣氛，偶爾會在比賽中給予適時一擊，因此他很受洋基球迷的喜愛。<ref>Rourke, Dan (January 26, 2018) [https://yanksgoyard.com/2018/01/26/yankees-strike-deal-todd-frazier/ The Yankees should try to strike a deal with Todd Frazier]. yanksgoyard.com</ref>

==個人生活==
他有2個都在打職業棒球的哥哥，Jeff和Charlie。其中Jeff在2010年曾短暫升上大聯盟，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/players/f/frazije01.shtml |title=Jeff Frazier Statistics and History |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date= |accessdate=July 14, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/minors/player.cgi?id=frazie001jef |title=Jeff Frazier Minor League Statistics & History |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date=August 10, 1982 |accessdate=July 14, 2015}}</ref>Charlie則是在馬林魚隊小聯盟打了6年。<ref name="cincinnati.com"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/minors/player.cgi?id=frazie001cha |title=Charlie Frazier Minor League Statistics & History |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date=July 6, 1980 |accessdate=July 14, 2015}}</ref>

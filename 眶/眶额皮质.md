'''眶额皮质'''（{{lang-en|orbitofrontal cortex}}，缩写{{lang|en|OFC}}），又译为'''眼窝前额皮质、眼眶額葉皮質、前額葉基底部'''，是位于[[大脑|大脑]][[额叶|额叶]]前下方的[[前额叶皮质|前额叶皮质]]，是与[[决策|决策]]的[[认知|认知]]过程有关的一个脑区。非人类灵长类动物的眶额皮质包括皮质联合区[[布罗德曼分区系统|布罗德曼]]11区、12区与13区，人类的眶额皮质则包括布罗德曼10区、11区与47区。<ref name="Kringelbach 2005">{{cite journal | author = Kringelbach M. L. | year = 2005 | title = The orbitofrontal cortex: linking reward to hedonic experience | url = | journal = Nature Reviews Neuroscience | volume = 6 | issue = | pages = 691–702 | doi=10.1038/nrn1747 | pmid=16136173}}</ref>

眶额皮质被认为与{{le|腹内侧前额叶皮质|Ventromedial prefrontal cortex}}在解剖学上是一致的。<ref name="Phillips2002">Phillips, LH., MacPherson, SE. & Della Sala, S. (2002). 'Age, cognition and emotion: the role of anatomical segregation in the frontal lobes: the role of anatomical segregation in the frontal lobes'. in J Grafman (ed.), Handbook of Neuropsychology: the frontal lobes. Elsevier Science, Amsterdam, pp. 73-98.</ref>它的独特之处在于其神经连接及其功能。<ref name="Barbas2002">Barbas H, Ghashghaei H, Rempel-Clower N, Xiao D (2002) Anatomic basis of functional specialization in prefrontal cortices in primates. In: Handbook of Neuropsychology (Grafman J, ed), pp 1-27. Amsterdam: Elsevier Science B.V.</ref>眶额皮质接受{{le|丘脑背内侧核|Medial dorsal nucleus}}的投射，涉及决策过程的情绪和奖赏。<ref>Fuster, J.M. ''The Prefrontal Cortex'', (Raven Press, New York, 1997).</ref>其覆盖于[[眼眶|眼眶]]之上，并因而得名。无论是在人类还是其他灵长类动物中，眶额皮质有着显然的个体差异。在鼠类大脑中也发现了与其相关的脑区。<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Uylings HB, Groenewegen HJ, Kolb B | year = 2003 | title = Do rats have a prefrontal cortex? | url = | journal = Behav Brain Res | volume = 146 | issue = 1–2| pages = 3–17 | doi = 10.1016/j.bbr.2003.09.028 | pmid = 14643455 }}</ref>

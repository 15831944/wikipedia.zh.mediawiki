《'''想像'''》（英語：''Imagine''）是美国歌手[[爱莉安娜·格兰德|爱莉安娜·格兰德]]的歌曲，收录于她发行的第五张录音室专辑《[[Thank_U,_Next|謝謝, 下一位]]》（2019年），由歌手本人、安德鲁·万塞尔、内森·佩雷斯和{{tsl|en|Priscilla Renea|普里西拉·雷纳}}共同创作，由{{tsl|en|Pop Wansel|波普·约翰}}以及哈皮·佩雷斯共同制作，发行于2018年12月14日，<ref name="Tidal">{{cite web |title=imagine / Ariana Grande - TIDAL |url=https://listen.tidal.com/album/100941483 |website=Tidal |accessdate=2018-12-14}}</ref><ref name="pitchfork">{{cite web |last1=Bloom |first1=Madison |title=Ariana Grande Shares New Song “imagine”: Listen |url=https://pitchfork.com/news/ariana-grande-shares-new-song-imagine-listen/ |website=Pitchfork |accessdate=2018-12-14}}</ref>歌词中讲述了爱莉安娜拒绝失败的感情。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8490016/ariana-grande-imagine-snippet-teaser|title=Ariana Grande Shares First Taste of Her New Song 'Imagine': Listen|website=Billboard|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref>

==背景和发行==
2018年12月11日，爱莉安娜在她的[[Instagram|Instagram]]故事中公布了这首歌曲，为专辑的发行倒计时。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cosmopolitan.com/uk/entertainment/a25567106/ariana-grande-teasing-new-song-imagine/
|title=Ariana Grande is teasing a new song|website=Cosmopolitan|access-date=2018-12-13}}</ref>一天后放出试听，<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://people.com/music/ariana-grande-new-song-imagine-denial-failed-relationship/|title=Ariana Grande Says New Song Alludes to 'Denial' of 'Failed Yet Beautiful Relationships'|website=People.com|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref>，并发了一则汉字歌名的推文。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cosmopolitan.com/entertainment/a25556483/ariana-grande-thank-u-next-imagine-song/|title=Ariana Grande Just Teased Another Song|last=Barbour|first=Shannon|date=2018-12-12|website=Cosmopolitan|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nme.com/news/music/ariana-grande-new-single-imagine-new-album-2419563|title=Ariana Grande teases new single 'Imagine', describes new album as "mourning failed yet important, beautiful relationships"|last=Maine|first=Samantha|date=2018-12-12|website=NME|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref>歌曲和官方歌词视频在2018年12月14日同时上线，视频的特色是冰川背景画面为故障效果。<ref name="hp">{{cite web |last1=Cantor |first1=Brian |title=Ariana Grande Releases Lyric Video For New Song “imagine” |url=https://www.google.com/search?q=ariana+imagine+lyric+video&oq=ariana+imagine+lyric+video&aqs=chrome..69i57j69i61.3506j1j7&sourceid=chrome&ie=UTF-8 |website=Headline Planet |accessdate=2018-12-14}}</ref>

==作曲==
歌曲由、安德鲁·万塞尔、内森·佩雷斯和{{tsl|en|Priscilla Renea|普里西拉·雷纳}}共同创作，由{{tsl|en|Pop Wansel|波普·约翰}}以及哈皮·佩雷斯共同制作。《想像》是一首以多个[[哨音|哨音]]为特点的“节奏曲风”歌曲。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hit.com.au/story/ariana-grande-brings-back-the-whistle-note-for-her-new-song-imagine-118028|title=Ariana Grande Brings Back The Whistle Note For Her New Song Imagine|date=2018-12-14|website=Hit|access-date=2018-12-14}}</ref>并且歌词中讲述了爱莉安娜拒绝失败的感情。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8490016/ariana-grande-imagine-snippet-teaser|title=Ariana Grande Shares First Taste of Her New Song 'Imagine': Listen|website=Billboard|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.usmagazine.com/entertainment/news/ariana-grande-teases-new-song-imagine-about-failed-relationships/|title=Ariana Grande Teases New Song About 'Failed Relationships'|date=2018-12-12|website=Us Weekly|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref>

== 现场演唱 ==
歌曲的首秀在2018年12月18日的节目《[[今夜秀|今夜秀]]》中播出。{{cn|date=December 2018}}

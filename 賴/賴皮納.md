'''賴皮納'''是[[愛沙尼亞|愛沙尼亞]]的城鎮，位於該國東南部的[[沃漢杜河|沃漢杜河]]下游，距離[[塔爾圖|塔爾圖]]64公里，屬[[珀爾瓦縣|珀爾瓦縣]]管轄，為[[賴皮納堂區|賴皮納堂區]]的行政中心。其面積為265.92平方公里，2009年人口2,867。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pub.stat.ee/px-web.2001/Dialog/varval.asp?ma=PO027&ti=POPULATION+BY+SEX%2C+AGE+GROUP+AND+ADMINISTRATIVE+UNIT+OR+TYPE+OF+SETTLEMENT%2C+1+JANUARY&path=../I_Databas/Population/01Population_indicators_and_composition/04Population_figure_and_composition/&lang=1|title=Population figure and composition|publisher=Statistics Estonia|accessdate=12 January 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131105130157/http://pub.stat.ee/px-web.2001/Dialog/varval.asp?ma=PO027&ti=POPULATION+BY+SEX%2C+AGE+GROUP+AND+ADMINISTRATIVE+UNIT+OR+TYPE+OF+SETTLEMENT%2C+1+JANUARY&path=..%2FI_Databas%2FPopulation%2F01Population_indicators_and_composition%2F04Population_figure_and_composition%2F&lang=1|archivedate=5 十一月 2013}}</ref>

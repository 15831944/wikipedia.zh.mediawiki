'''鼬耳螺'''（学名：''{{lang|la|Cassidula nucleus}}''）是一種會呼吸空氣的[[海螺|海螺]]，是[[肺螺類|肺螺類]]的[[耳螺目|耳螺目]][[耳螺科|耳螺科]][[鼬耳螺屬|鼬耳螺屬]]的一种{{r|shell_1869}}。

==分佈==
主要分布于、[[台湾|台湾]]{{r|shell_1869}}、[[中國|中國]][[華南|華南]][[雷州半島|雷州半島]]<ref>Wei-dong H., Jin-ke L., Ying-Ya C., Fu-liang Y., Li-qiang X. & Ning Y. (2003). "Shellfish and fish biodiversity of mangrove ecosystems in Leizhou Peninsula, China". ''[[Journal_of_Coastal_Development|Journal of Coastal Development]]'' '''7'''(1): 21-29. [http://www.vliz.be/imisdocs/publications/255067.pdf PDF].</ref>、[[印度|印度]]<ref>Kumar P. S. & Khan A. B. (2013). "The distribution and diversity of benthic macroinvertebrate fauna in Pondicherry mangroves, India". ''<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[Aquatic_biosystems|Aquatic biosystems]]'' '''9'''(1): 1.</ref>、[[越南|越南]]中部的[[芽莊|芽莊]]{{r|Zvonareva 2015}}、[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]<ref>Hookham B., Shau-Hwai A. T., Dayrat B. & Hintz W. (2014). "A Baseline Measure of Tree and Gastropod Biodiversity in Replanted and Natural Mangrove Stands in Malaysia: Langkawi Island and Sungai Merbok".

'''容器园艺'''是從底部引入水的澆水設備的方法，允許水通過[[毛細管作用|毛細管作用]]向上浸入植物。<ref>[http://www.plantertechnology.com/train/faq_cont.html Sub-irrigated planter FAQ] {{Wayback|url=http://www.plantertechnology.com/train/faq_cont.html |date=20080913214632 }}, from Planter Technology</ref>可以自動化澆水，因此SIP在建築物或城市環境中受到專業園林師的歡迎。<ref>[http://greenroofgrowers.blogspot.com/2008/07/how-make-two-bucket-sub-irrigated.html How To Make a Two Bucket Sub-Irrigated Planter]</ref>

封閉系統缺點之一是可溶性鹽隨著時間的推移而增長。<ref name="salts">{{cite journal |title=The Potential For The Sequential Production Of Vegetables In The Field With The ‘Earth Box’ System |journal=Acta Hortic |year=1998 |last=Csizinszky |first=A.A. |volume=513 |pages=137–144 |doi=10.17660/ActaHortic.1998.513.16 |url=http://www.actahort.org/books/513/513_16.htm |accessdate=2016-12-01 }}</ref>

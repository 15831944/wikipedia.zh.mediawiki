'''大巴窑体育场'''（{{lang-en|Toa Payoh Stadium}}），位于新加坡[[大巴窑|大巴窑]]规划区内，于1974年开放。球场可以容纳3896名观众。<ref name="ActiveSG"/><ref name="Soccerway 2012"/>大巴窑体育场育场是[[新加坡职业足球联赛|新加坡职业足球联赛]][[馬里士他卡沙足球會|馬里士他卡沙足球會]]的主场。<ref name="Osman 2018"/>

==体育场设施==
球场拥有一个可供[[新加坡职业足球联赛|新加坡职业足球联赛]]举办赛事的草皮。体育场也有也有一条8赛道的[[400米|400米]]塑胶跑步道。体育场傍边还有游泳池，健身室，网球场等设施。<ref name="ActiveSG1">{{cite web | title=Toa Payoh Sports Centre | website=ActiveSG | url=https://www.myactivesg.com/facilities/toa-payoh-sports-centre | language=en | access-date=2018-07-29 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160909063409/https://www.myactivesg.com/facilities/toa-payoh-sports-centre | archive-date=2016-09-09 | dead-url=no }}</ref> 体育场傍的也有一座室内体育馆，是[[2010年夏季青年奧林匹克運動會|2010年夏季青年奧林匹克運動會]][[举重|举重]]以及[[排球|排球]]项目的正赛场地。<ref>{{Cite web | title = Toa Payoh Sports Hall | publisher = Singapore Youth Olympic Games Organising Committee | url = http://www.singapore2010.sg/public/sg2010/en/en_venues/en_compvenues/en_toa_payoh_sports_hall.html | accessdate = 2010-08-04 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100529131936/http://www.singapore2010.sg/public/sg2010/en/en_venues/en_compvenues/en_toa_payoh_sports_hall.html | archivedate = 2010-05-29 }}</ref>游泳池则是青年奧林匹克運動會[[跳水|跳水]]项目的正赛场地。<ref>{{Cite web | title = Toa Payoh Swimming Complex | publisher = Singapore Youth Olympic Games Organising Committee | url = http://www.singapore2010.sg/public/sg2010/en/en_venues/en_compvenues/en_toa_payoh_swimming_complex.html | accessdate = 2010-08-04 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20101130192953/http://www.singapore2010.sg/public/sg2010/en/en_venues/en_compvenues/en_toa_payoh_swimming_complex.html | archivedate = 2010-11-30 }}</ref>

==重要庆典==
大巴窑体育场是1977年[[新加坡国庆庆典|新加坡国庆庆典]]的举办场地。<ref name="National Archives of Singapore">{{cite web | title=National Day Parade 1977 at Toa Payoh Stadium -- Minister … | website=National Archives of Singapore | url=http://www.nas.gov.sg/archivesonline/photographs/record-details/3d5af177-1162-11e3-83d5-0050568939ad | access-date=2018-07-29 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180729062615/http://www.nas.gov.sg/archivesonline/photographs/record-details/3d5af177-1162-11e3-83d5-0050568939ad | archive-date=2018-07-29 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

==军事活动==
大巴窑体育场也被[[新加坡武装部队|新加坡武装部队]]用来作为新兵入伍前以及战备军人体能检验（{{lang-en|IPPT}}）的场所。<ref name="Sen 2014">{{cite web | last=Sen | first=Ng Jun | title=TNP reporter tests new IPPT | website=The New Paper | date=2014-07-25 | url=https://www.tnp.sg/news/others/tnp-reporter-tests-new-ippt | access-date=2018-07-29 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180729062616/https://www.tnp.sg/news/others/tnp-reporter-tests-new-ippt | archive-date=2018-07-29 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

'''大衛·布林'''（David Brin） （生於1950年10月6日）是美國[[科學家|科學家]]和[[科幻小說|科幻小說]]作家。他曾獲得[[雨果獎|雨果獎]]<ref>[http://www.thehugoawards.org/?page_id=35 1984 Hugo Awards], Best Novel:Startide Rising by David Brin (Bantam, 1983), The Hugo Awards</ref><ref name=vote>[http://reason.com/archives/2008/10/29/whos-getting-your-vote/singlepage Who's Getting Your Vote?], October 29, 2008, ''[[Reason_magazine|Reason]]''</ref>，[[軌跡獎|軌跡獎]]<ref>[http://www.worldswithoutend.com/novel.asp?ID=31 Startide Rising], Science Fiction & Fantasy Books, WWEnd</ref><ref>[http://www.worldswithoutend.com/novel.asp?ID=207 The Postman], Science Fiction & Fantasy Books, WWEnd</ref><ref>[http://www.worldswithoutend.com/novel.asp?ID=35 The Uplift War], Science Fiction & Fantasy Books, WWEnd</ref>，[[坎貝爾獎|坎貝爾獎]]<ref>[http://www2.ku.edu/~sfcenter/campbell.htm 1986: 1st - The Postman, David Brin], 2003: 2nd - Kiln People, David Brin, The John W. Campbell Memorial Award</ref>和[[星雲獎|星雲獎]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sfwa.org/awards/archive/pastwin.htm |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2007-01-11 |0=2011-06-05 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605082209/http://www.sfwa.org/awards/archive/pastwin.htm |archivedate=2011-06-05 }} </ref>。

'''大岩桐属'''（[[学名|学名]]：''Gloxinia''），又名'''红鸟苣苔属'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hy.ynh.com.cn/Article/zixun/200608/763.html|title=红鸟苣苔花（Gloxinia）|publisher=[[花艺撷英|花艺撷英]]：花艺知识|language=zh-hans|date=2006-08-06}}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>，是[[苦苣苔科|苦苣苔科]]下的一个属，只有三個品種，均為[[開花植物|開花植物]]。三個品種有兩個生長於[[南美洲|南美洲]][[安第斯山脈|安第斯山脈]]，但''Gloxinia perennis''亦有在[[中美洲|中美洲]]及[[加勒比地区|加勒比地区]]發現。

''Gloxinia perennis''是本屬的[[模式種|模式種]]，亦是本屬最原始的品種。本屬原來有約20個品種，這是基於[[:en:Hans_Wiehler|Hans Wiehler]]在1976年的分類而劃分<ref>{{cite journal |last=Wiehler |first=H. |year=1976 |title=A report on the classification of ''Achimenes'', ''Eucodonia'', ''Gloxinia'', and ''Anetanthus'' (Gesneriaceae) |journal=''Selbyana'' |volume=1 |issue=4 |pages=374-404 |language=en }}</ref>。不過，近年對分子生物學及相關領域對本屬及其相關屬的分析，發現Wiehler當年的分類太廣泛，把很多不相關的品種亦包括進來，因而把這些品種重新分類。

== 品種 ==
*''[[Gloxinia_perennis|Gloxinia perennis]]''在中南美洲廣泛分佈。The plant is an herb with large nodding, purple, mint-scented flowers.  It is sometimes known as "Canterbury Bells" (not to be confused with members of the genus ''[[Campanula|Campanula]]'', which go by the same name).  It is cultivated in tropical regions and its original range is unknown.
*''[[Gloxinia_erinoides|Gloxinia erinoides]]'' (syn. ''Koellikeria erinoides'') occurs from Costa Rica to Bolivia.  The plant is a small herb with tiny white and maroon flowers.  This diminutive plant is notable for having tiny but distinctly coconut-scented flowers and is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
*''[[Gloxinia_xanthophylla|Gloxinia xanthophylla]]'' (syn. ''Anodiscus xanthophyllus'') occurs in Ecuador and Peru.  The plant is a shrubby herb with small white flowers and is rarely encountered in cultivation.  Unlike the other two species it lacks scaly rhizomes.

'''大衛·奧耶特昆伯·歐洛沃'''（{{lang-en|'''David Oyetokunbo Oyelowo'''}}，{{IPAc-en|ɵ|ˈ|j|ɛ|l|ɵ|w|oʊ}} {{respell|ə|YEL|ə-woh}}，{{bd|1976年|4月1日}}）是一位英国男演员、監製、導演與編劇。代表作品为2014年电影《[[塞尔玛游行|塞尔玛游行]]》，为其获得多项最佳男主角提名。<ref name="rebels 2">{{cite web|url=http://www.mirror.co.uk/tv/tv-news/star-wars-rebels-actor-david-4337217|title=Star Wars Rebels actor David Oyelowo had 'no idea' what he was auditioning for|first=Grace|last=Macaskil|date=2014-09-27|work=[[Daily_Mirror|Mirror Online]]|accessdate=2014-09-30}}</ref>

== 早期生活 ==
他生于英格兰[[牛津|牛津]]，具有[[尼日利亚|尼日利亚]]血统<ref name="independent1">{{cite web|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/theatre-dance/features/david-oyelowo-an-actors-life-432629.html |title=David Oyelowo: An actor's life – Features – Theatre & Dance |work=The Independent |date=2007-01-18 |accessdate=2012-11-18}}</ref>。母亲在[[铁路|铁路]]工作，父亲 Stephen 在[[航空公司|航空公司]]工作。<ref>http://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/3667799/As-he-likes-it.html</ref><ref name=ind1>Gerard Gilbert, [http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/profiles/upwardly-mobile-david-oyelowo-on-going-from-tooting-bec-to-tom-cruises-jet-8483779.html "Upwardly mobile: David Oyelowo on going from Tooting Bec to Tom Cruise's jet"], ''The Independent'', 9 February 2013. Retrieved 7 April 2013.</ref>

曾在 City and Islington College 学习[[戏剧|戏剧]]，后来在[[伦敦音乐与戏剧艺术学院|伦敦音乐与戏剧艺术学院]]学习了三年于1998年毕业。<ref>[http://www.lamda.org.uk/publications/notices.pdf "Notices"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120525022944/http://www.lamda.org.uk/publications/notices.pdf |date=2012-05-25 }}, ''LAMDA'', Spring 2011. Retrieved 7 April 2013.</ref>

==个人生活==
1998年他与白人女演员[[潔西卡·歐洛沃|潔西卡·歐洛沃]]结婚<ref name="independent1"/>，两人育有四个子女，他们居住在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]<ref name="metro">{{cite web|url=http://metro.co.uk/2013/02/15/from-spooks-to-lincoln-complicit-star-david-oyelowo-is-about-to-make-it-big-in-hollywood-3477715/|title=From Spooks to Lincoln, Complicit star David Oyelowo is about to make it big in Hollywood|work=Metro UK |date=2013-02-15 |accessdate=2013-02-15}}</ref>。

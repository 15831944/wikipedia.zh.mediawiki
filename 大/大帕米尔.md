'''大帕米尔'''（[[瓦罕语|瓦罕语]]: ''Past Pamir''; [[吉尔吉斯语|吉尔吉斯语]]: ''Chang Pamir''; {{lang-fa|''Pāmīr-e Bozorg'' or ''Pāmīr-e Kalan''<ref name=erdkunde>{{Cite journal  | last = Felmy  | first = Sabine  | authorlink =   | author2 = Kreutzmann, Hermann  | title = Wakhan Woluswali in Badakhshan  | journal = Erdkunde  | volume = 58  | issue =   | pages = 97–117  | publisher =   | location =   | year = 2004  | url = http://www.mtnforum.org/oldocs/831.pdf  | issn =   | doi = 10.3112/erdkunde.2004.02.01  | id =   | accessdate = 24 July 2010  }}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name=lp>[http://content.yudu.com/Library/A1nrka/AsiaTravelAFGHANISTA/resources/118.htm Lonely Planet (2007):'  ''Afghanistan'' p.170]</ref>}}）是[[帕米尔|帕米尔]]的[[八帕|八帕]]之一，位于阿富汗与塔吉克斯坦国界瓦罕东北部的[[帕米尔河|帕米尔河]]山谷。长60 km，<ref name=akdn>[http://www.akdn.org/publications/2010_akf_wakhan.pdf Aga Khan Development Network (2010):  ''Wakhan and the Afghan Pamir''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110123124819/http://www.akdn.org/publications/2010_akf_wakhan.pdf |date=2011-01-23 }}</ref>北侧为{{tsl|en|Southern Alichur Range|南阿尔楚尔山}}，南侧为{{tsl|en|Wakhan Range|瓦罕岭}}.

[[佐库里湖|佐库里湖]]位于大帕米尔的山谷中，湖水从西岸向西流出，成为阿-塔两国界河[[帕米尔河|帕米尔河]]，最后注入[[阿姆河|阿姆河]]。佐库里湖位于分水岭上。东侧的高原沼泽湖群是[[苏里斯提克河|苏里斯提克河]]（又名伊斯提克河）的河源，该河东北流注入阿克苏河，最后成为[[穆尔加布河|穆尔加布河]]。

大帕米尔是当地吉尔吉斯族牧民的夏季牧场。<ref>{{Citation|last=Ostrowski|first=Stéphane|title=Wakhi Livestock in Big Pamir in 2006|publisher=[[美国国际开发署|美国国际开发署]]|date=January 2007|url=http://pdf.usaid.gov/pdf_docs/PNADJ571.pdf|accessdate=2010-07-23}}</ref>

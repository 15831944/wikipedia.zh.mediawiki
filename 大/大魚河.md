'''大魚河'''（Great Fish River；{{lang-af|Groot-Visrivier}}） 是一條流經南非[[東開普省|東開普省]]的河流，長{{convert|644|km|mi}}。為了與納米比亞的[[魚河_(納米比亞)|魚河]]相區別而稱“大魚河”<ref>[http://myfundi.co.za/a/Riviere_van_Suid-Afrika Riviere van Suid-Afrika] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120430151102/http://myfundi.co.za/a/Riviere_van_Suid-Afrika |date=2012-04-30 }}</ref>。它原名因凡特河，得名自1480年代來此地探險的[[若昂·因凡特|若昂·因凡特]]。<ref>{{cite book|first=Peter Edmund |last=Raper |title=Dictionary of Southern African Place Names|url=https://archive.org/details/DictionaryOfSouthernAfricanPlaceNames|publisher=Human Science Research Council|location=Johannesburg|year=1987}} (public domain)</ref>

19世紀時它是[[開普殖民地|開普殖民地]]的界河<ref name="teal-1894-87">{{cite book|last=Theal|first=George McCall|title=South Africa (the Cape Colony, Natal, Orange Free State, South African Republic, and all other territories south of the Zambesi)|publisher=Unwin|location=London|year=1894|pages=87–88|url=https://archive.org/stream/southafricatheca00theauoft#page/87/mode/1up|accessdate=2010-02-07}}</ref>，在[[科薩戰爭|科薩戰爭]]時是[[科薩人|科薩人]]和荷蘭及英格蘭殖民者爭奪的前線要衝<ref name="bantu_tribes-333">{{cite book|authors=Doke,Clement M.; Dart,Raymond A.; Goodwin,A.J.H.; Marais,J.S.; Eiselen,W.M.|title=The Bantu Speaking Tribes Of South Africa|publisher=Routledge And Kegan Paul Limited|year=1962|pages=333–334|url=https://archive.org/stream/bantuspeakingtri032916mbp#page/n380/mode/1up}}</ref>。

《'''大帝'''》（{{lang-en|'''''The Great'''''}}）是一部[[美國|美國]][[劇情片|劇情]]類[[網絡劇|網絡]][[迷你劇|迷你劇]]，定於[[Hulu|Hulu]]首播。

== 概要 ==
劇集主要講述[[俄羅斯帝國|俄羅斯帝國]]女皇[[凱瑟琳大帝|凱瑟琳大帝]]的崛起以及她與丈夫[[彼得三世_(俄國)|彼得三世]]的激烈衝突<ref name="NearPilotOrder">{{cite web |last1=Andreeva |first1=Nellie |title=Catherine the Great Drama Starring Elle Fanning & Nicholas Hoult Nears Hulu Pilot Order |url=https://deadline.com/2018/08/the-great-catherine-the-great-drama-elle-fanning-star-nicholas-hoult-hulu-pilot-order-1202451945/ |website=[[Deadline_Hollywood|Deadline Hollywood]] |accessdate={{df|August 24, 2018}} |date={{df|August 24, 2018}}|language=en-US}}</ref>。

=== 開發 ===
2018年8月24日，[[Hulu|Hulu]]宣佈預訂關於[[凱瑟琳大帝|凱瑟琳大帝]]迷你劇的[[試播集|試播集]]。試播集由{{le|湯尼·麥克納馬拉|Tony McNamara (writer)}}執筆編劇，他與[[艾兒·芬妮|艾兒·芬妮]]以及瑪麗安·麥高恩共同擔當執行製片，製作公司包括{{le|媒體權力資本|Media Rights Capital}}、迴聲湖娛樂以及Thruline娛樂<ref name="NearPilotOrder"/>。11月20日，報道稱{{le|馬特·沙克曼|Matt Shakman}}將執導試播集<ref name="ShakmanFoxFilming">{{cite web |last1=Wiseman |first1=Andreas |title=‘The Aeronauts’ & ‘Curfew’ Actress Phoebe Fox Joins Elle Fanning & Nicholas Hoult In Hulu’s ‘The Great’; Pilot Underway |url=https://deadline.com/2018/11/phoebe-fox-elle-fanning-nicholas-hoult-hulu-the-great-catherine-1202505334/ |website=Deadline Hollywood |accessdate={{df|November 20, 2018}} |date={{df|November 20, 2018}}|language=en-US}}</ref>。

2019年2月11日，[[Hulu|Hulu]]在[[電視評論家協會|電視評論家協會]]年度冬季媒體見面會上宣佈預訂整個劇集<ref>{{cite web |last=White |first=Peter |title=Hulu Hands Out Series Orders To Femme Fatale Drama ‘Reprisal’ & Russian Period Romp ‘The Great’ – TCA |url=https://deadline.com/2019/02/hulu-reprisal-the-great-tca-1202554199/ |website=Deadline Hollywood |accessdate={{df|February 11, 2019}} |date={{df|February 11, 2019}}|language=en-US}}</ref>。

=== 選角 ===
隨著試播集確認製作，[[艾兒·芬妮|艾兒·芬妮]]和[[尼古拉斯·霍爾特|尼古拉斯·霍爾特]]確定將領銜出演主角[[凱瑟琳大帝|凱瑟琳大帝]]和她的丈夫[[彼得三世_(俄國)|彼得三世]]<ref name="NearPilotOrder"/>。2018年11月，{{le|菲比·福克斯|Phoebe Fox}}、{{le|沙查·達文|Sacha Dhawan}}、{{le|查麗蒂·威克菲德|Charity Wakefield}}和{{le|格威利姆·李|Gwilym Lee}}確認出演試播集的主要角色<ref name="ShakmanFoxFilming"/><ref name="DhawanWakefieldFilming">{{cite web |last1=Wiseman |first1=Andreas |title=‘Iron Fist’ Actor Sacha Dhawan And ‘Bounty Hunters’ Actress Charity Wakefield Join Hulu’s ‘The Great’ |url=https://deadline.com/2018/11/iron-fist-sacha-dhawan-bounty-hunters-charity-wakefield-hulu-the-great-1202505895/ |website=Deadline Hollywood |accessdate={{df|November 21, 2018}} |date={{df|November 21, 2018}}|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Wiseman |first1=Andreas |title=‘Bohemian Rhapsody’ Star Gwilym Lee Joins Hulu Pilot ‘The Great’ |url=https://deadline.com/2018/11/bohemian-rhapsody-gwilym-lee-brian-may-hulu-series-the-great-elle-fanning-nicholas-hoult-1202509414/ |website=Deadline Hollywood |accessdate={{df|November 27, 2018}} |date={{df|November 27, 2018}}|language=en-US}}</ref>。

'''大露營公園'''（{{lang-bn|জাম্বুরি উদ্যান}}、{{lang-en|Jamboree Park}}）是[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]][[吉大港|吉大港]]{{tsl|en|Agrabad|阿格拉巴德}}的一座公園，因公園用地本是童軍[[大露營|大露營]]使用的場地而得名，該用地後來被當地人用來種菜、擺攤，2016年2月，{{link-en|孟加拉国住房与公共事业部|Ministry of Housing and Public Works)Ministry of Housing and Public Works}}開始在此興建公園，於2018年9月完工開放。大露營公園佔地8.55英畝，地面特地加高3英尺以避免積水，共有六個入口，兩座噴水池<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.newagebd.net/article/50186/eye-catching-jamboree-park-opens-in-ctg|title=Eye-catching Jamboree Park opens in Ctg|publisher=New Age Bangladesh|date=2018-09-09|access-date=2018-11-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180909074923/http://www.newagebd.net/article/50186/eye-catching-jamboree-park-opens-in-ctg#|archive-date=2018-09-09|dead-url=no}}</ref>，園內有數百盞燈，於晚間點亮，形成耀眼的夜景<ref name="turns park">{{cite news |author1=Amir Hasan Shahriar |title=Jamboree playground turns park |url=https://www.thedailystar.net/backpage/news/jamboree-playground-turns-park-1630807 |accessdate=2018-11-19 |publisher={{tsl|en|The Daily Star (Bangladesh)|The Daily Star}} |date=2018-09-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180912092909/https://www.thedailystar.net/backpage/news/jamboree-playground-turns-park-1630807# |archive-date=2018-09-12 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dhakatribune.com/image-gallery/2018/09/08/in-pictures-chittagong-jamboree-playground-is-now-a-lit-park|title=In pictures: Chittagong Jamboree playground is now a lit park|publisher=Dhaka Tribune|date=2018-09-08|access-date=2018-11-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180908151650/https://www.dhakatribune.com/image-gallery/2018/09/08/in-pictures-chittagong-jamboree-playground-is-now-a-lit-park#|archive-date=2018-09-08|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

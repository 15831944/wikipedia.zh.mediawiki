'''大綱'''或作'''階層式大綱'''是顯示[[等級制度|層級]]關係和[[樹狀結構|樹狀結構]]型態的一種清單，用於呈現句子之中的要點或者某個主題的條列要旨<ref name="Chicago 2010">{{cite book |title=Chicago Manual of Style |author=<!--Staff writers; no by-line.--> |edition=16th |pages=<!--The online version is not paginaged.--> |chapter=Lists and Outlines (6.121–126) |publisher=U. of Chicago Press |location=Chicago |year=2010 |isbn= |accessdate=2011-10-20}}</ref>。當中的每個項目也許會劃分成更多的子項目。目前通用的主流寫作型態指南，建議其組織層級至少要有兩個子分類<ref>以下是相關寫作型態指南的未盡臚列清單：
:* {{Cite book |last=Turabian |first=K. L |title=A Manual for Writers of Research Papers, Theses, and Dissertations |edition=7th |publisher=U. of Chicago Press |year=2003 |location=Chicago |pages=63–64 |quote=You should have at least two items to list at each level; if you do not, reconsider the structure of the outline.}}
:* {{Cite book |last=Gibaldi |first =J |title=MLA Handbook for Writers of Research Papers |edition=6th |page=53 |publisher=Modern Language Association of America |year=2003 |location=New York |quote=Logic requires that there be a II to complement a I, a B to complement an A, and so on.}}
:* {{Cite web |last=Tardiff |first=Elyssa |author2=Brizee, Allen |title=Four Main Components for Effective Outlines |date=2010-01-08 |url=http://owl.english.purdue.edu/owl/resource/544/01/ |accessdate=2010-04-02 |quote=Division – How do I accomplish this? Each heading should be divided into 2 or more parts. }}
:* {{Cite web |title=How to Make an Outline |url=http://depts.washington.edu/psywc/handouts/pdf/outline.pdf |publisher=Psychology Writing Center, U. of Washington |accessdate=2010-04-02 |quote=Both topic and sentence outlines follow rigid formats... By convention, each category consists of a minimum of two entries. |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100820185827/http://depts.washington.edu/psywc/handouts/pdf/outline.pdf |archivedate=2010-08-20 }}
:* {{Cite web |title=How to Write an Outline |url=http://www.lavc.edu/library/outline.htm |accessdate=2010-04-02 |quote=Each heading and subheading must have at least two parts. |work=Los Angeles City College Library Online |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100330035036/http://www.lavc.edu/Library/outline.htm |archivedate=2010-03-30 }}
</ref>。這種結構可用於文書的草擬方法、作為文件內容的摘要或是整體理解的途徑。除了清單的形式，尚有多種類型選擇。

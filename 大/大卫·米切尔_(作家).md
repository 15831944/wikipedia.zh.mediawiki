'''大卫·米切尔'''（{{lang-en|'''David Stephen Mitchell'''}}，1969年1月12日-）是一位英格兰小说家。他已经发表了五本小说，其中有两本入围[[布克奖|布克奖]]。他曾居住在[[意大利|意大利]]、[[日本|日本]]和[[爱尔兰|爱尔兰]]。

==生平==
他生于[[英格兰|英格兰]][[紹斯波特|紹斯波特]]。成长于[[伍斯特郡|伍斯特郡]]的Malvern。他获得了[[肯特大学|肯特大学]]的[[比较文学|比较文学]]（[[英语文学|英语文学]]与[[美国文学|美国文学]]）硕士学位。

他曾在意大利[[西西里岛|西西里岛]]居住过一年，后来移居日本的[[广岛市|广岛市]]，在此教授[[英语|英语]]和写作达8年时间<ref>"My wife and I moved to the UK in 2002 because it dawned on us, midway through her second trimester, that back in England I could support the soon-to-be three of us from my earnings as a writer alone. If we had stayed in Japan, on the other hand, where the cost of living was higher, I would have had to stick with the day job in order to bring home enough yen, and I would have been unable to help with the imminent arrival any more than an average Japanese husband—that is, not a lot." http://www.theparisreview.org/interviews/6034/the-art-of-fiction-no-204-david-mitchell</ref>。現在他居住在[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]]南部。

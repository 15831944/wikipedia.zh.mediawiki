'''大黄石生态系统'''<ref>{{cite web|title=【大提顿国家公园】你不知道的黄石以南！|url=http://travel.sina.com/article/toutiao/2309404085645353074575|website=travel.sina.com|accessdate=2018-04-24|language=zh-cn}}</ref>（{{lang-en|'''G'''reater '''Y'''ellowstone '''E'''cosystem}}，简称{{lang|en|GYE}}）是整个地球北[[温带|温带]]地区现存为数不多、近乎完好的[[生态系统|生态系统]]。<ref name="ecosystem">{{cite web|last=Schullery |first=Paul |url=http://biology.usgs.gov/s+t/noframe/r114.htm |title=The Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem |work=Our Living Resources |publisher=U.S. Geological Survey |accessdate=2007-03-13 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060927172031/http://biology.usgs.gov/s%2Bt/noframe/r114.htm |archivedate=2006-09-27 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>它位于北[[洛磯山脈|洛磯山脈]]，西北[[怀俄明州|怀俄明州]]、西南[[蒙大拿州|蒙大拿州]]和东[[爱达荷州|爱达荷州]]，[[黃石國家公園|黃石國家公園]]和[[黄石火山|黄石火山]]都位于其内。<ref>Initial wikipedia article was based on [[公有领域|public domain]] [http://biology.usgs.gov/s+t/noframe/r114.htm Biology.usgs.gov content written by Paul Schullery] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060925064249/http://biology.usgs.gov/s+t/noframe/r114.htm |date=2006-09-25 }}, from the [[美国国家公园管理局|U.S. National Park Service]].</ref>

大黄石生态系统是世界上影响最深远的[[景观生态学|景观生态学]]、[[全新世|全新世]][[地质学|地质学]]自然实验室之一，也是有名的休闲胜地之一。它有着多种多样的[[本土植物|本土植物]]和{{tsl|en|animals of Yellowstone|黄石动物|动物}}。

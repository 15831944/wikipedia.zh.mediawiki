'''休斯敦–伍德蘭–舒格蘭'''為[[美国行政管理和预算局|美国行政管理和预算局]]定義的[[城市群|城市群]]，包括9個縣，位於[[美國墨西哥灣沿岸地區|美國墨西哥灣沿岸地區]][[德克萨斯州|德克萨斯州]]東南部，一般通稱該都會區為「'''大休士頓'''」。

休斯敦–伍德蘭–舒格蘭為美國第5大都會區，為德克薩斯州第2大都會區，僅次於{{tsl|en|Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex|達拉斯–沃斯堡都會區}}，根據2010年7月1日人口普查，該都會區人口為6,490,180。<ref>Ura, Alexa. [http://www.texastribune.org/2015/03/26/texas-counties-cities-among-fastest-growing-us/ Texas Counties, Metros Among Fastest-Growing in U.S.], ''{{tsl|en|Texas Tribune||Texas Tribune}}'', March 26, 2015</ref><ref>{{cite web |title = Houston is the fifth largest metro area in the United States | url = http://abclocal.go.com/ktrk/story?section=news/local&id=8609118 | publisher = | date = 2012-04-05 | accessdate = 2012-04-05 }}</ref><ref name="www.census.gov">{{cite web | last = U.S. Census Bureau | first = | title = Population Estimates, Metropolitan and Micropolitan Statistical Areas | url = http://www.census.gov/popest/data/metro/totals/2011/index.html | publisher = | date = 2012-04-05 | accessdate = 2012-04-05 }}</ref><ref name="USCensus04052012">{{cite web | last = U.S. Census Bureau | first = | title = Census Estimates Show New Patterns of Growth Nationwide | url = http://www.census.gov/newsroom/releases/archives/population/cb12-55.html | publisher = | date = 2012-04-05 | accessdate = 2012-04-05 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.bizjournals.com/houston/news/2011/03/07/houston-5th-in-metro-population.html | first=G. Scott | last=Thomas | title=Houston 5th in metro population rankings, study shows | date=2011-03-07}}</ref>休士頓都會區為[[美國墨西哥灣沿岸地區|美國墨西哥灣沿岸地區]]最大的人口聚居地。

都會區核心城市為[[休斯敦|休斯敦]]—為{{nowrap|[[美國南部|美國南部]]}}最大的經濟及文化中心，人口為220萬人。<ref>[http://www.census.gov/popest/cities/tables/SUB-EST2009-01.csv ] [http://webarchive.loc.gov/all/20101017143254/http%3A//www%2Ecensus%2Egov/popest/cities/tables/SUB%2DEST2009%2D01%2Ecsv Archived copy] at the [[國會圖書館_(美國)|國會圖書館 (美國)]] (October 17, 2010).</ref>

休士頓為美國成長最快的都會區之一。休斯敦–伍德蘭–舒格蘭都會區於2013-2014年間為人口增加最多的都會區，增加156,371人。<ref>{{cite news |last1=SCHNEIDER |first1=MIKE |last2=HOLLAND |first2=JESSE J. |date=2015-03-26 |title=CENSUS: FLORIDA CITY TOPS LIST OF FASTEST-GROWING AREAS |url=http://hosted.ap.org/dynamic/stories/U/US_CENSUS_GROWING_CITIES?SITE=AP&SECTION=HOME&TEMPLATE=DEFAULT |newspaper=Associated Press |location= |access-date=2015-03-27 |quote=The Houston-The Woodlands-Sugar Land metro area was also the top in metro area numerical increase with 156,371 people added between 2013 and 2014, followed by the Dallas-Fort Worth-Arlington area with a 131,217-person increase and the New York-Newark-Jersey City-Pennsylvania area with a 90,797-person increase. |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150328190418/http://hosted.ap.org/dynamic/stories/U/US_CENSUS_GROWING_CITIES?SITE=AP&SECTION=HOME&TEMPLATE=DEFAULT |archivedate=2015-03-28 }}</ref>都會區面積在1990至2000年間增加25.2%，人口增加超過950,000人，同期全國人口增加13.2%。2000年至2007年，大休士頓地區增加912,994人。<ref>[http://recenter.tamu.edu/data/popm00/pcbsa26420.html Houston-Sugar Land-Baytown, TX Metropolitan Statistical Area (CBSA) Population and Components of Change<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090125215914/http://recenter.tamu.edu/data/popm00/pcbsa26420.html |date=2009-01-25 }}</ref> 自2000年至2030年間，預估都會區將增加266萬人，人口增加數量為全國第五。<ref name="houston.org">{{cite web|url=http://www.houston.org/economic-development/facts-figures/index.aspx|title=Economic development|publisher=www.houston.org|accessdate=2017-08-31|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120907021814/http://www.houston.org/economic-development/facts-figures/index.aspx|archivedate=2012-09-07}}</ref>大休士頓都會區屬於[[德州三角地帶|德州三角地帶]]都會帶。

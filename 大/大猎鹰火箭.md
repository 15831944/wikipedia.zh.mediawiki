'''大猎鹰火箭'''（Big Falcon Rocket，官方简称'''BFR'''<ref name="musk20170929" />）是由[[私人股权投资|私人股权投资]]的[[SpaceX|SpaceX]]公司推出的下一代[[发射载具|发射载具]]和[[宇宙飞船|宇宙飞船]]，该项计划是由于[[伊隆·马斯克|伊隆·马斯克]]于2017年9月正式宣布；<ref name="sn20170929">{{cite news|url=http://spacenews.com/musk-unveils-revised-version-of-giant-interplanetary-launch-system/|title=Musk unveils revised version of giant interplanetary launch system|author=Jeff Foust|date=29 September 2017|work=[[SpaceNews|SpaceNews]]|accessdate=1 October 2017}}</ref><ref name="sfn20170929" />初代样机于2018年3月正式制造。<ref name="sn20180312">{{cite news|url=http://spacenews.com/musk-reiterates-plans-for-testing-bfr/|title=Musk reiterates plans for testing BFR|last=Foust|first=Jeff|date=2018-03-12|work=[[SpaceNews|SpaceNews]]|accessdate=2018-03-15|quote=Construction of the first prototype spaceship is in progress. 'We’re actually building that ship right now,' he said. 'I think we’ll probably be able to do short flights, short sort of up-and-down flights, probably sometime in the first half of next year.'}}</ref>整个航天器计划包可重复使用的[[运载火箭|运载火箭]]和航天器、地面基础设施快速发射和重启，以及在近地轨道部署零重力推进输送技术。新的航天器将比现有的SpaceX飞船大得多。作为[[超重型运载火箭|超重型运载火箭]]，BFR在[[地球轨道|地球轨道]]的最大有效载荷量将达到{{convert|150,000|kg|lb|lk=on|abbr=on}}。 

BFR系统预计将代替[[猎鹰9号|猎鹰9号]]、[[猎鹰重型运载火箭|猎鹰重型运载火箭]]以及[[龙飞船|"龙"号飞船]]，最初只用于[[地球轨道|地球轨道]]，但在长时间准备后，已可支持在地月间以及[[火星|火星]]飞行并完成任务。<ref name="musk20170929" />SpaceX公司打算用这种方法降低成本，并证明其开发费用的真正价值。<ref name="musk201803journal">{{cite journal|title=Making Life Multi-Planetary|url=https://doi.org/10.1089/space.2018.29013.emu|first1=Elon|date=1 March 2018|journal=New Space|accessdate=29 March 2018|issue=1|volume=6|last1=Musk}}</ref>

SpaceX最初设想于2016年9月完成BFR的发射，并使它成为[[行星际运输系统|行星际运输系统]]的一部分。<ref name="ars20160918">{{cite news|url=https://arstechnica.com/science/2016/09/spacexs-interplanetary-transport-system-will-go-well-beyond-mars/|title=Elon Musk scales up his ambitions, considering going "well beyond" Mars|author=Eric Berger|date=18 September 2016|work=[[Ars_Technica|Ars Technica]]|accessdate=19 September 2016}}</ref>行星际运输系统的航天器核心设计大小为12米，<ref name="NYT-20160927">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/09/28/science/elon-musk-spacex-mars-exploration.html|title=Elon Musk’s Plan: Get Humans to Mars, and Beyond|author=Kenneth Chang|date=27 September 2016|work=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]]|accessdate=27 September 2016}}</ref>而BFR则为9米。<ref name="musk20170929" />因此，BFR一直致力于[[地月轨道|地月轨道]]和[[火星|火星]]的用途，SpaceX总部于2017年将BFR的用途定义为：地球轨道飞船，月球轨道，行星际探测任务，甚至在地球进行载客运行。<ref name="musk20170929" /><ref name="Dent20170929" />

BFR的开发工作始于2012年，依靠[[猛禽火箭发动机|猛禽火箭发动机]]进行改造，共分为两阶段，于2016正式开始测试。新的火箭发动机的设计相比新的运载火箭通常拥有较长的[[前置时间|前置时间]]。航空设施也处于建设队列中：首个太空船于2018年3月开始制造；<ref name="sn20180312" />2019年预计将完成首次[[亚轨道飞行|亚轨道飞行]]测试。<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KORTP545vAc Falcon Heavy maiden flight press conference]</ref>SpaceX则表示其最初期望是在2022完成首次[[火星|火星]]的货运航班，在2024年完成完成载人计划。<ref name="sn20180312" /><ref name="sn20170929" />

2018年11月，[[伊隆·马斯克|伊隆·马斯克]]将第二级和飞船改名为'''Starship'''，而第一级则被他称为「'''Super Heavy'''」<ref>{{Cite web|title=Technically, two parts: Starship is the spaceship/upper stage & Super Heavy is the rocket booster needed to escape Earth’s deep gravity well (not needed for other planets or moons)|url=https://twitter.com/elonmusk/status/1064741356080222209|work=Twitter|date=2018-11-19|accessdate=2019-03-03|language=en|first=Elon|last=Musk}}</ref>。

== 系统命名 ==
在过去的五年内，SpaceX公司已公开发布于BFR的信息。BFR是马斯克于2017年9月宣布的名称。<ref name="musk20170929" /><ref name="sn20170929" /><ref name="sfn20170929">{{cite news|url=https://spaceflightnow.com/2017/09/29/elon-musk-revises-mars-plan-hopes-for-boots-on-ground-in-2024/|title=Elon Musk revises Mars plan, hopes for boots on ground in 2024|author=William Harwood|date=29 September 2017|work=SpaceflightNow|accessdate=30 September 2017|quote=The new rocket is still known as the BFR, a euphemism for 'Big (fill-in-the-blank) Rocket.' The reusable BFR will use 31 Raptor engines burning densified, or super-cooled, liquid methane and liquid oxygen to lift 150 tons, or 300,000 pounds, to low Earth orbit, roughly equivalent to NASA’s Saturn 5 moon rocket.}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://qz.com/1090435/spacexs-elon-musk-unveils-a-new-rocket-that-can-fly-to-the-moon-mars-and-shanghai/|title=SpaceX’s Elon Musk unveiled a rocket that can fly to the Moon, Mars—and Shanghai|author=Tim Fernholz|date=29 September 2017|work=Quartz|accessdate=30 September 2017|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.spacex.com/media-gallery/detail/149426/9376|title=Artist's Rendering Of The BFR|date=12 April 2017|publisher=SpaceX|accessdate=3 October 2017|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://money.cnn.com/2017/09/29/technology/future/elon-musk-spacex-mars-iac-conference/index.html|title=Elon Musk is aiming to land spaceships on Mars in 2022|date=29 September 2017|work=CNNMoney|author2=Jackie Wattles|accessdate=29 September 2017|author1=Sherisse Pham}}</ref> SpaceX公司总裁Gwynne Shotwell说BFR是BIG FALCON ROCKET的缩写，意思是大型猎鹰火箭。<ref name="Acronym Explained">{{cite web|url=https://www.space.com/38393-spacex-bfr-mars-colony-rocket-name.html|title=What's in a Name? SpaceX's 'BFR' Mars Rocket Acronym Explained|accessdate=11 February 2018|author=Mike Wall|website=space.com}}</ref>然而，[[伊隆·马斯克|伊隆·马斯克]]后来解释到，虽然BFR是官方的名字，但其实名字灵感来源于[[毁灭战士系列|毁灭战士系列]]游戏里的[[BFG_9000|BFG]]武器。<ref name="gq-elon-interview">{{cite web|url=https://www.gq.com/story/elon-musk-mars-spacex-tesla-interview|title=Elon Musk Is Ready to Conquer Mars|accessdate=14 February 2018|date=12 December 2015|language=en|last1=Heath|first1=Chris|website=GQ}}</ref>

从2016年9月到2017年8月，整体系统被称为SpaceX公司为“行星际运输系统”，其高度约为12米。而在2013年6月至2016年9月，BFR既作为[[火星殖民|火星殖民]]运输也作为[[行星际运输系统|行星际运输系统]]运作。

== BFR任务 ==
BFR系统预计将代替猎鹰9号、猎鹰重型运载火箭以及飛龙号太空船。<ref name="nsf20170929">{{cite news|url=https://www.nasaspaceflight.com/2017/09/the-moon-mars-earth-musk-updates-bfr-plans/|title=The Moon, Mars, & around the Earth – Musk updates BFR architecture, plans|author=Chris Gebhardt|date=29 September 2017|accessdate=2 October 2017|quote=In a move that would have seemed crazy a few years ago, Mr. Musk stated that the goal of BFR is to make the Falcon 9 and the Falcon Heavy rockets and their crew/uncrewed Dragon spacecrafts redundant, thereby allowing the company to shift all resources and funding allocations from those vehicles to BFR. Making the Falcon 9, Falcon Heavy, and Dragon redundant would also allow BFR to perform the same Low Earth Orbit (LEO) and Beyond LEO satellite deployment missions as Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy – just on a more economical scale as multiple satellites would be able to launch at the same time and on the same rocket thanks to BFR’s immense size.|website=[[NASASpaceflight.com|NASASpaceflight.com]]}}</ref><ref name="musk201803journal" /><ref name="musk20170929" />

BFR的主要飞行任务:<ref name="nsf20170929" />

* [[地球轨道|地球轨道]]探索
* 在[[地月轨道|地月轨道]]之间的长期飞行任务
* [[火星|火星]]任务, 作为货船和载人太空船
* 经济的太空远距离旅行: 在不到1小时的时间内完成次轨道内点对点运输。<ref name="sn20171015c" /><ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?time_continue=40&v=zqE-ultsWt0 BFR Earth to Earth], SpaceX, 28 September 2017, accessed 23 December 2017.</ref> 马斯克将其比喻为从地球到地球。<ref name="rollingstone20171115">{{cite news|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/culture/features/elon-musk-inventors-plans-for-outer-space-cars-finding-love-w511747|title=Elon Musk: The Architect of Tomorrow|author=Neil Strauss|date=15 November 2017|work=Rolling Stone|accessdate=17 February 2018}}</ref>

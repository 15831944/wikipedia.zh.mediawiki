'''大果紫檀'''（'''''Pterocarpus macrocarpus'''''；緬甸語：မြန်မာပိတောက်）是[[紫檀屬|紫檀屬]]的一種喬木，分佈于東南亞國家如緬甸、老撾、柬埔寨、越南<ref name=ildis/><ref name=danida>{{cite web | title = ''Pterocarpus macrocarpus'' | url = http://www.sl.kvl.dk/upload/pterocarpus_macrocarpus_int.pdf | publisher = Danida Forest Seed Centre | accessdate = 3 July 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100331203120/http://www.sl.kvl.dk/upload/pterocarpus_macrocarpus_int.pdf | archivedate = 31 March 2010 | deadurl = yes | df =  }}</ref><ref name=iitf>{{cite web | title = ''Pterocarpus macrocarpus'' | url = http://www.rngr.net/Publications/ttsm/Folder.2003-07-11.4726/PDF.2004-03-16.0514/file | publisher = International Institute of Tropical Forestry | accessdate = 3 July 2017 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20090116124438/http://www.rngr.net/Publications/ttsm/Folder.2003-07-11.4726/PDF.2004-03-16.0514/file | archivedate = 16 January 2009}}</ref>，後來引入印度和加勒比<ref name=danida/>。

== 形態==

大果紫檀高度在10–30米之間，樹幹直徑1.7米，樹皮呈灰褐色，分泌紅色樹膠<ref name=danida/><ref name=iitf/>。其木材耐用，可用來製作家具。大果紫檀每年在[[潑水節|潑水節]]時開花，在緬甸是一種國家象征。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.anbg.gov.au/emblems/world-emblems.html|title=Floral Emblems of the world - Australian Plant Information|last=Australia|first=Australian National Botanic Gardens, Parks|website=www.anbg.gov.au|language=en|access-date=2016-04-14}}</ref>

《'''下女的誘惑'''》（{{lang-ko|'''아가씨'''}}，{{lang-en|'''''The Handmaiden'''''}}）是一部2016年韓國[[情色片|情色]]{{link-en|心理驚悚片|Psychological thriller}}，為[[朴贊郁|朴贊郁]]執導和監製。電影改編自[[莎拉·華特絲|莎拉·華特絲]]的2002年小說《{{link-en|荊棘之城|Fingersmith (novel)}}》，並將原本的[[维多利亚时代|维多利亚时代]]變更至[[朝鮮日治時期|日本殖民統治時期]]<ref>{{cite web|last1=Dale|first1=Martin|title=Park Chan-wook Talks About Next Pic ''The Handmaiden''|url=http://variety.com/2015/film/global/park-chan-wook-next-pic-the-hand-maiden-1201658129/|website=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|accessdate=May 10, 2016|date=December 10, 2015}}</ref><ref name="Noh">{{cite web |url= http://www.screendaily.com/news/park-chan-wooks-handmaiden-pre-sells-to-116-countries/5100808.article|title= Park Chan-wook's ''Handmaiden'' pre-sells to 116 countries|first= Jean|last=  Noh|date= February 24, 2016|accessdate= February 25, 2016|work= [[Screen_Daily|Screen Daily]]}}</ref>。由[[金敏喜|金敏喜]]、[[河正宇|河正宇]]、[[金泰梨|金泰梨]]和[[趙震雄|趙震雄]]主演。

電影於2016年5月14日在[[第69屆坎城影展|第69屆坎城影展]]上首映並入選競爭[[金棕櫚獎|金棕櫚獎]]<ref name="IW">{{cite web |last=Erbland|first=Kate|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/cannes-live-stream-2016-film-festival-lineup-list |title=2016 Cannes Film Festival Announces Lineup |accessdate=April 14, 2016|date=April 14, 2016|work=[[IndieWire|IndieWire]]}}</ref><ref name="VarCannes">{{cite web |url=http://variety.com/2016/film/festivals/cannes-film-festival-official-selection-lineup-2016-1201753269/ |title=Cannes 2016: Film Festival Unveils Official Selection Lineup |accessdate=April 14, 2016|date=April 14, 2016|work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|last1=Debruge|first1=Peter|first2=Elsa|last2=Keslassy}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Kim|first1=Jae-heun|title=Park Chan-wook's new film to compete at Cannes|url=https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/art/2016/04/141_202712.html|website=[[The_Korea_Times|The Korea Times]]|accessdate=May 10, 2016|date=April 15, 2016}}</ref><ref>http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/park-chan-wook-handmaiden-homosexuality-893716</ref><ref>http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/south-korea-box-office-cannes-900292</ref>。

==劇情==
故事設定在1930年代日本殖民統治下的朝鮮半島。敘述獨自繼承龐大財產的秀子小-{}-姐受到了貪婪狡猾的騙子的覬覦，為了得到其財產而不擇手段，讓找來的女僕安排於小-{}-姐的身旁，而即將上演一場陰謀與情慾交織的懸疑局勢。

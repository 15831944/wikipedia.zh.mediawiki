'''飓风弗朗西丝'''（{{lang-en|Hurricane Frances}}）是[[2004年大西洋飓风季|2004年大西洋飓风季]]形成的第6场获得命名的风暴，也是第4场飓风和第3场大型飓风。

== 气象历史 ==

8月21日，一股强劲东风波离开非洲西海岸<ref name="TWO1">{{cite report | author = Lawrence | title = Tropical Weather Outlook | publisher = National Hurricane Center | year = 2004 | accessdate = 2015-11-26 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151005012506/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/text/TWOAT/2004/TWOAT.200408210913 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/text/TWOAT/2004/TWOAT.200408210913 | archivedate = 2015-10-05}}</ref>。系统连续数日沿[[副热带高压脊|副热带高压脊]]底部向西移动<ref name="tropdis1">{{cite report | author = Beven | title = Hurricane Frances Forecast Discussion #3 | publisher = National Hurricane Center | year = 2004 | accessdate = 2015-11-26 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151006044607/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2004/dis/al062004.discus.003.shtml | deadurl = no | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2004/dis/al062004.discus.003.shtml? | archivedate = 2015-10-06}}</ref>，虽然外界环境有利，但结构一直杂乱无章<ref name="TWO3">{{cite report | author = Stewart | title = Tropical Weather Outlook | publisher = National Hurricane Center | year = 2004 | accessdate = 2015-11-26 | archivedate = 2015-10-04 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151004233328/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/text/TWOAT/2004/TWOAT.200408231502 | url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/text/TWOAT/2004/TWOAT.200408231502 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

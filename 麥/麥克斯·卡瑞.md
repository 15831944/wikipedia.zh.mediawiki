'''麥克斯·喬治·卡瑞'''（{{lang-en|Max George Carey}}, {{bd|1890年|1月11日|1976年|5月30日|}}），原名Maximillian George Carnarius，為美國職棒大聯盟的外野手。先後待過海盜與羅賓隊。1932到1933年，他在道奇隊擔任總教練。

1925年，他帶領海盜隊拿下世界大賽冠軍。20年棒球生涯拿下10次盜壘王，生涯738次盜壘成功曾為國聯史上最高。1961年入選名人堂。
==職業生涯==
1909年，卡瑞在中央聯盟(小聯盟)打球。1910年，中央聯盟的主席向海盜隊推薦卡瑞。8月15日，卡瑞加入海盜隊。於10月3日登上大聯盟，接替老將Fred Clarke。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/teams/PIT/1910.shtml|title=1910 Pittsburgh Pirates|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>

1912年，卡瑞取代Tommy Leach的位置，成為球隊的中外野手。<ref name=hof>{{cite news |url=https://www.newspapers.com/clip/14759991/careys_path_to_the_hall_of_fame/ |title=Carey's Path to the Hall of Fame |newspaper=[[The_Miami_News|The Miami News]] |date=January 30, 1961 |accessdate=October 29, 2017 |via=newspapers.com}}</ref>該年他的打擊率為2成58。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/teams/PIT/1911.shtml|title=1911 Pittsburgh Pirates|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>1913年，卡瑞該年以61盜拿下國聯盜壘王。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1913-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1913 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>1915年到1918年，他連續4年拿下盜壘王。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1915-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1915 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1916-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1916 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1917-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1917 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>

1919年，卡瑞因為受傷，球季幾乎報銷。1920年，他以52盜再度問鼎盜壘王寶座。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1920-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1920 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>1922年到1925年，他再度蟬聯盜壘王。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1922-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1922 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1923-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1923 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1924-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1924 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1925-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1925 National League Batting Leaders|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>其中1922年，他嘗試53次盜壘，只有2次被抓到。

1925年，卡瑞的打擊率為3成43。海盜也睽違16年再度打進世界大賽。卡瑞在世界大賽的打擊率高達4成58。第7場，他面對參議員隊王牌[[華特·強森|華特·強森]]還擊出三支二壘安打，最終海盜4勝3敗贏得世界大賽冠軍。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1925_WS.shtml|title=1925 World Series - Pittsburgh Pirates over Washington Senators (4-3) - Baseball-Reference.com|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>

1926年，卡瑞的進攻火力稍微下滑。而當時因為卡瑞出賽而被壓縮上場空間的Fred Clarke，已經成為海盜總教練的助理，他向總教練建議將卡瑞移出名單。後來卡瑞與另外兩位不滿Clarke的球員發起投票，希望Clarke不要干涉球團運作，不過後來遭到反對。當時不滿Clarke的兩名球員後來都遭到釋出，卡瑞則是遭到禁賽。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1787&dat=19260814&id=-uIhAAAAIBAJ&sjid=GWQEAAAAIBAJ&pg=2881,5695304|title=Pirates Drop Vets, Suspend Their Captain|work=Sarasota Herald-Tribune|via=Google News Archive Search|publisher=|accessdate=November 7, 2014}}</ref>隨後海盜隊將卡瑞放進讓渡名單，最終被羅賓隊簽走。<ref name=Clarke>{{cite book|last1=Waldo|first1=Ronald|title=Fred Clarke: A Biography of the Baseball Hall of Fame Player-Manager|date=2010|publisher=McFarland|isbn=0786460164|pages=203–205|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=aLIW4IFait4C|accessdate=November 1, 2014}}</ref>1929年，卡瑞的身手已不如以往，於是決定退休。
==生涯後期==
1930年退休後，卡瑞重返海盜隊成為教練團的一員。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1129&dat=19301031&id=h7ZRAAAAIBAJ&sjid=j2gDAAAAIBAJ&pg=2057,849102|title=Mirrors of Sport|work=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|via=Google News Archive Search|first=Havey J.|last=Boyle|date=October 31, 1930|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>1932年，他接替Wilbert Robinson成為道奇隊的總教練。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1917&dat=19311024&id=yIxGAAAAIBAJ&sjid=ZekMAAAAIBAJ&pg=3224,6678371|work=Schenectady Gazette|title=Carey Gets Pilot's Job Of Dodgers|via=Google News Archive Search|agency=[[Associated_Press|AP]]|date=October 24, 1931|accessdate=November 7, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1955&dat=19320105&id=7Y8zAAAAIBAJ&sjid=V-EFAAAAIBAJ&pg=4063,662008|work=Reading Eagle|via=Google News Archive Search|title=Max Carey Says He Will Give Brooklyn Batter Baseball With Injection of New Ideas|first=Gayle|last=Talbot|date=January 5, 1932|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>1932年，道奇隊戰績為國聯第三。但是在1933年，道奇隊掉到國聯第六。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1144&dat=19340220&id=BJAcAAAAIBAJ&sjid=v44EAAAAIBAJ&pg=3824,6542842||title=Flatbush Betting 5 to 1 Max Carey Gets The Ax|work=The Pittsburgh Press|via=Google News Archive Search|agency=[[United_Press_International|UP]]|date=February 20, 1934|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>後來因為布魯克林當地報紙的批評，他被Casey Stangel給取代總教練一職。他也被戲稱是第一位被新聞報紙解雇的總教練。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1144&dat=19340221&id=BZAcAAAAIBAJ&sjid=v44EAAAAIBAJ&pg=4390,6946454|title=Brookly Dismisses Max Carey As Manager|work=The Pittsburgh Press|via=Google News Archive Search|date=February 21, 1934|accessdate=November 7, 2014}}</ref>

1940到1941年，他在小聯盟擔任總教練。1944年，他還在女子聯盟擔任總教練，甚至還幫助球隊拿下總冠軍。
==晚年與紀念==
卡瑞後來搬到佛羅里達州，並投資房地產。但是1929年爆發[[1929年華爾街股災|華爾街股災]]，他損失了10萬美元(約今146萬美元)。1950年代，他成為了作家。

1961年，名人堂資深委員會將卡瑞與19世紀的盜壘王Billy Hamilton入選名人堂。<ref name=hof2>{{cite news |url=https://www.newspapers.com/clip/14759831/dream_comes_true_carey_reaches_hall/ |title=Dream Comes True, Carey Reaches 'Hall' |first=Tommy |last=Fitzgerald |newspaper=[[The_Miami_News|The Miami News]] |date=January 30, 1961 |accessdate=October 29, 2017 |via=newspapers.com}}</ref>

1976年5月30日，高齡86歲的卡瑞於邁阿密辭世。<ref name=obit>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=2512&dat=19760601&id=uNZHAAAAIBAJ&sjid=5v8MAAAAIBAJ&pg=2494,65354|title=Carey top base-thief of his day|work=The Morning Record|via=Google News Archive Search|agency=[[Associated_Press|AP]]|date=June 1, 1976|accessdate=November 7, 2014}}</ref>

卡瑞接飛球的能力非常優異，<ref name=miami1963>{{cite news |url=https://www.newspapers.com/clip/14760248/two_exoutfielders_enter_hall_of_fame/ |title=Two Ex-Outfielders Enter Hall Of Fame |newspaper=[[The_Palm_Beach_Post|The Palm Beach Post]] |agency=[[Associated_Press|AP]] |date=January 30, 1961 |accessdate=October 29, 2017 |via=newspapers.com}}</ref>他生涯738盜的國聯紀錄更是直到1974年才被Lou Brock打破。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=950&dat=19760531&id=0lhQAAAAIBAJ&sjid=T1gDAAAAIBAJ&pg=6695,3274030|title=Max Carey Succcumbs|work=The Evening Independent|via=Google News Archive Search|agency=[[Associated_Press|AP]]|date=May 31, 1976|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>

在卡瑞年輕的時候，他母親曾在他球褲下縫製一層特殊的布料，防止他滑壘時造成腿部與臀部受傷。後來卡瑞申請專利，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1144&dat=19630528&id=30YqAAAAIBAJ&sjid=8U4EAAAAIBAJ&pg=6462,4649154|work=The Pittsburgh Press|title=Big Ideas Copied; Even Lillian Russell Got Into The Act|via=Google News Archive Search|first=Harry|last=Ferguson|agency=[[United_Press_International|UPI]]|date=May 28, 1963|accessdate=November 7, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1144&dat=19270910&id=XjkbAAAAIBAJ&sjid=LUoEAAAAIBAJ&pg=1997,4392266|work=The Pittsburgh Press|via=Google News Archive Search|title=Patents Granted|date=September 10, 1927|accessdate=November 7, 2014}}</ref>他將布料塗上止痛藥，稱之為Minute-Rub。

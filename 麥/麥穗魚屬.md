'''麥穗魚屬'''（''Pseudorasbora''） 是[[鯉科|鯉科]][[鮈亚科|鮈亚科]]下的一個屬，其下為生活在東亞和西伯利亞的淡水魚。其模式種[[麥穗魚|麥穗魚]]已被引入世界其他地區，成為[[入侵物種|入侵物種]]。

==種==
該屬下共5種：
* ''[[Pseudorasbora_elongata|Pseudorasbora elongata]]'' <small>[[Wu_Hsien-Wen|H. W. Wu]], 1939</small>
* ''[[Pseudorasbora_interrupta|Pseudorasbora interrupta]]'' <small>[[Xiao_Zhi_(biologist)|Z. Xiao]], [[Lan_Zong-Hui|Z. H. Lan]] & [[Chen_Xiang-Lin|X. L. Chen]], 2007</small>
* ''[[Pseudorasbora_parva|Pseudorasbora parva]]'' <small>[[Coenraad_Jacob_Temminck|Temminck]] & [[Hermann_Schlegel|Schlegel]], 1846</small>
* ''[[Pseudorasbora_pugnax|Pseudorasbora pugnax]]'' <small>[[Seigo_Kawase|Kawase]] & [[Kazumi_Hosoya|Hosoya]], 2015</small> <ref name=Kawase2015>{{cite journal | authors = Kawase, S. & Hosoya, K. | year = 2015 | title = ''Pseudorasbora pugnax'', a new species of minnow from Japan, and redescription of ''P. pumila'' (Teleostei: Cyprinidae) | journal = Ichthyological Exploration of Freshwaters | volume = 25 | issue = 4 | pages =  289–298 | url = http://www.pfeil-verlag.de/04biol/pdf/ief25_4_01.pdf }}</ref>
* ''[[Pseudorasbora_pumila|Pseudorasbora pumila]]'' <small>[[Denzaburo_Miyadi|Miyadi]], 1930</small>

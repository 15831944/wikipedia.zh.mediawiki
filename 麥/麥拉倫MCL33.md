==設計及開發==
===引擎供應===
2017年9月，麥拉倫稱[[本田車隊|本田]]無法提供穩定且具有競爭力的動力單元，故終止了與本田的引擎合作關係。<ref name="renault"/><ref name="bbc renault"/>在麥拉倫尋求新的供應商期間，[[雷诺车队|雷諾]]曾透露他們無法在滿足現有客戶[[紅牛車隊|紅牛車隊]]及[[紅牛第二車隊|紅牛第二車隊]]的需求下，再為麥拉倫提供所需的引擎。最終，麥拉倫與雷諾達成協議，令紅牛二隊終止與雷諾的合作並投奔本田，<ref name="toro rosso">{{cite web|url=http://www.scuderiatororosso.com/en_INT/article/scuderia-toro-rosso-join-forces-honda|title=Scuderia Toro Rosso to join forces with Honda|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170915232349/http://www.scuderiatororosso.com/en_INT/article/scuderia-toro-rosso-join-forces-honda|work=scuderiatororosso.com|publisher=[[紅牛第二車隊|Scuderia Toro Rosso]]|date=2017-09-15|archive-date=2017-09-15|deadurl=yes}}</ref>雷諾引擎[[供应链|供应链]]便足以為麥拉倫提供2018年款雷諾R.E.18。<ref name="Renault R.E.18"/>

===底盤設計===
麥拉倫將引擎供應商由本田轉為雷諾的這項決定，對麥拉倫技術總監所帶領的設計團隊帶來了一些挑戰。原先，本田的渦輪增壓器位於引擎後方、壓縮機則位於前方，熱能回收系統安裝在V型汽缸排之中；然而，前述三項部件皆安裝在雷諾R.E.18引擎的後方，致使麥拉倫必須更改車輛的底盤設計，重新開發引擎室、變速箱及後懸吊幾何。<ref name="challenges">{{cite news|url=https://www.speedcafe.com/2018/01/18/mclaren-design-not-compromised-renault-engine/|title=McLaren design not compromised by Renault engine|work=speedcafe.com|date=2018-01-18|accessdate=2018-01-18|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180118122551/https://www.speedcafe.com/2018/01/18/mclaren-design-not-compromised-renault-engine/|archivedate=2018-01-18}}</ref>R.E.18引擎讓麥拉倫在油箱尺寸及位置設計上有較多的彈性，使得引擎位置能夠更加接近車手，然而，引擎中的渦輪卻過於突出而佔據變速箱體的空間，增加了設計上的困難。因此，為了容納這具引擎，該隊必須重新設計變速箱並徹底檢修後懸吊的安裝點。<ref name="challenges"/>

麥拉倫開發出較激進的後懸吊系統幾何設計，將所有的懸吊上部元件組合成一體，進而以最大限度地減輕底盤關鍵區域的重量，達到降低阻力的效果，同時，讓車隊能夠有效控制後擴散器上的空氣流動，並產生更多的空氣動力學抓地力。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/134582/mclaren-introduces-innovative-rear-suspension|title=McLaren 2018 F1 car's innovative rear suspension|first1=Giorgio|last1=Piola|first2=Jonathan|last2=Noble|work=autosport.com|publisher=Motorsport Network|date=2018-02-28|accessdate=2018-03-01|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180228143913/https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/134582/mclaren-introduces-innovative-rear-suspension|archivedate=2018-02-28}}</ref>

季前測試期間，MCL33因為緊密的封裝及冷卻成效不彰，在引擎達到最高工作溫度時，造成車身引擎罩後端出現烤焦痕跡。在為車輛研擬新版車身前，車隊先為本次的測試製作了一具臨時性冷卻組件。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/134694/mclaren-to-make-cooling-fix-after-burning|title=F1 testing: McLaren to make cooling fix after bodywork burning|first=Edd|last=Straw|work=autosport.com|publisher=Motorsport Network|date=2018-03-06|accessdate=2018-03-07|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180307042245/https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/134694/mclaren-to-make-cooling-fix-after-burning|archivedate=2018-03-07}}</ref>季前測試結束後，車隊公開發布表示他們已經解決引擎的可靠性問題。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/134910/mclaren-problems-from-testing-are-resolved|title=McLaren says problems from F1 testing have been resolved|first=Jonathan|last=Noble|work=autosport.com|publisher=Motorsport Network|date=2018-03-20|accessdate=2018-03-20|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180320170015/https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/134910/mclaren-problems-from-testing-are-resolved|archivedate=2018-03-20}}</ref>

====規則變更====
由於運動規則的更動，MCL33必須安裝一具環繞車手頭部上方周圍並固定於座艙前方，由鈦合金製成的叉骨狀框架座艙保護裝置「光環」（{{lang|en|halo}}），以阻擋賽道上飛濺的碎片並提高駕駛安全。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/131961/details-of-halo-crash-testing-revealed-by-fia|title=FIA reveals details of F1 halo crash testing for 2018|last=Barretto|first=Lawrence|date=2017-09-21|website=autosport.com|publisher=Motorsport Network|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171001232146/https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/131961/details-of-halo-crash-testing-revealed-by-fia|archive-date=2017-10-01|dead-url=yes}}</ref>最終版本的光環重達數公斤，迫使車隊大幅修改底盤設計。車隊指出為了通過強制性碰撞測試，需要加固底盤以承受相當於一輛[[新倫敦巴士|倫敦巴士]]的重量。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.speedcafe.com/2018/02/09/halo-forces-significant-redesign-f1-teams/|title=Halo forces significant redesign for F1 teams|first=Mat|last=Coch|work=speedcafe.com|date=2018-02-09|accessdate=2018-02-09|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209122244/https://www.speedcafe.com/2018/02/09/halo-forces-significant-redesign-f1-teams/|archivedate=2018-02-09}}</ref>

經過麥拉倫向國際汽聯的遊說後，2018年規定禁止使用「鯊魚鰭」（一片從引擎罩延伸出來的碳纖維板）。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/133256/shark-fin-banned-for-2018-in-uturn|title=Formula 1 shark fin banned for 2018 in U-turn|last1=Noble|first1=Jonathan|date=2017-11-24|work=autosport.com|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171201040016/https://www.autosport.com/f1/news/133256/shark-fin-banned-for-2018-in-uturn|archive-date=2017-12-01|dead-url=yes|publisher=Motorsport Network|last2=Barretto|first2=Lawrence}}</ref>為了符合規則，MCL33採用一片從集氣箱延伸到排氣口且垂直於引擎罩上的碳纖維鰭板。<ref name="papaya"/>此外，自2018年賽季起，國際汽聯亦禁止車輛在尾翼前方的引擎罩上安裝T形翼（T-wing）。<ref name="t-wing">{{cite web|url=http://www.autosport.com/news/report.php/id/129160/f1-to-clamp-down-on-twings-and-shark-fins|title=F1 T-wings and shark fins to be clamped down on for 2018|last=Freeman|first=Glenn|date=2017-04-25|website=autosport.com|publisher=Motorsport Network|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170731160003/http://www.autosport.com/news/report.php/id/129160/f1-to-clamp-down-on-twings-and-shark-fins|archive-date=2017-07-31|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

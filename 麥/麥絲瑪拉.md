'''麦丝玛拉'''（Max Mara） 是意大利的一家服装公司，1951年由阿希尔·马拉莫迪（Achille Maramotti）成立于[[雷焦艾米利亚|雷焦艾米利亚]]。

==历史==
阿希尔·马拉莫迪（1927年1月7日 – 2005年1月12日）从1947年开始设计服装，1951年成立麦丝玛拉<ref name="Indy">{{Citation | last = Barbieri | first = Annalisa | author-link = | publication-date =2005-01-24 | date = | year =2005 | title =Tycoon who pioneered 'off the peg' designer clothes with his fashion label MaxMara | periodical =The Independent | publication-place = London | url =https://www.independent.co.uk/news/obituaries/achille-maramotti-488030.html | issn = | doi =
 | oclc = | accessdate =2008-04-09}}</ref>。现在该公司仍由马拉莫迪的后裔掌控。<ref name="Times">{{Citation | last =Ashworth | first =Anne | author-link = | publication-date =2008-03-14 | date = | year =2008 | title =MaxMara: A store that's always maximally stylish | periodical =The Times | publication-place =London | url = http://women.timesonline.co.uk/tol/life_and_style/women/fashion/article3547053.ece | accessdate =2008-04-09}}</ref>

==品牌==
该公司下共有35个品牌<ref name="Indy"/>，除去Max Mara本身以外，比较著名的还包括Sportmax、Sportmax Code、Weekend Max Mara、Marella、Pennyblack、iBlues、Max & Co.和[[玛瑞娜·瑞娜迪|玛瑞娜·瑞娜迪]]（Marina Rinaldi）<ref>[http://www.marinarinaldi.com/en/About-Us-History-and-Philosophy History & Philosophy’] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130203084223/http://www.marinarinaldi.com/en/About-Us-History-and-Philosophy |date=2013-02-03 }}, Marina Rinaldi.</ref><ref>Annalisa Barbieri, [https://www.independent.co.uk/news/obituaries/achille-maramotti-488030.html ‘Achille Maramotti’], The Independent, 24 January 2005.</ref>。

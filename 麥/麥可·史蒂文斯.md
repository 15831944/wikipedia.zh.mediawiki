'''麥可·大衛·史蒂文斯'''（Michael David Stevens，{{bd|1986年|1月23日}}），美國[[YouTuber|YouTuber]]、[[教育家|教育家]]、講師、影片編輯與[[喜劇演員|喜劇演員]]，知名於創立和主持YouTube頻道「[[Vsauce|Vsauce]]」，同時亦以該化名聞名。頻道最初的影片主題多為電子遊戲方面，後來因其影片系列《DOT》大受歡迎而慢慢將頻道的題材轉為科教方面<ref name="storybio">{{cite AV media |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MofGXVroQ3g |title=The Story of Michael Stevens, The Man Behind Vsauce. |publisher=YouTube |author=Think Fact |date=2014-01-23 |accessdate=2015-09-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150517224849/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MofGXVroQ3g |archive-date=2015-05-17 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，且集中解釋科學、[[哲學|哲學]]、文化和[[錯覺|錯覺]]<ref name="faze">{{cite web |url=http://faze.ca/youtube-creator-vsauce-michael-stevens/ |title=Meet The Man Behind Vsauce: Michael Stevens |publisher=Faze Media |author=Kate Vickery |date=2015 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160906213240/http://faze.ca/youtube-creator-vsauce-michael-stevens/ |archive-date=2016-09-06 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

作為「Vsauce」的創立者，史蒂文斯也順勢成為網路科教和[[大眾科學|大眾科學]]的主要推手<ref name="atlantic">{{cite web |url=https://www.theatlantic.com/education/archive/2014/10/what-can-teachers-learn-from-youtube/381887/ |title=What Teachers Can Learn From Vsauce's YouTube Show |publisher=The Atlantic |author=Jessica Lahey |date=2014-10-28 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908021646/https://www.theatlantic.com/education/archive/2014/10/what-can-teachers-learn-from-youtube/381887/ |archive-date=2017-09-08 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="wmeimg">{{cite web|url=http://wmeimgspeakers.com/speaker/michael-stevens#tab-biography |title=MICHAEL STEVENS: YouTube sensation aka Vsauce |publisher=WME IMG Speakers |accessdate=2016-09-02}}</ref>。截至2018年12月6日，該頻道擁有約1400萬訂閱者和15億觀看數<ref name="youtube">{{cite AV media|url=https://www.youtube.com/user/Vsauce/videos|title=Vsauce – YouTube: Videos|publisher=YouTube|author=|accessdate=2015-09-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150927040950/https://www.youtube.com/user/vsauce/videos|archive-date=2015-09-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>。2017年，他創建並主演[[YouTube_Premium|YouTube Red]]系列《{{link-en|心靈場|Mind Field}}》<ref name="tubefield"/>，同時也與《[[流言終結者|流言終結者]]》前共同主持人[[亞當·薩維奇|亞當·薩維奇]]一起舉辦名叫「Brain Candy Live!」的教學迴演<ref name="savagecandy"/>。

== 早年生活與教育 ==
史蒂文斯在1986年1月23日出生於[[密蘇里州|密蘇里州]][[堪薩斯城_(密蘇里州)|堪薩斯城]]<ref name="birthday">{{cite web|url=http://www.todaybirthdays.com/famous/michael-stevens.html|title=MICHAEL STEVENS: WEB VIDEO STAR|publisher=TodayBirthdays|author=|date=|accessdate=2016-09-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161026080344/http://www.todaybirthdays.com/famous/michael-stevens.html|archive-date=2016-10-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>，其母親是一位[[助教|助教]]，父親則為[[化學工程師|化學工程師]]，他們全家之後在他5歲時移居至堪薩斯城近郊的{{link-en|史迪威 (密蘇里州)|Stilwell, Missouri|史迪威}}。史蒂文斯長大後就讀於[[歐弗蘭帕克|歐弗蘭帕克]]的{{link-en|藍谷高中|Blue Valley High School}}，他在那裡育成了幽默風趣的性格和對知識的熱情，同時還加入了演講社和戲劇社<ref name="storybio"/>。從藍谷高中畢業後，史蒂文斯進入[[芝加哥大學|芝加哥大學]]修讀心理學與英語文學<ref name="tvfestival">{{cite web |url=http://www.thetvfestival.com/speaker/michael-stevens/ |title=MICHAEL STEVENS A.K.A VSAUCE |publisher=Edinburgh International Television Festival |date=2015 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170929000550/http://www.thetvfestival.com/speaker/michael-stevens/ |archive-date=2017-09-29 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>，在那時候他觀看了一條經過重新剪輯的《[[鬼店|鬼店]]》預告片，激發起其對影片剪輯的興趣<ref name="sixtysymbols">{{cite web |url=http://www.sixtysymbols.com/videos/vsauce.htm |title=Meet Vsauce |publisher=Sixty Symbols |author=Brady Haran |date=2013-03-02 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160805100231/http://www.sixtysymbols.com/videos/vsauce.htm |archive-date=2016-08-05 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="shining">{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sfout_rgPSA&feature=iv&src_vid=MofGXVroQ3g&annotation_id=annotation_1849958363 |title=Shining |publisher=YouTube |author=Robobos|date=2006-02-27 |accessdate=2015-09-28}}</ref>。

== 生涯 ==
=== YouTube影片剪輯（2007–2010） ===
在化名為「pooplicker888」的情況下，史蒂文斯在2007年上傳其首部影片，其中某些片段亦被{{link-en|CollegeHumor|CollegeHumor}}和{{link-en|Funny or Die|Funny or Die}}轉載<ref name="beforetheywerefamous">{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vcc83ahvHWw |title=V SAUCE – Before They Were Famous – Michael Stevens |publisher=YouTube |author=Michael McCrudden |date=2016-05-05 |accessdate=2016-10-20 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170818044400/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vcc83ahvHWw |archive-date=2017-08-18 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。同年，他以「CamPain 2008」的名義發佈由他自己配音並配上圖片，以幽默的風格介紹[[2008年美國總統選舉|2008年美國總統選舉]]候選人的短片<ref name="teneighty">{{cite web |url=http://teneightymagazine.com/2015/06/07/michael-stevens-spilling-the-sauce/ |title=MICHAEL STEVENS: SPILLING THE SAUCE |publisher=TenEighty Magazine |author= |date=2015-06-07 |accessdate=2015-09-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150711162209/http://teneightymagazine.com/2015/06/07/michael-stevens-spilling-the-sauce/ |archive-date=2015-07-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

史蒂文斯的影片引起了{{link-en|本·雷爾斯|Ben Relles}}的興趣，雷爾斯邀請他成為當時名叫{{link-en|The Key Of Awesome|The Key Of Awesome|Barely Political}}的網上喜劇小組的成員。2008年，史蒂文斯移居至紐約，在Barely Political和{{link-en|Next New Networks|Next New Networks}}的旗下工作，並得以與馬克·道格拉斯（Mark Douglas）、{{link-en|托德·沃馬克|Todd Womack}}、{{link-en|安德烈婭·費茨科|Andrea Feczko}}和{{link-en|安珀·李·埃丁格|Amber Lee Ettinger}}等喜劇演員合作<ref name="footofaferret"/>。史蒂文斯還為道格拉斯的《令人驚訝的關鍵》（''The Key of Awesome''）節目系列執導了一個[[貓頭鷹城市|貓頭鷹城市]]的{{link-en|模仿音樂|Parody music|模仿}}[[音樂影片|音樂影片]]<ref name="storybio"/><ref name="electronicwuss">{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RyasBBoQqCA |title=ELECTRONIC WUSS: Owl City Parody – Key Of Awesome #20 |publisher=YouTube |author=Barely Political |date=2010-05-14 |accessdate=2015-09-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151016081447/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RyasBBoQqCA |archive-date=2015-10-16 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

=== 早年「Vsauce」（2010–2012） ===
[[File:Michael_Stevens_WhiteHouse_Full.png|thumb]]和一眾YouTuber在2014年出席於[[白宮|白宮]]舉行的{{link-en|HealthCare.gov|HealthCare.gov}}會議]]
{{Main article|Vsauce}}
史蒂文斯於2010年創立「Vsauce」<ref name="adweek">{{cite web |url=http://www.adweek.com/news-gallery/technology/10-viral-content-creators-who-electrify-fans-millions-165991 |title=10 Viral Content Creators Who Electrify Fans by the Millions |publisher=Adweek |author=David Gianatasio |date=2015-07-19 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160821202740/http://www.adweek.com/news-gallery/technology/10-viral-content-creators-who-electrify-fans-millions-165991 |archive-date=2016-08-21 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，最初頻道上出現許多的合作者，題材亦主要集中在{{link-en|電玩文化|Video game culture}}上。之後「Vsauce」出現多個系列，且大多都是由史蒂文斯親自主持，包括《V-LIST》（電子遊戲相關列表）、《IMG》（[[網路爆紅|網路爆紅]]數位影像）、《DONG》（展示各種線上遊戲和工具）以及《LÜT》（展示可在[[網路購物|網上買到]]的有趣事物）。他在每一部影片的開場白為：「嘿，『Vsauce』！這裡是麥可」<ref name="footofaferret">{{cite AV media |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h6uRey7aJe4 |title=A Brief History of Vsauce |publisher=YouTube |author=FootofaFerret |date=2013-10-26 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170717164922/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h6uRey7aJe4 |archive-date=2017-07-17 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

然而，史蒂文斯的[[教育技術學|教育影片]]才是最受關注的系列。他本人表示是受到[[保羅·扎盧姆|保羅·扎盧姆]]的《[[怪頭博士|怪頭博士]]》啟發才開始拍攝科教類的影片<ref name="redditinterview">{{cite AV media |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5hClQ-OER3Y |title=IAMA: Michael Stevens of Vsauce |publisher=YouTube |author=Reddit |date=2014-04-29 |accessdate=2015-09-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150706025221/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5hClQ-OER3Y |archive-date=2015-07-06 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。史蒂文斯意識意識到他最受歡迎的內容往往是較為嚴肅的現實概念，且常是[[科際整合|科際整合]]。其中最著名的包括《眼睛的[[光學解析度|解析度]]有多少？》、《黑暗的[[光速|速度]]有多少？》<ref name="digiday">{{cite web |url=http://digiday.com/publishers/secret-sauce-behind-youtube-giant-vsauce/ |title=The secret sauce behind YouTube giant Vsauce |publisher=''Digiday'' |author=Eric Blattberg |date=2014-12-04 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160918000000/http://digiday.com/publishers/secret-sauce-behind-youtube-giant-vsauce/ |archive-date=2016-09-18 |dead-url=no }}</ref>；《為什麼你的屁股會在中間？》和《世界上到底有多少錢？》<ref name="cmc">{{cite web |url=http://www.thechildrensmediaconference.com/blog/2015/07/02/opening-keynote-michael-stevens-all-change-report/ |title=Opening Keynote, Michael Stevens: All Change? – Report |publisher=The Children's Media Conference |author=Simon Bor |date=2015-07-02 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161012224458/http://www.thechildrensmediaconference.com/blog/2015/07/02/opening-keynote-michael-stevens-all-change-report/ |archive-date=2016-10-12 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

2010年下旬，史蒂文斯開設了兩個名叫「Vsauce2」和「Vsauce3」的相關頻道，它們之後亦分別交由凱文·利伯（Kevin Lieber）和{{link-en|傑克·羅珀|Jake Roper}}。到了2011–12年，大部分關於[[網路文化|網路]]和電玩文化的內容都交予這兩個頻道負責，只留下教學討論的題材給「Vsauce」<ref name="storybio"/>。史蒂文斯的大部分影片標題都是一條問題，他會詳細回答或討論該話題，並涵蓋任何吸引觀眾興趣的教育相關領域的離題內容<ref name="storybio"/>。

|publisher=Veritasium |author=Derek Muller |date=2014-07-16 |accessdate=2015-09-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161012080009/http://veritasium.com/education/what-is-not-random/ |archive-date=2016-10-12 |dead-url=no }}</ref>、{{link-en|傑克·霍納|Jack Horner (paleontologist)}}與[[克里斯·普瑞特|克里斯·普瑞特]]（討論[[恐龍|恐龍學]]與《[[侏羅紀世界|侏羅紀世界]]》）<ref name="jurassic">{{cite web |url=http://www.cnet.com/au/news/chris-pratt-jack-horner-crack-dinosaur-mysteries-in-vsauce-video/ |title=Chris Pratt and paleontologist Jack Horner crack dino mysteries |publisher=CNET |author=Anthony Domanico |date=2015-06-19 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161012182809/https://www.cnet.com/au/news/chris-pratt-jack-horner-crack-dinosaur-mysteries-in-vsauce-video/ |archive-date=2016-10-12 |dead-url=no }}</ref>以及[[戴維·阿滕伯勒|戴維·阿滕伯勒]]（討論《[[行星地球2|行星地球2]]》）<ref name="laughingsquid">{{cite web |url=http://laughingsquid.com/michael-stevens-talks-with-sir-david-attenborough-about-storytelling-animals-and-technology/ |title=Michael Stevens Talks With Sir David Attenborough About Storytelling, Animals and Technology |publisher=Laughing Squid |author=Lori Dorn |date=2016-11-09 |accessdate=2016-11-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161114002207/http://laughingsquid.com/michael-stevens-talks-with-sir-david-attenborough-about-storytelling-animals-and-technology/ |archive-date=2016-11-14 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

===「Brain Candy Live」和《心靈場》（2016–現在）===
2016年8月，《[[流言終結者|流言終結者]]》的前共同主持人[[亞當·薩維奇|亞當·薩維奇]]表示會邀請史蒂文斯當2017年全國性教育迴演的嘉賓<ref name="savage">{{cite web |url=https://variety.com/2016/tv/awards/mythbusters-adam-savage-jamie-hyneman-tested-vsauce-1201838798/ |title=‘Mythbusters’ May Be Ending, But Star Adam Savage Sees New Beginnings |work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |author=Paula Hendrickson |date=2016-08-17 |accessdate=2016-09-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160902025054/http://variety.com/2016/tv/awards/mythbusters-adam-savage-jamie-hyneman-tested-vsauce-1201838798/ |archive-date=2016-09-02 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。該年的晚些時候，後者在「Vsauce」上發佈一條影片，宣佈將與前者一起到四十個美國城市作2017年初的「Brain Candy Live」的公開演出。該系列的迴演亦被形容是一個「介乎於TED演講和{{link-en|藍人樂團|Blue Man Group}}之間」的現場科教舞台秀<ref name="savagecandy">{{cite web |url=http://nerdist.com/adam-savage-talks-up-new-science-tour-brain-candy-live/ |title=ADAM SAVAGE TALKS UP NEW SCIENCE TOUR BRAIN CANDY LIVE! |work=Nerdist News |author=Blake Rodgers |date=2017-01-08 |accessdate=2017-01-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170129121243/http://nerdist.com/adam-savage-talks-up-new-science-tour-brain-candy-live/ |archive-date=2017-01-29 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="chicagocandy">{{cite web |url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/theater/news/ct-brain-candy-live-coming-to-chicago-20161024-story.html |title='Brain Candy Live!' coming to Chicago |work=[[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]] |author=Morgan Greene |date=2016-10-24 |accessdate=2017-01-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170202011919/http://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/theater/news/ct-brain-candy-live-coming-to-chicago-20161024-story.html |archive-date=2017-02-02 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。而另一次的美國巡演則在2018年的3月至5月間舉行<ref>{{cite web|title=Don't miss: 'Brain Candy Live' with Adam Savage (Tested, Mythbusters) & Michael Stevens (Vsauce) / Boing Boing|url=https://boingboing.net/2017/11/02/adam-savage-brain-candy.html|website=boingboing.net|accessdate=2017-11-05|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171104024403/https://boingboing.net/2017/11/02/adam-savage-brain-candy.html|archive-date=2017-11-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

史蒂文斯與[[YouTube_Premium|YouTube Red]]合作，創建並主持於2017年1月在「Vsauce」頻道上首映的科教節目《{{link-en|心靈場|Mind Field}}》。其每一集都會探討不同層面的[[人的行為|人類行為]]，並會聽取例如[[多米尼克·莫納漢|多米尼克·莫納漢]]等嘉賓的意見之後進行實驗<ref name="billboardred">{{cite web |url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/7416521/youtube-mobile-live-streaming-new-original-series |title=YouTube Announces Mobile Live Streaming, New Original Series |work=''[[告示牌_(雜誌)|Billboard]]'' |author=Dan Rys |date=2016-06-24 |accessdate=2017-01-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170202071312/http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/7416521/youtube-mobile-live-streaming-new-original-series |archive-date=2017-02-02 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="tubefield">{{cite web |url=http://www.tubefilter.com/2017/01/12/vsauce-trailer-youtube-red-mind-field/ |title=Vsauce Drops Trailer For YouTube Red Series ‘Mind Field’, Premiering Jan. 18 |work=Tubefilter |author=Geoff Weiss |date=2017-01-12 |accessdate=2017-01-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170120191419/http://www.tubefilter.com/2017/01/12/vsauce-trailer-youtube-red-mind-field/ |archive-date=2017-01-20 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。史蒂文斯自已表示曾經向多個[[電視聯播網|電視聯播網]]推銷《心靈場》，但都被他們回絕<ref>{{cite web|last1=Oravecz|first1=Nora|title=How Vsauce’s Michael Stevens Disruptive Pitch Finally Got Approved, and Led Him to His Top Series, Mind Field|url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/how-vsauces-michael-stevens-disruptive-pitch-finally_us_5a3428cce4b0e1b4472ae636|website=Huffington Post|accessdate=2017-12-22|date=2017-12-15}}</ref>。

== 個人生活 ==
史蒂文斯在2012年搬到英國倫敦居住<ref name="storybio"/>。2016年，他與妻子瑪妮（Marnie）結婚，並搬回到美國[[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]定居，那裡同時也住著「Vsauce3」的持有人{{link-en|傑克·羅珀|Jake Roper}}<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BAkT83rtCHw/ |title=Michael Stevens from Vsauce on Instagram |date=2016-01-15 |accessdate=2016-10-21 |quote=I've moved to LA!

'''馬修·肯特·歐森'''（{{lang-en|Matthew Kent Olson}}，{{bd|1994年|3月29日|}}），為[[美國職棒大聯盟|美國職棒大聯盟]][[奧克蘭運動家|奧克蘭運動家]]的[[一壘手|一壘手]]。

==職業生涯==
[[2012年美國職棒大聯盟選秀|2012年美國職棒大聯盟選秀]]，運動家隊在第1輪47順位選進了歐森。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://oakland.athletics.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20120604&content_id=32768792&vkey=news_oak&c_id=oak|title=Comp picks yield value in prep ranks for A's|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://patch.com/georgia/lilburn/parkview-s-matt-olson-picked-by-the-a-s|title=Parkview's Matt Olson Picked by the A's|date=5 June 2012|website=Lilburn-Mountain Park, GA Patch}}</ref>他放棄就讀范德堡大學，選擇與球隊簽約。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tennessean.com/story/sports/baseball/2016/07/28/one-time-vanderbilt-commitment-matt-olson-track-nashville-sounds/87568646/|title=One-time Vanderbilt commitment Matt Olson on track with Sounds|website=The Tennessean}}</ref>

2012年，他在亞利桑那秋季聯盟出賽50場，打擊率2成82，9轟45分打點。2013年，他在1A出賽134場，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gazettextra.com/archives/athletics-no-overall-prospect-brings-big-bat-to-beloit-lineup/article_311c1397-392e-5a12-9b90-8e376b9a75a4.html|title=Athletics' No. 5 overall prospect brings big bat to Beloit lineup|first=John|last=Barry|website=GazetteXtra}}</ref>打擊率2成25，23轟93分打點。

2014年，他在高階1A出賽138場，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gwinnettdailypost.com/archive/minor-league-notes-parkview-grad-matt-olson-breaks-out/article_2f3f913a-169f-549f-82cb-3a395cec2d9d.html|title=MINOR LEAGUE NOTES: Parkview grad Matt Olson breaks out|first=Guy|last=Curtright|website=Gwinnett Daily Post}}</ref>打擊率2成62，37轟97分打點。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fangraphs.com/statss.aspx?playerid=14344&position=1B|title=Matt Olson » Statistics » Batting - FanGraphs Baseball|website=www.fangraphs.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.recordnet.com/article/20140804/A_NEWS/140809954|title=Ports' Olson in contention to win home run award|first=The|last=Record|website=recordnet.com}}</ref>2015年他在2A出賽133場，打擊率2成49，17轟75分打點。2016年，他在3A出賽131場，打擊率2成35，17轟60分打點。

2016年9月12日，歐森登上大聯盟。<ref>{{cite news|last=Slusser|first=usan|title=A's call up prospects Matt Olson, Renato Nunez|url=http://www.sfgate.com/athletics/article/A-s-call-up-prospects-Matt-Olson-Renato-Nunez-9217388.php|accessdate=September 12, 2016|publisher=San Francisco Gate|date=September 12, 2016}}</ref>2017年，他在3A出賽79場，打擊率2成72，23轟60分打點。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.milb.com/player/index.jsp?player_id=621566#/career/R/hitting/2018/ALL|title=Matt Olson Stats, Highlights, Bio - MiLB.com Stats - The Official Site of Minor League Baseball |publisher=MiLB.com |accessdate=June 22, 2018 }}</ref>該年他也在運動家隊出賽59場，打擊率2成59，24轟45分打點。他在9月擊出13轟為大聯盟菜鳥紀錄，其中還包含連續5場開轟。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sportingnews.com/us/mlb/news/matt-olson-oakland-athletics-career-home-runs-sanchez-bellinger-mcgwire-berger-65-games/3maejujnn41o12yi1lc2wzsux|title=A's Matt Olson makes up for lost time with home run binge|date=19 September 2017|website=Sporting News}}</ref>

2018年4月18日，歐森擊出生涯首支再見安。9月26日擊出首支滿貫砲。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/matt-olson-hits-1st-career-grand-slam-in-win/c-296237112|title=Matt Olson hits 1st career grand slam in win|website=MLB.com}}</ref>該年他打擊率2成47，29轟84分打點。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pressreader.com/usa/the-news-times/20181002/281848644540999|title=PressReader.com - Connecting People Through News|website=www.pressreader.com}}</ref>他還贏得了生涯首座金手套。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nbcsports.com/bayarea/athletics/matt-chapman-matt-olson-win-first-career-gold-glove-awards|title=Golden Corners: Chapman, Olson win first Gold Glove awards|date=4 November 2018|website=NBCS Bay Area}}</ref>

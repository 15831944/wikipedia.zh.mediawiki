== 背景 ==
姆南加古瓦政府於2017年[[2017年津巴布韦政变|上台]]後，為令奄奄一息的經濟重現生氣，政府公佈一系列緊縮政策並著力改善國內的財政狀況<ref name="indzw">{{Cite web|url=https://www.theindependent.co.zw/2017/11/24/mnangagwa-plans-major-policy-shift/|title=Mnangagwa plans major policy shift|accessdate=15 January 2019|date=24 November 2017|last=Elias|first=Mambo|language=en-US}}</ref>。2018年10月，外幣短缺令國內多間商店倒閉，同時令進口商品出現短缺，包括燃油<ref name="iolOct2018">{{Cite web|url=https://www.iol.co.za/news/africa/shops-shut-doors-as-zimbabwe-financial-crisis-deepens-17414862|title=Shops shut doors as Zimbabwe financial crisis deepens|accessdate=15 January 2019|date=9 October 2018|language=en}}</ref>。最終燃油長期短缺，公務員罷工，甚至令國內經濟狀況更加差<ref name="times">{{Cite web|url=https://www.timeslive.co.za/news/africa/2019-01-12-fuel-price-shock-zims-petrol-price-to-more-than-double/|title=Fuel price shock! Zim's petrol price to more than double|accessdate=15 January 2019|date=12 January 2019|last=Ndlovu|first=Ray|language=en}}</ref><ref name="bbc">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-46865007|title=Where you wait seven hours and still get no fuel|accessdate=15 January 2019|date=15 January 2019|language=en-GB}}</ref>。

2019年1月12日，姆南加古瓦政府宣布津巴布韋能源監管局將會將燃油價格增加一倍，翌日凌晨開始生效<ref name="def">{{Cite web|url=http://www.defenceweb.co.za/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=54203:protests-follow-massive-zimbabwe-fuel-price-hike&Itemid=113|title=Protests follow massive Zimbabwe fuel price hike|accessdate=15 January 2019}}</ref>。每公升[[柴油|柴油]]由1.38美元（折合約10.82港元）調升至3.11美元（24.39港元）、每公升[[汽油|汽油]]由1.43美元（11.22港元）調升至3.31美元（25.96港元），調升幅度接近130%，年津巴布韋成為世界上燃油售價最高的國家<ref name="msn">{{Cite web|url=https://www.msn.com/en-us/news/world/zimbabwe-military-quells-fuel-price-hike-protests-8-killed/ar-BBSgxXS|title=Zimbabwe military quells fuel price protests; several deaths|accessdate=15 January 2019|date=15 January 2019|language=en-US}}</ref>。姆南加古瓦指出加價是有必要的，用以阻止非法交易和減輕燃油短缺的問題<ref name="iolZM">{{Cite web|url=https://www.iol.co.za/news/africa/zimbabwe-police-fire-tear-gas-as-fuel-price-hike-protest-continues-18822841|title=Zimbabwe police fire tear gas as fuel price hike protest continues|accessdate=15 January 2019|language=en}}</ref>。 津巴布韋工會聯盟大會呼籲舉行一連三日的大罷工抗議有關決定。

== 示威 ==
首輪示威於2019年1月13日在[[哈拉雷|哈拉雷]]開始，警方和軍方隨後進行聯合行動，包括搜查部分市民的住所<ref name="yahoo">{{Cite web|url=https://news.yahoo.com/5-killed-zimbabwe-fuel-hike-protests-activists-083826020.html|title=Zimbabwe military quells fuel price hike protests; 8 killed|accessdate=15 January 2019|language=en-US}}</ref>。首都以外的部分城市（譬如[[穆塔雷|穆塔雷]]和[[布拉瓦约|布拉瓦约]]）也爆發零星示威<ref name="bbc2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-46876397|title=Zimbabwe opposition office torched|accessdate=15 January 2019|date=15 January 2019|language=en-GB}}</ref>。當晚，政府公佈3人在示威中死亡，包括1名警察<ref name="busLiveP">{{Cite web|url=https://www.businesslive.co.za/bd/world/africa/2019-01-15-panicky-zimbabwe-government-shuts-down-internet/|title=Panicky Zimbabwe government shuts down internet|accessdate=15 January 2019|date=15 January 2019|last=Smaita|first=Kevin|language=en}}</ref>。非政府組織的消息指出當日有大約200人被捕，另外8人被警方槍殺。

津巴布韋人權醫生協會指出172人受傷，其中68人受槍傷<ref name="washingtonP">{{Cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/africa/the-latest-soldiers-hungry-zimbabweans-in-the-streets/2019/01/16/f6e3e0fe-1987-11e9-b8e6-567190c2fd08_story.html|title=The Latest: Zimbabwe doctor group reports 68 gunshot wounds|accessdate=17 January 2019|date=16 January 2019|language=en}}</ref>。報道指警方也不分青紅皂白的襲擊示威者和途人<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://citizen.co.za/news/news-africa/2063751/watch-looting-of-zimbabwe-shops-ahead-of-crackdown/|title=WATCH: Looting of Zimbabwe shops ahead of crackdown|accessdate=16 January 2019|last=AFP|language=en}}</ref>，部分示威者則在哈拉雷、布拉瓦约<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://citizen.co.za/news/news-africa/2062980/watch-looting-and-chaos-in-zimbabwe-as-angry-protesters-react-to-massive-fuel-hike/|title=WATCH: Looting and chaos in Zimbabwe as angry protesters react to massive fuel hike|accessdate=16 January 2019|last=Auntony|first=AFP / Fanuel Jongwe / Zinyange|language=en}}</ref>和[[卡多馬|卡多馬]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.moneyweb.co.za/news-fast-news/zimbabwe-strike-enters-third-day-as-excessive-force-criticised/|title=Zimbabwe strike enters third day as ‘excessive force’ criticised|accessdate=16 January 2019|date=16 January 2019|last=January 2019|first=Mike Schüssler / 16}}</ref>掠夺商舖。1月18日，人權醫生協會指出示威中出現844宗侵犯人權的案件，包括78人被警方槍傷、466人被任意拘捕、242宗襲擊或侮辱示威者的案件<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mg.co.za/article/2019-01-19-revolt-and-repression-in-zimbabwe/|title=Revolt and repression in Zimbabwe|accessdate=2019-01-19|date=19 January 2019|last=Crisis Group|language=en}}</ref>。

示威期間，政府[[封鎖_(網路)|封鎖]]多個社交媒體，包括[[WhatsApp|WhatsApp]]、[[Facebook|Facebook]]和[[Twitter|Twitter]]，示威者隨後轉用[[虛擬私人網路|VPN]]上網獲取有關示威的新聞，逼使政府封鎖整個互聯網<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.itnewsafrica.com/2019/01/zimbabwe-kills-internet-over-protests/|title=Zimbabwe kills its internet over protests|accessdate=16 January 2019|date=16 January 2019|publisher=IT News Africa}}</ref>。津巴布韋的最大網絡供應商Econet證實政府下令示威期間直接封鎖所有連接互聯網的途徑<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://allafrica.com/stories/201901160010.html|title=Zimbabwe: Econet Confirms Govt Directive to Block Internet - Masiyiwa Says Opposition Could Have Repealed Law During GNU|last=Bureau|first=U. K.|date=16 January 2019|work=New Zimbabwe (London)|accessdate=16 January 2019}}</ref>。

示威第三日，社運活躍分子的牧師馬愛歡（Evan Mawarire）被捕，其律師指出政府以馬愛歡在推特煽動暴力抗議活動為由將其拘捕。[[争取民主变革运动－茨万吉拉伊|民革运]]表示黨領袖也在示威期間被安全部門扣留<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.news24.com/Africa/Zimbabwe/zimbabwe-police-arrest-prominent-govt-critic-pastor-mawarire-20190116|title=PICS: Zimbabwe police arrest prominent govt critic Pastor Mawarire|accessdate=16 January 2019|date=16 January 2019|language=en}}</ref>。清場期間，據報數人也被安全部門人員綁架，確實數字暫不清楚<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2019/jan/16/authorities-launch-major-crackdown-amid-protests-zimbabwe|title=Civilians beaten and abducted in major Zimbabwe crackdown|last=correspondent|first=Jason Burke Africa|date=2019-01-16|work=The Guardian|accessdate=2019-01-18|language=en-GB|issn=0261-3077}}</ref>。

政府將是次示威的責任歸咎於民革運<ref name="ewn1">{{Cite web|url=https://ewn.co.za/2019/01/15/zimbabwe-fuel-protests-turn-deadly|title=Zimbabwe fuel protests turn deadly|accessdate=15 January 2019|last=AFP|language=en}}</ref>。民革運的辦公室遇襲並起火，民革運指責為[[辛巴威非洲民族聯盟－愛國陣線|愛國陣線]]的支持者所為<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://allafrica.com/stories/201901150073.html|title=Zimbabwe: MDC's Harvest House HQ Bombed - Lawyers Say Soldiers in Midnight Raids Against Civilians|last=Bureau|first=U. K.|date=15 January 2019|work=New Zimbabwe (London)|accessdate=15 January 2019}}</ref>。政府指出多項政府資產和愛國陣線的資產也遭到破壞，批評是民革運支持者所為<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://allafrica.com/stories/201901150060.html|title=Zimbabwe: Updated - MDC Violence Leaves Trail of Destruction|date=15 January 2019|work=The Herald (Harare)|accessdate=15 January 2019}}</ref>，政府又批評示威由反對派組織從中協調，形容示威有如[[恐怖活动|恐怖襲擊]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.latimes.com/world/la-fg-zimbabwe-crackdown-20190116-story.html|title=In harsh crackdown, Zimbabwe police arrest scores as protests grow|accessdate=2019-01-17|last=Press|first=Associated}}</ref>。民革運否認有關指控，但重申政府暴力對待示威者和平民<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dw.com/en/zimbabwe-opposition-we-are-not-to-blame-for-the-violence/a-47111040|title=Zimbabwe opposition: We are not to blame for the violence|accessdate=17 January 2019|date=16 January 2019|last=Welle (www.dw.com)|first=Deutsche|language=en-GB}}</ref>。

1月17日，哈拉雷的示威持續，導致在津巴布韋舉行的{{Le|2018至2019年度洛根杯|2018–19 Logan Cup}}被逼取消部分賽事<ref name="Logan">{{Cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/story/_/id/25784412/logan-cup-games-called-due-zimbabwe-unrest|title=Logan Cup games called off due to Zimbabwe unrest|accessdate=17 January 2019|work=ESPN Cricinfo}}</ref>。

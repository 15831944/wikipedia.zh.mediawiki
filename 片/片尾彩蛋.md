'''片尾彩蛋'''，指的是[[電影|電影]]或[[電視劇|電視劇]]在正片結束後增加的額外片段，大多在{{link-en|片尾字幕|Closing credits}}的中間或末尾出現。這一中文名稱中的「彩蛋」二字源於[[復活節|復活節]][[復活節彩蛋|尋找彩蛋的習俗]]<ref>{{cite web | url = http://yule.sohu.com/20110517/n307645967.shtml | title = 电影局否认删掉片尾“彩蛋” 称国内观众没耐心 | work = 新京報 | author = 孙琳琳 | date = 2011-05-17 | accessdate = 2018-09-04 | language = zh-cn}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://news.mtime.com/2013/01/28/1505788.html | title = "片尾彩蛋"频现国产电影 明星客串成宣传噱头 | work = 文匯報 | author = 李婷 | date = 2013-01-28 | accessdate = 2018-09-04 | language = zh-cn | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20130611005547/http://news.mtime.com/2013/01/28/1505788.html | archive-date = 2013-06-11 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，不過英文中通常只將這樣的片段稱為「字幕後片段」（{{lang|en|post-credits scene}}）<ref name=bustle>{{cite web | url = https://www.bustle.com/p/when-did-post-credits-scenes-start-the-trends-history-is-filled-with-twists-turns-3235313 | title = When Did Post-Credits Scenes Start? The Trend's History Is Filled With Twists & Turns | work = Bustle | first = Johnny | last = Brayson | date = 2017-11-14 | accessdate = 2018-09-04 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180101185533/https://www.bustle.com/p/when-did-post-credits-scenes-start-the-trends-history-is-filled-with-twists-turns-3235313 | archive-date = 2018-01-01 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，而「[[彩蛋_(媒體)|彩蛋]]」（{{lang|en|Easter egg}}）一詞則更常被用於指代影視作品中不易被注意到，但卻有特殊含義的細節<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/10/17/easter-eggs-movies_n_5985844.html | title = 11 Easter Eggs You Never Noticed In Your Favorite Movies | work = HuffPost | first = Todd | last = Van Luling | date = 2014-10-17 | accessdate = 2018-09-04 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180827072505/https://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/10/17/easter-eggs-movies_n_5985844.html | archive-date = 2018-08-27 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。片尾彩蛋通常是幽默性質的片段，也經常被用作[[續集|續集]]等相關影片的先導預告。

== 歷史 ==
20世紀60年代末之前，電影的字幕通常都{{link-en|片頭字幕|Opening credits|置於片頭}}。在60年代末至70年代初，隨著電影字幕所列出的工作人員數目與日俱增，片尾字幕開始成為常態。<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.nytimes.com/2004/01/11/us/who-was-that-food-stylist-film-credits-roll-on.html | title = Who Was That Food Stylist? Film Credits Roll On | work = The New York Times | first = Randy | last = Kennedy | date = 2004-01-11 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180213204717/http://www.nytimes.com/2004/01/11/us/who-was-that-food-stylist-film-credits-roll-on.html | archive-date = 2018-02-13 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref name=lwlies>{{cite web | url = https://lwlies.com/articles/is-it-time-to-do-away-with-post-credits-scenes/ | title = Is it time to do away with post-credits scenes? | work = Little White Lies | first = James | last = Luxford | date = 2018-02-13 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180216183209/http://lwlies.com/articles/is-it-time-to-do-away-with-post-credits-scenes | archive-date = 2018-02-16 | dead-url = no }}</ref>現代意義上的片尾彩蛋最早可以追溯到1966年的間諜題材喜劇片《{{link-en|超級情報員|The Silencers (film)|-{zh-tw:超級情報員;zh-hk:風流特務;zh-cn:神秘追魂枪;}-}}》，該片在片尾用戲謔性的片段預告了影片的續集《{{link-en|超級情報員麥漢續集|Murderers' Row (film)}}》。但真正讓片尾彩蛋成為電影業潮流的是1979年的《{{link-en|大青蛙布偶電影|The Muppet Movie}}》：本片的片尾字幕結束後，一隻布偶面向攝影機鏡頭，以打破[[第四面牆|第四面牆]]的形式向銀幕前的觀眾大喊「回家吧」。在此之後，80年代和90年代的好萊塢電影大量使用了片尾彩蛋。<ref name = bustle/><ref name=vulture>{{cite web | url = http://www.vulture.com/2014/04/brief-history-of-movie-post-credits-sequences.html | title = ‘You’re Still Here?’: A Brief History of the Movie Post-Credits Sequence | work = Vulture | first = Alex | last = Suskind | date = 2014-04-08 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180803163537/http://www.vulture.com/2014/04/brief-history-of-movie-post-credits-sequences.html | archive-date = 2018-08-03 | dead-url = no }}</ref>

21世紀初，包括《[[史瑞克系列電影|史瑞克]]》、《[[星球大戰|星球大戰]]》、《[[神鬼奇航|神鬼奇航]]》、《[[哈利波特系列電影|哈利·波特]]》等在內的大製作系列電影開始用片尾彩蛋來對續集進行預告<ref name = vulture/>。2008年，[[漫威影業|漫威影業]]在其[[漫威電影宇宙|電影宇宙]]的首部作品《[[鋼鐵人_(電影)|鋼鐵人]]》的結尾加入了一個彩蛋，預告[[超級英雄|超級英雄]]聯盟「[[復仇者_(漫畫)|復仇者聯盟]]」的組建。自此，片尾彩蛋成為漫威電影宇宙中每部電影的固定傳統，這些片段通常被用來建立起整個系列中各部電影之間的聯繫。<ref == 用途舉例 ==
系列電影的片尾彩蛋經常被用於預告續集等相關電影。例如，漫威電影宇宙中，《[[鋼鐵人_(電影)|鋼鐵人]]》的片尾彩蛋通過[[尼克·福瑞|尼克·福瑞]]與[[鋼鐵人|-{zh-cn:托尼·史塔克;zh-tw:東尼·史塔克;zh-hk:東尼·史達;}-]]的對話預告了《[[復仇者聯盟|復仇者聯盟]]》，《[[鋼鐵人2|鋼鐵人2]]》的彩蛋則以墜落在沙漠中的[[雷神之鎚|雷神之鎚]]預告了《[[雷神_(电影)|雷神索爾]]》<ref name=allmarvel>{{cite web | url = http://collider.com/every-marvel-after-credits-scene-explained/ | title = Every Marvel After-Credits Scene Explained | work = Collider | first = Adam | last = Chitwood | date = 2018-07-12 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180217171232/http://collider.com/every-marvel-after-credits-scene-explained/ | archive-date = 2018-02-17 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。在《[[寒戰|寒戰]]》的結尾，劉傑輝接到電話要求釋放囚犯，而李家俊在囚房裡神態輕鬆地讀書和打響指，預示了《[[寒戰2|寒戰2]]》的到來<ref>{{cite web | url = http://ent.qq.com/a/20160708/041273.htm | title = 告诉我哪里看不懂！给你全面解锁《寒战II》！ | work = 騰訊娛樂 | author = 陈媛 | date = 2016-07-08 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = zh-cn | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20170213003410/http://ent.qq.com/a/20160708/041273.htm | archive-date = 2017-02-13 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://ent.sina.com.cn/m/c/2012-11-23/10263794642.shtml | title = 《寒战》未解悬念引热议 导演回应七宗谜 | work = 新浪娛樂 | date = 2012-11-23 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = zh-cn}}</ref>。

片尾彩蛋有時也會被用於呈現劇情反轉，或是交代正片中沒有敘述完整的情節。《[[玩命關頭5|玩命關頭5]]》的片尾彩蛋不但是其續集的預告，也反轉了之前的情節，告訴觀眾在前作《[[玩命關頭4|玩命關頭4]]》中似乎已經死亡的角色-{zh-cn:莉蒂;zh-tw:莉蒂;zh-hk:莉迪;}-·歐提茲其實還活著<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.bustle.com/p/the-fate-of-the-furious-post-credits-scene-doesnt-exist-but-the-franchise-isnt-ending-51060 | title = 'The Fate Of The Furious' Post-Credits Scene Doesn't Exist, But The Franchise Isn’t Ending | work = Bustle | first = Johnny | last = Brayson | date = 2017-04-13 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en}}</ref>。而在《[[神鬼奇航3：世界的盡頭|神鬼奇航3：世界的盡頭]]》的字幕滾動完畢後，彩蛋片段展現了-{zh-cn:威尔·特纳;zh-hk:鄧維廉;zh-tw:威爾·杜納;}-與愛人-{zh-cn:伊丽莎白·斯旺;zh-hk:史伊莎;zh-tw:伊莉莎白·史旺;}-在正片情節結束十年之後重新相逢的場景<ref>{{cite web | url = https://screenrant.com/best-post-credits-scenes-movies/ | title = 15 Game-Changing Movie Post-Credits Scenes | work = ScreenRant | first = Mike | last = McGranaghan | date = 2016-09-09 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20170408212602/http://screenrant.com/best-post-credits-scenes-movies/ | archive-date = 2017-04-08 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

除去在情節上發揮作用的彩蛋之外，無論在系列電影還是非系列電影中，片尾彩蛋也都常常純粹起到幽默效果。例如，《[[復仇者聯盟|復仇者聯盟]]》的片尾彩蛋就並沒有推動影片情節發展，而是只展示了[[鋼鐵人|鋼鐵人]]、[[美國隊長|美國隊長]]等超級英雄坐在街頭餐廳吃阿拉伯小吃[[沙威瑪|沙威瑪]]的畫面<ref name = allmarvel/>；《[[決殺令|決殺令]]》片尾有一個時長僅幾秒鐘的彩蛋，其中三名坐在囚籠裡的黑奴驚異而困惑地望著-{zh-cn:姜戈;zh-tw:決哥;zh-hk:贊高;}-離去的背影<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.digitalspy.com/movies/feature/g24679/movies-post-credits-scenes-you-didnt-know/?slide=10 | title = 12 movies you didn't know had secret post-credits scenes | work = Digital Spy | first = Hugh | last = Armitage | date = 2018-05-13 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en }}</ref>；《[[春嬌與志明|春嬌與志明]]》的片尾彩蛋則是主演[[余文樂|余文樂]]男扮女裝出演的一支[[MV|MV]]<ref>{{cite web | url = http://yule.sohu.com/20120405/n339754446.shtml | title = 《春娇与志明》片尾藏彩蛋 余文乐反串引热议 | work = 瀟湘晨報 | author = 李芳明 | date = 2012-04-05 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = zh-cn | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20130131000522/http://yule.sohu.com/20120405/n339754446.shtml | archive-date = 2013-01-31 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

== 影響與評價 ==
以[[漫威電影宇宙|漫威電影宇宙]]為代表的系列電影通過片尾彩蛋進行了成功的電影營銷，大幅提升了觀眾對系列中新角色和新電影的期待程度<ref name = lwlies/>。超級英雄電影結尾的彩蛋也因其與[[漫畫書|漫畫書]]連載敘事方式的相似之處而受到漫畫愛好者的歡迎<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.cbr.com/logan-director-wrong-about-post-credits-scenes/ | title = Logan’s James Mangold Is Totally Wrong About Post-Credits Scenes | work = CBR | first = Charles | last = Dean | date = 2018-02-06 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en}}</ref>。有觀點認為，片尾彩蛋可以鼓勵觀眾走進電影院，幫助影院與[[流媒體|流媒體]]平台競爭<ref name = lwlies/>。此外，想要觀看彩蛋的觀眾必須在片尾字幕結束後才離場，因此彩蛋也被認為是表達對演職人員的尊重的一種途徑<ref name=jhsb/>。

然而，在片尾插入彩蛋的做法也受到了一些電影從業者和評論家的批評。執導[[X戰警系列電影|X戰警系列電影]]中《[[羅根_(電影)|-{zh-cn:金刚狼3：殊死一战;zh-hk:盧根;zh-tw:羅根;zh-sg:罗根;}-]]》一片的[[詹姆斯·曼高德|詹姆斯·曼高德]]曾公開表達對片尾彩蛋熱潮的強烈不滿，認為彩蛋傷害了影片含義的完整性，粉飾了某些影片質量的低劣，觀眾對於彩蛋本身的期待和討論也在影片上映前營造了虛假繁榮的氣氛<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.cinemablend.com/news/2305711/why-logans-james-mangold-really-hates-post-credit-scenes | title = Why Logan’s James Mangold Really Hates Post-Credit Scenes | work = Cinema Blend | first = Conner | last = Schwerdtfeger | date = 2018-02-02 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180203042541/https://www.cinemablend.com/news/2305711/why-logans-james-mangold-really-hates-post-credit-scenes | archive-date = 2018-02-03 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。有相似的批評聲音指出，片尾彩蛋不但破壞了影片自身的結尾，而且儘管系列電影的粉絲們可以理解這些片段的含義，彩蛋自身卻大多毫無敘事層面的價值<ref name = guardian/>。批評者認為，添加彩蛋以討粉絲歡心的做法反映了電影片廠的懶惰心理，而通過彩蛋來對正片中的重要情節進行「反轉」則體現了片廠牴觸冒險的保守理念<ref name = guardian/><ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.indiewire.com/2013/03/dear-filmmakers-please-stop-putting-horrible-stingers-in-the-closing-credits-of-your-movies-128258/ | title = Dear Filmmakers: Please Stop Putting Horrible Stingers in the Closing Credits of Your Movies | work = IndieWire | first = Matt | last = Singer | date = 2013-03-19 | accessdate = 2018-09-06 | language = en | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180119022749/http://www.indiewire.com/2013/03/dear-filmmakers-please-stop-putting-horrible-stingers-in-the-closing-credits-of-your-movies-128258/ | archive-date = 2018-01-19 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

'''戴安娜·瑞格'''（{{lang-en|'''Diana Rigg'''}}，{{bd|1938年|7月20日|||catIdx= R瑞格}}）出生于[[英国|英国]][[英格兰|英格兰]][[约克郡|约克郡]]，为英国著名女演员。<ref name="Parkinson2010">{{cite book|last=Parkinson|first=Michael|title=Parky's People|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=UBC8H8ra5qYC&pg=PT316|accessdate=2012-04-12|date=2010-10-14|publisher=Hodder & Stoughton|isbn=978-1-84894-696-5|page=316}}</ref><ref name="DiPaolo2011">{{cite book|last=DiPaolo|first=Marc|title=War, Politics and Superheroes: Ethics and Propaganda in Comics and Film|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=K8uZGWxFtC0C&pg=PA79|accessdate=2012-04-12|date=2011-03-31|publisher=McFarland|isbn=978-0-7864-4718-3|page=79}}</ref>

== 生平 ==
1938年，瑞格出生于[[英国|英国]][[英格兰|英格兰]][[约克郡|约克郡]]，父亲是铁路工程师，在[[英属印度|英属印度]]兴建铁路，儿时她也曾陪着父亲远赴英属印度。<ref name="bbc">[http://www.bbc.co.uk/southyorkshire/sense_of_place/sy_people/diana_rigg.shtml Meet... Dame Diana Rigg], ''BBC South Yorkshire''. accessed on 14 July 2006.</ref>

瑞格在英属印度读过书，能说一口流利的[[印地语|印地语]]，但是她讨厌当地的[[寄宿学校|寄宿学校]]。

瑞格回到英国之后，毕业于[[皇家戏剧艺术学院|皇家戏剧艺术学院]]。<ref name="farndale">{{cite news|author=Nigel Farndale|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/film/3555923/Diana-Rigg-her-story.html|publisher=[[Daily_Telegraph|Daily Telegraph]]|date=2008-08-17|title=Diana Rigg: her story|accessdate=2011-08-20}}</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
1973年，瑞格和一个[[以色列|以色列]][[画家|画家]]门那辰·梅纳结婚，1976年，两人离婚。

1982年，瑞格嫁给男演员阿奇博尔德·斯特灵，1990年，两人离婚，两人育有女儿[[蕾切尔·斯特灵|蕾切尔·斯特灵]]。<ref>{{citation |first=Viv |last=Groskop |date=2010-02-17 |url=http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/theatre/article-23806855-rachael-stirling-is-a-rising-stage-star-and-shes-in-love-with-her-ass.do |accessdate=2011-06-12 |title=Rachael Stirling is a rising stage star – and she's in love with her ass |publisher=''[[London_Evening_Standard|London Evening Standard]]'' |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605071255/http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/theatre/article-23806855-rachael-stirling-is-a-rising-stage-star-and-shes-in-love-with-her-ass.do |archivedate=2011-06-05 }}.</ref>

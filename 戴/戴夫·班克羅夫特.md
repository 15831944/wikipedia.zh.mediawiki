'''大衛·詹姆斯·班克羅夫特'''（{{lang-en|David James Bancroft}}, {{bd|1891年|4月20日|1972年|10月9日| }}），綽號「Beauty」，為美國職棒大聯盟的游擊手及總教練。16年棒球生涯效力於費城人、巨人、勇士與羅賓隊。

班克羅夫特為1921年和1922年巨人隊冠軍班底。1915年與1923年也打過世界大賽。他被認為是一名出色的游擊手及聰明的棒球員，他在1971年由名人堂資深委員會推薦入選名人堂。不過他的入選有受爭議，因為班克羅夫特被認為是經由他的親信推薦而入選的。

==棒球生涯==
===費城費城人===
1915年球季開打前，費城人以5000美金簽下班克羅夫特。1915年，費城人隊史首度闖入世界大賽，不過當時紅襪投手群實力堅強，因此費城人挑戰隊史首冠失利。不過班克羅夫特的打擊火力幫助費城人拿下了首戰勝利。<ref>{{cite news |url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F50E12F73A5C13738DDDA00894D8415B858DF1D3 |title=Phillies Triumph in First Game, 3–1 – Lucky "Breaks" on Muddy Infield Brings Victory to the National Leaguers. Alexander Below Form: Unsteadiness Most of the Time Keeps Him in Trouble and His Work Surprises Fans. Shore Allows Five Hits: Phillies Win in Eighth, When Scott Is Off Base on Barry's Attempt to Make Double Play. Phillies Triumph In The First Game|newspaper=The New York Times|date=October 9, 1915 |accessdate=July 8, 2012}}</ref>費城人在世界大賽全隊打擊率僅1成82，不過班克羅夫特的打擊率高達2成94。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1915_WS.shtml |title=1915 World Series — Boston Red Sox over Philadelphia Phillies (4–1) |work=Baseball-Reference.com |accessdate=July 8, 2012}}</ref>

1916年，他擔任球隊第三棒，但是打擊率卻下滑至2成12為生涯新低。<ref name=phillies1916>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=5JILAAAAIBAJ&sjid=PFQDAAAAIBAJ&pg=3387,2077844&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Phillies All Working Together In Effort To Land Two Pennants|first=William G.|last=Weart|page=10|newspaper=The Evening Independent|date=March 13, 1916|accessdate=July 11, 2012}}</ref>儘管進攻火力降低，但是防守能力依舊不減。該年費城人在國聯排名第二，僅次於羅賓隊。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=tuwbAAAAIBAJ&sjid=SUkEAAAAIBAJ&pg=3165,1443205&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=No Investigation To Be Made Of Charges By M'Graw: Giant Leader Angry|newspaper=The Pittsburgh Press|page=28|date=October 4, 1916|accessdate=July 11, 2012}}</ref><ref name=speed>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=VP0gAAAAIBAJ&sjid=onUFAAAAIBAJ&pg=4829,3169660&dq=dave-bancroft+phillies&hl=en|title=Bancroft's One of Season's Fastest|newspaper=The Day|date=November 30, 1916|accessdate=July 11, 2012}}</ref>

班克羅夫特當時與Gavvy Cravath(1919年的費城人總教練)不和。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=11BbAAAAIBAJ&sjid=PlANAAAAIBAJ&pg=3460,1939063&dq=dave-bancroft+phillies&hl=en|newspaper=Providence News|title=Bancroft Quietly Traded To Giants|page=13|date=June 8, 1920|accessdate=July 11, 2012}}</ref>因此在1919年球季開打前，班克羅夫特要求球隊將他交易至紅人隊，<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=hsgaAAAAIBAJ&sjid=hUkEAAAAIBAJ&pg=3214,5012183&dq=dave-bancroft+phillies&hl=en|newspaper=The Pittsburgh Press|title=Phillies Almost Intact|first=Ralph|last=Davis|date=March 27, 1919|page=36|accessdate=July 11, 2012}}</ref>但是談判破局。1920年，紐約巨人的教練約翰·麥格勞看中班克羅夫特的實力，將他交易過來。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=FA061EFC385E157A93CAA9178DD85F448285F9 |title=Fletcher Traded For Dave Bancroft — McGraw Gives Veteran Infielder for Younger Star of the Philadelphia Club|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=June 8, 1920|accessdate=April 27, 2012}}</ref>
===紐約巨人===
1920年，班克羅夫特單季得到102分，在球隊中排名第二。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1920-batting-leaders.shtml|title=1920 National League Batting Leaders |work=Baseball-Reference.com |accessdate=July 18, 2012}}</ref>1921年6月1日，在面對費城人的一場比賽中，班克羅夫特達成完全打擊。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/mlb/history/rare_feats/index.jsp?feature=hit_for_cycle|title=Rare Feats: Players who have hit for the cycle|work=MLB.com|accessdate=October 10, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.retrosheet.org/boxesetc/1921/B06012NY11921.htm|title=New York Giants 8, Philadelphia Phillies 3 (2)|date=June 1, 1921|work=Retrosheet.org|publisher=Retrosheet, Inc|accessdate=September 6, 2011}}</ref>該年他出賽153場，為國聯第二高。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1921-batting-leaders.shtml |title=1921 National League Batting Leaders |work=Baseball-Reference.com |accessdate=June 29, 2012}}</ref>巨人闖入了世界大賽，然而他在世界大賽的打擊率僅1成52，不過巨人還是以5勝3敗打敗洋基。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1921_WS.shtml |title=1921 World Series — New York Giants over New York Yankees (5–3) |work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=July 8, 2012}}</ref>

1922年，班克羅夫特出賽156場，得到117分，79次保送，單季209支安打。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1922-batting-leaders.shtml |title=1922 National League Batting Leaders |work=Baseball-Reference.com |accessdate=June 29, 2012}}</ref>該季3成21打擊率為生涯最高。巨人連續2年與洋基在世界大賽中對決，巨人四戰橫掃洋基，然而班克羅夫特的打擊率只有2成11。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1922_WS.shtml |title=1922 World Series — New York Giants over New York Yankees (4–0) |work=Baseball-Reference.com |accessdate=July 8, 2012}}</ref>

1923年，他因為腳受傷而缺席整個球季。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=qwVhAAAAIBAJ&sjid=DGQNAAAAIBAJ&pg=1155,258796|title=Travis Jackson Should Prove Good Utility Man For Giants In Series|page=13|newspaper=Providence News|date=September 28, 1923|accessdate=June 28, 2012}}</ref>不過他在該年的世界大賽回歸，他的打擊率僅有.091，<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=ihtlAAAAIBAJ&sjid=4YcNAAAAIBAJ&pg=5428,4806272&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Captain of Giants Sets Up Battling Average of .750; "Dave" Bancroft Swings Into Action and Routs Four Tire Thieves|page=1|newspaper=Edmonton Journal|date=October 20, 1923|accessdate=June 28, 2012}}</ref>洋基以4勝2敗打敗巨人。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1923_WS.shtml |title=1923 World Series — New York Yankees over New York Giants (4–2) |work=Baseball-Reference.com |accessdate=July 8, 2012}}</ref>

===波士頓勇士===
1923年，巨人隊把新秀游擊手[[崔維斯·傑克森|崔維斯·傑克森]]升上大聯盟，班克羅夫特的上場空間也被壓縮。1923年球季結束，麥格勞將班克羅夫特、Bill Cunningham及Casey Stangel交易至勇士隊換來Joe Oeschger和Billy Southworth。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=OLggAAAAIBAJ&sjid=eGkFAAAAIBAJ&pg=2728,3614675&dq=travis-jackson+dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=McGraw Staging Biggest Gamble In Young Jackson: Filling Shoes Left By Classy Dave Bancroft With 20 Year Old Boy|newspaper=The Lewiston Daily Sun|page=6|date=November 22, 1923|accessdate=June 28, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=NyFQAAAAIBAJ&sjid=wgoEAAAAIBAJ&pg=7056,6733731&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Dave Bancroft To Manage Braves In 1924 Race; Stengel, Cunningham Also Go to Hub Team: Giants Get Pitcher Oeschger and Outfielder Southworth in Big deal Consummated on Sunday|newspaper=The Milwaukee Sentinel|date=November 13, 1923|page=10|accessdate=June 28, 2012}}</ref>

1924年到1927年，班克羅夫特擔任球隊的球員兼教練。1924年，他因為闌尾炎而缺席大半個球季。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=4MpPAAAAIBAJ&sjid=iVQDAAAAIBAJ&pg=6160,6112872&dq=dave-bancroft+appendicitis&hl=en|title=Climbing Browns Fall Before Lowly Mackmen|date=August 8, 1924|page=14|newspaper=The Evening Independent|accessdate=June 28, 2012}}</ref>該年勇士的戰績為國聯最後一名。1925年，班克羅夫特全面改組勇士的陣容。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=OKohAAAAIBAJ&sjid=GJoFAAAAIBAJ&pg=5666,4272004&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Braves Much Stronger With New Material: Dave Bancroft Thinks Club Has Real Chance|agency=Associated Press|newspaper=Reading Eagle|page=30|date=March 20, 1925|accessdate=July 18, 2012}}</ref>他把游擊手Bob Smith轉為投手，Smith擔任投手也表現亮眼。<ref name=smith>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=JQhFAAAAIBAJ&sjid=0jEDAAAAIBAJ&pg=1998,1006532&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Detroit Amateur Bouts Have Plenty of Action|agency=Associated Press|newspaper=Ludington Daily News|page=8|date=January 28, 1926|accessdate=July 18, 2012}}</ref>1925年，勇士戰績為國聯第五。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/teams/BSN/1925.shtml|title=1925 Boston Braves Batting, Pitching, & Fielding Statistics|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=October 10, 2012}}</ref>不過勇士的打擊並不是太出色，1926和1927年，球隊戰績都只有第七名。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/teams/BSN/1926.shtml|title=1926 Boston Braves Batting, Pitching, & Fielding Statistics|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=October 10, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/teams/BSN/1927.shtml|title=1927 Boston Braves Batting, Pitching, & Fielding Statistics|work=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=October 10, 2012}}</ref>
===布魯克林羅賓及回歸巨人===
1927年球季結束，班克羅夫特被球隊解雇。被解雇的那天他與布魯克林羅賓隊簽約。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=FRhPAAAAIBAJ&sjid=nkwDAAAAIBAJ&pg=4906,4962685&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Dave Bancroft Is Given Release By Boston Club: Great Shortstop Will Play With Brooklyn During 1928 Campaign|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|page=14|newspaper=[[St._Petersburg_Times|St. Petersburg Times]]|date=October 15, 1927|accessdate=June 28, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=VqdQAAAAIBAJ&sjid=qSEEAAAAIBAJ&pg=6826,3928672&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Release of Bancroft No Surprise to Foster|first=John B.|last=Foster|page=2|newspaper=The Milwaukee Journal|date=October 15, 1927|accessdate=June 28, 2012}}</ref>

1929年，班克羅夫特被羅賓隊釋出。他也回到了巨人隊在麥格勞身旁擔任助理教練。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=TPQaAAAAIBAJ&sjid=NksEAAAAIBAJ&pg=6395,4291766&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Dave Bancroft Assistant to McGraw Now|agency=United Press International|newspaper=The Pittsburgh Press|page=33|date=November 5, 1929|accessdate=July 18, 2012}}</ref>1930年，他卸下球員身分，轉為教練的身分輔助年邁的麥格勞。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=fFgaAAAAIBAJ&sjid=6AwEAAAAIBAJ&pg=4744,3426435&dq=dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=Cards Beat Giants, Bancroft Is Chased|agency=Associated Press|newspaper=The Milwaukee Sentinel|page=13|date=July 22, 1931|accessdate=July 18, 2012}}</ref>1932年，麥格勞宣布退休。然而接替總教練位置的人，不是班克羅夫特，而是[[比爾·泰瑞|比爾·泰瑞]]。班克羅夫特對球隊感到大失所望，帶著遺憾離開巨人。
==個人生活==
班克羅夫特在小聯盟打球時與Edna Harriet Gisin結婚，不過兩人膝下無子，並定居在蘇必利爾。

班克羅夫特從棒球界退休後，便在零售店擔任管理員，並於1956年退休。1972年10月9日，班克羅夫特以81歲高齡辭世。<ref name=obit>{{cite news|url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F50B12FF3E591A7493C3A8178BD95F468785F9|title=Dave Bancroft, Star Shortstop For Phillies and Giants, Dead|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|agency=[[Associated_Press|AP]]|date=October 11, 1972|accessdate=July 7, 2012}}</ref>

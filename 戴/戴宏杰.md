'''戴宏杰''' ({{bd|1966年|5月2日||}})<ref name="mosher">{{citation|url=http://library.stanford.edu/depts/swain/history/mosher/biography.html |title=Stanford Chemistry Department History 1977 to 2000. VI.  Professors, Brief Biographical Summaries  1976–2000 |first=Harry S. |last=Mosher |publisher=Stanford University Library |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120212205847/http://library.stanford.edu/depts/swain/history/mosher/biography.html |archivedate=12 February 2012 }}.</ref>，美国华人化学家和应用物理学家，[[斯坦福大学|斯坦福大学]]化学系教授。<ref name="suchem">{{cite web|title=Chemistry Faculty: Faculty Research Interests - Hongjie Dai|url=http://www.stanford.edu/dept/chemistry/faculty/dai/|publisher=Stanford University|accessdate=9 June 2010}}.</ref>在[[碳纳米管|碳纳米管]]领域是领军人物。<ref>{{citation|title=A Wisp of Carbon, a Whiff of Gases|first=Anne|last=Eisenberg|date=2 March 2000|journal=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]]|url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=FB0A12F93B5D0C718CDDAA0894D8404482}}.</ref><ref>{{citation|title=Researchers Develop First Integrated Silicon Circuit With Nanotube Transistors|journal=ScienceDaily|date=7 January 2004|url=https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2004/01/040107072303.htm}}.</ref><ref>{{citation|title=Nanotubes smuggle anti-HIV molecules into cells
|date=21 February 2007|first=Celeste|last=Biever|journal=[[New_Scientist|New Scientist]]|url=https://www.newscientist.com/article/dn11226-nanotubes-smuggle-antihiv-molecules-into-cells.html}}.</ref><ref>{{citation|title=Nanotubes cut to ribbons: New techniques open up carbon tubes to create ribbons|first=Geoff|last=Brumfiel|journal=[[Nature_(journal)|Nature]]|date=15 April 2009|doi=10.1038/news.2009.367}}.</ref>曾经被《科学观察》杂志评选为世界前一百位化学家。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Top 100 Chemists, 2000-2010 - ScienceWatch.com - Thomson Reuters|url = http://archive.sciencewatch.com/dr/sci/misc/Top100Chemists2000-10/|website = archive.sciencewatch.com|access-date = 2016-01-31|first = Thomson|last = Reuters}}</ref>

==生平==
1989年毕业于[[清华大学|清华大学]]物理系，1991年毕业于[[哥伦比亚大学|哥伦比亚大学]]应用科学系，1994年毕业于[[哈佛大学|哈佛大学]]应用物理系，师从[[查尔斯·利伯|查尔斯·利伯]]。1997年进入[[斯坦福大学|斯坦福大学]]担任助理教授。<ref name="mosher"/><ref name="suchem"/>

==荣誉==
2004年被选为[[清华大学|清华大学]]第六批[[长江学者讲座教授|长江学者讲座教授]]。<ref>[http://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/publish/newthu/newthu_cnt/teacher/teacher-4.html 长江学者奖励计划入选者]，清华大学官网。</ref>2009年被选为[[美国文理科学院|美国文理科学院]]院士，2011年被选为[[美国科学促进会|美国科学促进会]]会员。<ref name="suchem"/><ref>{{citation |title=11 Stanford faculty inducted into AAAS |journal=Stanford Daily |date=23 April 2009 |url=http://www.stanforddaily.com/2009/04/23/brief-11-stanford-faculty-inducted-into-aaas/ }}{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{citation|title=Three Stanford scholars tapped as AAAS fellows|journal=Stanford Report|date=12 January 2011|url=http://news.stanford.edu/news/2011/january/aaas-science-fellow-011211.html}}.</ref>
2016年被选为[[美国国家科学院|美国国家科学院]]院士。<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.nasonline.org/news-and-multimedia/news/may-3-2016-NAS-Election.html|title=National Academy of Sciences Members and Foreign Associates Elected|department=News from the National Academy of Sciences|publisher=[[National_Academy_of_Sciences|National Academy of Sciences]]|date=May 3, 2016|accessdate=2016-05-14}}.</ref>

'''職業災害'''是指在工作場所發生的[[危害|危害]]，可以分為許多不同的種類，包括{{le|化學危害|chemical hazard}}、[[生物性危害|生物性危害]]、{{le|社會心理危害|psychosocial hazards}}及{{le|物理危害|physical hazards}}等。
==危害因子==
=== 化學危害 ===
{{le|化學危害|chemical hazard}}是指工作中會接觸到危險的[[化學物質|化學物質]]。暴露在化學物質下可能會造成急性或慢性的，不利健康的影響。有許多種不同危險化學物質的分類，包括[[神經毒素|神經毒素]]、免疫製劑<!--、皮膚病劑-->、[[致癌物質|致癌物質]]、[[生殖毒素|生殖毒素]]、系統性毒素、{{le|致哮喘劑|asthmagens}}、致[[肺塵病|肺塵病]]物質及[[過敏|過敏物]]等<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Chemical Safety - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/chemical-safety/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>
。

=== 生物性危害 ===
{{Main|生物性危害}}

[[生物性危害|生物性危害]]是指[[生物|生物]]造成的危害，來源包括[[微生物|微生物]]以及生物產生的[[毒素|毒素]]等。像[[流行性感冒|流行性感冒]]就是一種容易在同事之間散播的生物性危害<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Seasonal Influenza (Flu) in the Workplace - Guidance - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/flu/guidance.html|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>。

戶外的工作者有許多可能的生物危害，包括[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]、[[蜘蛛|蜘蛛]]、[[蛇|蛇]]及[[蠍子|蠍子]]的叮咬<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Insects and Scorpions - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/insects/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Venomous Snakes - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/snakes/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Venomous Spiders - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/spiders/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>，接觸到有毒[[漆属|漆樹]]植物的[[漆酚|漆酚]]產生的[[接觸性皮膚炎|接觸性皮膚炎]]<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title = CDC - Poisonous Plants - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/plants/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>、[[萊姆病|萊姆病]]<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Lyme Disease - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/lyme/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>、[[西尼羅河病毒|西尼羅河病毒]]<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title = CDC - West Nile Virus - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/westnile/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>及{{le|球孢子菌病|coccidioidomycosis}}<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Valley Fever (Coccidioidomycosis) - Jobs at Risk - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/valleyfever/risk.html|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>。根據[[美国国家职业安全卫生研究所|美国国家职业安全卫生研究所]]的統計，暴露在上述生物危害中的職業有農夫、林業工作者、園丁、畫家、屋頂工、鋪路工、建築工人、技師等會有許多時間在戶外的職業"<ref name=":0" />。

醫療保健人員容易暴露在{{le|血源性疾病|Blood-borne disease}}（例如[[人類免疫缺陷病毒|人類免疫缺陷病毒]]、[[B型肝炎|B型肝炎]]及[[C型肝炎|C型肝炎]]）的感染下<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Bloodborne Infectious Diseases - HIV/AIDS, Hepatitis B Virus, and Hepatitis C Virus - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/bbp/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>，也容易罹患[[新興傳染病|新興傳染病]]，尤其是在沒有足夠資源可以避免傳染病散播的情形下<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Emerging Infectious Diseases - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/emerginfectdiseases/default.html|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref> [[兽医学|兽医学]]工作者（例如[[兽医|兽医]]）也容易得到[[人畜共通傳染病|人畜共通傳染病]]<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Veterinary Health Care: Biological Safety - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/veterinary/biological.html|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>。進行田野工作或是實驗室研究的人若有為因[[西尼羅河病毒|西尼羅河病毒]]而死亡的鳥類[[驗屍|驗屍]]或是處理到受感染的組織，也有感染西尼羅河病毒的風險<ref name=":1" />。

其他會暴露在生物危害下的職業包括{{le|家禽飼養|Poultry farming|家禽工人}}容易受到細菌的感染<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Poultry Industry Workers - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/poultry/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>，而{{le|刺青藝術家|Tattoo artist}}及[[身體穿洞|穿洞師]]有受到血原性病原體感染的風險<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Body Art - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/body_art/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>。

=== 社會心理危害 ===
{{le|社會心理危害|Psychosocial hazards}}是指影響到人的社交生活或[[心理健康|心理健康]]的職業危害。職場上的社會心理危害包括[[职业倦怠|职业倦怠]]及{{le|工作壓力|occupational stress}}等，工作壓力也可能會導致倦怠<ref>{{Cite web|title = CDC - Stress at Work - NIOSH Workplace Safety and Health Topic|url = http://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/stress/|website = www.cdc.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>。

=== 物理危害 ===
{{le|物理危害|Physical hazard}}是指和環境有關，可能會造成傷害的職業危害。物理危害包括[[人體工學|人體工學]]危害、[[輻射|輻射]]、冷熱溫度變化、[[振動|振動]]危害及[[噪音|噪音]]危害等<ref>{{Cite web|title = Susan Harwood Grant Products By Topic|url = https://www.osha.gov/dte/grant_materials/material_listing_topic.html|website = www.osha.gov|accessdate = 2015-09-03}}</ref>。

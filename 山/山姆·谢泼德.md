'''塞缪尔·谢泼德·罗杰斯三世'''（{{lang-en|'''Samuel Shepard Rogers III'''}}<ref>{{cite Shepard|publisher=Perseus Books Group|first=Don|last=Shewey|isbn=9780306807701|year=1997|access-date=2015-12-14|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170414081128/https://books.google.com/books?id=xZ-8rdzvABgC&pg=PA13&dq=%22He%20was%20born%20Samuel%20Shepard%20Rogers%20III%20and%20called%20Steve%2C%20although%20if%20he%20were%20royalty%20his%20name%20would%20have%20been%20Samuel%20Shepard%20Rogers%20VII.%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=ylqtUrWiKMyBqQGLrIHAAw&ved=0CCQQ6AEwAA|archivedate=2017-04-14}}</ref>，{{bd|1943年|11月5日|2017年|7月27日}}），又名'''山姆·谢泼德'''（{{lang-en|'''Sam Shepard'''}}），美国演员、剧作家、作家、编剧和导演，活跃半个世纪。他获得10项{{le|奥比奖|Obie Awards}}编剧和导演奖，超越任何编剧和导演。他写过44部喜剧、多篇短篇故事、论文和回忆录。1979年，谢泼德凭借戏剧《{{le|被埋葬的孩子|Buried Child}}》赢得[[普利策戏剧奖|普利策戏剧奖]]，又凭借1983年电影《[[太空先锋_(电影)|太空先锋]]》扮演的航天员[[查克·叶格|查克·叶格]]一角，获得[[奥斯卡最佳男配角奖|奥斯卡最佳男配角奖]]。《[[纽约_(杂志)|纽约]]》称他是“当代美国最伟大的剧作家”<ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=VuUCAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA20&lpg=PA20&dq=%22why+is+sam+shepard+virtually%22&source=bl&ots=zPveS1eQD0&sig=7lUn5gKk1HufDLeDiyK9ZZbUI_U&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwieqc68x9DJAhVK4WMKHZfRBmsQ6AEIHDAA#v=onepage&q=%22why%20is%20sam%20shepard%20virtually%22&f=false|title=The Genius of Sam Shepard|work=[[紐約_(雜誌)|New York]]|first=Ross|last=Wetzsteon|date=1984-11-11|access-date=2015-12-09|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160503210715/https://books.google.com/books?id=VuUCAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA20&lpg=PA20&dq=%22why%20is%20sam%20shepard%20virtually%22&source=bl&ots=zPveS1eQD0&sig=7lUn5gKk1HufDLeDiyK9ZZbUI_U&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwieqc68x9DJAhVK4WMKHZfRBmsQ6AEIHDAA#v=onepage&q=%22why%20is%20sam%20shepard%20virtually%22&f=false|archivedate=2016-05-03}}</ref>。

谢泼德的戏剧悲凉，富有诗意，经常有超现实主义元素，有黑色用墨，聚焦于生活在美国社会郊区的人物<ref>{{fr}} [http://www.ina.fr/video/I17212445/wim-wenders-a-propos-de-sam-shepard-video.html ''Wim Wenders on Sam Shepard''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170819053823/http://www.ina.fr/video/I17212445/wim-wenders-a-propos-de-sam-shepard-video.html |date=2017-08-19 }} (video), Institut National de l'Audiovisue|, 1984.</ref>。他的风格多年来不断演变，从早期的[[外外百老汇|外外百老汇]]变成《被埋葬的孩子》和《{{le|饥饿阶级的诅咒|Curse of the Starving Class}}》的现实主义<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books/about/Sam_Shepard.html?id=T59milCjWBQC|title=Harold Bloom's Major Dramatists: Sam Shepard|publisher=Infobase Publishing|first=Harold|last=Bloom|isbn=9781438116464|date=2009|access-date=2015-12-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170730204911/http://www.thewittliffcollections.txstate.edu/research/a-z/shepard.html|archivedate=2017-07-30}}</ref>。

因[[肌萎缩性脊髓侧索硬化症|肌萎缩性脊髓侧索硬化症]]并发症，2017年7月27日于[[肯塔基|肯塔基]]家中逝世，享年73岁<ref>{{cite news|last1=Deb|first1=Sopan|title=Sam Shepard, Pulitzer-Winning Playwright and Actor, Is Dead at 73|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/07/31/theater/sam-shepard-dead.html|access-date=2017-07-31|work=The New York Times|issue=July 31, 2017|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170731155033/https://www.nytimes.com/2017/07/31/theater/sam-shepard-dead.html|archivedate=2017-07-31}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Hill|first1=Libby|title=Sam Shepard, Pulitzer Prize-winning playwright and Oscar-nominated actor, dies at 73|url=http://www.latimes.com/local/obituaries/la-me-sam-shepard-20170731-story.html|access-date=2017-08-01|work=Los Angeles Times|date=2017-07-31|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170731230715/http://www.latimes.com/local/obituaries/la-me-sam-shepard-20170731-story.html|archivedate=2017-07-31}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |last=Desta |first=Yohana |title=Sam Shepard, Prolific Playwright and Actor, Dies at 73 |url=https://www.vanityfair.com/hollywood/2017/07/sam-shepard-obit |date=2017-07-31 |access-date=2018-05-17 |magazine=Vanity Fair |publisher=Condé Nast}}</ref>。

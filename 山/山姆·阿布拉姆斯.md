'''山姆·阿布拉姆斯'''（Sam Abrams，{{bd|1935年|11月18日}}）出生于[[布鲁克林|布鲁克林]]，是一名美国[[作家|作家]]。<ref>[http://www.marijuana-uses.com/essays/009.html Marijuana Uses - Dr. Lester Grinspoon's Marijuana Uses<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090311022714/http://www.marijuana-uses.com/essays/009.html |date=2009-03-11 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.bigbridge.org/issue5/contribs.htm Big Bridge #5<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref><ref>[http://www.pw.org/directory/writer_detail.php?writer_id=10054 Poets & Writers, Inc<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>阿布拉姆斯曾以[[福布萊特|福布萊特]]教授的身份於[[雅典大学|雅典大学]]任教[[美国文学|美国文学]]，現於[[羅徹斯特理工學院|羅徹斯特理工學院]]的文學院擔任语言与文学[[名誉|名誉]][[教授|教授]]。阿布拉姆斯是[[布鲁克林|布鲁克林]][[詹姆斯·麦迪逊高中|詹姆斯·麦迪逊高中]]、[[布魯克林大學|布魯克林大學]] （1958年文学学士学位）及[[伊利諾伊大學厄巴納-香檳分校|伊利諾伊大學厄巴納-香檳分校]]（1950文學碩士）的毕业生。

==著作==
'''书籍'''：<br>
* ''Barbara''. Ferry Press (London, 1966), 1st Edition. 32 pp. Poems, limited edition of 350 copies.
* ''The Neglected Walt Whitman''. Sam Abrams, Editor. Sixty-five poems, fragments, and three prose pieces by Whitman.
* ''The Old Pothead Poems''. Louis Armstrong said it, "Marijuana is an assistant, a friend." These poems riff off that theme, a fifty-year-long set of improv-collaborations between two old friends, Miss Mary Jane and her man, Sam. Poems too of a classicist, on familiar terms with Sappho, Archilochus, Horace, Socrates -- regulars in the audience along with Miles, Billy, Bessie,...
* ''The Post-American Cultural Congress.''
* ''Book of Days'', with Paul Blackburn

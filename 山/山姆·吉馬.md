'''塞缪尔·菲利普·吉马'''（{{lang-en|Samuel Phillip Gyimah}}；{{IPAc-en|pron|ˈ|dʒ|iː|m|ɑː|}}；{{bd|1976年|8月10日}}）<ref name="SGW1">{{cite web |url=http://www.samgyimah.com/about/sam's-background |title=Sam's Background |website=Sam Gyimah |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140111074915/http://www.samgyimah.com/about/sam's-background |archivedate=11 January 2014 |accessdate=20 July 2016}}</ref>是一位英国政治人物，保守党党员。现任[[英国下议院|英国下议院]][[东萨里_(英国国会选区)|东萨里选区]]议员、<ref name="SGW2">{{cite web |url=http://www.samgyimah.com/about/sam-in-parliament |title=Sam in Parliament |website=Sam Gyimah |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140612183901/http://www.samgyimah.com/about/sam-in-parliament |archivedate=12 June 2014 |accessdate=20 July 2016}}</ref>{{le|商务能源和产业战略部|Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy}}大学和科学副大臣兼教育部副大臣。

曾任[[英国司法部|司法部]]监狱缓刑更生和量刑政务次官、[[英国教育部|教育部]]育儿和教育政务次官、<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/ministers/parliamentary-under-secretary-of-state--43 |title=Parliamentary Under Secretary of State for Childcare and Education |work=www.gov.uk |accessdate=20 July 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.gov.uk/government/news/her-majestys-government-12-may-2015 |title=Her Majesty's Government: December 2015 |work=Prime Minister's Office |date=12 May 2015 |accessdate=20 July 2016}}</ref>[[內閣辦公室|内阁办公厅]]宪制政务次官、<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/ministers/parliamentary-secretary-minister-for-the-constitution |title=Parliamentary Secretary (Minister for the Constitution) |work=www.gov.uk |accessdate=20 July 2016}}</ref> 政府[[党鞭长_(英国)|党鞭]]、卡梅伦{{le|首相国会私人秘书|Parliamentary Private Secretary to the Prime Minister}}等职。

== 早年经历 ==
吉马出生在[[白金汉郡|白金汉郡]][[比肯斯菲尔德|比肯斯菲尔德]]。六岁时父母分手各奔前程，他与母亲回到[[加纳|加纳]]，其父携其弟其妹留在英国。吉马入读加纳[[阿克拉|阿克拉]]{{le|阿奇摩塔学校|Achimota School}}，该校以培养出五位加纳总统而闻名。后重返英国就读[[比肯斯菲尔德|比肯斯菲尔德]]的公立{{le|弗里曼学院|Freman College}}<ref name="SGW1"/>并通过了[[中等教育普通证书|中等教育普通证书]]和[[普通教育高级程度证书|普通教育高级程度证书]]测验。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Cassidy|first1=Sarah|title=Sam Gyimah interview: Life-changing events often occur in early days of learning|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/sam-gyimah-interview-life-changing-events-often-occur-in-early-days-of-learning-9719518.html|website=The Independent|accessdate=23 October 2016}}</ref>升入[[牛津大学萨默维尔学院|牛津大学萨默维尔学院]]后研习[[哲学、政治学及经济学|哲学、政治学和经济学]]，曾当选{{le|牛津辩论社|Oxford Union}}主席。<ref name="SGW1"/>

==个人生活==
吉马与新西兰医生妮基·布莱克结婚，二人育有一子一女。<ref name="SGW1"/>

他是唐氏综合征协会和萨里郡圣凯瑟琳临终关怀的志愿者和危机筹款人。还是内伦敦一所学校的管理员、住房协会委员会和牛津大学萨默维尔学院发展委员会委员。他是萨里郡{{le|林菲尔德|Lingfield, Surrey}}新成立的癫痫慈善机构（前身是国家癫痫青年中心）的副主席。<ref name="SGW1"/>

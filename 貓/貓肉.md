== 各地食用情况 ==
=== 亚洲 ===
在中国的[[广东省|广东省]]和[[广西壮族自治区|广西壮族自治区]]，一些老年人认为猫肉是冬季的温性食物。在廣東，貓肉是傳統菜[[龍虎鳳|龍虎鳳]]的原料之一，当地人认为它能补身子。猫大腿的肉会制成肉丸煲汤，肠胃也会食用，頭部和其餘部分则会被丟棄。据估计，每年中国大陆有400万只猫被食用，而这一数字还在继续增长。<ref name="Xinhuanet">{{cite web|title=Some call it an indelicate trade, others, a delicacy|url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/english/indepth/2012-01/13/c_131358233.htm|website=Xinhuanet.com|publisher=China Daily|date=2012-01-13|accessdate=2017-02-08|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170314075006/http://news.xinhuanet.com/english/indepth/2012-01/13/c_131358233.htm|archivedate=2017-03-14}}</ref>

在日本，直到[[江户时代|江户时代]]末一直有食用猫肉的记录。<ref name="hanleycat66">{{Cite book |last=Hanley |first=Susan |title=Everyday Things in Premodern Japan |year=1997 |page=66 |language=en}}</ref>在[[琉球群島|琉球群島]]，猫肉是用于治疗[[胸膜炎|胸膜炎]]、[[气管炎|气管炎]]、[[肺病|肺病]]和[[痔瘡|痔瘡]]的传统食物。<ref>{{cite book|author1=渡口初美|title=沖縄の食養生料理|year=1979|location=国際料理学院|pages=12|language=ja}}</ref>

食用貓肉在[[朝鲜半岛|朝鲜半岛]]是神經痛和[[關節炎|關節炎]]的民間療法，而当地現代消費者也亲睐喝貓肉湯。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://saynotodogmeat.net/2014/02/19/boiled-alive-cat-meat-soup-south-korea/ |title=Boiled Alive Cat Meat Soup, South Korea |work=SAY NO TO DOG MEAT.NET |accessdate=2015-11-22 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151123001003/http://saynotodogmeat.net/2014/02/19/boiled-alive-cat-meat-soup-south-korea/ |archivedate=2015-11-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://our-compass.org/2012/04/09/ban-south-korean-dog-and-cat-meat-trade/|title=Ban South Korean dog and cat meat trade|work=Our Compass|accessdate=2015-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.animalliberationfront.com/ALFront/Actions-Korea/KoreaDogMeatUpdate.htm|title=Important Update On South Korean Dog And Cat Meat Trade|publisher=|accessdate=2015-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://koreandogs.org/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2015-10-19 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151023065537/http://koreandogs.org/ |archivedate=2015-10-23 |df= }}</ref>

在越南，食用猫肉属非法行为，但民众仍有食用。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thedailymeal.com/9-countries-eat-cats-and-dogs-slideshow|title=9 Countries That Eat Cats and Dogs (Slideshow)|work=The Daily Meal|accessdate=2015-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thedailymeal.com/9-countries-eat-cats-and-dogs/11414|title=9 Countries That Eat Cats and Dogs|work=The Daily Meal|accessdate=2015-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://vietnamcoracle.com/cat-meat/|title=Cat Meat|work=Vietnam Coracle|accessdate=2015-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thedropoutdiaries.com/2012/11/the-truth-about-cats-dogs-in-vietnam/|title=The Truth About Cats & Dogs In Vietnam - The Dropout Diaries|work=The Dropout Diaries|accessdate=2015-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/travel/international/5337677/Where-cat-sits-happily-on-the-menu|title=Where cat sits happily on the menu|work=Stuff|accessdate=2015-11-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.csmonitor.com/World/Global-News/2010/0722/Why-do-Vietnamese-keep-cats-on-a-leash-Hint-What-s-for-dinner|title=Why do Vietnamese keep cats on a leash? (Hint: What's for dinner?)|author=The Christian Science Monitor|work=The Christian Science Monitor|accessdate=2015-11-22}}</ref>当地貓肉市場需求较大，河內的貓通常由中國和寮國偷運入境。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.appledaily.com.tw/realtimenews/article/new/20150417/594681/|title=越南恐怖「貓餐廳」　一天吃掉30隻貓|work=[[苹果日报_(台湾)|苹果日报]]|accessdate=2017-02-08}}</ref>贩卖猫肉的餐厅通常以“[[wiktionary:tiểu_hổ|tiểu hổ]]”（意为“小虎”）代指猫肉。<ref name="Hopkins2004">{{cite book|author=Jerry Hopkins|title=Extreme Cuisine: The Weird and Wonderful Foods That People Eat|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=4ZPTAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA25&dq=northern+italy+eat+cats&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwik2qSL88LPAhXGGh4KHYk9ApkQ6AEIIjAB#v=onepage&q=northern%20italy%20eat%20cats&f=false|date=2004-05-15|publisher=Tuttle Publishing|isbn=978-1-4629-0472-3|pages=25–}}</ref><ref name="Hopkins2014"/>

印度的部分地区也有食用猫肉的习惯。猫肉是Narikurvar族人婚礼的菜肴之一。在男性婴儿出生时，Narikurvar族人有时也会烹饪猫肉汤庆祝。<ref name="huffindia">{{cite news|publisher=HuffingtonPost|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.in/2016/11/01/for-biryani-in-chennai-cats-are-being-boiled-alive/?ncid=fcbklnkinhpmg00000001|title=For biryani in Chennai, cats are being boiled alive, sold for Rs 100 per kg|accessdate=2017-02-08|date=2016-11-01}}</ref>

== 对食用的禁止 ==
=== 传统禁止 ===
伊斯蘭教传统上禁止食用貓肉。<ref>{{hadith-usc|usc=yes|muslim|21|4752}}</ref>

=== 法律禁止 ===
2010年1月26日，中国起草了该国第一份《反虐待动物法》草案，其中就有禁止人們食用貓肉的条文，违反规定者最高可被拘留15日。<ref name="China to jail people for up to 15 days who eat dog (China Daily)">{{cite web| url =http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/china/2010-01/26/content_9379689.htm | accessdate=2010-01-26 | publisher=Chinadaily |title = China to jail people for up to 15 days who eat dog}}</ref>但这一草案至今仍未实质进入立法阶段。

2013年10月，秘鲁当局下令停止举办当地年度的猫肉美食节（{{lang|es|Festival Gastronomico del Gato}}），当局称颁布这一禁令是出于人道与食品安全双重考虑。但这一规定遭到了当地民众批评，有民众認為，貓肉比兔肉或鴨肉更有营养，全球很多地方的人长期食用它，沒有任何坏处。<ref>{{cite web|author1=Golgowksi, Nina|title=Judge bans Peruvian town's annual cat-eating festival|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/news/world/judge-bans-peruvian-town-annual-cat-eating-festival-article-1.1486233|publisher=[[Daily_News_(New_York)|Daily News]] (New York).|date=2013-10-15|accessdate=2017-02-08}}</ref>

2014年，瑞士动物保护组织向[[瑞士国会|瑞士国会]]请愿，要求立法禁止食用猫肉与狗肉。组织的创办人托梅克表示，该请愿已获得1.6万人签名支持。<ref>{{cite web|author1=王忠会|title=瑞士动物保护组织要求国会立法 禁食猫肉与狗肉|url=http://www.chinanews.com/gj/2014/11-27/6821490.shtml|publisher=中国新闻网|accessdate=2017-02-08}}</ref>

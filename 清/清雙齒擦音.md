'''清雙齒擦音'''是一個極罕見的[[輔音|輔音]]。在自然語言中，這個發音只出現於[[阿迪格語|阿迪格語]]的{{tsl|en|Shapsugs|沙普色爾}}方言，並被當作獨立音素。這個發音也出現在人造語言[[Ithkuil|Ithkuil]]系列（包括舊的Ithkuil<ref name="IthorPhon">[http://www.ithkuil.net/ithkuil-ch1-phonology.htm#Sec1o2 The Phonology of Ithkuil, see section 1.2.3 Allophonic Distinctions] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090623073648/http://www.ithkuil.net/ithkuil-ch1-phonology.htm#Sec1o2 |date=June 23, 2009 }}</ref>，其繼承者[[Ilaksh|Ilaksh]]，<ref name="IlakshPhon">[http://www.ithkuil.net/ilaksh/Chapter_1.html The Phonology of Ilaksh], see section 1.2.5.1 {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090311041725/http://www.ithkuil.net/ilaksh/Chapter_1.html |date=March 11, 2009 }}</ref>以及新版本的Ithkuil<ref name="IthPhon">[http://ithkuil.net/01_phonology.html#Sec1o2 The Phonology of Ithkuil, see section 1.2.5.1 Consonantal allophones]</ref>）中，被當作[[輔音延長|長]][[清聲門擦音|清聲門擦音]]（{{IPA|/hː/}}）的[[同位異音|同位異音]]之一。

'''鄉野地蟹'''（''Gecarcinus ruricola''）是[[地蟹屬|地蟹屬]]的一個種，發現於[[古巴|古巴]]和整個[[安的列斯群島|安的列斯群島]]<ref name="Hartnoll06">{{cite journal |journal=[[Journal_of_Crustacean_Biology|Journal of Crustacean Biology]] |volume=26 |issue=3 |pages=316–325 |year=2006 |title=Population biology of the black land crab, ''Gecarcinus ruricola'', in the San Andres archipelago, Western Caribbean |author=Richard G. Hartnoll |author2=Mark S. P. Baine |author3=Yolima Grandas |author4=Jennifer James |author5=Helen Atkin |last-author-amp=yes |doi=10.1651/C-2640.1 }}</ref>。

[[File:Gecarcinus_ruricola.png|thumb]]

鄉野地蟹共有黑、紅、黃、綠四種顏色<ref name="Statia"/>，甲寬可達12釐米。壽命可達10年<ref name="Statia">{{cite journal |url=http://www.statiapark.org/downloads/downloads/newsletter/newsletter-sept09.pdf |journal=St Eustatius: National and Marine Parks and Botanical Gardens Newsletter |year=2009 |title=Why do we see Crabs in the Quill? |pages=5}}</ref>。

==與人類的關係==
鄉野地蟹數量極多，[[聖卡塔利娜島|聖卡塔利娜島]]上的種群數量一度達到每平米0.4隻，但由於人類的捕捉，其數量有所下降。它曾出現在[[坦桑尼亞|坦桑尼亞]]和[[烏干達|烏干達]]的貨比上<ref>{{cite journal |title=Crustaceans on postage stamps from 1870 to and including 2002: revised article for our paper in 2000 and addendum |author=Makoto Omori |author2=Lipke B. Holthuis |author2-link=Lipke Holthuis |last-author-amp=yes |journal=Journal of the Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology |volume=1 |pages=1–39 |year=2005 |url=http://lib.s.kaiyodai.ac.jp/library/kiyou/tkh01/tkh0101.pdf}}</ref>。

'''鳳蜆蝶屬'''（學名：'''''Chorinea'''''）是[[蜆蝶亞科|蜆蝶亞科]][[蜆蝶族|蜆蝶族]]裡的一個[[屬|屬]]。共有8個物種，外形獨特，分佈於[[新熱帶界|新熱帶界]]<ref>Tree of Life Web Project. 2008. Chorinea Gray 1832. Zeonia Swainson 1833 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym, Ethelida Westwood 1851 JOS of Zeonia Swainson. Version 04 June 2008 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Chorinea/104641/2008.06.04 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。

== 物種 ==
* [[亞馬遜鳳蜆蝶|亞馬遜鳳蜆蝶]] ''Chorinea amazon''
* [[黑紋鳳蜆蝶|黑紋鳳蜆蝶]] ''Chorinea batesii''
* ''Chorinea bogota''
* ''Chorinea gratiosa''
* [[哥倫比亞鳳蜆蝶|哥倫比亞鳳蜆蝶]] ''Chorinea heliconides'' = 提曼鳳蜆蝶 ''C. timandra''
* [[雙斑鳳蜆蝶|雙斑鳳蜆蝶]] ''Chorinea licursis'' = 鳳蜆蝶 ''C. xanthippe''
* [[長尾鳳蜆蝶|長尾鳳蜆蝶]] ''Chorinea octauius''
** 指名亞種 ''C. o. octauius'' = 紅刺鳳蜆蝶 ''C. faunus''
* [[紅臀鳳蜆蝶|紅臀鳳蜆蝶]] ''Chorinea sylphina''

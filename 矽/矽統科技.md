'''-{矽}-統科技股份有限公司'''（{{lang-en|Silicon Integrated Systems}}，常縮寫為'''SiS'''）是一家[[晶片|晶片]]設計公司，1987年成立，坐落於[[台灣|台灣]][[新竹科學園區|新竹科學園區]]。
<!--'''Silicon Integrated Systems''' (SiS) is a company that manufactures, among other things, [[motherboard|motherboard]]s. The company was founded in 1987 in [[Hsinchu_Science_Park|Hsinchu Science Park]], [[Taiwan|Taiwan]].-->

== 歷史 ==
在很長一段時間，-{矽}-統致力于整合晶片組研发，虽然价格低廉，但是其发布的晶片組效能、相容性不高，并面临驅動程式不完善的困扰。近几年，-{矽}-統也逐步研发出一系列高性能非整合晶片組，并且解決方案仍然很便宜，但也有了理想的穩定性和效能。
<!--The SiS645 and SiS650 are very similar and contain integrated ([[chipsets|chipsets]]) [[graphic_card|graphic card]]s.  For a long period SiS branded integrated [[graphic_card|graphic card]]s and add-in boards, enjoyed a reputation for low performance, poor compatibility, and buggy drivers. However, post 2000, while still not offering feature rich chipsets, SiS solutions have remained cheap, but also delivered reasonable stability and performance.-->

在1990年後期，-{矽}-統做了決定投資他們自己擁有的[[晶圓|晶圓]]廠。這樣的策略導致了公司財務上的壓力，以及降低了投資其他新產品上的可用經費。後來在管理經營階層上承認這個錯誤。
<!--In the late 1990s SiS took the decision to invest in their own fab facilities. This placed severe financial strain on the company, and reduced the amount of money available for investing in new products. A mistake the management eventually recognised.-->

-{矽}-統科技與[[冶天科技|ATI]]是仅有的兩家起初授權可以生產[[Pentium_4|Pentium 4]][[晶片組|晶片組]]的公司。從 [http://www.anandtech.com/cpuchipsets/showdoc.aspx?i=1660&p=1 648 chipset] 所獲得的利潤，扮演-{矽}-統在財務上持續成功的角色，尤其特別是面臨到[[威盛電子|威盛電子]]在[[Athlon|Athlon]]平台上的成功。
<!--SiS and ATI were the only two companies initially awarded licenses to produce third party chipsets for the Pentium 4, and the revenues from the [http://www.anandtech.com/cpuchipsets/showdoc.aspx?i=1660&p=1 648 chipset], has played an important part in keeping SiS financially viable, most especially in the face of the success of [[VIA_Technologies|VIA Technologies]] on the Athlon platform.-->

除了晶片組外，-{矽}-統亦研發過顯示核心產品，例如[[SIS_315|SIS 315]]和[[Xabre|Xabre]]系列。之後，公司的將繪圖晶片部門獨立出來，成立[[圖誠科技|圖誠科技]](XGI Technology)。

=== 大事記 ===
* 1987年8月，矽統科技成立。
* 1997年8月，於[[台灣證券交易所|台灣證券交易所]]上市，股票代號2363。
* 1999年10月，收購[[Rise_Technology|Rise Technology]]，取得其[[mP6|mP6]][[CPU|CPU]]技術。
* 2003年5月，分割原繪圖晶片事業處，成立子公司[[圖誠科技|圖誠科技]]。

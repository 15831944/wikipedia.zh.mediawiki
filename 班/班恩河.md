'''班恩河'''（{{lang-en|'''River Bann'''}}，{{lang-ga|'''an Bhanna'''}}，意即“白色的河流”<ref>[http://www.logainm.ie/116057.aspx Placenames Database of Ireland]</ref>）是[[北愛爾蘭|北愛爾蘭]]最長的河流，全長{{convert|129|km}}。河流橫貫北愛爾蘭，連接起北愛爾蘭的東南角<ref>{{coord|54.167|-6.083|display=inline|region:GB_scale:50000}}</ref> 和西北角。<ref>{{coord|55.163|-6.768|display=inline|region:GB_scale:50000}}</ref> 并灌入[[內伊湖|內伊湖]]。C.Michael Hogan認為班恩河畔是[[冰河時代|冰河時代]]結束后最早抵達北愛爾蘭的人類的聚居地。<ref>C.Michael Hogan. 2011. [http://www.eoearth.org/article/Celtic_Sea?topic=49523 ''Celtic Sea''. eds. P.Saundry & C.Cleveland. Encyclopedia of Earth. National Council for Science and the Environment. Washington DC.]</ref>

河流對北愛爾蘭工業發展有著重要影響。現今鰻魚和鮭魚是此河流出產的重要經濟魚類。此外，班恩河常被用作區分東西北愛爾蘭的標誌，即所謂的“Bann divide”。班恩河以西沒有東部發達，[[大貝爾法斯特|大貝爾法斯特]]位於河東，西部則多是農業用地，<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/northern_ireland/3685355.stm BBC News]</ref> 西部人群主要信仰天主教、英國國教，而東部主要信仰新教。<ref>[http://cain.ulst.ac.uk/csc/reports/register.htm Register Of Research On Northern Ireland 1993 Edition, CAIN web service]</ref><ref>[http://www.nwipp-newspapers.com/SC/free/288853275359968.php ''Strabane Chronicle] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080821163755/http://www.nwipp-newspapers.com/SC/free/288853275359968.php |date=2008-08-21 }}</ref>

作為內伊湖的主要水源，北愛爾蘭43%的用水都來自班恩河。<ref>{{cite web|title=Rivers Agency Website |work=Lough Neaghwater level maintenance |url=http://www.dardni.gov.uk/riversagency/index/rivers-in-northern-ireland/river-and-lough-levels/rivers-river-and-lough-level-maintenance/rivers-lough-neagh-water-level-maintenance.htm |accessdate=2012-05-21 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120425035655/http://www.dardni.gov.uk/riversagency/index/rivers-in-northern-ireland/river-and-lough-levels/rivers-river-and-lough-level-maintenance/rivers-lough-neagh-water-level-maintenance.htm |archivedate=2012-04-25 }}</ref> 愛爾蘭[[阿爾斯特|阿爾斯特]]地區也有許多地方的用水依靠班恩河。有專門管理班恩河的河流管理局，現在的用水體系主要基於1955年的法案。<ref>{{cite web | title = legislation.gov.uk | work = Lough Neagh and Lower Bann Drainage and Navigation Act (Northern Ireland) 1955| url = http://www.legislation.gov.uk/apni/1955/15| accessdate = 2012-05-21 }}</ref> 其水位一般在[[國家基準點|國家基準點]]以上12.45至12.60米。<ref>{{cite web | title = legislation.gov.uk | work = Lough Neagh (Levels) Scheme (Confirmation) Order(Northern Ireland) 2004| url = http://www.legislation.gov.uk/nisr/2004/513/pdfs/nisr_20040513_en.pdf| accessdate = 2012-05-21 }}</ref>

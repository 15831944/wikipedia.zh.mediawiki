'''梳邦再也站'''（{{lang-ms|Stesen Subang Jaya}}）位於[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[雪兰莪州|雪兰莪州]][[梳邦再也|梳邦再也]]市中心的SS16区，是[[巴生港線|巴生港線]]和[[格拉那再也線|格拉那再也線]]之间的[[转换站|转换站]]也是[[巴生港線|巴生港線]]與支線[[天空花園線|天空花園線]]的分叉處。本車站於1995年8月14日啟用。

== 车站概要 ==
該車站為1棟地上2樓和地下1樓之車站，附近社有梳邦百利商場和永旺霸级市場等商場。是一個高流量的交通樞紐，并提供至{{link-en|世紀大學|SEGi_University}}、{{link-en|泰萊大學|Taylor's University}}、[[莫納什大學|莫納什大學]]、[[英迪國際大學|英迪國際大學]]、英迪学院和{{link-en|雙威大學|Sunway University}}等大專院校的巴士服务。<ref>{{cite web |last=Syazana |first=Syarifah |url=http://www.timeout.com/kuala-lumpur/blog/all-new-stations-on-the-kelana-jaya-and-ampang-line-will-open-on-june-30-062816 |title=All new stations on the Kelana Jaya and Ampang Line will open on June 30 |publisher=Timeout.com |date=2016-06-28 |accessdate=2016-07-03 |language=en |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160701093518/http://www.timeout.com/kuala-lumpur/blog/all-new-stations-on-the-kelana-jaya-and-ampang-line-will-open-on-june-30-062816 |archivedate=2016-07-01 }}</ref>。

== 路線延伸 ==
=== 格拉那再也線 ===
作為輕快鐵延长线項目的一部分，格拉那再也線經過過梳邦再也站和UEP区之後延長17公里至[[布特拉高原站|布特拉高原站]]。梳邦再也站提供乘客在[[KTM通勤鐵路|KTM通勤鐵路]]和輕快鐵互相轉乘。格拉那再也線的延長線於2016年6月30日開始運營，主線與延伸線分屬2家不同公司經營<ref>{{cite web |author=by brenda châ€™ngandjasmin ho |url=http://www.thestar.com.my/metro/community/2016/07/01/new-riders-aboard-passengers-who-have-never-used-lrt-among-those-trying-out-the-ride-from-kelana-jay/ |title=New riders aboard - Community | The Star Online |publisher=Thestar.com.my |date=2016-06-29 |accessdate=2016-07-03 |language=en |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160704002458/http://www.thestar.com.my/metro/community/2016/07/01/new-riders-aboard-passengers-who-have-never-used-lrt-among-those-trying-out-the-ride-from-kelana-jay/ |archivedate=2016-07-04 }}</ref>。

=== 天空花园支线 ===
[[天空花園線|天空花園支線]]長度為8.15[[公里|公里]]。與[[巴生港線|巴生港線]]共軌并預計於2018年5月1日通車，於[[梳邦再也站|梳邦再也站]]轉向往終點站[[蘇丹阿都阿茲沙機場|蘇丹阿都阿茲沙機場]]3號航站樓的{{link-en|天空花園航站樓站|Terminal Skypark Komuter station}}，完工後將是馬來西亞第2個由吉隆坡市中心直達機場的鐵路<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/News/Community/2014/08/08/Full-steam-ahead-Work-on-railway-line-from-Subang-airport-to-KL-Sentral-has-begun/|title=Work on railway line from Subang airport to KL Sentral has begun|last=Menon|first=Priya|newspaper=The Star (Malaysia)|date=2014-08-08|accessdate=2016-04-30|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402145437/http://www.thestar.com.my/News/Community/2014/08/08/Full-steam-ahead-Work-on-railway-line-from-Subang-airport-to-KL-Sentral-has-begun/|archivedate=2015-04-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.spad.gov.my/ms/projek/projek-akan-datang/projek-landasan-keretapi-dari-subang-ke-terminal-skypark-subang|title=Projek Landasan Keretapi dari Subang ke Terminal Skypark Subang|website=Land Public Transport Commission of Malaysia|language=ms|trans-title=Railway Project from Subang to Subang Skypark Terminal|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924104345/http://www.spad.gov.my/ms/projek/projek-akan-datang/projek-landasan-keretapi-dari-subang-ke-terminal-skypark-subang|archivedate=2015-09-24|access-date=2018-04-17}}</ref>

'''芹鹽'''是一种调料，属于{{Tsl|en|Seasoned salt|调味盐}}的一种，通常是用[[西芹|西芹]]的种子来制取的，有时也使用产生干芹菜来制取。<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=UL9lpNrx0kwC&pg=PA177 Culinary Herbs - Ernest Small]. p. 177.</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=5B0xAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA270 Coffee and Tea Industries and the Flavor Field] . 1914. p. 270.</ref><ref name="Schwartz">[http://www.schwartz.co.uk/products/herbs-and-spices/spices/celery-salt.aspx Celery Salt | Ingredients, Nutritional information & Usage Ideas | Schwartz]</ref>西芹的种子也可用来制油。<ref name="Bart">[http://www.bart-ingredients.co.uk/ingredient/celery Celery | Bart Ingredients]</ref> 

作为一种蔬菜制品，其原料芹菜种子中含有钾离子的数量约为钠离子数量的九倍以上。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://ndb.nal.usda.gov/ndb/foods/show/257?manu=&fgcd= |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2018年3月21日 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180321193108/https://ndb.nal.usda.gov/ndb/foods/show/257?manu=&fgcd= |archivedate=2018年3月21日 |df= }}</ref>

== 用途 ==

芹盐中通常加入[[二氧化硅|二氧化硅]]或[[矽酸鈣|硅酸钙]]等作为吸水干燥剂。

某一些品牌的芹盐中是添加有很高的[[硝酸鈉|硝酸钠]]防腐剂的。{{Fact|date=April 2015}}当将芹鹽添加到食物中，会有一部分的硝酸钠在人体内转化为[[亚硝胺|亚硝胺]]，这是一种可能的致癌物质。<ref>[http://lpi.oregonstate.edu/f-w00/nitrosamine.html Nitrosamines and Cancer]</ref> 

芹盐在[[食品产业|食品产业]]中也经常用来防止产品变质。<ref>[http://culinaryarts.about.com/od/seasoningflavoring/a/nitrates.htm Nitrates: Facts About Sodium Nitrate]</ref>

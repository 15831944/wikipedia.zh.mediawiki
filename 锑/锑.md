'''锑'''（{{lang-la|'''Stibium'''}}，[[化学符号|化学符号]]为'''{{lang|la|Sb}}'''，{{标音|拼音=tī|注音=ㄊ一ˋ}}<ref>{{cite book |editor=[[夏征农|夏征农]]、[[陈至立|陈至立]] |title=《[[辞海|辞海]]》第六版彩图本 |year= 2009年|publisher=[[上海辞书出版社|上海辞书出版社]] |location=[[上海|上海]] |isbn= 9787532628599 |pages=第3227页}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.moedict.tw/%E9%8A%BB |language=zh-tw |title=銻 |publisher=萌典 }}</ref>）是[[化学元素|化学元素]]，[[原子序数|原子序数]]为51，是有金属光泽的[[类金属|类金属]]，在自然界主要存在于[[硫化物矿物|硫化物矿物]][[辉锑矿|辉锑矿]]（Sb<sub>2</sub>S<sub>3</sub>）中。目前已知锑化合物在古代就用作化妆品，<ref name=Egypt/>金属锑在古代也有记载，但那时却被误认为是[[铅|铅]]。大约17世纪时，人们知道了锑是化学元素之一。<ref name=Sweden/>

几十年以来，中国已成为世界上最大的锑及其化合物生产国，而其中大部分又都产自[[湖南省|湖南省]][[冷水江市|冷水江市]]的[[锡矿山|锡矿山]]。<ref name=production/>锑的工业制法是先焙烧，再用碳在高温下还原，或者是直接用金属铁还原辉锑矿。<ref name="of03"/>

金属锑最大的用途是与铅和锡制作[[合金|合金]]，以及[[铅酸电池|铅酸电池]]中所用的铅锑合金板。锑与铅和锡制成合金可用来提升[[焊接|焊接]]材料、[[子弹|子弹]]及[[轴承|轴承]]的性能。<ref name=Ullmann/>锑化合物是用途广泛的含氯及含溴[[阻燃剂|阻燃剂]]的重要添加剂。锑在新兴的[[微电子|微电子]]技术也有用途。<ref name=TVglass/>

== 特点 ==
=== 性质 ===
[[File:Antimon.PNG|thumb]]
[[File:Antimony_massive.jpg|left]]
[[File:SbAs_lattice.png|thumb]]]]
锑是[[氮族元素|氮族元素]]（15族），[[电负性|电负性]]为2.05。根据元素周期律，它的电负性比[[锡|锡]]和[[铋|铋]]大，比[[碲|碲]]和[[砷|砷]]小。锑在室温下的空气中是稳定的，但加热时能与氧气反应生成[[三氧化二锑|三氧化二锑]]。<ref name="wiberg_holleman"/>{{rp|758}}

锑是一种带有银色光泽的灰色金属，其[[莫氏硬度|莫氏硬度]]为3。因此，纯锑不能用于制造硬的物件：中国的[[贵州省|贵州省]]曾在1931年发行锑制的硬币，但因为锑很容易磨损，在流通过程损失严重。<ref>{{cite journal|author=高惠章|title=世界钱币史上的珍品:贵州当十锑币|journal=收藏界|year=2004|issue=3|url=http://www.cnki.com.cn/Article/CJFDTotal-SCJE200403015.htm|accessdate=2012-07-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ukcoinpics.co.uk/metal.html |title=Metals Used in Coins and Medals|publisher=ukcoinpics.co.uk|accessdate=}}</ref>锑會與[[稀硝酸|稀硝酸]]或溫暖的[[濃硫酸|濃硫酸]]發生化學反應<ref>{{cite book|author1=Robert Hare|title=A Compendium of the Course of Chemical Instruction in the Medical Department of the University of Pennsylvania|publisher=J.G. Auner|year=1836|pages=401|edition=3}}</ref>。

目前已知锑有四种[[同素异形体|同素异形体]]——一种稳定的金属锑和三种亚稳态锑（爆炸性锑、黑锑、黄锑）。金属锑是一种易碎的银白色有光泽的金属。把熔融的锑缓慢冷却，金属锑就会结成[[三方晶系|三方晶系]]的晶体，其与[[砷|砷]]的灰色同素异形体<!-- 加个“互为”？不然句子不通顺 -->[[异质同晶|异质同晶]]。罕见的爆炸性锑可由电解[[三氯化锑|三氯化锑]]制得，用尖锐的器具刮擦它就会发生[[放热反应|放热]]的化学反应，放出白烟并生成金属锑。如果在[[研钵|研钵]]中用[[研杵|研杵]]将它磨碎，就会发生剧烈的爆炸。黑锑是由金属锑的蒸汽急剧冷却形成的，它的晶体结构与[[红磷|红磷]]和[[黑砷|黑砷]]相同，在氧气中易被氧化甚至自燃。当温度降到100℃时，它逐渐转变成稳定的晶型。黄锑是最不稳定的一种，只能由[[锑化氢|锑化氢]]在-90℃下氧化而得。在这种温度和环境光线的作用下，[[亚稳态|亚稳态]]的同素异形体会转化成更稳定的黑锑。<ref name=kirk>Kirk-Othmer Encyclopedia of Chemical Technology, 5th ed. 2004. Entry for antimony.</ref><ref name="cww"/><ref>{{cite book | url = http://books.google.de/books?id=vVhpurkfeN4C&pg=PA50%7C page2 50–51 | title = Chemistry of arsenic, antimony, and bismuth | isbn = 978-0-7514-0389-3 | author1 = Norman | first1 = Nicholas C | year = 1998}}</ref>

金属锑的结构为层状结构（[[空间群|空间群]]：R{{overline|3}}m No. 166），而每层都包含相连的褶皱六元环结构。最近的和次近的锑原子形成变形八面体，在相同双层中的三个锑原子比其他三个相距略近一些。这种距离上的相对近使得金属锑的[[密度|密度]]达到6.697 g/cm<sup>3</sup>，但层与层之间的成键很弱也造成它很软且易碎。<ref name="wiberg_holleman"/>{{rp|758}}

=== 同位素 ===
{{main|锑的同位素}}
锑有两种稳定同位素，<sup>121</sup>Sb的自然丰度为57.36%，而<sup>123</sup>Sb的自然丰度为42.64%。锑还有35种放射性同位素，其中半衰期最长的<sup>125</sup>Sb为2.75年。此外，目前已发现了29种[[核同质异能素|亚稳态]]。这其中最稳定的是<sup>124</sup>Sb，半衰期为60.20天，它可以用作[[中子源|中子源]]。比稳定同位素<sup>123</sup>Sb轻的同位素倾向于发生[[正电子发射|β<sup>+</sup>衰变]]，而较重的同位素更易发生[[β衰变|β<sup>-</sup>衰变]]。当然也有一些例外。<ref name="NUBASE">{{cite journal| first = Audi| last = Georges|title = The NUBASE Evaluation of Nuclear and Decay Properties| journal = Nuclear Physics A| volume = 729| pages = 3–128| publisher = Atomic Mass Data Center| year = 2003| doi=10.1016/j.nuclphysa.2003.11.001| bibcode=2003NuPhA.729....3A| last2 = Bersillon| first2 = O.| last3 = Blachot| first3 = J.| last4 = Wapstra| first4 = A.H.}}</ref>

=== 卤化物 ===
锑能形成两类[[卤化物|卤化物]]——{{chem|SbX|3}}和{{chem|SbX|5}}。其中三卤化物（[[三氟化锑|{{chem]]、[[三氯化锑|{{chem]]、[[三溴化锑|{{chem]]和[[三碘化锑|{{chem]]）的空间构型都是[[三角锥|三角锥]]形。三氟化锑可以由[[三氧化二锑|三氧化二锑]]与[[氢氟酸|氢氟酸]]反应制得：<ref name="wiberg_holleman"/>{{rp|761–762}}
:{{chem|Sb|2|O|3}} + 6 HF → 2 {{chem|SbF|3}} + 3 {{chem|H|2|O}}

这种氟化物是[[路易斯酸|路易斯酸]]，能结合氟离子形成配离子{{chem|SbF|4|-}}和{{chem|SbF|5|2-}}。熔化的三氟化锑是一种弱的[[导体|导体]]。三氯化锑则由[[三硫化二锑|三硫化二锑]]溶于[[盐酸|盐酸]]制得：<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=cPAyYYsNj9sC&pg=PA56&dq=Sb2S3+++6+HCl+%E2%86%92+2+SbCl3+++3+H2S&hl=zh-CN&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwj18NOPsvvQAhUMO7wKHasMCywQ6AEIKzAC#v=onepage&q=Sb2S3%20+%206%20HCl%20%E2%86%92%202%20SbCl3%20+%203%20H2S&f=false|title=Antimony|last=|first=|publisher=PediaPress|year=|isbn=|location=|pages=}}</ref>{{rp|56}}
:{{chem|Sb|2|S|3}} + 6 HCl → 2 {{chem|SbCl|3}} + 3 {{chem|H|2|S}}
[[File:Antimony-pentafluoride-monomer-3D-balls.png|thumb]]

锑的卤氧化物比[[砷|砷]]和[[磷|磷]]更为常见。三氧化二锑溶于浓酸再稀释可形成锑酰化合物，例如SbOCl和{{chem|(SbO)|2|SO|4}}。<ref name="wiberg_holleman"/>{{rp|764}}

=== 锑化物、氢化物与有机锑化合物 ===
这类化合物通常被视作Sb<sup>3-</sup>的衍生物。锑能与金属形成锑化物，例如[[锑化铟|锑化铟]]（InSb）和[[锑化银|锑化银]]（{{chem|Ag|3|Sb}}）。<ref name="wiberg_holleman"/>{{rp|760}}碱金属和锌的锑化物，例如Na<sub>3</sub>Sb和Zn<sub>3</sub>Sb<sub>2</sub>比前者更为活泼。这些锑化物用酸处理可以生成不稳定的气体[[锑化氢|锑化氢]]（{{chem|SbH|3}}）：<ref>{{cite book
| title = Outlines of Chemistry – A Textbook for College Students
| author = Louis Kahlenberg
| publisher = READ BOOKS
| year = 2008
| isbn = 1-4097-6995-X
| pages = 324–325
}}</ref>
:{{chem|Sb|3-}} + 3 {{chem|H|+}} → {{chem|SbH|3}}
锑化氢也可用活泼氢化物（如[[硼氢化钠|硼氢化钠]]）还原三价锑化合物来制备。它在室温下就会自发分解，因为它的[[标准摩尔生成焓|标准摩尔生成焓]]为正值。正因为如此，它在热力学上[[热力学稳定性|不稳定]]，不能由锑和[[氢|氢]]气直接化合制得。<ref name="Greenwood">Greenwood, N. N.; & Earnshaw, A. (1997). Chemistry of the Elements (2nd Edn.), Oxford: Butterworth-Heinemann. ISBN 978-0-7506-3365-9.</ref>

[[有机锑化合物|有机锑化合物]]一般可由[[格氏试剂|格氏试剂]]对卤化锑的[[烷基化|烷基化]]反应制备。<ref>Elschenbroich, C. "Organometallics" (2006) Wiley-VCH: Weinheim. ISBN 978-3-527-29390-2</ref>已知有超過3,000種有机锑化合物<ref>{{cite web|author1=Robert E. Tapscott |author2=Ronald S. Sheinson |author3=Valeri Babushok |author4=Marc R. Nyden |author5=Richard G. Gann |title=Alternative Fire Suppressant Chemicals: A Research Review with Recommendations |url=http://fire.nist.gov/bfrlpubs/fire01/PDF/f01072.pdf |publisher=National Institute of Standards and Technology |accessdate=2016-12-17 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100531200207/http://fire.nist.gov/bfrlpubs/fire01/PDF/f01072.pdf |archivedate=2010-05-31 |deadurl=no |pages=65 }}</ref>，包括混合氯代衍生物，还有以锑为中心的阳离子和阴离子。例如Sb(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>3</sub>（[[三苯基锑|三苯基锑]]）、Sb<sub>2</sub>(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>4</sub>（含有一根Sb-Sb键）以及环状的[Sb(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)]<sub>n</sub>。五[[配位数|配位]]的有机锑化合物也很常见，例如Sb(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>5</sub>和一些类似的卤代物。

在[[迦勒底|迦勒底]]的[[泰洛赫|泰洛赫]]（今[[伊拉克|伊拉克]]），曾发现一块可追溯到公元前3000年的锑制史前花瓶碎片；而在[[埃及|埃及]]发现了公元前2500年至前2200年间的镀锑的铜器。<ref name=kirk/>奥斯汀在1892年赫伯特·格拉斯顿的一场演讲时<ref name=moorey>{{cite book |last = Moorey|first = P. R. === 生产过程 ===

从矿石中提取锑的方法取决于矿石的质量与成分。大部分锑以硫化物矿石形式存在。低品位矿石可用[[泡沫浮选|泡沫浮选]]的方法富集，而高品位矿石加热到500–600 °C使辉锑矿熔化，并得以从[[脉石|脉石]]中分离出来。锑可以用铁屑从天然硫化锑中还原并分离出来：<ref name="of03"/>

:{{chem|Sb|2|S|3}} + 3 Fe → 2 Sb + 3 FeS

三硫化二锑比三氧化二锑稳定，因此易于转化，而焙烧后又恢复成硫化物。<ref name=Ullmann/>这种材料直接用于许多应用中，可能产生的杂质是砷和硫化物。<ref name="Norm">{{cite book | url = http://books.google.de/books?id=vVhpurkfeN4C&pg=PA45 | page = 45 | title = Chemistry of arsenic, antimony, and bismuth | isbn = 978-0-7514-0389-3 | author1 = Norman | first1 = Nicholas C | year = 1998}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/j.envpol.2003.10.014 | title = Antimony distribution and environmental mobility at an historic antimony smelter site, New Zealand | year = 2004 | last1 = Wilson | first1 = N.J. | last2 = Craw | first2 = D. | last3 = Hunter | first3 = K. | journal = Environmental Pollution | volume = 129 | issue = 2 | pages = 257–66 | pmid = 14987811}}</ref>
将锑从氧化物中提取出来可使用碳的热还原法：<ref name="of03"/><ref name="Norm"/>

:2 {{chem|Sb|2|O|3}} + 3 C → 4 Sb + 3 {{chem|CO|2}}

低品味的矿石在高炉中还原，而高品味的则在[[反射炉|反射炉]]中还原。<ref name="of03"/>

== 应用 ==
60%的锑用于生产[[阻燃劑_(塑膠)|阻燃剂]]，而20%的锑用于制造电池中的合金材料、滑动轴承和焊接剂。<ref name="of03">{{cite journal | url = http://pubs.usgs.gov/of/2003/of03-019/of03-019.pdf | first1=C. | last1=Butterman| first2= J.F. | last2= Carlin, Jr.| year =2003 |publisher = Unites States Geological Survey | title = Mineral Commodity Profiles: Antimony}}</ref>

=== 阻燃剂 ===
锑的最主要用途是它的氧化物[[三氧化二锑|三氧化二锑]]用于制造[[阻燃劑_(塑膠)|耐火材料]]。除了含卤素的聚合物阻燃剂以外，它几乎总是与卤化物阻燃剂一起使用。三氧化二锑形成锑的卤化物的过程可以减缓燃烧，即为它具有阻燃效应的原因。<ref>{{cite book | url = http://books.google.de/books?id=ZG9VFSBnIPAC&pg=PA61 | chapter = Antimony trioxide and Related Compounds | title = Flame retardants for plastics and textiles: Practical applications | isbn = 978-3-446-41652-9 | author1 = Weil | first1 = Edward D | last2 = Levchik | first2 = Sergei V | date = 2009-06-04}}</ref><!--10.1016/S0141-3910(02)00067-8-->  这些化合物与氢原子、氧原子和羟基自由基反应，最终使火熄灭。<ref>JW Hastie, Mass spectrometric studies of flame inhibition: Analysis of antimony trihalides in flames, Combustion and Flame, vol. 21, Aug 1973, pp49-54</ref>商业中这些阻燃剂应用于儿童服装、玩具、飞机和汽车座套。它也用于[[玻璃纤维|玻璃纤维]][[复合材料|复合材料]]（俗称玻璃钢）工业中[[聚酯树脂|聚酯树脂]]的添加剂，例如轻型飞机的发动机盖。树脂遇火燃烧但火被扑灭后它的燃烧就会自行停止。<ref name=Ullmann>Sabina C. Grund, Kunibert Hanusch, Hans J. Breunig, Hans Uwe Wolf "Antimony and Antimony Compounds" in Ullmann's Encyclopedia of Industrial Chemistry 2006, Wiley-VCH, Weinheim. {{doi| 10.1002/14356007.a03_055.pub2}}</ref><ref>{{cite book | url = http://books.google.de/books?id=ZG9VFSBnIPAC&pg=PA15 | pages = 15–16 | title = Flame retardants for plastics and textiles: Practical applications | isbn = 978-3-446-41652-9 | author1 = Weil | first1 = Edward D | last2 = Levchik | first2 = Sergei V | date = 2009-06-04}}</ref>

=== 合金 ===
锑能与[[铅|铅]]形成用途广泛的[[合金|合金]]，这种合金硬度与机械强度相比锑都有所提高。大部分使用铅的场合都加入数量不等的锑来制成合金。在[[铅酸电池|铅酸电池]]中，这种添加剂改变电极性质，并能减少放电时副产物氢气的生成。<ref name=Ullmann/><ref>{{cite book|chapter = Types of Alloys|url = http://books.google.com/?id=1HSsx9fPAKkC&pg=PA60|title = Battery technology handbook |first = Heinz Albert|last = Kiehne|publisher = CRC Press|year = 2003|pages = 60–61|isbn =978-0-8247-4249-2 }}</ref>锑也用于减摩合金（例如[[巴比特合金|巴比特合金]]），<ref>{{cite book|pages = 46–47|isbn = 978-1-4067-4671-6|url = http://books.google.com/?id=KR82QRlAgUwC&pg=PA46 |title=Principles of Metallography| last = Williams|first = Robert S.|publisher=Read books|year=2007}}</ref>[[子弹|子弹]]、[[铅弹|铅弹]]、[[网线|网线]]外套、[[铅字合金|铅字合金]]（例如[[Linotype排字机|Linotype排字机]]<ref>{{cite book|url = http://books.google.com/?id=IYZezyEvZ78C&pg=PA399 |title = Inorganic Chemistry – A Textbooks for Colleges and Schools|first = E. J.|last = Holmyard|year = 2008|isbn = 978-1-4437-2253-7|pages = 399–400|work = Read Books}}</ref>）、[[銲料|銲料]]（一些[[危害性物质限制指令|无铅]]焊接剂含有5%的锑）、<ref>{{cite journal|first1 = H.|last1 = Ipser |first2 = H.|last2 = Flandorfer|first3 = Ch.|last3 = Luef|first4 = C.|last4 = Schmetterer|first5 = U.|last5 = Saeed |title = Thermodynamics and phase diagrams of lead-free solder materials|journal = Journal of Materials Science: Materials in Electronics|volume = 18|year = 2007|doi = 10.1007/s10854-006-9009-3|pages = 3–17|issue = 1–3}}</ref>[[铅锡锑合金|铅锡锑合金]]、<ref>{{cite book|last = Hull|first = Charles|title = Pewter|publisher = Osprey Publishing|year = 1992|url = http://books.google.com/?id=3_zyycVRw18C|isbn = 978-0-7478-0152-8|pages = 1–5}}</ref>以及硬化制作[[管风琴|管风琴]]的含[[锡|锡]]较少的合金。

W. S. | last2 = Beerkens | first2 = Ruud G. C. | last3 = Van Nijnatten | first3 = Peter A. | isbn = 3-527-30673-0}}</ref>这是因为锑离子与氧气接触后阻碍了气泡继续生成。<ref>Yamashita H. et al, Voltammetric Studies of Antimony Ions in Soda-lime-silica Glass Melts up to 1873 K, Analytical Sciences, Jan 2001, Vol. 17</ref>第三项应用则是颜料。<ref name="of03"/>锑在[[半导体|半导体]]工业中的应用正不断发展，主要是在超高电导率的[[n-型半导体|n-型]]硅[[晶圆|晶圆]]中用作[[掺杂_(半导体)|掺杂剂]]，<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/?id=COcVgAtqeKkC&pg=PA473|page=473|title=Handbook of semiconductor silicon technology |first1 =William C.|last1 =O'Mara| first2 = Robert B.|last2 =Herring| first3 = Lee Philip|last3 =Hunt|publisher=William Andrew|year=1990|isbn=978-0-8155-1237-0}}</ref>这种材料用于生产[[二极管|二极管]]、[[红外线|红外线]]探测器和[[霍尔效应|霍尔效应]]元件。20世纪50年代，小珠装的[[铅|铅]]锑合金用于给NPN型[[合金结晶体管|合金结晶体管]]的发射器和接收器上漆。<ref>{{cite book|url = http://books.google.com/?id=_7fOlKRDcCkC&pg=PA101|page = 101|title = Selected Works of Professor Herbert Kroemer |last = Maiti,|first=C. K.|publisher=World Scientific, 2008|isbn=978-981-270-901-1|year = 2008}}</ref>[[锑化铟|锑化铟]]是用于制作[[红外探测仪|中红外探测仪]]的材料。<ref>{{cite book | url = http://books.google.de/books?id=X-qeJG1k2jwC&pg=PA68| page = 68 | title = Expanding the vision of sensor materials | isbn = 978-0-309-05175-0 | author1 = Committee On New Sensor Technologies: Materials And Applications | first1 = National Research Council (U.S.) | year = 1995}}</ref><ref>{{cite book | url =http://books.google.de/books?id=wBQCKN_GKhAC&pg=PA35 | page =35 | title =Fundamentals of infrared detector materials | isbn =978-0-8194-6731-7 | author1 =Kinch | first1 =Michael A | year =2007}}</ref><ref>{{cite book | url =http://books.google.de/books?id=WR4_GzaAQM0C&pg=PA15 | page =15 | title =Infrared detectors | isbn =978-0-12-752105-3 | author1 =Willardson | first1 =Robert K | last2 =Beer | first2 =Albert C | year =1970}}</ref>

锑的生物学或医学应用很少。主要成分为锑的药品称作[[含锑药剂|含锑药剂]]（antimonial），是一种[[催吐剂|催吐剂]]。<ref>{{cite journal | title = Antimony's Curious History| first =Colin A. | last = Russell | jstor = 532063 | pages = 115–116 | volume = 54 | issue = 1 | journal = Notes and Records of the Royal Society of London | year = 2000 | doi = 10.1098/rsnr.2000.0101}}</ref>锑化合物也用作[[抗原虫剂|抗原虫剂]]。从1919年起，[[酒石酸锑钾|酒石酸锑钾]]（俗称吐酒石）曾用作治疗[[血吸虫病|血吸虫病]]的药物。它后来逐渐被[[吡喹酮|吡喹酮]]所取代。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1007/s00436-001-0588-x | title = Chemotherapeutic approaches to schistosomes: Current knowledge and outlook | year = 2002 | last1 = a. | first1 = Harder | journal = Parasitology Research | volume = 88 | issue = 5 | pages = 395–7 | pmid = 12049454 }}</ref>锑及其化合物用于多种[[兽医|兽医]]药剂，例如安修马林（硫苹果酸锑锂）用作[[反刍|反刍动物]]的皮肤调节剂。<ref>{{cite book | url =http://books.google.de/books?id=JAkOtJsGqiQC&pg=PA262| pages = 262–265 | title =Diseases of Warm Lands: A Clinical Manual | isbn =978-1-4102-0789-0 | author1 =Kassirsky | first1 =I. A | last2 =Plotnikov | first2 =N. N | date =2003-08-01}}</ref>锑对[[角质化|角质化]]的组织有滋养和调节作用，至少对动物是如此。

含锑的药物也用作治疗[[家畜|家畜]]的[[利什曼病|利什曼病]]的选择之一，例如[[葡甲胺锑酸盐|葡甲胺锑酸盐]]。可惜的是，它不仅[[治疗指数|治疗指数]]较低，而且难以进入一些利什曼原虫[[无鞭毛体|无鞭毛体]]所在的[[骨髓|骨髓]]，也就无法治愈影响内脏的疾病。<ref>{{cite book| url = http://books.google.de/books?id=bXhn6Gzxwu0C&pg=PA19| pages=19–21| title = Drugs used in parasitic diseases| isbn = 978-92-4-140104-3| author1 = Santé| first1 = Organisation Mondiale de la| date = 1995-10}}</ref>金属锑制成的[[锑丸|锑丸]]曾用作药。但它被其他人从空气中摄入后会导致中毒。<ref>{{cite book|title=Antimony in medical history.|year=1999|publisher=Pentland|location=Edinburgh|isbn=1-85821-642-7|last=McCallum|first=R. Ian}}</ref><!--http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC1044720/?page=1-->

在一些[[安全火柴|安全火柴]]的火柴头中使用了[[三硫化二锑|三硫化二锑]]。<ref name="Trends">{{cite book|url = http://books.google.com/?id=TyQrAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA50|publisher = National Academies|year = 1970|author = National Research == 防护 ==
锑和它的许多化合物有毒，作用机理为抑制[[酶|酶]]的活性，这点与砷类似；与同族的砷和铋一样，三价锑的毒性要比五价锑大。<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Winship | first1 = K. A. | title = Toxicity of antimony and its compounds | journal = Adverse drug reactions and acute poisoning reviews | volume = 6 | issue = 2 
| pages = 67–90 | year = 1987 | pmid = 3307336 }}</ref> 但是，锑的毒性比砷低得多，这可能是砷与锑之间在摄取、[[新陈代谢|新陈代谢]]和排泄过程中的巨大差别所造成的：如三价锑和五价锑在消化道的吸收最多为20%；五价锑在细胞中不能被定量地还原为三价（事实上在细胞中三价锑反而会被氧化成五价锑<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Foster | first1 = S. | last2 = Maher | first2 = W. | last3 = Krikowa | first3 = F. | last4 = Telford | first4 = K. | last5 = Ellwood | first5 = M. | title = Observations on the measurement of total antimony and antimony species in algae, plant and animal tissues | doi = 10.1039/b509202g | journal = Journal of Environmental Monitoring | volume = 7 | issue = 12 | pages = 1214–1219 | year = 2005 | pmid = 16307074 | pmc = }}</ref>）；由于体内不能发生[[甲基化|甲基化]]反应，五价锑的主要排泄途径是尿液。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/S0009-2797(97)00087-2 | title = Arsenic and antimony: Comparative approach on mechanistic toxicology | year = 1997 | last1 = Gebel | first1 = T | journal = Chemico-Biological Interactions | volume = 107 | issue = 3 | pages = 131–44 | pmid = 9448748}}</ref> 急性锑中毒的症状也与[[砷中毒|砷中毒]]相似，主要引起[[心脏毒性|心脏毒性]]（表现为[[心肌炎|心肌炎]]），不过锑的心脏毒性还可能引起[[阿－斯综合征|阿－斯综合征]]。有报告称，从搪瓷杯中溶解的锑等价于90毫克酒石酸锑钾时，锑中毒对人体只有短期影响；但是相当于6克酒石酸锑钾时，就会在三天后致人死亡。<ref name="McCallum1977" /> 吸入锑灰也对人体有害，有时甚至是致命的：小剂量吸入时会引起[[头疼|头疼]]、[[眩暈_(醫學)|眩晕]]和[[抑郁|抑郁]]；大剂量摄入，例如长期皮肤接触可能引起皮肤炎、损害肝肾、剧烈而频繁的[[呕吐|呕吐]]，甚至死亡。<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Sundar | first1 = S. | last2 = Chakravarty | first2 = J. | doi = 10.3390/ijerph7124267 | title = Antimony Toxicity | journal = International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health | volume = 7 | issue = 12 | pages = 4267–4277 | year = 2010 | pmid = 21318007 | pmc =3037053 }}</ref>

锑不能与强[[氧化剂|氧化剂]]、[[强酸|强酸]]、[[氢卤酸|氢卤酸]]、[[氯|氯]]或[[氟|氟]]一起存放，并且应与热源隔绝。<ref>[http://www.mallbaker.com/americas/msds/english/a7152_msds_us_default.pdf Antimony MSDS]{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}. Baker</ref>

锑在[[浸取|浸取]]时会从[[聚对苯二甲酸乙二酯|聚对苯二甲酸乙二酯]]（PET）瓶中进入液体。<ref>{{cite journal|pmid=17707454 |title=Antimony leaching from polyethylene terephthalate (PET) plastic used for bottled drinking water|year=2008|last1=Westerhoff|first1=P|last2=Prapaipong|first2=P|last3=Shock|first3=E|last4=Hillaireau|first4=A|volume=42|issue=3|pages=551–6|doi=10.1016/j.watres.2007.07.048|journal=Water research}}</ref>检测到的锑浓度标准则是[[瓶裝水|瓶裝水]]低于[[饮用水|饮用水]]，<ref name=shotyk>{{cite journal|doi=10.1039/b517844b|year=2006|author=Shotyk, W.; Krachler, M.; Chen, B.|title=Contamination of Canadian and European bottled waters with antimony from PET containers|volume=8|issue=2|pages=288–92|pmid=16470261|journal=Journal of Environmental Monitoring}}</ref>英国生产的浓缩[[果汁|果汁]]（暂无标准）被检测到含锑44.7 µg/L，远远超出欧盟[[自来水|自来水]]的标准5 µg/L。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Elevated antimony concentrations in commercial juices|first7=Helle Rüsz|last7=Hansen|first6=Bente|last6=Gammelgaard|first5=Stefan|last5=Stürup|first4=Spiros A.|last4=Pergantis|first3=Søren Alex|last3=Bak|first2=Alexandra|last=Hansen|last2=Tsirigotaki|journal=Journal of Environmental Monitoring|first=Claus|volume=12|issue=4|pages=822–4|date= 17 February 2010|pmid=20383361|doi=10.1039/b926551a}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-1254534/Fruit-juice-cancer-warning-scientists-harmful-chemical-16-drinks.html|title=Fruit juice cancer warning as scientists find harmful chemical in 16 drinks|last=Borland|first=Sophie|date= 1 March 2010|publisher=[[每日郵報|Daily Mail]]|accessdate=}}</ref>各个组织的标准分别是：
*[[世界卫生组织|世界卫生组织]]：20 µg/L
*日本：15 µg/L<ref>Wakayama, Hiroshi, [http://www.nilim.go.jp/lab/bcg/siryou/tnn/tnn0264pdf/ks0264011.pdf "Revision of Drinking Water Standards in Japan"], Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare (Japan), 2003; Table 2, p. 84</ref>
*[[美国国家环境保护局|美国国家环境保护局]]、加拿大卫生部和安大略省环境部：6 µg/L
*德国联邦环境部：5 µg/L<ref name=shotyk/>

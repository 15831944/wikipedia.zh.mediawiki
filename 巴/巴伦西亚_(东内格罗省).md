'''巴伦西亚'''（'''Valencia'''）是[[菲律宾|菲律宾]][[东内格罗省|东内格罗省]]的一座城市。面积147.49平方公里。<ref name=nscb>{{cite web |title=Province: Negros Oriental |url=http://www.nscb.gov.ph/activestats/psgc/province.asp?regName=REGION+VII+%28Central+Visayas%29&regCode=07&provCode=074600000&provName=NEGROS%20ORIENTAL |work=PSGC Interactive |publisher=National Statistical Coordination Board |accessdate=13 March 2013 |location=Makati City, Philippines |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130523100728/http://www.nscb.gov.ph/activestats/psgc/province.asp?regName=REGION+VII+(Central+Visayas)&regCode=07&provCode=074600000&provName=NEGROS%20ORIENTAL |archivedate=2013年5月23日 }}</ref>根据2015年人口普查结果，巴伦西亚拥有34,852名居民。<ref>{{cite web |title=Negros Island Region (NIR) Census of Population (2015): Total Population by Province, City, Municipality and Barangay (Report) |url=https://www.psa.gov.ph/sites/default/files/attachments/hsd/pressrelease/NIR.xlsx |work=[[Philippine_Statistics_Authority|PSA]] |publisher=National Statistical Coordination Board |accessdate=20 June 2016 |location=Makati City, Philippines}}</ref>

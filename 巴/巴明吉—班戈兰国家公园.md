'''巴明吉—班戈兰国家公园'''是[[中非共和国|中非共和国]]北部的一个[[国家公园|国家公园]]及[[生物圈保护区|生物圈保护区]]。它是几内亚-刚果森林生物群的组成部分。<ref name="africanbirdclub.org">{{cite web|url=http://www.africanbirdclub.org/countries/CentralAfricanRepublic/ibas.html|title=Central African Republic (CAR)    Important Bird Areas|publisher=africanbirdclub.org|accessdate=23 April 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110728103701/http://www.africanbirdclub.org/countries/CentralAfricanRepublic/ibas.html|archive-date=2011-07-28|dead-url=yes}}</ref> 它建立于1993年。<ref name="protectedplanet.net">{{cite web|url=http://www.protectedplanet.net/sites/639|title=Bamingui Bangoran National Park|publisher=protectedplanet.net|accessdate=23 April 2011}}</ref>[[瓦萨科-博洛自然保护区|瓦萨科-博洛自然保护区]]位于国家公园的中部。<ref name="Bouché2010">{{cite web|url=http://docs.google.com/viewer?a=v&q=cache:IAAVCZCI8PYJ:pachyderm.weebly.com/uploads/1/9/7/7/1977408/has_the_final_countdown_to_wildlife_extinction_in_northern_central_african_republic_begun.pdf+Bamingui-Bangoran+park&hl=en&gl=us&pid=bl&srcid=ADGEESgaJNmh9J2C5KZNwRB-effshr7eHKnUOvqOXj2_Ho2rp7qs2mDpJuBugSwikd6YcGp0f-aYYw5Veof6jq5YizVzWSX1leBBcDcKPdK-4v5zAP3Sx4vzynT4DJ3csphPHYXH4hYU&sig=AHIEtbQ97ZP1r9ZY2GTFJJ8W9hFBza-w9A|title=Has the ﬁnal countdown to wildlife extinction in Northern Central African Republic begun?|last=Bouché, P., Renaud, P.-C., Lejeune, P., Vermeulen, C., Froment, J.-M., Bangara, A., Fiongai, O., Abdoulaye, A., Abakar, R. and Fay, M.|year=2010|journal=African Journal of Ecology|pages=5|accessdate=23 April 2011}}</ref>

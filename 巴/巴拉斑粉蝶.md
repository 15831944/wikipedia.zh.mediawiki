'''巴拉斑粉蝶'''（學名：''Delias baracasa''），是屬於[[粉蝶科|粉蝶科]][[粉蝶亞科|粉蝶亞科]]的一種[[蝴蝶|蝴蝶]]，是[[斑粉蝶屬|斑粉蝶屬]]的一種。分佈於[[馬來西亞半島|馬來西亞半島]]及[[菲律賓|菲律賓]]一帶。

== 亞種 ==
* ''D. b. '''baracasa'''''
:- '''指名亞種'''，分佈於菲律賓[[棉蘭老島|棉蘭老島]]
* ''D. b. '''dives''''' <small>(Butler, 1869)</small>
:- 分佈於馬來西亞半島<ref>de Nicéville, 1897. On new or little-known Butterflies from the Indo- and Austro-Malayan Regions J. Asiat. Soc. Bengal 66 Pt.II (3) : [http://archive.org/stream/journalofasiatic66unse#page/n583/mode/1up 562], pl. [http://archive.org/stream/journalofasiatic66unse#page/n566/mode/1up 1], f. 1</ref>
* ''D. b. '''danala''''' <small>Hulstaert, 1923</small>
:- 分佈於印尼[[蘇門答臘|蘇門答臘]]<ref>de Nicéville, 1893. New Sumatran Butterflies J. Bombay Nat. Hist. Soc. 7 (4) : [http://archive.org/stream/journalofbombayn71892bomb#page/557/mode/1up 557]</ref>
* ''D. b. '''benguetana''''' <small>Inomata</small>
:- 分佈於菲律賓
* ''D. b. '''cathara''''' <small>Hulstaert, 1923</small>
:- 分佈於婆羅洲<ref>Grose-Smith, 1893. Descriptions of Two new Species of Butterflies from Kina Balu, North Borneo, in the collection of H. Grose-Smith, captured by Mr Everett Ann. Mag. Nat. Hist. (6) 12 (67) : [http://archive.org/stream/annalsmagazineof6121893lond#page/34/mode/1up 34]</ref>

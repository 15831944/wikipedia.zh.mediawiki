'''巴肯工業區'''（{{lang-he|איזור התעשיה ברקן}}）是[[約旦河西岸|約旦河西岸]]的十五座工業區之一，位於[[台拉維夫|台拉維夫]]東方約25公里處，毗鄰約旦河西岸的[[以色列定居點|以色列定居點]]{{link-en|巴肯|Barkan}}與[[阿里埃勒|阿里埃勒]]。巴肯工業區成立於1982年<ref name="Rudoren">{{cite web |author=Jodi Rudoren, |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2014/02/11/world/middleeast/palestinians-work-in-west-bank-for-israeli-industry-they-oppose.html?_r=0 |title=In West Bank Settlements, Israeli Jobs Are Double-Edged Sword |publisher=''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]'' |date=2014-2-10 |access-date=2018-11-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180719024721/https://www.nytimes.com/2014/02/11/world/middleeast/palestinians-work-in-west-bank-for-israeli-industry-they-oppose.html?_r=0# |archive-date=2018-07-19 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，目前包含約120家生產塑膠、金屬、食物與布料的工廠，約有兩萬人在此工作，其中有約半數是[[巴勒斯坦人|巴勒斯坦人]]<ref name="Schechter" />。

有報導指出許多高污染工廠因當地環保法規的缺乏，而從[[以色列|以色列]]本土遷入巴肯工業區等約旦河西岸的工業區中<ref>{{cite web |author=Eric Reidy, |url=http://www.aljazeera.com/indepth/features/2013/12/palestinians-thirst-water-treatment-plant-2013121612171192492.html |title=Palestinians thirst for water treatment plant |publisher=[[Al_Jazeera|Al Jazeera]] |date=2013-12-21 |access-date=2018-11-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181106150310/https://www.aljazeera.com/indepth/features/2013/12/palestinians-thirst-water-treatment-plant-2013121612171192492.html# |archive-date=2018-11-06 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。2016年1月，[[人權觀察|人權觀察]]發布的一則報告指巴肯工業區的設立加劇了以色列對佔領區資源的剝削，且其中巴勒斯坦工人受到薪資過低、工時過長等不公平的待遇，呼籲廠商撤出約旦河西岸<ref>{{cite web|author=Karl Vick|url=http://time.com/4183059/why-human-rights-watch-is-urging-companies-to-pull-out-of-israeli-settlements/|title=Why Human Rights Watch Is Urging Companies to Pull Out of Israeli Settlements|publisher=[[Time_Magazine|Time Magazine]]|date=2016-1-19|access-date=2018-11-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180426181838/http://time.com/4183059/why-human-rights-watch-is-urging-companies-to-pull-out-of-israeli-settlements/#|archive-date=2018-04-26|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="HRW2016">{{cite web|url=https://www.hrw.org/report/2016/01/19/occupation-inc/how-settlement-businesses-contribute-israels-violations-palestinian|title=Occupation, Inc. How Settlement Businesses Contribute to Israel’s Violations of Palestinian Rights,'|publisher=[[人權觀察|Human Rights Watch]]|date=2016-1-19|access-date=2018-11-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180913015724/https://www.hrw.org/report/2016/01/19/occupation-inc/how-settlement-businesses-contribute-israels-violations-palestinian#|archive-date=2018-09-13|dead-url=no}}</ref>。但也有觀點認為巴肯工業區是當地巴勒斯坦人重要的工作來源，並提供工人高於[[巴勒斯坦|巴勒斯坦]]平均薪資數倍的待遇<ref name="Schechter">{{cite web|author=Asher Schechter|url=http://www.haaretz.com/news/diplomacy-defense/.premium-1.540861|title=EU settlement ban casts shadow over Palestinian industry in the West Bank|publisher=[[Haaretz|Haaretz]]|date=2013-8-11|access-date=2018-11-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924172155/http://www.haaretz.com/news/diplomacy-defense/.premium-1.540861#|archive-date=2015-09-24|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="ynet">{{cite web|author=Elior Levy|url=http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4231245,00.html|title=Palestinians will lose jobs if boycott persists|publisher=[[Ynet|Ynet]]|date=2012-5-20|access-date=2018-11-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170724084301/http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4231245,00.html#|archive-date=2017-07-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

巴肯工業區生產的產品有超過八成為出口至外國，因而受到[[抵制、撤資、制裁|抵制、撤資、制裁]]運動的衝擊<ref name="Schechter" />。{{link-en|巴肯酒莊|Barkan Wine Cellars}}<ref>Giulio Meotti, [http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4115718,00.html 'Is BDS campaign working?,'] {{Wayback|url=http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4115718,00.html# |date=20180820142344 }} [[Ynet|Ynet]] 31 August 2011.</ref>與[[亞薩合萊|亞薩合萊]]<ref>[http://business-humanrights.org/en/assa-abloy-confirms-decision-to-relocate-mul-t-lock-factory-from-west-bank-due-to-criticism-factory-was-located-on-occupied-land ‘Assa Abloy confirms decision to relocate Mul-T-Lock factory from West Bank due to criticism factory was located on "occupied" land.’] {{Wayback|url=http://business-humanrights.org/en/assa-abloy-confirms-decision-to-relocate-mul-t-lock-factory-from-west-bank-due-to-criticism-factory-was-located-on-occupied-land# |date=20150403153539 }} Business & Human Rights Resource Centre</ref>等公司均已撤出巴肯工業區，將工廠移至以色列本土。

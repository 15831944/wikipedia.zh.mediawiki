'''巴鲁大家鼠'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|''Rattus baluensis''}}）为[[马来西亚|马来西亚]]特有的[[大家鼠屬|大家鼠屬]]物种。其与[[马来王猪笼草|马来王猪笼草]]之间存在着互惠关系。与[[山地树鼩|山地树鼩]]一样，其也会取食[[猪笼草|猪笼草]]捕虫笼笼盖下的蜜液，并将粪便排泄到捕虫笼内。<ref name=Rattus>Greenwood, M., C. Clarke, C.C. Lee, A. Gunsalam & R.H. Clarke 2011. A unique resource mutualism between the giant Bornean pitcher plant, ''Nepenthes rajah'', and members of a small mammal community. ''PLoS ONE'' '''6'''(6): e21114. {{DOI|10.1371/journal.pone.0021114}}</ref><ref>Wells, K., M.B. Lakim, S. Schulz & M. Ayasse 2011. Pitchers of ''Nepenthes rajah'' collect faecal droppings from both diurnal and nocturnal small mammals and emit fruity odour. ''Journal of Tropical Ecology'' '''27'''(4): 347–353. {{DOI|10.1017/S0266467411000162}}</ref>

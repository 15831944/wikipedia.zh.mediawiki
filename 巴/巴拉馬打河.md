'''巴拉馬打河'''（{{lang-en|Parramatta River}}）是位於澳大利亞[[新南威爾士州|新南威爾士州]]東部、[[悉尼|悉尼]]都會區的河流。地理上屬中級潮汐性的[[溺灣|溺灣]][[河口|河口]]。<ref name="roy">{{cite journal |author1=Roy, P. S |author2=Williams, R. J |author3=Jones, A. R |author4=Yassini, I |year=2001 |title=Structure and Function of South-east Australian Estuaries |journal=[[Estuarine,_Coastal_and_Shelf_Science|Estuarine, Coastal and Shelf Science]] |volume=53 |pages=351–384 |doi=10.1006/ecss.2001.0796|display-authors=etal}}</ref>河道平均深5.1米。<ref>[https://www.environment.nsw.gov.au/topics/water/estuaries/estuaries-of-nsw/parramatta-river Parramatta River] by [[Office_of_Environment_and_Heritage_(New_South_Wales)|Office of Environment and Heritage NSW]]</ref>巴拉馬打河是[[悉尼港|悉尼港]]的主要支流，而悉尼港是[[傑克遜灣|傑克遜灣]]的分支之一。次級支流包括[[蘭蔻河|蘭蔻河]]和{{link-en|鴨河|Duck River (New South Wales)}}。

巴拉馬打河上游來源是湯加比溪與達令磨坊溪，兩條溪流在{{link-en|北巴拉馬打|North Parramatta, New South Wales}}融匯。<ref>{{cite web |title=The creeks of Sydney's west: Upper Parramatta River |url=http://www.sydneytravellersguide.com/nsw_sydney_creeks2.htm |publisher=Stephen Yarrow |year=2010 |work=Sydney Travellers' Guide |accessdate=30 August 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120204063958/http://www.sydneytravellersguide.com/nsw_sydney_creeks2.htm |archivedate=4 February 2012 |df= }}</ref>巴拉馬打河向東流至位於{{link-en|贝茨格夫|Birchgrove, New South Wales}}區的余魯賓（Yurulbin）與{{link-en|格林尼治 (新南威爾士)|Greenwich, New South Wales|格林尼治}}區的曼角（Manns Point）之間的河口，<ref>{{NSW GNR|id=ujYbvqZTIt|title=Parramatta River|accessdate=2015-05-07}}</ref>在此匯入傑克遜灣，河口距[[塔斯曼海|塔斯曼海]]距離為{{convert|21|km}}。

巴拉馬打河的集水區覆蓋{{convert|252.4|km2}}，潮汐段的最上游點是位於巴拉馬打的查理街堰（Charles Street Weir)，此處距悉尼南北頭（悉尼港入海處）距離約{{convert|30|km}}。<ref name=PREDCRS>{{cite web |url=http://parramattariver.org.au/wp-content/uploads/Rep2429v3.pdf |format=PDF |title=Parramatta River Estuary: Data Compilation and Review Study |publisher=Parramatta River Estuary Management Committee |date=July 2008 |accessdate=30 August 2012}}</ref>

}}}}'''巴拉德大橋'''（{{lang-en|Ballard Bridge}}），又稱'''第十五大道大橋'''（{{lang-en|15th Avenue Bridge}}），是一座位於[[华盛顿州|华盛顿州]][[西雅圖|西雅图]]的双叶[[上開橋|上開橋]]，承載第十五大道西北，跨越過西雅图的{{tsl|en|Salmon Bay|鲑鱼湾}}，北面連接{{tsl|en|Ballard, Seattle|巴拉德區}}，南面則連接{{link-en|内灣 (西雅圖)|Interbay, Seattle|内灣}}。巴拉德大桥的下游是位於其東的[[費利蒙大橋|費利蒙大橋]]，是跨越{{tsl|en|Lake Washington Ship Canal|華盛頓湖運河}}的橋梁的連續的其中之一，而華盛頓湖運河則東面連接[[华盛顿湖|华盛顿湖]]，西面則連接[[普吉特海湾|普吉特海湾]]。

巴拉德大橋在1917年建成，大橋總跨度寬218英尺（即66米），總長度長2854英尺（即870米）。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.cityofseattle.net/transportation/bridges.htm#ballard |title=Ballard Bridge |accessdate=2009-11-17 |work=Bridges and Roadway Structures |author=Seattle Department of Transportation |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101121090450/http://cityofseattle.net/transportation/bridges.htm#ballard |archivedate=2010-11-21 }}</ref>巴拉德大橋在1982年被收錄進[[國家史蹟名錄|美國國家史蹟名錄]]。<ref name="nris" /><ref>{{Cite news|last=Dorpat|first=Paul|title=A Bridge Loses Track|date=2001-08-12|url=http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/pacificnw/2001/0812/nowthen.html|newspaper=[[西雅图时报|西雅图时报]]|accessdate=2009-11-17|publisher=Pacific Northwest Magazine|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090217203510/http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/pacificnw/2001/0812/nowthen.html|archivedate=2009-02-17}}</ref>

近年来，在巴拉德大橋骑[[自行车|自行车]]的人抱怨大橋需要進行改善工程，以解決大橋的安全问题。<ref>{{Citation|title=Bicyclists say city ignores safety concerns; Riding with danger every day|date=2008-07-29|url=http://www.seattlepi.com/local/372600_ballardbridge29.html|last1=Pulkkinen|last2=Gutierrez|first1=Levi|first2=Scott|newspaper=[[Seattle_P-I|Seattle P-I]]|accessdate=2009-11-17}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|title=Bicyclists face rough ride in region; Cycling club finds gaps in some area bike routes|date=2006-04-14|url=http://www.seattlepi.com/local/266713_bicycling14.html|last1=Cat Le|last2=Lange|first1=Phuong|first2=Larry|newspaper=[[Seattle_P-I|Seattle P-I]]|accessdate=2009-11-17}}</ref>

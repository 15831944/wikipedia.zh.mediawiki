'''巴滕伯格蛋糕'''（{{lang-en|Battenberg cake}}）是種表面果醬覆蓋在[[杏仁膏|杏仁膏]][[截面_(幾何)|截面]]的海綿蛋糕。顯示一個獨特2×2的{{link-en|棋盤樣式|Check (pattern)}}，這也是巴滕伯格蛋糕名字由來<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.oxforddictionaries.com/us/definition/american_english/Battenberg |title=Battenberg |work=Oxford Dictionary |accessdate=2015-04-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english/battenburg |title=Definition of 'Battenburg' |work=Collins English Dictionary |accessdate=2015-04-30}}</ref>；蛋糕分別是通過切割成方形的黃色和粉紅色海綿蛋糕烘烤並組合而成，並覆蓋果醬和杏仁膏<ref name=BBC>{{cite web|last1=Cook|first1=Sarah|title=Battenberg Cake|url=http://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/1120657/battenberg-cake|publisher=BBC|date=March 2011|accessdate=2015-05-10|journal=Good Housekeeping}}</ref>。

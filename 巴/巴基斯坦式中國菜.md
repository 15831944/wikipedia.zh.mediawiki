{{中国菜}}
'''巴基斯坦式中國菜'''（{{lang-ur|{{nastaliq|چینی پکوان|ur}}}}）指的是在[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]創造的，具有當地特色的[[中國菜|中國菜]]。巴式中菜源自移居當地的[[巴基斯坦华人|巴基斯坦华人]]，是一套獨特的，結合了中國和巴基斯坦飲食特色的菜系。<ref>{{Citation|page=[https://books.google.com/books?id=GQTABKAGaVgC&pg=PA118 118]|publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group|year=2006|title=Culture and customs of Pakistan|first=Iftikhar Haider|last=Malik|isbn=978-0-313-33126-8}}</ref>

== 歷史 ==
巴式中菜起源自1930年代位於現屬巴基斯坦的地區的中菜館。前[[中华人民共和国总理|中华人民共和国总理]][[周恩来|周恩来]]曾光顧位於[[卡拉奇|卡拉奇]]的ABC中菜館，这家中餐馆持續營業至1988年。<ref name="1n0">{{cite web|url=http://www.1n0.net/Article/zhjs/42135.html|title=中餐馆在巴基斯坦受青睐|date=2008-02-19|website=www.1n0.net|access-date=2019-03-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110723001754/http://www.1n0.net/Article/zhjs/42135.html|archive-date=2011-07-23|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

巴式中菜的特色與[[廣東菜|廣東菜]]有其相近之處。巴式中菜口味以甜、酸、辣為主，在調味上偏好使用[[番茄醬|番茄醬]]、[[醬油|醬油]]、[[辣椒醬|辣椒醬]]、[[醋|醋]]和[[味精|味精]]，而罕用新鮮香料。<ref name="tribune1">{{Cite web |url=https://blogs.tribune.com.pk/story/58713/have-you-heard-about-the-chinese-dish-called-chicken-manchurian-because-people-in-china-havent/ |title=Have you heard about the "Chinese" dish called Chicken Manchurian? Because people in China haven't! |accessdate=2019-03-22 |author=Shahid Wafa |date=2017-11-24 |publisher=The Express Tribune |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181001180504/https://blogs.tribune.com.pk/story/58713/have-you-heard-about-the-chinese-dish-called-chicken-manchurian-because-people-in-china-havent/ |archive-date=2018-10-01 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.oyeyeah.com/lifestyle/stereotypes-chinese-cuisine-pakistan-vs-reality/ |title=Stereotypes about Chinese Cuisine in Pakistan VS Reality |accessdate=2019-03-22 |date=2018-09-15 |work=Oyeyeah |language=en-US}}</ref>巴式中菜在巴基斯坦相當受歡迎，特別是如「滿洲雞」、中式肉串等當地發明的混合菜。<ref name="tribune1"/>

一些新開業的中菜餐廳，如Ginsoy在卡拉奇繁榮發展。卡拉奇的中菜館提供的菜式揉合了中國和巴基斯坦的烹飪風格。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/612077/from-shanghai-to-karachi-living-the-chinese-dream-in-the-eyes-of-a-diplomat/|title=From Shanghai to Karachi: Living the Chinese dream, in the eyes of a diplomat|date=2013-10-02|website=The Express Tribune|access-date=2019-03-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180703080858/https://tribune.com.pk/story/612077/from-shanghai-to-karachi-living-the-chinese-dream-in-the-eyes-of-a-diplomat/|archive-date=2018-07-03|dead-url=no}}</ref>截至2008年，在首都[[伊斯蘭堡|伊斯蘭堡]]的Phoenix中餐廳也變得廣為人知，著名食客包括了巴基斯坦前總統[[佩尔韦兹·穆沙拉夫|佩尔韦兹·穆沙拉夫]]和前總理[[肖卡特·阿齐兹|肖卡特·阿齐兹]]。據報導，穆沙拉夫尤其喜愛該餐廳的龍蝦、北京烤鴨、炒牛肉和蒜香羊排。<ref name="1n0"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.cctv.com/world/20080204/105787.shtml|title=穆沙拉夫常进中餐馆用餐 乐为普通食客买单|date=2008-02-04|website=news.cctv.com|access-date=2019-03-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180514141735/http://news.cctv.com/world/20080204/105787.shtml|archive-date=2018-05-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>而廣受歡迎的巴式中菜「滿洲雞」則是一道由印度的中餐廳所發明的菜式，以辣味見稱，並與傳統中菜的作法有著天淵之別。<ref>{{Citation|last=Mukherjee|first=Sipra|title=Calcutta Mosaic: Essays and Interviews on the Minority Communities of Calcutta|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=cSTEOx_Lw9MC|volume=|pages=131–142|editor-last=Banerjee|editor-first=Himadri|chapter=The Chinese community of Calcutta|publisher=Anthem Press|isbn=978-81-905835-5-8|last2=Gooptu|first2=Sarvani}}</ref>

另外，自從2013年起[[中巴經濟走廊|中巴經濟走廊]]計畫的開展以來，中餐廳在巴基斯坦的主要城市中，有如雨後春筍般湧現。與早年的巴式中菜相比，它們的菜式和口味相對貼近傳統中菜，顧客群也以在當地生活的華人為主。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.globaltimes.cn/content/1088789.shtml |title=Chinese food takes over Pakistani menus |accessdate=2019-03-22 |author=Muhammad Zamir Assadi |date=2018-02-08 |publisher=[[環球時報|環球時報]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180211092522/http://www.globaltimes.cn/content/1088789.shtml |archive-date=2018-02-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://scroll.in/article/910834/sights-and-sounds-of-china-in-pakistan-cpec-brings-a-wave-of-chinese-nationals-to-the-subcontinent |title=In Pakistan, a wave of Chinese residents rides to the subcontinent as trade ties flourish |accessdate=2019-03-22 |author=Danyal Adam Khan |date=2019-01-27 |publisher=scroll.in |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190215125015/https://scroll.in/article/910834/sights-and-sounds-of-china-in-pakistan-cpec-brings-a-wave-of-chinese-nationals-to-the-subcontinent |archive-date=2019-02-15 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

== 菜式 ==
以下是部份廣受歡迎的巴式中菜菜式：<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.sooperchef.pk/blog/quick-easy/top-five-chinese-dishes-loved-pakistanis/ |title=Top Five Chinese Dishes that are Loved by Pakistanis - Sooperchef Blog |accessdate=2019-03-22 |work=Sooperchef.pk}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://images.dawn.com/news/1174289/why-do-pakistanis-love-chinese-food-so-much|title=Why do Pakistanis love Chinese food so much?|work=Dawn|first=Aamir|author=Yasin|date=2015-11-23|access-date=2019-03-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170205213011/http://images.dawn.com/news/1174289/why-do-pakistanis-love-chinese-food-so-much|archive-date=2017-02-05|dead-url=no}}</ref>

* 「滿洲雞」：以[[番茄醬|番茄醬]]調味的爆炒雞肉，一般伴以雞蛋或雞肉炒飯。
* 「青檸雞」：以[[青檸|青檸]]或[[檸檬|檸檬]]調味的爆炒雞肉。
* 甜酸雞／蝦：很像[[咕嚕肉|咕嚕肉]]的一道菜，但以雞肉或蝦肉取代了豬肉。
* 米飯：當地的米飯主要採用的是印度香米，較受歡迎的是蛋炒飯和雞肉炒飯。
* 蜜味[[雞翅|雞翅]]：以蜂蜜醬汁調製的雞翅。
* 湯品：雞肉玉米湯和[[酸辣湯|酸辣湯]]在餐館、家庭和電視上無處不在，一般伴以醋和辣椒調味。
* 麵條：雞肉[[炒麵|炒麵]]在巴式中菜中很受歡迎。烹製炒麵時會用上大量的食油、醬油、味精、醋和辣椒醬，配料包括了蔬菜、雞肉和絞肉。

'''巴哈欧拉陵寝'''（Shrine of Bahá'u'lláh）位于以色列[[阿卡|阿卡]]附近的巴吉，是[[巴哈伊信仰|巴哈伊信仰]]的一处圣地，也是其信徒祷告的方向。陵寝毗邻[[巴哈欧拉|巴哈欧拉]]生前的居所[[巴吉宅邸|巴吉宅邸]]，陵寝内有巴哈欧拉的遗体。<ref name="manvell">{{cite web | title = Visits to the Shrine of Bahá'u'lláh and Meeting with the members of the Universal House of Justice | last = Manvell | first = Christ | date = November 1996| accessdate = 2006-08-12 | url= http://bahai-library.com/manvell_bahai_pilgrimage#2 | publisher = bahai-library.com}}</ref><ref>For pictures of the central room and the inner shrine, see ''The Bahá'í World'', Vol III, p. 5 and 11.</ref>

巴哈欧拉陵寝及其周围的花园，以及[[巴吉宅邸|巴吉宅邸]]，均于2008年7月列入[[世界遗产名录|世界遗产名录]]<ref>{{cite web | url = http://whc.unesco.org/en/news/452 | accessdate = 2008-07-08 | date = 2008-07-08 | title = Three new sites inscribed on UNESCO’s World Heritage List | author = UNESCO World Heritage Centre}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://whc.unesco.org/archive/2007/whc07-31com-8be.pdf | title = Convention concerning the protection of the world cultural and natural heritage | page = 34 | author = World Heritage Committee | date = 2007-07-02 | accessdate = 2008-07-08}}</ref>。

'''巴奇區'''（{{lang-so|''Degmada Baki''}}；{{lang-ar|''ضلع باکی''}}）是[[索馬利亞|索馬利亞]]的一個[[索馬利亞行政區劃|區]]，位於該國西北方的[[奧達勒州|奧達勒州]]<ref>[http://www.statoids.com/yso.html Districts of Somalia], Statoids</ref>，首府為[[巴奇_(索馬利亞)|巴奇]]。巴奇區的主要居民是{{tsl|en|Gadabuursi|佳達布爾斯人}}（{{lang-so|Gadabuursi}}）<ref>{{cite book|last1=Ambroso|first1=Guido|title=Pastoral society and transnational refugees: population movements in Somaliland  and eastern Ethiopia|date=August 2002|publisher=UNHCR Brussels|quote=Chart showing the Gadabursi exclusively inhabiting the Baki district}}</ref> ，而其中又以Reer Nuur族的Makahiil人最多<ref>{{cite book|last1=Ciabarri|first1=Luca|title=Dopo lo Stato. Storia e antropologia della ricomposizione sociale nella Somalia settentrionale: Storia e antropologia della ricomposizione sociale nella Somalia settentrionale|publisher=FrancoAngeli|page=258|language=it|quote=Baki region, the traditional region of the Gadabursi}}</ref>。

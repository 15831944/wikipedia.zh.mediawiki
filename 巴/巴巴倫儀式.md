'''巴巴倫儀式'''（{{lang-en|'''Babalon Working'''}}）是一系列[[魔法|魔法]][[儀式|儀式]]，於1946年1月至3月期間由作家、開創性火箭燃料科學家和[[神秘學|神秘學家]][[傑克·帕森斯|傑克·帕森斯]]，以及[[山達基|山達基]]（科學教）創始人[[L·羅恩·賀伯特|L·羅恩·賀伯特]]進行。<ref>{{cite book | title=''The Church of Scientology: A History of a New Religion'' | publisher=Princeton University Press | author=Urban, Hugh B. | year=2011 | pages=39–42 | isbn=9780691146089 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=8lgHtauc5R4C&pg=PA40|quote= The aim of Parson's "Babalon Working" was first to identify a female partner who would serve as his partner in esoteric sexual rituals; the partner would then become the vessel for the "magickal child" or "moonchild," a supernatural offspring that would be the embodiment of ultimate power.... According to Parson's account of March 2–3, 1946, Hubbard channeled the voice of Babalon, speaking as the beautiful but terrible lady....}}</ref>這個儀式本質上目的是顯現一名原型女神叫{{link-en|巴巴倫|Babalon}}的個體[[化身|化身]]。該計劃是基於[[阿萊斯特·克勞利|阿萊斯特·克勞利]]的想法，以及他在1917年小說《{{link-en|月童|Moonchild (novel)}}》中描述的一個類似計劃。<ref>{{cite book|title= ''Magia Sexualis: Sex, Magic, And Liberation in Modern Western Esotericism''| last = Urban | first = Hugh B. | publisher = University of California Press| year = 2006 |isbn = 9780520247765 | url= https://books.google.com/books?id=6wVBx9yriTUC&pg=PA137| pages=135–137|chapter=The Beast with Two Backs|quote=The ultimate goal of these operations, carried out during February and March 1946, was to give birth to the magical being, or "moonchild," described in Crowley's works.  Using the powerful energy of IX degree Sex Magick, the rites were intended to open a doorway through which the goddess Babalon herself might appear in human form.}}</ref>

== 儀式 ==
當帕森斯宣布這一系列儀式中的第一次儀式完成並取得成功時，他差不多立即與[[瑪喬麗·卡麥隆|瑪喬麗·卡麥隆]]在他自己的家中見面，並將她視為他和賀伯特透過該儀式呼喚出來的[[元素精靈|元素精靈]]。<ref>{{cite book|last=Pendle|first=George|title=''Strange Angel: The Otherworldly Life Of Rocket Scientist John Whiteside Parsons''|year=2006|publisher=Houghton Mifflin Harcourt|isbn=9780156031790|pages=263–271|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=sRFkFGsIyjoC&pg=PA266}}</ref>不久，帕森斯開始了這個儀式系列的下一個階段，嘗試透過{{link-en|性愛魔法|Sex magic}}來懷胎。雖然沒有懷胎，但並沒有影響儀式的結果。卡麥隆現在被帕森斯視為{{link-en|巴巴倫|Babalon|腥紅女士}}（Scarlet Woman），也就是由儀式召喚出來的巴巴倫，他們很快便結婚了。

這個儀式的施行很大程度上引用了英國作家和神秘學老師[[阿萊斯特·克勞利|阿萊斯特·克勞利]]所描述的儀式和性愛魔法。在巴巴倫儀式的過程中，克勞利與帕森斯一直保持著聯繫，並且警告帕森斯他正在進行的魔法對他產生潛在的過度反應，同時也嘲笑帕森斯的所作所為。<ref>{{cite book|last=Sutin |first=Lawrence| title= ''Do What Thou Wilt: A Life of Aleister Crowley''| pages=412–414| publisher=Macmillan |year=2002 | isbn = 9780312252434 |url = https://books.google.com/books?id=NU28kNVR_UsC}}</ref>

== 巴巴倫之書，Liber 49 ==
[[傑克·帕森斯|傑克·帕森斯]]撰寫了一本簡報文本名叫《巴巴倫之書》（''The Book of Babalon''）或者叫《''Liber 49''》，該文本關於在巴巴倫儀式期間他收到來自女神巴巴倫的傳話。<ref>{{cite book|last=Pendle|first=George|title=''Strange Angel: The Otherworldly Life Of Rocket Scientist John Whiteside Parsons''|year=2006|publisher=Houghton Mifflin Harcourt|isbn=9780156031790|pages=263–271|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=sRFkFGsIyjoC&pg=PA266}}</ref>

'''巴西島''' (葡萄牙語: Ilha Brasileira; 西班牙語: Isla Brasileña) 是一個位於[[烏拉圭河|烏拉圭河]]和[[夸拉伊河|夸拉伊河]]的匯流處的無人島。該島接近[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]、[[巴西|巴西]]和[[烏拉圭|烏拉圭]]三國的交點，同時也是巴西、烏拉圭二國的爭議地區。該島長3.7公里、寬0.95公里，面積2.2平方公里，座標為{{coord|30|10|56|S|57|37|43|W}}。

==概述==
===領土爭端===
該島為巴西和烏拉圭的爭議地區；其中巴西將此島劃入[[南里奧格蘭德州|南里奧格蘭德州]]的[[巴拉杜夸賴|巴拉杜夸賴]]市，而烏拉圭將此島劃入[[阿蒂加斯省|阿蒂加斯省]]的[[貝亞烏尼翁|貝亞烏尼翁]]市；但是兩國都未積極實施對該島的主權。此外，對該島的爭端也未影響兩國在經濟和外交上的友好關係。
===人類活動===
1964年，有一位名為José Jorge Daniel的巴西農民來到了這個島，並在上居住了四十多年。2011年，他離開了這個島嶼，搬到了附近的[[烏魯瓜亞納|烏魯瓜亞納]]和親戚居住。不久，他就去世了；那一年他已經九十三<ref name='Férias do Clark'>{{cite web | url = http://feriasdoclark.blogspot.com.br/2013/03/as-atuais-disputas-territoriais.html | title = As atuais disputas territoriais brasileiras | trans_title = The current territorial disputes of Brazil | accessdate = 2014-01-09 | last = Griswold | first = Clark | date = 2013-03-10 | work = Férias do Clark (personal blog) | language = pt}}</ref>(一說九十五<ref name='Central Sul'>{{cite news | title = Uma ilha sem guardião à espera de preservação | trans_title = An island without a guardian waiting to be preserved | date = 2011-07-04 | url = http://www.centralsuldejornais.com.br/IndexNoticia.asp?idNoticia=113782 | work = Central Sul de Jornais | accessdate = 2014-01-09 | language = pt | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304041854/http://www.centralsuldejornais.com.br/IndexNoticia.asp?idNoticia=113782 | archive-date = 2016-03-04 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>)歲了。自從他搬走之後，那個島嶼又一次變成了無人島。
===自然災害===
2009年8月7日，該島發生了火災，原因不明。大火最終被消防隊員撲滅，但是這場火災至少燒到了40％的天然植被。José Jorge Daniel的房子未被這場火災傷害<ref name='G1-incendio'>{{cite news | title = Fogo destrói vegetação na tríplice fronteira entre Brasil, Argentina e Uruguai | trans_title = Fire destroys vegetation on the triple border between Brazil, Argentina, and Uruguay | date = 2009-08-08 | url = http://g1.globo.com/Noticias/Brasil/0,,MUL1259708-5598,00-FOGO+DESTROI+VEGETACAO+NA+TRIPLICE+FRONTEIRA+ENTRE+BRASIL+ARGENTINA+E+URUGU.html | work = Globo.com (G1 portal) | accessdate = 2014-01-09 | language = pt}}</ref>。從那時起，附近的生物學家和學生就會偶爾到島上進行考察，了解火災對當地植物造成的破壞和嘗試恢復曾經的生態系統。<ref name='Central Sul'>{{cite news | title = Uma ilha sem guardião à espera de preservação | trans_title = An island without a guardian waiting to be preserved | date = 2011-07-04 | url = http://www.centralsuldejornais.com.br/IndexNoticia.asp?idNoticia=113782 | work = Central Sul de Jornais | accessdate = 2014-01-09 | language = pt | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304041854/http://www.centralsuldejornais.com.br/IndexNoticia.asp?idNoticia=113782 | archive-date = 2016-03-04 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>

==联合国会员国==
截至2012年11月，联合国的{{UNnum}}个成员国中共有{{Numrec|Pal|link=N||个（{{Numrec|Pal|link=N|pcent=UN}}）对巴勒斯坦予以承认}}<ref>{{Cite news|author=Staff writers|title=Which countries recognize Palestine already? – interactive|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/interactive/2011/sep/20/palestinain-state-israel-un-interactive|date=2011-09-20|newspaper=The Guardian|accessdate=2011-10-13}}</ref>。下表基于2011年巴勒斯坦加入联合国的运动中[[巴解组织|巴解组织]]<ref name="roadmap"/>以及联合国常驻观察员组织的表格<ref name="unmission" >[http://palestineun.org/about-palestine/diplomatic-relations/ "Diplomatic Relations"]. Permanent Observer of the State of Palestine to the United Nations. Accessed March 2016</ref>。

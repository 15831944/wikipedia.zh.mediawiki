'''[[巴林|巴林]]的官方宗教'''是[[伊斯蘭教|伊斯蘭教]]，大部分巴林公民是穆斯林，佔巴林總人口70.2％。巴林也有大約1000名基督徒公民<ref name="bahchrist">{{cite web|title=The Catholic Church in Bahrain |url=http://www.avona.org/bahrain/bahrain_about.htm|publisher=Catholic Church in Bahrain|accessdate=17 March 2012}}</ref> 和大約40名猶太裔公民<ref name="bahjew">{{cite web|title=Low profile but welcome: a Jewish outpost in the Gulf |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/middle-east/low-profile-but-welcome-a-jewish-outpost-in-the-gulf-398656.html|publisher=Independent|date=2 Nov 2007|accessdate=16 March 2012}}</ref>。巴林穆斯林屬於[[什葉派|什葉派]]和[[遜尼派|遜尼派]]。遜尼派穆斯林沒有官方統計的數字，但什葉派佔巴林穆斯林人口的60-70％。<ref name="fco">{{cite web|title=UK FCO |url=http://www.fco.gov.uk/en/travel-and-living-abroad/travel-advice-by-country/country-profile/middle-east-north-africa/bahrain/|publisher=UK FCO|accessdate=3 March 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.bici.org.bh/ |title=Bahrain Independent Commission of Inquiry |publisher=BICI |date=29 June 2011 |accessdate=26 January 2012}}</ref>巴林的外籍人士絕大多數來自[[南亞|南亞]]和其他鄰近的[[阿拉伯國家|阿拉伯國家]]，在2010年佔巴林人口的54％；其中，45％是穆斯林，55％為非穆斯林，包括來自南印度的基督徒（天主教、基督新教、敘利亞東方正教會和馬爾托馬敘利亞教會）、印度教徒、巴哈伊教徒、佛教徒和錫克教徒。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.state.gov/j/drl/rls/irf/2010_5/168261.htm |title=International Religious Freedom Report |publisher=US State Dept. |date=2011-09-13 |accessdate=2012-03-05}}</ref>

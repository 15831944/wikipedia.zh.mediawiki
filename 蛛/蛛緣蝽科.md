'''蛛緣蝽科'''（學名：{{lang|la|Alydidae}}）是一個[[半翅目|半翅目]][[昆蟲|昆蟲]]的[[科_(生物)|科]]。本科物種與{{tsl|en|Coreidae|緣椿象科}}物種有着密切關係。現時本科包括有至少50個[[属|属]]、 290個[[种_(生物)|物種]]。

分佈於[[温带|温带]]及地區上較溫暖的地區，本科物種主要為[[热带|热带]]及[[亚热带|亚热带]]昆蟲。舉例說：在[[欧洲|欧洲]]本科有10個屬的物種，當中只有2個屬有在較溫暖的[[地中海|地中海]]區域以外出現。

==型態描述==
{{TransH}}
[[File:Alydidae_by_kadavoor.jpg|thumb]]的''Riptortus''屬物種。]]
[[File:DulichusInflatus.jpg|thumb]]
Broad-headed bugs{{r|Yiu 2012}} are up to {{convert|10|-|12|mm|1}} long, and have slender bodies. Some have long and very thin legs. The most notable characteristics of the family are that the head is broad, often similar in length and width to the <!-- [[pronotum|pronotum]] -->[[前胸_(昆蟲)|前胸 (昆蟲)]] and the {{tsl|en|scutellum (entomology)|}}, and that the last <!-- [[antenna_(biology)|antennal]] -->[[觸角|觸角]] segments are elongated and curved. The <!-- [[compound_eye|compound eye]] -->[[複眼|複眼]]s are globular and protruding, and they also have {{tsl|en|ocelli|}}. The <!-- [[arthropod_leg|femora]] -->[[腿_(节肢动物)|腿 (节肢动物)]] of the hindlegs bear several strong spines; the tarsus has three segments. Most species have well-developed <!-- [[hemelytra|hemelytra]] -->[[翅鞘|翅鞘]] (forewings), allowing them to fly well, but in some the hemelytra are vestigial. The membranous part of the hemelytra have several closely spaced long veins.

Alydidae are generally of dusky or blackish coloration. The upperside of the <!-- [[abdomen|abdomen]] -->[[腹|腹]] is usually bright orange-red. this color patch is normally not visible as it is covered by the wings; it can be exposed, perhaps to warn would-be <!-- [[predator|predator]] -->[[捕食|捕食]]s of these animals' noxiousness: They frequently have scent glands that produce a stink considered to be worse than that of true stink bugs (<!-- [[Pentatomidae|Pentatomidae]] -->[[蝽科|蝽科]]). The stink is said to smell similar to a bad case of <!-- [[halitosis|halitosis]] -->[[口臭|口臭]].

Sometimes the adults have  reduced wings. Both, nymphs and adults of some species, such as ''{{tsl|en|Dulichius inflatus|}}'' and ''{{tsl|en|Hyalymenus|}}'' spp. are <!-- [[ant_mimic|ant mimic]] -->[[擬蟻現象|擬蟻現象]]s and live in ant nests.<ref>{{cite journal|title=On the mimetic association between nymphs of ''Hyalymenus'' spp. (Hemiptera: Alydidae) and ants| author=Oliveira PO|year=1985| journal=Zoological Journal of the Linnean Society| volume= 83| issue=4|pages=371–384|doi=10.1111/j.1096-3642.1985.tb01182.x}}</ref>

==棲息地與生態==
這些昆蟲主要棲息於相當{{tsl|en|arid|乾旱氣候|乾旱}}的沙質[[棲息地|棲息地]]，見於海濱、[[石楠荒原|石楠荒原]]、[[干草原|乾草原]]和[[疏林草原|疏林草原]]。牠們的主要以植物的種子為食：牠們會用其{{tsl|en|proboscis|長鼻}}刺穿種子，以飲用其中所含的營養液。部分物種在經濟上是重要的[[害蟲|害蟲]]，例如[[稻|水稻]]上的''{{tsl|en|Leptocorisa oratoria|}}''。

==分類==
本科物種為[[單系群|单源群]]{{r|Li & Zheng}}，可分為三條自然的{{link-en|家系 (生物學)|lineage (evolution)|家系}}。這三條家系原來對應本科的三個族，分別如下{{r|Li & Zheng}}：
* [[蛛缘蝽族|蛛缘蝽族]]（[[Alydini|Alydini]]）、
* [[稻缘蝽族|稻缘蝽族]]（[[Leptocorisini|Leptocorisini]]）和
* [[微翅缘蝽族|微翅缘蝽族]]（[[Micrelytrini|Micrelytrini]]）。

1965年，有兩份文獻對這分類作出不同的修訂：
* Ahmad (1965) {{r|Ahmad 1965}}將三個[[族_(生物)|族]]都提升至[[亚科|亞科級]]，分別為{{r|Li & Zheng |Ahmad 1965|ITIS_108249}}：
** [[蛛缘蝽亚科|蛛缘蝽亚科]] [[Alydinae|Alydinae]]
** [[稻缘蝽亚科|稻缘蝽亚科]] [[Leptocorinae|Leptocorinae]]
** [[微缘蝽亚科|微缘蝽亚科]] [[Microelytrinae|Microelytrinae]]
* 而Schaefer (1965)的提議將本科物種只分為兩個亞科{{r|Schaefer 1965}}：
** 蛛缘蝽亚科不變；
** [[稻缘蝽族|稻缘蝽族]]（[[Leptocorisini|Leptocorisini]]）與[[微翅缘蝽族|微翅缘蝽族]]（[[Micrelytrini|Micrelytrini]]）合組成為微缘蝽亚科。

中國昆蟲學家[[李新正|李新正]]和[[郑乐怡|郑乐怡]]於1993年發表研究，對蛛缘蝽科各個亚群合共16个代表属作[[比较形态学|比较形态学]]研究，結論認同Schaefer (1965)的分類建議才符合研究結果{{r|Li & Zheng}}。其後，Schaefer (1999){{r|Schaefer 1999}}再完善整個分類。不過，Ahmad (1965)的分類仍然有文獻在使用。

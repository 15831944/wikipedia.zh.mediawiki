'''网络广播'''（'''webcast'''）是使用[[流媒体|流媒体]]技术、通过互联网将单个内容源分发给众多同时收听或观看者的媒体呈现方式。网络广播可以是实况或按需分发。基本上，网络广播是通过互联网进行“[[广播|广播]]”。

大型的网络广播平台包括现有的广播电台和电视台（通过在线电视或广播的流媒体[[同步广播|同步广播]]），以及大量仅限于互联网平台的“电台”。网络广播通常包括提供非交互式线性流或事件。权利和许可机构向希望使用受版权保护的材料进行互联网广播的人提供具体的“网络广播许可”。

网络广播在商业部门广泛应用于投资者关系[[簡報|介绍]]（如年度大会）、[[教育技术学|信息化教学]]（传送[[講座|研讨会]]）和相关通信活动。然而，网络会议与{{tsl|en|Web conferencing|网络会议}}不太相同，网络会议通常是为多对多互动而设计。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://web.inxpo.com/casting-calls/bid/112104/Webcasting-101-Planning-and-Executing-High-Quality-Webcasts|title=Webcasting 101: Planning and Executing High Quality Webcasts|accessdate=15 May 2013|date=26 December 2012|last=Shiao|first=Dennis|publisher=INXPO}}</ref>

廉价、易访问的网络广播技术已使[[独立媒体|独立媒体]]蓬勃发展。已有不少引人关注的独立节目通过[[广播|广播]]定期在线播放。这些节目通常由普通市民在家中制作，范围涵盖有关计算机、[[技术|技术]]、新闻等诸多兴趣和主题，并在不断增加新的节目。

网络广播与[[播客|播客]]的不同之处在于，网络广播是直播，而播客是将媒体文件上传、存放到互联网上。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.webmarketcentral.com/webcast_podcast_overview.htm|title=Overview of Webcasting and Podcasting|accessdate=15 May 2013|publisher=WebMarketCentral}}</ref>

== 历史 ==
网络广播本质为通过互联网分发媒体文件。最早的网络广播相当于一个在线的[[音樂會|音樂會]]，最早的一个例子是[[蘋果公司|蘋果公司]]与企业家{{tsl|en|Michael Dorf (entrepreneur)|Michael Dorf}}和{{tsl|en|Andrew Rasiej}}合作的网络广播组。他们与苹果公司的{{tsl|en|David B. Pakman}}在1995年7月17日至22日合作推出了麦金托什纽约音乐节。此活动的音频网络广播由超过15个纽约市的俱乐部合办。{{TransH}}苹果公司之后在1996年6月10日还举办了[[金属乐队|金属乐队]] on June 10, 1996 live from Slim's in San Francisco.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://articles.sfgate.com/1996-05-30/business/17775287_1_xing-technology-technology-startups-fastest-growing-technology|title=Heavy Metal Metallica Plans to Rock Cyberspace: Apple Computer to 'Webcast' band's concert|date=May 30, 1996|publisher=SFGate|location=San Francisco, CA, USA}}</ref>{{TransF}}

1995年，Benford E. Standley制作了历史上第一批音频/视频网络广播（之一）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.digitalmediafestival.com/Benford_E_Standley.htm|title=Benford "Buffalo" Earl Sandley|publisher=Digital Media Festival}}</ref>
{{TransH}}
On October 31, 1996, UK rock band Caduseus broadcast their one-hour concert from 11pm to 12am (UT) at Celtica in Machynlleth, Wales, UK – the first live streamed audio and simultaneous live streamed video multicast – around the globe to more than twenty direct "mirrors" in more than twenty countries.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://downloads.bbc.co.uk/wales/archive/bbc-mid-wales-mystory-caduseus-1996-internet-broadcast.pdf|title=Internet Innovators|date=November 24, 2005|publisher=BBC}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EIBggEZqSW0|title=CADUSEUS – BBC news feature|date=January 12, 2010}}</ref>

In September 1997, Nebraska Public Television started webcasting Big Red Wrap Up from Lincoln, Nebraska which combined highlights from every Cornhusker football game, coverage of the coaches' weekly press conferences, analysis with Nebraska sportswriters, appearances by special guests and questions and answers with viewers.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://scarlet.unl.edu/scarlet/v7n25/v7n25nibs.html|title=Big Red Wrap-Up Now on World Wide Web|date=September 26, 1997|publisher=University of Nebraska-Lincoln|newspaper=Scarlet|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161116135028/http://scarlet.unl.edu/scarlet/v7n25/v7n25nibs.html|archivedate=2016年11月16日}}</ref>
{{TransF}}
1998年8月13日，一般意义上的第一次网络广播婚礼由Alan K'necht与Carrie Silverman在[[加拿大|加拿大]][[多伦多|多伦多]]举办。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.montrealmirror.com/ARCHIVES/1998/122498/august.html|title=Year in reviews August|date=December 25, 1998|publisher=The Montreal Mirror|location=Montreal, CA|accessdate=2008-11-24|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081201181502/http://www.montrealmirror.com/ARCHIVES/1998/122498/august.html|archivedate=2008-12-01|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=2359180177110889920|title=Various TV News Clips|date=August 13, 1998|work=Online|publisher=Various|location=Toronto, CA}}</ref>
{{TransH}}
On October 22, 1998, the first Billy Graham Crusade was broadcast live to a worldwide audience from the Raymond James Stadium in Tampa Florida courtesy of Dale Ficken and the WebcastCenter in Pennsylvania. The live signal was broadcast via satellite to PA, then encoded and streamed via the BGEA website.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.billygraham.org/assets/media/pdfs/development/annualreports/BGEA1998AnnualReport.pdf|title=Occupying Till He Returns|last=Graham|first=Billy|publisher=BillyGraham.org}}</ref>

The first teleconferenced/webcast wedding to date is believed to have occurred on December 31, 1998. Dale Ficken and Lorrie Scarangella wed on this date as they stood in a church in Pennsylvania, and were married by Jerry Falwell while he sat in his office in [[林奇堡_(維吉尼亞州)|林奇堡]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://old.post-gazette.com/regionstate/19981231wed5.asp|title=Hollidaysburg couple plans to be virtually wed on the Web|date=31 December 1998|last=Gibb|first=Tom|publisher=Post-Gazette}}</ref>

Virtually all major broadcasters now have a webcast of their output, from the [[英国广播公司|BBC]] to [[CNN|CNN]] to [[半岛电视台|Al Jazeera]] to [[DWAO-TV|UNTV]] in television to Radio China,  Vatican Radio,<ref>{{Citation|title=Radio|url=http://www.vaticanradio.org/|publisher=Vatican|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131515160700/http://www.vaticanradio.org/|archivedate=2018-04-11}}.</ref> United Nations Radio and the [[英國廣播公司國際頻道|World Service]] in radio.

On November 4, 1994, Stef van der Ziel distributed the first live video images over the web from the Simplon venue in Groningen.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.jet-stream.com/blog/first-webcast/|title=First Webcast|date=November 4, 1994|publisher=Jet-Stream|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120425134352/http://www.jet-stream.com/blog/first-webcast/|archivedate=2012年4月25日}}</ref> On November 7, 1994, [[WXYC|WXYC]], the [[college_radio|college radio]] station of the [[北卡罗来纳大学教堂山分校|University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill]] became the first radio station in the world to broadcast its signal over the internet.<ref>{{Cite news|title=Communications: Picture the scene|last=Grossman|first=Wendy|date=January 26, 1995|work=Online|publisher=''The Guardian''|location=Manchester, United Kingdom|pages=4}}</ref>{{TransF}}

包含[[字幕|字幕]]的翻译版本可以使用[[同步多媒体集成语言|同步多媒体集成语言]]（SMIL）。

== 网络广播 ==
有一类网络广播是对[[婚礼|婚礼]]的广播。<ref name="iht">{{Cite news|url=http://www.iht.com/articles/2007/10/23/business/webwed.php|title=Can't make the ceremony? Watch the wedcast|last=Blanton|first=Kimberly|date=October 22, 2007|publisher=''[[The_International_Herald_Tribune|The International Herald Tribune]]'' / ''[[The_Boston_Globe|The Boston Globe]]''}}</ref><ref name="time">{{cite news|url=http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,1692037,00.html|title=Wedcasting|last=Lee-St. John|first=Jeninne|date=December 6, 2007|publisher=''[[Time_Magazine|Time]]''}}</ref>它使夫妇的家人和朋友可以在互联网上实时观看婚礼。它有时用于在异国举办的婚礼，例如[[坎昆|坎昆]]和{{tsl|en|Riviera Maya}}、[[夏威夷州|夏威夷州]]或[[加勒比地区|加勒比]]，因为其他人一同旅行来亲自参加婚礼是相当昂贵和困难的。<ref name="iht">{{Cite news|url=http://www.iht.com/articles/2007/10/23/business/webwed.php|title=Can't make the ceremony? Watch the wedcast|last=Blanton|first=Kimberly|date=October 22, 2007|publisher=''[[The_International_Herald_Tribune|The International Herald Tribune]]'' / ''[[The_Boston_Globe|The Boston Globe]]''}}</ref>

通过网络广播播放葬礼也是一些殡仪馆提供的服务。虽然它已存在近十年，更便宜的宽带、旅行费用上涨以及对伊拉克和阿富汗的部署都导致了这种现象的增加。<ref>{{Citation|title=Funeral webcasting is alive and well|url=http://spectrum.ieee.org/at-work/innovation/funeral-webcasting-is-alive-and-well|journal=Spectrum|publisher=IEEE}}.</ref>

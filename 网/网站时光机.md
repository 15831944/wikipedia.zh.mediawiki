'''网站时光机'''（{{lang-en|Wayback Machine}}）<ref>{{cite web|title=印度政府突然全國封鎖「Wayback Machine」！事前未發出通知 {{!}} 香港 UNWIRE.HK 玩生活．樂科技|url=https://unwire.hk/2017/08/10/india-bans-wayback-machine/life-tech/|website=香港 UNWIRE.HK 玩生活．樂科技|accessdate=2018-05-03|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170810094653/https://unwire.hk/2017/08/10/india-bans-wayback-machine/life-tech/|archivedate=2017-08-10|language=zh-tw|date=2017-08-10|deadurl=no}}</ref>是[[万维网|万维网]]或[[互联网|互联网]]上传播的其他信息的一个数字[[檔案|档案]]网站，是美国[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[旧金山|旧金山]]的[[非營利組織|非營利組織]]——[[互联网档案馆|互联网档案馆]]最重要的服务之一。

== 历史 ==
[[互联网档案馆|网站时光机]]由[[互联网档案馆|互联网档案馆]]的创始人[[布魯斯特·卡利|Brewster Kahle]]和{{tsl|en|Bruce Gilliat||Bruce Gilliat}}于2001年推出，以解决网站在维护或关闭时无法查看内容的问题<ref>{{Cite journal|title=The Wayback Machine: The Web's Archive|url=|last=Notess|first=Greg R.|date=March–April 2002|journal=Online|volume=26|pages=59–61}}</ref>，此外还能查看[[網頁|网页]]的历史存档版本，创始人Kahle和Gilliat希望以此能为整个互联网提供“普遍获取所有知识”的途径<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://blog.archive.org/2016/10/25/20000-hard-drives-on-a-mission/|title=20,000 Hard Drives on a Mission {{!}} Internet Archive Blogs|work=blog.archive.org|language=en-US|accessdate=2018-10-15|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181020153727/https://blog.archive.org/2016/10/25/20000-hard-drives-on-a-mission/|archivedate=2018-10-20}}</ref>。

''Wayback Machine''这个名称源于动画片''{{tsl|en|The Rocky and Bullwinkle Show||The Rocky and Bullwinkle Show}}中的''“{{tsl|en|WABAC machine||WABAC机器}} ”（发音为''Way-back'' ），这是一个[[时间旅行|时间旅行]]装置<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.businessweek.com/technology/content/feb2002/tc20020228_1080.htm|title=A Library as Big as the World|author=Green|first=Heather|date=2002-02-28|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111220074306/http://www.businessweek.com/technology/content/feb2002/tc20020228_1080.htm|archivedate=2011-12-20|publisher=BusinessWeek}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2002/09/08/business/responsible-party-brewster-kahle-a-library-of-the-web-on-the-web.html|title=Responsible Party – Brewster Kahle; A Library Of the Web, On the Web|author=Tong|first=Judy|date=2002-09-08|work=New York Times|accessdate=2011-08-15|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110220181725/http://www.nytimes.com/2002/09/08/business/responsible-party-brewster-kahle-a-library-of-the-web-on-the-web.html|archivedate=2011-02-20|deadurl=no}}</ref>。在动画片的[[皮巴弟先生_(夢工廠)|皮博迪的不可能的历史]]一集中，角色使用这一机器来见证、参与甚至改变历史上的著名事件<ref>{{cite web|title=Can the Internet Be Archived?|website=The New Yorker|date=2015-01-26|url=https://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2015/01/26/cobweb|access-date=2019-01-23}}</ref>。

网站时光机于1996年开始存档[[缓存|缓存]]网页，目标是在五年后将服务公之于众<ref name="IA: Wayback">{{Cite web|url=https://archive.org/web/|title=Internet Archive: Wayback Machine|work=archive.org|language=en|accessdate=2018-10-15|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140103004344/http://archive.org/web/|archivedate=2014-01-03}}</ref>。从1996年到2001年，信息保存在数字磁带上，Kahle偶尔允许研究人员和科学家使用数据库<ref>{{Cite news|author=Cook|first=John|title=Web site takes you way back in Internet history|url=http://www.seattlepi.com/news/article/Web-site-takes-you-way-back-in-Internet-history-1070534.php|accessdate=2011-08-15|work=Seattle Post-Intelligencer|date=2001-11-01|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140812205508/http://www.seattlepi.com/news/article/Web-site-takes-you-way-back-in-Internet-history-1070534.php|archivedate=2014-08-12}}</ref>。2001年，互联网档案馆成立五周年时，[[加利福尼亞大學柏克萊分校|加州大学伯克利分校]]举行了网站时光机的公布仪式<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Wayback Goes Way Back on Web|url=https://www.wired.com/2001/10/wayback-goes-way-back-on-web/|last=|date=2001-10-28|journal=Wired|accessdate=2017-10-16|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171016225914/https://www.wired.com/2001/10/wayback-goes-way-back-on-web/|archivedate=2017-10-16|deadurl=no}}</ref>。当Wayback Machine推出时，它已经存档了超过100亿个页面<ref name="Arora (2015)">{{Cite journal|title=Using the wayback machine to mine websites in the social sciences: A methodological resource|last=Arora|first=Sanjay K.|last2=Li|first2=Yin|date=2015-05-05|journal=Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology|issue=8|doi=10.1002/asi.23503|volume=67|pages=1904–1915|language=en|issn=2330-1635|last3=Youtie|first3=Jan|last4=Shapira|first4=Philip}}</ref>。

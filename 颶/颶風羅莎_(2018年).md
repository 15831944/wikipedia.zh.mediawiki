'''颶風羅莎'''（{{lang-en|Hurricane Rosa}}），是[[2018年太平洋颶風季|2018年太平洋颶風季]]第十七個被命名的熱帶氣旋、第十個颶風、第七個主要颶風，也是繼[[1997年太平洋颶風季|1997年太平洋颶風季]]的[[1997年颶風諾拉|颶風諾拉]]以來，第一個造成[[下加利福尼亞半島|下加利福尼亞半島]]土石流災情的熱帶氣旋。

這場風暴誕生於9月22日，於墨西哥南部太平洋海面生成，並於9月25日升格為[[熱帶性低氣壓|熱帶性低氣壓]]，同日再升格為[[熱帶風暴|熱帶風暴]]並獲得命名。9月26日晚間，國家颶風中心將其升格為一級颶風。隔日，羅莎的強度迅速增強，於9月28日升格為[[四級颶風|四級颶風]]。9月29日，國家颶風中心將其降格為二級颶風。雖然有些微重新增強，羅莎的強度始終維持在二級颶風等級。9月30日，降格為一級颶風。10月1日，降格為熱帶風暴。10月2日，降格為熱帶性低氣壓，但因已接近陸地，在同時間造成[[下加利福尼亞半島|下加利福尼亞半島]]土石流災情。同日，退化成[[殘留低氣壓|殘留低氣壓]]，並持續向北進入[[美國|美國]][[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]]，為沙漠氣候區帶來可觀降雨。

==氣象歷史==
{{storm path|Rosa 2018 track.png|left}}
[[國家颶風中心|國家颶風中心]]於9月19日首次預測到太平洋海面上可能會生成一低壓區並關注其發展<ref>{{cite report |last1=Blake |first1=Eric |title=NHC Graphical Outlook Archive |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/xgtwo/gtwo_archive.php?current_issuance=201809192048&basin=epac&fdays=5 |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180928200713/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/xgtwo/gtwo_archive.php?current_issuance=201809192048&basin=epac&fdays=5 |archive-date=2018-09-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。9月22日。該低壓區在[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]南方約200英里外的太平洋海面生成<ref>{{cite report |last1=Zelinsky |first1=David |title=NHC Graphical Outlook Archive |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/xgtwo/gtwo_archive.php?current_issuance=201809222324&basin=epac&fdays=5 |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180928200702/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/xgtwo/gtwo_archive.php?current_issuance=201809222324&basin=epac&fdays=5 |archive-date=2018-09-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。9月25日下午，國家颶風中心將其升格為[[熱帶性低氣壓|熱帶性低氣壓]]，給予編號20E<ref>{{cite report |last1=Cangialosi |first1=John |title=Tropical Depression Twenty-E Advisory Number   1 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.001.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180930034019/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.001.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。晚間，國家颶風中心將其升格為熱帶風暴，命名為羅莎（Rosa）<ref>{{cite report |last1=Pasch |first1=Richard |title=Tropical Storm Rosa Advisory Number   2 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.002.shtml? 9月26日晚間，國家颶風中心將其升格為一級颶風，成為[[2018年太平洋颶風季|2018年太平洋颶風季]]的第十個颶風<ref>{{cite report |last1=Roberts |first1=Dave |title=Hurricane Rosa Advisory Number   6 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.006.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180928200753/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.006.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。9月27日晚間，國家颶風中心將其升格為二級颶風<ref>{{cite report |last1=Beven |first1=Jack |title=Hurricane Rosa Advisory Number  10 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.010.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180930035553/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.010.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。9月28日凌晨，國家颶風中心將其升格為三級颶風<ref>{{cite report |last1=Beven |first1=Jack |title=Hurricane Rosa Advisory Number  11 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.011.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180928200751/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.011.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。上午，國家颶風中心將其升格為四級颶風<ref>{{cite report |last1=Blake |first1=Eric |title=Hurricane Rosa Advisory Number  12 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.012.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180929233318/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.012.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-29 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

由於[[眼牆置換|眼牆置換]]與接近高[[風切|風切]]區域，羅莎在維持巔峰強度約六小時後開始減弱，於9月29日凌晨降格為三級颶風<ref>{{cite report |last1=Zelinsky |first1=David |title=Hurricane Rosa Discussion Number  14...Corrected |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.014.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180930035552/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.014.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。隨著眼牆置換持續以及進到海溫較低區域，上午國家颶風中心再將其降格為二級颶風<ref>{{cite report |last1=Blake |first1=Eric |title=Hurricane Rosa Discussion Number  16 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.016.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180930034036/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.016.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。稍晚，完成眼牆置換的羅莎略為增強，但因持續增高的風切影響，始終維持在二級颶風等級<ref>{{cite report |last1=Stewart |first1=Stacy |title=Hurricane Rosa Advisory Number  17 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.017.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180930033935/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.017.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite report |last1=Beven |first1=Jack |title=Hurricane Rosa Advisory Number  18 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.018.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180930035555/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.018.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。9月30日下午，國家颶風中心將其降格為一級颶風<ref>{{cite report |last1=Stewart |first1=Stacy |title=Hurricane Rosa Advisory Number  21 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.021.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181001030930/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.021.shtml |archive-date=2018-10-01 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。隨著風切、低溫海水、與乾空氣的綜合影響，羅莎持續減弱，10月1日上午，國家颶風中心將其降格為熱帶風暴<ref>{{cite report |last1=Stewart |first1=Stacy |title=Hurricane Rosa Discussion Number  21 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.021.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180930233234/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.021.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite report |last1=Pasch |first1=Richard |title=Hurricane Rosa Discussion Number  22 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.022.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-09-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180930231519/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.022.shtml |archive-date=2018-09-30 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite report |last1=Berg |first1=Robbie |title=Tropical Storm Rosa Intermediate Advisory Number 23A |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public_a.023.shtml? 10月2日下午，國家颶風中心其降格為熱帶性低氣壓<ref>{{cite report |last1=Brown |first1=Daniel |title=Tropical Depression Rosa Discussion Number  29 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.029.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-10-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181003014223/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.discus.029.shtml |archive-date=2018-10-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。10月2日上午，國家颶風中心將其降格為[[殘留低氣壓|殘留低氣壓]]<ref>{{cite report |last1=Cangialosi |first1=John |title=Remnants Of Rosa Advisory Number  30 |url=https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.030.shtml? |website=National Hurricane Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-10-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181003013835/https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/archive/2018/ep20/ep202018.public.030.shtml |archive-date=2018-10-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。爾後，向北進入[[美國|美國]][[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]]，為沙漠氣候區帶來可觀降雨，並於隔日逐漸消散<ref>{{cite report |last1=Otto |first1=Richard |title=Storm Summary Number 5 for Heavy Rainfall Associated with Rosa |url=https://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov/storm_summaries/storm29/stormsum_5.html |website=Weather Prediction Center |publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration |accessdate=2018-10-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181003014007/https://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov/storm_summaries/storm29/stormsum_5.html |archive-date=2018-10-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

== 防災措施與影響 ==
=== 墨西哥 ===
羅莎在[[米却肯州|米却肯州]]與[[錫那羅亞州|錫那羅亞州]]帶來可觀降雨，造成12人死亡、8人失蹤<ref>{{cite news|title=Rosa makes landfall in Mexico, threatens US southwest|url=http://www.newindianexpress.com/world/2018/oct/02/rosa-makes-landfall-in-mexico-threatens-us-southwest-1880242.html|location=Mexico City|publisher=New Indian Express|date=2018-10-02|accessdate=2018-10-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181002223520/http://www.newindianexpress.com/world/2018/oct/02/rosa-makes-landfall-in-mexico-threatens-us-southwest-1880242.html|archive-date=2018-10-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>。10月2日，[[索諾拉州|索諾拉州]]的Caborca有一名女性遭洪水沖走<ref name="one death">{{cite news |last1=Breslin |first1=Sean |title=Rosa in the Southwest: Overtaken by Floodwaters, Roads Shut Down; 1 Killed in Mexico {{!}} The Weather Channel |url=https://weather.com/news/news/2018-10-01-tropical-storm-rosa-impacts-southwest-baja-california |website=The Weather Channel |accessdate=2018-10-03 |quote=person reportedly drowned in Mexico after being swept away by floodwaters. |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181003061949/https://weather.com/news/news/2018-10-01-tropical-storm-rosa-impacts-southwest-baja-california |archive-date=2018-10-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

=== 美國 ===

North of downtown Phoenix, an underpass at Interstate 17 and Peoria Avenue was shut down Tuesday morning because of flooding. |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181003061949/https://weather.com/news/news/2018-10-01-tropical-storm-rosa-impacts-southwest-baja-california |archive-date=2018-10-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。羅莎帶給屬於沙漠氣候區域的亞利桑那州許多淹水災情，10月2日為[[鳳凰城|鳳凰城]]帶來歷史性的降雨量、[[尤馬_(亞利桑那州)|尤馬]]也有多處停電<ref>{{cite report|author=Chaffin Mitchell|title=Rosa soaks Baja, southwestern US with heavy downpours|url=https://www.accuweather.com/en/weather-news/photos-flooding-inundates-baja-southwestern-us-as-rosa-brings-heavy-rain/70006227|website=AccuWeather|publisher=AccuWeather|accessdate=2018-10-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181003101551/https://www.accuweather.com/en/weather-news/photos-flooding-inundates-baja-southwestern-us-as-rosa-brings-heavy-rain/70006227|archive-date=2018-10-03|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Breslin |first1=Sean |title=Rosa in the Southwest: Overtaken by Floodwaters, Roads Shut Down; 1 Killed in Mexico {{!}} The Weather Channel |url=https://weather.com/news/news/2018-10-01-tropical-storm-rosa-impacts-southwest-baja-california |website=The Weather Channel |accessdate=2018-10-03 |quote=Thousands of homes and businesses were without power across the Yuma area Sunday afternoon, according to the utility company APS. |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181003061949/https://weather.com/news/news/2018-10-01-tropical-storm-rosa-impacts-southwest-baja-california |archive-date=2018-10-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

'''优红蛱蝶'''（{{lang-xm|Vanessa atalanta}}）是一种中等大小的蝴蝶，翅膀通常为黑色，并有橘色条纹、白色斑点，其蝶翅翼展约2厘米左右<ref>{{cite book |last=Shalaway |first=Scott |title=Butterflies in the Backyard |isbn=0-8117-2695-9 |year=2004 |publisher=Stackpole Books |publicationplace=Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania |page=38}}</ref>。该品种在北非、美洲、欧洲、亚洲、加勒比海、新西兰等气候温和的地方均有分布。<ref name=":0">{{Cite book |url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/9412517 |title=Butterflies East of the Great Plains: An Illustrated Natural History |last1=Opler |first1=Paul A. |last2=Krizek |first2=George O. |year=1984 |publisher=Johns Hopkins University Press |isbn=0801829380 |location=Baltimore |oclc=9412517}}</ref>

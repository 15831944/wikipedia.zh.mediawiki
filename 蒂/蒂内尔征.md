'''Tinel征'''是一种[[神经|神经]]损害的表现。它是通过叩击神经损伤的部位引起“{{tsl|en|Paresthesia|如坐针毡}}”样的刺痛或蚁走感。它的名字来自法国神经学家Jules Tinel（1879-1952）。<ref>Tinel, J., Nerve wounds. London: Baillère, Tindall and Cox, 1917</ref><ref>Tinel, J. (1915) Le signe du fourmillement dans les lésions des nerfs périphériques. Presse médicale, 47, 388-389</ref><ref>Tinel, J. (1978) The "tingling sign" in peripheral nerve lesions (Translated by EB Kaplan). In: M. Spinner M (Ed.), Injuries to the Ma jor Branches of Peripheral Nerves of the Forearm. (2nd ed.) (pp 8-13). Philadelphia: WD Saunders Co</ref>

举个例子，在腕部正中神经被压缩的[[腕隧道症候群|腕隧道症候群]]中，Tinel征通常“积极”的造成[[拇指|拇指]]、[[食指|食指]]、[[中指|中指]]和半边[[无名指|无名指]]刺痛。Tinel征有时也被称为“前端叩击刺痛”或'''DTP'''。Tinel征一般用于帮助判断神经损伤部位以及判断神经修复后的神经纤维再生情况。<ref>{{cite journal|title=At which stage of sensory recovery can a tingling sign be expected? a review and proposal for standardization and grading|pages=298–308|issn=0894-1130|journal=Journal of Hand Therapy: Official Journal of the American Society of Hand Therapists|volume=12|issue=4|date=1999-10|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10622196|accessdate=2019-02-13|author=C. Spicher, G. Kohut, J. Miauton|pmid=10622196}}<!-- Spicher, C., Kohut, G. & Miauton,  J. At which stage of sensory recovery can a tingling sign be expected?

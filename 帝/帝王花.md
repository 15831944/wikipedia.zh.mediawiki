'''國王海神花'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Protea cynaroides''），又稱'''帝王花'''，是[[海神花屬|海神花屬]]的一種植物，廣泛分佈於[[南非|南非]][[凡波斯|凡波斯]]一帶，並為該國國花<ref name="flower">{{cite web |url=http://www.info.gov.za/aboutgovt/symbols/flower.htm |title=About government - National flower |work=South African Government Information |publisher=Government Communications (GCIS) |date=28 November 2007 |accessdate=14 October 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110926212958/http://www.info.gov.za/aboutgovt/symbols/flower.htm |archivedate=26 September 2011}}</ref>。本種花的花蜜可吸引{{link-en|橘胸太阳鸟|Orange-breasted sunbird}}、{{link-en|南方雙領花蜜鳥|Southern double-collared sunbird}}、{{link-en|孔雀石色花蜜鳥|Malachite sunbird}}與{{link-en|卡佛食蜜鳥|Cape Sugarbird}}等鳥類取食，這些鳥類啄食花蜜時會沾黏[[花粉|花粉]]於臉部與[[喙|喙]]上，進而協助花朵[[傳粉|傳粉]]。此外本種花的花蜜亦可吸引多種昆蟲，但牠們協助傳粉的重要性仍不清楚<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.proteaatlas.org.za/pollinat.htm|title=Pollination of Protea|publisher=Protea Atlas Project|access-date=2019-03-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151231233635/http://www.proteaatlas.org.za/pollinat.htm|archive-date=2015-12-31|dead-url=no}}</ref>。國王海神花與許多同屬其他植物皆能適應[[森林大火|森林大火]]頻繁的生長環境，本種屬於{{link-en|再萌蘖者|resprouter}}，在[[森林大火|森林大火]]後可由地下莖長出新芽<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.plantzafrica.com/plantnop/proteacyna.htm |title=Protea cynaroides |first=H.G. |last=Jamieson |publisher=South African National Biodiversity Institute |date=July 2001 |accessdate=14 October 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161210030606/http://www.plantzafrica.com/plantnop/proteacyna.htm |archive-date=2016-12-10 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

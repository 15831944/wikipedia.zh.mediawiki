'''若開民族黨'''（{{lang-my|ရခိုင်အမျိုးသားပါတီ}}）是一個緬甸政黨。該黨代表的是[[建邦|建邦]]和[[仰光地區|仰光地區]]的若開族的利益。該黨成立於2014年1月13日，並在當年的3月6日正式在緬甸聯邦選舉委員會註冊。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.moi.gov.mm/Moi:Eg/announcement/7/03/2014/id-1159 |title=Rakhine National Party allowed as political party | Ministry Of Information |publisher=Moi.gov.mm |date=2014-03-06 |accessdate=2014-04-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140415231404/http://www.moi.gov.mm/Moi%3AEg/announcement/7/03/2014/id-1159 |archivedate=2014-04-15 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.moi.gov.mm/Moi:Eg/announcement/7/03/2014/id-1160 |title=Formation of Rakhine National Party approved | Ministry Of Information |publisher=Moi.gov.mm |date= |accessdate=2014-04-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20140415030437/http://www.moi.gov.mm/Moi:Eg/announcement/7/03/2014/id-1160 |archivedate=2014-04-15 }}</ref><ref name="irrawaddy1">{{cite web|url=http://www.irrawaddy.org/burma/arakanese-political-parties-merge-form-anp.html |title=Arakanese Political Parties Merge to Form ANP |publisher=Irrawaddy.org |date= |accessdate=2014-04-14}}</ref>

==歷史==
2013年6月17日，[[若開民族發展黨|若開民族發展黨]]和[[若開族爭取民主同盟|若開族爭取民主同盟]]簽署了一項協議。按照該協議，這兩個黨會合併爲一個名叫「若開民族黨」的黨派<ref>{{cite news|last=Naw Say Phaw Waa|title=Rakhine parties formalise merger|url=http://www.mmtimes.com/index.php/national-news/7194-rakhine-parties-formalise-merger.html|accessdate=22 June 2013|newspaper=The Myanmar Times|date=21 June 2013}}</ref>。從2013年5月開始，這兩黨爲這次合併做了8個月的準備工作<ref name="irrawaddy1"/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bnionline.net/index.php/news/narinjara/16835-arakan-national-party-receives-official-recognition.html |title=Arakan National Party receives official recognition |publisher=Bnionline.net |date=2014-03-19 |accessdate=2014-04-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140414085654/http://bnionline.net/index.php/news/narinjara/16835-arakan-national-party-receives-official-recognition.html |archivedate=2014-04-14 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Mya |first=Nang |url=http://www.dvb.no/news/newly-formed-rakhine-national-party-appoints-leadership-burma-myanmar/33197 |title=Newly formed Rakhine National Party appoints leadership | DVB Multimedia Group |publisher=Dvb.no |date=2013-10-08 |accessdate=2014-04-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dvb.no/interview/arakan-alliance-burma-myanmar/38244 |title=Arakan alliance | DVB Multimedia Group |publisher=Dvb.no |date=2014-03-09 |accessdate=2014-04-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bnionline.net/index.php/news/narinjara/16854-sittwe-to-host-arakan-national-partys-important-meeting.html |title=Sittwe to host Arakan National Party’s important meeting |publisher=Bnionline.net |date=2014-03-24 |accessdate=2014-04-14 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140414085641/http://bnionline.net/index.php/news/narinjara/16854-sittwe-to-host-arakan-national-partys-important-meeting.html |archivedate=2014-04-14 }}</ref>。

'''若望·保祿二世藏品博物館'''（{{lang-pl|Muzeum Kolekcji im. Jana Pawła II}}）是[[波蘭|波蘭]]首都[[華沙|華沙]]的一座博物館，位於前證券交易所和國家銀行大樓內<ref name=Bedford>{{cite book |author=Neal Bedford |title=Poland |year=2008 |page=94|chapter= |chapterurl= |publisher=Lonely Planet |isbn=1-74104-479-0 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Qe3UzP5I2vsC&pg=PA94&dq=porczynski+stock+exchange&hl=pl&sa=X&ei=WDoAUZHMMsb24QTZqICIDw&ved=0CDIQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=porczynski%20stock%20exchange&f=false |quote=}}</ref>。博物館內收藏有約400件藏品，被認為是華沙最好的歐洲藝術博物館之一，主要收藏展示古典和印象派的美術品<ref name=Salter>{{cite book |author1=Mark Salter |author2=Jonathan Bousfield |title=Poland |year=2002 |pages=105–106|chapter= |chapterurl= |publisher=Rough Guides |isbn=1-85828-849-5 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=YgQ0B1CNYfQC&pg=PA105&lpg=PA105&dq=Kolekcja+im.+Jana+Paw%C5%82a+II&source=bl&ots=ArhX-YoQtI&sig=fGwV7PsmvO0wPkeKElveHGOBNO0&hl=pl&sa=X&ei=15NsUMiMLtHVsgbbzoGoBw&ved=0CDkQ6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=Kolekcja%20im.%20Jana%20Paw%C5%82a%20II&f=false |quote=}}</ref>。

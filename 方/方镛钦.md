'''方镛钦'''（英文名：'''Dennis Fong'''，亦以游戏ID名“Thresh”为人所知，{{bd|1977年|3月11日}}），[[香港|香港]]出生的[[美籍华人|美籍华人]]及[[企业家|企业家]]，前职业玩家<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-119679076.html | title = Entrepreneur Launches His Third Interactive Computer-Gaming Company. | work = San Jose Mercury News | date = 2003-09-01 | accessdate = 2013-01-10 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160409052606/https://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-119679076.html | archive-date = 2016-04-09 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>。作为一个职业玩家，他於1997年的[[雷神之锤系列|雷神之锤]]锦标赛中赢得了[[约翰·卡马克|约翰·卡马克]]的法拉利328轿车，被称为“电子游戏的[[迈克尔·乔丹|迈克尔·乔丹]]”<ref name="salon">{{cite web  | last = Kushner  | first = David  | title = The Michael Jordan of gaming  | publisher = [[Salon.com|Salon.com]]  | date = 2000-09-05  | url = http://archive.salon.com/tech/view/2000/09/05/thresh/index.html  | accessdate = 2013-01-10  | deadurl = yes  | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070624130155/http://archive.salon.com/tech/view/2000/09/05/thresh/index.html  | archivedate = 2007-06-24  }}</ref>，且在{{Tsl|en|ESEA League|ESEA联盟}}的“史上最佳北美电子竞技偶像”排行榜上跻身榜首<ref>{{cite web | title = "Top Ten North American Esports Figures" | url = http://www.esportsea.com/index.php?s=esports&d=content&id=83 | work = ESEA League | date = 2003-03-03 | accessdate = 2013-01-10 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20071101043507/http://www.esportsea.com/index.php?s=esports&d=content&id=83 | archivedate = 2007-11-01 }}</ref>；作为一个企业家，他是{{Tsl|en|Xfire}}、Lithium的创始人之一，并曾入围《[[紅鯡魚_(雜誌)|紅鯡魚]]》（Red Herring）杂志“35岁以下的前20名企业家”排行榜<ref>{{cite web  | title = "Top 20 Entrepreneurs Under 35" in Red Herring Magazine  | publisher = Red Herring, Inc.  | url = http://www.redherring.com/Article.aspx?a=13445&hed=20+Entrepreneurs+Under+35 | archiveurl = http://entrepreneurs.about.com/b/2005/09/18/young-moguls-20-outstanding-entrepreneurs-under-35.htm | archivedate = 2005-09-18 | accessdate = 2013-01-10 }}</ref>。

== 经历 ==
方镛钦于1977年生于香港，於11岁时迁至美国<ref name="salon" />。

他在游戏里早先用的ID是“Threshold of Pain”，以此喻示自己可以承受苦痛与来自敌方的火力。但由于许多游戏都将ID长度限制在8个字符内，而据此将原名截取下前八个字符所得的“Threshol”一名听起来却不怎么酷；考虑到他也很喜欢截取前六个字符所得的“Thresh”（连续击打）一词的意韵，他最终便选用了“Thresh”作为自己的ID<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Professional Gaming Life|url=http://professionalgaminglife.com/1542/pro-gamer-spotlight-dennis-thresh-fong/|title=Pro Gamer Spotlight: Dennis “Thresh” Fong|accessdate=2013-01-10|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121021052251/http://professionalgaminglife.com/1542/pro-gamer-spotlight-dennis-thresh-fong/|archivedate=2012年10月21日|df=}}</ref>。

作为一名职业玩家，他以反应速度、直觉和策略上的优势而著称，为此玩家甚至还创制“Thresh ESP”（Thresh式第六感）一词以形容他对对手的位置与行动的不同寻常的洞察力<ref name="wp">{{cite news | url = http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-srv/WPcap/1999-12/23/093r-122399-idx.html | title = 'Thresh' Rules Online Subculture of Gamers | author = Mark Leibovich | work = Washington Post | page = A1 | date = 1999-12-23 | accessdate = 2013-01-10 }}</ref>，他还曾撰写过《雷神之锤圣经》总结自己的经验<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.quaketerminus.com/quakebible/index.htm|title=Thresh's Quake Bible|author=Dennis "Thresh" Fong | accessdate = 2013-01-10 }}</ref>。

他职业生涯的最高峰是1997年的“Red Annihilation”锦标赛。他和汤姆·基兹梅（Tom Kizmey，游戏ID为“Entrophy”）从众选手中脱颖而出跻身决赛，并在“Castle of the Damned”一关进行决战，结果他以14：-1的比分取得了压倒性的胜利<ref name="mastersdoom">{{cite book | last=Kushner | first=David | title=[[Masters_of_Doom|Masters of Doom: How Two Guys Created An Empire And Transformed Pop Culture]] | publisher=[[Random_House|Random House]] | at=89 | year=2003 | isbn=0-375-50524-5 | accessdate = 2013-01-10 }}</ref> 。

在此之后，凭依职业生涯中所获得的奖金与代言广告的收入（每年约10万美元<ref name="wp" />），他与别人合伙创建了[[GX_Media|GX Media]]公司，并在1999年以从{{Tsl|en|CMGI}}所募集到的1100万美元为资本创建了游戏门户网站Gamers.com<ref>{{cite news | author = Marius Meland | work = Forbes | url = http://www.forbes.com/1999/11/12/mu8.html | title = Thresh plays the portal game | date = 1999-11-12 | accessdate = 2013-01-10 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.sfgate.com/business/article/Video-Game-Champ-Creates-Web-Portal-2889250.php | title = Video Game Champ Creates Web Portal | author = Kelly Zito | work = San Francisco Chronicle | date = 1999-12-15 | accessdate = 2013-01-10 }}</ref>，FiringSquad一站也是他所创建，随后他还创建了{{Tsl|en|Xfire}}公司，专注提供针对玩家需求而设计的即时通讯软件；另外，凭借Accel Partners的投资，他在2007年与他人合伙成立了{{Tsl|en|Raptr}}公司，以向玩家提供社交网络服务与相关软件<ref>{{cite news| url = http://www.g4tv.com/articles/67970/Raptrs-Dennis-Thresh-Fong-Interview/ | title = Raptr's Dennis "Thresh" Fong Interview | author = Jake Gaskill | date = 2009-07-30 | accessdate = 2013-01-10 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = http://pcworld.about.net/news/Feb172004id114813.htm | title = Xfire Brings IM to Gamers | author = Ramon G. McLeod | work = PC World | accessdate = 2013-01-10 }}{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。

'''方颖'''是一位[[女高音|女高音]]歌手<ref name="nyt">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2010/03/05/arts/05iht-madame.html?_r=0|title=China's First Lady of Opera|author=Robert Turnbull|date=2010-03-04|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]}}</ref>。

==生平==
她于2009年获得中国音乐金钟奖金奖，于2013年获得Gerda Lissner国际声乐比赛的一等奖，并于2015年获得林肯中心Segal奖。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chambermusicsociety.org/artists/artist/ying_fang|title=Ying Fang|accessdate=2015-11-13|work=Chamber Music Society of New York}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://artsbeat.blogs.nytimes.com/2015/02/09/lincoln-center-segal-awards-expand-field-of-honorees/|title=Lincoln Center Segal Awards Expand Field of Honorees|author=[[Robin_Pogrebin|Robin Pogrebin]]|date=2015-02-09|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]}}</ref> 她的表演出现在PBS的电视节目《伟大的演出》及大都会剧院高清直播中。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2015/01/16/arts/television/whats-on-tv-friday.html|title=What’s on TV Friday|author=Kathryn Shattuck|date=2015-01-16|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]}}</ref>

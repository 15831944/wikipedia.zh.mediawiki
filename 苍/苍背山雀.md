'''蒼背山雀'''（''Parus cinereus''）是[[山雀屬|山雀屬]]下的一種鳥類，是由原來被當做是[[大山雀|大山雀]]亞種的幾種山雀合併形成的。其特征為白腹灰背，而大山雀則是黃腹綠背<ref name=split>{{cite journal|author=Packert, Martin|author2=Jochen Martens|author3=Siegfried Eck|author4=Alexander A Nazarenko|author5=Olga P. Valchuk|author6=Bernd Petri|author7=Michael Veith |year=2005| title= The great tit (Parus major) – a misclassified ring species|journal= Biological Journal of the Linnean Society  |volume=86 |issue=2| pages=153–174|doi=10.1111/j.1095-8312.2005.00529.x}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Kvist, Laura|author2=Jochen Martens|author3=Hiroyoshi Higuchi|author4=Alexander A Nazarenko|author5=Olga P Valchuk|author6=Markku Orell|last-author-amp=yes |year=2003|title= Evolution and genetic structure of the great tit (Parus major) complex| journal= [[Proceedings_of_the_Royal_Society_B|Proceedings of the Royal Society B]] |volume=270|issue=1523|pages=1447–1454|doi=10.1098/rspb.2002.2321|pmid=12965008|pmc=1691391}}</ref>。它主要分佈于西亞到南亞及東南亞的廣大地區。

==分類==
蒼背山雀是由原來被當做是[[大山雀|大山雀]]亞種的幾種山雀合併形成的<ref name=split/><ref name=fbi>{{cite book|url=https://archive.org/stream/faunaofbritishin01bake#page/73/mode/1up/|pages=73–79|author=Blanford WT|year=1889|publisher=Taylor and Francis, London|title= The Fauna of British India, Including Ceylon and Burma. Birds. Volume 1}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Packert|first=Martin|author2=Jochen Martens|year=2008|title=Taxonomic pitfalls in tits – comments on the Paridae chapter of the Handbook of the Birds of the World|journal=Ibis|volume=154|issue=4|pages=829–831| url = http://www2.mnhn.fr/crbpo/IMG/pdf/tits_Ibis_150_4_.pdf| doi = 10.1111/j.1474-919X.2008.00871.x }}</ref>，其成年體均為白腹灰背，但幼年體與大山雀更為接近<ref name=fbi2>{{cite book|pages=74–78|title= The Fauna of British India, Including Ceylon and Burma. Birds|volume=1| edition=2nd| author=Baker, ECS|url=https://archive.org/stream/birds01bakeiala#page/74/mode/1up/ |publisher=Taylor and Francis, London| year=1924}}</ref>。不同地區之間的蒼背山雀背部灰色有差異，此外體型大小和尾巴上的白色部分也有差別。不過這主要是環境原因而導致的漸變。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://archive.org/stream/novitateszoologi12lond#page/497/mode/1up| author=Hartert, Ernst| year=1905| title= Miscellanea Ornithologica; Critical, nomenclatorial, and other notes, mostly on Palaearctic birds and their allies.

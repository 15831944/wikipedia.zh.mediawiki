'''深海蟹属'''（{{lang|la|''Hypothalassia''}}）是[[螃蟹|螃蟹]]的一个[[属|属]]，生长于[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]和[[日本|日本]]（[[房总半島|房总半島]]以南到[[九州|九州]]沿岸）的[[温带|温带]]和[[热带|热带]]50-100m深的海底。
现时有两个品种<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Encyclopedia_of_Life|Encyclopedia of Life]] |url=http://eol.org/pages/4260380/entries/29178872/overview |title=''Hypothalassia'' |accessdate=2011-12-03 |language=en }}</ref><ref>{{Cite WoRMS |author=Peter Davie |year=2010 |title=''Hypothalassia'' Gistel, 1848 |id=439403 |accessdate=2011-12-03 |language=en }}</ref>：
*[[香槟蟹|香槟蟹]] ''Hypothalassia acerba'' Koh & Ng, 2000<ref name="Poore">{{cite book |author=Gary C. B. Poore & Shane T. Ahyong |year=2004 |title=Marine decapod Crustacea of southern Australia: a guide to identification |publisher=[[CSIRO_Publishing|CSIRO Publishing]] |isbn=978-0-643-06906-0 |chapter=''Hypothalassia'' Gistel, 1848 |page=444 |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=ZZWnuGc0xlMC&pg=PA444}}</ref>
*[[武裝深海蟹|武裝深海蟹]] ''Hypothalassia armata'' (De Haan, 1835)
但[[NCBI|NCBI]]認為以上兩個品種其實是同一個品種<ref>{{NCBI|id=652083|''Hypothalassiidae'' }}</ref>。

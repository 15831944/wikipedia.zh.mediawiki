'''深静脉血栓'''是在[[深静脉|深静脉]]形成的[[血栓|血栓]]，常形成于[[下肢|下肢]]或[[骨盆|骨盆]]部位深处的静脉。有时也形成于[[上肢|上肢]]的静脉（这被称为{{Link-en|Paget-Schrötter综合症|Paget–Schroetter disease}}）。

== 诱因 ==
近期接受[[外科手术|外科手术]]、[[住院|住院]]<ref name="pmid17646600">{{cite journal |author=Spencer FA, Lessard D, Emery C, Reed G, Goldberg RJ |title=Venous thromboembolism in the outpatient setting |journal=Arch. Intern. Med. |volume=167 |issue=14 |pages=1471–5 |year=2007 |pmid=17646600 |doi=10.1001/archinte.167.14.1471 |pmc=2762787}}</ref><ref name="ReferenceA">Bulger CM, Jacobs C, Patel NH Epidemiology of acute deep vein thrombosis.Tech Vasc Interv Radiol. 2004 Jun;7(2):50-4. PMID 15252760</ref>、长期缺乏活动（例如在长程航班中<ref name="ReferenceA"/><ref>{{cite journal | author = Tsai A, Cushman M, Rosamond W, Heckbert S, Polak J, Folsom A | title = Cardiovascular risk factors and venous thromboembolism incidence: the longitudinal investigation of thromboembolism etiology | journal = Arch Intern Med | volume = 162 | issue = 10 | pages = 1182–9 | year = 2002 | pmid = 12020191 | doi = 10.1001/archinte.162.10.1182}}</ref>）、[[吸烟|吸烟]]、[[肥胖|肥胖]]、年长、服用特定药物（例如[[雌性激素|雌性激素]]或[[促红细胞生成素|促红细胞生成素]]）以及先天的[[血栓形成倾向|血栓形成倾向]]等因素会提高形成深静脉血栓的风险。服用[[复合口服避孕药|复合口服避孕药]]或处于[[产后护理|产后护理]]期的妇女由于雌性激素水平上升而有更高的风险形成深静脉血栓。

=== 旅行血栓症 ===
旅行血栓症<ref name="pmid11565823 ">{{cite journal |author=Bagshaw M|title=Traveller's thrombosis: a review of deep vein thrombosis associated with travel. The Air Transport Medicine Committee, Aerospace Medical Association. |journal=Aviation, space, and environmental medicine |volume=72 |issue=9 |pages=848–851 |year=2001 |pmid=11565823}}</ref>是指旅行者形成深静脉血栓。<ref name="pmid17351849">{{cite journal |author=Philbrick JT, Shumate R, Siadaty MS, Becker DM |title=Air travel and venous thromboembolism: a systematic review |journal=Journal of general internal medicine : official journal of the Society for Research and Education in Primary Care Internal Medicine |volume=22 |issue=1 |pages=107–14 |year=2007 |pmid=17351849 |pmc=1824715 |doi=10.1007/s11606-006-0016-0}}</ref>也有使用“'''经济舱综合征'''”或「'''經濟客位綜合征'''」来描述这个症状。<ref>{{cite journal | pmid = 2900413 | last1 = Cruickshank  | author-separator =, | first1 = JM | author-name-separator=  | last2 = Gorlin | first2 = R | last3 = Jennett | first3 = B | title = Air travel and thrombotic episodes: the economy class syndrome. | journal = Lancet| volume=2 | issue=8609 | pages=497–8|date=August 1988}}</ref>乘坐[[飞机|飞机]]远途旅行的人通常易患旅行血栓症。深静脉血栓可以引起[[肺栓塞|肺栓塞]]这一严重并发症。

旅行者形成血栓可能是缺乏活动、[[脱水|脱水]]和隐藏因素的共同作用。在飞行途中的环境因素也可能与此有关。<ref name="pmid11089830">{{cite journal |author=Bendz B, Rostrup M, Sevre K, Andersen TO, Sandset PM |title=Association between acute hypobaric hypoxia and activation of coagulation in human beings |journal=Lancet |volume=356 |issue=9242 |pages=1657–8 |year=2000 |pmid=11089830 |doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(00)03165-2}}</ref><ref>[http://www.ispub.com/ostia/index.php?xmlFilePath=journals/ijpm/vol3n2/aero2.xml]</ref>尽管和飞行有关系，但这个问题是和缺乏活动联系在一起的，所以乘坐巴士、火车和汽车的旅行者也有同样的风险。<ref name="pmid10027445">{{cite journal |doi=10.1378/chest.115.2.440 |author=Ferrari E, Chevallier T, Chapelier A, Baudouy M |title=Travel as a risk factor for venous thromboembolic disease: a case-control study |journal=Chest |volume=115 |issue=2|pages=440–4 |year=1999 |pmid=10027445}}</ref>

患有易形成血栓疾病（如[[抗磷脂综合征|抗磷脂综合征]]或[[癌症|癌症]]）的人患病风险会高出很多。风险最高的人群包括老人，有严重疾病者，例如癌症的病人、最近接受矫形外科手术（腿或膝盖）的人以及孕妇。<ref name="pmid18574271">{{cite journal |doi=10.1378/chest.08-0656 |author=Geerts WH, Bergqvist D, Pineo GF, Heit JA, Samama CM, Lassen MR, Colwell CW; American College of Chest Physicians |title=Prevention of venous thromboembolism: American College of Chest Physicians Evidence-Based Clinical Practice Guidelines (8th Edition)|journal=Chest |volume=133 |issue=6 Suppl|pages=381S–453S |year=2008 |pmid=18574271}}</ref>一些研究者认为耐力类运动员也是高危人群。<ref name="PMID14980135">{{cite journal |author=Meyering C, Howard T |title=Hypercoagulability in athletes |journal=Curr Sports Med Rep |volume=3 |issue=2|pages=77–83 |year=2004 |pmid=14980135}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | author=Harvey T, Reynolds P | title = Athletics, Fitness | work = | publisher = Airhealth.org | date = | url = http://www.airhealth.org/athletes.html | doi = | accessdate = February 3, 2010 }}</ref>

世界卫生组织的WRIGHT（World Health Organisation Research Into Global Hazards of Travel）项目研究了旅行和静脉血栓（包括深静脉血栓和/或[[肺栓塞|肺栓塞]]）的关系。<ref>{{cite journal |author=World health organization|title=WHO Research Into Global Hazards of Travel (WRIGHT) Project : final report of phase I|year=2007 }}</ref>据该项目报告，形成静脉血栓的风险大约在乘坐长程航班（大于4小时）后翻倍。其他让旅行者长时间保持坐着不动的旅行方式也会增加风险。风险随着旅行时间和其他旅行者具有的静脉血栓风险因素增长。

== 症状 ==
深静脉血栓出现时可能没有[[症状|症状]]，但多数情况下四肢会疼痛、肿大、发红、发热，可能使[[浅静脉|浅静脉]]胀大。深静脉血栓可能引起的最严重的并发症是血栓分裂流入肺部引发的[[肺栓塞|肺栓塞]]。未经治疗的下肢深静脉血栓引发肺栓塞而导致死亡的概率是3%。由上肢深静脉血栓导致的死亡非常罕见。<ref>{{cite web | author =. Turpie AGG | date = March 2008 | title = Deep Venous Thrombosis |work=The Merck's Manuals Online Medical Library | url = http://www.merck.com/mmpe/sec07/ch081/ch081b.html?qt=dvt&alt=sh#sec07-ch081-ch081b-1775}}</ref>

== 診斷 ==
常用血液[[D-二聚体|D-二聚体]]检测和对静脉的[[医学超声检查#.E5.A4.9A.E6.99.AE.E5.8B.92.E8.B6.85.E5.A3.B0|多普勒超声]]检查诊断深静脉血栓。有时，深静脉血栓的诱因需要进一步检查。

== 预防 ==
=== 旅行者 ===
有证据显示在旅行时穿压力袜或[[紧身衣|紧身衣]]可以减少在长程航班上形成深静脉血栓的情况。2001年的一个随机对照研究比较了2组长程航班乘客，其中一组穿紧身袜类而另一组不穿，并对所有那些乘客进行扫描和血液测试以检查深静脉血栓情况。结果显示10%没有穿紧身袜类的乘客出现了无症状的深静脉血栓。而穿了紧身袜类的一组没有出现深静脉血栓。研究者由此认为，穿弹性紧身袜类可以减少长程航班旅客出现深静脉血栓情况。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Scurr JH, Machin SJ, Bailey-King S, Mackie IJ, McDonald S, Smith PD |title=Frequency and prevention of symptomless deep-vein thrombosis in long-haul flights: a randomised trial |journal=Lancet |volume=357 |issue=9267 |pages=1485–9 |pmid=11377600 |doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(00)04645-6 |url=|date=May 2001}}</ref>

其它预防措施包括摄取足够水分（饮水，尽量不饮用含[[酒精|酒精]]和[[咖啡因|咖啡因]]饮料）、走动和活动小腿肌肉。<ref name="pmid17351849"/>有风险因素的旅行者应该寻求医疗建议并考虑预防性治疗。

坐在靠窗座位的飞机乘客，因為較不易起身走動，出现深静脉血栓的风险更大。<ref>{{cite journal|last=Schreijer|first=Anja J. M.|coauthors=Suzanne C. Cannegieter, Carine J. M. Doggen, Frits R. Rosendaal|date=Nov 25, 2008|title=The effect of flight-related behaviour on the risk of venous thrombosis after air travel|journal=British Journal of Haematology|volume=144|issue=3|pages=425–429|pmid=19036084|doi=10.1111/j.1365-2141.2008.07489.x}}</ref>

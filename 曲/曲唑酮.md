<!-- 定義及症狀 -->
'''曲唑酮'''（{{lang-en|Trazodone}}）是一種口服[[抗抑鬱藥|抗抑鬱藥]]<ref name=AHFS2018>{{cite web|title=Trazodone Hydrochloride|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/trazodone-hydrochloride.html|publisher=The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|accessdate= 8 January 2018}}</ref>，用來治療[[重性抑鬱障礙|重性抑鬱障礙]]、[[焦慮症|焦慮症]]，也配合其他藥品來治療[[酒精依賴|酒精依賴]]<ref name=AHFS2018/>。Trazodone以多種商品名行銷於世界各地<ref name=genericnames>[https://www.drugs.com/international/trazodone.html Drugs.com international listings for Trazodone] Page accessed Feb 10, 2016</ref><ref name=AHFS2018>{{cite web|title=Trazodone Hydrochloride|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/trazodone-hydrochloride.html|publisher=The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|accessdate= 8 January 2018}}</ref>。

<!-- 副作用及藥理機制 -->
常見的副作用包含[[口乾|口乾]]、[[頭昏|頭昏]]、[[嘔吐|嘔吐]]和[[頭痛|頭痛]]<ref name=AHFS2018/>，較為嚴重的副作用為：[[自殺|自殺]]、躁狂導致之雙極性精神症狀、[[心律不整|心律不整]]、[[陰莖異常勃起|陰莖異常勃起]]<ref name=AHFS2018/>。目前還不清楚在[[懷孕|懷孕]]或[[哺乳|哺乳]]期間使用是否安全<ref>{{cite web|title=Trazodone Use During Pregnancy|url=https://www.drugs.com/pregnancy/trazodone.html|website=Drugs.com|accessdate=7 January 2018}}</ref>。該藥物品屬於{{le|苯基哌嗪|phenylpiperazine}}（phenylpiperazine）類藥物，為一種{{le|血清素拮抗劑和再攝取抑制劑|Serotonin antagonist and reuptake inhibitor}}（SARI）<ref>{{cite book|last1=Stahl|first1=Stephen M.|title=Stahl's Essential Psychopharmacology: Neuroscientific Basis and Practical Applications|date=2008|publisher=Cambridge University Press|isbn=9780521857024|page=567|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=cWbYxSfKN3cC&pg=PA567|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Lemke|first1=Thomas L.|last2=Williams|first2=David A.|title=Foye's Principles of Medicinal Chemistry|date=2008|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9780781768795|page=586|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=R0W1ErpsQpkC&pg=PA586|language=en}}</ref>，Trazodone還具有鎮靜的副作用<ref name=BNF69>{{cite book|title=British national formulary : BNF 69|date=2015|publisher=British Medical Association|isbn=9780857111562|pages=257–258|edition=69}}</ref>。

<!-- 安全性及文化 -->
該藥品於1981年在美國批准用於醫療用途<ref name=AHFS2018/>，現已有[[通用名藥物|通用名藥物]]上市<ref name=AHFS2018/>。截至2019年，每月劑量在英國公醫（NHS）的採購藥價約7.46英鎊<ref name=BNF76>{{cite book|title=British national formulary : BNF 76|date=2018|publisher=Pharmaceutical Press|isbn=9780857113382|pages=367|edition=76}}</ref>。2018年，美國的每月劑量批發價約4.53美元<ref>{{cite web|title=NADAC as of 2018-01-03|url=https://data.medicaid.gov/Drug-Pricing-and-Payment/NADAC-as-of-2018-01-03/v54n-gpyk|website=Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services|accessdate=7 January 2018|language=en}}</ref>。2016年，它是美國第24大處方用藥，單年處方超過2500萬筆 <ref>{{cite web |title=The Top 300 of 2019 |url=https://clincalc.com/DrugStats/Top300Drugs.aspx |website=clincalc.com |accessdate=22 December 2018}}</ref>。

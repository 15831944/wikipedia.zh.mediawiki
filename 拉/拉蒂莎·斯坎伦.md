'''拉蒂莎·斯坎伦'''（{{lang-en|Laetisha Scanlan}}，{{bd|1990年|4月13日||}}）生于维多利亚州墨尔本，是一名澳大利亚女子射击运动员，主攻飞碟射击，毕业于[[蒙纳士大学|蒙纳士大学]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.monash.edu/__data/assets/pdf_file/0019/816400/2016-annual-report-final-web.pdf|title=Sport in Monash Annual Report 2016|work=Monash University|accessdate=2018-11-19|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171129190606/https://www.monash.edu/__data/assets/pdf_file/0019/816400/2016-annual-report-final-web.pdf|archivedate=2017-11-29}}</ref>。她在4岁时开始练习[[空手道|空手道]]，到14岁时，她已经拥有黑带<ref>{{cite web|url=https://results.gc2018.com/en/shooting/athlete-profile-n6030472-laetisha-scanlan.htm|title=Shooting {{!}} Athlete Profile: Laetisha SCANLAN|work=Gold Coast 2018|accessdate=2018-11-19|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20181119075230/https://results.gc2018.com/en/shooting/athlete-profile-n6030472-laetisha-scanlan.htm|archivedate=2018-11-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>。2005年，她受其父亲影响开始练习飞碟射击<ref>{{cite web|url=https://healthhq.defencehealth.com.au/2016/08/31/fifth-place-rio-defence-healths-laetisha-scanlan/|title=Fifth place in Rio for Defence Health's Laetisha Scanlan|date=2016-08-31|accessdate=2018-11-19|publisher=Defence Health|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180401185152/https://healthhq.defencehealth.com.au/2016/08/31/fifth-place-rio-defence-healths-laetisha-scanlan/|archivedate=2018-04-01}}</ref>，2007年，她首次参加国际射击比赛。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.beretta.com/en/world-of-beretta/team-beretta/laetisha-scanlan/|title=Laetisha Scanlan - Trap|work=Beretta|accessdate=2018-11-19|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170718014125/http://www.beretta.com/en/world-of-beretta/team-beretta/laetisha-scanlan/|archivedate=2017-07-18}}</ref>

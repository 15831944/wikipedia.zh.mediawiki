'''拉吉庫馬·拉奧'''（{{lang-en|'''Rajkummar Rao'''}}，{{bd|1984年|8月31日|catIdx=Rao, Rajkummar}}）<ref>[https://www.news18.com/news/movies/happy-birthday-rajkummar-rao-10-times-when-the-actor-proved-that-hes-here-to-stay-1862971.html “Happy Birthday Rajkummar Rao: 10 Times When The Actor Proved That He's Here to Stay”]. News 18. 31-08-2018.</ref>是一名[[印度|印度]][[宝莱坞|宝莱坞]]男演員。他获得了一个[[印度國家電影獎|印度國家電影獎]]奖项和两个[[印度電影觀眾獎|印度電影觀眾獎]]荣誉。<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/rajkummar-rao-films-career-stree-533404/ “Decoding Rajkummar Rao’s rise in Bollywood”]. The Indian Express. 31-08-2018.</ref>

拉吉庫馬·拉奧的出道电影为《[[斷線人生|斷線人生]]》。之后他拍摄了多部成功的电影，包括：《Shahid》、《Queen》、《Aligarh》、《Bareilly Ki Barfi》等。<ref>[https://www.ndtv.com/entertainment/rajkummar-raos-bollywood-journey-so-far-these-four-films-shaped-his-career-1909478 “Rajkummar Rao's Bollywood Journey So Far. These Four Films Shaped His Career”]. NDTV. 31-08-2018.</ref>

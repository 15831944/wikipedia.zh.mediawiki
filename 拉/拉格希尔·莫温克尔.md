'''拉格希尔·莫温克尔'''（{{lang-no|Ragnhild Mowinckel}}，{{bd|1992年|9月12日||}}）生于默勒-鲁姆斯达尔郡莫尔德<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rbnett.no/sport/2017/10/27/N%C3%A5-skal-Ragnhild-p%C3%A5-pallen-15518288.ece|script-title=no:Nå skal Ragnhild på pallen|website=rbnett.no|date=2017-10-27|accessdate=2019-01-09}}</ref>，是一名挪威女子高山滑雪运动员，主攻项目包括大曲道、超大曲道、全能和滑降，所属俱乐部是SK Rival<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.aftenposten.no/100Sport/vintersport/Gjorde-n-forandring-Da-ble-hun-plutselig-medaljekandidat-i-OL-248932b.html|script-title=no:Gjorde én forandring. Da ble hun plutselig medaljekandidat i OL.|work={{lang|no|Aftenposten}}|date=2018-01-24|accessdate=2019-01-09}}</ref>。她的哥哥同样也是一名高山滑雪运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://via.tt.se/pressmeddelande/mowinckel-satsar-pa-forsta-vm-pallen-jag-alskar-are?publisherId=2794146&releaseId=3246283|script-title=no:Mowinckel satsar på första VM-pallen: "Jag älskar Åre"|website=via.tt.se|date=2018-12-06|accessdate=2019-01-09}}</ref>。她在一岁的时候便已开始接触滑雪，当时她的哥哥正在练滑雪，她的父母也让她在很早的时候接触这项运动。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/alpine-skiing/athlete-profile-n3025422-ragnhild-mowinckel.htm|title=Alpine Skiing {{!}} Athlete Profile: Ragnhild MOWINCKEL|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-09|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180207004116/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/alpine-skiing/athlete-profile-n3025422-ragnhild-mowinckel.htm|archivedate=2018-02-07}}</ref>

'''《拉薩魔術師》'''（{{lang-en|''The Magician of Lhasa''}}）是大衛·米基的所寫的小說，情節以雙線進行。<ref name="PRWEB Tibet">{{Cite web|url=http://www.prweb.com/releases/2010/02/prweb3563494.htm|title=The Book the Chinese Government Doesn't Want You To Read|publisher=PRWEB|date=February 9, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=My favorite book of the moment: A review of 'The Magician of Lhasa' by David Mitchie|url=http://www.tahlianewland.com/my-favorite-book-of-the-moment-a-review-of-the-magician-of-lhasa-by-david-mitchie/|work=Tahlia Newland|date=2015-07-27|accessdate=2019-04-07|language=en-AU}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Book Review: The Magician of Lhasa|url=https://preciousmetal.wordpress.com/2010/03/17/book-review-the-magician-of-lhasa/|work=Precious Metal: the blog|date=2010-03-17|accessdate=2019-04-07|language=en|first=Nate|last=DeMontigny}}</ref> 第一條線設於六十年代，一位少年僧人身懷秘密任務，負責運送一個古老而不為人知的宗教秘密到鄰近的印度；第二條線則設現代，一位倫敦科學家的研究，突然被一間美國公司所接收，引發一連串明爭暗鬥。故事在這兩條線中穿插進行。此書將現代物理學及古代佛教哲理結合，在宗教與科學之中找到共同點，獲得不少好評。<ref name="PRWEB Physics">{{Cite web|url=http://www.prweb.com/releases/2010/01/prweb3521914.htm|title=Geeks and Buddhists To Collide This Week|publisher=PRWEB|date=January 28, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=a book review by John F. McDonald: The Magician of Lhasa|url=https://www.nyjournalofbooks.com/book-review/magician-lhasa|work=www.nyjournalofbooks.com|accessdate=2019-04-07}}</ref>

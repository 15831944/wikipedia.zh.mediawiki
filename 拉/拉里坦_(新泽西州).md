'''拉里坦'''（{{lang-en|Raritan}}）是美国[[新泽西州|新泽西州]][[萨默塞特县_(新泽西州)|萨默塞特县]]的一个城镇，在[[2010年美國人口普查|2010年美國人口普查]]中的人口为6,881人<ref name="Census2010">[http://factfinder.census.gov/bkmk/table/1.0/en/DEC/10_DP/DPDP1/0600000US3403561980 DP-1 - Profile of General Population and Housing Characteristics: 2010 for Raritan borough, Somerset County, New Jersey], [[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]. </ref><ref name="Districts2011">[http://www.njelections.org/2011-legislative-districts/towns-district.pdf#page=10 Municipalities Grouped by 2011-2020 Legislative Districts], New Jersey Department of State, p. 10. </ref><ref name="LWD2010">[http://lwd.dol.state.nj.us/labor/lpa/census/2010/dp/dp1_som/raritan1.pdf Profile of General Demographic Characteristics: 2010 for Raritan borough] {{Wayback|url=http://lwd.dol.state.nj.us/labor/lpa/census/2010/dp/dp1_som/raritan1.pdf |date=20140720190935 }}, New Jersey Department of Labor and Workforce Development. </ref>。

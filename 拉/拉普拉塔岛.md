'''拉普拉塔岛'''（{{lang-es|Isla de la Plata}}<ref>{{cite web|title=Isla de la Plata|language=es |author=Eduardo Almeida |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080325080659/http://www.dibam.cl/descarga_rec.asp?id_recurso=46 |accessdate=2008-03-25}}</ref>，又称'''银岛'''）是位于[[厄瓜多尔|厄瓜多尔]][[马纳比省|马纳比省]]太平洋海岸附近的一个小岛，是[[馬查利亞國家公園|馬查利亞國家公園]]的一部分。离该岛最近的城市是[[洛佩斯港|洛佩斯港]]。<ref name="guardian">{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/travel/2010/nov/27/ecuador-wildlife-poor-mans-galapagos|title=Isla de la Plata - Ecuador's other Galapagos|first=Sarah|last=Gilbert|date=2010-11-27|website=the Guardian}}</ref>该岛得名于传说中[[法蘭西斯·德瑞克|法蘭西斯·德瑞克]]船长在这里埋藏的宝藏<ref name=sen>{{cite web |title=El Parque Nacional Machalilla |url=http://www.exploringecuador.com/espanol/sp_ar_machallilla_parque.htm |accessdate=2018-11-28 |website=exploringecuador.com|language=es}}</ref>。

拉普拉塔岛上的动物种类非常丰富<ref>{{cite book|author=Amy E. Robertson|title=Moon Volunteer Vacations in Latin America|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SfsUAgAAQBAJ&pg=PT240|date=2013-11-19|publisher=Avalon Publishing|isbn=978-1-61238-642-3|pages=240}}</ref>，包括[[藍腳鰹鳥|藍腳鰹鳥]]、[[红脚鲣鸟|红脚鲣鸟]]、[[橙嘴蓝脸鲣鸟|橙嘴蓝脸鲣鸟]]<ref name="RestallFreile2018">{{cite book|author1=Robin Restall|author2=Juan Freile|title=Birds of Ecuador|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=GNo3DwAAQBAJ&pg=PA64|date=2018-03-08|publisher=Bloomsbury Publishing|isbn=978-1-4729-2565-7|pages=64–}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|author=Andrés Vásquez Noboa|title=Wildlife of Ecuador: A Photographic Field Guide to Birds, Mammals, Reptiles, and Amphibians|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=U2bYDQAAQBAJ&pg=PA78|date=2017-05-30|publisher=Princeton University Press|isbn=978-1-4008-8505-3|pages=78}}</ref>、[[南海狮|南海狮]]<ref>{{cite book|author1=Ronald M. Nowak|author2=Ernest Pillsbury Walker|title=Walker's Marine Mammals of the World|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=89ybgDBCYKoC&pg=PA16|date=2003-05-28|publisher=JHU Press|isbn=978-0-8018-7343-0|pages=16}}</ref>和[[座头鲸|座头鲸]]<ref name=acre/>。该岛也是[[潜水|潜水]]爱好者最受欢迎的地方之一<ref name=sen/>。

岛上还有[[印加帝國|印加帝國]]时期的遗迹<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/?id=5lGWwZ4tboIC&pg=PA311&lpg=PA311&dq=%22mollo+culture%22#v=onepage&q=%22mollo%20culture%22&f=false |title=Ancient South America - Karen Olsen Bruhns - Google Books |publisher=Books.google.co.uk |date=1994-08-04|accessdate=2014-04-19|isbn=9780521277617 }}</ref>。

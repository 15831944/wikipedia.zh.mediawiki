《'''赌城风云'''》（{{lang-en|Vegas}}，又译：'''拉斯维加斯往事'''）是一部由[[尼古拉斯·派勒吉|尼古拉斯·派勒吉]]和Greg Walker创作的美国[[時代劇|時代]][[电视剧|电视剧]]，于2012年9月25日起在[[哥倫比亞廣播公司|CBS]]播出<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2012/07/11/cbs-announces-2012-2013-premiere-dates-395400/20120711cbs01/|title=CBS Announces 2012-2013 Premiere Dates|date=July 11, 2012|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=July 21, 2012}}</ref>，本片将由[[Dennis_Quaid|Dennis Quaid]]和[[Michael_Chiklis|Michael Chiklis]]担任主角<ref>{{cite web|url=http://blog.zap2it.com/frominsidethebox/2012/05/vegas-can-dennis-quaid-and-michael-chiklis-hit-the-jackpot-for-cbs.html|title='Vegas': Can Dennis Quaid and Michael Chiklis hit the jackpot for CBS?|last=Eckstein|first=David|work=Zap2It|date=May 16, 2012|accessdate=May 29, 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6BoQ7AkU7?url=http://blog.zap2it.com/frominsidethebox/2012/05/vegas-can-dennis-quaid-and-michael-chiklis-hit-the-jackpot-for-cbs.html|archivedate=2012年10月31日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/cbs-upfronts-dennis-quaids-vegas-elementary-friend-me-323717|title=CBS Orders Dennis Quaid's 'Vegas,' 'Elementary,' 'Friend Me,' Four More|last=Rose|first=Lacey|work=The Hollywood Reporter|date=May 13, 2012|accessdate=May 29, 2012}}</ref>。

2012年10月23日官方宣布预订全季<ref name="full seasons">{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/10/23/vegas-elementary-get-full-season-orders-from-cbs/154441|title='Vegas' & 'Elementary' Get Full Season Orders From CBS|last=Kondolojy|first=Amanda|work=TV by the Numbers|date=October 23, 2012|accessdate=October 23, 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131104155337/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/10/23/vegas-elementary-get-full-season-orders-from-cbs/154441/|archivedate=2013年11月4日}}</ref>，然而在2012年11月15日CBS宣布在之前的订单下削减一集使得第一季缩减至21集<ref name="Elementary Vegas Revises Episode Orders">{{cite web|title='Guys With Kids' and 'Elementary' get more episodes, 'Vegas' cut by one|url=http://blog.zap2it.com/frominsidethebox/2012/11/guys-with-kids-and-elementary-get-more-episodes-vegas-cut-by-one.html|accessdate=15 November 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130119091633/http://blog.zap2it.com/frominsidethebox/2012/11/guys-with-kids-and-elementary-get-more-episodes-vegas-cut-by-one.html|archivedate=2013年1月19日}}</ref>。2013年5月10日，CBS在第一季後取消本劇。<ref>{{Cite web |url = https://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/broadcast/vegas-cancelled-by-cbs/182067/ |title = ‘Vegas’ Canceled by CBS After One Season |date = 2013-05-10 |publisher = TV by the Numbers |language = en}}</ref>

'''拉根水壩'''（{{lang-en|'''Laggan Dam'''}}）是一座位於[[蘇格蘭高地|蘇格蘭高地]]，[[拉根湖|拉根湖]]西南方及[[斯平河|斯平河]]的水壩。水壩長約{{convert|700|ft}}<ref>{{cite journal |author=International Commission on Large Dams |title=Transactions |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=oIUPAQAAIAAJ |year=1936 |page=79}}</ref>，地基與洩洪道頂部之間高{{convert|48|m}}<ref name=bd>{{cite report |url=http://www.britishdams.org/2004conf/papers/wallis.pdf |title=Remedial drainage to Laggan and Blackwater gravity dams |first1=R. P. |last1=Wallis |first2=A . C. |last2=Morison |first3=R. |last3=Gunstensen |publisher=}}</ref>，能容納拉根湖的{{convert|40000000|m3}}水量<ref name=bd/>。

==歷史==
水壩由[[保富集團|保富集團]]替[[英国铝业公司|英国铝业公司]]興建，於1934年完工。1985年，水壩被列入為B項[[登錄建築|登錄建築]]，在2011年升格為A項<ref name=hs>{{cite web |title=Listed Building Report - Lochaber Hydroelectric Scheme And Aluminium Smelter, Loch Laggan Dam |url=http://data.historic-scotland.gov.uk/pls/htmldb/f?p=2200:15:0::::BUILDING:6835 |publisher=Historic Scotland |accessdate=30 August 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Record Details - Loch Laggan Hydro Electric Dam |url=http://her.highland.gov.uk/SingleResult.aspx?uid=MHG17279 |work=Highland Historic Environment Record |publisher=Highland Council |accessdate=30 August 2013}}</ref>。

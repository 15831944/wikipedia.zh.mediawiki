'''拉格希尔·哈加'''（{{lang-no|Ragnhild Haga}}，{{bd|1991年|2月12日||}}）生于阿克什胡斯郡南内斯塔霍尔特，是一名挪威女子越野滑雪运动员。拉格希尔的叔父[[Anders_Bakken|Anders Bakken]]曾参加过[[1980年冬季奥林匹克运动会|1980年普莱西德湖冬奥]]越野滑雪项目<ref name=fis>{{cite web |url=http://www.fis-ski.com/cross-country/news-multimedia/news/article=cross-country-talk-ragnhild-haga-nor.html |title=Cross-Country Talk: Ragnhild Haga (NOR) |last=Trawka |first=Irek |date=2015-07-14 |work=[[国际滑雪联合会|International Ski Federation]]|access-date=2018-02-17|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151220223111/http://www.fis-ski.com/cross-country/news-multimedia/news/article=cross-country-talk-ragnhild-haga-nor.html|archivedate=2015-12-20}}</ref>。她的兄弟Magne、Havard和Anders也都从事冬季运动<ref name="pyeongchang2018">{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/cross-country-skiing/athlete-profile-n3026470-ragnhild-haga.htm|title=Cross-Country Skiing {{!}} Athlete Profile: Ragnhild HAGA|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-02-04|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180420060019/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/cross-country-skiing/athlete-profile-n3026470-ragnhild-haga.htm|archivedate=2018-04-20}}</ref>。她的男友[[Øyvind_Gløersen|Øyvind Gløersen]]同样也是一名越野滑雪选手<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tv2.no/a/9682791/|script-title=no:Haga: – Kjæresten min fikk sjokk|publisher=TV 2|date=2018-02-15|accessdate=2019-02-04|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190202043623/https://www.tv2.no/a/9682791/|archivedate=2019-02-02}}</ref>。除了越野滑雪以外，她也参加[[定向运动|定向滑雪]]运动<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.langrenn.com/dropper-et-vm-for-aa-bli-best-paa-ski.5237678-1743.html|script-title=no:Dropper et VM for å bli best på ski|website=langrenn.com|date=2013-11-07|accessdate=2019-02-04}}</ref>。

'''拉奈克'''（{{lang-en|Lanark}}，{{IPAc-en|ˈ|l|æ|n|ər|k}}；{{lang-gd|Lannraig}}<ref>[http://www.gaelicplacenames.org/databasedetails.php?id=528 Ainmean-Àite na h-Alba ~ Gaelic Place-Names of Scotland]</ref>，{{lang-sco|Lanrik}}）<ref>[http://www.scotslanguage.com/books/view/1/823/Names%20in%20Scots%20-%20Places%20in%20Scotland Scots Language Centre: Scottish Place Names in Scots]</ref>是位於蘇格蘭中部的一個小鎮。世界遺產[[新拉奈克|新拉奈克]]位於拉奈克的附近<ref>[http://www.newlanark.org/ New Lanark website, accessed 11th September, 2014]</ref>。拉奈克的地標還有拉奈克博物館<ref>[http://lanarkmuseum.org/ Lanark Museum website, accessed 12th September, 2014]</ref>。

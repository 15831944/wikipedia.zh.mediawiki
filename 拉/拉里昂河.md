'''拉里昂河'''（[[印尼语|印尼语]]：Sungai Lariang）是[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]][[苏拉威西岛|苏拉威西岛]]中部的一条河流<ref>Rand McNally, ''The New International Atlas'', 1993.</ref>。

==河道==
拉里昂河的支流佩巴图阿河（Pebatua）发源于[[中苏拉威西省|中苏拉威西省]]的[[希吉县|希吉县]]，在{{Coord|1|39|50|S|120|2|47|E|display=inline}}处汇合了南面来的科洛河（Koro，发源于[[南苏拉威西省|南苏拉威西省]][[北鲁乌县|北鲁乌县]]）后形成拉里昂河<ref>{{Citation|title=Pasangkaju, SA 50-8|series=Series T503|format=topographic map 1:250,000|publisher=United States Army Map Service|date=April 1964|url=http://www.lib.utexas.edu/maps/ams/indonesia/txu-oclc-21752461-sa50-8.jpg}}</ref>。之后向西流向[[望加锡海峡|望加锡海峡]]，拉里昂河的部分河段<ref>Some maps such as {{Cite web|title=Lariang, Sulawesi Barat, Sulawesi, Indonesia|publisher=Collins Maps|url=http://www.collinsmaps.com/maps/Indonesia/Sulawesi-Barat/Lariang/P766194.00.aspx|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130628231224/http://www.collinsmaps.com/maps/Indonesia/Sulawesi-Barat/Lariang/P766194.00.aspx|archivedate=2013-06-28}} and {{Cite web|title=City (town): Lariang|publisher=TipTopGlobe|url=http://www.tiptopglobe.com/city?i=965701&n=Lariang}} no longer use the name Lariang, but use Pebatua for the entire length.</ref>成为[[中苏拉威西省|中苏拉威西省]]与[[西苏拉威西省|西苏拉威西省]]的分界，入海口位于[[西苏拉威西省|西苏拉威西省]][[北马穆朱县|北马穆朱县]]的小镇拉里昂（Lariang）<ref>{{Cite book|chapter=Plate 19 Indonesia Central: Philippines|year=1985|title=Times Atlas of the World|edition=seventh|publisher=John Bartholomew and Sons, Ltd.|isbn=978-0-7230-0265-9}}</ref><ref>The 1964 U.S. Army topographic map ''Pasangkaju'' does not show the town of Lariang.</ref>。入海口的坐标为{{Coord|1|25|00|S|119|17|31|E|display=inline}}。

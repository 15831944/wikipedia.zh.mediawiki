'''拉合爾地鐵橘線'''（{{lang-ur|نارنجی راہ}}）是[[拉合爾地鐵|拉合爾地鐵]]首期3條路線中的一條路線，位於[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]][[旁遮普省|旁遮普省]][[拉合爾|拉合爾]]市<ref>[http://tribune.com.pk/story/711864/good-news-on-track-lahore-to-get-pakistans-first-metro-train/ "Good news on track: Lahore to get Pakistan’s first metro train"], ''{{tsl|en|Express Tribune||Express Tribune}}'', Lahore, 23 Mar 2014. Retrieved on 20 October 2014.</ref><ref>[http://tribune.com.pk/story/886037/development-agenda-lahore-metro-train-gets-green-signal/ Development agenda: Lahore metro train gets green signal]</ref>，将于2019年投入运营，將成为巴基斯坦首条地铁线路<ref>{{cite news|title=More delays and a new date for Pakistan’s first metro train|url=https://www.geo.tv/latest/205958-more-delays-and-a-new-date-for-pakistans-first-metro-train|accessdate=2018-08-13|agency=Geo News|date=2018-08-03|quote=Construction work on Pakistan’s first metro train service is 90 per cent complete, but it won’t be up and running till March 2019, say officials.}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Manufacturing of orange trains starts, says Kh Hassan|url=https://www.thenews.com.pk/print/122738-Manufacturing-of-orange-trains-starts-says-Kh-Hassan|accessdate=2017-01-24|agency=The News|date=2016-05-26|quote=Latest technology will be employed for fabricating these trains and the rolling-stock will be fully computerised, automatic and driverless.}}</ref>，为[[拉合爾地鐵|拉合爾地鐵]]三条线路中的一条。其長度为27.1公里，其中25.4公里在地面以上，1.72公里位于地下<ref name="Norinco">{{cite web|title=Norinco Technical Proposal|url=http://www.lahoremetroauraap.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Norinco-Technical-Proposal.pdf|accessdate=2017-01-25|date=January 2016|page=12}}</ref>。全线将开设26站，预计每天可运送250,000名乘客。该线路由{{tsl|en|Government of Punjab, Pakistan|巴基斯坦旁遮普政府|旁遮普政府}}拨款<ref>{{cite news|title=Orange Line not part of CPEC: NA body|url=http://nation.com.pk/national/17-Jan-2017/orange-line-lahore-not-part-of-cpec-na-body|accessdate=2017-01-24|agency=The Nation|date=2017-01-17}}</ref>，造價20億美元。2017年5月16日，[[中车株洲电力机车|中车株洲电力机车]]交付了首批27部地铁列車。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_1819909|title=环球｜地铁时代不再远，巴基斯坦迎来首列“中国造”地铁|date=2017-10-10|work=[[澎湃新闻|澎湃新闻]]|access-date=2018-09-24|language=zh-hans}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/1410624/first-orange-line-metro-train-rolls/|title=First Orange Line Metro train rolls out - The Express Tribune|date=2017-05-16|work=The Express Tribune|access-date=2017-05-16|language=en-US}}</ref>。2018年进行了多次成功测试<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thenews.com.pk/latest/285872-orange-line-metro-train-run-on-trial-basis-in-lahore|title=Orange Line Metro train runs on trial basis in Lahore|website=www.thenews.com.pk|language=en|access-date=2018-03-01}}</ref>。官方负责人为Sibtain Halim<ref>{{cite news |publisher=''[[华尔街日报|The Wall Street Journal]]'' |archive-url=https://archive.today/20180722224343/https://www.wsj.com/articles/chinas-global-building-spree-runs-into-trouble-in-pakistan-1532280460?mod=hp_lead_pos5 |quote=Sibtain Halim, Pakistan’s official in charge of the Orange Line, says no other countries expressed interest in bidding. |title=China’s Global Building Spree Runs Into Trouble in Pakistan |archive-date=2018-07-22 |date=2018-07-22 |first=Jeremy |last=Page |first2=Saeed |last2=Shah |url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/chinas-global-building-spree-runs-into-trouble-in-pakistan-1532280460?mod=hp_lead_pos5 |access-date=2018-09-24 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

'''拉斐拉·洛佩斯·席尔瓦'''（{{lang-pt|Rafaela Lopes Silva}}，{{bd|1992年|4月24日||}}）生于里约热内卢，是一名巴西女子柔道运动员，主攻57公斤级。席尔瓦一开始接触的运动是足球，后来由于她家附近的街道经常发生暴力事件，她在7岁时被父母送进柔道培训班<ref>{{cite web|last1=Paradella|first1=Rodrigo|title=Rafaela Silva troca futebol por judô e supera drama familiar com ouro|url=http://esporte.uol.com.br/judo/ultimas-noticias/2013/08/29/rafaela-silva-troca-futebol-por-judo-e-supera-drama-familiar-com-ouro.htm|publisher=UOL Esporte|accessdate=2016-08-08|date=2013-08-29}}</ref>。席尔瓦曾在2016年里约奥运期间获得该届奥运巴西代表团首金，她在获得金牌两天后的一次采访中公布自己[[出柜|出柜]]，她的女友名为Thamara Cezar，两人同属Institute Reaction俱乐部<ref>{{cite web|title=Brazilian Judo Gold Medalist Publicly Opens Up About Girlfriend For First Time|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/rafaela-silva-girlfriend_us_57ab93a2e4b0db3be07cfeda |publisher=Huffington Post|date=2016-08-10|accessdate=2016-12-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Brazilian gold medal judo champion comes out publicly as gay|url=http://www.outsports.com/2016/8/10/12425422/rafaela-silva-brazil-gay-judo-coming-out |publisher=Outsposts|date=2016-08-10|accessdate=2016-12-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|script-title=pt:O alicerce que ninguém vê: namorada cuida de tudo para que Rafaela só lute|url= http://globoesporte.globo.com/olimpiadas/judo/noticia/2016/08/o-alicerce-que-ninguem-ve-namorada-cuida-de-tudo-para-que-rafaela-so-lutee.html|publisher=Globo Esporte|accessdate=2016-12-19}}</ref>。2017年3月，她获得2016年年度最佳巴西女运动员奖<ref>{{cite web|url=https://esporte.ig.com.br/olimpiadas/2017-03-30/premio-brasil-olimpico-2016.html|script-title=pt:Isaquias Queiroz e Rafaela Silva vencem o Prêmio Brasil Olímpico|publisher=Esporte - iG|date=2017-03-30|accessdate=2018-11-21}}</ref>。

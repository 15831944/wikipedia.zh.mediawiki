'''拉娜·特蕾莎·康多'''（{{lang-en|Lana Therese Condor}}<ref>{{cite tweet|number=597915661680005120|title=It's my Bday!|user=lanacondor|first=Lana|last=Condor|date=May 11, 2015}}</ref>，{{bd|1997年|5月11日}}），本名'''陈东兰'''（{{lang-vi|Trần Đồng Lan}}），[[美国|美国]]演员、舞者。2016年凭借在超级英雄电影《[[X战警：天启|X战警：天启]]》中饰演[[歡歡_(漫畫)|李千欢]]一角正式出道，并于2018年小说《{{le|致所有我曾爱过的男孩|To All the Boys I've Loved Before}}》改编的{{le|致所有我曾爱过的男孩 (电影)|To All the Boys I've Loved Before|同名电影}}中饰演女主角劳拉·简·科维。她在科幻电影《[[艾莉塔：战斗天使|艾莉塔：战斗天使]]》（2019年）中饰演高野，并在即将播出的[[Syfy|Syfy]]电视剧《{{le|杀手一班|Deadly Class (TV series)}}》饰演Saya Kuroki。

==早年==
康多本名陈东兰，生于越南[[芹苴市|芹苴市]]，在美国[[伊利诺伊州|伊利诺伊州]][[芝加哥|芝加哥]]长大<ref name="AnthemMag">{{cite web|url=http://anthemmagazine.com/screen-test-lana-condor/|title=Screen Test: Lana Condor|website=Anthem|first=Kee|last=Chang|date=2015-11-07|access-date=2017-07-15}}</ref>。1997年10月6日，美国夫妇卡-{}-罗-{}-尔（{{lang|en|Carol}}，娘家姓哈伯德（{{lang|en|Haubold}}））和鲍勃·康多（{{lang|en|Bob Condor}}）收养了她和与她没有血缘关系的弟弟亚瑟<ref name="Adoption">{{cite web|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1997-12-21/features/9712220013_1_tree-sitting-8th-grader/10|title=Journey Of The Heart: How One Couple's Dream Of Having A Family Finally Came True|website=Chicago Tribune|first=Bob|last=Condor|date=1997-12-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.refinery29.com/2016/05/111230/alexandra-shipp-lana-candor-x-men-apocalypse|title=Young Mutant Power: Meet X-Men: Apocalypse's Alexandra Shipp & Lana Condor|website=Refinery29|first=Carrie|last=Bell|date=2016-05-24|access-date=2017-07-15}}</ref>。被收养后[[受洗|受洗]]改名拉娜·特蕾莎·康多（{{lang|en|Lana Therese Condor}}）<ref name="Adoption"/>。父亲是两次获得[[普利策奖|普利策奖]]提名的记者，也是{{le|雅虎体育|Yahoo Sports}}前副总裁<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.yahoo.com/movies/meet-x-men-apocalypse-breakout-star-lana-condor-222621061.html|title=Meet 'X-Men: Apocalypse' Breakout Star Lana Condor|website=Yahoo! Movies|first=Kevin|last=Polowy|date=2016-05-26}}</ref>。康多一家原本住在[[华盛顿州|华盛顿州]][[惠德比岛|惠德比岛]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/news/asian-america/all-boys-i-ve-loved-lana-condor-rom-com-star-n796961|title=In 'To All the Boys I've Loved Before,' Lana Condor Is the Rom-Com Star She's Always Wanted|website=NBC News|first=Taylor|last=Weik|date=2017-08-31}}</ref>，直到她15岁才搬到[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[圣莫妮卡|圣莫妮卡]]<ref name="AnthemMag"/>。

康多幼时学习[[芭蕾舞|芭蕾舞]]，在{{le|杰佛瑞芭蕾舞团|Joffrey Ballet}}、{{le|洛克舞蹈教育学校|The Rock School for Dance Education}}和{{le|艾文艾莉美国舞蹈剧场|Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater}}接受训练<ref>{{cite web|url=http://statusmagonline.com/features/lana-condor-interview-may-2016/|title=Lana Condor: New Flame|website=Status|first=Ida|last=Aldana|date=2016-05-10|accessdate=2017-07-15}}</ref>。之后她继续在{{le|洛杉矶芭蕾舞团|Los Angeles Ballet}}跳舞<ref name="Raine">{{cite web|url=https://rainemagazine.com/daily-raine/lana-condor/|title=Breakout Star of X-Men Apocalypse: Lana Condor|website=Raine|first=Kristopher|last=Johnson-Hoyle|date=2016-04-26|accessdate=2017-07-15}}</ref>，并于[[即兴剧场|即兴剧场]]{{le|底层|The Groundlings}}接受训练。康多参加了[[纽约电影学院|纽约电影学院]]表演课和耶鲁大学夏季音乐学院（{{lang|en|Yale Summer Conservatory for Actors}}）<ref name="AnthemMag"/>。2014年，获{{le|加州夏季艺术学校|California State Summer School for the Arts}}优秀戏剧学生<ref>{{cite web|url=http://legacy.ovationtv.com/the-california-state-summer-school-arts-foundation-is-pleased-to-announce-california-state-summer-school-for-the-arts-california-arts-scholars-2014/|title=California State Summer School for the Arts: California Art Scholars 2014!|website=Ovation|date=2014-05-20|access-date=2018-09-05|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180121071518/http://legacy.ovationtv.com/the-california-state-summer-school-arts-foundation-is-pleased-to-announce-california-state-summer-school-for-the-arts-california-arts-scholars-2014/|archive-date=2018-01-21|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。上高一时，康多在纽约{{le|职业表演艺术学校|Professional Performing Arts School}}进修。2015年，她从洛杉矶{{le|巴黎圣母院中学|Notre Dame Academy (Los Angeles, California)}}毕业<ref name="Raine"/>，并被[[洛约拉马利蒙特大学|洛约拉马利蒙特大学]]录取<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.papermag.com/lana-condor-x-men-apocalypse-1619987185.html|title=Meet Lana Condor, X-Men's Newest Star|website=Paper|first=Emily|last=Zemler|date=2016-02-23|accessdate=2017-07-15}}</ref>。

== 生涯 ==
康多在2016年5月27日上映的超级英雄电影《[[X战警：天启|X战警：天启]]》中饰演变种人[[欢欢_(漫画)|李千欢]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2015/film/news/x-men-apocalypse-jubilee-lana-condor-1201460107/|title='X-Men: Apocalypse' Casts Newcomer Lana Condor as Jubilee|website=Variety|first=Dave|last=McNary|date=March 25, 2015}}</ref>。之后，她出演改编自[[2013年波士頓馬拉松爆炸案|2013年波士頓馬拉松爆炸案]]的电影《[[恐袭波士顿|恐袭波士顿]]》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2016/film/news/x-men-actress-lana-condor-joins-patriots-day-1201748628/|title='X-Men' Actress Lana Condor Joins 'Patriots Day' (EXCLUSIVE)|website=Variety|first=Dave|last=McNary|date=April 7, 2016}}</ref>，影片于[[美国电影学会|美国电影学会]]电影节首映，2016年12月21日公映。翌年，与[[詹姆斯·法兰科|詹姆斯·法兰科]]和{{le|朱莉亚·琼斯|Julia Jones}}共同出演爱情惊悚电视电影《{{le|高中恋人|High School Lover}}》，影片于2017年2月4日在{{le|Lifetime电视台|Lifetime (TV network)}}首播<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/01/lifetime-michael-jackson-original-movie-james-franco-viola-davis-tca-1201885478/|title=Lifetime Orders Michael Jackson Original Movie, Picks Up James Franco, Viola Davis Films – TCA|website=Deadline Hollywood|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=January 13, 2017|accessdate=January 13, 2017}}</ref>。

康多在{{le|珍妮·韩|Jenny Han}}校园小说《{{le|致所有我曾爱过的男孩|To All the Boys I've Loved Before}}》改编的{{le|致所有我曾爱过的男孩 (电影)|To All the Boys I've Loved Before|同名电影}}中饰演女主角劳拉·简·科维<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2017/film/news/lana-condor-to-all-the-boys-ive-loved-before-jenny-han-novel-1202502572/|title=Lana Condor to Star in 'To All the Boys I've Loved Before' Movie Based on Jenny Han Novel|website=Variety|first=Todd|last=Spangler|date=July 21, 2017}}</ref>。影片于2018年8月17日在[[Netflix|Netflix]]上线<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/john-corbett-lana-condor-join-all-boys-ive-loved-before-film-1023303|title=John Corbett, Lana Condor Join 'To All the Boys I've Loved Before' Film (Exclusive)|website=The Hollywood Reporter|first=Natalie|last=Jarvey|date=July 21, 2017|accessdate=July 21, 2017}}</ref>。她还出演科幻电影《[[艾莉塔：战斗天使|艾莉塔：战斗天使]]》女配角高野，该片定于2018年12月21日上映<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2016/film/news/x-men-lana-condor-alita-battle-angel-1201885650/|title='X-Men' Star Lana Condor Joins James Cameron's 'Alita: Battle Angel'|website=Variety|first=Dave|last=McNary|date=October 11, 2016|accessdate=July 15, 2017}}</ref>。

她还出演成长浪漫喜剧电影《{{le|夏月冬夜|Summer Night}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2017/film/news/joseph-cross-james-ponsoldt-summer-night-1202613541/|title=Joseph Cross Teams With James Ponsoldt on Directorial Debut 'Summer Night' (EXCLUSIVE)|website=Variety|first=Justin|last=Kroll|date=November 13, 2017|accessdate=November 3, 2017}}</ref>和[[Syfy|Syfy]]电视剧《{{le|杀手一班|Deadly Class (TV series)}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/11/deadly-class-syfy-cast-comic-based-drama-pilot-1202205180/|title='Deadly Class': Syfy Sets Full Cast For Comic-Based Drama Pilot|website=Deadline Hollywood|first=Denise|last=Petski|date=November 9, 2017|accessdate=November 9, 2017}}</ref>。

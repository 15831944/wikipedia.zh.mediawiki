'''拉塞·诺曼·汉森'''（{{lang-da|Lasse Norman Hansen}}，{{bd|1992年|2月11日||}}）生于福堡-中菲英自治市，是一名丹麦男子自行车运动员，主攻公路自行车和场地自行车。2012年，他在伦敦奥运男子全能赛项目上夺得冠军，成为历史上首位夺得该项目冠军的选手<ref>{{cite news|author=|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/sports/olympics/omnium-world-champion-glenn-oshea-moves-to-first-overall/2012/08/05/de4b81fc-deea-11e1-8d48-2b1243f34c85_story.html|title=Lasse Norman Hansen of Denmark wins inaugural omnium title at London Olympics|publisher=The Washington Post|location=District of Columbia|date=2012-08-05|accessdate=2012-08-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=|url=http://espn.go.com/olympics/summer/2012/cycling/story/_/id/8236435/2012-summer-olympics-danish-cyclist-lasse-norman-hansen-wins-olympic-gold-men-omnium|title=Lasse Norman Hansen wins gold|publisher=[[ESPN|ESPN]]|location=Bristol, Connecticut|date=2012-08-05|accessdate=2014-08-14}}</ref>。2014年，他成为职业选手。<ref name="Profile at PROTEAM"/>他的妹妹路易丝同样也是一名自行车选手<ref>{{cite web|url=https://teamvirtucycling.com/en/nyheder/get-to-know-louise-norman-hansen/|title=Get to Know - Louise Norman Hansen|publisher=Team Virtu Cycling|date=2018-03-07|accessdate=2019-03-14|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190314092249/https://teamvirtucycling.com/en/nyheder/get-to-know-louise-norman-hansen/|archivedate=2019-03-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

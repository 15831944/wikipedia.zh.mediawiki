'''好望角国家天文台'''（{{lang-en|Royal Observatory, Cape of Good Hope}}）是南非现存最古老的科研机构。<ref name="ACBrown">{{cite book
|title=A History of Scientific Endeavour in South Africa: A Collection of Essays Published on the Occasion of the Centenary of the Royal Society of South Africa

|publisher=Royal Society of South Africa
|date=1977
|location=Cape Town
|author=Royal Society of South Africa
|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-X4IAAAAMAAJ&dq=History+of+Scientific+Endeavour+in+South+Africa&q=founded+1820
|page=60}}</ref>它由英国的[[开普殖民地|开普殖民地]]于1820年建造，现在是[[南非天文台|南非天文台]]的总部大楼。
该机构位于[[开普敦|开普敦]]市中心东南方向5公里（3英里）的一座小山上。在该机构成立后的一个世纪内它所处的这片郊区发展起来，这片郊区就以已经存在的皇家天文台命名为天文台区。 <!-- Its site has recently been declared as having "Grade I, Heritage Status" by the {{tsl|en|South African Heritage Resources Agency||South African Heritage Resources Agency}} (SAHRA).{{citation needed|date=October 2013}} -->它也是[[国际古迹遗址理事会|国际古迹遗址理事会]]关于[[世界遗产|世界遗产]]的一个研究案例。<ref name="unesco">{{cite web |url = http://www2.astronomicalheritage.net/index.php/show-entity?identity=52&idsubentity=1|title = Royal Observatory, Cape of Good Hope, Republic of South Africa|publisher=UNESCO}}</ref>

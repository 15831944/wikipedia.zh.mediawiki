'''好汉两个半第十季'''于2012年9月27日起在[[美国|美国]][[哥伦比亚广播公司|哥伦比亚广播公司]]正式播映.<ref name=s10premiere>{{cite web|last=Bibel|first=Sara|title=CBS Announces Fall 2012-13 Premiere Dates|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/07/11/cbs-announces-fall-premiere-dates/141201/|work=TV by the Numbers|publisher=[[CBS|CBS Broadcasting]]|date=July 11, 2012|accessdate=July 11, 2012}}</ref>，从这季开始，好汉两个半改为每周四晚八点半在[[生活大爆炸|生活大爆炸]]后播映<ref>{{cite web|last=Gorman|first=Bill|title=Fall 2012 TV Schedule Night by Night: Thursday|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/05/17/fall-2012-night-by-night-thursday/134742/|work=TV by the Numbers|accessdate=August 27, 2012|date=May 17, 2012}}</ref>。

==制作==

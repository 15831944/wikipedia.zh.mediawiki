<!-- Definition and medical uses -->
'''氯喹'''（Chloroquine），是一種用於治療及預防[[疟疾|疟疾]]的藥物<ref name=AHFS2015>{{cite web|title=Aralen Phosphate|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/aralen-phosphate.html|publisher=The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|accessdate=Dec 2, 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151208200339/http://www.drugs.com/monograph/aralen-phosphate.html|archivedate=8 December 2015}}</ref>。若需治療特定幾種抗藥病原株或複雜症狀，則需配合其他抗瘧藥物<ref name=AHFS2015/>。偶爾也會用於治療腸道外的{{tsl|en|amebiasis|阿米巴原蟲症}}、[[類風濕性關節炎|類風濕性關節炎]]，以及[[紅斑性狼瘡|紅斑性狼瘡]]<ref name=AHFS2015/>。本品為口服藥物<ref name=AHFS2015/>。

<!-- Side effects and mechanism -->
常見副作用包含肌肉功能問題、食慾不佳、腹瀉，以及紅疹<ref name=AHFS2015/>。嚴重副作用則包含視力模糊、肌肉損傷、[[癲癇發作|癲癇發作]]，以及[[再生不良性貧血|貧血]]<ref name=AHFS2015/>。妊娠期間用藥似為安全，但相關研究仍未完全<ref name=AHFS2015/>。氯喹屬於[[4-氨基喹啉|4-氨基喹啉]]類的藥物<ref name=AHFS2015/>，其作用機轉為對抗瘧原蟲在[[紅血球|紅血球]]內的無性繁殖期<ref name=AHFS2015/>。
<!-- History, society and culture -->
氯喹於1934年由{{tsl|en|Hans Andersag||Hans Andersag}}所發現<ref>{{cite book|last1=Zumla|first1=edited by Gordon C. Cook, Alimuddin I.|title=Manson's tropical diseases.|date=2009|publisher=Saunders|location=[Edinburgh]|isbn=9781416044703|page=1240|edition=22nd|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=CF2INI0O6l0C&pg=PA1240|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151208055446/https://books.google.com/books?id=CF2INI0O6l0C&pg=PA1240|archivedate=8 December 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Bhattacharjee|first1=Mrinal|title=Chemistry of Antibiotics and Related Drugs|date=2016|publisher=Springer|isbn=9783319407463|page=184|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=vgXWDAAAQBAJ&pg=PA184|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161101102221/https://books.google.com/books?id=vgXWDAAAQBAJ&pg=PA184|archivedate=1 November 2016}}</ref>。本品列名於[[世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单|世界卫生组织基本药物标准清单]]之中，為基礎公衛體系必備藥物之一<ref name=WHO19th>{{cite web|title=WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (19th List)|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|work=World Health Organization|accessdate=8 December 2016|date=April 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161213052708/http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|archivedate=13 December 2016}}</ref>。其屬於[[通用名药物|通用名药物]]<ref name=AHFS2015/>，於[[发展中国家|发展中国家]]每劑批發價約為0.04美金<ref>{{cite web|title=Chloroquine (Base)|url=http://mshpriceguide.org/en/single-drug-information/?DMFId=160&searchYear=2014|website=International Drug Price Indicator Guide|accessdate=4 December 2015}}</ref>。在美國，其每劑價格約為5.30美金<ref name=AHFS2015/>。

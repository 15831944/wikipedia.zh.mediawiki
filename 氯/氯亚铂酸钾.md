'''氯亚铂酸钾'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为K<sub>2</sub>PtCl<sub>4</sub>。它是橙红色的固体，是制备其它铂化合物的重要[[试剂|试剂]]。

== 制备及性质 ==
氯亚铂酸钾可以由[[氯铂酸钾|氯铂酸钾]]用[[肼|肼]]还原来制备。<ref name=Keller>{{cite journal |author1=Keller, R. N. |author2=Moeller, T. | title = Potassium Tetrachloroplatinate(II) | journal = [[Inorg._Synth.|Inorg. Synth.]] | year = 1963 | volume = 7 | pages = 247–250 | doi = 10.1002/9780470132333.ch79|series=Inorganic Syntheses |isbn=9780470132333 }}</ref>该配合物仅在水中appreciably soluble。在碱的存在下用乙醇处理，则会被还原为金属[[铂|铂]]。相应的有机的氯亚铂酸盐，如<nowiki>[</nowiki>PPN<nowiki>]</nowiki><sub>2</sub>PtCl<sub>4</sub>，可溶于[[氯代烃|氯代烃]]中。<ref>{{cite journal |author1=Elding, L. I. |author2=Oskarsson, A. |author3=Kukushkin, V. Yu | title = Platinum Complexes Suitable as Precursors for Synthesis in Nonaqueous Solvents | journal = [[Inorg._Synth.|Inorg. Synth.]] | year = 1997 | volume = 31 | pages = 276–279 | doi = 10.1002/9780470132623.ch47|series=Inorganic Syntheses |isbn=9780470132623 }}</ref>

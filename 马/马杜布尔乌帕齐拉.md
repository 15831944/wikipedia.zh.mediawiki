'''马杜布尔'''（{{lang-bn|মধুপুর}}，{{lang-en|Madhupur}}）是孟加拉国的一个乌帕齐拉，位於[[达卡专区|达卡专区]]的[[坦盖尔县|坦盖尔县]]。<ref>{{cite book |last=Abedin |first=Joinal |year=2012 |chapter=Madhupur Upazila |chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=Madhupur_Upazila |editor1-last=Islam |editor1-first=Sirajul |editor1-link=Sirajul Islam |editor2-last=Jamal |editor2-first=Ahmed A. |title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |edition=Second |publisher=[[Asiatic_Society_of_Bangladesh|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh]]}}</ref>

==人口==

據{{tsl|en|2011 Bangladesh census|2011年孟加拉國人口普查}}，馬杜布爾共有人口296729人，其中男性佔比51.13%，女性48.87%；其7歲以上人口之平均識字率為41.2%（其中男性42.7%、女性39.7%）。<ref name="community">{{cite web |url=http://203.112.218.65/WebTestApplication/userfiles/Image/PopCen2011/C_Tangail.pdf |title=Community Report: Tangail |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |website=Population & Housing Census 2011 |publisher=Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics |format=PDF |accessdate=2017-06-18 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161202175937/http://203.112.218.65/WebTestApplication/userfiles/Image/PopCen2011/C_Tangail.pdf |archivedate=2016-12-02 |df= }}</ref>

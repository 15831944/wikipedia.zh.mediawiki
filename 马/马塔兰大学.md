'''马塔兰大学''' ({{lang-en|University of Mataram}}, {{lang-id|Universitas Mataram}}) 是一所位于[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]][[西努沙登加拉省|西努沙登加拉省]][[马塔兰|马塔兰]]的公立大学，创建于1962年10月1日。

==历届校长==
* Kolonel M. Jusuf Abubakar
* Kolonel Gatot Suherman (caretaker) 6 May 1974.
*  Kolonel CKH Soebiyanto, (1974 – 1979) 21 April 1975.
*  Brigadir Jenderal Soebiyanto, (1979 – 1984) 31 July 1979.
*  Ir. M. Qazuini, (1984 – 1988) 26 May 1984.
*  Ir. M. Qazuini, (1988 – 1993) 30 August 1988.
*  Dr. Ir. Sri Widodo, (1993 – 1997) 8 February 1993.
*  Prof. Dr. dr. Mulyanto (1997 – 2001) 2 April 1997.
*  Ir. Mansur Ma’shum, (2001 – 2005) 
*  Prof. Ir. Mansur Ma’shum, (2005 – 2009) 
*  Prof. Ir. Sunarpi, (2009 – 2013)
*  Prof. Ir. Sunarpi, (2013 - 2017)

'''马瑙斯翼龙'''（英文：Manaus Pterosaur）是一种据传出没于[[巴西|巴西]][[亚马孙州_(巴西)|亚马逊州]][[马瑙斯|马瑙斯]]的翼展12英尺、头部平坦、颈部与喙较长、类似[[翼龙目|翼龙]][[神秘生物列表|神秘动物]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://cfz-usa.blogspot.com/2017/07/manaus-pterosaur.html|title=CFZ-USA: MANAUS PTEROSAUR|accessdate=2018-08-31|date=2017-07-12|last=Ronanus|first=Ego|work=CFZ-USA}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=z9gMsCUtCZUC&pg=PA313&lpg=PA313&dq=Manaus+Pterosaur&source=bl&ots=JVTrktlf4Y&sig=kYRdE4kgdCjNPcYTHg868Qfis_g&hl=zh-CN&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwiSotfgzpbdAhWxNOwKHQKCCq4Q6AEwDHoECAMQAQ#v=onepage&q=Manaus%20Pterosaur&f=false|title=Mysterious Creatures: A Guide to Cryptozoology|accessdate=|author=George M. Eberhart|date=|publisher=books.google.com}}</ref>1947年2月，JA·哈里森（JA Harrison）在马瑙斯看见五只翼展12英尺、以V形编队飞行疑似翼龙的生物。哈里森描述，它们的翅膀类似棕色皮革、没有羽毛、翅膀似乎有棱状条纹。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://karlshuker.blogspot.com/2013/10/i-thought-i-saw-terror-saur-do.html|title=ShukerNature: I THOUGHT I SAW A TERROR SAUR! - DO PREHISTORIC FLYING REPTILES STILL EXIST?|accessdate=2018-08-31|date=2013-10-07|last=Shuker|first=Dr Karl|work=ShukerNature}}</ref>有理论认为，马瑙斯翼龙是被误认的鸟类，比如：[[裸頸鸛|裸颈鹳]]、[[林鹳|林鹳]]、{{link-en|黑尾鹳|Maguari stork}}与[[鹈鹕|鹈鹕]]。<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://cryptidz.wikia.com/wiki/Manaus_Pterosaur|title=Manaus Pterosaur|newspaper=Cryptid Wiki|accessdate=2018-08-31|language=en}}</ref>

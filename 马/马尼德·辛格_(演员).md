'''马尼德·辛格'''（Maninder Singh）是印度演员。他在电影《Khelein Hum Jee Jaan Sey》中扮演印度独立活动家[[阿南塔·辛格|阿南塔·辛格]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.livemint.com/Leisure/Q75PX6l2Tc6np8iUywnjcN/Film-Review--Khelein-hum-jee-jaan-se.html|title=Film Review: Khelein Hum Jee Jaan Sey|accessdate=21 August 2014|author=Arun Janardhan|date=3 December 2010|publisher=''[[Live_Mint|Live Mint]]''}}</ref> 他在悬疑惊险电视节目《2612》（2012）<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tellychakkar.com/tv/tv-news/maninder-singh-play-the-lead-sphere-origins-next-life-ok|title=Maninder Singh to play the lead in Sphere Origins' next for Life OK|accessdate=September 25, 2017|date=June 13, 2012}}</ref> 担任主角兰德普·拉索尔（Randeep Rathore），并在下个季度再次担任角色。在2014年，他也在流行的电视节目《CID》<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tellychakkar.com/tv/tv-news/gaurav-khanna-and-maninder-singh-enter-cid-senior-cops-duty-582|title=Gaurav Khanna and Maninder Singh to enter CID as senior cops on duty|accessdate=September 25, 2017|date=March 24, 2014}}</ref> 描绘高级CID警察。<ref name="CID">{{Cite web|url=http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/television/actor-gaurav-khanna-to-enter-cid/|title=Actor Gaurav Khanna & Maninder Singh to enter 'CID' as senior cops|accessdate=21 August 2014|date=30 March 2014|publisher=''[[Indian_Express|Indian Express]]''}}</ref> 可以在Zee电视台的电视节目《Sanyukt》中见到他。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tellychakkar.com/tv/tv-news/exclusive-meet-the-cast-of-zee-tv-s-newest-nuclear-family-sanyukt-160816|title=Exclusive: Meet the cast of Zee TV’s newest ‘nuclear family’, Sanyukt|accessdate=September 25, 2017|date=August 16, 2016}}</ref> 目前可以在Star Bharat的《Kya Haal, Mr. Paanchal?》（电视连续剧）中看到他。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.tellychakkar.com/tv/tv-news/shikayat-peti-navratri-drama-mad-rush-kya-haal-mr-panchaal-170915|title=Shikayat Peti, Navratri drama & mad rush in Kya Haal Mr. Panchaal|accessdate=September 25, 2017|date=September 15, 2017}}</ref>

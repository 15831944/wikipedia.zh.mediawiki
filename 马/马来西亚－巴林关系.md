'''马来西亚－巴林关系'''（{{lang-ms|Hubungan Bahrain–Malaysia}}）是指[[巴林历史|巴林]]和[[马来西亚历史|马来西亚]]之间的双边关系，巴林在[[吉隆坡|吉隆坡]]设立大使馆，<ref name="bm">{{Cite web|url=http://www.gulf-daily-news.com/NewsDetails.aspx?storyid=240859|title=Embassy to boost Malaysia relations|accessdate=2014-01-12|author=Rebecca Torr|date=2009-01-23|publisher=Gulf Daily News}}</ref>，马来西亚也在[[麦纳麦|麦纳麦]]设有大使馆。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.kln.gov.my/web/bhr_manama/home|title=Official Website of Embassy of Malaysia, Manama|accessdate=2014-01-12|publisher=[[Ministry_of_Foreign_Affairs,_Malaysia|Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Malaysia]]}}</ref> 马来西亚也是2011年巴林内乱期间，支持巴林举行全国对话的国家之一，并愿意派遣一支维和部队帮助该国。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.bna.bh/portal/en/news/594450|title=Malaysia condemns acts of terror, reiterates support for National Dialogue: Ambassador Shahizan|accessdate=2014-01-12|date=2013-12-23|publisher=Bahrain News Agency}}</ref><ref name="im">{{Cite web|url=http://www.twentyfoursevennews.com/headline/bahrain-needs-branding-as-the-perfect-destination/|title=Bahrain needs branding as the perfect destination|accessdate=2014-01-12|author=Mahmood Rafique|date=2014-01-03|publisher=24x7 News}}</ref>

== 经济关系 ==
两国之间的贸易关系目前正在增加。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.gulf-daily-news.com/NewsDetails.aspx?storyid=122834|title=Bahrain-Malaysia seek stronger trade relations|accessdate=2014-01-12|author=Dalal Al Alawi|date=2005-09-25|publisher=Gulf Daily News}}</ref> 巴林已经邀请马来西亚的企业界在该国投资。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://investvine.com/qatar-bahrain-forge-closer-ties-to-asean/|title=Qatar, Bahrain forge closer ties to ASEAN|accessdate=2014-01-12|author=Arno Maierbrugger|date=2012-08-25|publisher=Investvine}}</ref> 因此，许多马来西亚的公司代表已经在巴林参与主要的项目包括建筑巴林国际赛车场，巴林市中心和其他基础设施项目，如巴林铜锣的扩展。
<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://themalaysianreserve.com/main/news/corporate-malaysia/5195-bahrain-seeking-biz-venture-with-msian|title=Bahrain seeking biz venture with M’sian|accessdate=2014-01-12|author=Kamalavacini Ramanathan|date=2013-12-16|publisher=The Malaysian Reserve|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140111213207/http://themalaysianreserve.com/main/news/corporate-malaysia/5195-bahrain-seeking-biz-venture-with-msian|archivedate=2014-01-11}}</ref> 2007年1月到10月之间，两国之间的双边贸易达1.91亿美元，在同年访问马来西亚的巴林游客有约7200人。

目前，巴林也正在成为一个商业目的地。

两国都在伊斯兰银行业领域展开竞争<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.dfat.gov.au/geo/bahrain/country_brief.html|title=Bahrain country brief (Economic Overview)|accessdate=2014-01-12|date=September 2013|publisher=Australian Government (Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade)}}</ref>。

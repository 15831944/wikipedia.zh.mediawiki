'''马永琳'''（{{bd|19世纪||1895年|catIdx=M}}），[[华寺清真寺|华寺清真寺]]的[[回族|中国穆斯林]]领导人，参与了对抗[[清朝|清朝]]的[[同治陕甘回变|同治陕甘回变]]和1895年的穆斯林起事。他组织[[回族|回族]]、[[东乡族|东乡族]]、[[保安族|保安族]]和[[撒拉族|撒拉族]]民众推翻清朝在[[青海|青海]][[循化|循化]]的统治。总兵[[汤彦和|汤彦和]]派军镇压。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=BwuSpFiOFfYC&pg=PA119&dq=hua+si+mosque&hl=en&ei=FjA1TIDmEIOclge1tOHjAQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCwQ6AEwAA#v=snippet&q=ma%20yonglin%20of%20the%20multicloured&f=false|title=China's Muslim Hui community: migration, settlement and sects|author=Michael Dillon|year=1999|publisher=Curzon Press|location=Richmond|page=136|isbn=0-7007-1026-4|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>马永琳属于[[苏菲主义|苏菲主义]][[虎夫耶|虎夫耶]]派系。他领导对清军的袭击。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=90CN0vtxdY0C&printsec=frontcover&dq=hua%20si%20mosque&source=gbs_slider_thumb#v=snippet&q=ma%20yonglin%20anti%20qing%20commanders%20assualts&f=false|title=Familiar strangers: a history of Muslims in Northwest China|author=Jonathan Neaman Lipman|year=2004|publisher=University of Washington Press|location=Seattle|page=155|isbn=0-295-97644-6|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>忠于朝廷的中国穆斯林如[[董福祥|董福祥]]、[[马安良|马安良]]等击败了马永琳的穆斯林叛军。中国穆斯林军官[[马福禄|马福禄]]和[[马福祥|马福祥]]反对反叛的穆斯林，在战斗中击败了他们。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=90CN0vtxdY0C&printsec=frontcover&dq=hua%20si%20mosque&source=gbs_slider_thumb#v=onepage&q=brothers%20opposed%20ma%20yonglin&f=false|title=Familiar strangers: a history of Muslims in Northwest China|author=Jonathan Neaman Lipman|year=2004|publisher=University of Washington Press|location=Seattle|page=168|isbn=0-295-97644-6|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>

最后马永琳及其子及其他一百多名穆斯林叛首被董福祥俘虏斩首。<ref>{{cite book|title=TRANSLATION OF THE PEKING GAZETTE for 1896|first=|last=|location=Shanghai|edition=|year=1897|publisher=REPRINTED FROM THE "NORTH-CHINA HERALD AND SUPREME COURT AND CONSULAR GAZETTE"|url=https://library.uoregon.edu/ec/e-asia/read/pg1896all.pdf|page=6|isbn=|accessdate=24 April 2014| ref=harv }}</ref>

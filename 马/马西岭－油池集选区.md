'''馬西嶺-油池集選區''' ({{lang-en|Marsiling-Yew Tee Group Representation Constituency}}; {{lang-ms|Kawasan Undi Perwakilan Berkumpulan Marsiling-Yew Tee}}; {{lang-ta|மார்சிலிங்–இயூ டீ குழுத் தொகுதி}})是一個位於新加坡[[马西岭|马西岭]]與[[油池|油池]]區的國會[[集选区|集选区]]。<ref name="ELD 2018">{{cite web | author=ELD | title=Map of Electoral Divisions | website=Singapore Elections Department | date=2018-06-04 | url=http://www.eld.gov.sg/elections_map_electoral.html | access-date=2018-07-24 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171205230859/http://www.eld.gov.sg/elections_map_electoral.html | archive-date=2017-12-05 | dead-url=no }}</ref>自选区2015年成立以来，一直由执政的[[新加坡人民行动党|人民行动党]]胜出。人民行动党也管辖了该选区的市镇会。

现在馬西嶺-油池集選區是由国家发展部长兼财政部第二部长{{link-en|黄循财|Lawerence Wong|}}领军。<ref name="Salleh 2015">{{cite web | last=Salleh | first=Nur Asyiqin Mohamad | title=Lawrence Wong and Halimah Yacob to co-anchor Marsiling-Yew Tee GRC | website=The Straits Times | date=2015-08-13 | url=https://www.straitstimes.com/politics/lawrence-wong-and-halimah-yacob-to-co-anchor-marsiling-yew-tee-grc | access-date=2018-07-24 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170816161437/http://www.straitstimes.com/politics/lawrence-wong-and-halimah-yacob-to-co-anchor-marsiling-yew-tee-grc | archive-date=2017-08-16 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

'''{{pagename}}'''（{{lang-ar|محمود الزعبي}}，{{bd|1935年||2000年|3月7日}}），[[敘利亞|敘利亞]]前[[叙利亚总理|總理]]，出生於敘利亞{{tsl|en|Khirbet Ghazaleh|杰尔贝特加沙列}}。他共有二兒一女<ref name=dnews22may>{{cite news|title=Former Syrian prime minister kills self|url=http://www.deseretnews.com/article/761462/Former-Syrian-prime-minister-kills-self.html?pg=all|accessdate=15 March 2013|newspaper=Deseret News|date=22 May 2000|location=Damascus}}</ref>。

1935年，祖阿比出生在一個[[遜尼派|遜尼派]]家庭中，其故鄉杰尔贝特加沙列是一座距首都[[大馬士革|大馬士革]]{{convert|75|m}}遠的村莊<ref name=dnews22may/><ref name=Rabil2006>{{cite book|author=Robert G. Rabil|title=Syria, The United States, and the War on Terror in the Middle East|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=M18Jj0bMMl0C&pg=PA31|accessdate=15 March 2013|year=2006|publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group|isbn=978-0-275-99015-2|pages=31}}</ref>。他是阿拉伯復興社會黨的成員<ref name=Hinnesbusch2002>{{cite book|author=Ray Hinnesbusch|title=Syria: Revolution from Above|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=WqvloHdKcRMC&pg=PA79|accessdate=15 March 2013|year=2002|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-0-415-28568-1|pages=79}}</ref><ref name=aps26march>{{cite news|title=Bashar Assad|url=http://www.thefreelibrary.com/SYRIA+-+Bashar+Al+Assad.-a073444743|accessdate=15 April 2013|newspaper=APS Diplomat Operations in Oil Diplomacy|date=26 March 2001}}</ref>，於1987年被任命為總理<ref name=euromoney01>{{cite news|title=Syria: A commitment to change|url=http://www.euromoney.com/Article/1003691/A-commitment-to-change.html?Type=Article&ArticleID=1003691|accessdate=10 February 2013|newspaper=Euromoney|date=July 2001}}</ref>；2000年3月7日，穆罕默德·穆斯塔法·米鲁繼任總理一職<ref name=Eur2003>{{cite book|author=Eur|title=The Middle East and North Africa 2003|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=4CfBKvsiWeQC&pg=PA1019|accessdate=15 March 2013|year=2003|publisher=Europa Publications|isbn=978-1-85743-132-2|pages=1019}}</ref>。

同年5月10日，[[哈菲兹·阿萨德|哈菲兹·阿萨德]]將祖阿比逐出阿拉伯復興黨並決定起訴之<ref name=dnews22may/>，後者的資產亦為政府所凍結<ref name=Eur2003/>。同月，祖阿比在軟禁期間自盡<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2000/07/11/world/syrians-vote-to-confirm-assad-s-son-as-president.html|title=Syrians Vote To Confirm Assad's Son As President|last=Kifner|first=John|work=The New York Times|date=11 July 2000|accessdate=10 April 2009}}</ref><ref name=Publications2012>{{cite book|title=Political Chronology of the Middle East|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eCSOT0_JAnwC&pg=PA2038|accessdate=10 February 2013|date=12 October 2012|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-135-35673-6|pages=2038}}</ref>，在被送往醫院的途中去世<ref name=dnews22may/>。

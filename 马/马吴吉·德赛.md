'''马吴吉·德赛'''（{{lang-gu|માવજી દેસાઈ}}）是[[印度|印度]][[巴纳斯坎塔县|巴纳斯坎塔县]]一名政客，{{tsl|en|Banas Dairy|巴纳斯戴丽}}副主席<ref>{{cite web|title=Banas dairy cuts ghee prices by 40/kg|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/rajkot/Banas-dairy-cuts-ghee-prices-by-40/kg/articleshow/50348138.cms|date=2015-12-28|access-date=2016-03-06|work=[[印度时报|The Times of India]]}}</ref><ref name=dg>{{cite web|title=Shankarbhai Chaudhary becomes new Chairman of Banas dairy,reduces Ghee price|url=http://deshgujarat.com/2015/12/27/shankarbhai-chaudhary-becomes-new-chairman-of-banas-dairy-reduces-ghee-price/|date=|access-date=2016-03-06|work=DeshGujarat.com}}</ref>。为[[印度人民党|印度人民党]]党员。他还是[[德埃萨|德埃萨]]{{tsl|en|APMC|农产品市场委员会}}（APMC）主席<ref name=dg />。2017年11月24日，印度人民党中央选举委员会提名他为{{tsl|en|Member of the Legislative Assembly (India)|印度立法议会议员|立法议会议员}}<ref>{{cite web|title=Declared BJP Candidate|url=http://www.timesnownews.com/gujarat-assembly-election-2017/article/gujarat-assembly-elections-2017-bjp-releases-fifth-list-with-names-of-13-candidates/132216|access-date=2017-11-24}}</ref>。

'''马克·佩特里'''（{{lang-en|'''Mark Petrie'''}}，{{bd|1979年|5月20日}}）是新西兰电影，电视和视频游戏作曲家。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://filmandgamecomposers.com/interviews/composer-interview-mark-petrie/|title=Composer Interview – Mark Petrie|date=29 August 2012|work=Film and Game Composers|access-date=14 April 2017|language=en-US}}{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://soundcloud.com/mark-22|title=Mark Petrie Composer|website=SoundCloud|language=en|access-date=14 April 2017}}</ref>他最知名的是为 Guardians of the Galaxy, Life of Pi, Mission Impossible: Ghost Protocol, Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows的预告片配乐，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://perspectiveforum.net/2017/01/20/mark-petrie/|title=Mark Petrie|last=Aletras|first=Adonis|date=20 January 2017|website=PERSPECTIVE: A FORUM FOR FILM, TV, AND MEDIA COMPOSERS|access-date=14 April 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.markpetrie.com/about/|title=About {{!}} Mark Petrie – music composer for trailers, games, film and TV|website=www.markpetrie.com|language=en-US|access-date=15 April 2017}}</ref>以及为Madden NFL撰写得分的预告片制作音乐。 他还为电影，电视剧，纪录片，政治运动和体育赛事编曲配乐。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1214868/bio|title=Mark Petrie|website=IMDb|access-date=15 April 2017}}</ref><br>

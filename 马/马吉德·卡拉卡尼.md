== 生平 ==
卡拉卡尼是20世纪60年代的毛派活动家<ref>Gilles Dorronsoro. ''Revolution Unending: Afghanistan, 1979 to the Present''. Columbia University Press, 2002.</ref>，亦是[[永恒火焰|永恒火焰]]运动成员。1939年，他出生在紹馬利平原的卡拉坎村。1945年，其父亲和祖父被穆罕默德·哈希姆·汗政权逮捕并处决<ref>Hafizullah Emadi. ''Dynamics of Political Development in Afghanistan: The British, Russian, and American Invasions''.Palgrave Macmillan, 2010.</ref>。1978年，卡拉卡尼创立了阿富汗人民解放组织<ref>Ludwig Adamec and Frank  Clements. ''Conflict in Afghanistan: A Historical Encyclopedia''. ABC‐Clio,  2003.</ref>。他是反抗[[阿富汗人民民主党|阿富汗人民民主党]]政权和苏联入侵的毛派领导人。<ref>Anthony Hyman. ''Afghanistan under Soviet Domination, 1964–91''. Palgrave Macmillan, 1992.</ref>1979年，他创立了阿富汗统一全国阵线<ref>Mohammed Kakar. ''Afghanistan: The Soviet Invasion and the Afghan Response, 1979-1982''.University of California Press, 1997.</ref>。1980年2月27日，卡拉卡尼于[[三号起义|三号起义]]期间在[[喀布尔|喀布尔]]附近被逮捕<ref>Halim Tanwir. ''Afghanistan: History, Diplomacy and Journalism Volume 1''. Xlibris, 2013.{{Self-published inline|certain=yes|date=December 2017}}</ref><ref>[https://www.afghanistan-analysts.org/six-days-that-shook-kabul-the-3-hut-uprising-first-urban-protest-against-the-soviet-occupation/ Six Days That Shook Kabul: The ‘3 Hut uprising’, first urban protest against the Soviet occupation]</ref>，并于1980年6月8日遭处决。

'''马里斯维尔'''（{{lang|en|Marysville}}）是[[美国|美国]][[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[联合县_(俄亥俄州)|联合县]]一个城市和县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx|accessdate=2011-06-07|title=Find a County|publisher=National Association of Counties}}</ref> 2010年人口普查时人口为22,094人。 这比2000年增长了38.59％。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://factfinder.census.gov/servlet/SAFFPopulation?_event=Search&_name=MARYSVILLE&_state=04000US39&_county=MARYSVILLE&_cityTown=MARYSVILLE&_zip=&_sse=on&_lang=en&pctxt=fph |title=2006 US Census estimate |publisher=Factfinder.census.gov |date= |accessdate=2010-04-25}}</ref>

马里斯维尔的长期标语是“这里的草更绿”。<ref>{{cite web | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=dNhLCAAAQBAJ&pg=PT43&dq=Marysville,+Ohio+where+the+grass+is+greener&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0CCUQ6AEwAmoVChMIhomStMPTyAIVghw-Ch2DkAgo#v=onepage&q=Marysville%2C%20Ohio%20where%20the%20grass%20is%20greener&f=false | title=The Journey - Bean Town to L.A. | publisher=Lulu Press, Inc | date=13 March 2013 | accessdate=21 October 2015 | author= William Malic | pages= Chapter 7}}</ref>

2008年12月，马里斯维尔被[[第一夫人|第一夫人]][[劳拉·布什|劳拉·布什]]指定为“保护美国社区”。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.marysvillejt.com/web/index.php?sid=50&id=3329|title=City receives special designation |date= 22 December 2008 |first= Ryan|last= Horns|newspaper= Times-Journal|location=Marysville, OH|accessdate= 7 August 2013}}</ref>

'''马步康'''（[[小儿经|小儿经]]：{{lang|zh-Arab|ﻣَﺎ  ﺑُﻮْ ﻛْﺎ}}），[[马家军|马家军]]成员，[[回族|回族]]将领和[[军阀|军阀]]。他是[[马兴旺|马兴旺]]的第五子[[马海瀚|马海瀚]]之孙，[[马政|马政]]之子。[[中华民国|中华民国]]年间，他在[[甘肃|甘肃]]周边有一支军队。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=mwDJ-3XPNooC&pg=PA230&dq=ma+bukang&hl=en&ei=TniqTMeiOML88Aa4-8m7Bw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCgQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=ma%20bukang%20dungan%20military%20force&f=false|title=In the footsteps of Genghis Khan|author=John DeFrancis|year=1993|publisher=University of Hawaii Press|location=|isbn=0-8248-1493-2|page=221|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>他的绰号是“小大马”。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=mwDJ-3XPNooC&dq=ma+bukang&q=ma+bukang#v=snippet&q=ma%20bukang%20little%20big%20horse&f=false|title=In the footsteps of Genghis Khan|author=John DeFrancis|year=1993|publisher=University of Hawaii Press|location=|isbn=0-8248-1493-2|page=227|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>在堂弟[[马仲英|马仲英]]失踪后，西甘肃无人管领，他控制了那里。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=mwDJ-3XPNooC&dq=ma+bukang&q=ma+bukang#v=snippet&q=ma%20bukang%20referred%20the&f=false|title=In the footsteps of Genghis Khan|author=John DeFrancis|year=1993|publisher=University of Hawaii Press|location=|isbn=0-8248-1493-2|page=229|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>

日本战机轰炸[[西宁|西宁]]时，马步康和堂弟[[马步芳|马步芳]]在商量兵力调动和补充抗日前线的堂叔[[马彪|马彪]]师兵员补充之事。<ref>http://3g.nuoha.net/www/book/157471/00037.html</ref>

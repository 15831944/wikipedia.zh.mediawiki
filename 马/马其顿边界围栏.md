'''马其顿边界围栏'''是[[馬其頓共和國|馬其頓共和國]]为应对[[欧洲移民危机|欧洲移民危机]]在其与[[希腊|希腊]]边界上修建的[[隔离墙|隔离墙]]。隔离墙始建于2015年11月，效仿类似的[[匈牙利邊界圍欄|匈牙利邊界圍欄]]，<ref>{{cite news|last1=Hall|first1=John|title=European refugee crisis: Macedonia builds 10ft-high razor-topped fence along Greek border|url=http://www.ibtimes.co.uk/european-refugee-crisis-macedonia-builds-10-foot-high-razor-topped-fence-along-greek-border-1537078|accessdate=2017-12-23|work=国际财经时报英国版|date=2016-01-10|language=en}}</ref>到2016年初为止，围栏修建了{{convert|30|km|abbr=off}}长。<ref name="dailymail March 6, 2016">{{cite news|title=Macedonia reveals plans to build 200 mile long fence on Greek border|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-3479372/Macedonia-reveals-plans-build-200-mile-long-fence-border-Greece-protected-guards-armed-Tasers-ahead-EU-summit-deal-migrant-crisis.html|accessdate=2017-12-23|work=邮报在线}}</ref>

== 历史 ==
[[File:Balkans_regions_map.png|thumbnail]]邻居（希腊在它的南边，灰色）：{{legend|#d5dc76|马其顿}}{{legend|#d56d76|[[阿尔巴尼亚|阿尔巴尼亚]]}}{{legend|#71b37b|[[保加利亚|保加利亚]]}}{{legend|#4f93c0|[[科索沃|科索沃]]}}{{legend|#b383b3|[[塞尔维亚|塞尔维亚]]}}]]

2015年11月29日，马其顿军队开始在希腊边界修建栅栏。<ref name="BBC News 2015">{{cite web | title=Stranded migrants battle police on Greece-Macedonia border | website=BBC新闻 | date=2015-11-28 | url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-34954127 | accessdate=2015-11-28}}</ref>一名摩洛哥男子在一次事故中受伤，导致警察和移民发生冲突，造成18人受伤。<ref name="AP news">{{cite web | title=Migrants clash with Macedonian police on Greek border | website=美联社新闻 | date=2015-11-28 | url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/babcfa8e41074c189aae9d765845bd6c/migrants-clash-macedonian-police-greek-border| accessdate=2015-11-28}}</ref>据《[[卫报|卫报]]》报道<ref>{{cite news|last1=Press|first1=Associated|title=Migrants attack Macedonian police as construction of Greek border fence begins|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/nov/29/migrants-attack-macedonian-police-as-construction-of-greek-border-fence-begins|accessdate=2017-12-23|work=卫报|date=2015-11-29}}</ref>，在修建隔离墙的初期，马其顿警察遭到了移民的袭击。二月，马其顿士兵开始在先前围栏一米处设置了第二个围栏。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.balkanews.org/index.php/2016/02/07/more-problems-at-fyrom-greek-border-slows-refugees-journey-north/|title=More problems at FYROM-Greek border slows refugees’ journey north|date=2016-02-07|author=欧洲新闻台|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160213130956/http://www.balkanews.org/index.php/2016/02/07/more-problems-at-fyrom-greek-border-slows-refugees-journey-north/|archivedate=2016-02-13}}</ref>

奥地利在二月份开始限制其境内的庇护申请后<ref>{{cite news|title=Migrant crisis: Austria asylum cap begins despite EU anger|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-35609823|accessdate=2017-12-27|work=BBC新闻|date=2016-02-19|language=en}}</ref>，[[斯洛文尼亚|斯洛文尼亚]]、[[塞尔维亚|塞尔维亚]]等[[巴尔干|巴尔干]]国家对移民入境实行限制。<ref name="BBC February 29, 2016">{{cite news|title=Migrants stranded by Macedonia closure|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-35629921|accessdate=2017-12-27|work=BBC新闻|date=2016-02-22|language=en}}</ref>因此，马其顿限制移民进入其境内，导致数千名移民被滞留在希腊境内，尤其是在[[伊多梅尼|伊多梅尼]]边境附近。<ref name="BBC February 29, 2016"/>2016年2月29日，数以百计的移民用石头袭击了防暴警察，试图用捣毁的路牌和灯柱制作的[[冲车|冲车]]来打破边界屏障。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/europe/greece/12177750/Migrant-crowd-uses-battering-ram-to-break-open-Macedonia-fences.html|title=Migrant crowd uses battering ram to break open Macedonia fences|first=Matthew|last=Holehouse|date=2016-02-29|publisher=[[每日电讯报|电讯报]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-35686104|publisher=[[英国广播公司|英国广播公司]]|title=Migrants use 'battering ram' on Greece-Macedonia border fence|date=2016-02-29}}</ref>

== 伊多梅尼移民营 ==
2016年1月，奥地利限制了允许同时进入该国的移民数量。<ref>{{cite news|title=Austria Imposes Asylum Cap to Tackle Refugee Crisis|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/storyline/europes-border-crisis/refugee-crisis-austria-limits-asylum-1-5-percent-population-n500326|accessdate=2017-12-27|work=NBC 新闻|date=2016-01-20|language=en}}</ref>2016年2月，奥地利内政部长米克尔·莱特纳宣布限制每天过境的移民人数。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Rankin|first1=Jennifer|title=Austria dismisses criticism of its plan to limit daily refugee numbers|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2016/feb/18/austria-dismisses-criticism-of-its-plan-to-limit-daily-refugee-numbers|accessdate=2017-12-27|work=卫报|date=2016-02-18}}</ref>随后，斯洛文尼亚禁止移民过境，马其顿宣布对移民关闭其与希腊的边境。<ref>{{cite news|title=Macedonia shuts border to migrants|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-35763101|accessdate=2017-12-27|work=BBC新闻|date=2016-03-09}}</ref>[[阿尔巴尼亚|阿尔巴尼亚]]增派警力保卫边境，并获得了[[意大利|意大利]]陆地和海上的“人员和设备”援助。<ref>{{cite news|title=Refugees may start to cross over Albania|url=https://www.economist.com/news/europe/21694411-just-twenty-five-years-ago-albanians-left-country-droves-refugees-may-start-cross|accessdate=2017-12-27|work=经济学人|date=2016-03-08}}</ref>3月，马其顿宣布计划将其{{convert|30|km|abbr=off}}的障碍物延长到{{convert|320|km|abbr=off}}。<ref name="dailymail March 6, 2016"/>

据[[路透社|路透社]]报道，2016年5月，有超过10,000名移民在边境定居，成为欧洲最大的难民营，其中有清真寺、学校和商业区。<ref>{{cite news|title=Tired of waiting, Greece's migrants turn to business to survive|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-europe-migrants-greece-idUSKCN0Y118J|accessdate=2017-12-27|work=[[路透社|路透社]]|date=2016-05-11}}</ref>

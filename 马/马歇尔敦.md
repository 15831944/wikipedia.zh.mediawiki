'''马歇尔敦'''（Marshalltown）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[马歇尔县_(爱阿华州)|马歇尔县]]的城市，是马歇尔县的县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx|accessdate=2011-06-07|title=Find a County|publisher=National Association of Counties}}</ref>2010年人口普查中的人口为27,552人，而2000年为26,009人。<ref name=USCB>{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table|title=Population & Housing Occupancy Status 2010|publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] American FactFinder|accessdate= 2011-08-04}}</ref><ref name=IADC>{{cite web|url= http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10|title= Data from the 2010 Census|publisher= State Data Center of Iowa|accessdate= 2011-08-04|deadurl= yes|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110718201045/http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10|archivedate= 2011-07-18}}</center></ref>

==历史==
[[File:Anson_Log_Cabin_Plaque.jpg|thumb]]
亨利·安森是马歇尔敦的第一位[[欧洲|欧洲]]定居者。 1851年4月，安森发现他所说的“爱荷华州最美丽的地方”。<ref>{{cite web | title = History | publisher = Marshalltown Iowa Community Link | url = http://communitylink.com/us/ia/marshalltown/profile/history.htm | accessdate = 2010-12-13 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110718075045/http://communitylink.com/us/ia/marshalltown/profile/history.htm | archivedate = 2011-07-18 }}</ref>在爱荷华河与林恩溪之间的高处，安森建造了一个木屋。 位于西大街112号的牌匾标志着小屋的所在地。<ref>{{cite web | title = Henry Anson| publisher = Anson Elementary School | url = http://www.marshalltown.k12.ia.us/anson/history.html| accessdate = 2010-12-13 }}</ref>在1853年，安森以他的前住所[[密歇根州|密歇根州]]的马歇尔（Marshall）命名为马歇尔镇（Marshall）。<ref>{{cite book|author=Chicago and North Western Railway Company|title=A History of the Origin of the Place Names Connected with the Chicago & North Western and Chicago, St. Paul, Minneapolis & Omaha Railways|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OspBAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA99|year=1908|page=99}}</ref>

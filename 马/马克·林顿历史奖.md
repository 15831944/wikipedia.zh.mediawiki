'''马克·林顿历史奖'''（{{lang-en|'''Mark Lynton History Prize'''}}）是一个文学奖，每年一次，奖金为1万美元，奖给历史学术著作的写作者。<ref name=Lukas>{{cite web|title=J. Anthony Lukas Prize Project|url=http://www.nieman.harvard.edu/NiemanFoundation/Awards/AwardsAtAGlance/JAnthonyLukasPrizeProject.aspx|publisher=Nieman Foundation for Journalism at Harvard|accessdate=16 March 2011}}</ref>该奖由[[哥伦比亚大学|哥伦比亚大学]]新闻学院建立。<ref name=Lukas /><ref>[http://www.journalism.columbia.edu/cs/ContentServer/jrn/1165270069782/page/1175374449484/JRNSimplePage2.htm The Lukas Prize Project - Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080509120529/http://www.journalism.columbia.edu/cs/ContentServer/jrn/1165270069782/page/1175374449484/JRNSimplePage2.htm |date=2008-05-09 }}</ref>奖项的赞助者是[[纳粹德国|纳粹德国]][[二战|二战]]军官马克·林顿。<ref>[http://www.journalism.columbia.edu/page/455-sam-freedman-on-j-anthony-lukas/189?printing=true "A Heart, A Brain, and a Pair of Shoes,"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120402144444/http://www.journalism.columbia.edu/page/455-sam-freedman-on-j-anthony-lukas/189?printing=true |date=2012-04-02 }} by Samuel G. Freedman, Salon, June 12, 1997</ref>

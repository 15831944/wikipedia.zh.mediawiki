'''马拉瓦赫'''（{{Lang-ar|ملوح}}，{{Lang-he|מלאווח or מלווח}}）是[[索马里|索马里]]、[[也门|也门]]的传统饮食中的一种炸面饼。

== 概述 ==
马拉瓦赫外形像是一个厚煎饼，但其实是由多层薄质的、涂有油或脂肪的[[酥皮|酥皮]]制成，并在平底锅上加温煎熟<ref>Rachel Yedid & Danny Bar-Maoz (ed.), ''Ascending the Palm Tree – An Anthology of the Yemenite Jewish Heritage'', E'ele BeTamar: Rehovot 2018, p. 132 {{ISBN|978-965-7121-33-7}}</ref><ref name=as />。传统上常与煮熟的鸡蛋、[[苏胡克|苏胡克]]（{{lang|ar|سَحاوِق}}）、碎番茄酱同食<ref name=as>{{Cite news|url=http://www.riverdalepress.com/stories/Old-memories-new-dishes-malawach-reinvented,48118?content_source=&category_id=&search_filter=cooking&event_mode=&event_ts_from=&list_type=&order_by=&order_sort=&content_class=&sub_type=&town_id=|title=Old memories, new dishes: malawach reinvented|newspaper=The Riverdale Press|accessdate=2018-11-02|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161009163748/http://www.riverdalepress.com/stories/Old-memories-new-dishes-malawach-reinvented,48118?content_source=&category_id=&search_filter=cooking&event_mode=&event_ts_from=&list_type=&order_by=&order_sort=&content_class=&sub_type=&town_id=|archive-date=2016-10-09|dead-url=no}}</ref>，也可佐以[[脱乳清酸奶|脱乳清酸奶]]和果酱<ref name="SolomonovCook">{{cite book|author1=Michael Solomonov|author2=Steven Cook|title=Israeli Soul Easy, Essential, Delicious|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=zRhDDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA298|date=2018-10-16|publisher=Houghton Mifflin Harcourt|isbn=978-0-544-97127-1|pages=298}}</ref>或者搭配蜂蜜食用<ref>{{Cite book| last = Roden| first = Claudia| authorlink = Claudia Roden| title =The Book of Jewish Food: An Odyssey from Samarkand to New York| publisher = Knopf | year = 1997| page = 549| isbn = 0-394-53258-9}}</ref><ref name="Scheft2016">{{cite book|author=Uri Scheft|title=Breaking Breads A New World of Israeli Baking--Flatbreads, Stuffed Breads, Challahs, Cookies, and the Legendary Chocolate Babka|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=iJ3RDAAAQBAJ&pg=PA145|date=2016-10-18|publisher=Artisan|isbn=978-1-57965-682-9|pages=145}}</ref>。

马拉瓦赫与{{le|雅赫南|jachnun}}类似，使用层压面团制成<ref name="SolomonovCook2018">{{cite book|author1=Michael Solomonov|author2=Steven Cook|title=Israeli Soul Easy, Essential, Delicious|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=zRhDDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA294|date=2018-10-16|publisher=Houghton Mifflin Harcourt|isbn=978-0-544-97127-1|pages=294}}</ref>，故而可能是[[塞法迪犹太人|塞法迪犹太人]]创制的[[酥皮糕點|酥皮糕点]]的变种之一<ref>{{Cite book|title=以色列和巴勒斯坦|last=丹尼尔·罗宾逊（Daniel Robinson）著|first=|publisher=北京：中国地图出版社|year=2016.05|isbn=978-7-5031-9115-2|location=|pages=395}}</ref>。有观点推测这种食物可能是被西班牙驱逐出境的犹太人带到也门的<ref name="Judaism2001">{{cite book|author=Steven M. Lowenstein Isadore Levine Professor of Jewish History University of Judaism|title=The Jewish Cultural Tapestry : International Jewish Folk Traditions International Jewish Folk Traditions|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=EWToS3VyqVMC|date=2001-05-17|publisher=Oxford University Press, USA|isbn=978-0-19-531360-4}}</ref><ref name="Travel2014">{{cite book|author=DK Travel|title=Top 10 Israel, Sinai, and Petra|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=y6FtBAAAQBAJ&pg=PT80|date=2014-10-01|publisher=DK Publishing|isbn=978-1-4654-3314-5|pages=80}}</ref>。马拉瓦赫目前是[[也门犹太人|也门犹太人]]的主食之一。这道菜也经犹太移民带到[[以色列|以色列]]，成为不同背景与族群的以色列人都喜欢的一种{{le|治愈美食|Comfort food}}<ref name="Grishaver1998">{{cite book|author=Joel Lurie Grishaver|title=Being Torah Student Commentary, 2|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=pjJut4rGyJcC&pg=PA84|date=1998-10-01|publisher=Torah Aura Productions|isbn=978-0-933873-10-0|pages=84}}</ref>。冷冻的马拉瓦赫可以代替面团，成为不同菜肴的主料<ref name=as /><ref name="Marks2010">{{cite book|author=Gil Marks|title=Encyclopedia of Jewish Food|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=gFK_yx7Ps7cC|date=2010-11-17|publisher=HMH|isbn=978-0-544-18631-6|access-date=2018-11-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160810014900/https://books.google.com/books?id=gFK_yx7Ps7cC|archive-date=2016-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

在也门的马拉瓦赫在制作、味道和质地上，均与[[索马里|索马里]]的马拉瓦赫（{{lang|en|malawax}}）相似，后者通常配以厚重的酥油与蜂蜜，作为周末的早饭。它也与[[印度南部|印度南部]]的{{le|印度油饼|Paratha}}（又名喀拉拉邦油饼，{{lang-en|Kerala paratha}}）、[[印度北部|印度北部]]的拉卡油饼（{{lang-en|Lachha paratha}}）相似，这两种油饼均是{{le|印度饮食|Indian cuisine}}中的流行面食<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.manjulaskitchen.com/lacha-paratha-layered-flat-bread/|title=Lacha Paratha - Whole Wheat Layered Flat Bread - Manjula's Kitchen - Indian Vegetarian Recipes|date=2013-02-03|newspaper=Manjula's Kitchen|accessdate=2018-11-02|language=en-US|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180406160721/http://www.manjulaskitchen.com/lacha-paratha-layered-flat-bread|archive-date=2018-04-06|dead-url=no}}</ref>。它也类似于常出现于北非[[摩洛哥|摩洛哥]]和[[阿尔及利亚|阿尔及利亚]]（{{le|柏柏尔饮食|Berber cuisine|柏柏尔}}）料理中的{{le|曼斯曼|msemmen}}。曼斯曼在食用时可搭配蜂蜜与奶油，也可搭配红辣椒与番茄等调料。

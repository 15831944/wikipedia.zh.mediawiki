'''马来西亚掌玺大臣'''（{{lang-ms|'''Penyimpan Mohor Besar Raja-raja'''}}；{{lang-en|'''Keeper of the Rulers' Seal'''}}）是[[马来西亚|马来西亚]]的掌[[玺|玺]]大臣，负责保管[[最高元首|最高元首]]陛下和各州[[马来统治者|马来统治者]]的皇玺。皇玺是马来统治者传统权威的象征。<ref>[http://www.majlisraja-raja.gov.my/makl_jabatan_01_fungsi_mbrr.html Fungsi Mohor Besar Raja-raja] {{Wayback|url=http://www.majlisraja-raja.gov.my/makl_jabatan_01_fungsi_mbrr.html |date=20100826073141 }} (in Malay). Office of the Keeper of the Rulers' Seal. Accessed June 8, 2010</ref>

掌玺大臣也可以在[[马来统治者会议|马来统治者会议]]的御准下使用最高元首陛下的皇玺。掌玺大臣同时也会兼任马来统治者会议的[[秘书|秘书]]，每年依法召开三次法定会议（最高元首陛下可随时要求召开会议）。<ref>[http://www.majlisraja-raja.gov.my/soalan_lazim.html Soalam lazim] {{Wayback|url=http://www.majlisraja-raja.gov.my/soalan_lazim.html |date=20100827184317 }} (in Malay). Office of the Keeper of the Rulers' Seal. Accessed June 8, 2010</ref>

掌玺大臣一职是依据《[[马来西亚联邦宪法|马来西亚联邦宪法]]》运作，由马来统治者会议委任。<ref>[http://www.malaysianmonarchy.org.my/portal_bi/rk1/rk1a.php?id=rk1_5&titleBI=Role%20of%20the%20Conference%20of%20Rulers%20in%20Constitutional%20Monarchy%20System%20in%20Malaysia Role Of The Conference Of Rulers In Constitutional Monarchy System In Malaysia] Malaysian Monarchy. Accessed June 8, 2010</ref>

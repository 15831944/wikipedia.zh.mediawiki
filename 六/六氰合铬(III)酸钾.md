'''六氰合铬(III)酸钾'''是一种[[配位化合物|配位化合物]]，化学式为K<sub>3</sub>[Cr(CN)<sub>6</sub>]，这种黄色晶体属[[正交晶系|正交晶系]]，[[空间群|空间群]]''Pcan''。<ref>JAGNER S, LJUNGSTROEM E, VANNERBERG N G. The crystal structure of potassium hexacyanochromate (III), K3Cr (CN) 6[J]. ACTA CHEMICA SCANDINAVICA SERIES A-PHYSICAL AND INORGANIC CHEMISTRY, 1974 (6): 623-630.</ref>它通常利用[[乙酸铬|乙酸铬]]和[[氰化钾|氰化钾]]反应得到。<ref name=Handbook />
: Cr(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> + 6 KCN → K<sub>3</sub>[Cr(CN)<sub>6</sub>] + 3CH<sub>3</sub>COOK

它可以在[[氰化铵|氰化铵]]和六氰合铬(0)酸钾的反应中产生：<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0020169300824470|issue=2|doi=10.1016/s0020-1693(00)82447-0|pages=143–145|title=Ammonium cyanide: its preparation and reactions with transition metal(0) cyanides|volume=89|journal=Inorganica Chimica Acta|date=1984-10|issn=0020-1693|accessdate=2018-08-01|author=Elaine S. Dodsworth, Janice P. Eaton, Michael P. Ellerby, David Nicholls}}</ref>
: 6 NH<sub>4</sub>CN + K<sub>6</sub>[Cr(CN)<sub>6</sub>] → 2 K<sub>3</sub>[Cr(CN)<sub>6</sub>] + 6 KCN + 6 NH<sub>3</sub> + 3 H<sub>2</sub>

它在氢气中加热至400°C时有[[氨|氨]]和[[氰化氢|氰化氢]]放出，并留下化学式为K<sub>3</sub>Cr(CN)<sub>4</sub>的橄榄绿色固体。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ic50056a019|issue=10|doi=10.1021/ic50056a019|pages=1849–1854|title=Reactions of potassium hexacyanochromate(III) and some transition metal cyanonitrosyls with molecular hydrogen at elevated temperatures|volume=6|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|date=1967-10|issn=0020-1669|language=EN|accessdate=2018-08-01|author=David F. Banks, Jacob Kleinberg}}</ref>在空气中150°C以上即可分解。其水溶液对光或热不稳定，易析出Cr(OH)<sub>3</sub>。<ref name=Handbook />

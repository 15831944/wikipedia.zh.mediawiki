'''六氢合铁(II)酸镁'''是一种[[配位化合物|配位化合物]]，化学式为Mg<sub>2</sub>FeH<sub>6</sub>。它是绿色[[抗磁性|抗磁性]]的固体，在干燥空气中稳定。

==制备==

六氢合铁(II)酸镁可由[[铁|铁]]和[[镁|镁]]的混合粉末在高压[[氢气|氢气]]中加热得到：<ref>J. J. Didisheim, P. Zolliker, K. Yvon, P. Fischer, J. Schefer, M. Gubelmann, A. F. Williams "Dimagnesium iron(II) hydride, Mg<sub>2</sub>FeH<sub>6</sub>, containing octahedral FeH<sub>6</sub><sup>4−</sup> anions" ''Inorganic Chemistry'' 1984, vol. 23, pp 1953–1957. {{DOI|10.1021/ic00181a032}}.</ref>
:2 Mg  +  Fe  +  3 H<sub>2</sub>   →   Mg<sub>2</sub>FeH<sub>6</sub>

[[氢化镁|氢化镁]]和铁粉在氩气中的研磨也能得到产物<ref>{{cite journal|title=Direct synthesis of Mg2FeH6 by mechanical alloying|volume=280|issue=1|pages=306–309|date=1998-10-23|issn=0925-8388|doi=10.1016/S0925-8388(98)00725-7|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0925838898007257|journal=Journal of Alloys and Compounds|accessdate=2019-02-11|author=J Huot, S Boily, E Akiba, R Schulz}}</ref>，有氢气存在时，氢气也会有所作用：<ref name=Gennari>{{cite journal|title=Synthesis of Mg2FeH6 by reactive mechanical alloying: formation and decomposition properties|volume=339|issue=1|pages=261–267|date=2002-06-13|issn=0925-8388|doi=10.1016/S0925-8388(01)02009-6|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0925838801020096|journal=Journal of Alloys and Compounds|accessdate=2019-02-11|author=F. C. Gennari, F. J. Castro, J. J. Andrade Gamboa}}</ref>
:2 MgH<sub>2</sub> + Fe + H<sub>2</sub> {{eqm}} Mg<sub>2</sub>FeH<sub>6</sub>
: 3 MgH<sub>2</sub> + Fe {{eqm}} Mg<sub>2</sub>FeH<sub>6</sub> + Mg
==结构及性质==
六氢合铁(II)酸镁和K<sub>2</sub>PtCl<sub>6</sub>[[同构|同构]]，中心的[FeH<sub>6</sub>]<sup>4−</sup>采取[[八面体形分子构型|八面体形分子构型]]，Fe-H键键长为1.56 Å。Mg<sup>2+</sup>与八面体的面连接，Mg-H键键长为2.38 Å。类似的阴离子还包括[RuH<sub>6</sub>]<sup>4−</sup>、[OsH<sub>6</sub>]<sup>4−</sup>和[PtH<sub>6</sub>]<sup>2−</sup>。<ref>Robert Bau, Mary H. Drabnis "Structures of transition metal hydrides determined by neutron diffraction" Inorganica Chimica Acta 1997, vol. 259, 27ff.{{DOI|10.1016/S0020-1693(97)89125-6}}</ref>

六氢合铁(II)酸镁受热分解，产物是铁、镁和氢气。<ref name=Gennari />
==衍生物==
尽管Mg<sub>2</sub>FeH<sub>6</sub>不溶于通常的溶剂，但其衍生物是可溶的。例如，Mg<sub>4</sub>Br<sub>4</sub>([[四氢呋喃|THF]])<sub>4</sub>FeH<sub>6</sub>及其[[醇盐|醇盐]]是可溶的。对这些化合物的分析测试可以表明，[[氢化物|负氢]][[配體|配體]]的[[晶体场理论|晶体场]]比[[氰化物|氰配体]]更弱。<ref>R.B. King "Structure and bonding in homoleptic transition metal hydride anions" Coordination Chemistry Reviews, 2000, vol. 200–202, p. 813–829. {{DOI|10.1016/S0010-8545(00)00263-0}}</ref>

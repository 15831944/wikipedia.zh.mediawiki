'''六氟锑酸铜'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Cu(SbF<sub>6</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。它对水敏感，痕量的水会使其水解，生成H<sub>3</sub>OSbF<sub>6</sub>、H<sub>3</sub>OSb<sub>2</sub>F<sub>11</sub>及H<sub>3</sub>OCu(SbF<sub>6</sub>)<sub>3</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0022113904002453|pages=1723–1733|title=Recent achievements in the synthesis and characterization of metal hexafluorantimonates and hexafluoroaurates|journal=Journal of Fluorine Chemistry|volume=125|issue=11|author=Zoran Mazej|doi=10.1016/j.jfluchem.2004.09.008}}</ref>它也对{{chem|OH|-}}敏感。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://doi.wiley.com/10.1002/zaac.19562850318|pages=262–270|title=Zur Kenntnis von SbCl5, HSbCl6 und HSbF6|journal=Zeitschrift f�r anorganische und allgemeine Chemie|volume=285|issue=3-6|date=1956-06-01|issn=1521-3749|language=de|accessdate=2018-04-12|doi=10.1002/zaac.19562850318|author=Alwin Meuwsen, Horst M�gling}}</ref>可用作有机反应的[[催化剂|催化剂]]。<ref name=Chang />

==制备==
六氟锑酸铜可由[[六氟锑酸银|六氟锑酸银]]和[[溴化铜|溴化铜]]在二氯甲烷（DCM）中反应得到：<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://doi.wiley.com/10.1002/anie.201611734|pages=3055–3058|title=Reaction of Donor-Acceptor Cyclobutanes with Indoles: A General Protocol for the Formal Total Synthesis of (±)-Strychnine and the Total Synthesis of (±)-Akuammicine|journal=Angewandte Chemie International Edition|volume=56|issue=11|date=2017-03-06|issn=1521-3773|language=en|accessdate=2018-04-12|doi=10.1002/anie.201611734|author=Liang-Wen Feng, Hai Ren, Hu Xiong, Pan Wang, Lijia Wang, Yong Tang}}</ref>
: 2 AgSbF<sub>6</sub> + CuBr<sub>2</sub> → 2 AgBr + Cu(SbF<sub>6</sub>)<sub>2</sub>

六氟锑酸铜也可以由[[氟磺酸铜|氟磺酸铜]]在[[五氟化锑|五氟化锑]]中于50 °C发生[[溶剂分解反应|溶剂分解反应]]得到：<ref name=Cader>{{cite journal|title=Hexafluoro antimonates(V) of divalent metals, synthesis and properties|date=1989-11-01|doi=10.1139/v89-262|journal=Canadian Journal of Chemistry|pages=1700–1707|volume=67|issue=11|issn=0008-4042|url=http://www.nrcresearchpress.com/doi/10.1139/v89-262|accessdate=2018-04-12|author=M. S. R. Cader, F. Aubke}}</ref>
:Cu(SO<sub>3</sub>F)<sub>2</sub> + 6 SbF<sub>5</sub> → Cu(SbF<sub>6</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + 2 Sb<sub>2</sub>F<sub>9</sub>SO<sub>3</sub>F

[[氟化铜|氟化铜]]和五氟化锑在无水[[氟化氢|氟化氢]]或[[二氧化硫|二氧化硫]]中反应也能生成六氟锑酸铜：<ref>Gantar, Darja; Leban, Ivan; Frlec, Boris; Holloway, John H. Metal(II) hexafluoroantimonates: preparation and characterization of MF<sub>2</sub>·2SbF<sub>5</sub> (M = Mg, Ni, Zn, Fe, Co, Cu, Cr, Ag, Cd, or Pb) and the x-​ray structure determination of silver bis(hexafluoroantimonate). ''Journal of the Chemical Society, Dalton Transactions: Inorganic Chemistry (1972-1999)''. 1987. 10: 2379-2383. ISSN:0300-9246.</ref>
: CuF<sub>2</sub> + 2 SbF<sub>5</sub> → Cu(SbF<sub>6</sub>)<sub>2</sub>

六氟锑酸铜六水合物可以在[[丙酮|丙酮]]中结晶，经[[五氧化二磷|五氧化二磷]]干燥得到。<ref name=Chang>{{cite book|url=http://doi.wiley.com/10.1002/047084289X.rn00567|title=Copper(II) Hexafluoroantimonate|language=en|publisher=American Cancer Society|accessdate=2018-04-12|author=Shuh-Kuen Chang, Peter Selvaraj|doi=10.1002/047084289x.rn00567}}</ref>

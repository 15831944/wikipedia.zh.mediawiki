'''六角長形螺母''', 也稱為延伸[[螺母|螺母]]，是用於連接兩個陽螺紋的螺紋緊固件，最常見的是[[螺紋|螺紋]]桿。<ref name="McMaster-Carr-1">{{cite web|title=Coupling Nuts |work=About Nuts, Materials, and Finishes |publisher=[[McMaster-Carr|McMaster-Carr]] |url=http://www.mcmaster.com/#hex-coupling-nuts/=m1m9ak|accessdate=26 March 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Extension Nut / Coupling Nut |url=http://www.kisanengineers.com/nuts.html|publisher=Kisan Engineers Machine Tools|website=KisanEngineers.com |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111227100709/http://kisanengineers.com/nuts.html |archivedate=27 December 2011 |deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|author=American Railway Master Mechanics' Association |year=1922 |title=Locomotive Cyclopedia of American Practice |edition=sixth|location=New York |publisher=Simmons-Boardman |page=[https://books.google.com/books?id=K4YMAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA32 32]}}</ref>緊固件的外側通常是六角形，因此扳手可以握住它。變型包括“減少連接螺母”，用於連接兩個不同尺寸螺紋的“視孔連接螺母”，它們有一個觀察孔，用於觀察接合量和用左手螺紋連接螺母。這些用於由較短長度的桿組成長桿組件。桿擰入聯接[[螺母|螺母]]一定距離。聯接螺母尺寸由工業緊固件協會以標準IFI-128描述。這是一個簡單的完成。根據所用的棒料，它可以是冷拉或熱軋。有兩種不同的螺紋尺寸和輪廓螺紋連接螺母被稱為異徑接頭螺母。<ref name="McMaster-Carr-1" />

可以使用延伸螺母向內擰緊桿組件或向外按壓桿組件。<ref>{{Cite book|last=Nunn|first=Richard V. |year=1982 |title=Popular Mechanics Guide to Do-It-Yourself Materials |location=New York |publisher=Hearst Books |page=54 |isbn=978-0-87851-150-1}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|last1=Olin |first1=L. |last2=Stanland |first2=A. |last3=Allard |first3=F. |chapter=Fiber Optic Hull Penetrators for Submarines |year=1987 |editor=O'Donnell-Leach, Karen |title=Military Applications of Fiber Optics |series=Fiber Optics Reprint Series Volume 14 |location=Brighton, Massachusetts|publisher=Information Gatekeepers Inc. |pages=58–63, page 61 }}</ref>

'''六羰基丙二硫醇二铁'''是一种有机[[铁化合物|铁化合物]]，化学式为Fe<sub>2</sub>(S<sub>2</sub>C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>6</sub>)(CO)<sub>6</sub>。它是红色[[抗磁性|抗磁性]]的固体。<ref>Winter, A., Zsolnai, L., Huttner, G., "Zweikernige und dreikernige Carbonyleisenkomplexe mit 1,2- und 1,3-Dithiolatobrückenliganden", Z. Naturforsch. 1982, 37b, 1430.</ref>它是具有6个末端[[羰基|羰基]]配体结构的化合物，<ref>Lyon, E. J., Georgakaki, I. P., Reibenspies, J. H., Darensbourg, M. Y., "Carbon Monoxide and Cyanide Ligands in a Classical Organometallic Complex Model for Fe-Only Hydrogenase", Angew. Chem. Int. Ed. 1999, 38, 3178.</ref>是氢化酶模拟物的前体。

该化合物可由[[1,3-丙二硫醇|1,3-丙二硫醇]]和[[十二羰基三铁|十二羰基三铁]]的反应制得： 
:2 Fe<sub>3</sub>(CO)<sub>12</sub> +  3 C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>6</sub>(SH)<sub>2</sub>   →   3 Fe<sub>2</sub>(S<sub>2</sub>C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>6</sub>)(CO)<sub>6</sub>  +  3 H<sub>2</sub> +  6 CO
化合物中的CO配体可以被[[氰基|氰基]]、[[膦|膦]]类、[[异腈|异腈]]、[[N-杂环卡宾|N-杂环卡宾]]及其它供体配体取代。单取代的[[乙腈|乙腈]]配合物可以[[原位|原位]]生成。<ref>Mack, A.; Rauchfuss, T. B., "(1,3-Propanedithiolato)hexacarbonyl and Cyanide Derivatives", Inorganic Synthesis. 2010, volume 35, 143. {{DOI|10.1002/9780470651568.ch7}}</ref><ref>Works, C., "Laboratory Experiment or Mini-Project for Inorganic Chemistry or Integrated Laboratory", Journal of Chemical Education. 2007, 84, 836.</ref>

用紫外光照射Fe<sub>2</sub>(S<sub>2</sub>C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>6</sub>)(CO)<sub>6</sub>，CO光解，生成短暂的不饱和物种中间体，紧接着得到[[溶剂|溶剂]][[加合物|加合物]]。<ref>Mack, A. R. Ridley, A. I. Stewart, K. Adamczyk, H. N. Ghosh, B. Kerkeni, Z. X. Guo, E. T. J. Nibbering, C. J. Pickett, N. T. Hunt "(1,3-Propanedithiolato)hexacarbonyl and Cyanide Derivatives", Inorg. Chem. 2008, 47, 7453-7455 {{DOI|10.1021/ic800568k}}</ref>

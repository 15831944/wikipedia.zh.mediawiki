'''六角管狀苔屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Hexagonocaulon'''''}}）是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的一[[屬|屬]][[苔蘚|苔蘚]]，生存於中[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]。其[[化石|化石]]分布於[[南半球|南半球]]<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author={{link-en|西里爾·沃克 (古生物學家)|Cyril Walker (palaeontologist)|Walker, Cyril}} | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=290 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>，包括[[南極半島|南極半島]]。<ref name="dk">{{cite book | title=Prehistoric Life: the Definitive Visual History of Life on Earth | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|DK Publishing]] | author=Palmer, Douglas| year=2009 | edition=first American | location=New York City | page=199 | isbn=978-0-7566-5573-0|display-authors=etal}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
這種很小的苔蘚植物為貼地生長，植物體扁而寬，每隔1~2[[公分|公分]]便會有引人注目的[[Y|Y]]型分枝。底部的[[假根|假根]]使其能附著在地面上。它們有時會產生簡單的[[孢子|孢子]]。<ref name="Fossils" />

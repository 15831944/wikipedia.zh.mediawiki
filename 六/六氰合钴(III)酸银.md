'''六氰合钴(III)酸银'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Ag<sub>3</sub>[Co(CN)<sub>6</sub>]，属[[六方晶系|六方晶系]]，具有Co-CN-Ag-NC-Co链状结构<ref name=Brett>{{cite journal|url=http://www.nrcresearchpress.com/doi/10.1139/v2012-072|issue=10|doi=10.1139/v2012-072|pages=891–901|title=A solid-state NMR investigation of the colossal expansion material, Ag3Co(CN)6|volume=90|journal=Canadian Journal of Chemistry|date=2012-10|issn=0008-4042|accessdate=2018-07-29|author=Brett C. Feland, Guy M. Bernard, Roderick E. Wasylishen}}</ref>。它可由[[六氰合钴(III)酸钾|六氰合钴(III)酸钾]]和[[硝酸银|硝酸银]]反应沉淀得到，<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0022369714002364|doi=10.1016/j.jpcs.2014.10.006|pages=151–156|title=Pressure-enhanced decomposition of Ag3[Co(CN)6]|volume=77|journal=Journal of Physics and Chemistry of Solids|date=2015-02|issn=0022-3697|accessdate=2018-07-29|author=J. Catafesta, P.R. Rovani, C.A. Perottoni, A.S. Pereira}}</ref>产物经过水洗、60 °C烘干，得到纯品。它是已知的晶体框架相对热膨胀率最高的物质。<ref name=Brett />

'''六氟磷酸四乙腈合铜(I)'''是一种[[配位化合物|配位化合物]]，化学式为[Cu(CH<sub>3</sub>CN)<sub>4</sub>]PF<sub>6</sub>。它是无色固体，可用于其它铜配合物的合成。它是一种{{le|过渡金属腈配合物|transition metal nitrile complex}}。<ref name=Rach>Silvana F. Rach, Fritz E. Kühn "Nitrile Ligated Transition Metal Complexes with Weakly Coordinating Counteranions and Their Catalytic Applications" Chem. Rev., 2009, volume 109, pp 2061–2080. {{DOI|10.1021/cr800270h}}</ref>

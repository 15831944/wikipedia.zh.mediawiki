'''达祖丁·阿都拉曼'''（{{lang-ms|Tajuddin bin Abdul Rahman}}），[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[霹雳州|霹雳州]]{{pc|巴西沙叻}}[[国阵|国阵]][[巫统|巫统]][[马来西亚下议院|国会议员]]<ref>''[http://www.parlimen.gov.my/index.php?parti=All&p=All&Filter=Lihat&uweb=dr&modload=ahlidewan Senarai Ahli Dewan Rakyat]''. [[马来西亚国会|Parlimen Malaysia]], [2012-7-23]</ref>，也是[[巫统|巫统]]中央委员和前甘榜牙也州议员。曾在2013年至2018年间担任[[马来西亚农业及农基工业部|农业及农基工业部]]副部长。

== 争议 ==
他在1995年因被指称他支付600万令吉以确保他担任司长的职位而被巫统开除<ref>{{Cite web|title = Umno’s ‘six million dollar man’ gets deputy minister post - The Malaysian Insider|url = http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/umnos-six-million-dollar-man-gets-deputy-minister-post|accessdate = 2015-10-02|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151003112409/http://www.themalaysianinsider.com/malaysia/article/umnos-six-million-dollar-man-gets-deputy-minister-post|archivedate = 2015-10-03}}</ref>。臭名昭著的他曾发表“掌掴华人论”，并说华人不要遇到什么问题就向外人投诉，否则他将掌掴华人，引起[[马来西亚华人公会|马来西亚华人公会]]和反对党的不满<ref>http://news.ifeng.com/a/20151007/44789541_0.shtml</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Deputy Minister 'threatens to slap' Chinese who complain outside Malaysia - The Rakyat Post|url = http://www.therakyatpost.com/news/2015/10/01/deputy-minister-threatens-to-slap-chinese-who-complain-outside-malaysia/|accessdate = 2015-10-02}}</ref>

达祖丁是一名「直言不讳」的国会议员<ref>{{Cite news| last =| first =| author2 =| title =Tajuddin strikes conciliatory tone| newspaper =[[The_Star_(Malaysia)|The Star (Malaysia)]]| location =| pages =| publisher =| date =17 July 2008| url =http://thestar.com.my/news/story.asp?file=/2008/7/17/parliament/21845597&sec=parliament| accessdate =6 January 2010| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20110604051635/http://thestar.com.my/news/story.asp?file=%2F2008%2F7%2F17%2Fparliament%2F21845597&sec=parliament| archivedate =2011年6月4日}}
</ref>。在2008年，他在国会内称在野党议员[[M.古拉|M.古拉]]为「杂种」，<ref name=Sun>{{Cite news| last =| first =| author2 =| title =DAP condemn Tajuddin for disrespectful remark| newspaper =[[The_Sun_(Malaysia)|The Sun (Malaysia)]]| location =| pages =| publisher =| date =6 November 2008| url =http://www.thesundaily.com/article.cfm?id=27401| accessdate =6 January 2010}}{{dead link|date=2018年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}
</ref>还不时讲黄色笑话<ref name=Sun/>，并对前[[民联|民联]]议员使用不雅语言如“吉零”（keling）和“疯子”（biol）等，最后往往收回言论来逃避制裁<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/93857|title=达祖丁讲“出水”黄色笑话<br>冯宝君要求议长采取行动|date=2008-11-27|publisher=}}</ref>。

2016年3月31日，达祖丁公开拥护首相[[纳吉阿都拉萨|纳吉阿都拉萨]]和他的妻子[[罗斯玛·曼梳|罗斯玛·曼梳]]豪华的生活，<ref>{{cite news |url=https://m.malaysiakini.com/news/335990|title='Girls like handbags,' says deputy minister in defending Rosmah|last1= |first1= |author=Kow Gah Chie |work= |date=31 March 2016|publisher= [[Malaysiakini|Malaysiakini]] |accessdate=9 September 2017}}</ref>并对记者在国会称只要有能力买名牌包没有罪，还说女生都爱买名牌包等<ref>{{cite news |url=http://time.com/4277324/1mdb-najib-razak-spending-corruption/|title=Malaysia's Prime Minister Najib Has Allegedly Spent 'Millions' on Luxury Goods |last1= |first1= |author=Nash Jenkins |work=[[Wall_Street_Journal|WSJ]] |date=31 March 2016 |publisher= Time  |accessdate=9 September 2017}}</ref>。5月16日，达祖丁受委为农业部副部长。

2016年11月21日，达祖丁在国会称[[民主行动党|民主行动党]]士布爹区国会议员[[郭素沁|郭素沁]]是唯一一个有「[[郭姓|Kok]]」的议员，说完后自己还发笑声，招惹反对党议员和[[马华公会|马华公会]]妇女组<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/1589281|title=指玩弄谐音侮辱郭素沁‧周美芬斥达祖丁幼稚|date=2016-11-22|publisher=}}</ref>抗议并要求他道歉<ref>{{Citation|last=KiniTV|title=Deputy minister: The only woman with a 'Kok' is in Seputeh|date=2016-11-20|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AiUB8aIq_fY|accessdate=2016-11-23}}</ref>。然而，他当时被下议院副议长拿督罗纳拥护，指达祖丁说的只是郭素沁的姓氏，没有别的意思。郭素沁也反击达祖丁是一名“不文明”的部长。随后，莎亚南区[[诚信党|诚信党]]国会议员[[卡立沙末|卡立沙末]]斥责他为“混帐”（sial）。达祖丁因此回骂卡立没教养，并挑战他会议结束后在庭外解决。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/1589035|title=副农长耍低级谐音称呼郭素沁|date=2016-11-21|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/1589188|title=达祖丁导火议长也束手·“KOK”“COCK”掀骂战|date=2016-11-21|publisher=}}</ref>

2016年11月24日，大约有10名来自[[巴西沙叻|巴西沙叻]]的男女支持者，其中包括达祖丁的儿子欲闯入国会大厦，对着已进入内的卡立沙末叫嚣，称“现在只是事件的开端”<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/1590244|title=纠众到国会爆冲突．达祖丁儿子围卡立沙末|date=2016-11-25|publisher=}}</ref>。[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CSXDzVvwjbM 影片]内展示当时情况。国会袭击事件中的8人在2017年4月被控以轻微罪行法令，并只罚款最高100令吉<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/364057|title=Tajuddin's son, nine others ordered to surrender over ambush on MP|last1= |first1= |author= |work= |date=24 November 2016 |publisher= [[Malaysiakini|Malaysiakini]] |accessdate=9 September 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/malaysia/article/tajuddins-son-parliament-rioters-arrested#oOHKSCOPDYpvkgID.97|title=Tajuddin’s son, Parliament rioters arrested|last1= |first1= |author= |work= |date=25 November 2016 |publisher= [[The_Malay_Mail|The Malay Mail]] |accessdate=9 September 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2017/02/02/deputy-ministers-son-among-eight-to-be-charged-over-parliament-ruckus/|title=Deputy minister's son among eight to be charged over Parliament ruckus |last1= |first1= |author= M. KUMAR |work= |date=2 February 2017 |publisher= [[The_Star_(Malaysia)|The Star]] |accessdate=9 September 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.beritadaily.com/jazlan-charge-against-tajudins-son-too-lenient/|title=Jazlan: Charge against Tajudin’s son too lenient|last1= |first1= |author=Alfian ZM Tahir |work= |date=8 April 2017 |publisher= Berita Daily |accessdate=9 September 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.malaysiakini.com/news/378363|title=Nur Jazlan also feels Parliament protesters given light charge|last1= |first1= |author=Hafiz Yatim |work= |date=7 April 2017 |publisher= [[Malaysiakini|Malaysiakini]] |accessdate=9 September 2017}}</ref>。

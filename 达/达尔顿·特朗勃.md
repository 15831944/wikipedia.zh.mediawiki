'''詹姆斯·达尔顿·特朗勃'''（{{lang-en|James Dalton Trumbo}}，{{bd|1905年|12月9日|1976年|9月10日|catIdx=}}）是一位美国[[编剧|编剧]]和[[小说家|小说家]]，曾為《[[羅馬假期|羅馬假期]]》、《{{link-en|出埃及记 (1960年电影)|Exodus (1960 film)|出埃及记}}》、《[[萬夫莫敵|萬夫莫敵]]》及《{{link-en|東京上空三十秒|Thirty Seconds Over Tokyo}}》編劇。

==經歷==
莊柏在1939年發表，以傷兵為題材的反戰小說《[[強尼上戰場|強尼上戰場]]》（原題：Johnny Got His Gun，後於1971年推出由他本人自編自導的電影版本）曾在二戰與韓戰時期成為禁書，並使他開始遭到聯邦調查局監視。

1947年，他被列为[[好莱坞十君子|好莱坞十君子]]的一员，並拒绝提供证词给[[众议院非美活动调查委员会|众议院非美活动调查委员会]]（HUAC）调查[[共产主义|共产主义]]对电影行业的影响。处于[[荷里活黑名單|荷里活黑名單]]期间，特朗勃获得2个[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]，其中為電影《[[鐵牛傳|鐵牛傳]]》所寫的劇本是以笔名勞勃·瑞奇（Robert Rich）名義获得。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/press/pressreleases/2005/05.07.18.html |title=AMPAS Press Release |accessdate=2008-03-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071008054115/http://www.oscars.org/press/pressreleases/2005/05.07.18.html |archivedate=2007-10-08 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/trivia.html AMPAS Oscar Trivia] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091216113055/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/trivia.html |date=2009-12-16 }}</ref>

1960年，黑名单被当时“好莱坞十君子”特朗勃正式打破，他公开承认自己是电影《{{link-en|出埃及记 (1960年电影)|Exodus (1960 film)|出埃及记}}》的编剧<ref>{{cite news |first= Jon |last= Nordheimer |title= Dalton Trumbo, Film Writer, Dies; Oscar Winner Had Been Blacklisted |url= http://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F7091EFE395C137B93C3A81782D85F428785F9&scp=3&sq=dalton%20trumbo%20exodus&st=cse |work = The New York Times |page= 17 |date=1976-09-11 |accessdate= 2008-08-11 |quote= ... it was Otto Preminger, the director, who broke the blacklist months later by publicly announcing that he had hired Mr. Trumbo to do the screenplay ...}}</ref>，[[柯克·道格拉斯|柯克·道格拉斯]]也表明特朗勃實際上为《[[風雲羣英會|風雲羣英會]]》的编剧。<ref>{{cite news |first=Steve |last=Harvey |title=Dalton Trumbo Dies at 70, One of the 'Hollywood 10' |url= |work= Los Angeles Times |page=1 |date=1976-09-10 |accessdate=2012-02-05 |quote=He recalled how his name returned to the screen in 1960 with the help of ''Spartacus'' star Kirk Douglas: 'I had been working on ''Spartacus'' for about a year ...}}</ref>

2011年12月19日，60年后美国作家协会宣布肯定了特朗勃在1953年擔任电影《[[羅馬假期|羅馬假期]]》的编剧工作。<ref name=kpcc>{{cite news|last=Cheryl Devall|first=Paige Osburn|title=Blacklisted writer gets credit restored after 60 years for Oscar-winning film|url=http://www.scpr.org/news/2011/12/19/30417/blacklisted-writer-gets-credit-restored-oscar-winn/|accessdate=2011-12-20|newspaper=89.3 KPCC|date=2011-12-19}}</ref><ref name=latimes-verrier>{{cite news|last=Verrier|first=Richard|title=Writers Guild restores screenplay credit to Trumbo for 'Roman Holiday'|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/entertainmentnewsbuzz/2011/12/writers-guild-restores-screenplay-credit-to-trumbo-for-roman-holiday.html|accessdate=2011-12-20|newspaper=Los Angeles Times|date=2011-12-19}}</ref>在該片推出DVD版本時，也以數位技術將他的名字植入片頭工作人員字幕畫面中，正式將他還原列名編劇。

==去世==
1976年9月10日特朗勃在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]因[[心肌梗死|心肌梗死]]而去世，享年71岁。他捐赠了遗体以供科学研究。<ref>{{cite news |title=Dalton Trumbo, Film Writer, Dies; Oscar Winner Had Been Blacklisted |url=http://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F7091EFE395C137B93C3A81782D85F428785F9 |quote=Dalton Trumbo, the Hollywood screen writer who was perhaps the most famous member of the blacklisted film industry authors called "the Hollywood Ten," died of a heart attack early today at his home here. He was 70 years old. He donated his body to science.  |publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |date=1976-09-11 |accessdate=2008-06-18 | first=Jon | last=Nordheimer}}</ref>
==相關電影==
*《[[好萊塢的黑名單|好萊塢的黑名單]]》（Trumbo，2015年）
:描述1947年，莊柏與其他幾位名列當時[[麥卡錫主義|麥卡錫主義]]時期黑名單的藝術家，由於拒絕向美國國會作證其在好萊塢電影裡充當共產主義宣傳工具，而在1950年在聯邦監獄中度過11個月的故事。莊柏一角由[[布萊恩·克蘭斯頓|布萊恩·克蘭斯頓]]飾演，並入圍2016年[[第88屆奧斯卡金像獎|第88屆奧斯卡金像獎]]最佳男主角獎項（但未獲獎）。

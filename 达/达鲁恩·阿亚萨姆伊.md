'''达鲁恩·阿亚萨姆伊'''（{{lang-en|Dharun Ayyasamy}}，{{lang-ta|தருண் அய்யாசாமி}}，{{bd|1996年|12月31日||}}）生于泰米尔纳德邦蒂魯布爾縣，是一名印度田径运动员，主攻400米赛跑以及400米跨栏。

阿亚萨姆伊出生于一个名为Ravuthampalayam的村庄<ref>{{cite news|title=Exporters' assn gives 1 lakh to Olympian|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/coimbatore/Exporters-assn-gives-1-lakh-to-Olympian/articleshow/53349563.cms|accessdate=2016-08-04|work=The Times of India|date=2016-07-23}}</ref>。他的母亲是一名教师，妹妹代表泰米尔纳德邦参加[[排球|排球]]比赛。阿亚萨姆伊在就读小学四年级时，他的父亲因[[结核病|结核病]]去世。<ref name=toi>{{cite news|last1=RSI|first1=Prasad|title=From Tamil Nadu bylanes to Rio track|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/indiario/athletics/From-Tamil-Nadu-bylanes-to-Rio-track/articleshow/53203475.cms|accessdate=2016-08-04|work=The Times of India|date=2016-07-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Ayyasamy Dharun Profile: 4x400m Relay|url=http://indianexpress.com/sports/rio-2016-olympics/ayyasamy-dharun-profile-india-400m-relay-medal-records-2947842/|accessdate=2016-08-04|work=The Indian Express|date=2016-08-01}}</ref>在他升入十年级前，他曾代表泰米尔纳德邦参加[[可可_(运动)|可可]]比赛，后来，他改练田径<ref name=toi/><ref>{{cite news|last1=Swaminathan|first1=Swaroop|title=Run that has changed quarter-miler Dharun's world|url=http://www.newindianexpress.com/sport/Run-that-has-changed-quarter-miler-Dharun%E2%80%99s-world/2016/07/12/article3524687.ece|accessdate=2016-08-04|work=The New Indian Express|date=2016-07-12}}</ref>。他在[[卡纳塔克邦|卡纳塔克邦]][[穆德比德里|穆德比德里]]的阿尔瓦文学院获得[[文学士|文学士]]学位<ref>{{cite news|last1=Kumar|first1=R Vimal|title=Youth from Avinashi qualifies for Olympics|url=http://www.pressreader.com/india/the-hindu/20160723/282531542758299|accessdate=2016-08-04|work=The Hindu|publisher=PressReader|date=2016-07-23}}</ref>。

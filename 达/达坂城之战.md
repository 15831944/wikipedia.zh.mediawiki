'''达坂城之战'''是1934年在中国[[新疆省|新疆省]][[迪化市|迪化市]]南发生的一场战斗。[[马仲英|马仲英]]领导的[[回族|回族]]部队[[中央陆军新编第36师|中央陆军新编第36师]]反对[[盛世才|盛世才]]领导的[[新疆|新疆]]政府，盛世才引[[苏联红军|苏联红军]]进入新疆与马仲英对抗，36师遭遇苏联红军[[装甲车|装甲车]]队列，撤退。[[白俄|白俄]]、[[旧土尔扈特部|蒙古土尔扈特]]骑兵和支持盛世才的省军一起追击36师，36师与[[苏联|苏联]]装甲车队激烈交锋，几乎全歼苏联部队，又与苏军进行肉搏战， 苏联装甲车队撞山。后来白俄[[归化军|归化军]]来援助苏联红军，马仲英撤退。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=bEzNwgtiVQ0C&pg=PA112&dq=ma+zhongying+russian+cars&cd=2#v=onepage&q=storming%20the%20red%20army&f=false|title=Wild West China: the taming of Xinjiang|author=Christian Tyler|year=2004|publisher=Rutgers University Press|location=New Brunswick, New Jersey|isbn=0813535336|page=112|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=IAs9AAAAIAAJ&pg=PA120&dq=soviet+GPU+troops&hl=en&ei=QuwWTLPEMoX7lwfu_pTjCw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCsQ6AEwAA#v=snippet&q=rolled%20by%20the%20victorious&f=false|title=Warlords and Muslims in Chinese Central Asia: a political history of Republican Sinkiang 1911-1949|author=Andrew D.

== 袭击经过 ==
根据{{Link-en|救助儿童会|Save the Children}}的说法，遇事校车正载送野餐归校的儿童们，当袭击发生时，校车司机恰好将遇事校车停在达海安的一个市场，而他则进入这个市场喝酒。<ref name=":4" /> 遇事校车上的儿童都背着蓝色印有“[[UNICEF|UNICEF]]”字样的书包，这些书包都是[[人道主义|人道主义]]援助之一。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2018/08/09/saudi-led-coalition-air-strikes-bus-carrying-children-yemen/|title=At least 29 children 'on their way to summer camp' among the dead after Saudi-led coalition air strike hits bus|last=Ensor|first=Josie|date=2018-08-09|work=The Telegraph|access-date=2018-08-10|agency=Agence France-Presse|language=en-GB|issn=0307-1235|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180809181239/https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2018/08/09/saudi-led-coalition-air-strikes-bus-carrying-children-yemen/|archive-date=2018-08-09|dead-url=no}}</ref> 按照[[国际红十字委员会|国际红十字委员会]]的说法，“大多数（遇难）儿童不满10岁”。<ref name=":3" /> 同时，本次袭击还导致多名[[现场应急人员|现场应急人员]]死亡。<ref name=":1" />

== 媒体报道 ==
一段描述袭击事件后汽车残骸与儿童尸体的视频在[[推特|推特]]上被曝光，随即引发媒体的关注。<ref name=":1" /> 沙特联军发表声明承认他们在[[萨达省|萨达省]]进行了空袭，但他们表示他们的目标是一个导弹发射架。<ref name=":1" />

== 各方反应 ==
* {{SAU}}：沙特阿拉伯官方通讯社称这次袭击是“一次合法的军事行动”，其目的是为了报复上周三在沙特阿拉伯城市[[吉赞|吉赞]]制造了恐怖袭击的负责人。<ref name=":4" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/08/09/world/middleeast/yemen-airstrike-school-bus-children.html|title=Saudi Coalition Bombs School Bus in Yemen, Killing Dozens|access-date=2018-08-10|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180810042419/https://www.nytimes.com/2018/08/09/world/middleeast/yemen-airstrike-school-bus-children.html|archive-date=2018-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref> 声明称“（这次袭击）符合国际人道主义法”，并表示[[胡塞武装组织|胡塞武装组织]]使用这些儿童作为人肉盾牌。<ref name=":4" />

* {{YEM}}：胡赛组织发言人默罕默德·阿卜杜勒-萨拉姆（{{lang|en|Mohammed Abdul-Salam}}）表示，沙特联军“完全无视平民生命”，这次袭击的目标是一个拥挤的公共场所。<ref>{{cite web |author1=Aden |title=Saudi-led coalition air strikes in Yemen kill dozens, including children on a bus |url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/world/article-yemen-air-strikes-kill-dozens-including-children-on-a-bus/ |website=The Globe and Mail |publisher=[[Reuters|Reuters]] |accessdate=2018-08-10}}</ref>

* {{Flag|UN}}：[[联合国秘书长|联合国秘书长]][[安东尼奥·古特雷斯|安东尼奥·古特雷斯]]谴责了这次袭击事件，并呼吁进行独立和迅速的调查。<ref name=":4" />
** [[联合国儿童基金会|联合国儿童基金会]]强烈谴责这起袭击事件，“我们对也门一辆校车遇袭的报道感到震惊。许多无辜的孩子在袭击中致死致残，而其中一些孩子还背着‘联合国儿童基金会’字样的背包。”“孩子无论何时都不应该成为目标。”<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.unicef.org/stories/reported-air-strike-school-bus-saada-northern-yemen|title=Reported air strike on school bus in Sa’ada, northern Yemen|website=www.unicef.org|language=en|access-date=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180810042346/https://www.unicef.org/stories/reported-air-strike-school-bus-saada-northern-yemen|archive-date=2018-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>

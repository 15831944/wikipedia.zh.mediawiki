'''达卡蒂亚河'''（{{lang-en|Dakatia River}}），是一条经流[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]]和[[印度|印度]]的河流<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://thedailynewnation.com/news/166460/eviction-drives-on-to-save-dakatia-river-in-chandpur.html|title=Eviction drives on to save Dakatia River in Chandpur|last=Nation|first=The New|newspaper=The New Nation|accessdate=2018-04-07|language=en}}</ref>，长约207km<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://zh.scribd.com/document/92941026/River-Management-in-Bangladesh-a-People-s-Initiative-to-Solve-Water-logging|title=River Management in Bangladesh_ a People's Initiative to Solve Water-logging |accessdate=2018-04-07|work=Scribd|language=en}}</ref>。

达卡提亚河是孟加拉国跨境河流之一；它从印度[[巴格萨拉|巴格萨拉]]的[[特里普拉邦|特里普拉邦]]进入孟加拉国<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Physico-chemical characteristics and accumulation of heavy metals in water and sediments of the river Dakatia, Bangladesh|author=Shanur Jahedul Hasan, Mohosena Begum Tanu, Md. Istiaque Haidar, Tayfa Ahmed and AKM Shafiqul Alam Rubel|url=http://www.fisheriesjournal.com/vol2issue5/Pdf/2-5-86.1.pdf|journal=International Journal of Fisheries and Aquatic Studies|issue=2|doi=|others=|year=2015|volume=5|page=300-304|pmid=}}</ref>。达卡提亚以前是最重要的贸易渠道<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.indiamapped.com/rivers-in-india/gumti-river/|title=Facts and Information about Gumti River|accessdate=2018-04-07|work=www.indiamapped.com}}</ref>。在季风季节，它仍然可以在较低的地区航行通过乡村。河流上游地区全年都可通航。

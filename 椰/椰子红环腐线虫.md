'''椰子红环腐线虫'''（学名：''{{lang|la |Bursaphelenchus ocophilus}}''），又名'''椰子细桿滑刃线虫'''，是[[椰树|椰树]]和[[油棕|油棕]]的一種常見的寄生虫，屬於[[迁移型寄生虫|迁移型]][[内寄生虫|内寄生]][[线虫|线虫]]{{r|Chen 2004}}。這種寄生蟲會令其所寄生的樹有[[椰子紅輪病|椰子紅輪病]]{{r|Chen 2004}}。

==型態特徵==
成虫体细长，长约1毫米，雌虫尾部近圆锥形，末端圆；雄虫尾部似鸟爪，向腹面弯曲<ref>[http://www.ipm.org.cn/bcsc/B050102.HTM 松材线虫病] {{Wayback|url=http://www.ipm.org.cn/bcsc/B050102.HTM |date=20121126165355 }}</ref>。
這種線蟲的顯著特徵是從J2到成熟的發育良好的元細胞，成年人的短探針11-15μm，成年人的長度通常為1mm。 女性的外陰位於距離身體三分二之處，有一個陰部皮瓣。 女性有一個長的後期子宮囊和一個圓形的尾巴。 男性在尾部區域有七個乳頭，不同的針狀體，以及形狀像鏟子的囊{{r|Goodey 1960}}。
{{transh}}
The distinguishing characteristics of this nematode are a well-developed metacorpus from J2 through adult, a short stylet 11-15 μm in adults, adults typically 1mm in length. Females have the vulva located two-thirds body length and have a vulval flap. Females have a long post uterine sac and a rounded tail. Males have seven papillae in the tail region, distinct spicules, and bursa shaped as a spade.
{{transf}}
==分佈與棲息地==
分佈於[[中美洲|中]][[南美洲|南美洲]]，也見於部分[[加勒比地区|加勒比地区]]的島嶼{{r|CAB 1999}}。

==生命週期==
紅環線蟲遵循典型的植物寄生生命週期，在成為成蟲之前具有四次蛻皮。 整個生命週期持續大約十天。 生存階段是J3。 [4]這種線蟲的傳播取決於它與其媒介的關係。
{{transh}}
The red ring nematode follows a typical plant parasitic life cycle, having 4 molts before becoming an adult. The whole life cycle lasts approximately ten days. The survival stage is the J3{{r|Blair & Darling 1968}}。 The dissemination of this nematode depends on its relationship with its vector.

===Insect vector relationship===
The vector, ''[[Rhynchophorus_palmarum|Rhynchophorus palmarum]]'' (the South American palm weevil), carries the J3 stage to healthy palms. Female weevils are internally infested around the oviducts, when they lay their eggs in the palm they also disseminate the nematode.<ref>Chinchilla 1991</ref>

==Host-parasite relationship==
The symptoms produced by this nematode are [[chlorosis|chlorosis]] beginning in the oldest leaves and a distinct red/brownish ring in the trunk of the tree.
{{transf}}
==經濟影響==
這種線蟲在經濟上可造成高達80%的損失，然而，[[椰樹|椰樹]]和[[油棕|油棕]]的損失一般在10-15%之間{{r|Esser & Meredith 1987}}。受感染的椰樹一般都有四年到七年大，但受感染後其經濟價值立即消失{{r|Chen 2004}}。

==管理==
{{transh}}
To manage this disease scouting is the most important aspect; early detection of infected trees may save plantations. If an infected tree is found it must be removed, treated with herbicide and cut down. Leaving the stump behind can lead to vector reproduction and spread the nematode. Trapping the vector is another strategy, reducing the disease incidence from 10% to 1%.<ref>Oehlschlager 2002</ref>
{{transf}}

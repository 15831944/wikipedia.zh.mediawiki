'''椰林'''（{{lang-en|Coconut Grove}}）是[[美國|美國]][[佛羅里達州|佛羅里達州]]城市[[邁阿密|邁阿密]]歷史最悠久的社區<ref>[http://www.miamigis.com/cityofmiamive/ City of Miami official map] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090416180151/http://www.miamigis.com/cityofmiamive/ |date=2009-04-16 }}</ref>。可通過[[邁阿密地鐵|邁阿密地鐵]]直接抵達。最早移民椰林的移民出現在1825年<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ci.miami.fl.us/nets/offices/grove/ |title=City of Miami – Neighborhood Enhancement Teams |accessdate=2011-10-14}}</ref><ref>Planning Your Vacation in Florida, Miami and Dade County [WPA Guide to Miami], Northport, New York: Bacon, Percy & Daggett, 1941, page 49.</ref>。現在椰林是一年一度的椰林藝術節的舉辦地點<ref>{{cite web  | title = website of Coconut Grove Arts Festival  | work =  | publisher =  | date =  | url = http://www.cgaf.com/  | accessdate = 2012-09-25}}</ref>。

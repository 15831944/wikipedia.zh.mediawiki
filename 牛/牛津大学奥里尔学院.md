'''奧里爾學院'''（Oriel College）<ref name="OUG">''Oxford University Calendar 2005–2006'' (2005) p.323 has the corporate designation as "The Provost and Scholars of the House of the Blessed Mary the Virgin in Oxford, commonly called Oriel College, of the Foundation of Edward the Second of famous memory, sometime King of England", p324 has people — [[Oxford_University_Press|Oxford University Press]] ISBN 0-19-928370-2.</ref> 是[[牛津大學|牛津大學]]下的一個[[牛津大學學院|學院]]，位於[[牛津|牛津]][[奧里爾廣場|奧里爾廣場]]。該學院歷史可追溯至1320年代，自稱是牛津最古老的皇家機構，為了顯示其皇家地位，又稱'''國王學院'''（King's College、King's Hall）。<ref name="OUAS">Watt, D. E. (editor), ''Oriel College, Oxford'' ([[Trinity_term|Trinity term]], 1953) — Oxford University Archaeological Society, uses material collected by C. R. Jones, R. J. Brenato, D. K. Garnier, W. J. Frampton and N. Covington, under advice from W. A. Pantin, particularly in respect of the architecture and treasures (manuscripts, printed books and silver plate) sections. 16 page publication, produced in association with the [[Ashmolean_Museum|Ashmolean Museum]] as part of a college guide series.</ref> 

[[愛德華二世_(英格蘭)|愛德華二世]]在1320年代提供贊助，由其廷臣[[亞當·德·布魯姆|亞當·德·布魯姆]]為該學院奠基，當時稱牛津聖母瑪麗學堂（House or Hall of the Blessed Mary at Oxford）。<ref name="OCR">Rannie, David, ''Oriel College'' (1900) — published by F. E. Robinson & Co. London (Part of the ''University of Oxford College Histories'' series) pp.1, 5–10, 102, 105, 108–110, 241 and 235.</ref> 該學院在16世紀之前只接受非本科生做成員，之後才開始接納本科生入學。<ref name="OCO">''Oriel College Oxford, A short guide'' — published by Oriel College Development Trust on behalf of Oriel College, Oxford.</ref> [[英格蘭內戰|英格蘭內戰]]期間，奧里爾學院是牛津議會（Oxford Parliament）駐地。<ref name="BCW">{{cite web|url= http://www.british-civil-wars.co.uk/glossary/oxford-parliament.htm|title= The Oxford Parliament|publisher= British Civil Wars, Commonwealth and Protectorate 1638–60|accessdate= 3 September 2006}}</ref>

該學院有四座中世紀建築構成，即Bedel Hal、St Mary Hall、St Martin Hall和Tackley's Inn。其中Tackley's Inn是該學院最古老的地產，也是牛津最古老的中世紀建築之一。<ref name="OCM">Oriel College Memorandum 2003–4.</ref> 該學院現有大約40名成員、300名本科生和160名研究生，男女人數基本持平。<ref name="OCO"/>

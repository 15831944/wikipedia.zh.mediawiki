Peruv. Prodr. 110, plate 24. 1794. ]</ref><ref>[http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=2&taxon_id=113183 Flora of China, Vol. 20-21 Page 864 <big>牛膝菊属</big> niu xi ju shu ''Galinsoga'' Ruiz & Pavon, Fl. Peruv. Prodr. 110. 1794. ]</ref>

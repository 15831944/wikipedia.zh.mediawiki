'''綠靴子'''（{{lang-en|Green Boots}}）為一具{{le|未識別死者|unidentified decedent|未識別的}}[[珠穆朗瑪峰|珠穆朗瑪峰]]登山者遺體，並成為珠穆朗瑪峰東北嶺登山路線上的顯著地標<ref> {{cite news|work=BBC Future|author=Nuwer, Rachel|date=8 October 2015|url=http://www.bbc.com/future/story/20151008-the-tragic-story-of-mt-everests-most-famous-dead-body |title=The tragic tale of Mt Everest’s most famous dead body}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=Q6Y_AAAAIBAJ&sjid=c1YMAAAAIBAJ&pg=1200,2723423&dq=green-boots+everest&hl=en | title = Everest's Trail of Corpses | work = The Victoria Advocate | date = 20 May 2007 | first = Tim | last = Johnson}}</ref>。雖然這名登山客的身份尚未得到官方確認，但據信其身份可能為1996年死於珠穆朗瑪峰的印度登山者{{le|澤旺·帕卓|Tsewang Paljor}}。「綠靴子」名稱源自於死者所穿的綠色登山靴。所有從北側攀登的遠征隊，皆能夠看見這具遺體，蜷曲於海拔{{convert|8500|m|abbr=on}}的石灰岩山洞中。2006年，另一位登山者[[大衛·夏普_(登山者)|大衛·夏普]]在獨自攀登珠穆朗瑪峰時死於同一個山洞中，被稱為「綠靴洞」（{{lang|en|Green Boots' Cave}}）。

2014年5月，據報綠靴子已經不見蹤影<ref> {{cite news|work=BBC Future|author=Nuwer, Rachel|title=Death In the Clouds|url=http://www.bbc.com/future/story/20151008-the-graveyard-in-the-clouds-everests-200-dead-bodies|date=9 October 2015|quote=Adventurer Noel Hanna made this discovery in May 2014, when he was surprised to find not only that Green Boots’ cave was devoid of its familiar resident, but also that many of the bodies on the north side – one stretch of which is sometimes referred to as “rainbow ridge,” for the colourful down suits of its many fallen climbers – seemed to have vanished. Hanna estimates that, previously, up to 10 bodies were visible on the push to the summit, but in 2014 he only counted two or three. “I would be 95% certain that [Paljor] has been moved or covered with stones,” Hanna says.}}</ref>，推測可能已移走並埋葬<ref name=Nuwer>{{cite news |last=Nuwer |first=Rachel |date=9 October 2015 |url=http://www.bbc.com/future/story/20151008-the-graveyard-in-the-clouds-everests-200-dead-bodies |title=Death in the clouds: The problem with Everest's 200+ bodies |work=BBC Future}}</ref>。2017年，隨著更多登山者返回現場，依據攀登至第二階平臺的登山者說法，這具遺體於海拔8,500公尺處再度被發現，可能已被少許石頭覆蓋<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.alanarnette.com/blog/2017/05/27/everest-2017-weekend-update-may-27/|website=alanarnette.com|date=May 27, 2017|title=Everest 2017: Weekend Update}}</ref>。

'''綠苑酒廊'''（{{lang-en|'''Tavern on the Green'''}}）是一家位於[[美國|美國]][[紐約市|紐約市]][[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]][[上西城|上西城]][[中央公園|中央公園]]內的[[美國烹飪|美國烹飪]]餐廳。經營時間是1934年至2009年，其間數次易手。2010年10月15日，原建築成為[[紐約市公園和娛樂場所管理局|紐約市公園和娛樂場所管理局]]經營的遊客中心和商品店，仍用舊名。2014年4月重新開張。

2007年該店總收入達3800萬美元，接待超過50萬顧客，成為美國第二高收入的獨立餐廳（第一是[[拉斯維加斯|拉斯維加斯]][[威尼斯人酒店_(拉斯維加斯)|威尼斯人酒店]]中的[[道家餐廳|道家餐廳]]（Tao restaurant），總收入6700萬美元）。<ref>{{cite news|publisher=Restaurants & Institutions|title=Special Report: Top 100 Independents|url=http://www.rimag.com/archives/2008/04b/sr-top100-ranking.asp|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080420092631/http://www.rimag.com/archives/2008/04b/sr-top100-ranking.asp|archivedate=2008-04-20}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Setting Restaurant Records by Selling the Sizzle |first=Joe |last=Drape |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2007/07/22/us/22vegas.html |newspaper=The New York Times |date=July 22, 2007 |accessdate=October 15, 2007}}</ref>酒廊中有數個廳，在著名的水晶廳中可以看到附近的中央公園。<ref name="nyc24.org">[http://www.nyc24.org/2005/centralpark/play/tavern.html Central Park: Play: Tavern on the Green] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061210002842/http://www.nyc24.org/2005/centralpark/play/tavern.html |date=2006-12-10 }}</ref>

== 歷史 ==
[[File:Sheepfold_Tavern_on_the_Green_Central_Park_NY.jpg|thumb]]

綠苑酒廊的現址在[[美國|美國]][[紐約市|紐約市]][[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]][[上西城|上西城]][[第八大道_(曼哈頓)|中央公園西]]和西[[66街_(曼哈頓)|66街]]。19世紀后末期這裡只是一個羊圈，圈養放牧在[[綿羊草地_(中央公園)|綿羊草地]]的綿羊。1934年在[[羅伯·摩斯|羅伯·摩斯]]翻新公園時成爲了餐廳。

1943年，阿諾德·施萊費爾、亞瑟·施萊費爾和朱利葉斯·貝爾曼得到了餐廳經營權。

1962年，[[喬伊·鮑姆|喬伊·鮑姆]]從從施萊費爾等人手中買下了該餐廳。<ref>{{cite news |title=Tavern-on-the-Green Sold |url=http://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F30916FB3B54157A93C7A9178FD85F468685F9 |newspaper=The New York Times |date=April 5, 1962 |accessdate=October 1, 2011}}</ref>

1974年，[[華納·勒羅伊|華納·勒羅伊]]掌管綠蔭酒廊的租賃權，投入1000萬美元翻新了酒廊，1976年綠蔭酒廊再次開張，增加了水晶廳並使座位數達到800。<ref>{{cite news |title=Tavern deal not Crystal Clear |first=Steve |last=Cuozzo |url=http://www.nypost.com/p/news/business/realestate/commercial/tavern_deal_not_crystal_clear_TXN7SjuFfo9ibfJ7SknBoI?CMP=OTC-rss&FEEDNAME= |newspaper=New York Post |date=January 31, 2011
 |accessdate=February 1, 2011}}</ref>2001年勒羅伊去世，酒廊交由他的女兒詹妮弗·奧茲·勒羅伊管理，直到2009年關張。<ref>{{cite news | author =Yaniv, Oren  | title =Tavern on the Green in the red | newspaper =Daily News  | location =New York | date =February 3, 2009 | url =http://www.nydailynews.com/money/2009/02/02/2009-02-02_tavern_on_the_green_in_the_red.html | accessdate=February 6, 2009}}</ref>

1980年代的一段時期內，紐約市治安混亂。結果一群暴徒襲擊了綠蔭酒廊，搶走了大量現金。<ref>[http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=jME0AAAAIBAJ&sjid=gxMEAAAAIBAJ&pg=2196,2249285&dq=tavern-on-the-green+youths&hl=en Youths attack concert fans]</ref>

許多社會名流都經常出入綠蔭酒廊。其前贊助人包括紐約市市長[[菲奧雷洛·亨利·拉瓜迪亞|菲奧雷洛·亨利·拉瓜迪亞]]、女演員[[葛莉絲·凱莉|葛莉絲·凱莉]]和[[費伊·雷|費伊·雷]]等。<ref>[http://www.nypost.com/pagesixmag/issues/20081012/Theres+No+Place+Tavern+Green "There's No Place Like Tavern on the Green"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090118213615/http://www.nypost.com/pagesixmag/issues/20081012/Theres+No+Place+Tavern+Green |date=2009-01-18 }}, ''Page Six Magazine'', October 12, 2008</ref>[[普利策獎|普利策獎]]得主[[羅伯特·奧倫·巴特勒|羅伯特·奧倫·巴特勒]]<ref>{{cite news |title=WEDDINGS: VOWS; Robert O. Butler, Elizabeth Dewberry |first=Lois Smith |last=Brady |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1995/05/07/style/weddings-vows-robert-o-butler-elizabeth-dewberry.html |newspaper=The New York Times |date=May 7, 1995 |accessdate=October 1, 2011}}</ref>和電影導演[[沃爾特·希爾_(導演)|沃爾特·希爾]]<ref>{{cite news |title=Hildy Gottlieb Is the Bride Of Walter Hill, a Director |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1986/09/08/style/hildy-gottlieb-is-the-bride-of-walter-hill-a-director.html |newspaper=The New York Times |date=September 8, 1986 |accessdate=October 1, 2011}}</ref>都曾在這裡舉行婚禮。因為[[約翰·藍儂|約翰·藍儂]]是華納·勒羅伊的鄰居且二人兒子關係不錯，故而1970年代末期約翰·藍儂和他的兒子[[西恩·列儂|西恩·列儂]]多次在綠蔭酒廊里慶祝生日。<ref>[http://www.thisdayinrock.com/index.php/general/1979-john-lennon-and-yoko-ono-celebrate-his-and-seans-birthdays/ "John Lennon and Yoko Ono celebrate his and Sean's birthdays," This Day in Rock, October 9, 1979.]</ref>

2008年5月，酒廊與[[西田集團|西田集團]]宣佈在[[舊金山|舊金山]]市中心的購物中心Metron開設分店，面積{{convert|40000|sqft|m2|}}。<ref>{{cite news|title=Tavern on the Green coming to the Metreon|url=http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/c/a/2008/05/07/BU8F10HO0F.DTL|author=James Temple|newspaper=San Francisco Chronicle|date=May 6, 2008|accessdate=May 6, 2008|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080508235146/http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=%2Fc%2Fa%2F2008%2F05%2F07%2FBU8F10HO0F.DTL|archivedate=2008-05-08|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|newspaper=The New York Times|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2008/05/07/dining/07off.html|date=May 7, 2008|title=Off the Menu|first=Florence|last=Fabricant|accessdate=October 1, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://sanfrancisco.bizjournals.com/sanfrancisco/stories/2008/05/05/daily20.html|title=San Francisco Business Times|date=May 5, 2008|author=J.K. Dineen|title=N.Y.'s famed Tavern On The Green to open S.F.'s biggest restaurant}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nrn.com/article.aspx?id=353936|date=May 7, 2008|publisher=Nations Restaurant News|title=NYC's Tavern on the Green plans San Francisco outpost}}</ref>但是到了預定的2009年，計劃卻沒能完成。<ref>{{cite news|publisher=NBC Bay Area|url=http://www.nbcbayarea.com/around-town/food-drink/San-Franciscos-Tavern-Chances-Drying-Up.html|title=San Francisco's Tavern Chances Are Drying Up|author=Sajid Farooq|date=October 14, 2009}}</ref>同年6月，綠蔭酒廊同意支付220萬美元，以解決[[公平就業機會委員會|公平就業機會委員會]]對其在雇傭員工時的性別和種族歧視提出的訴訟。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.yahoo.com/s/nm/20080602/us_nm/tavern_harassment_dc|publisher=reuters|title= Famed NY tavern to pay $2.2 million for discrimination|date=June 2, 2008|author=Daniel Trotta}}</ref>

2009年8月28日，[[紐約市公園和娛樂場所管理局|紐約市公園和娛樂場所管理局]]拒絕更新酒廊的營業執照，并勒令綠蔭酒廊在2010年1月1日前停止運營并撤走所有傢具。<ref>{{Cite news| last = Collins| first = Glenn| title = Why Did Tavern Fail? | newspaper = [[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]| page = D1| date = September 16, 2009| url = http://www.nytimes.com/2009/09/16/dining/16tavern.html | accessdate = September 17, 2009| postscript = .}}</ref>9月，綠蔭酒廊向地區法院申請破產保護。<ref>{{cite news |title=Tavern on the Green Requesting Chapter 11 Bankruptcy Protection |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2009/09/10/nyregion/10tavern.html |newspaper =[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |first=Glenn |last=Collins |date=September 9, 2009 |accessdate=September 10, 2009}}</ref>綠蔭酒廊一直營業到2009年12月31日。<ref>{{cite news |title=Lions and Tigers and Debt: Auctioning Off Tavern on the Green |first=Glenn |last=Collins |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2009/12/09/dining/09tavern.html |newspaper=The New York Times |date=December 8, 2009 |accessdate=October 1, 2011}}</ref>

[[File:TotG_sun_snow_jeh.jpg|thumb]]

2010年10月15日，原建築成為紐約市公園和娛樂場所管理局經營的遊客中心和商品店，仍用舊名。<ref name=ap10152010>[http://news.yahoo.com/video/environment-15749659/22472389 "NY's Tavern on the Green reopens as visitor ctr."], AP, October 15, 2010 '''(video)'''</ref> 水晶廳已經被移除，只展出19世紀的原建築。<ref name=ap10152010/>同時綠蔭酒廊也從私人財產變成了共有地。<ref name=ap10152010/> 2014年4月重新開張，5月13日舉行正式的開業典禮。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/life-style/eats/tavern-green-open-april-24-dinner-article-1.1743113|work=Daily News|title=Tavern on the Green to open April 24 for dinner; to add brunch, lunch in May |quote=The Central Park mainstay that boarded up New Year's Eve 2009 is slated to reopen two weeks from now. === 唐納德·川普投資 ===
[[纽约中央公园|纽约中央公园]]的Dean Poll被給予權利重新開設綠蔭酒廊，但是無法與代表原酒廊雇員利益的[[美國勞工聯合會-產業工會聯合會|美國勞工聯合會-產業工會聯合會]]達成一致。2011年1月，[[唐納德·川普|唐納德·川普]]說他將投資2000萬美元重新開設酒廊，并重建水晶廳，並不會改變酒廊的原名。<ref name="nypost1">{{cite news|last=Gould |first=Jennifer |url=http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/trump_goes_green_CCs8VjvB5ixTZgf9L4TqHI#ixzz1CFItmzBs |title=Donald Trump to ask City to allow him to reopen Tavern on the Green |publisher=NYPOST.com |date=January 27, 2011 |accessdate=February 1, 2011}}</ref>紐約市市長[[麥克·彭博|麥克·彭博]]和中央公園管理者並沒有對此公開表態。<ref name="nypost1"/>

== 餐廳名字的所有權爭議 ==
2009年10月，在[[破產法院|破產法院]]上，就綠蔭酒廊名字的所有權勒羅伊和紐約市官方產生了爭執。<ref name=trade>{{ cite news |author=Collins, Glenn |title=City Wants Tavern’s Trademark Name |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2009/10/10/nyregion/10tavern.html |work=The New York Times |date=October 9, 2009 }}</ref>當時其[[商標|商標]]被估價1900萬美元。<ref name=trade/>2009年11月，Poll註冊了一個備用名：Tavern in the Park。<ref>Fickenscher, Lisa. [http://www.crainsnewyork.com/article/20091119/FREE/911199987 "Back-up name chosen for Tavern on the Green"], ''[[Crain's_New_York_Business|Crain's New York Business]]'', November 19, 2009. [http://www.webcitation.org/5lR1j1f47 WebCite archive]</ref>

2010年4月，地區法院裁決該商標屬於紐約市。法官說：<ref>{{cite news |title=N.Y. City Wins Right to ‘Tavern on the Green’ Name |url=http://www.businessweek.com/news/2010-03-10/n-y-city-wins-right-to-tavern-on-the-green-name-update1-.html |date=March 10, 2010 |author1=Glovin, David |author2=Jeffrey, Don|magazine=[[Bloomberg_Businessweek|BusinessWeek]] |accessdate=June 30, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Judge Rules the City Owns the Name Tavern on the Green |url=http://dinersjournal.blogs.nytimes.com/2010/03/10/judge-rules-the-city-owns-the-name-tavern-on-the-green/ |first=Glenn |last=Collins |newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=March 10, 2010 |accessdate=June 30, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=City Beats LeRoys for 'Tavern on the Green' Name |url=http://www.observer.com/2010/real-estate/city-wins-battle-leroys-tavern-green  |first=Dana |last=Rubinstein |date=March 10, 2010 |newspaper=[[The_New_York_Observer|The New York Observer]] |accessdate=June 30, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Court: Rights to Tavern on the Green Name Belong to New York City |url=http://www.law.com/jsp/article.jsp?id=1202446051333&Court_Rights_to_Tavern_on_the_Green_Name_Belong_to_New_York_City |first=Noeleen G. |last=Walder |publisher=''[[New_York_Law_Journal|New York Law Journal]]'' |date=March 11, 2010 |accessdate=June 30, 2010}}</ref>“無可爭議的證據顯示，從1934年起，在中央公園的同一片區域，是紐約市建立并維護著“Tavern on the Green”名下的餐廳。”

'''哈爾·芬尼'''（{{lang-en|'''Hal Finney'''}}，{{bd|1956年|5月4日|2014年|8月28日}}），{{link-en|PGP公司|PGP Corporation}}的程式設計員，為[[菲爾·齊默爾曼|菲爾·齊默爾曼]]所聘任的第二位員工。他是第一位得到[[比特幣|比特幣]]的人。他曾經與[[多利安·中本|多利安·中本]]居住於同一社區達十年之久，使他成為比特幣的發明人[[中本聪|中本聪]]真身的其中一個嫌疑人<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/andygreenberg/2014/03/25/satoshi-nakamotos-neighbor-the-bitcoin-ghostwriter-who-wasnt/ |title=The Little Black Book of Billionaire Secrets Nakamoto's Neighbor: My Hunt For Bitcoin's Creator Led To A Paralyzed Crypto Genius |work=Forbes|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140326104029/http://www.forbes.com/sites/andygreenberg/2014/03/25/satoshi-nakamotos-neighbor-the-bitcoin-ghostwriter-who-wasnt/#42e4aeba4a37|archive-date=2014-03-26|dead-url=yes |accessdate=17 April 2017}}</ref>，但是這個說法被他本人否認了<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/andygreenberg/2014/03/25/satoshi-nakamotos-neighbor-the-bitcoin-ghostwriter-who-wasnt/ |title=The Little Black Book of Billionaire Secrets Nakamoto's Neighbor: My Hunt For Bitcoin's Creator Led To A Paralyzed Crypto Genius |work=Forbes|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140326104029/http://www.forbes.com/sites/andygreenberg/2014/03/25/satoshi-nakamotos-neighbor-the-bitcoin-ghostwriter-who-wasnt/#42e4aeba4a37|archive-date=2014-03-26|dead-url=yes |accessdate=17 April 2017}}</ref>。

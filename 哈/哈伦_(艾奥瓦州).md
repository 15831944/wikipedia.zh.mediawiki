'''哈伦'''（Harlan）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[谢尔比县_(爱阿华州)|谢尔比县]]的城市，是谢尔比县的县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx|accessdate=2011-06-07|title=Find a County|publisher=National Association of Counties}}</ref>[[美国2010年人口普查|美国2010年人口普查]]时人口为5,106人。

==历史==
哈兰在1858年被规划。<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Rk40AQAAMAAJ&pg=PA226#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=Past and Present of Shelby County, Iowa, Volume 1 | publisher=B.F. Bowen | author=White, Edward Speer | year=1915 | pages=226}}</ref>它以爱荷华州早期美国[[美国参议院|参议员]]之一詹姆斯·哈伦命名。<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9V1IAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA149#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=The Origin of Certain Place Names in the United States | publisher=Govt. Print. Off. | author=Gannett, Henry | year=1905 | pages=149}}</ref> 哈伦于1859年被定为县城。<ref>{{cite book|author=Chicago and North Western Railway Company|title=A History of the Origin of the Place Names Connected with the Chicago & North Western and Chicago, St. Paul, Minneapolis & Omaha Railways|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OspBAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA81|year=1908|page=81}}</ref>该城镇于1879年5月2日成立。

'''哈德逊河怪物'''（英文：Hudson River Monster）是指出没于[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]][[哈德遜河|哈德逊河]]的[[水怪|湖怪]]，它还拥有自己的名称“'''Kipsy'''”。哈德逊河怪物的历史可追溯至1610年，此后多次传出目击。[[纽约时报|《纽约时报》]]曾数度刊文报道哈德逊河怪物，1899年时刊登“SHARK OR SEA SERPENT?; Bathers in the Hudson River Startled by a Monster That Chased Them from the Water”一文，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1899/06/09/archives/shark-or-sea-serpent-bathers-in-the-hudson-river-startled-by-a.html|title=SHARK OR SEA SERPENT?; Bathers in the Hudson River Startled by a Monster That Chased Them from the Water.|accessdate=2018-08-31|language=en}}</ref>讲述了哈德逊河的怪物传闻，目击者将其描述为[[鲨鱼|鲨鱼]]或[[大海蛇|大海蛇]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://tylerscryptozoo.blogspot.com/2016/05/kipsy-hudson-river-monster.html|title=Kipsy, The Hudson River Monster|accessdate=2018-08-31|work=tylerscryptozoo.blogspot.com}}</ref>2006年，[[泰晤士报|《泰晤士报》]]报道哈德逊河哈德逊河出现神秘动物，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://cryptidsguide.com/kipsy-the-hudson-river-monster/|title=Kipsy the Hudson River Monster - Sightings - Monsters - Cryptids Guide|date=2017-10-10|newspaper=Cryptids Guide|accessdate=2018-08-31|language=en-US}}</ref>但之后的调查发现那是只[[海牛屬|海牛]]。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2006/08/07/nyregion/07manatee.html|title=Massive Manatee Is Spotted in Hudson River|last=Lee|first=Jennifer 8.|accessdate=2018-08-31|language=en}}</ref>

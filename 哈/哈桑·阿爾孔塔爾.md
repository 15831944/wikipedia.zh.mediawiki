== 背景故事 ==
2006年，阿爾孔塔爾从叙利亚移民到阿聯酋，並从事保險營銷經理<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://theintercept.com/2018/04/13/syrian-refugees-malaysia/|title=The Terminal: This Syrian Refugee Has Been Marooned in an Airport for a Month, With No End in Sight|date=13 April 2018|last=Miller|first=Anna Lekas}}</ref>。他的工作许可证在2011年到期，也就是[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]爆发的那一年。叙利亚大使馆拒绝延长阿尔孔塔的护照期限后，他決定非法滞留阿联酋，因為担心回国后会被征召参战。2017年，他遭到逮捕，并被驱逐到[[马来西亚|马来西亚]]。马来西亚是叙利亚公民可以免签证入境的国家之一。他试图前往[[厄瓜多尔|厄瓜多尔]]，但由[[土耳其航空公司|土耳其航空公司]]的工作人员在沒有提供任何理由下拒绝他登机，也沒有退还他的机票。 然后，他试图前往[[柬埔寨|柬埔寨]]，但被拒绝入境，并再被送回[[吉隆坡|吉隆坡]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.phnompenhpost.com/national/syrian-asylum-seeker-stuck-airport-limbo-after-being-denied-entry-cambodia|title=Syrian asylum seeker stuck in airport limbo after being denied entry to Cambodia|date=12 April 2018|last=Vichea|first=Pang}}</ref>。由于他的马来西亚旅游签证到期，他不被允许进入该国，因此被滞留在机场，处于「法律灰色地帶」<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/worldviews/wp/2018/04/12/a-syrian-man-has-been-trapped-in-the-malaysia-airport-for-37-days-and-counting/|title=A Syrian man has been trapped in a Malaysian airport for 37 days, and counting|date=12 April 2018|last=Erickson|first=Amanda}}</ref>。

马来西亚政府曾讨论过让他正式入境马来西亚，但由始至終都没有正式邀请過他，因為即使阿爾孔塔爾入境，馬來西亞在沒有簽署《[[難民地位公約|難民地位公約]]》下，他不会被承认为合法难民<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.malaymail.com/s/857781/malaysia-will-not-sign-un-convention-on-refugees-says-minister|title=Malaysia will not sign UN convention on refugees, says minister - Malay Mail}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://asiancorrespondent.com/2018/04/i-dont-want-to-be-killed-syrian-refugee-stuck-in-kuala-lumpur-airport-seeks-safety/#wKTDwuLHLqtzSZbl.97|title=‘I don’t want to be killed’: Syrian refugee stuck in Kuala Lumpur airport seeks safety|date=20 April 2018|last=Carson|first=Angela}}</ref>。

== 机场生活 ==
阿爾孔塔爾在椅子上或楼梯下休息，一些机场工作人员（大部分来自[[亞洲航空|亞航]]）會给他食物<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://nypost.com/2018/04/12/syrian-man-stranded-at-airport-for-more-than-a-month/|title=Syrian man stranded at airport for more than a month|date=12 April 2018}}</ref>。隨著他身上的儲蓄不斷減少，不同人士轉為捐錢給他，一位住在[[不列颠哥伦比亚省|不列颠哥伦比亚省]]的女士更為他筹集资金，以便資助他到加拿大，并有足够的钱生活至少一年<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/hassan-al-kontar-syria-stranded-malaysian-airport_us_5ad12c91e4b0edca2cb9dfe3|title=A Syrian Man Has Been Stranded In A Malaysian Airport Terminal For Weeks|date=14 April 2018|last=Mosbergen|first=Dominique}}</ref>。據知阿爾孔塔爾在[[加拿大|加拿大]]有家人<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/radio/asithappens/as-it-happens-friday-full-episode-1.4618548/syrian-stranded-at-malaysia-airport-says-canadians-renewed-his-hope-in-humanity-1.4618550|title=Syrian stranded at Malaysia airport says Canadians renewed his hope in humanity  - CBC Radio}}</ref>。另一方面，[[联合国难民署|联合国难民署]]一直努力提供法律援助<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://news.sky.com/story/syrian-stranded-at-malaysian-airport-for-a-month-11327224/|title=Syrian stranded at Malaysian airport for a month}}</ref>。

== 逮捕和拘留 ==
2018年10月1日，他因身处机场禁区而被警方拘留。其後马来西亚政府与叙利亚大使馆进行沟通，討論將其遣返回本国<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-45267414/|title=Syrian Hassan al-Kontar removed from Malaysia airport after months|publisher=''BBC'', Oct 1 2018}}</ref>。消息傳出後引起外界爭議和批評。

== 定居加拿大 ==
2018年11月26日，阿爾孔塔爾因獲得加拿大政府庇護和永久居民的身份，其乘機前往加國並在[[溫哥華國際機場|溫哥華國際機場]]降落，其後定居[[不列颠哥伦比亚省|不列颠哥伦比亚省]]的[[威士拿|威士拿]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://vancouversun.com/pmn/news-pmn/canada-news-pmn/syrian-man-stranded-for-months-at-airport-in-malaysia-arrives-in-vancouver/wcm/b990a6d3-0b7f-4378-841b-88ea340107fe|title=Syrian man stranded for months at airport in Malaysia arrives in Vancouver|publisher=Vancouver Sun, Nov 26 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2018/nov/26/hassan-al-kontar-syrian-refugee-stranded-airport-asylum-canada-vancouver|title=Syrian refugee stranded in airport for months is granted asylum in Canada|publisher=''The Guardian'', Nov 26 2018}}</ref>。

'''哈特利·索耶'''（{{lang-en|Hartley Sawyer}}）是美國演員，製片人和編劇。 他在Geek＆Sundry的超級英雄喜劇《Caper》，驚悚片《Kept Man》及CBS日間肥皂劇《The Young and the Restless》等作品聞名，目前於[[CW電視台|CW電視台]][[綠箭宇宙|綠箭宇宙]]系列劇集《[[閃電俠_(2014年電視影集)|閃電俠]]》演出{{link-en|收縮人|Elongated Man|Ralph Dibny / Elongated Man}} 。

==生涯==
2013年3月，Sawyer在CBS日間肥皂劇《The Young and the Restless》中飾演Kyle Abbott<ref>{{cite web|last=Bowe|first=Jillian|title=Blake Hood OUT; Hartley Sawyer IN as Kyle Abbott on The Young and The Restless!|url=http://daytimeconfidential.com/2013/03/11/blake-hood-out-hartley-sawyer-in-as-kyle-abbott-on-the-young-and-the-restless|work=[[Daytime_Confidential|Daytime Confidential]]|date=March 11, 2013|accessdate=March 12, 2013|archiveurl=https://archive.is/3IgSK |archivedate=April 12, 2013 |deadurl=no}}</ref>。他於2013年4月24日首次亮相<REF>{{cite book|chapter=Y&R Recasts Kyle |title=[[Soap_Opera_Digest|Soap Opera Digest]] |date=April 8, 2013 |page=5}}</ref>。12月，宣布Sawyer在首演八個月後退出該職位<ref>{{cite web|url=http://daytimeconfidential.com/2013/12/14/hartley-sawyer-out-at-the-young-and-the-restless|title=Hartley Sawyer OUT at The Young and the Restless|date=December 14, 2013|first=Jamey|last=Giddens|work=[[Daytime_Confidential|Daytime Confidential]]|accessdate=December 15, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Y&R Actor Out|url=http://www.soapoperadigest.com/content/yr-actor-out|work=[[Soap_Opera_Digest|Soap Opera Digest]]|publisher=[[American_Media_(publisher)|American Media, Inc.]]|accessdate=December 15, 2013|date=December 15, 2013}}</ref>。他於2014年1月27日最後一次露面<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.soapoperanetwork.com/2013/12/hartley-sawyer-out|title=Hartley Sawyer OUT!|date=December 14, 2013|first=Errol|last=Lewis|work=[[Soap_Opera_Network|Soap Opera Network]]|accessdate=December 15, 2013}}</ref>。

2014年，他主演了驚悚片《Kept Man》<ref>{{cite web|last=Ehrman-Dupre|first=Joseph|title=The Art of the Short Film: Kept Man|url=http://blogs.indiewire.com/bent/the-art-of-the-short-film-kept-man-20141209|publisher=[[IndieWire|IndieWire]]|accessdate=December 9, 2014|date=December 9, 2014}}</ref>，以及來自Eureka創作家Amy Berg和執行製片人Felicia Day的Geek＆Sundry超級英雄喜劇《Caper》<ref>{{cite web|last=McMillan|first=Graeme|title=Superhero Comedy "Caper" Debuts Its First Trailer|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/superhero-comedy-caper-debuts-first-675337|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|accessdate=January 29, 2014|date=January 29, 2014}}</ref> 。 2015年，Sawyer主持並共同撰寫了紀錄片系列《Courageous Leaders》<ref>{{cite web|first=Andrew|last=McMains|title=Young CEOs Open Up About Their Failures In new branded videos from insurer Hiscox and Vox|url=http://www.adweek.com/news/advertising-branding/young-ceos-talk-about-their-faiures-these-videos-hiscox-165106|work=[[Adweek|Adweek]]|accessdate=June 3, 2015|date=June 3, 2015}}</ref>，並出演了華爾街劇集《SPiN》<ref>{{cite web|last=Ehrman-Dupre|first=Joseph|title=The Art of the Short Film: SPiN|url=http://blogs.indiewire.com/bent/the-art-of-the-short-film-spin-20150306|publisher=[[IndieWire|IndieWire]]|accessdate=March 6, 2015|date=March 6, 2015}}</ref>
。

2017年7月，Sawyer參加了CW的超級英雄劇《[[閃電俠_(2014年電視影集)|閃電俠]]》飾演拉爾夫·迪布尼/收縮人，這是一個快速說話的私家偵探，能夠將自己的身體伸展成任何形狀或形式<ref name="ElongatedMan">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/07/the-flash-hartley-sawyer-elongated-man-ralph-dibny-season-4-the-cw-1202139510/|title='The Flash': Hartley Sawyer To Play Elongated Man In Season 4|first=Denise|last=Petski|website=[[Deadline_Hollywood|Deadline Hollywood]]|date=July 31, 2017|accessdate=July 31, 2017|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6sNV3pB8C?url=http://deadline.com/2017/07/the-flash-hartley-sawyer-elongated-man-ralph-dibny-season-4-the-cw-1202139510/|archivedate=2017-07-31|deadurl=no}}</ref> 。次年6月，第五季被提升為主演角色<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tvline.com/2018/06/15/flash-season-5-hartley-sawyer-series-regular-ralph-elongated-man/|title='The Flash' Promotes Hartley Sawyer to Series Regular for Season 5|website=TVLine|first=Matt Webb|last=Mitovich|date=June 15, 2018}}</REF>。

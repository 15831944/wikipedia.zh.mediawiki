'''哈里王子与梅根·马克尔的婚礼'''于2018年5月19日在[[英格兰|英格兰]][[温莎城堡|温莎城堡]]的[[圣乔治礼拜堂_(温莎城堡)|圣乔治礼拜堂]]举行。新郎为[[英国王室|英国王室]]成员[[薩塞克斯公爵亨利王子|哈里王子]]，新娘则是美国籍女演员[[萨塞克斯公爵夫人梅根|梅根·马克尔]]。

== 宣布订婚 ==
[[File:Prince_Harry_and_Meghan_Markle.jpg|250px]]
威尔士的亨利王子（通常称为哈里王子）是[[威爾斯親王查爾斯|威尔士亲王查尔斯]]与[[威尔士王妃戴安娜|威尔士王妃戴安娜]]的次子，也是[[英国王位继承|英国王位的第六继承人]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.royal.uk/prince-harry|title=Prince Harry|work=The Royal Household|access-date=2017-11-28|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171201035322/https://www.royal.uk/prince-harry|archivedate=2017-12-01}}</ref>。梅根·马克尔是一位美国女演员，因出演美剧《[[金装律师|金装律师]]》而知名。他们二人于2016年6月开始交往。2016年11月8日英国王室首次承认了两人的关系。当时王室的新闻秘书发表了一项官方声明，谴责外界对梅根的辱骂和骚扰<ref name=":2">{{Cite news|url=https://www.royal.uk/statement-communications-secretary-prince-harry|title=A Statement by the Communications Secretary to Prince Harry|last=Vallance|first=Adam|date=2016-11-08|work=The Royal Family|access-date=2017-09-26|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170927052849/https://www.royal.uk/statement-communications-secretary-prince-harry|archivedate=2017-09-27}}</ref>。

2017年11月27日，[[克拉伦斯府|克拉伦斯府]]宣布哈里王子将于2018年春迎娶梅根·马克尔<ref>{{Cite tweet|number=935085955383427072|user=ClarenceHouse|title=The Prince of Wales is delighted to announce the engagement of Prince Harry to Ms. Meghan Markle. pic.twitter.com/zdaHR4mcY6|author=Clarence House|author-link=Clarence House|date=2017-11-27|access-date=2017-12-04|archive-url=http://archive.is/2017.12.04-223702/https://twitter.com/clarencehouse/status/935085955383427072|archive-date=2017-12-04|dead-url=no|link=yes}}</ref>。二人当月稍早在伦敦订婚。哈里王子送给梅根的订婚戒指由王室官方珠宝商Cleave and Company定制。其上镶有三颗钻石，包括采自[[博茨瓦纳|博茨瓦纳]]的一颗大钻石以及从他母亲戴安娜王妃珠宝收藏中挑选的两颗小钻石<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2017/11/27/prince-harry-meghan-markle-marry-couple-announce-engagement/|title=Prince Harry and Meghan Markle engaged: Royal knew fiancee was 'the one' from 'very first time we met'|author=<!--Not stated-->|date=2017-11-27|website=The Daily Telegraph|access-date=2017-12-04|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171204003411/http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2017/11/27/prince-harry-meghan-markle-marry-couple-announce-engagement/|archivedate=2017-12-04}}</ref>。同时，声明表示他们婚后将住在[[肯辛顿宫|肯辛顿宫]]的诺丁汉别墅（Nottingham Cottage）<ref name="guardian"/>。

威尔士亲王、女王以及爱丁堡公爵都对二人订婚的消息表达了喜悦之情。同时，英国首相[[文翠珊|特雷莎·梅]]、反对党领袖[[杰里米·科尔宾|杰里米·科尔宾]]等政界人物亦向新人表示了祝贺。在宣布订婚之后，二人接受了[[英國廣播公司新聞|英国广播公司新闻]]主播[[米莎·侯賽因|米莎·侯賽因]]（Mishal Husain）的独家专访<ref name="guardian">{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2017/nov/27/prince-harry-to-marry-meghan-markle|title=Prince Harry to marry Meghan Markle|last1=Siddique|first1=Haroon|date=2017-11-27|work=The Guardian|accessdate=2017-11-27|last2=Watt|first2=Holly|last3=Booth|first3=Robert|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171127200809/https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2017/nov/27/prince-harry-to-marry-meghan-markle|archivedate=2017-11-27}}</ref>。

次日，婚礼确定于2018年5月19日在[[温莎城堡|温莎城堡]]的[[圣乔治礼拜堂_(温莎城堡)|圣乔治礼拜堂]]举行<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/KensingtonRoyal/status/935531264140070912|title=The marriage of Prince Harry and Ms. Meghan Markle will take place at St. George's Chapel, Windsor Castle in May 2018.pic.twitter.com/lJdtWnbdpB|first=Kensington|last=Palace|date=2017-11-28|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171128162622/https://twitter.com/KensingtonRoyal/status/935531264140070912|archivedate=2017-11-28}}{{Primary source inline|date=2017年12月}}</ref>。

== 婚礼 ==
=== 筹备 ===

=== 场地 ===
典礼于2018年5月19日星期六在[[温莎城堡|温莎城堡]][[圣乔治礼拜堂_(温莎城堡)|圣乔治礼拜堂]]举行<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.stgeorges-windsor.org/news/royal-wedding/ |title=Royal Wedding |date=28 November 2017 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171227154052/https://www.stgeorges-windsor.org/news/royal-wedding/ |archivedate=27 December 2017 |website=St George's Chapel }}</ref>。礼拜堂之前是哈里王子的叔父[[威塞克斯伯爵爱德华王子|威塞克斯伯爵爱德华王子]]和表兄[[彼得·菲利浦斯|彼得·菲利浦斯]]婚礼、威尔士亲王和哈利继母[[康沃爾公爵夫人卡蜜拉|康沃爾公爵夫人卡蜜拉]]婚礼祝颂仪式举办地<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.townandcountrymag.com/society/tradition/g15881437/royal-weddings-history-windsor-castle-st-georges-chapel/|title=This Is What Royal Weddings at Windsor Are Really Like|date=25 January 2018|work=Town & Country|access-date=2 February 2018|language=en-US|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180203065514/http://www.townandcountrymag.com/society/tradition/g15881437/royal-weddings-history-windsor-castle-st-georges-chapel/|archivedate=3 February 2018}}</ref>。

=== 开销 ===
皇室宣布负责典礼一切开销<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-42156565 |title=Prince Harry and Meghan Markle to wed at Windsor Castle in May |author=Adam Whitty |publisher=BBC |accessdate=29 November 2017 |quote=The Royal Family will pay for the wedding, including the service, music, flowers and reception |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171128201103/http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-42156565 |archivedate=28 November 2017 }}</ref>，其中蛋糕、花卉和菜肴饮品的费用分别预估为5万、11万和28.6万欧元<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/business-44154438|title=Royal wedding 2018: Who's paying?|publisher=BBC|date=17 May 2018|accessdate=18 May 2018}}</ref>，总费用预计在3200万欧元左右<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/women/life/inside-prince-harry-meghan-markles-32-million-wedding/|title=How much will Prince Harry and Meghan Markle's Royal wedding cost?|publisher=The Daily Telegraph|first=Cara|last=McGoogan|date=9 May 2018|accessdate=18 May 2018}}</ref>。安保费用比2011年剑桥公爵和公爵夫人的婚礼要低<ref name="CNN-Tax">{{cite web|url=http://money.cnn.com/2018/05/17/pf/royal-wedding-cost-who-pays-queen-prince-charles/index.html|title=The Royal Wedding: Who's picking up the tab?|publisher=CNN|first=Alanna|last=Petroff|date=17 May 2018|accessdate=18 May 2018}}</ref>。到5月底，安保费用据估计在“200万到400万欧元”。如果费用超过{{le|泰晤士谷警察部队|Thames Valley Police}}年度预算1%，警察和犯罪局局长可以申请特别资金，然而那时的开销“比申请款项的标准线要少400万欧”<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-england-berkshire-44303756|title=Royal wedding: Grant 'may cover cost of policing'|publisher=BBC|date=30 May 2018|accessdate=14 October 2018}}</ref>。[[溫莎-梅登黑德|温莎-梅登黑德皇家自治市镇]]据报花了260万欧元清理城镇和道路<ref name="CNN-Tax"/>。据估计婚礼会刺激旅游业，带来高达5亿欧元的经济收入<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-britain-royals-economy/prince-harrys-wedding-to-meghan-markle-set-to-boost-uk-economy-idUSKBN1ES0PN|title=Prince Harry's wedding to Meghan Markle 'set to boost UK economy'|publisher=Reuters|date=3 January 2018|accessdate=18 May 2018}}</ref>。

=== 婚礼服装 ===
婚礼请柬要求男士穿着“正装、[[晨礼服|晨礼服]]或休闲[[西装|西装]]”，女士穿着“日常装和帽子”。

梅根·马克尔的婚纱由英国设计师{{le|克莱尔·怀特·凯勒|Clare Waight Keller}}在服装品牌[[纪梵希|纪梵希]]的支持下设计。服装以“双层真丝布料制作，衬裙配以三层丝绸透明硬纱”制作，领口采用一字型设计，长袖，裙摆落地。丝绸头纱长4.9米，绣有55朵花，其中53朵代表[[英联邦国家|英联邦国家]]，1朵是梅根和哈利住所{{le|诺丁汉小屋|Nottingham Cottage}}门前种植的腊梅，1朵是加利福尼亚州州花[[花菱草|加州罂粟]]。头冠采用束发设计，上面镶嵌着1932年为[[特克的玛丽|玛丽女王]]打造，经伊丽莎白二世借给马克尔的钻石。中心别针是1893年[[林肯郡|林肯郡]]的婚礼贡品。铂金头带由11个部分组成，中央的可拆卸别针交织着蛋白石和钻石。鞋子也由纪梵希提供，采用尖头设计。

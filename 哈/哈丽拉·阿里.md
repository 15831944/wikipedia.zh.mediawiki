'''哈麗拉·阿里'''（Khalilah Camacho Ali，{{bd|1950年|3月17日||}}）是已故拳王[[穆罕默德·阿里|穆罕默德·阿里]]的前妻。<ref name="people.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20071058,00.html|title=Bitter About Ali ('i Call Him Cassius Clay'), Ex-Wife Khalilah Forges a Life of Her Own|work=people.com}}</ref> 她在17岁的时候（1967年）和阿里结婚，<ref name="people.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20071058,00.html|title=Bitter About Ali ('i Call Him Cassius Clay'), Ex-Wife Khalilah Forges a Life of Her Own|work=people.com}}</ref> 在27岁的时候（1977年）离婚。<ref name="Company1977">{{cite journal|title=Ebony|url={{google books |plainurl=y |id=4MsDAAAAMBAJ|page=82}}|last=Kisner|first=Ron|date=September 1977|publisher=Johnson Publishing Company|page=82|ISSN=0012-9011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/01/22/muhammad-ali-ex-wife-_n_4646970.html|title=Muhammed Ali's Ex-Wife, Khalilah Camacho Ali, Opens Up About Their Marriage|work=The Huffington Post}}</ref> 他们共生育了四个孩子。<ref name="people.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20071058,00.html|title=Bitter About Ali ('i Call Him Cassius Clay'), Ex-Wife Khalilah Forges a Life of Her Own|work=people.com}}</ref>

她学习过[[空手道|空手道]]，并与1977年获得[[Black_belt_(martial_arts)|黑带]]三段。<ref name="Company1977">{{cite journal|title=Ebony|url={{google books |plainurl=y |id=4MsDAAAAMBAJ|page=82}}|last=Kisner|first=Ron|date=September 1977|publisher=Johnson Publishing Company|page=82|ISSN=0012-9011}}</ref> Khalilah 继续跟 Jim Kelly and Steve Saunders学习空手道，<ref name="Company1977">{{cite journal|title=Ebony|url={{google books |plainurl=y |id=4MsDAAAAMBAJ|page=82}}|last=Kisner|first=Ron|date=September 1977|publisher=Johnson Publishing Company|page=82|ISSN=0012-9011}}</ref> 最终获得了黑带九段。<ref name="franciscoalvarado">{{cite web|url=http://www.miaminewtimes.com/news/khalilah-camacho-ali-was-in-the-champs-corner-through-some-of-boxings-greatest-moments-6380493|title=Khalilah Camacho-Ali was in the Champ's corner through some of boxing's greatest moments|author=Francisco Alvarado|work=Miami New Times}}</ref> 她七次出现在''Ebony Magazine'' 的封面上。<ref name="franciscoalvarado">{{cite web|url=http://www.miaminewtimes.com/news/khalilah-camacho-ali-was-in-the-champs-corner-through-some-of-boxings-greatest-moments-6380493|title=Khalilah Camacho-Ali was in the Champ's corner through some of boxing's greatest moments|author=Francisco Alvarado|work=Miami New Times}}</ref> 她在20世纪80年代再婚，后来又离婚了两次。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.browardpalmbeach.com/news/khalilah-camacho-ali-stood-by-muhammad-ali-through-exile-and-triumph-6344636|title=Khalilah Camacho-Ali Stood by Muhammad Ali Through Exile and Triumph|author=Francisco Alvarado|work=New Times Broward-Palm Beach}}</ref>

她出现在[[珍·芳達|珍·芳達]]的电影[[危機_(電影)|危机]]中。<ref>[[imdbtitle:0078966|Internet Movie Database]]</ref>

== 漢字名字 ==
'''安喜延'''為父母在她出生時取的名字。父為[[成均館大學|成均館大學]]學生，母為[[延世大學|延世大學]]學生，父母紀念兩人在[[延世大學|延世大學]]相遇，故把'''哈妮'''取名為「'''喜延'''」（有「喜歡」及「延世大學」的意思）。未正名前，經常被誤寫為'''安希妍

== 早年生活 ==
哈妮是家中長女，下有一個弟弟，小時候家境窮困<ref>{{Citation|last=vincent5218|title=EXID Hani吃麵包流淚 回想起貧困的日子 (中字)|date=2017-01-27|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rHubxlfG9Jo|accessdate=2017-01-28}}</ref>。曾為[[Cube娛樂|cube娛樂、]][[JYP娛樂|JYP娛樂]]旗下練習生，曾與[[Sistar|Sistar]]成員[[孝琳|孝琳]]、[[Secret_(組合)|Secret]]成員[[宋枝恩|枝恩]]、[[BESTie|BESTie]]成員[[有智|有智]]一起組成四人女子組合，後從[[JYP娛樂|JYP娛樂]]離開後赴中國留學大約一年。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://mwave.interest.me/enewsworld/tw/article/89348 | title=Hani當過JYP練習生　比孝琳更早被除名 | publisher=interest.me | date=2015-03-17 | accessdate=2015-08-11 | language=zh-hk}}</ref>

== 演藝經歷 ==
哈妮的母親曾經反對哈妮成為藝人，並給她在3年限期內成功，最後哈妮在限期內以[[EXID|EXID]]成員身份成名。<ref>[http://hk.on.cc/hk/bkn/cnt/entertainment/20170305/mobile/bkn-20170305205447858-0305_00862_001.html 做藝人遭反對　Hani：媽媽畀我三年限期]</ref>

2012年2月16日作為EXID女子組合成員出道，出道主打曲為《Whoz That Girl》。2013年，與率智組成小分隊DASONI於02月15日推出出道單曲《Good Bye》。2014年組合EXID轉至[[Wellmade_STAR_M|Yedang娛樂]]（現更名為Banana Culture<ref>{{cite web |url=http://kpopn.niusnews.com/=K20qses9 |title=Yedang更名 |publisher=kpopn 來源：sportschosun / yonhapnews / sportskhan 翻譯 & 撰寫：Kpopn 的 Elly |date=2016-03-02 }}</ref>）旗下，以《Up & Down》數位單曲回歸歌壇，但並未得到耀眼的成績，直至2014年10月9日一名[[YouTube|YouTube]]用戶([https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCuN9yuqJWM6CeqqUKPFckhA @pharkil])在頻道上上傳一段哈妮公演《Up & Down》時的Fancam（歌迷直接拍攝影片）<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cmKuGxb23z0 직캠/Fancam 141008 EXID(하니) 위아래 @ 파주 한마음 위문공연]</ref>，使所屬組合及個人得到了前所未有的關注。

2016年2月，福布斯公布2016[[韩国福布斯名人榜|韩国福布斯名人榜]]（即2015年度），哈妮以個人身份首次打入福布斯名人榜，排名第37位。<ref>[https://jmagazine.joins.com/forbes/view/310316]</ref>

{{TransH}}
在 2016, Hani became the co-host of ''[[Baek_Jong-won's_Three_Great_Emperors|Baek Jong-won's Three Great Emperors]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Hani to co-host 'Baek Jong-won's Three Great Emperors'|url=http://english.yonhapnews.co.kr/culturesports/2016/01/28/0701000000AEN20160128009800315.html|website=[[Yonhap_News|Yonhap News]]|date=28 January 2016|accessdate=2016-02-15}}</ref> In February 2016, she appeared on ''[[King_of_Mask_Singer|King of Mask Singer]]'' as "Little Match Girl", a get-up completed with a mask and costumes to hide her identity. Hani surprised the panel of judges and an audience with her rich and husky voice (mostly due to the higher vocal range used as a vocalist in EXID).<ref>{{cite web|title=Hani wows with performance on King of Mask Singer|url=http://www.koreaboo.com/netizens/hani-wows-with-performance-on-king-of-masked-singer/}}</ref> Hani defeated [[Kim_Feel|Kim Feel]] and Jo Hang-jo but she was subsequently defeated by the Mask King at the time, Ha Hyun-woo of [[Guckkasten|Guckkasten]]. Nevertheless, Hani's performances attracted attention and recognition for her unique vocal and passion for singing.

On January 12, 2017, it was announced that [[f(x)_(band)|f(x)]]'s [[Luna_(singer)|Luna]], Hani, and [[Mamamoo|Mamamoo]]'s Solar would release a collaborative dance song on January 19, produced by Park Geun-tae, who also wrote [[Exo_(band)|EXO]]'s [[Byun_Baek-hyun|Baekhyun]] and [[Miss_A|Miss A]]'s [[Bae_Suzy|Suzy]]'s duet "[[Dream_(Suzy_and_Baekhyun_song)|Dream]]".<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.newsen.com/news_view.php?uid=201701120851336710|title=루나X하니X솔라 미스틱서 뭉쳤다, 핵폭탄급 걸그룹 컬래버 성사|last=|first=|date=January 12, 2017|work=Newsen|language=ko|access-date=January 16, 2017|via=Naver}}</ref> It was later revealed that the song's title is "Honey Bee", and is of the pop soul genre, incorporating [[Roland_TR-808|808]] bass and saxophone.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://osen.mt.co.kr/article/G1110568889|title=루나X하니X솔라, 콜라보 곡명은 ‘허니 비’..팝소울|last=|first=|date=January 16, 2017|work=OSEN|language=ko|access-date=January 16, 2017|via=}}</ref> On January 19, the song "Honey Bee" was officially released at [[Mystic_Entertainment|Mystic Entertainment]]'s YouTube channel.

== 個人生活 ==
曾經在中國留學的'''哈妮'''在[[我去上學啦_(韓國)|我去上學啦]]節目中表現出驚人的[[中文|中文]]、英文和[[微积分学|微積分]]實力，更透露自己曾在高中時期準備了兩個月時間就取得了[[多益|多益]]考試900分，[[IQ|IQ]]高達145。<ref>[http://www.ettoday.net/news/20150403/488307.htm Hani頭腦超好！多益只讀2個月成績這麼驚人]</ref>由此可看出她不凡的學習能力。不僅如此，在[[我們小區藝體能|我們小區藝體能]]節目之中提起中學時期是[[鐵人三項|鐵人三項]]選手<ref>[http://www.ettoday.net/news/20150723/539223.htm?from=fb_et_beauty Hani游泳好驚人！　自曝：太好動被迫參加「鐵人三項」]</ref>，曾在[[出发梦之队2|出发梦之队2]]中展現過人的游泳實力，擅長[[仰泳|仰泳]] （小學有得獎）、[[蹼泳|雙蹼泳]]及[[蝶泳|蝶泳]]。<ref>{{Citation|last=Sylvia Liao|title=【中字】我們小區藝體能 HANI 游泳cut|date=2015-07-22|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XrkEslPF_C0|accessdate=2016-08-06}}</ref> 後來被演藝圈公認是『多讀王』，常常在社交網站分享讀書心得。<ref>《[[鍋墊|鍋墊]]》最終回擔任偶像界書評人。</ref>

2016年1月1日，韓國知名媒體Dispatch報導哈妮與[[JYJ|JYJ]]的[[金俊秀|金俊秀]]交往消息，雙方隨即透過經紀公司承認已交往半年。

2016年9月13日，與JYJ成員金俊秀近一年的戀情，經雙方經紀公司證實二人因繁忙的行程而分手，回歸前後輩的關係。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2016/09/exids-hani-and-jyjs-junsu-confirm-breakup |title=EXID's Hani and JYJ's Junsu confirm breakup |language=en |publisher=allkpop |date=2016-09-13 |accessdate=2016-09-13 }}</ref>

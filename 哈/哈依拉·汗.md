'''哈依拉·汗'''（{{lang-ur|{{nastaliq|ہاجرہ خان|ur}}}}，{{bd|1993年|12月29日}}）是巴基斯坦国家女子足球队的[[队长_(足球)|队长]]。她在2010年加入国家队，自2014年起担任队长。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Hajra's footballing journey and her four aspirations|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/821803/pakistan-womens-team-move-up-to-fourth/|work=The Express Tribune|date=2015-01-14|accessdate=2019-04-05|language=en|author=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802224950/https://tribune.com.pk/story/821803/pakistan-womens-team-move-up-to-fourth/|archive-date=2018-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 俱乐部生涯 ==
哈依拉幼年时曾梦想成为[[田径|田径]]运动员而非足球运动员。2008年，时任蒂亚女子足球俱乐部的教练萨蒂亚·谢伊克在招募省队球员时发现了她，并把她收入队中。她在3场比赛中灌进9个进球，获得了联赛最佳射手的称号。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Hajra Khan – national Hero of women football – FootballPakistan.com (FPDC)|url=https://www.footballpakistan.com/2013/09/hajra-khan-national-hero-of-women-football/|accessdate=2019-04-05|language=en|author=|date=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802131947/http://www.footballpakistan.com/2013/09/hajra-khan-national-hero-of-women-football/|archive-date=2018-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>次年，凭借在3场比赛中的8个进球，她再次获此殊荣。<ref name=":0" />

2014年，哈依拉离开蒂亚俱乐部，转投俾路支联俱乐部。在当年的{{Le|巴基斯坦全国女子足球联赛|National Women Football Championship}}上，她面对老东家蒂亚使出了[[帽子戏法|帽子戏法]]，帮助俾路支联以7－0战胜对手。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Winner of All Sindh Noor Women Football Championship crowned: Balochistan United Women Football Club. – FootballPakistan.com (FPDC)|url=https://www.footballpakistan.com/2014/02/winner-of-all-sindh-noor-women-football-championship-crowned-balochistan-united-women-football-club/|accessdate=2019-04-05|language=en|author=|date=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802142011/http://www.footballpakistan.com/2014/02/winner-of-all-sindh-noor-women-football-championship-crowned-balochistan-united-women-football-club/|archive-date=2018-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>之后，她前往马尔代夫为阳光度假村俱乐部效力。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Hajra's footballing journey and her four aspirations|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/821803/pakistan-womens-team-move-up-to-fourth/|work=The Express Tribune|date=2015-01-14|accessdate=2019-04-05|language=en|author=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802224950/https://tribune.com.pk/story/821803/pakistan-womens-team-move-up-to-fourth/|archive-date=2018-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>

2015年夏天，哈依拉前往德国与当地的俱乐部进行季前训练。但由于签证问题，她不能留在德国踢球。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Back from Germany: Hajra content with her stint in Europe|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/942926/back-from-germany-hajra-content-with-her-stint-in-europe/|work=The Express Tribune|date=2015-08-24|accessdate=2019-04-05|language=en|author=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802193358/https://tribune.com.pk/story/942926/back-from-germany-hajra-content-with-her-stint-in-europe/|archive-date=2018-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 国家队生涯 ==
2010年12月，她参加了首次[[南亞女子足球錦標賽|南亚女子足球锦标赛]]，并带领球队打进半决赛。在这场半决赛中，巴基斯坦国家队以0－8不敌印度国家队。<ref>{{Cite web|title=11th South Asian Federation Games 2010 (Bangladesh)|url=http://www.rsssf.com/tabless/safg2010.html#wom|work=www.rsssf.com|accessdate=2019-04-05|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150226120927/http://www.rsssf.com/tabless/safg2010.html#wom|archive-date=2015-02-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 其他运动 ==
在2011年于[[斯里兰卡|斯里兰卡]][[汉班托塔|汉班托塔]]举行的{{Le|2011年南亚沙滩运动会|2011 South Asian Beach Games|第1届南亚沙滩运动会}}上，不满18周岁的哈伊拉在[[籃網球|篮网球]]项目中为巴基斯坦国家队夺得了铜牌。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Hajra Khan handed Trials in German Bundesliga|url=https://www.desiblitz.com/content/hajra-khan-handed-trials-in-german-bundesliga|work=DESIblitz|date=2015-07-07|accessdate=2019-04-05|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190328142228/https://www.desiblitz.com/content/hajra-khan-handed-trials-in-german-bundesliga|archive-date=2019-03-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>

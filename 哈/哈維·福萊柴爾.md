'''哈維·福萊柴爾'''（{{lang-en|Harvey Fletcher}}，{{bd|1884年|9月11日|1981年|7月23日}}）是一位[[美国|美国]]的[[物理学家|物理学家]]，<ref>{{cite journal|author=Gardner, Mark B.|title=Obituary: Harvey Fletcher|journal=Physics Today|date=October 1981|volume=34|issue=10|pages=116|url=http://www.physicstoday.org/resource/1/phtoad/v34/i10/p116_s1?bypassSSO=1|doi=10.1063/1.2914315|bibcode=1981PhT....34j.116G}}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>因发明了2-A {{tsl|en|audiometer|听力计}}<ref>{{cite web|last1=Fletcher|first1=Tom|title=In Memory of Harvey Fletcher|url=http://www.et.byu.edu/~tom/family/Harvey_Fletcher/harvey_fletcher.html}}</ref>和早期电子[[助聽器|助聽器]]而被称作“[[立體聲|立體聲]]”之父。<ref>{{cite book |author=William M. Hartmann |title=Signals, Sound, and Sensation |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=3N72rIoTHiEC&pg=PA72|date=1997-01-09 |publisher=Springer |isbn=978-1-56396-283-7 |pages=72–}}</ref><ref name=SHFletch-NAS>{{cite web|last1=Fletcher|first1=Stephen H.|title=HARVEY FLETCHER 1884-1981|url=http://www.nasonline.org/publications/biographical-memoirs/memoir-pdfs/fletcher-harvey.pdf|website=NAS Online|publisher=National Academy of Sciences|accessdate=2015-11-20|date=1992}}</ref>

另外，他是羅伯特·密立根[[油滴實驗|油滴實驗]]的實際操作者。{{cn}}

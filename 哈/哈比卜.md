'''哈比卜'''（{{lang-ar|حبيب|''ḥabīb''}}，{{IPA-ar|ħabiːb }}），另译'''哈比布'''或'''哈比''', 阿拉伯男子名字或姓氏，意为“亲爱的”。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.behindthename.com/name/habib|title=Behind the Name: Meaning, Origin and History of the Name Habib|author=Mike Campbell|work=Behind the Name}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.namearabic.com/en/product/free/habib|title=NameArabic: Meaning, Arabic names with Calligraphy and meaning|author=Nihad Nadam|work=Name Arabic}}</ref>该名字在[[穆斯林世界|穆斯林世界]]很常见，尤其是在[[中东|中东]]和[[非洲|非洲]]。在以[[也门|也门]]和[[东南亚|东南亚]]国家诸如[[汶莱|汶莱]]、[[新加坡|新加坡]]、[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]]和[[马来西亚|马来西亚]]，也是一个对[[賽義德|賽義德]]家族的穆斯林学者的尊称。
值得注意的是，名字与其他阿拉伯语名字一样，并不局限于穆斯林。以哈比卜为名的著名基督教徒包括“哈比卜执事”<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.newadvent.org/fathers/0857.htm|title=CHURCH FATHERS: Martyrdom of Habib the Deacon|website=www.newadvent.org|access-date=2017-12-17}}</ref>、加布里埃尔·哈比卜<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=TugMX1cW1UwC&pg=PA92&lpg=PA92&dq=is+habib+ever+a+christian+name&source=bl&ots=Ya1LDtqem5&sig=tEOjWMHcSJhCYS6rAjuvQJr7cUI&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiWw4b21fzXAhWqAMAKHQhLAQM4ChDoAQgtMAE#v=onepage&q=is%20habib%20ever%20a%20christian%20name&f=false|title=World Christianity in the Twentieth Century: A Reader|last=Davies|first=Noel|last2=Conway|first2=Martin|date=2008|publisher=Hymns Ancient and Modern Ltd|isbn=9780334040446|language=en}}</ref>和哲人哈比卜。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=9MsUG7o1wuwC&pg=PA700&lpg=PA700&dq=is+habib+ever+a+christian+name&source=bl&ots=P-AfOYHdQ8&sig=hEYeEM4C2sLRnsy-nzxQEQdWNww&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiWw4b21fzXAhWqAMAKHQhLAQM4ChDoAQgvMAI#v=onepage&q=is%20habib%20ever%20a%20christian%20name&f=false|title=The Ante-Nicene Fathers: The Writings of the Fathers Down to A.

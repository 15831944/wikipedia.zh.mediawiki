'''哈利法克斯爆炸'''發生在1917年12月6日，[[加拿大|加拿大]][[新斯科舍省|新斯科舍省]][[哈利法克斯|哈利法克斯]]，法國貨船SS Mont-Blanc滿載炸藥，與[[挪威|挪威]]船舶SS伊莫在海峽發生碰撞<ref>The ship had been named with the initials - JMO - after the senior owner of the company, Johan Martin Osmundsen (aka Jurgens M. Osmond), but people started calling her ''Imo'' and the name stuck. Source: ''Methods of Disaster Research'' by Robert A. Stallings (International Research Committee on Disasters ©2002), pp. 281-282.</ref>，海峽與[[哈利法克斯港|哈利法克斯港]]及[[貝德福德盆地|貝德福德盆地]]相接。大約二十分鐘後，法國貨船在港中爆發，摧毀哈利法克斯列治文區，並觸發18米高[[海嘯|海嘯]]。大約有2000人死於海嘯，火災和建築物倒塌，近9000人受傷<ref name=cbc>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/halifaxexplosion/he2_ruins/he2_ruins_explosion.html |title=CBC - Halifax Explosion 1917 |publisher=CBC.ca |date=2003-09-19 |accessdate=2011-02-25}}</ref>。哈利法克斯爆炸是[[核武器|核武器]]發展前最大的人造爆炸<ref name=TimeDisaster>{{cite book|title=Time: Disasters that Shook the World|publisher=Time Home Entertainment|location=New York City|year=2012|page=56|isbn=1-60320-247-1}}</ref>，約2900噸TNT炸藥。1918年5月，[[達爾豪西大學|達爾豪西大學]]教授霍華德·L.布朗森估計爆炸約2400噸TNT炸藥<ref>John Griffith Armstrong, ''The Halifax Explosion and the Royal Canadian Navy: Inquiry and Intrigue'' (2004, UBC Press), pp. 41-42</ref>。

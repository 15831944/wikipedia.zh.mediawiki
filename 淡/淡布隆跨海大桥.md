'''文莱淡布隆跨海大桥'''是一个将在2019年通车的跨海大桥，其长度约为30公里，建成后将连接[[文莱摩拉县|文莱摩拉县]]和[[淡布隆县|淡布隆县]]，使得文莱人不需要绕道至[[林梦|林梦]]进出两次。<ref>http://www.bt.com.bn/frontpage-news-national/2015/09/11/2-temburong-bridge-contracts-inked</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.pwd.gov.bn/images/temburong_bridge/industry%20day%20-%20session%200%20-%20background%20to%20the%20project.pdf|title = Proposed Temburong Bridge Project - Industry Briefing Day Session 0|website = Public Works Department Brunei|accessdate = 19 June 2014}}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.bt.com.bn/news-national/2013/05/10/temburong-bridge-ready-2018|title = Temburong Bridge ready 2018|last = Kamit|first = Rabiatul|date = 10 May 2013|work = The Brunei Times|accessdate = 29 May 2013|archiveurl = http://archive.bt.com.bn/news-national/2013/05/10/temburong-bridge-ready-2018|archivedate = 19 October 2013}}</ref>这项工程由南韓大林產業與中国建築承建。这也将成为文莱的新地标。

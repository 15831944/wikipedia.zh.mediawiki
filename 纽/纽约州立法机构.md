'''纽约州立法机构'''（{{lang-en|'''New York State Register'''}}）是[[纽约州政府|纽约州政府]]的官方期刊，其中包含有关拟议法规和规则制定活动的信息。<ref name=dar/> 每周由纽约州国务院管理规则处出版。<ref name=dar>{{cite web|title=Division of Administrative Rules|publisher=[[New_York_State_Department_of_State|New York State Department of State]]|url=http://www.dos.ny.gov/info/|accessdate=27 May 2014}}</ref>通用性和永久性法规被编入 [[w:New_York_Codes,_Rules_and_Regulations|New York Codes]]（NYCRR）.

从中分离出来的''The New York State Contract Reporter''包含了采购合同机会的通知，并由州经济发展部（与国家城市发展公司合并）发布。<ref>[[Consolidated_Laws_of_New_York|Economic Development Law]] article 4-C</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=State Authority Contract Manual: The New York State Contract Reporter|publisher=[[New_York_State_Comptroller|New York State Comptroller]]'s Office|accessdate=14 February 2016|url=http://www.osc.state.ny.us/stateauthority/MyWebHelp/Content/Manual/contract_repr.htm}}</ref>

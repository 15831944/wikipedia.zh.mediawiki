'''纽约大苹果马戏团'''是一个位于美国纽约州纽约市的[[马戏|马戏团]]。它于1977年开业，后来成为一个[[非营利组织|非营利组织]]和旅游景点<ref name="bigac">{{Cite web|url=http://www.circusesandsideshows.com/circuses/bigapplecircus.html|title=Big Apple Circus|publisher=Circuses and side shows|work=Circuses and side shows|accessdate=2016-10-10}}</ref>。马戏团以其社区外展计划而闻名，包括{{link-en|Clown Care|小丑护理}}、对动物的人道待遇<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thedailybeast.com/articles/2014/11/07/how-the-circus-got-a-social-conscience.html|title=How the Circus Got a Social Conscience|author=Jones|first=Justin|date=2014-11-07|work=The Daily Beast|accessdate=2017-01-14}}</ref>。纽约大苹果马戏团于2016年11月[[美國法典第11章破產法|申请破产保护]]并于2017年2月被Compass Partners收购<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2016/11/21/big-apple-circus-files-for-bankruptcy.html|title=That's all folks? Big Apple Circus files for bankruptcy|author=Whitten|first=Sarah|date=2016-11-21|work=CNBC|accessdate=2017-01-14}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/news/big-apple-circus-springs-new-owner/|title=Big Apple Circus springs back to life|author=|first=|date=|work=CBS News|accessdate=2017-03-06|language=en}}</ref><ref name=":3">{{Cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/big-apple-circus-finds-buyer-in-compass-partners-1486644176|title=Big Apple Circus Finds Buyer in Compass Partners|author=Randles|first=Jonathan|date=2017-02-09|work=Wall Street Journal|accessdate=2017-03-23|issn=0099-9660}}</ref>。马戏团于2017年10月举行了40周年纪念季<ref name=":2">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/02/14/arts/big-apple-circus-sold-to-the-highest-bidder-will-return-this-fall.html|title=Big Apple Circus, Sold to the Highest Bidder, Will Return This Fall|author=Barone|first=Joshua|date=2017-02-14|work=The New York Times|accessdate=2017-03-06|issn=0362-4331}}</ref>。

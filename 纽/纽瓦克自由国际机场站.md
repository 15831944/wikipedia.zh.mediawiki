'''纽瓦克自由国际机场站'''<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.panynj.gov/airports/ewr-to-from.html |title=To and From Newark|work=panynj.gov|accessdate=November 13, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.amtrak.com/servlet/ContentServer?pagename=am/am2Station/Station_Page&code=EWR|title=Newark Liberty International Airport, NJ Train Station (EWR) - Amtrak|work=amtrak.com|accessdate=November 13, 2015}} </ref>（通常又被称为'''纽瓦克国际机场站'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.njtransit.com/rg/rg_servlet.srv?hdnPageAction=TrainStationLookupFrom&selStation=37953|title=Newark Airport|work=njtransit.com|accessdate=November 13, 2015}}</ref>）是位于[[新澤西州|新澤西州]][[紐華克_(新澤西州)|紐瓦克]]的[[东北走廊|东北走廊]]铁路车站。车站通过单轨系统{{link-en|紐華克國際機場捷運|AirTrain Newark}}与[[纽瓦克自由国际机场|纽瓦克自由国际机场]]（EWR）的各个航站楼及停车场连接。车站有[[新泽西通勤铁路|新泽西通勤铁路]]的[[东北走廊线|东北走廊线]]和[[新泽西海岸线|新泽西海岸线]]、以及[[美铁|美铁]]的[[東北區域號|東北區域號]]及Keystone号列车停靠。车站附近没有任何行人路、巴士及行车道经过<ref>{{cite report   | title = Dayton Street Transformation Plan   | publisher = Newark Housing Authority   | date = April 2014   | url = http://www.newarkha.org/cni/CNIDOCS/CNI%20TRANSFORMATION%20PLAN/FinalDaytonStreetTransformationPlan%20(10.29.14).pdf   | accessdate = November 16, 2015   | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304033102/http://www.newarkha.org/cni/CNIDOCS/CNI%20TRANSFORMATION%20PLAN/FinalDaytonStreetTransformationPlan%20(10.29.14).pdf   | archive-date = 2016-03-04   | dead-url = yes   }}</ref>。

==概要==
车站于2001年10月21日启用<ref>{{cite news |title=New Train Service To Newark Airport |first=Elissa |last=Gootman |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2001/10/22/nyregion/metro-briefing-new-york-manhattan-new-train-service-to-newark-airport.html |newspaper=The New York Times |date=October 22, 2001 |accessdate=February 19, 2010}}</ref>。车站由同时管理[[纽瓦克机场|纽瓦克机场]]的[[纽新港务局|纽新港务局]]（PANYNJ）管理，因此与其他的美铁或新泽西通勤铁路有着不一样的设计，尤其是采用与机场风格一致的车站站内标识及导向指引<ref>[http://www.subwaynut.com/njt/newark_airport/index.php Newark-Liberty International Airport Station (The Subway Nut)]</ref>。港务局原先倾向于使用“纽瓦克机场”作为车站站名，但最后车站因机场在[[九一一袭击|九一一袭击]]事件后更名为“纽瓦克自由国际机场”而使用现名<ref name="name">{{cite news |title=Governors Seek a Name Change for Newark Airport |first=Michael |last=Wilson |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2002/08/22/nyregion/governors-seek-a-name-change-for-newark-airport.html |work=The New York Times |date=August 22, 2002 |publisher=NYTimes.com |accessdate=March 14, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Port Authority Extends Lease of a Renamed Newark Airport |first=Ronald |last=Smothers |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2002/08/30/nyregion/port-authority-extends-lease-of-a-renamed-newark-airport.html |newspaper=The New York Times |publisher=NYTimes.com |date=August 30, 2002 |accessdate=March 13, 2010}}</ref>。

===东北走廊===
由本站乘坐大约6分钟的[[东北走廊|东北走廊]]列车至[[紐瓦克賓夕法尼亞車站|纽瓦克宾夕法尼亚车站]]后，可转乘PATH、{{link-en|紐瓦克輕鐵|Newark Light Rail}}或巴士前往泽西市、霍伯肯及曼哈顿下城区域。由本站乘坐大约22-25分钟车程的列车至[[紐約賓夕法尼亞車站|纽约宾夕法尼亚车站]]后可转乘[[长岛铁路|长岛铁路]]或[[纽约地铁|纽约地铁]]路线。至{{link-en|特倫頓車站|Trenton Transit Center}}需31-50分钟，至[[30街車站|費城30街車站]]需59-67分钟<ref>Summer 2014 Amtrak Timetable, http://www.amtrak.com/ccurl/749/127/Northeast-Regional-1-Schedule-060914.pdf</ref><ref>NJ Transit, Summer 2014 North Jersey Coast Timetable,  http://www.njtransit.com/pdf/rail/R0080.pdf</ref><ref>NJ Transit, Summer 2014 Northeast Corridor Timeable, http://www.njtransit.com/pdf/rail/R0070.pdf</ref> 。通过新泽西通勤列车服务可到达{{link-en|塞卡烏卡斯交匯處|Secaucus Junction}}并可转乘其他新泽西通勤列车服务。

===机场捷运===
乘坐机场捷运需5.50美元的车费。车费通常包含在NJT发售的前往本站的列车的车费中，这些车票票面上通常标有「**EWR**」字样。NJT月票持有者若无机场特别版需支付相应车费，但11岁以下的乘客可得到豁免<ref>[http://www.njtransit.com/tm/tm_servlet.srv?hdnPageAction=FYI9To]</ref>。在东北走廊和机场捷运站台之间设有美铁售票窗口。

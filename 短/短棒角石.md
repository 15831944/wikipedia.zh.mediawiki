'''短棒角石'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Plectronoceras'''''}}）是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[鸚鵡螺亞綱|鸚鵡螺類]]。為[[短棒角石科|短棒角石科]]的模式屬。牠們生存在晚[[寒武紀|寒武紀]]的[[海洋|海洋]]中，是目前已知最古老的鸚鵡螺類，也是迄今為止所發現最早的[[頭足類|頭足類]]。<ref name=Dzik1981>{{Cite journal
 | last = Dzik | first =  J.
 | year = 1981
 | title = Origin of the cephalopoda
 | journal = Acta Palaeontologica Polonica
 | volume = 26
 | issue = 2
 | pages = 161–191
 | url = http://www.paleo.pan.pl/people/Dzik/Publications/Cephalopoda.pdf
}}</ref><ref name=FWPlectronoceras>[http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=12319 ''Plectronoceras''] at [[Fossilworks|Fossilworks]].org</ref><!-- Its 14 known specimens hail from the basal [[Fengshan_Formation|Fengshan Formation]] (north-east China) of the earliest [[Fengshanian|Fengshanian]] stage. --><ref name=mollusca12-2>{{The Mollusca|volume=12|chapter=Main features of cephalopod evolution|author=Clarke, M.R.; Trueman, E.R.}}</ref>其形態與所有頭足類最後共同祖先的假設密切相符。<ref name=mollusca12-2/>牠們的化石目前僅發現於[[美國|美國]][[德克薩斯州|德克薩斯州]]的[[聖薩巴石灰岩|聖薩巴石灰岩]]中。<ref name=FWPlectronoceras />

== 特徵 ==
短棒角石有著短角狀的外殼，古生物學家根據其[[肌肉|肌肉]]與殼接合的痕跡，推斷其可能保留有近似於[[單板綱|單板綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]]的[[腹足|腹足]]結構。<ref name="Nautilus">{{Cite web |url = https://www.bilibili.com/read/cv194164/ |title = 頭足綱二三事（2）鸚鵡螺篇（上） |language = en |accessdate = 2018-05-23 }}</ref>目前尚未發現完整的短棒角石化石，尚無標本含有其殼體的尖端或開口。<ref name=mollusca12-2/>

'''槍蝦科'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|Alpheidae}}），又名'''鼓虾科'''<ref name=blueanimalbio>{{cite web |url=http://www.blueanimalbio.com/bugs/ruanjia/zhenxia.htm |title=真虾下目 Caridea |publisher=[http://www.blueanimalbio.com/ 蓝色动物学] |language=zh-hans |accessdate=2013-01-12 |date=2012-07-31 }}</ref>，是[[真蝦下目|真蝦下目]][[槍蝦總科|槍蝦總科]]的其中一個科，特徵是兩邊的爪不對稱，而較大那邊的爪可發出巨響，就如槍聲一樣，因而得名（详见[[鼓虾|鼓虾]]）。

槍蝦科在世界的分布廣泛而且分散，包括有約600個品種，可分成最少38個屬<ref name="multiple">{{cite journal |author=A. Anker, S. T. Ahyong, P. Y. Noel, and A. R. Palmer |title=Morphological phylogeny of alpheid shrimps: parallel preadaptation and the origin of a key morphological innovation, the snapping claw |journal=[[Evolution|Evolution]] |volume=60 |issue=12 |year=2006 |pages=2507–2528 |doi=10.1554/05-486.1 | pmid = 17263113 }}</ref>。當中最重要的兩個屬是[[槍蝦屬|槍蝦屬]]（{{lang|la|''Alpheus''}}）和[[合鼓蝦屬|合鼓蝦屬]]（{{lang|la|''Synalpheus''}}），分別有超過250種和100種<ref>{{cite journal |author=W. Kim & L. G. Abele |title=The snapping shrimp genus ''Alpheus'' from the Eastern Pacific (Decapoda: Caridea: Alpheidae) |journal={{tsl|en|Smithsonian Contributions to Zoology|Smithsonian Contributions to Zoology}} |volume=454 |year=1988 |pages=1–119 |url=http://si-pddr.si.edu/dspace/bitstream/10088/5122/2/SCtZ-0454-Lo_res.pdf}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author={{tsl|en|Fenner A. Chace, Jr.|Fenner A. Chace, Jr.}} |title=The Caridean Shrimps (Crustacea: Decapoda) of the Albatross Philippine Expedition, 1907–1910, Part 5: Family Alpheidae |journal={{tsl|en|Smithsonian Contributions to Zoology|Smithsonian Contributions to Zoology}} |volume=466 |year=1988 |pages=1–99 |url=http://si-pddr.si.edu/dspace/bitstream/10088/5144/3/SCtZ-0466-Hi_res.pdf}}</ref>。槍蝦科的物種會在[[珊瑚礁|珊瑚礁]]、[[海床|海床]]上的[[海草|海草]]和[[牡蠣礁|牡蠣礁]]居住，還會在居所挖洞穴來捕獵。幾乎所有本科的物種均在熱帶和溫帶的沿海和海洋水域中發現，就只有棲息於寒冷海洋的[[乙鼓蝦屬|乙鼓蝦屬]]（{{lang|la|''Betaeus''}}）跟只在淡水洞穴發現的[[溪槍蝦屬|溪槍蝦屬]]（{{lang|la|''Potamalpheops''}}）。

在蝦群裡，槍蝦科物種的水下通信和聲納會互相干擾<ref name="BBC2">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/science/nature/935855.stm|title=Shrimp, bubble and pop|date=2000-09-21 |publisher=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] |accessdate=2011-07-02 |language=en }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2000/09/26/science/sleuths-solve-case-of-bubble-mistaken-for-a-snapping-shrimp.html|title=Sleuths solve case of bubble mistaken for a snapping shrimp |author=Kenneth Chang |date=2000-09-26 |publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |page=5 |accessdate=2011-07-02 |language=en }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F20E16F9355A107A93C0A91789D85F438485F9|title=Sea creatures trouble sonar operators – new enzyme |publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |accessdate=2011-07-02 |date=1947-02-02 |language=en }}</ref>。這些蝦群發出的聲音，是海洋中噪聲的主要來源<ref name="BBC2"/>。

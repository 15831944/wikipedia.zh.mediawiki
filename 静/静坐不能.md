'''静坐不能'''是一种运动障碍，主要表现为内心 烦躁不安和无法保持静止。<ref name="For2016">{{Cite journal|title=Antipsychotic-induced akathisia in delirium: A systematic review.|last=Forcen|first=FE|last2=Matsoukas|first2=K|date=February 2016|journal=Palliative & supportive care|issue=1|doi=10.1017/S1478951515000784|volume=14|pages=77-84|pmc=5516628|pmid=26087817|last3=Alici|first3=Y}}</ref><ref name="La2014">{{Cite journal|title=5-HT2A receptor antagonists for the treatment of neuroleptic-induced akathisia: a systematic review and meta-analysis.|last=Laoutidis|first=ZG|last2=Luckhaus|first2=C|date=May 2014|journal=The international journal of neuropsychopharmacology|issue=5|doi=10.1017/S1461145713001417|volume=17|pages=823-32|pmid=24286228}}</ref> 通常腿部受到最突出的影响。 静坐不能会使人坐立不安，来回摇摆，踱步。<ref name="Tho2015">{{Cite journal|title=The Incidence of Akathisia in the Treatment of Schizophrenia with Aripiprazole, Asenapine and Lurasidone: A Meta-Analysis.|last=Thomas|first=JE|last2=Caballero|first2=J|date=2015|journal=Current neuropharmacology|issue=5|doi=10.2174/1570159x13666150115220221|volume=13|pages=681–91|pmc=4761637|pmid=26467415|last3=Harrington|first3=CA}}</ref> 或可能只是感到不安。 并发症包括[[自殺|自杀]]。

'''如果我不在乎'''（{{lang-en|'''If I Didn't Care'''}}）是一首由{{link-en|傑克·勞倫斯|Jack Lawrence}}所寫的歌曲，在1939年由{{link-en|The Ink Spots|The Ink Spots}}及{{link-en|比爾·肯尼|Bill Kenny (singer)}}演唱和錄製。该歌曲在当时的畅销榜上位列第10名，在全球范围内销量超过一千万张<ref>{{cite web|url=http://songwritershalloffame.org/artists/C4055|title=Songwriters Friends: The Ink Spots|publisher=[[Songwriters_Hall_of_Fame|Songwriters Hall of Fame]]|accessdate=2010-11-13}}{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>[http://www.jacklawrencesongwriter.com/songs/if_i_didnt_care.html Jack Lawrence Songwriter website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120709104850/http://www.jacklawrencesongwriter.com/songs/if_i_didnt_care.html |date=2012-07-09 }}</ref>。

Dean Pentcheff, Shane T. Ahyong ''et al.'' |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s21/s21rbz1-109.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]]}}</ref>。這16個屬分別如下：

*† ''[[Calappilia|Calappilia]]'' <small>A. Milne-Edwards, 1873</small>
*''[[Calappula|Calappula]]'' <small>Galil, 1997</small>
*''[[Cryptosoma|Cryptosoma]]'' <small>Brullé, 1839</small>
*[[圆蟹属|圆蟹属]] ''Cycloes'' <small>De Haan, 1837</small>
*''[[Cyclozodion|Cyclozodion]]'' <small>Williams & Child, 1989</small>
*[[筐形蟹属|筐形蟹属]] ''Mursia'' <small>A. G. Desmarest, 1823</small>
*† ''[[Mursilata|Mursilata]]'' <small>C.-H. Hu & Tao, 1996</small>

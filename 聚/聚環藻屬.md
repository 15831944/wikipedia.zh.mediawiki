'''聚環藻屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Collenia'''''}}），又名'''聚環疊層石屬'''，是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的一[[屬|屬]][[藍藻|藍藻]]。它們在[[前寒武紀|前寒武紀]]晚期的[[熱帶|熱帶]][[海洋|海洋]]中十分普遍。<ref name="Rich1996">{{cite book |author=Rich, Thomas H. V. |author2=Fenton, Carroll Lane |author2-link=Carroll Lane Fenton |author3=Fenton, Mildred Adams |author3-link=Mildred Adams Fenton |author4=Rich, Pat Vickers |title=The fossil book: a record of prehistoric life |publisher=Dover Publications |location=New York |year=1996 |pages= |isbn=0-486-29371-8 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref><ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author={{link-en|西里爾·沃克 (古生物學家)|Cyril Walker (palaeontologist)|Walker, Cyril}} | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=286 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
聚環藻形成的[[疊層石|疊層石]]中具有[[錐體|錐體]]、[[圓柱體|圓柱體]]等菌落結構，由藻絲與[[海床|海床]]中的泥沙聚集構成。組成疊層石的細微藻絲可在[[顯微鏡|顯微鏡]]下看見。<ref name="Fossils" />

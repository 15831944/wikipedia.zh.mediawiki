'''苏胡克'''（{{lang|en|Zhug/Sahawiq}}）是一种[[也门|也门]]特产的[[辣酱|辣酱]]，由[[犹太人|犹太移民]]带入也门。苏胡克的主要配料包括：新鲜的红[[辣椒|辣椒]]或[[青椒|青椒]]，以及[[大蒜|大蒜]]、[[丁香|丁香]]、[[小豆蔻|小豆蔻]]、[[孜然|孜然]]等香料<ref name=Connell2017>{{cite book|author=約翰‧歐康奈（John O’Connell）|title=香料共和國：從洋茴香到鬱金，打開A-Z的味覺秘語: The Book of Spice: From Anise to Zedoa|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UcU-DwAAQBAJ&pg=PA375|date=2017-08-17|publisher=聯經出版事業公司|isbn=978-957-08-4985-1|page=375}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Goldstein|first1=Nili|title=PASSOVER: Yemenite Flavor at the Seder|url=https://jewishjournal.com/news/world/12805/|accessdate=23 April 2018|agency=Jewish Journal|publisher=Tribe Media|date=6 Apr 2006}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Kremezi|first1=Aglaia|title=Recipe: Zhug (Yemeni Hot Sauce)|url=https://www.theatlantic.com/health/archive/2010/06/recipe-zhug-yemeni-hot-sauce/58454/|accessdate=23 April 2018|publisher=The Atlantic|date=21 Jun 2010}}</ref>。传统的苏胡克由两块石头制作，一块大石头充当工作台，另一块小石头用于研磨。目前亦有使用研钵或食物加工机的做法<ref>[http://www.jannagur.com/108704/Zhug Janna Gur brings you the taste of Israel: Zhug]</ref>。苏胡克有三种类型：青苏胡克、烟熏苏胡克，以及红苏胡克<ref>[https://cookpad.com/sa/search/%D8%B3%D8%AD%D8%A7%D9%88%D9%82 Various Yemeni Sahawiq varieties]</ref>。苏胡克通常搭配[[炸豆丸子|炸豆丸子]]、[[沙威玛|沙威玛]]，以及[[皮塔饼|皮塔饼]]等食物食用<ref name=Connell2017/><ref>[http://humus101.com/EN/2011/01/22/red-skhug-a-recipe-and-a-story/ Red Skhug: A recipe and a story]</ref>。

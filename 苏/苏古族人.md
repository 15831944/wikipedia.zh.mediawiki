'''苏古族人'''（{{lang-en|Suku 族人}}），也称为 '''Basuku'''族人，是居住于[[刚果民主共和国|刚果民主共和国]]西南部以及[[安哥拉|安哥拉]]西北部的部族。人口约有200万名。<ref name="Encyclopedia2013">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ISAuAgAAQBAJ&pg=PT115|title=Encyclopedia of African Peoples|last=The Diagram Group|publisher=Routledge|year=2013|isbn=978-1-135-96334-7|page=194|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180712045348/https://books.google.com/books?id=ISAuAgAAQBAJ&pg=PT115|archivedate=2018-07-12|df=}}</ref><ref name="Britannica">{{Cite web|url=https://www.britannica.com/topic/Suku-people|title=Suku|accessdate=March 17, 2018|date=December 4, 2017|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180318055806/https://www.britannica.com/topic/Suku-people|archivedate=2018年3月18日|df=}}</ref>

==历史==
苏古族人于16世纪就居住于刚果民主共和国，当时约有80万人，主要以耕种为生。妇女多数栽种[[芋头|芋头]]等农作物，男士者前往深山采集野菜，打猎等。<ref name="African art museum. Tribal masks and statues of Africa.">{{cite web | title=index.html | website=African art museum. Tribal masks and statues of Africa. | url=http://www.zyama.com/suku/ | access-date=2018-07-04 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180705062722/http://www.zyama.com/suku/ | archivedate=2018-07-05 | df= }}</ref>

==语言==
苏古族人说的语言多数是苏古语。苏古语被列入[[尼日尔-刚果语系|尼日尔-刚果语系]]中的其中一种语言。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://glottolog.org/resource/languoid/id/suku1259|title=Glottolog 3.2 -Suku|website=glottolog.org|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304040857/http://glottolog.org/resource/languoid/id/suku1259|archivedate=2016-03-04|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ethnologue.com/18/language/sub/|title=Suku|publisher=ethnologue|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161201115928/http://www.ethnologue.com/18/language/sub/|archivedate=2016-12-01|df=}}</ref>

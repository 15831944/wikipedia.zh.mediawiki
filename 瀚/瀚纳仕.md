'''瀚纳仕'''（{{lang-en|'''Hays plc'''}}）是一家英国招聘和人力资源管理咨询公司。也是[[富时250指数|富时250指数]]成分股之一。

== 历史 ==
创建于1867年的[[泰晤士河|泰晤士河]]南岸，<ref name=find>[http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_gx5202/is_2007/ai_n19123721 International Directory of Company Histories]</ref>截止2018年，在全球34个国家和地区的262间办事处拥有11,700位员工。<ref>[http://www.haysgalleria.co.uk/ Hays Galleria] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20051024084351/http://www.haysgalleria.co.uk/ |date=24 October 2005 }}</ref> <ref name=Clelland>{{cite news|last=Clelland |first=Grant |title=FD Profile - Paul Venables: Hays puts its faith in global jobs market |url=http://www.efinancialnews.com/story/2007-10-01/fd-profile-paul-venables-hays-puts-its-faith-in-global-jobs-market |accessdate=19 October 2011 |newspaper=Financial News |date=1 October 2007 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/62Ydkbxgb?url=http://www.efinancialnews.com/story/2007-10-01/fd-profile-paul-venables-hays-puts-its-faith-in-global-jobs-market |archivedate=19 October 2011 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>

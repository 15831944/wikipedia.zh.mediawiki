'''斯蒂普島'''（{{lang-en|Steep Holm}}，{{lang-cy|Ynys Rhonech}}）是位於[[英國|英國]][[布里斯托灣|布里斯托灣]]的一個島嶼。斯蒂普島在高潮時面積{{convert|48.87|acre}}，低潮時面積{{convert|63.26|acre|abbr=on}}，島上最高點{{convert|78|m}}。在行政區劃上，斯蒂普島屬於北薩默塞特郡<ref>{{cite web | url= http://www.official-documents.gov.uk/document/cm70/7032/7032_iv.pdf | format= PDF | page= 7 | publisher= [[Boundary_Commissions_(United_Kingdom)|The Boundary Commission for England]] | title= Fifth periodical report – Volume 4 Mapping for the Non-Metropolitan Counties and the Unitary Authorities | date= 26 February 2007 | accessdate= 6 September 2007 | archive-url= https://www.webcitation.org/6I5t6P5t1?url=http://www.official-documents.gov.uk/document/cm70/7032/7032_iv.pdf | archive-date= 2013-07-14 | dead-url= yes }}</ref>。現在斯蒂普島無人居住，並且是一個重要的自然保護區。

'''斯韦勒·伦德·佩德森'''（{{lang-no|Sverre Lunde Pedersen}}，{{audio-IPA|SverreLundePedersen.ogg|/<sup>2</sup>sʋære <sup>2</sup>lʉnde <sup>1</sup>peːdersen/}}，{{bd|1992年|7月17日||}}）生于霍达兰郡卑尔根，是一名挪威男子速度滑冰运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/pe/sverre-lunde-pedersen-1.html|title=Sverre Lunde Pedersen|work=Sports Reference|accessdate=2019-01-30}}</ref>。斯韦勒的父亲是前速度滑冰选手[[Jarle_Pedersen|Jarle Pedersen]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bt.no/sport/vintersport/Paa-pallen-for-foerste-gang-979427.html |script-title=no:På pallen for første gang |date=2009-12-07|work={{lang|no|Bergens Tidende}}|accessdate=2010-02-13|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091213040147/http://www.bt.no/sport/vintersport/Paa-pallen-for-foerste-gang-979427.html|archivedate=2009-12-13}}</ref>，受其父亲以及哥哥的影响，他在1岁时便接触滑冰<ref name="pyeongchang2018">{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/speed-skating/athlete-profile-n3024289-sverre-lunde-pedersen.htm|title=Speed Skating {{!}} Athlete Profile: Sverre Lunde PEDERSEN|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-29|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180421155227/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/speed-skating/athlete-profile-n3024289-sverre-lunde-pedersen.htm|archivedate=2018-04-21}}</ref>。Jarle在Fana Idrettslag俱乐部执教期间曾担任斯韦勒的教练<ref name=nrk>{{cite news|url=http://www.nrk.no/nyheter/distrikt/troms_og_finnmark/1.6879834|script-title=no:Jarle som ny skøytetrener|last= Eilertsen|first=Kita|date=2009-11-24|publisher=NRK|accessdate=2009-11-24}}</ref>。

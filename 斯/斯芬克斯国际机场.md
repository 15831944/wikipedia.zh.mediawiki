'''斯芬克斯国际机场'''（{{airport codes|SPX|HESX}}）是埃及的一座民用机场，服务于[[开罗|开罗]]西侧城市[[吉萨|吉萨]]，机场毗邻{{tsl|en|Cairo West Air Base|开罗西空军基地}}并与该机场共享一些基础设施。其位于{{tsl|en|Cairo-Alexandria desert road|开罗-亚历山大沙漠公路}}上<ref name="Finalizing">{{cite news |title=Egypt’s Aviation Ministry finalizes preparations to inaugurate Sphinx Airport |url=https://egyptindependent.com/egypts-aviation-ministry-finalizes-preparations-to-inaugurate-sphinx-airport/ |accessdate=2018-10-15 |work=Egypt Independent |date=2018-10-10}}</ref>。

主跑道方向16R/34L，长度为{{convert|2|km}}，位于空军基地跑道以西，舷梯及一些设施位于跑道的西侧<ref name="gm" />。

空军基地的[[戰術空中導航系統|戰術空中導航系統]]位于空地上，{{tsl|en|VOR-DME||VOR-DME}}系统位于机场以东{{convert|25.6|nmi|km|0}}<ref>{{cite web |title=Cairo VOR (CVO) |url=http://ourairports.com/navaids/CVO/Cairo_VOR-DME_EG/ |website=Our Airports |accessdate=2018-11-24}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ourairports.com/navaids/BLA/Cairo_West_TACAN_EG/#lat=30.11549949645996,lon=30.911699295043945,zoom=10,type=Satellite|title=Cairo West TACAN (BLA) @ OurAirports|website=ourairports.com|access-date=2018-08-20}}</ref>。

2017年10月，宣布机场将在次年夏季开通商业航班，并于2018年10月开始试运营<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.egyptindependent.com/inaugural-flights-land-at-sphinx-international-airport-in-giza-next-summer/|title=Inaugural flights land at Sphinx International Airport in Giza next summer - Egypt Independent|date=2017-10-29|work=Egypt Independent|access-date=2018-08-20|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.egyptindependent.com/sphinx-international-airport-to-begin-experimental-operation-in-january/|title=Sphinx International Airport to begin 'experimental operation' in January - Egypt Independent|date=2017-12-21|work=Egypt Independent|access-date=2018-08-20|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ch-aviation.com/portal/news/62671-cairo-sphinx-egypt-to-open-for-tests-in-1q18|title=Cairo Sphinx, Egypt to open for tests in 1Q18|work=ch-aviation|access-date=2018-08-20|language=en-US}}</ref>。

2019年1月26日，[[埃及快運航空|埃及快運航空]]推出飞往[[古尔代盖国际机场|古尔代盖]]、[[沙姆沙伊赫国际机场|沙姆沙伊赫]]、[[卢克索国际机场|卢克索]]以及{{tsl|en|Aswan International Airport|阿斯旺国际机场|阿斯旺}}的定期航班<ref name="Launch">{{Cite news|url=http://www.egypttoday.com/Article/9/63886/Sphinx-International-Airport-to-operate-its-first-internal-flight|title=Sphinx International Airport to operate its first internal flight - Egypt Today|date=2019-01-26|work=Egypt Today|access-date=2019-01-28|language=en-US}}</ref>。

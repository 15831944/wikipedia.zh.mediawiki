'''斯威夫特坑'''是[[火星|火星]]的[[天然衛星|天然衛星]][[火衛二|火衛二]]上的[[撞擊坑|撞擊坑]]，它的直徑大約{{convert|3|km}}<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.uapress.arizona.edu/onlinebks/mars/chap14.htm | title = Chapter 14: The Hurtling Moons of Mars | work = The University of Arizona | accessdate = 2007-10-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://planetarynames.wr.usgs.gov/jsp/FeatureTypesData2.jsp?systemID=4&bodyID=21&typeID=9&system=Mars&body=Deimos&type=Crater,%20craters&sort=AName&show=Fname&show=Lat&show=Long&show=Diam&show=Stat&show=Orig | title = Gazetteer of Planetary Nomenclature}}</ref>。斯威夫特坑是以[[喬納森·斯威夫特|喬納森·斯威夫特]]之名命名的，他預測到[[火星的衛星|火星有衛星]]的存在<ref>[http://www.mathpages.com/home/kmath151/kmath151.htm MathPages - Galileo's Anagrams and the Moons of Mars].</ref>。斯威夫特坑是火衛上已命名的兩個地型特徵之一，另一個是[[伏爾泰_(火衛二隕石坑)|伏爾泰坑]]。在2006年7月10日，[[火星全球探勘者號|火星全球探勘者號]]從{{convert|22985|km}}的距離上拍攝了[[火衛二|火衛二]]的影像，可以看見斯威夫特坑<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.msss.com/mars_images/moc/2006/08/11/ | title = Deimos! | work = Malin Space Science Systems | accessdate = 2007-10-27}}</ref>。

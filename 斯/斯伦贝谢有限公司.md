'''斯伦贝谢有限公司'''（{{lang-en|Schlumberger Limited}}，简称'''斯伦贝谢'''）是世界上最大的油田服务公司<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bloomberg.com/news/2012-01-20/schlumberger-fourth-quarter-earnings-post-increase-as-oil-drilling-booms.html|title=Bloomberg|date=2012-01-20}}</ref>。斯伦贝谢员工约为126,000人<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=301966|title=Schlumberger Limited: Private Company Information|website=Bloomberg|accessdate=2018-01-17}}</ref>，他们来自超过140个国家，在超过85个国家工作<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.slb.com/about/who.aspx|title= Schlumberger Website }}</ref>。主要的办公室在[[休斯顿|休斯顿]]、[[巴黎|巴黎]]、[[伦敦|伦敦]]和[[海牙|海牙]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://images.businessweek.com/ss/09/11/1116_best_business_undergrad_employers/51.htm|title=Bloomberg Business Week|accessdate=2010-07-14}}</ref>。

'''斯米玳灰蝶'''（學名：''Deudorix smilis''）是屬於[[線灰蝶亞科|線灰蝶亞科]][[玳灰蝶族|玳灰蝶族]]的一種[[蝴蝶|蝴蝶]]，是玳灰蝶屬的一種。共有6個亞種。分佈於亞洲[[中南半島|中南半島]]至[[澳洲|澳洲]]及[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]]一帶，模式產地為[[印度東部|印度東部]]<ref>Hewitson, 1863. Illustrations of diurnal Lepidoptera. Lycaenidae Ill. diurn. Lep. Lycaenidae (1): [https://archive.org/stream/illustrationsofd01hewi#page/n63/mode/1up 18], pl. [https://archive.org/stream/illustrationsofd02hewi#page/n48/mode/1up 8], f. 22-23</ref>。2018於香港亦發現有分佈<ref>[https://www.hk01.com/%E7%A4%BE%E6%9C%83%E6%96%B0%E8%81%9E/257693/%E6%96%B0%E8%9D%B4%E8%9D%B6%E5%93%81%E7%A8%AE%E6%96%AF%E7%B1%B3%E7%8E%B3%E7%81%B0%E8%9D%B6%E9%80%A3%E7%BA%8C%E5%85%A9%E5%B9%B4%E7%8F%BE%E8%BA%AB%E9%A6%99%E6%B8%AF-%E7%92%B0%E5%9C%98-%E5%8F%AF%E8%83%BD%E5%B7%B2%E5%AE%9A%E5%B1%85 新蝴蝶品種斯米玳灰蝶連續兩年現身香港，環團：可能已定居] - 香港01</ref>。幼蟲的寄主為[[馬錢科|馬錢科]][[馬錢屬|馬錢屬]]之亮葉馬錢（''Strychnos lucida''）<ref>K. L. & L. E. Dunn, 1991 Review of Australian Butterflies: distribution, life history and taxonomy. Pushlished by authors, Melbourne Review Austr. Butts. (1-4): (120-140)</ref>。

== 亞種 ==
* ''D. s. '''smilis'''''
:- '''指名亞種'''，分佈於印度[[阿薩姆邦|阿薩姆邦]]及[[安達曼-尼科巴群島|安達曼-尼科巴群島]]，[[馬來半島|馬來半島]]之[[緬甸|緬甸]]，[[老撾|老撾]]，[[越南|越南]]及[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]東部等地<ref name = f>Fruhstorfer, 1912. Uebersicht der Lycaeniden des Indo-Australischen Gebiets. Begründet auf die Ausbeute und die Sammlung des Autors Berl. ent. Zs. 56 (3/4) : [https://archive.org/stream/berlinerentomolo561911berl#page/267/mode/1up 267] - [https://archive.org/stream/berlinerentomolo561911berl#page/268/mode/1up 268]</ref>
* ''D. s. '''silo''''' <small>Fruhstorfer, 1909</small>
:- 分佈於[[婆羅洲|婆羅洲]]<ref name = f/>
* ''D. s. '''vocetius''''' <small>Fruhstorfer, 1912</small>
:- 分佈於[[巴拉望島|巴拉望島]]等[[菲律賓|菲律賓]]諸島<ref name = f/>
* ''D. s. '''nicevillei''''' <small>(Tytler, 1926)</small>
:- 分佈於印度安達曼-尼科巴群島<ref>Tytler, 1926. Notes on some new and interesting Butterflies from India and Burma J. Bombay nat. Hist. Soc. 31 : (3): [https://archive.org/stream/journalof313419261927bomb#page/589/mode/1up 589]</ref>
* ''D. s. '''sylvia''''' <small>(D'Abrera, 1971)</small>
:- 分佈於印尼[[奧比群島|奧比群島]]及[[巴占群島|巴占群島]]<ref>D'Abrera, 1971. Butterflies of the Australian Region (1st edn) Butts. Aust. Region: 304</ref>
* ''D. s. '''dalyensis''''' <small>(Le Souff & Tindale, 1970)</small>
:- 分佈於[[澳洲|澳洲]]{{link-en|戴利河|Daly River, Northern Territory}}一帶<ref>Le Souff & Tindale, 1970 A new subspecies of Virachola smilis (Hewitson) (Lepidoptera: Lycaenidae) from northern Australia [http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1440-6055.1970.tb00794.x/epdf J.

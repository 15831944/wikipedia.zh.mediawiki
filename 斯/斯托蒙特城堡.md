'''斯托蒙特城堡'''（Stormont Castle）是[[英國|英國]][[北愛爾蘭|北愛爾蘭]]的一處莊園建築，位於[[貝爾法斯特|貝爾法斯特]]東郊。該建築雖然名為城堡，但在歷史上卻從來不是一座城堡。在1921年至1972年期間，這裡曾是北愛爾蘭首相的官邸。斯托蒙特城堡在每年的歐洲遺產公開日週末對公眾開放<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.northernireland.gov.uk/index/history-and-tour.htm | title = Stormont Castle History and tour | publisher = [[Northern_Ireland_Executive|Northern Ireland Executive]] | access-date = 2016-03-02 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110424094306/http://www.northernireland.gov.uk/index/history-and-tour.htm | archivedate = 2011-04-24 | df =  }}</ref>。在2016年是9月10日及11日<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.discovernorthernireland.com/niea/ehod.aspx | title = European Heritage Open Days | publisher = [[Northern_Ireland_Tourist_Board|Discover Northern Ireland]] | access-date = 2016-03-02 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160323192342/http://www.discovernorthernireland.com/NIEA/EHOD.aspx | archivedate = 2016-03-23 }}</ref>。

'''斯洛博丹·普拉利亚克'''（{{Lang-hr|Slobodan Praljak}}，{{bd|1945年|1月2日|2017年|11月29日|}}）是[[波斯尼亚和黑塞哥维那|波斯尼亚和黑塞哥维那]]的一位[[波士尼亞與赫塞哥維納的克羅埃西亞人|克罗地亚裔]]工程师、电视导演和商人。1992年至1995年期间，他是{{link-en|克罗地亚陆军|Croatian Army}}将领，也是[[黑塞哥-波斯尼亚克罗地亚共和国|黑塞哥-波斯尼亚克罗地亚共和国]]的“{{link-en|克罗地亚国防委员会|Croatian Defence Council}}”的一位将军。

2004年，普拉利亚克受到[[前南斯拉夫问题国际刑事法庭|前南斯拉夫问题国际刑事法庭]]的起诉，并自发地向法庭投降。<ref name="TrialIn">{{cite web|url=https://trialinternational.org/latest-post/slobodan-praljak/|title=Slobodan Praljak|date=6 May 2016|publisher=Trial International|accessdate=30 November 2017}}</ref>2013年，他与另外五名波黑的克罗地亚裔官员一起，被认定为在[[波黑内战|波黑内战]]中对[[波什尼亚克人|波什尼亚克人]]犯下战争罪，判处20年监禁。<ref name="ICTY">{{cite web|url=http://www.icty.org/x/cases/prlic/ind/en/prl-ii040304e.htm|title=Prlic et al. Initial Indictment|date=2 March 2004|publisher=ICTY}}</ref><ref name="LAT">{{cite news|url=http://beta.latimes.com/world/la-fg-croatia-slobadan-praljak-20171129-story.html|title='I am not a war criminal,' convicted Bosnian Croat cries as he takes a fatal dose of poison|date=29 November 2017|work=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=29 November 2017|quote=Ironically, Praljak, who surrendered to the tribunal in April 2004 and had already been jailed for 13 years, could have soon walked free because those who are convicted are generally released after serving two-thirds of their sentences.}}</ref>他在2017年年初发起上诉。2017年11月维持原判，普拉利亚克当庭抗议并服毒自尽。<ref>{{cite news|title=War crimes suspect 'takes poison' in court|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-42163613|date=29 November 2017|publisher=BBC|accessdate=29 November 2017}}</ref>数个小时后抢救无效死亡。<ref>{{cite news|author=Alan McGuinness|title=War criminal Slobodan Praljak dies after taking poison in court|url=https://news.sky.com/story/war-criminal-slobodan-praljak-dies-after-taking-poison-in-court-11148960|date=29 November 2017|publisher=Sky News|accessdate=29 November 2017}}</ref><ref name="Guardian">{{cite news|author=Owen Bowcott|title=Bosnian Croat war criminal dies after taking poison in UN courtroom|url=https://www.theguardian.com/law/2017/nov/29/un-war-crimes-defendant-claims-to-drink-poison-at-trial-in-hague-slobodan-praljak|date=29 November 2017|work=The Guardian|accessdate=29 November 2017}}</ref><ref name="Reuters">{{cite news|author1=Stephanie van den Berg|author2=Bart H.

'''丽莎·维托齐'''（{{lang-it|Lisa Vittozzi}}，{{bd|1995年|2月4日||}}）生于贝卢诺省皮耶韦迪卡多雷，是一名意大利女子冬季两项运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://corrieredelveneto.corriere.it/belluno/sport/18_febbraio_20/olimpiadi-invernali-2018-biathlon-vittozzi-bronzo-staffetta-mista-85cd96e0-163f-11e8-8b0f-cb4ce62f294b.shtml|script-title=it:Olimpiadi invernali 2018: biathlon, Vittozzi bronzo nella staffetta mista|publisher={{lang|it|Corriere Del Vento}}|date=2018-02-20|accessdate=2019-01-19}}</ref>。她在同位于贝卢诺省的[[萨帕达|萨帕达]]成长<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.oasport.it/2018/02/biathlon-lisa-vittozzi-simbolo-di-sappada-e-delle-alpi-carniche-una-predestinata-della-carabina/|script-title=it:Biathlon, Lisa Vittozzi: simbolo di Sappada e delle Alpi Carniche, una predestinata della carabina|publisher={{lang|it|OA Sport}}|date=2018-02-20|accessdate=2019-01-19}}</ref>。2007年，她开始练习冬季两项<ref name="pyeongchang2018">{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/biathlon/athlete-profile-n3028851-lisa-vittozzi.htm|title=Biathlon {{!}} Athlete Profile: Lisa VITTOZZI|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-18|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180228030403/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/biathlon/athlete-profile-n3028851-lisa-vittozzi.htm|archivedate=2018-02-28}}</ref>。2014年，她在[[瑞典|瑞典]][[厄斯特松德|厄斯特松德]]完成了个人的[[冬季两项世界杯|世界杯分站赛]]首秀<ref name="pyeongchang2018"/>。

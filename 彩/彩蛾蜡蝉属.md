'''彩蛾蜡蝉属'''（學名：''{{lang|la|Cerynia}}''）是{{tsl|sv|Flatidae|蛾蜡蝉科}}（又名[[青翅飛蝨科|青翅飛蝨科]]）一個[[半翅目|半翅目]][[昆蟲|昆蟲]]的[[属|属]]{{r|COL|Wang et Peng 2007}}，也是蛾蜡蝉科的模式属{{r|HD}}，由Stål於1862年建立{{r|Wang et Peng 2007}}。本屬的[[模式種|模式種]]是''[[Flata_albata|Flata albata]]'' {{small|Stål, 1862}}，[[模式產地|模式產地]]是當時的[[英屬馬來亞|英屬馬來亞]]{{r|Wang et Peng 2007}}。

==物種==
{{as of|2019|2|18}}，[[半翅目數據庫|半翅目數據庫]]紀錄本屬有十個物種及五個亞種，分別如下{{r|HD}}：
* ''[[Cerynia_albata|Cerynia albata]]'' {{small|(Stål, 1854)}}：模式種。
** ''[[Cerynia_albata_incurva|Cerynia albata incurva]]'' {{small|(Melichar, 1901)}}
* ''[[Cerynia_bilineata|Cerynia bilineata]]'' {{small|Ossiannilsson, 1940}}
* [[指突彩蛾蜡蝉|指突彩蛾蜡蝉]] ''[[Cerynia_digitula|Cerynia digitula]]'' {{small|Wang et Peng, 2007}}{{r|Wang et Peng 2007}}
* ''[[Cerynia_fulgida|Cerynia fulgida]]'' {{small|Melichar, 1901}}
* [[线彩蛾蜡蝉|线彩蛾蜡蝉]] ''[[Cerynia_lineola|Cerynia lineola]]'' {{small|Melichar, 1901}}{{r|Wang et Peng 2007}}
* [[彩蛾蜡蝉|彩蛾蜡蝉]] ''[[Cerynia_maria|Cerynia maria]]'' {{small|(White, 1846)}}{{r|Wang et Peng 2007}}
** ''[[Cerynia_maria_lutescens|Cerynia maria lutescens]]'' {{small|Melichar, 1901}}
** ''[[Cerynia_maria_rosea|Cerynia maria rosea]]'' {{small|Atkinson, 1886}}
** ''[[Cerynia_maria_tenella|Cerynia maria tenella]]'' {{small|(Walker, 1851)}}
** [[Cerynia_maria_completa|[''Cerynia maria'' var. ''completa'']] {{small|(Walker, 1851)}}
* ''[[Cerynia_mixana|Cerynia mixana]]'' {{small|Medler, 1996}}
* ''[[Cerynia_monacha|Cerynia monacha]]'' {{small|(Gerstaecker, 1895)}}
* [[梅花彩蛾蜡蝉|梅花彩蛾蜡蝉]] ''[[Cerynia_parnassioides|Cerynia parnassioides]]'' {{small|China, 1925}}{{r|Wang et Peng 2007}}
* ''[[Cerynia_trilineata|Cerynia trilineata]]'' {{small|Melichar, 1901}}

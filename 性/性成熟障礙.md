'''性成熟障礙'''（{{lang-en|Sexual maturation disorder}}）是一種[[焦慮症|焦慮症]]或[[抑鬱症|抑鬱症]]，與一個人的[[性別認同|性別認同]]或[[性取向|性取向]]的不確定性有關。[[世界衛生組織|世界衛生組織]]在「與性發育和性取向相關的心理和行為障礙（{{lang-en|Psychological and behavioural disorders associated with sexual development and orientation}}）」下列出了[[國際疾病與相關健康問題統計分類|國際疾病與相關健康問題統計分類]]（ICD-10）中的性成熟障礙。<ref name=ICD>{{ICD10|F|66|0|f|60}}</ref>

[[性取向|性取向]]本身並不是一種疾病，不屬於本條目討論的內容。<ref>[http://www.who.int/classifications/apps/icd/icd10online/?gf60.htm+f661 ICD-10]: See part F66.</ref>它不同於[[自我矛盾的性取向|自我矛盾的性取向]]（[[性取向|性取向]]或[[性別認同|性別認同]]被壓抑或否定）。<ref>[http://aerzteblatt.lnsdata.de/pdf/DI/105/48/m834.pdf Gender Identity Disorders in Childhood and Adolescence]  {{webarchive|url=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20091008003518/http://aerzteblatt.lnsdata.de/pdf/DI/105/48/m834.pdf |date=2009-10-08 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | pmc = 2697020 | pmid=19578420 | doi=10.3238/arztebl.2008.0834 | volume=105 | title=Gender identity disorders in childhood and adolescence: currently debated concepts and treatment strategies | year=2008 | journal=Dtsch Arztebl Int | pages=834–41 |vauthors=Korte A, Lehmkuhl U, Goecker D, Beier KM, Krude H, Grüters-Kieslich A }}</ref><ref>American Psychiatric Association (APA) Diagnostic and statistical manual of mental disorders. 4th edn.

'''性別分化障礙'''（英語: Disorders of sex development, DSDs）是涉及生殖系统的医学症狀。<ref>{{cite journal|last=Diamond|first=Milton|author2=Hazel G. Beh|authorlink1= Milton Diamond |title=Changes in the management of children with intersex conditions|journal=Nat Clin Pract Endocrinol Metab|date=January 2008|volume=4|issue=1|pages=4–5|doi=10.1038/ncpendmet0694|pmid=17984980|url=http://www.hawaii.edu/PCSS/biblio/articles/2005to2009/2008-changes-in-management.html|accessdate=18 September 2012}}</ref>更具体地说，这些术语是指“染色体，性腺不符合男性或女性身體的典型二元概念的先天性症狀”。<ref name="lee">{{cite journal | author = Lee P. A., Houk C. P., Ahmed S. F., Hughes I. A. | year = 2006 | title = Consensus statement on management of intersex disorders | url =http://pediatrics.aappublications.org/cgi/reprint/118/2/e488 | journal = Pediatrics | volume = 118 | issue = 2| pages = e488–500 | doi=10.1542/peds.2006-0738| pmid = 16882788 | last2 = Houk | last3 = Ahmed | last4 = Hughes}}</ref>

该术语一直存在争议，研究表明，受影响的人士会受到负面影响，术语会影响医疗服务提供者的醫療行為。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Attitudes towards “disorders of sex development” nomenclature among affected individuals|url=https://www.jpurol.com/article/S1477-5131(17)30183-3/fulltext|last=Johnson|first=Emilie K.|last2=Rosoklija|first2=Ilina|date=2017-12|journal=Journal of Pediatric Urology|issue=6|doi=10.1016/j.jpurol.2017.03.035|volume=13|pages=608.e1–608.e8|language=en|issn=1477-5131|last3=Finlayson|first3=Courtney|last4=Chen|first4=Diane|last5=Yerkes|first5=Elizabeth B.|last6=Madonna|first6=Mary Beth|last7=Holl|first7=Jane L.|last8=Baratz|first8=Arlene B.|last9=Davis|first9=Georgiann}}</ref>[[世界卫生组织|世界卫生组织]]和许多医学期刊仍将DSD列为[[双性人|双性人]]的特征或条件。<ref name="bmj.g2926">{{cite journal | author = [[Rebecca_Jordan-Young|Rebecca Jordan-Young]], Peter Sönksen, and [[Katrina_Karkazis|Katrina Karkazis]] | year = 2014 | title = Sex, health, and athletes | url = http://www.bmj.com/content/348/bmj.g2926 | journal = [[British_Medical_Journal|British Medical Journal]] | volume = 348 | issue = | page = g2926 | doi = 10.1136/bmj.g2926 | pmid=24776640}}</ref>[[歐洲理事會|歐洲理事會]]和美洲人权委员会呼吁对医疗分类进行审查，以免对[[双性人|双性人]]特征进行不必要的医学治疗。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://rm.coe.int/16806da5d4|title=Human rightsand intersex people|accessdate=|author=Council of Europe; Commissioner for Human Rights|date=|publisher=}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oas.org/es/cidh/informes/pdfs/ViolenciaPersonasLGBTI.pdf|title=ViolenciaPersonasLGBTI|accessdate=|author=Comisión Interamericana de Derechos Humanos|date=|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://fra.europa.eu/sites/default/files/fra-2015-focus-04-intersex.pdf|title=The fundamental rights situation of intersex people|accessdate=|author=European Union Agency for Fundamental Rights|date=|publisher=}}</ref>

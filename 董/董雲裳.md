'''董雲裳'''（{{lang-en|Susan Thornton}}），[[美國|美國]][[外交官|外交官]]，2017年起代理[[美國國務院|美國國務院]][[美國東亞暨太平洋事務助理國務卿|東亞暨太平洋事務助理國務卿]]（亞太助卿）一職<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.state.gov/p/eap/index.htm |title=Bureau of East Asian and Pacific Affairs |work=[[United_States_Department_of_State|United States Department of State]] |accessdate=2017-08-19 |archiveurl=https://archive.today/20180321062817/https://www.state.gov/p/eap/index.htm |archivedate=2018-03-21 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Panda |first1=Ankit |url=http://thediplomat.com/2017/03/straight-from-the-us-state-department-the-pivot-to-asia-is-over/ |title=Straight From the US State Department: The 'Pivot' to Asia Is Over |work=[[外交家雜誌|The Diplomat]] |date=2017-03-14 |accessdate=2017-08-19 |archiveurl=https://archive.today/20170326114841/http://thediplomat.com/2017/03/straight-from-the-us-state-department-the-pivot-to-asia-is-over/ |archivedate=2017-03-26 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。

==略歷==
董雲裳進入國務院工作前，曾於[[約翰·霍普金斯大學|約翰·霍普金斯大學]]{{Link-en|保羅·H·尼采高級國際研究學院|Paul H. Nitze School of Advanced International Studies}}的{{Link-en|外交政策研究所 (尼采高級國際研究學院)|Foreign Policy Institute (SAIS)|外交政策研究所}}，並曾於該所研究{{Link-en|蘇聯政治|Politics of the Soviet Union}}、[[俄語|當代俄語]]，能口說俄語和[[漢語|漢語]]，並被視為國務院內首要的[[漢學家|漢學家]]之一<ref>{{Cite news |last=Morello |first=Carol |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/national-security/tillerson-scores-a-personnel-win-as-top-east-asia-adviser-is-nominated/2017/12/24/fd1bd20a-e743-11e7-a65d-1ac0fd7f097e_story.html?undefined=&wpisrc=nl_headlines&wpmm=1 |title=Tillerson scores a personnel win, as top East Asia adviser is nominated |publisher=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]] |date=2017-12-24 |archiveurl=https://archive.today/20180322025124/https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/national-security/tillerson-scores-a-personnel-win-as-top-east-asia-adviser-is-nominated/2017/12/24/fd1bd20a-e743-11e7-a65d-1ac0fd7f097e_story.html?undefined=&utm_term=.19f445609e1d&wpisrc=nl_headlines&wpmm=1 |archivedate=2018-03-22 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。身為1991年起入院工作的{{Link-en|外事官|Foreign Service Officer|職業外交官}}，歷任東亞事務首席副助卿（2016年2月起）、美國駐[[土庫曼|土庫曼]]大使館[[使領館副館長|副館長]]、國務院[[中國|中國]]暨[[蒙古|蒙古]]事務處副處長、[[韓國|韓國]]事務處經濟組長等職，曾駐外館包括[[美國駐華大使館|駐華大使館]]、[[美國駐成都總領事館|駐成都總領事館]]、{{Link-en|美國駐亞美尼亞大使館|Embassy of the United States, Yerevan|駐亞美尼亞大使館}}、駐[[哈薩克|哈薩克]]大使館<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.state.gov/r/pa/ei/biog/bureau/229824.htm |title=Susan Thornton |work=[[United_States_Department_of_State|United States Department of State]] |accessdate=2017-08-19 |archiveurl=https://archive.today/20170817050310/https://www.state.gov/r/pa/ei/biog/bureau/229824.htm |archivedate=2017-08-17 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。

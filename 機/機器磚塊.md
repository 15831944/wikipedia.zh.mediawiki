{{vgname|機器磚塊|en=Roblox}}是一款由Roblox公司開發的[[大型多人線上遊戲|大型多人線上]]{{le|遊戲創建系統|Game creation system|遊戲創建平台}}，該平台允许用戶設計自己的遊戲，以及遊玩自己和其他開發者創建的各種不同類型的遊戲。与《[[我的世界|我的世界]]》相比，《機器磚塊》是一個網站和應用程序，主要是由[[樂高|樂高]]般的虛擬磚塊所構建的[[社群網路遊戲|社群網路]]虛擬世界遊戲<ref name="cnet">{{cite news|url=http://news.cnet.com/8301-19882_3-20070969-250/roblox-a-virtual-world-of-Lego-like-blocks/ |title=Roblox: A virtual world of Lego-like blocks |publisher=[[CNET|CNET]] |accessdate=2012-02-29|date = 2011-06-14|last = Needleman|first = Rafe}}</ref>。

創始人兼[[首席執行長|首席執行長]]大衛·巴斯佐茲基於2004年開始以《DynaBlocks》的名義進行初步的樣本測試<ref name="Hughes_Inc">{{cite news|last1=Neil C.|first1=Hughes|title=How This User-Generated Video Game Is Leading The Way With Innovation and VR|url=http://www.inc.com/neil-c-hughes/how-this-user-generated-video-game-is-leading-the-way-with-innovation-and-vr.html|agency=Inc Magazine|date=2016-07-15}}</ref>。2005年，它更名為《機器磚塊》，運行平台為[[個人電腦|個人電腦]]<ref name="teen" />。該平台於2006年由Roblox公司正式發布<ref name="DynaBlocks">{{cite web|last1=Fennimore|first1=Jack|title=Roblox: 5 Fast Facts You Need to Know|url=http://heavy.com/games/2017/07/roblox-youtube-free-download-corporation-baszucki-cassel-nerfmodder/|website=Heavy|accessdate=2017-07-30|date=2017-07-12}}</ref>。截至2016年，《機器磚塊》每月有著約3000萬名的活躍用戶<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.mercurynews.com/2016/10/20/kid-gaming-company-roblox-builds-a-following/|title = Roblox: Is unusual virtual playground the next Minecraft?|date = 2016-10-20|publisher = The Mercury News|last = Wolverton|first = Troy}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://venturebeat.com/2016/12/20/at-10-roblox-surpasses-30-million-monthly-users-and-300-million-hours-of-engagement/|title = At 10, Roblox surpasses 30 million monthly users and 300 million hours of engagement|date = 2016-12-20|publisher = Venture Beat|last = Takahashi|first = Dean}}</ref>。

== 反響 ==

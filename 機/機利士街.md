'''機利士街'''（英語：Gilles Street）是一條位於[[南澳州|南澳州]][[阿德萊德|阿德萊德]][[阿德萊德市中心|市中心]]東南部的街道。<ref name="ubd">{{cite book|title=2003 Adelaide Street Directory, 41st Edition|publisher=UBD (A Division of Universal Press Pty Ltd)|year=2003|isbn=0-7319-1441-4}}</ref><ref name="map">[http://www.soulunderground.com.au/national_files/CBDMAP.pdf Map] of the [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adelaide Adelaide] CBD, [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Adelaide North Adelaide] and the [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adelaide_Parklands Adelaide Parklands].</ref> 道路呈東西走向，東接[[東邊臺|東邊臺]]、穿過[[克街|克街]]和[[保定尼街|保定尼街]]，西止於[[英皇威廉街|英皇威廉街]]。該街以[[南澳州庫務司|前庫務司]][[機利士|機利士]]（Osmond Gilles）命名。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://gillesstps.sa.edu.au/02_AboutUs03_History.htm|title=Gilles Street Primary School - History|accessdate=2009-04-24|date=2008-02-25}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.historysouthaustralia.net/STlist2.htm#gill|title=History of Adelaide Through Street Names - Streets Named on the 23rd May, 1837|accessdate=2009-04-24|date=2005-03-04}}</ref>機利士街是阿德萊德市中心內最窄的街道，闊度僅為1[[鏈_(量度單位)|鏈]]。

== 沿路建築物 ==
[[File:The_former_Beresford_and_Oddfellows_Inn,_Gilles_Street,_Adelaide.jpg|右]]
位於機利士街的 Beresford and Oddfellows 酒店，是一座法定歷史建築。在1840年1861年間，為 Beresford Arms（後為 Oddfellows Arms）。直至1873年，轉為一間德國印刷廠。<ref>[http://www.environment.gov.au/cgi-bin/ahdb/search.pl?mode=place_detail;search=street_name%3Dgilles%3Bstate%3DSA%3Bkeyword_PD%3Don%3Bkeyword_SS%3Don%3Bkeyword_PH%3Don%3Blatitude_1dir%3DS%3Blongitude_1dir%3DE%3Blongitude_2dir%3DE%3Blatitude_2dir%3DS%3Bin_region%3Dpart;place_id=14445 Department of Sustainability, Environment, Water, Population and Communities: Australian Heritage Database]</ref>

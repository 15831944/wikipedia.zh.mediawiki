'''綦江龍屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Qijianglong''}}）是[[馬門溪龍科|馬門溪龍類]]恐龍的一屬，化石發現於[[中國|中國]][[重慶|重慶]]的[[重慶綦江木化石-恐龍足跡國家地質公園|重慶綦江木化石-恐龍足跡國家地質公園]]，生活於[[侏羅紀|侏羅紀]]晚期（於[[遂寧組|遂寧組]]發現）<ref name=Xingtal2015>{{en}}{{ cite journal | authors = Xing, L; Miyashita, T; Zhang, J; Li, Daqing; Ye, Y; Sekiya, T.; Wang F; Currie, P | year = 2015 | title = "A new sauropod dinosaur from the Late Jurassic of China and the diversity, distribution, and relationships of mamenchisaurids" | journal = Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology | volume = 35 | issue = 1 | pages = 17–26 | doi = 10.1080/02724634.2014.889701 | issn = 1937-2809 }}</ref>。

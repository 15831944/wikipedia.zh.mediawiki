'''慢性疼痛'''（{{lang-en|Chronic Pain}}）指的是持续时间较长的[[疼痛|疼痛]]症状。在医学领域，急性疼痛和慢性疼痛一般是由持续时间划分，最常见的是用“持续3个月”或者“持续6个月”作为两种疼痛的分水岭，<ref name=Turk1>{{cite book |last1= Turk|first1= D.C. |last2= Okifuji |first2= A.|editor1-first= D.|editor1-last= Loeser |editor2-first= S. H. |editor2-last= Butler|editor3-first= J.J. |editor3-last= Chapman|editor4-first= D. C. |editor4-last= Turk |title= Bonica's Management of Pain |edition= 3rd|year= 2001|publisher= Lippincott Williams & Wilkins |isbn= 0-683-30462-3 |pages= 18–25 |chapter= Pain terms and taxonomies |chapterurl= https://books.google.com/books?id=TyNEicOiJqQC&pg=RA1-PA18&dq=Pain+terms+and+taxonomies&cd=1#v=onepage&q=Pain%20terms%20and%20taxonomies&f=false}}</ref>但也有学者用“持续12个月”来区分。<ref>{{cite book |last1= Main|first1= C.J. |last2= Spanswick |first2= C.C. |title= Pain management: an interdisciplinary approach |year= 2001|publisher= Elsevier |isbn= 0-443-05683-8 |page= 93 |url= https://books.google.com/?id=wcEQPzTOEAoC&pg=PA93&dq=chronic+acute+subacute+pain&cd=48#v=onepage&q=chronic%20acute%20subacute%20pain}}</ref>

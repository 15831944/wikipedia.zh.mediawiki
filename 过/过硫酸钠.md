'''过硫酸钠'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Na<sub>2</sub>S<sub>2</sub>O<sub>8</sub>。它是[[过二硫酸|过二硫酸]]的钠盐，是一种强[[氧化剂|氧化剂]]。它几乎不潮解，可以长时间储存。

==制备==
过硫酸钠可以通过[[硫酸氢钠|硫酸氢钠]]的电解氧化制得：
:2 NaHSO<sub>4</sub>   →    Na<sub>2</sub>S<sub>2</sub>O<sub>8</sub>  +  H<sub>2</sub>
氧化在铂阳极发生。<ref>Pietzsch, A.; Adolph, G. J. Chem. Technol. Biotechnol. 1911, 30, 85.</ref> In this way about 165,000 tons were produced in 2005.<ref name = Ullmann>{{Ullmann | title = Peroxo Compounds, Inorganic | author = Harald Jakob, Stefan Leininger, Thomas Lehmann, Sylvia Jacobi, Sven Gutewort | doi = 10.1002/14356007.a19_177.pub2}}</ref> <!--( [http://sdbs.db.aist.go.jp/sdbs/cgi-bin/direct_frame_disp.cgi?sdbsno=40242 Link to IR Spectra] <ref>Anonymous. Spectral Database for Organic Compounds. http://sdbs.db.aist.go.jp/sdbs/cgi-bin/direct_frame_disp.cgi?sdbsno=40242 (accessed Oct 7, 2013).</ref> )-->

过硫酸钠的标准氧化还原电位为2.1 V，高于过氧化氢（1.8 V），低于臭氧（2.2 V）。<ref>Block, Philip A., Richard A. Brown, and David Robinson. "Novel activation technologies for sodium persulfate in situ chemical oxidation." Proceedings of the Fourth International Conference on the remediation of chlorinated and recalcitrant compounds. 2004.</ref>原位生成的硫酸根自由基（sulfate radical）的[[标准电极电势|标准电极电势]]为2.7 V。

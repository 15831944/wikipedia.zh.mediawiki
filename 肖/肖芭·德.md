'''肖芭·拉哈德亚克莎'''（'''Shobha Rajadhyaksha'''，{{bd|1948年|1月7日||}}）也被称为'''肖芭·德'''（'''Shobhaa De'''），是一位[[印度|印度]]专栏作家和小说家。<ref name="toi">{{cite news|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/india-business/Shobhaa-De-Penguin-script-new-chapter/articleshow/5775981.cms|title=Shobhaa De, Penguin script new chapter|date=2010-04-09|work={{tsl|en|Indiatimes||The Times of India}}|accessdate=2012-09-09}}</ref>德因在小说作品中描绘社交名媛和性爱而闻名<ref>{{cite web|url=https://blogs.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/politically-incorrect/how-i-became-india-ki-lady-kamasutra/|title=How I became India Ki Lady Kamasutra}}</ref>，被称为“印度的{{tsl|en|Jackie Collins||杰基·柯林斯}}”。<ref name=latimes-jackie-collins>{{cite news|title='The Jackie Collins of India'|publisher=Los Angeles Times |date=1992-04-01 |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1992-04-01/news/vw-221_1_jackie-collins}}</ref><ref name=guardian-bollywood>{{cite news|title=Hooray for Bollywood|publisher=The Guardian|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2007/may/04/india.gender|date=2007-05-03}}</ref><ref name=abc-jackie-collins>{{cite news|title=Meet India's Jackie Collins, Shobhaa De|publisher=Australian Broadcasting Corporation|url=http://www.abc.net.au/radionational/programs/booksandartsdaily/meet-india27s-jackie-collins2c-shohba-de/4524476|date=2013-02-18}}</ref>

==早年生活==
肖芭·德出生于[[印度|印度]][[马哈拉施特拉邦|马哈拉施特拉邦]][[萨塔拉|萨塔拉]]的一个[[薩拉斯瓦蒂婆羅門|薩拉斯瓦蒂婆羅門]]{{tsl|en|Hindu||印度教徒}}家庭<ref>{{Cite news| url=http://www.iloveindia.com/indian-heroes/shobha-de.html| title= Biography | work= I love India }}</ref>，在[[孟买|孟买]]{{tsl|en|Girgaon||吉尔冈}}长大。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.freebase.com/view/en/shobha_de|title=Freebase Infograph}}</ref>她跟随{{tsl|en|Zeenat Aman||泽尼特·阿曼}}开始了[[模特兒|模特兒]]职业生涯。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://ww.itimes.com/people/shobha-de|title=iTimes DB}}</ref>

==职业生涯==
在成为模特之后，她又于1970年开始从事[[新闻学|新闻]]事业，在此期间她创办并编辑了三本杂志。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://geocities.com/chinthacn/authors/De-shobha.htm |title=Bio-Bibliographical Information Biographical references |accessdate=2010-10-08 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091027095851/http://geocities.com/chinthacn/authors/De-shobha.htm |archivedate=2009-10-27  }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://stewardshipreport.com/veejay-sai-interviews-shobhaa-de-indias-sexiest-sexagenarian/ |title=Veejay Sai Interviews Shobhaa De – India’s Sexiest Sexagenarian |first=Veejay |last=Sai |work=stewardshipreport.com |date=2010-11-10 |accessdate=2015-05-08}}</ref>20世纪80年代，她为《[[印度时报|印度时报]]》周日杂志撰稿。在她的专栏中，她经常去探索名人在[[孟买|孟买]]的社交生活。目前，她是几家报纸和杂志的[[自由職業|自由]]撰稿人和专栏作家。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.punemirror.in/columns/columnists/shobhaa-de/articlelist/msid-38994883,curpg-1.cms|title=-Pune Mirror|work={{tsl|en|Pune Mirror||The Times Group}}|accessdate=2016-03-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.mumbaimirror.com/columnist/Shobhaa-De/articlelist/19864555.cms|title=-Mumbai Mirror|work=[[Mumbai_Mirror|The Times Group]]|accessdate=2016-03-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ahmedabadmirror.com/columnist/Shobhaa-De/articlelist/48204482.cms|title=-Ahmedabad Mirror|work=[[印度时报|The Times Group]]|accessdate=2016-03-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.bangaloremirror.com/columns/others/How-to-be-a-chick-Magical-detours-in-life/articleshow/50778045.cms|title=How to be a chick: Magical detours in life -Bangalore Mirror|work={{tsl|en|Bangalore Mirror||The Times Group}}|date=2016-01-29 |accessdate=2016-03-05}}</ref>也是几个电视剧的编剧。

==争议==

[[湿婆军|湿婆军]]抗议肖芭·德发表的一些观点，包括将孟买与马哈拉施特拉分开，建立一个独立的实体。<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/other-states/shobhaa-de-interview-on-shiv-sena-scandal/article7099528.ece |title=My DNA is 100% Maharashtrian, says Shobhaa De |first1=Satish |last1=Nandgaonkar |first2=Omar |last2=Rashid |work=thehindu.com |date=2015-04-14 |accessdate=2015-05-08}}</ref>

在[[2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會|2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會]]期间，德在一条推文中认为印度运动员参加奥运会只是为了自拍并花费政府资金。她的评论得到了各方的广泛批评。<ref name="indi_This">{{Cite web | title = This is what Shobhaa De said after getting slammed on Twitter for her comment on Indian Olympians | work = The Indian Express | date = 2016-08-10 | accessdate = 2016-08-11 | url = http://indianexpress.com/article/trending/trending-in-india/this-is-what-shobhaa-de-said-after-getting-slammed-on-twitter-for-her-comment-on-indian-olympians-2966103/}}</ref>

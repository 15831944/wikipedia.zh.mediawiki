'''肖松尼河'''（{{lang-en|Shoshone River}}）是美国[[怀俄明州|怀俄明州]]北部的一条河流，长{{convert|100|mi|km}}，发源于[[肖松尼国家森林|肖松尼国家森林]][[阿布萨罗卡岭|阿布萨罗卡岭]]，注入[[洛弗尔_(怀俄明州)|洛弗尔]]境内的{{tsl|en|Big Horn River|大角河}}，途经[[科迪_(懷俄明州)|科迪]]、[[鲍威尔_(怀俄明州)|鲍威尔]]、[[拜伦_(怀俄明州)|拜伦]]和[[洛弗尔_(怀俄明州)|洛弗尔]]这四个城市。它在科迪境内流经{{tsl|en|Colter's Hell||Colter's Hell}}，一处[[火山噴氣孔|火山噴氣孔]]活跃地区，<ref name="chjh1">{{cite web|url=http://www.nps.gov/history/history/online_books/grte1/chap4.htm|title=Chapter IV: "Colter's Hell": A Case of Mistaken Identity|last=Mattes|first=Merrill J.|year=1962|work=Colter's Hell and Jackson's Hole|publisher=Yellowstone Association & Grand Teton Natural History Association|accessdate=2009-07-29|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091231133006/http://www.nps.gov/history/history/online_books/grte1/chap4.htm|archivedate=2009-12-31}}</ref>也因此在怀俄明州的老版地图上被标注为“臭水河”（{{lang-en|Stinking Water River}}）。该河于1901年更名为当前更为通用的名称。<ref name="GNIS">{{Gnis|1594293|Shoshone River}}, USGS GNIS</ref>

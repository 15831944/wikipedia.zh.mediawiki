'''肖恩·怀特'''（{{lang-en|Shaun White}}，{{bd|1986年|9月3日||}}）是一名美国专业[[单板滑雪|单板滑雪]]运动员、[[滑板|滑板]]运动员和音乐家。他是三届冬奥会单板滑雪男子U型池金牌得主<ref>{{cite web|last1=Passan|first1=Jeff|title=Shaun White delivers dramatic finish to win third Olympic goal medal in halfpipe|url=https://sports.yahoo.com/shaun-white-delivers-dramatic-finish-win-third-olympic-gold-medal-halfpipe-030559354.html|publisher=Yahoo Sport|date=2018-02-14|accessdate=2019-01-05|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180214120116/https://sports.yahoo.com/shaun-white-delivers-dramatic-finish-win-third-olympic-gold-medal-halfpipe-030559354.html|archivedate=2018-02-14}}</ref>。他也拥有获得最多[[世界极限运动会|世界极限运动会]]金牌和最多[[冬奥会|冬奥会]]单板滑雪金牌的纪录，另外他还获得十个ESPY奖。他的外号是“飞翔的番茄”<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/news/profile/story/9363650/shaun_white_attack_of_the_flying_tomato|title=Shaun White: Attack of the Flying Tomato |last=Edwards|first=Gavin|work=Rolling Stone|publisher=RealNetworks|accessdate=2010-02-26}}</ref>。

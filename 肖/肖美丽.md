== 活动 ==
肖美丽参与的著名运动有：在2014年，为了提高对于[[性虐待|性虐待]]受害者的关注度，从[[北京市|北京]]到[[广州市|广州]]的2000多公里的徒步行动；在2015年，为了挑战对于女性美的刻板印象，在[[新浪微博|新浪微博]]组织了展示女性未刮[[腋毛|腋毛]]的照相比赛。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://sinosphere.blogs.nytimes.com/2015/06/09/in-womens-rights-battle-a-call-to-underarms/?_r=1|title=In Women's Rights Battle, a Call to Underarms|author=Tatlow|first=Vanessa Piao and Didi Kirsten|work=Sinosphere Blog|accessdate=2016-03-07}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/jun/10/chinese-feminists-armpit-hair-photo-contest|title=Chinese feminists hold armpit hair photo contest|author=Phillips|first=Tom|date=2015-06-10|work=The Guardian|language=en-GB|issn=0261-3077|accessdate=2016-03-07}}</ref> 肖美丽表示过，她的行动经费来源于网上的私人捐款。<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://blog.chinadaily.com.cn/blog-1123562-17450.html|title=Xiao Meili: A Young Feminist's Journey - The blog of Women_of_China -  Chinadaily Forum|work=blog.chinadaily.com.cn|accessdate=2016-03-08}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|author=Liao|first=Shannon|title=China’s feminist movement braves a government crackdown on #MeToo|url=https://www.theverge.com/2018/2/9/16897048/metoo-china-feminism-activism|accessdate=1 January 2019|work=The Verge|publisher=Verge|date=9 February 2018}}</ref>

2012年，为了抗议在中国的[[家庭暴力|家庭暴力]]，肖美丽创造了“血腥婚纱”[[迷因|米姆]]，此米姆在中国版的[[阴道独白|阴道独白]]中被引用''。''<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://bookwormfestival.com/author/xiao-meili/|title=Xiao Meili {{!}} The Bookworm Literary Festival|work=bookwormfestival.com|accessdate=2016-03-08}}</ref> 2015年，肖美丽发声抗议中国政府于[[国际妇女节|国际妇女节]]前夕逮捕五位女性权利活动家。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2015/05/14/opinion/xiao-meili-chinas-feminist-awakening.html|title=China’s Feminist Awakening|author=Meili|first=Xiao|date=2015-05-13|work=The New York Times|issn=0362-4331|accessdate=2016-03-08}}</ref> 这五位女性——[[李婷婷|李婷婷]]、[[韦婷婷|韦婷婷]]、[[郑楚然|郑楚然]]、[[武嵘嵘|武嵘嵘]]、[[王曼|王曼]]——通常被称为“女权五姐妹”。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://foreignpolicy.com/2015/04/17/china-feminist-bail-interview-released-feminism-activist/|title=The Inspirational Backstory of China's 'Feminist Five'|work=Foreign Policy|language=en|accessdate=2018-03-06}}</ref>

2018年初，肖美丽参加了抗争公共交通上女性面临的[[性骚扰|性骚扰]]的活动。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/03/04/business/china-women-only-subway-cars.html|title=China’s Women-Only Subway Cars, Where Men Rush In|author=Marchi|first=Giulia|date=2018-03-04|work=The New York Times|accessdate=2018-03-06|language=en-US|issn=0362-4331}}</ref>

肖美丽称[[西蒙·波娃|西蒙·波娃]]的[[第二性|第二性]]对她的女权行动事业有重要影响。

== 传记 ==
肖美丽出生于1989年[[四川省|四川省]]。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://time.com/1778/in-china-a-young-feminist-battles-sexual-violence-step-by-step/|title=In China, a Young Feminist Battles Sexual Violence Step by Step|author=Changsha|first=Emily Rauhala /|work=TIME.com|accessdate=2016-03-07}}</ref> 她就读于位于北京的[[中国传媒大学|中国传媒大学]]。 除了她的女权行动事业，肖美丽也是一个[[淘宝网|淘宝网]]服装店店主。<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://blog.chinadaily.com.cn/blog-1123562-17450.html|title=Xiao Meili: A Young Feminist's Journey - The blog of Women_of_China -  Chinadaily Forum|work=blog.chinadaily.com.cn|accessdate=2016-03-08}}</ref>

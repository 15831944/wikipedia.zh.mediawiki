'''国际癫痫署'''（{{lang-en|'''International Bureau for Epilepsy'''}}）是一家成立于1961年的非营利组织<ref>{{cite web|title=Mission, Objectives and Strategy for Epilepsy|url=http://www.ibe-epilepsy.org/about-us/mission-objectives-strategy/|work=International Bureau for Epilepsy|accessdate=10 January 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140108143900/http://www.ibe-epilepsy.org/about-us/mission-objectives-strategy/|archivedate=2014年1月8日}}</ref>，其成员既包括专业人士，亦包括普通大众，<ref name=EA2013>{{cite web|title=International epilepsy organisations|url=https://www.epilepsy.org.uk/about/international-epilepsy-organisations|accessdate=10 January 2014}}</ref>活动范围覆盖全球。<ref name=EA2013/>国际癫痫署经常与[[国际抗癫痫联盟|国际抗癫痫联盟]]合作，促进大众对癫痫病的理解认识。<ref>{{cite web|title=Epilepsy.org|url=http://www.epilepsy.org/|accessdate=14 May 2014}}</ref>

国际癫痫署、国际抗癫痫联盟与世界卫生组织在1997年发起了“全球抗癫痫运动”（Global Campaign Against Epilepsy），以促进各国努力解决癫痫病人的问题<ref>{{cite book|last=al.]|first=Jerome Engel ... [et|title=Epilepsy global issues for the practicing neurologist|year=2005|publisher=Demos Medical Pub.|location=New York, N.Y.|isbn=9781934559123|page=138|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=hFk4kt1o3mwC&pg=PT138}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last=Prasher|first=edited by Vee P.|title=Epilepsy and intellectual disabilities|year=2008|publisher=Springer|location=New York|isbn=9781848002593|page=212|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=Oze51wrSUGEC&pg=PA212|author2=Kerr, Mike P. }}</ref>

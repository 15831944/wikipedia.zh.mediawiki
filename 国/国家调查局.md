'''国家调查局'''（{{lang-en|National Investigation Agency}}，缩写为NIA）是印度政府为打击印度境内恐怖活动而设立的中央机构。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/defence/nia-to-have-new-hq-complex-on-tuesday/articleshow/60993539.cms|title=NIA to have new HQ complex on Tuesday}}</ref> 它是中央反恐执法机构，被授权在没有得到各邦特别许可的情况下，处理各邦与恐怖主义有关的犯罪。2008年12月31日，[[印度议会|印度议会]]颁布了《2008年国家调查局法》，该机构随之成立。<ref name="aboutus">{{Cite web|url=http://www.nia.gov.in/aboutus.aspx|title=National Investigation Agency: About Us|accessdate=16 June 2013|publisher=National Investigation Agency}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2008-12-16/india/27904311_1_special-anti-terror-law-nia-terror-related|title=Finally, govt clears central terror agency, tougher laws|accessdate=2013-09-28|author=TNN 16 Dec 2008, 12.04am IST|date=2008-12-16|publisher=Articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ndtv.com/convergence/ndtv/mumbaiterrorstrike/Story.aspx?ID=NEWEN20080076587&type=News|title=Cabinet clears bill to set up federal probe agency|accessdate=2012-12-09|date=|publisher=Ndtv.com|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130508110740/http://www.ndtv.com/convergence/ndtv/mumbaiterrorstrike/Story.aspx?ID=NEWEN20080076587&type=News|archivedate=8 May 2013}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2008-12-16/india/27918792_1_terror-act-mumbai-terror-attacks-terror-related|title=Govt tables bill to set up National Investigation Agency|accessdate=2013-09-28|author=PTI 16 Dec 2008, 07.40pm IST|date=2008-12-16|publisher=Articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com}}</ref> 其总部位于新德里。

创建于2008年孟买恐怖袭击之后，因为需要一个中央机构来打击恐怖主义。这个反恐怖主义机构的定罪率目前为95% ，因为该机构自成立以来在其登记的185个案件中已设法将167名被告定罪。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.indiatvnews.com/news/india-our-conviction-rate-is-95-nia-dg-yc-modi-424044|title=Our conviction rate is 95%: NIA DG YC Modi|date=2018-01-24|accessdate=2018-01-28|language=en-US}}</ref>

NIA的创始总干事是拉达·维诺德·拉朱，任职至2010年1月31日。之后由 Sharad Chandra Sinha 继任。<ref name="dhfeb10">{{Cite news|url=http://www.deccanherald.com/content/51872/F|title=Sharad Chandra Sinha new NIA chief|date=February 2010|work=Deccan Herald|accessdate=11 July 2013}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.jagranjosh.com/current-affairs/sc-sinha-appointed-director-general-of-nia-1287983712-1|title=S.C. Sinha- Appointed Director General of NIA|accessdate=2012-12-09|date=|publisher=Jagranjosh.com}}</ref> 2013年7月，沙拉德·库马尔被任命为国家调查局局长。 2017年9月，Y.C.Modi被任命为 NIA 的负责人。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/yc-modi-named-nia-chief/article19708485.ece|title=Y.C. Modi appointed NIA chief|date=2017-09-18|work=The Hindu|accessdate=2018-01-28|others=Special Correspondent|language=en-IN|issn=0971-751X}}</ref>

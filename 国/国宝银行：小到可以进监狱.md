《'''国宝银行：小到可以进监狱'''》是2016年{{le|史蒂夫·詹姆斯|Steve James (producer)}}制作的[[美国|美国]][[纪录片|纪录片]]<ref>[http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/abacus-small-jail-review-928175 'Abacus: Small Enough to Jail': Film Review | TIFF 2016]. ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hollywood_Reporter The Hollywood Reporter]'', 12 September 2016.</ref>，影片以[[纽约|纽约]][[曼哈頓華埠|曼哈頓華埠]]的家族社区银行[[国宝银行|国宝银行]]为中心，记录了其因“小到可以进监狱”而非“[[大到不能倒|大到不能倒]]”，成为在[[次貸危機|次貸危機]]后面临实际刑事指控的唯一金融机构<ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/radio/asithappens/as-it-happens-monday-edition-1.3758431/abacus-small-enough-to-jail-tells-story-of-only-bank-indicted-after-2008-mortgage-crisis-1.3758436 "'Abacus: Small Enough to Jail' tells story of only bank indicted after 2008 mortgage crisis"]. ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/As_It_Happens As It Happens]'', 12 September 2016.</ref>。

影片于[[2016年多倫多國際影展|2016年多倫多國際影展]]首演，并获得[[人民选择奖|人民选择奖]]纪录片类亚军<ref>[http://news.nationalpost.com/arts/movies/la-la-land-wins-the-peoples-choice-award-at-the-2016-toronto-international-film-festival "La La Land wins the People’s Choice Award at the 2016 Toronto International Film Festival"]. ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Post National Post]'', 18 September 2016.</ref>。2017年在[[公共電視網_(美國)|公共电视网]]纪录片系列“Frontline”播出<ref>[https://www.pbs.org/wgbh/frontline/article/a-sneak-peek-at-abacus-small-enough-to-jail/ "A Sneak Peek at “Abacus: Small Enough to Jail”"]. ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frontline_(U.S._TV_series) Frontline]'', 9 September 2016.</ref>，并可免费在线播放<ref>[http://www.pbs.org/wgbh/frontline/film/abacus/ "Abacus: Small Enough to Jail"], Retrieved 13 September 2017.</ref>。影片被提名为[[第90屆奧斯卡金像獎|第90屆奧斯卡]][[奥斯卡最佳纪录片奖|最佳纪录片奖]]。

== 评价 ==
该影片受到了普遍好评。[[爛番茄|爛番茄]]上影片获得93%的评分<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/abacus_small_enough_to_jail/|title=Abacus: Small Enough to Jail (2017)|accessdate=27 February 2018|publisher=[[Fandango_(company)|Fandango]]}}</ref>，[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]上影片获得73分，表示“总体评价很好”<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/abacus-small-enough-to-jail|title=Abacus: Small Enough to Jail Reviews|accessdate=21 May 2017|publisher=[[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive Inc.]]}}</ref>。

'''培生教育'''出版集团（Pearson Education）是教育类出版物出版社，旗下品牌有[[艾迪生韋斯利|艾迪生韋斯利]]、[[BBC_Active|BBC Active]]、[[Bug_Club|Bug Club]]、[[eCollege|eCollege]]、[[Fronter|Fronter]]、[[朗文出版社|朗文出版社]]、[[MyEnglishLab|MyEnglishLab]]、[[Penguin_Readers|Penguin Readers]]、[[普林帝斯霍爾|普林帝斯霍爾]]、[[Poptropica|Poptropica]]和[[Financial_Times_Press|Financial Times Press]]。

培生北美公司总部位于纽约市哈德逊大街330号。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pearson.com/contact-us/head-offices.html|title=Contact Us|accessdate=March 1, 2015}}</ref> 它之前位于新泽西州的Upper Saddle River。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.northjersey.com/news/pearson-trims-upper-saddle-river-employees-sheds-19-cites-shift-to-digital-1.609979|title=Pearson trims Upper Saddle River employees|accessdate=May 1, 2013}}</ref>

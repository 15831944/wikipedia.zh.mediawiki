'''彼得泰勒狸藻'''（[[學名|學名]]：'''''Utricularia petertaylorii'''''）为[[狸藻屬|狸藻屬]][[一年生植物|一年生]][[陆生植物|陸生]][[食虫植物|食虫植物]]。其[[种加词|种加词]]“''petertaylorii''”来源于[[英国|英国]]植物学家[[彼得·泰勒_(植物學家)|彼得·泰勒]]。<ref>Lowrie, Allen. (2002). {{PDFlink|[http://florabase.dec.wa.gov.au/science/nuytsia/374.pdf ''Utricularia petertaylorii'' (Lentibulariaceae), a new species from the south-west of Western Australia.]}} ''Nuytsia'' '''14'''(3): 405–410.</ref>彼得泰勒狸藻為[[西澳大利亞|西澳大利亞]]西南部的[[特有種|特有種]]。<ref name="FloraBase">Spooner, Amanda. (2002). ''[http://florabase.calm.wa.gov.au/browse/flora?f=323&level=s&id=19921 Utricularia petertaylorii]''. FloraBase, Western Australian Herbarium, Department of Environment and Conservation, Western Australian Government. Accessed online: 24 July 2007.</ref>彼得泰勒狸藻花冠為深紫色，實際栽培時壽命較長，可为[[多年生植物|多年生植物]]。

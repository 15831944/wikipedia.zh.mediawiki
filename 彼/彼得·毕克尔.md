==生平==
毕克尔曾在[[加州理工学院|加州理工学院]]学习物理学<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=W0U57b3QG18C&pg=PA125&dq=Peter+J.+Bickel&cd=9#v=onepage&q=Peter%20J.%20Bickel&f=false ''Reminiscences of a Statistician: The Company I Kept''],Erich Leo Lehmann,Springer,2007, </ref>，后转往[[加州大学伯克利分校|加州大学伯克利分校]]跟随著名统计学家埃里希·莱曼(Erich Leo Lehmann)学习统计学，并于1963年(时年仅22岁)就取得了博士学位。<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=qZMrkvtCU9QC&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_navlinks_s#v=onepage&q=%20&f=false ''Frontiers in statistics: dedicated to Peter John Bickel in honor of his 65th birthday''], Jianqing Fan, Hira L. Koul, Imperial College Press, 2006, {{ISBN|978-1-86094-670-7}}, page 1</ref>

毕克尔不仅是当代统计学领域公认的旗帜性人物{{r|selected-works}}，还是一位出色的导师。他培养出很多优秀的学生，其中著名者包括(按毕业时间顺序)[[吴建福|吴建福]]、唐纳德·安德鲁斯、里托夫·雅科夫、[[范剑青|范剑青]]、马克·范·德·兰、[[伊丽莎维塔·莱维娜|伊丽莎维塔·莱维娜]]等。

他1964年与南希·克雷默结婚；他们有两个孩子。<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=qZMrkvtCU9QC&printsec=frontcover&source=gbs_navlinks_s#v=onepage&q=%20&f=false ''Frontiers in statistics: dedicated to Peter John Bickel in honor of his 65th birthday''], Jianqing Fan, Hira L. Koul, Imperial College Press, 2006, {{ISBN|978-1-86094-670-7}}, page xix</ref>

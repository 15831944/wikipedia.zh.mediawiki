芝加哥樂團在憑藉著第十張專輯中由塞特拉創作的歌曲《{{link-en|If You Leave Me Now}}》奪得了第一座[[葛萊美獎|葛萊美獎]]，<ref name=BBMar5_77>{{cite journal |last=Gurza |first=Agustin |date=1977-03-05 |title=Wonder's Grammy Streak Continues |journal=[[Billboard_(magazine)|Billboard]] |volume=89|issue=9 |url={{Google books|iUUEAAAAMBAJ|page=PT98&l|plainurl=yes}}|page=65|accessdate=2019-03-06|via=Google Books}}</ref><ref name=ChicagoGrammy1>{{cite web|title = Past Winners Search|url = http://www.grammy.com/nominees/search?artist=Chicago&field_nominee_work_value=&year=1976&genre=All|website = The GRAMMYs|access-date = 2016-02-10|deadurl = no|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160211134603/http://www.grammy.com/nominees/search?artist=Chicago&field_nominee_work_value=&year=1976&genre=All|archivedate = 2016-02-11}}</ref>而同時這也是樂團第一首冠軍單曲。<ref name=ChicagoChartsHot100>{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/299102/chicago/chart?f=379|title=Chicago – Chart history {{!}} Billboard Hot 100|website=Billboard|access-date=2017-01-17|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160617040803/http://www.billboard.com/artist/299102/chicago/chart?f=379|archivedate=2016-06-17}}</ref>

個人方面，塞特拉擁有6首前40名單曲，其中兩首：《{{link-en|Glory of Love}}》和《{{link-en|The Next Time I Fall}}》在1986年的[[告示牌百强单曲榜|《告示牌》的百大單曲榜]]上名列第一。<ref name="Billboard Hot100-1986">{{cite web|url= Karate Kid Part II}}》主題曲《Glory of Love》獲得了[[奥斯卡最佳原创歌曲奖|奧斯卡獎]]和[[金球獎最佳原創歌曲|金球獎]]的最佳電影原創歌曲提名。<ref name=Oscars1987>{{Cite news|url=https://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1987|title=The 59th Academy Awards {{!}} 1987|newspaper=Oscars.org {{!}} Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences|accessdate=2017-01-15|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170102081445/https://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1987|archivedate=2017-01-02}}</ref><ref Love|website=''goldenglobes.com''|access-date=2017-01-15|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170816232515/http://www.goldenglobes.com/song/glory-love|archivedate=2017-08-16}}</ref>1987年，塞特拉以《Glory of Love》獲頒[[美国作曲家、作家和发行商协会|ASCAP]]的「最佳電影歌曲演出」（Most Performed Songs from Motion Pictures）獎項，<ref name="ASCAP87">{{cite magazine |date=1987-05-09|title=ASCAP's 1987 Film & Television Music }}</ref>此外也獲得葛萊美最佳流行男歌手獎的提名。<ref name="Orlando Sentinel Feb1987">{{cite news |date=1987-02-22 |title=Grammy Nominees |url=http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1987-02-22/entertainment/0110160139_1_winwood-peter-gabriel-warwick-and-friends |newspaper=[[Orlando_Sentinel|Orlando Sentinel]] |location=Orlando, FL }}</ref>同年，塞特拉與{{link-en|艾美·葛蘭特|Amy Grant}}合唱的《The Next Time I Fall》獲得葛萊美最佳流行組合或團體獎的提名。<ref name="Orlando Sentinel Feb1987" />

除了大衛·福斯特和艾美·葛蘭特，塞特拉還曾與其他國際知名的藝人合作，包括：[[海灘男孩|海灘男孩]]、[[比利·乔尔|比利·喬]]、[[凱倫·卡本特|凱倫·卡本特]]、[[保羅·安卡|保羅·安卡]]、[[昂內塔·費爾特斯科格|昂內塔·費爾特斯科格]]、{{link-en|理查·史特班|Richard Sterban}}、[[邦妮·雷特|邦妮·雷特]]、[[麥當娜|瑪丹娜]]、[[大衛·吉爾摩|大衛·吉爾摩]]、{{link-en|Az Yet}}、[[雪兒|雪兒]]、[[夏卡·康|夏卡·康]]、{{link-en|克莉斯托·貝爾納|Crystal Bernard}}、{{link-en|蘿娜·里維斯|Ronna Reeves}}和[[艾莉森·克勞絲|艾莉森·克勞絲]]。他的歌曲也數度出現在電影和電視節目的原聲帶中。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.hollywood.com/celebrities/peter-cetera-57390098/|title=Peter Cetera {{!}} Biography and Filmography {{!}} 1944|last=Staff|first=Hollywood.com|date=2015-02-03|newspaper=Hollywood.com|access-date=2017-01-16|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170118032604/http://www.hollywood.com/celebrities/peter-cetera-57390098/|archivedate=2017-01-18}}</ref>

2014年，有塞特拉參與錄製的芝加哥樂團首張專輯《{{link-en|Chicago Transit Authority (專輯)|Chicago Transit Authority (album)|Chicago Transit Authority}}》被引入{{link-en|葛萊美名人堂|Grammy Hall of Fame}}中。<ref name=GrammyHOF>{{cite web|title=2014 GRAMMY HALL OF FAME® INDUCTEES|url=https://www.grammy.org/recording-academy/press-release/the-recording-academy-announces-2014-grammy-hall-of-fame-inductees|website=''grammy.org''|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170131193846/https://www.grammy.org/recording-academy/press-release/the-recording-academy-announces-2014-grammy-hall-of-fame-inductees|archivedate=2017-01-31}}</ref>2016年4月，塞特拉以芝加哥樂團成員身分入選[[搖滾名人堂|搖滾名人堂]]。<ref name=rockhall>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rockhall.com/inductees/chicago|title=Chicago|website=Rock & Roll Hall of Fame|access-date=2017-01-13|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170116154323/https://www.rockhall.com/inductees/chicago|archivedate=2017-01-16}}</ref>2017年，塞特拉、{{link-en|羅伯特·蘭姆|Robert Lamm}}和{{link-en|詹姆斯·潘科|James Pankow}}以芝加哥樂團主要詞曲作家身分入選{{link-en|詞曲作家名人堂|Songwriters Hall of Fame}}。<ref name=SongHallFeb2017>{{cite press release |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |title=Songwriters Hall of Fame Announces 2017 Inductees |url=http://songhall.org/news/entry/songwriters_hall_of_fame_announces_2017_inductees |publisher=Songwriters Hall of Fame |date=2017-02-22 |access-date=2017-02-22 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170222220908/http://songhall.org/news/entry/songwriters_hall_of_fame_announces_2017_inductees |archivedate=2017-02-22  }}</ref><ref name=CBSFeb2017>{{Cite news|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/news/songwriters-hall-of-fame-2017-inductees-announced/|title=2017 Songwriters Hall of Fame inductees announced|date=2017-02-22|access-date=2017-02-22|publisher=CBS Interactive Inc|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170222192855/http://www.cbsnews.com/news/songwriters-hall-of-fame-2017-inductees-announced/|archivedate=2017-02-22}}</ref>

'''彼得·恩格尔'''（{{lang-en|Peter Engel}}，{{bd|1959年||}}）是美国[[折纸|折纸]]艺术家、理论家、科普作家、图形设计师以及建筑师。<ref>{{cite web|title=Peter Engel, Origami designer {{!}} Cribb Visuals|url=https://cribbvisuals.photoshelter.com/image/I0000B7Bqs8Yr2Ug|website=cribbvisuals.photoshelter.com|accessdate=2017-10-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=The Zen of Folding: Origami and Japanese Design|url=http://aiaeb.org/2015/03/the-zen-of-folding-origami-and-japanese-design/|website=The American Institute of Architects, East Bay|accessdate=2017-10-14|date=2015-03-23}}</ref>他出有若干本有关折纸的著作，包括《折纸——从神仙鱼到禅》（{{lang|en|Origami from Angelfish to Zen}}）<ref>{{cite web|author1=Jan Polish|title=Interview with Peter Engel|url=https://origamiusa.org/files/article_polish_Engel%20interview.pdf|website=origamiusa.org|publisher=The Paper|accessdate=2017-10-14}}</ref>、《折纸奥德赛》（{{lang|en|Origami Odyssey}}）等。

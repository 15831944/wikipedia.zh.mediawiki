'''彼得二世'''（{{lang-en|Peter II}}）是[[英國|英國]]的一隻黑[[貓|貓]]，牠在1946年和1947年間被任命為[[英國內閣辦公廳首席捕鼠大臣|英國內閣辦公廳首席捕鼠大臣]]<ref>Day, Christopher (2016). ''Larry, the Chief Mouser and other official cats''. Stroud: The History Press. ISBN 9781841657615. OCLC 983197736.</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://media.nationalarchives.gov.uk/index.php/bureau-cats/|title=Bureau-cats: A short history of Whitehall's official felines|newspaper=The National Archives|accessdate=2018-04-13|language=en}}</ref>。該隻年輕小貓的任期非常短，在上任不久的六個月之後<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/4194725/Cats-that-left-a-mark-in-the-corridors-of-power.html|title=Cats that left a mark in the corridors of power|newspaper=Telegraph.co.uk|accessdate=2018-04-13|language=en}}</ref>，牠在[[英國內政部|英國內政部]]外被一輛[[汽車|汽車]]撞死<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-england-42737193|title=Whiskers in the workplace: More cats with careers|last=Bell|first=Bethan|date=2018-02-03|newspaper=BBC News|accessdate=2018-04-13|language=en-GB}}</ref>；肇事的司機僅支付兩[[先令|先令]]來安葬彼得二世<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://news.ltn.com.tw/news/world/paper/1454|title=英曾有公務貓  專抓公署老鼠|author=魏國金|date=2005-01-05|work=自由時報電子報|accessdate=2018-04-13}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.people.com.cn/BIG5/keji/25895/3098667.html|title=每天一便士奉養貓公務員 英國公開"官貓"秘檔|accessdate=2018-04-13|author=陳濟朋|date=2005-01-05|publisher=人民網}}</ref>。

兩個月後，由另一隻名叫彼得的貓（彼得三世）接任牠的職位<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/4143423.stm|title=Home Office cat history revealed|date=2005|accessdate=2018-04-13|language=en-GB}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://blog.nationalarchives.gov.uk/blog/bureau-cats-heart-government/|title=The cats at the heart of government|date=2016-06-07|newspaper=The National Archives blog|accessdate=2018-04-13|language=en-US}}</ref>。

'''彼得·杰拉德·斯卡里'''（{{lang-en|Peter Gerard Scully}}，{{bd|1963年|1月13日}}）是一名澳大利亚被定罪的儿童性虐待者。2018年6月，他被判犯有一项人口贩卖罪和五项强奸罪，并被判处终身监禁<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2018-06-14/australian-peter-scully-convicted-in-philippines/9868958 | title=Australian Peter Scully given life sentence for human trafficking, rape in Philippines, reports say | publisher=[[ABC_News_(Australia)|ABC News (Australia)]] | date=2018-06-14 | access-date=2018-08-26 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180618050524/http://www.abc.net.au/news/2018-06-14/australian-peter-scully-convicted-in-philippines/9868958 | archive-date=2018-06-18 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。他还因其他针对儿童的罪行受到审判，包括制作和传播虐待[[兒童色情|儿童色情]]、酷刑和谋杀。

== 背景 ==
2011年，斯卡里从[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]][[维多利亚州|维多利亚州]][[墨尔本|墨尔本]]逃往[[菲律宾|菲律宾]]，之后他因涉嫌参与财产欺诈计划，被指控犯有117起欺诈和欺骗罪<ref name="SMH bground">{{Cite web |last1= Mills |first1= Tammy |last2= Vedelago |first2= Chris |last3= Murdoch |first3= Lindsay |date= 2015-03-06 |title= Alleged paedophile Peter Gerard Scully fled a sordid past in Melbourne |url= http://www.smh.com.au/national/alleged-paedophile-peter-gerard-scully-fled-a-sordid-past-in-melbourne-20150306-13x5m8.html |work= [[雪梨晨鋒報|The Sydney Morning Herald]] |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170719064850/http://www.smh.com.au/national/alleged-paedophile-peter-gerard-scully-fled-a-sordid-past-in-melbourne-20150306-13x5m8.html |archive-date= 2017-07-19 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。据称，他在[[棉兰老岛|棉兰老岛]]建立了一个利润丰厚的国际[[恋童癖|恋童癖]]圈，在[[暗网|暗网]]上提供儿童遭受酷刑和性虐待的[[按次付费电视|按次付费]]的影音流。在通过互联网销售的录像中，其中一个显示一名五岁的女孩倒挂着，斯卡里和两名同伙强奸并折磨她<ref name="NewsAUS deathpen">{{Cite web |last= Sutton |first= Candace |date= 2016-09-23 |title= Australian child molester Peter Scully faces death penalty in Philippines |url= http://www.news.com.au/world/asia/australian-child-molester-peter-scully-faces-death-penalty-in-philippines/news-story/f15a28a8b971d95f815b5f6105814ff9 |website= news.com.au |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170706100407/http://www.news.com.au/world/asia/australian-child-molester-peter-scully-faces-death-penalty-in-philippines/news-story/f15a28a8b971d95f815b5f6105814ff9 |archive-date= 2017-07-06 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。

斯卡里从贫困的父母手中买来女孩，他承诺给父母工作，并由斯卡里的两个菲律宾女友卡梅·安·阿尔瓦雷斯（Carme Ann Alvarez）和利兹·马格罗（Liezyl Margallo）<ref name="SMH deathpen">{{Cite web |last= Murdoch |first= Lindsay |date= 2016-09-20 |title= Death penalty call for accused Australian child sex predator Peter Scully in Philippines |url= http://www.smh.com.au/world/death-penalty-call-for-accused-australian-child-sex-predator-peter-scully-in-philippines-20160920-grk65r.html |work= [[雪梨晨鋒報|The Sydney Morning Herald]] |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170703135944/http://www.smh.com.au/world/death-penalty-call-for-accused-australian-child-sex-predator-peter-scully-in-philippines-20160920-grk65r.html |archive-date= 2017-07-03 |dead-url= no }}</ref>以及其他女性熟人教育他们的孩子<ref name="ABSCBN another">{{Cite web |last= Mendoza |first= Greanne |date= 2017-02-17 |title= Another suspect linked to Australian sex fiend arrested |url= http://news.abs-cbn.com/news/02/17/17/another-suspect-linked-to-australian-sex-fiend-arrested |website= [[ABS-CBN_News_and_Current_Affairs|ABS-CBN News]] |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170629104700/http://news.abs-cbn.com/news/02/17/17/another-suspect-linked-to-australian-sex-fiend-arrested |archive-date= 2017-06-29 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。阿尔瓦雷斯和马格罗都在斯卡里的视频中虐待过儿童。

其中一个视频涉及两名表姐妹，年龄分别为9岁和12岁，她们差点遭到谋杀。阿尔瓦雷斯从前是一名雏妓，斯卡里在街上发现了她并[[兒童性誘拐|诱导]]其成为一个顺从的帮凶。她承诺给两个女孩食物，哄骗她们前往斯卡里的住所，斯卡里强迫她们彼此进行性行为，而他拍摄并录像。在阿尔瓦雷斯的帮助下，斯卡里也对两人实施了性虐待。女孩被锁在狗项圈中，在一次失败的逃跑尝试之后，她们被迫挖掘自己将来的坟墓<ref name="SMH deathpen" />。经过五天的虐待后，阿尔瓦雷斯可怜并释放了她们，让她们逃跑。两女孩报警后，阿尔瓦雷斯被捕，而斯卡里則開始逃亡。

=== 《雏菊的毁灭》 ===
斯卡里最臭名昭著的影片为《雏菊的毁灭》{{efn|又译《黛西的毁灭》、《摧毁黛西》、《摧毁迪诗》}}（Daisy's Destruction），他以高达一万美元的价格卖给了客户<ref name="SMH deathpen" />。此系列影片制作于2012年，因为过于极端，有一段时间被视为[[都市传说|都市传说]]<ref>{{Cite web |last= Waugh |first= Rob |date= 2016-09-26 |title= What is Daisy's Destruction? 'Snuff film' urban legend actually exists |url= http://metro.co.uk/2016/09/26/what-is-daisys-destruction-snuff-film-urban-legend-actually-exists-6153626/ |website= [[metro.co.uk|metro.co.uk]] |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170710095822/http://metro.co.uk/2016/09/26/what-is-daisys-destruction-snuff-film-urban-legend-actually-exists-6153626/ |archive-date= 2017-07-10 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。影片内容是斯卡里和一些菲律宾同伙对一些女孩的酷刑和野蛮强奸，三个年龄最大的受害者是丽莎（Liza，12岁）、辛蒂（Cindy，11岁）和黛西（Daisy，18个月）<ref name="9news monster">{{Cite web |last= Brown |first= Tara |authorlink= Tara Brown |title= Catching a monster: The global manhunt for alleged paedophile Peter Gerard Scully |url= http://www.9news.com.au/national/2015/03/16/02/19/tracking-the-australian-man-allegedly-responsible-for-horrific-child-sexual-abuse |website= [[Ninemsn|9news.com.au]] |access-date= 2017-07-11 |date= 2015-03-16 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170709164025/http://www.9news.com.au/national/2015/03/16/02/19/tracking-the-australian-man-allegedly-responsible-for-horrific-child-sexual-abuse |archive-date= 2017-07-09 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。

在斯卡里的敦促下，他的一个女朋友——19岁的前雏妓利兹·马格罗——对儿童实施了一些极为严重的身体虐待和性虐待<ref>{{Cite web |last1= Alipon |first1= Joworski |last2= Andrade |first2= Angelo |date= 2017-01-27 |title= Filipina behind brutal 'Daisy' sex videos arrested |url= http://news.abs-cbn.com/news/01/27/17/filipina-behind-brutal-daisy-sex-videos-arrested |website= ABS-CBN News |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170625165632/http://news.abs-cbn.com/news/01/27/17/filipina-behind-brutal-daisy-sex-videos-arrested |archive-date= 2017-06-25 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。事实上，马格罗似乎是对黛西唯一的实际加害者：影片邀请观众观看黛西的“精神毁灭”，因为她“学会了如何取悦她的主人”<ref name="60 Mins monster" />。

斯卡里将《雏菊的毁灭》放在他的无极限乐趣（No Limits Fun）“制作公司”之下，通过[[暗网|暗网]]卖给其他恋童癖者。在获得它的人中，斯卡里的澳大利亚同胞马修·大卫·格雷厄姆（Matthew David Graham）是有史以来最大的儿童色情制品供应商之一。格雷厄姆在22岁时被捕，他经营了一系列名為“痛核”（Hurtcore）的儿童色情网站<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.smh.com.au/national/lux-captured-the-simple-error-that-brought-down-the-worlds-worst-hurtcore-paedophile-20160513-goum54.html|title=Lux captured: The simple error that brought down the world's worst hurtcore paedophile|first=Chris|last=Johnston|date=2016-05-14|website=The Sydney Morning Herald|access-date=2018-08-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180618175302/https://www.smh.com.au/national/lux-captured-the-simple-error-that-brought-down-the-worlds-worst-hurtcore-paedophile-20160513-goum54.html|archive-date=2018-06-18|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last= Dunn |first= Matthew |date= 2016-02-20 |title= FBI describe dark net paedophile kingpin as one of the most prolific child sex offenders ever |url= http://www.news.com.au/technology/online/censorship/fbi-describe-dark-net-paedophile-kingpin-as-one-of-the-most-prolific-child-sex-offenders-ever/news-story/eb468fcdb238a09883a1a6fe3b69d0a2 |website= news.com.au |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170715234936/http://www.news.com.au/technology/online/censorship/fbi-describe-dark-net-paedophile-kingpin-as-one-of-the-most-prolific-child-sex-offenders-ever/news-story/eb468fcdb238a09883a1a6fe3b69d0a2 |archive-date= 2017-07-15 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。格雷厄姆表示，該视频可以为他的网站吸引更多观众。

== 逮捕与审判 ==
在《雏菊的毁灭》浮出水面之后，警方发起了一场国际搜捕。警方最终追踪到菲律宾的{{tsl|en|Malaybalay|马来巴来}}市，并于2015年2月20日抓捕了斯卡里。调查人员有6份逮捕令<ref name="60 Mins monster">{{Cite episode|title=Catching a Monster|episode-link=|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zldAjOxMwsM|access-date=2017-07-11|series=[[60_Minutes_(Australian_TV_program)|60 Minutes]]|series-link=|first=|last=|network=[[Nine_News|Nine News]]|station=|date=2015|season=|series-no=|number=|minutes=|time=|transcript=|transcript-url=|quote=|language=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171014164820/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zldAjOxMwsM|archive-date=2017-10-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>，都涉及对两名表兄妹的绑架和性虐待<ref name="SMH deathpen" />。警方在菲律宾搜捕斯卡里时，设法揭开了《雏菊的毁灭》中三个主要受害者的命运。丽莎和黛西一样仍存活，但她受到持久的身体伤害，情况很恶劣。据称，11岁的辛蒂后来被斯卡里谋杀。这名女孩遭受了强奸和折磨，并被迫挖掘自己的坟墓，最后被绳子勒死。根据马格罗的说法，斯卡里录下了自己杀死辛蒂的视频<ref name="60 Mins monster" />。

斯卡里共面临75项指控。他与其他协助制作色情内容的四名男子一起受审，包括德国人Christian Rouche，菲律宾人Alexander Lao和Althea Chia以及巴西医生Haniel Caetano de Oliveira<ref name="NewsAUS deathpen" /><ref>{{Cite web |last= Reformina |first= Ina |date= 2015-03-06 |title= Australian behind 'Daisy' sex videos charged |url= http://news.abs-cbn.com/nation/03/06/15/australian-behind-daisy-sex-videos-charged |website= ABS-CBN News |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170715014858/http://news.abs-cbn.com/nation/03/06/15/australian-behind-daisy-sex-videos-charged |archive-date= 2017-07-15 |dead-url= no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last1= Rocha |first1= Alex |last2= Idaló |first2= Eduardo |date= 2015-12-21 |title= Médico de Uberaba admite pedofilia e se diz doente, segundo PF |url= http://g1.globo.com/minas-gerais/triangulo-mineiro/noticia/2015/12/medico-de-uberaba-admite-relacao-com-pedofilia-e-se-diz-doente-diz-pf.html |language=  |website= [[环球集团|g1.globo.com]] |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170803085241/http://g1.globo.com/minas-gerais/triangulo-mineiro/noticia/2015/12/medico-de-uberaba-admite-relacao-com-pedofilia-e-se-diz-doente-diz-pf.html |archive-date= 2017-08-03 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。

2015年10月，一场大火严重破坏了包含斯卡里计算机日志和视频的证据室，破坏了关键证据<ref name="SMH mastermind">{{Cite web |last= Murdoch |first= Lindsay |date= 2017-01-31 |title= Alleged child abuser Peter Scully 'may still be masterminding porn network' |url= http://www.smh.com.au/world/alleged-child-abuser-peter-scully-may-still-be-masterminding-porn-network-20170131-gu25sw.html |website= smh.com.au |access-date= 2017-07-11 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20170626031311/http://www.smh.com.au/world/alleged-child-abuser-peter-scully-may-still-be-masterminding-porn-network-20170131-gu25sw.html |archive-date= 2017-06-26 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。

2017年，菲律宾政界人士在一项将[[死刑|死刑]]重新引入菲律宾的法案中，排除了强奸、叛国和掠夺罪；法案的支持者同意将此类罪行排除在可判处死刑的人员名单之外，以便[[菲律宾国会|国会]]能更顺利地通过该法案。最終，法案以216票赞成，54票反对通过<ref>{{Cite web |last=Murdoch |first=Lyndsay |date=2017-03-08 |title=Alleged sex predator Peter Scully to escape Philippines death penalty if convicted |url=http://www.smh.com.au/world/alleged-sex-predator-peter-scully-to-escape-philippines-death-penalty-if-convicted-20170307-gusyz4.html |website=smh.com.au |access-date=2017-08-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170803061725/http://www.smh.com.au/world/alleged-sex-predator-peter-scully-to-escape-philippines-death-penalty-if-convicted-20170307-gusyz4.html |archive-date=2017-08-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。但因此，斯卡里逃脱了迫近的死刑威胁。

'''彼得·贝克'''（{{lang-en|Peter Beck}}）是一位[[新西兰|新西兰]]工程师，航天公司[[火箭实验室|火箭实验室]]的创始人。

== 早期生活 ==
贝克在新西兰的[[因弗卡吉尔|因弗卡吉尔]]长大，父亲曾是一名博物馆、艺术画廊的主任以及一名宝石专家，<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11715402|title=Peter Beck: The man with the one million-horsepower rocket|author=Bradley|first=Grant|date=2016-10-13|work=NZ Herald|accessdate=2018-07-02|language=en-NZ|issn=1170-0777}}</ref> 母亲是一位教师。<ref name=":3">{{Cite news|url=https://idealog.co.nz/venture/2015/04/innovation-heroes-peter-beck|title=Innovation Heroes: Peter Beck|date=2015-04-16|work=Idealog|accessdate=2018-07-02|language=en}}</ref>

贝克的家人喜欢机器，<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11715402|title=Peter Beck: The man with the one million-horsepower rocket|author=Bradley|first=Grant|date=2016-10-13|work=NZ Herald|accessdate=2018-07-02|language=en-NZ|issn=1170-0777}}</ref> 少年时的贝克对发动机产生强烈兴趣，花费大量时间用于研究旧机器并对其进行涡轮增压。<ref name=":0" />

儿童时期的贝克就梦想长大后从事火箭制造，<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/business/industries/93375532/national-portrait-peter-beck--nz-space-pioneer-once-mocked-for-his-vision|title=National Portrait: Peter Beck - NZ space pioneer once mocked for his vision|work=Stuff|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-02}}</ref> 高中学校的就业顾问因此要求与他的父母会面，他们担心贝克梦想的工作不符合任何"预先设置的框架"，并且是"荒谬而无法实现的"。<ref name=":1" />

== 职业生涯 ==
贝克将他的职业生涯描述为“让我能学到更多，并提供资源以最终建立更大的和更好的火箭"。<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/business/industries/93375532/national-portrait-peter-beck--nz-space-pioneer-once-mocked-for-his-vision|title=National Portrait: Peter Beck - NZ space pioneer once mocked for his vision|work=Stuff|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-02}}</ref>

17岁时贝克离开家庭，成为一名工具制造的学徒，<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11715402|title=Peter Beck: The man with the one million-horsepower rocket|author=Bradley|first=Grant|date=2016-10-13|work=NZ Herald|accessdate=2018-07-02|language=en-NZ|issn=1170-0777}}</ref><ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.noted.co.nz/currently/innovation/rocket-labs-peter-beck-and-mark-rocket-on-their-space-plans/|title=Rocket Lab's Peter Beck and Mark Rocket talk to Metro Magazine about their space plans  - Metro|author=Noted|work=Noted|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-02}}</ref> 在工作期间，他利用公司的工作室试验火箭。<ref name=":4" />

之后他进入工业研究院工作，<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11715402|title=Peter Beck: The man with the one million-horsepower rocket|author=Bradley|first=Grant|date=2016-10-13|work=NZ Herald|accessdate=2018-07-02|language=en-NZ|issn=1170-0777}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/business/industries/93375532/national-portrait-peter-beck--nz-space-pioneer-once-mocked-for-his-vision|title=National Portrait: Peter Beck - NZ space pioneer once mocked for his vision|work=Stuff|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-02}}</ref> 从事智能材料、复合材料和超导体的研究，<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.noted.co.nz/currently/innovation/rocket-labs-peter-beck-and-mark-rocket-on-their-space-plans/|title=Rocket Lab's Peter Beck and Mark Rocket talk to Metro Magazine about their space plans  - Metro|author=Noted|work=Noted|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-02}}</ref> 工作期间他结识了斯蒂芬廷德尔，后者为他的创业公司火箭实验室进行了投资。<ref name=":0" />

=== 火箭实验室 ===
2006年，贝克创办了[[火箭实验室|火箭实验室]]。<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11715402|title=Peter Beck: The man with the one million-horsepower rocket|author=Bradley|first=Grant|date=2016-10-13|work=NZ Herald|accessdate=2018-07-02|language=en-NZ|issn=1170-0777}}</ref>

== 获奖 ==
贝克获得了皇家航空学会颁发的特殊奖章，以表彰他为新西兰航空的贡献。<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/person.asp?personId=271932069&privcapId=269286866&previousCapId=269286866&previousTitle=Rocket%20Lab%20Ltd|title=Peter Beck: Executive Profile & Biography - Bloomberg|work=www.bloomberg.com|accessdate=2018-07-02}}</ref>

他还被授予了皇家学会的库珀勋章。<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/person.asp?personId=271932069&privcapId=269286866&previousCapId=269286866&previousTitle=Rocket%20Lab%20Ltd|title=Peter Beck: Executive Profile & Biography - Bloomberg|work=www.bloomberg.com|accessdate=2018-07-02}}</ref>

2014年，他获得了新西兰创新奖中的设计和工程创新奖。<ref name=":3">{{Cite news|url=https://idealog.co.nz/venture/2015/04/innovation-heroes-peter-beck|title=Innovation Heroes: Peter Beck|date=2015-04-16|work=Idealog|accessdate=2018-07-02|language=en}}</ref>

2016年，他被评为年度安永企业家。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/business/news/article.cfm?c_id=3&objectid=11728350|title=Rocket Lab's Peter Beck name EY Entrepreneur of the Year|date=2016-10-13|work=NZ Herald|accessdate=2018-07-02|language=en-NZ|issn=1170-0777}}</ref>

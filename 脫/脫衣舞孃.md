'''脫衣舞娘'''指的是一種職業的[[艷舞|艷舞]]舞者，一般以表演[[脫衣舞|脫衣舞]]為主<ref>{{cite book|author=Catherine M. Roach |title=Stripping, sex, and popular culture |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=uqd25KYuLiwC&pg=PA37 |accessdate=30 September 2011 |year=2007 |publisher=Berg |isbn=978-1-84520-129-6 |pages=37–}}</ref>。並不是所有的脫衣舞娘在表演末尾會褪去所有衣物，但在一般仍以[[全裸|全裸]]為主<ref name="Bernard 2003">{{cite journal |last1= Bernard|first1= Constance|last2= DeGabrielle|first2= C.|last3= Cartier|first3= L.|last4= Monk-Turner|first4=E. |last5= Phill|first5=C. |last6= Sherwood|first6= J.|last7= Tyree|first7=T. |year=2003 |title=Exotic Dancers: Gender Differences in Societal Reaction, Subcultural Ties, and Conventional Support |journal=Journal of Criminal Justice and Popular Culture |volume=10 |issue=1 |pages=1–11 |url=http://www.albany.edu/scj/jcjpc/vol10is1/bernard.pdf }}</ref>。

《'''脫韁野馬'''》（{{lang-en|''On the Loose''}}）是愛爾蘭創作型歌手[[奈爾·霍蘭|奈爾·霍蘭]]的一首歌曲，收錄於其首張錄音室專輯《{{tsl|en|Flicker (album)|閃耀瞬間|閃耀瞬間}}》中。歌曲由霍蘭、{{tsl|en|John Ryan (musician)|約翰·萊恩 (音樂人)|約翰·萊恩}}和朱利安·布內塔創作，其製作則由布內塔完成。2018年2月16日，歌曲的改版釋出。20日，歌曲被派往美國[[當代流行電台|當代流行電台]]發行為專輯的第4張單曲。2018年3月6日，歌曲的音乐录像带正式推出。

==背景与发行==
2017年5月13日，霍蘭在聖地牙哥為Channel 93.3的Summer Kick Off音樂會表演時首播了這首歌曲。<ref>{{cite news|last=Reilly|first=Nicholas|title=Niall Horan's new song is giving off serious Fleetwood Mac vibes|url=http://metro.co.uk/2017/05/13/niall-horans-new-song-is-giving-off-serious-fleetwood-mac-vibes-6635106/|accessdate=2018-02-08|work=Metro|date=2017-05-13|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209063223/http://metro.co.uk/2017/05/13/niall-horans-new-song-is-giving-off-serious-fleetwood-mac-vibes-6635106/|archivedate=2018-02-09}}</ref>5月29日，他在電視節目《[[今天_(NBC)|今天]]》的城市系列音樂會（{{lang|en|Citi Concert Series}}）的結尾首次對歌曲進行現場電視表演。<ref>{{cite news|last=Weatherby|first=Taylor|title=Watch Niall Horan's 'On the Loose' TV Debut, One Direction Sing-Along on 'Today'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/7809650/niall-horan-today-show-performance-videos-recap|accessdate=2018-02-08|work=Billboard|date=2017-05-29|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170910234703/http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/7809650/niall-horan-today-show-performance-videos-recap|archivedate=2017-09-10}}</ref>在接受《{{tsl|en|Herald Sun|太陽先驅報|太陽先驅報}}》的採訪時，霍蘭表示，這首歌曲導入了[[佛利伍麥克|佛利伍麥克]]的風格。他稱：“你能在那（首歌）裡聽到麥克的風格（……）我聽過許多不同類型的歌，但只有他的那些作品一直縈繞著我。”<ref>{{cite news|last=Adams|first=Cameron|title=Niall Horan didn't see fan defacing of his album title Flicker coming|url=http://www.heraldsun.com.au/lifestyle/melbourne/niall-horan-didnt-see-fan-defacing-of-his-album-title-flicker-coming/news-story/3fbdd5f54097cca50538409845535214|accessdate=2018-02-08|work=Herald Sun|date=2018-02-07}}</ref>

2018年2月5日，霍蘭透過社交媒體宣佈《脫韁野馬》將成為專輯的第4支單曲。單曲封面由凱勒·馬茨繪製。在封面中，一名女性手拿一顆心，被花朵包圍，這些花朵有一些有眼睛和腿。<ref>{{cite news|last=Macke|first=Johnni|title=Niall Horan Makes A Major Announcement|url=http://peopleschoice.com/2018/02/05/niall-horan-makes-a-major-announcement/|accessdate=2018-02-08|work=[[人民选择奖|People's Choice Awards]]|date=2018-02-05|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209063312/http://peopleschoice.com/2018/02/05/niall-horan-makes-a-major-announcement/|archivedate=2018-02-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Ross|first=Alex|title=Niall Horan announces brand new single|url=https://planetradio.co.uk/hits/entertainment/music/niall-horan-on-the-loose-single/|accessdate=2018-02-08|work={{tsl|en|Key 103||Key 103}}|date=2018-02-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209003103/https://planetradio.co.uk/hits/entertainment/music/niall-horan-on-the-loose-single/|archivedate=2018-02-09}}</ref>2月16日，歌曲的改版釋出。20日，歌曲被派往美國[[當代流行電台|當代流行電台]]發行為單曲。<ref>{{cite news|last=McDonald|first=Keisha|title=Niall Horan drops a surprise 'alternative version' of his new single On The Loose|url=http://metro.co.uk/2018/02/16/niall-horan-drops-surprise-alternative-version-new-single-loose-7318000/|accessdate=2018-02-20|work={{tsl|en|Metro (British newspaper)||Metro}}|date=2018-02-16|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180220212804/http://metro.co.uk/2018/02/16/niall-horan-drops-surprise-alternative-version-new-single-loose-7318000/|archivedate=2018-02-20}}</ref>

==樂評==
{{tsl|en|Idolator (website)|Idolator (網站)|Idolator}}的克里絲汀·李寫道，相對於《{{tsl|en|Slow Hands (Niall Horan song)|柔情雙手|柔情雙手}}》，這首歌曲“聽起來像一篇警醒人的故事”。<ref>{{cite news|last=Lee|first=Christina|title=Niall Horan Performs New Song "On The Loose": Watch|url=https://www.idolator.com/7662928/niall-horan-new-song-on-the-loose-video|accessdate=2018-02-08|work=Idolator|date=2017-05-13|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209063014/https://www.idolator.com/7662928/niall-horan-new-song-on-the-loose-video|archivedate=2018-02-09}}</ref>該網站的麥克·瓦斯則指出，歌曲與《柔情雙手》都有著相似的快節奏，相對於他的一些“極簡主義歌曲”更討電台喜歡。<ref>{{cite news|last=Wass|first=Mike|title=Niall Horan Picks "On The Loose" As His Next Single|url=https://www.idolator.com/7674605/niall-horan-next-single-loose|accessdate=2018-02-08|work=Idolator|date=2018-02-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180207062937/http://www.idolator.com/7674605/niall-horan-next-single-loose|archivedate=2018-02-07}}</ref>

== 音樂錄影帶 ==
歌曲的動畫歌詞錄影帶於2018年2月12日首播。在首播前一天，霍蘭曾發佈一時長20秒的預告片。<ref>{{cite news|last=Braca|first=Nina|title=Niall Horan Teases Lyric Video for 'On The Loose'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8099127/niall-horan-lyric-video-on-the-loose|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=Billboard|date=2018-02-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Macke|first=Johnni|title=Niall Horan 'Can't Wait' For Fans To See His Latest Project|url=http://peopleschoice.com/2018/02/12/niall-horan-cant-wait-for-fans-to-see-his-latest-project/|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=People's Choice|date=2018-02-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180221100148/http://peopleschoice.com/2018/02/12/niall-horan-cant-wait-for-fans-to-see-his-latest-project/|archivedate=2018-02-21}}</ref>歌曲的歌詞音樂錄影帶展示了一名女性在一有活動摺篷的汽車上巡遊城市，<ref>{{cite news|last=Sterling|first=Scott|title=Niall Horan Gets Animated in 'On The Loose' Lyric Video: Watch|url=http://news.radio.com/2018/02/12/niall-horan-animated-on-the-loose-lyric-video/|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=[[CBS电台|CBS Radio]]|date=2018-02-12|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180220212220/http://news.radio.com/2018/02/12/niall-horan-animated-on-the-loose-lyric-video/|archivedate=2018-02-20}}</ref>並勾引多名男性的故事。<ref name="Billboard">{{cite news|last=Braca|first=Nina|title=Niall Horan Drops Colorful Lyric Video for 'On the Loose'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8099216/niall-horan-on-the-loose-lyric-video|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=[[告示牌_(雜誌)|Billboard]]|date=2018-02-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180329192657/https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8099216/niall-horan-on-the-loose-lyric-video|archivedate=2018-03-29}}</ref>

2018年3月5日，霍蘭發佈了歌曲正式音樂錄影帶的預告片。次日，歌曲的音乐录像带正式推出。<ref>{{cite web|author1=Braca, Nina|title=Niall Horan Teases 'On The Loose' Music Video: Watch|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8231867/niall-horan-teases-on-the-loose-video-watch|website=Billboard|accessdate=2018-03-29}}</ref>

'''智庫與公民社會計畫'''({{lang-en|'''Think Tanks and Civil Societies Program'''; '''TTCSP'''}})是位於[[費城|費城]][[賓夕法尼亞大學|賓夕法尼亞大學]]的非營利計劃，智庫與公民社會計畫成立於1989年的[[外交政策研究所|外交政策研究所]]，<ref>Foreign Policy Research Institute, "Think Tanks and Foreign Policy Program", 2001-2010, "[http://www.fpri.org/research/thinktanks/objectives.html Source 1] {{Wayback|url=http://www.fpri.org/research/thinktanks/objectives.html |date=20121022010537 }}", 6 July 2010</ref>計劃主任是{{tsl|en|James McGann}}。<ref>{{cite web|title=James G. McGann - Foreign Policy Research Institute|url=http://www.fpri.org/contributor/james-mcgann/|website=Foreign Policy Research Institute|accessdate=21 May 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Bennett|first1=Amanda|title=Are think tanks obsolete?|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/in-theory/wp/2015/10/05/are-think-tanks-obsolete/|accessdate=21 May 2016|work=The Washington Post|date=5 October 2015}}</ref>計畫研究世界各地智庫，<ref>{{cite web|title=The Think Tanks and Civil Society Program {{!}} About Us|url=http://gotothinktank.com/about-us/|website=gotothinktank.com|accessdate=21 May 2016}}</ref>並維護來自世界各地約7,500家[[智庫|智庫]]的資料庫。<ref>International Relations @ UPenn, "2009 Global Rankings of Think Tanks", 2010, "[http://www.sas.upenn.edu/irp/2009GlobalRankingsofThinkTanks.htm Source 7] {{Wayback|url=http://www.sas.upenn.edu/irp/2009GlobalRankingsofThinkTanks.htm |date=20110607155633 }}", 6 July 2010</ref><ref>{{Cite web| title = 2017 Global Go To Think Tank Index Speech | url = https://repository.upenn.edu/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?article=1013&context=think_tanks | publisher = University of Pennsylvania | author = James G. McGann | language = en | date = 2018-01}}}</ref>

== 計畫背景 ==
智庫與公民社會計畫成立於1989年的[[外交政策研究所|外交政策研究所]]，到1990年計劃因為中歐與東歐政治與經濟轉變逐漸發展為全球化，<ref>Foreign Policy Research Institute, "Selected List of Publications", 2001-2010, "[http://www.fpri.org/research/thinktanks/publications.html Source 5] {{Wayback|url=http://www.fpri.org/research/thinktanks/publications.html |date=20121022010629 }}", 6 July 2010</ref>由[[世界銀行|世界銀行]]贊助並請求[[布魯金斯學會|布魯金斯學會]]的{{tsl|en|R. Kent Weaver}}<ref>Brookings Institution, "R. Kent Weaver", 2010, "[http://www.brookings.edu/experts/weaverr.aspx Source 3] {{Wayback|url=http://www.brookings.edu/experts/weaverr.aspx |date=20100215235040 }}", 6 July 2010</ref> 與[[外交政策研究所|外交政策研究所]]的James McGann合作構想出在[[巴塞隆納|巴塞隆納(西班牙)]]舉辦的{{tsl|en|Global Development Network|全球發展研究網路}}({{lang-en|Global Development Network (GDN)}})研討會，<ref>Global Development Network, "Global Development Network (GDN): Background and History", 2010, "[http://www.gdnet.org/cms.php?id=gdn_history Source 4] {{Wayback|url=http://www.gdnet.org/cms.php?id=gdn_history |date=20130521014812 }}", 6 July 2010</ref>最終研究結果出版為2000年的智庫與公民社會：知識與行動的催化劑({{lang-en|''Think Tanks and Civil Societies: Catalysts for Ideas and Action''}})，<ref>Foreign Policy Research Institute, "Selected List of Publications", 2001-2010, "[http://www.fpri.org/research/thinktanks/publications.html Source 5] {{Wayback|url=http://www.fpri.org/research/thinktanks/publications.html |date=20121022010629 }}", 6 July 2010</ref>直到2008年'''智庫與公民社會計畫'''轉移到[[費城|費城]][[賓夕法尼亞大學|賓夕法尼亞大學]]的國際關係計畫({{lang-en|International Relations Program}})。

== 全球關鍵智庫指標 ==
主任James McGann與該計畫會發表年度全球關鍵智庫指標({{lang-en|''Global Go To Think Tank Index''}})，<ref>{{cite news|title=A&M think tank aims to solve state issues|url=http://www.houstonchronicle.com/local/education/campus-chronicles/article/A-M-think-tank-aims-to-solve-state-s-problems-5761811.php|accessdate=21 May 2016|work=[[Houston_Chronicle|Houston Chronicle]]|date=17 September 2014}}</ref>自2010年起，該指標會交由包括政治家、科學家、智庫金主、智庫等使用三階段審查以決定智庫排名。<ref>Seiler, Christian, and Klaus Wohlrabe. "A critique of the 2009 global 'Go-to Think Tanks' ranking." ''CESifo DICE Report'' 8.2 (2010): 60.</ref>

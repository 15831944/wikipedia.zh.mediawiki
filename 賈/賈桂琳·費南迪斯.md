'''賈桂琳·費南迪斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Jacqueline Fernandez'''}}，{{bd|1985年|8月11日|catIdx=F}}）是一名主要在[[印度|印度]][[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]工作的[[斯里兰卡|斯里兰卡]]籍女演員及模特兒。她曾是斯里兰卡環球小姐（Miss Universe Sri Lanka）冠軍並在2006年代表斯里兰卡出戰環球小姐比賽。<ref>[https://www.vogue.in/content/jacqueline-fernandes-bollywood-celebrity-diet-fitness-tips/ "Jacqueline Fernandez on the one food on top of her diet don’ts"]. Vogue India. 09-02-2018.</ref>

她的首套宝莱坞电影是2009年的《Aladin》,其後較為成功的电影有《Housefull 2》 及《Race 2》 等等。<ref>[http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/happy-birthday-jacqueline-fernandez-10-lesser-known-facts-about-the-judwaa-2-star-4788955/ “Happy Birthday Jacqueline Fernandez: From her French abode to fitness inspiration, 10 lesser known facts about the Judwaa 2 star”]. The Indian Express. 11-08-2017.</ref>

費南迪斯出生在[[巴林|巴林]]，父親是一名因逃离[[斯里兰卡内战|斯里兰卡内战]]而定居巴林的斯里兰卡人。她亦是一名[[混血兒|混血兒]]、母親是一名[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]空中小姐、而外祖母則是加拿大人。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/I-would-easily-take-a-bullet-for-my-family-Jacqueline/articleshow/17743133.cms “I would easily take a bullet for my family: Jacqueline”]. Times of India. 25-12-2012.</ref>她毕业于澳州[[悉尼大學|悉尼大學]]的傳理媒體系。<ref>[https://fridaymagazine.ae/life-culture/-kick-was-the-best-thing-that-happened-to-m-1.1437405 “‘Kick was the best thing that happened to me’”]. Friday Magazine. 09-01-2015.</ref>

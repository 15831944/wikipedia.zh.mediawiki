== 職業生涯 ==
馬克雷出生在[[印第安納波利斯|印第安納波利斯]]<ref name=iafd/><ref name=af>{{cite web|title=VCX Finds 'Angel Face' Star Marie McCray|publisher=xbiz.com|url=http://www.xbiz.com/news/87212/2052/|accessdate=2011-04-25}}</ref>。她在16歲時做{{translink|en|Webcam model|Webcam model|網路直播模特兒}}的工作大約僅四日，當時被網路直播服務的官方人員發現是未成年而被踢除<ref name=CLTInterview>{{cite web|url= http://cltampa.com/dailyloaf/archives/2013/01/28/the-roots-of-redheaded-porn-star-marie-mccray#.UvyMnGJdW8E|title= The roots of redheaded porn star, Marie McCray|accessdate= 2014-02-13|author= Shawn Alff|date= 2013-01-28|publisher= ''{{translink|en|Creative Loafing|Creative Loafing|Creative Loafing Tampa}}''}}</ref>。她在18歲生日後當了三日的裸體模特兒<ref name=CLTInterview/>。做[[脫衣舞孃|脫衣舞孃]]的工作期間有助於她的色情事業<ref name=CLTInterview/>。她在2007年大約22歲時正式進入[[性相關行業|性相關行業]]。<p>在2010年，{{translink|en|Complex (magazine)|Complex|Complex雜誌}}公佈五十位紅髮正妹排行榜{{notetag|五十位紅髮正妹排行榜，{{lang-en|the 50 Hottest Redheads of All Time}}}}，她被列為第39名<ref>{{cite news|title=The 50 Hottest Redheads of All Time|url=http://www.complex.com/girls/2010/03/The-50-Hottest-Redheads-of-All-Time/marie-mccray#gallery|accessdate=14 March 2013|newspaper={{translink|en|Complex (magazine)|Complex|Complex雜誌}}|date=16 March 2010|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110420152227/http://www.complex.com/girls/2010/03/The-50-Hottest-Redheads-of-All-Time/marie-mccray#gallery|archivedate=20 April 2011}}</ref>。

'''瑪麗娜·魏斯曼'''（{{lang-en|Malina Weissman}}，{{bd|2003年|3月12日||}}）是[[美國|美國]]女演員、[[模特兒|模特兒]]，最出名的角色是在電視劇《[[波特萊爾的冒險_(電視劇)|波特萊爾的冒險]]》中飾演的[[紫兒·波特萊爾|紫兒·波特萊爾]]。

== 生平 ==
魏斯曼從8歲起便開始擔任模特兒<ref>{{cite web|title=Ones to Watch-Malina Weissman|url=http://lapetitemag.com/ones-to-watch-malina-weissman/|publisher=lapetitemag|accessdate=2019-01-21}}</ref>。2014年，她在電影《[[忍者神龟_(2014年电影)|忍者龜]]》中飾演年輕時的{{le|艾波·歐尼爾|April O'Neil}}<ref>{{cite news|title=Malina Weissman to Co-Star with Kevin Spacey in ‘Nine Lives’ (EXCLUSIVE)|url=https://variety.com/2015/film/news/malina-weissman-to-co-star-with-kevin-spacey-in-nine-lives-exclusive-1201459343/|accessdate=2019-01-21|publisher=Variety|date=2015-03-25}}</ref>。2015年，在電視劇《[[女超人_(電視劇)|女超人]]》中飾演年輕時的[[超级少女_(卡拉·佐·艾尔)|女超人]]<ref>{{cite news|title=CBS Casts Young 'Supergirl' (Exclusive)|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/cbs-casts-young-supergirl-779887|accessdate=2019-01-21|publisher=The Hollywood Reporter|date=2015-03-09}}</ref>。2016年，在電影《[[我的老爸喵星人|我的老爸喵星人]]》中飾演主角湯姆的女兒蕾貝卡<ref>{{cite news|title=Nine Lives Review: A Half-Hearted Attempt At Comedy That'll Make You Groan|url=https://www.news18.com/news/movies/nine-lives-review-a-half-hearted-attempt-at-comedy-thatll-make-you-groan-1285770.html|accessdate=2019-01-21|publisher=News18|date=2016-08-26}}</ref>。

2017至2019年間，在改編自著名小說《[[波特萊爾大遇險|波特萊爾大遇險]]》的電視劇《波特萊爾的冒險》中飾演紫兒·波特萊爾<ref>{{cite news|author1=Nellie Andreeva|title=Netflix’s ‘A Series Of Unfortunate Events’ Finds Its Violet & Klaus|url=https://deadline.com/2016/01/a-series-of-unfortunate-events-cast-violet-klaus-netflix-1201690862/|accessdate=2019-01-21|publisher=Deadline|date=2016-01-26}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title='A Series of Unfortunate Events’s Malina Weissman on Violet Baudelaire and Working with Neil Patrick Harris|url=https://www.teenvogue.com/story/a-series-of-unfortunate-eventss-malina-weissman-on-violet-baudelaire-and-working-with-neil-patrick-harris|accessdate=2019-01-21|publisher=Teen Vogue|date=2019-01-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title='A Series Of Unfortunate Events' Star Malina Weissman Dishes On Season 2's Ending – EXCLUSIVE|url=https://www.elitedaily.com/p/a-series-of-unfortunate-events-star-malina-weissman-dishes-on-season-2s-ending-exclusive-8715742|accessdate=2019-01-21|publisher=Elite Daily|date=2018-04-07}}</ref>。

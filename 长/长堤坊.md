'''长堤坊'''（{{lang-en|Causeway Point}}）是位于[[新加坡|新加坡]]北部[[兀兰|兀兰]]新镇的购物中心。长堤坊直到2017年{{tsl|en|Northpoint City, Singapore||纳福城}}开幕前是新加坡北部最大型的购物商场。<ref name="TODAYonline 20171">{{cite web | title=Singapore’s largest mall in the north to open in Yishun this year | website=TODAYonline | date=2017-07-25 | url=https://www.todayonline.com/business/property/largest-mall-northern-spore-open-yishun-year | access-date=2018-08-28 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180721102847/https://www.todayonline.com/business/property/largest-mall-northern-spore-open-yishun-year | archive-date=2018-07-21 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="TODAYonline 2017"/>

==历史==
长堤坊于1998年11月开张，开张时候长堤坊是兀兰地区的首家大型综合商场。{{tsl|en|Cathay Cineplexes||嘉泰电影院集团}}的电影院也同时开放。其他主要业主包括{{tsl|en|John Little (department store)||John Little百货商场}}以及{{tsl|en|Metro (department store)||美羅百货商场}}。<ref name="TODAYonline 2017">{{cite web | title=Old Woodlands Town Centre now a shadow of its vibrant past | website=TODAYonline | date=2017-03-31 | url=https://www.todayonline.com/singapore/town-centre-now-shadow-its-vibrant-past | access-date=2018-08-28 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170804030340/http://www.todayonline.com/singapore/town-centre-now-shadow-its-vibrant-past | archive-date=2017-08-04 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

2010年间，长堤坊开始翻新工程，目标是为了改善长堤坊的购物体验。翻新工程历时30个月。长堤坊于2012年间重新开放。商场新的主要业主包括[[大食代|大食代]]、[[優衣庫|優衣庫]]以及{{tsl|en|Courts Singapore||Courts}}。<ref name="Teng 2013">{{cite web | last=Teng | first=Amelia | title=Woodlands: From back of the woods to a buzzing town | website=The Straits Times | date=2013-12-27 | url=https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/woodlands-from-back-of-the-woods-to-a-buzzing-town | access-date=2018-08-28 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151223155505/http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/woodlands-from-back-of-the-woods-to-a-buzzing-town | archive-date=2015-12-23 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

'''长安标致雪铁龙''' ({{lang-en|Changan PSA}}) 是一家位于[[深圳|深圳]]的汽车公司，由[[长安汽车|长安汽车]]和[[标致雪铁龙集团|标致雪铁龙集团]]各出资50%设立。

==历史==
是[[标致雪铁龙集团|标致雪铁龙集团]]继[[神龙汽车|神龙汽车]]之后在中国的第二家合资企业。<ref>[http://www.chinacartimes.com/2011/06/15/capsa-changan-psas-new-joint-venture-company/ CAPSA – Chang’an-PSA’s new joint venture company] chinacartimes.com, June 15, 2011</ref>年产值20万辆，总部位于中国[[深圳|深圳]]。<ref>For Shenzhen production base, see {{cite web | url=http://www.capsa.com.cn/en/capsa_qyjs.aspx | title=About CAPSA: Changan PSA Automobiles Co., Ltd Profile | publisher=Changan Automobiles Co Ltd | work=capsa.com.cn | accessdate=27 September 2013 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131222040014/http://www.capsa.com.cn/en/capsa_qyjs.aspx# | archive-date=2013-12-22 | dead-url=yes }}</ref>

工厂设立于2014年，主要生产[[DS_5LS|DS 5LS]]和[[DS_6WR|DS 6WR]]系列。<ref>{{cite web|title=PSA launches DS compact crossover in China|url=http://europe.autonews.com/article/20141010/ANE/141009817/psa-launches-ds-compact-crossover-in-china|website=Automotive News Europe|accessdate=1 November 2016|date=10 October 2014}}</ref>

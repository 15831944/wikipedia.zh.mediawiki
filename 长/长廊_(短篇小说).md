'''《长廊》'''（{{lang-en|'''Corridor'''}}）是一本由新加坡作者{{tsl|en|Alfian Sa'at|奥费安·萨安}}撰写的[[短篇小说|短篇小说]]集。<ref name="Singapore Writers Festival 2018">{{cite web | title=Alfian Sa'at | website=Singapore Writers Festival | date=2018-04-10 | url=https://www.singaporewritersfestival.com/nacswf/nacswf/author-speaker/Alfian-Sa-at.html | access-date=2018-07-23 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150214021958/https://www.singaporewritersfestival.com/nacswf/nacswf/author-speaker/alfian-sa-at.html | archive-date=2015-02-14 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

==出版史==
长廊于1999年由SNP Editions首先出版，并且于2015年由{{link-en|社会思潮出版社|Ethos Books}}重新出版。

==内容==
长廊共收集了12篇短篇小说。这些小说主要是描绘[[新加坡|新加坡]]人们于国家独立后的生活的点滴。重点叙述居住在高度城市化的环境的新一代新加坡国人生活的所见所闻。<ref name="Google Books">{{cite web | title=The Routledge Concise History of Southeast Asian Writing in English | website=Google Books | url=https://books.google.com/books/about/The_Routledge_Concise_History_of_Southea.html?id=t2yNAgAAQBAJ | page=240| access-date=2018-07-23}}</ref>
*"Project"  （计划）
*"Video"    （视频）
*"Orphans"  （孤儿）
*"Pillow"   （枕头）
*"Corridor" （长廊）
*"Duel"     （斗争）
*"Winners"  （赢家）
*"Cubicle"  （柜)
*"Umbrella"  (雨伞）
*"Bugis"     （{{link-en|武吉士|Bugis, Singapore}})
*"Birthday"  (生日）
*"Disco"     （迪斯科）

==奖项==
长廊于1998年间荣获[[新加坡文學獎|新加坡文學獎]]表彰奖。<ref name="Council 2018">{{cite web | last=Council | first=Singapore Book | title=Singapore Literature Prize - Awards | website=Singapore Book Council | date=2018-07-20 | url=https://bookcouncil.sg/awards/singapore-literature-prize | access-date=2018-07-23 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170916100658/http://bookcouncil.sg/awards/singapore-literature-prize | archive-date=2017-09-16 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

==改编==
2000年[[新傳媒朝陽頻道|新傳媒朝陽頻道]]将《长廊》改编为电视剧。此电视剧是媒体业者与作者的紧密合作后诞生的。时任新加坡通讯，新闻与艺术部高级政务次长雅迪曼于2013年的一场演说中称赞了媒体业者与作者的良好团队精神。<ref name="Base 2018">{{cite web | title=Speech by Senior Parliamentary Secretary Yatiman Yusof at the Singapore Writers' Festival 2003 Opening Reception, Asian Civilisations Museum, Empress Place | website=Base | date=2018-07-19 | url=http://www.mci.gov.sg/pressroom/news-and-stories/pressroom/2003/8/speech-by-senior-parliamentary-secretary-yatiman-yusof-at-the-singapore-writers-festival-2003-opening-reception-asian-civilisations-museum-empress-place?page=16 | access-date=2018-07-23 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180723105726/https://www.mci.gov.sg/pressroom/news-and-stories/pressroom/2003/8/speech-by-senior-parliamentary-secretary-yatiman-yusof-at-the-singapore-writers-festival-2003-opening-reception-asian-civilisations-museum-empress-place?page=16 | archive-date=2018-07-23 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="AsiaOne, Asias Leading News Portal, Get Latest News at AsiaOne 2013">{{cite web | title=Malay screen legend S.

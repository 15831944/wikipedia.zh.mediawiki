'''乙酸铥'''是[[铥|铥]]的[[乙酸盐|乙酸盐]]，化学式为Tm(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub>，它以无水物和四水合物<ref>{{cite journal|date=1971-08-01|title=Spectroscopic study of thulium acetate tetrahydrate single crystals|pages=1058–1060|volume=15|issue=2|issn=1573-8647|url=https://doi.org/10.1007/BF00607309|doi=10.1007/BF00607309|journal=Journal of Applied Spectroscopy|language=en|accessdate=2019-02-01|author=K. I. Petrov, M. G. Zaitseva, L. M. Sukova}}</ref>的形式存在。

==性质==
乙酸铥和[[乙酰丙酮铁|乙酰丙酮铁]]在丁二醇中于300 °C反应，可以得到六方晶系的TmFeO<sub>3</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Glycothermal Reaction of Rare-Earth Acetate and Iron Acetylacetonate: Formation of Hexagonal ReFeO3|journal=Journal of the American Ceramic Society|volume=80|issue=8|pages=2157–2160|doi=10.1111/j.1151-2916.1997.tb03103.x|url=https://ceramics.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/j.1151-2916.1997.tb03103.x|date=1997|issn=1551-2916|language=en|accessdate=2019-02-01|author=Masashi Inoue, Toshihiro Nishikawa, Tomohiro Nakamura, Tomoyuki Inui}}</ref>

和[[三氟乙酸|三氟乙酸]]反应，生成三氟乙酸盐：<ref>{{cite journal|date=2006-04-01|title=Thulium(III) trifluoroacetates Tm(CF3COO)3 · 3H2O and Tm2(CF3COO)6 · 2CF3COOH · 3H2O: Synthesis and crystal structure|pages=541–548|volume=51|issue=4|issn=1531-8613|url=https://doi.org/10.1134/S0036023606040061|doi=10.1134/S0036023606040061|journal=Russian Journal of Inorganic Chemistry|language=en|accessdate=2019-02-01|author=S. I. Gutnikov, E. V. Karpova, M. A. Zakharov, A. I. Boltalin}}</ref>
: Tm(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> + 3 CF<sub>3</sub>COOH → Tm(CF<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> + 3 CH<sub>3</sub>COOH

'''乙酸铕'''是铕的[[乙酸盐|乙酸盐]]，化学式为Eu(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub>，可以以[[无水物|无水物]]、[[水合物|倍半水合物]]<ref>注释：含有1.5个结晶水的水合物是倍半水合物，对于乙酸铕，其结构为[Eu<sub>2</sub>(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>6</sub>(H<sub>2</sub>O)](H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>2</sub></ref><ref name=Sonia>{{cite journal|title=Direct Oxidation of Europium Metal with Acetic Acid: Anhydrous Europium(III) Acetate, Eu(OAc)3, its Sesqui-hydrate, Eu(OAc)3(H2O)1.5, and the “Hydrogendiacetate”, [Eu(H(OAc)2)3](H2O)|journal=Zeitschrift für anorganische und allgemeine Chemie|volume=632|issue=12-13|pages=1989–1994|doi=10.1002/zaac.200600154|url=https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/zaac.200600154|date=2006|issn=1521-3749|language=en|accessdate=2019-02-01|author=Sonia Gomez Torres, Ingo Pantenburg, Gerd Meyer}}</ref>和[[水合物|四水合物]]<ref>{{cite journal|title=The Salts of Europium|date=1939-09-01|doi=10.1021/ja01878a055|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|pages=2455–2456|volume=61|issue=9|issn=0002-7863|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ja01878a055|accessdate=2019-02-01|author=Herbert N. McCoy}}</ref>的形式存在。

==制备==
乙酸铕可由[[乙酸|乙酸]]和[[氧化铕|氧化铕]]在加热的条件下搅拌反应，加水稀释后结晶得到：<ref>唐慧安，刘艳芝，赵爱萍. 一种醋酸铕的合成方法:, CN104387254A[P]. 2015.</ref>

: Eu<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + 6 CH<sub>3</sub>COOH → 2 Eu(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> + 3 H<sub>2</sub>O

金属[[铕|铕]]也能直接参与反应：<ref name=Sonia />
: 2 Eu + 6 CH<sub>3</sub>COOH → 2 Eu(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> + 3 H<sub>2</sub>↑
==化学性质==
将乙酸铕溶解在水中，用乙酸酸化，通过电化学还原法可以得到二价铕的化合物[Eu(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>(CH<sub>3</sub>COOH)(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>2</sub>]。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Structure and spectroscopy of diaqua(μ3-acetato)(acetato-O)(acetic acid-O)europium(II), [Eu(OAc)2(AcOH)(H2O)2]|volume=14|issue=23|pages=3573–3577|date=1995-12-01|issn=0277-5387|doi=10.1016/0277-5387(95)00174-Q|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/027753879500174Q|journal=Polyhedron|accessdate=2019-02-01|author=P. Starynowicz}}</ref>

乙酸铕在过量的冰乙酸中结晶，可以得到酸式盐[Eu(H(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>)<sub>3</sub>](H<sub>2</sub>O)。<ref name=Sonia />

乙酸盐受热可以分解，水合物先失水得到无水物，然后经过碱式乙酸盐EuOCH<sub>3</sub>COO、碱式碳酸盐Eu<sub>2</sub>O<sub>2</sub>CO<sub>3</sub>，最终得到[[氧化铕|氧化铕]]。<ref>Ogawa M, Manabe K. [https://www.jstage.jst.go.jp/article/jcersj1988/96/1117/96_1117_890/_pdf Thermal Decomposition of Europium (III) Acetate Tetrahydrate]（酢酸ユウロピウム(Ⅲ)4水和物の熱分解）. ''Journal of the Ceramic Society of Japan'', 1988, 96(1117): 890-893.</ref>

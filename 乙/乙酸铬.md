'''乙酸铬'''是[[铬|铬]]的[[乙酸盐|乙酸盐]]之一，化学式为Cr(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub>。乙酸铬的六水合物（[Cr(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>6</sub>](O<sub>2</sub>CCH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>）可由Cr(OH)<sub>3</sub>·3H<sub>2</sub>O和冰乙酸反应得到，它在空气中不稳定。乙酸铬的市售品通常是其碱式盐[Cr<sub>8</sub>(OH)<sub>8</sub>(O<sub>2</sub>CCH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>16</sub>]·30H<sub>2</sub>O。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Polynuclear chromium(III) carboxlylates: Part 2. Chromium(III) acetate — what's in it?|volume=320|issue=1|pages=127–132|date=2001-08-13|issn=0020-1693|doi=10.1016/S0020-1693(01)00492-3|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0020169301004923|journal=Inorganica Chimica Acta|accessdate=2019-02-02|author=Michal Eshel, Avi Bino}}</ref>

与乙酸铬相关的化合物包括[Cr<sub>3</sub>O(O<sub>2</sub>CCH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>6</sub>(OH<sub>2</sub>)<sub>3</sub>]Cl(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>6</sub>，其中含有[Cr<sub>3</sub>O(O<sub>2</sub>CCH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>6</sub>(OH<sub>2</sub>)<sub>3</sub>]<sup>+</sup>。<ref name=Schenk>{{cite journal|title=Low-temperature structural and spectroscopic properties of [Cr<sub>3</sub>O(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>6</sub>(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>3</sub>]Cl<sup>.</sup>6H<sub>2</sub>O|authors=Kurt J. Schenk, Hans U. Güdel|journal=Inorg. Chem.|year=1982|volume=21|pages=2253–2256|doi=10.1021/ic00136a025}}</ref>

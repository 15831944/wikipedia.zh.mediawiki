'''乙酸镍'''是镍的[[乙酸盐|乙酸盐]]，化学式为Ni(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>，有时简写为Ni(OAc)<sub>2</sub>或NiAc<sub>2</sub>。乙酸镍可以以无水物、二水合物和四水合物的形式存在。它常用于[[电镀|电镀]]。

==制备==
乙酸镍可以由[[氢氧化镍|氢氧化镍]]或[[碳酸镍|碳酸镍]]和[[乙酸|乙酸]]反应得到：
: NiCO<sub>3</sub> +  2 CH<sub>3</sub>CO<sub>2</sub>H   + 3 H<sub>2</sub>O →   Ni(CH<sub>3</sub>CO<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>·4 H<sub>2</sub>O  +  CO<sub>2</sub>

==性质==
四水合乙酸镍是绿色晶体，[[X射线衍射|X射线衍射]]表明它具有八面体结构。中心镍原子由四个[[水|水]]分子和两个[[乙酸根|乙酸根]]配位。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1107/S0365110X5300171X | journal = [[Acta_Crystallogr.|Acta Crystallogr.]] | title = The crystal structures of nickel acetate, Ni(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>·4H<sub>2</sub>O, and cobalt acetate, Co(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>·4H<sub>2</sub>O | year = 1953 | last1 = Van Niekerk | first1 = J. N. | last2 = Schoening | first2 = F. R. L. | volume = 6 | issue = 7 | pages = 609–612}}</ref>四水合物可以在真空中和[[乙酸酐|乙酸酐]]反应<ref>Lascelles, Keith; Morgan, Lindsay G.; Nicholls, David; Beyersmann, Detmar (2005), "Nickel Compounds", Ullmann's Encyclopedia of Industrial Chemistry, Weinheim: Wiley-VCH, doi:10.1002/14356007.a17_235.pub2</ref>或直接加热<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1021/ic50008a039 | journal = [[Inorg._Chem.|Inorg. Chem.]] | title = Cobalt and Nickel Acetates in Anhydrous Acetic Acid | year = 1963 | last1 = Tappmeyer | first1 = W. P. | last2 = Davidson | first2 = Arthur W. | volume = 2 | issue = 4 | pages = 823–825}}</ref>来脱水。

乙酸镍可以促进[[硼氢化钠|硼氢化钠]]的水解。<ref>庞美丽, 吴川, 吴锋,等. [http://www.cngspw.com/doc2/Thesis/2000/20110903206937/20110903206937.pdf 醋酸镍促进硼氢化钠水解制氢的研究][J]. 华南师范大学学报(自然科学版), 2009(s1):88-89.</ref>

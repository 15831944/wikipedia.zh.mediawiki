'''乙酰丙酮双氧钼'''是[[钼|钼]]的[[乙酰丙酮配合物|乙酰丙酮配合物]]之一，化学式为MoO<sub>2</sub>(acac)<sub>2</sub>。

==制备==
乙酰丙酮钼可由[[三氧化钼|三氧化钼]]和[[乙酰丙酮|乙酰丙酮]]在[[氨水|氨水]]中反应，[[硝酸|硝酸]]酸化得到。<ref>Francisco J. Arnaiz. Microscale synthesis of MoO<sub>2</sub>(acac)<sub>​2</sub>. ''Journal of Chemical Education'', 1995. 72 (1): A7-A8. DOI:10.1021/ed072pA7.</ref>二乙氧基二氧化钼和[[乙酰丙酮|乙酰丙酮]]在甲苯中反应，也能得到乙酰丙酮双氧钼。<ref>Kim, Gyu Shik; Huffman, David; DeKock, Carroll W. Preparation and properties of dioxomolybdenum alkoxides, phenoxide, and siloxides prepared by the cocondensation reaction of molybdenum trioxide with alkoxy- or (aryloxy)​silanes. ''Inorganic Chemistry'', 1989. 28(7): 1279-1283. DOI:10.1021/ic00306a015.</ref>

'''乙酸亚锡'''是锡(II)的[[乙酸盐|乙酸盐]]，化学式为Sn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>，最早发现于1882年。<ref name=Varvara>{{cite journal|journal=Polyhedron|volume=26|issue=18|language=en|date=2007-11|pages=5365–5369|doi=10.1016/j.poly.2007.08.010|url=https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0277538707004950|title=Crystal structure and chemical bonding in tin(II) acetate|accessdate=2019-04-19|author=Varvara S. Stafeeva, Alexander S. Mitiaev, Artem M. Abakumov, Alexander A. Tsirlin, Artem M. Makarevich, Evgeny V. Antipov}}</ref>

==制备==
将[[氧化亚锡|氧化亚锡]]溶于冰乙酸中回流，冷却时得到黄色的Sn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>·2CH<sub>3</sub>COOH。减压加热可除去乙酸，升华得到白色的Sn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>晶体。<ref name=Varvara />

==性质==
Sn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>·2CH<sub>3</sub>COOH在常压下加热发生歧化分解，有[[二氧化锡|二氧化锡]]、[[氢气|氢气]]等产物生成。而无水Sn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>的分解得到的是蓝黑色的[[氧化亚锡|氧化亚锡]]。<ref name=JD>Donaldson, J. D., Moser, W., & Simpson, W. B. (1964). 1147. Tin (II) acetates. ''Journal of the Chemical Society'', 5942-5947.</ref>

无水Sn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>2</sub>在水中分解<ref name=JD />，但是在碱金属或碱土金属乙酸盐中可以形成KSn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO<sub>2</sub>)<sub>3</sub>、Ba[Sn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub>]<sub>2</sub>等配合物。<ref>Donaldson, J. D., & Knifton, J. F. (1966). Complex tin (II) acetates. ''Journal of the Chemical Society A: Inorganic, Physical, Theoretical'', 332-336.</ref>

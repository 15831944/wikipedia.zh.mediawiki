'''乙酸钆'''是[[镧系金属|镧系金属]][[钆|钆]]和[[乙酸|乙酸]]形成的盐，化学式为Gd(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub>，是可溶于水的无色晶体，可以形成水合物。<ref>{{книга

==制备==
乙酸钆的四水合物可由氧化钆和乙酸反应，从水溶液中结晶得到：<ref name=Stephan>{{cite journal|title=Unexpected Appearance of Molecular Ferromagnetism in the Ordinary Acetate [Gd(OAc)3(H2O)22]⋅4 H2O|journal=Angewandte Chemie International Edition|volume=42|issue=25|pages=2862–2864|doi=10.1002/anie.200250738|url=https://www.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/anie.200250738|date=2003|issn=1521-3773|accessdate=2019-02-01|author=Stephan T. Hatscher, Werner Urland}}</ref>
: Gd<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + 6 HAc + 5 H<sub>2</sub>O → [(GdAc<sub>3</sub>(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>]·4H<sub>2</sub>O

==性质==
乙酸钆和[[乙酰丙酮|乙酰丙酮]]在[[三乙胺|三乙胺]]的存在下在[[甲醇|甲醇]]溶液中回流反应，可以得到配合物[Gd<sub>4</sub>(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>4</sub>(acac)<sub>8</sub>(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>4</sub>]。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Polynuclear and Polymeric Gadolinium Acetate Derivatives with Large Magnetocaloric Effect|date=2012-01-02|doi=10.1021/ic2018314|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|pages=405–413|volume=51|issue=1|issn=0020-1669|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ic2018314|accessdate=2019-02-01|author=Fu-Sheng Guo, Ji-Dong Leng, Jun-Liang Liu, Zhao-Sha Meng, Ming-Liang Tong}}</ref>

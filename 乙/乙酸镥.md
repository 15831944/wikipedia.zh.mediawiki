'''乙酸镥'''是[[镥|镥]]的[[乙酸盐|乙酸盐]]，化学式为Lu(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub>。

==制备==
乙酸镥可由[[氧化镥|氧化镥]]和气态[[乙酸|乙酸]]<ref>Janina Wysocka-Lisek. Study of topochemical preparation of rare earth acetates{{pl}}. ''Biuletyn Lubelskiego Towarzystwa Naukowego, Matematyka-Fizyka-Chemia'', 1981. 23 (2): 145-149.</ref>或50%乙酸溶液<ref>Xina Graehlert, Graehlert Starke. Preparation, thermogravimetry, and IR spectra of rare earth acetates. ''Wissenschaftliche Zeitschrift der Technischen Universitaet Chemnitz'', 1992. 34 (2): 219-229.</ref>反应得到。

==性质==
乙酸镥和[[氟化铵|氟化铵]]反应，生成[[氟化镥|氟化镥]]：
: Lu(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> + 3 NH<sub>4</sub>F → LuF<sub>3</sub> + 3 CH<sub>3</sub>COONH<sub>4</sub>
氟硼酸盐（如[BMIM]BF<sub>4</sub><ref>注释：原文为BMIM = 1-butyl-2-methylimidazolium，疑似写错，正确的是1-butyl-3-methylimidazolium</ref>）水解生成的{{chem|F|-}}也能与{{chem|Lu|3+}}产生沉淀。<ref>Becerro, Ana I.; Gonzalez-Mancebo, Daniel; Ocana, Manuel. Uniform, luminescent Eu:LuF<sub>3</sub> nanoparticles. ''Journal of Nanoparticle Research'', 2015. 17 (1): 1-10. DOI:10.1007/s11051-015-2874-z.</ref>

和[[磷酸|磷酸]]反应，生成[[磷酸镥|磷酸镥]]：<ref>Becerro, Ana I.; Ocana, Manuel. Quick synthesis, functionalization and properties of uniform, luminescent LuPO4-​based nanoparticles. ''RSC Advances'', 2015. 5 (44): 34517-34524. DOI:10.1039/C5RA05305F.</ref>

: Lu(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> + H<sub>3</sub>PO<sub>4</sub> → LuPO<sub>4</sub> + 3 CH<sub>3</sub>COOH

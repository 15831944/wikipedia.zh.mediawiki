'''乙基溴化镁'''是一种[[格氏试剂|格氏试剂]]，化学式为C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>5</sub>MgBr，该分子中乙基和镁以共价键相连，而溴以离子存在，因此化学式也可写成C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>5</sub>Mg<sup>+</sup>·Br<sup>−</sup>。它广泛用于实验室的有机物合成之中。
==制备==
乙基溴化镁可由[[溴乙烷|溴乙烷]]和[[镁|镁]]在乙醚中反应得到：<ref>{{ OrgSynth | author = Moyer, W. W.; Marvel, C. S. | title = Triethyl Carbinol | year = 1931 | volume = 11 | pages = 98 | collvol = 2 | collvolpages = 602 | prep = cv2p0602 }}</ref>

:EtBr + Mg → EtMgBr
== 反应 ==
乙基溴化镁除了用作亲核加成反应的乙基阴离子合成子外，还可以用作强碱来消除各种底物的质子，如炔烃：<ref>{{ OrgSynth | author = Taniguchi, H.; Mathai, I. M.; Miller, S. I. | title = 1-Phenyl-1,4-Pentadiyne and 1-Phenyl-1,3-Pentadiyne | year = 1970 | volume = 50 | pages = 97 | collvol = 6 | collvolpages = 925 | prep = cv6p0925 }}</ref><ref>{{ OrgSynth | author = Quillinan, A. J.; Scheinmann, F. | title = 3-Alkyl-1-alkynes Synthesis: 3-Ethyle-1-hexyne | year = 1978 | volume = 58 | pages = 1 | collvol = 6 | collvolpages = 595 | prep = cv6p0595 }}</ref><!--double deprotonation of hexyne followed by ethylation--><ref>{{ OrgSynth | author = Newman, M. S.; Stalick, W. M. | title = 1-Ethoxy-1-butyne | year = 1977 | volume = 57 | pages = 65 | collvol = 6 | collvolpages = 564 | prep = cv6p0564 }}</ref><!--ethylation of acetylide-->

:RC≡CH  +  C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>5</sub>MgBr  →  RC≡CMgBr  +  C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>6</sub>

但这种应用已经被更常用的[[有机锂试剂|有机锂试剂]]所取代。

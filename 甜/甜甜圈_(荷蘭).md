'''荷蘭甜甜圈'''（{{lang-nl|Oliebol}}，{{IPA-nl|ˈoːlibɔl}}, plural '''{{Audio|Nl-oliebollen2.ogg|oliebollen}}'''）是荷蘭和[[比利时|比利时]]在除夕的一種傳統食物，在荷蘭被稱為“Oliebol”或“Oliebollen”，而在比利時則被稱為法文的“Smoutebollen”和“Croustillons”<ref>{{cite news|title=Oliebollen: Welcoming the New Year with a Dutch Tradition|publisher=Paste (magazine)|date=2017-01-09|author=Joan MacDonald|url=https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2017/01/oliebollen-welcoming-the-new-year-with-a-dutch-tra.html|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171226081035/https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2017/01/oliebollen-welcoming-the-new-year-with-a-dutch-tra.html|archivedate=2017-12-26|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。

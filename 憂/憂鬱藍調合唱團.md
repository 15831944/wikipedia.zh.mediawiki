'''憂鬱藍調合唱團'''（{{lang-en|'''The Moody Blues'''}}）是1964年在[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[伯明罕|伯明罕]]成立的英國搖滾樂團，他們最初以[[節奏藍調|節奏藍調]]音樂為主，但自從1967年的第二張專輯《Days of Future Passed》開始融合了搖滾和古典音樂，使憂鬱藍調合唱團成為[[藝術搖滾|藝術搖滾]]和[[前衛搖滾|前衛搖滾]]發展的先驅之一。<ref name="ray"/><ref name="Greene">{{cite web | url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/pictures/readers-poll-the-10-bands-who-should-enter-the-rock-and-roll-hall-of-fame-in-2014-20130424/5-the-moody-blues-0026246 | title=Readers' Poll: 10 Bands Who Should Enter the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2014 | work=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]] | first=Andy | last=Greene | date=24 April 2013 | accessdate=25 August 2015 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924213620/http://www.rollingstone.com/music/pictures/readers-poll-the-10-bands-who-should-enter-the-rock-and-roll-hall-of-fame-in-2014-20130424/5-the-moody-blues-0026246 | archivedate=24 September 2015 }}</ref>該專輯被認為是「里程碑」和「最成功的[[概念專輯|概念專輯]]之一」。 <ref name="ray"/> 

樂團最為人所知的單曲包括〈{{link-en|Go Now|Go Now}}〉、〈{{link-en|Nights in White Satin|Nights in White Satin}}〉、〈{{link-en|Tuesday Afternoon|Tuesday Afternoon}}〉和〈{{link-en|Question|Question (The Moody Blues song)}}〉。憂鬱藍調合唱團共獲得了18張白金與金唱片的成績，並在全球賣出7千萬份專輯。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.musicradar.com/news/guitars/justin-hayward-on-the-moody-blues-and-writing-in-corners-580967 | title=Justin Hayward on The Moody Blues and writing in corners | publisher=[[MusicRadar|MusicRadar]] | first=Matt | last=Parker | date=25 July 2013 | accessdate=27 August 2015 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150910035324/http://www.musicradar.com/news/guitars/justin-hayward-on-the-moody-blues-and-writing-in-corners-580967 | archivedate=10 September 2015 }}</ref>2018年，樂團被引入[[搖滾名人堂|搖滾名人堂]]。

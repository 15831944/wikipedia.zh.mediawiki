'''努斯雷特·戈克切'''（{{lang-tr|Nusret Gökçe}}；IPA：{{IPA-tr|nusˈɾet ɟøcˈt͡ʃe|}})；{{bd|1983年|||}}），暱稱'''灑鹽寶貝'''（Salt Bae），[[土耳其|土耳其]][[库尔德人|库尔德人]]，為一名[[廚師|廚師]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ensonhaber.com/rudaw-nusretin-ailesiyle-roportaj-yapti.html|title=Rudaw, Nusret'in ailesiyle röportaj yaptı|website=Ensonhaber}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rudaw.net/turkish/lifestyle/11062017|title=Meşhur Nusret Kürt çıktı!|publisher=}}</ref>，也是土耳其{{le|牛排館|steak house}}「Nusr-Et」的擁有者。並在土耳其、[[杜拜|杜拜]]、[[阿布達比|阿布達比]]、[[邁阿密|邁阿密]]、[[紐約|紐約]]開設分店，由於他獨特的灑[[食鹽|鹽]]手勢而在[[網路爆紅事物|網路上爆紅]]<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.thewrap.com/who-the-hell-is-salt-bae/|title=Who the Hell is Salt Bae?|date=2017-02-06|newspaper=TheWrap|access-date=2017-02-24|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://ftw.usatoday.com/2017/02/simone-biles-salt-bae-laureus-sports-awards-food-seasoned-salted-video|title=Simone Biles happily had food seasoned by ‘the one and only’ Salt Bae at Laureus Awards|date=2017-02-15|publisher=|accessdate=2017-05-25}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://nymag.com/thecut/2017/01/everything-you-need-to-know-about-saltbae.html|title=Everything You Need to Know About #SaltBae|first=Jenni|last=Miller|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ettoday.net/news/20170210/863716.htm|title=叫他「灑鹽寶貝」！土耳其天菜主廚獨特手勢　女性嗨了|publisher=東森新聞雲|date=2017-02-10|accessdate=2018-01-20}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.ltn.com.tw/news/world/breakingnews/1960042|title=先別管好不好吃了！爆紅「灑鹽寶貝」 超性感手勢讓女網友為之瘋狂！|publisher=自由時報|date=2017-02-09|accessdate=2018-01-20}}</ref>。他也是一名專業[[屠夫|屠夫]]<ref>[http://www.miamiherald.com/entertainment/restaurants/article187900759.html ‘Salt Bae’ caught posing as Fidel Castro — and he just opened a Miami restaurant] CARLOS FRÍAS [[Miami_Herald|Miami Herald]] DECEMBER 03, 2017</ref>。

== 早年经历 ==
努斯雷特·戈克切出生于[[埃尔祖鲁姆|埃尔祖鲁姆]]。他的父亲是一名廚師。由于家庭经济困难，他在六年级时辍学，开始在[[卡德柯伊|卡德柯伊]]的屠夫那里当学徒。

=== 网络爆红 ===
在一段网络影片中，其烹调烤肉时，用手轻轻地撮一点盐，随后弓起手臂，将手里的盐顺着手肘滑落在烤肉上，这个独特的撒盐手法成为其网络爆红的起因。

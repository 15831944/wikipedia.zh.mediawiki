'''玻利維亞總統專機迫降事件'''，是指2013年7月2日[[玻利維亞|玻利維亞]]總統[[埃沃·莫拉萊斯|埃沃·莫拉萊斯]]专机在从[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]回国途中，因欧洲多国怀疑机上載有美國前[[中情局|中情局]]職員[[爱德华·斯诺登|爱德华·斯诺登]]而拒绝其进入领空，迫使专机最終於[[奧地利|奧地利]]迫降的事件。

2013年7月1日，到俄罗斯莫斯科参加[[天然气输出国论坛|天然气输出国论坛]]的[[玻利維亞|玻利維亞]]總統[[埃沃·莫拉萊斯|埃沃·莫拉萊斯]]在接受[[RT电视台|RT电视台]]採訪時，立場似乎傾向於为斯諾登提供[[政治庇護|政治庇護]]<ref name="emol-">{{Cite web|url=http://www.emol.com/noticias/internacional/2013/07/01/606824/evo-morales-se-abre-a-ceder-asilo-a-edward-snowden-si-lo-solicita.html|title=Evo Morales se abre a ceder asilo a Edward Snowden si lo solicita|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=El Mercurio On-Line|date=2013-07-01|work=El Mercurio On-Line|language=es|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7guwSjr?url=http://www.emol.com/noticias/internacional/2013/07/01/606824/evo-morales-se-abre-a-ceder-asilo-a-edward-snowden-si-lo-solicita.html|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref>。斯諾登是美國前[[中情局|中情局]]職員，他在2013年5月20日於[[香港|香港]]向傳媒披露[[美国国家安全局|美国国家安全局]]自2007年开始实施的[[网络监控|网络监控]]計劃“[[棱镜计划|棱镜计划]]”，后于6月23日飛赴莫斯科[[谢列梅捷沃国际机场|谢列梅捷沃国际机场]]，并在机场逗留。7月2日，莫拉萊斯的{{link-en|達索獵鷹 900|Dassault Falcon 900}}專機從莫斯科[[伏努科沃機場|伏努科沃機場]]起飛，计划返回玻利維亞，途中需经过多个欧洲国家的领空。但[[法國|法國]]、[[西班牙|西班牙]]和[[意大利|意大利]]三國因懷疑斯諾登在機上都拒絕该机進入其領空，最终迫使專機因缺乏燃油調頭飛往[[奧地利|奧地利]]維也納迫降。<ref name="sol.sapo-">{{cite web|url=http://sol.sapo.pt/inicio/Politica/Interior.aspx?content_id=79361|title=Portas: Portugal autorizou o sobrevoo de Morales|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=''Sol''-Autor Lusa|date=2013-07-09|work=Sol|language=pt|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140327080337/http://sol.sapo.pt/inicio/Politica/Interior.aspx?content_id=79361|archivedate=2014-03-27|deadurl=yes|df=}}</ref><ref name="bbc-morales-vienna">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-latin-america-23201767|title=Spain 'told Edward Snowden was on Bolivia president's plane'|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=British Broadcasting Corporation|date=2013-07-05|work=British Broadcasting Corporation|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7fAs1dm?url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-latin-america-23201767|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref>而事實上，斯諾登并不在专机上，自從抵達俄羅斯後一直待在谢列梅捷沃国际机场。

== 迫降后 ==
奧地利副總理{{link-en|米夏埃爾·施平德勒格|Michael Spindelegger}}称奥方曾經搜查專機，但被玻利維亞國防部長否認，稱莫拉萊斯拒絕他們進入飛機。<ref name="washingtonpost-morales-vienna-erzählungen">{{Cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/worldviews/wp/2013/07/03/evo-morales-controversial-flight-over-europe-minute-by-heavily-disputed-minute/|title=Evo Morales’s controversial flight over Europe, minute by heavily disputed minute|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=Max Fisher|date=2013-07-03|work=The Washington Post|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7eyoP8K?url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/worldviews/wp/2013/07/03/evo-morales-controversial-flight-over-europe-minute-by-heavily-disputed-minute/|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref><ref name="in.reuters-morales-vienna">{{cite web|url=http://in.reuters.com/article/2013/07/03/usa-security-snowden-jet-bolivia-idINDEE96207B20130703|title=Snowden still in Moscow despite Bolivian plane drama|accessdate=2014-03-16|date=2013-07-04|work=Reuters|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7egNjku?url=http://in.reuters.com/article/2013/07/03/usa-security-snowden-jet-bolivia-idINDEE96207B20130703|archivedate=2014-03-16|quote="Austrian Deputy Chancellor Michael Spindelegger said Morales personally denied that Snowden was aboard his jet and agreed to a voluntary inspection. "Based on this invitation from Bolivia, a colleague boarded the plane, looked at everything and there was no one else on board," Spindelegger told reporters. But Bolivian Defence Minister Ruben Saavedra said Morales' plane was not searched because Morales had refused Austrian authorities entry."|author1=Angelika Gruber|author2=Emma Farge|deadurl=yes|df=}}</ref> [[玻利維亞|玻利維亞]]、[[厄瓜多爾|厄瓜多爾]]等多个南美洲國家強烈譴責法國、西班牙和意大利因所謂「技術原因」拒絕專機進入領空，並指真正原因是美國傳播斯諾登藏身於機上的謠言。<ref name="bigstory.ap-morales-wien">{{cite web|url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/bolivian-leaders-plane-rerouted-snowden-fear|title=Bolivian leader's plane rerouted on Snowden fear|accessdate=2014-03-16|date=2013-07-03|work=The Big Story|publisher=Associated Press|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7f2Ugdx?url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/bolivian-leaders-plane-rerouted-snowden-fear|archivedate=2014-03-16|author1=Philipp-Moritz Jenne|author2=Carlos Valdez|deadurl=yes|df=}}</ref><ref name="edition.cnn-morales-wien">{{cite web|url=http://edition.cnn.com/2013/07/02/world/americas/bolivia-presidential-plane|title=Bolivia: Presidential plane forced to land after Snowden rumors|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=Catherine E. Shoichet|date=2013-07-03|work=Cable News Network|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7f6dyPw?url=http://edition.cnn.com/2013/07/02/world/americas/bolivia-presidential-plane|archivedate=2014-03-16|deadurl=yes|df=}}</ref>西班牙外交部長{{link-en|何塞·加西亞·馬加洛|José Manuel García-Margallo}}公開表示，他們被告知斯諾登在機上，但沒有具體說明是誰通知他們。<ref name="bbc-morales-vienna"></ref>

奧地利媒體後來報導称，當飛機降落在維也納加油時，美國駐奧地利大使{{link-en|威廉·伊科|William Eacho}}“非常肯定地聲稱斯諾登在機上”，並提到一張“要求引渡斯諾登的外交照會”。<ref name="rt-snowden-3countries-asylum">{{Cite web|url=http://rt.com/news/maduro-snowden-asylum-venezuela-723/|title=‘Free from imperial persecution’: Three Latin American countries offer shelter to Edward Snowden|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=Russia Today|date=2013-07-06|work=Russia Today|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7fE1feo?url=http://rt.com/news/maduro-snowden-asylum-venezuela-723/|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref>

奧地利總統[[海因茨·菲舍爾|海因茨·菲舍爾]]登上飛機迎接莫拉萊斯並與他共享早餐。在奧地利官員確認斯諾登不在機上後，飛機再次起飛。<ref name="theguardian-morales-complains-un">{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2013/jul/03/bolivia-un-evo-morales-plane|title=Bolivia complains to UN after Evo Morales' plane 'kidnapped'|accessdate=2014-03-16|date=2013-07-03|last=Sara Shahriari|work=The Guardian|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7fHczq2?url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2013/jul/03/bolivia-un-evo-morales-plane|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref>

== 事後 ==
[[File:Ban_Ki-moon_1-2.jpg|缩略图]]
事後法國立即為此事件道歉。<ref name="aljazeera-morales-vienna-france-apologieses">{{Cite web|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/europe/2013/07/2013747914179924.html|title=France apologises to Bolivia over jet row|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=Al Jazeera|date=2013-07-04|work=Al Jazeera|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7fPZHdM?url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/europe/2013/07/2013747914179924.html|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref>兩週後，西班牙駐玻利維亞大使以非正式的程序道歉。<ref name="ramos-2013">{{Cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/2013/07/16/us-usa-security-snowden-latinamerica-idUSBRE96F06020130716|title=Spain apologizes to Bolivia for presidential plane delay|accessdate=2014-03-16|date=2013-07-15|last=Daniel Ramos|work=Reuters|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7fW82zi?url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/07/16/us-usa-security-snowden-latinamerica-idUSBRE96F06020130716|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref>意大利和葡萄牙向玻利維亞政府發出正式解釋。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://atlasweb.it/2013/07/18/caso-snowden-morales-le-note-esplicative-di-italia-e-portogallo-579.html|title=Caso Snowden-Morales, le "note esplicative" di Italia e Portogallo|work=Atlas}}</ref>

7月3日，[[美國國務院|美國國務院]]發言人[[珍·莎琪|珍·莎琪]]承認，美國一直“與世界各地有可能讓斯諾登入境甚至過境”的國家接觸。<ref name="state-morales-us-press-briefing">{{Cite web|url=https://www.state.gov/r/pa/prs/dpb/2013/07/211535.htm#SNOWDEN|title=Daily Press Briefing - July 3, 2013|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=Jen Psaki|date=2013-07-03|work=state.gov|publisher=US Department of State|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7fjYVV3?url=http://www.state.gov/r/pa/prs/dpb/2013/07/211535.htm|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref><ref name="theguardian-morales-live">{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2013/jul/03/edward-snowden-asylum-live|title=US admits contact with other countries over potential Snowden flights – as it happened|accessdate=2014-03-16|date=2013-07-03|last=Oliver Laughland|work=The Guardian|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7g5a2YT?url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2013/jul/03/edward-snowden-asylum-live|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref>

在委內瑞拉總統[[尼古拉斯·马杜罗|馬杜羅]]請求飛越[[波多黎各|波多黎各]]的授權被推遲後，於9月20日，埃沃·莫拉萊斯宣布對美國政府提出[[反人類罪|反人類罪]]的訴訟，指控美國政府不斷阻礙總統專機跨越領空。馬杜羅當時正在前往北京與[[中華人民共和國|中華人民共和國]]進行雙邊會談。<ref name="live.huffingtonpost-morales-sue-us-gov">{{cite web|url=http://live.huffingtonpost.com/r/archive/segment/bolivian-president-to-sue-us-government-for-crimes-against-humanity/523c9e5e78c90a4e90000491|title=Bolivian President to sue U.S. Government for Crimes against Humanity|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=Huffington Post|date=2013-09-20|format=Video 2:25 Min|work=Huffington Post|language=}}</ref>

在事件發生後，七個拉丁美洲國家：[[玻利維亞|玻利維亞]]，[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]，[[古巴|古巴]]，[[厄瓜多爾|厄瓜多爾]]，[[尼加拉瓜|尼加拉瓜]]，[[烏拉圭|烏拉圭]]和[[委內瑞拉|委內瑞拉]]，向時任聯合國秘書長[[潘基文|潘基文]]表達了他們對事件的關注。潘基文指出：「[[國家元首|國家元首]]和他的飛機享有[[外交豁免權|外交豁免權]]和不可侵犯性。」<ref name="un">{{Cite web|url=https://www.un.org/apps/news/story.asp?NewsID=45374&Cr=Latin+America&Cr1=#.UhN673-_S28|title=Latin American nations voice concerns to Ban over rerouting of Bolivian leader's plane|accessdate=2014-03-16|author=United Nations|date=2013-07-09|work=United Nations|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6O7gd9D1i?url=http://www.un.org/apps/news/story.asp?NewsID=45374&Cr=Latin+America&Cr1=#.UhN673-_S28|archivedate=2014-03-16}}</ref>潘基文還強調，防止此類事件在未來再次發生非常重要。<ref name="un"></ref>

== 追究責任 ==
2015年4月，[[維基解密|維基解密]]創始人[[朱利安·阿桑奇|朱利安·阿桑奇]]承認他故意向美國洩露有關莫拉萊斯專機上載有美國前中情局職員愛德華·斯諾登的虛假信息，作為分散特工注意的“特別措施”。其後，玻利維亞駐俄羅斯大使要求阿桑奇為使得莫拉萊斯的生命處於危險之中而道歉。<ref name="assange">{{Cite web|url=http://www.telesurtv.net/english/news/Bolivia-Accuses-Assange-of-Putting-Evo-Morales-Life-at-Risk-20150413-0024.html|title=The Bolivian ambassador to Russia asked Julian Assange to apologize to the president for having put his life at risk in July 2013.|accessdate=2015-04-13|author=teleSUR / OA-gp|date=2015-04-13|work=teleSUR / OA-gp}}</ref>阿桑奇在2015年8月接受玻利維亞報紙El Deber采訪時說維基解密曾和委內瑞拉政府討論使用委內瑞拉或玻利維亞的總統專機上將斯諾登帶出俄羅斯。阿桑奇不知道玻利維亞政府是否知道這些談判，他本人也沒有與玻利維亞溝通，但他說委內瑞拉應該警告玻利維亞。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.eldeber.com.bo/especiales/julian-assange-wikileaks-estudio-maduro.html|title=Julian Assange: Wikileaks negoció con Maduro para que Snowden viaje con Evo.|accessdate=2017-11-14|date=2015-08-30|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150902010740/http://www.eldeber.com.bo/especiales/julian-assange-wikileaks-estudio-maduro.html|archivedate=2015-09-02}}</ref>

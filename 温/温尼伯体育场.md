'''温尼伯体育场'''（{{lang-en|Winnipeg Stadium}}）即'''投资人集团体育场'''（{{lang-en|Investors Group Field}}），是[[加拿大|加拿大]][[曼尼托巴省|曼尼托巴省]][[温尼伯|温尼伯]]的一个加式橄榄球场馆<ref>{{cite news |title = Blue Bombers to get new stadium and new owner |publisher = TSN |url = http://www.tsn.ca/cfl/story/?id=273524|accessdate=2014-06-01}}</ref>。该场馆于2013年开业，临近曼尼托巴大学校园的体育场<ref name="Ready for 2012">{{cite web|title=Winnipeg football stadium to be ready for 2012 season|url=http://www.journalofcommerce.com/article/id43587|publisher=Reed Business Information|accessdate=14 July 2013|author=Peter Caulfield|date=March 28, 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130503073826/http://www.journalofcommerce.com/article/id43587|archivedate=2013年5月3日}}</ref>。业主Triple B Stadium Inc.是温尼伯、曼尼托巴省、温尼伯橄榄球球俱乐部和曼尼托巴大学的财团。体育场是[[加拿大加式足球联盟|加拿大加式足球联盟]][[温尼伯蓝色轰炸机|温尼伯蓝色轰炸机]]的主场，也是曼尼托巴大学野牛足球队和温尼伯步枪队的主场<ref name="CBC Lawsuit">{{cite news |author = Sean Kavanagh |title = Lawsuit alleges Winnipeg's IGF stadium leaky, mouldy |publisher = CBC Manitoba|date = 5 March 2015 |url = http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/manitoba/lawsuit-alleges-winnipeg-s-igf-stadium-leaky-mouldy-1.2983608|accessdate=2015-03-05}}</ref>。该体育场可容纳33500人观赛（部分覆盖），包含波纹金属棚顶、餐厅、52间包间和星光大道等设施。

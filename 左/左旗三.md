
'''左旗三'''（'''天箭座δ'''/'''δ Sge'''）是一个位于[[天箭座|天箭座]]的[[双星|双星]]，[[视星等|视星等]]3.68。主星是个红色的M型[[亮巨星|亮巨星]]，伴星是[[B型主序星|B型主序星]]。基于[[视差|视差]]，它距离地球大约590[[光年|光年]]。

左旗三是一个[[光谱联星|光谱联星]]，[[轨道周期|轨道周期]]大约10年，[[偏心率|偏心率]]0.44。

左旗三以相对太阳9.8km/s的速度在银河系里移动。它的轨道让它距离银心23,800到35,300光年。

'''Delta Sagittae''' ('''Delta Sge''', '''δ Sagittae''', '''δ Sge''') is a [[聯星|聯星]] in the constellation of [[天箭座|天箭座]], with an [[视星等|视星等]] of +3.68. The primary component is a red [[恒星光谱|恒星光谱]] [[亮巨星|亮巨星]], and the secondary is a [[B型主序星|B型主序星]].<ref name=SIMBAD/> It is approximately 590 [[光年|光年]] from [[地球|地球]], based on its [[恆星視差|恆星視差]].<ref name=HIP/>

Delta Sagittae is a [[聯星|聯星]] with a composite spectrum, meaning that light from both stars can be detected. It has an [[轨道周期|轨道周期]] of about 10 years and an [[軌道離心率|軌道離心率]] of about 0.44.<ref name=Eaton/>

Delta Sagittae is moving through the Galaxy at a speed of 9.8 km/s relative to the Sun. Its projected Galactic orbit carries it between 23,800 and 35,300 light years from the center of the Galaxy.<ref name=astrostudio>[http://www.astrostudio.org/xhip.php?hip=97365 Delta Sagittae (HIP 97365)]</ref>

'''群体免疫'''（{{lang-en|herd immunity}}或{{lang|en|community immunity}}）是指人或动物群体中的很大一部分因[[接种疫苗|接种疫苗]]而获得[[免疫力|免疫力]]，使得其他没有免疫力的个体因此受到保护而不被传染。<ref>{{cite journal |author=John TJ, Samuel R |title=Herd immunity and herd effect: new insights and definitions |journal=Eur. J. Epidemiol. |volume=16 |issue=7 |pages=601–6 |year=2000 |pmid=11078115| doi = 10.1023/A:1007626510002}}</ref>群体免疫理论表明，当群体中有大量个体对某一[[传染病|传染病]]免疫或[[易感个体|易感个体]]很少时，那些在个体之间传播的传染病的[[感染|感染]]链便会被中断。拥有抵抗力的个体的比例越高，易感个体与受感染个体间接触的可能性便越小。<ref name=Smallpox>[http://www.bt.cdc.gov/agent/smallpox/training/overview/pdf/eradicationhistory.pdf  History and Epidemiology of Global Smallpox Eradication] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070715234749/http://www.bt.cdc.gov/agent/smallpox/training/overview/pdf/eradicationhistory.pdf |date=2007-07-15 }} From the training course titled "Smallpox: Disease, Prevention, and Intervention". The CDC and the [[World_Health_Organization|World Health Organization]]. Slide 16-17.</ref>

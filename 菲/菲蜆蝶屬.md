'''菲蜆蝶屬'''（學名：'''''Pheles'''''）是[[蜆蝶亞科|蜆蝶亞科]][[蜆蝶族|蜆蝶族]]裡的一個[[屬|屬]]。共有8個物種，分佈於[[新熱帶界|新熱帶界]]<ref>Tree of Life Web Project. 2008. Pheles Herrich-Schaeffer 1853. Lepricornis C. Felder & R. Felder 1865 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym, Otacustesis Dyar 1914 currently viewed as a subjective junior synonym. Version 05 June 2008 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Pheles/104662/2008.06.05 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。外型[[擬態|擬態]]模仿各種[[綃蝶族|綃蝶]]。

== 物種 ==
* [[無色蕾蜆蝶|無色蕾蜆蝶]] ''Pheles atricolor''
* [[雙色蕾蜆蝶|雙色蕾蜆蝶]] ''Pheles bicolor''
* ''Pheles eulesca''
* [[菲蜆蝶|菲蜆蝶]] ''Pheles heliconides''
* [[雲彩蕾蜆蝶|雲彩蕾蜆蝶]] ''Pheles incerta''
* [[黑紋蕾蜆蝶|黑紋蕾蜆蝶]] ''Pheles melanochroia'' = 單色黑紋蜆蝶 ''M. unicolor''
* [[赭色蕾蜆蝶|赭色蕾蜆蝶]] ''Pheles ochracea'' = 赭斑蜆蝶
* [[條紋蕾蜆蝶|條紋蕾蜆蝶]] ''Pheles strigosus''
** 指名亞種 ''P. s. strigosa''

*** = 毛短尾蜆蝶 ''B. trichroma''

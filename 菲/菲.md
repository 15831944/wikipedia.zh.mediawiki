'''菲'''旧称'''芠'''（[[分子式|分子式]]：C<sub>14</sub>H<sub>10</sub>），是一个[[多环芳香烃|多环芳香烃]]，由三个[[苯|苯]]环稠合而成，与[[蒽|蒽]]为[[同分异构体|同分异构体]]。无色片状结晶，有蓝色[[螢光|螢光]]，易[[昇华|昇华]]，具刺激性。存在于吸烟时的烟雾中。[[折射率|折射率]](n<sub>D</sub><sup>20</sup>)为1.6415。

== 化学性质 ==

菲的[[化学性质|化学性质]]介于[[萘|萘]]和[[蒽|蒽]]之间。反应主要在9和10位上。可以被[[铬酸|铬酸]][[氧化|氧化]]生成[[菲醌|菲醌]]<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Ray Wendland, John LaLonde | title = Phenanthrenequinone | collvol = 4 | collvolpages = 757 | year = 1963 | prep = http://www.orgsynth.org/orgsyn/pdfs/CV4P0757.pdf }}</ref>，被[[氢气|氢气]]（与[[雷尼镍|雷尼镍]]）还原为9,10-二氢菲<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Donald D. Phillips | title = 9,10-Dihydrophenanthrene | collvol = 4 | collvolpages = 313 | year = 1963 | prep = http://www.orgsynth.org/orgsyn/pdfs/CV4P0313.pdf}}</ref>，发生[[臭氧化反应|臭氧化反应]]生成2-[[联苯|联苯]]醛的衍生物<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Philip S. Bailey, Ronald E. Erickson1. | title = Diphenaldehyde | collvol = 5 | collvolpages = 489 | year = 1973 | prep = http://www.orgsynth.org/orgsyn/pdfs/CV5P0489.pdf }}</ref>，也可发生[[溴化|溴化]]<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Clinton A. Dornfeld, Joseph E. Callen, George H. Coleman | title = 9-bromophenanthrene | collvol = 3 | collvolpages = 134 | year = 1955 | prep = http://www.orgsynth.org/orgsyn/pdfs/CV3P0134.pdf}}</ref> 和[[磺化|磺化]]<ref>{{OrgSynth | author = Louis F. Fieser | title = 2- and 3-phenanthrenesulfonic acids | collvol = 2 | collvolpages = 482 | year = 1943 | prep = http://www.orgsynth.org/orgsyn/pdfs/CV2P0482.pdf}}</ref> 等[[取代反应|取代反应]]。

== 制备及应用 ==

由[[煤焦油|煤焦油]]的[[蒽油|蒽油]][[馏分|馏分]]中分离。也可由某些[[二苯乙烯|二苯乙烯]]衍生物受光环化得到。用作[[有机合成|有机合成]]原料，用于合成[[农药|农药]]、[[合成树脂|合成树脂]]、[[染料|染料]]、[[医药|医药]]、[[防霉剂|防霉剂]]、[[鞣革剂|鞣革剂]]等，还可作为[[炸药|炸药]]的[[稳定剂|稳定剂]]。

一个合成菲的人名反应是“'''Bardhan-Sengupta菲合成'''”：<ref>J. C. Bardhan and S. C. Sengupta, ''J. Chem. Soc.'' '''1932''', 2520, 2798.</ref><ref>R. P. Linstead, ''Ann. Repts.'' (''Chem. Soc.'', London) 33, 319 (1936)</ref><ref>L. F. Fieser and M. Fieser, ''Natural Products Related to Phenanthrene'' (New York, 1949), p 88.</ref><ref>W. B. Renfrow ''et al.'', ''[[J._Am._Chem._Soc.|J. Am. Chem. Soc.]]'' 73, 317 (1951).</ref>

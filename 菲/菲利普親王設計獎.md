'''菲利浦親王設計獎'''，是一個在[[英國|英國]]年度舉辦的設計獎項，素有英國設計奧斯卡之稱。該獎項開始於1959年，是英國歷史最悠久的設計大獎。主持人和獎項提供者，都是英國愛丁堡公爵[[菲利普亲王_(爱丁堡公爵)|菲利普親王]]，獎項以[[產品設計|產品設計]]為主，頒發給該年度最出色，美觀的產品<ref name="longest">{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/life-style/house-and-home/brompton-bike-creator-wins-uks-longestrunning-design-award-1804152.html|title=Brompton bike creator wins UK's longest-running design award|work=Independent|date=16 October 2009|accessdate=2009-10-20|quote=Andrew Ritchie was named the winner of the 50th Prince Philip Design Award}}</ref><ref name="bbc-fold-up">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/uk/8309975.stm|title=Royal award for fold-up bike man|work=[[BBC_News_Online|BBC News Online]]|date=2009-10-16|accessdate=2009-10-20|quote=The Duke set up the prize, run by the Design Council, in 1959}}</ref>。獲得該獎的設計師，經常視該獎為個人設計生涯上的重大里程碑<ref name="dc-2005">{{cite web|url=http://www.designcouncil.org.uk/Design-Council/2/Press/Prince-Philip-Designers-Prize-goes-to-zero-hero-Max-Fordham/|title=Notes to editors|date=2008-11-12|accessdate=2009-10-20|publisher=Design Council|work=Prince Philip Designers Prize goes to ‘zero hero’ Max Fordham|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://webarchive.nationalarchives.gov.uk/20091115082358/http://www.designcouncil.org.uk/Design-Council/2/Press/Prince-Philip-Designers-Prize-goes-to-zero-hero-Max-Fordham/|archivedate=2009-11-15}} </ref>。

'''井上康生'''（{{bd|1978年|5月15日|}}）是一名日本男子柔道运动员。他在2000年悉尼夏季奥林匹克运动会中，参加了男子柔道比赛并获得100公斤级金牌。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.sky.com/skynews/Home/UK-News/Japans-Judo-Star-Kosei-Inoue-Moves-To-Edinburgh-To-Enjoy-The-Quiet-Life/Article/200903115236375?lpos=UK_News_Second_Home_Page_Feature_Teaser_Region_0&lid=ARTICLE_15236375_Japans_Judo_Star_Kosei_Inoue_Moves_To_Edinburgh_To_Enjoy_The_Quiet_Life |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2009-03-08 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090321164100/http://news.sky.com/skynews/Home/UK-News/Japans-Judo-Star-Kosei-Inoue-Moves-To-Edinburgh-To-Enjoy-The-Quiet-Life/Article/200903115236375?lpos=UK_News_Second_Home_Page_Feature_Teaser_Region_0&lid=ARTICLE_15236375_Japans_Judo_Star_Kosei_Inoue_Moves_To_Edinburgh_To_Enjoy_The_Quiet_Life |archivedate=2009-03-21 }}</ref>

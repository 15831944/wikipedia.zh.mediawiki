'''包斯卡城堡'''（{{Lang-lv|Bauskas pils}}；{{lang-de|Schloss Bauske}}）是[[拉脫維亞|拉脫維亞]]的一個城堡遺跡建築群，位於[[包斯卡|包斯卡]]。包斯卡城堡地處兩座河流的匯合處，這裡最初的城堡修建於1443年至1456年之間<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=tv0VAQAAIAAJ&dq=bauska+castle+1443+1456&focus=searchwithinvolume&q=1456|title=Castles and manors of Latvia|last=Zarāns|first=Alberts|date=2006|publisher=A. Zarāns|year=|isbn=9789984785059|location=|pages=80|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=df8WAQAAIAAJ&dq=bauska+castle+1443&focus=searchwithinvolume&q=1443|title=Castles around the Baltic Sea: the illustrated guide|last=Kjaergaard|first=Thorkild|date=1994|publisher=Castle Museum|year=|isbn=9788386206032|location=|pages=77|language=en}}</ref>。大北方戰爭期間，城堡被撤退的俄羅斯人拆除<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=tv0VAQAAIAAJ&dq=bauska+castle+1706&focus=searchwithinvolume&q=1706|title=Castles and manors of Latvia|last=Zarāns|first=Alberts|date=2006|publisher=A. Zarāns|year=|isbn=9789984785059|location=|pages=80|language=en}}</ref>。現在包斯卡城堡的城堡部分雖然只有遺跡，但宮殿建築部分則得到修復<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=98em68M_SmsC&pg=PA190|title=Latvia|last=Baister|first=Stephen|last2=Patrick|first2=Chris|date=2007|publisher=Bradt Travel Guides|year=|isbn=9781841622019|location=|pages=190|language=en}}</ref>。

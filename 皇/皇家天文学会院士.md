'''皇家天文学会院士''' （'''FRAS'''）是由[[英国皇家天文学会|皇家天文学会]]（RAS）批准授予给个人的荣誉学术称号<ref name=fras>{{cite web|url=https://www.ras.org.uk/membership|website=ras.org.uk|author=Anon|location=London|year=2016|title=Membership and fellowship of the Royal Astronomical Society|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161106015602/https://www.ras.org.uk/membership|archivedate=2016-11-06|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。

==院士人数==
截至2016年，共约有3800名院士<ref name=yvonne>{{cite web|url=https://www.ras.org.uk/news-and-press/news-archive/259-news-2015/2634-prof-yvonne-elsworth-elected-to-fellowship-of-the-royal-society|title=Yvonne Elsworth (FRAS) elected a Fellow of the Royal Society (FRS)|website=ras.org.uk|author=Anon|year=2015}}</ref>， 其中大约一半为[[哲学博士|博士级]]专业科学家，四分之一是[[学士后|学士后]]研究生或退休科学家、业余科学家和[[本科教育|本科生]]<ref name=fras/>。大约三分之一的院士来自[[英国|英国]]本土以外<ref name=fras/>.院士享有使用FRAS[[勋衔|勋衔]]的资格<ref name=fras/>。

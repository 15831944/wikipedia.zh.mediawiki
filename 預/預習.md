'''預習'''或者'''預覽'''、'''瀏覽'''指的是快速、大略讀過一篇文章，以掌握文章的核心思想，之後才開始完整的、仔細地將文章從頭到尾讀過。
<ref name="Nordquist 2011">{{cite web | last=Nordquist | first=Richard | title=The Process of Prereading | website=ThoughtCo | date=2011-04-18 | url=https://www.thoughtco.com/prereading-definition-1691529 | access-date=2018-08-25 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180414113103/https://www.thoughtco.com/prereading-definition-1691529 | archive-date=2018-04-14 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Study Guides and Strategies">{{cite web | title=Prereading strategies | website=Study Guides and Strategies | url=http://www.studygs.net/preread.htm | access-date=2018-08-25 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180120035452/http://www.studygs.net/preread.htm | archive-date=2018-01-20 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Salvia 2013 p. ">{{cite book | last=Salvia | first=John | title=Assessment in special and inclusive education | publisher=Wadsworth/Cencage Learning | publication-place=Belmont, CA | year=2013 | isbn=1-111-83341-9 | oclc=778371151 | page=}}</ref>

預習能在仔細[[閱讀|閱讀]]一篇文章前先了解文章的大綱，並有助於增加稍後仔細閱讀時的閱讀速度和效率。預習通常包含閱讀文章的標題、讀完標題後思考關於文章標題的事情、章節介紹、摘要、小標題、小小標題、問題探討、掌握點出誰、為什麼、哪裡、什麼時候 的敘述和結論等。<ref name="Nordquist 2011"/><ref name="Center">{{cite web | author=Center for Open Educational Resources and Language Learning (COERLL) | title=Lesson 2: From Pre-Reading to Intitial Reading | website=coerll.utexas.edu | url=https://coerll.utexas.edu/methods/modules/reading/02/prereading.php | access-date=2018-08-25 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170728235417/http://coerll.utexas.edu/methods/modules/reading/02/prereading.php | archive-date=2017-07-28 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

'''路易-塞巴斯蒂安·雷诺曼'''（{{lang-fr|Louis-Sébastien Lenormand}}，{{bd|1757年|5月25日|1837年|4月4日}}<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://data.bnf.fr/ark:/12148/cb12536073k|title=Louis-Sébastien Le Normand (1757-1837) - Author - Resources from the BnF|website=data.bnf.fr|access-date=2016-03-21}}</ref>）是[[法国|法国]][[化学家|化学家]]、[[物理学家|物理学家]]、[[发明家|发明家]]<ref>{{cite web |title=Louis-Sébastien Lenormand, le premier parachutiste et base jumper |url=http://www.laboiteverte.fr/louis-sebastien-lenormand-premier-parachutiste-base-jumper/|publisher=La boite verte |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170904234107/http://www.laboiteverte.fr/louis-sebastien-lenormand-premier-parachutiste-base-jumper/ |archivedate=2017-09-04 |language=fr}}</ref>以及[[跳伞|跳伞]]运动的先驱<ref>{{cite book |author=Louis Guilbert |title=La Formation du vocabulaire de l'aviation |date=1965 |publisher=Larousse (Mayenne, impr. Floch)|url=https://books.google.com/books?vid=0NVuXpcSICdhtZVdjo&id=FUMTAAAAMAAJ&q=sebastien+lenormand&dq=sebastien+lenormand&pgis=1|language=fr}}</ref>。
==生平==
雷诺曼是当时法兰西“特殊手艺”的杰出代表，他用通俗的观点向公众解释工业艺术，致力于普及通用技术教育<ref>{{cite journal |author=Joost Mertens |title=Technology as the science of the industrial arts: Louis-Sébastien Lenormand (1757-1837) and the popularization of technology |journal=History and Technology |date=2002 |volume=18 |issue=3 |pages=203-231 |doi=10.1080/0734151022000034125 |language=en}}{{Closed access}}</ref>。他曾担任[[巴黎國立高等音樂舞蹈學院|法兰西国立工艺院]]的技工教授<ref>{{cite book |author=Garrett Soden |title=Defying Gravity: Land Divers, Roller Coasters, Gravity Bums, and the Human Obsession with Falling |date=2005 |publisher=W. W. Norton & Company |isbn=9780393326567 |page=20 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=B4K0rfx_E_kC&lpg=PA20&ots=BijjUCnnPL&dq=Louis-Sebastien%20Lenormand&hl=zh-CN&pg=PA20#v=onepage&q=Louis-Sebastien%20Lenormand&f=false |language=en}}</ref>。
===跳伞===
雷诺曼利用木制框架和14[[英尺|英尺]]长的布制作了一种简单的降落装置。1783年12月末<ref>{{cite book |author=Raymond Fontaine |title=La Manche en ballon: Blanchard contre Pilâtre de Rozier |date=1982 |publisher=Westhoek-Éditions |pages=155-156 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=zn5GAAAAYAAJ |language=fr}}</ref>，他在[[蒙彼利埃|蒙彼利埃]]天文台进行了实验，最终安全着陆。他也利用两个[[词根|词根]]创造了“parachute”一词，用来表示这种降落时的保护装置<ref>{{cite book |author1=Carla Mooney |title=Skydiving |date=2015 |publisher=The Rosen Publishing Group |isbn=9781499435788 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=k0dgDwAAQBAJ&lpg=PA8&dq=Louis-S%C3%A9bastien%20Lenormand&hl=zh-CN&pg=PA8#v=onepage&q=Louis-S%C3%A9bastien%20Lenormand&f=false |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |author=Anita Yasuda |title=EXPLORE FLIGHT!: WITH 25 GREAT PROJECTS |date=2014 |publisher=Nomad Press |isbn=9781619301788 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Jy70BQAAQBAJ&lpg=PT53&dq=Louis-S%C3%A9bastien%20Lenormand&hl=zh-CN&pg=PT53#v=onepage&q=Louis-S%C3%A9bastien%20Lenormand&f=false |language=en}}</ref>。

'''犀貘'''（[[學名|學名]]''Heptodon''）是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[沼貘科|沼貘科]]。牠們生存於[[始新世|始新世]]的[[北美洲|北美洲]]。<ref>[http://paleodb.org/cgi-bin/bridge.pl?action=checkTaxonInfo&taxon_no=43095&is_real_user=1 PaleoBiology Database]</ref>

==特徵==
犀貘約長1米，外觀像現今的[[貘|貘]]。從其[[頭顱骨|頭顱骨]]的形狀估計牠們沒有貘的長鼻，但可能有稍長的上唇。<ref name=EoDP>{{cite book en|editor=Palmer, D.|year=1999 |title= The Marshall Illustrated Encyclopedia of Dinosaurs and Prehistoric Animals|publisher= Marshall Editions|location=London|page=261|id=ISBN 1-84028-152-9}}</ref>從其標本估計牠們重15.5公斤。<ref>{{Cite journal en|author=M. Mendoza, C. M. Janis, and P. Palmqvist|year=2006|title=Estimating the body mass of extinct ungulates: a study on the use of multiple regression|journal=Journal of Zoology|volume=270}}</ref>

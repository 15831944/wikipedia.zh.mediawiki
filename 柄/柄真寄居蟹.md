'''柄真寄居蟹'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|''Dardanus pedunculatus''}}）为[[寄居蟹|寄居蟹]]的一種。分布于[[印度洋|印度洋]][[海域|海域]]，包含了[[夏威夷|夏威夷]]、[[日本|日本]]和[[澳洲|澳洲]]<ref>{{cite journal |author=Dwi Listyo Rahayu |year=2000 |title=Hermit crabs from the South China Sea (Crustacea: Decapoda: Anomura: Diogenidae, Paguridae, Parapaguridae) |journal=[[Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |volume=Suppl. 8 |pages=377–404 |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s8/s08rbz377-404.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]]}}</ref>。

柄真寄居蟹會與[[海葵|海葵]][[共生|共生]]，特別是[[螅形美麗海葵|螅形美麗海葵]]。柄真寄居蟹是常被養來當[[寵物|寵物]]的寄居蟹之一<ref name="Debelius">{{cite book |author=Helmut Debelius |year=1994 |title=Marine atlas: The joint aquarium care of invertebrates and tropical marine fishes, Volume 1 |publisher=[[Steven_Simpson_Books|Steven Simpson Books]] |isbn=9783882440515 |chapter=''Dardanus pedunculatus'' |page=606 |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=EOY28s5U9ukC}}</ref>。

'''棕狐蝠'''（''Pteropus brunneus''）是一種已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[狐蝠科|狐蝠科]]。牠們是[[澳洲|澳洲]][[麥凱|麥凱]]東南岸對出的海島上的[[特有種|特有種]]。<ref>{{Cite web|author=Environmental Protection Agency, Queensland Government|url=http://www.epa.qld.gov.au/nature_conservation/wildlife/living_with_wildlife/flyingfoxes/|year=2008|title=Flying-foxes|accessdate=2009-02-09|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090120074133/http://www.epa.qld.gov.au/nature_conservation/wildlife/living_with_wildlife/flyingfoxes/|archivedate=2009-01-20}}</ref>牠們只有一個於1859年採集的標本，自此以後就沒有再發現牠們。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.bathouses.com/dusky_flying_fox.htm|author=BatHouses.com|title=Dusky Flying Fox: Extinct?|accessdate=2009-02-09}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.amonline.net.au/bats/records/bat8.htm|author=Australian Museum|year=1999|title=Bats in Australia: Percy Island Flying-fox|accessdate=2009-02-09}}</ref>這個標本現正存放在[[倫敦|倫敦]]的[[自然歷史博物館|自然歷史博物館]]。到了20世紀末，牠們才被認為是獨立的[[物種|物種]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/threatened/publications/action/bats/30.html|author=Australian Government|work=Environment Australia|year=1999|title=The Action Plan for Australian Bats|accessdate=2009-02-09}}</ref>

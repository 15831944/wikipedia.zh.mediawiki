'''僵人综合征'''，也作'''僵体症候群'''（{{lang-en|stiff person syndrome}} 或 {{lang|en|stiff man syndrome}}<ref name="DarrasJr.2014">{{cite book|last1=Darras|first1=Basil T.|last2=Jr.|first2=H. Royden Jones, Jr.|last3=Ryan|first3=Monique M.|title=Neuromuscular Disorders of Infancy, Childhood, and Adolescence|accessdate=|date=3 December 2014|publisher=Elsevier Science|language=en|isbn=9780124171275|page=188|quote=The stiff-man syndrome (SMS, also known as stiff-person syndrome) is a rare central nervous system autoimmune disease, but is likely underrecognized.}}</ref>）是一种罕见的[[神經內科|神经]]失调症状<ref>{{cite web|url=https://gene.hpa.gov.tw/index.php?mob=1&mo=DiseasePaper&action=paper1_show&cate=Set1&csn=77&sn=614|website=gene.hpa.gov.tw|publisher=卫生福利部国民健康署|accessdate=2017-07-12|language=zh-tw|date=2014-12-29|title=Stiffperson Syndrome|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170128102418/https://gene.hpa.gov.tw/index.php?mob=1|archive-date=2017-01-28|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，病因不明，病人临床表现为逐渐开始变得僵硬，肌肉强直等。僵人综合症主要影响[[軀幹|躯干肌肉]]，此外还可能伴有[[痉挛|痉挛]]，会导致姿势畸形。慢性疼痛、机动性差、[[脊柱前凸|脊柱前凸]]是常见的症状。

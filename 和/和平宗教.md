'''和平宗教'''（{{lang-en|religion of peace}}），是一个在政治上用以解释[[伊斯兰教|伊斯兰教]]的[[新词|新词]]。在2001年[[九一一袭击事件|九一一袭击事件]]发生后，一些诸如马来西亚第四任首相[[马哈迪·莫哈末|马哈迪·莫哈末]]在内的政治家为了将温和的[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]与[[伊斯蘭恐怖主義|伊斯兰恐怖分子]]区别开来而将伊斯兰教描述为“和平宗教”。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://asiasociety.org/islam-terrorism-and-malaysias-response|title=Islam, Terrorism and Malaysia's Response|date=2002-02-04|website=[[亚洲协会|亚洲协会]]|access-date=2017-07-31}}</ref>在[[阿拉伯语|阿拉伯语]]中，“[[伊斯兰|伊斯兰]]”一词与意为“[[和平|和平]]”的词语“{{le|萨拉姆 (消歧义)|Salaam|萨拉姆}}”（音译，另译“色兰”）在[[词源学|词源学]]上有关联。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,175987,00.html|title=The True, Peaceful Face Of Islam|date=2001-09-23|work=Time|accessdate=2017-07-22|quote=The very word Islam, which means "surrender," is related to the Arabic salam, or peace.|last1=Armstrong|first1=Karen}}</ref>

== 使用 ==

过去，“和平宗教”的表述适用于[[基督教|基督教]]。<ref>''Defensive War not Inconsistent with a Religion of Peace'', a cermon, Seeley, Jackson, & Halliday, 1860, [https://books.google.com/books?id=al1u2i6q5aQC&pg=PA11 p. 11] Quote: "While however it is manifest and unquestionable that Christianity is emphatically a religion of peace and good will towards men — men of all countries and of all climes  — it is assuredly not a religion of absolute and passive non-resistance."</ref>

2001年9月，在[[九一一袭击事件|九一一袭击事件]]发生后，美国总统[[乔治·W·布什|乔治·W·布什]]谈到：“恐怖的一面不是真正的伊斯兰信仰。这不是伊斯兰教所关心的。伊斯兰教是和平的。这些恐怖分子并不代表和平。他们代表邪恶与战争。”<ref>{{cite press release|title="Islam is Peace" Says President|publisher=Office of the Press Secretary|date=2001-09-17|url=http://georgewbush-whitehouse.archives.gov/news/releases/2001/09/20010917-11.html|accessdate=2007-11-22}}</ref>该论点引起了一些人的批评<ref name="SkepReview">{{cite web|url=http://www.infidels.org/library/magazines/tsr/2001/6/016front.html|title=The Real Culprit|accessdate=2007-11-22|date=November 2001|last=Till|first=Farrell|publisher=The Skeptical Review|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/66GYIvd3A?url=http://www.theskepticalreview.com/tsrmag/016front.html|archivedate=2012-03-18}}</ref>，2002年美国[[福音主義|福音派]][[新教|新教]]领袖的民意调查显示只有10%的人同意布什将伊斯兰视为和平代名词的说法。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.beliefnet.com/story/124/story_12447_1.html|title=Evangelical Views of Islam|accessdate=2007-11-22|date=2003-04-07|last=Green|first=John|publisher=EPPC and beliefnet}}</ref>

马来西亚前首相[[马哈迪·莫哈末|马哈迪·莫哈末]]在2002年谈到：“正如我所说的那样，伊斯兰教是一个和平的宗教。然而，几个世纪以来，偏离伊斯兰教真正教义的行为时有发生。因此，尽管他们的教义禁止杀戮特别是对无辜人民的杀戮，但（所谓的）穆斯林还是在进行着杀戮。
<ref name="MALAY">{{cite web|url=http://asiasociety.org/islam-terrorism-and-malaysias-response?page=0,1|title=Islam, Terrorism, and Malaysia's Response (page 2)|date=2002-02-04|website=Asia Society}}</ref>

[[巴黎大清真寺|巴黎大清真寺]][[穆夫提|穆夫提]]{{le|达利勒·博巴克尔|Dalil Boubakeur}}在2006年谈到：“[[穆罕默德|先知]]没有创立一个恐怖主义的宗教，而是一个和平的宗教。”<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.iht.com/articles/2006/02/02/news/toon.php|title=Prophet cartoons enraging Muslims|accessdate=2007-11-22|date=2006-02-02|publisher=[[國際紐約時報|國際紐約時報]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060204165912/http://www.iht.com/articles/2006/02/02/news/toon.php|archive-date=2006-02-04|deadurl=yes}}</ref>

== 批判 ==
[[File:2012_Sydney_protest.jpg|thumb]]
{{le|谢尔曼·杰克逊|Sherman Jackson}}认为，西方政客关于伊斯兰教是和平宗教的评论是对[[政治正確|政治正确性]]的尝试。然而，杰克逊声称，这一表达并不意味着拒绝{{le|伊斯兰教和暴力|Islam and violence|伊斯兰的暴力行为}}，而是承认与非穆斯林{{le|和平共存|peaceful coexistence}}。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Jihad and the Modern World|url=http://users.tpg.com.au/dezhen/jihad_and_the_modern_world.html|last=Jackson|first=Sherman|date=Spring–Summer 2002|journal=Journal of Islamic Law and Culture|accessdate=2007-11-22|quote="Religion of peace" does not imply that Islam is a paciﬁst religion, that it rejects the use of violence altogether, as either a moral or a metaphysical evil. "Religion of peace" connotes, rather, that Islam can countenance a state of permanent, peaceful coexistence with other nations and peoples who are not Muslims...This position, I shall argue, is no more than the result of an objective application of principles of Islamic jurisprudence which no jurist or activist, medieval or modern, has claimed to reject.|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071217162911/http://users.tpg.com.au/dezhen/jihad_and_the_modern_world.html|archivedate=2007-12-17}}</ref>

“和平宗教”这一术语也被{{le|对伊斯兰教的批判|Criticism of Islam|伊斯兰教批判者}}使用，例如右翼评论家[[安·庫爾特|安·庫爾特]]。<ref name="counterp">{{cite web|url=http://www.counterpunch.org/siddiqi06052003.html|title=Ann Coulter's Foul Mouth: The Blond Hate Machine|accessdate=2007-11-22|date=2003-06-05|last=Siddiqi|first=Imraan|publisher=Counterpunch|quote=Ann on the other hand, apparently missed this Sunday school lesson, and continues to ridicule Islam sarcastically as the "religion of peace" whenever a negative story arises within the Muslim world.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20030613090741/http://counterpunch.org/siddiqi06052003.html|archive-date=2003-06-13}}</ref>哲学家与[[新無神論|新無神論]]作家[[山姆·哈里斯|山姆·哈里斯]]写道：“穆斯林社区面对所有挑衅的立场几乎都是：伊斯兰教是和平的宗教，如果你说不是，我们就会杀了你。”<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/sam-harris/losing-our-spines-to-save_b_100132.html|title=Losing Our Spines to Save Our Necks|date=2008-05-05|last=Harris|first=Sam|website=Huffingtonpost.com|access-date=2011-03-19}} (updated 25 May 2011)</ref>

2005年，在被记者问及伊斯兰教是否是和平宗教时，[[本篤十六世|本篤十六世]]提到伊斯兰教的一些因素可以促成和平，但也有其他的因素。 
<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.catholicnewsagency.com/news/pope_says_terror_attacks_cannot_be_defined_as_antichristian/|title=Pope says terror attacks cannot be defined as anti-Christian|date=2005-07-25|work=Catholic News Agency|accessdate=2016-02-25|quote=I would not like to use big words to apply generic labels. It certainly contains elements that can favor peace, it also has other elements: we must always seek the best elements.}}
</ref>

有影响力的[[伊斯兰主义|伊斯兰主义者]][[赛义德·库特布|赛义德·库特布]]写道，伊斯兰教在所有人类顺从于[[安拉|安拉]]的意义上是和平的宗教。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://www.islamqa.com/index.php?ref=43087&ln=eng|title=Fiqh al-Da’wah|last=Qutb|first=Sayyid|authorlink=Sayyid Qutb|work=Fiqh al-Da’wah|publisher={{tsl|en|IslamQA}}|accessdate=2007-11-22|pages=217–222|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061017053855/http://www.islamqa.com/index.php?ref=43087&ln=eng|archivedate=2006-10-17|quote=The defeatists should fear Allah lest they distort this religion and cause it to become weak on the basis of the claim that it is a religion of peace. Yes, it is the religion of peace but in the sense of saving all of mankind from worshiping anything other than Allah and submitting all of mankind to the rule of Allah.}}</ref>

2015年5月13日，[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]发布了[[阿布·贝克尔·巴格达迪|阿布·贝克尔·巴格达迪]]的谈话录音，他声称伊斯兰教不是和平的宗教，而是斗争的宗教。
<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.christianpost.com/news/isis-caliph-baghdadi-in-new-audio-message-islam-was-never-a-religion-of-peace-our-jihad-is-the-war-of-all-muslims-139166/|title=ISIS Caliph Baghdadi in New Audio Message: 'Islam Was Never a Religion of Peace,' Our Jihad Is 'The War of All Muslims'|date=2015-05-15|work=The Christian Post|accessdate=2015-05-16|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150517001734/http://www.christianpost.com/news/isis-caliph-baghdadi-in-new-audio-message-islam-was-never-a-religion-of-peace-our-jihad-is-the-war-of-all-muslims-139166/|archivedate=2015-05-17|quote=Islam was never a religion of peace. Islam is the religion of fighting. No one should believe that the war that we are waging is the war of the Islamic State. It is the war of all Muslims, but the Islamic State is spearheading it. It is the war of Muslims against infidels. O Muslims, go to war everywhere. It is the duty of every Muslim.}}</ref>

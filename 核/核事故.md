依照[[国际原子能机构|国际原子能机构]]（{{abbr|IAEA|International Atomic Energy Agency}}）的标准，'''核事故'''是指对人、环境或设备造成严重后果的事件。核事故导致的后果有[[急性辐射综合症|致死的影响]]、对[[自然环境|自然环境]]造成的[[游離輻射|大量辐射泄漏]]、[[爐心熔毀|爐心熔毀]]等。<ref name="INES-DefinitionOfAccident">{{cite book|title=International Nuclear and Radiological Events Scale Users' Manual, 2008 Edition |publisher=International Atomic Energy Agency |url=http://www-pub.iaea.org/MTCD/publications/PDF/INES-2009_web.pdf |author=Staff, IAEA, AEN/NEA |accessdate=2010-07-26 |location=Vienna, Austria |page=184 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110515164252/http://www-pub.iaea.org/MTCD/publications/PDF/INES-2009_web.pdf |archivedate=2011-05-15 }}</ref>“重大核事故”，比如1986年的[[切尔诺贝利核事故|切尔诺贝利核事故]]，则通常指{{tsl|en|reactor core|堆芯}}损坏，大量[[放射性|放射性]]材料泄漏的事故。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Yablokov|first1=Alexey V.|last2=Nesterenko|first2=Vassily B.|last3=Nesterenko|first3=Alexey|last4=Sherman-Nevinger, consulting editor|first4=Jannette D.|title=Chernobyl: Consequences of the Catastrophe for People and the Environment|date=2009|publisher=Blackwell Publishing for the Annals of the New York Academy of Sciences|location=Boston, MA|isbn=978-1-57331-757-3|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=g34tNlYOB3AC&printsec=frontcover&dq=chernobyl+consequences+of+the+catastrophe+for+people+and+the+environment&hl=en&src=bmrr&ei=Q5-dTfadJc-2tgfCtvThBA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCwQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false|accessdate=2016-06-11}}</ref>

[[Image:Castle_romeo2.jpg|thumb]]爆炸產生的蘑菇雲，1954年]]
'''核浩劫'''或'''核子末日'''是大規模[[核戰爭|核戰爭]]及[[核輻射|核輻射]]導致的一種[[全球災難危機|世界末日]]。在這種情況下，[[核冬天|核冬天]]發生<ref name="RobockSAD">{{cite journal|last1=Robock|first1=Alan|last2=Toon|first2=Owen B|title=Self-assured destruction: The climate impacts of nuclear war|journal=Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists|date=2012|volume=68|issue=5|pages=66–74|doi=10.1177/0096340212459127|url=http://thebulletin.org/2012/september/self-assured-destruction-climate-impacts-nuclear-war|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref>，世界上大多數的城市會被摧毀，很多人類科技已不能再使用，而地表也變得難以居住。

[[冷戰|冷戰]]早期的一些研究顯示核戰爭之後仍然會有數十億人倖存<ref name="bmartin.cc">{{cite journal|url=http://www.bmartin.cc/pubs/82jpr.html |doi=10.1177/002234338201900401 |title=Critique of Nuclear Extinction |author= Martin, Brian| journal=Journal of Peace Research|volume=19|issue=4|year= 1982|pages=287–300}}</ref><ref>[http://www.johnstonsarchive.net/nuclear/nuclearwar1.html The Effects of a Global Thermonuclear War]. Johnstonsarchive.net. Retrieved on 2013-07-21.</ref><ref name="ReferenceB">{{cite journal|url=http://www.bmartin.cc/pubs/82cab/index.html|title=The global health effects of nuclear war|journal=Current Affairs Bulletin|volume=59|issue=7|date=December 1982|pages=14–26|author=Martin, Brian }}</ref><ref>[http://trove.nla.gov.au/work/21437545?selectedversion=NBD238850 Long-term worldwide effects of multiple nuclear-weapons detonations. Assembly of Mathematical and Physical Sciences, National Research Council.]</ref>，但後來一些研究指出核浩劫將導致人類文明終結<ref name="IPPNWStudy">{{cite web|last1=Helfand|first1=Ira|title=Nuclear Famine: Two Billion People at Risk?|url=http://www.ippnw.org/pdf/nuclear-famine-two-billion-at-risk-2013.pdf|website=International Physicians for the Prevention of Nuclear War|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref>，核浩劫的發生時間自1947年以來就由[[末日時鐘|末日時鐘]]指出。

核浩劫是[[流行文化中的核戰爭|流行文化中]]較常見的一個主題，以核子末日為主題的[[推想小說|推想小說]]也成為了科幻小說的一大亞分類。

== 後果 ==
1979年美國參議院的[[技术评估办公室|技术评估办公室]]曾評價過不同程度的核戰爭導致的後果，結果顯示如果美蘇之間爆發全面核戰爭，那麼美國可能有35%到77%的人口會消失（其中7000萬到1.6億人直接死於核武器的攻擊），而蘇聯則可能有20%到40%的人死去。<ref name="Report1979">{{cite book|last1=Johns|first1=Lionel S|last2=Sharfman|first2=Peter|last3=Medalia|first3=Jonathan|last4=Vining|first4=Robert W|last5=Lewis|first5=Kevin|last6=Proctor|first6=Gloria|title=The Effects of Nuclear War|url=https://www.princeton.edu/~ota/disk3/1979/7906/7906.PDF|publisher=Library of Congress|accessdate=13 February 2016|date=1979}}</ref> 雖然冷戰過後經歷了[[核裁軍|核裁軍]]，但倘若核戰爭爆發，人類社會還是有可能遭受毀滅性打擊。

=== 核冬天 ===
{{main|核冬天}}
1980年代早期科學家開始研究核武器導致的大氣層問題，結果顯示核武器爆炸後產生的煙塵可能會在大氣層中漂流數周并遮蔽太陽光<ref name="Britannica">{{cite web|title=Nuclear winter|url=http://www.britannica.com/topic/nuclear-winter|website=Encyclopædia Britannica|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref>。1983年TTAPS的研究首次提出了“核冬天”這一概念<ref>{{cite journal |title=Nuclear Winter: Global Consequences of Multiple Nuclear Explosions |journal=Science |volume=222 |issue=4630 |pages=1283–92 |date=23 December 1983 |pmid=17773320 |doi=10.1126/science.222.4630.1283|bibcode = 1983Sci...222.1283T }}</ref>。

2007年的一項研究顯示如果爆發較大規模的核戰爭，美國、歐洲和中國這些主要農業產區的氣溫可能降低12–20 °C，俄羅斯地區甚至可能會降低35 °C<ref name="Robock2007">{{cite journal|last1=Robock|first1=Alan|last2=Oman|first2=Luke|last3=Stenchikov|first3=Georgiy L.|title=Nuclear winter revisited with a modern climate model and current nuclear arsenals: Still catastrophic consequences|journal=Journal of Geophysical Research|date=2007|volume=112|issue=D13107|pages=14|doi=10.1029/2006JD008235|url=http://climate.envsci.rutgers.edu/pdf/RobockNW2006JD008235.pdf|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref>。而其預估的核冬天延續時間也加長數十年<ref name="ToonandRobock2010" />，就算是小規模的核戰爭也會導致十幾年的核冬天<ref name="ToonandRobock2010">{{cite journal|last1=Robock|first1=A|last2=Toon|first2=OB|title=Local nuclear war, global suffering|journal=Scientific American|date=2010|volume=302|pages=74–81|url=http://academic.evergreen.edu/z/zita/articles/SciAm/GW_CC/NuclearWar2010.pdf|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref>。2008年的一項研究顯示和核戰爭還會造成一個巨大的[[臭氧層空洞|臭氧層空洞]]，進一步影響人類生存。<ref name=Mills2008>{{cite journal |title=Massive global ozone loss predicted following regional nuclear conflict |journal=Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. U.S.A. |date=2008 |pmid=18391218 |pmc=2291128 |doi=10.1073/pnas.0710058105 |last1=Mills |first1=M. J. |last2=Toon |first2=O. B. |last3=Turco |first3=R. P. |last4=Kinnison |first4=D. E. |last5=Garcia |first5=R. R. |volume=105 |issue=14 |pages=5307–12 |bibcode = 2008PNAS..105.5307M }}[http://acd.ucar.edu/~mmills/pdf/2008MillsPNAS_MassiveOzoneLoss.pdf as PDF] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304100404/http://acd.ucar.edu/~mmills/pdf/2008MillsPNAS_MassiveOzoneLoss.pdf |date=2016-03-04 }}</ref>

核冬天會影響人類農業產量，因此進一步引發核饑荒<ref>Harwell, M., and C. Harwell. (1986). "Nuclear Famine: The Indirect Effects of Nuclear War", pp. 117–135 in Solomon, F. and R. Marston (Eds.). ''The Medical Implications of Nuclear War''. Washington, D.C.: National Academy Press. {{ISBN|0309036925}}.</ref>。2013年[[国际防止核战争医生组织|国际防止核战争医生组织]]的報告稱光印度和巴基斯坦之間爆發核戰爭就會使得世界上三分之一的人口陷入饑荒<ref name="IPPNWStudy">{{cite web|last1=Helfand|first1=Ira|title=Nuclear Famine: Two Billion People at Risk?|url=http://www.ippnw.org/pdf/nuclear-famine-two-billion-at-risk-2013.pdf|website=International Physicians for the Prevention of Nuclear War|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref><ref name="IPPNWPress">{{cite web|last1=Loretz|first1=John|title=Nobel Laureate Warns Two Billion at Risk from Nuclear Famine|url=http://www.ippnw.org/pdf/nuclear-famine-2013-press-release-english.pdf|website=IPPNW|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref>。

=== 電子設備癱瘓===
{{See also|核爆电磁脉冲|高空核爆炸}}

[[核爆电磁脉冲|核爆电磁脉冲]]將導致電子設備線入癱瘓。比如光一個用以進行電磁脈衝攻擊的核武器在美國領土中心上空爆炸，整個美國的電子設施就有可能會陷入癱瘓<ref>{{cite web|last1=Kessler|first1=Ronald|title=EMP Attack Would Send America into a Dark Age|url=http://www.newsmax.com/RonaldKessler/emp-electromagnetic-pulse/2009/09/23/id/335160/|website=Newsmax|accessdate=16 January 2016}}</ref>。醫療設備、通訊設備的失靈都會導致進一步的人類滅絕。這種影響可能持續數月甚至數年之久<ref name="EMP report">{{cite web|title=Report of the Commission to Assess the Threat to the United States from Electromagnetic Pulse (EMP) Attack|url=http://commdocs.house.gov/committees/security/has204000.000/has204000_0.HTM|accessdate=16 January 2016}}</ref>。2013年美國眾議院就“致命攻擊下高壓電基礎設施安全性”一題進行過討論<ref>{{cite web |last1=McCormack|first1=John |title=Lights out: House plan would protect nation's electricity from solar flare, nuclear bomb |date=2013-06-17 |url=http://www.washingtonexaminer.com/lights-out-house-plan-would-protect-nations-electricity-from-solar-flare-nuclear-bomb/article/2532038 |website=[[Washington_Examiner|Washington Examiner]] |accessdate=2016-01-16}}</ref>，歐盟也曾就類似議題進行過探討<ref>House of Commons
Defence Committee, [https://publications.parliament.uk/pa/cm201012/cmselect/cmdfence/1552/1552.pdf "Developing Threats: Electro-Magnetic Pulses (EMP)"]. Tenth Report of Session 2010–12.</ref>。但核爆電磁脈衝的危險性和可能性可能也是被一些媒體高估了<ref name="PT2016">{{cite journal |first1=Steven T. |last1=Corneliussen |title=Conservative media sustain alarm about a possible electromagnetic-pulse catastrophe |date=2016-06-23 |doi=10.1063/PT.5.8178 |journal=[[Physics_Today|Physics Today]] |url=http://physicstoday.scitation.org/do/10.1063/PT.5.8178/full |accessdate=2016-12-16}}</ref>，《[[今日物理|今日物理]]》稱世界上還沒有能造成大規模傷害的電磁脈衝武器<ref name="PT2016"/>。

'''蘇普麗亞·帕塔克·卡普爾'''（[[英語|英語]]：'''Supriya Pathak Kapur'''，{{bd|1961年|1月7日|catIdx=Pathak, Supriya}}）是一名[[印度|印度]]女演員，主要作品是[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]電影和印度电视连续剧。她最為人所知的作品包括电视连续剧《Khichdi》、电影《Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela》（2013）、《[[甘地傳|甘地傳]]》及《Bazaar》等。<ref>[https://www.hindustantimes.com/bollywood/supriya-pathak-i-have-learnt-everything-i-know-about-acting-from-pankaj-sir/story-S8zx48Zozxqq12CQSMQ11H.html “Supriya Pathak: I have learnt everything I know about acting from Pankaj sir”]. Hindustan Times. 04-04-2018.</ref><ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/television/supriya-pathak-khichdi-shahid-kapoor-misha-5123042/ “Khichdi actor Supriya Pathak: Shahid is very dear to me and he gave me the best gift in Misha”]. The Indian Express. 04-04-2018.</ref>

帕塔克的丈夫是印度男演員[[潘卡吉·卡普爾|潘卡吉·卡普爾]]，他们育有一子一女。她的継子[[沙希德·卡普爾|沙希德·卡普爾]]也是一名演员。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Supriya-Pathak-As-a-man-I-give-Pankaj-Kapur-9-out-of-10/articleshow/37521407.cms “Supriya Pathak: As a man, I give Pankaj Kapur 9 out of 10”]. Times of India. 01-07-2014.</ref>

2016年'''蘇爾特攻勢'''是[[利比亞|利比亞]][[民族團結政府|民族團結政府]]在[[蘇爾特省|蘇爾特省]]展開的軍事行動，以收復被[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]武裝分子佔據的地區。

==攻勢==
[[Image:Libya.sire.2017.jpg|thumb]]
蘇爾特攻勢於2016年5月12日展開。<ref name="freed"/>5月16日，政府軍收復[[阿布古来恩|阿布古来恩]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.libyaherald.com/2016/05/16/misratans-report-they-have-recaptured-abu-grein-from-is/|work=Libya Herald|title=Misratans report they have recaptured Abu Grain from IS |access-date=16 May 2016|date=16 May 2016}}</ref>5月17日，政府軍進抵距離[[蘇特_(利比亞)|蘇爾特]]將近50公里處。<ref name="GNAvsISIS">{{cite web|url=https://www.libyangazette.net/2016/05/17/libyan-troops-clash-with-isis-militants-leaving-6-dead-and-17-injured/|work=Libyan Gazette|title=Libyan Troops Clash With ISIS Militants Leaving 6 Dead and 17 Injured|access-date=17 May 2016|date=17 May 2016|accessdate=2016-05-17|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160601164546/https://www.libyangazette.net/2016/05/17/libyan-troops-clash-with-isis-militants-leaving-6-dead-and-17-injured/|archivedate=2016-06-01|deadurl=yes}}</ref>

5月19日，一名伊斯蘭國[[自殺炸彈|自殺炸彈]]客在蘇爾特以西90公里處襲擊政府軍，30名士兵喪生，50人傷。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://aa.com.tr/en/middle-east/daesh-bombing-kills-30-soldiers-near-libya-s-sirte/574869|title=Daesh bombing kills 30 soldiers near Libya’s Sirte|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>

5月後期，親政府部隊攻佔鄰近蘇爾特市的多個地點，包括蘇爾特發電站。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.libyaherald.com/2016/05/29/misratans-say-they-have-taken-sirte-power-station/|title=Misratans say they have taken Sirte power station |website=libyaherald.com|access-date=2016-05-31|date=2016-05-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.libyanexpress.com/misurata-forces-advance-to-southern-sirte/|title=Misurata forces advance to southern Sirte|website=libyaherald.com|access-date=2016-05-31|date=2016-05-31}}</ref>石油設施守備隊也在蘇爾特的東部前線發起行動，攻佔Bin Jawad和Noufiliyah。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-libya-security-idUSKCN0YL1V0|title=Libyan security forces pushing Islamic State back from vicinity of oil terminals|website=www.reuters.com|access-date=2016-05-31|date=2016-05-31}}</ref>

6月4日，政府軍攻佔蘇爾特市以南的Ghardabiya空軍基地。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/libyan-forces-liberate-key-airport-near-isis-controlled-sirte/|title=Libyan Forces liberate key airport near ISIS controlled Sirte|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=5 June 2016|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-libya-security-idUSKCN0YQ0TA|title=Libyan brigades capture air base from Islamic State south of Sirte: spokesman|date=4 June 2016|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016|via=Reuters}}</ref>伊斯蘭國於次日奪回該基地。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-retakes-air-base-near-sirte-clashing-libyan-troops/|title=ISIS retakes air base near Sirte after clashing with Libyan troops|first=Chris|last=Tomson|date=7 June 2016|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>

6月8日，政府軍在攻佔蘇爾特市西郊一條橋後首度攻入該市。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/libyan-soldiers-enter-isis-stronghold-sirte/|title=ISIS on the verge of losing its Libyan capital after government troops storm Sirte|first=Chris|last=Tomson|date=8 June 2016|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>

6月10日，政府軍攻佔蘇爾特兩個兵營、一條橋和一個十字路口。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2016/06/libyan-forces-gains-isil-sirte-160609084405138.html|title=Libyan forces make gains against ISIL in Sirte|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>利比亞海軍封鎖了蘇爾特的海岸，阻止伊斯蘭國武裝分子從海路逃走。<ref name="navy">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/libyan-navy-asserts-control-isil-controlled-sirte/|title=Libyan Navy asserts control over ISIL-controlled Sirte|first=News|last=Desk|date=10 June 2016|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>6月12日，政府軍控制了蘇爾特的港口和機場，<ref name="freed">{{cite web|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2016/06/libyan-forces-retake-port-isil-bastion-sirte-160611131347272.html|title=Freed Gaddafi loyalists found dead in Libya's Tripoli|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>以及東面的一個住宅區。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/libyan-forces-retake-sirte-port-isil/|title=Libyan Forces retake Sirte Port from ISIL|first=News|last=Desk|date=12 June 2016|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>伊斯蘭國武裝分子以狙擊手阻止政府軍推進，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/middle_east/libyan-forces-brace-for-street-battles-against-is-in-sirte/2016/06/12/c7868b2a-3075-11e6-b9d5-3c3063f8332c_story.html|title=IS suicide bombers hit Libyan forces in Sirte, killing 1|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>並且以一浪接一浪的自殺炸彈客襲擊政府軍。<ref name="16libyan"/>

6月13日，政府軍抵達市中心附近的住宅區後，進展速度慢了下來。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.middle-east-online.com/english/?id=77323|title=.:Middle East Online:::.|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>6月16日有10名士兵於一次自殺炸彈襲擊中喪生。有6名士兵於另一行動中遇襲喪生。<ref name="16libyan">{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/middle_east/10-killed-in-suicide-attack-outside-libyas-is-stronghold/2016/06/16/377721f0-33ab-11e6-ab9d-1da2b0f24f93_story.html|title=16 Libyan militiamen killed in 2 IS attacks near Sirte|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>

政府軍在6月21日的進攻中有39人喪生，140人傷。<ref name="39dead">[https://www.libyaherald.com/2016/06/21/many-dead-in-fresh-bunyan-marsous-offensive-in-sirte-is-loses-ground/ 39 dead in fresh Bunyan Marsous offensive in Sirte; IS loses ground]</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/libya-clashes-blast-ammunition-depot-kill-60-40036719|title=Libya Clashes, Blast at a Depot Kill More Than 60|first=ABC|last=News|publisher=|accessdate=24 June 2016}}</ref>政府軍撤出港口，但是仍然控制進出港口的道路。

6月28日，英軍[[特殊舟艇隊|特殊舟艇隊]]在蘇爾特附近與伊斯蘭國武裝分子進行一場歷時30分鐘的槍戰，20至30名武裝分子被擊斃。<ref name="SBS">{{cite web|url=http://www.libyanexpress.com/three-british-isis-members-killed-by-sbs-soldiers-in-sirte/|title=Three British ISIS members killed by SBS soldiers in Sirte - Libyan Express|date=4 July 2016|publisher=|accessdate=13 July 2016}}</ref>

7月2日，政府軍攻佔市中心，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.enca.com/africa/libya-forces-control-of-strategic-locations-in-sirte|title=Libya forces control of  strategic locations in Sirte|publisher=|accessdate=13 July 2016}}</ref>或至少攻佔了市中心的一部分，港口也再次落入政府軍手中。<ref name="battle">{{cite web|url=http://uk.reuters.com/article/us-libya-security-idUKKCN0ZH5IL|title=Libyan forces battle Islamic State street-to-street in Sirte|publisher=|accessdate=13 July 2016}}</ref>

7月15日在瓦加杜古中心（Ouagadougou Centre）一帶的戰鬥造成政府軍21人喪生，139人傷。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/wires/afp/article-3692537/20-loyalist-troops-killed-fighting-IS-Libyas-Sirte.html|title=20 loyalist troops killed fighting IS in Libya's Sirte|date=15 July 2016|publisher=|accessdate=15 July 2016}}</ref><ref>[http://www.reuters.com/article/us-libya-security-sirte-idUSKCN0ZV2OR Libyan forces battling Islamic State in Sirte suffer losses in push to advance]</ref><ref>[http://www.libyanexpress.com/misurata-central-hospital-21-killed-and-139-injured-in-sirte-clashes-against-isis/ Misurata Central Hospital: 21 killed and 139 injured in Sirte clashes against ISIS]</ref>約10日後，政府軍攻佔伊斯蘭國在蘇爾特最大的炸彈工廠，<ref name="factory">[http://www.france24.com/en/20160724-libya-loyalists-take-bomb-factory-sirte Libya loyalists take IS bomb factory in Sirte] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160725144925/http://www.france24.com/en/20160724-libya-loyalists-take-bomb-factory-sirte |date=2016-07-25 }}</ref>也攻佔了安全總部。<ref>[http://www.wsj.com/articles/libyan-forces-advance-in-islamic-state-held-city-1469462978 Libyan Forces Advance in Islamic State-Held City]</ref>

8月10日，政府軍攻佔伊斯蘭國在蘇爾特的總部瓦加杜古中心。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-africa-37037544 |title=Libyans 'oust' so-called IS from Sirte headquarters |work=BBC News |date=2016-08-10}}</ref>

到2016年10月底，據報在蘇爾特的伊斯蘭國武裝分子只剩下約100人。<ref name="About100">{{cite web|url=http://www.voanews.com/a/libya-kerry-haftar-saraj/3573427.html|title=Libya's Chaos Prompts New US Calls for Action |first=Luis|last=Ramirez |publisher=|accessdate=2016-11-16}}</ref>至11月中，伊斯蘭國的控制區只剩下約1平方公里。大約400名伊斯蘭國武裝分子於攻勢開始後成功逃離蘇爾特，在政府軍後方發動襲擊。<ref>[http://www.reuters.com/article/us-libya-security-idUSKBN13D222 Far from Mosul, Islamic State close to defeat in Libya's Sirte], 2016-11-18.</ref>到11月22日，蘇爾特的武裝分子仍佔據數十座房屋負隅頑抗。<ref name="37perish">{{cite web|url=https://www.libyaherald.com/2016/11/22/surrender-calls-as-37-terrorists-perish-in-shrinking-sirte-enclave/|title=Surrender calls as 37 terrorists perish in shrinking Sirte enclave|publisher=Libya Herald|date=2016-11-22 |accessdate=2016-11-24}}</ref>

自2016年8月1日起，美國向蘇爾特的伊斯蘭國武裝分子進行了至少495次空襲。2016年12月6日，伊斯蘭國在蘇爾特的最後據點被政府軍攻克。<ref name=reuters20161206>{{Cite news |url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-libya-security-sirte-idUSKBN13V15R |title=Libyan forces clear last Islamic State holdout in Sirte |publisher=Reuters |date=2016-12-06}}</ref>

'''蘇格蘭皇家軍樂團'''為{{le|皇家蘇格蘭兵團|Royal Regiment of Scotland}}的{{le|軍樂隊|military band}}。軍團的{{le|國防義勇軍 (英國)|Territorial Army (United Kingdom)|國防義勇軍}}營隊中有兩支軍樂隊，分別為蘇格蘭皇家高地軍樂團以及{{le|蘇格蘭皇家低地軍樂團|Lowland Band of the Royal Regiment of Scotland}}。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.edintattoo.co.uk/the-experience/scottish-regiments/51st-highland,-7th-battalion-the-royal-regiment-of-scotland|title=51st Highland, 7th Battalion, Royal Regiment of Scotland|publisher=Edinburgh Tattoo|accessdate=2018-08-02|language=en}}</ref>

蘇格蘭皇家軍樂團亦為{{le|陸軍軍樂隊|Corps of Army Music}}轄下兩支步兵軍樂隊的其中一支。

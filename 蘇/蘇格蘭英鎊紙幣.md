'''蘇格蘭英鎊紙幣'''（Banknotes of Scotland）是指在[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]流通的[[英鎊紙幣|英鎊紙幣]]。目前有三家銀行有權在蘇格蘭發行英鎊紙幣，包括蘇格蘭銀行、皇家蘇格蘭銀行、克萊茲戴爾銀行。蘇格蘭英鎊紙幣十分特殊，由[[零售銀行|零售銀行]]而非[[中央銀行|中央銀行]]發行。在技術上，蘇格蘭英鎊紙幣在英國各地（包括蘇格蘭在內）都不得[[法償|法償]]<ref name="bankofengland_faqs">{{cite web| url=http://www.bankofengland.co.uk/banknotes/about/faqs.htm#15| title=Are Scottish & Northern Irish notes legal tender?| accessdate=2007-10-14| author = Bank of England- Frequently Asked Questions| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20071012030427/http://www.bankofengland.co.uk/banknotes/about/faqs.htm| archivedate= 12 October 2007 | deadurl= no}}</ref><ref name="rm-legal-tender">{{cite web| title=Legal Tender Guidelines| url=http://www.royalmint.com/corporate/policies/legal_tender_guidelines.aspx| publisher=[[Royal_Mint|Royal Mint]]| accessdate=2008-11-08| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081217182521/http://www.royalmint.com/corporate/policies/legal_tender_guidelines.aspx| archivedate=2008年12月17日| deadurl=yes}}</ref>，因此被分類為[[本票|本票]]<ref>{{London Gazette |issue=58254 |date=21 February 2007 |page=2544 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Scottish voters in favour of keeping the pound |url=http://www.express.co.uk/news/uk/778000/scotland-currency-pounds-nicola-sturgeon-indyref2-snp |newspaper=Daily Express |date=12 March 2017 |accessdate=2017-04-20}}</ref>。

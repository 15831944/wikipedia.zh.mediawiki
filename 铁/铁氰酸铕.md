'''铁氰酸铕'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Eu[Fe(CN)<sub>6</sub>]。它可以由[[三氯化铕|三氯化铕]]和[[铁氰酸钾|铁氰酸钾]]的复分解反应制得。它也能在亚铁氰酸铕钾的电化学反应中产生：<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S016943320901705X|issue=10|doi=10.1016/j.apsusc.2009.11.089|pages=3148–3154|title=Construction of europium hexacyanoferrate film and its electrocatalytic activity to tyrosine determination|volume=256|journal=Applied Surface Science|date=2010-03|issn=0169-4332|accessdate=2018-07-30|author=Yingju Liu, Zehao Yang, Yuanwen Zhong, Jiayu Yu}}</ref>
: KEu[Fe(CN)<sub>6</sub>] → Eu[Fe(CN)<sub>6</sub>] + K<sup>+</sup> + e<sup>−</sup>

铁氰酸铕在真空中加热按下式分解：<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ic50093a026|issue=11|doi=10.1021/ic50093a026|pages=2510–2512|title=Further studies of the thermal decomposition of europium hexacyanoferrate(III) and ammonium europium hexacyanoferrate(II)|volume=9|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|date=1970-11|issn=0020-1669|language=EN|accessdate=2018-07-30|author=Patrick K. Gallagher, Betty Prescott}}</ref>
: 2 Eu[Fe(CN)<sub>6</sub>]·5H<sub>2</sub>O → 2 EuOOH + 2 Fe(CN)<sub>2</sub> + (CN)<sub>2</sub>↑ + 6 H<sub>2</sub>O + 6 HCN↑

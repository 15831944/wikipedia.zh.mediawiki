'''铁氰酸铵'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为(NH<sub>4</sub>)<sub>3</sub>[Fe(CN)<sub>6</sub>]。

==制备==
铁氰酸铵可由[[铁氰酸钾|铁氰酸钾]]和[[草酸铵|草酸铵]]反应，析出[[草酸钾|草酸钾]]沉淀。将溶液浓缩，得到铁氰酸铵。<ref>Kondo, Nobuyuki; Itoh, Hiroyuki; Kurihara, Masato; Sakamoto, Masatomi; Aono, Hiromichi; Sadaoka, Yoshihiko. New high-​yield preparation procedure of Ln[Fe(CN)​6]​·nH2O (Ln=La, Gd, and Lu) and their thermal decomposition into perovskite-​type oxides. ''Journal of Alloys and Compounds'', 2006. 408-412: 1026-1029. 10.1016/j.jallcom.2004.11.095</ref>

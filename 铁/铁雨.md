《'''铁雨'''》（{{lang-ko|'''강철비'''}}）是一部由{{Tsl|en|Yang_Woo-suk|楊宇碩}}导演的韩国电影，于2017年首映，以[[朝鲜民主主义人民共和国|朝鲜民主主义人民共和国]]和[[大韩民国|大韩民国]]之间的冲突为题材。电影由楊宇碩在2011年创作的同名[[Webtoon|韩国漫画]]改编而成，由[[郭度沅|郭度沅]]和[[鄭雨盛|鄭雨盛]]主演。<ref>{{cite web|author1=Pierce Conran|title=JUNG Woo-sung & KWAK Do-won Back Together for STEEL RAIN|url=http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/news/news.jsp?mode=VIEW&seq=4113|website=Korean Film News|accessdate=2018-01-25|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180125051717/http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/news/news.jsp?mode=VIEW&seq=4113|archivedate=2018-01-25|language=en|date=2016-12-27|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20170926000745|title=Upcoming film imagines a North Korea upended by coup|first=The Korea|last=Herald|date=2017-09-26|website=koreaherald.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title='Steel Rain' takes 'cool-headed' view on inter-Korean relations, says director|url=http://english.yonhapnews.co.kr/news/2017/11/15/0200000000AEN20171115007300315.html|website=Yonhap News Agency}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title='Steel Rain' beats 'Star Wars' at weekend box office|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20171218000379|website=The Korea Herald|accessdate=2017-12-21|date=2017-12-18}}</ref>

在[[计算_(计算机科学)|计算]]领域，{{地区用词4|start={{lang-en|output device}}|cn=输出设备|tw=輸出裝置}}是一种[[计算机硬件|计算机硬件]]，根据从{{Tsl|en|Information processor|信息处理器|信息处理系统}}（如[[计算机|计算机]]或[[资讯设备|资讯设备]]）中接收到的数据和指令执行特定任务。<ref name=NIOS>{{cite web |title= Data Processing Concept |url= http://www.nios.ac.is/srsec330/330L2.pdf |publisher= The National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) |accessdate= 2 June 2012 |pages= 24–37 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name="PC Mag">{{cite web |agency= PCMag.com |title= output device Definition from PC Mag |url= https://www.pcmag.com/encyclopedia_term/0,1237,t=output+device&i=48677,00.asp|work=Encyclopedia |publisher= The Computer Language Company Inc. |access-date= 2 June 2012}}</ref>

输出的类型包括[[文本|文本]]、[[图形|图形]]、[[触觉|触觉]]<ref name="Office of the Chief information Officer">{{cite web|title=Accommodations For Vision Disabilities|url=http://energy.gov/cio/accommodations-vision-disabilities|work=Energy.gov|publisher=Office of the Chief information Officer |access-date=3 June 2012}}</ref>、[[音频|音频]]、[[视频|视频]]等。

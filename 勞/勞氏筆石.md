'''勞氏筆石'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Loganograptus''}}）是[[筆石綱|筆石綱]][[正筆石目|正筆石目]]下的一[[屬|屬]]筆石，生存於[[奧陶紀|奧陶紀]][[海洋|海洋]]的更深處，是一種群居性[[浮游生物|浮游生物]]。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=46 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
勞氏筆石群體的體型較大，從最初的單體分叉成有16條均勻分開的莖節的群體，群體上每1[[微米|微米]]有1個簡單的[[胞管|胞管]]。<ref name="Fossils"></ref>

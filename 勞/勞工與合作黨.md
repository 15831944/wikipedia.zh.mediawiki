The Co-operative Party and the Labour Party, 1931-1951|url= http://clok.uclan.ac.uk/11485/3/Whitecross%20Angela%20Final%20e-Thesis%20%28Master%20Copy%29.pdf|location= |publisher= University of Central Lancashire|page= 79|isbn= |author-link= }}</ref>

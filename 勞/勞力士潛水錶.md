'''勞力士蠔式潛水錶'''（Rolex Oyster Perpetual Submariner）是[[勞力士|勞力士]]設計生產的一系列體育手錶，[[臺灣|臺灣]]與[[香港|香港]]俗稱'''水鬼'''，以耐水和耐腐蝕而著稱<ref name="GQ">[http://www.gq.com/how-to/fashion/200704/gq-guide-buy-a-watch-slideshow#slide=3 GQ magazine] GQ: How to Buy a Watch The Classics Buy any one of these signature watches and you can’t go wrong 1. Rolex Oyster Perpetual Submariner ''Unveiled in 1953, the Submariner was the first watch water-resistant to 330 feet. Its "combination of unparalleled elegance and spy-friendly versatility" appealed to Sean Connery’s James Bond and none other than Che Guevara.''</ref>。首款潛水錶於1954年在巴塞爾鐘錶展上公諸於眾。勞力士潛水錶被認為是「世界上辨識度最高的奢侈品之一」，也被公認為是手錶中的經典<ref name="CNN Money">[http://money.cnn.com/magazines/moneymag/moneymag_archive/1997/05/01/225689/index.htm CNN Money] Quote: ''That explains why big recognizable brands like Cartier, Patek Philippe and Rolex rule. They can be thought of, in fact, as portable status symbols, owing to their tradition of quality workmanship.''</ref><ref name="Time Magazine">{{cite news |url= http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,1664358,00.html |work= Time Magazine |title= China: Breaking out the largest logos |date=21 September 2007}}</ref><ref name="New York Times">[https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9B0DEFDD1531F935A35751C1A961948260 New York Times Magazine] quote: ''...became as much a status symbol as a silver Porsche or a gold-faced Rolex watch.''</ref><ref name="Guardian UK">[https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2005/dec/01/shopping.jesscartnermorley Guardian UK: What is it with men and their watches?] quote: ''It used to be so simple – rich men adorned themselves with that ultimate macho status symbol the Rolex, while the less well-to-do strapped on Timex or Casio.''</ref>。但也由於其極受歡迎，因此有大量仿冒品。

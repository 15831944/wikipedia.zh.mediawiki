'''肿瘤微环境'''（{{lang-en|Tumor microenvironment}}）是指肿瘤细胞存在的周围微环境，包括周围的血管、免疫细胞、[[成纤维细胞|成纤维细胞]]、骨髓源性炎性细胞、各种信号分子和[[细胞外基质|细胞外基质]]（ECM）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cancer.gov/dictionary?cdrid=561725|title=NCI Dictionary of Cancer Terms|work=National Cancer Institute}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.sciencemag.org/content/348/6230/74.full|title=T cell exclusion, immune privilege, and the tumor microenvironment|accessdate=2015-06-25|date=|last=Joyce|first=Johanna A.|publisher=Science Magazine|pages=74–80|doi=10.1126/science.aaa6204|publication-date=3 April 2015|last2=Fearon|first2=Douglas T.|volume=348|issue=6230}}</ref><ref name="physicalMicroenvironment">{{Cite journal|title=Impact of the physical microenvironment on tumor progression and metastasis|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0958166916300301|last=Spill|first=F.|last2=Reynolds|first2=D.S.|journal=Current Opinion in Biotechnology|doi=10.1016/j.copbio.2016.02.007|volume=40|pages=41–48|last3=Kamm|first3=R.D.|last4=Zaman|first4=M.H.}}</ref> 

肿瘤和周围环境密切相关，不断进行交互作用，肿瘤可以通过释放细胞信号分子影响其微环境环境，促进肿瘤[[血管新生|的血管生成]]和诱导[[免疫耐受|免疫耐受]]，而微环境中的免疫细胞可影响癌细胞增长和发育。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=TLR-signaling and proinflammatory cytokines as drivers of tumorigenesis.|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1043466616300229|last=Korneev|first=KV|last2=Atretkhany|first2=KN|date=January 2017|journal=Cytokine|doi=10.1016/j.cyto.2016.01.021|volume=89|pages=127–135|pmid=26854213|last3=Drutskaya|first3=MS|last4=Grivennikov|first4=SI|last5=Kuprash|first5=DV|last6=Nedospasov|first6=SA}}</ref>

肿瘤微环境有助于{{le|肿瘤异质性|Tumour heterogeneity}}的形成。

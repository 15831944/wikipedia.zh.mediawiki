'''肿瘤学'''（[[肿瘤学|英：Oncology]]）是一种研究[[肿瘤|肿瘤]]（尤其是[[恶性肿瘤|恶性肿瘤]]）的病因、预防、诊断、治疗以及基础研究的综合性、独立性医学二级学科。

提高肿瘤患者生存率的方法主要包括以下三方面:

# 预防 - 主要包括减少与烟、酒等肿瘤[[风险|风险]]因素的接触。<ref>{{Cite journal|title = Modifiable risk factors for cancer|journal = British Journal of Cancer|date = 2004-01-26|issn = 0007-0920|pmc = 2410150|pmid = 14735167|pages = 299–303|volume = 90|issue = 2|doi = 10.1038/sj.bjc.6601509|first = C. J.|last = Stein|first2 = G. A.|last2 = Colditz}}</ref>
# 早期诊断 - 对常见癌症的筛查<ref>{{Cite journal|title = Effect of screening for cancer in the Nordic countries on deaths, cost and quality of life up to the year 2017|journal = Acta Oncologica|date = 1997-01-01|issn = 0284-186X|pmid = 9143316|pages = 1–60|volume = 36 Suppl 9|first = L.|last = Hristova|first2 = M.|last2 = Hakama}}</ref> 以及进行综合诊断、分期
# 治疗 - 综合治疗<ref>{{Cite journal|title = Multimodality treatment of cancer|journal = The Australian and New Zealand Journal of Surgery|date = 1982-08-01|issn = 0004-8682|pmid = 6956307|pages = 341–346|volume = 52|issue = 4|first = J. F.|last = Forbes|doi=10.1111/j.1445-2197.1982.tb06005.x}}</ref> 通过肿瘤专家组讨论，或在综合型癌症中心接受治疗<ref>{{Cite journal|title = Impact of National Cancer Institute Comprehensive Cancer Centers on ovarian cancer treatment and survival|journal = Journal of the American College of Surgeons|date = 2015-05-01|issn = 1879-1190|pmid = 25840536|pages = 940–950|volume = 220|issue = 5|doi = 10.1016/j.jamcollsurg.2015.01.056|first = Robert E.|last = Bristow|first2 = Jenny|last2 = Chang|first3 = Argyrios|last3 = Ziogas|first4 = Belinda|last4 = Campos|first5 = Leo R.|last5 = Chavez|first6 = Hoda|last6 = Anton-Culver}}</ref>

对癌症的治疗通常需要跨学科的会诊。<ref>{{cite journal |title=Multidisciplinary management of cancer patients: chasing a shadow or real value? An overview of the literature |journal=Current Oncology |date=2012-08-01 |issn=1198-0052 |pmc=3410834 |pmid=22876151 |pages=e232-e238 |volume=19 |issue=4 |doi=10.3747/co.19.944 |first=J.M. |last=Croke |first2=S. |last2=El-Sayed}}</ref> 医学肿瘤学家，外科肿瘤学家，放射肿瘤学家，病理学家，放射科医师和器官特异性肿瘤学家应该在会诊中对于癌症病人的病情进行探讨，并根据病人的身体、心理、情绪、经济、社会关系等多方面因素制定最适宜的治疗方案。同时，由于肿瘤的治疗方案时常会改进，肿瘤学家们应及时跟进肿瘤学的最新动态。对于病情恶化的患者，或者标准疗法不适用的患者，如果符合标准，应该进行临床试验。

==风险因素==
;烟: 吸烟是最容易致癌或致死的因素。吸烟增加患有[[肺癌|肺癌]]，[[喉癌|喉癌]]，[[口腔癌|口腔癌]]，[[食道癌|食道癌]]，[[咽喉癌|咽喉癌]]，[[膀胱癌|膀胱癌]]，[[肾癌|肾癌]]，[[肝癌|肝癌]]，[[胃癌|胃癌]]，[[胰腺癌|胰腺癌]]，[[结肠癌|结肠癌]]，[[直肠癌|直肠癌]]，[[子宫颈癌|子宫颈癌]]和[[急性骨髓性白血病|急性骨髓性白血病]]癌症风险。无烟烟草（例如[[鼻烟|鼻烟]]或[[嚼菸|嚼菸]]）亦增加口腔癌，食道癌和胰腺癌的患病风险。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Tobacco|url = http://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/tobacco|website = National Cancer Institute|access-date = 2016-01-18}}</ref>
;酒: It can increase your risk of cancer of the mouth, throat, oesophagus, larynx, liver and breast. The risk of cancer is much higher for those who drink alcohol and also use tobacco.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Alcohol|url = http://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/alcohol|website = National Cancer Institute|access-date = 2016-01-18}}</ref>
;肥胖: Obese individuals have an increased risk of cancers of breast, colon, rectum, endometrium, oesophagus, kidney, pancreas, and gallbladder.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Obesity|url = http://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/obesity|website = National Cancer Institute|access-date = 2016-01-18}}</ref>
;年龄: Advanced age is a risk factor for many cancers. The median age of cancer diagnosis is 66 years.<ref>{{Cite web|title = Age|url = http://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/causes-prevention/risk/age|website = National Cancer Institute|access-date = 2016-01-18}}</ref>

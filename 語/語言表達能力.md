'''語言表達能力'''（{{lang-en|Expressive language skills}}）指的是把腦海中的想法轉換為符合邏輯及文法的詞彙和句子的能力。<ref name="Pediatric Therapy Network">{{cite web | title=Speech-Language Definitions | website=Pediatric Therapy Network | url=http://www.pediatrictherapynetwork.org/services/speech_language_definitions.cfm | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref><ref name="Baister">{{cite web | last=Baister | first=Julie | title=What is the difference between receptive vocabulary and expressive vocabulary? | website=Dyslexia Positive | url=http://www.dyslexiapositive.org.uk/discussion/what-is-the-difference-between-receptive-vocabulary-and-expressive-vocabulary/ | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref>語言表達能力包含[[面部表情|面部表情]]、[[手势|手势]]、[[意向性|意向性]]、[[词汇|词汇]]、[[语义学|语义学]]、[[词法学|形态学]]和[[语法学|语法学]]等。<ref name="Pediatric Therapy Network"/>

語言表達能力的缺陷可能與語言發育遲緩、聽力受損有關。<ref name="Young Killen 2002 pp. 802–810">{{cite journal | last=Young | first=Gretchen A. | last2=Killen | first2=Deanne H. | title=Receptive and Expressive Language Skills of Children with Five Years of Experience Using a Cochlear Implant | journal=The Annals of otology, rhinology, and laryngology | publisher=SAGE Publications | volume=111 | issue=9 | year=2002 | issn=0003-4894 | pmid=12296335 | doi=10.1177/000348940211100908 | pages=802–810}}</ref>語言表達能力也可能和腦中的資訊處理、[[自閉症|自閉症]]、[[X染色體易裂症|X染色體易裂症]]和學習方式有關。<ref name="Young Killen 2002 pp. 802–810"/>

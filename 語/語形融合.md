'''語形融合'''（syncretism (linguistics)、形式合一、混合論、不同變化形式的合併）在[[語言學|語言學]]中，指出現當單一[[語素|語素]]的功能性不同而形式上是相同的時之情況。本術語出現在[[歷史語言學|歷史語言學]]中，指的是在曲折規範下之形態的融合。在這種情況下，以前的區別已經被"融合"掉了。<ref>Crystal, David. ''A Dictionary of Linguistics and Phonetics''. Blackwell Publishing, 2008, pp. 469-70.</ref>

For example, in [[English_language|English]], the [[Nominative_case|nominative]] and [[Accusative_case|accusative]] forms of ''you'' are the same, whereas ''he''/''him'', ''she''/''her'', etc., have different forms depending on [[grammatical_case|grammatical case]].  In [[Latin|Latin]], the nominative and [[Vocative_case|vocative]] of [[Latin_declension#Third_declension_.28i.29|third-declension]] nouns have the same form (e.g. ''rēx'' "king" is both nominative and vocative singular). Similarly, in [[German_language|German]], the [[infinitive|infinitive]], [[Grammatical_person|first person]] plural present, and [[Grammatical_person|third person]] plural present of almost all verbs are identical in form (e.g. ''nehmen'' "to take", ''wir nehmen'' "we take", ''sie nehmen'' "they take"). In the [[Finnic_languages|Finnic languages]], such as [[Finnish_language|Finnish]] and [[Estonian_language|Estonian]], there is syncretism between the accusative and genitive singular case forms, and the nominative and accusative plural case forms.

例如，在英語中，你的名義和指責形式是一樣的，而他/她，她/她等，根據語法的不同，有不同的形式。在拉丁語中，第三個名詞的名詞和職業具有相同的形式（例如，rēx“king”既是名詞，也是獨一無二的）。同樣，在德語中，幾乎所有動詞的不定式，第一人復數存在和第三人復數存在形式相同（例如，nehmen“take”，“我們採取的”nehmen“，”他們採取“）。在芬蘭語和愛沙尼亞語的芬蘭語，芬蘭語和愛因斯坦語之間存在著指責性和特殊的單一形式之間的並存，以及具有特殊和指責性的複數形式。

Syncretism can arise through either [[phonology|phonological]] or morphological change. In the case of phonological change, forms that were originally distinct come to be pronounced identically, so that their distinctness is lost. Thus in the German case, the infinitive ''nehmen'' comes from [[Old_High_German|Old High German]] ''neman'', the first person plural ''nehmen'' comes from ''nemēm'', and the third person plural ''nehmen'' comes from ''nemant''. In the case of morphological change, one form simply stops being used and is replaced by the other: this is the case with the Latin example, where the nominative simply displaced the vocative in the third declension.

通過語音或形態變化可以產生共鳴。在語音變化的情況下，原來不同的形式相同地發音，從而使它們的明顯性消失。因此，在德國的情況下，不定尼泊爾人來自古老的德國人，第一個複數的人是來自尼曼的，第三個人的複數是來自神的人。在形態變化的情況下，一種形式簡單地停止使用，並被另一種形式所取代：拉丁語例子就是這種情況，在這種情況下，名詞簡單地在第三種聲明中取代了職業。-->

'''酒石酸锑钾'''（{{lang-en|'''Antimony potassium tartrate'''}}），又称'''酒石酸氧锑钾'''（{{lang-en|'''Potassium antimonyl tartrate'''}}），俗称'''吐酒石'''（{{lang-en|'''Tartar emetic'''或'''Emetic tartar'''}}），化学式为K<sub>2</sub>Sb<sub>2</sub>(C<sub>4</sub>H<sub>2</sub>O<sub>6</sub>)<sub>2</sub>，为[[酒石酸|酒石酸]]的[[锑|锑]][[钾|钾]][[复盐|复盐]]。酒石酸锑钾自古以来一直被用作一种强效的[[催吐剂|催吐剂]]；除此之外，它还曾被用于治疗[[血吸虫病|血吸虫病]]和[[利什曼病|利什曼病]]。

==催吐作用==
[[File:Brechweinstein.jpg|thumb]]
在[[中世纪|中世纪]]时，人们已经知道酒石酸锑钾具有[[催吐|催吐]]作用。由于它的毒性较大，人们在那时一般不直接服用——而是将[[酒|酒]]在[[锑|锑]]杯中存放24小时，并多次少量地饮用，直至达到需要的催吐效果为止。<ref>{{cite journal | pmc = 1543508 | title = President's address. Observations upon antimony | year = 1977 | volume = 70 | issue = 11 | pmid = 341167 | last1 = McCallum | first1 = RI | pages = 756–63 | journal = Proceedings of the Royal Society of Medicine}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | pmc = 1948687 | title = Antimonyall Cupps: Pocula Emetica or Calices Vomitorii | year = 1926 | volume = 19 | issue = Sect Hist Med | pmid = 19985185 | last1 = Thomson | first1 = SC | pages = 122.2–128 | journal = Proceedings of the Royal Society of Medicine}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1084/jem.37.1.97 | title = The Mechanism of the Vomiting Induced by Antimony and Potassium Tartrate (Tartar Emetic) | year = 1922 | last1 = Weiss | first1 = S. | journal = Journal of Experimental Medicine | volume = 37 | pages = 97–111 | pmid = 19868716 | last2 = Hatcher | first2 = RA | issue = 1 | pmc = 2128404}}</ref>

目前酒石酸锑钾仍被用于[[动物学|动物学]]研究中被捕获动物的催吐，以了解它们的食谱。<ref>{{cite journal | jstor = 1369067 | pages = 98–104 | last1 = Poulin | first1 = B. | last2 = Lefebvre | first2 = G. t. | last3 = McNeil | first3 = R. | title = Effect and Efficiency of Tartar Emetic in Determining the Diet of Tropical Land Birds | volume = 96 | issue = 1 | journal = The Condor | year = 1994 | doi = 10.2307/1369067}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | jstor = 27639314 | pages = 126–135 | last1 = Carlisle | first1 = J. D. | last2 = Holberton | first2 = R. L. | title = Relative Efficiency of Fecal versus Regurgitated Samples for Assessing Diet and the Deleterious Effects of a Tartar Emetic on Migratory Birds | volume = 77 | issue = 2 | journal = Journal of Field Ornithology | year = 2006 | doi = 10.1111/j.1557-9263.2006.00032.x}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | jstor =27715221| title = Commentary: Ipecac: An Improved Emetic for Wild Birds| first1 =Antony W. | last1 = Diamond | first2= V. C. | last2 = Fayad | first3= Peter S. | last3 = McKinley | journal = Journal of Field Ornithology | volume = 78 | issue = 4 | year = 2007 | pages = 436–439 | doi =10.1111/j.1557-9263.2007.00136.x }}</ref>

==药用==
酒石酸锑钾最早的药用是在1906年用于治疗[[锥虫病|锥虫病]]的试验，并随即开展了用这种化合物治疗其他[[热带病|热带病]]的研究。<ref name="Low">{{cite journal | doi =10.1016/S0035-9203(16)90068-3 | title =The history of the use of intravenous injections of tartar emetic (Antimonium tartaratum) in tropical medicine | year =1916 | last1 =Low | first1 =George C. | journal =Transactions of the Royal Society of Tropical Medicine and Hygiene | volume =10 | issue =2 | pages =37 }}</ref>

在1913年，酒石酸锑钾开始用于治疗利什曼病。随着毒性更小的五价锑药物（如[[葡萄糖酸锑钠|葡萄糖酸锑钠]]和[[葡甲胺锑酸盐|葡甲胺锑酸盐]]）的发现，酒石酸锑钾被逐渐淘汰。<ref name="Low"/><ref>{{cite journal | doi =10.1016/j.drudis.2010.10.005 | title =Metal-based drugs for malaria, trypanosomiasis and leishmaniasis: Recent achievements and perspectives | year =2010 | last1 =Navarro | first1 =Maribel | last2 =Gabbiani | first2 =Chiara | last3 =Messori | first3 =Luigi | last4 =Gambino | first4 =Dinorah | journal =Drug Discovery Today | volume =15 | issue =23–24 | pages =1070–8 | pmid =20974285}}</ref>

[[英国|英国]]医生John B. Christopherson于1918年发现了酒石酸锑钾能够治愈血吸虫病，在这之后它开始被广泛使用。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/S0140-6736(01)02807-0 | title = The Successful Use of Antimony in Bilharziosis | year = 1918 | last1 = Christopherson | first1 = J.B. | journal = The Lancet | volume = 192 | issue = 4958 | pages = 325}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | url = http://books.google.de/books?id=L3OsNIofmHQC | title = Poison in small measure: Dr. Christopherson and the cure for bilharzia | isbn = 978-90-04-17541-9 | author1 = Crichton-Harris | first1 = Ann | date = 2009-07-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/0014-4894(86)90184-0 | title = Schistosoma mansoni: Chemotherapy of infections of different ages | year = 1986 | last1 = Sabah | first1 = A.A. | last2 = Fletcher | first2 = Cathy | last3 = Webbe | first3 = G. | last4 = Doenhoff | first4 = M.J. | journal = Experimental Parasitology | volume = 61 | issue = 3 | pages = 294–303 | pmid = 3086114}}</ref> 但是，人们发现注射酒石酸锑钾能够引起十分严重甚至致命的副作用（如[[阿－斯综合征|阿－斯综合征]]），<ref>{{cite journal|title=Cardiac manifestations of the toxic action of potassium antimony tartrate in schistosomiasis patients:  paroxysmal ventricular tachycardia and fibrillation|pages=365–378|issn=1002-0187|journal=Chinese Medical Journal|volume=75|issue=5|date=1957-5|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/13447130|accessdate=2019-02-13|author=S. C. T'ao|pmid=13447130}}</ref> 因此研究者开始寻找更好的替代药物。20世纪70年代[[吡喹酮|吡喹酮]]的发现使得酒石酸锑钾等[[锑|锑]]剂逐渐被停止使用。<ref>{{cite journal | doi =10.1017/S003118200999093X | title =Schistosomiasis – a rich vein of research | year =2009 | last1 =Hagan | first1 =Paul | journal =Parasitology | volume =136 | issue =12 | pages =1611–9 | pmid =19691867 }}</ref><ref>{{cite book | url=http://books.google.de/books?id=GlICpnVArb8C&pg=PA304 | pages =304–305 | chapter =The Antimonials | title=Current Medicinal Chemistry | author1=Publishers | first1=Bentham Science | date=1996-10}}</ref>

==毒性==
与所有锑化合物一样，酒石酸锑钾具有毒性。<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Winship | first1 = K. A. | title = Toxicity of antimony and its compounds | journal = Adverse drug reactions and acute poisoning reviews | volume = 6 | issue = 2 | pages = 67–90 | year = 1987 | pmid = 3307336 }}</ref> 中毒的主要原因曾是药物过量；目前则多由误服引起：如曾有报道四人在食用由含酒石酸锑钾（误作[[酒石酸氢钾|酒石酸氢钾]]而掺入）的[[泡打粉|泡打粉]]制作的蛋糕后发生了严重中毒，其中一位93岁的中毒者死于[[多器官衰竭|多器官衰竭]]；<ref>{{cite journal|title=Oral antimony intoxications in man|pages=324–326|issn=0090-3493|journal=Critical Care Medicine|volume=18|issue=3|date=1990-3|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2302961|accessdate=2019-02-13|author=L. F. Lauwers, A. Roelants, P. M. Rosseel, B. Heyndrickx, L. Baute|pmid=2302961|journal=British Medical Journal|issn=0007-1447|title=Address on the Progress of Medicine|volume=1|issue=697|pages=604–605|date=1874-05-09|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2302961/|accessdate=2019-02-13|author=John Richard Wardell|pmid=20747493|pmc=PMC2302961}}</ref> 以及在[[中美洲|中美洲]]有一位[[酗酒|酗酒]]者在其妻子给其服用未知成分（后来得知为不纯的酒石酸锑钾）的“戒酒药”之后发生持续的呕吐，之后在[[重症监护病房|重症监护病房]]中检测出严重的[[心脏|心脏]]、[[肝脏|肝脏]]、[[肾脏|肾脏]]功能异常并几乎因此而死亡。<ref>{{cite journal | doi =10.1056/NEJMcpc1111580 | title =Case 22-2012 | year =2012 | last1 =Cabot | first1 =Richard C. | last2 =Harris | first2 =Nancy Lee | last3 =Shepard | first3 =Jo-Anne O. | last4 =Rosenberg | first4 =Eric S. | last5 =Cort | first5 =Alice M. | last6 =Ebeling | first6 =Sally H. | last7 =Peters | first7 =Christine C. | last8 =MacÍas Konstantopoulos | first8 =Wendy | last9 =Burns Ewald | first9 =Michele | journal =New England Journal of Medicine | volume =367 | issue =3 | pages =259}}</ref>

==制取==
通过将[[三氧化二锑|三氧化二锑]]溶解在酒石酸中能够很容易制得酒石酸锑钾。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1002/jlac.18390320107 | title = Zur Bildungsgeschichte des Brechweinsteins | year = 1839 | last1 = Knapp | first1 = Fr. | journal = Annalen der Pharmacie | volume = 32 | pages = 76}}</ref>

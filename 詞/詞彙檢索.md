'''詞彙檢索'''、'''詞彙記憶'''、'''詞彙吸收'''（{{lang-en|Word retrieval}}）指的是找到與自己腦海中的想法相對應的詞彙以便將該想法以符合邏輯和文法的方式表達出來讓大家聽得懂的能力。<ref name="Wiegel-Crump Koenigsknecht 1973 pp. 411–418">{{cite journal | last=Wiegel-Crump | first=Carole | last2=Koenigsknecht | first2=Roy A. | title=Tapping the Lexical Store of the Adult Aphasic: Analysis of the Improvement Made in Word Retrieval Skills | journal=Cortex | publisher=Elsevier BV | volume=9 | issue=4 | year=1973 | issn=0010-9452 | doi=10.1016/s0010-9452(73)80040-1 | pages=411–418}}</ref><ref name="Oxford University Press (OUP) 1985 pp. 817–829">{{cite journal | title=TREATMENT OF WORD RETRIEVAL DEFICITS IN APHASIA | journal=Brain | publisher=Oxford University Press (OUP) | volume=108 | issue=4 | year=1985 | issn=0006-8950 | doi=10.1093/brain/108.4.817 | pages=817–829}}</ref>詞彙檢索缺陷（詞窮）常見於[[失語症|失語症]]的患者身上。<ref name="Wiegel-Crump Koenigsknecht 1973 pp. 411–418"/><ref name="Oxford University Press (OUP) 1985 pp. 817–829"/>

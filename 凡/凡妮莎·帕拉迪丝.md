'''凡妮莎·尚塔尔·帕拉迪丝'''（'''Vanessa Chantal Paradis'''，{{IPA-fr|vanɛsa ʃɑ̃tal paʁadi}}，{{bd|1972年|12月22日|catIdx=P}}<ref name="bio">{{cite web | url=http://www.biography.com/people/vanessa-paradis-448362 | title=Vanessa Paradis biography | publisher=[[Biography.com|Biography.com]] | accessdate=2012-06-25}}</ref><ref name="hol">{{cite web | url=http://www.hollywoodlife.com/2010/12/22/happy-birthday-vanessa-paradis-youre-38-today-december-22/ | title=Happy Birthday, Vanessa Paradis! You’re 38 Today, December 22! | work=[[荷里活報道|The Hollywood Reporter]] | date=2010-12-22 | accessdate=2012-06-25 | author=Leigh}}</ref>）是一位[[法国|法国]]歌手，模特和演员。她14岁时凭借单曲''《[[Joe_le_taxi|Joe le taxi]]》''而成为红遍欧洲的童星。自1991年以来，帕拉迪丝一直是[[香奈兒|香奈兒]]的[[宣傳模特兒|宣傳模特兒]]。
1998年到2012年她曾与演员[[约翰尼·德普|约翰尼·德普]]保持恋人关系。帕拉迪丝的姐妹[[Alysson_Paradis|Alysson Paradis]]，是一位法国女演员。<ref name="sister">{{cite web | url=http://www.purePeople.com/people/alysson-paradis_p245 | title=Alysson Paradis | publisher=PurePeople.com | accessdate=2012-07-03| quote = Née en 1982, la petite soeur de - la chanteuse et comédienne - Vanessa Paradis…. / Born in 1982, the little sister [of] singer and actress  Vanessa Paradis….}}</ref><ref name="ac">{{cite web | url= http://www.allocine.fr/personne/fichepersonne_gen_cpersonne=99706.html | title=Alysson Paradis | publisher=[[AlloCiné|AlloCiné]] | accessdate=2012-06-30}}</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
1988年到1991年，帕拉迪丝与法国歌手[[Florent_Pagny|Florent Pagny]]恋爱。<ref name="msn">{{cite web | url=http://divertissements.fr.msn.com/people/johnny-depp-et-vanessa-paradis-zoom-sur-leurs-anciennes-conquetes?page=3 | title=Vanessa Paradis et Florent Pagny | publisher=[[MSN|MSN]] | language=fr | date=2012-06-12 | accessdate=2012-06-30}}</ref><ref name="pp">{{cite web | url=http://www.purepeople.com/article/florent-pagny-parle-de-son-ex-vanessa-paradis-n-a-pas-voulu-faire-de-duo-avec-lui_a31981/1 | title=Florent Pagny parle de son ex : Vanessa Paradis n'a pas voulu faire de duo avec lui ! | publisher=PurePeople.com | language=fr | date=2009-05-25 | accessdate=2012-06-30 | quote =  ...Vanessa Paradis, avec qui il est resté trois ans de 1988 à 1991. / ...Vanessa Paradis , with whom he remained for three years from 1988 to 1991.}}</ref>1992年，她开始与[[藍尼·克羅維茲|藍尼·克羅維茲]]约会。<ref name="bio" /><ref name="dm">{{cite web | url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-206964/Will-Lenny-tamed.html | title=Will Lenny ever be tamed? | work=[[每日郵報|Daily Mail]] | accessdate=2012-06-25 | author=Boshoff, Alison}}</ref>后来，她和法国的斯洛文尼亚裔演员[[Stanislas_Merhar|Stanislas Merhar]]传出恋情，直到1998年他们分手。<ref name="msn1">{{cite web | url=http://entertainment.in.msn.com/gallery/johnny-depp-and-vanessa-paradis-split-after-14-years?page=8 | title=Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis split after 14 years | publisher=[[MSN|MSN]] | date=2012-06-20 | accessdate=2012-06-30 | author=Roper, Kerri-Ann}}</ref><ref name="ac1">{{cite web | url=http://www.allocine.fr/article/fichearticle_gen_carticle=314141.html | title=Vanessa Paradis vit un enfer | publisher=[[AlloCiné|AlloCiné]] | language = fr | date=1998-03-11 | accessdate=2012-06-30 | author=J.H. | quote = Tout n'est pas si facile pour Vanessa Paradis.

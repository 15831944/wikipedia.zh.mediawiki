'''球腔菌科'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''Mycosphaerellaceae'''}}）是一種[[子囊菌门|子囊菌门]][[座囊菌纲|座囊菌纲]][[煤炱目|煤炱目]][[真菌|真菌]]的[[科_(生物)|科]]。本科的多個品種都是[[植物病理學|植物病原體]]，會影響很多常見的植物品種，例如：[[桉樹|桉樹]]、[[Myrtaceae|myrtle]] family等<ref name="PérezWingfield2012">{{cite journal|last1=Pérez|first1=C.A.|last2=Wingfield|first2=M.J.|last3=Altier|first3=N.|last4=Blanchette|first4=R.A.|title=Species of Mycosphaerellaceae and Teratosphaeriaceae on native Myrtaceae in Uruguay: Evidence of fungal host jumps|journal=Fungal Biology|year=2012|issn=18786146|doi=10.1016/j.funbio.2012.12.002}}</ref>及[[Proteaceae|Proteaceae]]<ref name="TaylorGroenewald2003">{{cite journal|last1=Taylor|first1=Joanne E.|last2=Groenewald|first2=J.Z. Ewald|last3=Crous|first3=Pedro W.|title=A phylogenetic analysis of Mycosphaerellaceae leaf spot pathogens of Proteaceae|journal=Mycological Research|volume=107|issue=6|year=2003|pages=653–658|issn=09537562|doi=10.1017/S0953756203007950}}</ref>等，分佈非常廣泛。

'''偏磷酸铀酰'''是由[[铀|铀]]、[[磷|磷]]和[[氧|氧]]构成的[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为[UO<sub>2</sub>(PO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub>]<sub>n</sub>，这种长链化合物可以通过UO<sub>2</sub>(H<sub>2</sub>PO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>·3H<sub>2</sub>O的热分解反应得到。<ref name="KamoOhashi1970">{{cite journal|last1=Kamo|first1=Mutsukazu|last2=Ohashi|first2=Shigeru|title=Thermal Decomposition of Uranyl Dihydrogen Orthophosphate Trihydrate|journal=Bulletin of the Chemical Society of Japan|volume=43|issue=1|year=1970|pages=84–89|issn=0009-2673|doi=10.1246/bcsj.43.84}}</ref>它可以形成一些复盐，例如NaUO<sub>2</sub>(PO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>和CsUO<sub>2</sub>(PO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>。<ref>Lavrov, A. V. Study of condensed uranyl phosphates. ''Izvestiya Akademii Nauk SSSR, Neorganicheskie Materialy'', 1979. 15 (6): 942-946. ISSN: 0002-337X.</ref>

在较高温度，它可以分解为UP<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>并放出氧气。<ref name="Barten1988">{{cite journal|last1=Barten|first1=H.|title=The thermochemistry of uranyl phosphates|journal=Thermochimica Acta|volume=126|year=1988|pages=375–383|issn=00406031|doi=10.1016/0040-6031(88)87282-4}}</ref>

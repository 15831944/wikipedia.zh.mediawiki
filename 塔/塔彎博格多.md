'''塔彎博格多''' （{{lang-mn|Таван богд}}，「五聖」的意思）是[[蒙古國|蒙古國]]的一座山嶺，是[[阿爾泰山脈|阿爾泰山脈]]的一部份。最高點[[友誼峰|友誼峰]]海拔4,374米，是蒙古國的最高峰。而'''奎屯峰'''是蒙古、[[中國|中國]]和[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]三國的交匯點，海拔4081或4104m，坐标位于{{coord|49|10|13.5|N|87|48|56.3|E|display=inline}}<ref name=TripointProtocol>
[http://old.lawru.info/base32/part9/d32ru9848.htm] (Protocol between the Government of the Russian Federation, the Government of Mongolia, and the Government of the People's Republic of China,  describing the western junction point of the borders of the three states. Signed in Beijing, June 24, 1996)  {{ru icon}}</ref><ref name=tripointAgreement>[http://old.lawru.info/base33/part6/d33ru6020.htm] (The Agreement between the Government of the Russian Federation, the Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Government of Mongolia on the determination of the points of junction of the national borders of the three states) {{ru icon}}</ref><ref name=TopoM45.101>Soviet [http://www.mountain.ru/world_mounts/altai/2006/M45-104.jpg Topo map M45-104], scale 1:100,000, {{ru icon}}</ref>。

蒙古政府在這裡設立了[[阿爾泰塔彎博格多國家公園|阿爾泰塔彎博格多國家公園]]。

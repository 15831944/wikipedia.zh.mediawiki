《'''塔納島之戀'''》（''Tanna''）是一部2015年[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]和[[瓦努阿图|瓦努阿图]]合拍剧情片，背景设定在[[南太平洋|南太平洋]]的[[塔納島|塔納島]]，讲述一对[[部落|部落]]男女的爱情故事<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2015/nov/05/tanna-review-volcanic-south-pacific-love-story-vanuatu|title=Tanna review – volcanic South Pacific love story shot entirely in Vanuatu|author=Luke Buckmaster|work=the Guardian}}</ref>，改编自真实事件。<ref name="Australia">{{cite web|url=http://www.sbs.com.au/movies/article/2016/08/23/australia-selects-tanna-foreign-language-oscar-contender |title=Australia selects 'Tanna' as foreign-language Oscar contender |work=SBS |date=23 August 2016 |accessdate=23 August 2016}}</ref>

本片获得[[第72届威尼斯影展|第72届威尼斯影展]]观众奖<ref name="auto">{{cite web|url=http://www.sicvenezia.it/3559/edizione-2015/news-2015/premio-del-pubblico-pietro-barzisa-a-tanna/?lang=en|title=All Awards 2015 announced: Audience Award Pietro Barzisa to Tanna by Bentley Dean and Martin Butler|author=Settimana Internazionale della Critica|work=sicvenezia.it}}</ref>，并入围[[第89届奥斯卡金像奖|第89届奥斯卡金像奖]]最佳外语片<ref name="Australia"/><ref name="Variety">{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2016/film/asia/australia-tanna-foreign-language-oscar-1201842356/ |title=Australia Selects ‘Tanna’ as Foreign-Language Oscar Contender |first=Patrick |last=Frater |work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |date=23 August 2016 |accessdate=23 August 2016}}</ref><ref name="Oscar">{{cite news|last1=Samuelson|first1=Kate|title=Here Are the 2017 Oscar Nominations|url=http://time.com/4635815/2017-oscars-nominations-list/|accessdate=24 January 2017|work=[[Time_(magazine)|Time]]|date=24 January 2017}}</ref>，成为首部入围[[奥斯卡最佳外语片|奥斯卡最佳外语片]]的澳洲电影。

==劇情==
南太平洋火山島「塔納」，眾部落圍繞神山而居，世代承襲上千年的傳統文化；早與酋長之孫私訂終身的女孩，淪為政治聯姻的犧牲品，小倆口不顧反對逃跑生活，希望能有一席之地，而在愛情與親族之間，他們最後抉擇竟然是在火山口上吃下劇毒的香菇死去，酋長們最後都希望婚姻能自由化，並化解部落的殺戳。

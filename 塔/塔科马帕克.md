'''塔科马帕克'''（{{lang en|'''Takoma Park'''}}）是[[美國|美國]][[馬里蘭州|馬里蘭州]][[蒙哥马利县_(马里兰州)|蒙哥马利县]]的一个城市，是[[华盛顿特区|华盛顿特区]]的[[郊区|郊区]]，也是华盛顿大都会地区的一部分。 1883年成立，1890年建市，非正式名为“[[杜鹃花|杜鹃花]]城”的塔科马帕克是美国树城和无核区。 计划的[[通勤|通勤]]郊区，位于华盛顿特区东北部的历史悠久的[[巴尔的摩与俄亥俄铁路|巴尔的摩与俄亥俄铁路]]大都会分支沿线，毗邻华盛顿特区塔科马，由当选市长和六名当选议员组成市议会，一个任命的城市经理，根据议会-经理制政府的方式管理。 2010年全国人口普查的城市人口为16715人。<ref name="about">{{Cite web

自2013年以来，十六岁以上塔科马帕克的居民可以投票参加市政选举。这是美国第一个在城市选举中向16岁和17岁的人开放投票的城市。<ref name= grants>{{cite news |title= Takoma Park grants 16-year-olds right to vote |first= Lindsay A. |last= Powers |work= The Washington Post |date= May 14, 2013 |url= https://www.washingtonpost.com/local/takoma-park-grants-16-year-olds-right-to-vote/2013/05/14/b27c52c4-bccd-11e2-89c9-3be8095fe767_story.html |quote= ''During its Monday meeting, the Takoma Park City Council passed a series of city charter amendments regarding its voting and election laws, including one allowing 16- and 17-year-olds to vote in city elections. ... With Monday’s vote, Takoma Park became the first city in the United States to lower its voting age — which was previously 18 — to 16.'' }}</ref>此后，海厄茨维尔市也跟进。<ref>{{cite news |title= Council lowers Hyattsville voting age to 16 years old |first= Rebecca |last= Bennett |work= Hyattsville Life & Times |date= January 6, 2015 |url= http://hyattsvillelife.com/breaking-news-council-lowers-hyattsville-voting-age-to-16-years-old/ |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20150307045341/http://hyattsvillelife.com/breaking-news-council-lowers-hyattsville-voting-age-to-16-years-old/ |archivedate= March 7, 2015 }}</ref>

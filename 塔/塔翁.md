'''塔翁'''（{{lang|en|''Taung''}}）是[[南非|南非]][[西北省_(南非)|西北省]]的一個小鎮。塔翁在當地原住民語言[[茨瓦纳语|茨瓦纳语]]的意思是「獅子之地」，得名於當地過往一位名叫「獅子」（{{lang|ts|''tau''}}）的酋長。

==埋藏學==
[[File:Taung1.jpg|thumb]]
當初塔翁小童發現之時，[[:en:Raymond_Dart|Raymond Dart]]提出了Osteo-Dento-Keratic文化假說，認為塔翁小童是被其他人科生物殺死的。其後，later work by [[Charles_Kimberlin_Brain|C.K. "Bob" Brain]] demonstrated that the child was probably killed by some sort of mammalian [[carnivore|carnivore]] such as a [[leopard|leopard]].  Recently, however, studies of the associated baboons by [[Ronald_J._Clarke|Ron Clarke]] and [[Lee_R._Berger|Lee Berger]], and identification of specific marks on the Taung Child skull have demonstrated that the Taung Child may have been killed and eaten by a large [[bird_of_prey|bird of prey]].

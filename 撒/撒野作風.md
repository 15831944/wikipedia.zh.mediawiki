'''撒野作風'''（Wildstyle Records）<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://absurdcreationmusic.wordpress.com/2015/12/02/interview-wildtyle-records%EF%BC%88%E6%92%92%E9%87%8E%E4%BD%9C%E9%A2%A8%EF%BC%89/|title=Interview: Wild$tyle Records（撒野作風）|accessdate=|author=|date=|publisher=absurdcreation music}}</ref>是位於香港的一家獨立唱片廠牌。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://neocha.com/magazine/wildstyle-and-youngqueenz/|title=WILD$TYLE & YoungQueenz|accessdate=|author=|date=April 26, 2016|publisher=neocha}}</ref>
== 簡介 ==
由[[YoungQueenz|YoungQueenz]]以及GrymeMan等人於2011年成立。Matt Force, Mike Waves和Triple G都是該公司最初擁有的歌手。<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://juicestore.com/blogs/editorial/youngqueenz-flexing|title=YOUNGQUEENZ FLEXING ON THE FAR EASTERN STYLE|author=|date=|work=|accessdate=|quote=How did Wildstyle Records happen?
It started with GrymeMan, USB (former member of Wildstyle Records), and I. Grymeman, USB and I have been talking ever since I dropped my first mixtape “OZMA of OZ” when I was 15 years old. Since we have similar interests we eventually wanted to start a label together and gather other like-minded talents. Then Matt Force and MicLi joined the label soon after. We kept releasing more and more music while also scouting for talents; eventually we included N.O.L.Y, Klassick, and others.}}</ref>

== 代理發行 ==
根據2018年十月的一篇訪問<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.weibo.com/ttarticle/p/show?id=2309404293917355998862&is_all=1|title=从我们的镜头出发，香港暗夜下的YoungQueenz {{!}} HUMANS OF WHOOSIS|author=WHOOSIS|date=|work=|accessdate=|quote=" YoungQueenz : ... Wildstyle Records 同時是一個發佈平台。除了自家固定的音樂人，WSR也會幫其他不屬於WSR ，但我們覺得「正」的音樂人製作MV和作為一個平台發佈，讓更多人知道他們..."}}</ref>，撒野作風 Wildstyle Records 除了有旗下固定的藝人，其YouTube頻道還會作為開放的平台為其他不屬於撒野作風 Wildstyle Records 的音樂人作代理發行。曾經代理發行過的音樂人有「VROSKIII」「火炭麗琪」,「柒羊」等等。

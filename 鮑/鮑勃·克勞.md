'''鮑勃·克勞'''（{{lang-en|Robert Crow}}；{{bd|1961年|6月13日|2014年|3月11日}}）是一名[[英国|英国]]工会领袖。2002年起担任[[铁路、海事和运输工人全国联盟|铁路、海事和运输工人全国联盟]]总书记直至逝世，他帮助这个协会成为英国发展最快的工会<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/business/3850251.stm Bob Crow: workers' friend?], BBC, 5 September 2007</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.rmt.org.uk/Templates/Internal.asp?NodeID=105008&int1stParentNodeID=89732 | title = RMT Membership passes 80,000 | accessdate = 21 July 2010 | publisher = RMT Union | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100830122402/http://www.rmt.org.uk/Templates/Internal.asp?NodeID=105008&int1stParentNodeID=89732 | archivedate = 30 八月 2010 }}</ref>。

2014年，他因为心脏病和[[动脉瘤|动脉瘤]]，病逝于英国{{link-en|威普大学医院|Whipps Cross University Hospital}}，享年52岁<ref name="BBC">{{cite web |url= http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-26527325 |title= RMT union general secretary Bob Crow dies |date= 11 March 2014 |accessdate= 11 March 2014 |work= [[BBC_News|BBC News]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/uknews/road-and-rail-transport/10689336/Bob-Crow-leader-of-the-RMT-dies.html |title= Bob Crow, leader of the RMT, dies |date= 11 March 2014 |accessdate= 11 March 2014 |first= Matthew |last= Holehouse |work= The Telegraph}}</ref>。

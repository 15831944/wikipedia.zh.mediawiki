'''溴化羟胺'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为NH<sub>2</sub>OH<sub>2</sub>Br。它可由[[硫酸羟胺|硫酸羟胺]]和[[溴化钡|溴化钡]]反应得到<ref name=Adams>Adams M. [http://storage.lib.uchicago.edu/pres/2006/pres2006-0482.pdf On some hydroxylamine compounds.][M]. Press of The Chemical Publishing Co., 1902.</ref>，或者由[[氯化羟胺|氯化羟胺]]在[[氢溴酸|氢溴酸]]溶液中通过蒸发结晶得到。它可以用于制备含[[一氧化二氮|一氧化二氮]]配体的配合物。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://doi.wiley.com/10.1002/9780470132470.ch22|publisher=John Wiley & Sons, Inc.|pages=75–77|title=(Dinitrogen Oxide)Pentaammineruthenium(II) Salts|date=2007-01-05|isbn=9780470132470|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-26|author=F. Bottomley, J. N. Armor|doi=10.1002/9780470132470.ch22}}</ref>

溴化羟胺和氯化羟胺同构，空间群<math>C_{2h}^5</math>-P2<sub>1</sub>/''c''，晶胞参数a=7.29, b=6.13, c=8.04 A; β=120.8°。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://scripts.iucr.org/cgi-bin/paper?S0365110X48000053|issue=1|doi=10.1107/s0365110x48000053|pages=21–27|title=The structure of hydroxylammonium chloride, NH3OHCl, and hydroxylammonium bromide, NH3OHBr|volume=1|journal=Acta Crystallographica|date=1948-03-01|issn=0365-110X|language=en|accessdate=2018-07-26|author=B. Jerslev}}</ref>它和氯化羟胺相比，是更强的还原剂。<ref name=Adams />

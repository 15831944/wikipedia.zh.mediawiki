'''溴氯海因'''，即'''1-溴-3-氯-5,5-二甲基乙内酰脲'''是一种[[乙内酰脲|乙内酰脲]]类化合物，缩写为'''BCDMH'''。它是白色晶体，略有溴和丙酮的气味，难溶于水，可溶于丙酮。

溴氯海因可以很好地作为氯和溴的来源，它和水缓慢反应生成[[次氯酸|次氯酸]]和[[次溴酸|次溴酸]]，可用于游泳池用水、饮用水的净化与消毒。<ref name=NSF>{{Cite web

| title = NSF/ANSI 60 - Drinking Water Treatment Chemicals - Health Effects
| access-date = November 14, 2018
| author = [[NSF_International|NSF International]]

| quote =  Bromochlorodimethylhydantoin[CL] - Bromicide Tablets - Algicide - Disinfection & Oxidation. [CL] The residual levels of chlorine (hypochlorite ion and hypochlorous acid), chlorine dioxide, chlorate ion, chloramine and disinfection by-products shall be monitored in the finished drinking water to ensure compliance to all applicable regulations.}}</ref>它和水按下式反应（R为出除去卤素的部分）：<ref>South Australian Health Commission, "Standard for the Operation of Swimming Pools and Spa Pools in South Australia",  [http://www.dh.sa.gov.au/pehs/publications/code-bromine.pdf Supplement C: Bromine Disinfection], page 8. Retrieved on 2009-05-12.</ref>

: BrClR + 2 H<sub>2</sub>O → HOBr + HOCl + RH<sub>2</sub>

生成的次溴酸会将细菌氧化，自身还原为Br<sup>−</sup>：

: Br<sup>−</sup> + HOCl → HOBr + Cl<sup>−</sup>

因此，次氯酸本身并不直接参与消毒过程。

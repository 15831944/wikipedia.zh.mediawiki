'''溴氧化铋'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为BiOBr。它在冷碱溶液中稳定，加热转化为[[氧化铋|Bi<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub>]]。<ref>无机化学丛书 第四卷 氮 磷 砷分族. 科学出版社. pp 496. "4.4 铋的卤氧化物"</ref>

==制备==
将[[硝酸铋|硝酸铋]]和[[溴化钠|溴化钠]]的稀溶液混合搅拌，滴加氢氧化钠，可以制得溴氧化铋（BiOBr）。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0926337315301776|doi=10.1016/j.apcatb.2015.09.050|pages=431–438|title=Thickness-dependent photocatalytic activity of bismuth oxybromide nanosheets with highly exposed (0  1  0) facets|volume=182|journal=Applied Catalysis B: Environmental|date=2016-03|issn=0926-3373|accessdate=2018-08-20|author=Haiping Li, Tingxia Hu, Jianqiang Liu, Shue Song, Na Du, Renjie Zhang, Wanguo Hou}}</ref>不同的反应条件也能得到不同比例的化合物，如Bi<sub>4</sub>O<sub>5</sub>Br<sub>2</sub><ref>{{cite journal|url=http://xlink.rsc.org/?DOI=C5TA02388B|issue=29|doi=10.1039/c5ta02388b|pages=15108–15118|title=Controllable synthesis of Bi4O5Br2 ultrathin nanosheets for photocatalytic removal of ciprofloxacin and mechanism insight|volume=3|journal=Journal of Materials Chemistry A|date=2015|issn=2050-7488|language=en|accessdate=2018-08-20|author=Jun Di, Jiexiang Xia, Mengxia Ji, Sheng Yin, Hongping Li, Hui Xu, Qi Zhang, Huaming Li}}</ref>、Bi<sub>24</sub>O<sub>31</sub>Br<sub>10</sub><ref>{{cite journal|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/cs401025u|issue=3|doi=10.1021/cs401025u|pages=954–961|title=Bismuth Oxybromide with Reasonable Photocatalytic Reduction Activity under Visible Light|volume=4|journal=ACS Catalysis|date=2014-02-19|issn=2155-5435|language=EN|accessdate=2018-08-20|author=Jun Shang, Weichang Hao, Xiaojun Lv, Tianmin Wang, Xiaolin Wang, Yi Du, Shixue Dou, Tengfeng Xie, Dejun Wang, Jiaou Wang}}</ref>等。

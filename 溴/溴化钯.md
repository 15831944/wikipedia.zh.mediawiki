'''溴化钯'''是一种[[钯|钯]]的溴化物，化学式为{{化學式|鈀|溴|2}}，25度密度5.173 g/mL。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.chemicalbook.com/ProductChemicalPropertiesCB8853059.htm|title=溴化钯|accessdate=2016-08-22|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref> 它广泛作为商品出售，用作钯化学的起始原料，尽管比[[氯化钯|氯化钯]]少见。与氯化钯类似，溴化钯不溶于水，但加热时能溶于[[乙腈|乙腈]]，并以单聚体形式形成[[加合物|加合物]]：<ref>{{cite journal | title = Palladium complexes based on optically active terpene derivatives of ethylenediamine | journal = Russian Journal of Coordination Chemistry | volume = 34 | issue = 11 | year = 2008 | doi = 10.1134/S1070328408110110 | pages = 855–857 | author = O. A. Zalevskaya, E. G. Vorob’eva1, I. A. Dvornikova and A. V. Kuchin}}</ref>

: PdBr<sub>2</sub> + 2 MeCN → PdBr<sub>2</sub>(MeCN)<sub>2</sub>

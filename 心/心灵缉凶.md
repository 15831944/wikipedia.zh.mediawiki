《'''心灵缉凶'''》（{{lang-en|Cracked}}）是一部[[加拿大|加拿大]][[电视剧|电视剧]]，由Tracey Forbes和Calum de Hartog创造，于2013年1月8日起在[[加拿大国营频道|加拿大国营频道]]播出<ref name="CBC RG">{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/revenuegroup/cracked.html |title=CBC Revenue Group - Cracked |accessdate=7 January 2013}}</ref>。


== 简介 ==
本剧讲述一名压力过大而精神不稳定的警探艾丹被上级调到精神病犯罪专案组，原本只是希望这里的同事能帮忙关注他的精神状况；当大家认为艾丹状态不佳的时候他却在新部门大展身手再度侦破悬案。

== 开发和制作 ==
2012年4月CBC在宣布续订节目时就表示将在下一季度（2012-2013）播出一部名为Cracked的犯罪剧集，该剧集将由White Pine Pictures制作<ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/mediacentre/despite-challenges-cbc-tv-announces-a-strong-roster-of-canadian-shows-for-the-2012-13-season.html DESPITE CHALLENGES, CBC TV ANNOUNCES A STRONG ROSTER OF CANADIAN SHOWS FOR THE 2012-13 SEASON]</ref><ref>[http://mediaincanada.com/2012/04/19/arctic-air-dragons-den-doyle-to-return-to-cbc/ Arctic Air, Dragons’ Den, Doyle to return to CBC]</ref><ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2012/04/21/april-21-text-messages-and-ratings-reported/ 4月21日简讯及收视率报告]</ref>，随后5月份CBC发布新闻稿宣布将在冬季播出该剧<ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/news/arts/story/2012/05/10/cbc-fall-season.html "CBC fall season reflects reduced budget"]. [[CBC_News|CBC News]], May 10, 2012.</ref>，本剧于2012年7月开始拍摄场景主要在多伦多取景<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tv-eh.com/2012/08/07/cbcs-cracked-starts-production/ |title=CBC's Cracked starts production |work=www.tv-eh.com |date=7 August 2012 |accessdate=2 October 2013}}</ref>。

2013年4月3日CBC宣布续订本剧第二季<ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/news/arts/story/2013/04/03/blades-cbc.html Battle of the Blades returns to CBC]</ref><ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/revenuegroup/cbc-television-announces-21-renewals-expanded-seasons-of-hit-shows-and-new-titles-for-2013-14.html CBC Television Announces 21 Renewals, Expanded Seasons of Hit Shows, and New Titles for 2013-14]</ref><ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/live/battle-is-back-cbc-announces-fall-season-lineup.html Battle is Back! CBC Announces Fall Season Lineup]</ref><ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2013/04/04/shows-in-development-2013-04-04/ 4月4日新剧开发及剧集变动信息]</ref>。

'''疊盤角石'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Discosorus'''''}}）是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[鸚鵡螺亞綱|鸚鵡螺類]]。為[[疊盤角石科|疊盤角石科]]的模式屬。生存在[[奧陶紀|奧陶紀]]末至[[志留紀|志留紀]]的[[海洋|海洋]]中。<ref name="Fossilworks">{{Cite web |url = http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=12841 |title = Fossilworks: Discosorus |language = en |accessdate = 2018-05-23 }}</ref>牠們的[[化石|化石]]分布於[[北美|北美]]東部。其外殼和[[盤角石|盤角石]]相像，但盤角石的外殼盤捲得更為緊密。<ref>''Discoceras'', p K360 in Vol K of the ''{{tsl|en|Treatise on Invertebrate Paleontology||Treatise on Invertebrate Paleontology}}'' in the section on the Trocholitidae, pp K359-K362, in the Chapter on the Tarphycerida by Furnish and Glenister starting with page K343.</ref>

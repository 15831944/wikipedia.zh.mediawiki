'''三氟乙酸钕'''是钕的三氟乙酸盐，化学式为Nd(CF<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub>，存在无水物和三水合物。它可由[[氧化钕|氧化钕]]和[[三氟乙酸|三氟乙酸]]反应，从溶液中结晶得到。
: Nd<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + 6 CF<sub>3</sub>COOH → 2 Nd(CF<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> + 3 H<sub>2</sub>O

它在300°C受热分解，生成[[氟化钕|氟化钕]]以及一些气态产物，如CF<sub>3</sub>COF、CO和CO<sub>2</sub>等。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ja01466a020|issue=5|doi=10.1021/ja01466a020|pages=1087–1088|title=Lanthanum and Neodymium Salts of Trifluoroacetic Acid|volume=83|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|date=1961-03|issn=0002-7863|language=EN|accessdate=2018-10-22|author=John E. Roberts}}</ref>它可用于制备掺杂钕的纳米颗粒材料。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0925838816326627|doi=10.1016/j.jallcom.2016.08.262|pages=530–536|title=Controlled synthesis, multicolor luminescence, and optical thermometer of bifunctional NaYbF 4 :Nd 3+ @NaYF 4 :Yb 3+ active-core/active-shell colloidal nanoparticles|volume=691|journal=Journal of Alloys and Compounds|date=2017-01|issn=0925-8388|accessdate=2018-10-24|author=Xiangfu Wang, Tongtong Xu, Peiqing Cai, ThiQuynh Vu, Hyo Jin Seo}}</ref>
: 2 Nd(CF<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub> → 2 NdF<sub>3</sub> + 3CO + 3 CO<sub>2</sub> + 3 CF<sub>3</sub>COF

'''三氯化六脲合铁(III)'''是一种[[配位化合物|配位化合物]]，化学式为[Fe(CON<sub>2</sub>H<sub>4</sub>)<sub>6</sub>]Cl<sub>3</sub>。它是浅绿色固体，可由六水合[[氯化铁|氯化铁]]和[[尿素|尿素]]在室温下按1:6化学计量比混合并研磨得到，反应吸热。<ref>Zhou, Yi-Ming; Xu, Juan; Sun, Dong-Mei; Wang, Qing; Lu, Tian-Hong; Xin, Xin-Quan. Solid state synthesis of urea complexes with iron(III)<small>（尿素Fe(Ⅲ)配合物的固相合成）</small>. Yingyong Huaxue<small>（应用化学）</small> (2003), 20(3), 305-306. (Retrieved from SciFinder)</ref>它还可以通过氯化铁的乙醇溶液和尿素反应，缓慢挥发或者加入[[己烷|己烷]]来得到。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/0022190279803875|issue=1|doi=10.1016/0022-1902(79)80387-5|pages=25–30|title=Mössbauer characterization of some new high-spin iron complexes with urea and thiourea derivatives|volume=41|journal=Journal of Inorganic and Nuclear Chemistry|date=1979-01|issn=0022-1902|accessdate=2018-10-08|author=Umberto Russo, Sandro Calogero, Nicola Burriesci, Michele Petrera}}</ref>该配合物在198°C乙二醇溶液中分解，可以制得[[四氧化三铁|Fe<sub>3</sub>O<sub>4</sub>]]。<ref>{{cite journal|issue=10|doi=10.1088/0957-4484/20/10/105603|pages=105603|title=A simple one-pot synthesis of single-crystalline magnetite hollow spheres from a single iron precursor|volume=20|journal=Nanotechnology|date=2009-02-17|url=http://dx.doi.org/10.1088/0957-4484/20/10/105603|issn=0957-4484|author=Ningning Guan, Yaotao Wang, Dejun Sun, Jian Xu|accessdate=2018-10-08}}</ref>

'''三碘合锡(II)酸甲铵'''是一种有机[[钙钛矿_(结构)|钙钛矿材料]]，化学式为CH<sub>3</sub>NH<sub>3</sub>SnI<sub>3</sub>，它作为含铅钙钛矿的替代品，在光电领域有着潜在应用。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Lead-Free Perovskite Nanowire Array Photodetectors with Drastically Improved Stability in Nanoengineering Templates|date=2017-01-11|doi=10.1021/acs.nanolett.6b04587|journal=Nano Letters|pages=523–530|volume=17|issue=1|issn=1530-6984|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.nanolett.6b04587|accessdate=2019-01-12|author=Aashir Waleed, Mohammad Mahdi Tavakoli, Leilei Gu, Ziyi Wang, Daquan Zhang, Arumugam Manikandan, Qianpeng Zhang, Rongjun Zhang, Yu-Lun Chueh, Zhiyong Fan}}</ref>

==制备==
将[[碘化亚锡|碘化亚锡]]（或[[氧化亚锡|氧化亚锡]]<ref name=Dang>{{cite journal|title=Formation of Hybrid Perovskite Tin Iodide Single Crystals by Top-Seeded Solution Growth|journal=Angewandte Chemie International Edition|volume=55|issue=10|pages=3447–3450|doi=10.1002/anie.201511792|url=https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/anie.201511792|date=2016|issn=1521-3773|language=en|accessdate=2019-01-12|author=Yangyang Dang, Yian Zhou, Xiaolong Liu, Dianxing Ju, Shengqing Xia, Haibing Xia, Xutang Tao}}</ref>）溶解在氮气保护的[[氢碘酸|氢碘酸]]和[[次磷酸|次磷酸]]混合溶液中并加热，得到亮黄色溶液，向溶液中加入甲胺氢碘酸盐固体，于120 °C蒸去一半溶剂，冷却后析出黑色的MASnI<sub>3</sub>晶体。该化合物在空气中放置很快变质。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Semiconducting Tin and Lead Iodide Perovskites with Organic Cations: Phase Transitions, High Mobilities, and Near-Infrared Photoluminescent Properties|date=2013-08-05|doi=10.1021/ic401215x|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|pages=9019–9038|volume=52|issue=15|issn=0020-1669|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ic401215x|accessdate=2019-01-12|author=Constantinos C. Stoumpos, Christos D. Malliakas, Mercouri G. Kanatzidis}} ([https://pubs.acs.org/doi/suppl/10.1021/ic401215x/suppl_file/ic401215x_si_001.pdf Supporting Information (PDF)])</ref>碘化亚锡和甲胺氢碘酸盐在[[2-丙醇|2-丙醇]]中反应，也可以得到MASnI<sub>3</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Synthesis and Characterization of Organic−Inorganic Perovskite Thin Films Prepared Using a Versatile Two-Step Dipping Technique|date=1998-01-19|doi=10.1021/cm970568f|journal=Chemistry of Materials|pages=403–411|volume=10|issue=1|issn=0897-4756|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/cm970568f|accessdate=2019-01-12|author=Kangning Liang, David B. Mitzi, Michael T. Prikas}}</ref>

在MASnI<sub>3</sub>的制备反应中，用DMF作为溶剂时，结晶速率要比同构物MAPbI<sub>3</sub>要快。而在DMSO中的反应则会产生黄色的SnI<sub>2</sub>·3DMSO中间体，该中间体与同族的PbI<sub>2</sub>·2DMSO晶体结构不同。[[N-甲基吡咯烷酮|N-甲基吡咯烷酮]]和[[γ-丁内酯|γ-丁内酯]]作为溶剂的反应也有研究。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Solvent-Mediated Crystallization of CH3NH3SnI3 Films for Heterojunction Depleted Perovskite Solar Cells|date=2015-09-09|doi=10.1021/jacs.5b06658|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|pages=11445–11452|volume=137|issue=35|issn=0002-7863|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/jacs.5b06658|accessdate=2019-01-12|author=Feng Hao, Constantinos C. Stoumpos, Peijun Guo, Nanjia Zhou, Tobin J. Marks, Robert P. H. Chang, Mercouri G. Kanatzidis}}</ref>

==性质==
三碘合锡(II)酸甲铵的[[带隙|带隙]]是1.15 eV，比多晶或薄膜的样品要小，但在空气中放置一个月后，带隙升至1.46 eV，并伴随着分子中的二价锡氧化为四价锡。

三碘合锡(II)酸甲铵加热至200 °C分解，主要产生是碘化亚锡。<ref name=Dang />

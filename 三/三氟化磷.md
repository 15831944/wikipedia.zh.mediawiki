'''三氟化磷'''是一种无色无味的气体，化学式为PF<sub>3</sub>，高毒，可以和水缓慢反应。它在配合物中可以作为配体，与[[一氧化碳|一氧化碳]]（羰基）作为配体时性质相似。<ref name="ref6">{{cite journal | author = Chatt, J. | title = The Co-Ordinate Link in Chemistry | journal = Nature | year = 1950 | volume = 165 | issue = 4199 | pages = 637–638 | doi = 10.1038/165637a0 | pmid = 15416738 }}</ref>其毒性机理和一氧化碳类似，与[[血红蛋白|血红蛋白]]中的铁结合成稳定物质。它在过渡金属配合物中可以作为强的π受体，<ref name="ref1 p494">{{Greenwood&Earnshaw | page = 494 }}</ref>能够和过渡金属形成低价配合物。三氟化磷可以形成一些配合物，而相应羰基配合物不稳定或不存在。如Pd(PF<sub>3</sub>)<sub>4</sub>是已知的，Pd(CO)<sub>4</sub>却未有报道。<ref name="ref9">{{cite book | author = Nicholls, D. | title = Complexes and First-Row Transition Elements | publisher = Macmillan Press | location = London | year = 1973 }}</ref><ref name="ref10">{{cite journal | author = Kruck, T. | title = Trifluorphosphin-Komplexe von Übergangsmetallen | journal = Angewandte Chemie | year = 1967 | volume = 79 | issue = 1 | pages = 27–43 | doi = 10.1002/ange.19670790104 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author1=Clark, R. J. |author2=Busch, M. A. | title = Stereochemical Studies of Metal Carbonyl-Phosphorus Trifluoride Complexes | journal = Accounts of Chemical Research | year = 1973 | volume = 6 | issue = 7 | pages = 246–252 | doi = 10.1021/ar50067a005 }}</ref>

'''三(2-萘基)膦'''是一种[[有机磷化合物|有机磷化合物]]，化学式为C<sub>30</sub>H<sub>21</sub>P，它的同分异构体之一是[[三(1-萘基)膦|三(1-萘基)膦]]。

==制备==
镁在无水THF中的悬浊液和[[2-溴萘|2-溴萘]]反应，之后将[[三氯化磷|三氯化磷]]滴加至混合物中，回流，得到三(2-萘基)膦。<ref>Elard, Maxime; Denis, Julien; Ferreira, Michel, ''et al.'' Rhodium catalyzed hydroformylation assisted by cyclodextrins in biphasic medium: Can sulfonated naphthylphosphanes lead to active, selective and recyclable catalytic species? ''Catalysis Today'', 2015. 247: 47-54. DOI: 10.1016/j.cattod.2014.06.002</ref>

'''三氟甲磺酸锌'''是[[锌|锌]]的[[三氟甲磺酸盐|三氟甲磺酸盐]]。它通常用作[[路易斯酸|路易斯酸]][[催化剂|催化剂]]，例如用于{{le|硅烷化反应|silylation}}。<ref>{{cite journal |author1=H. Jiang  |author2=S. Zhu  |lastauthoramp=yes | title = Silylation of 1-alkynes with chlorosilanes promoted by Zn(OTf)2: an efficient way to the preparation of alkynylsilanes | journal = [[Tetrahedron_Letters|Tetrahedron Letters]] | volume = 46 | issue = 3 | pages = 517–519 | doi = 10.1016/j.tetlet.2004.10.175 | year = 2005}}</ref>

三氟甲磺酸锌是白色粉末，尽管它存在市售品，但是一些科研人员会通过不同的方法制备它。<ref>{{cite journal |author1=R. J. Rahaim  |author2=J. T. Shaw  |lastauthoramp=yes | title = Zinc-Catalyzed Silylation of Terminal Alkynes | journal = [[J._Org._Chem.|J. Org. Chem.]] | volume = 73 | issue = 7 | pages = 2912–2915 | doi = 10.1021/jo702557d | pmid = 18331056 | year = 2008}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = J. E. D. Kirkham, T. D. L. Courtney, V. Lee and J. E. Baldwin | title = Asymmetric synthesis of cytotoxic sponge metabolites R-strongylodiols A and B and an analogue | journal = [[Tetrahedron_(journal)|Tetrahedron]] | volume = 61 | issue = 30 | pages = 7219–7232 | doi = 10.1016/j.tet.2005.05.034 | year = 2005}}</ref>例如，它可以由[[三氟甲磺酸|三氟甲磺酸]]和金属[[锌|锌]]在丙酮中反应得到，<ref>{{cite journal | author = J. Lombard, S. Romain, S. Dumas, J. Chauvin, M.-N. Collomb, D. Daveloose, A. Deronzier and J.-C. Leprêtre | title = Tetranuclear Polypyridyl Complexes of RuII and FeII: Synthesis, Electrochemical, Photophysical and Photochemical Behaviour | journal = [[European_Journal_of_Inorganic_Chemistry|European Journal of Inorganic Chemistry]] | volume = 2005 | issue = 16 | pages = 3320–3330 | doi = 10.1002/ejic.200500106 | year = 2005}}</ref>或者在[[甲醇|甲醇]]中和[[碳酸锌|碳酸锌]]反应来制备：<ref>{{cite journal |author1=E. J. Corey  |author2=K. Shimoji  |lastauthoramp=yes | title = Magnesium and zinc-catalyzed thioketalization | journal = [[Tetrahedron_Letters|Tetrahedron Letters]] | volume = 24 | issue = 2 | pages = 169–172 | doi = 10.1016/S0040-4039(00)81357-X | year = 1983}}</ref>

:Zn + 2 HOTf → Zn(OTf)<sub>2</sub> + H<sub>2</sub>↑
:ZnCO<sub>3</sub> + 2 HOTf → Zn(OTf)<sub>2</sub> + H<sub>2</sub>O + CO<sub>2</sub>↑ (OTf = CF<sub>3</sub>SO<sub>2</sub>)

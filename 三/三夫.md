《'''三夫'''》（{{lang-en|'''Three Husbands'''}}）是一部2018年香港導演[[陈果_(导演)|陳果]]的劇情長片，為「妓女三部曲」的第三作<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/films/0/three-husbands-review-grotty-exasperating-deeply-unsatisfying/|title=Three Husbands review: a grotty, exasperating, and deeply unsatisfying superhuman sex comedy|last=Collin|first=Robbie|date=2018-10-29|work=The Telegraph|access-date=2018-12-02|language=en-GB|issn=0307-1235}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://variety.com/2018/film/reviews/three-husbands-review-1203016994/|title=Tokyo Film Review: ‘Three Husbands’|last=Kuipers|first=Richard|date=2018-11-02|work=Variety|access-date=2018-12-02|language=en-US}}</ref>（前兩部分別為《[[榴槤飄飄|榴槤飄飄]]》及《[[香港有個荷里活|香港有個荷里活]]》）。於[[第31屆東京國際影展|第31屆東京國際影展]]主競賽單元首映，也是2018年[[香港亞洲電影節|香港亞洲電影節]]的閉幕片<ref>{{cite news|title=徐譽庭獲頒「亞洲新導演獎」|publisher=星島日報|url=http://www.singtao.ca/toronto/3596680|date=2018-11-27|author=黃佩麗|accessdate=2018-12-09|language=zh-hant}}</ref>。

'''三溴化锝'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为TcBr<sub>3</sub>。
==制备与结构==
用氢于750℃还原[[二氧化锝|二氧化锝]]，得到灰色的金属[[锝|锝]]。将制得的金属锝和[[溴|溴]]按[[化学计量比|化学计量比]]1:3反应，得到TcBr<sub>3</sub>。<ref name="Poineau Rodriguez Forster Sattelberger pp. 910–911">{{cite journal | last=Poineau | first=Frederic | last2=Rodriguez | first2=Efrain E. | last3=Forster | first3=Paul M. | last4=Sattelberger | first4=Alfred P. | last5=Cheetham | first5=Anthony K. | last6=Czerwinski | first6=Kenneth R. | title=Preparation of the Binary Technetium Bromides: TcBr3and TcBr4 | journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society | publisher=American Chemical Society (ACS) | volume=131 | issue=3 | date=2009-01-28 | issn=0002-7863 | doi=10.1021/ja808597r | pages=910–911}}</ref>
: 2 Tc + 3 Br<sub>2</sub> → TcBr<sub>3</sub>

其结构为无限延伸的链状[TcBr<sub>6</sub>]八面体。而同族[[铼|铼]]的三溴化物为三聚体Re<sub>3</sub>Br<sub>9</sub>。

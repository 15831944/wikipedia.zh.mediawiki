'''三氧化硫吡啶'''是一种[[有机化合物|有机化合物]]，化学式为C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>5</sub>NSO<sub>3</sub>。它是无色固体，可溶于极性有机溶剂。它是[[路易斯碱|路易斯碱]][[吡啶|吡啶]]和[[路易斯酸|路易斯酸]][[三氧化硫|三氧化硫]]的[[加合物|加合物]]。它主要用于提供三氧化硫，如由[[醇|醇]]类制备[[硫酸酯|硫酸酯]]：<ref>Thomas T. Tidwell "Sulfur Trioxide–Pyridine" Encyclopedia of Reagents for Organic Synthesis 2001,  John Wiley & Sons.  {{DOI|10.1002/047084289X.rs139m}}. Article Online Posting Date: April 15, 2001</ref>
:ROH  +  C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>5</sub>NSO<sub>3</sub>   →   [C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>5</sub>NH]<sup>+</sup>[ROSO<sub>3</sub>]<sup>−</sup>

这个试剂可用于很多磺酰化反应，如[[呋喃|呋喃]]的磺酰化反应。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.imperial.ac.uk/media/imperial-college/research-centres-and-groups/spivey-group/teaching/org2heteroaromatics/lecture451112.pdf|title=Heteroaromatic Chemistry Lectures 4 and 5|last=Spivey|first=Alan|date=12 March 2012|website=Imperial College Research|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=7 February 2017}}</ref>它在[[Parikh–Doering氧化反应|Parikh–Doering氧化反应]]中作为[[亲电试剂|亲电试剂]]。<ref>{{cite journal | title = Sulfur trioxide in the oxidation of alcohols by dimethyl sulfoxide | author = Jekishan R. Parikh, William v. E. Doering |journal = [[J._Am._Chem._Soc.|J. Am. Chem. Soc.]] | year = 1967 | volume = 89 | pages = 5505–5507 | doi = 10.1021/ja00997a067 | issue = 21}}</ref>

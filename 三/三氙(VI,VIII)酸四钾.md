'''三氙(VI,VIII)酸四钾'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为K<sub>4</sub>Xe<sub>3</sub>O<sub>12</sub>。

==历史==
K<sub>4</sub>Xe<sub>3</sub>O<sub>12</sub>最初在XeF<sub>6</sub>于[[氢氧化钾|KOH]]溶液中水解被观察到，并通过分析确认化合物中1/3的Xe为+8价，2/3的Xe为+6价。<ref name=Appelman>{{cite journal|title=Hydrolysis of Xenon Hexafluoride and the Aqueous Solution Chemistry of Xenon|date=1964-06-01|doi=10.1021/ja01065a009|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|pages=2141–2148|volume=86|issue=11|issn=0002-7863|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ja01065a009|accessdate=2019-01-28|author=E. H. Appelman, J. G. Malm}}</ref>后来，人们研究了物质的谱图及化学性质。<ref>{{cite journal|title=The Preparation and Properties of the Yellow Potassium Salt Containing Xenon in Oxidation States VI and VIII|date=1966-07-01|doi=10.1021/ja00965a012|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|pages=2942–2943|volume=88|issue=13|issn=0002-7863|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ja00965a012|accessdate=2019-01-28|author=T. M. Spittler, Bruno Jaselskis}}</ref>直到2016年，它的晶体结构才被确认。<ref name=Sergey>{{cite journal|title=Xenon in Rigid Oxide Frameworks: Structure, Bonding and Explosive Properties of Layered Perovskite K4Xe3O12|date=2016-10-26|doi=10.1021/jacs.6b09056|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|pages=13838–13841|volume=138|issue=42|issn=0002-7863|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/jacs.6b09056|accessdate=2019-01-28|author=Sergey N. Britvin, Sergei A. Kashtanov, Sergey V. Krivovichev, Nikita V. Chukanov}}</ref>

==制备==
将无水[[高氙酸钠|高氙酸钠]]用5N的KOH溶液于80 °C处理，结晶得到K<sub>2</sub>Na<sub>2</sub>XeO<sub>6</sub>·8H<sub>2</sub>O。将晶体浸入Na<sub>2</sub>CO<sub>3</sub>-K<sub>2</sub>CO<sub>3</sub>溶液中，逐渐转化为K<sub>4</sub>Xe<sub>3</sub>O<sub>12</sub>。<ref name=Sergey />

==性质==
K<sub>4</sub>Xe<sub>3</sub>O<sub>12</sub>是一种亮黄色固体，震荡或者对其干燥都会引发它的爆炸。<ref name=Appelman />分子中的Xe…O aerogen键被认为是导致爆炸的成因之一。<ref name=Sergey />

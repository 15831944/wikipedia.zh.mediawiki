'''三角洲4號重型運載火箭''' (Delta 9250H)為{{tsl|en|Evolved Expendable Launch Vehicle|改進型一次性運載火箭|一次性}} [[重型運載火箭|重型運載火箭]]，為[[三角洲4號運載火箭|三角洲4號系列運載火箭]]中最大者，且為現役酬載量第二大的[[火箭|火箭]]，約為[[SpaceX|SpaceX]]的[[獵鷹重型運載火箭|獵鷹重型運載火箭]]之一半。<ref>{{cite web |title=Mission Status Center |url=http://www.spaceflightnow.com/delta/d364/status.html |website=SpaceflightNow |accessdate=July 26, 2014 |quote=The ULA Delta 4-Heavy is currently the world’s largest rocket, providing the nation with reliable, proven, heavy lift capability for our country's national security payloads from both the east and west coasts.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Falcon Heavy, SpaceX’s Big New Rocket, Succeeds in Its First Test Launch|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/02/06/science/falcon-heavy-spacex-launch.html |website=NYTimes |accessdate=Feb 6, 2018 |quote=The Falcon Heavy is capable of lifting 140,000 pounds to low-Earth orbit, more than any other rocket today.}}</ref>本火箭由[[聯合發射聯盟|聯合發射聯盟]]生產，並於2004年進行首次發射。<ref>[http://www.boeing.com/news/releases/2004/q4/nr_041222m.html "Boeing Delta IV Heavy Achieves Major Test Objectives in First Flight"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120419170931/http://www.boeing.com/news/releases/2004/q4/nr_041222m.html |date=2012-04-19 }}, Boeing, 2004, accessed March 22, 2012.</ref>

三角洲4號重型運載火箭使用兩枚[[公共助推核心|公共助推核心]](CBCs)[[液体火箭助推器|液体火箭助推器]]代替其他三角洲4號運載火箭所使用的{{tsl|en|Graphite-Epoxy Motor||GEM-60}}[[固體燃料火箭|固體燃料火箭]]。三枚公共助推核心於發射時為全推力運作，經過44秒後，中央的公共助推核心降低推力至55%，以節省燃料至助推器分離時。助推器推進至發射後242秒並分離，而中央公共助推核心則恢復全推力，並於助推器分離後86秒後，由第二節接續將衛星送入軌道。<ref name=d4ppg/>

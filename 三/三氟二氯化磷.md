'''三氟二氯化磷'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为PF<sub>3</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>。

==制备==
三氟二氯化磷可由[[氯气|氯气]]和[[三氟化磷|三氟化磷]]反应得到：<ref name=Muetterties>{{cite journal|journal=Journal of Inorganic and Nuclear Chemistry|volume=16|issue=1-2|language=en|date=1960-11|pages=52–59|doi=10.1016/0022-1902(60)80086-3|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/0022190260800863|title=Chemistry of phosphorus fluorides|accessdate=2019-02-25|author=E.L. Muetterties, T.A. Bither, M.W. Farlow, D.D. Coffman}}</ref>
: PF<sub>3</sub> + Cl<sub>2</sub> → PF<sub>3</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>
三氟化磷或[[三氟硫磷|三氟硫磷]]和[[一氯化碘|一氯化碘]]在室温下平和反应，生成PF<sub>3</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>。<ref>Kumar, H. P. Sampath; Padma, D. K. Reaction of phosphorus trifluoride and thiophosphoryl fluoride with iodine monochloride and oxidation of phosphorus trifluoride with nitryl chloride, iodic acid, periodic acid, sodium nitrite and potassium nitrite. ''Journal of Fluorine Chemistry'', 1990. 49 (3): 301-311. ISSN: 0022-1139</ref>

==性质==
PF<sub>3</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>受热分解，生成PF<sub>5</sub>和PCl<sub>5</sub>。和[[氟化钙|氟化钙]]加热反应，生成PF<sub>5</sub>。<ref name=Muetterties />和F<sub>3</sub>CSO<sub>2</sub>NNaCl反应，生成F<sub>3</sub>CSO<sub>2</sub>N=PF<sub>3</sub>；<ref>Radchenko, O. A.; Nazaretyan, V. P.; Yagupol'skii, L. M. Derivatives of perfluoroalkylsulfonic acids. II. Oxidative imination of phosphorus(III) compounds by nitrogenous derivatives of trifluoromethanesulfonic acid. ''Zhurnal Obshchei Khimii'', 1976. 46 (3): 565-568. ISSN: 0044-460X</ref>类似地，它和PF<sub>3</sub>NH<sub>2</sub>的反应，也能得到含P=N键的化合物F<sub>3</sub>P=NPF<sub>2</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|volume=10|issue=9|language=en|issn=0020-1669|date=1971-9|pages=2083–2084|doi=10.1021/ic50103a055|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ic50103a055|title=Synthesis of trifluorophosphazodifluorophosphine from aminodifluorophosphine|accessdate=2019-02-25|author=Guy E. Graves, D. W. McKennon, Max Lustig}}</ref>

PF<sub>3</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>在CH<sub>3</sub>CN中的导电性很弱。<ref>无机化学丛书. 第四卷 氮 磷 砷分族. 科学出版社. 8.3.4 混合五卤化磷. pp 240</ref>

==用途==

PF<sub>3</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>和HF的反应可以用于制备PF<sub>5</sub>。<ref>Tokunaga, Atsuyuki; Kawashima, Tadayuki; Ito, Hisakazu; Tsujioka, Akikazu; Takahata, Mitsuo. Preparation of phosphorus pentafluoride. ''Jpn. Kokai Tokkyo Koho'', 1999. JP 11171517</ref>

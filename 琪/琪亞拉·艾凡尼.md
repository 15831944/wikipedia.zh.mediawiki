'''琪亞拉·艾凡尼'''（{{lang-en|'''Kiara Advani'''}}，{{bd|1992年|7月31日|catIdx=Advani, Kiara}}）是[[印度|印度]]电影女演员。她主要出現在[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]電影。2014年拍摄爱情电影《Fugly》出道。<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/machine-actor-kiara-advani-says-its-difficult-to-get-a-foothold-in-anything-4548408/ “Machine actor Kiara Advani says initially it’s difficult to get a foothold in anything”]. The Indian Express. 28-02-2017.’.</ref>艾凡尼之后較成功的電影包括：《M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story》、[[Netflix|Netflix]]电影《Lust Stories》和《Bharat Ane Nenu》等。<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/kiara-advani-did-screen-test-for-role-of-ms-dhonis-girlfriend-wife-3052425/ “Kiara Advani did screen test for role of MS Dhoni’s girlfriend, wife”]. The Indian Express. 28-09-2016.</ref>

艾凡尼的父亲是一名信奉印度教的[[信德族|信德族]]商人，母亲則是一名擁有旁遮普、苏格兰，爱尔兰和西班牙血统的教师。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/My-father-saw-3-Idiots-and-decided-To-let-me-do-what-I-wanted-to-Kiara-Advani/articleshow/34635261.cms “My father saw '3 Idiots' and decided to let me do what I wanted to: Kiara Advani”]. Times of India. 05-05-2014.</ref>宝莱坞女星[[菊希·曹拉|菊希·曹拉]]亦是她的亲人。

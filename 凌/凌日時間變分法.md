'''凌日時間變分法'''是通過觀察凌日時間變化以檢測[[系外行星|系外行星]]的一種方法。這提供了一種極其靈敏，可以用來檢測地球大小[[系外行星|系外行星]]的方法<ref>{{cite journal|author1=Miralda-Escude|title=Orbital perturbations on transiting planets: A possible method to measure stellar quadrupoles and to detect Earth-mass planets|year=2001|doi=10.1086/324279|journal=The Astrophysical Journal|volume=564|issue=2|pages=1019|arxiv=astro-ph/0104034|bibcode = 2002ApJ...564.1019M }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author1=Holman |author2=Murray| title=The Use of Transit Timing to Detect Extrasolar Planets with Masses as Small as Earth|year=2004 |doi=10.1106/science.1107822 |volume=307 |issue=1291 |journal=Science :-,2005 |arxiv=astro-ph/0412028}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author1=Agol|author2=Sari|author3=Steffen|author4=Clarkson|title=On detecting terrestrial planets with timing of giant planet transits|year=2004|doi=10.1111/j.1365-2966.2005.08922.x|journal=Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society|volume=359|issue=2|pages=567–579 |arxiv=astro-ph/0412032|bibcode = 2005MNRAS.359..567A }}</ref>。"時間變異"需要精確的測量凌發生的時刻，才可以可測量出週期的改變。 

第一顆以凌日時間變異檢測出的非凌日行星是由NASA的[[克卜勒任務|克卜勒任務]]衛星完成的。凌日的[[克卜勒-19b|克卜勒-19b]]顯示在300天的週期中有著5分鐘的振幅變異，顯示有另一顆行星[[克卜勒-19c|克卜勒-19c]]存在著，它的合理週期可能接近這顆凌日行星的數倍<ref>[http://kepler.nasa.gov/news/nasakeplernews/index.cfm?FuseAction=ShowNews&NewsID=148 Invisible World Discovered], NASA Kepler News, 8 September 2011</ref><ref>{{cite arxiv |eprint=1109.1561|author1=Ballard|author2=et. al.|title=The Kepler-19 System: A Transiting 2.2 R_Earth Planet and a Second Planet Detected via Transit Timing Variations|class=astro-ph.EP|year=2011 |author3=Francois Fressin |author4=David Charbonneau |author5=Jean-Michel Desert |author6=Guillermo Torres |author7=Geoffrey Marcy |author8=Burke |author9=Howard Isaacson}}</ref>。

在2010年，研究人員依據凌日時間變分法建議[[WASP-3|WASP-3]]也有第二顆的行星存在<ref>[http://www.astronomynow.com/news/n1007/09exo/ Planet found tugging on transits] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100713155937/http://www.astronomynow.com/news/n1007/09exo/ |date=2010-07-13 }}, Astronomy Now, 9 July 2010</ref><ref>{{cite arXiv|eprint=1006.1348|version=v1|class=astro-ph.EP|author=G.Maciejewski, D.Dimitrov, R.Neuhaeuser, A.Niedzielski, St.Raetz, Ch.Ginski, Ch.Adam, C.Marka, M.Moualla, M.Mugrauer|title=Transit timing variation in exoplanet WASP-3b|year=2010}}</ref>，但是在2012年這項建議被否定了<ref name=Montalto>{{cite journal |
title=A new analysis of the WASP-3 system: no evidence for an additional companion

凌日時間變分法的技術在2012年被用來發現[[克卜勒-9d|克卜勒-9d]]，並被普遍的用於驗證[[系外行星|系外行星]]的發現<ref>[http://kepler.nasa.gov/news/nasakeplernews/index.cfm?FuseAction=ShowNews&NewsID=226 The Transit Timing Variation (TTV) Planet-finding Technique Begins to Flower]</ref>。

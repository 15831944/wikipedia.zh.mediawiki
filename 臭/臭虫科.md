'''床蝨'''，俗稱'''臭蟲'''，在中国古时又称床虱、壁虱，是一種很小及難以捕捉的寄生[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]，屬於[[臭蟲科|臭蟲科]]（''Cimicidae''），是[[半翅目|半翅目]][[異翅亞目|異翅亞目]][[臭蟲下目|臭蟲下目]][[臭蟲總科|臭蟲總科]]的生物種類。臭虫有一对臭腺，能分泌一种异常臭液，此种臭液有防御天敌和促进交配之用，臭虫爬过的地方，都留下难闻的臭气，故名臭虫。

一般來說，臭蟲泛指所有偏好以人體血液為食糧的種屬；但其實臭蟲科包括所有以吸食[[溫血動物|溫血動物]]的血液為生的昆蟲<ref name=bioofbedbug>{{Cite journal |last1 = Reinhardt |first1 = Klaus |last2 = Siva-Jothy |first2 = Michael T. |title = Biology of the Bed Bugs (Cimicidae) |journal = [[Annual_Review_of_Entomology|Annual Review of Entomology]] |volume = 52 |pages = 351–374 |publisher =  |location =  |url = http://www.falw.vu.nl/nl/Images/siva%202006_tcm19-30750.pdf |doi = 10.1146/annurev.ento.52.040306.133913 |accessdate = 2010-05-26 |date = Jan 2007 |deadurl = yes |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100705084911/http://www.falw.vu.nl/nl/Images/siva%202006_tcm19-30750.pdf |archivedate = 2010-07-05 }}
</ref><ref name=mednewstoday/>。臭蟲的首選棲息地是房子的床板，尤其是病床或其他公共地方的床。臭蟲雖然不是嚴格只在夜間活動，但夜間仍然是牠們的主要活動時間，亦可以在宿主不留意下吸取宿主的血液。

臭蟲曾經在1940年代早期開始在已發展國家間蔓延，但後來得到治理；不過，從1995年開始，臭蟲再度在世界為患<ref>{{Cite journal |last1 = Kilpenen |first1 = O. |last2 = Vagn Jensen |first2 = K-M. |last3 = Kristensen |first3 = M. |title = Bed Bug Problems in Denmark, with a European Perspective |pages = 395–399 |url = http://www.icup.org.uk/reports/ICUP858.pdf |accessdate = 2010-05-27 |deadurl = yes |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20111111175304/http://www.icup.org.uk/reports/ICUP858.pdf |archivedate = 2011-11-11 }}，收錄於{{Cite book |last1 = Robinson | first1 = WH | last2 = Bajomi | first2 = D. (eds) |title = Proceedings of the Sixth International Conference on Urban Pests |publisher = OOK-Press Kft | year = 2008| location = Hungary }}
</ref>，並開始在北美造成嚴重的衛生問題<ref name=n2983446>{{cite news |url=http://www.epochtimes.com/gb/10/8/2/n2983446.htm |title=最新调查：北美臭虫泛滥接近大流行水平 |publisher=[[大紀元時報|大紀元時報]] |language=zh-hans |date=2010-08-08 |accessdate=2010-08-08}}</ref>，無論是[[多伦多参考书图书馆|多伦多参考书图书馆]]<ref name=n2989175 />或[[紐約市|紐約市]]的豪宅區均無一幸免<ref name=n2989175>{{cite news |url=http://www.epochtimes.com/gb/10/8/8/n2989175.htm |title=臭虫攻陷纽约住宅与名店 |publisher=[[大紀元時報|大紀元時報]] |language=zh-hans |date=2010-08-02 |accessdate=2010-08-08}}</ref>。

== 簡述 ==

成年的臭蟲是一種褐紅色的昆蟲，扁平帶橢圓形，少数有翅膀且善飞。相對於其身軀，臭蟲的頭比較短而闊<ref name=Cranshaw />。臭蟲的身上長有微細的毛，使之看起來像是有條紋。成蟲普遍長4到5毫米，闊1.5到3毫米。臭蟲在進食前身體扁平如紙，進食後身體變深紅色，並且脹起<ref name="singtao_ca">{{cite news |url=http://www.singtao.ca/tor/2008-11-18/1226993220d1434898.html |title=床蝨知多少？ |date=2008-11-18 |accessdate=2010-08-10 |language=zh-hant |author=多倫多公共衛生署 |publisher=[[星島日報|星島日報]]加東版}}</ref>。
[[若蟲|若蟲]]在剛剛孵化出來時呈半透明，顏色較淺；但當牠們達至性成熟時會[[脫皮|脫皮]]，並轉為褐色。臭蟲可以換殼5次，完成第5次換殼就成為成蟲。

臭蟲之間的溝通全靠[[信息素|信息素]]及[[益它素|益它素]]，用以傳遞有關結巢地點、攻擊及繁殖的信息。

臭蟲的生命周期視乎其品種及食物供應而有所不同，一般來說，可存活一年<ref name="singtao_ca" />。床蝨擁有儲精囊，雌性床蝨一生只需交配一次，便終生不用交配都具繁殖能力。期間雌性床蝨可產卵200至400顆，其數量亦是視乎食物供應而定，但也受溫度影響<ref name="singtao_ca" />。蟲卵約於十天後孵化<ref name="singtao_ca" />。

=== 生存環境 ===

在臭蟲科眾多品種裡，以[[溫帶臭蟲|溫帶臭蟲]]（''[[Cimex_lectularius|Cimex lectularius]]''）{{r|Wang2016}}對人類環境最為適應。牠們可在世界上所有[[溫帶氣候|溫帶氣候]]地點裡發現。其他品種如[[熱帶臭蟲|熱帶臭蟲]]（''[[Cimex_hemipterus|Cimex hemipterus]]''）{{r|Wang2016}}則可在[[熱帶地區|熱帶地區]]發現，亦同時會感染[[家禽|家禽]]、[[蝙蝠|蝙蝠]]及''[[Leptocimex_boueti|Leptocimex boueti]]''；而在[[西非|西非]]及[[南美洲|南美洲]]的熱帶地區發現的，可令人類及蝙蝠感染。''[[Cimex_pilosellus|Cimex pilosellus]]''及''[[Cimex_pipistrella|Cimex pipistrella]]''主要感染蝙蝠，而一種在北美發現的品種''[[Haematosiphon_inodora|Haematosiphon inodora]]''則會感染家禽<ref name=Cranshaw>{{Cite web |last1 = Cranshaw | first1 = W.S. |last2 = Camper | first2 = M. |last3 = Peairs | first3 = F.B. |title = Bat Bugs and Bed Bugs |publisher = Colorado State University Extension |url = http://www.ext.colostate.edu/PUBS/INSECT/05574.html |accessdate = 2010-05-27 | language=en|date=Feb 2009}}</ref>。

臭蟲可以在一個相當廣泛的溫度和大氣成分比例環境裡存活。當環境溫度下降至16.1℃時，成蟲會進入半休眠狀態，使之能夠存活更久<ref>http://www.augustineexterminators.com/galleryDetail.asp?gallery_id=26</ref>；即使溫度下跌至-10℃，臭蟲仍然能夠存活至少五天；但若暴露於-32℃的低溫，則會在15分鐘後死亡<ref name="birc.org"/>。臭蟲具高度[[抗旱性|抗旱性]]，即使在氣溫達35–40 °C度而且濕度低的環境裡，在失去了體重的三分之一後仍能存活；但在其較早期的生長階段，其抗寒能力較差<ref>{{Cite journal |last1 = Benoit | first1 = J. B. |last2 = del Grosso | first2 = N. |last3 = Yoder | first3 = J. A. |last4 = Denlinger | first4 = D. L. |title = Resistance to dehydration between bouts of blood feeding in the bed bug, Cimex lectularius, is enhanced by water conservation, aggregation, and quiescence |journal = [[American_Journal_of_Tropical_Medicine_and_Hygiene|American Journal of Tropical Medicine and Hygiene]] | volume = 76 | issue = 5 | pages = 987–993 |year = 2007 |url = http://www.ajtmh.org/cgi/content/full/76/5/987 | pmid = 17488928 | issn = 0002-9637 |accessdate = 2010-05-27 }}</ref>。''C. lectularius''在眾多臭蟲科品種裡的[[熱死點|熱死點]]最高：達45℃，而當溫度高達46℃時，只要停留七分鐘時間，所有臭蟲不管在哪一個生命階段都會捱不過而死掉<ref name=birc.org>{{Cite journal |last = Quarles | first = William |title = Bed Bugs Bounce Back |journal = IPM Practitioner | volume = 24 | issue = 3/4 | pages = 1–8 |publisher = Bio-Integral Resource Center |location = Berkeley, CA |date = March 2007 |url = http://www.birc.org/MarApril2007.pdf |format = PDF | accessdate = 27 May 2010 }}</ref>。臭蟲在缺氧環境下也有頗強的生命力，雖然在高濃度[[二氧化碳|二氧化碳]]或近乎全[[氮|氮]]氣的大氣環境裡顯然不能生存，但也可以支撐超過72小時<ref name=herrmannetal>{{Cite conference |last1 = Herrmann |first1 = J. |last2 = Adler |first2 = C. |last3 = Hoffmann |first3 = G. |last4 = Reichmuth |first4 = C. |title = Efficacy of controlled atmospheres on ''Cimex lectularius'' (L.) (Heteroptera: Cimicidae) and ''Argas reflexus'' Fab. (Acari: Argasidae) |booktitle = Proceedings of the 3rd International Conference on Urban Pests |editor1-last = Robinson |editor1-first = Wm H. |editor2-last = Rettich |editor2-first = F. |editor3-last = Rambo |editor3-first = G.W. |page = 637 |publisher = Grafické Závody |location = Hronov, Czech Republic |year = 1999 |url = http://www.icup.org.uk/reports/ICUP511.pdf |accessdate = 31 May 2010 |deadurl = yes |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20101026030904/http://www.icup.org.uk/reports/ICUP511.pdf |archivedate = 2010年10月26日 }} (abstracted from a poster presentation in Prague, Jul 19-22)
</ref>。

=== 食性 ===

[[File:CDC_11739_Cimex_lectularius_SEM.jpg|thumb]]下的臭蟲，並利用數碼技術把其可穿透皮膚的口器以紫色和紅色加以強調。]]
臭蟲是一種強制性[[吸血_(生物学)|吸血]]昆蟲。大多數品種都只會在沒有人類可供吸血的情況，才會吸食其他動物的血。臭蟲尋找宿主的方法，主要是透過找尋宿主排出的[[二氧化碳|二氧化碳]]，其次是其熱量，之後才到其他化合物<ref name=anderson09>{{Citation | title = A carbon dioxide, heat and chemical lure trap for the bedbug, Cimex lectularius | url = http://insect-interceptor.com/Anderson,Ferrandino,McKnight,Nolen,Miller.pdf | year = 2009 | journal = [[Medical_and_Veterinary_Entomology|Medical and Veterinary Entomology]] | pages = 99–105 | volume = 23 | issue = 2 | last1 = Anderson | first1 = J.F. | last2 = Ferrandino | first2 = F.J. | last3 = McKnight | first3 = S. | last4 = Nolen | first4 = J. | last5 = Miller | first5 = J. | doi = 10.1111/j.1365-2915.2008.00790.x | accessdate = 2010-05-27 }}{{dead link|date=2018年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。

當臭蟲以其口器刺穿宿主的皮膚時，會在皮膚上留下兩個孔洞，因為其口器有兩條空心的進食管：一條用來向宿主注射含有[[抗凝血劑|抗凝血劑]]和[[麻醉劑|麻醉劑]]的唾液，而另一條用來從宿主吸取血液。在進食約五分鐘後，臭蟲會返回其藏身處<ref name=mednewstoday>{{Cite web |title = What Are Bed Bugs? How To Kill Bed Bugs |work = Medical News Today | publisher = MediLexicon International Ltd |date = 2009-07-20 |url = http://www.medicalnewstoday.com/articles/158065.php |accessdate = 2010-05-27 }}</ref>。

雖然臭蟲可以在沒有進食時仍能生存整整一年<ref>{{Cite journal |last = Loye | first = Jenella E. |title = The life history and ecology of the cliff swallow bug, ''Oeciacus vicarius'' (Hemiptera : Cimicidae) |journal = Ent med. et Parasitol | publisher = Cah. OSTROM | location = |volume = 32 | issue = 2 | pages = 133–139 | issn = 0029-7224 | year = 1985 |url = http://horizon.documentation.ird.fr/exl-doc/pleins_textes/cahiers/entomo/28405.pdf |accessdate = 2010-05-26 }}
N.B. This paper reports an abandoned swallow colony with ''[[Oeciacus_vicarius|Oeciacus vicarius]]'' surviving three consecutive years. However, that does not preclude the availability of other species (e.g., bats, other mammals, other birds) to fall back on for occasional feeding.</ref>，日常會每隔五到十天進食一次。當天氣寒冷時，臭蟲也可以存活一年；而在更有利於活動和覓食的溫度時，可生存約五個月<ref name=Boase2001>{{Citation |title = Bedbugs - back from the brink |year = 2001 | pages = 159–162 | volume = 12 | issue = 4 |last = Boase | first = C. |journal = Pesticide Outlook |url = http://www.rsc.org/delivery/_ArticleLinking/DisplayArticleForFree.cfm?doi=b106301b |doi = 10.1039/b106301b | accessdate = 2010-05-27 }}</ref>。

在2009年[[美國昆蟲學會|美國昆蟲學會]]的第57屆年會，有報告指在[[維珍尼亞州|維珍尼亞州]]發現新一代的抗殺蟲劑臭蟲在沒有進食時只能存活二個月<ref name=diydetector>{{Cite web |last = Milius | first = Susan |title = Do-it-yourself bed-bug detector |work = Science News | publisher = Society for Science and the Public |date = 2010-01-16 | volume = 177 | number = 2 | page = 88 |url = http://www.sciencenews.org/view/generic/id/51188/title/Do-it-yourself_bed-bug_detector |accessdate = 2010-05-27 }} 引述自 {{Cite journal |last = Polanco-Pinzón | first = Andrea | coauthors = et al. |title = Survivorship and growth potential of modern bed bug populations (''Cimex lectularius'') in the United States |journal = Conference proceedings of 57th Annual Meeting |publisher = Entomological Society of America |year = 2009 }}</ref>。

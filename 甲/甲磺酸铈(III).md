'''甲磺酸铈(III)'''是[[铈|铈]]的[[甲磺酸|甲磺酸]]盐，为白色固体，通常以二水合物Ce(CH<sub>3</sub>SO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>·2H<sub>2</sub>O的形式由[[碳酸铈|碳酸铈]](III)和[[甲磺酸|甲磺酸]]反应沉淀得到。它最初于1979年由L.B. Zinner制得。<ref name=Z1>{{cite journal|last1=Zinner|first1=L.B.|title=Hydrated lanthanide methanesulfonates|journal=Academia Brasileira de Ciências|date=1979|volume=30|page=27}}</ref><ref name=Z2>{{cite journal|last1=Zinner|first1=L.B.|title=Anhydrous lanthanide (III) methanesulfonates|journal=Academia Brasileira de Ciências|date=1980|volume=52|issue=4|page=715}}</ref>其晶体具有[[单斜|单斜]]聚合结构，每个[[甲磺酸|甲磺酸]]根离子与两个8配位数的铈原子键合。<ref name=A1>{{cite journal|last1=Aricó|first1=E.M|last2=Zinner|first2=L.B.|last3=Apostolidis|first3=C.|last4=Dornberger|first4=E.|last5=Kanellakopulos|first5=B.|last6=Rebizant|first6=J.|title=Structures of the anhydrous Yb(III) and the hydrated Ce(III), Sm(III) and Tb(III) methanesulfonates|journal=Journal of Alloys and Compounds|date=1997|volume=249|pages=111–115|doi=10.1016/s0925-8388(96)02756-9 }}</ref>无水盐可以通过水合物在120 °C的失水得到。其它镧系金属甲磺酸盐也能通过类似的方法制得。<ref name=A2>{{cite journal|last1=Aricó|first1=E.M.|last2=Zinner|first2=L.B.|last3=Kanellakopulos|first3=B.|last4=Dornberger|first4=E.|last5=Rebizante|first5=J.|last6=Apostolidis|first6=C.|title=Structure and properties of hydrated La(III), Nd(III) and Er(III) methanesulfonates|journal=Journal of Alloys and Compounds|date=2001|volume=323–324|pages=39–44|doi=10.1016/s0925-8388(01)00975-6 }}</ref> 甲磺酸铈(III)的溶液可以作为电化学法制备Ce(IV)的前体，Ce(IV)作为强氧化剂可用于有机合成。<ref name=Kreh>{{cite journal|last1=Kreh|first1=Robert P.|title=Mediated electrochemical synthesis of aromatic aldehydes, ketones, and quinones using ceric methanesulfonate|journal=The Journal of Organic Chemistry|date=1989|volume=54|issue=7|pages=1526–1531|doi=10.1021/jo00268a010}}</ref>电化学法生成Ce(IV)是锌-铈电池阳极的基本半反应。

'''甲氧那明'''（Methoxyphenamine），化学式C<sub>11</sub>H<sub>17</sub>NO，[[安非他命衍生物|安非他命衍生物]]的一种，是一种[[支气管扩张药|支气管扩张药]]，主要作用于β肾上腺素受体。<ref name="isbn3-88763-075-0">{{cite book | author = Swiss Pharmaceutical Society | title = Index Nominum 2000: International Drug Directory (Book with CD-ROM) | publisher = Medpharm Scientific Publishers | location = Boca Raton | year = 2000 | pages = | isbn = 3-88763-075-0 | oclc = | doi = | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=5GpcTQD_L2oC&lpg=PA670&pg=PA670#v=onepage&q}}</ref>

==化学性质==
甲氧那明最初在Upjohn公司由Woodruff等人合成，<ref>E. H. Woodruff, J. P. Lambooy and W. E. Burt (1940). "Physiologically active amines. III. Secondary and tertiary β-phenylpropylamines and β-phenylisopropylamines." ''J. Am. Chem. Soc.'' '''62''' 922-924.</ref>之后由同一公司的Heinzelman合成，并修正了早期文献中甲氧那明盐酸盐的熔点，改进了合成方法，并对外消旋甲氧那明进行拆分。<ref>R. V. Heinzelman (1953). "Physiologically active secondary amines. β-(o-Methoxyphenyl)-isopropyl-N-methylamine and related compounds." ''J. Am. Chem. Soc.'' '''75''' 921-925.</ref>

'''甲酸铅'''是铅的[[甲酸盐|甲酸盐]]，化学式为Pb(HCO<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。
==制备及性质==
甲酸铅可由[[碳酸铅|碳酸铅]]和[[甲酸|甲酸]]反应得到：<ref>Khokhlacheva, N. M.; Tolstaya, M. A.; Flegontova, L. N.; Poluden, R. I. Production and thermal decomposition of formates of some nonferrous metals. (Report){{ru}} ''Deposited Doc.'', 1980. CAN96:114829</ref>
: PbCO<sub>3</sub> + 2 HCOOH → Pb(HCO<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + H<sub>2</sub>O + CO<sub>2</sub>↑

甲酸铅在氮气下，500 K以上开始分解，经过[[碳酸铅|碳酸铅]]、[[碱式碳酸铅|碱式碳酸铅]]，最终于565~635 K得到[[氧化铅|氧化铅]]和[[铅|铅]]的混合物。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Low-temperature heat capacity and thermodynamic properties of crystalline lead formate|journal=Journal of Thermal Analysis and Calorimetry|doi=10.1007/s10973-006-7510-9|url=https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10973-006-7510-9|date=2007-08-01|volume=89|issue=2|language=en|pages=643–647|issn=1388-6150|accessdate=2018-04-12|author=J. Zhang, Y. Y. Liu, Z. H. Zhang, X. C. Lv, L. X. Sun, F. Xu, Z. C. Tan, T. Zhang, Y. Sawada}}</ref>

'''H10N7'''是一种[[甲型流感病毒|甲型流感病毒]]（有时称为[[禽流感|禽流感]]）的亞种。在2004年，[[埃及|埃及]]首次爆發有人类感染的H10N7疫症。<ref name="Fields">{{cite book|title=Fields Virology|publisher=Wolters Kluwer/Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|year=2013|isbn=978-1-4511-0563-6|editor1-first=DM|editor2-last=Howley|editor2-first=PM|edition=6th|volume=1|location=Philadelphia, PA|pages=1201|chapter=41-Orthomyxoviruses|vauthors=Wright PF, Neumann G, Kawaoka Y|editor1-last=Knipe}}</ref>此次爆發感染了一些[[伊斯梅利亞|伊斯梅利亞]]的居民，包括两个一岁的婴儿和有一个孩子的[[家禽|家禽]]商人。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.paho.org/English/AD/DPC/CD/eid-eer-07-may-2004.htm|title=EID Weekly Updates - Emerging and Reemerging Infectious Diseases, Region of the Americas|accessdate=2007-10-20|date=2004-05-07|publisher=Pan American Health Organization}}</ref>

全球首三度爆发H10N7（但未感染人类）的個案在美国[[明尼蘇達州|明尼蘇達州]]出現。首兩宗是在1979年，於一个火雞农场爆發；而該農场在1980年第三度爆发同一病毒。据亚临床研究：雖然H10N7的临床病徵不严重，但火雞的[[死亡率|死亡率]]卻高达31%。未感染病毒的火雞之後已和感染了其時无法辨認的病毒（即H10N7）的火雞隔离，並被安置在一个邻近該土耳其农场的池塘。<ref name="pmid6870718">{{cite journal|title=Influenza A outbreaks in Minnesota turkeys due to subtype H10N7 and possible transmission by waterfowl|journal=Avian Dis.|publisher=American Association of Avian Pathologists, Inc.|issue=2|doi=10.2307/1590162|year=1983|volume=27|pages=357–66|jstor=1590162|pmid=6870718|vauthors=Karunakaran D, Hinshaw V, Poss P, Newman J, Halvorson D}}</ref>

H10N7病毒也在欧洲造成了嚴重的影响：在2014年，[[港海豹|港海豹]]因感染H10N7而導致死亡率大幅增加。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Spatiotemporal Analysis of the Genetic Diversity of Seal Influenza A(H10N7) Virus, Northwestern Europe|url=http://jvi.asm.org/content/90/9/4269|last=Bodewes|first=Rogier|last2=Zohari|first2=Siamak|date=2016-05-01|journal=Journal of Virology|issue=9|doi=10.1128/jvi.03046-15|volume=90|pages=4269–4277|language=en|issn=0022-538X|pmid=26819311|last3=Krog|first3=Jesper S.|last4=Hall|first4=Matthew D.|last5=Harder|first5=Timm C.|last6=Bestebroer|first6=Theo M.|last7=Bildt|first7=Marco W. G. van de|last8=Spronken|first8=Monique I.|last9=Larsen|first9=Lars E.}}</ref>第一个個案是在2014年春季於[[瑞典|瑞典]]爆發。随后，H10N7的疫情扩散到[[丹麦|丹麦]]。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Avian influenza A(H10N7) virus involvement in mass mortality of harbour seals (Phoca vitulina) in Sweden, March through October 2014|url=http://www.eurosurveillance.org/content/10.2807/1560-7917.ES2014.19.46.20967|last=Zohari|first=S|last2=Neimanis|first2=A|date=2014-11-20|journal=Eurosurveillance|issue=46|doi=10.2807/1560-7917.es2014.19.46.20967|volume=19|language=en|issn=1560-7917|last3=Härkönen|first3=T|last4=Moraeus|first4=C|last5=Valarcher|first5=J F}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=Influenza A(H10N7) Virus in Dead Harbor Seals, Denmark|url=http://wwwnc.cdc.gov/eid/article/21/4/14-1484_article.htm|last=Krog|first=Jesper S.|last2=Hansen|first2=Mette S.|journal=Emerging Infectious Diseases|issue=4|doi=10.3201/eid2104.141484|volume=21|pages=684–687|language=en-us|last3=Holm|first3=Elisabeth|last4=Hjulsager|first4=Charlotte K.|last5=Chriél|first5=Mariann|last6=Pedersen|first6=Karl|last7=Andresen|first7=Lars O.|last8=Abildstrøm|first8=Morten|last9=Jensen|first9=Trine H.}}</ref>几个月后，H10N7扩散到[[德国|德国]]和[[荷兰|荷兰]]附近的[[瓦登海|北海]]，並造成当地港口区內10%海豹死亡。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Avian Influenza A(H10N7) Virus–Associated Mass Deaths among Harbor Seals|url=http://wwwnc.cdc.gov/eid/article/21/4/14-1675_article.htm|last=Bodewes|first=Rogier|last2=Bestebroer|first2=Theo M.|journal=Emerging Infectious Diseases|issue=4|doi=10.3201/eid2104.141675|volume=21|pages=720–722|language=en-us|last3=Vries|first3=Erhard van der|last4=Verhagen|first4=Josanne H.|last5=Herfst|first5=Sander|last6=Koopmans|first6=Marion P.|last7=Fouchier|first7=Ron A.M.|last8=Pfankuche|first8=Vanessa M.|last9=Wohlsein|first9=Peter}}</ref>

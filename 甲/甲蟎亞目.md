'''甲蟎亞目'''（{{lang|la|Oribatida}}），又名'''隱氣門亞目'''（{{lang|la|Cryptostigmata}}），
是[[蛛形綱|蛛形綱]][[蜱蟎亞綱|蜱蟎亞綱]][[蟎形總目|蟎形總目]][[恙蟎目|恙蟎目]]之下的四個亞目之一<ref name="Zootaxa">{{cite book |first1=Zhi-Qiang |last1=Zhang |first2=Qing-Hai |last2=Fan |first3=Vladimir |last3=Pesic |first4=Harry |last4=Smit |first5=Andre V. |last5=Bochkov |first6=A. A. |last6=Khaustov |first7=Anne |last7=Baker |first8=Andreas |last8=Wohltmann |first9=Tinghuan |last9=Wen |first10=James W. |last10=Amrine |first11=P. |last11=Beron |first12=Jianzhen |last12=Lin |first13=Grzegorz |last13=Gabrys |first14=Robert |last14=Husband |chapter=Order '''Trombidiformes''' <small>Reuter, 1909</small> |chapterurl=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2011/f/zt03148p138.pdf  |url=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/list/2011/3148.html |title=Animal biodiversity: An outline of higher-level classification and survey of taxonomic richness |editor=Zhang, Z.-Q. |journal=[[Zootaxa|Zootaxa]] |issue=3148 |year=2011 |pages=129-147 |publisher=[[Magnolia_Press|Magnolia Press]] |isbn=9781869778491 |language=en }}</ref>，以[[青苔|青苔]]或[[甲蟲|甲蟲]]為食<ref name="Hoy">{{cite book |editor=John L. Capinera |year=2008 |title=Encyclopedia of Entomology, Volume 1 |edition=2nd |publisher=[[Springer_Science+Business_Media|Springer]] |isbn=978-1-4020-6242-1 |chapter=Soil mites |author=Marjorie A. Hoy |pages=3463–3466 |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=i9ITMiiohVQC&pg=PA3464}}</ref>。亦有其他文獻把本分類歸為[[目_(生物)|目級]]，學名不變<ref name="qiao" /><ref>Evans, 1992</ref>。牠們的大小從0.2至1.4毫米<ref name="Hoy"/><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nmns.edu.tw/public/exhibit/2005/Acari/7.html|title=|accessdate=|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://m.book118.com/html/2018/0601/169931312.shtm|title=|accessdate=|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref>。

甲蟎的[[新陳代謝|新陳代謝]]速率一般比較低，發展也慢，繁殖力也低<ref name="Hoy"/>。
Species are [[Reproduction#Other_types_of_reproductive_strategies|iteroparous]] with adults living a relatively long time; for example, estimates of development time from [[Egg_(biology)|egg]] to adult vary from several months to two years in [[temperate_forest|temperate forest]] soils.<ref name="Hoy"/> Oribatid mites have six active [[instar|instar]]s: prelarva, [[larva|larva]], 3 [[nymph_(biology)|nymphal]] instars and the adult.<ref name="Hoy"/> All these stages after the prelarva feed on a wide variety of material including living and dead [[plant|plant]] and [[fungus|fungal]] material, [[lichen|lichen]]s and [[carrion|carrion]]; some are [[predator|predator]]y, but none is [[parasitism|parasitic]] and feeding habits may differ between immatures and adults of the same species.

甲蟎是多種[[絛蟲綱|絛蟲綱]]物種的宿主，有助泥土內的有機物質分解，就好像[[蚯蚓|蚯蚓]]那樣，所以在農業經濟上有其重要之處<ref>{{cite book |author=Edward W. Baker & G. W. Wharton |year=1952 |title=An Introduction to Acarology |location=New York |publisher=[[Macmillan_Publishers_(United_States)|Macmillan]] |url=http://www.archive.org/details/introductiontoac00bake |chapter=Oribatei Dugès, 1833 |pages=387–438}}</ref>。

==分類==
舊屬[[疥蟎亞目|疥蟎亞目]]（Sarcoptiformes）<ref name="qiao">{{cite journal |url=http://max.book118.com/html/2014/0712/9039470.shtm |title=我国小赫甲螨科系统分类研究.pdf |pages=105页 |language=zh-hans |author=喬文娟 |year=2011 |accessdate=2016-01-06 |journal=內蒙古師範大學碩士學位論文 |publisher=[[內蒙古師範大學|內蒙古師範大學]] }}</ref>。根據2011年時的分類，本亞目現時下領五個下目、249個科、2399個屬，16,197個物種，這還未計算化石種的兩個科、20個屬共108個物種<ref name="Zootaxa_Oribatida">{{cite book |chapter=Suborder '''Oribatida''' <small>van der Hammen, 1968</small> |chapterurl=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2011/f/zt03148p148.pdf |format=[[PDF|PDF]] |authors=HEINRICH SCHATZ, VALERIE M. BEHAN-PELLETIER, BARRY M. OCONNOR & ROY A. NORTON |url=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/list/2011/3148.html |title=Animal biodiversity: An outline of higher-level classification and survey of taxonomic richness |editor=Zhang, Z.-Q. |journal=[[Zootaxa|Zootaxa]] |issue=3148 |year=2011 |pages=141-148 |publisher=[[Magnolia_Press|Magnolia Press]] |isbn=978-1-86977-850-7 |issn=1175-5334 |language=en }}</ref>。這五個下目分別如下<ref name="Zootaxa_Oribatida" />：
Oribatida is divided into the following taxa:<ref>{{cite web |author=Luis S. Subías |year=2007 |title=Listado sistemático, sinonímico y biogeográfico de los ácaros oribátidos (Acariformes: Oribatida) del mundo (Excepto fósiles) |language=es |trans_title=Systematic and biogeographic list, with synonymies, of the oribatid mites (Acariformes: Oribatida) of the world (excluding fossils) |url=http://escalera.bio.ucm.es/usuarios/bba/cont/docs/RO_1.pdf |format=[[PDF|PDF]] |accessdate=2008-01-05 }}</ref>
這五個下目分別如下<ref name="Zootaxa_Oribatida" />：
*[[Desmonomata|Desmonomata]]下目<small>Woolley, 1973</small>：最大的分支，還可再細分為三個小目；
**[[Nothrina|Nothrina]]小目 <small> van der Hammen, 1982</small>：只有一個總科；
**[[Brachypylina|Brachypylina]]小目 <small>Hull, 1918</small>：即[[Circumdehiscentiae|Circumdehiscentiae]] <small>Grandjean, 1954</small>，有25個總科，是全個亞目最龐大的分支；
**[[無氣門小目|無氣門小目]]（[[Astigmata|Astigmata]] <small>Canestrini, 1891</small>）：即[[Astigmatina|Astigmatina]] <small>Krantz & Walter, 2009</small>，亦作[[Acaridida|Acaridida]]：有11個總科。
*[[Enarthronota|Enarthronota]]下目<small>Grandjean, 1969</small>：五個總科；
*[[Mixonomata|Mixonomata]]下目 <small>Grandjean, 1969</small>：七個總科； 
*[[Palaeosomata|Palaeosomata]]下目 <small>Grandjean, 1969</small>：三個總科； 
*[[Parhyposomata|Parhyposomata]]下目 <small>Grandjean, 1969</small>：只有一個總科；

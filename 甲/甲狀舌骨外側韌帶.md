'''甲狀舌骨外側韌帶'''（lateral thyrohyoid ligament、lateral hyothyroid ligament）是一種圓形彈性[[韌帶|韌帶]]，形成[[甲狀舌骨膜|甲狀舌骨膜]]的後緣，並通過[[甲狀軟骨|甲狀軟骨]]的[[甲狀軟骨|上角]]尖端及[[舌骨|舌骨]]的[[舌骨|大角]]的末端。 

{{le|喉上神經|Superior laryngeal nerve}}的內分支位於該韌帶的側面。<ref>{{Cite journal |pages=695–8 |doi=10.1002/(SICI)1097-0347(199812)20:8<695::AID-HED6>3.0.CO;2-3 |title=Suspensory ligament of Berry: Its relationship to recurrent laryngeal nerve and anatomic examination of 24 autopsies |year=1998 |last1=Sasou |first1=Shunichi |last2=Nakamura |first2=Shin-Ichi |last3=Kurihara |first3=Hideo |journal=Head & Neck |volume=20 |issue=8 |pmid=9790290}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal |pmid=9706527 |year=1998 |last1=Leow |first1=CK |last2=Webb |first2=AJ |title=The lateral thyroid ligament of Berry |volume=83 |issue=1 |pages=75–8 |journal=International surgery}}</ref>

==麥粒軟骨==
<!--Triticeal cartilage redirects here-->
"麥粒軟骨"(triticeal cartilage)在甲狀舌骨外側韌帶中經常被發現、有時是骨質的，是一個小的"軟骨結節"（軟骨麥粒體(cartilago triticea)）。

'''甲胄海葵屬（Actinostola）'''是[[海葵目|海葵目]]下的一[[屬|屬]]，其下的物種均生活於深海之中，且有些物種生活於[[海底熱泉|海底熱泉]]裡。<ref>{{cite book|title=Advances in Marine Biology |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=M5cou8mZDh4C&pg=PA365 |year=1998 |publisher=Academic Press|isbn=978-0-08-057957-3 |page=365}}</ref>

==物種==
以下是甲冑海葵屬下，在[[世界海洋物種目錄|世界海洋物種目錄]]中有紀錄的物種<ref name=WoRMS/>：

*''Actinostola abyssorum''<small> (Danielssen, 1890)</small>
*''Actinostola bulbosa''<small> (Carlgren, 1928)</small>
*[[硬甲冑海葵|硬甲冑海葵]] ''Actinostola callosa''<small> (Verrill, 1882)</small>
*''Actinostola capensis''<small> (Carlgren, 1928)</small>
*[[卡氏甲冑海葵|卡氏甲冑海葵]] ''Actinostola carlgreni''<small> Wassilieff, 1908</small>
*''Actinostola chilensis''<small> McMurrich, 1904</small>
*''Actinostola crassicornis''<small> (Hertwig, 1882)</small>
*''Actinostola faeculenta''<small> (McMurrich, 1893)</small>
*''Actinostola georgiana''<small> Carlgren, 1927</small>
*''Actinostola groenlandica''<small> Carlgren, 1899</small>
*''Actinostola kerguelensis''<small> Carlgren, 1928</small>

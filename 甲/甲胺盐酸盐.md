'''甲胺盐酸盐'''是一种化合物，化学式为CH<sub>3</sub>NH<sub>3</sub>Cl，它是无色吸湿性晶体，可溶于水。<ref>[https://www.chemicalbook.com/ProductChemicalPropertiesCB7122563.htm 甲胺盐酸盐]. ChemicalBook. [2019-1-11]</ref>

==制备==
甲胺盐酸盐可由[[甲胺|甲胺]]和[[盐酸|盐酸]]反应得到，其晶体可以在无水乙醇中进行[[重结晶|重结晶]]。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Thermodynamic Properties of the Three Crystalline Forms of Methylammonium Chloride|date=1946-08-01|doi=10.1021/ja01212a003|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|pages=1405–1413|volume=68|issue=8|issn=0002-7863|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ja01212a003|accessdate=2019-01-11|author=John G. Aston, Charles W. Ziemer}}</ref>
: CH<sub>3</sub>NH<sub>2</sub> + HCl → CH<sub>3</sub>NH<sub>3</sub>Cl

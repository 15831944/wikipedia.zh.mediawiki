<!-- 定義及症狀 -->
'''鴉片類藥物過量'''是指因為[[鴉片類藥物|鴉片類藥物]]使用過量造成的[[中毒|中毒]]現象<ref>{{cite web|title=Commonly Used Terms Drug Overdose|url=https://www.cdc.gov/drugoverdose/opioids/terms.html|website=www.cdc.gov|access-date=29 November 2017 |date=29 August 2017}}</ref><ref name=NEJM2012/>。鴉片類藥物包括[[嗎啡|嗎啡]]、[[海洛因|海洛因]]、[[芬太尼|芬太尼]]、[[曲馬多|曲馬多]]和[[美沙酮|美沙酮]]<ref name=WHO2014/><ref name=NIH2017Cri/>。症狀包括{{link-en|呼吸抑制|respiratory depression|呼吸量不足}}、{{le|瞳孔縮小|small pupils}}和{{le|意識不清|unconsciousness}}<ref name=WHO2014/>。症狀的發作有部分取決於用藥的途徑<ref>{{cite book|last1=Malamed|first1=Stanley F.|title=Medical Emergencies in the Dental Office - E-Book|date=2007|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=978-0323075947|page=387|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=14LVeHRLEPoC&pg=PA387 }}</ref>。存活的患者初期會出現的併發症包括[[橫紋肌溶解症|橫紋肌溶解症]]、{{le|肺水腫|pulmonary edema}}、[[腔室症候群|腔室症候群]]和{{le|永久性腦損傷|permanent brain damage}}<ref name=NIH2017Her>{{cite web|title=Heroin|url=https://www.drugabuse.gov/publications/drugfacts/heroin|website=National Institute on Drug Abuse|access-date=29 November 2017|date=July 2017}}</ref><ref name=NEJM2012>{{cite journal | vauthors = Boyer EW | title = Management of opioid analgesic overdose | journal = The New England Journal of Medicine | volume = 367 | issue = 2 | pages = 146–55 | date = July 2012 | pmid = 22784117 | pmc = 3739053 | doi = 10.1056/NEJMra1202561 }}</ref>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
其風險因素包括{{le|鴉片類藥物使用疾患|Opioid use disorder|鴉片類藥物依賴}}、注射鴉片類藥物、使用劑量過高、[[精神疾患|精神疾患]]，和[[酒精|酒精]]、[[苯二氮䓬类|苯二氮䓬类]]或[[可卡因|可卡因]]一起使用<ref name=WHO2014/><ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Park TW, Lin LA, Hosanagar A, Kogowski A, Paige K, Bohnert AS | title = Understanding Risk Factors for Opioid Overdose in Clinical Populations to Inform Treatment and Policy | journal = Journal of Addiction Medicine | volume = 10 | issue = 6 | pages = 369–381 | date = 2016 | pmid = 27525471 | doi = 10.1097/ADM.0000000000000245 }}</ref><ref name=NIDA-deaths>[http://www.drugabuse.gov/related-topics/trends-statistics/overdose-death-rates Overdose Death Rates]. By National Institute on Drug Abuse (NIDA).</ref>。藥物{{le|戒斷|detoxification}}初期發生中毒的風險特別高<ref name=WHO2014/>。鴉片類藥物的依賴性可能是因為用這類藥物來治療[[慢性疼痛|慢性疼痛]]開始的<ref name=WHO2014>{{cite web|title=Information sheet on opioid overdose|url=http://www.who.int/substance_abuse/information-sheet/en/|website=WHO|date=November 2014}}</ref>，一般會依照其症狀進行診斷<ref name=NEJM2012/>。

<!-- 治療 -->
對患者的初步治療方式為協助其維持呼吸並提供氧氣<ref name=AHA2015Part12/>，若患者身體無法自行逐漸緩解毒性時，則建議使用[[納洛酮|納洛酮]]加以治療<ref name=AHA2015Part12>{{cite journal | vauthors = de Caen AR, Berg MD, Chameides L, Gooden CK, Hickey RW, Scott HF, Sutton RM, Tijssen JA, Topjian A, van der Jagt ÉW, Schexnayder SM, Samson RA | title = Part 12: Pediatric Advanced Life Support: 2015 American Heart Association Guidelines Update for Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation and Emergency Cardiovascular Care | journal = Circulation | volume = 132 | issue = 18 Suppl 2 | pages = S526–42 | date = November 2015 | pmid = 26473000 | pmc = 6191296 | doi = 10.1161/cir.0000000000000266 }}</ref><ref name=NEJM2012/>，，以[[鼻腔給藥|鼻腔]]或[[肌肉注射|肌肉注射]]方式給藥均可，兩者效果相近<ref name=Chou2017/>，預防使用鴉片類藥物過量致死的方法包括提高患者接受戒斷治療及使用納洛酮的機會<ref name=WHO2014/>。不過對那些症狀已緩解但拒絕前往醫院進一步檢查的患者而言，在短期間內又惡化至無可挽救的風險似乎也不高<ref name=Chou2017>{{cite journal | vauthors = Chou R, Korthuis PT, McCarty D, Coffin PO, Griffin JC, Davis-O'Reilly C, Grusing S, Daya M | title = Management of Suspected Opioid Overdose With Naloxone in Out-of-Hospital Settings: A Systematic Review | journal = Annals of Internal Medicine | volume = 167 | issue = 12 | pages = 867–875 | date = December 2017 | pmid = 29181532 | doi = 10.7326/M17-2224 }}</ref>。

<!-- 流行病學 -->
2015年時全球發生了12.2萬起因{{le|鴉片類藥物使用疾患|Opioid use disorder|鴉片類藥物依賴}}而死亡的案例，1990年則是 1.8萬起<ref name=GBD2015De/><ref name=GDB2013>{{cite journal | author = GBD 2013 Mortality and Causes of Death Collaborators | title = Global, regional, and national age-sex specific all-cause and cause-specific mortality for 240 causes of death, 1990-2013: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2013 | journal = Lancet | volume = 385 | issue = 9963 | pages = 117–71 | date = January 2015 | pmid = 25530442 | pmc = 4340604 | doi = 10.1016/S0140-6736(14)61682-2 }}</ref>。2017年，在美國有超過4.9萬起死亡跟使用鴉片有關<ref name=NIDA-deaths/>，其中約2萬人使用了[[處方藥|處方藥]]鴉片類藥物，1.6萬人使用了海洛因<ref name=NIDA-deaths/>。2017年，美國的[[藥物過量|藥物過量]]案例中，超過65%是鴉片類藥物造成的死亡<ref name=NIDA-deaths/>。，據信[[鴉片類藥物氾濫|鴉片類藥物的氾濫]]，有可能是因為1990年代[[製藥工程|藥品產業]]保證處方藥鴉片是安全的<ref name=NIH2017Cri>{{cite web|title=Opioid Overdose Crisis|url=https://www.drugabuse.gov/drugs-abuse/opioids/opioid-overdose-crisis|website=National Institute on Drug Abuse|access-date=29 November 2017|date=1 June 2017}}</ref>。

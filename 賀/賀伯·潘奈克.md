'''赫伯特·傑佛瑞斯·潘奈克'''（{{lang-en|Herbert Jefferis Pennock}}, {{bd|1894年|2月10日|1948年|1月30日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟的投手，生涯23年效力過運動家、紅襪及洋基。

1912年，運動家總教練康尼·馬克簽下潘奈克。1915年季中他被交易至紅襪。1922年被交易至洋基，在洋基拿下4座冠軍。

潘奈克被譽為當時最強的左投，馬克後來也覺得當時把他交易至紅襪是錯誤的決定。1948年，潘奈克死於腦溢血，同年他入選名人堂。

==職業生涯==
===費城運動家===
1912年5月14日，年僅18歲的潘奈克於大聯盟初登板，主投4局僅被擊出一支安打無失分。他為當時最年輕的美聯選手。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/AL/1912-other-leaders.shtml |title=1912 American League Awards, All-Stars, & More Leaders |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date= |accessdate=September 10, 2012}}</ref>

1913年，潘奈克因為生病而幾乎缺席整個球季，不過最後有參與世界大賽。<ref name=1914reach>{{cite book|title=1914 Reach Guide|page=45|url=http://library.si.edu/digital-library/book/reachofficialame19141phil|accessdate=2017-01-03}}</ref>1914年，潘奈克11勝4敗，防禦率2.79。在世界大賽還投了3局無失分，但是運動家最後遭到勇士直落四橫掃。1915年，陣中王牌投手Chief Bender轉隊至聯邦聯盟球隊打球，潘奈克因此成為開幕戰先發。在開幕戰先發面對紅襪，潘奈克投出了一安打完封勝。不過季中球隊以積極度不夠為由，將潘奈克交易至紅襪。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/?id=Ub0qMHIYcFEC&pg=PT46&lpg=PT46&dq=herb-pennock+waiver#v=onepage&q=herb-pennock%20waiver&f=false|title=Connie Mack: The Turbulent and Triumphant Years, 1915–1931|first=Norman L.|last=Macht|page=29|publisher=University of Nebraska Press|year=2012|isbn= 9780803240353}}</ref>
===波士頓紅襪===
由於紅襪先發陣容實力強大，因此潘奈克先被下放到[[國際聯盟_(棒球)|國際聯盟]]。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=DoRRAAAAIBAJ&sjid=GWgDAAAAIBAJ&pg=2493,1272828&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Herb Pennock Released|newspaper=The Gazette Times|page=10|date=August 13, 1915|accessdate=September 12, 2013}}</ref>1916年，潘奈克在國際聯盟投出1.67的防禦率，拿下聯盟冠軍。該年紅襪也蟬連世界大賽冠軍，不過這2年潘奈克都未在世界大賽登板。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1915_WS.shtml |title=1915 World Series – Boston Red Sox over Philadelphia Phillies (4–1) |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date= |accessdate=September 10, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1916_WS.shtml |title=1916 World Series – Boston Red Sox over Brooklyn Robins (4–1) |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date= |accessdate=September 10, 2012}}</ref>

1918年，潘奈克入美軍服役。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1918/03/22/archives/baseball-stars-in-navy-many-strong-teams-to-represent-sailors-of.html|title=Baseball Stars in Navy. – Many Strong Teams to Represent Sailors of Nation|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=March 22, 1918|accessdate=September 8, 2012}}</ref>1919年回歸球場，投出16勝8敗，防禦率2.71。1922年，潘奈克僅10勝17敗，7次暴投是美聯最多。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/AL/1922-pitching-leaders.shtml |title=1922 American League Pitching Leaders |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date= |accessdate=September 10, 2012}}</ref>該季結束後，紅襪用潘奈克向洋基換來3名選手。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=zCcrAAAAIBAJ&sjid=uNMEAAAAIBAJ&pg=5707,2348542&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Yankees Get Pennock From Boston Red Sox|newspaper=The Southeast Missourian|page=2|date=February 1, 1923|accessdate=September 8, 2012}}</ref>
===紐約洋基===
在洋基的第一年，潘奈克就投出19勝6敗。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/AL/1923-pitching-leaders.shtml|title=1923 American League Pitching Leaders|publisher=Baseball-Reference.com|date=|accessdate=September 9, 2012}}</ref>在該年世界大賽第2戰，潘奈克面對巨人獲得勝投，中止巨人世界大賽的8連勝。第4戰拿下救援成功，<ref name=ws1923>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1923_WS.shtml|title=1923 World Series – New York Yankees over New York Giants (4–2)|publisher=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=September 9, 2012}}</ref>第6戰拿下第2勝，助洋基擊敗巨人，拿下世界大賽首冠。潘奈克的好表現連當時的主審Billy Evans都讚譽有加。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=4FtbAAAAIBAJ&sjid=UlANAAAAIBAJ&pg=778,3422542&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Southpaw Herb Pennock Saved World Series For Yankees By Marvelous Brand of Pitching|first=Billy|last=Evans|newspaper=The Providence News|page=29|date=November 2, 1923|accessdate=September 12, 2013}}</ref>

1924年，潘奈克投出21勝9敗，防禦率2.83，101次三振是生涯新高。在聖投方面僅次於參議員隊的[[華特·強森|華特·強森]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/AL/1924-pitching-leaders.shtml|title=1924 American League Pitching Leaders|publisher=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=September 9, 2012}}</ref>1925年，潘奈克投了277局，1.220WHIP皆是聯盟第一。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/AL/1925-pitching-leaders.shtml|title=1925 American League Pitching Leaders|publisher=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=September 9, 2012}}</ref>1926年，他拿下生涯新高23勝，不過印地安人的George Uhle拿下27勝奪下勝投王。該年世界大賽，他在首戰與第5戰皆拿下勝投。第7戰接替投後面3局無失分，不過洋基以2比3惜敗於紅雀。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1926_WS.shtml|title=1926 World Series – St. Louis Cardinals over New York Yankees (4–3)|publisher=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=September 9, 2012}}</ref>

1927年，潘奈克拿下19勝8敗，洋基以單季110勝44敗進入世界大賽。他於第3戰登板，前七局投出無安打。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=pI1FAAAAIBAJ&sjid=87wMAAAAIBAJ&pg=4372,467082&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Babe Has Praise For Herb Pennock: Yanks' Southpaw Pitches Wonderful Game Against Pirates|newspaper=The Telegraph-Herald and Times-Journal|page=19|location=Dubuque, Iowa|date=October 9, 1927|accessdate=September 12, 2013}}</ref>最後洋基以4連勝橫掃海盜。<ref name=ws1927>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1927_WS.shtml|title=1927 World Series – New York Yankees over Pittsburgh Pirates (4–0)|publisher=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=September 9, 2012}}</ref>1928年，潘奈克拿下17勝6敗，卻因為手臂過於操勞而缺席該年的世界大賽。不過洋基投手群靠著George Pipgras、Waite Hoyt及[[湯姆·薩克里|湯姆·薩克里]]的好表現以4連勝橫掃紅雀。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1928_WS.shtml|title=1928 World Series – New York Yankees over St. Louis Cardinals (4–0)|publisher=Baseball-Reference.com|accessdate=September 9, 2012}}</ref>

1929年，潘奈克僅9勝11敗，防禦率高達4.92。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=5SEbAAAAIBAJ&sjid=NUsEAAAAIBAJ&pg=2780,5586451&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Herb Pennock Has Neuritis Again|newspaper=The Pittsburgh Press|page=11|date=May 3, 1930|accessdate=September 12, 2013}}</ref>該年他拿下生涯200勝，成為史上第3位達成此成就的左投。1932年世界大賽，潘奈克轉為牛棚，拿下2次救援成功，洋基最後橫掃小熊。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1932_WS.shtml |title=1932 World Series – New York Yankees over Chicago Cubs (4–0) |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date= |accessdate=September 10, 2012}}</ref>

1933年，潘奈克轉為救援投手，拿下7勝4敗。球季結束後，潘奈克遭到釋出。<ref name=yanksrelease>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=yXUyAAAAIBAJ&sjid=87UFAAAAIBAJ&pg=2883,309589&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Pennock Released By New York Yanks|agency=Associated Press|newspaper=The Palm Beach Post|date=January 6, 1934|accessdate=September 12, 2013}}</ref>
===重回波士頓===
[[艾迪·柯林斯|艾迪·柯林斯]]，潘奈克在運動家的前隊友，同時也是紅襪的總教練，簽下了潘奈克。他在波士頓拿下2勝，防禦率3.05。球季結束後退休。

他生涯總計240勝162敗，防禦率3.60。生涯在世界大賽出場是5勝0敗，3次救援成功。
==晚年==
1943年，潘奈克成為費城人的總經理。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=ewkeAAAAIBAJ&sjid=lUwEAAAAIBAJ&pg=2884,3438743&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Herb Pennock Takes Philly Position|agency=United Press International|newspaper=The Pittsburgh Press|page=35|date=November 30, 1943|accessdate=September 8, 2012}}</ref>另外潘奈克很反對黑人進入大聯盟。1947年，[[傑基·羅賓森|傑基·羅賓森]]成為首位加入大聯盟的黑人球員，潘奈克甚至威脅當時道奇總教練Branch Rickey，如果讓羅賓森上場，他就取消和道奇隊的比賽。<ref name=statue>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=A6ksAAAAIBAJ&sjid=pB4EAAAAIBAJ&pg=4585,732085&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Herb Pennock: Racial stand snags statue plan|newspaper=Star-News|page=2A|date=July 18, 1998|accessdate=September 14, 2013}}</ref>

1948年，53歲的潘奈克被發現倒臥在飯店走廊，原因是腦溢血，最後送醫搶救宣告不治。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=91sbAAAAIBAJ&sjid=H00EAAAAIBAJ&pg=3813,4799727&dq=herb-pennock&hl=en|title=Herb Pennock Dies Suddenly: Phils' Official Collapses in Hotel|agency=United Press International|newspaper=The Pittsburgh Press|page=26|date=January 30, 1948|accessdate=September 8, 2012}}</ref>

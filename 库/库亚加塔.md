'''库亚加塔'''（{{lang-bn|কুয়াকাটা}}，Kuakata），又譯'''库雅加塔'''，是[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]][[巴里萨尔专区|巴里萨尔专区]][[博杜阿卡利縣|博杜阿卡利縣]]的一个小镇，以其全景海滩而闻名<ref name=bpc>{{cite web |url=http://www.bangladeshtourism.gov.bd/beach_kuakata.php |title=Tourist Attractions in Bangladesh |website=[[Bangladesh_Parjatan_Corporation|Bangladesh Parjatan Corporation]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110719081254/http://www.bangladeshtourism.gov.bd/beach_kuakata.php |archive-date=19 July 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=List of Bangladeshi Villages |url=http://traveltips.usatoday.com/list-bangladeshi-villages-18631.html |work=Travel Tips |publisher=USA Today |access-date=4 April 2015}}</ref>。庫亞加塔位於孟加拉國東南部，其海灘長{{convert|18|km}}、寬{{convert|3|km}}<ref name=Banglapedia>{{cite book |last=Kabir |first=Ekramul |year=2012 |chapter=Kalapara Upazila |chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=Kalapara_Upazila |editor1-last=Islam |editor1-first=Sirajul |editor1-link=Sirajul Islam |editor2-last=Jamal |editor2-first=Ahmed A. |title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |edition=Second |publisher=[[Asiatic_Society_of_Bangladesh|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh]]}}</ref>，從此處望去，孟加拉灣日出與日落的景色皆一覽無餘<ref name=bpc/>。

==地理==
庫亞加塔位於博杜阿卡利縣的卡拉帕拉乌帕齐拉<ref>{{cite web |url=http://geonames.nga.mil/namesgaz/ |title=NGA GeoName Database |publisher=[[美國國家地理空間情報局|National Geospatial Intelligence Agency]] |accessdate=9 January 2016}}</ref>，在首都[[達卡|達卡]]南{{convert|320|km}}處，距縣行政中心{{convert|70|km}}<ref name="bpc" />。

==人口==
根據[[2011年孟加拉国人口普查|2011年孟加拉国人口普查]]，庫亞加塔有2065戶人家，共9077人<ref name=tableC01>{{cite web |url=http://www.bbs.gov.bd/Census2011/Barisal/Patuakhali/Patuakhali_C01.pdf |archive-url=https://wayback.archive-it.org/all/20141113201037/http://www.bbs.gov.bd/Census2011/Barisal/Patuakhali/Patuakhali_C01.pdf |dead-url=yes |archive-date=2014-11-13 |title=Population Census 2011: Patuakhali Table C-01 |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |website=Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics |format=PDF |accessdate=11 July 2014 }}</ref>。

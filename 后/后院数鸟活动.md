'''后院数鸟活动'''（{{lang-en|Great Backyard Bird Count}}，GBBC）或'''北美数鸟活动'''是一项[[康乃爾鳥類學實驗室|康乃爾鳥類學實驗室]]以及[[奥杜邦学会|奥杜邦学会]]联合主办的[[觀鳥|觀鳥]]活动。每年2月中旬主办。此活動始於1998年，原本活動僅在於北美洲，從2013年起，各地的相關組織陸續參與，觀鳥的範圍達全球。

==活动详情==
后院数鸟活动是一项[[康乃爾鳥類學實驗室|康乃爾鳥類學實驗室]]以及[[奥杜邦学会|奥杜邦学会]]联合主办的[[鳥類學|鳥類學]]活动，是一项全球活动，全球[[观鸟|观鸟]]爱好者都可受邀参与。活动为期4天。观鸟爱好者通过网络平台汇报住家附近鸟类数量以及鸟类的其他讯息，[[鸟类学家|鸟类学家]]之后会把这些取得的讯息综合起来，并且用于鸟类学科研项目中<ref name="Gibbons">{{cite web|url=//www.thetimes-tribune.com/news/pennsylvania-makes-strong-showing-in-great-backyard-bird-count-1.1845138|title=Pennsylvania makes strong showing in Great Backyard Bird Count|last=Gibbons|first=Brendan|website=The Times-Tribune|access-date=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180216084423/http://thetimes-tribune.com/news/pennsylvania-makes-strong-showing-in-great-backyard-bird-count-1.1845138|archive-date=2018-02-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>。北美数鸟活动是第一项由全民参与、收集鸟类讯息、并即時上傳上網的[[公民科學|公民科學]]项目<ref name="GBBC 20182">{{cite web|url=http://gbbc.birdcount.org/about/|title=About the GBBC|date=2018-02-20|website=GBBC|access-date=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180212155821/http://gbbc.birdcount.org/about/|archive-date=2018-02-12|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

活动采集的数据会经过鸟类学专家的审核以及验证。此举的用意是要保证业余观鸟爱好者收集到的数据的可靠度<ref name="Plec 2013 p. 156">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.co.in/books?id=oIdjq52glZoC&pg=PA156|title=Perspectives on Human-animal Communication: Internatural Communication|last=Plec|first=E.|publisher=Routledge|year=2013|isbn=978-0-415-64005-3|series=Routledge Studies in Rhetoric|page=156|access-date=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180810173652/https://books.google.co.in/books?id=oIdjq52glZoC&pg=PA156|archive-date=2018-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。活动收集到的数据有助於提升鸟类研究员对鸟类习性以及数量的認識<ref>{{cite news|title=Disappearing common birds send environmental wake-up call|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1062&dat=20070621&id=z7QkAAAAIBAJ&sjid=KA8GAAAAIBAJ&pg=1720,2421240&hl=en|accessdate=2015-04-15|work=Grand Saline Sun|issue=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160510005438/https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1062&dat=20070621&id=z7QkAAAAIBAJ&sjid=KA8GAAAAIBAJ&pg=1720,2421240&hl=en|archive-date=2016-05-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>，並監測鳥類族群長期的動態變化，以分析氣候、疾病與環境因子如何影響不同種鳥類的分布<ref name="GBBC 2018">{{cite web | title=About the GBBC | website=GBBC | date=2018-02-20 | url=http://gbbc.birdcount.org/about/ | access-date=2018-08-11 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180212155821/http://gbbc.birdcount.org/about/ | archive-date=2018-02-12 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。

==活动历史==
1998年后院数鸟活动开始。1998年2月的第二周[[美国|美国]]民众首先开始参与活动<ref name="Mobley 2008 p. 153">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.co.in/books?id=gccfdvSUTg4C&pg=PA153|title=Birds of the World|last=Mobley|first=J.A.|publisher=Marshall Cavendish|year=2008|isbn=978-0-7614-7775-4|page=153|access-date=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180810174501/https://books.google.co.in/books?id=gccfdvSUTg4C&pg=PA153|archive-date=2018-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。 

2013年起，活动开放给全球观鸟爱好者参与。全球（例如：[[印度|印度]]）其他生态保育机构以及研究所也陆续支持或参与活动<ref name="Fort Pulaski National Monument (U.S. National Park Service) 2015">{{cite web|url=https://www.nps.gov/fopu/learn/nature/the-great-backyard-bird-count.htm|title=The Great Backyard Bird Count|date=2015-04-14|website=Fort Pulaski National Monument (U.S. National Park Service)|access-date=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180810175130/https://www.nps.gov/fopu/learn/nature/the-great-backyard-bird-count.htm|archive-date=2018-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Wildlife Institute of India participate in GBBC 2015|url=http://www.wii.gov.in/Great_backyard_bird_count%20(GBBC)%202015_at_WII|website=Wildlife Institute of India|publisher=Wildlife Institute of India|accessdate=2018-08-10}}</ref><ref name="Sankaran 2015">{{cite web|url=http://www.conservationindia.org/articles/india-takes-part-in-global-bird-count|title=India Takes Part In Global Bird Count|date=2015-02-09|last=Sankaran|first=Pavithra|website=Conservation India|access-date=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180713014859/http://www.conservationindia.org/articles/india-takes-part-in-global-bird-count|archive-date=2018-07-13|dead-url=no}}</ref>。一些社区也主办了的独立的观鸟活动，但是日期选择以及筹备过程也与北美数鸟活动配合<ref name="Sandhu 2015">{{cite web|url=https://www.business-standard.com/article/specials/a-bird-watcher-in-every-balcony-115020601047_1.html|title=A bird watcher in every balcony|date=2015-02-06|last=Sandhu|first=Veenu|website=Business Standard News|access-date=2018-08-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180810205015/https://www.business-standard.com/article/specials/a-bird-watcher-in-every-balcony-115020601047_1.html|archive-date=2018-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

2015年，活动参与者成功紀錄了5090種鳥類，為將近一半的全球鸟类物种。这些参与者也汇报了观鸟详情<ref name="GBBC 20183">{{cite web|url=http://gbbc.birdcount.org/2015-gbbc-summary/|title=2015 GBBC Summary|date=2018-02-20|website=GBBC|access-date=2018-08-10}}</ref>。2018年全球約有20萬人參與後院數鳥活動，來自160個國家或地區，共紀錄了6310個物種<ref name="Iliff Leonard Wilcox 2018">{{cite web | last=Iliff | first=Marshall | last2=Leonard | first2=Pat | last3=Wilcox | first3=Kerrie | title=2018 GBBC Summary | website=GBBC | date=2018-03-16 | url=http://gbbc.birdcount.org/all-summaries/2018-gbbc-summary/ | access-date=2018-08-11}}</ref>。

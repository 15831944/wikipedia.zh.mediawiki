'''葡萄瓮属'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Cyphostemma''）是[[葡萄科|葡萄科]]的一個[[屬|屬]]，本屬有約250種物種，分布於熱帶、亞熱帶地區，過去曾歸屬於[[白粉藤屬|白粉藤屬]]<ref name=trop1/><ref name="G. 1988">{{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/20798112|title=Plants of Dhofar, the southern region of Oman : traditional, economic, and medicinal uses|last=G.|first=Miller, Anthony|date=1988|publisher=Prepared and published by the Office of the Adviser for Conservation of the Environment, Diwan of Royal Court, Sultanate of Oman|others=Morris, Miranda., Stuart-Smith, Susanna., Oman. Office of the Adviser for Conservation of the Environment.|isbn=071570808-2|location=[Muscat]|oclc=20798112}}</ref>。本屬的學名來自希臘文的''kyphos''（意為隆起）與''stemma''（意為花環）。[[分子系統發生學|分子系統發生學]]的研究顯示葡萄科中，葡萄瓮属與[[烏蘞莓屬|烏蘞莓屬]]和[[崖爬藤屬|崖爬藤屬]]的親緣關係最為接近<ref>{{citation | doi = 10.3732/ajb.93.2.278 | title = Phylogenetic analysis of the grape family (Vitaceae) based on three chloroplast markers | year = 2006 | author = Soejima, A. | journal = American Journal of Botany | volume = 93 | issue = 2 | pages = 278 | last2 = Wen | first2 = J. | pmid=21646189}}</ref>。

'''葡萄糖酸钙'''（{{lang-en|'''Calcium gluconate'''}}）是[[葡萄糖酸|葡萄糖酸]]的[[钙|钙]]盐，主要在临床上用于钙质的补充。生产中一般用葡萄糖酸与[[石灰|石灰]]或[[碳酸钙|碳酸钙]][[中和反應|中和]]而成。

==用途==
葡萄糖酸钙常作为[[保健品|保健品]]的成分用于补钙。临床上葡萄糖酸钙则用于：
===低钙血症===
[[低钙血症|低钙血症]]患者补充钙时最常用的是[[静脉注射|静脉注射]]10%的葡萄糖酸钙[[溶液|溶液]]。不过10%的葡萄糖酸钙的吸收程度并不如[[乳酸钙|乳酸钙]]，<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Spencer | first1 = H. | last2 = Scheck | first2 = J. | last3 = Lewin | first3 = I. | last4 = Samachson | first4 = J. | title = Comparative absorption of calcium from calcium gluconate and calcium lactate in man | journal = The Journal of nutrition | volume = 89 | issue = 3 | pages = 283–292 | year = 1966 | pmid = 4288031}}</ref> 且它只含0.93%（每100毫升含930毫克）的钙离子。因此，当低钙血症为[[急性|急性]]且十分严重时，治疗会改为使用[[氯化钙|氯化钙]]水溶液。

===硫酸镁中毒===
葡萄糖酸钙还被用于解救[[硫酸镁|硫酸镁]][[药物过量|药物过量]]。硫酸镁是一种[[钙拮抗剂|钙拮抗剂]]，常用于预防[[妊娠毒血症|妊娠毒血症]]引起的[[癫痫|癫痫]]发作，以及缓解[[早产|早产]][[孕妇|孕妇]]的宫缩症状。<ref name="pmid18408392">{{cite journal |author=Omu AE, Al-Harmi J, Vedi HL, Mlechkova L, Sayed AF, Al-Ragum NS |title=Magnesium sulphate therapy in women with pre-eclampsia and eclampsia in Kuwait |journal=Med Princ Pract |volume=17 |issue=3 |pages=227–32 |year=2008 |pmid=18408392 |doi=10.1159/000117797 |url=http://content.karger.com/produktedb/produkte.asp?typ=fulltext&file=000117797}}</ref> 过量的硫酸镁会导致[[中毒|中毒]]，引起[[呼吸抑制|呼吸抑制]]，[[牵张反射|牵张反射]]减弱，甚至[[心脏停搏|心脏停搏]]。<ref>{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1097/00005721-200405000-00006 | last1 = Simpson | first1 = K. R. 
| last2 = Knox | first2 = G. E. | title = Obstetrical accidents involving intravenous magnesium sulfate: Recommendations to promote patient safety | journal = MCN. the American journal of maternal child nursing | volume = 29 | issue = 3 | pages = 161–169; quiz 169–1 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15123972 }}</ref> 葡萄糖酸钙通过提供[[钙|钙]]离子解毒，因此也适用于其他钙拮抗剂过量。

===氢氟酸烧伤===
葡萄糖酸钙[[凝胶|凝胶]]被[[外用|外用]]于治疗[[氢氟酸|氢氟酸]]烧伤。<ref name="pmid2741315">{{cite journal |author=el Saadi MS, Hall AH, Hall PK, Riggs BS, Augenstein WL, Rumack BH |title=Hydrofluoric acid dermal exposure |journal=Vet Hum Toxicol |volume=31 |issue=3 |pages=243–7 |year=1989 |pmid=2741315 |doi=}}</ref><ref name="pmid17091088">{{cite journal |author=Roblin I, Urban M, Flicoteau D, Martin C, Pradeau D |title=Topical treatment of experimental hydrofluoric acid skin burns by 2.5% calcium gluconate |journal=J Burn Care Res |volume=27 |issue=6 |pages=889–94 |year=2006 |pmid=17091088 |doi=10.1097/01.BCR.0000245767.54278.09}}</ref> 这是因为它能与氢氟酸反应生成无毒且不溶于水的[[氟化钙|氟化钙]]。

===高钾血症===
在[[高钾血症|高钾血症]]的治疗中，葡萄糖酸钙被用作[[心脏|心脏]]保护剂。虽然它并不能影响[[血液|血液]]中[[钾|钾]]离子的浓度，但它能够降低[[心肌细胞|心肌细胞]]的兴奋度，从而降低发生[[心律失常|心律失常]]的可能性。<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Parham | first1 = W. A. | last2 = Mehdirad | first2 = A. A. | last3 = Biermann | first3 = K. M. | last4 = Fredman | first4 = C. S. | title = Hyperkalemia revisited | journal = Texas Heart Institute journal / from the Texas Heart Institute of St. Luke's Episcopal Hospital, Texas Children's Hospital | volume = 33 | issue = 1 | pages = 40–47 | year = 2006 | pmid = 16572868 | pmc = 1413606 }}</ref>

==副作用==
葡萄糖酸钙的[[副作用|副作用]]包括[[恶心|恶心]]、[[便秘|便秘]]和消化不良。过快的[[静脉注射|静脉注射]]能引起短时间的[[高钙血症|高钙血症]]，从而导致[[血管舒张|血管舒张]]、[[血压|血压]]下降、[[心跳过缓|心跳过缓]]及心律失常。注射时药物外渗会引起[[蜂窝组织炎|蜂窝组织炎]]。肌肉注射可能导致局部[[坏死|坏死]]及脓肿形成。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Yui-Ming Lam, Hung-Fat Tse, Chu-Pak Lau |title=Continuous Calcium Chloride Infusion for Massive Nifedipine Overdose |journal=CHEST |volume=119 |issue=4 |pages=1280–1282 |pmid=11296202 |doi=10.1378/chest.119.4.1280|date=April 2001}}</ref>

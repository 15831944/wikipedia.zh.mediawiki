'''葡萄冰淇淋'''（{{lang-en|Grape ice cream}}）是一種含有葡萄味的冰淇淋，在一些食譜製作時有使用到[[果汁|葡萄汁]]<ref>[http://www.timesfreepress.com/news/2012/sep/15/grape-ice-cream-back-on-menu-at-cleveland/ Grape ice cream back on menu at Cleveland, Tenn., restaurant | Times Free Press]</ref><ref>[http://clevelandbanner.com/view/full_story/8791681/article-Grape-ice-cream--Dooley%E2%80%99s-eyed "Grape ice cream, Dooley’s eyed"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140407091136/http://clevelandbanner.com/view/full_story/8791681/article-Grape-ice-cream--Dooley%E2%80%99s-eyed |date=2014-04-07 }}. ''Cleveland Daily Banner''</ref><ref>[http://www.starbeacon.com/local/x703126022/Grape-Jamboree-turns-50 Grape Jamboree turns 50 » Local News » The Star Beacon; Ashtabula, Ohio]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=2lINaJNFfdUC&pg=PA165 The Great Southern Food Festival Cookbook - Mindy Henderson]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=_jDkclYI1bQC&pg=PA335 ''American Profile Hometown Cookbook: A Celebration of America's Table'']. p. 313.</ref>，其變化包括葡萄{{link-en|冰淇淋三明治|Ice cream sandwich}}和葡萄{{link-en|冰淇淋蘇打|Ice cream float}}<ref>[https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1893&dat=19830914&id=_rUfAAAAIBAJ&sjid=itcEAAAAIBAJ&pg=1233,1043492 The Southeast Missourian - Google News Archive Search]</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.nytimes.com/2005/10/12/dining/122crex.html?_r=0 | title=Recipe: Concord Grape Ice Cream Soda | publisher=''The New York Times'' | date=2005-10-12 | accessdate=2014-04-06 | author=Adapted from Pierre Reboul, Thor}}</ref>。有時會在{{link-en|葡萄節|Grape festival}}上提供，比如{{link-en|那不勒斯葡萄節|Naples Grape Festival}}<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=aeNoa8C4c8AC&pg=PA100 ''New York Curiosities'' - Cindy Perman]. pp. 100-101.</ref>；葡萄冰淇淋不是常見的味道<ref>{{cite news|last1=McGlynn|first1=Ann|title=Ice cream lovers scream for … grape?|url=http://qctimes.com/news/local/ice-cream-lovers-scream-for-grape/article_d4c83e57-8ed5-5400-869a-05941f9e3427.html|work=Quad-City Times|date=2005-05-27|accessdate=2015-08-13}}</ref>。

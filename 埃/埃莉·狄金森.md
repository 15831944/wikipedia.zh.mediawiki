'''埃莉诺·梅·“埃莉”·狄金森'''（{{lang-en|Eleanor May "Ellie" Dickinson}}，{{bd|1998年|6月4日||}}）来自坎布里亚郡卡莱尔<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.newsandstar.co.uk/sport/other/Carlisle-star-Ellie-Dickinson-named-in-Great-Britains-team-for-the-UCI-Track-Cycling-World-Championships-cde84a80-a605-4bfe-b91d-33460822ffd7-ds/|title=Carlisle star Ellie Dickinson has been named in Great Britain's team for the UCI Track Cycling World Championships|publisher=News and Star|date=2018-02-08|accessdate=2019-03-14}}</ref>，是一名英国女子自行车运动员，主攻公路自行车和场地自行车<ref name="goldcoast2018">{{cite web|url=https://results.gc2018.com/en/cycling-track/athlete-profile-n6033414-eleanor-dickinson.htm|title=Cycling Track {{!}} Athlete Profile: Eleanor DICKINSON|work=Gold Coast 2018|accessdate=2019-03-14}}</ref>。她在童年时期并不经常接触自行车，后来她在祖父的影响下开始正式练习自行车。2016/17赛季，她正式加入英国国家自行车队<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.britishcycling.org.uk/gbcyclingteam/new/bio/Ellie_Dickinson|title=Rider Profile: Ellie Dickinson|accessdate=2019-03-14|work=British Cycling|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190314071928/https://www.britishcycling.org.uk/gbcyclingteam/new/bio/Ellie_Dickinson|archivedate=2019-03-14}}</ref>。

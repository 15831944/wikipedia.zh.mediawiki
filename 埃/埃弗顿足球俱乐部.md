'''埃弗顿足球俱乐部'''（{{lang-en|'''Everton Football Club'''}}，發音為{{IPAc-en|ˈ|ɛ|v|ər|t|ən}}），[[英格兰|英格兰]]西北部的足球俱乐部，位于[[利物浦|利物浦]]市，是[[英格兰足球超级联赛|英格兰足球超级联赛]]的球隊之一。成立于1878年，比-{zh-hans:同市宿敌; zh-hk:同城死敵;zh-tw:同城宿敵}-[[利物浦足球俱樂部|利物浦足球俱樂部]]早了14年，也是1888年[[英格兰足球联赛|英格蘭足球聯賽]]（the Football League）-{zh-hans:成立; zh-hk:成立;zh-tw:草創}-时的-{zh-hans:创立成员;zh-tw:初始成員}-<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.evertonfc.com/~/link.aspx?_id=F6DE6AD0D2E8454780348D0D24512679&_z=z|title=1878 - 1930 {{!}} Everton Football Club|accessdate=2018-08-20|work=www.evertonfc.com|language=en}}</ref>。主场在[[古迪逊公园球場|葛迪遜公園球場]]。

愛華頓自創立以來，曾九次取得英格蘭頂級聯賽冠軍，也曾在80年代獲得[[欧洲杯赛冠军杯|-{zh-hans:欧洲优胜者杯;zh-tw:歐洲盃賽冠軍盃}-]]的冠軍；然而，愛華頓在英超成立初期的成績-{zh-hans:一般般; zh-hk:一般;zh-tw:並不出色}-，經常於-{zh-hans:保级; zh-hk:護級;zh-tw:保級}-邊緣苦苦爭扎。直到[[大衛·莫耶斯|大衛·莫耶斯]]於2002年接任總教練<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/football/eng_prem/1873803.stm|title=Winning start for Moyes|date=2002-03-16|accessdate=2018-08-26|work=www.bbc.com|language=en-GB}}</ref>後，球隊才開始逐漸進步，於[[2004年至2005年英格蘭超級聯賽|2004至2005年英超賽季]]取得第4名，同時獲得[[欧洲冠军联赛|欧洲冠军联赛]]-{zh-hans:外围赛; zh-hk:附加賽;zh-tw:預賽}-的參賽權，締造了邁入21世紀後的最佳表現；而此後球隊表現趨於穩定，聯賽排名從未低於第11名，且近10季聯賽計有8季排名保持在積分榜的前半段。

== 主場球場 ==
[[File:Goodisonview1.JPG|right]]
:{{main|葛迪遜公園球場}}
-{zh-hans:古迪逊公园球場; zh-hk:葛迪遜公園球場;}-位於[[利物浦|利物浦]]市，為愛華頓的主場，建於1892年，是全世界最-{zh-hans:古老; zh-hk:古舊;zh-tw:古老}-的球場之一。現時為容量39,572人全座位的現代化球場，暱稱「高貴老婦」（Grand Old Lady）<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.evertonfc.com/content/history/history-of-goodison-park|title=History Of Goodison Park {{!}} Everton Football Club|accessdate=2018-08-25|work=www.evertonfc.com|language=en}}</ref>。葛迪遜公園球場共有四面看台，分別為'''The Park End'''（6,000人）、'''Bullens Road'''（8,067人）、'''Gwladys Street End'''（10,155人）及'''Main Stand'''（16,347人）。

;新球場
2003年愛華頓計劃在[[利物浦|利物浦]]國王船塢（King's Dock）興建55,000座的新球場，結果因資金不足而告終<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/football/teams/e/everton/2940481.stm Everton fail in King's Dock bid]</ref>。2006年6月15日愛華頓再次探討搬遷地點<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/england/merseyside/5083198.stm Everton in talks on stadium move]</ref>，12月12日愛華頓周年股東大會證實新球場選址距離利物浦約4[[哩|哩]]諾斯利（Knowsley）附近的科克比（Kirkby），計劃內包括專門店（high street shops）、一所[[酒店|酒店]]及一個[[特易購|特易購]]（Tesco）[[超級市場|超級市場]]<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/england/merseyside/6170725.stm Everton confirm new stadium talks]</ref>。2007年2月6日愛華頓呈上新球場申請<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/england/merseyside/6335101.stm New stadium proposals submitted]</ref>。6月26日在進行公眾諮詢時，由愛華頓球迷組成的「保留愛華頓在市內」（Keep Everton in Our City）及科克比市民的「科克比市民行動組」（Kirkby Residents Action Group）分別在諾斯利市議會外舉行示威<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/england/merseyside/6239956.stm Protests over Everton's move plan]</ref>。7月20日愛華頓首次發放新球場的形象，預計可以容納50,000名觀眾，造價預算1億5,000萬[[英鎊|英鎊]]，其中5,000萬[[英鎊|英鎊]]由特易購支持，將於2010年落成<ref>[http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/football/teams/e/everton/6908229.stm Everton show new stadium images]</ref>。2009年11月25日，愛華頓新球場興建計劃被當地政府否決<ref>{{cite news |language=en |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/football/2009/nov/25/everton-stadium-plan-rejected |title=Everton's plan for new stadium in Kirkby rejected by the government|publisher=theguardian |date=2009-11-25 |accessdate=2012-12-31}}</ref>。
2019年,愛華頓捲土重來，對外宣佈新球場的形象，據官網宣布，新球場位於Bramley Moore Dock，球場容量達55,000個座位。

At the end of the [[1937–38_in_English_football|1937–38]] season, Everton secretary [[Theo_Kelly|Theo Kelly]], who later became the club's first manager, wanted to design a club necktie. It was agreed that the colour be blue and Kelly was given the task of designing a crest to be featured on the necktie. Kelly worked on it for four months, until deciding on a reproduction of [[Prince_Rupert's_Tower|Prince Rupert's Tower]], which stands in the heart of the [[Everton,_Liverpool|Everton district]].<ref name=crest/>

The Tower has been inextricably linked with the Everton area since its construction in 1787. It was originally used as a [[village_lock-up|bridewell]] to incarcerate mainly drunks and minor criminals, and it still stands today on Everton Brow in Netherfield Road. The tower was accompanied by two laurel wreaths on either side and, according to the [[College_of_Arms|College of Arms]] in London, Kelly chose to include the [[laurel_wreath|laurels]] as they were the sign of winners. The crest was accompanied by the club motto, ''"[[Nil_Satis_Nisi_Optimum|Nil Satis Nisi Optimum]]"'', meaning "Nothing but the best is good enough".<ref name=crest/>

The ties were first worn by Kelly and the Everton chairman, Mr. E. Green, on the first day of the [[1938–39_in_English_football|1938–39]] season.<ref name=crest>{{cite web |title=History of Everton crest from official site |publisher=Everton F.C. |url=http://www.evertonfc.com/history/history-of-the-club-crest.html|accessdate=21 August 2006}}</ref>

The club rarely incorporated a badge of any description on its shirts. An interwoven "EFC" design was adopted between 1922 and 1930 before the club reverted to plain royal blue shirts, until 1972 when bold "EFC" lettering was added. The crest designed by Kelly was first used on the team's shirts in 1978 and has remained there ever since, undergoing gradual change to become the version used today.

In May 2013, the club launched a new crest to improve the reproducibility of the design in print and broadcast media, particularly on a small scale.<ref>{{cite web|title=Breakdown of Elements|url=http://www.evertonfc.com/breakdown-of-elements|publisher=Everton F.C.

'''埃勃拉语'''又称'''古叙利亚语'''，是一个[[语言灭绝|已灭绝]]的[[闪米特语族|闪米特语言]]，公元前3千纪分布于叙利亚北部<ref name="archi">{{cite book|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=F2SRqDzB50wC&pg=PA313#v=onepage&q&f=false|title= Concise Encyclopedia of Languages of the World|author= Keith Brown, Sarah Ogilvie|page= 313|year= 2010|deadurl= no|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20171018002349/https://books.google.com/books?id=F2SRqDzB50wC&pg=PA313#v=onepage&q&f=false|archivedate= 2017-10-18|df= }}</ref>。埃勃拉语得名于西叙利亚的古城[[埃勃拉|埃勃拉]]<ref name="archi" />。埃勃拉语的变体也曾流行于[[马里_(叙利亚)|马里]]和{{le|特尔布拉克|Tell Brak|纳加尔}}<ref name="archi" /><ref>{{cite book|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=IiXVqyEkPKcC&pg=PA52#v=onepage&q&f=false|title= Semitic Languages: Outline of a Comparative Grammar|author= Edward Lipiński|page= 52|year= 2001|deadurl= no|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20171017224855/https://books.google.com/books?id=IiXVqyEkPKcC&pg=PA52#v=onepage&q&f=false|archivedate= 2017-10-17|df= }}</ref>。根据[[塞勒斯·戈登|塞勒斯·戈登]]的研究，埃勃拉语更像是一个当地流行的兼有东西两支闪米特语言特点的书面[[通用语|通用语]]，不是常用的口语，只有抄写员经常使用<ref name=gordon>Gordon, "Amorite and Eblaite", page 101</ref>。

埃勃拉语兼具东西两支闪米特语支的特性<ref name="arol2">{{cite book|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=NffLn98SoIQC&pg=PA550|title= Semitic studies, Volume 1|author= Alan S. Kaye|page= 550|year= 1991|deadurl= no|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20180320153250/https://books.google.com/books?id=NffLn98SoIQC&pg=PA550|archivedate= 2018-03-20|df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=aWhHAQAAQBAJ&pg=PA101|title= The Semitic Languages|author= Robert Hetzron|page= 101|year= 2013|deadurl= no|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20160423215104/https://books.google.com/books?id=aWhHAQAAQBAJ&pg=PA101|archivedate= 2016-04-23|df= }}</ref>，在语法上和东闪米特语支的[[阿卡德语|阿卡德语]]很接近，但在词汇和屈折形式上接近西闪米特语言<ref>{{cite book|url= https://books.google.com/books?id=goq0VWw9rGIC&pg=PA226|title= Mercer Dictionary of the Bible|author= Watson E. Mills,Roger Aubrey Bullard|page= 226|year= 1990|deadurl= no|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20160521214518/https://books.google.com/books?id=goq0VWw9rGIC&pg=PA226|archivedate= 2016-05-21|df= }}</ref>。

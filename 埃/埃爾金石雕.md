'''埃爾金石雕'''（Elgin Marbles；/ˈel gin/；又稱Parthenon Marbles）是[[古希臘|古希臘]]時期雕塑家[[菲狄亚斯|菲狄亚斯]]及其助手創作的一組[[大理石雕|大理石雕]]，原藏於[[帕特农神庙|帕特农神庙]]和[[雅典衛城|雅典衛城]]的其他建築中<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.britishmuseum.org/explore/highlights/article_index/w/what_are_the_elgin_marbles.aspx|title=What are the 'Elgin Marbles'?|publisher = britishmuseum.org|accessdate = 2009-05-12}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.britannica.com/eb/topic-184554/Elgin-Marbles|title=Elgin Marbles – Greek sculpture |work= [[Encyclopædia_Britannica|Encyclopædia Britannica]] | accessdate = 2009-05-12}}</ref>。

1801年第七代埃爾金伯爵[[湯瑪斯·布魯斯，第七代額爾金伯爵|湯瑪斯·布魯斯]]从當時統治者希臘的[[奧斯曼帝國|奧斯曼帝國]][[樸特|高門]]那裡獲得許可<ref>{{cite book |last=Great Britain. Parliament. House of Commons. Select Committee on the Earl of Elgin's Collection of Sculptured Marbles. |date=1816 |title=Report from the Select Committee of the House of Commons on the Earl of Elgin's collection of sculptured marbles |place=London |publisher=Printed for J. Murray, by W. Bulmer and Co.}}</ref>，將這些浮雕陸續從希臘運往英國。[[拜倫|拜倫]]稱這種行為簡直就是搶劫<ref name=BritA>Encyclopædia Britannica, ''The Acropolis'', p.6/20, 2008, O.Ed.</ref><ref>{{Cite book|author1=Linda Theodorou |author2=Dana Facaros |title=Greece (Cadogan Country Guides) |publisher=Cadogan Guides |location= |year=2003 |page= 55|isbn=1-86011-898-4 |oclc= |doi= }}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|author=Dyson, Stephen L. |title=Eugenie Sellers Strong: portrait of an archaeologist |publisher=Duckworth |location=[[London|London]] |year=2004 |pages= |isbn=0-7156-3219-1 |oclc= |doi= }}</ref><ref>Mark Ellingham, Tim Salmon, Marc Dubin, Natania Jansz, John Fisher, Greece: The Rough Guide, Rough Guides, 1992,{{ISBN|1-85828-020-6}}, p.39</ref><ref>Chester Charlton McCown, The Ladder of Progress in Palestine: A Story of Archaeological Adventure, Harper & Bros., 1943, p.2</ref><ref>Graham Huggan, Stephan Klasen, Perspectives on Endangerment, Georg Olms Verlag, 2005, {{ISBN|3-487-13022-X}}, p.159</ref><ref name="Neils2005">{{cite book|author=Jenifer Neils|title=The Parthenon: From Antiquity to the Present|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=gA81kINAI9cC&pg=PA1|date=5 September 2005|publisher=Cambridge University Press|isbn=978-0-521-82093-6|pages=1|quote=Its iconic status was certainly helped by Lord Elgin's looting of the marbles...}}</ref>。英國議會在一番爭辯之後<ref name="Report from the Select Committee of the House of Commons on the Earl of Elgin's 
Collection of  Sculptured Marbles">{{Cite news|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NwUFAAAAYAAJ&lpg=RA1-PR1&ots=GiMElOqrbf&dq=lord%20elgin%20testified%201816%20british%20parliamentary%20committee&hl=el&pg=PA35#v=onepage&q=lord%20elgin%20testified%201816%20british%20parliamentary%20committee&f=false |title=Great Britain. Parliament. House of Commons. Select Committee on the Earl of Elgin's Collection of Sculptured Marbles, Printed for J. Murray, by W. Bulmer and Co., 1816 |publisher=Google ebook}}</ref>，決定不追訴埃爾金的行為，而埃爾金則在1816年將這些石雕賣給了英國政府，最後藏於[[大英博物館|大英博物館]]<ref name="The Parthenon Sculptures: The position of the Trustees of the British Museum claim Elgin is now Mr Steal Your Marbles." >{{Cite news|url=https://www.britishmuseum.org/about_us/news_and_press/statements/parthenon_sculptures/trustees_statement.aspx |title=The Parthenon Sculptures: The position of the Trustees of the British Museum |publisher=British Museum}}</ref>。

1832年[[希臘獨立|希臘獨立]]之後，希臘政府開始追索這些失去的石雕<ref name="Ministry of Culture: The Background of the Removal">{{Cite news|url=http://odysseus.culture.gr/a/1/12/ga125.html |title=The Background of the Removal |publisher=Greek Ministry of Culture}}</ref><ref name="UNESCO: Acropolis, Athens">{{Cite news|url=http://whc.unesco.org/en/list/404 |title=Acropolis, Athens |publisher=UNESCO}}</ref>，多次要求英國政府物歸原主。2014年[[聯合國教科文組織|聯合國教科文組織]]從中協調，但最終大英博物館方面仍然拒絕歸還埃爾金石雕。<ref name="UNESCO Letter to British Government for the return of Parthenon's Marbles">{{Cite news |url=http://www.iemc-unesco.org/unesco-letter-to-british-government-for-the-return-of-parthenons-marbles/ |title=UNESCO Letter to British Government for the return of Parthenon's Marbles |publisher=UNESCO |access-date=2018-04-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141019111639/http://www.iemc-unesco.org/unesco-letter-to-british-government-for-the-return-of-parthenons-marbles/ |archive-date=2014-10-19 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref name="UK has not written back to UNESCO Letter ">{{Cite news|url=http://www.unesco.org/new/fileadmin/MULTIMEDIA/HQ/CLT/19_ICPRCP_Recommendations_en_final.pdf |title=UK has not written back to UNESCO Letter |publisher=UNESCO}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Elgin Marbles: UK declines mediation over Parthenon sculptures|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-32204548|website=BBC News|accessdate=2015-04-19}}</ref>

==收购==
[[File:Elgin_horse_2d.jpg|thumb]]
[[File:Ac_marbles.jpg|thumb]]和[[拉皮斯人|拉皮斯人]]打架]]

1798年11月，埃尔金侯爵获封英王遣土耳其苏丹大使（Ambassador Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of His Britannic Majesty to the Sublime Porte of Selim III, Sultan of Turkey），临走前他询问英国政府是否有意雇佣一些艺术家临摹帕台农神庙的雕塑，但收到了令人沮丧的回复。<ref name="Casey">{{Cite web|last=Casey |first=Christopher |date=30 October 2008 |title="Grecian Grandeurs and the Rude Wasting of Old Time": Britain, the Elgin Marbles, and Post-Revolutionary Hellenism |work=Foundations. Volume III, Number 1 |url=http://ww2.jhu.edu/foundations/?p=8 |accessdate=2009-06-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090513053304/http://ww2.jhu.edu/foundations/?p=8 |archivedate=13 May 2009 |df= }}</ref>

于是他自己出资雇佣了[[乔凡尼·巴蒂斯塔·鲁西埃里|乔凡尼·巴蒂斯塔·鲁西埃里]]在内的一批艺术家<ref name="Casey" />。虽然一开始他只是想临摹一些神庙里的雕塑作品，但随即发现神庙的保护状况相当糟糕，很多大理石雕被当地人拿走烧成了石灰以建造其他建筑<ref name="Casey" />。于是1801年埃尔金决定将帕台农神庙的雕塑搬回英国。<ref name="guardian faq">{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/education/2004/jul/21/highereducation.parthenon |title=Elgin Marbles: fact or fiction? |publisher=The Guardian |date= 2004-07-21|accessdate=2009-06-25 | location=London | first=Dorothy | last=King}}</ref>

1812年整个搬迁项目完工，总共花去了他7万英镑<ref>''Encyclopædia Britannica'' Online{{Cite news|url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/184554/Elgin-Marbles |title=Elgin Marbles |publisher=Encyclopædia Britannica |date= |accessdate=2011-04-18}}</ref>。他本想用它们来装饰自己在[[邓弗姆林|邓弗姆林]]的家，但由于和妻子离婚引发个人经济危机，所以不得不把这些石雕卖掉还债<ref>As reported by John E. Simmons in: [https://web.archive.org/web/20170109104920/https://books.google.com/books?id=wCFsDAAAQBAJ&pg=PT167&lpg=PT167&dq=%20 Museums: A History], chapter 7, Rowman & Littlefield, 07.07.2016 - 326 pages</ref>。他以不足7万镑的价格将其卖给大英博物馆，拒绝了出价更高的[[拿破仑|拿破仑]]等人。<ref name="guardian faq"/>

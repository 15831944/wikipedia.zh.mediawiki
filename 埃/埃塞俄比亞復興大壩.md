'''埃塞俄比亞復興大壩'''（Grand Ethiopian Renaissance Dam）是[[埃塞俄比亞|埃塞俄比亞]]境內[[青尼罗河|青尼罗河]]正在興建中的一條[[重力壩|重力壩]]，位於[[本尚古勒-古馬茲州|本尚古勒-古馬茲州]]，鄰近蘇丹邊境。<ref>{{cite web|title=Ethiopia’s biggest dam to help neighbours solve power problem|url=http://www.inewsone.com/2011/04/17/ethiopias-biggest-dam-to-help-neighbours-solve-power-problem/43904|publisher=News One|accessdate=17 April 2011|date=17 April 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120322030951/http://www.inewsone.com/2011/04/17/ethiopias-biggest-dam-to-help-neighbours-solve-power-problem/43904|archivedate=2012年3月22日}}</ref>該壩完工後的裝機容量可達6,000[[兆瓦|兆瓦]]，將會是非洲最大的[[水力發電站|水力發電站]]。<ref>{{cite news|title=Ethiopia lays foundation for Africa’s biggest dam|url=http://www.ertagov.com/erta/erta-news-archive/38-erta-tv-hot-news-addis-ababa-ethiopia/574-ethiopia-lays-foundation-for-africas-biggest-dam.html|accessdate=19 April 2011|newspaper=ERTA News|date=2 April 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110412060039/http://www.ertagov.com/erta/erta-news-archive/38-erta-tv-hot-news-addis-ababa-ethiopia/574-ethiopia-lays-foundation-for-africas-biggest-dam.html|archivedate=2011年4月12日}}</ref>大壩蓄水而成的水庫預計有630億立方米容量。<ref name=salini>{{cite web|title=Salini will build the biggest dam in Africa|url=http://www.salini.it/index.php/english/content/show_news/77|publisher=Salini Construttori|accessdate=17 April 2011|date=31 March 2011}}</ref>

[[苏丹共和国|蘇丹]]支持埃塞俄比亞興建大壩，不過[[尼羅河|尼羅河]]最下游的[[埃及|埃及]]擔心復興大壩會減少流入埃及的尼羅河水量。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-26679225 |title=Will Ethiopia's Grand Renaissance Dam dry the Nile in Egypt? |work=BBC News |date=2014-03-22 |accessdate=2015-03-24}}</ref>2015年3月，三國領導人就建壩爭議達成初步協議。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-32016763 |title=Egypt, Ethiopia and Sudan sign deal to end Nile dispute |work=BBC News |date=2015-03-23 |accessdate=2015-03-24}}</ref>2018年1月，埃塞俄比亞總理[[海爾馬里亞姆·德薩萊尼|海爾馬里亞姆·德薩萊尼]]拒絕埃及提出由[[世界银行|世界银行]]進行[[仲裁|仲裁]]的提議。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.xinhuanet.com/world/2018-01/23/c_129796540.htm |title=尼罗河水权之争关乎生死 埃塞拒世界银行仲裁 |publisher=新華網 |date=2018-01-23}}</ref>

2018年7月26日，復興大壩的項目經理{{Link-en|西莫格努·貝克萊|Simegnew Bekele}}的屍體在埃塞俄比亞首都[[亚的斯亚贝巴|亚的斯亚贝巴]]被發現，死因是槍傷。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-44981490 |title=Why Ethiopia is grieving for 'hero' dam engineer Simegnew Bekele |work=BBC News |date=2018-07-29}}</ref>

'''埃塔利得斯'''（{{Lang-grc|Αἰθαλίδης}}）是[[赫尔墨斯|赫尔墨斯]]与[[欧波勒米亚|欧波勒米亚]]（{{link-en|密尔米东 (英雄)|Myrmidon (hero)}}之女）的儿子<ref>{{Citation|last=Schmitz|first=Leonhard|title=[[Dictionary_of_Greek_and_Roman_Biography_and_Mythology|Dictionary of Greek and Roman Biography and Mythology]]|year=1867|volume=1|pages=49|contribution=Aethalides|contribution-url=http://www.ancientlibrary.com/smith-bio/0058.html|place=Boston, MA|editor-last=Smith|editor-first=William}}</ref>。他是[[阿爾戈英雄|阿尔戈英雄]]的先锋。他的父亲允许他挑选除了不朽外的任何能力，于是他选择了“过目不忘”，即生死轮回亦无法抹去的，永不消退的记忆力。作为赫尔墨斯的儿子，他被特别准许在{{link-en|陽間 (古希臘)|Upper World (Greek)|陽間}}和[[冥界_(希臘神話)|冥界]]之间往返。埃塔利得斯保留着记忆依次转生为{{link-en|欧福耳布斯|Euphorbus}}、{{link-en|赫尔墨提姆斯|Hermotimus of Clazomenae}}、[[皮鲁士|皮鲁士]]和[[毕达哥拉斯|毕达哥拉斯]]<ref>[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollonius_of_Rhodes Apollonius of Rhodes], i. 54, 640, &c.</ref><ref>''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Argonautica_Orphica Argonautica Orphica]'' 131</ref><ref>[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaius_Julius_Hyginus Gaius Julius Hyginus], ''Fabulae'' 14</ref><ref>[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diogenes_La%C3%ABrtius Diogenes Laërtius], viii. 1. § 4, &c.</ref><ref>[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaius_Valerius_Flaccus Gaius Valerius Flaccus], i.437</ref>。

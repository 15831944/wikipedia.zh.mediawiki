'''埃及－日本关系'''指[[埃及|埃及]]和[[日本国|日本国]]的外交关系。两国关系被埃及驻日本大使誉为“深厚的友谊”。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.embassy-avenue.jp/egypt/ |title=Retrieved 2009-05-04 |publisher=Web.archive.org |date=2008-10-09 |accessdate=2016-10-01 |deadurl=bot: unknown |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081009174007/http://www.embassy-avenue.jp/egypt/ |archivedate=2008-10-09 |df= }}</ref> 目前，两国互设[[大使馆|大使馆]]，<ref>[http://www.eg.emb-japan.go.jp/e/bilateral/japan_egypt/recent_progress/2008/index.htm|] Retrieved 2009-05-04. {{Dead link|date=2015-03}}</ref> 并保持着紧密的经贸关系。<ref>[http://weekly.ahram.org.eg/2004/717/ec2.htm Japan and Egypt open up] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130827225102/http://weekly.ahram.org.eg/2004/717/ec2.htm |date=2013-08-27 }} Retrieved 2009-05-04.</ref>
 
==历史==
两国关系可追溯到[[19世纪|19世纪]]，但直到1922年日本承认埃及的独立，两国才建立正式的外交关系。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://mdn.mainichi.jp/nationalday/etc/2009/egypt/ |title=Egyptian Japanese Relations: The Year of Egypt Tourism Promotion in Japan 2009 |work=Mainichi Daily News |accessdate=2009-05-04 |archivedate=2009-05-17 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090517075524/http://mdn.mainichi.jp/nationalday/etc/2009/egypt/ |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref> 自此，两国保持着亲密的关系，两国的高级外交官进行了几次会面。在1995年，[[日本首相|日本首相]][[村山富市|村山富市]]访问埃及，而埃及前总统[[穆罕默德·胡斯尼·穆巴拉克|穆罕默德·胡斯尼·穆巴拉克]]分别在1983年、1995年、1999年访问日本。<ref name=mofa>{{cite web|url=http://www.mofa.go.jp/region/Africa/egypt/index.html |title=Japan-Egypt Relations |publisher=[[Ministry_of_Foreign_Affairs_(Japan)|Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Japan]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090524040743/http://www.mofa.go.jp/region/Africa/egypt/index.html |archivedate=2009-05-24|accessdate=2009-05-04 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref><ref>[http://www2.sis.gov.eg/En/Politics/Foreign/EAsia/EJties/040314020000000001.htm|] Retrieved 2009-05-04.  {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090223093120/http://www2.sis.gov.eg/En/Politics/Foreign/EAsia/EJties/040314020000000001.htm |date=2009-02-23 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.yousefgamaleldin.com/programs/news-hour/91-egyptian-japanese-relations-kaoru-ishikawa |title=Egyptian-Japanese Relations - Kaoru Ishikawa |date=2008-12-24 |accessdate=2009-05-04|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090614012634/http://www.yousefgamaleldin.com/programs/news-hour/91-egyptian-japanese-relations-kaoru-ishikawa |archivedate=2009-06-14 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>
 
从1998年到2002年，日本向埃及放贷达35亿美元。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://weekly.ahram.org.eg/2004/717/ec2.htm|title=Japan and Egypt open up|author=Gamal Essam El-Din|work=[[Al-Ahram|Al-Ahram]]|date=2004-11-18|accessdate=2009-05-04|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130827225102/http://weekly.ahram.org.eg/2004/717/ec2.htm|archivedate=2013-08-27|df=}}</ref> 2002年，两国贸易额超过10亿美元。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.accessmylibrary.com/coms2/summary_0286-25637125_ITM|title=Egyptian–Japanenese Trade Exchange Hits $1B.|author=MEO Research Center|date=2002-06-05|publisher=Asia Africa Intelligence Wire|accessdate=2009-04-09}}</ref> 日本并为埃及[[帝王谷|帝王谷]]提供救护车、建造桥梁和游客中心。
 
截至2009年10月，有1051名日本国民居住在埃及。2009年，累计9万日本人访问了埃及；2007年，累计3,500名埃及人访问了日本。<ref name=mofa/>

==外交==
日本将埃及视为在[[中东地区|中东地区]]的关键角色，并将埃及外交事务是为其中东事务的重要组成部分。<ref name=mofa/> 两国政府首脑在中东和平进程有关的问题上相互给予支持。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.arabicnews.com/ansub/Daily/Day/990413/1999041305.html |title=Egypt, Japan for delay in Palestinian state declaration, resuming deadlocked peace talks |publisher=[[Arabic_News|Arabic News]] |date=1999-04-13 |accessdate=2009-05-04|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120214181815/http://www.arabicnews.com/ansub/Daily/Day/990413/1999041305.html |archivedate=2012-02-14 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>
 
两国设有“联合委员会”，致力于探讨两国共同关心事态的发展。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eg.emb-japan.go.jp/e/birateral/japan_egypt/human_exchange.htm |title=Japan-Egypt Relations |publisher=Eg.emb-japan.go.jp |date= |accessdate=2016-10-01}}</ref>

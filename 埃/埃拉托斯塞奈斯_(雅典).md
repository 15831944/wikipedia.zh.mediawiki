''Handbook to Life in Ancient Greece''.Oxford University Press,USA.ISBN 9780195124910.</ref>公元前411年的[[寡头|寡头]]，并成为[[三十僭主|三十僭主]]之一。三十僭主垮台（公元前403年）后，[[演说家|演说家]][[吕西亚斯|吕西亚斯]]指控他谋杀，但被宣判无罪。<ref>Erskine (ed.) 2003: ''chapters on various Hellenistic rules, with detailed further reading''.See also reading for chapter 2.</ref>

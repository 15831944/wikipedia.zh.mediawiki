'''鳞肉总科'''（學名：{{lang|la|Lepocreadioidea}}）是[[吸蟲綱|吸蟲綱]][[複殖亞綱|複殖亞綱]]{{r|Dollfus 1958}}[[斜睪目|斜睪目]][[鱗肉亞目|鱗肉亞目]]之下的一個[[總科|總科]]，包括下列各科{{r|WoRMS_108408}}：
* [[艾菲吸虫科|艾菲吸虫科]] [[Aephnidiogenidae|Aephnidiogenidae]] {{small|Yamaguti, 1934}}{{r|cnki}}
* [[Deropristidae|Deropristidae]] <small>Cable & Hunninen, 1942</small>
* [[Enenteridae|Enenteridae]] <small>Yamaguti, 1958</small>
* [[Gorgocephalidae|Gorgocephalidae]] <small>Manter, 1966</small>
* [[Gyliauchenidae|Gyliauchenidae]] <small>Fukui, 1929</small>
* [[Lepidapedidae|Lepidapedidae]] <small>Yamaguti, 1958</small>
* [[鳞肉科|鳞肉科]] [[Lepocreadiidae|Lepocreadiidae]] {{small|Odhner, 1905}}{{r|cnki}}
* [[Liliatrematidae|Liliatrematidae]] <small>Gubanov, 1953</small>
* [[Lepocreadioidea|Lepocreadioidea]] ''[[incertae_sedis|incertae sedis]]''

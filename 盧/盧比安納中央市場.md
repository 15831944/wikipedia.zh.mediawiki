'''盧比安納中央市場'''（{{lang-sl|Osrednja ljubljanska tržnica}}）是[[斯洛維尼亞|斯洛維尼亞]]首都的一座市場建築，設計者是Jože Plečnik，修建於1931年至1939年<ref name="DEDI295" />，位於[[三橋_(橋樑)|三橋]]和[[龍橋|龍橋]]之間<ref name="DEDI295">{{cite encyclopedia |url=http://www.dedi.si/dediscina/295-ljubljanske-trznice |title=Ljubljanske tržnice |trans-title=Ljubljana Markets |encyclopedia=Enciklopedija naravne in kulturne dediščine na Slovenskem – DEDI [Encyclopedia of Natural and Cultural Heritage in Slovenia] |first1=Marjana |last1=Lutman |editor1-first=Mateja |editor1-last=Šmid Hribar |editor2-first=Gregor |editor2-last=Golež |editor3-first=Dan |editor3-last=Podjed |editor4-first=Drago |editor4-last=Kladnik |editor5-first=Bojan |editor5-last=Erhartič |editor6-first=Primož |editor6-last=Pavlin |editor7-first=Jerele |editor7-last=Ines |accessdate=20 June 2012 |language=sl}}</ref><ref name="slovenia-life">[http://www.slovenia-life.com/ljubljana/sightseeing/ Ljubljana Life Magazine] {{Wayback|url=http://www.slovenia-life.com/ljubljana/sightseeing/ |date=20101123171045 }}, retrieved 5 October 2010</ref>。盧比安納中央市場也是斯洛維尼亞的國家文化紀念建築。

'''盧卡·蘇利克 ''' ({{lang-en|'''Luka Sulic'''}}，{{bd|1987年|8月25日}}) 是一個[[克羅埃西亞|克羅埃西亞]]-[[斯洛維尼亞|斯洛維尼亞]] [[大提琴家|大提琴家]].<ref name="cellos.com">{{cite news|url=http://www.2cellos.com/biography/|title=Biography|last=|first=|date=|work=|access-date=2018-05-18|language=en}}</ref><ref name="gloria">{{Cite web|url=http://www.gloria.com.hr/vijesti/showpage.php?id=16842|title=Gloria - EKSKLUZIVNO / Luka Šulić i Stjepan Hauser: Dečki od milijun dolara|last=|first=|date=2011-11-21|website=|language=hr|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111124072243/http://www.gloria.com.hr/vijesti/showpage.php?id=16842|archive-date=2011-11-24|dead-url=yes|access-date=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://old.slovenskenovice.si/bulvar/domaci-trac/celist-luka-ponosen-da-je-slovenec|title=Čelist Luka ponosen, da je Slovenec|work=Slovenskenovice.si|access-date=2018-05-18|language=sl}}</ref> 他與[[斯蒂潘·豪瑟|斯蒂潘·豪瑟]]組成組成“提琴雙傑”（[[2CELLOS|2Cellos]]）。

== 早年生活 ==
蘇利克出生於[[斯洛維尼亞|斯洛維尼亞]]的[[馬里博爾|馬里博爾]]。 蘇利克的父親博佐（蘇利克除[[斯洛維尼亞|斯洛維尼亞]]外還擁有[[克羅埃西亞|克羅埃西亞]]公民身份）來自[[克羅埃西亞|克羅埃西亞]]的[[杜布羅夫尼克|杜布羅夫尼克]]，他的母親奧佳來自[[斯洛文尼亞|斯洛文尼亞]]的[[伊佐拉_(斯洛維尼亞)|伊佐拉]]。<ref name="gloria"/>他的父親也是一名大提琴家，他的許多家庭成員都與音樂有關。<ref name=dv>{{cite news|url=http://dubrovacki.hr/clanak/25976/pozdrav-mojim-pilarima|title=Luka Šulić virtuoz na violončelu; Pozdrav mojim Pilarima|publisher=Dubrovački vjesnik|work=www.dubrovacki.hr|language=hr|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110511175928/http://dubrovacki.hr/clanak/25976/pozdrav-mojim-pilarima|archivedate=2011-05-11|df=}}</ref>

蘇利克五歲時開始在馬里博爾接受音樂教育。 當他十五歲時，他成為了有史以來進入薩格勒布音樂學院{{link-en|瓦爾特．迪什帕利 | ValterDešpalj}}教授門下最年輕的學生之一，他畢業時年僅18歲。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.matica.hr/zbivanja/953/|title=Koncert: LUKA ŠULIĆ (1987) – violončelo|publisher=[[Matica_hrvatska|Matica hrvatska]]|work=www.matica.hr|language=hr|accessdate=17 April 2017}}</ref>他繼續在維也納接受{{link-en| 萊因哈德·拉茲科|Reinhard Latzko }}教授的教育。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.rtvslo.si/kultura/glasba/mariborski-celist-bo-z-eltonom-johnom-nastopil-tudi-v-izoli/257124|title=Mariborski čelist bo z Eltonom Johnom nastopil tudi v Izoli|date=9 May 2011|work=[[Radiotelevizija_Slovenija|Radiotelevizija Slovenija]]|publisher=MMC RTV Slovenija|language=sl|accessdate=10 June 2011}}</ref>蘇利克於2011年在倫敦皇家音樂學院跟隨{{link-en|麥茲·里茲特拉姆 | Mats Lidstrom}}完成碩士學位。<ref name=yamaha>{{cite news|url=http://www.yamaha.com/artists/artistdetailb.html?CNTID=5802568&CTID=5070070|title=2Cellos|work=www.yamaha.com}}</ref>

== 職業生涯 ==
盧卡·蘇利克和他的搭檔在世界各地表演獨奏古典音樂。<ref name=harrogate>{{cite news|url=http://www.harrogate-news.co.uk/2012/05/14/2cellos-join-elton-john-for-harrogate-concert/|work=www.harrogate-news.co.uk|title=2Cellos join Elton John for Harrogate concert|accessdate=20 April 2013}}</ref>他在著名的國際音樂比賽中獲得了一系列頂級獎項 ，包括2009年在華沙舉辦的第十二屆魯托斯拉夫斯基國際大提琴比賽<ref name="croatia.org">{{cite news|url=http://www.croatia.org/crown/articles/9735/1/Luka-Sulic-Croatian-cellist-wins-the-first-prize-in-Warszaw-Poland-2009.html|work=www.croatia.org|title=Luka Sulic Croatian cellist wins the first prize in Warszaw, Poland 2009|accessdate=20 April 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.slobodnadalmacija.hr/Mozaik/tabid/80/articleType/ArticleView/articleId/44343/Default.aspx|title=Hrvatskom violončelistu 1. nagrada u Varšavi|publisher=[[Slobodna_Dalmacija|Slobodna Dalmacija]]|work=www.slobodnadalmacija.hr|language=hr}}</ref>得到一等獎和特別獎 ，2006年在歐洲廣播聯盟“新銳”競賽中獲得一等獎<ref name=ebu>{{cite news|url=http://www.ebu.ch/en/radio/competitions/Copy_of_index.php|work=www.ebu.ch|title=New Talent - History of Prize Winners|accessdate=21 April 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091011150631/http://www.ebu.ch/en/radio/competitions/Copy_of_index.php|archivedate=11 October 2009|df=}}</ref>  ，並於2011年在{{link-en|威格摩爾音樂廳|Wigmore Hall}}獲得皇家音樂學院贊助人獎一等獎。<ref name=wigmore>{{cite news|title=Royal Academy of Music Patrons Award Prizewinners Recital|url=http://www.wigmore-hall.org.uk/whats-on/productions/royal-academy-of-music-patrons-award-prizewinners-recital-27853 |work=www.wigmore-hall.org.uk |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120723065352/http://www.wigmore-hall.org.uk/whats-on/productions/royal-academy-of-music-patrons-award-prizewinners-recital-27853 |archivedate=July 23, 2012 }}</ref>

他曾在歐洲，南美和日本等主要場所舉辦過多場獨唱和室內樂，如威格莫爾音樂廳，阿姆斯特丹音樂廳，維也納音樂廳和柏林音樂廳等等。<ref name="wigmore"/><ref name=concertgebouw>{{cite news|title=Peter Fancovic, Luka Sulic, Jelger Blanken - Beethoven, Chopin, Franck, Liszt, Martinu, Tsjaikovski in Amsterdam|url=http://www.eventful.com/amsterdam/events/peter-fancovic-luka-sulic-jelger-blanken-beethov-/E0-001-020456159-3|work=www.eventful.com}}</ref><ref name=yahoo>{{cite news|title=Crossover Classical Music Sensation 2CELLOS Play Yamaha|url=https://news.yahoo.com/crossover-classical-music-sensation-2cellos-play-yamaha-040452776.html|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130209214845/http://news.yahoo.com/crossover-classical-music-sensation-2cellos-play-yamaha-040452776.html|dead-url=yes|archive-date=2013-02-09|work=www.news.yahoo.com}}</ref> 作為獨奏家，他與德意志電台愛樂樂團，澳大利亞室內樂團，華沙愛樂樂團，俄羅斯交響樂團等樂團合作演出。<ref name="croatia.org"/><ref name=emcy>{{cite news|url=http://www.emcy.org/prize-winners/nikola-dragosavac/388-on-the-spring-board-press-review-of-the-euroradio-youth-concert.html|work=www.emcy.org|title=On the Spring Board - Press Review of the EuroRadio Youth Concert}}</ref><ref name=vecernji>{{cite news|url=http://www.vecernji.hr/kultura/mladi-violocelist-luka-sulic-australski-komorni-orkestar-clanak-181747|title=Mladi violočelist Luka Šulić uz Australski komorni orkestar|work=www.vecernji.hr|language=hr}}</ref>

他的主要成功始於他和前大提琴競爭對手[[斯蒂潘·豪瑟|斯蒂潘·豪瑟]]聯手。 2011年1月，他們將[[麥可·傑克森|麥可·傑克森]]“[[Smooth_Criminal|Smooth Criminal]]”的大提琴版上傳到YouTube。 在短短幾週內，他們的視頻變成[[網路爆紅短片|網路爆紅影片]]，獲得了700多萬次觀看。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.jutarnji.hr/zestoki--smooth-criminal---zelimo-svjetski-spektakl--jackson-je-tek-pocetak/919904/|title=VIDEO: Stjepan i Luka postali hit na YouTube u samo dva dana. I Hollywood je poludio za njima|publisher=[[Jutarnji_list|Jutarnji list]]|work=www.jutarnji.hr|language=hr}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://tv.jutarnji.hr/video/show/e9d88bed4c250b49103ed30f96812613|title=Hrvatski čelisti hit na YouTubeu|publisher=[[Jutarnji_list|Jutarnji list]]|work=www.jutarnji.hr|language=hr|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110504005320/http://tv.jutarnji.hr/video/show/e9d88bed4c250b49103ed30f96812613|archivedate=2011-05-04|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.novilist.hr/hr/Scena/Glazba/Smooth-criminal-na-violoncelu-hit-na-YouTubeu|title="Smooth criminal" na violončelu hit na YouTubeu|publisher=[[Novi_list|Novi list]]|work=www.novilist.hr|language=hr|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110125175246/http://www.novilist.hr/hr/Scena/Glazba/Smooth-criminal-na-violoncelu-hit-na-YouTubeu|archivedate=2011-01-25|df=}}</ref> 這導致他與Sony MASTERWORKS簽下了唱片合約，並獲邀加入[[艾爾頓·強|艾爾頓·強]]的全球巡演。<ref name=masterworks>{{cite news|url=http://www.sonymasterworks.com/|work=www.sonymasterworks.com/}}</ref><ref name=wsj>{{cite news|url=http://www.online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424052970204542404577156802762808124.html|work=www.online.wsj.com/|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120908154847/http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424052970204542404577156802762808124.html|archivedate=2012-09-08|df=}}</ref>

這對二人組出現在全國電視節目中，如[[傑·雷諾|傑·雷諾]]的{{link-en|今夜秀場|The Tonight Show}}，每六個月兩次的[[艾倫·狄珍妮秀|艾倫·狄珍妮秀]]和{{link-en|史蒂芬・拉博|Stefan Raab}}在德國的{{link-en|電視總合|TV_total}}等節目。<ref name=youtube1>{{cite news|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1wtBXkGrxws|work=www.youtube.com/}}</ref><ref name=youtube2>{{cite news|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bZk5OJxaQ8|work=www.youtube.com/}}</ref><ref name=leno>{{cite news|url=http://www.theaudioperv.com/2012/02/08/2cellos-smooth-criminal-27-leno/|work=www.theaudioperv.com/|title=2Cellos – “Smooth Criminal” 2/7 Leno|accessdate=8 February 2017}}</ref><ref name=tvtotal>{{cite news|url=http://www.tvtotal.prosieben.de/tvtotal/videos/player/?contentId=114114&initialTab=related|work=www.tvtotal.prosieben.de/}}</ref>

2012年1月，他們在福斯熱播的電視劇“[[歡樂合唱團|歡樂合唱團]]”中擔任特別音樂嘉賓，他們在向[[麥可·傑克森|麥可·傑克森]]致敬的表演中用大提琴演奏了“[[Smooth_Criminal|Smooth Criminal]]”。 這是第一次樂器二人組在秀場現場客座演出。<ref name=glee>{{cite news|url=http://www.noise11.com/news/2cellos-score-a-hit-from-glee-20120209|work=www.noise11.com/}}</ref> '''提琴雙傑[[2CELLOS|2Cellos]]'''安排知名藝人[[葛蘭·高斯汀|葛蘭·高斯汀]]和[[娜婭·里維拉|娜婭·里維拉]]一起合作表演這首曲子，在《[[公告牌_(杂志)|公告牌]]》熱門百大數位音樂榜單上擠進排名第10名，並將2Cellos的專輯推上了排行榜前100名。<ref name=glee2>{{cite news|url= http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/smooth-criminal-single-featuring-2cellos-and-glees-grant-gustin-and-naya-rivera-debuts-at-10-on-the-billboard-digital-songs-chart-138971724.html|title="Smooth Criminal" Single Featuring 2Cellos and Glee's Grant Gustin and Naya Rivera Debuts At #10 on the Billboard Digital Songs Chart|accessdate=21 April 2013}}</ref>

'''盧啟賢'''（{{lang-en|Calvin Lo}}）是香港的億萬富翁企業家和慈善家。<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://forbesmiddleeast.com/spending-power-of-a-secretive-billionaire|title=Spending Power of a Secretive Billionaire|author=|first=|date=2018-12-09|work=Forbes|language=en|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.entrepreneur.com/article/322745|title=Hong Kong's Most Under-the-Radar Billionaire: Calvin Lo|author=|first=|date=2018-11-02|work=Entrepreneur|language=en|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.8days.sg/en/sceneandheard/entertainment/hongkong-actress-bernice-liu-s-billionaire-boyfriend-splurges-s-10134556|title=Hongkong Actress Bernice Liu’s Billionaire Boyfriend Splurges S$323mil On Champagne|author=|first=|date=2018-04-15|work=8 Days|language=en|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref>

== 事業 ==
盧啟賢是人壽保險經紀公司 R.E. Lee International 的[[首席执行官|行政總裁]]，專注為超高淨值的個人和企業提供產業規劃和業務繼承服務。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/brandfeatures/venture-capital/article?id=19303|title=Calvin F. Lo, CEO of R.E. Lee International, Launches The Ultimate Kaiser Food and Travel Website|author=|first=|date=2017-10-26|work=Reuters|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.thelondoneconomic.com/lifestyle/money/the-powerful-tycoon-you-have-never-heard-of-before/27/11/|title=The Powerful Tycoon You Have Never Heard of Before|author=|first=|date=2018-11-27|work=The London Economic|language=en-GB|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=2018-12-24}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://thriveglobal.com/stories/the-powerful-tycoon-you-have-never-heard-of-before/|title=The Powerful Tycoon You Have Never Heard of Before|author=|first=|date=2019-01-11|work=Thrive Global|language=en|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref> 據估計，他的團隊每年獲得10億美元的保費。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.entrepreneur.com/article/322745|title=Hong Kong's Most Under-the-Radar Billionaire: Calvin Lo|author=|first=|date=2018-11-02|work=Entrepreneur|language=en|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.financialbusiness.us/business/the-secretive-billionaire-calvin-lo.html|title=The Secretive Billionaire: Calvin Lo|author=Sarceda|first=Mariana|date=2018-11-14|work=Financial Business|language=en-US|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://55money.net/hong-kongs-most-under-the-radar-billionaire-calvin-lo/|title=Hong Kong’s Most Under-the-Radar Billionaire: Calvin Lo {{!}} 55 Money - Time to money world.|author=Sinder|first=Tim|date=2018-11-13|work=55 Money|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref> 在盧啟賢的領導下，公司成立了R.E. Lee Capital，提供財富管理和諮詢服務，管理著幾十億美元。<ref name=":5">{{Cite web|url=https://londonlovesbusiness.com/the-secretive-billionaire-calvin-lo/|title=The Secretive Billionaire: Calvin Lo|author=|first=|date=2018-11-23|work=London Business News {{!}} London Loves Business|language=en-GB|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref name=":6">{{Cite web|url=https://www.thelondoneconomic.com/lifestyle/money/the-powerful-tycoon-you-have-never-heard-of-before/27/11/|title=The Powerful Tycoon You Have Never Heard of Before|author=|first=|date=2018-11-27|work=The London Economic|language=en-GB|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref>

== 財富及慈善 ==
據美國《[[福布斯|福布斯]]》2018年12月的估算，盧啟賢的個人淨資產為17億美元。<ref name=":3" /><ref name=":0" />  在2018年的《[[伦敦经济会议|倫敦經濟報]]》文章中，他被描述為“聞所未聞的巨富”。<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://vulcanpost.com/652660/calvin-lo-hong-kong-billionaire/|title=The Real 'Crazy Rich Asian' Of Hong Kong: Billionaire Calvin Lo|author=|first=|date=2018-12-05|work=Vulcan Post|language=en-US|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref>。2017年，《[[赫芬顿邮报|赫芬頓郵報]]》將盧啟賢評為「2018年值得關注的營銷大師」之一。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Huffington Post names top marketing guru of 2018|url=https://thefinancialexpress.com.bd/trade/huffington-post-names-top-marketing-guru-of-2018-1515477207|work=The Financial Express|accessdate=2019-01-29|language=en|first=The Financial|last=Express}}</ref>

盧啟賢於2006年至2018年間擔任[https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jane_Goodall_Institute 國際珍古德]的董事會成員，倡導動物、社區及環境的福祉。<ref name=":5" /><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://vulcanpost.com/652660/calvin-lo-hong-kong-billionaire/|title=The Real 'Crazy Rich Asian' Of Hong Kong: Billionaire Calvin Lo|author=|first=|date=2018-12-05|work=Vulcan Post|language=en-US|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref>

於2019年，《Vulcan Post》透露盧啟賢已為他的慈善計劃設立價值2.45億美元的不同慈善信託基金，此一數額可媲美亞洲眾多歷史悠久的捐赠基金。<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />

== 個人愛好 ==
盧啟賢是亞洲最大的法國[[香槟酒|香檳]]採購者和收藏家，並於2018年為他的藏品花費了2.3億美元。<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=https://forbesmiddleeast.com/spending-power-of-a-secretive-billionaire|title=Spending Power of a Secretive Billionaire|author=|first=|date=2018-12-09|work=Forbes|language=en|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.entrepreneur.com/article/322745|title=Hong Kong's Most Under-the-Radar Billionaire: Calvin Lo|author=|first=|date=2018-11-02|work=Entrepreneur|language=en|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.8days.sg/en/sceneandheard/entertainment/hongkong-actress-bernice-liu-s-billionaire-boyfriend-splurges-s-10134556|title=Hongkong Actress Bernice Liu’s Billionaire Boyfriend Splurges S$323mil On Champagne|author=|first=|date=2018-04-15|work=8 Days|language=en|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref> 他亦是一位[[超級跑車|超級跑車]]收藏家，擁有罕見的[[帕加尼风神|帕加尼]]Huayra BC。<ref name=":5">{{Cite web|url=https://londonlovesbusiness.com/the-secretive-billionaire-calvin-lo/|title=The Secretive Billionaire: Calvin Lo|author=|first=|date=2018-11-23|work=London Business News {{!}} London Loves Business|language=en-GB|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.moneydigest.sg/calvin-lo-camera-shy-billionaires-car-collection/|title=Calvin Lo: Camera-shy Billionaire’s Car Collection|author=|first=|date=2018-11-06|work=Money Digest|language=en-US|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref> 盧啟賢也是亞洲第一批私人飛機[[灣流G650|灣流G650]]的擁有者之一。<ref name=":3" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.opptrends.com/calvin-lo-secretive-billionaire-splurges-250-million/|title=Calvin Lo: Secretive Billionaire Splurges $250 million in Champagne|author=|first=|date=2018-11-09|work=Opptrends|language=en-US|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref>

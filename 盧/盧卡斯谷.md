'''盧卡斯谷'''（{{lang-en|Lucas Valley}}）是位於[[美國|美國]][[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[馬林縣_(加利福尼亞州)|馬林縣]]的一個[[非建制地區|非建制地區]]。該地的面積和人口皆未知。<ref name="GR8">{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|美国人口调查局]]|accessdate=2011-05-14|title=American FactFinder}}</ref>

1978年，[[星球大战|星球大战]]导演[[乔治·卢卡斯|乔治·卢卡斯]]曾在该处购地兴建{{link-en|Skywalker Ranch}}。但盧卡斯谷是以十九世纪一位名为卢卡斯的农场工人命名，与该导演并无关系<ref name="star-tribune">{{cite web | url = http://www.startribune.com/389/story/26653.html | title = Skywalker Ranch: George Lucas creates a magic world in real life | work = Minneapolis-St. Paul Star Tribune | author = Strickler, Jeff | date = 2002-05-18 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20071010052547/http://www.startribune.com/389/story/26653.html | archivedate = 2007-10-10 | df =  }}</ref><ref name="MALT">{{cite web | url = http://www.malt.org/about/profiles/index.php?item=big_rock_ranches | title = Profiles in Preservation - Marin Agricultural Land Trust (MALT)}}</ref>。
== 地理 ==

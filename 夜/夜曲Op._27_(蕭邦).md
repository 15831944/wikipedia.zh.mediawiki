[[File:Op.27_Nocturne.jpg|thumb]]
'''夜曲Op. 27'''是[[蕭邦|蕭邦]]的一組[[夜曲|夜曲]]作品，於1836年創作<ref>{{cite book|last=Huneker|first=James G.|title=Chopin : the man and his music|publisher=Dover Publ.|location=New York|year=1966|page=251|isbn=0-486-21687-X|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MPFwo8BrkqQC&printsec=titlepage}}</ref>，1837年出版，為兩首互成對比的樂曲。David Dubal評道：「(這些夜曲)被形容為小型的[[敍事曲|敍事曲]]更為恰當<ref name="dubal461">{{cite book|last=Dubal|first=David|title=The art of the piano : its performers, literature, and recordings|publisher=Amadeus Press|location=Pompton Plains, N.J.|year=2004|page=461|isbn=1-57467-088-3|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=8698CZGx1ZQC&printsec=frontcover}}</ref> 。」Blair Johnson也指出這兩首曲與[[約翰·菲爾德|約翰·菲爾德]]的夜曲已是幾乎沒有相似之處了<ref name="allmusic">{{cite book |title=AllMusic Guide to Classical Music |last=Woodstra |first=Chris |coauthors=Brennan, Gerald; Schrott, Allen |year=2005 |publisher= Hal Leonard Corporation|isbn= 0-87930-865-6|page= 287 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=n29DHVKhZggC&printsec=frontcover |accessdate=21 March 2009 }}</ref>。

==Op. 27, No. 1==
[[File:Chopin_nocturne_op27_1a.png|thumb]]
夜曲Op. 27 No. 1是[[升C小調|升C小調]]，[[速度_(音樂)#基本速度標記|甚緩板]]，4/4拍。在第29[[小節|小節]]轉為樂曲的B段，並註''più mosso''（稍快點），3/4拍，中間一部分為[[降A大調|降A大調]]。第84小節轉回A段，99-101小節以[[慢板|慢板]]作結。

==Op. 27, No. 2==
[[File:Chopin_Nocturne_Op_27_No_2_opening.png|thumb]]
[[File:Chopin_nocturne_op27_2b.png|thumb]]
夜曲Op. 27 No. 2是[[降D大調|降D大調]]，[[速度_(音樂)#基本速度標記|緩板]]，註''Sostenuto''（將音符延長），6/8拍，共77小節。

此曲在流行文化中也有出現，如[[占士邦|占士邦]]電影[[海底城|海底城]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.007museum.com/The_Spy_Who_Loved_Me.htm|title=Trivia - The Spy Who Loved Me|publisher=James Bond Museum|accessdate=2009-03-30}}</ref><ref name="allmusic">{{cite book |title=AllMusic Guide to Classical Music |last=Woodstra |first=Chris |coauthors=Brennan, Gerald; Schrott, Allen |year=2005 |publisher= Hal Leonard Corporation|isbn= 0-87930-865-6|page= 287 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=n29DHVKhZggC&printsec=frontcover |accessdate=21 March 2009 }}</ref>。{{-}}

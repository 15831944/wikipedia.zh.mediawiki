'''進步黨'''（[[书面挪威语|书面挪威语]]：Fremskrittspartiet；[[挪威语|挪威语]]：Framstegspartiet，缩写为FrP）是一個[[挪威政党列表|挪威政党]]，1973年成立，奉行[[古典自由主义|古典自由主义]]<ref name=BriefIntroduction>{{cite web|url=http://www.frp.no/filestore/Introduction_To_The_Progress_Party.pdf|title=Ideology and Principles of the Progress Party|publisher=FrP.no|accessdate=11 November 2009|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110716202346/http://www.frp.no/filestore/Introduction_To_The_Progress_Party.pdf|archivedate=2011-07-16 }}</ref>和[[保守自由主义|保守自由主义]]。<ref name=klass>{{Cite news|url=http://www.klassekampen.no/kontakt_oss/54853/article/item/null|title=Sier nei til Kjærsgaard|work=[[Klassekampen|Klassekampen]]|first=Hans Petter|last=Sjøli|authorlink=Hans Petter Sjøli|date=25 September 2008|accessdate=16 November 2009|language=no |deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110717135641/http://www.klassekampen.no/kontakt_oss/54853/article/item/null|archivedate=2011-07-17 }}</ref>媒体和学者则认为该党奉行[[自由主义|自由主义]]<ref name="Osterud2013">{{cite book|author=Oyvind Osterud|title=Norway in Transition: Transforming a Stable Democracy|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=pc2MAQAAQBAJ&pg=PA114|date=18 October 2013|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-317-97037-8|pages=114–}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|author=Alan Siaroff|title=Comparative European Party Systems: An Analysis of Parliamentary Elections Since 1945|url=http://books.google.no/books?id=qpf8-q0etuIC&pg=PA344|year=2000|publisher=Routledge|isbn=9781135580247|page=344}}</ref><ref name="BackesMoreau">{{cite book|author=Uwe Backes, Patrick Moreau|title=The Extreme Right in Europe: Current Trends and Perspectives|url=http://books.google.no/books?id=uMkEZnJQHksC&pg=PA37|year=2011|publisher=Vandenhoeck & Ruprecht|isbn=9783647369228|page=37}}</ref>和[[右翼民粹主义|右翼民粹主义]]（适度）。<ref name="BackesMoreau"/><ref>Elin Haugsgjerd Allern (2010). ''[http://books.google.no/books?id=SZdHAQAAQBAJ&pg=PA26 Political Parties and Interest Groups in Norway]''. ECPR Press. p.26. "The Norwegian Progress Party is, like its Danish equivalent, traditionally characterised as a borderline case of the extreme or radical right (Ignazi 1992: 13–15; Kitschelt 1995: 121; Ignazi 2003: 157), and Mudde (2007:19) characterises FrP as a non-radical populist party."</ref><ref>Krisztina Arató, Petr Kaniok (2009). ''[http://books.google.no/books?id=0mGBy6HeVIEC&lpg=PP1&hl=no&pg=PA264#v=onepage&q&f=false Euroscepticism and European Integration]''. CPI/PSRC. p.264. "Yet the Norwegian case is not unique, and not only inasmuch as the Progress Party is somewhat more moderate than the abovecited cases. The free market orientation of the Danish and Norwegian Progress Parties long provided a modifying factor to any Euroscpticism (sic) they might harbour ..."</ref><ref>"[http://www.vg.no/nyheter/innenriks/forskere-frp-er-hoeyrepopulistisk/a/10126475/ Forskere: Frp er høyrepopulistisk]", ''Verdens Gang'' (NTB), 14.09.2013. "- Ja, de er høyrepopulister. Men sammenlignet med andre slike partier i Europa er de en moderat utgave og har sterkere innslag av liberalkonservative strømninger, sier Jupskås." 進步黨由[[安德斯·兰格|安德斯·兰格]]（Anders Lange）在1973年创立，主张减税，精简官僚机构，支持市场自由主义，对外国移民和申请避难者态度较苛刻，赞成加入欧盟。<ref name=ndla>{{cite web|url=http://ndla.no/nb/node/11473|work=Nasjonal Digital Læringsarena|title=Hva står de politiske partiene for?|trans_title=What do the political parties stand for?|language=no |first1=Jan-Arve|last1=Overland|first2=Ragnhild|last2=Tønnessen|accessdate=27 August 2010}}</ref>同时提倡增加使用独特的挪威公众的“石油基金”投资于基础设施。<ref name=frpo>{{cite web|url=http://www.frp.no/no/Vi_mener/Andre_temaer/Okonomisk_politikk/|work=Frp.no|title=Økonomisk politikk|trans_title=Economic policy|language=no|accessdate=12 November 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110929011506/http://www.frp.no/no/Vi_mener/Andre_temaer/Okonomisk_politikk/|archivedate=2011年9月29日}}</ref>[[卡尔·哈根|卡尔·哈根]]（Carl I. Hagen）从1978年到2006年长期担任党主席，<ref name=snlcih>{{cite encyclopedia|url=http://www.snl.no/.nbl_biografi/Carl_I_Hagen/utdypning|title=Carl I Hagen|encyclopedia=[[Norsk_biografisk_leksikon|Norsk biografisk leksikon]]|publisher=Kunnskapsforlaget|first=Gudleiv|last=Forr|authorlink=Gudleiv Forr|editor=Helle, Knut|accessdate=27 August 2010|language=no |location=Oslo|editor-link=Knut Helle}}</ref>现任主席[[西芙·延森|西芙·延森]]（Siv Jensen），2013年出任[[埃尔娜·索尔伯格|埃尔娜·索尔伯格]]联合政府的财政大臣。

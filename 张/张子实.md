'''张子实'''，男，1998年出生于[[山东省|山东省]][[临沂市|临沂市]]。2017年美联航拖拽乘客事件白宫请愿发起人<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://nextshark.com/zishi-zhang-student-launched-chineselivesmatter-petition/|title=Meet the 18-Year-Old U.K. Student Who Launched the #ChineseLivesMatter Petition|accessdate=2017-04-30|work=nextshark.com|language=en-US}}</ref><ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=http://huaren.haiwainet.cn/n/2017/0424/c232657-30877234.html|title=18岁留学生为美联航亚裔白宫请愿 2天签名20万 - 华人新闻_海外华人新闻网 - 海外网|accessdate=2017-04-30|last=L_204267|work=huaren.haiwainet.cn}}</ref>。

== 生平 ==

=== 中国大陆 ===
1998年，张子实出生于山东省临沂市一个“家境良好”的家庭中<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://blog.sina.com.cn/s/blog_815a93730101ajml.html|title=张子实，你在他乡还好吗_崔宗会_新浪博客|accessdate=2017-04-30|work=blog.sina.com.cn}}</ref>。2010年，进入临沂市第九中学学习。据新浪博客认证为“临沂九中语文教师”的崔宗会介绍，张子实在校期间成立蒙山清泉读书社并任社长，他创办的《清泉》报纸后来成为临沂九中校报。<ref name=":0" />

张子实后来接受《纽约时报》采访时提及他的家庭，其母亲在英国的一个语言中心当中文老师，其父“自己工作”。张子实的父母都比较重视教育，一直努力培养他的兴趣。张子实在中国大陆曾“学过很长时间的美术，拿过国家和省级的绘画奖。”<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://cn.nytimes.com/china/20170419/cc19chinese-lives-matter/|title=美联航事件后续：中国小伙白宫请愿起纷争|date=2017-04-20|newspaper=纽约时报中文网 国际纵览|accessdate=2017-04-30|language=zh}}</ref>

=== 海外留学 ===
2012年，张子实到英国读高中，并逐渐开始积极参与各种社会活动、慈善活动，参加青年组织<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.ses.lincs.sch.uk/news/?pid=6&nid=4&storyid=1592|title=Stamford Endowed Schools_Independent Day and Boarding School - Students Lend Some Company at Tixover Grange|author=|date=|work=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.stamfordmercury.co.uk/news/stamford-school-pupil-runs-brighton-marathon-to-raise-cash-for-cash-strapped-schools-1-7893219|title=Stamford School pupil runs Brighton Marathon to raise cash for cash-strapped schools.|accessdate=2017-04-30|work=www.stamfordmercury.co.uk|language=en}}</ref>，还曾报名参加英国广播公司(BBC)的辩论节目。<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=http://mt.sohu.com/20160902/n467272716.shtml|title=中国小留学生一封请愿书,惊动了整个欧美政坛,他说我将来要当英国首相-搜狐|accessdate=2017-04-30|work=mt.sohu.com}}</ref>

在BBC举办的关于2016年英国脱欧公投电视辩论中，支持退欧的张子实呼吁时任英国首相卡梅伦“正视人们对建制派和专家失去希望”，不要只听专家的意见。除此之外，他还参加过BBC一次关于一夫多妻制的电视直播辩论、支持女权运动、并导演了一部鼓励青年人参政的宣传片。<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.womensequality.org.uk/instate_women_onto_philosophy_a_level|title=Instate women onto Philosophy A-level|newspaper=Women's Equality|accessdate=2017-04-30|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.co.uk/hanna-naima-mccloskey/women-exam-syllabus_b_9070962.html|title=Why 17 Year Olds Campaigning for Women in the Syllabus Must End|accessdate=2017-04-30|work=The Huffington Post|language=en-GB}}</ref>

2016年，参与北京电视台《留学圈儿》节目录制。<ref name=":1" />

2017年，张子实就美联航拖拽乘客事件在白宫网站上发起了名为“[[华人|华人]]的命也是命（#ChineseLivesMatters）”的请愿，引发了巨大反响和社会争议。<ref name=":1" />

== 活动 ==

=== 美联航拖拽乘客事件白宫请愿 ===
2017年4月9日，美国联航快运3411号班机上发生暴力事件，69岁乘客David Dao（当时被传为华裔，后证实为美籍越南裔）在登机就坐后因拒绝将座位让给临时登机的[[联合航空|联合航空]]员工，遭地面安保人员蛮力拖拽出飞机并受伤，引发美国、中国、越南等国网民强烈反应。由于受拖拽乘客当时被误传为华裔，该事件在华人圈中引发的“种族主义”争论极为强烈。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/news/latest-news/fury-in-china-over-us-passengers-removal/news-story/d25a4b04ec888fa0258bea151f457f4f|title=Fury in China over US passenger's removal|date=2017-04-11|accessdate=2017-04-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-china-39562288|title=United Airlines: Chinese and Vietnamese anger at passenger removal|date=2017-04-11|newspaper=BBC News|accessdate=2017-04-30|language=en-GB}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-ual-passenger-idUSKBN17E1GN|title=United passenger launches legal action over forceful removal|date=2017-04-13|newspaper=Reuters|accessdate=2017-04-30}}</ref>

4月11日，张子实借鉴黑人平权运动中Black Lives Matter（黑人的命也是命）的口号，在[[白宫|白宫]]网站上发起了名为“[[华人|华人]]的命也是命（#ChineseLivesMatters）”的请愿，要求美国联邦政府调查[[美联航3411号班机事件|美联航拖拽乘客事件]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://petitions.whitehouse.gov/petition/chineselivesmatters-calling-federal-investigation-united-airlines-incident-10th-april-2017|title=#ChineseLivesMatters Calling a federal investigation into the United Airlines incident on 10th April 2017 {{!}} We the People: Your Voice in Our Government|accessdate=2017-04-30|work=petitions.whitehouse.gov|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://hk.on.cc/int/bkn/cnt/news/20170415/bknint-20170415160909707-0415_17011_001.html|title=英國華人：聯合航空虐客　華生發起白宮請願|newspaper=on.cc東網|accessdate=2017-04-30|language=zh-hk}}</ref>。随后，他又在中国大陆的知乎和微博发布了请愿内容和链接，呼吁网友签名、转发。请愿行为在中国网络空间引发巨大反响，张子实的知乎一天内收到点赞数将近两万，留言约三千。该请愿17小时内就收集到了10万个签名，两天内签名人数已达到了20万。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://news.ifeng.com/a/20170427/51010426_0.shtml|title=美联航事件后 这个山东小伙向白宫请愿|accessdate=2017-04-30|work=news.ifeng.com}}</ref><ref name=":1" /><ref name=":3" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.malaysia-chronicle.com/he-was-a-heart-specialist-world-class-poker-player-dr-david-dao-who-was-forced-to-beg-for-his-life-was-beaten-up-for-nothing-united-screwed-up-flight-was-not-overbooked/|title=HE WAS A HEART SPECIALIST & WORLD CLASS POKER PLAYER: DR DAVID DAO, WHO WAS FORCED TO BEG FOR HIS LIFE, WAS BEATEN UP FOR NOTHING - UNITED SCREWED UP, FLIGHT WAS "NOT" OVERBOOKED!|accessdate=2017-04-30|language=en-US|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170419022724/http://www.malaysia-chronicle.com/he-was-a-heart-specialist-world-class-poker-player-dr-david-dao-who-was-forced-to-beg-for-his-life-was-beaten-up-for-nothing-united-screwed-up-flight-was-not-overbooked/|archivedate=2017-04-19}}</ref>

但同时，也有部分中国网民对请愿抱抵制态度。知乎专栏作者“庄稼”在题为《请不要参加张子实发起的海外请愿》的文章中批评称“张子实在请愿信中强调‘Chinese’，而不是‘Asian’”认为其目的可能是激化华人和其他种族的矛盾<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/26303804|title=请不要参加张子实发起的海外请愿 - 知乎专栏|accessdate=2017-04-30|work=zhuanlan.zhihu.com|language=zh-hans}}</ref>。对此张子实回应称“Chinese Lives Matter这个标签，我现在看来也觉得有些不合适”，并强调“接受批评意见，有则改之无则加勉”<ref name=":1" />。

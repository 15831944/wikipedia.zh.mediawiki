'''特倫頓'''（{{lang-en|Trenton, New Jersey}}）是[[新澤西州|新澤西州]]的州府、[[默瑟縣_(新澤西州)|默瑟縣]]縣治。位於該州中部，西隔[[特拉華河|特拉華河]]與[[賓夕法尼亞州|賓夕法尼亞州]]相望。

==文化==
特伦特是新泽西州文化重镇，[[新泽西州博物馆|新泽西州博物馆]]<ref>[http://www.nj.gov/state/museum/dos_museum_collection.html 4 Museums in One], [[New_Jersey_State_Museum|New Jersey State Museum]]. Accessed January 5, 2015.</ref>、[[紐澤西州图书馆|紐澤西州图书馆]]<ref>[http://www.njstatelib.org/about/ About the NJ State Library], [[New_Jersey_State_Library|New Jersey State Library]]. Accessed January 5, 2015.</ref>、特伦特市立博物馆<ref>[http://www.ellarslie.org/TrentonCityMuseum-Welcome.htm Welcome to the Trenton City Museum] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110726030105/http://www.ellarslie.org/TrentonCityMuseum-Welcome.htm |date=July 26, 2011 }}, Trenton City Museum. Accessed September 7, 2013.</ref>、[[特伦特战争纪念馆|特伦特战争纪念馆]]（1932年建立以纪念一战阵亡士兵）<ref>[http://www.nj.gov/state/memorial/dos_wm_faq.html Frequently Asked Questions about the War Memorial], [[New_Jersey_Department_of_State|New Jersey Department of State]]. Accessed September 7, 2013.</ref>等重要文化设施均位于该市。

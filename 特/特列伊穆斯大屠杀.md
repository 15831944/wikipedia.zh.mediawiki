'''特列伊穆斯大屠杀'''（{{lang-ar|مجزرة التريمسة}}）是在2012年7月12日，发生于敘利亞[[哈马省|哈马省]][[特列伊穆斯|特列伊穆斯]]的一场军事行动与屠杀事件<ref name=th1213>{{cite news|last=Borger|first=Julian|title=Syria: at least 200 killed in Hama province massacre, say activists|url= http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2012/jul/13/syria-hama-massacre-activists?intcmp=239|accessdate=13 July 2012|newspaper=The Guardian|date=13 July 2012}}</ref>。事件起初由反对派报道，后来[[联合国|联合国]]觀察員於2012年7月14日發表聲明，表示敘利亞軍方主要針對反政府叛軍和活動人士的住屋，對此BBC表示與最初反對派聲稱是針對平民的屠殺有矛盾。<ref name="WP20120716">{{cite news|title=U.N. observers say Syrian government targeted opposition supporters in Tremseh|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/world/middle_east/un-observers-say-syrian-government-targeted-opposition-supporters-in-tremseh/2012/07/15/gJQADxTInW_story.html|accessdate=18 July 2012|newspaper=The Washington Post|date=16 July 2012|author=Babak Dehghanpisheh}}</ref><ref name="bbc-1">{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-18840535|title=Syria: Tremseh killings targeted rebels, UN says|publisher=BBC|date=14 July 2012|accessdate=18 July 2012 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140220235356/http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-18840535 |archivedate=2014-02-20}}</ref>

== 事件 ==
由于敘政府軍一支車隊在[[哈马|哈马]]附近遭到反對派伏擊，导致敘軍決定進行反擊，以夺回反对派控制的该地区<ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-18823303 Syrian army shelling 'kills more than 100' in Tremseh]</ref><ref>http://www.thejakartaglobe.com/afp/tough-sanctions-urged-after-syria-village-killings/530472</ref>。

哈马的LCC与叙人权观察SOHR报道，政府军与[[沙比哈|沙比哈]]在坦克和武装直升机的掩护下，在反对派撤退后进入了该地，超过100多人在集体处决中遇难。最初报道死亡人数为100多人<ref>[http://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4254937,00.html Report: At least 100 killed in Syrian village]</ref>，后来死亡人数上升到220人至250人<ref>[http://edition.cnn.com/2012/07/12/world/meast/syria-unrest/index.html?hpt=hp_t2 Syrian opposition reports 'massacre' in Hama province]</ref><ref name=ajaz12>{{cite news|title=Syrian activists report new massacre|url= http://www.aljazeera.com/news/middleeast/2012/07/201271221406575369.html|accessdate=13 July 2012|newspaper=Al Jazeera|date=13 July 2012}}</ref><ref name=theguard12>{{cite news|last=Betty|first=David|title=Syrian opposition activists: more than 200 dead in Hama village massacre|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2012/jul/12/syrian-opposition-activists-hama-massacre?CMP=twt_gu|accessdate=13 July 2012|newspaper=The Guardian|date=12 July 2012}}</ref>。在当局部队离开之后，有超过150具遗体在当地的[[清真寺|清真寺]]被发现<ref>[http://english.alarabiya.net/articles/2012/07/12/225996.html Syrian forces commit new massacre in Hama; Russia rejects new U.N. draft resolution]</ref>。周围许多建筑明显受到了炮击并损毁<ref>[http://www.thejakartaglobe.com/afp/syrian-troops-massacre-more-than-200-rebel-leader/530264 Syrian troops massacre more than 200: rebel leader]</ref>。

SOHR报道说遇难者中有不少反对派士兵<ref name=conflicting>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-18832286 Syria crisis: Conflicting reports of Tremseh killings]</ref><ref>[http://www.usatoday.com/news/world/story/2012-07-13/video-syria-massacre/56186824/1 Syria activists: Regime killed scores in village]</ref>，其中包括陆军中尉Ibrahim Zuait al-Tarkawai<ref>[http://www.chicagotribune.com/news/sns-rt-us-syria-crisisbre8610sh-20120702,0,1984433.story Syrian opposition reports massacre of 220 civilians]</ref>。SOHR报道总的死亡人数是150，目前只有40人的真实身份得到确认<ref>[http://english.alarabiya.net/articles/2012/07/13/226131.html Activist claims Syria massacre dead mostly rebels; videos show victims]</ref>。一名逃离该地的村民表示，[[阿拉维派|阿拉维派]]武装分子在反对派撤退后进入该地实施了大屠杀。生还者说许多房屋甚至清真寺都被政府军点燃<ref>http://www.thehindu.com/news/international/article3634445.ece</ref>。

一名反对派成员说：“很明显在反对派退却后，支持政府的[[阿拉维派|阿拉维派]]武装分子（[[沙比哈|沙比哈]]）包围了该地，之后展开了杀戮行动。几乎所有的房屋都因炮击而被毁和燃烧。”<ref>[http://www.thestar.com/news/world/article/1225858--syria-200-killed-in-new-massacre Syria: 200 killed in new massacre]</ref>

另一人说：“在凌晨六点，超过800名阿薩德部隊在重型火炮的支援下包围了该村，炮击发生后，人们逃离他们的住所前往学校和清真寺寻找掩护。阿萨德部队也炮轰学校与清真寺，大量建筑被毁的同时也造成大量伤亡。沙比哈也进村屠杀了许多无辜的村民。”<ref>[http://blogs.aljazeera.com/topic/syria/hama-activist-describes-massacre Hama activist describes massacre]</ref>

叙政府否认是政府军做的，并说是“武装黑帮成员”干的，还有三名安全部队士兵在交火中被杀。敘利亞政府起初表示有50名平民遇難<ref name=conflicting/>，不过后来撤回先前的宣稱並否認有平民在事件中死亡，说“政府军针对反对派展开了一次特别行动，并打死与俘获许多反对派成员”，没有平民在行动中死亡<ref>http://dawn.com/2012/07/13/terrorists-no-civilians-killed-in-treimsa-syria-army/</ref>。

截止晚上，[[叙利亚全国委员会|叙利亚全国委员会]]一名成员宣稱总计305人死亡<ref name=morethan300>{{cite news|url=http://www.smh.com.au/world/more-than-300-people-feared-killed-in-syrias-bloodiest-massacre-20120714-222d9.html|title=More than 300 people feared killed in Syria's bloodiest massacre|newspaper=SMH|accessdate=14 July 2012}}</ref>。一名当地活动家说他已经确认了74人被杀，其中20人的名字得到确认。另一人提供了103位遇难者的名单<ref name=UNblames>[http://onlineathens.com/national-news/2012-07-13/un-blames-regime-forces-syria-massacre-0 UN blames regime forces for Syria massacre]</ref>。其他组织给出的死亡数字也都在100人以上<ref>[http://www.thisdaylive.com/articles/death-grief-in-syrian-village-us-cries-murder-/119990/ -{Death, Grief in Syrian Village, US Cries ‘Murder’}-] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120715052414/http://www.thisdaylive.com/articles/death-grief-in-syrian-village-us-cries-murder-/119990/ |date=2012-07-15 }}</ref>。

== 事后 ==
[[科菲·安南|科菲·安南]]证实叙軍使用坦克、攻击直升机等重武器实施了这次袭击行动。他要求暂时停火以让监督员能够进入该地进行调查<ref>[http://english.alarabiya.net/articles/2012/07/13/226069.html Annan condemns Syria massacre, says Assad forces have used heavy weaponry]</ref>。

监督团负责人Robert Mood说，敘軍于7月13日继续对该镇进行攻击行动<ref>http://english.alarabiya.net/articles/2012/07/13/226169.html</ref>。

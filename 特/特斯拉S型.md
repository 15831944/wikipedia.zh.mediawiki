'''Tesla Model S'''是一款由[[美国|美国]][[特斯拉汽車|特斯拉汽車]]公司開發的全尺寸[[純電動車|純電動]]四門[[掀背车|掀背]][[轎車|轎車]]。作为原型在2009年[[法兰克福车展|法兰克福车展]]首次向公众展示，在2012年6月于美國开始零售交付使用<ref name=MSlaunch>{{cite news|url=http://www.mercurynews.com/business/ci_20919722/silicon-valley-milestone-tesla-motors-begins-delivering-model?refresh=no|title=In a Silicon Valley milestone, Tesla Motors begins delivering Model S electric cars |author=John Boudreau|work=[[San_Jose_Mercury_News|San Jose Mercury News]] |date=2012-06-22|accessdate=2012-06-22}}</ref>。2013年8月初特斯拉S型在欧洲发布，首批交货市场在挪威、瑞士和荷兰<ref name=DeliveryEU>{{cite web|url=http://files.shareholder.com/downloads/ABEA-4CW8X0/2450489059x0x682962/f9e27702-90ed-4516-a16e-dc720c411089/Q2'13%20Tesla%20Motors%20Shareholder%20Letter.pdf|title=Tesla Motors, Inc. – Second Quarter 2013 Shareholder Letter|author=[[Tesla_Motors|Tesla Motors]]|publisher=Tesla Motors|date=2013-08-07|accessdate=2013-08-07}}</ref>。

=== 动力总成 ===

2012年款特斯拉S型高性能型具有416马力（310千瓦）和443英尺·磅（600 N·M），后置式电动马达。基本型采用了362马力（270千瓦）和325英尺·磅（440 N·m）电机。据特斯拉汽车公司称该款电动汽车的[[风阻系数|风阻系数]]为0.24<ref name=DC>{{cite web|url=http://reviews.cnet.com/8301-13746_7-57459125-48/tesla-model-s-first-drive-quiet-satisfaction/ |title=Tesla Model S first drive |publisher=cnet.com |date= |accessdate=2013-01-28}}</ref>，比当时市面上的任何一款车都低（除了一些在S型之后发布的[[梅赛德斯-奔驰|梅赛德斯-奔驰]]CLA级车）<ref name=MT20120622>{{cite news|url=http://www.motortrend.com/roadtests/alternative/1206_2012_tesla_model_s_first_drive/|title=2012 Tesla Model S First Drive|author=Frank Markus|work=[[Motor_Trend|Motor Trend]]|date=2012-06-22|accessdate=2012-06-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2012/09/30/automobiles/autoreviews/one-big-step-for-tesla-one-giant-leap-for-evs.html?pagewanted=2&_r=1&ref=automobiles|title=One Big Step for Tesla, One Giant Leap for E.V.’s|author=Bradley Berman|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=2012-09-28|accessdate=2012-09-29}}</ref>。<!--The 2012 Tesla Model S Performance model has a {{convert|416|hp|abbr=on}} and {{convert|443|ftlb|Nm|abbr=on|-1}} rear-mounted [[electric_motor|electric motor]].

'''特龙科内斯'''是[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]的一个村庄，位于[[格雷罗州|格雷罗州]]的{{tsl|en|La Unión de Isidoro Montes de Oca|拉乌尼翁自治区}}。其距该州首府[[锡瓦塔内霍|锡瓦塔内霍]]约{{convert|20|mi|km}}<ref name="chaplin">{{cite news |title= On Mexico’s Coast, a Surfer’s Secret  |first=Julia |last=Chaplin |url= http://travel.nytimes.com/2006/12/10/travel/10surfing.html  |newspaper=New York Times |location=New York |date=2006-12-10 |accessdate=2009-10-19 }}</ref>。2005年时的人口为593<ref name="inegi">{{cite web |title=Principales resultados por localidad 2005 (ITER) |url=http://www.inegi.org.mx/est/contenidos/espanol/sistemas/conteo2005/localidad/iter/default.asp?s=est&c=10395 |publisher=INEGI |location=Mexico |language=es |accessdate=2009-10-19 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110722200612/http://www.inegi.org.mx/est/contenidos/espanol/sistemas/conteo2005/localidad/iter/default.asp?s=est&c=10395 |archivedate=2011-07-22 |df= }}</ref>。

[[Image:TurtleReleaseTroncones.JPG|thumb]]

2008年，特龙科内斯发生了鲨鱼袭击人类的事件，造成了两人死亡，亦是该地区30年来首次发生鲨鱼攻击事件<ref name="reuters">{{cite news |title= La Armada de México, tras tiburones en Zihuatanejo  |url= http://www.jornada.unam.mx/2008/05/27/index.php?section=estados&article=033n2est |agency=Reuters |newspaper=La Jornada |date=2008-05-27 |accessdate=2009-10-19 |language=es |trans-title=The Navy of Mexico behind sharks in Zihuatanejo }}</ref>。[[公牛鯊|公牛鯊]]大量聚集在海滩不远处，但原因尚不明确，普遍认为是由于当时的寒流导致鲨鱼不得不较以往更加靠近海岸<ref name="currents">{{cite news |title= Is it safe to go back in the water? Looking for answers to the three shark attacks in Mainland Mexico |url= http://www.surfline.com/surf-news/looking-for-answers-to-the-three-shark-attacks-in-mainland-mexico-is-it-safe-to-go-back-in-the-water_15897/ |newspaper=Surfline |date=2008-06-05}}</ref>。

==地理==
特龙科内斯西南部临[[太平洋|太平洋]]，距离{{tsl|en|Mexican Federal Highway 200|墨西哥200号高速公路}}约3公里。

'''特洛伊学区'''<ref>"[http://www.oakland.k12.mi.us/china/China_one.pdf 2007 Oakland Schools Chinese Language & Culture Initiative]{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}." [[Oakland_Schools|Oakland Schools]]. June 2007.</ref> ('''Troy School District''')是[[密歇根|密歇根]]县的学区，总部在[[特洛伊_(密歇根州)|特洛伊]]。

特洛伊学区营运12间小学、4间初中、1间高中、和1间选择高中，学生人数达多于12,000人。<ref>"[http://www.troy.k12.mi.us/files/_nIAs0_/8da28d76659fa6113745a49013852ec4/TSD_Annual_Report_2011.12.pdf 2011-2012 Annual Report]{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}." 特洛伊学区. p. 6. "More than 12,000 students are enrolled in twelve elementary, four middle schools and two traditional and one non traditional high school."</ref>

'''特拉维斯·科德尔·卡兰尼克'''（{{lang-en|'''Travis Cordell Kalanick'''}}, {{bd|1976年|8月6日||}}），美国程序员和企业家，他是[[對等網路檔案分享|對等網路檔案分享]][[Red_Swoosh|Red Swoosh]]和[[交通网络公司|交通网络公司]][[优步|优步]]的联合创始人。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.biznespreneur.com/uber-president-jeff-resigns-leadership-beliefs/|title=Uber President Jeff Resigns Over Leadership Beliefs. – Biznespreneur|last=Tomiwa|date=2017-03-20|work=Biznespreneur|access-date=2017-03-22|language=en-US}}</ref>

==生平==
[[File:Travis_Kalanick_LeWeb.jpg|thumbnail]]
1976年出生于美国[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]，1998年肄业于[[加州大学洛杉矶分校|加州大学洛杉矶分校]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www2.technologyreview.com/tr35/profile.aspx?TRID=263 |title=Innovator Under 35: Travis Kalanick, 25 – MIT Technology Review |publisher=.technologyreview.com |date= |accessdate=2013-01-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://dcweek2012.sched.org/speaker/traviskalanick1#.UPMZlok3c5w |title=Travis Kalanick - DCWEEK 2012 |publisher=Dcweek2012.sched.org |date= |accessdate=2013-01-17}}</ref><ref name=IncResistance>[http://www.inc.com/magazine/201307/christine-lagorio/uber-the-car-service-explosive-growth.html Inc Magazine: "Resistance Is Futile" BY Christine Lagorio-Chafkin] July 2013</ref> 2001年成为[[對等網路檔案分享|對等網路檔案分享]][[Red_Swoosh|Red Swoosh]]联合创始人，2007年该公司被另一家美国网路[[Akamai|Akamai]]以1900万美元价格收购。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://techcrunch.com/2007/04/12/payday-for-red-swoosh-15-million-from-akamai/ |title=Payday for Red Swoosh: $15 million from Akamai |work= TechCrunch}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.akamai.com/html/about/press/releases/2007/press_041207.html |title=April 12, 2007 - Akamai Acquires Red Swoosh |publisher=Akamai.com |date=2007-04-12 |accessdate=2013-01-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://allthingsd.com/20111108/uber-ceo-travis-kalanick-on-how-he-failed-and-lived-to-tell-the-tale/ |title=Uber CEO Travis Kalanick on Failure and Red Swoosh – Liz Gannes – News |publisher=AllThingsD |date=2011-11-08 |accessdate=2013-01-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://gigaom.com/2007/04/12/akamai-goes-p2p-buys-red-swoosh/|title=Gigaom – Akamai goes P2P, buys Red Swoosh|author=Om Malik|publisher=|accessdate=April 22, 2016}}</ref>2009年伙同[[格瑞特·坎普|格瑞特·坎普]]，共同成为[[交通网络公司|交通网络公司]][[优步|优步]]的联合创始人。

2014年他成为当年《[[福布斯|福布斯]]》杂志400名最富有美国人中名列190位，估值达到63亿美元。<ref>{{Cite news|title=Forbes Announces Its 33rd Annual Forbes 400 Ranking Of The Richest Americans |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbespr/2014/09/29/forbes-announces-its-33rd-annual-forbes-400-ranking-of-the-richest-americans/#66530f2049da |work=Forbes.com |publisher=''[[Forbes|Forbes]]'' |date=29 September 2014 |accessdate=12 November 2014 }}</ref> [[路透社|路透社]]报道，他是一个比较不顾及别人感受的老板。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-uber-board-ceo-idUSKBN19205S|title=Uber board to discuss CEO absence, policy changes: source|last=|first=|date=2017-06-11|work=Reuters|access-date=2017-06-11|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|quote=Kalanick has developed a reputation as an abrasive leader, and his approach has rubbed off on his company. The 40-year-old executive was captured on video in February berating an Uber driver.}}</ref>

2017年6月21日，基于众多投资人的压力，特拉维斯·卡兰尼克宣布不再担任[[优步|优步]]首席执行官，但仍保留董事会成员位置。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2017/jun/20/uber-ceo-travis-kalanick-resigns|title=Travis Kalanick: Uber CEO resigns following months of chaos|publisher=The Guardian|date=21 June 2017}}</ref>.

==家族==
卡蘭尼克的母親邦妮·霍羅威茨·卡蘭尼克（{{lang|en|Bonnie Horowitz Kalanick}}）在《{{le|洛杉磯日報|Los Angeles Daily News}}》擔任廣告企劃；父親唐納·愛德華·卡蘭尼克（{{lang|en|Donald Edward Kalanick}}）為洛杉磯當地的土木工程師，<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/theobserver/2014/dec/21/travis-kalanick-uber-cab-app-observer-proifile |publisher= [[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] |title=Travis Kalanick: Uber-capitalist who wants to have the world in the back of his cabs|first=Andrew |last=Anthony |date=2014-12-20 |accessdate=2017-05-01 }}</ref><ref name=Betrothals>{{cite web|url=https://www.newspapers.com/newspage/19378172/ |title=Independent Press-Telegram: "BETROTHALS TOLD"|location=Long Beach, California |page=118 |date=1966-01-16 |publisher=Independent Press-Telegram |accessdate=2017-05-01}}</ref>自幼在天主教家庭中長大。祖父母為麥可·安德魯·「麥克」· 卡蘭尼克（{{lang|en|Michael Andrew "Mike" Kalanick}}）及瑪麗·澤洛克（{{lang|en|Mary Zeyock}}）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.havredailynews.com/story/2015/04/03/obituaries/obituary-steve-kalanick/503482.html|title=Obituary – Steve Kalanick|work=Havre Daily News|accessdate=2016-04-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ancestry.com/1940-census/usa/California/Mike-A-Kalanick_2mqgt5|title=Mike A Kalanick in the 1940 Census|publisher=|accessdate=2016-04-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://betterbydrbrooke.com/lessons-from-my-grandma-on-love-life-hard-work/ |title=Lessons From My Grandma On Love, Life & Hard Work |author=Dr. Brooke Kalanick-Larson |date=2011-11-21 |work=Better By Dr. Brooke |accessdate=2016-04-22}}</ref>卡蘭尼克具有斯洛伐克及奧地利血統，在其曾祖父母那代移民至美國生活。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://dennikn.sk/696344/zakladatel-ubera-ma-slovenske-korene-jeho-pradedo-prisiel-zo-zaniknutej-dediny-pod-vihorlatom/?ref=tit|title=Zakladatel Uberu ma Sloveske korene|publisher= dennikn.sk |language=sk|accessdate=2017-03-03}}</ref>

卡蘭尼克有兩位同父異母的姊姊，其中一位為美國演員[[爱丽森·阿什莉·阿姆|爱丽森·阿什莉·阿姆]]的母親，另外還一名身為消防員的弟弟柯里（{{lang|en|Cory}}）。<ref name="Betrothals" /><ref>{{cite news|title=Alyson Shontell: All Hail The Uber Man!

'''特洛伊'''（{{lang|en|Troy}}）是[[美国|美国]][[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[迈阿密县_(俄亥俄州)|迈阿密县]]一个城市，是迈阿密县的县城<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx|accessdate=2011-06-07|title=Find a County|publisher=National Association of Counties}}</ref>，2010年人口普查人口为25,058人，是该县最大的城市，是俄亥俄州第61大城市。 它是[[代顿_(俄亥俄州)|代顿]]大都会统计区的一部分。 特洛伊是六月份第一个周末的草莓节的举办地。<ref name="GR7">{{cite web|url=https://gostrawberries.com|title=Troy Strawberry Festival Homepage|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150523120555/http://gostrawberries.com/|archivedate=2015-05-23}}</ref>

==历史==
特洛伊1807年被规划。<ref>{{cite book|last=Overman|first=William Daniel|title=Ohio Town Names|url=https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015015361465;view=1up;seq=150|year=1958|publisher=Atlantic Press|location=Akron, OH|page=134}}</ref>一个叫特洛伊的邮局自1824年开始运作。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?state=OH | title=Post offices | publisher=Jim Forte Postal History | accessdate=21 June 2016}}</ref>

特洛伊是1913年“大洪水”遭受严重洪水影响的城市之一。<ref name=flood>{{cite web| url=http://www.daytondailynews.com/n/content/oh/story/news/local/2008/03/21/ddn032208lifefloodplus.html| title=Flood of 1913 remains Ohio's greatest| accessdate=2008-04-01| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110522020900/http://www.daytondailynews.com/n/content/oh/story/news/local/2008/03/21/ddn032208lifefloodplus.html| archivedate=2011-05-22}}</ref>

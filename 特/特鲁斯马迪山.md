'''特鲁斯马迪山'''（[[马来文|马来文]]：'''Gunung Trus Madi'''）为[[马来西亚|马来西亚]]第二高山，海拔2642米。其位于[[沙巴|沙巴]]，毗邻[[基纳巴卢山|基纳巴卢山]]。该山上存在着大量[[特有种|特有的]]动植物，如[[大叶猪笼草|大叶猪笼草]]{{small|（''Nepenthes macrophylla''）}}。<ref>Marabini, J. 1984. {{PDFlink|[http://www.carnivorousplants.org/cpn/articles/CPNv13n2p38_40.pdf A Field Trip to Gunong Trusmadi.]|442 [[Kibibyte|KiB]]<!-- application/pdf, 453522 bytes -->}} ''[[食虫植物通讯|Carnivorous Plant Newsletter]]'' '''13'''(2): 38–40.</ref><ref>Kitayama, K., J. Kulip, J. Nais & A. Biun 1993. Vegetation Survey on Mount Trus Madi, Borneo a Prospective New Mountain Park. ''Mountain Research and Development'' '''13'''(1): 99–105. {{DOI|10.2307/3673647}}</ref><ref>Bourke, G. 2007. Exploring the upper reaches of Gunung Trus Madi. ''[[澳大利亚食虫植物|Carniflora Australis]]'' (9): 9–16.</ref>

''Mitteilungen der Botanischen Staatssammlung München'' '''19''': 449–452.</ref><ref name=Briggs>Briggs, J.G.R. 1984. The discovery of ''Nepenthes × trusmadiensis''—an impressive new pitcher-plant.

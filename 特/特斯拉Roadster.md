'''特斯拉Roadster'''（{{lang-en|Tesla Roadster}}）是[[特斯拉汽车|特斯拉汽车]]公司于2008年推出、2012年停产的一款[[纯电动车|纯电动]][[跑车|跑车]]。Roadster既是首款使用[[锂离子电池|锂离子电池]]的[[量产|量产]]全电动跑车，也是首款续航里程达到{{convert|200|mi}}的纯电动车。<ref>{{cite web| url=https://cleantechnica.com/2015/04/26/electric-car-history/ | title=Electric Car Evolution |first=Zachary |last= Shahan |publisher=Clean Technica |date = 2015-04-26| accessdate=2016-09-08}} ''2008: The Tesla Roadster becomes the first production electric vehicle to use lithium-ion battery cells as well as the first production electric vehicle to have a range of over 200 miles on a single charge.''</ref>

据[[美国环保署|美国环保署]]的数据显示，Roadster的续航里程为{{convert|244|mi}}<ref name="Tesla 2008-09-09">{{cite web | url=http://www.teslamotors.com/about/press/releases/tesla-motors-selects-borgwarner-production-new-gearbox-tesla-roadster | date=2011-02-25 | publisher=Tesla Motors |title=Tesla Motors Selects BorgWarner for Production of New Gearbox for Tesla Roadster }}</ref>，{{convert|0|to|60|mph|abbr=on}}加速为3.7至3.9秒。其最高车速可达{{convert|125|mph|abbr=on}}，等效油耗则为120mpg（2.0 L/100 km）。同时，其电池-里程的转换率则能达到135[[千瓦·时|Wh]]/km，平均效率为88%。<ref name="motor technology">{{cite web |url= http://www.teslamotors.com/roadster/technology/motor |title=Roadster Innovations: Motor |publisher=Tesla Motors |accessdate=2011-02-25 |quote=The Roadster achieves an overall driving efficiency of 88%, about three times the efficiency of a conventional car.}}</ref>

'''特拉卡埃莱尔'''（{{lang-nah|Tlācaēllel}}，1397－1487），[[阿兹特克|阿兹特克]]王族，[[建筑师|建筑师]]，自15世纪20年代开始终身担任[[阿兹特克帝国|阿兹特克帝国]]的首席顾问<ref>Durán, Diego, ''History of the Indies of New Spain'', Norman, OK: University of Oklahoma Press, 1994, pp. 74–101, {{ISBN|0-8061-2649-3}}.</ref><ref>Malmstrom, Vincent H. (1997), ''Cycles of the Sun, Mysteries of the Moon: The Calendar in Mesoamerican Civilization'', Austin, TX: University of Texas Press, p. 238, {{ISBN|0-292-75196-6}}.</ref>。他是[[特拉托阿尼|特拉托阿尼]][[维齐利维特尔|维齐利维特尔]]和卡卡玛西瓦特尔女王的儿子，[[伊兹科瓦特尔|伊兹科瓦特尔]]之侄。

在伊兹科瓦特尔统治年间，他被任命为将军（[[特拉科奇卡尔卡特尔|特拉科奇卡尔卡特尔]]），在15世纪20年代和[[特帕内克人|特帕内克人]]作战期间被擢升为国王的首席顾问，称为[[西瓦科瓦特尔|西瓦科瓦特尔]]。他终身担任这一职务，辅佐过四代君王。

特拉卡埃莱尔在思想上强调阿兹特克人是[[选民_(宗教)|被众神选中的子民]]，定战神[[维齐洛波奇特利|维齐洛波奇特利]]为守护神<ref>Brotherston, Gordon (1974), "Huitzilopochtli and What Was Made of Him", p. 159 ''in'' Hammond, Norman (ed.) (1974), ''Mesoamerican Archaeology - New Approaches: Proceedings of a Symposium on Mesoamerican Archaeology held by the University of Cambridge Centre of Latin American Studies, August 1972'', Austin, TX: University of Texas Press, pp. 155–66, {{ISBN|0-292-75008-0}}.</ref>。他极其强调军事<ref>Burke, John Francis (2002), ''Mestizo Democracy: The Politics of Crossing Borders'', College Station: Texas A & M University Press, p. 137, {{ISBN|1-58544-208-9}}.</ref>，为赋予军事活动以宗教意义，他提出[[荣冠战争|荣冠战争]]，并扩大[[阿兹特克活人献祭|人祭]]规模。

他强制推行节约令，禁止平民穿戴奢侈服饰，以巩固贵族地位。他亦实施[[焚书|焚书]]，销毁阿兹特克人迁居前的当地史料<ref>Ostler, Nicholas (2005), ''Empires of the Word: A Language History of the World'', New York: HarperCollins, p. 354 {{ISBN|0-06-621086-0}}.</ref><ref>Madrid Codex, VIII, 192v, as quoted in León-Portilla, p. 155.
{{cite book |author=León-Portilla, Miguel |year=1963 |title=Aztec Thought and Culture: A Study of the Ancient Náhuatl Mind |series=Civilization of the American Indian series, no. 67|others=Jack Emory Davis (trans.) |location=Norman |publisher=University of Oklahoma Press |oclc=181727}} Note that León-Portilla finds Tlacaelel to be the instigator of this burning, despite lack of specific historical evidence.</ref>。

阿兹特克国家在特拉卡埃莱尔主政期间达到国力巅峰。他于1487年逝世。特拉卡埃莱尔逝世后，阿兹特克帝国继续向北方的[[奇奇梅克|奇奇梅克]]和南方的[[玛雅|玛雅]]土地进军<ref>{{cite book|last1=Schroeder|first1=Susan|title=Talcaelel Remembered: Mastermind of the Aztec Empire|date=2016|publisher=University of Oklahoma Press}}</ref>。

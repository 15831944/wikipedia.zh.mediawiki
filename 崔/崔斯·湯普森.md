'''崔斯·尼可拉斯·湯普森'''（{{lang-en|Trayce Nikolas Thompson}}，{{bd|1991年|3月15日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟的外野手，目前效力於芝加哥白襪。哥哥是NBA[[金州勇士|金州勇士]]隊的明星[[克雷·湯普森|克雷·湯普森]]。

==棒球生涯==
===芝加哥白襪===
2009年選秀，白襪在第2輪第61順位選走湯普森。

2013年11月20日，湯普森被選進40人名單。<ref name="chicago.whitesox.mlb.com">{{cite web |url=http://chicago.whitesox.mlb.com/news/article/cws/white-sox-add-carlos-sanchez-and-trayce-thompson-claim-jake-elmore?ymd=20131120&content_id=64079778&vkey=news_cws |title=White Sox add Sanchez and Thompson, claim Elmore |publisher=Chicago.whitesox.mlb.com |date=November 20, 2013 |accessdate=December 14, 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203020941/http://chicago.whitesox.mlb.com/news/article/cws/white-sox-add-carlos-sanchez-and-trayce-thompson-claim-jake-elmore?ymd=20131120&content_id=64079778&vkey=news_cws |archivedate=2013年12月3日 |df= }}</ref>2015年8月3日，湯普森升上大聯盟，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxsports.com/mlb/story/white-sox-call-up-of-trayce-thompson-brother-of-nba-star-klay-thompson-080315|title=White Sox call up OF Trayce Thompson, brother of NBA star Klay Thompson|author=Fox Sports|work=FOX Sports|accessdate=August 12, 2015}}</ref>並於隔日完成初登板。<ref name="ocregister.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.ocregister.com/angels/thompson-677077-angels-hit.html|title=VIDEO: O.C. rookie Trayce Thompson hits first career home run against Angels|work=The Orange County Register|accessdate=August 12, 2015}}</ref>8月11日，湯普森擊出生涯首轟。<ref name="ocregister.com"/>
===洛杉磯道奇===
2015年12月16日，白襪將湯普森,Micah Johnson和Frankie Montas交易至道奇隊，完成白襪道奇紅人三方交易。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.dodgers.mlb.com/news/article/159795544/reds-trade-todd-frazier-to-white-sox|title=White Sox acquire Frazier in 3-team deal|work=mlb.com|first=Andrew|last=Simon|date=December 16, 2015|accessdate=December 16, 2015}}</ref>2016年，他在道奇出賽80場，打擊率2成25，13轟及32分打點。<ref name=br>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/players/t/thomptr01.shtml|title=Trayce Thompson Statistics & History|work=Baseball Reference}}</ref>7月16日，他因為背部疼痛而進入傷兵名單，並缺席剩餘的賽季。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nbclosangeles.com/news/local/Los-Angeles-Dodgers-Injury-Updates-Kazmir-Misses-Out-on-Minor-League-Playoffs-393868421.html|title=Los Angeles Dodgers Injury Updates: Kazmir Misses Out on Minor League Playoffs|work=NBCLosAngeles|first=Michael|last=Duarte}}</ref>

2017年，道奇將他下放3A。他僅在大聯盟出賽27場，打擊率僅1成22，OPS僅.483。

2018年3月27日，道奇將湯普森指定轉讓。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlbtraderumors.com/2018/03/dodgers-designate-trayce-thompson-claim-cory-mazzoni-from-cubs.html|title=Dodgers Designate Trayce Thompson, Claim Cory Mazzoni From Cubs|work=MLB Trade Rumors|first=Mark|last=Pollshuk|date=March 27, 2018|accessdate=March 27, 2018}}</ref>

===奧克蘭運動家===
2018年4月3日，外野傷兵滿營的洋基隊簽下湯普森，不過2天後就將他釋出。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlbtraderumors.com/2018/04/yankees-claim-trayce-thompson-waivers-dodgers.html|title=Yankees Claim Trayce Thompson|work=mlb trade rumors|first=Steve|last=Adams|date=April 3, 2018|accessdate=April 3, 2018}}</ref>再過2天被運動家隊簽下來。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlbtraderumors.com/2018/04/athletics-claim-trayce-thompson.html|title=Athletics Claim Trayce Thompson|work=mlb trade rumors|first=Jeff|last=Todd|date=April 5, 2018|accessdate=April 5, 2018}}</ref>4月17日，他被運動家隊指定轉讓。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlbtraderumors.com/2018/04/athletics-designate-trayce-thompson-for-assignment.html|title=Athletics Designate Trayce Thompson for Assignment|work=mlb trade rumors|first=Jeff|last=Todd|date=April 17, 2018|accessdate=April 17, 2018}}</ref>
===重回白襪===
2018年4月19日，湯普森又重回白襪隊。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/baseball/whitesox/ct-spt-white-sox-trade-tyler-saladino-get-trayce-thompson-20180419-story,amp.html|title=White Sox trade Tyler Saladino to Brewers, pick up Trayce Thompson from Athletics |first=Paul|last=Sullivan|work=Chicago Tribune|date=April 19, 2018|accessdate=April 20, 2018}}</ref>6月22日被白襪隊指定轉讓。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cbssports.com/fantasy/baseball/news/white-soxs-trayce-thompson-designated-for-assignment/|title=White Sox's Trayce Thompson: Designated for assignment|publisher=CBSSports|date=June 22, 2018|accessdate=June 22, 2018}}</ref>11月2日成為自由球員。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseballamerica.com/stories/minor-league-free-agents-2018/|title=Minor League Free Agents 2018|work=Baseball America|first=Matt|last=Eddy|date=November 6, 2018|accessdate=November 8, 2018}}</ref>

===克里夫蘭印地安人===
2018年12月1日，他與印地安人隊簽下小聯盟約。

==個人生活==
湯普森的父親是前NBA名將[[麥卡爾·湯普森|麥卡爾·湯普森]]，他曾效力於湖人隊。他有兩個哥哥：一位是在發展聯盟效力的麥可·湯普森，另一位哥哥是目前效力於NBA[[金州勇士|金州勇士]]的[[克雷·湯普森|克雷·湯普森]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/baseball/whitesox/ct-trayce-thompson-klay-thompson-sullivan-spt-0223-20150222-story.html|title=White Sox prospect Trayce Thompson inspired by brother and NBA star Klay|author=Chicago Tribune|date=February 22, 2015|work=chicagotribune.com|accessdate=August 12, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/baseball/whitesox/ct-klay-thompson-recalls-brother-trayce-s-white-sox-debut-20150811-story.html|title=Klay Thompson recalls brother Trayce's 'surreal' White Sox debut|author=Chicago Tribune|date=August 11, 2015|work=chicagotribune.com|accessdate=August 12, 2015}}</ref>

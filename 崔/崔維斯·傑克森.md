'''崔維斯·卡爾文·傑克森'''（{{lang-en|Travis Calvin Jackson}}, {{bd|1903年|11月2日|1987年|7月27日| }}），為美國職棒大聯盟的選手。大聯盟生涯15年皆效力於紐約巨人隊。他在1933年世界大賽拿下冠軍，並在1934年入選明星賽。1960年球季，傑克森曾一度在小聯盟執教。

傑克森在14歲時，參與一場小聯盟比賽被前大聯盟選手Kid Elberfeld相中，並被推薦給當時執教紐約巨人的[[約翰·麥格勞_(棒球選手)|約翰·麥格勞]]。他優異的守備能力使他獲得了「石牆」這個綽號。他在1982年入選名人堂。

==早年==
傑克森是家中獨子，他父親為了紀念死於[[阿拉摩之戰|阿拉摩之戰]]的中校威廉·崔維斯，因而將他取名崔維斯。<ref name=sabr/>他父親在傑克森三歲時就開始帶他打球，也奠定他的棒球基礎。<ref name=sportingnews>{{cite news|title=Travis Jackson Began Tossing a Ball at Age of Three, Playing with Dad – and Kept on Until he Became Star|publisher=[[The_Sporting_News|The Sporting News]]|date=June 6, 1930|accessdate=March 30, 2012}}</ref>

傑克森的叔叔在他14歲時帶他加入小岩城旅行者，一支南方協會的小聯盟球隊。他叔叔也向Kid Elberfeld推薦他的姪子，<ref name=sabr>{{cite web|first=Greg |last=Erion|url=http://sabr.org/bioproj/person/cf84ae81 |title=Travis Jackson |publisher=[[Society_for_American_Baseball_Research|Society for American Baseball Research]] |date= |accessdate=April 15, 2012}}</ref>而Elberfeld經過觀察後，就和傑克森表示若自己準備好進入大聯盟，就可以跟他簽約。<ref name=sabr/><ref name=sportingnews/>

傑克森之後加入了沃希托浸會大學。<ref name=nytobit/>在大學時期他膝蓋受過傷，而這個傷也在他日後大聯盟生涯裡再度復發。<ref name=nytobit/>
==職業生涯==
===小聯盟及紐約巨人===
Elberfeld在傑克森完成大學學業後就和他簽約。1921及1922年，他在小岩城打球。<ref name=sabr/>1922年，他單季就發生了72次失誤，他認為他的單季失誤次數已是金氏紀錄。<ref name=nytobit/>

{{quote box|align=left|width=25%|quote="我覺得我設下了世界上最多失誤的紀錄。我的手臂狀況確實是很良好，但是我的控球還有待加強。大部分的失誤都是雙失誤(一個球兩次失誤)。坐在一壘和右外野的觀眾們都知道我的情況，每當球擊向我的防區時，觀眾們總會鼓譟著：快看，他竟然接到球了！"|source= – 崔維斯·傑克森對於他1922年單季72次失誤的說法<ref name=nytobit/>}}

儘管如此，Elberfeld還是向麥格勞推薦傑克森。6月30日，麥格勞向傑克森簽約，也結束了1922年在小岩城的生涯。<ref>{{cite book|first=Noel|last=Hynd|title=The Giants of the Polo Grounds: The Glorious Times of Baseball's New York Giants|location=New York|publisher=Doubleday|year=1988|page=232|accessdate=March 30, 2012}}</ref>

1922年9月22日，傑克森在巨人初登板，並出賽三場。由於先發游擊手Dave Bancroft和三壘手Heinie Groh都受傷，因此由傑克森做為替補。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=qwVhAAAAIBAJ&sjid=DGQNAAAAIBAJ&pg=1155,258796&dq=travis-jackson&hl=en |title=Travis Jackson Should Prove Good Utility Man For Giants in Series|page=13|newspaper=[[Providence_News|Providence News]]|date=September 28, 1923|accessdate=April 19, 2012}}</ref>而總教練也相信他的實力，因此在1924年將Bancroft交易至勇士，讓傑克森做為先發游擊手。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=NyFQAAAAIBAJ&sjid=wgoEAAAAIBAJ&pg=3859,6732727&dq=travis-jackson&hl=en|title=Jackson To Succeed Dave|page=10|agency=Associated Press|date=November 13, 1923|newspaper=[[The_Milwaukee_Sentinel|The Milwaukee Sentinel]]|accessdate=April 19, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=OLggAAAAIBAJ&sjid=eGkFAAAAIBAJ&pg=2728,3614675&dq=travis-jackson+dave-bancroft&hl=en|title=McGraw Staging Biggest Gamble in Young Jackson: Filling Shoes Left by Classy Dave Bancroft with 20 Year Old Boy |newspaper=[[The_Lewiston_Daily_Sun|The Lewiston Daily Sun]]|page=6|date=November 22, 1923|accessdate=April 19, 2012}}</ref>1924年，傑克森出賽151場，<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=sXZOAAAAIBAJ&sjid=gkADAAAAIBAJ&pg=4865,6008192&dq=travis-jackson+1924&hl=en|title=Young Shortstops Have Busy Season: Wright and Jackson Miss Few Games with Respective Teams in 1924|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|date=December 31, 1924|accessdate=April 19, 2012|newspaper=Ludington Daily News}}</ref>擊出11支全壘打，打擊率3成02。在1924年世界大賽時，傑克森在第七戰發生關鍵失誤，因而痛失冠軍獎盃。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=mitkAAAAIBAJ&sjid=snsNAAAAIBAJ&pg=7282,1234059|title=Johnson, a Baseball Legend|first=Bob|last=Mamini|date=December 12, 1946|accessdate=April 19, 2012|newspaper=[[The_Calgary_Herald|The Calgary Herald]]}}</ref>

傑克森被認為是1920年代最好的游擊手。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=0JAWAAAAIBAJ&sjid=vCEEAAAAIBAJ&pg=6224,326103&dq=travis-jackson&hl=en|title=The Best Shortstop|first=Grantland|last=Rice|authorlink=Grantland Rice|date=March 3, 1931|accessdate=April 19, 2012|page=2|newspaper=[[The_Milwaukee_Journal|The Milwaukee Journal]]}}</ref>1931年，他以.970的守備率傲視全聯盟。<ref name=nytobit/>不過他的傷病史也相當豐富，1925年他膝傷復發，<ref name=nytobit/>在1926年缺席不少場比賽。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/358489772.html?dids=358489772:358489772&FMT=CITE&FMTS=CITE:AI&type=historic&date=May+17%2C+1926&author=&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=GIANTS+LOSE+TWO+PLAYERS&pqatl=google |title=Giants Lose Two Players |newspaper=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]|page=B2|agency=Associated Press|date=May 17, 1926 |accessdate=April 16, 2012}} {{subscription required|date=April 2012}}</ref>1927年又因盲腸炎動手術。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=7ccVAAAAIBAJ&sjid=cg8EAAAAIBAJ&pg=4114,166528&dq=travis-jackson&hl=en|title=Travis Jackson Under Knife; Lost to Giants|agency=Associated Press|date=April 2, 1927|accessdate=April 16, 2012|page=11|newspaper=The Milwaukee Sentinel}}</ref>1930年因耳下腺炎缺席數場比賽。<ref>{{cite news|last=Drebinger |first=John |url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F50D15FE3E5C157A93C2AB178ED85F448385F9 |title=Crippled Giants Kept Idle By Rain — Prevented From Meeting the Braves, Who Lead, 3 Games to 1, in Series Thus Far. 傑克森在1934年再度成為先發游擊手。他跑回101分，並入選該年明星賽。<ref>{{cite news|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|url=https://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F60E14F93958177A93C6A9178CD85F408385F9 傑克森被認為是防守型游擊手的始祖之一。不過他有6個球季單季打擊率超過3成，<ref name=nytobit/>其中1930年他打擊率3成39為生涯最高。在他的時期，巨人拿下四次國聯冠軍，並贏得一次世界大賽。傑克森生涯共擊出135支全壘打，2成91打擊率。<ref name=nytobit/>
===執教生涯===
傑克森在1936年向澤西市巨人(紐約巨人2A)簽了三年約，傑克森擔任球員兼教練。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=3L0tAAAAIBAJ&sjid=oZgFAAAAIBAJ&pg=6719,3699591&dq=travis-jackson+jersey-city&hl=en|title=Giants Set to Buy Albany Franchise: New York Club Says Agreement Reached, But Owner Cambria Differs. Will Shift Franchise: Move to Jersey City With Travis Jackson as Manager Planned if Giants Acquire I.L. Club|agency=Associated Press|date=December 30, 1936|page=15|newspaper=[[The_Montreal_Gazette|The Montreal Gazette]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/chicagotribune/access/440489022.html?dids=440489022:440489022&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:AI&type=historic&date=Jan+06%2C+1937&author=&pub=Chicago+Tribune&desc=Travis+Jackson+Signs+to+Manage+Jersey+City+Club&pqatl=google |title=Travis Jackson Signs to Manage Jersey City Club |newspaper=[[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]] |page=27|date=January 6, 1937 |accessdate=April 16, 2012}} {{subscription required|date=April 2012}}</ref>他的膝傷依舊影響他的上場時間，而他持續擔任教練直到1938年。<ref name=sabr/>之後巨人讓傑克森以教練身分重回大聯盟，並擔任教練18個月。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=0pEjAAAAIBAJ&sjid=dqgFAAAAIBAJ&pg=2103,1827336&dq=travis-jackson&hl=en|title=Travis Jackson Back With Giants As Coach|agency=Associated Press|date=July 16, 1938|page=12|accessdate=April 16, 2012|newspaper=The Montreal Gazette}}</ref><ref name=coaches>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/sf/history/coaches.jsp |title=Giants All-Time Coaches|work=[[MLB.com|MLB.com]]|publisher=[[Major_League_Baseball_Advanced_Media|Major League Baseball Advanced Media]]|accessdate=April 16, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=Q3gtAAAAIBAJ&sjid=mZgFAAAAIBAJ&pg=6168,1777290&dq=travis-jackson&hl=en|title=Hartnet to Giants; Jackson is Retired|agency=Associated Press|date=December 11, 1940|accessdate=April 16, 2012|page=17|newspaper=The Montreal Gazette}}</ref>

接下來的五年，傑克森都在跟肺結核搏鬥。<ref name=nytobit/>最後他在1946年執教波士頓勇士的小聯盟球隊。1947-1948年，傑克森再回到巨人當教練。<ref name=coaches/><ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=x7hRAAAAIBAJ&sjid=-2kDAAAAIBAJ&pg=3557,4312349&dq=travis-jackson&hl=en |title=Travis Jackson to Coach Giants|agency=Associated Press|date=October 15, 1946|accessdate=April 16, 2012|page=14|newspaper=[[Pittsburgh_Post-Gazette|Pittsburgh Post-Gazette]]}}</ref>1948年起，傑克森在多個小聯盟球隊執教直到1960年。

==個人生活==
傑克森和他妻子瑪麗生了兩個小孩，桃樂絲·芬雀和威廉·崔維斯，6個孫子和4個曾孫。<ref name=nytobit/>1987年，傑克森死於阿茲海默症。<ref name=nytobit>{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1987/07/29/obituaries/travis-jackson-a-shortstop-who-made-the-hall-of-fame.html|first=Robert|last=McG.

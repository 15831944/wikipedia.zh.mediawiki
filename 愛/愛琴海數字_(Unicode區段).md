'''爱琴海数字'''是一个位于第一輔助平面的[[Unicode区段|Unicode区段]]，收录了用于[[线形文字A|线形文字A]]，[[线形文字B|线形文字B]]和[[塞浦路斯音節文字|塞浦路斯音節文字]]的数字。<ref>{{cite book | title = The Unicode Standard, Version 6.2 | publisher = Unicode, Inc | date = September 2012 | location = Mountain View, CA | pages = 473 | url = http://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode6.2.0/ch14.pdf | isbn = 978-1-936213-07-8 | chapter = Chapter 14: Additional Ancient and Historic Scripts}}</ref>

'''愛麗絲·華克'''（{{lang-en|'''Alice Walker'''}}，{{bd|1944年|2月9日|catIdx=W}}），美國作家和社会活动家，曾獲[[普立茲小說獎|普立茲小說獎]]、[[美國國家圖書獎|美國國家圖書獎]]<ref name=nba1983>[http://www.nationalbook.org/nba1983.html "National Book Awards - 1983"]. National Book Foundation. Retrieved 2012-03-15. <br>(With essays by Anna Clark and Tarayi Jones from the Awards 60-year anniversary blog.)</ref><ref>From 1980 to 1983 there were dual hardcover and paperback awards of the [[National_Book_Award_for_Fiction|National Book Award for Fiction]]. Walker won the award for hardcover fiction.</ref><ref name=pulitzer>[http://www.pulitzer.org/bycat/Fiction "Fiction"]. ''Past winners and finalists by category''. The Pulitzer Prizes. Retrieved 2012-03-17.</ref>，代表作為《[[紫色姐妹花|紫色姐妹花]]》，[[史蒂芬·史匹柏|史蒂芬·史匹柏]]曾將其改編為[[紫色姐妹花_(電影)|同名電影]]。

==早期生活==
愛麗絲·華克出生於[[喬治亞州|喬治亞州]]的一個小鎮<ref>{{cite book|authors=Logue, Victoria and Logue, Frank|year=1997|title=Touring the Backroads of North and South Georgia|location=Winston-Salem NC|publisher=John F. Blair|page=165|isbn=978-0-89587-171-8|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=H6asKSPCcQMC&dq=Touring+the+Backroads+of+North+and+South+Georgia&source=gbs_navlinks_s}}</ref>，有兄弟姊妹八人，她最小。母親為幫傭<ref name="Apr 2009">''World Authors 1995-2000'', 2003. Biography Reference Bank database. Retrieved 2009-04-10.</ref>，從小便必須打理家務<ref>{{cite web |author=Walker, Alice |work=[[The_Tavis_Smiley_Show|The Tavis Smiley Show]] |date=2010-05-06 |url=http://www.pbs.org/kcet/tavissmiley/archive/201005/20100506_walker.html |title=Alice Walker |publisher=The Smiley Group}}</ref>。八歲時因哥哥不慎導致右眼失明<ref>{{cite web |last=Walker |first=Alice |url= http://enloehs.wcpss.net/resources/kingsberry/propaganda.pdf |title= Beauty: When the Other Dancer is the Self |publisher=Kingsberry |year=2006 |accessdate=2012-08-29}}</ref>。曾任教於[[加州大學|加州大學]]，致力介紹非洲作家，並長期關注民權、婦女運動。

==个人生活==
1965年，沃克与[[犹太|犹太]]民权律师Melvyn Rosenman Leventhal相遇。他们1967年3月17日在纽约市结婚。那年晚些时候夫妇搬迁到密西西比州[[杰克逊_(密西西比州)|杰克逊]]，成为“密西西比第一对合法结婚的跨种族夫妇”。<ref name="entertainment.timesonline.co.uk">{{cite news|url=http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/books/article3866798.ece |title=The day feminist icon Alice Walker resigned as my mother |newspaper=The Times |location=London |date=2008-05-04 |author=Driscoll, Margarette |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080511201732/http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/books/article3866798.ece |archivedate=五月 11, 2008 }}</ref><ref name="democracy">{{cite news|title=Inner Light in a Time of Darkness: A Conversation with Author and Poet Alice Walker |work=[[Democracy_Now!|Democracy Now!]] |date=2006-11-17 |url=http://www.democracynow.org/article.pl?sid=06/11/17/1454228 |accessdate=2007-06-14 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070613182120/http://www.democracynow.org/article.pl?sid=06%2F11%2F17%2F1454228 |archivedate=2007-06-13 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>他们受到白人的骚扰和威胁，包括[[三K党|三K党]]。他们夫妇1969年生了一个女儿Rebecca。沃克和她的丈夫于1976年离婚。<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.guardian.co.uk/lifeandstyle/2007/may/26/familyandrelationships.family2 |title=Can I survive having a baby? Will I lose myself ... ? |newspaper=The Guardian |date=2007-05-26 |location =London |author=Krum, Sharon}}</ref>

90年代中期，沃克与女创作歌手[[特蕾西·查普曼|特蕾西·查普曼]]开始了一段恋情。<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2006/dec/15/gender.world |newspaper=The Guardian |date= 2006-12-15 |title= No Retreat |accessdate= 2013-05-26 |location =London |author=Wajid, Sara}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| title =No retreat| work =The Guardian| url =http://www.theguardian.com/books/2006/dec/15/gender.world}}</ref>

'''愛普克·松德蘭德'''（{{lang-nl|Epke Zonderland}}；{{bd|1986年|4月16日}}）是一位[[荷蘭|荷蘭]]體操運動員。他代表荷蘭參加了2012年倫敦奧運會，並且在[[2012年夏季奧林匹克運動會體操男子單槓比賽|單槓比賽]]上獲得金牌。<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.guardian.co.uk/sport/2012/aug/07/london-2012-gymnastic-gold-epke-zonderland |title= London 2012: Gymnastic gold for true flying Dutchman Epke Zonderland |author= Emma John |publisher=Guardian |date=7 August 2012 |accessdate=9 August 2012 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/olympics/19171008 |title=Olympic gymnastics: Zonderland takes men's bar gold |date=3 October 2013 |publisher=BBC }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://edition.cnn.com/2013/02/06/sport/gymnastics-olympics-zonderland-flying-dutchman/ |title=The most incredible sporting gamble? |author= Paul Gittings and Brooke Bowman |work=CNN |date=6 February 2013 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://edition.cnn.com/2012/08/12/sport/olympic-top-ten-sport-memories-2012/index.html |title=London 2012's top-10 sporting moments |author= Ben Wyatt |work= CNN |date=31 January 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2012/08/14/sports/olympics/14iht-srolathlete14.html?_r=0 |title=The Good, the Bad and the Unforgettable |work=The New York Times |author=Christopher Clarey |date=13 August 2012 }}</ref>他是第一位赢得体操个人项目奥运奖牌的荷兰运动员<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/olympics/18903127 |title=Olympics: Epke Zonderland claims high bar gold for Netherlands |publisher=BBC |date=7 August 2012}}</ref>。他也在2013年世界體操錦標賽和2011年及2014年歐洲體操錦標賽上獲得單槓項目的金牌。他在[[格羅寧根大學|格羅寧根大學]]主修醫學。

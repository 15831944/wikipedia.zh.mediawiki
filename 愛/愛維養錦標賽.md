'''愛維養錦標賽'''是一個於[[法國|法國]]舉行的女子職業高爾夫賽事，每年7月在[[埃維昂萊班|埃維昂萊班]][[愛維養度假高爾夫俱樂部|愛維養度假高爾夫俱樂部]]舉行。

2011年7月它宣布從2013年開始，'''愛維養名人賽'''被改名為'''愛維養錦標賽'''和成為[[LPGA巡迴賽|LPGA巡迴賽]]的第五個大滿貫賽事，賽事移至9月舉辦。<ref name="fifth-major">{{cite press release |url=http://www.lpga.com/content_1.aspx?pid=29220&mid=1 |title=LPGA Adds The Evian as a Major Championship in 2013 |publisher=LPGA |date=2011-07-20 |accessdate=2011-07-24}}</ref><ref name="LPGA major">{{cite news |url=http://espn.go.com/golf/story/_/id/6786719/evian-masters-now-fifth-major-lpga-tour-2013 |title=Evian Masters to be 5th major in 2013 |agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]] |work=[[ESPN|ESPN]] |date=20 July 2011 |accessdate=21 July 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.lpga.com/golf/tournaments/lpga/the-evian-championship.aspx |title=The Evian Championship |publisher=LPGA |accessdate=15 January 2013}}</ref>

'''愛爾蘭衛隊'''（{{lang-en|'''Irish Guards'''}}，簡稱'''IG'''）是英國的[[衛兵師|衛兵師]]之一，也是[[英國陸軍|英國陸軍]]的一部分，和皇家愛爾蘭軍團並為英國陸軍現在僅有的兩個愛爾蘭步兵團之一<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.irishtimes.com/newspaper/weekend/2010/0731/1224275874706.html|title=The fighting Irish|date=31 July 2010|work=The Irish Times|accessdate=26 December 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.independent.ie/opinion/columnists/kevin-myers/kevin-myers-however-we-view-war-lets-wish-our-lads-a-safe-return-2368618.html|title=Kevin Myers: However we view war, let's wish our lads a safe return|work=Independent.ie|accessdate=26 December 2015}}</ref>。愛爾蘭衛隊主要在[[北愛爾蘭|北愛爾蘭]]和英國的愛爾蘭人社區招募隊員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.armyjobs.mod.uk/westmidlands/rolesandregiments/regiments/pages/default.aspx|title=Joining the Army|publisher=British Army|accessdate=26 December 2015}}</ref>。雖然[[愛爾蘭共和國|愛爾蘭共和國]]法律規定愛爾蘭公民參加其他國家軍隊違法<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1954/en/act/pub/0018/sec0312.html#zza18y1954s312|title=Defence Act, 1954|publisher=|accessdate=26 December 2015}}</ref>，但現在愛爾蘭衛隊有四人來自愛爾蘭共和國<ref>{{cite news | title=Lure of combat draws Irish men and women to British army | date= 6 September 2008 | publisher=''The Irish Times'' | url=http://www.irishtimes.com/newspaper/ireland/2008/0906/1220629535638.html?via=mr |quote=Subscription required to view}}</ref>。

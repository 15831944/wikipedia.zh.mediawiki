'''愛氏海葵科（Edwardsiidae）'''是[[海葵目|海葵目]]下的一[[科|科]]<ref name="WoRMS">{{Cite WoRMS |author=WoRMS |year=2018 |title=''Edwardsiidae'' |id=100665 |accessdate=August 29, 2018}}</ref>，其下物種有細長的身體，平常會埋在沙子、洞穴或石頭的縫隙之中<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Rodríguez|first1=Estefanía|last2=Barbeitos|first2=Marcos S.|last3=Brugler|first3=Mercer R.|last4=Crowley|first4=Louise M.|last5=Grajales|first5=Alejandro|last6=Gusmão|first6=Luciana|last7=Häussermann|first7=Verena|last8=Reft|first8=Abigail|last9=Daly|first9=Marymegan|last10=Colgan|first10=Donald James |title=Hidden among sea anemones: The first comprehensive phylogenetic reconstruction of the order Actiniaria (Cnidaria, Anthozoa, Hexacorallia) reveals a novel group of hexacorals |journal=PLoS ONE |date=2014 |volume=9 |issue=5 |page=e96998 |doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0096998}}</ref>。

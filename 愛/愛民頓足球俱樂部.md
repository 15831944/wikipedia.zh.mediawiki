'''愛民頓足球俱樂部'''（{{lang-en|'''FC Edmonton'''}}）是一家位於[[加拿大|加拿大]][[亞伯達省|亞伯達省]][[愛民頓|愛民頓]]的[[足球|足球]]俱樂部，最早成立於2010年，曾為[[北美足球聯賽|北美足球聯賽]]成員。2017年末曾一度中止職業球隊運作<ref name="FCEdmonton_fin">{{Cite web |language=en |title=FC Edmonton withdraws from North American Soccer League |url=http://www.fcedmonton.com/news/2017/11/24/club-statement--fc-edmonton-withdraws-from-north-american-soccer-league |date=2017-11-24 |website=FCEdmonton |accessdate=2017-11-14 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171126233048/http://www.fcedmonton.com/news/2017/11/24/club-statement--fc-edmonton-withdraws-from-north-american-soccer-league |archive-date=2017-11-26 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>，2018年轉投[[加拿大超級足球聯賽|加拿大超級足球聯賽]]<ref>{{Cite web |language=en |title=FC Edmonton returns, this time as a member of the Canadian Premier League |url=https://www.guelphtoday.com/national-sports/fc-edmonton-returns-this-time-as-a-member-of-the-canadian-premier-league-949625 |date=2018-06-08 |website=Guelph Today|accessdate=2018-06-08}}.</ref>。

《'''愛的奧秘'''》（英语：''Mystery of Love''）是在電影《[[请以你的名字呼唤我_(电影)|以你的名字呼喚我]]》（{{lang|en|''Call Me by Your Name''}}）中演唱的歌曲，被收錄於[[以你的名字呼喚我：電影原聲帶|電影原聲帶]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/2017/11/call-me-by-your-name-soundtrack-sufjan-stevens-1201894358/|title=‘Call Me by Your Name’ Soundtrack: Sufjan Stevens, the Psychedelic Furs, and More Lend Their Musical Stylings — Listen|work=IndieWire|date=November 3, 2017|accessdate=November 3, 2017|first=Michael |last=Nordine}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vulture.com/2017/11/call-me-by-your-names-sufjan-stevens-songs-are-here.html|title=If You’ve Got 10 Minutes to Privately Ugly Cry, Call Me by Your Name’s Sufjan Stevens Songs Are Now Available|work=Vulture|date=November 3, 2017|accessdate=November 3, 2017|first=Hunter|last=Harris}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B075TYM28J|title='Call Me By Your Name' (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack)|publisher=[[Amazon_(company)|Amazon]]||date=|accessdate=October 20, 2017|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171103125615/https://www.amazon.com/Call-Original-Motion-Picture-Soundtrack/dp/B075TYM28J|archivedate=November 3, 2017|deadurl=no}}</ref>。並於2017年12月1日發行單曲。其[[音樂錄影帶|音樂錄影帶]]於2018年1月4日發行，在[[拿坡里國立考古博物館|拿坡里國立考古博物館]]拍攝完成<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/watch-sufjan-stevens-new-video-for-call-me-by-your-name-song-mystery-of-love/|title=Watch Sufjan Stevens’ New Video for Call Me By Your Name Song 'Mystery of Love'|first=Michelle|last=Kim|date=January 4, 2018|accessdate=January 4, 2018|work=[[Pitchfork_Media|Pitchfork]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.spin.com/2018/01/sufjan-stevens-mystery-of-love-video/|title=Video: Sufjan Stevens – 'Mystery of Love'|first=Arielle|last=Gordon|date=January 4, 2018|accessdate=January 4, 2018|work=Spin}}</ref>。

該歌曲被[[喬治亞影評人協會|喬治亞影評人協會]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.georgiafilmcritics.org/p/2017-awards.html|title=2017 Awards|publisher=[[Georgia_Film_Critics_Association|Georgia Film Critics Association]]|date=January 12, 2018|accessdate=January 8, 2018|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20180113112325/http://www.georgiafilmcritics.org/p/2017-awards.html|archivedate=2018年1月13日}}</ref>和[[奧斯卡金像獎|奧斯卡金像獎]]提名為最佳原創歌曲獎<ref>{{cite news|last1=Coggan|first1=Devan|title=Oscar nominations 2018: See the full list|url=http://ew.com/awards/2018/01/23/oscars-2018-nominations/|accessdate=January 23, 2018|work=[[Entertainment_Weekly|Entertainment Weekly]]|date=January 23, 2018}}</ref>。

== 概念與發展 ==
{{Quote box|| quote =「我想從音樂的角度來爲作品作出貢獻，這樣能夠讓電影在超越80年代語言學方面和古典音樂的評論性音樂有個準確身份。而這就是爲什麽我要尋求一種聲音來注入電影的聲音－由演員所飾演的角色呈現－而我正正想到蘇揚·史蒂文斯。」|width=25%|source=－盧卡·格達戈尼諾在討論他與蘇揚·史蒂文斯在《[[以你的名字呼喚我|以你的名字呼喚我]]》的合作<ref name="Billboard magazine interview" />。|style=padding:8px;}}

《愛的奧秘》由{{le|蘇揚·史蒂文斯|Sufjan Stevens}}創作<ref name="Windy City Times review">{{cite news|title='Call Me By Your Name,' Dee Rees earn Oscar nods|url=http://www.windycitymediagroup.com/lgbt/Call-Me-By-Your-Name-Dee-Rees-earn-Oscar-nods/61669.html|accessdate=2018-01-27|work=Windy City Times|date=2018-01-23|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180124200448/http://www.windycitymediagroup.com/lgbt/Call-Me-By-Your-Name-Dee-Rees-earn-Oscar-nods/61669.html|archivedate=2018-01-24|df=}}</ref>。在一場訪問中，電影導演和製作人[[盧卡·格達戈尼諾|盧卡·格達戈尼諾]]說到他雖然通常會爲自己的電影選擇音樂，但他想透過音樂來找到一個「致電影的情感旁白」<ref name="mongrel">{{cite press release|url=http://www.mongrelmedia.com/MongrelMedia/files/9a/9a539f31-154b-4d83-a3ce-e421191266fb.pdf|title=Mongrel Presents: Call Me by Your Name|publisher=Mongrel Media|date=|accessdate=2017-10-17|location=Toronto, Ontario|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171028132924/http://www.mongrelmedia.com/MongrelMedia/files/9a/9a539f31-154b-4d83-a3ce-e421191266fb.pdf|archivedate=2017-10-28|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。他還希望電影的音樂與艾里歐產生聯繫，艾里歐是一位年輕的鋼琴家，喜歡抄錄及改編鋼琴譜以加深與奧利佛之間的關係<ref name="Screenprism">{{cite web|url=http://screenprism.com/insights/article/luca-guadagnino-talks-call-me-by-your-name-at-the-55th-new-york-film-festiv|title=Luca Guadagnino Discusses 'Call Me By Your Name' at the 55th New York Film Festival|work=Screenprism|date=2017-10-11|accessdate=2018-01-27|first1=Meghan|last1=Gilligan|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180203015503/http://screenprism.com/insights/article/luca-guadagnino-talks-call-me-by-your-name-at-the-55th-new-york-film-festiv|archivedate=2018-02-03|df=}}</ref>。由於史蒂文斯是一位「保守的藝人」，因此格達戈尼諾稱說服他的過程爲挑戰，他最終說服史蒂文斯爲電影創作音樂，並提供劇本和原著小說[[安德列·艾席蒙|安德列·艾席蒙]]的《[[以你的名字呼喚我_(小說)|以你的名字呼喚我]]》作爲靈感來源<ref name="The Film Stage interview">{{cite web|last1=Encinias|first1=Joshua|title='Call Me by Your Name' Team on Romance, Sufjan Stevens, Maurice Pialat, and Sequel Potential|url=https://thefilmstage.com/features/call-me-by-your-name-team-on-romance-sufjan-stevens-maurice-pialat-and-sequel-potential/|website=The Film Stage|accessdate=2018-01-27|date=2017-10-11|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180119004051/https://thefilmstage.com/features/call-me-by-your-name-team-on-romance-sufjan-stevens-maurice-pialat-and-sequel-potential/|archivedate=2018-01-19|df=}}</ref>。史蒂文斯的靈感還來自與格達戈尼諾關於角色的對話<ref name="The Film Stage interview" />。史蒂文斯在詞曲創作時獲得完全控制，他爲此表示感謝。他還感謝格達戈尼諾願意去相信其他人的創作機會<ref name="Entertainment Weekly music interview">{{cite news|last1=Bacle|first1=Ariana|title=Sufjan Stevens blends romance, heartbreak in gorgeous ''Call Me by Your Name'' songs|url=http://ew.com/movies/2017/12/06/call-me-by-your-name-sufjan-stevens-soundtrack/|accessdate=2018-01-28|work=[[娱乐周刊|Entertainment Weekly]]|date=2017-12-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180130091528/http://ew.com/movies/2017/12/06/call-me-by-your-name-sufjan-stevens-soundtrack/|archivedate=2018-01-30|df=}}</ref>。雖然他只被要求爲《以你的名字呼喚我》起筆一首歌曲，史蒂文斯提供給格達戈尼諾兩首歌曲（《愛的奧秘》和《金色幻象》），而兩首均在電影中使用<ref name="Billboard magazine interview">{{cite news|last1=Caulfield|first1=Keith|title=How Sufjan Stevens' Music Became a 'Sort of Narrator' for 'Call Me by Your Name'|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/8093818/sufjan-stevens-call-me-by-your-name-music-luca-guadagnino-interview|accessdate=2018-01-27|work=[[告示牌_(雜誌)|Billboard]]|date=2018-01-10|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180111070821/https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/8093818/sufjan-stevens-call-me-by-your-name-music-luca-guadagnino-interview|archivedate=2018-01-11|df=}}</ref>。

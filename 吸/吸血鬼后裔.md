《'''吸血鬼后裔'''》（{{lang-en|''Legacies''}}）是一部由{{link-en|朱莉·普莱克|Julie Plec}}開創，於2018年10月25日在[[CW電視網|The CW]]開播的[[美国|美国]]电视剧。<ref name="TVSF">{{cite web |last1=Pena |first1=Jessica |title=Legacies: CW Orders Spinoff of The Originals for Fall 2018 |url=https://tvseriesfinale.com/tv-show/legacies-cw-orders-spinoff-of-the-originals-for-fall-2018/ |website=TV Series Finale |accessdate=July 11, 2018 |date=May 17, 2018}}</ref>

2019年1月31日，本劇獲CW第2季續訂。<ref name="S2">{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/cw-renews-flash-charmed-riverdale-supernatural-6-more-1181714|title=CW Renews 'The Flash,' 'Charmed,' 'Riverdale,' 'Supernatural,' 6 More|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|work=The Hollywood Reporter|date=January 31, 2019|accessdate=January 31, 2019}}</ref>

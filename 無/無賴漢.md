《'''無賴漢'''》（{{lang-ko|무뢰한}}；{{lang-en|'''''The Shameless'''''}}）是2015年5月上映的一部韓國黑色浪漫[[犯罪電影|犯罪電影]]，由吳承旭執導，[[全道嬿|全道嬿]]和[[金南佶|金南佶]]主演。講述了刑警和他所追查殺人事件嫌疑犯的女人之間產生的無法逃避的愛情故事<ref>http://www.koreastardaily.com/tc/video/61934</ref>。本電影入選[[第68屆坎城影展|第68屆坎城影展]][[一種注目|一種注目]]單元開幕片。<ref>[http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/article/61306.html The 2015 Official Selection]</ref><ref>[http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/news/news.jsp?mode=VIEW&seq=3403 Korean Trio Invited to Cannes Official Selection]</ref><ref>[http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20150417000358 Three Korean films invited to Cannes]</ref><ref>[http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2015/04/17/2015041701559.html Korean Films Invited to Cannes]</ref><ref>[http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/shameless-mu-roe-han-cannes-795731 ''The Shameless'' (''Mu-roe-han''): Cannes Review]</ref><ref>[http://www.screendaily.com/reviews/the-shameless-review/5087457.article ''The Shameless'': Review]</ref><ref>[http://variety.com/2015/film/festivals/the-shameless-review-cannes-1201498338/ Cannes Film Review: ''The Shameless'']</ref><ref>[http://www.filmbiz.asia/reviews/the-shameless The Shameless] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150519085050/http://www.filmbiz.asia/reviews/the-shameless |date=2015-05-19 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/news/news.jsp?mode=VIEW&seq=3434 THE SHAMELESS Bows to Positive Reviews in Cannes]</ref>

'''無氣門小目'''（{{lang|la|'''Astigmata'''}}），
是[[蛛形綱|蛛形綱]][[蜱蟎亞綱|蜱蟎亞綱]][[蟎形總目|蟎形總目]][[恙蟎目|恙蟎目]][[甲蟎亞目|甲蟎亞目]][[Desmonomata|Desmonomata]]下目之下的三個小目之一<ref name="Zootaxa_Oribatida">{{cite book |chapter=Suborder '''Oribatida''' <small>van der Hammen, 1968</small> |chapterurl=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2011/f/zt03148p148.pdf |format=[[PDF|PDF]] |authors=HEINRICH SCHATZ, VALERIE M. BEHAN-PELLETIER, BARRY M. OCONNOR & ROY A. NORTON |url=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/list/2011/3148.html |title=Animal biodiversity: An outline of higher-level classification and survey of taxonomic richness |editor=Zhang, Z.-Q. |journal=[[Zootaxa|Zootaxa]] |issue=3148 |year=2011 |pages=141-148 |publisher=[[Magnolia_Press|Magnolia Press]] |isbn=978-1-86977-850-7 |issn=1175-5334 |language=en }}</ref>。
原來與甲蟎亞目同屬[[疥蟎亞目|疥蟎亞目]]（Sarcoptiformes）<ref name="qiao">{{cite journal |url=http://max.book118.com/html/2014/0712/9039470.shtm |title=我国小赫甲螨科系统分类研究.pdf |pages=105页 |language=zh-hans |author=喬文娟 |year=2011 |accessdate=2016-01-06 |journal=內蒙古師範大學碩士學位論文 |publisher=[[內蒙古師範大學|內蒙古師範大學]] }}</ref>之下。

本分類的物種大多數都是[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]身上的[[寄生蟲|寄生蟲]]。
Most notorious among these are the [[Psoroptidia|Psoroptidia]], which contain such notable [[taxa|taxa]] as [[feather_mite|feather mite]]s and ''[[Sarcoptes_scabiei|Sarcoptes scabiei]]''.

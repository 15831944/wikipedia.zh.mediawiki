{{cite web |url=http://mytv.tvb.com/tc/ch_gotv/theadventuresofthewomanreporter/162009 |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-12-07 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160216083624/http://mytv.tvb.com/tc/ch_gotv/theadventuresofthewomanreporter/162009 |archivedate=2016-02-16 }}</ref>。

'''“無畏”巡航導彈'''（{{lang-en|Nirbhay}}，{{lang-sa|Dauntless/Fearless}}）是[[印度|印度]][[國防研究及發展組織|國防研究及發展組織]]设计和研发的一种远程全天候[[亞音速|亚音速]][[巡航导弹|巡航导弹]]。该导弹可以从多种平台发射，并能够携带常规弹头与核弹头。该巡航导弹目前处在开发阶段，并正在进行飞行试验。<ref name="et1">https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/defence/india-test-fires-subsonic-cruise-missile-nirbhay/articleshow/61543937.cms</ref><ref name="The_hindu">{{cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/article2968219.ece|title=Nirbhay likely to be test-fired in April|last=T. S. Subramanian|date=March 2012|newspaper=The Hindu|accessdate=10 March 2012|location=Chennai, India}}</ref><ref name="toinov">[https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/india-successfully-tests-its-first-nuclear-capable-cruise-missile/articleshow/61550465.cms "India successfully tests its first nuclear-capable cruise missile."], [[The_Times_of_India|The Times of India]], 8 November 2017.</ref><ref name="hindd">{{cite web|url=http://www.hindu.com/2008/05/09/stories/2008050955301300.htm|title=DRDO developing hypersonic missile|accessdate=8 October 2015|work=The Hindu}}</ref>

== 设计 ==
“無畏”巡航導彈由先进系统实验室（ASL）开发的[[固体火箭|固体火箭]]助推器提供动力。在达到所需的速度和高度后，导弹的[[涡轮风扇发动机|涡轮风扇发动机]]接替进行后续推进。该型导弹由Research Center Imarat（RCI）开发的[[惯性导航系统|惯性导航系统]]和一个[[无线电高度表|无线电高度表]]引导。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://aviationweek.com/defense/india-fly-subsonic-cruise-missile|title=India To Fly Subsonic Cruise Missile|date=25 January 2013|publisher=AWIN First|last1=Menon|first1=Jay|website=Aviation Week}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thehindubusinessline.com/news/india-test-fires-nirbhay-missile/article9946323.ece|title=Long-range cruise missile ‘Nirbhay’ test-fired|date=7 November 2017|work=The Hindu Business Line|agency=PTI}}</ref>它配有基于[[环式激光陀螺仪|环式激光陀螺仪]]（RLG）的制导、控制和导航系统，并还具有基于惯性导航系统（附带[[GPS|GPS]]系统）的[[微机电系统|微机电系统]]。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://pib.nic.in/newsite/erelease.aspx?relid=173291|title=Press Information Bureau|access-date=2017-11-08}}</ref>该导弹长6米，宽0.52米，翼展2.7米，重约1500公斤。其射程约为1000公里，可根据任务要求发射重量在200-300公斤之间的24种不同类型的弹头。<ref name="et1">https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/defence/india-test-fires-subsonic-cruise-missile-nirbhay/articleshow/61543937.cms</ref><ref name="toinov">[https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/india-successfully-tests-its-first-nuclear-capable-cruise-missile/articleshow/61550465.cms "India successfully tests its first nuclear-capable cruise missile."], [[The_Times_of_India|The Times of India]], 8 November 2017.</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://indianexpress.com/article/india/india-news-india/nuke-capable-nirbhay-cruise-missile-fails-again-flight-aborted/|title=Nuke-capable Nirbhay cruise missile fails again, flight aborted|accessdate=2015-10-22|date=2015-10-17|website=The Indian Express}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dnaindia.com/report.asp?NewsID=1111269|title=Nirbhay to beef up missile muscle|date=21 July 2007|work=DNA India}}</ref>

该型导弹声称具有巡飞（loitering）能力，即可以绕过目标执行几次机动后再重新瞄准目标。它也可以在多个目标中挑选一个目标进行攻击。<ref name="The_hindu">{{cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/article2968219.ece|title=Nirbhay likely to be test-fired in April|last=T. S. Subramanian|date=March 2012|newspaper=The Hindu|accessdate=10 March 2012|location=Chennai, India}}</ref>它配有两个侧翼，能够在距地面100米到4公里的不同高度飞行，也可在低海拔（如树梢高度）飞行以避免敌方雷达的探测。<ref name="toinov">[https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/india-successfully-tests-its-first-nuclear-capable-cruise-missile/articleshow/61550465.cms "India successfully tests its first nuclear-capable cruise missile."], [[The_Times_of_India|The Times of India]], 8 November 2017.</ref><ref name="toi25j">{{cite news|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2013-01-25/india/36547436_1_cruise-missile-brahmos-km-range|title=India to test subsonic cruise missile in February|date=25 January 2013|newspaper=The Times of India|accessdate=25 January 2013}}</ref><ref name="hindd">{{cite web|url=http://www.hindu.com/2008/05/09/stories/2008050955301300.htm|title=DRDO developing hypersonic missile|accessdate=8 October 2015|work=The Hindu}}</ref>该导弹最终将供印度武装部队作为[[布拉莫斯导弹|Brahmos]]的继任者，提供超过布拉莫斯导弹450公里的射程。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/india-news/brahmos-cruise-missile-range-to-increase-to-450km-drdo-chief/story-iJi2AKJBlEblDEyoxnLyKO.html|title=From 290 km to 450 km: India to soon test extended range BrahMos missile|accessdate=7 November 2017|publisher=}}</ref>

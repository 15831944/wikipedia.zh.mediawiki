'''硬孔菌屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Rigidoporus''）是[[多孔菌目|多孔菌目]][[亞灰樹花菌科|亞灰樹花菌科]]的一個[[屬_(生物)|屬]]，最早於1905年由美國真菌學家{{link-en|威廉·阿方索·默里爾|William Alphonso Murrill}}命名<ref name="Murrill 1905">{{cite journal |author=Murrill, W.A. |title=The Polyporaceae of North America: XII. A synopsis of the white and bright-colored pileate species |journal=Bulletin of the Torrey Botanical Club |year=1905 |volume=32 |issue=9 |pages=469–493 |jstor=2478463 |url=https://archive.org/stream/jstor-2478463/2478463#page/n1/mode/2up |doi=10.2307/2478463}}</ref>，詞源由來為拉丁文的''{{lang|la|rigidus}}''（意指堅硬）與希臘文的{{lang|el|πόρος}}（意指孔洞）<ref name="Donk 1960">{{cite journal |author=Donk, M.A. |year=1960 |title=The generic names proposed for Polyporaceae |journal=Persoonia |volume=1 |issue=2 |pages=173–302 |url=http://www.cybertruffle.org.uk/cyberliber/61056/0001/002/0173.htm}}</ref>。硬孔菌屬中的許多物種都是[[植物病理學|植物病原菌]]。本屬分布廣泛，包括約有40個物種<ref name=Kirk2008>{{cite book|author=Kirk PM, Cannon PF, Minter DW, Stalpers JA|title=Dictionary of the Fungi (10th ed.)|publisher=Wallingford, UK|page=603|ISBN=9780851998268|language=en}}</ref>。

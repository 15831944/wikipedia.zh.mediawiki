'''硬件限制'''，有时被称为'''硬件DRM'''<ref>http://www.hpl.hp.com/techreports//2003/HPL-2003-110.pdf HP Laboratories</ref>，是由[[電子元件|電子元件]]强制执行的[[复制保护|内容保护]]。硬件限制可以用作完善[[软件|软件]]的[[数字版权管理|数字版权管理]]。 应用硬件限制的例子包括[[电子游戏机|电子游戏机]]、[[智能手机|智能手机]]<ref>[https://www.nytimes.com/2007/01/14/business/yourmoney/14digi.html Want an iPhone? Beware the iHandcuffs - New York Times]</ref>、[[平板電腦|平板電腦]]、[[麥金塔|Mac]]<ref>[https://arstechnica.com/apple/news/2008/11/apple-brings-hdcp-to-a-new-aluminum-macbook-near-you.html Apple brings HDCP to a new aluminum MacBook - Ars Technica]</ref>和使用[[統一可延伸韌體介面#Secure_Boot|安全启动]]的[[个人电脑|个人电脑]]等。

==应用==

===可升级处理器===
某些[[英特尔|英特尔]]处理器有“锁定”的性能，可以付费“解锁”<ref>[https://www.engadget.com/2010/09/18/intel-wants-to-charge-50-to-unlock-stuff-your-cpu-can-already-d/ Intel wants to charge $50 to unlock stuff your CPU can already do - Engadget]</ref><ref>[http://boingboing.net/2010/09/19/intel-drm-a-crippled.html Intel + DRM: a crippled processor that you have to pay extra to unlock - Boing Boing]</ref>。

[[IBM|IBM]]的[[System/370大型计算机|System/370大型计算机]]的某些型号包含附加的硬件，如果客户支付了额外的费用，IBM会派出客服人员来启用，通常是断开一个电阻器。

===Intel Insider===
[[Intel_Insider|Intel Insider]]是一种为数字内容提供[[路径保护|路径保护]]的技术<ref>[http://www.computerworld.com/s/article/9203799/Intel_Sandy_Bridge_s_Insider_is_not_DRM Intel: Sandy Bridge's Insider is not DRM - Computerworld]</ref>，也可以被认为是一种DRM<ref>[http://www.techdirt.com/articles/20110107/10153912573/intel-claims-drmd-chip-is-not-drm-its-just-copy-protection.shtml Intel Claims DRM'd Chip Is Not DRM, It's Just Copy Protection | Techdirt]</ref><ref>[https://www.pcmag.com/article2/0,2817,2375215,00.asp Is Intel Insider Code for DRM in Sandy Bridge? | PCMag.com]</ref><ref>[http://www.theinquirer.net/inquirer/news/1934536/intels-sandy-bridge-sucks-hollywood-drm Intel's Sandy Bridge sucks up to Hollywood with DRM - The Inquirer]</ref>。

===安全启动===
{{See also|統一可延伸韌體介面#Secure_Boot}}
某些设备实施了安全启动（英语：Secure Boot，又称Verified Boot或Trusted Boot），除非用户能够关闭此限制功能或签名认证第三方软件，设备将仅允许运行有[[數位簽章|数字签名]]、通常来自设备制造商的软件。

===安卓设备===
某些[[Android|Android]]设备预设锁死了[[啟動程式|引导程序]]。

'''切拉曼主麻清真寺'''（{{lang-en|Cheraman Juma Mosque}}）是[[印度|印度]][[喀拉拉邦|喀拉拉邦]][[特里蘇爾縣|特里蘇爾縣]]的一座[[清真寺|清真寺]]，位於[[科東格阿爾盧爾|科東格阿爾盧爾]]的南邊。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.deccanherald.com/content/174990/oldest-indian-mosque-sets-precedent.html|title=Oldest Indian mosque sets new precedent}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=1400-year-old mosque to be restored to its original form|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/kerala/1400yearold-mosque-to-be-restored-to-its-original-form/article2142818.ece}}</ref><ref name="Juma Mosque">Kerala Tourism - Official Website [http://www.keralatourism.org/muziris/cheraman-juma-masjid.php Cheraman Juma Mosque]</ref>傳說中該寺興建於公元629年，<ref>{{cite book |author=Ron Geaves |title=Islam and Britain: Muslim Mission in an Age of Empire |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=H1s1DwAAQBAJ&pg=PA6 |year=2017 |publisher=Bloomsbury |isbn=978-1-4742-7175-2 |pages=6 }}</ref>為印度最古老的清真寺。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/hyderabad/Mosque-in-Kerala-dates-back-to-the-Prophets-time/articleshow/48138989.cms|title=Mosque in Kerala dates back to the Prophet's time}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/kerala/tinkering-with-the-past/article7442583.ece|title=Tinkering with the past}}</ref>

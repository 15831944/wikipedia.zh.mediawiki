'''捷豹F-Type'''（Jaguar F-Type）是一款雙門、雙座的[[捷豹|捷豹]]跑車，2013年上市<ref name="ae_2011-07">{{Cite news |title=Jaguar XE roadster spied |date=July 2011 |work=Auto Express |url=http://www.autoexpress.co.uk/news/autoexpressnews/269950/jaguar_xe_roadster_spied.html |accessdate=11 September 2011}}</ref>，是在[[捷豹XK|捷豹XK]]的平台上生產的<ref name="ae_2011-07"/>，取替了舊的XK和[[捷豹_E-Type|E-Type]]<ref>{{Cite news |title=Jaguar F Type Coupe |date=August 2014 |work=[[Bloomberg_TV_India|Bloomberg TV India]] |url=http://www.btvin.com/videos/watch/8335/driven:-jaguar-f-type-coupe- |accessdate=14 August 2014}}</ref>。

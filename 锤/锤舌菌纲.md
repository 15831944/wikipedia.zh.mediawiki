'''锤舌菌纲'''（[[学名|学名]]：''Leotiomycetes''）是[[真菌界|真菌界]][[子囊菌门|子囊菌门]]下的一个[[纲|纲]]。锤舌菌纲下的許多真菌物种都是[[植物病理學|植物病原菌]]。<ref name="Google Books111">{{cite web | title=Plant Pathology Concepts and Laboratory Exercises | website=Google Books | url=https://books.google.com/books/about/Plant_Pathology_Concepts_and_Laboratory.html?id=a6yKDQAAQBAJ | access-date=2018-08-22}}</ref>

==系統分類學==
锤舌菌纲下有許多物種過去因未發現有性世代而被歸為[[半知菌|半知菌]]，直到最近才被歸入本綱中。過去的分類系統將锤舌菌纲歸屬於{{tsl|en|Discomycetes||盘菌类}}中<ref name="Hustad Miller 2015 pp. 1–13">{{cite journal | last=Hustad | first=Vincent P. | last2=Miller | first2=Andrew N. | title=Phylogenetic placement of four genera within the Leotiomycetes (Ascomycota) | journal=North American Fungi | volume=6 | issue=9 | date=2015-07-25 | issn=1937-786X | doi=10.2509/naf2011.006.009 | pages=1–13 | url=https://experts.illinois.edu/en/publications/phylogenetic-placement-of-four-genera-within-the-leotiomycetes-as | access-date=2018-08-21}}</ref>，後來[[分子系统发生学|分子系统发生学]]的研究表明盘菌类並非[[單系群|單系群]]，而將其分拆成包含锤舌菌纲在內的數個獨立的綱。多数学者认为[[盘菌亚门|盘菌亚门]]中，锤舌菌纲是[[糞殼菌綱|糞殼菌綱]]的[[旁系群|旁系群]]。分子證據也顯示锤舌菌纲下可分為若干亚纲，但锤舌菌纲本身是否為[[单系群|单系群]]，仍需更多實驗證據證明。<ref name="Hustad Miller 2011 pp. 1–13">{{cite journal | last=Hustad | first=Vincent P. | last2=Miller | first2=Andrew N. | title=Phylogenetic placement of four genera within the Leotiomycetes (Ascomycota) | journal=North American Fungi | volume=6 | issue=0 | date=2011-08-12 | issn=1937-786X | doi=10.2509/naf2011.006.009 | pages=1–13 | url=https://openjournals.wsu.edu/index.php/pnwfungi/article/view/1100 | access-date=2018-08-21 | author= | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180602173716/https://openjournals.wsu.edu/index.php/pnwfungi/article/view/1100 | archive-date=2018-06-02 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Google Books">{{cite web | title=Systematics and Evolution | website=Google Books | url=https://books.google.com/books/about/Systematics_and_Evolution.html?id=mt69BwAAQBAJ | access-date=2018-08-21}}</ref>

==特征==
锤舌菌纲下的多数物种大多在子囊盤（apothecia）中產生子囊。[[子囊|子囊]]是圆柱形的并且没有囊蓋（Operculum）。产生的[[子囊孢子|子囊孢子]]大多是透明的，形状各异。孢子透過圆形的{{tsl|en|apical pore||顶孔}}散播。<ref name="Encyclopedia of Life 2014">{{cite web | title=Overview - Encyclopedia of Life | website=Encyclopedia of Life | date=2014-12-02 | url=http://eol.org/pages/2861056/overview | access-date=2018-08-21 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180129005121/http://eol.org/pages/2861056/overview | archive-date=2018-01-29 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

==分布==
锤舌菌纲分布全球。主要分布地点包括[[北美洲|北美洲]]、[[欧洲|欧洲]]、[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]、[[纽西兰|纽西兰]]以及[[南美洲|南美洲]]西部。<ref name="iNaturalist.org 2010">{{cite web | title=Class Leotiomycetes | website=iNaturalist.org | date=2010-02-07 | url=https://www.inaturalist.org/taxa/55523-Leotiomycetes | access-date=2018-08-21}}</ref><ref name="PaDIL 2014">{{cite web | title=Search | website=PaDIL | date=2014-04-15 | url=http://www.padil.gov.au/maf-border/search?queryText1=&queryType1=All&sortType=ScientificName&viewType=Details&pageSize=10&page=1&taxonomy=Ascomycota:Leotiomycetes&facetItems=9233 | access-date=2018-08-21}}</ref>

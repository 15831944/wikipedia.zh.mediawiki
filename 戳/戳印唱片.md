'''戳印唱片'''（英文名为“STMPD RCRDS”，读作“Stamped Records”）是由[[唱片騎師|DJ]]及[[音樂製作人|音乐制作人]][[馬汀·蓋瑞克斯|马汀·盖瑞克斯]]于2016年3月4日创立的一家[[唱片公司|唱片公司]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.musictimes.com/articles/66941/20160304/martin-garrix-label-stmpd-rcrds-now-that-ive-found-you.htm|title=Martin Garrix Launches Label STMPD RCRDS With 'Now That I've Found You'|date=2016-03-04|website=Music Times|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{cite web|url=http://wegotthiscovered.com/music/martin-garrix-to-launch-stmpd-rcrds-label-with-showcase-in-miami/|title=Martin Garrix To Launch STMPD RCRDS Label With Showcase In Miami|website=We Got This Covered|language=en-US|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://thisisedm.it/la-nuova-label-martin-garrix-stmpd-rcrds/|title=La nuova label di Martin Garrix: ecco STMPD RCRDS|website=This Is EDM|language=it|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://thebanginbeats.com/2016/03/05/martin-garrix-announces-brand-new-label-teases-upcoming-single/|title=Martin Garrix Announces Brand New Label, Teases Upcoming Single|last=Murthi|first=Rajrishi|website=Bangin Beats|access-date=2016-03-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160313045018/http://thebanginbeats.com/2016/03/05/martin-garrix-announces-brand-new-label-teases-upcoming-single/|archivedate=2016-03-13}}</ref>

==历史==
马汀·盖瑞克斯在他的[[YouTube|Youtube]]迷你剧系列《The Martin Garrix Show》和荷兰电视节目上首次公开了他正创立他自己的唱片公司的消息。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2015/11/19/breaking-martin-garrix-announces-the-creation-of-his-own-label/|title=BREAKING: Martin Garrix Reveals His Own Label After Leaving Spinnin' Records [Video]|last=Lucas|website=Your EDM|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref>这条新闻很快在盖瑞克斯离开[[斯宾尼唱片|斯宾尼唱片]]和音乐全明星（MusicAllStars Management）后传遍大江南北。<ref name=":22">{{cite web|url=http://wegotthiscovered.com/music/martin-garrix-to-launch-stmpd-rcrds-label-with-showcase-in-miami/|title=Martin Garrix To Launch STMPD RCRDS Label With Showcase In Miami|website=We Got This Covered|language=en-US|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref><ref name=":3">{{cite web|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2016/03/martin-garrix-announces-new-single-first-release-now-ive-found/|title=Martin Garrix announces new label STMPD RCRDS - Dancing Astronaut|website=Dancing Astronaut|language=en-US|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref>盖瑞克斯的离开被他自己引述为和斯宾尼唱片的音乐版权之间的分歧。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/6678380/martin-garrix-leaves-spinnin-records-musicallstars-management|title=Martin Garrix Leaves Spinnin' Records and MusicAllStars Management|website=Billboard|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://wegotthiscovered.com/music/martin-garrix-leaves-spinnin-records/|title=Martin Garrix Leaves Spinnin' Records|website=We Got This Covered|language=en-US|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2015/08/26/breaking-martin-garrix-leaves-spinnin-records-music-all-stars-management-behind/|title=BREAKING: Martin Garrix Leaves Spinnin' Records & Music All Stars Management Behind|last=Lucas|website=Your EDM|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref> 

厂牌名指的即是邮票，这是他父亲的邮票拍卖公司所给予他的灵感。<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2016/03/06/martin-garrix-reveals-meaning-behind-label-name-talks-new-album/|title=Martin Garrix Reveals How He Got His Face Burnt & The Real Meaning Behind His Label|last=Sachs|first=Elliot|website=Your EDM|access-date=2016-03-10}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/dance/7461645/5-things-you-didnt-know-martin-garrix-label-stmpd-rcrds|title=5 Things You Didn't Know About Martin Garrix's STMPD RCRDS Label|newspaper=Billboard|access-date=2016-10-08}}</ref>

盖瑞克斯放出了厂牌的第一首歌——《Now That I've Found You》，由约翰·马丁（[[John_Martin|John Martin]]）和米歇尔·西特龙（[[Michel_Zitron|Michel Zitron]]）所演唱，原曲在2016年3月11日<ref name=":2" /><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2016/03/04/martin-garrix-unleashes-teaser-for-debut-release-on-new-label/|title=Martin Garrix Finally Gives Fans What They've Been Waiting For [New Video]|last=Lucas|website=Your EDM|access-date=2016-03-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://thebanginbeats.com/2016/03/11/martin-garrix-feat-john-michel-now-that-ive-found-you-original-mix/|title=Martin Garrix Feat. John & Michel- Now That I've Found You (Original Mix)|last=Murthi|first=Rajrishi|website=Bangin Beats|access-date=2016-03-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160313193832/http://thebanginbeats.com/2016/03/11/martin-garrix-feat-john-michel-now-that-ive-found-you-original-mix/|archivedate=2016-03-13}}</ref>曾被称为《Don't Crack Under Pressure》<ref name=":32">{{cite web|url=http://www.dancingastronaut.com/2016/03/martin-garrix-announces-new-single-first-release-now-ive-found/|title=Martin Garrix announces new label STMPD RCRDS - Dancing Astronaut|language=en-US|website=Dancing Astronaut|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref>。歌曲的旋律由盖瑞克斯于2015年4月的歌舞汇（Dancefair）期间所写，尤其还曾在[[布達佩斯島節|布达佩斯岛节]]作为他的结束曲播放过。<ref name=":1">{{cite web|url=http://www.musictimes.com/articles/67454/20160311/martin-garrix-now-that-i-found-you-review.htm|title=Martin Garrix 'Now That I Found You' Review: STMPD RCRDS Starts Off With A Bang|date=2016-03-11|website=Music Times|access-date=2016-03-13}}</ref>

为了庆祝新厂牌的建立，盖瑞克斯在2016年3月18日在[[迈阿密|迈阿密]][[超世代音乐节|超世代音乐节]]结束之后于故事夜总会（STORY Nightclub）开了一场派对。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.magneticmag.com/2016/03/martin-garrix-unveils-teaser-for-debut-release-on-his-own-label/|title=MARTIN GARRIX UNVEILS TEASER FOR DEBUT RELEASE ON HIS OWN LABEL|last=Tyler|first=|date=4 March 2016|website=Magnetic Mag|publisher=Magnetic Mag|access-date=13 March 2016}}</ref>

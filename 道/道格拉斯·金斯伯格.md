'''道格拉斯·霍華德·金斯伯格''' （英文：{{lang|en|Douglas H. Ginsburg}}，（{{bd|1946年|5月25日}}），[[美國哥倫比亞特區聯邦巡迴上訴法院|美國哥倫比亞特區聯邦巡迴上訴法院]]資深法官。1986年獲[[美國總統|總統]][[里根|里根]]提名此職。自2001年至2008年任該院[[首席法官|首席法官]]。1987年10月，40歲的金斯伯格獲列根提名為[[美國最高法院大法官|美國最高法院大法官]]，<ref name=msdomong>{{cite news |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=O6tRAAAAIBAJ&sjid=ixIEAAAAIBAJ&pg=4318%2C6506375 |newspaper=Milwaukee Sentinel |agency=Associated Press |title=Democrats open-minded on Ginsburg |date=October 30, 1987 |page=1, part 1 }}</ref><ref name=ppynj>{{cite news |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=tuhVAAAAIBAJ&sjid=eeEDAAAAIBAJ&pg=5230%2C7290671|newspaper=Eugene Register-Guard |location=Oregon |agency=Associated Press  |title=President picks young, novice judge |date=October 30, 1987 |page=1A}}</ref>，以填補路易斯·鮑威爾退休後的空缺。唯其早年吸食[[大麻|大麻]]一事引起政界紛爭，列根撤銷提名。<ref name=lnsgaupi>{{cite news |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=U68zAAAAIBAJ&sjid=JzIHAAAAIBAJ&pg=5946%2C555078 |newspaper=Lodi News-Sentinel |agency=UPI |title=Ginsburg admits marijuana use |date=November 6, 1987 |page=1 }}</ref><ref name=gwacnmerg>{{cite news |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=QehVAAAAIBAJ&sjid=euEDAAAAIBAJ&pg=5388%2C1691294 |newspaper=Eugene Register-Guard |location=Oregon|agency=wire service reports|title=Ginsburg withdraws as court nominee|date=November 8, 1987 |page=1A}}</ref><ref name=srdffgwd>{{cite news |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=ZbsyAAAAIBAJ&sjid=m-8DAAAAIBAJ&pg=5995%2C4998133 |newspaper=Spokesman-Review |location=Spokane, Washington |agency=Associated Press |title=Drug furor forces Ginsburg's withdrawal |date=November 8, 1987 |page=A1}}</ref>

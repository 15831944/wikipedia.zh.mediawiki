'''道格·范恩''' （{{lang-en|Doug Fine}}）是一位美国男作家、记者、幽默作家和牧羊人。<ref>{{cite news | title=Doug Fine, Journalist, New Mexico | url=http://www.smithsonianmag.com/people-places/15545322.html | work=www.smithsonianmag.com | accessdate=2010-12-28}}</ref><ref name="wweek.com">{{cite news | title=Doug Fine: Author of Farewell, My Subaru talks to WW about green ranching, veggie-powered monster trucks, and the mystique of goat ice cream | url=http://wweek.com/editorial/3423/10823/ | work=wweek.com | accessdate=2010-12-28 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090824225259/http://wweek.com/editorial/3423/10823/ | archivedate=2009-08-24 }}</ref>

==生平==
1989年离开美国东海岸进入[[斯坦福大学|斯坦福大学]]学习。<ref name="Not Really An Alaskan Mountain Man">{{cite web|title= About The Author|publisher= Alaska Northwest Books}}</ref>之后成为一名背包客，开始自由记者的生涯，为多家媒体诸如''[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]]''、''[[沙龙_(网站)|沙龙]]''、''[[美国新闻与世界报道|美国新闻与世界报道]]''、''[[塞拉俱乐部|塞拉俱乐部]]''、''[[连线|连线]]''、''[[Outside_(magazine)|Outside]]''、[[全国公共广播电台|全国公共广播电台]]撰写专栏。<ref>{{cite news| publisher=[[The_Oregonian|Oregonian]] | title=Interview: Author Doug Fine | url=http://blog.oregonlive.com/books/2008/04/interview_author_doug_fine.html | work=blog.oregonlive.com | accessdate=2010-12-28 | date=2008-04-17}}</ref>他的调查报告遍布世界五大洲，经常变换住处，曾经在[[缅甸|缅甸]]、[[卢旺达|卢旺达]]、[[老挝|老挝]]、[[危地马拉|危地马拉]]和[[塔吉克斯坦|塔吉克斯坦]]居住过。<ref>{{cite news | work= [[Santa_Fe_New_Mexican|Santa Fe New Mexican]] | title=Writer finds funny side to carbon-light life | url=http://www.santafenewmexican.com/news/local_news/writer-finds-funny-side-to-carbon-light-life/article_a011d073-2753-5f50-b251-5255e1ce3099.html |author=Staci Matlock | date=2008-03-25 | accessdate=2010-12-28}}</ref>其中一次远行，是關於缅甸民主努力的報道，写进了[[国会记录|国会记录]]。<ref>{{cite news | publisher=University of Denver | title=RMSS Featured Speakers | url=http://www.du.edu/green/rmss/speakers.html | accessdate=2010-12-28 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101004035116/http://www.du.edu/green/rmss/speakers.html | archivedate=2010-10-04 }}</ref>道格·范恩在[[阿拉斯加州|阿拉斯加州]]的偏远山区写下了多篇获奖广播稿，之后搬去[[新墨西哥州|新墨西哥州]]。<ref>{{cite news| publisher=[[Alaska_Press_Club|Alaska Press Club]]| title=Alaska Press Club Awards 2003| url=http://www.alaskapressclub.org/index.php/awards/2003awards| work=www.alaskapressclub.org| accessdate=2010-12-29| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110124133121/http://www.alaskapressclub.org/index.php/awards/2003awards| archivedate=2011-01-24}}</ref>

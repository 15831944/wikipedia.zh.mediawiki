'''道奇城公共圖書館'''（{{lang-en|Dodge City Public Library}}）位於[[美國|美國]][[堪薩斯州|堪薩斯州]]{{le|道奇城 (堪薩斯州)|Dodge City, Kansas|道奇城}}第二北大道1001號，為道奇城一座主要的圖書館。此圖書館屬於堪薩斯州西南部圖書館系統（Southwest Kansas Library System）的成員之一，館藏約123,000冊，且每年書籍流通次數超過189,000次<ref>{{cite web | title = Dodge City Public Library – Dodge City, KS | publisher = lib-web-cats - A directory of libraries throughout the world | url = http://www.librarytechnology.org/lwc-displaylibrary.pl?RC=3959 | accessdate = 2011-09-23}}</ref>。此圖書館提供了多項服務，包含電腦課程、公共上網設施，以及適合兒童和成人的方案等<ref>{{cite web | title = Public Services | publisher = Dodge City Public Library | url = http://www.dcpl.info/publicservices.htm | accessdate = 2011-09-23 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20111122050422/http://www.dcpl.info/publicservices.htm | archivedate = 2011-11-22 }}</ref>。

1905年，道奇城居民團體請求慈善家[[安德鲁·卡内基|安德鲁·卡内基]]在城市內投資興建一座圖書館。卡内基答應，並撥款7,500美元；1906年開始在市區內的第二北大道與西杉木街路口興建，1907年2月完工啟用。1979年，此棟建築收錄在[[國家史蹟名錄|國家史蹟名錄]]中<ref name=ks-nrhp>[https://www.kshs.org/natreg/natreg_listings/search/page:1/county:FO "National and State Registers of Historic Places: Ford County".] [http://www.kshs.org/ Kansas Historical Society.] Retrieved 2015-03-29.</ref>。1981年，道奇城藝術委員會（Dodge City Arts Council）獲得經費來維護此棟建築，並且成立卡內基藝術中心<ref>[http://www.westernkansasnews.com/2013/03/dodge-city-main-street-launches-historical-downtown-building-promotional-campaign/ "Dodge City Main Street Launches Historical Downtown Building Promotional Campaign".] [http://www.westernkansasnews.com/ Western Kansas News.] 2013-03-14. Retrieved 2015-03-29.</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Facebook site|url=https://www.facebook.com/pages/Carnegie-Center-for-the-Arts/124799050885381|publisher=Carnegie Center for the Arts|accessdate=2016-03-01}}</ref>。

圖書館在1981年搬遷至當前館址<ref>{{cite web | title = About DCPL | publisher = Dodge City Public Library | url = http://www.dcpl.info/about.htm | accessdate = 2011-09-23 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070726163126/http://www.dcpl.info/about.htm | archivedate = 2007-07-26 }}</ref>。

==市內其他圖書館==
其他在城市內的圖書館包含{{le|道奇城社區學院|Dodge City Community College|道奇城社區學院圖書館}}以及堪薩斯遺產中心（Kansas Heritage Center），其中道奇城社區學院圖書館館藏30,000冊，並且也是{{le|聯邦送存圖書館計畫|Federal Depository Library Program|聯邦送存圖書館}}之一；而堪薩斯遺產中心為非營利資源中心與研究圖書館，由{{le|道奇城公立學校|Dodge City Public Schools}}{{efn|道奇城公立學校屬於聯合學區。}}所營運，以堪薩斯州、北美大平原以及美國舊西部的歷史為主<ref>{{cite web | title = Facilities | publisher = [[Dodge_City_Community_College|Dodge City Community College]] | url = http://www.dc3.edu/handbook/facilities.htm | accessdate = 2011-09-23 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110814094942/http://dc3.edu/handbook/facilities.htm | archivedate = 2011-08-14 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = GPO Federal Library Directory | publisher = [[United_States_Government_Printing_Office|United States Government Printing Office]] | url = http://catalog.gpo.gov/fdlpdir/FDLPdir.jsp?flag=searchp&st_12=KS | accessdate = 2011-09-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = About the Kansas Heritage Center | publisher = Kansas Heritage Center | url = http://ksheritagestore.org/about_the_center.html | accessdate = 2011-10-10}}</ref>。

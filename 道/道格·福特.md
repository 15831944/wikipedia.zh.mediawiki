'''道格·福特''' {{post-nominals|country=CAN|MPP}} （{{lang|en|Douglas Robert "Doug" Ford}}，{{bd|1964年|11月20日||}}），[[加拿大|加拿大]]政治人物和商人，2018年起出任[[安大略省|安大略省]]第26任[[安大略省省長|省長]]、[[安大略省議會|安大略省議會]]怡陶碧谷北選區議員和[[安大略進步保守黨|安大略進步保守黨]]黨魁。在此之前他曾活躍於[[多倫多|多倫多]]市級政壇，2010年-14年間出任多倫多市議會怡陶碧谷北（第二市選區）議員，並於2014年市選中角逐市長職位但敗於[[莊德利|莊德利]]。他的弟弟為已故多倫多市長[[羅柏特·福特|羅柏特·福特]]，而兩人的父親老道格·福特（{{lang|en|Doug Ford, Sr.}}）亦曾任安大略省議員。

==早年生活及事業==
福特於1964年在[[怡陶碧谷|怡陶碧谷]]（現為[[多倫多|多倫多]]市一部分）出生，為老道格·福特和戴安（{{lang|en|Diane}}）四名子女中的次子<ref>{{cite web|last=Goldsbie |first=Jonathan |url=http://www.thegridto.com/city/places/a-beautiful-day-in-rob-fords-neighbourhood/ |title=The Rob Ford walking tour |publisher=Thegridto.com |date=May 8, 2012 |accessdate=November 6, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=Canada |url=http://v1.theglobeandmail.com/servlet/story/Announcements.20100922.93242769/BDAStory/BDA/births |title=globeandmail.com: FORD, Douglas Bruce |publisher=V1.theglobeandmail.com |date= |accessdate=November 6, 2013 |location=Toronto}}</ref>，曾於當地的史嘉烈高地高中（{{lang|en|Scarlett Heights Collegiate Institute}}）就讀<ref>{{cite web |title=Facts about the other Ford: A look at mayoral candidate Doug Ford |url=https://www.cp24.com/news/facts-about-the-other-ford-a-look-at-mayoral-candidate-doug-ford-1.2005035 |publisher=Canadian Press |website=CP24.com |date=September 12, 2014 |accessdate=April 4, 2018}}</ref>。

老道格於1962年與友人創辦德科標籤公司（{{lang|en|Deco Labels}}），該公司年度營業額約為1億[[加元|加元]]{{sfn|McDonald|2012|p=43}}。小道格則於1990年代加入該公司，2002年起出任公司總裁，期間負責把公司業務擴展至[[芝加哥|芝加哥]]<ref>{{cite web|last=Daubs |first=Katie |url=https://www.thestar.com/news/gta/2014/02/03/5_things_you_didnt_know_about_rob_fords_family_revelations_from_the_book_crazy_town.html |title=5 things you didn't know about Rob Ford's family: Revelations from the book Crazy Town |newspaper=《[[多倫多星報|多倫多星報]]》 |date=February 3, 2014 |accessdate=May 27, 2014}}</ref><ref name="DecoInsideStory">{{cite news|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/toronto/doug-ford-at-deco/article21067584/|title=Doug Ford at Deco: The inside story|first=Robyn|last=Doolittle|date=October 10, 2014|accessdate=April 5, 2018|newspaper=《[[環球郵報|環球郵報]]》}}</ref>。福特於2010年參政後將德科業務交予兄長蘭迪（{{lang|en|Randy Ford}}）管理<ref>{{cite news |title=Ford family business 'a nightmare' since Doug handed managerial control to Randy, ex-employees say |first=Richard |last=Warnica |newspaper=National Post |date=June 4, 2014 |accessdate=January 31, 2018 |url=http://nationalpost.com/posted-toronto/ford-family-business-a-nightmare-since-doug-handed-managerial-control-to-randy-ex-employees-say}}</ref><ref name="DecoInsideStory"/>。

荷禮狄（{{lang|en|Doug Holyday}}）於1994年競逐怡陶碧谷市長時從德科公司訂購競選貼紙，福特隨後為他助選<ref>{{cite news|author=Torstar News Service|title=Doug Ford never a mere Toronto councillor|newspaper=Toronto Metro|url=http://www.metronews.ca/news/toronto/2014/09/12/doug-ford-was-never-a-mere-toronto-councillor.html|date=September 13, 2014|accessdate=April 6, 2018}}</ref>。老道格於1995年和1999年安大略省選中代表[[安大略進步保守黨|安大略進步保守黨]]競逐省議員席位，福特亦參與父親的競選團隊<ref>{{cite news|last=Sherwood|first=Matthew|title=Doug Ford: Riding shotgun in the Fordmobile|newspaper=《[[環球郵報|環球郵報]]》|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/toronto/doug-ford-riding-shotgun-in-the-fordmobile/article1322412/|date=June 11, 2011|accessdate=April 6, 2018}}</ref>。

==晉身市政==
福特的弟弟，原任多倫多市議會怡陶碧谷北（第二市選區）議員[[羅柏特·福特|羅柏特·福特]]於2010年市選中改為角逐多倫多市長職位，道格遂於同屆市選競逐該市議會議席。兩兄弟競選皆告捷，而道格當選市議員後則表示會將十萬加元年薪捐贈予社區組織<ref>{{cite news |newspaper=《多倫多太陽報》 |title=Doug Ford to donate salary to charities  |url=http://www.torontosun.com/news/torontovotes2010/2010/10/26/15840566.html |last=Peat |first=Don |date=October 26, 2010 |accessdate=June 4, 2013}}</ref>。道格·福特出任市議員期間表決贊成[[央街|央街]]以西的垃圾收集服務外判、將[[多倫多運輸局|多倫多運輸局]]定為必需服務、減低市議員的辦公室預算、以及取消市政府的車輛登記税；他亦於超過90%市議會表決中與羅柏特·福特持相同立場<ref>{{cite news |newspaper=《[[多倫多星報|多倫多星報]]》 |title=Ford Brothers have near unanimous voting record on council|url=https://www.thestar.com/news/city_hall/toronto2014election/2014/09/12/ford_brothers_have_a_nearunanimous_voting_record_on_council.html |accessdate=March 30, 2018}}</ref>。

2013年5月一篇《[[環球郵報|環球郵報]]》報道引述匿名來源，指福特曾於1980年代出售[[哈希什|哈希什]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.theatlantic.com/international/archive/2013/05/toronto-deputy-mayor-doug-ford-robs-brother-former-hash-dealer/314893/ |newspaper=The Atlantic |title=Is Toronto City Councillor Doug Ford, Rob's Brother, a Former Hash Dealer? |first=Connor |last=Simpson |date=May 25, 2013 |accessdate=January 2, 2018}}</ref><ref name=drug>{{cite news|last=McArthur |first=Greg |url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/toronto/globe-investigation-the-ford-familys-history-with-drug-dealing/article12153014/?page=all |title=Globe investigation: The Ford family's history with drug dealing |newspaper=《[[環球郵報|環球郵報]]》 |date=May 25, 2013 |accessdate=May 27, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.ca/2013/05/25/doug-ford-drug-dealing-globe-and-mail_n_3336567.html |title=Doug Ford: Drug Dealing Allegations 'Disgusting' |newspaper=[[HuffPost|HuffPost]] |date=May 25, 2013 |accessdate=January 2, 2018}}</ref>。福特一直未有就此受到檢控，他否認這些指控並指該報對待其弟（即時任市長）羅柏特·福特有欠公允<ref name="Maclean's">{{cite journal |url=http://www.macleans.ca/news/canada/doug-ford-denies-globe-report-that-he-dealt-hashish-in-80s/ |title=Doug Ford denies Globe report that he dealt hashish in 80s |first1=Diana |last1=Mehta |first2=Will |last2=Campbell |agency=The Canadian Press |journal=[[麥克林|麥克林]]雜誌 |date=May 26, 2013 |accessdate=January 2, 2018}}</ref>。安大略新聞業議會就此展開聆訊後駁回福特的投訴，並裁定《環球郵報》的報道「公正且合乎道德」（{{lang|en|"fair and ethical"}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://j-source.ca/article/updated-ontario-press-council-rules-star-globe-rob-ford-stories-ethical/|title=Updated: Ontario Press Council rules Star, Globe Rob Ford stories ethical - JSource|date=October 16, 2013}}</ref>。福特當時表示計劃起訴該報誹謗<ref>{{cite news|author=White, Patrick|date=September 12, 2014|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/toronto/can-doug-ford-win-torontos-mayoral-race/article20591133/|title=Can Doug Ford win Toronto's mayoral race?|newspaper=The Globe and Mail|accessdate=September 14, 2014}}</ref>；2018年他被問及為何最終沒有對簿公堂時，他表示此舉只會浪費時間<ref>{{cite web |title=Doug Ford Sure Disliked A Lot of Questions in This CBC Interview |url=http://www.huffingtonpost.ca/2018/03/13/doug-ford-sure-disliked-a-lot-of-questions-in-this-cbc-interview_a_23384590/ |website=HuffPost |first=Emma |last=Prestwich |date=March 13, 2018 |accessdate=March 20, 2018}}</ref>。

羅柏特·福特任職市長後期受到吸毒醜聞等因素困擾，施政多番遇到掣肘，道格·福特亦對市級政治漸生去意，並於2013年6月表示不會在翌年市選中尋求連任市議員<ref>{{cite news |newspaper=《多倫多太陽報》 |title=Doug Ford says he won't run for council in 2014  |url=http://www.torontosun.com/2013/06/12/doug-ford-says-he-wont-run-for-council-in-2014 |last=Peat |first=Don |date=June 12, 2013}}</ref>。當時外界推測福特有可能代表[[安大略進步保守黨|安大略進步保守黨]]在未來省選競逐省議員席位，或角逐該黨黨魁<ref>{{cite news |newspaper=Now |location=Toronto, Ontario |url=http://www.nowtoronto.com/news/story.cfm?content=193787 |title=The real fight in Etobicoke |last=Di Matteo |first=Enzo |date=August 1, 2013 |accessdate=August 7, 2013}}</ref>。然而他於2014年2月與時任進步保守黨黨魁胡達克（{{lang|en|Tim Hudak}}）會面後表示不會參與同年6月舉行的省議會選舉，以讓他集中協助弟弟羅柏特連任市長<ref>{{cite news|title=Councillor Doug Ford will not run in provincial election|url=https://www.thestar.com/news/city_hall/toronto2014election/2014/02/20/live_doug_ford_makes_announcement.html|accessdate=February 21, 2014|newspaper=《[[多倫多星報|多倫多星報]]》|date=February 20, 2014}}</ref>。

羅柏特·福特於2014年5月展開戒毒復康療程後，道格·福特表示不排除取代他競逐市長<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.torontosun.com/2014/05/06/doug-ford-wont-rule-out-running-for-mayor |title=Doug Ford won't rule out running for mayor |first=Don |last=Peat |newspaper=《多倫多太陽報》 |date=May 6, 2014}}</ref>。羅柏特完成戒毒療程後恢復競選工作，但同年9月確診患上惡性脂肪肉瘤，遂於9月12日市長候選人報名截止期限前退選並由道格取代<ref>{{cite news|title=Rob Ford drops out of mayoral race, Doug Ford running in his place|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/toronto/ford-dropping-out-of-toronto-mayoral-race/article20576741/|newspaper=《[[環球郵報|環球郵報]]》|date=September 12, 2014}}</ref>。道格保着羅柏特原有的支持度，但並未能超越[[莊德利|莊德利]]，最終於10月27日舉行的市選中以33.7%的得票率敗於莊德利（得票40.3%），無緣為福特家族保著市長寶座<ref name=CBCTOPOLI>{{cite web|title=Toronto election: CBC declares John Tory winner|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/toronto-election-cbc-declares-john-tory-winner-1.2814902|publisher=[[加拿大廣播公司|加拿大廣播公司]]|accessdate=2014-10-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=2014 General Election Results: Mayor|url=http://election.toronto.ca/epr2014/eprDetail.do?M;000#1414553823660|publisher=多倫多市政府|accessdate=2014-10-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141028172804/http://election.toronto.ca/epr2014/eprDetail.do?M;000#1414553823660#1414553823660|archive-date=2014-10-28|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。福特團隊在競選期間非法在市内多處擺放478個競選標牌，被罰款1萬1950元<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cp24.com/news/ford-facing-11-950-fine-over-illegal-signs-chow-and-tory-on-hook-for-lesser-amounts-1.2076898|title=Ford facing $11,950 fine over illegal signs, Chow and Tory on hook for lesser amounts|accessdate=October 30, 2014}}</ref>。

胡達克於2014年省選失利後辭任進步保守黨黨魁，而敗選市長的福特再受外界關注會否競逐該職。福特原本表示正在考慮參選該黨黨魁<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/toronto/doug-ford-says-ontario-pc-leadership-bid-is-on-the-table/article21358827/|title=Doug Ford says Ontario PC leadership bid is 'on the table'|newspaper=《[[環球郵報|環球郵報]]》|date=October 28, 2014|accessdate=November 20, 2015}}</ref>，但2014年11月末則表示支持葉勵雅（{{lang|en|Christine Elliott}}）參選<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com/news/queenspark/2014/11/27/doug_ford_declines_to_enter_race_for_ontario_pc_leadership.html|title=Doug Ford won't enter race for Ontario PC leadership|newspaper=《[[多倫多星報|多倫多星報]]》|date=November 27, 2014|accessdate=November 20, 2015}}</ref>。

==轉戰省政==
接替胡達克出任進步保守黨黨魁的彭建邦（{{lang|en|Patrick Brown}}）於2018年1月25日宣布辭職，該黨遂宣布於同年6月省選舉行前進行黨魁選舉。福特於同月29日宣布角逐黨魁，為首位公布參選的候選人<ref>{{cite news |title=Doug Ford says he will run in Ontario PC leadership race |url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/doug-ford-announcement-etobicoke-ontario-pc-leader-1.4508622 |first=Muriel |last=Draaisma |publisher=CBC News |date=January 29, 2018 |accessdate=July 19, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.thestar.com/news/gta/2018/01/29/doug-ford-expected-to-make-announcement-at-noon-monday.html |title=Doug Ford seeks Conservative leadership amid party turmoil |newspaper=《[[多倫多星報|多倫多星報]]》 |first=Robert |last=Benzie |date=January 29, 2018 |accessdate=January 29, 2018}}</ref>，再於同月31日宣布尋求怡陶碧谷北選區省議員議席的進步保守黨候選人資格<ref>{{cite news|title=Ford to seek nomination for Etobicoke North|url=http://torontosun.com/news/provincial/ford-to-seek-nomination-for-etobicoke-north|accessdate=January 31, 2018|newspaper=《多倫多太陽報》|agency=Postmedia News|date=January 31, 2018}}</ref>。

黨魁選舉結果於同年3月10日公布，福特在第三輪投票後得分為50.62%，險勝獲取49.38%的葉勵雅，當選進步保守黨黨魁<ref name="CTVnewleader">{{cite web|url=https://www.ctvnews.ca/canada/ontario-pc-party-fails-to-announce-new-leader-as-review-underway-1.3837319#_gus&_gucid=&_gup=twitter&_gsc=yXZAi2Y|title=Doug Ford named new Ontario PC leader|publisher=Canadian Press|website=CTVnews.ca|date=March 10, 2018}}</ref>。他於同月27日在無對手競爭下自動成為該黨的怡陶碧谷北選區省議員候選人<ref name=":0">{{Cite news |url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/doug-ford-etobicoke-north-1.4595998 |title=Doug Ford to be acclaimed Etobicoke North Ontario PC Candidate |work=CBC News |access-date=March 28, 2018 |date=March 27, 2018}}</ref>。

[[安大略自由黨|安大略自由黨]]執政十多年後選民求變殷切，進步保守黨從中受惠。[[加拿大廣播公司|加拿大廣播公司]]於2018年4月初綜合多項民意調查，顯示福特領導下的進步保守黨平均支持度為42.1%，高於執政自由黨的27.2%、新民主黨的23.4%和綠黨的5.7%<ref>{{cite news |last1=Grenier |first1=Éric |title=With nine weeks to go, the Ontario election is Doug Ford's to lose |url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/grenier-ontario-poll-tracker-1.4603291 |accessdate=April 7, 2018 |newspaper=CBC News |date=April 6, 2018}}</ref>。新民主黨其後超越自由黨並一度威脅進步保守黨，但進步保守黨終在同年6月7日舉行的省選贏取[[安大略省議會|省議會]]124個議席中的76席，以多數政府姿態上台執政<ref>{{cite web |last1=Artuso |first1=Antonella |title=Doug Ford wins majority with promise to fix Ontario |url=http://torontosun.com/news/provincial/doug-ford-declared-winner |newspaper=《多倫多太陽報》 |accessdate=June 23, 2018 |date=June 8, 2018}}</ref>，而福特亦當選怡陶碧谷北選區省議員<ref name="Résultats du soir de l'élection 2018">{{cite web |title=Unofficial Election Results: Etobicoke North |url=https://www.elections.on.ca/content/ngw/en/election-results/etobicoke-north.html |publisher=Elections Ontario |accessdate=July 19, 2018}}</ref>。他於同月29日宣誓就任[[安大略省省長|安大略省省長]]<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.thestar.com/news/queenspark/2018/06/29/doug-ford-sworn-in-as-ontario-premier.html |title=Doug Ford sworn in as Ontario premier |first1=Robert |last1=Benzie |first2=Rob |last2=Ferguson |first3=Kristin |last3=Rushowy |newspaper=《[[多倫多星報|多倫多星報]]》 |date=June 29, 2018 |accessdate=July 17, 2018}}</ref>，為1934年起首位剛當選安大略省議員便出任省長的人物<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/doug-ford-ontario-legislature-speaker-election-1.4740842|title=For 1st time since Doug Ford elected, Ontario Legislature sits |work=CBC News|publisher=}}</ref>。

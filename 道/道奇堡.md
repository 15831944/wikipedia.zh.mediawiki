'''道奇堡'''（Fort Dodge）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[韦伯斯特县_(爱阿华州)|韦伯斯特县]]得梅因河沿岸的城市，是韦伯斯特县的县城<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=2011-06-07 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531210815/http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=May 31, 2011 }}</ref>。2010年人口普查中的人口为25,206人，多于2000年人口普查的25,136人<ref name="USCB">{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |title=Population & Housing Occupancy Status 2010 |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] American FactFinder |accessdate=2011-03-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721034521/http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |archivedate=July 21, 2011 }}</ref><ref name="IADC">{{cite web|url=http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |title=Data from the 2010 Census |publisher=State Data Center of Iowa |accessdate=2011-03-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718201045/http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |archivedate=July 18, 2011 }}</ref>。道奇堡是北中部和西北爱荷华州的主要商业中心。169号和[[20号美国国道|20号美国国道]]经过境内。

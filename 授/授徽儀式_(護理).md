'''授徽儀式'''（{{lang-en|Pinning ceremony}}）為護理系學生畢業進入職場前的一種[[儀式|儀式]]<ref>{{cite web |title= Pinning Ceremony |url= http://www.olhcc.edu/x1292.xml |publisher= [[Our_Lady_of_Holy_Cross_College|Our Lady of Holy Cross College]] |date=  |accessdate= 2013-11-09 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20110720024611/http://www.olhcc.edu/x1292.xml |archive-date= 2011-07-20 |dead-url= yes }}</ref><ref name= "Rivers_Reporter">{{cite news |last= Rivers |first= Kathy |title= Pinning ceremony honors School of Nursing students  |url= http://www.mc.vanderbilt.edu/reporter/index.html?ID=6597 |newspaper= Reporter |publisher= [[Vanderbilt_University_Medical_Center|Vanderbilt University Medical Center]] |location= Nashville, TN |date= }}
</ref>，通常是由護理學校的師長為畢業生別上[[護理徽章|護理徽章]]，但畢業生也可以自行選擇由親屬或好友為自己別上徽章<ref>[http://www.southark.edu/ South Arkansas Community College]</ref>。護理系學生也會在此儀式上朗頌[[南丁格爾誓言|南丁格爾誓言]]<ref>{{cite web |title= Nurse Pinning Ceremony  |url= http://www.alamo.edu/newsDetail.aspx?id=26531 |publisher= [[San_Antonio_College|San Antonio College]], [[The_Alamo_Colleges|The Alamo Colleges]] |date= 2012-12-13 |accessdate= 2013-11-09}}</ref>。

'''叫鶴目'''（學名：Cariamiformes）是[[生物分類學|生物分類學]]上是[[鳥綱|鳥綱]]中的一個[[目_(生物)|目]]，本目多含多種原始不會飛行的鳥類，最古老可以追朔到六千萬年以前，其中包括[[叫鶴科|叫鶴科]]與已絕種的[[駭鳥科|駭鳥科]]。傳統上被認為是[[鶴形目|鶴形目]]底下的分支，如今由形態學與基因學上的研究<ref>{{cite journal | last = Hackett | first = Shannon J. | authorlink = | title = A Phylogenomic Study of Birds Reveals Their Evolutionary History | journal = Science | volume = 320 | issue = 5884 | pages = 1763–1768 | publisher =  | location =  | date = 2008-06-27 | url = http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/abstract/320/5884/1763 | doi = 10.1126/science.1157704 | id =  | accessdate = 2008-10-18 | pmid = 18583609|display-authors=etal}}</ref>顯示叫鶴目實屬於另外一個名為[[南鳥類|南鳥類]]的獨立分支（其他成員包括[[隼形目|隼形目]]、[[鸚形目|鸚形目]]與[[雀形目|雀形目]]）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://darrennaish.blogspot.com/search/label/phorusrhacids|title=Darren Naish: Tetrapod Zoology|author=|date=|website=darrennaish.blogspot.com|accessdate=12 April 2018}}</ref>

叫鶴目最早的化石紀錄來自於[[南極洲|南極洲]][[維加島|維加島]][[白堊紀|白堊紀]]晚期（約 6,600 萬年前）{{link-en|Lopez de Bertodano Formation|Lopez de Bertodano Formation}}地層所發掘的一根[[股骨|股骨]]，與現代[[叫鶴科|叫鶴科]]的股骨並無二致，屬於一種將近{{convert|1|m|ft}}高的鳥類。根據挖掘的地點與時期，科學家推測這種鳥類為叫鶴與駭鳥的共同祖先。<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Case | first1 = J. | last2 = Reguero | first2 = M. | last3 = Martin | first3 = J. | last4 = Cordes-Person | first4 = A. | year = 2006 | title = A cursorial bird from the Maastrictian of Antarctica | url = | journal = Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology, ''Supplement to''| volume = 26 | issue = 3| page = 48A }}</ref>

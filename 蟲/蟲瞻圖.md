'''蟲瞻圖'''（英語：'''worm's-eye view'''），又稱'''仰視圖'''，是從下向上觀看的影像，彷彿觀察者是一隻蟲 ; 它與鳥瞰圖相反<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nyfa.edu/student-resources/point-view-photography/|title=Point Of View In Photography|date=2014-12-09|work=Student Resources|language=en|access-date=2017-06-13}}</ref>。 蟲瞻圖可以用來仰望某物，使其看起來高大、強壯、有力，而觀眾會感覺自己如幼童或無能為力<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.videomaker.com/article/c10/1387-camera-work-whats-your-angle|title=Camera Work: What's Your Angle|work=Videomaker.com|language=en|access-date=2017-06-13}}</ref>。蟲瞻圖通常採用三點透視，上面一個消失點、一個在左邊、另一個在右邊<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://thehelpfulartteacher.blogspot.com/2011/01/three-point-perspectivethe-really.html|title=The Helpful Art Teacher: THREE POINT PERSPECTIVE... WORM'S EYE vs. BIRD'S EYE VIEW|date=2011-01-12|last=Teacher|first=The Helpful Art|website=The Helpful Art Teacher|access-date=2017-06-13}}</ref>。

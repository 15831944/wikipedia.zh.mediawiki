《'''誠實國度的愛麗絲'''》（{{kor|k='''성실한 나라의 앨리스'''}}；{{lang-en|'''''Alice in Earnestland'''''}}），是2015年8月上映的一部韓國[[黑色幽默|黑色幽默]]電影，由{{link-en|安國鎮 (導演)|Ahn Gooc-jin|安國鎮}}執導，[[李貞賢|李貞賢]]主演<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/news/kofic_news.jsp?blbdComCd=601007&seq=1671&mode=VIEW|title=Alice Works Earnestly in Cinemas|website=Korean Film Biz Zone|accessdate=2017-09-02|date=2015-08-14}}</ref>。本片於16屆[[全州國際電影節|全州國際電影節]]進行首映並大獲好評<ref>{{cite web|last=Conran|first=Pierce|url=http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/news/news.jsp?mode=VIEW&seq=3424|title=POET ON A BUSINESS TRIP and ALICE IN EARNESTLAND Triumph in Jeonju|website=Korean Film Biz Zone|accessdate=2017-09-02|date=2015-03-07}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Song|first=Soon-jin|url=http://koreanfilm.or.kr/webzine/sub/feature.jsp?mode=A_VIEW&wbSeq=252|title=Wrapping up the 16th Jeonju International Film Festival|website=Korean Cinema Today|accessdate=2017-09-02|date=2015-05-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=ALICE IN EARNESTLAND Hits 30,000 Admissions Mark|url=http://www.koreanfilm.or.kr/jsp/news/news.jsp?mode=VIEW&seq=3557|website=Korean Film Biz Zone|accessdate=2017-09-02|date=2015-08-26}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.koreastardaily.com/tc/photo/64524|title=李貞賢主演《誠實國度的愛麗絲》榮獲全州國際電影節大獎|publisher=韓星網|accessdate=2017-09-02|date=2015-07-06}}</ref>。本片講述了以為努力生活就能變幸福的秀南跌宕起伏的人生大逆轉<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.koreastardaily.com/tc/photo/66585|title=2NE1 Sandara、文瑾瑩捧場《誠實國度的愛麗絲》|publisher=韓星網|accessdate=2017-09-02|date=2015-08-11}}</ref>。

'''二氧化铑'''是一种无机化合物，化学式为RhO<sub>2</sub>。
==结构及性质==
二氧化铑属于[[四方晶系|四方晶系]]，有着[[金红石|金红石]]结构。<ref name=ssc>{{cite journal|author=R. D. Shannon|title=Synthesis and properties of two new members of the rutile family RhO<sub>2</sub> and PtO<sub>2</sub>|doi=10.1016/0038-1098(68)90019-7 |journal=Solid State Communications |volume=6|year=1968|pages=139–143}}</ref>

二氧化铑难溶于[[王水|王水]]。<ref name=muller>{{cite journal|author=O. Muller and R. Roy|title=Formation and stability of the platinum and rhodium oxides at high oxygen pressures and the structures of Pt<sub>3</sub>O<sub>4</sub>, β-PtO<sub>2</sub> and RhO<sub>2</sub>|doi=10.1016/0022-5088(68)90070-2 |journal=Journal of the Less Common Metals |volume=16|year=1968|pages=129–146}}</ref>

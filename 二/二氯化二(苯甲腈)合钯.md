'''二氯化二(苯甲腈)合钯'''是一种[[配位化合物|配位化合物]]，化学式为PdCl<sub>2</sub>(NCC<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>2</sub>，它是[[苯甲腈|苯甲腈]]（PhCN）配体和[[氯化钯|氯化钯]]形成的加合物。它是黄棕色固体，可溶于有机溶剂。它可用作反应试剂或催化剂，来参与需要可溶性Pd(II)的反应。<ref>{{cite encyclopedia|title=Bis(benzonitrile)dichloropalladium(II)|authors=Jiro Tsuji, Hao Guo, Shengming Ma, Daniela Sustac Roman|doi=10.1002/047084289X.rb101.pub3|year=2015|encyclopedia=e-EROS Encyclopedia of Reagents for Organic Synthesis}}</ref>

该配合物可以通过将PdCl<sub>2</sub>溶于温热的[[苯甲腈|苯甲腈]]制得。<ref>{{cite journal | title = Bis(Benzonitrile)Dichloro Complexes of Palladium and Platinum | pages =60–63 | journal = [[Inorg._Synth.|Inorg. Synth.]] | volume = 28 | author = Gordon K. Anderson, Minren Lin | doi = 10.1002/9780470132593.ch13 | year = 1990}}</ref>苯甲腈配体不稳定，它在非配位性溶剂中转化为PdCl<sub>2</sub>。[[X射线衍射|X射线衍射]]结果表明，两个PhCN配体互相为trans构型。<ref>{{cite journal|authors=Olmstead, M. M.; Wei, P.-P.; Ginwalla, A. S.; Balch, A. L.|title=Bis(Benzonitrile)Palladium(II) Dihalides:  Structures and Cocrystallization of the Cubic Cluster Pd<sub>6</sub>Cl<sub>12</sub> with (''E'')-Stilbene and with Bis(Benzonitrile)Palladium(II) Dichloride|journal=Inorganic Chemistry |year=2000|volume=39|pages=4555-4559|doi=10.1021/ic0000597}}</ref>

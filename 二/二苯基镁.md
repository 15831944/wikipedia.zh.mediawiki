'''二苯基镁'''是一种金属有机化合物，化学式为Mg(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>2</sub>，它在标准状况下是无色固体。它可由[[镁|镁]]和[[二苯基汞|二苯基汞]]反应得到。
: Mg + Hg(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>2</sub> → Mg(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + Hg
它和[[二甲基镁|二甲基镁]]有如下可逆反应：<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ja00828a019|issue=21|doi=10.1021/ja00828a019|pages=6658–6663|title=Molecular dynamics at equilibrium. Carbon-magnesium bond exchange in the system dineopentylmagnesium-diphenylmagnesium|volume=96|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|date=1974-10|issn=0002-7863|language=EN|accessdate=2018-08-06|author=Gideon Fraenkel, Simon H. Yu}}</ref>
: Mg(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + Mg(CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub> {{eqm}} CH<sub>3</sub>MgC<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>
而和[[氯化镁|氯化镁]]之间的可逆反应，在[[乙醚|乙醚]]溶剂中倾向于正向（化合）反应，而在[[四氢呋喃|四氢呋喃]]中倾向于逆向（分解）反应：<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/jp035653r|issue=1|doi=10.1021/jp035653r|pages=133–139|title=Theoretical Study of Magnesium Compounds:  The Schlenk Equilibrium in the Gas Phase and in the Presence of Et2O and THF Molecules|volume=108|journal=The Journal of Physical Chemistry A|date=2004-01|issn=1089-5639|language=en|accessdate=2018-08-06|author=Jaana Tammiku-Taul, Peeter Burk, Ants Tuulmets}}</ref>

: Mg(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + MgCl<sub>2</sub> {{eqm}} C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>MgCl

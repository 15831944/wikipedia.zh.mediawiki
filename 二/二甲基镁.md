'''二甲基镁'''是一种[[有机镁化合物|有机镁化合物]]，化学式为(CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub>Mg，为白色易自燃的固体。

==制备==
二甲基镁可以由不少于化学计量比的[[二噁烷|二噁烷]]和甲基卤化镁反应得到：<ref>{{cite journal | authorlink = Arthur C. Cope | last1 = Cope | first1 = A. C. | title = The Preparation of Dialkylmagnesium Compounds from Grignard Reagents | journal = [[J._Am._Chem._Soc.|J. Am. Chem. Soc.]] | year = 1935 | volume = 57 | issue = 11 | pages = 2238–2240 | doi = 10.1021/ja01314a059 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Anteunis | first1 = M. | title = Studies of the Grignard Reaction. II. Kinetics of the Reaction of Dimethylmagnesium with Benzophenone and of Methylmagnesium Bromide-Magnesium Bromide with Pinacolone | journal = [[J._Org._Chem.|J. Org. :2 CH<sub>3</sub>MgX  +  dioxane   {{eqm}}   (CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub>Mg  +  MgX<sub>2</sub>(dioxane)

二卤化镁和二恶烷的配合物是一种沉淀，可以使{{le|史兰克平衡|Schlenk equilibrium}}向生成二烷基镁（可溶于反应溶剂）的方向进行。

钙、镁以及[[碘甲烷|碘甲烷]]在[[乙醚|乙醚]]中反应，或者镁和[[二甲基汞|二甲基汞]]反应，也能得到二甲基镁。<ref>{{citation/core|Title=Houben-Weyl Methods of Organic Chemistry Vol. XIII/2a, 4th Edition Organometallic Compounds of Group II of the Periodic Table (except mercury)|Publisher=Georg Thieme Verlag|Year=2014|At=p. 215|ISBN=3-13-180654-0|Date=   2014|language=German|URL={{Google books|cSqGAwAAQBAJ||page=215}}

==结构==
二甲基镁的结构由X射线晶体衍射测定。它是一种聚合物，与[[二硫化硅|二硫化硅]]的结构类似，有着四面体镁中心，由甲基桥联。其中，Mg-C键键长为224 pm。<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Weiss | first1 = E. | title = Die Kristallstruktur des Dimethylmagnesiums | journal = [[J._Organomet._Chem.|J. Organomet. Chem.]] | year = 1964 | volume = 2 | issue = 4 | pages = 314–321 | doi = 10.1016/S0022-328X(00)82217-2 }}</ref>同族的二甲基铍有着同样的结构。<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Snow | first1 = A.I.|last2 = Rundle| first2=R.E.|title = Structure of Dimethylberyllium

'''二氯二氧化钼'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>。它是浅黄色可溶于水的潮解性固体，可用于制备其它钼化合物。它的加合物MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>(ether)<sub>2</sub>可溶于有机溶剂。二氯二氧化钼可用作一些氧化反应的催化剂。<ref>{{cite journal|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|volume=56|issue=17|language=en|issn=0020-1669|date=2017-09-05|pages=10570–10575|doi=10.1021/acs.inorgchem.7b01529|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.inorgchem.7b01529|title=[MoO 2 ] 2+ -Mediated Oxygen Atom Transfer via an Unusual Lewis Acid Mechanism|accessdate=2019-03-28|author=Marta Castiñeira Reis, Marta Marín-Luna, Carlos Silva López, Olalla Nieto Faza}}</ref>

==制备==
二氯二氧化钼可由[[三氧化钼|三氧化钼]]和浓[[盐酸|盐酸]]反应得到：<ref>{{cite journal||journal=Inorganic Syntheses|title=Dichlorodioxobis(Dimethylsulphoxide) Molybdenum(VI)|authors=Francisco J. Arnaiz|year=1997|volume=31|page=246-7|doi=10.1002/9780470132623.ch39}}</ref>
:MoO<sub>3</sub>  +  2 HCl  →  MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>  +  H<sub>2</sub>O

MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>也能以[[四氯一氧化钼|MoOCl<sub>4</sub>]]为原料制备：<ref>{{cite journal |author1=Gibson, V. C. |author2=Kee, T.  P. |author3=Shaw, A. | title = New, improved synthesis of the group 6 oxyhalides, W(O)Cl<sub>4</sub>, W(O)<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub> and Mo(O)<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub> | journal = [[Polyhedron_(journal)|Polyhedron]] | year = 1988 | volume = 7 | issue = 7 | pages = 579–80 | doi = 10.1016/S0277-5387(00)86336-6}}</ref>
:MoOCl<sub>4</sub>  +  O(Si(CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub>  →  MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>  +  2 ClSi(CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>

二氧化钼于500~700 °C和氯气反应<ref name=RRS/>，或者[[五氯化钼|五氯化钼]]在空气中加热也能得到产物：
:MoO<sub>2</sub>  +  Cl<sub>2</sub>  →  MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>
:2 MoCl<sub>5</sub> + 2 O<sub>2</sub> → 2 MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub> + 3 Cl<sub>2</sub>

MoO<sub>3</sub>和CCl<sub>4</sub>在713~753 K反应，可以得到二氯二氧化钼，气态产物包括[[二氧化碳|二氧化碳]]、[[氯气|氯气]]和[[光气|光气]]。<ref>Del Carmen Ruiz, Maria; Rivarola, Juan B.; Quiroga, Oscar D. Chlorination of molybdenum trioxide with carbon tetrachloride. ''Latin American Applied Research'', 1990. 20 (2): 107-111. ISSN: 0327-0793.</ref>

==化学性质==
MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>可以形成一系列的[[加合物|加合物]]，例如MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>(THF)<sub>2</sub>。它和大分子[[苯胺|苯胺]]反应，生成二亚胺配合物MoCl<sub>2</sub>(NAr)<sub>2</sub>([[乙二醇二甲醚|L]])，这种配合物是Schrock型卡宾Mo(OR)<sub>2</sub>(NAr)(CH-t-Bu)的前驱体。<ref name=RRS>{{cite journal|authors=Schrock, R. R.; Murdzek, J. S.; Bazan, G. C.; Robbins, J.; DiMare, M.; O'Regan, M.|title=Synthesis of molybdenum imido alkylidene complexes and some Reactions Involving Acyclic Olefins|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|year=1990|volume=112|pages=3875-3886|doi=10.1021/ja00166a023}}</ref>

MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>可以和干燥的[[氟化氢|氟化氢]]或[[三氧化硫|三氧化硫]][[磺酰氯|磺酰氯]]溶液反应：
: MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub> + 2 HF → MoO<sub>2</sub>F<sub>2</sub> + 2 HCl
: MoO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub> + 3 SO<sub>3</sub> → MoO(SO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + SO<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>

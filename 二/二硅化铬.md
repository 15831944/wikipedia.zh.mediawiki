'''二硅化铬'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为CrSi<sub>2</sub>。二硅化铬可由无水[[氯化亚铬|氯化亚铬]]作为前体，在高温下与[[硅|硅]]反应制得。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/jp071707b|issue=26|doi=10.1021/jp071707b|pages=9072–9076|title=Synthesis and Electrical Properties of Single Crystalline CrSi2Nanowires|volume=111|journal=The Journal of Physical Chemistry C|date=2007-07|issn=1932-7447|language=en|accessdate=2018-06-21|author=Kwanyong Seo, Kumar S. K. Varadwaj, Dongkyu Cha, Juneho In, Jiyoung Kim, Jeunghee Park, Bongsoo Kim}}</ref>用金属[[钾|钾]]在高温下还原[[氯化铬|氯化铬]]和[[氟硅酸钠|氟硅酸钠]]的混合物也能得到二硅化铬。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0925838803013951|issue=1-2|doi=10.1016/j.jallcom.2003.12.015|pages=176–179|title=Synthesis and thermal stability of nanocrystalline chromium disilicide|volume=376|journal=Journal of Alloys and Compounds|date=2004-08|issn=0925-8388|accessdate=2018-06-21|author=Jianhua Ma, Yunle Gu, Liang Shi, Luyang Chen, Zeheng Yang, Yitai Qian}}</ref>

'''二硒化铂'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为PtSe<sub>2</sub>。它是层状物质，可以分成薄层至三原子厚的单层。厚度不同，PtSe<sub>2</sub>可以展现出[[半金属|半金属]]或[[半导体|半导体]]特性。

二硒化铂在自然界中以硒铂矿（Sudovikovite）的形式存在，该矿以俄罗斯石油学家N.G. Sudovikov（1903 - 1966）命名。矿石的硬度在2到2.5之间。硒铂矿发现于俄罗斯[[卡累利阿共和国|卡累利阿共和国]]的Zaonezhie半岛的Velikaya Guba铀钒矿床的Srednyaya Padma矿区中。<ref>{{cite web|title=Sudovikovite: Sudovikovite mineral information and data.|url=https://www.mindat.org/min-7321.html|website=www.mindat.org|accessdate=2017-03-19}}</ref>
==制备==
在1909年，Minozzi第一个报道了通过相应的元素[[单质|单质]]制备二硒化铂。<ref name="gron"/>

将铂薄箔在400℃时与[[硒|硒]]蒸气加热可以得到二硒化铂。<ref name=ultra/><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Yim|first1=Chanyoung|last2=Lee|first2=Kangho|last3=McEvoy|first3=Niall|last4=O’Brien|first4=Maria|last5=Riazimehr|first5=Sarah|last6=Berner|first6=Nina C.|last7=Cullen|first7=Conor P.|last8=Kotakoski|first8=Jani|last9=Meyer|first9=Jannik C.|last10=Lemme|first10=Max C.|last11=Duesberg|first11=Georg S.|title=High-Performance Hybrid Electronic Devices from Layered PtSe2 Films Grown at Low Temperature|journal=ACS Nano|date=2016-10-25|volume=10|issue=10|pages=9550–9558|doi=10.1021/acsnano.6b04898}}</ref>

铂的[[密勒指数|111晶面]]暴露于硒蒸气中，270℃可以形成单层PtSe<sub>2</sub>.<ref name=wang/>

除了这些硒化法外，PtSe<sub>2</sub>也可以通过在水溶液中用[[硒化氢|硒化氢]]处理Pt(IV)得到，或者是加热[[四氯化铂|四氯化铂]]和[[硒|硒]]的混合物。<ref name="gron">{{cite journal|last1=Grønvold|first1=Fredrik|last2=Haakon|first2=Haraldsen|last3=Kjekshus|first3=Arne|title=On the sulfides, selenides and tellurides of platinum|journal=[[Acta_Chemica_Scandinavica|Acta Chemica Scandinavica]]|date=1960|volume=14|issue=9|url=http://actachemscand.org/pdf/acta_vol_14_p1879-1893.pdf}}{{open access}}</ref>

==性质==
二硒化铂的晶体结构为[[碘化镉|CdI<sub>2</sub>]]，形成的是层状结构。每个单层有铂原子中心，硒原子在铂的上方和下方。这种结构也称为“1T”，属于三角[[晶系|晶系]]。每层仅由弱键结合在一起，因此可以将片剥离为双层或单层。<ref name=raman>{{cite journal|last1=O’Brien|first1=Maria|last2=McEvoy|first2=Niall|last3=Motta|first3=Carlo|last4=Zheng|first4=Jian-Yao|last5=Berner|first5=Nina C|last6=Kotakoski|first6=Jani|last7=Elibol|first7=Kenan|last8=Pennycook|first8=Timothy J|last9=Meyer|first9=Jannik C|last10=Yim|first10=Chanyoung|last11=Abid|first11=Mohamed|last12=Hallam|first12=Toby|last13=Donegan|first13=John F|last14=Sanvito|first14=Stefano|last15=Duesberg|first15=Georg S|title=Raman characterization of platinum diselenide thin films|journal=2D Materials|date=2016-04-13|volume=3|issue=2|pages=021004|doi=10.1088/2053-1583/3/2/021004}}</ref>

块状的PtSe<sub>2</sub>是半金属材料，单层是半导体材料。<ref name=raman/>块状材料的电导率为620,000 S/m。<ref name="fets">{{cite journal|last1=Zhao|first1=Yuda|last2=Qiao|first2=Jingsi|last3=Yu|first3=Zhihao|last4=Yu|first4=Peng|last5=Xu|first5=Kang|last6=Lau|first6=Shu Ping|last7=Zhou|first7=Wu|last8=Liu|first8=Zheng|last9=Wang|first9=Xinran|last10=Ji|first10=Wei|last11=Chai|first11=Yang|title=High-Electron-Mobility and Air-Stable 2D Layered PtSe2 FETs|journal=Advanced Materials|date=February 2017|volume=29|issue=5|pages=1604230|doi=10.1002/adma.201604230}}</ref>

XPS光谱显示了在Pt 4f处有72.3eV的峰，在Pt 5p<sub>3/2</sub>有55.19和54.39eV的峰<ref name=raman/>；Se 3d<sub>3/2</sub>和3d<sub>5/2</sub>分别有55.19和54.39eV的峰。<ref name=wang>{{cite journal|last1=Wang|first1=Yeliang|last2=Li|first2=Linfei|last3=Yao|first3=Wei|last4=Song|first4=Shiru|last5=Sun|first5=J. T.|last6=Pan|first6=Jinbo|last7=Ren|first7=Xiao|last8=Li|first8=Chen|last9=Okunishi|first9=Eiji|last10=Wang|first10=Yu-Qi|last11=Wang|first11=Eryin|last12=Shao|first12=Yan|last13=Zhang|first13=Y. Y.|last14=Yang|first14=Hai-tao|last15=Schwier|first15=Eike F.|last16=Iwasawa|first16=Hideaki|last17=Shimada|first17=Kenya|last18=Taniguchi|first18=Masaki|last19=Cheng|first19=Zhaohua|last20=Zhou|first20=Shuyun|last21=Du|first21=Shixuan|last22=Pennycook|first22=Stephen J.|last23=Pantelides|first23=Sokrates T.|last24=Gao|first24=Hong-Jun|title=Monolayer PtSe2, a New Semiconducting Transition-Metal-Dichalcogenide, Epitaxially Grown by Direct Selenization of Pt|journal=Nano Letters|date=2015-06-10|volume=15|issue=6|pages=4013–4018|doi=10.1021/acs.nanolett.5b00964}}</ref>

声子振动由红外活性A<sub>2u</sub>（Se在Pt面外振动）、E<sub>u</sub>（层振动，Se相对于Pt）和拉曼活性A<sub>1g</sub>（Se顶部和底部原子沿相反方向205cm<sup>-1</sup>移动）、E<sub>g</sub>（在平面Se原子相对于顶部和底部移动175cm<sup>−1</sup>）。在拉曼光谱中，当测量与入射光线垂直偏振的受激发射时，A<sub>1g</sub>被减弱。堆叠更多的层时，E<sub>g</sub>模式发生红移。（双层材料为166cm<sup>−1</sup>，块状材料为155cm<sup>−1</sup>）。当厚度变化时，A<sub>lg</sub>排放仅略有变化。<ref name=raman/>

对于单层，带隙为1.2eV（计算值），双层的为0.21eV（计算值）。对于三层或更厚的物质，会失去一个带隙并变为半金属性质。<ref name=wang/>

PtSe<sub>2</sub>可以在特定气体（如[[二氧化氮|二氧化氮]]）存在下改变其电导性。在几秒钟内，NO<sub>2</sub>吸附在PtSe<sub>2</sub>材料的表面，降低了电阻。当气体不存在时，大约一分钟内会回到高电阻的状态。<ref name=ultra>{{cite web|title=Ultra-fast, ultra-sensitive PtSe2 gas sensors|url=https://phys.org/news/2017-01-ultra-fast-ultra-sensitive-ptse2-gas-sensors.html|website=PhysOrg|accessdate=2017-03-17|date=2017-01-13}}</ref>PtSe<sub>2</sub>的{{le|塞贝克系数|Seebeckcoefficient}}为40 μV/K。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Hulliger|first1=F.|title=Electrical properties of some nickel-group chalcogenides|journal=Journal of Physics and Chemistry of Solids|date=March 1965|volume=26|issue=3|pages=639–645|doi=10.1016/0022-3697(65)90140-X}}</ref>

虽然二硒化铂是非磁性的，但是当存在高达10%的铂空位并且其被置于5％的应变下时，它预计将变为铁磁性。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Zulfiqar|first1=Muhammad|last2=Zhao|first2=Yinchang|last3=Li|first3=Geng|last4=Nazir|first4=Safdar|last5=Ni|first5=Jun|title=Tunable Conductivity and Half Metallic Ferromagnetism in Monolayer Platinum Diselenide: A First-Principles Study|journal=The Journal of Physical Chemistry C|date=2016-11-03|volume=120|issue=43|pages=25030–25036|doi=10.1021/acs.jpcc.6b06999}}</ref>

二硒化铂的单层显示螺旋旋转纹理，对诸如此类的[[中心对称图形|中心对称]]材料来说，这一性质并不是我们想要的。这个性质可能是由于局部偶极诱导的{{le|拉什巴效应|Rashba effect}}，这意味着PtSe<sub>2</sub>是潜在的[[自旋电子|自旋电子]]材料。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Yao|first1=Wei|last2=Wang|first2=Eryin|last3=Huang|first3=Huaqing|last4=Deng|first4=Ke|last5=Yan|first5=Mingzhe|last6=Zhang|first6=Kenan|last7=Miyamoto|first7=Koji|last8=Okuda|first8=Taichi|last9=Li|first9=Linfei|last10=Wang|first10=Yeliang|last11=Gao|first11=Hongjun|last12=Liu|first12=Chaoxing|last13=Duan|first13=Wenhui|last14=Zhou|first14=Shuyun|title=Direct observation of spin-layer locking by local Rashba effect in monolayer semiconducting PtSe2 film|journal=Nature Communications|date=2017-01-31|volume=8|pages=14216|doi=10.1038/ncomms14216}}</ref>

==化学反应==
目前已知的是[[水|水]]和[[氧|氧]]在室温下仅与二硒化铂发生[[吸附|物理吸附]]，所对应的能量分别为−0.19 eV和−0.13 eV，远低于室温下发生反应所需的能量。<ref name="fets"/>

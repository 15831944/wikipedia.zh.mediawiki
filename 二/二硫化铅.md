S. | title=High-pressure (70-kilobar) Synthesis of New Crystalline Lead Dichalcogenides| journal=[[无机化学_(期刊)|Inorganic Chemistry]]|date= 1966|volume= 5|issue=11| pages=2067–9 | doi=10.1021/ic50045a056}}</ref>{{citation needed|PbS<sub>2</sub>与[[二硫化锡|二硫化锡]]（SnS<sub>2</sub>）类似，晶型为[[碘化镉|碘化镉]]型，因此可以证明铅是+4价的。}}

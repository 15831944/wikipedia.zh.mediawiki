'''二乙胺盐酸盐'''是{{le|二乙胺|diethylamine}}和[[盐酸|盐酸]]形成的盐，化学式为C<sub>4</sub>H<sub>12</sub>NCl，是可燃性具有氨味的吸湿性固体，易溶于水。<ref>Entry to [http://gestis.itrust.de/nxt/gateway.dll?f=id$t=default.htm$vid=gestisdeu:sdbdeu$id=491029 CAS no. 660-68-4] in the GESTIS Substance [http://gestis.itrust.de/nxt/gateway.dll?f=id$t=default.htm$vid=gestisdeu:sdbdeu$id=491029 Database] of the IFA , retrieved on October 24, 2011 .</ref>它可用于有机合成。<ref>[https://www.chemicalbook.com/ProductChemicalPropertiesCB6854087.htm 二乙胺盐酸盐]. ChemicalBook. [2019-1-15]</ref>

==制备==
二乙胺盐酸盐可由二乙胺和盐酸反应得到。<ref>American Chemical Society: Journal of the American Chemical Society, Volume 79, page 1091 . 1957th</ref>

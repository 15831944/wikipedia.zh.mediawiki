'''二磷酸腺苷核糖基化'''（{{lang-en|ADP-ribosylation}}，'''ADP-核糖基化'''）是将额外的单个或多个[[二磷酸腺苷核糖|二磷酸腺苷核糖]]（ADP核糖）基团添加到[[蛋白质|蛋白质]]的氨基酸残基上的[[轉譯后修饰|轉譯后修饰]]过程<ref>{{Cite journal|title=NAD+ metabolism in health and disease|url=http://www.dartmouth.edu/~brenner/belenky07a.pdf|journal=Trends Biochem. Sci.|issue=1|doi=10.1016/j.tibs.2006.11.006|year=2007|volume=32|pages=12–9|pmid=17161604}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=New functions of a long-known molecule. Emerging roles of NAD in cellular signaling|author=Ziegler M|journal=Eur. J. Biochem.|issue=6|doi=10.1046/j.1432-1327.2000.01187.x|year=2000|volume=267|pages=1550–64|pmid=10712584}}</ref>。这一过程可逆，涉及许多细胞中的过程，包括[[细胞信号传送|细胞信号传送]]、[[DNA修復|DNA修復]]、基因表达调控和[[细胞凋亡|细胞凋亡]]<ref>{{Cite journal|title=The new life of a centenarian: signalling functions of NAD(P)|journal=Trends Biochem. Sci.|issue=3|doi=10.1016/j.tibs.2004.01.007|year=2004|volume=29|pages=111–8|pmid=15003268}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=NEW EMBO MEMBER'S REVIEW: Functional aspects of protein mono-ADP-ribosylation|journal=EMBO J.|issue=9|doi=10.1093/emboj/cdg209|year=2003|volume=22|pages=1953–8|pmc=156081|pmid=12727863}}</ref>。

异常的ADP-核糖基化已被证明和一些癌症相关<ref name="Scarpa">{{Cite journal|title=. A role in intracellular mono-ADP-ribosylation in cancer biology|journal=FEBS Journal|doi=10.1111/febs.12290|year=2013|volume=280|pages=3551–3562}}</ref>。它也是來自细菌的毒素之致病机理，如[[霍乱毒素|霍乱毒素]]、[[白喉毒素|白喉毒素]]等<ref name="Krueger">{{Cite journal|title=The family of bacterial ADP-ribosylating exotoxins.|last=Krueger|first=KM|last2=Barbieri|first2=JT|date=January 1995|journal=Clinical Microbiology Reviews|issue=1|volume=8|pages=34–47|pmc=172848|pmid=7704894}}</ref>。

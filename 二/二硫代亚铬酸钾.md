'''二硫代亚铬酸钾'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为KCrS<sub>2</sub>。

==制备及性质==
它可由[[碳酸钾|碳酸钾]]、[[三氧化二铬|三氧化二铬]]在[[硫化氢|硫化氢]]气氛中加热反应得到：
::<math>\mathsf{ K_2CO_3 + Cr_2O_3 + 4H_2S \ \xrightarrow{800-900^oC}\ 2KCrS_2 + CO_2 + 4H_2O }</math>

在800~900 °C加热[[铬|铬]]、[[硫|硫]]和[[碳酸钾|碳酸钾]]的混合物<ref>{{cite journal|title=KCrS<sub>2</sub> Cathode with Considerable Cyclability and High Rate Performance: The First K+ Stoichiometric Layered Compound for Potassium-Ion Batteries|journal=Small|volume=14|issue=49|pages=1803495|doi=10.1002/smll.201803495|url=https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/smll.201803495|date=2018|issn=1613-6829|language=en|accessdate=2019-02-05|author=Nirmalesh Naveen, Woon Bae Park, Satendra Pal Singh, Su Cheol Han, Docheon Ahn, Kee-Sun Sohn, Myoungho Pyo}}</ref>，或者[[重铬酸钾|重铬酸钾]]在[[氢气|氢气]]-[[硫化氢|硫化氢]]气氛中于600 °C还原，都能得到KCrS<sub>2</sub>。<ref name=Laar>{{cite journal|title=The magnetic structure of KCrS<sub>2</sub>|volume=6|issue=3|pages=384–386|date=1973-03-01|issn=0022-4596|doi=10.1016/0022-4596(73)90227-2|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0022459673902272|journal=Journal of Solid State Chemistry|accessdate=2019-02-05|author=B. Van Laar, F. M. R. Engelsman}}</ref>

它是四方晶系的晶体，空间群R{{overline|3}}m，晶胞参数a=0.3618 nm, c=2.116 nm。它是极易潮解的铁磁性物质，居里点112 K。<ref>{{книга

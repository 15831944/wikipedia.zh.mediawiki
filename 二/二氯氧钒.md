'''二氯氧钒'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为VOCl<sub>2</sub>。

==制备==
二氯氧钒可由[[三氯氧钒|三氯氧钒]]与[[硫|硫]]<ref>Paul, Ram Chand; Sharma, Naresh C.; Singh, Shamsher; Sharma, Ashok K. Vanadium(IV) oxodichloride. Preparation and a study of its solvolytic reactions. ''Proceedings of the Indian National Science Academy, Part A: Physical Sciences'', 1974. 40 (1): 64-75</ref>或[[硫化氢|硫化氢]]<ref>Yajima, Akimasa; Matsuzaki, Ryoko; Saeki, Yuzo. Reaction between vanadium trichloride oxide and hydrogen sulfide. ''Bulletin of the Chemical Society of Japan'', 1978. 51 (4): 1098-1100. DOI:10.1246/bcsj.51.1098</ref>的还原反应制得。
: 2 VOCl<sub>3</sub> + 2 S → 2 VOCl<sub>2</sub> + S<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>
: 2 VOCl<sub>3</sub> + H<sub>2</sub>S → 2 VOCl<sub>2</sub> + S + 2 HCl
:: <small>2 VOCl<sub>3</sub> + S<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub> → 2 VCl<sub>4</sub> + S + SO<sub>2</sub>（副反应）</small>
:: <small>2 VCl<sub>4</sub> + H<sub>2</sub>S → 2 VCl<sub>3</sub> + S + 2 HCl</small>

'''二硒代亚铬酸钾'''是一种无机化合物，化学式为KCrSe<sub>2</sub>。它是一种[[反铁磁性|反铁磁性]]化合物，居里温度Θ为250 K，有序温度T<sub>N</sub>为40 K。<ref>{{cite journal|doi=10.1088/0953-8984/8/24/007|url=https://doi.org/10.1088%2F0953-8984%2F8%2F24%2F007|date=1996-6|volume=8|issue=24|pages=4381–4388|title=Electronic structure and magnetic properties of <math>{\mathrm{KCrSe}}_{2}</math>|journal=Journal of Physics: Condensed Matter|issn=0953-8984|language=en|accessdate=2019-02-05|author=C. M. Fang, P. R. Tolsma, C. F. van Bruggen, R. A. de Groot, G. A. Wiegers, C. Haas}}</ref>该化合物可由[[氰化钾|氰化钾]]与[[三氧化二铬|三氧化二铬]]和[[硒|硒]]共熔得到。它和水与二氧化碳反应，生成[[碳酸钾|碳酸钾]]、[[硒化铬|硒化铬]]和水。<ref>{{cite journal|title=The crystal structure of sodium selenochromite, NaCrSe<sub>2</sub>, and preliminary investigations on related compounds|journal=Acta Crystallographica|volume=1|pages=196–200|date=1948-09-02|doi=10.1107/S0365110X48000545|issn=0365-110X|language=en|issue=4|url=http://scripts.iucr.org/cgi-bin/paper?S0365110X48000545|accessdate=2019-02-05|author=A. Scherhaufer, W. R. Ruston, W. Rüdorff}}</ref>

'''二月九日杀手'''（[[英文|英文]]：February 9 Killer）是一名身份不明的[[连环杀手|连环杀手]]。<ref name="Fox13Now">[http://www.fox13now.com/news/kstu-feb-9-serial-killer,0,4132258.story "Police searching for Feb. 9 serial killer"] ''Fox13Now'', 5 February 2011 {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110209015156/http://www.fox13now.com/news/kstu-feb-9-serial-killer,0,4132258.story |date=February 9, 2011 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.fox13now.com/videobeta/?watchId=57b8b12a-f71c-44c4-af1b-fa1ca3fc853d "Police seek help in Feb. 9 killer"] ''Fox13Now'' {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110723203753/http://www.fox13now.com/videobeta/?watchId=57b8b12a-f71c-44c4-af1b-fa1ca3fc853d |date=July 23, 2011 }}</ref><ref name="CNN"/><ref name="ABC 4">{{cite web | title = UNSOLVED CASES: Who killed Sonia Mejia? | work = abc4.com | publisher =  | date = 25 October 2010 | url = http://www.abc4.com/content/news/unsolved_cases/story/UNSOLVED-CASES-Who-killed-Sonia-Mejia/DnXoFTIssUyXg6XHJ--2Bw.cspx | accessdate =  | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120403011212/http://www.abc4.com/content/news/unsolved_cases/story/UNSOLVED-CASES-Who-killed-Sonia-Mejia/DnXoFTIssUyXg6XHJ--2Bw.cspx | archivedate = 2012年4月3日 }}</ref>相信他是美国犹他州[[盐湖县|盐湖县]]2006年的索妮娅·梅嘉谋杀案，以及2008年的达米阿那·卡斯蒂略谋杀案的凶手。由于两起谋杀案均发生于同一日期，2月9日，其因此得名二月九日杀手。

==侦查==
于2009年，发生于犹他州泰勒斯维尔的索妮娅·梅嘉谋杀案和发生于犹他州西瓦利城的达米阿那·卡斯蒂略谋杀案通过DNA分析被联系起来。与此同时，相应的警察部门公布了一份嫌疑人描述：一位十几岁或二十岁出头的西班牙裔男性。<ref name="CNN">{{cite web | last = Hanna | first = Jason | title = Utah cities on alert as anniversary of 2 slayings hits | work = CNN Justice | publisher =  | date = 9 February 2009 | url = http://articles.cnn.com/2009-02-09/justice/utah.killings.anniversary_1_slayings-serial-killer-forensic-evidence?_s=PM:CRIME | accessdate =  | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121006102802/http://articles.cnn.com/2009-02-09/justice/utah.killings.anniversary_1_slayings-serial-killer-forensic-evidence?_s=PM%3ACRIME | archivedate = 2012年10月6日 }}</ref>此后，一个由二十人组成的特别调查组成立。

截至2011年，该起案件被列为{{tsl|en|cold case|冷案}}。<ref name="Fox13Now"/>

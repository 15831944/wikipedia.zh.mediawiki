'''二环己基甲酮'''是一种[[有机化合物|有机化合物]]，化学式为C<sub>13</sub>H<sub>22</sub>O，该化合物可看作丙酮的[[甲基|甲基]]替换为环己基。二环己基甲酮可燃，其燃烧产物为碳氧化物。<ref>[https://www.sigmaaldrich.com/MSDS/MSDS/DisplayMSDSPage.do?country=CN&language=zh&productNumber=D80401&brand=ALDRICH&PageToGoToURL=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sigmaaldrich.com%2Fcatalog%2Fproduct%2Faldrich%2Fd80401%3Flang%3Dzh 二环己基甲酮 化学品安全技术说明书]. SigmaAldrich. [2018-7-16]</ref>

==合成==
二环己基甲酮可以由环己酰氯和环己基溴化镁反应得到。<ref>Mousseron, Max; Granger, Robert. Alicyclic series. VI. The organomagnesium compounds. ''Bulletin de la Societe Chimique de France''. 1946. 251-256.</ref>环己腈和[[格氏试剂|格氏试剂]]的反应也能得到二环己基甲酮。<ref>Canonne, Persephone; Foscolos, George B.; Lemay, Gilles. [https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0040403900714006?via%3Dihub Effet du benzene dans la reaction de Grignard sur les nitriles]{{fr}}. ''Tetrahedron Letters'', 1980. 21 (2): 155-158. {{DOI|10.1016/S0040-4039(00)71400-6}}</ref>

==性质==
二环己基甲酮的羰基可以和三乙基硅烷在[[二氯甲烷|二氯甲烷]]中反应，在一种苯并喹啉茂基铱催化剂的存在下，生成(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>11</sub>)<sub>2</sub>CH-O-Si(C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>3</sub>。<ref>Hamdaoui, Mustapha; Desrousseaux, Camille; Habbita, Houda; Djukic, Jean-Pierre. Iridacycles as Catalysts for the Autotandem Conversion of Nitriles into Amines by Hydrosilylation: Experimental Investigation and Scope. ''Organometallics'', 2017. 36 (24): 4864-4882. {{DOI|10.1021/acs.organomet.7b00749}}</ref>它也可以和[[苯肼|苯肼]]盐酸盐在[[乙酸|乙酸]]中回流，制得含氮杂环化合物：<ref>Bakulina, Olga; Ivanov, Alexander; Suslonov, Vitalii; Dar'in, Dmitry; Krasavin, Mikhail. A speedy route to sterically encumbered, benzene-​fused derivatives of privileged, naturally occurring hexahydropyrrolo[1,​2-​b]​isoquinoline. ''Beilstein Journal of Organic Chemistry'', 2017 (13): 1413-1424. {{DOI|10.3762/bjoc.13.138}}</ref>
:[[File:Dicyclohexyl_ketone_+_phenylhydrazine.png|525px]]
它在铁催化剂（如[[乙酰丙酮铁|乙酰丙酮铁]]）的存在下可以被三乙基硼氢化钠还原为相应的醇。<ref>Tamang, Sem Raj; Findlater, Michael. Iron Catalyzed Hydroboration of Aldehydes and Ketones. ''Journal of Organic Chemistry'', 2017. 82 (23): 12857-12862. {{DOI|10.1021/acs.joc.7b02020}}</ref>以锂粉-萘-无水[[氯化镍|氯化镍]]作为还原体系，在[[四氢呋喃|四氢呋喃]]溶剂中对其进行还原，在室温下便可得到相应的醇。<ref>Alonso, Francisco; Candela, Pablo; Gomez, Cecilia; Yus, Miguel. The NiCl2-​Li-​arene (cat.) combination as reducing system, part 9: Catalytic hydrogenation of organic compounds using the NiCl2-​Li-​(naphthalene or polymer-​supported naphthalene) (cat.) combination. ''Advanced Synthesis & Catalysis''. 2003, 345 (1+2): 275-279. {{DOI|10.1002/adsc.200390022}}.</ref>
:[[File:Dicyclohexyl_ketone_reduction.png|335px]]
甲基溴化镁与其作用得到的是1,1-二环己基乙醇。<ref>Son, Eun-Kyung; Kang, Jae-Hyun; Kim, Deog-Bae; Kim, Jae-Hyun. Chemically amplified polymer having pendant group with dicyclohexyl for photoresist composition. ''PCT Int. Appl.'', 2004. WO 2004042477.</ref>

二环己基甲酮在5~10 °C和[[三溴化磷|三溴化磷]]进行溴化反应，得到二溴代产物二(1-溴环己基)甲酮。<ref>Montana, Angel M.; Barcia, Joan A.; Grima, Pedro M.; Kociok-Kohn, Gabriele. New methodology for the synthesis of tetrahydrofuro[3,​2-​b]​furan-​2(3H)​-​one derivatives, synthons of natural products with biological interest. ''Tetrahedron'', 2016. 72 (43). 6794-6806. {{DOI|10.1016/j.tet.2016.09.004}}</ref>

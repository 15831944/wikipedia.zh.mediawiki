'''二硫代草酰胺'''是一种[[有机化合物|有机化合物]]。化学式为C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>4</sub>N<sub>2</sub>S<sub>2</sub>。有文献报道其分解温度为140 °C<ref>Hoppe, H.; Archiv der Pharmazie (Weinheim, Germany) 1975, V308(7), P526-41 CAPLUS</ref>，也有文献指出其分解温度为170 °C<ref>"PhysProp" data were obtained from Syracuse Research Corporation of Syracuse, New York (US). Retrieved from SciFinder.</ref>

==制备==
二硫代草酰胺可由[[草酰胺|草酰胺]]和[[劳森试剂|劳森试剂]]在无水[[乙腈|乙腈]]反应得到。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/10426509808036982|issue=1|pages=147–153|title=STUDIES ON REACTIONS OF LAWESSON′S REAGENT WITH PHENYLTHIOUREA AND OXAMIDE|volume=132|journal=Phosphorus, Sulfur, and Silicon and the Related Elements|date=January 1998|issn=1042-6507|author=Liang-Nian He, Tian-Bao Huang, Fei Cai, Ru-W Chen|doi=10.1080/10426509808036982}}</ref>

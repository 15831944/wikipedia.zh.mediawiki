'''二氧化钼'''是+4价[[钼|钼]]的[[氧化物|氧化物]]，化学式为MoO<sub>2</sub>。它是紫色的金属导体。

它在自然界以罕见的{{le|秋格瑞诺夫矿|tugarinovite}}的形式存在。
==制备==
二氧化钼可以由下述方法制备：
*[[钼|钼]]在800°C还原[[三氧化钼|三氧化钼]]：
:2 MoO<sub>3</sub>  +  Mo  <nowiki>→</nowiki>  3 MoO<sub>2</sub>
*[[氢气|氢气]]或[[氨|氨]]在470°C下还原[[三氧化钼|三氧化钼]]。<ref>{{Cotton&Wilkinson6th}}</ref>

单晶可通过[[碘|碘]]参与的[[化学转移反应|化学转移反应]]制备。碘和MoO<sub>2</sub>可逆地结合，产生挥发性的MoO<sub>2</sub>I<sub>2</sub>来使转移进行。<ref>Conroy, L. E.; Ben-Dor, L. "Molybdenum(IV) Oxide and Tungsten(IV) Oxides Single-Crystals" Inorganic Syntheses 1995, volume 30, pp. 105–107. {{ISBN|0-471-30508-1}}</ref>

氧化钼是“工业氧化钼”的成分之一，它在[[二硫化钼|MoS<sub>2</sub>]]的冶炼中产生：<ref>''Metallurgical furnaces'' Jorg Grzella, Peter Sturm, Joachim Kruger, Markus A. Reuter, Carina Kogler, Thomas Probst,  Ullmans Encyclopedia of Industrial Chemistry</ref><ref>"Thermal Analysis and Kinetics of Oxidation of Molybdenum Sulfides" Y. Shigegaki, S.K. Basu, M.Wakihara and M. Taniguchi, J. Therm. Analysis 34 (1988), 1427-1440</ref>

:2 MoS<sub>2</sub> + 7O<sub>2</sub>   <nowiki>→</nowiki> 2MoO<sub>3</sub> + 4SO<sub>2</sub>
:MoS<sub>2</sub> + 6MoO<sub>3</sub>  <nowiki>→</nowiki> 7MoO<sub>2</sub> + 2SO<sub>2</sub>
:2 MoO<sub>2</sub> + O<sub>2</sub>    <nowiki>→</nowiki> 2MoO<sub>3</sub>

==用途==
MoO<sub>2</sub>可用作[[醇|醇]]类的脱氢的催化剂<ref>A. A. Balandin and I. D. Rozhdestvenskaya, Russian Chemical Bulletin, 8, 11, (1959), 1573 {{doi|10.1007/BF00914749 }}</ref>，也可用作[[碳氢化合物|碳氢化合物]]<ref>''Molybdenum based catalysts. I. MoO<sub>2</sub> as the active species in the reforming of hydrocarbons'' A. Katrib, P. Leflaive, L. Hilaire and G. Maire Catalysis Letters, 38, 1–2, (1996) {{doi|10.1007/BF00806906}}</ref>或[[生物柴油|生物柴油]]<ref>Catalytic partial oxidation of a biodiesel surrogate over molybdenum dioxide, C.M. Cuba-Torres, et al, Fuel (2015), {{doi|10.1016/j.fuel.2015.01.003}}</ref>的重整催化剂。通过还原在石墨上沉积的二氧化钼，可以制得钼纳米线。<ref>''Synthesis of Molybdenum Nanowires with Millimeter-Scale Lengths Using Electrochemical Step Edge Decoration'' M. P. Zach, K. Inazu, K. H. Ng, J. C. Hemminger, and R. M. Penner Chem. Mater.

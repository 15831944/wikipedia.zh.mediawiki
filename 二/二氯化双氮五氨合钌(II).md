'''二氯化双氮五氨合钌(II)'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为[Ru(NH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>5</sub>(N<sub>2</sub>)]Cl<sub>2</sub>。它是近似无色的固体，但形成的溶液为黄色。它作为第一个N<sub>2</sub>键接在金属中心的配合物而受到很大关注。<ref>Alen. A. D.; Senoff, C. V. (1965) "Nitrogenopentammineruthenium(II) complexes" ''Chemical Communications'' 24: 621-2. {{doi|10.1039/C19650000621}}</ref><ref>Senoff, C.V. (1990) "The discovery of [Ru(NH<sub>3</sub>)5NN<sub>2</sub>]<sup>2+</sup>: A case of serendipity and the scientific method" ''Journal of Chemical Education'', volume 67: 368. {{doi|10.1021/ed067p368}}</ref>[Ru(NH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>5</sub>(N<sub>2</sub>)]<sup>2+</sup>采取具有C<sub>4v</sub>对称性的八面体结构。<ref>Fergusson, J. E.; Love, J. L.; Robinson, W. T. (1972) "The Crystal and Molecular Structure of  Dinitrogenpentaammineosmium(II) Chloride, [Os(NH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>5</sub>N<sub>2</sub>]Cl<sub>2</sub>, and Related Ruthenium Complexes" ''Inorg. Chem.'', 11, pp. 1662–1666. {{DOI|10.1021/ic50113a042}}</ref>

==制备==
二氯化双氮五氨合钌(II)可由[[二氯化一氯五氨合钌(III)|二氯化一氯五氨合钌(III)]]、[[叠氮化钠|叠氮化钠]]与[[甲磺酸|甲磺酸]]反应得到：<ref name=Allen>Allen, A. D., Bottomley, F., Harris, R. O., Reinsalu, V. P., Senoff, C. V. "Pentaammine(Nitrogen)Ruthenium(II) Salts and Other Ammines of Ruthenium" ''Inorganic Syntheses'', 2007, volume 12, pp. 2-8. {{doi| 10.1002/9780470132432.ch1}}</ref>
:[Ru(NH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>5</sub>Cl]Cl<sub>2</sub> + NaN<sub>3</sub> → [Ru(NH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>5</sub>N<sub>2</sub>]Cl<sub>2</sub> + ...
[[三氯化钌|三氯化钌]]和[[水合肼|水合肼]]可以通过更方便的原位反应得到：<ref name=Allen/>
:RuCl<sub>3</sub> + 4 N<sub>2</sub>H<sub>4</sub> → [Ru(NH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>5</sub>N<sub>2</sub>]<sup>2+</sup> + ...

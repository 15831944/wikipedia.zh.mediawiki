'''纳米比亚怪兽'''（英语：Namibian Monster）是一种出没于[[纳米比亚|纳米比亚]]的[[神秘生物列表|神秘生物]]。纳米比亚怪兽通常被描述为有着[[犬|狗]]的头部、[[野豬|野猪]]的身体、站立高4英尺、重150-200磅、具有侵略性的动物。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.pinebarrensinstitute.com/post/149276570148/quick-cryptid-snippet-the-namibian-monster-the|title=Quick Cryptid Snippet – The Namibian Monster The... - The Pine Barrens Institute|accessdate=2018-06-08|work=The Pine Barrens Institute}}</ref>已知最早的媒体报道2009年纳米比亚报纸《New Era 》的报道，当地居民向警方报告一只不明动物正在袭击家畜。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/02/28/namibia-villagers-dog-headed-pig-monster_n_1307723.html|title='Dog-Headed Pig Monster' Sightings Reported By Namibian Villagers|date=2012-02-28|newspaper=Huffington Post|accessdate=2018-06-08|language=en-US}}</ref>据报道，纳米比亚怪兽在Onheleiwa、Oidiva与Oikango皆有出现，许多[[家山羊|山羊]]遭吸食血液或被吞噬。当地警方前往调查，但却无法辨识脚印属于何种动物，而对脚印的跟踪则由于脚印于空地消失而失败。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://cryptoreports.com/mysterious-beasts-torment-villagers-chupacabra|title=Mysterious Beasts Torment Villagers – Chupacabra ? {{!}} CryPtoReports.com|accessdate=2018-06-08|work=cryptoreports.com|language=en-US}}</ref>当地民众认为神秘动物是由Onheleiwa村的一名老人召唤而来，甚至有官员赞成纳米比亚怪物是黑魔法使用者的召唤物。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.livescience.com/18691-dog-monster-terrorizes-africa.html|title=Mysterious 'Dog-Headed Pig Monster' Terrorizes Africa|newspaper=Live Science|accessdate=2018-06-08}}</ref>神秘动物学家{{link-en|洛伦·科尔曼|Loren Coleman}}认为，纳米比亚怪兽可能是[[土豚|土豚]]或[[蜜獾|蜜獾]]。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://cryptomundo.com/cryptozoo-news/namibia-beast/|title=What Is Namibia’s White Mystery Beast?|last=Cryptids|first=Cryptomundo,|date=2012-02-27|newspaper=Cryptomundo|accessdate=2018-06-08|language=en-US}}</ref>

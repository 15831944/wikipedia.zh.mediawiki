[http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/ The National Map]  {{webarchive|url=https://www.webcitation.org/66gupqQDM?url=http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/ |date=2012-04-05 }}, accessed October 3, 2011</ref>，流经[[麻薩諸塞州|麻薩諸塞州]]和[[新罕布什尔州|新罕布什尔州]]。

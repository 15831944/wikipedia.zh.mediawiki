'''纳迪娅·穆拉德·巴西·塔哈'''（{{lang-ku|نادیە موراد باسی تەھا}}；轉寫：Nadiye Murad Basî Teha，{{lang-ar|نادية مراد باسي طه}}，{{lang-en|Nadia Murad Basee Taha}}，{{bd|1993年||||Murad, Nadia}}），[[伊拉克|伊拉克]][[雅兹迪|雅兹迪]]人权活动人士<ref name="وكالة سكاي برس">{{cite news|title=نادية مراد حكاية ضحية ام خطة مخفية|url=http://www.skypressiq.net/10925-نادية%20مراد%20حكاية%20ضحية%20ام%20خطة%20مخفية.html|work=وكالة سكاي برس|date=2015-12-29}}</ref><ref name=Yazda-Statement-2016>{{cite web|last1=Khudida|first1=Ahmed|url=http://www.yazda.org/a-statement-by-nadia-murad-and-yazdas-communication-team-on-nadia-and-yazda-visit-to-australia/|title=A Statement by Nadia Murad and Yazda`s Communication Team on Nadia and Yazda Visit to Australia|work=Yazda: A Global Yazidi Organization|date=2016-08-18|access-date=2016-09-17}}</ref>，2018年[[诺贝尔和平奖|诺贝尔和平奖]]得主<ref name=Ekurd-Nobel-IraqNomination-2016>{{cite news|last1=Editorial Staff in Yazidis|title=Iraq nominates Islamic State Yazidi victim Nadia Murad for Nobel prize|url=http://ekurd.net/yazidi-nadia-murad-nobel-prize-2016-01-06|accessdate=2016-09-22|work=Ekurd Daily|date=2016-01-06|location=Baghdad}}</ref><ref name=IntlBusinessTimes-NobelPeacePrize-2016>{{cite news|url=http://www.ibtimes.co.uk/yazidi-woman-nadia-murad-former-isis-sex-slave-could-win-next-nobel-peace-prize-1536877|title=Yazidi woman Nadia Murad: Former Isis sex slave could win next Nobel Peace Prize|last1=Mogul|first1=Priyanka|work=International Business Times|date=2016-01-08|access-date=2016-09-17}}</ref>。

==经历==
2014年8月，21岁的穆拉德与其他数千名[[雅兹迪|雅兹迪]]妇女一起被[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]囚禁，后遭到虐待并沦为性奴。 她的多位家人则被伊斯兰国杀害。同年11月，她成功逃离伊斯兰国的掌控<ref name=NationalViews-2016>{{cite news|title=Everything about Nadia Murad – The Yazidi Woman Who Escaped ISIS Slavery!|work=NationalViews|url=http://nationalviews.com/nadia-murad-facts-biography-history-yazidi-women-isis-sex-slavery|date=2016-09-16}}</ref>，并最终前往[[德国|德国]][[斯图加特|斯图加特]]避难<ref name=Time-Escaped-2015>{{cite news|last1=Alter|first1=Charlotte|title=Yezidi Girl Who Escaped Isis Sex Slavery: Please Help Us|url=http://time.com/4152127/isis-yezidi-woman-slavery-united-nations/|accessdate=2016-09-19|work=Time|date=2015-12-20}}</ref><ref name=Vice-Broadly-2016>{{cite news|last1=Whyte|first1=Lara|title='Every Part of Me Changed in Their Hands': A Former ISIS Sex Slave Speaks Out|url=https://broadly.vice.com/en_us/article/every-part-of-me-changed-in-their-hands-former-isis-sex-slave-nadia-murad-speaks-out|accessdate=2016-09-19|work=Broadly|publisher=Vice|date=2016-02-18}}</ref>。

在此之后，穆拉德成为了一名人权活动人士。2016年9月，她被任命为首位[[联合国|联合国]][[人口贩卖|人口贩卖]]幸存者尊严亲善大使<ref name=UNODC-GoodwillAmbassador-PostEvent-2016>{{cite web|url=https://www.unodc.org/unodc/en/frontpage/2016/September/human-trafficking-survivor-nadia-murad-named-unodc-goodwill-ambassador.html?ref=fs1|title=Human trafficking survivor Nadia Murad named UNODC Goodwill Ambassador|last1=del Campo|first1=Carlos Gomez|website=United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime (UNODC)|access-date=2016-09-17|date=2016-09-16}}</ref>。同年10月，她先后获得了[[欧洲委员会|欧洲委员会]]{{le|瓦茨拉夫·哈维尔人权奖|Václav Havel Award for Human Rights}}与[[欧洲议会|欧洲议会]][[萨哈罗夫奖|萨哈罗夫奖]]<ref name=Sakharov-BeatrizBecerra-2016>{{cite news|title=Why I am nominating Nadia Murad for Sakharov Prize|url=http://beatrizbecerra.eu/2016/09/12/why-i-am-nominating-nadia-murad-for-sakharov-prize/|accessdate=2016-10-08|work=Beatriz Becerra|date=2016-09-12}}</ref><ref name=EurActiv-Sakharov-2016>{{cite news|last1=Becerra Basterrechea|first1=Beatriz|title=Yazidi genocide victims deserve European Parliament prize|url=https://www.euractiv.com/section/global-europe/opinion/yazidi-genocide-victims-deserve-european-parliament-prize/|accessdate=2016-10-08|work=[[EurActiv|EurActiv]]|date=2016-07-20}}</ref><ref name=Sakharov-Reuters-2016>{{cite news|title=EU Parliament awards Sakharov prize to Yazidi women|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-eu-prize-idUSKCN12R14X?il=0|date=2016-10-27}}</ref>。

'''纳米网络'''是由一组几百纳米至几微米级的[[分子機器|纳米机器]]连接而成的网络。它们能够执行如[[電腦運算|计算]]、[[儲存裝置|数据储存]]、[[感官|检测]]以及驱动等非常简单的任务。主要应用在[[生物医学|生物医学]]、环境研究、军事技术等领域。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Nanonetworks: A New Frontier in Communications|author=J. M. Jornet and M. Pierobon|url=http://cacm.acm.org/magazines/2011/11/138218-nanonetworks-a-new-frontier-in-communications/fulltext|date=2011-11 |doi=10.1145/2018396.2018417|volume=54|pages=84–89}}<code style="color:inherit; border:inherit; padding:inherit;">|author=</code>和<code style="color:inherit; border:inherit; padding:inherit;">|last=</code>只需其一 ([[Help:引文格式1错误#redundant_parameters|帮助]])
[[Category:含有冗余参数的引用的页面|Category:含有冗余参数的引用的页面]]</ref><ref>Nanoscale Communication Networks, Bush, S. F., {{ISBN|978-1-60807-003-9}}, Artech House, 2010. [https://www.amazon.com/Nanoscale-Communication-Networks-Stephen-Bush/dp/1608070034]</ref>纳米网络的通信协议是在IEEE P1906.1中定义的。

== 通信方法 ==
=== 电磁 ===
新型[[纳米材料|纳米材料]]可以传输和接收电磁辐射<ref>C. Rutherglen and P. J. Burke "Nano-Electromagnetics: Circuit and Electromagnetic Properties of Carbon Nanotubes," Small, 5(8), 884–906 (2009)</ref>。新一代纳米电子组件有纳米电池<ref>A. E. Curtright, P. J. Bouwman, R. C. Wartane and K. E. Swider-Lyons, "Power Sources for Nanotechnology," International Journal of Nanotechnology, Vol. 1, pp. 226–239, 2004.</ref>、纳米能量收集系统<ref>Z. L. Wang, "Towards Self-Powered Nanosystems: From Nanogenerators to Nanopiezotronics," Advanced Functional Materials, Vol. 18, pp. 3553–3567, 2008.</ref> 、纳米存储器<ref>Bennewitz, R.; Crain, J. N.; Kirakosian, A.; Lin, J.-L.; McChesney, J. L.; Petrovykh, D. Y. & Himpsel, F. J. Atomic scale memory at a silicon surface Nanotechnology, Vol. 13, pp. 499–502, 2002.</ref>、纳米逻辑电路、纳米天线等。<ref>Peter J. Burke, Shengdong Li, Zhen Yu "Quantitative theory of nanowire and nanotube antenna performance," IEEE Transactions on Nanotechnology Vol. 5 n. 4, pp. 314–334, 2006.</ref><ref>Peter J. Burke, Chris Rutherglen, and Zhen Yu, "Carbon Nanotube Antennas," in Proc. SPIE Int. Soc. Opt. Eng. 6328, 632806-1, 2006 .</ref>
=== 分子 ===
借助分子也可以实现信息的传输和接收，有行径、流动和扩散等三种方式。

*基于行径的通信方式如[[分子马达|分子马达]]<ref>M. Moore, A. Enomoto, T. Nakano, R. Egashira, T. Suda, A. Kayasuga, H. Kojima, H. Sakakibara, and K. Oiwa, "A Design of a Molecular Communication System for Nanomachines Using Molecular Motors," in Proc. Fourth Annual IEEE Conference on Pervasive Computing and Communications and Workshops, March 2006</ref>，可以通过[[大腸桿菌|大肠杆菌]]的[[趨化性|趋化性]]来实现<ref>M. Gregori and [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ian_F._Akyildiz Ian F. Akyildiz], "A New NanoNetwork Architecture using Flagellated Bacteria and Catalytic Nanomotors," IEEE JSAC (Journal of Selected Areas in Communications), Vol. 28, No. 4, pp. 612–619, May 2010.</ref>。

*基于流动的通信方式如[[激素|荷尔蒙]]通过血液实现在人体内的传播、[[信息素|费洛蒙]]长程分子通信等。<ref>L. Parcerisa and [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ian_F._Akyildiz Ian F. Akyildiz], "Molecular Communication Options for Long Range Nanonetworks," Computer Networks Journal (Elsevier), Vol. 53, No. 16, pp. 2753–2766, November 2009.</ref>

*基于扩散的通信方式如输送钙信号的细胞 <ref>M. T. Barros. "Ca2+-signaling-based molecular communication systems: design and future research directions". Elsevier Nano Communication Networks. vol 11, pp 103–113. 2017. [http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1878778916300904] </ref>，以及[[群体感应|群体感应]]中的细菌等。<ref name="TechReview">"The challenge of molecular communication", Technology Review (Physics arXiv blog), 28 June 2010. [http://www.technologyreview.com/blog/arxiv/25365/]</ref>

'''纳加尔布尔'''（{{lang-bn|নাগরপুর}}，{{lang-en|Nagarpur}}）是孟加拉国的一个乌帕齐拉，位於[[达卡专区|达卡专区]]的[[坦盖尔县|坦盖尔县]]。
==人口==
據{{tsl|en|2011 Bangladesh census|2011年孟加拉國人口普查}}，納加爾布爾共有戶數66523戶、人口288092人<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bbs.gov.bd/Census2011/Dhaka/Tangail/Tangail_C01.pdf |format=PDF |title=Tangail Table C-01 : Area, Households, Population, Density by Residence and Community |website=bbs.gov.bd |accessdate=2016-03-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://wayback.archive-it.org/all/20141113225509/http://www.bbs.gov.bd/Census2011/Dhaka/Tangail/Tangail_C01.pdf |archivedate=2014-11-13 |df= }}</ref>，其平均識字率為42.7%（男性46.3%、女性39.6%）。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bbs.gov.bd/Census2011/Dhaka/Tangail/Tangail_C06.pdf |format=PDF |title=Tangail Table C-06 : Distribution of Population aged 7 years and above by Literacy, Sex, Residence and Community |website=bbs.gov.bd |accessdate=2016-02-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://wayback.archive-it.org/all/20141113225744/http://www.bbs.gov.bd/Census2011/Dhaka/Tangail/Tangail_C06.pdf |archivedate=2014-11-13 |df= }}</ref>

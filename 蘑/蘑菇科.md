'''蘑菇科'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|Agaricaceae}}）又名'''伞菌科'''，是[[担子菌门|担子菌门]][[伞菌目|伞菌目]]的一科，包含了[[伞菌属|伞菌属]]等合共85個屬、1340個品種<ref name=Kirk2008>{{cite book |author=Kirk PM, Cannon PF, Minter DW, Stalpers JA. |title=Dictionary of the Fungi. 10th ed |publisher=CABI |location=Wallingford |year=2008|page=11|isbn=0-85199-826-7}}</ref>。

== 屬 ==
[[白环蘑属|白环蘑属]]（{{lang|la|''Leucoagaricus''}}）跟[[白鬼伞属|白鬼伞属]]（{{lang|la|''Leucocoprinus''}}）這兩個屬均已知與[[螞蟻|螞蟻]][[共生|共生]]<ref>{{cite book |author=B. Hölldobler & E.O. Wilson |year=2009 |title=The superorganism: the beauty, elegance, and strangeness of insect societies |location=New York NY |publisher=W. W. Norton & Company }}</ref>。The extinct genus ''[[Coprinites|Coprinites]]'' is one of four known agaricalean genera in the fossil record.<ref name="Hibetal2003">{{Cite journal|last1=hibbet |first1=D.S. |coauthors = ''et al'' |year=2003 |title=Another Fossil Agaric from Dominican Amber |journal= Mycologia |volume=95 |issue=4 |pages=685–687 |jstor=3761943 |doi=10.2307/3761943}}</ref>Others include ''[[Aureofungus|Aureofungus]]'', ''[[Protomycena|Protomycena]]'', and ''[[Archaeomarasmius|Archaeomarasmius]]''. ''Archaeomarasmius leggeti'', from [[Atlantic_Coastal_Plain|Atlantic Coastal Plain]] amber, is 90–94 [[Million_years_ago|Ma]]); the other fossil genera are from [[Dominican_amber|Dominican amber]] and date to 15–20 Ma.<ref name="Hibbett 2003"/>

'''喀俄涅'''（{{lang-grc|Χιόνη}}，表“雪”之意<ref> Liddell and Scott, [http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Perseus%3Atext%3A1999.04.0057%3Aentry%3Dxiw%2Fn s.v. χιών]</ref>）又譯'''齊昂妮'''，是[[希腊神话|希腊神话]]中代表[[雪|雪]]与[[冬季|冬季]]的女神。她是{{tsl|en|north wind|北风 (气象学)|北风}}之神[[阿涅摩伊#玻瑞阿斯|玻瑞阿斯]]与女仙[[歐麗泰亞|歐麗泰亞]]之女<ref>Grimal, s.v. Chione; Smith, [http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Perseus%3Atext%3A1999.04.0104%3Aalphabetic+letter%3DC%3Aentry+group%3D20%3Aentry%3Dchione-bio-1 s.v. Chione 1.]; [[書庫|Apollodorus]], [http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Apollod.+3.15.2 3.15.2], [http://data.perseus.org/citations/urn:cts:greekLit:tlg0548.tlg001.perseus-eng1:1.9.21 1.9.21]; [[保萨尼亚斯_(地理学家)|Pausanias]], [http://data.perseus.org/citations/urn:cts:greekLit:tlg0525.tlg001.perseus-eng1:1.38.2 1.38.2].</ref>。其兄弟姊妹包括[[色雷斯|色雷斯]]王{{tsl|en|Phineus|菲纽斯}}之妻——克勒俄帕特拉、[[阿尔戈英雄|阿尔戈英雄]]{{tsl|en|Boreads|卡拉伊斯和仄忒斯}}。

据部分资料记载，喀俄涅生下一子{{tsl|en|Eumolpus|欧摩尔波斯}}，并因惧怕其父知悉情况而将其扔进大海。而后欧摩尔波斯为[[波塞冬|波塞冬]]所救<ref>Grimal, s.v. Eumolpus; Smith, [http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Perseus%3Atext%3A1999.04.0104%3Aalphabetic+letter%3DE%3Aentry+group%3D11%3Aentry%3Deumolpus-bio-1 s.v. Eumolpus]; [[書庫|Apollodorus]], [http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Apollod.+3.15.4 3.15.4]; [[保萨尼亚斯_(地理学家)|Pausanias]], [http://data.perseus.org/citations/urn:cts:greekLit:tlg0525.tlg001.perseus-eng1:1.38.2 1.38.2]. For other traditions regarding Eumolpus' parentage see Frazer's [http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Apollod.+3.15.4 3.15.4 n. 1]</ref>。

'''病毒性出血熱'''（viral hemorrhagic fever）是一類由不同的[[RNA病毒|RNA病毒]]於人類或其他動物身上所造成的[[傳染病|傳染病]]，以發燒及出血為典型[[症狀|病徵]]。患者的病情要視乎[[病原體|病原體]]的種類：有些病毒只造成輕微的症狀，另一些則會使病人發高燒、休克甚至死亡。<ref>{{cite book|editor-last=Woods |editor-first=Lt Col Jon B. |url=http://www.usamriid.army.mil/education/bluebookpdf/USAMRIID%20BlueBook%206th%20Edition%20-%20Sep%202006.pdf |title=USAMRIID’s Medical Management of Biological Casualties Handbook |edition=6th |publisher=U.S. Army Medical Institute of Infectious Diseases |location=Fort Detrick MA |year=2005 |pages=143–4 |format=PDF |ref=harv |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070609104204/http://www.usamriid.army.mil/education/bluebookpdf/USAMRIID%20BlueBook%206th%20Edition%20-%20Sep%202006.pdf |archivedate=2007-06-09 }}</ref><ref name=Grard2012>{{cite journal |author=Grard G, Fair JN, Lee D, ''et al.'' |title=A novel rhabdovirus associated with acute hemorrhagic fever in central Africa |journal=PLoS Pathog. |volume=8 |issue=9 |pages=e1002924 |year=2012 ||date=September |pmid=23028323 |pmc=3460624 |doi=10.1371/journal.ppat.1002924 |url=http://dx.plos.org/10.1371/journal.ppat.1002924}}</ref>

==出血热病毒==
'''出血热病毒'''（hemorrhagic fever virus）是由[[节肢动物|节肢动物]]或[[啮齿动物|啮齿动物]]传播，引起病毒性出血熱的一大类[[病毒|病毒]]统称，分别属于5个[[病毒種類列表|病毒科]]。中国目前已发现的有[[漢坦病毒|汉坦病毒]]、新疆出血热病毒、[[登革热病毒|登革热病毒]]及[[屈公病|基孔肯雅热]]病毒。

{{vgname2|象牙塔|fr=Ivory Tower}}是设立在法国里昂的游戏开发商。

== 歷史 ==
* 2007年，该公司由首席运营官Ahmed Boukhelifa，制作人Stephane Beley和首席财务官Emmanuel Oualid成立。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.edge-online.com/features/the-eden-project-why-the-crew-creator-ivory-tower-quit-test-drive-unlimited-2-to-start-all-over-again/ |title=The Eden project: why The Crew creator Ivory Tower quit Test Drive Unlimited 2 to start all over again |publisher=[[Edge_(magazine)|Edge]] |first=Neil |last=Long |date=27 November 2013|accessdate=25 March 2014}}</ref>
* 2014年12月2日，该工作室发布第一款游戏《The Crew》。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.joystiq.com/2013/06/10/the-crew-announced-e3/ |title=The Crew announced by Ubisoft, coming early 2014 |publisher=[[Joystiq|Joystiq]] |first=Sinan |last=Kubba |date=10 June 2013|accessdate=10 June 2013}}</ref>
* 2015年10月，[[育碧|育碧]]宣布收购象牙塔。<ref>{{cite web|title=Ivory Tower Joins the Ubisoft Family|url=http://blog.ubi.com/ivory-tower-joins-the-ubisoft-family/|website=Ubisoft|accessdate=7 October 2015}}</ref>

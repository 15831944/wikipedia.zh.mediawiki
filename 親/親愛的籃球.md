'''《親愛的籃球》'''是一部创作于2017年的美國[[動畫|動畫]][[短片|短片]]，由[[NBA|NBA]]著名篮球运动员[[科比·布莱恩特|科比·布莱恩特]]编剧和敘述，[[葛連·基恩|葛連·基恩]]導演并制作<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=http://www.foxsports.com/nba/story/kobe-bryant-animated-film-dear-basketball-john-williams-050516|title=Kobe Bryant is teaming up with John Williams to make an animated film|first=2016 at 10:50a|last=ET|date=2016-05-05|website=FOX Sports|access-date=2019-02-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190209180014/https://www.foxsports.com/nba/story/kobe-bryant-animated-film-dear-basketball-john-williams-050516|archive-date=2019-02-09|dead-url=no}}</ref>。這部短片改编于科比·布萊恩特在2015年11月29日寫給《{{Le|球員論壇報|The Players' Tribune}}》的一封信。这部短片赢得了[[第90屆奧斯卡金像獎|第90届奥斯卡金像奖]][[奥斯卡最佳动画短片奖|最佳动画短片奖]]。

== 概述 ==
这部影片改编于[[科比·布莱恩特|科比·布萊恩特]]在2015年11月29日寫給《{{Le|球員論壇報|The Players' Tribune}}》的一封同名的信，科比在这封信中宣布自己退役<ref name="Kobe's letter">{{cite web|last1=Bryant|first1=Kobe|authorlink=Kobe Bryant|title=Dear Basketball|url=https://www.theplayerstribune.com/dear-basketball/|website=The Players' Tribune|publisher=The Players' Tribune, Inc.|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180119180935/https://www.theplayerstribune.com/dear-basketball/|archivedate=2018-01-19|accessdate=|author=|date=}}</ref><ref name="dream team">{{cite web|last1=Wolff|first1=Ellen|title=Kobe Bryant Assembles His Animated Dream Team for ‘Dear Basketball’|url=https://variety.com/2018/film/spotlight/kobe-bryant-dear-basketball-glen-keane-john-williams-1202662198/|website=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|publisher=Variety Media, LLC|ref=dear basketball|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180119164823/http://variety.com/2018/film/spotlight/kobe-bryant-dear-basketball-glen-keane-john-williams-1202662198/|archivedate=2018-01-19}}</ref>。影片由[[NBA|NBA]]著名[[篮球|篮球]]运动员[[科比·布莱恩特|科比·布莱恩特]]编剧和旁白配音，[[葛連·基恩|葛連·基恩]]導演并制作<ref name=":0" />。影片的配乐师是[[約翰·威廉斯|约翰·威廉斯]]，科比是他的忠实粉丝。影片由格兰尼蒂工作室（{{Lang|en|Granity Studios}}）和相信娱乐集团（{{Lang|en|Believe Entertainment Group}}）合作制作<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thewrap.com/members/2017/12/18/go90-picks-up-kobe-bryant-and-believe-entertainments-dear-basketball/|title=go90 Picks Up Kobe Bryant and Believe Entertainment Group's 'Dear Basketball'|first=Matt|last=Lopez|date=2017-12-18|publisher=}}</ref>。制作完成后，通过{{Le|go90|go90}}在线发布<ref>{{Cite web|title=“Dear Basketball,” Assembled by Legendary Team of Creators, Premieres on Verizon’s go90|url=https://www.broadcastingcable.com/post-type-the-wire/dear-basketball-assembled-legendary-team-creators-premieres-verizon-s-go90-170761|website=Broadcasting & Cable|access-date=2019-02-17|language=en-us|first=Gretchen|last=Wagner|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180623033303/https://www.broadcastingcable.com/post-type-the-wire/dear-basketball-assembled-legendary-team-creators-premieres-verizon-s-go90-170761|archive-date=2018-06-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

这部影片讲述了在從[[NBA|NBA]]退役前夕，科比描述了他對篮球的熱愛，这种热爱始於他還是個孩子的時候。從他年輕的辉煌夢想到他20年的職業生涯，科比描述了當他處於巔峰狀態時，籃球和他给予對方所有的一切。<ref>{{cite web|title=Dear Basketball: Short Film (Animated) - Oscar Nominees 2018|url=http://oscar.go.com/nominees/short-film-animated/dear-basketball|website=The Oscars 2018|publisher=[[The_Walt_Disney_Company|Disney Enterprises, Inc.]]|accessdate=2018-01-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180126140237/http://oscar.go.com/nominees/short-film-animated/dear-basketball|archive-date=2018-01-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 反應 ==
在[[爛番茄|烂番茄]]上，根据12条评论，这部影片的“新鲜度”（即好评率）为75%<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/dear_basketball|title=''Dear Basketball'' (2017)|work=[[Rotten_Tomatoes|Rotten Tomatoes]]|publisher=[[Fandango_Media|Fandango Media]]|accessdate=2019-02-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180323153740/https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/dear_basketball/|archive-date=2018-03-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。同时，这部短片赢得了[[第90届奥斯卡金像奖|第90届奥斯卡金像奖]]奥斯卡最佳动画短片奖，这是NBA球员第一次获得奥斯卡奖<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://oscar.go.com/nominees/short-film-animated/|title=90th Academy Award Animated Short Film Nominees|website=The Oscars 2018|publisher=[[The_Walt_Disney_Company|Disney Enterprises, Inc.]]|date=2018-01-23|accessdate=2018-01-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180124070653/http://oscar.go.com/nominees/short-film-animated|archive-date=2018-01-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

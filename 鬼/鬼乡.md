''world.kbs.co.kr''</ref><ref>[http://english.yonhapnews.co.kr/news/2016/03/05/0200000000AEN20160305002500315.html "Spirits' Homecoming (2016)"]. ''english.yonhapnews.co.kr''</ref>该电影曾因为财务问题暂停过多次，但经由七万多人的[[众筹|众筹]]，募集了足够资金。随后，由于缺乏院线的支持，上映日期也多次推迟。然而，人们提前订票，并在[[社交网络|社交网络]]相互宣传，让这部电影得以在更多的院线上映。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://english.hani.co.kr/arti/english_edition/e_national/732760.html|title=Going to see "Spirits’ Homecoming" becoming much more than just a trip to the movies|website=english.hani.co.kr|access-date=2016-03-15}}</ref>

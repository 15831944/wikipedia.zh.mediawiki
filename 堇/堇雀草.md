'''堇雀草'''（学名：{{sname|''Delphinium peregrinum''}}；{{lang-en|violet larkspur}}）是[[毛茛科|毛茛科]][[翠雀属|翠雀属]]一年生草本植物，原产[[东南欧|东南欧]]及[[西亚|西亚]]。<ref>{{Cite web|title=''Delphinium peregrinum'' L.|url=https://npgsweb.ars-grin.gov/gringlobal/taxonomydetail.aspx?id=404517|accessdate=2019-03-02|author=USDA, Agricultural Research Service, National Plant Germplasm System|date=|publisher=Germplasm Resources Information Network (GRIN-Taxonomy)}}</ref>堇雀草是[[翠雀属|翠雀属]]的模式种。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=The system of the genus ''Delphinium'' (Ranunculaceae) based on the morphological features of seeds|author=Malyutin, N.I.|url=|journal=Bot. Zhurn. (Moscow & Leningrad)|issue=5|doi=|others=|year=1987|volume=72|page=683-693|pmid=}}</ref>

== 名称 ==
[[File:DELPHINIUM_PEREGRINUM_-_TORA-2.JPG|左]]
属名{{sname|''Delphinium''}}源自古希腊语{{lang|el|δελφίνιον}}（{{lang|la|''delphínion''}}），意思是“海豚”。<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|edition=4th ed|url=|publisher=Cambridge University Press|date=2008|location=Cambridge|isbn=9780511480232|oclc=348190404|first=D.|last=Gledhill|title=The Names of Plants}}</ref>[[迪奥斯科里德斯|迪奥斯科里德斯]]的《药材志》记载了一种叫{{lang|el|δελφίνιον}}的翠雀属植物，因花朵形似海豚而得其名。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://ia800507.us.archive.org/20/items/b23982500_0001/b23982500_0001.pdf|title=''Pedanii Dioscoridis Anazarbei De materia medica libri quinque''|last=Dioscorides|first=P.|publisher=Knobloch|year=1829|isbn=|editor-last=Sprengel|editor-first=K.P.J.|volume=''Tomus Primus''|location=Leipzig|pages=420-421}}</ref>

种加词{{sname|''peregrinum''}}意思是“异域的”。<ref name=":0" />

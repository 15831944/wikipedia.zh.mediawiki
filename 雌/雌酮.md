'''雌酮'''（{{lang-en|Estrone}}或oestrone，'''E1'''，或译为'''雌酚酮'''、'''雌激素酮'''）是一种较弱的[[雌激素|雌性]][[甾体|甾体]][[性类固醇|性激素]]<ref name="pmid16112947">{{cite journal | vauthors = Kuhl H | title = Pharmacology of estrogens and progestogens: influence of different routes of administration | journal = Climacteric | volume = 8 Suppl 1 | issue =  | pages = 3–63 | date = August 2005 | pmid = 16112947 | doi = 10.1080/13697130500148875 | url = http://hormonebalance.org/images/documents/Kuhl%2005%20%20Pharm%20Estro%20Progest%20Climacteric_1313155660.pdf }}</ref>，是三种主要的[[内源|内源性]][[雌激素|雌激素]]之一，另外两种为[[雌二醇|雌二醇]]和[[雌三醇|雌三醇]]<ref name="pmid16112947" />。雌酮等雌激素的[[生物合成|生物合成]]从[[膽固醇|膽固醇]]开始，大部分由[[生殖腺|生殖腺]][[分泌|分泌]]，少部分为[[脂肪組織|脂肪組織]]对来自[[腎上腺|腎上腺]]的[[雄激素|雄激素]]的转化<ref name="HornsteinSchwerin2012">{{cite book|first1 = Theresa | last1 = Hornstein | first2 = Jeri Lynn | last2 = Schwerin | name-list-format = vanc |title=Biology of Women|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ibgKAAAAQBAJ&pg=PA369|date=1 January 2012|publisher=Cengage Learning|isbn=978-1-285-40102-7|pages=369–}}</ref>。相对于雌二醇而言，雌酮和雌三醇的活性都很小<ref name="pmid16112947" />。雌酮可转化为雌二醇，是主要的雌二醇[[代謝中間產物|代謝]][[前体|前体]]<ref name="pmid16112947" />。

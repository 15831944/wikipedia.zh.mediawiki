'''降鈣素'''（{{lang-en|Calcitonin}}，又譯为'''抑鈣激素'''）是一種含有32個[[胺基酸|胺基酸]]的直線型[[多肽|多肽]]類[[激素|激素]]，在人體裡是由[[甲狀腺|甲狀腺]]的[[濾泡旁細胞|濾泡旁細胞]]（parafollicular cells，又稱C細胞）製造。在[[魚類|魚類]]、[[爬蟲類|爬蟲類]]、[[鳥類|鳥類]]、[[哺乳類|哺乳類]]身上都有發現這種激素。主要功能是'''降低血鈣'''。但降鈣素對於調節人體血液中[[鈣離子|鈣離子]](Ca<sup>2+</sup>)的恆定通常並沒有很顯著的重要性。<ref name="Costoff_Calcitonin_Biological_Actions">{{cite web| url =http://www.lib.mcg.edu/edu/eshuphysio/program/section5/5ch6/s5ch6_23.htm| title =Sect. 5, Ch. 6: Biological Actions of CT| author =Costoff A| authorlink =| coauthors =| date =| format =| work =| publisher =Medical College of Georgia| pages =| language =| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20080705203238/http://www.lib.mcg.edu/edu/eshuphysio/program/section5/5ch6/s5ch6_23.htm| archivedate =2008-07-05| quote =| accessdate =2008-08-07| deadurl =yes}}</ref>

==主要作用==
降鈣素影響了鈣離子和磷酸鹽的代謝過程，其功能多半是拮抗[[副甲狀腺素|副甲狀腺素]]的作用。<ref name="isbn1-4160-2328-3">{{cite book | author = Boron WF, Boulpaep EL | authorlink = | editor = | others = | title = Medical Physiology: A Cellular And Molecular Approach | edition = | language = | publisher = Elsevier/Saunders | location = | year = 2004 | origyear = | pages = | chapter = Endocrine system chapter | quote = | isbn = 1-4160-2328-3 | oclc = | doi = | url = | accessdate = }}</ref>

降鈣素主要透過下列四種方式降低血鈣濃度：
#抑制[[小腸|小腸]]對於鈣離子的吸收。<ref name="Costoff_Calcitonin_Small_Intestine">{{cite web| url =http://www.lib.mcg.edu/edu/eshuphysio/program/section5/5ch6/s5ch6_26.htm| title =Sect. 5, Ch. 6: Effects of CT on the Small Intestine| author =Costoff A| authorlink =| coauthors =| date =| format =| work =| publisher =Medical College of Georgia| pages =| language =| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20081006132959/http://www.lib.mcg.edu/edu/eshuphysio/program/section5/5ch6/s5ch6_26.htm| archivedate =2008-10-06| quote =| accessdate =2008-08-07| deadurl =yes}}</ref>
#'''抑制'''{{en-link|蝕骨細胞|osteoclast}}（Osteoclast），'''減少'''骨骼中的鈣離子流失到血液中。<ref name="Costoff_Calcitonin_Bone">{{cite web| url =http://www.lib.mcg.edu/edu/eshuphysio/program/section5/5ch6/s5ch6_24.htm| title =Sect. 5, Ch. 6: Effects of CT on Bone| author =Costoff A| authorlink =| coauthors =| date =| format =| work =| publisher =Medical College of Georgia| pages =| language =| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20080622221510/http://www.lib.mcg.edu/edu/eshuphysio/program/section5/5ch6/s5ch6_24.htm| archivedate =2008-06-22| quote =| accessdate =2008-08-07| deadurl =yes}}</ref>
#抑制[[腎小管|腎小管]]對磷酸根的[[再吸收|再吸收]]作用。<ref name="pmid9058369">{{cite journal | author = Carney SL | title = Calcitonin and human renal calcium and electrolyte transport | journal = Miner Electrolyte Metab | volume = 23 | issue = 1 | pages = 43–7 | year = 1997 | pmid = 9058369 | url = | issn = | accessdate = }}</ref>
#'''抑制'''[[腎小管|腎小管]]對鈣離子的再吸收作用，'''增加'''鈣離子自[[尿液|尿液]]流失。<ref name="pmid9058369">{{cite journal | author = Carney SL | title = Calcitonin and human renal calcium and electrolyte transport | journal = Miner Electrolyte Metab | volume = 23 | issue = 1 | pages = 43–7 | year = 1997 | pmid = 9058369 | url = | issn = | accessdate = }}</ref>

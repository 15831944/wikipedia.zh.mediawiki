'''神話作品'''（'''Mythic fiction'''）泛指大量研習[[神話|神話]]、[[民間傳說|民間傳說]]和[[童話|童話]]，再進行延伸的作品而言<ref>"A Mythic Fiction Reading List, ''The Journal of Mythic Arts,'' online archive: http://endicottstudio.typepad.com/jomareadinglists/2007/10/a-mythic-fictio.html</ref>。神話作品一般而言是介於[[:en:Literary_fiction|文學作品]]和[[奇幻作品|奇幻作品]]之間<ref>Julie Bartel, ''The Continuum Encyclopedia of Young Adult Literature'', "Mythic Fiction for Yong Adults," The Continuum International Publishing Group, Inc., 2005.</ref>。

'''忠犬巴比雕像'''（{{lang-en|Greyfriars Bobby Fountain}}）是一座位在[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]][[愛丁堡|愛丁堡]]，用於紀念{{le|忠犬巴比|Greyfriars Bobby}}的[[青銅|青銅]][[雕像|雕像]]<ref>{{Cite name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/scotland-now/lonely-planet-edinburgh-guidebooks-ask-11005462|title=Edinburgh guidebooks to ask tourists not to rub Greyfriars Bobby's 當時[[英國|英國]]{{Le|皇家防止虐待動物協會|Royal Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals}}的婦女委員會主席{{Le|安吉拉·伯德特-庫茨|Angela Burdett-Coutts, 1st Baroness Burdett-Coutts}}深受忠犬巴比的事蹟感動，在她要求與贊助下建立了這座立在[[花崗岩|花崗岩]][[噴泉|噴泉]]上的忠犬巴比雕像。雕像由[[雕塑家|雕塑家]]{{Le|威廉·布洛迪|William Brodie (sculptor)}}製作，啟用於1873年11月<ref name=":0" />，地點位在愛丁堡[[喬治四世橋|喬治四世橋]]的南端<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.victorianweb.org/sculpture/brodie/2.html|title=Greyfriars Bobby, by William Brodie (Edinburgh)|accessdate=2018-05-05|work=www.victorianweb.org|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161220082124/http://www.victorianweb.org/sculpture/brodie/2.html|archivedate=2016-12-20}}</ref>，[[灰衣修士教堂墓地|灰衣修士教堂墓地]]和[[蘇格蘭國立博物館|蘇格蘭國立博物館]]的附近。

該雕像在1977年成為英國A級保護建築<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/200365253-george-iv-bridge-greyfriars-bobby-fountain-edinburgh#.Wu2Yb4iFNPY|title=George Iv Bridge, Greyfriars Bobby Fountain, Edinburgh, Edinburgh|accessdate=2018-05-05|publisher=www.britishlistedbuildings.co.uk}}</ref>。

多年來，忠犬巴比雕像成為當地的知名觀光景點；一些人為了追求好運會去摩擦雕像的[[鼻子|鼻子]]，致使該部位嚴重掉漆，地方當局不得不時常修復破壞<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thenational.scot/news/15469267.Campaign_launched_to_stop_tourists_to_stop_rubbing_Greyfriars_Bobby_s_nose/|title=Campaign launched to stop tourists to stop rubbing Greyfriars Bobby's nose|accessdate=2018-05-05|work=The National|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180505103201/http://www.thenational.scot/news/15469267.Campaign_launched_to_stop_tourists_to_stop_rubbing_Greyfriars_Bobby_s_nose/|archivedate=2018-05-05}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://stv.tv/news/east-central/1395909-lonely-planet-may-highlight-greyfriars-bobby-campaign/|title=Tourists to be urged to keep hands off Greyfriars Bobby|accessdate=2018-05-05|author=|date=2017-08-17|publisher=STV News|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180505103200/https://stv.tv/news/east-central/1395909-lonely-planet-may-highlight-greyfriars-bobby-campaign/|archivedate=2018-05-05}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-edinburgh-east-fife-24402574|title=Greyfriars Bobby nose vandals sought|date=2013-10-04|newspaper=BBC News|accessdate=2018-05-05|language=en-GB}}</ref>。

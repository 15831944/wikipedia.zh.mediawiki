'''残影余像'''<ref name="douban">{{cite web|url=https://movie.douban.com/subject/26677427/|title=残影余像 (豆瓣)|work=豆瓣网|accessdate=2017-12-10}}</ref>（{{lang-en|'''Afterimage'''}}，{{lang-pl|'''Powidoki'''}}）是一部由[[安杰依·瓦伊达|安杰依·瓦伊达]]执导的2016年波兰[[剧情片|剧情片]]。<ref name="pl">{{cite web|url=http://culture.pl/en/article/major-polish-films-premiering-at-toronto-film-festival|title=Major Polish Films Premiering at Toronto Film Festival|work=Cultural|date=2 September 2016|accessdate=2 September 2016}}</ref>在[[2016年多伦多国际影展|2016年多伦多国际影展]]上映。<ref name="TIFF">{{cite web|url=http://www.tiff.net/films/afterimage/|title=Afterimage|work=TIFF|date=2 September 2016|accessdate=2 September 2016|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160906142902/http://www.tiff.net/films/afterimage/|archivedate=2016年9月6日|df=}}</ref>该片被选为[[第89届奥斯卡金像奖|第89届奥斯卡金像奖]][[奥斯卡最佳外语片奖|最佳外语片奖]]的波兰参赛作品，但未获殊荣。<ref name="Poland">{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2016/film/news/poland-enters-afterimage-andrzej-wajda-oscar-foreign-language-1201870531/ |title=Poland Enters Andrzej Wajda’s ‘Afterimage’ into Oscar Contention |work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |first=Robert |last=Mitchell|date=28 September 2016|accessdate=28 September 2016}}</ref><ref name="HWR">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscars-poland-selects-afterimage-foreign-933331|title=Oscars: Poland Selects 'Afterimage' for Foreign-Language Category|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|first=Nick|last=Holdsworth|date=28 September 2016|accessdate=28 September 2016}}</ref>本片是-{zh-cn:瓦伊达;zh-tw:華伊達}-（逝于2016年10月）的最后一部作品。

'''果阿石'''（[[英语|英语]]：{{lang|en|Goa Stone}}）是一种被认为具有药用和[[护身符|护身符]]的性质的人造[[牛黄|牛黄]]，因产自[[印度|印度]][[果阿|果阿]]，故得此名<ref>{{cite web|title=Goa Stone with Gold Case|url=https://www.metmuseum.org/art/collection/search/454738|website=The Metropolitan Museum of Art|publisher=The Metropolitan Museum of Art|accessdate=2017-11-25 |language=英语}}</ref>。果阿石很有可能是[[十七世纪|十七世纪]]晚期在[[印度|印度]][[果阿|果阿]]的[[耶稣会|耶稣会]]制造的。因为自然牛黄不足以满足需求，印度果阿的耶稣会便开始大量生产果阿石<ref>{{cite web|last1=Bailly|first1=Muriel|title=Foolish Remedies: Goa Stone|url=https://wellcomecollection.org/articles/foolish-remedies-goa-stone|website=Wellcome Collection|publisher=Wellcome Collection|accessdate=2017-11-25|language=英语|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171201033146/https://wellcomecollection.org/articles/foolish-remedies-goa-stone|archive-date=2017-12-01|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。果阿石的发明者被确认为是{{link-en|凡人修士|lay brother}}加斯帕尔·安东尼奥。1691年3月6日，[[葡萄牙人|葡萄牙人]]确认了该耶稣会在当地的果阿石垄断<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-aA5AQAAIAAJ&pg=PA317|title=An Historical and Archæological Sketch of the City of Goa: Preceded by a Short Statistical Account of the Territory of Goa|last=Fonseca|first=José Nicolau da|date=1878|publisher=Thacker & Company, limited|year=|isbn=|location= |pages=317 |accessdate=2017-11-25 |language=英语}}</ref>。

== 简介 ==
果阿石是由[[有机物|有机物]]和[[无机物|无机物]]共同结合而成的。通过将[[毛发|毛发]]、[[贝壳|贝壳]]、[[象牙|象牙]]、[[树脂|树脂]]和破碎的[[宝石|宝石]]等原材料塑造成一个[[球体|球体]]并加入[[色素|色素]]。如[[马宝|马宝]]一样，果阿石被认为可以预防[[疾病|疾病]]和治疗[[中毒|中毒]]。可以通过将果阿石碎成小块后剃成口服溶液、[[茶|茶]]或[[酒|酒]]等服用<ref>{{cite web|title=Bezoar Stone with Case and Stand|url=https://www.metmuseum.org/art/collection/search/452963|website=The Metropolitan Museum of Art|publisher=The Metropolitan Museum of Art|accessdate=2017-11-25 |language=英语}}</ref>。由于被认为可以提高药用性质，果阿石必须被保存在华丽的容器内，如纯[[金|金]]或[[镀金|镀金]]的球型容器内<ref>{{cite web|last1=Grundhauser|first1=Eric|title=The Man-Made Gut Stones Once Used to Thwart Assassination Attempts|url=https://www.atlasobscura.com/articles/the-manmade-gut-stones-once-used-to-thwart-assassination-attempts|website=Atlas Obscura|publisher=Atlas Obscura|accessdate=2017-11-25 |language=英语}}</ref>。这些容器通常被装饰以精细的{{link-en|金银花丝|Filigree}}，有时会点缀一些动物的形状，如[[猴子|猴子]]、[[独角兽|独角兽]]、[[狗|狗]]和[[鹦鹉|鹦鹉]]等<ref>{{cite web|last1=Ekhtiar|first1=Maryam|title=Paradox|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4I_5H5EetEE|website=YouTube|publisher=The Metropolitan Museum of Art|accessdate=2017-11-25 |language=英语}}</ref>。

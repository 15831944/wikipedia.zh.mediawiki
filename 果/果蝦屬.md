== 特徵 ==
果蝦有著短小[[圓筒|圓筒狀]]的[[背甲|背甲]]，甲上有一條縱向裂紋。[[頭|頭]]部的[[喙|喙]]狀突起要比背甲稍長一些。[[腹|腹]]部有短小的線狀[[鞭毛|鞭毛]]。[[胸|胸]]節相互交疊，尾刺呈[[紡錘形|紡錘狀]]。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=70 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

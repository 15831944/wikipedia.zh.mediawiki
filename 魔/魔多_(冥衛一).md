'''魔多''' （{{lang|en|Mordor}}） 是靠近[[冥衛一|冥衛一]]北極的一塊直徑超過 {{convert|320|km|mi}} 的暗色區域。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Nemiroff|first1=Robert|last2=Bonnell|first2=Jerry|title=APOD: 2015 July 17 －Charon|url=http://apod.nasa.gov/apod/ap150717.html|website=Astronomy Picture of the Day|accessdate=17 July 2015|date=17 July 2015}}</ref><ref name="MyUser_Extremetech.com_July_15_2015c">{{cite web |url=http://www.extremetech.com/extreme/209998-nasa-unveils-historic-high-resolution-pluto-charon-and-hydra-photos-from-new-horizons-mission |title=NASA unveils historic, high-resolution Pluto, Charon, and Hydra photos from New Horizons mission |newspaper=Extremetech.com |date=  |author= |accessdate= July 15, 2015}}</ref>

它的名稱來自[[J.R.R.托爾金|J.R.R.托爾金]]的小說《[[魔戒|魔戒]]》中黑暗魔君[[索倫|索倫]]的領地[[魔多|魔多]]。雖然魔多並非官方名稱，但是[[美國國家航空暨太空總署|美國國家航空暨太空總署]]的科學家凱西·奧爾金（{{lang|en|Cathy Olkin}}）在7月15日的NASA新聞發布會上提到新視野號團隊已經在非正式場合使用該名稱。<ref name="Wired article CHARON COMES INTO FOCUS IN NEW HORIZONS’ LATEST PHOTOS">[http://www.wired.com/2015/07/charon-comes-focus-new-horizons-latest-photos/ Charon Comes into Focus in New Horizons' Latest Photos], Article from Wired.</ref>

魔多究竟是什麼目前還尚不明確，它可能是一個巨大的[[撞擊坑|撞擊坑]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.planetary.org/blogs/emily-lakdawalla/2015/07151720-first-look-at-new-horizons-pluto-charon.html |title=First look at New Horizons' Pluto and Charon images: "baffling in a very interesting and wonderful way"|date=  |author=Emily Lakdawalla |accessdate= July 16, 2015}}</ref>

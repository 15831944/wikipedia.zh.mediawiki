'''管柱珊瑚'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Siphonodendron'''''}}）是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[珊瑚|珊瑚]]，生存於[[石炭紀|石炭紀]]的淺[[海|海]]中。為群生珊瑚，具有互相分開的枝，枝的中心有珊瑚單體([[珊瑚蟲|珊瑚蟲]])。群體濃密，呈放射狀向外生長，中軸大致上互相[[平行|平行]]。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=51 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

[[教宗若望二十三世|教宗若望二十三世]]在1958年10月28日當選教宗，並在1963年6月3日離世。<ref>{{cite web|title=John XXIII|url=http://w2.vatican.va/content/vatican/en/holy-father/giovanni-xxiii.html|language=en|publisher=The Holy See|accessdate=2017-06-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622112513/http://w2.vatican.va/content/vatican/en/holy-father/giovanni-xxiii.html|archive-date=2017-06-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>他在4年7個月的任期內發表了8道教宗[[通諭|通諭]]，其中《[[慈母與導師|慈母與導師]]》和《[[和平於世|和平於世]]》是特別重要的。<ref>{{cite web|title=The Papacy: Vatican Revolutionary|url=http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,874758-1,00.html|language=en|publisher=TIME|date=1963-06-07|accessdate=2017-06-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622180713/http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,874758-1,00.html|archive-date=2017-06-22|subscription=yes|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=POPE JOHN XXIII|url=http://www.vatican.va/news_services/liturgy/saints/ns_lit_doc_20000903_john-xxiii_en.html|publisher=L'Osservatore Romano|language=en|date=2000-09-06|accessdate=2017-06-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622180926/http://www.vatican.va/news_services/liturgy/saints/ns_lit_doc_20000903_john-xxiii_en.html|archive-date=2017-06-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>教宗通諭是教宗向某一地區或全世界的天主教會[[主教#天主教|主教]]發出的信件。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Sägmüller|first1=S.B.|title=Ecclesiastical Letters|url=http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/09202a.htm|publisher=Robert Appleton Company|accessdate=2018-02-08|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180208121447/http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/09202a.htm|archivedate=2018-02-08|location=New York|language=en|year=1910|work=The Catholic Encyclopedia|dead-url=no|via=New Advent}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Thurston|first1=H.|title=Bulls and Briefs|url=http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03052b.htm|publisher=Robert Appleton Company|accessdate=2018-02-08|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180208122055/http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03052b.htm|archivedate=2018-02-08|location=New York|language=en|year=1908|work=The Catholic Encyclopedia|dead-url=no|via=New Advent}}</ref>通諭有可能會譴責人犯下的錯誤、指出對信仰和道德的威脅、在某些議題上建議忠於信仰的做法或為當前和將來對教會的威脅提供補救措施。通諭代表的權威會根據論及的議題而有所變化，但是它並不一定代表「[[教宗無謬誤|教宗無謬誤]]」。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Thurston|first1=H.|title=Encyclical|url=http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05413a.htm|publisher=Robert Appleton Company|accessdate=2018-02-08|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180208122405/http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05413a.htm|archivedate=2018-02-08|location=New York|language=en|year=1909|work=Catholic Encyclopedia|dead-url=no|via=New Advent}}</ref>教宗通諭的名稱取自文本的首幾個字。<ref name="Ad Petri cathedram">{{cite web|title=Religion: Ad Petri Cathedram |url=http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,869179,00.html |publisher=TIME |date=1959-07-13 |accessdate=2017-06-20 |language=en |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170620200546/http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0%2C9171%2C869179%2C00.html |archive-date=2017-06-20 |subscription=yes|dead-url=no}}</ref>

若望二十三世的第1道通諭《[[在伯多祿座位|在伯多祿座位]]》在他即位8個月後發出。它不是一份記述社會狀況的重要文件，而若望二十三世亦沒有闡述教義。相反，它以與眾不同的親近語調和對議題的關注來論述[[真理|真理]]、團結和和平。<ref name="Ad Petri cathedram" />第2道通諭《[[我們的鐸職之始|我們的鐸職之始]]》就談及{{link-en|若望·維雅納|John Vianney|聖若望·維雅納}}<ref name="Sacerdotii nostri primordia">{{cite web|author1=Ioannis PP. XXIII|title=SACERDOTII NOSTRI PRIMORDIA|url=http://w2.vatican.va/content/john-xxiii/la/encyclicals/documents/hf_j-xxiii_enc_19590801_sacerdotii.html|publisher=The Holy See|language= la|date=1959-08-01|accessdate=2017-06-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621150934/http://w2.vatican.va/content/john-xxiii/la/encyclicals/documents/hf_j-xxiii_enc_19590801_sacerdotii.html|archive-date=2017-06-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>，而第3道通諭《[[歡快回憶|歡快回憶]]》則講述《[[玫瑰經|玫瑰經]]》的使用<ref name="Grata recordatio">{{cite web|author1=Ioannis PP. XXIII|title=GRATA RECORDATIO|url=http://w2.vatican.va/content/john-xxiii/la/encyclicals/documents/hf_j-xxiii_enc_26091959_grata-recordatio.html|publisher=The Holy See|language= la|date=1959-09-26|accessdate=2017-06-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621151107/http://w2.vatican.va/content/john-xxiii/la/encyclicals/documents/hf_j-xxiii_enc_26091959_grata-recordatio.html|archive-date=2017-06-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>。第4道通諭《[[牧者的王子|牧者的王子]]》論述天主教會的使命，它使用《[[伯多祿前書|伯多祿前書]]》拉丁語版本裏第五章第4節的部分文字作為標題。<ref name="Princeps pastorum">{{cite web|author1=Ioannis PP. XXIII|title=PRINCEPS PASTORUM|url=http://w2.vatican.va/content/john-xxiii/la/encyclicals/documents/hf_j-xxiii_enc_28111959_princeps.html|publisher=The Holy See|language= la|date=1959-11-28|accessdate=2017-06-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621151202/http://w2.vatican.va/content/john-xxiii/la/encyclicals/documents/hf_j-xxiii_enc_28111959_princeps.html|archive-date=2017-06-21|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=EPISTULA I PETRI|url=http://www.vatican.va/archive/bible/nova_vulgata/documents/nova-vulgata_nt_epist-i-petri_lt.html|publisher=The Vatican|accessdate=2018-02-08|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180208143451/http://www.vatican.va/archive/bible/nova_vulgata/documents/nova-vulgata_nt_epist-i-petri_lt.html|archivedate=2018-02-08|language=la|dead-url=no}}</ref>

第5道通諭《[[慈母與導師|慈母與導師]]》將[[利奧十三世|教宗-{zh-hans:利奥;zh-hk:良;zh-tw:良;}-十三世]]於1891年發表的《[[新事通諭|新事]]》和[[教宗庇護十一世|教宗庇護十一世]]於1931年發表的《{{link-en|四十周年|Quadragesimo anno}}》裏所提倡的各個想法推進下去。它指出社會倫理最重要的一點是要將[[自然法|自然法]]應用於國際社會。<ref>{{Cite journal|last= Brown|first= Robert McAfee |year= 1964|title= Review of The Challenge of "Mater et magistra" |journal= Review of Religious Research|volume= 6|issue= 1|pages= 44–45|doi= 10.2307/3510885 |jstor= 3510885 |publisher= Religious Research Association, Inc. |last2= Moody |first2= Joseph N. |last3= Lawler |first3= J. George |postscript= . |language=en}}</ref>這道約20000字的文件{{NoteTag|name=word|字數以[[拉丁語|拉丁語]]（[[聖座|聖座]]官方語言<ref>{{cite web|title=About the Holy See|url=http://www.canadainternational.gc.ca/holy_see-saint_siege/consular_services_consulaires/about-a_propos.aspx?lang=eng|publisher=Government of Canada|language=en|accessdate=2017-06-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622180143/http://www.canadainternational.gc.ca/holy_see-saint_siege/consular_services_consulaires/about-a_propos.aspx?lang=eng|archive-date=2017-06-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>）版本作準。}}是其中一道最長的通諭。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Mater et Magistra |publisher=TIME |date=1961-07-21 |url=http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,897825,00.html |language=en |accessdate=2017-06-21 |subscription=yes |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621151710/http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0%2C9171%2C897825%2C00.html |archive-date=2017-06-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>第6道通諭《[[上主永恆的智慧|上主永恆的智慧]]》是為了紀念[[教宗|教宗]][[利奧一世_(教宗)|聖-{zh-hans:利奥;zh-hk:良}-一世]]離世1500周年而發出，當中若望二十三世呼籲基督徒在面對[[共產主義|共產主義]]和[[世俗主義|世俗主義]]的外部運動時要保持團結。<ref>{{Cite web|title=A Shepherd Calls |publisher=TIME |language=en |date=1961-12-15 |url=http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,827100,00.html |accessdate=2017-06-21 |subscription=yes |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621151821/http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0%2C9171%2C827100%2C00.html |archive-date=2017-06-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>教宗在第7道通諭《[[為罪懺悔|為罪懺悔]]》就懺悔和後來的[[梵蒂岡第二次大公會議|梵蒂岡第二次大公會議]]提出了一些看法。若望二十三世任內最後一道通諭《[[和平於世|和平於世]]》在他離世前兩個月發表。這道約15000字的文件{{NoteTag|name=word}}是歷史上第一道寫給「所有心存善意的人」的通諭，而不像先前的只寫給天主教會內的主教和[[平信徒|平信徒]]。有人認為它是「當時最令人印象深刻和最重要的文件之一」。<ref>{{Cite web|title=The Lasting Vision of Pope John |publisher=TIME |language=en |date=1965-02-26 |url=http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,833488,00.html |accessdate=2017-06-21 |subscription=yes |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170621151512/http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0%2C9171%2C833488%2C00.html |archive-date=2017-06-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>

《'''量子杂志'''》（''{{lang-en|Quanta Magazine}}''）是由{{le|西蒙斯基金会|Simons Foundation}}于2012年起发行的编辑独立的免费[[电子出版|在线出版物]]，内容涵盖[[数学|数学]]、[[理论物理学|理论物理学]]、[[理论计算机科学|理论计算机科学]]与基础[[生命科学|生命科学]]等。<ref>{{cite web |title=About Quanta Magazine |url=https://www.quantamagazine.org/about/ |work=Quanta Magazine |publisher=Simons Foundation}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Quanta Magazine |url=https://www.lms.ac.uk/editorial/quanta-magazine |publisher=[[London_Mathematical_Society|London Mathematical Society]] |accessdate=29 September 2016 |series=Press release}}</ref>其最初名为《西蒙斯科学新闻》（''Simons Science News''），2013年7月起改为现名。<ref>{{cite web |author=Thomas Lin|title=Simons Science News Is Renamed Quanta Magazine |url=https://www.quantamagazine.org/20130716-simons-science-news-is-renamed-quanta-magazine/ |work=Quanta Magazine |publisher=Simons Foundation |accessdate=30 September 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=Carl Zimmer|title=My New Story for Scientific American & Quanta Magazine |url=http://phenomena.nationalgeographic.com/2013/07/17/how-things-get-complex-my-new-story-for-scientific-american-quanta-magazine/ |publisher=National Geographic |accessdate=17 July 2013}}</ref>

《[[科学美国人|科学美国人]]》<ref>{{cite news|title=Stories by Quanta Magazine|url=http://www.scientificamerican.com/author/quanta-magazine/|accessdate=5 October 2016|work=Scientific American}}</ref>、《[[连线|连线]]》<ref>{{cite news|title=Quanta Magazine|url=https://www.wired.com/tag/quanta-magazine/|accessdate=5 October 2016|work=Wired}}</ref>、《[[大西洋_(杂志)|大西洋]]》等杂志也会采用《量子杂志》的稿件。科学新闻聚合网站''RealClearScience''将《量子杂志》列为2016年十大科学网站的第二名。<ref>{{cite news|title=The Top 10 Websites for Science in 2016 |url=http://www.realclearscience.com/blog/2016/11/14/the_top_10_websites_for_science_in_2016.html |publisher=RealClearScience |accessdate=14 November 2016}}</ref>

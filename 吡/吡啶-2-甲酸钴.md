'''吡啶-2-甲酸钴'''是一种有机酸盐，化学式为Co(C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>4</sub>NCOO)<sub>2</sub>，其二水合物中的水以配位水的形式存在<ref name=A />，四水合物除了两分子的配位水外，还有两分子的结晶水<ref name=Li />。它可由[[乙酸钴|乙酸钴]]和[[吡啶-2-甲酸|吡啶-2-甲酸]]按化学计量比反应得到。<ref name=A>{{cite journal|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0020169303002433|doi=10.1016/s0020-1693(03)00243-3|pages=129–138|title=Synthesis, characterisation and magnetic properties of cobalt(II) complexes with picolinic acid derivatives: the crystal and molecular structures of [Co(MeC5H3NCOO)2(H2O)2] and [CoCl2(C5H4NCOOPri)2]|volume=353|journal=Inorganica Chimica Acta|date=2003-09|issn=0020-1693|accessdate=2018-08-15|author=Ricard March, William Clegg, Robert A Coxall, Lourdes Cucurull-Sánchez, Luis Lezama, Teófilo Rojo, Pilar González-Duarte}}</ref>四水合物在96°C失去结晶水，104°C失去配位水，在401°C生成[[2,2'-联吡啶|2,2'-联吡啶]]以及[[草酸钴|草酸钴]]。<ref name=Li />三价钴的吡啶-2-甲酸盐也有研究。<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://xlink.rsc.org/?DOI=dt9860000619|issue=3|doi=10.1039/dt9860000619|pages=619|title=Redox reactions of some iron(II), iron(III), and cobalt(II) picolinate complexes|journal=Journal of the Chemical Society, Dalton Transactions|date=1986|issn=0300-9246|volume=0|language=en|accessdate=2018-08-15|author=A. Martin Lannon, A. Graham Lappin, Michael G. Segal}}</ref>

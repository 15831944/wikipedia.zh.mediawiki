'''吡啶-2-甲酸锌'''是一种有机酸盐，化学式为Zn(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>4</sub>O<sub>2</sub>N)<sub>2</sub>，或简写为ZnPic。<ref>Paavo Lumme, Georg Lundgren, Wanda Mark, [http://actachemscand.org/pdf/acta_vol_23_p3011-3022.pdf "The crystal structure of zinc picolinate tetrahydrate], ''Acta Chemica Scandinavica'', vol. 23, pp. 3011-3022, 1969</ref>它可由[[吡啶-2-甲酸|吡啶-2-甲酸]]和[[硫酸锌|硫酸锌]]反应制得，所用的吡啶-2-甲酸可由[[2-甲基吡啶|2-甲基吡啶]]的氧化反应得到。<ref>丁彩峰, 朱小刚, 刘 芳,等. 2-吡啶甲酸锌的合成方法:, CN 101597257 A[P]. 2009.</ref><ref>张爱娟. 吡啶羧酸金属络活物的合成和应用[J]. 乙醛醋酸化工, 2005(12):34-36.</ref>

它可用作膳食补锌剂。<ref>Carol Walsh, Harold Sandstead, Ananda S Prasad, Paul M Newberne,Pamela J, Fraker, [https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC1567081/ "Zinc: health effects and research priorities for the 1990s"] ''Environmental Health Perspectives'', vol. 102 (supplement 2), pp. 5-46, June,1994.</ref><ref>Fumitaka Sakai, Shinya Yoshida, Sohei Endo & Hiroshi Tomita, [https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/00016480260046517 "Double-blind, Placebo-controlled Trial of Zinc Picolinate for Taste Disorders"], ''Acta Oto-Laryngologica'', vol. 122, iss. 4, pp. 129-133, 2002.</ref><ref>S A Barrie, J V Wright, J E Pizzorno, E Kutter, P C Barron, [https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/BF01974946 "Comparative absorption of zinc picolinate, zinc citrate and zinc gluconate in humans"], ''Agents and Actions'', vol. 21, iss. 1-2, pp. 223-228, June 1987.</ref>

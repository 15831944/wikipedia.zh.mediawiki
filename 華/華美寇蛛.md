'''華美寇蛛'''（'''''Latrodectus elegans'''''）是[[寇蛛属|寇蛛属]]的一種蜘蛛<ref name="ITIS standard report - Latrodectus elegans  Thorell, 1898">{{cite web|url=http://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt?search_topic=TSN&search_value=859128|title=''Latrodectus elegans'' Thorell, 1898|publisher=[[Integrated_Taxonomic_Information_System|Integrated Taxonomic Information System]]|accessdate=14 October 2012}}</ref><ref name="American Museum of Natural History - World Spider Catalog - Latrodectus elegans Thorell, 1898">{{cite web|url=http://research.amnh.org/oonopidae/catalog/references.php?id=38316|title=''Latrodectus elegans'' Thorell, 1898|work=World Spider Catalog|publisher=[[American_Museum_of_Natural_History|American Museum of Natural History]]|accessdate=14 October 2012}}</ref>，分佈於東亞，如中國西南、華南和日本等地<ref>{{cite web|last=Nieuwenhuys|first=Ed|title=The demystification of the toxicity of spiders|url=http://ednieuw.home.xs4all.nl/Spiders/Nasty-Spiders/Demystification-toxicity-spiders.htm}}</ref>，正模標本發現於[[緬甸|緬甸]]山區<ref>{{cite journal|author1=Kananbala, A.|author2=Manoj, K.|author3=Bhubaneshwari, M.|author4=Binarani, A.|author5=Siliwal, Manju|date=2012|title=The first report of the widow spider  ''Latrodectus elegans'' (Araneae:  Theridiidae) from India|journal=Journal of Threatened Taxa|volume=4|issue=7|pages=2718–22|url=http://threatenedtaxa.org/ZooPrintJournal/2012/July/July_2012.pdf#page=48|doi=10.11609/jott.o3152.2718-22|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121101074818/http://threatenedtaxa.org/ZooPrintJournal/2012/July/July_2012.pdf#page=48|archivedate=2012年11月1日|df=}}</ref>。成年雌蛛体长约8-9mm，生物學分類上曾被當做[[紅斑寇蛛|紅斑寇蛛]]<ref>{{cite web | url= http://courseware.eduwest.com/courseware/0425/content/ziyuan/u4.htm  |title= 球蛛科 |  publisher= 西南大學 | accessdate= 2018-03-01}}</ref>。

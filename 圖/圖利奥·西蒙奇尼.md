'''圖利奥·西蒙奇尼'''（Tullio Simoncini，{{bd|1951年}}）曾經是義大利醫生，也是[[替代医学|替代医学]]的倡導者。他在[[罗马大学|罗马大学]]獲得博士學位。他聲稱[[癌症|癌症]]是因為[[白色念珠菌|白色念珠菌]]所造成的，認為癌症是白色念珠菌過度繁殖的症狀<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/kim-evans/antibiotics-cause-cancer_b_186968.html | title=Antibiotics Cause Cancer? | work=Huffington Post | date=2009-04-15 | accessdate=2014-03-20 | author=Evans, Kim}}</ref>，他也提出可以用注射[[碳酸氢钠|碳酸氢钠]]的方式來治療癌症<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.express.co.uk/expressyourself/310367/Dangerous-ideas-of-doctor-who-defies-baking-soda-cancer-cure | title=Dangerous ideas of doctor who defies baking soda cancer cure | work= Daily Express | date=2012-03-25 | accessdate=2014-03-20 | author=Parsley, David}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://alternativemedicinenis.com.au/Cancer%20Fungus.pdf | title=Is the Cause of Cancer a Common Fungus? | work=Nexus | date=2007-08 | accessdate=2014-03-23 | author=Simoncini, Tullio}}</ref>。圖利奥·西蒙奇尼在他的網站上自稱是[[肿瘤学|肿瘤学家]]，不過因為他用注射碳酸氢钠來治療癌症的作法，醫學社會已質疑他的專業身份<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-england-devon-17441970 | title=Totnes cancer conference investigated by Trading Standards | work=BBC News | date=2012-03-20 | accessdate=2014-03-20}}</ref>

有關圖利奥·西蒙奇尼的假說，還無法找到經過同行評審，且支持其論點的研究，因此目前主流醫學反對他癌症治療的論點<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.cancer.org/treatment/treatmentsandsideeffects/complementaryandalternativemedicine/herbsvitaminsandminerals/sodium-bicarbonate | title=Sodium Bicarbonate | work=American Cancer Society|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140203155835/http://www.cancer.org/treatment/treatmentsandsideeffects/complementaryandalternativemedicine/herbsvitaminsandminerals/sodium-bicarbonate|archivedate=2014-02-03 | accessdate=2014-03-20}}</ref>。《{{link-en|Quackwatch|Quackwatch}}》將碳酸氢钠注射治療列為「可疑的治療方式」<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.quackwatch.org/00AboutQuackwatch/altseek.html | title=A Special Message for Cancer Patients Seeking "Alternative" Treatments | work=Quackwatch | date=2013-09-21 | accessdate=2014-03-20}}</ref>。

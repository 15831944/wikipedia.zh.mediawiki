'''圖書館貓'''（{{lang-en|Library cat}}）是指生活在全世界[[公共圖書館|公共圖書館]]裡的[[貓|貓]]。貓與圖書館的關係可以追溯到古代，[[古埃及|古埃及]]神廟的圖書館為了控制[[囓齒動物|囓齒動物]]而飼養著貓<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.catchannel.com/magazines/catfancy/january-2010/history-of-library-cats.aspx|title=History of Library Cats|accessdate=2018-04-05|author=Allie B. Kagamaster|date=2009-11-11|publisher=Cat Breeders|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140202092837/http://www.catchannel.com/magazines/catfancy/january-2010/history-of-library-cats.aspx|archivedate=2014-02-02}}</ref>。

== 歷史 ==
[[File:Ju_IMG_1254.JPG|缩略图]]一間圖書館裡的貓咪。]]
貓和圖書館之間的關係已有數個世紀的歷史<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2002/feb/18/news/lv-cat18|title=In the Wonderland of Libraries Are Cats Like Alis|last=NORIYUKI|first=DUANE|date=2002-02-18|newspaper=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=2018-04-04|language=en-US|deadurl=no|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20130621015851/http://articles.latimes.com/2002/feb/18/news/lv-cat18|archivedate=2013-06-21}}</ref>。[[希羅多德|希羅多德]]描述了古埃及神廟中的圖書館飼養[[貓科動物|貓科動物]]，以防止囓齒動物和[[蛇|蛇]]破壞[[莎草|莎草]]卷。[[中世紀|中世紀]]的[[修道院|修道院]]也會養貓，用於防止[[老鼠|老鼠]]吃掉手稿。[[十九世紀|十九世紀]]期間，[[英國政府|英國政府]]對飼養貓的圖書館提供了[[補助|補助]]，因為貓能捕捉囓齒動物，保護書本<ref name=":0" />。

在現代，圖書館貓已成為出現在書和[[電影|電影]]中的角色，且在公共關係與營銷上有著重要地位<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.wesleyancollege.edu/academics/library/cats.cfm|title=Wesleyan's Library Cats|accessdate=2018-04-04|date=2015-04-02|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402100523/http://www.wesleyancollege.edu/academics/library/cats.cfm|archivedate=2015-04-02}}</ref>。但有時圖書館貓也會引起爭議，例如曾經有對貓[[過敏|過敏]]的顧客試圖要驅逐[[麻薩諸塞州|麻薩諸塞州]][[斯溫西公共圖書館|斯溫西公共圖書館]]裡的貓，並聲稱圖書館的作法違反[[美國殘疾人法案|美國殘疾人法案]]，最後案子以雙方和解告終<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://lj.libraryjournal.com/2013/05/opinion/editorial/send-me-your-library-cats/|title=Send Me Your Library Cats|accessdate=2018-04-04|author=Michael Kelley|date=2013-05-17|publisher=lj.libraryjournal.com|language=en-US|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171010104553/http://lj.libraryjournal.com/2013/05/opinion/editorial/send-me-your-library-cats/|archivedate=2017-10-10}}</ref>。

除了圖書館之外，[[美國|美國]]的一些[[獨立書店|獨立書店]]也會養貓<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.abebooks.com/books/bookseller-bookshop-bookstore/cats.shtml?clickid=UzCzFzz-4wlPwzzSgl3i60fFUkWUqo0P0V4awQ0&cm_mmc=aff-_-ir-_-27795-_-81804&afn_sr=impact|title=A Catalog of Bookstore Cats|accessdate=2018-04-04|work=AbeBooks.com|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304064602/http://www.abebooks.com/books/bookseller-bookshop-bookstore/cats.shtml?clickid=UzCzFzz-4wlPwzzSgl3i60fFUkWUqo0P0V4awQ0&cm_mmc=aff-_-ir-_-27795-_-81804&afn_sr=impact|archivedate=2016-03-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://journalstar.com/entertainment/small-screen/television-and-radio/what-is-a-cat/article_db1f1969-37d9-53cb-ba10-293971218abd.html|title=What is a cat?|last=Korbelik|first=Jeff|newspaper=JournalStar.com|accessdate=2018-04-04|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171010155234/http://journalstar.com/entertainment/small-screen/television-and-radio/what-is-a-cat/article_db1f1969-37d9-53cb-ba10-293971218abd.html|archivedate=2017-10-10}}</ref>。

== 名貓 ==
[[杜威·樂讀·布克斯|杜威·樂讀·布克斯]]也許是最著名的圖書館貓，牠住在美國[[愛荷華州|愛荷華州]]的[[斯潘塞_(艾奥瓦州)|史班賽]]公共圖書館已有十九年，知名於全世界。在杜威去世後，曾任史班賽公共圖書館館長的[[薇琪·麥蓉|薇琪·麥蓉]]出版了牠的傳記《[[圖書館裡的貓|-{zh-hans:小猫杜威;zh-hant:圖書館裡的貓;}-]]》及一些相關書籍<ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2007/04/04/books/04cat.html|title=Iowa Library’s Cat Has a Rich Second Life as a Biography|last=Rich|first=Motoko|date=2007-04-04|newspaper=The New York Times|accessdate=2018-04-04|language=en-US|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171111214428/http://www.nytimes.com/2007/04/04/books/04cat.html|archivedate=2017-11-11}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://zqb.cyol.com/html/2013-07/09/nw.D110000zgqnb_20130709_3-09.htm|title=图书馆之猫：安静陪伴的力量|accessdate=2018-04-05|author=蒋肖斌|date=2013-07-09|publisher=中国青年报|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150829221727/http://zqb.cyol.com/html/2013-07/09/nw.D110000zgqnb_20130709_3-09.htm|archivedate=2015-08-29}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.merit-times.com.tw/NewsPage.aspx?Unid=220897|title=【出版眼】圖書館貓杜威 躍上有聲書|accessdate=2018-08-09|author=王岫|date=2011-03-20|publisher=人間福報}}</ref>。

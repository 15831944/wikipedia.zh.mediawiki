'''箭烏賊'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Belemnotheutis'''''}}）是生存於晚[[侏羅紀|侏羅紀]]的一[[屬|屬]][[箭石|箭石]]，生活在中等深度的[[海域|海域]]中，其身體特徵與現代的[[烏賊|烏賊]]相似<ref>{{cite web | language = zh-cn | publisher = 中國日報網| title =英國發現侏羅紀箭烏賊化石1.5億年前墨汁仍能寫出字| url = http://www2.chinadaily.com.cn/hqzx/2009-08/20/content_8591347.htm| date = 2009-08-20| accessdate = 2011-02-16 }}</ref>，與烏賊一樣利用從套膜後部噴水的方式在水中游動，利用帶有小鉤和吸盤的10條臂肢捕捉獵物。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author={{link-en|西里爾·沃克 (古生物學家)|Cyril Walker (palaeontologist)|Walker, Cyril}} | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=165 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>牠們的[[化石|化石]]目前只被發現於[[歐洲|歐洲]]。<ref name="jeletzky">Jeletzky, J.A. 1966. Comparative Morphology, Phylogeny, and Classification of Fossil Coleoidea; Mollusca pp 1–162; The University of Kansas, Paleontological Contributions [http://kuscholarworks.ku.edu/dspace/handle/1808/3816]</ref>

== 特徵 ==
箭烏賊長有十條臂肢，臂肢上有小鉤及吸盤。頭部和烏賊一樣有二片[[鰭|鰭]]。後部是帶腔室的多隔[[圓錐|圓錐]]，向後呈錐狀縮小至尖端。尾部薄薄的護甲有時會保存下來。<ref name="Fossils" />

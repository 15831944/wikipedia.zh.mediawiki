'''始爪獸科'''（[[學名|學名]]Eomoropidae）是一[[科|科]][[奇蹄目|奇蹄目]]。牠們是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[爪獸科|爪獸科]]的近親，不單外觀相似，更有可能是其直接祖先。牠們的體型較為細小，只有[[羊|羊]]的大小。牠們的腳上有爪。<ref name=MamEv>{{cite book en|author=Savage, RJG, & Long, MR|year=1986|title=Mammal Evolution: an illustrated guide|publisher=Facts on File|location=New York|pages=199|id=ISBN 0-8160-1194-X}}</ref>始爪獸科很有可能是[[爪獸總科|爪獸總科]]內的[[並系群|並系群]]。<ref name=EoTMNA>{{Cite book en|last=Jacobs|first=Louis L.|coauthors=Scott, Kathleen Marie|title=Evolution of Tertiary Mammals of North America: Terrestrial carnivores, ungulates, and ungulatelike mammals|publisher=Cambridge University Press|date=1998|id=ISBN 0521355192, 9780521355193}}</ref>

'''伍德拜恩'''（{{lang-en|Woodbine}}）是美国[[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[哈里森縣_(愛阿華州)|哈里森县]]的一座城市，名字来源于原来城市周围密集生长的忍冬属植物（woodbine）<ref>{{cite book|author=Chicago and North Western Railway Company|title=A History of the Origin of the Place Names Connected with the Chicago & North Western and Chicago, St. Paul, Minneapolis & Omaha Railways|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OspBAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA140|year=1908|page=140}}</ref>。坐标为{{coord|41|44|15|N|95|42|20|W|type:city}}<ref name="GR1">{{cite web|url=https://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/gazette.html|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2011-04-23|date=2011-02-12|title=US Gazetteer files: 2010, 2000, and 1990}}</ref>；根据[[美国人口调查局|美国人口调查局]]的数据，城市总面积为{{convert|1.33|sqmi|sqkm|2}}，其中陆地面积为{{convert|1.28|sqmi|sqkm|2}}，水域面积为{{convert|0.05|sqmi|sqkm|2}}<ref name ="Gazetteer files">{{cite web|title=US Gazetteer files 2010|url=https://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2012-05-11}}</ref>。

[[2010年美國人口普查|2010年美國人口普查]]数据显示，当地共有1,459位居民，611户，389个家庭，[[人口密度|人口密度]]为{{convert|1139.8|PD/sqmi|PD/km2|1}}；种族构成中，98.8%为白人，0.1%为非裔美国人，0.1% 为土著，0.5%为亚裔，0.2%为其他种族，0.3%为两个或以上种族混血；居民年龄中位数为44.9岁，男女性别比例分别为47.8%与52.2%<ref name ="FactFinder">{{cite web|title=American FactFinder|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/nav/jsf/pages/index.xhtml|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2012-05-11}}</ref>。

'''伍林试剂'''（'''Woollins试剂'''、'''WR'''）是一种含[[磷|磷]]和[[硒|硒]]的有机化合物。它是[[劳森试剂|劳森试剂]]的类似物，主要用于硒化反应。它的命名来自任职于[[圣安德鲁斯大学|圣安德鲁斯大学]]的研究副校长John Derek Woollins教授。<ref>[http://chemistry.st-andrews.ac.uk/staff/jdw/group/home.html Prof J. Derek Woollins主页]. [2017-8-18]</ref>

== 制备 ==
伍林试剂可以直接从试剂供应商购买，也能通过加热[[二氯苯基膦|二氯苯基膦]]和[[硒化钠|硒化钠]]的混合物得到，其中硒化钠通过[[硒|硒]]和[[钠|钠]]在[[液氨|液氨]]中的反应制备。<ref name="gray">{{cite journal | author = Ian P. Gray, Pravat Bhattacharyya, Alexandra M. Z. Slawin and J. Derek Woollins | journal = [[Chem._Eur._J.|Chem. Eur. J.]] | doi = 10.1002/chem.200500291 | title = A New Synthesis of (PhPSe2)2 (Woollins Reagent) and Its Use in the Synthesis of Novel P-Se Heterocycles | year = 2005 | volume = 11 | pages = 6221–7 | pmid = 16075451 | issue = 21}}</ref>五聚物(PPh)<sub>5</sub>和单质[[硒|硒]]的反应也能制得该物。<ref name="pilkington">{{cite journal | author = M. J. Pilkington, Alexandra M. Z. Slawin and J. Derek Woollins | journal = [[Heteroatom_Chemistry|Heteroatom Chemistry]] | year = 1990 | pages = 351 | doi = 10.1002/hc.520010502 | issue = 5 | title = The preparation and characterization of binary phosphorus-selenium rings | volume = 1}}</ref>

== 用途 ==
伍林试剂的主要用途是[[羰基|羰基]]化合物的硒化。<ref name="Bhattacharyya">{{cite journal |author1=Pravat Bhattacharyya  |author2=J. Derek Woollins  |lastauthoramp=yes | journal = [[Tet._Lett.|Tet. Lett.]] | volume = 42 | issue = 34 | year = 2001 | pages = 5949 | doi = 10.1016/S0040-4039(01)01113-3 | title = Selenocarbonyl synthesis using Woollins reagent}}</ref>例如，伍林试剂可以将羰基转化为硒代羰基（=CSe）。此外，伍林试剂也被用于[[羧酸|羧酸]]、[[烯烃|烯烃]]、[[炔烃|炔烃]]或[[腈|腈]]的二聚反应。<ref name="Hua">{{cite journal |author1=Guoxiong Hua  |author2=J. Derek Woollins  |lastauthoramp=yes | journal = [[Angewandte_Chemie|Angew. Chem. Int. Ed.]] | volume = 48 | issue = 8 | year = 2009 | pages = 1368–1377 | doi = 10.1002/anie.200800572 | title = Formation and Reactivity of Phosphorus-Selenium Rings | pmid = 19053094}}</ref>

'''衍射分光鏡'''<ref name="www.amazon.com">[http://www.amazon.com/Diffraction-Gratings-Applications-Optical-Engineering/dp/0824799232#reader_0824799232 Diffraction Gratings and Applications, Loewen, Erwin C. and Popov, Evgeny. Marcel Dekker, Inc. 1997.]</ref>
<ref name="www.amazon.com1">[http://www.amazon.com/Digital-Diffractive-Optics-Introduction-Technology/dp/0471984477#reader_0471984477 Digital diffractive optics: an introduction to planar diffractive optics and related technology, Bernard C. Kress, Patrick Meyrueis , 2005.]</ref>為一[[光學儀器|光學儀器]]，能將一個光束分成多個光束<ref name="books.google.com">[http://books.google.com/books?id=NFrG-zFrIDYC&lpg=PP3&dq=diffractive%20optics&pg=PA83#v=twopage&q&f=false Diffractive Optics – Design, Fabrication and Test, O'Shea, Suleski, Kathman and Prather, 2004. p.83]</ref>，並排成陣列（1xN）或矩陣（MxN），而這些光束的特性都是一樣的。一個衍射分光鏡是專為一種[[波長|波長]]的光和產生特定繞射角的光束而設計。

==原理==
[[File:Phase_image.jpg|thumb]]
此儀器運用了光線的[[繞射|繞射]]特性，亦類似於[[繞射光柵|繞射光柵]]的設計，用特定的重複圖樣令繞射光束排列成特定的組合。

==應用==
此儀器可與一[[凸透鏡|凸透鏡]]合用，用以聚焦繞射光於一平面之上，並能用於科研及工業用途，如：
* [[雷射切割|雷射切割]]和刻劃，用以製造[[太陽能電池|太陽能電池]]和[[液晶顯示器|液晶顯示器]]等
* 光束取樣<ref name="LC">[http://www.lasercomponents.com/fileadmin/user_upload/home/Datasheets/holoor/beam_sampler.pdf Beam sampler]</ref>
* 動作感應，如[[Kinect|Kinect]]等<ref name="primesense">[http://www.google.com/patents/about?id=6WFWAQAAEBAJ OPTICAL DESIGNS FOR ZERO ORDER REDUCTION(patent)]</ref>
* 皮膚治療及美化<ref name="medical1">[http://www.photonics.com/Article.aspx?AID=42337 Fractional Laser Skin Treatment Using Diffractive Optics]</ref>

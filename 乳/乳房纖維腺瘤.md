'''纖維腺瘤'''（{{lang-en|Fibroadenoma}}）是從乳房小葉組織中生長出來，混和上皮與間質組織的腫瘤，發生率為18～20％，好發於年輕女性，為女性最常見的良性乳房疾病<ref name = Greenberg>{{cite journal | vauthors = Greenberg R, Skornick Y, Kaplan O | title = Management of breast fibroadenomas | journal = J Gen Intern Med | volume = 13 | issue = 9 | pages = 640-5 | date = 1998}}</ref>，通常沒有其他徵候<ref name="Pilnik">{{cite book |editor=Samuel Pilnik |title=Common Breast Lesions: A Photographic Guide to Diagnosis and Treatment |publisher=Cambridge University Press |year=2003 |isbn=9780521823579 |url=https://books.google.com.tw/books?id=Xu1ff3CnkZ8C |page=4 |access-date=2018-09-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180906195720/https://books.google.com.tw/books?id=Xu1ff3CnkZ8C |archive-date=2018-09-06 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。與乳癌典型症狀不同，乳房纖維腺瘤通常有界線明確、易於移動的特色<ref>{{MerckHome|22|251|c|Fibroadenomas}}</ref>。因為乳房腫塊為[[乳癌|乳房癌症]]的可能症狀，建議要接受超音波攝影和組織取樣來確診<ref name="WHO">{{cite book |editor=Tavassoli, F.A. |editor2=Devilee, P. |title= World Health Organization Classification of Tumours: Pathology & Genetics: Tumours of the breast and female genital organs |publisher=IARC Press |location=Lyon |year=2003 |isbn=92-832-2412-4 |url=}}{{page needed|date=February 2017}}</ref>。

== 流行病學 ==

乳房纖維腺瘤最常出現於生育年齡的女性，特別是小於30歲，不過仍可生長於各種年齡層<ref name="WHO 2012">{{cite book |editor=Lakhani, S.R. |editor2=Ellis. I.O. |title=WHO Classification of Tumours of the Breast |publisher=IARC Press |location=Lyon |year=2012 |isbn=9789283224334 |url=http://apps.who.int/bookorders/anglais/detart1.jsp?codlan=1&codcol=70&codcch=4004 |page=142 |access-date=2018-09-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180112005842/http://apps.who.int/bookorders/anglais/detart1.jsp?codlan=1&codcol=70&codcch=4004 |archive-date=2018-01-12 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

== 症狀 ==
典型症狀為生育年齡的女性胸部出現單顆無痛結實、邊界清晰、生長緩慢、可自由推動的腫塊，通常小於3公分<ref name=WHO/><ref name = DeMay>{{cite book |author=DeMay, M. |title=Practical Principles of Cytopathology |publisher=ASCP Press |isbn=0-89189-549-3 |pages=2007 |edition=Revised}}{{page needed|date=February 2017}}</ref><ref name=POW>Pathology Outlines Website. [http://pathologyoutlines.com/breast.html#fibroadenoma] {{Wayback|url=http://pathologyoutlines.com/breast.html |date=20180907144758 }} Accessed 12 February 2009.</ref>。在少數情況，同側或兩個乳房中，同時或先後出現多顆腫塊，甚至可以成長到20公分；或者在乳房攝影中發現結節、鈣化或觸摸不出來的小型腫塊<ref name="WHO 2012" />。當腫瘤內出血時，疼痛可能是某些個案的第一個徵候<ref name="Pilnik" />。

乳房纖維腺瘤大部分是良性的，癌化的機會在1000人中约有3人。因為乳房纖維腺瘤從小葉中生長出來，{{le|小葉癌|Invasive lobular carcinoma}}是乳房纖維腺瘤裡面最常發現的癌症，也有期刊報告過[[乳腺管原位癌|乳腺管原位癌]]的發生<ref name="Pilnik" />。

在男性乳房發現的大部分是{{le|乳腺叶状肿瘤|Phyllodes tumor}}，乳房纖維腺瘤非常罕見，除非是接受[[抗雄激素|抗雄激素]]治療的患者<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Shin SJ, Rosen PP | title = Bilateral presentation of fibroadenoma with digital fibroma-like inclusions in the male breast | journal = Archives of Pathology & Laboratory Medicine | volume = 131 | issue = 7 | pages = 1126–9 | date = July 2007 | pmid = 17617003 | doi = 10.1043/1543-2165(2007)131[1126:BPOFWD]2.0.CO;2  | doi-broken-date = 2017-08-21 }}</ref>。

== 發生原因 ==

乳房纖維腺瘤與荷爾蒙有關，經常在停經後消退。大量攝取蔬菜水果、高生育數量、少使用口服避孕藥以及適當運動與腫瘤低發生率相關<ref name=Nelson_2010>{{cite journal | vauthors = Nelson ZC, Ray RM, Wu C, Stalsberg H, Porter P, Lampe JW, Shannon J, Horner N, Li W, Wang W, Hu Y, Gao D, Thomas DB | title = Fruit and vegetable intakes are associated with lower risk of breast fibroadenomas in Chinese women | journal = The Journal of Nutrition | volume = 140 | issue = 7 | pages = 1294–301 | date = July 2010 | pmid = 20484549 | pmc = 2884330 | doi = 10.3945/jn.109.119719 }}</ref>。

== 病理學 ==

===巨觀===
腫瘤呈現圓形或卵形，具有彈性與光滑表面，切面通常為同質紮實的灰白色或黃褐色組織。小管周型具有完整被膜與漩渦狀的外表，而小管內型則擁有不完整的被膜<ref name="Rosen">{{cite book |author=Rosen, PP. |title=Rosen's Breast Pathology |isbn=978-0-7817-7137-5 |edition=3rd|year=2009 }}{{page needed|date=February 2017}}</ref>。

===微觀===
乳房纖維腺瘤呈現基質與上皮組織的雙重增生，可以呈現小管周型（基質圍繞著上皮結構）和小管內型（增生的基質壓縮上皮結構而形成裂縫）兩種型態，但沒有臨床表現的差別<ref name="WHO" />。與惡性腫瘤相比，乳房纖維腺瘤擁有較少血管的基質<ref name="WHO" /><ref name="POW" /><ref name="Rosen">{{cite book |author=Rosen, PP. |title=Rosen's Breast Pathology |isbn=978-0-7817-7137-5 |edition=3rd|year=2009 }}{{page needed|date=February 2017}}</ref>。而且，在單一終末腺管單元中，纖維母細胞基質包圍增生的上皮而形成管狀空間<ref name="titleFibroadenoma of the breast">{{cite web |url=http://www.pathologyatlas.ro/fibroadenoma-breast-pathology.php |title=Fibroadenoma of the breast |accessdate=2007-12-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090310094645/http://www.pathologyatlas.ro/fibroadenoma-breast-pathology.php |archive-date=2009-03-10 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

===分子===
高達66％的乳房纖維腺瘤有{{le|MED12|MED12}}基因的外顯子突變，但這些突變侷限於基質部分<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Lim WK, Ong CK, Tan J, Thike AA, Ng CC, Rajasegaran V, Myint SS, Nagarajan S, Nasir ND, McPherson JR, Cutcutache I, Poore G, Tay ST, Ooi WS, Tan VK, Hartman M, Ong KW, Tan BK, Rozen SG, Tan PH, Tan P, Teh BT | title = Exome sequencing identifies highly recurrent MED12 somatic mutations in breast fibroadenoma | journal = Nature Genetics | volume = 46 | issue = 8 | pages = 877–80 | date = August 2014 | pmid = 25038752 | doi = 10.1038/ng.3037 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Piscuoglio S, Murray M, Fusco N, Marchiò C, Loo FL, Martelotto LG, Schultheis AM, Akram M, Weigelt B, Brogi E, Reis-Filho JS | title = MED12 somatic mutations in fibroadenomas and phyllodes tumours of the breast | journal = Histopathology | volume = 67 | issue = 5 | pages = 719–29 | date = November 2015 | pmid = 25855048 | pmc = 4996373 | doi = 10.1111/his.12712 }}</ref>。

==治療==
大多數的乳房纖維腺瘤只需要持續追蹤，其他則是接受切除手術，只需要少量切口邊緣組織<ref name = Rosen/><ref name = Rosai>{{cite book |author=Rosai, J. |title=Rosai and Ackerman's Surgical Pathology |year=2004 |isbn=978-0-323-01342-0 |edition=9th}}{{page needed|date=February 2017}}</ref>。因為細針抽吸細胞診斷常是可靠的診斷工具，有些醫師決定不開刀切除，而使用臨床理學檢查與乳房攝影追蹤病灶的生長速度。小於50歲的女性每個月小於16％的生長速度，或大於50歲女性有小於13％的速度，是可以持續追蹤不需要開刀的安全速度<ref name = Gordon>{{cite journal | vauthors = Gordon PB, Gagnon FA, Lanzkowsky L | title = Solid breast masses diagnosed as fibroadenoma at fine-needle aspiration biopsy: acceptable rates of growth at long-term follow-up | journal = Radiology | volume = 229 | issue = 1 | pages = 233–8 | date = October 2003 | pmid = 14519878 | doi = 10.1148/radiol.2291010282 }}</ref>。

奧美昔芬對某些乳房纖維腺瘤患者有效<ref name=Dhar_2007>{{cite journal | vauthors = Dhar A, Srivastava A | title = Role of centchroman in regression of mastalgia and fibroadenoma | journal = World Journal of Surgery | volume = 31 | issue = 6 | pages = 1178–84 | date = June 2007 | pmid = 17431715 | pmc =  | doi = 10.1007/s00268-007-9040-4 }}</ref>。

在完全切除後，乳房纖維腺瘤大部分沒有復發的現象<ref name = WHO/>。

===冷凍療法===
[[美國食品藥品監督管理局|美國食品藥品監督管理局]]在2001年核准冷凍療法為手術切除外的一種安全有效、最小侵入性治療選項<ref name="Breast Surgeons">{{Cite web |url=https://www.breastsurgeons.org/new_layout/about/statements/PDF_Statements/Fibroadenoma.pdf |title=Management of Fibroadenomas of the Breast |access-date=2018-09-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170710124943/https://www.breastsurgeons.org/new_layout/about/statements/PDF_Statements/Fibroadenoma.pdf |archive-date=2017-07-10 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。在此治療過程中，超音波導引探針進入乳房腫瘤部位，然後使用極度低溫來摧毀異常細胞<ref name="WebMD">{{Cite web |url=http://women.webmd.com/news/20031204/cryotherapy-shrinks-benign-breast-lumps |title=WebMD — Cryotherapy Shrinks Benign Breast Lumps |access-date=2018-09-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140208115743/http://women.webmd.com/news/20031204/cryotherapy-shrinks-benign-breast-lumps |archive-date=2014-02-08 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，細胞隨後被重新吸收到體內。這項療法可以在門診進行，並只需使用局部麻醉，比起手術留下較少疤痕組織，而且較少乳房變形的後遺症<ref name="WebMD"/>。

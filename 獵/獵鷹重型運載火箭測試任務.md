'''獵鷹重型運載火箭演示任务'''（Falcon Heavy demonstration mission），也称为'''獵鷹重型運載火箭測試飞行'''（Falcon Heavy test flight），是[[SpaceX|SpaceX]]於2018年2月6日20:45[[UTC|UTC]]的任務，於任務中SpaceX第一次嘗試發射[[獵鷹重型運載火箭|獵鷹重型運載火箭]]。<ref name="falconheavypresskit_v1">{{cite web|url=http://www.spacex.com/sites/spacex/files/falconheavypresskit_v1.pdf|title=Falcon Heavy Demonstration Press Kit|date=February 2018|publisher=[[SpaceX|SpaceX]]|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180206143738/http://www.spacex.com/sites/spacex/files/falconheavypresskit_v1.pdf|archivedate=2018-02-06|deadurl=no}}</ref>該任務所使用的獵鷹重型運載火箭也成為了目前推力最大的現役商用火箭，<ref name="Guardian-2018-02-6">{{cite newspaper|newspaper=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|url=https://www.theguardian.com/science/live/2018/feb/06/spacex-falcon-heavy-launch-elon-musk-live-updates|title=SpaceX Falcon Heavy launch: world's most powerful rocket blasts off – live|first=Alan|last=Yuhas|date=2018-02-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180206215645/https://www.theguardian.com/science/live/2018/feb/06/spacex-falcon-heavy-launch-elon-musk-live-updates|archivedate=2018-02-06}}</ref> 擁有<span>22,000,000[[牛頓_(單位)|牛頓]]的</span>推力，是[[NASA|NASA]][[太空梭|太空梭]]的兩倍以上。<ref name="Space.com-2018-02-06">{{cite web|url=https://www.space.com/39603-spacex-falcon-heavy-rocket-by-the-numbers.html|title=SpaceX's Falcon Heavy Rocket: By the Numbers|date=2018-02-06|last=Salazar|first=Doris Elin|publisher=Space.com|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180206145556/https://www.space.com/39603-spacex-falcon-heavy-rocket-by-the-numbers.html|archivedate=2018-02-06|deadurl=no}} </ref>

== 任務概要 ==
该任务是[[獵鷹重型運載火箭|獵鷹重型運載火箭]]的測試飛行，用以演示此火箭的能力，同时在過程中收集遙測數據。

=== 運載物 ===
用于这次试飞的{{Tsl|en|Test payload|測試運載物}}是SpaceX執行長[[伊隆·马斯克|伊隆·马斯克]]的[[伊隆·馬斯克的特斯拉Roadster|櫻桃紅跑车]]，為[[特斯拉Roadster|特斯拉Roadster]]中的第一代。SpaceX指出，這次任務的運載物必須是「有趣的、没有無可取代的情感价值」的東西。坐在跑車驾驶座的假人「Starman」，是一個穿着SpaceX太空衣的假太空人。他的右手放在方向盘，左肘搁在打开的窗台上。假人的名字來自[[大卫·鲍伊|大卫·鲍伊]]的歌曲《{{Tsl|en|Starman|Starman (歌曲)|Starman}}》。<ref name="PopMech-2018-02-052">{{cite magazine|author=Joe Pappalardo|date=2018-02-05|title=Elon Musk's Space Tesla Isn't Going to Mars. It's Going Somewhere More Important.|url=https://www.popularmechanics.com/space/moon-mars/a16571489/elon-musk-space-tesla-mars/|magazine=Popular Mechanics|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180206230725/https://www.popularmechanics.com/space/moon-mars/a16571489/elon-musk-space-tesla-mars/|archivedate=2018-02-06}}</ref> 車內播放著鲍伊的代表歌曲《[[太空怪談|太空怪談]]》和《Life on Mars》。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wired.com/story/spacex-successfully-launches-the-falcon-heavyand-elon-musks-roadster/|title=SpaceX Successfully Launches the Falcon Heavy—And Elon Musk's Roadster|accessdate=2018-02-07|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180207003954/https://www.wired.com/story/spacex-successfully-launches-the-falcon-heavyand-elon-musks-roadster/|archivedate=2018-02-07|website=WIRED|deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.out.com/news-opinion/2018/2/07/elon-musk-sent-tesla-playing-david-bowie-mars-yesterday|title=Elon Musk Sent a Tesla Playing David Bowie to Mars Yesterday|date=2018-02-07|publisher=|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180208035605/https://www.out.com/news-opinion/2018/2/07/elon-musk-sent-tesla-playing-david-bowie-mars-yesterday|archivedate=2018-02-08|deadurl=no}}</ref>

=== 测试 ===
[[File:Elon_Musk's_Tesla_Roadster_(40110304192).jpg|缩略图]]
此次任務的主要目的是实现以下几个目标： 
* 发射獵鷹重型運載火箭穿过大气层，包括{{Tsl|en|Max Q|最大动压}}狀態
* 自火箭上節與中心火箭分離兩側助推器
* 將两侧助推器飛回[[卡纳维拉尔角|卡纳维拉尔角]]，並同时降落在{{Tsl|en|Landing Zones 1 and 2|着陆区域1和2}}
* 分離中心火箭，點燃火箭上節，執行轨道調整
* 將中心火箭降落在大西洋{{Tsl|en|Autonomous spaceport drone ship|無人航天降落船}}「當然我仍然爱你」號
* 在[[范艾伦辐射带|范艾伦辐射带]]重新點燃上節火箭几个小时以示抗辐射性
* 重新点燃上節火箭將跑車送入[[日心軌道|日心轨道]]上，以示此火箭有能力將26噸重的運載物送上[[地球同步轨道|地球同步轨道]]，或低地軌道60噸的運載能力。

火箭於20:45[[UTC|UTC]]在[[佛羅里達州|佛羅里達州]][[甘迺迪太空中心|甘迺迪太空中心]]的39A發射台發射；敞篷跑车成功送入轨道，且兩個側火箭也於數分鐘後順利降落於降落區1與2。唯一没有完成的目標為中央火箭的降落；雖然由于信号遺失和濃煙的緣故，其真實狀況不得而知，但馬斯克仍公開表明了：中央火箭並未成功降落，因此無法完成回收。<ref name="spaceflightnow_FH_livefeed">{{cite web|url=https://spaceflightnow.com/2018/02/05/falcon-heavy-demo-flight-mission-status-center/|title=Live coverage: Falcon Heavy blasts off for first time, set for Earth departure burn|date=2018-02-06|last=Clark|first=Stephen|publisher=Spaceflight Now|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180206211055/https://spaceflightnow.com/2018/02/05/falcon-heavy-demo-flight-mission-status-center/|archivedate=2018-02-06|deadurl=no}}</ref> 随后调查报告显示中央火箭三个引擎中的兩個在降落時无法重新啟動，導致其在距離降落船100公尺遠之處以500km/h的速度墜毀於水面。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.engadget.com/2018/02/06/spacex-falcon-heavy-center-core-lost/|title=SpaceX confirms it lost the center core of the Falcon Heavy|date=2018-02-06|last=Krishna|first=Swapna|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180207063242/https://www.engadget.com/2018/02/06/spacex-falcon-heavy-center-core-lost/|archivedate=2018-02-07|website=[[Engadget|Engadget]]|deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.cbsnews.com/news/spacex-falcon-heavy-launch-spectacular-maiden-flight/|title=SpaceX Falcon Heavy launch puts on spectacular show in maiden flight|last=Harwood|first=William|date=2018-02-06|publisher=[[CBS_News|CBS News]]|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180206230140/https://www.cbsnews.com/news/spacex-falcon-heavy-launch-spectacular-maiden-flight/|archivedate=2018-02-06}}</ref>

'''獵豹屬'''（{{lang-en|''Acinonyx''}}）為[[貓科|貓科]]下的一個[[屬|屬]]<ref name=msw3>{{MSW3 Wozencraft| pages = 532–533 |id=14000005}}</ref>，現僅存有[[獵豹|獵豹]]（''Acinonyx jubatus''）1 [[種|種]]，生活在[[非洲|非洲]]和[[亞洲|亞洲]]的開放草原上<ref name=Krausman2005>{{cite journal | last1 = Krausman | first1 = P. R. | last2 = Morales | first2 = S. M. | year = 2005 | title = ''Acinonyx jubatus'' | url = | journal = Mammalian Species | volume = 771 | issue = | pages = 1–6 | doi=10.1644/1545-1410(2005)771[0001:aj]2.0.co;2}}</ref>。

== 歷史分布 ==
獵豹屬的成員最早出現於[[上新世|上新世]]晚期的非洲、歐洲與亞洲，在 10,000 年前除了獵豹以外的其他成員均已[[絕種|絕種]]<ref>{{cite journal |author=Hemmer, H. |author2=Kahlke, R.-D. |author3=Keller, T. |year=2008 |title=Cheetahs in the Middle Pleistocene of Europe: ''Acinonyx pardinensis'' (sensu lato) ''intermedius'' (Thenius, 1954) from the Mosbach Sands (Wiesbaden, Hessen, Germany) |journal=Neues Jahrbuch für Geologie und Paläontologie, Abhandlungen |volume=249 |pages=345–356 |url= |doi=10.1127/0077-7749/2008/0249-0345}}</ref>。在北美洲有外形相似的[[北美獵豹|北美獵豹]]，然而北美獵豹在血緣關係上其實更近似於[[美洲獅|美洲獅]]<ref name="mammalian_species">{{cite journal |author=Krausman, P. R. |author2=Morales, S. M. |last-author-amp=yes |year=2005 |title=''Acinonyx jubatus'' |journal=Mammalian Species |pages=1–6 |url=http://www.science.smith.edu/departments/biology/VHAYSSEN/msi/pdf/i1545-1410-771-1-1.pdf |access-date=2015-07-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150703024054/http://www.science.smith.edu/departments/biology/VHAYSSEN/msi/pdf/i1545-1410-771-1-1.pdf |archive-date=2015-07-03 |dead-url=yes |df= }}</ref>。

== 分類 ==
獵豹屬最早由{{link-en|喬舒亞·布魯克斯|Joshua Brookes}}於 1828 年提出。在1993年，獵豹屬被放入[[單系群|單系群]] {{link-en|Acinonychini}} 之下。然而[[分子系統發生學|分子系統發生學]]顯示獵豹屬為[[美洲金貓屬|美洲金貓屬]]的[[旁系群|旁系群]]，因此現今將獵豹屬歸於[[貓亞科|貓亞科]]之下<ref name=msw3/>。

在 18 世紀晚期至 20 世紀早期之間，共描述了以下這些獵豹的種與[[亞種|亞種]]<ref name=MSW3>{{MSW3 Wozencraft |id=14000005 |heading=''Acinonyx'' |pages=532–533}}</ref>：
* [[獵豹|獵豹]] ''Acinonyx jubatus'' — [[約翰·克里斯蒂安·丹尼爾·馮·施雷貝爾|Schreber]] 1775<ref>{{cite book |author=Schreber, J. C. D. |year=1777 |chapter= |title=Die Säugthiere in Abbildungen nach der Natur mit Beschreibungen |publisher=Wolfgang Walther |location=Erlangen}}</ref>
** {{link-en|東南非獵豹|Acinonyx jubatus jubatus}} ''Acinonyx jubatus jubatus'' — [[指名亞種|指名亞種]]<ref name=MSW3/>
** [[亞洲獵豹|亞洲獵豹]] ''Acinonyx jubatus venaticus'' — Griffith, 1821
** {{link-en|東北非獵豹|Acinonyx jubatus soemmeringii}} ''Acinonyx jubatus soemmeringii'' — [[Leopold_Fitzinger|Fitzinger]], 1855
** [[西北非獵豹|西北非獵豹]] ''Acinonyx jubatus hecki'' — Hilzheimer 1913

令外，也有發現以下獵豹屬的化石種：
* [[巨獵豹|巨獵豹]] ''Acinonyx pardinensis'' [[Extinction|†]]  — Croizet et Jobert 1828<ref>Croizet, J. B. et Jobert, A. C. G. (1862). Recherches sur les ossemens fossiles du département du Puy-de-Dôme. Chez les principaux libraires, Paris</ref>
* 間獵豹 ''Acinonyx intermedius'' [[Extinction|†]] — Thenius 1954<ref>Thenius, E. (1954). Gepardreste aus dem Altquartär von Hundsheim in Niederösterreich. Neues Jahrbuch für Geologie und Paläontologie, Monatshefte: 225–238.</ref>
* ''Acinonyx aicha''  [[Extinction|†]] — Geraads 1997<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Geraads | first1 = D | year = 1997 | title = Carnivores du Pliocène terminalde Ahl al Oughlam (Casablanca, Maroc) | url = | journal = Geobios | volume = 30 | issue = 1| pages = 127–164 | doi=10.1016/s0016-6995(97)80263-x}}</ref>
* "''{{link-en|Acinonyx kurteni}}''" [[Extinction|†]] — Christiansen et Mazák 2008<ref>{{cite journal | last1 = Christiansen | first1 = P. | last2 = Mazák | first2 = J. H. | title = A primitive Late Pliocene cheetah, and evolution of the cheetah lineage | journal = Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences | volume = 106 | pages = 512–5 | year = 2009 | doi = 10.1073/pnas.0810435106 | pmid=19114651 | issue=2 | pmc=2626734}}</ref>：在中國地質年代為上新世的地層發現一具顱骨，被認為是獵豹屬最古老的成員。然而於 2012 年，''A. kurteni'' 遭到除名，因為其模式標本被發現是由數個[[中新世|中新世]]化石碎片所拼湊而成的偽造品<ref>{{Cite web  |last = Knevitt | first = Oliver | title = 5 Greatest Palaeontology Fakes Of All Time #5: The Linxia Cheetah | publisher = Science 2.0 | year=2011 | url = http://www.science20.com/between_death_and_data/5_greatest_palaeontology_fakes_all_time_5_linxia_cheetah  | accessdate = 13 January 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = Mazák, J. H.| title = Retraction for Christiansen and Mazák. A primitive Late Pliocene cheetah, and evolution of the cheetah lineage | journal = Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America | volume = 109 | issue = 37 | pages = 15072 | year = 2012 |pmid= 22908293| doi = 10.1073/pnas.1211510109 | pmc=3443189}}</ref>。

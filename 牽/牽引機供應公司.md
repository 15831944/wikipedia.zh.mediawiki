'''牽引機供應公司'''（Tractor Supply Company，缩写：ESCO）是一家美国杂货零售商，是美国最大的杂货零售商之一<ref name=Semiotic>{{Cite journal| issn = 1051-0834| volume = 92| issue = 3-4| last1 = Rhoades| first1 = Emily B.| last2 = Irani| first2 = Tracy| title = "The stuff you need out here": A semiotic analysis case study of an agricultural company's advertisements| journal = Journal of Applied Communications| date = 2008}}</ref>。

1938年该公司成立，当时是经营[[牵引机|牵引机]]（拖拉机）部件销售生意，后来经营范围逐渐扩大，形成了现在的规模，在美国49个州拥有约1,700家商店<ref>{{cite web|title=Tractor Supply Company Reports First Quarter Results|url=http://ir.tractorsupply.com/|website=TractorSupply.com|accessdate=27 April 2017}}</ref>。

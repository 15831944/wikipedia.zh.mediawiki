'''永恒之蓝'''（{{lang-en|EternalBlue}}<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/thomasbrewster/2017/05/12/nsa-exploit-used-by-wannacry-ransomware-in-global-explosion/#18cc790ae599|title=An NSA Cyber Weapon Might Be Behind A Massive Global Ransomware Outbreak|last=Fox-Brewster|first=Thomas|work=[[Forbes|Forbes]]|accessdate=13 May 2017|language=en-us}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://arstechnica.com/security/2017/04/nsa-leaking-shadow-brokers-just-dumped-its-most-damaging-release-yet/|title=NSA-leaking Shadow Brokers just dumped its most damaging release yet|accessdate=13 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://arstechnica.com/security/2017/05/an-nsa-derived-ransomware-worm-is-shutting-down-computers-worldwide/|title=An NSA-derived ransomware worm is shutting down computers worldwide|accessdate=13 May 2017|work=[[Ars_Technica|Ars Technica]]}}</ref>）是[[美国国家安全局|美国国家安全局]]开发的[[漏洞利用|漏洞利用]]程序，于2017年4月14日被黑客组织[[影子掮客|影子掮客]]洩漏<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.ibtimes.co.uk/president-trump-what-fk-are-you-doing-say-shadow-brokers-dump-more-nsa-hacking-tools-1616141|title='President Trump what the f**k are you doing' say Shadow Brokers and dump more NSA hacking tools|last=Ghosh|first=Agamoni|date=April 9, 2017|work=International Business Times UK|access-date=April 10, 2017|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/technology-39553241|title='NSA malware' released by Shadow Brokers hacker group|date=April 10, 2017|work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|accessdate=April 10, 2017|language=en-GB}}</ref>。該工具利用445/TCP端口的[[伺服器訊息區塊|檔案分享協議]]的漏洞進行散播。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://technews.tw/2017/05/15/anti-blocking-eternalblue/|title=勒索病毒不上身，首重防堵「永恆之藍」|accessdate=7 Aug 2018}}</ref>

尽管[[微软|微软]]于2017年3月14日已经发布过[[Windows|Microsoft Windows]][[补丁_(计算机)|补丁]]修补了这个[[漏洞|漏洞]]，然而在5月12日[[WannaCry|WannaCry]][[勒索軟體|勒索軟體]]利用这个漏洞传播时，很多用户仍然因为没有安装补丁而受害<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.wired.com/2017/05/ransomware-meltdown-experts-warned/|title=The Ransomware Meltdown Experts Warned About Is Here|accessdate=13 May 2017|last=Newman|first=Lily Hay}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://arstechnica.co.uk/security/2017/05/what-is-wanna-decryptor-wcry-ransomware-nsa-eternalblue/|title=Wanna Decryptor: The NSA-derived ransomware worm shutting down computers worldwide|work=Ars Technica UK|accessdate=May 13, 2017|language=en-us}}</ref>。

由于WannaCry的严重性，微软于2017年5月13日为已超过支持周期的[[Windows_XP|Microsoft Windows XP]]、[[Windows_8|Microsoft Windows 8]]和[[Windows_Server_2003|Windows Server 2003]]发布了紧急安全更新，以阻止其散布造成更大的危害<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://mspoweruser.com/microsoft-release-wannacrypt-patch-unsupported-windows-xp-windows-8-windows-server-2003/|title=Microsoft release Wannacrypt patch for unsupported Windows XP, Windows 7 and Windows Server 2003|last=Surur|date=13 May 2017|accessdate=13 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://blogs.technet.microsoft.com/msrc/2017/05/12/customer-guidance-for-wannacrypt-attacks/|title=Customer Guidance for WannaCrypt attacks|accessdate=13 May 2017|last=MSRC Team}}</ref>。

2017年6月27日，有勒索软件冒充是Petya利用永恒之蓝漏洞在欧洲地区大肆传播，该勒索软件被命名为NotPetya。其带有较强的政治目的进行攻击即进行破坏而不是索要赎金，即便受害者支付赎金也不会得到任何密钥，因为NotPetya根本就没有编写密钥上传模块到控制者的服务器。此后真正的Petya作者公布解密密钥并声明与欧洲地区的攻击无关，但解密密钥仅可解密Petya而不支持NotPetya加密的文件。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.landiannews.com/archives/37278.html|title=勒索软件Petya作者公布密钥试图与欧洲攻击划清界限{{!}}蓝点网|accessdate=2018-08-07|work=www.landiannews.com}}</ref>

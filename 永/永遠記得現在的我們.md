《'''永遠記得現在的我們'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Always Remember Us This Way'''''}}）是 2018年電影《[[一個巨星的誕生|一個巨星的誕生]]》[[一個巨星的誕生_(2018年版原聲帶)|同名原聲帶]]所收錄的歌曲。歌曲由主流明星女神卡卡獻唱。她同時也與娜塔莉·漢姆比、希拉禮·林賽、以及洛瑞·麥肯納一同作曲，並也參與了製作人戴夫·科布的製作過程中。該曲獲得了普遍好評。

== 製作 ==
《永遠記得現在的我們》 是以鋼琴樂為主要配樂的[[鄉村音樂|鄉村]]抒情樂，<ref name="Ryan">{{Cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/music/2018/10/05/star-born-soundtrack-lady-gaga-bradley-cooper-shallow-not-fluke/1532534002/|title=Lady Gaga, Bradley Cooper's stunning 'A Star is Born' soundtrack proves 'Shallow' was no fluke}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/entertainment/music/review-a-star-is-born-soundtrack-is-a-five-star-marvel/2018/10/04/6956aef2-c7e2-11e8-9c0f-2ffaf6d422aa_story.html|title=Review: 'A Star is Born' soundtrack is a five-star marvel}}</ref> 作曲人為女神卡卡、娜塔莉·漢姆比、希拉禮·林賽、以及洛瑞·麥肯納，製作人則同樣有女神卡卡，以及戴夫·科布。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://americansongwriter.com/2018/09/watch-lady-gaga-bradley-coopers-video-shallow-star-born/|title=Watch Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper's Video for "Shallow" from a Star is Born « American Songwriter|date=September 27, 2018}}</ref><ref name="Yahr">{{Cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/arts-entertainment/2018/10/08/star-is-born-some-nashvilles-most-powerful-voices-helped-shape-soundtrack/|title='A Star is Born': Some of Nashville's most powerful voices helped shape the soundtrack}}</ref>

== 反響 ==
紐約時報的喬·帕瑞雷斯描述這首歌「從寂靜到雄壯」、以及艾爾頓‧強式的「Showstopper」。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/10/08/arts/music/lady-gaga-star-is-born-authenticity-pop.html|title=In 'A Star is Born,' Lady Gaga Pits Pop Against Authenticity|author=Pareles|first=Jon|date=October 8, 2018|work=The New York Times}}</ref> [[滾石_(雜誌)|滾石雜誌]]的布莉塔尼‧斯班諾以及[[华盛顿邮报|華盛頓郵報]]的艾美利‧耶爾稱讚這首歌具「爆炸性」且「無法忘懷」。<ref name="Yahr">{{Cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/arts-entertainment/2018/10/08/star-is-born-some-nashvilles-most-powerful-voices-helped-shape-soundtrack/|title='A Star is Born': Some of Nashville's most powerful voices helped shape the soundtrack}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-album-reviews/review-lady-gaga-and-bradley-cooper-shine-bright-on-the-a-star-is-born-soundtrack-733184/|title=Review: Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper Shine Bright on the 'A Star is Born' Soundtrack|date=October 5, 2018}}</ref> [[今日美國|今日美國]]的麥文‧麥昆德梅特表示《永遠記得現在的我們》是整張原聲帶中「最有趣的曲目」。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://chicago.suntimes.com/entertainment/lady-gaga-star-is-born-soundtrack-shallow-always-remember-us-this-way-music-review-movies/|title=Lady Gaga needed redemption after last album and finds it with 'A Star is Born'}}</ref> 同日報中的專欄作者派崔克‧萊恩稱讚這首歌「與2009年的《無言以對》以及2013年的《[[麻醉自己|麻醉自我]]》同等級，成為了卡卡目前最棒的抒情曲之一。」<ref name="Ryan">{{Cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/music/2018/10/05/star-born-soundtrack-lady-gaga-bradley-cooper-shallow-not-fluke/1532534002/|title=Lady Gaga, Bradley Cooper's stunning 'A Star is Born' soundtrack proves 'Shallow' was no fluke}}</ref> [[誠懇家日報|誠懇家日報]]的裘依‧莫隆納在蘋論中寫道「她出色的聲樂能力及掌控能力...在這場發自內心的慢調即興演奏中清晰可見。」<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cleveland.com/entertainment/index.ssf/2018/10/a_star_is_born_soundtrack_is_m.html|title='A Star is Born' soundtrack is more than an excuse to listen to 'Shallow' on repeat}}</ref>

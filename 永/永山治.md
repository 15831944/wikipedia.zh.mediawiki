'''永山治''' ({{lang-en|Osamu Nagayama}}, {{bd|1947年|4月21日||}}) 是一位日本企业家，[[中外制药|中外制药]]董事长。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=1OsfymtMnOUC&pg=PA320&dq=Osamu+Nagayama&hl=en&sa=X&ei=qcB6VPWTPKHbsASG8YDwDQ&ved=0CB8Q6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=Osamu%20Nagayama&f=false|title=Conflicts of Interest and the Future of Medicine: The United States, France and Japan|publisher=|year=2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=yScL0jnOJOwC&pg=PA224&dq=Osamu+Nagayama&hl=en&sa=X&ei=VcF6VIyCDJPhsATtnoGwDA&ved=0CDwQ6AEwBQ#v=onepage&q=Osamu%20Nagayama&f=false|title=Conflict and Change: Foreign Ownership and the Japanese Firm|publisher=Cambridge University Press |author=George Olcott |year=2009}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2iK8AAAAIAAJ&q=Osamu+Nagayama&dq=Osamu+Nagayama&hl=en&sa=X&ei=VcF6VIyCDJPhsATtnoGwDA&ved=0CFEQ6AEwCQ|title=Volume 173, Issues 1–3|work=Forbes|year=2004}}</ref><ref name=bus>{{cite web|url=http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=688917&ticker=ROG:VX|title=Osamu Nagayama: Executive Profile & Biography |work=Business Week}}</ref><ref name=bloom>{{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/profiles/people/1872195-osamu-nagayama|title=Osamu Nagayama|work=Bloomberg}}</ref><ref name="roche.com4">{{cite web|url=http://www.roche.com/about/governance/executive_committee/executive_commitee-osamu-nagayama.htm|title=Roche – Osamu Nagayama|work=roche.com}}</ref>

==生平==
1947年出生于[[东京都|东京都]]，父亲是旧[[通商产业省|通商产业省]]初代[[大臣官房|大臣官房]][[永山时雄|永山时雄]]。1971年毕业于[[庆应义塾大学|庆应义塾大学]]。毕业后进入[[日本长期信用银行|日本长期信用银行]](现[[新生银行|新生银行]])。1978年进入[[中外制药|中外制药]]。2008年成为[[中外制药|中外制药]]董事长。2013年6月担任[[索尼|索尼]]董事长。<ref name=bus/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sony.net/SonyInfo/News/Press/201306/13-075E/|title=Sony Corporation Executive Appointments|publisher=Sony}}</ref><ref>[http://finance.sina.com.cn/chanjing/rsbd/20130520/005915513295.shtml 索尼任命日本中外制药公司CEO永山治为新董事长]，新浪财经。</ref>

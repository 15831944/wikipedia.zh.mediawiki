'''永和大王'''（'''Yonghe King'''）是[[中国|中国]]连锁[[快餐店|速食餐廳]]，总部在[[上海|上海]][[闵行区|闵行区]]莱茵虹桥中心B栋4楼。<ref>"[http://www.yonghe.com.cn/contact-us/contact/ 联系我们]."（[http://archive.is/0c749 Archive]）永和大王. "永和大王总公司地址：上海市合川路3051号莱茵虹桥中心B栋4楼"</ref>

永和大王是[[快乐蜂|快乐蜂]]的子公司。2013年在中国永和大王营运309个店。<ref>Morales, Neil Jerome C. "[http://www.philstar.com/business/2013/09/02/1160731/jollibee-aims-become-asias-largest-homegrown-chain Jollibee aims to become Asia’s largest homegrown chain]." ''[[The_Philippine_Star|The Philippine Star]]''. Updated September 2, 2013. "Foreign operations of JFC is composed of 569 stores: Yonghe King with 309 stores, Hong Zhuang Yuan with 44 and San Pin Wang with 41, all in China;[...]"</ref>

2011年[[中華民國經濟部|中華民國經濟部]]认证了台灣永和大王的许可证。<ref>Staff writer, with CNA. "[http://www.taipeitimes.com/News/biz/archives/2010/12/02/2003489895 Ministry approves permit for China’s ‘Yonghe King’]." [[台北时报|台北时报]]. Thu, Dec 02, 2010 - Page 11.</ref>

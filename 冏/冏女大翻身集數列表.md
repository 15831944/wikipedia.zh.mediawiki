《[[冏女大翻身|冏女大翻身]]》（{{lang-en|''The Mick''}}）是一部由戴夫·徹寧與約翰·徹寧開創，於2017年1月1日在[[福斯廣播公司|Fox]]開播的[[美國|美國]][[情景喜劇|情境喜劇]]。<ref name="Premiere">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2015/08/fox-comedy-john-dave-chernin-1201503373/|title=Fox Nabs Comedy From John & Dave Chernin With Penalty|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=2016-10-26|accessdate=2017-01-11}}</ref>

2017年1月11日，FOX於[[電視評論家協會|電視評論家協會]]上宣布追加第1季4集集數至17集。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/01/the-mick-new-fox-comedy-four-additional-episodes-1201883224/|title=‘The Mick’: New Fox Comedy Gets Order For Four Additional Episodes – TCA|publisher=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|accessdate=2017-01-11|date=2017-01-11|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva}}</ref>2月21日，FOX宣布續訂第2季。<ref name="Season 2 Renewed">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/02/comedy-the-mick-renewed-season-2-fox-kaitlin-olson-1201925383/|title=Kaitlin Olson Comedy ‘The Mick’ Renewed For Season 2 By Fox|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline.com|date=2017-02-21|accessdate=2017-02-21}}</ref>11月7日，FOX宣布追加預訂第2季7集集數至全季集數20集。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2017/11/07/the-mick-full-season-2-episode-count-20-fox/|title=''The Mick'' Scores Full-ish Season 2 Order|work={{le|TVLine|TVLine}}|author=Michael Ausiello|date=2017-11-07|accessdate=2017-11-07}}</ref>2018年5月10日，FOX宣布取消本劇。<ref name="Canceled">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2018/05/the-mick-canceled-by-fox-after-2-seasons-1202385687/|title=‘The Mick’ Canceled By Fox After 2 Seasons|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline.com|date=2018-05-10|accessdate=2018-05-10}}</ref>

《'''霸凌'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Bully'''''}}，原題：{{lang|en|'''''The Bully Project'''''}}）是一部於2011年上映，以[[美國|美國]][[校園霸凌|校園霸凌事件]]為主題的紀錄片。導演為[[李·赫希|李·赫希]]，他追蹤五名經常遭到校園霸凌的學生，紀錄他們每天的日常生活。2011年，本片受邀在[[翠貝卡影展|翠貝卡影展]]首映。<ref>{{cite news|last=Coyle|first=Jake|title=International slate heads Tribeca Film Festival|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2011/03/07/international-slate-heads_n_832426.html|accessdate=26 March 2011|date=March 7, 2011|agency=Associated Press (hosted by Huffington Post)}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Itzkoff|first=Dave|title=Musical Couples of All Kinds on the Bill at Tribeca Festival|url=http://artsbeat.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/03/07/musical-couples-of-all-kinds-in-the-spotlight-at-tribeca-festival/?partner=rss&emc=rss|accessdate=18 March 2012|newspaper=New York Times|date=March 7, 2011}}</ref>

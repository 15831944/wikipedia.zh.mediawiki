'''城堡岩'''（'''Castle Rock'''）是英國[[愛丁堡|愛丁堡]]的[[愛丁堡城堡|愛丁堡城堡]]所坐落的[[粗玄岩|粗玄岩]]岩石。据估计，它是在三亿五千万年前的[[石炭紀|石炭紀]]早期出現的一個[[火山管|火山管]]的殘餘。<ref>{{cite book |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=LL06OAAACAAJ |page=16 |title=Edinburgh and West Lothian: A Landscape Fashioned by Geology |isbn=9781853973277 |author1=McAdam |first1=David |year=2003}}</ref> 其海拔約{{convert|130|m|ft}}，南北西部都是懸崖，整體高於周圍地表{{convert|80|m|ft}}。<ref name="MacIvor16"/> 良好的戰略位置是建造愛丁堡城堡時的主要考量之一，不過因為沒有充足的蓄水層而使得供水困難。<ref>{{cite book |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=DJ5IxteYPWEC |page=192 |title=Scottish Royal Palaces: The Architecture of the Royal Residences During the Late Medieval and Early Renaissance Periods |isbn=9781862320420 |author1=Dunbar |first1=John G |year=1999}}</ref><ref name=Potter137>{{cite book |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=BQ0XAQAAIAAJ |page=137 |title=Edinburgh Under Siege: 1571-1573 |isbn=9780752423326 |author1=Potter |first1=Harry |year=2003}}</ref>

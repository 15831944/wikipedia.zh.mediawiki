'''佛陀笈多石碑'''（{{lang-en|Buddhagupta stone}}）是出土于[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[威省|威省]]北部的一座[[梵语|梵文]]石碑，是由一位名为「佛陀笈多」的航海者于公元四至五世纪时所献。佛陀笈多石碑是研究[[佛教|佛教]]在[[东南亚|东南亚]]传播以及印度商人在[[马来半岛|马来半岛]]海上贸易的重要文物。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Lost Kingdoms: Hindu-Buddhist Sculpture of Early Southeast Asia|last=John Guy|first=|publisher=Yale University Press|year=2014|isbn=9780300204377|location=|pages=|url=https://books.google.com.my/books?id=vO_-AgAAQBAJ&pg=PA52&source=gbs_toc_r&cad=4#v=onepage&q&f=false}}</ref>{{rp|10}}

== 发现 ==
佛陀笈多石碑是在1834年由[[威省|威斯利省]]英国殖民官詹姆斯·刘上校（Colonel James Low）发现的。<ref>{{Cite book|title=An Account of Several Inscriptions found in Province Wellesley, on the Peninsula of Malacca|last=James Low|first=|publisher=|year=1848|isbn=|location=|pages=62-66|url=https://ia902706.us.archive.org/5/items/papersrelatingto01rost/papersrelatingto01rost.pdf|work=Journal of the Asiatic Society of Bengal, Vol. XVII , part II}}</ref>{{rp|223-226}}

佛陀笈多石碑出土于威省北部的沙地，是一根石柱的上半部分。<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Indianized States of Southeast Asia|last=G. Cœdès|first=|publisher=Australian National University|year=1975|isbn=0 7081 0140 2|location=Canberra|pages=|chapter=The States of the Malay Peninsula and Indonesia from the Fourth to the Sixth Centuries|url=https://openresearch-repository.anu.edu.au/bitstream/1885/115019/2/b11055005.pdf}}</ref>{{rp|50}}出土后，这块石碑引起了考古界的关注，之后该石碑被送到{{le|印度加尔各答博物馆|Indian Museum, Kolkata}}检验，并保存至今。<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Malay Peninsula: Crossroads of the Maritime Silk-Road (100 Bc-1300 Ad)|last=Michel Jacq-Hergoualc'h|first=|publisher=Brill|year=2002|isbn=90 04 11973 6|location=Leiden|pages=193-233|chapter=Chapter VIII. Jiecha (South Kedah): From the 5th to the End of the 8th Century. Chitu: At the Beginning of the 7th Century|url=https://books.google.com.my/books/about/The_Malay_Peninsula.html?id=a5rG6reWhloC&redir_esc=y}}</ref>{{rp|214}}[[马来西亚国家博物馆|马来西亚国家博物馆]]内藏有复刻品。

== 碑铭 ==
[[File:The_Inscriptions_of_the_Buddhagupta_Stone.jpg|250px]]

佛陀笈多石碑是一块[[板岩|板岩]]材质的长方形石碑，长约66厘米，宽约30厘米，石碑左下方有破损。<ref>{{Cite book|title=An Inscribed Tablet from Kedah, Malaysia: Comparison with Earlier Finds|last=Jane Allen|first=|publisher=|year=1986-1987|isbn=|location=|pages=35-57|url=https://core.ac.uk/download/pdf/5105211.pdf|work=Asian Perspectives XXVII, no. 1}}</ref>{{rp|41-43}}在石碑正面，中间刻绘着一座[[窣堵坡|窣堵坡]]，但它的下端不是常见的半圆形而作圆形，在圆形之上有七个{{le|华盖（佛教）|Chatra (umbrella)|华盖}}。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Tamadun Awal Lembah Bujang|last=Mohd Sufian Sabtu|first=|publisher=|year=|isbn=983-62-7230-5|location=Selangor|pages=|chapter=Inskripsi daripada Buddhagupta}}</ref>{{rp|127}}

石碑上也刻有用[[梵语|梵文]]和[[帕拉瓦文|跋罗婆字母]]书写的[[佛教|佛教]]教义，其铭文与另一个发现于[[吉打|吉打]]{{tsl|ms|Kampung Bukit Meriam|武吉马里奄}}的[[武吉马里奄石碑|石碑]]的第二节铭文相似。不同的是，佛陀笈多石碑边上还写有「罗旦帝迦大航海者佛陀笈多」等字，推测是航海者佛陀笈多为表达他对旅途平安的一种虔诚的感恩心理，或是祈求能够安全归来。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Buddhist Dynamics in Premodern and Early Modern Southeast Asia|last=Peter Skilling|first=|publisher=Institute of Southeast Asian Studies|year=2015|isbn=9789814519069|location=Singapore|pages=18-79|url=https://books.google.com.my/books/about/Buddhist_Dynamics_in_Premodern_and_Early.html?id=wgGhCgAAQBAJ&redir_esc=y|chapter=An Untraced Buddisht Verse Inscription from (Pen)insular Southeast Asia}}</ref>

据荷兰学者{{le|肯氏|Johan Hendrik Caspar Kern}}考证，碑文里的「罗旦帝迦」（Raktamṛttika，梵文意为红色泥土），就是中国典籍《[[隋书|隋书]]》和《[[通典|通典]]》里所记载的「[[赤土|赤土]]国」。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Concerning some old Sanskrit Inscriptions in the Malay Peninsula|last=H. Kern|first=|publisher=Malaysian Branch of the Royal Asiatic Society|year=December, 1907|isbn=|location=|pages=95-101|url=https://www.jstor.org/stable/41561175?seq=1#page_scan_tab_contents|work=Journal of the Straits Branch of the Royal Asiatic Society No. 49}}</ref>{{rp|98}}不过，也有印度学者认为罗旦帝迦位于今天的[[西孟加拉邦|西孟加拉邦]]内，即是唐[[玄奘|玄奘]]《[[大唐西域記|大唐西域记]]》里记载的「络多末知」，唐言「赤泥」的地方。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Introduction: A Short Sketch of the Hindu-Buddhist Period of Javanese History|last=M. C. Das|first=|publisher=|year=|isbn=|location=|pages=|url=http://dspace.wbpublibnet.gov.in:8080/jspui/bitstream/10689/21563/4/Chapter%201_1-80p.pdf}}</ref>{{rp|6}}

因佛陀笈多石碑的出土，佛陀笈多成为马来西亚历史上最早有名姓记载的人物。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Lembah Bujang, The Bujang Valley|last=Rolland Braddell|first=|publisher=Persatuan Sejarah Malaysia|year=1980|isbn=|location=Kuala Lumpur|pages=32-54|chapter=Most Ancient Kedah}}</ref>{{rp|36}}

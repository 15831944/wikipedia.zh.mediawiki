《'''佛州中部上帝養成史'''》（{{lang-en|''On Becoming a God in Central Florida''}}）是一部[[美國|美國]][[黑色幽默|黑色喜劇]][[網絡劇|網絡電視劇集]]，由羅伯·方克和麥特·盧茨基開創，預定於2019年在[[YouTube_Premium|YouTube Premium]]首播。

== 概要 ==
克莉絲朵·吉爾（[[克絲汀·鄧斯特|克絲汀·鄧斯特]]飾）是一家[[水上樂園|水上樂園]]的低薪員工，她懷抱夢想、不甘平庸，加入業績達數十億美元的[[層壓式推銷|傳銷組織]]並愈陷愈深，以致摧毀家庭<ref name="SeriesOrder">{{cite web |last1=Andreeva |first1=Nellie |title=YouTube Orders Kirsten Dunst-Starring Dark Comedy Series From George Clooney, Grant Heslov & TriStar Television |url=https://deadline.com/2018/06/youtube-kirsten-dunst-on-becoming-a-god-in-central-florida-dark-comedy-series-george-clooney-grant-heslov-tristar-television-suzanne-patmore-gibbs-1202416941/ |website=Deadline|date=2018-06-25|accessdate=2019-01-07|language=en-US}}</ref>。

=== 開發 ===
2017年1月6日，報道稱[[AMC_(電視頻道)|AMC]]將開發製作本劇。劇集由羅伯·方克和麥特·盧茨基開創並執筆試播集劇本，方克和麥特·盧茨基與[[克絲汀·鄧斯特|克絲汀·鄧斯特]]、[[尤格·藍西莫|尤格·藍西莫]]、[[喬治·克隆尼|喬治·克隆尼]]和[[葛蘭·海斯洛夫|葛蘭·海斯洛夫]]共同擔任執行製片人，尤格·藍西莫還將執導劇集。製作公司包括{{le|Smokehouse Pictures}}、{{le|TriStar Television}}和AMC工作室<ref name="Development">{{cite web |last1=Andreeva |first1=Nellie |title=Kirsten Dunst To Topline Dark Comedy Series From George Clooney, Grant Heslov & ‘The Lobster’ Helmer In Works At AMC |url=https://deadline.com/2017/01/kirsten-dunst-star-comedy-series-amc-george-clooney-grant-heslov-the-lobster-helmer-1201880036/ |website=Deadline |date=2017-01-06|accessdate=2019-01-07|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Stanhope |first1=Kate |title=Kirsten Dunst and George Clooney Team for Dark Comedy at AMC |url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/kirsten-dunst-george-clooney-team-dark-comedy-works-at-amc-961617 |website=The Hollywood Reporter |date=2017-01-06|accessdate=2019-01-07|language=en-US}}</ref>。

2018年6月25日，製作團隊從AMC移至[[YouTube_Premium|YouTube Premium]]，後者直接預訂了第一季，共10集。後續報道稱{{le|愛斯塔·斯伯丁|Esta Spalding}}和{{le|查理·麥道威爾|Charlie McDowell}}增加為執行製片人。愛斯塔·斯伯丁同時擔任[[節目統籌|節目統籌]]，查理·麥道威爾代替尤格·藍西莫執導劇集<ref name="SeriesOrder"/><ref>{{cite web |last1=Nemetz |first1=Dave |title=Kirsten Dunst '90s-Set Comedy Lands at YouTube With 10-Episode Order |url=https://tvline.com/2018/06/25/kirsten-dunst-youtube-series-order-on-becoming-a-god-in-central-florida/ |website=TVLine |date=2018-06-25|accessdate=2019-01-07|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Otterson |first1=Joe |title=Kirsten Dunst-Led Dark Comedy From Producer George Clooney Scores Series Order at YouTube Premium |url=https://variety.com/2018/tv/news/kirsten-dunst-george-clooney-on-becoming-a-god-in-central-florida-youtube-premium-1202856705/ |website=Variety |date=2018-06-25|accessdate=2019-01-07|language=en-US}}</ref>。

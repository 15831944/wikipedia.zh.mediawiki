'''背楯亞目'''（學名：{{lang|la|Notaspidea}}），原作'''背楯目'''，是[[海蛞蝓|海蛞蝓]]的一個人工分類群組，現時已經被分成兩個互不相關的群組：{{link-en|傘螺目|Umbraculida}}及[[側鰓目|側鰓目]]（{{lang|la|Pleurobranchmorpha}}）<ref name="WoRMS_1754">{{WoRMS |id=1754 |last1=Gofas |first1=S. |year=2014 |title=Notaspidea |access-date=2018-03-20 }}</ref>。從2018年起，本分類被認為是[[裸側類|裸側類]]支序之下的[[側鰓目|側鰓目]]（Pleurobranchida）的異名{{r|WoRMS_1754}}。

==分類學變更歷史==
本分類原最初由1883年由法國動物學家{{link-de|保羅·亨利·菲舍爾|Paul Henri Fischer (Malakologe)}}設置，當時包括{{Le|側鰓海牛屬|Pleurobranchus}}（''Pleurobranchus''）及{{Le|傘螺屬|Umbraculum (gastropod)}}（''Umbrella''）兩屬，並歸類於{{Le|覆鰓類|Tectibranchia}}（Tectibranchiata）之中<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.marinespecies.org/aphia.php?p=taxdetails&id=1754#|title=WoRMS - World Register of Marine Species - Notaspidea|accessdate=2018-04-04|work=www.marinespecies.org|language=en}}</ref>。4

背楯類物種包括有海蛞蝓和一種被稱為「假笠貝」的[[海洋生物|海洋]][[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]]的分類，[[直腹足亞綱|直腹足亞綱]][[後生腹足下綱|後生腹足下綱]][[後鰓目|後鰓目]]的成員。然而，在較新的[[布歇特和洛克羅伊的腹足類分類_(2005年)|2005年布歇特和洛克羅伊的腹足類分類]]，the families Umbraculidae and Tylodinidae belong to the superfamily Umbraculoidea {{tsl|en|W.H. Dall||Dall}}, 1889, part of the [[演化支|clade]] Umbraculida. Grande ''et al.'' (2004) found Umbraculoidea to be a sister clade to the [[頭楯目|Cephalaspidea]] (Acteonoidea excluded).<ref>{{cite journal |author1=Grande C. |author2=J. Templado |author3=J.L. Cervera |author4=R. Zardoya | title=Phylogenetic relationships among Opisthobranchia (Mollusca, Gastropoda) based on mitochondrial cox1, tmV, and rmL genes| journal=Molecular Phylogenetics and Evolution| year=2004| volume=33| pages=378–388| doi=10.1016/j.ympev.2004.06.008 | pmid=15336672 | issue=2}}</ref>

The families {{tsl|en|Tylodinidae||Tylodinidae}} and {{tsl|en|Umbraculidae||Umbraculidae}} have large limpet-like external shells and a small [[外套膜|mantle]], while the species in the family [[侧鳃科|Pleurobranchidae]] have a prominent mantle and an internal shell that becomes reduced or is lost in adults.<ref name="Notaspidea">{{cite journal | author=R. Willan| title=Phylogenetic systematics of the Notaspidea (Opisthobranchia) with a reappraisal of families and genera| journal=Am. Malacol. Bull.| year=1987| volume=5| pages=215–241}}</ref> Many species produce mantle secretions as a chemical defense  against predators <ref name="mucus">{{cite journal |author1=Aldo Spinella |author2=Ernesto Mollo |author3=Enrico Trivellone |author4=Guido Cimino | title=Testudinariol A and B, two unusual triterpenoids from the skin and the mucus of the marine mollusc ''Pleurobrancus testudinarius''.| journal=Tetrahedron|date=December 1997| volume=53| issue=49| url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science?_ob=ArticleURL&_udi=B6THR-3SDCT91-V&_user=10&_rdoc=1&_fmt=&_orig=search&_sort=d&view=c&_acct=C000050221&_version=1&_urlVersion=0&_userid=10&md5=b54fd176224b1354702ef12999bd20d2| pages=16891–16896| doi=10.1016/S0040-4020(97)10124-7}}</ref>

由於側鰓科與傘螺科並不具單系性，因此兩後來被拆分，本分類元也於被處理為[[側鰓目|側鰓目]]（Pleurobranchida）的同物異名<ref name=":0" />。

'''作用方式'''（{{lang|en|mode of action}}, MoA）在[[药理学|药理学]]和[[毒理学|毒理学]]中是指一个特定分子（如[[药物|药物]]分子、[[毒物|毒物]]分子）在[[细胞|细胞]]层面上发挥特定药理或毒理作用的机理。“作用方式”是一个与“[[作用机制|作用机制]]”（{{lang|en|Mechanism of action (MOA)}}）相似的术语。作用方式与作用机制的区别在于，作用方式是指特定分子在细胞层面发挥作用的机理，而作用机制是指特定分子在分子层面发挥作用的机理。举例而言，某一分子在细胞层面的作用方式是“[[转录|转录]]调控”，这一分子在分子层面上的“作用机制”则可能与[[DNA|DNA]]分子结合<ref name=Toxtutor3-1>{{cite web |author1=NIH |title=Toxic Effects |url=https://toxtutor.nlm.nih.gov/03-001.html |website=NIH |accessdate=2018-12-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170125174012/https://toxtutor.nlm.nih.gov/03-001.html |archive-date=2017-01-25 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://cfpub.epa.gov/ncea/iris_drafts/dioxin/nas-review/pdfs/part3/dioxin_pt3_ch03_oct2004.pdf|title=Mechanisms and mode of dioxin action|publisher=U.S. Environmental Protection Agency|accessdate=2012-06-11|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161228231745/https://cfpub.epa.gov/ncea/iris_drafts/dioxin/nas-review/pdfs/part3/dioxin_pt3_ch03_oct2004.pdf|archive-date=2016-12-28|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name=Ng2003>{{cite journal | vauthors = Ng WL, Kazmierczak KM, Robertson GT, Gilmour R, Winkler ME | title = Transcriptional regulation and signature patterns revealed by microarray analyses of Streptococcus pneumoniae R6 challenged with sublethal concentrations of translation inhibitors | journal = Journal of Bacteriology | volume = 185 | issue = 1 | pages = 359–70 | date = Jan 2003 | pmid = 12486074 | doi = 10.1128/JB.185.1.359-370.2003 | pmc=141824}}</ref>。不过，因为学界对作用方式和作用模式的定义并无明确共识，一些文献中也存在作用方式和作用机制两词混用的情况<ref>{{cite web | title = Herbicide Group Classification by Mode of Action | url = http://www1.agric.gov.ab.ca/$department/deptdocs.nsf/all/prm6487 | publisher = Alberta Agriculture and Rural Development | accessdate = 11 June 2012 }}</ref>。

作用方式是连接分子层面的作用机制与生物体[[生理学|生理学]]上改变的桥梁。如果某一分子分子水平的作用机制尚未阐明，作用机制就显得尤为重要<ref name=Ng2003/>。

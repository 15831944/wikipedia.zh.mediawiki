'''作用机制'''，亦称为'''作用机理'''（{{lang|en|Mechanism of action (MOA)}}），在[[药理学|药理学]]和[[毒理学|毒理学]]中是指一个特定分子（如[[药物|药物]]分子、[[毒物|毒物]]分子）在[[分子|分子]]层面上发挥特定药理或毒理作用的机理<ref name=Toxtutor3-1>{{cite web |author1=NIH |title=Toxic Effects |url=https://toxtutor.nlm.nih.gov/03-001.html |website=NIH |accessdate=2018-12-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170125174012/https://toxtutor.nlm.nih.gov/03-001.html |archive-date=2017-01-25 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name=spratto>{{cite book|title=Delmar Nurse's Drug Handbook|year=2010| author = Spratto, G.R.; Woods, A.L.|publisher=Cengage Learning|isbn=978-1-4390-5616-5}}</ref>。一般而言，特定分子的作用机制通常是与[[酶|酶]]、[[受体_(生物化学)|受体]]等生物大分子发生相互作用<ref>Grant, R.L.; Combs, A.B.; Acosta, D. (2010) "Experimental Models for the Investigation of Toxicological Mechanisms". In McQueen, C.A. ''Comprehensive Toxicology'' (2nd ed.). Oxford: Elsevier. p. 204. {{ISBN|978-0-08-046884-6}}.</ref>。

“[[作用方式|作用方式]]”（{{Lang|en|Mode of action (MoA)}}）是一个与[[作用机制|作用机制]]相似的术语。作用方式与作用机制的区别在于，作用方式是指特定分子在[[细胞|细胞]]层面发挥作用的机理，而作用机制是指特定分子在分子层面发挥作用的机理<ref name=Toxtutor3-1/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://cfpub.epa.gov/ncea/iris_drafts/dioxin/nas-review/pdfs/part3/dioxin_pt3_ch03_oct2004.pdf|title=Mechanisms and mode of dioxin action|publisher=U.S. Environmental Protection Agency|accessdate=2012-06-11|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161228231745/https://cfpub.epa.gov/ncea/iris_drafts/dioxin/nas-review/pdfs/part3/dioxin_pt3_ch03_oct2004.pdf|archive-date=2016-12-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==重要性==
阐明药物的作用机制有助于药物毒理学和安全性的评估。例如，以[[细胞膜|细胞膜]]和[[电子传递链|电子传递链]]为靶点的抗感染药物：副作用就更可能比以{{le|β-葡聚糖|Beta-glucan}}、[[肽聚糖|肽聚糖]]等[[细菌|细菌]][[细胞壁|细胞壁]]成分以及[[原核生物|原核生物]]的70S[[核糖体|核糖体]]具有更强的副作用。这是因为人体细胞无[[细胞壁|细胞壁]]结构；人作为[[真核生物|真核生物]]，核糖体的沉降系数是80S，与细菌的70S核糖体在结构上有显著区别，但细胞膜的结构以及电子传递链的组成却与原核生物有相似性<ref name=":3">{{ Cite journal|title =Morphological and ultrastructural changes in bacterial cells as an indicator of antibacterial mechanism of action|author1 =Cushnie, T.P.|author2 =O’Driscoll, N.H.|author3 =Lamb, A.J.|journal =Cellular and Molecular Life Sciences|year =2016|volume =73|issue =23|pages =4471–4492|pmid =27392605|doi =10.1007/s00018-016-2302-2|url =https://zenodo.org/record/883501#.WdiUZ1uCzIU|author =|access-date =2018-12-21|archive-url =https://web.archive.org/web/20171007220157/https://zenodo.org/record/883501#.WdiUZ1uCzIU#.WdiUZ1uCzIU|archive-date =2017-10-07|dead-url =no}}</ref><ref name="archivesoftoxicology2017">{{ Cite journal |title =New developments and directions in the clinical application of the echinocandins|author =Chang, C.C.; Slavin, M.A.; Chen, S.C.|journal = Archives of Toxicology|year =2017|volume =91|issue =|pages =1613–1621|pmid =28180946|doi =10.1007/s00204-016-1916-3}}</ref>。

新的药物作用机制的发现，能使已获批的药物得到新的应用。例如，[[西地那非|西地那非]]本来是一种治疗[[勃起功能障碍|勃起功能障碍]]的药物。西地那非能抑制{{le|PDE-5|cGMP-specific phosphodiesterase type 5|PDE-5}}这一作用机制的发现，使得这一药物也能用于[[肺动脉高压|肺动脉高压]]的治疗中<ref name="plosone2012">{{ Cite journal|title =Identifying novel drug indications through automated reasoning|author =Tari, L.; Vo, N.; Liang, S.; Patel, J.; Baral, C.; Cai, J.|journal =PLOS ONE|year =2012|volume =7|issue =7|pages =Article e40946|pmid =22911721|doi =10.1371/journal.pone.0040946|url =http://journals.plos.org/plosone/article/file?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0040946&type=printable|pmc =3402456|access-date =2018-12-21|archive-url =https://web.archive.org/web/20171004075632/http://journals.plos.org/plosone/article/file?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0040946&type=printable|archive-date =2017-10-04|dead-url =no}}</ref><ref name="tauwebcast2014">{{cite AV media |people=Hayardeny, L. |date=2014 |title=Why is it important to know the mode of action of drugs? |trans-title= |medium= |language=en |url=http://video.tau.ac.il/events/index.php?option=com_k2&view=item&id=5005:why-is-it-important-to-know-the-mode-of-action-of-drugs&Itemid=557 |access-date=2017-03-18 |format=Conference presentation |location=New Frontiers in Neuroscience and Methods of Transdisciplinary Education Workshop, Tel Aviv University, Israel |publisher=Tel Aviv University |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170318173231/http://video.tau.ac.il/events/index.php?option=com_k2&view=item&id=5005:why-is-it-important-to-know-the-mode-of-action-of-drugs&Itemid=557 |archive-date=2017-03-18 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

药物作用机制的阐明，对药物剂量的确定也有帮助。[[羟甲基戊二酸单酰辅酶A还原酶抑制剂|斯达汀]]的用量就是通过病人血液中的[[胆固醇|胆固醇]]浓度确定的<ref name="nature2010">{{ Cite journal |title =Mechanism matters|author = No authors listed|journal = Nature Medicine|year =2010|volume =16|issue =4|pages =347|pmid = 20376007|doi =10.1038/nm0410-347}}</ref>。对[[靶向药物|靶向药物]]而言，阐明的药物作用机制相当重要。例如，治疗乳腺癌的[[单克隆抗体|单抗]]类药物[[曲妥珠单抗|曲妥珠单抗]]以[[HER2/neu|HER2]]为靶点。知道该药物这一作用机制后，结合对乳腺肿瘤组织的[[病理学|病理学]]诊断，就能确定这一药物是否能对特定病人起效<ref name="nature2010"/><ref name="cancertreatmentreviews2017">{{ Cite journal |title =Escalating and de-escalating treatment in HER2-positive early breast cancer|author = Joensuu, H. |journal = Cancer Treatment Reviews |year =2017|volume =52|issue =|pages =1–11|pmid = 27866067 |doi = 10.1016/j.ctrv.2016.11.002|url=https://helda.helsinki.fi/handle/10138/176779}}</ref>。另外，[[联合疗法|联合疗法]]常常需选用数种作用机制不同、作用于不同靶点的药物。确定不同药物的作用机制，能帮助确定哪些药物能组合用于联合疗法中<ref name=":3" /><ref name="currentopinioninvirology2016">{{ Cite journal |title =Current status and prospects of HIV treatment|author =Cihlar, T.; Fordyce, M.|journal = Current Opinion in Virology |year =2016|volume =18|issue =|pages =50–56|pmid =27023283|doi =10.1016/j.coviro.2016.03.004}}</ref><ref name="tropicalparasitology2016">{{ Cite journal |title =Antimalarial drug resistance: An overview|author =Antony, H.A.; Parija, S.C.|journal = Tropical Parasitology |year =2016|volume =6|issue =1|pages =30–41|pmc=4778180|doi =10.4103/2229-5070.175081|pmid=26998432}}</ref><ref name="eLife2013">{{ Cite journal |title =Evolutionary dynamics of cancer in response to targeted combination therapy|author = Bozic, I.; Reiter, J.G.; Allen, B.; Antal, T.; Chatterjee, K.; Shah, P.; Moon, Y.S.; Yaqubie, A.; Kelly, N.; Le, D.T.; Lipson, E.J.; Chapman, P.B.; Diaz, L.A.; Vogelstein, B., Nowak, M.A. |journal = eLife|year =2013|volume =2|issue =|pages = Article ID e00747|pmid = 23805382|doi = 10.7554/eLife.00747|pmc=3691570}}</ref>。

==作用机制的阐明==
===显微镜的观察===
根据一些细胞水平上的变化，可以推测出特定分子在分子水平上的作用机制<ref name=":2">{{ Cite journal |title =Target identification by image analysis|author1=Fetz, V. |author2=Prochnow, H. |author3=Brönstrup, M. |author4=Sasse, F. |journal = Natural Product Reports|year =2016|volume =33|issue =5|pages =655–667|pmid = 26777141|doi =10.1039/c5np00113g}}</ref>。比如，进行抗生素处理后，如果细菌变成了{{le|原生质球|Spheroplast}}，可推定抗生素的作用机制是抑制[[肽聚糖|肽聚糖]]或{{le|β-葡聚糖|Beta-glucan}}等细菌细胞壁成分的合成。如果细菌形成了丝状体，那么可以推测抗生素的靶点是细菌的[[DNA|DNA]]或[[FtsZ|FtsZ]]蛋白<ref name=":3"/>。如果肿瘤细胞经过[[化疗药物|化疗药物]]处理后，如果能在显微镜下观察到{{le|小疱|Bleb_(cell_biology)}}结构的形成，可推测这种化疗药物的靶点是肿瘤细胞的[[细胞膜|细胞膜]]<ref name=":4">{{ Cite journal |title =Latarcins: versatile spider venom peptides|author1=Dubovskii, P.V. |author2=Vassilevski, A.A. |author3=Kozlov, S.A. |author4=Feofanov, A.V. |author5=Grishin, E.V. |author6=Efremov, R.G. |journal = Cellular and Molecular Life Sciences|year =2015|volume =72|issue =23|pages =4501–4522|pmid =  26286896|doi =10.1007/s00018-015-2016-x}}</ref>。这种方法比较耗费时间和人力，不过现在一些软件能帮助提高显微镜观察方法的效率<ref name=":3"/><ref name=":2"/>。

===生物化学方法===
可以通过亲和纯化的方法，确认所有能和药物或毒物分子发生相互作用的生物分子，进而推测出药物的作用机制。此外，也可以使特定分子带上标记的方法找出该分子的作用靶点。通过[[建模|建模]]的方法，利用[[计算机|计算机]]生成特定分子与生物大分子的作用模型也可以推测出这种分子的作用靶点和作用机制<ref name="SchenoneDančík2013">{{cite journal|last1=Schenone|first1=Monica|last2=Dančík|first2=Vlado|last3=Wagner|first3=Bridget K|last4=Clemons|first4=Paul A|title=Target identification and mechanism of action in chemical biology and drug discovery|journal=Nature Chemical Biology|volume=9|issue=4|year=2013|pages=232–240|issn=1552-4450|doi=10.1038/nchembio.1199}}</ref>。

===组学方法===
特定分子的作用机制可以通过[[基因组学|基因组学]]、[[转录组|转录组学]]的分析方法间接确认。如果[[基因敲除|敲除]]或使用[[RNA干扰|RNA干扰]]的方法敲低某个基因后，细胞或组织基因表达或表型的变化与经过某种药物处理后相似，那么可以推定这种药物的作用机制就是通过抑制这些蛋白的功能实现的<ref name="SchenoneDančík2013"/>。

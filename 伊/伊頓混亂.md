'''伊頓混亂'''（{{lang-en|Eton mess}}）是一種由草莓、蛋白脆饼和鮮奶油製成的傳統[[英國飲食|英式]]甜點<ref name="GoldsteinMintz2015">{{cite book|author1=Darra Goldstein|author2=Sidney Mintz|author3=Michael Krondl|author4=Laura Mason|title=The Oxford Companion to Sugar and Sweets|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=jbi6BwAAQBAJ&pg=PA243|year=2015|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-931339-6|pages=243–}}</ref>。最初在1893年提到印刷品，通常被誤認為來自於伊頓公學並由{{link-en|伊頓·v·哈洛|Eton v Harrow}}服務於[[哈罗公学|哈罗公学]]的學生<ref>{{Cite book|title=Marlborough House and Its Occupants: Present and Past|author=Arthur Henry Beavan|year=1896|page=162}}</ref>。

伊頓混亂最初於1930年代在學校的襪子[[士多|士多]]店販賣，是以草莓或[[香蕉|香蕉]]冰淇淋和[[黄油|黄油]]製成的，之後才加入蛋白脆饼<ref>{{cite book|last=Weir|first=Robin|author2=Caroline Liddell |author3=Peter Brears |title=Recipes from the Dairy|location=London|publisher=National Trust for Places of Historic Interest or Natural Beauty|year=1995|isbn=0-7078-0243-1}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=The appliance of science|publisher=The Guardian|date=2005-02-05|url=https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2005/feb/05/foodanddrink.shopping4|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170622235459/https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2005/feb/05/foodanddrink.shopping4|archivedate=2017-06-22|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref name="Guardian">{{cite news|last=Blumenthal|first=Heston|title=No messing|url=https://www.theguardian.com/weekend/story/0,,985398,00.html|publisher=''The Guardian''|date=2003-06-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last=Smith|first=Michael|title=Fine English Cookery|location=London|publisher=Faber and Faber|year=1973|isbn=0-571-10349-9}} (Revised edition London: Serif (publisher), 1998, Foreword by Geraldene Holt; {{ISBN|978-1897959367}}.) See {{cite news|last=Dupleix|first=Jill|title=Eton mess : Strawberries and cream make a superb summer pudding for lazy, hazy days|url=http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/life_and_style/food_and_drink/recipes/article442202.ece|publisher=[[泰晤士报|泰晤士报]]|date=2004-06-03 | location=London}}</ref>；可以與許多其他的夏季水果類型製成，但草莓被認為是最傳統的<ref>A recipe by Heston Blumenthal, for instance, uses bananas: see {{cite news|last=Blumenthal|first=Heston|title=Eton mess : Look, no berries – this Eton mess is a crispy, creamy, zingy heap of a treat|url=http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/life_and_style/food_and_drink/heston_blumenthal/article725886.ece|publisher=[[星期日泰晤士報|星期日泰晤士報]]|date=2006-02-12 | location=London}}</ref>{{notetag|name=mess一詞解釋|1=“''mess''”一詞可能指菜的外觀<ref name="Guardian"/>。}}。

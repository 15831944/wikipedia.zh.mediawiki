'''伊豆雀鯛屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''{{lang|la|Izuus}}''）是已滅絕的一種原始[[雀鯛科|雀鯛科]]魚類，生存於[[中新世|中新世]]早期，化石發現於[[日本|日本]][[靜岡縣|靜岡縣]][[伊豆市|伊豆市]]的[[湯島_(伊豆市)|湯島]]（{{lang|ja|湯ケ島}}）<ref name=Izuus>{{cite journal | author = Tokunaga, S. & Saito, K. | title = A fossil pomacentrid fish from the Miocene of Izu, Japan | journal = Japanese Journal of Geology and Geography | volume = 15 | pages = 83–86 | year = 1938 | url = }}</ref><ref name="sepkoskidb">{{cite journal | last = Sepkoski | first = Jack | title =  A compendium of fossil marine animal genera | journal = Bulletins of American Paleontology | volume = 364 | pages = p.560 | year = 2002 | url = }}</ref>。

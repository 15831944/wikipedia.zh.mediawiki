'''伊恩·愛德華·哈普'''（{{lang-en|Ian Edward Happ}}, {{bd|1994年|8月12日}}），為美國職棒大聯盟芝加哥小熊隊的選手。哈普大學就讀於辛辛那提大學，他在2015年大聯盟選秀於第一輪第9順位被小熊隊選中。

==職業生涯==
哈普在高中四年內就繳出打擊率4成49，12支全壘打以及65分打點的成績<ref>{{cite web|url=http://triblive.com/sports/hssports/1072073-85/happ-lebanon-baseball-mccloskey-baseman-blue-devils-practice-team-aaaa#axzz3Rhp5nMVA|title=Happ's effort contagious for Mt. Lebanon baseball|work=TribLIVE.com|accessdate=June 21, 2015}}</ref>。在大一時期，他出賽56場，繳出.322/.483/.451的打擊三圍，為全隊最高<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thealmanac.net/article/20130712/SPORTS/130719963|title=The Almanac - Lebo’s Happ an All-American|work=The Almanac|accessdate=June 21, 2015}}{{dead link|date=2018年5月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。2014年在棒球的夏季聯盟，他出賽50場，打擊三圍為.322/.443/.497，另有5支全壘打和19次盜壘。

2015年大聯盟選秀，小熊隊以第一輪第9順位將他選入<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.post-gazette.com/sports/pirates/2015/06/08/Mt-Lebanon-s-Happ-selected-by-Cubs-with-ninth-pick/stories/201506080202|title=Mt. Lebanon's Happ selected by Cubs with ninth pick|work=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|accessdate=June 21, 2015}}</ref>。2016年，哈普升上高階1A，大聯盟官網在小熊新秀排名中將哈普評為第三名<ref>{{cite news|last1=Leboff|first1=Michael|title=Happ's two homers power Myrtle Beach|url=http://www.milb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20160418&content_id=173114014&fext=.jsp&vkey=news_milb|accessdate=June 6, 2016|publisher=MILB.com|date=April 19, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Peng|first1=Michael|title=Happ extends RBI streak in Pelicans' rout|url=http://www.milb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20160605&content_id=182377958&fext=.jsp&vkey=news_milb&sid=milb|accessdate=June 6, 2016|publisher=MILB.com|date=June 5, 2016}}</ref>。在2A時期，哈普打擊率2成79，15支全壘打，73分打點，隨後升上秋季聯盟<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.azcentral.com/story/sports/mlb/2016/11/19/cubs-prospect-ian-happ-leads-mesa-solar-sox-arizona-fall-league-title/94142222/ |title=Cubs prospect Ian Happ leads Mesa Solar Sox to Arizona Fall League title |publisher=Azcentral.com |date=November 19, 2016 |accessdate=May 14, 2017}}</ref>。

2017年5月13日，哈普升上大聯盟<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.desmoinesregister.com/story/sports/baseball/iowa-cubs/2017/05/13/cubs-call-up-ian-happ-triple-iowa/321194001/ |title=Cubs call up Ian Happ from Triple-A Iowa |publisher=Desmoinesregister.com |date= |accessdate=May 14, 2017}}</ref>，生涯大聯盟首安就擊出一支413英尺的全壘打<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.stltoday.com/sports/baseball/professional/cardinal-beat/martinez-serves-up-rookie-s-first-major-league-home-run/article_2514e034-4b94-575d-9b69-064b6078d352.html |title=Game blog: Cardinals bounce back to beat Cubs | Cardinal Beat |publisher=stltoday.com |date= |accessdate=May 14, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/230011112/cubs-ian-happ-called-up-homers-in-debut/ |title=Cubs prospect Happ homers for first MLB hit: Just called up, slugger debuts right away |work=mlb.com |last=Muskat |first=Carrie |date=May 13, 2017 |accessdate=May 30, 2017}}</ref>。6月13日，哈普成為史上第5位單場吞下4K且擊出滿貫砲的選手<ref>{{cite news|last1=Adler|first1=Lindsey|title=Ian Happ Did Something Only Four Other Players In History Have Done|url=http://deadspin.com/ian-happ-did-something-only-five-other-players-in-histo-1796097585|accessdate=14 June 2017|work=[[Deadspin|Deadspin]]|date=June 14, 2017}}</ref>。8月30日面對海盜隊投手[[伊萬·諾瓦|伊萬·諾瓦]]時擊出生涯第20轟<ref>{{cite web|last1=Wilson|first1=Jason|title=Cubs set MLB record with 5 players, 25 or younger, hitting 20 HRs in season|url=https://www.thescore.com/mlb/news/1362171|website=www.thescore.com/|publisher=The Score|accessdate=31 August 2017}}</ref>。最後他單季24轟寫下國聯左右開弓的菜鳥單季全壘打紀錄<ref>{{cite web|last1=Skrbina|first1=Paul|title=Ian Happ's 8th-inning homer lifts Cubs to 5-4 win over Reds|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/baseball/cubs/ct-cubs-reds-spt-0930-20170929-story.html|website=www.chicagotribune.com|publisher=The Chicago Tribune|accessdate=29 September 2017}}</ref>。

2018年3月29日，開幕戰對決馬林魚，哈普面對馬林魚先發投手Jose Urena首球首打席就擊出全壘打。最終小熊8比4贏球<ref>{{cite web|title=Ian Happ hits a home run on the 1st pitch in Cubs' 8-4 win on opening day|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/baseball/cubs/ct-spt-cubs-marlins-season-opener-20180329-story.html|publisher=Chicago Turbine|accessdate=March 29, 2018}}</ref>。

'''伊丽莎白·安·霍利'''（{{Lang-en|'''Elizabeth Ann Hawley'''}}，{{bd|1923年|11月9日|2018年|1月26日|catIdx=Hawley}}）<ref name="channelnewsasia">{{cite web|title='Sherlock Holmes' of Nepal's Himalayas dies at 94|url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/asiapacific/-sherlock-holmes--of-nepal-s-himalayas-dies-at-94-9899052|website=Channel News Asia|publisher=[[法新社|法新社]]|accessdate=2018-01-31|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180127004402/https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/asiapacific/-sherlock-holmes--of-nepal-s-himalayas-dies-at-94-9899052|archivedate=2018-01-27}}</ref><ref name="CNA">{{cite web|author1=嚴思祺|title=喜馬拉雅山福爾摩斯 美國名記者辭世|url=http://www.cna.com.tw/news/aopl/201801260259-1.aspx|publisher=[[中央社|中央社]]|accessdate=2018-01-31|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180127202430/http://www.cna.com.tw/news/aopl/201801260259-1.aspx|archivedate=2018-01-27}}</ref>是一名[[美国|美国]][[记者|记者]]和[[喜马拉雅山|喜马拉雅山]]探险[[编年史|编年史]]的编纂者。她于1959年2月前往[[尼泊尔|尼泊尔]]，从此就没有再离开过。

伊丽莎白·霍利出生于[[伊利诺伊州|伊利诺伊州]]的[[芝加哥市|芝加哥市]]，在[[密歇根大学|密歇根大学]]获得了[[荣誉学位|荣誉]][[文学士|文学士]]学位和[[文学硕士|文学硕士]]学位。<ref name="Biography">{{cite web|title=Biography Miss Elizabeth Hawley|url=http://www.king-albert-foundation.ch/html/winners/98/hawley_bi.html|accessdate=2018-01-31|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928051405/http://www.king-albert-foundation.ch/html/winners/98/hawley_bi.html|archivedate=2007-09-28}}</ref> 她在放弃《[[财富_(杂志)|财富]]》杂志的研究员职位后开始环球旅行，期间她抵达[[尼泊尔|尼泊尔]][[加德满都|加德满都]]。因为记者的工作缘故，霍利曾短暂回到[[旧金山|旧金山]]生活，随后几年她又以《[[时代周刊|时代周刊]]》记者的身份前往加德满都。<ref name="channelnewsasia" />

霍利在[[路透社|路透社]]找到了一份有关登山职业的报道工作，其中她也报道了1963年的美国远征队，这是美国首支攀登[[珠穆朗玛峰|珠穆朗玛峰]]的登山队。<ref>{{cite web |last=Jolly |first=Joanna |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-south-asia-10268549 |title=Elizabeth Hawley, unrivalled Himalayan record keeper |publisher=BBC新闻 |date=2010-08-28 |accessdate=2014-03-21 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150218015713/http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-south-asia-10268549 |archivedate=2015-02-18 }}</ref> 她的工作为她赢得了“登山世界的福尔摩斯”的美誉。<ref name="CNA" />

尽管伊丽莎白·霍利本人从来没有参与过登山运动，但她却是过去四十年以来，有关喜马拉雅山攀登探险历史的最著名记录者。虽然她没有正式的官方身份，但霍利因为其准确详实的记录而得到了国际登山界的尊重。伊丽莎白·霍利将她的数据记录在一个数据库里，<ref>{{Cite web | url = http://www.himalayandatabase.com/ | title = The Himalayan Database | last = Hawley | first = Elizabeth | last2 = Salisbury | first2 = Richard | publisher = American Alpine Club | accessdate = 2014-04-24 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140425185042/http://www.himalayandatabase.com/ | archivedate = 2014-04-25 }}
</ref> 这里有些数据被用于分析登山的成功和失败几率。<ref>{{Cite book  | last = Hawley  | first = Elizabeth  | last2 = Salisbury  | first2 = Richard  | title = The Himalaya by the Numbers, A Statistical Analysis of Mountaineering in the Nepal Himalaya  | date = 2011  | location = Kathmandu  | publisher = Vajra Publications  | url = http://www.himalayandatabase.com/downloads/HimalayaByNbrs.pdf  | accessdate = 2014-04-24  | deadurl = no  | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140910101108/http://www.himalayandatabase.com/downloads/HimalayaByNbrs.pdf  | archivedate = 2014-09-10  }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url = http://www.himalayandatabase.com/research.html | title = Research (bibliography) | publisher = American Alpine Club | accessdate = 2014-04-24 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140425234535/http://www.himalayandatabase.com/research.html | archivedate = 2014-04-25 }}</ref>

2008年5月9日，[[法国|法国]]登山者{{Link-fr|弗朗索瓦·达米兰诺|François Damilano}}首次独自登顶了{{tsl|en|Dhaulagiri|道拉奇里群山}}（{{lang|en|Dhaulagiri Group}}）中一座海拔6182m的山峰，并将其命名为霍利峰。<ref>[http://www.climbing.com/news/newly-climbed-peak-named-for-elizabeth-hawley/ Newly Climbed Peak Named for Elizabeth Hawley] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140325195346/http://www.climbing.com/news/newly-climbed-peak-named-for-elizabeth-hawley/ |date=2014-03-25 }} by Dougald MacDonald on ''climbing.com''</ref><ref>{{Cite journal  | volume = 51 | issue = 83 | journal = American Alpine Journal | author = Francois Damilano | title = Asia, Nepal, Dhaulagiri Himal, Peak Hawley (6,182m) | year = 2009 | pages = 321 | publisher = American Alpine Club | url = http://publications.americanalpineclub.org/articles/12200932101/Asia-Nepal-Dhaulagiri-Himal-Peak-Hawley-6182m | accessdate = 2014-04-24 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140424232855/http://publications.americanalpineclub.org/articles/12200932101/Asia-Nepal-Dhaulagiri-Himal-Peak-Hawley-6182m | archivedate = 2014-04-24}}</ref>

2018年1月，伊丽莎白·霍利因为肺部感染而入院治疗。一周后，霍利辞世，原因可能為中風。<ref name="CNA" />

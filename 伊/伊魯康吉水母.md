'''伊魯康吉水母'''（Irukandji jellyfish），是一種具有致命毒性的[[立方水母綱|立方水母]]。牠們是世界上體型最小，毒性最強的[[水母|水母]]之一，體型約只有1 cm<sup>3</sup>。主要棲息在澳洲周遭的海域。牠們能夠用刺絲胞把毒液注入受害者體內，並引發特定的症狀，統稱為「{{le|伊魯康吉症候群|Irukandji syndrome}}」。目前伊魯康吉水母共有16個已知的物種，其中''{{le|Carukia barnesi|Carukia barnesi}}''、''{{le|Malo kingi|Malo kingi}}''、 ''{{le|Malo maximus|Malo maximus}}''、''{{le|Malo filipina}}'' 以及 ''{{link-it|Malo bella|Malo bella}}''都是為人所熟知的種類<ref>{{Cite journal| title = Malo kingi: A new species of Irukandji jellyfish (Cnidaria: Cubozoa: Carybdeida),  lethal to humans, from Queensland, Australia| last=Gershwin| first=Lisa-Ann| accessdate = 2010-07-23| url = http://eprints.jcu.edu.au/2670/| work = [[Zootaxa|Zootaxa]]| year = 2007| issue=1659| pages=55–68}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal| title = The pharmacology of Malo maxima jellyfish venom extract in isolated cardiovascular tissues: A probable cause of the Irukandji syndrome in Western Australia| last=Li| first=R.| work = [[Tox_Letters|Tox Letters]]| year = 2011| issue=201(3)| pages=221–9| pmid=21237252| doi=10.1016/j.toxlet.2011.01.003| volume=201}}</ref><ref name="kingslayer_2016_11_06_crew_sci_am">Crew, Becky, [https://blogs.scientificamerican.com/running-ponies/the-smallest-and-deadliest-kingslayer-in-the-world/ "The Smallest and Deadliest Kingslayer in the World,"] October 7, 2013, ''Scientific American'' blog, retrieved Nov. 6, 2016</ref>。

伊魯康吉症候群的症狀最早於1952年為{{le|Hugo Flecker}}所記錄<ref>{{cite book | last=Pearn| first=J. H. | title = Australian Dictionary of Biography, Volume 14| year = 1990 | publisher = [[Melbourne_University_Press|Melbourne University Press]] | pages=182–184|isbn = 0-522-84717-X | url = http://www.adb.online.anu.edu.au/biogs/A140195b.htm|accessdate=2013-10-01}}</ref>。伊魯康吉這個名字，是命名自居住於澳洲[[昆士蘭省|昆士蘭省]][[凱恩斯_(昆士蘭州)|凱恩斯]]北海岸的{{le|伊魯康吉人|Irukandji people}}<ref name="kingslayer_2016_11_06_crew_sci_am" /><ref>{{Cite journal | issn = 0025-729X | volume = 2 | issue = 3 | pages = 89–91 | last = Flecker | first = Hugo | title = Irukandji sting to North Queensland bathers without production of weals but with severe general symptoms | journal = [[The_Medical_Journal_of_Australia|The Medical Journal of Australia]] | date = 1952-07-19 | pmid = 14956317 }}</ref>。在1964年，Jack Barnes辨別出第一種伊魯康吉水母，''Carukia barnesi''；為了證實伊魯康吉症候群的症狀，他便在兒子與救生員的觀察下讓水母螫了自己；最終，他很幸運地只得了普通的小病。澳洲的{{le|毒物學家|toxinologist}}{{le|Jamie Seymour|Jamie Seymour}}曾製作一部關於水母的記錄片，名叫《「母」形殺手》（''Killer Jellyfish''）<ref name="kingslayer_2016_11_06_crew_sci_am" /><ref name="MJA1964-Barnes">{{cite journal | last = Barnes| first= J. | title = Cause and effect in Irukandji stingings | journal = Med J Aust | volume = 1 | issue = | pages = 897–904 | year = 1964 | pmid = 14172390}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | pmid = 21237252 | doi=10.1016/j.toxlet.2011.01.003 | volume=201 | issue=3 | title=The pharmacology of Malo maxima jellyfish venom extract in isolated cardiovascular tissues: A probable cause of the Irukandji syndrome in Western Australia. | date=Mar 2011 | journal=Toxicol Lett | pages=221–9}}</ref>。

== 分佈 ==
伊魯康吉水母主要棲息於澳洲的北方海域。生長在較南方海域的族群，則在澳洲的東南沿岸，目前被認為是因氣候變遷而有往南遷移的趨勢<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2016-01-18/researcher-warns-dangerous-jellyfish-moving-south/7095422|title=Researcher warns dangerous irukandji jellyfish moving south|accessdate=30 December 2016|publisher=ABC News (Australia)}}</ref>。

== 生物學 ==
[[File:Irukandjijellyfishsize.png|缩略图]]. Below the jelly's [[Medusa_(biology)|medusa]] bell are two [[polyp|polyp]] forms of the species.]]
伊魯康吉水母體型相當嬌小，傘蓋部的寬度大概約5~25毫米，4隻修長的觸手（≤1m）<ref name=Tibballs>{{cite journal |last=Tibballs| first=J. |title=Australian venomous jellyfish, envenomation syndromes, toxins and therapy |journal=[[Toxicon|Toxicon]] |volume=48 |issue=7 |pages=830–59 |date=December 2006 |pmid=16928389 |doi=10.1016/j.toxicon.2006.07.020}}</ref>。其中''Malo Maximus''這個種類的4條觸手上具有環狀的組織。本種亦為所有伊魯康吉水母中毒性最強的一種。該種的幼體在發展出環狀組織以及性腺之前，其毒性遠弱於成熟個體<ref name="kingslayer_2016_11_06_crew_sci_am" />。螫針聚集成叢狀，並且以形式如同紅點組成的環，圍繞著傘蓋部以及觸手<ref name=Tibballs/>。

由於伊魯康吉水母體型嬌小，加上體色透明，使得在水中難以發現牠們<ref name="kingslayer_2016_11_06_crew_sci_am" />。有關伊魯康吉水母的生活史及其毒性目前所知甚少，因為牠們身體太小太脆弱，需要經過特殊處理以及容器來容納。伊魯康吉水母毒性甚強，曾於短短三個月內造成5名到澳洲旅遊的觀光客喪命<ref>{{cite web|title=Fifth tourist in 3 months dies while snorkeling at Great Barrier Reef|url=http://www.foxnews.com/travel/2017/02/03/fifth-tourist-in-3-months-dies-while-snorkeling-at-great-barrier-reef.html|website=Fox News|publisher=The Sun|accessdate=28 June 2017|date=3 February 2017}}</ref>。科學家們猜測，伊魯康及水母的毒液有著能使獵物迅速昏厥的能力，動作迅速的小型魚也不在話下。根據統計，科學家們相信尚有其他種類的水母能引起伊魯康及症候群；截至目前，''Carukia barnesi'' 以及''Malo kingi''兩個種類已被證實擁有這項能力<ref name="kingslayer_2016_11_06_crew_sci_am" /><ref>{{Cite journal | issn = 0025-729X | volume = 1 | pages = 897–904 | last = Barnes | first = J. H. | title = Cause And Effect In Irukandji Stingings | journal = [[The_Medical_Journal_of_Australia|The Medical Journal of Australia]] | date = 1964-06-13 | pmid = 14172390 }}</ref>。

== 螫針(刺絲胞) ==
不像刺絲胞只長在出手上的大部分水母，伊魯康吉水母的傘蓋部也長有螫針(刺絲胞)。目前生物學家們尚未找出這個特殊構造的演化目的。有個假說解釋道，有了這樣的特殊構造，水母便有更大的機會抓到牠的獵物<ref name="kingslayer_2016_11_06_crew_sci_am" />。

伊魯康吉水母能夠從牠觸手末端的螫針(刺絲胞)向受害者這入毒液<ref name="ReferenceA">''Killer Jellyfish'', [[Oasis_(TV_channel)|Oasis]]</ref>。

受伊魯康吉水母的螫傷會產生嚴重的症狀（伊魯康吉症候群），例如引發致命的腦出血；這個症狀造每年共計50~100個人被送進醫院<ref>[http://www.australiangeographic.com.au/news/2014/05/predicting-deadly-australian-jellyfish-movement/ Predicting deadly Australian jellyfish movement], Australian Geographic, 13 May 2014</ref>。受到伊魯康吉水母螫傷1次，比起被眼鏡王蛇攻擊100次，以及受大蘭多蜘蛛攻擊1000次；更能彰顯其毒性的猛烈<ref>[https://books.google.co.in/books?id=v3uDCwAAQBAJ&pg=PA176&lpg=PA176&dq=100+times+as+potent+as+that+of+a+cobra+and+1,000+times+as+strong+as+a+tarantula&source=bl&ots=YZYWXqQdtb&sig=b9EdwrDzGYpRJ82c4Zw8kwK9NlU&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjrk5H1s43PAhWBSpQKHaakBwcQ6AEINzAE#v=onepage&q=100%20times%20as%20potent%20as%20that%20of%20a%20cobra%20and%201%2C000%20times%20as%20strong%20as%20a%20tarantula&f=false The Beach: An Australian Passion, Robert Drewe - Page 176]</ref>。

== 伊魯康吉症候群 ==
{{Main article|伊魯康吉症候群}}
少量的毒液就能引起伊魯康吉症候群，當毒液注入人體，會引起手腳的肌肉極度痙攣、背痛以及腎痛、皮膚與臉部的灼熱感、頭痛、噁心、嘔吐、心神不寧、出汗、心跳加速、血壓升高，以及體會到像是心理現象<ref>{{cite web|last=Carrette |first=Teresa |last2=Seymour |first2=Jamie |title=Jellyfish responsible for causing Irukandji syndrome |url=http://www.jcu.edu.au/interest/stingers/irukandji%20brochure1.pdf |work=[[James_Cook_University|James Cook University]] |accessdate=2011-10-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110608200749/http://www.jcu.edu.au/interest/stingers/irukandji%20brochure1.pdf |archivedate=2011-06-08 |df= }}</ref>。這個症狀有部分的成因源自於[[兒茶酚胺|兒茶酚胺]]的釋放<ref name=Tibballs/>。毒液中也包含了[[鈉離子通道|鈉離子通道]]的調節子<ref name=Tibballs/>。

被螫傷的瞬間並不會產生劇烈的不適，但嚴重的症狀可能會於5~120分鐘後發生。這個些症狀能維持短至幾小時，長至幾星期之久，且病患通常必須住院。多篇研究指出醋能減低水母毒液的毒性，雖然截至目前為止證據依然不足<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Are we using the correct first aid for jellyfish?|url=https://www.mja.com.au/journal/2017/206/6/are-we-using-correct-first-aid-jellyfish|last=Seymour|first=Jamie E.|date=2017-04-03|journal=The Medical Journal of Australia|issue=6|volume=206|pages=249–250}}</ref>，但澳洲復甦醫學委員會（Australian Resuscitation Council）依然推薦使用醋<ref>{{cite web|last1=Wilcox|first1=Christie|title=Should We Stop Using Vinegar To Treat Box Jelly Stings? Not Yet—Venom Experts Weigh In On Recent Study - Science Sushi|url=http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/science-sushi/2014/04/09/stop-using-vinegar-treat-box-jelly-stings-yet-venom-experts-weigh-recent-study/#.WRzZf58pDqA|website=Science Sushi|accessdate=28 June 2017|date=9 April 2014}}</ref>。

螫傷只能針對症狀進行症狀治療，藉著[[抗組織胺|抗組織胺]]和抗高血壓藥物來控制發炎反應以及[[高血壓|高血壓]]；靜脈注射[[嗎啡|嗎啡]]及[[芬太尼|芬太尼]]等[[鴉片類藥物|鴉片類藥物]]能舒緩疼痛<ref name=Greenland>{{cite journal | last1=Greenland| first1=P.| last2=Hutchinson| first2=D.| last3=Park| first3=T. |title=Irukandji Syndrome: what nurses need to know |journal=[[Nursing_and_Health_Sciences|Nursing and Health Sciences]] |volume=8 |issue=1 |pages=66–70 |date=March 2006 |pmid=16451431 |doi=10.1111/j.1442-2018.2006.00255.x |url=}}</ref> 。[[硫酸鎂|硫酸鎂]]曾被報導對於伊魯康吉症候群引起的疼痛及高血壓有效，但進一步研究認為其證據力尚不足以支持此一論點<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Review article: Role of magnesium sulphate in the management of Irukandji syndrome: A systematic review|url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/1742-6723.12694/abstract|last=Rathbone|first=John|last2=Franklin|first2=Richard|date=2017-02-01|journal=Emergency Medicine Australasia|issue=1|doi=10.1111/1742-6723.12694|volume=29|pages=9–17|language=en|issn=1742-6723|last3=Gibbs|first3=Clinton|last4=Williams|first4=David}}</ref>。

伊魯康吉水母喜好棲息於海水較溫暖的沿岸地帶，但也曾被發現在離岸5公里處形成水華的現象。若治療得當，單一個刺絲胞正常來說並不足以致命。2002年時，在澳洲北部沿海，有兩個人被認為死於伊魯康吉水母的螫傷<ref name="kingslayer_2016_11_06_crew_sci_am" /><ref name="MJA2002-Fenner">{{Cite journal | issn = 0025-729X | volume = 177 | issue = 7 | pages = 362–3 | last1 = Fenner | first1 = Peter J. | first2 = John C.| last2 = Hacock | title = Fatal envenomation by jellyfish causing Irukandji syndrome | journal = [[The_Medical_Journal_of_Australia|The Medical Journal of Australia]] | date = 2002-10-07 | pmid = 12358578}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/health/2713211.stm | work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] | title=Hope for lethal jellyfish cure | date=2003-01-31 | accessdate=2010-05-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite book | first=Wendy| last=Lewis | title=See Australia and Die | publisher=[[New_Holland_Publishers|New Holland Publishers]] | year=2007 | isbn=978-1-74110-583-4|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eQw8bJU07nkC&pg=PT21|accessdate=2013-10-02}}</ref>；因為這個事件，大大地提昇了大眾對伊魯康及症候群的認知。不知道有多少死於伊魯康吉症候群的案例被誤認為是其他的死因。除了''Carukia barnesi'' 以及''Malo kingi''之外，目前其他能引發伊魯康吉症候群的水母種類，仍然是個未知<ref>{{cite web|last=Seymour|first=Jamie|last2=Carrette|first2=Teresa|title=Identification of cubozoans responsible for causing Irukandji syndrome|url=http://www.jcu.edu.au/interest/stingers/research%205cuboidentify.htm|work=[[James_Cook_University|James Cook University]]|accessdate=2011-10-02|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080919044131/http://www.jcu.edu.au/interest/stingers/research%205cuboidentify.htm |archivedate=2008-09-19 }}</ref>。

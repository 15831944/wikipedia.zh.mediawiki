'''伊朗阿拉伯裔'''指[[阿拉伯裔伊朗人|在伊朗定居的阿拉伯社區]]，而他們大多數居住於[[伊朗|伊朗]]西南部比鄰[[伊拉克|伊拉克]]的[[胡齊斯坦省|胡齊斯坦省]]<ref name="Middle East Eye">{{cite news |author1=Mamoon Alabbasi |title=Iran's Ahwazi Arab minority: dissent against 'discrimination' |url=http://www.middleeasteye.net/news/irans-ahwazi-arab-minority-dissent-against-discrimination-1658579305|access-date=2015-12-28 |work={{tsl|en|Middle East Eye|}}|date=2015-02-28 |language=en }}</ref>。在胡齊斯坦省，這些阿拉伯裔主要聚居於其首府[[阿瓦士|阿瓦士]]，所以這些阿拉伯裔又被稱為'''阿瓦士阿拉伯人'''<ref name="amnesty">{{cite web |url=https://www.amnesty.org/en/documents/mde13/056/2006/en/ |title=Iran: Defending Minority Rights: The Ahwazi Arabs |date=17 May 2006 |publisher=Amnesty International |accessdate=}}</ref>。

==人口==
[[File:Map_of_Arabian-inhabited_provinces_of_Iran,_according_to_a_poll_in_2010.PNG|thumb]]
根據[[美國|美國]][[國會圖書館_(美國)|國會圖書館]]的資料，伊朗阿拉伯裔在整個伊朗的人口佔了1-2%<ref name="Congress">{{cite web|url=http://lcweb2.loc.gov/frd/cs/profiles/Iran.pdf |title=Ethnic Groups and Languages of Iran |first=Federal Research Division |last=Library of Congress |accessdate=December 2, 2009 |deadurl=bot: unknown |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150316120456/http://lcweb2.loc.gov/frd/cs/profiles/Iran.pdf |archivedate=March 16, 2015 }}</ref>。這些人口絕大多數都是[[什叶派|什叶派]]信眾，就只有沿岸的部分居民為[[遜尼派|遜尼派]]人口<ref name="minahan" >{{cite book|author1=James Minahan|title=Encyclopedia of the Stateless Nations: A-C|date=2002|publisher={{tsl|en|Greenwood Publishing Group|}}|isbn=9780313321092|pages=158-9}}</ref>。

'''伊留申-14'''（'''{{lang|ru|Ил-14}}'''）是[[蘇聯|蘇聯]][[伊留申設計局|伊留申設計局]]研製的双發動機[[客機|客機]]。[[北约命名|北约命名]]为 '''Crate'''。

== 開發 ==
[[File:Ilyushin.Avia_Av-14T_OK-LCA_LBG_29.05.57_edited-2.jpg|thumb]]上的伊尔-14T]] 
用于替换苏联的[[里-2|里-2]]运输机。最初设计出的[[伊尔-12|伊尔-12]]于1945年首飞。<ref name ="gunston russian">{{Cite book|author=Gunston, Bill|authorlink =Bill_Gunston
|title=The Osprey Encyclopedia of Russian Aircraft from 1875 – 1995|publisher=Osprey Aerospace|location=London|year=1995|isbn= 1-85532-405-9}}</ref>)但存在发动机失效后的{{tsl|en|Deadstick landing|滑翔降落}}性能差的问题。载荷仅18人，低于设计时的32人。<ref name ="gunston russian"/>

伊尔-14做了重大改进。重新设计机翼，更宽的尾翼。滑翔性能更好。<ref name ="gunston russian"/> 两者外部最明显的区别就是垂直尾翼伊尔-12是三角形，伊尔-14是矩形；另外，伊尔-12的排气管外露，而伊尔-14的排气管在发动机包皮内。{{Dubious|date=2018年5月}}1950年首飞。1954年开始商飞。适合用于农村与较差质量机场。伊尔-14飞机着重提高了飞机的安全性和舒适性，有较完善的除冰、防冰设备，有效防止了在机翼、尾翼和螺旋桨前缘出现的积冰现象。最有特色的设计是，它的驾驶舱挡风玻璃可以电加温，不会产生积霜而影响飞行员视线。伊尔－14飞机的仪表板上还装有航向下滑仪，配合地面的航向下滑仪，可以保证在云层高30米的复杂天气条件下安全着陆。此外，它的机舱密封性较好，即使在暴雨天气下飞行，机舱内仍能保持干燥、舒适。中国从1955年起购买了49架伊尔-14，承担专机和国内的客运、货运任务。1986年后逐步退役。
 
总产量1,345架：莫斯科机械厂从1956年到1958年（30厂）生产了1,065架。塔什干84厂从1954年到1958年厂生产了。东德VEB Flugzeugwerke Dresden (FWD)从1956年至1959年生产了80架。捷克斯洛伐克巴拉格Avia从1956年到1960年生产了203架。  
 
伊尔-14被[[安-24|安-24]]与[[雅克-40|雅克-40]]取代。

{{TransH}}
;{{CZS}}: [[Czechoslovakian_Air_Force|Czechoslovakian Air Force]]. 50 were operated from 1958, though most were locally built examples delivered from 1968. Most were retired prior to the split of Czechoslovakia, though a small number may have served briefly with its successor states.<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{DDR}}: [[Air_Forces_of_the_National_People's_Army|East German Air Force]]. 30 were delivered, beginning with 11 Ilyushin built aircraft from 1956 and deliveries of East Germany built aircraft commencing the following year and totaling 19 examples. 20 remained by 1979,<ref name="chant_waf"/> and all were withdrawn by 1990, with none being passed on to the unified German [[Luftwaffe|Luftwaffe]].<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{EGY}}: [[Egyptian_Air_Force|Egyptian Air Force]]. 70 were operated by the [[Egyptian_Air_Force|Egyptian Air Force]] from 1955. Most were Soviet built models, but at least one East Germany built Il-14P was delivered in 1957. A number of aircraft were destroyed during fighting with [[Israel|Israel]], but 26 survived to the peace of 1979.<ref name="chant_waf"/> Acquisition of Western aircraft from then on led to the retirement of the Il-14 by 1994.<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{ETH}}: [[Ethiopian_Air_Force|Ethiopian Air Force]]. 2 were acquired in 1965, with one remaining in service by 1979<ref name="chant_waf"/> and finally retired by 1994.<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{GNB}}: 4 were in operation in 1979.<ref name="chant_waf"/>
;{{HUN}}: [[Hungarian_Air_Force|Hungarian Air Force]]. Two Il-14Ps were operated from 1959 to 1976.
;{{IND}}: [[Indian_Air_Force|Indian Air Force]]. 26 were delivered from 1955<ref name="waf_listing"/> but were withdrawn by 1979.<ref name="chant_waf"/>
;{{IDN}}: [[Indonesian_Air_Force|Indonesian Air Force]]. 22 were delivered from 1957 and withdrawn by 1975.<ref name="waf_listing"/><ref name="chant_waf"/>
;{{IRQ}}: [[Iraqi_Air_Force|Iraqi Air Force]]. 13 Il-14M were delivered in 1958, with 3 remaining by 1979.<ref name="chant_waf"/> The last aircraft were withdrawn after the first [[Gulf_War|Gulf War]].<ref name="waf_listing"/>

;{{POL}}: [[Polish_Air_Force|Polish Air Force]]. 12 or more served from 1955, including Soviet built Il-14P, Il-14S, and Il-14T, as well as East Germany built Il-14P and Il-14T models. These served until the 1980s.<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{flag|Romania|1952}}: [[Romanian_Air_Force|Romanian Air Force]]. 33 were delivered from 1955, including 30 East Germany built Il-14P models as well as 3 Il-14M aircraft delivered in 1961. Only 4 remained in service by 1979,<ref name="chant_waf"/> with the last Il-14M being retired in 1983. None remained in service by 1993.<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{flag|South Yemen}}: South Yemen Air Force. 4 were delivered from 1966, serving as late as 1988.<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{USSR}}: [[Soviet_Air_Force|Soviet Air Force]] and [[Soviet_Naval_Aviation|Soviet Naval Aviation]]. Serving from 1954, 235 were in service in 1979.<ref name="chant_waf"/>

;{{VIE}}: [[Vietnam_People's_Air_Force|Vietnam People's Air Force]]. 45 were delivered from 1958, with 12 remaining in service by 1979.<ref name="chant_waf"/> None remained in service by 1998.<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{YEM}}: [[Yemen_Air_Force|Yemen Air Force]]. 1 was inherited from North Yemen in 1990, serving for a short time before being retired.<ref name="waf_listing"/>
;{{YUG}}: [[SFR_Yugoslav_Air_Force|SFR Yugoslav Air Force]]. Six others were given to the Air Force by [[Yugoslav_Airlines|Yugoslav Airlines]] in 1963. and were used until 1974. The one presented to Tito is preserved in the [[Museum_of_Yugoslav_Aviation|Museum of Yugoslav Aviation]] in Belgrade.

== 空難及事件 ==
*  1958年4月5日，从成都飞往北京的中国民航成都管理处伊尔-14型632号飞机在陕西[[佛坪|佛坪]]四方台山撞山坠毁，机上9名乘客及5名机组人员全部罹难，是为[[中国民航|中国民航]](CAAC)首次空难。
*1958年4月5日　民航广州管理局第六飞行大队伊尔－14型660号飞机在[[衡阳|衡阳]]附近遇滚轴云，飞机失控，随急剧上升下降气流颠簸，飞机结构受损，造成二等事故。
* 1968年12月5日，民航兰州管理局第八飞行大队伊尔-14型640号飞机执飞兰州-西安-北京航班，在北京机场着陆时触地起火造成一等事故。
* 1970年11月14日　民航广州管理局第六飞行大队使用伊尔－14型616号飞机执行成都－重庆－贵阳－桂林－广州航班任务（民航广州局于当年10月1日使用伊尔－14型飞机开辟该航线），飞机在贵阳[[磊庄机场|磊庄机场]]下降时在远台外5100米处的摆平山撞山失事，机组6人遇难，1人受伤。
* 1973年1月14日　民航成都管理局第七飞行大队使用伊尔－14型644号飞机执行成都－重庆－贵阳－桂林－广州航班任务，飞机在贵阳[[磊庄机场|磊庄机场]]中心5度11km处岩坡失事，造成一等事故。
* 1988年10月7日，山西航空公司一架伊尔—14型B—4218号飞机，在山西省临汾市执行游览飞行任务中失事，机上旅客44名，机组4名，除4名旅客被救出以外，其他人员全部遇难，另有2名地面人员也遇难。
* 1992年10月8日[[武汉航空公司|武汉航空公司]]伊尔-14执行兰州—西安包机任务，飞机在飞行途中左发故障返航，准备备降榆中机场，后飞机在甘肃定县撞上山坡后解体。机组5人和旅客9人罹难；机组2人和旅客19人受伤。

'''伊莫珍·盖伊·波茨'''（{{lang-en|'''Imogen Gay Poots'''}}，{{bd|1989年|6月5日|||catIdx= G波茨}}）出生于[[英国|英国]][[英格兰|英格兰]][[伦敦|伦敦]]，英国女演员，因出演《[[28週後|28周后]]》而闻名。<ref>[http://ftvdb.bfi.org.uk/sift/individual/1209623 BFI Biography]</ref><ref name="power">Power, Chris (29 April 2007) [http://observer.guardian.co.uk/magazine/story/0,,2065851,00.html "Rising star: Imogen Poots"] ''[[The_Observer|The Observer]]''</ref><ref>Newman, Sara (21 May 2007) [http://news.independent.co.uk/people/profiles/article2565129.ece The 5-Minute Interview: Imogen Poots, Actress] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080108012807/http://news.independent.co.uk/people/profiles/article2565129.ece |date=2008-01-08 }}, ''[[The_Independent|The Independent]]''</ref>

== 生平 ==
1989年，波茨出生于[[英国|英国]][[英格兰|英格兰]][[伦敦|伦敦]]，母亲菲欧娜，父亲特雷弗·波茨（Trevor Poots），是一个电视制作人。<ref name="indep">{{Cite web|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/people/profiles/imogen-poots-a-bright-young-thing-who-wont-suffer-for-her-art-1960382.html|title=Imogen Poots: A bright young thing who won't suffer for her art|date=2 May 2010|work=[[The_Independent|The Independent]]|first= |last= |accessdate=2 May 2010}}</ref>

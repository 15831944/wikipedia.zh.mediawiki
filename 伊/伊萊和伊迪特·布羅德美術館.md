'''伊萊和伊迪特·布羅德美術館'''（Eli and Edythe Broad Art Museum）是[[密西根州|密西根州]][[東蘭辛|東蘭辛]][[密西根州立大學|密西根州立大學]]的當代藝術美術館。它於2012年11月10日開幕<ref>{{cite web|title=Eli and Edythe Broad Art Museum at MSU to open Nov. 10|url=http://news.msu.edu/story/eli-edythe-broad-art-museum-msu-open-nov-9/|accessdate=12 August 2012}}</ref><ref name="MuseumOpening">{{cite news|first=Brandon|last=Howell |title=Broad Art Museum draws thousands to Michigan State during opening weekend; $40 million fundraising goal met|url=http://www.mlive.com/lansing-news/index.ssf/2012/11/broad_art_museum_draws_thousan.html#incart_river_default|location=East Lansing|work=MLive Lansing|date=November 13, 2012}}</ref>。

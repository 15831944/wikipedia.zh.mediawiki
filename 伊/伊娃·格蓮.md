'''伊娃·格蓮'''（{{lang-sv|Eva Gaëlle Green}}，{{IPA-sv|ˈɡreːn|lang}}，{{IPA-fr|ɡʁin|lang}}，{{bd|1980年|7月5日|catIdx=G}}），生于法国巴黎，[[法國|法國]]女[[演员|演员]]及[[电影|电影]]演員。她最著名的角色是在第21部007系列電影《[[007首部曲：皇家夜總會|007首部曲：皇家夜總會]]》飾演[[慧絲柏·蓮|慧絲柏·蓮]]。

== 早年生活 ==
伊娃的父親是瑞典牙醫，華特·格蓮（Walter Green），曾在一個電視劇客串演出；母親是法國影星，{{Le|瑪琳·祖拔|Marlène Jobert}}（Marlène Jobert）為[[黑腳|黑腳]][[塞法迪犹太人|塞法迪犹太人]]<ref>{{cite web|first=Stuart|last=Jeffries|url=https://www.theguardian.com/tv-and-radio/2016/may/03/eva-green-penny-dreadful-witch-golden-compass-casino-royale-camelot|title=Eva Green: ‘I don’t want to be put in a box marked Weird Witch’|work=The Guardian|date=May 3, 2016|accessdate=April 29, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Elizabeth|last=Day|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2011/jun/05/eva-green-interview-playing-evil|title=Eva Green interview: Playing evil|work=The Guardian|date=June 5, 2011|accessdate=April 29, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://film.avclub.com/eva-green-1798225065|title=Eva Green|last=Adams|first=Sam|date=April 14, 2011|work=A.V. Film Club|accessdate=May 27, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.vogue.co.uk/article/eva-green-on-john-galliano|title=My Friend John|last=Milligan|first=Lauren|date=June 6, 2011|work=Vogue UK Magazine|accessdate=May 27, 2018}}</ref>；她還有一位晚於她四分鐘出生的异卵双胞胎妹妹Joy（Joanne）。

== 個人生活 ==
她覺得自己是個[[Nerd|怪胎]]<ref name=noteasy>{{cite news |author=Verghis, Sharon |title=Not easy being Green |work=The Age |location=Australia |date=3 December 2006 |url=http://www.theage.com.au/news/film/not-easy-being-green/2006/11/30/1164777722472.html |accessdate=27 August 2007}}</ref>，她還說「當人們初次見到我的時候，他們感受到我的冷酷…我表現出與人們之間的距離感，我想這就是為何我會朝向表演工作的原因，這就像是戴上面具的感覺。」<ref name=twominutes>{{cite news |author=Williamson, Charlotte |title=Green Goddess |publisher=Harpers & Queen |date = June 2005|page=111}}</ref><ref name=fla>{{cite news|author=Palmer, Martyn |title=Faith No More|work=[[Total_Film|Total Film]]|date=December 2007|page=90}}</ref><ref name=noteasy/>。根據她自己的描述，她自己一人單獨居住，除了工作以外，就只有過著低調生活。接受[[面談|記者訪問]]時，被詢問到是否有令人感到驚訝的事，她說：{{cquote|我猜想人們會對我的宅女性格而感到驚訝。我不喜歡夜店生活或狂歡[[派對|派對]]。在一整天的拍攝工作之後，我喜歡回到家裡休息，待在[[壁爐|壁爐]]旁邊，與一杯[[葡萄酒|葡萄酒]]還有一本書。這樣的生活聽起來很無聊，對吧？{{notetag|我猜想人們會對我的宅女性格而感到驚訝。我不喜歡夜店生活或狂歡派對。在一整天的拍攝工作之後，我喜歡回到家裡休息，待在壁爐旁邊，與一杯葡萄酒還有一本書。這樣的生活聽起來很無聊，對吧？（{{lang-en|I guess people would be surprised to find out that I am a bit of a homebody. I do not like clubbing or going to wild parties. After a day of shooting, I love to come home and relax by the fire with a glass of wine and a good book. Boring, huh?）}}}}<ref>{{cite web|title=Exclusive EvaGreenWeb.com Q&A with Eva Green – Part II|url=http://evagreenweb.com/2011/05/06/exclusive-evagreenweb-com-qa-with-eva-green-–-part-ii/|publisher=evagreenweb.com|accessdate=24 April 2014}}</ref>}}她說[[剝製|動物標本]]與[[昆虫学|昆虫学]]是她的興趣，她會收集[[颅骨|颅骨]]與昆蟲<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gq-magazine.co.uk/girls/articles/2011-10/05/gq-girls-eva-green-interview-film-movies-perfect-sense-dark-shadows|title=GQ&A: Eva Green|work=GQ}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.shortlist.com/entertainment/films/eva-green-im-basically-an-old-lady|title=Eva Green: "I'm Basically An Old Lady" – ShortList Magazine|work=ShortList Magazine}}</ref>。她表達出對於想回到劇院表演的興趣<ref name=onstage>{{cite news |author=Russell, Steve |title = Auteur's Muse |work=Rolling Stone |date = 24 March 2005 |url = https://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/7203748/eva_green |accessdate=27 August 2007|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20071001202011/http://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/7203748/eva_green |archivedate = 1 October 2007|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。<p>她說她對於[[好莱坞|好莱坞]]的全職表演工作還沒有相關的計畫，因為「好萊塢的問題就在於[[电影制片厂|电影制片厂]]掌管的控制權力是非常大，他們的管理權比[[電影導演|電影導演]]還大很多…此時此刻我只想找到好的[[剧本|剧本]]」{{notetag|好萊塢的問題就在於電影製片廠掌管的控制權力是非常大，他們的管理權比電影導演還大很多…此時此刻我只想找到好的劇本（{{lang-en|the problem with Hollywood is that the studios are super powerful, they have far more power than the directors... [my] ambition at this moment is just to find a good script}}）}}<ref name=womanlike>{{cite news |author=Bottelier, Steffanie |title=Een vrouw ais Eva |publisher=Netherlands ''[[Elle_(magazine)|Elle]]'' |date = September 2007|page=230 |language=nl}}</ref>。<p>她支持[[联合国儿童基金会|联合国儿童基金会]]<ref name=fla/>。在2014年，她在[[英國恐怖故事|英國恐怖故事]]飾演一名通靈人士，她談論到她的精神層面，她說雖然她沒有宗教信仰，但是她相信超自然力量<ref>[http://www.belfasttelegraph.co.uk/entertainment/news/eva-green-shares-spiritual-side-30502079.html Belfast Telegraph – Eva Green shares spiritual side] Retrieved 13 August 2014</ref>。在2017年，她透露說[[哈維·溫斯坦|哈維·溫斯坦]]曾經在開會時做出不適當的行為而差點毀了她的職業生涯<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2017/film/news/eva-green-harvey-weinstein-1202590025/|title=Eva Green Says Harvey Weinstein ‘Behaved Inappropriately … I Had to Push Him Off’ (EXCLUSIVE)|date=14 October 2017|website=Variety|accessdate=24 November 2018}}</ref>。

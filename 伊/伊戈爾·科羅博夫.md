'''伊戈爾·科羅博夫'''[[大将|大将]]（{{lang-ru|'''Игорь Коробов'''}}，{{bd|1956年|8月3日|2018年|11月21日}}），俄罗斯[[軍事情報|軍事情報]]總局（[[格鲁乌|格魯烏]]）局長。

== 早年 ==
伊戈尔 ·科罗博夫於1956年8月3日在[[斯摩棱斯克州|斯摩棱斯克州]][[維亞濟馬|維亞濟馬]]出生<ref name="OFAC">{{cite news|url=https://www.treasury.gov/resource-center/sanctions/OFAC-Enforcement/Pages/20161229.aspx|title=Issuance of Amended Executive Order 13694; Cyber-Related Sanctions Designations|author=[[Office_of_Foreign_Assets_Control|Office of Foreign Assets Control]]|date= 2016-12-29|publisher=[[美国财政部|United States Department of the Treasury]]|quote=KOROBOV, Igor (a.k.a. KOROBOV, Igor Valentinovich); DOB 03 Aug 1956; nationality Russia; Passport 100119726 (Russia); alt. Passport 100115101 (Russia); Chief of GRU (individual) [CYBER2] (Linked To: MAIN INTELLIGENCE DIRECTORATE).|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161229210346/https://www.treasury.gov/resource-center/sanctions/OFAC-Enforcement/Pages/20161229.aspx|archive-date=December 29, 2016}}</ref><ref name="MoDBio">{{cite news|url=http://structure.mil.ru/management/details.htm?id=12076428@SD_Employee|title=Коробов Игорь Валентинович|publisher=[[俄罗斯联邦国防部|Ministry of Defence of the Russian Federation]]|deadurl=yes|language=ru|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161230062052/http://structure.mil.ru/management/details.htm?id=12076428%40SD_Employee|archive-date=2016-12-30}}</ref>。1977年，科羅博夫以优异的成绩毕业于[[北高加索|北高加索]]軍區的[[斯塔夫罗波尔高等军事航空学校|斯塔夫罗波尔高等軍方飛行員及領航員航空学校]]，成为苏联空军的一名军官<ref name="MoDBio" /><ref>{{cite news|url=http://bmpd.livejournal.com/1723886.html|title=Новый начальник ГРУ: штрихи к портрету|date=2016-02-07|publisher=[[LiveJournal|LiveJournal]]|language=ru|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161229214059/http://bmpd.livejournal.com/1723886.html|archive-date=2016-12-29}}</ref>。

== 职业生涯 ==
科罗博夫曾經担任战略情报局局長（''Upravlenie strategicheskoi razvedky''）<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.janes.com/article/57724/new-russian-military-intelligence-chief-appointed|title=New Russian military intelligence chief appointed|last=Jones|first=Bruce|date=2016-02-04|publisher=''[[简氏防务周刊|Jane's Defence Weekly]]''|quote=The appointment comes one month after the sudden death of Lt Gen Korobov's predecessor at the Main Intelligence Directorate of the General Staff (Glavnoye Razvedyvatel'noye Upravleniye), General Igor Dmitrievich Sergun. Prior to the appointment, Lt Gen Korobov was a first deputy director within the organisation, responsible for strategic intelligence. He also acted as caretaker leader after Gen Sergun's death.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161229211336/http://www.janes.com/article/57724/new-russian-military-intelligence-chief-appointed|archive-date=2016-12-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://warontherocks.com/2016/02/what-putins-security-appointments-say-about-how-russia-works/|title=What Putin’s Security Appointments Say About How Russia Works|last=Galeotti|first=Mark|date=2016-02-09|publisher=''War on the Rocks''|quote=From the first, it was clear that all the main stakeholders — Defense Minister Sergei Shoigu, Chief of the General Staff Valeri Gerasimov, and the GRU hierarchy itself — favored the promotion of one of Sergun’s depties: Vyacheslav Kondrashev, Sergei Sizunov, Igor Lelin, and Igor Korobov. Of them all, Korobov, head of the Strategic Intelligence Directorate (USR: Upravlenie strategicheskoi razvedky) was clearly the front-runner.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161229205959/http://warontherocks.com/2016/02/what-putins-security-appointments-say-about-how-russia-works/|archive-date=2016-12-29}}</ref>。2016年1月，時任情報總局局長Igor Sergun突然去世，科罗博夫被總統[[普京|普京]]任命接替<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.rferl.org/a/russia-putin-korobov-new-gru-chief-military-intelligence/27528279.html|title=Putin Names Korobov As New Military Intelligence Chief|date=2016-02-02|publisher=[[自由欧洲电台／自由电台|Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty]]|quote=President Vladimir Putin has appointed Lieutenant General Igor Korobov to head the Russian military intelligence agency, known as the GRU, following the death of his predecessor in early January.|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161229205621/http://www.rferl.org/a/russia-putin-korobov-new-gru-chief-military-intelligence/27528279.html|archive-date=2016-12-29}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/u-s-punishes-russia-over-election-hacking-with-sanctions-1483039178|title=U.S. Sanctions Russia Over Election Hacking; Russia Threatens to Retaliate|first1=Carol E.|date=2016-12-29|publisher=''[[The_Wall_Street_Journal|The Wall Street Journal]]''|last2=Sonne|first2=Paul|accessdate=2016-12-29|quote=The U.S. sanctions include GRU General Lieutenant Igor Korobov, who took over as Russia’s military intelligence chief early this year after his predecessor died while on the job.|last1=Lee}}</ref>。

2016年12月29日，美国财政部以「利用網絡進行影響[[美國|美國]]國安的惡意活動」為由制裁多人，包括科罗博夫<ref name="OFAC"/>。尽管如此，他还是在2018年1月底和其他俄罗斯高级安全官员一起正式访问了美国<ref>[http://tass.com/world/988213 US suspends sanctions against Russian security chiefs during their visit to Washington] [[TASS|TASS]], 2018-02-02.</ref>。科罗博夫亦被指控為干預美國總統選舉、盜取化武監察組織資料、和[[英國前俄羅斯間諜毒殺案|英國梳士巴利毒劑襲擊]]的幕後主謀。

俄罗斯官方媒體引述[[俄罗斯联邦国防部|國防部]]的消息：科罗博夫在2018年11月21日因長期患重病而去世，終年62歲<ref>[https://tvzvezda.ru/news/forces/content/201811220142-fmoq.htm Умер начальник ГУ ГШ РФ Игорь Коробов] tvzvezda.ru, 22 November 2018.</ref><ref name="Tass-Died">{{cite news|url=https://tass.ru/armiya-i-opk/5820117?fbclid=IwAR3VXsvsZFm7Fc-E3kbDddD0xKt8Swm5-jh3xcb3JHj7vEhIffbv-DAlFj4|title=Источник: умер начальник Главного управления Генштаба ВС РФ Игорь Коробов|date=2018-11-22|agency=[[TASS|TASS]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rt.com/news/444590-russia-gru-head-dead/|title=Russian military intelligence GRU chief dies after ‘serious illness’ – MoD}}</ref>。科羅博夫成為繼Igor Sergun後第二位任內逝世的情報總局局長。

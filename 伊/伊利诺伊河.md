'''伊利诺伊河'''（{{lang-en|Illinois River}}）是[[密西西比河|密西西比河]]的一条主要[[支流|支流]],，长约{{convert|273|mi|0}}，位于[[伊利诺伊州|伊利诺伊州]]<ref>[http://www.riverwebmuseums.org/river_facts/basics/illinois/ Riverweb Illinois River basics] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160601161221/http://www.riverwebmuseums.org/river_facts/basics/illinois/ |date=2016-06-01 }}</ref>，其流域面积为{{convert|28070|sqmi|0}}<ref name="rg" />，覆盖了伊利诺伊州中部，并延伸至[[威斯康辛州|威斯康辛州]]和[[印第安纳州|印第安纳州]]境内。对于[[美国原住民|美国原住民]]和早期法国商人而言，这条河是重要的连接[[五大湖|五大湖]]地区和密西西比河的水道。The French colonial settlements along the river formed the heart of the area known as the [[Illinois_Country|Illinois Country]]. After the construction of the [[Illinois_and_Michigan_Canal|Illinois and Michigan Canal]] and the [[Hennepin_Canal_Parkway_State_Park|Hennepin Canal]] in the 19th century, the role of the river as link between [[Lake_Michigan|Lake Michigan]] and the Mississippi was extended into the era of modern industrial shipping.

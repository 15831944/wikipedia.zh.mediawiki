-'''伊恩·麥可·金斯勒'''（{{lang-en| Ian Michael Kinsler}}，{{bd|1982年|6月22日}}），出生在[[圖森_(亞利桑那州)|圖森 (亞利桑那州)]]， 現為[[美國職棒大聯盟|美國職棒大聯盟]][[聖地牙哥教士|聖地牙哥教士]]的[[二壘手|二壘手]]。金斯勒是具有力量、跑壘技術及速度、守備力、選球
和打擊能力的5項功能選手（five-tool player）<ref name="jewishvirtuallibrary1">{{cite web|url=http://www.jewishvirtuallibrary.org/jsource/biography/Ian_Kinsler.html|last=Ariel|first=Bard|title=Ian Kinsler|publisher=''[[Jewish_Virtual_Library|Jewish Virtual Library]]''|date=|accessdate=May 28, 2009}}</ref><ref>[http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/2009/writers/jonah_freedman/05/11/ian.kinsler/?eref=sircrc Freedman, Jonah, "Are Kinsler's gaudy stats another creation of Rangers Ballpark?," ''[[Sports_Illustrated|Sports Illustrated]]'', 5/11/09, accessed 8/7/09]</ref>。

== 早期生活 ==
金斯勒在2000年6月5日參加大聯盟選秀會，在第29輪第879名被[[亞利桑那響尾蛇|亞利桑那響尾蛇]]所選中，但是他拒絕簽約，隔年再度參加大聯盟選秀會，在第26輪第788名再度被響尾蛇所選中，但是他還是拒絕簽約，而選擇就讀[[亞利桑那州立大學|亞利桑那州立大學]] <ref name= rel >{{cite web|last=Clemmons|first=Anna Katherine|title=Kinsler relishes 'under the radar' role|publisher=''[[ESPN|ESPN]]''|url= http://sports.espn.go.com/highschool/rise/baseball/news/story?id=4226656|date=2009-06-04|accessdate=2009-08-13}}</ref>，和[[波士頓紅襪|波士頓紅襪]]的[[達斯汀·佩德羅亞|達斯汀·佩德羅亞]]是隊友。他們倆當時在爭搶[[游擊手|游擊手]]的位置，最終還是由佩德羅伊亞守游擊，而金斯勒守二壘<ref>[http://msn.foxsports.com/mlb/story/9772962/Surprised-Kinsler-was-overlooked-again?-Don't-be Surprised Ringolsby, Tracy, "Kinsler was overlooked again? Don't be," ''[[Fox_Sports|Fox Sports]]'', 7/7/09, accessed 8/10/09] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090806004959/http://msn.foxsports.com/mlb/story/9772962/Surprised-Kinsler-was-overlooked-again?-Don%27t-be |date=2009-08-06 }}</ref>。金斯勒後來轉學去了[[密蘇裏大學|密蘇裏大學]]。2003年進入密蘇裏大學以後，金斯勒在49場比賽裡面，繳出0.335打擊率、0.416上壘率和16次盜壘的成績<ref name=bre>{{cite web|last=Manuel|first=John|title=Kinsler's Breakout Defies Even Self-Explanation|publisher=''[[Baseball_America|Baseball America]]''|url=http://www.baseballamerica.com/today/minors/040625pulse.html|date= 2004-05-06|accessdate=2009-08-04}}</ref><ref name=ff>{{cite web|last=Winston|first=Lisa  |title=Kinsler moves fast forward|publisher= ''[[USA_Today|USA Today]]''|url=http://www.usatoday.com/sports/bbw/columnist/winston/2004-11-17-minors_x.htm|date= 2004-11-17|accessdate=2009-08-04}}</ref>。

== 小聯盟生涯 ==
在2003年，金斯勒第三次參加大聯盟選秀會，他在第17輪第496名被德州遊騎兵所選中，這次他選擇簽下合約，並在這一年進入1A以游擊手身分出場比賽，他繳出0.277打擊率、11次盜壘和6支三壘安打的成績<ref>[http://www.oursportscentral.com/services/releases/?l_id=47&t_id=477&id=3035888 "Indians Alumni Climbing The Ladder," ''OurSports Central'', 5/17/04, accessed 8/4/09]</ref>。2004年，金斯勒在1A和2A共繳出0.345打擊率、20發全壘打、99分打點、0.413上壘率和23盜壘的成績<ref name=mino>{{cite web|last=|first=|title=Ian Kinsler, Minor League Stats |publisher=''[[Baseball-Reference|Baseball-Reference]]''|url= http://www.baseball-reference.com/minors/player.cgi?id=kinsle001ian |date=|accessdate=2009-08-13}}</ref>。2005年春訓，金斯勒繳出0.327打擊率的成績<ref name= HB>{{cite web|last=Hindman |first=M.J.|title=Five Questions: Texas Rangers|publisher= ''[[The_Hardball_Times|The Hardball Times]]''|url=http://www.hardballtimes.com/main/article/five-questions-texas-rangers1/|date=2006-03-18|accessdate=2009-08-05}}</ref>，之後球隊將其送至3A，而沒有讓他上到大聯盟擔任球隊的二壘手<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://texas.rangers.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20050601&content_id=1071527&vkey=news_tex&fext=.jsp&c_id=tex |title=Kinsler, Ian, "A Star is Born," ''MLB.com'' 6/1/05, accessed 9/14/09 |access-date=2010-02-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605000000/http://texas.rangers.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20050601&content_id=1071527&vkey=news_tex&fext=.jsp&c_id=tex |archive-date=2011-06-05 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref>[http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/vault/article/web/COM1038612/index.htm Borhorst, Mike, "Minor League Watch," ''Sports Illustrated'', 6/8/05, accessed 9/14/09]</ref>。金斯勒在3A繳出0.274打擊率、23發全壘打、94分打點和16次盜壘成功的成績，但這兩年加起來的失誤有53個，守備方面是金斯勒須再加強的地方<ref name="Player Profile01">{{Cite web|url=http://mlb.com/team/player_career.jsp?player_id=435079&y=2005|title=Player Profile: Ian Kinsler (Biography : 2005 Career Highlights). |work=MLB.com|language=en|accessdate=2008年2月9日}}</ref>。

2009年4月15日，金斯勒在先發面對[[巴爾的摩金鶯|巴爾的摩金鶯]]比賽中，擊出[[完全打擊|完全打擊]]<ref>[http://bleacherreport.com/articles/157650-ranger-ian-kinslers-6-hit-cycle-emulates-the-great-jackie-robinson Irby, Matthew, "Ranger Ian Kinsler's Six-Hit Cycle Emulates the Great Jackie Robinson," ''Bleacher Report'', 4/17/09, accessed 5/28/09]</ref>，他成為遊騎兵第4位完成此紀錄的選手（是隊史唯一右打的選手）。在這場比賽裡面，金斯勒在正規9局的比賽裡面有6支6的表現，是遊騎兵隊史第二位達成此記錄的選手（第一位有此記錄的選手是在2004年5月8日由[[阿方索·索利安諾|阿方索·索利安諾]]所完成），此外，金斯勒還有5分得分。4月20日，金斯勒獲得美國聯盟單月表現最佳球員<ref>[http://www.bbtia.com/home/2009/4/21/tuesday-morning-rangers-notes-washingtons-vote-of-confidence.html Matschulat, Joey, "Tuesday Morning Rangers Notes: Washington's Vote Of Confidence ," ''Baseball Time in Arlington'', 4/21/09, accessed 5/28/09]</ref><ref>[http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20090420&content_id=4353780&vkey=news_mlb&fext=.jsp&c_id=mlb Sanchez, Jesse,"Greinke, Kinsler AL co-Players of the Week," ''MLB.com'', 5/28/09, accessed 5/28/09]</ref>。6月16日對休士頓太空人的比賽中，金斯勒擊出生涯第9次的單場兩發全壘打<ref>[http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20090503&content_id=4551512&vkey=recap&fext=.jsp&c_id=mlb Sullivan, T.R., "Harrison helps Rangers wrap up series," ''MLB.com'', 5/28/09, accessed 5/28/09]</ref>。9月25日，金斯勒擊出今年球季第30發全壘打，讓他成為單季有30發全壘打和30次盜壘成功的[[30-30俱樂部|30-30俱樂部]]球員<ref>[http://www.dallasnews.com/sharedcontent/dws/spt/baseball/rangers/stories/092609dnsporanglede.1b7754b04.html Andro, Anthony, "Texas Rangers defeat Rays, 8-3," ''Fort Worth Star-Telegram'', 9/26/09, accessed 9/26/09]</ref><ref>[http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20091120&content_id=7692912&vkey=news_tex&fext=.jsp&c_id=tex&partnerId=rss_tex Jackson, Josh, "Path of the Pros: Ian Kinsler; All-Star outplayed expectations from Day 1", MLB.com, November 20, 2009, accessed January 21, 2010]</ref>。這是遊騎兵隊史第一位達成此紀錄的二壘手（上一位有此紀錄的隊友為[[阿方索·索利安諾|阿方索·索利安諾]]在2005年所完成）<ref>[http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20090925&content_id=7156404&vkey=recap&fext=.jsp&c_id=mlb Friedlander, Andrews, "Kinsler joins 30/30 club in Rangers' win:  Slugger hits 30th homer; Holland hurls five solid innings," ''MLB.com'', 9/26/09, accessed 9/26/09]</ref>。

== 私人生活 ==
金斯勒是[[猶太教|猶太教]]人，也是猶太社區（Jewish community）一位著名的人物<ref>[http://www.jewishsf.com/content/2-0-/module/displaystory/story_id/29266/format/html/displaystory.html Bloom, Nate, "Celebrity Jews," ''JWeekly'', 5/19/06, 8/7/09]</ref><ref>[http://www.boston.com/sports/baseball/redsox/extras/extra_bases/2008/07/manny_noshows_f.html Edes, Gordon, "Manny no-shows for media gabfest: World still round," ''[[The_Boston_Globe|The Boston Globe]]'', 7/14/08, 3/21/09]</ref>。金斯勒在2006年11月18日結婚，太太是他高校時候交往的戀人，他的女兒在2008年12月5日出生<ref>[http://www.dallasnews.com/sharedcontent/dws/spt/stories/030406dnspokinsler.216e20c5.html Grant, Evan, "Rangers' Kinsler eyeing second base," ''The Dallas Morning News'', 3/4/06, accessed 3/21/09]</ref>。

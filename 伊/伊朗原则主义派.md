'''原则主义派'''（{{lang-fa|اصول‌گرایان|Osul-Garâyân}}），也被称作'''伊朗保守派'''<ref>{{cite book|author1=Said Amir Arjomand|author2=Nathan J. Brown|title=The Rule of Law, Islam, and Constitutional Politics in Egypt and Iran|year=2013|publisher=SUNY Press|isbn=978-1-4384-4597-7|page=150|quote=“Conservative” is no longer a preferred term in Iranian political discourse. Usulgara', which can be clumsily translated as “principlist” is the term now used to refer to an array of forces that previously identified themselves as conservative, fundamentalist, neo-fundamentalist, or traditionalist. It developed to counter the term ''eslahgara'', or reformist, and is applied to a camp of not necessarily congrous groups and individuals.}}</ref><ref name="SRD">{{cite thesis|last=Randjbar-Daemi|first=Siavush|date=2012|title=Intra-State Relations in the Islamic Republic of Iran: The Presidency and the Struggle for Political Authority, 1989-2009|chapter=Glossary of the most commonly-used Persian terms and abbreviations|type=Ph.D. thesis|publisher=[[Royal_Holloway,_University_of_London|Royal Holloway, University of London]]|url=https://pure.royalholloway.ac.uk/portal/en/publications/intrastate-relations-in-the-islamic-republic-of-iran-the-presidency-and-the-struggle-for-political-authority-1989-2009(c2be4094-7393-4d82-a321-a65cb127eb1a).html|others=Martin, Vanessa (Supervisor)|page=11}} [[File:Cc_by-nc-nd_euro_icon.svg|30px]] Open access material licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-NoDerivs 3.0 Unported License.</ref>、'''保守-强硬派'''<ref>{{cite journal |author1=李国富 |title=展望伊朗总统鲁哈尼第二任的内外政策 |journal=当代世界 |doi=10.19422/j.cnki.ddsj.2017.07.014 |url=http://kns.cnki.net/KXReader/Detail?TIMESTAMP=636884481974050000&DBCODE=CJFQ&TABLEName=CJFDLAST2017&FileName=JSDD201707015&RESULT=1&SIGN=xEMGgvPh9SK%2bkA3symTN39B%2f2CI%3d&UID=WEEvREcwSlJHSldRa1Fhb09jT0pkRlgzdEJCWVpPei9DL0xsWUxHaExyTT0=$9A4hF_YAuvQ5obgVAqNKPCYcEjKensW4IQMovwHtwkF4VYPoHbKxJw!!&filetitle=%e5%b1%95%e6%9c%9b%e4%bc%8a%e6%9c%97%e6%80%bb%e7%bb%9f%e9%b2%81%e5%93%88%e5%b0%bc%e7%ac%ac%e4%ba%8c%e4%bb%bb%e7%9a%84%e5%86%85%e5%a4%96%e6%94%bf%e7%ad%96_%e6%9d%8e%e5%9b%bd%e5%af%8c#}}</ref>或'''伊朗右翼'''<ref name="SRD"/><ref>{{cite book|last1=Haddad Adel|first1=Gholamali|last2=Elmi|first2=Mohammad Jafar|last3=Taromi-Rad|first3=Hassan|title=Political Parties: Selected Entries from Encyclopaedia of the World of Islam|publisher=EWI Press|entry=Jāme'e-ye Rowhāniyyat-e Mobārez|isbn=9781908433022|pages=108|date=2012-08-31}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|editor=Robin B. Wright|title= The Iran Primer: Power, Politics, and U.S. Policy|publisher=US Institute of Peace Press|year=2010|isbn=978-1601270849|page=37}}</ref>，[[伊朗伊斯兰革命|伊斯兰革命]]之后与[[伊朗改革派|改革派]]构成两大主要政治阵营。某些欧美媒体称其为“强硬派”，通常指的是阵营中的某些极右派系<ref>{{Citation|author=Masoud Kazemzadeh|title= Intra-Elite Factionalism and the 2004 Majles Elections in Iran|journal=Middle Eastern Studies|year=2008|volume=44|number=2|doi=10.1080/00263200701874867|page=189–214|quote=In Western sources, the term ‘hard-liners’ is used to refer to the faction under the leadership of Supreme Leader Ali Khamanehi. Members of this group prefer to call themselves ‘''Osul-gara''’. The word ‘''osul''’ means ‘fundamentals’, or ‘principles’ or ‘tenets’. And the suffix ‘''gara''’ means ‘those who uphold or promote’. The more radical elements in the hard-line camp prefer to call themselves ‘''Ommat Hezbollah''’. ‘''Ommat''’ is a technical Arabic-Islamic term referring to people who are Muslim. ‘''Hezbollah''’ literally means ‘Party of Allah’. Before the rise of Ahmadinejad to the presidency in 2005, many official sources in the Islamic Republic referred to this group as ‘''mohafezeh-kar''’ (‘conservative’). Between 1997 and 2006, many Iranians inside Iran used the terms ‘''eqtedar-gara''’ (authoritarian) and ‘''tamamiyat-khah''’ (totalitarian) for what many Western observers have termed ‘hard-liners’. Members of the reformist faction of the fundamentalist oligarchy called the hard-liners ‘''eqtedar-gara''’.}}</ref>，但阵营也包含部分中间倾向的派系 <ref>{{Citation|author=Banafsheh Keynoush|title= Iran after Ahmadinejad|journal=Survival: Global Politics and Strategy|place=New York|year=2012|volume=54|number=3|doi=10.1080/00396338.2012.690988|page=127–146|quote=What is important, however, is that the principlist camp now increasingly represents not just hardliners but also more centre-right factions.}}</ref>。 

在伊朗政治中，“原则”指的是支持[[伊朗最高领袖|伊朗最高领袖]]，并倡导保护伊斯兰革命早期的意识形态。<ref name="Bloomberg">{{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2013-06-15/rohani-clinches-iran-presidency-in-surprise-victory|title=Iranians Celebrate Surprise Rohani Win as Reason for Hope|author1=Ladane Nasseri |author2=Kambiz Foroohar |author3=Yeganeh Salehi |date=2013-06-16|website=|publisher=[[Bloomberg_News|Bloomberg]]|accessdate=2015-03-10}}</ref>[[侯赛因·穆萨维|侯赛因·穆萨维]]指出，伊朗原则主义派构筑了伊朗绝大多数的右翼/保守主义，倾向于宗教主义，且比起改革派，他们与[[库姆|库姆]]的[[伊朗教权主义|教权组织]]有更多联系。<ref>{{citation|title=The Iranian Nuclear Crisis: A Memoir|year=2012|publisher=Brookings Institution Press|author=Seyed Hossein Mousavian|page=486|isbn=9780870033025}}</ref>原则主义派声明，会始终保持对[[伊斯兰教|伊斯兰教]]和伊斯兰革命的忠诚，服从最高领袖的命令和[[法基赫的监护|法基赫的监护]]。<ref name="Tehran Bureau">{{cite web|url=https://www.pbs.org/wgbh/pages/frontline/tehranbureau/2011/09/irans-conservatives-the-headstrong-new-bloc.html|title=Iran's Conservatives: The Headstrong New Bloc|last1=SHAUL |first1=BAKHASH|date=2011-09-12|work=[[Woodrow_Wilson_International_Center_for_Scholars|Woodrow Wilson International Center for Scholars]] |publisher=[[Tehran_Bureau|Tehran Bureau]]|accessdate=2015-03-10}}</ref>该阵营反对国际社会否认伊朗政治体制的现状<ref name="CI"/>，支持伊朗国内政权形式。<ref>{{citation|title=Sanctions, Statecraft, and Nuclear Proliferation|editor=Etel Solingen|date=2012|isbn=9781107010444|publisher=Cambridge University Press|pages=222}}</ref>

目前原则主义派在[[伊朗专家会议|专家会议]]、[[宪法监督委员会|宪法监督委员会]]和司法机构中占据主导权。<ref name="Tehran Bureau"/>

==派系==
*'''极端保守派'''，也被称作'''强硬派'''、'''新保守主义派'''—由[[伊斯兰革命卫队|伊斯兰革命卫队]]等党派组成<ref name="sherill-orbis">{{cite journal |title=After Khamenei: Who Will Succeed Iran's Supreme Leader? |first=Clifton |last=Sherrill |journal=Orbis |volume=55 |issue=4 |pages=631–47 |doi=10.1016/j.orbis.2011.07.002 |year=2011 }}</ref>该派支持伊斯兰政府，对欧美国家具有更强的对抗性。<ref name="sherill-orbis" />代表人物为前伊朗总统[[马哈茂德·艾哈迈迪内贾德|马哈茂德·艾哈迈迪内贾德]]。<ref>{{cite web |title=伊朗：“新保守派”在崛起_网易新闻 |url=http://news.163.com/05/0708/10/1O4Q4EOM00011EOK.html |website=news.163.com |accessdate=2019-03-17}}</ref>

*'''传统保守派'''，伊斯兰革命中协助组建了新政府，与[[鲁霍拉·穆萨维·霍梅尼|鲁霍拉·穆萨维·霍梅尼]]有传统的连接。<ref name="sherill-orbis">{{cite journal |title=After Khamenei: Who Will Succeed Iran's Supreme Leader? |first=Clifton |last=Sherrill |journal=Orbis |volume=55 |issue=4 |pages=631–47 |doi=10.1016/j.orbis.2011.07.002 |year=2011 }}</ref>该派支持伊斯兰政府并倡导文职统治。<ref>{{cite book |title=Mullahs, Guards, and Bonyads: An Exploration of Iranian Leadership Dynamics |authors=Thaler et. al |publisher=[[RAND_Corporation|RAND Corporation]] |location=Sacramento, CA |date=2010 |isbn=978-0-8330-4773-1 }}</ref>

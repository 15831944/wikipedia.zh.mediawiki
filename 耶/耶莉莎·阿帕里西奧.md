'''耶莉莎·阿帕里西奧·马丁内斯'''（{{Lang-es|'''Yalitza Aparicio Martínez'''}}，{{bd|1993年|12月11日|catIdx=Aparicio, Yalitza}}）是一名[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]女演員與教師。她的第一部电影是墨西哥知名导演[[艾方索·柯朗|艾方索·柯朗]]執導的《[[羅馬_(2018年電影)|羅馬]]》（2018）等。阿帕里西奧在電影中扮演一名墨西哥原住民家傭Cleo並因此獲得[[第91屆奧斯卡金像獎|第91屆奧斯卡金像獎]]的[[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|最佳女主角]]提名。<ref>[https://www.reuters.com/article/amp/idUSKCN1QC0TT “Star of Mexican film 'Roma' prompts raw discussion of race, class”]. Reutes.</ref>

== 早年生活 ==
阿帕里西奧來自墨西哥一个单亲[[原住民|原住民]]家庭。父亲是[[米斯特克人|米斯特克人]]，母亲則是[[特里基族|特里基族]]人。在成為演員之前，她是一名學校教師。<ref>[https://www.vanityfair.com/hollywood/2019/02/yalitza-aparicio-oscars-roma-best-actress/amp “No Matter What Happens at the Oscars, Yalitza Aparicio Is Having the Time of Her Life”]. Vanity Fair. 23-02-2019.</ref><ref>[https://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-6725583/amp/Roma-star-Yalitza-Aparicio-lived-ramshackle-home-Mexico.html “EXCLUSIVE: This is the ramshackle hut where Roma star lived and trudged five miles on foot to her teaching job in a poverty-stricken school – before becoming the first-ever Netflix star up for an Oscar”]. Daily Mail. 22-02-2019.</ref>阿帕里西奧參與在本地文化中心舉行的試鏡活動後得到电影《羅馬》的角色。<ref>[https://amp.theguardian.com/world/2019/feb/22/roma-star-yalitza-aparicio-hometown-mexico-success-story-oscars “Yalitza Aparicio: Roma star's hometown wills her on to Oscars glory”]. The Guardian. 22-02-2019.</ref>

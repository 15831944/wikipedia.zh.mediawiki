'''耶特'''（{{lang-en|Yetter}}）是美国[[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[卡爾霍恩縣_(愛阿華州)|卡爾霍恩縣]]的一个城市。名字来源于其建立者L·M·耶特<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=yZAUAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA162#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=Past and Present of Calhoun County, Iowa: A Record of Settlement, Organization, Progress, and Achievement, Volume 1 | publisher=Pioneer Publishing Company | author=Stonebraker, Beaumont E. | year=1915 | pages=162}}</ref>。其坐标为{{coord|42|18|59|N|94|50|38|W|type:city}}<ref name="GR1">{{cite web|url=https://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/gazette.html|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2011-04-23|date=2011-02-12|title=US Gazetteer files: 2010, 2000, and 1990}}</ref>。根据[[美国人口调查局|美国人口调查局]]，城市总面积为{{convert|0.13|sqmi|sqkm|2}}，且皆为陆地<ref name="Gazetteer files">{{cite web|title=US Gazetteer files 2010|url=https://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2012-05-11|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/64vfLAeJ2?url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt|archivedate=2012-01-24}}</ref>。2010年人口普查数据显示，总人口数量为34，共16户，9个家庭，[[人口密度|人口密度]]为{{convert|261.5|PD/sqmi|PD/km2|1}}。种族构成为97.1%的白人及2.9%的亚裔。人口年龄中位数为49.5岁，男女分别占比61.8%与38.2%<ref name ="FactFinder">{{cite web|title=American FactFinder|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/nav/jsf/pages/index.xhtml|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2012-05-11}}</ref>。

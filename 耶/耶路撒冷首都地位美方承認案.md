== 背景 ==
[[File:Jerusalem_Ost-Jerusalem_2.JPG|缩略图]]中占领[[东耶路撒冷|东耶路撒冷]]，1980年吞并该地区<ref>{{cite web |title=East Jerusalem |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/shared/spl/hi/middle_east/03/v3_israel_palestinians/maps/html/1967_and_now.stm |website=BBC News |access-date=2019-01-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181019211120/http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/shared/spl/hi/middle_east/03/v3_israel_palestinians/maps/html/1967_and_now.stm |archive-date=2018-10-19 |dead-url=no }}</ref>]]

1949年以色列国成立后， [[美国|美国]]承认新国家，但希望为耶路撒冷建立国际制度，<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://unispal.un.org/UNISPAL.NSF/0/510EF41FAC855100052566CD00750CA4|title=See General Assembly, A/L.523/Rev.1, 4 July 1967|accessdate=2017-12-06|publisher=|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150514100955/http://unispal.un.org/UNISPAL.NSF/0/510EF41FAC855100052566CD00750CA4|archivedate=2015-05-14}}</ref>通过谈判解决其最终地位。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.haaretz.com/news/u-s-only-israel-palestinians-should-decide-jerusalem-s-future-1.2548|title=U.S.: Only Israel, Palestinians should decide Jerusalem's future|accessdate=2017-12-08|author=Mozgovaya|date=2009-12-08|first=Natasha|work=Haaretz|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171208174827/https://www.haaretz.com/news/u-s-only-israel-palestinians-should-decide-jerusalem-s-future-1.2548|archive-date=2017-12-08|dead-url=no}}</ref>美国在1949年反对以色列宣布耶路撒冷为首都，并反对约旦在1950年计划将耶路撒冷为宣布的第二个首都。<ref name="crsMark">{{Cite web|url=https://fas.org/man/crs/RS20339.pdf|title=Jerusalem: The U.S. Embassy and P.L. 104-45|accessdate=2011-04-01|author=Mark|first=Clyde|work=CRS Report for Congress|publisher=Congressional Research Service. [[1992年美国总统选举|1992年选举总统]]期间，[[比尔·克林顿|比尔·克林顿]]承诺政府会“支持耶路撒冷为以色列国的首都”，批评总统[[乔治·H·W·布什|乔治·H·W·布什]]“多次挑战以色列统一耶路撒冷的主权”。然而，1993年签署《奥斯陆协定》时，克林顿政府并未继续他们的计划，以免扰乱以色列和巴勒斯坦人之间的谈判。<ref name="Never_ending_Story">{{Cite news|first=Amir|last=Tibon|url=https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/1.769498|title=From Bill Clinton to Trump: The Never-ending Story of the Jerusalem Embassy Move|date=2017-02-05|work=Haaretz Daily|access-date=2019-01-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180114122843/https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/1.769498|archive-date=2018-01-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>

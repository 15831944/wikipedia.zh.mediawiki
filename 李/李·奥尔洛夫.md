'''李·奥尔洛夫'''（{{lang-en|Lee Orloff}}）为一位美国[[音讯工程师|音讯工程师]]。他曾获得过1次[[奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖|奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖]]，并6次提名这一奖项。自1984年起他参与了超过60部电影的制作。

==精选影视作品==
奥尔洛夫共赢得过1次奥斯卡奖，并获得过6次提名。

获奖：
* [[终结者2：审判日|终结者2：审判日]] (1991)<ref name="Oscars1992">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1992 |title=The 64th Academy Awards (1992) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-10-22|work=oscars.org}}</ref>

提名：
* [[深渊_(电影)|深渊]] (1989)<ref name="Oscars1990">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1990 |title=The 62nd Academy Awards (1990) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-10-17|work=oscars.org}}</ref>
* {{tsl|en|Geronimo: An American Legend||杰罗尼莫-印第安之鹰}} (1993)<ref name="Oscars1994">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1994 |title=The 66th Academy Awards (1994) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-10-22|work=oscars.org}}</ref>
* {{tsl|en|The Insider (film)||惊曝内幕}} (1999)<ref name="Oscars2000">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/2000 |title=The 72nd Academy Awards (2000) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-11-19|work=oscars.org}}</ref>
* [[爱国者_(电影)|爱国者]] (2000)<ref name="Oscars2001">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/2001 |title=The 73rd Academy Awards (2001) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-11-19|work=oscars.org}}</ref>

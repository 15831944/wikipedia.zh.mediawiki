== 早期生活 ==
李查理出生於[[科特迪瓦|科特迪瓦]]，他於13歲時移居美國並在1995年高中畢業。2000年，他畢業於[[麻省理工学院|麻省理工学院]]並取得[[计算机科学|计算机科学]]的學士及碩士學位。<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.investopedia.com/news/who-charlie-lee-litecoin-founder/|title=Who Is Charlie Lee, Litecoin Founder?|author=Reiff|first=Nathan|date=May 7, 2018|work=Investopedia|accessdate=May 9, 2018|language=en-US}}</ref>

李查理的兄弟李鲍勃（Bobby Lee）是[[加密貨幣交易所|加密貨幣交易所]][[上海萨图西网络有限公司|比特幣中國]]的創辦人。<ref name="hill2013">{{Cite news|author=Hill|first=Kashmir|title=From Walmart To Bitcoin: The CEO Behind The Chinese Exchange Sending BTC To New Highs|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/kashmirhill/2013/11/08/from-walmart-to-bitcoin-the-ceo-behind-the-chinese-exchange-sending-bitcoin-to-new-highs/|work=Forbes|date=2013-11-08|accessdate=2013-11-08}}</ref>

== 职业生涯 ==
2000年起，李查理進入[[Google|谷歌]]公司，展開了長達十數年的谷歌工作生涯。他的工作包括編寫代碼與及[[Chrome_OS|Chrome OS]]的開發。<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://www.wired.com/2013/08/litecoin/|title=Ex-Googler Gives the World a Better Bitcoin|author=Robert|first=McMillan|date=August 30, 2013|work=Wired|accessdate=May 9, 2018|language=en-US}}</ref>

2011年，李查理開始對[[比特幣|比特幣]]產生興趣。<ref name=":3">{{Cite news|url=http://fortune.com/2017/12/12/litecoin-bitcoin-price-2018/|title=What Is Litecoin, and Why Is It Beating Bitcoin This Year?|author=Shen|first=Lucinda|date=December 12, 2017|work=Fortune|accessdate=May 26, 2018|language=en}}</ref> 同年10月，他在[[Bitcointalk|Bitcointalk]]論壇上發表了[[莱特币|莱特币]]。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/kashmirhill/2014/01/13/a-100-worth-of-litecoin-a-year-ago-is-worth-30000-today/|title=A $100 Worth Of Litecoin A Year Ago Is Worth $30,000 Today|author=Hill|first=Kashmir|date=January 13, 2014|work=Forbes|accessdate=May 26, 2018|language=en}}</ref>李查理利用在[[Google|谷歌]]工作期間的空餘時間著手編寫基於比特幣的[[區塊鏈|區塊鏈]]技術。[[莱特币|莱特币]]發佈的時候，公眾已開採的代幣數量只有150枚。李查理表示他並不打算與比特幣競爭，而是打算將[[莱特币|莱特币]]應用於面額較小的交易。<ref name=":3" />

2013年7月，李查理離開[[Google|谷歌]]並進入[[Coinbase|Coinbase]]工作，當時[[加密貨幣交易所|加密貨幣交易所]][[Coinbase|Coinbase]]仍未接納他所創建的[[莱特币|莱特币]]。<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://www.wired.com/2013/08/litecoin/|title=Ex-Googler Gives the World a Better Bitcoin|author=Robert|first=McMillan|date=August 30, 2013|work=Wired|accessdate=May 9, 2018|language=en-US}}</ref>

2017年12月，李查理宣布在[[Reddit|Reddit]]宣佈基由利益沖突的關係，他已幾乎將所有持有的[[莱特币|莱特币]]出售。由於此番言論可能對[[莱特币|莱特币]]造成價格影響，李查理亦受到外界的批評。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2017/12/20/litecoin-charlie-lee-conflict-of-interest/|title=Litecoin founder Charlie Lee has sold all of his LTC – TechCrunch|author=Russell|first=Jon|date=December 20, 2017|work=Techcrunch|language=en-US|accessdate=May 23, 2018}}</ref>李查理將大部份的貪幣出售或捐出，只餘下少量作為收藏的實體貨幣。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2017/12/20/litecoin-founder-charlie-lee-sells-his-holdings-in-the-cryptocurrency.html|title=Litecoin founder Charlie Lee says he’s sold all his holdings in the cryptocurrency|author=Browne|first=Ryan|date=December 20, 2017|work=CNBC|accessdate=May 23, 2018}}</ref>

李查理目前全職在[[莱特币|莱特币]]基金會工作，推廣[[莱特币|莱特币]]的發展與普及。 <ref>{{Cite tweet|user=SatoshiLite|number=1042648826027241472|date=7 February 2015|title=I'm working on Litecoin full-time and focused on Litecoin adoption.}}</ref>

== 言論 ==
2018年5月，李查理在推特質疑區塊鏈平台[[EOS.IO|EOS.IO]]在[[首次代幣發行|首次代幣發行]]中籌集了鉅額資金，卻只願撥出極小部份作為除錯獎勵。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Raises $4B, offers to pay only $10k for catastrophic bugs 2 days before mainnet launch.|url=https://twitter.com/SatoshiLite/status/1001650165759991808|work=@SatoshiLite|date=2018-05-29|accessdate=2019-02-15|language=en|first=Charlie|last=Lee [LTC ⚡|author=|publisher=}}</ref>

2018年7月，有報導指出分散式交易所Bancor遭受黑客攻擊，在資產被凍結前損失為1350萬美元。李查理在推特發文指出，如果一家交易所可以令用戶損失資產或有權凍結用戶資產，那麼該交易所便不可能是分散式的。<ref>{{Cite web|title=The crypto world’s latest hack sees Bancor lose $23.5M|url=http://social.techcrunch.com/2018/07/10/bancor-loses-23-5m/|work=TechCrunch|accessdate=2019-02-15|language=en-US}}</ref>

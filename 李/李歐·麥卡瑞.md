'''李歐·麥卡瑞'''（Leo McCarey）[[美國|美國]]电影[[導演|導演]]，获得两次[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|奥斯卡最佳导演奖]]和一次金球奖最佳导演奖，死於[[肺氣腫|肺氣腫]]<ref name=obit>{{cite news |author= |coauthors= |title=Leo McCarey, Director, is Dead. Won Oscars for Going My Way. Was Also a Winner in 1937 of Academy Award for 'The Awful Truth' |url=http://query.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=9B06E5DF1739EF3BBC4E53DFB1668382679EDE |quote= |newspaper=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |date=July 6, 1969  |accessdate=2014-12-10 }}</ref>。

==生平==
李歐·麥卡瑞出生在[[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]，畢業於聖約瑟夫天主教學校、洛杉磯高中。李歐·麥卡瑞與哈羅德·勞埃德曾製作一部拳擊喜劇《銀河》（1936）。

李歐·麥卡瑞從[[南加州大學|南加州大學]]法學院畢業<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.biography.com/articles/Leo-McCarey-37937|title=Leo McCarey Biography|publisher=Biography.com|accessdate=May 10, 2010}}</ref>，曾擔任礦物開採員，拳擊和作曲<ref name=hfa>{{Cite web |url=http://hcl.harvard.edu/hfa/films/2008mayjune/mccarey.html |title="Leo McCarey", Harvard Film Archive |access-date=2014-12-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203005831/http://hcl.harvard.edu/hfa/films/2008mayjune/mccarey.html |archive-date=2013-12-03 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>李歐·麥卡瑞於1919年成為托德·勃朗寧助理導演<ref name=harrit>[http://sensesofcinema.com/2002/great-directors/mccarey/ Harrit, Paul. "Leo McCarey", ''Great Directors'', Issue 23, Senses of Cinema]</ref>。

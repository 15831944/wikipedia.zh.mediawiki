'''柴納'''（{{lang-en|China}}）為美國[[密蘇里州|密蘇里州]][[豪厄爾縣_(密蘇里州)|豪厄爾縣]]之[[非建制地區|非建制地區]]<ref>{{GNIS|735143}}</ref>。

==歷史==
一間名為「柴納」的郵政局於1892年開業，後於1897年裁撤<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?task=display&state=MO&county=Howell |title=Post Offices |publisher=Jim Forte Postal History |accessdate=2016-10-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150410073413/http://www.postalhistory.com/postoffices.asp?task=display&state=MO&county=Howell |archivedate=2015-04-10 |df= }}</ref>。此地區以當地同名教堂為名<ref>{{cite web|url=http://shsmo.org/manuscripts/ramsay/ramsay_howell.html |title=Howell County Place Names, 1928–1945 (archived) |publisher=The State Historical Society of Missouri |accessdate=2016-10-10 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160624071834/http://shsmo.org/manuscripts/ramsay/ramsay_howell.html |archivedate=2016-06-24 |df= }}</ref>。

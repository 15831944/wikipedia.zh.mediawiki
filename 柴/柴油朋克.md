'''柴油朋克'''（{{lang-en|Dieselpunk}}）是一种类似[[蒸汽朋克|蒸汽朋克]]的幻想题材，结合了[[战间期|战间期]]至20世纪50年代的柴油机技术、[[复古未来主义|复古未来主义]]技术<ref name="dailydot">{{cite web|url=http://www.dailydot.com/fandom/dieselpunk-steampunk-beginners-guide/|title=Dieselpunk for beginners: Welcome to a world where the '40s never ended|author=Aja Romano|date=2013-10-08|publisher=[[The_Daily_Dot|The Daily Dot]]|accessdate=2017-12-09}}</ref><ref name="Discovering Dieselpunk p3">{{Citation | url=http://www.ottens.co.uk/gatehouse/Gazette%20-%201.pdf|title=Discovering Dieselpunk | last='Piecraft' |first2= Nick |last2=Ottens| journal = The Gatehouse Gazette|issue =Issue 1|date = 2008-07 |page =3|accessdate=2017-12-09}}</ref>和[[后现代主义|后现代主义]]。<ref name="What’s in a Name?">{{cite web|url=http://www.ottens.co.uk/gatehouse/2013/10/whats-in-a-name/|title=What’s in a Name?|author=Larry Amyett|date=2013-10-24|publisher=The Gatehouse|accessdate=2014-12-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150626125851/http://www.ottens.co.uk/gatehouse/2013/10/whats-in-a-name/|archive-date=2015-06-26|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

柴油朋克也衍生出相关的电影、音乐、视觉艺术、小说、电子游戏。

==起源==
“柴油朋克”一词是20世纪80年代的[[科學幻想|科学幻想]]体裁[[赛博朋克|赛博朋克]]的衍生物，<ref name="SciFi Wire">{{cite web|author=Dellamonica, A.M. |title=Cyberpunk, steampunk and now stitchpunk? Your guide to 11 sci-fi punks |url=http://scifiwire.com/2009/09/guide-to-sci-fi-punks.php |date=2009-09-08 |accessdate=2017-12-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091003195055/http://scifiwire.com/2009/09/guide-to-sci-fi-punks.php |archivedate=2009-10-03 |df= }}</ref>柴油朋克多以柴油机为西方文化焦点的[[战间期|战间期]]至20世纪50年代为背景。<ref name="Diesel History">{{cite web|author=DW Productions|title=A History of the Diesel Engine|url=http://www.goadvice.com/GreenDCoop/DieselHistory.htm|accessdate=2017-12-09|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/65WHB3TxU?url=http://www.goadvice.com/GreenDCoop/DieselHistory.htm|archivedate=2012-02-17}}</ref>

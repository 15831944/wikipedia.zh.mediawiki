'''姬馬陸目'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''Julida'''}}）是[[倍足綱|倍足綱]][[馬陸總目|馬陸總目]]的生物。本目的物種均為長及圓柱形，長度由{{convert|10|到|120|毫米}}不等<ref>{{cite web|title=Diagnostic features of Millipede Orders|url=http://fieldmuseum.org/sites/default/files/Identification_Table_1.pdf|work=Milli-PEET Identification Tables|publisher=The Field Museum, Chicago|accessdate=25 October 2013}}</ref>。

有眼睛，但不一定存在；性成熟的雄性物種，其第一對足異化成為鉤裝<ref name="Blower 1985">{{cite journal|author=J. Gordon Blower|title=Millipedes: Keys and Notes for the Identification of the Species|publisher=[[Linnaean_Society_of_London|Linnaean Society of London]]|year=1985|isbn=90-04-07698-0}}</ref>。
Additionally, both pairs of legs on the 7th body segment of males are modified into [[gonopod_(millipede)|gonopods]].<ref name="Shelley 1999">{{cite journal|last=Shelley|first=Rowland M.|title=Centipedes and Millipedes with Emphasis on North American Fauna|journal=The Kansas School Naturalist|year=1999|volume=45|issue=3|pages=1–16|url=http://www.emporia.edu/ksn/v45n3-march1999/}}</ref>

==分佈==
本目物種主要生活於[[溫帶|溫帶]]地區，從[[北美洲|北美洲]]到[[中美洲|中美洲]]的[[巴拿馬|巴拿馬]]，亦有分佈於[[歐洲|歐洲]]、[[亞洲|亞洲]]的[[喜馬拉亞山脈|喜馬拉亞山脈]]以北及[[東南亞|東南亞]]等地<ref>{{cite web|title=Geographic distribution of Millipede Families|url=http://fieldmuseum.org/sites/default/files/Identification_Table_3.pdf|work=Milli-PEET Identification Tables|publisher=The Field Museum, Chicago|accessdate=25 October 2013}}</ref>。

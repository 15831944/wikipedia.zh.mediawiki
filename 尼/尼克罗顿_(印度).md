'''尼克罗顿 (印度)''' ({{lang-en|'''Nickelodeon TV (India)'''}})是印度的尼克罗顿电视台，由[[維亞康姆|维亚康姆]]和{{tsl|en|TV18||TV18}}的合资公司{{tsl|en|Viacom 18||Viacom 18}}创办，尼克罗顿在印度有四种语言版本：[[印地语|印地语]], [[泰米尔语|泰卢固语]], [[泰卢固语|泰米尔语]]和[[卡纳达语|卡纳达语]]。
==历史==
印度的尼克罗顿电视台最初由[[Zee_TV|Zee TV]]于1999年10月16日推出<ref name="Nick">{{cite web|url=http://www.indiantelevision.com/news_analysis/newsletter/111099/zee111099.htm|title=ZEE TV TO LAUNCH NICKELODEON|date=1999-10-11|accessdate=2017-06-21}}</ref>。

[[Zee_TV|Zee TV]]推出了Nickelodeon品牌的编程模块，作为[[維亞康姆|维亚康姆]]国际与{{tsl|en|Zee Entertainment Enterprises||Zee Entertainment Enterprises}}之间分销协议的一部分。它在2002 年被{{tsl|en|Cartoon Network India||Cartoon Network}}分部取代<ref name=Cartoon>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiantelevision.com/y2k2/aug/aug87.htm|title=Cartoon Network block replaces Nick on Zee TV|date=2002-08-14|accessdate=2017-09-19}}</ref>。

2004年，[[維亞康姆|维亚康姆]]在印度改造了Nickelodeon，以增加其收视率，包括将其品牌化为尼克，本地化和增加印地语。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.animationxpress.com/index.php/interviews/nickelodeon-india-business-and-operations-head-pradeep-hejmadi|title=Nickelodeon India business and operations head Pradeep Hejmadi  - AnimationXpressAnimationXpress|website=www.animationxpress.com|language=en-US|access-date=2018-07-20}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=http://www.indiantelevision.com/headlines/y2k4/may/may182.htm|title=Nick to get 'MTV India treatment' for makeover|date=2004-05-22|work=Indian Television Dot Com|access-date=2018-07-20|language=en}}</ref>



[[維亞康姆|维亚康姆]]于2007年与{{tsl|en|Sun Network||Sun Network}}签署了一项播出协议，根据该协议，Nickelodeon将在{{tsl|en|Chutti TV||Chutti TV}}中推出[[泰米尔语|泰米尔语]]和[[泰卢固语|泰卢固语]]版本。当Nickelodeon决定将泰米尔语和泰卢固语语言添加到他们自己的频道后，这项协议被取消。<ref name=Tar>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiantelevision.com/headlines/y2k9/dec/dec197.php|title=Sun's Chutti TV targets 25% ad growth in 2010|date=2009-12-19|accessdate=2018-01-09}}</ref>

2010年6月25日，尼克印度公司使用现有标识重新命名。这是进行这次改造的最后一个主要市场。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/industry/media/entertainment/media/nick-india-undergoes-makeover-to-don-new-logo-from-june-25/articleshow/6087665.cms|title=Nick India undergoes makeover, to don new logo from June 25|date=2010-06-24|work=The Economic Times|access-date=2018-03-03}}</ref>

2011年，[[維亞康姆|维亚康姆]]18推出了一个名为{{tsl|en|Nickelodeon Sonic||Sonic}}的新频道<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.campaignindia.in/article/viacom18-to-launch-new-channel-sonic-for-new-young-adults/415351|title=Viacom18 to launch new channel Sonic for "new young adults"|work=Campaign India|access-date=2017-11-01}}</ref>。最初，频道专注于行动和冒险，然后在2016年将焦点转移到喜剧<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.animationxpress.com/index.php/latest-news/nick-rebrands-their-kids-channel-sonic-with-refreshing-new-logo-and-their-successful-show-shiva|title=Nick rebrands their kids channel Sonic with refreshing new logo and their successful show 'Shiva' - AnimationXpressAnimationXpress|website=www.animationxpress.com|language=en-US|access-date=2017-11-01}}</ref>。

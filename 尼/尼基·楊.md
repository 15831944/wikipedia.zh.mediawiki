'''尼基·楊'''，出生於南韓首爾，是[[大韩民国|南韓]][[動畫師|動畫師]]。畢業於[[弘益大學|弘益大學]]及[[加州藝術學院|加州藝術學院]]，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nikiyang.com/resume.htm|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20041009091743/http://www.nikiyang.com/resume.htm|archivedate=2004-10-09|title=Resume|accessdate=2011-06-17}}</ref>在轉至[[Frederator_Studios|Frederator Studios]]前曾擔任《[[蓋酷家庭|蓋酷家庭]]》的故事編劇之一<ref name="Random Thoughts">{{cite web|url=http://www.awn.com/articles/people/random-thoughts-frederator-cartoonists/page/3,1|title=Random Thoughts from Frederator Cartoonists|author=Farago, Andrew|date=2009-01-29|work=Animation World Network|accessdate=2011-07-29|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120626040621/http://www.awn.com/articles/people/random-thoughts-frederator-cartoonists/page/3%2C1|archivedate=2012-06-26}}</ref>。她因在《[[探險時光|探險時光]]》動畫影集中聲演彩虹姐姐而廣為人知。楊在該影集中只說韓語。

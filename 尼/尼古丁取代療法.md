'''尼古丁取代療法'''（{{lang-en|Nicotine replacement therapy}}，簡稱：NRT）是一種治療方式，目的是用[[尼古丁|尼古丁]]漸進式地取代[[香煙|香菸]]。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Pharmacological intervention of nicotine dependence.|first1=R|last2=Majumder|first2=P|date=2013|journal=BioMed research international|volume=2013|pages=278392|pmid=24490153|last3=Gupta|first3=T|last1=Jain}}</ref> 用於幫助人們戒菸或嚼食菸草。<ref name="AHFS2015" /><ref>{{cite journal|title=Clinical inquiries. What interventions can help patients stop using chewing tobacco?|first1=KD|last2=Scott|first2=MA|date=April 2005|journal=The Journal of family practice|issue=4|volume=54|pages=368-9|pmid=15833231|last3=Ketterman|first3=E|last4=Smith|first4=PO|last1=Smith}}</ref> 使用尼古丁取代療法可以增加50%到70%的戒菸成功率。<ref name="Stead2012">{{cite journal|title=Nicotine replacement therapy for smoking cessation|last=Stead|first=LF|date=2012-11-14|journal=The Cochrane database of systematic reviews|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD000146.pub4|volume=11|pages=CD000146|pmid=23152200|author3=Bullen, C|author4=Mant, D|author5=Hartmann-Boyce, J|author6=Cahill, K|author7=Lancaster, T|author2=Perera, R|editor1-last=Stead|editor1-first=Lindsay F}}</ref> 通常還會配合行為治療法使用。<!-- <ref name=AHFS2015/> --> 尼古丁取代療法除了協助戒菸外，也用於治療[[溃疡性结肠炎|潰瘍性結腸炎]]。<!-- <ref name=AHFS2015/> -->尼古丁取代療法有許多形式：包括尼古丁貼片、口香糖、喉錠、鼻噴劑以及吸入劑型。<ref name="AHFS2015">{{cite web|url=http://www.drugs.com/monograph/nicotine.html|title=Nicotine|accessdate=Dec 2015|publisher=The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists}}</ref> 同時使用不同方式的尼古丁取代療法，也許能增加療效。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Update on medicines for smoking cessation.|first1=M|date=August 2015|journal=Australian prescriber|issue=4|volume=38|pages=106-11|pmid=26648633|quote=Evidence suggests that combinations of nicotine replacement therapy may be more effective than using a single formulation|last1=McDonough}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=Pharmacological interventions for smoking cessation: an overview and network meta-analysis.|first1=K|last2=Stevens|first2=S|date=2013-05-31|journal=The Cochrane database of systematic reviews|volume=5|pages=CD009329|pmid=23728690|quote=Combination NRT also outperformed single formulations|last3=Perera|first3=R|last4=Lancaster|first4=T|last1=Cahill}}</ref>

<!-- Side effects -->
成癮是常見的副作用之一。<!-- <ref name=AHFS2015/> --> 嚼食尼古丁口香糖的常見副作用是噁心、打嗝和口中的刺激感；使用尼古丁貼片則容易造成皮膚刺激和口乾舌燥；使用吸入劑型則會造成咳嗽、流鼻水或頭痛。<!-- <ref name=AHFS2015/> -->較嚴重的副作用包括尼古丁中毒和無法戒斷。<ref name="AHFS2015" /> 目前認為並不會增加心肌梗塞的機率。<ref name="Stead2012" /> 如果在懷孕期間使用，可能對胎兒造成傷害。<ref name="AHFS2015" /><ref>{{cite journal|title=Is it safe to use smoking cessation therapeutics during pregnancy?|first1=NE|last2=Barra|first2=NG|date=December 2014|journal=Expert opinion on drug safety|issue=12|volume=13|pages=1721-31|pmid=25330815|last3=Hardy|first3=DB|last4=Holloway|first4=AC|last1=De Long}}</ref> 尼古丁取代療法的原理是藉由降低對於尼古丁的渴求來戒除菸癮。<ref name="AHFS2015" /><ref>{{cite journal|title=Nicotine Replacement Therapy for Smoking Cessation or Reduction: A Review of the Clinical Evidence|date=2014-01-16|publisher=Canadian Agency for Drugs and Technologies in Health|pmid=24741730}}</ref>

<!-- History, society, and culture -->
尼古丁取代療法於1984年在美國被核准使用。<ref name="AHFS2015" /> 尼古丁取代療法是[[世界衛生組織基本藥物標準清單|世界衛生組織基本藥物標準清單]]中的一部份，是基層健康衛生系統中非常重要的藥物。<ref name="World Health Organization">{{cite web|url=http://apps.who.int/iris/bitstream/10665/93142/1/EML_18_eng.pdf?ua=1|title=WHO Model List of EssentialMedicines|accessdate=2014-04-22|date=October 2013|work=World Health Organization}}</ref> 目前已有許多學名藥物 。<ref name="AHFS2015" /> 在美國，使用貼片或尼古丁口香糖，每個月需要花費100到200美金，其他型態的尼古丁取代療法，則相對更為昂貴。<ref name="Ric2015">{{cite book|title=Tarascon Pocket Pharmacopoeia 2015 Deluxe Lab-Coat Edition|first1=Richart|date=2015|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|isbn=9781284057560|page=441|last1=Hamilton}}</ref>

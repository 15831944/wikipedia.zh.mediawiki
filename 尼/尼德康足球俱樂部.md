'''尼德康足球俱樂部'''（'''Football Club Progrès Niedercorn'''）是[[盧森堡|盧森堡]]的一家足球俱樂部。主場位於[[尼德康|尼德康]]。球隊參加[[盧森堡國家足球聯賽|盧森堡國家足球聯賽]]的賽事。

==歐洲賽事==
The club has only won one tie in European competition, (2-1 aggregate) against [[流浪者足球俱乐部|Rangers FC]] in the 2017-18 Europa League First Qualifying Round. Before then, they only managed two draws and one goal; the first was against [[格伦杜兰足球俱乐部|Glentoran]] in the [[1981–82年歐洲冠軍盃|1981–82]] [[欧洲冠军联赛|European Cup]], where they drew 1–1; the second was against [[沙姆洛克流浪足球俱乐部|Shamrock Rovers]] in the [[2015–16年歐霸盃|2015–16 UEFA Europa League]].

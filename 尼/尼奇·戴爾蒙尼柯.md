'''尼可拉斯·約翰·戴爾蒙尼柯'''（{{lang-en|Nicholas John Delmonico}}, {{bd|1992年|7月12日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟芝加哥白襪的外野手及一壘手。父親是大聯盟教練Rod Delmonico。
==職業生涯==
2011年大聯盟選秀，金鶯隊在第6輪選進戴爾蒙尼柯。2013年7月7日，金鶯將他交易至釀酒人隊，換來[[法蘭西斯科·羅德里奎茲|法蘭西斯科·羅德里奎茲]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20130723&content_id=54506100&vkey=news_mlb&c_id=mlb |title=The Orioles acquired reliever Francisco Rodriguez from the Brewers | MLB.com: News |publisher=Mlb.mlb.com |date=2013-07-23 |accessdate=2013-12-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.brewcrewball.com/2013/7/23/4551028/francisco-rodriguez-traded-to-the-baltimore-orioles-for-inf-nick |title=Francisco Rodriguez traded to the Baltimore Orioles for INF Nick Delmonico |publisher=Brew Crew Ball |date= |accessdate=2013-12-14}}</ref>

2014年在高階1A時，因為對[[阿得拉爾|阿得拉爾]]藥物呈陽性反應，被禁賽50場比賽。但是他患有[[注意力不足過動症|注意力不足過動症]]，因此需要使用此藥物來控制病情。<ref name=addiction>{{cite web|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/columnists/ct-nicky-delmonico-haugh-spt-0823-20170822-column.html|title=White Sox rookie sensation Nicky Delmonico beat addiction on way to majors|first=David|last=Haugh|work=Chicago Tribune|date=2017-08-22|accessdate=2017-08-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Brewers minor leaguer gets 50-game drug suspension|url=http://sports.espn.go.com/espn/wire?section=mlb&id=11277870|accessdate=2014-07-29|agency=Associated Press|publisher=ESPN.com|date=2014-07-28}}</ref>後來他成功與球隊溝通，球隊也同意他使用此藥，但是必須在非比賽的狀態下才可服用。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.csnchicago.com/chicago-white-sox/nick-delmonico-takes-advantage-fresh-start-white-sox|title=Nick Delmonico takes advantage of fresh start with White Sox|date=2017-03-26|publisher=|accessdate=2017-08-01}}</ref>

2015年2月，釀酒人隊將他釋出，而他隨後與白襪隊簽約。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.csnchicago.com/white-sox/white-sox-hope-nick-delmonico-has-learned-mistakes|title=White Sox hope Nick Delmonico has learned from mistakes|work=CSN Chicago|accessdate=2015-05-22|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150521015104/http://www.csnchicago.com/white-sox/white-sox-hope-nick-delmonico-has-learned-mistakes|archivedate=2015-05-21}}</ref>2015年從2A開季。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.al.com/sports/index.ssf/2015/07/birmingham_barons_3b_nicky_del.html|title=Birmingham Barons 3B Nicky Delmonico missed the grind|website=al.com|accessdate=2017-08-05}}</ref>2016年升上3A。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.charlotteobserver.com/sports/mlb/charlotte-knights/article83766602.html|title=Charlotte Knights’ Nicky Delmonico holding onto big-league dreams|publisher=|accessdate=2017-08-01}}</ref>

2017年8月1日，戴爾蒙尼柯登上大聯盟。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/baseball/whitesox/ct-white-sox-blue-jays-spt-0802-20170801-story.html|title=Matt Davidson exits with bruised wrist in White Sox' 8-4 loss|first=Colleen|last=Kane|website=chicagotribune.com|accessdate=2017-08-05}}</ref>初登板就敲出大聯盟首安。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.knoxnews.com/story/sports/local/2017/08/02/former-farragut-star-gets-hit-mlb-debut/532629001/|title=Former Farragut star gets hit in MLB debut|website=knoxnews.com|accessdate=2017-08-05}}</ref>8月3日，他面對紅襪隊投手[[瑞克·波瑟羅|瑞克·波瑟羅]]，擊出大聯盟首轟。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/baseball/whitesox/ct-nicky-delmonico-first-home-run-fenway-park-20170804-story.html|title=Nicky Delmonico's first Fenway experience as player includes 1st career home run|first=Colleen|last=Kane|website=chicagotribune.com|accessdate=2017-08-05}}</ref>戴爾蒙尼柯在他大聯盟生涯頭13場比賽都有上壘記錄，頭19場比賽更是敲出6轟。

9月27日，他面對天使隊擊出再見2分全壘打。這支全壘打也間接把雙城隊送入季後賽。

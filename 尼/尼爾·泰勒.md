'''尼爾·約翰·泰勒'''（{{lang-en|Neil John Taylor}}，{{bd|1989年|2月7日|catidx=Taylor, Neil}}），是一名[[威爾士|威爾士]]足球運動員，司職[[後衞_(足球)|左後衞]]。於[[威爾士|威爾士]][[聖亞薩|聖亞薩]]出世，現效力[[英格蘭足球冠軍聯賽|英冠]]球隊[[阿士東維拉足球會|阿士東維拉]]，並為[[威爾斯足球代表隊|威爾斯]]代表。

作為一名[[曼城|曼城]]青訓，他於2007年在[[雷克斯汉姆足球俱乐部|雷克斯漢姆]]展開其職業生涯並在2010年以15萬鎊加盟史雲斯，並在綽號「天鵝」的史雲斯上陣超過150場。母親是一名印度移民，他是在英國職業足球中相對小數的[[亞裔英國人|亞裔英國人]]之一。<ref name="mirror.co.uk"/>

泰勒在2010年開始為[[威爾斯足球代表隊|威爾斯]]出戰國際賽事，他目前已經累積超過30頂喼帽，也獲選為[[2016年歐洲足球錦標賽|2016年歐洲足球錦標賽]]23人名單之一。他也在[[2012年夏季奧林匹克運動會男子足球比賽|2012年夏季奧林匹克運動會]]代表[[英国奥林匹克足球队|英國]]出賽。

==俱樂部生涯==
===雷克斯漢姆===
Taylor began his career at [[Manchester_City_F.C.|Manchester City]], but left the club at the age of 15.<ref name=Taylorswans>{{cite web instead moved to [[Wrexham_F.C.|Wrexham]] at the age of 16, progressing through the youth system at the club,<ref name="profile">{{cite web|url=http://www.wrexhamafc.premiumtv.co.uk/page/ProfilesDetail/0,,10311~41477,00.html|title=Neil Taylor|publisher=Wrexham F.C|accessdate=2008-06-07|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080515203439/http://www.wrexhamafc.premiumtv.co.uk/page/ProfilesDetail/0%2C%2C10311~41477%2C00.html|archivedate=2008-05-15}}</ref> professional debut on 28 August 2007 in the second round of the [[Football_League_Cup|League Cup]], as a 79th-minute substitute for [[Eifion_Williams|Eifion Williams]] in a 0–5 home loss to [[Aston_Villa|Aston Villa]] at the [[Racecourse_Ground|Racecourse Ground]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Wrexham 0-5 Aston Villa|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/league_cup/6963522.stm|accessdate=14 November 2015|publisher=BBC Sport|date=28 August 2007}}</ref> On 22 September, he made his league debut, sign new deals|date=13 March 2008|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=2008-06-07}}</ref> Wrexham finished the season with relegation out of [[The_Football_League|The Football League]].

2008年10月7日， Taylor scored his first career goal, in a 3–1 home win over [[York_City|York City]] in the [[Conference_Premier|Conference Premier]], a match in which he was [[Captain_(association_football)|captain]],<ref>{{cite news|title=Wrexham 3-1 York|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_conf/7646274.stm|accessdate=14 November 2015|date=7 October 2008}}</ref> and followed it up with another in a 5–0 rout of [[Eastbourne_Borough|Eastbourne Borough]] on 20 December.<ref>{{cite news|title=Wrexham 5-0 Eastbourne|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_conf/7779494.stm|accessdate=14 November 2015|publisher=BBC Sport|date=20 December 2008}}</ref> His only other Wrexham goal came in his last appearance on 10 April 2010, volleying to conclude a 2–0 win at relegated [[Grays_Athletic|Grays Athletic]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Grays 0-2 Wrexham|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/eng_conf/8605364.stm|accessdate=14 November 2015|publisher=BBC Sport|date=10 April 2010}}</ref>

===斯旺西===
2010年6月30日，剛約滿[[雷克斯汉姆足球俱乐部|域斯咸]]的21歲[[威爾斯足球代表隊|威爾斯足球代表隊]]代表泰萊加盟另一支[[威爾士|威爾士]]球隊[[英冠|英冠]]的[[斯旺西城足球俱乐部|斯旺西]]，合約為期三年<ref>{{cite news |language=en |url=http://www.givemesport.com/130231-swans-complete-taylor-signing |title=Swans complete Taylor signing  |publisher=''Givemesport'' |date=2010-06-30 |accessdate=2013-11-04}}</ref>。但由於球員轉會年齡於24歲以下，就算約滿轉會，青訓球會仍有權向新球會索取賠償金，兩間球會仍商討中。

2010年9月30日，[[斯旺西城足球俱乐部|斯旺西]]與[[雷克斯汉姆足球俱乐部|域斯咸]]達成協議，[[斯旺西城足球俱乐部|斯旺西]]會支付15萬[[英鎊|英鎊]]賠償金給[[雷克斯汉姆足球俱乐部|域斯咸]]，另加如日後[[斯旺西城足球俱乐部|斯旺西]]出售泰萊中所得到轉會費盈利10%<ref>{{cite news |language=en |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/football/teams/s/swansea_city/9049463.stm |title=Swansea agree Neil Taylor compensation |publisher=''[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]'' |date=2010-09-30 |accessdate=2013-11-04 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120714190517/http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/football/teams/s/swansea_city/9049463.stm |archivedate=2012-07-14 }}</ref>。

2011年6月7日，[[斯旺西城足球俱乐部|斯旺西]]證實收到[[纽卡斯尔联足球俱乐部|紐卡素]]報價提出收購泰萊<ref>{{cite news |language=en |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/13678229 |title=Newcastle make bid for Swansea left-back Neil Taylor  |publisher=''[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]'' |date=2011-06-07 |accessdate=2013-11-04}}</ref>。

2011年7月14日，泰萊與[[斯旺西城足球俱乐部|斯旺西]]簽署一份新的四年合約<ref>{{cite news |language=en |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/14161511 |title=Neil Taylor signs new Swansea City contract  |publisher=''[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]'' |date=2011-07-14 |accessdate=2013-11-04}}</ref>。

[[File:Robin_Van_Persie_Nathan_Dyer_&_Neil_Taylor_2011.jpg|thumb]]在2011年對陣阿仙奴的比賽聯合對[[罗宾·范佩西|罗宾·范佩西]]進行攔截]]
Taylor suffered a broken ankle in the game against [[Sunderland_A.F.C.|Sunderland]] on 1 September 2012 after falling awkwardly during a challenge on [[Craig_Gardner|Craig Gardner]] in the early stages of the game and was ruled out until the end of the season.<ref name=Goal.com>{{cite web |url=http://www.goal.com/en/news/9/england/2012/09/02/3348975/swansea-defender-taylor-out-for-rest-of-the-season-with?source=breakingnews |title=Swansea defender Taylor out for rest of the season with broken ankle |publisher=Goal.com/en |accessdate=2012-09-02}}</ref> On 14 December 2012, Taylor signed a new three-and-a-half-year contract with Swansea, lasting until 2016.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/20724026 |title=Neil Taylor signs fresh Swansea deal |publisher=BBC Sport |date=14 December 2012}}</ref>

In late February, Taylor returned to full training after six months out with injury.<ref>{{cite news|title=Manager Michael Laudrup sets Swansea Premier League target|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/21622045|publisher=BBC Sport|date=1 March 2013|accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref> Shortly before his return, Swansea City won the [[2013_Football_League_Cup_Final|League Cup Final]] following a 5–0 win over League Two side [[Bradford_City_A.F.C|Bradford City]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Bradford City 0–5 Swansea City|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/21489673|publisher=BBC Sport|date=24 February 2013|accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref> In late April, he played 80 minutes for [[Swansea_City_A.F.C._Reserves|Swansea City U21s]] as part of his rehabilitation.<ref>{{cite news|title=Michael Laudrup says Neil Taylor is close to first-team return|url=http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11095/8617190/|publisher=Sky Sports|date=4 April 2013|accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref> Taylor made his first senior appearance since his injury as a substitute for [[Ben_Davies_(footballer_born_1993)|Ben Davies]] in a 2–0 loss against [[Chelsea_F.C.|Chelsea]] on 28 April 2013.<ref>{{cite news|title=Chelsea 2–0 Swansea|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/22237295|publisher=BBC Sport|date=28 April 2013|accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref> After the match, he expressed relief at his return to the first team.<ref>{{cite news|title=Swansea City star Neil Taylor relieved to finally end eight-month injury nightmare|url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/swansea-city-star-neil-taylor-3185298|publisher=Wales Online|date=29 April 2013|accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref>

After Taylor's return, Swansea manager [[Michael_Laudrup|Michael Laudrup]] described the battle for the left-back slot between Taylor and Davies as “a beautiful problem for a manager to have”.<ref>{{cite news|title=Swansea City rivalry between Neil Taylor and Ben Davies 'a beautiful problem'|url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/swansea-city-rivalry-between-neil-4007511|publisher=Wales Online|date=24 May 2013|accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref> Following the transfer of Davies to [[Tottenham_Hotspur_F.C.|Tottenham Hotspur]] in 2014, Taylor reestablished himself as first choice left-back for Swansea. Taylor signed a new four year contract in June 2015, tying him to the club until 2019.<ref name ="Taylor Contract">{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/32864842 |title=Neil Taylor signs new contract with Swansea City |publisher=BBC Sport |date=1 June 2015}}</ref>

==國家隊生涯==

===威爾斯===
[[File:Neil_Taylor_Wales.jpg|thumb]]
Taylor was also eligible for [[India_national_football_team|India]] through his mother.<ref name="mirror.co.uk">{{cite web|url=http://www.mirror.co.uk/sport/football/news/swanseas-neil-taylor-still-only-6827657|title=Why is Swansea's Neil Taylor the only British Asian in the Premier League?|date=13 November 2015|work=Daily Mirror}}</ref> He made his debut for [[Wales_national_football_team|Wales]] on 23 May 2010 in a friendly against [[Croatia_national_football_team|Croatia]] at the [[Stadion_Gradski_vrt|Stadion Gradski vrt]], replacing [[Andy_Dorman|Andy Dorman]] for the final 23 minutes of the 2–0 loss; he and [[Mark_Bradley|Mark Bradley]] had been promoted from the under-21 team in an emergency.<ref>{{cite news|title=Croatia 2-0 Wales|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/league_of_wales/8678876.stm|accessdate=26 June 2016|publisher=BBC Sport|date=23 May 2010}}</ref> In May 2011, he played two games at the [[2011_Nations_Cup|Nations Cup]] in Dublin.<ref>{{cite news|title=Wales 2-0 N Ireland|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/13540749|accessdate=26 June 2016|publisher=BBC Sport|date=27 May 2011}}</ref>

On 9 September 2014, in Wales' first match of [[UEFA_Euro_2016_qualification|UEFA Euro 2016 qualification]], he gave away a penalty to [[Andorra_national_football_team|Andorra]] in the fifth minute, converted by [[Cristian_Martínez_(Andorran_footballer)|Cristian Martínez]], but the Welsh fought back for a 2–1 victory.<ref>{{cite news|title=Gareth Bale double rescues feeble Wales from embarrassment in Andorra|url=http://www.theguardian.com/football/2014/sep/09/andorra-wales-euro-2016-qualifier-match-report|accessdate=10 September 2014|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|agency=Press Association|date=9 September 2014}}</ref>

===英國===
[[Stuart_Pearce|Stuart Pearce]] named Taylor in his 18-man squad for the [[2012_Summer_Olympics|2012 Summer Olympics]] in [[London|London]] as one of three [[Swansea_City_A.F.C.|Swansea City]] players.<ref>{en}}{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/olympics/football/9369976/London-2012-Olympics-Stuart-Pearce-names-Team-GB-football-squad.html |title=London 2012 Olympics: Stuart Pearce names Team GB football squad |publisher=Telegraph |date= |accessdate=2012-08-18}}</ref> He played his first game for [[Great_Britain_Olympic_football_team|Great Britain]] in a friendly against [[Brazil_national_under-23_football_team|Brazil]] at the [[Riverside_Stadium|Riverside Stadium]] on 20 July.<ref>{en}}{{cite web|last=Fletcher |first=Paul |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/olympics/18929315 |title=BBC Sport – Team GB suffer defeat by Brazil |publisher=Bbc.co.uk |date=20 July 2012 |accessdate=2012-08-18}}</ref> He then went on to appear in all of the team's group stage fixtures, helping to secure progression into the knockout stages.

== 学术背景 ==
'''尼可籁·霍奇艾诺夫'''的音乐天赋很早就被发掘，五岁开始学习弹奏钢琴，随后前往[[莫斯科音乐学院|莫斯科音乐学院]]附属中央音乐学校就读。<ref name="Cavalletti Artists Management">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.cavartists.com/en/pianists/nikolay-khozyainov|title=Nikolay Khozyainov|website=Cavalletti Artists Management}}</ref>  7岁时，他在莫斯科音乐学院大音乐厅（the Great Hall）首演，演奏韩德尔钢琴协奏曲。<ref name=":5">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://europe.yamaha.com/en/artists/pianos_keyboards_instruments/nikolay_khozyainov/|title=Nikolay Khozyainov|date=July 2015|last=|first=|publisher=|website=Yamaha|access-date=July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{Template:Citation|last=NikolayKhozyainov|title=Nikolay Khozyainov Handel Piano Concerto F-major Moscow, February 2000|date=2016-05-27|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HO84GV3lplw|accessdate=2016-05-31}}</ref> 2010年， 他继续在莫斯科柴可夫斯基音乐学院求学并顺利完成学业。<ref name=":5">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://europe.yamaha.com/en/artists/pianos_keyboards_instruments/nikolay_khozyainov/|title=Nikolay Khozyainov|date=July 2015|last=|first=|publisher=|website=Yamaha|access-date=July 2015}}</ref><ref name=":4">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.theaterjones.com/ntx/2013vancliburninternationalpianocompetition/20130519072048/2013-05-19/Meet-the-Pianists-Nikolay-Khozyainov|title=Meet the Pianists: Nikolay Khozyainov|accessdate=4 April 2016|last1=Farrington|first1=Jones|website=Theater Jones}}</ref> 目前他投入Arie Vardi教授的门下，在德国汉诺威音楽学院（Musikhochschule Hannover）研究所深造。 <ref name=":3">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://www.nikolaykhozyainov.com/#!biography/c1enr|title=Nikolay Khozyainov Biography|date=|last=|first=|publisher=|website=Nikolay Khozyainov Official Website|access-date=}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Template:Cite web|url=http://jp.yamaha.com/sp/products/musical-instruments/keyboards/pianist-lounge/hope/012/|title=Yamaha Pianist Lounge|date=August 2014|last=Kosaka|first=Haruka|publisher=|website=Yamaha|access-date=}}</ref>

'''环戊二烯基亚硝酰镍'''是一种血红色液体。它由来自国际镍公司（The International Nickel Company）的一个团队发现。<ref>US Patent 3088959 - Process of making cyclopentadienyl nickel nitrosyl compounds</ref>它的分子式为(C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)NiNO，可以通过[[二茂镍|二茂镍]]和[[硝酸|硝酸]]的反应制备。<ref>{{cite book | last = Pauling | first = Linus | title = The nature of the chemical bond and the structure of molecules and crystals : an introduction to modern structural chemistry | publisher = Cornell University Press | location = Ithaca, N.Y | year = 1960 | isbn = 978-0-8014-0333-0 }}[https://books.google.com/books?id=L-1K9HmKmUUC&pg=PA391&lpg=PA391&dq=cyclopentadienyl+nickel+nitrosyl&source=bl&ots=zVnC-SWXn4&sig=VpvCakT6xh80vLQUvvzc4Nvg-5o&hl=en&sa=X&ei=FP8vT7nACJTQgAfEuKT0Dw&ved=0CCQQ6AEwATgK#v=onepage&q=cyclopentadienyl%20nickel%20nitrosyl&f=false]</ref>剧毒。

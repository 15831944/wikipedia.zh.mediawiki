'''灭虱草属'''（学名：{{sname|''Staphisagria''}}）是[[毛茛科|毛茛科]][[翠雀族|翠雀族]]的一个属，分布于[[地中海地区|地中海地区]]。灭虱草属原本是[[翠雀属|翠雀属]]的一支，然而分子证据表明其应当独立成属，模式种为[[灭虱草|灭虱草]]。<ref name=":0">{{Cite journal|title=Resurrection of the genus ''Staphisagria'' J. Hill, sister to all the other Delphinieae (Ranunculaceae)|url=http://www.pensoft.net/journals/phytokeys/article/2010/abstract/resurrection-of-the-genus-staphisagria-j-hill-sister-to-all-the-other-delphinieae-ranunculaceae-|last=Jabbour|first=Florian|last2=Renner|first2=Susanne|date=2011-11-29|journal=PhytoKeys|issue=0|doi=10.3897/phytokeys.7.2010|volume=7|pages=21|issn=1314-2003|pmc=PMC3261041|pmid=22287922}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=A phylogeny of Delphinieae (Ranunculaceae) shows that ''Aconitum'' is nested within ''Delphinium'' and that Late Miocene transitions to long life cycles in the Himalayas and Southwest China coincide with bursts in diversification|url=https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S1055790311005112|last=Jabbour|first=Florian|last2=Renner|first2=Susanne S.|date=2012-3|journal=Molecular Phylogenetics and Evolution|issue=3|doi=10.1016/j.ympev.2011.12.005|volume=62|pages=928–942|language=en}}</ref>

== 开发和制作 ==
本片由《[[邪恶力量|邪恶力量]]》主创人埃里克·克莱普克开发，并和J. J. Abrams以及Bryan Burk两人一同负责执行制作。NBC为了不让其它电视网参与本剧竞标，向[[联邦通讯委员会|联邦通讯委员会]]支付了200万美元的独占惩罚金<ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2012/05/14/the-2012-broadcast-network-drama-keys-nbc-revolution-revolution/ 2012广播网新剧全接触：NBC——《革命》（Revolution）]</ref><ref>[http://meijufans.com/?p=5806 NBC重点新剧推荐 2012-2013播出季秋季新剧：革命Revolution 剧照剧透预告片] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140810234314/http://meijufans.com/?p=5806 |date=2014-08-10 }}</ref>。

2012年10月2日NBC在播放了前三集后宣布预订全季<ref>[http://nbcumv.com/mediavillage/networks/nbcentertainment/pressreleases?pr=contents/press-releases/2012/10/02/nbcpicksupfulls1349209144667.xml NBC PICKS UP FULL SEASONS OF THREE ACCLAIMED FRESHMAN SERIES -- ‘REVOLUTION,’ ‘GO ON’ AND ‘THE NEW NORMAL’]</ref><ref>[http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2012/10/02/nbc-picks-up-full-seasons-of-three-acclaimed-freshman-series-revolution-go-on-and-the-new-normal-133210/20121002nbc06/ NBC Picks Up Full Seasons of Three Acclaimed Freshman Series -- "Revolution," "Go On" and "The New Normal"]</ref>，但在电视评论协会冬季媒体见面会上NBC表示将削减两集将第一季从22集缩减至20集<ref name=reduced>{{cite web|title=NBC at TCA: "Deception" and "Revolution" Get Reduced Orders|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/01/06/nbc-at-tca-deception-and-revolution-get-reduced-orders-554213/10141/|publisher=The Futon Critic|accessdate=6 January 2013|author=Brian Ford Sullivan}}</ref><ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2013/01/07/newsletter-2013-01-07-2013-winter-tca-nbc/ 1月7日简讯：电视评论协会冬季媒体见面会专题报道（NBC）]</ref>。

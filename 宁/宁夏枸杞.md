'''宁夏枸杞'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|''Lycium barbarum''}}）为[[茄科|茄科]][[枸杞属|枸杞属]]下的一个种。原產於東南歐和亞洲，主要人工種植地區在中國[[寧夏|寧夏]]<ref>{{cite book |first1=Harrison Leigh |last1=Flint |chapter=''Lycium barbarum'' |chapterurl = https://books.google.com/books?id=Q1_fAywb_bkC&pg=PA326 |title=Landscape plants for eastern North America: exclusive of Florida and the immediate Gulf Coast |publisher=John Wiley & Sons |location=Chichester |year=1997 |page=326 |isbn=978-0-471-59919-7}}</ref>。1730年代被第三代[[阿蓋爾公爵|阿蓋爾公爵]]阿齊巴爾德·坎貝爾引入英國，現在仍被用作樹籬<ref name=grin>{{cite web|url=http://www.ars-grin.gov/cgi-bin/npgs/html/taxon.pl?22939|title=GRIN Taxonomy for ''Lycium barbarum''|publisher=[[United_States_Department_of_Agriculture|United States Department of Agriculture]] [[Germplasm_Resources_Information_Network|Germplasm Resources Information Network]]|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131109045754/http://www.ars-grin.gov/cgi-bin/npgs/html/taxon.pl?22939|archivedate=2013-11-09}}</ref>，其果實也已經可以合法售賣<ref>[http://www.food.gov.uk/news/newsarchive/2007/jun/goji Food Standards Agency, June 2007, ''Responses on goji berries reviewed'']</ref>。

2018年，宁夏枸杞种植面积占全国种植面积的46%。<ref>{{Cite web|title=宁夏枸杞红了 行业发展趋势和前景喜人-中新网|url=http://www.chinanews.com/cj/2018/05-29/8525709.shtml|work=www.chinanews.com|accessdate=2019-01-22}}</ref>

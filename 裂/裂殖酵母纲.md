'''裂殖酵母纲'''的[[真菌|真菌]]类似[[酵母菌|酵母菌]]，但不论[[基因|基因]]的组织、结构及基因的表达调控等方面，还是细胞周期、rRNA的生物合成都和酵母菌并不相同，在某些方面裂殖酵母纲的真菌甚至和高等[[动物|动物]]有一定的相似性，本纲真菌从不进行有性繁殖，都是以无性分裂方式繁殖。

本纲研究得最多的物種是{{le|粟酒裂殖酵母|Schizosaccharomyces pombe}}(S. pombe)<ref name="pmid26447128">{{cite journal|author1=Hoffman CS |author2=Wood V |author3=Fantes PA. | title = An Ancient Yeast for Young Geneticists: A Primer on the ''Schizosaccharomyces pombe'' Model System.|journal = [[Genetics_(journal)|Genetics]]| volume = 201| issue = 2| pages = 403–23|date=Oct 2015| pmid = 26447128 | doi = 10.1534/genetics.115.181503 | issn = | pmc=4596657}}</ref><ref name="pmid27270696">{{cite journal|author1= Fantes PA |author2=Hoffman CS| title = A Brief History of Schizosaccharomyces pombe Research: A Perspective Over the Past 70 Years.|journal = [[Genetics_(journal)|Genetics]]| volume = 203| issue = 2| pages = 621–9|date=2016| pmid = 27270696| doi =10.1534/genetics.116.189407| url = | issn =}}</ref>。目前已經描述了四種粟酒裂殖酵母（S. pombe，S. japonicus，S. octosporus和S. cryophilus）<ref>{{cite journal|vauthors=Rhind N, Chen Z, Yassour M, Thompson DA, Haas BJ, Habib N, Wapinski I, Roy S, Lin MF, Heiman DI, Young SK, Furuya K, Guo Y, Pidoux A, Chen HM, Robbertse B, Goldberg JM, Aoki K, Bayne EH, Berlin AM, Desjardins CA, Dobbs E, Dukaj L, Fan L, FitzGerald MG, French C, Gujja S, Hansen K, Keifenheim D, Levin JZ, Mosher RA, Müller CA, Pfiffner J, Priest M, Russ C, Smialowska A, Swoboda P, Sykes SM, Vaughn M, Vengrova S, Yoder R, Zeng Q, Allshire R, Baulcombe D, Birren BW, Brown W, Ekwall K, Kellis M, Leatherwood J, Levin H, Margalit H, Martienssen R, Nieduszynski CA, Spatafora JW, Friedman N, Dalgaard JZ, Baumann P, Niki H, Regev A, Nusbaum C |title=Comparative Functional Genomics of the Fission Yeasts|journal=Science|date=21 April 2011|volume=332|issue=6032|pages=930–936|doi=10.1126/science.1203357|pmid=21511999|pmc=3131103}}</ref>。

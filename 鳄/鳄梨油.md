'''鳄梨油'''是指用[[鳄梨|鳄梨]]（''Persea americana''）果实压榨而成的[[植物油|植物油]]。除作为[[食用油|食用油]]外，鳄梨油还被用作润滑油，或用于制作[[化妆品|化妆品]]。

鳄梨油富含[[单不饱和脂肪酸|单不饱和脂肪酸]]与[[维生素E|维生素E]]，同时可以促进[[类胡萝卜素|类胡萝卜素]]等营养素的吸收。<ref>{{cite journal|last=Unlu|first=Nuray Z.|author2=Bohn, Torsten|author3= Clinton, Steven K.|author4= Schwartz, Steven J.|date=1 March 2005|title=Carotenoid Absorption from Salad and Salsa by Humans Is Enhanced by the Addition of Avocado or Avocado Oil|journal=Journal of Nutrition|publisher=The American Society for Nutritional Sciences|volume=135|issue=3|pages=431–436|url=http://jn.nutrition.org/content/135/3/431.short}}</ref>

鳄梨油的[[冒烟点|冒烟点]]相当高。未精炼的鳄梨油的冒烟点可达{{convert|480|°F|0|lk=on}}，精炼后更是能达到{{convert|520|°F|0}}。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.aocs.org/Membership/FreeCover.cfm?itemnumber=1099|title=What is unrefined, extra virgin, cold pressed avocado oil?}}</ref>因此，其适用于[[油炸|油炸]]、[[烧烤|烧烤]]等高温烹饪方式。

'''柯柏德·甘迪'''（{{lang-en|Kobad Ghandy}}；{{bd|1951年||}}）是[[印度共产党（毛主义）|印度共产党（毛主义）]]成员。他被赋予在城市地区传播毛派影响力和管理其宣传部门的任务。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.indianexpress.com/news/maoist-who-went-to-school-in-doon-london/520448/|title=Maoist who went to school in Doon, London|accessdate=28 September 2009|date=23 September 2009|work=Indian Express}}</ref>

甘迪主管西南局，协调[[泰米尔纳德邦|泰米尔纳德邦]]、[[卡纳塔克邦|卡纳塔克邦]]、[[喀拉拉邦|喀拉拉邦]]和[[马哈拉施特拉邦|马哈拉施特拉邦]]的纳萨尔派活动，在那里他们的活动仍然停滞不前，尽管党做出了艰苦努力。考虑到他分析国家和国际发展的能力，他还被赋予了在城市地区建立纳萨尔派运动的工作。  

甘迪承认，尽管印度中部和北部的纳萨尔派运动迅速蔓延，但它并未在其他邦扎根。即使在[[奥里萨邦|奥里萨邦]]、[[贾坎德邦|贾坎德邦]]、[[比哈尔邦|比哈尔邦]]和[[恰蒂斯加尔邦|恰蒂斯加尔邦]]、该党也未能赢得平原地区和城镇的人民，而这一运动在部落地带得到加强。随着毛主义党意识到它一直未能获得中产阶级和知识分子的支持，它已经要求甘迪制定战略和措施以赢得这两个人群。为此毛派组建了群众组织分委员会（Sub-Committee on Mass Organisations），由甘迪领导。  

== 生平 ==
生于孟买一个富裕的[[帕西人|帕西人]]家庭。父母是Nergis和阿迪（Adi），阿迪是[[葛兰素史克|葛兰素史克]]公司的高级财务主管。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/news/india/Cancer-landed-Kobad-in-police-net/articleshow/5048799.cms|title=Cancer landed Kobad in police net|last=Vishwa Mohan|first=Rahul Tripathi|date=24 September 2009|publisher=[[The_Times_of_India|The Times of India]]|accessdate=2009-09-24}}</ref> 先在[[杜恩公學|杜恩公學]]学习，然后在孟买St. Xavier's College学习。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/south_asia/8270400.stm|title=India's unlikely Maoist revolutionary|date=23 September 2009|work=BBC News}}</ref> 他去英国进修特许会计课程，深受巴苏·阿查里亚（Basu Acharya）的革命思想的影响。<ref>https://www.amazon.in/s/ref=dp_byline_sr_book_1?ie=UTF8&field-author=Basu+Acharya&search-alias=stripbooks</ref> 在那里他参与了左翼政治，但很快被逮捕并被驱逐出境。<ref name="this naxal">{{cite news |author= Nauzer Bharucha and Soumittra S Bose |title= This Naxal leader studied at Doon |url= http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/news/india/This-Naxal-leader-studied-at-Doon/articleshow/5044812.cms |work= Times of India|date= 23 September 2009 |accessdate=28 September 2009}}</ref> 未完成其学业。<ref name="The Rebel">{{cite web |author= Rahul Pandita |title= The Rebel |url= http://www.openthemagazine.com/article/nation/the-rebel |work = Open magazine |date= 26 September 2009 |accessdate= 26 September 2009}}</ref>

[[英迪拉·甘地|英迪拉·甘地]]总理任期内，他首先在孟买进行社会政治活动。<ref name="BBC1">{{Cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/south_asia/8270400.stm|title=India's unlikely Maoist revolutionary|accessdate=20 November 2014|publisher=BBC}}</ref> 到1982年，他和妻子离开孟买到[[那格浦尔|那格浦尔]]。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/south_asia/8270400.stm|title=India's unlikely Maoist revolutionary|date=23 September 2009|work=BBC News|accessdate=28 September 2009}}</ref>

他是保护民主权利委员会（Committee for the Protection of Democratic Rights）的创始成员。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/india-news/the-kobad-ghandy-i-knew/article1-456970.aspx|title=The Kobad Ghandy I knew|accessdate=20 November 2014|date=22 September 2009|last=Punwani|first=Jyoti|publisher=[[Hindustan_Times|Hindustan Times]]}}</ref>

接受癌症治疗时，<ref>{{cite news |author= Rahul Tripathi and Vishwa Mohan |title= Cancer landed Kobad in police net |url= http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/news/india/Cancer-landed-Kobad-in-police-net/articleshow/5048799.cms |work= Times of India  |date= 24 September 2009 |accessdate=28 September 2009}}</ref> 于2009年9月20日在南德里被捕。<ref>{{cite web |author= B Vijay Murty and Karan Choudhury |title= Top Maoist leader arrested in Delhi |url= http://www.hindustantimes.com/newdelhi/Highest-ranking-Maoist-leader-arrested-in-Delhi/456662/H1-Article1-456658.aspx |work= Hindustan Times |date= 22 September 2009 |accessdate= 28 September 2009 |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20090926042526/http://www.hindustantimes.com/newdelhi/Highest-ranking-Maoist-leader-arrested-in-Delhi/456662/H1-Article1-456658.aspx |archivedate= 2009年9月26日}}</ref>

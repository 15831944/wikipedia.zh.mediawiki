'''柯蒂斯展演中心槍擊案'''，是指發生在[[美国中部时间|美国中部时间]]2015年5月3日，於[[德州|德州]][[加蘭_(德克薩斯州)|加蘭]]{{link-en|柯蒂斯展演中心|Curtis Culwell Center}}（Curtis Culwell Center）外的一起[[槍擊|槍擊]]案<ref name="cbslocal">{{cite web|url=http://dfw.cbslocal.com/2015/05/03/breaking-shooting-in-garland/|title=BREAKING: Shooting At Muhammad Art Exhibit In Garland « CBS Dallas / Fort Worth|publisher=dfw.cbslocal.com|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>。當時，該中心正在展出{{link-en|穆罕默德的描繪|depictions of Muhammad|描繪穆罕默德}}的[[漫畫|漫畫]]作品。兩名使用[[突击步枪|突击步枪]]的槍手突然在展場外開槍攻擊，並封鎖展場，致使一名{{link-en|加蘭獨立學區|Garland Independent School District}}的警員受傷<ref name="GunmenFire">{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/people-shot-texas-prophet-muhammad-art-exhibit-article-1.2208953|title=Two gunmen shot dead by cops after opening fire outside controversial 'Prophet Muhammad' art exhibit in Texas|work=New York Daily News|date=May 3, 2015|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name="dallasnews">{{cite web|url=http://www.dallasnews.com/news/local-news/20150503-breaking-gunfire-reported-at-anti-islam-event-at-garland-isd-facility.ece|title=Police kill 2 men who opened fire outside Muhammad art show in Garland | Dallas Morning News|publisher=dallasnews.com|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>，最終兩名槍手被[[SWAT|SWAT]]擊斃，傷者在經過醫治後出院<ref name="cbs19">{{cite web|url=http://www.cbs19.tv/story/28965671/two-shot-near-muhammad-art-event-in-garland|title=2 dead after gunfire outside Muhammad cartoon exhibit in Garland - KYTX CBS19.tv - News, Weather, & Sports | Tyler-Longview|author=Gerardo Martinez|publisher=cbs19.tv|date=May 3, 2015|accessdate=May 4, 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150505021123/http://www.cbs19.tv/story/28965671/two-shot-near-muhammad-art-event-in-garland|archivedate=2015年5月5日|df=}}</ref>。

== 背景 ==
===描繪穆罕默德的展覽與競賽===
[[Image:Geert_Wilders_op_Prinsjesdag_2014_(cropped).jpg|thumb]]的政治人物[[基爾特·威爾德斯|基爾特·威爾德斯]](資料照)]]
[[File:Pamela_Geller_2011.jpg|帕梅拉·蓋勒(資料照)]]
槍擊案的起因是源自於{{link-en|美國捍衛自由陣線|American Freedom Defense Initiative}}<ref>（American Freedom Defense Initiative，AFDI，或稱「停止美國伊斯蘭化」，Stop Islamization of America）</ref>所主辦的一項活動。該組織是一個極端[[伊斯兰恐惧症|反伊斯蘭]]團體。他們舉辦了「第一屆穆罕默德藝術競賽展」（First Annual Muhammad Art Exhibit and Contest），從350件描繪穆罕默德的動漫作品中選出首獎，並頒贈1萬美元；得主是一位前[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]，現為穆斯林批評者的[[漫畫家|漫畫家]]Bosch Fawstin<ref name="TrafficCop"/>，而他在本次競賽中總共創作了6件作品，獲得了12500美元的獎金<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.americanthinker.com/blog/2015/05/the_muhammad_cartoon_that_won_the_draw_muhammad_contest.html|title=The Muhammad cartoon that won the Draw Muhammad contest|publisher=American Thinker|author=Thomas Lifson|date=2015-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://thescoopblog.dallasnews.com/2015/05/garland-cartoon-contest-winner-reacts-to-shooting-they-came-to-kill-us-and-died-for-it-justice.html/|title=Garland cartoon contest winner reacts to shooting: "They came to kill us and died for it. Justice."|publisher=Dallas Morning News|author=Naheed Rajwani|date=2015-05-04}}</ref><ref name="ChuckRoss">{{cite news|url=http://dailycaller.com/2015/05/03/breaking-shooting-in-texas-outside-of-muslim-cartoon-event-attended-by-geert-wilders|newspaper=Daily Caller|title=Shooting in Texas Outside of Muslim Cartoon Event|author=Chuck Ross|date=May 4, 2015}}</ref>。

本次活動邀請了{{link-en|帕梅拉·蓋勒|Pamela Geller}}和[[基爾特·威爾德斯|基爾特·威爾德斯]]演講。蓋勒是美國捍衛自由陣線的創辦人之一，威爾德斯則是一位[[荷蘭|荷蘭]]的[[國會議員|國會議員]]，兩人都是著名的[[反伊斯蘭|反伊斯蘭]]人士，兩位美國穆斯林議員[[:en:Keith_Ellison|Keith Ellison]]和[[:en:André_Carson|André Carson]]曾試圖阻止威爾德斯入境美國出席此次活動，但並未成功<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/news/volokh-conspiracy/wp/2015/04/29/congressmen-keith-ellison-and-andre-carson-call-for-denial-of-visa-to-dutch-legislator-geert-wilders|title=Congressmen Keith Ellison and André Carson call for denial of visa to Dutch legislator Geert Wilders|date=April 29, 2015|author=Eugene Volokh|author-link=Eugene Volokh|work=The Washington Post}}</ref>。

而發起此次活動的原因，是因為4個月前，在[[法國|法國]]的{{link-en|諷刺雜誌列表|List of satirical magazines|諷刺雜誌}}[[查理周刊|查理周刊]]，其總部遭到了[[查理周刊总部枪击案|恐怖攻擊]]<ref name="ChuckRoss"/>，本次槍擊案發生時，柯蒂斯展演中心內有約200人出席了此次活動<ref name="2Gunmen"/>。

=== 對伊斯蘭教的褻瀆 ===
{{seealso|對伊斯蘭教的褻瀆}}
對於大多數穆斯林而言，任何描繪[[伊斯蘭教的先知|先知]][[穆罕默德|穆罕默德]]形象的作品，都具有{{link-en|對伊斯蘭教的褻瀆|Islam and blasphemy|褻瀆}}性質<ref name="橫路" />。

=== 保安準備 ===
本次活動的主辦單位，在事前便已支付了一萬美元，聘請40名<ref name="GunmenFire"/>未當值的[[警察|警察]]與私人保安。而[[FBI|FBI]]、一個[[SWAT|SWAT]]小組，與[[美國菸酒槍炮及爆裂物管理局|美國菸酒槍炮及爆裂物管理局]]也派員進駐，以防發生任何意外事故<ref name="reuters1">{{cite web|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2015/05/04/us-usa-shooting-texas-idUSKBN0NP01G20150504|title=Police raid home of gunman in Mohammad cartoon attack in Texas|publisher=Reuters|date=May 4, 2015}}</ref>。據報導，當局在案發前沒有收到可信的攻擊威脅<ref name="2Gunmen">{{cite web|url=http://abcnews.go.com/US/shooting-vicinity-texas-community-center-hosting-draw-prophet/story?id=30777312|title=2 Gunmen Killed Outside Community Center Hosting 'Draw the Prophet' Show|work=ABC News|date=May 4, 2015|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>。

== 攻擊 ==
在活動預定結束時間的下午七時前<ref name="2Gunmen"/>，兩名身穿[[防彈衣|防彈衣]]，手持[[突擊步槍|突擊步槍]]的男子，拉開設置在展演中心前方的路障，自車內朝著停在一旁的警車開火，警車內一名沒有武裝的[[保全員|保全員]]受到槍擊。槍手被隨後趕到的加蘭警方和特勤人員擊斃<ref name="SuspectsThere">{{cite web|url=http://www.wfaa.com/story/news/crime/2015/05/03/garland-curtis-culwell-center-swat/26848435|title=Garland police: Suspects were 'there to shoot people'|work=WFFA|date=May 4, 2015|accessdate=May 4, 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150505060356/http://www.wfaa.com/story/news/crime/2015/05/03/garland-curtis-culwell-center-swat/26848435/|archivedate=2015年5月5日|df=}}</ref><ref name="TrafficCop">{{cite web|url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/nation/2015/05/04/prophet-mohammed-cartoon-shootings-texas/26858741/|title=Traffic cop killed both attackers at Mohammed exhibit in Texas|work=USA Today|date=May 4, 2015|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name="nbcdfw">{{cite web|url=http://www.nbcdfw.com/news/local/Shots-Fired-at-Prophet-Muhammad-Art-Event-302362751.html|title=Garland ISD Officer Injured, 2 Dead in Shooting Outside Culwell Center in Garland | NBC 5 Dallas-Fort Worth|publisher=nbcdfw.com|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name="GunmenFire">{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/people-shot-texas-prophet-muhammad-art-exhibit-article-1.2208953|title=Two gunmen shot dead by cops after opening fire outside controversial 'Prophet Muhammad' art exhibit in Texas|work=New York Daily News|date=May 3, 2015|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name="TimelineNews">{{cite web|url=http://dfw.cbslocal.com/2015/05/03/breaking-shooting-in-garland/|title=2 Dead In Shooting At Muhammad Art Exhibit In Garland|work=CBS DFW|date=May 4, 2015|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>。

加蘭獨立學區的新聞官證實傷者是58歲的Bruce Joiner，他被擊中[[腳踝|腳踝]]<ref name="wfaa">{{cite web|url=http://www.wfaa.com/story/news/local/2015/05/03/garland-curtis-culwell-center-swat/26848435/|title=2 gunmen shot dead outside Muhammad Art Exhibit in Garland|publisher=wfaa.com|accessdate=May 4, 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150505021224/http://www.wfaa.com/story/news/local/2015/05/03/garland-curtis-culwell-center-swat/26848435/|archivedate=2015年5月5日|df=}}</ref><ref name="GunmenFire"/>；當晚9時，他在當地的一家醫院接受治療<ref name="TimelineNews"/>。

由於擔心嫌犯的車內可能裝有引爆裝置，警方疏散了當地的商家，封鎖了大範圍的區域，並派遣了拆彈小組到達現場，及三架[[直升機|直升機]]在上空搜索<ref name="foxnews">{{cite web|url=http://www.foxnews.com/us/2015/05/04/police-shooting-at-muhammad-cartoon-contest-in-texas/|title=Two suspects killed, officer injured after shooting outside Muhammad cartoon contest in Texas|publisher=foxnews.com|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>。一位身穿特警服裝的人員上台告知與會者活動結束，因為發生了槍擊案，有一名保安與兩名嫌犯受到槍擊<ref name="fox4kc">{{cite web|url=http://fox4kc.com/2015/05/03/report-gunfire-erupts-outside-texas-anti-islam-event|title=Report: gunfire erupts outside Texas anti-Islam event|publisher=fox4kc.com|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>。事後證實，嫌犯的車內並沒有[[炸藥|炸藥]]<ref name="PoliceSearch">{{cite web|url=http://alaska-native-news.com/police-search-phoenix-apartment-of-curtis-culwell-center-attack-17166|title=Police Search Phoenix Apartment of Curtis Culwell Center Attack|work=Alaska Native News|date=May 4, 2015|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>。

== 槍手 ==
本案的兩名槍手是30歲的艾爾頓·辛普森（Elton Simpson），和34歲的納迪爾·索菲（Nadir Soofi），都是[[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]][[鳳凰城|鳳凰城]]人，也是同住一間[[公寓|公寓]]的[[室友|室友]]。槍擊案發生後，鳳凰城警方已開始搜索他們居住的公寓<ref name="PoliceSearch"/><ref name="橫路">{{cite news|author1=橫路|title=得州襲擊案：槍手曾被列恐怖嫌疑名單|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/zhongwen/trad/world/2015/05/150505_usa_gunmen_terror_suspects|accessdate=2015-05-05|agency=[[BBC中文網|BBC中文網]]|date=2015-05-05}}</ref>。

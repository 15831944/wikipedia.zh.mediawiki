'''段曰活'''（'''Đoàn Viết Hoạt'''，{{bd|1942年|12月24日||}}）星一位[[越南|越南]]記者、教育家和民主活動家，並多次因批評越南共產黨領導而被監禁。他曾獲得多個國際獎項，其中包括[[国际新闻自由奖|CPJ国际新闻自由奖]]（1993年）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cpj.org/attacks96/frontmatter/ipfa.html |title=Journalists Receive 1996 Press Freedom Awards |year=1996 |publisher=[[Committee_to_Protect_Journalists|Committee to Protect Journalists]] |accessdate=28 May 2011}}</ref>、[[罗伯特·弗朗西斯·肯尼迪人权奖|罗伯特·弗朗西斯·肯尼迪人权奖]]（1995年）<ref name=STTP>{{cite book |title=Speak truth to power: human rights defenders who are changing our world |last=Kennedy |first=Kerry |authorlink= |coauthors=Kerry Kennedy Cuomo, Eddie Adams, Nan Richardson |year=2000 |publisher=Umbrage Editions |location= |isbn= |page=102 |pages= |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=zhHVKKBmZKEC&pg=PA102&lpg=PA102&dq=%22Doan+Viet%22+Sakharov&source=bl&ots=k3aDyJOBui&sig=2Rkw8yUrqBLns3HR7FGzQ5VT9MA&hl=en&sa=X&ei=uO4UT8XDCKrt0gHmo5m0Aw&ved=0CCcQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=%22Doan%20Viet%22%20Sakharov&f=false |accessdate=17 January 2012}}</ref>、[[自由新聞金筆獎|自由新聞金筆獎]]（1998年）<ref name=WAN>{{cite web |url=http://www.ifex.org/vietnam/1997/11/04/imprisoned_editor_doan_viet_hoat_2/ |title=Imprisoned editor Doan VIet Hoat wins WAN's 1998 Golden Pen of Freedom award |author= |date=4 November 1997 |work= |publisher=[[International_Freedom_of_Expression_Exchange|IFEX]] |accessdate=17 January 2012}}</ref>、以及[[世界新聞自由英雄|世界新聞自由英雄]]（2000年）。<ref name=IPI>{{cite web |url=http://www.freemedia.at/awards/doan-viet-hoat.html |title=Doan Viet Hoat, Vietnam World Press Freedom Hero (Honoured in 2000) |author= |year=2010 |work= |publisher=[[International_Press_Institute|International Press Institute]] |accessdate=17 January 2012}}</ref>

'''星云奖'''（'''Nebula Award'''）是[[美國科幻及奇幻作家協會|美國科幻及奇幻作家協會]]（Science Fiction & Fantasy Writers of America Inc.）颁发的[[科幻小說|科幻]]及[[奇幻小說|奇幻]]艺术年度大奖，因奖品为嵌在荧光树脂中的螺旋状[[星云|星云]]而得名。與[[雨果獎|雨果獎]]（Hugo Award）同為科幻、奇幻界最受矚目的年度獎項<ref name="Guardianquote">{{cite web|last=Flood |first=Allison |title=Ursula K Le Guin wins sixth Nebula award |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2009/apr/28/ursula-k-le-guin-nebula |work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] |accessdate=2011-12-12 |date=2009-04-28 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090801031449/http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2009/apr/28/ursula-k-le-guin-nebula |archivedate=2009-08-01 |deadurl=no }}</ref> 。

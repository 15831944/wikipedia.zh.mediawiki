{{vgname|星球大戰 絕地：組織殞落|en=Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order}}是一款預計於2019年出品的[[動作冒險遊戲|動作冒險]][[電子遊戲|電子遊戲]]，由[[Respawn_Entertainment|Respawn Entertainment]]開發、[[美商藝電|美商藝電]]發行。故事設定在《[[星際大戰三部曲：西斯大帝的復仇|星際大戰三部曲：西斯大帝的復仇]]》之後。遊戲定於2019年11月15日在[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]]、[[PlayStation_4|PlayStation 4]]和[[Xbox_One|Xbox One]]發售。

== 概要 ==
故事發生在[[絕地大屠殺|66號密令]]後，玩家可以化身倖存的絕地學徒卡爾·凱斯蒂斯（Cal Kestis，[[卡梅隆·莫納漢|卡梅隆·莫納漢]]飾演）<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ign.com/articles/2019/04/14/star-wars-jedi-fallen-order-release-date-announced-gameplay-story-details-revealed|title=Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order Release Date Announced, Gameplay, Story Details Revealed|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190414071854/https://www.ign.com/articles/2019/04/14/star-wars-jedi-fallen-order-release-date-announced-gameplay-story-details-revealed|archive-date=2019-04-14|first=Jonahton|last=Durnsbush|work=[[IGN|IGN]]|publisher=[[Ziff_Davis|Ziff Davis]]|date=2019-04-13|accessdate=2019-04-14}}</ref>。

== 開發 ==
2014年，{{link-en|史狄克·艾斯穆森|Stig Asmussen}}加入[[Respawn_Entertainment|Respawn Entertainment]]擔任遊戲總監，領導該工作室的第二個開發團隊<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gamespot.com/articles/e3-2014-veteran-god-of-war-dev-joins-titanfall-studio/1100-6420459/|title=E3 2014: Veteran God of War Dev Joins Titanfall Studio|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181231120052/https://www.gamespot.com/articles/e3-2014-veteran-god-of-war-dev-joins-titanfall-studio/1100-6420459/f|archive-date=2018-12-21 |first=Eddie|last=Makuch|work=[[GameSpot|GameSpot]]|publisher=[[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive]]|date=2014-06-12|accessdate=2019-04-14}}</ref>。2018年5月，該項目被揭示為《[[星際大戰|星際大戰]]》宇宙中設置的[[第三人稱視角遊戲|第三人稱]][[動作冒險遊戲|動作冒險遊戲]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.eurogamer.net/articles/2016-05-04-titanfall-developer-respawn-is-making-a-third-person-star-wars-game|title=Titanfall developer Respawn is making a third-person Star Wars game|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180912140557/https://www.eurogamer.net/articles/2016-05-04-titanfall-developer-respawn-is-making-a-third-person-star-wars-game|archive-date=2018-09-12|first=Wesley|last=Yin-Poole|work=[[Eurogamer|Eurogamer]]|publisher=Gamer Network|date=2016-05-04|accessdate=2019-04-14}}</ref>。2018年6月，[[美商藝電|美商藝電]]在[[2018年E3電子娛樂展|2018年E3電子娛樂展]]宣佈遊戲正在開發中<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.polygon.com/e3/2018/6/9/17443230/respawn-star-wars-jedi-fallen-order-release-date-ea-play|title=Respawn’s Star Wars game is called Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180623110215/https://www.polygon.com/e3/2018/6/9/17443230/respawn-star-wars-jedi-fallen-order-release-date-ea-play|archive-date=2018-06-23|first=Michael|last=McWhertor|work=[[Polygon_(website)|Polygon]]|publisher=[[Vox_Media|Vox Media]]|date=2018-06-09|accessdate=2019-04-14}}</ref>，並在2019年4月舉行的[[星戰慶典|星戰慶典]]上透露更多的內容。

撰寫《[[四海兄弟III|四海兄弟III]]》故事的亞倫·康崔拉斯（Aaron Contreras）領導了遊戲的敘事團隊，其中包括{{link-en|克里斯·阿維隆|Chris Avellone}}和其他四位編劇<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gamespot.com/articles/star-wars-jedi-fallen-orders-story-is-very-importa/1100-6466227/|title=Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order's Story Is "Very Important" To The Game, Says Chris Avellone |first=Jordan |last=Ramée |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190414072542/https://www.gamespot.com/g00/articles/star-wars-jedi-fallen-orders-story-is-very-importa/1100-6466227/?i10c.ua=1&i10c.encReferrer=&i10c.dv=17 |archive-date=2019-04-14 |date=2019-04-13|accessdate=2019-04-14|work=[[GameSpot|GameSpot]]|publisher=[[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive]]}}</ref>。該工作室還與[[盧卡斯影業|盧卡斯影業]]合作創建新的角色和地點<ref>{{cite web|url=https://kotaku.com/our-first-look-at-star-wars-jedi-fallen-order-a-singl-1834022274|title=Our First Look At Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order, A Single-Player Game Starring A Jedi Survivor|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190414072927/https://kotaku.com/our-first-look-at-star-wars-jedi-fallen-order-a-singl-1834022274|archive-date=2019-04-14|first=Zack|last=Zwiezen|work=[[Kotaku|Kotaku]]|publisher=[[Univision_Communications|Univision Communications]]|date=2019-04-13|accessdate=2019-04-14}}</ref>。

== 發行 ==
遊戲定於2019年11月15日在[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]]、[[PlayStation_4|PlayStation 4]]和[[Xbox_One|Xbox One]]發售<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gamesradar.com/star-wars-jedi-fallen-order-release-date/|title=Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order release date is official and it's coming in November|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190414073510/https://www.gamesradar.com/star-wars-jedi-fallen-order-release-date/|archivedate=2019-04-14|first=Connor|last=Sheridan|work=[[GamesRadar+|GamesRadar+]]|publisher=[[Future_plc|Future Publishing]]|date=2019-04-13|accessdate=2019-04-14}}</ref>。

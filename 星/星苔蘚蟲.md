'''星苔蘚蟲'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Constellaria'''''}}）是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[苔蘚蟲|苔蘚蟲]]，生存於[[奧陶紀|奧陶紀]]至[[志留紀|志留紀]]。<ref>{{Cite web |url = http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=25121 |title = Fossilworks: Constellaria |language = en |accessdate = 2017-11-07 }}</ref>牠們是以濃密的群體形式生長，並有厚實、壓緊的枝。枝的表面規則地覆蓋著顯著的星狀小[[圓|圓]]丘，眾多的單體位於小圓丘上。群體的小丘成了[[排洩|排洩]][[進食|進食]]水流的管道。其群體生活生活在[[海床|海床]]上，以濾食為生<ref>{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=36 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

'''星木'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Asteroxylon'''''}}），又名'''星狀木'''，是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的陸生[[維管植物|維管植物]]，繁盛於早[[泥盆紀|泥盆紀]]時期。它們被認為是[[石松綱|石松綱]]下最基本的成員。<ref name="Hao and Xue 2013">{{Cite book|title=The Early Devonian Posongchong Flora of Yunnan - A Contribution to an Understanding of the Evolution and Early Diversification of Vascular Plants|last=Hao|first=Shougang|first2=Jinzhuang |last2=Xue |publisher=Science Press|year=2013|isbn=978-7-03-036616-0|location=Beijing|pages=244–245}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
星木的植株高約12至40[[公分|公分]]。<ref name="Strullu-Derrien etal 2015">{{cite journal|last1=Strullu‐Derrien|first1=Christine|last2=Wawrzyniak|first2=Zuzanna|last3=Goral|first3=Tomasz|last4=Kenrick|first4=Paul|title=Fungal colonization of the rooting system of the early land plant ''Asteroxylon mackiei'' from the 407‐Myr‐old Rhynie Chert (Scotland, UK)|journal=Botanical Journal of the Linnean Society|date=2015|volume=179|issue=1|pages=201–213|doi=10.1111/boj.12307}}</ref>[[根|根]]狀[[莖|莖]]可延伸到地表下約20公分的深度。<ref name=Smoot1981>{{cite journal
 | author = Smoot, E.L.
 |author2= Jansen, R.K.|author3= Taylor, T.N.
 | year = 1981
 | title = A Phylogenetic Analysis of the Land Plants: A Botanical Commentary
 | journal = Taxon
 | volume = 30
 | issue = 1
 | pages = 65–67
 | doi = 10.2307/1219392
 | jstor = 1219392
 | publisher = International Association for Plant Taxonomy (IAPT)
}}</ref>莖中央的[[木質部|木質部]]的橫切面為明顯的星形，故名星木。<ref name="Kerp etal 2013">{{cite journal|last1=Kerp|first1=Hans|last2=Wellman|first2=Charles H.|last3=Krings|first3=Michael|last4=Kearney|first4=Patricia|last5=Hass|first5=Hagen|title=Reproductive organs and in situ spores of ''Asteroxylon mackiei'' Kidston & Lang, the most complex plant from the lower Devonian Rhynie chert|journal=International Journal of Plant Sciences|date=2013|volume=174|issue=3|pages=293–308|doi=10.1086/668613}}</ref>它們沒有真正的[[葉|葉]]，而只有約5[[公釐|公釐]]長的無支撐帶狀結構（[[延伸葉|延伸葉]]），從莖中心的主束分支出的管狀構造痕跡終止於每個帶狀結構的基部。<ref name="Hao and Xue 2013" /><ref name="Kerp etal 2013" />[[氣孔|氣孔]]的構造說明了它們的組織能夠行[[光合作用|光合作用]]。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Wilson|first1=Jonathon P.|last2=Fischer|first2=Woodward W.|title=Hydraulics of ''Asteroxylon mackei'', an early Devonian vascular plant, and the early evolution of water transport tissue in terrestrial plants|journal=Geobiology|date=2011|volume=9|issue=2|pages=121–130|doi=10.1111/j.1472-4669.2010.00269.x}}</ref>

星木和其他與其相近的早泥盆紀[[石松綱|石松類]]（如[[鐮木|鐮木]]、[[巴拉曼蕨|巴拉曼蕨]]）的不同之處在於後者[[化石|化石]]中的[[維管束|維管束]]痕跡有延伸到其葉狀構造中<ref name="Hao and Xue 2013" />，因此鐮木與巴拉曼蕨被認為擁有真正的「葉」，星木則否。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Tomescu|first1=Alexandru M.F.|title=Megaphylls, microphylls and the evolution of leaf development|journal=Trends in Plant Science|date=2009|volume=14|issue=1|pages=5–12|doi=10.1016/j.tplants.2008.10.008|pmid=19070531}}</ref>

== 物種 ==
星木屬的[[模式種|模式種]]為[[馬基星木|馬基星木]]（''Asteroxylon mackiei''）。另一個物種──''Asteroxylon elberfeldense''已被描述<ref name=Fair67>{{Cite journal |last1=Fairon |first1=M. |date=1967 |title=Asteroxylon elberfeldense Kräusel et Weyland porte-t-il des axes terminaux du type ''Hostimella hostimensis'' Potonié et Bernard |journal=Annales de la Société Géologique de Belgique |volume=10 |pages=1–30 }}</ref>，但現在一般被認為是不同類的物種，並已被分配到另一屬''{{tsl|en|Thursophyton|Thursophyton}}''。<ref name="KenrickCrane1997">{{Cite book |last=Kenrick |first=Paul |last2=Crane |first2=Peter R. |year=1997 |title=The Origin and Early Diversification of Land Plants: A Cladistic Study |publication-place=Washington, D.C. |publisher=Smithsonian Institution Press |isbn=978-1-56098-730-7 |lastauthoramp=yes }}</ref>此外最初被命名為''Asteroxylon setchellii''的化石現已被認為是[[蕨類植物|蕨類植物]]，而被改稱為''Stenokoleos setchellii''。

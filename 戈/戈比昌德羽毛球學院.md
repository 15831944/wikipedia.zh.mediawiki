'''普萊勒·戈比昌德羽毛球學院'''（{{lang-en|Pullela Gopichand Badminton Academy}}，PGBA），通常簡稱其為'''戈比昌德羽毛球學院'''，是一所位於[[印度|印度]][[泰倫加納邦|泰倫加納邦]][[海得拉巴_(印度)|海德拉巴]]的[[羽球|羽球]]訓練機構，由印度羽球名宿、現任[[印度國家羽毛球隊|印度國家羽毛球隊]]總教練[[普萊勒·戈比昌德|普萊勒·戈比昌德]]於2008年成立，為印度羽球界培育多位世界級球員，如：[[塞娜·內維爾|塞娜·內維爾]]、[[普薩拉·文卡塔·辛德胡|普薩拉·文卡塔·辛德胡]]、[[斯里坎特·基達姆比|斯里坎特·基達姆比]]等。

== 歷史 ==
[[File:P._Gopichand_with_CM_Naveen_Patnaik.jpg|thumb]]成立羽毛球學院的合作意向書。]]
印度羽球運動員普萊勒·戈比昌德贏得2001年[[全英羽毛球公開賽|全英羽毛球公開賽]]男單冠軍時已經27歲，往後因受傷病所苦成績下滑，最終於2003年宣布退役<ref>{{zh-cn}}{{Cite news |url=http://sports.sohu.com/51/00/sports_news164060051.shtml |title=印度戈比昌德积极养伤 以图卫冕全英羽毛球冠军 |date=2002-02-06 |accessdate=2019-04-13 |deadurl=no |agency=[[新华网|新华网]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171201041335/http://sports.sohu.com/51/00/sports_news164060051.shtml |archivedate=2017-12-01}}</ref><ref name="retirement">{{en}}{{Cite news |url=https://www.telegraphindia.com/1031116/asp/sports/story_2577346.asp |title=Gopichand to quit next year |date=2003-11-15 |newspaper=[[每日電訊報|每日電訊報]] |accessdate=2019-04-13 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190412161958/https://www.telegraphindia.com/sport/gopichand-to-quit-next-year-life-without-badminton-will-be-very-boring-says-former-all-england-champion/cid/791436 |archivedate=2019-04-12}}</ref>。在退役前夕，戈比昌德決定為下一代創辦一間世界級訓練機構，並遵循這個想法創辦了一間羽毛球學院。依靠他在全英賽所闖下的名聲，[[安得拉邦|安得拉邦]][[首席部長_(印度)|首席部長]]{{link-en|錢德巴布·奈杜|N. Chandrababu Naidu}}在2003年批准將一塊位於[[海得拉巴_(印度)|海德拉巴]]的5英畝（2公頃）土地租其使用45年，並且只收取一筆象徵性費用<ref name="rediff">{{en}}{{cite news|title=Yonex to fund Gopi's academy|url=http://inhome.rediff.com/sports/2004/may/28gopi.htm|newspaper={{link-en|雷迪夫網|Rediff.com}}|date=2004-05-28|access-date=2019-04-13|dead-url=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171107005122/http://inhome.rediff.com/sports/2004/may/28gopi.htm|archivedate=2017-11-07}}</ref><ref name="Wall Street">{{en}}{{cite news|title=adminton Academy Trains Saina but Still Struggles|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB10001424052748703726404575533462260048940|newspaper=[[華爾街日報|華爾街日報]]|date=2010-10-06|access-date=2019-04-13|dead-url=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180207163632/https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB10001424052748703726404575533462260048940|archivedate=2018-02-07}}</ref>。戈比昌德也同時與[[YONEX|YONEX]]洽談贊助<ref name="retirement"/><ref name="rediff"/>、計畫聘請國外教練<ref>{{en}}{{cite news|title=Badminton academy at Hyderabad soon|url=http://www.indiaexpress.com/news/sports/20030422-0.html|newspaper=[[印度快報|印度快報]]|date=2003-04-22|access-date=2019-04-13|dead-url=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110724201109/http://www.indiaexpress.com/news/sports/20030422-0.html|archivedate=2011-07-24}}</ref>。

戈比昌德在接下來的幾年逐步實行他的計畫，並找上企業家{{link-en|尼馬加達·普拉薩德|Nimmagadda Prasad}}籌措資金，以「為印度培育出奧運獎牌得主」為條件，獲得5000萬盧比的贊助款項<ref>{{en}}{{cite news|title=All that went into the making of Gopichand Academy|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/telangana/All-that-went-into-the-making-of-Gopichand-Academy/article14593383.ece|newspaper=[[印度報|印度報]]|date=2016-08-28|access-date=2019-04-13|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171116162205/https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/telangana/All-that-went-into-the-making-of-Gopichand-Academy/article14593383.ece|archivedate=2017-11-16}}</ref>。在籌辦期間，戈比昌德的妻子、前印度羽球運動員與奧運代表{{link-en|P·V·V·拉克希米|P.V.V. Lakshmi}}也一同參與籌措資金<ref>{{en}}{{cite news|title=Master of multi-tasking|url=http://www.hindu.com/thehindu/thscrip/print.pl?file=2004040800680400.htm&date=2004/04/08/&prd=mp&|newspaper=印度報|date=2004-04-08|access-date=2019-04-13|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20130125082846/http://www.hindu.com/thehindu/thscrip/print.pl?file=2004040800680400.htm&date=2004/04/08/&prd=mp&|archivedate=2013-01-25}}</ref>，兩人甚至將房子抵押以籌措剩餘的款項，最終籌得約250萬美元<ref name="Wall Street"/>。

在羽毛球學院落成之後，正在備戰[[2010年大英國協運動會羽毛球比賽|2010年大英國協運動會羽毛球比賽]]的選手們轉往此處受訓。政府同時也將支付選手的補助從每日5美元提高至20美元。截至2010年，戈比昌德每年花費約10萬美元培訓約60名選手<ref name="Wall Street"/>。

由於現有設施不敷使用，戈比昌德羽球學院決定於海德拉巴興建新的場館，並於2015年落成，新場館擁有九面羽球場地<ref>{{en}}{{cite news|title=Pullela Gopichand sets up new badminton academy in Hyderabad|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/sports/pullela-gopichand-sets-up-new-badminton-academy-in-hyderabad/articleshow/49299952.cms|newspaper={{link-en|經濟時代|The Economic Times}}|date=2015-10-10|access-date=2019-04-14|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20190413145552/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/sports/pullela-gopichand-sets-up-new-badminton-academy-in-hyderabad/articleshow/49299952.cms|archivedate=2019-04-13}}</ref>。除了海德拉巴，戈比昌德羽球學院在[[中央邦|中央邦]]的[[瓜廖爾|瓜廖爾]]、[[古吉拉特邦|古吉拉特邦]]的[[巴羅達_(古吉拉特邦)|巴羅達]]及[[北方邦|北方邦]]的[[諾伊達|諾伊達]]皆設有訓練基地<ref>{{en}}{{cite news|title=Gopichand badminton academy to start training players from March 2017|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/sports/badminton/gopichand-badminton-academy-to-start-training-players-from-march-2017/articleshow/57044092.cms|newspaper=[[印度時報|印度時報]]|date=2017-02-08|access-date=2019-04-14|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190414095544/https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/sports/badminton/gopichand-badminton-academy-to-start-training-players-from-march-2017/articleshow/57044092.cms|archivedate=2019-04-14}}</ref>。

== 爭議事件 ==
2012年，[[印度國家羽毛球隊|印度國家羽毛球隊]]隊員[[普拉加克塔·沙旺特|普拉加克塔·沙旺特]]向孟買高等法院控告印度羽總（BAI）和國家隊總教練戈比昌德處事不公和偏袒，並從2012年開始一直在精神上騷擾她，並聲稱戈比昌德身為國家隊主教練，但同時卻經營私人羽毛球學院，由於他有權決定國家隊代表的入選資格，他可以挑選自己學院的學員進入國家隊，有關身分構成了利益和道德上的衝突<ref>{{en}}{{cite news|title=Prajakta-Gopichand controversy turns ugly as Jwala Gutta backs budding shuttler|url=http://indiatoday.intoday.in/story/pullela-gopichand-prajakta-sawant-jwala-gutta-backs-budding-shuttler/1/228480.html|newspaper=[[今日印度|今日印度]]|date=2012-11-09|access-date=2019-04-14|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180112061944/https://www.indiatoday.in/sports/other-sports/story/pullela-gopichand-prajakta-sawant-jwala-gutta-backs-budding-shuttler-121060-2012-11-09|archivedate=2018-01-12}}</ref>；事實上，戈比昌德就曾挑選排名比沙旺特低的[[普拉迪亚·加德雷|普拉迪亚·加德雷]]和[[阿帕娜·巴兰|阿帕娜·巴兰]]，而沙旺特卻落選<ref>{{en}}{{cite news|title=Top Badminton Champ Doesn't Feel Like Playing For India Anymore|url=https://www.mid-day.com/articles/top-badminton-champ-doesn-t-feel-like-playing-for-india-anymore/15310192|agency={{link-en|Mid Day|Mid Day}}|date=2014-05-20|accessdate=2019-04-14|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180112061955/https://www.mid-day.com/articles/top-badminton-champ-doesn-t-feel-like-playing-for-india-anymore/15310192|archivedate=2018-01-12}}</ref>。

'''戈爾諾普利'''（{{lang-bn|কর্ণফুলী}}，{{lang|en|Karnaphuli}}）是[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]]的一個[[烏帕齊拉|烏帕齊拉]]，位於[[吉大港專區|吉大港專區]]的[[吉大港縣|吉大港縣]]。該烏帕齊拉之同名塔納初建於2000年<ref>{{cite book |last=Baten |first=Md. Abdul |year=2012 |chapter=Karnafuli Thana |chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=Karnafuli_Thana |editor1-last=Islam |editor1-first=Sirajul |editor1-link=Sirajul Islam |editor2-last=Jamal |editor2-first=Ahmed A. |title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |edition=Second |publisher={{tsl|en|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh||Asiatic Society of Bangladesh}}}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Mahmud |first=Tarek |date=9 June 2012 |title=Ctg police stations in shabby state |url=http://archive.thedailystar.net/newDesign/news-details.php?nid=237517 |newspaper=The Daily Star}}</ref>，後轉為烏帕齊拉<ref>{{cite news |title=Karnaphuli becomes Bangladesh's 490th Upazila |url=https://bdnews24.com/bangladesh/2016/05/09/karnaphuli-becomes-bangladeshs-490th-upazila |newspaper=bdnews24.com |date=9 May 2016}}</ref>。該地之主要河流為[[戈爾諾普利河|戈爾諾普利河]]。

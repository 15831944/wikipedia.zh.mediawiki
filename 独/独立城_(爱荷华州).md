'''独立城'''（Independence）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[布坎南县_(爱阿华州)|布坎南县]]的城市和县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=2011-06-07 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531210815/http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=May 31, 2011 }}</ref>[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]中的人口为5966人，比2000年人口普查的6014人少。<ref name="USCB">{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |title=Population & Housing Occupancy Status 2010 |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] American FactFinder |accessdate=2011-06-17 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721034521/http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |archivedate=July 21, 2011 }}</ref><ref name="IADC">{{cite web|url=http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |title=Data from the 2010 Census |publisher=State Data Center of Iowa |accessdate=2011-06-17 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718201045/http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |archivedate=2011-07-18 }}</ref>

==地理==
据美国人口调查局统计，该市总面积为16.11平方公里，其中15.75平方公里为土地，0.36平方公里为水域。<ref name="Gazetteer files">{{cite web |title=US Gazetteer files 2010 |url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] |accessdate=2012-05-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/64vfLAeJ2?url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt |archivedate=2012年1月24日 }}</ref>
===气候===
这个地区一般季节性温差很大，夏季温暖热（往往潮湿），冬季寒冷（有时甚至严寒）。 降水年均相对较好地分布。 这种气候的[[柯本气候分类|柯本气候分类]]亚型是[[温带大陆性湿润气候|温带大陆性湿润气候]]。<ref>http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=250431&cityname=Independence+-+Airport%2C+Iowa%2C+United+States+of+America&units=</ref>

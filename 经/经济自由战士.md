'''经济自由战士'''（[[英语|英语]]：Economic Freedom Fighters，简称EFF）是一个[[南非|南非]][[左翼|左翼]]政党。前[[非洲人国民大会|非洲人国民大会]] (ANC) 青年联盟主席[[朱利叶斯·马勒马|朱利叶斯·马勒马]]（Julius Malema）被开除出非国大后，与他的的盟友在2013年创建。<ref name="Dispatch">{{cite news |title=Malema launches his Economic Freedom Fighters |author=Meggan Saville |url=http://www.dispatch.co.za/malema-launches-his-economic-freedom-fighters/ |newspaper=Dispatch Online |date=12 July 2013 |accessdate=16 July 2013}}</ref>马勒马已被宣布为该党“总司令”（commander in chief），指挥“中央司令部团队”的党中央结构。<ref name="BD">{{cite news |title=Malema takes command of Economic Freedom Fighters |author=Setumo Stone |url=http://www.bdlive.co.za/national/politics/2013/07/11/malema-takes-command-of-economic-freedom-fighters |newspaper=[[Business_Day_(South_Africa)|Business Day]] |date=11 July 2013 |accessdate=16 July 2013}}</ref>高级成员包括弗洛伊德·施范布（Floyd Shivambu）和报纸专栏作家安迪尔·姆恩格西塔马（Andile Mngxitama）。<ref name="EFF Central Command">{{cite web |author=Economic Freedom Fighters |date= |title=Central Command Team |url=http://effighters.org.za/structure/officials/ |website=http://effighters.org.za |accessdate=5 August 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130812200344/http://effighters.org.za/structure/officials/ |archivedate=2013年8月12日 }}</ref>

2013年7月28日，在[[索韦托|索韦托]]的新闻发布会上，马勒马宣布新政党已经有了超过1000的成员，双倍于独立选举委员会(IEC)所需的注册资格。<ref name="EFF 1000">{{cite news |title=EFF readies for election registration as over 1 000 members recruited |author=SAPA |url=http://www.timeslive.co.za/politics/2013/07/28/eff-readies-for-election-registration-as-over-1-000-members-recruited |newspaper=[[时代_(南非)|时代]] |date=28 July 2013 |accessdate=28 July 2013}}</ref>在[[新自由阵线|新自由阵线]] (FF+)被剔除选举资格后，成为参选政党之一。<ref name="It'sOfficial">{{cite news |title=It's official: Malema's EFF is a political party |author=Verashni Pillay |url=http://mg.co.za/article/2013-09-05-malemas-eff-to-contest-2014-elections |newspaper=[[Mail_&_Guardian|Mail & Guardian]] |date=5 September 2013 |accessdate=5 September 2013}}</ref>

==支持基础==
根据2013年11月益普索（Ipsos）调查显示，该党的支持者多为年轻人，24岁以下的占49%，绝大多数为黑人(99%)和男性，女性代表只有33%的支持基础。不成比例的支持者们多数是马勒马故乡林波波的同乡(28%)，而只有1%的人住在人口大省[[夸祖鲁-纳塔尔省|夸祖鲁-纳塔尔省]]。<ref name=ipsos>{{cite web|title=The supporter profiles of SA`s three largest parties|url=http://www.politicsweb.co.za/politicsweb/view/politicsweb/en/page72308?oid=534039&sn=Marketingweb+detail&pid=90389|publisher=Politicsweb|accessdate=6 February 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140222022154/http://www.politicsweb.co.za/politicsweb/view/politicsweb/en/page72308?oid=534039&sn=Marketingweb+detail&pid=90389|archivedate=2014年2月22日}}</ref>

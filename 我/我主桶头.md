'''我主桶头'''（英文：Lord Buckethead） 是一名英国{{link-en|搞笑政治家|Political satire}}。这名角色在三次[[英国大选|英国大选]]中分别和三名不同的[[保守党党魁_(英国)|保守党党魁]]同台竞选[[英国首相|英国首相]]职务。<ref>{{cite news|title=Who is Lord Buckethead, the man who stood against Theresa May? – BBC Newsbeat|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/newsbeat/article/40217791/who-is-lord-buckethead-the-man-who-stood-against-theresa-may|accessdate=9 June 2017|publisher=BBC Newsbeat|date=9 June 2017}}</ref>
{{TransH}}
Representing the Gremloids, a [[List_of_frivolous_political_parties|frivolous political party]], he ran against [[Margaret_Thatcher|Margaret Thatcher]] for parliament in [[Finchley_(UK_Parliament_constituency)#Elections_in_the_1980s|Finchley]] in 1987, against [[John_Major|John Major]] in [[Huntingdon_(UK_Parliament_constituency)#Elections_in_the_1990s|Huntingdon]] in 1992, and against [[Theresa_May|Theresa May]] in [[Maidenhead_(UK_Parliament_constituency)#Elections_in_the_2010s|Maidenhead]] in 2017.

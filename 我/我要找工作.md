《'''我要找工作'''》（{{lang-en|The Job}}，又译：'''为理想工作而战'''<ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2012/05/17/2012-broadcast-network-drama-keys-cbs-the-ideal-work-of-war-the-job/ 2012广播网新剧全接触：CBS——《为理想工作而战》（The Job）]</ref>）是一部[[美国|美国]][[真人秀|真人秀]]，2012年5月13日[[哥伦比亚广播公司|哥伦比亚广播公司]]宣布正式预订本节目并计划于2013年初播出<ref name="fc120513">{{cite news |url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2012/05/13/development-update-series-pick-up-elementary-friend-me-golden-boy-the-job-made-in-jersey-partners-vegas-cbs-198205/9780/ |title=Development Update: Series Pick Up - "Elementary," "Friend Me," "Golden Boy," "The Job," "Made in Jersey," "Partners," "Vegas" (CBS) |work=The Futon Critic |date=May 13, 2012}}</ref>。本节目将于2013年2月8日起在周五档期播出<ref name="premiere">{{cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/12/cbs-new-reality-series-the-job-gets-friday-8-pm-slot/|title=CBS's New Reality Series Gets 8 PM Slot|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=December 20, 2012|accessdate=December 20, 2012}}</ref>。

然而在播放了两集后CBS在2013年2月18日宣布将节目撤出档期<ref>[http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/02/18/cbs-pulls-the-job-after-two-episodes-704310/10211/ CBS Pulls "The Job" After Two Episodes]</ref>。

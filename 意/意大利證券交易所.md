'''意大利證券交易所'''（'''Borsa Italiana S.p.A.'''）是一家位于[[意大利|意大利]][[米蘭|米蘭]]的證券交易所，因位于[[梅扎诺特宫|梅扎诺特宫]]而又常稱爲“Piazza Affari”。它成立于1808年2月6日，直到1998年1月才私營化，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.londonstockexchange-ir.com/lse/news/releases/2007/2007-10-01a/|title=Borsa Italiana and London Stock Exchange Combine (archived 20.11.2007)|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071120092023/http://www.londonstockexchange-ir.com/lse/news/releases/2007/2007-10-01a/|archivedate=2007-11-20}}</ref> 2007年成爲[[倫敦證券交易所集團|倫敦證券交易所集團]]的一部分。<ref>[http://www.historytour.it/default.php?idp=s&ids=11&iddoc=172&tipodoc=articolo History Tour – Borsa Italiana]</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.londonstockexchange-ir.com/lse/news/releases/2007/2007-10-01a/|title=Borsa Italiana and London Stock Exchange Combine (archived 20.11.2007)|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071120092023/http://www.londonstockexchange-ir.com/lse/news/releases/2007/2007-10-01a/|archivedate=2007-11-20}}</ref>

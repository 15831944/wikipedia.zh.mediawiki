'''印尼雞湯'''（{{lang-id|Soto ayam}}）是一種黃色、帶香辣的雞湯，<ref>{{cite book | last=Von Holzen | first=H. | last2=Arsana | first2=L. | title=Authentic Recipes from Indonesia | publisher=Tuttle Publishing | year=2013 | isbn=978-1-4629-0535-5 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Pw3RAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA53 | accessdate=February 1, 2015 | page=53}}</ref>內有隆東飯（或稱[[馬來粽|馬來粽]]）、[[粉絲|粉絲]]<ref>{{cite book | last=Harpham | first=Z. | last2=Books | first2=M. | title=The Essential Wok Cookbook | publisher=Murdoch Books | year=2004 | isbn=978-1-74045-413-1 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=U_M8FVVCyh4C&pg=PA34 | accessdate=Feb 1, 2015 | page=34}}</ref>或麵條。流行於[[印尼|印尼]]、<ref name="Chow"/><ref name="Food"/>新加坡<ref name="FlavoursofSingapore">{{cite web |url=http://www.flavoursofsingapore.nl/recipe-soto-ayam.htm | title= Singapore Chicken Soto Soup (Singapore Soto Ayam)}}</ref>、[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]和<ref name=Detik-Malaysia>{{cite news | url=http://news.detik.com/berita/3083497/ini-haji-paijan-orang-indonesia-yang-populerkan-soto-ayam-di-malaysia | title=Ini Haji Paijan, Orang Indonesia yang Populerkan Soto Ayam di Malaysia (This is Haji Paijian, the Indonesian who Popularised Soto Ayam in Malaysia) | work=DetikNews | date=30 November 2015 | accessdate=21 June 2016}}</ref><ref name="PMO-Malaysia">{{cite news | url=http://www.pmo.gov.my/home.php?menu=newslist&news_id=11837&news_cat=13&cl=1&page=1731&sort_year=2013&sort_month= | title=PM CALLS ON MALAYSIANS TO SAFEGUARD PREVAILING HARMONY | date=8 August 2013 | agency=Bernama | accessdate=21 June 2016}}</ref>[[蘇里南|蘇里南]]。在湯中加入[[薑黃|薑黃]]為湯水帶來黃色。

這是印尼菜中最受歡迎的[[湯_(印尼)|湯品]]。除了雞肉和粉絲，其他配料有水煮[[雞蛋|雞蛋]]、炸馬鈴薯塊、唐芹葉和炸[[紅蔥頭|紅蔥頭]]。有時可加[[椰奶|椰奶]]。<ref name="Kruger 2014 p. 162"/>也可加入蝦片粉末和炸蒜頭或[[參巴醬|參巴醬]]。蝦片或[[恩兵|恩兵]]也是常見的配料。<ref name="soto ayam">{{cite web |url=http://www.resepsotoayam.com/ |title=Soto Ayam |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161026185624/http://www.resepsotoayam.com/ |archivedate=2016-10-26 |df= }}</ref>

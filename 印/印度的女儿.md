《'''印度的女儿'''》（{{lang-en|''India's Daughter''}}）是[[英国广播公司|英国广播公司]]（BBC）拍摄的一部[[纪录片|纪录片]]，为该电视台的系列节目「Storyville」當中的一集，讲述了[[印度黑公交轮奸案|2012年发生在印度德里的轮奸案]]。<ref name=SoulFilmMaker>{{cite news |title = Interview with Delhi gang rapist left 'stain on my soul', says British film maker |url = http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/asia/india/11446337/Interview-with-Delhi-gang-rapist-left-stain-on-my-soul-says-British-film-maker.html |accessdate=6 March 2015 |work=''[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]]'' |date=3 March 2015}}</ref>在该事件中，一名23岁的女学生被六名男性轮姦并杀害。<ref name=YoutubeBlock /><ref name=IndiaBansTV>{{cite news |title=India bans TV stations from showing interview with man who raped student |url = http://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/mar/03/india-outraged-interview-man-convicted-gang-rape-student |accessdate=6 March 2015 |work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|date=4 March 2014}}</ref>

该纪录片原计划于3月8日[[国际妇女节|国际妇女节]]当日在全球播出。对于此影片的数条新闻被报道后，[[印度政府|印度政府]]撤销了该片在印度的播放许可。BBC遵从了印度当局的决定，最终该片没有在印度播出。该片亦被上传到视频分享网站[[YouTube|YouTube]]，随即在社交网站上大面积传播。3月5日，印度政府屏蔽了在YouTube上的该段视频。<ref name=YoutubeBlock>{{cite news |title = India asks YouTube to block banned rape film |url = https://news.yahoo.com/delhi-rape-victims-father-urges-viewing-documentary-080939613.html |accessdate=6 March 2015 |work=[[Yahoo_News|Yahoo News]] |date=5 March 2015}}</ref><ref name=YoutubeBlocks>{{cite news |title = YouTube blocks BBC's Nirbhaya documentary video |url = http://ibnlive.in.com/videos/532286/youtube-blocks-access-to-bbc-documentary-nirbhaya-in-india.html |accessdate=6 March 2015 |work=[[India_Today|India Today]]|date=5 March 2015}}</ref>此纪录片以及印度政府的行为在全世界引起了广泛讨论。

==背景==
该影片讲述了2012年12月16日发生在南德里市的轮奸案。<ref name=Chaotic>{{cite news | url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2258306/Delhi-gang-rape-Five-men-charged-Jyoti-Singh-Pandey-murder-appear-court.html | title=Judge bars Delhi gang rape defendants from chaotic courtroom after 150 people cram into space meant for 30 | work=[[Daily_Mail|Daily Mail]] | date=7 January 2013 | accessdate=5 March 2015 | last=Hills | first=Suzannah}}</ref>事件受害者乔蒂·辛格（Jyoti Singh）在与一位男性朋友看完电影《[[少年派的奇幻漂流_(电影)|少年派的奇幻漂流]]》后搭上了一辆私人运营的公交车回家。<ref name=tragedy>{{cite news | url=http://www.spiegel.de/international/world/exploring-the-lives-of-the-rape-victim-and-suspects-in-india-a-879187.html|title= Rape Tragedy in India: Dreams of 'the Fearless One'|work=Der Spiegel|date=25 January 2013 |first1=Matthias | last1=Schulz | first2=Wieland | last2=Wagner}}</ref><ref name=Rahman>{{cite news|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/mar/03/india-outraged-interview-man-convicted-gang-rape-student|title=India bans TV stations from showing interview with man who raped student |work=The Guardian|date=2015-03-04|first=Maseeh | last=Rahman}}</ref>她在车上被六人殴打和轮奸，她的朋友也受到了身体伤害，随后两人被扔下车。受害者乔蒂在被人发现后送往医院，她先在印度接受治疗，后来被转移到新加坡，最终在2012年12月29日死于新加坡。<ref name=VicDies>{{cite news | url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-india-20860569 | title=Delhi gang-rape victim dies in hospital in Singapore | work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] | date=29 December 2012 | accessdate=5 March 2015}}</ref>该事件引起了媒体的广泛关注，激起了公众对印度政府没有提供对妇女足够保护的指责和抗议。<ref name=magnifies>{{cite news | url=http://www.nytimes.com/2012/12/30/world/asia/weak-response-of-india-government-in-rape-case-stokes-rage.html?pagewanted=all&_r=0 | title=<nowiki>Leaders’ Response Magnifies Outrage in India Rape Case</nowiki> | work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] | date=29 December 2012 | accessdate=5 March 2015 | last=Yardley | first=Jim}}</ref>

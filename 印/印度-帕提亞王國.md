'''印度-帕提亚王国'''为公元1世纪前后的一个王国，由冈多法勒斯王朝及其他一些[[中亚|中亚]]君主统治，疆域约为今[[阿富汗|阿富汗]]部分、[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]以及[[印度|印度]]西北部。大多数时期，冈多法勒斯王朝都居于[[塔克西拉|塔克西拉]]（今[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]][[旁遮普省|旁遮普省]]），但在王朝最后几年里，首都在[[喀布尔|喀布尔]]和[[白沙瓦|白沙瓦]]之间变动。虽然王国铸造的硬币多启发自[[安息帝國|安息帝國]]，但他们本身可能属于居住在[[帕提亚|帕提亚]]以东的[[伊朗人|伊朗人]]部落。并无证据显示所有国王都冠以「冈多法勒斯」（意为「荣耀获得者」）。此外，王国还以[[世界遗产|世界遗产]]——[[佛教|佛教]][[僧院|僧院]][[塔克特依巴依|塔克特依巴依]]而知名。

==历史==
[[File:GondopharesCoin.JPG|thumb]]

{{tsl|en|Gondophares|冈多法勒斯|冈多法勒斯一世}}起初可能为今伊朗东部{{tsl|en|Seistan|锡斯坦}}统治者。大约在公元前20年代<ref>The chronology of the Gondopharid kings has long been uncertain, predominantly based on coins. This reconstruction is based on "Indo-Scythian Coins and History IV" by Robert Senior, CNG 2006, as the four volumes of Senior's work provide an almost complete catalogue of the coinage of the period. Senior's chronology is based on the existence of only one king Azes, a theory that was vindicated when it was shown that a coin of the so-called Azes II was overstruck with a type attributed to Azes I (see Senior, "The final nail in the coffin of Azes II", Journal of the Oriental Numismatic Society 197, 2008).</ref>，其征服了原[[印度-塞西亞王國|印度-塞西亞王國]]的领土，这一切可能发生于印度-塞西亚王国重要统治者{{tsl|en|Azes|阿泽斯}}离世之后。冈多法勒斯所统治的区域囊括了[[阿拉霍西亞|阿拉霍西亞]]、{{tsl|en|Seistan|锡斯坦}}、[[信德省|信德]], [[旁遮普地区|旁遮普]]以及[[喀布尔|喀布尔]]谷，但应未超过[[旁遮普地区|旁遮普地区]]东部<ref>Rosenfield, p129</ref>。他自称为「众王之王」，此头衔恰当地反映出王国内部松散的结构：许多小王朝仍旧存在其中，得以保全，来换取他们对冈多法勒斯及其继任者承认与效忠。[[西薩特拉普王朝|西薩特拉普王朝]]踞于[[古吉拉特邦|古吉拉特]]，可能就与王朝相邻。

[[File:Takht-e-bahi.jpg|thumb]]]]
冈多法勒斯去世之后，王国开始分裂。{{tsl|en|Sarpedones}}（可能为前者之子）成为「冈多法勒斯二世」。尽管冈多法勒斯二世宣称自己为主要统治者，但其统治仍旧摇摇欲坠。他在信德、旁遮普东部、阿富汗南部的阿拉霍西亞发行了货币。之后冈多法勒斯的侄子{{tsl|en|Abdagases}}继位，但不久之后即为{{tsl|en|Orthagnes}}篡位，后者成为冈多法勒斯三世「伽达纳」。之后伽达纳的儿子Ubouzanes继位。公元20年后，{{tsl|en|Sases}}夺回Abdagases的位置，成为「冈多法勒斯四世」。[[塔克特依巴依|塔克特依巴依]]的题字正是他所书<ref>A votive inscription of the 26th year of Gudavhara or Gondophares, is reported to have been found on a stone at {{tsl|en|Takht-i-Bahi||Takht-i-Bahi}}, northeast of [[白沙瓦|Peshawar]] with a date in the year 103 of an unspecified era reckoning. This era is likely to have been the Malva or Vikrama era, founded in 57 BCE, this would give a date of 20 CE for this king's ascension (see {{tsl|en|Hindu calendar||Hindu calendar}}). The stone was formerly in the museum at [[拉合爾|Lahore]]. The point is especially important for those Christians who consider that a germ of history is embedded in the ''{{tsl|en|Acts of Thomas||Acts of Thomas}}.''</ref>。

此外，还有一些不甚知名的国王，如曾短暂篡位的Sanabares，再如也叫做Abdagases的国王等。

但是印度-帕提亚王国再未企及冈多法勒斯一世的高度，公元1世纪中期，[[丘就却|丘就却]]治下的[[貴霜帝國|貴霜帝國]]开始蚕食印度北部的领土。到最后一任国王{{tsl|en|Pacores||Pacores}}（约在公元100年之前）时<ref>Pacores is not dated by Senior in Indo-Scythian Coins and History, but as Senior’s chronology is generally antedates the Indo-Parthian kings by a few decades, it follows that Pacores is probably also earlier than the date 100-130 AD, that was previously suggested.</ref>，仅残余{{tsl|en|Seistan|锡斯坦}}及[[坎大哈|坎大哈]]。

==文学记载==
在一些古老的文学作品中对印度-帕提亚王国亦有所提及。在圣经《[[新约|新约]]》有关[[多馬_(使徒)|多马]]的故事中提到，多马曾在印度宫廷中为国王冈达法尔做过木匠<ref>see Senior, "The final nail in the coffin of Azes II".</ref>。其中冈达法尔应该即为冈多法勒斯。

古希腊哲学家[[提亚纳的阿波罗尼乌斯|提亚纳的阿波罗尼乌斯]]曾前往印度，并于约46年到往[[塔克西拉|塔克西拉]]。他描绘了此处希腊风格的建筑<ref>Description of the Hellenistic urbanism of Taxila:<br />
* "Taxila, they tell us, is about as big as Nineveh, and was fortified fairly well after the manner of Greek cities" [http://www.livius.org/ap-ark/apollonius/life/va_2_16.html#§20 (Life of Apollonius Tyana, II 20)] {{Wayback|url=http://www.livius.org/ap-ark/apollonius/life/va_2_16.html |date=20160310044312 }}
*"I have already described the way in which the city is walled, but they say that it was divided up into narrow streets in the same irregular manner as in Athens, and that the houses were built in such a way that if you look at them from outside they had only one story, while if you went into one of them, you at once found subterranean chambers extending as far below the level of the earth as did the chambers above." [http://www.livius.org/ap-ark/apollonius/life/va_2_21.html#§23 (Life of Apollonius Tyana, II 23)] {{Wayback|url=http://www.livius.org/ap-ark/apollonius/life/va_2_21.html |date=20160310023858 }}</ref>。在交谈中，冈多法勒斯提及自己从其父那里学得一口流利的希腊语<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.livius.org/ap-ark/apollonius/life/va_2_26.html |title=(Life of Apollonius Tyana, II 29) |access-date=2018-11-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160307154937/http://www.livius.org/ap-ark/apollonius/life/va_2_26.html |archive-date=2016-03-07 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.livius.org/ap-ark/apollonius/life/va_2_31.html |title=(Life of Apollonius Tyana, II 31) |access-date=2018-11-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303172439/http://www.livius.org/ap-ark/apollonius/life/va_2_31.html |archive-date=2016-03-03 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

《[[厄立特利亚海航行记|厄立特利亚海航行记]]》提到，冈多法勒斯于信德地区作战，在[[印度河|印度河]]河口一带，斯基提亚的重要城市明纳伽拉落入在帕提亚人手中，后者驱逐每一个异邦人离开。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.fordham.edu/halsall/ancient/periplus.html |title=Periplus of the Erythraean Sea, Chap 38 |access-date=2018-11-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140814160845/http://www.fordham.edu/halsall/ancient/periplus.html |archive-date=2014-08-14 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

对于战争的时间，[[塔克特依巴依|塔克特依巴依]]题词中有两个记法，其一为Guduvhara国王26年（推测为冈多法勒斯），另一个为未知纪年103年<ref>Rosenfield, p130.</ref>。

==考古研究==
在其货币及犍陀罗艺术品中，印度-帕提亚人被描绘为身着交叉的短夹克及宽松的长裤。<ref>Described in "Rome's enemies, Parthians and Sassanid Persians", {{ISBN|0-85045-688-6}}</ref>。他们的夹克装饰有一排排奖章等。他们通常头发浓密，并带有头带（这种做法在公元1世纪主要由帕提亚人采用）<ref>"Parthians, from about the 1st century AD, seem to have preferred to show off their carefully tonsured hair, usually only wearing a fillet of thick ribbon; before then, the Scythian cap or {{tsl|en|bashlyk||bashlyk}} was worn more frequently". In "Parthians and Sassanid Parthians" Peter Willcox {{ISBN|0-85045-688-6}}, p12</ref>。

在犍陀罗发现了许多石制带有雕刻的圆盘，代表着印度-帕提亚王国的艺术。这些雕刻结合了希腊和波斯的风格，被认为是帕提亚艺术特征的表现形式。但其只在[[印度-希臘王國|印度-希臘王國]]、印度-塞西亚王国和印度-帕提亚王国相对应的考古层中发现，在之前的[[孔雀王朝|孔雀王朝]]及之后的[[贵霜王朝|贵霜王朝]]并未发现。<ref>"Let us remind that in {{tsl|en|Sirkap||Sirkap}}, stone palettes were found at all excavated levels. On the contrary, neither Bhir-Mound, the [[孔雀王朝|Maurya]] city preceding Sirkap on the [[塔克西拉|Taxila]] site, nor Sirsukh, the [[貴霜帝國|Kushan]] city succeeding her, did deliver any stone palettes during their excavations", in "Les palettes du Gandhara", p89. "The terminal point after which such palettes are not manufactured anymore is probably located during the Kushan period. In effect, neither [[马图拉|Mathura]] nor Taxila (although the Sirsukh had only been little excavated), nor {{tsl|en|Begram||Begram}}, nor {{tsl|en|Surkh Kotal||Surkh Kotal}}, neither the great Kushan archaeological sites of Soviet [[中亚|Central Asia]] or [[阿富汗|Afghanistan]] have yielded such objects. Only four palettes have been found in Kushan-period archaeological sites. They come from secondary sites, such as Garav Kala and Ajvadz in Soviet Tajikistan and Jhukar, in the Indus Valley, and Dalverzin Tepe. They are rather roughly made." In "Les Palettes du Gandhara", Henri-Paul Francfort, p91. (in French in the original)</ref>

'''印第安納波利斯中央圖書館'''（{{Lang-en|Indianapolis Central Library}}）是[[美國|美國]][[印第安納州|印第安納州]]首府[[印第安納波利斯|印第安納波利斯]]的一座[[公共圖書館|公共圖書館]]，為{{link-en|印第安納波利斯公共圖書館|Indianapolis Public Library|印第安納波利斯公共圖書館}}22座分館中的旗艦館。

==簡介==
[[File:Indianapolis_Central_Library_(3440328630).jpg|thumb]]
印第安納波利斯中央圖書館的土地是由出身印州的美國作家{{link-en|詹姆斯·惠特康比·雷利|James Whitcomb Riley|詹姆斯·惠特康比·雷利}}所捐贈的，建築由[[建築師|建築師]]{{link-en|保羅·菲利普·克里特|Paul Philippe Cret|保羅·菲利普·克里特}}所設計，以[[希臘|希臘]][[多立克柱式|多立克柱式]]風格建造。內部材料包括印第安納石灰石，核桃木和白橡木，建築工程於1917年10月完成<ref name="SHAARD">{{cite web| url = https://secure.in.gov/apps/dnr/shaard/welcome.html| title = Indiana State Historic Architectural and Archaeological Research Database (SHAARD) | publisher = Department of Natural Resources, Division of Historic Preservation and Archaeology | format = Searchable database| accessdate = 2016-08-01}}</ref>，被認為是美國最優秀的圖書館建築之一<ref>[http://www.indypl.org/about/central/  Central Library Centennial]</ref>。

2001年，位於印第安納波利斯的{{link-en|Woollen, Molzan and Partners|Woollen, Molzan and Partners|Woollen, Molzan and Partners}}建築公司受委託對這座歷史建築進行翻新。其設計為增建新的6層樓現代化[[玻璃帷幕|玻璃帷幕]]大樓，並與原有的古典建築結合。施工問題導致工程曾在2004年暫時停止，經過翻新的中央圖書館及其新中庭最終於2007年12月9日開放<ref>{{cite journal| author=Megan Fernandez| title =The Pillar: Evans Woollen | journal =Indianapolis Monthly | volume = | issue = | page=73 | publisher = | location =Indianapolis, Indiana | date =June 2010| url = https://www.indianapolismonthly.com/features/the-pillar-evans-woollen/| accessdate =December 18, 2017}} See also: "Biographical" Sketch in {{cite book | author=| title =Woollen, Molzan and Partners, Inc. Architectural Records, ca. 1912–2011 | publisher =Indiana Historical Society | series = | volume = | edition = | year =2017 | location =Indianapolis | pages = | url =http://www.indianahistory.org/our-collections/collection-guides/woollen-molzan-partners-inc-architectural-records  | isbn =}}</ref><ref>[[Woollen,_Molzan_and_Partners|Woollen, Molzan and Partners]] website project page {{cite web |url=http://www.woollenmolzan.com/projectview.asp?pid=2&cid=1&st=3 |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2010-07-13 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718083115/http://www.woollenmolzan.com/projectview.asp?pid=2&cid=1&st=3 |archivedate=2011-07-18 }}</ref><ref name=Library>{{cite news|last=Swiatek|first=Jeff|title=Storybook ending?: Next chapter in Central Library saga could yield a commercial boom for surrounding area|url=http://www.indystar.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=2007712220380|date=December 21, 2007|newspaper=The Indianapolis Star|subscription=yes|accessdate=January 5, 2016}}</ref>。

2016年，Elle Decor雜誌將中央圖書館列入『美國50家最佳圖書館』名單<ref>[https://www.elledecor.com/life-culture/travel/g3291/best-libraries-in-every-state/?slide=14 THE 50 BEST LIBRARIES IN THE UNITED STATES] Elle Deco. 2016.</ref>。

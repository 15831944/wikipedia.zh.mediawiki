'''{{PAGENAME}}'''是源自美國的[[電單車|電單車]]品牌，生產線在1901至1953年間設於[[马萨诸塞州|马萨诸塞州]][[斯普林菲尔德_(马萨诸塞州)|斯普林菲尔德]]<ref name=IndianHistoryHome>[http://www.indianmotorcycle.com/History/HistoryHome/tabid/78/Default.aspx Indian History Home] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080913162912/http://www.indianmotorcycle.com/History/HistoryHome/tabid/78/Default.aspx |date=September 13, 2008 }}</ref><ref name=legal>Spelling as per [http://supreme.justia.com/us/283/570/ U.S. Supreme Court], 1929-31</ref>。1911年在{{link-en|曼島TT賽車|Isle of Man TT}}奪得頭三名的獎杯<ref name="Jacob DeRosier at The Motorcycle Hall of Fame">[http://www.motorcyclemuseum.org/halloffame/hofbiopage.asp?id=95 Jacob DeRosier at The Motorcycle Hall of Fame] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081216134845/http://www.motorcyclemuseum.org/halloffame/hofbiopage.asp?id=95 |date=2008-12-16 }}</ref>，其風頭一時無兩，亦造就它於1910年代成為全球最大的電單車生產商。1953年宣佈[[破產|破產]]，往後幾十年數度易手生意都未見好轉。2011年被{{link-en|北極星工業|Polaris Industries}}收購，生產線遷往[[愛荷華州|愛荷華州]]<ref name="MO_USA_12Chief1stPics">{{cite web| title       = First Pics of Polaris 2012 Indian Chief Released| first       = Bryan| last        = Harley| url         = http://www.motorcycle-usa.com/744/10925/Motorcycle-Article/First-Pics-of-Polaris-2012-Indian-Chief-Released.aspx| format      = aspx| agency      = |website   = motorcycle-usa.com| id          = | date        = August 30, 2011| accessdate  = October 28, 2011}}</ref>，並且推出了多個公路巡航設計的[[重型電單車|重型電單車]]型號。

'''印度－美國关系'''，是指[[印度|印度共和国]]和[[美国|美利坚合众国]]的[[國際關係學|国际关系]]  。

在21世纪，印度外交政策一直寻求利用印度的战略的自主权以维护主权权利，并促进国家利益在一个多极世界。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indianembassy.org/archives_details.php?nid=1743|title=Welcome to Embassy of India, Washington D C, USA|publisher=|accessdate=2 April 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.dpcc.co.in/pdf/civilnuclearcooperation.pdf|title=The tenets of India's independent foreign policy|author=|date=|work=dpcc.co.in|accessdate=March 25, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title=Retaining India's Strategic Autonomy|url=http://www.newindianexpress.com/columns/Retaining-Indias-Strategic-Autonomy/2015/09/09/article3017314.ece|work=Indian Express|date=8 September 2015}}</ref> 根据布什总统和奥巴马，联合国已经表明住印度的核心国家的利益，并承认未决问题。<ref>{{Cite news|title=One year of Modi Government: Us versus them|url=http://indianexpress.com/article/opinion/columns/one-year-of-narendra-modi-government-us-versus-them/|work=Indian Express|date=25 May 2015}}</ref>

通过技术分享，例如增加双边[[贸易|贸易]]和[[投资|投资]]合作在[[Global_security|全球安全]]事项，包括印度在决策上的事项的全球管理([[联合国安全理事会|联合国安全理事会]])、升级表示在贸易和投资论坛([[世界银行|世界银行]]，[[國際貨幣基金組織|货币基金组织]]，[[亚太经济合作组织|亚太经合组织]])、接纳入多边出口管制制度([[飛彈科技管制建制|导弹技术管制制度]]，[[瓦聖納協定|瓦塞纳尔安排]]，[[Australia_Group|澳大利亚集团]]) 和支持[[核供應國集團|核供应国集团]]，已经成为了許多增進美国–印度关系的关键里程碑。<ref>{{Cite news|title=Indo-US joint statement after Obama-Modi talks|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/resources/indous-joint-statement-after-us-president-barack-obama-and-prime-minister-narendra-modi-talks/article6821279.ece|work=The Hindu|date=25 January 2015}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title=An India-US Tussle Over Technology Transfer|url=http://thediplomat.com/2015/06/an-india-us-tussle-over-technology-transfer/|work=The Diplomat|date=11 June 2015}}</ref> 2016年，美国和印度签署了《物流交换协定备忘录》<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://thediplomat.com/2016/08/india-us-sign-logistics-exchange-agreement-what-you-need-to-know/|title=India, US Sign Logistics Exchange Agreement|work=[[The_Diplomat|The Diplomat]]|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2016/08/30/asia-pacific/key-step-toward-stronger-defense-ties-u-s-india-ink-military-logistics-agreement/|title=In key step toward stronger defense ties, U.S., India ink military logistics agreement|work=[[The_Japan_Times|The Japan Times]]|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-india-usa-military-idUSKCN114241|title=U.S., India sign military logistics agreement|work=[[Reuters|Reuters]]|accessdate=30 August 2016}}</ref> 並成為了防御合作伙伴。<ref name="India US Defence">{{Cite web|title=India, US finalise Major Defence Partner agreement|url=http://indianexpress.com/article/india/india-us-major-defence-partner-agreement-manohar-parrikar-ashton-carter-4418019/|work=[[The_Indian_Express|The Indian Express]]|accessdate=9 December 2016}}</ref>

根据[[盖洛普|盖洛普]]於2015年的年度世界事务调查，印度以71％得票率被认为是[[美國人|美国人]]第6喜欢的国家。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.gallup.com/poll/181961/canada-great-britain-americans-favored-nations.aspx|title=Canada, Great Britain Are Americans' Most Favored Nations|publisher=Gallup.com|date=2015-03-13}}</ref>於2017年盖洛普民意调查发现，有74％的美国人喜欢印度。<ref>{{Cite web|title=North Korea Remains Least-Popular Country Among Americans|url=http://news.gallup.com/poll/204074/north-korea-remains-least-popular-country-among-americans.aspx|publisher=Gallup.com|accessdate=6 February 2018|language=en-us}}</ref>

在2017年的双边贸易(商品和服务)上增长了9.8％，總價值为126.1亿[[美元|美元]]。其中印度出口到美国的物資總價值為$76.7亿美元，而美国的出口到印度物資總價值為49.4亿美元 <ref>http://www.business-standard.com/article/current-affairs/us-exports-to-india-increase-19-trump-admin-still-cries-foul-over-tariffs-118040500070_1.html</ref><ref>https://ustr.gov/countries-regions/south-central-asia/india# </ref>

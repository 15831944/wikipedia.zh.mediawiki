'''印度共产党（马列）红星'''（[[英语|英语]]：Communist Party of India (Marxist-Leninist) Red Star）是[[印度|印度]]的一个[[共产主义|共产主义]]政党。该党成立于2009年，分裂自[[印度共产党（马列）阶级斗争|印度共产党（马列）阶级斗争]]。<ref>''Red Star''. ''[https://web.archive.org/web/20120403084918/http://www.cpiml.in/home/files/Red%20Star/Red_Star_Sept_2011_pdf.pdf From the 8th Party Congress of the CPI(ML) in 1970 to 9th Congress in 2011 - an overview of developments]''</ref><ref>''The Hindu''. ''[http://www.hindu.com/2009/05/27/stories/2009052754930800.htm CPI(ML) unity breaks down]''</ref> 总书记是K.N. Ramchandran。<ref name="th">''The Hindu''. ''[http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/tp-otherstates/cpiml-red-star-to-contest-five-ls-seats-in-state/article5697869.ece CPI(ML) Red Star to contest five LS seats in State]''</ref> 该党是[[革命政党和组织国际协调|革命政党和组织国际协调]]成员。

在西孟加拉邦，该党派在班戈（Bhangar）反对正在进行的电网项目。<ref>https://scroll.in/article/828841/the-little-known-red-star-party-is-making-its-mark-in-bengal-with-the-bhangar-land-stir</ref> 虽然它的许多成员被逮捕，包括其妇女组织领导人沙米什哈·乔杜里（Sharmishtha Chowdhury），但这一运动仍在继续。<ref>http://indianexpress.com/article/india/bhangor-clash-cpiml-red-star-member-arrested-4491726/</ref>

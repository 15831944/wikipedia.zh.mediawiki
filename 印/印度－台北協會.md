'''印度－台北協會'''（{{lang-en|India-Taipei Association}}）是[[印度|印度]]對[[台灣|台灣]]派駐的[[實質大使館|代表機構]]，1995年成立<ref name="India Foreign Policy and Government Guide">[https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=bgGCCP5Fxg0C&pg=PA71&dq=India-Taipei+Association+(ITA)+was+established+in+Taipei+in+1995&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwi9h-n_osXLAhVEVBQKHcNDCUAQ6AEIUTAI#v=onepage&q=India-Taipei%20Association%20(ITA)%20was%20established%20in%20Taipei%20in%201995&f=true ''India Foreign Policy and Government Guide''], Volume 1, International Business Publications,  2001, page 71</ref>。

另外，台灣方面相應的是駐[[印度|印度]][[新德里|新德里]]的[[駐印度臺北經濟與文化中心|駐印度臺北經濟與文化中心]]<ref name="roc-taiwan">{{Cite web|url=http://www.roc-taiwan.org/IN|title=首頁 - Taipei Economic and Cultural Center in India 駐印度代表處|accessdate=2016-03-16|publisher=roc-taiwan.org}}</ref>。

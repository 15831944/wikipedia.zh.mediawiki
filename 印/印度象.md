'''印度象'''（''Elephas maximus indicus''）是[[亞洲象|亞洲象]]的[[亞種|亞種]]之一，生活在[[亞洲|亞洲]]大陸上。自1986年以來被[[IUCN|IUCN]]列爲瀕危物种<ref name=iucn>{{IUCN |assessor=Choudhury, A. |assessor2=Lahiri Choudhury, D. K. |assessor3=Desai, A. |assessor4=Duckworth, J. W. |assessor5=Easa, P. S. |assessor6=Johnsingh, A. J. T. |assessor7=Fernando, P. |assessor8=Hedges, S. |assessor9=Gunawardena, M. |assessor10=Kurt, F. |display-assessors=etal |year=2008 |id=7140 |taxon=Elephas maximus |version=2012.2}}</ref>。雖然叫做印度象，但實際其分佈範圍還包括[[泰國|泰國]]等東南亞國家及[[中國|中國]]。<ref name="sukumar93">Sukumar, R. (1993). [https://books.google.com/books?id=95MoRwdQlcYC&printsec=frontcover&hl=en#v=onepage&q&f=false ''The Asian Elephant: Ecology and Management''] Second edition. Cambridge University Press. ISBN 0-521-43758-X</ref>

== 特徵==
它體型比[[非洲象|非洲象]]要小<ref>Shoshani, J., Eisenberg, J. F. (1982). [http://www.science.smith.edu/departments/Biology/VHAYSSEN/msi/pdf/i0076-3519-182-01-0001.pdf ''Elephas maximus'']. Mammalian Species 182: 1–8.</ref>，其肩部高度在{{convert|2|-|3.5|m|ft}}之間，重量在{{convert|2000|-|5000|kg|lb}}之間。雌性通常小於雄性，有的雌性擁有較短的象牙<ref name="Shoshani">Shoshani, J. (2006). [https://books.google.com/books?hl=en&lr=%20&id=oCpiZA61tyQC&oi=fnd&pg=PA3&ots=2IMfIdn35g&sig=9LKyLo77Kb4BehHoiX_CSJsREXs#v=onepage&q&f=false ''Taxonomy, Classification, and Evolution of Elephants''] In: Fowler, M. E., Mikota, S. K. (eds.) Biology, medicine, and surgery of elephants. Wiley-Blackwell. ISBN 0-8138-0676-3. pp. 3–14.</ref>。

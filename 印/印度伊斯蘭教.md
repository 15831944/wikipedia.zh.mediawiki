'''[[伊斯兰教|伊斯兰教]]'''是'''[[印度|印度]]'''信仰人口第二多的宗教（僅次於[[印度教|印度教]]）。2011年統計顯示印度約有1.72億名穆斯林<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/religion.php|title=Religion Data - Population of Hindu / Muslim / Sikh / Christian - Census 2011 India|website=www.census2011.co.in|access-date=2017-07-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170711153053/http://www.census2011.co.in/religion.php|archive-date=2017-07-11|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name=":3">{{Cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/Muslim-population-growth-slows/article10336665.ece|title=Muslim population growth slows|work=The Hindu|access-date=2017-07-28|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170703073933/http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/Muslim-population-growth-slows/article10336665.ece|archive-date=2017-07-03|dead-url=no}}</ref>，2018年印度穆斯林人口約有201,000,000人，占[[印度人口|印度人口]]的15%、[[各國伊斯蘭教|世界穆斯林人口]]的11%（僅次於[[印尼|印尼]]）。

伊斯蘭教最早於七世紀由阿拉伯商人傳至印度西岸的[[馬拉巴爾海岸|馬拉巴爾海岸]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/Trade-not-invasion-brought-Islam-to-India/articleshow/2144414.cms|title='Trade, not invasion brought Islam to India' - Times of India|work=The Times of India|access-date=2017-07-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170505071910/http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/Trade-not-invasion-brought-Islam-to-India/articleshow/2144414.cms|archive-date=2017-05-05|dead-url=no}}</ref>、[[康坎|康坎]]與[[古吉拉特邦|古吉拉特]]等地<ref>{{cite book|last=Wink|first=André|title=Al-Hind, the making of the Indo-Islamic world|year=1990|publisher=E.J.

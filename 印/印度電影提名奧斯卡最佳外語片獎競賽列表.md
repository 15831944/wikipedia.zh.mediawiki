[[印度|印度]]是在1957年首次遞交[[奧斯卡金像獎|奧斯卡金像獎]]的[[奧斯卡金像獎最佳外語片獎|最佳外語片獎]]<ref>{{cite web |title=History of the Academy Awards – Page 2|url=http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/history02.html|publisher=[[Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts_and_Sciences|Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]]|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080406060609/http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/history02.html|archivedate=6 April 2008|accessdate=7 April 2013}}</ref>。奧斯卡最佳外語片獎由[[美國電影藝術與科學學院|美國電影藝術與科學學院]]頒發，為[[奧斯卡獎|奧斯卡獎]]中頒發給年度美國以外最佳非英語電影的獎<ref name="Rules">{{cite web|title=Rule Thirteen: Special Rules for the Foreign Language Film Award |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |publisher=[[Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts_and_Sciences|Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/86/rule13.html |accessdate=2013-08-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130822101822/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/86/rule13.html |archivedate=22 August 2013 |df= }}</ref>。

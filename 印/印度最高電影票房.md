'''印度最高電影票房'''是包括所有印度語言的[[印度電影|印度電影]]在全球的保守估計[[票房|票房]]紀錄列表，此乃根據信譽良好消息來源的報導。印度官方並無統計{{link-en|電影在印度本土的票房數字|List of highest-grossing films in India}}，而發布數據的印度網站時常被促使去增加國內票房預估的更新頻率<ref name="NoBO">{{cite news|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2013-11-23/news-interviews/44388852_1_weekend-numbers-box-office-numbers-small-films|title=Box Office column discontinued|last=Priya Gupta|date=23 Nov 2013|work=[[印度时报|The Times of India]]|accessdate=30 December 2013}}</ref>。

[[印度電影|印度電影]]已自20世紀初開始在{{link-en|全球市場|List of highest-grossing Indian films in overseas markets}}放映<ref name=Burra&Rao>* Burra, Rani Day & Rao, Maithili (2006), "Cinema", ''Encyclopedia of India (vol. 1)'', Thomson Gale, {{ISBN|978-0-684-31350-4}}.</ref> 。截至2003年，共有超過90個國家已放映過印度電影<ref name="Khanna">* Khanna, Amit (2003), "The Future of Hindi Film Business", ''Encyclopaedia of Hindi Cinema: historical record, the business and its future, narrative forms, analysis of the medium, milestones, biographies'', Encyclopædia Britannica (India) Private Limited, {{ISBN|978-81-7991-066-5}}. p 158</ref>。在2000~2010年間，票價穩定地上漲、戲院數增加了三倍、電影的發行量也有所增加，導致在票房方面大幅上漲<ref name="ETAug">{{cite news|url=http://articles.economictimes.indiatimes.com/2012-08-26/news/33386102_1_box-office-movie-rotten-tomatoes|title=Business of Rs 100-cr films: Who gets what and why|last=Binoy Prabhakar|date=26 Aug 2012|work={{tsl|en|Indiatimes||Indiatimes}} ''The Economic Times''|accessdate=30 December 2013}}</ref>。

列表上大多數電影是[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]([[印地語|印地語]])的電影。截至2014年，寶萊塢佔印度票房淨收入的43％，而{{link-en|泰盧固語|Telugu cinema}}和{{link-en|坦米爾語|Tamil cinema}}電影佔收入36％，剩餘地區佔21％。<ref name="deloitte">{{cite web|title=The Digital March Media & Entertainment in South India|url=http://www2.deloitte.com/content/dam/Deloitte/in/Documents/technology-media-telecommunications/in-tmt-economic-contribution-of-motion-picture-and-television-industry-noexp.pdf|publisher=Deloitte|accessdate=21 April 2014}}</ref>

'''{{le|印度副總統|Vice President of India}}'''乃憲法規定下[[印度政府|印度政府]]第二高的職位，僅比[[印度總統|印度總統]]為低。

[[印度宪法|印度宪法]]第65條指出：總統辭職、被免職、逝世、被彈劾、或未能履行總統職責時，副總統將會履行總統職責，作為應急方案。副總統亦擔任[[印度议会|议会]][[联邦院_(印度)|联邦院]]的[[官守議員|官守主席]]<ref name="nn">{{Cite news|url=https://www.newsnation.in/india-news/powers-and-responsibilities-of-vice-president-of-india-article-177178.html|title=Powers and responsibilities of Vice President of India|date=2017-07-17|accessdate=2019-03-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190302001814/https://www.newsnation.in/india-news/powers-and-responsibilities-of-vice-president-of-india-article-177178.html|archive-date=2019-03-02|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>
{{Cite news|url=https://www.jagranjosh.com/current-affairs/fact-box-vice-president-of-india-1502173602-1|title=Fact Box: Vice President of India|author=Jha|first=Jitesh|work=Dainik Jagran|date=2017-08-08|accessdate=2019-03-02|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171205003517/http://www.jagranjosh.com/current-affairs/fact-box-vice-president-of-india-1502173602-1|archivedate=2019-03-04|deadurl=no}}</ref>。

副總統是由選舉人團以[[可轉移單票制|可轉移單票]][[比例代表制|比例代表制]]的不記名投票方式選出，選舉人團由印度國會兩院所有議員組成，投票過程由{{le|印度選舉委員會|Election Commission of India|選舉委員會}}負責。副總統任期為五年，理論上可以無限期連任，直至繼位者就任為止<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.prsindia.org/theprsblog/examining-anti-trafficking-bill-2018|title=Following the elections of the Vice President of India|author=Relhan|first=Vibhor|publisher=PRS Legislative Research|date=2017-08-05|accessdate=2019-03-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190302001814/https://www.prsindia.org/theprsblog/examining-anti-trafficking-bill-2018|archive-date=2019-03-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>。若联邦院以「有效半數{{efn|即除空缺外的過半數議席<ref>{{Cite web|title=Types of Majorities Used in the Indian Parliament - Absolute, Effective, Simple, and Special Majority|url=https://www.clearias.com/majorities-indian-parliament/|work=ClearIAS|date=2016-02-05|accessdate=2019-03-18|language=en-US|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180611223509/https://www.clearias.com/majorities-indian-parliament/|archive-date=2018-06-11|dead-url=no}}</ref>}}」通過決議，副總統即可被免職<ref>
{{Cite web|url=https://rajyasabha.nic.in/rsnew/practice_procedure/book1.asp|title=The Upper House of Indian Parliament|publisher=Rajya Sabha|accessdate=2019-03-02|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180407101930/http://rajyasabha.nic.in/rsnew/practice_procedure/book1.asp|archivedate=2019-03-04}}
</ref>。副總統亦負責維護联邦院議員的權利和特權<ref name="nn">{{Cite news|url=https://www.newsnation.in/india-news/powers-and-responsibilities-of-vice-president-of-india-article-177178.html|title=Powers and responsibilities of Vice President of India|date=2017-07-17|accessdate=2019-03-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190302001814/https://www.newsnation.in/india-news/powers-and-responsibilities-of-vice-president-of-india-article-177178.html|archive-date=2019-03-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

自1950年副總統一職成立以來，共有13人擔任此職位。首位副總統為[[萨瓦帕利·拉达克里希南|萨瓦帕利·拉达克里希南]]，其在1952年3月13日於{{le|印度總統府|Rashtrapati Bhavan|總統府}}宣誓就職<ref>
{{Cite news|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/from-sarvepalli-radhakrishnan-to-venkaiah-naidu-all-the-vice-presidents-of-india/articleshow/59932876.cms|title=From Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan to Venkaiah Naidu: All the Vice Presidents of India|work=The Times of India|date=2017-08-05|accessdate=2019-03-02|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170911065932/http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/from-sarvepalli-radhakrishnan-to-venkaiah-naidu-all-the-vice-presidents-of-india/articleshow/59932876.cms|archivedate=2019-03-04|deadurl=no}}
</ref>。拉达克里希南後來亦擔任總統<ref>
{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1975/04/17/archives/radhakrishnan-of-india-philosopher-dead-at-86.html|title=Radhakrishnan of India, Philosopher, Dead at 86|author=Greenhouse|first=Linda|work=The New York Times|date=1975-04-17|accessdate=2019-03-02|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181212090229/https://www.nytimes.com/1975/04/17/archives/radhakrishnan-of-india-philosopher-dead-at-86.html|archivedate=2019-03-04|deadurl=no}}
</ref>。
1969年，[[扎基尔·侯赛因|扎基尔·侯赛因]]逝世，時任副總統[[瓦拉哈吉里·文卡塔·吉里|瓦拉哈吉里·文卡塔·吉里]]隨即請辭參加總統選舉，最終當選<ref name="it">{{Cite web|url=https://www.indiatoday.in/india/story/venkaiah-naidu-gopalkrishna-gandhi-vice-presidential-election-1024892-2017-07-18|title=Venkaiah Naidu vs Gopalkrishna Gandhi: 6 vice-presidents who went on to become presidents|work=India Today|date=2017-07-18|accessdate=2019-03-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190302001814/https://www.indiatoday.in/india/story/venkaiah-naidu-gopalkrishna-gandhi-vice-presidential-election-1024892-2017-07-18|archive-date=2019-03-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>。13位副總統中，其中6人最後都成功擔任總統<ref name="it" />。{{le|克里希納·康德|Krishna Kant}}是唯一一位任內逝世的副總統<ref>
{{Cite web|url=http://m.rediff.com/news/2002/jul/27vp1.htm|title=Krishan Kant is first vice-president to die in office|author=Jafri|first=Syed Amin|publisher=Rediff.com|date=2002-07-27|accessdate=2019-03-02|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181216031948/http://m.rediff.com/news/2002/jul/27vp1.htm|archivedate=2019-03-04}}
</ref>。2017年8月11日，[[溫凱亞·奈都|溫凱亞·奈都]]宣誓就任第13任印度副總統<ref>
{{Cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/venkaiah-naidu-sworn-in-as-vice-president/article19471240.ece|title=Venkaiah Naidu sworn in as Vice-President|date=2017-08-11|work=The Hindu|accessdate=2018-01-14|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140209173947/http://rstv.nic.in/rstv/aboutus.asp|archivedate=2019-03-04|deadurl=no}}
</ref>。

'''飞机信息管理系统'''（Airplane Information Management System，缩写'''AIMS'''）是[[波音777|波音777]]飞机的“大脑”。它采用四个[[ARINC|ARINC]] {{le|ARINC 629||629}}总线传输信息。每个机翼上各有两台机柜。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.boeing.com/boeing/commercial/777family/background/back6.page|title=777 Family: Flight Deck and Airplane Systems|accessdate=2013-10-05|publisher=[[Boeing|Boeing]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.stevens.edu/sse/sites/default/files/777%20Systems%20Integration.pdf|title=System Integration of the 777 Airplane Information Management System|accessdate=2013-10-05|date=April 1996|last=Witwer|first=Bob|publisher=[[Honeywell|Honeywell]]|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131007004801/https://www.stevens.edu/sse/sites/default/files/777%20Systems%20Integration.pdf|archivedate=2013-10-07}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.davi.ws/avionics/TheAvionicsHandbook_Cap_29.pdf|title=The Avionics Handbook: Boeing B-777|accessdate=2013-10-05|last=Morgan|first=Michael J.|year=2001|publisher=[[Honeywell|Honeywell]]}}</ref>

== 历史 ==
该系统最早使用[[X86|Intel 80x86]]处理器，结合[[Ada|Ada]]编程语言的[[编译器|编译器]]和[[运行时系统|运行时系统]]。从1988年起的多年来，{{le|霍尼韦尔航空运输系统公司|Honeywell Aerospace}}与{{le|DDC-I}}的顾问合作，将DDC-I Ada编译器改为{{le|AMD 29050|AMD Am29000}}架构编写和优化，用于全面开发。<ref name="rehmer">{{Cite book|title=Beautiful Teams: Inspiring and Cautionary Tales from Veteran Team Leaders|last=Rehmer|first=Karl|publisher=O'Reilly|year=2009|editor-last=Stellman|editor-first=Andrew|editor2-last=Greene|editor2-first=Jennifer|location=Sebastopol, California|pages=299–312|chapter=The HADS Team}}</ref>“飞机信息管理系统”软件可以说是目前最知名的民用与军用Ada项目。<ref name="queue">{{Cite news|url=http://queue.acm.org/detail.cfm?id=1035608|title=There's Still Some Life Left in Ada|last=Wolfe|first=Alexander|date=October 2004|work=[[ACM_Queue|ACM Queue]]}} Also at http://delivery.acm.org/10.1145/1040000/1035608/p28-wolfe.pdf{{dead link|date=June 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>霍尼韦尔有大约550名开发人员为此飞行系统工作。<ref name="ic-b777">{{Cite news|url=http://archive.adaic.com/projects/atwork/boeing.html|title=Boeing Flies on 99% Ada|publisher=Ada Information Clearinghouse|accessdate=October 24, 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151105021316/http://archive.adaic.com/projects/atwork/boeing.html|archivedate=November 5, 2015}}</ref>

== 功能 ==
===主要功能===
* 驾驶舱显示系统<ref>[https://aerospace.honeywell.com/zh-cn/products/cockpit-systems/airplane-information-management-system 飞机信息管理系统（AIMS）]，霍尼韦尔国际公司{{zh-cn}}</ref>（如[[主飞行显示器|主飞行显示器]]）
* 飞行管理系统
* [[推力|推力]]管理系统（[[自动油门|自动油门]]）
* 飞行状态监控系统
* 数据[[通信|通信]]管理（[[数据链|数据链]]）
* 驾驶舱通信
* 中央维护系统
* 飞行数据采集系统

'''扬·布洛克伊森'''（{{lang-nl|Jan Blokhuijsen}}，{{IPA-nl|jɑn ˈblɔkɦœysən}}，{{bd|1989年|4月1日||}}）生于北荷兰省朗戴克南斯哈尔沃德，是一名荷兰男子速度滑冰运动员。布洛克伊森于2009年11月在[[海伦芬|海伦芬]]完成个人世界杯分站赛首秀<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.speedskatingstats.com/index.php?file=skater&code=1989040101&item=wc|title=Jan Blokhuijsen|website=speedskatingstats.com|accessdate=2019-01-31|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190130150616/http://www.speedskatingstats.com/index.php?file=skater&code=1989040101&item=wc|archivedate=2019-01-30}}</ref>。他在以速度滑冰为主项的同时也有参加[[競速輪滑|競速輪滑]]的比赛<ref name="pyeongchang2018">{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/speed-skating/athlete-profile-n3036049-jan-blokhuijsen.htm|title=Speed Skating {{!}} Athlete Profile: Jan BLOKHUIJSEN|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-31|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180421163223/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/speed-skating/athlete-profile-n3036049-jan-blokhuijsen.htm|archivedate=2018-04-21}}</ref>。

2018年平昌冬奥，布洛克伊森获得一枚铜牌，来自于男子追逐赛项目。在该项目比赛结束后，他在记者会上称“希望韩国人善待他们的狗”，批评主办国[[韩国|韩国]]的狗肉消费<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.washingtonpost.com/sports/olympics/dutch-apologies-galore-after-double-faux-pas-at-olympics/2018/02/22/642b1b4e-17b5-11e8-930c-45838ad0d77a_story.html |title= Dutch apologies galore after double faux pas at Olympics |author= Raf Casert, AP |date= 2018-02-22 |publisher=Washington Post |accessdate=2019-01-31}}</ref>，引致韩国网民愤怒，不少网民纷纷指责其种族歧视及不尊重韩国文化<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/sports/2018/02/702_244583.html |title= Dutch skater sparks dog meat controversy at PyeongChang|date=2018-02-22|publisher=Korea Times|accessdate=2018-02-22}}</ref>。最终布洛克伊森在[[Twitter|Twitter]]上道歉，称自己无意冒犯韩国人，只是关心动物权益的保护<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dutchnews.nl/news/archives/2018/02/dutch-speed-skater-apologises-for-dog-eating-comment |title= Dutch speed skater apologises for dog-eating comment|date=2018-02-22 |publisher=Dutch News|accessdate=2018-02-22}}</ref>。

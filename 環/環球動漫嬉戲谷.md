'''环球动漫嬉戏谷'''是一个位于的中华人民共和国[[江苏省|江苏]][[常州市|常州]]南部的主题公园。<ref>{{Cite web|title=World Joyland: The Warcraft/Starcraft-themed amusement park|work={{tsl|en|Gothamist||shanghaiist}}|first=Jessica|author=Colwell|date=15 July 2011|url=http://shanghaiist.com/2011/07/15/joyland_the_blizzard_bits.php#photo-1|accessdate=12 December 2013}}</ref> 公园的主题是受视频游戏系列[[魔兽世界|魔兽世界]]和[[星海爭霸系列|星际争霸]]的啟發的。据报道建立费用为48兆美元{{Failed verification}}，并不是[[动视暴雪|动暴风雪]]正式授权或认可的。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/technology-21029851|work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|title=In pictures: Unlicensed World of Warcraft theme park in China|date=15 January 2013|accessdate=12 December 2013}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title=Video Tour of China's Unlicensed 'World of Warcraft' Theme Park|first=Mark|author=Milian|date=25 June 2013|work=[[Bloomberg_L.P.|Bloomberg]]|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/2013-06-25/video-tour-of-china-s-unlicensed-world-of-warcraft-theme-park.html|accessdate=12 December 2013}}</ref>

'''環口螺科'''（學名：{{lang|la|Cyclophoridae}}），又名'''山蝸牛科'''、'''旋風蝸牛科'''{{r|shelldoc_100325}}或'''叢螺科'''{{r|shelldoc_000008}}，是一個小型到大型的[[熱帶|熱帶]]陸地蝸牛的[[系統分類|系統分類]][[科_(生物)|科]]，而且牠們都有{{link-en|口蓋|operculum (gastropod)}}。根據2005年的《[[布歇特和洛克羅伊的腹足類分類_(2005年)|布歇特和洛克羅伊的腹足類分類]]》，牠們都屬於[[新進腹足類|新進腹足類]][[主扭舌目|主扭舌類]]非正式群組的{{link-en|陸地軟體動物|Terrestrial molluscs|陸地}}[[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]]；在2017年的《[[布歇特等人的腹足類分類_(2017年)|布歇特等人的腹足類分類]]》，牠們的分類層級不變。

這個包含很多物種的高度多樣性分類單元，現時只包括舊世界和新世界熱帶及亞熱帶的物種。

本科的化石物種歷史最早可以追遡至[[古近纪#生物|古近纪]]，正常腹足綱物種爆發時期<ref>{{cite book |last1=Gordon |first1=MS |last2=Olson |first2=EC |year=1995 |title=Invasions of the Land: The Transitions of Organisms from Aquatic to Terrestrial Life |publisher=Columbia University Press |location=New York |pages=312 pp |language=en }}</ref>。

==型態描述==
The dextral shells are mostly of small and rarely medium size. The form of the shell varies from discoidal to turbinate. 其圓形的[[殻口|aperture]]一般都有修飾：有時會有一個裂口，又或 a constriction. 其最後的{{link-en|螺層|Whorl (mollusc)|whorl}} can sometimes be disconnected  and then extends strongly from the winding plane. The {{link-en|口蓋|Operculum (gastropod)|operculum}} is generally circular, which can be retracted deeply into the shell. Its form is multispiral and can be calcified or lacking calcareous overlay. The outer layer of the operculum can contain accessory deposits.

軟體的頭部有一個短短的{{link-en|吻部|proboscis}}。 The tentacles are round in cross-section, relatively long and taper to the end. The eyes are located at the base of the antenna on flat papillae. The longitudinal muscular foot is not divided. The mantle cavity acts as a lung cavity.其[[齒舌#七種齒舌形態|扭舌型齒舌]]每列都有七顆舌齒： has seven elements per transverse row. The central row of the radula contains usually five, rarely three or seven teeth.The animals are <!-- [[dioecious|dioecious]] -->[[雌雄異體|雌雄異體]].

'''環甲肌'''（cricothyroid muscle、環甲狀肌、環盾肌）是[[喉|喉]]部輔助{{le|發聲|phonation}}的唯一張力肌肉。它附著於[[甲狀軟骨|甲狀軟骨]]的較低{{le|平面層|Planar lamina|層}}及[[甲狀軟骨|大角]]下、以及[[環狀軟骨|環狀軟骨]]的前外側，其作用在使[[甲狀腺|甲狀腺]]向前傾斜以幫助張緊的[[声带|声带]]。勿與{{le|後環勺肌|posterior cricoarytenoid muscle}}混淆；環勺後肌是直接負責打開（綁持）聲帶之間的空間裂隙致能呼吸的唯一肌肉。
<!--
==運作==
The cricothyroid muscle produces tension and elongation of the [[vocal_fold|vocal fold]]s by drawing up the arch of the cricoid cartilage and tilting back the upper border of the cricoid cartilage lamina; the distance between the vocal processes and the angle of the thyroid is thus increased, and the folds are consequently elongated, resulting in higher pitch [[phonation|phonation]].

==支配==
This muscle is the only laryngeal muscle supplied by the Superior laryngeal branch of the [[vagus_nerve|vagus nerve]] known as the [[superior_laryngeal_nerve|external branch of the superior laryngeal nerve]] (rather than the [[recurrent_laryngeal_nerve|recurrent laryngeal nerve]]).

'''均苯三甲酰氯'''，即1,3,5-苯三甲酰氯，是一种有机化合物，化学式为C<sub>9</sub>H<sub>3</sub>Cl<sub>3</sub>O<sub>3</sub>，是一种[[酰氯|酰氯]]。

==制备==
均苯三甲酰氯可由[[均苯三甲酸|均苯三甲酸]]和[[氯化亚砜|氯化亚砜]]回流反应得到。<ref>Wirth, David D. Thionyl chloride. ''e-EROS Encyclopedia of Reagents for Organic Synthesis'' (John Wiley & Sons, Ltd. Chichester, UK), 2001. pp 1-4. </ref>

均苯三甲酸和[[三氯氧磷|三氯氧磷]]于60°C反应也能得到该物质。<ref>Pan, Chunyue; Yu, Guipeng; Cui, Jidi; Liu, Qingshan; Zheng, Datian. Multi-​citric acid triester as plasticizer of plastics and rubber, and its preparation method[P]. 2014.11.26. CN 104163760</ref>

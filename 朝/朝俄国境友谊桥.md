'''朝俄国境友谊桥''' (朝鲜语： 우정의 다리;俄语: мост дружбы)是[[朝俄边界|朝鲜俄罗斯国境]]界河图们江上的一座铁路桥，连接俄罗斯[[哈桑铁路支线|哈桑铁路支线]][[哈桑站|哈桑站]]与朝鲜[[洪儀線|洪儀線]]和[[豆滿江線|豆滿江線]]的[[豆满江站|豆满江站]]。桥面铺设[[雙軌距|雙軌距]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.logistics.ru/9/7/i77_6557p0.htm|title=Железнодорожные переговоры {{!}} www.logistics.ru|website=www.logistics.ru|language=ru|access-date=2017-10-03}}</ref> <ref>Strategic Thinking about the Korean Nuclear Crisis 0230116396 G. Rozman - 2011 "In 2001–2002 Russia's importance was stressed by the North and it has since served as a “little counterweight” to the four ... Barely ten freight cars a week crossed the “Friendship Bridge” into North Korea as reconstruction funds budgeted in ..."</ref>。

2017年10月，沿桥铺设了俄朝国际通信光缆。由[[俄罗斯铁路|俄铁]]的子公司{{tsl|en|TransTelekom}}提供接入服务，以避免朝鲜对外电讯线路完全依赖于中国的尴尬。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.38north.org/2017/10/mwilliams100117/|title=Russia Provides New Internet Connection to North Korea {{!}} 38 North: Informed Analysis of North Korea|date=2017-10-01|work=38 North|access-date=2017-10-03|language=en-US}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/national-security/trump-signed-presidential-directive-ordering-actions-to-pressure-north-korea/2017/09/30/97c6722a-a620-11e7-b14f-f41773cd5a14_story.html|title=Trump signed presidential directive ordering actions to pressure North Korea|last=DeYoung|first=Karen|date=2017-09-30|work=Washington Post|access-date=2017-10-03|last2=Nakashima|first2=Ellen|language=en-US|issn=0190-8286|last3=Rauhala|first3=Emily}}</ref>

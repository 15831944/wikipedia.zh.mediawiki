'''朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学院''' ({{lang-ko|조선민주주의인민공화국 과학원}})或称'''国家科学院'''<ref name=choson-20160312/>，是[[朝鲜民主主义人民共和国|朝鲜民主主义人民共和国]]最高科學研究机构{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=36}}。

==历史==
[[朝鲜战争|朝鲜战争]]炮火纷飞的战乱时期，朝鲜科学院在1952年春季筹建。<ref name="Lankov2007">{{Cite web|last=Lankov|first=Andrei|date=1 April 2007|title=Academies|work=The Korea Times|url=http://times.hankooki.com/lpage/opinion/200704/kt2007040116363254140.htm|archive-url=http://www.nkeconwatch.com/2007/04/01/academies/|archive-date=1 April 2007}}</ref>1952年12月1日正式成立。{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=xxxi}}当时有9个研究所、43个研究室、10名院士、15名通讯院士。首任院长Hong Myng-hi 第二任院长Paek Nam-un.<ref name="Lankov2007"/>

1955年，开始和平利用原子能项目。<ref>{{cite book|title=North Korea: A Country Study|editor-last=Worden|editor-first=Robert L.|url=https://www.loc.gov/item/2008028547/#.pdf|edition=Fifth|date=2008|location=Washington|publisher=Federal Research Division of the Library of Congress|ISBN=978-0-8444-1188-0|page=260}}</ref> 1957年，苏联派遣原子能专家。<ref>{{cite book|ref=harv|editor-last1=Clay|editor-first1=James Moltz|editor-last2=Mansourov|editor-first2=Alexandre Y.|title=The North Korean Nuclear Program: Security, Strategy and New Perspectives from Russia|last=Zhebin|first=Alexander|chapter=A Political History of Soviet-North Korean Nuclear Cooperation|chapter-url=https://books.google.com/books?id=iPyeOflxTsMC&pg=PA30|year=2011|publisher=Routledge|location=New York|isbn=978-1-136-74991-9|page=30}}</ref>

1959年，在苏联援助下在[[宁边|宁边]]开始建设第一个研究堆。<ref>{{Cite book|editor-last=Savada|editor-first=Andrea Matles|date=1994|title=North Korea: A Country Study|edition=Fourth|location=Washington|publisher=Federal Research Division of the Library of Congress|url=https://ia800408.us.archive.org/28/items/PAM550-81/PAM550-81.pdf#212|ISBN=0-8444-0794-1|page=254}}</ref>

1970年代，大部分研究机构从平壤迁入郊区科学城：[[平城市|平城市]]。<ref>{{cite book|ref=harv|editor-last1=Clay|editor-first1=James Moltz|editor-last2=Mansourov|editor-first2=Alexandre Y.|title=The North Korean Nuclear Program: Security, Strategy and New Perspectives from Russia|last=Denisov|first=Valery I.|chapter=Nuclear Institutions and Organizations in North Korea|chapter-url=https://books.google.com/books?id=iPyeOflxTsMC&pg=PA23|year=2011|publisher=Routledge|location=New York|isbn=978-1-136-74991-9|page=23}}</ref> The Academy continues to be the most important center of the nuclear program.{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=36}}

1981年，从朝鲜科学院分立出[[朝鲜社会科学院|朝鲜社会科学院]]、[[朝鲜农业科学院|朝鲜农业科学院]]、[[朝鲜医学科学院|朝鲜医学科学院]]、[[轻工业科学分院|轻工业科学分院]].<ref name="Bunge1981">{{cite book|editor-last=Bunge|editor-first=Frederica M.|title=North Korea: A Country Study|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kba6AAAAIAAJ|edition=Third|year=1981|location=Washington|publisher=American University, Foreign Area Studies|oclc=855170869|page=92}}</ref> 

1999年1月11日, [[金正日|金正日]]视察朝鲜科学院，宣布当年为“科学年”。<ref name="Kim2012">{{cite book|author=Sung Chull Kim|title=North Korea under Kim Jong Il: From Consolidation to Systemic Dissonance|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=P3u7A04Cn-MC&pg=PA189|year=2012|publisher=State University of New York Press|location=Albany|isbn=978-0-7914-8093-9|page=189}}</ref>

==组织结构==
朝鲜科学院隶属于朝鲜内阁。{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=36}}<ref name="Lankov2007"/>设六个学部。{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=36}}<ref name="Lankov2007"/>以及科学出版社。<ref name="ZimKorEu">{{cite book|title=The Europa World Year: Kazakhstan – Zimbabwe|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=gP_-8rXzQs8C&pg=PA2483|year=2004|publisher=Europa Publications|location=London|isbn=978-1-85743-255-8|page=2483}}</ref>出版大约40种期刊。<ref name="Lankov2007"/> ''[[Kwahakwon_Tongbo|Kwahakwon Tongbo]]'' ({{Langnf||Korean|''Bulletins of the Academy of Sciences''|links=no}}), is the organ of its standing committee and is published six times a year.<ref name="ZimKorEu"/>
虽然有独立的[[朝鲜社会科学院|朝鲜社会科学院]]，但朝鲜科学院仍有下属的[[社会科学|社会科学]]领域的研究所，如语言学与古典著作翻译。{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=36}} 1962年建立{{tsl|en|Academy of Koryo Medicine|高丽医学科学院}}研究[[朝鲜传统医学|朝鲜传统医学]]。{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=244}}

院部设在平壤市[[西城区域|西城区域]]。<ref name="Martino2013">{{cite book|editor-last=Martino|editor-first=John|title=Worldwide Government Directory with Intergovernmental Organizations 2013|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=CQWhAQAAQBAJ&pg=PA890|year=2013|publisher=Sage Reference|location=Los Angeles|isbn=978-1-4522-9937-2|page=890}}</ref>大部分科研机构都在[[平城市|平城市]]。{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=36}}以及平壤市[[恩情区域|恩情区域]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nknews.org/2013/05/located-state-academy-of-sciences-turf-institute/|title=Located: State Academy of Science's Turf Institute|last=Melvin|first=Curtis|date=21 May 2013|accessdate=12 January 2017|website=NK News}}</ref>

平壤市保留了生物、建筑学、建材、电子与自动、轻工业等研究。<ref name="Corfield2014">{{cite book|last=Corfield|first=Justin |title=Historical Dictionary of Pyongyang|chapter-url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5zKWBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA3|date=1 December 2014|publisher=Anthem Press|location=London|isbn=978-1-78308-341-1|page=3|chapter=Academy of Sciences}}</ref>

[[咸興市|咸興市]]设有分院。{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=36}}

1994至1998年曾更名为国家科学院。{{sfn|Hoare|2012|p=36}}

朝鲜科学院官网只能在境内访问。<ref>{{cite book|title=Digital Review of Asia Pacific 2007/2008|chapter-url=https://books.google.com/books?id=wSlOkuwWKo4C&pg=PA246|year=2008|publisher=IDRC|isbn=978-0-7619-3674-9|page=246|chapter=.kp North Korea|author1=Kyungmin Ko|author2=Seungkwon Jang|author3=Heejin Lee}}</ref>

目前有40个研究所，200个研究室。<ref name="Lankov2007"/>50,000人员。<ref name="Madden2013">{{cite web|url=https://www.nknews.org/2013/10/analysis-north-koreas-proposed-special-economic-zones/|title=Analysis: North Korea’s proposed special economic zones|last=Madden|first=Michael|date=17 October 2013|accessdate=12 January 2017|website=NK News}}</ref> <ref name=choson-20160312>{{cite report |url=https://static1.squarespace.com/static/52dd9f05e4b0089d6701446a/t/571459b622482eca2d3ec3ab/1460951497983/CE+Annual+Report+2015+EN.pdf |title=Annual Report 2015 |publisher=Choson Exchange |date=12 March 2016 |accessdate=17 April 2017 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160422235851/http://static1.squarespace.com/static/52dd9f05e4b0089d6701446a/t/571459b622482eca2d3ec3ab/1460951497983/CE+Annual+Report+2015+EN.pdf |archivedate=2016年4月22日 }}</ref>

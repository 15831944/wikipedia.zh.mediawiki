'''[[朝鮮|朝鮮]]'''，由於缺乏可靠的研究、該國普遍的審查與廣泛的[[媒體操縱|媒體操縱]]而難以獲得可靠的統計數據，但朝鮮貧困的情形普遍被認為十分嚴重。曾在[[平壤|平壤]]生活过的瑞士商人费利斯·丹尼尔直言，北朝鲜最贫困的地方是[[咸镜北道|咸镜北道]]。

朝鮮的貧困經常被西方媒體所報導<ref name="Lee2001">{{cite book|author=Hy-Sang Lee|title=North Korea: A Strange Socialist Fortress|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=6Rx8Q_cxqvkC&pg=PR11|year=2001|publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group|isbn=978-0-275-96917-2|page=11}}</ref><ref name="Walker2012">{{cite book|author=Hugh Dyson Walker|title=East Asia: A New History|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=3Z3a0NU4RHMC&pg=PA657|date=November 2012|publisher=AuthorHouse|isbn=978-1-4772-6516-1|page=657}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/asia/northkorea/11260650/Six-charts-that-show-how-North-Korea-became-the-most-miserable-place-on-earth.html# |title=Six charts that show how North Korea became the most miserable place on earth|publisher=The Telegraph|access-date=2018-10-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181012134816/https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/asia/northkorea/11260650/Six-charts-that-show-how-North-Korea-became-the-most-miserable-place-on-earth.html# |archive-date=2018-10-12 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，其中多數提到了20世紀90年代中期影響該國的[[朝鮮飢荒|朝鮮飢荒]]<ref name="KwakJoo2014">{{cite book|author1=Professor Tae-Hwan Kwak|author2=Professor Seung-Ho Joo|title=North Korea and Security Cooperation in Northeast Asia|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eumKBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA65|date=28 March 2014|publisher=Ashgate Publishing, Ltd.|isbn=978-1-4724-1786-2|page=65}}</ref>。2006年的一份報告顯示，朝鮮每年估計需要530萬噸穀物但估計僅收穫450萬噸糧食，因此依靠外國援助來克服赤字<ref>{{cite web|title=A Matter of Survival：The North Korean Government’s Control of Food and the Risk of Hunger|publisher=Human Rights Watch|year=2006|url=https://www.hrw.org/sites/default/files/reports/northkorea0506webwcover.pdf|access-date=2018-10-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110810213856/http://www.hrw.org/sites/default/files/reports/northkorea0506webwcover.pdf#|archive-date=2011-08-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

北韓的貧困可歸因於[[極權主義|極權主義]]政權的國家治理不善<ref>"North Korea is the quintessential case of despotic rule leading to extreme poverty despite otherwise favorable economic potential. Quote from "Global Profile of Extreme Poverty ", Sustainable Development Solutions Network, [http://unsdsn.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/121015-Profile-of-Extreme-Poverty.pdf] {{Wayback|url=http://unsdsn.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/121015-Profile-of-Extreme-Poverty.pdf# |date=20180328201635 }}</ref>。

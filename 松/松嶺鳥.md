'''松嶺鳥屬'''（[[屬|屬]]名：{{lang|la|''Songlingornis''}}）是[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期的史前[[鳥類|鳥類]]。其[[化石|化石]]是在[[中國|中國]][[遼寧|遼寧]][[九佛堂组|九佛堂组]]（Jiufotang Formation）發現。<ref name=hou1997>{{Cite book|author=侯連海|year=1997|title=中國中生代鳥類|publisher=[[鳳凰谷鳥園|臺灣省立鳳凰谷鳥園]]|location=台灣, 南投|pages=pp.153}}</ref>九佛堂組的年代受到質疑，有可能是屬於[[阿普第階|阿普第階]]。其下只有一個[[物種|物種]]，就是'''凌河松嶺鳥'''。

松嶺鳥是[[燕鳥|燕鳥]]的近親，連同[[義縣鳥|義縣鳥]]祖成一個初期鳥類的[[分支|分支]]。<ref name=Mortimer2004>{{Cite web|author=Mortimer, Michael |year=2004 |publisher=The Theropod Database |url=http://home.comcast.net/~eoraptor/Phylogeny%20of%20Taxa.html |title=Phylogeny of taxa |accessdate=2008-08-14 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120630212337/http://home.comcast.net/~eoraptor/Phylogeny%20of%20Taxa.html |archivedate=2012-06-30 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref name=Clarke2006>{{Cite journal|author=Clarke, Julia A.; Zhou, Zhonghe & Zhang, Fucheng|year=2006|title=Insight into the evolution of avian flight from a new clade of Early Cretaceous ornithurines from China and the morphology of ''Yixianornis grabaui''|journal=Journal of Anatomy|volume=208|issue=3|pages=287-308|doi=10.1111/j.1469-7580.2006.00534.x|url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1469-7580.2006.00534.x/pdf}}</ref>它們有時會被認為與義縣鳥是同一[[屬|屬]]<ref name=Gong2004>{{Cite journal|author=Gong, Enpu; Hou, Lianhai & Wang, Lixia|year=2004|title=Enantiornithine Bird with Diapsidian Skull and Its Dental Development in the Early Cretaceous in Liaoning, China|journal=Acta Geologica Sinica|volume=78|issue=1|pages=1-7}}</ref>，但卻不被廣泛接受。<ref name=Mortimer2004/>

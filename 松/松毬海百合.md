'''松毬海百合'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Cupressocrinites'''''}}）是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[海百合|海百合]]，其[[化石|化石]]分布於[[亞洲|亞洲]]、[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]、[[歐洲|歐洲]]、[[摩洛哥|摩洛哥]]及[[北美|北美]]等地。<ref name="pl">{{cite book | title=Prehistoric Life: The Definitive Visual History of Life on Earth | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Palmer, Douglas | year=2009 | location=New York | isbn=9780756655730 | page=126 | edition=first American|display-authors=etal}}</ref>松毬海百合具有[[圓柱|圓柱]]形的莖節和[[圓錐|圓錐]]狀的萼，萼由兩圈相互交替的十塊大骨板組成。共有五支短小且無分叉的[[三角形|三角形]]肢臂，肢臂組合成短小的尖頭冠頂。<ref>{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=167 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

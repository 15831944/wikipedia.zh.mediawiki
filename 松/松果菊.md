  | title = Echinacea purpurea - (L.)Moench.
  | publisher = Plants For A Future
  | date = June 2004

==形态==
松果菊可以长到{{convert|120|cm}}高，生长在开阔的林地、草原甚至荒漠地带，对土壤干湿及酸碱度没有太高的要求，从春天到夏末都可能开花，具体时间取决于当地气候。野生的松果菊通常开紫红色花<ref name=b>[http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=1&taxon_id=220004561 Flora of North America, Eastern purple coneflower, ''Echinacea purpurea'' (Linnaeus) Moench, Methodus. 591. 1794.]</ref>，其种加词的意思就是“紫色的”<ref name="gledhill">Gledhill, David (2008). "The Names of Plants". Cambridge University Press. {{ISBN|9780521866453}} (hardback), {{ISBN|9780521685535}} (paperback).  pp 149, 321</ref>。

==用途==
===观赏===

[[File:Blick_Richtung_Süden_-_panoramio_(3).jpg|thumb]]

松果菊是一种较为常见的观赏植物，有许多个变种，其中“Ruby Giant”<ref>{{cite web|title=RHS Plant Selector - ''Echinacea purpurea'' 'Ruby Giant'|url=http://apps.rhs.org.uk/plantselector/plant?plantid=4725|accessdate=16 July 2013}}</ref>和“Elbrook”<ref>{{cite web
| url = https://www.rhs.org.uk/Plants/179552/i-Echinacea-purpurea-i-Font-Face-times-New-Roman-Elton-Knight-FONT-Elbrook-sup-(PBR)-sup/Details| title = RHS Plantfinder - ''Echinacea'' ‘Elbrook’ | accessdate = 14 February 2018}}</ref>曾获得[[皇家园艺学会|皇家园艺学会]]“优秀园艺奖”（Award of Garden Merit）。<ref>{{cite web  | url= https://www.rhs.org.uk/plants/pdfs/agm-lists/agm-ornamentals.pdf | title = AGM Plants - Ornamental | date = July 2017 | page = 33 | publisher = Royal Horticultural Society | accessdate = 6 February 2018}}</ref>

===药用===
[[美国原住民|美国原住民]]使用松果菊来治疗普通外伤、烧伤以及蚊虫咬伤，其根捣碎还可以用来当牙膏或治疗喉咙痛，内服可治咳嗽、胃痛、蛇咬伤。<ref>{{Cite journal |vauthors=Wang L, Waltenberger B, Pferschy-Wenzig EM, Blunder M, Liu X, Malainer C, Blazevic T, Schwaiger S, Rollinger JM, Heiss EH, Schuster D, Kopp B, Bauer R, Stuppner H, Dirsch VM, Atanasov AG | title = Natural product agonists of peroxisome proliferator-activated receptor gamma (PPARγ): a review | journal = Biochem. Pharmacol. | volume = 92 | issue = 1 | pages = 73–89 | date = 2014  | doi = 10.1016/j.bcp.2014.07.018 | pmid = 25083916 | pmc = 4212005 }}</ref>

现在在美国和欧洲松果菊仍然是常见药用植物之一，被普遍认为具有增强免疫力的作用<ref name=":0" /><ref name=中科院>{{cite web | url = http://www.cas.cn/kxcb/kpwz/201307/t20130731_3907492.shtml | title = 挥着“翅膀”的“松果”——松果菊| publisher = 中国科学院转载武汉植物园|date=2013-07-23}}</ref>。但一些研究显示松果菊似乎并没有太大的医疗作用<ref name="Hart & Dey">{{cite journal |vauthors=Hart A, Dey P |title=Echinacea for prevention of the common cold: an illustrative overview of how information from different systematic reviews is summarised on the internet |journal=Preventive Medicine |volume=49 |issue=2–3 |pages=78–82 |year=2009 |pmid=19389422 |doi=10.1016/j.ypmed.2009.04.006 |url=}}</ref><ref name="LancetInfDis_Shah">{{cite journal|title=Evaluation of echinacea for the prevention and treatment of the common cold: a meta-analysis |journal=The Lancet Infectious Diseases |date=July 2007 |author1=Sachin A Shah |author2=Stephen Sander |author3=C Michael White |author4=Mike Rinaldi |author5=Craig I Coleman |volume=7 |issue=7 |pages=473–480 |pmid=17597571 |doi= 10.1016/S1473-3099(07)70160-3 |issn=1473-3099 }}</ref><ref name="Woelkart et al.">{{cite journal |vauthors=Woelkart K, Linde K, Bauer R |title=''Echinacea'' for preventing and treating the common cold |journal=Planta Medica |volume=74 |issue=6 |pages=633–7 |date=May 2008 |pmid=18186015 |doi=10.1055/s-2007-993766 |url=|pmc=4068831 }}</ref><ref name="Kar2014">{{cite journal |vauthors=Karsch-Völk M, Barrett B, Kiefer D, Bauer R, Ardjomand-Woelkart K, Linde K |title=Echinacea for preventing and treating the common cold |journal=Cochrane Database Syst Rev |volume=2 |issue= |pages=CD000530 |year=2014 |pmid=24554461 |pmc=4068831 |doi=10.1002/14651858.CD000530.pub3 |type=Systematic review}}</ref>，而且可能会使患者产生过敏反应，导致出疹子、气喘，甚至出现生命危险。<ref name=NCCIH>{{cite web | url = http://nccih.nih.gov/health/echinacea/ataglance.htm | title = Echinacea | publisher = National Center for Complementary and Integrative Health}}</ref>

'''松下信治'''（{{jpn|j=松下信治|kn=まつした のぶはる|rm=Matsushita Nobuharu}}，{{bd|1993年|10月13日|catIdx=Nobuharu, Matsushita}}）是一名[[日本|日本]][[賽車手|賽車手]]，2014年[[日本三級方程式錦標賽|日本三級方程式錦標賽]]冠軍。

== 職業生涯 ==
=== 卡丁車 ===
松下生於[[日本|日本]][[埼玉縣|埼玉縣]][[埼玉市|埼玉市]]，自2005年展開賽車生涯，駕駛[[卡丁車|卡丁車]]參賽全日少年卡丁車錦標賽（{{lang|ja|全日本ジュニアカート選手権}}）。2008年，他參加卡丁車公開大師賽（{{lang|ja|オープンマスターズカート}}），取得{{link-jp|ARTA|ARTA}}挑戰賽級別的冠軍。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nobuharu.com/about/|title=About Me|publisher=nobuharu.com|language=ja|accessdate=2017-06-16|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618125334/http://www.nobuharu.com/about/|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no}}</ref>2009年，松下在全日卡丁車錦標賽（{{lang|ja|全日本カート選手権}}）的{{le|KF1|KF1}}級別取得第7名，接著在隔年參加Super-KF級別獲得第3名的成績，並結束了卡丁車生涯。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.honda.co.jp/HFDP/race2017/formation/matsushita_nobuharu/|title=松下信治|publisher=[[Honda|Honda]]|work=honda.co.jp|language=ja|accessdate=2017-06-16|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618125342/http://www.honda.co.jp/HFDP/race2017/formation/matsushita_nobuharu/|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no}}</ref>

=== 青年冠軍方程式及日本方程式挑戰賽 ===
2011年，松下晉升至[[單人座賽車|單人座賽車]]賽事，為Super License車隊參加[[青年冠軍方程式|青年冠軍方程式]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gicgd.com/news_view.asp?id=5872|title=Formula Pilota（赛手学院方程式）参赛车手名单|work=gicgd.com|publisher=[[广东国际赛车场|广东国际赛车场]]|accessdate=2017-06-18|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618131537/http://www.gicgd.com/news_view.asp?id=5872|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>他缺席了[[鄂爾多斯國際賽車場|鄂爾多斯]]分站，不過他的表現在回歸後變得更加出色。他在[[雪邦國際賽道|雪邦]]贏得賽季的收官站冠軍，並在該季車手積分榜上名列第4。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.driverdb.com/championships/standings/formula-pilota-china/2011/|title=Formula Pilota China 2011 standings|work=driverdb.com|publisher=Driver Database|accessdate=2015-02-13|language=en}}</ref>

他在2012年轉戰{{le|日本方程式挑戰賽|Formula Challenge Japan}}，整季12場分站中10度登上頒獎台，其中包含5場分站冠軍，最終取得該賽季的冠軍。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.driverdb.com/championships/standings/formula-challenge-japan/2012/|title=Formula Challenge Japan 2012|publisher=Driver Database|accessdate=2015-02-13|language=en}}</ref>

=== 日本三級方程式錦標賽 ===
2013年，松下加入HFDP車隊並晉級至[[日本三級方程式錦標賽|日本三級方程式錦標賽]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://world.honda.com/news/2013/c130208Motorsports-Overview/index.html|date=2013-02-08|accessdate=2015-02-13|work=[[Honda|Honda]] Motor Co., Ltd|publisher=Honda Motor Co., Ltd|title=Honda 2013 Motorsports Overview|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304085734/http://world.honda.com/news/2013/c130208Motorsports-Overview/index.html|archivedate=2016-03-04|deadurl=yes|language=en}}</ref>賽季結束後，他取得5次頒獎台完賽並在積分榜上名列第5，是[[本田|本田]]車手中表現最佳的一位。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.driverdb.com/standings/19-2013/|title=Japanese Formula 3 Championship – Championship Class 2013|work=driverdb.com|publisher=Driver Database|accessdate=2013-08-24|language=en}}</ref>

隔年，松下決定續留該隊並繼續出戰這項系列賽。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://world.honda.com/motorsports/activities/2014/2014HondaGlobalMotorSportsActivitiesAutomobile.pdf|date=2014-02-07|accessdate=2014-02-07|work=[[Honda|Honda]] Motor Co., Ltd|publisher=Honda Motor Co., Ltd|title=2014 Honda Global Motor Sports Activities Automobile[PDF]|format=PDF|language=en}}</ref>他在{{le|茂木賽車場|Twin Ring Motegi|茂木}}、[[富士國際賽車場|富士]]及{{le|菅生賽車場|Sportsland SUGO|菅生}}站贏得勝利，在積分榜上以12分之差擊敗{{link-jp|山下健太|山下健太}}，贏得年度冠軍。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.paddockscout.com/paddockscout-roundup-gp2gp3-sochi-f3f4-imola/|title=PaddockScout Roundup: GP2/GP3 Sochi, F3/F4 Imola|last=Allen|first=Peter|date=2014-10-13|publisher=Paddock Scout|accessdate=2015-02-13|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618134801/http://www.paddockscout.com/paddockscout-roundup-gp2gp3-sochi-f3f4-imola|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>

=== GP2系列賽 ===
松下於2015年加入{{le|ART大獎賽|ART Grand Prix}}車隊，首度在[[GP2系列賽|GP2系列賽]]亮相。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://world.honda.com/news/2015/c150213Motorsports-Overview/index.html|date=2015-02-13|accessdate=2015-02-13|work=[[Honda|Honda]] Motor Co., Ltd|publisher=Honda Motor Co., Ltd|title=Honda 2015 Motorsports Program Overview|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618134805/http://world.honda.com/news/2015/c150213eng.html|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>他在賽季首場分站[[巴林國際賽道|巴林]]取得第2的排位，與隊友[[斯托弗·范多恩|斯托弗·范多恩]]一同在隔日的正賽中從頭排發車，<ref name=autosportquali>{{cite web|last=Straw|first=Edd|title=McLaren F1 junior Stoffel Vandoorne takes Bahrain GP2 pole|url=http://www.autosport.com/news/report.php/id/118551|work=Autosport|publisher=Haymarket Publications|date=2015-04-17|accessdate=2016-09-29|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304074939/http://www.autosport.com/news/report.php/id/118551|archivedate=2016-03-04|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=GP2: Spotlight on Vandoorne with fifth consecutive pole|url=http://www.crash.net/gp2/news/217736/1/spotlight-on-vandoorne-with-fifth-consecutive-gp2-pole.html#Gl3PKMUiXZWdEBVA.99|work=crash.net|date=2015-04-17|accessdate=2016-09-29|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151217071256/http://www.crash.net/gp2/news/217736/1/spotlight-on-vandoorne-with-fifth-consecutive-gp2-pole.html#Gl3PKMUiXZWdEBVA.99|archivedate=2015-12-17|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>最終，他在兩場比賽中皆拿下積分，並在衝刺賽中取得最快圈速紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|title=GP2 Series 2015 Results - Sakhir|url=http://www.gp2series.com/Results/?raceid=949&seasonid=172|work=GP2 Series|accessdate=2016-03-14|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160314143022/http://www.gp2series.com/Results/?raceid=949&seasonid=172|archivedate=2016-03-14|deadurl=yes|language=en}}</ref>來到[[紅牛賽道|紅牛賽道]]，松下在衝刺賽取得第3名的成績，首度登上GP2的頒獎台。<ref>{{cite web|title=GP2: Implacable Haryanto denies Vandoorne his Austria sweep|url=http://www.crash.net/gp2/race-report/220292/1/implacable-haryanto-denies-vandoorne-his-austria-sweep.html|work=crash.net|date=2015-06-21|accessdate=2017-06-16|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618134836/http://www.crash.net/gp2/race-report/220292/1/implacable-haryanto-denies-vandoorne-his-austria-sweep.html|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Edd|last=Straw|title=Red Bull Ring GP2: Rio Haryanto denies Stoffel Vandoorne double win|url=http://www.autosport.com/news/report.php/id/119610|work=Autosport|date=2015-06-21|accessdate=2017-06-16|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618134842/http://www.autosport.com/news/report.php/id/119610|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>他於{{le|匈牙利賽道|Hungaroring}}的衝刺賽中取得首座冠軍，同時幫助ART車隊以一、二名完賽。<ref>{{cite web|title=GP2: Maiden win for Matsushita at the Hungaroring|url=http://www.crash.net/gp2/race-report/221399/1/maiden-gp2-win-for-matsushita-at-the-hungaroring.html|work=crash.net|date=2015-07-26|accessdate=2017-06-16|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618135128/http://www.crash.net/gp2/race-report/221399/1/maiden-gp2-win-for-matsushita-at-the-hungaroring.html|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>最終，他在年度積分榜名列第9。<ref>{{cite web|title=Driver Standings - GP2 Series |url=http://www.gp2series.com:80/Standings/Driver/ |work=GP2 Series |accessdate=2015-12-18 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151218204733/http://www.gp2series.com/Standings/Driver/ |archivedate=2015-12-18 |deadurl=yes |language=en }}</ref>

2016年2月，ART車隊證實松下將續留該隊，並與同為2015年新秀的[[謝爾蓋·希洛金_(賽車手)|謝爾蓋·希洛金]]搭檔。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gp2series.com/News-Room/News/2016/02_February/ART-Grand-Prix-confirm-Matsushita-for-a-second-season/|title=ART Grand Prix confirm Matsushita for a second season|work=[[GP2_Series|gp2series.com]]|publisher=GP2 Motorsport Limited|date=2016-02-12|accessdate=2016-02-12|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160215231407/http://www.gp2series.com/News-Room/News/2016/02_February/ART-Grand-Prix-confirm-Matsushita-for-a-second-season/|archivedate=2016-02-15|deadurl=yes|language=en}}</ref>到了賽季的第4站奧地利，松下因為前一場巴庫分站的危險駕駛舉動而遭到禁賽。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gp2series.com/News-Room/News/2016/06_June/Matsushita-suspended-for-next-event/ |date=2016-06-19 |accessdate=2016-06-21 |work=[[GP2_Series|GP2 Series]] |publisher=GP2 Series Limited |title=Matsushita suspended for next event |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160622025449/http://www.gp2series.com/News-Room/News/2016/06_June/Matsushita-suspended-for-next-event/ |archivedate=2016-06-22 |deadurl=yes |language=en }}</ref>

2017年2月13日，松下再度與ART車隊簽約，為該隊出賽GP2在2017年更名的{{le|國際汽車聯盟二級方程式錦標賽|FIA Formula 2 Championship}}，這是松下為ART出賽的第三個GP2賽季。<ref>{{cite web|title=ART re-sign Matsushita - Formula 2|url=http://www.fiaformula2.com/News-Room/News/2017/02_February/ART-re-sign-Matsushita-/|work=fiaformula2.com|date=2017-02-13|accessdate=2017-06-18|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618135317/http://www.fiaformula2.com/News-Room/News/2017/02_February/ART-re-sign-Matsushita-/|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Jamie|last=Klein|title=Matsushita, Fukuzumi retained by ART for 2017|url=https://www.motorsport.com/fia-f2/news/matsushita-fukuzumi-retained-by-art-for-2017-867614/|work=motorsport.com|date=2017-02-13|accessdate=2017-06-18|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618135321/https://www.motorsport.com/fia-f2/news/matsushita-fukuzumi-retained-by-art-for-2017-867614/|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>

===一級方程式賽車===
2016年2月20日，松下簽約成為[[麥拉倫車隊|麥拉倫車隊]]的開發車手。<ref>{{cite web|first=Jonathan|last=Noble|url=http://www.motorsport.com/f1/news/matsushita-named-as-mclaren-f1-test-driver-674527/|title=Matsushita named as McLaren F1 test driver|date=2016-02-20|work=motorsport.com|accessdate=2016-02-20|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618135335/https://www.motorsport.com/f1/news/matsushita-named-as-mclaren-f1-test-driver-674527/|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>

2017年2月22日，[[本田車隊|本田]]宣布松下將在[[2017年世界一級方程式錦標賽|2017年賽季]]繼續擔任麥拉倫-本田車隊的測試兼開發車手。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://world.honda.com/news/2017/c170222eng.html|title=Nobuharu Matsushita To Continue McLaren-Honda as Formula One Test and Development Driver|date=2017-02-22|work=[[Honda|Honda]]|accessdate=2017-06-18|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170618135339/http://world.honda.com/news/2017/c170222eng.html|archivedate=2017-06-18|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>

2017年7月24日，隨著[[薩伯車隊|薩伯車隊]]宣布將在下一賽季使用本田引擎後，該隊也宣布松下將在[[2017年匈牙利大獎賽|2017年匈牙利大獎賽]]後的季中測試第二日，駕駛{{le|薩伯C36|Sauber C36|C36}}進行測試，這也是他的一級方程式賽車測試處女秀。<ref>{{cite web|first=Jonathan|last=Noble|url=https://www.motorsport.com/f1/news/matsushita-sauber-hungary-test-933992/|title=Matsushita joins Sauber for Hungary test|date=2017-07-24|work=Motorsport.com|accessdate=2017-07-25|language=en}}</ref>

'''狗爬式游泳'''，又称'''狗刨'''、'''狗仔式'''，是一种游泳姿势。游泳员將胸部向下臥平，头露出水面，前后反覆移動手和腿，像[[狗|狗]]和其他[[哺乳动物|哺乳动物]]游泳一样。<ref>{{cite book|title=Boys' Own Book: A Complete Encyclopedia of Athletic, Scientific, Outdoor and Indoor Sports|year=1881|publisher=J Miller|author=William Clarke|url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=RiAMAAAAYAAJ}}</ref>這種泳姿就像是在水中的「疾行」<ref>{{cite book|title=At the Water's Edge: Fish with Fingers, Whales with Legs, and How Life Came Ashore But Then Went Back to Sea|author=Carl Zimmer|publisher=[[Simon_&_Schuster|Simon & Schuster]]|year=1999|isbn=0-684-85623-9|url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=OZZpBy-lLTgC|page=183}}</ref>

它是古人類使用的第一個泳姿，據信是自觀察動物而習得。<ref>{{cite book|title=Breakthrough Swimming: Stroke Mechanics, Training Methods, Racing Techniques|page=12|author=Cecil Colwin|publisher=Human Kinetics|year=2002|isbn=0-7360-3777-2}}</ref> 埃及史前的[[洞穴壁畫|洞穴壁畫]]裡的人物做的可能就是狗爬式。<ref>{{cite book|title=Olympic Swimming and Diving|author=Greg Kehm|page=4|isbn=1-4042-0970-0|publisher=Rosen|year=2007}}</ref>

狗爬式通常是小孩學習游泳的第一種泳姿。<ref>{{cite book|title=A Framework for Physical Education in the Early Years|author=H. Manners, M. E. Carroll|year=1995|publisher=Routledge|isbn=0-7507-0417-9|page=110|url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=2945yExihiEC}}</ref>

因為進行狗爬式泳姿時雙臂及雙腿不會打破水面，所以成為有靜默需求的軍事泳姿之一。<ref>{{cite book|title=Survival at Sea|author=U.S. Departments of the Army and Air Force|year=1950|publisher=United States Government Printing Office|page=58|url=http://www.scribd.com/doc/76323994/Survival-at-Sea-1950}}</ref>

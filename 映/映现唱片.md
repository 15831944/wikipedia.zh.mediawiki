== 历史 ==
厂牌于2010年4月11日由[[电子音乐|电子音乐]]制作人DJ Hardwell所创办。<ref name="RR">{{Cite web|url=http://revealedrecordings.com/about-us/|title=Revealed Recordings|accessdate=2013-10-16|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016180556/http://revealedrecordings.com/about-us/|archivedate=2013-10-16}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.dailypioneer.com/vivacity/hardwell--knocks-again.html|title=Hardwell  knocks again|accessdate=2016-12-21}}</ref>映现唱片的曲风主要专注于[[Big_room_house|大房浩室]]、[[流行舞曲|流行舞曲]]及[[電子浩室|电子浩室]]。

厂牌取得了一些成功，包括最高登上了[[荷兰40强单曲榜|荷兰40强单曲榜]]的第26名的《Apollo》（《阿波罗》，由Hardwell制作，[[安芭·謝普赫爾德|安芭·謝普赫爾德]]演唱）<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.top40.nl/hardwell-featuring-amba-shepherd/hardwell-featuring-amba-shepherd-apollo_23797|title=hardwell featuring amba shepherd - apollo|accessdate=October 16, 2013|publisher=top40.nl}}</ref>；Hardwell 2014年的单曲《[[Dare_You_(song)|Dare You]]》（《你敢》）同样取得了成功并夺得了[[英國單曲排行榜|英國單曲排行榜]]的第18名。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/charts/singles-chart/20140112/7501/|title=Official Singles Chart Top 100 {{!}} Official Charts Company|accessdate=2016-12-21}}</ref>而且，《Dare You》 同样用于诸如《Strictly Come Dancing》的电视节目广告中。{{Fact|date=October 2016}}

2015年映现唱片启动了名为《映现电台》（英文名为Revealed Radio）的电台节目，每周由来自不同厂牌的不同艺术家主持，首先由Hardwell开始。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmusic/article/Revealed-Recordings-Launches-New-Radio-Show-Revealed-Radio-20150306|title=Revealed Recordings Launches New Radio Show 'Revealed Radio'|date=March 6, 2015}}</ref>映现唱片已有超过200张曲目发布。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.musictimes.com/articles/50624/20151012/hardwell-mad-world-review-revealed-records-boss-200-releases.htm|title=Hardwell 'Mad World' Review: Revealed Records Boss Celebrates 200 Releases|last=Ryan|first=Middleton|date=2015-10-12|work=Music Times|newspaper=Music Times|accessdate=2017-01-30}}<code style="color:inherit; border:inherit; padding:inherit;">|work=</code>和<code style="color:inherit; border:inherit; padding:inherit;">|newspaper=</code>只需其一 ([[Help:引文格式1错误#redundant_parameters|帮助]])
[[Category:含有冗余参数的引用的页面|Category:含有冗余参数的引用的页面]]</ref>

2016年，映现唱片发布了清唱包以庆祝厂牌成立六周年。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youredm.com/2016/05/01/hardwell-celebrates-6-years-revealed-recordings-acapella-pack/|title=Hardwell Has An EPIC Free Gift For Fans In Celebration Of A Big Milestone - Get It Here|accessdate=2017-01-30|date=2016-05-01|last=Kusnierek|first=Timmy}}</ref>

2018年，映现唱片宣布将成立名为宝石唱片（Gemstone Records）的全新子厂牌。宝石厂牌将专注于电子音乐的[[流行_(音樂類型)|流行]]一面。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwmusic/article/Revealed-Recordings-Announce-Brand-New-Label-Gemstone-Records-20180410/|title=Revealed Recordings Announce Brand New Label Gemstone Records|accessdate=2018-04-25|date=2018-04-10}}{{Dead link|date=2019年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

'''剝脫性骨軟骨炎'''（{{lang-en|osteochondritis dissecans}} 普遍縮寫為'''OCD'''或'''OD'''）是一種[[軟骨|關節軟骨]]和下層的[[骨骺|軟骨下骨]]中形成碎裂的[[關節|關節]]疾病<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.medterms.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=11789|title=Definition of Osteochondritis dissecans|accessdate=2009-02-20|author=Shiel WC Jr|publisher=MedicineNet, Inc.}}</ref>，肇因於軟骨下骨區的血運障礙。缺乏血液導致軟骨下骨發生[[股骨頭缺血性壞死|股骨頭缺血性壞死]]（avascular necrosis），而後被人體再吸收，造成覆蓋在表面的關節軟骨受損、退化。最終，軟骨和骨質的碎裂（[[解剖|解剖體]]），以及關節空隙中骨軟骨碎片的自由游動，會引發疼痛或造成更嚴重的損傷<ref name="pmid7273527">{{cite journal|author=Pappas AM|title=Osteochondrosis dissecans|journal=Clinical Orthopaedics and Related Research|issue=158|pages=59–69|year=1981|pmid=7273527}}</ref><ref name="pmid1157398">{{cite journal|author=Woodward AH, Bianco AJ|title=Osteochondritis dissecans of the elbow|journal=Clinical Orthopaedics and Related Research|issue=110|pages=35–41|year=1975|pmid=1157398|doi=10.1097/00003086-197507000-00007|volume=110}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|last=Pettrone|first=FA|year=1986|title=American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons Symposium on Upper Extremity Injuries in Athletes|location=St. Louis, Missouri|publisher=CV Mosby|isbn=978-0801600265|pages=193–232}}</ref>。

對人類而言，剝脫性骨軟骨炎是一種[[罕見疾病|罕見疾病]]，每年總母體群平均每10萬人中只有15到30人會發病<ref name="pmid9012566">{{cite journal|author=Obedian RS, Grelsamer RP|title=Osteochondritis dissecans of the distal femur and patella|journal=Clinical Journal of Sports Medicine|volume=16|issue=1|pages=157–74|pmid=9012566|doi=10.1016/S0278-5919(05)70012-0|date=January 1997}}</ref>。雖然罕見，它卻是[[青少年|青少年]]運動性關節傷害的主要病因之一。青少年由於骨骼仍在發育，骨骼具有修繕受損或壞死骨骼組織及軟骨、即醫學上稱為骨重塑（bone remodeling）的能力，他們比成年人更容易復原<ref name="Inoue1991">{{cite journal|author=Inoue, Goro|title=Bilateral osteochondritis dissecans of the elbow treated by Herbert screw fixation|journal=British Journal of Sports Medicine|volume=25|issue=3|pages=142–144|year=1991|month=|url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC1478853/pdf/brjsmed00027-0032.pdf|pmid=1777781|accessdate={{Nowrap|9 January}} 2010|doi=10.1136/bjsm.25.3.142|pmc=1478853}} See introduction and discussion sections on incidence</ref>。膝關節是最常罹患剝脫性骨軟骨炎的關節部位，約佔75%的病例。根據[[哈科特港大學|哈科特港大學]]研究，由於當地飲用及灌溉水源受到化學汙染，剝脫性骨軟骨炎病況在[[奈及利亞|奈及利亞]]農村地區有漸增的趨勢，尤其好發於婦女群體<ref name="ijrr">{{cite journal|doi=10.1097/00004356-198406000-00004|last=Enwemeka|first=Chukuka S.|date=June 1984|title=Physical deformities in Nigerian schools: the Port Harcourt Cohort study|journal=International Journal of Rehabilitation Research|volume=7|issue=2|pages=163–172|issn=0342-5282|oclc=4554227|pmid=6238005|accessdate=August 18, 2010}}</ref>。

動物也會患有剝脫性骨軟骨炎，特別是[[馬|馬]]<ref name="Thomas2002">{{cite journal|author=Thomas, Heather Smith|title=Osteochondritis Dissecans in Thoroughbreds. Weanlings: A Field Study|journal=California Thoroughbred|volume=|issue=|pages=65–67|url=http://www.ctba.com/02magazine/aug02/HORSECARE.pdf|pmid=|accessdate={{Nowrap|9 January}} 2010|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061021235616/http://www.ctba.com/02magazine/aug02/HORSECARE.pdf|archivedate=2006年10月21日|deadurl=yes|date=August 2002}}</ref>；某些[[品種|品種]]的馬匹可能具有OCD相關基因，強迫發育的餵食和篩選出高壯體型的人為育種也是因素之一。剝脫性骨軟骨炎也常見於狗——尤其[[德國牧羊犬|德國牧羊犬]]<ref name="Ch.84OCD">{{cite book |author=Lenehan TM, Van Sickle DC|editor=Nunamaker DM, Newton CD (eds.)|chapter=Chapter 84: Canine osteochondrosis|chapterurl=http://cal.vet.upenn.edu/projects/saortho/chapter_84/84mast.htm|title=Textbook of small animal orthopaedics|url=http://cal.vet.upenn.edu/projects/saortho/index.html|publisher=Lippincott|location=Philadelphia|year=1985|isbn=0-397-52098-0}}</ref>，是中大型犬種肘關節發育不良的主因<ref name="Pead and Guthrie">{{cite web|last=Matthew Pead and Sue Guthrie|title=Elbow Dysplasia in dogs – a new scheme explained|url=http://www.bva.co.uk/public/documents/chs_elbow.pdf|publisher=[[British_Veterinary_Association|British Veterinary Association]] (BVA)|accessdate=2010-07-16|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111002022122/http://www.bva.co.uk/public/documents/chs_elbow.pdf|archivedate=2011-10-02}}</ref>。

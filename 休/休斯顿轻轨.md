'''休斯顿轻轨'''（METRORail），是在[[美国|美国]][[德克萨斯州|德克萨斯州]][[休斯顿|休斯顿]]地区由[[哈里斯县都会区交通局|哈里斯县都会区交通局]]（Metropolitan Transit Authority of Harris County，简称“METRO”）运营的一个[[轻轨|轻轨]]系统。该系统只有一条线路，16个车站，总长{{convert|7.5 |mi|km|2}} ，日客流量3.6万人。<ref name="Monthly Ridership Report">{{citation | author = Prepared by Ridership Analysis and Service Evaluation | contribution = Fiscal Year 2012 Monthly Ridership Report METROBus and METRORail | title = Monthly Ridership Report | publisher = METRO | page = 17 | date = 3/7 | year = 2012| id = | contribution-url = http://www.ridemetro.org/News/Documents/pdfs/Ridership%20Reports/2012/0112_Ridership_Report_FY12.pdf}}</ref><ref name="Urbanrail">{{cite web | url=http://urbanrail.net/am/hous/houston.htm | title=Urbanrail | accessdate=2013-01-20}}</ref>

==线路==
该系统现正进行大规模扩张，正在向北、东、东南三个方向延长，预计延长线将在2015年完成。<ref name="Urbanrail">{{cite web | url=http://urbanrail.net/am/hous/houston.htm | title=Urbanrail | accessdate=2013-01-20}}</ref><ref name="rail">{{cite web|title=休斯顿轻轨将会延长（英文） |url=http://www.rail.co/2011/12/08/900m-awarded-to-extend-houstons-light-rail-system/|publisher=Rail.co|accessdate=December 9, 2011 |date=December 8, 2011}}</ref>

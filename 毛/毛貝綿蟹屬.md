'''毛貝綿蟹屬'''（{{lang|la|'''''Hirsutodynomene'''''}}）是[[螃蟹|螃蟹]]的一個屬，屬於[[綿蟹派|綿蟹派]][[綿蟹總科|綿蟹總科]][[贝绵蟹科|贝绵蟹科]][[贝绵蟹亚科|贝绵蟹亚科]]的一個分支，原來本屬物種均為[[贝绵蟹属|贝绵蟹属]]（''Dynomene''）的物種<ref name=WoRMS_204960>{{cite WoRMS |id=204960 |title=''Dynomene'' Desmarest, 1823 |accessdate=2015-09-26 |author1=Vanden Berghe, Edward |author2=Martinez, Olga |author3=Davie, Peter |date=2011-01-18 }}</ref><ref name=zootaxa>{{Zootaxa |2008|1850|1|26|last1=Guinot |first1=Danièle |date=2008-07-02 |title=A re-evaluation of the Dynomenidae Ortmann, 1892 (Crustacea, Decapoda, Brachyura, Podotremata), with the recognition of four subfamilies }}</ref>。本屬的[[模式種|模式種]]是：[[刺贝绵蟹|刺贝绵蟹]] ''Dynomene spinosa'' <small>(Rathbun, 1911)</small><ref name=WoRMS_391604>{{cite WoRMS |id=391604 |title=''Hirsutodynomene'' McLay, 1999  |accessdate=2015-09-26 |author1=Davie, Peter |date=2011-01-19 }}</ref><ref name=WoRMS_440134>{{cite WoRMS |id=440134 |title=''Hirsutodynomene spinosa'' (Rathbun, 1911) |accessdate=2015-09-26 |author1=Davie, Peter |date=2009-12-17 }}</ref>。

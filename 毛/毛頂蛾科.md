'''毛頂蛾科'''（'''Eriocraniidae'''）是[[鱗翅目|鱗翅目]]下的一個[[科|科]]，有6個現存[[屬|屬]]。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Donald R. Davis |year=1978 |title=A revision of the North American moths of the superfamily Eriocranioidea with the proposal of a new family, Acanthopteroctetidae (Lepidoptera) |journal=[[Smithsonian_Contributions_to_Zoology|Smithsonian Contributions to Zoology]] |volume=251 |pages=1–131 |url=http://www.sil.si.edu/smithsoniancontributions/Zoology/pdf_hi/SCTZ-0251.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |author=Hitomi Mizukawa, Toshiya Hirowatari & Satoshi Hashimoto |year=2004 |title=Biosystematic study of ''Issikiocrania japonicella'' Moriuti (Lepidoptera: Eriocraniidae), with description of immature stages |journal=[[Entomological_Science|Entomological Science]] |volume=7 |issue=4 |pages=389–397 |doi=10.1111/j.1479-8298.2004.00088.x}}</ref> 這種蛾在白天飛行，常見於溫帶的夏季，它們由[[喙|喙]]可以協助吸水，幼蟲是[[山毛櫸目|山毛櫸目]]的[[潛葉蟲|潛葉蟲]]（尤其是[[樺木屬|樺木屬]]和[[櫟屬|櫟屬]]），在[[楊柳目|楊柳目]]和[[薔薇目|薔薇目]]的樹上則少見。<ref>{{cite book |author=N. P. Kristensen |year=1999 |chapter=The homoneurous Glossata |pages=51–64 |editor=N. P. Kristensen |title=Lepidoptera, Moths and Butterflies.

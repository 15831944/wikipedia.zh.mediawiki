'''女同性恋，男同性恋，双性恋和跨性别（[[LGBT|LGBT]]）'''人群在'''毛里塔尼亚'''面临着法律问题。[[毛里塔尼亚|毛里塔尼亚]]将男性或女性的同性间性行为视为犯罪。[[男男性接触人群|与男性发生性关系]]的穆斯林男子面临[[石刑|石刑]]（一种钝击致死的死刑执行方式），而[[女女性行为|与女性发生性关系]]的妇女则面临监禁。<ref>{{cite news |title=Here are the 10 countries where homosexuality may be punished by death |first=Terri |last=Rupar |newspaper=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]] |date=2014-02-24 |url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/worldviews/wp/2014/02/24/here-are-the-10-countries-where-homosexuality-may-be-punished-by-death/ |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6QhZAkj4Z?url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/worldviews/wp/2014/02/24/here-are-the-10-countries-where-homosexuality-may-be-punished-by-death/ |archivedate=2014-06-29 |quote=Mauritania: Muslim men engaging in homosexual sex can be stoned to death, according to a 1984 law. Women face prison. |deadurl=yes }}</ref>

==有关同性性行为的法律==
{{See also|LGBT與伊斯蘭教}}

[[毛里塔尼亚|毛里塔尼亚]]适用[[伊斯兰教法|伊斯兰教法]]。<ref>{{cite web|title="Compilation prepared by the Office of the High Commissioner for Human Rights in accordance with paragraph 15(b) of the annex to Human Rights Council resolution 5/1: Mauritania"|publisher=United Nations Human Rights Council, Working Group on the Universal Periodic Review, Ninth session, Geneva, A/HRC/WG.6/9/MRT/2 |date=2010-08-10|url=http://lib.ohchr.org/HRBodies/UPR/Documents/session9/MR/A_HRC_WG.6_9_MRT_2_Mauritania_eng.pdf|page=3}}</ref>

根据1983年《刑法典》第308条的非正式英文译本“任何与同性发生不自然关系的成年穆斯林男子将面临公开石刑的处罚。”<ref>{{cite journal|title= Deciphering Motive: Establishing Sexual Orientation as the 'One Central Reason' for Persecution in Asylum Claims|work= Columbia Law School|volume= 43|issue= 2|first= Ritu|last= Ghai|year= 2012|url= http://www3.law.columbia.edu/hrlr/hrlr_journal/43.2/Ghai.pdf|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140509234158/http://www3.law.columbia.edu/hrlr/hrlr_journal/43.2/Ghai.pdf|archivedate= 2014-05-09 }}</ref>{{rp|522}}第308条的法文正式文本为“Tout musulman majeur qui aura commis un acte impudique ou contre nature avec un individu de son sexe sera puni de peine de mort par lapidation publique（任何成年穆斯林男子与和他的性别相同的个体发生猥亵行为或违反自然的行为会被判处公开石刑惩罚。”<ref>{{cite web|title="Ordonnance 83-162 du 09 juillet 1983 portant institution d'un Code Pénal"|publisher=''Journal Officiel de la République Islamique de Mauritanie n° 608-609'' |date=1984-02-29|url=http://www.wipo.int/wipolex/en/text.jsp?file_id=194180|page=63}}</ref>

==生存现状==
[[美国国务院|美国国务院]]2011年人权报告注意到：“这一年没有关于LGBT人群的刑事起诉。没有证据表明社会暴力、社会歧视或基于性取向的系统性政府歧视。没有组织倡导性取向或性别认同的权利，但这些群体的运作没有任何法律障碍。”<ref>{{cite web|title=''2011 Country Reports on Human Rights Practices: Mauritania''|publisher=Bureau of Democracy, Human Rights and Labor, U.S. Department of State |date=2011|url=https://www.state.gov/documents/organization/186431.pdf|page=24-5}}</ref>

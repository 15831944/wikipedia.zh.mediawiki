'''运动力学'''是[[量化研究|量化研究]]與[[分析|分析]][[專業人士|專業]][[运动员|运动员]]在一般運動中的力學研究。透過[[数学模型|数学模型]]、[[计算机模拟|计算机模拟]]和[[量度|量度]]对动作的角度和力进行分析用以提高运动员的性能。<ref>{{cite book | author = Wolfgang Baumann| title = Grundlagen der Biomechanik | publisher = Verlag Karl Hofman | year = 1989 | isbn = 3-7780-8141-1}}</ref>运动力学中有兩個研究領域：「[[静力学|静力学]]」靜止狀態（無運動）或以恆定速度移動的恆定運動狀態的系統研究和「[[動力學|動力學]]」包含加速度[[时间|时间]]、[[位移|位移]]、[[速度|速度]]和[[速率|速率]]中產生的力<ref>{{cite web|last=Boone|first=Tommy|title=Basic Concepts in Sports Biomechanics|url=http://www.exercisephysiologists.com/BiomechanicalCONCEPTS/index.html|accessdate=27 October 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111028063415/http://www.exercisephysiologists.com/BiomechanicalCONCEPTS/index.html|archivedate=28 十月 2011}}</ref>

[[生物力学|生物力学]] is the study of the structure and function of biological systems by means of the methods of “mechanics.” – which is the branch of [[物理学|物理学]] involving analysis of the actions of forces.  Within “mechanics” there are two sub-fields of study: [[静力学|静力学]], which is the study of systems that are in a state of constant motion either at rest (with no motion) or moving with a constant velocity; and [[動力學|動力學]], which is the study of systems in motion in which acceleration is present, which may involve [[运动学|运动学]] (the study of the motion of bodies with respect to [[时间|时间]], [[位移|位移]], [[速度|速度]], and [[速率|速率]] of movement either in a straight line or in a rotary direction) and {{tsl|en|kinetics (physics)}} (the study of the forces associated with [[運動_(物理學)|運動 (物理學)]], including forces causing motion and forces resulting from motion).

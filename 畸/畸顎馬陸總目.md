'''畸顎馬陸總目'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''Colobognatha'''}}）是[[倍足綱|倍足綱]][[唇颚亚纲|唇颚亚纲]][[蠕形马陆下纲|蠕形马陆下纲]]（Helminthomorpha）之下的一個總目<ref name="Shear 2011 class.">{{cite journal|last=Shear|first=W.|title=Class Diplopoda de Blainville in Gervais, 1844. In: Zhang, Z.-Q. (Ed.) Animal biodiversity: An outline of higher-level classification and survey of taxonomic richness|journal=Zootaxa|year=2011|volume=3148|pages=159–164|url=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2011/f/zt03148p164.pdf|ref=Shear2011}}</ref>。

==簡述==
畸顎馬陸總目的各物種間都有[[共有衍徵|共有衍徵]]：雄性物種有兩對簡單的{{link-en|生殖肢|gonopod (millipede)}}（{{lang|en|gonopods}}），位於第七體節的一對後腿及第八體節的一對前腿。They possess tubular defensive glands that open on the sides of the body, and lack a [[palp|palp]]-like structure on their [[gnathochilarium|gnathochilaria]]. 本分類元的一大特徵是其特別窄的頭部，而且沒有[[嗅感器|嗅感器]]，但這特徵亦有見於其他馬陸的物種<ref>{{cite web|title=Millipede Body Organization|work=Milli-PEET: The class Diplopoda|publisher=The Field Museum, Chicago|accessdate=18 March 2014|url=http://fieldmuseum.org/explore/milli-peet-millipede-body-organization}}</ref><ref name=PutativeApomorphies>{{cite web|title=Putative apomorphies of millipede clades|url=http://fieldmuseum.org/sites/default/files/millipede_apomorphies.pdf|work=Milli-PEET: Millipede Systematics|publisher=The Field Museum, Chicago, IL|date=26 September 2006}}</ref>。

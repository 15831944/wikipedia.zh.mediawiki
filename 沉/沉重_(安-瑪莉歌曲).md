'''沉重''' ({{lang-en|Heavy}})是[[英國|英國]][[創作歌手|創作歌手]][[安-瑪莉_(英國歌手)|安-瑪莉]]首張專輯《[[說出你的想法|說出你的想法]]》的第三[[單曲|主打歌]]，於{{start_date|2017|09|22}}<ref name= "iTunes"/>由Major Tom's和庇護所唱片在全球數位發行。

== 創作背景 ==
沉重是一首[[流行_(音樂類型)|流行]]歌曲<ref name="The Interns">{{Cite news|url=https://theinterns.net/2017/09/22/anne-marie-goes-island-bop-route-heavy/|title=Anne-Marie Goes Down The Island Bop Route Again For 'Heavy'|date= {{start_date|2017|09|22}}|work= The Interns|language= en}}</ref>，受熱帶音樂影響<ref>{{Cite news|url= http://www.baeblemusic.com/musicblog/9-22-2017/song-of-the-day-heavy-by-anne-marie.html|title=  "沉重", 安-瑪莉歌曲|work= Baeble Music|language= en}}</ref>，由The Invisible Men、娜娜·羅格斯<ref>{{Cite news|url= http://glittermagrocks.com/connect/2017/09/22/uk-pop-star-anne-marie-unveils-her-new-single-heavy/|title= 英國流行歌星安-瑪莉推出她的新單曲,"沉重"|work= Glitter Magazine|date= {{start_date|2017|09|22}}|language= en}}</ref>、喬納森·懷特、Team Salut<ref name="Tidal">{{Cite web|url= https://listen.tidal.com/album/78814335|title= TIDAL - 安-瑪莉歌曲 - 沉重|work= [[Tidal|Tidal]]}}</ref>和MNEK製作<ref>{{Cite news|url= https://www.thelineofbestfit.com/news/latest-news/anne-marie-unveils-hard-hitting-single-heavy|title= 安-瑪莉推出她的新單曲,"沉重"|work= The Line of Best Fit|language= en}}</ref>。

{{Cquote|“Heavy” is a song which deals with the perils of a crumbling relationship.|安-瑪莉}}

“沉重”是一首講述搖搖欲墜而且危險的愛情的歌曲<ref>{{cite web|url= https://genius.com/Anne-marie-heavy-lyrics|title= 安-瑪莉 - 沉重 - 歌詞|work= [[AllMusic|AllMusic]]|language= en}}</ref>。

'''絲瓦拉·巴斯卡'''（{{lang-en|'''Swara Bhaskar'''}}，{{bd|1988年|4月9日|catIdx=Bhaskar}}）是[[印度|印度]]女演員，她主要在[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]電影擔任配角和[[獨立電影|獨立電影]]的女主角。

她較受歡迎的電影有《Tanu Weds Manu》（2011）和《Raanjhanaa》（2013）等等。<ref>[https://www.hindustantimes.com/bollywood/on-swara-bhasker-s-birthday-quotes-that-define-her-stardom-feminism-and-swag/story-YFZrWeYbZl7JIpB1bQUHdK.html “On Swara Bhasker’s birthday, quotes that define her stardom, feminism and swag”]. The Hindustan Times. 09-04-2018.</ref>

她的父亲Chitrapu Uday Bhaskar是印度最頂尖的智庫學者之一，母親亦是一名大學教授。<ref>[http://indianexpress.com/article/lifestyle/life-style/womens-day-how-swara-bhaskars-dad-taught-her-that-menstruation-is-not-a-taboo/ “Women’s Day: How Swara Bhaskar’s dad taught her that menstruation is not a taboo”]. The Indian Express. 08-03-2016.</ref><ref>[http://www.thehindu.com/features/cinema/i-was-always-a-dramebaaz/article4966379.ece “I was always a dramebaaz’”]. The Hindu. 29-07-2013.</ref>

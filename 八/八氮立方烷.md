'''八氮立方烷'''也称为“'''立方氮烷'''”，是[[氮元素|氮元素]]的一种假想[[单质|单质]]，[[分子式|分子式]]为N<sub>8</sub>。八氮立方烷与[[氮气|N<sub>2</sub>]]、[[氮4|N<sub>4</sub>]]等互为[[同素异形体|同素异形体]]。八氮立方烷[[分子|分子]]由8个围成[[立方体|立方体]]氮[[原子|原子]]构成，可以看作所有[[次甲基|次甲基]]都被氮原子代替了的[[立方烷|立方烷]]分子，所以八氮立方烷是立方烷的[[衍生物|衍生物]]之一。<ref name="b_muir">{{cite web |url=http://www.ch.ic.ac.uk/local/projects/b_muir/Cubane/Cubanepro/Start.html | title=Cubane | author = B. Muir|postscript=（参见该文“further topics”章节）}}</ref>八氮立方烷分子可能是一种[[亚稳态分子|亚稳态分子]]。若忽略由[[应力_(化学)|键应力]]引起的[[化学热力学|化学热力学]]不稳定性和[[氮氮单键|氮氮单键]]的较高[[键能|键能]]，只从[[化学动力学|化学动力学]]角度来看，该分子因[[分子对称性|轨道对称]]而具有一定稳定性。<ref>{{cite article |url=http://www.springerlink.com/content/w3pap8xmmju00j3e/ | title=Theoretical studies on the molecular electron densities and electrostatic potentials in azacubanes| author = Ujwala N. Patil, Nilesh R. Dhumal and Shridhar P. Gejji |journal=Theoretical Chemistry Accounts: Theory, Computation, and Modeling (Theoretica Chimica Acta)|volume=112|page=27-32}}</ref>

==可能用途==
八氮立方烷预计拥有约22.9 [[百萬|M]][[焦耳|J]]/kg的[[能量密度|能量密度]]（假设完全分解为N<sub>2</sub>），<ref>{{cite article |url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ic9606237 | title=Besides N<sub>2</sub>, What Is the Most Stable Molecule Composed Only of Nitrogen Atoms?| author=Mikhail N. Glukhovtsev, Haijun Jiao, and Paul von Ragué Schleyer|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|volume=35|page=7124-7133}}</ref>是[[三硝基甲苯|TNT]][[TNT当量|标准当量]]的5倍。所以八氮立方烷与其他特殊[[氮单质|氮单质]]都曾被建议开发为[[炸药|炸药]]或高性能[[火箭|火箭]][[推进剂|推进剂]]。<ref>{{cite article |url=http://www.entrepreneur.com/tradejournals/article/60525404.html | title=Exploding the mysteries of nitrogen.|journal=Chemistry and Industry|author=}}</ref>

'''素裙鋸眼蝶'''（學名：''Elymnias vasudeva''）是屬於[[蛺蝶科|蛺蝶科]][[眼蝶亞科|眼蝶亞科]]的一種[[蝴蝶|蝴蝶]]，是[[鋸眼蝶屬|鋸眼蝶屬]]的一種。分佈於印度至泰國一帶，模式產地為印度[[大吉嶺|大吉嶺]]<ref>Moore, 1857; in Horsfield & Moore, A Catalogue of the Lepidopterous Insects in the Museum of the Hon. East-India Company in Horsfield & Moore, Cat. lep. Ins. Mus. East India Coy 1: [http://archive.org/stream/catalogueoflepid01east#page/238/mode/1up 238]</ref>。外表[[擬態|擬態]]模仿[[報喜斑粉蝶|報喜斑粉蝶]]（''Delias pasithoe''）和[[得失斑粉蝶|得失斑粉蝶]]（''D. descombesi''）<ref>Wynter-Blyth, 1957 Butterflies of the Indian Region (1982 Reprint)</ref>。

== 亞種 ==
* '''''E. v. vasudeva''''' － 指名亞種，分佈於印度[[錫金|錫金]]和[[尼泊爾|尼泊爾]]<ref>Fruhstorfer, 1907, Monographie der Elymniinae Dt. ent. Z. Iris 20 (1/2): pl. 5 (1 August), (3): [http://archive.org/stream/deutscheentomolo201907ento#page/247/mode/1up 247]</ref>
* '''''E. v. thycana''''' <small>Wallace, 1869</small>－ 分佈於印度<ref>Wallace, 1869; Notes on eastern Butterflies (3 parts) Trans. ent. Soc. Lond. 1869 (4): [http://archive.org/stream/transactionsofen1869roya#page/323/mode/1up 323]</ref>
* '''''E. v. deva''''' <small>(Moore, 1893)</small>－ 分佈於印度[[阿薩姆邦|阿薩姆邦]]<ref name=m1893>Moore, 1893; Lepidoptera Indica 2 (19): [http://archive.org/stream/lepidopteraindic02moor#page/167/mode/1up 167]-168, pl. [http://archive.org/stream/lepidopteraindic02moor#page/n408/mode/1up 142]-143</ref>
* '''''E. v. burmensis''''' <small>(Moore, 1893)</small>－ 分佈於[[緬甸|緬甸]]、雲南南部等<ref name=m1893/>
* '''''E. v. oberthuri''''' <small>Fruhstorfer, 1902</small>－ 分佈於泰國<ref>Fruhstorfer, 1902; Neue ostasiatische [sic] Rhopaloceren Soc. Ent. 17 (11): [http://archive.org/stream/societasentomolo1518190004inte#page/n485/mode/1up 82]</ref>
* '''''E. v. sinensis''''' <small>Chou, Zhang & Xie, 2000</small>－ 分佈於雲南<ref>Chou, Zhang & Xie, 2000; New species, subspecies and new records of butterflies from China (I) Entomotaxonomia 22 (3): 224, 228, f. 7-8</ref>

{{Annotation|340|220|體質|font-weight=bold|font-size=25|color=green}}}}'''素質—壓力模型'''（{{Lang-en|diathesis-stress model}}）<ref>{{Cite web|title=APA Dictionary of Psychology|url=https://dictionary.apa.org/|work=dictionary.apa.org|accessdate=2019-03-22|language=en}}</ref>是一個[[心理學|心理學]]理論，嘗試去解釋心理疾病的成因，主要應用於[[精神病理學|精神病理學]]<ref name="Sigelman">Sigelman, C. K. & Rider, E. A. (2009). [https://books.google.com/books?id=8smBuRecmDsC&pg=PT656&dq=Life-span+human+development+6th+ed&hl=en&sa=X&ei=P1_HUJqFG8iy0AH5ioGoAw&ved=0CD4Q6AEwAA Developmental psychopathology.]  ''Life-span human development'' (6th ed.) (pp. 468-495). Belmont, CA: Wadsworth Cengage Learning.</ref>。這個模型也可以用來研究[[先天与后天|先天與後天]]在人一生中對於心理疾病的交互作用<ref name="Sigelman2">Sigelman, C. K. & Rider, E. A. (2009). [https://books.google.com/books?id=8smBuRecmDsC&pg=PT656&dq=Life-span+human+development+6th+ed&hl=en&sa=X&ei=P1_HUJqFG8iy0AH5ioGoAw&ved=0CD4Q6AEwAA Developmental psychopathology.]  ''Life-span human development'' (6th ed.) (pp. 468-495). Belmont, CA: Wadsworth Cengage Learning.</ref>。

當一個預先存在且易受傷害（{{Lang-en|vulnerability}}）的脆弱體質或身體素質，被極其緊張的生活事件所激活，兩者加起超過一定閂值，就會導致例如疾病的發生<ref name="Lazarus">{{cite journal|title=From psychological stress to the emotions: A history of changing outlooks|author=Lazarus R. S.|url=http://commonweb.unifr.ch/artsdean/pub/gestens/f/as/files/4660/18222_105619.pdf|journal=Annual Review of Psychology|issue=1|doi=10.1146/annurev.ps.44.020193.000245|year=1993|volume=44|pages=1–21|pmid=8434890}}</ref>。例如，這個模型可以解釋兩個人雖然面對一樣壓力，但只有其中一個會病發出[[抑鬱症|抑鬱症]]<ref name="Sigelman3">Sigelman, C. K. & Rider, E. A. (2009). [https://books.google.com/books?id=8smBuRecmDsC&pg=PT656&dq=Life-span+human+development+6th+ed&hl=en&sa=X&ei=P1_HUJqFG8iy0AH5ioGoAw&ved=0CD4Q6AEwAA Developmental psychopathology.]  ''Life-span human development'' (6th ed.) (pp. 468-495). Belmont, CA: Wadsworth Cengage Learning.</ref>。另外，其他如焦慮和藥物濫用是可以共同發生的其他精神問題。兒童時期的創傷也與抑鬱症的嚴重程度，對治療缺乏反應和患病時間長有關。童年受創傷後更容易引發精神疾病如抑鬱症。也提出了各種基因控制易患病性<ref>{{cite web|author=Department of Health and Human Services|year=1999|month=|url=http://www.surgeongeneral.gov/library/mentalhealth/pdfs/c2.pdf|title=The fundamentals of mental health and mental illness|work=Mental Health: A Report of the Surgeon General|accessdate=2008-11-11|format=PDF}}</ref>。不同的家庭病史也解釋了有些人會更高風險對某種疾病<ref name="Gazelle">{{cite journal|title=Anxious solitude and peer exclusion: A diathesis stress model of internalizing trajectories in childhood|journal=Child Development|issue=|doi=10.1111/1467-8624.00534|year=2003|volume=74|pages=257–278|author1=Gazelle H.|author2=Ladd G. W.}}</ref>。

[[精神心理疾病|精神心理疾病]]是由於患者體質上的某些弱點，如基因異常<ref name="Sigelman5">Sigelman, C. K. & Rider, E. A. (2009). [https://books.google.com/books?id=8smBuRecmDsC&pg=PT656&dq=Life-span+human+development+6th+ed&hl=en&sa=X&ei=P1_HUJqFG8iy0AH5ioGoAw&ved=0CD4Q6AEwAA Developmental psychopathology.]  ''Life-span human development'' (6th ed.) (pp. 468-495). Belmont, CA: Wadsworth Cengage Learning.</ref>、腦部或身體其他系統的異常等，使其對環境壓力的承受能力降低。如果他在生活中遭遇到的壓力不高，則先天脆弱的系統還能承受；一旦在身體、親情、學業、工作或社會方面遭遇到困難與壓力，脆弱的系統就無法支撐而失能，甚至崩潰，此時精神疾病就會發作。例如，知覺失調症雖然與遺傳有密切關係，致病基因從出生後就存在，但它之所以過了青春期後才發作，可能的原因就是青春期無論在身體發育、心智負荷或人際關係上，都會出現重大的衝擊與變化，需要應付的壓力變得比從前沉重，使得帶有致病基因的個體再也無法因應，從而出現精神疾病的不適症狀。

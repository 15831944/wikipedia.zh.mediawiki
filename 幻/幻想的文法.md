'''《幻想的文法》'''（{{lang-it|''Grammatica della fantasia''}}）是意大利[[儿童文学|儿童文学]]作家[[贾尼·罗大里|贾尼·罗大里]]1973年出版的童书理论作品<ref>{{cite book |author=Pietro Greco |title=L'universo a dondolo: La scienza nell'opera di Gianni Rodari |date=2011 |publisher=[[Springer_Science_&_Business_Media|Springer Science & Business Media]] |isbn=9788847017085 |page=338|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=yF6JkDFLJj8C&pg=PA338& |language=it}}</ref>，对[[童话|童话]][[故事|故事]]写作的基础理论进行了探讨，并结合[[弗拉基米尔·雅可夫列维奇·普罗普|弗拉基米尔·普罗普]]等作家的实例进行了分析<ref>{{cite book |title=Beyond Babar: The European Tradition in Children's Literature |date=2006 |publisher=Scarecrow Press |isbn=9781461656791 |page=105 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ryCpjXGT4P0C&pg=PA105& |language=en}}</ref>。

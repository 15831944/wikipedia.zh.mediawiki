《食人族》（{{lang-en|'''Cannibal'''}}）為[[美國|美國]]歌手[[凱莎|凱莎]]於2010年11月19日發行的首張[[EP|EP]]，美國本土由[[RCA_Records|RCA]]發行，美國本土以外由[[索尼音樂娛樂|索尼音樂娛樂]]發行。

==簡介==
*原本是做為[[派對動物|派對動物]]的豪華版發行，但後來改成了單獨發行成[[EP|EP]]的型式。<ref name="Montgomery1">{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1649918/20101013/kesha_.jhtml|title=Kesha To Return With Cannibal On November 22. Animal companion will feature eight new songs|date=2010-10-13|author=Montgomery, James|work=''[[MTV_News|MTV News]]''| publisher=[[MTV_Networks|MTV Networks]] ([[Viacom|Viacom]])|accessdate=2010-10-13}}</ref>
*首波主打單曲《[[莎世代|莎世代]]》（{{lang-en|'''We R Who We R'''}}）獲得[[告示牌百強單曲榜|告示牌百強單曲榜]]冠軍，是繼首張單曲《[[跑趴滴答|跑趴滴答]]》後再度獲得冠軍。

==單曲==
《[[莎世代|莎世代]]》（{{lang-en|We R Who We}}）是EP中的首波主打單曲，於2010年10月22日發行<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://itunes.apple.com/ca/album/we-r-who-we-r-single/id398439652|title=We R Who We R – Single (Canada)|work=''[[iTunes_Store|iTunes Store]]''|publisher=[[Apple_Inc|Apple Inc]]|accessdate=2010-10-26}}</ref>，凱莎表示他對於青少年自殺有所感觸，特別是[[Suicide_of_Tyler_Clementi|Tyler Clementi]]因被室友出櫃而自殺的事件<ref name="RollingStone">{{cite web|last=Doyle|first=Patrick|title=Kesha Says New Single Addresses Teen Suicides {{!}} Rolling Stone Music|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/51942/221573|date=2010-10-18|work=Rolling Stone|publisher=Wenner Media LLC|accessdate=2010-11-03}}</ref>。她認為每個人應該努力「變得更好」以及及「慶祝自己」（celebrate who you are），這首歌是來自對於「慶祝自己」的情感。<ref name="RollingStone"/> 《莎世代》獲得了[[告示牌百強單曲榜|告示牌百強單曲榜]]冠軍，銷售約280,000張（數位）。<ref name="Lamb40">{{cite web|url=http://top40.about.com/b/2010/11/03/kehas-we-r-who-we-r-to-debut-at-1-on-billboard-hot-100.htm|title=Kesha's "We R Who We R" To Debut At #1 on Billboard Hot 100|date=2010-11-03|author=Lamb, Bill|work=''[[About.com|About.com]]''|publisher=[[The_New_York_Times_Company|The New York Times Company]]|accessdate=2010-11-03}}</ref>另外在[[澳洲|澳洲]]也拿下冠軍，[[英國|英國]]與[[加拿大|加拿大]]則是第2名。<ref name="CAN">{{Cite web|url=http://en-ca.nielsen.com/content/dam/nielsen/en_ca/documents/pdf/newsletters/billboard_canadian-update/Nielsen%20Music%20Canadian%20Update.pdf|title=Nielsen Music: Canadian Update (November 5, 2010)|publisher=Nielsen Business Media, Inc|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5tzBIIElp?url=http://en-ca.nielsen.com/content/dam/nielsen/en_ca/documents/pdf/newsletters/billboard_canadian-update/Nielsen%20Music%20Canadian%20Update.pdf|archivedate=2010-11-04|accessdate=November 4, 2010|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Ke%24ha&titel=We+R+Who+We+R&cat=s|title=Australian-charts.com – Kesha – We R Who We R|work=''ARIA Top 50 Singles''|publisher=Hung Medien|accessdate=2010-11-13}}</ref>樂評給與此歌正面的評價，是一首有著強烈的鼓點、激勵人心的訊息、以及真正的幽默。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://top40.about.com/od/singles/gr/Kesha-We-R-Who-We-R.htm|title=Kesha – "We R Who We R"|author=Lamb, Bill|work=''About.com''|publisher=The New York Times Company|accessdate=2010-10-27}}</ref>

《[[駭到爆炸|駭到爆炸]]》（{{lang-en|Blow}}）為EP第二波主打單曲，首次於2011年2月8日在美國的廣播打單。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allaccess.com/top40-mainstream/future-releases|title=Home > Top 40/Mainstream > Future Releases|publisher=All Access|accessdate=2011-01-03|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5vtAJiS9W?url=http://www.allaccess.com/top40-mainstream/future-releases|archivedate=2011-01-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>樂評給與此曲多為正面或混合的評價，樂評大多認為此曲的hook以及開頭是值得讚許，但對於副歌部分的意見相左，有些認為這首歌帶來了派對氣氛，有些則認為相當平淡而普通。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://top40.about.com/od/singles/gr/Ke-Ha-Blow.htm|title=Kesha – "Blow"|author=Lamb, Bill|work=''About.com''|publisher=The New York Times Company|accessdate=2011-04-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110219150912/http://top40.about.com/od/singles/gr/Ke-Ha-Blow.htm|archive-date=2011-02-19|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.digitalspy.com/music/singlesreviews/a312292/keha-blow.html|title=Ke$ha: 'Blow'|author=Copsey, Robert|work=''[[Digital_Spy|Digital Spy]]''|publisher=[[Hachette_Filipacchi_UK|Hachette Filipacchi UK]]|accessdate=2011-04-01}}</ref>但依然在美國及加拿大有不錯成績，是她連續六張都在TOP10內的單曲。<ref name="BlowSales">{{Cite web|url=http://new.music.yahoo.com/blogs/chart_watch/74340/week-ending-march-6-2011-songs-rated-pg/|title=Week Ending March 6, 2011: Songs: Rated PG|date=2011-03-09|first=Paul|last=Grein|work=Chart Watch|publisher=[[Yahoo!_Music|Yahoo!

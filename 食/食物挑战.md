'''食物挑战'''，例如[[牛奶挑战|牛奶挑战]]或[[威化饼挑战|威化饼挑战]]，这种挑战会涉及到食物。<ref>[http://espn.go.com/espn/story/_/id/13197879/matt-stonie-upsets-joey-chestnut-wins-nathan-hot-dog-eating-contest "Matt Stonie beats Joey Chestnut to win Nathan's Hot Dog Eating Contest"] ''ESPN''. </ref> 牛奶挑战是一项在大学校园中受欢迎的[[大胃王|大胃王]]挑战。<ref name="tech">{{cite web|url=http://www.technicianonline.com/features/chugging-for-glory-1.1110141|title=Chugging for glory|accessdate=2010-07-12|date=2006-08-29|last=Young|first=Luke|work=[[Technician_(newspaper)|Technician]]|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120805231343/http://www.technicianonline.com/features/chugging-for-glory-1.1110141|archivedate=2012-08-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.newsobserver.com/news/politics-government/politics-columns-blogs/under-the-dome/article24636634.html|title=NC legislators chug milk in annual contest|date=June 16, 2015|work=News & Observer|author1=Colin Campbell|author2=Taylor Knop}}</ref>

随着[[互联网|互联网]]的发展，已有越来越多[[網路爆紅|網路爆紅]]的渠道，如[[连环信|连环信]]和[[社会化媒体|社会媒体]]，鼓励人们通过传播信息"挑战"他们的朋友，造成病毒式[[網路爆紅|網路爆紅]]。

一些互联网上的"挑战"可能会严重危害的挑战者。如[[肉桂粉大挑戰|肉桂粉大挑戰]]，尝试在一分钟内吃指定数量的肉桂，一些人可能会把肉桂吸入肺部。<ref name="Painter">{{Cite journal|title=Cinnamon challenge:|url=http://yourlife.usatoday.com/health/healthyperspective/post/2012-03-19/cinnamon-challenge-viral-videos-with-health-risks/651200/1|last=Painter|first=Kim|publisher=[[USA_Today|USA Today]]|accessdate=2012-03-19}}</ref> 2015年7月一个四岁男孩死于吃肉桂造成的窒息。<ref name="MedicalDaily">{{Cite web|url=http://www.medicaldaily.com/kentucky-mom-urges-kids-stop-cinnamon-challenge-after-son-dies-cinnamon-can-kill-337268|title=Kentucky Mom Urges Kids To Stop 'Cinnamon Challenge' After Son Dies: 'Cinnamon Can Kill'|date=June 9, 2015|website=[[Medical_Daily|Medical Daily]]|access-date=June 10, 2016}}</ref>

《'''食糧日記'''》（{{kor|k='''식량일기'''}}，{{lang-en|'''Food Diary'''}}）為[[韓國|韓國]][[SM_C&C|SM C&C]]製作<ref>{{cite web|url=https://onehallyu.com/topic/661101-new-sm-cc-produced-variety-show-food-diary-reportedly-in-discussion-to-air-on-tvn/|title=New SM C&C produced variety show "Food Diary" reportedly in discussion to air on tvN|publisher=onehallyu|accessdate=2018-04-25|date=2018-03-19|language=ko}}</ref>而於[[tvN|tvN]]放映綜藝節目<ref>{{cite web|url=http://entertain.naver.com/read?oid=109&aid=0003739972&cid=1073787|title=[단독] 이수근·보아에 박성광까지..SM패밀리 新예능 '식량일기' 론칭|publisher=Naver Entertain|accessdate=2018-03-19|date=2018-03-19|language=ko}}</ref>，由[[李壽根|李壽根]]、[[寶兒|寶兒]]、{{link-ko|朴聖光|박성광}}、[[徐章勳|徐章勳]]、[[泰容|泰容]]（[[NCT|NCT]]）、[[YooA|YooA]]（[[OH_MY_GIRL|OH MY GIRL]]）、{{link-ko|Niklas Klabunde|니클라스 클라분데}}共同主持<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sportalkorea.com/butterfly/view.php?gisa_uniq=201803191600772272&key=&field=&section_code=C1000&search_key=y|title=[단독] 이수근·보아에 박성광까지..SM패밀리 新예능 '식량일기' 론칭|publisher=sportalkorea|accessdate=2018-03-19|date=2018-03-19|language=ko}}</ref>，節目主軸為透過親自種植食糧的方式，學習如何不浪費並愛護珍惜食糧<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2018/03/19/2018031901940.html|title=[단독] 이수근·보아에 박성광까지..SM패밀리 新예능 '식량일기' 론칭|publisher=chosun news|accessdate=2018-03-19|date=2018-03-19|language=ko}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.soompi.com/2018/03/19/ncts-taeyong-boa-lee-soo-geun-star-new-variety-show/|title=NCT’s Taeyong, BoA, Lee Soo Geun, And More To Star In New Variety Show|publisher=soompi|accessdate=2018-04-25|date=2018-03-19|language=en-US}}</ref>。

'''食肉袋鼠屬'''（[[學名|學名]]''Propleopus''）是[[澳洲|澳洲]]一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[有袋類|有袋類]]。屬下包括 3 種：生存於[[上新世|上新世]]至[[更新世|更新世]]的 ''P. chillagoensis''，以及僅生存於[[更新世|更新世]]的 ''P. oscillans'' 與 ''P. wellingtonensis''<ref name = Wroe1996>Stephen Wroe, 1996. [http://www.create.unsw.edu.au/research/files/Wroe%20(1996)%20An%20Investigation%20of%20Phylogeny%20in%20the%20Giant%20Extin.pdf An Investigation of Phylogeny in the Giant Extinct Rat Kangaroo ''Ekaltadeta'' (Propleopinae, Potoroidae, Marsupialia)]. ''Journal of Paleontology'', Vol. 70, No. 4. (Jul., 1996), pp. 681-690.</ref>。和牠們現存的近親[[麝袋鼠|麝袋鼠]]一樣，牠們可能同樣為[[雜食性|雜食性]]的。

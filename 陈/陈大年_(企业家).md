陈大年（{{lang-en|Danny Chen}}，{{bd|1978年||}}），中国互联网企业家。他创立了[[连尚网络|连尚网络]]并担任首席执行官<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/founder-and-ceo-of-linksure-danian-chen-named-in-fortunes-2017-list-of-top-50-most-influential-business-leaders-in-china-300446905.html|title=Founder and CEO of LinkSure Danian Chen Named in Fortune's 2017 List of Top 50 Most Influential Business Leaders in China|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=27 April 2017|publisher=[[PR_Newswire|PR Newswire]]}}</ref>，该公司推出了世界上首个最大的[[WiFi|WiFi]]分享软件[[WiFi万能钥匙|WiFi万能钥匙]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/wifi-master-keys-android-app-reaches-100-million-installs-on-google-play-300483188.html|title=WiFi Master Key's Android app reaches 100 million installs on Google Play|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=5 July 2017|publisher=[[PR_Newswire|PR Newswire]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://e27.co/born-into-poverty-rural-china-this-guy-went-free-wifi-used-800m-users-raised-us52m-20160712/|title=Born into poverty in rural China, this guy made an app that gives free WiFi, got 900M users and raised US$52M|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=14 July 2016|publisher=[[e27|e27]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.prnasia.com/mnr/wifi_201703.shtml|title=WiFi Master Key App Adds 100 Million Users in 3 Months, Now Surges Past 900 Million Users|accessdate=11 July 2017|date=20 March 2017|publisher=[[PR_Newswire|PR Newswire]]}}</ref>。

==生平==
陈大年是[[盛大网络|盛大]]的联合创始人，他曾担任盛大[[首席运营官|首席运营官]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/linyang/2016/04/10/crowd-source-internet-in-china-a-qa-with-shanda-cofounder-chen-danian/#6e6045335004|title=Crowd Source Internet In China: A Q&A With Shanda Cofounder Chen Danian|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=10 April 2016|publisher=[[Forbes|Forbes]]}}</ref>。2014年盛大成为中国市值最大的互联网公司 <ref>{{cite web|url=http://tech.sina.com.cn/i/w/2004-08-10/0021401192.shtml|title=Shanda became the highest market value of China stocks|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=10 August 2004|publisher=[[Sina|Sina]]}}</ref>。

从2006年到2010年，陈大年均有上榜福布斯中国400富豪榜<ref>{{cite web|url=http://63.240.4.179/lists/2006/74/biz_06china_Chen-Danian_KR2G.html|title=The 400 Richest Chinese #364 Chen Danian|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=11 February 2006|publisher=[[Forbes|Forbes]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/business/lists/2007/74/biz_07china_Chen-Danian_KR2G.html|title=The 400 Richest Chinese #175 Chen Danian|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=11 January 2007|publisher=[[Forbes|Forbes]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/lists/2008/74/chinarichest08_Chen-Danian_KR2G.html|title=The 400 Richest Chinese #140 Chen Danian|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=11 February 2008|publisher=[[Forbes|Forbes]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://beta.forbes.com/lists/2009/74/china-billionaires-09_Chen-Danian_KR2G.html|title=The 400 Richest Chinese #148 Chen Danian|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=11 April 2009|publisher=[[Forbes|Forbes]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.forbes.com/lists/2010/74/china-billionaires-10_Chen-Danian_KR2G.html|title=The 400 Richest Chinese #302 Chen Danian|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=2010|publisher=[[Forbes|Forbes]]}}</ref>。

2016年，陈大年做为中国最有影响力的年轻商业领袖，被财富杂志评为[[40位40岁以下精英|40位40岁以下精英]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fortunechina.com/rankings/c/2016-12/20/content_275655.htm|title=2016 Chinese 40 under the age of 40 business elite|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=20 December 2016|publisher=[[Fortune_(magazine)|Fortune (magazine)]]}}</ref>。2017年，陈大年以43名入选《财富》2017中国最具影响力的50位商界领袖<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fortunechina.com/rankings/c/2017-04/19/content_281498.htm|title=2017 China's most influential 50 business leaders|accessdate=10 July 2017|date=19 April 2017|publisher=[[Fortune_(magazine)|Fortune (magazine)]]}}</ref>。

'''陳容'''（{{lang-en|Armando Chin Yong}}，{{bd|1958年|7月6日|2011年|2月2日}}），[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]][[聲樂|聲樂家]]，也是唯一曾先後在歐洲和亞洲歌劇殿堂演出的馬來西亞華裔[[男高音|男高音]]。<ref>{{cite news |title=Il Diva |author=Ho, Lisa |newspaper=Kakiseni |date=2006-07-04 |url=http://www.kakiseni.com/articles/reviews/MDg5MQ.html/all |accessdate=2009-11-23 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5lUlGmfFL?url=http://www.kakiseni.com/articles/reviews/MDg5MQ.html/all |archivedate=2009-11-23 |deadurl=yes }}</ref> 他學習聲樂於[[意大利|意大利]][[羅馬|羅馬]]和[[奥地利|奥地利]][[維也納|維也納]]，曾在歐洲數個歌唱比賽中獲得優秀成績和好評。<ref>{{cite news |title=Telling of beauty in song |author=Bhatt, Himanshu |newspaper=[[新海峽時報|新海峽時報]] |date=2004-10-31 |url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P1-101921306.html |accessdate=2009-11-23 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5lUlLT736?url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P1-101921306.html |archivedate=2009-11-23 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref name="waccm.com">Pages 9 and 10 of write-up on the 19th World Chinese Church Music Conference. http://www.waccm.com/images/19th_Camp_Lecturers.pdf</ref> 

陳容於2011年2月2日在[[吉隆坡|吉隆坡]]住家附近遛狗時，因心臓問題而與世長辞，享年52歲，留下遺孀祝書婉（{{lang|en|Chu Shoo Woan}}）和12歲獨子陳毅（{{lang|en|Ian Chin Yi}}）。<ref>Statement by Armando Chin Yong's widow, Chu Shoo Woan on 5 February 2011.</ref><ref name="Obituary">{{cite news |title=本地著名歌唱家‧陳容心臟病發逝世 |url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/192291?tid=1 |newspaper=[[星洲日報|星洲日報]] |date=2011-02-03 |accessdate=2011-02-11 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5wDdhTA2F?url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/192291?tid=1 |archivedate=2011-02-03 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>

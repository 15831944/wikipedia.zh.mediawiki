《'''华盛顿自由灯塔'''》（{{lang-en|The Washington Free Beacon}}）是[[美国|美国]]一家保守派{{tsl|en|Political journalism|政治新闻业|政治新闻}}网站，2012年上线，号称“致力于揭发权力希望永不见光明的新闻”并发表“多个领域问题的深度调查报道，包括公共政策、政府事务、国际安全和媒体方面”<ref name="wfb-aboutus">{{Cite web|url=http://freebeacon.com/about/|title=About Us|accessdate=2014-09-08|publisher=Washington Free Beacon|language=en}}</ref>。

== 历史 ==
《自由灯塔》由{{tsl|en|Michael Goldfarb (political writer)|Michael Goldfarb}}、Aaron Harrison和{{tsl|en|Matthew Continetti}}创办，最后一位是现在的总编。该站是[[501(c)条款|501(c)4]]组织“美国自由中心”（Center for American Freedom）的项目之一，正式上线时间为2012年2月7日<ref name="Politico">{{cite news|url=http://www.politico.com/news/stories/0112/71099.html|title=How to fight liberals: Imitate them|last=Smith|first=Ben|date=2012-01-05|newspaper=Politico|accessdate=2014-09-08|language=en}}</ref>。2014年8月，网站宣布将以营利性质运作<ref name="DailyCaller">{{cite news|url=http://dailycaller.com/2014/08/04/washington-free-beacon-goes-private/|title=Washington Free Beacon goes private|last=Rothstein|first=Betsy|date=2014-08-04|newspaper=Daily Caller|accessdate=2014-09-08|language=en}}</ref>。

该站以其[[保守主义|保守派]]报道而闻名，形式上参照类似的{{tsl|en|Modern liberalism in the United States|美国现代自由主义|自由派}}媒体（如{{tsl|en|ThinkProgress}}、{{tsl|en|Talking Points Memo}}），旨在传播自己的报道并影响{{tsl|en|Mainstream media|主流媒体}}的报道范围<ref name="NewYorker">{{cite news|url=http://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2014/06/02/the-hillary-show|title=The Hillary Show|last=Auletta|first=Ken|date=2014-06-02|newspaper=The New Yorker|accessdate=2014-09-08|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theatlantic.com/politics/archive/2012/02/the-worst-mission-statement-in-the-history-of-dc-journalism/252744/|title=The Worst Mission Statement in the History of D.C. Journalism|last=Friedersdorf|first=Conor|language=en|website=The Atlantic|access-date=2016-05-29}}</ref>。[[傑克·亨特_(廣播主持人)|杰克·亨特]]原为[[美国参议院|美国参议员]][[兰德·保罗|兰德·保罗]]办公室的一名工作人员，在《自由灯塔》报道他过去是被称为“南方复仇者”的{{tsl|en|Shock jock|大嘴电台主持人|大嘴DJ}}，戴的[[墨西哥摔角|自由摔角]]面具还印有[[美利坚联盟国国旗|美利坚联盟国国旗]]后，他于2013年辞职<ref name="DailyCaller2">{{cite news|url=http://dailycaller.com/2013/07/21/controversial-rand-paul-aide-leaves-staff-returns-to-punditry/|title=Southern Avenger no more: Rand Paul aide Jack Hunter leaves staff, returns to punditry|last=Antle|first=Jim|date=2013-07-21|newspaper=Daily Caller|accessdate=2014-09-08|language=en}}</ref>。该站还曾数次报道前[[第一夫人|第一夫人]]、[[美国国务卿|国务卿]][[希拉里·克林顿|希拉里·克林顿]]在1975年为强奸嫌疑人辩护成功，并引起全国性媒体的关注<ref name="ABC">{{cite news|url=http://abcnews.go.com/blogs/politics/2014/06/hillary-clinton-dogged-by-1975-rape-case/|script-title=en:Hillary Clinton’s Handling of 1975 Rape Case Emerges Again|last=Kreutz|first=Liz|date=2014-06-20|newspaper=ABC News|accessdate=2014-09-08|language=en}}</ref>。

== 评价 ==
《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》的{{tsl|en|Jim Rutenberg}}在文章中将《自由灯塔》的报道风格比作“愉快的除脏手术”<ref name="NYT">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2013/02/24/us/politics/michael-goldfarb-gleeful-provocateur-at-intersection-of-many-worlds.html?pagewanted=all|title=A Conservative Provocateur, Using a Blowtorch as His Pen|last=Rutenberg|first=Jim|date=2014-02-23|newspaper=New York Times|accessdate=2014-09-08|language=en}}</ref>，《MotherJones》杂志称该站在报道重大新闻方面“真正成功地进行了[[耙粪记者|黑幕揭发]]”<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.motherjones.com/media/2015/03/washington-free-beacon-conservative-investigative-media|title=Conservatives started a news website and it's actually kind of good|website=Mother Jones|access-date=2016-05-29|language=en}}</ref>。在评论《自由灯塔》揭发新闻的成功时，《{{tsl|en|Washingtonian (magazine)|华盛顿人 (杂志)|华盛顿人}}》的Andrew Beaujon写道：“《自由灯塔》定期敲出每个政治记者都会羡慕的抢先新闻。其许多报道都让涉及的对象难以忽略，因为他们提供的是互联网最稀缺的商品：真实新闻。”<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.washingtonian.com/2015/08/06/the-problem-with-the-mainstream-media-its-not-more-like-the-washington-free-beacon/|title=The Problem With the Mainstream Media: It's Not More Like the Washington Free Beacon|date=2015-08-06|website=Washingtonian|access-date=2016-05-29|language=en}}</ref>

该站报道策略亦引发了媒体评论人士和监督组织的批评。《[[大西洋_(雜誌)|大西洋]]》杂志的{{tsl|en|Conor Friedersdorf}}认为《自由灯塔》的使命“堕落且不道德”<ref name="Atlantic">{{cite news|url=https://www.theatlantic.com/politics/archive/2013/01/the-ascendant-smear-wing-of-the-conservative-movement/266903/|title=The ascendant ‘smear wing’ of the conservative movement|last=Friedersdorf|first=Conor|date=2014-01-08|newspaper=The Atlantic|accessdate=2014-09-08|language=en}}</ref>。2014年，{{tsl|en|Progressivism in the United States|美国进步主义|进步派}}{{tsl|en|Watchdog journalism|监督新闻业|媒体监督组织}}“{{tsl|en|Media Matters for America}}”创办者{{tsl|en|David Brock}}在发给新闻组织的信件中写道：“如果可信媒体认为《自由灯塔》的不道德行为是可行的，整个新闻业价值都会降低。”<ref name="MediaMatters">{{cite web|url=http://mediamatters.org/blog/2014/06/23/david-brock-advises-media-of-the-washington-fre/199835|title=David Brock Advises Media Of The Washington Free Beacon's Unethical Practices|accessdate=2014-09-08|publisher=Media Matters for America|language=en}}</ref>

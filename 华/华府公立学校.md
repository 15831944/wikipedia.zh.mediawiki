'''华府公立学校'''<ref>"[http://www.dc.gov/DCPS/Files/downloads/ABOUT%20DCPS/Announcements/DCPS-Chinese_Welcome_Back_Parents_2010.pdf Welcome Letter to Parents (Chinese)]." 华府公立学校</ref>('''District of Columbia Public Schools''', '''DCPS''')是[[华盛顿哥伦比亚特区|华盛顿哥伦比亚特区]]的[[学区|学区]]。DCPS有45，000名学生和4，000名老师。<ref>"[http://www.dc.gov/DCPS/About+DCPS/Who+We+Are Who We Are]." 华府公立学校.</ref> DCPS有66个小学，13个初中，18个高中，19个学区(education campus)”，6个特殊教育学校(special education school)，和7个其他学校(other program)。<ref>"[http://www.dc.gov/DCPS/About+DCPS/Who+We+Are/Facts+and+Statistics Facts and Statistics]." 华府公立学校.</ref>

'''尚·嘉賓'''（Jean Gabin）是一位[[法國|法國]][[演員|演員]]，曾主演許多經典[[電影|電影]]。

==生平==
尚·嘉賓出生於巴黎，雙親是瑪德蓮·普蒂（Madeleine Petit）與費迪南·蒙柯吉（Ferdinand Moncorgé）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://books.google.ca/books?id=pRkMAQAAMAAJ&q=Ferdinand+Moncorg%C3%A9+Petit&dq=Ferdinand+Moncorg%C3%A9+Petit&hl=en&redir_esc=y |title=Qui est qui en France - Jacques Lafitte, Stephen Taylor - Google Books |publisher=Books.google.ca |date=2010-07-02 |accessdate=2014-07-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.filmreference.com/Actors-and-Actresses-El-Ga/Gabin-Jean.html |title=Jean Gabin - Actors and Actresses - Films as Actor:, Publications |publisher=Filmreference.com |date= |accessdate=2014-07-16}}</ref>

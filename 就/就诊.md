《'''就诊'''》（{{lang-en|''In Treatment''}}）是一部[[HBO|HBO]][[电视剧|电视剧]]，讲述一位[[心理医生|心理医生]]和他每周预约的[[病人|病人]]的故事。该剧首播于2008年1月28日，每周5集，每集主要讲述一个病人。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://smallscreen.monstersandcritics.com/news/article_1383048.php/HBO_announces_January_2008_schedule# |title=HBO announces January 2008 schedule |work=[[Monsters_and_Critics|Monsters and Critics]] |first=Stone |last=Martindale |date=2007-12-24 |accessdate=2008-03-05 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080117064914/http://smallscreen.monstersandcritics.com/news/article_1383048.php/HBO_announces_January_2008_schedule |archivedate=2008-01-17 }}</ref> 本剧的模式、情节、音乐均根据曾在[[以色列|以色列]]大获成功的电视剧《{{link-he|就诊 (以色列电视剧)|בטיפול|就诊}}》改编。

'''阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦和平奖'''（{{lang-en|'''Albert Einstein Peace Prize'''}}）设立于1980年，是由'''阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦和平奖基金会'''每年颁发一次的和平奖项，主要奖励对核裁军运动做出贡献者。 该基金会成立于1979年，即[[阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦|阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦]]诞辰一百周年，并唤起了人们对敦促[[核裁军运动|核裁军]]的[[罗素—爱因斯坦宣言|罗素–爱因斯坦宣言]]的关注。在爱因斯坦的[[財產|地产]]受托人的赞助下<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://profiles.nlm.nih.gov/BB/A/P/L/A/_/bbapla.pdf|title=Letter from the Albert Einstein Peace Prize Foundation to Joshua Lederberg|accessdate=11 January 2016|date=14 December 1979|last=Cousins|first=Norman|authorlink=Norman Cousins|format=PDF|work=The Joshua Lederberg Papers|publisher=[[United_States_National_Library_of_Medicine|United States National Library of Medicine]]}}</ref>，威廉·M·施瓦茨（1912-1987）成立了[[阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦和平奖基金会|阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦和平奖基金会]]，后者是一个富有的商人，也是活动家[[亚伦·斯沃茨|阿龙·施瓦茨]]的祖父。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.suntimes.com/business/17594002-420/aaron-swartz-killed-by-government-his-father-says-at-funeral.html|title=Aaron Swartz was ‘killed by government,’ father says at funeral|accessdate=10 January 2016|date=15 January 2013|last=Guy|first=Sandra|work=Sun-Times|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130118040220/http://www.suntimes.com/business/17594002-420/aaron-swartz-killed-by-government-his-father-says-at-funeral.html|archivedate=18 January 2013}}</ref><ref name="Heise1987">{{Cite news|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1987-11-13/news/8703250765_1_albert-einstein-peace-prize-nuclear-weapons-mr-swartz|title=William Swartz, Peace Agency Founder|last=Heise|first=Kenan|date=November 13, 1987|work=[[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]]|accessdate=10 January 2016}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=William M. Swartz, 1912–1987|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5gUAAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA42|date=January–February 1988|journal=Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists|publisher=Educational Foundation for Nuclear Science, Inc.|accessdate=10 January 2016|page=42}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=The Life and Afterlife of Aaron Swartz|url=http://nymag.com/news/features/aaron-swartz-2013-2/index2.html|last=Yang|first=Wesley|date=February 8, 2013|journal=New York magazine}}</ref> 威廉·M·施瓦茨曾参与[[帕格沃什科学和世界事务会议|帕格沃什会议科学和世界事务会议]]，他建立基金会来作为对帕格沃什会议的支持。本奖项的奖励为50,000美元。

1992年以後，未持續頒發。

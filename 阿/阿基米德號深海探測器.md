'''阿基米德號'''（{{lang-en|'''Archimède'''}}）是法國海軍建造的深海探測器。她由Pierre Willm和Georges Houot設計<ref>Collins, "Exploring the Deep", '''Andrei Aksyonov, Aleksandr Alekseevich Chernov''', ''1979'', p.118</ref>，使用42,000加侖的[[己烷|己烷]]作為浮力控制來源<ref>Popular Science, [https://books.google.com/books?id=2CUDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA42&dq=bathyscaphe+archimede&hl=en&ei=z3GMTJ2cBcGC8gbawdCdDA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CCwQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=bathyscaphe%20archimede&f=false "Jeeps in the Deep"], '''Wallace Cloud''', ''August 1964'' (accessed 11 September 2010)</ref>。阿基米德號是第一艘潛入[[大西洋|大西洋]]最深處的載具（[[波多黎各海溝|波多黎各海溝]]，深度27,510英尺（8,390米））<ref>Popular Mechanics, [https://books.google.com/books?id=yykDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA88&dq=bathyscaphe+archimede&hl=en&ei=z3GMTJ2cBcGC8gbawdCdDA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=31&ved=0CLIBEOgBMB4#v=onepage&q=archimede&f=false "The Ocean Adventure"], '''Gardner Soule''', ''September 1966'', pp.92 (accessed 11 September 2010)</ref>。

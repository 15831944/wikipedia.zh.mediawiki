{{瑣羅亞斯德教}}

'''阿胡那·法里耶'''（[[阿維斯陀語|阿維斯陀語]]：Ahuna Vairya）或稱'''阿塔·阿胡·瓦伊里尤'''，是[[瑣羅亞斯德教|瑣羅亞斯德教]]最重要的祈禱詩，位於《亞斯納》第二十七章第十三節。


:「偉大的瑣羅亞斯德因真誠而被選中，那上界的首領在自己行為的寶庫捍衛人們的善行，復活日之際將其奉獻給馬茲達。馬茲達的天國屬於貧窮百姓的庇護者。」--《阿胡那·法里耶》<ref>《阿維斯塔：瑣羅亞斯德教聖書》，25頁（篇首）</ref>


:''<big>yaϑā ahū vairyō aϑā ratuš aṣ̌āt̰cīt̰ hacā</big>''
:''<big>vaŋhə̄uš dazdā manaŋhō š́yaoϑananąm aŋhə̄uš mazdāi</big>''
:''<big>xṣ̌aϑrəmcā ahurāi ā yim drigubyō dadat̰ vāstārəm</big>'' --《阿胡那·法里耶》原文Geldner版<ref>Karl F. Geldner, Avesta, the Sacred Books of the Parsis, Stuttgart, 1896</ref>


:"As judgment is to be chosen by the world, so the judgment (which is) in accord with the truth,
:(which is to be passed) on the actions of good throughout the world, is assigned to the Wise (Lord) (''Mazdāi''),
:and the power (is assigned) to the (Wise) Lord (''ahurāi'') whom they established as shepherd to the needy." --《阿胡那·法里耶》Humbach英文版<ref>Humbach, Helmut; Elfenbein, Josef; Skjærvø, Prods O. (1991), The Gāthās of Zarathushtra and other Old Avestan texts, Part II, Commentary, Heidelberg: Winter, pp. 1–15, 248 
</ref>

'''阿拉万区'''是[[吉尔吉斯斯坦|吉尔吉斯斯坦]]西南州份[[奧什州|奧什州]]下辖的一个[[区_(苏联)|区]]。该区治所位于阿拉万。该区面积为{{convert|1340|km2}}，是奥什州最小的区。2009年常住人口为106,134人。<ref name=stat>{{cite web|url=http://212.42.101.100:8088/nacstat/sites/default/files/%D0%9E%D1%88%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B0%D1%8F%20%D0%BE%D0%B1%D0%BB%D0%B0%D1%81%D1%82%D1%8C.pdf |title=2009 population census of the Kyrgyz Republic: Osh Region |accessdate=2011-03-22 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110810173811/http://212.42.101.100:8088/nacstat/sites/default/files/%D0%9E%D1%88%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B0%D1%8F%20%D0%BE%D0%B1%D0%BB%D0%B0%D1%81%D1%82%D1%8C.pdf |archivedate=10 August 2011 }}</ref>

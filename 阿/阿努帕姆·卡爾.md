'''阿努帕姆·卡爾'''（{{lang-en|'''Anupam Kher'''}}，{{bd|1955年|3月7日|catIdx=Kher, Anupam}}）<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/happy-birthday-anupam-kher-as-anupam-kher-turns-61-here-is-wishing-bollywood-best-on-screen-dad-4558285/ “Happy Birthday Anupam Kher: As Anupam Kher turns 61, here’s wishing Bollywood’s best on-screen dad”]. The Indian Express. 07-03-2017.</ref>是一名[[印度|印度]]知名男演員。他获得了两个[[印度國家電影獎|印度國家電影獎]]奖项和八个[[印度電影觀眾獎|印度電影觀眾獎]]荣誉。

阿努帕姆·卡爾最為人所知的[[宝莱坞|宝莱坞]]电影有《Daddy》、《Saaransh》、《Lamhe》、《Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge》等。宝莱坞以外，他也在多个国际电影中出现，包括：《[[我愛碧咸|我愛碧咸]]》、《[[色，戒_(電影)|色，戒]]》和《[[乌云背后的幸福线|乌云背后的幸福线]]》。<ref>[https://www.hindustantimes.com/tv/anupam-kher-to-star-in-bbc-one-s-mrs-wilson-memoir-of-alison-wilson/story-bR1WjrRZj3SD4imMWrHVVK.html “Anupam Kher to star in BBC One’s Mrs Wilson, memoir of Alison Wilson Inspired by the memoir of Alison Wilson”]. Hindustan Times. 20-04-2018.</ref><ref>[https://mashable.com/2017/07/09/anupam-kher-the-big-sick/?europe=true “Bollywood's dad, Anupam Kher, on the unique warmth of 'The Big Sick'”]. Mashable. 09-07-2017.</ref>

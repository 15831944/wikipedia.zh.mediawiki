'''阿卡波語'''('''Aka-Bo'''、亦稱:巴語<Ba>)為一種[[絕跡語言|已滅絕]]的[[大安達曼語系|大安達曼語]]。它過去使用於[[安达曼群岛|北安達曼群島]]的中西部海岸<ref name=Brown>Radcliffe-Brown, Alfred R. (1922). ''The Andaman Islanders''. Cambridge:  Cambridge University Press.</ref> 及印度的[[安达曼群岛|安达曼群岛]]的北礁島。阿卡波語的阿卡(Aka-)為大安達曼語系有關[[舌|舌頭]]方面字詞的[[前綴|前綴]]詞，也是關於[[語言|語言]]方面字詞的前綴詞。<ref name=Templ>Temple, Richard C. (1902). ''A Grammar of the Andamanese Languages, being Chapter IV of Part I of the Census Report on the Andaman and Nicobar Islands''. Superintendent's Printing Press: Port Blair.</ref>

老寶兒(Boa Sr.)女士為最後一位阿卡波語的說話人，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.andamanese.net/generalia.html|title=GA Community|publisher=Vanishing Voices of the Great Andamanese (VOGA)|accessdate=2011-01-21}}</ref> 死於2010年1月26日，享壽約85歲。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.andamanese.net/BoaSr_Obituary.html|title=Obituary for Boa Sr.|publisher=Vanishing Voices of the Great Andamanese (VOGA)|accessdate=2010-02-05|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100210000858/http://www.andamanese.net/BoaSr_Obituary.html|archivedate=10 二月 2010|deadurl=no}}</ref>

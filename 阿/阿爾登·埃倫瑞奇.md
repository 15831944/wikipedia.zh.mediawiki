'''艾登·迦勒·艾倫瑞克'''（{{lang-en|'''Alden Caleb Ehrenreich'''}}，{{bd|1989年|11月22日||}}） 是一位[[美國|美國]][[男演員|男演員]]<ref name="Alden Caleb Ehrenreich">{{cite web|url=https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:VG3G-KGH|title=Alden Caleb Ehrenreich|website=[[FamilySearch|FamilySearch]]|access-date=November 9, 2015}}</ref><ref name="Interview Mag">{{cite web|url=http://www.interviewmagazine.com/film/alden-ehrenreich/|title=Alden Ehrenreich|website=[[Interview_(magazine)|Interview]]|last=Martin|first=Michael|date=May 1, 2010|access-date=May 27, 2010}}</ref>，他出演的第一部電影是[[獨立製片|獨立製片]]《{{le|柯波拉之家族秘辛|Tetro}}》，知名作品有2013年{{le|理查·拉葛瑞夫尼斯|Richard LaGravenese}}導演的奇幻電影《[[美麗魔物|美麗魔物]]》。2016年5月，[[迪士尼影業|迪士尼影業]]與[[盧卡斯影業|盧卡斯影業]]宣布艾倫瑞克將在《[[星際大戰|星際大戰]]》外傳電影《[[星際大戰外傳：韓索羅|星際大戰外傳：韓索羅]]》飾演年輕版[[韓·索羅|韓·索羅]]<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.hypesphere.com/archives/73810|title= [新聞] 年輕版韓索羅人選出爐！《美麗魔物》艾登艾倫瑞克將主演韓索羅外傳電影|work= Hypesphere狂熱球電影資訊網|date=2016-05-06|accessdate=2016-05-06}}</ref>。

==個人經歷==
艾倫瑞克出生在美國加利福尼亞州洛杉磯。艾倫瑞克擁有八分之七的[[猶太|猶太]]血統和八分之一的英國血統<ref name=":2" /><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.thejc.com/culture/film/alden-ehrenreich-the-new-han-solo-in-star-wars-1.464503|title=Alden Ehrenreich: Going Solo|last=Allen|first=Robert|date=May 24, 2018|website=The Jewish Chronicle|access-date=May 24, 2018}}</ref><ref name=temref>{{cite web|last=|first=|authorlink=|coauthors=|title=Kvell Corner|publisher=Kehillat Israel Reconstructionist Congregation|date=January–February 2008|url=http://kehillatisrael.org/_downloader.php?file_id=191|accessdate=2010-05-27|quote=Mazel Tov to Alden Ehrenreich, son of Sari Ehrenreich, who was "discovered by Steven Spielberg"|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110726214729/http://kehillatisrael.org/_downloader.php?file_id=191|archivedate=2011-07-26}}</ref>。他以導演{{tsl|en|Phil Alden Robinson|菲爾·阿爾登·羅賓森}}的名字命名<ref name=":0" />。導演{{tsl|en|Phil Alden Robinson|菲爾·阿爾登·羅賓森}}是他們一家人的朋友<ref name=":0" />。

艾倫瑞克的父親Mark Ehrenreich是會計師<ref name=":0" />，父親曾經是[[史蒂芬·史匹柏|史蒂芬·史匹柏]]的會計師<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://radaronline.com/exclusives/2018/05/alden-ehrenreich-father-murdered-stabbed-death-schizophrenic-uncle/|title=Star Wars' Actor Alden Ehrenreich Saw Father Get Murdered By Schizophrenic Uncle|last=Tillett|first=Andy|date=May 22, 2018|work=Radar Online|access-date=May 30, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thesun.co.uk/tvandshowbiz/6295711/han-solo-actor-alden-ehrenreich-saw-his-uncle-stab-his-father-to-death-at-the-age-of-8/|title=With Solo set for UK release, we reveal star's secret childhood torment|last=Rollings|first=Grant|date=May 15, 2018|work=The Sun|access-date=May 30, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://metro.co.uk/2018/05/15/han-solo-actor-alden-ehrenreich-saw-dad-stabbed-death-uncle-age-eight-7549248/|title=Han Solo actor Alden Ehrenreich 'saw his dad stabbed to death by his uncle'|last=Baillie|first=Katie|date=2018-05-15|work=|newspaper=Metro|accessdate=2018-07-25}}</ref>。在艾倫瑞克7歲時，父親去世<ref name=":1" />。繼父Harry Aronowitz是牙醫<ref name="Alden Caleb Ehrenreich"/><ref name="Interview Mag"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.courant.com/topic/la-hm-holidecor1dec01,0,5897061.story?page=2|title=Tradition, but with a twist|website=Hartford Courant|first=David A.|last=Keeps|date=December 1, 2005|access-date=May 27, 2010}}</ref>。母親Sari是室內設計師<ref name=latimes1>{{cite news|first=Jeanie|last=Pyun|title=The Hollywood Insider's Guide to L.A. Private Schools|work=The Hollywood Reporter|date=August 17, 2017|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/lists/hollywood-insiders-guide-la-private-schools-1029445/item/hollywood-guide-la-schools-westside-1030388|accessdate=May 6, 2018}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{cite web|first=Alex|last=Pappademas|title=ALDEN EHRENREICH IS READY TO PROVE HE'S WORTHY OF HAN SOLO|url=https://www.esquire.com/entertainment/movies/a19834126/alden-ehrenreich-star-wars-han-solo-interview/|work=Esquire magazine|date=April 24, 2018|accessdate=May 6, 2018}}</ref>。

在成長的過程中，艾倫瑞克出席[[猶太教重建派|猶太教重建派]]的[[猶太會堂|猶太會堂]]<ref name=":0" />。並且，他接受了猶太教的成年禮（Bar Mitzvah）儀式<ref name=":2">{{cite news | title = Alden Ehrenreich - Biography | url= http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2403277/bio | work = IMDb }}</ref>。艾倫瑞克認為自己是[[猶太人|猶太人]]<ref>{{cite web|first=Amos|last=Barshad|url=http://www.thefader.com/2018/04/24/alden-ehrenreich-interview-new-han-solo-solo-a-star-wars-story|title=Alden Ehrenreich can be your intergalactic hero|work=The Fader magazine|date=April 24, 2018|accessdate=April 27, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Alden Ehrenreich to play Han Solo in Star Wars spin-off|url=https://www.thejc.com/news/world/alden-ehrenreich-to-play-han-solo-in-star-wars-spin-off-1.60904|work=The Jewish Chronicle|date=July 18, 2016|accessdate=May 6, 2018}}</ref>。

14歲時，艾倫瑞克為了猶太朋友自導自演一部影片，並且在這一位猶太女孩的成年禮儀式上播出，而[[史蒂芬·史匹柏|史蒂芬·史匹柏]]也是這個成年禮的客人，在看了影片之後決定發掘艾倫瑞克<ref>{{cite news | title = Meet the Coen Brothers' New Breakout Star: Alden Ehrenreich | url= http://www.rollingstone.com/movies/news/meet-the-coen-brothers-new-breakout-star-alden-ehrenreich-20160204 | work = Rolling Stone magazine | date = February 4, 2016 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Hill |first=Logan |url=http://www.pastemagazine.com/around_the_web/2009/06/francis-ford-coppolas-new-star-alden-ehrenreich-on.html |title=Francis Ford Coppola’s New Star Alden Ehrenreich on Not ‘Trickling Up’ - Vulture |publisher=Pastemagazine.com |date= |accessdate=2012-07-26}}</ref>。在史蒂芬·史匹柏的推薦幫助下，艾倫瑞克與[[夢工廠|夢工廠]]的選角導演見面<ref>{{cite news | title = The Man Who Would Be Han Solo | url= https://www.nytimes.com/2016/05/06/fashion/mens-style/alden-ehrenreich-actor-hollywood.html | work = The New York Times | date = May 6, 2016 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news | title = Alden Ehrenreich Cast as Young Han Solo: 5 Things to Know About Star Wars' Newest Star | url= http://www.eonline.com/news/762802/alden-ehrenreich-cast-as-young-han-solo-5-things-to-know-about-star-wars-newest-star | work = E! News | date =  May 6, 2016 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.tnp.sg/entertainment/movies/alden-ehrenreich-hits-big-time-and-flies-solo-star-wars-story|title=Alden Ehrenreich hits big time and flies Solo in A Star Wars Story|last=Tatna|first=Meher|date=May 23, 2018|newspaper=The New Paper|accessdate=June 21, 2018}}</ref>。談起這段往事，[[史蒂芬·史匹柏|史蒂芬·史匹柏]]說：「是在一個成年禮的影片中，我的女兒和艾倫瑞克一起演戲為了他們最好的朋友。他們給我看了這一部影片，我很喜歡它，所以我幫艾倫瑞克找了經紀人。這一切都是這樣開始的<ref>{{cite news | title = EXCLUSIVE: Steven Spielberg on Discovering Young Han Solo: 'People Don't Know How Really Funny He Is' | url= http://www.etonline.com/news/190763_exclusive_steven_spielberg_on_discovering_young_han_solo_alden_ehrenreichin | work = Entertainment Tonight | date = June 10, 2016 }}</ref>。」

由於史蒂芬·史匹柏的推薦，艾倫瑞克客串演出電視劇《[[CSI犯罪現場|CSI犯罪現場]]》2006年其中一集（飾演Sven）以及在2005年《[[超自然檔案|超自然檔案]]》其中一集裡飾演Ben Collins<ref>{{cite news | title = Alden Ehrenreich is tipped to star as Han Solo but who is he? | url= http://www.bbc.co.uk/newsbeat/article/36046031/alden-ehrenreich-is-tipped-to-star-as-han-solo-but-who-is-he | work = BBC | date = April 15, 2016 }}</ref>。

2009年，艾倫瑞克花幾個月時間待在[[阿根廷|阿根廷]][[布宜諾斯艾利斯|布宜諾斯艾利斯]]，主演[[法蘭西斯·柯波拉|法蘭西斯·柯波拉]]執導的劇情片《柯波拉之家族秘辛》<ref name=interviewmag>{{cite news|last=Martin|first=Michael|coauthors=|title=Alden Ehrenreich|pages=|publisher=Interview Magazine|date=2010-05-01|url=http://www.interviewmagazine.com/film/alden-ehrenreich/|accessdate=2010-05-27}}</ref>。2011年再度跟法蘭西斯·柯波拉合作驚悚片《[[此刻與日出之間|此刻與日出之間]]》，此片其他演員包括[[方·基墨|方·基墨]]、[[布魯斯·鄧恩|布魯斯·鄧恩]]和[[艾兒·芬妮|艾兒·芬妮]]。

2016年5月5日，迪士尼影業與盧卡斯影業宣布艾倫瑞克將在[[韓·索羅|韓·索羅]]獨立電影中飾演年輕的韓，電影將由[[朗·霍華|朗·霍華]]執導，主要敘述《[[星際大戰四部曲：曙光乍現|星際大戰四部曲：曙光乍現]]》前韓與[[丘巴卡|丘巴卡]]的原創故事<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2016/05/star-wars-han-solo-movie-alden-ehrenreich-frontrunner-cast-1201737425/|title=Alden Ehrenreich Lands The Lead In ‘Star Wars’ Han Solo Film|first=Anita|last=Busch|website=Deadline.com|date=May 5, 2016|access-date=May 5, 2016}}</ref>。

'''阿拉歐阿帕哈''' ({{lang-ar|الله أبهى}}, Alláh-u-Abhá; lit. ''God is the Most Glorious'')是巴哈伊信徒见面时的问候语。<ref name="log">{{cite book |author = Compilations |editor = Hornby, Helen (Ed.) |year = 1983 |title = Lights of Guidance: A Bahá'í Reference File |publisher = Bahá'í Publishing Trust, New Delhi, India |isbn = 81-85091-46-3 |url = http://bahai-library.com/hornby_lights_guidance_2&chapter=1#n893 | pages = 266}}</ref>''阿帕哈''是巴哈一词的最高级形态<ref name="lambden">{{cite journal | journal = Bahá'í Studies Review | volume = 3 | issue = 1 | year = 1993 | first = Stephen | last = Lambden | title = The Word Bahá': Quintessence of the Greatest Name | url = http://bahai-library.com/lambden_quintessence_greatest_name}}</ref>，是“至大圣名”的一种形式。<ref name="log"/>根据[[巴哈伊信仰|巴哈伊信仰]]的创立者[[巴哈欧拉|巴哈欧拉]]在其律法书《[[亚格达斯经|亚格达斯经]]》中的描述，巴哈伊信徒被要求每日吟诵九十五遍“阿拉歐阿帕哈”。<ref name="smith">{{cite encyclopedia |last= Smith |first= Peter |encyclopedia= A concise encyclopedia of the Bahá'í Faith |title= prayer |year= 2000 |publisher=Oneworld Publications |location= Oxford |pages= 274–275 |isbn= 1-85168-184-1}}</ref>这句话的意义和和伊斯兰教中[[安拉胡阿克巴|安拉胡阿克巴]] （阿拉伯语：“真主至大”）或 [[苏卜哈南拉西|苏卜哈南拉西]]（“安拉超绝”）的使用场景类似。阿拉歐阿帕哈在表达问候和告别时均可使用。

“{{transl|ar|DIN|阿拉歐}}”是“阿拉”的主格形态，意为“上帝”；“{{transl|ar|DIN|阿帕哈}}”是“巴哈”的从格形态，意为“美丽”、“光辉”。“阿拉歐阿帕哈”常被翻译为“上帝是至为荣耀的”。

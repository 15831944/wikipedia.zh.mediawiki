'''阿斯玛·马夫兹'''（{{lang-en|'''Asmaa Mahfouz'''}}，{{lang-ar|'''أسماء محفوظ'''}}，{{bd|1985年|2月1日|||Mahfouz, Asmaa}}），埃及女性人权活动家，是[[2011年埃及革命|2011年埃及革命]]中[[4月6日青年运动|4月6日青年运动]]的发起人<ref name='NYT110202'>{{cite news | first = Mona | last = El-Naggar | title = Equal rights takes to the barricades | date = 1 February 2011 | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2011/02/02/world/middleeast/02iht-letter02.html | work=The New York Times | accessdate =6 February 2011}}</ref>：她在运动爆发之前一周的1月18日通过发布网络视频号召人民参与示威<ref>{{cite news | title = Women play vital role in Egypt's uprising | url = http://www.npr.org/2011/02/04/133497422/Women-Play-Vital-Role-In-Egypts-Uprising | date = 4 February 2011 | work=National Public Radio | format = transcript | accessdate =6 February 2011}}</ref><ref> {{cite news | title = Revolutionary blogger Asma threatened | date = 5 February 2011 | url = http://gulfnews.com/news/region/egypt/revolutionary-blogger-asma-threatened-1.757171 | work=Gulf News | accessdate =6 February 2011}}</ref>。她是[[:en:Coalition_of_the_Youth_of_the_Revolution|革命青年聯盟]]的主要成员以及革命期间的领袖之一<ref> {{cite news | first = Heba | last = Fahmy | title = Youth Coalition says army agrees to remove cabinet and other demands | date = 1 March 2011 | url = http://www.thedailynewsegypt.com/egypt/youth-coalition-says-army-agrees-to-remove-cabinet-and-other-demands.html | work = Daily News Egypt / International Herald Tribune | accessdate = 4 March 2011 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110307145615/http://www.thedailynewsegypt.com/egypt/youth-coalition-says-army-agrees-to-remove-cabinet-and-other-demands.html | archivedate = 7 三月 2011 }}</ref>。

2011年10月27日，她与另外其他阿拉伯国家的四人因促进本国的人权与自由而获得[[欧洲议会|欧洲议会]]颁发的[[萨哈罗夫奖|萨哈罗夫奖]]。

== 生平 ==

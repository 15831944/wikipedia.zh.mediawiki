'''阿爾塔蒙特自由音樂會'''（{{lang-en|'''Altamont Free Concert'''}}）是於1969年12月6日星期六在[[美國|美國]][[北加利福尼亞州|北]][[加利福尼亚州|加州]]{{link-en|阿爾塔蒙特賽車場|Altamont Raceway Park}}舉辦的[[1960年代反文化运动|反文化]]搖滾音樂會。<ref name=chitrib3h>{{cite news |url=http://archives.chicagotribune.com/1969/12/07/page/1/article/300-000-jam-musical-bash |work=Chicago Tribune |title=300,000 jam musical bash |date=December 7, 1969 |page=1, sec. 1}}</ref><ref name=rfjfwtrf>{{cite news |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=-eYRAAAAIBAJ&sjid=aOsDAAAAIBAJ&pg=7307%2C2326828 |work=Spokesman-Review |location=(Spokane, Washington) |agency=Associated Press |title=Rockfest jams freeway traffic |date=December 7, 1969 |page=2}}</ref><ref name=benbul8>{{cite news |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=LAJYAAAAIBAJ&sjid=HPcDAAAAIBAJ&pg=3201%2C983078 |work=The Bulletin |location=(Bend, Oregon) |agency=UPI |title=Biggest rock concert ends |date=December 8, 1969 |page=7}}</ref>

這場音樂會因其爆發的暴力事件而聞名，其中包括{{link-en|梅雷迪思·杭特之死|Death of Meredith Hunter|梅雷迪思·杭特遭捅死}}與和三起意外死亡事件：兩起由一場[[肇事逃逸|肇事逃逸]]車禍造成，一起是由吸食[[LSD|LSD]]引發的灌溉渠[[遇溺|溺水]]造成。<ref name=benbul8/>數起的受傷事件、許多汽車被盜並被遺棄，造成大量財產損失。<ref>{{cite web|last=Ortega |first=Tony |url=http://blogs.villagevoice.com/runninscared/archives/2010/08/in_the_wake_of_1.php |archive-url=https://archive.is/20120701173943/http://blogs.villagevoice.com/runninscared/archives/2010/08/in_the_wake_of_1.php |dead-url=yes |archive-date=2012-07-01 |title=Viewing the Remains of a Mean Saturday Village Voice December 18, 1969 |work=Village Voice |date=2010-08-24 |accessdate=2010-10-25 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.livermorehistory.com/Newsletters/2011-01-02_Jan_Feb.pdf "Altamont Rock Festival of 1969: The Aftermath"], ''Livermore Heritage Guild Journal'', January/February 2011</ref>

這場演出的嘉賓有[[山塔那合唱團|山塔那合唱團]]、[[傑佛森飛船|傑佛森飛船]]、{{link-en|飛行捲餅兄弟|The Flying Burrito Brothers}}、[[克羅斯比、史提爾斯、納許與尼爾·楊|克羅斯比、史提爾斯、納許與尼爾·楊（CSNY）]]以及壓軸登場的[[滚石乐队|滾石樂團]]。<ref name=lib>{{cite journal |authors=Bangs, Lester; Brown, Reny; Burks, John; Egan, Sammy; Goodwin, Michael; Link, Geoffrey; Marcus, Greil; Morthland, John; Schoenfeld, Eugene; Thomas, Patrick; Winner, Langdon|title=The Rolling Stones Disaster at Altamont: Let It Bleed|journal=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]]|date=21 January 1970|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/the-rolling-stones-disaster-at-altamont-let-it-bleed-19700121 |accessdate=8 January 2015}}</ref>身為主辦方的[[感恩至死|感恩至死]]原本也有計畫在CSNY表演結束後上場演出，但由於現場暴力事件不斷增加而作罷。<ref>{{cite journal |last=Lydon |first=Michael |title=An Evening with the Grateful Dead |journal=Rolling Stone |date=September 1970}}</ref>一位《''[[滾石_(雜誌)|滾石雜誌]]''》的工作人員在關於這次活動的詳細敘述中如此寫道：「這就是阿爾塔蒙特的事件發展—非常糟糕，糟到連身為組織和宣傳這個活動的感恩至死都沒有上場演出。這是搖滾樂史上最爛的一天，12月6日，一切都完全出錯了。」<ref>{{cite journal|title=Disaster at Altamont: Let It Bleed|journal=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]]|date=21 January 1970|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/the-rolling-stones-disaster-at-altamont-let-it-bleed-19700121?page=12|accessdate=18 March 2016}}</ref><ref name=rrwd>{{cite journal|last=Burks|first=John|title=Rock & Roll's Worst Day|journal=[[Rolling_Stone|Rolling Stone]]|date=7 February 1970|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/5934386/rock__rolls_worst_day|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080314073233/http://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/5934386/rock__rolls_worst_day|archivedate=14 March 2008|accessdate=24 May 2013}}</ref>

大約有三十萬人參加了音樂會，<ref name=chitrib3h/><ref name=benbul8/> 其中一些人預期這將會是「西部版的[[胡士托音樂節|胡士托]]」。<ref>[http://www.livermorehistory.com/Newsletters/2010_03-04_MarApr.pdf "Altamont Rock Festival: '60s Abruptly End"], ''Livermore Heritage Guild Journal'', March/April 2010</ref>電影製片人{{link-en|艾伯特和大衛·梅斯萊斯|Albert and David Maysles}}拍攝了這整起事件的片段，並將其製作為1970年的紀錄片電影《''{{link-en|Gimme Shelter (1970電影)|Gimme Shelter (1970 film)|Gimme Shelter}}''》。

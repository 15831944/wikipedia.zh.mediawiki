'''阿爾斯特蘇格蘭語'''（{{lang-en|'''Ulster Scots'''}}或'''Ulster-Scots'''，阿爾斯特蘇格蘭語：'''Ulstèr-Scotch'''）<ref name="Ulster-Scots Agency">[http://www.ulsterscotsagency.com/ Ulster-Scots Agency]</ref><ref name="Anent Oorsels">[http://www.ulsterscotslanguage.com/usl/about-us/ Anent Oorsels] The Ulster-Scots Language Society.</ref>指的是在[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]][[阿爾斯特省|阿爾斯特省]]境內所說的一種[[低地蘇格蘭語|低地蘇格蘭語]][[方言|方言]]。<ref name="Gregg R.J. 1972">Gregg R.J. (1972) "The Scotch-Irish Dialect Boundaries in Ulster" in Wakelin M. F., ''Patterns in the Folk Speech of The British Isles'', London</ref><ref name="C. Macafee 2001 p121">C. Macafee (2001) "Lowland Sources of Ulster Scots" in J.M. Kirk & D.P. Ó Baoill, ''Languages Links: The Languages of Scotland and Ireland'', Cló Ollscoil na Banríona, Belfast, p121</ref><ref>J. Harris (1985) ''Phonological Variation and Change: Studies in Hiberno English'', Cambridge, p15</ref> 一些區分方法把帶著阿爾斯特蘇格蘭語口音的[[標準英語|標準英語]]也算在內。<ref>Gregg R.J. (1964) "Scotch-Irish Urban Speech in Ulster: A Phonological Study of the Regional Standard English of Larne, County Antrim" in Adams G.B. ''Ulster Dialects: an Introductory Symposium'', Cultura: Ulster Folk Museum</ref><ref name="Harris 1985 p14">J. Harris (1985) ''Phonological Variation and Change: Studies in Hiberno English'', Cambridge. p.14</ref> 阿爾斯特蘇格蘭與受到了[[愛爾蘭英語|愛爾蘭英語]]，特別是[[中阿爾斯特英語|中阿爾斯特英語]]和[[阿爾斯特愛爾蘭語|阿爾斯特愛爾蘭語]]的影響。因為受英語和愛爾蘭語的影響不同，其可分為偏向英語的或偏向愛爾蘭語的阿爾斯特蘇格蘭語。<ref name="Harris 1985 p14"/>

17世紀早期蘇格蘭移民將低地蘇格蘭語帶到了阿爾斯特地區，<ref name="Montgomery & Gregg 1997: 572">Montgomery & Gregg 1997: 572</ref> 而阿爾斯特地區最早的低地蘇格蘭語書寫歷史也只能追溯到這個時期。到20世紀末，阿爾斯特地區的低地蘇格蘭語書寫開始與蘇格蘭本地發生區分。<ref name="Montgomery & Gregg 1997: 585">Montgomery & Gregg 1997: 585</ref> 新的[[拼字法|拼字法]]便被創造出來了。<ref name="aodan">[http://www.bbc.co.uk/northernireland/learning/history/stateapart/agreement/culture/support/cul2_c013.shtml Language, Identity and Politics in Northern Ireland by Aodan Mac Poilin]</ref>

==名稱==

阿爾斯特蘇格蘭語在英語中曾被書寫為“Scotch-Irish”，但現在通常寫作“Ulster Scots”。<ref>Harris J. (1985) ''Phonological Variation and Change: Studies in Hiberno English'', Cambridge, p13</ref> 說這種語言的人通常稱其為“Braid Scots”、<ref name="Traynor, 36" /> “Scotch”、<ref name="Traynor, 244" /><ref>Nic Craith M. (2002) Plural Identities—singular Narratives. Berghahn Books. p.107</ref> 或“hamely tongue”。<ref>Fenton J. (1995) The Hamely Tongue: A Personal Record of Ulster-Scots in County Antrim, Ulster-Scots Academic Press</ref> 1980年代起，一個[[新詞|新詞]]“Ullans”開始普及，這個詞是由<ref>Falconer G. (2006) "The Scots Tradition in Ulster", ''Scottish Studies Review'', Vol. 7, No. 2. p.97</ref> “Ulster”和“[[Lallans|Lallans]]”（蘇格蘭語“蘇格蘭低地”）組合而成的，ref>Hickey R. (2004) A Sound Atlas of Irish English. Walter de Gruyter. p.156</ref> 但同時也是“'''U'''lster-Scots '''l'''anguage in '''l'''iterature '''a'''nd '''n'''ative '''s'''peech”<ref name="Tymoczko M. 2003 p.159">Tymoczko M. & Ireland C. A. (2003) Language and Tradition in Ireland: Continuities and Displacements, Univ of Massachusetts Press. p.159</ref> 的首字母縮寫。但也有人使用老式的“Ulstèr-Scotch”。<ref name="Ulster-Scots Agency"/><ref name="Anent Oorsels"/> 間或有人使用“Hiberno-Scots”，<ref>Wells J.C. (1982) ''Accents of English: The British Isles'', Cambridge University Press p.449</ref> 但這個詞一般指的是[[阿爾斯特蘇格蘭人|阿爾斯特蘇格蘭人]]。<ref>Winston A. (1997) ''Global Convulsions: Race, Ethnicity, and Nationalism at the End of the Twentieth Century'', SUNY Press p.161</ref>

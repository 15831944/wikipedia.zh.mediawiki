'''阿拉斯加虎'''（英文：Alaskan Tiger）亦称“'''白色死神'''”（White Death）是一种出没于[[美国|美国]][[阿拉斯加州|阿拉斯加州]]{{link-en|帕克森|Paxson, Alaska}}附近沼泽地区的[[神秘生物列表|神秘动物]]。据记载，阿拉斯加虎长约9英尺（2.74米）、肩高5-6英尺（1.5-1.8米），<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://cryptid-wendigo.tumblr.com/post/137088424079/the-alaskan-tiger-also-known-as-white-death-is|title=Cryptozoology Informational Blog • The Alaskan Tiger, also known as White Death, is...|newspaper=Cryptozoology Informational Blog|accessdate=2018-08-25|language=en}}</ref>在很多年前就有目击，但由于沼泽的阻碍，因此很少有目击者。有研究者提出阿拉斯加虎可能是游荡至阿拉斯加的[[东北虎|西伯利亚虎]]，但阿拉斯加可能缺乏西伯利亚虎的猎物。<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://visitcryptoville.com/2014/10/10/alaskan-tiger-another-great-beast-of-the-north/|title=Alaskan Tiger – Another Great Beast of the North?|date=2014-10-10|newspaper=CryptoVille|accessdate=2018-08-25|language=en-US}}</ref>并且有研究表明老虎会主动攻击狼，<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.ecology.com/2015/05/29/putins-tigers-thriving-wild/|title='Putin's Tigers' are Thriving in the Wild {{!}} Ecology Global Network|date=2015-05-29|newspaper=Ecology Global Network|accessdate=2018-08-25|language=en-US}}</ref>使其局部灭绝或维持数量较低水平。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ndb0QOvq2LYC&lpg=PR10&ots=vfG8Oj8k9_&pg=PA179#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Large Carnivores and the Conservation of Biodiversity|accessdate=|author=Justina Ray， Kent H. Redford， Robert Steneck， Joel Berger|date=|publisher=books.google.com}}</ref>但阿拉斯加却生存有[[北極狼|北极狼]]和{{link-en|加拿大森林狼|Northwestern wolf}}的狼群，除此外还分布有[[北極熊|北极熊]]、[[棕熊|棕熊]]与[[美洲黑熊|黑熊]]，这些动物亦会与老虎竞争。<ref name=":0" />电视节目《阿拉斯加怪兽》第一季第4集以阿拉斯加虎为主题展开。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://cryptomundo.com/bigfoot-report/alaska-monsters-the-alaskan-tiger/|title=Alaska Monsters: The Alaskan Tiger|last=Cryptids|first=Cryptomundo,|date=2014-10-10|newspaper=Cryptomundo|accessdate=2018-08-25|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dailymotion.com/video/x39eic6|title=Alaska Monsters Season 1 Episode 4 {{!}} The Alaskan Tiger Part 1 - Dailymotion Video|accessdate=2018-08-25|date=2015-10-12|work=Dailymotion|language=zh}}</ref>

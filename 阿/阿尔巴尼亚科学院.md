'''阿尔巴尼亚科学院'''（{{lang-sq|Akademia e Shkencave e Shqipërisë}}）是[[阿尔巴尼亚|阿尔巴尼亚]]最重要的学术研究机构，创立于1972年<ref>The World of Learning 2004 By Michael Salzman, Europa Publications {{ISBN|1-85743-182-0}} Page 42 ([https://books.google.com/books?id=jVURdQhlYp8C&pg=PA42&dq=academy+of+sciences+of+albania&cd=1#v=onepage&q=academy%20of%20sciences%20of%20albania&f=false])</ref>。{{as of|2010}}，学院目前有28名院士，11名伙伴院士，26名名誉院士<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.akad.edu.al/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=53&Itemid=88 |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2012-02-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120302045032/http://www.akad.edu.al/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=53&Itemid=88 |archivedate=2012-03-02 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.akad.edu.al/index.php?option%3Dcom_content%26view%3Darticle%26id%3D52%26Itemid%3D89 |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2010-01-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531152613/http://www.akad.edu.al/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=52&Itemid=89 |archivedate=2011-05-31 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.akad.edu.al/index.php?option%3Dcom_content%26view%3Darticle%26id%3D52%26Itemid%3D89 |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2010-01-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531152613/http://www.akad.edu.al/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=52&Itemid=89 |archivedate=2011-05-31 |df= }}</ref>。学院包括两个分部，一是社会科学与阿尔巴尼亚学部，二是自然与技术科学部。此外，阿尔巴尼亚科学院还开设有技术与创新发展项目、外交及公共关系分部、图书馆和出版社<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.akad.edu.al/index.php?option%3Dcom_content%26view%3Darticle%26id%3D68%26Itemid%3D63 |archivedate=2010-02-16 |df= }}</ref>。阿尔巴尼亚科学院图书馆是阿尔巴尼亚最大的学术图书馆，于1975年创立，截至1986年有8,120,000本藏书，而在创立之初仅有10,000本<ref>World Encyclopedia of Library and Information Services By Robert Wedgeworth Third Edition {{ISBN|0-8389-0609-5}} Page 41 ([https://books.google.com/books?id=HSFu99FCJwQC&pg=PA41&dq=academy+of+sciences+of+albania&cd=7#v=onepage&q=academy%20of%20sciences%20of%20albania&f=false])</ref>。

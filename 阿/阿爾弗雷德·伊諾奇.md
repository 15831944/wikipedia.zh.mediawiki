'''阿爾弗雷德·路易斯·伊諾奇'''（英語：Alfred Lewis Enoch，{{bd|1988年|12月2日}}），是一位英國演員，因飾演《[[哈利波特系列電影|哈利波特]]》系列電影中的丁·湯馬斯及電視影集《[[謀殺入門課|謀殺入門課]]》裡的衛斯·吉賓斯而受到大家矚目。

==生涯與職業==
伊諾奇出生於[[倫敦|倫敦]][[西敏市|西敏市]]，為英國演員威廉·羅素與第二任妻子巴西籍醫生Balbina Gutierrez之子<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.eurweb.com/2014/10/from-potter-to-murder-brit-alfred-enoch-on-his-hollywood-ride/ |title=存档副本 |access-date=2018-02-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906040815/http://www.eurweb.com/2014/10/from-potter-to-murder-brit-alfred-enoch-on-his-hollywood-ride/# |archive-date=2017-09-06 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。高中時就讀於[[倫敦|倫敦]]的[[西敏公學|西敏公學]]<ref>{{cite news|newspaper=[[The_Globe_and_Mail|The Globe and Mail]]|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/arts/film/how-matthew-lewis-grew-up-at-hogwarts-with-harry-and-the-gang/article4259536/|title=Matthew Lewis grew up at Hogwarts with Harry and the gang|author=Gayle Macdonald|date=14 July 2011|accessdate=25 October 2014}}</ref>。2001年，他在電影《[[哈利波特：神秘的魔法石|哈利波特：神秘的魔法石]]》中飾演[[哈利·波特_(角色)|哈利]]的同學兼室友丁·湯馬斯，並在該系列八部電影中參與了七部的演出。另外他也為哈利波特的遊戲作配音。

伊諾奇的葡萄牙語十分流利，他畢業於[[牛津大学王后学院|牛津大學王后學院]]，取得葡萄牙語和西班牙語學位<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.scarpotter.com/noticias/assista-entrevista-de-alfred-enoch-ao-humorista-blue-em-portugu-s.html|title=Alfred Enoch|work=scarpotter.com|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906035558/http://www.scarpotter.com/noticias/assista-entrevista-de-alfred-enoch-ao-humorista-blue-em-portugu-s.html|archivedate=6 September 2017|df=dmy-all}}</ref>。

在[[哈利波特系列電影|哈利波特系列電影]]拍攝結束後，伊諾奇參與許多倫敦的戲劇演出，包括《[[科利奧蘭納斯|科利奧蘭納斯]]》<ref name=Donmare1>{{cite web |url=http://www.donmarwarehouse.com/whats-on/donmar-warehouse/2013/coriolanus |publisher=Donmar Warehouse |title=Coriolanus – Donmar Warehouse |accessdate=13 November 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141112204547/http://www.donmarwarehouse.com/whats-on/donmar-warehouse/2013/coriolanus |archive-date=2014-11-12 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>，《[[雅典的泰门|雅典的泰門]]》<ref name= "London Play">{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/stage/2012/jul/20/timon-of-athens-paul-mason|title=Timon of Athens: The Power of Money|work=The Guardian|last=Mason|first=Paul|date=20 July 2012|accessdate=13 November 2014}}</ref>， 《[[安蒂岡妮|安蒂岡妮]]》，音樂劇《Happy New Year》，以及《The Ballard of Salomon Pavey》<ref name="abc">{{cite web|url=http://www.abcmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=3166630&type=enoch|title=Alfred Enoch|publisher=[[Disney–ABC_Television_Group|Disney–ABC Television Group]] ([[The_Walt_Disney_Company#Disney_Media_Networks|Disney Media Networks]])|work=[[American_Broadcasting_Company|ABC]]|date=24 July 2014|accessdate=24 July 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140728233747/http://www.abcmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=3166630&type=enoch|archivedate=28 July 2014|df=dmy-all}}</ref>。另外他也出現在《[[新世紀福爾摩斯|新世紀福爾摩斯]]》第三季第二集“[[三人征兆|三的徵兆]]”中，飾演侍衛班布里奇。

2014年，伊諾奇在電視影集《[[謀殺入門課|謀殺入門課]]》裡飾演法學院的學生衛斯·吉賓斯<ref name="Murder">{{cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2014/05/abc-picks-up-shonda-rhimes-how-to-get-away-with-murder-anthony-anderson-american-crime/|title=ABC New Series Pickups: 'Selfie', 'Forever', Galavant', 'Whispers', 'How To Get Away With Murder', 'American Crime', 'Black-ish', Jeff Lowell Comedy |publisher=Deadline.com |last=Andreeva|first= Nellie|date=8 May 2014 |accessdate=12 May 2014}}</ref><ref name="Murder1">{{cite web|url=http://www.cosmopolitan.com/entertainment/tv/q-and-a/a31716/how-to-get-away-with-murder-alfred-enoch-interview/|title="How to Get Away With Murder" Star Alfred Enoch on Abandoning His British Accent, Emma Watson, and "Legally Blonde" "That should have been my research."|publisher=[[Hearst_Corporation|Hearst Corporation]]|work=[[Cosmopolitan_(magazine)|Cosmopolitan]]|last=Greco|first=Patty|date=2 October 2014|accessdate=13 November 2014}}</ref>。

2016年，在Talawa劇院公司與曼徹斯特皇家交易所聯合製作的戲劇《[[李爾王|李爾王]]》中飾演愛德伽/湯姆·白德蘭，由於他精湛的演技而獲得了許多好評<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.talawa.com/articles/king-lear-reviewed/|title=King Lear Reviewed|work=talawa.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/theatre/what-to-see/don-warringtons-king-lear-is-a-heartbreaking-tour-de-force/|title=Don Warrington's King Lear is a heartbreaking tour de force|work=The Daily Telegraph}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestage.co.uk/reviews/2016/king-lear-review-at-the-royal-exchange-theatre-manchester/|title=King Lear review at Royal Exchange Theatre, Manchester|work=thestage.co.uk}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.britishtheatreguide.info/reviews/king-lear-royal-exchange-12733|title=Theatre review: King Lear at Royal Exchange Theatre|work=britishtheatreguide.info}}</ref>。

2018年，他回到倫敦西區，在[[温德姆剧院|溫德姆劇院]]與[[阿尔弗雷德·莫里纳|艾佛烈·蒙利納]]一起將曾獲得[[百老匯|百老匯]][[東尼獎|東尼獎]]「最佳戲劇」的《紅》搬上英國的舞台<ref>{{cite web|title=Red - About the Show|url=https://www.delfontmackintosh.co.uk/tickets/red/index.php|website=Delfont Mackintosh Theatres|accessdate=29 November 2017}}</ref>。

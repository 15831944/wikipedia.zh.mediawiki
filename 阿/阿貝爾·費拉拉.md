'''阿貝爾·費拉拉'''（{{lang-en|Abel Ferrara}}；{{bd|1951年|7月19日}}）是美國電影工作者<ref>Nicole Brenez, [https://books.google.com/books?id=k8iLpYdw6mMC&pg=PA2&lpg=PA2&dq=nicholas+st+john+abel+ferrara+high+school&source=bl&ots=xLXrJxAX6Y&sig=JKpzm8OSYq06AvZKWPcyo2TkUeU&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjs1K3BpdPMAhUo6IMKHUNKA_gQ6AEINjAE#v=onepage&q=nicholas%20st%20john%20abel%20ferrara%20high%20school&f=false ''Abel Ferrara''], University of Illinois Press, 2007 page 2</ref>，他執導的電影以激進與爭議題材聞名。

== 生平 ==
阿貝爾·費拉拉出生在[[布朗克斯|布朗克斯]]一個信奉[[天主教|天主教]]的[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]]與[[義大利|義大利]]後裔家庭<ref name="ref1">{{cite news|last=Goldstein|first=Patrick|title=MOVIES The Prince of Darkness Director Abel Ferrara practices a kind of gonzo filmmaking, and his violent vision isn't a particularly popular one in Hollywood|work=Los Angeles Times|date=1990-10-28|url=https://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/59945608.html?dids=59945608:59945608&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&type=current&date=Oct+28%2C+1990&author=PATRICK+GOLDSTEIN&pub=Los+Angeles+Times+(pre-1997+Fulltext)&desc=MOVIES+The+Prince+of+Darkness+Director+Abel+Ferrara+practices+a+kind+of+gonzo+filmmaking%2C+and+his+violent+vision+isn't+a+particularly+popular+one+in+Hollywood&pqatl=google|accessdate=2009-11-21}}</ref>，天主教背景影響了許多他電影題材；2007年，他正式皈依[[佛教|佛教]]<ref>{{cite web|last=Shoard|first=Catherine|title=Abel Ferrara at Cannes: 'You gotta be careful what you say … but I'm not'|date=23 May 2014|publisher=''[[衛報|衛報]]''|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2014/may/23/abel-ferrara-interview|accessdate=25 July 2016}}</ref>。

他曾以《{{link-en|死亡遊戲 (電影)|Dangerous Game (1993 film)|死亡遊戲}}》(1993)、《{{link-en|江湖白事|The Funeral (1996 film)}}》(1996)、《{{link-en|新玫瑰旅館|New Rose Hotel (film)}}》(1998)、《{{link-en|瑪利亞再生|Mary (2005 film)}}》(2005)、《{{link-en|四點四十四地球上的最後一日|4:44 Last Day on Earth}}》(2011)與《{{link-en|帕索里尼 (電影)|Pasolini (film)|帕索里尼}}》(2014)六度進入[[威尼斯影展|威尼斯影展]]正式競賽單元，並以《{{link-en|瑪利亞再生|Mary (2005 film)}}》於[[第62届威尼斯国际电影节|第62屆威尼斯影展]]獲得[[威尼斯影展評審團特別獎|評審團特別獎]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.americancinemapapers.com/files/VENICE_2005.htm|title=VENICE 2005 – THE 62nd MOSTRA DEL CINEMA|accessdate=October 6, 2013}}</ref>；另外，1993年他執導的《{{link-en|異形基地|Body Snatchers (1993 film)}}》獲選為[[第46屆坎城影展|第46屆坎城影展]]正式競賽片<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1993/allSelections.html |title=Official Selection 1993: All the Selection |work=festival-cannes.fr |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131214195528/http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1993/allSelections.html |archivedate=14 December 2013 }}</ref>，1995年他也以《{{link-en|夜癮|The Addiction}}》進入[[柏林影展|第49屆柏林影展]]正式競賽單元<ref name="Programme">{{cite web|url=https://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/1995/02_programm_1995/02_Programm_1995.html |title=PROGRAMME 1995|accessdate=9 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131109180633/https://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/1995/02_programm_1995/02_Programm_1995.html| archivedate=9 November 2013 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>。

費拉拉在[[九一一襲擊事件|九一一事件]]後移居[[義大利|義大利]][[羅馬|羅馬]]<ref>{{cite web|last=Righelato|first=Rowan|title=Abel Ferrara: 'Pasolini's death is not some kind of fictional event'|date=11 September 2015|publisher=''[[衛報|衛報]]''|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2015/sep/11/abel-ferrara-pier-paolo-pasolini-death-interview|accessdate=25 July 2016}}</ref>，因為他認為在歐洲為他電影計畫找資金較為容易。

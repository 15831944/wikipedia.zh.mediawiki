'''阿富汗护照'''，是[[阿富汗政府|阿富汗政府]]发放给[[阿富汗|阿富汗]]公民的[[护照|护照]]（一种国际旅行文件），于1880年首次签发。

2011年，阿富汗政府发放外交和公务[[可機讀護照|可機讀護照]]<ref name="Foreign ministry issues computerised passports">{{cite news |url=http://www.pajhwok.com/en/2011/09/17/foreign-ministry-issues-computerised-passports |title=Foreign ministry issues computerised passports |editor=Meer Agha Nasrat Samimi |publisher=[[Pajhwok_Afghan_News|Pajhwok Afghan News]] (PAN) |date=September 17, 2011 |accessdate=2012-12-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url = http://www.pajhwok.com/en/2011/09/18/foreign-ministry-issues-computerised-passports-video |title=Foreign ministry issues computerised passports (Video) |publisher=Pajhwok Afghan News |editor=Ataullah Khpelwak |date=September 18, 2011 |accessdate=2011-10-18}}</ref>，而阿富汗于2013年向该国所有公民发放[[可機讀護照|可機讀護照]]<ref>{{cite news |url = http://www.pajhwok.com/en/2013/03/24/machine-readable-passport-system-put-place |title=Machine-readable passport system put in place |publisher=Pajhwok Afghan News |editor=Ahmad Quraishi |date=March 24, 2013 |accessdate=2013-03-31}}</ref>，申请费为5,000[[阿富汗尼|阿富汗尼]]（折合100美元）<ref>{{cite news |url = http://www.pajhwok.com/en/2014/03/05/countrywide-electronic-passports-soon |title=Countrywide electronic passports soon |publisher=Pajhwok Afghan News |editor=Azizullah Hamdard |date=March 5, 2013 |accessdate=2014-03-05}}</ref>。

'''阿瑟·亚当斯'''（{{lang-en|Arthur Adams}}，{{bd|1820年||1878年|}}）是[[英格兰|英格兰]][[医生|医生]]和[[自然历史|博物学家]]。

==经历==
亚当斯是1843年至1846年{{tsl|en|HMS Samarang (1822)|萨马朗号 (1822)|萨马朗号}}调查亚洲东部群岛的随行[[外科醫師|外科醫師]]。<ref>{{Cite book |title=The History of the Collections Contained in the Natural History Departments of the British Museum |publisher=Adamant Media |year=2000 |isbn=978-1-4021-8140-5 |chapter=Adams (Arthur) [1820–1878] |pages=23 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ZWtjNPH5w58C&pg=PA23}}</ref>作为[[軟體動物學|軟體動物學]]家，他描述了数百种新物种，但是其中很少有充分详尽的描述。<ref>{{cite book |author=S. Peter Dance |year=1966 |title=Shell Collecting. An Illustrated History |isbn=978-0-571-06762-6 |publisher=Faber & Faber}} Cited in {{cite journal|author=E. Alison Kay |year=1968 |title=Spurious Species Part III. Shells Erroneously Described From The Hawaiian Islands |journal=Hawaiian Shell News |volume=16 |issue=3 |series=New Series No. 99 |pages=1, 5 }}</ref>他和哥哥[[亨利·亚当斯|亨利·亚当斯]]合著了《根据组织对软体动物的最新分类》一书，并于1858年出版。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://archive.org/search.php?query=creator%3A%22Adams%2C%20Arthur%2C%201829-1878%22 |title=''The genera of recent mollusca: arranged according to their organization''|year=1858|author1=A. Adams|author2=H. Adams}}</ref>他还写了《[[日本|日本]]和[[满洲里|满洲里]]游记》<ref>{{cite book|title=''Travels of a naturalist in Japan and Manchuria''|date=1870|publisher=Hurst and Blackett|author= A. Adams|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2a8aAAAAYAAJ|language=en}}</ref>，以及旅行中见到的一些奇异[[蜘蛛|蜘蛛]]的文章。<ref>{{cite journal|author=A. Adams|journal=Annals and Magazine of Natural History |year=1847 |volume=20 |pages=289–297 |title=Notes on the habits of certain exotic spiders |url=https://archive.org/stream/annalsmagazineof20lond#page/289/mode/1up |doi=10.1080/037454809496053}}</ref>

为了纪念他对动物学的贡献，很多物种以亚当斯命名，其中包括[[斑蟹|斑蟹]]（''{{lang|la|Zebrida adamsi}}''）。<ref>[http://test.b-neat.org/bemon/?page=petymol.a.html Biographical Etymology of Marine Organism Names: Henry Adams] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111005202151/http://test.b-neat.org/bemon/?page=petymol.a.html |date=2011-10-05 }}</ref>

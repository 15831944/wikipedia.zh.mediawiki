'''阿斯佩蒂亚'''（{{lang-eu|Azpeitia}}，意思是「在岩石之下」），是[[西班牙|西班牙]][[巴斯克自治区|巴斯克自治区]][[基普斯夸省|基普斯夸省]]的一个[[市镇_(西班牙)|市镇]]。总面积69平方公里，总人口13708人（2001年），人口密度199人/平方公里。

同樣位於[[烏羅拉河|烏羅拉河]]之上，阿斯佩蒂亞距離[[阿斯科伊蒂亚|阿斯科伊蒂亚]]以東數公里，位處省會[[圣塞瓦斯蒂安|圣塞瓦斯蒂安]]的西南方26公里。根據2001年的統計，有13,708人口。

阿斯佩蒂亞是[[耶穌會|耶穌會]]

Azpeitia is the birthplace of [[Ignatius_of_Loyola|Ignatius of Loyola]]. His birth home is now preserved as a part of large [[Jesuit|Jesuit]] compound, the [[Sanctuary_of_Loyola|Sanctuary of Loyola]], a major attraction of tourist and pilgrims alike. Azpeitia lies at the foot of the massive [[Izarraitz|Izarraitz]] towering over the town and much visited by town-dwellers. [[Azpeitia_Railway_Museum|Azpeitia Railway Museum]] is located in the town.

'''阿迪语'''（Adi）又称'''阿博语'''（Abhor, Abor-Miri）或'''[[珞巴语|珞巴语]]'''（Lho-Pa, Luoba），是[[汉藏语系|汉藏语系]][[达尼语支|达尼语支]]的语言，主要通行于[[印度|印度]]的[[阿鲁纳恰尔邦|阿鲁纳恰尔邦]]（中国称[[藏南地区|藏南地区]]），由[[阿迪人|阿迪人]]使用。

阿迪语目前在阿迪族学校有相应教学，目前是阿迪人社群的第三语言<ref>[http://www.northeasttoday.in/arunachal-to-preserve-dying-local-dialects/   Arunachal to Preserve ‘Dying’ Local Dialects - North East Today]</ref>。阿迪语的母语人口在阿鲁纳恰尔邦占到了全邦人口的17.57%（2001年）<ref name="Census of India">{{cite web |url=http://censusindia.gov.in/Census_Data_2001/Census_Data_Online/Language/parta.htm |title=Distribution of the 22 Scheduled Languages |work=Census of India |publisher=Registrar General & Census Commissioner, India |year=2001 |accessdate=4 January 2014}}</ref><ref name="censusindia.gov.in">{{cite web |url=http://censusindia.gov.in/Census_Data_2001/Census_data_finder/A_Series/Total_population.htm |title=Census Reference Tables, A-Series – Total Population |work=Census of India |publisher=Registrar General & Census Commissioner, India |year=2001 |accessdate=4 January 2014}}</ref><ref>[http://www.censusindia.gov.in/Census_Data_2001/Census_Data_Online/Language/partb.htm] Census 2011 Non scheduled languages</ref>。

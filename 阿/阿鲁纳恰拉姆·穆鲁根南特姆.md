'''阿鲁纳恰拉姆·穆鲁根南特姆'''（{{lang-ta|அருணாச்சலம்_முருகானந்தம்}}，{{bd|1962年}}<ref name=tehelka>{{cite news|title=The Pad That Does Not Whisper |author=PC Vinoj Kumar, |publisher=Tehelka |url=http://archive.tehelka.com/story_main42.asp?filename=cr290809the_pad.asp |date=29 August 2009 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140426201334/http://archive.tehelka.com/story_main42.asp?filename=cr290809the_pad.asp |archivedate=26 April 2014 }}</ref>），绰号'''护垫侠'''（{{lang|en|Pad Man}}），[[印度|印度]][[泰米尔纳德邦|泰米尔纳德邦]][[哥印拜陀|哥印拜陀]][[公益创业|社会企业家]]。他是廉价[[卫生巾|卫生巾]]制造机器的发明者，被公认为对印度农业地区女性改变以往处理[[月经|月经]]的不卫生方法起到关键作用。他的小机器制造的卫生巾价钱是市面上卫生巾三分之一不到，已经在印度29个邦中的23个邦投入使用。目前，他计划将这种机器的生产拓展到106个国家<ref name="bbc">{{cite web |url=http://m.bbc.com/news/magazine-26260978 |title=The Indian sanitary pad revolutionary |publisher=BBC News |accessdate=4 March 2014}}</ref>。

2014年，阿鲁纳恰拉姆入选《[[時代雜誌|時代雜誌]]》[[时代百大人物|全球100位最有影响力的人物]]榜单<ref>{{cite web |title=The 100 Most Influential People – Pioneers: Arunachalam Muruganantham |publisher=TIME.com |url=http://time.com/70861/arunachalam-muruganantham-2014-time-100/|date=23 April 2014 |access-date=26 April 2014}}</ref>。2016年，[[印度政府|印度政府]]授予他[[莲花士勋章|莲花士勋章]]<ref name="Padma Awards">{{cite web | url=http://mha.nic.in/sites/upload_files/mha/files/PadmaAwards_25012016.pdf | title=Padma Awards | publisher=Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India | date=2016 | accessdate=January 3, 2016 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170803085913/http://mha.nic.in/sites/upload_files/mha/files/PadmaAwards_25012016.pdf | archivedate=2017年8月3日 | df= }}</ref>。

==早年==
1962年，穆鲁根南特姆出生，父亲S·穆鲁根南特姆和母亲A·万尼达是[[印度|印度]][[哥印拜陀|哥印拜陀]]的手织机纺织工。父亲在一场车祸中去世后，穆鲁根南特姆在贫穷中长大<ref name=nif />。母亲转行当农民工，赚钱供他上学。然而，他14岁从学校辍学<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://dropoutdudes.com/arunachalam-muruganantham-pad-man-sanitary-pads-india/|title=How Arunachalam Muruganantham – Pad Man Brought Revolutionary Change in Sanitary Pads Market of India|date=2017-12-23|work=Dropout Dudes|access-date=2017-12-27}}</ref>。他为工厂工人煮饭，当过机床操作员、山药销售代理、农民工和焊接工<ref name=nif />。

==发明==
1998年，他和妻子珊蒂（Shanthi）结婚。不久后，他发现妻子舍不得买[[跨国公司|跨国公司]]制造的昂贵卫生巾，[[月经|月经]]时用的是破布和报纸<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/world/indias-improbable-champion-for-affordable-feminine-hygiene/article4587288/ |title=India's champion for affordable feminine hygiene|accessdate =30 June 2015}}</ref><ref name="fastco">{{cite web |url=http://www.fastcoexist.com/1679008/an-indian-inventor-disrupts-the-period-industry |title=An Indian Inventor Disrupts The Period Industry |publisher=Fast Company |accessdate=18 August 2012 |author=Sandhana, Lakshmi}}</ref>。深受其困扰的他开始尝试设计卫生巾。最初，他用棉花制造，但妻子和妹妹都不大敢用<ref name="hindu">{{cite news |title=A man in a woman's world |author=Akila Kannadasan |publisher=The Hindu |url=http://www.thehindu.com/features/metroplus/society/a-man-in-a-womans-world/article2875390.ece |date=13 February 2012}}</ref>。最终，她们不再配合他，拒绝成为新发明的实验对象。他意识到，卫生巾原材料成本仅为10派士，但成本售价高出40倍<ref name="bbc" />。他寻找女性志愿者测试他的发明，但大多数人羞于他们的生理问题讲出口。他开始尝试自己测试，在身上系了一个装有牲畜血的膀胱，实验失败，他成了村民嘲笑的对象<ref name="mea">{{cite news |title=One man's mission to improve women's lives |author=Letitia Rowlands |publisher=Ministry of External Affairs, Govt of India |url=http://www.mea.gov.in/bilateral-documents.htm?dtl/23081/One+mans+mission+to+improve+to+womens+lives |date=14 March 2014}}</ref>。由于印度人对月经一直很[[禁忌|忌讳]]，这使得他被村民和家人嘲笑<ref name="independent">{{cite web |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/asia/the-tampon-king-who-sparked-a-period-of-change-for-indias-women-7897093.html |title=The 'Tampon King' who sparked a period of change for India's women |publisher=The Independent |date=29 June 2012 |accessdate=18 August 2012 |author=Buncombe, Andrew}}</ref>。他把自己的产品免费派发给当地医学院的女孩们，希望得到他们的反馈。

花了两年时间，他终于发现商用卫生巾用的是从[[松树|松树]]皮[[木浆|木浆]]中提取的[[纤维素|纤维素]]纤维<ref name=tehelka />。纤维帮助卫生巾在吸水的同时维持形状<ref name=nif />。进口生产卫生巾机器的价格是3500万卢布<ref name="marysue">{{cite web |url=http://www.themarysue.com/tampon-king/ |title=Tampon King |publisher=The Mary Sue |date=29 June 2012 |accessdate=18 August 2012 |author=Foxx-Gonzalez, Kellie}}</ref>，而他设计了一款成本低廉的机器，不需要复杂的培训便可操作，相比之下成本仅为6.5万卢布<ref name="hindu" />。他向[[孟买|孟买]]一家供货商采购加工过的松木浆，木浆经机器研磨、脱脂、压缩和[[紫外线|紫外线]]辐照消毒<ref>{{cite web|url=http://newinventions.in/linkview.aspx |title=New Inventions: Jayaashree Industries |accessdate=18 August 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121121014259/http://newinventions.in/linkview.aspx |archivedate=21 November 2012 }}</ref>，成品的卫生巾便可打包出售。

2006年，他到{{le|印度理工学院马德拉斯分校|IIT Madras}}展示他的创意，听取意见。业内人士将他的发明提交给{{le|印度国家创新基金|National Innovation Foundation - India}}，结果获奖<ref name="nif">{{cite news|url=http://nif.org.in/innovation/mini_sanitary/10|title=MINI SANITARY NAPKIN MAKING MACHINE A. Muruganantham|author=National Innovation Foundation|accessdate=9 Jun 2016}}</ref><ref name="hindu" />。因此，他拿着种子投资创办了Jayaashree Industries，将机器推销给全印度的农村妇女<ref name="economic">{{cite web |url=http://articles.economictimes.indiatimes.com/2012-01-18/news/30639078_1_sanitary-napkins-rags-menstruating-women |title=Blood, sweat & a few tears: Arunachalam Muruganantham's lessons for consumer product firms |publisher=The Economic Times |date=18 January 2012 |accessdate=18 August 2012 |author=Kumar, Vikas}}</ref>。机器被-{zh-hans:赞; zh-hant:讚}-操作简单、成本低廉，他帮助社会的承诺也为他赚得多个大奖<ref name="independent" />。尽管多家公司实体希望将他的产业[[贸易|商业化]]，但被他拒绝，他继续将这些机器提供给由妇女牵头的{{le|经济自助团体|Self-help group (finance)}}<ref name="independent" /><ref name="guardian">{{cite web |url=https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2012/jan/22/sanitary-towels-india-cheap-manufacture |title=India's women given low-cost route to sanitary protection |publisher=The Guardian |date=21 January 2012 |accessdate=18 August 2012 |author=Sandhana, Lakshmi}}</ref><ref name="guardian" />。

穆鲁根南特姆的发明被公认为改变印度妇女生活状况的关键一步<ref name="think">{{cite web |url=http://www.thinkchangeindia.org/2012/01/31/enabling-access-through-low-cost-sanitary-pads-jayashree-industries/ |title=Enabling Access through Low-cost Sanitary Pads: Jayashree Industries |publisher=Think Change India |date=31 January 2012 |accessdate=18 August 2012 |author=Ramdoss, Santhosh |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120702065017/http://www.thinkchangeindia.org/2012/01/31/enabling-access-through-low-cost-sanitary-pads-jayashree-industries/ |archive-date=2012-07-02 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref name=economist>{{cite news |title=Social entrepreneurship in India : Cut from a different cloth |publisher=The Economist |url=https://www.economist.com/news/business/21586328-building-business-around-solving-chronic-female-health-care-problem-cut-different |date=14 September 2013}}</ref>。机器为许多妇女带来了工作和收入，经济实惠的卫生巾也让更多女性在月经期间正常工作<ref name="jezebel">{{cite web |url=http://jezebel.com/5922361/meet-indias-tampon-king |title=Meet India’s ‘Tampon King’ |publisher=Jezebel |date=29 June 2012 |accessdate=18 August 2012 |author=Baker, Katie J.M.}}</ref>。除了在宣传上亲力亲为，穆鲁根南特姆的工作也激励其他企业家进入该领域<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.changemakers.com/healthbiz/entries/promoting-menstrual-hygiene-rural-areas-use-ivrs-techno |title=Promoting hygiene |accessdate=14 September 2013}}</ref>，其中有企业家提出使用废弃香蕉纤维或竹子<ref name=economist />。

==流行文化==

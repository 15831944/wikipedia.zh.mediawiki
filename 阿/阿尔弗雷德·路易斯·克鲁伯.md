'''阿尔弗雷德·路易斯·克鲁伯'''（'''Alfred Louis Kroeber'''，{{bd|1876年|6月11日|1960年|10月5日|catIdx=K}}），美国[[人类学家|人类学家]]，20世纪前半期美国人类学的领军人物之一。小说家娥苏拉·勒瑰恩的父亲。

==部分著作==
* ''Indian Myths of South Central California'' (1907), in ''University of California Publications in American Archaeology and Ethnology'' 4:167-250. Berkeley (Six ''Rumsien Costanoan'' myths, pp. 199-202); online at [http://www.sacred-texts.com/nam/ca/scc Sacred Texts].
* ''The Religion of the Indians of California'' (1907), in ''University of California Publications in American Archaeology and Ethnology'' 4:6. Berkeley, sections titled "Shamanism", "Public Ceremonies", "Ceremonial Structures and Paraphernalia", and "Mythology and Beliefs"; available at [http://www.sacred-texts.com/nam/ca/ric Sacred Texts]
* ''Handbook of the Indians of California'' (1925). Washington, D.C: ''Bureau of American Ethnology Bulletin'' No. 78
* ''Anthropology: Culture Patterns & Processes'' (1963). Harcourt: Brace & World, Inc.

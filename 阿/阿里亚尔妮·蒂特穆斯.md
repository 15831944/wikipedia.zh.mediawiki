'''阿里亚尔妮·蒂特穆斯'''（{{lang-en|Ariarne Titmus}}，{{bd|2000年|9月7日||}}）生于塔斯马尼亚州朗塞斯顿，是一名澳大利亚女子游泳运动员，主攻中长距离[[自由泳|自由泳]]。蒂特穆斯的母亲是一名前短跑选手，父亲是一名[[板球|板球]]选手<ref name="GoldCoast"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.couriermail.com.au/sport/commonwealth-games/swimmer-laps-up-life-in-her-new-state-but-move-wasnt-easy/news-story/79f705e8061713b10bb624ef1f099af0|title=Swimmer laps up life in her new state, but move wasn't easy|publisher=The Courier Mail|date=2018-03-31|accessdate=2018-12-12}}</ref>。她从小便热爱游泳，她童年时的居所附近正好有一个泳池，为她练习游泳提供方便<ref name="GoldCoast"/>。2015年，她的父母为了她能够更好地实现她的梦想，一家人从家乡迁至昆士兰州布里斯班居住<ref name="examiner">{{cite web|last1=Shaw|first1=Rob|title=Titmus on move|url=http://www.examiner.com.au/story/3090536/titmus-on-move/|publisher=The Examiner|date=2015-05-19|accessdate=2017-11-09}}</ref>，她也在当地的高中就读<ref name="heraldsun">{{cite web|url=https://www.heraldsun.com.au/kids-news/ariarne-titmus-is-swimmings-latest-sensation-and-shes-ready-to-break-records-at-the-comm-games/news-story/168564301f23a8bd28bb58c15eb3d4ee|title=Kids News: Ariarne Titmus is swimming's latest sensation and she's ready to break records at the Commonwealth Games|publisher=Herald Sun|date=2018-03-08|accessdate=2018-12-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.goldcoastbulletin.com.au/sport/commonwealth-games/swimming/comm-games-swimmer-ariarne-titmus-makes-school-swim-carnival-a-onesided-affair/news-story/de39320544a2ee72155943e348998343|title=Comm Games swimmer Ariarne Titmus makes school swim carnival a one-sided affair|publisher=Gold Coast Bulletin|date=2018-03-08|accessdate=2018-12-12}}</ref>。

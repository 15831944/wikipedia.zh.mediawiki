'''阿拉法日'''（{{lang-ar|يوم عرفة}}） 是[[伊斯兰历|伊斯兰历]][[都爾黑哲月|苏尔海哲月]]（伊斯兰历法中的12月）九日的伊斯兰节日。这是[[朝觐_(伊斯兰教)|朝觐]]最重要的事件之一。阿拉法日是朝觐的第二日，阿拉法日过后一天是伊斯兰教节日[[古尔邦节|古尔邦节]]的第一天<ref name="Sheikho">{{cite book|last1=Sheikho|first1=Mohammad Amin|title=Pilgrimage Hajj: The Fifth High Grade of Al-Taqwa: Volume 5|date=1783|publisher=CreateSpace Independent Publishing Platform|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2PcFIf3nxL8C&pg=PA57&dq=Day+of+Arafah&hl=fa&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiy0bie78nVAhWGYVAKHauIBpcQ6AEIMzAC#v=onepage&q=Day%20of%20Arafah&f=false}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=DAVID BENTLEY|title=Eid al-Adha 2016 – What is the Day of Arafah before the Eid celebrations and why is it so important?|url=http://www.birminghammail.co.uk/whats-on/whats-on-news/eid-al-adha-2016-what-11862345|accessdate=11 September 2016|publisher=birminghammail.co.uk}}</ref>。在这一天的黎明，朝觐的穆斯林需要从米那到附近的[[阿拉法平原|阿拉法平原]]和[[阿拉法山|阿拉法山]]进行祈祷、[[礼拜|礼拜]]。在阿拉法平原和阿拉法山一带，伊斯兰教先知[[穆罕默德|穆罕默德]]晚年曾做过他最后、也是最著名的讲道之一<ref name=Long19>{{cite book |last=Long |first=David E.|title=The Hajj Today: A Survey of the Contemporary Pilgrimage to Makkah |url= https://books.google.com./books?id=2Uk3Gh6xrUUC&pg=PA19 |year=1979 |isbn= 0-87395-382-7 |page=19}}</ref>。

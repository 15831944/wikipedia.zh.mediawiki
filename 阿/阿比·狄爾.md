'''阿比·狄爾'''（{{lang-en|'''Abhay Deol'''}}，{{bd|1976年|3月15日|catIdx=Deol, Abhay}}）<ref>[https://www.deccanchronicle.com/entertainment/bollywood/150318/katrina-kaif-abhay-deol-mile-dobara-zero-birthday-celebrations.html “Katrina Kaif, Abhay Deol ‘mile dobara’ for Zero and his birthday celebrations”]. The Deccan Chronicle. 15-03-2018.</ref>是[[印度|印度]]電影男演員，主要出现在[[宝莱坞|宝莱坞]]电影中。他較成功的電影包括：《Dev.D》、《[[人生不再重来|人生不再重来]]》、《Raanjhanaa》、《Happy Bhag Jayegi》和《[[无法避免的战争|无法避免的战争]]》等。<ref>[https://www.firstpost.com/entertainment/abhay-deol-is-back-and-how-with-all-new-film-slate-actor-jokes-hes-battling-an-early-mid-life-crisis-4417991.html “Abhay Deol is back, and how: With all-new film slate, actor jokes he's battling an 'early mid-life crisis'”]. First Post. 04-04-2018.</ref>

狄爾是寶萊塢七十年代的动作巨星[[達曼德拉|達曼德拉]]的姪子，表亲[[伊莎·狄爾|伊莎·狄爾]]、辛尼和波比均是演員。<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/abhay-deol-dharmendra-5113178/ “Abhay Deol is keen to work with family but fears performing in front of Dharmendra”]. Times of India. 27-03-2018.</ref>

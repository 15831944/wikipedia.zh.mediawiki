'''阿里雷扎·萨利希内贾德'''（'''Alireza Salehi Nejad'''）是一名{{cn|伊朗-土耳其籍}}的社会科学家及多学科研究者，目前隶属于[[德黑兰大学|德黑兰大学]]世界研究学院。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbvaopenmind.com/en/user/arashtitan/|title=Alireza Salehi Nejad|accessdate=2018-05-03|work=OpenMind|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ucccdsw.ut.ac.ir/en/people/ViewProfile/8ee97164-62f1-4e84-80e1-f671a0ed4b35|title=Faculty of World Studies, ViewProfile|accessdate=2018-05-03|last=Starz|first=Theme|work=ucccdsw.ut.ac.ir|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ccsenet.org/journal/index.php/res/about/editorialTeamBio/38549|title=Review of European Studies - Editorial Team|accessdate=2018-05-03|author=|date=|work=www.ccsenet.org|publisher=|language=en-US}}</ref>他也是总部位于[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[吉隆坡|吉隆坡]]的商业资讯公司泰坦公司（Titan Inc.）的创始人及前任[[首席执行官|首席执行官]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://a-salehi.co.uk/#!About.html|title=Alireza Salehi Nejad|accessdate=2018-05-03|work=a-salehi.co.uk|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mei.edu/profile/alireza-salehi-nejad|title=Alireza Salehi Nejad {{!}} Middle East Institute|accessdate=2018-05-03|work=www.mei.edu|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.mei.edu/content/iranians-malaysia-businessman-alireza-salehi-nejad|title=Iranians in Malaysia: Businessman Alireza Salehi Nejad|newspaper=Middle East Institute|accessdate=2018-05-03|language=en}}</ref>

阿里雷扎目前担任多个机构的董事，包括医疗保健沟通国际组织，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://usern.tums.ac.ir/Group/Info/IACH|title=IACH - USERN|accessdate=2018-05-03|work=usern.tums.ac.ir}}</ref>美国语言学会伦理委员会<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.linguisticsociety.org/about/who-we-are/committees/ethics-committee|title=Ethics Committee {{!}} Linguistic Society of America|accessdate=2018-05-03|work=www.linguisticsociety.org|language=en}}</ref>，网络空间研究政策中心<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://fws.ut.ac.ir/en/?p=2381|title=FWS researcher is the recipient of peer review award|date=2018-01-28|newspaper=Faculty Of World Studies|accessdate=2018-05-03|language=en-US}}</ref>，教科文组织网络空间与文化教席<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ucccdsw.ut.ac.ir/en/people|title=UNESCO Chair on Cyberspace & Culture, Peoples|accessdate=2018-05-03|author=|date=|last=Starz|first=Theme|work=ucccdsw.ut.ac.ir|publisher=|language=en-US}}</ref>以及濒危语言基金会咨询委员会。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ogmios.org/committee/index.php|title=Foundation For Endangered Languages - Committee|accessdate=2018-05-03|work=www.ogmios.org}}</ref><ref>[https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Alireza_Salehi_Nejad Alireza Salehi Nejad | University of Tehran, Tehrān | UT | Faculty of World Studies | ResearchGate]</ref>

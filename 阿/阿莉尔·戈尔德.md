'''阿莉尔·汤森德·戈尔德'''（{{lang-en|Arielle Townsend Gold}}，{{bd|1996年|5月4日||}}）生于科罗拉多州斯廷博特斯普林斯，是一名美国女子单板滑雪运动员，主攻U型池。她的哥哥[[Taylor_Gold|Taylor Gold]]同样也是单板滑雪运动员<ref name="jewishexponent1">{{cite web|author=Amishai Gottlieb |url=http://www.jewishexponent.com/snowboarding-siblings-among-jewish-athletes-seeking-gold-in-sochi |title=Snowboarding Siblings Among Jewish Athletes Seeking Gold in Sochi |work=Jewish Exponent |date=2014-02-05 |accessdate=2014-04-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Finkelstein |first=Seth |url=http://www.jpost.com/Sports/Sizing-up-some-of-the-non-Israeli-Jews-in-Sochi-342254 |title=Sizing up some of the non-Israeli Jews in Sochi |work=The Jerusalem Post |date=2014-02-23 |accessdate=2014-04-01}}</ref>。阿莉尔三岁时开始练习滑雪<ref name="soc"/>，七岁时，她的哥哥说服她去改练单板滑雪<ref name="maxpreps1">{{cite web|author=John Coon |url=http://www.maxpreps.com/news/xDN_jyn-p0O5bXw0kZttAw/from-students-to-sochi--arielle-gold-soars-straight-from-steamboat-springs-high-school.htm |title=From Students to Sochi: Arielle Gold soars straight from Steamboat Springs High School |publisher=MaxPreps |date=2014-02-11 |accessdate=2014-04-01}}</ref>。2011年8月，她在[[新西兰|新西兰]]卡德罗纳完成了个人的世界杯分站赛首秀，在U型池项目获得第八名<ref name="autogenerated1">{{cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/olympics/winter/2014/athletes/_/athlete/50871/arielle-gold |title=Arielle Gold – 2014 Winter Olympics – Olympic Athletes – Sochi, Russia |publisher=ESPN |accessdate=2014-04-01 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140407072231/http://espn.go.com/olympics/winter/2014/athletes/_/athlete/50871/arielle-gold |archivedate=2014-04-07|deadurl=no}}</ref>。

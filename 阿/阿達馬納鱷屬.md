'''阿達馬納鱷屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Adamanasuchus''）是[[堅蜥目|堅蜥目]]的一屬，化石發現於[[美國|美國]][[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]]的數個地點，屬於[[欽爾組|欽爾組]]（Chinle Formation）<ref>Heckert, A. B., Lucas, S. G., Hunt, A. P., and Spielmann,J. A. (2007). Late Triassic aetosaur biochronology revised. ''In:'' Lucas, S.G. and Spielmann, J.A., eds., ''The Global Triassic''. New Mexico Museum of Natural History and Science Bulletin 41.</ref>，地質年代為[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]晚期的[[卡尼階|卡尼階]]<ref>Lucas, S. G., Hunt, A. P., and Spielmann, J. A. (2007). A new aetosaur from the Upper Triassic (Adamanian: Carnian) of Arizona. ''In:'' Lucas, S. G. and Spielmann, J. A., eds., ''Triassic of the American West''. New Mexico Museum of Natural History & Science, Bulletin 40 p. 241-247</ref>。屬名是以Adamanian動物群為名。

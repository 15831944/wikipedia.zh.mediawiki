[[伊斯蘭教|伊斯蘭教]]是[[阿拉伯聯合酋長國|阿拉伯聯合酋長國]]所有酋長國的官方宗教，穆斯林佔阿聯酋76％的人口，主要的教派為遜尼派。<ref name="The World Factbook">{{cite web|url=https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/fields/2122.html?countryName=Pakistan&countryCode=pk&regionCode=sas&#pk| publisher=CIA | title=The World Factbook}}</ref>其他宗教包括[[基督宗教|基督宗教]]、[[佛教|佛教]]、[[印度教|印度教]]、[[猶太教|猶太教]]和[[錫克教|錫克教]]，信徒主要是阿聯酋的外籍人士。<ref name="irf127360">{{cite web|url=https://www.state.gov/g/drl/rls/irf/2009/127360.htm |title=United Arab Emirates |publisher=State.gov |date= |access-date=2010-11-24}}</ref>

==伊斯蘭教==
{{see also|{{link-en|阿拉伯聯合酋長國伊斯蘭教|Islam in the United Arab Emirates}}|{{link-en|阿拉伯聯合酋長國伊斯蘭教什葉派| Shia Islam in the United Arab Emirates}}}}
阿聯酋遜尼派穆斯林比什葉派穆斯林多，[[伊斯瑪儀派|伊斯瑪儀派]]（Ismaili Shias）和[[阿赫邁底亞|阿赫邁底亞]]穆斯林（Ahmadi Muslims）的人數較少。<ref name="The World Factbook"/> 阿聯酋的司法制度源於[[大陸法系|大陸法系]]和[[伊斯蘭教法|伊斯蘭教法]]。法院系統由民事法庭和伊斯蘭教法庭組成。據“人權觀察組織”報導，阿聯酋的刑事和民事法院將伊斯蘭教法納入其刑法典和家庭法中，被視為是歧視婦女的法律。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/expat/expatnews/11012631/Human-Rights-Watch-warns-expat-women-about-the-UAE.html|title=Human Rights Watch warns expat women about the UAE}}</ref>阿聯酋的伊斯蘭教法擁有相當的權力。

如果犯了刑事罪行，如通姦，婚前性行為和飲酒，會受到[[鞭刑|鞭刑]]懲罰。<ref name=bergner>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NqIkQz68_fgC&pg=PA2203|title=Country Reports on Human Rights Practices for 2008|work=Jeffrey T. Bergner|page=2203}}</ref><ref name="flog">{{cite web |url=http://fanack.com/en/countries/uae/administration-politics/torture-and-flogging/ |title=Torture and flogging |work=Fanack |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140809185136/http://fanack.com/en/countries/uae/administration-politics/torture-and-flogging/ |archive-date=9 August 2014 |dead-url=yes |quote=UAE Sharia courts sentence to flogging persons found guilty of drug use, adultery, and prostitution in all emirates except Dubai.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.humanrightsvoices.org/EYEontheUN/un_101/figures/?p=2484|title=U.N. Her would-be husband, AM, Omani, was sentenced to 100 lashes and one year in prison.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://gulfnews.com/news/gulf/uae/crime/adulterer-to-be-lashed-jailed-in-sharjah-1.624274|title=Adulterer to be lashed, jailed in Sharjah|date=9 May 2010|work=Gulf News}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amnesty.org/en/region/uae/report-2007|title=UAE – Amnesty International 2007|quote=In June, in the Emirate of Fujairah, a Shari'a (Islamic) court imposed a sentence of death by stoning on Shahin 'Abdul Rahman, a Bangladeshi national, after convicting him of adultery with Asma Bikham Bijam, a migrant domestic worker, who was sentenced to receive a flogging of 100 lashes and to be imprisoned for one year.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/babylonbeyond/2010/06/the-court-in-abu-dhabi-ruled-today-that-the-18-year-old-emirati-woman-who-accused-six-men-of-gang-rape-will-serve-a-one-year.html|title=DUBAI: Alleged victim of gang rape sentenced to one year in prison|quote= At that point, she was facing a penalty for extramarital sex, which is 100 lashes and a minimum of three years in prison.}}</ref>

在[[阿布扎比|阿布扎比]]，有案例公開接吻而被判處80下鞭刑。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thenational.ae/news/uae-news/courts/couple-deny-kissing-on-abu-dhabi-corniche?fb_action_ids=|title=Couple deny kissing on Abu Dhabi Corniche|quote=A man jailed and sentenced to 80 lashes for drunkenly kissing his girlfriend on the Corniche.}}</ref>在阿布扎比和阿吉曼有幾個穆斯林因購買酒精飲料而被判80下鞭刑。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://gulfnews.com/news/gulf/uae/crime/man-to-get-80-lashes-for-drinking-alcohol-1.585233|title=Man to get 80 lashes for drinking alcohol in Ajman|date=19 February 2010|work=Gulf News}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thenational.ae/news/uae-news/courts/man-appeals-80-lashes-for-drinking-alcohol-in-abu-dhabi|title=Man appeals 80 lashes for drinking alcohol in Abu Dhabi|date=9 August 2012|work=The National}}</ref> 2006年，一名愛沙尼亞人因醉酒而被判40下鞭刑。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://gulfnews.com/news/gulf/uae/crime/estonian-soldier-to-be-lashed-1.233170|title=Estonian soldier to be lashed|last=Al Jandaly|first=Bassma|date=16 April 2006|work=Gulf News}}</ref>有案例因非法性行為被判處60鞭刑。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://gulfnews.com/news/gulf/uae/crime/girl-to-receive-60-lashes-for-illicit-sex-1.185126|title=Girl to receive 60 lashes for illicit sex|date=20 June 2007|work=Gulf News}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.khaleejtimes.com/DisplayArticleNew.asp?xfile=data/theuae/2006/July/theuae_July99.xml&section=theuae&col=|title=Two sex workers are sentenced to lashes|date=3 July 2006|work=Khaleej Times}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://expressindia.indianexpress.com/news/fullstory.php?newsid=11353|title=Indian lover in UAE sentenced to 60 lashes}}</ref> <ref name="drm">{{cite web|url=https://www.hrw.org/zh-hans/node/129797/section/10|title=VI. 根據阿聯酋的伊斯蘭法，婚前性行為會被判100下鞭刑。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thenational.ae/news/uae-news/woman-denies-affair-after-hearing-she-faces-stoning|title=Woman denies affair after hearing she faces stoning|quote=Under the same law, premarital sex is punishable by 100 lashes.}}</ref>在阿聯酋，[[石刑|石刑]]是一種法律上的懲罰。2006年，一名外籍人士因通姦罪被判死刑。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amnesty.org/en/library/info/MDE25/005/2006/en|title=UAE: Death by stoning/ flogging|publisher=Amnesty International}}</ref>2009年至2013年間，有數人被判處石刑。<ref name="khaleejtimes.com" /><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dnaindia.com/world/report-man-faces-stoning-in-uae-for-incest-1090813|title=Man faces stoning in UAE for incest}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thenational.ae/news/uae-news/woman-denies-affair-after-hearing-she-faces-stoning|title=Woman denies affair after hearing she faces stoning|date=29 July 2009|work=[[The_National_(Abu_Dhabi)|The National]]}}</ref>伊斯蘭教法規定了婚姻和子女監護等事項的[[家庭法|家庭法]]。伊斯蘭教法的家庭法適用於穆斯林，有時也適用於非穆斯林。<ref name="lia" />非穆斯林僑民可以決定是否要伊斯蘭法對婚姻和子女監護權的裁決。<ref name="lia">{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-28638553|title=Britons 'liable to Sharia divorces' in UAE|publisher=BBC}}</ref> 伊斯蘭教法庭對家庭法案件擁有專屬管轄權，並對通姦，婚前性行為，搶劫和有關罪行等一些刑事案件具有管轄權。阿聯酋穆斯林公民[[叛教|叛教]]會被判處死刑。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/2013/12/10/us-religion-atheists-idUSBRE9B900G20131210|title=Atheists face death in 13 countries, global discrimination: study}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://beaveronline.co.uk/the-international-briefing-persecution-of-atheists-and-apostates/|title=The International Briefing: Persecution of Atheists and Apostates|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150428131415/http://beaveronline.co.uk/the-international-briefing-persecution-of-atheists-and-apostates/|archivedate=2015-04-28}}</ref> 

阿拉伯聯合酋長國在其“刑法典”中納入了伊斯蘭教法有組織的犯罪，而叛教是其中之一。<ref name="shar">{{cite journal|author=Butti Sultan Butti Ali Al-Muhairi |year=1996|jstor=3381546 |title=The Islamisation of Laws in the UAE: The Case of the Penal Code|journal=Arab Law Quarterly|volume=11|issue=4 |pages=350–371}}</ref>在公共場合接吻是觸犯法律的，並可能導致被[[驅逐出境|驅逐出境]]。<ref name="ReferenceB">{{cite web|url=http://www.thenational.ae/news/uae-news/public-kissing-can-lead-to-deportation |title=Public kissing can lead to deportation|work=[[The_National_(Abu_Dhabi)|The National]]}}</ref>同性戀在阿聯酋是非法的，會被判死罪。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://arc-international.net/global-advocacy/universal-periodic-review/u/united-arab-emirates|title=United Arab Emirates|quote=Facts as drug trafficking, homosexual behaviour, and apostasy are liable to capital punishment.}}</ref><ref name="arc.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.care2.com/causes/man-accused-of-gay-handshake-stands-trial-in-dubai.html|title=Man Accused of 'Gay Handshake' Stands Trial in Dubai}}</ref> 

阿布扎比“刑法典”第80條規定，雞姦罪最高可判處14年監禁，“迪拜刑法”第177條對自願雞姦者判處10年監禁。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.glapn.org/sodomylaws/world/uae/united_arab_emirates.htm |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060614053249/http://sodomylaws.org/world/uae/united_arab_emirates.htm |archive-date=14 June 2006 |title=Federal criminal statute In UAE |work=Sodomylaws.Org |deadurl=no}}</ref>[[齋戒月|齋戒月]]期間，在太陽下山前公開進食，飲酒或吸煙是非法的行為<ref name=ramadan>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xs98Nv0Pe5E|title=Sharia law and Westerners in Dubai: should non-Muslims in UAE be made to face Islamic justice?}}</ref>，但孕婦和兒童不再此限。此法律適用於穆斯林和非穆斯林，<ref name="ramadan" />不遵守可能會遭到逮捕。<ref name="Riazat Butt">{{cite news |url= https://www.theguardian.com/uk/2011/jul/31/dubai-tourists-must-respect-ramadan |title=Britons warned to respect Ramadan while holidaying in Dubai |author=Riazat Butt |work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] |date=31 July 2011 |location=London, UK|oclc=60623878 }}</ref> 

在公共空間跳舞在阿聯酋屬於非法行為。<ref>{{cite web|title=Criminal Law of Dubai|url=http://www.lawyersuae.com/court-cases/criminal-law-of-dubai|publisher=lawyersuae.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Dubai bans shisha tobacco smoking, dancing and holding hands|url=http://al-shorfa.com/en_GB/articles/meii/features/2009/03/20/feature-03|work=Al-Shorfa}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=No dancing in public: Dubai|url=http://www.arabnews.com/node/322024|work=Arab News}}</ref>

==基督宗教==
{{main article|{{link-en|阿拉伯聯合酋長國基督教|Christianity in the United Arab Emirates}}}}
阿聯酋的基督徒大都屬於[[新教|新教]]或[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]，該國至少有33個基督教堂。<ref name="irf127360"/>公立學校沒有基督宗教的宗教教育。<ref>{{cite web|author=John Pike |url=http://www.globalsecurity.org/military/world/gulf/uae-religion.htm |title=United Arab Emirates-Religion |publisher=Globalsecurity.org |date=2006-04-17 |accessdate=2010-11-24}}</ref>許多阿拉伯聯合酋長國的基督徒都是[[亞裔|亞裔]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.refdag.nl/nieuws/groeiende_en_vitale_kerk_in_arabische_golf_1_494237 |title=Groeiende en vitale kerk in Arabische Golf - Nieuws - Reformatorisch Dagblad |publisher=Refdag.nl |date= |access-date=2010-11-24}}</ref>

==印度教、錫克教和耆那教==
{{see also|{{link-en|阿拉伯聯合酋長國印度教|Hinduism in the United Arab Emirates}}|{{link-en|阿拉伯聯合酋長國錫克教|Sikhism in the United Arab Emirates}}}}
阿聯酋的[[印度教|印度教]]和[[耆那教|耆那教]]為在阿聯酋居住的印度裔（[[信德人|信德人]]）所信仰。為了感謝印度商人對迪拜貿易港口的早期發展所作的貢獻，阿拉伯聯合大公國前副總統兼總理[[拉希德·本·賽義德·阿勒馬克圖姆|拉希德·本·賽義德·阿勒馬克圖姆]]（Rashid bin Saeed Al Maktoum）批准在杜拜 Bur Dubai區建造一座印度教寺廟，此外，迪拜也有錫克教寺廟。

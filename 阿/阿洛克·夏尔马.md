'''阿洛克·夏尔马'''（{{lang-en|Alok Sharma}}<ref>{{cite web |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/democracylive/hi/representatives/profiles/62774.stm |title=Alok Sharma MP |publisher=BBC |work=BBC Democracy Live |accessdate=25 July 2010 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110303055225/http://news.bbc.co.uk/democracylive/hi/representatives/profiles/62774.stm |archivedate=3 March 2011 |deadurl=yes}}</ref>，{{bd|1967年|9月7日}}）是一位生于印度的英国政治人物，保守党人。现任[[西雷丁_(英國國會選區)|西雷丁选区]]议员，梅政府{{le|社区和地方政府部|Department for Communities and Local Government|住房和规划副大臣}}。曾任[[英國外交及聯邦事務部|亚洲和太平洋政务次官]]。

夏尔马成长于英国南部[[伯克郡|伯克郡]]的{{le|厄雷|Earley}}和{{le|威特利伍德|Whitley Wood}}，在该郡北部{{le|桑宁|Sonning}}{{le|雷丁蓝衣学校|Reading Blue Coat School}}读完高中。<ref name=contest>{{cite web | url = http://www.newburytoday.co.uk/News/Article.aspx?articleID=13025 | title = As Reading West MP prepares to stand down the contest hots up | accessdate = 25 July 2010 | date = 17 April 2010 | publisher = ''[[Newbury_Weekly_News|Newbury Today]]'' | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110929061402/http://www.newburytoday.co.uk/News/Article.aspx?articleID=13025 | archivedate = 2011年9月29日 }}</ref>后考入索尔福德大学研习[[应用物理|应用物理]]和[[电子学|电子学]]，1988年以理学士学位毕业。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ukwhoswho.com/view/article/oupww/whoswho/U251666/|title=Who's Who|publisher=}}</ref>

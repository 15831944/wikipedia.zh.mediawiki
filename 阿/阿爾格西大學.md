'''阿爾格西大學'''（'''Argosy University'''）是[[美國|美國]]的一個[[營利學校|營利性]][[私立大學|私立大學]]系統，由美國的{{link-en|教育管理公司|Education Management Corporation}}於2001年收購阿爾格西教育集團（Argosy Education Group）而成。<ref name=NYTJul01>{{cite news |title=Company News |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2001/07/10/business/company-news-trade-school-operator-making-78-million-acquisition.html |work=The New York Times |date=10 July 2001 |accessdate=7 August 2013}}</ref><ref name=ModzelewskiJuly01>{{cite news |title=Education Management Buys Rival |first=Eve |last=Modzelewski |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1129&dat=20010711&id=8ZkNAAAAIBAJ&sjid=dHADAAAAIBAJ&pg=6805,97931 |newspaper=[[Pittsburgh_Post-Gazette|Pittsburgh Post-Gazette]] |date=11 July 2001 |accessdate=6 August 2013}}</ref> 該大學曾在美國各地有28個校區，并得到過[[西部學校和學院協會|西部學校和學院協會]]等教育認證機構的認可。<ref name=WASC>{{cite web |title=Statement of Accreditation Status, Argosy University |url=http://directory.wascsenior.org/argosy-university#zoom=15&lat=33.78713&lon=-117.89367&layers=TF0BT |work=wascsenior.org |publisher=Western Association of Schools and Colleges|accessdate=November 10, 2011}}</ref> 2015年《[[美國新聞與世界報道|美國新聞與世界報道]]》未將其列入排名。<ref name="USnews">{{cite web|title=Argosy University|url=http://colleges.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-colleges/argosy-university-21799|publisher=[[美國新聞與世界報道|美國新聞與世界報道]]|accessdate=2015-03-29}}</ref>

2019年3月，該大學所有校區均徹底關閉<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.azcentral.com/story/news/local/arizona-education/2019/03/06/argosy-university-could-close-this-week-without-buyer-school-says/3086670002/|title=Argosy University may close campuses across the country as soon as Friday|author=|date=|website=azcentral|access-date=March 8, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wscuc.org/news/information-about-argosy-university|title=Latest Updates on Argosy University - WASC Senior College and University Commission|author=|date=|website=www.wscuc.org|access-date=March 8, 2019}}</ref>。

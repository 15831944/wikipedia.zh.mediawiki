'''阿什莉·安库迪诺夫'''（{{lang-en|Ashlee Ankudinoff}}，{{bd|1990年|8月20日||}}）生于悉尼，是一名澳大利亚女子自行车运动员，主攻场地自行车<ref name="act">{{cite web|url=https://www.australiancyclingteam.com/ashleeankudinoff.html|title=Rider Profile: Ashlee Ankudinoff|work=Australian Cycling Team|accessdate=2019-03-06|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190306122300/https://www.australiancyclingteam.com/ashleeankudinoff.html|archivedate=2019-03-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://olympics.com.au/athlete/ashlee-ankudinoff|title=Ashlee Ankudinoff|work=Australian Olympic Committee|accessdate=2019-03-06|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190306123959/http://olympics.com.au/athlete/ashlee-ankudinoff|archivedate=2019-03-06}}</ref>。安库迪诺夫十五岁时开始练习自行车<ref name="act"/>。她原本主要练习[[铁人三项|铁人三项]]，也曾接触过[[曲棍球|曲棍球]]，后来，她开始将主要精力集中在自行车项目上<ref>{{cite web|url=https://results.gc2018.com/en/cycling-track/athlete-profile-n6028622-ashlee-ankudinoff.htm|title=Cycling Track {{!}} Athlete Profile: Ashlee ANKUDINOFF|work=Gold Coast 2018|accessdate=2019-03-06}}</ref>。

'''阿河'''（'''Aa'''）是[[北歐|北歐]]不少小河的名稱。

<!---
這個詞源自[[古日耳曼語|古日耳曼語]]的''aha''，來自[[拉丁語|拉丁語]]的''aqua''，就是“[[水|水]]”(cf. [[German_(language)|German]] -ach). In German also ''Au'' and ''Aue'' exists, similar to ''[[å|å]]'' in [[North_Germanic_language|North Germanic language]]s, with the meaning of moderately sized [[river|river]]. It is also an [[English_language|English]] [[noun|noun]], beloved of [[Scrabble|Scrabble]] players, meaning a [[stream|stream]].

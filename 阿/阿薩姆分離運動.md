'''阿薩姆分離運動'''是[[印度|印度]]東北部[[阿薩姆邦|阿薩姆邦]]的[[分離主義|分離主義]]運動，始於1970年代<ref name="peopleandpower">{{cite web|url=http://english.aljazeera.net/programmes/peopleandpower/2007/05/2008525184748958544.html|title=Bloody Tea|publisher=|accessdate=28 November 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605071849/http://english.aljazeera.net/programmes/peopleandpower/2007/05/2008525184748958544.html|archive-date=2011-06-05|dead-url=no}}</ref>，[[阿薩姆人|阿薩姆人]]認為中央政府長期對其進行經濟剝削，為[[內部殖民主義|內部殖民主義]]，而與之展開對抗<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2010/may/19/assam-independence-talks-india | location=London | work=The Guardian | first=Aruni | last=Kashyap | title=India needs talks for Assam's peace | date=19 May 2010 | access-date=2018-11-30 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180928135145/https://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2010/may/19/assam-independence-talks-india | archive-date=2018-09-28 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2004/08/15/world/bomb-kills-10-at-india-independence-parade.html|title=Bomb Kills 10 at India Independence Parade|date=15 August 2004|publisher=The New York Times|page=15 (section 1)|access-date=2018-11-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180626111259/https://www.nytimes.com/2004/08/15/world/bomb-kills-10-at-india-independence-parade.html|archive-date=2018-06-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

參與阿薩姆分離運動的組織包括[[阿萨姆联合解放阵线|阿萨姆联合解放阵线]]、[[波多民族民主阵线|波多民族民主阵线]]與{{tsl|en|Karbi Longri N.C. Hills Liberation Front|卡尔比隆里民族解放阵线}}等，其中以阿萨姆联合解放阵线規模最大，該組織於1979年4月<ref name="ulfa">{{cite web|url=http://www.satp.org/satporgtp/countries/india/states/assam/terrorist_outfits/ulfa.htm|title=United Liberation Front of Asom (ULFA) - Terrorist Group of Assam|publisher=|accessdate=28 November 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091117092038/http://www.satp.org/satporgtp/countries/india/states/assam/terrorist_outfits/ulfa.htm|archive-date=2009-11-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>創立於有阿薩姆古國[[阿洪姆王国|阿洪姆王国]]遺跡的[[讓加爾|讓加爾]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.assam.org/node/2382 |title=Where Have They All Gone? | Assam Portal |publisher=Assam.org |date= |accessdate=2010-11-07| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20101025065821/http://www.assam.org/node/2382| archivedate= 25 October 2010 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>，以武裝鬥爭的方式追求阿薩姆的獨立<ref name="papers">{{Cite web |url=http://www.southasiaanalysis.org/%5Cpapers23%5Cpaper2257.html |title=Assam: ULFA’s Rerun of Violence against Migrant Workers |access-date=27 September 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101128074934/http://southasiaanalysis.org/papers23/paper2257.html |archive-date=28 November 2010 |dead-url=yes  }}</ref>，印度政府於1990年將其歸為[[恐怖主義|恐怖主義]]組織<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nia.gov.in/banned_org.aspx|title=NIA :: Banned Terrorist Organisations|publisher=Nia.gov.in|accessdate=12 October 2014|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140119203117/http://www.nia.gov.in/banned_org.aspx|archivedate=19 January 2014}}</ref>，[[美国国务院|美国国务院]]則將其視為「其他需要關注」的組織<ref>[https://www.state.gov/s/ct/rls/crt/2006/82738.htm]  {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080109121220/http://www.state.gov/s/ct/rls/crt/2006/82738.htm|date=9 January 2008}}</ref>。

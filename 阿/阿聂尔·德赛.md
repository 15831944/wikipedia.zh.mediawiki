'''阿聂尔·德赛'''（{{Lang-en|Anil Desai}}，{{Bd|1957年|5月2日}}）是印度[[湿婆军|湿婆军]]政治人物，[[联邦院_(印度)|联邦院]]议员<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://164.100.47.5/newmembers/Website/Main.aspx|title=联邦院资料|accessdate=|author=联邦院|date=|publisher=|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/politics-and-nation/cabinet-rejig-bjp-shiv-sena-bury-hatchet-rajya-sabha-member-anil-desai-to-be-in-union-cabinet/articleshow/45079657.cms|title=Cabinet rejig: BJP, Shiv Sena bury hatchet, Rajya Sabha member Anil Desai to be in Union Cabinet|author=|date=2014-11-08|work=|publisher=The Economic Times|accessdate=}}</ref>，他擅於运营选举、法律事务和后台活动。此外，他还在管理政党资源及创作口号上发挥重要的作用，「UTha Maharashtra」（醒來，[[馬哈拉施特拉|馬哈拉施特拉]]）的口號即是由他所設計。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.newindianexpress.com/thesundaystandard/Uddhav%E2%80%99s-Shadow-Anil-Desai-Shines-in-Shiv-Sena-Eclipse/2014/11/02/article2503626.ece|title=Uddhav's Shadow Anil Desai Shines in Shiv Sena Eclipse kalyan shivsena vile nagar and godrej hill has given lazer activation to Udhav Thakare and operating him.

'''阿肯色郵報'''（{{lang-en|Arkansas Post}}）是位於[[美國|美國]][[阿肯色州|阿肯色州]][[阿肯色縣_(阿肯色州)|阿肯色縣]]的一個[[非建制地區|非建制地區]]。該地的面積和人口皆未知。此地位於[[阿肯色河|阿肯色河]]北側，{{link-en|169号阿肯色州州道|Arkansas Highway 169}}其中一段的一個端點即位於此地<ref>{{Cite map |publisher = DeLorme |title = Arkansas Atlas and Gazetteer |edition = Second |section = 5 }}</ref>。

==歷史==
1686年，法國探險家{{link-en|亨利·德·唐提|Henri de Tonti}}隨著[[勒內-羅貝爾·卡弗利耶·德·拉薩勒|勒內-羅貝爾·卡弗利耶·德·拉薩勒]]的探險隊探索[[密西西比河|密西西比河]]，他在此地設立了一座名為{{link-en|阿肯色貿易站|Arkansas Post}}的[[皮草貿易|皮草貿易站]]，以與當地的{{link-en|夸保人|Quapaw}}等北美原住民進行貿易<ref>[http://www.tshaonline.org/handbook/online/articles/fto19 "Henri de Tonti"] {{Wayback|url=http://www.tshaonline.org/handbook/online/articles/fto19# |date=20171201040130 }}, ''Handbook of Texas Online''</ref>，為歐洲人在阿肯色州的第一個據點，貿易站先後被法國人與西班牙人掌控，是當地重要的貿易中心，戰略地位重要，1819年[[阿肯色領地|阿肯色領地]]建立時，即以此地為首府，直到1821年將首府遷至[[小岩城|小岩城]]<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.tn/books?id=B3QaH1Q3dHcC&pg=PA304&lpg=PA304&dq=Arkansas+Post+was+the+first+territorial+capital+(1819%E2%80%931821)+and+Little+Rock+was+the+second+(1821%E2%80%931836).&source=bl&ots=hn7Zy3ggJt&sig=KB4FIhT2wToxjgVv7Sf4_VorLkM&hl=fr&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiE6ZvZp83XAhXQKewKHaUNDJEQ6AEIRTAE#v=onepage&q=Arkansas%20Post%20was%20the%20first%20territorial%20capital%20(1819%E2%80%931821)%20and%20Little%20Rock%20was%20the%20second%20(1821%E2%80%931836).&f=false|title=My Neck of the Woods: The Lewis Families of Southeastern North Carolina and Northeastern South Carolina|last=Lewis|first=Jerry Dale|date=2005|publisher=Genealogical Publishing Com|isbn=9780806352664|language=en|access-date=2018-11-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171201043918/https://books.google.tn/books?id=B3QaH1Q3dHcC&pg=PA304&lpg=PA304&dq=Arkansas+Post+was+the+first+territorial+capital+(1819%E2%80%931821)+and+Little+Rock+was+the+second+(1821%E2%80%931836).&source=bl&ots=hn7Zy3ggJt&sig=KB4FIhT2wToxjgVv7Sf4_VorLkM&hl=fr&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiE6ZvZp83XAhXQKewKHaUNDJEQ6AEIRTAE#v=onepage&q=Arkansas%20Post%20was%20the%20first%20territorial%20capital%20(1819%E2%80%931821)%20and%20Little%20Rock%20was%20the%20second%20(1821%E2%80%931836).&f=false#v=onepage&q=Arkansas%20Post%20was%20the%20first%20territorial%20capital%20(1819%E2%80%931821)%20and%20Little%20Rock%20was%20the%20second%20(1821%E2%80%931836).&f=false|archive-date=2017-12-01|dead-url=no}}</ref>。[[南北戰爭|南北戰爭]]中，此處是交戰雙方爭奪的據點之<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tshaonline.org/handbook/online/articles/qea04|title=ARKANSAS POST|publisher=''Handbook of Texas Online''|access-date=2018-11-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160315052705/https://www.tshaonline.org/handbook/online/articles/qea04#|archive-date=2016-03-15|dead-url=no}}</ref>。阿肯色貿易站已被指定為[[美國國家歷史名勝|美國國家歷史名勝]]與{{link-en|美國國家紀念公園|List 1836年，此地曾成為一個建制的城鎮<ref>{{cite book|last=Hempstead|first=Fay|title=A Pictorial History of Arkansas: From Earliest Times to the Year 1890|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OFD_49lddgAC&pg=PA827|year=1890|publisher=Southern Historical Press|page=827}}</ref>。

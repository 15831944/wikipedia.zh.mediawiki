{{Vgname|阿玛拉王国：惩罚|en=Kingdoms of Amalur: Reckoning|cn=阿玛拉王国：惩罚|tw=大地王國：罪與罰}}是一款于2012年在[[Windows|Windows]]、[[PlayStation_3|PlayStation 3]]和[[Xbox_360|Xbox 360]]平台发行的[[动作角色扮演游戏|动作角色扮演游戏]]，由[[38_Studios|38 Studios]]和[[Big_Huge_Games|Big Huge Games]]开发，[[艺电|艺电]]发行。<ref>{{cite web | date = 2010-07-20 | title = Kingdoms of Amalur: Reckoning™ Revealed! | url = http://38studios.com/news/kingdoms-amalur-reckoning%E2%84%A2-revealed | publisher = 38 Studios | language=en | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100723094030/http://38studios.com/news/kingdoms-amalur-reckoning%E2%84%A2-revealed | archivedate = 2010-07-23 | deadurl = yes | accessdate = 2017-12-16}}</ref>本作是38 Studios宣布破产前开发的唯一一款游戏。<ref name="Amalur sales 90 days">{{cite web | last = Yoon | first = Andrew | date = 2012-05-24 | title = Kingdoms of Amalur needed 3 million sales 'to break even,' RI governor says | url = http://www.shacknews.com/article/73968/kingdoms-of-amalur-needed-3-million-sales-to-break-even/ | language=en | publisher = ''[[Shacknews|Shacknews]]'' | accessdate = 2017-12-16}}</ref>

本作的首席设计师是Ken Rolston，游戏剧本交由[[R·A·萨尔瓦多|R·A·萨尔瓦多]]编写，[[托德·麦克法兰|托德·麦克法兰]]负责艺术设计，Grant Kirkhope负责音乐创作。<ref name="rpg">{{cite web | last = Yeager | first = Dave | date= 2011-03-07 | title = Kingdoms of Amalur: Reckoning (Preview: Studio Business) | url = http://www.rpgfan.com/previews/Kingdoms_of_Amalur_Reckoning/index.html | publisher = RPGFan | language=en | accessdate = 2017-12-16 }}</ref>

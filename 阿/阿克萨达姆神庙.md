'''阿克萨达姆神庙'''（Akshardham；{{lang-gu|દિલ્હી અક્ષરધામ}}，[[天城文|天城文]]：दिल्ली अक्षरधाम)是[[印度|印度]][[德里|德里]]的一组[[印度教|印度教]]寺庙建筑群<ref name="What is Akshardham">{{cite web|url=http://www.akshardham.com/whatisakdm/index.htm|title=What is Akshardham|publisher=BAPS Swaminarayan Sanstha|accessdate=2008-10-28|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081017103623/http://www.akshardham.com/whatisakdm/index.htm|archivedate=2008-10-17}}</ref>，展示了古老的印度教文化、精神和建筑。3,000名志愿者协助7,000名工匠完成了兴建工程<ref name="What is Akshardham" /><ref name="Mandir">{{cite web|url=http://www.akshardham.com/whattosee/mandir/index.htm|title=Mandir|year=2005|publisher=BAPS|accessdate=2008-09-12|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080912050941/http://www.akshardham.com/whattosee/mandir/index.htm|archivedate=2008-09-12}}</ref>。

这座寺庙吸引了访问德里70%的游客<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/StoryPage/StoryPage.aspx?id=2d0e0cd3-85f9-49ea-bbe9-aa7dc53659c5 |title=Magnificent monuments of Delhi |accessdate=2008-01-08 |author=Manoj Sharma |last=Sharma |first=Manoj |date=2007-12-28 |publisher=[[Hindustan_Times|Hindustan Times]] |quote= }}{{dead link|date=十一月 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot }}</ref>。它于2005年11月6日建成开放<ref name="What is Akshardham" />。它座落在[[亞穆納河|亞穆納河]]河岸，毗邻[[2010年英聯邦運動會|2010年英聯邦運動會]]村。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/StoryPage/StoryPage.aspx?id=310ca27e-d125-467e-a232-73e476d21f99 |title=Games Village gets going as DDA clears lone bid |accessdate=2008-01-08 |author=Moushumi Das Gupta |last=Gupta |first=Moushumi |date=2007-07-04 |publisher=Hindustan Times |quote= }}{{dead link|date=十一月 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot }}</ref> 中心建筑完全用石块砌筑。

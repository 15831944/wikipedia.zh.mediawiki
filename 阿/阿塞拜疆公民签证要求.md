根據亨利簽證限制指數，阿塞拜疆公民前往'''62'''個国家可享受免签证或者落地签（2017年）。<ref>[https://henleyglobal.com/files/download/hvri/HP_Visa_Restrictions_Index_170301.pdf 亨利與合作夥伴簽證限制指數全球排名2017]{{en}}</ref>阿塞拜疆和{{EU}}之間也正在討論放寬簽證要求。<ref>[[東方夥伴關係|東方夥伴關係]]</ref><ref>[http://visa-free-europe.eu/2012/01/azerbaijan-has-begun-the-negotiations-on-visa-facilitation-with-eu-2/ visa-free-europe.eu "Azerbaijan has arranged the negotiations on visa facilitation with EU" 14.01.2012]{{en}}</ref>

'''阿尔伯特'''（出生年月不详，1948年6月11日逝世），是世界上第一名猴[[航天员|航天员]]。

阿尔伯特是一只恒河猴，1948年6月11日坐V2火箭升空，飞行至63千米高空，在飞行中因窒息而死。<ref>V2 Chronology, {{cite web |url=http://www.astronautix.com/lvs/v2cology.htm |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2013-04-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100505160956/http://www.astronautix.com/lvs/v2cology.htm |archivedate=2010-05-05 }}, [[Encyclopedia_Astronautica|Encyclopedia Astronautica]]</ref><ref name= "Air Force">{{cite web |url=http://history.nasa.gov/afspbio/part1.htm |title=The Beginnings of Research in Space Biology at the Air Force Missile Development Center, 1946-1952 |accessdate=2008-01-31 |last= |first= |date= |work=History of Research in Space Biology and Biodynamics |publisher=[[NASA|NASA]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.wsmr.army.mil/pao/FactSheets/V2/v-2tab.htm |title=V-2 Firing Tables |accessdate=2008-01-31 |publisher=[[White_Sands_Missile_Range|White Sands Missile Range]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080125175018/http://www.wsmr.army.mil/pao/FactSheets/V2/v-2tab.htm |archivedate=2008-01-25 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>

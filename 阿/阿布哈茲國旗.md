== 阿布哈茲共和國 ==
{{flag gallery
|File:Flag of Abkhazia.svg|阿布哈茲國旗
|File:Flag of the President of Abkhazia.svg|總統專用旗
}}

阿布哈茲共和國的國旗於1991年設計<ref name=Argun>Аргун Ю.Г. (''Argun Yu. G.''), [http://www.kolhida.ru/index.php3?path=_abigi/book/XLVIII&source=argun О Государственном флаге] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928014609/http://www.kolhida.ru/index.php3?path=_abigi%2Fbook%2FXLVIII&source=argun |date=2007-09-28 }} (''On the state flag'' (XLVIII итоговая научная сессия (11-13 мая). тезисы докладов. - Сухум: АбИГИ, 2004, с. 3-4)</ref>，隔年7月23日正式通過<ref name="fotw">{{cite web|url=http://www.crwflags.com/fotw/flags/ge-abkha.html|title=Abkhazia (Georgia)|date=2009-08-15|publisher=FOTW Flags Of The World|accessdate=2009-10-31}}</ref>。左上方的紅色是中世紀[[阿布哈茲王國|阿布哈茲王國]]的旗色，右手代表的意思是：「Hello to friends！Stop to Enemies！」<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ahakuytra.com/abhazya-sehitleri/247-abhazya-bayraginin-anlami.html |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2017-11-27 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110707094542/http://www.ahakuytra.com/abhazya-sehitleri/247-abhazya-bayraginin-anlami.html |archivedate=2011-07-07 }}</ref>。七顆五角星代表阿布哈茲的七個地區的歷史<ref name=abkhazgov>[http://www.abkhaziagov.org/ru/state/sovereignty/symbols.php Государственные символы<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928080832/http://www.abkhaziagov.org/ru/state/sovereignty/symbols.php |date=2007-09-28 }}</ref>。其餘部分則是白、綠相間的七個條紋，代表[[基督教|基督教]]與[[伊斯蘭教|伊斯蘭教]]之間的寬容。

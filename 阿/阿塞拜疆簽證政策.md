== 一般簽證要求 ==
[[File:Azerbaijan-visa.jpg|300px]]
申請者的護照必須在阿塞拜疆簽證期滿後至少6個月仍然有效<ref name="AZV">[http://azerbaijan24.com/visas/34-azerbaijan-visa/45-azerbaijan-tourist-visa.html Azerbaijan Tourist Visa / Azerbaijan Visa Requirements] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110926085933/http://azerbaijan24.com/visas/34-azerbaijan-visa/45-azerbaijan-tourist-visa.html |date=2011-09-26 }}</ref>。護照持有人如在3個月內會失效，阿塞拜疆當局不會發出簽證<ref>[http://mfa.gov.az/eng/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=606&Itemid=1 Republic of Azerbaijan Ministry of Foreign Affairs Visa Requirements - Documents required for granting visa] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111006091832/http://mfa.gov.az/eng/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=606&Itemid=1 |date=2011-10-06 }}</ref>。需獲得簽證的普通護照或公務護照持有人需在前往阿塞拜疆前獲得簽證；簽證是在申請者到達阿塞拜疆的過境站後，由[[大使館|大使館]]或[[領事館|領事館]]發出的<ref>[http://mfa.gov.az/eng/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=611&Itemid=1 Republic of Azerbaijan Ministry of Foreign Affairs Visa Requirements] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111006091805/http://mfa.gov.az/eng/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=611&Itemid=1 |date=2011-10-06 }}</ref>。

==電子簽證==
阿塞拜疆在2017年1月向部分國家公推出電子簽證政策。系統稱為 ''ASAN Visa''而簽證可以單次入境阿塞拜疆並逗留30日。<ref>{{en}}[http://en.apa.az/xeber_azerbaijan_announces_rules_of_e-visa_iss_244959.html Azerbaijan announces rules of e-visa issuance through ASAN Visa system]</ref> 電子簽證在申請成功後必須以紙張打印並在出入境時連同護照 (護照有效期至少有3個月以上並多於電子簽證有效期限)出示予移民局官員。<ref>{{en}}[http://en.apa.az/azerbaijani-news/social-news/azerbaijan-to-launch-asan-visa-portal-on-jan-10.html Azerbaijan to launch ASAN Visa portal on Jan. 10]</ref> 目前共有82個國家適用於本計劃:<ref>[http://en.apa.az/azerbaijan-politics/foreign-news/azerbaijan-publicizes-list-of-countries-that-will-use-asan-visa-system.html Azerbaijan publicizes list of countries that will use ASAN Visa system]. == 禁止入境 ==
由於阿塞拜疆與[[亞美尼亞共和國|亞美尼亞共和國]]至今仍維持[[戰爭|戰爭]]狀態，阿塞拜疆政府禁止{{ARM}}公民、以及亞美尼亞裔的外國公民進入該國。<ref>{{az}}[http://www.ncsj.org/Azerbaijan.shtml Azerbaijan Country Page] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090308021931/http://www.ncsj.org/Azerbaijan.shtml |date=2009-03-08 }}. NCSJ: Advocates on Behalf of Jews in Russia, Ukraine, the Baltic States & Eurasia. Accessed 23 May 2010.</ref><ref>{{en}}[http://www.panarmenian.net/eng/world/news/25889/ Azerbaijan doesn't allow Armenians in the country - Panarmenian.net]</ref>。

阿塞拜疆政府嚴禁外國公民前往[[納戈爾諾-卡拉巴赫|納戈爾諾-卡拉巴赫]]和{{link-en|亞美尼亞控制的納戈爾諾-卡拉巴赫周圍地區|Armenian-controlled territories surrounding Nagorno-Karabakh|其周圍地區}}，以及被亞美尼亞控制、阿塞拜疆法律上的領土（[[飛地|飛地]]）{{link-en|卡爾基|Karki, Azerbaijan}}、{{link-en|奧賈利-埃斯基帕拉|Yuxarı Əskipara}}、[[巴庫達利|巴庫達利]]和{{link-en|蘇富祿|Sofulu, Qazakh}}；外國公民如進入以上地區，將被阿塞拜疆共和國永久拒絕入境，並列入「[[不受歡迎人物|不受歡迎人物]]名單」<ref>{{en}}[http://azembassy.ro/consular-issues-2/consular-issues/ The Embassy of the Republic of Azerbaijan in Romania - Consular Issues - Visa Section]</ref>。

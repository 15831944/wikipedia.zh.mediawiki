'''阿拔萨'''(Abbasa)(约公元765-803年之后死亡)是一名[[阿拔斯王朝|阿拔斯王朝]]宗室女。

[[哈伦·拉希德|哈伦·拉希德]]对阿拔萨痴迷，然而，因他们是同父异母的兄妹而感到困扰。为了让阿拔萨留在自己的身边，他把妹妹许配给了[[贾法尔·伊本·叶海亚|贾法尔·伊本·叶海亚]]。这次婚姻本来是一场设计，然而阿拔萨却爱上了她被安排的丈夫。一晚，一个女奴被派往贾法尔的寝室，而阿拔萨占据了女奴的住处一晚，她的丈夫感到惊讶。阿拔萨怀孕后并生下了两个男孩。孩子可能是在[[麦加|麦加]]出生。哈里发哈伦最终发现了此事，他处死了贾法尔。阿拔萨可能被杀，或者流放。<ref name="gale">{{cite book|url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G2-2591300042.html|title=Women in World History: A Biographical Encyclopedia|publisher=Gale|year=2002}}</ref><ref name="Betham">{{cite book|url=https://archive.org/stream/biographicaldict00bethuoft/biographicaldict00bethuoft_djvu.txt|title=Dictionary of the Celebrated Women, Every Age and Country|last=Betham|first=Matilda|publisher=B. Crosby and Co.|year=1804|location=Warick Lake}}</ref><ref name="Knapp1846">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=27AqAAAAMAAJ&pg=PA69|title=Female biography: containing notices of distinguished women, in different nations and ages|author=Samuel Lorenzo Knapp|publisher=T. Wardle|accessdate=29 December 2012|year=1846|pages=69–70}}</ref>

'''阿勒河峽谷'''（{{lang-de|Aareschlucht}}）是位於[[瑞士|瑞士]]伯爾尼高原，由[[阿勒河|阿勒河]]雕琢而成的一個石灰岩峽谷。阿勒河峽谷是冰川作用的產物<ref name=asloc>{{cite web | url = http://www.aareschlucht.ch/en/Angebot/The_Aare_Gorge_/location_plan | title = Location Plan | publisher = Aareschlucht AG | accessdate = 30 September 2014}}</ref><ref name=asgeo>{{cite web | url = http://www.aareschlucht.ch/en/Angebot/The_Aare_Gorge_/Geology | title = Geology | publisher = Aareschlucht AG | accessdate = 30 September 2014}}</ref><ref name=swmap>{{cite map | publisher = Swiss Confederation | title = map.geo.admin.ch | url = http://map.geo.admin.ch/?zoom=9&X=174354.99&Y=659195.82&lang=en&topic=ech&bgLayer=ch.swisstopo.pixelkarte-farbe | accessdate = 0 September 2014}}</ref>，現在是瑞士著名的觀光景點。

'''钒(IV)酸镥'''是一种具有[[铁磁性|铁磁性]]和[[半导体|半导体]]特性的[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Lu<sub>2</sub>V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>，<ref>Ichikawa H, Kano L, Saitoh M, et al. [https://www.researchgate.net/publication/1878039_Orbital_Ordering_in_Ferromagnetic_Lu_2_V_2_O_7 Orbital Ordering in ferromagnetic Lu<sub>2</sub>V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>]. ''Journal of the Physical Society of Japan'', 2005, 74(3):1020-1025. DOI: 10.1143/JPSJ.74.1020 </ref>，属于[[烧绿石|烧绿石]]类晶体。<ref>Kitayama K, Katsura T. [http://www.journal.csj.jp/doi/10.1246/cl.1976.815 A new compound, Lu<sub>2</sub>V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>]. ''Chemistry Letters'', 2006(8):815-816. DOI: 10.1246/cl.1976.815</ref>

==制备==
钒(IV)酸镥可由[[氧化镥|氧化镥]]、[[三氧化二钒|三氧化二钒]]和[[五氧化二钒|五氧化二钒]]在高温（1400 °C）、氩气气氛、氧压2.0×10<sup>-5</sup>bar的条件下反应得到。<ref>A.A.Haghighirad, C.Gross, W.Assmus. Powder synthesis and crystal growth of Y<sub>2</sub>V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub> under high pressure and its physical properties. ''Journal of Crystal Growth'', 2008. Vol 310, Issue 7-9. pp 2277-2283. DOI: 10.1016/j.jcrysgro.2007.10.069</ref>
: 2 Lu<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>5</sub> → 2 Lu<sub>2</sub>V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>

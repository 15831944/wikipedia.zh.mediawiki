'''钒(IV)酸钇'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Y<sub>2</sub>V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>。

==制备==
钒(IV)酸钇可由[[氧化钇|氧化钇]]、[[三氧化二钒|三氧化二钒]]和[[五氧化二钒|五氧化二钒]]在高温（1200 °C）和高压氧（5 GPa）的条件下反应得到。<ref>A.A.Haghighirad, C.Gross, W.Assmus. Powder synthesis and crystal growth of Y<sub>2</sub>V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub> under high pressure and its physical properties. ''Journal of Crystal Growth'', 2008. Vol 310, Issue 7-9. pp 2277-2283. DOI: 10.1016/j.jcrysgro.2007.10.069</ref>其反应的高压有助于稳定+4价的钒。<ref>A-A Haghighirad. Crystal growth of A(2)V(2)O7 (A = Y, Er, and Dy) pyrochlores using high pressure. Crystal Growth & Design, 2008. 8(6):1961-1965. DOI: 10.1021/cg800010g</ref>
: 2 Y<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> + V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>5</sub> → 2 Y<sub>2</sub>V<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>

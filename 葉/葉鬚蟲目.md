'''葉鬚蟲目'''（學名：{{lang|la|'''Phyllodocida'''}}）是[[多毛綱|多毛綱]][[足刺亞綱|足刺亞綱]]之下一個[[目_(生物)|目]]<ref name=WoRMS_892>{{WoRMS |id=892 |title=Phyllodocida |accessdate=2016-02-25 |date=2014-02-18 |last1=Read |first1=Geoffrey |last2=Fauchald |first2=Kristian |author=WoRMS }}</ref>。這些物種主要在海洋生活，而有少量生活於[[鹹淡水交界|鹹淡水交界]]處。本目物種大多數是活躍的[[底棲|底棲]]生物，在沉積物的表面上移動或在當中挖洞，又或居住在[[基岩|基岩]]的裂縫和縫隙中。當中有部分物種會建造[[棲管|棲管]]，又有部分是[[浮游生物界|浮游生物]]。據估計，約有3500種[[物種|物種]]<ref name=Tolweb>[http://tolweb.org/Phyllodocida Tree of Life Web Project]</ref>。

==特徵==
葉鬚蟲目是有[[體節|體節]]物種，身長從數毫米到超過一米不等。每個體節均有一對[[槳|槳]]似的[[疣足|疣足]]。[[口前葉|口前葉]]一般有一對到兩對眼睛，背部有一對觸角，a ventral pair of sensory [[Pedipalp|palps]] and a pair of organs on the neck. The [[peristomium|peristomium]] is a ring, often hidden dorsally by the prostomium and the first segment. There is a muscular [[proboscis|proboscis]] with one or more pairs of jaws. The next few segments tend to differ from those further back in having enlarged dorsal and ventral cirri (fine appendages) and reduced parapodial lobes and [[chaeta|chaeta]]e (bristles). Some species have appendages with specialised functions but most have many segments that are similar to each other but which vary in size and shape along the length of the body without abrupt changes in the chaetae and parapodia from one to the next.<ref name=Tolweb/>

==食性==
本目物種一般皆為[[捕食|捕食性動物]]或[[食腐動物|腐食性動物]]<ref>{{EOL|2561579}}</ref>。

==支序分類關係==
本目的三個主要子群體為：[[鱗沙蠶亞目|鱗沙蠶亞目]]（[[Aphroditiformia|Aphroditiformia]]）、[[吻沙蠶亞目|吻沙蠶亞目]]（[[Glyceriformia|Glyceriformia]]）及[[沙蠶亞目|沙蠶亞目]]（[[Nereidiformia|Nereidiformia]]）：
* 鱗沙蠶亞目的特色為其物種每隔一個體節會長有[[鞘|鞘]]質的[[背鱗|背鱗]]（''elytrae''或''scales''）；
* The Glyceriformia are characterised by the presence of unique cone-shaped and ringed prostomiums. 
* The Nereidiformia are more problematic, having no universally distinguishing common features. 
There is agreement on the [[monophyly|monophyly]] of a group including the [[Hesionidae|Hesionidae]], [[Nereididae|Nereididae]] and [[Chrysopetalidae|Chrysopetalidae]] families but opinions differ about [[Pilargidae|Pilargidae]], and molecular and morphological studies continue.<ref>{{cite journal |last=Glasby |first=C.J. |year=1993 |title=Family revision and cladistic analysis of the Nereidoidea (Polychaeta: Phyllodocida) |journal=Invertebr.

'''肌联蛋白'''（{{lang-en|'''titin'''<!--★★★★★请不要在此加入那个18万个字母的IUPAC名★★★★★請不要在此加入那個18萬個字母的IUPAC名★★★★★-->}}）是[[人体|人体]]中是由肌联蛋白基因（TTN）编码的[[蛋白质|蛋白质]]。<ref name= "Entrez_ 7273">{{cite web | title = Entrez Gene: TTN titin| url = http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/entrez?Db=gene&Cmd=ShowDetailView&TermToSearch=7273| accessdate = }}</ref><ref name="pmid2129545">{{cite journal | author = Labeit S, Barlow DP, Gautel M, Gibson T, Holt J, Hsieh CL, Francke U, Leonard K, Wardale J, Whiting A | title = A regular pattern of two types of 100-residue motif in the sequence of titin | journal = Nature | volume = 345 | issue = 6272 | pages = 273–6 | pmid = 2129545 | doi = 10.1038/345273a0 | url = | issn = |date=May 1990}}</ref>肌联蛋白是一个巨大的蛋白质，为[[肌肉|肌肉]]收缩的弹性元件。它由244个[[结构域|结构域]]以及之间的[[肽|肽]]序列连接组成。<ref name="pmid7569978">{{cite journal | author = Labeit S, Kolmerer B | title = Titins: giant proteins in charge of muscle ultrastructure and elasticity | journal = Science | volume = 270 | issue = 5234 | pages = 293–6 | pmid = 7569978 | doi = 10.1126/science.270.5234.293| url = | issn = |date=October 1995}}</ref>这些结构域在蛋白拉伸时[[蛋白质折叠|去折叠]]，而在张力去除后重新折叠。<ref name="pmid11222304">{{cite journal | author = Minajeva A, Kulke M, Fernandez JM, Linke WA | title = Unfolding of titin domains explains the viscoelastic behavior of skeletal myofibrils | journal = Biophys. 肌联蛋白对[[横纹肌|横纹肌]]的收缩很重要，它横跨[[肌节|肌节]]从[[Z线|Z线]]到[[M线|M线]]的区域，同粗肌丝的装配和位置固定有关。在这个基因的[[突变|突变]]能引起肌肉疾病，例如肢带型肌营养不良。<ref name="Gliedergürteldystrophie">{{cite journal | author = Finsterer J| title = Klinik und Genetik der Gliedergürteldystrophien | journal = Nervenarzt | volume = 75 | issue = 12 | pages = 1153-66 | year = 2004 | date = December | doi = 10.1007/s00115-004-1769-5 }}</ref>

肌聯蛋白有已知最長的[[IUPAC命名|IUPAC命名]]（如[[氧|氧]]的IUPAC命名是{{lang|en|oxygen}}）有18萬個字母長，關於它的化學名稱請參考[[:en:wiktionary:Appendix:List_of_protologisms/Long_words/Titin#Noun|Titin#Noun]]。

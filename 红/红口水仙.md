'''紅口水仙'''（學名：''Narcissus poeticus''）是[[水仙屬|水仙屬]]的模式種，原產於中南歐，後來作為觀賞植物引入北美等其他地區<ref>Pugsley, Herbert William. 1915. Journal of Botany, British and Foreign 53 (Suppl. 2): 36, as ''Narcissus hellenicus''</ref><ref>Sell, Peter Derek. 1996. Flora of Great Britain and Ireland 5: 363, as ''Narcissus poeticus'' subsp. ''majalis''</ref><ref>Dulac, Joseph. 1867. Flore du Département des Hautes-Pyrénées,  133, as ''Stephanophorum purpuraceum''</ref>。其花有香氣<ref>{{cite book|last=Woodhead|first=Eileen|author2=William W. Custead|title=Early Canadian Gardening|publisher=McGill-Queen's Press – MQUP|year=1998|page=171|isbn=978-0-7735-1731-8}}</ref>，高{{convert|20|-|40|cm|in}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=1&taxon_id=220008994|title=Narcissus poetica|work=Flora of North America|accessdate=2008-12-25}}</ref><ref>[https://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/page/358308#page/301/mode/1up Linnaeus, Carl von. 1753.  Species Plantarum 1: 289, ''Narcissus poeticus'']</ref><ref>Rafinesque, Constantine Samuel. 1838. Flora Telluriana 4: 20, as ''Autogenes angustifolius'' and ''Autogenes poeticus''</ref>。從中提取的精油可用來製作香水<ref name="Groom">{{cite book|last=Groom|first=Nigel|title=The New Perfume Handbook|publisher=Springer|year=1997|page=225|isbn=978-0-7514-0403-6 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UYrDPqLVD-kC&pg=PA225&dq=%22narcissus+poeticus%22+perfume}}</ref>

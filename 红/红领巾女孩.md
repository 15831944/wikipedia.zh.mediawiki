《'''红领巾女孩：文化大革命回忆录'''》（{{lang-en| Red Scarf Girl: A Memoir of the Cultural Revolution}}）是Ji-li Jiang的回忆录，关于她在[[中國|中国]][[文化大革命|文化大革命]]中的经历，[[黄哲伦|黄哲伦]]作序。作者受《[[安妮日记|安妮日记]]》启发，撰写本书的目的是使[[美国|美国]]人理解中国。<ref>{{cite web|author=Comerford, Linda Brill|url=https://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/print/19971110/41074-pw-the-story-of-a-red-scarf-girl-a-talk-with-ji-li-jiang.html|title=PW: The Story of a Red Scarf Girl: A Talk With Ji-Li Jiang |work=[[Publishers_Weekly|Publishers Weekly]]|date=1997-11-10|accessdate=2018-12-26}}</ref>

'''打巴奴里猩猩'''（[[学名|学名]]：''{{lang|la|Pongo tapanuliensis}}''，{{lang-en|Tapanuli orangutan}}）是[[猩猩属|猩猩属]]的一种，生活于[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]][[苏门答腊岛|苏门答腊岛]]北部{{le|南打巴奴里|South Tapanuli Regency}}的森林之中。打巴奴里猩猩最初于1997年被发现，2017年确立为独立的新种。这是自[[婆罗洲猩猩|婆罗洲猩猩]]与[[苏门达腊猩猩|苏门达腊猩猩]]之后猩猩属的第三个物种。<ref name="curbio">{{Cite journal|last=Nater|first=A.|last2=Mattle-Greminger|first2=M. P.|last3=Nurcahyo|first3=A.|last4=Nowak|first4=M. G.|last5=et al.|date=2017-11-02|title=Morphometric, Behavioral, and Genomic Evidence for a New Orangutan Species|url=http://www.cell.com/current-biology/fulltext/S0960-9822(17)31245-9|journal=Current Biology|volume=|issue=|pages=|doi=10.1016/j.cub.2017.09.047|via=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Nater|first=Alexander|last2=Mattle-Greminger|first2=Maja P.|last3=Nurcahyo|first3=Anton|last4=Nowak|first4=Matthew G.|last5=Manuel|first5=Marc de|last6=Desai|first6=Tariq|last7=Groves|first7=Colin|last8=Pybus|first8=Marc|last9=Sonay|first9=Tugce Bilgin|title=Morphometric, Behavioral, and Genomic Evidence for a New Orangutan Species|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0960982217312459|journal=Current Biology|doi=10.1016/j.cub.2017.09.047|language=en}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/science-environment-41848816|title=New great ape species identified|last=Gill|first=Victoria|date=2017-11-02|work=BBC News|access-date=2017-11-03|language=en}}</ref>同时，这也是1929年[[倭黑猩猩|倭黑猩猩]]种建立之后的近百年以来，首次确立新的[[人科|人科]][[现生种|现生种]]。<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Reese|first=April|title=Newly discovered orangutan species is also the most endangered|url=http://www.nature.com/doifinder/10.1038/nature.2017.22934|journal=Nature|language=en|doi=10.1038/nature.2017.22934}}</ref>

据估计，现存的打巴奴里猩猩仅有约800只，是目前人科中数量最为稀少的物种<ref name=":4">{{Cite news|url=http://www.theguardian.com/science/2017/nov/02/new-species-of-orangutan-discovered-in-northern-sumatra-tapanuli-pongo-tapanuliensis|title=New species of orangutan discovered in Sumatra – and is already endangered|last=Davis|first=Nicola|date=2017-11-02|work=The Guardian|access-date=2017-11-03|language=en|issn=0261-3077}}</ref>，并被划为[[极危物种|极危物种]]。<ref name=":0" />

基因对比分析显示打巴奴里猩猩大约在距今340万年前与同在[[苏门达腊岛|苏门达腊岛]]发现的[[苏门达腊猩猩|苏门达腊猩猩]]发生分化<ref name="curbio" /><ref name="Anderson_N">{{Cite news|url=http://www.sci-news.com/biology/tapanuli-orangutan-pongo-tapanuliensis-05389.html|last= Anderson|first= Natali|title=New Great Ape Species Found on Sumatra: Tapanuli Orangutan (''Pongo tapanuliensis'') |work=Breaking Science News {{!}} Sci-News.com|access-date=2017-11-03|language=en-US}}</ref><ref name="Wright_S">{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/energy-environment/frizzy-haired-smaller-headed-orangutan-may-be-new-great-ape/2017/11/02/c201d200-bfe8-11e7-9294-705f80164f6e_story.html|title=Frizzy-haired, smaller-headed orangutan may be new great ape|last=Wright |first=Stephen|date=2017-11-02|work=Washington Post|access-date=2017-11-03|language=en-US|issn=0190-8286}}</ref>，而前者的基因种群也因为距今7.5万年前[[多峇巨灾理论|多峇湖]]火山喷发而与后者更加隔离。他们间偶发性的接触也在距今1万到2万年前停止。 对比[[婆罗洲猩猩|婆罗洲猩猩]]与苏门达腊猩猩在距今约67万年前的分化，打巴奴里猩猩与苏门达腊猩猩的分化要更加早。其原因是因为苏门达腊岛与[[婆罗洲|婆罗洲]]岛在最近的大陆冰川期相连并构成[[巽他古陆|巽他古陆]]的一部分<ref name="curbio" />。

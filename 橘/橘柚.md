'''橘柚'''（[[学名|学名]]：''Citrus × tangelo''）是一类[[柑橘|柑橘]]类水果，由[[瓯柑|瓯柑]]、[[橘子|橘子]]与[[柚子|柚子]]或[[葡萄柚|葡萄柚]]杂交而成。常见品种包括奥兰多橘柚、明尼橘柚、诺瓦橘柚、[[牙买加丑橘|牙买加丑橘]]等。<ref name=tangelo>[https://hort.purdue.edu/newcrop/morton/tangelo.html Morton, J. 1987. Tangelo. p. 158–160. In: Fruits of warm climates. Julia F. Morton, Miami, FL.]</ref>

奥兰多橘柚（{{lang|en|Orlando}}）、明尼橘柚（{{lang|en|Minneola}}）皆以邓肯葡萄柚与丹西红橘为亲本选育而成。<ref>{{cite web |last1=Jackson |first1=Larry K. |last2=Futch |first2=Stephen H. |url=http://edis.ifas.ufl.edu/ch077 |title=Orlando Tangelo |publisher=Institute of Food and Agricultural Sciences, University of Florida |accessdate=February 4, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Jackson |first1=Larry K. |last2=Futch |first2=Stephen H. |url=http://edis.ifas.ufl.edu/ch072 |title=Minneola Tangelo |publisher=Institute of Food and Agricultural Sciences, University of Florida |accessdate=June 21, 2013}}</ref>明尼橘柚因其果梗部突出成颈状、侧看时果实形似钟形，故又被称为“蜜鐘”（{{lang|en|honeybell}}）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nmns.edu.tw/public/BotanicalGarden/flowers/2015/winter/1215.htm|title=明尼橘柚|work=國立自然科學博物館|date=2016-03-10}}</ref>

诺瓦橘柚（{{lang|en|Nova}}）是由[[克里曼丁红橘|克里曼丁红橘]]与奥兰多橘柚杂交育成。
[[牙买加丑橘|牙买加丑橘]]则是在[[牙买加|牙买加]]野外发现的橘柚品种。<ref name=tangelo />

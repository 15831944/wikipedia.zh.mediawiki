《'''玉子'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Okja'''''}}，{{lang-ko|'''옥자'''}}）是一部於[[2017年電影|2017年]][[韓國|韓國]]和[[美國|美國]]合拍的[[動作片|動作]][[冒險片|冒險片]]，由[[奉俊昊|奉俊昊]]執導並和{{le|強·朗森|Jon Ronson}}共同編劇。電影由韓國童星[[安瑞賢|安瑞賢]]、英國演員[[蒂達·史雲頓|蒂達·史雲頓]]、美國演員[[保羅·迪諾|保羅·迪諾]]和[[傑克·葛倫霍|傑克·葛倫霍]]主演。

《玉子》於2017年5月的[[第70屆坎城影展|第70屆坎城影展]]上的「正式競賽」單元參選[[金棕櫚獎|金棕櫚獎]]<ref name="Main">{{cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/actualites/articles/the-2017-official-selection |title=The 2017 Official Selection |work=Cannes |date=2017-04-13 |accessdate=2017-04-13 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170413234941/http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/actualites/articles/the-2017-official-selection |archivedate=2017-04-13 }}</ref><ref name="IW">{{cite web |url=http://www.indiewire.com/2017/04/cannes-2017-lineup-list-film-festival-schedule-1201804813/ |title=2017 Cannes Film Festival Announces Lineup: Todd Haynes, Sofia Coppola, ‘Twin Peaks’ and More |work=IndieWire |date=2017-04-13 |accessdate=2017-04-13 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170413235104/http://www.indiewire.com/2017/04/cannes-2017-lineup-list-film-festival-schedule-1201804813/ |archivedate=2017-04-13 }}</ref>。後於2017年6月28日在[[Netflix|Netflix]]上發行。

== 劇情 ==
講述了巨大生物“玉子”和與它一起在山谷長大的少女美子之間結下真摯友誼並展開奇異征程的故事。本來玉子是一間公司的比賽產品，因比賽快將完結，玉子需要回到公司接受評分而離開少女美子。因為對玉子的不捨令美子抱著必死的決心踏上了尋找玉子的征程，並被捲入了意想不到的陰謀...

== 製作 ==
2015年10月，宣布[[奉俊昊|奉俊昊]]的下一部電影將由韓國女演員擔任主角，英語演員飾演配角，並預定於[[紐約|紐約]]拍攝<ref>{{cite news|last1=Trumbore|first1=Dave|title=‘Okja': Bong Joon-ho’s Monster Movie Adds Jake Gyllenhaal, Paul Dano, and Bill Nighy|url=http://collider.com/okja-jake-gyllenhaal-paul-dano-bill-nighy-kelly-macdonald/|accessdate=2015-10-05|work=Collider|date=2015-11-05|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151008205502/http://collider.com/okja-jake-gyllenhaal-paul-dano-bill-nighy-kelly-macdonald/|archivedate=2015-10-08}}</ref>。2015年11月10日，[[Netflix|Netflix]]和[[B計劃娛樂|B計劃娛樂]]以5000萬美元的預算收購了本電影，並打算從2016年底開始製作、於2017年發行<ref name=Budget>{{cite news|last1=Trumbore|first1=Dave|title=Netflix Backs Bong Joon-ho’s ‘Okja’ with $50 Million|url=http://collider.com/okja-bong-joon-ho-netflix-brad-pitt-plan-b/|accessdate=2015-11-10|work=Collider|date=2015-11-10|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151112184047/http://collider.com/okja-bong-joon-ho-netflix-brad-pitt-plan-b/|archivedate=2015-11-12}}</ref>。{{le|達利爾斯·康德吉|Darius Khondji}}於2016年2月加入擔任電影的攝影師<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://asianmoviepulse.com/2016/02/bong-joon-ho-netflix-darius-khondji-join-forces-okja/|title=Bong Joon-ho, Netflix and Darius Khondji join forces for Okja|website=Asian Movie Pulse|access-date=2016-04-26|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160501124419/http://asianmoviepulse.com/2016/02/bong-joon-ho-netflix-darius-khondji-join-forces-okja/|archivedate=2016-05-01}}</ref>。2016年3月，[[莉莉·柯林斯|莉莉·柯林斯]]加入演出<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.joblo.com/movie-news/lily-collins-joins-jake-gyllenhaal-tilda-swinton-in-bong-joon-hos-okja-897|title=Lily Collins joins Jake Gyllenhaal & Tilda Swinton in Bong Joon-ho's Okja|website=www.joblo.com|access-date=2016-04-26|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160426171901/http://www.joblo.com/movie-news/lily-collins-joins-jake-gyllenhaal-tilda-swinton-in-bong-joon-hos-okja-897|archivedate=2016-04-26}}</ref>。

主要攝影於2016年4月22日在韓國的[[首爾|首爾]]開始<ref name=Apr2016CS>{{cite news|last1=Evry|first1=Max|title=Okja Begins Filming with Tilda Swinton and Jake Gyllenhaal|url=http://www.comingsoon.net/movies/news/679197-bong-joon-hos-okja|accessdate=2016-04-25|work=ComingSoon.net|date=2016-04-22|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160425153723/http://www.comingsoon.net/movies/news/679197-bong-joon-hos-okja|archivedate=2016-04-25}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://collider.com/bong-joon-ho-okja-starts-filming/|title=Bong Joon Ho's Okja Starts Filming with Tilda Swinton|website=Collider|access-date=2016-04-26|deadurl=no|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20170211112801/http://collider.com/bong-joon-ho-okja-starts-filming/|archivedate=2017-02-11}}</ref>。結束後，劇組於2016年7月31日移至[[加拿大|加拿大]]的[[溫哥華|溫哥華]]攝製<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://collider.com/the-predator-filming-dates/|title=‘The Predator,’ ‘Death Note’ and ‘Okja’ Filming Dates and Locations Revealed|website=Collider|access-date=2016-06-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160613040036/http://collider.com/the-predator-filming-dates/|archivedate=2016-06-13}}</ref>。

導演奉俊昊聲稱，「玉子（Okja）」在片中是一種「很害羞內向的動物，這是我們以前從未見過的獨特動物。」<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nme.com/blogs/the-movies-blog/okja-exclusive-images-netflixs-2017-thriller-starring-tilda-swinton-jake-gyllenhaal-1921370|title=Okja: exclusive images from Netflix's 2017 thriller starring Tilda Swinton and Jake Gyllenhaal - NME|date=2016-12-22|newspaper=NME|access-date=2017-01-18|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170202023920/http://www.nme.com/blogs/the-movies-blog/okja-exclusive-images-netflixs-2017-thriller-starring-tilda-swinton-jake-gyllenhaal-1921370|archivedate=2017-02-02}}</ref>

== 發行 ==
《玉子》最先於2017年5月18日在[[第70屆坎城影展|第70屆坎城影展]]上首映<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/04/cannes-film-festival-2017-lineup-full-list-official-selection-1202068596/|title=Cannes Lineup: Todd Haynes, Sofia Coppola, Noah Baumbach, ‘Twin Peaks’|website=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|first1=Nancy|last1=Tartaglione|first2=Greg|last2=Evans|date=2017-04-13|accessdate=2017-04-13|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170413235223/http://deadline.com/2017/04/cannes-film-festival-2017-lineup-full-list-official-selection-1202068596/|archivedate=2017-04-13}}</ref>。電影定於2017年6月28日在[[Netflix|Netflix]]平台上發行<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.comingsoon.net/movies/trailers/819645-first-teaser-for-bong-joon-hos-okja-coming-to-netflix|title=First Teaser for Bong Joon-ho’s Okja, Coming to Netflix|website=ComingSoon.net|date=2017-02-28|accessdate=2017-02-28|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170301010453/http://www.comingsoon.net/movies/trailers/819645-first-teaser-for-bong-joon-hos-okja-coming-to-netflix|archivedate=2017-03-01}}</ref>。

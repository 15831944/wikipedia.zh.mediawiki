'''玉寶地铁站'''（{{lang-en|Gek Poh MRT Station}}，代號'''JW1'''）是[[新加坡地鐵裕廊區域線|新加坡地鐵裕廊區域線]]上的車站，位于[[新加坡|新加坡]]本岛{{link-en|裕廊西|Jurong West}}规划区。车站沿着裕廊西75街而建，于2019年动工，预计将于2026年启用。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Sim|first1=Fann|title=Jurong Region Line, Singapore's 7th MRT line, to open in phases from 2026|url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/jurong-region-line-mrt-stations-open-2026-10216126|accessdate=9 May 2018|work=Channel NewsAsia|date=9 May 2018|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180509050814/https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/jurong-region-line-mrt-stations-open-2026-10216126|archivedate=2018年5月9日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/singapore/story20180509-857486|title=裕廊区域线2026年起分阶段通车|date=2018-05-09|newspaper=早报|accessdate=2018-07-07|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180707173304/https://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/singapore/story20180509-857486|archivedate=2018-07-07|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/singapore/story20180509-857506|title=裕廊区域线预计明年初动工兴建|date=2018-05-09|newspaper=早报|accessdate=2018-07-07|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180510111036/https://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/singapore/story20180509-857506|archivedate=2018-05-10|df=}}</ref>

==历史==
後來在2013年，政府決定在裕廊西建造地鐵綫，以應付未來的需求，并且以改善新加坡西部的整體交通網絡。<ref name="TODAYonline 2018">{{cite web | title=New Jurong Region Line will connect NTU to existing Choa Chu Kang and Boon Lay stations | website=TODAYonline | date=2018-05-09 | url=https://www.todayonline.com/singapore/new-jurong-region-line-will-connect-ntu-existing-choa-chu-kang-and-boon-lay-stations | access-date=2018-07-08 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180512181907/https://www.todayonline.com/singapore/new-jurong-region-line-will-connect-ntu-existing-choa-chu-kang-and-boon-lay-stations | archivedate=2018-05-12 | df= }}</ref>

2018年5月9日，交通部長[[許文遠|許文遠]]視察[[坎贝拉地铁站|坎贝拉站]]建築地盤時，正式宣佈落實興建裕廊區域線－全長24公里、合共24個車站。同時公佈路線走線及玉寶地铁站位置。玉寶地铁站会是首批启用的鐵裕廊區域線地铁站，连同其他9个车站预计将于2026年通車。<ref name="gov.sg 2018">{{cite web | title=New Jurong Region Line will connect NTU to existing Choa Chu Kang and Boon Lay stations [TODAY Online] | website=gov.sg | date=2018-05-06 | url=http://www.gov.sg/news/content/today-online---new-jurong-region-line-will-connect-ntu-to-existing | access-date=2018-07-08 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180708222329/https://www.gov.sg/news/content/today-online---new-jurong-region-line-will-connect-ntu-to-existing | archivedate=2018-07-08 | df= }}</ref><ref>[https://www.lta.gov.sg/content/ltaweb/en/public-transport/projects/jurong-region-line.html JURONG REGION LINE — Land Transport Authority] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906151019/https://www.lta.gov.sg/content/ltaweb/en/public-transport/projects/jurong-region-line.html |date=2017-09-06 }}</ref>

==反应==
许多居住在地铁站旁的居民，以及地铁站周围的商家都十分期待该地铁站的落成。但有一部分的居民觉得地铁线的路线有一点混乱。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.todayonline.com/singapore/residents-and-students-welcome-jurong-region-line-find-it-confusing|title=Residents and students welcome Jurong Region Line but find it confusing|work=TODAYonline|access-date=2018-05-11|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180512112421/https://www.todayonline.com/singapore/residents-and-students-welcome-jurong-region-line-find-it-confusing|archivedate=2018-05-12|df=}}</ref>

*[[英文|英文]]（以[[牛津拼寫|牛津拼寫]]的[[英國英語|英國英語]]）<ref>{{cite web|title=Spelling|url=http://dd.dgacm.org/editorialmanual/ed-guidelines/style/spelling.htm|publisher=United Nations Editorial Manual Online|accessdate=30 October 2015|postscript=none}}; {{cite web

'''穆罗斯-诺亚湾'''（{{lang-gl|Ría de Muros e Noia}}<ref>{{cite book |author=Emilio G. Portosín |title=Léxico-gráfico na Ría de Muros e Noia |date=2014 |publisher=Asociación Mar de Noia |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=dt4CogEACAAJ|accessdate=2019-01-18|language=gl}}</ref>；{{lang-es|Ría de Muros y Noya}}<ref>{{cite book |title=Diccionario enciclopédico hispano-americano de literatura, ciencias y artes: Apéndice 24-25. Segundo apéndice 26-28 |date=1893 |publisher=Montaner y Simón |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kE1eAAAAcAAJ&pg=PA663&#v=onepage&q&f=false |accessdate=2019-01-18 |language=es}}</ref>）是西班牙加利西亚自治区[[拉科鲁尼亚省|拉科鲁尼亚省]]西南部[[下溺湾|下溺湾]]的一部分，也是[[坦布雷河|坦布雷河]]的[[河口|入海口]]<ref>{{cite book |author=Miguel Anxo Lareo |title=Nagegando Galicia: De Ribadeo al Miño |date=2013 |publisher=Alvarellos, s.l. Abooks |isbn=9788415045212 |page=259 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=sm-fAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA259& |language=es}}</ref>。
==地理==
[[File:Rias_bajas_ES.svg|thumb]]
穆罗斯-诺亚湾属于[[溺灣|溺灣]]地形，它位于[[巴爾班薩山脈|巴爾班薩山脈]]西部<ref>{{cite book |author=Pedro Riudavets y Tudury, Espanya |title=Derrotero de las costas de España y de Portugal: desde el Cabo Trafalgar hasta el puerto de La Corũna |date=1867 |publisher=Dirección de Hidrografía |page=481 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=BmEvAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA481& |language=es}}</ref>，北临[[科尔库维翁湾|科尔库维翁湾]]，南临[[阿罗萨湾|阿罗萨湾]]<ref>{{cite book |author=Augusto Pérez Alberti |title=A xeografía: o espacio xeográfico e o home |date=1986 |publisher=Editorial Galaxia |isbn=9788471545299 |pages=98-116 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=RD9v3jekKbAC&pg=PA98& |language=gl}}</ref>。
==经济==
穆罗斯-诺亚湾沿岸地区的经济以[[淡菜類|贻贝]]养殖为主，主要养殖方式为[[筏|筏]]式养殖。根据1997年统计数据，穆罗斯-诺亚湾共设有118个筏位，每筏的年产量为6.97万[[吨|吨]]<ref>{{cite book |editor1=Olav Vadstein|editor2=Yngvar Olsen |title=Sustainable Increase of Marine Harvesting: Fundamental Mechanisms and New Concepts |date=2013|page=122|publisher=[[施普林格出版社|Springer Science & Business Media]] |isbn=9789401731904 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=WsV9CAAAQBAJ&pg=PA122& |language=en}}</ref>。
==旅游==
溺湾沿岸分布着五个[[市镇_(西班牙)|市镇]]：[[穆罗斯_(拉科鲁尼亚省)|穆罗斯]]、[[欧特斯|欧特斯]]、[[诺亚_(拉科鲁尼亚省)|诺亚]]、[[洛萨梅|洛萨梅]]和[[普埃尔托德尔松|松港]]<ref name=elmundo/>，连接市镇的沿岸公路旁种有[[桉树|桉树]]<ref>{{cite book |editor=Sheila Hawkings |title=Fodor's See It Spain |date=2011 |publisher=Fodor's Travel Publications |isbn=9781400005567 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=FAClHv69ckEC&pg=PA148& |accessdate=2019-01-18 |language=en}}</ref>。这里拥有许多[[海滩|海滩]]，是开展[[冲浪|冲浪]]和[[帆板|帆板]]等[[水上运动|水上运动]]的理想场所<ref>{{cite web |title=Muros Noia, donde confluyen la Costa da Morte y las Rías Baixas |publisher=[[五日报|Cinco Días]]|url=https://cincodias.elpais.com/cincodias/2018/05/16/fortunas/1526486251_535937.html|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180523141925/https://cincodias.elpais.com/cincodias/2018/05/16/fortunas/1526486251_535937.html |archivedate=2018-05-23 |language=es |date=2018-05-16}}</ref>。

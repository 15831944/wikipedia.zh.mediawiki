'''穆罕默德·讨西迪'''（1983年生-）是一名[[波斯人|伊朗裔]]澳大利亚籍[[什叶派|什叶派]][[伊玛目|伊玛目]]。2010年，在[[库姆神学院|库姆神学院]][[大阿亞圖拉|大阿亚图拉]][[赛义德·萨迪克·设拉子|赛义德·萨迪克·设拉子]]的宣布下成为伊玛目。
== 简历 ==
讨西迪生于伊朗[[库姆|库姆]]。家庭是伊拉克什叶派的背景。<ref name='yahoo news'>{{cite web|url=https://au.news.yahoo.com/a/35757467/london-attack-australian-muslim-agrees-with-immigration-bans/|publisher=[[Yahoo!_News|Yahoo! News]]|title='Stop Muslims coming from Middle East': Australian imam supports Hanson's call for immigration bans|date=5 June 2017|accessdate=13 June 2017}}</ref>在他12岁时，其家庭移民至[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]。他于[[珀斯|珀斯]]的澳大利亚伊斯兰学院就读。<ref>Piotrowski, Daniel (8 April 2017). "[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-4388672/Mohammad-Tawhidi-hiding-following-chilling-Islam-threat.html Exclusive: 'The Islamic ruling for this is beheading': Chilling threat to imam who wants Muslim schools shut down - as he reveals he would never have come to Australia if he knew a 'mosque on every corner' was being built]". ''Daily Mail Australia''. Retrieved 27 July 2018.</ref>2009年，他就读于库姆的[[穆斯塔法国际大学|穆斯塔法国际大学]]，随后于2012年退学。<ref name="Problem">{{cite web|url=http://www.abc.net.au/news/2017-06-23/imam-tawhidi-the-problem-with-the-medias-favourite-muslim/8643726|publisher=[[ABC_News_(Australia)|ABC News]]|title=Imam Mohammad Tawhidi: The problem with the media's favourite Muslim|date=23 June 2017|accessdate=12 July 2017}}</ref>但他在库姆和[[卡爾巴拉|卡尔巴拉]]的神学院完成了学习并毕业。<ref name="royal">{{Cite news|url=https://imamtawhidi.com/royalinvestigation/|title=Royal Institute Investigation Verifies Imam Tawhidi's Credentials|work=The Office of Shaikh M. Tawhidi|access-date=2018-02-13}}</ref>他是大阿亚图拉赛义德·萨迪克·设拉子的门生。设拉子于2010年宣布讨西迪为伊玛目。之后他曾为设拉子设立在卡尔巴拉的一所电视台工作过。2015年，他回到澳洲并居住于[[阿德莱德|阿德莱德]]。讨西迪能流利地说阿拉伯语、英语以及波斯语。<ref>Rodway, Nick (26 August 2017). "[https://www.spectator.co.uk/2017/08/man-of-peace/ Man of peace]". ''The Spectator''. Retrieved 28 July 2018.</ref>虽然曾一度称设拉子为导师，他于2015年后都不再追随任何宗教领袖。

讨西迪是2016年由他自己建立的南澳大利亚伊斯兰协会主席。自2016年1月起，他偶尔为[[赫芬顿邮报|赫芬顿邮报]]撰稿。<ref name="huffingtonpost">{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/author/imam-tawhidi||work=[[HuffPost|HuffPost]]|title=Imam Tawhidi|accessdate=9 June 2017}}</ref>讨西迪自称“和平伊玛目”。
== 立场 ==
讨西迪认为伊斯兰教为了存续就必须改革。<ref name="sky">{{cite web|url=http://www.skynews.com.au/news/national/2017/03/29/islam-must-reform-or-it-won-t-survive--imam.html|publisher=[[Sky_News_Australia|Sky News Australia]]|title=Islam must reform or it won't survive: Imam|date=29 March 2017|accessdate=12 June 2017}}</ref>他曾声称应当禁止流行如[[布哈里聖訓|布哈里圣训]]这类被用作恐怖主义圭臬的回教经籍。<ref name='welcome'>{{cite web|url=http://www.abc.net.au/religion/articles/2017/04/11/4651763.htm|publisher=[[Australian_Broadcasting_Corporation|Australian Broadcasting Corporation]]|title=Welcome to the Weird World of Australia's 'Fake Sheikh', Mohammad Tawhidi |date=11 April 2017|accessdate=12 June 2017}}</ref>他还支持将极端伊斯兰教神职人员驱逐出澳大利亚。<ref name="yahoo">{{cite web|url=https://au.news.yahoo.com/a/35087866/sheikh-mohammad-tawhidi-demands-radical-islamic-leaders-be-deported-from-australia/#page1|publisher=[[Yahoo!_News|Yahoo! News]]|title=Sheikh Mohammad Tawhidi demands radical Islamic leaders be deported from Australia|date=19 April 2017|accessdate=9 June 2017}}</ref>他曾要求皇家委员会调查[[瓦哈比派|瓦哈比主义]]、[[萨拉菲运动|萨拉菲主义]]和激进组织[[伊扎布特|伊扎布特]]的流行程度。<ref name="yahoo" />讨西迪反对那些为家庭暴力与仇叛教者行为辩护的穆斯林。<ref name="sky"/>他支持限制建造清真寺<ref name="M">{{cite web|url=https://www.memri.org/tv/australian-shiite-imam-mohammad-tawhidi-we-did-not-come-australia-have-burqas-running-around-don/transcript|title=Australian Shiite Imam Mohammad Tawhidi: We did not come to Australia to have burqas running around; Don't like it? Hop on a kangaroo back to where you came from|accessdate=9 June 2017|date=20 March 2017|publisher=[[Middle_East_Media_Research_Institute|Middle East Media Research Institute]]}}</ref><ref name="welcome" />并宣称清真认证制度危及澳大利亚人的生活方式。他还曾说“巴勒斯坦是以色列的土地”。

讨西迪赞同[[阿亞安·希爾西·阿里|阿亚安·希尔西·阿里]]的意见，认为为了安全起见，应关闭或改革伊斯兰学校。在做出以上陈述、见解后，他声称自己已在警察护送下躲避至一处以避免穆斯林社群的报复。然而警方在澳大利亚人报的采访中声称并没有人员从清真寺中被驱逐出来。<ref name="SMH2">{{cite web|url=http://www.smh.com.au/comment/our-public-discourse-is-weakened-by-onetrick-contrarians-20170524-gwbuji.html|title=Our public discourse is weakened by one-trick contrarians|accessdate=12 June 2017|date=24 May 2017|work=[[The_Sydney_Morning_Herald|The Sydney Morning Herald]]}}</ref><ref name="welcome" />

有关伊斯兰教对待女性这一议题，讨西迪也多有触及。据讨西迪所言，有些穆斯林在澳大利亚实行[[重婚|重婚]]。他建议澳大利亚女性政客不应在穆斯林多数国家穿戴罩头，且支持女性在澳大利亚伊玛目委员会出任职位。

讨西迪认为所有[[恐怖主义|恐怖主义]]行径按[[古兰经|古兰经]]来说都应该受到谴责。<ref name="Huff">{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/shaikh-m-tawhidi/national-security-the-rol_b_11635624.html|title=National Security: The Roles of Muslim Faith Leaders|accessdate=15 June 2017|date=22 August 2016|work=[[HuffPost|HuffPost]]}}</ref>他谴责[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]并认为它不能代表伊斯兰宗教。[[2017年6月伦敦袭击|2017年6月伦敦袭击]]爆发后，讨西迪将激进派别称为“（伊斯兰宗教的）癌变”。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/home-news/london-terror-attack-latest-muslims-help-victims-free-rides-food-shelter-borough-market-isis-a7771796.html|title=Muslim taxi drivers offer free rides home after London terror attacks|accessdate=12 June 2017|date=4 June 2017|work=[[The_Independent|The Independent]]}}</ref>

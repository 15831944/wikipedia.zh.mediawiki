'''褐蝦'''（學名：''Crangon crangon''）是[[褐蝦屬|褐蝦屬]]的一種蝦，原產於[[北海_(大西洋)|北海]]南部，但在地中海、波羅的海、黑海也有其蹤跡，是一種重要的經濟水產，原產地附近國家都有食用褐蝦的習慣<ref name="FAO">{{cite web |url=http://www.fao.org/figis/servlet/species?fid=3435 |title=''Crangon crangon'' (Linnaeus, 1758) |work=Species Fact Sheets |publisher=[[Food_and_Agriculture_Organization|Food and Agriculture Organization]] |accessdate=June 24, 2011}}</ref>。

==形態==
褐蝦長{{convert|30|-|50|mm|abbr=on}}，最長可達{{convert|90|mm|abbr=on}}<ref name="ARKive">{{cite web |url=http://www.arkive.org/species/ARK/invertebrates_marine/Crangon_crangon/ |title=''Crangon crangon'' |publisher=[[ARKive|ARKive]] |accessdate=June 24, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080517033109/http://www.arkive.org/species/ARK/invertebrates_marine/Crangon_crangon/ |archive-date=2008-05-17 |dead-url=yes |df= }}</ref>。它生活于淺海，可依據環境改變體色<ref name="ARKive"/>。

雌蝦在體長達到{{convert|22|-|43|mm|abbr=on}}時、雄蝦在達到{{convert|30|-|45|mm|abbr=on}}長時達到性成熟<ref name="Campos_Veer">{{cite book |author=Joana Campos |author2=Henk W. van der Veer |last-author-amp=yes |title=Autecology of ''Crangon crangon'' (L.) with an emphasis on latitudinal trends |journal=Oceanography and Marine Biology: An Annual Review |volume=46 |editor1=R. N. Gibson |editor2=R. J. A. Atkinson |editor3=J. D. M. Gordon |publisher=[[CRC_Press|CRC Press]] |year=2008 |pages=65–104 |isbn= 978-1-4200-6575-6 |doi=10.1201/9781420065756.ch3}}</ref>。

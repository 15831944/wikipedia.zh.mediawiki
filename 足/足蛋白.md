'''足蛋白'''（{{lang|en|podocin}}、{{lang|en|nephrosis 2, idiopathic, steroid-resistant}}）即[[腎|腎臟]]的組成[[腎單位|腎元]]之[[腎小體|腎小體]]內的[[鮑氏囊|鮑氏囊]]上之[[足細胞|足細胞]][[濾過裂隙|裂隙隔膜]](slit diaphragm)的蛋白質成分。[[腎小球|腎小球毛細血管內皮細胞]]，[[腎小球基底膜|腎小球基底膜]]及裂隙隔膜充當的[[腎小球|腎小球]]濾過屏障。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Update on the glomerular filtration barrier|pages=226–232|issn=1473-6543|journal=Current Opinion in Nephrology and Hypertension|volume=18|issue=3|date=2009-5|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19374010|accessdate=2019-02-12|author=George Jarad, Jeffrey H. Miner}}</ref> 足蛋白基因[[NPHS2|NPHS2]]的突變能引起[[腎病症候群|腎病症候群]](NS)，如[[局灶節段性腎小球硬化症|局灶節段性腎小球硬化症]](FSGS)或[[微小病變|微小病變]](MCD)。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Podocin inactivation in mature kidneys causes focal segmental glomerulosclerosis and nephrotic syndrome|pages=2181–2189|issn=1533-3450|journal=Journal of the American Society of Nephrology: JASN|volume=20|issue=10|date=2009-10|doi=10.1681/ASN.2009040379|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19713307|accessdate=2019-02-12|author=Géraldine Mollet, Julien Ratelade, Olivia Boyer, Andrea Onetti Muda, Ludivine Morisset, Tiphaine Aguirre Lavin, David Kitzis, Margaret J.

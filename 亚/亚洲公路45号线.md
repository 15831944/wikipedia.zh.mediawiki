'''亚洲公路45号线'''（[[英语|英语]]：{{lang|en|'''Asian Highway 45'''}}），编号{{Asian Highway nopic|45}}，是[[亚洲公路网|亚洲公路网]]系統中的一条全线均位于[[印度|印度]]的道路<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.roadtraffic-technology.com/projects/asian-highway-network/ |title=Asian Highway Network, Great Asian Highway |publisher=Road Traffic |language=en |access-date=2018-11-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180324000319/http://www.roadtraffic-technology.com/projects/asian-highway-network |archive-date=2018-03-24 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.unescap.org/our-work/transport/asian-highway/database|title=United Nations - ESCAP - Asian Highway Database|last=|first=|date=|publisher=[[联合国亚洲及太平洋经济社会委员会|联合国亚洲及太平洋经济社会委员会]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180913011325/https://www.unescap.org/our-work/transport/asian-highway/database|archive-date=2018-09-13|dead-url=no|access-date=2018-11-01|language=en}}</ref>。其线路全长约为{{convert|2030|km}}<ref name=2011r>{{Cite web|url=http://www.egazette.nic.in/WriteReadData/2011/E_574_2012_016.pdf|title=New National Highways notification - Government of India|last=|first=|date=|website=The Gazette of India|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180504155047/http://www.egazette.nic.in/WriteReadData/2011/E_574_2012_016.pdf|archive-date=2018-05-04|dead-url=no|access-date=2018-11-01|language=en}}</ref>。线路起点位于[[西孟加拉邦|西孟加拉邦]][[加尔各答|加尔各答]]的[[国道5号_(印度)|国道16号]]的交叉处，终点位于[[卡纳塔克邦|卡纳塔克邦]][[班加罗尔|班加罗尔]]的{{link-en|国道48号 (印度)|National Highway 48 (India)|国道48号}}的交叉处<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://morth.nic.in/showfile.asp?lid=365 |title=Number wise National Highways |access-date=2018-11-01 |language=en |publisher=印度公路运输和公路部 |archive-url=https://archive.is/20121022113344/http://www.morth.nic.in/showfile.asp?lid=365 |archive-date=2012-10-22 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

== 线路 ==
{{double image|center|National Highway 16 (old NH 5) at Visakhapatnam.jpg|255|NH5.jpg|235|左：拍摄于[[安得拉邦|安得拉邦]][[维沙卡帕特南|维沙卡帕特南]]<br />右：拍摄于[[卡纳塔克邦|卡纳塔克邦]][[班加罗尔|班加罗尔]]}}
亚洲公路45号线主要经过城市有：[[布拉赫马普尔|布拉赫马普尔]] - [[布巴内什瓦尔|布巴内什瓦尔]] - [[克塔克|克塔克]] - [[贾贾普尔|贾贾普尔]] - [[巴德拉克|巴德拉克]] - [[巴拉索尔|巴拉索尔]] - [[巴里帕达|巴里帕达]] - {{link-en|哈尔普卡利亚|Jharpokharia}} - [[班加罗尔|班加罗尔]]

亚洲公路45号线主要经过5个[[印度行政区划|邦]]，包括西孟加拉邦、奥里萨邦、安得拉邦、泰米尔纳德邦和卡纳塔克邦。其中，[[西孟加拉邦|西孟加拉邦]]路段全长161千米，[[奥里萨邦|奥里萨邦]]路段全长488千米，[[安得拉邦|安得拉邦]]路段全长1024千米，[[泰米尔纳德邦|泰米尔纳德邦]]路段全长45千米<ref name="lengthNHAI">{{cite web |url=http://www.nhai.org/doc/23june12/nh_nh%20wise.pdf |title=National Highways and their length |date= |accessdate=2018-11-01 |language=en |work=report |format=pdf |publisher=National Highway Authority of India |author= |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6DonKzsUk?url=http://www.nhai.org/Doc/23june12/NH_NH%20wise.pdf |archive-date=2013-01-20 |dead-url=yes |df= }}</ref>。

== 相接道路 ==
亚洲公路45号线与[[亚洲公路1号线|亚洲公路1号线]]、[[亚洲公路43号线|亚洲公路43号线]]、[[亚洲公路46号线|亚洲公路46号线]]和[[亚洲公路47号线|亚洲公路47号线]]交汇。其中与1号线在加尔各答交汇；与43号线在班加罗尔至[[克里斯赫纳吉里|克里斯赫纳吉里]]段共线；与46号线在克勒格布尔附近交汇；与47号线在班加罗尔交汇。<ref name="handbook">{{Cite web|publisher=Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific |url=http://www.unescap.org/ttdw/Publications/TIS_pubs/pub_2303/Full%20version.pdf |title=2003 Asian Highway Handbook |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120414184019/http://www.unescap.org/ttdw/Publications/TIS_pubs/pub_2303/Full%20version.pdf |archivedate=2012-04-14 |date=2003 |pages=41 |accessdate=2018-11-01|language=en }}</ref>

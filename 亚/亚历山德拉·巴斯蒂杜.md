'''亚历山德拉·巴斯蒂杜'''（{{lang-en|Alexandra Bastedo}}，{{bd|1946年|3月9日|2014年|1月12日}}），是一名英国演员和活动家。她最著名的表演是出演1968年的《{{link-en|冠军们|The Champions}}》<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.express.co.uk/posts/view/195819/What-happened-to-the-70s-sexpots-What-happened-to-the-70s-sexpots-What-happened-to-the-70s-sexpots-What-happened-to-the-70s-sexpots-What-happened-to-the-70s-sexpots-What-happened-to-the-70s-sexpots-What-happened-to-the-70s-sexpots-What-happened-to-the-70s-sexpots-|title=WHAT HAPPENED TO THE 70S SEXPOTS?|work=[[The_Express|The Express]]|date=27 August 2010|accessdate=3 November 2010}}</ref>。她还是一名素食主义者，她并以动物保护活动者而闻名<ref>{{cite web|title=Alexandra Bastedo|url=http://www.abcanimalsanctuary.co.uk/page_2333988.html|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130723021240/http://abcanimalsanctuary.co.uk/page_2333988.html|archivedate=2013-07-23}}</ref>。

2014年1月12日，她因为[[乳腺癌|乳腺癌]]病重，在英国医院去世<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-25708277|title=Alexandra Bastedo dies aged 67|publisher=[[BBC_NEWS|BBC NEWS]]|date=13 January 2014}}</ref>。

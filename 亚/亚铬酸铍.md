'''亚铬酸铍'''是一种无机化合物，化学式为BeCr<sub>2</sub>O<sub>4</sub>，或写作Be(CrO<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。有毒。它在自然界中以Mariinskite矿的形式存在。<ref>Pautov, L. A., Popov, M. P., Erokhin, Y. V., Khiller, V. V., & Karpenko, V. Y. (2013). Mariinskite, BeCr2O4, a new mineral, chromium analog of chrysoberyl. ''Geology of Ore Deposits'', ''55''(8), 648-662. DOI: 10.1134/S1075701513080096</ref>

==结构==
亚铬酸铍具有Mg<sub>2</sub>SiO<sub>4</sub>结构，晶胞参数为a=4.555 A，b=5.663 A，c=9.792 A。<ref>R C Ropp. Encyclopedia of the Alkaline Earth Compounds. Elsevier, 2013. ISBN 978-0-444-59550-8. pp 798. 10.2.1 Beryllium chromates</ref>

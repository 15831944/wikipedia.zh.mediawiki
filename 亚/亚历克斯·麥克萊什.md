'''亞歷山大·"亞歷斯"·麥利殊'''（{{lang-en|'''Alexander "Alex" McLeish'''}}，{{bd|1959年|1月21日|catidx=McLeish, Alex}}<ref name="manager profile - Alex McLeish">{{cite web |language=en |url=http://www.4thegame.com/club/glasgow-rangers-fc/manager-profile/29192/alex_mcleish.html |title=Manager Profile – Alex McLeish |work=''4thegame'' |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080306112803/http://www.4thegame.com/club/glasgow-rangers-fc/manager-profile/29192/alex_mcleish.html |archivedate=2008年3月6日 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>）是一名[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]前[[足球|足球]][[運動員|運動員]]，退役後擔任[[領隊_(足球)|領隊]]，曾兩度執教[[蘇格蘭國家足球隊|蘇格蘭]]。生於[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]][[格拉斯哥|格拉斯哥]]，麥利殊在1980年代效力處於黃金時期的[[阿伯丁足球俱乐部|鴨巴甸]]，司職[[後衛_(足球)|中堅]]，為球會上陣接近500場，並曾為[[蘇格蘭足球代表隊|蘇格蘭]]參賽77場國際賽。

他於[[马瑟韦尔足球俱乐部|馬瑟韋爾]]和[[希伯尼安足球俱乐部|喜百年]]開展領隊事業，其後轉投[[流浪者足球俱乐部|格拉斯哥流浪]]，任教5年期間贏得兩次聯賽錦標和5項盃賽冠軍。麥利殊出任[[蘇格蘭足球代表隊|蘇格蘭]]主帥10個月，差一點未能帶隊闖入[[2008年欧洲足球锦标赛|2008年欧洲足球锦标赛]]決賽圈。他於2007年11月辭職加盟[[英超|英超]]的[[伯明翰足球會|伯明翰城]]，雖然球隊於球季結束後降班，但一年後即重返[[英超|英超]]。伯明翰城贏得[[2011年英格蘭聯賽盃決賽|聯賽盃]]冠軍，但球隊再次於季後降班[[英冠|英冠]]。麥利殊請辭後轉投伯明翰城同市死敵[[阿士東維拉足球會|阿士東維拉]]，是首名直接由伯明翰城加盟維拉的領隊，亦是繼[[朗·桑達士|朗·桑達士]]（Ron Saunders）後第二位曾分別任教[[伯明翰|伯明翰]]兩支頂級球隊的領隊。麥利殊帶領下維拉於[[2011–12賽季英格蘭超級聯賽|2011/12年球季]]僅以兩分之微倖免降班，但他仍難逃被辭退的命運。約隱11個月後，於2012年12月獲聘為[[英冠|英冠]][[诺丁汉森林足球俱乐部|諾定咸森林]]的新帥，惟上任僅40日便下堂求去。

為表揚他對蘇格蘭體壇的貢獻，[[阿伯丁大学|阿伯丁大学]]於2008年頒授[[名誉学位|榮譽博士學位]]予麥利殊<ref>{{cite news |language=en |url=http://www.birminghammail.net/birmingham-sport/birmingham-city-fc/birmingham-city-fc-news/2008/07/03/alex-mcleish-honoured-by-university-of-aberdeen-97319-21220729/ |title=Alex McLeish honoured by University of Aberdeen |newspaper=''Birmingham Mail'' |date=3 July 2008 |accessdate=4 July 2008}}</ref>。

==生平==
===早年===
麥利殊在[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]][[格拉斯哥|格拉斯哥]]的杜克街醫院（Duke Street Hospital）出生，父親老阿歷斯（Alex）是一名船廠工人，母親名為珍（Jean）<ref name="DonOfAnEra">{{cite book |last= McLeish |first= Alex |coauthors= Alastair Macdonald |title= The Don Of An Era |publisher= John Donald Publishers |year= 1988 |month= |isbn= 0-85976-242-4}}</ref>，家裡還有兩名弟妹安琪拉（Angela）和伊安（Ian）。曾分別居於格拉斯哥的柏克黑德（Parkhead）和堅寧公園（Kinning Park），但於麥利殊達到上學年齡後不久舉家遷移到伦弗鲁郡（Renfrewshire）的巴尔黑德（Barrhead）定居。他就讀於斯普林希尔小学（Springhill Primary）和巴尔黑德高中（Barrhead High School），其後轉到[[佩斯利|佩斯利]]的约翰·尼尔森高中（John Neilson High School）<ref name="DonOfAnEra"/>。

少年時期麥利殊代表巴尔黑德青年会（Barrhead Youth Club）和格拉斯哥聯（Glasgow United）上陣，亦曾短時間在[[咸美顿学院足球俱乐部|咸美頓]]接受訓練。1976年麥利殊為格拉斯哥聯在一場地區盃賽決賽上陣，包括[[阿伯丁足球俱乐部|鴨巴甸]]時任領隊[[艾利·麦李欧德|艾利·麦李欧德]]（Ally MacLeod）在內的代表團到場觀戰，翌日麥利殊便簽約正式加盟鴨巴甸<ref name="DonOfAnEra"/>。

===球員===
====球會====
;鴨巴甸年代
麥利殊加盟[[阿伯丁足球俱乐部|鴨巴甸]]後的首兩個球季大部分時間在預備隊參賽，並曾外借到地區初級球隊[[刘易斯联足球俱乐部|刘易斯联]]（Lewis United）。他在[[比利·麦克尼尔|比利·麦克尼尔]]（Billy McNeill）麾下首次為一隊上陣是於1978年1月2日的新年檔期對[[邓迪联足球俱乐部|邓迪联]]<ref name="DonOfAnEra"/>；而他的首場主要決賽是在[[亚历克斯·弗格森|亚历克斯·弗格森]]任內於1978/79年度[[蘇格蘭聯賽盃|蘇格蘭聯賽盃]]以1-2負於[[流浪者足球俱乐部|格拉斯哥流浪]]中後補登場。但其後的7個球季麥利殊取得重大的成就，分別贏得8個本土及2個歐洲錦標。職業生涯亮點包括為「导师」（Dons）上陣第200場於1982年[[蘇格蘭足總盃|蘇格蘭足總盃]]決賽以4-1擊敗格拉斯哥流浪中取得入球，及在1983年的[[欧洲优胜者杯|欧洲优胜者杯]]決賽擊敗[[皇家马德里足球俱乐部|皇家马德里]]奪標<ref name="Factfile">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/scotland/1704404.stm |title=Alex McLeish Factfile |date=11 December 2007 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref>。 

During this period, McLeish formed a formidable [[defender_(football)|defensive]] triumvirate with [[威利·米勒|威利·米勒]]（Willie Miller） and [[吉姆·礼顿|吉姆·礼顿]]（Jim Leighton） for both club and country. When Ferguson left in 1986 to go to [[曼彻斯特联足球俱乐部|曼联]], he tried to get McLeish to sign, but it did not work out; he also had talks with [[托特纳姆热刺足球俱乐部|热刺]]. 他在1990年贏得[[蘇格蘭FWA足球先生|蘇格蘭FWA足球先生]] ,<ref name="Factfile"/> after a season in which Aberdeen won both domestic cups. He was captain of Aberdeen after the retirement of Willie Miller.

Even after he had won his first [[蘇格蘭足球代表隊|蘇格蘭]] cap, McLeish's father asked then Aberdeen boss [[亞歷克斯·弗格森|亞歷克斯·弗格森]]to persuade him to continue training as an [[會計師|會計師]].

====國家隊====
麥利殊是代表[[蘇格蘭足球代表隊|蘇格蘭]]歷史以來擁有出場次數最多排名第三位，由1980年開始至1993年期間總共77場。<ref name="Roll of Honour">{{cite news |url=http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/scottish_football.cfm?curpageid=350 |title=International Roll of Honour |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[Scottish_Football_Association|Scottish Football Association]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071024132216/http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/scottish_football.cfm?curpageid=350 |archivedate=2007年10月24日 |deadurl=yes }}</ref> He was first capped for the [[蘇格蘭21歲以下足球代表隊|蘇格蘭U21]] team while still a reserve player at Aberdeen by his former club manager Ally MacLeod, eventually gaining six caps at that level. His full international debut came on 26 March 1980 against [[葡萄牙國家足球隊|葡萄牙]], manager [[乔克·斯泰恩|乔克·斯泰恩]] playing him in midfield alongside [[阿奇·格米尔|阿奇·格米尔]]（Archie Gemmill） and [[格雷姆·索内斯|格雷姆·索内斯]].<ref name="DonOfAnEra"/> He played in three [[世界盃足球賽|世界盃]] with [[蘇格蘭足球代表隊|蘇格蘭]], in [[1982年世界盃足球賽|1982年]], [[1986年世界盃足球賽|1986年]] and [[1990年世界盃足球賽|1990年]].<ref name="Factfile"/> He is a member of both the [[蘇格蘭足球名人堂|蘇格蘭足球名人堂]] and the [[蘇格蘭足球國際賽代表榮譽榜|蘇格蘭足球國際賽代表榮譽榜]].<ref name="Roll of Honour"/> On the occasion of his 50th cap, against [[盧森堡國家足球隊|盧森堡]] in 1987, McLeish was appointed team captain for the game.<ref name="DonOfAnEra"/> His final cap came on 17 February 1993 in a 3–0 win over [[馬爾他國家足球隊|馬爾他]] at [[咸普頓公園球場|咸普頓公園球場]] during the [[1994年世界盃足球賽|1994年世界盃]] qualifiers. He never scored in his 77 appearances for Scotland.<ref>[http://www.sporting-heroes.net/football/scotland/alex-mcleish-4800/scottish-caps-part-2-1986-93_a11925/]</ref>

===領隊===
;馬瑟韋爾
After his successful playing career he quickly went into football [[領隊_(足球)|管理]] with [[马瑟韦尔足球俱乐部|馬瑟韋爾]] in 1994, one year before he retired as a player.<ref name="Factfile"/> His first season at Motherwell saw him take the 拉纳克郡（Lanarkshire） club to second in the Premier Division behind [[沃尔特·史密斯|沃尔特·史密斯]]'s [[流浪者足球俱乐部|格拉斯哥流浪]].<ref name="Factfile"/> However, he failed to build on this success and the next two season were spent in relegation battles. He resigned as manager to take over at [[希伯尼安足球俱乐部|喜百年]] in 1998.<ref name="Factfile"/>

;喜百年
McLeish took over a struggling Hibernian side, which was relegated from the [[蘇格蘭足球聯賽超級聯賽|蘇格蘭足球聯賽超級聯賽]] in 1998 despite a slight upturn in fortunes under McLeish. He then guided the [[爱丁堡|爱丁堡]] team back to the [[蘇格蘭足球聯賽超級聯賽|蘇超]] at the first attempt by winning the [[蘇格蘭足球甲級聯賽|蘇甲]] championship.<ref name="Factfile"/> 

Hibernian consolidated in their first season back in the top division, finishing mid-table and reaching the [[蘇格蘭足總盃|蘇格蘭足總盃]] semi-final.<ref name="Factfile"/> In the 2000–01 season, Hibs started very strongly. Eventually they had to settle for third place and a Scottish Cup final appearance.<ref name="Factfile"/> This performance attracted the attention of bigger clubs, including [[西汉姆联足球俱乐部|韋斯咸]] and 格拉斯哥流浪.

McLeish attracted top players such as [[拉塞尔·拉塔皮|拉塞尔·拉塔皮]]（Russell Latapy） and former [[法國國家足球隊|法國國腳]] [[弗兰克·索济|弗兰克·索济]]（Franck Sauzée） to Hibs. He also helped develop young striker [[肯尼·米勒|肯尼·米勒]]（Kenny Miller）, who would later go on to play for Scotland.

During this period McLeish worked towards and was awarded a 歐洲足協專業牌照（UEFA Pro Licence）.<ref name="Andy caps his return to the big time">{{cite news |url=http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_qn4156/is_20001105/ai_n13953165/ |title=Andy caps his return to the big time |first=Michael |last=Grant |date=5 November 2000 |accessdate=25 March 2010 |newspaper=[[Sunday_Herald|Sunday Herald]] |format=reprint |publisher=[[FindArticles|FindArticles]]}}</ref><ref name="England could learn from Scottish system">{{cite news |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/international/scotland/2327013/England-could-learn-from-Scottish-system.html |title=England could learn from Scottish system |first=Henry |last=Winter |date=29 November 2007 |accessdate=25 March 2010 |newspaper=[[The_Daily_Telegraph|The Daily Telegraph]]}}</ref>

;格拉斯哥流浪
His work at Hibs was noticed, and he was linked with several moves to England, before he was appointed as [[流浪者足球俱乐部|格拉斯哥流浪]] manager in December 2001<ref name="Rangers appointment">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/r/rangers/1701609.stm |title=Rangers unveil McLeish |date=11 February 2001 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> after outgoing Rangers manager [[迪克·艾德沃卡特|迪克·艾德沃卡特]] recommended McLeish to chairman [[大卫·穆雷|大卫·穆雷]]（David Murray）.<ref name="">{{cite news |url=http://www.heraldscotland.com/advocaat-s-vested-interest-in-the-improbable-dream-1.866731 |title=Advocaat's vested interest in the improbable dream |date=9 October 2007 |accessdate=25 March 2010 |work=[[The_Herald_(Glasgow)|The Herald]] |first=Darryl |last=Broadfoot |authorlink=Darryl Broadfoot}}</ref>

McLeish was an instant success at Rangers, winning both the [[蘇格蘭足總盃|蘇格蘭足總盃]]<ref name="Celtic 2-3 Rangers">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/scottish_cup/1966673.stm |title=Rangers win Old Firm final |date=4 May 2002 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> and [[蘇格蘭聯賽盃|蘇格蘭聯賽盃]]<ref name="Ayr Utd 0-4 Rangers">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/cis_ins_cup/1875551.stm |title=Rangers lift CIS Cup |date=17 March 2002 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> in his first season, but the big prize of the league title was essentially lost before his arrival. His second season saw him go one better when he won the domestic treble, with the help of players such as [[罗纳德·德波尔|罗纳德·德波尔]] and [[巴利·費格遜|巴利·費格遜]].<ref name="Treble win">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/scot_div_1/2946904.stm |title=Rangers complete Treble |date=31 May 2003 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> 

Rangers' worsening financial state saw many of his top players leave in the summer of 2003. [[Celtic_F.C.|Celtic]] won the league comfortably in [[2003–04_in_Scottish_football|season 2003–04]], and Rangers failed to win any trophies.<ref name="Rangers Bio">{{cite web |url=http://www.rangers.premiumtv.co.uk/page/formermanagers/0,,5~1555142,00.html |title=Past Managers – Alex McLeish |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[Rangers_F.C|Rangers F.C]] }}{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> McLeish was consequently put under pressure from fans after his poor signings and a record run of seven consecutive derby losses to Celtic.<ref name="Bosses talk up Old Firm meeting">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/scot_div_1/3997583.stm |title=Bosses talk up Old Firm meeting |date=9 November 2004 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref>

The high profile [[博斯曼法案|波士文法案]] signings of [[让-阿兰·布姆松|让-阿兰·布姆松]] and [[达多·普尔绍|达多·普尔绍]] in the close season of [[2004–05_in_Scottish_football|2004–05]] gave Rangers renewed hope of regaining the title from Celtic's grasp.<ref name="Prso capture sees McLeish celebrate">{{cite news |url=http://sport.scotsman.com/top-stories/Prso-capture-sees-McLeish-celebrate.2527597.jp |title=Prso capture sees McLeish celebrate |date=10 May 2004 |accessdate=25 March 2010 |work=[[The_Scotsman|The Scotsman]] |first=Angus |last=Wright}}</ref> McLeish's team won the 2005 league title on a dramatic last day, an outcome that had looked highly unlikely after Rangers fell five points behind leaders Celtic with just four games remaining.<ref name="Rangers in dramatic title triumph">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/scot_prem/4570627.stm |title=Rangers in dramatic title triumph |date=22 May 2005 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref>

After this unexpected success, McLeish and his Rangers team headed into the [[2005年至2006年蘇格蘭足球超級聯賽|2005/06年球季]] SPL campaign as favourites to retain the championship. McLeish made a number of signings, including [[朱利安·罗德里格斯|朱利安·罗德里格斯]]（Julien Rodriguez） and [[伊恩·穆雷|伊恩·穆雷]]（Ian Murray）, despite having little money to spend. After a reasonable start to the season, including a win over Celtic,<ref name="Rangers 3-1 Celtic">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/scot_prem/4164472.stm |title=Rangers 3–1 Celtic |date=20 October 2005 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> Rangers suffered a series of poor results between September and November. This period included a club record of 10 games without a win. However the tenth match of this run, a 1–1 draw with [[国际米兰足球俱乐部|國際米蘭]] in the [[欧洲冠军联赛|欧洲冠军联赛]], took Rangers into the knockout stages of the tournament for the first time.<ref name="Rangers 1-1 Inter Milan">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/europe/4494912.stm |title=Rangers 1–1 Inter Milan |date=6 December 2005 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref>

Despite the poor domestic form, McLeish guided Rangers to the last 16 of the Champions League, where they were defeated on the [[作客入球優惠制|作客入球優惠制]] by [[比利亞雷阿爾足球俱樂部|比利亞雷阿爾]].<ref name="Villarreal 1-1 Rangers">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/europe/4778246.stm |title=Villarreal 1–1 Rangers (agg 3–3) |date=7 March 2006 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> They became the first Scottish team to progress this far in the [[UEFA_Champions_League|European Cup]] since 1993, and the first Scottish team to progress through a European group stage.<ref name="Rangers 1-1 Inter Milan"/> In December, chairman [[David_Murray_(Scottish_businessman)|David Murray]] publicly announced his support for McLeish.<ref name="McLeish receives Murray's backing">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/r/rangers/4503564.stm |title=McLeish receives Murray's backing |date=8 February 2005 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> Rangers then went on a good run of results in December and January. 

This run of good results came to a sudden halt when they were defeated 3–0 by Hibernian in the Scottish Cup,<ref name="Rangers 0-3 Hibernian">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/scot_cups/4676068.stm |title=Rangers 0–3 Hibernian |date=4 February 2006 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> prompting protests outside Ibrox against both McLeish and David Murray.<ref name="Fans expect more Ibrox protests">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/r/rangers/4685366.stm |title=Fans expect more Ibrox protests |date=6 February 2006 |accessdate=16 October 2007|publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> On 9 February 2006, it was announced by chairman David Murray that McLeish would be standing down as manager at the end of that season.<ref name="McLeish to leave Rangers in May">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/r/rangers/4697444.stm |title=McLeish to leave Rangers in May |date=9 February 2006 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> 

It was later announced that he would be succeeded by former [[奥林匹克里昂|里昂]] manager [[保罗·勒冈|保罗·勒冈]]（Paul Le Guen）. Rangers beat [[哈茨足球俱乐部|赫斯]] 2–0 at [[埃布羅克斯球場|埃布羅克斯球場]] in his final match as manager.<ref name="Rangers 2-0 Hearts">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/scot_prem/4976560.stm |title=Rangers 2–0 Hearts |date=7 May 2006 |accessdate=16 October 2007|publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref>

McLeish said after leaving Rangers that he would not manage another Scottish club, because he felt that he had achieved everything in the Scottish game.<ref name="'I'm a Rangers man - I couldn't manage another Scottish club'">{{cite news |url=http://sport.scotsman.com/rangersfc/Im-a-Rangers-man-.2750134.jp |title='I'm a Rangers man – I couldn't manage another Scottish club' |first=Glenn |last=Gibbons |date=11 February 2006 |accessdate=25 March 2010 |newspaper=[[The_Scotsman|The Scotsman]]}}</ref> He was linked in the media with a number of managerial positions in England while he worked as a television pundit for the [[英国广播公司|英国广播公司]] and [[塞坦塔體育頻道|塞坦塔體育頻道]]（Setanta Sports）.

McLeish took charge of the [[蘇格蘭足球代表隊|蘇格蘭國家隊]] on 29 January 2007.<ref name="McLeish unveiled as Scotland boss">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6289073.stm |title=McLeish unveiled as Scotland boss |date=29 January 2007 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> His assistants in the job were [[羅伊·艾特肯|羅伊·艾特肯]]（Roy Aitken） and [[安迪·沃森|安迪·沃森]]（Andy Watson）.

McLeish's first game in charge of the national team was a [[2008年欧洲足球锦标赛外围赛|2008年欧洲足球锦标赛外围赛]] match, a 2–1 victory against [[格魯吉亞國家足球隊|格魯吉亞]] on 24 March 2007 at [[咸普頓公園球場|咸普頓公園球場]].<ref name="Scotland 2-1 Georgia">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6449543.stm |title=Scotland 2–1 Georgia |first=Julian |last=Taylor |date=24 March 2007 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071022213733/http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6449543.stm |archivedate=2007年10月22日 |deadurl=no }}</ref> His second game was an away fixture against [[意大利國家足球隊|意大利]] on 28 March 2007 which ended in a 2–0 defeat.<ref name="Italy 2-0 Scotland">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6495949.stm |title=Italy 2–0 Scotland |first=Colin |last=Moffat |date=28 March 2007 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071012183043/http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6495949.stm |archivedate=2007年10月12日 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

McLeish's Scotland side then went on to defeat the [[法羅群島足球代表隊|法羅群島]] away in June,<ref name="Faroe Islands 0-2 Scotland">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6713997.stm |title=Faroe Islands 0–2 Scotland |first=Julian |last=Taylor |date=6 June 2007 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071120045635/http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6713997.stm |archivedate=2007年11月20日 |deadurl=no }}</ref> [[立陶宛國家足球隊|立陶宛]] at home in September<ref name="Scotland 3-1 Lithuania">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6981599.stm |title=Scotland 3–1 Lithuania |first=Colin |last=Moffat |date=8 September 2007 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071119000413/http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6981599.stm |archivedate=2007年11月19日 |deadurl=no }}</ref> before recording a historic victory in [[巴黎|巴黎]] four days later by defeating [[法國國家足球隊|法國]] 1–0 in the [[王子公园体育场|王子公园体育场]].<ref name="France 0-1 Scotland">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/internationals/6986781.stm |title=France 0–1 Scotland |first=Thomas |last=McGuigan |date=12 September 2007 |accessdate=16 October 2007 |publisher=[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]}}</ref> [[詹姆斯·麦克法登|詹姆斯·麦克法登]]（James McFadden）'s 64th-minute strike from 30 yards was enough to earn Scotland the win and returned them to the top of [[UEFA_Euro_2008_qualifying_Group_B|Group B]] with three games to play. 麥利殊於2012年12月27日就任[[英冠|英冠]]球會[[诺丁汉森林足球俱乐部|諾定咸森林]]新領隊<ref>{{cite web |language=en |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/20855227 |title=Nottingham Forest name Alex McLeish as new manager |work=''[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]'' |date=27 December 2012 |accessdate=27 December 2012}}</ref>。

2013年2月5日，麥利殊上任領隊40天後因成績欠佳，與經雙方同意，離職諾定咸森林。在任期間比賽7場(6場聯賽、1場[[英格蘭足總盃|足總盃]])，只取得1勝2和4負成績<ref>{{cite web |language=en |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/21338533 |title=Alex McLeish: Nottingham Forest manager exits City Ground |work=''[[BBC_Sport|BBC Sport]]'' |date=5 February 2013 |accessdate=2 June 2013}}</ref>。

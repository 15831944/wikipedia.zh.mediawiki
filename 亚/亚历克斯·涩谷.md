'''亚历克斯·涩谷'''（{{lang-en|Alex Hideo Shibutani}}，{{bd|1991年|4月25日||}}）生于马萨诸塞州波士顿，是一名美国男子花样滑冰运动员，主攻冰舞项目<ref name=ISU-1314>{{cite web |url= http://www.isuresults.com/bios/isufs00011168.htm |title= Maia SHIBUTANI / Alex SHIBUTANI: 2013/2014 |publisher= International Skating Union |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140623025350/http://www.isuresults.com/bios/isufs00011168.htm |accessdate=2019-01-26 |archivedate= 2014-06-23 |deadurl= unfit }}</ref>。他的搭档是妹妹[[迈娅·涩谷|迈娅·涩谷]]。他的父母都有[[日本|日本]]血统<ref name=fambusiness>{{cite news |url= http://espn.go.com/espnw/more-sports/7116870/alex-maia-shibutani-make-ice-dancing-family-business |title= Shibutanis make ice dancing the family business |first= Meri-Jo |last= Borzilleri |work= ESPN |date= 2011-10-18 |accessdate= 2011-10-18 |archiveurl= https://archive.today/20130124043701/http://espn.go.com/espnw/more-sports/7116870/alex-maia-shibutani-make-ice-dancing-family-business |archivedate= 2013-01-24 |dead-url= no }}</ref>。迈娅和亚历克斯在1998年开始练习花样滑冰<ref name=fambusiness/><ref name=ISU-1617>{{cite web |url= http://www.isuresults.com/bios/isufs00011168.htm |title= Maia SHIBUTANI / Alex SHIBUTANI: 2016/2017 |publisher= International Skating Union |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20170630211437/http://www.isuresults.com/bios/isufs00011168.htm |accessdate=2019-01-26 |archivedate=2017-06-30 |deadurl= unfit }}</ref>。两人在2004年春开始搭档<ref name=fambusiness/><ref name=GS100620>{{cite news |url= http://www.goldenskate.com/2010/06/shibutanis-make-move-to-the-big-leagues/ |title= Shibutanis make move to the big leagues |first= Elvin |last= Walker |work= GoldenSkate.com |date= 2010-06-20 |accessdate= 2011-03-28 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120311210957/http://www.goldenskate.com/2010/06/shibutanis-make-move-to-the-big-leagues/|archivedate=2012-03-11}}</ref>。

'''亚历克·波茨'''（{{lang-en|Alec Potts}}，{{bd|1996年|2月19日||}}）生于维多利亚州克莱顿，是一名澳大利亚男子射箭运动员。波茨2008年时开始练习射箭，2011年首次参加国际比赛，2015年首次参加成人比赛。<ref name="olympics.com.au"/>他在2016年入选了里约奥运澳大利亚射箭队<ref>{{cite news|title=Archery Australia's men's team for Rio revealed|url=https://worldarchery.org/news/140817/archery-australias-mens-team-rio-revealed|publisher=World Archery|date=2016-05-31|accessdate=2018-04-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Archery men targeting team podium|url=http://corporate.olympics.com.au/dmNews/8570020B-3A4D-4044-9ACFAFEE05FCCA57|publisher=Australian Olympic Committee|date=2016-05-31|accessdate=2018-04-01|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180401212841/http://corporate.olympics.com.au/dmNews/8570020B-3A4D-4044-9ACFAFEE05FCCA57|archivedate=2018-04-01}}</ref>。他在奥运期间联同另外两名队友[[瑞安·泰亚克|瑞安·泰亚克]]和[[泰勒·沃思|泰勒·沃思]]在男子团体项目中获得铜牌<ref>{{cite news|last=Webb|first=Carolyn|title=Rio Olympics 2016: Archer Alec Potts' family elated with bronze medal|url=https://www.smh.com.au/sport/rio-olympics-2016-archer-alec-potts-family-elated-with-bronze-medal-20160807-gqmylg.html|publisher=[[悉尼先驱晨报|Sydney Morning Herald]]|date=2016-08-07|accessdate=2018-04-01}}</ref>。十几天后，他和另外几名澳大利亚代表团的选手因擅自进入篮球赛场观看奥运男子篮球半决赛澳大利亚对阵塞尔维亚的比赛而被巴西警方逮捕<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.insidethegames.biz/articles/1041091/investigation-alleges-80-more-australian-athletes-gained-access-to-olympic-basketball-semi-final|title=Investigation alleges 80 more Australian athletes gained access to Olympic basketball semi-final|publisher=Inside the Games|date=2016-08-29|accessdate=2018-12-25}}</ref>。

[[File:Jáchym_Topol_s_knihou_2010-09-22_b.jpg|thumb]]'''亚希姆·托波尔'''（Jáchym Topol，出生于1962年8月4日），捷克作家。1970年代末和80年代初，他开始为由他的弟弟菲利普领导的摇滚乐团Psí vojáci写歌词。 1982年，他与人共同创办了杂志Violit。他还是七七宪章的签署者之一。

== 作品 ==
=== 诗歌 ===

* ''Miluju tě k zbláznění'' (I love you madly; samizdat, 1988) <br />/ Topol's first collection of poetry, published in samizdat, received the Tom Stoppard Prize for Unofficial Literature (founded in 1983 by [[Tom_Stoppard|Tom Stoppard]] and awarded by the [[Charta_77|Charta 77]] Foundation in Stockholm); first non-samizdat edition published by Atlantis in 1990.

* ''V úterý bude válka'' (The war will be on Tuesday; Edice 13x18, 1992) <br />/ Five poems from this collection were published in [[Alex_Zucker|Alex Zucker]]'s translation in the Spring 1994 issue of ''Trafika: an international literary review''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.think.cz/issue/50/1.html |title=Alexander Zaitchik: "Let the Kazoos Sound: A Decade of English Press in Prague," '&#39;Think'&#39; magazine, no. 50, Nov/Dec 2001 |publisher=Think.cz |date=11 October 2009 |accessdate=24 June 2011}}</ref>

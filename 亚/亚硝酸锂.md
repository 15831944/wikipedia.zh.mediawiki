'''亚硝酸锂'''是[[锂|锂]]的[[亚硝酸盐|亚硝酸盐]]，化学式为LiNO<sub>2</sub>。该化合物具有潮解性，易溶于水，可用于[[砂浆|砂浆]]的{{le|阻腐剂|corrosion inhibitor}}<ref>[http://www.sciencedirect.com/science?_ob=ArticleURL&_udi=B6V2G-4JJ849F-1&_user=10&_coverDate=01%2F31%2F2007&_rdoc=1&_fmt=%20&_orig=search&_sort=d&view=c&_acct=C000050221&_version=1&_urlVersion=0&_userid=10&md5=5fcb55984ff51a5b73f52aa1d2c3f934 "Evaluation on the effect of lithium nitrite corrosion inhibitor by the corrosion sensors embedded in mortar"]</ref>，也能够用于[[炸药|炸药]]的制造，这是源于它对[[酮|酮]]的[[亚硝基化合物|亚硝化]]的能力。<ref>[http://article.pubs.nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/ppv/RPViewDoc?issn=1480-3291&volume=52&issue=9&startPage=1760 Nitrosation with Lithium Nitrite]{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

==制备==
亚硝酸锂可由[[硝酸锂|硝酸锂]]在500℃时的热分解制备，反应放出氧气：<ref name="Greenwood"> Greenwood, N. N. and Earnshaw, A. ''Chemistry of the Elements'', 2nd ed.; Reed Educational and Professional Publishing Ltd: Oxford, 1997.</ref>

:2LiNO<sub>3</sub> → 2LiNO<sub>2</sub> + O<sub>2</sub> (~500 °C)

它也能通过[[一氧化氮|一氧化氮]]和[[氢氧化锂|氢氧化锂]]的反应得到：<ref name="Greenwood" />

:4NO + 2LiOH → 2LiNO<sub>2</sub> + N<sub>2</sub>O + H<sub>2</sub>O 

:6NO + 4LiOH → 4LiNO<sub>2</sub> + N<sub>2</sub> + 2H<sub>2</sub>O

'''亚洲公路46号线'''（[[英语|英语]]：{{lang|en|'''Asian Highway 46'''}}），编号{{Asian Highway nopic|46}}，是[[亚洲公路网|亚洲公路网]]系統中的一条全线均位于[[印度|印度]]的道路<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.roadtraffic-technology.com/projects/asian-highway-network/ |title=Asian Highway Network, Great Asian Highway |publisher=Road Traffic |language=en |access-date=2018-11-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180324000319/http://www.roadtraffic-technology.com/projects/asian-highway-network |archive-date=2018-03-24 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.unescap.org/our-work/transport/asian-highway/database|title=United Nations - ESCAP - Asian Highway Database|last=|first=|date=|publisher=[[联合国亚洲及太平洋经济社会委员会|联合国亚洲及太平洋经济社会委员会]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180913011325/https://www.unescap.org/our-work/transport/asian-highway/database|archive-date=2018-09-13|dead-url=no|access-date=2018-11-01|language=en}}</ref>。其线路全长约为{{convert|1514|km}}，部分路段与[[国道6号_(印度)|（旧）国道6号]]、{{link-en|国道49号 (印度)|National Highway 49 (India)|国道49号}}和{{link-en|国道53号 (印度)|National Highway 53 (India)|国道53号}}共线<ref name=2011r>{{Cite web|url=http://www.egazette.nic.in/WriteReadData/2011/E_574_2012_016.pdf|title=New National Highways notification - Government of India|last=|first=|date=|website=The Gazette of India|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180504155047/http://www.egazette.nic.in/WriteReadData/2011/E_574_2012_016.pdf|archive-date=2018-05-04|dead-url=no|access-date=2018-11-01|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://morth.nic.in/showfile.asp?lid=365 |title=Number wise National Highways |access-date=2018-11-01 |language=en |publisher=印度公路运输和公路部 |archive-url=https://archive.is/20121022113344/http://www.morth.nic.in/showfile.asp?lid=365 |archive-date=2012-10-22 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。线路起点位于印度[[西孟加拉邦|西孟加拉邦]][[克勒格布尔|克勒格布尔]]，中途与[[亚洲公路43号线|亚洲公路43号线]]、[[亚洲公路45号线|亚洲公路45号线]]和[[亚洲公路47号线|亚洲公路47号线]]连接，终点位于印度[[马哈拉施特拉邦|马哈拉施特拉邦]][[图莱|图莱]]<ref name=":0" /><ref name="handbook">{{Cite web|publisher=Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific |url=http://www.unescap.org/ttdw/Publications/TIS_pubs/pub_2303/Full%20version.pdf |title=2003 Asian Highway Handbook |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120414184019/http://www.unescap.org/ttdw/Publications/TIS_pubs/pub_2303/Full%20version.pdf |archivedate=2012-04-14 |date=2003 |pages=41 |accessdate=2018-11-01|language=en }}</ref>。

== 相接道路 ==
亚洲公路46号线中途与[[亚洲公路43号线|亚洲公路43号线]]、[[亚洲公路45号线|亚洲公路45号线]]和[[亚洲公路47号线|亚洲公路47号线]]连接。其中与43号线在那格浦尔附近交汇；与45号线在克勒格布尔附近交汇；与47号线在图莱附近交汇。<ref name="handbook"/>

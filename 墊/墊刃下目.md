'''墊刃下目'''（學名：{{lang|la|Tylenchomorpha}}）是[[小桿目|小桿目]]之下的一個下目級分類單元{{r|WoRMS_834834}}。

==分類==
墊刃下目原為[[胞管腎綱|胞管腎綱]]之下的[[墊刃目|墊刃目]]，現時整個目都連隨降兩級。

{{asof|2018|1|26}}在[[WoRMS|WoRMS]]的紀錄，墊刃下目由下列五個總科組成{{r|WoRMS_834834}}：
* [[滑刃總科|滑刃總科]] [[Aphelenchoidea|Aphelenchoidea]] {{small|Fuchs,1937}}
* [[Criconematoidea|Criconematoidea]] {{small|Taylor, 1936}}
** Family  [[Criconematidae|Criconematidae]]   
** Family  [[Tylenchulidae|Tylenchulidae]]
* [[Myenchoidea|Myenchoidea]] {{small|Pereira, 1931}}
* [[墊刃總科|墊刃總科]] [[Tylenchoidea|Tylenchoidea]] {{small|Örley, 1880}}
** Family  [[Anguinidae|Anguinidae]]     
** Family  [[Belonolaimidae|Belonolaimidae]]
** Family  [[Dolichodoridae|Dolichodoridae]]
** Family  [[Ecphyadophoridae|Ecphyadophoridae]] 
** Family  [[Hoplolaimidae|Hoplolaimidae]]     
** Family  [[Heteroderidae|Heteroderidae]]

* [[Sphaerularioidea|Sphaerularioidea]] {{small|Lubbock, 1861}}

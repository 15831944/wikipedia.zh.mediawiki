'''魯斯塔維2'''（{{lang-ka| სამაუწყებლო კომპანია რუსთავი 2}}）是[[喬治亞|喬治亞]]的一家電視台，也是喬治亞最為成功的商業電視台。魯斯塔維2成立於1994年，因設立於魯斯塔維而得名<ref>http://www.civil.ge/eng/article.php?id=19352</ref><ref>http://www.eurasianet.org/departments/insight/articles/eav032608b.shtml</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.geotimes.ge/index.php?m=home&newsid=1534 |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2008-10-05 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110128212151/http://geotimes.ge/index.php?m=home&newsid=1534 |archivedate=2011-01-28 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ejc.net/media_landscape/article/georgia |title=Georgian Government Controls Six TV Stations |publisher=HumanRights.ge |date=2008-03-24 |accessdate=2015-08-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=http://arquivo.pt/wayback/20091013021950/http%3A//www.ejc.net/media_landscape/article/georgia/ |archivedate=2009-10-13 }}</ref><ref>http://www.humanrights.ge/index.php?a=article&id=2523&lang=en</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.jamestown.org/edm/email-to-friend.php?article_id=2368328 |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2008-10-05 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061020204538/http://www.jamestown.org/edm/email-to-friend.php?article_id=2368328 |archivedate=2006-10-20 }}</ref>。魯斯塔維2在2003年玫瑰革命中起到了重要作用。

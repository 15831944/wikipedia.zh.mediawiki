'''谢丽尔·丹尼丝·斯伍普斯'''（{{lang-en|'''Sheryl Denise Swoopes'''}}，{{bd|1971年|3月25日|}}）<ref name="NCAA Coaches">{{cite web|last1=|first1=|title=Women's Basketball Coaches Career|date=|url=http://web1.ncaa.org/stats/StatsSrv/careersearch|website=NCAA|accessdate=September 29, 2015}}</ref>，美国前职业[[篮球|篮球]]运动员。她曾三次获得WNBA最有价值球员，并在2011年WNBA全明星赛中被评为WNBA历史15大球员之一。她于2016年入选[[奈史密斯篮球名人纪念堂|奈史密斯篮球名人堂]]，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.hoophall.com/news/2016/4/4/naismith-memorial-basketball-hall-of-fame-class-of-2016-anno.html|title=Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame Class of 2016 Announcement presented by Haggar Clothing Company|work=Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame|date=April 4, 2016|accessdate=April 4, 2016|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160417204345/http://www.hoophall.com/news/2016/4/4/naismith-memorial-basketball-hall-of-fame-class-of-2016-anno.html|archivedate=April 17, 2016}}</ref>2017年入选女子篮球名人堂。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.knoxnews.com/story/sports/local/2017/06/09/sheryl-swoopes-enjoying-her-hall-fame-moment/381548001/|title=Sheryl Swoopes enjoying her Hall of Fame moment|author=Fleser|first=Dan|date=June 10, 2017|work=[[今日美國|USA Today]]|accessdate=June 11, 2017}}</ref>

'''侏儒盧安達睡蓮'''是世上最細小的睡蓮品種，葉長只有約一釐米。由[[德國|德國]]植物學家Eberhard Fischer 發現。原只生長於非洲[[盧旺達|盧旺達]]Mashyuza的一個[[溫泉|溫泉]]旁，但當地農夫截流導致棲地乾涸而完全消失，繼而於2008年起野外絕種。幸專家在物種在野外滅絕前及時將活樣本送至[[波恩大學|波恩大學]]植物園栽培，惟未能令種子發芽<ref name="Independent">{{cite news |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/environment/nature/smallest-lily-saved-from-extinction-1976449.html |title=Smallest lily saved from extinction |last=McCarthy |first=Michael |date=2010-05-19 |work=[[The_Independent|The Independent]] |location=London |accessdate=19 May 2010}}</ref>。直至2009年英國皇家植物園發現種子發芽條件異於其他睡蓮—需在含[[二氧化碳|二氧化碳]]之[[壤土|壤土]]和淺水中發芽<ref name="BBC">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/science_and_environment/10124250.stm|title=Waterlily saved from extinction|last=Ghosh|first=Pallab|date=2010-05-18|work=BBC News|accessdate=19 May 2010}}</ref><ref name="WGI">{{cite journal|last=Magdalena|first=Carlos|date=November 2009|title=The world's tiniest waterlily doesn't grow in water! |journal=Water Gardeners International |volume=4 |issue=4 |url=http://www.watergardenersinternational.org/journal/4-4/carlos/thermarum_gallery1.html |accessdate=2010-05-19}}</ref>，此外，加上攝氏25度的恆溫，種子才發芽，並在2009年首次開花<ref>[http://www.australiangeographic.com.au/journal/extinct-waterlily-back-from-the-dead.htm/ "'Extinct' Waterlily back from the dead"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110716104717/http://www.australiangeographic.com.au/journal/extinct-waterlily-back-from-the-dead.htm/ |date=2011-07-16 }}, ''[[Australian_Geographic|Australian Geographic]]'', May 21, 2010.</ref>，於是得以避過絕種一劫。現時本種的活體皆培植於[[邱園|邱園]]（英國皇家植物園）。

本種花瓣為白色，雄蕊黃色。花萼和花瓣一樣大小，帶絨毛<ref name="Kew">{{cite web|url=http://www.kew.org/plants-fungi/Nymphaea-thermarum.htm|title=Nymphaea thermarum|last=Magdalena|first=Carlos|work=Plants & Fungi|publisher=Kew Gardens|accessdate=19 May 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100523083311/http://www.kew.org/plants-fungi/Nymphaea-thermarum.htm|archive-date=2010-05-23|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。花為[[順序性雌雄同體|順序性雌雄同體]]，即花在第一日早上至下午開時為雌性，並在下午開始閉合，接著第二日開花時便成為雄性<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Povilus|first1=RA|last2=Losada|first2=JM|last3=Friedman|first3=WE|title=Floral biology and ovule and seed ontogeny of Nymphaea thermarum, a water lily at the brink of extinction with potential as a model system for basal angiosperms|journal=Annals of Botany|date=2015|volume=115|pages=211–226|doi=10.1093/aob/mcu235|pmid=25497514|pmc=4551091}}</ref>。

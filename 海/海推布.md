在[[伊斯蘭教|伊斯蘭教]]中，'''海推布'''（{{lang-ar|خطيب}} ''khaṭīb''）是[[主麻日|主麻日]]禮拜時在[[清真寺|清真寺]]裡演講（即[[呼圖白|呼圖白]]）的神職人員<ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?hl=en&lr=&id=MKBCEaZHW-cC&oi=fnd&pg=PR9|title=The Ethical Soundscape|work=google.com|accessdate=19 January 2015}}</ref>。海推布一職常由[[伊瑪目|伊瑪目]]兼任，但其實兩職務可由不同人擔任，任何嫻熟伊斯蘭教教義的成人都有資格擔任海推布<ref name=Oxford>{{cite web|url=http://www.oxfordislamicstudies.com/article/opr/t125/e1278|title=Khatib|publisher=Oxford Islam Studies Online|access-date=2018-08-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180829072921/http://www.oxfordislamicstudies.com/article/opr/t125/e1278#|archive-date=2018-08-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>。在[[阿拔斯帝國|阿拔斯帝國]]時期，海推布一職即是由帝國派任的，現代此職亦常是由政府任命管理<ref name=Oxford/>。絕大多數海推布是由男性擔任，但少數情況下女性亦可擔任此職（{{lang|ar|خطيبة}} khaṭībah），2015年，{{link-en|美國婦女清真寺|Women's Mosque of America}}的開幕儀式中，即是由女性穆斯林{{link-en|伊代纳·莱科维奇|Edina Lekovic}}擔任海推布<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://america.aljazeera.com/articles/2015/2/3/first-all-female-mosque-opens-in-los-angeles.html|title=First all-female mosque opens in Los Angeles|last=Street|first=Nick|date=3 February 2015|work=Al-Jazeera|access-date=2017-06-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160923160448/http://america.aljazeera.com/articles/2015/2/3/first-all-female-mosque-opens-in-los-angeles.html#|archive-date=2016-09-23|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.altmuslimah.com/2015/06/how-i-became-the-womens-mosque-of-americas-first-khateebah/|title=How I became The Women’s Mosque of America’s first khateebah - altM|last=Lekovic|first=Edina|date=2015-06-26|work=altM|access-date=2017-06-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180220082357/http://www.altmuslimah.com/2015/06/how-i-became-the-womens-mosque-of-americas-first-khateebah/#|archive-date=2018-02-20|dead-url=no|language=en-US}}</ref>。

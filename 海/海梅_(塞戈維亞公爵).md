'''塞戈維亞及安茹公爵海梅'''，（Infante Jaime of Spain, Duke of Segovia, Duke of Anjou,；{{bd|1908年|6月23日|1975年|3月20日}}），本名'''海梅·利奧波多·伊薩貝里諾·恩里克·亞歷山德羅·阿爾貝托·阿方索·維克多·阿卡基奧·佩德羅·帕布羅·瑪利亞·德·波旁-巴滕堡'''（{{lang-es|'''Jaime Leopoldo Isabelino Enrique Alejandro Alberto Alfonso Víctor Acacio Pedro Pablo María de Borbón y Battenberg'''}}），是[[波旁王朝|波旁王朝]]的王室成員，及{{link-en|正統派 (法國)|Legitimists|法國正統派}}承認的法國國王，稱'''亨利六世'''（'''Henri VI'''）。

==出生和家世==
海梅是聾人<ref>{{cite journal | title = ROYALTY: A Wonderful Woman | journal = Time | date = 2 January 1950| id = | url = http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,780178,00.html | accessdate = 2011-05-25 | quote = Spain's 41-year-old Don Jaime de Bourbon, Duke of Segovia, born deaf & dumb, has always led the quiet life, devoted himself intently to lip reading and to learning to talk which he now does, blurrily but intelligibly, in three languages. The elder of the late King Alfonso's two living sons, he gave up his rights to the Spanish throne 16 years ago in favor of his healthy younger brother Juan, and set off in pursuit of the happiness that has traditionally eluded his hemophilic Bourbon family.}}</ref>，並在1933年6月21日和兄长[[阿方索，阿斯图里亚斯亲王_(1907–1938)|阿方索]]放棄本身和後代的西班牙王位繼承權<ref>{{cite book | last1 = Preston | first1 = Paul | title = Juan Carlos: Steering Spain From Dictatorship To Democracy | publisher = W. W. Norton & Company | year = 2004 | location = New York, NY | pages = 4 | url = http://books.google.com/books?id=KWPBvZEHstMC&lpg=PA1&dq=Marriage%20of%20Don%20Jaime%201935&pg=PA4#v=onepage&q=Marriage%20of%20Don%20Jaime%201935&f=false | accessdate = 2011-05-25 | isbn = 978-0393058048 | quote = In Fontainebleau, 21 June 1933, Don Jaime, who was single at the time, finally agreed to renounce his rights to the throne, as well as those of his future heirs. The renunciation was irrevocable and would be ratified on 23 July 1945.}}</ref>，因此父親阿方索十三世為他創立了一個新的爵位——塞哥維亞公爵。<ref>{{cite book | last1 = Montague-Smith, ed. | first1 = Patrick | title = Debrett's Peerage, Baronetage, Knightage, and Companionage | publisher = Kelly's Directories | year = 1963 | location = London | pages = 10 | accessdate = 2011-05-25 | quote = Assumed the title Duke of Segovia}}</ref>

在他的父親去世以後，他自稱是{{link-en|正統派 (法國)|Legitimists|法國正統派}}王位的繼承人，並使用[[安茹公爵|安茹公爵]]頭銜。

'''海洋城市博物馆'''（{{lang-en|SeaCity Museum}}）是一座位于[[英国|英国]][[南安普敦|南安普敦]]的博物馆，于2012年4月12日开放，以纪念100年前[[泰坦尼克号|泰坦尼克号]]从这里出发。馆址位于[[登錄建築|二级登录建筑]]{{tsl|en|Civic Centre, Southampton|南安普顿市政中心}}大楼以内，本来是[[裁判法院_(英格蘭及威爾斯)|裁决法院]]与[[警察局|警察局]]的所在地。博物馆设有两个永久展览厅，一处用于展示南安普顿与泰坦尼克号的渊源，另一处则用于展示城市充当世界门户的角色。第三处临时展览厅位于市政中心的专用展馆内，将来可能还会分期进行扩建工程。<ref name=BuildingProjects>{{cite journal| title=The Icebergs are all in the Past| author=Steve Menary| journal=Building Projects| publisher=netMAGmedia| date=January 2012 | pages=43–47| url=http://content.yudu.com/Library/A1vk1d/BuildingProjectsJanu/resources/index.htm| accessdate=2012-04-21}}</ref>

该博物馆由{{tsl|en|Kier Group|基尔南方公司}}承建，并由{{tsl|en|Wilkinson Eyre|威尔金森·艾尔}}担任设计师。工程造价为1500万英镑，其中{{tsl|en|Heritage Lottery Fund|文化遗产博彩基金}}拨出大约500万英镑，其余由南安普顿市议会以及南安普敦文化发展信托基金会承担。<ref name=BuildingProjects/>市议会曾计划出售市政艺术收藏品，来为博物馆筹募资金，但因遭到各方强烈反对而被搁置。<ref name=EchoArtUTurn>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/politics/5015621.Art_sell_off_U_turn_by_council/| title=Southampton City Council back track on art sell off plans for museum funds| author=Matt Smith| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2010-02-18 | accessdate=2012-05-06}}</ref>

==背景==
南安普顿市议会在2002年开始筹建文化遗产中心，并组建了遗产工作组，以研讨各种方案，规划大纲与进程表。<ref name=SCCFirstStepsPR>{{cite press release| url=http://www.southampton.gov.uk/news-events/pressoffice/NewsItem.asp?ID=924&M=72002| title=First Steps to 'Story of Southampton' Centre| publisher={{tsl|en|Southampton City Council||Southampton City Council}}| date=2002-07-08| accessdate=2012-04-24| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=http://webarchive.nationalarchives.gov.uk/20111104071604/https%3A//www.southampton.gov.uk/news%2Devents/pressoffice/NewsItem.asp?ID%3D924%26M%3D72002| archivedate=2011-11-04}}</ref>2004年，以下几处被列入候选地点，当时所定的名称尚为“南安普敦故事”文化遗产中心：<ref name=SCCSitesPR>{{cite press release| url=http://www.southampton.gov.uk/news-events/pressoffice/NewsItem.asp?ID=1951&M=52004| title='Story of Southampton' a Step Closer| publisher={{tsl|en|Southampton City Council||Southampton City Council}}| date=2004-05-24| accessdate=2012-04-24| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131005002511/http://www.southampton.gov.uk/news-events/pressoffice/NewsItem.asp?ID=1951&M=52004| archivedate=2013-10-05}}</ref>

*{{tsl|en|Woolston, Southampton|伍尔斯顿 (南安普顿)|伍尔斯顿}}前{{tsl|en|VT Group||VT集团}}造船厂，位置：{{coord|50.895617|-1.382861|region:GB_type:landmark_scale:20000|name=前VT集团造船厂}}
但是在名单列出的地点兴建博物馆，花费高昂之余还不切实际。2006年，{{tsl|en|Hampshire Constabulary|南安普顿警方}}宣布，由于空间不足，他们打算将警察总部迁离市政中心。<ref name=SCCScutinyPanel>{{cite web| url=http://www.southampton.gov.uk/moderngov/Data/Economic%20Development,%20Leaders%20and%20Leisure%20Scrutiny%20Panel/20080325/Agenda/ShowDocumentaspPKID7621.pdf| title=Item No. 19 – Proposed New Heritage Centre| work=Meeting of Economic Development, Leaders and Leisure Scrutiny Panel| publisher={{tsl|en|Southampton City Council||Southampton City Council}}| date=2008-03-25 | accessdate=2012-04-24}}</ref>加之地方法院于2001年迁出大楼，整个市政中心已经空置。因此，市政中心被纳入候选，到了2007年，更成为方案中的领跑者。<ref name=EchoFeasibility>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/1085783.heritage_centre_plan_for_old_magistrates_court/| title=Heritage centre plan for old magistrates court| author=David Newble| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2006-02-21 | accessdate= 2012-04-24}}</ref><ref name=EchoShortlistKill>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/1200352.Could_a_deal_at_last_be_on_the_cards_for_a_city_arts_complex_/| title=Could a deal at last be on the cards for a city arts complex?| author=Matt Smith| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2007-02-17 | accessdate=2012-04-27}}</ref>

该方案于2008年正式亮相。原计划中打算将市政中心扩建为三层，并从附近的[[以撒·華茲|华兹]]公园引流打造水景。然而这两项计划皆遭抛弃，这样一来预算减少了1000万英镑，从而压缩至2800万英镑。
<ref name=EchoPlanUnveil>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/1998163.civic_centre_plans_unveiled/| title=Civic centre plans unveiled| author=Peter Law| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2008-01-28 | accessdate= 2012-04-24}}</ref><ref name=EchoBuildingScaleBack>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/heritage/titanic/news/2409358.Titanic_vision_for___30m_Museum/| title=Titanic vision for £30m Museum| author=Peter Law| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2008-07-25 | accessdate= 2012-04-27}}</ref>整个项目分为两个阶段。一期工程着力展现南安普顿与泰坦尼克号之间的关联。该阶段预算为1500万英镑，并于2012年完工。<ref name=SCCFundingPR>{{cite press release| url=http://www.southampton.gov.uk/news-events/pressoffice/NewsItem.asp?ID=3819&M=32009| title=Titanic Sea City Museum plans gather steam| publisher={{tsl|en|Southampton City Council||Southampton City Council}}| date=2009-03-31| accessdate=2012-04-27| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131005000258/http://www.southampton.gov.uk/news-events/pressoffice/NewsItem.asp?ID=3819&M=32009| archivedate=2013-10-05}}</ref>

==募资==
南安普顿市议会向{{tsl|en|Heritage Lottery Fund|文化遗产博彩基金}}寻求资金支持。后者于2009年资助了50万英镑，又于2010年追加了460万英镑。<ref name=SCCFundingPR/><ref name=EchoOnMap>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/district/southampton/4267132.Titanic_museum_could_help_city_100_years_after_tragedy/| title=Sea City Museum could help put Southampton on the map| author=Peter Law| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2009-04-05 | accessdate= 2012-04-27}}</ref><ref name=HLFFundingPR>{{cite press release| url=http://www.hlf.org.uk/news/Pages/SouthamptonSeaCityMuseum.aspx| title=Southampton Sea City Museum sets sail| publisher={{tsl|en|Heritage Lottery Fund||Heritage Lottery Fund}}| date=2012-03-29| accessdate=2012-08-14| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131005012945/http://www.hlf.org.uk/news/Pages/SouthamptonSeaCityMuseum.aspx| archive-date=2013-10-05| dead-url=yes}}</ref>

为了筹集剩余资金，2009年，南安普顿市议会计划出售部分市政艺术收藏品，并拟定将其中两件售出：一件是{{tsl|en|Alfred Munnings|阿尔弗雷德·蒙宁斯}}的油画作品《[[賽車之後|賽車之後]]》（1937），另一件则从[[奥古斯特·罗丹|奥古斯特·罗丹]]的两件青铜像——《[[夏娃|夏娃]]》（1880）或《{{tsl|en|Crouching Woman|蹲着的女人}}》（1882）中选出其一。市议会确信这几件艺术品并非美术馆的核心展品，因该展馆主要展览20及21世纪英国现代艺术作品。加之南安普敦美术馆囿于规模，只能同时陈列3500件艺术品的的200件。故议会认定，将这两件作品出售，可为海洋城市博物馆落成一座新的展馆，使得另外100件藏品能得以展出。该计划有望为博物馆募集到500万英镑资金。<ref name=EchoArtSale>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/4520012.Southampton_s_great_art_sell_off/| title=Works by Rodin and Munnings could raise £5m| author=Peter Law| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2009-07-30 | accessdate=2012-04-21}}</ref>

这一计划得到当地报刊《{{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo|每日回音}}》的支持，该报纸三年来一直发起运动，致力于让多余的藏品流出。<ref name=EchoArtSale/>尽管市议会中的[[保守黨_(英國)|保守党]]领袖亚历克·萨缪尔斯断言：「如果不出售一些画作，我们就无法建成文化遗产中心。」但是，市内仍旧异议不断。[[自由民主党_(英国)|自民党]]及[[工党_(英国)|工党]]议员声称此举「背叛了公众的信任」，并有损于博物馆的声誉。<ref name=EchoUltimatum>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/4500168.___Sell_art_or_no_Titanic_museum____says_council_leader/| title='Sell Art or no Titanic Museum' says Council Leader| author=Matt Smith| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2009-07-18 | accessdate= 2012-05-06}}</ref>[[南安普頓泰斯特_(英國國會選區)|泰斯特选区]]议会成员[[阿倫·懷特希德|阿倫·懷特希德]]形容这是一个「显而易见的坏主意」，并认为此举会打消人们向城市捐赠或遗赠的积极性，且会毁掉南安普顿美术馆在这个国家的地位。<ref name=MPArtSale>{{cite web| url=http://www.alan-whitehead.org.uk/articles/articles_2009/artgallery.htm| title=Don't sell Southampton short!| author=[[阿倫·懷特希德|Alan Whitehead]]| date=2009-08-10| accessdate=2012-08-14| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130103062216/http://www.alan-whitehead.org.uk/articles/articles_2009/artgallery.htm| archivedate=2013-01-03}}</ref>工党政客提议[[债务|借入资金]]以作为替代方案，但遭执政的保守党派驳回，后者认为偿还款项与利息会导致一线服务支出削减，或者使得税负增加。<ref name=EchoBorrow>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/district/southampton/4652610.Borrow_cash_but_don_t_sell_our_city_s_art/| title=Borrow cash but don't sell our city's art| author=Matt Smith| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2009-09-28 | accessdate=2012-08-14}}</ref>

反对者成立了“拯救我们的藏品（ Save our Collection）”小组，反对这一计划。他们在市政中心外组织抗议，并联名2500多人递交了一份请愿书，以示反对。尽管如此，议会投票决定继续执行计划，并于2009年9月与{{tsl|en|Patricia Scotland, Baroness Scotland of Asthal|苏格兰男爵夫人阿斯塔尔}}正式接洽，随后经由{{tsl|en|Attorney General for England and Wales|英格兰和威尔士总检察长}}批准出售。<ref name=EchoProtest>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/briefing/southampton_art_sale/4632403.Protestors_art_sale_demonstration_in_vain/| title=Plans to sell off art are voted through| author=Peter Law| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2009-09-16 | accessdate= 2012-08-14}}</ref>批评声浪自各方而来，{{tsl|en|Museums Association|博物馆协会}}表示，这一出售违背了协会的道德准则，并指责南安普顿市议会没有努力寻找其他资金来源。向{{tsl|en|Chipperfield|奇普菲尔德}}遗赠的蒙宁斯家族以及负责顾问的[[泰特美術館|泰特美術館]]也加入到批判行列之中，其指出出售遗赠给奇普菲尔德的作品以筹集资金，且将其用于资本项目上的行为极不可取，并认为此举已违背了遗赠的初衷。<ref name=MAEthics>{{cite web| url=http://www.museumsassociation.org/news/36433| title=Southampton sale does not meet MA's code of ethics| author=Patrick Steel| work={{tsl|en|Museums Association||Museums Association}}| date=2009-09-23 | accessdate= 2012-08-14}}</ref><ref name=GuardianArtSale>{{cite web| url=https://www.theguardian.com/artanddesign/2009/sep/27/southampton-council-art-selloff| title=Southampton council warned over plan to sell £5m worth of art works to fund gallery complex| author={{tsl|en|Martin Wainwright||Martin Wainwright}}| work=[[衛報|The Guardian]]| date=2009-09-27 | accessdate=2012-04-21}}</ref> {{tsl|en|The Art Fund|艺术基金会}}认为销售艺术品的计划会成为糟糕的先例，即将公有藏品出手，并用以支持其他领域。<ref name=EchoArtFund>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/district/southampton/4575972.Painter_s_call_to_stop_art_sale/| title=Bridget Riley signs petition to stop sell-off of Southampton's art collection| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2009-09-02 | accessdate= 2012-08-14}}</ref>

2009年11月，因强大的反对声浪，南安普顿市议会将艺术品销售搁置，转而谋求其他的资金来源。<ref name=EchoArtSaleHold>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/district/southampton/4726676.Art_sale_put_on_hold/| title=Southampton Council bows to pressure from Museums Association| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2009-11-07 | accessdate= 2012-08-14}}</ref>次年2月，议会取消了销售计划，并宣称，房地产市场的走势促使其开始重新考虑出售先前认为无法出售的资产。市议会亦准备向其他组织寻求财政支持，例如经过艺术品借贷计划向邻接的{{tsl|en|Hampshire County Council|汉普郡县议会}}获取贷款。如果以上努力皆未能奏效，所有差额都将通过借款来弥补。<ref name=EchoArtUTurn/><ref name=MJArtUTurn>{{cite web| url=http://www.museumsassociation.org/museums-journal/news/26022010-southampton-art-sale| title=Southampton does U-turn on art sale| author=Felicity Heywood| work={{tsl|en|Museums Journal||Museums Journal}}| date=2010-02-26 | accessdate=2012-04-21}}</ref>

南安普顿文化发展信托基金会于2010年成立，并致力于为项目筹募资金。<ref name=EchoSCDT>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/4807123.A_first_look_inside_the_Sea_City_museum/| title=Southampton's Titanic Story plans unveiled| author=Peter Law| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2009-12-18 | accessdate=2012-05-06 }}</ref>随后，博物馆获得了{{tsl|en|Department for Culture, Media and Sport|文化，媒体及体育部}}的资助，并与{{tsl|en|Wolfson Foundation|沃尔夫森基金会}}及{{tsl|en|Garfield Weston Foundation|加菲尔德韦斯顿基金会}}达成合作。<ref name=EchoWolfson>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/district/southampton/9488864.__200_000_boost_for_SeaCity_museum/| title=Southampton's SeaCity Museum successfully bids for £200,000 funding| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2012-01-14| accessdate=2012-08-15}}</ref><ref name=SCDTGarfield>{{cite web| url=http://www.culturesouthampton.org.uk/content/garfield-weston-foundation-awards-%C2%A3200000-support-seacity-museum| title=Garfield Weston Foundation awards £200,000 in support of SeaCity Museum| work=Southampton Cultural Development Trust| date=June 2012 | accessdate=2012-08-15}}</ref>

博物馆开放后的头两年中，收入较预期低23%，<ref name=bbc9june />游客数量比预期的24万少了1万人。 <ref name=bbc9june>{{cite news |date=2014-06-09  |title=Southampton's SeaCity Museum fails to meet targets
 |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-hampshire-27759394 |newspaper=BBC News |publisher=BBC |accessdate=2014-06-11}}</ref>随后几年里，访客数量持续下滑，引发了对其资金周转以及长远境况的担忧。<ref name=BBC0909>{{cite news |date=2015-06-09 |title=Southampton's SeaCity Museum: Visitors almost halve |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-hampshire-33059279 |newspaper=BBC News |publisher=BBC |accessdate=2015-06-09}}</ref>

==建设==

2009年7月，南安普顿市议会委派以{{tsl|en|Wilkinson Eyre|威尔金森·艾尔}}为首的团队进行设计。一年后，又委托{{tsl|en|Kier Group|基尔南方公司}}为[[承建商|承建商]]。2010年9月，[[英格蘭遺產委員會|英格蘭遺產委員會]]批准了最终规划。<ref name=EchoEnglishHeritage>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/5065305.English_Heritage_back_Sea_City_Museum_plans/| title=English Heritage back Sea City Museum plans| author=Peter Law| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2010-03-17 | accessdate=2012-08-15}}</ref><ref name=EchoWorkBegins>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/8422296.Work_starts_next_week_on_new_museum/| title=Work starts next week on Southampton's Sea City museum| author=Matt Smith| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2010-09-29 | accessdate=2012-08-15}}</ref>该项工程于11月破土动工，但在施工过程中却遇到了一些阻碍。工程队发现原有的钢架结构已经腐蚀不堪，不得不对其进行维护。为保护建筑内的展品而采用必要的安全措施也显得颇具挑战。<ref name=BuildingProjects/>原建筑的一些特征则得以保留，监狱牢房完整留存并被用作厕所，法庭也得以留存，改为展厅。<ref name=WilkinsonEyrePR>{{cite press release| url=http://www.wilkinsoneyre.com/Media.ashx?id=6240fcb9-cc2d-44c6-8474-a036010bb8fc| title=The SeaCity Museum, Southampton, set to open on the 100th anniversary of the Titanic's departure from the city| publisher={{tsl|en|Wilkinson Eyre||Wilkinson Eyre}}| date=2012-04-10| accessdate=2012-04-21| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120429041750/http://www.wilkinsoneyre.com/Media.ashx?id=6240fcb9-cc2d-44c6-8474-a036010bb8fc| archive-date=2012-04-29| dead-url=yes}}</ref>

该工程于2011年8月[[封頂_(建築)|封顶]]。<ref name=KierPR>{{cite press release| url=http://www.kier.co.uk/construction/newsdetails.asp?x=1&co=7&n=2038| title=Sea City museum reaches highest point| publisher={{tsl|en|Kier Group||Kier Group}}| date=2011-08-11| accessdate=2012-08-15| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130420222108/http://www.kier.co.uk/construction/newsdetails.asp?x=1&co=7&n=2038| archivedate=2013-04-20}}</ref>因北侧较南侧升高了两米，为适应起伏的地势，新场馆被设计为三个相扣的海湾形状结构。展馆的外部建材皆使用石料，以保证与原建筑[[波特兰石|波特兰石]]材质的外观协调一致。整个展馆共提供了2,000 m<sup>2</sup>的学习与展览空间。海洋城市博物馆于2012年4月10日正式开放，以纪念一个世纪前[[泰坦尼克号|泰坦尼克号]]从这里离开。根据南安普顿市议会的预测，博物馆每年将会迎来150,000名游客，但实际上只有第一年达到了预期数量，此后几年中，访客数量持续递减。<ref name=BBC0909 /><ref name=WilkinsonEyrePR/><ref name=MJReview>{{cite journal| title=Review: SeaCity Museum, Southampton| author=Oliver Green| journal={{tsl|en|Museums Journal||Museums Journal}}| date=July 2012 | volume=112| issue=7| pages=44–47| issn=0027-416X| url=http://www.museumsassociation.org/museums-journal/reviews/01072012-seacity-museum-southampton}}</ref><ref name=EchoVisitors>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/district/southampton/9672981.10_000_flock_into_SeaCity_Museum/| title=10,000 visit Southampton's SeaCity Museum in less than two weeks| author=Matt Smith| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2012-04-26 | accessdate=2012-08-22}}</ref>

海洋城市博物馆的设计引起了行业组织的积极关注。{{tsl|en|Ramboll||Ramboll公司}}因其结构设计工作而膺获{{tsl|en|Institution of Structural Engineers|结构工程师协会}}2012年度{{tsl|en|Structural Awards|结构奖}}；《{{tsl|en|Architects' Journal|建筑师杂志}}》则授予威尔金森·艾尔等人2012年度改造奖。<ref name=StructuralAwardShortlist>{{cite web| url=http://www.istructe.org/events-awards/awards/structural-awards/structural-awards/2012/arts-or-entertainment/sea-city-museum| title=Structural Awards 2012: Sea City Museum| work={{tsl|en|Structural Awards||Structural Awards}}| publisher={{tsl|en|Institution of Structural Engineers||Institution of Structural Engineers}}| accessdate=2012-08-15}}</ref><ref name=EchoStructuralAward>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/9875744.City_museum_up_for_major_award/| title=SeaCity Museum up for Structural Award| author=Patrick Knox| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2012-08-16 | accessdate=2012-08-16 }}</ref><ref name=AJRetrofitShortlist>{{cite web| url=http://www.architectsjournal.co.uk/news/daily-news/aj-exclusive-finalists-for-retrofit-awards/8632480.article| title=AJ exclusive: finalists for Retrofit Awards| author=Richard White| work={{tsl|en|Architects' Journal||Architects' Journal}}| date=2012-07-03 | accessdate=2012-08-14}}</ref>

==展览==
海洋城市博物馆中共有三处展览，其中，两处永久展览设于[[警察局|警察局]]及[[裁判法院_(英格蘭及威爾斯)|裁决法院]]的旧址上。

*通往世界的门户（ Gateway to the World）：探讨了南安普顿的历史，以及其作为[[人口遷徙|人口遷徙]]枢纽的角色。此处的展品包括一个1吨重，7米长的{{tsl|en|RMS Queen Mary|玛丽王后号}}{{tsl|en|Ship model|复制船}}，该展品先前位于{{tsl|en|Southampton Maritime Museum|南安普顿海事博物馆}}中。<ref name=HeartReplica>{{cite web| url=http://www.heart.co.uk/southcoast/news/local/first-exhibit-arrives-seacity-museum/| title=First Exhibit Arrives at SeaCity Museum| work={{tsl|en|Heart South Coast||Heart South Coast}}| date=2012-02-27 | accessdate= 2012-08-16}}</ref><ref name=Culture24News>{{cite web| url=http://www.culture24.org.uk/history%20%26%20heritage/transport/maritime%20history/art378234| title=Southampton ready for Titanic SeaCity Museum| work=[[24小時博物館|Culture24]]| date=2012-02-27 | accessdate= 2012-08-15}}</ref>而在2011年9月，海事博物馆与{{tsl|en|God's House Tower|南安普顿考古博物馆}}皆永久关闭，以便将所有展品移至海洋城市博物馆及{{tsl|en|Tudor House Museum|都铎故居博物馆}}。<ref name=EchoWoolHouse>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/9269843.Maritime_Museum_to_close_doors/| title=Southampton's Maritime Museum to close doors| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2011-09-24 | accessdate=2012-04-21}}</ref><ref name=EchoGodsHouse>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/district/southampton/9079656.Council_puts_another_historic_building_on_the_market/| title=God's House Tower lease up for sale| author=Matt Smith| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2011-06-12 | accessdate= 2012-08-16}}</ref><ref name=SCCGodsHouse>{{cite web| url=http://www.southampton.gov.uk/s-leisure/artsheritage/museums-galleries/ghtower-museumofarchaeology/history.aspx| title=God's House Tower: A History of the Museum| publisher={{tsl|en|Southampton City Council||Southampton City Council}}| accessdate=2012-08-16| deadurl=yes| archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120805150008/http://www.southampton.gov.uk/s-leisure/artsheritage/museums-galleries/ghtower-museumofarchaeology/history.aspx| archivedate=2012-08-05}}</ref>

*「南安普顿的泰坦尼克号故事」（ Southampton's Titanic Story）：通过泰坦尼克号船员的视角，来探索这场悲剧，他们大多数人的联系地址都填的是南安普顿。该展览借一间保存完好的法庭，采用视觉及听觉元素，来重新演绎[[英國沉船專員鐵達尼號沉沒調查|英国沉船专员对事故的调查]]，并其所产生的影响进行了探究。市政中心的钟楼高度与泰坦尼克号的烟囱高度相近，当参观者进入展厅时，可以通过屋顶灯一眼看到它，从而留下船舶规模的大致印象。该展览还将幸存者提供的录音以及一些互动元素结合起来，允许游客驾驶虚拟船只并将其引擎引爆。<ref name=WilkinsonEyrePR/><ref name=AV>{{cite web| url=http://www.avinteractive.com/features/40841/reliving-titanic| title=Reliving Titanic| author=Clive Couldwell| work=AV| publisher={{tsl|en|Metropolis International||Metropolis International}}| date=2012-06-07 | accessdate=2012-08-15}}</ref><ref name=Culture24Review>{{cite web| url=http://www.culture24.org.uk/history%20%26%20heritage/transport/maritime%20history/art383178| title=SeaCity Museum brings Titanic story home to Southampton| author=Catherine Roberts| work=[[24小時博物館|Culture24]]| date=2012-04-18 | accessdate=2012-08-14}}</ref><ref name=EchoFirstLook>{{cite web| url=http://www.dailyecho.co.uk/news/9614317.First_look_inside___15m_SeaCity_Museum/| title=First look inside Southampton's £15m SeaCity Museum and Titanic exhibition| author=Jon Reeve| work={{tsl|en|Southern Daily Echo||Southern Daily Echo}}| date=2012-03-27 | accessdate= 2012-04-21 }}</ref>

而新展馆则用来举办一些临时展览。第一场展览名为「泰坦尼克号：传奇」，其通过写照[[鐵達尼號的流行文化|鐵達尼號的流行文化]]，来探究公众对泰坦尼克号的持久迷恋。这场展览亦有播放一些有关泰坦尼克号的电影场景，例如1912的《[[在黑夜與冰山|在黑夜與冰山]]》以及1997的《[[泰坦尼克号_(1997年电影)|泰坦尼克号]]》。该展览所展示的纪念品还包括哀悼小熊，{{tsl|en|Titanic Brewery|泰坦尼克号啤酒厂 
}}生产的啤酒，相关拼图游戏以及其他一些自互联网搜寻到的东西。<ref name=EchoFirstLook/><ref name=APTitanomania>{{cite web| url=https://news.yahoo.com/titanomania-museum-charts-obsession-titanic-201431707.html| title=Titanomania: Museum charts obsession with Titanic| author=Raphael Satter| work=[[美联社|Associated Press]]| date=2012-04-03 | accessdate=2012-04-21}}</ref>

《[[24小時博物館|24小時博物館]]》的凯瑟琳·罗伯特对「南安普顿的泰坦尼克号故事」展览进行了评论，并将市政中心与泰坦尼克号的高度做了比较。她称赞这些图像使得人们更容易了解这个故事。尽管她提到，故事不断重复，而其主题只是围绕着几名船员，但其仍旧认为这是一个精心设计的展览，并称：「没有什么地方，可以比此处更好地用来缅怀那些在泰坦尼克号首航，亦是其最后一次航行中，逝去或是生还的人们了。」"<ref name=Culture24Review/>

《{{tsl|en|Museums Journal|博物馆杂志}}》的奥利弗·格林亦对其表示欢迎。他称赞多媒体技术的使用「既有创造性又很内敛」，并强调了其与展品的精心整合。其对项目充满希望，对市政中心尚未开发的空间以及进一步的拓展十分关注。他认为这个项目停滞会是城市的耻辱，并认为进一步扩大规模对城市的将来有利。<ref name=MJReview/>

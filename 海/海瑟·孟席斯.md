'''海瑟·孟席斯'''（{{lang-en|Heather Menzies-Urich,  Heather Margaret Brotherston Menzies}}，{{bd|1949年|12月3日||2017年|12月24日}}），是一名加拿大裔美国模特和演员，曾出演1965年电影《[[音乐之声|音乐之声]]》中的路易莎·冯·特拉普和电视连续剧《{{le|洛根的奔跑|Logan's Run (TV series)}}》<ref name=":3">{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2017/film/news/sound-of-music-heather-menzies-urich-dead-68-1202648607|title='Sound of Music' Actress Heather Menzies-Urich Dies at 68|date=2017-12-25|last=McNary|first=Dave|work=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]|publisher=[[Penske_Media_Corporation|Penske Media Corporation]]|location=[[Los_Angeles|Los Angeles]]|access-date=2017-12-25}}</ref><ref name="BBC 2017">{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/entertainment-arts-42479649|title=Heather Menzies-Urich, The Sound of Music's Louisa von Trapp, dies|date=2017-12-25|work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|publisher=[[BBC|BBC]]|accessdate=2017-12-25|location=[[London|London]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/pictures/celebrity-deaths-in-2017-stars-weve-lost-w485884/heather-menzies-urich/|title=Celebrity Deaths in 2017: Stars We've Lost|first=Megan|last=French|magazine=[[Us_Weekly|Us Weekly]]|publisher=[[American_Media,_Inc.|American Media, Inc.]]|location=[[New_York_City|New York City]]|date=2017-12-25|access-date=2017-12-25}}</ref>。

== 早期生涯 ==
1949年12月3日，海瑟·孟席斯出生于多伦多，父母在二战后由[[苏格兰|苏格兰]]移民到加拿大<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ancestrylibrary.com/interactive/3998/40735_1220706418_0236-00750?pid=1362777&backurl=https://search.ancestrylibrary.com/cgi-bin/sse.dll?_phsrc=wks5&_phstart=successSource&usePUBJs=true&gss=angs-c&new=1&rank=1&msT=1&gsfn=Heather&gsfn_x=0&gsln=Menzies&gsln_x=0&msbdy=1949&MSAV=1&uidh=57k&pcat=40&h=1362777&dbid=3998&indiv=1&ml_rpos=1&treeid=&personid=&hintid=&usePUB=true&_phsrc=wks5&_phstart=successSource&usePUBJs=true|title=Ancestry Library Edition|website=www.ancestrylibrary.com}}</ref><ref name="Sue 1978">{{cite magazine|url=http://people.com/archive/actress-heather-menzies-bets-ride-on-husband-bob-urich-a-winner-on-tv-in-vega-vol-10-no-17|title=Actress Heather Menzies' Bets Ride on Husband Bob Urich, a Winner on TV in Vega$|first=Sue|last=Reilly|work=[[People_(magazine)|People]]|publisher=[[Time_Inc.|Time Inc.]]|location=United States|date=1978-10-23|access-date=2017-12-25}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.msn.com/en-us/movies/celebrity/sound-of-music-actress-heather-menzies-urich-dead-at-68/ar-BBHkKab|title='Sound of Music' Actress Heather Menzies-Urich Dead at 68|author=[[Us_Weekly|Us Weekly]]|work=[[MSN|MSN]]|publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]]|location=United States|date=2017-12-25|access-date=2017-12-25}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.beachmetro.com/2015/06/29/heather-menzies-urich-talks-sound-music/|title=Heather Menzies-Urich talks about The Sound of Music|date=2015-06-29|author=Fletcher, Bernie|publisher=BeachMetro}}</ref>。她的父亲是一位苦苦谋生的艺术家。孟席斯14岁生日那天后，她陆续在[[温哥华|温哥华]]、[[迈阿密|迈阿密]]、[[伦敦|伦敦]]和南[[加利福尼亚|加利福尼亚]]居住，她还有一个妹妹希拉<ref name="Sue 1978"/>。

孟席斯毕业于{{le|好莱坞高中|Hollywood High School}}，之后就读于艺术大学<ref name="ci">{{cite journal |title=Heather Menzies, 68 |journal=Classic Images |date=February 2018 |issue=512 |page=46}}</ref>。

== 职业生涯 ==
1964年，孟席斯首次出现于荧幕上，出演电视连续剧《{{le|农夫的女儿|The Farmer's Daughter (TV series)}}》。她在《[[音乐之声|音乐之声]]》中扮演了路易莎，是冯·特拉普的第三个孩子，年仅14岁，此前没有任何表演经验<ref name="Lockett 2017">{{Cite news|url=https://www.thesun.co.uk/news/5209925/heather-menzies-ulrich-dead-sound-of-music-age-68-louisa-von-trapp/|title=Heather Menzies-Urich dead aged 68 – Sound of Music star who played Louisa von Trapp dies after battling brain cancer|last=Lockett|first=Jon|date=2017-12-25|work=[[The_Sun_(United_Kingdom)|The Sun]]|publisher=[[News_UK|News UK]]|accessdate=2017-12-25|location=[[London|London]]}}</ref>。孟席斯在电影中演唱了《再会》（{{lang-en|So Long, Farewell}}）和《孤独的牧羊人》（{{lang-en|Lonely Goatherd}}）<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2017/dec/25/sound-of-music-actor-heather-menzies-urich-dies-aged-68|title=Sound of Music actor Heather Menzies-Urich dies aged 68|last=Gregory|first=Julia|date=2017-12-25|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|publisher=[[Guardian_Media_Group|Guardian Media Group]]|location=London, UK|access-date=2017-12-26}}</ref>。

孟席斯继续出现在一系列电视剧中，比如《{{le|化名史密斯和琼斯|Alias Smith and Jones}}》、《{{le|T·J·胡克|T. J. Hooker}}》、《{{le|德拉戈内特|Dragnet (franchise)}}》、《{{le|邦纳萨|Bonanza}}》、《{{le|马库斯·威尔比医生|Marcus Welby, M.D.}}》和《{{le|鲍勃·纽瓦特秀|The Bob Newhart Show}}》。她在电视连续剧《洛根的奔跑》中饰演杰西卡6号<ref name=":1"/><ref name="Epoch Newsroom">{{Cite news|url=https://www.theepochtimes.com/sound-of-music-actress-heather-menzies-urich-dies-report_2395634.html|title='Sound of Music' Actress Heather Menzies-Urich Dies: Report|author=Epoch Newsroom|work=[[Epoch_Times|Epoch Times]]|publisher=Epoch Media Group|location=[[New_York_City|New York City]]|date=2017-12-25|access-date=2017-12-26}}</ref>。此外，孟席斯参演了《{{le|夏威夷 (1966年电影)|Hawaii (1966 film)|夏威夷}}》，《{{le|它是多么甜蜜！|How Sweet It Is!}}》、《{{le|英雄万岁！|Hail, Hero!}}》 、《{{le|食人鱼 (1978年电影)|Piranha (1978 film)|食人鱼}}》与《{{le|危险物种 (1982年电影)|Endangered Species (1982 film)|危险物种}}》<ref name="Lockett 2017"/><ref name="Epoch Newsroom"/>。

1973年，孟席斯还为《花花公子》杂志拍摄图片，标题为"温柔的特拉普"，指的是她的《音乐之声》的角色<ref name="Lockett 2017"/>。她后来出演了四部电视电影：《凯根》、《詹姆斯·迪恩》、《尾炮手乔》和《{{le|美国队长 (1979年电影)|Captain America (1979 film)|美国队长}}》<ref name="The Keegans">{{Cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/469635/Keegans-The/full-credits.html|title=The Keegans|accessdate=2017-12-25|work=[[Turner_Classic_Movies|Turner Classic Movies]]|publisher=[[Turner_Broadcasting_System|Turner Broadcasting System]]}}</ref><ref name="James Dean">{{Cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/467024/James-Dean/full-credits.html|title=James Dean|accessdate=2017-12-25|work=[[Turner_Classic_Movies|Turner Classic Movies]]|publisher=[[Turner_Broadcasting_System|Turner Broadcasting System]]}}</ref><ref name="TailGunnerJoe">{{Cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/477003/Tail-Gunner-Joe/full-credits.html|title=Tail Gunner Joe|accessdate=2017-12-25|work=[[Turner_Classic_Movies|Turner Classic Movies]]|publisher=[[Turner_Broadcasting_System|Turner Broadcasting System]]}}</ref><ref name="Captain America">{{Cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/462456/Captain-America/full-credits.html|title=Captain America|accessdate=2017-12-25|work=[[Turner_Classic_Movies|Turner Classic Movies]]|publisher=[[Turner_Broadcasting_System|Turner Broadcasting System]]}}</ref>。

== 个人生活 ==
1969年，孟席斯与约翰·克莱特结婚，1973年离婚。1975年，她嫁给了{{le|罗伯特·乌里奇|Robert Urich}}，两人于1974年首次见面，当时他们正在拍摄一个他们"结婚"的广告<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.classicfilmtvcafe.com/2017/11/heather-menzies-urich-interview.html|title=Interview with Heather Menzies Urich'|accessdate=2017-12-25|date=2017-11-27|last=Armstrong|first=Richard}}</ref>。他在2002年去世后，孟席斯建立了罗伯特·乌里奇基金会，在她的最后几年里，她花了大部分时间致力于为支持癌症患者以及癌症研究募集资金。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/wireStory/sound-music-actress-heather-menzies-urich-dies-68-51990039|title='Sound of Music' actress Heather Menzies-Urich dies at 68|last=Yan|first=Holly|date=2017-12-25|work=[[ABC_News|ABC News]]|publisher=[[American_Broadcasting_Company|ABC]]|last2=Chan|first2=Stella|accessdate=2018-03-17|location=[[New_York_City|New York City]]|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171226073846/http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/wireStory/sound-music-actress-heather-menzies-urich-dies-68-51990039|archive-date=2017-12-26|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/local/obituaries/heather-menzies-urich-actress-in-the-sound-of-music-dies-at-68/2017/12/27/e8e4c03e-ea96-11e7-b698-91d4e35920a3_story.html|title=Heather Menzies-Urich, actress in ‘The Sound of Music,’ dies at 68|last=Press|first=Associated|date=2017-12-27|work=Washington Post|language=en-US|issn=0190-8286|access-date=2017-12-30}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite news|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/people/2017/12/25/heather-menzies-urich-who-played-von-trapp-sister-sound-music-dies-68/980655001/|title=Heather Menzies-Urich, who played a von Trapp sister in 'The Sound of Music,' dies at 68|last=Levin|first=Gary|date=2017-12-25|work=[[USA_Today|USA Today]]|publisher=[[Gannett_Company|Gannett Company]]|location=[[McLean,_Virginia|McLean, Virginia]]|access-date=2018-03-17}}</ref>

== 去世 ==
2017年11月，海瑟·孟席斯被诊断出患有晚期脑癌。 2017年12月24日去世。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/12/25/entertainment/actress-heather-menzies-urich-dead/index.html|title='Sound of Music' star Heather Menzies Urich dies at 68|accessdate=2018-03-17|date=2017-12-25|last=Yan|first=Holly|work=[[CNN|CNN]]|publisher=[[Turner_Broadcasting_System|Turner Broadcasting System]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/12/25/entertainment/actress-heather-menzies-urich-dead/index.html|title='Sound of Music' star Heather Menzies Urich dies at 68|accessdate=2018-03-17|author=Holly Yan|last=Holly Yan}}</ref> 海瑟·孟席斯有三个孩子，八个孙子和一个曾孙。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deseretnews.com/article/765618345/Park-City-woman-Heather-Menzies-still-feels-Music.html?pg=all|title=About Utah: Park City woman Heather Menzies still feels 'Music'|accessdate=2015-08-16|date=2012-12-20|last=Benson|first=Lee|work=[[Deseret_News|Deseret News]]|publisher=[[Deseret_News_Publishing_Company|Deseret News Publishing Company]]}}</ref>

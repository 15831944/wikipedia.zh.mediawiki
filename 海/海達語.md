'''海達語'''({{lang|en|Xaat Kíl}})是海達族的語言。該語言有8個元音和超過30個輔音。語言學家[[愛德華·薩丕爾|愛德華·薩丕爾]]曾在1915年把海達語歸為[[納－德內語系|納－德內語系]]，該提議隨即受到諸多語言學家（如Pinnow, Greenberg, Enrico, Ruhlen, Manaster Ramer, and Bengtson，詳見參考文獻列表）的支持；然而，今天大多數語言學家將海達語看作是一个[[孤立语言|孤立语言]]。<ref name=Schoonmaker>{{cite book | last = Schoonmaker| first = Peter K. | coauthors = Bettina Von Hagen, Edward C. Wolf | title = The Rain Forests of Home: Profile Of A North American Bioregion | publisher = Island Press | year = 1997 | isbn = 1559634804 | page = 257}}</ref> 2008年語言學界提議美洲的[[納－德內語系|納－德內語系]]和[[北亚|北亚]]（中西伯利亞）的[[叶尼塞语系|叶尼塞语系]]同源並構成[[德內－葉尼塞語門|德內－葉尼塞語門]]，<ref name=symposium>[http://www.uaf.edu/anlc/dy2008.html Dene-Yeniseic Symposium]</ref> 却没有任何跡象表明海達語屬於該語門。

大约100年前，全體海達族都使用海達語，<ref name=haidal>{{cite web | title=UBC World Language Fair’s HAIDA webpage| url =http://www.lerc.educ.ubc.ca/LERC/courses/489/worldlang/haida/Distribution%20Page.htm | accessdate=2008-05-23}}</ref>但今天海達語却是極度瀕危語言，只有20個人在使用，<ref>[https://dq.yam.com/post.php?id=10930 只剩20個人會說！加拿大導演拍片護原民族語]</ref> 而其幾乎都是老人家。<ref name=Ethnologue>{{cite web | title=Ethnologue report for language code:hdn| url =http://www.ethnologue.com/show_language.asp?code=hdn | accessdate=2008-03-17}}</ref><ref name="hlorg">{{cite web | title=Haida Language Mainpage| url =http://haidalanguage.org/ | accessdate=2008-05-23}}</ref> 
<!--
Although the number of native speakers has diminished along the years, according to a 2001 [[Canadian_Census|Canadian Census]] there are now about 275 speakers in [[British_Columbia|British Columbia]] alone,<ref name=haidal/> and with revived interest in the language, this figure is expected to grow.

Currently Haida citizens and friends in all three dialect communities are working to revitalizing the language. In [[Skidegate|Skidegate]], fluent speakers gather on a daily basis to work on the southern or Skidegate dialect and have produced a large series of recordings. In [[Masset,_British_Columbia|Masset]], a group of younger learners is working with their fluent elders to reintegrate the northern or Masset dialect into their daily lives. In Alaska, the community conducts regular language classes for teens and adults, and has built a website complete with online recordings of the Kaigani dialect.

==歷史==
The Rev. [[John_Henry_Keen|John Henry Keen]], a missionary, translated the [[Book_of_Common_Prayer|Book of Common Prayer]] into Haida. Keen returned to England on leave in 1898 and his translated prayer book was published in 1899 in London by the [[Missionary_Society|Missionary Society]].<ref name="bo">{{cite book|last=Beolens et al|first=Bo|title=The Eponym Dictionary of Mammals p.220 |year=2009|publisher=JHU Press|pages=574|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=I-kSmWLc6vYC&lpg=PA220&ots=WonmyES52o&dq=rev%20john%20henry%20keen&pg=PA220#v=onepage&q=rev%20john%20henry%20keen&f=false}}</ref> Sadly the book went out of print as the people it was intended to be read by, were dying out. It is estimated that before the Europeans arrived there were 10,000 people who spoke Haida. By 1900 there were about 700.<ref>[http://justus.anglican.org/resources/bcp/Haida/index.html The Book of Common Prayer in Haida], Society for Promoting Christian Knowledge, London, 1899, accessed 16 November 2010</ref> Keen was to go on to translate the gospels of Luke and John and the Acts from the New Testament into Haida.<ref name="keenbio">{{cite journal|last=Hatch|first=Melville H.|title=A Biographical Memoir of Rev. Keen|journal=The Coleopterists Bulletin|year=1957|month=Autumn|volume=XI|pages=62–64|url=http://www.jstor.org/pss/3999009|accessdate=24 November 2010}}</ref>

===Tone===
Haida features phonemic [[tone_(linguistics)|tone]], the nature of which differs by dialect. In Kaigani the system is one of [[pitch_accent|pitch accent]], with at most one syllable per word featuring high tone; in Masset and Skidegate tone is contrastive in [[syllable_weight|heavy syllables]]. All the above systems feature two tones: high and low.

==書寫系統==
海達語語有兩套基於拉丁字母的書寫系統：在加拿大常用的，约翰·恩里科（{{Lang|en|John Enrico}}）提出的恩里科系統，用來書寫南海達語；在美國常用的，{{tsl|en|Alaska Native Language Center|阿拉斯加原住民族語言中心}}（Alaska Native Language Center）提出的ANLC系統，用來書寫北海達語。

* ANLC系統區分兩個[[同位異音|同位異音]]{{IPA|[t͡ʃ]}}（英語的{{Lang|en|ch}}）和{{IPA|[ts]}}（漢語拼音的{{Unicode|c}}）。

* 恩里科系統是一種跨方言文字（儘管只跨了兩個加拿大方言），它在不同方言中給了不同的語音影射，使得在兩种方言中發音相同的詞有著不同的寫法，而寫法相同的詞有著不同的發音。<Ref name="HLWS">[http://www.haidalanguage.org/ways-of-writing.html Ways of Writing Haida]</Ref>

此外還有一個不常用的系統，由John R. Swanton給出。<Ref name="HLWS" />它更像是基於[[语音|语音]]的，而不像是基於[[音位|音位]]的。

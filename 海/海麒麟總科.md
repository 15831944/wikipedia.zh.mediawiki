'''海麒麟總科'''（學名：{{lang|la|Doridoidea}}），或作'''海蛞蝓總科'''、'''海牛總科'''或'''隱鰓海牛超科'''{{r|flickr}}，是[[海牛亞目|海牛亞目]][[海牛下目|海牛下目]]之下四個[[總科|總科]]級[[分类级别|分类级别]]之一，在[[生物分类学|生物分类学]]屬於[[裸鰓目|裸鰓目]]（舊作[[隱鰓目|隱鰓目]]）的[[海洋生物|海洋]][[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]]{{r|Bouchet 2005}}。

==語源==
海牛總科的學名源於[[海麒麟屬|海牛屬]]；而海牛屬的學名''{{lang|la|Doris}}''又源於[[希臘神話|希臘神話]]的[[海怪|海怪]][[寧芙|寧芙]][[多里斯|多里斯]]。

===2005年之後的變更===
Cadlinidae科 {{small|[[Rudolph_Bergh|Bergh]], 1891}}在2005年的分類被視為多彩海麒麟科的異名{{r|Bouchet 2005}}；但在R.F. Johnson (2011)的研究{{r|Johnson 2011}}顯示''[[Cadlina|Cadlina]]''並不屬於多彩海麒麟科，所以這個屬不應再歸屬於多彩海麒麟科，而是應該獨立成為一個科。而多彩海麒麟科在踢走了''Cadlina''之後成為了[[單系群|單系群]]，很可能是[[Actinocyclidae|Actinocyclidae]]的姊妹分類單元。2017年《[[布歇特等人的腹足類分類_(2017年)|布歇特等人的腹足類分類]]》亦加入了這個修訂{{r|Bouchet 2017}}。

===跟原來的隱鰓目的差異===
<!--anchor of redirect from merged article]-->
隱鰓目被認為是海牛總科的異名：
Cryptobranch dorid nudibranchs (previously known as the taxon Cryptobranchia), are [[nudibranch|nudibranch]] [[sea_slug|sea slug]]s within the clade Doridacea. These slugs are called "cryptobranch," meaning "hidden gill", because they are able to retract their [[gill|gill]]s into a gill pocket, in contrast to nudibranchs in the traditional group phanerobranchs (or Phanerobranchia), which taxon is probably [[paraphyletic|paraphyletic]] (in other words, composed of more than one evolutionary lineage).

A. Valdés distinguishes two major [[clade|clade]]s within the Cryptobranchia: the dorids that have no [[radula|radula]] (the ''Porostomata''); and those with a radula (''the Labiostomata''). The Labiostomata include the [[monophyletic|monophyletic]] families: [[Actinocyclidae|Actinocyclidae]], [[Chromodorididae|Chromodorididae]], [[Dorididae|Dorididae]] and [[Discodorididae|Discodorididae]].

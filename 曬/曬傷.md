'''曬傷'''是一種由于過度暴露于[[紫外線|紫外線]]（通常為[[日光|日光]]）下導致的[[射線燒傷|射線燒傷]]。它促進[[細胞凋亡|細胞凋亡]]<ref>{{EMedicine|emerg|798|Sunburn}}</ref>，引起的症狀為皮膚發紅、發痛，在少數極端案例中會導致生命危險，而輕度暴露于紫外線下則只會導致[[曬黑|曬黑]]，而稍微有些曬黑的皮膚對紫外線的抵禦力更強一些。

曬傷是導致非惡性皮膚腫瘤的主要原因之一<ref name="Do sunscreens prevent skin cancer">[[World_Health_Organization|World Health Organization]], International Agency for Research on Cancer [http://www.iarc.fr/ENG/Press_Releases/archives/pr132a.html "Do sunscreens prevent skin cancer"] {{Wayback|url=http://www.iarc.fr/ENG/Press_Releases/archives/pr132a.html |date=20061126075700 }} Press release No. 132, 5 June 2000</ref><ref name="Solar and ultraviolet radiation">[[World_Health_Organization|World Health Organization]], International Agency for Research on Cancer [http://monographs.iarc.fr/ENG/Monographs/vol55/volume55.pdf "Solar and ultraviolet radiation"] IARC Monographs on the Evaluation of Carcinogenic Risks to Humans, Volume 55, November 1997</ref>，此外還可能導致某些種類的[[皮膚癌|皮膚癌]]，因此在長期暴露于紫外光下時應該外敷[[防曬霜|防曬霜]]。

==症狀==
[[File:Sun_Blisters.jpg|thumb]]

曬傷之初皮膚僅會發紅，之後才會出現各種症狀。這些症狀包括程度不一的疼痛、水疱、[[水腫|水腫]]、[[瘙癢|瘙癢]]、皮膚剝落、[[皮疹|皮疹]]、惡心、發燒、發冷、昏厥等。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aad.org/public/skin-hair-nails/injured-skin/treating-sunburn|title=How to treat sunburn {{!}} American Academy of Dermatology|website=www.aad.org|access-date=2018-03-29}}</ref>

此外，曬傷還可能會導致三種皮膚腫瘤，即[[黑素瘤|黑素瘤]]、[[基底细胞癌|基底细胞癌]]和[[鳞状细胞癌|鳞状细胞癌]]<ref name="Do sunscreens prevent skin cancer"/><ref name="Solar and ultraviolet radiation"/><ref name="Facts About Sunburn and Skin Cancer">[http://www.skincancer.org/prevention/sunburn/facts-about-sunburn-and-skin-cancer "Facts About Sunburn and Skin Cancer"], [[Skin_Cancer_Foundation|Skin Cancer Foundation]]</ref>。其中黑素瘤是由長期頻繁曬傷導致的<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Dennis LK, Vanbeek MJ, Beane Freeman LE, Smith BJ, Dawson DV, Coughlin JA | title = Sunburns and risk of cutaneous melanoma: does age matter? A comprehensive meta-analysis | journal = Annals of Epidemiology | volume = 18 | issue = 8 | pages = 614–27 | date = August 2008 | pmid = 18652979 | pmc = 2873840 | doi = 10.1016/j.annepidem.2008.04.006 }}</ref>，而美國和澳大利亞三分之一的黑素瘤患者其實本來都可以通過提前防曬來阻止其發生。<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Olsen CM, Wilson LF, Green AC, Biswas N, Loyalka J, Whiteman DC | title = How many melanomas might be prevented if more people applied sunscreen regularly? | journal = The British Journal of Dermatology | volume = 178 | issue = 1 | pages = 140–147 | date = January 2018 | pmid = 29239489 | doi = 10.1111/bjd.16079 }}</ref>

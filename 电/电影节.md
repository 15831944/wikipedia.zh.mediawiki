通常FIAPF認證的非专门类竞赛型电影节被称为“A-list”，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.blog.filmfestivallife.com/2012/09/13/a-list-festivals/|title=A List Festivals, a tightly curated list|accessdate=|author=Jana Dietze|date=2012-09-03|publisher=}}</ref>专门类竞赛型电影节被称为“B-list”，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://variety.com/2007/film/asia/gilmore-speaks-out-against-fiapf-1117967083/|title=Sundance chief comments on ratings system|author=|date=|work=|accessdate=}}</ref>非竞赛型电影节被称为“C-list”<ref>{{Cite book|title=Film Festivals: Cinema and Cultural Exchange.|last=Diestro-Dópido|first=Mar|publisher=伦敦玛丽王后大学|year=|isbn=|location=|pages=第64页}}</ref>，

或者被称为A-class（A類）、B-class（B類）<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://odessareview.com/guide-2018-molodist-kyiv-international-film-festival/|title=Your Guide To The 2018 Molodist Kyiv International Film Festival|accessdate=|author=Ulyana Dovgan|date=2018-05-25|publisher=}}</ref>，一些知名非专门类竞赛型电影节例子如多倫多國際電影節、釜山影展或富川國際奇幻電影節等。

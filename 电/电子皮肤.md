'''电子皮肤'''是一类柔韧、可拉伸、能够[[自修复材料|自我修复]]的电子产品，它能够模仿人类或动物皮肤的功能。<ref name=":0">{{Cite journal|title=Stretchable and self-healing polymers and devices for electronic skin|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S007967001300097X|last=Benight|first=Stephanie J.|last2=Wang|first2=Chao|journal=Progress in Polymer Science|issue=12|doi=10.1016/j.progpolymsci.2013.08.001|volume=38|pages=1961–1977|last3=Tok|first3=Jeffrey B.H.|last4=Bao|first4=Zhenan}}</ref>电子皮肤通常具有感知能力，它旨在重现人类皮肤响应环境因素变化的能力（如热量和压力的变化）。<ref name=":0" /> <ref name=":1">{{Cite journal|title=A chameleon-inspired stretchable electronic skin with interactive colour changing controlled by tactile sensing|url=https://www.nature.com/articles/ncomms9011|last=Chou|first=Ho-Hsiu|last2=Nguyen|first2=Amanda|date=2015-08-24|journal=Nature Communications|doi=10.1038/ncomms9011|volume=6|pages=8011|language=En|last3=Chortos|first3=Alex|last4=To|first4=John W. == 自我修复能力 ==
电子皮肤的自我修复能力对于电子皮肤在软机器人等领域的潜在应用至关重要。<ref name=":4">{{Cite journal|title=An electrically and mechanically self-healing composite with pressure- and flexion-sensitive properties for electronic skin applications|url=http://www.nature.com/articles/nnano.2012.192|last=Tee|first=Benjamin C-K.|last2=Wang|first2=Chao|date=December 2012|journal=Nature Nanotechnology|issue=12|doi=10.1038/nnano.2012.192|volume=7|pages=825–832|language=En|issn=1748-3395|last3=Allen|first3=Ranulfo|last4=Bao|first4=Zhenan}}</ref> 正确设计能够自我修复的电子皮肤不仅需要可愈合材料作为基底，还需要重新设计其传感功能，例如触觉传感或导电性。 <ref name=":4" />

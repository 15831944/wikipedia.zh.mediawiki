电臀舞是一种1980年代末起源于新奥尔良的舞蹈。个人表演，主要但不完全是由女性表演<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120811171214/http://www.nytimes.com/2010/07/25/magazine/25bounce-t.html?pagewanted=1&_r=1&hp|title="Sissy Bounce, New Orleans's Gender-Bending Rap - NYTimes.com"|author=Dee, Jonathan|date=2012-08-11|work=The New York Times.|accessdate=2017.06.30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|title=Bounce: Rap Music and Local Identity in New Orleans.|last=Miller|first=Matt|publisher=Univ of Massachusetts Press.|year=2012|isbn=|location=Boston|pages=}}</ref>，舞者的动作是投掷或将臀部向后推或摇动臀部，通常是低蹲的姿势。电臀舞是新奥尔良嘻哈乐“Bounce”的一大特色动作之一<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/us/twerk|title="Twerk: Definition of Twerk in Oxford Dictionary - American English (US)|date=|access-date=|website=|last=|first=|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.wheretheyatnola.com/|title="Where They At: New Orleans Hip-Hop and Bounce in Words and Pictures. Aubrey Edwards and Alison Fensterstock. New Orleans 2010".|date=2017-06-30|access-date=|website=|last=|first=|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=}}</ref>。动作包括“混合”、“锻炼”、“弯腰”、“挤肩膀”、“拍手”、“拍手”和“野林”——这些动作都被认为是“摇晃臀部”或“弹跳”。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fuse.tv/2013/11/big-freedia-best-non-twerk-dances|title="Peter Pan and Bending Over: Big Freedia's 5 Best Non-Twerk Dances|date=2017-06-30|access-date=|website=|last=|first=|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y1Lf90zwk4o|title=Big Freedia on New Orleans Bounce Music & Inventing New Dance Moves|date=2013-10-09|access-date=|website=|last=|first=|archive-url=|archive-date=2017-06-30|dead-url=}}</ref>电臀舞只是bounce的一种舞蹈动作

作为一种由当地援助组织、娱乐俱乐部、街区聚会和第二排人组成的传统<ref name=":0">{{Cite journal|title=A Review of Matt Miller's Bounce: Rap Music and Local Identity in New Orleans".|author=Holly, Hobbs|url=|journal=Southern Spaces.|issue=|doi=10.18737/M7ZC82.|others=|year=2012|volume=|page=|pmid=}}</ref>，这种舞蹈是“由新奥尔良非裔美国人社区的艺术家们表演的娘们腔的弹跳音乐的历史情境的核心，(2005年卡特里娜飓风之后)，<ref name=":1">{{Cite journal|title=Dirty Decade: Rap Music and the US South, 1997–2007". Southern Spaces.|author=Matt, Miller|url=|journal=Southern Spaces.|issue=|doi=10.18737/M78P5T|others=|year=2008|volume=|page=|pmid=}}</ref>这种音乐的流行[导致]迅速上升。”20世纪90年代，电臀舞在被称为“肮脏的南方”的嘻哈/说唱乐地区，包括新奥尔良、孟菲斯、弗吉尼亚海滩、迈阿密、亚特兰大和休斯顿，在黑人派对文化中有着广泛的吸引力。<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":0" />2013年，它成为谷歌搜索引擎上最热门的“What is”搜索。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2013/dec/17/twerking-2013-google-search-rankings|title=Twerking dances its way into 2013 Google search rankings|author=Hern, Alex|date=2013-12-17|work=The Guardian|accessdate=2018-06-30}}</ref>

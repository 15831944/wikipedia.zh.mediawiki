'''混合國家'''（[[國際奧委會國家編碼列表|國家編碼]]：MIX）是[[青年奥林匹克运动会|青年奥林匹克运动会]]由不同的[[國家奧林匹克委員會|國家奧林匹克委員會]] (NOCs)組成的代表隊。該代表隊於[[2010年夏季青年奧林匹克運動會|2010年夏季青年奧林匹克運動會]]引入，來自不同國家的運動員在同一個隊伍中進行比賽。<ref>{{cite web |title=Countries unite for mixed NOC fencing event |publisher=Chinese Olympic Committee |date=19 August 2010 |url=http://en.olympic.cn/news/olympic_news/2010-08-19/2059337.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120523113533/http://en.olympic.cn/news/olympic_news/2010-08-19/2059337.html |archive-date=2012-05-23}}</ref>並在此後的青年奧運會都有這個代表團。<ref>{{cite web |title=Four nations combine to win first ever Youth Olympic Games Speed Skating Mixed NOC Team Sprint gold |date=17 February 2016 |publisher=International Olympic Committee |url=http://iocnewsroom.com/video/vnr_yog_20160217_mixed-speed-skating-noc |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160324133159/http://iocnewsroom.com/video/vnr_yog_20160217_mixed-speed-skating-noc |archive-date=2016-03-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Mixed NOC duo from Japan and Switzerland claim Lillehammer 2016 gold in the Curling Mixed Doubles |date=21 February 2016 |publisher=International Olympic Committee |url=http://iocnewsroom.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/SCRIPT_VNR_YOG_20160221_MIXEDDoublesCurling-1.pdf |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160702014445/http://iocnewsroom.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/SCRIPT_VNR_YOG_20160221_MIXEDDoublesCurling-1.pdf |archive-date=2016-07-02}}</ref>

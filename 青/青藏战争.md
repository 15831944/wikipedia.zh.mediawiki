'''青藏战争'''是1932年因为[[西藏地方政府|西藏地方政府]]想从中国西北[[马家军|马家军]]手中夺取[[青海|青海]][[玉树|玉树]]地区的控制权而爆发的一场战争。1932年，受[[英国|英国]]支持的西藏方面想将当时与[[西康|西康]]方面[[刘文辉|刘文辉]]（时西康省主席）的战事扩大化，转而开始向青海进军。3月，藏军以玉树地区隶属权有争议的寺庙[[尕丹寺|尕丹寺]]黄白教派冲突为借口，开始进攻<ref>[http://www.qh.xinhuanet.com/2009-11/14/content_18232222_1.htm 青藏战争的内幕] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924083534/http://www.qh.xinhuanet.com/2009-11/14/content_18232222_1.htm |date=2015-09-24 }} 新华网青海频道  2009-11-14  2014-10-22查阅</ref>。青海回民将军[[马步芳|马步芳]]大破藏军，并将西康的[[石渠县|石渠县]]、[[德格县|德格县]]等自1919年即被藏军所占县份收回<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=ak3SQTVS7acC&pg=PA150&dq=ma+bufang+liu+wenhui&hl=en&ei=hBshTPqEIsGqlAf_9rDHBA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=7&ved=0CEUQ6AEwBg#v=onepage&q=ma%20bufang%20liu%20wenhui&f=false|title=The historical status of China's Tibet|author=Jiawei Wang, Nimajianzan|year=1997|publisher=五洲传播出版社|location=|page=150|isbn=7-80113-304-8|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?ei=hBshTPqEIsGqlAf_9rDHBA&ct=result&id=0bsKAAAAYAAJ&dq=ma+bufang+liu+wenhui&q=ma+bufang|title=The biographies of the Dalai Lamas|author=Hanzhang Ya, Ya Hanzhang|year=1991|publisher=Foreign Languages Press|location=|pages=352, 355|isbn=0-8351-2266-2|pages=442|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?ei=qRwhTMCjKIWdlgfoo6HAAQ&ct=result&id=Rh1uAAAAMAAJ&dq=ma+bufang+liu+wenhui&q=ma+bufang|title=India & China, 1904–2004: a century of peace and conflict|author=B.

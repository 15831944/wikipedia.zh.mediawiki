'''青年艺术家奖'''（{{lang-en|'''Young Artist Award'''}}）由青年艺术家基金会复制资金运营，1978年首次颁奖，鼓励青年艺术家，给予他们经济上的奖励以缓解他们的经济压力，更好地位艺术做出贡献。<ref name="reelwinners">{{cite book|last=Crouse|first=Richard |title=Reel Winners|publisher=Dundurn Press Ltd.|year=2005|edition=illustrated|pages=42–43|isbn=1-55002-574-0|oclc=9781550025743}}</ref><ref name="contemporaraytft">{{cite book|last=Riggs|first=Thomas |title=Contemporary Theatre, Film and Television|publisher=Gale / Cengage Learning|year=2007|volume=74|isbn=0-7876-9047-3|oclc=9780787690472}}</ref><ref name="Golden Globes">{{cite web|url=http://www.goldenglobes.org/blog/2011/03/young-artist-foundation-recognizes-the-young-and-talented/|title=HFPA Golden Globes - Young Artist Foundation|accessdate=2011-03-31|work=GoldenGlobes.org|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110317192954/http://www.goldenglobes.org/blog/2011/03/young-artist-foundation-recognizes-the-young-and-talented/|archivedate=2011-03-17}}</ref><ref name="ABC Local">{{cite web|url=http://abclocal.go.com/kabc/story?section=news/entertainment&id=7380555|title=KABC-TV - Budding stars shine at Young Artist Awards|accessdate=2011-03-31|work=ABCLocal.go.com}}</ref> 

青年艺术家奖主要颁发给四类人物：电影、电视、戏剧和音乐，而且年龄必须未满21周岁。<ref name="reelwinners"/><ref name="Young Artist Awards 1">{{cite web|url=http://www.youngartistawards.org/presidents.html|title=Young Artist Awards - President's Message|accessdate=2011-03-31|work=YoungArtistAwards.org}}</ref><ref name="Young Artist Awards 16">{{cite web|url=http://www.youngartistawards.org/forms.html|title=Young Artist Awards - Nomination Submission Requirements|accessdate=2012-02-27|work=YoungArtistAwards.org|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150420142432/http://youngartistawards.org/forms.html|archivedate=2015-04-20}}</ref>

第一届青年艺术家奖的颁发是在1979年11月，颁发地点是在喜来登环球酒店。<ref name="Jet">{{cite web|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=SEIDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA58&lpg=PA58&dq=%22hollywood+women%27s+photo+and+press+club%22#v=onepage&q=%22hollywood%20women%27s%20photo%20and%20press%20club%22&f=false|title=Coleman, Jackson and King Garner Youth Awards|date=1979-11-08|work=Jet Magazine}}</ref><ref name="Ottawa Citizen">{{cite web|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=uqMyAAAAIBAJ&sjid=8u4FAAAAIBAJ&pg=2933,2876434&dq=youth-in-film-awards&hl=en|title=Young actor receives TV award|date=1979-11-24|work=The Ottawa Citizen}}</ref>  最近第三十三届是在加利福尼亚州。<ref name="Young Artist Awards 15">{{cite web|url=http://www.youngartistawards.org/noms33.html|title=33rd Annual Young Artist Awards|accessdate=2012-03-31|work=YoungArtistAwards.org}}</ref>

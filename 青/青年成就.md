'''青年成就'''（Junior Achievement）是一个非营利性青年组织，由 Horace A. Moses，Theodore Vail和Winthrop M. Crane在1919年建立，Junior Acheveiment与当地企业和政府一起，给从幼儿园到大学的同学，传授财务知识，进行职业教育，培养企业家精神。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jachina.org/cn/who_we_are.html|title=组织介绍|language=zh-hans|author=Junior Achievement|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120118113525/http://www.jachina.org/cn/who_we_are.html|archivedate=2012-01-18}}</ref><ref name="1990 NY Times article">{{cite news|last=Daley|first=Suzanne|title=New World for Junior Achievement|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1990/11/28/education/new-world-for-junior-achievement.html?src=pm|accessdate=16 May 2012|newspaper=The New York Times|date=28 November 1990}}</ref><ref name="1997 NY Times article">{{cite news|last=Singer|first=Penny|title=For Junior Achievers, Volunteers Are Key|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1997/05/18/nyregion/for-junior-achievers-volunteers-are-key.html?src=pm|accessdate=16 May 2012|newspaper=The New York Times|date=18 May 1997}}</ref><ref name="2012 Washington Post article">{{cite news|last=Heath|first=Thomas|title=Value Added: This English major prefers the language of money|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/business/economy/value-added-this-english-major-prefers-the-language-of-money/2012/05/13/gIQAmYcGNU_story.html|accessdate=16 May 2012|newspaper=The Washington Post|date=13 May 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Duchon|first=Dennis|coauthors=Green, Stephen G.; Taber, Thomas D.|title=Vertical dyad linkage: A longitudinal assessment of antecedents, measures, and consequences.|journal=Journal of Applied Psychology|date=1 January 1986|volume=71|issue=1|pages=56–60|doi=10.1037/0021-9010.71.1.56|url=http://psycnet.apa.org/journals/apl/71/1/56/|accessdate=16 May 2012}}</ref>

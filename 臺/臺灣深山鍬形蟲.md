'''臺灣深山鍬形蟲'''（[[學名|學名]]：''{{sname|Lucanus formosanus}}''），屬於[[鞘翅目|鞘翅目]]的[[鍬形蟲|鍬形蟲]]科，臺灣特有種<ref>{{cite journal |title=Phylogenetic position of a threatened stag beetle, ''Lucanus datunensis'' (Coleoptera: Lucanidae) in Taiwan and implications for conservation |journal=[[Conservation_Genetics|Conservation Genetics]] |doi=10.1007/s10592-009-9996-8 |year=2009 |author=Chung-Ping Lin, Jen-Pan Huang, Yat-Hung Lee & Ming-Yu Chen |volume=12 |pages=337–341}}</ref>，日夜出沒，中、低海拔，具[[趨光性|趨光性]]，與[[高砂深山鍬形蟲|高砂深山鍬形蟲]]同屬相似。<ref name="TaiBNET-333951"/><ref name="taubif-namecode-333951"/>{{sfn|楊維晟|2008|p=18}}

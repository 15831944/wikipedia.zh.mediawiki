'''達科他公寓'''（{{lang-en|The Dakota}}）是一座位於[[美國|美國]][[紐約市|紐約市]][[曼哈頓|曼哈頓]][[上西城|上西城]]72號街和中央公園西路口西北角的[[公寓|公寓樓]]，修建於1884年，是曼哈頓最為著名的住宅樓之一。修建於1880年10月25日至1884年10月27日期間<ref name="habs3">Historic American Buildings Survey (HABS), [http://memory.loc.gov/cgi-bin/ampage?collId=hhdatapage&fileName=ny/ny0300/ny0360/data/hhdatapage.db&recNum=2 "The Dakota, HSBS No. NY-5467"]{{dead link|date=十月 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot }}, pp. 1-11; retrieved July 3, 2013.</ref><ref>Brockmann, Jorg ''et al.'' (2002), {{Google books|gJR_PahlUtIC&dq|''One Thousand New York Buildings,'' pp. 342–343.|page=342}}</ref>。1969年，達科他公寓被評為紐約市地標<ref>Birmingham, Stephen. (1996). [https://books.google.com/books?id=OGEDiKOujjgC&pg=PA131&dq=%20  ''Life at the Dakota: New York's Most Unusual Address,'' pp. 130-131].</ref>，1972年列為[[國家史蹟名錄|國家史蹟名錄]]（NRHP）<ref name="nris">{{NRISref|2006a}}</ref>，1976年獲指定為[[美國國家歷史名勝|美國國家歷史名勝]]（NHL）<ref name="nhlsum">{{cite web | url=http://tps.cr.nps.gov/nhl/detail.cfm?ResourceId=1244&ResourceType=Building | title=Dakota Apartments | date=September 11, 2007 | work=National Historic Landmark summary listing | publisher=National Park Service | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605222244/http://tps.cr.nps.gov/nhl/detail.cfm?ResourceID=1244&resourceType=Building | archivedate=June 5, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | url={{NHLS url |id=72000869}} | title=National Register of Historic Places Inventory: Dakota Apartments | author=Carolyn Pitts | format=PDF | date=August 10, 1976 | publisher=National Park Service | accessdate=June 21, 2009}} and {{NHLS url | id=72000869 | title=Accompanying photos, exterior, undated | photos=y}} {{small|(1.65 MB)}}</ref>。2013年開始，達科他公寓的外牆進行了翻新作業<ref>{{cite web |url=http://ny.curbed.com/archives/2015/04/03/the_iconic_dakota_built_in_1884_is_getting_some_work_done.php |title=The Iconic Dakota, Built in 1884, Is Getting Some Work Done |work=Curbed NY}}</ref>。

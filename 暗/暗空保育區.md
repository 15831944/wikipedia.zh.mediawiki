'''暗空保育區'''（英語：{{lang|en|dark-sky preserve}}，{{lang|en|DSP}}）常見於公園或[[觀測臺|觀測臺]]附近，是一個免於人造[[光污染|光污染]]影響的區域。設立暗空保育區的目的通常是為了[[觀星|觀星]]。[[國際暗天協會|國際暗天協會]]設有國際認證制度，以鼓勵世界各國推動暗空保育。

==历史==
2007年加拿大[[魁北克省|魁北克省]]的{{Link-en|莫干迪克峰天文臺|Mont Mégantic Observatory}}獲該協會認證為首個國際暗空保育區；<ref>{{cite web| last =| first =| authorlink =| title =First International Dark Sky Reserve Is Announced in Québec!| work =| publisher =International Dark-Sky Association| date =2007-09-21| url =http://data.nextrionet.com/site/idsa/PR_Mont_Megantic_2007.pdf| format =| doi =| accessdate =2008-11-26 |language=en| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20081115023048/http://data.nextrionet.com/site/idsa/PR_Mont_Megantic_2007.pdf| archivedate =2008-11-15| df =}}</ref>其後，該協會又認證美國[[猶他州|猶他州]]的[[天生橋國家保護區|天生橋國家保護區]]為首個國際暗天公園。<ref>{{cite web| last =| first =| authorlink =| title =Utah’s Natural Bridges National Monument Becomes First International Dark Sky Park| work =| publisher =International Dark-Sky Association| date =2007-04-19| url =http://data.nextrionet.com/site/idsa/PR_Natural_Bridges_2007.pdf| format =| doi =| accessdate =2008-11-26| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20081115023700/http://data.nextrionet.com/site/idsa/PR_Natural_Bridges_2007.pdf| archivedate =2008-11-15 |language=en| df =}}</ref>在普遍認知中，暗空保育區應該要暗得足以觀星，實務上卻並非總是如此。暗空保育區是按照野生動植物對人造光源的敏感度來管制人類照明。<ref>{{Cite web|author=Robert Dick|url=http://www.rasc.ca/sites/default/files/RASC%20DSP%20GOL%20-%20Summer%202013_0.pdf|format=pdf|title=Guidelines for Outdoor Lighting in Dark-sky Preserves|location=[[Toronto|Toronto]]|publisher={{Link-en|加拿大皇家天文學會|Royal Astronomical Society of Canada|Royal Astronomical Society of Canada}}|year=2008|language=en|accessdate=2018-04-15}}</ref>

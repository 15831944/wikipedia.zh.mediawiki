'''都市車'''是一種小型[[汽車|汽車]]<ref>{{cite news | title=Little Cars in the Big Apple: Still Only a Good Idea| first=Phil| last=Patton| url=http://www.nytimes.com/2005/03/28/automobiles/28CARS.html| newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]| publisher=[[The_New_York_Times_Company|The New York Times Company]]| location=New York| date=March 28, 2005| accessdate=March 2, 2010| quote=In Europe, the "city car" is a well-understood concept ... | archiveurl=http://www.webcitation.org/5nwXkWwE4| archivedate=March 2, 2010}}</ref>，主要是在[[市區|市區]]使用<ref>{{cite news| title=Motoring: The City car to be seen in| first=James| last=Ruppert| url=http://www.independent.co.uk/money/spend-save/motoring-the-city-car-to-be-seen-in-1177204.html| agency=| newspaper=[[The_Independent|The Independent]]| publisher=[[Independent_News_&_Media|Independent News & Media]]| location=London| date=October 10, 1998| accessdate=March 1, 2010| quote=(City cars) are meant to be cheap, small, easy to park and yet practical, with good manners on the open road.| archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5nv2yk5DO?url=http://www.independent.co.uk/money/spend-save/motoring-the-city-car-to-be-seen-in-1177204.html| archivedate=2010-03-02| dead-url=no}}</ref>。都市車遍布世界各地，許多汽車製造廠都有生產這種車款。[[北美洲|北美洲]]稱都市車為[[次緊湊型車|次緊湊型車]]（subcompacts），[[歐洲|歐洲]]稱為A-segment，[[日本|日本]]稱為[[輕型車|輕型車]]。


'''都市奇幻'''（{{lang-en|'''Urban fantasy'''}}）是設定於都市場景的[[奇幻作品|奇幻]]類型<ref name=definition>{{cite news|first=Jeannie |last=Holmes |title=Writing Urban Fantasy, Part 1 |work=jeannieholmes.com |date=2010-12-21 |accessdate=2012-05-17 |quote= |url=http://www.jeannieholmes.com/index.php/2010/12/21/writing-urban-fantasy-part-1-oh-yeah-says-who/  |archiveurl=http://www.jeannieholmes.com/index.php/2010/12/21/writing-urban-fantasy-part-1-oh-yeah-says-who/ |archivedate=2015-04-07  }} (Archived by WebCite® at )</ref><ref name=Charles>{{cite book|last=Datlow|first=Ellen|title=Naked City: Tales of Urban Fantasy|year=2011|publisher=St. Martin's Press|location=New York|isbn=978-0-312-38524-8|pages=xii-xiii}}</ref>。該類型的作品主要設定於現實世界中，並常有著魔法、外來種族的到來、神話生物的發現、人與超自然存在的共存或衝突以及其他為都市生活帶來的變化等情節<ref name=BPD>{{cite news |title=''The Better Part of Darkness'' review |work=[[Publishers_Weekly|Publishers Weekly]] |accessdate=2011-04-11 |quote= |url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/978-1-4391-0965-6}}</ref><ref name=Holzner>{{cite news |title=''Deadtown'' by Nancy Holzner |work=Publishers Weekly |accessdate=2011-04-11 |quote= |url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/978-0-441-01813-0}}</ref>。

都市奇幻和[[當代奇幻|當代奇幻]]不同，因為當代奇幻不一定設定於都市場景，當代奇幻也可能發生在未來和歷史背景的世界<ref name=definition/><ref name=BPD/>。

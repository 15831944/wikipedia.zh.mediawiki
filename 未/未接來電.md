'''未接來電'''（{{lang-en|missed call}}）是呼叫者在接听者接听前有意挂断的电话。这是一种{{tsl|en|one-bit message|单方消息}}。

在移动电话用户呼出数量有限的[[新兴市场|新兴市场]]中，未接来电得到广泛的使用：因为电话实际未接通，所以运营商并不会向呼叫者收取费用，进而不会影响他们的信用。在某些国家，连续的响一声来电被用来传递特定的信息。在非洲的某些地区，响一声来电被称作beeping<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/1132926.stm|title=Uganda's 'beeping' nuisance|date=2001|accessdate=2018-07-09|language=en-GB|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180709070137/http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/1132926.stm|archivedate=2018-07-09}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite news|url=https://blogs.scientificamerican.com/news-blog/rules-of-beeping/|title=Rules of beeping|last=Stix|first=Gary|newspaper=Scientific American Blog Network|accessdate=2018-07-09|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180709094402/https://blogs.scientificamerican.com/news-blog/rules-of-beeping/|archivedate=2018-07-09}}</ref>，在印尼被称为memancing（钓鱼）<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://knowledge.wharton.upenn.edu/article/missed-call-marketing-taken-hold-india/|title=Why ‘Missed Call’ Marketing Has Taken Hold in India - Knowledge@Wharton|newspaper=Knowledge@Wharton|accessdate=2018-07-09|language=en-US|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180709064712/http://knowledge.wharton.upenn.edu/article/missed-call-marketing-taken-hold-india/|archivedate=2018-07-09}}</ref>，在尼日利亚被称为flashing<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/910816446|title=Glocal English: The Changing Face and Forms of Nigerian English in a Global World|last=Kperogi|first=Farooq A.,|date=2015|publisher=Peter Lang|year=|isbn=9781453914946|location=New York|pages=|chapter=Glocal English : the changing face and forms of Nigerian English in a global world|oclc=910816446}}</ref>，巴基斯坦称作flashcall<ref name=":0" />，菲律宾称作miskol（miss call的另一种写法）<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|url=http://newsinfo.inquirer.net/breakingnews/infotech/view_article.php?article_id=82266|title='Miskol' is Filipino word of the year at conference - INQUIRER.net, Philippine News for Filipinos|accessdate=2018-07-09|date=2008-03-16|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080316042417/http://newsinfo.inquirer.net/breakingnews/infotech/view_article.php?article_id=82266|archivedate=2008-03-16}}</ref><ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=http://www.philstar.com/headlines/10684/%E2%80%98miskol%E2%80%99-word-year|title=‘Miskol’ is word of the year， Philstar.com|accessdate=2018-07-09|author=|date=|work=philstar.com|publisher=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171007071603/http://www.philstar.com/headlines/10684/%E2%80%98miskol%E2%80%99-word-year|archivedate=2017-10-07}}</ref>。中国大陆有时把商业化的未接來電称作'''响一声来电'''<ref name=":1" />。

在印度，未接來電拥有比较高的知名度。作为一种[[行销企划|行销企划]]，未接來電已经不仅仅是一种通常的通信方式，更作为诱导用户回拨或回信那些含有广告的电话的手段。由于印度的[[功能机|功能机]]用户比[[智能手机|智能手机]]用户更为常见，所以在宣传方面，不只是未接來電，还有其他的方法。

== 理由和影响 ==
由于收入的问题，{{tsl|en|prepay mobile phone|预付电话}}在新兴市场中广受欢迎<ref name=":2" /><ref name=":0" />。预付电话合同所允许的呼出次数是有限的，而未接來電因为没有接通所以不会收费<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":5">{{Cite web|url=http://infotech.indiatimes.com/Telecom/Telcos_miss_moolah_on_missed_calls/articleshow/1108866.cms|title=Telcos miss moolah on missed calls - Service Providers - Telecom - Indiatimes Infotech|accessdate=2018-07-09|date=2007-03-09|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070309021729/http://infotech.indiatimes.com/Telecom/Telcos_miss_moolah_on_missed_calls/articleshow/1108866.cms|archivedate=2007-03-09}}</ref>。没有接通的电话不存在语言或文字的交流，所以未接來電还有超语言的优势<ref name=":5" /><ref name=":0" />。印度[[科泽科德|科泽科德]]管理学院市场管理教授Keyoor Purani认为，未接來電是一种“经济且广泛”的通信机制<ref name=":0" />。

== 使用情况 ==
=== 社交用途 ===
本质上未接來電传递的信息是经过预先认可和与接听者环境相关的<ref name=":7" />。未接来电可能被用来揭示拨打者的状态，例如告诉接听者他们已到达某一位置，或者快递到货等<ref name=":8" />。但具体到不同的国家，未接來電实际承担着不同的职责。例如在孟加拉国，两次连续的未接來電暗示接听者迟到，而在叙利亚，五次连续未接來電象征着拨打着试图与接听者网上聊天<ref name=":8" />。在另外一些国家，没来得及接电话可能是要机主用语音信箱或直接回拨，从而承担通信费用<ref name=":2" />。

此外，一些单向通信应用程序（例如{{tsl|en|Yo (app)|Yo}}，知名于只能向收信人发送同名的问候语）也被认为采用了未接來電的上述理念<ref name=":0" />。

=== 市场营销和服务 ===
未接来电正逐渐成为[[移动营销|移动营销]]的方式（英：missed call marketing，下称MCM）<ref name=":9">{{Cite news|url=https://www.economist.com/blogs/schumpeter/2013/08/mobile-advertising-india|title=Marketing a missed call|newspaper=The Economist|accessdate=2018-07-09|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171007120151/https://www.economist.com/blogs/schumpeter/2013/08/mobile-advertising-india|archivedate=2017-10-07}}</ref>。MCM商家通常利用运营商可以接收无限量来电和[[短信息|短信息]]的优势，让消费者拨打一个号码（需等到建立连接再挂断，但无需接通，一般这些号码都配置成建立连接即挂断）<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":11">{{Cite news|url=http://techcircle.vccircle.com/2011/03/14/this-missed-call-got-the-right-number-for-zipdial/|title=This Missed Call Got The Right Number For Zipdial|date=2011-03-14|newspaper=Techcircle|accessdate=2018-07-09|language=en-US|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180710195617/https://techcircle.vccircle.com/2011/03/14/this-missed-call-got-the-right-number-for-zipdial/|archivedate=2018-07-10}}</ref>。此号码之后将回拨给消费者，提供例如产品信息、打折促销等内容。广告商可以借此建立顾客数据库，以便之后进一步营销<ref name=":10">{{Cite news|url=https://digiday.com/marketing/wtf-is-missed-call-marketing-zipdial/|title=WTF is missed-call marketing? - Digiday|date=2015-01-21|newspaper=Digiday|accessdate=2018-07-09|language=en-US|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180710133017/https://digiday.com/marketing/wtf-is-missed-call-marketing-zipdial/|archivedate=2018-07-10}}</ref><ref name=":9" />。

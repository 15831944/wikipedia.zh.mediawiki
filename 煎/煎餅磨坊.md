'''煎餅磨坊'''（{{lang-fr|Moulin de la Galette}}）是位於[[法國|法國]]首都[[巴黎|巴黎]][[蒙馬特|蒙馬特]]地區的一座[[風車_(農具)|風車]]建築<ref name=Frommer>{{cite book | title=Frommer's Memorable Walks in Paris| author=Mroue, H | publisher=Wiley Publishing Inc. | location=Hoboken | year=1999 | page=123 | isbn=0-471-77648-3 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=4r9itsvPqyQC&pg=PA123 }}</ref><ref name=Williams>{{cite book | title=Picasso's Paris: walking tours of the artist's life in the city | author=Williams, E | publisher=The Little Bookroom | location=New York | year=1999 | page=19| isbn=0-9641262-7-3 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=vQ63kxbSm4gC&pg=PA19 }}</ref>，自17世紀開始就已經工作。煎餅磨坊現在仍被用於磨製麵粉。煎餅磨坊也是[[煎餅磨坊的舞會|煎餅磨坊的舞會]]等名畫的舞台<ref>[http://usuarios.lycos.es/ramoncasas/obra/pintura/balmoulin_es.htm Bal du Moulin de la Galette – Ramon Casas<!-- bot-generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090121063110/http://usuarios.lycos.es/ramoncasas/obra/pintura/balmoulin_es.htm |date=2009-01-21 }} at usuarios.lycos.es</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.artrenewal.org/artwork/313/2313/10297/moulin_de_la_galette-large.jpg |title=Quinsac's painting ''Moulin de la Galette'' |access-date=2017-12-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121009213041/http://www.artrenewal.org/artwork/313/2313/10297/moulin_de_la_galette-large.jpg |archive-date=2012-10-09 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.christies.com/LotFinder/lot_details.aspx?intObjectID=4654145 |title=Kees van Dongen (1877-1968), Le Moulin de la Galette |year=2011 |work= |publisher=Christie's |accessdate=2011-05-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.christies.com/lotfinder/lot_details.aspx?pos=1&intObjectID=5019942&sid= |title=Maurice Utrillo (1883-1955), Le Moulin de la Galette  |work= |publisher=Christie's |accessdate=2011-05-04}}</ref>。

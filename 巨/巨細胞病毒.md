在人类和[[哺乳动物|哺乳动物]]中，CMV感染一般发生在[[唾液腺|唾液腺]]。<ref name="isbn0-521-82714-0">{{cite book |author=Koichi Yamanishi; Arvin, Ann M.; Gabriella Campadelli-Fiume; Edward Mocarski; Moore, Patrick; Roizman, Bernard; Whitley, Richard |title=Human herpesviruses: biology, therapy, and immunoprophylaxis |publisher=Cambridge University Press |location=Cambridge, UK |year=2007 |pages= |isbn=0-521-82714-0 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref>CMV在健康人身体中通常难以察觉，但有时可能是致命的，<ref>

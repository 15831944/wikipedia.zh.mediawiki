'''靴帶蠕蟲'''（{{lang-en|bootlace worm}}）或稱作'''巨縱溝紐蟲'''（{{lang-en|Lineus longissimus}}），是已知最長的[[無脊椎動物|無脊椎動物]]之一<ref name="Sci-News.com(2018-03-27)">{{cite news|url=http://www.sci-news.com/biology/bootlace-worm-toxin-05852.html|title=Bootlace Worm: Earth’s Longest Animal Produces Powerful Toxin|work=Sci-News.com|date=2018-03-27|accessdate=2018-10-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180327113722/http://www.sci-news.com/biology/bootlace-worm-toxin-05852.html|archive-date=2018-03-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>，其標本報告指出，該蟲長達{{convert|55|m|ft|abbr=on}}<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.uq.edu.au/news/article/2018/03/potential-insecticide-discovered-earth%E2%80%99s-longest-animal|title=Potential insecticide discovered in Earth’s longest animal|work=UQ News|access-date=2018-03-30|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180330211509/https://www.uq.edu.au/news/article/2018/03/potential-insecticide-discovered-earth%E2%80%99s-longest-animal|archive-date=2018-03-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>。其釋放的[[黏液|黏液]]帶有劇毒<ref>Strand, M., Sundberg, P. 2010. ''Lineus longissimus'' Långsnöre, p. 104 - In: ''Nationalnyckeln till Sveriges flora och fauna.'' Stjärnmaskar - slemmaskar. Sipuncula - Nemertea. Artdatabanken, SLU, Uppsala, {{ISBN|978-91-88506-72-6}}</ref>。

==特徵==
巨縱溝紐蟲是已知世上最長的動物之一，但其寬度通常只有{{convert|5|to|10|mm|in}}。該蟲的身體呈深棕色且有（縱向的）細條紋<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.marlin.ac.uk/species/detail/2075|title=MarLIN - The Marine Life Information Network - Bootlace worm (Lineus longissimus)|website=www.marlin.ac.uk|language=en-GB|access-date=2018-03-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180330211745/https://www.marlin.ac.uk/species/detail/2075|archive-date=2018-03-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>。牠的[[黏液|黏液]]具有強力的[[神經毒素|神經毒素]]，可用於對付食肉動物的防禦手段。當牠釋放出其黏液時，會產生微弱的刺激性氣味，聞起來就如同金屬或污水般<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sciencenews.org/article/toxins-bootlace-worm-can-kill-cockroaches|title=Toxins from the world’s longest animal can kill cockroaches|last=Milius|first=Susan|date=2018-03-30|work=Science News|access-date=2018-03-30|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180330120800/https://www.sciencenews.org/article/toxins-bootlace-worm-can-kill-cockroaches|archive-date=2018-03-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>。這種有毒的粘液已被證明足以殺死[[螃蟹|螃蟹]]和[[蟑螂|蟑螂]]，並可做為農業的[[殺蟲劑|殺蟲劑]]使用<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2018/03/180326140226.htm|title=Insect toxin detected in the world's longest animal|work=ScienceDaily|access-date=2018-03-30|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180330211506/https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2018/03/180326140226.htm|archive-date=2018-03-30|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="天天快报(2018-04-02)">{{cite web|url=https://kuaibao.qq.com/s/20180402B113IP00?refer=spider|title=这种世界上最长的动物可以杀死小强|work=天天快报|date=2018-04-02|accessdate=2018-10-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181023115500/https://kuaibao.qq.com/s/20180402B113IP00?refer=spider|archive-date=2018-10-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

在1864年的一場暴風雨而沖上[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]][[圣安德鲁斯|圣安德鲁斯]]海岸的遺體標本指出，該蟲身長超過{{convert|55|m|ft|abbr=on}}<ref>Carwardine, M. 1995. ''The Guinness Book of Animal Records''. Guinness Publishing. p. 232.</ref>，比已知[[最大生物列表|世上最長]]的動物[[獅鬃水母|獅鬃水母]]還長。但因[[紐形動物門|紐形動物門]]的身體柔軟而具延展性，所以巨縱溝紐蟲的最長長度紀錄仍有待商確<ref name="中天(2017-01-29)">{{cite news|url=http://gotv.ctitv.com.tw/2017/01/351540.htm|title=世界上最長動物不是蟒蛇　竟然是「牠」！|work=中天快點TV|date=2017-01-29|accessdate=2018-10-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181024062606/http://gotv.ctitv.com.tw/2017/01/351540.htm|archive-date=2018-10-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

與其他紐形動物門一樣，巨縱溝紐蟲可利用伸出的{{le|吻管|Proboscis}}捕食。其細長的吻管並未帶刺，而是在末端帶有用來固定獵物的黏性細絲<ref name="Sci-News.com(2018-03-27)" />。

==棲息地==
巨縱溝紐蟲能在[[瑞典|瑞典]]的西海岸、[[挪威|挪威]]的海岸及[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]的海岸發現其蹤跡<ref name="天天快报(2018-04-02)" />。

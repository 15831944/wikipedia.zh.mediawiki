'''接收詞彙'''、'''接收性詞彙'''（{{lang-en|Receptive vocabulary}}）即為接收性質的詞彙，乃[[接收語言|接收語言]]的一部分，指的是所有可以被人理解的詞彙；包含口語、以印刷或書寫方式呈現、人為設定的詞彙（manually signed words）。<ref name="Kid Sense Child Development">{{cite web | title=Expressive Language (Using Words and Language) | website=Kid Sense Child Development | url=https://childdevelopment.com.au/areas-of-concern/using-speech/expressive-language-using-words-and-language/ | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref><ref name="Baister">{{cite web | last=Baister | first=Julie | title=What is the difference between receptive vocabulary and expressive vocabulary? | website=Dyslexia Positive | url=http://www.dyslexiapositive.org.uk/discussion/what-is-the-difference-between-receptive-vocabulary-and-expressive-vocabulary/ | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref>

對於閱讀有困難的成人可能存有加強接收詞彙能力的空間。<ref name="Hall Greenberg Laures-Gore Pae pp. S87–S100">{{cite journal | last=Hall | first=Ryan | last2=Greenberg | first2=Daphne | last3=Laures-Gore | first3=Jacqueline | last4=Pae | first4=Hye K. | title=The relationship between expressive vocabulary knowledge and reading skills for adult struggling readers | journal=Journal of research in reading | publisher=Wiley | volume=37 | issue=S1 | date=2012-08-16 | issn=0141-0423 | pmid=24778459 | pmc=3999701 | doi=10.1111/j.1467-9817.2012.01537.x | pages=S87–S100}}</ref>

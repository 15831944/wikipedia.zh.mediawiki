'''接收語言能力'''（{{lang-en|Receptive language skills}}）指的是理解資訊的能力，涵蓋理解[[詞彙|詞彙]]、[[句子|句子]]、他人說的話、所閱讀的文章等。接收語言能力與[[表達語言能力|表達語言能力]]不同。[[接收詞彙|接收詞彙]]是接收語言能力的一部分。<ref name="Afasic – Voice for Life 2016">{{cite web | title=More about speech, language and communication – Afasic | website=Afasic – Voice for Life | date=2016-08-04 | url=https://www.afasic.org.uk/about-talking/more-about-speech-language-and-communication/ | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref><ref name="Kid Sense Child Development">{{cite web | title=Receptive Language (understanding words and language) | website=Kid Sense Child Development | url=https://childdevelopment.com.au/areas-of-concern/understanding-language/receptive-language-understanding-words-and-language/ | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref><ref name="Flack 2018">{{cite web | last=Flack | first=Kirstie | title=Receptive Language | website=cambscommunityservices.nhs.uk | date=2018-08-16 | url=http://www.cambscommunityservices.nhs.uk/what-we-do/children-young-people-health-services-cambridgeshire/specialist-services/childrens-speech-and-language-therapy/activities-ideas-and-info/primary-and-secondary-schools/receptive-language | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref>

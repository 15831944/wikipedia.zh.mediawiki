'''霍洛'''（{{lang-en|Jolo}}、{{lang-tsg|Dāira sin Tiyanggi}}）是[[菲律宾|菲律宾]][[民答那峨穆斯林自治區|民答那峨穆斯林自治區]][[苏禄省|苏禄省]]的首府，位於[[霍洛島|霍洛島]]西北部，歷史上曾是[[蘇祿蘇丹國|蘇祿蘇丹國]]的首都。2015年的人口統計顯示和樂人口為125,564人<ref name=pop/>，其中90%信仰[[伊斯蘭教|伊斯蘭教]]、10%信仰[[基督宗教|基督宗教]]<ref>(2002-08-22). [http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/asia-pacific/2209589.stm "Jolo: Unstable Paradise"]. BBC News World Edition.</ref>。

2019年1月27日，位於霍洛的{{link-en|卡梅爾山聖母大教堂|Our Lady of Mount Carmel Cathedral, Jolo}}發生[[2019年霍洛天主教堂爆炸案|爆炸案]]，造成18-20人死亡，82-111人受傷<ref>{{cite web|url=https://theaseanpost.com/article/deadly-twin-bomb-attacks-philippine-church|title=Deadly twin bomb attacks on Philippine church|work=Agence France-Presse|publisher=The ASEAN Post|date=2019-01-28|accessdate=2019-01-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190128075610/https://theaseanpost.com/article/deadly-twin-bomb-attacks-philippine-church|archive-date=2019-01-28|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="casualties">{{cite web|url=http://www.xinhuanet.com/english/2019-01/27/c_137779207.htm|title=20 dead, 111 wounded in church twin blasts in southern Philippines|author=Shi Yinglun|publisher=Xinhua News Agency|date=2019-01-27|accessdate=2019-01-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190128135623/http://www.xinhuanet.com/english/2019-01/27/c_137779207.htm|archive-date=2019-01-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>。菲律賓國家警察認為該爆炸案的策劃者為[[阿布沙耶夫|阿布沙耶夫]]成員<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/asia/explainer--who-is-behind-the-philippine-church-bombings--11175254|title=Explainer: Who is behind the Philippine church bombings?|publisher=Channel NewsAsia|date=2019-01-28|accessdate=2019-01-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190129090335/https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/asia/explainer--who-is-behind-the-philippine-church-bombings--11175254|archive-date=2019-01-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

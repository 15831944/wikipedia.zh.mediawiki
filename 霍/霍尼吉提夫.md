'''霍尼吉提夫'''（Hornedjitef）是[[古埃及|古埃及]][[托勒密三世|托勒密三世]]时期[[阿蒙神庙|阿蒙神庙]]的一位大祭司。

霍尼吉提夫大祭司的[[木乃伊|木乃伊]]于19世纪出土，制作年代约为公元前220年左右。木乃伊装在一副棺椁之中，其中内棺上雕有代表其神职身份的镀金面具，并刻有记载其生平的铭文。1835年起，霍尼吉提夫木乃伊及其棺木被[[大英博物馆|大英博物馆]]所收藏。2010年，时任大英博物馆馆长[[尼尔·麦格雷戈|尼尔·麦格雷戈]]将其选为大英博物馆《[[一百件文物中的世界史|一百件文物中的世界史]]》的首件馆藏文物。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.britishmuseum.org/explore/highlights/highlight_objects/aes/o/outer_coffin_of_the_priest_h-1.aspx |title=Outer coffin of the priest Hornedjitef |publisher=British Museum |date= |accessdate=2010-06-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100706100551/http://www.britishmuseum.org/explore/highlights/highlight_objects/aes/o/outer_coffin_of_the_priest_h-1.aspx |archivedate=2010-07-06 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.britishmuseum.org/explore/highlights/highlight_objects/aes/i/inner_coffin_of_the_priest_h-1.aspx |title=Mummy of Hornedjitef › The British Museum |publisher=Britishmuseum.org |date= |accessdate=2010-06-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100123011525/http://www.britishmuseum.org/explore/highlights/highlight_objects/aes/i/inner_coffin_of_the_priest_h-1.aspx |archivedate=2010-01-23 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.britishmuseum.org/explore/highlights/highlight_objects/aes/m/mummy_mask_of_hornedjitef.aspx |title=Mummy mask of Hornedjitef |publisher=British Museum |date= |accessdate=2010-06-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100310191642/http://www.britishmuseum.org/explore/highlights/highlight_objects/aes/m/mummy_mask_of_hornedjitef.aspx |archivedate=2010-03-10 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=John Taylor, Curator, British Museum |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/ahistoryoftheworld/objects/sogITE3FSKStlk12qd2W3w |title=A History of the World - Object: Mummy of Hornedjitef |publisher=BBC |date= |accessdate=2010-06-09}}</ref>

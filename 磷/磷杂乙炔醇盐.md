'''磷杂乙炔醇盐'''（化学式：PCO<sup>−</sup>或OCP<sup>−</sup>）是氰酸盐（OCN<sup>−</sup>）的类似物，<ref name="Quan">Quan, Z. J. and Wang, X. C. (2014) ‘The 2-phosphaethynolate anion: Convenient synthesis and the reactivity’, ''Organic Chemistry Frontiers''. [https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2014/qo/c4qo00189c#!divAbstract doi:10.1039/c4qo00189c].</ref>其阴离子具有线性结构，通常分离为盐来使用。这个阴离子也是一种两可（ambidentate）配体，即形成配合物时，配位原子既可以是磷原子，也可以是氧原子。<ref name="Quan"/><ref name="Camp">Camp, C., Settineri, N., Lefèvre, J., Jupp, A. R., Goicoechea, J. M., Maron, L. and Arnold, J. (2015) ‘Uranium and thorium complexes of the phosphaethynolate ion’, ''Chemical Science''. [https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2015/sc/c5sc02150b#!divAbstract doi: 10.1039/c5sc02150b].</ref><ref name="Heift">Heift, D., Benko, Z. and Grützmacher, H. (2014) ‘Is the phosphaethynolate anion, (OCP)-, an ambident nucleophile? A spectroscopic and computational study’, ''Dalton Transactions''. [https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2014/dt/c3dt53569j#!divAbstract doi: 10.1039/c3dt53569j].</ref>这种多功能性让它可以和很多[[过渡金属|过渡金属]]及[[锕系元素|锕系元素]]结合，但目前研究最热门的是用作合成砌块来构建有机磷烷类化合物。<ref name="Heift"/><ref name="JuppUrea">Jupp, A. R., and Goicoechea, J. M. (2013) 'Phosphinecarboxamide: A Phosphorus-Containing Analogue of Urea
and Stable Primary Phosphine', ''J. Am. Chem. Soc.''. [https://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ja4115693 DOI:10.1021/ja4115693].</ref>

磷杂乙炔醇盐最初由Becker等人于1992年合成并表征，<ref name="becker"/>它们通过双(三甲基硅基)锂和碳酸二甲酯的反应以87%的产率分离出锂盐。<ref name="becker">Becker, G., Schwarz, W., Seidler, N. and Westerhausen, M. (1992) ‘Acyl‐ und Alkylidenphosphane. XXXIII. Lithoxy‐methylidenphosphan · DME und ‐methylidinphosphan · 2 DME — Synthese und Struktur’, ''ZAAC ‐ Journal of Inorganic and General Chemistry''. [https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/zaac.19926120113 doi: 10.1002/zaac.19926120113].</ref>
<ref name="review">Grutzmacher, H., and Goicoechea, J. (2018) ‘The chemistry of the 2‐phosphaethynolate anion’, ''Angew. Chem. Int. Ed''. doi: [https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/anie.201803888 10.1002/anie.201803888].</ref>

'''磷酸甘油酸变位酶'''（{{lang-en|Phosphoglycerate mutase}}，'''PGM'''，{{EC编号|5.4.2.11}}）是一种[[催化|催化]][[3-磷酸甘油酸|3-磷酸甘油酸]]（3PG）转变为[[2-磷酸甘油酸|2-磷酸甘油酸]]（2PG）的酶，这个[[化学反应|反应]]是[[糖酵解途径|糖酵解途径]]的第八步，其中间产物是[[2,3-二磷酸甘油酸|2,3-二磷酸甘油酸]]（2,3BPG）反应式如下：<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Johnsen|first1=U|last2=Schönheit|first2=P|title=Characterization of cofactor-dependent and cofactor-independent phosphoglycerate mutases from Archaea.|journal=Extremophiles : life under extreme conditions|date=September 2007|volume=11|issue=5|pages=647–57|pmid=17576516|doi=10.1007/s00792-007-0094-x}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Jedrzejas|first1=MJ|title=Structure, function, and evolution of phosphoglycerate mutases: comparison with fructose-2,6-bisphosphatase, acid phosphatase, and alkaline phosphatase.|journal=Progress in biophysics and molecular biology|date=2000|volume=73|issue=2–4|pages=263–87|pmid=10958932|doi=10.1016/s0079-6107(00)00007-9}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Galperin|first1=MY|last2=Bairoch|first2=A|last3=Koonin|first3=EV|title=A superfamily of metalloenzymes unifies phosphopentomutase and cofactor-independent phosphoglycerate mutase with alkaline phosphatases and sulfatases.|journal=Protein Science|date=August 1998|volume=7|issue=8|pages=1829–35|pmid=10082381|doi=10.1002/pro.5560070819|pmc=2144072}}</ref>

: 3PG + P-Enzyme → 2,3BPG + Enzyme → 2PG + P-Enzyme<br>

'''磷酸银'''，或'''正磷酸银'''，'''原磷酸银'''，是对光敏感的，黄色的，不溶于水的化合物，由[[银|银]]和[[磷酸盐|磷酸根]]离子组成，化学式为Ag<sub>3</sub>PO<sub>4</sub>或Ag<sub>3</sub>O<sub>4</sub>P <ref name="ChemBK">{{cite web|title=原磷酸银|url=http://www.chembk.com/cn/chem/原磷酸银|publisher=化工百科|accessdate=2015-05-28}}</ref>

== 合成，反应和特性 ==
磷酸银作为一种黄色沉淀，可由可溶性银化合物（如[[硝酸银|硝酸银]]）与可溶性[[磷酸盐|磷酸盐]]的反应制备。<ref name="eb">[http://www.1911encyclopedia.org/Phosphates Phosphates] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100828122117/http://www.1911encyclopedia.org/Phosphates |date=2010-08-28 }} ''www.1911encyclopedia.org''</ref>它的[[溶度积|溶度积]]为8.89×10<sup>−17</sup> mol<sup>4</sup>dm<sup>−12</sup>。<ref>[http://www.solubilityofthings.com/water/ions_solubility/ksp_chart.php Ksp solubility constant for common salts] ''www.solubilityofthing.com''</ref><ref>[http://www.ktf-split.hr/periodni/en/abc/kpt.html SOLUBILITY PRODUCT CONSTANTS] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120615020049/http://www.ktf-split.hr/periodni/en/abc/kpt.html |date=2012-06-15 }} ''www.ktf-split.hr''</ref>这个反应沉淀非常显著<ref>Inorganic chemistry , Egon Wiberg, Nils Wiberg, Arnold Frederick Holleman , Academic Press , 2001] p.721 [http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=vEwj1WZKThEC&pg=PA721 Google Books excerpt]</ref>，可用于定性或定量检测磷酸盐。

该化合物溶于硝酸或氨。<ref name="eb"/>随着磷酸银氨溶液中的氨逐渐消失，它也可以形成大的晶体；该反应可用于磷酸根离子的定量分析。<ref name="prec">{{cite journal | title = Precipitation of Silver Phosphate from Homogenous Solution | author = F.H. Firsching | journal = [[Anal._Chem.|Anal. Chem.]] | volume = 33 | pages = 873 | year = 1961 | doi = 10.1021/ac60175a018 | issue = 7 }}</ref>根据不同晶形的磷酸银的准备方法，可以产生出相同的晶格结构。<ref name="prec"/>

== 用途 ==
磷酸银沉淀不仅在[[分析化学|分析化学]]中有重要地位，它也被用在[[银染|银染]]生物材料上（之后将银还原）—作为磷酸盐的指示。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Effects of interleukin-1 and tumor necrosis factor- on osteoblastic expression of osteocalcin and mineralized extracellular matrix in vitro|author=|url=http://deepblue.lib.umich.edu/bitstream/2027.42/44508/1/10753_2004_Article_BF00919342.pdf|last=Taichman|first=Russell S.|last2=Hauschka|first2=Peter V.|journal=Inflammation|publisher=Springer Nature|issue=6|doi=10.1007/bf00919342|others=|year=1992|volume=16|page=|pages=587–601|issn=0360-3997|pmid=}}</ref> 

在早期摄影中，磷酸银也被发现可以作为一种光敏剂使用。<ref>Cassell's cyclopaedia of photography , Bernard Edward Jones , Ayer Publishing , 1973 , p.401 'Phosphate plates and papers' , [http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=pqkR3Po010sC&pg=PA401&lpg=PA401&dq=silver+phosphate&source=bl&ots=dgNMaTZdM9&sig=9hB0eoOYbqdZlNCJdJsrlEilkBE&hl=en&ei=tlFETPztF4Tu0gTjjYyoDw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=10&ved=0CEIQ6AEwCTgy#v=onepage&q=silver%20phosphate&f=false googlebooks link]</ref>

在2010年，有报告称，作为可见光[[光解|分解]]水中的[[光催化剂|光催化剂]]，磷酸银具有高[[量子产率|量子产率]]（90％），也可用于以相同方法生产活性氧。<ref>{{cite journal | last=Yi | first=Zhiguo | last2=Ye | first2=Jinhua | last3=Kikugawa | first3=Naoki | last4=Kako | first4=Tetsuya | last5=Ouyang | first5=Shuxin | last6=Stuart-Williams | first6=Hilary | last7=Yang | first7=Hui | last8=Cao | first8=Junyu | last9=Luo | first9=Wenjun | last10=Li | first10=Zhaosheng | last11=Liu | first11=Yun | last12=Withers | first12=Ray L. | title=An orthophosphate semiconductor with photooxidation properties under visible-light irradiation | journal=Nature Materials | publisher=Springer Nature | volume=9 | issue=7 | date=2010-06-06 | issn=1476-1122 | doi=10.1038/nmat2780 | pages=559–564}}</ref><ref>[http://www.nims.go.jp/eng/news/press/2010/06/p201006070.html Discovery of a Novel High Activity Photocatalyst Material: A Great Step Toward the Realization of Artificial Photosynthesis] Discovery of a Revolutionary Oxidation Property in Silver Phosphate with Quantum Yield of Approximately 90% in Visible Light , 2010/06/07 , press release , National Institute for Materials Science (NIMS) Japan , ''www.nims.go.jp''</ref>

磷酸银也是一种潜在的材料，可将银离子的抗菌性能加入其他材料。<ref>[http://www.nanowerk.com/news/newsid=8997.php Nanocoated film as a bacteria killer] 23/1/2009 , ''www.nanowerk.com''</ref>

== 其他银的磷酸盐 ==
'''焦磷酸银'''（Ag<sub>4</sub>P<sub>2</sub>O<sub>7</sub>）（CAS号：13465-97-9）<ref>[http://www.chemicalbook.com/ChemicalProductProperty_EN_CB3421040.htm Silver pyrophosphate] ''www.chemicalbook.com''</ref>可由银(I)和[[焦磷酸盐|焦磷酸根]]离子生成沉淀制得。就像磷酸银一样，它对光敏感。磷酸银暴露在光线下会变成红色。<ref>[http://www.scribd.com/doc/30122611/Silver-Compounds Silver Compounds] p.5 , section 2.22 , from Kirk-Othmer Encyclopedia of Chemical Technology , Authors: SAMUEL F. ETRIS (The Silver Institute), C. ROBERT CAPPEL (Eastman Kodak Company) , via ''www.scribd.com''</ref>密度为5.306g/cm<sup>3</sup>，熔点为585 °C.<ref name="bul">Bulletin of the National Research Council , National Research Council (U.S.A) , 1950 , pp.56-57 [http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=8SUrAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA57&lpg=PA57&dq=silver+phosphate&source=bl&ots=Nnf8dYoVo0&sig=gF8fUBcr-7e184GIKNCo4-rMOLA&hl=en&ei=81JETKuvB47y0gTm5dybDw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=6&ved=0CDQQ6AEwBThG#v=onepage&q=silver%20phosphate&f=false google books link]</ref>水合物也存在，分解温度为110 °C.<ref name="bul"/>

'''偏磷酸银'''（AgPO<sub>3</sub>）（CAS号：13465-96-8）<ref>[http://www.chemicalbook.com/ChemicalProductProperty_EN_CB0263971.htm Silver metaphosphate] ''www.chemicalbook.com''</ref>是一种白色固体，密度为6.370g/cm<sup>3</sup>，熔点为482 °C。水合物也存在，分解温度为240 °C。<ref name="bul"/>

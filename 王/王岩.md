'''王岩'''（{{bd|1986年|catIdx=W}}），中国东北[[和龙市|和龙市]][[动物福利|动物福利]]活动家。<ref name=lyme>{{cite news|last1=Lyme|first1=Chloe|title=Puppy love: Chinese millionaire alleges that he spent all his fortune saving stray dogs from the slaughterhouse after his beloved pooch. This has not been verified in any way. disappeared|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/peoplesdaily/article-3325353/Chinese-millionaire-spends-fortune-saving-stray-dogs-slaughterhouse.html|accessdate=23 November 2015|work=Mail Online|publisher=Associated Newspapers Ltd|date=19 November 2015}}</ref>他曾是[[钢铁冶金学|钢铁冶金]]业主、百万富翁。他自称2012年他的狗走失使得他致力于救狗。从那年起，他在和龙市建立了救狗中心。2015年他称他救了2,000多条[[流浪犬|流浪犬]]，在中心养了200多条狗。他称他把所有的钱都花在了救狗上，已经破产。<ref name=malay>{{cite news|title=RICHES TO RAGS: Millionaire in China goes into debt to save dogs from slaughterhouse|url=http://www.malaysia-chronicle.com/index.php?option=com_k2&view=item&id=607189:riches-to-rags-millionaire-in-china-goes-into-debt-to-save-dogs-from-slaughterhouse&Itemid=4#axzz3sHDQQA2a|accessdate=23 November 2015|work=Malaysia Chronicle|date=23 November 2015}}</ref>

== 活动 ==
王岩称大约2005年中国的钢铁工业有所改善时开始，他的生意开始成功。王岩说，当他的狗丢了后，他数次调查无果，有人建议他去城里的[[屠房|屠房]]看看。他在屠房查看了一星期，无果。<ref name=curt>{{cite news|last1=Curtis|first1=Kieron|title=Millionaire Goes Broke Saving Hundreds Of Dogs From Slaughterhouse|url=http://www.unilad.co.uk/news/millionaire-animal-lover-goes-broke-in-bid-to-save-hundreds-of-dogs/|accessdate=23 November 2015|work=UNILAD|date=20 November 2015}}</ref>

反而，王岩坚称他对骇人听闻的屠宰行为的观察使他开始为动物福利工作。在市场里，[[狗肉|狗肉]]被出售，它们主要来自流浪犬。杀狗取肉是一项传统生意，中国以狗肉闻名。甚至有个[[玉林荔枝狗肉节|玉林荔枝狗肉节]]，每年消费10,000-15,000条狗。<ref name=lyme/>

[[山东省|山东省]][[济南|济南]]的大杨新区政府甚至试图杀光包括有证宠物在内的狗。2015年9月，该政府下令，“任何人不准养各种犬，包括有证无证犬，否则村委组织人入户当场将犬砸死及处理。”<ref name=chao>{{cite news|last1=Chao-Fong|first1=Léonie|title=Millionaire goes broke building dog shelter to save thousands from the slaughterhouse|url=http://metro.co.uk/2015/11/19/millionaire-goes-broke-building-dog-shelter-to-save-thousands-from-the-slaughterhouse-5514031/|accessdate=23 November 2015|work=Metro|date=19 November 2015}}</ref>5,000多条狗被消灭。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Siddiqui|first1=Sheena|title=Chinese Millionaire Spends His Entire Fortune on Rescuing Stray Dogs From Being Slaughtered|url=http://baysidejournal.com/wp/chinese-millionaire-spends-his-entire-fortune-on-rescuing-stray-dogs-from-being-slaughtered/|accessdate=23 November 2015|work=The Bayside Journal|date=November 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151123110159/http://baysidejournal.com/wp/chinese-millionaire-spends-his-entire-fortune-on-rescuing-stray-dogs-from-being-slaughtered/|archive-date=2015-11-23|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

王岩买下了屠房和[[长春|长春]]和龙市的一家废弃厂房，发展为用于流浪犬的救助中心。他给它命名为“长春小动物救助基地”。<ref>{{cite news|title=Millionaire becomes pauper saving hundreds of dogs|url=http://english.sina.com/china/2015/1118/866278.html|accessdate=27 November 2015|work=SINA English|date=9 November 2015}}</ref>他声称已经将其扩建到一次可至少容纳1000条狗。<ref name=curt/>他称开始自费无收益地买狗粮和药。他称他已经花费了3,000,000多元（$470,186）。<ref>{{cite news|title=Man spends millions to save 2,000 dogs from slaughter|url=https://gbtimes.com/man-spends-millions-save-2000-dogs-slaughter|accessdate=23 November 2015|work=GBTimes|date=19 November 2015}}</ref>他说他说服人们收养他的流浪犬，但不接受对他的捐款。他只想要狗粮作为赠品。他在2015年11月的一场访问中说：

{{Quote|我不接受货币捐赠。我只希望善良的人们能够捐赠一些物资帮助为这200只狗建立一个家。<ref name=curt/>}}

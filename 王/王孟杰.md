'''王孟杰'''{{NoteTag|1=中文名参考自[[BBC中文网|BBC中文网]]报道《[http://www.bbc.com/zhongwen/simp/world-43138334 佛州校园枪击案：西点军校追授录取华裔遇难学生王孟杰]》。}}（{{Lang-en|'''Peter Wang'''}}，{{bd|2002年|11月9日|2018年|2月14日|catIdx=Wang}}）是[[美国|美国]]{{Link-en|初级预备军官训练团|Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps}}一名学员，同时他也是[[2018年佛罗里达校园枪击案|2018年佛罗里达校园枪击案]]中罹难的{{Link-en|玛乔里·斯通曼·道格拉斯中学|Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School}}学生<ref name="DiazSS150218">{{cite news|last1=Diaz|first1=Johnny|title=Peter Wang, Florida school shooting victim: Brave teen seen holding door open for others during shooting|url=http://www.sun-sentinel.com/local/broward/parkland/florida-school-shooting/fl-florida-school-shooting-peter-wang-obit-20180215-story.html|accessdate=2018-02-15|work=Sun Sentinel|date=2018-02-15}}</ref>。王孟杰出生于[[美国|美国]][[纽约市|纽约市]]的[[布鲁克林|布鲁克林区]]，而在[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]]的[[布劳沃德县|布劳沃德县]]成长，他在去世时还是一名[[高中|高中]]新生。

在玛乔里·斯通曼·道格拉斯中学枪击案发生的时候，王孟杰帮助同学打开一个出口以逃避枪击者的追杀<ref name="Cardona150218">{{cite news|last1=Cardona|first1=Alexi|title=Peter Wang: Online petition for full military burial for JROTC student who 'died a hero'|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/crime/2018/02/18/peter-wang-online-petition-died-hero-helping-students-stoneman-douglas-asks-military-funeral/349973002/|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=USA Today|date=2018-02-15}}</ref>。为表彰王孟杰的行为，初级预备军官训练团追授其{{Link-en|预备军官训练团英勇勋章|ROTC Medal for Heroism}}；同时美国[[西点军校|西点军校]]以其行为彰显该校所倡导信条为由而极其罕见地破例在王孟杰身后录取他<ref name="wapo-svrluga-2018">{{cite news |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/grade-point/wp/2018/02/20/heroic-west-point-admits-a-student-who-was-killed-in-the-parkland-shooting/ |title=‘Heroic’: West Point admits a student who was killed in the Parkland shooting |first=Susan |last=Svrluga |date=2018-02-20 |newspaper=The Washington Post }}</ref>。

== 生平 ==
王孟杰于2002年11月9日出生在[[美国|美国]][[纽约市|纽约市]]的[[布鲁克林|布鲁克林]]，<ref>{{cite web|title=In Memory of Peter Wang|url=http://obits.dignitymemorial.com/dignity-memorial/obituary.aspx?n=Peter-Wang&lc=7089&pid=188193080&mid=7763159|publisher=Kraeer Funeral Home and Cremation Center|accessdate=2018-02-21}}</ref> 他的父母是[[美籍华裔|美籍华裔]]餐厅业主，他还有两个年幼的弟弟。<ref name="CBSNY190218">{{cite news|title=Friends Want Military Honors For JROTC Cadet Killed In Florida Massacre|url=http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2018/02/19/jrotc-petition/|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=CBS New York|date=2018-02-19}}</ref> 他在[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]]的[[布劳沃德县|布劳沃德县]]长大，并于学龄前到二年级的时候就读于[[迈阿密花园_(佛罗里达州)|迈阿密花园市]]的一所名为“笔板学院（{{lang|en|Pentab Academy}}）”私立学校。<ref name="DiazSS150218"/> 之所以在这里就读的原因是当时王孟杰的父母在学校对面拥有一家餐厅。

后来王孟杰转学进入韦斯切斯特小学（{{lang|en|Westchester Elementary School}}）和索格拉斯温泉中学（{{lang|en|Sawgrass Springs Middle School}}）继续接受教育，这两所学校是{{Link-en|布劳沃德县公立学校|Broward County Public Schools}}的组成部分。<ref name="Harris190218">{{cite news|last1=Harris|first1=Alex|title=Peter Wang, who died a ‘hero’ in the Parkland shooting, remembered as brave and funny|url=http://www.miamiherald.com/news/local/community/broward/article200992699.html|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=Miami Herald|date=2018-02-19}}</ref>

2017年，王孟杰在{{Link-en|玛乔里·斯通曼·道格拉斯中学|Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School}}开始了其高中新生生涯，同时他也成为了该校{{Link-en|初级预备军官训练团|Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps}}的一名活跃学员。在[[2018年佛罗里达校园枪击案|2018年枪击案]]中，王孟杰和其他几名初级预备军官训练团学员都在事件中罹难。<ref name="Rozsa190218">{{cite news|last1=Rozsa|first1=Lori|title=‘Selfless service’: JROTC remembers three slain in Florida high school shooting|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/selfless-service-jrotc-remembers-three-slain-in-florida-high-school-shooting/2018/02/19/1e3138f2-158b-11e8-8b08-027a6ccb38eb_story.html?utm_term=.065aca394bd7|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=Washington Post|date=2018-02-19}}</ref>

王孟杰的邻居杰西·潘（{{lang|en|Jesse Pan}}）表示王孟杰正计划考取[[西点军校|西点军校]]并“为国奉献”。<ref name="DiazSS150218"/>

王孟杰会说[[英语|英语]]和[[汉语|汉语]]。<ref name="Harris190218"/>

== 死讯葬礼 ==
{{See|2018年佛罗里达校园枪击案}}

2018年2月14日，19岁的{{Link-en|玛乔里·斯通曼·道格拉斯中学|Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School}}学生尼可拉斯·克鲁兹（{{lang|en|Nikolas Cruz}}）疑似在启动校内火警报警设备之后，开始持枪在校园内射击。此时王孟杰正在校内的学习大厅里，而大厅的门锁被密码锁死。<ref name="Harris190218"/> 据报道，王孟杰当时身着{{Link-en|初级预备军官训练团|Junior Reserve Officers' Training Corps}}制服。当他听到枪声后，他想办法打开了大门并协助其他师生逃离。.<ref name="NBC6150218">{{cite news|title=These Are the Lives Lost in the Parkland High School Shooting|url=https://www.nbcmiami.com/news/national-international/Parkland-Douglas-High-School-Shooting-Victims-474179453.html|work=NBC 6 South Florida|date=2018-02-15}}</ref> 而他被随后赶来的枪手射中，后被送医。起初他家人被告知王孟杰失踪，但随后获知王孟杰在枪击案中伤重不治。<ref name="Huss180218">{{cite news|last1=Hussain|first1=Selima|title=Petition: JROTC Cadet Killed In Stoneman Shooting Deserves Full Honors Military Burial|url=https://www.nbcmiami.com/news/local/Petition-JROTC-Cadet-Killed-In-Stoneman-Shooting-Deserves-Full-Honors-Military-Burial-474417123.html|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=NBC 6 South Florida|date=2018-02-18}}</ref> 王孟杰罹难时得年15岁。2月20日，王孟杰的葬礼在[[科勒爾斯普林斯|科勒爾斯普林斯]]举行。<ref name="Bon190218">{{cite news|last1=Bonvillian|first1=Crystal|title=Petition seeks military burial for JROTC cadet who died saving fellow students|url=http://www.wsbtv.com/news/trending-now/petition-seeks-military-burial-for-jrotc-cadet-who-died-saving-fellow-students/703108977|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=WSB-TV|date=2018-02-19}}</ref><ref name="Ham200218">{{cite news|last1=Plasencia|first1=Amanda|last2=Hamacher|first2=Brian|title=Loved Ones Hold Funeral Services for Parkland Victim Peter Wang, Await Word on Military Burial|url=https://www.nbcmiami.com/news/local/Loved-Ones-Hold-Funeral-Services-for-Parkland-Victim-Peter-Wang-Await-Word-on-Military-Burial-474598173.html|accessdate=2018-02-21|work=NBC News|date=2018-02-20}}</ref>

== 身后影响 ==
王孟杰去世后，他的朋友、家人和老师都谈及了他。有人形容他在[[2018年佛罗里达校园枪击案|2018年佛罗里达校园枪击案]]中的行为表现堪称英勇。<ref name="Bars180218">{{cite news|last1=Barszewski|first1=Larry|title=Peter Wang: Petition seeks full honors military funeral for Douglas JROTC cadet who 'died a hero'|url=http://www.sun-sentinel.com/local/broward/parkland/florida-school-shooting/fl-florida-school-schooting-weng-petition-military-funeral-20180218-story.html|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=Sun Sentinel|date=2018-02-18}}</ref> 而玛乔里·斯通曼·道格拉斯中学的历史老师厄尼·露史庇爱尔史奇（{{lang|en|Ernie Rospierski}}）说道：
{{Cquote|……我亲眼目睹了我的一个学生，他叫王孟杰，当子弹朝着他飞来的时候，他却撑着门来帮助其他人撤离。我不知道有多少成年人能有他那样的勇气，而他却还只有15岁。<ref name="Morgan190218">{{cite news|last1=Morgan|first1=David|title=Heroic Florida teacher: "There's no point to somebody having an AR-15"|url=https://www.cbsnews.com/news/hero-parkland-teacher-ernie-rospierski-no-point-to-somebody-having-ar-15/|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=CBS News|date=2018-02-19}}</ref>}}

许多美国人民在[[我们人民|我们人民]][[请愿|请愿]]，希望[[美军|美军]]能够因为王孟杰在枪击案中为同学和老师打开门的英勇行为给予他完全军事荣誉制的葬礼。<ref name="Willingham190218">{{cite news|last1=Willingham|first1=AJ|title=People are calling for this Florida shooting victim to be buried with military honors|url=https://www.cnn.com/2018/02/19/us/peter-wang-parkland-shooting-jrotc-burial-trnd/index.html|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=CNN|date=2018-02-19}}</ref> “他的无私和英勇行为使得十余人幸存下来，王孟杰的死像一名英雄，他值得受到这样的待遇，他值得享有完全军事荣誉制的葬礼。”请愿书中如是写道。<ref name="Willingham190218"/> 目前该份请愿书已经收获超过10万份签名，按例应该得到[[白宫|白宫]]的回应。<ref name="Pusa210218">{{cite news|last1=Pusatory|first1=Matt|title=Westpoint admits Parkland JROTC student who died saving classmates|url=http://www.khq.com/story/37561595/westpoint-admits-parkland-jrotc-student-who-died-saving-classmates|accessdate=2018-02-22|work=KHQ Local News|date=2018-02-21}}</ref> 但是鉴于[[唐纳德·特朗普|特朗普总统]]自上任以来就从未回应过这些网络请愿，因此尚不得知这份请愿书是否会得到回复。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Nelson|first1=Steven|title=White House reopens petition website that promises official responses|url=http://www.washingtonexaminer.com/white-house-reopens-petition-website-that-promises-official-responses/article/2647796|accessdate=2018-02-20|work=The Washington Examiner|date=2018-02-21}}</ref>

2018年11月22日，其生前同学凯尔·卡舒夫（Kyle Kashuv）再次在[[我们人民|我们人民]]请愿，希望能授予其[[总统自由勋章|总统自由勋章]]。截止至12月16日，该请愿仅获得3万签名，与其目标——至12月22日获得10万签名——相去甚远。<ref>{{cite web | url=https://petitions.whitehouse.gov/petition/award-presidential-medal-freedom-parkland-shooting-hero-and-victim-peter-wang-who-died-saving-others | title=Award the Presidential Medal of Freedom to Parkland Shooting HERO and victim, Peter Wang, who died saving others | trans-title=授予帕克兰枪击案中为救他人而牺牲的英雄王孟杰总统自由勋章 | publisher=我们人民 | date=2018-11-22 | accessdate=2018-12-16 | language=en}}</ref>

== 所获奖励 ==
[[File:Peter_Wang_Certificate_of_Appointment.jpg|thumb]]追授给王孟杰的录取通知书，将录取其成为2025届的新生。]]

王孟杰在枪击案中的英勇表现得到了美国上下的表彰。在联邦政府层面，他被[[美国陆军|美国陆军]]追授{{Link-en|预备军官训练团英勇勋章|ROTC Medal for Heroism}}；<ref name="BBC022118">{{cite news|title=Florida shooting: West Point admits murdered hero|url=http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-43132215|accessdate=2018-02-21|work=BBC|date=2018-02-20}}</ref> 同时他还被[[西点军校|西点军校]]追认为2025届学生，这是一个“非常罕见”的行为。<ref name="wapo-svrluga-2018" /> 此外，佛罗里达州州政府下令佛罗里达州国民警卫队为王孟杰主持葬礼。<ref name="Pan210218">{{cite news|last1=Panzino|first1=Charlsy|title=Army honors three JROTC cadets killed in Florida shooting|url=https://www.armytimes.com/news/your-army/2018/02/20/army-honors-three-jrotc-cadets-killed-in-florida-shooting/|accessdate=2018-02-21|work=ArmyTimes|date=2018-02-21}}</ref><ref name="Sie200218">{{cite news|last1=Siemaszko|first1=Corky|title=West Point military academy admits Parkland student Peter Wang who died saving classmates in mass shooting|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/west-point-military-academy-admits-parkland-student-peter-wang-who-n849721|accessdate=2018-02-21|work=NBC News|date=2018-02-20}}</ref>

关于追认王孟杰学生资格一事，西点军校表示“这是本校向这位勇敢年轻人致敬的恰当表示”。<ref name="Neuman210218">{{cite news|last1=Neuman|first1=Scott|title=Army Awards Medal For Heroism To 3 JROTC Cadets Killed In Florida Shooting  Facebook  Twitter  Flipboard  Email|url=https://www.npr.org/sections/thetwo-way/2018/02/21/587496899/army-awards-medal-for-heroism-to-3-jrotc-cadets-killed-in-florida-shooting|accessdate=2018-02-21|work=NPR|date=2018-02-21}}</ref><ref name="USMAPRPW">{{cite web|title=USMA Press Release on Peter Wang|url=https://www.usma.edu/news/Shared%20Documents/Statement%20Marjory%20Stoneman%20Douglas%20High%20School%20Student.docx.pdf|publisher=United States Military Academy|accessdate=2018-02-21}}</ref>

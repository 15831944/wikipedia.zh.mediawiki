'''王州迪'''<ref name="明報151105"/> {{post-nominals|country=CAN|PC|QC|MP}} （{{lang|en|Jody Wilson-Raybould}}，{{bd|1971年|3月23日|||catIdx=Wilson-Raybould, Jody}}），[[加拿大|加拿大]][[夸夸嘉夸族|夸夸嘉夸族]]的政治人物和律師，2015年起擔任[[加拿大國會下議院|加拿大國會下議院]]議員，代表[[溫哥華固蘭湖選區|溫哥華固蘭湖選區]]，並於2015年[[加拿大自由黨|加拿大自由黨]]上台執政後晉身[[加拿大内閣|聯邦内閣]]成為[[加拿大司法部|司法部長兼檢察總長]]，為首位出任該職的[[加拿大原住民|加拿大原住民]]，2019年則調任退伍軍人事務部長。從政前她曾任[[卑詩省|卑詩省]]檢控官、卑詩條約委員會委員及卑詩原住民議會地區酋長。

== 早年生活及事業 ==
王州迪在溫哥華綜合醫院誕生，父親比爾·威爾遜（{{lang|en|Bill Wilson}}）為卑詩原住民政治人物<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/british-columbia/old-video-of-pierre-trudeau-and-bill-wilson-foreshadows-political-success-of-kids-1.3305538?cmp=rss |title=Old Video of Pierre Trudeau and Bill Wilson Foreshadows Political Success of Kids  |author=Karen Larsen |publisher=[[加拿大廣播公司|CBC News]] |date=2015-11-05 |accessdate=2016-02-05}}</ref>，母親桑德拉（{{lang|en|Sandra Wilson}}）則為一名教師<ref name="CBC151104">{{cite news |url= http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/british-columbia/justice-minister-jody-wilson-raybould-1.3303609 |title=B.C. MP Jody Wilson-Raybould named justice minister |publisher=[[加拿大廣播公司|CBC News]] |date=2015-11-04 |accessdate=2016-02-05}}</ref>。王州迪擁有[[夸夸嘉夸族|夸夸嘉夸族]]血統，為該族旗下威懷凱族國（We Wai Kai Nation）一員，其夸夸嘉夸語名稱「Puglaas」意思為「擁有貴族血統的女人」<ref name="明報151105">{{cite news |url= http://www.mingpaocanada.com/van/htm/News/20151105/vaa4_r.htm |title=三名卑詩閣員小檔案 |publisher=《[[明報|明報]]》加西版 |date=2015-11-05 |accessdate=2016-02-05}}</ref><ref name="CBC151104"/>。她童年先後於[[溫哥華|溫哥華]]和科莫克斯（{{lang|en|Comox}}）居住<ref>{{cite news|last=Farrell|first=Terry|date=November 4, 2015|title=Rachel Blaney represents diversity in Parliament|url=http://www.comoxvalleyrecord.com/news/340262361.html|newspaper=Comox Valley Record|access-date=February 5, 2016|quote=''Liberal MP Jody Wilson-Raybould, who won the Vancouver Granville seat, has North Island roots. She is a member of the  We Wai Kai Nation (Cape Mudge) of Quadra Island and went to middle (Robb Road) and high school (Highland) in Comox.''}}</ref>。

她於[[維多利亞大學_(加拿大)|維多利亞大學]]修讀政治學和歷史，1996年獲得文學士學位<ref>{{cite web |url=https://ring.uvic.ca/news/three-uvic-alumnae-new-federal-cabinet |title=Three UVic alumnae in new federal cabinet |publisher=[[維多利亞大學_(加拿大)|維多利亞大學]] |date=2015-11-04 |accessdate=2016-02-05 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160206131337/https://ring.uvic.ca/news/three-uvic-alumnae-new-federal-cabinet |archivedate=2016-02-06 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>，後來到[[卑詩大學|卑詩大學]]修讀法律並於1999年畢業<ref name="CBC151104"/><ref>{{cite web |url= http://news.ubc.ca/2015/11/04/ubc-alumnus-justin-trudeau-sworn-in-as-canadas-23rd-prime-minister/ |title=UBC alumnus Justin Trudeau sworn in as Canada's 23rd prime minister |publisher=[[卑詩大學|卑詩大學]] |date=2015-11-04 |accessdate=2016-02-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.allard.ubc.ca/news-events/ubc-law-news/canada%E2%80%99s-newest-attorney-general |title=Canada's Newest Attorney General |publisher=[[卑詩大學|卑詩大學]]法學院 |date=2015-11-05 |accessdate=2016-02-05}}</ref>。她於2000年獲得卑詩省律師資格，同年成為卑詩省檢控官<ref name="CBC151104"/>。2003年，她成為卑詩條約委員會的顧問，再於翌年獲選為該組織的其中一名委員<ref name="CBC151104"/>；該組織專責監督省政府與原住民族之間的條約談判程序。2009年，她先後當選威懷凱族國議會議員及卑詩原住民議會地區酋長<ref name="CBC151104"/>，再於2012年連任地區酋長職位<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bramham|first1=Daphne|title=Raised to lead, Wilson-Raybould units B.C. chiefs|url=http://www.vancouversun.com/life/Daphne+Bramham+Raised+lead+Wilson+Raybould+unites+chiefs/7624509/story.html|accessdate=15 July 2015|publisher=Vancouver Sun|date=10 December 2012}}</ref>。

== 聯邦政壇 ==
2013年加拿大原住民發起「不再懈怠」運動（{{lang|en|Idle No More}}）以捍衛原住民權益，期間王州迪聯同國内其他原住民領袖與時任[[加拿大總理|總理]][[史提芬·哈珀|哈珀]]及其他内閣官員會面。她有感當時的[[加拿大保守黨|保守黨]]聯邦政府無視在場原住民領袖的意見，因此生了加入聯邦政壇的念頭<ref name="CTV150710">{{cite news|title=Idle No More talks influenced former chief's decision to run for Liberals |last1=Kirkup |first1=Kristy |url=http://www.ctvnews.ca/politics/idle-no-more-talks-influenced-former-chief-s-decision-to-run-for-liberals-1.2462841 |accessdate=2016-02-05|agency=加新社|publisher=CTV News|date=2015-07-10}}</ref><ref name="CBC151030">{{cite news |url= http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/aboriginals-liberal-wilson-raybould-1.3296163 |title=Jody Wilson-Raybould says Liberals will make 'true reconciliation' with Aboriginal Peoples |last1=Kirkup|first1=Kristy|agency=加新社|publisher=[[加拿大廣播公司|CBC News]] |date=2015-10-30 |accessdate=2016-02-05}}</ref>。同年她在[[白馬市_(加拿大)|白馬市]]出席加拿大原住民議會周年大會時與[[加拿大自由黨|自由黨]]黨魁[[賈斯汀·杜魯多|杜魯多]]碰面，在他游說下決定代表該黨參與[[2015年加拿大國會下議院選舉|2015年聯邦大選]]<ref name="CTV150710"/>。她於2014年7月在無對手競爭的情況下獲取[[溫哥華固蘭湖選區|溫哥華固蘭湖選區]]的自由黨候選人資格<ref name="CBC140731">{{cite news |url= http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/jody-wilson-raybould-b-c-aboriginal-leader-to-run-for-liberals-in-vancouver-1.2724335 |title=Jody Wilson-Raybould, B.C. aboriginal leader, to run for Liberals in Vancouver |publisher=[[加拿大廣播公司|CBC News]] |date=2014-07-31 |accessdate=2016-02-05}}</ref>，並於2015年6月離任卑詩原住民議會地區酋長<ref>{{cite book|title=Resolution 01/2014: Transition of Regional Chief Jody Wilson-Raybould|date=9 September 2014|publisher=BCAFN 11th Annual General Meeting|accessdate=16 July 2015}}</ref>。

在2015年10月舉行的聯邦大選，王州迪以44%得票率贏取溫哥華固蘭湖選區議席，首度晉身[[加拿大國會下議院|國會下議院]]<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/british-columbia/liberal-mp-elect-jody-wilson-raybould-talks-aboriginal-issues-1.3282730|title = Liberal MP-elect Jody Wilson-Raybould talks aboriginal issues|last = |first = |date = 21 October 2015|work = CBC News|access-date = 22 October 2015|via = }}</ref>。她於同年11月獲總理杜魯多委任為[[加拿大司法部|聯邦司法部長兼檢察總長]]<ref name="CBC151104"/><ref>{{Cite news|url =http://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/full-list-of-justin-trudeau-s-cabinet-1.3300699|title = Full list of Justin Trudeau's cabinet|last = |first = |date = 4 November 2015|work = CBC News|access-date = 4 November 2015|via = }}</ref>，2016年1月成為[[御用大律師|御用大律師]]<ref>{{Cite web|url =http://www.pco-bcp.gc.ca/oic-ddc.asp?lang=eng&txtToDate=&txtPrecis=counsellor&Page=&txtOICID=&txtAct=&txtBillNo=&txtFromDate=&txtDepartment=&txtChapterNo=&txtChapterYear=&rdoComingIntoForce=&DoSearch=Search+%2F+List&viewattach=31704&blnDisplayFlg=1|title = Appointment of THE HONOURABLE JODY WILSON-RAYBOULD as Queen's Counsellor|date = 2016-01-26|work = [[加拿大樞密院|加拿大樞密院]]辦公室|accessdate = 2017-08-24}}</ref>。

=== 司法部長 ===
王州迪就任司法部長半年内在國會引入議案，就[[安樂死|安樂死]]進行立法。在時任衞生部長費普真（{{lang|en|Jane Philpott}}）協助下，王州迪在下議院提出C-14號法案，2016年5月31日獲下議院通過<ref>{{Cite news|url =https://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/assisted-dying-vote-third-reading-1.3609647|title = MPs send assisted dying bill to Senate as C-14 deadline looms|last =Zimonjic |first =Peter |date = 2016-05-31|work = CBC News|access-date = 2019-01-18|via = }}</ref>，同年6月16日被上議院加入七項修正案後獲該院通過並送回下議院<ref>{{Cite news|url =https://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/assisted-dying-c14-senate-amendments-1.3638240|title = Assisted dying bill: Senate sends C-14 back to House with 7 amendments|last =Bryden|first =Joan|date = 2016-06-16|work = Canadian Press|access-date = 2019-01-18|via = CBC News}}</ref>。下議院稍為修定原有法案後將之呈交上議院，並於同月17日獲該院通過並獲取[[御准|御准]]<ref>{{Cite news|url =https://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/assisted-dying-bill-senate-approval-1.3640195|title = Liberals' assisted-dying bill is now law after clearing final hurdles|last =Tunney|first =Catharine |date = 2016-06-17|work = CBC News|access-date = 2019-01-18|via = }}</ref>，在特定情況下進行安樂死自此合法。

王州迪又於2016年5月在下議院提出C-16號法案，建議修定加拿大人權法，禁止以他人的性別認同或性別表達為由而作出歧視，並修定加拿大刑法以擴充防範仇恨宣傳的保障。法案於2017年6月19日獲取御准<ref>{{Cite web|url =http://www.parl.ca/LegisInfo/BillDetails.aspx?Language=E&billId=8269852|title = House Government Bill C-16 (42-1)|work = [[加拿大國會|加拿大國會]]|accessdate = 2019-01-18}}</ref>。

[[大麻合法化|大麻合法化]]為自由黨2015年大選的政綱之一，為了落實該項政綱，王州迪於2017年4月在下議院提出C-45號法案（即大麻法）。法案於2018年6月獲國會兩院通過<ref>{{citation|title=Canada Moves One Step Closer To Marijuana Legalization – Thanks to the overwhelming support in the House of Commons, Canada moves one step closer to marijuana legalization.|author=Chloé Harper Gold |date= November 28, 2017 |url=https://hightimes.com/news/canada-moves-one-step-closer-marijuana-legalization/}}</ref><ref>{{citation|title=Canada Senate to Vote on Marijuana Legalization: Canada's Senate is expected to vote on Bill C-45, the Cannabis Act, on Thursday evening, which would make Canada the first G7 nation to legalize marijuana.|author=Tala Salem|date=June 6, 2018|publisher=US News|url=https://www.usnews.com/news/national-news/articles/2018-06-07/canada-senate-to-vote-on-marijuana-legalization}}</ref><ref>{{citation|title=Trudeau says pot will be legal as of Oct. 17, 2018|publisher=CBC News|date=June 20, 2018|url=https://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/cannabis-pot-legalization-bill-1.4713839}}</ref>，大麻的娛樂用途和個人種植則於同年10月17日正式合法化，加拿大遂成為全球第二個允許大麻娛樂用途的國家<ref>{{Cite news|url =https://www.cnn.com/2018/06/20/health/canada-legalizes-marijuana/index.html|title = Canada becomes second nation in the world to legalize marijuana|last =Sapra|first =Bani|date = 2018-06-20|work = CNN|access-date = 2019-01-18|via = }}</ref>。

=== 退伍軍人事務部長 ===
總理杜魯多於2019年1月14日重組內閣，王州迪調任退伍軍人事務部長<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/liberal-cabinet-shuffle-2019-1.4976936|title=David Lametti named Canada's new justice minister in Liberal cabinet shuffle |last=Harris|first=Kathleen|date=2019-01-14|work=CBC News|language=en|access-date=2019-01-14}}</ref>，但於不足一個月後請辭。

===回歸國會後座===
{{main|賈斯汀·杜魯多#司法干预丑闻}}
在請辭後一日，王州迪卻獲淮參與內閣會議，被指是與政府涉貪醜聞有關；之後她在2月27日於下議院司法委員會作供時指控，在仼司法部長和檢察總長、處理著名建築公司SNC-Lavalin涉賄案時，受到杜魯多等人壓力、被要求取消調查<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hk.on.cc/hk/bkn/cnt/amenews/20190228/mobile/bkn-20190228104608065-0228_00972_001.html|title=干預司法風波案　王州迪供稱受杜魯多不當施壓|date=2019-02-1.28|work=東方日報東網|language=zh|access-date=2019-02-28}}</ref>。2019年4月2日，[[王州迪|王州迪]]和{{en-link|简·菲尔波特|Jane Philpott}}被特鲁多宣布逐出[[加拿大國會|议会]][[加拿大自由党|自由党]]党团<ref>[http://world.huanqiu.com/exclusive/2019-04/14658640.html?agt=15422 加拿大总理特鲁多“深夜大屠杀”，将两名“倒戈”前部长扫地出门]</ref>，並被禁止代表自由黨參加2019年大選。

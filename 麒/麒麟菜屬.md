'''麒麟菜屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Eucheuma'''''}}）是[[紅藻門|紅藻門]][[紅藻綱|紅藻綱]][[杉藻目|杉藻目]]'''麒麟菜族'''（{{lang|la|'''''Eucheumatoideae'''''}}）之下的唯一一個屬，是化粧品及食品生產工業的重要原料[[卡拉胶|卡拉胶]]生產的主要原材料，亦是在[[印尼|印尼]]及[[菲律賓|菲律賓]]居民的重要食物來源<ref name="Neish">{{cite journal|last=Neish|first=I.C.|title=The ABC of Eucheuma Seaplant Production |journal=SuriaLink Infomedia|year=2003|series=Seaplants|language=en}}</ref><ref name="Trono, 2011">{{cite web|last=Trono|first=Gavino C Jr|title=Cultured Aquatic Species Information Programme Eucheuma spp.|url=http://www.fao.org/fishery/culturedspecies/Eucheuma_spp/en|work=Aquaculture Management and Conservation Service ([[FIMA|FIMA]]) |publisher=FAO|accessdate=2011-09-29 |language=en }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Handbook on Eucheuma Seaweed cultivation in Fiji|url=http://www.fao.org/docrep/field/003/AC287E/AC287E01.htm|work=FAO Corporate Document Repository|publisher=FAO|accessdate=011-09-29 |language=en }}</ref>。

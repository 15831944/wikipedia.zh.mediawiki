'''復甦安妮'''或'''{{lang|en|CPR}}安妮'''（{{lang-en|'''Resusci Anne'''}}、{{lang|en|'''Rescue Anne'''}}、{{lang|en|'''Resusci Annie'''}}或{{lang|en|'''CPR Annie'''}}）是[[心肺復甦術|心肺復甦術]]訓練時使用的人體模型。復甦安妮是由挪威玩具製造商人{{link-no|奧斯蒙·萊達爾|Åsmund S. Lærdal}}、捷克裔奧地利醫師{{link-en|彼得·沙法|Peter Safar}}與美國醫師{{link-en|詹姆士·埃蘭|James Elam}}研發<ref name = "Science Heroes">{{cite web | title = Elam, James: Science Heroes | url = http://www.scienceheroes.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=346&Itemid=292 | website = scienceheros.com | accessdate = 2018-03-30 | language = en}}</ref><ref name = "BBC">{{cite news | title = Resusci Anne and L'Inconnue: The Mona Lisa of the Seine | first = Jeremy | last = Grange | publisher = [[BBC新聞|BBC]] | url = http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/magazine-24534069 | date = 2013-10-16 |accessdate = 2018-03-30 | language = en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = Annie...Annie.. Are You OK? Resusci Anne | first = Mark | last = Peck | publisher = The Virtual EMS Museum | url = http://www.emsmuseum.org/virtual-museum/Equipment/articles/399766-Annie-Annie-Are-You-OK-Resusci-Anne | archive-url = https://archive.today/20141227115243/http://www.emsmuseum.org/virtual-museum/Equipment/articles/399766-Annie-Annie-Are-You-OK-Resusci-Anne | archive-date = 2014年12月27日 | dead-url = yes | accessdate = 2018-03-30 | language = en | df =  }}</ref>，由{{link-en|萊達爾醫療器材|Laerdal}}生產。

復甦安妮的臉是以1880年代後期溺斃的[[塞納河的無名少女|塞納河的無名少女]][[死亡面具|面容]]為藍本製作<ref name = "BBC"/><ref>{{cite web | title = The Girl from the River Seine | publisher =  Laerdal medical | url = http://www.laerdal.com/docid/1117082/The-Girl-from-the-River-Seine | accessdate = 2018-03-30 | language = en}}</ref>。

'''广州圆大厦'''是位於中國[[廣東省|廣東省]][[廣州市|廣州市]]的一座地標性建築物，鴻達興業集團的總部<ref>鸿达兴业集团, http://www.hdxy.cn/index.jsp</ref>和廣東塑料交易所總部所在地。<ref>[http://www.laprovinciadicomo.it/stories/Economia/architetto-comasco-firma-edificio-di-33-piani-in-cina_1036819_11/ ''La Provincia di Como''] Architetto comasco firma edificio di 33 piani in Cina</ref><ref>[http://www.corrierecomo.it/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=56870:archistar-lariana-in-cina&catid=256:vip-sul-lario&Itemid=130 ''Corriere di Como''] Archistar lariana in Cina</ref>廣州圓大廈位於廣州市西南邊[[珠江|珠江]]江畔。大廈由意大利建築師[[约瑟夫·迪·帕斯卡利|约瑟夫·迪·帕斯卡利]]設計，總高度為138米，共有33層，<ref>[http://skyscraperpage.com/diagrams/?buildingID=17392 ''Skyscraperpage''] Drawings of GDPE Landmark Building</ref>樓面總面積為85,000平方米。大廈興建的全球總投資額約有10億人民幣。<ref>[http://www.lifeofguangzhou.com/node_981/node_989/node_997/node_1007/2013/12/20/1387511250147413.shtml ''Life of Guangzhou''] Guangzhou Circle Opens</ref><ref>[http://www.gizmag.com/coin-shaped-gdpe-building-construction/21518/ ''Gizmag''] Innovative "lucky coin" building under way in China</ref>

== 設計 ==
廣州圓大廈呈圓形，中間設有直徑將近50米的圓孔。<ref>http://gohongkong.about.com/od/Guide-to-Guangzhou-Travel/fl/Guangzhou-Circle-building-what-where-how.htm</ref>它是世界上最高的圓形建築物。<ref>[http://www.lifeofguangzhou.com/node_981/node_989/node_997/node_1007/2013/12/20/1387511250147413.shtml ''Life of Guangzhou''] Guangzhou Circle Opens</ref><ref>[http://www.designboom.com/architecture/am-project-rounds-out-double-disk-guangzhou-circle-mansion-12-11-2013/ ''Designboom''] AM project rounds out double disk guangzhou circle mansion</ref>負責設計的建築師表示，其設計理念考慮到東方人的心態，大廈形狀取玉璧。大廈在珠江形成的倒影與其形成兩個圓形，形似「雙環玉璧」。這一「8」字外形也對中國人有特殊的意義。<ref>[http://www.amprogetti.it/indexamp.php?idlanguage=2&zone=7&id=579&back=aWRsYW5ndWFnZT0y ''AM Project''] Guanghzou Circle internet gallery</ref>

截止2014年初，大廈的公眾空間仍未開放，但其前方的公共廣場已經開放。<ref>http://gohongkong.about.com/od/Guide-to-Guangzhou-Travel/fl/Guangzhou-Circle-building-what-where-how.htm</ref>

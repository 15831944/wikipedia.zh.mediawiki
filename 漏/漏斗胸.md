'''胸凹陷'''（{{lang-en|Pectus excavatum}}）是一种前{{tsl|en|thoracic wall|胸廓壁|胸廓壁}}的<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[胸骨|胸骨]]和{{tsl|en|rib cage|胸腔|}}向内凹陷的一类先天畸形，中文将这种胸凹陷的畸形称为'''漏斗胸'''（{{lang-en|Funnel Chest}}）。这一畸形使得<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[胸|胸]]部向下形成漏斗状内陷，影响正常的<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[心血管系统|心血管]]和<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[呼吸系统|呼吸系统]]功能，患者在胸腔和背部会伴有痛感。这一先天性疾病既可能在出生时发病，也可能在青春期之后才逐渐出现，对患者可能产生严重的心理负担，使其尽可能避免一切有可能暴露该畸形的活动。<ref>{{cite encyclopedia | title=Pectus excavatum | encyclopedia=MedLine Plus Medical Encyclopedia | publisher=[[美国国家医学图书馆|美国国家医学图书馆]] and the [[國立衛生研究院_(美國)|國立衛生研究院 (美國)]] | url=https://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/003320.htm#Considerations | date=2007-11-12 }}</ref>

==征兆和症状==
[[File:Sternum_front.png|thumb]]
胸骨内塌陷为本病决定特征。最常见的为胸板下端的杯状内陷，但也存在上肋软骨塌陷造成的较大的胸内陷。<ref name="pmid22294949">{{cite journal |vauthors=Blanco FC, Elliott ST, Sandler AD |title=Management of congenital chest wall deformities |journal=Seminars in Plastic Surgery |volume=25 |issue=1 |pages=107–16 |year=2011 |pmid=22294949 |pmc=3140238 |doi=10.1055/s-0031-1275177 |type=Review}}</ref>最下端的肋骨可能凸起，（英语中称为"flared ribs"）。<ref>See for example {{cite journal |vauthors=Bosgraaf RP, Aronson DC |title=Treatment of flaring of the costal arch after the minimally invasive pectus excavatum repair (Nuss procedure) in children |journal=Journal of Pediatric Surgery |volume=45 |issue=9 |pages=1904–6 |year=2010 |pmid=20850643 |doi=10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2010.05.037 |url=}}</ref> 胸内陷既可能是对称分布的，也可能是非对称分布的。患者会伴前胸或后背疼痛，痛觉通常源于骨骼肌系统。<ref>{{cite web |title=Pectus Excavatum Clinical Presentation: History |url=http://emedicine.medscape.com/article/1004953-clinical |website=Medscape |accessdate=7 September 2016 |date=30 June 2015}}</ref>

温和病例中，心血管呼吸功能不受到影响，但伴心脏异位、旋转或二者兼具。<ref name="pmid19632563">{{cite journal |vauthors=Fokin AA, Steuerwald NM, Ahrens WA, Allen KE |title=Anatomical, histologic, and genetic characteristics of congenital chest wall deformities |journal=Seminars in Thoracic and Cardiovascular Surgery |volume=21 |issue=1 |pages=44–57 |year=2009 |pmid=19632563 |doi=10.1053/j.semtcvs.2009.03.001 |type=Review}}</ref>严重病例中，右心房受到压迫，诱发{{tsl|en|mitral valve prolapse|二尖瓣脱垂|二尖瓣脱垂}}（即在心脏中单向摆动、控制血流的二尖瓣叶在心室收缩期脱入左心房），肺功能因为肺容量减少而受到限制。<ref name="test">{{cite web|url=http://www.medscape.com/viewarticle/571222_3|title=Cardiopulmonary Manifestations of Pectus Excavatum|accessdate=|author=|date=|publisher=|website=Medscape}}{{subscription required}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Jaroszewski|first=Dawn E.|last2=Warsame|first2=Tahlil A.|last3=Chandrasekaran|first3=Krishnaswamy|last4=Chaliki|first4=Hari|date=December 2011|title=Right Ventricular Compression Observed in Echocardiography from Pectus Excavatum Deformity|journal=Journal of Cardiovascular Ultrasound|volume=19|issue=4|pages=192–195|doi=10.4250/jcu.2011.19.4.192|issn=1975-4612|pmc=3259543|pmid=22259662}}</ref>疾病常伴尴尬感、羞耻感、<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[社交焦慮|社交焦慮]]在内的心理压力，导致患者与外人的活动和交流减少，情绪负面化、极端化，失望情绪浓重，以致于产生<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[重度抑郁症|重度抑郁症]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://pectusexcavatuminfo.com/psychological-effects-pectus-excavatum/|title=Orthopedic approach to pectus deformities: 32 years of studies|last=Brandon|first=Mike|date=2016-02-04|website=Pectus Excavatum Info|publisher=Pediatric Orthopedist and Physiatrist, Orthopectus Clinical Center and Asa Norte Regional Hospital. 2Doctor in Orthopedics, School of Medicine, University de Sāo Paulo, Ribeirāo Preto, SP. Pediatric Orthopedist, Orthopectus Clinical Center. Preceptor, Adult Foot and Pediatric Orthopedics, Federal District Hospital, Brasilia, DF|access-date=2016-02-04}}</ref>

'''万吉輕軌站'''（{{lang-en|Bangkit LRT Station}}，代號BP9）是[[新加坡輕軌列車系統|新加坡輕軌列車系統]][[武吉班讓輕軌|武吉班讓輕軌]]上的一個車站，由SMRT輕軌（[[SMRT集團|SMRT集團]]）經營，並於1999年11月6日正式啟用。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lta.gov.sg/content/ltaweb/en/public-transport/projects/bukit-panjang-lrt.html|title=Bukit Panjang LRT - Projects - Public Transport|accessdate=2018-07-07|author=Land Transport Authority|date=|work=www.lta.gov.sg|publisher=|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180129195645/https://www.lta.gov.sg/content/ltaweb/en/public-transport/projects/bukit-panjang-lrt.html|archivedate=2018-01-29|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://landtransportguru.net/train/bplrt/|title=Bukit Panjang LRT Line|author=|date=2016-02-28|work=|newspaper=Land Transport Guru|accessdate=2018-07-07|language=en-US|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171216182829/http://landtransportguru.net/train/bplrt/|archivedate=2017-12-16|df=}}</ref>

==名称由来==
该輕軌站附近有条万吉路，附近的住宅区也以万吉命名。因此该輕軌站也取名为万吉。万吉是[[马来语|马来语]]“Bangkit”的中文翻译。“Bangkit”在马来语中指的是“升起”的意识。<ref name="Closing Closing Closing Closing 2018">{{cite web | last=Closing | first=Doors Are | last2=Closing | first2=Doors Are | last3=Closing | first3=Doors Are | last4=Closing | first4=Doors Are | title=Bangkit LRT Station | website=Land Transport Guru | date=2018-01-31 | url=https://landtransportguru.net/bangkit-station/ | access-date=2018-07-12 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180515043727/http://landtransportguru.net/bangkit-station/ | archivedate=2018-05-15 | df= }}</ref>

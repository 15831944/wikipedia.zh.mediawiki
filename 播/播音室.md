《'''播音室'''》（{{lang-en|''Next Caller''}}）是一部由斯蒂芬·福尔克创造的[[美国|美国]][[情景喜剧|情景喜剧]]，2012年5月[[全国广播公司|全国广播公司]]正式预订本剧<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://insidetv.ew.com/2012/05/11/nbc-orders-series-from-dane-cook/|title=NBC orders series from Dane Cook|date=May 11, 2012|last=Gonzalez|first=Sandra|work=[[Entertainment_Weekly|Entertainment Weekly]]|accessdate=May 16, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/05/nbc-picks-up-comedy-pilot-next-caller-to-series/|title=NBC Picks Up 'Next Caller' To Series|date=May 11, 2012|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=[[Deadline.com|Deadline.com]]|accessdate=May 16, 2012}}</ref>。本片定于2013年初在[[全国广播公司|全国广播公司]]播出<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://thefutoncritic.com/news/2012/05/13/nbc-reveals-its-2012-13-primetime-schedule-505412/20120513nbc01|title=NBC Reveals Its 2012-13 Primetime Schedule|date=May 13, 2012|work=The Futon Critic|accessdate=May 16, 2012}}</ref>，第一季预计将只有7集<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deadline.com/2012/05/upfront-2012-more-returning-than-new-series-with-shorter-orders-this-year|title=Upfront 2012: More Returning Than New Series With Shorter Orders This Year|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=May 17, 2012|accessdate=July 12, 2012}}</ref><ref>[http://tvfantasy.net/2012/05/18/bulletins-and-wednesday-ratings-report-for-may-18/ 5月18日简讯及星期三收视率报告]</ref>。

然而在2012年10月12日NBC宣布取消本剧，已经拍摄好的四集也不会播出<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/nbc-next-caller-dane-cook-378771|title=NBC Pulls Plug on Dane Cook Comedy 'Next Caller'|last=Goldberg|first=Lesley|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|date=October 12, 2012|accessdate=October 13, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/10/12/nbc-cancels-mid-season-comedy-next-caller-before-it-airs/152807/|title=NBC Cancels Mid-Season Comedy 'Next Caller' Before It Airs|last=Seidman|first=Robert|work=TV by the Numbers|date=October 12, 2012|accessdate=October 14, 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121015021511/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/10/12/nbc-cancels-mid-season-comedy-next-caller-before-it-airs/152807/|archivedate=2012年10月15日}}</ref>。本剧也因此成为续[[律政宝贝|律政宝贝]]后2012年至2013年电视播出季第二个被取消的电视剧<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/10/14/bubble-watch-im-still-not-sure-about-revolutions-prospects-but-i-didnt-run-over-your-dog/152876/|title=Bubble Watch: I'm Still Not Sure About "Revolution's" Prospects But I Didn't Run Over Your Dog!|last=Seidman|first=Robert|work=TV by the Numbers|date=October 14, 2012|accessdate=October 15, 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121016194253/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/10/14/bubble-watch-im-still-not-sure-about-revolutions-prospects-but-i-didnt-run-over-your-dog/152876/|archivedate=2012年10月16日}}</ref>。

'''灵气疗法'''是由[[日本人|日本人]][[臼井甕男|臼井甕男]]在1922年开创的一种[[替代医学|替代医学]]疗法，<ref>Semple, D.; Smyth, R. (2013). "Ch. 1: Psychomythology". ''Oxford Handbook of Psychiatry'' (3rd ed.). Oxford University Press. p. 20. ISBN 9780199693887.</ref><ref>Novella, Steven (19 October 2011). "Reiki". ''Science-Based Medicine''. Archived from the original on 11 April 2015.</ref>過程只需透過雙手，把{{link-en|能量 (神秘主义)|Energy (esotericism)|能量}}傳輸到受者使其身體回復平衡，以促进情绪稳定，改善健康状况，稱為「心身改善臼井靈氣療法」。灵气疗法的从业者称这是一种普遍的[[活力論|生命能量]]，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://hk.lifestyle.appledaily.com/lifestyle/culture/daily/article/20040727/4198497|title=全港不過50人靈氣治療師好馨香|author=|date=2004-07-27|work=蘋果日報|accessdate=2018-10-27|language=zh-hk}}</ref>但没有任何证据证明这一说法。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sciencebasedmedicine.org/reiki-fraudulent-misrepresentation/|title=Reiki: Fraudulent Misrepresentation|accessdate=2018-09-14|work=sciencebasedmedicine.org|language=en-US}}</ref>

由于目前缺乏大量實證，灵气疗法被认为是种[[安慰劑|安慰剂]]效果。[[美国癌症协会|美国癌症协会]]表示，灵气疗法不应取代常规癌症治疗，<ref>Russell J, Rovere A, eds. (2009). "Reiki". ''American Cancer Society Complete Guide to Complementary and Alternative Cancer Therapies'' (2nd ed.). American Cancer Society. pp. 243–45. ISBN 9780944235713.</ref>这一声明得到{{link-en|英国癌症研究所|Cancer Research UK}}<ref>"Reiki". Cancer Research UK. Archived from the original on 18 March 2015.</ref>与{{link-en|美国国家补充和综合健康中心|National Center for Complementary and Integrative Health}}的支持。<ref>"Reiki: What You Need To Know". National Center for Complementary and Integrative Health. Archived from the original on 11 April 2015.</ref>

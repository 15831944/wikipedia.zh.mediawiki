'''惡魔、變異人、異形、食人魔、地獄邊緣的僵屍化的活死人攜妻帶子大舉歸來恐怖進攻不分日夜殺出個黎明2：極度震驚2D版'''（{{lang-en|Night of the Day of the Dawn of the Son of the Bride of the Return of the Revenge of the Terror of the Attack of the Evil, Mutant, Alien, Flesh Eating, Hellbound, Zombified Living Dead Part 2: In Shocking 2-D}}，簡稱'''NOTDOT'''）是一部由James Riffel編寫及執導的[[恐怖電影|恐怖]][[嘲諷電影|惡搞]]電影<ref>{{cite book|last=Wetmore|first=Kevin|title=Back from the Dead: Remakes of the Romero Zombie Films as Markers of Their Times|year=2011|publisher=McFarland|isbn=0786446420|pages=99|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=NrdnmslLKSEC&pg=PA99&dq=%22James+Riffel%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=-9zGUOjqCKTC0AG7q4GQBQ&ved=0CDwQ6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=%22James%20Riffel%22&f=false}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Kevin Smith Offered Ten Grand To Review New Jersey Filmmaker's Movie|url=http://www.cinemablend.com/new/Kevin-Smith-Offered-Ten-Grand-Review-Jersey-Filmmaker-Movie-27574.html|publisher=Cinema Blend|accessdate=11 December 2012}}</ref>。這電影將1968年上映的《[[活死人之夜|活死人之夜]]》更新配音，並加入幽默對白。儘管這電影只在全美500間電影院播放，然而，NOTDOT已被視為[[邪典電影|邪典電影]]（Cult Film）的一部份。

==名稱==
NOTDOT的英文全名共由42字、164個字母所組成<ref>[http://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/comment/columnists/showbiz-tv-columnists/brian-mciver/2008/02/23/movies-with-the-most-86908-20328852/ Movies With The Most..] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080304172110/http://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/comment/columnists/showbiz-tv-columnists/brian-mciver/2008/02/23/movies-with-the-most-86908-20328852/ |date=2008-03-04 }}</ref>。

==續集==
NOTDOT的續集為《邪惡變種地獄食人僵屍、活死人之侵襲恐怖復仇新娘之子殺出個黎明3》(Night of the Day of the Dawn of the Son of the Bride of the Return of the Revenge of the Terror of the Attack of the Evil, Mutant, Hellbound, Flesh-Eating Subhumanoid Zombified Living Dead, Part 3)。這電影為1962年恐怖科技電影《[[不死之腦|不死之腦]]》(The Brain That Wouldn't Die)重新配音，並在2005年上映。

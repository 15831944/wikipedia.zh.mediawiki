'''利维奥卡斯蒂格尼'''（{{lang-it|'''Livio Castiglioni'''}}，{{bd|1911年|1979年|catIdx=Castiglioni, Livio}}), 是一位知名的[[意大利|意大利]][[列尔纳|列尔纳]]，出生於[[米兰|米兰]]。他的設計風格相當有個人特色，作品領域包括有[[建築|建築]]、建筑技术, 家具設計，生活用品設計，交通產品設計和[[室內設計|室內設計]]等，相當廣泛<ref>[https://books.google.it/books?id=UUZJAQAAIAAJ&q=livio+castiglioni+Lierna&dq=livio+castiglioni+Lierna&hl=it&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwj-l5KV4LrTAhVHPFAKHa2cCywQ6AEIIjAA] ''Abitare, Edizioni 433-434'', Editrice Segesta, 2003, Pagina 115</ref><ref>[http://www.fontanaarte.com/it/designer/livio-castiglioni] Livio Castiglioni, FontanaArte</ref>.
她决定把自己从他的兄弟分开，奉献在链接到光的研究设计界的第一感受，处理[[建筑光学|建筑光学]]<ref>[https://books.google.it/books?id=i3Od9bcGus0C&pg=PA208&dq=livio+castiglioni&hl=it&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjyzYPFzbrTAhXKbFAKHZzgB_8Q6AEINDAE#v=onepage&q=livio%20castiglioni&f=false] [[Gordon_Campbell|Gordon Campbell]] ''The Grove Encyclopedia of Decorative Arts'', Volume 1, Pag. 208, Oxford University Press, New York, 2006</ref><ref>[https://books.google.it/books?id=i3Od9bcGus0C&pg=PA208&dq=livio+castiglioni&hl=it&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjyzYPFzbrTAhXKbFAKHZzgB_8Q6AEINDAE#v=onepage&q=livio%20castiglioni&f=false] [[Anty_Pansera|Anty Pansera]] ''Il Design del mobile italiano dal 1946 a oggi'', Pagina 168, 1990</ref>
<ref>[https://books.google.it/books?id=uR5JAAAAYAAJ&q=livio+castiglioni+Lierna&dq=livio+castiglioni+Lierna&hl=it&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwj-l5KV4LrTAhVHPFAKHa2cCywQ6AEIKjAC] [[Gio_Ponti|Gio Ponti]] ''Who's who in Interior Design'', Barons Who's Who, 2000, Pag. 50, ISBN 1882292170</ref>.

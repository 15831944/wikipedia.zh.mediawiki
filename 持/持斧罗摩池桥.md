'''持斧罗摩池桥'''位于印度最东北部的[[魯西特河|魯西特河]]（察隅河）出山口处的一座桥。属于NH13公路。行政区划属于[[阿鲁纳恰尔邦|阿鲁纳恰尔邦]][[鲁西特县|鲁西特县]]，但位于中国主张国境线以南。

==地名==
{{tsl|en|Parashurama|持斧罗摩}}是[[印度教|印度教]][[毗湿奴|毗湿奴]]的第六个化身。该地一处池水，为印度教圣地，水池的起源与印度圣典中描述的持斧罗摩弑母的故事有关。<ref>{{cite web | title = Thousands gather at Parshuram Kund for holy dip on Makar Sankranti | publisher=The News Mill| url = http://thenewsmill.com/thousands-gather-parshuram-kund-holy-dip-makar-sankranti/ | accessdate = 2017-01-13  }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = 70,000 devotees take holy dip in Parshuram Kund|publisher=Indian Express | url = http://archive.indianexpress.com/news/70000-devotees-take-holy-dip-in-parshuram-kund/1061283/ |date=Jan 18, 2013| accessdate = 2014-06-29  }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = Arunachal Pradesh planning to promote tourism at Parsuram Kund | publisher=Daily News & Analysis| url = http://www.dnaindia.com/india/report-arunachal-pradesh-planning-to-promote-tourism-at-parsuram-kund-1779235 | accessdate = 2014-06-29  }}</ref>

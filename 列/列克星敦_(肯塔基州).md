'''列克星顿'''（{{Lang-en|'''Lexington'''}}）位于[[美国|美国]][[肯塔基州|肯塔基州]]中北部，与其所在的[[费耶特县_(肯塔基州)|费耶特县]]区域完全重合。它是肯塔基州仅次于[[路易斯维尔_(肯塔基州)|路易斯维尔]]的第二大城市，也是[[藍草地區|藍草地區]]的核心城市。其居民教育程度排在美國國內第10，39.5%的列克星敦人都擁有至少一個學士學位<ref>{{cite encyclopedia|url=http://encarta.msn.com/encnet/Departments/elearning/?article=EducatedCities |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5kwbbHG3L?url=http://encarta.msn.com/encnet/Departments/elearning/?article=EducatedCities |title=Degrees & Training – Most-Educated Cities in the United States |archivedate=October 31, 2009 |publisher=MSN Encarta |accessdate=August 21, 2015 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>。2017年其市區人口325,813，大都會區人口506,751<ref>{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?src=bkmk |title=American FactFinder – Results |author=Data Access and Dissemination Systems (DADS) |work=census.gov |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140815134909/http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?src=bkmk |archivedate=August 15, 2014 }}</ref>。由於人口增長過快，該市遭遇嚴重的交通問題。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/magazine-19716687|title=10 monster traffic jams from around the world|date=2012-10-01|work=BBC News|access-date=2017-12-08|language=en-GB}}</ref>

==教育==
[[肯塔基大学|肯塔基大学]]是州内最大的公立大学。而[[特兰西瓦尼亚大学|特兰西瓦尼亚大学]]則是一所私立文科学院。

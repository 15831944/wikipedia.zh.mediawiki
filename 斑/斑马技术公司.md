'''斑马技术'''（Zebra Technologies）是一家位于美国[[伊利诺伊州|伊利诺伊州]]在NASDAQ上市的计算机外设制造公司。主要从事票据、[[条码|条码]]标签打印机、RFID智能标签打印机、固定或手持读码器。<ref name=finances />

全世界170多个国家或地区，世界500强企业超过90%都在使用斑马打印机。<ref>[http://news.medill.northwestern.edu/chicago/news.aspx?id=170823 Medill Reports] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140530051459/http://news.medill.northwestern.edu/chicago/news.aspx?id=170823 |date=2014-05-30 }}</ref>

==历史==
[[File:Zebra_Technologies_Heerenveen-2.jpg|thumb]]的办公地点]]
1969年创立时称"Data Specialties Incorporated"，从事告诉电磁设备的制造。1982年专注于票据与标签打印，1986年改为现名。1991年上市。

1998年合并了Eltron International, Inc.<ref>[http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1998-10-26/news/9810270245_1_plastic-card-printers-eltron-international-zebra Chicago Tribune: Eltron]</ref>  2000年合并了Comtec Information Systems<ref>[http://www.fundinguniverse.com/company-histories/zebra-technologies-corporation-history Funding Universe]</ref>。2003年合并了Atlantek, Inc.<ref>[http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2003-11-20/business/0311200291_1_zebra-technologies-card-printers-imaging Chicago Tribune: Atlantek]</ref> 2004年进入了RFID智能标签制造业，<ref>[https://www.bloomberg.com/apps/news?pid=newsarchive&sid=apFnsV9IOZR4 Bloomberg]</ref>，2005年合并了Retail Systems International, Inc. (RSI).<ref>[http://www.wikinvest.com/stock/Zebra_Technologies_(ZBRA)/Retail_Systems_International_Inc Wikinvest] {{Wayback|url=http://www.wikinvest.com/stock/Zebra_Technologies_(ZBRA)/Retail_Systems_International_Inc |date=20160925201403 }}</ref> 随后几年合并了Swecoin,<ref>[http://www.selfserviceworld.com/news/zebra-technologies-buys-swecoin-2/ Self Service World] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20140529215256/http://www.selfserviceworld.com/news/zebra-technologies-buys-swecoin-2/ |date=2014-05-29 }}</ref> WhereNet Corp,<ref>[http://www.rfidjournal.com/articles/view?6652 RFID Journal]</ref> Proveo AG,<ref>[http://www.rfidjournal.com/articles/view?3682 RFID Journal]</ref>,Navis Holdings, LLC<ref>[http://www.chicagobusiness.com/article/20110131/NEWS08/110139990/zebra-technologies-sells-navis-unit-for-190m Chicago Business News]</ref>.

2008年起绝大部分打印机制造业务外包给[[捷普科技|捷普科技]]设在[[广州|广州]]的工厂。<ref>http://articles.chicagotribune.com/keyword/zebra/featured/5 Chicago Tribune: Manufacturing</ref> 
 
2009年合并了Multispectral Solutions, Inc.<ref>[http://www.crunchbase.com/organization/zebra-technologies Crunchbase]</ref> 2012 年合并了LaserBand, LLC<ref>[http://www.bizjournals.com/stlouis/news/2012/07/13/laserband-sold-to-zebra-technologies.html St. Louis Business Journal]</ref>与StepOne Systems.<ref>[http://www.retailtouchpoints.com/news-brief/2215-zebra-technologies-acquires-stepone-systems Retail Touch Points] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140530055002/http://www.retailtouchpoints.com/news-brief/2215-zebra-technologies-acquires-stepone-systems |date=2014-05-30 }}</ref> 2013年合并了Hart Systems.<ref name="ZD Net">[http://www.zdnet.com/zebra-technologies-buys-motorola-enterprise-unit-for-3-5bn-7000028440/ ZD Net]</ref> 2014年合并了[[Motorola_Solutions|Motorola Solutions]]的企业客户业务.<ref name="ZD Net"/>

==2017年联航快运暴力驱赶乘客事件==
{{main|联航快运3411号班机事件}}
2017年4月9日晚上<ref>[http://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-39554421 United Airlines: Passenger forcibly removed from flight]</ref>，一名亚裔乘客被强行拖下共和航空运营的联航快运的班机。当时登机已经完成，联合航空却称飞机需要为机组人员寻求4个座位，因此要求4名乘客离开满员的航班<ref>[https://www.hkcnews.com/article/3275/%E8%81%AF%E5%90%88%E8%88%AA%E7%A9%BA-3275/business-insider%EF%BC%9A%E8%81%AF%E5%90%88%E8%88%AA%E7%A9%BA%E7%82%BA%E5%9B%9B%E8%81%B7%E5%93%A1%E6%90%AD%E6%A9%9F%E8%B6%95%E4%BA%BA%E8%90%BD%E6%A9%9F 聯合航空為四職員搭機趕人落機]</ref>。 聯合航空公司一开始提供每人16張50美元分次使用的聯航折價卷作為補償(總額800美元)，但是没有乘客自愿离开，于是联合航空使用抽籤決定选择4名需要下機的乘客。其中有一位69岁的亚裔乘客拒绝下機要求，遭到航警暴力對待，並被強行帶離飛機<ref>[https://www.nytimes.com/2017/04/10/business/united-flight-passenger-dragged.html?_r=0 United Airlines Passenger Is Dragged From an Overbooked Flight]</ref><ref>[http://www.chicagotribune.com/business/ct-united-passenger-dragged-fallout-stock-0412-biz-20170411-story.html United faces more questions as dragged passenger hires high-powered attorney]</ref><ref>[https://www.bloomberg.com/view/articles/2017-04-11/united-is-feeling-the-sway-of-consumers-in-asia United Is Feeling the Sway of Consumers in Asia]</ref>。涉及争议的乘客为[[越南華人|越南華人]]美国籍医生David Dao<ref>[http://www.courier-journal.com/story/news/local/2017/04/11/david-dao-passenger-removed-united-flight-doctor-troubled-past/100318320/ David Dao, passenger removed from United flight, now in spotlight]</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://foreignpolicy.com/2017/04/11/chinese-blame-america-for-united-airlines/|title=Chinese Blame America for United Airlines|work=FP|access-date=2017-04-12}}</ref><ref name=kimberlyyam>{{cite news|title=After United Controversy, Chinese Social Media Users Express Outrage|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/united-social-media-controversy_us_58ecde88e4b0df7e20455dbf|agency=The Huffington Post|publisher=Kimberly Yam|date=April 11, 2017}}</ref>。

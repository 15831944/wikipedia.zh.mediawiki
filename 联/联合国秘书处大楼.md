'''联合国秘书处大楼'''（{{lang-en|United Nations Secretariat Building}}）位于[[美国|美国]][[纽约市|纽约市]][[曼哈頓中城|曼哈顿中城]]{{link-en|海龟湾|Turtle Bay, Manhattan}}，高154米，是[[联合国总部大楼|联合国总部大楼]]的中心，也是纽约市第一座使用[[玻璃幕墙|玻璃幕墙]]的摩天大楼<ref>
{{Cite web|title=In Midtown, Modernist Perfection in a Glass Box|url=https://www.nysun.com/arts/in-midtown-modernist-perfection-in-a-glass-box/83819/|work=The New York Sun|first=Francis|last=Morrone|date=2008-08-08|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180819114905/https://www.nysun.com/arts/in-midtown-modernist-perfection-in-a-glass-box/83819/|archive-date=2018-08-19|dead-url=no}}
</ref>。该建筑所在地块被认定为联合国领土，但仍是美国的一部分<ref>
{{Cite book|title=The Law of the United Nations: A Critical Analysis of Its Fundamental Problems : with Supplement|last=Kelsen|first=Hans|publisher=The Lawbook Exchange, Ltd.|year=2000|isbn=9781584770770|location=|pages=|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=BWPa0MB_AyQC&pg=PA350#v=onepage&q&f=false|access-date=2019-03-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170222102734/https://books.google.com/books?id=BWPa0MB_AyQC&pg=PA350#v=onepage&q&f=false#v=onepage&q&f=false|archive-date=2017-02-22|dead-url=no}}
</ref>。

== 历史 ==
1948年9月14日举行了联合国秘书处大楼的[[動工儀式|动工仪式]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=U.N. Breaks Ground for Its Capital; O'Dwyer Welcomes 'Plan for Peace'; BREAKING GROUND FOR UNITED NATIONS HEADQUARTERS HERE U.N.BREAKS GROUND FOR WORLD CAPITAL|url=http://timesmachine.nytimes.com/timesmachine/1948/09/15/84016864.html|work=timesmachine.nytimes.com|language=en|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref>。联合国选择了[[曼哈顿|曼哈顿]]和[[皇后区|皇后区]]的四家公司组成财团，并与之签订了3000万美元的合同，建造此楼是该合同的一部分<ref>{{Cite web|title=4 Companies Join Forces To Construct U.N.'s Home; Group of New York's Biggest Contractors Forms New Corporation to Build the World Body's East River Skyscraper|url=http://timesmachine.nytimes.com/timesmachine/1948/12/19/96442051.html|work=timesmachine.nytimes.com|accessdate=2019-03-06|language=en}}</ref>。巴西建筑学家[[奧斯卡·尼邁耶|奥斯卡·尼迈耶]]和瑞士[[柯布西耶|柯布西耶]]参与设计了秘书处大楼的工作。该大楼高39层，于1952年建成<ref>
{{Cite news|title=WORK COMPLETED ON U. N. BUILDINGS; $68,000,000 Plant Finished -- Lie Announces a Plan to Reorganize Top Staff WORK COMPLETED ON U. N. BUILDINGS|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1952/10/10/archives/work-completed-on-u-n-buildings-68000000-plant-finished-lie.html|newspaper=The New York Times|date=1952-10-10|issn=0362-4331|language=en|first=Thomas J. Hamiltonspecial|last=Hamiltonspecial|work=|accessdate=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190124085915/https://www.nytimes.com/1952/10/10/archives/work-completed-on-u-n-buildings-68000000-plant-finished-lie.html|archive-date=2019-01-24|dead-url=no}}
</ref>。此楼以北是[[联合国大会|联合国大会]]和[[安全理事会|安全理事会]]所在的会议大楼，以南有一座图书馆。作为联合国建筑群的一部分，秘书处大楼适用美国与联合国签订的相关协议<ref>{{Cite web|title=Agreement between the United Nations and the United States regarding the headquarters of the UN|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100603050415/http://archive.usun.state.gov/hc_docs/Headquarters%20Agreement%20PL80-357.pdf|work=web.archive.org|date=2010-06-03|accessdate=2019-03-06|author=|publisher=}}</ref>。

秘书处大楼在2010年进行了翻新，自2012年7月起分阶段恢复办公<ref>{{Cite web|title=United Nations Capital Master Plan - Timeline|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120904102130/http://www.un.org/wcm/content/site/cmp/home/about/pid/22303|work=web.archive.org|date=2012-09-04|accessdate=2019-03-06}}</ref>。2012年10月29日，受[[颶風桑迪|飓风珊迪]]的影响，联合国建筑群的地下室淹水，秘书处大楼关闭三天，其中的一些办公室须易地办公<ref>{{Cite news|title=NY inquiry into Sandy overpricing|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-20212288|date=2012-11-05|accessdate=2019-03-06|language=en|author=|work=}}</ref>。

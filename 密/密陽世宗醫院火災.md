'''密陽世宗醫院火災'''（{{kor|諺=밀양세종병원화재|hanja=密陽世宗病院火災}}），是2018年1月26日於[[大韩民国|大韓民國]][[慶尚南道|慶尚南道]][[密陽市|密陽市]]駕谷洞的世宗醫院發生的火災。<ref name="death">{{cite news|author=박정양·강대한·남경문|title=밀양 세종병원 화재 사망 37명…의사 등 직원 3명도(종합4보)|url=http://news1.kr/articles/?3218356|date=2018-01-26|accessdate=2018-01-26|publisher=뉴스1|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180126094603/http://news1.kr/articles/?3218356|archivedate=2018-01-26}}</ref>這是[[堤川市|堤川]]發生[[堤川体育中心火灾事故|體育館火災]]造成29人喪生一個月後的一件嚴重火災。起火原因在調查中。<ref name="CBS News 2018">{{cite web | title=Hospital fire breaks out in South Korea, kills more than 30 | website=CBS News | date=25 January 2018 | url=https://www.cbsnews.com/news/south-korea-hospital-fire-sejong-hospital-miryang-today-2018-01-26/ | access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | first=Sang-Hun | last=Choe | title=South Korea Hospital Fire Kills at Least 37 People | newspaper=The New York Times | date=26 January 2018 | url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/01/25/world/asia/fire-hospital-south-korea.html | access-date=26 January 2018}}</ref>

火災於早7時32分從本館1層[[急診室|急診室]]中發生，約在早9時半左右撲滅<ref>{{cite web|author=이재민|title=밀양 세종병원 화재, 상황 정리|url=http://imnews.imbc.com/replay/2018/nw1200/article/4510141_22606.html|date=2018-01-26|accessdate=2018-01-26|publisher=MBC|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180127052619/http://imnews.imbc.com/replay/2018/nw1200/article/4510141_22606.html|archivedate=2018-01-27}}</ref>。截至當日下午3時半，事故已造成37人死亡，131人重輕傷<ref name="death"/>。

'''密蘇里堪薩斯城聖殿'''（Kansas City Missouri Temple）是[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]第137座聖殿，也是大堪薩斯城地區的首座耶穌基督後期聖徒教會聖殿。密蘇里堪薩斯城聖殿的修建計劃在2008年10月4日公佈<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.deseretnews.com/article/700264138/Rome-LDS-temple-four-others-announced.html |newspaper= [[Deseret_News|Deseret News]] |date= October 5, 2008  |first= James M |last= Dougherty |title= Rome LDS temple, four others announced |accessdate= 2012-10-15 }}</ref>。2012年4月7日至28日期間，聖殿對公眾開放<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.lds.org/church/events/temple-square-events/2012-kansas-city-temple-open-house |contribution= Kansas City Temple Open House |title= Temple Square Events |work= LDS.org |publisher= [[LDS_Church|LDS Church]] |accessdate= 2012-10-15 }}</ref>。2012年5月5日，聖殿舉行了奉獻儀式<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/62320/Kansas-City-Missouri-Temple-dedicated-by-President-Thomas-S-Monson.html |title= Kansas City Missouri Temple dedicated by President Thomas S. Monson |date= May 6, 2012 |newspaper= [[Church_News|Church News]] |accessdate= 2012-10-15 }}</ref>。

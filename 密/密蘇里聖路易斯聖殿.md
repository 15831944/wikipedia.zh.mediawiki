'''密蘇里聖路易斯聖殿'''（St. Louis Missouri Temple）是[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的第50座聖殿，位於[[密蘇里州|密蘇里州]][[聖路易斯_(密蘇里州)|聖路易斯 (密蘇里州)]]郊區。密蘇里聖路易斯聖殿在1993點動工<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/23454/Ground-is-broken-on-cold-day-for-temple-in-St-Louis-Mo.html |title= Ground is broken on cold day for temple in St. Louis, Mo. |first= John L. |last= Hart |date= Nov 6, 1993 |newspaper= [[Church_News|Church News]] }}</ref>，1997年4月26日至5月17日對公眾開放參觀<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/29776/St-Louis-Temple-doors-open-to-public-in-April.html |title= St. Louis Temple doors open to public in April |date= Jan 11, 1997 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/30041/Missourians-welcome-new-temple.html |title= Missourians welcome new temple |first= R. Scott |last= Lloyd |date= May 3, 1997 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/29462/260000-visit-temple-during-open-house.html |title= 260,000 visit temple during open house |first= R. Scott |last= Lloyd |date= May 31, 1997 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref>，並在1997年6月1日奉獻<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/29146/St-Louis-Missouri-Temple-May-it-be-preserved-by-Thy-power.html |title= St. Louis Missouri Temple: 'May it be preserved by Thy power' |date= June 7, 1997 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/29944/Church-dedicates-its-50th-temple.html |title= Church dedicates its 50th temple |first= R. Scott |last= Lloyd |date= June 7, 1997 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref>。聖殿面積{{convert|58749|sqft|m2}}。

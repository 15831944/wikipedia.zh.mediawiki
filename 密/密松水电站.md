'''密松水电站''' （{{lang-my|မြစ်ဆုံ တာတမံ}}）是[[缅甸|缅甸]][[伊洛瓦底江|伊洛瓦底江]]干流上一座在建的水电站大坝。是世界上第15大水电站。由[[缅甸政府|缅甸政府]]合同商“Asia World”<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2011/sep/30/us-embassy-cables-myitsone-dam-document|title=US embassy cables: how Rangoon office helped opponents of Myitsone dam|date=30 September 2011|work=The Guardian|accessdate=4 March 2012}}</ref>、[[缅甸电力部|缅甸电力部]]、[[中国电力投资集团|中国电力投资集团]]承建。将为缅甸提供600万千瓦电力，缅甸国内消化不了的电力将销售到邻国。<ref name="Min">{{Cite news|url=http://www.minnpost.com/globalpost/2010/02/01/15488/proposed_dam_to_flood_burma_while_powering_china|title=Proposed dam to flood Burma, while powering China|last=Libre|first=Ryan|date=February 1, 2010|publisher=MinnPost.com|accessdate=15 February 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110430041735/http://www.minnpost.com/globalpost/2010/02/01/15488/proposed_dam_to_flood_burma_while_powering_china|archive-date=2011-04-30|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref>The Economist, [http://www.economist.com/node/21564279 "Relations with Myanmar: Less thunder out of China"], 6 October 2012.</ref>

2011年9月30日，缅甸总统[[吴登盛|吴登盛]]宣布在他的任期内密松水电站停建。<ref name="halt">{{cite web | url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-asia-pacific-15121801 | title=Burma dam: Work halted on divisive Myitsone project | publisher=BBC | date=30 September 2011 | accessdate=August 30, 2011}}</ref>[[中华人民共和国外交部发言人|中国外交部发言人]]对此表示需要对话。<ref name="Hong Lei">{{cite web | url=http://www.mfa.gov.cn/chn/gxh/tyb/fyrbt/t864634.htm | title=A reporter asked  Foreign Ministry spokesman Hong Lei | publisher=Government of China | accessdate=September 1, 2011}}</ref>2011年10月，[[缅甸外交部|缅甸外交部]]部长U Wunna Maung Lwin访问北京。<ref name=travel>{{cite web | url=http://www.mmtimes.com/2011/news/597/news59707.html | title=China, Myanmar close in on agreement to settle dispute over Myitsone suspension | publisher=MM Times | accessdate=November 13, 2011 | author=San Oo | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111119030521/http://www.mmtimes.com/2011/news/597/news59707.html | archivedate=2011年11月19日 }}</ref>

==位置==

==历史==
密松水电站是[[迈立开江|迈立开江]]与[[恩梅开江|恩梅开江]]汇流区水电规划（Confluence Region Hydropower Project）七座大坝之一。七座水电站规划装机容量20000 MW。2001年时，缅甸制订的30年电力发展规划要建设64座水电站、3座燃煤电厂，总装机4000万千瓦。而汇流区水电计划就占了41%的装机容量。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.mmtimes.com/2011/news/595/news59501.html | title=President halts dam | publisher=MM times | accessdate=October 8, 2011 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111006061636/http://mmtimes.com/2011/news/595/news59501.html | archivedate=2011年10月6日 }}</ref>

早在1952年缅甸政府就提出开发密松大坝的计划。此后找了欧洲、日本、中国等多个国家的投资者，因资金、电力市场等问题，都不愿投资缅甸。2001年，缅甸电力企业及缅甸农业与灌溉部制订了“伊洛瓦底江密松大坝多用途水利项目”。2003年开始勘测。[[昆明|昆明]]水电勘测设计院负责踏勘坝址。2006年10月，缅甸政府在第三届中国东盟投资峰会上邀请中国电力投资集团投资开发缅甸的水电，以利用中国的资金和技术。2006年12月，缅甸政府与中国电力投资集团签订理解备忘录，建设600万千瓦的密松水电站与340万千瓦的其培（Chibwe）水电站。

2007年由长江水利勘测设计院实施地质钻探、库区勘测、水电设计。2007年4月，为大坝建设供电的9.9万千瓦的小其培水电站，2011年9月建成。 

2009年6月16日,中国电力投资集团与缅甸水电实施部签订备忘录，开发、运营、转交在密松上游的恩梅开江、迈立开江建设水电站。<ref name="MOU">{{Cite web|url=http://www.burmariversnetwork.org/news/news-archives/202-memorandum-of-agreement-on-nmaimaliirrawaddy-dams-signed.html|title=Memorandum of Agreement on N’Mai/Mali/Irrawaddy Dams Signed|date=2009-06-21|work=Excerpts from ''New Light of Myanmar''|publisher=Burma Rivers Network|accessdate=16 February 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110911115945/http://www.burmariversnetwork.org/news/news-archives/202-memorandum-of-agreement-on-nmaimaliirrawaddy-dams-signed.html|archivedate=2011年9月11日|df=}}</ref> 

2009年12月21日，密松水电站正式开工仪式。<ref name="bni">{{Cite news|url=http://www.bnionline.net/news/kng/7775-exiled-kachins-urge-china-to-stop-irrawaddy-myitsone-dam-project-.html|title=Exiled Kachins urge China to stop Irrawaddy Myitsone dam project|last=Kachin News Group|date=2010-01-27|publisher=Burma News International|accessdate=3 February 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100616045632/http://bnionline.net/news/kng/7775-exiled-kachins-urge-china-to-stop-irrawaddy-myitsone-dam-project-.html|archivedate=2010年6月16日}}</ref><ref name="Resisting">{{Cite web|url=http://www.scribd.com/doc/22702866/Resisting-theFlood|title=Resisting the Flood|date=10-2009|publisher=Kachin Development Networking Group|pages=12, 13|accessdate=5 February 2010}}</ref>

==经济==
缅甸政府获得10%的发电量与15%的项目股份。此外，缅甸政府还将征收所得税与出口税<ref name="never back">{{cite web | url=http://www.mmtimes.com/2011/news/593/news59302.html | title=Despite protest, minister vows govt will continue dam project | publisher=MM times | accessdate=August 31, 2011 | author=Ei Ei Toe Lwin and Aye Thidar Kyaw | date=September 19–25, 2011 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110926101948/http://www.mmtimes.com/2011/news/593/news59302.html | archivedate=2011年9月26日 }}</ref> 运营50年后，将完全无偿移交给缅甸政府。<ref name="interview"/>在头50年内，缅甸政府将获得170亿美元收入。<ref name="funding">{{cite web | url=http://eversion.news-eleven.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=1481:dr-than-htut-aung&catid=42:weekly-eleven-eversion&Itemid=109 | title=Speech of Eleven Media Group Chairman & CEO Dr. Than Htut Aung on Myitsone Hydropower Project | publisher=Eleven News | accessdate=August 31, 2011 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111007153736/http://eversion.news-eleven.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=1481:dr-than-htut-aung&catid=42:weekly-eleven-eversion&Itemid=109 | archivedate=2011年10月7日 | df= }}</ref>

对于伊洛瓦底江盆地的7座规划水电站，缅甸政府可获得540亿美元的税收、发电量的免费份额、股份分红。缅甸政府得到的经济收益超过总收益的60%，而投资者不足40%。<ref name="interview"/>

==设计==
密松水电站装机容量6000MW相当于[[三峡水电站|三峡水电站]]的16%。<ref name="Min"/><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.industcards.com/top-100-pt-4.htm |title=top-100-pt-4 |publisher=Industcards.com |date= |accessdate=2009-08-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090728230646/http://www.industcards.com/top-100-pt-4.htm |archivedate=2009-07-28 }}</ref>

缅甸电力部长Zaw Min称密松大坝位于7度震区，大坝可以抵抗9度地震，抗震能力强，大坝非常安全。<ref name="reviewed">{{cite web | url=http://eversion.news-eleven.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=1466:myitsone-hydropower-project-to-be-reviewed-report&catid=42:weekly-eleven-eversion&Itemid=109 | title=Myitsone Hydropower Project to be reviewed: Report | publisher=Eleven | accessdate=October 1, 2011 }}{{dead link|date=2018年2月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

==社会影响==
[[File:Relocated_village_Tang_Hpre.jpg|thumb]]
[[File:New_concrete_road_and_electricity_supply_for_Tang_Hpre_village.jpg|thumb]]
密松水库将淹没{{convert|390|km2|abbr=on|1}}，包括47座村庄，搬迁约10 000人，<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.internationalrivers.org/files/China%20Southern%20Grid%20Letter%20-%20English.pdf|title=CSG’s Initial Public Offering and Project Risks in Southeast Asia|publisher=[[International_Rivers|International Rivers]]|date=May 13, 2008|accessdate=February 15, 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100626054149/http://www.internationalrivers.org/files/China%20Southern%20Grid%20Letter%20-%20English.pdf|archivedate=2010年6月26日}}</ref><ref name="Naing"/><ref name=gov>{{cite web | url=http://www.myanmar.com/newspaper/nlm/Jan25_01.html | title=In future, large hydropower projects must be implemented successfully with domestic industrial products, national experts and national skilled workers only | publisher=New Light of Myanmar | date=22 January 2001 | accessdate=August 31, 2011 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111113165109/http://www.myanmar.com/newspaper/nlm/Jan25_01.html | archivedate=2011年11月13日 }}</ref>电站坝区移民2146人，树木补偿总估计为41亿[[缅甸元|缅甸元]]，安置费用已超过2500万美元。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.uachc.com/Liems/esite/content/showDetail.jsp?nid=6859&newtype_no=2248 | title=Resettlement work | publisher=China Power Investment | accessdate=October 3, 2011 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref> 

反对者认为坝址距离Sagaing断层约{{convert|100|km|abbr=on|1}}，这给大坝下游的伊洛瓦底盆地，特别是[[密支那|密支那]]的居民，带来了很大的地震溃坝风险。<ref name="Why halted">{{cite web | url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/mobile/world-asia-pacific-15123833 | title=Burma dam: Why Myitsone plan is being halted | publisher=BBC | date=August 31, 2011 | accessdate=August 31, 2011 | author=Rachel Harvey}}</ref>缅甸政府称，大坝设计标准抗御9度地震。有反对者建议建设两座小型的水电站替代密松水电站。设计专家认为，经过数年的研究，包括两个小型的替代方案研究，密松电站在地质、安全、防洪能力、环境、经济等方面都是最好的选择。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.nytimes.com/2011/09/22/world/asia/controversy-over-dam-fuels-rare-public-outcry-in-myanmar.html?pagewanted=all | title=Controversy Over Dam Fuels Rare Public Outcry in Myanmar | publisher=New York Times | date=September 21, 2011 | accessdate=August 31, 2011}}</ref>中电投集团总经理陆启洲称密松大坝建设标准能抗9级[[地震烈度|地震烈度]]，比[[汶川大地震|汶川大地震]]震中的[[紫坪铺水利枢纽|紫坪铺水电站]]高出两个等级。<ref name="interview">{{cite web | url=http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/china/2011-10/04/content_13836037.htm | title=Myitsone Hydropower Project mutually beneficial | publisher=Xinhua News | date=October 4, 2011 | accessdate=October 4, 2011}}</ref>

==环境影响==
[[File:Wildlife_in_Tang_Hpre_village_near_Myitsone_Dam_project_area.jpg|thumb]] 
密松大坝与其他规划中的拦江大坝将改变伊洛瓦底江的水流特性，改善当地烧荒轮作和狩猎等传统的生计方式，对生态保护具有重要作用。<ref>William L. Graf (1999) [http://www.fiu.edu/~envstud/courses/evr5320/Graf_1999.pdf'' Dam nation: A geographic census of American dams and their large-scale hydrologic impacts ''] page 5, published Water Resources Research, Vol. 35, No. 4, pages 1305-1311, April 1999</ref>雨季洪水下降，旱季水量增加，对下游[[伊洛瓦底三角洲|伊洛瓦底三角洲]]也有好处。缅甸官方称密松大坝设计已经考虑了库区泥沙沉积，有能力下泄沉积的泥沙。

==反对==
2010年2月，设在英国的克钦民族组织（Kachin National Organization)在英、日、澳洲、美等地的缅甸大使馆抗议兴建密松水电站。<ref name="bni"/>[[维基解密|维基解密]]披露的美国外交文件证实，在[[仰光|仰光]]的美国大使馆资助了一些反对密松水电站的活动分子团体。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2011/sep/30/us-embassy-cables-burma-myitsone-dam | title=US embassy cables: how Rangoon office helped opponents of Myitsone dam | publisher=Gurdians | work=United States government | accessdate=October 2, 2011 | author=Wikileak}}</ref>

2011年6月，[[缅甸军事|缅甸政府军]]与[[克钦独立军|克钦独立军]]爆发武装冲突<ref name="Reuters">{{cite web | url=http://in.reuters.com/article/2011/09/30/idINIndia-59646920110930 | title=Myanmar govt shelves $3.6 bln mega dam - officials | publisher=Reuters | accessdate=October 2, 2011}}</ref>。

==项目挂起==
2011年9月30日，缅甸总统吴登盛在缅甸国会宣布密松水电站在他的总统任期内搁置。这一决定得到了极端环保分子、政治活动家、远在两千公里外的仰光等地反对组织的欢迎，西方国家包括欧盟与美国欢迎吴登盛的决定<ref>{{cite web | url=http://blogs.voanews.com/breaking-news/2011/10/03/eu-welcomes-burmese-decision-on-myitsone-dam/ | title=EU Welcomes Burmese Decision on Myitsone Dam | publisher=VOA | date=October 3, 2011 | accessdate=October 3, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.taiwannews.com.tw/etn/news_content.php?id=1722578 | title=US welcomes Myanmar decision to halt dam | publisher=Taiwan News | date=October 3, 2011 | accessdate=October 3, 2011}}</ref> 。而{{Fact|当地移民确始终没有反对，项目搁置使大量当地居民失业，生计困难|time=2018-01-07T07:31:01+00:00}}。

2013年3月，部分因為工程被逼遷的居民曾嘗試遷回原地，但被緬甸陸軍驅趕離開<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.kachinnews.com/news/2260-burma-army-sent-to-myitsone-dam-site-to-enforce-evictions.html|title=Burma army sent to Myitsone dam site to enforce evictions|last=Maung|first=Thomas|date=22 March 2012|publisher=Kachin News Group|accessdate=5 April 2012}}</ref>。{{Fact|同月中華人民共和國方面撤出了全部参建单位和设备|time=2018-01-07T07:31:01+00:00}}。

'''澳大利亚国际车展'''（{{lang-en|'''Australian International Motor Show'''}}）是在[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]][[悉尼|悉尼]]或[[墨尔本|墨尔本]]举办的一年一次的汽车展览，在2010年之前，墨尔本和悉尼的车展是单独举行，之后正式合并轮流举行。

== 沿革 ==
墨尔本国际车展的时间跨度是从1925年到2009年，<ref name="Motor Show Background and History">{{cite web|url=http://www.motorshow.com.au/AbouttheShow/History/tabid/80/Default.aspx |title=Motor Show Background and History |publisher=AIMS Joint Venture |accessdate=2010-07-24 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110319012952/http://www.motorshow.com.au/AbouttheShow/History/tabid/80/Default.aspx |archivedate=2011-03-19 }}</ref>是澳大利亚规模最大的车展。<ref>{{cite web | last = Harman | first = Alan | date = 2008-03-31 | url = http://wardsauto.com/ar/oz_drop_sydney/ | title = Oz Auto Makers Drop Out of Sydney Show | work = [[Ward's|Ward's]] | publisher = [[Penton_Media|Penton Media]] | accessdate = 2009-08-30}}</ref>它的名字之前叫“悉尼车展”，2004年开始叫“澳大利亚国际车展”。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.autoweb.com.au/cms/A_102389/newsarticle.html |title=New Name for Sydney's Motor Show |work=AutoWeb |publisher=Web Publications |date=2004-07-29 |accessdate=2007-10-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20050327065020/http://www.autoweb.com.au/cms/A_102389/newsarticle.html |archivedate=2005-03-27 }}</ref>

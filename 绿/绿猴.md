'''绿猴'''（''Chlorocebus sabaeus''）是[[绿猴属|绿猴属]]下的一种猴子<ref name= Kingdon>{{cite book |last=Kingdon |first=J. |year=1997 |title=The Kingdon Guide to African Mammals |publisher=Academic Press Limited |location=London |isbn=0-12-408355-2}}{{page needed|date=February 2015}}</ref>，分布于西非。

==体型==

绿猴有着金色的尾巴和灰绿色的毛皮。绿猴体型上两性差异明显，雄性个头较大。野生成年绿猴体重{{convert|3.9|-|8.0|kg|lbs}}，体长{{convert|420|-|600|mm|ft}}。雌性重{{convert|3.4|-|5.3|kg|lbs}}，体长{{convert|300|-|495|mm|ft}}。<ref name=cawthon>{{cite web |author=Cawthon Lang, K. A. |year=2006 |url=http://pin.primate.wisc.edu/factsheets/entry/vervet |title=Primate Factsheets: Vervet (Chlorocebus) Taxonomy, Morphology, & Ecology |accessdate=2007-08-13}}</ref>

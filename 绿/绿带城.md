'''绿带城'''（Greenbelt）是[[美国|美国]][[马里兰州|马里兰州]][[乔治王子县_(马里兰州)|乔治王子县]]的一个城市。<ref>{{gnis|597493}}</ref> 位于{{coord|39|0|2|N|76|53|18|W|type:city}}<ref name="GR1">{{cite web|url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/gazette.html|publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2011-04-23|date=2011-02-12|title=US Gazetteer files: 2010, 2000, and 1990}}</ref>，面积{{convert|6.34|sqmi|sqkm|2}}<ref name="Gazetteer files">{{cite web |title=US Gazetteer files 2010 |url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] |accessdate=2013-01-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/64vfLAeJ2?url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt |archivedate=2012-01-24 |df= }}</ref>。人口23,068人（[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]）。

绿带城是1930年代罗斯福新政时期联邦政府规划建设的三个绿色规划城镇之一，另外两个是威斯康辛州[[密尔沃基|密尔沃基]]附近的[[格林代尔|格林代尔]]和俄亥俄州[[辛辛那提|辛辛那提]]附近的[[格林希尔|格林希尔]]<ref name="Halpern">{{Citation  | last = Halpern  | first = Sue  | title = New Deal City  | newspaper = Mother Jones| date = May–June 2002  | accessdate = 2007-07-07  | url = http://www.motherjones.com/politics/2002/05/new-deal-city }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://greendaleoriginals.com/greenbelt.html|title=Greendale Originals - The shops, history and events in the Village of Greendale, Wisconsin.|publisher=|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120207045417/http://www.greendaleoriginals.com/greenbelt.html|archivedate=2012-02-07}}</ref>。

绿带城的大部分现在列为绿带城历史区，于1980年列入[[国家史迹名录|国家史迹名录]]。

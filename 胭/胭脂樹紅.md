'''胭脂樹紅'''（{{lang-en|Annatto}}）又稱為「婀娜多」，是從生長在[[熱帶|熱帶]]和[[亞熱帶|亞熱帶]]的[[紅木|紅木]]（又稱胭脂樹）種子中提取的物質，可以作為[[食用色素|食用色素]]將食物染成黃色、橘色，并添加特有的風味。

胭脂樹紅提取自包覆在紅木種子外面的紅色[[果皮|果皮]]。被用來為許多[[芝士|芝士]]（例如[[車打芝士|車打芝士]]、[[格洛斯特芝士|格洛斯特芝士]]）、[[加工乾酪|加工乾酪]]（例如[[美國芝士|美國芝士]]）或[[乳醬|乳醬]]（例如[[人造黃油|人造黃油]]）染色。一些[[熏魚|熏魚]]、[[調味料|調味料]]和[[零食|零食]]中也會加入胭脂樹紅。

紅木原產自[[南美洲|南美洲]]，傳播到了[[亞洲|亞洲]]的許多地區。在[[拉丁美洲|拉丁美洲]]和[[加勒比地區|加勒比地區]]，經常需要用到胭脂樹紅來製作菜肴。中南美洲的一些土著居民用它來製作[[人體彩繪|人體彩繪]]和[[口紅|口紅]]。因此有時紅木也被叫做“口紅樹”。成熟的紅木果實呈褐色或紅褐色，外面覆蓋著堅硬的短毛，果實完全成熟後就會裂開，露出裡面深紅色的種子。雖然可以產生可食用的胭脂樹紅，但是果實本身並不可食用。<ref>{{cite web |last= Smith |first= James |title= Annatto Extracts |work= Chemical and Technical Assessment |publisher= JECFA |year= 2006 |url= ftp://ftp.fao.org/ag/agn/jecfa/cta_annatto.pdf |format= PDF |accessdate= 3 Feb 2012 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>

== 歷史 ==

[[File:Bixa_orellana_fruit_roucou_kourou_2.jpg|thumb]][[庫魯|庫魯]]]]

胭脂樹紅被認為起源於[[巴西|巴西]]，當地人稱“urucum”。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.indio.net/taino/main/remedio/bija.htm |title=Bija - Achiote |publisher=Indio.net |date= |accessdate=2011-08-24 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110724104743/http://www.indio.net/taino/main/remedio/bija.htm |archivedate=2011年7月24日 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>最初可能並不是用來做食物添加劑的，而是用在人體彩繪、治療[[胃灼熱|胃灼熱]]和胃痛、驅逐昆蟲以及抵擋魔鬼上。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.getjamaica.com/Jamaican%20Annato.asp |title=Jamaican Annatto |publisher=GetJamaica.com |accessdate=2011-08-24 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110711090828/http://www.getjamaica.com/Jamaican%20Annato.asp |archivedate=2011年7月11日 |deadurl=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last= Smith |first= Nigel J.H. |title= Geography of Crop Plants |work= Geo 3315, Lecture Notes: Part 2 |publisher= Department of Geography, University of Florida |date= 2005 |url= http://www.geog.ufl.edu/courses/Fall_Syllabus/3315Part2.pdf |format= pdf |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20070922113838/http://www.geog.ufl.edu/courses/Fall_Syllabus/3315Part2.pdf |archivedate= 2007-09-22 }}</ref><ref>{{cite book |last= Lovera |first= José Rafael |title= Food Culture in South America |publisher= Greenwood Publishing Group |series= Food Culture Around the World |date= 2005 |page= 51 |url= http://books.google.com/books?id=paihl6TEZpUC&pg=PA51&lpg=PA51&dq=annatto+venezuela+onoto |isbn= 0-313-32752-1 |accessdate= 2011-08-24 }}</ref>在[[加勒比原住民|加勒比]]和[[南美洲原住民|南美洲原住民]]文化中有長期使用胭脂樹及其果實的歷史，[[阿茲特克人|阿茲特克人]]稱胭脂樹紅為 “achiotl”，16世紀見於墨西哥人的手稿畫。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://aic.stanford.edu/sg/bpg/annual/v16/bp16-05.html |title=Colorants Used During Mexico's Early Colonial Period |publisher=Stanford University |date=1997 |accessdate=2011-08-24 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718111553/http://aic.stanford.edu/sg/bpg/annual/v16/bp16-05.html |archivedate=2011年7月18日 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

在[[印度|印度]]，胭脂樹紅被用來點朱砂痣，以區分一個女性是否已婚。在[[菲律賓|菲律賓]]，它被稱“atsuete”，也被用在製作傳統食物中。<ref name="Spice Pages: Annatto" /><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.philippinesinsider.com/filipino-cuisine/common-spices-in-modern-philippine-recipes/ |title=Common Spices in Modern Philippine Recipes |publisher=PhilippinesInsider.com |accessdate=2011-08-24 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715075231/http://www.philippinesinsider.com/filipino-cuisine/common-spices-in-modern-philippine-recipes/ |archivedate=2011年7月15日 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

=== 食物染色 ===
[[File:Colby_Cheese.jpg|thumb]]]]
16世紀以來，胭脂樹紅就被用來為[[格洛斯特芝士|格洛斯特芝士]]染色，這樣一些制法低劣的芝士也能呈現出橙色色調，而這種色調本來應是指示高級芝士的標誌。因此後來這種做法被普及到了包括[[萊斯特芝士|萊斯特芝士]]和[[切達乾酪|切達乾酪]]等的製作中。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.britishcheese.com/doublegloucester |title='British Cheese Board' |publisher=BritishCheese.com |accessdate=2011-08-24 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110726112327/http://www.britishcheese.com/doublegloucester |archivedate=2011年7月26日 |deadurl=no }}</ref>

很多拉丁美洲的菜肴都會使用胭脂樹紅，西班牙人抵達後，也吸收了這一做法，例如在[[西班牙雞飯|西班牙雞飯]]中就會用到胭脂樹紅。在[[委內瑞拉|委內瑞拉]]，胭脂樹紅被稱為“onoto”<ref name="Spice Pages: Annatto">{{cite web|url=http://gernot-katzers-spice-pages.com/engl/Bixa_ore.html |title=Spice Pages: Annatto |publisher=Gernot Katzer |date=February 19, 2012 |accessdate=2012-12-02}}</ref>，並被用在[[佩里科_(食品)|佩里科]]等傳統菜肴中。

在[[歐盟|歐盟]]國家，胭脂樹紅擁有[[E編碼|E編碼]]E160b。在[[美國|美國]]，胭脂樹紅提取物被列為“免認證”的著色劑。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.access.gpo.gov/nara/cfr/waisidx_02/21cfr73_02.html |title=Title 21 Code of Federal Regulations part 73 |publisher=U.S. Government Printing Office |date= |accessdate=2011-08-24 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090509082220/http://www.access.gpo.gov/nara/cfr/waisidx_02/21cfr73_02.html |archivedate=2009年5月9日 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfcfr/CFRSearch.cfm?fr=73.30 |title=CFR - Code of Federal Regulations Title 21 |publisher= U.S. FDA |date=2011-04-01 |accessdate=2011-08-24}}</ref>

[[File:bixin.png|thumb]]，胭脂樹紅中包含70-80%的胭脂素<ref name="ntp">{{cite web|url=https://ntp.niehs.nih.gov/ntp/htdocs/chem_background/exsumpdf/bixin_508.pdf|title=Executive Summary Bixin|work=National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences|publisher=National Institutes of Health|date=Nov 1997|accessdate=2011-08-24|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721055506/http://ntp-server.niehs.nih.gov/?objectid=F59ACAC5-F1F6-975E-7C563568F5F7351B#selection|archivedate=2011年7月21日|deadurl=yes|author=}}</ref>]]

其粗提取物中包含脂溶性的[[胭脂素|胭脂素]]，可與水溶性的[[降胭脂樹素|降胭脂樹素]]發生[[皂化反應|皂化反應]]，其黃色至橙色的色彩就是由這兩種[[類胡蘿蔔素|類胡蘿蔔素]]形成的，胭脂素越多色彩就越偏向橙色。胭脂樹紅中包含4.5-5.5%的色素和70-80%的胭脂素。<ref name="ntp" />

== 健康 ==
{{medical small}}

胭脂樹紅中富含[[三烯生育酚|三烯生育酚]]和[[抗氧化劑|抗氧化劑]]。<ref>{{cite web |last1= Barrie |first1= Tan |last2= Mueller |first2= Anne |title= Vitamin E Component Dramatically More Effective at Supporting Heart Health |work= Annatto Tocotrienols: The Antioxidant Vitamin E Supports Heart Health |publisher= Vitamin Research Products |date= 2011 |url= http://www.vrp.com/antioxidants/annatto-tocotrienols-vitamin-e-component-dramatically-more-effective-at-supporting-heart-health |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110929015729/http://www.vrp.com/antioxidants/annatto-tocotrienols-vitamin-e-component-dramatically-more-effective-at-supporting-heart-health |archivedate= 2011-09-29 }} </ref>三烯生育酚被認為可以有抗[[血管新生|血管新生]]的作用，因此在防治癌癥中扮演著重要角色。<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/j.tifs.2011.07.004 |title=Health benefits of vitamin E in grains, cereals and green vegetables |year=2011 |last1=Miyazawa |first1=Teruo |last2=Nakagawa |first2=Kiyotaka |last3=Sookwong |first3=Phumon |journal=Trends in Food Science & Technology |volume=22 |issue=12 |pages=651}}</ref>

[[哥倫比亞|哥倫比亞]]的醫療衛生水平不發達，窮人們只有依靠一些土方法來治病，胭脂樹紅在哥倫比亞民間醫藥中可用來治療微生物來源的感染。<ref>{{cite web |title= Health benefits of Achiote (Bixa orellana) |publisher= Herbcyclopedia |url= http://www.herbcyclopedia.com/index.php?option=com_zoo&task=item&item_id=377&Itemid=193 |accessdate= 14 Dec 2012 |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20121224041825/http://www.herbcyclopedia.com/index.php?option=com_zoo&task=item&item_id=377&Itemid=193 |archivedate= 2012-12-24 }}</ref>除去類胡蘿蔔素帶來的好處外，胭脂樹紅中的[[萜|倍半萜烯]]也可以抗真菌活動。<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1007/s11418-010-0459-9 |title=A bioactive sesquiterpene from Bixa orellana |year=2010 |last1=Raga |first1=Dennis D. |last2=Espiritu |first2=Rafael A. |last3=Shen |first3=Chien-Chang |last4=Ragasa |first4=Consolacion Y. |journal=Journal of Natural Medicines |volume=65 |pages=206–11 |pmid=20882359 |issue=1}}</ref>類胭脂樹素有抗菌活性。<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=12801012 |year=2003 |last1=Galindo-Cuspinera |first1=V |last2=Westhoff |first2=DC |last3=Rankin |first3=SA |title=Antimicrobial properties of commercial annatto extracts against selected pathogenic, lactic acid, and spoilage microorganisms |volume=66 |issue=6 |pages=1074–8 |journal=Journal of food protection}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1021/jf048056q |title=Bioautography and Chemical Characterization of Antimicrobial Compound(s) in Commercial Water-Soluble Annatto Extracts |year=2005 |last1=Galindo-Cuspinera |first1=Veronica |last2=Rankin |first2=Scott A. |journal=Journal of Agricultural and Food Chemistry |volume=53 |issue=7 |pages=2524–9 |pmid=15796589}}</ref>

=== 過敏反應 ===

食用胭脂樹紅對大多數人來說都是沒有危險的，但是極少數人會對其產生[[過敏|過敏]]反應。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.webmd.com/vitamins-supplements/ingredientmono-23-ANNATTO.aspx?activeIngredientId=23&activeIngredientName=ANNATTO |title=ANNATTO: Uses, Side Effects, Interactions and Warnings |publisher=WebMD |date=1999-07-30 |accessdate=2011-08-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last= Magee |first= Elaine |url= http://blogs.webmd.com/healthy-recipe-doctor/2010/07/whats-up-with-food-dyes.html |title=What’s Up With Food Dyes? |work=Healthy Recipe Doctor |publisher=WebMD |date=2010-07-09 |accessdate=2011-08-24}}</ref>曾有幾起對胭脂樹紅的過敏記錄，但是它並不是所謂的“八大[[過敏原|過敏原]]”（牛奶、蛋、花生、堅果、魚、貝類、大豆、麥）之一。<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1097/WOX.0b013e3181c5be33 |title=An Allergy to Goldfish? Highlighting Labeling Laws for Food Additives |year=2009 |last1=Myles |first1=Ian A. |last2=Beakes |first2=Douglas |journal=World Allergy Organization Journal |volume=2 |issue=12 |pages=314–316 |pmid=20076772 |pmc=2805955}}</ref>[[美國食品藥品監督管理局|美國食品藥品監督管理局]]和[[內布拉斯加大學|內布拉斯加大學]]食物過敏研究及資源項目（Food Allergy Research and Resource Program）的專家不認為它應當算是一種主要過敏原。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allergenonline.org/databasebrowse.shtml |title=AllergenOnline Database |publisher=University of Nebraska-Lincoln |accessdate= 3 Feb 2012}}</ref>

1978年的一個實驗中，61個患有[[慢性蕁麻疹|慢性蕁麻疹]]和/或[[血管性水腫|血管性水腫]]的病人中，有56個在排除飲食中發現其癥狀是由服用了胭脂樹紅引起的。<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=150265 |year=1978 |last1=Mikkelsen |first1=H |last2=Larsen |first2=JC |last3=Tarding |first3=F |title=Hypersensitivity reactions to food colours with special reference to the natural colour annatto extract (butter colour) |issue=1 |pages=141–3 |journal=Archives of Toxicology. Supplement |doi=10.1007/978-3-642-66896-8_16 |series=Archives of Toxicology |isbn=978-3-540-08646-8 |volume=1}}</ref>

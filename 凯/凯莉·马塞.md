'''凯莉·杰奎琳·马塞'''（{{lang|en|Kylie Jacqueline Masse}}，{{bd|1996年|1月18日||}}）生于安大略省艾塞克斯县拉萨勒，是一名加拿大女子游泳运动员，主攻仰泳。马塞在10岁时开始练习游泳<ref name="np20160809">{{cite web|url=https://nationalpost.com/sports/olympics/overcome-with-emotions-kylie-masse-still-beaming-after-bronze-medal-swim|title=Overcome with emotions': Swimmer Kylie Masse still beaming after bronze medal-winning performance|publisher=National Post|date=2016-08-09|accessdate=2018-12-19}}</ref>。她在2014年进入[[多伦多大学|多伦多大学]]运动机能学及体育运动系<ref>{{cite web|url=https://kpe.utoronto.ca/varsity-blues-news/kylie-masse-blues-athlete-our-generation|title=Kylie Masse: the Blues athlete of our generation|publisher=UofT - Faculty of Kinesiology & Physical Education|date=2017-09-18|accessdate=2018-12-19}}</ref>，同时加入该校校队<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.swimmingworldmagazine.com/news/university-of-toronto-signs-backstroke-sprinter-kylie-masse/|title=University of Toronto Signs Backstroke Sprinter Kylie Masse|publisher=Swimming World Magazine|date=2014-01-20|accessdate=2018-12-19}}</ref>。她曾在2015-16赛季获得[[加拿大大學校際體育總會|加拿大大學校際體育總會]]颁发的年度最佳游泳运动员奖<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.macleans.ca/education/kylie-masse-olympian-on-campus/|title=Kylie Masse: Olympian on University of Toronto campus|publisher=Maclean's|date=2017-10-23|accessdate=2018-12-19}}</ref>。

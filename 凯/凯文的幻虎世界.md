'''《凯文的幻虎世界》'''（{{lang-en|Calvin and Hobbes}}），又名'''《凯文和跳跳虎》'''、'''《凯文与虎伯》'''，是美国卡通漫画家{{tsl|en|Bill Watterson||Bill Watterson}}所绘制的每日[[连环漫画|连环漫画]]，1985年11月18日首发，1995年12月31日完稿。该漫画常常被称为“最后一部伟大的报纸漫画”，<ref name="suellentrop2005">{{cite magazine |last=Suellentrop |first=Chris |date=2005-11-07 |title=Calvin and Hobbes: The last great newspaper comic strip |magazine={{tsl|en|Slate (magazine)||Slate}} |url=http://www.slate.com/articles/arts/culturebox/2005/11/calvin_and_hobbes.html |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151103141705/http://www.slate.com/articles/arts/culturebox/2005/11/calvin_and_hobbes.html |archivedate=2015-11-03 |deadurl=no |accessdate=2015-12-16}}</ref><ref name="ohehir2013">{{cite web |url=http://www.salon.com/2013/11/13/dear_mr_watterson_remembering_the_last_great_newspaper_comic/ |title='Dear Mr. Watterson': Remembering the last great newspaper comic |first=Andrew |last=O'Hehir |date=2013-11-13 |work={{tsl|en|Salon (website)||Salon}} |publisher=Salon Media Group |location=San Francisco, California |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924100511/http://www.salon.com/2013/11/13/dear_mr_watterson_remembering_the_last_great_newspaper_comic/ |archivedate=2015-09-24 |deadurl=no |accessdate=2015-11-21 |quote='Calvin and Hobbes' has been immensely influential – but mostly in TV animation, in stand-up and sketch comedy, and in graphic novels and in Internet culture. The newspaper comic, like the newspaper itself, has lost its social meaning.}}</ref>并且广受欢迎，影响深远，也在学术界被广泛研究。

'''凯莉·罗谢尔·麦基翁'''（{{lang-en|Kaylee Rochelle McKeown}}，{{bd|2001年|7月12日||}}）生于昆士兰州红崖<ref name="swimmingaustralia" />，是一名澳大利亚女子游泳运动员，主攻仰泳。她的姐姐[[泰勒·麦基翁|泰勒·麦基翁]]同样也是一名游泳选手<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theaustralian.com.au/sport/olympics/mckeown-sisters-plot-a-course-to-the-commonwealth-games/news-story/7dbeb0ffd4d1500d0e42de8ee2317977|title=McKeown sisters plot a course to the Commonwealth Games|publisher=The Australian|date=2018-01-26|accessdate=2018-12-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.perthnow.com.au/entertainment/confidential/taylor-and-kaylee-mckeowns-father-diagnosed-with-brain-cancer-ng-dcd26dc04c37685a793e24f241ee7f43|title=Taylor and Kaylee McKeown's father diagnosed with brain cancer|publisher=PerthNow|date=2018-10-04|accessdate=2018-12-19}}</ref>。凯莉小时候曾患有[[哮喘|哮喘]]，为了治好自己的病，加之家中正好有泳池，她开始练习游泳<ref name="gc2018"/>。

'''凯利·简·托赫斯特'''（{{lang|en|Kelly Jane Tolhurst}}<ref>{{London Gazette |issue=61961 |date=19 June 2017 |page=11776}}</ref>，{{bd|1978年|8月23日}}）是一位英国保守党政治人物，现任{{le|商务、能源和产业策略大臣|Secretary of State for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy}}政务次官、国会[[罗彻斯特和斯特鲁德_(英国国会选区)|罗彻斯特和斯特鲁德选区]]议员。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/politics/constituencies/E14000898 |title= Election 2015: Rochester & Strood parliamentary constituency | work =[[BBC_News|BBC News]] |date=9 June 2017 |accessdate=9 June 2017}}</ref>曾任政府助理党鞭、商务大臣国会私人秘书、国际发展大臣国会私人秘书、肯特郡{{le|梅德韦区议会|Medway Council}}西罗切斯特选区议员。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.kentonline.co.uk/medway/news/mp-resigns-as-local-councillor-158438/|title=MP resigns as local councillor|work=Kent Online|access-date=2018-01-13|language=en-GB}}</ref>支持英国留欧。<ref>https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-politics-eu-referendum-35616946</ref>

== 早年经历 ==
托赫斯特是罗切斯特船舶建筑业者莫里斯·托赫斯特的女儿，在当地柴普特高中接受教育。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kelly4rochesterandstrood.com/about-me|title=Personal website|publisher=MP |date= |accessdate=13 April 2018}}</ref>自2008年始，她与父亲一起经营一家名为托赫斯特协会的海洋调查公司，在此之前她从事市场营销工作。<ref>{{cite news| last = Holehouse | first = Matthew |url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/politics/conservative/11163114/Stay-at-home-mother-lined-up-to-halt-Ukip-advance.html|title=Stay-at-home mother lined up to halt Ukip advance|work=[[Daily_Telegraph|Daily Telegraph]]| date = 14 October 2014|accessdate=9 May 2015}}</ref> <ref>{{cite news| last = Letts | first = Quentin |url=http://www.tatler.com/article/quentin-letts-sketchbook-kelly-tolhurst|title=Stay-at-home mother lined up to halt Ukip advance|work=Tatler|date = 22 July 2015|accessdate=13 April 2018}}</ref>

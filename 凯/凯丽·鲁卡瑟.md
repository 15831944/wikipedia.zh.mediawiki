'''凯丽·鲁卡瑟·布朗'''({{lang-en|Clare Rewcastle Brown}})，某些报章以及新闻传媒使用'''克莱尔'''作为她的译名，是出生于[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[砂拉越|砂拉越州]]的[[英国|英国]][[调查报导|调查报导]]，网络媒体[[砂拉越报告|《砂拉越报告》]]和[[自由砂拉越电台|自由砂拉越电台]]创办人。其创办的电台以揭露砂拉越政治丑闻著称，在推动媒体自由上获得国际组织的肯定。<ref>{{en}}{{cite web|url=http://www.freemedia.at/press-room/public-statements/singleview/article/ipi-hands-2013-awards-to-two-women-journalists-killed-in-syria-independent-malaysian-radio-station.html |author=Bruce Blake & Barbara Trionfi |title=IPI hands 2013 awards to two women journalists killed in Syria, independent Malaysian radio station |publisher=International Press Institute |date=2013-05-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130507120247/http://www.freemedia.at/press-room/public-statements/singleview/article/ipi-hands-2013-awards-to-two-women-journalists-killed-in-syria-independent-malaysian-radio-station.html |archivedate=2013-05-07 }}</ref>

==生平简介==

1960年代，凯丽出生于英国殖民统治下的砂拉越，其母亲是当地的助产士。幼年时期，她的玩伴多是当地原住民。8岁那年，凯丽返回英国受教育，1983年她成为[[英国广播公司|英国广播公司]]记者。<ref>{{zh}}{{cite web|url=http://www.merdekareview.com/news_v2.php?n=17141|author=|title=创立自由砂拉越电台报真相，布朗弟媳揭发泰益贪污腐败|publisher=独立新闻在线|date=2011-02-25}}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

2005年，凯丽受邀到砂州出席环境会议，发现当地的非法伐木问题严重，因此开始通过[[:en:_Bruno_Manser|布鲁诺曼梳]]基金会调查当地的环境问题。此后，她持续撰写关于砂州环境课题的文章，揭发当地政治领袖转售原住民土地的恶行。<ref>{{en}}{{cite web|url= http://www.independent.co.uk/environment/green-living/fight-for-the-borneo-rainforest-gordon-brown-celebrates-the-role-of-journalist-clare-rewcastle-2237427.html|author=|title=Fight for the Borneo rainforest: Gordon Brown celebrates the role of journalist Clare Rewcastle |publisher=The Independent|date=2011-03-10}}</ref>

==《砂拉越报告》和自由砂拉越电台==

2010年，凯丽创办[[砂拉越报告|《砂拉越报告》]]和[[自由砂拉越电台|自由砂拉越电台]]，更积极地揭露砂拉越官员的贪污滥权行为。她也与社运分子合作，协助募款捐赠收音机予内陆居民，让他们能够接收非主流资讯。<ref>{{en}}{{cite web|url= http://www.independent.co.uk/environment/green-living/fight-for-the-borneo-rainforest-gordon-brown-celebrates-the-role-of-journalist-clare-rewcastle-2237427.html|author=Clare Rewcastle|title=Message from the founder of Radio Free Sarawak, Clare Rewcastle|publisher=Buy A Radio To Free Sarawak|date=2012-04-26}}</ref>因其新闻报导，凯丽多次接到黑函恐吓。<ref>{{en}}{{cite web|url= http://www.independent.co.uk/environment/green-living/fight-for-the-borneo-rainforest-gordon-brown-celebrates-the-role-of-journalist-clare-rewcastle-2237427.html|author=|title=Fight for the Borneo rainforest: Gordon Brown celebrates the role of journalist Clare Rewcastle |publisher=The Independent|date=2011-03-10}}</ref>2013年，砂拉越移民局开始拒绝让她入境砂州。<ref>{{zh}}{{cite web|url= http://www.orientaldaily.com.my/index.php?option=com_k2&view=item&id=63358:&Itemid=113|author=|title=《砂拉越报告》负责人被拒入境砂州|publisher=东方日报|date=2013-07-03}}</ref>

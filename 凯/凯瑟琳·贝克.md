'''凯瑟琳·贝克'''（{{lang-en|Kathleen Baker}}，{{bd|1997年|2月28日||}}）生于北卡罗来纳州温斯顿-撒冷，是一名美国女子游泳运动员，主攻仰泳。她的母亲曾是[[查尔斯顿学院|查尔斯顿学院]]游泳队成员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.charlotteobserver.com/sports/spt-columns-blogs/scott-fowler/article87268122.html|title=For swimmer Kathleen Baker, 19, road to Olympics ran between Winston-Salem, Charlotte|publisher=The Charlotte Observer|date=2016-07-01|accessdate=2018-12-13}}</ref>，姐姐雷切尔是[[华盛顿与李大学|华盛顿与李大学]]游泳队的成员<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.swimmingworldmagazine.com/news/sister-sister-rachel-kathleen-bakers-unbreakable-bond/|title=Sister, Sister: Rachel & Kathleen Baker's Unbreakable Bond|publisher=Swimming World Magazine|date=2015-01-13|accessdate=2018-12-13}}</ref>。凯瑟琳·贝克高中毕业于福赛斯乡村日校<ref>{{cite web|last1=Lowe|first1=Kegan|title=Winston-Salem's Kathleen Baker takes silver in 100m backstroke|url=http://www.journalnow.com/sports/olympics/winston-salem-s-kathleen-baker-takes-silver-in-m-backstroke/article_f7fab43f-d6a5-590c-8626-8c7e93e555ac.html|publisher=Winston-Salem Journal|date=2016-08-09|accessdate=2016-08-17}}</ref>，大学就读于[[加利福尼亞大學柏克萊分校|加利福尼亞大學柏克萊分校]]，她在大学期间代表[[加利福尼亚金熊|加利福尼亚金熊]]队参加国内比赛<ref>{{cite web|url=https://calbears.com/news/2018/8/2/womens-swimming-diving-kathleen-baker-to-turn-professional.aspx|title=Kathleen Baker To Turn Professional|work=University of California Golden Bears Athletics|date=2018-08-02|accessdate=2018-12-13}}</ref>。她曾在2010年被确诊患有[[克隆氏症|克隆氏症]]<ref>{{cite web|last1=Crouse|first1=Karen|title=Swimmer Kathleen Baker Overcomes Crohn's Disease to Become an Olympian|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/07/15/sports/olympics/kathleen-baker-crohns-disease-swimming-olympics.html?_r=0|publisher=New York Times|date=2016-07-14|accessdate=2016-08-17}}</ref>。

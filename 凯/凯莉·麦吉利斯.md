At Ancestry.com</ref><ref>[http://www.filmreference.com/film/8/Kelly-McGillis.html Kelly McGillis Biography (1957-)<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref><ref>[http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/453033732.html?dids=453033732:453033732&FMT=CITE&FMTS=CITE:AI&type=historic&date=Dec+10%2C+1955&author=%20&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=Miss+Snell+Picks+Date&pqatl=google]</ref>是一位美国女演员。她曾因电影《[[捍衛戰士|捍衛戰士]]》、《[[被告_(电影)|被告]]》和《{{link-en|证人 (1985年电影)|Witness (1985 film)|证人}}》得到[[金球獎_(影視獎項)|金球奖]]提名。

==早年生活==
1957年7月9日麦吉利斯出生于加利福尼亚州[[新港滩|新港滩]]，母亲是家庭主妇，父亲是医生。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.filmreference.com/film/8/Kelly-McGillis.html | title = Kelly McGillis Biography (1957-) | publisher = filmreference.com | access-date = 2008-01-27 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20080514004751/http://www.filmreference.com/film/8/Kelly-McGillis.html | archive-date = 2008-05-14 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref>{{cite news | url = https://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/453033732.html?dids=453033732:453033732&FMT=CITE&FMTS=CITE:AI&type=historic&date=Dec+10%2C+1955&author=&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=Miss+Snell+Picks+Date&pqatl=google | title = Miss Snell Picks Date | newspaper = [[洛杉磯時報|洛杉磯時報]] | date = 1955-12-10 | access-date = 2018-09-29 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20121105010154/http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/453033732.html?dids=453033732:453033732&FMT=CITE&FMTS=CITE:AI&type=historic&date=Dec+10%2C+1955&author=&pub=Los+Angeles+Times&desc=Miss+Snell+Picks+Date&pqatl=google | archive-date = 2012-11-05 | dead-url = no }}</ref>她有苏格兰裔爱尔兰，德国和威尔士血统。<ref>[https://www.collectors.com/entertainment-item/kelly-mcgillis-out-of-ireland-pbs-original-press-glossy-mov/-8733482004185975670 Kelly McGillis ‘Out Of Ireland’] - PBS</ref>

麦吉利斯在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]长大。<ref name="Stark 1985">{{cite news | url = http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20089958,00.html | title = Kelly McGillis Plays the Amish Love of Harrison Ford in ''Witness''—and Kisses Waitressing Goodbye | first = John | last = Stark | date = 1985-02-18 | work = [[人物_(雜誌)|人物 (雜誌)]] | access-date = 2012-04-13 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20121120183415/http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20089958,00.html | archive-date = 2012-11-20 | dead-url = no }}</ref>1975年高中毕业后，她搬到[[纽约|纽约]]并到[[茱莉亞學院|茱莉亞學院]]学习表演。<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20100476,00.html | title = Memoir of a Brief Time in Hell | first = Kelly | last = McGillis | date = 1988-11-14 | work = [[人物_(雜誌)|人物 (雜誌)]] | access-date = 2012-04-13 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20120423040551/http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20100476,00.html | archive-date = 2012-04-23 | dead-url = no }}</ref>1983年[[畢業|畢業]]。<ref name="Stark 1985" /><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.newjerseynewsroom.com/movies/top-gun-actress-kelly-mcgillis-working-for-nj-non-profit-enjoying-life |title='Top Gun' actress Kelly McGillis working for N.J. rehab, enjoying life |first=Alicia |last=Cruz |publisher=NewJerseyNewsRoom.com |date=2011-05-23 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130212004200/http://www.newjerseynewsroom.com/movies/top-gun-actress-kelly-mcgillis-working-for-nj-non-profit-enjoying-life |archivedate=2013-02-12 }}</ref>

==个人生活==

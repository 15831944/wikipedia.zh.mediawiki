'''凯蒂·阿奇博尔德'''，{{post-nominals|country=GBR|MBE}}（{{lang-en|Katie Archibald}}，{{bd|1994年|3月12日||}}）是一名英国女子自行车运动员，代表英国和[[苏格兰|苏格兰]]参赛<ref name="BBCScot">{{cite web| url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/cycling/24874867| title=Katie Archibald on track to reach cycling's summit| author=Keir Murray| date=2013-11-10| publisher=BBC Sport Scotland| accessdate=2013-11-15}}</ref>。凯蒂在正式练习自行车前曾接触过[[游泳|游泳]]<ref name="BCbio2013" />。她的父亲伊恩曾是一名[[田径|田径]]选手，哥哥约翰同样也是一名自行车运动员<ref name="brother">{{cite web |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/scotland/42865105 |title=Scottish cyclist John Archibald says sister's success lead him to victory |date=2018-04-06 |accessdate=2018-04-06 |work=BBC Sport}}</ref>。<ref name="goldcoast2018">{{cite web|url=https://results.gc2018.com/en/cycling-track/athlete-profile-n6025013-katie-archibald.htm|title=Cycling Track {{!}} Athlete Profile: Katie ARCHIBALD|work=Gold Coast 2018|accessdate=2019-03-14}}</ref>

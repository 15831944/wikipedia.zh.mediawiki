'''四氢合镍(0)酸镁'''是一种[[配位化合物|配位化合物]]，化学式为Mg<sub>2</sub>[NiH<sub>4</sub>]，它是单斜晶系的晶体，其中含有[NiH<sub>4</sub>]<sup>4−</sup>离子。<ref>Klaus Yvon. Complex Transition-Metal Hydrides <sub>（[https://www.ingentaconnect.com/content/scs/chimia/1998/00000052/00000010/art00017?crawler=true PDF]）</sub>. ''Chimia'' 52 (1998) 613-619. ISSN 0009-4293</ref>该化合物在220 °C时转变为[[立方晶系|立方晶系]]的Mg<sub>2</sub>NiH<sub>3.9</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Structural studies of the hydrogen storage material Mg<sub>2</sub>NiH<sub>4</sub>.  1.  Cubic high-temperature structure|date=1981-09-01|doi=10.1021/ic50223a006|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|pages=2776–2778|volume=20|issue=9|issn=0020-1669|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ic50223a006|accessdate=2019-02-11|author=K. Yvon, J. Schefer, F. Stucki}}</ref>

==制备==
该配合物可由[[镁|镁]]和[[镍|镍]]的金属粉末在[[氢气|氢气]]中加热反应得到。<ref name=Selvam>{{cite journal|title=Synthesis of Mg2FeH6, Mg2CoH5 and Mg2NiH4 by high-pressure sintering of the elements|volume=16|issue=9|pages=615–617|date=1991-01-01|issn=0360-3199|doi=10.1016/0360-3199(91)90085-W|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/036031999190085W|journal=International Journal of Hydrogen Energy|accessdate=2019-02-11|author=P. Selvam, K. Yvon}}</ref>
: 2 Mg + Ni + 2 H<sub>2</sub> → Mg<sub>2</sub>NiH<sub>4</sub>

金属间化合物Mg<sub>2</sub>Ni和氢气在350 °C反应也能得到Mg<sub>2</sub>NiH<sub>4</sub>。<ref name=Reilly>{{cite journal|title=Reaction of hydrogen with alloys of magnesium and nickel and the formation of Mg2NiH4|date=1968-11-01|doi=10.1021/ic50069a016|journal=Inorganic Chemistry|pages=2254–2256|volume=7|issue=11|issn=0020-1669|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ic50069a016|accessdate=2019-02-11|author=James J. Reilly, Richard H. Wiswall}}</ref>但另一种化合物MgNi<sub>2</sub>却不和氢气反应。<ref name=Reilly />

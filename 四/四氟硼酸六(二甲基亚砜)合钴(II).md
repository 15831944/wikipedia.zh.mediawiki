'''四氟硼酸六(二甲基亚砜)合钴(II)'''是一种[[配位化合物|配位化合物]]，化学式为[Co(C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>6</sub>OS)<sub>6</sub>](BF<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。这个离子化合物由[Co(C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>6</sub>OS)<sub>6</sub>]<sup>2+</sup>和{{chem|BF|4|-}}构成<ref>Van Leeuwen, P. W. N. M.; Groeneveld, Willem L. Complexes of ligands containing sulfinyl. V. Ligand field spectra of cobalt(II) and nickel(II) compounds. ''Recueil des Travaux Chimiques des Pays-Bas'', 1967. 86 (11): 1219-1226. ISSN:0165-0513</ref>。它在非极性溶剂中难溶，在空气中稳定。<ref name=Kutec>Kutek, F. Chemistry of the tetrafluoroborates. V. Coordination compounds of tetrafluoroborates of some transition metals with dimethyl sulfoxide. ''Collection of Czechoslovak Chemical Communications'', 1968. 33 (6): 1930-1932. ISSN:0010-0765</ref>

==制备==
将[Co(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>6</sub>](BF<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>溶解在热的[[二甲基亚砜|DMSO]]中，真空蒸发溶剂，析出晶体，晶体过滤后用[[丙酮|丙酮]]洗涤，用[[五氧化二磷|五氧化二磷]]干燥，得到纯净的[Co(DMSO)<sub>6</sub>](BF<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Phase Polymorphism of [Co(DMSO)6](BF4)2 Studied by Differential Scanning Calorimetry|journal=Zeitschrift für Naturforschung A|volume=61|issue=3-4|pages=180–188|issn=1865-7109|doi=10.1515/zna-2006-3-411|url=https://www.degruyter.com/view/j/zna.2006.61.issue-3-4/zna-2006-3-411/zna-2006-3-411.xml|date=2014|accessdate=2019-01-21|author=Anna Migdał-Mikuli, Łukasz Skoczylas, Elżbieta Szostak}}</ref>
: [Co(H<sub>2</sub>O)<sub>6</sub>](BF<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + 6 DMSO → [Co(DMSO)<sub>6</sub>](BF<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub> + 6 H<sub>2</sub>O
将氟硼酸钴的水合物溶解在丙酮中，加入DMSO，发生略微的放热反应，也能得到产物。<ref name=Kutec />另一种方法则是利用钴在DMSO存在下在[[氟硼酸|氟硼酸]]中发生电化学氧化反应来制备。<ref>Habeeb, Jacob J.; Said, Farouq F.; Tuck, Dennis G. Direct electrochemical synthesis of cationic complexes. ''Journal of the Chemical Society, Dalton Transactions: Inorganic Chemistry (1972-1999)'', 1981. 1: 118-120. ISSN:0300-9246</ref>

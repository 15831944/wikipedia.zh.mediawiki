'''四天工作制'''是一种工作场所或学校使其雇员或学生每[[星期|星期]]上班或上学四天而非传统的五天的一种安排<ref>Rex L. Facer II and Lori L. Wadsworth. 2010. “Four-day Workweeks: Current Research and Practice” Connecticut Law Review 42, (4): 1031-1046.</ref>。这种安排既有可能是因为[[弹性工时|弹性工作制]]，亦有可能是为了减少成本。

2008年，[[美国|美国]][[犹他州|犹他州]]州政府公务员开始执行每周星期一至星期四工作四天，每天工作10小时的安排<ref>Rex L. Facer II and Lori L. Wadsworth. 2010. “Four-day Workweeks: Current Research and Practice” Connecticut Law Review 42, (4): 1031-1046.</ref><ref name=Utah>{{cite news |title=Utah is going to a 4-day workweek |agency=Associated Press |date=July 3, 2008 |work=MSNBC |url=http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/25518225 }}</ref>。通过政府机构在星期五一天的关闭，犹他州政府期望可以减少在电费、供暖费及公车油费等费用上的运营成本<ref name=Utah/>。许多美国地方政府已执行四天工作制较长时间<ref>Lori L. Wadsworth, Rex L. Facer II, and Chyleen A. Arbon. 2010. “Alternative Work Schedules in Local Government: Qui Bono?” Review of Public Personnel Administration 30, (3): 322-340.</ref><ref>Rex L. Facer II and Lori L. Wadsworth. 2008. “Alternative Work Schedules and Work Family Balance: A Research Note.” Review of Public Personnel Administration 28, (2): 166-177.</ref><ref> Rex L. Facer II, Lori L. Wadsworth, and Chyleen Arbon. 2009. “Cities Leading the Way: The Use of Alternative Work Schedules by Cities. 2009 Municipal Yearbook. ICMA Press: Washington DC, pp. 28-33.</ref>。

[[美国|美国]][[夏威夷州|夏威夷州]]的[[公立学校|公立学校]]在2010年5月17日一天关闭<ref>{{cite news |title=Schools' New Math: The Four-Day Week |first=Chris Herring |date=March 8, 2010 |work=The Wall Street Journal |url=http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424052748704869304575104124088312524.html }}</ref>。美国农村地区超过100个[[学区|学区]]执行每周上四天学的安排。这些学区通常将每天的教学时间延长一小时或更长时间<ref name=rural>{{cite news |title=In rural areas, the four-day school week is growing in popularity |agency=Associated Press |first=Greg |last=Toppo |date=August 20, 2002 |work=The Christian Science Monitor |url=http://www.csmonitor.com/2002/0820/p14s02-lecs.html }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Four-Day School Weeks |work=National Conference of State Legislatures |accessdate=February 3, 2011 |url=http://www.ncsl.org/default.aspx?tabid=12934 }}</ref>。四天工作制在学校的实行通常是为了在[[交通|交通]]、供暖及[[代课教师|代课教师]]等方面节省开支<ref name=rural/>。

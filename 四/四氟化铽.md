'''四氟化铽'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为TbF<sub>4</sub>。它是一种白色晶体，为强氧化剂。它可由高纯的[[三氟化铽|三氟化铽]]和[[二氟化氙|二氟化氙]]、[[三氟化氯|三氟化氯]]或[[氟|氟]]气反应得到：<ref>无机化学丛书 第七卷 钪 稀土元素. 科学出版社. pp 244-246. 1. 卤素化合物</ref>
: 2 TbF<sub>3</sub> + F<sub>2</sub> → 2 TbF<sub>4</sub>

==性质==
四氟化铽在热水中迅速分解，生成[[三氟化铽|三氟化铽]]和少量的氟氧化铽（TbOF）。加热四氟化铽分解为三氟化铽和单质氟：<ref>{{cite journal|title=Thermal decomposition of curium tetrafluoride and terbium tetrafluoride|issn=0022-4596|date=1988-04-01|journal=Journal of Solid State Chemistry|volume=73|issue=2|doi=10.1016/0022-4596(88)90140-5|pages=524–530|url=https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0022459688901405|language=en|accessdate=2018-12-09}}</ref>
: 2 TbF<sub>4</sub> → 2 TbF<sub>3</sub> + F<sub>2</sub>↑
分解反应会生成Tb(TbF<sub>5</sub>)<sub>3</sub>这一混合价态的中间产物，其晶型和Ln(HfF<sub>5</sub>)<sub>3</sub>一致。<ref>Nikulin V V, Goryachenkov S A, Korobov M V, et al. [https://inis.iaea.org/search/search.aspx?orig_q=RN:17053937 On thermal stability of terbium tetrafluoride][J]. ''Zhurnal Neorganicheskoj Khimii'', 1985, 30(10): 2530-2533.</ref>
四氟化铽在加热时可以将[[三氟化钴|三氟化钴]]氧化为[[四氟化钴|四氟化钴]]：<ref>{{cite journal|title=Atomic fluorine in thermal reactions involving solid TbF4|doi=10.1016/S0022-1139(00)00259-1|date=2000-07-01|journal=Journal of Fluorine Chemistry|volume=104|issue=2|pages=291–295|issn=0022-1139|url=https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0022113900002591|language=en|accessdate=2018-12-09}}</ref>
: TbF<sub>4</sub> + CoF<sub>3</sub> → TbF<sub>3</sub> + CoF<sub>4</sub>↑
也能在320~460 °C将[60]富勒烯氟化。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://www.tandfonline.com/action/captchaChallenge?redirectUri=%2Fdoi%2Fabs%2F10.1080%2F10641229809350215|issue=3|doi=10.1080/10641229809350215|pages=469–479|title=Reaction of [60]Fullerene with Terbium(IV) Fluoride|volume=6|journal=Fullerene Science and Technology|date=1998-05|issn=1064-122X|language=en|accessdate=2018-12-13|author=O. V. Boltalina, A. Yu. Lukonin, V. K. Pavlovich, L. N. Sidorov, R. Taylor, A. K. Abdul-Sada}}</ref>

以四氟化铽为原料，和氯化钾在氟气气氛中反应，可以生成混合价态氟化物KTb<sub>3</sub>F<sub>12</sub>；<ref>{{cite journal|title=Synthesis and Crystal Structure of a New Mixed-Valence Terbium Fluoride, KTbIIITbIV2F12, and Related KLnIIIMIV2F12Compounds (MIV=Tb, Zr, Hf;LnIII=Ce–Lu)|doi=10.1006/jssc.1998.7837|date=1998-09-01|journal=Journal of Solid State Chemistry|volume=139|issue=2|pages=248–258|issn=0022-4596|url=https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0022459698978379|language=en|accessdate=2018-12-10}}</ref>在[[氟化铷|氟化铷]]-[[氟化铝|氟化铝]]-四氟化铽体系中，得到的是Rb<sub>2</sub>AlTb<sub>3</sub>F<sub>16</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Synthesis and crystal structure of Rb2AlTb3F16: a new mixed-valence terbium fluoride|doi=10.1016/S1293-2558(03)00131-6|date=2003-08-01|journal=Solid State Sciences|volume=5|issue=8|pages=1141–1148|issn=1293-2558|url=https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1293255803001316|language=en|accessdate=2018-12-10}}</ref>

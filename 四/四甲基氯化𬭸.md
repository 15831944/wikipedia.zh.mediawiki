'''四甲基氯化𬭸'''是一种季𬭸盐，化学式为[(CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>4</sub>P]Cl。

==制备==
它可由[[氯甲烷|氯甲烷]]和[[白磷|白磷]]在300~310 °C于Pyrex玻璃弹中反应得到。<ref name=Ludwig>Ludwig Maier. Organic phosphorus compounds. XXVII. The direct synthesis of tetramethylphosphonium halides{{de}}. ''Helvetica Chimica Acta'', 1966. 49 (8): 2458-2465. ISSN: 0018-019X</ref>

[(CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>P]<sub>4</sub>MoCl<sub>2</sub>和[[二氯甲烷|二氯甲烷]]的反应中会有产物之一的四甲基氯化𬭸生成。<ref>{{cite journal|journal=Journal of Organometallic Chemistry|volume=541|issue=1-2|language=en|date=1997-8|pages=367–375|doi=10.1016/S0022-328X(97)00088-0|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0022328X97000880|title=Synthesis, structure and properties of bisallyltris(trimethylphosphine) molybdenum(II) X-ray crystal structures of [MO(η1: η2-C3H5)2(PMe3)3] and PMe4+[MoCl4(PMe3)2]−|accessdate=2019-04-12|author=Cornelia Borgmann, Christian Limberg, Alexander Driess}}</ref>
==性质==
它受热分解得到[[三甲基膦|三甲基膦]]和[[氯甲烷|氯甲烷]]。<ref name=Ludwig />也有文献指出它的热分解产物是[[乙烯|乙烯]]、[[三甲基膦|三甲基膦]]和[[氯化氢|氯化氢]]。<ref>{{cite journal|journal=Journal of the Chemical Society (Resumed)|language=en|issn=0368-1769|date=1933|pages=989|doi=10.1039/jr9330000989|url=http://xlink.rsc.org/?DOI=jr9330000989|title=232. Influence of poles and polar linkings on the course pursued by elimination reactions. Part XIX. Thermal decomposition of phosphonium chlorides|accessdate=2019-04-12|author=G. F. Fenton, L. Hey, C. K. Ingold}}</ref>

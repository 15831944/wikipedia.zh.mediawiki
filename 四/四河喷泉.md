'''四河喷泉'''（{{lang-it|Fontana dei Quattro Fiumi}}）坐落于意大利罗马[[纳沃纳广场|纳沃纳广场]]中心。喷泉由[[济安·贝尼尼|济安·贝尼尼]]设计，建于1651年，是[[巴洛克艺术|巴洛克艺术]]高峰期的代表作。

四河喷泉代表文艺复兴时代地理学者心目中四大洲的四条大河：非洲的[[尼罗河|尼罗河]]<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.nietvervelen.nl/Rome/Rome%20-%20Piazza%20Navona%20-%20fontein%20detail.jpg |title=存档副本 |access-date=2008-11-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070929012844/http://www.nietvervelen.nl/Rome/Rome%20-%20Piazza%20Navona%20-%20fontein%20detail.jpg |archive-date=2007-09-29 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>，亚洲的[[恒河|恒河]]<ref>[http://www2.siba.fi/~kkoskim/rooma/pages/371_028B.HTM Piazza Navona]</ref>，欧洲的[[多瑙河|多瑙河]]，<ref>http://bhersh.warp10.org/photos/rome2004b/fountains/slides/piazza_navona_2.html{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>[http://www.scultura-italiana.com/Galleria/Bernini%20Gian%20Lorenzo/imagepages/image28.html Gian Lorenzo Bernini - Enea, Anchise e Ascanio in fuga da Troia (Roma, Galleria Borghese, 1618-1619) .jpg] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081014083158/http://www.scultura-italiana.com/Galleria/Bernini%20Gian%20Lorenzo/imagepages/image28.html |date=2008-10-14 }}</ref>和美洲的[[拉普拉塔河|拉普拉塔河]]<ref>[http://www.scultura-italiana.com/Galleria/Bernini%20Gian%20Lorenzo/imagepages/image29.html Gian Lorenzo Bernini - Enea, Anchise e Ascanio in fuga da Troia (Roma, Galleria Borghese, 1618-1619).jpg] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081013010842/http://www.scultura-italiana.com/Galleria/Bernini%20Gian%20Lorenzo/imagepages/image29.html |date=2008-10-13 }}</ref>。

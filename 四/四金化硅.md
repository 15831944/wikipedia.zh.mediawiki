'''四金化硅'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，[[化学式|化学式]]为SiAu<sub>4</sub>，在这种化合物中，[[金|金]]作为电子的接受体，显-1价。四金化硅作为金代硅烷的一种已被分离出来。<ref>Kiran, Xi Li, Hua-Jin Zhai, ''et al''. [https://www.researchgate.net/publication/8620668_SiAu4_Aurosilane <nowiki>[</nowiki>SiAu<sub>4</sub><nowiki>]</nowiki>: Aurosilane]. Angewandte Chemie, 2004. DOI: 10.1002/anie.200353602</ref>SiAu<sub>4</sub>的[[LUMO|LUMO]]和四个Si—Au成键轨道均与SiH<sub>4</sub>相似。<ref>D.P. WOODRUFF. [https://books.google.com.hk/books?id=uKZPnMw4uQMC&pg=PA126&lpg=PA126&dq=silicon+auride&source=bl&ots=W4g48LHygm&sig=0S9l4p1_Qjci6FmX3zDYOBjawe4&hl=zh-CN&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwjgn4_S0I_LAhUN6WMKHS_-AAUQ6AEIHTAA#v=onepage&q&f=false Atomic Clusters: From Gas Phase to Deposited]. 5. GOLD AS HYDROGEN IN Si-Au AND B-Au CLUSTERS. pp 126</ref>

此外，负价的金和[[硅|硅]]还可以形成Si<sub>3</sub>Au<sub>3</sub>等化合物。<ref>Boggavarapu Kiran, Xi Li, Hua-Jin Zhai, ''et al.'' [http://casey.brown.edu/chemistry/research/LSWang/publications/245.pdf Gold as hydrogen: Structural and electronic properties and chemical bonding in Si<sub>3</sub>Au<sub>3</sub><sup>+/0/−</sup> and comparisons to Si<sub>3</sub>H<sub>3</sub><sup>+/0/−</sup>]. The Journal of Chemical Physics. 125, 133204 (2006)</ref>

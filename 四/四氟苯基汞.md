'''四氟苯基汞'''是一种[[有机汞化合物|有机汞化合物]]，化学式为C<sub>18</sub>F<sub>12</sub>Hg<sub>3</sub>。

==制备==
四氟苯基汞可由[[四氟二碘苯|四氟二碘苯]]和[[汞|汞]]在200°C反应得到。<ref>Woodard, C. M.; Hughes, G.; Massey, A. G. Perfluorophenyl derivatives of the elements. XXIX. The synthesis of some heterocycles of mercury, sulfur, selenium, tellurium, arsenic and antimony. ''Journal of Organometallic Chemistry'', 1976. 112 (1): 9-19. DOI:10.1016/S0022-328X(00)89361-4</ref>

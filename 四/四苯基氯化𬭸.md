'''四苯基氯化𬭸'''是一种[[季𬭸盐|季𬭸盐]]，化学式为(C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>4</sub>PCl，可简写为Ph<sub>4</sub>PCl或PPh<sub>4</sub>Cl。它可以以无水物的形式结晶，这也是最常见的市售品形式。<ref>{{cite journal|first1=J. F. |last1=Richardson |first2=J. M. |last2=Ball |first3=P. M. |last3=Boorman |title=Structure of Tetraphenylphosphonium Chloride |journal=Acta Crystallographica |date=1986 |volume=C42 |pages=1271–1272 |doi=10.1107/S0108270186092612}}</ref>此外，它还存在一水合物<ref>{{cite journal|first1=E. E. |last1=Schweizer |first2=C. J. |last2=Baldacchini |first3=A. L. |last3=Rheingold |title=Tetraphenylphosphonium Chloride Monohydrate, Tetraphenylphosphonium Bromide and Tetraphenylphosphonium Iodide |journal=Acta Crystallographica |date=1989 |volume=C45 |pages=1236–1239 |doi=10.1107/S0108270189000363}}</ref>和二水合物<ref>{{cite journal|first1=A. J. |last1=Blake |first2=C. D. |last2=Garner |first3=J. M. |last3=Tunney |title=Tetraphenylphosphonium Chloride Dihydrate |journal=Acta Crystallographica |date=2003 |volume=E59 |page=o9–o10 |doi=10.1107/S1600536802021682}}</ref>。

四苯基氯化𬭸可由[[氯苯|氯苯]]和[[三苯基膦|三苯基膦]]在[[镍化合物|镍盐]]的催化下反应得到：<ref>{{cite journal|first1=David |last1=Marcoux |first2=André B. |last2=Charette |title=Nickel-Catalyzed Synthesis of Phosphonium Salts from Aryl Halides and Triphenylphosphine |journal=Adv. Synth. Catal. |date=2008 |volume=350 |pages=2967–2974 |doi=10.1002/adsc.200800542}}</ref>
:PhCl + PPh<sub>3</sub> → Ph<sub>4</sub>PCl

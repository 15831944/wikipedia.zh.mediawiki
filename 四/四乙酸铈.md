'''四乙酸铈'''是一种[[乙酸盐|乙酸盐]]，化学式为Ce(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>4</sub>。它可由[[臭氧|臭氧]]氧化Ce<sup>3+</sup>的[[乙酸|乙酸]]溶液得到。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Mechanisms of aromatic substitution.  Role of cation-radicals in the oxidative substitution of arenes by cobalt(III)|date=1973-10-01|doi=10.1021/ja00802a036|journal=Journal of the American Chemical Society|pages=7114–7123|volume=95|issue=21|issn=0002-7863|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/ja00802a036|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=J. K. Kochi, R. T. Tang, T. Bernath}}</ref><ref name=Michael>{{cite journal|title=Aromatic acetonylation promoted by manganese(III) and cerium(IV) salts|date=1984-05-01|doi=10.1021/jo00183a022|journal=The Journal of Organic Chemistry|pages=1603–1607|volume=49|issue=9|issn=0022-3263|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/jo00183a022|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Michael E. Kurz, Vijayalakshmi Baru, P. Nhi Nguyen}}</ref>它可以作为氧化剂进行芳环的丙酮基化反应（acetonylation），但其产率比[[三乙酸锰|三乙酸锰]] [Mn(CH<sub>3</sub>COO)<sub>3</sub>]低。<ref name=Michael />它也能参与芳香烃自由基硝甲基化反应（nitromethylation）。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Free-radical aromatic nitromethylation promoted by cerium(IV)|date=1981-11-01|doi=10.1021/jo00336a008|journal=The Journal of Organic Chemistry|pages=4672–4676|volume=46|issue=23|issn=0022-3263|url=https://doi.org/10.1021/jo00336a008|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=Michael E.

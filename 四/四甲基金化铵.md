[http://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2001/cc/b105648b#!divAbstract Synthesis and crystal structure determination of tetramethylammonium auride]. Chem. Commun., 2001, 2208-2209. DOI: 10.1039/B105648B</ref>

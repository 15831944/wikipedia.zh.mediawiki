'''迷你影集'''（{{Lang-en|miniseries 或 mini-series，在[[英國|英國]]又稱作serial}}），又稱'''迷你劇集'''或'''電視連續短劇'''，是一種在[[設定_(敘事學)|設定]]上集數甚少的[[連續|連續]]性[[電視劇|電視劇]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/miniseries|title=miniseries - definition of miniseries in English|accessdate=2017-05-12|author=|date=|work=Oxford Dictionaries {{!}} English|publisher=[[Oxford_University_Press|Oxford University Press]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.museum.tv/eotv/miniseries.htm|title=Miniseries|accessdate=2017-05-12|author=|date=|work=Encyclopedia of Television|publisher=The Museum of Broadcast Communications|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20170512023524/http://www.museum.tv/eotv/miniseries.htm|archivedate=2017-05-12}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://filmlexikon.uni-kiel.de/index.php?action=lexikon&tag=det&id=3894|title=Miniserie - Lexikon der Filmbegriffe|accessdate=2017-05-12|work=filmlexikon.uni-kiel.de}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/miniseries|title=Definition of MINISERIES|accessdate=2017-05-12|work=www.merriam-webster.com|language=en}}</ref>迷你影集之下又衍生出「限定劇」（{{Lang-en|limited series}}）及「事件劇」（{{Lang-en|event series}}）等電視劇種類。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://screenertv.com/news-features/what-is-the-difference-between-a-miniseries-limited-series-and-event-series/|title=What is the difference between a miniseries, limited series and event series?|date=2014-01-21|newspaper=Screener|accessdate=2017-05-12|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://filmescape.com/mini-series-event-series-limited-series-whats-the-difference|title=Mini Series, Event Series & Limited Series - What's the Difference?|author=|date=2015-04-25|work=|newspaper=FilmEscape|accessdate=2017-05-12|language=en-US|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20170512025117/http://filmescape.com/mini-series-event-series-limited-series-whats-the-difference|archivedate=2017-05-12}}</ref>

迷你影集與電視連續劇最大的不同是其集數已預先設定，且播出[[日期|日期]]通常不會延續超過一[[年|年]]。

'''港务局交通公司高速线'''（{{lang-en|PATCO Speedline}}，一般被称为'''PATCO High Speed Line'''，全称Port Authority Transit Corporation Speedline），是一条由[[特拉华河港务局|特拉华河港务局]]（Delaware River Port Authority）运营的[[地铁|地铁]]线路，该线路通过[[本杰明·富兰克林大桥|本杰明·富兰克林大桥]]横跨[[特拉华河|特拉华河]]，连接分处两岸的[[宾夕法尼亚州|宾夕法尼亚州]][[费城|费城]]及[[新泽西州|新泽西州]]的[[康登縣_(新澤西州)|康登郡]]。该线路在费城市内可以换乘到[[费城地铁|费城地铁]]上去，所以理论上讲可以视作费城地铁系统的一部分，但是要出闸才能换乘。<ref>{{cite news |title=Man attacked, robbed in Mellon Center concourse |author=Graham, Troy |url=http://articles.philly.com/2011-01-07/news/27015058_1_concourse-hair-appointment-federal-workers |newspaper=Philadelphia Inquirer |date=January 7, 2011 |accessdate=January 27, 2011}} The concourse connects to the Market-Frankford Line, the Ridge Avenue spur line, and the '''PATCO High Speed Line'''.</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Google Transit features PATCO schedule |author=Stillwell, Eileen |url=http://www.courierpostonline.com/article/20110120/NEWS01/110120043/Google-Transit-features-PATCO-schedule |newspaper=Courier-Post |date=January 20, 2011 |accessdate=January 27, 2011}} "If you're not a regular user of the '''PATCO Hi-Speedline'''"</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=DRPA to look at sale of PATCO |author=Barna, John |url=http://www.nj.com/gloucester/index.ssf?/base/news-6/1294302327240670.xml&coll=8 |newspaper=Gloucester County Times |date=January 6, 2011 |accessdate=January 27, 2011}} "Exploring the sale or privatization of the '''PATCO High Speed Line'''"</ref> 该线路24小时全天运营，也是[[纽约地铁|纽约地铁]]、[[紐新航港局過哈德遜河捷運|PATH]]及[[芝加哥地铁|芝加哥地铁]]以外美国唯一全天运营的系统。<ref name="PATCO Stations are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.">{{cite web | url=http://www.ridepatco.org/stations/routemap.html | title=PATCO Stations are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. | accessdate=2012-09-13}}</ref>

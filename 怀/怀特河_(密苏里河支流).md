'''怀特河'''（{{lang-en|White River}}）是[[密苏里河|密苏里河]]的一条支流，长约{{Convert|580|mi|km}}<ref name="NHD">{{Cite web|url=http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/|title=National Hydrography Dataset|accessdate=March 30, 2011|publisher=United States Geological Survey}}</ref>，流经[[美国州份|美国]]的[[內布拉斯加州|內布拉斯加州]]和[[南达科他州|南达科他州]]，这名称源自流经[[恶地国家公园|恶地国家公园]]时，河水中混入的火山灰和泥沙，而显得灰白的颜色<ref>Benke and Cushing, p. 445</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=http://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015027015455;view=1up;seq=375|title=South Dakota place-names, v.3|last=[[Federal_Writers'_Project|Federal Writers' Project]]|publisher=University of South Dakota|year=1940|page=6}}</ref>，流域面积约{{Convert|10200|mi2|km2|-3}}，其中约{{Convert|8500|sqmi}}在南达科他州<ref name="hogan">{{Cite book|title=The Geography of South Dakota|last=Hogan|first=Edward Patrick|last2=Fouberg|first2=Erin Hogan|publisher=The Center for Western Studies – [[Augustana_College_(South_Dakota)|Augustana College]]|year=2001|isbn=0-931170-79-6|edition=Third|location=Sioux Falls, SD}}</ref>。

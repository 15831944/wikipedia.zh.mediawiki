'''怀俄明'''（{{lang-en|Wyoming}}）是[[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[瓊斯縣_(愛阿華州)|琼斯县]]的一座城市，于1873年10月21日设立<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.city-data.com/city/Wyoming-Iowa.html |title= Wyoming, Iowa|publisher= ePodunk|accessdate=2012-08-31}}</ref>，名字来源于纽约[[怀俄明县|怀俄明县]]<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.epodunk.com/cgi-bin/genInfo.php?locIndex=7761|title= Profile for Wyoming, Iowa, IA|publisher= ePodunk|accessdate=2012-08-31}}</ref>。坐标为{{coord|42|3|33|N|91|0|18|W|type:city}}<ref name="GR1">{{cite web|url=https://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/gazette.html|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2011-04-23|date=2011-02-12|title=US Gazetteer files: 2010, 2000, and 1990}}</ref>。根据[[美国人口调查局|美国人口调查局]]数据，城市总面积为{{convert|0.51|sqmi|sqkm|2}}，且皆为陆地<ref name="Gazetteer files">{{cite web |title=US Gazetteer files 2010 |url=https://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt |publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]] |accessdate=2012-05-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/64vfLAeJ2?url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/files/Gaz_places_national.txt |archivedate=2012-01-24 }}</ref>。根据2010年人口普查数据，当地共有515人，243户，136个家庭，[[人口密度|人口密度]]为{{convert|1009.8|PD/sqmi|PD/km2|1}}；种族构成中，99.0%为白人，0.6%为土著，0.4%为两个或以上民族混血；居民年龄中位数为44.9岁；男女性别比例为50.5%与49.5%<ref name ="FactFinder">{{cite web|title=American FactFinder|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/nav/jsf/pages/index.xhtml|publisher=[[美国人口调查局|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2012-05-11}}</ref>。

'''槓鈴'''是一種[[舉重|舉重]]及[[負重訓練|負重訓練]]的器材，由一條鐵支及套在兩旁的鐵餅（槓片）所組成<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pickyguide.com/sports_and_recreation/barbells_guide.html|title=Pickyguide Guide to Barbells|publisher=Pickyguide.com|accessdate=2009-04-23|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090417193152/http://www.pickyguide.com/sports_and_recreation/barbells_guide.html|archivedate=2009年4月17日|deadurl=no}}</ref>。

==奧運標準==
===槓鈴===
====男子標準====
長{{convert|2.2|m|ft|2}}，重{{convert|20|kg|lbs|2}}，粗{{convert|50|mm|inch|2}}，最高負荷為{{convert|540|kg|lbs}}，槓鈴上刻有握手的記號<ref name="IWF Hand Book Annexes">{{cite web|title=IWF Hand Book 2009-2012 Annexes|url=http://www.iwf.net/wp-content/plugins/download-monitor/download.php?id=28|work=Hand Book 2009-2012 Annexes|publisher=International Weightlifting Federation|accessdate=September 10, 2012}}</ref>。

====女子標準====
長{{convert|2.05|m|ft|2}}，重{{convert|15|kg|lbs|2}}，粗{{convert|25|mm|inch|2}}<ref>http://www.eleikosport.se/en/46/product.aspx?ac=111-0150&cn=1</ref>。

'''峇里貓'''（Balinese）是一种长毛的[[家猫|家猫]]，毛色近似[[暹罗猫|暹罗猫]]，有天蓝色的眼睛。它实际就是由暹罗猫培育出来的长毛品种<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://breedguide.trupanion.com/cat/balinese|title=Balinese - Trupanion Breed Guide|website=breedguide.trupanion.com|language=en-us|access-date=2018-04-20}}</ref>。与暹罗猫一样，其性格十分活跃。<ref name=":0">{{Cite book |title=Barron's Encyclopedia of Cat Breeds: A Complete Guide to the Domestic Cats of North America |last=Helgren |first=J. Anne |publisher=Barron's Educational Series |year=2013 |isbn=9780764165801 |edition=2nd |location=Hauppauge, NY |oclc=818865672}}</ref>

虽然名字里有巴里二字，但它本身与[[巴厘岛|巴厘岛]]没有关系<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=http://www.dailymotion.com/video/x2hyew7|title=CATS 101 - Balinese [ENG] - Video Dailymotion|date=2015-02-24|website=Dailymotion|language=en|access-date=2018-04-20}}</ref>。

==历史==
[[File:Sylvia_Moberly_Holland.png|thumb]]

长毛的暹罗猫至少在1900年代就已经出现了<ref>{{Cite book |title=The Cat Fanciers' Association complete cat book |date=2004 |publisher=HarperResource |last=Siegal |first=Mordecai |isbn=0062702335 |edition=1st |location=New York, NY |oclc=56713821}}</ref>，后来逐渐在美国流行开来。美国插画家西尔维娅·霍兰德（Sylvia Holland）在1960至70年代建立了巴厘猫评定标准。<ref name=":0" />

=== 爪哇猫 ===
[[File:Ghislaine_6028.jpg|thumb]]
巴厘猫和某种短毛猫产生了[[爪哇猫|爪哇猫]]这一品种，一些猫协会认为它们不应算是两个品种<ref name=":5">{{cite web |url=http://cfa.org/Portals/0/documents/minutes/20080201-transcript.pdf |title=CFA Executive Board Meeting |year=2008 |publisher=CFA |pages=79-91 |access-date=9 May 2018}}</ref><ref name=":0" /><ref name=":3">{{Cite book |title=Balinese cats |last=Finne |first=Stephanie |publisher=ABDO Publishing |year=2015 |isbn=9781624033223 |location=Minneapolis, MN |oclc=864753000}}</ref>。

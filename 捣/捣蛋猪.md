在遊戲開始後，玩家亦可操縱氣球、汽水瓶、馬達、沙包等零件。

=== 角色 ===
小豬是遊戲的主要角色，但部分關卡會出現在睡觉的《[[愤怒的小鸟|愤怒的小鸟]]》中的鳥類，部分關卡中的鳥類角色如靠近小豬一點，就會跳上彈弓向小豬攻擊。<ref>{{cite news| title =“捣蛋猪”最新版1.20强势来袭 | url =http://roll.sohu.com/20130228/n367407671.shtml| publisher =IT168网 | pages = | date =2013-02-28 | accessdate = 2013-05-15 | quote =但要小心睡觉也睁着一只眼睛的愤怒小鸟“Angry Birds”!尽量悄悄地从它们身边溜过!在新增的30个黑夜飞行“Flight in the Night”关卡中驾驶飞行器，但是尽量保持低调!否则吵醒脾气暴躁的小鸟，后果可不堪设想! }}</ref>

=== 沙盒關卡 ===
在沙盒關卡內，玩家可以從多種零件中拼裝出可移動的物品，然後取得20個星盒。玩家可一次或分次取星盒，當玩家取得20個星盒後，就能過關。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.angrybirdsnest.com/bad-piggies-complete-sandbox-guide/ |title=Bad Piggies Complete Sandbox Guide |accessdate=2013-05-14 |author=AMslimfordy|quote=The goal in each level is to obtain all twenty (20) star boxes scattered across a massive, expansive arena-like level. You can use as many tries and as many contraptions as you’d like, but despite that aid, some of the boxes are very difficult.}}</ref><ref>{{cite video

== 外界評價 ==
有[[Pocket_Gamer|Pocket Gamer]]的玩家認為此遊戲有創意，而且讓人愛不釋手。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pocketgamer.co.uk/r/iPad/Bad+Piggies/review.asp?c=45256|title=Bad Piggies Review|author=Mark Brown|work=PocketGamer.co.uk|date=2012-09-26|accessdate=2012-12-17}}</ref>也有的玩家認為，《捣蛋猪》比[[憤怒鳥|憤怒鳥]]好玩很多<ref name="bbbBBB" />。有[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]的玩家給予《捣蛋猪》4星(滿分為5星)的分數。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/technology/appsblog/2012/sep/26/bad-piggies-angry-birds-review|title=Bad Piggies - review|author=Stuart Dredge|work=The Guardian|accessdate=2013-05-15}}</ref>

[[IGN|IGN]]把捣蛋猪評為2012年最好的電子遊戲。

在網上遊戲網站[[Games.co.za|Games.co.za]]上，《捣蛋猪》的得分是8.9分（滿分為10分）。<ref>[http://games.co.za/bad-piggies.html Play Bad Piggies online!]</ref>

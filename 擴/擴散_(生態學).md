在[[生態學|生態學]]中，'''擴散'''是指少數[[個體|生物個體]]從一個地點移動到另一個地點的過程，比如從出生地移動到其他地區，或從高[[密度制约|種群密度]]地區移動到低種群密度地區，或從[[種群分佈|種群分佈]]的中心往其他地區移動<ref>{{cite book|author1=Lisa A. Urry|author2=etal|title=Campbell Biology|edition=11th|pages=1181|ISBN=9780134093413|year=2017|pages=1181-1182}}</ref>。

==描述==
擴散可以分爲兩種：密度非依賴型擴散（Density-independent dispersal），或稱爲被動擴散（passive dispersal），是指生物利用環境中的動能進行得擴散，比如[[蒲公英|蒲公英]]種子的擴散<ref>{{cite journal|author=Maguire Jr., B.|year= 1963|title= The passive dispersal of small aquatic organisms and their colonization of isolated bodies of water|doi=10.2307/1948560|jstor=1948560|volume=33|journal=Ecological Monographs|page=161}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Nathan, R.|year=2001|title=The challenges of studying dispersal|url=http://bio.huji.ac.il/ese/PDFs/Nathan2001TREE.pdf|journal=Trends in Ecology & Evolution|doi=10.1016/S0169-5347(01)02272-8|volume=16|issue=9|pages=481|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130928204701/http://www.bio.huji.ac.il/ese/PDFs/Nathan2001TREE.pdf|archivedate=2013-09-28|df=}}</ref>。密度依賴型擴散（Density-dependent dispersal），是指生物個體權衡環境的好壞、種群密度、[[種內競爭|種內競爭]]的強度等因素後，向更適宜生存的地區移動的過程<ref>{{cite journal|author1=Johst, K.  |author2=Brandl, R.  |lastauthoramp=yes |year=1997|title= The effect of dispersal on local population dynamics|doi=10.1016/S0304-3800(97)00112-9|journal= Ecological Modelling|volume=104|pages=87}}</ref><ref name=Bowler>{{cite journal|author1=Bowler, D. E.  |author2=Benton, T.G.  |lastauthoramp=yes |year=2005|title= Causes and consequences of animal dispersal strategies: relating individual behaviour to spatial dynamics|journal= Biol. Rev. |volume=80|issue=2|pages= 205–225|doi=10.1017/S1464793104006645|pmid=15921049}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Fernández, L. |year=2015|title= Source-sink dynamics shapes the spatial distribution of soil protists in an arid shrubland of northern Chile|doi=10.1016/j.jaridenv.2014.10.007|journal= Journal of Arid Environment|volume=113|pages=121–125}}</ref>。

擴散和{{link-en|遷移|Migration (ecology)}}（Migration）的主要區別在於，遷移是大量個體從一個地點轉移到另一個地點的行爲，而擴散僅是少部分個體的行爲。對於獨居動物來說，遷移和擴散沒有本質上的區別<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.glyndwr.ac.uk/en/migrationanddispersal/|title=MIGRATION AND DISPERSAL|publisher=Wrexham Glyndwr University}}</ref>。擴散和[[地理擴散|地理擴散]]（Geodispersal）有一定區別，後者是和[[地理隔離|地理隔離]]相反的過程，指兩個最初被地理因素隔開的[[種群|種群]]或[[生態系統|生態系統]]因地理屏障的消失而融合的過程<ref name="AlbertReis2011">{{cite book|author1=James S. Albert|author2=Roberto E. Reis|title=Historical Biogeography of Neotropical Freshwater Fishes|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_Suu7a-ERdMC&pg=PA308|year=2011|publisher=University of California Press|page=308|isbn=978-0-520-26868-5}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Lieberman|year= 2005|title= Geobiology and paleobiogeography: tracking the coevolution of the Earth and its biota|journal= Palaeogeography, Palaeoclimatology, Palaeoecology |volume=219|pages= 23–33|doi=10.1016/j.palaeo.2004.10.012}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|doi=10.1016/j.palaeo.2004.10.012|title=Geobiology and paleobiogeography: Tracking the coevolution of the Earth and its biota|year=2005|last1=Lieberman|first1=Bruce S.|journal=Palaeogeography, Palaeoclimatology, Palaeoecology|volume=219|pages=23}}</ref>。

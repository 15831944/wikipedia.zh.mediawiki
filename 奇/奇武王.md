'''奇武'''（{{lang-sa|विचित्रवीर्य}}，[[IAST|IAST]]：{{IAST|Vicitravīrya}}），印度古代史诗《[[摩诃婆罗多|摩诃婆罗多]]》人物。他是[[福身王|福身王]]与王后[[贞信|贞信]]所育第二子，[[般度族|般度族]]与[[俱卢族|俱卢族]]的祖父<ref>{{cite book|title=Jaya: An Illustrated Retelling of the Mahabharata|ISBN=9780143104254|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=gzN2_fu0mIYC}}</ref>。据载，他同[[双马童|双马童]]一般俊美，能够轻松吸引住任何一个貌美的女人<ref name="sacred-text">{{cite web|url=http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m01/m01103.htm|title=''Shambava Parva'', Section CII.|publisher=''The Mahabharata'', Book 1: Adi Parva|website=Sacred-Texts.com|accessdate=2017-11-03|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170922025819/http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m01/m01103.htm|archive-date=2017-09-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

其兄长[[花钏王|花钏王]]在交战中英年早逝，奇武继其位统治[[哈斯蒂纳普尔|象城]]，并在[[毗湿摩|毗湿摩]]的帮助下找到了[[迦尸国|迦尸国]]公主[[安必迦|安必迦]]、[[安波利迦|安波利迦]]两位妻子<ref name="MKDV">{{cite book|title=The Mahabharata of Krishna-Dwaipayana Vyasa|ISBN=9781465526373|url=https://books.google.com.ph/books?id=ygA2240G74kC}}</ref>，两位妻子都如天女一般美丽，但他在享受七年婚姻后即逝世，未留子嗣<ref name="sacred-text"/>。而后，[[毗耶娑|毗耶娑]]行[[尼育伽|尼育伽]]之术，同其两位遗孀诞下二子，即[[持国|持国]]和[[般度|般度]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m01/m01107.htm|title=''Shambava Parva'', Section CVI.|publisher=''The Mahabharata'', Book 1: Adi Parva|website=Sacred-Texts.com|accessdate=2018-08-31|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209004215/http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m01/m01107.htm|archive-date=2018-02-09|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=FdIkaccgneAC|last=Parmeshwaranand|first=Swami|title=Encyclopaedic dictionary of Purāṇas|year=2001|publisher=Sarup & Sons|location=New Delhi|isbn=9788176252263|edition=1st|access-date=2018-08-31|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802001856/https://books.google.com/books?id=FdIkaccgneAC|archive-date=2018-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

'''奇塔门'''（{{lang-ur|{{nastaliq|چٹا دروازہ|ur}}}}；别称“白门”）是一个位于[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]{{le|拉合尔城墙|Walled City of Lahore}}的城门，建于1650年。<ref name=planning>{{cite book|title=Master Plan for Greater Lahore|date=1973|publisher=Master Plan Project Office|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=yGlPAAAAMAAJ&dq=chitta+gate+lahore&focus=searchwithinvolume&q=chitta+1650|accessdate=2017-10-07}}</ref>这座城门曾是拉合尔最初的[[德里门_(拉合尔)|德里门]]所在地和进入拉合尔城的主要入口。<ref name="uk">{{cite web|title=Lahore and its historic Gates|url=http://news.ukpha.org/2010/02/lahore-and-is-historic-gates/|publisher=The United Kingdom Punjab Heritage Association|accessdate=2017-10-07|date=2010-02-03}}</ref>

==词源==
这座城门的名字来源于曾经覆盖其表面的石灰膏。<ref>{{cite web|title=Chitta Gate|url=https://www.google.com/culturalinstitute/beta/asset/chitta-gate/LQGGgnr1n78-Qw|website=Walled City of Lahore Authority|publisher=Google Arts & Culture|accessdate=2017-10-07}}</ref>

==位置==
城门位于连接[[拉合尔堡|拉合尔堡]]和[[德里门_(拉合尔)|德里门]]的沙希古扎尔赫路（Shahi Guzargah，意为“皇家通道”）上。城门通向{{le|瓦齐尔汗·乔克广场|Wazir Khan Chowk}}，位于装饰华美的{{le|瓦齐尔汗清真寺|Wazir Khan Mosque}}和{{le|沙希哈曼|Shahi Hammam}}之间。城门与{{le|迪娜纳斯井|Well of Dina Nath}}和赛义德神殿（Shrine of Syed Sūf）相邻。<ref>{{cite book|title=Study No. P. P. & H.|date=1964|publisher=Government of Pakistan, Planning Commission, Physical Planning & Housing Section|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=sTFjAAAAIAAJ&q=chitta+gate+lahore&dq=chitta+gate+lahore&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiRvPiplN7WAhXmhFQKHQfbCkoQ6AEINDAD|accessdate=2017-10-07}}</ref>

==历史==
城门建于1650年，<ref name=planning/> 当时是[[莫卧儿帝国|莫卧儿帝国]]皇帝[[沙贾汉|沙贾汉]]统治时期。该城门是[[拉合尔|拉合尔]]最初的[[德里门_(拉合尔)|德里门]]，但被距离现在的城门大约100米的新德里门所取代。在莫卧儿时代，这座城门是进入拉合尔城的主要入口。<ref name="uk"/>

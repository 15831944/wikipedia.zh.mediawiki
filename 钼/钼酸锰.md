'''钼酸锰'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为MnMoO<sub>4</sub>。α-MnMoO<sub>4</sub>晶体属于[[单斜晶系|单斜晶系]]。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/268809383_Synthesis_and_Characterization_of_Manganese_Molybdate_for_Symmetric_Capacitor_Applications|title=Synthesis and Characterization of Manganese Molybdate for Symmetric Capacitor Applications (PDF Download Available)|website=ResearchGate|language=en|access-date=2017-03-10}}</ref>它在低温具有[[反铁磁性|反铁磁性]]。<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Ehrenberg|first=H.|last2=Schwarz|first2=B.|last3=Weitzel|first3=H.|date=2006-10-01|title=Magnetic phase diagrams of -MnMoO4|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0304885305011480|journal=Journal of Magnetism and Magnetic Materials|volume=305|issue=1|pages=57–62|doi=10.1016/j.jmmm.2005.11.027}}</ref>在630℃发生相变，转化为具有[[钨酸镍|NiWO<sub>4</sub>]]结构的''H''型MnMoO<sub>4</sub>。<ref name=Zhu>朱文祥. 无机化合物制备手册. 化学工业出版社, 2006. pp 263-264 【Ⅵ-111】 钼酸锰. ISBN 7-5025-8537-0</ref>

== 制备 ==
钼酸锰可以由[[氯化锰|氯化锰]]和[[钼酸钠|钼酸钠]]于180℃的水热条件下反应，产物用水和乙醇清洗、干燥，得到MnMoO<sub>4</sub>微晶。<ref>许家胜, 张杰, 贺祥珂. [http://xueshu.baidu.com/s?wd=paperuri%3A%285dd5a81033a1b0ef0390fde35af794e3%29&filter=sc_long_sign&tn=SE_xueshusource_2kduw22v&sc_vurl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.doc88.com%2Fp-0748393225745.html&ie=utf-8&sc_us=2536050185322264044 钼酸锰微晶的水热合成与光催化性能研究][J]. 化学研究与应用, 2014(6):914-917.</ref>
: MnCl<sub>2</sub> + Na<sub>2</sub>MoO<sub>4</sub> → MnMoO<sub>4</sub>↓ + 2 NaCl

450~550℃下烧结锰的氧化物（如[[一氧化锰|MnO]]、[[二氧化锰|MnO<sub>2</sub>]]等）和[[三氧化钼|三氧化钼]]的混合物，可以制得钼酸锰。<ref name=Zhu />
== 应用 ==
MnMoO<sub>4</sub>有着快速的假电位（pseudocapacitive）的[[氧化还原反应|氧化还原反应]]<ref name=":1">{{Cite journal|last=Yan|first=Xiaodong|last2=Tian|first2=Lihong|last3=Murowchick|first3=James|last4=Chen|first4=Xiaobo|date=2016-03-01|title=Partially amorphized MnMoO4for highly efficient energy storage and the hydrogen evolution reaction|url=http://xlink.rsc.org/?DOI=C6TA00744A|journal=J. Mater. Chem. A|language=en|volume=4|issue=10|pages=3683–3688|doi=10.1039/c6ta00744a|issn=2050-7496}}</ref>，可用作水溶液中[[双电层电容器|双电层电容器]]的活性电极材料<ref name=":0" />和[[水电解|氢析出]][[催化剂|催化剂]]。<ref name=":1" />

'''假如王子睡着了'''（{{lang-en|''The Dreaming Man''}}）是一部爱情喜剧，导演是[[王郢_(导演)|王郢]]，主演有[[陈柏霖|陈柏霖]]、[[林允|林允]]、[[张云龙_(演员)|张云龙]]和[[陈思_(SNH48)|陳思]](SNH48)。是[[华特迪士尼影业集团|华特迪士尼影业集团]]投资的首部华语爱情电影。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2017/film/asia/disney-strikes-chinese-language-development-pact-with-wudi-pictures-1202429403|title=Disney Strikes Chinese-Language Development Pact With Wudi Pictures|date=May 16, 2017|work=Variety}}</ref>电影已在2017底初映，且将会在台灣2018年初上映。<ref name=disney>{{cite web|url=http://ent.sina.com.cn/m/c/2017-05-10/doc-ifyeychk7225607.shtml|title=陈柏霖林允《假如王子睡着了》将于年底上映|date=10 May 2017|work=Sina}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://ent.sina.com.cn/m/f/2017-05-12/doc-ifyfeivp5616525.shtml?markt=apx1494653109973|title=林允亮相《加勒比海盗5》首映 将领衔迪士尼新片|date=May 12, 2017|work=Sina}}</ref>

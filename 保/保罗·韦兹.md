'''保罗·韦兹'''（{{lang-en|'''Paul Weitz'''}}，{{bd|1965年|11月19日|||catIdx=W韦兹}}）出生于[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]][[纽约市|纽约市]]，为美国电影制作人、[[编剧|编剧]]、[[导演|导演]]，是[[克里斯·魏茲|克里斯·魏茲]]的哥哥。

== 生平 ==
1965年，保羅·韋茲出生于美国纽约州[[纽约市|纽约市]]。母亲苏珊·科勒（Susan Kohner）是美國女演员，有[[猶太|猶太]]血統和墨西哥血統<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0463435/bio|title=Susan Kohner - Biography|work=IMDb}}</ref>。父亲约翰·韦兹（John Weitz）是时装设计师兼小说家<ref>[http://www.nytimes.com/2002/10/04/nyregion/john-weitz-79-fashion-designer-turned-historian-dies.html John Weitz, 79, Fashion Designer Turned Historian, Dies]</ref>。保羅·韋茲的父亲和祖父都是[[犹太人|犹太人]]<ref>{{cite web|first=Gay|last=Pauley|url=https://www.upi.com/Archives/1983/01/04/Many-facets-of-John-Weitz/2282410504400/|title=Many facets of John Weitz|work=United Press International|date=January 4, 1983|accessdate=April 23, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Naomi|last=Pfefferman|url=http://jewishjournal.com/mobile_20111212/101733/|title=Paul Weitz on dads, De Niro and “Being Flynn”|work=Jewish Journal|date=March 5, 2012|accessdate=April 23, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Paula|last=Chin |url=http://people.com/archive/john-weitz-vol-38-no-17/|title=John Weitz|work=People Magazine|date=October 26, 1992|accessdate=April 24, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Naomi|last=Pfefferman|url=http://jewishjournal.com/culture/arts/92681/|title=An Immigrant Struggles for a ‘Better Life’ for His Son|work=Jewish Journal|date=June 1, 2011|accessdate=April 24, 2018}}</ref>。外祖母Lupita Tovar是墨西哥女演員，信奉[[天主教|天主教]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.irishtimes.com/life-and-style/people/lupita-tovar-dracula-star-who-became-the-sweetheart-of-mexico-dies-aged-106-1.2876554|title=Lupita Tovar: ‘Dracula’ star who became the ‘Sweetheart of Mexico’ dies, aged 106|work=The Irish Times|date=November 21, 2016|accessdate=April 24, 2018}}</ref><ref>[http://www.jewishjournal.com/arts/article/about_two_boys_20030314/ Paul Weitz ]</ref><ref>[http://www.interfaithfamily.com/arts_and_entertainment/popular_culture/Interfaith_Celebrities_Inglourious_Basterds_Surprises_and_Connections.shtml Paul Weitz]</ref>。外祖父Paul Kohner是猶太人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0463434/bio|title=Paul Kohner - Biography|work=IMDb}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Adrian|last=Florido|url=https://www.npr.org/2016/11/15/502111417/mexican-film-actress-lupita-tovar-dies-at-106|title=Mexican Film Actress Lupita Tovar Dies At 106|work=National Public Radio|date=November 15, 2016|accessdate=April 24, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first=Oliver |last=Lyttelton|url=http://www.indiewire.com/2012/04/5-things-you-may-not-know-about-douglas-sirks-imitation-of-life-111627/|title=5 Things You May Not Know About Douglas Sirk’s ‘Imitation Of Life’|work=IndieWire|date=April 17, 2012|accessdate=April 24, 2018}}</ref>。

保羅·韋茲曾经在Allen-Stevenson School学校读书，后来在[[卫斯理大学|卫斯理大学]]毕业。

'''保持佔有'''（{{lang-la|'''uti possidetis'''}}），亦稱'''佔领地保有'''，是[[国际法|国际法]]中的一项原则，主张除非[[條約|條約]]另行規定，否則{{link-en|交戰團體|Belligerent|交戰國}}在戰爭結束後可以保有其在戰爭期間藉由武力所佔有的[[領土|領土]]和[[財產|財產]]。<ref name="Uti possidetis Law & Legal Definition">{{cite web|title=Uti possidetis Law & Legal Definition|url=http://definitions.uslegal.com/u/uti-possidetis/|publisher=USLegal, Inc. (uslegal.com)|accessdate=16 August 2010}}</ref>

与保持佔有相对的国际法原則為[[战前状态|战前状态]]（{{lang-la|status quo ante bellum}}），主张交战各方恢復到战争发生前的势力状态。戰爭結束時，敵對雙方簽訂的條約可以採納保持佔有原則，或戰前狀態原則，或兩者之組合。如果條約對於藉由武力所佔領的領土和財產之歸屬沒有特別規定，則以保持佔有原則優先。<ref name="Uti possidetis Law & Legal Definition" />

保持佔有原則是古典國際法承認的領土移轉方式，但晚近則不再承認其為取得領土的合法方式，或對其取得領土的法律效力有所爭論<ref>{{cite journal | author = 李明峻 | date = 2005年6月 | title = 從國際法角度看琉球群島主權歸屬 | url = http://www.tisanet.org/quarterly/1-2-4.pdf | journal = 台灣國際研究季刊 | volume = 1 | number = 2 | page = 59}}</ref>。隨著《[[聯合國憲章|聯合國憲章]]》於1945年問世，藉由武力取得領土違反《聯合國憲章》，因此保持佔有原則已不再是取得武力征服之領土的可行方式<ref>{{cite web | author = Fozia Lone | title = Uti Possidetis Iuris | url = http://www.oxfordbibliographies.com/view/document/obo-9780199796953/obo-9780199796953-0065.xml | website = {{link-en|牛津線上參考書目資料庫|Oxford Bibliographies|Oxford Bibliographies}} | date = March 23, 2012 | accessdate = June 20, 2016 | doi = 10.1093/obo/9780199796953-0065 | language = en | quote = However, with the adoption of the UN Charter in 1945, uti possidetis no longer served as a justified principle to seal title to territory acquired by conquest.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | author = {{link-it|朱塞佩·納西|Giuseppe Nesi|Giuseppe Nesi}} | title = Uti possidetis Doctrine | url = http://opil.ouplaw.com/view/10.1093/law:epil/9780199231690/law-9780199231690-e1125 | website = Oxford Public International Law | date = January 2011 | accessdate = June 20, 2016 | language = en | subscription = yes | quote = This notion of uti possidetis is no longer viable since the well-established principles on the use of force do not allow for any acquisition of territory that is in violation of the United Nations Charter.}}</ref>。

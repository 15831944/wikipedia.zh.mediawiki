'''保時捷瑪卡'''（Porsche Macan）是[[保時捷|保時捷]]的一款[[跨界休旅車|跨界休旅車]]，2014年上市。

==歷史==
保時捷瑪卡自2010年11月開始開發<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.porsche.com/uk/aboutporsche/pressreleases/pcgb/?lang=none&pool=international-de&id=2010-11-29-02 |title=Porsche press release 29 November 2010 |publisher=Porsche.com |date=2010-11-29 |accessdate=2016-11-17}}</ref>，保時捷公司在2011年3月正式對外宣佈開發計劃，2012年將其命名為“瑪卡”<ref>{{cite web |url=http://press.porsche.com/news/release.php?id=694 |title=Name for new Porsche SUV is Macan |publisher=Press.porsche.com |date= |accessdate=2017-08-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150415063934/http://press.porsche.com/news/release.php?id=694 |archive-date=2015-04-15 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。

2013年[[洛杉磯車展|洛杉磯車展]]<ref>{{cite web|last=Wilkinson|first=Leo|title=Porsche Macan SUV unveiled|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/motoring/car-manufacturers/porsche/10462521/Porsche-Macan-SUV-unveiled.html|work=Daily Telegraph|accessdate=3 March 2014|date=20 November 2013}}</ref>和[[東京車展|東京車展]]<ref>{{cite web|last=Mihalascu|first=Dan|title=Porsche Debuts Macan, Calls It the Sports Car of Compact SUVs|url=http://www.carscoops.com/2013/11/porsche-officially-reveals-macan-calls.htmll|work=Carscoops|accessdate=10 March 2014|date=20 November 2013}}{{Dead link|date=2019年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>上面世，2014年春開始在歐洲上市，當時只有Macan S、Macan Turbo兩個型號<ref>{{cite web|title=Porsche Macan price and release date|url=http://www.autoexpress.co.uk/porsche/macan/64159/porsche-macan-price-and-release-date|work=Auto Express|accessdate=10 June 2013|date=29 May 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Seyth Miersma Nov 20th 2013 at 12:01AM |url=http://www.autoblog.com/2013/11/20/2014-porsche-macan-official-specs-pictures/ |title=Porsche finally gives up the goods on 2015 Macan |publisher=Autoblog.com |date=2013-11-20 |accessdate=2017-08-13}}</ref>。

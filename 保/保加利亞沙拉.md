'''保加利亞沙拉'''<ref>[http://www.gustos.ro/retete-traditionale/bulgaria/salata-bulgareasca-1.html Salata bulgareasca]</ref>（保加利亞語：''Шопска салата''；波斯尼亞語和克羅埃西亞語：''Šopska salata''；{{lang-ro|Salata bulgărească}}；{{lang-cs|Šopský salát}}；{{lang-pl|Szopska sałata}}；{{lang-sq|Salat Shope}}；{{lang-hu|Sopszka saláta}})是[[保加利亞|保加利亞]]的一種冷盤[[沙拉|沙拉]]，流行於[[巴爾幹|巴爾幹]]和中歐地區<ref>[http://books.google.bg/books?id=1Dz0srxxDFoC&pg=PA101&dq=shopska+salad&hl=bg&sa=X&ei=GYtiUaXzKcbUPPXvgcAE&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=shopska%20salad&f=false Culinary Cultures of Europe: Identity Diversity And Dialogue, Stephen Mennell, Darra J. Goldstein, Kathrin Merkle, Fabio Parasecoli, Council of Europe, 2005, ISBN 9287157448, p. 101.]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.bg/books?id=NTo6c_PJWRgC&pg=RA3-PA67&dq=shopska+salad&hl=bg&sa=X&ei=wItiUeq5FYLCO6H8geAF&ved=0CF4Q6AEwCA#v=onepage&q=shopska%20salad&f=false Food Cultures of the World Encyclopedia, Ken Albala, ABC-CLIO, 2011, ISBN 0313376263, p. 67.]</ref><ref>Mangia Bene! New American Family Cookbooks, Kate DeVivo, Capital Books, 2002, ISBN 1892123851, [https://books.google.bg/books?id=pKC53rTid4cC&pg=PA170&dq=shopska+salad&hl=en&sa=X&ei=gfVKVf2EItGy7Qaf74DABw&ved=0CDUQ6AEwBA#v=onepage&q&f=false p. 170.]</ref>。

保加利亞沙拉的原料包括[[番茄|番茄]]、[[黃瓜|黃瓜]]、[[洋蔥|洋蔥]]、[[辣椒|辣椒]]、[[香菜|香菜]]等。保加利亞沙拉起源於1960年代，是旅遊宣傳的一部分<ref>Raymond Detrez, ''Historical Dictionary of Bulgaria'', 3rd ed, 2015, ISBN 978-1-4422-4179-4, [https://books.google.com/books?id=hywaBgAAQBAJ&pg=PA451 p. 451]</ref>。

保加利亞沙拉是保加利亞早期社會主義的產物，也是社會主義食譜中唯一的倖存者<ref>[http://www.monitor.bg/article?id=331193 в-к "Монитор", Шопската салата забъркана в „Балкантурист”, Паулина Йоргова, 21.03.2012.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140504204909/http://www.monitor.bg/article?id=331193 |date=2014-05-04 }}</ref>。

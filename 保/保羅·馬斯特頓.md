'''保羅·馬斯特頓'''（{{lang-en|Paul Masterton}}；{{bd|1985年|11月2日}}）是[[英國|英國]][[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]的一位政治人物，黨籍是[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]。自2017年開始，他是[[東倫弗魯郡_(英國國會選區)|東倫弗魯郡]]選區選出的[[英國眾議院|英國眾議院]]議員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.barrheadnews.com/news/15338003.Conservative_candidate_wins_East_Renfrewshire_seat_in_General_Election/|title=Conservative candidate wins East Renfrewshire seat in General Election|website=Barrhead News}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.parliament.uk/biographies/commons/paul-masterton/4625|title=Paul Masterton MP|website=UK Parliament}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.glasgowsouthandeastwoodextra.co.uk/news/politics/it-s-a-win-for-the-conservatives-paul-masterton-and-a-resounding-one-at-that-1-4470746|title=It’s a win for the Conservatives Paul Masterton - and a resounding one at that!|website=www.glasgowsouthandeastwoodextra.co.uk}}</ref>。在當選國會議員之前，他曾競選[[蘇格蘭議會|蘇格蘭議會]]議員。馬斯特頓畢業於[[鄧迪大學|鄧迪大學]]<ref>{{Who's Who | surname = Masterton | othernames = Paul | id = U289513 | volume = 2018 | edition = February 2018 online | accessed = 13 February 2018 }}</ref>。

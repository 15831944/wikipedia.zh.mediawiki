<!-- 定義與症狀 -->
'''椎管狹窄'''是因為{{tsl|en|spinal canal|椎孔|}}或[[椎間孔|椎間孔]]不正常狹窄而壓迫到[[脊髓|脊髓]]或{{tsl|en|nerve roots|神經根|}}的一種狀態<ref name=NIH2017Over>{{cite web|title=Spinal Stenosis|url=https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/spinal-stenosis/advanced|website=National Institute of Arthritis and Musculoskeletal and Skin Diseases|accessdate=19 December 2017|language=en|date=11 April 2017}}</ref>。其症狀包括上肢或下肢的疼痛、{{tsl|en|paraesthesia|感覺異常|}}或無力<ref name=NIH2017Sym/>，症狀一般為漸進發展，身體向前彎曲時則會緩解<ref name=NIH2017Sym/>。嚴重的患者可能會有[[尿失禁|尿失禁]]、[[糞便失禁|糞便失禁]]或[[性功能障碍|性功能障碍]]<ref name=NIH2017Sym>{{cite web|title=Spinal Stenosis|url=https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/spinal-stenosis/advanced#tab-symptoms|website=National Institute of Arthritis and Musculoskeletal and Skin Diseases|accessdate=19 December 2017|language=en|date=11 April 2017}}</ref>。

<!-- 成因和診斷 -->
椎管狹窄的成因很多，包括[[骨关节炎|退化性關節炎]]、[[類風濕性關節炎|類風濕性關節炎]]、{{tsl|en|spinal tumor|脊髓腫瘤|}}、創傷、{{tsl|en|Paget's disease of the bone|骨的佩吉特病|佩吉特病}}、[[脊椎側彎|脊椎側彎]]、[[椎間滑脫|椎間滑脫]]和基因缺陷如[[軟骨發育不全症|軟骨發育不全症]]<ref name=NIH2017Cau>{{cite web|title=Spinal Stenosis|url=https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/spinal-stenosis/advanced#tab-causes|website=National Institute of Arthritis and Musculoskeletal and Skin Diseases|accessdate=19 December 2017|language=en|date=11 April 2017}}</ref>。椎管狹窄可依受影響的節段{{tsl|en|cervical spinal stenosis|頸椎狹窄|頸椎}}、[[胸椎|胸椎]]和{{tsl|en|lumbar spinal stenosis|腰椎狹窄|}}<ref name=Can2014>{{cite book|last1=Canale|first1=S. Terry|last2=Beaty|first2=James H.|title=Campbell's Operative Orthopaedics E-Book|date=2012|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=978-0323087186|page=1994|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=1Bvfn-1ZRWgC&pg=PA1994|language=en}}</ref>，腰椎狹窄最常見，其次是頸椎狹窄<ref name=Can2014/>。診斷則有賴臨床症狀和[[醫學影像|醫學影像]]<ref name=Dom2010/>。

<!-- 預防和治療 -->
治療的方式有藥物治療、{{tsl|en|back brace|背架|}}和手術<ref name=NIH2017Over/>。常用的藥物有[[非甾体抗炎药|非甾体抗炎药]]、[[对乙酰氨基酚|对乙酰氨基酚]]或[[皮質類固醇|皮質類固醇]]<ref name=NIH2017Tx>{{cite web|title=Spinal Stenosis|url=https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/spinal-stenosis/advanced#tab-treatment|website=National Institute of Arthritis and Musculoskeletal and Skin Diseases|accessdate=19 December 2017|language=en|date=11 April 2017}}</ref>。伸展運動可能是有用的方式<ref name=NIH2017Sym/>，一般也會建議避免做特定的動作<ref name=NIH2017Over/>。手術通常只有在所有方法都無效時才會施行，最常使用的術式為{{tsl|en|decompressive laminectomy|椎板切除術|椎板減壓切除術}}<ref name=NIH2017Tx/>。

<!-- 流行病學與歷史 -->椎管狹窄的盛行率高達8%<ref name=Dom2010>{{cite book|last1=Domino|first1=Frank J.|title=The 5-Minute Clinical Consult 2011|date=2010|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9781608312597|page=1224|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=BbJjfMjDM7cC&pg=PA1224|language=en}}</ref>，通常發生在50歲以上的人<ref name=NIH2017Risk>{{cite web|title=Spinal Stenosis|url=https://www.niams.nih.gov/health-topics/spinal-stenosis/advanced#tab-risk|website=National Institute of Arthritis and Musculoskeletal and Skin Diseases|accessdate=19 December 2017|language=en|date=11 April 2017}}</ref>，男性和女性的比率沒有明顯區別<ref name=AO2013>{{cite web|title=Lumbar Spinal Stenosis|url=https://orthoinfo.aaos.org/en/diseases--conditions/lumbar-spinal-stenosis/|website=OrthoInfo - AAOS|accessdate=19 December 2017|date=December 2013}}</ref>。第一個以現代醫學描述此種現象的為{{tsl|en|Antoine Portal|安東·波爾塔|}}<ref name=Boo2008>{{cite book|last1=Boos|first1=Norbert|last2=Aebi|first2=Max|title=Spinal Disorders: Fundamentals of Diagnosis and Treatment|date=2008|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=9783540690917|pages=21–22|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=kFhZGjFwjVYC&pg=PA21|language=en}}</ref>，但此種現象最早可以回溯到[[古埃及|古埃及]]時期<ref name=Boo2008/>。

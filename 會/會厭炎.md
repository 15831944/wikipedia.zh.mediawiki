<!-- 定義及症狀 -->
'''會厭炎'''（Epiglottitis）是[[會厭|會厭]]部位（舌根部上方的軟骨，可避免食物進入[[氣管|氣管]]）的[[炎症|發炎]]<ref name=Pink2015>{{cite book|last1=Hamborsky|first1=j|title=Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Epidemiology and Prevention of Vaccine-Preventable Diseases|date=2015|publisher=Public Health Foundation|isbn=9780990449119|page=Chapter 8|edition=13|url=https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/pubs/pinkbook/hib.html|accessdate=14 July 2016|chapter=Haemophilus influenzae type b|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160720235241/http://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/pubs/pinkbook/hib.html|archivedate=20 July 2016}}</ref>。症狀一般會出現的很快，症狀包括吞嚥困難，也會造成流口水、聲音改變、發燒以及呼吸加快等症狀<ref name=Ric2016/><ref name=AFP2012>{{cite journal|last1=Zoorob|first1=R|last2=Sidani|first2=MA|last3=Fremont|first3=RD|last4=Kihlberg|first4=C|title=Antibiotic use in acute upper respiratory tract infections.|journal=American Family Physician|date=1 November 2012|volume=86|issue=9|pages=817–22|pmid=23113461}}</ref>，因為會厭在[[上呼吸道|上呼吸道]]，因此吞嚥問題也會影響[[呼吸|呼吸]]<ref name=Pink2015/>，人們可能會讓上半身往前傾，以{{le|三腳架姿態|Tripod position}}讓呼吸道打開<ref name=Ric2016/>。當症狀惡化時，會有{{le|喘鳴|Stridor}}及[[發紺|發紺]]的情形<ref name=Ric2016/>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
以往最常見的會厭炎病因是由於[[流感嗜血桿菌|流感嗜血桿菌]]的感染<ref name=Ric2016>{{cite journal|last1=Richards|first1=AM|title=Pediatric Respiratory Emergencies.|journal=Emergency medicine clinics of North America|date=February 2016|volume=34|issue=1|pages=77–96|pmid=26614243|doi=10.1016/j.emc.2015.08.006}}</ref>，不過在有相關疫苗後，會厭炎大部份是由其他細菌感染所造成<ref name=Ric2016/>。其他可能病因包括會厭部位的[[灼傷|灼傷]]以及創傷<ref name=Ric2016/>。最準確的診斷方式是直接檢視會厭部位<ref name=West2013>{{cite journal|last1=Westerhuis|first1=B|last2=Bietz|first2=MG|last3=Lindemann|first3=J|title=Acute epiglottitis in adults: an under-recognized and life-threatening condition.|journal=South Dakota medicine : the journal of the South Dakota State Medical Association|date=August 2013|volume=66|issue=8|pages=309–11, 313|pmid=24175495}}</ref>。若有會厭炎，頸部X光可能會看到{{le|拇指征|thumbprint sign}}，但也有可能在會厭炎的情形下，沒有出現拇指征<ref name=Ric2016/>。

<!-- 預防及治療 -->
[[流感嗜血桿菌疫苗|流感嗜血桿菌疫苗]]可以有效的預防會厭炎，自1980年代開始就已開始採用<ref name=Sch2015>{{cite book|last1=Schlossberg|first1=David|title=Clinical infectious disease|date=2015|isbn=9781107038912|page=202|edition=Second|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=meFwBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA202|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160816002723/https://books.google.com/books?id=meFwBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA202|archivedate=2016-08-16}}</ref>。若是已暴露在致病菌中的高危險群，也可以用[[抗生素|抗生素]][[利福平|利福平]]來預防<ref name=Black2004>{{cite book|last1=Blacklow|first1=Neil R.|title=Infectious diseases|date=2004|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|location=Philadelphia|isbn=9780781733717|page=461|edition=3rd|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=91altE1evAsC&pg=PA461|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160816003125/https://books.google.com/books?id=91altE1evAsC&pg=PA461|archivedate=2016-08-16}}</ref>。治療最重要的部份是保護呼吸道，一般會透過[[气管插管|气管插管]]來進行<ref name=Ric2016/>。也可能會預射抗生素，例如[[頭孢曲松|頭孢曲松]]，也可能會用[[萬古黴素|萬古黴素]]或是[[克林黴素|克林黴素]]<ref name=AFP2012/><ref name=Sch2015/>。一般也會使用[[皮質類固醇|皮質類固醇]]<ref name=Ric2016/>。若有適當的治療，患病兒童的死亡率約1%，成人約7%<ref name=West2013/>。

<!-- 流行病學及預後 -->
在使用流感嗜血桿菌疫苗來預防之後，會厭炎的病例下降了95%<ref name=Ter2015>{{cite book|last1=Des Jardins|first1=Terry|title=Clinical Manifestations & Assessment of Respiratory Disease|date=2015|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9780323358972|page=529|edition=7|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=PjQwBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA529|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160815232934/https://books.google.com/books?id=PjQwBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA529|archivedate=2016-08-15}}</ref>，以往是嬰幼兒最容易感染，現在是年齡較大的兒童及成人較容易罹患<ref name=Sch2015/>。在美國每十萬名兒童中，一年約有1.3個兒童會罹患會厭炎<ref name=Ric2016/>，成人則是每十萬人中會有1至4人罹患<ref name=Text2016>{{cite book|title=Textbook of Adult Emergency Medicine|date=2014|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9780702054389|page=291|edition=4|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=gP1sAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA291|accessdate=15 July 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160815204745/https://books.google.com/books?id=gP1sAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA291|archivedate=15 August 2016}}</ref>。會厭炎在開發中國家比較常見<ref>{{cite book|last1=Boons|first1=Geert-Jan|title=Carbohydrate-Based Vaccines and Immunotherapies.|date=2009|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|location=Hoboken|isbn=9780470473276|page=1222|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=FfOzFBnZWlkC&pg=PA122|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160815212229/https://books.google.com/books?id=FfOzFBnZWlkC&pg=PA122|archivedate=2016-08-15}}</ref>。兒童患病的死亡率約為6%，不過若有插管，可以降到1%以下<ref>{{cite book|title=Infectious Diseases|date=2004|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9780781733717|page=461|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=91altE1evAsC&pg=PA461|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170206185244/https://books.google.com/books?id=91altE1evAsC&pg=PA461|archivedate=2017-02-06}}</ref>。

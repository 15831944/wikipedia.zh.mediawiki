'''伯斯'''（{{lang-sco|Pairth}},<ref>[http://www.scotslanguage.com/books/view/1/823/Names%20in%20Scots%20-%20Places%20in%20Scotland Scots Language Centre: Scottish Place Names in Scots]</ref> {{lang-gd|Peairt}}）<ref>[http://www.gaelicplacenames.org/databasedetails.php?id=154 Ainmean-Àite na h-Alba ~ Gaelic Place-Names of Scotland]</ref>為[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]中部的城市，位於[[泰河|泰河]]河畔，伯斯為[[珀斯-金罗斯|珀斯-金罗斯]][[苏格兰行政区划|議會範圍]]的[[行政中心|行政中心]]，也為過去[[伯斯郡|伯斯郡]]的[[郡治|郡治]]。根據2008年的估計，伯斯人口約44,820人<ref name="Population Estimates">{{cite web |url=http://www.gro-scotland.gov.uk/files2/stats/population-estimates/08mye-localities-table2.xls |title=Mid-2008 Population Estimates – Localities in order of size |publisher=General Register for Scotland |year=2008 |accessdate=11 September 2010 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100702235600/http://www.gro-scotland.gov.uk/files2/stats/population-estimates/08mye-localities-table2.xls |archivedate=2010年7月2日 }}</ref>。

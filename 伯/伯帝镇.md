'''伯帝'''<ref>{{cite web|title=伯帝镇|url=https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E4%BC%AF%E5%B8%9D%E9%95%87|website=百度百科|accessdate=2017-10-08}}</ref>或'''波地'''<ref>{{cite web|title=波地|url=http://www.visitcalifornia.com/cn/attraction/%E6%B3%A2%E5%9C%B0|website=Visit California|accessdate=2017-10-08}}</ref>（{{lang-en|Bodie}}）是[[美国|美国]][[加州|加州]]最著名的[[鬼镇|鬼镇]]之一，位于[[内华达山脉_(美国)|内华达山脉]]东面的[[莫诺县|莫诺县]]。伯帝镇被列为美国[[国家史迹名录|国家史迹名录]]、[[美国国家历史名胜|美国国家历史名胜]]和[[加州历史名胜|加州历史名胜]]，每年接待20万旅客。<ref>{{cite journal|last=DeLyser|first=Dydia|date=December 1999|title=Authenticity on the Ground: Engaging the Past in a California Ghost Town|journal=Annals of the Association of American Geographers|publisher=Association of American Geographers|volume=89|issue=4|page=602|doi=10.1111/0004-5608.00164}}</ref>

在[[加利福尼亚淘金潮|加利福尼亚淘金潮]]时伯帝镇最高达到了5~7千人口，<ref>{{cite journal|last=Jimenez|first=Corri Lyn|title=Bodie, California: Understanding the Architecture and Built Environment of a Gold Mining Town|version=Master's Thesis|publisher=University of Oregon|year=2000}}</ref>等1940年代已经成为了鬼城。<ref name="moffat">{{cite book |last=Moffat |first=Riley |title=Population History of Western U.S. Cities and Towns, 1850–1990 |publisher=Scarecrow Press, Inc. |location=Lanham, Maryland |year=1996 |page=21 |isbn=0-8108-3033-7}}</ref>

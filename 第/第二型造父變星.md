'''第二型造父變星'''（Type II Cepheid）是[[變星|變星]]的一種，它的脈動通常介於1至50天之間<ref name=wallerstein02>{{cite journal |bibcode=2002PASP..114..689W |title=The Cepheids of Population II and Related Stars |author1=Wallerstein, George |volume=114 |year=2002 |pages=689 |journal=The Publications of the Astronomical Society of the Pacific |doi=10.1086/341698 |issue=797}}</ref><ref name=so08>{{cite journal |bibcode=2008AcA....58..293S |title=The Optical Gravitational Lensing Experiment. The OGLE-III Catalog of Variable Stars. II.Type II Cepheids and Anomalous Cepheids in the Large Magellanic Cloud |author1=Soszyński, I. |author2=Udalski, A. |author3=Szymański, M. K. |author4=Kubiak, M. |author5=Pietrzyński, G. |author6=Wyrzykowski, Ł. |author7=Szewczyk, O. |author8=Ulaczyk, K. |author9=Poleski, R. |volume=58 |year=2008 |pages=293 |journal=Acta Astronomica}}</ref>。第二型造父變星通常是[[金屬量#第二星族星|第二星族星]]，因此是老年、通常貧金屬、低質量的恆星<ref name=wallerstein02/>。 

過去 (歷史上) 常常以[[室女座W型變星|室女座W型變星]]取代第二型造父變星的名稱，但現在依據周期分為幾個子群，室女座W型變星只是其中的一種。週期在1-4天的屬於[[武仙座BL型變星|BL Her子群]]、10-20天的屬於[[室女座W型變星|W Vir子群]]、週期超過20天的屬於[[金牛座RV型變星|RV Tau子群]]<ref name=wallerstein02/><ref name=so08/>。  

第二型造父變星遵守著[[周光關係|周光關係]]，因此是重要的[[標準燭光|標準燭光]]<ref name=so08/>，但是它們比同週期的[[經典造父變星|經典造父變星]]暗了1.6星等<ref name=cwtop>{{cite web|title=Cepheid Variables|url=http://www.caglow.com/info/wtopic/cepheid|work=Weekly Topic|publisher=Caglow|accessdate=30 January 2012}}</ref>。第二型造父變星常用來測量和建立[[銀心|銀河中心]]、[[球狀星團|球狀星團]]和[[星系|星系]]的距離<ref name=ku02>{{cite journal |bibcode=2003AcA....53..117K |title=The Optical Gravitational Lensing Experiment. Population II Cepheids in the Galactic Bulge |author1=Kubiak, M. |author2=Udalski, A. |volume=53 |year=2003 |pages=117 |journal=Acta Astronomica|arxiv = astro-ph/0306567 }}</ref><ref name=mat06>{{cite journal |bibcode=2006MNRAS.370.1979M |title=The period-luminosity relation for type II Cepheids in globular clusters |author1=Matsunaga, Noriyuki |author2=Fukushi, Hinako |author3=Nakada, Yoshikazu |author4=Tanabé, Toshihiko |author5=Feast, Michael W. |author6=Menzies, John W. |author7=Ita, Yoshifusa |author8=Nishiyama, Shogo |author9=Baba, Daisuke |volume=370 |year=2006 |pages=1979 |journal=Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society |doi=10.1111/j.1365-2966.2006.10620.x |issue=4|arxiv = astro-ph/0606609 }}</ref><ref name=feast08>{{cite journal |bibcode=2008MNRAS.386.2115F |title=The luminosities and distance scales of type II Cepheid and RR Lyrae variables |author1=Feast, Michael W. |author2=Laney, Clifton D. |author3=Kinman, Thomas D. |author4=van Leeuwen, Floor |author5=Whitelock, Patricia A. |volume=386 |year=2008 |pages=2115 |journal=Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society |doi=10.1111/j.1365-2966.2008.13181.x |issue=4}}</ref><ref name=majaess09a>{{cite journal |bibcode=2009MNRAS.398..263M |title=Characteristics of the Galaxy according to Cepheids |author1=Majaess, Daniel J. |author2=Turner, David G. |author3=Lane, David J. |volume=398 |year=2009 |pages=4206 |arxiv=0903.4206 <!--|class=astro-ph.GA--> |doi=10.1111/j.1365-2966.2009.15096.x |journal=Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society}}</ref><ref name=majaess09>{{cite journal |bibcode=2009AcA....59..403M |title=Type II Cepheids as Extragalactic Distance Candles |author1=Majaess, D. |author2=Turner, D. |author3=Lane, D. |volume=59 |year=2009 |pages=403 |journal=Acta Astronomica}}</ref><ref name=majaess09c>{{cite journal |bibcode=2010JAVSO..38..100M |title=RR Lyrae and Type II Cepheid Variables Adhere to a Common Distance Relation |author1=Majaess, D. J. |volume=38 |year=2010 |pages=100 |journal=The Journal of the American Association of Variable Star Observers}}</ref><ref name=mat09>{{cite journal |bibcode=2009MNRAS.397..933M |title=Period-luminosity relations for type II Cepheids and their application |author1=Matsunaga, Noriyuki |author2=Feast, Michael W. |author3=Menzies, John W. |volume=397 |year=2009 |pages=933 |journal=Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society |doi=10.1111/j.1365-2966.2009.14992.x |issue=2}}</ref>。

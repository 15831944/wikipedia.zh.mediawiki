'''第69届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1996年电影|1996年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1997年3月24日下午18点（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上21点）在美国加利福尼亚州[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的神殿礼堂举行，共颁发24个类别的[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）。晚会通过[[美国广播公司|美国广播公司]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播，由吉尔·凯茨担任[[制片人|制片人]]，路易斯·{{lang|en|J}}·霍维茨导演<ref>{{cite news | last = Williams | first = Jeannie | title = Crystal takes on role of Oscar host again|work= USA Today | date = 1996-11-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last = Hindes | publisher = Variety Media | first = Andrew | title = Horvitz set to direct Oscar telecast | accessdate = 2016-03-08 | work = Variety | deadurl = no | url = http://variety.com/1996/scene/vpage/horvitz-set-to-direct-oscarcast-1117466566/ | date = 1996-12-19 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304121431/http://variety.com/1996/scene/vpage/horvitz-set-to-direct-oscarcast-1117466566/ | archivedate = 2016-03-04 }}</ref>。男演员[[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]第5度担任主持人，他曾于1990年的[[第62届奥斯卡金像奖|第62届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]中首度出任主持，上一次则是1993年的[[第65届奥斯卡金像奖|第65届颁奖典礼]]<ref>{{cite news | last = Sinclair | first = Tom | title = Monitor | url = http://www.ew.com/article/1996/11/29/monitor | accessdate = 2016-03-08 | work = [[娱乐周刊|Entertainment Weekly]] | date = 1996-11-29 | archivedate = 2015-09-19 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150919232210/http://www.ew.com/article/1996/11/29/monitor}}</ref>。3个星期前，女演员[[海伦·亨特|海伦·亨特]]于3月1日在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的威尔希尔丽晶酒店主持颁发[[奥斯卡科技成果奖|奥斯卡科技成果奖]]<ref>{{cite news | title = Past Scientific & Technical Awards Ceremonies | accessdate = 2014-09-24 | deadurl = yes | url = http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html | publisher = [[美国电影艺术与科学学院|Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] | archivedate = 2014-09-24 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140924114003/http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html}}</ref>。

《[[英国病人|英国病人]]》赢得包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]在内的9项大奖，是这个夜晚的最大赢家<ref>{{cite news | last = Bates | first = James | title = An 'English' Epic | newspaper = [[洛杉矶时报|Los Angeles Times]] | date = 1997-03-25 | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1997-03-25/news/mn-41851_1_english-patient | accessdate = 2016-03-08 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160306064228/http://articles.latimes.com/1997-03-25/news/mn-41851_1_english-patient | archivedate = 2016-03-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last = Dudek | first = Duane | title = Oscar night belongs to 'English Patient' | accessdate = 2016-03-08 | newspaper = Milwaukee Journal Sentinel | date = 1997-03-25 | url = https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1683&dat=19970325&id=RHUaAAAAIBAJ&sjid=Fy4EAAAAIBAJ&pg=5596,1224920&hl=zh-CN}}</ref>。其他获奖电影包括胜出2项的《[[冰血暴|冰血暴]]》，以及获奖1项的《马克·奥布莱恩的生活和工作》（{{lang|en|''Breathing Lessons: The Life and Work of Mark O'Brien''}}）、《[[亲爱的日记|亲爱的日记]]》（{{lang|en|''Dear Diary''}}）、《[[艾玛_(1996年电影)|艾玛]]》（{{lang|en|''Emma''}}）、《[[贝隆夫人_(电影)|贝隆夫人]]》、《[[黑夜幽灵|黑夜幽灵]]》、《[[独立日_(电影)|独立日]]》、《[[征服情海|征服情海]]》、《[[给我一个爸|给我一个爸]]》（{{lang|cs|Kolja}}）、《[[肥佬教授|肥佬教授]]》（{{lang|en|''The Nutty Professor''}}）、《[[追寻_(动画短片)|追寻]]》（{{lang|en|''Quest''}}）、《[[闪亮的风采|闪亮的风采]]》、《[[弹簧刀_(电影)|弹簧刀]]》（{{lang|en|''Sling Blade''}}）和《[[一代拳王|一代拳王]]》（{{lang|en|''When We Were Kings''}}）。

== 获奖和提名 ==
1997年2月11日，[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]主席罗伯特·莱姆（{{lang|en|Robert Rehme}}）和女演员[[米拉·索维诺|米拉·索维诺]]一起在加利福尼亚州[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布第69届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscar watch | url = http://variety.com/1997/scene/vpage/oscar-watch-88-1117435978/ | accessdate = 2016-03-08 | work = Variety | date = 1997-02-04 | archivedate = 2016-03-04 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304101903/http://variety.com/1997/scene/vpage/oscar-watch-88-1117435978/}}</ref>。《[[英国病人|英国病人]]》以12项提名领跑，《[[冰血暴|冰血暴]]》和《[[闪亮的风采|闪亮的风采]]》均以7项排在第2<ref>{{cite news | last = Dudek | first = Duane | title = Oscar Declares Independent's Day 'English Patient' benefits as academy turns the spotlight on outsiders with artistic integrity | newspaper = Milwaukee Journal Sentinel | date = 1997-02-12 | page = 8}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last1 = Bates | first1 = James | last2 = Puig | first2 = Claudia | title = Independents Day for Oscars | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160305210454/http://articles.latimes.com/1997-02-12/news/mn-27914_1_independents-day | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1997-02-12/news/mn-27914_1_independents-day | accessdate = 2016-03-08 | work = Los Angeles Times | date = 1997-02-12 | archivedate = 2016-03-05}}</ref>。

== 典礼资讯 ==
[[File:Billy_Crystal_VF_2012_Shankbone.JPG|thumb]]是第69届奥斯卡颁奖典礼的主持人]]

1996年11月，美国电影艺术与科学学院决定聘请吉尔·凯茨（{{lang|en|Gil Cates}}）第7度负责颁奖晚会的制作，他上一次获聘制作的是[[第67届奥斯卡金像奖|第67届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]<ref name="Bona-2002" />{{rp|89}}。凯茨马上就决定请男演员兼笑匠[[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]担任主持，称他敏捷的反应中闪烁着智慧的光芒，面对现场直播过程中的突发事件也能游刃有余，如同演奏[[安东尼奥·史特拉第瓦里|安东尼奥·史特拉第瓦里]]制作的[[斯特拉迪瓦里琴|小提琴]]。凯茨声称，克里斯托的主持已经成为衡量其他主持人的标杆。<ref>{{cite news | last = Marcus | first = Errico | title = Billy Crystal to Host Oscars...Again | url = http://www.eonline.com/news/33780/billy-crystal-to-host-oscars-again | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | publisher = E! | date = 1996-11-18 | archivedate = 2015-09-06 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150906041246/http://www.eonline.com/news/33780/billy-crystal-to-host-oscars-again}}</ref>克里斯托对能够第5度成为奥斯卡主持深感兴奋，他还开玩笑说：“听说[[巴里·谢克|巴里·谢克]]（{{lang|en|Barry Scheck}}）不干之后，我就觉得自己应该是有戏了<ref>{{cite news | title = Billy Crystal Back At Helm Of Oscars | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304040938/http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1996-11-19/news/9611190187_1_oscars-host-billy-crystal-lawyers | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | work = [[芝加哥论坛报|Chicago Tribune]] | date = 1996-11-19 | deadurl = no | url = http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1996-11-19/news/9611190187_1_oscars-host-billy-crystal-lawyers | archivedate = 2016-03-04}}</ref>。”此外，他还专门开设网站“{{lang|en|www.whyistheshowsolong.com}}”，邀请公众提供笑料，入选后会在颁奖典礼上采用<ref>{{cite news | last = Snead | first = Elisabeth | title = Crystal out to Net jokes for Oscar show | url = http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/special/oscars/l97os022.htm | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | work = USA Today | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304033428/http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/special/oscars/l97os022.htm | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2016-03-04}}</ref>。

盖茨此前制作的几届颁奖典礼都会围绕主题展开，本次也不例外，这年的新主题定为“电影和欢聚”。据盖茨所说，电影的一大魅力就是可以同他人一起欣赏，细思之下，人们除此以外只有在宗教或体育场合才会这样聚在一起。在他看来，电影院正是大家可以欢聚在一起，共同欢乐、一起感动的美妙所在。<ref>{{cite news | last = Bowles | first = Jennifer | title = Oscar show to celebrate communion of moviegoing | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | work = Deseret News | date = 1997-03-21 | url = http://www.deseretnews.com/article/550000/Oscar-show-to-celebrate-communion-of-moviegoing.html?pg=all | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304034826/http://www.deseretnews.com/article/550000/Oscar-show-to-celebrate-communion-of-moviegoing.html?pg=all | archivedate = 2016-03-04 | deadurl = no}}</ref>为切合主题，女演员[[薇诺娜·瑞德|薇诺娜·瑞德]]在晚会现场引出多部电影的剪辑片段，镜头上除了能看到包括《[[马提尼_(电影)|马提尼]]》（{{lang|en|''Matinee''}}）、《[[卡萨布兰卡_(电影)|卡萨布兰卡]]》和《[[欲望号街车|欲望号街车]]》在内的多部电影片段外，还能看到电影院内形形色色的观众<ref name="Bona-2002" />{{rp|111}}。

另外还有多人参与颁奖典礼的制作。晚会的官方海报由纪录片电影人阿诺德·施瓦茨曼（{{lang|en|Arnold Schwartzman}}）设计，上面有过去68届奥斯卡最佳影片获奖作品的的片名，组合成奥斯卡小金人的形状<ref>{{cite news | last = Hindes | first = Andrew | title = Acad bows poster for 69th Oscars | work = Variety | date = 1996-12-10 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160305035118/http://variety.com/1996/scene/vpage/acad-bows-poster-for-69th-oscars-1117466260/ | url = http://variety.com/1996/scene/vpage/acad-bows-poster-for-69th-oscars-1117466260/ | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | archivedate = 2016-03-05}}</ref>。电影配曲家、音乐家[[比尔·康堤|比尔·康堤]]（{{lang|en|Bill Conti}}）担任晚会音乐总监和管弦乐队指挥<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscar Watch | url = http://variety.com/1997/scene/vpage/oscar-watch-70-1117433844/ | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | work = Variety | date = 1997-01-12 | publisher = Variety Media | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304220637/http://variety.com/1997/scene/vpage/oscar-watch-70-1117433844/ | archivedate = 2016-03-04}}</ref>。歌舞曲目《{{lang|en|That Thing You Do}}》的现场演出由编舞家奥蒂斯·萨里德（{{lang|en|Otis Sallid}}）督导<ref name="Pond-2005">{{Cite book | last = Pond | first = Steve | title = The Big Show: High Times and Dirty Dealings Backstage at the Academy Awards | year = 2005 | isbn = 0-571-21193-3 | location = New York, United States | publisher = Faber and Faber}}</ref>{{rp|156}}。[[麦可·佛莱利|麦可·佛莱利]]及音乐剧《[[舞王|舞王]]》的多位演员在现场播映蒙太奇向电影剪辑师致敬时表演舞蹈<ref name="Bona-2002" />{{rp|113}}。[[杰弗里·拉什|杰弗里·拉什]]在电影《闪亮的风采》中扮演的钢琴家戴维·赫尔夫戈特（{{lang|en|David Helfgott}}）也上台演奏一曲[[尼古拉·安德烈耶维奇·里姆斯基-科萨科夫|尼古拉·安德烈耶维奇·里姆斯基-科萨科夫]]创作的《[[大黄蜂的飞行|大黄蜂的飞行]]》<ref name="Bona-2002" />{{rp|114}}。

电影《双面镜》的插曲《{{lang|en|I Finally Found Someone}}》获得原创歌曲奖提名，但作者兼原唱[[芭芭拉·史翠珊|芭芭拉·史翠珊]]不愿到晚会现场演出，学院为此请[[娜塔莉·科尔|娜塔莉·科尔]]（{{lang|en|Natalie Cole}}）献唱<ref name="Bona-2002" />{{rp|96}}<ref>{{cite news | last = Marcano | first = Tony | title = Chronicle | url = http://www.nytimes.com/1997/03/24/style/chronicle-263419.html | accessdate = 2016-03-08 | newspaper = [[纽约时报|The New York Times]] | date = 1997-03-24 | publisher = The New York Times Company | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160305224526/http://www.nytimes.com/1997/03/24/style/chronicle-263419.html | archivedate = 2016-03-05 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。然而，科尔意外染上流感，这首歌最终由[[席琳·狄翁|席琳·狄翁]]现场演唱，另一首提名歌曲《{{lang|en|Because You Loved Me}}》也是由她演出<ref name="Bona-2002" />{{rp|112}}<ref name="Pond-2005" />{{rp|157}}。

截至1997年2月11日提名公布时，获最佳影片奖提名的5部电影在美国的总票房成绩为2亿零997万2703美元，平均每部4199万4541美元<ref name="BoxOfficeOscars">{{cite news | title = 1996 Academy Award Nominations and Winner for Best Picture | work = [[Box_Office_Mojo|Box Office Mojo]] | url = http://www.boxofficemojo.com/oscar/chart/?yr=1996&p=.htm | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304120022/http://www.boxofficemojo.com/oscar/chart/?yr=1996&p=.htm | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | publisher = IMDb.com | archivedate = 2016-03-04 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。其中《甜心先生》以1亿2150万3645美元居首，其后分别是收入4231万9574美元的《英国病人》、进账2408万3318美元的《冰血暴》、收入1613万1059美元的《闪亮的风采》和进账593万5107美元的《秘密与谎言》<ref name="BoxOfficeOscars" />。

=== 专业评价 ===
本次颁奖典礼所获评价褒贬不一。《[[丹佛邮报|丹佛邮报]]》（{{lang|en|''The Denver Post''}}）认为主持人比尔·克里斯托虽然令节目前10分钟高潮迭起，但《英国病人》压倒性的胜出令晚会气氛变得沉闷，甚至连克里斯托也无法救场<ref>{{cite news | last = Ostrow | first = Joanne | title = Even Crystal Runs Out of Steam | newspaper = The Denver Post | date = 1997-03-25}}</ref>。《[[洛杉矶时报|洛杉矶时报]]》专栏作家布莱恩·洛瑞（{{lang|en|Brian Lowry}}）也称，这年颁奖典礼中各奖项的归属远比节目本身重要得多，同时事实也证明，晚会整体表现与过去几届相比显得平淡无奇；洛瑞还表示，克里斯托表现虽然还算合格，但现场讲的一些小段子总令人感觉有些勉强和过时<ref>{{cite news | last = Lowry | first = Brian | title = Fine Crystal Competes With a Lot of Dull Sheen | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1997-03-25/entertainment/ca-41957_1_billy-crystal | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | work = [[洛杉矶时报|Los Angeles Times]] | date = 1997-03-25 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160305235452/http://articles.latimes.com/1997-03-25/entertainment/ca-41957_1_billy-crystal | archivedate = 2016-03-05}}</ref>。《[[明星纪事报|明星纪事报]]》（{{lang|en|''The Star-Ledger''}}）的[[阿兰·塞平沃尔|阿兰·塞平沃尔]]（{{lang|en|Alan Sepinwall}}）也有类似看法，觉得克里斯托虽然还是精力十足，但笑料已不像过去那么高超；此外，他还批评典礼上《{{lang|en|That Thing You Do}}》的演出和向电影剪辑师致敬桥段的舞蹈曲目臃肿得令人发指<ref>{{cite news | last = Sepinwall | first = Alan | title = It was no Crystal ball as host came up flat after 3-year hiatus | newspaper = The Star-Ledger | date = 1997-03-25 | page = 6}}</ref>。

其它媒体出版物对节目有更为正面的看法。《[[费城询问报|费城询问报]]》影评人[[嘉莉·瑞基|嘉莉·瑞基]]（{{lang|en|Carrie Rickey}}）称赞克里斯托的主持表现令人眼前一亮，颁奖典礼令人感到优雅和亲切<ref>{{cite news | last = Riceky | first = Carey | title = 9 Oscars For `English Patient' It Captured Best Picture And Director. Acting Honors Went To Frances Mcdormand And Geoffrey Rush | url = http://articles.philly.com/1997-03-25/news/25571786_1_oscar-winners-69th-academy-awards-marge-gunderson | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | work = [[费城询问报|The Philadelphia Inquirer]] | date = 1997-03-25 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304002043/http://articles.philly.com/1997-03-25/news/25571786_1_oscar-winners-69th-academy-awards-marge-gunderson | archivedate = 2016-03-04}}</ref>。《[[芝加哥论坛报|芝加哥论坛报]]》专栏作家史蒂夫·约翰逊（{{lang|en|Steve Johnson}}）称赞克里斯托的主持，称即便不考虑各奖项的归属，这场晚会依然十分娱乐<ref>{{cite news | last = Johnson | first = Steve | title = The Broadcast | accessdate = 2016-03-09 | work = Chicago Tribune | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304025607/http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1997-03-25/news/9703250242_1_beavis-and-butt-head-show-producer-gil-cates-los-angeles-shrine-auditorium | archivedate = 2016-03-04 | date = 1997-03-25 | url = http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1997-03-25/news/9703250242_1_beavis-and-butt-head-show-producer-gil-cates-los-angeles-shrine-auditorium | deadurl = no }}</ref>。《[[橘郡纪事报|橘郡纪事报]]》（{{lang|en|''Orange County Register''}}）电视评论员金尼·李特佛尔德（{{lang|en|Kinney Littlefield}}）感叹，克里斯托上次担任主持人已是3年前，这3年来的几位主持人多多少少会有些外行人的局促，但只要他一回来，这种包容感和电影内线人士挥洒自如的感觉也跟着回来了。她认为晚会整体表现甚佳，动力十足、乐观向上、充满乐趣，引人注目。<ref>{{cite news | last = Littlefield | first = Kinney | title = Host Billy Crystal makes Oscar classy and sassy again | work = Orange County Register | date = 1997-03-25 | page = F4}}</ref>

=== 收视率和奖项 ===
本届颁奖典礼通过美国广播公司在美国直播，其播出时段里平均有4008万观众收看，与[[第68届奥斯卡金像奖|前一年]]相比降低了9%<ref>{{cite news | last = Johnson | first = Greg | title = Call It the Glamour Bowl | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160305204939/http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 | archivedate = 2016-03-05 | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | work = Los Angeles Times | date = 1999-03-18}}</ref>，所有曾观看部分时段节目的观众总数则估计有7383万<ref>{{cite news | last = Lowry | first = Brian | title = Bright Oscar Lights Shine in Cities, Not Elsewhere | work = Los Angeles Times | date = 1997-03-26 | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1997-03-26/entertainment/ca-42098_1_oscar-ceremony | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160306154927/http://articles.latimes.com/1997-03-26/entertainment/ca-42098_1_oscar-ceremony | archivedate = 2016-03-06 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。从尼尔森收视率调查数据来看，节目表现也不及前一年，吸引到27.49%的家庭观看，收视率为46.31%<ref name="TVB Oscars">{{cite news|title=Academy Awards ratings |publisher=Television Bureau of Advertising |format=PDF |url=http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |accessdate=2016-03-11 |archivedate=2016-03-03 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303174230/http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。此外，节目在18至49岁年龄段观众群中收获的收视率为16.55 / 34.32，这意味着全美所有18至49岁年龄段人群里有16.55%观看颁奖典礼，而当时有看电视的该年龄段观众群中则有34.32%选择收看该节目，数字同样低于上一年<ref name="TVB Oscars" />，是[[第59届奥斯卡金像奖|10年间]]电视观众最少的奥斯卡颁奖典礼和1986年的[[第58届奥斯卡金像奖|第58届颁奖晚会]]过后观众最少的一届<ref name="Pond-2005" />{{rp|159}}。

1997年7月，颁奖典礼获得[[第49届黄金时段艾美奖|第49届]][[黄金时段艾美奖|黄金时段艾美奖]]7项提名<ref>{{cite news | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160304212834/http://www.emmys.com/shows/69th-annual-academy-awards | deadurl = no | title = Primetime Emmy Award database | url = http://www.emmys.com/shows/69th-annual-academy-awards | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | publisher = Academy of Television Arts and Sciences | archivedate = 2016-03-04}}</ref>，并在两个月后赢得综艺、音乐或特别节目类杰出音效奖<ref>{{cite news | last = Lowry | first = Brian | title = NBC Takes Home 15 Emmys in Early Award Presentations | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1997/sep/09/entertainment/ca-30178/2 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160306105144/http://articles.latimes.com/1997/sep/09/entertainment/ca-30178/2 | newspaper = Los Angeles Times | date = 1997-09-09 | accessdate = 2016-03-11 | archivedate = 2016-03-06}}</ref>。

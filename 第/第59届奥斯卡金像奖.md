'''第59届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1986年电影|1986年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1987年3月30日下午18点（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上21点）开始在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]][[好莱坞|好莱坞]]的多萝茜·钱德勒大厅举行，共计颁发了23个类别的[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）。晚会通过[[美国广播公司|美国广播公司]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播，小塞缪尔·戈尔德温担任[[制片人|制片人]]，马蒂·帕赛塔导演<ref>{{cite news | title = 59th Annual Academy Awards Presentation | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = The New York Times | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140715182447/http://www.nytimes.com/tv/show/38877/59th-Annual-Academy-Awards-Presentation/details | publisher = The New York Times Company | archivedate = 2014-07-15 | url = http://www.nytimes.com/tv/show/38877/59th-Annual-Academy-Awards-Presentation/details}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | title = Shirley Verrett Is Star of Friday's Opera Gala | work = The Miami Herald | publisher = The McClatchy Company | date = 1987-01-13 | page = 5D}}</ref>。切维·切斯、[[歌蒂·韩|歌蒂·韩]]和[[保罗·霍根|保罗·霍根]]3位演员共同担任[[主持人|主持人]]。这是切斯和霍根首度主持奥斯卡颁奖典礼，也是韩第二次担任主持人，她曾主持过前一年举行的[[第58届奥斯卡金像奖|第58届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]<ref>{{cite news | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = Variety | publisher = PMC | date = 2005-02-13 | title = Hosting chores | url = http://variety.com/2005/film/awards/hosting-chores-1117917933/ | archivedate = 2014-04-19 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140419022509/http://variety.com/2005/film/awards/hosting-chores-1117917933/ | deadurl = no}}</ref>。8天前的3月22日，[[女演员|女演员]][[凯瑟琳·海克丝|凯瑟琳·海克丝]]（{{lang|en|Catherine Hicks}}）在加利福尼亚州[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[比佛利希尔顿酒店|比佛利希尔顿酒店]]主持颁发了[[奥斯卡科技成果奖|奥斯卡科技成果奖]]<ref>{{cite news | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140707000407/http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html | deadurl = yes | url = http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html | title = Past Scientific & Technical Awards Ceremonies | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences | publisher = AMPAS | archivedate = 2014-07-07}}</ref>。

《[[野战排_(电影)|野战排]]》赢得了包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]、[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|导演]]在内的四项大奖，是这个夜晚的最大赢家<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Harmetz | first1 = Aljean | title = "Platoon" Wins Oscar as the Best Movie of 1986 | url = http://www.nytimes.com/1987/03/31/movies/platoon-wins-oscar-as-the-best-movie-of-1986.html | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = The New York Times | publisher = The New York Times | date = 1987-03-31 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140714120901/http://www.nytimes.com/1987/03/31/movies/platoon-wins-oscar-as-the-best-movie-of-1986.html | archivedate = 2014-07-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last1 = Sharbutt | first1 = Jay | title = 'Platoon' Is Top Film; Newman Is Best Actor | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1987-03-31/news/mn-1504_1_oscar | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1987-03-31 | archivedate = 2014-08-11 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140811094415/http://articles.latimes.com/1987-03-31/news/mn-1504_1_oscar | deadurl = no}}</ref>。其它获奖电影包括胜出三项的《[[汉娜姐妹|汉娜姐妹]]》和《[[看得见风景的房间_(电影)|看得见风景的房间]]》，获奖两项的《[[异形2|异形2]]》，以及胜出一项的《你只拥有时间》（{{lang|en|''Artie Shaw: Time Is All You've Got''}}）、《[[战火葬童年|战火葬童年]]》（{{lang|nl|''De aanslag''}}）、《[[失宠于上帝的孩子们|失宠于上帝的孩子们]]》、《[[金钱本色|金钱本色]]》、《在美国潦倒》（{{lang|en|''Down and Out in America''}}）、《[[变蝇人|变蝇人]]》、《希腊悲剧》（{{lang|en|''A Greek Tragedy''}}）、《[[教会_(电影)|教会]]》、《[[珍贵图像|珍贵图像]]》（{{lang|en|''Precious Images''}}）、《[[午夜旋律|午夜旋律]]》（{{lang|en|''Round Midnight''}}）、《[[壮志凌云_(1986年电影)|壮志凌云]]》和《女人——为了美国，为了世界》（{{lang|en|''Women – for America, for the World''}}）。

== 获奖和提名 ==
第59届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单于1987年2月11日由学院主席[[罗伯特·怀斯|罗伯特·怀斯]]与两位演员[[唐·阿米契|唐·阿米契]]（{{lang|en|Don Ameche}}）和[[安杰丽卡·休斯顿|安杰丽卡·休斯顿]]一起在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996">{{citation | last = Wiley | first = Mason | last2 = Bona | first2 = Damien | title = Inside Oscar: The Unofficial History of the Academy Awards | year = 1996 | isbn = 0-345-40053-4 | location = New York, United States | edition = 5 | OCLC = 779680732 | publisher = Ballantine Books}}</ref>{{rp|689}}。《[[野战排_(电影)|野战排]]》和《[[看得见风景的房间_(电影)|看得见风景的房间]]》均以8项提名领跑<ref>{{cite news | last = Matthews | first = Jack | title = Newman Gets 7th Chance; Will He Finally Get Oscar? : Nominated for 'Color of Money' | archivedate = 2014-04-13 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140413132725/http://articles.latimes.com/1987-02-11/news/mn-1853_1_newman-s-nomination | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1987-02-11/news/mn-1853_1_newman-s-nomination | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1987-02-11 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

获奖名单在1987年3月30日举行的颁奖典礼上公布<ref>{{cite news|title=Nominees & Winners for the 59th Academy Awards |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/oscarlegacy/1980-1989/59nominees.html |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140415065336/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/oscarlegacy/1980-1989/59nominees.html |accessdate=2014-09-09 |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |publisher=AMPAS |deadurl=yes |archivedate=2014-04-15 }}</ref>。[[玛丽·玛特琳|玛丽·玛特琳]]与为首位获奥斯卡金像奖肯定的聋哑演员，并且截至[[第86届奥斯卡金像奖|2014年]]仍是最年轻的[[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|女主角奖]]得主<ref name="Brill-2006">{{cite book | last = Brill | first = Marlene Targ | title = Illinois | year = 2006 | isbn = 0-7614-1735-4 | location = New York, United States | publisher = Marshall Cavendish | page = 130}}</ref><ref name="Osborne-2008" />{{rp|396}}。获[[奥斯卡最佳男主角奖|男主角奖]]的[[保罗·纽曼|保罗·纽曼]]曾在[[1961年电影|1961年]]的《[[江湖浪子_(电影)|江湖浪子]]》中扮演“快枪手艾迪”·费尔森一角，成为历史上第四位在两部电影中出演同一角色并且双双获提名的男演员<ref name="Osborne-2008" />{{rp|397}}。纽曼的妻子[[乔安娜·伍德沃德|乔安娜·伍德沃德]]曾因1957年的《[[三面夏娃|三面夏娃]]》（{{lang|en|''The Three Faces of Eve''}}）获[[第30届奥斯卡金像奖|第30届]]奥斯卡最佳女主角奖，两人至此成为历史上第二对奥斯卡获奖演员夫妇<ref name="Osborne-2008" />{{rp|396}}。

== 典礼资讯 ==
{{double image|right|Chevy Chase (1990).jpg|130|Goldie_Hawn_cropped.jpg|145|切维·切斯（左）、[[歌蒂·韩|歌蒂·韩]]（右）和[[保罗·霍根|保罗·霍根]]（不在图片中）共同主持了第59届奥斯卡颁奖典礼}}
为了重振奖项声威，扭转收视率下跌的困局，[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]于1986年11月聘请小塞缪尔·戈尔德温担任奥斯卡颁奖典礼电视直播的[[制作人|制作人]]，这也是他首次担此要职<ref>{{cite news | title = Goldwyn's Son to Produce Academy Awards Show | work = Observer–Reporter | publisher = Thomas Northrop | date = 1986-11-13 | url = http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=2519&dat=19861113&id=Y0ZiAAAAIBAJ&sjid=NXcNAAAAIBAJ&pg=3592,1505802 | accessdate = 2014-09-09}}</ref>。次年3月，戈尔德温宣布喜剧演员切维·切斯、奥斯卡获奖女演员[[歌蒂·韩|歌蒂·韩]]以及曾获奥斯卡奖提名的[[保罗·霍根|保罗·霍根]]将共同承担1987年颁奖晚会的主持职责<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Beck | first1 = Marilyn | title = Paul Hogan at the Oscars: The Monologue, Please | work = Los Angeles Daily News | publisher = MediaNews Group | date = 1987-03-20 | page = 4}}</ref>。男演员[[罗宾·威廉斯|罗宾·威廉斯]]本来也会成为主持人之一，但由于他已经承诺出演《[[早安越南|早安越南]]》而不得不退出<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Hartl | first1 = John | title = The Academy Award Remain Live and Unpredictable | work = The Seattle Times | publisher = The Seattle Times Company | date = 1987-03-29 | page = TV2}}</ref>。

戈尔德温工作中的一个重大举措就是将晚会的时长缩短到不超过3小时。对此他告诉记者，获奖者的得奖感言时间会定在45秒，摄像机旁边的“灯光会在45秒时开始闪烁，到55秒时就会变成红色。过了一分钟的时候我们就要么会切换到广告，要么会进入别的程序。我们还要求一起上台领奖的多人挑选出一位发言人”<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Ryan | first1 = Desmong | title = Prescription For The Oscarcast Once Again, The Goal Is To Shorten And Sweeten The Annual Program | url = http://articles.philly.com/1987-03-29/living/26221486_1_oscar-show-production-numbers-acceptance-speeches | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = The Philadelphia Inquirer | publisher = Philadelphia Media Network | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140728105026/http://articles.philly.com/1987-03-29/living/26221486_1_oscar-show-production-numbers-acceptance-speeches | archivedate = 2014-09-09 | deadurl = no | date = 1987-03-29}}</ref>。此外，全部5首获原创歌曲奖提名的作品也不会再像以前那样分别演出，而是会全部合并到一起作为表演曲目，女演员伯纳黛特·彼得斯（{{lang|en|Bernadette Peters}}）演唱每首歌的短暂片段<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Kelley | first1 = Bill | title = Academy Broadcast Committed Many Sins | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = Sun-Sentinel | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1987-03-31 | archivedate = 2014-07-28 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140728193728/http://articles.sun-sentinel.com/1987-03-31/news/8701200936_1_presenter-oscar-ceremony-three-awards | url = http://articles.sun-sentinel.com/1987-03-31/news/8701200936_1_presenter-oscar-ceremony-three-awards}}</ref>。戈尔德温还打算把纪录片和短片类奖项改到另一次颁奖仪式上进行，但美国电影艺术与科学学院没有同意<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|691}}。

另外还有多人参与了颁奖典礼的制作。曾获奥斯卡金像奖肯定的服装设计师西奥尼·阿尔德里奇（{{lang|en|Theoni V. Aldredge}}）获聘成为晚会的时尚顾问，监督了5部获服装设计奖提名电影的“时装秀”部分<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|716}}<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Matthews | first1 = Jack | title = Goldwyn Touch For Oscars | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140725192700/http://articles.latimes.com/1986-12-08/entertainment/ca-1799_1_oscar-show | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1986-12-08/entertainment/ca-1799_1_oscar-show | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1986-12-08 | archivedate = 2014-07-25}}</ref>。莱昂内尔·纽曼担任典礼的音乐总监和指挥<ref name="Osborne-2008" />{{rp|387}}。男演员多姆·德路易斯（{{lang|en|Dom DeLuise}}）、[[森田则之|森田则之]]和[[特利·萨瓦拉斯|特利·萨瓦拉斯]]在晚会开场时表演了音乐剧《红男绿女》（{{lang|en|''Guys and Dolls''}}）插曲“{{lang|en|Fugue for Tinhorns}}”<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|694}}。

=== 提名电影票房表现 ===
截对1987年2月11日提名公布，5部获最佳影片奖提名的电影在美国的总[[票房|票房]]收入为1亿1940万8531美元，平均每部2388万1706美元<ref name="BoxOfficeOscars">{{cite news | title = 1989 Academy Award Nominations and Winner for Best Picture | url = http://boxofficemojo.com/oscar/chart/?yr=1986&p=.htm | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140826172518/http://boxofficemojo.com/oscar/chart/?yr=1986&p=.htm | work = Box Office Mojo | publisher = IMDb.com | archivedate = 2014-08-26}}</ref>。其中《野战排》以3929万7361美元排在首位，其后分别是进账3539万2203美元的《汉娜姐妹》，收入2213万1104美元的《失宠于上帝的孩子们》，1148万9130美元的《看得见风暴的房间》和1109万8733美元的《教会》<ref name="BoxOfficeOscars" />。

在1986年美国电影票房50强中，有18部电影获得了共计55项提名。其中《鳄鱼邓迪》（[[亚军|亚军]]）、《异形2》（第6位）、《金钱本色》（第11位）、《与我同行》（第12位）、《佩姬苏要出嫁》（第18位）、《野战排》（第23位）、《汉娜姐妹》（第29位）、《清晨之后》（第38位）、《金钱本色》（第40位）和《芳心之罪》（第43位）获得了最佳影片、导演、演员或编剧类奖项提名<ref name="boxofficemojooscars">{{cite news | title = 2007 Domestic Grosses (as of January 23, 2007) | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = [[Box_Office_Mojo|Box Office Mojo]] | publisher = IMDb.com | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140725105845/http://boxofficemojo.com/yearly/chart/?asof=1987-02-10&view=releasedate&view2=domestic&yr=1986&sort=gross&order=DESC&p=.htm | archivedate = 2014-07-25 | deadurl = no | url = http://boxofficemojo.com/yearly/chart/?asof=1987-02-10&view=releasedate&view2=domestic&yr=1986&sort=gross&order=DESC&p=.htm}}</ref>。另外8部获提名的电影分别是《壮志凌云》（[[冠军|冠军]]）、《小子难缠续集》（[[季军|季军]]）、《星际旅行IV：抢救未来》（第4位）、《美国鼠谭》（第5位）、《战火云霄》（第17位）、《鬼驱人2》（第19位）、《变蝇人》（第22位）和《异形奇花》（第30位）<ref name="boxofficemojooscars" />。

=== 专业评价 ===
本届晚会所获评价褒贬不一，部分媒体看法较为负面。《[[和风日报|和风日报]]》（{{lang|en|''Daily Breeze''}}）专栏作家杰里·罗伯茨（{{lang|en|Jerry Roberts}}）批评节目乱成一团，仿佛某种狂欢节上的怪胎展览，极尽奢华之能事，仿佛一场高调自负、哗众取宠的风暴。虽然切斯和韩都已尽力把晚会的气氛炒热，但缓慢而繁琐的节目进程让他们徒劳无功<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Roberts | first1 = Jerry | title = Tedium Overcomes Oscar's Best Efforts | work = Daily Breeze | publisher = MediaNews Group|date= 1987-03-31 | page = A16}}</ref>。《[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]]》电视评论员[[汤姆·谢尔斯|汤姆·谢尔斯]]（{{lang|en|Tom Shales}}）在文章中称，今年的奥斯卡颁奖典礼像往常一样未能把握好节奏，到处都是嘈杂的活动风暴，来宾的领口一个比一个开得低，内容空洞，并且也像近几年那样缺乏惊喜。他还调侃道，节目中加入的服装设计奖提名作品展示拖累了晚会的节奏<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Shales | first1 = Tom | title = Platoon' Seizes Oscar; Newman, Matlin Win; `Hannah` and `Room With A View' Take 3 Awards Each |work= The Washington Post | publisher = The Washington Post Company | date = 1987-03-31 | page = C1}}</ref>。《[[费城询问报|费城询问报]]》影评人[[卡丽·瑞基|卡丽·瑞基]]（{{lang|en|Carrie Rickey}}）批评晚会节奏过于缓慢，“仿佛观看蛞蝓和蜗牛赛跑。”她之后还称颁奖典礼最致命的的问题是患有“奥斯卡硬化症”（{{lang|en|Oscarsclerosis}}），导致节目中再次充斥着太多[[拉斯维加斯|拉斯维加斯]]风格的表演曲目<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Rickey | first1 = Carrie | title = A Dull Start, And It Barely Rebounded Forget The Telecast: The Oscars Offer Hope For Hollywood Quality | url = http://articles.philly.com/1987-04-01/news/26193495_1_59th-academy-awards-oscars-three-movies | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = The Philadelphia Inquirer | publisher = Philadelphia Media Network | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140728133010/http://articles.philly.com/1987-04-01/news/26193495_1_59th-academy-awards-oscars-three-movies | date = 1987-04-01 | archivedate = 2014-07-28 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

其他媒体对节目的评价更为正面。《[[西雅图时报|西雅图时报]]》影评人约翰·哈特尔（{{lang|en|John Hartl}}）觉得颁奖典礼的“节奏恰到好处，充满了漫画和动漫电影片段。”他还称赞制片人戈尔德温请来了包括主持人切斯这样的喜剧演员，以及罗德尼·邓吉菲尔德（{{lang|en|Rodney Dangerfield}}）这样的颁奖嘉宾，“保持节目轻松而风趣”<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Hartl | first1 = John | title = No Surprises, But a Good Oscar Show | work = The Seattle Times | publisher = The Seattle Times Company | date = 1987-03-31 | page = F1}}</ref>。《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》专栏作家珍妮特·马斯林（{{lang|en|Janet Maslin}}）写道：“这是多年来最有条不紊、最精彩多年并且节奏把握最恰当的节目。”她还表示，如果没有主持人霍根和切斯的诙谐与戏谑，“节目看上去会非常黯淡无光”<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Maslin | first1 = Janet | title = TV Review: Academy Awards Ceremony on ABC | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = The New York Times | publisher = The New York Times Company | date = 1987-04-01 | archivedate = 2013-10-14 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131014081754/http://www.nytimes.com/1987/04/01/movies/tv-review-academy-award-ceremony-on-abc.html | url = http://www.nytimes.com/1987/04/01/movies/tv-review-academy-award-ceremony-on-abc.html}}</ref>。《橘郡纪事报》电视评论员迈克尔·伯克特（{{lang|en|Michael Burkett}}）评价：“周一晚上的第59届奥斯卡颁奖典礼已经非常接近你可以希望拥有的一切：相当强的娱乐性、相对较快的进程，那些无趣的怀旧段落也异常之短，还有足够的优秀电影片段满足一轮视觉上的追求”<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Beckett | first1 = Michael | title = Little films were big winners and we went home happy | work = Orange County Register | publisher = Freedom Communications | date = 1987-03-31 | page = C1}}</ref>。

=== 收视率 ===
本届颁奖典礼通过美国广播公司在美国直播，其播出时段里平均有3719万观众收看，与[[第58届奥斯卡金像奖|前一年]]相比降低了2%<ref>{{cite web | first = Bill | last = Gorman | title = Academy Awards Averages 41.3 Million Viewers; Most Since 2005 | archivedate = 2013-11-01 | deadurl = no | url = http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2010/03/08/academy-awards-averages-41-3-million-viewers-most-since-2005/44217/ | work = TV by the Numbers | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 2010-03-08 | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131101231748/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2010/03/08/academy-awards-averages-41-3-million-viewers-most-since-2005/44217/}}</ref>。不过在尼尔森收视率调查的数据看，节目的表现却要好过前一年，吸引了27.5%的家庭观众，收视率超过43%<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Sharbutt | first1 = Jay | title = Oscar Dunks NCAA | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1987-04-01/entertainment/ca-827_1_ncaa-basketball | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140811085138/http://articles.latimes.com/1987-04-01/entertainment/ca-827_1_ncaa-basketball | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1987-04-01 | archivedate = 2014-08-11 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。媒体报道中指出，与同晚在[[CBS_(电视网)|CBS电视网]]播出的[[1987年全美大学体育协会男子第一组篮球联赛|1987年全美大学体育协会男子第一组篮球联赛]]决赛相比，晚会的收视率更高<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Schwed | first1 = Mark | title = Oscars Win Ratings War | url = http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=799&dat=19870401&id=FrFPAAAAIBAJ&sjid=U1IDAAAAIBAJ&pg=4599,110020 | accessdate = 2014-09-09 | work = The Bryan Times | publisher = Christopher Cullis | date = 1987-04-01}}</ref>。

'''第60屆[[柏林影展|柏林影展]]'''（{{lang-de|Internationale Filmfestspiele Berlin 2010}}），於2010年2月11日至2月21日於[[德國|德國]][[柏林|柏林]]舉辦，開幕片為中國導演[[王全安|王全安]]執導的《[[團圓_(電影)|團圓]]》，閉幕片則為日本導演[[山田洋次|山田洋次]]執導的《{{link-ja|春之櫻-吟子和她的弟弟|おとうと (2010年の映画)}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/berlin_comes_together_again_for_chinese_opener |title=With Chinese Opener, Berlin Comes "Together" for 60th Fest |accessdate=10 June 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/photo/berlin |title=Berlin Fest Reveals Full Competition Slate for 60th Anniversary |accessdate=10 June 2014}}</ref>，評審團主席則由德國導演[[韋納·荷索|韋納·荷索]]擔任。<ref name="Berlinale President">{{cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/presse/pressemitteilungen/alle/Alle-Detail_5364.html |title=Werner Herzog to be President of the Jury of the 60th Berlinale |accessdate=22 December 2009 |work=berlinale.de |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091125161234/http://www.berlinale.de/en/presse/pressemitteilungen/alle/Alle-Detail_5364.html |archivedate=25 November 2009 |deadurl=yes |df=dmy }}</ref><ref name="thelocal.de">{{cite web |url=http://www.thelocal.de/society/20091119-23385.html |title=Werner Herzog to head Berlin film festival jury |accessdate=2009-12-22 |work=thelocal.de| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20091120175701/http://www.thelocal.de/society/20091119-23385.html| archivedate= 20 November 2009 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref><ref name="bbc news">{{cite news |url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/entertainment/8370440.stm |title=Werner Herzog is to head the Berlin Film Festival jury  |accessdate=2009-12-22 |work=bbc news | date=2009-11-20| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20091123114011/http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/entertainment/8370440.stm| archivedate= 23 November 2009 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>

這是一項《'''第87屆奧斯卡金像獎最佳外語片角逐名單'''》。在[[美國電影藝術與科學學會|美國電影藝術與科學學會]]邀請各國或地區的電影業提交他們[[奧斯卡金像獎|奧斯卡金像獎]]的最佳外語片獎，每年自該獎項於1956年創建的。<ref name="HistPg2">{{cite web | title = History of the Academy Awards - Page 2 | publisher = [[Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts_and_Sciences|Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] | url = http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/history02.html | accessdate = 19 September 2014 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080622191328/http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/history02.html | archivedate = 2008年6月22日 | deadurl = yes }}</ref> 該獎項是頒發每年由學院到長篇以外的美國生產的電影，包含主要非英語對話。<ref name="HistPg2"/><ref name="Rules">{{cite web | title = Rule Thirteen: Special Rules for the Foreign Language Film Award | work = Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences | publisher = [[Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts_and_Sciences|Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] | url = http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/87/pdf/87aa_rules.pdf | accessdate = 19 September 2014 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140827034453/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/87/pdf/87aa_rules.pdf | archivedate = 2014年8月27日 | deadurl = yes }}</ref> 奧斯卡的外語片獎委員會監督的過程和審查所有提交的電影。<ref name="Rules"/>九入圍的競爭者將在奧斯卡提名公佈前一個星期提交出來。<ref name="Rules"/>

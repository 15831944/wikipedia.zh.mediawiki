'''第61届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1988年电影|1988年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1989年3月29日下午18点（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上21点）在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的神殿礼堂举行<ref name="TMS">{{cite book | last = Osborne |first=Robert |title=80 Years of the Oscar: The Complete History of the Academy Awards |year=2008 |isbn=0-7892-0992-6 |location=New York, United States | publisher = Abbeville Publishing Group |page=296}}</ref>，共计颁发了23座[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）。本次颁奖典礼没有设置[[主持人|主持人]]，通过[[美国广播公司|美国广播公司]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播<ref name="TMS" />。10天前的3月19日，[[女演员|女演员]][[安吉·迪金森|安吉·迪金森]]（{{lang|en|Angie Dickinson}}）在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亞州)|比佛利山]]的比华利山酒店主持颁发了[[奥斯卡科技成果奖|奥斯卡科技成果奖]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html |title=Past Scientific & Technical Awards Ceremonies |accessdate=2013-11-23 |publisher=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131013220908/http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html |archivedate=2013-10-13 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。

《雨人》赢得了包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]、[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|导演]]、[[奥斯卡最佳男主角奖|男主角]]在内的四项大奖<ref>{{cite news | last = Rickey |first=Carrie |title=The Oscars Dustin Hoffman And His Film, 'Rain Man,' Win The Top Awards Ads by Google | url = http://articles.philly.com/1989-03-30/news/26129442_1_three-statuettes-top-awards-oscar |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=The Philadelphia Inquirer | publisher = Philadelphia Media Network | date =1989-03-30 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131109003316/http://articles.philly.com/1989-03-30/news/26129442_1_three-statuettes-top-awards-oscar | archivedate = 2013-11-09 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。其他获奖电影包括同样获奖四项的《谁陷害了兔子罗杰》，获奖三项的《[[孽戀焚情|孽戀焚情]]》，以及获奖一项的《[[被告_(电影)|被告]]》、《[[意外的旅客|意外的旅客]]》、《[[一条叫旺达的鱼|一条叫旺达的鱼]]》、《丹尼斯·詹宁斯的任命》（{{lang|en|''The Appointments of Dennis Jennings''}}）、《[[阴间大法师|阴间大法师]]》、《{{link-en|火鸟重生|Bird (film)}}》、《终点酒店：克劳斯·芭比的生活和时代》（{{lang|en|''Hôtel Terminus: The Life and Times of Klaus Barbie''}}）、《{{link-en|豆田战役|The Milagro Beanfield War}}》、《[[密西西比在燃烧|密西西比在燃烧]]》、《[[征服者佩尔|征服者佩尔]]》、《[[小锡兵|小锡兵]]》、《[[上班女郎|上班女郎]]》和《你不必去死》（{{lang|en|''You Don't Have to Die''}}）。颁奖典礼的电视直播在美国平均吸引了近4300万观众收看。

== 获奖和提名 ==
第61届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单於1989年2月15日由学院主席理查德·卡恩和女演员{{link-en|安妮·阿彻|Anne Archer}}一起在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亞州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布<ref>{{cite news | last = Williams |first=Jeannie |title=Michael's high-profile feast |work=USA Today | publisher = Gannett Company |page=2D | date =1989-02-16}}</ref>，《雨人》以8项提名领跑，《孽戀焚情》、《密西西比在燃烧》和《谁陷害了兔子罗杰》均以7项紧随其后<ref>{{cite news | last = Cieply |first=Michael |title='Rain Man' Given 8 Oscar Nominations; Sigourney 2 : Hoffman Wins 6th Acting Nod | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1989-02-15/news/mn-2644_1_rain-man |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date =1989-02-16 | archivedate = 2013-11-09 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131109004140/http://articles.latimes.com/1989-02-15/news/mn-2644_1_rain-man | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

获奖名单在1989年3月29日举行的颁奖典礼上公布<ref name="ChicagoTribuneOscars">{{cite news | last = Kehr | first = Dave | title = `Rain Man` The Big Winner, But Upsets Put Zip In Oscars | archivedate = 2013-11-09 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131109010944/http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1989-03-30/news/8903300960_1_61st-academy-awards-rain-man-screenplay-award | url = http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1989-03-30/news/8903300960_1_61st-academy-awards-rain-man-screenplay-award |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=Chicago Tribune | publisher = Tribune Company | date =1989-03-30}}</ref>。获男主角奖的[[达斯汀·霍夫曼|达斯汀·霍夫曼]]成为历史上第五位两次获得该奖项的男演员<ref>{{cite news | last = Rosen |first=Christopher |title=Daniel Day-Lewis Oscar: Best Actor At Academy Awards Announced | archivedate = 2013-10-21 | deadurl = no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131021110933/http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/02/24/daniel-day-lewis-oscar_n_2707644.html | url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/02/24/daniel-day-lewis-oscar_n_2707644.html |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=The Huffington Post | publisher = AOL | date =2013-02-24}}</ref>。[[西格妮·韦弗|西格妮·韦弗]]也成为历史上[[一年获两项奥斯卡金像奖提名演员列表|第五位]]在同一届奥斯卡金像奖角逐中获得两项表演类奖项提名的演员<ref name="Levy">{{cite book | last = Levy |first=Emanuel |title=All About Oscar: The History and Politics of the Academy Awards |year=2003 |isbn=0-8264-1452-4 |location=New York, United States | publisher = Continuum International Publishing Group}}</ref>{{rp|83}}，不过她也成了这其中首位没有获得任何一个奖项的演员<ref>{{cite news | last = White |first=Penny |title=Avatar's Sigourney Weaver, Still Sexy at Sixty | archivedate = 2013-10-03 | deadurl = no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131007022334/http://voices.yahoo.com/avatars-sigourney-weaver-still-sexy-sixty-5395507.html?cat=40 | url = http://voices.yahoo.com/avatars-sigourney-weaver-still-sexy-sixty-5395507.html |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=''Yahoo! === 开场歌舞 ===
为了让晚会展示出更多的内容，让节目变得更有魅力，制片人卡尔聘请[[剧作家|剧作家]]史蒂夫·西尔弗（{{lang|en|Steve Silver}}）和他长期的合作歌剧团“{{link-en|巴比伦沙滩摊子|Beach Blanket Babylon}}”一起制作一段开场歌舞<ref name="SnowLowe">{{cite web|title=And the loser is... |archivedate=2013-12-04 |deadurl=yes |accessdate=2013-12-04 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131204080026/http://www.theenvelope.latimes.com/awards/oscars/env-worstoscars27feb27%2C0%2C2820773%2Cfull.story |url=http://theenvelope.latimes.com/awards/oscars/env-worstoscars27feb27,0,2820773,full.story |first=Steve |last=Pond |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=2005-02-27 }}</ref>。这段节目包括一个精心设计的舞台表演，女演员艾琳·鲍曼打扮成[[华特迪士尼公司|迪士尼公司]]动画长片《[[白雪公主与七个小矮人|白雪公主与七个小矮人]]》中的白雪公主，来到[[好莱坞|好莱坞]]后被其魅力迷得神魂颠倒<ref name="SnowLowe" />。与“巴比伦沙滩摊子”的表演类似，这场开场歌舞的舞蹈演员同样戴有精心制作的巨大帽子<ref name="SnowLowe" />。在另一个造型酷似{{link-en|洛杉矶大使酒店|Ambassador Hotel (Los Angeles)|椰树林}}夜总会的段落中，老牌演员[[多丽丝·黛|多丽丝·黛]]和[[赛德·查里斯|赛德·查里斯]]处在突出位置，歌手兼电视制片人{{link-en|梅夫·格里芬|Merv Griffin}}演唱自己1949年的畅销歌曲“{{lang|en|I've Got a Lovely Bunch of Coconuts}}”。鲍曼和男演员[[罗伯·劳|罗伯·劳]]演唱{{link-en|清水复兴合唱团|Creedence Clearwater Revival}}歌曲“{{lang|en|Proud Mary}}”的改编版本，其中的歌词改写成以电影业为参考。但这段开场歌舞所获得的评价很差<ref name="SnowLowe" />。

=== 专业评价和公众反应 ===
大部分媒体对本届晚会的评价不佳。《[[洛杉矶时报|洛杉矶时报]]》电视评论员霍华德·罗森伯格（{{lang|en|Howard Rosenberg}}）感叹：“美国广播公司直播的奥斯卡颁奖典礼气闷得出奇。与上个月的[[格莱美奖|格莱美颁奖典礼]]相比实在是死气沉沉。”<ref>{{cite news | last = Rosenberg |first=Howard |title=Overcast for Allan Carr's Oscarcast | archivedate = 2013-10-23 | deadurl = no | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1989-03-30/entertainment/ca-1030_1_allan-carr |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date =1989-03-30 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131023044428/http://articles.latimes.com/1989-03-30/entertainment/ca-1030_1_allan-carr}}</ref>。《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》影评人珍妮特·马斯林（{{lang|en|Janet Maslin}}）严厉批评了开场歌舞，称应该把这一段永久性地载入奥斯卡的丢人史册。她还惋惜《我想拿奥斯卡奖》这首歌过于混乱，没有激发出观众对好莱坞未来的任何信心<ref>{{cite news | last = Maslin |first=Janet |title=Review/Television; The Oscars as Home Entertainment | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2013-10-13 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131013121417/http://www.nytimes.com/1989/03/31/movies/review-television-the-oscars-as-home-entertainment.html | url = http://www.nytimes.com/1989/03/31/movies/review-television-the-oscars-as-home-entertainment.html |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=The New York Times | publisher = The New York Times Company | date =1989-03-31}}</ref>。《[[综艺|综艺]]》杂志电视栏目编辑托尼·斯科特批评第61届奥斯卡颁奖典礼在91个国家——并且还首次包括[[苏联|苏联]]在内——看来都是不值得观看的电视节目。他还觉得群星演绎的开场曲段中，这些明星围绕着一个巨大的奥斯卡金像嬉戏，仿佛这是一个[[金牛犊|金牛犊]]般<ref name="reviews">{{cite book | last = Hofler |first=Robert |title=Party Animals : A Hollywood Tale of Sex, Drugs, and Rock 'n' Roll Starring the Fabulous Allan Carr |year=2010 |isbn=0-306-81655-5 |location=Cambridge, Massachusetts, United States |oclc=779680732 | publisher = Da Capo Press |page=416}}</ref>。

女演员鲍曼声称，自己在次日被迫签署了一份{{link-en|禁言令|gag order}}，禁止她在今后13年的时间里向媒体透露有关自己当晚表演的内容。她最终于2013年接受采访时表明了自己的看法，形容这场表演看起来就像是“同性恋的成人仪式”<ref name="i was snow white">{{cite web | last = Abramovitch |first=Seth |title='I Was Rob Lowe's Snow White': The Untold Story of Oscar's Nightmare Opening | archivedate = 2013-11-05 |accessdate=2013-12-18 | deadurl = no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131105082813/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscars-rob-lowes-snow-white-422225 | url = http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscars-rob-lowes-snow-white-422225 | publisher = The Hollywood Reporter}}</ref>。

[[华特迪士尼公司|华特迪士尼公司]]向法庭起诉了美国电影艺术与科学学院，指控对方未经许可使用了白雪公主的肖像<ref>{{cite news |title=Disney Sues Over Use of Snow White at Oscars | archivedate = 2013-10-04 | deadurl = no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131004232619/http://articles.latimes.com/1989-03-31/local/me-749_1_snow-white-character | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1989-03-31/local/me-749_1_snow-white-character |work=Los Angeles Times| publisher=Tribune Company |first=Nina | last = Easton | date =1989-03-31 |accessdate=2013-12-18}}</ref>，要求对“侵犯著作权、不正当竞争，以及商业信誉的损失”给予赔偿<ref name="SnowJob">{{cite news |last=Hofler |first=Robert |title=Snow Job |url=http://www.lamag.com/features/2010/03/01/snow-job |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=Los Angeles |publisher=Emmis Communications |date=2010-03-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131005190927/http://www.lamag.com/features/2010/03/01/snow-job |archive-date=2013-10-05 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。学院主席理查德·卡恩立即向该公司道了歉，这起诉讼随后予以了撤消<ref>{{cite news | last = Vaughan |first=Vicki |title=Disney Accepts Apology, Drops Academy Suit | archivedate = 2013-10-12 | deadurl = no | url = http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1989-04-07/business/8904070295_1_walt-disney-academy-disney-snow |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=Orlando Sentinel | publisher = Tribune Company | date =1989-04-07 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131012154129/http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1989-04-07/business/8904070295_1_walt-disney-academy-disney-snow}}</ref>。

=== 收视率和余波 ===
虽然节目制作所获评价不佳，但其通过美国广播公司在美国[[首演|首播]]的时段还是平均吸引了4268万观众收看，与[[第60届奥斯卡金像奖|去年的颁奖典礼]]相比提高了1%<ref name="Oscar ratings">{{cite news|last=Gorman |first=Bill |title=With No Blockbusters Up For Best Picture, Expect 'Academy Awards' Viewership To Fall; Ratings History + Your Guess For This Year (Poll) |archivedate=2013-12-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131210015818/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/02/24/with-no-blockbusters-up-for-best-picture-expect-academy-awards-viewership-to-fall-ratings-history-your-guess-for-this-year-poll/120239/ |url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2012/02/24/with-no-blockbusters-up-for-best-picture-expect-academy-awards-viewership-to-fall-ratings-history-your-guess-for-this-year-poll/120239/ |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=''TV by the Numbers'' |publisher=Tribune Company |date=2012-02-24 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last = Johnson |first=Greg |title=Call It the Glamour Bowl | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 |accessdate=2013-12-18 |work=Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date =1999-03-18 | archivedate = 2013-10-22 | deadurl = no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131022224101/http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2}}</ref>。同时与前一届晚会相比，本届在尼尔森收视率上的成绩也更好，吸引了29.8%的[[家庭|家庭]]观看，收视率达50%<ref name="Oscar ratings" />，是继[[第56届奥斯卡金像奖|第56届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]以来收视成绩最好的一届<ref name="SnowJob" />。

美国电影艺术与科学学院组建了一个颁奖典礼检讨委员会来评估和确定为什么这次晚会直播无论是在媒体还是娱乐界的评价都这么差<ref name="SnowJob" />。该委员会之后认定，卡尔最大的失误在于让一个效果存疑的开场曲目持续了12[[分钟|分钟]]之久。委员会主席，制片人兼{{link-en|美国导演工会|Directors Guild of America}}主席{{link-en|吉尔伯特·凯茨|Gilbert Cates}}称，如果这段曲目时间大幅缩短，那么卡尔也不会受到这么严苛的批评<ref name="SnowJob" />。凯茨随后获聘成为了[[第62届奥斯卡金像奖|第62届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]的制片人<ref name="Cates">{{cite book | last = Wiley | first = Mason | last2 = Bona | first2 = Damien | title = Inside Oscar: The Unofficial History of the Academy Awards |year=1996 |isbn=0-345-40053-4 |location=New York, United States |edition=5 |oclc=779680732 | publisher = Ballantine Books |page=768}}</ref>。

据演艺圈多位业内人士和[[记者|记者]]表示，这场晚会带来的批评和反弹导致卡尔再也没有制作过电影或文艺晚会。他于1999年6月29日因[[肝癌|肝癌]]并发症去世，终年62岁<ref name="SnowLowe" /><ref name="SnowJob" />。

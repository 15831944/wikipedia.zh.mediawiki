'''第69屆[[柏林影展|柏林影展]]'''（{{lang-de|'''''69. Internationalen Filmfestspiele Berlin'''''}}）于2019年2月7日至2月17日於[[德國|德國]][[柏林|柏林]]舉辦，這是{{link-en|迪特·科斯利克|Dieter Kosslick}}擔任影展主席的最後一屆，下屆開始將由義大利籍的{{link-en|卡洛·卡特里安|Carlo Chatrian}}接任<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2018/06/berlin-film-festival-director-carlo-chatrian-dieter-kosslick-1201976452/|title=Berlin Film Festival Hires Carlo Chatrian as New Festival Director to Replace Dieter Kosslick|work=[[Variety|Variety]]|date=2018-6-19|language=en}}</ref>。丹麥導演[[瓏·薛爾菲格|瓏·薛爾菲格]]執導的英語片《{{link-en|陌生人的善意|The Kindness of Strangers (film)}}》被選為開幕片<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.screendaily.com/news/lone-scherfigs-the-kindness-of-strangers-to-open-2019-berlin-film-festival/5135078.article|title=Lone Scherfig's 'The Kindness Of Strangers' to open 2019 Berlin Film Festival|work=Screen Daily|date=2018-12-6|language=en}}</ref>。法國演員[[茱麗葉·畢諾許|茱麗葉·畢諾許]]則擔任評審團主席<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2018/12/juliette-binoche-jury-president-2019-berlin-film-festival-1202027082/|title=Juliette Binoche to Serve as Jury President of 2019 Berlin Film Festival|last=Sharf|first=Zack|work=[[IndieWire|IndieWire]]|date=December 11, 2018|accessdate=December 11, 2018}}</ref>。

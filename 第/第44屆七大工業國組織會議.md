'''第44屆七大工業國組織會議'''（{{lang-en|44th G7 Summit}}）于2018年6月8日到6月9日在[[加拿大|加拿大]][[魁北克省|魁北克省]]拉马巴克举行<ref name="gcnews">{{cite web|url=http://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2017/05/27/canada-host-2018-g7-summit-charlevoix-quebec|title=Canada to host 2018 G7 Summit in Charlevoix, Quebec|date=2017-05-27|accessdate=2017-05-27|website=Prime Minister of Canada|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170527232147/http://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2017/05/27/canada-host-2018-g7-summit-charlevoix-quebec|archivedate=2017-05-27|df=}}</ref>，是加拿大自1981年来第六次举办会议<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com/news/canada/2017/05/25/canada-to-host-2018-meeting-of-g7-leaders-in-charlevoix-quebec.html|title=Canada to host 2018 meeting of G7 leaders in Charlevoix, Quebec|date=2017-05-25|accessdate=2017-05-29|publisher=Toronto Star|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170525220228/https://www.thestar.com/news/canada/2017/05/25/canada-to-host-2018-meeting-of-g7-leaders-in-charlevoix-quebec.html|archivedate=2017-05-25|df=}}</ref>。

2014年3月，[[七国集团|七国集团]]宣布目前和俄罗斯在[[八国集团|八国集团]]的框架内举行有意义的会谈是不可能的<ref name="nyetrussia">{{cite web|url=http://www.independent.ie/world-news/leaders-plan-brussels-g7-in-june-instead-of-g8-in-sochi-30120833.html|title=Leaders plan Brussels G7 in June instead of G8 in Sochi|publisher=Irish Independent|date=2014-03-24|accessdate=2017-05-29}}.</ref>，便从那时起以七国集团程序继续会议。而在本届会议首日，美国公布了让俄罗斯回归的想法，不久后得到意大利的支持<ref name=":3">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/08/us/politics/trump-russia-g7-readmitted-tariffs.html|title=Trump Calls for Russia to Be Readmitted to G-7|date=2018-06-08|work=The New York Times|access-date=2018-06-08|issn=0362-4331|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180608131154/https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/08/us/politics/trump-russia-g7-readmitted-tariffs.html|archivedate=2018-06-08|df=}}</ref><ref name="Politico">{{Cite web|url=https://www.politico.com/amp/story/2018/06/08/new-italian-leader-backs-trump-on-russia-rejoining-g7-633712|title=New Italian leader backs Trump on Russia rejoining G-7|last=Herszenhorn|first=David|date=2018-06-08|website=Politico|accessdate=2018-06-08|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180611100605/https://www.politico.com/amp/story/2018/06/08/new-italian-leader-backs-trump-on-russia-rejoining-g7-633712|archivedate=2018-06-11|df=}}</ref>。

会议备受瞩目的焦点是各会员国和美国关系的恶化<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.fxstreet.com/amp/analysis/risk-aversion-seen-ahead-of-hostile-g61-summit-201806081104|title=Risk Aversion Seen Ahead of Hostile G6+1 Summit|last=Erlam|first=Craig|date=2018-06-08|website=FXStreet|access-date=2018-06-08|quote=The G7 meeting has become more like a G6+1, with Trump choosing to isolate the US on a number of issues from trade to Iran and climate change.|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180611100559/https://www.fxstreet.com/amp/analysis/risk-aversion-seen-ahead-of-hostile-g61-summit-201806081104|archivedate=2018-06-11|df=}}</ref>。因此，法国及部分成员国的媒体称本届峰会是“6+1集团峰会”，意味着美国因最近发生的事件遭到孤立<ref name="NBC">{{Cite web|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/news/amp/ncna880961|title=Welcome to the G6+1: Trump reps an isolated America at the G-7 summit|last=Allen|first=Jonathan|date=2018-06-08|website=NBC News|access-date=2018-06-08|quote=The G-7 this year looks more like a G6+1. == 背景 ==
会议主办地是风景优美、保密条件高的加拿大魁北克省{{le|拉马巴克|La Malbaie}}{{le|美娜丽莎饭店|Fairmont Le Manoir Richelieu}}举行<ref>{{cite news |date=June 8, 2017 |title=PM prepares Charlevoix for next year's G7 summit |url=https://montreal.ctvnews.ca/pm-prepares-charlevoix-for-next-year-s-g7-summit-1.3450332 |publisher=CTV Montreal |accessdate=2018-06-08 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170609042313/http://montreal.ctvnews.ca/pm-prepares-charlevoix-for-next-year-s-g7-summit-1.3450332 |archivedate=2017年6月9日 |df= }}</ref>，是自1981年以来魁北克省首次举办八国集团峰会。拉马巴克昔日是美国总统的[[度假小镇|度假小镇]]。准备工作需要耗费4.65亿美元，工作包括高速网络服务、新的通讯塔站和安全栅栏<ref name="NYTimes-Resort">{{cite news |last1=Lavoie |first1=Jasmin |last2=Bilefsky |first2=Dan |date=2018-06-07 |title=A Quebec Resort Town Hopes the Group of 7 Will Revive a Glorious Past |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/07/business/g7-la-malbaie-charlevoix-quebec.html |work=The New York Times |accessdate=2018-06-08 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180608105300/https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/07/business/g7-la-malbaie-charlevoix-quebec.html |archivedate=2018-06-08 |df= }}</ref>。[[不列颠哥伦比亚|不列颠哥伦比亚]][[基隆拿|基隆拿]]也被考虑为主办地<ref name="NYTimes-Resort"/>。

2017年5月，加拿大总理[[贾斯汀·特鲁多|贾斯汀·特鲁多]]表示他希望“展示加拿大在国内和国际上先进的地方，扩大中产阶段、推进[[性别平等|性别平等]]、对抗气候变化并推动对多样性和包容性的尊重”<ref name="gcnews" />。7月，自2012年来担任加拿大七国集团会议向导<ref>{{cite web| url=http://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2017/06/23/peter-boehm| title=Peter Boehm| date=2017-06-23| website=pm.gc.ca| accessdate=2017-06-24| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170624185753/http://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2017/06/23/peter-boehm| archivedate=2017-06-24| df=}}</ref>的{{le|彼得·贝姆|Peter Boehm}}或任命为峰会副部长兼总理个人代表<ref>{{cite web| url=http://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2017/06/23/prime-minister-announces-changes-senior-ranks-public-service| title=The Prime Minister announces changes in the senior ranks of the Public Service| date=2017-06-23| accessdate=2017-06-24| publisher=Office of the Prime Minister| website=pm.gc.ca| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170629091035/http://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2017/06/23/prime-minister-announces-changes-senior-ranks-public-service| archivedate=2017-06-29| df=}}</ref>。12月，特鲁多公布峰会标志及五大关键主题<ref>{{cite web| url=https://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2017/12/14/prime-minister-unveils-themes-canadas-2018-g7-presidency| title=Prime Minister unveils themes for Canada's 2018 G7 Presidency| date=2017-12-14| publisher=Office of the Prime Minister| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180429032731/https://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2017/12/14/prime-minister-unveils-themes-canadas-2018-g7-presidency| archivedate=2018-04-29| df=}}</ref>。

2018年6月8日，美国总统唐纳德·特朗普提出要恢复八国集团<ref name=":3" />，意大利总理朱塞佩·孔特表示支持<ref name="Politico" />。

== 议程 ==
“对每个人都起到作用的经济增长”，又称“公平增长”，法语“Croissance Équitable”<ref>{{cite news|title=7e Forum Mondial des Fonds de Pension : Croissance Équitable et Financement|url=http://analysefinanciere.org/2018/04/06/7e-forum-mondial-fonds-de-pension-croissance-equitable-financement-pme-eti-innovantes/|accessdate=2018-06-02|work=Revue Analyse Financière|publisher=Société Française des Analystes Financiers|date=2018-04-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180611100600/http://analysefinanciere.org/2018/04/06/7e-forum-mondial-fonds-de-pension-croissance-equitable-financement-pme-eti-innovantes/|archivedate=2018-06-11|df=}}</ref>，扎根于罗马教皇[[通谕|通谕]]《[[新事通谕|新事通谕]]》，对法国的经济增长和魁北克的制定者，包括[[皮埃尔·特鲁多|皮埃尔·特鲁多]]和[[蒙特利尔大学|蒙特利尔大学]]的校友产生了深远的影响<ref>{{cite news|last1=Bischoff|first1=Peter|title= La réception de Rerum novarum dans un sol préparé d’avance : la province de Québec|url= https://www.academia.edu/25634964/_La_réception_de_Rerum_novarum_dans_un_sol_préparé_d_avance_la_province_de_Québec_Bulletin_du_RCHTQ_35_2_automne_2010_4-8|accessdate=2018-06-07|work=Bulletin du RCHTQ|publisher=Université de Montréal|date=2010-10-01}}</ref>。该理论指出，政府和{{le|机构投资者|Institutional investor}}在做出投资决策时，必须始终考虑到[[员工|员工]]的最佳利益，包括体面的工资和养老金福利。参与其中的由员工提名的退休金委员会成员有望为公平投资理念的发展作出贡献，从而推动这种经济方式<ref>{{cite news|author=The World Pensions Council|title=6th World Pensions Forum: Greening, Governance and Asset Ownership|url=https://amnt.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/6th-World-Pensions-Forum2017.pdf|accessdate=2018-06-07|work=Revue Analyse Financière|publisher=SFAF|date=2017-04-05|format=PDF}}</ref>。

加拿大和法国的思想领袖利用现代化、定量的管理工具强调了在所有经济范畴内实现真正性别平等的需要，帮助精确测量{{le|性别薪资差距|Gender pay gap}}，从而在企业和政府层面修正政策措施。峰会开始前几个星期在巴黎、蒙特利尔和多伦多举办的筹备会议中，加拿大和欧洲[[社會保障基金|社會保障基金]]和政治制定者一致同意提高所有资产类别中的妇女权利和其他“相关社会参数”的进程中，有必要强化并承担更多的责任<ref>{{cite news|last1=Firzli|first1=Nicolas|title=Greening, Governance and Growth in the Age of Popular Empowerment|url=http://www.pensions-expert.com/Special-Features/The-Cut/Greening-governance-and-growth-in-the-age-of-popular-empowerment|accessdate=2018-04-27|work=FT Pensions Experts|publisher=Financial Times|date=2018-04-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=7e Forum Mondial des Fonds de Pension : Croissance Équitable et Financement|url=http://analysefinanciere.org/2018/04/06/7e-forum-mondial-fonds-de-pension-croissance-equitable-financement-pme-eti-innovantes/|accessdate=2018-06-02|work=Revue Analyse Financière|publisher=Société Française des Analystes Financiers|date=2018-04-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180611100600/http://analysefinanciere.org/2018/04/06/7e-forum-mondial-fonds-de-pension-croissance-equitable-financement-pme-eti-innovantes/|archivedate=2018-06-11|df=}}</ref>。在这一方面，加拿大总理贾斯汀·特鲁多预计宣布一项独特的七国集团可持续性倡议，专门聚焦全球资本市场的性别多样化<ref>{{cite news|last1=Smith|first1=Marie-Danielle|title=Canadian government to announce G7 sustainability initiative with focus on gender diversity in global capital markets|url=http://nationalpost.com/news/politics/pension-funds-canadian-government-to-announce-g7-sustainability-initiative|accessdate=2018-06-03|work=National Post|publisher=NP|date=2018-05-28}}</ref>。

{{le|世界养老金和投资论坛|World Pensions & Investments Forum}}经济学家Nicolas J. Firzli认为，尽管美国撤出巴黎协定，七国集团推动的国际共识建设进程，让可能倾向于重新合作，以实现可持续发展目标，这归功于贾斯汀·特鲁多的[[多边主义|多边主义]]方针<ref>{{cite news|last1=Firzli|first1=Nicolas|title=The New Geopolitics of Globalization and the Road to Charlevoix|url=https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3187450|accessdate=2018-06-01|work=Revue Analyse Financière|publisher=SFAF|date=2018-01-15}}</ref>，以及美国和加拿大机构投资者对{{le|环境、社会和公司治理|ESG}}知情投资政策的迅速转变<ref>{{cite news|last1=Firzli|first1=Nicolas|title=Greening, Governance and Growth in the Age of Popular Empowerment|url=http://www.pensions-expert.com/Special-Features/The-Cut/Greening-governance-and-growth-in-the-age-of-popular-empowerment|accessdate=2018-04-27|work=FT Pensions Experts|publisher=Financial Times|date=2018-04-03}}</ref>。

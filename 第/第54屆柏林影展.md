'''第54屆[[柏林影展|柏林影展]]'''（{{lang-de|Internationale Filmfestspiele Berlin 2004}}），於2004年2月5日至2月15日於[[德國|德國]][[柏林|柏林]]舉辦<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2004/01_jahresblatt_2004/01_Jahresblatt_2004.html |title=54TH BERLIN INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL FEBRUARY 5 - 15, 2004 |accessdate=10 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140419102256/http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2004/01_jahresblatt_2004/01_Jahresblatt_2004.html| archivedate=19 April 2014 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>，開幕片為美國導演[[安東尼·明格拉|安東尼·明格拉]]執導的《[[冷山|冷山]]》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2004/feb/05/festivals.berlinfilmfestival2004 |title=Cold Mountain opens Berlin festival |accessdate=10 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://filmmakermagazine.com/1467-berlinale-2004-competition/ |title=BERLINALE 2004 COMPETITION |accessdate=10 June 2014 |work=filmmakermagazine.com |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130921052203/http://filmmakermagazine.com/1467-berlinale-2004-competition/ |archivedate=2013年9月21日 }}</ref>，閉幕片則為比利時導演Stéphane Vuillet執導的《{{link-de|溫馨快遞|25 Grad im Winter}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2004/08_pressemitteilungen_2004/08_Pressemitteilungen_2004-Detail_653.html |title=BERLINALE 2004: COMPETITION SELECTION NOW COMPLETE|accessdate=10 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110109111643/http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2004/08_pressemitteilungen_2004/08_Pressemitteilungen_2004-Detail_653.html| archivedate=9 January 2011 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>，評審團主席則由美國演員[[法蘭西絲·麥朵曼|法蘭西絲·麥朵曼]]擔任<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2004/04_jury_2004/04_Jury_2004.html |title=JURIES 2004 |accessdate=10 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131023125449/http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2004/04_jury_2004/04_Jury_2004.html| archivedate=23 October 2013 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>。

'''第二十大道东北大桥'''（{{lang-en|20<sup>th</sup> Avenue NE Bridge}}），又称'''拉文纳公园大桥'''（{{lang-en|Ravenna Park Bridge}}），是一座位于[[美国|美国]][[西雅图|西雅图]]{{tsl|en|Ravenna Park|拉文纳公园}}深谷之上的三铰[[桁架桥|钢桁]][[拱桥|拱桥]]。大桥由弗兰克·M·约翰逊在城市工程师阿瑟·迪莫克的指导下设计。<ref name="Long 2008-07-23"/>大桥于{{startdate|1913}}建成投入使用，并被列入[[美国|美国]][[国家史迹名录|国家史迹名录]]和{{tsl|en|List of landmarks in Seattle|西雅图地标名录}}中。<ref>{{cite book|title=National Register of Historic Places; Annual Listing of Historic Properties (Part II)|date=1983-03-01|publisher=National Park Service|page=8669|url=http://www.cr.nps.gov/nr/listings/Weekly_List_FR-March-1-1983-listingyear1982.pdf|access-date=2011-03-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111103001017/http://www.cr.nps.gov/nr/listings/Weekly_List_FR-March-1-1983-listingyear1982.pdf|archive-date=2011-11-03|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Individual Landmarks|url=http://www.seattle.gov/neighborhoods/preservation/t.htm|publisher=City of Seattle Department of Neighborhoods|accessdate=2011-03-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100604232253/https://www.seattle.gov/neighborhoods/preservation/t.htm|archive-date=2010-06-04|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

大桥总长{{convert|108|m|feet}}，横跨整个深谷的主拱长{{convert|76.3|m|feet|0}}，高{{convert|12.4|m|feet|0}}。大桥桥面为{{convert|5.5|m|feet}}宽的[[水泥|水泥]]路面。大桥自{{startdate|1975}}起对机动车关闭，并作为人行道桥向行人开放。<ref name="Long 2008-07-23">{{cite web|last=Long|first=Priscilla|title=Seattle's Ravenna Park Bridge is constructed in 1913.|url=http://www.historyink.org/index.cfm?DisplayPage=output.cfm&file_id=8699|work=HistoryLink.org Online Encyclopedia of Washington State History|date=2008-07-23|accessdate=2011-03-24|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111003055710/http://www.historyink.org/index.cfm?DisplayPage=output.cfm&file_id=8699|archive-date=2011-10-03|dead-url=no}}</ref>

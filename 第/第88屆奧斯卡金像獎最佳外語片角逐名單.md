本条目是'''[[第88届奥斯卡金像奖|第88届奥斯卡金像奖]][[奥斯卡金像奖最佳外语片奖|最佳外语片奖]]'''的角逐名单。[[美国电影艺术与科学学会|美国电影艺术与科学学会]]自1956年设立该奖以来，每年都会邀请各国电影行业提交他们当年最出色的电影参加最佳外语片奖评选<ref name="HistPg2">{{cite web|title=History of the Academy Awards - Page 2 |publisher=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |url=http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/history02.html |accessdate=2014-09-19 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080622191328/http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/history02.html |archivedate=2008-06-22 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。该奖项每年由学院颁发主要包含非英语对白的非美国制作电影长片<ref name="HistPg2"/><ref name="Rules">{{cite web|title=Rule Thirteen: Special Rules for the Foreign Language Film Award |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |publisher=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/87/pdf/87aa_rules.pdf |accessdate=2014-09-19 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140827034453/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/87/pdf/87aa_rules.pdf |archivedate=2014-08-27 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。外语片奖委员会负责监督评选过程、审阅所有被提交的电影。奥斯卡提名公布的前一个星期，九名入围的竞争者被公布<ref name="Rules"/>。

所有被提交的电影必须于2014年10月1日到2015年9月30日期间在制片国家公映。提交截至时间为2015年10月1日，当月晚些时候学院将公布符合条件的影片名单<ref name="Dates">{{cite web |url=http://variety.com/2015/film/awards/oscar-foreign-language-entries-start-piling-up-1201585370/ |title=Oscar Foreign-Language Entries Start Piling Up |last=Dore |first=Shalini |date=2015-09-03 |accessdate=2015-09-03 |work=Variety}}</ref><ref name="MovieOn">{{cite web|url=http://movie-on.blogspot.co.uk/2015/10/88th-academy-awards-foreign-language.html |title=88th Academy Awards Foreign Language Film Submissions |work=Movie On|date=2015-10-10 |accessdate=2015-10-10}}</ref>。在合条件影片名单中脱颖而出的九部入围正式提名名单的电影于12月17日公布<ref name="Dec15Short">{{cite web|url=https://www.oscars.org/news/9-foreign-language-films-advance-oscarr-race-1 |title=9 Foreign Language Films Advance In Oscar Race |work=Oscars |date=2015-12-17 |accessdate=2015-12-18}}</ref><ref name="Dec15ShortHW">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscars-nine-titles-advance-foreign-850070 |title=Oscars: Nine Titles Advance in Foreign Language Category |work=The Hollywood Reporter |last=Kilday |first=Gregg |date=2015-12-17 |accessdate=2015-12-18}}</ref>。最终提名名单定于2016年1月14日公布<ref name="Dates" /><ref name="VarietyOct">{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2015/film/news/oscars-foreign-language-entries-deadline-closes-1201606716/ |title=Oscars: Foreign-Language Films Deadline Closes Thursday |work=variety |last=Dore |first=Shalini |date=2015-10-01 |accessdate=2015-10-01}}</ref><ref name="88nom">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscar-nominations-2016-full-list-855670 |title=Oscar Nominations: The Complete List |work=The Hollywood Reporter |last=Ford |first=Rebecca |date=2016-01-14 |accessdate=2016-01-14}}</ref>。

共有81个国家在截止日期前提交了作品<ref name="Final">{{cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/news/81-countries-competition-2015-foreign-language-film-oscar |title=81 Countries In Competition For 2015 Foreign Language Film Oscar |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |date=2015-10-09 |accessdate=2015-10-09}}</ref><ref name="Final-HWR">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscars-81-films-submitted-foreign-829813 |title=Oscars: 81 Films Submitted for Foreign-Language Award |work=The Hollywood Reporter |last=Kilday |first=Gregg |date=2015-10-09 |accessdate=2015-10-09}}</ref>。今年[[巴拉圭|巴拉圭]]首次提交作品，是纪录片《{{le|多云天气|Cloudy Times}}》<ref name="Para1st">{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/for-your-consideration-how-the-foreign-language-oscar-race-is-shaping-up-20150923 |title=For Your Consideration: How the Foreign Language Oscar Race is Shaping Up |last=Knegt |first=Peter |work=IndieWire |date=2015-09-23 |accessdate=2015-09-23}}</ref>。

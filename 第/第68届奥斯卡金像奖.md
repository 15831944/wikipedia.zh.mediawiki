'''第68届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1995年电影|1995年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1996年3月25日下午18点（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上21点）在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的多萝茜·钱德勒大厅举行<ref name="VarietyOscars">{{cite news |last=Lowry |first=Brian |title=Review: “The 68th Annual Academy Awards” |url=http://variety.com/1996/tv/reviews/the-68th-annual-academy-awards-1200445226/ |accessdate=2013-11-23 |work=Variety |publisher=PMC |date=1996-03-26 |archivedate=2013-10-23 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131023144229/http://variety.com/1996/tv/reviews/the-68th-annual-academy-awards-1200445226/}}</ref>，共计颁发了24座[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）<ref name="VarietyOscars" />。本届颁奖典礼由[[昆西·琼斯|昆西·琼斯]]担任[[制片人|制片人]]，杰夫·马格里斯执导，通过[[美国广播公司|ABC]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播<ref name="VarietyOscars" />。[[女演员|女演员]][[乌比·戈德堡|乌比·戈德堡]]第二次担任[[主持人|主持人]]，她上一次主持是在1994年举行的[[第66届奥斯卡金像奖|第66届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]<ref>{{cite news |last=Speers |first=W. |title=Prince Rainier's Behavior Is Just A Slap In The Face |url=http://articles.philly.com/1995-11-01/living/25682754_1_bobbitt-prince-rainier-prince-albert |deadurl=no |accessdate=2013-11-23 |work=The Philadelphia Inquirer |publisher=Philadelphia Media Network |date=1995-11-01 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131020033634/http://articles.philly.com/1995-11-01/living/25682754_1_bobbitt-prince-rainier-prince-albert |archivedate=2013-10-20}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Whoopi Goldberg To Be Oscars Host |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1995/11/01/movies/whoopi-goldberg-to-be-oscars-host.html |accessdate=2013-11-23 《勇敢的心》赢得了包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]、[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|导演]]在内的五项大奖，成为本届奥斯卡金像奖角逐中的最大赢家<ref>{{cite news |last=Welkos |first=Robert W. |title='Braveheart' Is Top Film; Cage, Sarandon Win |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-26/news/mn-51422_1_sarandon-win |accessdate=2013-11-23 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1996-03-26 |archivedate=2013-10-23 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131023035359/http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-26/news/mn-51422_1_sarandon-win}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Hartl |first=John |title=One More Victory For `Braveheart' – Mel Gibson's Epic About A Battle For Freedom Tops The Oscars |url=http://community.seattletimes.nwsource.com/archive/?date=19960326&slug=2321029 |accessdate=2013-11-23 |work=The Seattle Times |publisher=The Seattle Times Company |date=1996-03-25 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016121134/http://community.seattletimes.nwsource.com/archive/?date=19960326&slug=2321029 |archivedate=2013-10-16}}</ref>。其它获奖电影包括获奖两项的《[[阿波罗13号_(电影)|阿波罗13号]]》、《[[风中奇缘_(电影)|风中奇缘]]》、《[[乱世情缘|乱世情缘]]》和《[[普通的嫌疑犯|普通的嫌疑犯]]》，获奖一项的《{{link-en|安妮·弗兰克记得|Anne Frank Remembered}}》、《{{link-en|安东尼娅家族|Antonia's line}}》、《[[小猪宝贝|小猪宝贝]]》、《{{link-en|超级无敌掌门狗：剃刀边缘|A Close Shave}}》、《[[死囚漫步|死囚漫步]]》、《{{link-en|邮差 (电影)|Il Postino: The Postman|邮差}}》、《[[离开拉斯维加斯|离开拉斯维加斯]]》、《恋爱中的利伯曼》（{{lang|en|Lieberman in Love}}）、《[[非强力春药|非强力春药]]》、《一个幸存者的回忆》（{{lang|en|One Survivor Remembers}}》和《[[理智与情感_(电影)|理智与情感]]》。本届颁奖典礼的电视转播一共吸引了近4500万美国观众收看。

== 获奖和提名 ==
第68届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单於1996年2月13日由学院主席[[阿瑟·米勒|阿瑟·米勒]]和音乐制作人昆西·琼斯一起在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亞州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布<ref name="Bona">{{cite book |last=Bona |first=Damien |title=Inside Oscar 2 |year=2002 |isbn=0-345-44970-3 |location=New York, United States |publisher=Ballantine Books}}</ref>{{rp|31}}，《勇敢的心》以10项提名领跑，《阿波罗13号》以9项紧随其后<ref>{{cite news |last=Welkos |first=Robert W. |title=Gibson's Epic Gets 10, 'Babe' Ties for Third |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1996-02-14/entertainment/ca-40073_1_oscar-nomination |accessdate=2013-11-23 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1996-02-14 |author2=Claudia Puig |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131023035403/http://articles.latimes.com/1996-02-14/entertainment/ca-40073_1_oscar-nomination |archivedate=2013-10-23 |deadurl=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Weinraug |first=Bernard |title=Oscar Nominations Are Just One Surprise After Another |archivedate=2013-10-04 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131004214825/http://www.nytimes.com/1996/02/14/movies/oscar-nominations-are-just-one-surprise-after-another.html?pagewanted=2&src=pm |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1996/02/14/movies/oscar-nominations-are-just-one-surprise-after-another.html?pagewanted=2&src=pm |accessdate=2013-11-23 |work=The New York Times |publisher=The New York Times Company |date=1996-02-14}}</ref>。

=== 原创配乐奖的分裂 ===
从本届颁奖典礼开始，美国电影艺术与科学学院的音乐分支将原创配乐奖按电影类型分成两个奖项，分别是正剧类和音乐剧/喜剧类<ref>{{cite book |last=Levy |first=Emanuel |title=All About Oscar: The History and Politics of the Academy Awards |year=2003 |isbn=0-8264-1452-4 |location=New York, United States |edition= |oclc= |publisher=Continuum International Publishing Group |page=55}}</ref>。这被视为学院对[[华特迪士尼动画工作室|华特迪士尼动画工作室]]制作的动画长片在过去多年中垄断原创配乐奖和原创歌曲奖所做出的回应<ref name="Pond" />{{rp|99}}。四年后，这两个奖项又重新合并成了原来的这一个奖<ref>{{cite news |last=Pandolfi |first=Chris |title=The Academy's Ever-Evolving Best Original Score Category |url=http://voices.yahoo.com/the-academys-ever-evolving-best-original-score-category-10987670.html?cat=33 |accessdate=2013-11-23 |publisher=Yahoo! Voices |date=2012-02-21 |archivedate=2013-10-20 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131020222130/http://voices.yahoo.com/the-academys-ever-evolving-best-original-score-category-10987670.html?cat=33}}</ref>。

=== 彩虹联盟的抗议 ===
颁奖典礼开始数天前，牧师[[杰西·杰克逊|杰西·杰克逊]]带领激进团体彩虹联盟（{{lang|en|Rainbow Coalition}}），计划针对电影业中有关[[非裔美国人|非裔美国人]]和其他[[少数群体|少数群体]]所面临的问题进行一次抗议活动<ref name="Protest">{{cite news |last=Braxton |first=Greg |title=Jackson Plans Oscar Protest |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-17/local/me-48073_1_jesse-jackson |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1996-03-17 |archivedate=2013-10-22 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131022224601/http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-17/local/me-48073_1_jesse-jackson}}</ref>。该团体对电影和[[电视|电视]]中对少数族裔进行的直言不讳刻划表示反对，还抗议了娱乐业中这些族群就业比例较低的情况<ref name="Protest" />。杰克逊还进一步指出，获真人短片奖提名的戴安妮·休斯顿是这年唯一一位获提名的非裔美国人，由此可见少数族裔在[[好莱坞|好莱坞]]所存在的差距<ref>{{cite news |title=Mixed Reviews for Oscar Protest |archivedate=2013-10-20 |url=http://articles.philly.com/1996-03-21/news/25637680_1_african-american-preston-l-holmes-def-pictures |deadurl=no |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=The Philadelphia Inquirer |publisher=Philadelphia Media Network |date=1996-03-21 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131020033004/http://articles.philly.com/1996-03-21/news/25637680_1_african-american-preston-l-holmes-def-pictures}}</ref>。该团队起初计划在多萝茜·钱德勒大厅外举行示威，但之后杰克逊和制片人琼斯之间达成了一项协议，抗议活动改在邻近的美国广播公司子公司{{lang|en|[[KABC-TV|KABC-TV]]}}的广播设施前进行<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|38}}。不过琼斯表示，奥斯卡颁奖典礼不是进行此类抗议的合适场所，声称：“凭什么电影业就要和美国的其他任何行业不同？全国普遍存在这个问题，方方面面都存在着歧视。”<ref>{{cite news |last=Kleid |first=Beth |title=Oscar Watch |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-25/entertainment/ca-50951_1_oscar-show |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1996-03-25 |archivedate=2013-10-22 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131022224615/http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-25/entertainment/ca-50951_1_oscar-show}}</ref>

=== 专业评价 ===
本届晚会获奖了大部分媒体的正面评价。《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》影评人珍妮特·马斯林（{{lang|en|Janet Maslin}}）对之赞不绝口：“琼斯先生有针对性地将今年的颁奖典礼转变成了对好莱坞新星的展示。”她还称赞了主持人乌比·戈德堡的开场独白，称其“树立了今年插科打诨段落的尖锐标准”<ref>{{cite news |last=Maslin |first=Janet |title=Television Review: Energy, Gallantry, Graphics And Glamour at the Oscars |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131016204921/http://www.nytimes.com/1996/03/27/movies/television-review-energy-gallantry-graphics-and-glamour-at-the-oscars.html |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1996/03/27/movies/television-review-energy-gallantry-graphics-and-glamour-at-the-oscars.html |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=The New York Times |publisher=The New York Times Company |date=1996-03-27}}</ref>。《[[人物_(杂志)|人物]]》周刊专栏作家珍妮丝·明（{{lang|en|Janice Min}}）写道，“3月25日举办的第68届奥斯卡颁奖典礼中最令人震惊的犯罪行为就是——哦天呐！——那永无止境的优雅和高品味……这是最高级的笑料！”<ref>{{cite news|last=Min |first=Janice |title=An Affair To Remember |quote=the most egregious crime at the 68th Academy Awards on March 25 was–egad!–the relentless elegance and good taste that deprived viewers of genuine, Grade A snicker fodder. |url=http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0,,20103212,00.html |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=People |publisher=Time Warner |date=1996-04-08 |archivedate=2013-10-21 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131021085547/http://www.people.com/people/archive/article/0%2C%2C20103212%2C00.html }}</ref>。《[[洛杉矶时报|洛杉矶时报]]》电视评论员霍华德·罗森伯格（{{lang|en|Howard Rosenberg}}）为戈德堡的表现叫好，指出她“自信的表演……象征着继[[第66届奥斯卡金像奖|1994年颁奖典礼]]后惊人的进步”<ref>{{cite news |last=Rosenberg |first=Howard |title=Real Drama? It Didn't Come From Ribbons |quote=confident performance [...] was symbolic of her whopping improvement as host over her showing on the 1994 Oscars. |archivedate=2013-10-22 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131022224734/http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-26/entertainment/ca-57051_1_ribbons |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1996-03-26/entertainment/ca-57051_1_ribbons |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1996-03-26}}</ref>。

一些媒体对晚会的评价不佳。《[[芝加哥论坛报|芝加哥论坛报]]》电影评论员史蒂夫·约翰逊（{{lang|en|Steve Johnson}}）感叹戈德堡的表现平淡无奇，像去年大卫·莱特曼无法预料的胡思乱想那样把节目变得漫长。他还表示，以服装设计奖提名作品的表演作为开场的做法，是继[[第61届奥斯卡金像奖|第61届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]上[[罗伯·劳|罗伯·劳]]和白雪公主演唱“{{lang|en|Proud Mary}}”作开场以来最愚蠢的<ref>{{cite news |last=Johnson |first=Steve |title=Whoopi Rises Above The Bland, But Not For Long |quote=settled into bland script reading that made one long for David Letterman's cranky unpredictability in the role last year. |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131016124203/http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1996-03-26/news/9603260063_1_quincy-jones-moments-christopher-reeve |url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1996-03-26/news/9603260063_1_quincy-jones-moments-christopher-reeve |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=Chicago Tribune |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1996-03-26}}</ref>。《[[娱乐周刊|娱乐周刊]]》的{{link-en|肯·塔克|Ken Tucker}}对最佳影片奖得主《勇敢的心》的主导地位感到哀叹，还认为这个夜晚因缺乏时尚魅力而变得有些让人讨厌<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ew.com/ew/article/0,,292003,00.html |title=Oscars 1996: The Show |date=1996-04-05 |first=Ken |last=Tucker |work=Entertainment Weekly |publisher=Time Warner |deadurl=no |accessdate=2013-11-24 |archivedate=2013-10-17 |quote=had the makings of a tiresome evening. |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131017073939/http://www.ew.com/ew/article/0%2C%2C292003%2C00.html }}</ref>。

=== 收视率和奖项 ===
本届颁奖典礼通过美国广播公司在美国直播，其播出时段里平均有4481万观众收看，与[[第67届奥斯卡金像奖|去年]]相比提高了9%<ref name="TV By the Numbers">{{cite web | first=Bill | last=Gorman | title=Academy Awards Averages 41.3 Million Viewers; Most Since 2005 |url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2010/03/08/academy-awards-averages-41-3-million-viewers-most-since-2005/44217/ | work=''TV by the Numbers'' | publisher=Tribune Company | date=2010-03-08 | accessdate=2013-11-24 | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131101231748/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2010/03/08/academy-awards-averages-41-3-million-viewers-most-since-2005/44217/ | archivedate= 2013-11-01 | deadurl= no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Johnson |first=Greg |title=Call It the Glamour Bowl |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1999-03-18 |archivedate=2013-10-22 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131022224101/http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 |deadurl=no}}</ref>。不过根据尼尔森收视率调查的数据显示，本届晚会吸引了30.3%的美国[[家庭|家庭]]收看，与去年的32.5%相比有所下降<ref name="TVB Oscars">{{cite news|title=Academy Awards ratings |work=''Television Bureau of Advertising'' |format=PDF |url=http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |accessdate=2013-11-24 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016061652/http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |deadurl=yes |archivedate=2013-10-16 }}</ref>。而在18-49岁年龄段人段中，节目的收视率为18.6%，也不及前一年的21.7%<ref name="TVB Oscars" />。

1996年7月，颁奖典礼获得了第48届[[黄金时段艾美奖|黄金时段艾美奖]]的7项提名<ref>{{cite news |title=Primetime Emmy Award database |url=http://www.emmys.com/shows/68th-annual-academy-awards | accessdate=2013-11-24 | publisher=Academy of Television Arts and Sciences |archivedate=2013-10-17 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131017130150/http://www.emmys.com/shows/68th-annual-academy-awards}}</ref>，并在两个月后为格雷格·布伦顿（{{lang|en|Greg Brunton}}）赢得了一座电视转播照明设计和指导奖<ref>{{cite news |title=Who Won What: The Winners of Television's Nighttime Emmy Awards |archivedate=2013-10-23 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131023035408/http://articles.latimes.com/1996-09-09/entertainment/ca-42203_1_nighttime-emmy-awards/2 |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1996-09-09/entertainment/ca-42203_1_nighttime-emmy-awards/2 |accessdate=2013-11-24 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1996-09-09}}</ref>。

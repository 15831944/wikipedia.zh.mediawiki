'''第66届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1993年电影|1993年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1994年3月21日下午18点（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上21点）在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的多萝茜·钱德勒大厅举行，共计颁发了23座[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）。本届颁奖典礼通过[[美国广播公司|ABC]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播，由吉尔·凯茨担任[[制片人|制片人]]，杰夫·马格里斯执导。<ref>{{cite web |title=Credits |url=http://jeffmargolisproductions.com/credits/ |publisher=Jeff Margolis Productions |accessdate=2014-01-22 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131014114430/http://jeffmargolisproductions.com/credits/ |archivedate=2013-10-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=66th Annual Academy Awards Presentation |archivedate=2014-01-18 |url=http://www.nytimes.com/tv/show/38901/66th-Annual-Academy-Awards-Presentation/credits |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=The New York Times |publisher=The New York Times Company |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140118063507/http://www.nytimes.com/tv/show/38901/66th-Annual-Academy-Awards-Presentation/credits}}</ref>[[女演员|女演员]][[乌比·戈德堡|乌比·戈德堡]]首次担任[[主持人|主持人]]<ref>{{cite news |title=Goldberg To Replace Crystal As Academy Awards Host |archivedate=2014-01-03 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140103183157/http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1994-02-07/news/9402070032_1_year-academy-academy-awards-oscar |url=http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1994-02-07/news/9402070032_1_year-academy-academy-awards-oscar |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=Orlando Sentinel |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-02-07}}</ref>。近一个月前的2月26日，[[蘿拉·鄧恩|蘿拉·鄧恩]]在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亞州)|比佛利山]]的比佛利希尔顿酒店主持颁发了[[奥斯卡科技成果奖|奥斯卡科技成果奖]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html |title=Past Scientific & Technical Awards Ceremonies |accessdate=2013-11-23 |publisher=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131013220908/http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html |archivedate=2013-10-13 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。

《辛德勒的名单》赢得了包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]、[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|导演]]在内的七项大奖，成为本届奥斯卡金像奖角逐中的最大赢家<ref>{{cite news |last=Eller |first=Claudia |title='Schindler's List' Brings Triumph for Spielberg : Movies: Director wins for best picture. Tom Hanks is honored as best actor and Holly Hunter is best actress. |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-03-22/news/mn-37067_1_tom-hanks |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=Los Angeles Times |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131101040913/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-03-22/news/mn-37067_1_tom-hanks |archivedate=2013-11-01 |deadurl=no |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-03-22}}</ref>。其它获奖电影包括获奖三项的《[[侏罗纪公园|侏罗纪公园]]》和《[[钢琴课|钢琴课]]》，获奖两项的《[[費城_(電影)|费城]]》和获奖一项的《[[纯真年代_(电影)|纯真年代]]》、《[[四千金的情人|四千金的情人]]》、《保卫我们的生活》（{{lang|en|''Defending Our Lives''}}）、《[[亡命天涯_(1993年电影)|亡命天涯]]》、《我是承诺》（{{lang|en|''I Am a Promise: The Children of Stanton Elementary School''}}）、《[[窈窕奶爸|窈窕奶爸]]》、《黑骑士》（{{lang|de|''Schwarzfahrer''}}）和《{{link-en|超级无敌掌门狗：引鹅入室|The Wrong Trousers}}》。本届颁奖典礼的电视转播一共吸引了超过4600万美国观众收看。

== 获奖和提名 ==
第66届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单於1994年2月9日由学院主席[[阿瑟·希勒|阿瑟·希勒]]（{{lang|en|Arthur Hiller}}）和女演员[[克里斯汀·拉蒂|克里斯汀·拉蒂]]（{{lang|en|Christine Lahti}}）一起在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亞州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布<ref>{{cite news |title=12 Oscar nominations for 'Schindler's List' |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?id=-jpOAAAAIBAJ&sjid=1xMEAAAAIBAJ&pg=4396,77565&dq=arthur-hiller+christine-lahti&hl=en |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=New Straits Times |publisher=Media Prima |date=1994-02-11}}</ref>。《辛德勒的名单》以12项提名领跑，《钢琴课》和《[[告別有情天|告別有情天]]》均以8项排在第二位<ref>{{cite news |last=Fox |first=David J. |title=Oscar's Favorite 'List' : The Nominations : 'Schindler's' Sweeps Up With 12 Nods : 'The Piano' and 'The Remains of the Day' both receive eight nominations; 'Fugitive,' 'In the Name of the Father' earn seven |archivedate=2013-12-03 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203080052/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-02-10/entertainment/ca-21449_1_oscar-nominations |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-02-10/entertainment/ca-21449_1_oscar-nominations |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-02-10}}</ref>。

获奖名单在1994年3月21日举行的颁奖典礼上公布<ref>{{cite news|title=Nominees & Winners for the 66th Academy Awards |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/oscarlegacy/1990-1999/66nominees.html |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |publisher=AMPAS |archivedate=2013-12-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203080052/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-02-10/entertainment/ca-21449_1_oscar-nominations }}</ref>。《[[辛德勒的名单|辛德勒的名单]]》成为继1960年的《[[公寓春光|公寓春光]]》以来首部获最佳影片奖的[[黑白电影|黑白电影]]<ref>{{cite book |last=Levy |first=Emanuel |title=All About Oscar: The History and Politics of the Academy Awards |year=2003 |isbn=0-8264-1452-4 |location=New York, United States |publisher=Continuum International Publishing Group |page=191}}</ref>，获导演奖提名的[[简·坎皮恩|简·坎皮恩]]成为历史上第二位获该奖项提名的女性<ref>{{cite news |last=Fox |first=David J. |title='Schindler's' Adds a Pair to the List : Awards: Spielberg epic takes more honors--for screenwriting and editing. Jane Campion's 'The Piano' also wins. |archivedate=2013-10-24 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131024101413/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-03-14/entertainment/ca-33988_1_jane-campion |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-03-14/entertainment/ca-33988_1_jane-campion |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-03-14}}</ref>。[[霍利·亨特|霍利·亨特]]和[[艾玛·汤普森|艾玛·汤普森]]都既获得了[[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|女主角奖]]提名，又得到了[[奥斯卡最佳女配角奖|女配角奖]]提名，这也是奥斯卡奖历史上首次有两位演员在[[一年获两项奥斯卡金像奖提名演员列表|同一届颁奖典礼中获得两项表演类奖项提名]]<ref>{{cite news |last=Brennan |first=Judy |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-02-11/entertainment/ca-21862_1_oscar-nominations |title=The Hollywood Buzz on Oscar Snubs and Surprises |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=Los Angeles Times |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131218102340/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-02-11/entertainment/ca-21862_1_oscar-nominations |archivedate=2013-12-18 |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-02-11}}</ref>。获女配角奖的[[安娜·帕奎因|安娜·帕奎因]]年仅11岁，成为奥斯卡表演类奖项历史上第二年轻的获奖者，仅次于因《[[纸月亮|纸月亮]]》获奖时年仅10岁的{{link-en|塔图姆·奥尼尔|Tatum O'Neal}}<ref>{{cite news |last=Johnson |first=Allan |title=Anna Steals The Show |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131021181400/http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1994-03-29/features/9403290026_1_piano-new-zealand-actress-award |url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1994-03-29/features/9403290026_1_piano-new-zealand-actress-award |deadurl=no |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=Chicago Tribune |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-03-29 |archivedate=2013-10-21}}</ref>。

== 典礼资讯 ==
[[File:Whoopi_Goldberg_at_a_NYC_No_on_Proposition_8_Rally.jpg|130px]]担任第76届奥斯卡颁奖典礼的主持人]]
由于[[第65届奥斯卡金像奖|之前一年的颁奖典礼]]所获评价不佳，连续担任了四届主持人的[[男演员|男演员]][[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]宣布不会主持1994年的电视转播<ref>{{cite news |title=Company Town Annex |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-01-21/business/fi-14034_1_hosted-crystal-years |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-01-21 |archivedate=2013-12-18 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131218082408/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-01-21/business/fi-14034_1_hosted-crystal-years}}</ref>。他在一份由自己公关发布的声明中表示：“经过三届[[葛萊美獎|葛萊美獎]]，四项奥斯卡奖和六届《[[喜剧救济|喜剧救济]]》后，我想暂时不干主持人这行了。我总是对能够担任主持感到荣幸，并且尽自己的最大努力继承[[鲍勃·霍普|鲍勃·霍普]]和[[约翰尼·卡森|约翰尼·卡森]]的传统。我希望新的主持人能够像我一样拥有一段愉快的时光”<ref>{{cite news |title=Billy Crystal Decides Not to Be Oscars Host |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1994/01/21/arts/billy-crystal-decides-not-to-be-oscars-host.html |accessdate=2014-01-22 |work=The New York Times |publisher=The New York Times |date=1994-01-21 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131014003159/http://www.nytimes.com/1994/01/21/arts/billy-crystal-decides-not-to-be-oscars-host.html |archivedate=2013-10-14}}</ref>。由于克里斯托不会再担任主持，许多媒体都在猜测制片人吉尔·凯茨将选择谁来接手。电影专栏[[作家|作家]]杰克·马修斯（{{lang|en|Jack Matthews}}）建议由男演员汤姆·汉克斯来主持，因为他集“魅力、尊严、才华、机智”于一身，是个“值得一提”的真正“电影明星”<ref name="HostingRole">{{cite news |last=Matthews |first=Jack |title=Commentary: Who Will Host the Oscars? It's Not Crystal Clear |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-01-22/entertainment/ca-14265_1_oscar-host |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-01-22 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131218102251/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-01-22/entertainment/ca-14265_1_oscar-host |archivedate=2013-12-18}}</ref>。凯茨还有向[[史提夫·馬丁|史提夫·馬丁]]、[[贝蒂·米勒|贝蒂·米勒]]和[[约翰尼·卡森|约翰尼·卡森]]发出邀请，但他们都谢绝了<ref name="HostingRole" />。

经过几天的炒作，凯茨宣布他请来了曾因《[[人鬼情未了|人鬼情未了]]》获奥斯卡最佳女配角奖的[[喜剧演员|喜剧演员]]乌比·戈德堡首次出任这一晚会的主持人<ref>{{cite news |title=Whoopi Goldberg To Play Host at Oscars |archivedate=2013-12-20 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131220231708/http://www.nytimes.com/1994/02/07/arts/whoopi-goldberg-to-play-host-at-oscars.html |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1994/02/07/arts/whoopi-goldberg-to-play-host-at-oscars.html |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=The New York Times |publisher=The New York Times |date=1994-02-07}}</ref>，后者因此成为首位单独主持奥斯卡颁奖典礼的女性和[[非裔美国人|非裔美国人]]<ref>{{cite news |title=Comedian Whoopi Goldberg to Host Oscars |work=The Gazette |publisher=Postmedia Network |date=1994-02-07 |page=F5}}</ref>。凯茨在接受《[[洛杉矶时报|洛杉矶时报]]》[[采访|采访]]时表示：“她是位拥有数百万粉丝，辨识度极高的明星。”<ref>{{cite news |last=Fox |first=David J. |title=Whoopi! There It Is: Goldberg to Host Oscars : Awards: Performer breaks new ground - first woman and first African American as solo master of ceremonies. |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-02-07/entertainment/ca-20044_1_african-american-host |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-02-07 |archivedate=2013-12-18 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131218082916/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-02-07/entertainment/ca-20044_1_african-american-host |deadurl=no}}</ref>。对于一些媒体对戈德堡不修边幅和直言不讳幽默风格的担忧他表示：“有些人可能会觉得她是个随时会爆发的炸弹，总是心里想到什么就说什么。但我对此感到很兴奋，最重要的是，她有心要做好，而且她很聪明，所以不管她说什么都会是合适的。”<ref>{{cite news |last=Ryan |first=Desmond |title=Whoopi pushes the envelope |work=Austin American-Statesman |publisher=Cox Enterprises |date=1994-03-20 |page=7}}</ref>。戈德堡本人表示对得以担任主持深感荣幸：“从在一旁看着，到真正获奖，再到担任主持，这是人生的重大飞跃。”<ref>{{cite news |last=Marx |first=Andy |title=Goldberg set to host Oscarcast |url=http://variety.com/1994/film/news/goldberg-set-to-host-oscarcast-118072/ |deadurl=no |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Variety |publisher=PMC |date=1994-02-06 |archivedate=2013-12-16 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131216223032/http://variety.com/1994/film/news/goldberg-set-to-host-oscarcast-118072/}}</ref>。

凯茨之前制作的晚会都会围绕一个主题展开，这一年也不例外。他选择的主题是“幕后英雄”（{{lang|en|People Behind the Camera}}），称本届颁奖典礼“将会向那些幕后英雄致敬，正是他们让我们看到了银幕上的精彩，这些[[艺术家|艺术家]]和工匠成就了电影的魔术”<ref name="Wiley & Bona">{{cite book |last=Wiley |first=Mason |last2=Bona |first2=Damien |title=Inside Oscar: The Unofficial History of the Academy Awards |year=1996 |isbn=0-345-40053-4 |location=New York, United States |edition=5 |oclc=779680732 |publisher=Ballantine Books |page=917}}</ref>。为配合该主题，晚会的开场段落包含一段查克·沃克曼（{{lang|en|Chuck Workman}}）制作的短片，其中向许多参与电影制作的导演、剪辑师和[[作曲家|作曲家]]致敬。短片播放时，[[歌手|歌手]]伯纳黛特·彼得斯一边演唱[[史蒂芬·桑坦|史蒂芬·桑坦]][[音乐剧|音乐剧]]《星期天与乔治同游公园》插曲“{{lang|en|Putting It Together}}”的改编版本<ref name="Pond" />{{rp|45}}。电影制作人兼剪辑师卡罗尔·A·斯特赖特（{{lang|en|Carol A. Streit}}）编辑制作了另一段蒙太奇，向为电影事业做出贡献的[[摄影师|摄影师]]致敬<ref>{{cite news |title=The 66th Annual Academy Awards Presentation |archivedate=2013-12-12 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131212090027/http://www.hollywood.com/tv/55443482/the-66th-annual-academy-awards-presentation |url=http://www.hollywood.com/tv/55443482/the-66th-annual-academy-awards-presentation |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=''Hollywood.com'' |publisher=Hollywood.com, LLC}}</ref>。

另外还有多人参与了颁奖典礼的制作。艺术指导设计师罗伊·克里斯托弗（{{lang|en|Roy Christopher}}）为晚会设计了一个新的舞台，其上在显眼的位置放有共五个大型奥斯卡金像塑像<ref name="Oscar Time">{{cite news |last=Welkos |first=Robert W. |title=Oscar Time--Without a Safety Net : Welcome to Live TV--When Even Dogs Can Have Their Day |archivedate=2013-12-18 ||deadurl=no |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-03-21/entertainment/ca-36771_1_oscar-time/2 |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-03-21 |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131218082936/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-03-21/entertainment/ca-36771_1_oscar-time/2}}</ref>。电影配乐师兼作曲家比尔·康堤担任晚会的音乐总监<ref>{{cite news|title=Conti to tune Awards |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140104113409/http://variety.com/1994/film/news/conti-to-tune-awards-117111/ |deadurl=no |archivedate=2014-01-04 |url=http://variety.com/1994/film/news/conti-to-tune-awards-117111/ |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Variety |publisher=PMC |date=1994-01-03}}</ref>。舞蹈家{{link-en|黛比·艾伦|Debbie Allen}}编排了原创配乐奖提名作品的混合舞蹈曲目，由八个来自世界各国的知名芭蕾和舞蹈团体演出<ref name="BalletDance" />。

=== 专业评价 ===
本届晚会获得了大部分媒体的正面评价。《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》影评人珍妮特·马斯林（{{lang|en|Janet Maslin}}）兴高采烈地表示，这场电视转播的“愚蠢行径和闲聊家常都更少，巧妙地把侧重点放在电影片段和正在纪念的真正好莱坞魔术上”。她还称赞了主持人戈德堡的表现，称其持续保持的轻松基调对这个因《辛德勒的名单》而可能变得非常严肃的节目来说非常重要<ref>{{cite news |last=Maslin |first=Janet |title=Critic's Notebook; Minus Suspense Factor, Oscars Cut to the Chase |archivedate=2013-12-19 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131219142507/http://www.nytimes.com/1994/03/23/movies/critic-s-notebook-minus-suspense-factor-oscars-cut-to-the-chase.html |url=http://www.nytimes.com/1994/03/23/movies/critic-s-notebook-minus-suspense-factor-oscars-cut-to-the-chase.html |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=The New York Times |publisher=The New York Times Company |date=1994-03-23}}</ref>。《[[匹兹堡邮报|匹兹堡邮报]]》（{{lang|en|Pittsburgh Post-Gazette}}）电视评论员罗伯特·比安科（{{lang|en|Robert Bianco}}）也称赞了戈德堡的表现，称“她从不会表现得似乎对于主持的节目来说太过聪明或是过分时髦，她从不会把自己表现得似乎就比别人更聪明，更冷静。”他还表彰了[[制片人|制片人]]凯茨，称他选择了一个恰当的主题，并选择了丰富的片段来将其展现出来<ref>{{cite news |last=Bianco |first=Robert |title=Telecast shines spotlight on movies, not distractions |url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1129&dat=19940322&id=FHgxAAAAIBAJ&sjid=2W8DAAAAIBAJ&pg=3153,3920374 |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette |publisher=Crain Communications |date=1994-03-22}}</ref>。《[[娱乐周刊|娱乐周刊]]》影评人安妮·汤普森（{{lang|en|Anne Thompson}}）同样赞扬了节目主持人，称“她那优雅的出场（至少上半场期间），她异常的克制（她没骂人），和她让每一段致辞（即使是很忧伤的讲话也不例外）都变得搞笑的能力，让这场晚会值得一看”<ref>{{cite news|last=Thompson |first=Anne |title=Oscar's List |archivedate=2014-01-01 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140101081310/http://www.ew.com/ew/article/0%2C%2C301647%2C00.html |url=http://www.ew.com/ew/article/0,,301647,00.html |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Entertainment Weekly |publisher=Time Warner |date=1994-04-01 }}</ref>。

其他一些媒体对节目的评价较为负面。《洛杉矶时报》电视评论员霍华德·罗森伯格（{{lang|en|Howard Rosenberg}}）感叹，戈德堡的幽默感“在这段幸好只有3小时18分钟的电视节目中枯萎了”，还称与比利·克里斯托相比，她的主持技巧太显“温和”<ref>{{cite news |last=Rosenberg |first=Howard |title=Calendar Goes to the Oscars : Whoopi, Better Naughty Than Boring : Awards: Goldberg fails to hit a funny bone or stir up any of the controversy that was hyped the week before the telecast. In fact, her hosting is balmy , particularly compared to predecessor Billy Crystal |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131022101725/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-03-22/entertainment/ca-37195_1_lorena-bobbitt-telecast-color-purple |archivedate=2013-10-22 |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-03-22/entertainment/ca-37195_1_lorena-bobbitt-telecast-color-purple |deadurl=no |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-03-22}}</ref>。《奥兰多哨兵报》（{{lang|en|''Orlando Sentinel''}}）影评人杰伊·博伊尔（{{lang|en|Jay Boyar}}）称：“今年奥斯卡颁奖典礼给人带来的兴奋感只比一个酒鬼得到自己最喜欢的葡萄酒时强那么一丁点儿。”<ref>{{cite news |last=Boyar |first=Jay |title=Oscar Show's Rating: Plenty Of Class, Not Much Excitement |archivedate=2014-01-01 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140101214020/http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1994-03-25/entertainment/9403240457_1_oscarcast-political-slogans-academy |url=http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1994-03-25/entertainment/9403240457_1_oscarcast-political-slogans-academy |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Orlando Sentinel |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-03-25}}</ref>。《盐湖城论坛报》（{{lang|en|''The Salt Lake City Tribune''}}）的哈罗德·辛德勒（{{lang|en|Harold Schindler}}）指出，主持人讲的笑话中大部分要“局内人”才能听懂，以至于台下观众都在窃窃私语，试图了解其中含意，而[[电视机|电视机]]前的大部分观众就难免会摸不着头脑了。他还补充表示，本场晚会让人最失望的地方在于，戈德堡在帮助观众理解节目内容方面一无是处<ref>{{cite news |last=Schindler |first=Harold |title=Spielberg shines at Oscars, but all that gliters isn't Goldberg |work=The Salt Lake City Tribune |publisher= MediaNews Group |date=1994-03-23}}</ref>。

=== 收视率和奖项 ===
本届颁奖典礼通过美国广播公司在美国直播，其播出时段里平均有4626万观众收看，与[[第65届奥斯卡金像奖|去年]]相比提高了1%<ref>{{cite news |last=Johnson |first=Greg |title=Call It the Glamour Bowl |archivedate=2013-10-22 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131022224101/http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Los Angeles Times |deadurl=no |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1999-03-18}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |first=Bill |last=Gorman |title=Academy Awards Averages 41.3 Million Viewers; Most Since 2005 |url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2010/03/08/academy-awards-averages-41-3-million-viewers-most-since-2005/44217/ | work=TV by the Numbers |publisher=Tribune Company | date=2010-03-08 | accessdate=2014-01-23 | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131101231748/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2010/03/08/academy-awards-averages-41-3-million-viewers-most-since-2005/44217/ | archivedate= 2013-11-01 | deadurl= no}}</ref>。不过根据尼尔森收视率调查的数据显示，本届晚会吸引了31.1%的美国[[家庭|家庭]]观看，不及前一年的31.2%<ref name="TVB Oscars">{{cite news|title=Academy Awards ratings |work=''Television Bureau of Advertising'' |format=PDF |url=http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |accessdate=2013-11-24 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016061652/http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |deadurl=yes |archivedate=2013-10-16 }}</ref>。同时在18至49岁年龄段观众群里的收视率为19.5%，也不及前一年的20.1%<ref name="TVB Oscars" />。

1994年7月，颁奖典礼获得了第46届[[黄金时段艾美奖|黄金时段艾美奖]]的7项提名<ref>{{cite news |title=Primetime Emmy Award database |url=http://www.emmys.com/shows/66th-annual-academy-awards | accessdate=2014-01-23 | publisher=Academy of Television Arts and Sciences |archivedate=2013-12-24 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131224113639/http://www.emmys.com/shows/66th-annual-academy-awards}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Emmy Award Nominations 1994 : Nighttime Nominees: A Complete Rundown |archivedate=2013-10-15 |deadurl=no |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-07-22/entertainment/ca-18748_1_nypd-blue |accessdate=2014-01-23 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131015032226/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-07-22/entertainment/ca-18748_1_nypd-blue |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-07-22}}</ref>，并在两个月后赢得了迷你剧或特别节目类杰出技术指导/镜头/视频奖<ref>{{cite news |title=The 46th Annual Emmy Awards: Who Won What: The List of Winners on TV's Annual Night of Nights |archivedate=2013-12-24 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131224092822/http://articles.latimes.com/1994-09-12/entertainment/ca-37696_1_nighttime-emmy-awards/2 |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-09-12/entertainment/ca-37696_1_nighttime-emmy-awards/2 |accessdate=2014-01-23 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1994-09-12}}</ref>。

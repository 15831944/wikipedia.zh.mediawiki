本条目是'''[[第90届奥斯卡金像奖|第90届奥斯卡金像奖]][[奥斯卡金像奖最佳外语片奖|最佳外语片奖]]'''的角逐名单。[[美国电影艺术与科学学会|美国电影艺术与科学学会]]自1956年设立该奖以来，每年都会邀请各国电影行业提交他们当年最出色的电影参加最佳外语片奖评选<ref name="HistPg2">{{cite web|title=History of the Academy Awards - Page 2 |publisher=[[Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts_and_Sciences|Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] |url=http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/history02.html |accessdate=2014-09-19 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080622191328/http://www.oscars.org/aboutacademyawards/history02.html |archivedate=2008-06-22 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>。该奖项每年由学院颁发主要包含非英语对白的非美国制作电影长片<ref name="HistPg2"/><ref name="Rules">{{cite web|title=Rule Thirteen: Special Rules for the Foreign Language Film Award |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |publisher=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/87/pdf/87aa_rules.pdf |accessdate=2014-09-19 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140827034453/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/87/pdf/87aa_rules.pdf |archivedate=2014-08-27 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。外语片奖委员会负责监督评选过程、审阅所有被提交的电影<ref name="Rules"/>。

所有被提交的電影必須於2016年10月1日到2017年9月30日期間在製片國家公映<ref name="OctSep">{{cite web|url=http://www.filmcontact.com/news/south-africa/call-oscars-foreign-language-film-category-submissions |title=Call for Oscars Foreign Language Film Category Submissions |work=Film Contact |date=2017-08-25 |accessdate=2017-09-07}}</ref>。

電影提交截至時間為2017年10月2日<ref name="HWdates">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/race/oscars-a-crowded-field-doc-foreign-contenders-has-begun-emerge-1019549 |title=Oscars: A Crowded Field of Doc and Foreign Contenders Has Begun to Emerge |work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |last=Feinberg |first=Scott |date=2017-07-14 |accessdate=2017-07-14}}</ref>，而學院于10月5日宣佈合資格的電影名單<ref name="list">{{cite web|url=https://www.oscars.org/news/92-countries-competition-2017-foreign-language-film-oscarr |title=92 Countries in Competition in for 2017 Foreign Language Film Oscar |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |date=2017-10-05 |accessdate=2017-10-05}}</ref>。一共有92個地區提交電影，其中有六個地區首次提交電影<ref name="THR">{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscars-92-films-submitted-foreign-language-film-academy-award-1046070 |title=Oscars: 92 Films Submitted in Foreign-Language Category |last=Kilday |first=Gregg |work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |date=2017-10-05 |accessdate=2017-10-05}}</ref>。其中包括[[海地|海地]]的《海地，我的愛》、[[宏都拉斯|宏都拉斯]]的《莫拉桑》<ref name="THR"/>、[[寮國|寮國]]的《鬼姐姐》<ref name="Laos1st">{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2017/film/asia/laos-dearest-sister-first-foreign-language-oscar-film-1202563352/ |title=Laos Selects ‘Dearest Sister’ as First Foreign-Language Oscar Submission |work=Variety |first=Patrick |last=Frater |date=2017-09-18 |accessdate=2017-09-19}}</ref>、[[莫三比克|莫三比克]]的《鹽糖火車》<ref name="Mozambique1st">{{cite web|url=https://filmpt.com/comboio-de-sal-e-acucar-e-o-candidato-de-mocambique-aos-oscares/ |title=“Comboio de Sal e Açucar” é o candidato de Moçambique aos Óscares |work=filmpt |date=2017-09-29 |accessdate=2017-09-29}}</ref>、[[塞內加爾|塞內加爾]]的《[[費莉絲蒂|費莉絲蒂]]》<ref name="THR"/>和[[敘利亞|敘利亞]]的《小甘地》<ref name="THR"/>。

學院將會稍後公佈一系列符合資格的電影。數十部電影將於2017年年底公佈九部入围的電影，而于2018年年初公佈五部最終候選作品名單。

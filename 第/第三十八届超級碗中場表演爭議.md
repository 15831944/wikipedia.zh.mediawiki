'''[[第三十八屆超級盃|第三十八屆超級盃]]'''的中場表演有不少爭議，因為[[珍妮·杰克逊|珍妮·杰克逊]]的上衣被[[贾斯汀·提姆布莱克|贾斯汀·提姆布莱克]]扯下，使珍妮使右乳通過轉播完全暴露了半秒鐘。與其他半場演出一起，它導致了對廣播中被視為猥褻的立即譴責和廣泛爭論。該事件還被稱為'''Nipplegate'''。<ref name="apologeticjackson">{{cite news|title=Janet Jackson Apologizes for Bared Breast |url=http://customwire.ap.org/dynamic/stories/S/SUPER_BOWL_JACKSON?SITE=MAHYC&SECTION=BUSINESS&TEMPLATE=DEFAULT|author=Nekesa Mumbi Moody|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]|date=2004-02-03|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20040203025228/http://customwire.ap.org/dynamic/stories/S/SUPER_BOWL_JACKSON?SITE=MAHYC&SECTION=BUSINESS&TEMPLATE=DEFAULT|archivedate=2004-02-03}} Also published by CNN.com as "[http://www.cnn.com/2004/US/02/02/superbowl.jackson/ Apologetic Jackson says 'costume reveal' went awry]".</ref>本事件在之後被收入2007年的《[[吉尼斯世界纪录大全|吉尼斯世界纪录大全]]》中称这一事件是'''互联网史上被搜索最多的'''以及'''被搜索最多的新闻事件'''。<ref>{{Citation | title = Star-studded 2007 edition of Guinness World Records released | publisher=[[CBC_News|CBC News]] | date = 2006-09-29 | url = http://www.cbc.ca/arts/story/2006/09/29/guinness-record-book.html | accessdate = 2006-09-30}}</ref>

該次事件還創造了“[[衣服滑落|衣服滑落]]”這個詞，後來被添加到[[韦氏词典|韦氏词典]]中。<ref name="dictionary">{{cite news|title='Credit crunch', 'wardrobe malfunction' squeeze into dictionary|url=http://afp.google.com/article/ALeqM5hHgjjv3FP-QbjXUIzpzulUn_UAAg|agency=[[Agence_France-Presse|Agence France-Presse]]|date=August 14, 2008|accessdate=June 18, 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090427165939/http://afp.google.com/article/ALeqM5hHgjjv3FP-QbjXUIzpzulUn_UAAg|archivedate=April 27, 2009|df=}}</ref>

該事件使珍妮被MTV和Infinity Broadcasting在全球許多廣播格式和音樂頻道上將她的[[單曲|單曲]]和[[音樂錄影帶|音樂錄影帶]]列入[[黑名單|黑名單]]。<ref name="blender">{{cite book|title=America's Most Wanted|author=Robert Tannenbaum|work=[[Blender_Magazine|Blender Magazine]]|page=128|year=2004}}</ref>但是兩人並未被禁止參與中場表演。<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/8007033/janet-jackson-not-banned-super-bowl-halftime-NFL-timberlake |title=NFL: Janet Jackson Not Banned From Super Bowl Halftime Show, 'No Clue' Who Justin Timberlake Might Invite |date=October 23, 2017 |work=Billboard}}</ref> 提姆布萊克後來於2018年2月4日在[[第五十二屆超級碗|第52屆超級碗]]上演出。

== 過程 ==
[[珍妮·杰克逊|珍妮·杰克逊]]與[[贾斯汀·提姆布莱克|贾斯汀·提姆布莱克]]受邀在[[超级盃|超级盃]]半场休息时表演，在她与歌手[[贾斯汀·提姆布莱克|贾斯汀·提姆布莱克]]一同表演了后者的歌曲《{{tsl|en|Rock Your Body|Rock Your Body}}》。当贾斯汀唱完歌中的歌词“最后让你脱光光（gonna have you naked by the end of this song）”后，他扯掉了珍妮的上衣，使其右乳通过卫星转播在将近9000万观众面前完全暴露（虽然[[乳頭|乳頭]]有被一个金黃色圓狀的[[乳貼|乳貼]]遮住而沒有暴露）了0.8秒。<ref>[http://tvbythenumbers.com/2009/01/18/historical-super-bowl-tv-ratings/11044 TVbythenumbers.com]</ref>表演结束后珍妮立即向大众道歉，并声称这只是一次事故而非刻意安排，原先预想的舞台效果只是贾斯汀撕掉外侧的皮质紧身衣，而留红色的胸罩在身上。<ref>{{Citation | title = Apologetic Jackson says 'costume reveal' went awry |publisher=CNN | date = 2004-02-03 | url = http://www.cnn.com/2004/US/02/02/superbowl.jackson/ | accessdate = 2006-05-20}}</ref>她还表示：“如果有冒犯到任何人，我真心的表示歉意。但这真的不是我的本意……MTV、CBS以及[[国家橄榄球联合会|国家橄榄球联盟]]都不曾预计到会发生什么，但是不幸的是，最后还是出现了差错。”<ref>{{Citation | title = Jackson's apology can't stem mass anger | publisher=[[ESPN|ESPN]] | url = http://sports.espn.go.com/nfl/playoffs03/news/story?id=1724968&partnersite=espn | accessdate = 2008-03-09}}</ref>

傑克遜的公關人員表示，該段表演只是為了揭示傑克遜的紅色蕾絲內衣，但結果是：蕾絲內衣與外層衣物一起被意外扯下。<ref name=cogin>{{cite web|title=In the beginning, there was a nipple|url=http://espn.go.com/espn/feature/story/_/id/10333439/wardrobe-malfunction-beginning-there-was-nipple|author=Marin Cogan|work=[[ESPN_the_Magazine|ESPN the Magazine]]|date=January 28, 2014|accessdate=January 30, 2014}}</ref><ref name=ew>{{cite news|title=Fallout|url=http://www.ew.com/ew/article/0,,586683,00.html|author=Gary Susman|work=Entertainment Weekly|date=February 3, 2004|accessdate=October 3, 2013}}</ref>

美国联邦通信委会最後以本事件向CBS开出了一张55万美元的巨额罚单。<ref>{{Citation |last=Davidson |first=Paul | title=FCC loses appeal of 'wardrobe malfunction' fine | newspaper=USA Today | page=2b| date=2008-07-22|issn=0734-7456}}</ref>CBS對此案上訴但在2012年被[[美国联邦第三巡回上诉法院|美国联邦第三巡回上诉法院]]駁回。<ref name="arstechnica2011-11-02">{{cite web|title=Court rules Janet Jackson nipplegate fine was unfair to CBS|url=https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/news/2011/11/court-rules-nipplegate-fine-was-unfair-to-cbs.ars|author=Timothy B. == 影響 ==
=== 2004年總統大選 ===

=== 對傑克遜 ===
ABC电视台主演传奇女歌手、民权主义斗士[[莲纳·荷恩|莲纳·荷恩]]的电视传记影片的计划也和珍妮終止合約。當時荷恩對其露乳事件非常生氣，並邀ABC电视台找其他演员来饰演自己。<ref>{{Citation | title = Horne: Janet Jackson, don't play me | agency = Associated Press | publisher = CNN | date = 2004-02-04 | url = http://www.cnn.com/2004/SHOWBIZ/TV/02/24/tv.janetjackson.ap/ | accessdate = 2008-04-03 | format = – <sup>[http://scholar.google.co.uk/scholar?hl=en&lr=%20&q=intitle%3AHorne%3A+Janet+Jackson%2C+don%27t+play+me&as_publication=%20&as_ylo=%20&as_yhi=%20&btnG=Search Scholar search]</sup> | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20050220135401/http://www.cnn.com/2004/SHOWBIZ/TV/02/24/tv.janetjackson.ap/ | archivedate = 2005年2月20日 | deadurl = yes }}</ref>

== 後世影響 ==
第三十八届超級碗中場表演事件在當時被認為是[[流行文化|流行文化]]中最具爭議的電視和媒體事件，傑克遜被認為是世界上“最有爭議的音樂家”，由於事件對審查的巨大影響而“改變了文化景觀”和電視和娛樂[[廣播|廣播]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=Jackson Turns 38|url=http://www.castanet.net/news/Entertainment/1498/Jackson-Turns-38|publisher=Castanet.net|date=May 16, 2004|accessdate=September 30, 2013}}</ref>

[[YouTube|YouTube]]聯合創辦人[[賈德·卡林姆|賈德·卡林姆]]稱，他無法輕易找到事件的視頻片段而感到沮喪，這為YouTube和後來的Vevo創作提供了靈感。<ref>{{cite web|title=Readers' Poll: The Greatest Super Bowl Halftime Shows|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/pictures/readers-poll-the-greatest-super-bowl-halftime-shows-20130130/9-janet-jackson-0629268|work=Rolling Stone|date=January 30, 2013|accessdate=April 1, 2014}}</ref>  在事件發生三天后，[[Facebook|Facebook]]的推出開始，可能試圖通過社交網絡利用其宣傳。<ref name=cogin>{{cite web|title=In the beginning, there was a nipple|url=http://espn.go.com/espn/feature/story/_/id/10333439/wardrobe-malfunction-beginning-there-was-nipple|author=Marin Cogan|work=[[ESPN_the_Magazine|ESPN the Magazine]]|date=January 28, 2014|accessdate=January 30, 2014}}</ref>

這一事件也被宣佈為「歷史上最令人難忘的超級碗中場表演」，以及「最具爭議性的」，還補充說「你不應該談論這場中場表演，或任何後來的中場表演從這裡到永恆，沒有提到衣服滑落」。<ref>{{cite web|title=Anderson Silva and the Top 10 Jaw Dropping Moments of the Last Decade|url=http://bleacherreport.com/articles/432589-decades-top-ten-most-jaw-dropping-moments-in-sports/page/11|work=Bleacher Report|date=October 8, 2010|accessdate=January 21, 2014}}</ref><ref name=memorable>{{cite web|title=NFL: The 10 Most Memorable Halftime Shows in History|url=http://bleacherreport.com/articles/695831-the-top-10-most-memorable-halftime-shows-in-nfl-history/page/11|work=Bleacher Report|date=October 5, 2011|accessdate=September 30, 2013}}</ref>

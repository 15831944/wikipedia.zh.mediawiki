'''第63屆柏林電影節'''于2013年2月7日至2月17日在[[柏林|柏林]]举行<ref name="Berlin63">{{Cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/HomePage.html |title=Berlinale |accessdate=2012-12-13|work=Berlinale}}</ref>，香港导演[[王家卫|王家卫]]任主竞赛评审团主席<ref name="Wong Kar Wai">{{Cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/presse/pressemitteilungen/alle/Alle-Detail_17172.html|title=BERLINALE 2013: INTERNATIONAL JURY |accessdate=2013-02-17 |work=Berlinale}}</ref>，他执导的作品《[[一代宗师|一代宗师]]》为影展的开幕片<ref name="Grandmaster">{{Cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/presse/pressemitteilungen/alle/Alle-Detail_16084.html |title=Wong Kar Wai's The Grandmaster to open 63rd Berlinale |accessdate=2012-12-19|work=Berlinale}}</ref><ref name="reuters">{{Cite web|url=http://uk.reuters.com/article/2013/02/07/uk-berlin-idUKBRE91600120130207 |title=Martial arts drama kicks off Berlin film festival |accessdate=2013-02-07 |work=reuters}}</ref>。

[[罗马尼亚|罗马尼亚]]导演[[卡林·皮特·内策尔|卡林·皮特·内策尔]]执导的电影《[[孩童之姿|孩童之姿]]》夺得[[金熊奖|金熊奖]]<ref name="BerlinPrizes">{{Cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/das_festival/preise_und_juries/preise_internationale_jury/index.html |title=Prizes of the International Jury |accessdate=2013-02-16 |work=berlinale}}</ref><ref name="BBCBerlin">{{Cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-21485735 |title=Romanian film Child's Pose wins Golden Bear in Berlin |accessdate=2013-02-16 |work=BBC News}}</ref>。[[法国|法国]][[纪录片|纪录片]]制作人[[克劳德·朗兹曼|克劳德·朗兹曼]]被授予终身成就荣誉金熊奖<ref name="Lanzmann">{{Cite web|url=http://www.screendaily.com/news/claude-lanzmann-to-receive-berlins-honorary-golden-bear/5049510.article |title=Claude Lanzmann to receive Berlin’s Honorary Golden Bear |accessdate=2012-12-13 |work=screendaily}}</ref>。

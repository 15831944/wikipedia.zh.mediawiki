'''第65届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1992年电影|1992年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1993年3月29日下午18点（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上21点）在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的多萝茜·钱德勒大厅举行，共计颁发了23个类别的[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）。晚会通过[[美国广播公司|美国广播公司]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播，由吉尔·凯茨担任[[制作人|制作人]]，路易斯·{{lang|en|J}}·霍维茨执导<ref>{{cite news | last = Marx | first = Andy | title = 4th Oscarcast for Cates | url = http://variety.com/1992/film/awards/4th-oscarcast-for-cates-100501/ | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151010030129/https://variety.com/1992/film/awards/4th-oscarcast-for-cates-100501/ | work = [[综艺_(杂志)|Variety]] | publisher = Penske Media Corporation | date = 1992-11-11 | archivedate = 2015-10-10 | deadurl = no}}</ref><ref name="Osborne-2013">{{Cite book | last = Osborne | first = Robert | title = 85 Years of the Oscar: The Complete History of the Academy Awards | year = 2013 | isbn = 0-7892-1142-4 | location = New York, United States | publisher = Abbeville Publishing Group | ref = harv | postscript = .}}</ref>{{rp|418}}。男演员[[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]连续第4年担任主持<ref>{{cite news | last = MacMinn | first = Aleene | title = Morning Report: Movies | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151016175816/http://articles.latimes.com/1993-02-10/entertainment/ca-1151_1_major-movie | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Austin Beutner | date = 1993-02-10 | archivedate = 2015-10-16 | deadurl = no | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1993-02-10/entertainment/ca-1151_1_major-movie}}</ref>。两个多星期前的3月6日，女演员[[莎朗·斯通|莎朗·斯通]]在洛杉矶的[[世纪广场凯悦丽晶酒店|世纪广场酒店]]主持颁发[[奥斯卡科技成果奖|奥斯卡科技成果奖]]<ref>{{cite news | title = Past Scientific & Technical Awards Ceremonies | url = http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | publisher = Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS) | archivedate = 2014-10-07 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141007214118/http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html | deadurl = yes}}</ref>。

[[克林特·伊斯特伍德|克林特·伊斯特伍德]]执导的《[[不可饶恕|不可饶恕]]》获得包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]、[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|导演]]在内的4项大奖，是这个夜晚的最大赢家，还为[[吉恩·哈克曼|吉恩·哈克曼]]赢得[[奥斯卡最佳男配角奖|男配角奖]]。[[艾尔·帕西诺|艾尔·帕西诺]]和[[艾玛·汤普森|艾玛·汤普森]]分别因《[[闻香识女人|闻香识女人]]》和《[[霍华德庄园|霍华德庄园]]》获[[奥斯卡最佳男主角奖|男]]、[[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|女主角奖]]。[[玛丽莎·托梅|玛丽莎·托梅]]凭《[[我的表兄维尼|我的表兄维尼]]》（{{lang|en|''My Cousin Vinny''}}）拿下[[奥斯卡最佳女配角奖|女配角奖]]。<ref>{{cite news | last = Fox | first = David J. | title = 'Unforgiven' Top Film; Pacino, Thompson Win : Academy Awards: Eastwood named best director. Oscars for supporting roles go to Hackman and Tomei. | publisher = Austin Beutner | date = 1993-03-31 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150419104850/http://articles.latimes.com/1993-03-30/news/mn-16785_1_film-academy | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1993-03-30/news/mn-16785_1_film-academy | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | work = [[洛杉矶时报|Los Angeles Times]] | archivedate = 2015-04-19 | deadurl = no}}</ref>晚会的电视直播平均吸引了约4,570万美国观众收看<ref name="Glamour Bowl">{{cite news | last = Johnson | first = Greg | title = Call It the Glamour Bowl | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141229085722/http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 | archivedate = 2014-12-29 | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Austin Beutner | date = 1999-03-18}}</ref>。

== 获奖和提名 ==
1993年2月17日，[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]主席罗伯特·莱姆（{{lang|en|Robert Rehme}}）和女演员[[梅塞迪丝·鲁尔|梅塞迪丝·鲁尔]]一起在[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布第65届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单<ref>{{cite news | last = Weinraub | first = Bernard | title = 3 Films Dominate Nominees In Oscar Contest | url = http://www.nytimes.com/1993/02/18/movies/3-films-dominate-nominees-in-oscar-contest.html | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150620004322/http://www.nytimes.com/1993/02/18/movies/3-films-dominate-nominees-in-oscar-contest.html | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | work = [[纽约时报|The New York Times]] | publisher = Arthur Ochs Sulzberger, Jr. | date = 1993-02-18 | archivedate = 2015-06-20}}</ref>。《[[霍华德庄园|霍华德庄园]]》和《[[不可饶恕|不可饶恕]]》均以9项提名领跑<ref>{{cite news | last = Fox | first = David J. | title = The 65th Academy Award Nominations: The Declaration of Independents : The nominations: 'Howards End' and 'Unforgiven' get nine apiece, 'The Crying Game' six. Non-studio and maverick filmmakers have a field day. | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | work = Los Angeles Times | deadurl = no | date = 1993-02-18 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150620002426/http://articles.latimes.com/1993-02-18/entertainment/ca-463_1_howards-end | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1993-02-18/entertainment/ca-463_1_howards-end | publisher = Austin Beutner | archivedate = 2015-06-20}}</ref>。

获奖名单在1993年3月29日举行的颁奖典礼上公布。获导演奖的[[克林特·伊斯特伍德|克林特·伊斯特伍德]]成为历史上第7个因同一部电影获导演和主演奖提名的电影人<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Ebert | first1 = Roger | title = Oscars Honor Period Pieces But `Player,' `Malcolm X' Passed Over: | work = Chicago Sun-Times | publisher = Tim Knight | date = 1993-02-18 | page = 37}}</ref>。男主角奖得主[[艾尔·帕西诺|艾尔·帕西诺]]是历史上第6位[[一年获两项奥斯卡金像奖提名演员列表|在一年里同时获主角和配角奖提名的演员]]<ref>{{cite news | last = Rea | first = Steven | title = In Line For Oscars "Howards End" And Clint Eastwood's "Unforgiven" Got Nine Academy Award Nominations Each. And Makers Of "The Crying Game" May Get The Last Laugh, With Six Shots At The Statuette. | publisher = H.F. Gerry Lenfest | date = 1993-02-18 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20151006152339/http://articles.philly.com/1993-02-18/entertainment/25955125_1_academy-award-nominations-unforgiven-mary-mcdonnell | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | work = [[费城询问报|The Philadelphia Inquirer]] | url = http://articles.philly.com/1993-02-18/entertainment/25955125_1_academy-award-nominations-unforgiven-mary-mcdonnell | archivedate = 2015-10-06}}</ref>，也是首位同时获主、配角奖提名后赢得主角奖的演员<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|1173}}。[[亚伦·孟肯|亚伦·孟肯]]在两个音乐类奖项中胜出，成为历史上第3个连续两年获奖的电影人<ref name="Osborne-2013" />{{rp|424}}。

== 典礼资讯 ==
由于[[第64届奥斯卡金像奖|上届颁奖典礼]]获得成功，得到评论界好评的同时还获得多项[[艾美奖|艾美奖]]，美国电影艺术与科学学院因此再度聘请吉尔·凯茨（{{lang|en|Gil Cates}}）前来连续第4次为晚会操刀<ref>{{cite news | title = Cates to Repeat As Oscars Producer | work = San Francisco Chronicle | publisher = Hearst Corporation | date = 1992-11-13 | page = C2}}</ref>。1993年2月，凯茨决定请男演员兼笑匠[[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]连续第4度担任主持人<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Williams | first1 = Jeannie | title = Bily Crystal, back as Mr. Oscar night | work = [[今日美国|USA Today]] | publisher = Gannett Company | date = 1993-02-05 | page = 2D}}</ref>。凯茨对此表示：“他是大牌电影明星，有单独掌控这个娱乐之夜的能力”<ref>{{cite news | title = 'Perfect host' appointed | work = The Globe and Mail | publisher = Phillip Crawley | date = 1993-02-06 | page = C6}}</ref>。[[阿米·阿彻德|阿米·阿彻德]]（{{lang|en|Army Archerd}}）在《[[综艺_(杂志)|综艺]]》杂志发文称，克里斯托起初因忙于拍摄电影《[[周末夜先生|周末夜先生]]》（{{lang|en|''Mr. Saturday Night''}}）和《[[城市滑头2|城市滑头2]]》（{{lang|en|''City Slickers II: The Legend of Curly's Gold''}}）而谢绝邀请<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Williams | first1 = Jeannie | title = Roping Crystal into Oscar duty | work = USA Today | publisher = Gannett Company | date = 1993-02-18 | page = 2D}}</ref>，凯茨于是给他寄去一个葬礼花圈，上面附有诗文，称“没有你，节目和我都死定了”，再加上一颗仿制的死马头（类似电影《[[教父_(电影)|教父]]》），克里斯托于是改变了主意<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Archerd | first1 = Army | title = Cates ‘convinces’ Crystal to m.c. Oscars again | url = http://variety.com/1993/voices/columns/cates-convinces-crystal-to-m-c-oscars-again-1117862127/ | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150112143742/http://variety.com/1993/voices/columns/cates-convinces-crystal-to-m-c-oscars-again-1117862127/ | accessdate = 2015-11-18 | work = Variety | publisher = Penske Media Corporation | date = 1993-02-16 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2015-01-12}}</ref>。

与之前几届颁奖典礼一样，凯茨也让节目围绕一个主题展开。受多位女性当选[[美国参议院|联邦参议员]]的[[女性年|女性年]]启发，凯茨将1993年颁奖典礼的主题定为“祝贺女性和电影的奥斯卡”<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|872}}。为了切合这一主题，艺术与科学学院请来各个奖项的共计67名女性获奖者一起拍摄照片，在晚会开始时展示<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|875}}。女演员兼歌手[[丽莎·明奈利|丽莎·明奈利]]现场演唱《{{lang|en|Ladies' Day}}》，这首歌是由[[弗雷德·埃伯|弗雷德·埃伯]]（{{lang|en|Fred Ebb}}）和[[约翰·坎德|约翰·坎德]]（{{lang|en|John Kander}}）专门为本届晚会创作<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|886}}。曾获奥斯卡奖的纪录片电影人林恩·利特曼（{{lang|en|Lynne Littman}}）制作了一段蒙太奇，凸显电影中的女性<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|880}}。

另外还有多人参与颁奖典礼的制作。比尔·康堤（{{lang|en|Bill Conti}}）担任晚会音乐总监和管弦乐队指挥<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscar watch | url = http://variety.com/1993/film/news/oscar-watch-7-102639/ | accessdate = 2015-11-18 | work = Variety | publisher = Penske Media Corporation | date = 1993-01-05}}</ref>。编舞家[[黛比·艾伦|黛比·艾伦]]（{{lang|en|Debbie Allen}}）督导原创歌曲奖提名作品的表演和《{{lang|en|Ladies' Night}}》曲目演出<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscar Dance Tryouts Sunday | url = http://variety.com/1993/film/news/oscar-dance-tryouts-sunday-104202/ | accessdate = 2015-11-18 | work = Varierty | publisher = Penske Media Corporation | date = 1993-02-22}}</ref>。配音演员兰迪·托马斯（{{lang|en|Randy Thomas}}）成为晚会电视转播的报幕员，是史上首位女性奥斯卡颁奖典礼报幕员<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|879}}。

=== 专业评价 ===
本次颁奖典礼所获评价不佳，大部分媒体看法倾向负面。[[美联社|美联社]]电视评论员[[弗雷泽·摩尔|弗雷泽·摩尔]]（{{lang|en|Frazier Moore}}）感叹克里斯托“看起来没精打采得不可思议”。他还质疑所谓的“女性年”主题，因为“本届奥斯卡颁奖典礼本身似乎就违背了它的女权主题。”<ref>{{cite news | last = Moore | first = Frazier | title = Billy Crystal's Performance Lame | work = The Daily Gazette | publisher = John DeAugustine | date = 1993-03-30 | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | url = https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1957&dat=19930330&id=Bg0xAAAAIBAJ&sjid=IOEFAAAAIBAJ&pg=3345,7229336&hl=zh-CN}}</ref>罗伯特·比安科（{{lang|en|Robert Bianco}}）在《[[匹兹堡邮报|匹兹堡邮报]]》（{{lang|en|''Pittsburgh Post-Gazette''}}）撰文嘲笑黛比·艾伦编导的音乐曲目就像1989年[[第61届奥斯卡金像奖|第61届颁奖典礼]]灾难性的开场曲目一样<ref>{{cite news | last = Bianco | first = Robert | title = Crystal Can't Save Disastrous Oscars Show | url = https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1129&dat=19930330&id=xNdRAAAAIBAJ&sjid=Sm4DAAAAIBAJ&pg=6977,6838257&hl=zh-CN | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | work = Pittsburgh Post-Gazette | publisher = John Robinson Block | date = 1993-03-30}}</ref>。《[[今日美国|今日美国]]》专栏作家马特·劳什（{{lang|en|Matt Roush}}）则批评克里斯托经过多年主持却没有长进，套路在老调重弹之下变得无趣，多次提及电影《周末夜先生》之举令人感觉仿佛业余人士自作聪明<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|892}}。

多位女权主义公众人物对晚会同样评价不佳。作家兼社会活动家[[贝蒂·傅瑞丹|贝蒂·傅瑞丹]]在接受《[[洛杉矶每日新闻|洛杉矶每日新闻]]》（{{lang|en|''Los Angeles Daily News''}}）采访时谴责颁奖典礼的“女性年”主题，称该主题根本“没有现实基础”，在她看来，这样的主题对于女性导演、摄影师和制片人来说与其说是致敬，倒不如说是令人憎恶的嘲讽<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|893}}。[[全美女性组织|全美女性组织]]洛杉矶分会主席[[塔米·布鲁斯|塔米·布鲁斯]]（{{lang|en|Tammy Bruce}}）也有类似看法，她批评晚会的女权主义主题是“我所见过最虚伪、最傲慢的东西之一”<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Karlak | first1 = Pat | last2 = Swertlow | first2 = Frank | title = Hollywood's Hollow Salute Oscars' 'Year of Woman' Patronizing, Many Say | work = The Plain Dealer | publisher = Advance Publications | page = 3C}}</ref>。面对这些指责，吉尔·凯茨表示：“在我看来，这一主题及其提升意识的方式都是积极向上的，对所有的普通民众如此，对单独的女性也是如此”<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|893}}。他还引用古老的[[历史上的中国|中国]]谚语称：“与其诅咒黑暗，不如燃起蜡烛”，这句话因前[[美国第一夫人|第一夫人]][[埃莉诺·罗斯福|埃莉诺·罗斯福]]而闻名<ref name="Osborne-2013" />{{rp|313}}。

=== 收视率 ===
虽然评价不佳，但本届颁奖典礼经美国广播公司在美国直播期间还是平均吸引到4570万观众收看，比[[第64届奥斯卡金像奖|上届颁奖典礼]]还多3%<ref name="Glamour Bowl" />。从尼尔森收视率调查的数据看，节目的表现也有提升，吸收了31.2%的家庭观众，收视率超过51%<ref>{{cite news|last1=Schwed |first1=Mark |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20151116093712/http://variety.com/1993/tv/news/kudocast-s-nielsen-ratings-highest-in-10-years-105508/ |url=http://variety.com/1993/tv/news/kudocast-s-nielsen-ratings-highest-in-10-years-105508/ |accessdate=2015-11-18 |work=Variety |publisher=Penske Media Corporation |title=Kudocast’s Nielsen ratings highest in 10 years |date=1993-03-30 |archivedate=2015-11-16 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last1 = Carter | first1 = Bill | title = TV Notes;Oscar Numbers Slip | url = http://www.nytimes.com/1996/03/27/movies/tv-notes-oscar-numbers-slip.html | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150526173756/http://www.nytimes.com/1996/03/27/movies/tv-notes-oscar-numbers-slip.html | accessdate = 2015-11-16 | work = The New York Times | publisher = Arthur Ochs Sulzberger, Jr. | date = 1996-03-27 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2015-05-26}}</ref>。此外，节目在18至49岁年龄段观众中的收视率为20.1<ref name="TVB Oscars">{{cite news|title=Academy Awards ratings |publisher=Television Bureau of Advertising |format=PDF |url=http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |accessdate=2015-11-18 |archivedate=2015-09-05 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150905061007/http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。

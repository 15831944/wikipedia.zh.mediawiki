'''第54屆[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]'''於2001年5月14日至5月25日在[[法國|法國]][[坎城|坎城]]舉辦，開幕片為[[巴茲·雷曼|巴茲·魯曼]]執導的《[[情陷紅磨坊|紅磨坊]]》，最高榮譽頒給了義大利導演[[南尼·莫莱蒂|南尼·莫瑞提]]執導的《[[人間有情天|人間有情天]]》。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/2001_cannes_film_festival_competition_lineup|title=2001 Cannes Film Festival Competition Lineup|accessdate=11 December 2013|work=Deadline}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/2001_cannes_film_festival_un_certain_regard_lineup|title=2001 Cannes Film Festival Un Certain Regard Lineup|accessdate=11 December 2013|work=Deadline}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/film/2001/mar/21/cannes2001.cannesfilmfestival|title=Moulin Rouge confirmed as Cannes opener|accessdate=11 December 2013|work=Deadline}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.urbancinefile.com.au/home/view.asp?a=4837&s=Features|title=CANNES 2001 - THE FESTIVAL FILMS|accessdate=11 December 2013|work=Deadline}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/cannes_2001_more_cannes_deals_newport_lineup_method_fest_plans_and_the_cast|title=CANNES 2001: More Cannes Deals; Newport Lineup; Method Fest Plans; and the Castro Renovation|accessdate=11 December 2013|work=Deadline}}</ref>

'''第二次冷战'''，也稱為'''冷战 2.0'''（英語：Second Cold War、Cold War II）<ref>{{cite web |url=http://foreignpolicy.com/2014/03/04/welcome-to-cold-war-ii/ |title=欢迎进入第二次冷战|author=德米特里·特列宁|date=2014年4月4日|website=Foreign Policy |publisher=Graham Holdings |accessdate=2015年12月4日}}</ref><ref>[http://www.huffingtonpost.com/nikolas-kozloff/as-cold-war-ii-looms-wash_b_8307470.html As Cold War II Looms, Washington Courts Nationalist, Rightwing, Catholic, Xenophobic Poland], ''[[赫芬頓郵報|赫芬頓郵報]]'', 15 October 2015.</ref><ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/politics/elections/2016/2015/10/03/rubio-us-barreling-toward-second-cold-war/73288022/ |title= Rubio: U.S. 'barreling toward a second Cold War' |first= Ryan |last= Mackenzie |newspaper= The Des Moines Register |date= 3 October 2015 |agency= USA Today |accessdate= 28 January 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.themoscowtimes.com/opinion/article/who-will-win-the-new-cold-war/518322.html |title= Who Will Win the New Cold War? |first= George |last= Bovt |date= 31 March 2015 |newspaper= The Moscow Times |accessdate= 28 January 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.theguardian.com/commentisfree/2014/mar/02/crimea-crisis-russia-ukraine-cold-war |title= The crisis in Crimea could lead the world into a second cold war |first= Dmitri |last= Trenin |newspaper= The Guardian |date= 2 March 2014 |accessdate= 28 January 2016}}</ref>亦称'''新冷战'''<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/nov/19/new-cold-war-back-to-bad-old-days-russia-west-putin-ukraine |title=The new cold war: are we going back to the bad old days? |author=Simon Tisdall |date=19 November 2014 |website=The Guardian |publisher=Guardian News and Media Limited |accessdate=4 February 2015}}</ref>和'''冷战2.0'''<ref>{{cite web |url=http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2014/09/140912-cold-war-geography-russia-ukraine-sanctions/ |title=Is the Cold War Back? |author=Eve Conant |date=12 September 2014 |website=National Geographic |publisher=National Geographic Society |accessdate=4 February 2015}}</ref>，自2010年代起開始發生，指的是在对立的地缘政治集团之间再度发生的政治与军事紧张局势，第二次冷战由[[美国|美国]]领导的[[西方世界|西方]]世界，与[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]]和[[中华人民共和国|中国]]领导的[[東方集團|東方集團]]的对峙。由于[[2014年克里米亞危機|2014年克里米亞危機]]后，[[烏克蘭|烏克蘭]]加入西方陣營，导致[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]]占領[[克里米亞|克里米亞]]，美俄关系及俄欧关系的紧张，以及[[南海争端|南海争端]]和[[2018年美中貿易戰|2018年美中貿易戰]]爆發，导致中美关系紧张，致使第二次冷战即将来临。

自1991年[[冷战|冷战]]結束以來，一直維持[[美國|美國]]一強獨大的現象。新冷戰的出現，普遍認為一方是[[美国|美国]]、[[加拿大|加拿大]]、[[英国|英国]]、[[欧盟|欧盟]]及[[北大西洋公约组织|北大西洋公约组织]]、[[以色列|以色列]]、[[澳洲|澳洲]]、[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]]、[[日本|日本]]领导的[[西方|西方]]陣營，另一方领导者通常被认为是[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]]及[[中华人民共和国|中国]]<ref>{{cite web|last1=Powell|first1=Bill|title=A New Cold War, Yes. But It's With China, Not Russia|url=http://europe.newsweek.com/us-china-cold-war-327551?rm=eu|website=Newsweek.com|publisher=Newsweek|accessdate=16 April 2016}}</ref>，體現在區域之間的競爭。

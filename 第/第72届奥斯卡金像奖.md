'''第72届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1999年电影|1999年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]2000年3月26日下午17点30分（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上20点30分）在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的神殿礼堂举行，共计颁奖了23座奥斯卡金像奖（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]），[[男演员|男演员]][[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]第7次担任[[主持人|主持人]]。他于1990年举行的[[第62届奥斯卡金像奖|第62届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]上首次主持，上一次则是1998年的[[第70屆奧斯卡金像獎|第70届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]。本届颁奖典礼由[[美国广播公司|ABC]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播，在[[北美|北美]]地区共计吸引了约4700万观众收看。三个星期前的3月4日，[[莎瑪·希恩|莎瑪·希恩]]在[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亞州)|比佛利山]]的[[比佛利山威尔希尔酒店|威尔希尔丽晶酒店]]主持颁发了奥斯卡科技成果奖<ref>{{cite news |title=Oscar Watch: Hayek to present Scientific and Technical Awards |url=http://variety.com/2000/film/news/oscar-watch-154-1117776404/ |accessdate=2013-11-20 |work=Variety |publisher=PMC |date=2000-02-13 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131023142717/http://variety.com/2000/film/news/oscar-watch-154-1117776404/ |archivedate=2013-10-23 |deadurl=no}}</ref>。

《美国佳人》赢得了包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]、[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|导演]]、[[奥斯卡最佳男主角奖|男主角]]奖在内的五项大奖<ref>{{cite news |last=Lyman |first=Rick |title='American Beauty' Tops the Oscars; Main Acting Awards Go to Kevin Spacey and Hilary Swank |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2000/03/27/movies/american-beauty-tops-oscars-main-acting-awards-go-kevin-spacey-hilary-swank.html?pagewanted=all&src=pm |date=2000-03-27 |accessdate=2013-10-14 |work=The New York Times |publisher=The New York Times Company |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131016233100/http://www.nytimes.com/2000/03/27/movies/american-beauty-tops-oscars-main-acting-awards-go-kevin-spacey-hilary-swank.html?pagewanted=all&src=pm}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Brooks |first=Xan |title=Oscar ceremony sticks to the script |url=http://www.theguardian.com/culture/2000/mar/27/awardsandprizes.oscars20001 |accessdate=2013-10-14 |work=The Daily Telegraph |publisher=Telegraph Media Group |date=2000-03-27 |archivedate=2009-06-25 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090625061641/http://www.guardian.co.uk/culture/2000/mar/27/awardsandprizes.oscars20001 }}</ref>。其它获奖电影包括获奖四项的《[[黑客帝国|黑客帝国]]》，获奖两项的《[[苹果酒屋法则_(电影)|苹果酒屋法则]]》和《{{link-en|酣歌畅戏|Topsy-Turvy}}》，获奖一项的《[[关于我的母亲|关于我的母亲]]》、《[[男孩別哭|男孩別哭]]》、《移魂女郎》、《{{link-en|残疾画家|King Gimp}}》、《{{link-en|撒旦在纽约|My Mother Dreams the Satan's Disciples in New York}}》、《{{link-en|老人与海 (动画短片)|The Old Man and the Sea (1999 film)|老人与海}}》、《{{link-en|九月的某一天|One Day in September}}》、《{{link-en|红色小提琴|The Red Violin}}》、《[[斷頭谷|斷頭谷]]》和《[[泰山_(电影)|泰山]]》。

== 典礼资讯 ==
[[File:Billy_Crystal_VF_2012_Shankbone.JPG|thumb]]
新千年到来之际，美国电影艺术与科学学院希望缩短颁奖典礼的时长，并且给节目新的特色<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|279, 224}}。[[制片人|制片人]]夫妇[[理查·賽納克|理查·賽納克]]和莉莉·菲妮·扎努克获聘监督2000年晚会的制作<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|279}}，学院主席罗伯特·莱姆表示，请来两人是为了给节目带来全新的视角<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|279}}。这也是历史上首次有女性担任奥斯卡颁奖典礼的制片人<ref>{{cite news |last=Snow |first=Shauna|title=Arts And Entertainment Reports From The Times, News Services And The Nation's Press |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1999/dec/11/entertainment/ca-42694 |accessdate=2013-11-19 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=1999-12-11 |archivedate=2013-10-23 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131023034037/http://articles.latimes.com/1999/dec/11/entertainment/ca-42694}}</ref>。不过理查和莉莉虽保证会作出改变，但还是请来男演员兼老牌奥斯卡主持人比利·克里斯托主持颁奖典礼，这已经是后者第7度主持，他的首次主持是1990年举行的[[第62届奥斯卡颁奖典礼|第62届奥斯卡颁奖典礼]]<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|279}}。

报道称，本届颁奖典礼的制作远较往届奢华和雄心勃勃<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|225}}。艺术指导设计师鲍勃·基恩（{{lang|en|Bob Keene}}）设计了一个尽显其野心的科技舞台用于电视转播，其使用的地板装饰有闪光灯，还有多台支持高清视频的35英尺大屏幕显示器<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|225}}。颁奖嘉宾登上舞台时，这些显示器上将会显示出往届颁奖典礼的镜头、获提名电影的镜头以及奖项揭晓时几位提名者的面部表情等<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|301}}。由于这些设计对移动、照明和散热等方面技术存在较高要求，基恩和自己的团体先是在美国广播公司的展望工作室测试后，再将设备安装到神殿礼堂<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|235}}。

另外还有多人参与了颁奖典礼的制作。男演员彼德·考约特担任电视广播的报幕员<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|301}}<ref>{{cite news |last=Ryan |first=Joal |title=Taps for Oscar Night Taps |url=http://www.eonline.com/news/39121/taps-for-oscar-night-taps |accessdate=2013-11-19 |work=''E!'' |publisher=NBCUniversal |date=1999-12-10 |archivedate=2013-10-15 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131015154742/http://www.eonline.com/news/39121/taps-for-oscar-night-taps}}</ref>。音乐总监伯特·巴卡拉克、唐·沃斯和罗布·施洛克为典礼创作了[[铁克诺音乐|铁克诺]]和[[流行音乐|流行]]混合风格的音乐，来取代现场管弦乐队的大部分曲目<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|238-239, 279}}<ref name="Pond">{{cite book |last=Pond |first=Steve |title=The Big Show: High Times and Dirty Dealings Backstage at the Academy Awards |year=2005 |isbn=0-571-21193-3 |location=New York, United States |edition= |OCLC= |publisher=Faber and Faber}}</ref>{{rp|238-239}}。此外，巴卡拉克还安排了包括[[菲丝·希尔|菲丝·希尔]]、[[雷·查尔斯|雷·查尔斯]]、[[拉蒂法女皇|拉蒂法女皇]]和[[艾萨克·海耶斯|艾萨克·海耶斯]]在内的多位[[音乐家|音乐家]]表演过去曾获奥斯卡最佳原创歌曲奖提名的歌曲<ref name="oldsongs">{{cite news |last=Burlingame |first=Jon |title=The Sound of Change |url=http://articles.latimes.com/2000/mar/20/entertainment/ca-10676 |accessdate=2013-11-19 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=2000-03-20 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131023021344/http://articles.latimes.com/2000/mar/20/entertainment/ca-10676 |archivedate=2013-10-23 |deadurl=no}}</ref>，编舞家[[肯尼·奥特加|肯尼·奥特加]]督导了“{{lang|en|Blame Canada}}”曲目的表演<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|285}}。

=== 选票遗失 ===
投票选出获奖者的时间截止两周前，美国电影艺术与科学学院报称有约4000张邮寄给学院成员的选票遗失。装有这些选票邮件的包裹被错标成了第三级邮件<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|231}}。2000年3月6日，[[贝尔_(加利福尼亚州)|贝尔]]的[[美國郵政署|美國郵政署]]区域配送中心找到了其中约1000张选票<ref>{{cite news |last=Ryfle |first=Steve |title=Oscar Ballots: Lost... And Found! |url=http://www.hollywood.com/news/brief/312040/oscar-ballots-lost-and-found?page=all |accessdate=2013-11-19 |work=''Hollywood.com'' |date=2000-03-19 |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131016025107/http://www.hollywood.com/news/brief/312040/oscar-ballots-lost-and-found?page=all}}</ref>。为了让受到影响的成员可以投票，学院重新用黄色信封寄出了选票，并且将投票截止日期延迟到3月23日<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|289}}。

本届晚会获奖了大部分媒体的正面评价。《{{link-en|波士顿先驱报|Boston Herald}}》电视评论员莫妮卡·科林斯称赞两位制片人理查德和莉莉奉献了一台干净利落、生动活泼、光辉四射的高质量节目。她也调侃比利·克里斯托这回不需要再来力挽狂澜了，因为晚会看来进展很顺利<ref name="Bona" />{{rp|314}}。《{{link-en|旧金山考察报|The San Francisco Examiner}}》的韦斯利·莫里斯（{{lang|en|Wesley Morris}}）表扬典礼比之前几十年的表现更时尚，对其中的音乐和舞台设计也有着高度评价<ref>{{cite news |last=Morris | first=Wesley |title=An 'American' tale |url=http://www.sfgate.com/style/article/An-American-tale-3067796.php#page-2 |accessdate=2013-10-16 |work=The San Francisco Examiner |publisher=Hearst Corporation |date=2000-03-27 |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131016071138/http://www.sfgate.com/style/article/An-American-tale-3067796.php#page-2}}</ref>。《[[洛杉磯時報|洛杉磯時報]]》专栏作家保罗·布朗菲尔德（{{lang|en|Paul Brownfield}}）兴高采烈地声称：“第72届奥斯卡颁奖典礼比以往多年都要新潮，有更时尚的视觉感官和更前卫的基调”，他还称克里斯托是“整个故作严肃、装腔作势夜晚最完美的解药”<ref>{{cite news |last=Brownfield |first=Paul |title=Hipper, Sleeker but Longer |url=http://articles.latimes.com/2000/mar/27/entertainment/ca-13067 |accessdate=2013-10-16 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Company |date=2000-03-27 |quote=the 72nd annual Academy Awards telecast was hipper than in years past, sleeker in look and edgier in tone....the perfect antidote to the entire evening's self-serious posturing. |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131023032600/http://articles.latimes.com/2000/mar/27/entertainment/ca-13067 |deadurl=no |archivedate=2013-10-23}}</ref>。

一些媒体对晚会的评价不佳。《[[旧金山纪事报|旧金山纪事报]]》的约翰·卡曼（{{lang|en|John Carman}}）感叹，节目虽然表现平稳、整齐，但始终没有给观众带来期望的惊喜<ref>{{cite news |last=Carman |first=John |title=The Show{{spaced ndash}}Onstage and Off / TV Oscarcast Was Efficient, Lacked Emotion |quote=the show never quite managed the big surprises, sloppy excesses and emotional highs we hope to see. |url=http://www.sfgate.com/entertainment/article/The-Show-Onstage-and-Off-TV-Oscarcast-Was-3240642.php |accessdate=2013-10-16 |work=San Francisco Chronicle |publisher=Hearst Corporation |date=2000-03-27 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016071339/http://www.sfgate.com/entertainment/article/The-Show-Onstage-and-Off-TV-Oscarcast-Was-3240642.php |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no}}</ref>。《[[匹兹堡邮报|匹兹堡邮报]]》电视评论员{{link-en|罗布·欧文|Rob Owen (journalist)}}批评晚会的节奏不均衡，比利·克里斯托的20分钟恶搞和歌唱段落也没有过去那么好笑，缓慢的节拍始终没有加快<ref>{{cite news |last=Owen |first=Rob |title=Crystal just ho-hum in a really slow Oscars show |url=http://old.post-gazette.com/tv/20000327owen.asp |work=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette | publisher=Crain Communications |accessdate=2013-11-19 |date=2000-03-27 |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131016082906/http://old.post-gazette.com/tv/20000327owen.asp}}</ref>。《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》的卡恩·詹姆斯（{{lang|en|Caryn James}}）称“这四个小时的节目变得了行尸走肉”，她还认为晚会因带有太多向过去致敬的内容而变得臃肿<ref>{{cite news |last=James |first=Caryn |title=CRITIC'S NOTEBOOK; Growing Pains as the Oscars Play to Jaded Stargazers |quote=the four-hour show turned into a zombie. |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2000/03/28/movies/critic-s-notebook-growing-pains-as-the-oscars-play-to-jaded-stargazers.html |accessdate=2013-11-19 |work=The New York Times | publisher=The New York Times Company |date=2000-03-28 |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131016072236/http://www.nytimes.com/2000/03/28/movies/critic-s-notebook-growing-pains-as-the-oscars-play-to-jaded-stargazers.html}}</ref>。

=== 收视和奖项 ===
本届颁奖典礼通过美国广播公司在美国直播，其播出时段里平均有4652万观众收看，与[[第71届奥斯卡颁奖典礼|去年]]相比提高了3%<ref name="LA Times Oscar Ratings" /><ref>{{cite web|first=Bill |last=Gorman |title=Academy Awards Averages 41.3 Million Viewers; Most Since 2005 |url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2010/03/08/academy-awards-averages-41-3-million-viewers-most-since-2005/44217/ |work=''TV by the Numbers'' |publisher=Tribune Company |date=2010-03-08 |accessdate=2013-10-16 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130114121751/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2010/03/08/academy-awards-averages-41-3-million-viewers-most-since-2005/44217/ |archivedate=2013-01-14 |deadurl=no }}</ref>，而所有曾观看部分播出时段节目的观众总数则有7911万<ref name="LA Times Oscar Ratings" />。从尼尔森收视率调查的数量看，节目的表现也强过去年，吸引了29.2%的[[家庭|家庭]]观看，比前一年提升了约2.1%<ref name="TVB Oscars">{{cite news|title=Academy Awards ratings |work=''Television Bureau of Advertising'' |format=[[PDF|PDF]] |archivedate=2013-09-27 |deadurl=yes |url=http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf |accessdate=2013-10-16 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130927155318/http://www.tvb.org/media/file/Academy_Awards.pdf }}</ref>，在18至49岁年龄段人群中收获了19.1%的收视率<ref name="TVB Oscars" />。

2000年7月，颁奖典礼获得了第52届[[黄金时段艾美奖|黄金时段艾美奖]]的9项提名<ref>{{cite news|title=Primetime Emmy Award database |url=http://www.emmys.com/shows/72nd-annual-academy-awards |accessdate=2013-10-16 |work=''Academy of Television Arts and Sciences'' |publisher=ATAS |archivedate=2013-06-23 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130623165922/http://www.emmys.com/shows/72nd-annual-academy-awards }}</ref>，并在两个月后成功为路易斯·J·霍维茨拿下了电视转播指导奖<ref>{{cite news |title=List of Emmy winners |url=http://www.enquirer.com/editions/2000/09/11/loc_list_of_emmy_winners.html |accessdate=2013-11-19 |work=The Cincinnati Enquirer |publisher=Gannett Company |date=2000-09-11}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Zurawik |first=David |title=Cornering the Emmys |url=http://articles.baltimoresun.com/2000-09-11/features/0009110053_1_miniseries-west-wing-drama-series/2 |accessdate=2013-11-19 |work=The Baltimore Sun |publisher=Tribune Company |date=2000-09-11 |archivedate=2013-10-16 |deadurl=no |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131016094542/http://articles.baltimoresun.com/2000-09-11/features/0009110053_1_miniseries-west-wing-drama-series/2}}</ref>。

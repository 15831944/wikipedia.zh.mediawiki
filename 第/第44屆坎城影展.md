'''第44屆[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]'''於1991年5月9日至5月20日在[[法國|法國]][[坎城|坎城]]舉辦，開幕片為美國導演{{link-en|大衛·馬密|David Mamet}}執導的《{{link-en|殺人拼圖|Homicide (1991 film)}}》<ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1991-05-07/features/9102100510_1_maurice-pialat-opening-night-film-cannes |title=The Cinematic Carnival Of Cannes |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref>，閉幕片則為美國導演[[雷利·史考特|雷利·史考特]]執導的《[[塞尔玛与路易丝|末路狂花]]》<ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1991-05-21/news/9102150436_1_barton-fink-european-film-awards-d-or-for-best-film |title=`Barton Fink` Makes History At Cannes Fest |work=chicagotribune.com |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref>，評審團主席則由波蘭/法國導演[[羅曼·波蘭斯基|羅曼·波蘭斯基]]擔任<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1991/juryLongFilm.html |title=Juries 1991: Feature film |work=festival-cannes.fr |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160415042033/http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1991/juryLongFilm.html |archivedate=15 April 2016}}</ref>。

'''第53屆柏林电影节'''（{{lang-de|'''''Internationale Filmfestspiele Berlin 2003'''''}}）於2008年2月6日至2月16日舉行在德國柏林舉辦<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2003/01_jahresblatt_2003/01_Jahresblatt_2003.html |title=53RD BERLIN INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL FEBRUARY 6 - 16, 2003 |accessdate=9 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131104212926/http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2003/01_jahresblatt_2003/01_Jahresblatt_2003.html| archivedate=9 June 2014 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>，開幕片為美國導演[[勞勃·馬歇爾|勞勃·馬歇爾]]執導的歌舞片《[[芝加哥_(電影)|芝加哥]]》，閉幕片則為美國導演[[馬丁·史柯西斯|馬丁·史柯西斯]]執導的《[[紐約黑幫|紐約黑幫]]》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2003/02/12/movies/grim-reality-dominates-berlinale-film-festival.html |title=Grim Reality Dominates Berlinale Film Festival |accessdate=9 June 2014 |work=nytimes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.filmthreat.com/festivals/1103/ |title=53RD BERLIN INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL WRAP UP |accessdate=9 June 2014 |work=filmthreat |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140714212201/http://www.filmthreat.com/festivals/1103/ |archivedate=2014-07-14 |deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2003/08_pressemitteilungen_2003/08_Pressemitteilungen_2003-Details_995.html |title=GANGS OF NEW YORK TO BE FINAL FILM OF THE BERLINALE 2003 |accessdate=9 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de }}</ref>。評審團主席則為加拿大導演[[艾騰·伊格言|艾騰·伊格言]]。

'''第55屆[[柏林影展|柏林影展]]'''（{{lang-de|Internationale Filmfestspiele Berlin 2005}}），於2005年2月10日至2月20日於[[德國|德國]][[柏林|柏林]]舉辦<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2005/01_jahresblatt_2005/01_Jahresblatt_2005.html |title=55TH BERLIN INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL FEBRUARY 10 - 20, 2005 |accessdate=10 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131104212716/http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2005/01_jahresblatt_2005/01_Jahresblatt_2005.html| archivedate=4 November 2013 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>，開幕片為法國導演{{link-fr|雷吉斯·瓦格涅|Régis Wargnier}}執導的《{{link-en|野人傳奇|Man to Man (2005 film)}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2005/feb/10/berlinfilmfestival2005.festivals |title=In brief: Berlin festival celebrates European film |accessdate=10 June 2014}}</ref>，閉幕片則為美國導演[[比爾·坎登|比爾·坎登]]執導的《[[引人入性|金賽性學教室]]》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dw.de/berlin-film-festival-set-for-scandal/a-1475449 |title=Berlin Film Festival Set for Scandal |accessdate=10 June 2014}}</ref>，評審團主席則由德國導演[[羅蘭·艾默瑞奇|羅蘭·艾默瑞奇]]擔任。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2005/04_jury_2005/04_Jury_2005.html |title=JURIES 2005 |accessdate=10 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131023122539/http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2005/04_jury_2005/04_Jury_2005.html| archivedate=23 October 2013 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>

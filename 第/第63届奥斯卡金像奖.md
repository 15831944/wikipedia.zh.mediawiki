'''第63届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1990年电影|1990年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1991年3月25日下午18点（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上21点）在美国[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的神殿礼堂举行，共计颁发23个类别的[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）。晚会通过[[美国广播公司|美国广播公司]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播，由吉尔·凯茨担任[[電影監製|制作人]]，杰夫·马格里斯执导<ref>{{cite web | title = Credits | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150217151951/http://jeffmargolisproductions.com/credits/ | archivedate = 2015-02-17 | url = http://jeffmargolisproductions.com/credits/ | publisher = Jeff Margolis Productions | accessdate = 2015-03-28}}</ref>，男演员[[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]连续第二年担任[[主持人|主持人]]<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Kleid | first1 = Beth | title = Awards Update | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1991-01-24/entertainment/ca-810_1_academy-awards | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140714125925/http://articles.latimes.com/1991-01-24/entertainment/ca-810_1_academy-awards | accessdate = 2015-03-28 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1991-01-24 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-07-14}}</ref>。3个星期前的3月2日，女演员[[吉娜·戴维斯|吉娜·戴维斯]]在加利福尼亚州[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[比佛利山希尔顿酒店|比佛利山希尔顿酒店]]主持颁发了[[奥斯卡科技成果奖|奥斯卡科技成果奖]]<ref>{{cite news|title=Past Scientific & Technical Awards Ceremonies |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140707000407/http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html |deadurl=yes |accessdate=2014-07-10 |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |publisher=AMPAS |archivedate=2014-07-07 }}</ref>。

《[[与狼共舞|与狼共舞]]》赢得包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]、[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|导演]]在内的7项大奖，是这个夜晚的最大赢家<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Rother | first1 = Larry | title = Kevin Costner and 'Dances With Wolves' Win Top Oscar Prizes | url = http://www.nytimes.com/1991/03/26/movies/kevin-costner-and-dances-with-wolves-win-top-oscar-prizes.html | accessdate = 2015-03-28 | work = The New York Times | publisher = Arthur Ochs Sulzberger | date = 1991-03-26 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141024052525/http://www.nytimes.com/1991/03/26/movies/kevin-costner-and-dances-with-wolves-win-top-oscar-prizes.html | archivedate = 2014-10-24 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。其他获奖电影包括胜出3项的《[[迪克·特雷西|迪克·特雷西]]》，获奖2项的《[[人鬼情未了|人鬼情未了]]》，以及胜出1项的《[[美国梦_(纪录片)|美国梦]]》（{{lang|en|''American Dream''}}）、《[[衣食住行_(短片)|衣食住行]]》（{{lang|en|''Creature Comforts''}}）、《[[大鼻子情圣|大鼻子情圣]]》、《[[等待的日子|等待的日子]]》（{{lang|en|''Days of Waiting''}}）、《[[好家伙|好家伙]]》、《[[猎杀红色十月_(电影)|猎杀红色十月]]》、《[[希望之旅|希望之旅]]》（{{lang|de|''Reise der Hoffnung''}}）、《[[共进午餐|共进午餐]]》（{{lang|en|''The Lunch Date''}}）、《[[米泽丽|米泽丽]]》、《[[豪门孽债_(美国电影)|豪门孽债]]》（{{lang|en|''Reversal of Fortune''}}）和《[[全面回忆|全面回忆]]》。晚会的电视直播平均吸引了近4300万美国观众收看。

== 典礼资讯 ==
[[File:Billy_Crystal_VF_2012_Shankbone.JPG|thumb]]担任第63届奥斯卡颁奖典礼的主持人]]

由于[[第62届奥斯卡金像奖|前一年的颁奖典礼]]获得评论界赞誉，因此美国电影艺术与科学学院再度聘请电影[[電影監製|制作人]]、前[[美国导演工会|美国导演工会]]主席吉尔·凯茨（{{lang|en|Gil Cates}}）再次担任颁奖典礼电视转播的制作人<ref>{{cite news | title = Short Takes: Dates for '91 Oscars Scheduled | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1990-09-21 | archivedate = 2014-07-14 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140714122947/http://articles.latimes.com/1990-09-21/entertainment/ca-956_1_oscars-scheduled | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1990-09-21/entertainment/ca-956_1_oscars-scheduled}}</ref>。距晚会还有两个月时，凯茨选择曾主持上届颁奖典礼的喜剧演员[[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]再度担任主持人<ref name="Orlando">{{cite news | title = Crystal Will Again Be Host Of Academy Awards Show | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Orlando Sentinel | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1991-01-25 | archivedate = 2014-07-14 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140714164020/http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1991-01-25/news/9101250672_1_academy-awards-show-crystal-shrine-auditorium | url = http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1991-01-25/news/9101250672_1_academy-awards-show-crystal-shrine-auditorium}}</ref>。克里斯托在经学院发布的声明中开玩笑称：“这真让人倍感荣幸，我希望能把节目时长控制在9个钟头以内”<ref name="Orlando" />。

凯茨会为颁奖典礼选择主题，将节目围绕主题展开，其中上一年主题是“三个半小时环游世界”。他把1991年这场盛会的主题定为“电影百年”，庆祝[[爱迪生|爱迪生]]的[[活动电影放映机|活动电影放映机]]和[[伊士曼柯达公司|伊士曼柯达公司]]的赛璐璐电影胶片诞生100周年<ref>{{cite news | last = Sloan | first = Eugene | title = Movies Are the Star of the Show | work = USA Today | publisher = Gannett Company | date = 1991-03-25 | page = 5D}}</ref>。为配合主题，晚会的开场片段可谓雄心勃勃。男演员[[迈克尔·凯恩|迈克尔·凯恩]]在[[巴黎|巴黎]][[大咖啡馆|大咖啡馆]]通过卫星联线引出节目，重现电影短片《[[火车进站|火车进站]]》于1895年在此首映的短暂片段<ref>{{cite news | last = Scott | first = Jay | title = Oscar awards | work = The Globe and Mail | publisher = The Globe and Mail Inc. | date = 1991-03-26 | page = C1}}</ref>。接下来镜头切回神殿礼堂，女演员杰斯米·盖（{{lang|en|Jasmine Guy}}）及其他多位舞者在台上起舞，同时背景银幕上还在放映多部电影的片段<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|812}}。纪录片电影人查克·沃克曼（{{lang|en|Chuck Workman}}）拍摄了一段短片，其中有包括[[莎莉·菲尔德|莎莉·菲尔德]]、安迪·加西亚和[[安杰丽卡·休斯顿|安杰丽卡·休斯顿]]在内的多位演员谈论自己看过的第一部电影<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|806}}。

另外还有多人参与颁奖典礼的制作。电影配乐师兼作曲家比尔·康提（Bill Conti）担任颁奖典礼的音乐总监<ref>{{cite web|title=Events |url=http://www.billconti.org/works/events/ |website=Bill Conti |publisher=Bill Conti |accessdate=2014-06-22 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150109153751/http://www.billconti.org/works/events/ |archivedate=2015-01-09 }}</ref>。舞蹈演员[[黛比·艾伦|黛比·艾伦]]（{{lang|en|Debbie Allen}}）编排了一段展现原创歌曲奖提名作品的舞蹈曲目<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|805}}。因空难而失去8名乐队同伴的[[瑞芭·麦肯泰尔|瑞芭·麦肯泰尔]]出场表演原创歌曲奖提名作品、《[[来自边缘的明信片|来自边缘的明信片]]》插曲《{{lang|en|I'm Checkin' Out}}》<ref>{{cite news | last = MacCambridge | first = Michael | title = Wolves' leads the pack with seven awards | work = Austin American-Statesman | publisher = Cox Enterprises | date = 1991-03-26 | page = D1}}</ref>。晚会开场时，牧马人丽莎·布朗（{{lang|en|Lisa Brown}}）牵着一匹名叫“{{lang|en|Beechnut}}”的马出场，马上还驮着主持人克里斯托，他主演的电影《[[城市滑头|城市滑头]]》即将上映，“{{lang|en|Beechnut}}”也是片中的重要角色<ref>{{cite book | last = Wilson | first = Staci Lynne | title = Animal Movies Guide | year = 2007 | isbn = 0-967518-539 | OCLC = 779680732 | publisher = Running Free Press | page = 150}}</ref>。

=== 提名电影票房表现 ===
截至2月12日提名公布时，全部5部获最佳影片奖提名的电影在美国[[票房|票房]]市场共计进账4亿5821万3897美元，平均每部9164万2779美元<ref name="BoxOfficeOscars">{{cite news | title = 1990 Academy Award Nominations and Winner for Best Picture | url = http://boxofficemojo.com/oscar/chart/?yr=1990&p=.htm | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150324124316/http://boxofficemojo.com/oscar/chart/?yr=1990&p=.htm | work = Box Office Mojo | publisher = Amazon.com | archivedate = 2015-03-24 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。其中《人鬼情未了》以2亿1356万5446美元位居榜首，其后分别是收入1亿零434万9829美元的《与狼共舞》，进账6250万4417美元的《教父3》，收入4101万零330美元的《好家伙》和进账3678万3875美元的《无语问苍天》<ref name="BoxOfficeOscars" />。

在1990年[[美国电影|美国电影]]票房50强中，有12部电影获得了共计51项提名。其中《人鬼情未了》（亚军）、《漂亮女人》（季军）、《与狼共舞》（第8名）、《迪克·特雷西》（第9名）、《教父3》（第17名）、《好家伙》（第30名）和《无语问苍天》（第34名）获得了最佳影片、导演或任意一项演员、编剧类奖项提名。另外5部获提名的电影分别是《小鬼当家》（冠军）、《猎杀红色十月》（第5名）、《全面回忆》（第6名）、《霹雳男儿》（第12名）和《剪刀手爱德华》（第22名）。<ref>{{cite news | title = 1990 Box Office Grosses (as of February 12, 1991) | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Box Office Mojo | publisher = Amazon.com | archivedate = 2014-07-14 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140714154754/http://boxofficemojo.com/yearly/chart/?asof=1991-02-12&view=releasedate&view2=domestic&yr=1990&sort=gross&order=DESC&p=.htm | deadurl = no | url = http://boxofficemojo.com/yearly/chart/?asof=1991-02-12&view=releasedate&view2=domestic&yr=1990&sort=gross&order=DESC&p=.htm}}</ref>

=== 专业评价 ===
媒体出版物对本届颁奖晚会评价褒贬不一，部分媒体反响较为负面。《[[洛杉矶时报|洛杉矶时报]]》的里克·杜布罗（{{lang|en|Rick DuBrow}}）写道：“经过一天漫长的等待，我们迎来了奥斯卡，全年最有效的安眠药之一”<ref>{{cite news | last = DuBrow | first = Rick | title = Oscar Endures Another Hard Day's Night | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1991-03-26 | archivedate = 2015-02-04 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150204230633/http://articles.latimes.com/1991-03-26/entertainment/ca-984_1_oscar-show | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1991-03-26/entertainment/ca-984_1_oscar-show}}</ref>。《[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]]》电视评论员[[汤姆·夏尔斯|汤姆·夏尔斯]]（{{lang|en|Tom Shales}}）指出，主持人克里斯托“多次针对观众的反应来抖包袱，似乎是观众在取悦他，而不是他取悦观众。”他认为这年的奥斯卡与往年相比更加失败<ref>{{cite news | last = Shales | first = Tom | title = The Show Doesn't Make A Spectacle of Itself | work = The Washington Post | publisher = The Washington Post Company | date = 1991-03-27 | page = B1}}</ref>。《{{lang|en|Pantagraph}}》日报专栏作家丹·克拉夫特（{{lang|en|Dan Craft}}）指出：“奥斯卡颁奖典礼已经悄然变得更加嬉皮和雅痞化，媚俗和怀旧已向效率和上进心让位。每个人的行为都恰当得让人烦闷，并且更糟的是，他们都还一副衣冠楚楚的模样。”他还觉得克里斯托的那些有关娱乐圈内幕的笑料太过平淡，并且电视观众可能会感到摸不着头脑。<ref>{{cite news | title = New Academy Awards version isn't much fun | work = The Pantagraph | publisher = Lee Enterprises | date = 1991-03-29 | page = C2}}</ref>

其他媒体对节目的评价更为正面。《[[盐湖城论坛报|盐湖城论坛报]]》（{{lang|en|''The Salt Lake Tribune''}}）专栏作家[[哈罗德·辛德勒|哈罗德·辛德勒]]（{{lang|en|Harold Schindler}}）写道：“比利·克里斯托以如此洒脱的方式让星期一晚上（的节目）顺利进行，特别是额外的那15分钟节目效果显著。”他还认为，晚会的电影历史主题让艺术与科学学院有效利用了其电影片库的优势。<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Schindler | first1 = Harold | title = Academy Gives Its Best Performance As Crystal Sparkles on Oscar Night | work = The Salt Lake Tribune | publisher = MediaNew Group | date = 1991-03-27 | page = A5}}</ref>影评人[[伦纳德·马尔丁|伦纳德·马尔丁]]（{{lang|en|Leonard Maltin}}）表示：晚会让人“情绪高涨，让我们有机会去体会赢得这些奖项的感觉……优秀的人就能拿第一，这也是我们最喜欢好莱坞的部分：快乐结局”<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|812}}。《[[奥兰多哨兵报|奥兰多哨兵报]]》（{{lang|en|''Orlando Sentinel''}}）影评人杰伊·博伊尔（{{lang|en|Jay Boyar}}）称赞克里斯托搞活了晚会的现场气氛，称他巧妙地在有关好莱坞内幕消息的包袱和普通观众能够理解的俏皮话之间找到平衡<ref>{{cite news | last = Boyar | first = Jay | title = Oscar Night's Improvements Are Crystal-clear | archivedate = 2015-02-03 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150203204949/http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1991-03-29/entertainment/9103280111_1_billy-crystal-sonny-bono-broadcast | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Orlando Sentinel | publisher = Tribune Company | url = http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1991-03-29/entertainment/9103280111_1_billy-crystal-sonny-bono-broadcast | deadurl = no | date = 1991-03-29}}</ref>。

=== 收视率和奖项 ===
本届颁奖典礼通过美国广播公司在美国直播，其播出时段{{里}}平均有4270万观众收看，与[[第62届奥斯卡金像奖|前一年]]相比提高了6%<ref>{{cite news | last = Johnson | first = Greg | title = Call It the Glamour Bowl | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141229085722/http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2014-12-29 | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1999-03-18}}</ref>，估计曾观看部分时段节目的观众总数有7600万<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Margulies | first1 = Lee | title = TV Ratings: The Ratings Award Goes to Oscar | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150227105435/http://articles.latimes.com/1991-04-03/entertainment/ca-1885_1_ratings-award | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1991-04-03/entertainment/ca-1885_1_ratings-award | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1991-04-03 | archivedate = 2015-02-27 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。从尼尔森收视率调查的数据看，节目的表现也好过前一年，吸引了28.4%的家庭收看，收视率超过48%<ref>{{cite news | last = DuBrow | first1 = Rick | title = Ratings Up Slightly for ABC's Oscar Telecast | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Los Angelese Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1991-03-27 | archivedate = 2015-02-27 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150227104312/http://articles.latimes.com/1991-03-27/entertainment/ca-1084_1_abc-s-oscar-telecast | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1991-03-27/entertainment/ca-1084_1_abc-s-oscar-telecast}}</ref>。第63届奥斯卡颁奖典礼由此成为继1984年的收视人数是[[第56届奥斯卡金像奖|第56届颁奖晚会]]以来电视观众人数最多的奥斯卡颁奖典礼<ref>{{cite news | last = Gorman | first = Bill | title = 1-Featured With No 'Avatar' Expect 'Academy Awards' Viewership To Fall; Ratings History + Your Guess For This Year (Poll) | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = TV by the Numbers | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 2011-02-26 | archivedate = 2015-02-26 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20150226012912/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/02/26/with-no-avatar-expect-academy-awards-viewership-to-fall-ratings-history-your-guess-for-this-year-poll/83754/ | url = http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2011/02/26/with-no-avatar-expect-academy-awards-viewership-to-fall-ratings-history-your-guess-for-this-year-poll/83754/}}</ref>。

1991年7月，颁奖典礼获得第43届[[黄金时段艾美奖|黄金时段艾美奖]]的9项提名<ref>{{cite news | title = Primetime Emmy Award database | url = http://www.emmys.com/shows/63rd-annual-academy-awards | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | work = Academy of Television Arts and Sciences | publisher = ATAS | archivedate = 2014-07-14 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140714204016/http://www.emmys.com/shows/63rd-annual-academy-awards}}</ref>。一个月后，晚会在3项提名中胜出，分别是杰出综艺、音乐或喜剧节目奖（吉尔·凯茨获奖），综艺或音乐节目杰出个人表现奖（比利·克里斯托获奖），以及综艺或音乐节目杰出编剧奖（[[哈尔·坎特|哈尔·坎特]]、[[布斯·可汗|布斯·可汗]]、比利·克里斯托、[[大卫·斯坦伯格|大卫·斯坦伯格]]、[[布鲁斯·维兰奇|布鲁斯·维兰奇]]和[[罗伯特·乌尔|罗伯特·乌尔]]获奖）<ref>{{cite news | title = Emmy Awards: The Other Winners | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1991-08-26/entertainment/ca-995_1_academy-awards | accessdate = 2015-03-29 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140714112217/http://articles.latimes.com/1991-08-26/entertainment/ca-995_1_academy-awards | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1991-08-26 | archivedate = 2014-07-14 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

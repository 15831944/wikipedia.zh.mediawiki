'''第111届[[美国国会|美国国会]]'''（{{lang-en|111th United States Congress}}）的任期从2009年1月3日至2011年1月3日，也是[[奥巴马|奥巴马]]总统任期开始的两年。经过2008年[[美国参议院|参议院]]与[[美国众议院|众议院]]选举，[[民主党_(美国)|民主党]]成为了两院的多数党。这一届国会被认为是自[[林登·约翰逊|林登·约翰逊]]总统时的[[第89届美国国会|第89届国会]]以来，最有效率的国会之一。<ref>{{cite web|author=CARL HULSE and DAVID M. HERSZENHORN|title=111th Congress - One for the History Books|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2010/12/23/us/politics/23cong.html|publisher=New York Times|date=December 20, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=David A. Fahrenthold, Philip Rucker and Felicia Sonmez|title=Stormy 111th Congress was still the most productive in decades|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2010/12/22/AR2010122205620.html|publisher=Washington Post|date=December 23, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Lisa Lerer & Laura Litvan|title=No Congress Since '60s Makes as Much Law as 111th Affecting Most Americans|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2010-12-22/no-congress-since-1960s-makes-most-laws-for-americans-as-111th|publisher=Bloomberg News|date=December 22, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Guy Raz|title=This Congress Did A Lot, But What's Next?|url=http://www.npr.org/2010/12/26/132329542/this-congress-did-a-lot-but-whats-next#|publisher=NPR|date=December 26, 2010}}</ref>

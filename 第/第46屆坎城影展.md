'''第46屆[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]'''於1993年5月13日至5月24日在[[法國|法國]][[坎城|坎城]]舉辦<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cinema-francais.fr/cannes/cannes_1993.htm |title=46ème Festival International du Film - Cannes |work=cinema-francais.fr |language=fr |accessdate=7 June 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cannes-fest.com/an1993.htm |title=1993 - La leçon de cinéma (The cinema lesson) |work=cannes-fest.com |language=fr |accessdate=7 June 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.infoplease.com/ipa/A0150071.html |title=1993 Cannes Film Festival |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref>，開幕片為法國導演[[安德烈·泰希內|安德烈·泰希內]]執導的《{{link-fr|鍾愛一生|Ma saison préférée}}》，閉幕片則為法國導演{{link-fr|菲洛梅納·艾斯波希多|Philomène Esposito}}執導的《{{link-fr|激情佳人|Toxic Affair (film)}}》<ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1993-05-14/news/9305140105_1_la-croisette-cannes-film-festival-year-s-festival |title=Will French Film Queens Deneuve, Adjani Get Along? No Cannes Do |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1993-05-30/entertainment/9305300171_1_body-snatchers-cannes-film-festival-palme-d-or |title=Cannes Goods |accessdate=25 May 2017 |work=Chicago Tribune |date=30 May 1993}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.baltimoresun.com/1993-05-21/features/1993141222_1_cannes-film-festival-croisette-arnold-schwarzenegger |title=All the glitter but few glitterati Cannes Film Festival sadly lacking stars Cannes Film Festival |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1993/05/14/movies/at-cannes-more-of-everything-including-films.html |title=At Cannes, More Of Everything, Including Films |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://variety.com/1993/film/reviews/my-favorite-season-1200432251/ |title=Review: ‘My Favorite Season’ |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref>。

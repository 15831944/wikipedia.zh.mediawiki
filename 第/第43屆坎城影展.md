'''第44屆[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]'''於1990年5月10日至5月21日在[[法國|法國]][[坎城|坎城]]舉辦，開幕片為日本導演[[黑澤明|黑澤明]]執導的《[[夢_(1990年電影)|夢]]》<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.apnewsarchive.com/1990/Kurosawa-s-Dreams-Opens-Cannes-Festival/id-da4c17b83b611ad747eef9690d0dc069 |title=Kurosawa's "Dreams" Opens Cannes Festival |work=apnewsarchive.com |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.orlandosentinel.com/1990-05-11/news/9005111133_1_kurosawa-dreams-burkina-faso-cannes-film-festival |title=Cannes Festival Opens With Showing Of 'Dreams' |work=orlandosentinel.com |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref>，閉幕片則為美國導演[[保羅·許瑞德|保羅·許瑞德]]執導的《{{link-en|迷情殺機|The Comfort of Strangers (film)}}》<ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.latimes.com/1990-05-09/entertainment/ca-426_1_cannes-film-festival |title=Cannes Film Festival Reflects World Change |work=latimes.com |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.focusfeatures.com/article/harold_pinter_1930_2008 |title=Harold Pinter 1930-2008 |work=focusfeatures.com |accessdate=10 December 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131214175810/http://www.focusfeatures.com/article/harold_pinter_1930_2008 |archivedate=14 December 2013}}</ref>，評審團主席則由義大利導演[[貝納多·貝托魯奇|貝納多·貝托魯奇]]擔任<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1990/juryLongFilm.html |title=Juries 1990: Feature film |work=festival-cannes.fr |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160415030613/http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1990/juryLongFilm.html |archivedate=15 April 2016}}</ref>。

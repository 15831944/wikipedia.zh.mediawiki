'''第62届奥斯卡颁奖典礼'''是[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]旨在奖励[[1989年电影|1989年最优秀电影]]的一场晚会，于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1990年3月26日下午18点（[[北美东部时区|北美东部时区]]晚上21点）在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的多萝西·钱德勒大厅举行，共计颁发了23项[[奥斯卡金像奖|奥斯卡金像奖]]（也称[[学院奖|学院奖]]）。晚会通过[[美国广播公司|美国广播公司]]在[[美国电视|美国]]直播，由吉尔·凯茨担任[[制作人|制作人]]，杰夫·马格里斯执导，男演员[[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]首次担任[[主持人|主持人]]<ref name="lat" />。三个星期前的3月3日，男演员[[理查德·戴萨特|理查德·戴萨特]]和女演员[[戴安·拉德|戴安·拉德]]在加利福尼亚州[[比佛利山_(加利福尼亞州)|比佛利山]]的[[比佛利山希尔顿酒店|比佛利山希尔顿酒店]]主持颁发了[[奥斯卡科技成果奖|奥斯卡科技成果奖]]<ref>{{cite news|title=Past Scientific & Technical Awards Ceremonies |url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140707000407/http://www.oscars.org/awards/scitech/pastawards.html |deadurl=yes |accessdate=2014-07-10 |work=Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences |publisher=AMPAS |archivedate=2014-07-07 }}</ref>。

《[[为黛茜小姐开车|为黛茜小姐开车]]》赢得了包括[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片]]和[[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|女主角]]在内的4项大奖，是这个夜晚的最大赢家，[[杰西卡·坦迪|杰西卡·坦迪]]也刷新了最年长的奥斯卡获奖演员纪录<ref>{{cite news | last = Cieply | first = Michael | title = 'Driving Miss Daisy,' Tandy Win Top Oscars : Academy Awards: Day-Lewis is named best actor. Stone is best director for 'Fourth of July.' | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1990-03-27/enews/mn-252_1_miss-daisy | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140710033836/http://articles.latimes.com/1990-03-27/news/mn-252_1_miss-daisy | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1990-03-27 | archivedate = 2014-07-10 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。其他获奖电影包括胜出三项的《[[光荣战役|光荣战役]]》，获奖两项的《[[生于七月四日_(电影)|生于七月四日]]》、《[[小美人鱼|小美人鱼]]》和《[[我的左脚|我的左脚]]》，以及胜出一项的《[[深渊_(电影)|深渊]]》、《[[平衡_(1989年电影)|平衡]]》（{{lang|en|''Balance''}}）、《[[蝙蝠侠_(1989年电影)|蝙蝠侠]]》、《[[天堂电影院|天堂电影院]]》、《[[共同的线索|共同的线索]]》（{{lang|en|''Common Threads: Stories from the Quilt''}}）、《[[死亡诗社|死亡诗社]]》、《[[亨利五世_(1989年电影)|亨利五世]]》（{{lang|en|''Henry V''}}）、《[[圣战奇兵|圣战奇兵]]》、《约翰斯通大洪水》（{{lang|en|''The Johnstown Flood''}}）和《工作经历》（{{lang|en|''Work Experience''}}）。晚会的电视直播平均吸引了超过4000万美国观众收看。

== 获奖和提名 ==
第62届奥斯卡金像奖提名名单于[[太平洋时区|太平洋时区]]1990年2月14日早上5点38分（[[协调世界时|协调世界时]]下午13点38分）由学院主席[[卡尔·莫尔登|卡尔·莫尔登]]（{{lang|en|Karl Malden}}）和女演员[[吉娜·戴维斯|吉娜·戴维斯]]一起在[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[比佛利山_(加利福尼亚州)|比佛利山]]的[[塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院|塞缪尔·戈尔德温剧院]]公布<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|764}}。《[[为黛茜小姐开车|为黛茜小姐开车]]》以9项提名领跑，《[[生于七月四日_(电影)|生于七月四日]]》以8项紧随其后<ref>{{cite news | title = 'Miss Daisy' in the Driver's Seat : Movie Is Nominated for 9 Oscars | archivedate = 2013-10-05 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131005011901/http://articles.latimes.com/1990-02-14/news/mn-811_1_driving-miss-daisy | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1990-02-14/news/mn-811_1_driving-miss-daisy | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | date = 1990-02-15 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company}}</ref>。

获奖者在1990年3月26日举行的颁奖典礼上宣布<ref>{{cite news | title = The Winners | archivedate = 2013-10-05 | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1990-03-27/entertainment/ca-357_1_driving-miss-daisy | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131005004736/http://articles.latimes.com/1990-03-27/entertainment/ca-357_1_driving-miss-daisy | date = 1990-03-27 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | deadurl = no}}</ref>。《为黛茜小姐开车》成为历史上第3部没有获得[[奥斯卡最佳导演奖|导演奖]]提名但却最终拿下[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片奖]]的作品<ref>{{cite news | last = Hughes | first = Mark | title = Ten Facts You Should Know About The Oscars | deadurl = no | url = http://www.forbes.com/sites/markhughes/2013/02/23/ten-facts-you-should-know-about-the-oscars/ | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | work = Forbes | publisher = Forbes, Inc. | date = 2013-02-23 | archivedate = 2013-10-31 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131031050959/http://www.forbes.com/sites/markhughes/2013/02/23/ten-facts-you-should-know-about-the-oscars/}}</ref>；同时因该片获[[奥斯卡最佳女主角奖|女主角奖]]的[[杰西卡·坦迪|杰西卡·坦迪]]已是80岁高龄，创下奥斯卡获奖演员的年龄新纪录<ref>{{cite news | last = Kehr | first = Dave | title = `Miss Daisy,` Jessica Tandy Win Top Oscars | url = http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1990-03-27/news/9001250287_1_oldest-best-actress-winner-screenplay-daisy | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | work = Chicago Tribune | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1990-03-27 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140407090121/http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1990-03-27/news/9001250287_1_oldest-best-actress-winner-screenplay-daisy | archivedate = 2014-04-07}}</ref>。此外，[[肯尼思·布拉纳|肯尼思·布拉纳]]还是第五位因同一部电影获得导演和[[奥斯卡最佳男主角奖|男主角奖]]提名的电影人<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|1163}}。

== 典礼资讯 ==
[[File:Billy_Crystal_VF_2012_Shankbone.JPG|thumb]]担任第62届奥斯卡颁奖典礼的主持人]]
[[第61届奥斯卡金像奖|前一年的晚会]]所获评价不佳，[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]为此组建了一个颁奖典礼评审委员会，试图找到电视直播无论是在媒体还是娱乐业都不受待见的原因<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|750}}<ref name="SnowJob">{{cite news | last = Hofler | first = Robert | title = Snow Job | archivedate = 2013-10-16 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131016124505/http://www.lamag.com/features/2010/03/01/snow-job | deadurl = yes | url = http://www.lamag.com/features/2010/03/01/snow-job | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | work = Los Angeles | publisher = Emmis Communications | date = 2010-03-01 }}</ref>。委员会的结论认为，上届晚会制片人[[阿伦·卡尔|阿伦·卡尔]]（{{lang|en|Allan Carr}}）最大的失误在于让开场歌舞持续了12分钟之久。担任委员会主席的制片人兼前任[[美国导演工会|美国导演工会]]主席吉尔伯特·凯茨称，如果这段曲目的长度大幅缩短，那么卡尔也不会受到这么严苛的批评<ref name="SnowJob" />。新当选的学院主席卡尔·莫尔登也针对前一年的电视转播表示：“学院中有些人觉得那次节目有点失控”<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|768}}。

1989年9月，凯茨获聘成为1990年颁奖典礼电视转播的制作人<ref>{{cite news | last = Ryan | first = Desmond | title = New Producer Is Chosen To Plan The 1990 Oscar Presentation | archivedate = 2013-10-22 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131022115405/http://articles.philly.com/1989-09-24/entertainment/26104285_1_oscar-show-allan-carr-gilbert-cates | url = http://articles.philly.com/1989-09-24/entertainment/26104285_1_oscar-show-allan-carr-gilbert-cates | work = The Philadelphia Inquirer | publisher = Philadelphia Media Network | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | date = 1989-09-24}}</ref>。对此莫尔登表示：“凯茨是位资深电影和电视导演，他在这两种媒体上都有高雅的作品，这将有助于恢复因上次奥斯卡颁奖典礼而受到损害的学院声誉”<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|768}}。1990年1月，喜剧演员[[比利·克里斯托|比利·克里斯托]]获选成为晚会的主持人<ref>{{cite news | title = Billy Crystal to be Host of Oscar Ceremonies | url = http://www.nytimes.com/1990/01/02/arts/billy-crystal-to-be-host-of-oscar-ceremonies.html | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | work = The New York Times | publisher = The New York Times Company | date = 1990-01-02 | archivedate = 2013-10-14 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131014082840/http://www.nytimes.com/1990/01/02/arts/billy-crystal-to-be-host-of-oscar-ceremonies.html | deadurl = no}}</ref>。对此凯茨在记者招待会上表示：“我们对能请来比利主持这次节目感到非常高兴，他的独特才华和对意外情况的掌控能力将成为今年晚会的重要资产”<ref>{{cite news | title = Billy Crystal Will Host Oscar Ceremonies | archivedate = 2014-07-10 | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | work = Deseret News | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140710153452/http://www.deseretnews.com/article/79543/BILLY-CRYSTAL-WILL-HOST-OSCAR-CEREMONIES.html?pg=all | publisher = Deseret News Publishing Company | date = 1990-01-01 | url = http://www.deseretnews.com/article/79543/BILLY-CRYSTAL-WILL-HOST-OSCAR-CEREMONIES.html?pg=all}}</ref>。

凯茨将这次节目的主题定为“三个半小时环游世界”，称这将是一次“围绕全世界的聚会”<ref name="nyt">{{cite news | title = New Host and New Technology, But a Familiar Oscar Show | archivedate = 2014-07-10 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140710153820/http://www.nytimes.com/1990/03/28/movies/review-television-new-host-and-new-technology-but-a-familiar-oscar-show.html?pagewanted=al | publisher = The New York Times Company | first = Janet | url= http://www.nytimes.com/1990/03/28/movies/review-television-new-host-and-new-technology-but-a-familiar-oscar-show.html?pagewanted=al | work = The New York Times | date = 1990-03-28 | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | last = Maslin}}</ref>。他对此还表示：“全世界都在不断变化，希望颁奖典礼能够跟上这种变化”<ref name="Wiley-Bona-1996" />{{rp|768}}。为了配合节目的主题，多位颁奖嘉宾都是在世界上多个不同地点宣布奖项得主，这些地点包括[[布宜诺斯艾利斯|布宜诺斯艾利斯]]、[[伦敦|伦敦]]、[[莫斯科|莫斯科]]和[[悉尼|悉尼]]<ref>{{cite news | last = Ryan | first = Desmond | title = Moscow To Take Part In The Oscar Ceremony | url = http://articles.philly.com/1990-03-11/entertainment/25904911_1_foreign-language-film-moscow-oscar | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131022115339/http://articles.philly.com/1990-03-11/entertainment/25904911_1_foreign-language-film-moscow-oscar | accessdate = 2014-07-10 | work = The Philadelphia Inquirer | publisher = Philadelphia Media Network | date = 1990-03-11 | archivedate = 2013-10-22 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。

在1989年美国电影票房50强中，有14部电影获得了共计43项提名。其中《温馨家族》（第8位）、《死亡诗社》（第9位）、《当哈利遇到莎莉》（第10位）、《梦幻之地》（第17位）、《生于七月四日》（第25位）、《为黛茜小姐开车》（第36位）和《性、谎言和录像带》（第45位）7部获得了最佳影片、导演，或任何一项表演、编剧类提名<ref name="1989boxoffice">{{cite news | title = 1989 Box Office Grosses (as of February 13, 1990) | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140710160813/http://boxofficemojo.com/yearly/chart/?asof=1990-02-12&view=releasedate&view2=domestic&yr=1989&sort=gross&order=DESC&p=.htm | archivedate = 2014-07-10 | deadurl = no | url = http://boxofficemojo.com/yearly/chart/?asof=1990-02-12&view=releasedate&view2=domestic&yr=1989&sort=gross&order=DESC&p=.htm | accessdate = 2014-07-11 | work = Box Office Mojo | publisher = Amazon.com}}</ref>。另外7部获提名的分别是《蝙蝠侠》（[[冠军|冠军]]）、《圣战奇兵》（[[亚军|亚军]]）、《致命武器2》（[[季军|季军]]）、《回到未来II》（第6位）、《小美人鱼》（第12位）、《深渊》（第22位）和《黑雨》（第27位）<ref name="1989boxoffice" />。

=== 专业评价 ===
本届晚会收到的媒体评价褒贬不一，部分媒体看法较为负面。《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》影评人珍妮特·马斯林（{{lang|en|Janet Maslin}}）对克里斯托的评价还过得去，但她批评节目试图变得国际化的做法“除了让人头痛外一无是处”<ref name="nyt" />。《[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]]》电视评论员[[汤姆·谢尔斯|汤姆·谢尔斯]]（{{lang|en|Tom Shales}}）感叹：“虽然克里斯托的开场独白看起来恰到好处，但随着夜晚的进行，他的表现却江河日下，在奖项之间的大部分插科打诨都表现平平。”他还批评了晚会的歌舞曲段以及多个“环绕世界”的切换镜头，觉得这毫无意义<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Shales | first1 = Tom | title = On the Air: Billy Crystal And the Night That Wouldn't End | accessdate = 2014-07-11 | work = The Washington Post | publisher = The Washington Post Company | page = C1 | url = http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-1117876.html | via = HighBeam Research | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20140714223902/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P2-1117876.html | archive-date = 2014-07-14 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>。 《[[洛杉矶时报|洛杉矶时报]]》的霍华德·罗森伯格（{{lang|en|Howard Rosenberg}}）则批评节目有些过于严肃，“仿佛是在谈生意”。他对克里斯托的表现予以好评，觉得节目的国际化也不失为一次好的尝试，但这些内容过于冗长，让人感到无聊，称自己身在[[堪薩斯城_(密蘇里州)|堪萨斯城]]的母亲看到无聊的时候就只好去看报纸<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Rosenberg | first1 = Howard | title = TV Review: Another Dose of Business as Usual | archivedate = 2014-07-11 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140711015746/http://articles.latimes.com/1990-03-27/entertainment/ca-361_1_big-business | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1990-03-27/entertainment/ca-361_1_big-business | accessdate = 2014-07-11 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1990-03-27}}</ref>。

其他媒体对节目的评价更为正面。《[[今日美国|今日美国]]》电视评论员马特·劳什（{{lang|en|Matt Roush}}）为比利·克里斯托的“巧舌如簧和精明”叫好，称赞他让晚会“充满活力、妙趣横生”，并且看上去还能“持续一整夜，真是件苦差事。”他在总结中认为：“好莱坞无疑是在快乐中入睡的，因为这次事情变了，奥斯卡没有丢自己的脸”<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Roush | first1 = Matt | title = Crystal sparkles on safe and stately show | work = USA Today | page = 4D | publisher = Gannett Company | date = 1990-03-27}}</ref>。《[[密尔沃基哨兵日报|密尔沃基哨兵日报]]》（{{lang|en|''Milwaukee Journal Sentinel''}}）的迈克·德鲁（{{lang|en|Mike Drew}}）认为，“克里斯托虽然过于‘内向’，而且也没有好莱坞以为的那么风趣，但他仍是位高效的主持人”<ref>{{cite news|last1=Drew|first1=Mike|title=Oscar show slow, but not bad|work= Milwaukee Journal Sentinel | page = 4 | publisher = Journal Communications | date = 1990-03-28}}</ref>。《[[费城问询报|费城问询报]]》影评人[[卡丽·瑞基|卡丽·瑞基]]（{{lang|en|Carrie Rickey}}）写道：“吉尔伯特·凯茨借此机会强调电影而非合唱团女孩之举令人鼓舞。”她还赞扬了克里斯托的表现，称他“巧妙的开场歌舞拥有[[约翰尼·卡森|约翰尼·卡森]]的漫画基调”<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Rickey | first1 = Carrie | title = An Oscar Telecast with Taste | archivedate = 2013-11-06 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131106051245/http://articles.philly.com/1990-03-28/news/25902145_1_race-relations-first-feature-film-editing | url = http://articles.philly.com/1990-03-28/news/25902145_1_race-relations-first-feature-film-editing | deadurl = no | accessdate = 2014-07-11 | work = The Philadelphia Inquirer | publisher = Philadelphia Media Network | date = 1990-03-28}}</ref>。

=== 收视率和奖项 ===
本届颁奖典礼通过美国广播公司在美国直播，其播出时段里平均有4024万观众收看，与[[第61届奥斯卡金像奖|前一年]]相比降低了5%<ref>{{cite news | last = Johnson | first = Greg | title = Call It the Glamour Bowl | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131022224101/http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 | archivedate = 2013-10-22 | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1999/mar/18/business/fi-18458/2 | accessdate = 2014-07-11 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1999-03-18}}</ref>，而所有曾观看部分时段节目的观众总数则估计有6931万<ref>{{cite news | title = Oscars Push ABC to Win Ratings Race | archivedate = 2014-07-11 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140711022630/http://articles.latimes.com/1990-04-03/entertainment/ca-864_1_ratings-race | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1990-04-03/entertainment/ca-864_1_ratings-race | accessdate = 2014-07-11 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | date = 1990-04-03}}</ref>。从尼尔森收视率调查的数据看，节目的表现也不及前一年，吸引了27.82%的家庭观看，收视率为49.42%<ref>{{cite news | title = ABC gets an Oscar boost | work = USA Today | page = 3D | publisher = Gannett Company | date = 1990-04-04}}</ref>。

1990年7月，颁奖典礼获得了第42届[[黄金时段艾美奖|黄金时段艾美奖]]的12项提名<ref>{{cite news | title = Primetime Emmy Award database | url = http://www.emmys.com/shows/62nd-annual-academy-awards | accessdate = 2014-07-11 | deadurl = no | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140711022927/http://www.emmys.com/shows/62nd-annual-academy-awards | work = Academy of Television Arts and Sciences | publisher = ATAS | archivedate = 2014-07-11}}</ref>，并在两个月后赢得了综艺或音乐节目类最佳艺术指导奖<ref>{{cite news | last1 = Lipton | first1 = Lauren | title = 'The Simpsons' Named Best Animated Series in Early Awards | url = http://articles.latimes.com/1990-09-17/entertainment/ca-663_1_comedy-series/2 | accessdate = 2014-07-11 | work = Los Angeles Times | publisher = Tribune Company | archivedate = 2014-07-11 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140711023116/http://articles.latimes.com/1990-09-17/entertainment/ca-663_1_comedy-series/2 | deadurl = no }}</ref>。

'''第二次薩摩亞內戰'''是1898年美國、德國與英國為了薩摩亞島鏈的控制權而引發的衝突。戰爭結束在1899年三方會議之後，美國獲得島的東半部，德國取得島的西半部，而英國獲得其它原為德屬的太平洋島鏈。<ref>Ryden, George Herbert. ''The Foreign Policy of the United States in Relation to Samoa''. New York: Octagon Books, 1975. (Reprint by special arrangement with Yale University Press. Originally published at New Haven: Yale University Press, 1928), p. 574; the Tripartite Convention (United States, Germany, Great Britain) was signed at Washington on 2 December 1899 with ratifications exchanged on 16 February 1900</ref>目前德屬薩摩亞已經成為獨立國家:[[萨摩亚|薩摩亞獨立國]]，[[美屬薩摩亞|美屬薩摩亞]]則繼續在美國政府管轄之下作為美國領土。<ref>Mains, P. John; McCarty, Louis Philippe (1906). The Statistician and Economist: Volume 23. pg. 249</ref>

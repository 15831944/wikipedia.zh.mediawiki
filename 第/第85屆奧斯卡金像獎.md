== 典礼资讯 ==
[[File:Seth_MacFarlane_by_Gage_Skidmore_5.jpg|thumb]]担任第85届奥斯卡颁奖典礼主持人。]]
由于之前几届颁奖典礼收视率有所下降，美国电影艺术与科学学院聘请了新的制作团队，希望提高收视率和人们对晚会的兴趣。有报道称当时的学院主席汤姆·谢拉克（{{lang|en|Tom Sherak}}）联系了[[电视节目|电视节目]]制作人[[洛恩·迈克尔斯|洛恩·迈克尔斯]]（{{lang|en|Lorne Michaels}}）负责本届晚会的制作，[[喜剧演员|喜剧演员]][[吉米·法伦|吉米·法伦]]（{{lang|en|Jimmy Fallon}}）担任主持人<ref>{{cite news|last=Sperling |first=Nicole |title=Jimmy Fallon in talks to host 2013 Oscars, but ABC said to object |accessdate=2014-04-11 |work=Los Angeles Times |publisher=Tribune Comapny |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140320155530/http://articles.latimes.com/2012/aug/02/entertainment/la-et-mn-jimmy-fallon-in-talks-to-host-2013-oscars-lorne-michaels-to-produce-20120802 |archivedate=2014-03-20 |date=2012-08-02 |url=http://articles.latimes.com/2012/aug/02/entertainment/la-et-mn-jimmy-fallon-in-talks-to-host-2013-oscars-lorne-michaels-to-produce-20120802 }}</ref>。但是，电视节目广播公司{{lang|en|[[美国广播公司|ABC]]}}否决了这两位人选，两人之后也谢绝了邀请<ref>{{cite news|last=Newcomb |first=Tim |title=Jimmy Fallon as Oscar Host?

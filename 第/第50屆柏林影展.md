'''第50屆[[柏林影展|柏林影展]]'''（{{lang-de|'''''Internationale Filmfestspiele Berlin 2000'''''}}）於2000年2月9日至2月20日於[[德國|德國]][[柏林|柏林]]舉辦<ref name="Berlinale 2000">{{cite web |url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2000/01_jahresblatt_2000/01_Jahresblatt_2000.html|title=50th Berlin International Film Festival |accessdate=2012-10-20 |work=berlinale.de}}</ref>，開幕片為德國導演[[文·溫德斯|文·溫德斯]]執導的英語電影《{{link-en|百萬大飯店|The Million Dollar Hotel}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nitrateonline.com/2000/fberlin2K-2.html |title=Magnolia Blooms in Berlin |accessdate=1 September 2014 |work=nitrateonline.com | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071110183850/http://www.nitrateonline.com/2000/fberlin2K-2.html| archivedate=10 November 2007 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wsws.org/en/articles/2000/02/bff2-f26.html |title=The tension between cinematic vision and life itself |accessdate=1 September 2014 |work=wsws.org | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140825190445/http://www.wsws.org/en/articles/2000/02/bff2-f26.html| archivedate=25 August 2014 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>，閉幕片則是巴西導演{{link-en|布魯諾·巴雷托|Bruno Barreto}}執導的《{{link-en|都是老師惹的禍|Bossa Nova (film)}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://app1.chinadaily.com.cn/star/history/00-02-25/l01-berlin.html |title=	
'Magnolia' blooms in Berlin |accessdate=1 September 2014 |work=wsws.org | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070625162701/http://app1.chinadaily.com.cn/star/history/00-02-25/l01-berlin.html| archivedate=25 June 2007 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>。評審團主席則由中國女演員[[鞏俐|鞏俐]]擔任<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2000/04_jury_2000/04_Jury_2000.html |title=JURIES 2000 |accessdate=9 June 2014 |work=berlinale.de | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20131023083300/http://www.berlinale.de/en/archiv/jahresarchive/2000/04_jury_2000/04_Jury_2000.html| archivedate=9 June 2014 <!--DASHBot-->| deadurl= no}}</ref>。

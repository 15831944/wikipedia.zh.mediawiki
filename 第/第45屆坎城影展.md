'''第45屆[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]'''於1992年5月7日至5月18日在[[法國|法國]][[坎城|坎城]]舉辦，開幕片為荷蘭導演[[保罗·费尔胡芬|保羅·范赫文]]執導的《[[第六感追緝令|第六感追緝令]]》<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1992/05/17/movies/film-view-on-the-beach-at-cannes-with-the-american-force.html |title=Film View- On the Beach at Cannes With the American Force |work=nytimes.com |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref>，閉幕片則為美國導演[[朗·侯活|朗·霍華]]執導的《[[遠離家園|遠離家園]]》<ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/1992-05-19/news/9202140889_1_barton-fink-cannes-film-festival-palme |title=August Film Tops Cannes |work=chicagotribune.com |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://articles.baltimoresun.com/1992-04-24/features/1992115059_1_cannes-film-festival-festival-jury-abel-ferrara |title=U.S. films will dominate Cannes film festival |work=baltimoresun.com |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref>，評審團主席由法國演員[[傑哈·德巴狄厄|傑哈·德巴狄厄]]擔任<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1992/juryLongFilm.html |title=All Juries 1992 |work=festival-cannes.fr |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304085124/http://www.festival-cannes.fr/en/archives/1992/allJury.html |archivedate=4 March 2016}}</ref>。

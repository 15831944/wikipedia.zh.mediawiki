'''第五大道长老会教堂'''（Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church，FAPC）是美国最大的[[长老会|长老会]]堂会之一，成立于1808年<ref name="Histbox">{{cite web|url=http://www.thehistorybox.com/ny_city/nycity_worship_fifth_ave_church_presby_article00557.htm|title=Fifth Avenue Church: Presbyterian|date=2007-03-15|publisher=The History Box|accessdate=2008-05-14}}</ref> 称为柏树街长老会教堂（Cedar Street Presbyterian Church）<ref>[http://www.fapc.org/about-us/our-church/history Our Church History] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120828032930/http://www.fapc.org/about-us/our-church/history |date=2012-08-28 }}</ref> 自1875年起坐落在[[曼哈顿中城|曼哈顿中城]]的第五大道与55街转角<ref name="Tomfletch">{{cite web|url=http://www.nyc-architecture.com/MID/MID041.htm|title=Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church|publisher=Tom Fletcher's New York Architecture|accessdate=2008-05-14}}</ref> 。

第五大道长老会教堂长期以高标准的讲道和音乐著称。

第五大道长老会教堂以其优良的音响效果，古老的煤气灯著称。该堂曾经帮助创立了普林斯顿神学院、长老会医院等。<ref name="Time">{{cite news|url=http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,748552,00.html|title=Call to Fifth Avenue|publisher=Time|accessdate=2008-05-14 | date=1935-03-18}}</ref>

'''森林眼鏡蛇'''（[[學名|學名]]：'''''{{lang|la|Naja melanoleuca}}'''''），又名'''黑白眼鏡蛇'''<ref name="nm">{{cite web|title=Naja melanoleuca|url=http://www.toxinology.com/fusebox.cfm?fuseaction=main.snakes.display&id=SN0183|work=Clinical Toxinology Resource|publisher=University of Adelaide（[[阿德雷得大學|阿德雷得大學]]）|accessdate=2013-11-15|accessdate=2014-03-22}}</ref>，為一種分佈於[[非洲|非洲]]中西部的毒蛇，常見於低地[[森林|森林]]及[[熱帶草原|熱帶草原]]，但亦能適應更乾燥的[[生境|生境]]及擅長游泳。<ref name="Oshea">{{cite book | title=Venomous Snakes of the World | publisher=New Holland Publishers | last=O'Shea | first=M. | year=2005 | location=United Kingdom | page=71 | isbn=0-691-12436-1}}</ref>其為[[眼鏡蛇屬|眼鏡蛇屬]]中體型最大的成員，體長可達3.1[[米|米]]，也是非洲最大的[[眼鏡蛇|眼鏡蛇]]。<ref name=Burton>{{cite book|last=Burton|first=M.|title=International Wildlife Encyclopedia|year=2002|publisher=Marshall Cavendish Corp; 3rd edition|location=United States|isbn=0-7614-7270-3|pages=481–482}}</ref>此蛇食性廣泛，[[獵物|獵物]]涵蓋[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]及各類體型適合的[[爬行動物|爬行]]與[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]。<ref name="nm"/>這種眼鏡蛇機警、敏捷、危險性高<ref name=Oshea/><ref name=Snakes>{{cite web|last=Haji |first=R. |title=Venomous snakes and snake bite |url=http://www.zoocheck.com/Reportpdfs/Venomous%20snakes.pdf |work=Zoocheck Canada Inc. |page=14 |accessdate=2012-02-27 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120425231856/http://www.zoocheck.com/Reportpdfs/Venomous%20snakes.pdf |archivedate=2012-04-25 }}</ref>，在受騷擾時會鼓起頸部並抬起前半身，作出攻擊的姿勢。由於不同因素，被森林眼鏡蛇咬傷的人不多，但一旦被咬則需盡快就醫，因其可危及生命。<ref name=Davidson>{{cite web|title=Immediate First Aid for bites by the Forest cobra (Naja melanoleuca)|url=http://toxicology.ucsd.edu/Snakebite%20Protocols/Naja1.htm|work=Toxicology|publisher=University of California, San Diego|accessdate=2014-04-26|accessdate=2014-03-22|quote=Clinical experience with Naja melanoleuca has been very sparse, and few recorded bites have been documented.}}</ref>

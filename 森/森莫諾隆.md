'''森莫諾隆'''是[[柬埔寨|柬埔寨]][[蒙多基里省|蒙多基里省]]的省會及唯一的大型城鎮。2009年居民數達到7,500人。

森莫諾隆居住著土著的Pnong人。<ref>[http://www.tourismcambodia.com/travelguides/provinces/mondulkiri/ Welcome To Mondulkiri, Cambodia], Tourism Cambodia.</ref>

因為發展太快和土地投機，森莫諾隆的土地價格迅速上漲。<ref>[http://asiaviews.org/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=11748:featuresalias2082&catid=5:features&Itemid=27 Tourists and lowlanders threaten hill-tribe ways], AsiaViews, December 2010.</ref>

Sroh-Plom山是一座位於森莫諾隆附近的[[乳房形山|乳房形山]]，Phong人認為這座山是神聖的并每年在此舉辦節日。<ref>[http://www.thefreelibrary.com/Cambodian+Resort+%22Virtuous+Woman's+Breast%22+Mountain-a01073746871 Vicheka Lay, ''An introduction to Cambodia''s hidden resorts''], The Free Library.</ref>

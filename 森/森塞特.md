==地理==
日落镇位于{{coord|35|13|18|N|90|12|18|W|type:city}} (35.221540, -90.204965)<ref name="GR1">{{cite web|url=http://www.census.gov/geo/www/gazetteer/gazette.html|publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2011-04-23|date=2011-02-12|title=US Gazetteer files: 2010, 2000, and 1990}}</ref>，坐落于克里登县中东部。本镇四周被[[马里昂市,阿肯色州|马里昂市]]环绕，距东南方[[孟菲斯城，田纳西州|孟菲斯城]]{{convert|12|mi}}远，经镇西的[[55号州际公路|55号州际公路]]可达。

根据[[美国人口统计局|美国人口统计局]]的统计数据,日落镇的总面积为{{convert|0.53|km2|disp=flip}},全部为陆地。<ref name="Census 2010"/>

==人口==
As of the [[census|census]]<ref name="GR2">{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov|publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]]|accessdate=2008-01-31|title=American FactFinder}}</ref> of 2000, there were 348 people, 135 households, and 85 families residing in the city.  The [[population_density|population density]] was 1,676.8 people per square mile (639.8/km²).  There were 156 housing units at an average density of 751.7/sq mi (286.8/km²).  The racial makeup of the city was 4.89% [[Race_(United_States_Census)|White]], 91.09% [[Race_(United_States_Census)|Black]] or [[Race_(United_States_Census)|African American]], 2.87% from [[Race_(United_States_Census)|other races]], and 1.15% from two or more races.  4.02% of the population were [[Race_(United_States_Census)|Hispanic]] or [[Race_(United_States_Census)|Latino]] of any race.

There were 135 households out of which 26.7% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 22.2% were [[Marriage|married couples]] living together, 34.8% had a female householder with no husband present, and 37.0% were non-families. 33.3% of all households were made up of individuals and 13.3% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older.  The average household size was 2.58 and the average family size was 3.35.

In the city the population was spread out with 29.6% under the age of 18, 8.0% from 18 to 24, 25.6% from 25 to 44, 20.7% from 45 to 64, and 16.1% who were 65 years of age or older.  The median age was 37 years. For every 100 females there were 87.1 males.  For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 84.2 males.

本镇的住户年收入中位数为17,788美元，家庭年收入中位数为17,250美元。男性居民的年收入中位数为21,750美元，与之相比女性居民则为20,625美元。日落镇的[[人均年收入|人均年收入]]为7,766美元。本镇约44.4%的家庭和49.9%的人口生活在[[贫困线|贫困线]]以下，而且79.6%的贫困人口为不满18岁的未成年人，27.9%则为65岁以上的老人。

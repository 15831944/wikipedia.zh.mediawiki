'''森特维尔'''（Centerville）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[阿珀努斯县_(艾奥瓦州)|阿珀努斯县]]的城市和县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=2011-06-07 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531210815/http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=May 31, 2011 }}</ref>[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]时人口为5528人，比2000年的5924人少。<ref name="USCB">{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |title=Population & Housing Occupancy Status 2010 |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] American FactFinder |accessdate=April 28, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721034521/http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |archivedate=July 21, 2011 }}</ref><ref name="IADC">{{cite web|url=http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |title=Data from the 2010 Census |publisher=State Data Center of Iowa |accessdate=April 28, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718201045/http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |archivedate=July 18, 2011 }}</ref>二十世纪之交，森特维尔的煤矿业蓬勃发展，吸引了许多欧洲移民。 现在仍然是许多瑞典裔美国人，[[意大利裔美国人|意大利裔美国人]]，克罗地亚裔美国人，阿尔巴尼亚裔美国人和其他在矿工工作的移民后裔的家乡。

'''舍'''（{{lang|nan|Sia}}）是[[荷屬東印度|荷屬東印度]]時期的[[華人|華人]]世襲[[稱謂|稱謂]]，屬於高層的華人稱謂（詳見[[甲必丹|甲必丹]]）<ref name="Sidharta (2003)">{{cite book|last1=Blussâe|first1=Lâeonard|last2=Chen|first2=Menghong|author3=Myra Sidharta|authorlink1=The Role of the Go-Between in Chinese Marriages in Batavia|title=The Archives of the Kong Koan of Batavia|date=2003|publisher=BRILL|location=Amsterdam|isbn=9004131574|url=https://books.google.com/books?hl=id&id=WTnrUMIpwIYC&dq=archives+of+the+kong+koan+batavia&q=sia#v=snippet&q=sia&f=false|accessdate=9 May 2017|language=en}}</ref><ref name="Pax Benedanto & Marcus A. S. (2002)">{{cite book|last1=Benedanto|first1=Pax|last2=A. S.|first2=Marcus|title=Kesastraan Melayu Tionghoa|date=2002|publisher=Kepustakaan Populer Gramedia bekerjasama dengan Yayasan Adikarya IKAPI dan the Ford Foundation|location=Jakarta|isbn=9789799023360|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=IphkAAAAMAAJ&q=%22gelar+sia%22&dq=%22gelar+sia%22&hl=en&sa=X&redir_esc=y|accessdate=9 May 2017|language=id}}</ref>。這個稱謂源於[[閩南語|閩南語]]中對男子的敬稱。許多擁有此一稱號的人後來都接受荷蘭殖民當局的職務<ref name="Lohanda (1996)">{{cite book|last1=Lohanda|first1=Mona|title=The Kapitan Cina of Batavia, 1837-1942: A History of Chinese Establishment in Colonial Society|date=1996|publisher=Djambatan|location=Jakarta|isbn=9789794282571|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=xKlwAAAAMAAJ&q=kapitan+cina+of+batavia&dq=kapitan+cina+of+batavia&hl=en&sa=X&redir_esc=y|accessdate=9 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>。

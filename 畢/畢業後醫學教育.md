'''畢業後醫學教育'''（'''Graduate Medical Education'''）簡稱'''GME'''，在美國是指獲得[[醫學博士|醫學博士]]（M.D.）或{{le|骨科医学博士|Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine}}（D.O.）學位後的正式醫學教育，一般是由醫院贊助的教育，或是以醫院為基礎的教育。教育內容包括{{le|住院醫師實習期|internship (medicine)|實習期}}、{{le|住院醫師|residency (medicine)}}、分科以及專科住院醫師（fellowship program），最後目標是所在州行醫執照（state licensure）以及專科執照（board certification）<ref>[http://www.ama-assn.org/ama/pub/education-careers/graduate-medical-education.page Graduate Medical Education (American Medical Association)]</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.osteopathic.org/inside-aoa/Education/OGME-development-initiative/Pages/what-is-osteopathic-graduate-medical-education.aspx |title=What is Osteopathic Graduate Medical Education |accessdate=2011-12-07 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110615213636/http://www.osteopathic.org/inside-aoa/Education/OGME-development-initiative/Pages/what-is-osteopathic-graduate-medical-education.aspx |archivedate=2011-06-15 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。

[[Image:Giant_Haasts_eagle_attacking_New_Zealand_moa.jpg|thumb]]島上的[[恐鳥|恐鳥]]與[[哈斯特鷹|哈斯特鷹]]（均已絕種）]]
'''島嶼巨型化'''（{{lang-en|Island gigantism}}）是指孤岛上動物变得巨大化的生态现象。當[[人類|人類]]或是其他[[掠食者|掠食者]]（例如：[[狗|狗]]、[[貓|貓]]、[[老鼠|老鼠]]、[[豬|豬]]）來到這些島嶼時，這些巨型動物與島嶼上的特有種往往會因此[[絕種|絕種]]。

==可能成因==
通常出现島嶼巨型化的地方没有大型肉食性哺乳动物，出现的地方多数是飘移的孤島，而牠們[[顶级掠食者|顶级掠食者]]的[[生態位|生態位]]會被當地的鳥類、爬蟲類與小型肉食動物取代，例如：[[哈斯特鷹|哈斯特鷹]]与[[馬島長尾狸貓|馬島長尾狸貓]]。

較小的體型通常能幫助[[草食動物|草食動物]]逃離掠食者，而當島上缺乏掠食者的威脅時，這些草食動物的體型也隨之巨型化<ref name = "Herczeg2009">{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1111/j.1558-5646.2009.00781.x| pmid = 19624722| title = Evolution of Gigantism in Nine-Spined Sticklebacks| journal = [[Evolution_(journal)|Evolution]]| volume = 63| issue = 12| pages = 3190–3200| date = 2009-07-16| last1 = Herczeg | first1 = G. B. | last2 = Gonda | first2 = A. L. | last3 = Merilä | first3 = J. }}</ref>{{efn | 島上缺乏掠食者的威脅時，島上的動物也會變得較溫馴而缺乏警戒心<ref name="Cooper2014">{{Cite journal | last1 = Cooper | first1 = W. E. | last2 = Pyron | first2 = R. A. | last3 = Garland | first3 = T. | doi = 10.1098/rspb.2013.3019 | title = Island tameness: Living on islands reduces flight initiation distance | journal = Proceedings of the Royal Society B: Biological Sciences | volume = 281 | issue = 1777 | pages = 20133019 | date = 2014-01-08| pmid =  24403345| pmc = 3896029}}</ref><ref name="Yong2014">{{Cite journal | last1 = Yong | first1 = E. | doi = 10.1038/nature.2014.14462 | title = Islands make animals tamer | journal = Nature | date = 2014-01-08| pmid =  | pmc = }}</ref>。}}，其他可能的原因是因為缺乏與其他大型草食動物的競爭壓力。

因此，島嶼巨大化通常為缺乏限制這些動物體型因素（例如：天敵掠食或食物、棲地競爭）時的演化趨勢<ref name = "Barahona2000">{{cite journal|last1=Barahona|first1=F.|last2=Evans|first2=S.E.|last3=Mateo|first3=J.A.|last4=Garcia-Marquez|first4=M.|last5=Lopez-Jurado|first5=L.F.|title=Endemism, Gigantism and Extinction in Island Lizards: The Genus ''Gallotia'' on the Canary Islands|journal=Journal of Zoology|date=March 2000|volume=250|issue=3|pages=373–388|url= http://journals.cambridge.org/article_S0952836900003101|doi=10.1017/s0952836900003101|hdl=10553/19918}}</ref>。但是由於缺乏掠食者的威脅，有些大型草食動物來到島嶼後反而傾向演化出較小的體型，稱為[[島嶼侏儒化|島嶼侏儒化]]<ref name = "Raia">{{cite journal| last = Raia | first = P. | authorlink = |author2=Meiri, S.| title = The island rule in large mammals: paleontology meets ecology| journal = [[Evolution_(journal)|Evolution]]| volume = 60 | issue = 8 | pages = 1731–1742| date = August 2006 | language = | doi = 10.1111/j.0014-3820.2006.tb00516.x}}</ref>。此外，由於島上獵物資源的限制，許多掠食者的體型也會隨之侏儒化<ref name = "Raia"/>。

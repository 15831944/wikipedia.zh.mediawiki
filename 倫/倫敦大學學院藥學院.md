'''倫敦大學學院藥學院'''（'''{{lang|en|UCL School of Pharmacy}}'''）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ucl.ac.uk/pharmacy/about|title=About the UCL School of Pharmacy|publisher=University College London|accessdate=2015-04-15}}</ref>，是由[[英國皇家藥物協會|英國皇家藥物協會]]（The Royal Pharmaceutical Society of Great Britain，RPSGB）於1842年所創立之皇家藥物協會學院(College of the Pharmaceutical Society)，創校校址為No 17, Bloomsbury Square, London，自1925年起開始授予[[倫敦大學|倫敦大學]]學歷。
<br>

自1935年起，於[[倫敦|倫敦]]29/39 Brunswick Square啟動新校區建築工程，該工程於1939因[[第二次世界大戰|第二次世界大戰]]而停工，該校也在二戰期間遷校自卡爾地夫(Cardiff)直到1943年9月始遷回[[倫敦|倫敦]]，並於1949年正式脫離皇家藥物協會而成為[[倫敦大學|倫敦大學]]轄下其中的一所專門學院，為[[英國|英國]]第一所頒授[[藥學|藥學]]學位之教育機構，同年更名為更名為[[倫敦大學藥學院|倫敦大學藥學院]](The School of Pharmacy, University of London 或縮寫為ULSOP或SOP)，該校[[紋章|紋章]]於1950年誕生，其上註有拉丁文Salutifer Orbi，英文翻譯為 Bringing health to the world，也就是將健康帶來這個世界之[[校訓|校訓]](motto)。[[皇家特許狀|皇家特許狀]]([[Royal_Charter|Royal Charter]])於1952年獲頒。位於29/39 Brunswick Square的新校建築終於在1960年完工啟用，並由[[伊莉莎白王太后|伊莉莎白王太后]](Queen Elizabeth，The Queen Mother 時任倫敦大學校長)舉行落成啟用典禮，[[伊莉莎白王太后|伊莉莎白王太后]]在落成典禮上稱之為倫敦當年最古老的新建築(因其始造於1938年)，該建築亦成為英國史上第一間專門為[[藥學院|藥學院]]而修建的[[建物|建物]]。由於該校校址皆位於倫敦的Squares旁，該校師生與校友亦暱稱該校為"The Square"，該校之正方形校徽也因此而來。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ucl.ac.uk/pharmacy/about/history-school|title=History of the School|publisher=University College London|accessdate=2019-01-06}}</ref>
<br>

經過長時間和廣泛的咨詢，在該校師生抗議未果之下，倫敦大學藥學院在2011年5月13日正式公布與[[倫敦大學學院|倫敦大學學院]]的合併計劃，最後一屆[[倫敦大學藥學院|倫敦大學藥學院]]畢業證書於2011年9月頒發。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/news-articles/1105/11051201|title=School of Pharmacy will merge with UCL|publisher=University College London|accessdate=2015-04-15}}</ref>有關合併計劃於2012年1月起生效，該校改隸[[倫敦大學學院|倫敦大學學院]]，並更為現名([[倫敦大學學院|倫敦大學學院]] 藥學院 [[UCL_School_of_Pharmacy|UCL School of Pharmacy]])，自2012年起改與頒發[[倫敦大學學院|倫敦大學學院]]之畢業證書，校友亦併入[[倫敦大學學院|倫敦大學學院]]管理。

The shield has a blue band across the top and a blue diagonal band running from the top right-hand corner* on either side of which stands a mortar and pestle in gold on a silvery white ground.

On the diagonal band is the Staff of Asclepius, entwined by a serpent in gold.
Upon the transverse blue band is in the centre a Tudor rose surrounded by golden rays and on either side of it an open book, with gilt edges and clasps, these being part of the arms of the University of London.

The helm is the plain-helm of an esquire and on top of the helm is the crest consisting of two poppy heads with stalks crossed in the form of a St Andrew's cross, arranged in front of a foxglove. Covering the helm behind is the mantle. This consists of stylized foliage ending in two tassels on either side below the motto 'Salutifer Orbi' and two more tassels on either side of the helm.
*In heraldic decriptions, the right- and left-hand sides of a shield are indicated in the same way as the right and left of the person carrying the shield and not from the point of view of the observer.

==學科==
[[File:School_of_pharmacy_mckay.jpg|right]]
The School is organised into four academic departments, each with one or more associated specialist research centres.

===藥物化學與生物化學===
The Department of Pharmaceutical and Biological Chemistry is the largest of the School's departments. Its research is focused on cancer, natural products and phytomedicines, molecular neurosciences and biopharmaceutical analysis.

The department's staff help teach the undergraduate MPharm degree in the areas of drug discovery, medicinal chemistry, pharmaceutical chemistry and pharmacognosy/medicinal plants, and an MSc in Pharmacognosy is offered.

===藥理學===
The Wellcome Department of Pharmacology is one of the oldest departments of pharmacology in the UK. The department has played a major role in the development of Pharmacology in the UK and many pharmacologists who trained here are to be found in academies and in industries all over the world.

The department's research focuses on the nervous system, and a wide range of approaches are used to study normal brain function and the causes of many neurological and psychiatric diseases.

===藥劑學===
The Department of Pharmaceutics is home to a wide range of research activities, such as in Materials Science and Processing and Clinical Pharmaceutical Science.

The department's research in Materials Science and Processing is centred on the fundamental properties of materials and their adaptation to optimise processing and enhance drug delivery. It operates a number of joint ventures, including the Centre for Paediatric Pharmacy Research, a joint venture with Great Ormond Street Hospital and the Institute of Child Health, and in the Clinical Pharmaceutics with University College Hospitals and Camden and Islington's NHS Trust.

The Microbiology Research Group is also well-established, with work focusing mainly in overcoming antibiotic resistance and obtaining new actives from natural sources. The Group has been particularly successful in investigating new approaches to the treatment of the ‘superbug’ [[Methicillin-resistant_Staphylococcus_aureus|MRSA]].

===Practice and Policy===
The Department of Practice and Policy focuses upon making the use of medicines safer and more effective through teaching, service and research.

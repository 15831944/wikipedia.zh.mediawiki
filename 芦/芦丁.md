'''芦丁'''（{{lang-en|Rutin}}，也叫'''芸香苷'''、'''路丁'''、'''络通'''或'''槲皮素-3-O-芸香糖苷'''）是[[黄酮醇|黄酮醇]][[槲皮素|槲皮素]]与[[二糖|二糖]][[芸香二糖|芸香二糖]]（''α''-<small>L</small>-鼠李吡喃糖基-(1→6))-''β''-<small>D</small>-葡萄吡喃糖）之间形成的[[糖苷|糖苷]]。在巴西芸香（''Dimorphandra mollis''）中，芦丁的合成是经过[[芦丁合酶|芦丁合酶]]的活性而完成的<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://cat.inist.fr/?aModele=afficheN&cpsidt=21997861|title=Rutin synthase in fava d'anta: Purification and influence of stressors|author=Lucci|author2=Mazzafera|journal=Canadian journal of plant science|year=2009|volume=89|issue=5|pages=895–902|doi=10.4141/CJPS09001}}</ref>。

芦丁是一种存在于[[黏核桃|黏核桃]]中的主要的黄酮醇之一<ref name=Chang>Low-Density Lipoprotein Antioxidant Activity of Phenolic Compounds and Polyphenol Oxidase Activity in Selected Clingstone Peach Cultivars. Susan Chang, Christine Tan, Edwin N. Frankel and Diane M. Barrett, J. Agric. Food Chem., 2000, 48, pages 147−151, {{doi|10.1021/jf990456}}</ref>。

== 化学亲属 ==
芦丁（槲皮素芸香糖苷），与[[槲皮苷|槲皮苷]]一样，是一种[[黄酮类化合物|黄酮类化合物]]槲皮素的糖苷。就其本身说，他们的化学结构式是非常相似的，然而所携带的[[羟基|羟基]]并不一样。槲皮素和芦丁两者都被多国作为提供血管保护的用药，且是多种多维生素制剂与草药疗法的成分。
芦丁存在于多种植物，特别是蓼科的荞麦中<ref>Kreft S, Knapp M, Kreft I. Extraction of rutin from buckwheat (Fagopyrum esculentum Moench) seeds and determination by capillary electrophoresis. J Agric Food Chem 1999 ; 47 (11):4649–4652.</ref><ref name="naturalstandard">http://www.naturalstandard.com/index-abstract.asp?create-abstract=/monographs/herbssupplements/patient-rutin.asp</ref>。

==作为配体==
芦丁可以结合一些[[阳离子|阳离子]]{{Which?|date=June 2009}}，以从[[土壤|土壤]]吸取营养供应[[植物|植物]]细胞{{Citation needed|date=June 2009}}。在人体中，它可以结合二价铁离子（[[铁|Fe]]<sup>2+</sup>），从而防止它与[[过氧化氢|过氧化氢]]相结合，否则后者可能会产生高反应性的[[自由基|自由基]]，而自由基会损伤细胞。芦丁也是一种[[抗氧化剂|抗氧化剂]]。

此外，在[[in_vitro|体外]]中显示出芦丁可以将[[血管内皮生长因子|血管内皮生长因子]]<ref>{{cite journal|author=Luo|author2=et al.|title=Inhibition of Cell Growth and VEGF Expression in Ovarian Cancer Cells by Flavonoids|journal=Nutrition and Cancer|year=2008|volume=60|issue=6|pages=800–9|doi=10.1080/01635580802100851|pmid=19005980|last3=King|first3=Sarah M.|last4=Chen|first4=Yi Charlie}}</ref>抑制在低毒性浓度下，因此它可以作为一种[[血管发生|血管发生]]抑制剂。这一结果可潜在地被用于相关[[癌症|癌症]]的控制上。

==健康效应==
在小鼠<ref>{{cite journal|author=Enkhmaa|author2= et al.|pmid=15795425|title=Mulberry (Morus alba L.) leaves and their major flavonol quercetin 3-(6-malonylglucoside) attenuate atherosclerotic lesion development in LDL receptor-deficient mice|journal=J Nutr|year=2005|volume=135|issue=4|pages=729–34|last3=Katsube|first3=T|last4=Kitajima|first4=K|last5=Anuurad|first5=E|last6=Yamasaki|first6=M|last7=Yamane|first7=Y}}</ref>、大鼠
<ref>{{cite journal|author=Santos|author2=et al.|title=HYPOLIPIDAEMIC EFFECTS OF NARINGENIN, RUTIN, NICOTINIC ACID AND THEIR ASSOCIATIONS|journal=Pharmacological Research|volume=40|issue=6|year=1999|pages=493–6|doi=10.1006/phrs.1999.0556|pmid=10660947|last3=Nagem|first3=TJ|last4=Pinto|first4=AS|last5=Oliveira|first5=MG}}</ref>、仓鼠<ref>{{cite journal|author=Auger|author2=et al.|title=Dietary wine phenolics catechin, quercetin, and resveratrol efficiently protect hypercholesterolemic hamsters against aortic fatty streak accumulation|journal=J Agric Food Chem.|year= 2005|volume=53|issue=6|pages=2015–21|pmid=15769129|doi=10.1021/jf048177q|last3=Gérain|first3=Peggy|last4=Lequeux|first4=Nadine|last5=Bornet|first5=Aurélie|last6=Serisier|first6=Samuel|last7=Besançon|first7=Pierre|last8=Caporiccio|first8=Bertrand|last9=Cristol|first9=Jean-Paul}}</ref>与兔子<ref>{{cite journal|author=Juźwiak|author2=et al.|title=Effect of quercetin on experimental hyperlipidemia and atherosclerosis in rabbits|journal=Pharmacol Rep.|year=2005|volume=57|issue=5|pages=604–9|pmid=16227643|last3=Mokrzycki|first3=K|last4=Marchlewicz|first4=M|last5=Białecka|first5=M|last6=Wenda-Rózewicka|first6=L|last7=Gawrońska-Szklarz|first7=B|last8=Droździk|first8=M}}</ref>以及[[in_vitro|离体]]研究<ref>{{cite journal|author=Shen|author2=et al.|title=In vitro and in vivo inhibitory activities of rutin, wogonin, and quercetin on lipopolysaccharide-induced nitric oxide and prostaglandin E2 production|journal=European Journal of Pharmacology|year=2002|volume=446|issue=1–3|pages=187–94|doi=10.1016/S0014-2999(02)01792-2|pmid=12098601|last3=Lin|first3=HY|last4=Huang|first4=HC|last5=Ko|first5=CH|last6=Yang|first6=LL|last7=Chen|first7=YC}}</ref>中得到了芦丁与[[槲皮素|槲皮素]]效果的证据，并无临床研究表明芦丁作为一种膳食补充品在人体中有的显著的且良好的效果。

*芦丁抑制[[血小板|血小板]]凝集<ref>{{cite journal|author=Navarro-Núñez|author2=et al.|doi=10.1021/jf0723209|title=Apigenin Inhibits Platelet Adhesion and Thrombus Formation and Synergizes with Aspirin in the Suppression of the Arachidonic Acid Pathway|journal=J. Agric. Food Chem.|year=2008|volume=56|issue=9|pages=2970–6|last3=Palomo|first3=M.|last4=Martínez|first4=C.|last5=Vicente|first5=V.|last6=Castillo|first6=J.|last7=Benavente-García|first7=O.|last8=Diaz-Ricart|first8=M.|last9=Escolar|first9=G.|pmid=18410117}}</ref>，同时减少毛细血管的通透性，使得血液更稀薄些且能促进循环{{Citation needed|date=October 2010}}。
*芦丁在某些动物与离体模型中显示出[[抗炎性|抗炎性]]<ref>{{cite journal|author=Guardia|author2=et al.|title=Anti-inflammatory properties of plant flavonoids. Effects of rutin, quercetin and hesperidin on adjuvant arthritis in rat|journal=Il Farmaco|volume=56|issue=9|year=2001|pages=683–7|doi=10.1016/S0014-827X(01)01111-9|pmid=11680812|last3=Juarez|first3=AO|last4=Pelzer|first4=LE}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Chan Hun Jung|author2=et al.|title=Anti-asthmatic action of quercetin and rutin in conscious guinea-pigs challenged with aerosolized ovalbumin|journal=Arch. Pharmacal Research|volume=30|issue=12|pages=1599–1607|url=http://www.springerlink.com/content/3265v57604507v87|doi=10.1007/BF02977330|year=2007|last3=Cho|first3=Chul Hyung|last4=Kim|first4=Chang Jong}}</ref>。
*芦丁抑制[[醛糖还原酶|醛糖还原酶]]的活性{{Citation needed|date=October 2010}}。醛糖还原酶是一种通常存在于眼睛与人体其他部位的酶，这种酶使得葡萄糖转化为[[山梨醇|山梨醇]]这种糖醇。
*芦丁也会强化[[毛细血管|毛细血管]]{{Citation needed|date=October 2010}}，故因此也会缓解[[血友病|血友病]]的症状。它也会帮助阻止常见的、不好看的腿部静脉[[水肿|水肿]]；然而，对含有芦丁的荞麦茶功效的一项双盲临床研究并未显示出高于安慰剂组的显著效果<ref>Ihme et al. "Leg oedema protection from a buckwheat herb tea in patients with chronic venous insufficiency: a single-centre, randomised, double-blind, placebo-controlled clinical trial", European Journal of Clinical Pharmacology, 50(6):443-447, [http://www.springerlink.com/content/0uayqpwqj3gkrr2p/ doi:10.1007/s002280050138]</ref>。
* 芦丁，就像[[阿魏酸|阿魏酸]]一样，可以减少被氧化的[[低密度脂蛋白|低密度脂蛋白]]所造成的细胞毒性，且会降低[[心脏病|心脏病]]的风险{{Citation needed|date=October 2010}}。
*有一些证据显示芦丁可以被用来治疗[[血友病|血友病]]、[[静脉曲张|静脉曲张]]以及[[微血管病|微血管病]]<ref name="naturalstandard" />。
*芦丁也是一种抗氧化剂<ref>Metodiewa et al. "Evidence for antiradical and antioxidant properties of four biologically active N,N-Diethylaminoethyl ethers of flavaone oximes: A comparison with natural polyphenolic flavonoid rutin action", IUBMB Life 1997, 41(5):1067–1075, [http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1080/15216549700202141/abstract doi:10.1080/15216549700202141]</ref>；相较于槲皮素、金合欢素、桑色素、高车前素、橘皮苷与柚皮苷来说，它被认为是最强的<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.diet-and-health.net/Supplements/Rutin.html |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2012-01-25 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111227134813/http://www.diet-and-health.net/Supplements/Rutin.html |archivedate=2011-12-27 }}</ref>。然而，在其他实验中，芦丁的效果相对于[[槲皮素|槲皮素]]的来说是较少或微乎其微的<ref>Bando et al. "Ingested quercetin but not rutin increases accumulation of hepatic β-carotene in BALB/c mice" Mol. Nutr. Food Res. 54(S2):S261-7 [http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/mnfr.200900329/full doi:10.1002/mnfr.200900329]</ref><ref>Chow et al. "Quercetin, but not rutin and quercitrin, prevention of H2O2-induced apoptosis via anti-oxidant activity and heme oxygenase 1 gene expression in macrophages", Biochemical Pharmacology 2005,
69(12):1839-1851, [http://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.bcp.2005.03.017 doi:10.1016/j.bcp.2005.03.017]</ref>。
[[羟乙基芦丁|羟乙基芦丁]]，是芦丁的一种合成的羟乙基酰化产物，被用于下肢静脉功能不全的治疗。

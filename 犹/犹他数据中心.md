'''犹他数据中心''' ('''Utah Data Center'''), 也称'''情报体系综合性国家计算机安全计划数据中心''' ('''Intelligence Community Comprehensive National Cybersecurity Initiative Data Center'''),<ref name="facs1">{{cite news |title = NSA Utah Data Center |url = http://www.facilitiesmagazine.com/utah/buildings/nsa-utah-data-center |newspaper = Facilities Magazine |date = 14 September 2011 |accessdate = 24 April 2013}}</ref> 是[[美国情报体系|美国情报体系]]使用的一座数据存储设施, 拥有[[尧字节|尧字节]] (yottabyte) 级的设计存储能力 (1 尧字节即 10<sup>24</sup> 字节)。<ref name="bamford1">{{cite news |title = The NSA Is Building the Country’s Biggest Spy Center (Watch What You Say) |first = James |last = Bamford |authorlink = James Bamford |url = http://www.wired.com/threatlevel/2012/03/ff_nsadatacenter/all/1 |newspaper = Wired Magazine |date = 15 March 2012 |accessdate = 5 April 2012
}}</ref><ref name="Kenyon" /> 它的目的是支持综合性国家计算机安全计划 (Comprehensive National Cybersecurity Initiative, CNCI), 但其具体职责则属于保密信息。<ref name="deseret1">{{cite news |title = Utah's $1.5 billion cyber-security center under way
|first = Steve |last = Fidel |authorlink = Steve Fidel |url = http://www.deseretnews.com/article/705363940/Utahs-15-billion-cyber-security-center-under-way.html |newspaper = Deseret News |date = 6 Jane 2011 |accessdate = 29 March 2013}}</ref> [[美国国家安全局|美国国家安全局]]将负责领导该设施的运作, 也是国家情报总监 (DNI) 的执行机构。<ref name="reuters1">{{cite news |title = U.S. agency denies data center to monitor citizens' emails |url = http://news.yahoo.com/u-agency-denies-data-center-monitor-citizens-emails-004448507.html |newspaper = [[Reuters|Reuters]] |date = 15 April 2013 |accessdate = 16 April 2013}}</ref> 

该数据中心位於威廉姆斯营 (Camp Williams), 靠近[[犹他州|犹他州]][[布拉夫戴尔_(犹他州)|布拉夫戴尔市]], 地处[[犹他湖|犹他湖]]和[[大盐湖|大盐湖]]之间。

== 缘由 ==
[[File:Utah_Data_Center_Panorama.jpg|thumbnail]]
据称, 该数据中心将能够[[窃听|捕获]] “所有形式的通信的全部内容, 包括私人[[电子邮件|电子邮件]], 电话通话, 互联网搜索, 以及所有类型的个人数据记录—停车收据, 旅行日程表, 书店购物记录, 和其他的杂项记录”。<ref group="quotes">原文: “… all forms of communication, including the complete contents of private emails, cell phone calls, and Internet searches, as well as all sorts of personal data trails—parking receipts, travel itineraries, bookstore purchases, and other digital ‘pocket litter’”。</ref>

国家安全局发言人在回应有关该数据中心将被用于非法监听美国公民的电子邮件的指控时说, “人们对犹他数据中心做出了许多没有根据的指控, … 有关国家安全局的最大误解之一就是我们非法监听, 或者阅读, 美国公民的电子邮件。 事实根本并非如此。”<ref name="reuters1" />

== 规划 ==
按照规划, 该数据中心的占地 100 万到 150 万平方英尺,<ref name="Kenyon">{{cite news |url = http://defensesystems.com/Articles/2011/01/07/NSA-spy-cyber-intelligence-data-center-Utah.aspx |title=New NSA data center breaks ground on construction -- Defense Systems |last=Kenyon |first=Henry |date=Jan 7, 2011|publisher=Defense Systems|accessdate=11 August 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url = http://crave.cnet.co.uk/gadgets/nsa-to-store-yottabytes-in-utah-data-centre-49304118/ |title=NSA to store yottabytes in Utah data centre ||work=[[CNET_Networks|CNET Networks]]|accessdate=11 August 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url = http://www.nybooks.com/articles/archives/2009/nov/05/whos-in-big-brothers-database/ |title=Who’s in Big Brother’s Database? by James Bamford |last=Bamford |first=James |work=[[The_New_York_Review_of_Books|The New York Review of Books]]|accessdate=11 August 2011}}</ref> 到 2013 年 9 月完工时预计共耗资 $1,500,000,000 美元至 $2,000,000,000 美元。<ref>{{cite news |title=New NSA center unveiled in budget documents |url = http://www.sltrib.com/news/ci_12744661 |work=Salt Lake Tribune|publisher=MediaNews Group|author=LaPlante, Matthew D.|date=July 2, 2009|accessdate=2009-07-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|author=LaPlante, Matthew D.|title=Spies like us: NSA to build huge facility in Utah |url = http://www.sltrib.com/ci_12735293|date=July 2, 2009|publisher=MediaNews Group|work=Salt Lake Tribune |accessdate=2009-07-05}}</ref><ref name="deseret1" /><ref name=bamford1 /><ref name="Kenyon"/> 一份报告称, 另外 $2,000,000,000 美元将被用于硬件, 软件, 及维护开销。<ref name="Kenyon"/> 完工后的设施预计将需要 65 [[瓦特|兆瓦]]的[[功率|功率]], 耗资约每年 $40,000,000 美元。<ref name=bamford1 /><ref name="Kenyon"/>

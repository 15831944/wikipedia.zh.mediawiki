'''犹太女人玛利亚'''是[[古希腊|古希腊]]时期生活在[[亚历山大|亚历山大]]的一名女[[炼金术|煉金術士]]。约活动于前3世纪到前2世纪间。<ref>''Chemical History Tour, Picturing Chemistry from Alchemy to Modern Molecular Science'' Adele Droblas Greenberg Wiley-Interscience 2000 ISBN 0-471-35408-2</ref>她发明了数种煉金術仪器及技术，被誉为西方[[炼金术|煉金術]]的奠基人之一。<ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=LorvA_5Ex_UC&pg=PA60&dq=Maria+the+Jewess&hl=en&ei=ne5yTPe-CMP7lwe3s9S6Dg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCUQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=Maria%20the%20Jewess&f=false Patai, p. 60.]</ref>

2017年'''東阿勒頗攻勢'''（{{lang-ar|هجوم شرق حلب}}）是[[敘利亞|敘利亞]]政府軍於2017年1月17日<ref name="jan17"/>發起的軍事行動，目的是攻佔[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]在[[阿勒颇省|阿勒颇省]]的僅餘控制區，阻止[[土耳其|土耳其]]支持的[[叙利亚反对派|叙利亚反对派]]在阿勒颇省東部推進，同時控制供應[[阿勒颇|阿勒颇]]市用水的水源。<ref name="cut off Turkish Army">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-cutoff-turkish-army-southeast-aleppo-map/|title=Syrian Army to cutoff Turkish Army from southeast Aleppo: map|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=19 February 2017|publisher=}}</ref><ref name=against>{{cite web|url=http://af.reuters.com/article/worldNews/idAFKBN1650AB|title=Syrian army advances against Islamic State near Aleppo - monitor|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.breitbart.com/news/army-presses-is-in-north-syria-monitor-military-source/|title=Army presses IS in north Syria: monitor, military source - Breitbart|publisher=}}</ref>

==攻勢==
2017年1月17日，敘政府軍在[[巴卜|巴卜]]以南向伊斯蘭國發起攻勢。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://southfront.org/syrian-army-makes-first-gains-anti-isis-operation-south-al-bab|title=Syrian Army Makes First Gains In Anti-ISIS Operation South Of Al-Bab|date=18 January 2017|publisher=}}</ref>

在敘政府軍於2017年2月10日推進至距離[[巴卜|巴卜]]市不到2公里處後，<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.aljazeera.com/news/2017/02/kremlin-turkey-gave-target-mistaken-syria-strike-170210135054884.html|title=Kremlin: Turkey gave target for mistaken Syria strike|date=10 February 2017|access-date=10 February 2017|publisher=Al Jazeera}}</ref>據報土耳其支持的敘反對派在2月11日攻佔巴卜以南的一個具戰略價值的迴旋處，意味敘政府軍無法在不與反對派部隊交戰的情況下繼續向巴卜推進。敘利亞當局為阻止反對派部隊繼續向南深入，決定向Kuweires空軍基地以東的伊斯蘭國控制區進攻，以阻斷反對派南下之路。

2月12日至16日間，敘政府軍攻佔在Kuweires空軍基地東北方的9個村莊，距離伊斯蘭國控制的[[代爾哈菲爾|代爾哈菲爾]]（Dayr Hafir）鎮不到5公里。<ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=60936 The regime forces are about 5 km northwest of Deir Hafer town after controlling 9 villages in the road between it and between al-Bab area]</ref>

2月21日至25日間，敘軍再收復十餘個村莊，<ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=61567 The regime forces achieved a broad advancement in the road of al-Bab – al-Tabaqa and move towards al-Khafseh area]</ref><ref name=ontherun>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-run-syrian-army-liberates-two-villages-eastern-aleppo/|title=ISIS on the run as the Syrian Army liberates two more villages in eastern Aleppo|first=Chris|last=Tomson|date=24 February 2017|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/busy-day-in-east-aleppo-as-syrian-army-obliterates-isis/|title=Busy day in East Aleppo as Syrian Army obliterates ISIS|first=Paul|last=Antonopoulos|date=25 February 2017|publisher=}}</ref>代爾哈菲爾已處於敘軍炮火控制範圍之內。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/tiger-forces-liberate-new-village-isis-east-aleppo-map/|title=Tiger Forces liberate new village from ISIS in east Aleppo: map|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=24 February 2017|publisher=}}</ref>由於伊斯蘭國在代爾哈菲爾可能設置了大量的防禦工事，敘軍不強攻代爾哈菲爾，繞過它繼續向東推進。<ref name=ontherun/>

在土耳其支持的敘反對派[[巴卜之戰|攻佔巴卜]]後，據守巴卜以南的塔代夫（Tadef）鎮的伊斯蘭國分子已有大部分撤出。2月26日，敘軍進入塔代夫並控制該鎮。<ref name=against/><ref name="Tadef liberated">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-syrian-army-liberates-isis-held-town-neighboring-al-bab/|title=BREAKING: Syrian Army liberates ISIS-held town neighboring Al-Bab|first=Chris|last=Tomson|date=26 February 2017|publisher=}}</ref>隨後敘政府軍與反對派部隊在塔代夫附近爆發衝突。反對派宣稱打死至少22名政府軍士兵。<ref name="Rudaw">{{cite web|url=http://www.rudaw.net/english/middleeast/syria/27022017|title=Reports: Syrian rebels, loyalists clash south of al-Bab near Tedef|date=2017-02-27 |publisher=Rudaw}}</ref>

2月27日，敘政府軍再攻佔數個村莊，到達[[曼比季|曼比季]]的[[敘利亞民主力量|敘利亞民主力量]]控制區，反對派趁伊斯蘭國撤退，佔領了5個村莊。<ref name="vie">{{cite web |url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/field-report-syrian-army-turkish-backed-forces-vie-control-northern-syria-map-update/|title=Field Report: Syrian Army, Turkish-backed forces vie for control in northern Syria. Map Update|first=Zen|last=Adra|date=2017-02-27 |publisher=}}</ref><ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=61754 -{The “Islamic State” organization withdraws from 23 villages east of al-Bab city and the regime forces finish drawing their border with the “Euphrates Shield” operation according to the Turkish – Russian agreement}-]</ref>敘利亞民主力量隨後向曼比季西南和以南的伊斯蘭國分子進攻，攻佔9個村莊。<ref name="restart">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/massive-gains-isis-kurdish-troops-restart-operations-south-manbij/|title=Massive loss for ISIS as Kurdish troops restart operations south of Manbij|first=Chris|last=Tomson|date=2017-02-27 |publisher=}}</ref>

2月28日，土耳其支持的反對派部隊在Arima附近攻擊敘利亞民主力量，奪取了2個村莊。<ref name="3_villages"/><ref>{{cite web|url=https://southfront.org/pro-turkish-forces-seize-village-ypg-west-manbij-syrian-army-liberates-areas-isis/|title=Pro-Turkish forces seize village from YPG west of Manbij. Syrian Army liberates more areas from ISIS|publisher=South Front|date=2017-03-01}}</ref>

3月1日，敘民主力量從伊斯蘭國手上攻佔5個村莊，<ref name="push">[https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/turkish-army-seizes-two-villages-kurdish-forces-amid-push-towards-manbij/ Turkish Army seizes two villages from Kurdish forces amid push towards Manbij]</ref>敘政府軍攻佔1個村莊。<ref>[https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-run-east-aleppo-syrian-army-liberates-territory/ ISIS on the run in east Aleppo as the Syrian Army liberates more territory]</ref>敘反對派向[[曼比季|曼比季]]進軍，<ref name="push"/>攻佔3個原由敘民主力量控制的村莊。<ref name="3villages">[http://www.syriahr.com/en/?p=61944 Rebels advance over Menbej military council in 3 villages]</ref>

3月2日，[[土耳其|土耳其]]方面警告庫爾德族武裝離開[[曼比季|曼比季]]，否則將會進攻他們。3月6日，美國國防部發言人表示美軍已進駐曼比季執行任務，並暗示土耳其不應該對曼比季地區的敘利亞民主力量採取軍事行動。<ref name=xh20170308>{{cite news|url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/world/2017-03/08/c_129503462.htm |title=美国增兵曼比季 叙北部重镇上演“四国演义” |publisher=新華網 |date=2017-03-08}}</ref><ref name=reuters20170306>{{Cite news |url=http://www.reuters.com/article/us-mideast-crisis-syria-usa-idUSKBN16D29G?il=0 |title=U.S. military deploys forces in Syria's Manbij in new effort |publisher=Reuters |date=2017-03-06}}</ref>

3月6日，敘政府軍從伊斯蘭國手上收復6個村莊。<ref name="6villages">[https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/six-villages-liberated-eastern-aleppo-syrian-army-closes-jarrah-airbase/ Six villages liberated in eastern Aleppo as the Syrian Army closes in on Jarrah Airbase]</ref>3月7日，敘政府軍攻佔[[幼发拉底河|幼发拉底河]]西岸al-Khafsa的一個主要抽水站，該抽水站主要負責向[[阿勒颇|阿勒颇]]市供水，自從伊斯蘭國在多個星期前截斷供水後，阿勒頗市就陷入供水緊張。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.voanews.com/a/syrian-forces-capture-key-water-supply-aleppo-monitors-say/3754297.html |title=Monitors: Syrian Forces Capture Key Water Supply Near Aleppo |date=2017-03-07 |publisher=美國之音}}</ref>同日敘軍[[老虎部隊|老虎部隊]]從伊斯蘭國手上收復另外23個村莊。<ref name=jan17>{{Cite web |url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/20-villages-liberated-syrian-army-seizes-isis-pocket-stunning-aleppo-offensive/ |title=+20 villages liberated as the Syrian Army seizes ISIS pocket in stunning Aleppo offensive |date=2017-03-07 |work=Al Masdar News}}</ref>3月8日敘軍收復另外21個村莊，並控制幼發拉底河另一個抽水站。<ref name="8villages">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/photos-another-amazing-day-syrian-army-isis-faces-full-annihilation-rural-aleppo/|title=PHOTOS: Another amazing day for the Syrian Army as ISIS faces full annihilation in rural Aleppo|first=Paul|last=Antonopoulos|date=2017-03-08 |publisher=|accessdate=2017-03-11}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-seizes-control-21-settlements-24-hours/|title=Syrian Army seizes control of 21 settlements in 24 hours|first=News|last=Desk|date=2017-03-09 |publisher=|accessdate=2017-03-11}}</ref>3月9日敘軍開始進攻吉拉赫（Jirah）空軍基地，<ref name="Jirah">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-brink-liberating-key-military-airport-east-aleppo/|title=Syrian Army on the brink of liberating key military airport in east Aleppo|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=2017-03-09 |publisher=|accessdate=2017-03-11}}</ref>但是於3月10日受沙塵暴影響導致攻勢被擊退。

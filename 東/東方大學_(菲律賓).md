'''東方大學'''(''Pamantasan ng Silangan'')，簡稱'''UE'''，是一家位於[[菲律賓|菲律賓]][[馬尼拉|馬尼拉]]的私立大學。大學創立於[[1946年|1946年]]，後在[[1990年|1990年]]被富翁[[陳永栽|陳永栽]]收購。UE在高峰期曾錄取超過65,000名學生，並且被成為「亞洲最大大學」。<ref name="Manila Bulletin">{{cite news|title=University of the East: Nearly 60 years of quality education for the masses |publisher=[[Manila_Bulletin|Manila Bulletin]] |url=http://www.mb.com.ph/issues/2004/07/30/SCAU2004073015212.html |accessdate=2006-09-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20040816231724/http://www.mb.com.ph/issues/2004/07/30/SCAU2004073015212.html |archivedate=August 16, 2004 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title = UE History|publisher = University of the East|url = http://online.ue.edu.ph/manila/?page=about&link=story|accessdate = 2008-02-04|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20080222174146/http://online.ue.edu.ph/manila/?page=about&link=story|archive-date = 2008-02-22|dead-url = yes|df = }}</ref>除了本校以外，UE還有[[加洛坎菲律賓東方大學|加洛坎]]和[[東方大學拉蒙·麥格塞塞紀念醫療中心|拉蒙·麥格塞塞紀念醫療中心]]兩所分校。

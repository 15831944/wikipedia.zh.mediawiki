'''東尼·庫許納'''（Tony Kushner）是一名美國[[猶太|猶太]]裔劇作家及編劇<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://jewishjournal.com/culture/lifestyle/134378/|title=‘Finding Your Roots’ explores Jewish genealogy|author=|last=Miller|first=Gerri|date=October 23, 2014|work=|newspaper=Jewish Journal|accessdate=June 5, 2018|language=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2011/may/05/tony-kushner-university-snub-israel|title=University snub for 'anti-Israel' playwright Tony Kushner|accessdate=June 5, 2018|author=|date=May 5, 2011|last=Harris|first=Paul|work=The Guardian|publisher=|language=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://jewishjournal.com/cover_story/98640/|title=Tony Kushner awarded $100,000 prize for challenging status quo|author=|last=Berrin|first=Danielle|date=November 29, 2011|work=|newspaper=Jewish Journal|accessdate=June 5, 2018|language=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/stage/2017/may/14/tony-kushner-interview-angels-in-america-to-love-someone-recognise-life-not-eternal-national-theatre|title=Tony Kushner: ‘To love someone puts you at the risk of loss’|accessdate=June 5, 2018|author=|date=May 14, 2017|last=Kellaway|first=Kate|work=The Guardian|publisher=|language=}}</ref>，代表作有《[[天使在美国|天使在美国]]》，更憑此獲[[普利策奖|普利策]][[普立茲戏剧奖|戲劇獎]]及[[托尼奖|托尼奖]][[托尼奖最佳话剧|最佳话剧]]。

庫許納亦曾因為電影《[[慕尼黑_(電影)|慕尼黑]]》及《[[林肯_(2012年電影)|林肯]]》當編劇，而入圍[[奧斯卡獎|奧斯卡]][[奧斯卡最佳改編劇本獎|最佳改編劇本獎]]。於2013年，庫許納獲當時的美國總統[[奧巴馬|奧巴馬]]頒發{{tsl|en|List of recipients of the National Medal of Arts|國家藝術獎}}。

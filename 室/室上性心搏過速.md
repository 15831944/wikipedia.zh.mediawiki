<!-- 定義及症狀 -->
'''室上性心搏過速'''（Supraventricular tachycardia），簡稱'''SVT'''，是因為[[心臟電傳導系統|心臟電傳導系統]]異常造成的[[心跳过速|心跳过速]]<ref name=NIH2011Type>{{cite web|title=Types of Arrhythmia|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/arr/types|website=NHLBI|date=July 1, 2011|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150607165144/http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/arr/types|archivedate=June 7, 2015|df=}}</ref>。主要可分四型：[[心房顫動|心房顫動]]、[[陣發性室上性心搏過速|陣發性室上性心搏過速]]（PVST）、[[心房撲動|心房撲動]]、[[沃夫巴金森懷特症候群|沃夫巴金森懷特症候群]]<ref name=NIH2011Type/>。症狀包括[[心悸|心悸]]、頭暈、盜汗、[[呼吸困难|呼吸困难]]及[[胸痛|胸痛]]等<ref name=NIH2011Sym>{{cite web|title=What Are the Signs and Symptoms of an Arrhythmia?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/arr/signs|website=NHLBI|accessdate=27 September 2016|date=July 1, 2011|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150219081446/http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/arr/signs|archivedate=19 February 2015|df=}}</ref>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
症狀從[[心房|心房]]或[[房室结|房室结]]開始<ref name=NIH2011Type/>。通常此症狀是由這二個機轉中的一個造成：{{le|折返性心律失常|reentrant dysrhythmia|折返性}}或是{{le|自發性心肌|Cardiac muscle automaticity}}<ref name=Al2016>{{cite journal|last1=Al-Zaiti|first1=SS|last2=Magdic|first2=KS|title=Paroxysmal Supraventricular Tachycardia: Pathophysiology, Diagnosis, and Management.|journal=Critical care nursing clinics of North America|date=September 2016|volume=28|issue=3|pages=309–16|pmid=27484659|doi=10.1016/j.cnc.2016.04.005}}</ref>。另一種快速心律是[[心律不整#心室|心室顫動]]—在心室產生開始急速顫動<ref name=NIH2011Type/>。可藉由[[心电图|心电图]]（ECG）、[[动态心电图|动态心电图]]或{{le|事件監視器|event monitor}}診斷出來<ref name=NIH2011Diag/>。可藉由抽血檢驗排除特定潛在因素，例如[[甲状腺功能亢进症|甲状腺功能亢进症]]或[[電解質不平衡|電解質不平衡]]<ref name=NIH2011Diag>{{cite web|title=How Are Arrhythmias Diagnosed?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/arr/diagnosis|website=NHLBI|date=July 1, 2011|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150218075708/http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/arr/diagnosis|archivedate=February 18, 2015|df=}}</ref>。

<!--治療及流行病學 -->
根據此症發生原因的不同，會採取不同的治療方式<ref name=NIH2011Tx/>，包括藥物治療、手術或其他的醫療程序<ref name=NIH2011Tx/>。有些型的疾病可能可以用{{link-en|迷走神經興奮法|Vagal maneuvers}}或是稱為{{le|導管電氣燒灼術|catheter ablation}}來治療<ref name=NIH2011Tx/>。若是心房顫動，可以用[[钙离子通道阻滞剂|钙离子通道阻滞剂]]或是[[Β受体阻滞剂|Β受体阻滞剂]]<ref name=NIH2011Tx/>。某些病患則可長期服用如[[阿斯匹靈|阿斯匹靈]]或是[[華法林|華法林]]等[[抗凝剂|抗凝血藥物]]來控制病情<ref name=NIH2011Tx>{{cite web|title=How Are Arrhythmias Treated?|url=http://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/health-topics/topics/arr/treatment|website=NHLBI|accessdate=27 September 2016|date=July 1, 2011}}</ref>。在歐洲，平均每1千人中約有25人會受心房顫動所苦<ref name=Zoni2014>{{cite journal|last1=Zoni-Berisso|first1=M|last2=Lercari|first2=F|last3=Carazza|first3=T|last4=Domenicucci|first4=S|title=Epidemiology of atrial fibrillation: European perspective.|journal=Clinical epidemiology|date=2014|volume=6|pages=213–20|pmid=24966695|doi=10.2147/CLEP.S47385|pmc=4064952}}</ref>、另約有2.3人則會受陣發性室上性心動過速影響<ref name=Kat2016>{{cite book|last1=Katritsis|first1=Demosthenes G.|last2=Camm|first2=A. John|last3=Gersh|first3=Bernard J.|title=Clinical Cardiology: Current Practice Guidelines|date=2016|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=9780198733324|page=538|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=VX7ADAAAQBAJ&pg=PA538|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161002094014/https://books.google.ca/books?id=VX7ADAAAQBAJ&pg=PA538|archivedate=2016-10-02|df=}}</ref>。此外，每1千人有2人會罹患沃夫巴金森懷特症候群<ref name=Fer2017>{{cite book|last1=Ferri|first1=Fred F.|title=Ferri's Clinical Advisor 2017: 5 Books in 1|date=2016|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9780323448383|page=1372|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=rRhCDAAAQBAJ&pg=PA1372|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161002094100/https://books.google.ca/books?id=rRhCDAAAQBAJ&pg=PA1372|archivedate=2016-10-02|df=}}</ref>、另0.8人則會出現心房撲動<ref>{{cite book|last1=Bennett|first1=David H.|title=Bennett's Cardiac Arrhythmias: Practical Notes on Interpretation and Treatment|date=2012|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=9781118432402|page=49|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=QjZV23sZTV4C&pg=PA49|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161002093537/https://books.google.ca/books?id=QjZV23sZTV4C&pg=PA49|archivedate=2016-10-02|df=}}</ref>。

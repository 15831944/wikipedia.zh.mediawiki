'''室女座SS'''是明顯呈現紅色的[[米拉變星|米拉變星]]，它的變光範圍從9.5等至7.4等<ref name="carbon miras">{{cite journal |title = Atlas of Hα emission lines and V light curves of 30 carbon Miras |last1 = Mikulášek |first1 = Z. |last2 = Gráf |first2 = T. |year = 2005 |volume = 35 |pages = 83-106 |journal = Astronomical Institute of the Slovak Academy of Sciences |accessdate = 14 July 2012 |url = http://articles.adsabs.harvard.edu/cgi-bin/nph-iarticle_query?2005CoSka..35...83M&data_type=PDF_HIGH&whole_paper=YES&type=PRINTER&filetype=.pdf}}</ref>，週期為361天。它也被考慮是一顆[[半規則變星|半規則變星]]，因為在過去數十年內他的最大和最小亮度本身也是個變數<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.aavso.org/lcotw/ss-virginis |title = SS Virginis |publisher = American Association of Variable Star Observers |accessdate = 14 July 2012}}</ref>。它因為富含[[碳|碳]]，所以[[光譜類型|光譜類型]]是C6.3e，與[[雙子座T|雙子座T]]相似。室女座SS，與含碳的米拉變星一樣，有一條變化很大的[[H-α|H-α]]發射譜現，並與整體的光度變化同步，這條Hα譜線在變亮時顯得更為明顯<ref name="carbon miras"/>。在[[近紅外|近紅外]]波段上的觀測顯示，它的半徑是500倍[[太陽半徑|太陽半徑]]，表面溫度介於2405-2485[[熱力學溫標|k]]<ref>{{cite journal |url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2006A%26A...451.1041R |title = Near-infrared observations of the carbon stars TU Geminorum and SS Virginis at milliarcsecond resolution |last1 = Richichi |first1 = A. |last2 = Chandrasekhar |first2 = T. |journal = Astronomy and Astrophysics |volume = 451 |issue = 3 |date = 1 June 2006 |accessdate = 14 July 2012 |pages = 1041-1044}}</ref>。

'''拿撒勒人會'''（{{lang-en|'''Church of the Nazarene'''}}），或稱'''宣聖會'''，是[[基督教|基督教]][[福音派|福音派]]下的一個[[基督教派系|派系]]，出現於北美19世紀的[[聖潔運動|聖潔運動]]中，其成員常被稱為'''Nazarenes'''（拿撒勒人）。它是世界上最大的[[循道宗|循道宗-聖潔教派]]。<ref>"Pentecostalism", in ''Encyclopedia of Religion and Society'', eds. William H. Swatos and Peter Kivisto (Rowman Altamira, 1998):358.</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.encyclopediaofarkansas.net/encyclopedia/entry-detail.aspx?entryID=4330 |title='&#39;The Encyclopedia of Arkansas History and Culture: Church of the Nazarene'&#39; |publisher=Encyclopediaofarkansas.net |date= |accessdate=2014-06-23}}</ref> 2014年9月末，該會在世界各地記有2,295,106會眾、29,395座教堂。<ref>[http://www.ncnnews.com/nphweb/html/ncn/article.jsp?sid=10005006&id=10014168 "General secretary releases 2014 stats", ''NCN News'' (December 10, 2014) ]</ref><ref>"The Church of the Nazarene uses "world area" to describe countries and areas that are a part of a country, but may be known by a different name. More than 20 of the denomination's 159 world areas fall into this category, such as Guam, a U.S. territory on the Asia-Pacific Region." See [http://www.ncnnews.com/nphweb/html/ncn/article.jsp?sid=10000012&id=10013302 Nazarene Research Services releases stats on 30-year world area growth], ''NCN News'' (January 10, 2014).</ref>

[[美國宗教|美國]]的拿撒勒人會信徒最多，計有631,454人，<ref>[http://nazarene.org/files/docs/StatisticsAnnual.pdf "Church of the Nazarene Growth, 2004-2014 Annual Statistics from the General Secretary's Reports" ] {{Wayback|url=http://nazarene.org/files/docs/StatisticsAnnual.pdf |date=20150919023155 }}</ref> 其次是[[莫桑比克宗教|莫桑比克]]（157,119）、[[巴西宗教|巴西]]（111,694）、[[印度宗教|印度]]（107,175）、[[孟加拉國宗教|孟加拉國]]（79,550）、<ref name="engagemagazine.com">Gina Grate Pottenger, [http://engagemagazine.com/Magazine-Content/Articles/April-2010/Bangladesh--It-started-with-a-letter.aspx "Bangladesh: It Started with a Letter"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100921151437/http://engagemagazine.com/Magazine-Content/Articles/April-2010/Bangladesh--It-started-with-a-letter.aspx |date=2010-09-21 }}, ''Engage'' 12 (April 21, 2010)</ref> [[危地馬拉宗教|危地馬拉]]（78,212）<ref>[http://www.ncnnews.com/nphweb/html/ncn/article.jsp?sid=10005114&id=10012977 "GMC Flags of the Nations: Guatemala"], ''NCN Global News Summary'' (September 17, 2013)</ref> 和[[海地宗教|海地]]（64,071）。而人均教徒數最多的國家是[[佛得角|佛得角]]。<ref name="Membership Stats">{{cite web|url=http://www.adherents.com/largecom/com_naz.html |title=Membership Stats |publisher=Adherents.com |date= |accessdate=2014-06-23}}</ref>

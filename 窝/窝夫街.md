'''窝夫街'''（Worth Street）是[[纽约市|纽约市]][[曼哈顿下城|曼哈顿下城]]的一条街道，东西走向。西起[[翠貝卡|翠貝卡]]的哈德逊街（Hudson Street），东到[[曼哈顿华埠|曼哈顿华埠]]的[[且林士果|且林士果]]（Chatham Square）。

窝夫街中段的[[弗利广场|弗利广场]]集中分布了政府办公楼和法院大楼。窝夫街125号（中央街路口）设有纽约市健康医疗署总部<ref>{{cite web | title=Contact | website=NYC Health and Hospitals | date=2015-10-19 | url=http://www.nychealthandhospitals.org/hhc/html/contact/Contact.shtml | accessdate=2016-02-06 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160121133635/http://www.nychealthandhospitals.org/hhc/html/contact/Contact.shtml | archive-date=2016-01-21 | dead-url=yes }}</ref>、健康与心理卫生署<ref>{{cite web | title=Ordering In Person or By Mail | website=Welcome to NYC.gov | date=2015-08-07 | url=http://www.nyc.gov/html/doh/html/services/vr-order-other.shtml | accessdate=2016-02-06 | quote=NYC Department of Health and Mental Hygiene, Office of Vital Records, 125 Worth St. | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160206111400/http://www.nyc.gov/html/doh/html/services/vr-order-other.shtml | archive-date=2016-02-06 | dead-url=yes }}</ref>和卫生署<ref>{{cite web | title=Business  - Nonputrescible, | website=City of New York, Solid Waste Transfer Station | url=https://www1.nyc.gov/nycbusiness/description/solid-waste-transfer-station-nonputrescible | accessdate=2016-02-06}}</ref>。此外，[[纽约最高法院|纽约最高法院]]设于中央街60号[[纽约县法院大楼|纽约县法院大楼]] 和中央街80号（路易·莱夫科维茨大楼），[[美国纽约南区联邦地区法院|美国纽约南区联邦地区法院]]设于珍珠街500号[[丹尼尔·帕特里克·莫伊尼汉|丹尼尔·帕特里克·莫伊尼汉]]联邦法院大楼，均在窝夫街设有入口。

[[纽约地铁|纽约地铁]]的[[IRT萊辛頓大道線|IRT萊辛頓大道線]]曾在1904-1962年之间设有窝夫街站。<ref>{{cite web | title=Abandoned Stations : Worth St | website=Columbia University in the City of New York | url=http://www.columbia.edu/~brennan/abandoned/worth.html | accessdate=2016-02-06}}</ref>

部長聯盟理事會在[[印度|印度共和国]]行使[[行政机构|行政機構]]的权力<ref name="CONSt2">[[wikisource:Constitution_of_India/Part_V#Article_74_.7BCouncil_of_Ministers_to_aid_and_advise_President.7D|Wikisource:Constitution of India/Part V#Article 74 .7BCouncil of Ministers to aid and advise President.7D]]</ref>。理事會由高级[[部長|部長]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/union-minister-ananth-kumar-passes-away/articleshow/66583172.cms|title=Karnataka's iconic leader Ananth Kumar leaves an indelible mark on national politics - Times of India ►|newspaper=The Times of India|accessdate=2018-11-17}}</ref>、低級部長<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.hindustantimes.com/india-news/union-minister-ananth-kumar-dies-at-59/story-ift0L6TCQmt3l84CkUSxoO.html|title=Union minister Ananth Kumar dies at 59, Karnataka declares 3-day mourning|date=2018-11-12|newspaper=https://www.hindustantimes.com/|accessdate=2018-11-17|language=en}}</ref>、甚或副部長<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.news18.com/news/india/union-cabinet-condoles-demise-of-senior-bjp-leader-ananth-kumar-1937467.html|title=Union Cabinet Condoles Demise of Senior BJP Leader Ananth Kumar|newspaper=News18|accessdate=2018-11-17}}</ref>組成。

理事會由[[印度总理|总理]]领导，一个规模较小的执行机构「'''聯盟理事會'''」是印度的最高决策机构<ref name="const1">[[wikisource:Constitution_of_India/Part_XVIII|Wikisource:Constitution of India/Part XVIII]]</ref>。根据第352条，只有总理和内阁部长是聯盟理事會的成員<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiankanoon.org/doc/1018568/|title=Article 352 in The Constitution Of India   1949|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=indiankanoon.org}}</ref>。

== 规例 ==
根据第75(3)条，部长理事會对[[印度议会|印度议会]]的[[人民院_(印度)|人民院]]负有集体责任<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiankanoon.org/doc/1336375/|title=Article 75(3) in The Constitution Of India   1949|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=indiankanoon.org}}</ref>。当一位部长在人民院提出的法案被否決時，整個理事會均需要負責<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiankanoon.org/doc/1324537/|title=Article 75 in The Constitution Of India   1949|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=indiankanoon.org}}</ref>，而非單單該部長。一旦理事會失去人民院的信任，理事會應該集體辭職，並促进新政府的组建<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gkseries.com/general-knowledge/indian-polity/constitutional-development-in-india/discussion-86|title=According to Article 75(3) of the Constitution of India the Council of Ministers are collectively responsible to the|accessdate=2018-11-17|last=GkSeries|work=www.gkseries.com|language=en}}</ref>。

部长不得在未经部长会议按照第78(c)条进行审议的情况下，作出任何决定<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ndtv.com/india-news/ananth-kumar-death-ananth-kumar-funeral-leaders-pay-tribute-to-ananth-kumar-1945798|title=Ananth Kumar Tributes: 3-Day Mourning Declared After Union Minister's Death|newspaper=NDTV.com|accessdate=2018-11-17}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tvr.by/eng/news/politika/podgotovku_k_zasedaniyu_soyuznogo_sovmina_obsudili_v_dome_pravitelstva_v_minske/|title=Preparations for meeting of Federal Council discussed in Minsk|accessdate=2018-11-15|work=www.tvr.by}}</ref>。若理事會決定總統宣佈國家進入緊急狀態，所有理事會成员均应以书面形式向总统提出根据第352条的建议<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.legalservicesindia.com/article/1834/Three-types-of-emergencies-under-the-Indian-Constitution.html|title=Three types of emergencies under the Indian Constitution|accessdate=2018-11-17|last=India|first=legal Service|work=www.legalservicesindia.com|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.lawteacher.net/free-law-essays/administrative-law/emergency-provisions-under-constitution-of-india-administrative-law-essay.php|title=Emergency Provisions Under Constitution Of India|accessdate=2018-11-17|language=en}}</ref>。  

根据《[[印度宪法|印度宪法]]》，理事會的部长总数不得超过人民院議席的15%<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/cabinet-not-to-exceed-15-of-ls-strength/article6047184.ece|title=Cabinet not to exceed 15% of LS strength|last=Reddy|first=B. Muralidhar|date=2014-05-26|newspaper=The Hindu|accessdate=2018-11-17|language=en-IN|issn=0971-751X}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/union-council-of-ministers-and-their-portfolios/article19614564.ece|title=Full list of Union Cabinet and Council of Ministers|last=Desk|first=The Hindu Net|date=2017-09-03|newspaper=The Hindu|accessdate=2018-11-17|language=en-IN|issn=0971-751X}}</ref>。部长必须是议员，任何部长若非议会任何一院的成员，其將被自動剝奪部長職位，為期六個月<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.quora.com/What-is-the-criteria-for-becoming-union-cabinet-minister|title=What is the criteria for becoming union cabinet minister? - Quora|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=www.quora.com|language=en}}</ref>。

== 各邦部長理事会 ==
印度的每个邦都由其部长理事会管理，其规则和程序均与中央政府的联盟部长理事会相類似，均按照第163<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiankanoon.org/doc/674146/|title=Article 163 in The Constitution Of India   1949|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=indiankanoon.org}}</ref>、164<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiankanoon.org/doc/578636/|title=Article 164 in The Constitution Of India   1949|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=indiankanoon.org}}</ref>和167(c)<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiankanoon.org/doc/1091985/|title=Article 167 in The Constitution Of India   1949|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=indiankanoon.org}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://indiankanoon.org/doc/607914/|title=Article 167(c) in The Constitution Of India   1949|accessdate=2018-11-17|work=indiankanoon.org}}</ref>条的规定和程序處理。 邦部长理事會中没有内阁级部长的概念。

'''Sony Tablet'''是[[索尼|索尼]]旗下的[[平板電腦|平板電腦]]品牌，搭載[[Android|Android]]作業系統。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://edition.cnn.com/2011/TECH/gaming.gadgets/07/18/android.tablets/ |title=Team Android Tablet adds players |last1=Millian |first1=Mark |date=2011-07-18 |work=CNNTech |publisher=Cable News Network}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2011/09/01/us-sony-idUSTRE77U19U20110901 |title=Sony tablets face tough sell on price, hardware |last=Reynolds |first=Isabel |date=2011-09-01 |work=Reuters}}</ref>


== 歷史 ==
2011年4月26日，索尼宣布開發兩款平板電腦，分別為[[Sony_Tablet_S|S1（Tablet S）]]和[[Sony_Tablet_P|S2（Tablet P）]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sony.net/SonyInfo/News/Press/201104/11-055E/ |title=Sony Announces Optimally Designed "Sony Tablet" with Android 3.0 that Complements Network Services for an Immersive Entertainment Experience |publisher=sony.net |accessdate=2011-12-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sony.net/Products/tablet/special/twowill/ |title="Two Will" Story of two will and the two will be |publisher=sony.net |accessdate=2011-12-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140717215944/http://www.sony.net/Products/tablet/special/twowill/ |archivedate=2014-07-17 }}</ref>

== 產品 ==
* [[Sony_Tablet_S|Sony Tablet S]]：索尼首部平板電腦，採用獨特的不對稱、上厚下輕的設計，設計符合[[人體工學|人體工學]]，採用Android 3.2。
* [[Sony_Tablet_P|Sony Tablet P]]：索尼發佈的一款對摺式雙觸控屏幕顯示的平板電腦，外形與[[NDS|NDS]]相似，採用Android 3.2。

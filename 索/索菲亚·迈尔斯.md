== 简介 ==
[[Image:Sophia_Myles.jpg|thumb]]

迈尔斯出生于[[伦敦市|伦敦市]]，她的母亲简（尼勒·阿伦）在一家教育出版社工作，他的父亲彼得·R·迈尔斯是[[英格兰|英格兰]][[教会|教会]]的[[牧师|牧师]]。<ref>[http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-106434331.html Myles ahead; Sophia Myles beat Sophie Dahl and Rosamund Pike to the coveted role of Lady Penelope in next year's 'Thunderbirds'. Now she's about to star in Hollywood's vampire tale 'Underworld'. Not bad for a vicar's daughter from Isleworth, says Emi...<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>
她的姥姥讲俄语，并且是半俄国血统半威尔士血统。<ref>[http://www.thisislondon.co.uk/lifestyle/article-23879224-spooks-star-sophia-myles-journey-from-wronged-maiden-to-kickass-spy.do/ From wronged maiden to kickass spy: Spooks star Sophia Myles]</ref><ref name="Guardian1">{{cite news|last=Husband|first=Stuart|coauthors=|title=Curtain call for Miss Myles|pages=|publisher=Guardian Unlimited|date=2007-08-05|url=http://film.guardian.co.uk/features/featurepages/0,,2140149,00.html|accessdate=2007-08-05}}</ref>
迈尔斯在[[诺丁山|诺丁山]]长大，曾就讀于英国[[剑桥大学|剑桥大学]]研習[[哲学|哲学]]，但為了從事演藝事業而中斷學業。<ref name="Guardian1"/>

== 个人生活 ==
迈尔斯和男演员[[查尔斯·丹斯|查尔斯·丹斯]]在合作演出《[[尼古拉斯的冒险生活|尼古拉斯的冒险生活]]》建立了关系。<ref name="Guardian1"/> 

2005年到2007年，[[苏格兰|苏格兰]]演员[[大卫·田纳特|大卫·田纳特]]一直和她在拍拖，两人曾合演过《[[弗洛伊尔的战争|弗洛伊尔的战争]]》《Doctor Who（神秘博士）》。<ref>{{cite news |first=Marianne |last=Macdonald |title=Myles above the clouds |url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/fashion/main.jhtml?xml=/fashion/2006/04/09/stsophie09.xml |work=[[Daily_Telegraph|Daily Telegraph]] |date=2006-04-09 |accessdate=2007-02-12 }}</ref>

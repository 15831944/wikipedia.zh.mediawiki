'''索托鱷屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Stolokrosuchus''）是[[鱷形超目|鱷形超目]][[新鱷類|新鱷類]]的一屬，生存於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期的[[非洲|非洲]]。

化石發現於[[尼日|尼日]]，只有一個頭顱骨，口鼻部長而薄，[[前額骨|前額骨]]有骨質瘤。在2000年，[[漢斯·拉森|漢斯·拉森]]（Hans Larsson）等人將這個頭顱骨進行敘述、命名，[[模式種|模式種]]是'''拉氏索托鱷'''（''S. lapparenti''）。當時被認為屬於[[佩羅鱷科|佩羅鱷科]]，或者是佩羅鱷科的近親<ref name=HLBG00>{{cite journal |last=Larrson |first=Hans C. E. |coauthors=and Gado, Boubacar |year=2000 |title=A new early Cretaceous crocodyliform from Niger |journal=Neues Jahrbuch für Geologie und Paläontologie, Abhandlungen |volume=217 |issue=1 |pages=131–141}}</ref>。之後研究發現索托鱷是[[埃羅鱷科|埃羅鱷科]]的近親<ref name=FDB02>{{cite journal |last=de Broin |first=F. de L. |year=2002 |title=''Elosuchus'', a new genus of crocodile from the Lower Cretaceous of the North of Africa |journal=Comptes Rendus Palevol |volume=1 |issue=5 |pages=275–285 |doi=10.1016/S1631-0683(02)00049-0}}</ref>。近年研究發現索托鱷是最原始的[[新鱷類|新鱷類]]之一<ref name=TS10>{{cite journal |last=Turner |first=Alan H. |coauthor=and Sertich, Joseph J. W. |year=2010 |title=Phylogenetic history of ''Simosuchus clarki'' (Crocodyliformes: Notosuchia) from the Late Cretaceous of Madagascar |journal=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology |volume=30 |issue=6, Memoir 10 |pages=177–236 |doi=10.1080/02724634.2010.532348}}</ref>，是埃羅鱷的遠親；而埃羅鱷本身屬於埃羅鱷科<ref name=BML12>Bronzati, M.; Montefeltro, F. C.; Langer, M. C. (2012). "A species-level supertree of Crocodyliformes". Historical Biology: 1. {{doi|10.1080/08912963.2012.662680}}</ref><ref name=G.kiplingi>{{Cite journal |author=Marco Brandalise de Andrade, Richard Edmonds, Michael J. Benton and Remmert Schouten |title=A new Berriasian species of ''Goniopholis'' (Mesoeucrocodylia, Neosuchia) from England, and a review of the genus |url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1096-3642.2011.00709.x/abstract |journal=Zoological Journal of the Linnean Society |volume=163 |issue=s1 |pages=S66–S108 |year=2011 |doi=10.1111/j.1096-3642.2011.00709.x}}</ref>或[[大頭鱷科|大頭鱷科]]<ref name=Meridiosaurus>{{Cite journal |author=Daniel Fortier, Daniel Perea and Cesar Schultz |title=Redescription and phylogenetic relationships of ''Meridiosaurus vallisparadisi'', a pholidosaurid from the Late Jurassic of Uruguay |url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1096-3642.2011.00722.x/abstract |journal=Zoological Journal of the Linnean Society |volume=163 |issue=s1 |pages=S66–S108 |year=2011 |doi=10.1111/j.1096-3642.2011.00709.x}}</ref>。

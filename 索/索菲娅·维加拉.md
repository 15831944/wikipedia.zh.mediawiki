'''索菲娅·玛格丽塔·维加拉·维加拉'''（{{Lang-es|Sofía Margarita Vergara Vergara}}，{{bd|1972年|7月10日|||catIdx= W维加拉}}）<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2015/01/15/sofia-vergara-throwback_n_6470704.html|title = Sofía Vergara's Life Before 'Modern Family' Was Very Different|last =|first =|date = 2015-01-20|work = Huffington Post|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref>出生于[[哥伦比亚|哥伦比亚]]北部城市[[巴蘭幾亞|巴蘭幾亞]]，是哥伦比亚裔的美国女演员、喜剧演员、制片人、电视主持人、模特儿和商人。

== 早期生活 ==
维加拉出生在一个位於哥伦比亚北部城市巴兰基利亚罗马天主教的家庭。她的母亲玛格丽塔·戴维·德·范盖拉，是一名家庭主妇；她的父亲，胡里奥·恩里克·范盖拉·罗伯，则是为一家肉类公司畜养牛只。她的五个兄弟姊妹及表亲会以「Toti」的昵称来称呼她。<ref name=":0" />

维加拉原本在一间位於哥伦比亚的大学就读牙科医学，但在拿到学位的前两个学期因为投入了模特儿和走秀的事业，因而无法完成学业。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,20317432,00.html|title = 5 Things to Know About Sofia Vergara|last =|first =|date = 2009-11-04|work = People|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url = http://parade.com/133115/dotsonrader/sofia-vergara-2/|title = Sofia Vergara, Not Just Another Pretty Face|last =|first =|date = 2011-07-24|work = Parade|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref>维加拉的哥哥在1998年的一桩绑架案成为受害的死者，她而後迁居至迈阿密，远离哥哥在哥伦比亚被绑架谋杀的动荡。<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url = http://nypost.com/2015/01/24/drugs-murder-mafia-inside-sofia-vergaras-rocky-road-to-fame/|title = Drugs, murder, mafia: Inside Sofia Vergara’s rise to fame|last =|first =|date = 2015-01-24|work = New York Post|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref>她的表亲和被领养的妹妹珊卓·维加拉，同样也是美国的电视女演员。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2828503/Good-looks-run-family-Sofia-Vergara-s-sister-Sandra-steals-limelight-eye-catching-black-netted-gown-arrives-film-awards-gala-LA.html|title = Good looks run in the family! Sofia Vergara's sister Sandra steals the limelight in an eye-catching black netted gown as she arrives at film awards gala in LA|last =|first =|date = 2014-11-10|work = Daily Mail|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
1991年，维加拉在18岁时与她的高中男友乔·冈萨雷斯结婚。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2015/01/07/sofia-vergara-wedding_n_6431810.html|title = 18-Year-Old Sofia Vergara's Wedding Pics Prove She Doesn't Age|last =|first =|date = 2015-01-08|work = Huffington Post|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-style/news/sofia-vergara-wedding-photos-18-years-old-vintage-photos-201581|title = Sofia Vergara's Wedding Photos From When She Was 18 are as Glorious and Gorgeous as You'd Imagine: See Them Here!|last =|first =|date = 2015-01-08|work = Us Weekly|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref><ref name=":1" />两人育有一子，名为马诺洛。1993年，维加拉与冈萨雷斯离婚。<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" />2011年5月9日，维加拉的弟弟胡利欧因为在同年4月遭逮捕，而从美国被遣送回哥伦比亚。胡利欧患有长期的毒瘾，并且曾经触法过。「必须眼睁睁地看着某人在十年中一点一点的死去（意指她所认识的弟弟死去，现在弟弟已成为另一个她所不认识的陌生人一样），那是最糟糕的惩罚，」维加拉在Parade杂志中说道。「现在的他完全是另外一个人。」<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2022124/Sofia-Vergaras-brother-Julio-deported-US-30-drug-related-arrests.html|title = Revealed: Sofia Vergara's brother deported from the U.S. after 30 drug related arrests|last =|first =|date = 2011-08-03|work = Daily Mail|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref><ref name=":1" />

维加拉在2000年时被诊断出[[甲状腺癌|甲状腺癌]]。她已移除了她的甲状腺，并接受过[[放射性碘|放射性碘]]治疗，目前已完全康复。她需要透过服药以防止[[甲狀腺機能低下症|甲状腺功能减退症]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.health.com/health/article/0,,20411050,00.html|title = Sofia Vergara Beats Thyroid Cancer|accessdate = 2016-02-28|author =|date = 2008-04-21|publisher = Health}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.foxnews.com/health/2013/04/21/sofia-vergara-opens-up-about-struggle-with-thyroid-cancer.html|title = Sofia Vergara opens up about struggle with thyroid cancer|accessdate = 2016-02-28|author =|date = 2013-04-21|publisher = Fox News}}</ref><ref name=":1" />

2012年7月10日，据报导指出，维加拉与她的男朋友尼克·勒布订婚。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.eonline.com/news/329087/sofia-vergara-engaged-shows-off-ring-during-birthday-celebration|title = Sofia Vergara Engaged, Shows Off Ring During Birthday Celebration|last =|first =|date = 2012-07-11|work = E!online|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref>2014年5月23日，维加拉宣布订婚已被取消。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.eonline.com/news/545114/sofia-vergara-and-fiance-nick-loeb-break-up-find-out-why|title = Sofía Vergara and Fiancé Nick Loeb Break Up—Find Out Why|last =|first =|date = 2014-05-25|work = E!online|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref>2015年5月1日，据报导指出，维加拉和勒布为了两个他们交往时已经冻结的胚胎，因而起了争执。<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.people.com/article/sofia-vergara-nick-loeb-embryo-battle-lawsuit|title = Sofia Vergara and Ex-Fiancé Nick Loeb's Legal Battle Over Embryos Heats Up|last =|first =|date = 2015-05-22|work = People|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/gossip/la-et-mg-sofia-vergara-nick-loeb-embryos-20150430-story.html|title = Sofia Vergara's ex Nick Loeb speaks out on suing her for their embryos|last =|first =|date = 2015-04-30|work = Los Angeles Times|accessdate = 2016-02-28}}</ref>

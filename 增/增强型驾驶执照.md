'''增强型驾驶执照'''（{{lang-en|'''enhanced driver's license'''}}）是[[美国|美国]]部分州和[[加拿大|加拿大]]部分省为居民签发的一种[[驾驶执照|驾驶执照]]。除了通常的显示驾驶资格和个人身份的功能，这种增强型证件还能拥有一些有限的护照特性，供居民来往[[美加边境|美加陆路边境]]。<ref>{{cite web |url = https://www.dhs.gov/enhanced-drivers-licenses-what-are-they |title = Enhanced Drivers Licenses: What Are They? |publisher = Department of Homeland Security |access-date = August 25, 2012 |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20131025185259/http://www.dhs.gov/enhanced-drivers-licenses-what-are-they |archive-date = October 25, 2013 }}</ref><ref name=bc>{{cite web|url=http://www.icbc.com/driver-licensing/edl-eic/edl-guide.pdf |title=Your guide to British Columbia’s enhanced driver’s licence program |website=ICBC.com |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130929030112/http://www.icbc.com/driver-licensing/edl-eic/edl-guide.pdf |archivedate=September 29, 2013 }}</ref>在不同的州、省，该种证件的申请人被要求是[[美国公民|美国公民]]或[[加拿大公民|加拿大公民]]，并有证据显示在所在州或省的实际居住。增强型驾驶执照遵循{{Link-en|西半球旅行计划|Western Hemisphere Travel Initiative}}。

'''真蹄螺属'''（學名：''{{lang|la|Euchelus}}''）是一個[[Chilodontaidae|Chilodontaidae]]科[[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]][[海螺|海螺]]的[[屬_(生物)|屬]]{{r|zgdwz|WoRMS_204535}}。本屬舊屬[[鐘螺科|鐘螺科]]{{r|zgdwz}}，今屬[[陀螺總科|陀螺總科]]的[[唇齒螺科|唇齒螺科]]{{r|WoRMS_204535}}。

==型態描述==
近球形或馬蹄形。

The umbilicate or imperforate shell has a globose-turbinate shape. It is umbilicate or imperforate. The [[Whorl_(mollusc)|whorl]]s are rounded and with spirally granose revolving ribs. The [[Aperture_(mollusc)|aperture]] is subcircular. The outer [[Lip_(gastropod)|lip]] is thickened, and crenulate within. The [[Columella_(gastropod)|columella]] shows  a central tooth or a notch at the base. The [[Operculum_(gastropod)|operculum]] has few whorls that are rapidly increasing.<ref>[https://archive.org/details/manualofconcholo111tryo Tryon (1889), Manual of Conchology XI, Academy of Natural Sciences, Philadelphia]</ref>

==分佈==
本物種分佈於[[印度洋|印度洋]]及[[西太平洋|西太平洋]]海域。

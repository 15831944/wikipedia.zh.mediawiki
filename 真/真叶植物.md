'''真叶植物'''（{{lang-en|'''Euphyllophytina'''}}）是一个未分级的植物类群，是[[维管植物|维管植物]]的子类群，[[石松门|石松门]]的姊妹类群。真叶植物包括[[种子植物|种子植物]]和[[蕨类植物|蕨类植物]]两大类，以及一些灭绝的化石类群。根据多项分子生物学证据，现存的维管植物都可以归为石松、蕨类、种子植物这三个[[单系群|单系群]]<ref>Smith, A. R., K. M. Pryer, et al. (2006). "A classification for extant ferns." ''Taxon'' '''55'''(3): 705-731</ref><ref>Pryer, K. M., H. Schneider, et al. (2001). "Horsetails and ferns are a monophyletic group and the closest living relatives to seed plants." ''Nature'' '''409'''(6820): 618-22</ref><ref name=pry04>Pryer, K. M., E. Schuettpelz, et al. (2004). "Phylogeny and evolution of ferns (monilophytes) with a focus on the early leptosporangiate divergences 1". ''Botanical Soc America'' '''91''': 1582-1598</ref>。其它的研究表明，将化石种类列入会得到更为复杂的的谱系图<ref>{{Citation |last=Rothwell |first=G.W. |last2=Nixon |first2=K.C. |year=2006 |title=How Does the Inclusion of Fossil Data Change Our Conclusions about the Phylogenetic History of Euphyllophytes? |journal=International Journal of Plant Sciences |volume=167 |issue=3 |pages=737–749 |doi=10.1086/503298 |lastauthoramp=yes }}</ref>。

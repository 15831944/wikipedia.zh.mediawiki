{{vgname|真·女神转生II|ja=真・女神転生II}}是一款由[[Atlus|Atlus]]制作和发行的[[角色扮演类游戏|角色扮演类游戏]]。本游戏于1994年3月18日在日本地区[[超级任天堂|超级任天堂]]发行。<ref name="gamefaqs">{{cite web | url = http://www.gamefaqs.com/snes/581299-shin-megami-tensei-ii/data| title = Release information | publisher = [[GameFAQs|GameFAQs]]| date = | accessdate = 2015-10-23}}</ref>

在真·女神转生II，玩家控制着角斗士鹰，它能够与恶魔进行交流。<ref name="nocturne guide">{{cite book |date=2004 |title=Shin Megami Tensei Nocturne: The Official Strategy Guide |publisher=DoubleJump Publishing |pages=384–387 |isbn=978-0974170046 |chapter=Series History; An Interview By You}}</ref>游戏剧情与前作《[[真·女神转生|真·女神转生]]》类似，玩家为第一人物视角，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gamasutra.com/view/feature/131985/a_japanese_rpg_primer_the_.php |title=A Japanese RPG Primer: The Essential 20 |author=Kalata, Kurt |date=2008-03-19 |website=[[Gamasutra|Gamasutra]] |access-date=2015-07-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141111040047/http://www.gamasutra.com/view/feature/131985/a_japanese_rpg_primer_the_.php |archive-date=2014-11-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref>需要探索地牢和恶魔进行战斗。

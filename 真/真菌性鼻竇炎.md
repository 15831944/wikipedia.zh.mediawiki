'''真菌性鼻竇炎'''（Fungal sinusitis）是[[真菌|真菌]]感染[[鼻竇|鼻竇]][[粘膜|粘膜]]組織所造成[[鼻竇炎|鼻竇炎]]<ref name="emed">{{Cite journal|title=Fungal Sinusitis: Background, History of the Procedure, Problem|url=http://emedicine.medscape.com/article/863062-overview|website=eMedicine|accessdate=25 November 2016|date=28 June 2016}}</ref>，此疾病並不常見，但在近幾十年發生率有上升趨勢<ref name="fung">{{Cite journal|last=Karthikeyan|first=P.|last2=Nirmal Coumare|first2=V.|date=2017-01-29|title=Incidence and Presentation of Fungal Sinusitis in Patient Diagnosed with Chronic Rhinosinusitis|pmc=3266098|journal=Indian Journal of Otolaryngology and Head & Neck Surgery|volume=62|issue=4|pages=381–385|doi=10.1007/s12070-010-0062-0|issn=2231-3796|pmid=22319697}}</ref>，通常發生於[[免疫缺陷|免疫缺陷]]者。真菌性鼻竇炎的症狀包括臉部疼痛、眼睛周圍疼痛、[[鼻塞|鼻塞]]、[[鼻漏|鼻漏]]、[[頭痛|頭痛]]，甚至還可能造成{{link-en|眼肌麻痹|ophthalmoparesis}}<ref name=emed/>。造成真菌性鼻竇炎的[[致病真菌|致病真菌]]有約九成為[[煙麴黴|煙麴黴]]，另外還有[[黑麴黴|黑麴黴]]與[[黃麴霉|黃麴霉]]<ref name=Incidence>{{cite journal |authors=P. Karthikeyan, V. Nirmal Coumare |title=Incidence and presentation of fungal sinusitis in patient diagnosed with chronic rhinosinusitis |journal=Indian Journal of Otolaryngology Head and Neck Surgery |volume=62 |pages=381–5 |number=4 |date=October–December 2010 |doi=10.1007/s12070-010-0062-0 |pmid=22319697 |pmc=3266098}}subscription needed</ref>。

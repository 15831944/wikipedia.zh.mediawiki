'''真空塔望遠鏡'''是位在[[加那利群島|加那利群島]]的[[特內里費島|特內里費島]]上{{link-en|泰德天文台| Teide Observatory}}的抽真空光學[[太陽望遠鏡|太陽望遠鏡]]。由{{link-en|基彭霍伊爾學院太陽物理所|Kiepenheuer-Institut für Sonnenphysik}}（KIS）管理<ref name="KIS">[http://www.kis.uni-freiburg.de/index.php?id=23&L=1 Kiepenheuer-Institut für Sonnenphysik (KIS)]</ref><ref name=vtt_kis>{{cite web|title=Vacuum Tower Telescope|url=http://www.kis.uni-freiburg.de/?id=575&L=1|work=[[Kiepenheuer-Institut_für_Sonnenphysik|KIS]] website|publisher=[[Kiepenheuer-Institut_für_Sonnenphysik|Kiepenheuer-Institut für Sonnenphysik]]|accessdate=11 January 2014}}</ref>。 

它在1983年至1986年間製造，於1988年才第一次使用<ref name=vtt_iac>{{cite web|title=Vacuum Tower Telescope (VTT)|url=http://www.iac.es/eno.php?op1=3&op2=6&lang=en&id=1|work=[[Instituto_de_Astrofísica_de_Canarias|IAC]] website|publisher=[[Instituto_de_Astrofísica_de_Canarias|Instituto de Astrofísica de Canarias]]|accessdate=11 January 2014}}</ref>。它的[[主鏡|主鏡]]口徑是70釐米（28英寸），[[焦距|焦距]]長46公尺（151英尺）。多虧在2000年安裝了[[調適光學|調適光學]]系統''KAOS''（''K''iepenheuer-institute ''A''daptive ''O''ptic ''S''ystem）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kis.uni-freiburg.de/AOVortrag_Soltau_0004/ |title=KAOS- The Kiepenheuer-institute Adaptive Optic System |accessdate=May 31, 2007 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070610234500/http://www.kis.uni-freiburg.de/AOVortrag_Soltau_0004/ |archivedate=June 10, 2007 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kis.uni-freiburg.de/kishomepage_36_e.html |title=Solar observations of highest resolution obtained with Adaptive Optics and Speckle-Interferometry at the VTT |accessdate=May 31, 2007 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070610200044/http://www.kis.uni-freiburg.de/kishomepage_36_e.html |archivedate=June 10, 2007 }}</ref>並開始運作，它對[[太陽|太陽]]表面的解像力能夠達到0.2弧秒（相當於150公里）的細節<ref>{{Cite book | last1 = Van Der Luehe | first1 = Oskar | last2 = Soltau | first2 = Dirk | last3 = Berkefeld | first3 = Thomas | last4 = Schelenz | first4 = Thomas | editor1-last = Keil | editor1-first = Stephen L. | editor2-first = Sergey V. | editor2-last = Avakyan | doi = 10.1117/12.498659 | chapter = KAOS: Adaptive optics system for the Vacuum Tower Telescope at Teide Observatory| title = Innovative Telescopes and Instrumentation for Solar Astrophysics | series = Innovative Telescopes and Instrumentation for Solar Astrophysics | volume = 4853 | pages = 187 | year = 2003 | bibcode = 2003SPIE.4853..187V| pmid =  | pmc = }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Berkefeld|first=Thomas|author2=Dirk Schmidt |title=KAOS - a flexible control system for AO and MCAO|date=December 2012|volume=2|series=Real Time Control for Adaptive Optics Workshop|url=https://www.eso.org/sci/meetings/2012/RTCWorkshop/7_4_bekerfeld.pdf|accessdate=11 January 2014|publisher=[[ESO|ESO]]|location=Garching}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=Rimmele|first=Thomas R.|author2=Jose Marino |title=Solar Adaptive Optics|journal=Living Reviews in Solar Physics|date=2011|volume=8|issue=2|doi=10.12942/lrsp-2011-2|url=http://www.livingreviews.org/lrsp-2011-2|accessdate=11 January 2014|bibcode = 2011LRSP....8....2R }}</ref>。

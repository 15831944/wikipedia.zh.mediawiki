'''以色列金蠍'''（學名：''Leiurus quinquestriatus''）是屬於[[蠍目|蠍目]][[鉗蠍科|鉗蠍科]]的生物，也稱為'''巴勒斯坦黃蠍'''、'''巴勒斯坦毒蠍'''、'''以色列殺人蠍'''<ref name="Animals">{{cite book |title=Venom diseases |last=Minton |first=Sherman A. |year=1974 |publisher=Thomas |isbn=978-0-398-03051-3 |pages=235 |url=http://books.google.com/?id=aEtrAAAAMAAJ&q=%22palestine+yellow+scorpion%22&dq=%22palestine+yellow+scorpion%22&cd=13 |accessdate=April 30, 2010}}</ref><ref name="Biology">{{cite book |title=Entomology abstracts, Volume 24, Issues 10-12 |last=The Staff of Cambridge Scientific Abstracts |year=1993 |publisher=Cambridge Scientific Abstracts |url=http://books.google.com/?id=FdosAAAAMAAJ&q=%22palestine+yellow+scorpion%22&dq=%22palestine+yellow+scorpion%22&cd=6 |accessdate=April 30, 2010}}</ref><ref name="Ethnozoology">{{cite book |title=The Continuum encyclopedia of animal symbolism in art |last=Werness |first=Hope B. |year=2004 |publisher=Continuum International Publishing Group |isbn=978-0-8264-1525-7 |pages=476 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=fr2rANLrPmoC&pg=PA360&dq=palestine+yellow+scorpion&cd=2#v=onepage&q=palestine%20yellow%20scorpion&f=false |accessdate=April 30, 2010}}</ref><ref name="World records">{{cite book |title=The Guinness Book of World Records 1998 |last=Young |first=Mark |year=1998 |publisher=Bantam Books |isbn=978-0-553-57895-9 |pages=768 |url=http://books.google.com/?id=ZSyswNFqTycC&q=%22palestine+yellow+scorpion%22&dq=%22palestine+yellow+scorpion%22&cd=11 |accessdate=April 30, 2010}}</ref>，顏色約為黃色，身長三至八公分，平均約六公分<ref>{{cite journal |author=Zuhair S. Amr & Ratib El-Oran |year=1994 |title=Systematics and distribution of scorpions (Arachnida, Scorpionida) in Jordan |journal=[[Italian_Journal_of_Zoology|Italian Journal of Zoology]] |volume=61 |issue=2 |pages=185–190 |doi=10.1080/11250009409355881}}</ref>。分布於[[北非|北非]]和[[中東|中東]]的[[沙漠|沙漠]]地區。

==研究價值==
其毒液富含[[chlorotoxin|蠍氯毒素]]能辨認腫瘤及脊椎腫瘤，還可用以治療[[瘧疾|瘧疾]]；[[Kaliotoxin|卡利蠍毒素]]可用以治療老鼠骨骼疾病<ref name=手工擠>{{cite news|title=世上最貴液體！「以色列金蠍」毒液1公升要價3億　全靠手工擠 |url=https://www.ettoday.net/news/20181020/1286001.htm?ercamp=hot_news |author=  國際中心 |publisher =[[Ettoday|Ettoday]] | archivedate =2018年10月20日}}</ref>。
毒液採集需採用人工手擠，每隻金蠍僅能生產2 mg毒液，一加侖價值3,900萬美元<ref name=手工擠/>。

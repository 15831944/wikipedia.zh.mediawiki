'''以色列國家路線'''（{{lang-he|שביל ישראל}}，''Shvil Yisra'el''）是一條遠足路線，實現於1995年，橫跨以色列全國<ref>{{cite web|title=Israel National Trail|url=http://www.natureisrael.org/INT|publisher=[[Society_for_the_Protection_of_Nature_in_Israel|Society for the Protection of Nature in Israel]]|accessdate=21 April 2013}}</ref>。這條路線北起以色列和黎巴嫩國境的Dan，南至紅海沿岸的[[埃拉特|埃拉特]]，全長{{convert|1015|km|abbr=on}}<ref>[http://www.haaretz.com/news/national/national-geographic-names-israel-national-trail-as-one-of-world-s-best.premium-1.458527 National Geographic names Israel National Trail as one of world's best, [[Haaretz|Haaretz]]]</ref><ref>[http://www.jpost.com/Business-and-Innovation/Environment/Google-Street-View-cameras-to-bring-Israel-National-Trail-alive-402038 Google Street View cameras to bring Israel National Trail alive, [[Jerusalem_Post|Jerusalem Post]]]</ref>。

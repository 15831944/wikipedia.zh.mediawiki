'''以色列國家棒球隊'''，是以色列體育國家隊。2017年經典賽前世界及歐洲分別為第41及第16名。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.i24news.tv/en/news/sport/44453-140920-israel-s-baseball-team-moves-up-in-world-rankings|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141018060639/http://www.i24news.tv/en/news/sport/44453-140920-israel-s-baseball-team-moves-up-in-world-rankings|archivedate=2014-10-18|title=Israel's baseball team moves up in world rankings|work=i24news|accessdate=14 October 2014|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wbsc.org/rankings/|title=The WBSC World Rankings 2016 --  WBSC|publisher=|accessdate=13 December 2016}}</ref>但是在經典賽後快速上升到第19名。

以色列有從10到30歲的棒球隊。他們目前的總教練是Jerry Weinstein。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.baseball.org.il/en/cat-heb-2/307-senior-national-team-on-their-way-to-vienna|title=Senior National Team on their Way to Vienna - Israel Baseball - Official Site|publisher=|accessdate=31 July 2015}}</ref>

2015年6月，國家隊成員[[Dean_Kremer|Dean Kremer]]成為第一個大聯盟選上的預備球員。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jpost.com/Israel-News/Sports/Kremer-first-Israeli-to-be-picked-in-MLB-draft-405811|title=Kremer first Israeli to be picked in MLB draft|publisher=Jerusalem Post}}</ref>他在第38輪被[[聖地牙哥教士|聖地牙哥教士]]選中。7月22日宣布他與UNLV [[UNLV_Rebels|Rebels]]棒球隊簽約<ref>{{cite news|last1=Heller|first1=Aron|title=Israeli baseball player selected in MLB draft for 1st time|url=http://www.washingtontimes.com/news/2015/jul/22/israeli-baseball-player-selected-in-mlb-draft-for-/|accessdate=7 March 2017|work=The Washington Times|agency=Associated Press}}</ref>[[UNLV_Rebels|Rebels]]2016年賽季後， Dean Kremer從[[洛杉矶道奇|洛杉矶道奇]]起始並分配給[[Ogden_Raptors|Ogden Raptors]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/player/665152/dean-kremer|title=Dean Kremer Stats, Fantasy & News|publisher=Major League Baseball}}</ref>

==== 2013====
2012年9月參加在[[朱庇特_(佛罗里达州)|朱庇特]]的[[2013年世界棒球經典賽資格賽_-_第一組|2013年世界棒球經典賽資格賽 - 第一組]]，與[[西班牙棒球代表隊|西班牙]]、[[France_national_baseball_team|法國]]和[[South_Africa_national_baseball_team|南非]]同組。以色列贏了西班牙，西班牙淘汰了南非再與以色列對戰。西班牙最後10局以9–7打敗以色列進入決賽圈。

====2017====
{{main article|Israel at the World Baseball Classic#2017 tournament}}
2016年9月,以色列隊參與[[2017_World_Baseball_Classic_–_Qualifier_4|資格賽第四組]]. [[科羅拉多落磯|科羅拉多落磯]]教練[[Jerry_Weinstein|Jerry Weinstein]]成為該隊教練。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://forward.com/culture/qa/341319/how-a-survivors-son-is-bringing-baseball-to-israel/|title=How a Survivor’s Son Is Bringing Baseball to Israel|publisher=}}</ref>以色列的名冊包括20名MLB附屬小聯盟比賽，佔隊伍的86％，超過任何其他隊伍的資格賽，甚至包括最近的大聯盟隊員。<ref>[http://www.jpost.com/Israel-News/Sports/Israel-off-to-great-start-in-Baseball-Classic-Qualifier-tournament-in-Brooklyn-468522 "Israel off to great start in Baseball Classic Qualifier tournament in Brooklyn" - Israel News<!-- Bot generated title -->], ''Jerusalem Post''</ref>

以色列三戰全勝，先後打敗[[Great_Britain_national_baseball_team|英國]]2次和[[巴西棒球代表隊|巴西]]一次。<ref name=17game1>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/gameday/great-britain-vs-israel/2016/09/22/467858?#game_tab=box,game=467858,game_state=final|title=Great Britain vs. Israel - 09/22/16|publisher=}}</ref><ref name=17game3>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/gameday/great-britain-vs-israel/2016/09/25/467862?#game_tab=box,game=467862,game_state=final|title=Great Britain vs. Israel - 09/25/16|publisher=}}</ref><ref name=17game2>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/gameday/brazil-vs-israel/2016/09/23/467859?#game_tab=box,game=467859,game_state=final|title=Brazil vs. Israel - 09/23/16|publisher=}}</ref>

在此情況進入[[大韩民国|南韓]]在2017年3月的[[2017年世界棒球經典賽－A組|A組]]，與[[韓國棒球代表隊|南韓]]，[[中華成棒隊|中華台北]]及[[荷蘭棒球代表隊|荷蘭]]同組<ref>{{cite news|title=Israel Advances to the World Baseball Classic|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/09/26/sports/baseball/israel-advances-to-the-world-baseball-classic.html|accessdate=7 March 2017|work=The New York Times|date=25 September 2016}}</ref>

在2017年賽事開始時，[[ESPN|ESPN]]曾評論以色列世界排名為41名，將是巨大的劣勢，稱他為"WBC的[[Jamaica_national_bobsleigh_team|牙買加雪橇隊]]".<ref>[http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/18805572/team-israel-wbc-biggest-underdog-ever "Team Israel -- and its mascot, Mensch on a Bench -- is the Jamaican bobsled team of the WBC"]</ref><ref>[http://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/sports/1.775667 "World Baseball Classic: Israel's Cinderella Run Forges Ahead After Upsetting Taiwan"]</ref>以色列在小組賽中先後勝世界第3名的韓國和世界第4名的中華台北<ref>{{cite web|title=Israel wins at World Baseball Classic again|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/mlb/2017/03/07/israel-chinese-taipei-world-baseball-classic/98840740/|accessdate=2017年4月8日|date=2017年3月7日|language=en|quote=Israel entered the tournament ranked No. 41 by the World Baseball Softball Confederation and has already beaten third-ranked South Korea and fourth-ranked Taiwan.}}</ref>及荷蘭<ref>[http://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/mlb/2017/03/09/israel-beats-netherlands-advances-in-world-baseball-classic/98942326/ World Baseball Classic newcomer Israel advances to second round AP]</ref>。從初賽進入複賽在日本的[[2017年世界棒球經典賽|E組]]並可直接參與2021年賽事。<ref>[http://www.timesofisrael.com/liveblog_entry/israel-advances-to-2nd-round-of-world-baseball-classic/ Israel advances to 2nd round of World Baseball Classic]</ref>

'''以太坊经典'''是一個[[开放源代码|开源]]、公开、 以[[区块链|区块链]]為基础的[[分布式计算|分布式计算]]平台。<ref name="CD">{{cite web|url=http://www.coindesk.com/blockchain-finance-buzzword-watchword-2016/|title=Blockchain in Finance: From Buzzword to Watchword in 2016|accessdate=22 December 2016|date=22 December 2016|publisher=|first1=Farzam|last1=Ehsani|website=CoinDesk|type=News}}</ref><ref name="Vigna-28-Oct-2015">{{cite web|url=https://blogs.wsj.com/moneybeat/2015/10/28/bitbeat-microsoft-to-offer-ethereum-based-services-on-azure/|title=BitBeat: Microsoft to Offer Ethereum-Based Services on Azure|accessdate=17 February 2016|date=28 October 2015|publisher=News Corp|first1=Paul|last1=Vigna|website=The Wall Street Journal|type=Blog}}</ref> 它提供了一个分散的具有[[圖靈完備性|圖靈完備性]]的[[虛擬機器|虛擬機]]。該虛擬機可以利用全世界的虛擬機節點網絡執行腳本。 以太坊經典还提供了一種可在各用戶之間流通，名為「經典以太坊」的代幣。用戶可把代幣存到[[錢包_(加密貨幣)|密碼货币钱包]]裏，為各節點參與者的算力提供酬勞。為防止以癱瘓系統為目的之大額交易的出現，經典以太坊設有名為Gas的交易定價制度，所有願意付出較高交易費者之交易均會獲優先處理。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ethdocs.org/en/latest/contracts-and-transactions/account-types-gas-and-transactions.html|title=Account Types, Gas, and Transactions — Ethereum Homestead 0.1 documentation|website=ethdocs.org|access-date=2017-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ethereum.gitbooks.io/frontier-guide/content/costs.html|title=Gas and transaction costs {{!}} Ethereum Frontier Guide|last=Ethereum|website=ethereum.gitbooks.io|access-date=2017-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cryptocompare.com/coins/guides/what-is-the-gas-in-ethereum/|title=What is the "Gas" in Ethereum?|website=CryptoCompare|access-date=2017-01-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://media.consensys.net/ethereum-gas-fuel-and-fees-3333e17fe1dc#.22a3bv1ot|title=Ethereum, Gas, Fuel, & Fees|last=ConsenSys|date=2016-06-23|newspaper=ConsenSys Media|access-date=2017-01-15}}</ref>

以太坊經典是以太坊平台分叉而成的加密貨幣。在分叉前，經典以太坊被直接稱為[[以太坊|以太坊]]。分叉以後，舊分叉以太坊經典，新分叉則保留原名。在第1920000資料塊出現前便擁有以太坊者都已獲得相等數量的以太坊經典。

==Callisto==
Callisto是以太坊經典的分支。Callisto將會被分拆為另一種[[加密貨幣|加密貨幣]]，代號CLO。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://news.bitcoin.com/an-ethereum-classic-fork-snapshot-is-coming-next-week/|title=An Ethereum Classic Fork Snapshot Is Coming Next Week - Bitcoin News|date=2018-02-23|work=Bitcoin News|language=en-US|access-date=2018-05-06}}</ref>

'''以東'''（{{IPAc-en|ˈ|iː|.|d|ʌ|m}};<ref>[http://www.lds.org/scriptures/bofm/pronunciation?lang=eng LDS.org: "Book of Mormon Pronunciation Guide"] (retrieved 2012-02-25), [[Wikipedia:IPA_for_English|IPA]]-ified from «ē´dum»</ref>，希伯来语：'''אֱדוֹם'''; [[阿卡德語|阿卡德語]]: ''Udumi''; [[敘利亞語|敘利亞語]]: '''ܐܕܘܡ'''）是[[閃米特人|閃米特人]]曾居住過的一個區域，位在{{link-en|南黎凡特|Southern Levant}}，位在[[犹大山地|犹大山地]]及[[死海|死海]]的南邊。聖經中有記錄在西元前第十七左右世紀[[铁器时代|铁器时代]]的以東王國<ref name="books.google.com.au">[http://books.google.com.au/books?id=YzQe_4Waz34C&printsec=frontcover&dq=The+Land+That+I+Will+Show+You:+Essays+in+History+and+Archaeology+of+the+Ancient+Near+East&source=bl&ots=Jjp2Y0h3Sz&sig=jauEX7cJJ8_AIVgGe9KfjDjYQzg&hl=en&ei=IQbxS-OzIpCTkAXp59HWBg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CBgQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q&f=false Piotr Bienkowski, "New Evidence on Edom in the Neo-Babylonian and Persian Periods", in John Andrew Dearman, Matt Patrick Graham, (eds), "The land that I will show you: essays on the history and archaeology of the Ancient Near East in honour of J. Maxwell Miller" (Sheffield Academic Press, 2001), pp.2198ff]</ref>，在希臘[[古典時代|古典時代]]也用名源詞Idumea來指此地區一個較小的區域。以東這個名字在希伯來文中為「紅」，一開始是給[[以撒|以撒]]的長子、[[雅各_(舊約聖經)|雅各]]的哥哥[[以掃|以掃]]的名字。因為他因為紅豆湯而將長子的名分賣給弟弟，故得此名<!--<ref>{{bibleref2|Genesis|25:30}}</ref>-->。[[摩西五经|摩西五经]]、[[塔納赫|塔納赫]]及[[新約|新約]]均認為以東人是以掃的後裔。

'''设计保障安全'''（Safety by design）是一個概念及運動，鼓勵[[營建管理|營建管理]]者及[[產品設計|產品設計]]者在設計開發階段就將健康及安全風險納入設計考量。此概念支持[[品質|品質]]、程序及成本的觀點，安全性在設計階段就已納入考慮<ref>Behm M, 2005, Linking Construction Fatalities to the Design for Construction Safety Concept, Safety Science, Number 43, Pages 589-611</ref><ref>Churcher D W, Alwani-Starr G M,Incorporating construction health and safety into the design process”, Implementation of Health and Safety in Construction Sites, Alvels, Dias & Coble (eds), ISBN 90-5410-847-9, 1996</ref><ref>ECI 2000 Designing for Safety and Health, Proceedings of the ECI/CIB/HSE international Conference, London, European Construction Institute, Loughborough, ed A G F Gibb, June 245pp ECI, Publications</ref><ref>Gambatese JA,  Hinze J and Haas C, Tool to Design for Construction Worker Safety, Journal of Architectural Engineering, Volume 3, Part 1, Pages 32-42, 1997</ref><ref>Gambatese JA, 2003, Safety in design: A Proactive Approach to Construction Worker Safety and Health, Applied Occupational and Environmental Hygiene, Vol 18 (5): pp 339-342</ref><ref>Gambatese JA, Hinze J, Addressing Construction Worker Safety in the Design Phase – Designing for worker Safety, Automation in Construction, Volume 8, Issue 6, Pages 643-649, August 1999</ref><ref>Hecker S, Gambatese J, Weinstein M, Designing for Worker Safety- Moving the Construction Safety Process Upstream, Professional Safety, Pages 32-44, September 2005</ref>。

歐洲立法要求營建設計者在設計者就考慮風險，減少建造時{{link-en|對建筑工人的危害|Construction site safety#Hazards to construction workers}}，並配合像移动工地指令（英國的CDM法規）中的階段<ref>[http://www.hse.gov.uk/construction/cdm.htm Health and Safety Executive CDM]</ref>,此概念符合法律的要求。

許多[[非政府组织|非政府组织]]是為了支持此一目的而成立，主要是在英國、澳洲及美國<ref>[http://www.saferdesign.org/Pages/default.aspx Safer Design] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140102015131/http://www.saferdesign.org/Pages/default.aspx |date=2014-01-02 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.safeworkaustralia.gov.au/swa/HealthSafety/SafeDesign/ Safe Work Australia] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091214053507/http://www.safeworkaustralia.gov.au/swa/HealthSafety/SafeDesign/ |date=2009-12-14 }}</ref><ref>[https://secure.sph.harvard.edu/ccpe/programs.cfm?CSID=SDC0210&pg=cluster&CLID=1  Harvard University School of Public health] {{Wayback|url=https://secure.sph.harvard.edu/ccpe/programs.cfm?CSID=SDC0210&pg=cluster&CLID=1 |date=20100816172545 }}</ref>。

有些{{link-en|公證單位|Notified Body}}提供測試及設計驗證服務，確認[[守规|符合]]像[[美國機械工程師學會|美國機械工程師學會]]等機構訂定的[[安全標準|安全標準]]<ref>[http://www.tuv.com/en/corporate/business_customers/plants_machinery_1/pressure_equipment_2/asme_1/asme.html Boiler and Pressure Vessel Inspection According to ASME]</ref>。

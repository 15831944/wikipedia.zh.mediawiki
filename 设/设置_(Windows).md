'''设置'''，曾称“'''电脑设置'''”（'''PC Settings'''），它是一个[[Metro-style_apps|UWP应用]]，搭载于[[Windows_8|Windows 8]]及之后，[[Windows_Server_2012|Windows Server 2012]]及之后，以及[[Windows_10_Mobile|Windows 10 Mobile]]中。

== 功能 ==
在[[Windows_Server_2012|Windows Server 2012]]和[[Windows_8|Windows 8]]中，“电脑设置”被设计为一个为触摸设备优化的简单设置界面。它展示了一小部分通过桌面的[[控制面板|控制面板]]可更改的设置。此外，部分功能（包括添加用户帐户和更改用户图片）只能在“电脑设置”中完成。<ref name="pcw-addingmanaging8">{{Cite web|url=http://www.pcworld.com/article/258875/adding_and_managing_users_in_windows_8.html|title=Adding and Managing Users in Windows 8|accessdate=20 September 2015|publisher=IDG|website=PC World}}</ref>Windows 8.1改进了此组件以包括更多原独占于控制面板的选项。<ref name="ars-wblue">{{Cite web|url=http://arstechnica.com/information-technology/2013/03/windows-blue-leaks-more-metro-more-multitasking/|title=Windows Blue leaks: More Metro, more multitasking|accessdate=January 20, 2014|date=March 25, 2013|last=Bright|first=Peter|work=[[Ars_Technica|Ars Technica]]|publisher=[[Condé_Nast|Condé Nast]]}}</ref>

在Windows 10中，此应用被更名为“设置”并与其他Windows应用一样运行在桌面窗口中。它包括更多以前只存在于控制面板中的选项。<ref name="pcpro-settingsmenu">{{Cite web|url=http://www.pcpro.co.uk/software/1000331/windows-10-review-technical-preview-january-2015|title=Windows 10 review: Technical Preview (January 2015)|accessdate=7 February 2015|website=PC Pro}}</ref><ref name="cnet-settingsmenu">{{Cite web|url=http://www.cnet.com/how-to/windows-10-settings-menu-the-system-tab/|title=Windows 10 Settings Menu: The System tab|accessdate=7 February 2015|website=CNET}}</ref>因为它是一个通用Windows应用，此版本的设置也被适用于[[智能手机|智能手机]]和[[平板电脑|平板电脑]]的[[Windows_10_Mobile|Windows 10 Mobile]]使用。<ref name="verge-universalpreview">{{Cite web|url=http://www.theverge.com/2015/1/21/7866941/windows-previews-universal-apps-for-windows-10-pcs-tablets-and-phones|title=Microsoft previews universal apps for Windows 10 PCs, tablets, and phones|accessdate=February 7, 2015|website=The Verge}}</ref><ref name="ars-febleak">{{Cite web|url=http://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2015/02/leaks-of-windows-10-for-phones-show-us-what-to-expect/|title=Leaked images of Windows 10 for phones show us what to expect|accessdate=February 7, 2015|website=Ars Technica}}</ref>

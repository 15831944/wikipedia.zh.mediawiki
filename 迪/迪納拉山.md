'''迪納拉山'''（Dinara）是位於[[克羅埃西亞|克羅埃西亞]]和[[波黑|波黑]]兩國交界處的一座山峰。迪納拉山有兩座主峰，分別是特羅格拉夫峰（Troglav，1913米）和迪納拉峰（1831米）<ref name="2013 Yearbook">''Statistical Yearbook of the Republic of Croatia 2015'', p. 48</ref>。迪納拉山是克羅埃西亞的最高峰，相對海拔高度有728米<ref name="pb">[http://www.peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=10325 "Dinara" on Peakbagger.com] Retrieved 1 October 2011</ref><ref>[http://sites.google.com/site/europeaklist/Home/croatia Geography of Croatia on Europeaklist] Gives a topographic prominence of 728 based on an elevation of 1,831 m. Retrieved 1 October 2011</ref>。

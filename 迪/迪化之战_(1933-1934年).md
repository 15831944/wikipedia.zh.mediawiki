'''第二次迪化之战'''是1933年—1934年冬在[[新疆省_(中華民國)|新疆省]][[迪化县|迪化县]]的一场发生于[[盛世才|盛世才]]领导的新疆省政府军和中国穆斯林将领[[马仲英|马仲英]]和汉族将领[[张培元|张培元]]联军之间的冲突。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=IAs9AAAAIAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=warlords+and+muslims&source=bl&ots=KzhN9ZfrrL&sig=9H6HE7DDEdFGnZVWBE905SPdX4E&hl=en&ei=h-8iTMTuBIOclgeXlcC9Dg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&ved=0CBsQ6AEwAg#v=snippet&q=chang%20secret%20negotiations%20with%20ma%20chung-ying%20ousting&f=false|title=Warlords and Muslims in Chinese Central Asia: a political history of Republican Sinkiang 1911-1949|author=Andrew D. W. Forbes|year=1986|publisher=CUP Archive|location=Cambridge, England|isbn=0-521-25514-7|page=238|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=P3xCAAAAYAAJ&q=zhang+peiyuan+ma+zhongying&dq=zhang+peiyuan+ma+zhongying&hl=en&ei=P_EiTPysL8T_lgfHyaC1Dg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&ved=0CDQQ6AEwAg|title=Xinjiang: the land and the people|author=Pʻing Cheng|year=1989|publisher=New World Press|location=|page=54|isbn=7-80005-078-5|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>

张培元夺取了塔城和新疆省会迪化之间的道路。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=t6QOAAAAQAAJ&pg=PA256&dq=soviets+defeated+by+tungans&hl=en&ei=W-EiTIeUO4SClAfJoeilDQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=6&ved=0CDwQ6AEwBQ#v=onepage&q=Chang%20pei-yuen%20ma%20chung-yin%20tihwa%20road%20tacheng&f=false|title=China and the Soviet Union|author=Taylor & Francis|year=|publisher=|location=|page=257|isbn=|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>盛世才指挥[[东北义勇军|东北义勇军]]旧部和一队由巴品古特上校率领的[[归化军|归化军]]。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=IAs9AAAAIAAJ&printsec=frontcover&dq=warlords+and+muslims&source=bl&ots=KzhN9ZfrrL&sig=9H6HE7DDEdFGnZVWBE905SPdX4E&hl=en&ei=h-8iTMTuBIOclgeXlcC9Dg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&ved=0CBsQ6AEwAg#v=snippet&q=Pappengut%20white%20russian%20volunteer&f=false|title=Warlords and Muslims in Chinese Central Asia: a political history of Republican Sinkiang 1911-1949|author=Andrew D. W. Forbes|year=1986|publisher=CUP Archive|location=Cambridge, England|isbn=0-521-25514-7|page=119|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=[[News_from_Tartary:_A_Journey_from_Peking_to_Kashmir|News from Tartary: A Journey from Peking to Kashmir]]|author=Peter Fleming|authorlink = Peter Fleming (writer)|year=1999|publisher=Northwestern University Press|location=Evanston Illinois|isbn=0-8101-6071-4|page=251|pages=}}<!--|accessdate=2010-06-28--></ref>尽管[[国民政府|国民政府]]准备宣誓任盛世才为新疆自治省政府主席，却因为盛世才与[[苏联|苏联]]的联系，已秘密煽动张培元和马仲英推翻盛世才。国民党领袖[[蒋介石|蒋介石]]派[[罗文干|罗文干]]到新疆见了马、张，劝说他们灭盛。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=rsLQdBUgyMUC&pg=PA39&dq=ma+lin+muslim&hl=en&ei=NX2qTLHbJoH98AahxJGCDQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=4&ved=0CDcQ6AEwAzgK#v=onepage&q=luo%20wen'gan%20turfan%20ili%20ma%20zhongying%20zhang%20peiyuan&f=false|title=Modern China's Ethnic Frontiers: A Journey to the West|author=Hsiao-ting Lin|year=2010|publisher=Taylor & Francis|location=|isbn=0-415-58264-4|page=41|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>

马、张的汉回联军几乎击败了盛世才，盛世才求助于苏联。这导致了[[苏联入侵新疆|苏联入侵新疆]]和马仲英在[[头屯河战役|头屯河战役]]后撤军。

此时蒋介石准备亲自参战，以15万余军队和1500万元帮助马仲英驱逐盛世才。但他被告知不能确保军队有足够的食物、水和其他补给，于是蒋介石取消了行动。盛世才说：“蒋介石不喜欢我的政策，但他对我无可奈何。我距离他太远了。”<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SK7Jdfnf9RIC&pg=PA186&dq=did+not+allow+the+russians+sheng+chiang&hl=en&ei=IxRPTMKzBoT48Aas7_WRBg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CCoQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=Chiang%20planned%20militay%20against%20sheng%20troops%20supplies&f=false|title=Dilemmas of victory: the early years of the People's Republic of China|author=Jeremy Brown, Paul Pickowicz|year=2007|publisher=Harvard University Press|location=|isbn=0-674-02616-0|page=186|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>

'''迪士尼+'''（{{lang-en|'''Disney+'''}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/11/19/business/media/disney-fox-china-approval.html|title=Disney Wins Chinese Approval for Fox Deal, Clearing Major Hurdle|first=Brooks|last=Barnes|date=2018-11-19|publisher=''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]''|accessdate=2019-02-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/business-46148040|title=Netflix chief ready for Disney battle|author=|date=2018-11-09|publisher=[[BBC_Online|BBC Online]]|accessdate=2019-02-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/videos/2019-02-06/ryvicker-going-to-need-very-little-marketing-for-disney-plus-video|title=Ryvicker: Going to Need Very Little Marketing for Disney Plus|author=|date=2019-02-06|publisher=[[Bloomberg_L.P.|Bloomberg L.P.]]|accessdate=2019-02-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.irishtimes.com/business/media-and-marketing/disney-entertainment-empire-joins-battle-for-streamers-credit-cards-1.3781943|title=Disney entertainment empire joins battle for streamers’ credit cards|first=Laura|last=Slattery|date=2019-02-05|publisher=''The Irish Times''|accessdate=2019-02-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2019/biz/news/disney-disney-plus-150-million-revenue-launch-1203129481/|title=Disney to Forgo $150 Million in Fiscal 2019 as it Prepares to Launch Disney Plus|first=Cynthia|last=Littleton|date=2019-02-05|publisher=''[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]''|accessdate=2019-02-06}}</ref>是{{le|迪士尼流媒體服務|BAMTech}}（前為BAMTech）即將推出的在線[[流媒體|流媒體]][[OTT服務|OTT]][[視頻點播|視頻點播]]服務平台，是[[華特迪士尼公司|華特迪士尼公司]]的{{le|華特迪士尼直接面向消費者和國際|Walt Disney Direct-to-Consumer and International}}分部的子公司。

該平台計劃於2019年九月在[[美國|美國]]開啟運營，主要專注於來自[[華特迪士尼影業集團|華特迪士尼影業集團]]製作的影視內容。此外還計劃開發現有和新的影視項目，包括[[漫威娛樂|漫威娛樂]]和《[[星際大戰|星際大戰]]》系列。

迪士尼+將與[[Hulu|Hulu]]一起同在線流媒體平台[[Netflix|網飛]]展開競爭，它將在迪士尼與網飛簽訂的分銷協議到期後，於2019年11月12日正式運行。

== 歷史 ==
2016年8月，迪士尼以10億美元的價格收購了{{le|BAMTech}}（{{le|美國職棒大聯盟進階媒體公司|MLB Advanced Media}}經營流媒體技術業務的分拆公司）的少數股權，並在將來可能擁有更多股權。隨後，迪士尼旗下[[ESPN|ESPN]]宣佈基於其技術{{le|ESPN+}}開展「探索性[[OTT服務|OTT服務]]項目」，以取代現有的線性電視平台<ref name="variety-disneybam">{{cite web|title=What’s Behind Disney’s $1 Billion Investment in Major League Baseball’s Digital Arm|url=https://variety.com/2016/digital/news/disney-major-league-baseball-digital-deal-1201838818/|website=Variety|accessdate=2016-12-18|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Miller|first1=Daniel|title=Walt Disney Co. buys stake in video streaming service BAMTech|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/envelope/cotown/la-et-ct-disney-earnings-20160809-snap-story.html|accessdate=2016-08-17|work=[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]|publisher=Tronc|date=2016-08-09|language=en-US}}</ref>。2017年8月8日，迪士尼以15.8億美元的價格收購了BAMTech的控股權，將其股權增持至75%。迪士尼宣佈計劃推出第二個迪士尼品牌的直接面向消費者的娛樂服務，將在2019年與[[Netflix|網飛]]簽訂的分銷協議到期時正式運行<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.cnet.com/news/marvel-and-star-wars-films-will-ditch-netflix-for-disney/ |title=Marvel and Star Wars films will ditch Netflix for Disney's own service |work=[[CNET|CNET]] |publisher=[[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive]] |access-date=2017-09-07|language=en-US |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170907190259/https://www.cnet.com/news/marvel-and-star-wars-films-will-ditch-netflix-for-disney/ |archivedate=2017-09-07 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Kastrenakes |first=Jacob |title=Disney to end Netflix deal and launch its own streaming service |url=https://www.theverge.com/2017/8/8/16115254/disney-launching-streaming-service-ending-netflix-deal |website=[[The_Verge|The Verge]] |accessdate=2018-04-05 |date=2017-08-08|deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180406102020/https://www.theverge.com/2017/8/8/16115254/disney-launching-streaming-service-ending-netflix-deal |archivedate=2018-04-06|language=en-US  }}</ref>。

2017年12月，迪士尼宣佈有意收購[[二十一世紀福斯|二十一世紀福斯]]的主要娛樂資產。此次收購旨在為其流媒體平台擴充迪士尼自製內容<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://variety.com/2017/biz/news/disney-fox-merger-deal-52-4-billion-merger-1202631242/|title=Disney to Buy 21st Century Fox Assets for $52.4 Billion in Historic Hollywood Merger|last=Littleton|first=Cynthia|last2=Steinberg|first2=Brian|work=Variety|date=2017-12-14|accessdate=2017-12-15|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2017-12-14/disney-buys-fox-assets-in-52-billion-breakup-of-murdoch-empire|title=Disney Buys Fox Assets in $52 Billion Split of Murdoch Realm|last=Sakoui|first=Anousha|work=Bloomberg|date=2017-12-14|accessdate=2017-12-15|language=en-US}}</ref>。2019年3月20日，迪士尼正式完成[[迪士尼收購21世紀福斯|收購二十一世紀福斯‎]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2019/03/12/disney-fox-deal-closing/|title=Disney says Fox acquisition will close on March 20|date=2019-03-12|last=Ha|first=Anthony|website=Tech Crunch|accessdate=2019-03-12}}</ref>。

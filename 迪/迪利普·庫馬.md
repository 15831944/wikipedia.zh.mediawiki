'''迪利普·庫馬'''（{{lang-ur|'''دلیپ کمار'''}}，{{lang-en|'''Dilip Kumar'''}}，真實姓名為：穆罕默德·尤薩夫·汗（Muhammad Yusuf Khan）；{{bd|1922年|12月11日|catIdx=K}}）是[[印度|印度]]知名电影男演員。迪利普·庫馬是印度電影界最受尊崇的演員之一，有「悲劇之王」（Tragedy King）的綽號。<ref>[http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/dilip-kumar-happy-birthday-turns-94-a-look-at-his-journey-india-first-method-actor-4420625/ “Happy Birthday Dilip Kumar: As Dilip Kumar turns 94, a look at his titanic reputation as India’s finest method actor”]. The Indian Express. 11-12-2016.</ref>

他曾八次榮獲[[印度電影觀眾獎|印度電影觀眾獎]]的最佳男演員殊榮。<ref>[http://www.bbc.com/news/35638217 "Dilip Kumar: India actor cleared in bounced cheque case"]. BBC. 23-02-2016.</ref>庫馬最為知名的電影作品是1960年的《[[我王莫臥兒|我王莫臥兒]]》（Mughul-E-Azam）而他和女主角[[瑪杜芭拉|瑪杜芭拉]]的一段感情至今仍是話題之一。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/home/sunday-times/deep-focus/Why-the-Dilip-Kumar-Madhubala-love-story-had-a-sad-ending/articleshow/35871456.cms “Why the Dilip Kumar-Madhubala love story had a sad ending”]. Times of India. 01-06-2014.</ref>

2015年，迪利普·庫馬獲得印度政府頒發的[[莲花赐勋章|莲花赐勋章]]（Padma Vibhushan），以表揚他在演藝界的成就。<ref>[https://www.hindustantimes.com/bollywood/dilip-kumar-honoured-with-padma-vibhushan/story-uQbbiwn4VxXAtoIrwRI8IL.html “Honoured with Padma Vibhushan, Dilip Kumar tears up”]. Hindustan Times. 14-12-2015.</ref>

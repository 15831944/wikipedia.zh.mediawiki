'''迪雅·汗'''（{{lang-ur|دیا خان}}，{{lang-en|Deeyah Khan}}，{{bd|1977年|8月7日|catIdx=K}}）是一名[[挪威|挪威]]電影女导演和人权活动家。<ref>{{Cite web
|author= |url=http://www.thepixelproject.net/2012/02/13/activism-101-10-music-artistes-who-support-the-cause-to-end-violence-against-women/|title=10 Music Artistes Who Support The Cause To End Violence Against Women activist
|publisher= www.thepixelproject.net|date= |accessdate= 13 February 2012}}</ref>她的父親是一名來自[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]的[[旁遮普人|旁遮普人]]，母亲則是一名[[阿富汗|阿富汗]][[普什圖人|普什圖人]]。<ref>[https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2017/dec/04/the-muslim-director-who-filmed-neo-nazis-i-thought-im-not-going-to-make-it-out “The Muslim director who filmed neo-Nazis: ‘I thought – I’m not going to make it out’”]. The Guardian. 04-12-2017.</ref>

她出道作品是2012年伊朗電影
《Banaz a Love Story》並因此榮獲2013年[[艾美奖|艾美奖]]的最佳外語影片獎（Best International Current Affairs Film）及[[皮博迪獎|皮博迪獎]]。<ref>[https://www.uio.no/english/about/facts/awards/human-rights/2015/deeyah-khan-artist-and-champion-of-womens-rights.html "Deeyah Khan, artist and champion of women’s rights, is awarded the University of Oslo’s Human Rights Award"]. University of Oslo. 16-09-2015.</ref>

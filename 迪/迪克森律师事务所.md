'''迪克森律师事务所'''('''Dickinson Wright PLLC''', 旧名字: '''Dickinson, Wright, Moon, Van Dusen & Freeman''')是[[美国|美国]]律师事务所,总部在[[底特律|底特律]][[:en:Downtown_Detroit|Downtown Detroit]][[:en:One_Detroit_Center|One Detroit Center]]。<ref>"[http://www.crainsdetroit.com/article/20131112/NEWS/131119961/ Dickinson Wright gaming practice enters Europe with law firm agreement in Bulgaria]." Retrieved on November 23, 2013.</ref> 迪克森律师事务所有10个办公室和小于300个律师。<ref>{{cite web|title=Organizational Profile of Dickinson Wright|url=http://www.natlawreview.com/organization/dickinson-wright-pllc|publisher=''{{link-en|The National Law Review|The National Law Review}}''|accessdate=23 November 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20131123063148/http://www.natlawreview.com/organization/dickinson-wright-pllc|archivedate=23 十一月 2013}} ()</ref>

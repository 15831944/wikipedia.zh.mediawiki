'''迪娜·杜伯倫'''（'''Dina Dublon'''，{{bd|1953年|||catIdx=Dublon}}）是一名[[哈佛商学院|哈佛商学院]]高级教授<ref>{{cite web|title=Harvard Business School|url=http://drfd.hbs.edu/fit/public/facultyInfo.do?facInfo=ovr&facId=634110|publisher=Harvard Business School|access-date=2012-09-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120514030552/http://drfd.hbs.edu/fit/public/facultyInfo.do?facInfo=ovr&facId=634110#|archive-date=2012-05-14|dead-url=yes}}</ref> 和[[微软公司|微软公司]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Microsoft bio|url=http://www.microsoft.com/presspass/bod/dublon/default.mspx|publisher=Microsoft|access-date=2012-09-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080726185930/http://www.microsoft.com/presspass/bod/dublon/default.mspx|archive-date=2008-07-26|dead-url=yes}}</ref>、[[埃森哲|埃森哲]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Accenture bio|url=http://www.accenture.com/Global/About_Accenture/Corporate_Governance/Board_of_Directors/DinaDublon.htm|publisher=Accenture|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060915162119/http://www.accenture.com/Global/About_Accenture/Corporate_Governance/Board_of_Directors/DinaDublon.htm|archivedate=2006-09-15}}</ref> 和[[百事可乐|百事可乐]]<ref>{{cite web|title= PepsiCo bio|url=http://www.pepsico.com/PEP_Investors/CorporateGovernance/BoardofDirectors/index.cfm|publisher=PepsiCo}}</ref> 董事会现任成员。她也是[[卡內基美隆大學|卡內基美隆大學]]以及一些非盈利组织（包括[[Global_Fund_for_Women|Global Fund for Women]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Global Fund for Women|url=http://www.globalfundforwomen.org/cms/content/blogcategory/36/73/|publisher=Global Fund for Women|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070520072836/http://www.globalfundforwomen.org/cms/content/blogcategory/36/73/|archivedate=2007-05-20}}</ref> 与[[Women's_Refugee_Commission|Women's Refugee Commission]]<ref>{{cite web|title= Women's Refugee Commission|url=http://www.womensrefugeecommission.org/about/board|publisher=Women's Refugee Commission}}</ref>）的一名理事。1998年至2004年她担任[[摩根大通|摩根大通]]的副财务长。

== 生平 ==
1953年她生于[[巴西|巴西]]。她获得了以色列[[希伯来大学|希伯来大学]]的[[经济学|经济学]]与[[数学|数学]]学位，后来取得[[卡內基美隆大學|卡內基美隆大學]]商学院的研究生学位。她获得了许多荣誉和奖项，如 The International Center in New York's Award of Excellence。并被[[财富杂志|财富杂志]]评为“美国企业界最有权力的50位女性”之一<ref>{{cite news|title= FORTUNE's 50 Most Powerful Women|url=http://money.cnn.com/magazines/fortune/fortune_archive/1999/10/25/267818/index.htm|publisher=CNN | first1=Jane M.|last1=Folpe|first2=Tyler|last2=Maroney|date=25 October 1999}}</ref>。

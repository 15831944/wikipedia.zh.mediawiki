2017年12月14日，[[華特迪士尼公司|華特迪士尼公司]]宣佈以524億美元的股票收購[[二十一世紀福斯|二十一世紀福斯]]的最終協議。收購的資產包括[[二十世紀福斯|二十世紀福斯]]電影和電視工作室、福斯電視集團（以及[[FX_(電視頻道)|FX]]等有線電視頻道）、[[福斯傳媒集團|福斯傳媒集團]]、[[國家地理學會|國家地理合股公司]]的股份，印度電視台[[星空傳媒_(印度)|星空傳媒]]，另外30%的[[Hulu|Hulu]]股份和其他關鍵資產。二十一世紀福斯將[[福斯廣播公司|福斯廣播公司]]、{{link-en|福斯電視台|Fox Television Stations}}、[[福斯新聞頻道|福斯新聞頻道]]、[[福斯財經網|福斯財經網]]、{{link-en|福斯體育一台|Fox Sports 1}}、{{link-en|福斯體育二台|Fox Sports 2}}、{{link-en|福斯體育西語頻道|Fox Deportes}}和{{link-en|十大聯盟電視網|Big Ten Network}}分拆成新的“福斯”公司<ref name="Disney Old">{{cite web|url=https://www.thewaltdisneycompany.com/walt-disney-company-acquire-twenty-first-century-fox-inc-spinoff-certain-businesses-52-4-billion-stock-2/|title=The Walt Disney Company To Acquire Twenty-First Century Fox, Inc., After Spinoff of Certain Businesses, For $52.4 Billion in Stock|date=2017-12-14|work=The Walt Disney Company|access-date=2018-08-05|language=en-US|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180731133830/https://www.thewaltdisneycompany.com/walt-disney-company-acquire-twenty-first-century-fox-inc-spinoff-certain-businesses-52-4-billion-stock-2/|archive-date=2018-07-31|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="Fox Official">{{cite web|title=21st Century Fox to Spin off Businesses and Create New "Fox"; A Growth Company Centered on Live News and Sports Brands and the Iconic Fox Brand|url=https://www.21cf.com/news/21st-century-fox/2017/21st-century-fox-spin-businesses-create-new-fox-growth-company-2/|work=21st Century Fox|date=2017-12-14|accessdate=2018-12-10}}</ref>。

[[康卡斯特|康卡斯特]]（[[NBC環球|NBC環球]]的母公司）於2018年6月13日提出了他們的報價，以650億美元的現金收購迪士尼即將購買的福斯資產，觸發了兩家公司之間的競購戰<ref name="LDWA">{{cite web|url=https://corporate.comcast.com/stories/comcast-makes-superior-all-cash-proposal-to-acquire-21st-century-fox-after-spinoff-of-new-fox|title=Comcast Makes Superior All-Cash Proposal to Acquire 21st Century Fox After Spinoff of ‘New Fox’|work=Comcast Corp.|date=2018-06-13|accessdate=2018-12-10}}</ref>。一星期後，迪士尼以713億美元的報價反擊<ref name="Disney Official">{{Cite news|url=https://www.thewaltdisneycompany.com/the-walt-disney-company-signs-amended-acquisition-agreement-to-acquire-twenty-first-century-fox-inc-for-71-3-billion-in-cash-and-stock/|title=The Walt Disney Company Signs Amended Acquisition Agreement To Acquire Twenty-First Century Fox, Inc., For $71.3 Billion in Cash And Stock|date=2018-06-20|work=The Walt Disney Company|accessdate=2018-08-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180622032442/https://www.thewaltdisneycompany.com/the-walt-disney-company-signs-amended-acquisition-agreement-to-acquire-twenty-first-century-fox-inc-for-71-3-billion-in-cash-and-stock/|archive-date=2018-06-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。康卡斯特於7月19日正式結束與迪士尼的競購戰，以專注收購[[英國天空廣播|英國天空廣播]]的控股權<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cmcsa.com/static-files/1c3ad80a-d137-4e81-ad33-34fc2229ebec|title=
Comcast Corporation Statement on 21st Century Fox|work=Comcast Corp.|date=2018-07-19|accessdate=2018-12-10}}</ref>。迪士尼和福斯的股東於7月27日批准了該收購計劃<ref name="Approval">{{cite web|url=https://www.thewaltdisneycompany.com/21st-century-fox-and-disney-stockholders-approve-acquisition-by-disney/|title=21st Century Fox And Disney Stockholders Approve Acquisition By Disney|work=The Walt Disney Company|date=2018-07-27|accessdate=2018-12-10}}</ref>。該交易已於2019年3月20日完成<ref name="Sports">{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2019/biz/news/fox-disney-regional-sports-networks-bid-1203105421|title=Fox Confirms It Won't Bid on Disney's Regional Sports Networks|last=Littleton|first=Cynthia|work=Variety|date=2019-01-11|accessdate=2019-01-11}}</ref>。

== 歷史 ==
=== 早期 ===
2017年11月6日，據[[消費者新聞與商業頻道|CNBC]]報導稱，[[華特迪士尼公司|華特迪士尼公司]]正在與[[魯柏·梅鐸|魯柏·梅鐸]]洽談收購[[二十一世紀福斯|二十一世紀福斯]]電影娛樂、有線娛樂和直播衛星部門，包括[[二十世紀福斯|二十世紀福斯]]、[[FX_(電視頻道)|FX電視台]]和[[國家地理學會|國家地理合股公司]]。據報導，該交易將不包括[[福斯廣播公司|福斯廣播公司]]、二十世紀福斯的工作室、{{link-en|福斯電視站|Fox Television Stations}}、[[福斯新聞頻道|福斯新聞頻道]]集團和[[FOX體育_(美國)|福斯體育]]，這些公司將分拆成由梅鐸家族經營的新獨立公司<ref>{{cite news|last=Faber|first=David|title=21st Century Fox has been holding talks to sell most of the company to Disney: Sources|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2017/11/06/21st-century-fox-has-been-holding-talks-to-sell-most-of-company-to-disney-sources.html|accessdate=2018-07-05|work=CNBC|date=2017-11-06|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180706021750/https://www.cnbc.com/2017/11/06/21st-century-fox-has-been-holding-talks-to-sell-most-of-company-to-disney-sources.html|archivedate=2018-07-06|dead-url=no }}</ref>。

此外，該交易還包括某些的電影版權，例如《[[X戰警|X戰警]]》和《[[驚奇4超人|驚奇4超人]]》，以及《[[星際大戰四部曲：曙光乍現|星際大戰四部曲：曙光乍現]]》的發行權，這些版權都並非由迪士尼的[[漫威影業|漫威影業]]和[[盧卡斯影業|盧卡斯影業]]所擁有。該洽談在當天沒有達成協議的情況下停滯不前<ref>{{cite news|last=Shaw|first=Lucas|last2=Sakoui|first2=Anousha|title=Disney Explored Buying Fox Assets; Talks Now Dead|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2017-11-06/disney-looked-at-buying-most-of-fox-talks-now-dead-cnbc-says|work=Bloomberg|date=2017-11-06|accessdate=2017-11-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171106214434/https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2017-11-06/disney-looked-at-buying-most-of-fox-talks-now-dead-cnbc-says|archivedate=2017-11-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=White|first=Adam|title=Why Disney buying 20th Century Fox could be good news for Marvel and Star Wars fans|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/films/2017/11/07/disney-buying-20th-century-fox-could-good-news-marvel-star-wars/|work=The Daily Telegraph|date=2017-11-07|accessdate=2017-12-03|language=en-GB|issn=0307-1235|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171204064336/http://www.telegraph.co.uk/films/2017/11/07/disney-buying-20th-century-fox-could-good-news-marvel-star-wars/|archivedate=2017-12-04}}</ref>，但在11月10日，據報導交易尚未完全放棄<ref>{{cite news|last=Moyer|first=Liz|title=Fox shares jump as 'pencils aren't down' on possible Disney deal|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2017/11/10/pencils-arent-down-on-disney-21st-century-fox-deal.html|work=[[CNBC|CNBC]]|date=2017-11-10|accessdate=2017-11-10|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6usi9jQb4?url=https://www.cnbc.com/2017/11/10/pencils-arent-down-on-disney-21st-century-fox-deal.html|archivedate=2017-11-11}}</ref>。

11月28日，儘管有傳聞說迪士尼和福斯之間的談判正在快速發展<ref>{{cite news|last=Fleming Jr.|first=Mike|title=Bart & Fleming: Sparks Rekindling On Disney-Fox Deal? 12月11日，康卡斯特宣佈放棄對福斯資產的收購<ref>{{cite news|last=Littleton|first=Cynthia|title=Disney Nearing Finish Line With 21st Century Fox as Comcast Bows Out of Acquisition Hunt|url=https://variety.com/2017/biz/news/disney-21st-century-fox-comcast-sale-out-1202636618/|work=Variety|date=2017-12-11|accessdate=2017-12-11|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171211234648/http://variety.com/2017/biz/news/disney-21st-century-fox-comcast-sale-out-1202636618/|archivedate=2017-12-11}}</ref>。12月14日，迪士尼宣佈以524億美元收購二十一世紀福斯的大部分資產，並等待{{link-en|美國司法部反壟斷部門|United States Department of Justice Antitrust Division}}的批准<ref name="Disney Old"/><ref>{{cite news|last=Castillo|first=Michelle|title=Disney to buy 21st Century Fox assets in a deal worth more than $52 billion in stock|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2017/12/14/disney-to-buy-21st-century-fox-assets.html|work=CNBC|date=2017-12-14|accessdate=2017-12-14|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171214133050/https://www.cnbc.com/2017/12/14/disney-to-buy-21st-century-fox-assets.html|archivedate=2017-12-14}}</ref>。

=== 迪士尼與康卡斯特之間的競購戰 ===
2018年5月7日，據報導康卡斯特向投資銀行發表講話，談到了迪士尼收購福斯的提議<ref>{{cite web|last=Roumeliotis|first=Greg|title=Exclusive: Comcast prepares all-cash bid to gate-crash Disney-Fox deal — sources|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-fox-m-a-comcast-exclusive/exclusive-comcast-prepares-all-cash-bid-to-gate-crash-disney-fox-deal-sources-idUSKBN1I82I7|work=Reuters|date=2018-05-07|accessdate=2018-05-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180507225039/https://www.reuters.com/article/us-fox-m-a-comcast-exclusive/exclusive-comcast-prepares-all-cash-bid-to-gate-crash-disney-fox-deal-sources-idUSKBN1I82I7|archive-date=2018-05-07|dead-url=no}}</ref>。不久之後，迪士尼執行長[[勞勃·艾格|勞勃·艾格]]表示他願意放棄[[天空公司|天空公司]]以確保收購福斯<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/disney-could-give-up-sky-get-fox-assets-1110153|title=Disney Could Give Up Sky to Get Fox Assets|website=The Hollywood Reporter|last=Bond|first=Paul|date=2018-05-10|accessdate=2018-05-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180510004054/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/disney-could-give-up-sky-get-fox-assets-1110153|archive-date=2018-05-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

幾位福斯投資者表示，若康卡斯特計劃以600億美元現金繼續競爭，他們將公開終止與迪士尼的協議。由於持有特別投票權的股票，梅鐸家族信託控制了福斯39%的股份。然而，根據該公司的章程，這些特殊權利並不適用於對迪士尼/福斯交易的投票，而梅鐸信託只控制了17%的選票，這使得其他股東更容易擊敗他，並預期在下個月發生<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2018/05/15/comcasts-all-cash-bid-could-pit-murdoch-against-fox-shareholders.html|title=Comcast's all-cash bid could pit Murdoch against Fox shareholders|work=CNBC|date=2018-05-15|accessdate=2018-05-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180515181006/https://www.cnbc.com/2018/05/15/comcasts-all-cash-bid-could-pit-murdoch-against-fox-shareholders.html|archive-date=2018-05-15|dead-url=no}}</ref>。同月，確認由[[拉克蘭·梅鐸|拉克蘭·梅鐸]]接管新的福斯公司，而不是{{link-en|詹姆斯·梅鐸|James Murdock}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://money.cnn.com/2018/05/16/media/lachlan-murdoch-new-fox-ceo/index.html|title=Lachlan Murdoch, not James, will lead New Fox company|last=Disis|first=Jill|work=CNN Money|date=2018-05-16|accessdate=2018-05-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180517012745/http://money.cnn.com/2018/05/16/media/lachlan-murdoch-new-fox-ceo/index.html|archive-date=2018-05-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

12月14日，合併受到墨西哥的監管，其中迪士尼和福斯將佔所有類型內容的分發的27.8%。體育廣播是墨西哥的主要關注點<ref name="ElUniversal">{{cite web|url=https://www.eluniversal.com.mx/english/strict-regulation-needed-disney-fox-merger-in-mexico-0|title=Strict regulation needed for Disney-Fox Merger in Mexico|work=El Universal|date=2018-12-14|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>。

12月26日，據[[NBC新聞|NBC新聞]]報導該交易預計將在2019年1月的最後一周完成<ref name="NBC">{{cite web|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/news/all/disney-verizon-headed-blackout-battle-n952026|title=Disney and Verizon headed for blackout battle|work=NBC News|date=2018-12-26|accessdate=2018-12-29}}</ref>。

2019年1月3日，據《彭博社》報導巴西經濟防衛行政委員會預計將批准該交易，且不會強迫迪士尼出售任何資產。CADE預計會看到迪士尼和福斯的提案，其中包括2018年12月兩家公司之間的一些“來回會議”後的變化。交易預計在2019年1月30日作出裁決<ref>{{cite web|url=https://seekingalpha.com/news/3420509-bloomberg-brazil-expected-ok-fox-disney-deal-without-divestment|title=Bloomberg: Brazil expected to OK Fox-Disney deal without divestment|last=Aycock|first=Jason|work=Seeking Alpha|date=2019-01-03|accessdate=2019-01-03}}</ref>。

1月7日，福斯以“新福斯”的名義向[[美國證券交易委員會|美國證券交易委員會]]提交了註冊聲明<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2019/01/21st-century-fox-files-registration-statement-sec-new-fox-1202530940/|title=21st Century Fox Files Registration Statement With SEC To Form 'New' Fox|last=Chmielewski|first=Dawn|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=2019-01-07|accessdate=2019-01-08}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.21cf.com/news/21st-century-fox/2019/21st-century-fox-announces-filing-of-registration-statement-on-form-10-for-fox-corporation/|title=21st Century Fox Announces Filing of Registration Statement on Form 10 for Fox Corporation|work=21st Century Fox|date=2019-01-07|accessdate=2019-01-08}}</ref>。

1月11日，據報導該交易預計將於2019年2月或3月完成<ref name="Sports"/>。

3月20日，該交易完成。

[[Image:Treponema_pallidum_-_very_high_mag_-_extreme_crop.jpg|thumb]]'''迪特勒染色'''（Dieterle stain）是'''微生物'''學中的一種染色手段，屬於一種銀染法的變體，染色劑爲[[硝酸銀|硝酸銀]]，可以將目標微生物染成深棕色、黑色或灰色，而背景則是黃色<ref>{{cite web|url=https://medical-dictionary.thefreedictionary.com/Dieterle+stain|title=Dieterle stain|publisher=Farlex}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.americanmastertech.com/instructions/ktdie.pdf|title=protocol for Dieterle stain}}</ref>。該方法可以對引發[[貓抓病|貓抓病]]的[[漢氏巴爾通體|漢氏巴爾通體]]（''Bartonella henselae''）、引發梅毒的[[梅毒螺旋體|梅毒螺旋體]]（''Treponema pallidum''）進行染色。較新的研究發現，該染色對[[結核分枝桿菌|結核分枝桿菌]]（''Mycobacterium tuberculosis''）也是有效的<ref name=pmid10463293>{{Cite journal  | last1 = Brady | first1 = JG. | last2 = Schutze | first2 = GE. | last3 = Seibert | first3 = R. | last4 = Horn | first4 = HV. | last5 = Marks | first5 = B. | last6 = Parham | first6 = DM. | title = Detection of mycobacterial infections using the Dieterle stain. | journal = Pediatr Dev Pathol | volume = 1 | issue = 4 | pages = 309–13 | month =  | year =  | doi =  | PMID = 10463293 }}</ref>。

'''飛虎'''，於1995年在[[丹麥|丹麥]][[哥本哈根|哥本哈根]]成立<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2013/jul/19/tiger-homewares-danish-high-street | title=The Tiger who came to town | publisher=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] | date=19 July 2013 | accessdate=27 April 2015 | author=Booth, Hannah}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.theguardian.com/business/2014/mar/30/posh-pound-shop-tiger-uk-denmark | title=Posh pound shop: Tiger sinks its claws into UK high street | publisher=The Guardian | date=30 March 2014 | accessdate=27 April 2015 | author=Wood, Zoe}}</ref>，是個「[[10元店|10元店]]」連鎖集團，直到2015年全球已經有超過500間店舖，其最主要市場為丹麥、[[英國|英國]]、[[西班牙|西班牙]]和[[意大利|意大利]]。根據其創辦人於2014年所述，飛虎的顧客人數達3千9百萬<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.limerickleader.ie/news/business/business-news/tiger-confirm-plans-to-open-limerick-store-1-6464362 | title=Tiger confirm plans to open Limerick store | publisher=The Limerick Reader | date=9 December 2014 | accessdate=27 April 2015 | author=Owens, Alan}}</ref>。

==收購合併==

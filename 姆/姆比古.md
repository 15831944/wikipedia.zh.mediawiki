'''姆比古'''（Mbigou）是[[加彭|加彭]]南部的一座城鎮，為[[恩古涅省|恩古涅省]]{{link-en|Boumi Louetsi縣|Boumi Louetsi}}的首府<ref>{{cite book|title=Gabon: Sao Tome et Principe|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-tkaf_C2fuoC&pg=PA280|accessdate=31 March 2012|publisher=Petit Futé|isbn=978-2-7469-5154-9|page=280}}</ref>。[[姆比古機場|姆比古機場]]是當地的聯外機場<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ourairports.com/airports/FOGG/pilot-info.html#general|title=General pilot information for M'Bigou Airport|publisher=OurAirports|access-date=2019-02-13|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181216073958/http://ourairports.com/airports/FOGG/pilot-info.html#general#general|archive-date=2018-12-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==歷史==
20世紀早期，姆比古有一座[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]教堂與學校設立，該學校在當地相當重要，有來自周圍城鎮的學生前來就學<ref name="DickieRake1973">{{cite book|last1=Dickie|first1=John|last2=Rake|first2=Alan|title=Who's Who in Africa: the political, military and business leaders of Africa|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=DA4ZAQAAIAAJ|accessdate=31 March 2012|date=1 January 1973|publisher=African Development|isbn=978-0-9502755-0-5|page=141}}</ref>。1990年代，姆比古得到[[加拿大|加拿大]]13.8億[[中非法郎|中非法郎]]的投資，用於當地發電、供水與[[水力發電|水力發電]]等基礎建設<ref name="Ltd1993">{{cite book|author=Africa Research, Ltd|title=Africa research bulletin: Economic, financial and technical series|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kceGAAAAIAAJ|accessdate=31 March 2012|year=1993|publisher=Blackwell|page=11260}}</ref><ref name="Marchés tropicaux et méditerranéens">{{cite book|title=Marchés tropicaux et méditerranéens|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=RTMOAQAAMAAJ|accessdate=31 March 2012|year=1993|page=39}}</ref>。

==特產==
姆比古出產的一種{{link-en|皂石|soapstone}}稱為{{link-fr|姆比古石|Pierre de Mbigou}}，開採過程相當費時，顏色可為棕色、灰色或綠色<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=g99xBAAAQBAJ&pg=PA95&dq=mbigou+airport&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwi_8tzWs7jgAhUE2VQKHSTyCq0Q6AEIODAC#v=onepage&q=mbigou%20stone&f=false|title=Gabon|page=61|author=Annelies Hickendorff|publisher=Bradt Travel Guides|year=2014|isbn=184162554X}}</ref>，是許多加彭雕塑家的創作素材<ref name="Warne2003">{{cite book|last=Warne|first=Sophie|title=Gabon, São Tomé and Príncipe|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=wTCyVLanWZwC&pg=PA118|accessdate=31 March 2012|date=1 November 2003|publisher=Bradt Travel Guides|isbn=978-1-84162-073-2|page=118}}</ref>。另外當地部落是珍貴的非洲藝術品{{link-en|普努-隆博面具|Punu-Lumbo mask}}的產地之一<ref name="amis2005">{{cite book|author=Musée Barbier-Mueller. Association des amis|title=Arts and Cultures|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=wMLpAAAAMAAJ|accessdate=31 March 2012|year=2005|publisher=The Association of Friends of the Barbier-Mueller Museum|isbn=978-88-7439-229-2|page=190}}</ref>。

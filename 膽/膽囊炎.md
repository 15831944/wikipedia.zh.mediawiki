<!-- 定義及症狀 -->
'''膽囊炎'''（Cholecystitis）是指發生於[[膽囊|膽囊]]的[[炎症|炎症]]<ref name=NICE2014>{{cite journal|last1=Internal Clinical Guidelines Team|title=Gallstone Disease: Diagnosis and Management of Cholelithiasis, Cholecystitis and Choledocholithiasis. Clinical Guideline 188|date=October 2014|pmid=25473723|page=101}}</ref>，症狀包括右上[[腹痛|腹部疼痛]]、噁心、嘔吐、偶有發燒<ref name=NEJM2008/>。多半在急性膽囊炎之前會有{{le|膽絞痛|Biliary colic}}的症狀<ref name=NEJM2008/>。但膽囊炎造成的疼痛會比一般膽絞痛的時間要長<ref name=NEJM2008/>，膽囊炎若沒有適當的治療，常會有反覆發作的情形<ref name="NEJM2008" />。急性膽囊炎的併發症包括有[[胰腺炎|胰腺炎]]、{{le|膽總管結石|Common bile duct stone}}、或是{{le|上行性膽管炎|Ascending cholangitis}}<ref name=NEJM2008>{{cite journal|last1=Strasberg|first1=SM|title=Clinical practice. Acute calculous cholecystitis.|journal=The New England Journal of Medicine|date=26 June 2008|volume=358|issue=26|pages=2804–11|pmid=18579815|doi=10.1056/nejmcp0800929}}</ref><ref name=NICE2014/>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
急性膽囊炎有90%以上是起因於{{le|膽囊管|cystic duct}}被[[膽石|膽石]]阻塞<ref name=NEJM2008/>，而造成膽石的風險因子包括{{le|口服避孕藥|Birth control pill}}、[[懷孕|懷孕]]、家族中有膽石病史、[[肥胖|肥胖]]、[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]、[[肝硬化|肝臟疾病]]、快速[[體重下降|體重下降]]<ref name=NIH2013>{{cite web|title=Gallstones|url=https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/health-topics/digestive-diseases/gallstones/Pages/facts.aspx|website=NIDDK|accessdate=27 July 2016|date=November 2013|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160728005016/https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/health-topics/digestive-diseases/gallstones/Pages/facts.aspx|archivedate=28 July 2016|df=}}</ref>有時則是因為[[血管炎|血管炎]]、[[化學療法|化學療法]]，或是正在[[重大創傷|重大創傷]]或[[燒傷|燒傷]]的復原期間<ref name=Acal2015>{{cite book|title=Sleisenger and Fordtran's Gastrointestinal and Liver Disease: Pathophysiology, Diagnosis, Management|date=2015|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9781455749898|page=1154|edition=10|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=4CMJBgAAQBAJ&pg=RA1-PA21|chapter=Section VIII|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908192055/https://books.google.com/books?id=4CMJBgAAQBAJ&pg=RA1-PA21|archivedate=2017-09-08|df=}}</ref>。膽囊炎是由症狀或檢驗發現<ref name=WS2016/>，通常再經過腹部[[超音波|超音波]]確診<ref name=WS2016/>。

<!-- 預防及治療 -->
治療通常是盡可能在24小時內，以[[腹腔鏡|腹腔鏡]]{{le|膽囊切除術|Cholecystectomy|移除膽囊}}<ref name=Pat2015/><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Schuld|first1=J|last2=Glanemann|first2=M|title=Acute Cholecystitis.|journal=Viszeralmedizin|date=June 2015|volume=31|issue=3|pages=163–5|pmid=26468309|doi=10.1159/000431275|pmc=4569253}}</ref>，建議在手術當中拍攝膽管<ref name=Pat2015>{{cite journal|last1=Patel|first1=PP|last2=Daly|first2=SC|last3=Velasco|first3=JM|title=Training vs practice: A tale of opposition in acute cholecystitis.|journal=World journal of hepatology|date=18 October 2015|volume=7|issue=23|pages=2470–3|pmid=26483868|doi=10.4254/wjh.v7.i23.2470|pmc=4606202}}</ref> 在手術中多半會使用[[抗生素|抗生素]]，但這其實較具爭議性<ref name=WS2016/><ref name=Van2016>{{cite journal|last1=van Dijk|first1=AH|last2=de Reuver|first2=PR|last3=Tasma|first3=TN|last4=van Dieren|first4=S|last5=Hugh|first5=TJ|last6=Boermeester|first6=MA|title=Systematic review of antibiotic treatment for acute calculous cholecystitis.|journal=The British journal of surgery|date=June 2016|volume=103|issue=7|pages=797–811|pmid=27027851|doi=10.1002/bjs.10146}}</ref>，在無法及時進行手術或個案較為複雜時才建議使用<ref name=WS2016/>。膽總管結石可在術前透過{{le|內視鏡逆行性膽胰管攝影術|ERCP}}移除，或是在手術當中移除<ref name=Pat2015/>。手術很少見併發症<ref name=NIH2013/>，若是無法進行手術者，可嘗試{{le|膽囊造口術|Cholecystostomy|膽囊引流術}}<ref name=WS2016/>。

<!-- 歷史、流行病學及文化 -->
已開發國家的成年人約有10%-15%有膽結石<ref name=WS2016/>，較常見於女性，並較好發於40歲過後<ref name=NIH2013/>，也有某些[[族群|族群]]的人口較容易罹患膽結石，例如[[美國原住民|美國原住民]]當中有48%為患者<ref name=NIH2013/>。在所有膽結石的患者當中，每年有1-4%發生膽絞痛<ref name=WS2016/>，若未接受治療，則約有20%膽絞痛患者會罹患膽囊炎<ref name=WS2016>{{cite journal|last1=Ansaloni|first1=L|title=2016 WSES guidelines on acute calculous cholecystitis.|journal=World journal of emergency surgery : WJES|date=2016|volume=11|pages=25|pmid=27307785|doi=10.1186/s13017-016-0082-5|pmc=4908702}}</ref>。在移除膽囊後的情形多半良好<ref name=NIH2013/>。若沒有治療，可能會有慢性膽囊炎<ref name=Sle2010>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=zEZOqB6r9hwC&pg=PA1065|title=Sleisenger & Fordtran's Gastrointestinal and liver disease pathophysiology, diagnosis, management|date=2010|publisher=MD Consult|isbn=9781437727678|edition=9|location=[S.l.]|page=1065|last1=Feldman|first1=Mark|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908192055/https://books.google.com/books?id=zEZOqB6r9hwC&pg=PA1065|archivedate=2017-09-08|df=}}</ref>。膽囊炎的英文Cholecystitis源自希臘文的cholecyst-（膽囊）及-itis（發炎）<ref>{{cite book|last1=Collins|first1=Edwards|title=A Short Course in Medical Terminology|date=2013|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9781469835785|page=246|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5mWaAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA246|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908192055/https://books.google.com/books?id=5mWaAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA246|archivedate=2017-09-08|df=}}</ref>。

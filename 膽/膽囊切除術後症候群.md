'''膽囊切除術後症候群'''（Postcholecystectomy syndrome）是描述在{{tsl|en|cholecystectomy|膽囊切除術}}之後出現的一系列腹部症狀。

大約5-40%的膽囊切除術患者會出現此類術後症候群<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.webmd.com/hw-popup/Postcholecystectomy-syndrome |title=Postcholecystectomy syndrome |accessdate=2009-03-07 |work=WebMD |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070702214922/http://www.webmd.com/hw-popup/Postcholecystectomy-syndrome |archivedate=2007-07-02}}</ref>，此類症狀可能暫時出現，可能持續一陣子，也有一些人伴隨終身<ref name=pmid21977493>{{cite journal |vauthors=Danley T, St Anna L |title=Clinical inquiry. Postcholecystectomy diarrhea: what relieves it? |journal=The Journal of Family Practice |volume=60 |issue=10 |pages=632c–d |date=October  2011 |pmid=21977493 |url=http://www.jfponline.com/Pages.asp?AID=9932 }}{{dead link|date=March 2018 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name=pmid1449156>{{cite journal |vauthors=Sciarretta G, Furno A, Mazzoni M, Malaguti P |title=Post-cholecystectomy diarrhea: evidence of bile acid malabsorption assessed by SeHCAT test |journal=The American Journal of Gastroenterology |volume=87 |issue=12 |pages=1852–4 | date=December  1992 |pmid=1449156}}</ref>。有約10%的術後患者出現慢性症狀。

術後症候群的腹痛症狀可能來自於{{tsl|en|sphincter of Oddi dysfunction|歐蒂氏括約肌功能不全}}或[[沾黏|術後沾黏]]<ref name="finl">{{cite journal |vauthors=Hyvärinen H, Sipponen P, Silvennoinen E |title=Intestinal adhesions: an overlooked cause of the postcholecystectomy syndrome |journal=Hepatogastroenterology |volume=37 |issue=Suppl 2 |pages=58–61 |date=December 1990 |pmid=2083937 }}</ref>。一項近期研究顯示術後症候群可能肇因於{{tsl|en|biliary microlithiasis|膽道微結石}}<ref name=pmid18577477>{{cite journal |vauthors=Okoro N, Patel A, Goldstein M, Narahari N, Cai Q |title=Ursodeoxycholic acid treatment for patients with postcholecystectomy pain and bile microlithiasis |journal=Gastrointestinal Endoscopy |volume=68 |issue=1 |pages=69–74 | date=July  2008 |pmid=18577477 |doi=10.1016/j.gie.2007.09.046}}</ref>。約50%的病例是源自於膽道系統，例如殘餘結石、膽道損傷、膽道運動不良，以及{{tsl|en|choledochal cyst|膽道囊腫}}。非膽道源性術後症候群則可能肇因於腸胃道功能性失調，例如[[消化不良|消化不良]]<ref>[https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3319963/ Post-cholecystectomy symptoms were caused by persistence of a functional gastrointestinal disorder]</ref>。

慢性膽囊切除術後症候群屬於一種{{tsl|en|bile acid diarrhea|膽酸性腹瀉}}<ref name=pmid1449156/>，此類患者可以使用{{tsl|en|bile acid sequestrant|膽酸結合劑}}進行治療，如{{tsl|en|cholestyramine|可林斯提拉明酯}}（cholestyramine）<ref name=pmid1449156/>、{{tsl|en|colestipol|考來替泊}}（colestipol）<ref name=pmid21977493/>，或{{tsl|en|colesevelam|colesevelam}}<ref>{{cite journal  |vauthors=Odunsi-Shiyanbade ST, Camilleri M, McKinzie S, etal |title=Effects of chenodeoxycholate and a bile acid sequestrant, colesevelam, on intestinal transit and bowel function |journal=Clinical Gastroenterology and Hepatology |volume=8 |issue=2 |pages=159–65 | date=February  2010 |pmid=19879973 |pmc=2822105 |doi=10.1016/j.cgh.2009.10.020}}</ref>等藥物都能夠治療且安全<ref name="pmid26913381">{{cite journal |vauthors=Mottacki N, Simrén M, Bajor A |title=Review article: bile acid diarrhoea - pathogenesis, diagnosis and management |journal=Aliment. Pharmacol. Ther. |volume= 43|issue= |pages= 884–898|year=2016 |pmid=26913381 |doi=10.1111/apt.13570 |url=}}</ref>。

==症狀==
相關症狀包含<ref>nhs.uk, [http://www.nhs.uk/Conditions/Laparoscopiccholecystectomy/Pages/Riskspage.aspx Complications of a gallbladder removal]</ref>：

* [[消化不良|消化不良]]、[[恶心|恶心]]，和嘔吐
* [[屁|放屁]]、[[腹部脹氣|腹部脹氣]]，和[[腹瀉|腹瀉]]。
* 持續性右上腹痛<ref name="pmid23052498">{{cite journal |vauthors=Lamberts MP, Lugtenberg M, Rovers MM, Roukema AJ, Drenth JP, Westert GP, van Laarhoven CJ |title=Persistent and de novo symptoms after cholecystectomy: a systematic review of cholecystectomy effectiveness |journal=Surg Endosc |volume=27 |issue=3 |pages=709–18 |year=2013 |pmid=23052498 |doi=10.1007/s00464-012-2516-9 |url=}}</ref>。

==診斷==

* 腹部超音波
* 常規性血液檢查及生化檢驗
* 靜脈膽管造影術
* 胃十二指腸內視鏡
* {{tsl|en|endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography|經內視鏡逆行性膽胰管攝影術}}（ERCP）
* {{tsl|en|endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography|經內視鏡逆行性膽胰管攝影術}}（ERCP）取得{{tsl|en|biliary sludge|膽泥}}分析
* {{tsl|en|SeHCAT|SeHCAT}}或其他與{{tsl|en|bile acid diarrhea|膽酸性腹瀉}}相關檢驗

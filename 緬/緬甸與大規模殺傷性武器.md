[[緬甸|緬甸]]普遍被懷疑發起了一個[[核武器|核武器]]計畫。<ref>[http://www.smh.com.au/world/burmax2019s-nuclear-secrets-20090731-e4fv.html], ''Sydney Morning Herald'', August 24, 2009, Accessed November 17, 2009.</ref>如果這樣的計畫確實存在，緬甸的技術和[[財政|財政]]限制可能使該計畫難以成功。<ref>{{cite web|title=Myanmar (Country Profiles)|url=http://www.nti.org/country-profiles/myanmar/|work=NTI|accessdate=9 August 2014|quote="Kelley further argued, however, that it would be extremely difficult for Myanmar, given its limited technical and financial capabilities, to develop nuclear weapons successfully."|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140709010126/http://www.nti.org/country-profiles/myanmar/|archivedate=2014-07-09}}</ref>

緬甸長期以來一直受到使用[[化學武器|化學武器]]的指控，然而，{{link-en|核威脅倡議組織|Nuclear Threat Initiative}}表示「沒有證據證明緬甸有化武計畫」。<ref name="NTI_Profile">{{cite web|title=Myanmar (Country Profiles)|url=http://www.nti.org/country-profiles/myanmar/|work=NTI|accessdate=26 October 2013|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140709010126/http://www.nti.org/country-profiles/myanmar/|archivedate=2014-07-09}}</ref>另外一方面，緬甸近年來也開始經濟改革和開放，與西方世界的關係亦沒有之前那麼緊張，發展大規模殺傷性和[[核武器|核武器]]計劃的誘因也沒有那麼迫切的必要。

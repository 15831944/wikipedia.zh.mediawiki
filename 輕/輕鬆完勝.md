《'''輕鬆完勝'''》（{{lang-en|''Swish Swish''}}）是美国歌手[[凱蒂·佩芮|凱蒂·佩芮]]的一首歌曲，并由[[妮姬·米娜|妮姬·米娜]]伴唱。歌曲收录於她的第5張錄音室專輯《[[见证_(凯蒂·佩芮专辑)|见证]]》中，并于2017年5月19日作为专辑第3支单曲发行。其由[[凱蒂·佩芮|凱蒂·佩芮]]、[[妮姬·米娜|奧妮卡·米娜]]、{{le|杜克·杜蒙|Duke Dumont|亚当·杜蒙}}、{{le|莎拉·哈德森|Sarah Hudson (singer)}}和{{le|Starrah|Starrah|
布列塔尼·哈扎德}}创作，并由杜蒙、诺亚·帕索沃利和PJ雪橇制作。《轻松完胜》是一首受到[[浩室音乐|浩室]]影响的[[电子舞曲|EDM]]歌曲，并取样自[[流線胖小子|流線胖小子]]的《{{le|星星69|Star 69}}》，其同时也取样于{{le|勇强·克拉克|Roland Clark}}的《我不断深陷》。

==发行==
2017年5月8日，佩芮透过[[Instagram|Instagram]]宣布单曲将会发行于午夜东区时间。《轻松完胜》可供数位下载的时间与《[[见证_(凯蒂·佩芮专辑)|见证]]》的开始预购时间相同<ref>{{cite web|last1=Trust|first1=Gary|title=Hot 100 Chart Moves: Katy Perry's 'Bon Appetit' Re-Enters, But Single Switches to 'Swish Swish'|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/7809493/hot-100-chart-moves-katy-perry-bon-appetit-swish-swish|website=Billboard|accessdate=2017-05-26}}</ref><ref name="time">{{cite news|last1=Bruner|first1=Raina|title=The Internet Has Feelings About Katy Perry's 'Swish Swish'|url=http://time.com/4786126/katy-perry-nicki-minaj-swish-swish/|website=Time|accessdate=2017-05-19}}</ref>。在《轻松完胜》发行后，评论家写道这张单曲看来好像是一张羞辱单曲，且回应[[泰勒絲|泰勒絲]]的2014年歌曲《[[敵對_(歌曲)|敌对]]》<ref>{{cite web|last1=Cohen|first1=Jess|title=Does Katy Perry's New Song Swish Swish Have Hidden Messages About Taylor Swift? Watch to Get the Scoop! on E! News|url=http://www.eonline.com/shows/e_news/news/854850/does-katy-perry-s-new-song-swish-swish-have-hidden-messages-about-taylor-swift-watch-to-get-the-scoop|website=E! Online|accessdate=2017-05-23}}</ref>。当出席《{{link-en|詹姆斯柯登深夜秀|The Late Late Show with James Corden}}》并问道这首歌是否指向某个人时，佩芮只是简单回答这是一首反霸凌的歌曲<ref>{{cite web|url=http://metro.co.uk/2017/05/21/katy-perry-reveals-that-taylor-swift-diss-track-swish-swish-is-about-being-liberated-from-bullies-6651130/|title=Katy Perry reveals 'Taylor Swift diss track' Swish Swish is about bullies|last=Duncan|first=Amy|date=2017-05-21|website=Metro|accessdate=2017-05-21}}</ref>。

==词曲==

布列塔尼·哈扎德}}创作，并由杜蒙、诺亚·帕索沃利和PJ雪橇制作<ref name=iTunes>{{cite web|title=Witness by Katy Perry on Apple Music|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/witness/id1236460498|publisher=iTunes Store|accessdate=2017-05-19|format=Credits can be obtained from the songs once added to the iTunes library}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Berry|first1=Peter A.|title=Nicki Minaj Links With Katy Perry on New Song 'Swish Swish' – XXL|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2017/05/nicki-minaj-katy-perry-new-song-swish-swish/|website=XXL Mag|accessdate=2017-05-19}}</ref>。其是一首受到[[浩室音乐|浩室]]影响的[[电子舞曲|EDM]]歌曲，并取样自[[流線胖小子|流線胖小子]]的《{{le|星星69|Star 69}}》，其同时也取样于{{le|勇强·克拉克|Roland Clark}}的《我不断深陷》<ref name="mic">{{cite web|last1=Whaley|first1=Natelegé|title=Unpacking the possible Taylor Swift disses on Katy Perry, Nicki Minaj's "Swish Swish"|url=https://mic.com/articles/177621/unpacking-the-possible-taylor-swift-disses-on-katy-perry-and-nicki-minaj-s-swish-swish#.CfJkjToAY|website=Mic|accessdate=2017-05-24}}</ref><ref name="RS">{{cite web|last1=Spanos|first1=Brittany|title=Katy Perry, Nicki Minaj's 'Swish Swish' Warns Adversary: 'Don't Come For Me'|url=http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/hear-katy-perry-nicki-minajs-boastful-swish-swish-w483182|website=Rolling Stone|accessdate=2017-05-19}}</ref><ref name="billboard">{{cite web|title=Katy Perry and Nicki Minaj Score with 'Swish Swish': Listen|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7800969/katy-perry-and-nicki-minaj-score-with-swish-swish-listen|website=Billboard|accessdate=2017-05-19}}</ref>。这首单曲共长4分02秒<ref name=iTunes/>。歌曲由[[降E大調|降E大調]]演奏，節奏為每分鐘120拍。这首歌曲的和弦进程为Cm{{small|7}}–A♭maj{{small|7}}–Gm{{small|7}}–Cm{{small|7}}–A♭maj{{small|7}}–Fm{{small|7}}，而佩芮演唱时的音域为E♭<sub>3</sub>至 A♭<sub>4</sub><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.musicnotes.com/sheetmusic/mtd.asp?ppn=MN0174471|title=Katy Perry feat.

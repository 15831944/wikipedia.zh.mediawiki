'''波里哇利'''（Polewali）是[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]][[西苏拉威西省|西苏拉威西省]]的最大城镇，是[[波里哇利曼达尔县|波里哇利曼达尔县]]的县治所在。这里因2007年1月1日发生在附近海域的[[亚当航空574号班机空难|亚当航空574号班机空难]]而闻名。城镇南滨[[曼达尔湾|曼达尔湾]]，2010年统计，波里哇利所在的波里哇利区（Kecamatan Polewali）全区共有54,843人<ref>Biro Pusat Statistik, Jakarta, 2011.</ref>。

根据[[柯本气候分类法|柯本气候分类法]]，波里哇利属[[热带雨林气候|热带雨林气候]]（''Af''）。年平均温度25.5℃，最暖月是3月，月平均温度为26.2℃；最冷月是7月，月平均温度为24.8℃。平均年降雨量2895.6 mm，降雨量最多的月份是1月，达358.1 mm；降雨量最少的月份是8月，为127 mm。每年平均有149.5天降雨，1月降雨天数最多，达21.8天；9月最少，平均仅5.6天降水。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=602474&cityname=Polewali%2C+Sulawesi+Barat%2C+Indonesia&units= |title=POLEWALI, INDONESIA |last= |first= |date= |website=weatherbase.com |publisher= |access-date=2017-07-21 |quote=The average temperature for the year in Polewali is 77.9°F (25.5°C). The warmest month, on average, is March with an average temperature of 79.2°F (26.2°C). The coolest month on average is July, with an average temperature of 76.6°F (24.8°C). The average amount of precipitation for the year in Polewali is 114.0" (2895.6 mm). The month with the most precipitation on average is January with 14.1" (358.1 mm) of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is August with an average of 5.0" (127 mm). There are an average of 149.5 days of precipitation, with the most precipitation occurring in January with 21.8 days and the least precipitation occurring in September with 5.6 days.}}</ref>

'''波羅鱷科'''（Baurusuidae）是[[中真鱷類|中真鱷類]]的一[[科|科]]，生存於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]晚期。波羅鱷科是群陸生、[[肉食性|肉食性]]鱷形類。化石已在[[南美洲|南美洲]]（[[巴西|巴西]]與[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]）、[[印度|印度]]。波羅鱷科的定義為：[[波羅鱷|波羅鱷]]與''Stratiotosuchus''的最近共同祖先，以及其最近共同祖先的所有後代。<ref name="Carvalho et al., 2005">Carvalho IS, Campos ACA, Nobre PH. 2005. ''Baurusuchus salgadoensis'', a new Crocodylomorpha (Cretaceous), Brazil. ''Gondwana Research'' '''8''': 11–30.</ref>

==屬==
*[[波羅鱷|波羅鱷]]（''Baurusuchus''）：生存於[[土侖階|土侖階]]到[[桑托階|桑托階]]的[[巴西|巴西]]。<ref name="Candeiro et al., 2006">Candeiro CRA, Martinelli AG, Avilla LS, Rich TH. 2006. Tetrapods from the Upper Cretaceous (Turonian-Maastrichtian) Bauru Group of Brazil: a reappraisal. ''Cretaceous Research'' '''27''' (6): 923-946.</ref>
*[[犬齒鱷|犬齒鱷]]（''Cynodontosuchus''）：生存於[[康尼亞克階|康尼亞克階]]到桑托階的[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]。<ref name="Carvalho et al., 2005"/>
*''Pabweshi''：生存於[[馬斯垂克階|馬斯垂克階]]的[[印度|印度]]。<ref name="Wison et al., 2001">Wilson JA, Malkane MS, Gingerich PD. 2001. New crocodyliform (Reptilia, Mesoeucrocodylia) from the Upper Cretaceous Pab Formation of Vitakri, Balochistan (Pakistan). ''Contributions from the Museum of Paleontology, University of Michingan'' '''30''': 321-336.</ref>
*''Stratiosuchus''：生存於土侖階到桑托階的巴西。<ref name="Candeiro et al., 2006"/>
*[[座狼鱷|座狼鱷]]（''Wargosuchus''）：生存於桑托階的阿根廷。<ref name="Martinelli & Pais, 2008">Martinelli AG, Pais DF. e-published 2008. A new baurusuchid crocodyliform (Archosauria) from the Late Cretaceous of Patagonia (Argentina). ''Comptes Rendus Palevol''.</ref>

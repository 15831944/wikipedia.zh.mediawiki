'''波多黎各居民代表'''（{{lang-en|Resident Commissioner of Puerto Rico}}，{{lang-es|El Comisionado Residente de Puerto Rico}}）是[[波多黎各|波多黎各]]在[[美国众议院|美国众议院]]的代表，仅有发言权而没有投票权。居民代表由波多黎各人选举产生，任期为四年，也是众议院中唯一任期四年的成员。<ref name=cq> {{cite news|first=|last=|title=111th House Freshmen: Pedro Pierluisi, D-Puerto Rico (AL)|url=http://www.cqpolitics.com/wmspage.cfm?docID=news-000002982727|work=Congressional Quarterly|publisher=|date=2008-11-05|accessdate=2008-11-05|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081109031849/http://www.cqpolitics.com/wmspage.cfm?docID=news-000002982727|archivedate=2008-11-09}}</ref>

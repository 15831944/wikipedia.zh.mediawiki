'''波氏爪龍屬'''（屬名：''Bonapartenykus''）是[[獸腳亞目|獸腳亞目]][[阿瓦拉慈龍科|阿瓦拉慈龍科]][[恐龍|恐龍]]的一屬，生存於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]晚期的[[阿根廷|阿根廷]][[巴塔哥尼亞|巴塔哥尼亞]]，地質年代相當於[[坎潘階|坎潘階]]到[[馬斯垂克階|馬斯垂克階]]。[[模式種|模式種]]是''B. ultimus''，屬名是以阿根廷古生物學家[[約瑟·波拿巴|約瑟·波拿巴]]（José Bonaparte）為名<ref name="agnolinetal2012">{{cite journal | url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0195667111001923 | title=New alvarezsaurid (Dinosauria, Theropoda) from uppermost Cretaceous of north-western Patagonia with associated eggs | author=Federico L. Agnolin, Jaime E. Powell, Fernando E. Novas and Martin Kundrát | journal=Cretaceous Research | volume=35 | pages=33–56 | doi=10.1016/j.cretres.2011.11.014|date=June 2012}}</ref>。

波氏爪龍的一個成年個體標本，被發現腹部有兩顆蛋化石，大約位於[[輸卵管|輸卵管]]位置<ref name="about.com" /><ref name="discovery">{{cite news | url=http://news.discovery.com/animals/dinosaur-mom-died-120411.html | title=Dinosaur Mom Died with Eggs Still Inside Her | work=Discovery News | date=April 11, 2012 | accessdate=April 19, 2012 | author=Jennifer Viegas | publisher=Discovery Communications}}</ref>；這兩顆蛋化石也可能是位於蛋巢內，在化石化過程移動到腹部位置<ref name="csm">{{cite news | url=http://www.csmonitor.com/Science/2012/0411/Which-came-last-the-dinosaur-or-the-egg | title=Which came last, the dinosaur or the egg? | work=The Christian Science Monitor | date=April 11, 2012 | accessdate=April 19, 2012 | author=Nola Doyle-Burr}}</ref>。這個波氏爪龍的身長估計約2.6公尺<ref name="discovery" />，體重估計約45公斤<ref name="about.com" />。波氏爪龍被估計是以[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]為食<ref name="about.com" />。古生物學家[[奧尼拉斯·諾瓦斯|奧尼拉斯·諾瓦斯]]（Fernando Novas）發現牠們的外形類似現代[[美洲小鴕|美洲小鴕]]，一種生存於[[南美洲|南美洲]]的[[美洲鴕鳥|美洲鴕鳥]]<ref name="bbc">{{cite news | url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-17688939 | title=Bird-like dinosaur found with eggs in Patagonia | work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] | date=April 12, 2012 | accessdate=April 19, 2012 | publisher=[[BBC|BBC]]}}</ref>。

'''波特兰空中缆车'''（{{lang-en|'''Portland Aerial Tram'''}}）又稱'''OHSU纜車'''，是一条服务[[俄勒岡州|俄勒岡州]][[波特蘭_(俄勒岡州)|波特蘭市]]的往复式[[空中缆车|空中缆车]]，该缆车连接{{link-en|俄勒冈州卫生科学大学|Oregon Health & Science University}}（OHSU）和{{link-en|南海滨区|South Waterfront}}。本線是在[[羅斯福島空中纜車|羅斯福島空中纜車]]後美國第二條通勤性質的纜車系統<ref>{{cite news |title=Portland's commuter tram offers postcard views of Mount St. Helens, Mount Hood |url=http://www.iht.com/articles/ap/2007/03/05/america/NA-FEA-GEN-US-View-From-a-Tram.php |newspaper=[[International_Herald_Tribune|International Herald Tribune]] Americas |newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times_Company|The New York Times Company]] |date=March 4, 2007 |accessdate=2007-03-10 }}</ref>。

部分社區的居民認為這條纜車有侵犯到[[隱私權|隱私權]]的疑慮<ref>{{cite news | last = Redden | first = Jim | title = Tram OK lifts the gate on growth | newspaper = [[Portland_Tribune|Portland Tribune]] | date = May 3, 2005 | url = http://thetribonline.net/news/story.php?story_id=29697 | accessdate = 2012-11-15 }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite news | last = Murphy | first = Todd | title = City, neighbors are poles apart | newspaper = [[Portland_Tribune|Portland Tribune]] | date = November 4, 2005 | url = http://thetribonline.net/news/story.php?story_id=32533 | accessdate = 2012-11-15 }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>，而投資成本的金額亦造成各界的爭論<ref name="4bucks">{{cite news | title = Tram ride will now set you back $4 | url = http://www.katu.com/news/5138496.html | date = January 9, 2007 | publisher = [[KATU|KATU]] | author = Brian Barker | accessdate = 2007-10-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite web

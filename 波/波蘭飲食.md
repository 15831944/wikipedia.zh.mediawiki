'''波蘭飲食'''是指[[波蘭|波蘭]]的飲食文化。波蘭飲食和其他斯拉夫國家的飲食文化有很多相似之處，特別是[[捷克飲食|捷克]]、[[斯洛伐克飲食|斯洛伐克]]、[[白俄羅斯飲食|白俄羅斯]]、[[烏克蘭飲食|烏克蘭]]和[[俄羅斯飲食|俄羅斯]]<ref name="Roberts">Nigel Roberts (Apr 12, 2011), [https://books.google.com/books?id=AHHpbnUNiW4C&pg=PA81 ''The Bradt Travel Guide'' 2, Belarus, page 81,] (2nd), ISBN 1841623407. "Like Ukrainians, Russians and Poles, Belarusians are still fond of borscht with a very large dollop of sour cream (smyetana) and it is particularly warming and nourishing in the depths of winter."</ref>。波蘭飲食也受到中歐飲食的影響，包括[[德國飲食|德國]]、[[奧地利飲食|奧地利]]和[[匈牙利飲食|匈牙利]]<ref name="Journal">Melvil Dewey, Richard Rogers Bowker, L. Pylodet, ''[[Library_Journal|Library Journal]]'', Volume 110, 1985; "Poland's cuisine, influenced by its German, Austrian, Hungarian, Russian, and other conquerors over the centuries."</ref>。此外，波蘭飲食也受到[[猶太人飲食|猶太人]]<ref>http://www.myjewishlearning.com/culture/2/Food/Ashkenazic_Cuisine/Poland_and_Russia.shtml</ref>、[[法國飲食|法國]]、[[土耳其飲食|土耳其]]和[[義大利飲食|義大利]]的影響<ref name="wplywy" />。

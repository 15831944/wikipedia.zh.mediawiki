'''波士頓市政廳'''（{{lang-en|Boston City Hall}}）是[[美國|美國]][[波士頓|波士頓]]市政府所在地，{{link-en|波士頓市長|Mayor of Boston}}的辦公室與{{link-en|波士頓市議會|Boston City Council}}皆位於此。現在的市政廳修建於1968年<ref name=nytimes>{{cite news|first=Dennis|last=Hevesi|title= Gerhard Kallmann, Architect, Is Dead at 97 |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2012/06/25/arts/design/gerhard-kallmann-architect-dies-at-97.html?_r=1 |work=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |publisher=|date=2012-06-24 |accessdate=2012-07-16}}</ref>。波士頓市政廳是一座典型的野獸派建築<ref name=throwback2014>{{citation |title=Throwback Thursday: When Boston’s City Hall Was New (and Already Unloved) |date=February 13, 2014 |work=Boston Magazine |url= http://www.bostonmagazine.com/news/blog/2014/02/13/throwback-thursday-bostons-city-hall-new-already-unloved/ |accessdate=February 13, 2014 }}</ref>，外觀極具爭議。波士頓市政廳被建築周刊的網站評為最偉大建築之一<ref>{{cite web | title = Boston City Hall listing on Great Buildings Online | url = http://www.greatbuildings.com/buildings/Boston_City_Hall.html | accessdate =2006-12-13}}</ref>，卻也獲得網路評選被評為世界最醜陋建築<ref>[http://www.boston.com/news/local/breaking_news/2008/11/boston_city_hal_1.html Boston City Hall tops ugliest-building list]. ''[[The_Boston_Globe|The Boston Globe]]''</ref><ref>reuters.com [http://www.reuters.com/article/lifestyleMolt/idUSTRE4AD2V720081114?pageNumber=1&virtualBrandChannel=0 Travel Picks: 10 top ugly buildings and monuments]</ref>。2013年，波士頓的一家報紙將這座建築評為波士頓最醜陋建築，並建議拆除<ref>''[[The_Boston_Globe|The Boston Globe]]'' [http://www.bostonglobe.com/opinion/2013/09/23/boston-city-hall-should-torn-down/ City Hall Should Torn Down] 2013/09/23</ref>。波士頓市政府也有重新開發市政廳一帶土地的想法<ref>Casey Ross. [http://www.boston.com/business/articles/2011/03/16/after_many_false_starts_a_new_city_hall_plaza_plan/ A 10-year plan for City Hall Plaza: New incremental approach starts with remodeled T station, trees]. Boston Globe, March 16, 2011</ref><ref>[http://www.boston.com/business/gallery/boston_city_hall_plaza_ideas/ What do you think should be done to City Hall Plaza?] Boston Globe, March 16, 2011</ref>。{{when|加利福尼亞家庭設計將波士頓市政廳評為「25座現在就該拆除的建築」}}<ref>http://www.californiahomedesign.com/inspiration/25-buildings-demolish-right-now/slide/5074</ref>。

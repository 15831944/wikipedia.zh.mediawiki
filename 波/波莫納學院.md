'''波莫納學院'''（'''Pomona College'''）是位於[[美國|美國]][[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[克萊蒙特_(加利福尼亞州)|克萊蒙特]]的一所[[文理學院|文理學院]]，創辦於1887年，為[[克萊蒙特諸校|克萊蒙特諸校]]的始創成員院校之一。

==校區==
[[File:Gates_at_Pomona_College.jpg|thumb]]
波莫納學院靠近[[聖蓋博山|聖蓋博山]]，校區面積約{{convert|140|acre|ha}}，共63棟建築，其中14個是宿舍樓。它的第一所建築是一所未完工的酒店，即今日之薩姆納堂（Sumner Hall）。1909年[[詹姆斯·布莱斯德尔|詹姆斯·布莱斯德尔]]成為校長時該學院只有7所建築，但在其任內迅速擴張<ref>{{cite web |url= http://claremontconversation.org/tcourse/tndy4010/page/James+Blaisdell-The+Visionary |title= James Blaisdell and the Claremont Colleges |accessdate=2007-01-11 |author= Anderson, Seth |date=2007-12-14 |publisher= Claremont Graduate University |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20070928024653/http://claremontconversation.org/tcourse/tndy4010/page/James+Blaisdell-The+Visionary |archivedate= 2007-09-28 |deadurl=yes |df=}}</ref>。他買下了校園周圍的空地，為後來學院進一步擴大奠定了基礎。<ref name="fisk"/> 
[[File:Marston_Quad,_Pomona_College.jpg|thumb]]
該學院大一和大二的社會人文學生基本都在南部校區住宿、上課<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.pomona.edu/adwr/campuslife/residentiallife/southcampus.shtml |title=Residence Halls – South Campus |publisher= Pomona College |accessdate=2007-01-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20060901082546/http://www.pomona.edu/adwr/campuslife/residentiallife/southcampus.shtml |archivedate= 2006-09-01 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.pomona.edu/about/pomoniana/oldenborg-center.aspx |title= Oldenborg "The Borg" Center - Pomona College |publisher=Pomona College |accessdate= 2012-02-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120223045433/http://pomona.edu/about/pomoniana/oldenborg-center.aspx |archivedate= 2012-02-23 |df= }}</ref>。薩姆納堂就位於此地，招生辦在這裡辦公。布里奇斯禮堂（Bridges Auditorium）裡會舉辦音樂會和演講<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cuc.claremont.edu/bridges/background/index.html |title=About Bridges Auditorium |accessdate=2007-01-11 |publisher=Claremont University Consortium |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070202013344/http://www.cuc.claremont.edu/bridges/background/index.html |archivedate=2007-02-02 }}</ref>，不過該學院也有專門的音樂廳布里奇斯音樂廳（Bridges Hall of Music，即Little Bridges），它建造于1915年，可容納600人<ref name="fisk">{{cite web |url= http://www.cbfisk.com/fisk_files/organs/op117_01.html |title= CB Fisk #117 Pomona College, Claremont, CA |accessdate= 2007-01-11 |author= Peterson, William |year=2002 |publisher= C.B. Fisk, Inc. |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20070623013523/http://www.cbfisk.com/fisk_files/organs/op117_01.html |archivedate= 2007-06-23 }}</ref>。波莫納學院美術館是一個小型美術館，收藏有歐洲和美國的藝術作品<ref>{{Cite news |url= https://www.pomona.edu/museum/collections |title=Collections |work= Pomona College Museum of Art |access-date= 2017-04-04 |language= en}}</ref>。卡內基大樓（Carnegie Building）是政治和經濟學部所在地，建於1929年，本來是學院圖書館。卡內基大樓和布里奇斯禮堂之間是馬斯頓方庭（Marston Quadrangle）。此外南校區內還有一個有機農場<ref>{{cite news |title= Pomona College’s Studio Art Hall Wins National Steel Building Award |url= https://www.aisc.org/newsdetail.aspx?id=41122 |accessdate= 15 November 2015 |publisher= AISC |date= 4 November 2015 |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20151117033729/https://www.aisc.org/newsdetail.aspx?id=41122 |archivedate= 2015年11月17日 }}</ref>。科學類學科多位於北部校區<ref>{{cite web |url= http://biology.pomona.edu/facilities/seaverbiology.shtml |title= Richard C. Seaver Biology Building |accessdate= 2007-01-11 |publisher= Pomona College Biology Department}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title= $25 Million Gift Establishes Center for Creativity and Collaboration for the 5Cs |url= http://www.pomona.edu/news/2015/09/10-25-million-gift-establishes-center-creativity-and-collaboration-5cs |accessdate= 15 November 2015 |publisher= Pomona College |date= 10 September 2015}}</ref>，高年級學生也多住在這裡<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.pomona.edu/adwr/campuslife/residentiallife/northcampus.shtml |title=Residence Halls – North Campus |accessdate=2007-01-11 |publisher=Pomona College |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060901082554/http://www.pomona.edu/adwr/campuslife/residentiallife/northcampus.shtml |archivedate=2006-09-01 |df= }}</ref>。

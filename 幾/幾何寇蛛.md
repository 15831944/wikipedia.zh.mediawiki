'''幾何寇蛛'''（'''''Latrodectus geometricus'''''），又稱'''棕色寡婦蛛'''<ref name=dodge>{{cite news|title=It's officially confirmed: There's a new spider in southwest Kansas |url=http://www.dodgeglobe.com/news/x386655782/Its-officially-confirmed-Theres-a-new-spider-in-southwest-Kansas |first=Mark |last=Reagan |newspaper=[[Dodge_City_Daily_Globe|Dodge City Daily Globe]] |date=12 August 2011 |accessdate=28 September 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120325113522/http://www.dodgeglobe.com/news/x386655782/Its-officially-confirmed-Theres-a-new-spider-in-southwest-Kansas |archivedate=25 March 2012 }}</ref>，为[[寇蛛属|寇蛛属]]的一種蜘蛛，分佈遍及全球，原產地可能是南非<ref name="dodge" />，但學界尚無定論<ref name="Vetter CISR 2012">{{cite web | url= http://cisr.ucr.edu/brown_widow_spider.html  |title= The brown widow spider, ''Latrodectus geometricus'' |  first= Richard S. | last= Vetter | publisher= Department of Entomology, Center for Invasive Species Research, [[University_of_California,_Riverside|University of California, Riverside]] | year=2013 | accessdate= 15 July 2013}}</ref>。它和同屬的[[黑寡婦蜘蛛|黑寡婦蜘蛛]]較為相似，只是體型較小、顏色較淡。

==毒性==

它和黑寡婦蜘蛛一樣具有蛛毒。但其毒性本身就比黑寡婦蜘蛛的蛛毒小得多<ref name= "taking over">{{cite news| url= http://www.latimes.com/news/science/sciencenow/la-sci-sn-brown-widow-spider-california-20120702,0,4442357.story | title= Brown widow spiders 'taking over' in Southern California | first= Eryn | last= Brown | work= Science Now | date= 2 July 2012 | publisher= [[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]] | accessdate= 28 September 2012}}</ref>，影響的一般只有傷口附近位置。此外加上幾何寇蛛體形本來就比較小，所以能注入傷口的毒也就較少<ref name="Vetter CISR 2012"/>。其毒在小鼠身上的[[半數致死量|半數致死量]]為0.43 mg/kg<ref>{{cite journal|last=Rauber|first=Albert|title=Black Widow Spider Bites|journal=Clinical Toxicology|date=1 January 1983|volume=21|issue=4–5|pages=473–485|doi=10.3109/15563658308990435|pmid=6381753}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last=McCrone|first=J.D.|title=Comparative lethality of several Latrodectus venoms|journal=Toxicon|date=1 December 1964|volume=2|issue=3|pages=201–203|doi=10.1016/0041-0101(64)90023-6}}</ref>。

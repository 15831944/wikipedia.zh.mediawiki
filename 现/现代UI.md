'''现代UI'''<ref>[https://msdn.microsoft.com/zh-cn/library/windows/desktop/dd371554(v=vs.85).aspx DirectWrite 简介]</ref>（{{lang-en|'''Modern UI'''}}），也称{{lang|en|'''Metro UI'''}}<ref>[http://techcrunch.com/2012/10/24/clearing-up-the-confusion-its-the-new-windows-8-ui-not-metro-ui/ Clearing Up The Confusion: It’s “The New Windows 8 UI” – Not “Metro UI”]</ref>、{{lang|en|'''Windows 8 Style UI'''}}，是[[微软|微软]]基于设计語言设计的一种界面风格，此设计已被用于[[Windows_Phone|Windows Phone]]、[[Windows_8|Windows 8]]、[[Windows_10|Windows 10]]、[[Xbox_360|Xbox 360]]等多款微软产品。<ref name="WindowsPhone7UI">{{cite web|url= http://windowsteamblog.com/windows_phone/b/wpdev/archive/2010/03/18/windows-phone-7-series-ui-design-amp-interaction-guide.aspx| title= Windows Phone 7 Series UI Design & Interaction Guide|accessdate= 2010-10-09|date= March 18, 2010}}</ref><ref>[http://blog.seattlepi.com/microsoft/2011/06/06/e3-xbox-360-getting-tv-tuner-bing-search-metro-interface E3: Xbox 360 getting TV tuner, Bing search, Metro interface]</ref>採用微软WP为此设计的標準字体。<ref name = "WindowsPhone7UI" />在2012年[[Computex_Taipei|Computex Taipei]]大会，[[微软|微软]]确认此设计风格会用于[[Windows_8|Windows 8]]<ref name="windows 8">[http://www.technewsdaily.com/video-windows-8-uses-wp7-design-runs-from-usb-stick-2529/  "Video: Windows 8 Uses WP7 Design, Runs from USB Stick"]. Published Friday 15 April 2011. Retrieved Friday 15 April 2011.</ref>和[[Office_2013|Office 2013]]上。<ref name="Office">[http://livesino.net/archives/3294.live Office 15 新界面泄漏：Metro UI 与新动画效果]</ref>

== 历史 ==
Metro是根據瑞士[[平面设计|平面设计]]的设计原则，此设计的原则曾在[[Windows_XP|Windows XP]]的[[Windows_Media_Center|Windows Media Center]]中体现，这有利于以文字为主的界面导航。2006年，[[Zune|Zune]]开始使用类似Metro的设计风格。[[微软|微软]]的设计师计划重新设计现有[[用户界面|用户界面]]、更清爽的排版和较少的重点以便于用户使用。<ref name="Metro">{{cite web|url=http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkID=189338|title=Windows Phone Design System: Codenamed ‘Metro’|accessdate=2010-10-09|format= [[PDF|PDF]]}}</ref>[[Zune|Zune]]的电脑端配套程序也使用了不同于以往[[Portable_Media_Center|Portable Media Center]][[用户界面|用户界面]]的清爽排版和设计。

2012年末，以“Metro”為名的公司麦德龙（Metro AG）聲稱微軟的介面Metro UI "Metro"一字已經侵權將要提告；後雖沒有提告，但微軟為了不再與該公司名稱衝突，決定取消"Metro"名稱，改用"Windows 8 Style UI"或者"Modern UI"取代了Metro UI，實質上是一樣的。

=== 开发 ===
[[机场|机场]]和[[地铁|地铁]]的指示牌给了[[微软|微软]]设计团队灵感，其风格大量採用大字体，能吸引觀众之注意力。[[微软|微软]]认为 Metro 设计主题应该是：“直覺、易懂、现代”。Modern的图标设计也会不同于[[Android|Android]]和[[iOS|iOS]]。

== 命名問題 ==
2012年8月，有謠言開始流傳，微軟的零售合作夥伴德國麥德龍（Metro AG）已經對軟件巨擘微軟的「Metro」商標侵權行為採取法律行動。一封要求停止使用「Metro」字眼直至代替字眼或案件結案的備忘錄亦被發送到軟件開發者和微軟員工。<ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.theverge.com/2012/8/2/3216545/microsoft-metro-branding-memo-european-partner | title=Exclusive: Microsoft's Metro branding to be replaced 'this week' according to internal memo | publisher=[[Vox_Media|Vox Media]] | work=[[The_Verge|The Verge]] | date=2 August 2013 | accessdate=29 May 2013 | last=Warren | first=Tom}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.bbc.com/news/technology-19108952 |title=Microsoft to drop 'Metro' name for Windows |work=[[BBC|BBC]] |date=3 August 2012 |accessdate=28 May 2012}}</ref> 

同月4日，電腦雜誌[[PC_World|PC World]]報導，微軟將暫時稱其設計語言為「Windows 8 Style UI」，以填補「Metro」暫停使用期間的空白。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.pcworld.com/businesscenter/article/260367/windows_8_metro_is_dead_but_ui_still_needs_a_name.html |title=Windows 8 'Metro' Is Dead, But UI Still Needs a Name |last=Bradley |first=Tony |date=4 August 2012 |work=[[PC_World|PC World]] |publisher=[[IDG|IDG]] |accessdate=10 August 2012}}</ref>美國雜誌[[Wired|Wired]]的Alexandra Chang則建議微軟應直接使用「Windows 8」一詞，而非另選其他字眼。<ref>{{cite web|last=Chang|first=Alexandra|url=http://www.wired.com/gadgetlab/2012/08/microsoft-doesnt-need-a-name-for-its-ui-metro-or-not/|title=Microsoft Doesn’t Need a Name for Its User Interface|work=[[Wired|Wired]]|publisher=[[Condé_Nast_Publications|Condé Nast]]|accessdate=28 May 2013|date=8 August 2012}}</ref>

同月9日，微軟計劃在消費者市場使用「Windows 8」，而對計劃使用微軟新設計語言來開發軟件的開發人員則使用「Modern UI」。<ref name="elreg">{{Cite news |url=http://www.theregister.co.uk/2012/08/10/metro_is_modern_ui_now/ |title=Microsoft: It was never 'Metro,' it was always 'Modern UI' |work=[[The_Register|The Register]] |date=10 August 2012 |accessdate=28 May 2013 |first=Neil |last=McAllister}}</ref>

自2012年9月，「微軟設計語言（Microsoft design language）」開始被用到[[微软开发者网络|微软开发者网络]]的文件內<ref>{{cite web | url=http://msdn.microsoft.com/library/windows/apps/hh464920.aspx | title=Make great Windows Store apps | publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]] | work=[[MSDN|MSDN]] | accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/apps/hh868262.aspx | title=Design case study: iPad to Windows Store app | publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]] | work=[[MSDN|MSDN]] | accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/apps/jj553415.aspx | title=Guidelines for typography | publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]] | work=[[MSDN|MSDN]] | accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/apps/xaml/jj126138.aspx | title=Roadmap for Windows Store apps using DirectX and C++ | publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]] | work=[[MSDN|MSDN]] | accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref> 以及[[Build年度會議|Build年度會議]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://channel9.msdn.com/Events/Build/2012/2-116 | title=The Microsoft design language | publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]] | work=[[Channel9|Channel9]] | accessdate=28 May 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.zdnet.com/microsoft-design-language-the-newest-official-way-to-refer-to-metro-7000006526/ | title=Microsoft Design Language: The newest official way to refer to 'Metro' | publisher=[[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive]] | work=[[ZDNet|ZDNet]] | accessdate=28 May 2013 | first=Mary Jo | last=Foley}}</ref>。

現時，微軟已經放棄了使用Metro風格（Metro-style）應用程序來形容根據微軟件設計語言來開發的[[Windows_Runtime|Windows Runtime]]應用程序。同時，在正式使用Windows 8 apps這一名詞之前暫時使用Windows Store apps代替。<ref name=zdnet-metroname>{{cite web|title=Microsoft finally comes clean(er) on post-Metro naming plans|url=http://www.zdnet.com/microsoft-finally-comes-cleaner-on-post-metro-naming-plans-7000004151/|work=[[ZDNet|ZDNet]]|publisher=[[CBS_Interactive|CBS Interactive]]|accessdate= 28 May 2013|date=12 September 2012}}</ref>

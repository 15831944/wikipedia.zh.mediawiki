'''非常时刻'''（{{lang-ga|Ré na Práinne / An Éigeandáil}}）指爱尔兰第二次世界大战期间的'''紧急状态'''。<ref name="Spelman2004p60">{{Cite journal|title=Ireland's Neutrality Policy in World War II: The Impact of Belligerent Pressures on the Implementation of Neutrality|url=https://escholarship.org/uc/item/4262j2gj|last=Spelman|first=Greg|journal=UCLA Historical Journal|year=2004|volume=20|pages=60–82: 60}}</ref>  爱尔兰在整个战争中保持中立。<ref name="Spelman2004p60" /> “非常时刻” 在历史和文化评论中被用于借喻战争中的状态。<ref name="Spelman2004p60" /> 1939年9月2日，[[爱尔兰众议院|爱尔兰众议院]]宣布紧急状态，<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Existence of National Emergency|url=http://oireachtasdebates.oireachtas.ie/debates%20authoring/debateswebpack.nsf/takes/dail1939090200008|date=1939-09-02|journal=[[爱尔兰众议院|Dáil]] debates|publisher=[[Government_of_Ireland|Government of Ireland]]|accessdate=15 July 2015|volume=77|pages=No.1 p.8 cc.19–20|nopp=Y}}
</ref> 允许[[爱尔兰议会|爱尔兰议会]]第二天通过1939年紧急权力法案。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1939/en/act/pub/0028/print.html|title=Emergency Powers Act, 1939|accessdate=2007-11-02|date=1939-09-03|publisher=[[Government_of_Ireland|Government of Ireland]]}}</ref> 在紧急状态期间，这给了政府全新的权力，包括拘禁、新闻和通信审查、政府对经济的控制。紧急权力法案于1946年9月2日失效。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.irishstatutebook.ie/1945/en/act/pub/0026/print.html|title=Emergency Powers (Continuance and Amendment) Act, 1945|accessdate=2007-11-02|date=1945-07-29|publisher=[[Government_of_Ireland|Government of Ireland]]|pages=§4(1)|quote=The Principal Act shall, unless previously terminated under subsection (2) of this section, continue in force until the 2nd day of September, 1946, and shall then expire unless the Oireachtas otherwise determines.}}</ref><ref name="1976Motion">{{Cite journal|title=National Emergency: Motion (Resumed)|url=http://oireachtasdebates.oireachtas.ie/debates%20authoring/debateswebpack.nsf/takes/dail1976090100004?opendocument|date=1976-09-01|journal=[[爱尔兰众议院|Dáil]] debates|publisher=[[Government_of_Ireland|Government of Ireland]]|accessdate=2016-03-28|volume=292|pages=119–256|quote=[[John_M._Kelly_(politician)|Mr. Kelly]]: All the 1939 emergency legislation lapsed not later than 1946.}}
</ref> 虽然紧急状态本身并没有在1976年9月1日之前取消，<ref name=1976Motion /> 1946年以后没有任何紧急法律可以利用这种异常情况。

'''檸檬冰盒派'''（{{lang-en|Lemon ice box pie}}）是一種由餡餅皮、檸檬汁、雞蛋和煉乳組成的點心<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=QQJLKCwcxvUC&pg=PA499 The Dinner Doctor - Anne Byrn - Google Books]</ref><ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=3lsniJfoz7oC&pg=PT398 Screen Doors and Sweet Tea: Recipes and Tales from a Southern Cook - Martha Hall Foose - Google Books]</ref>，通常由格雷厄姆餅乾和黄油製成<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=4uu1Id6UxAgC&pg=PA119 The Southern Foodie: 100 Places to Eat in the South Before You Die (and the Recipes That Made Them Famous) - Chris Chamberlain - Google Books]</ref>。這是一種[[基韋斯特派|基韋斯特派]]品種，其檸檬酸的酸度設置在烘烤時[[蛋黃|蛋黃]]最少；在[[新奥尔良|新奥尔良]]{{link-en|克蘭西餐廳|Clancy's}}是個招牌菜<ref>{{Cite web|title=Former Clancy's chef to open Annunciation restaurant in New Orleans' Warehouse District|author=Brett Anderson|work=The Times-Picayune NOLA.com|date=2012-03-26|url=http://www.nola.com/dining/index.ssf/2012/03/former_clancys_chef_to_open_an.html}}</ref>。

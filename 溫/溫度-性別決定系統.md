'''溫度-性別決定系統'''是一種{{en-link|環境性別決定系統|environmental sex-determination}}。性別於[[胚胎|胚胎]]時期或[[幼體|幼體]]時期時，溫度為決定其性別的因素。<ref name="nature09512">{{cite journal |author=Pen, Ido, Tobias Uller, Barbara Feldmeyer, Anna Harts, Geoffrey M. While, and Erik Wapstra |year=2010 |title=Climate-driven population divergence in sex-determining systems |journal=[[Nature_(journal)|Nature]] |volume=468 |pages=436–439 |doi= 10.1038/nature09512 |pmid=20981009}}</ref>這種狀況只見於[[爬行動物|爬行動物]]和[[真骨類|真骨類]]的魚中。<ref name="nature06519">{{cite journal|first=|year=2008|title=The adaptive significance of temperature-dependent sex determination in a reptile|url=http://www.nature.com/nature/journal/vaop/ncurrent/abs/nature06519.html|journal=[[Nature_(journal)|Nature]]|volume=451|issue=7178|pages=566–568|doi=10.1038/nature06519|pmid=18204437|via=|vauthors=Warner DA, Shine R}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Bachtrog|first=Doris|last2=Mank|first2=Judith E.|last3=Peichel|first3=Catherine L.|last4=Kirkpatrick|first4=Mark|last5=Otto|first5=Sarah P.|last6=Ashman|first6=Tia-Lynn|last7=Hahn|first7=Matthew W.|last8=Kitano|first8=Jun|last9=Mayrose|first9=Itay|date=2014-07-01|title=Sex determination: why so many ways of doing it?|journal=PLOS Biology|volume=12|issue=7|pages=e1001899|doi=10.1371/journal.pbio.1001899|issn=1545-7885|pmc=4077654|pmid=24983465}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Tree of Sex Consortium|date=2014-01-01|title=Tree of Sex: a database of sexual systems|journal=Scientific Data|volume=1|pages=140015|doi=10.1038/sdata.2014.15|pmc=4322564|pmid=25977773}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Ospina-Alvarez|first=Natalia|last2=Piferrer|first2=Francesc|date=2008-07-30|title=Temperature-dependent sex determination in fish revisited: prevalence, a single sex ratio response pattern, and possible effects of climate change|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed?term=temperature-dependent%20sex%20determination%20in%20fish%20revisited%20prevalence,%20a%20single%20sex%20ratio%20response%20pattern,%20and%20possible%20effects%20of%20climate%20change&cmd=correctspelling|journal=PLoS One|volume=3|issue=7|pages=e2837|doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0002837|issn=1932-6203|pmc=2481392|pmid=18665231}}</ref> 
溫度-性別決定系統不同於普遍存在於脊椎動物中的染色體[[性別決定系統|性別決定系統]]，溫度-性別決定系統是最多被研究的環境性別決定系統。其他的狀況下，密度，酸鹼值，以及環境的背景顏色也被發現能夠改變群體的性別比例。

'''溫美現代藝術館'''是位於[[加拿大|加拿大]][[沙斯卡寸旺省|沙斯卡寸旺省]][[沙士加通|沙士加通]]市的一座公立[[美術館|美術館]]，置於[[南沙斯卡寸旺河|南沙斯卡寸旺河]]西岸<ref>http://www.remaimodern.org/#backgroundtest</ref>。溫美面積一萬一千五百八十二平方米，耗資八千萬加元蓋設<ref>{{cite news

==歷史==
二〇一一年，主贊助人艾倫·溫美捐助一千五百萬加元於建設溫美藝術館、一千五百萬加元於藝術館的國際展覽計劃<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/saskatchewan/family-donates-30m-to-saskatoon-art-gallery-1.1067319 |title=Family donates $30M to Saskatoon art gallery - Saskatchewan - CBC News |publisher=Cbc.ca |date=2011-06-03 |accessdate=2015-03-09}}</ref>，及捐贈世界最大的[[畢加索|畢加索]][[油毡|油毡]]浮雕版系列於藝術館永久收藏，價值兩千萬加元<ref>{{cite web |last=Trembath |first=Sean |url=http://www.thestarphoenix.com/entertainment/Appraising+Picasso+What+million+collection+means+Saskatoon/10716872/story.html |title=Appraising Picasso: What a $20 million collection means to Saskatoon |publisher=Thestarphoenix.com |date=2015-01-10 |accessdate=2015-03-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150114015541/http://www.thestarphoenix.com/entertainment/Appraising+Picasso+What+million+collection+means+Saskatoon/10716872/story.html |archivedate=2015-01-14 }}</ref>。溫美現代藝術館的前身是'''孟德爾美術館'''，將會收藏它的七千七百多件藝術品。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mendel.ca/about/ |title=About – Mendel Art Gallery |publisher=Mendel.ca |date= |accessdate=2015-03-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150307055656/http://www.mendel.ca/about/ |archivedate=2015-03-07 }}</ref>孟德爾美術館於一九六四年由腓特烈·所羅門·孟德爾成立，二〇一五年六月關閉<ref>{{cite web |last=Hill |first=Andrea |url=http://www.thestarphoenix.com/entertainment/Remai+gets+budget+boost+from+city/10397390/story.html |title=Remai gets budget boost from city |publisher=Thestarphoenix.com |date=2014-11-20 |accessdate=2015-03-09 }}{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> 。新藝術館將會把其中一個藝廊及一個國際講述系列為孟德爾命名<ref>{{cite web |author=Reported by Ashley Wills |url=http://cjme.com/story/mendel-family-delighted-new-saskatoon-gallery/444666 |title=Mendel family 'delighted' with new Saskatoon gallery | News Talk 980 CJME |publisher=Cjme.com |date=2014-11-05 |accessdate=2015-03-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402161837/http://cjme.com/story/mendel-family-delighted-new-saskatoon-gallery/444666 |archivedate=2015-04-02 }}</ref>。

溫美藝術館首任館長及行政總裁[[Gregory_Burke_(curator)|Gregory Burke]]於二〇一三年委任<ref>{{cite web|author=James Adams |url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/arts/art-and-architecture/former-toronto-power-plant-director-to-take-helm-of-saskatoons-mendel/article8383553/ |title=Former Toronto Power Plant director to take helm of Saskatoon’s Mendel |publisher=The Globe and Mail |date=2013-02-08 |accessdate=2015-03-09}}</ref>。二〇一四年九月十七日，慈善家及歐洲印版專家Frederick Mulder宣佈設於英國的Frederick Mulder基金將會捐贈二十三件畢加索陶瓷給溫美藝術館<ref>{{cite web|url=http://saskatoon.ctvnews.ca/remai-art-gallery-receives-23-picasso-works-1.2013008 |title=Remai art gallery receives 23 Picasso works | CTV Saskatoon News |publisher=Saskatoon.ctvnews.ca |date=2014-09-18 |accessdate=2015-03-09}}</ref>。

== 開幕前 ==
二〇一七年開幕前，溫美藝術館為促進未來展覽計劃，宣佈一個開幕前計劃<ref>{{cite web|url=http://remaimodern.org/prelaunch/projects/supercommunity/|title=Remai Modern|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|date=2015-06-14|website=http://remaimodern.org|publisher=Remai Modern|access-date=2015-06-14|quote=|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150810124557/http://remaimodern.org/prelaunch/projects/supercommunity/|archivedate=2015-08-10}}</ref>，包括“博物館3.0”公衆節目系列及[[SUPERCOMMUNITY|SUPERCOMMUNITY]]（超級社居），一個在第五十六屆[[威尼斯雙年展|威尼斯雙年展]]展出的''[[e-flux|e-flux]] journal''作品<ref>{{cite web |url=http://supercommunity.e-flux.com/about/|title=SUPERCOMMUNITY About |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |date=2015-05-06 |website=http://supercommunity.e-flux.com |publisher=e-flux journal |access-date=2015-06-04 |quote=}}</ref><ref>{{cite press release |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |title=e-flux journal at the 56th Venice Biennale|url=http://www.e-flux.com/announcements/e-flux-journal-at-the-56th-venice-biennale/ |location=New York |publisher=e-flux |agency= |date=April 23, 2015 |access-date=2015-06-04}}</ref>。加拿大藝術基金報道：“雙年展由五月至八月期間將會選拔參加者於二〇一五年秋季在沙士加通參加座談會。”<ref>{{cite journal |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |title=News in Brief: Scotiabank Photo Award Win, Bau-Xi Turns 50, Remai Partners with e-flux |url=http://canadianart.ca/news/2015/05/08/news-in-brief-scotiabank-photo-award-win-bau-xi-turns-50-remai-partners-with-e-flux/ |journal=Canadian Art |location=Toronto, Ontario |publisher= |date=2015-05-08 |access-date=2015-06-04}}</ref>

== 展覽 ==
溫美藝術館的首行展覽名爲''Field Guide''（圖鑑），將包括藝術館收藏品及當代藝術品。展覽將包括腓特烈·孟德爾為孟德爾美術館一九六四年開幕所捐贈的十三分作品，其中有[[Lawren_Harris|Lawren Harris]]、 [[Emily_Carr|Emily Carr]]及[[David_Milne_(artist)|David Milne]]的作品。<ref name=":0" />

== 爭議 ==
二〇一五年十二月，沙士加通市本地藝術家發出公開信，要求溫美藝術館董事會甲除董事[[John_Kenneth_Gormley|John Gormley]]<ref>{{Cite web|title = Artists call for John Gormley's removal from Remai Modern Art Gallery board|url = http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/saskatoon/artists-call-for-john-gormley-s-removal-from-remai-modern-art-gallery-board-1.3361337|website = www.cbc.ca|access-date = 2016-02-18}}</ref>。公開信指 Gormley於[[2015年11月巴黎襲擊事件|二〇一五年巴黎恐襲後]]在[[Twitter|Twitter]]發出短訊建議對[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]施暴<ref>[http://i.cbc.ca/1.3325571.1447901448!/fileImage/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/original_300/john-gormley-has-since-deleted-this-tweet.jpg]</ref>。獨立第三方對Gormley進行審核<ref>{{Cite web|title = Saskatoon's Remai Modern reviews John Gormley's conduct|url = http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/saskatchewan/remai-modern-review-gormley-1.3406466|website = www.cbc.ca|access-date = 2016-02-18}}</ref>，二〇一六年一月決定Gormley沒有違反溫美藝術館的操守指引，但Gormley於該年四月十二日辭退董事職務。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://thestarphoenix.com/news/local-news/john-gormley-resigns-from-remai-moderns-board-of-directors|title=John Gormley resigns from Remai Modern's board of directors|last=|first=|date=|website=www.cbc.ca|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2016-02-18}}</ref>

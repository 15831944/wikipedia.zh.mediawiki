'''溫泉紅藻'''(Cyanidiophyceae，簡稱Cyanidia)是一群生活在高溫高酸環境中的[[單細胞|單細胞]][[紅藻|紅藻]]<ref name="SeckbachChapman2010">{{cite book|author1=Joseph Seckbach|author2=David J. Chapman|title=Red Algae in the Genomic Age|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=fegCa9G-c90C&pg=PA250|accessdate=31 January 2011|date=30 August 2010|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-90-481-3794-7|pages=250–}}</ref>，主要分佈於世界各地[[火山|火山]]活動仍然活躍，並具有硫酸成分高之溫泉水體之地區，如美國黃石國家公園、義大利火山區、紐西蘭火山區、印尼火山區及日本火山區<ref name=Toplin>Toplin et al. (2008) Biogeographic and phylogentic diversity of thermoacidophilic Cyanidiales in YNP Japan and New Zealand. Applied and Environmental Microbiology 74: 2822–2833</ref>。根據目前的[[分類系統|分類系統]]，溫泉紅藻目前主要有三屬六種(''Cyanidioschyzon merolae''、''Cyanidium caldarium''、''Galdieria sulphuraria''、''Galdieria maxima''、''Galdieria partita''和''Galdieria daedala'')<ref name=Toplin />。其中，''Cyanidioschyzon merolae''之[[細胞核|細胞核]]基因組已被解密<ref>Matsuzaki et al. (2004) Genome sequence of the ultrasmall unicellular red alga Cyanidioschyzon merolae 10D. Nature 428: 653-657.</ref>，也是目前唯一一種[[真核生物|真核生物]]之基因組達到100%的解密<ref>Nozaki et al. (2007) A 100%-complete sequence reveals unusually simple genomic features in the hot-spring red alga Cyanidioschyzon merolae. BMC Biology 5:28.</ref>。另外，其[[粒線體|粒線體]]及[[葉綠體|葉綠體]]之基因組也完成解密。因此，對於溫泉紅藻的研究中，''Cyanidioschyzon merolae''可說是最佳的模式物種。部分藻種能棲息在硫磺口噴氣處潮濕之土壤表層，另有些藻種能夠存活於具有地熱酸性潮濕之岩石表面下，靠著穿透石縫微弱之光線進行光合作用<ref>Ciniglia et al. (2004) Hidden biodiversity of the extremophilic Cynidiales red algae. Molecular Ecology 13: 1827-1838.</ref>。

In older texts it has been known as "Cyanidales". It was granted phylum status in the Saunders and Hommersand 2004 classification (as "Cyanidophyta"), but demoted to subphylum in the Yoon et al. classification of 2006.<ref name="BrodieD.)2007">{{cite book|author1=Juliet Brodie|author2=Jane Lewis (Ph. D.)|title=Unravelling the algae: the past, present, and future of algal systematics|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=-YEYFhgUBsQC&pg=PA109|accessdate=31 January 2011|year=2007|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=978-0-8493-7989-5|pages=109–}}</ref>

'''歐洲卡通網絡工作室'''（{{lang-en|'''Cartoon Network Development Studio Europe'''}}）是一間2007年成立於[[英國|英國]][[倫敦|倫敦]]的動畫公司。他與[[卡通網絡工作室|卡通網絡工作室]]相同，只是位於歐洲。<ref>[http://www.licensing.biz/features/14/INTERVIEW-Alan-Fenwick-VP-Cartoom-Network-Europe-EMEA Licensing Biz] {{Wayback|url=http://www.licensing.biz/features/14/INTERVIEW-Alan-Fenwick-VP-Cartoom-Network-Europe-EMEA |date=20110727084054 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.zoominfo.com/people/Arnesen_Finn_127210384.aspx |title=Zoom Info |access-date=2012-04-14 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121011041424/http://www.zoominfo.com/people/Arnesen_Finn_127210384.aspx |archive-date=2012-10-11 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref>[http://www2.kidscreensummit.com/2007/networking.html Kidscreen Summit] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110713155715/http://www2.kidscreensummit.com/2007/networking.html |date=2011-07-13 }}</ref><ref>[http://media2.pl/media/56661-powstaje-europejski-serial-cartoon-network.html media2.pl]</ref>

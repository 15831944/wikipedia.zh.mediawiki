'''歐洲足協足球場分類'''是由[[歐洲足協|歐洲足協]]所頒佈，對足球場進行分類，取代舊的五個星級球場標準<ref>{{cite web | title = UEFA Stadium Infrastructure Regulations Edition 2010 | publisher = [[UEFA|UEFA]] | date = March 2010 | accessdate = 20 July 2010 | url = http://www.uefa.com/MultimediaFiles/Download/Regulations/uefaorg/Stadium&Security/01/48/48/85/1484885_DOWNLOAD.pdf}}</ref>。舉行[[欧足联欧洲联赛|欧足联欧洲联赛]]或[[歐洲冠軍聯賽|歐洲冠軍聯賽]]的足球場必需達到第四類(後者更需達至五星級)<ref>{{cite web | title = Regulations for the UEFA Europa League 2010/11 | date = March 2010 | accessdate = 20 July 2010 | url = http://en.uefa.com/MultimediaFiles/Download/Regulations/competitions/Regulations/01/48/48/90/1484890_DOWNLOAD.pdf}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = Regulations of the UEFA European Football Championship 2010–12 | publisher = [[UEFA|UEFA]] | date = September 2009 | accessdate = 20 July 2010 | url = http://en.uefa.com/MultimediaFiles/Download/Regulations/uefa/Others/91/48/36/914836_DOWNLOAD.pdf}}</ref>。雖然第四類的最低容納人數為8,000人，但座位數目低於40,000和60,000的球場不會被考慮舉行欧足联欧洲联赛和歐洲冠軍聯賽的決賽。在[[2007年歐洲冠軍聯賽決賽|2007年歐洲冠軍聯賽決賽]]後，歐足協主席[[米歇爾·普拉蒂尼|米歇爾·普拉蒂尼]]表示希望把舉行球場容量提升至70,000人以解決保安問題<ref>{{cite web |url=http://uk.reuters.com/article/UK_WORLDFOOTBALL/idUKL3082273420070830 |title=Update 1-Soccer-Platini wants Champions League final at weekend |accessdate=10 November 2008|date=30 August 2008 |publisher=[[Reuters|Reuters]]}}</ref>。

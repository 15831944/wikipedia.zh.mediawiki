'''歐洲山松'''（學名：'''''Pinus mugo'''''）<ref>{{cite book|author=Andersson, F.|year=2005|title=Coniferous Forests|publisher=Elsevier|isbn=9780444816276|url=https://books.google.com/?id=_YoO8xKYSJAC&pg=PA613&lpg=PA613&dq=%22creeping+pine%22+pinus+mugo#v=onepage&q=%22creeping%20pine%22%20pinus%20mugo&f=false}}</ref>，又稱'''矮松'''<ref name=BSBI07>{{cite web |title=BSBI List 2007 |publisher=Botanical Society of Britain and Ireland |url=http://www.bsbi.org.uk/BSBIList2007.xls |format=xls |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6VqJ46atN?url=http://www.bsbi.org.uk/BSBIList2007.xls |archive-date=2015-01-25 |accessdate=2014-10-17 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>，是[[松屬|松屬]]下的一種松樹，原產於歐洲中部和西南部海拔{{convert|1000|-|2200|m|ft}}的高山地帶。它共有三個亞種。此外作为受欢迎的观赏植物，它还有许多栽培种。<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.rhs.org.uk/plants/pdfs/agm-lists/agm-ornamentals.pdf | title = AGM Plants - Ornamental | date = July 2017 | page = 78 | publisher = Royal Horticultural Society | accessdate = 2 May 2018}}</ref>

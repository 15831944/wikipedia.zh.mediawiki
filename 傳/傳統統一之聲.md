'''傳統統一之聲'''（{{lang-en|Traditional Unionist Voice，缩写为TUV}}）是[[北愛爾蘭|北愛爾蘭]]的一個[[愛爾蘭聯合主義|愛爾蘭聯合主義]]政黨<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.electoralcommission.org.uk/regulatory-issues/regpoliticalparties.cfm?frmGB=0&frmPartyID=800&frmType=partydetail |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2008-01-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080309073914/http://www.electoralcommission.org.uk/regulatory-issues/regpoliticalparties.cfm?frmGB=0&frmPartyID=800&frmType=partydetail |archivedate=2008-03-09 }} </ref>，成立於2007年12月7日，系自[[民主統一黨_(北愛爾蘭)|民主統一黨]]內反對聖安德魯協定的人士分裂出的政黨<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/northern_ireland/7131912.stm |title=UK | Northern Ireland | New unionist group to be launched |publisher=BBC News |date=7 December 2007 |accessdate=23 February 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.4ni.co.uk/northern_ireland_news.asp?id=69467 |title=Northern Ireland News - Allister Announces 'Alternative Ulster' Voice |publisher=4ni.co.uk |date=7 December 2007 |accessdate=23 February 2011}}</ref>。現在傳統统一之聲在北愛爾蘭議會有1個議席。

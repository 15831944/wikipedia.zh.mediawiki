'''[[新加坡|新加坡]][[轻轨运输|轻轨系统]]'''（{{lang-en|Singapore Light Rail Transit (LRT) System}}) 共有43个[[鐵路車站|車站]]。其中42个已经投入运作，1个还未开放。<ref name="Land Transport Authority 2018">{{cite web|url=https://www.lta.gov.sg/content/ltaweb/en/public-transport/mrt-and-lrt-trains/train-system-map.html|title=Train System Map - MRT & LRT Trains - Public Transport|date=2018-05-31|website=Land Transport Authority|access-date=2018-07-07|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180123161347/https://www.lta.gov.sg/content/ltaweb/en/public-transport/mrt-and-lrt-trains/train-system-map.html|archivedate=2018-01-23|df=}}</ref>新加坡轻轨系统是[[新加坡地鐵|新加坡地鐵]]系统的分支，主要作为链接[[住宅|住宅]]区与地铁站的代步工具。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/infopedia/articles/SIP_538_2005-01-05.html|title=First Light Rail Transit system|accessdate=2018-07-07|author=National Library Board Infopedia|date=|last=|first=|work=eresources.nlb.gov.sg|publisher=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171231160523/http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/infopedia/articles/SIP_538_2005-01-05.html|archivedate=2017-12-31|df=}}</ref>

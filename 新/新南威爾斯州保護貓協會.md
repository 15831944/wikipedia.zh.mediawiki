'''新南威爾斯州保護貓協會'''（{{lang-en|Cat Protection Society of NSW}}）是[[澳洲|澳洲]][[新南威爾斯州|新南威爾斯州]]的一家[[動物保護|動物保護]]組織<ref>{{Cite web|title=Longer breeding seasons, more kittens: are cats reacting to climate change?|url=https://www.smh.com.au/national/nsw/longer-breeding-seasons-more-kittens-are-cats-reacting-to-climate-change-20160208-gmo5bl.html|work=The Sydney Morning Herald|date=2016-02-08|accessdate=2019-03-22|language=en|first=Kim|last=Arlington}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Outbreak of rare feline virus|url=https://www.dailytelegraph.com.au/newslocal/inner-west/cat-protection-society-to-subsidise-vaccinations-after-outbreaks-of-feline-virus/news-story/532b6a9be0e5b5aed95faa50380d18c2|date=2017-08-25|accessdate=2019-03-22|language=en|author=|publisher=Daily Telegraph}}</ref>，成立於1958年。該協會的願景是「讓每隻[[貓|貓]]都擁有一個充滿愛的家」。現任首席執行官為Kristina Vesk女士<ref>{{Cite web|title=Project to keep cats happy indoors|url=https://www.dailytelegraph.com.au/newslocal/inner-west/indoor-cats-make-good-neighbours-cat-protection-society-of-nsw-says/news-story/d0b3f69d078c00e9b8f4f6fb8ec81b0b|date=2017-07-02|accessdate=2019-03-22|language=en|author=|publisher=Daily Telegraph}}</ref>。

'''新碟貝科'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''Neopilinidae'''}}），是[[软体动物门|软体动物门]][[单板纲|单板纲]][[罩螺目|罩螺目]][[罩螺超科|罩螺超科]]的一個科。如同其他單板綱的物種一樣，本科的分類在不同的參考都有爭議。例如：在[[WoRMS|WoRMS]]把本科與Laevipilinidae、[[微碟貝科|微碟貝科]]（Micropilinidae）、[[單板科|單板科]]（Monoplacophoridae）視為同名<ref name=WoRMS>{{cite WoRMS |id=23078 |title=Neopilinidae |date=2012-09-19 |accessdate=2013-02-28 }}</ref><ref name=Zoologica>{{cite journal 
|first1=Yasunori |last1=Kano |first2=Shoichi |last2=Kimura |first3=Taeko |last3=Kimura
|first4=Anders |last4=Warén |title=Living Monoplacophora: morphological conservatism or recent diversification? |journal=[[Zoologica_Scripta|Zoologica Scripta]] |volume=41 |issue=5 |pages=471-488 |url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1463-6409.2012.00550.x/pdf |doi=10.1111/j.1463-6409.2012.00550.x |language=en |accessdate=2013-02-28 |date=September 2012}}</ref>，但有其他分類不認同<ref name=taxonomicon />。

== 分類 ==
以下為新碟貝科之下的分類<ref name=taxonomicon />：
* ''[[Adenopilina|Adenopilina]]'' <small>Starobogatov & Moskalev, 1987</small>
** ''Adenopilina adenensis'' <small>(Tebble, 1967)</small>
* [[新碟貝屬|新碟貝屬]] ''Neopilina'' <small>H. Lemche, 1957</small>：又名[[新笠螺屬|新笠螺屬]]、[[新帽貝屬|新帽貝屬]]<ref>{{Cite web |url = http://shell.sinica.edu.tw/chinese/classification.php?id=1 |title = 台灣貝類資料庫 |language = zh-hant }}</ref>
** ''Neopilina bruuni'' <small>Menzies, 1968</small>
** ''Neopilina galatheae'' <small>Lemche, 1957</small>
** ''Neopilina rebainsi'' <small>Moskalev, Starobogatov & Filatova, 1983</small>
* ''[[Rokopella|Rokopella]]'' <small>Starobogatov & Moskalev, 1987</small>
** ''Rokopella brummeri'' <small>Goud & Gittenberger, 1993</small>
** ''Rokopella capulus'' <small>Marshall, 2006</small>
** ''Rokopella euglypta'' <small>(Dautzenberg & Fischer, 1897)</small>
** ''Rokopella goesi'' <small>(Warén, 1988)</small>
** ''Rokopella oligotropha'' <small>(Rokop, 1972)</small>
** ''Rokopella segonzaci'' <small>Warén & Bouchet, 2001</small>
* ''[[Veleropilina|Veleropilina]]'' <small>Starobogatov & Moskalev, 1987</small>
** ''Veleropilina reticulata'' <small>(Seguenza, 1876)</small>
** ''Veleropilina veleronis'' <small>(Menzies & Layton, 1963)</small>
** ''Veleropilina zografi'' <small>(Dautzenberg & Fischer, 1896)</small>
* ''[[Vema|Vema]]'' <small>(Clarke & Menzies, 1959)</small>
** ''Vema bacescui'' <small>(Menzies, 1968)</small>
** ''Vema ewingi'' <small>(Clarke & Menzies, 1959)</small>
** ''Vema hyalina''
** ''Vema levinae'' <small>Warén, 1996</small>
** ''Vema occidua'' <small>Marshall, 2006</small>

以下為[[WoRMS|WoRMS]]認定屬於本科的屬<ref name=WoRMS /><ref name=Zoologica />：
* ''[[Adenopilina|Adenopilina]]'' <small>Starobogatov & Moskalev, 1987</small>
* ''[[Laevipilina|Laevipilina]]'' <small>McLean, 1979</small>
* [[微碟貝屬|微碟貝屬]] ''Micropilina'' <small>Warén, 1989</small>
* [[單板屬|單板屬]] ''Monoplacophorus'' <small>Moskalev, Starobogatov & Filatova, 1983</small>
* [[新碟貝屬|新碟貝屬]] ''Neopilina'' <small>Lemche, 1957</small>
另外，WoRMS亦認為''Rokopella''是''Veleropilina''的同義詞，而''Lemchephyala''亦是''Neopilina''的同義詞<ref name=WoRMS />。

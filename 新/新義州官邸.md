'''新義州官邸'''（{{lang-ko|신의주 관저}}）是[[朝鮮民主主義人民共和國|朝鮮民主主義人民共和國]]前領導人[[金正日|金正日]]的眾多官邸之一。<ref>http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/picturegalleries/7782145/A-birds-eye-view-of-the-prisons-and-palaces-of-Kim-Jong-ils-North-Korea.html?image=1</ref><ref>See also: http://online.wsj.com/article/SB124295017403345489.html#project%3DNKGOOGLE0509%26articleTabs%3Dinteractive</ref>金正日的父親[[金日成|金日成]]於1994年去世，爾後金正日接管了此官邸。<ref name=bomb>http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/asia/northkorea/5413447/Leaders-live-in-luxury-while-North-Koreans-starve-to-pay-for-nuclear-bomb.html</ref>官邸臨近[[平安北道|平安北道]][[新義州|新義州]]，位於[[新義州市|新義州市]]中心以東8.5公里左右。<ref>http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/picturegalleries/7782145/A-birds-eye-view-of-the-prisons-and-palaces-of-Kim-Jong-ils-North-Korea.html</ref>該官邸隨著[[北韓揭秘計劃|北韓揭秘計劃]]的曝光而進入公眾視野。<ref>The project was mentioned on MSNBC here: http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/30970772</ref><ref>See also: http://online.wsj.com/article/SB124295017403345489.html</ref>

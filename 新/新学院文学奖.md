'''新学院文学奖'''（{{lang-en|New Academy Prize in Literature}}）是瑞典文化界于2018年设立的文學獎，代替未于2018年颁发的[[诺贝尔文学奖|诺贝尔文学奖]]，僅頒發一屆。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/07/13/books/alternative-nobel-prize-literature-new-academy-prize.html|title=An Alternative to the Nobel Prize in Literature, Judged by You|work=[[纽约时报|The New York Times]]|first=Alex|last=Marshall|date=July 13, 2018|accessdate=September 18, 2018}}</ref>获奖者于2018年10月12日公布<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.latimes.com/books/la-et-jc-murakami-new-academy-20180917-story.html|title=Haruki Murakami takes his name out of the running for alternative literature Nobel|work=[[洛杉磯時報|Los Angeles Times]]|first=Michael|last=Schaub|date=September 17, 2018|access-date=September 18, 2018}}</ref>。新学院将于2018年12月解散<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/books/2018/jul/02/alternative-nobel-literature-prize-planned-in-sweden|title=Alternative Nobel literature prize planned in Sweden|work=[[衛報|The Guardian]]|first=Alison|last=Flood|date=July 2, 2018|access-date=September 18, 2018}}</ref>。

在对瑞典图书管理员提名的47名候选人的公众投票后，新学院宣布四名最终获提名人为[[玛丽斯·孔戴|玛丽斯·孔戴]]、[[尼爾·蓋曼|尼爾·蓋曼]]、[[村上春樹|村上春樹]]和[[金翠|金翠]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dennyaakademien.com/nominated|title=The finalists: The New Academy Prize in Literature 2018|website=Den Nya Akademien (The New Academy)|access-date=September 18, 2018}}</ref> 。

2018年9月17日，[[村上春樹|村上春樹]]要求撤回他的提名，称他希望“专注于写作，远离媒体关注”<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2018/09/16/national/japans-haruki-murakami-withdraws-consideration-alternative-nobel-award/|title=Japan’s Haruki Murakami withdraws from consideration for alternative Nobel award|work=[[日本時報|The Japan Times]]|date=September 16, 2018|access-date=September 18, 2018}}</ref>。

新学院文学奖最终授予了玛丽斯·孔戴<ref>[https://www.nytimes.com/2018/10/12/books/maryse-conde-alternative-lobel-literature.html Maryse Condé Wins an Alternative to the Literature Nobel in a Scandal-Plagued Year], nytimes.com. Retrieved October 12, 2018.</ref>。

'''新春教会'''（NewSpring Church）是[[美南浸信会|美南浸信会]]的一个巨型教会，位于[[安德森_(南卡罗来纳州)|安德森 (南卡罗来纳州)]]。教会成立于2000年1月。<ref name=NobleFired/>

教堂平均每周出席人数有32,000人。<ref name="Blume">{{cite web|last=Blume |first=Butch |url=http://baptistcourier.com/2014/03/state-church-s-c-s-newspring-among-nations-largest-fastest-growing-churches/ |title=State of the Church: S.C.’s NewSpring among nation’s largest, fastest-growing churches |publisher=http://baptistcourier.com/ |date=2014-03-04 |accessdate=2014-09-24}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Smith |first=Charmaine |url=http://www.independentmail.com/news/2010/jan/22/newspring-church-celebrating-10th-anniversary-bi-l/?cid=Facebook |title=NewSpring Church celebrating 10th anniversary at the Bi-Lo Center » Anderson Independent Mail |publisher=Independentmail.com |date=2014-06-09 |accessdate=2014-06-15 }}{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Wyatt |first=Dustin |url=http://www.goupstate.com/article/20140321/ARTICLES/403211000?p=1&tc=pg |title=NewSpring Church converts former Best Buy into worship center |publisher=goupstate.com |date=2014-03-21 |accessdate=2014-08-24}}</ref>此外，平均每周有7200人参加在线聚会。它是美南浸信会增长第二快和第四大的教会<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bpnews.net/41086/fastestgrowing--largest-churches-tallied |title=Fastest-growing & largest churches tallied - Baptist Press |publisher=Bpnews.net |date=2013-09-13 |accessdate=2014-06-15}}</ref>，也是南卡罗来纳州最大的教会。<ref name=NobleFired/>总的来说，它是美国100个最大，增长最快的巨型教会之一<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hirr.hartsem.edu/megachurch/database.html |title=Hartford Institute for Religion Research, database of Megachurches |publisher=Hirr.hartsem.edu |date= |accessdate=2010-02-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100213110739/http://hirr.hartsem.edu/megachurch/database.html |archivedate=2010-02-13 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://baptistcourier.com/2014/03/state-church-s-c-s-newspring-among-nations-largest-fastest-growing-churches/ |title=State of the Church: S.C.’s NewSpring among nation’s largest, fastest-growing churches |publisher=Baptist Courier |date=2014-03-04 |accessdate=2014-06-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.outreachmagazine.com/2013-outreach-100-largest-churches-america.html |title=2013 Outreach 100 Largest Churches in America |accessdate = 2014-04-14}}</ref>，是美国增长第二快的教会。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.outreachmagazine.com/2013-outreach-100-fastest-growing-churches-america.html |title=2013 Outreach 100 Fastest-Growing Churches in America |accessdate = 2014-04-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.outreachmagazine.com/2013-outreach-100-fastest-growing-churches-america.html |title=2013 Outreach 100 Fastest-Growing Churches in America |accessdate = 2014-04-14}}</ref>

新春教会分别设立在南卡罗来纳州的[[安德森_(南卡罗来纳州)|安德森]]、[[艾肯_(南卡罗来纳州)|艾肯]]、Boiling Springs、[[查尔斯顿_(南卡罗来纳州)|查尔斯顿]]、[[克莱姆森_(南卡罗来纳州)|克莱姆森]]、[[哥伦比亚_(南卡罗来纳州)|哥伦比亚]]、North East Columbia、[[佛罗伦萨_(南卡罗来纳州)|佛罗伦萨]]、[[格林维尔_(南卡罗来纳州)|格林维尔]]、[[格林伍德_(南卡罗来纳州)|格林伍德]]、Hilton Head、列克星敦、[[默特尔比奇_(南卡罗来纳州)|默特尔比奇]]、Powdersville、萨姆特、Rock Hill和[[斯帕坦堡_(南卡罗来纳州)|斯帕坦堡]]。<ref name="Blume"/><ref>{{cite web|last=Wyatt |first=Dustin |url=http://www.goupstate.com/article/20130830/ARTICLES/130839995/0/search |title=NewSpring Church experiences major growth |publisher=GoUpstate.com |date=2013-08-30 |accessdate=2014-06-15}}</ref>

2016年7月10日，该教会宣布其创始人佩里·诺贝尔已从2016年7月1日起因与酒精相关的个人问题被教会领导团队撤销主任牧师职务<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ns.downloads.s3.amazonaws.com/newspring/editorial/NewSpring%20Church%20Statement.pdf |title=Perry Noble removed as senior pastor at NewSpring |accessdate = 2016-07-11}}</ref>。

'''新真诚'''（与[[Post-postmodernism|后-后现代主义]]密切相关并时常被描述为它的同义词）是在[[音乐|音乐]]、[[美学|美学]]、文学小说、[[電影評論|影评]]、[[诗歌|诗歌]]、[[文学批评|文学评论]]和[[哲学|哲学]]上兴起的一种趋势。它通常描述了扩大和摆脱[[后现代主义|后现代主义]]的[[反諷|讽刺]]与[[犬儒主義|犬儒主义]]概念的创作，有部分回归[[現代主義|现代主义]]的表现。尽管它是由[[美国|美国]]作家[[大卫·福斯特·华莱士|戴维·福斯特·华莱士]]所推广的，其使用最早可以追溯到1980年代中期。<ref name=":0">David Foster Wallace, [http://jsomers.net/DFW_TV.pdf "E Unibus Pluram: Television and U.S. Fiction"].</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=(New) Sincerity in David Foster Wallace's "Octet"|url=http://dx.doi.org/10.1080/00111619.2014.899199|last=Williams|first=Iain|date=2015-05-27|journal=Critique: Studies in Contemporary Fiction|issue=3|doi=10.1080/00111619.2014.899199|volume=56|pages=299–314|issn=0011-1619}}</ref>

== 在音乐上 ==
1985至1990年间，“新真诚”被用作以[[奧斯汀|奧斯汀]]为中心的一群零散[[另类摇滚|另类摇滚]]乐队的总称，它们被视作为对当时尚不知名的音乐运动（例如[[朋克搖滾|朋克摇滚]]和[[新浪潮|新浪潮]]）的讽刺性的展望的回应。在使用中将“新真诚”一词与这些乐队联系起来始于奥斯丁朋克摇滚作家[[Jesse_Sublett|Jesse Sublett]]对他的朋友，当地音乐作家[[Margaret_Moser|Margaret Moser]]的一条不客气的评论。根据作者Barry Shank的说法，Sublett说：“所有这些新真诚乐队都是废物。”<ref name="Shank">Barry Shank, [https://books.google.com/books?as_isbn=9780819562760 ''Dissonant Identities: The Rock'N'Roll Scene in Austin, Texas''] ([//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wesleyan_University_Press Wesleyan University Press], 1994) ({{ISBN|9780819562760}}), p. 148–149 & p.271 n.84. ([https://books.google.com/books?id=v79of-KXnfgC&lpg=PP1&vq=crap&pg=RA1-PA171#v=snippet&q=%22all%20those%20new%20sincerity%20bands%22&f=false excerpt available] at [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_Books Google Books]).</ref> Sublett（在他自己的网站上）称他的话被错误引用了，他实际上告诉Moser的是，“这对我来说都是新的诚意...这不是我的菜。”<ref>[http://jessesublett.wordpress.com/the-skunks-the-band-that-blasted-austin-out-of-the-1970s/ "Jesse's Music Bio"]{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} at [http://jessesublett.wordpress.com/ ''Jesse Sublett's Little Black Book''] (retrieved September 18, 2009).</ref> 无论如何，Moser开始使用这些句子，其最终成为了这些乐队的流行语。<ref>Peter Blackstock, [http://www.nodepression.net/blogs/peter/2008/01/_is_it_worth_the_admission.html "'is it worth the admission....'"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081204134302/http://www.nodepression.net/blogs/peter/2008/01/_is_it_worth_the_admission.html |date=2008-12-04 }}, ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/No_Depression_(magazine) No Depression]'' blog post dated January 15, 2008.</ref><ref>Regarding [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jesse_Sublett Jesse Sublett] and his band, [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Skunks The Skunks], ''see'' Ken Lieck, [http://www.austinchronicle.com/gyrobase/Issue/story?oid=oid%3A79685 "Young, Loud, and Cheap: The Skunks, the Band That Broke Austin Out of the Seventies"], ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austin_Chronicle Austin Chronicle]'', December 8, 2000, ''and'' Jesse Sublett, ''Never the Same Again: A Rock 'N' Roll Gothic'' (Ten Speed Press, 2004), {{ISBN|978-1-58008-598-4}} ([https://books.google.com/books?id=-6yPxcrGqfMC&lpg=PP1&dq=Jesse%20Sublett&pg=PP1#v=onepage&q=%20&f=false excerpts available] at [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_Books Google Books]).</ref>

在美国国内，最成功的“新真诚”乐队是在1985到1991年间发行了四个受到好评的专辑的[[The_Reivers|The Reivers]]（原名Zeitgeist）乐队。由[[Alejandro_Escovedo|Alejandro Escovedo]]和[[Jon_Dee_Graham|Jon Dee Graham]]领导的[[True_Believers|True Believers]]乐队也受到了广泛的批评性的赞美和来自奥斯汀当地的好评，但是乐队存在难以在录制时捕获现场声音等问题。<ref>[{{Allmusic|class=artist|id=p27102|pure_url=yes}} True Believers] at [[Allmusic|Allmusic]].</ref>其它重要的“新真诚”乐队包括有[[Doctors_Mob|Doctors Mob]]乐队、<ref>Kent H. Benjamin, [http://weeklywire.com/ww/08-30-99/austin_music_feature2.html "Why Should Anyone Care Now?"], ''[[Austin_Chronicle|Austin Chronicle]] Weekly Wire'' August 30, 1999.</ref><ref>[{{Allmusic|class=artist|id=p26360|pure_url=yes}} Doctors Mob] at [[Allmusic|Allmusic]].</ref>[[Wild_Seeds|Wild Seeds]]乐队<ref>[{{Allmusic|class=artist|id=p27169|pure_url=yes}} Wild Seeds] at [[Allmusic|Allmusic]].</ref>和[[Glass_Eye|Glass Eye]]乐队。<ref>[{{Allmusic|class=artist|id=p4370|pure_url=yes}} Glass Eye] at [[Allmusic|Allmusic]].</ref>另一个标志性的“新真诚”人物是古怪但是备受赞誉的歌曲作者[[Daniel_Johnston|Daniel Johnston]]。<ref name="Shank" />

尽管受到了广泛的关注（包括《[[滾石_(雜誌)|滚石]]》杂志在全美国的覆盖以及1985年的[[音樂電視網|MTV]]节目''[[I.R.S._Records_Presents_The_Cutting_Edge|The Cutting Edge]]''），但没有一个“新真诚”乐队取得了较大的商业成功，而“光景”在几年内就结束了。<ref>Kristin Gorski, [http://annabellemagazine.com/annabelle%20issue%2012/08Z.html Almost Famous: The Austin Texas Soundtrack Circa 1985], ''Annabelle Magazine'', No. 12 (2006).</ref><ref>Michael Corcoran, "The New Sincerity: Austin in the Eighties", reprinted in Michael Corcoran, ''All Over the Map: True Heroes of Texas Music'' (University of Texas Press, 2005), {{ISBN|978-0-292-70976-8}}, pp. 150–156.</ref>

其它音乐作家也使用“新真诚”来描述后来的表演者，例如[[拱廊之火|拱廊之火]]、<ref>Huffington Post, [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/jonathan-d-fitzgerald/mumford-and-sons-god-and-the-new-sincerity_b_2694876.html "ultra-sincere indie artists from Arcade Fire"] ''[[Huffington_Post|Huffington Post]], May 16, 2013.</ref>[[Conor_Oberst|Conor Oberst]]、<ref>The New York Times,[https://www.nytimes.com/2005/01/16/arts/music/16sann.html "Mr. Sincerity tries a new trick"] ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]], January 16, 2005.</ref>、[[貓女魔力|貓女魔力]]、[[Devendra_Banhart|Devendra Banhart]]、[[Joanna_Newsom|Joanna Newsom]]、<ref name="jacket35">Jason Morris, [http://jacketmagazine.com/35/morris-sincerity.shtml “The Time Between Time: Messianism & the Promise of a “New Sincerity”], ''[[Jacket_(magazine)|Jacket]]'' 35 (2008)</ref>[[中性牛奶飯店|中性牛奶飯店]]、<ref>Huffington Post, [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/jonathan-d-fitzgerald/mumford-and-sons-god-and-the-new-sincerity_b_2694876.html "many significant indie artists"] ''[[Huffington_Post|Huffington Post]], May 16, 2013.</ref>[[Sufjan_Stevens|Sufjan Stevens]]<ref>Huffington Post, [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/jonathan-d-fitzgerald/mumford-and-sons-god-and-the-new-sincerity_b_2694876.html " many significant indie artists"] ''[[Huffington_Post|Huffington Post]], May 16, 2013.</ref>和[[Idlewild|Idlewild]]<ref>[[Robert_Christgau|Robert Christgau]], [http://www.villagevoice.com/2001-03-27/music/vibrators/2/ "Vibrators"], ''[[The_Village_Voice|The Village Voice]]'', March 27, 2001.</ref>，此外还有奥斯汀的[[Okkervil_River|Okkervil River]]<ref>Kate X. Messer, [http://www.austinchronicle.com/gyrobase/Issue/story?oid=oid:76071 "Okkervil River: The New Sincerity"], ''[[Austin_Chronicle|Austin Chronicle]]'', March 3, 2000.</ref>、[[Leatherbag|Leatherbag]]<ref>Austin Powell, [http://www.austinchronicle.com/gyrobase/Issue/review?oid=oid:754184 "Texas Platters: deEP end"], ''[[Austin_Chronicle|Austin Chronicle]]'', March 13, 2009.</ref>和[[Michael_Waller|Michael Waller]]。<ref>[http://www.cookylamoo.com/boringlikeadrill/2015/09/michael-vincent-waller-the-south-shore.html]</ref>

== 作为一个文化运动 ==
“新真诚主义”自2002年开始受到[[Public_Radio_International|PRI]]的''[[The_Sound_of_Young_America|The Sound of Young America]]''，其自称为“关于酷炫事物的公共广播节目”，的主持人[[Jesse_Thorn|Jesse Thorn]]的赞扬。Thorn将新真诚视为一场由格言“最多乐趣（Maximum Fun）”和“更加酷炫（Be More Awesome）”组成的文化运动。它颂扬特大规模的欢乐庆祝，并反对讽刺，尤其是对文化作品的讽刺。Thorn在他的节目和采访中提出了这一观点，<ref>Ben Kharakh, [http://gothamist.com/2006/11/02/jesse_thorn_ame.php "Jesse Thorn, America's Radio Sweetheart"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080929115220/http://gothamist.com/2006/11/02/jesse_thorn_ame.php |date=2008-09-29 }} in [[Gothamist|Gothamist]], posted November 2, 2006.</ref><ref>Dennis McCafferty, [http://www.usaweekend.com/07_issues/070114/070114tech.html "Top podcast picks: Favorites from experts in their category"]{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}, [[USA_Weekend|USA Weekend]], January 14, 2007. (Thorn's program was the in-print "podcast pick" of comedian [[Patton_Oswalt|Patton Oswalt]].)</ref><ref>[http://www.themerlinshow.com/ep/019-interview-jesse-thorn-part-1 "Interview: Jesse Thorn, Part 1"], ''[[The_Merlin_Show|The Merlin Show]]'', posted June 4, 2007.</ref><ref>Dan Brodnitz, [http://blogs.oreilly.com/digitalmedia/2008/09/an-interview-with-the-sound-of.html "An Interview with The Sound of Young America's Jesse Thorn"] {{Wayback|url=http://blogs.oreilly.com/digitalmedia/2008/09/an-interview-with-the-sound-of.html |date=20081019134825 }}, ''[[O'Reilly_Media|O'Reilly Digital Media]]'', posted September 15, 2008.</ref><ref>''But see'' Bill Forman, [http://www.metroactive.com/papers/cruz/03.08.06/muz-0610.html "Müz: The New Ambiguity"], ''[[Metro_Santa_Cruz|Metro Santa Cruz]]'', March 8–15, 2006 (opining that New Sincerity is "just another ironic hoax").</ref>一份俄罗斯后苏联审美理论的学术研究中以“新真诚”一词的美国普及者的身份提及了Thorn。<ref name="Yurchak n3">Alexei Yurchak, "Post-Post-Communist Sincerity: Pioneers, Cosmonauts, and Other Soviet Heroes Born Today", in Thomas Lahusen and Peter H. Solomon, ''What Is Soviet Now?: Identities, Legacies, Memories'' (LIT Verlag Berlin-Hamburg-Münster, 2008), {{ISBN|978-3-8258-0640-8}}, p.258 n.3, [https://books.google.com/books?id=3GonhERyzwEC&printsec=frontcover#PPA258,M1 excerpt] available at [[Google_Books|Google Books]]. ("In the United States, the term 'new sincerity' has been popularized since 9/11 by some youth media (for example, by Jesse Thorn, the host of a popular music program ''Sound of Young America'', on New York's National Public Radio station WNYC").)</ref>Thorn提出的概念的典型解释是这份2006年的“新真诚宣言”：
<blockquote>
什么是新真诚主义？你可以认为它像[[聖戰士_(VOLTRON)|战神金刚]]一样拥有讽刺和诚意，以形成一种令人惊叹的力量；或者认为它是缺乏讽刺和诚意的，那里更少便（显然）是更多。如果这让你的大脑感到紧张的话，想想[[Evel_Knievel|Evel Knievel]]。容我坦率地说，没有办法可以从字面上来欣赏Evel Knievel。Evel是那种连小说都无法描绘的人，因为现实实在是太夸张了。这是一个穿着红白蓝三色皮衣连身裤，开着某种火箭车，通过从东西上面跳过去来收获名利的人。这是一个就像[[蜘蛛人|小蜘蛛]]出现在在漫画书封面上一样自然的真人。简而言之，Evel Knievel令大家惊叹。但同样的道理，他也不是讽刺的。实际情况是，Evel他是，一言以蔽之，酷炫的……我们的问候方式：两个竖起的大拇指。我们的信条：“更加酷炫”。我们的生活方式：“最多乐趣”。将警惕置之脑后吧，朋友，用新真诚主义的方式活着。<ref>Jesse Thorn, [http://www.maximumfun.org/blog/2006/02/manifesto-for-new-sincerity.html "A Manifesto for The New Sincerity"], February 17, 2006.</ref>
</blockquote>

'''新西兰航天局'''（英语：New Zealand Space Agency）是[[新西兰|新西兰]]的公共服务机构，负责该国太空活动的“政策、管理和商业发展”。<ref name="NZSAaboutus">{{Cite web|url=https://www.mbie.govt.nz/science-and-technology/space/about-us/|title=About us|date=12 April 2017|accessdate=21 March 2019|publisher=Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment}}</ref>

== 历史 ==
新西兰航天局设立于2016年4月，隶属于该国商业、创新和就业部。目的在于促进新西兰航天产业的发展以及获得经济利益。<ref name="NZSAaboutus">{{Cite web|url=https://www.mbie.govt.nz/science-and-technology/space/about-us/|title=About us|date=12 April 2017|accessdate=21 March 2019|publisher=Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mbie.govt.nz/info-services/sectors-industries/space/new-zealand-space-agency/document-image-library/folder-pdf-library/factsheet-development-nz-based-space-industry.pdf|title=Fact sheet: Development of a New Zealand-based space economy|accessdate=24 August 2017|publisher=Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment}}</ref> 政府还设立了太空机构来管理该国日益增长的商业航天产业，特别是允许美国航天公司[[火箭实验室|火箭实验室]]在新西兰的附属机构进行航天发射活动。<ref>{{Cite book|url=http://www.mbie.govt.nz/info-services/sectors-industries/space/new-zealand-space-agency/about-us|title=Space Operations: Contributions from the Global Community|editor-last=Cruzen|editor-first=Craig|editor2-last=Schmidhuber|editor2-first=Michael|editor3-last=Lee|editor3-first=Young H.|editor4-last=Kim|editor4-first=Bangyeop|year=2017|accessdate=24 August 2017|publisher=Springer International Publishing AG|isbn=9783319519401|doi=10.1007/978-3-319-51941-8|lccn=2017936667}}</ref>

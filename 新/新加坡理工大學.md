'''新加坡理工大學'''（{{lang-en|Singapore Institute of Technology}}，縮寫：{{lang|en|SIT}}）是一座[[新加坡|新加坡]]的[[自治大學|自治大學]]，成立於2009年。

[[新加坡國會|新加坡國會]]在2014年2月17日通過一項議案，准許該大學頒發自己的學位證書，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.parliament.gov.sg/sites/default/files/Singapore%20Institute%20of%20Technology%20Bill%207-2014.pdf|title=Singapore Institute of Technology Bill|publisher=parliament.gov.sg|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140307195603/http://www.parliament.gov.sg/sites/default/files/Singapore%20Institute%20of%20Technology%20Bill%207-2014.pdf|archivedate=2014-03-07}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.moe.gov.sg/media/parliamentary-replies/2014/02/second-reading-speech-on-the-singapore-institute-of-technology-bill.php |title=Second Reading Speech on the Singapore Institute of Technology Bill |publisher=moe.gov.sg |accessdate=2014-04-05 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140309162535/http://www.moe.gov.sg/media/parliamentary-replies/2014/02/second-reading-speech-on-the-singapore-institute-of-technology-bill.php |archivedate=2014-03-09 }}</ref>此議案在當年生效。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.asiaone.com/news/edvantage/sit-becomes-5th-autonomous-uni-here|title=SIT Becomes Fifth Autonomous University |publisher=asiaone.com}}</ref>

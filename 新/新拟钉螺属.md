'''新拟钉螺属'''（學名：{{lang|la|''Neotricula''}}）屬於[[蓋螺科|蓋螺科]]（[[Pomatiopsidae|Pomatiopsidae]]）的一個{{tsl|en|freshwater snail|淡水螺|淡水}}[[微小貝|微小貝]]的[[属|属]]，是一種有[[鳃|鳃]]和{{tsl|en|operculum (gastropod)|口蓋}}的[[腹足纲|腹足纲]][[软体动物|软体动物]]。 

==分佈==
新拟钉螺属有至少五個物種分佈於[[中國|中國]]的[[湖南省|湖南省]]{{r|Attwood 2008}}。
{{tsl|en|List of non-marine molluscs of China|}}, {{tsl|en|List of non-marine molluscs of Laos|}} (one species),<ref name="Attwood 2008"/> {{tsl|en|List of non-marine molluscs of Thailand|}}<ref name="Attwood 2003"/> and {{tsl|en|List of non-marine molluscs of Cambodia|}}.<ref name="Attwood 2003">Attwood S. W., Ambu S., Meng X. H., Upatham E. S., Xu F.-S. et al. (2003). "The phylogenetics of triculine snails (Rissooidea: Pomatiopsidae) from south-east Asia and southern China: historical biogeography and the transmission of human schistosomiasis. ''{{tsl|en|Journal of Molluscan Studies|}}'' '''69'''(3): 263-271. {{doi|10.1093/mollus/69.3.263}}.</ref>

'''新开罗'''（{{lang-ar|لقاهرة الجديدة}}；El Qāhera El Gedīda），是[[埃及|埃及]][[开罗省|开罗省]]东南部的一座城市，距离[[马底|马底]]25千米<ref>{{cite web|title=New Urban Communities Authority Portal|url=http://www.newcities.gov.eg/english/New_Communities/Cairo/default.aspx|website=New Cities Government of Egypt|accessdate=15 October 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161218175439/http://www.newcities.gov.eg/english/New_Communities/Cairo/default.aspx|archivedate=18 December 2016}}</ref>，占地面积3万[[公顷|公顷]]。新開羅是[[開羅|開羅]]周邊地區已經建成的新城市之一，以緩解開羅市中心的擁堵。它是在2000年通過第191號總統令成立的<ref>{{cite news|last1=Cambanis|first1=Thanassis|title=To Catch Cairo Overflow, 2 Megacities Rise in Sand|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2010/08/25/world/africa/25egypt.html?_r=0|accessdate=15 October 2016|publisher=New York Times|date=24 August 2010}}</ref>。

新开罗位於以前的[[赫勒万|赫勒万]]<ref>{{cite web|title=City of Helwan in Egypt|url=http://www.touregypt.net/cairo/helwan.htm|website=Tour Egypt|accessdate=15 October 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161011195635/http://www.touregypt.net/cairo/helwan.htm|archivedate=11 October 2016}}</ref>和富裕的[[马底|马底]]及{{le|赫利奧波利斯 (開羅)|Heliopolis, Cairo|赫利奧波利斯}}之間<ref>{{cite web|title=Katameya District of New Cairo|url=http://www.touregypt.net/katameya.htm|website=Tour Egypt|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161011162639/http://www.touregypt.net/katameya.htm|archivedate=11 October 2016}}</ref>，海拔在{{convert|250|and|307|m|ft}}之間。

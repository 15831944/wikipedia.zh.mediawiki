'''紐馬克特'''（Newmarket）是[[英國|英國]][[薩福克郡|薩福克郡]]的一個城市，位於倫敦以北65英里（105公里）。紐馬克特最著名的是其的養馬場<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/horseracing/8341960/Housing-turf-war-divides-Newmarket-the-home-of-horse-racing.html|title=Housing turf war divides Newmarket, the home of horse racing|publisher=Daily Telegraph|date=February 23, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Heritage bid hit by cost fears|url = http://www.newmarketjournal.co.uk/news/latest-news/heritage-bid-hit-by-cost-fears-1-1348171|website = www.newmarketjournal.co.uk|accessdate = 2015-10-11|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20151119212421/http://www.newmarketjournal.co.uk/news/latest-news/heritage-bid-hit-by-cost-fears-1-1348171|archive-date = 2015-11-19|dead-url = yes}}</ref>，[[香港賽馬會|香港賽馬會]]常譯為新市場。

'''新民謠'''（'''Neofolk'''）是一種由[[民謠音樂|民謠音樂]]衍生而來的[[實驗音樂|實驗音樂]]，最初發端於[[:en:Post-industrial_music|後工業音樂]]的音樂圈內。<ref name=Webb>{{Cite book|title=Exploring the Networked Worlds of Popular Music |first=Peter |last=Webb |publisher= [[Routledge|Routledge]]|year=2007 |isbn=9780415956581  |chapter=Neo-Folk or Postindustrial Music |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=bURqJRy4KsMC&pg=PA60&dq=Neofolk+post-industrial&hl=e&nsa=X&ei=95UMT6HyIMXbsgaCxtjCBA&ved=0CDUQ6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=Neofolk%20post-industrial&f=false |quote=One milieu to develop out of Punk was the scene that later became known as either apocalyptic folk, postindustrial, or later neo-folk.}}</ref> 新民謠可以僅包含民謠音樂的元素，亦可以包含傳統民謠音樂配之以鋼琴、弦樂以及[[工業搖滾|工業音樂]]和[[實驗音樂|實驗音樂]]的元素。<ref name=NeumanBraun>{{Cite book|title=Die Welt der Gothics: Spielräume düster konnotierter Transzendenz [The World of the Goths: Scopes of a darkly-connoted Transcendence] |language=de |first=Klaus |last=Neumann-Braun |first2=Axel |last2=Schmidt |publisher=VS Verlag für Sozialwissenschaften | year=2008 |edition=2nd |isbn=3-531-15880-5 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=0ykjKReN-ygC&pg=PA280&dq=Neofolk&hl=en&sa=X&ei=wo0MT4jYBcn2sgbhhfmqBA&sqi=2&ved=0CEQQ6AEwBQ#v=onepage&q=Neofolk&f=false |page=280 }}</ref> 該流派通常囊括了各式各樣的主題。新民謠藝人有時也會嘗試其他的風格諸如[[:en:Neoclassical_dark_wave|新古典暗潮]]和[[:en:Martial_industrial|軍事工業音樂]]。

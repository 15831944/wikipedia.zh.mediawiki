'''新加坡氣象局'''（{{lang-en|Meteorological Service Singapore}}，[[縮寫|縮寫]]：'''MSS'''）是[[新加坡|新加坡]]{{tsl|en|National Environment Agency|國家環境局}}轄下的部門，專責觀察天氣及氣候，并提供氣象信息服務。該機構收集及維持天氣記錄，評估自然災害的風險與影響。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.weather.gov.sg/about-our-organisation/ |title=About | Our Organisation | |publisher=Weather.gov.sg |date=1980-01-01 |accessdate=2019-04-09 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190126052620/http://www.weather.gov.sg/about-our-organisation/ |archive-date=2019-01-26 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author=Andrea Ngandreang |url=http://www.straitstimes.com/news/singapore/more-singapore-stories/story/meteorological-service-singapore-launches-one-stop-weath |title=Meteorological Service Singapore launches one-stop weather information website, Singapore News & Top Stories |publisher=The Straits Times |date=2015-05-20 |accessdate=2016-04-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150613205927/http://www.straitstimes.com/news/singapore/more-singapore-stories/story/meteorological-service-singapore-launches-one-stop-weath |archive-date=2015-06-13 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

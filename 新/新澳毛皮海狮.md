'''新澳毛皮海狮'''（[[学名|学名]]：''{{lang|la|Arctocephalus fosteri}}''；[[毛利语|毛利语]]：kekeno<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.arkive.org/new-zealand-fur-seal/arctocephalus-forsteri/video-01.html|title=New Zealand fur seal video - Arctocephalus forsteri - 01|author=|date=|website=Arkive|access-date=2018-12-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170819070330/http://www.arkive.org/new-zealand-fur-seal/arctocephalus-forsteri/video-01.html|archive-date=2017-08-19|dead-url=yes}}</ref>；英语：New Zealand fur seal）是[[毛皮海狮属|毛皮海狮属]]下的一种海狮，主要分布于[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]南岸和[[新西兰|新西兰]][[南岛|南岛]]沿岸<ref name=iucn/>。
==形态==
雄性体重可达160千克，平均体重则为126千克<ref name=iucn/><ref name=adv>Harcourt, R.G., (2001). [http://www.tandfonline.com/doi/pdf/10.1080/03014223.2001.9517644 "Advances in New Zealand mammalogy 1990–2000: Pinnipeds"]. ''Journal of The Royal Society of New Zealand'', Retrieved 6 October 2011</ref>，体长可达2米。雌性体重在30-50千克之间，体长最多1.5米。<ref name=DOC>Department of Conservation. [http://www.doc.govt.nz/conservation/native-animals/marine-mammals/seals/nz-fur-seal/ "New Zealand fur seal/kekeno"] Retrieved 6 October 2011.</ref>

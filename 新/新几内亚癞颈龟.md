'''新幾內亞癩頸龜'''（[[學名|學名]]：'''''Elseya novaeguineae'''''）是[[齒緣癩頸龜屬|齒緣癩頸龜屬]]下的一種淡水龜，發現於[[印度尼西亞|印度尼西亞]]和[[巴布亞新幾內亞|巴布亞新幾內亞]]。

新幾內亞癩頸龜曾在2010年被重新劃分到寬胸癩頸龜屬下，<ref name=gt10>Georges, A. & Thomson, S. 2010. [http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2010/f/zt02496p037.pdf Diversity of Australasian freshwater turtles, with an annotated synonymy and keys to species.] Zootaxa  2496: 1–37.</ref> 但是不久被發現是一個錯誤，於是重新移回。<ref name=iucn>Anders G.J. Rhodin,  Peter Paul van Dijk, John B. Iverson, and H. Bradley Shaffer. 2010. [http://www.iucn-tftsg.org/wp-content/uploads/file/Accounts/crm_5_000_checklist_v3_2010.pdf Turtles of the World, 2010 Update: Annotated Checklist of Taxonomy, Synonymy, Distribution, and Conservation Status]  {{webarchive|url=https://www.webcitation.org/5uzfktoIh?url=http://www.iucn-tftsg.org/wp-content/uploads/file/Accounts/crm_5_000_checklist_v3_2010.pdf |date=2010-12-15 }}</ref>

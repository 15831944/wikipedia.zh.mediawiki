'''新造教會'''是個創立於1984年至今，以[[新加坡|新加坡]]為宣教區、擁有31,000名信眾的大型教會。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.newcreation.org.sg/about-us/history |title=History |publisher=''New Creation Church'' |accessdate=2010-06-15 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100723021646/http://www.newcreation.org.sg/about-us/history |archivedate=2010-07-23 }}</ref> [[約瑟夫·普林斯|約瑟夫·普林斯]]是此教會的資深牧師。
制度上，新創世教會不從屬於新加坡國外的任何一個教會；目前為新加坡基督教協進會的會員。此教會的成立宗旨為『to [[五旬節運動|see]] [[耶穌|Jesus]] in all the loveliness of His Person and the perfection of His work and to make Him known through the preaching of the [[福音|gospel]]』。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.newcreation.org.sg/about-us/mission-statement-core-values |title=Mission Statement & Core Values |publisher=New Creation Church |accessdate=2010-07-09}}</ref>

大多數新加坡裔英國人實際上大部分族裔都是[[華裔|華裔]]（[[新加坡華人|新加坡華人]]），英國新加坡裔社群的大致與[[英國華人|英國華人]]社群相同。<ref name="Born abroad" />

== 人口 ==
[[2001年英國人口普查|2001年英國人口普查]]有40474英國公民在新加坡出生<ref name=OECD>{{cite web|url=http://www.oecd.org/dataoecd/18/23/34792376.xls|title=Country-of-birth database|publisher=[[Organisation_for_Economic_Co-operation_and_Development|Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development]]|accessdate=2009-11-08}}</ref> ，自1991年人口普查以來，在新加坡出生的英國公民增加了19％。<ref name="Born abroad">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/shared/spl/hi/uk/05/born_abroad/countries/html/singapore.stm|title=Born abroad: Singapore|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=2009-11-08 | date=2005-09-07}}</ref>

[[英國國家統計局|英國國家統計局]]統計，2009年有41000新加坡出生的居民是居住在英國。<ref name="2009 estimates">{{cite web | url=http://www.statistics.gov.uk/downloads/theme_population/Population-by-country-of-birth-and-nationality-Oct08-Sep09.zip | title=Estimated population resident in the United Kingdom, by foreign country of birth (Table 1.3) | publisher=[[Office_for_National_Statistics|Office for National Statistics]] | date=September 2009 | accessdate=8 July 2010 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5vvnKhoUi?url=http://www.statistics.gov.uk/downloads/theme_population/Population-by-country-of-birth-and-nationality-Oct08-Sep09.zip | archivedate=22 January 2011 | df= }}</ref>

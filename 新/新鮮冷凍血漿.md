<!-- 定義及醫療用途 -->
'''新鮮冷凍血漿'''（FFP）為一從[[全血|全血]]製備而成的[[血液製品|血液製品]]<ref name=BNF69/>，一般是在採集全血後八小時內分離製備<ref name="血液成分精要">[http://intra.blood.org.tw/upload/98f89100-2f08-4499-8c06-77bde34ead8c.pdf 台灣血液學基金會 血液成分精要]</ref>，用於治療體內[[凝血|凝血因子]]過低（INR>1.5）或是[[血漿蛋白|血漿蛋白]]過少之患者<ref name=BNF69/><ref name=Sh2013/>，也做為{{le|血漿置換|Plasmapheresis}}的置換液<ref name=Drugs2017/><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Schwartz|first=Joseph|last2=Padmanabhan|first2=Anand|last3=Aqui|first3=Nicole|last4=Balogun|first4=Rasheed A.|last5=Connelly-Smith|first5=Laura|last6=Delaney|first6=Meghan|last7=Dunbar|first7=Nancy M.|last8=Witt|first8=Volker|last9=Wu|first9=Yanyun|date=June 2016|title=Guidelines on the Use of Therapeutic Apheresis in Clinical Practice-Evidence-Based Approach from the Writing Committee of the American Society for Apheresis: The Seventh Special Issue|journal=Journal of Clinical Apheresis|volume=31|issue=3|pages=149–338|doi=10.1002/jca.21470|issn=0733-2459}}</ref>，通常會建議（但非必要）使用ABO血型相符的血漿<ref>{{cite book |last1=Sikka |first1=Paul K. |last2=Beaman |first2=Shawn T. |last3=Street |first3=James A. |title=Basic Clinical Anesthesia |date=2015 |publisher=Springer |isbn=9781493917372 |page=102 |url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=QE0SCAAAQBAJ&pg=PA102 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |last1=Aglio |first1=Linda S. |last2=Lekowski |first2=Robert W. |last3=Urman |first3=Richard D. |title=Essential Clinical Anesthesia Review: Keywords, Questions and Answers for the Boards |date=2015 |publisher=Cambridge University Press |isbn=9781107681309 |page=218 |url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=VJzWBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA218 |language=en}}</ref>。不建議使用新鮮冷凍血漿作為{{le|代用血漿|volume expander}}<ref name=BNF69>{{cite book|title=British national formulary : BNF 69|date=2015|publisher=British Medical Association|isbn=9780857111562|page=172|edition= 69}}</ref>。使用方式為緩慢輸注至靜脈內<ref name=Drugs2017>{{cite web|title=Plasma Intravenous Advanced Patient Information - Drugs.com|url=https://www.drugs.com/cons/plasma-intravenous.html|website=www.drugs.com|accessdate=10 January 2017|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170111002446/https://www.drugs.com/cons/plasma-intravenous.html|archivedate=11 January 2017|df=}}</ref>。

<!-- 副作用及機制 -->
副作用包含噁心和發癢<ref name=BNF69/>，並有低機率發生[[過敏反應|過敏反應]]、[[血栓|血栓]]，或[[感染|感染]]<ref name=BNF69/><ref name=Sh2013/>。在[[懷孕|懷孕]]期間或[[哺乳|哺乳]]期間使用，對嬰兒的安全性仍未知<ref name=Drugs2017/>。患有{{link-en|蛋白質S缺乏症|protein S deficiency}}、{{link-en|免疫球蛋白A缺乏|IgA deficiency}}，或[[心臟衰竭|心臟衰竭]]的病人在使用上須特別注意<ref name=Drugs2017/>。新鮮冷凍血漿是由水，[[蛋白質|蛋白質]]，[[碳水化合物|碳水化合物]]，脂質，與[[維生素|維生素]]組成的複雜混合物<ref name=Sh2013/>。零下18度冷凍下可保存約一年<ref name=Sh2013>{{cite book|last1=Shaz|first1=Beth H.|last2=Hillyer|first2=Christopher D.|last3=Roshal|first3=Mikhail|last4=Abrams|first4=Charles S.|title=Transfusion Medicine and Hemostasis: Clinical and Laboratory Aspects|date=2013|publisher=Newnes|isbn=9780123977885|pages=209–212|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=8pBrgAGcoWMC&pg=PA209|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170923210341/https://books.google.com/books?id=8pBrgAGcoWMC&pg=PA209|archivedate=2017-09-23}}</ref>。

<!-- 歷史及文化 -->
[[第二次世界大戰|第二次世界大戰]]期間血漿製品第一次被應用於醫療中<ref name=Sh2013/><ref>[http://history.amedd.army.mil/booksdocs/wwii/blood/chapter7.htm Plasma Equipment and Packaging, and Transfusion Equipment]</ref>，名列[[世界衛生組織基本藥物清單|世界衛生組織基本藥物清單]]，該清單羅列一個[[醫療系統|健康照護體系]]中所需要的各項最安全有效的藥物。<ref name=WHO19th>{{cite web|title=WHO Model List of Essential Medicines (19th List)|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|work=World Health Organization|accessdate=8 December 2016|date=April 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161213052708/http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/essentialmedicines/EML_2015_FINAL_amended_NOV2015.pdf?ua=1|archivedate=13 December 2016}}</ref>。在英國一單位的新鮮冷凍血漿價錢約30英磅<ref name=Yen2013>{{cite book|last1=Yentis|first1=Steven M.|last2=Hirsch|first2=Nicholas P.|last3=Ip|first3=James|title=Anaesthesia and Intensive Care A-Z: An Encyclopedia of Principles and Practice|date=2013|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9780702053757|page=147|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Te7TAAAAQBAJ&pg=PA147|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170923210341/https://books.google.com/books?id=Te7TAAAAQBAJ&pg=PA147|archivedate=2017-09-23}}</ref>。亦有其他種類的新鮮冷凍血，包含{{le|採血後24小時內冷凍血漿|Plasma frozen within 24 hours}}、{{le|冷凍沉澱品減除血漿|Cryoprecipitate reduced plasma}}、冷凍血漿、以及[[溶劑清潔劑處理血漿|溶劑清潔劑處理血漿]]<ref name=Sh2013/>。其中冷凍血漿類似新鮮冷凍血漿，不過是在採集全血八小時分離，在零下18度冰箱可存放五年<ref name="血液成分精要"/>。

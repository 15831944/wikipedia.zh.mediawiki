'''新鮮奶油'''（{{lang-en|Whipped cream}}）或稱'''生奶油'''，是通過攪拌器或攪拌機的奶油，直到它明亮和蓬鬆；有時會搭配[[香莢蘭|香莢蘭]]調味，使其味道變甜。將其液態的奶油不停地攪拌直到被打成蓬鬆的半液態軟膠狀，這是因為奶油中含有超過30%的[[脂肪|脂肪]]在攪拌過程中與空氣混和，空氣被油脂的網絡鎖住而導致得出來的奶油是攪拌前的大約兩倍體積<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Viet|last2=Cua|title=Effect of thermal treatment on physical properties and stability of whipping and whipped cream|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0260877415001934|journal=Journal of Food Engineering|volume= 163, October 2015, Pages 32–36}}</ref>。如果在形成生奶油的狀態後繼續攪拌，油脂會黏在一起變成固體的[[牛油|牛油]]，其液態成分會被完全地分離出來成為[[白脫牛奶|白脫牛奶]]，這種奶因為上述原因所以其脂肪含量比一般的[[牛奶|牛奶]]要低<ref name='mcgee'>Harold McGee, ''On Food and Cooking'', p. 32</ref>。有時候為了令生奶油的質感更挺身及避免過度攪拌成牛油會在事前加入[[糖霜_(餐飲)|糖霜]]<ref>{{cite book|author1=E. Dentu|title=La Cuisine classique: études pratiques, raisonnées et démonstratives de l'Ecole française appliquée au service à la russe|date=1868|url=https://books.google.de/books?id=A7DX5_x_hrAC&pg=PA122&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q&f=false|accessdate=2016-11-01}}</ref>。

生奶油通常加在[[咖啡|咖啡]]或[[可可|可可]]飲料上<ref>{{cite book|author1=Jules Gouffé|title=Le livre de patisserie|date=1873|publisher=Hachette|url=https://books.google.de/books?id=eNUqAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA138&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q&f=false|accessdate=2016-11-01}}</ref>，或塗在西式甜點上如[[蛋糕|蛋糕]]、[[派|派]]或[[雪糕|雪糕]]等<ref>Rose Levy Beranbaum, ''The Pie and Pastry Bible'', 2009, ISBN 1439130876, p. 550</ref>。

==历史==
生奶油在16世纪开始流行<ref name='scully'>Terence Scully, trans., ''The Opera of Bartolomeo Scappi (1570): L'arte et prudenza d'un maestro Cuoco; The Art and Craft of a Master Cook'', 2008, ISBN 0-8020-9624-7, [https://books.google.com/books?id=GrvhZvK5pCgC&pg=PA105 p. 105, note 2.39], with many menus including "neve di latte servita con zuccaro sopra" 'milk snow with sugar on top', [https://books.google.com/books?id=GrvhZvK5pCgC&q=%22neve+di+latte%22 ''passim'']</ref>，在多本著作中有提及<ref>Catherine Frances Frere, ''Prepere newe Booke of Cokerye'', 1545 (modern edition 1913) -- cited in Scully</ref><ref>Michelle Berriedale-Johnson, ''Festive Feasts Cookbook'' (British Museum), 2004, ISBN 0-299-19510-4, [https://books.google.com/books?id=qiDBvjhUqnUC&pg=PA33 p. 33], citing Messisbugo's ''Banchetti, composizioni di vivande e apparecchio generale''</ref><ref>''Ouverture de cuisine'',  "Pour faire neige", p. 123 [http://www.uni-giessen.de/gloning/tx/ouv3.htm transcription]</ref>，曾被称为“牛奶雪”（英语：milk snow）<ref>Rabelais, ''Quart livre'', LIX, 1552, éd. R. Marichal, p. 241, cited in the ''Trésor de la langue française''</ref>。雪奶油（英语：snow cream）的名称一直到17世纪还在使用 <ref>''Dictionarium Rusticum, Urbanicum & Botanicum'', 1726, ''s.v.'' 'Syllabub' [https://books.google.com/books?id=v2FJAAAAYAAJ&pg=RA1-PT296 full text]</ref><ref>Sarah Harrison, ''The house-keeper's pocket-book, and compleat family cook'', 1749, p. 173. [https://books.google.com/books?id=3IcEAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA173 full text]</ref>。

==制备==
生奶油可以买现成的<ref>Wayne Gisslen, ''Professional Baking'', 2008, ISBN 0471783498, p. 264</ref>，也可以自己做。打發之前最好是冷藏<ref>{{cite web|author1=Hirsheimer Hamilton|title=Whip it Real Good: How to Make Whipped Cream at Home|url=http://www.bonappetit.com/test-kitchen/common-mistakes/article/whipped-cream-common-mistakes|website=bon appetit|accessdate=2016-11-01|date=2014-11-19}}</ref>。可以使用{{link-en|擠花袋|pastry bag}}在糕點上擠出生奶油裝飾花紋，添加稳定剂可长时间保持形状<ref>Alan Imeson, ed., ''Food Stabilisers, Thickeners and Gelling Agents'', 2011, ISBN 1444360337, ''passim''</ref><ref>oetker.[http://www.oetker.ca/ca-en/our-products/dr-oetker-whip-it/dr-oetker-whip-it.html Dr. Oetker Whip It]</ref>。

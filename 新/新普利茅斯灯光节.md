'''新普利茅斯灯光节'''({{lang-en|TSB Festival of Lights}}) 是位于新西兰城市[[新普利茅斯|新普利茅斯]]公园的一个节庆活动。每年历时从12月中旬到2月上旬。各种彩光灯将公园打造为绚烂的光影世界。

==历史==
[[image:Waterfall_at_the_Festival_of_Lights.jpg|thumb]]公园]]
灯光节开始于1993年，作为[[新普利茅斯|新普利茅斯]]夏季旅游和文化活动。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nz2011.govt.nz/realnzfestival/events/131-taranaki-festival-of-lights |title=Taranaki Festival of Lights |publisher=NewZealand2011 |accessdate=4 February 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101114193140/http://www.nz2011.govt.nz/realnzfestival/events/131-taranaki-festival-of-lights |archivedate=14 November 2010 |df= }}</ref>每年吸引10万游客前来旅游。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/taranaki-daily-news/news/4601980/Festival-of-Lights-attracts-community-visitors|title=Festival of Lights attracts community, visitors|first=Kate |last=Saunders |date=1 February 2011 |accessdate=4 February 2011|publisher=Taranaki Daily News}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/taranaki-daily-news/news/76461466/new-plymouths-festival-of-lights-wraps-up-another-successful-year.html| title=New Plymouth's Festival of Lights wraps up another successful year| first=Tara| last=Shaskey| publisher=Taranaki Daily News| date=1 February 2016}}</ref>
2013年举办过二十周年庆典活动。<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.festivaloflights.nz| title=TSB Bank Festival of Lights| publisher=NPDC}}</ref>

==主办方==
节日由新西兰[[TSB银行|TSB银行]]主要赞助。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nzonline.org.nz/festivaloflights.htm |title=TSB Bank Festival of Lights |publisher=nzonline.org.nz}}</ref>

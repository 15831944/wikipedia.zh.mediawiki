'''傑克·漢密爾頓'''（{{lang-en|Jack Hamilton}}；{{bd|1994年|3月23日}}）是一位[[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]]足球運動員。在場上的位置是守門員。他現在效力於[[蘇格蘭足球超級聯賽|蘇格蘭足球超級聯賽]]球隊[[哈茨足球俱樂部|哈茨足球俱樂部]]<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.heartsfc.co.uk/articles/jack-hamilton-hearts_2241487_56368 | title=Hearts Profile | publisher=[[Heart_of_Midlothian_F.C.|Heart of Midlothian F.C.]] | date=10 September 2011 | accessdate=12 September 2011 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110927172649/http://www.heartsfc.co.uk/articles/jack-hamilton-hearts_2241487_56368 | archivedate=2011年9月27日 }}</ref>。他也代表蘇格蘭各級青年國家足球隊參賽<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/football_player_profile.cfm?page=454&playerID=63097&squadID=21 | title=Jack Hamilton – U15 Squad | publisher=[[Scottish_Football_Association|Scottish Football Association]] | accessdate=12 September 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/football_player_profile.cfm?page=454&playerID=63097&squadID=17 | title=Jack Hamilton – U16 Squad | publisher=[[Scottish_Football_Association|Scottish Football Association]] | accessdate=12 September 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.scottishfa.co.uk/football_player_profile.cfm?page=454&playerID=63097&squadID=13 | title=Jack Hamilton – U17 Squad | publisher=[[Scottish_Football_Association|Scottish Football Association]] | accessdate=12 September 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.uefa.com/under17/teams/player=250022719/index.html | title=Uefa Profile | publisher=Uefa.com | accessdate=12 September 2011}}</ref>。

'''傑佛瑞·D·內桑森'''（{{lang-en|Jeffrey D. Nathanson}}，{{bd|1965年|10月12日}}），是一名美國男編劇及導演。

== 早期生活 ==
內桑森出生於[[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[洛杉磯郡|洛杉磯郡]]<ref name='fs'>{{cite web |url=https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:VGJ9-GFB |title=Jeffrey D Nathanson, 12 Oct 1965 |website=Familysearch.org |accessdate=2015-09-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://funeralinnovations.com/obituary/114730/Gertrude-Penner/ |title=Gertrude Penner |website=Funeralinnovations.com |accessdate=2015-09-01}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hollywood.com/celebrities/jeff-nathanson-57313726/ |title=Hollywood.com biography |website=Hollywood.com |accessdate=2015-09-01}}</ref>。1983年至1985年間，內桑森在[[加利福尼亞大學聖塔芭芭拉分校|加利福尼亞大學聖塔芭芭拉分校]]主修英語，<ref name='ucsbdn1'>{{cite news |last=Quiambao |first=Carissa |date=2011-10-31 |title=Former Gaucho Unveils New Film |url=http://dailynexus.com/2011-10-31/gaucho-unveils-film/ |newspaper=Daily Nexus |location=[[Santa_Barbara,_California|Santa Barbara, California]] |accessdate=2015-09-01}}</ref><ref name='stumped'>{{cite web |url=http://www.stumpedmagazine.com/interviews/jeff-nathanson/ |title=Jeff Nathanson Interview |last=Neumer |first=Chris |website=Stumpedmagazine.com |accessdate=2015-09-01}}</ref>；在校期間，他曾參與學校報紙《{{le|Daily Nexus|Daily Nexus}}》的工作。內桑森曾表示他在聖塔芭芭拉的學習經歷讓他想成為一名編劇<ref name='ucsbdn1'/><ref name='ucsbdn2'>{{cite news |last=Quiambao |first=Carissa |date=2011-10-27 |title=Renowned Director To Visit Campus |url=http://dailynexus.com/2011-10-27/renowned-director-visit-campus/ |newspaper=Daily Nexus |location=[[Santa_Barbara,_California|Santa Barbara, California]] |accessdate=2015-09-01}}</ref>，隨後內桑森又在{{le|AFI戲劇學院|AFI Conservatory}}上了一年的編劇課<ref name='stumped'/><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.afi.com/Conservatory/alumni/alumni.aspx |title=AFI ALUMNI |website=Afi.com |accessdate=2015-09-01}}</ref>。

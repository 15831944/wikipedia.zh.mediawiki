'''傑辛達·凱特·勞雷爾·阿德恩'''<ref>{{cite web|editor=王天僚|url=http://www.cankaoxiaoxi.com/ym/20170922/2234078.shtml|title=9月22日译名发布：Jacinda Ardern|work=《[[参考消息|参考消息]]》|date=2017-09-22|accessdate=2017-10-19|language=zh-cn}}</ref>（{{lang-en|'''Jacinda Kate Laurell Ardern'''}}；{{bd|1980年|7月26日}}），[[新西蘭|新西蘭]]政治人物，現任[[新西蘭總理|新西蘭總理]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.parliament.nz/en-NZ/PB/Debates/Debates/5/c/4/49HansD_20081208_00000010-Members-Sworn.htm|title=New Zealand Hansard – Members Sworn (Volume:651;Page:2)|publisher=[[新西蘭國會|Parliament of New Zealand]]|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130223070945/http://www.parliament.nz/en-NZ/PB/Debates/Debates/5/c/4/49HansD_20081208_00000010-Members-Sworn.htm|archivedate=2013-02-23}}</ref>。她最早在[[2008年新西蘭大選|2008年新西蘭大選]]期間作爲議員進入國會<ref>[http://2008.electionresults.govt.nz/ Election results] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20120701105222/http://2008.electionresults.govt.nz/ |date=2012-07-01 }}</ref>，并且自2017年3月8日以來在國會代表艾伯特山（{{lang|en|Mount Albert}}）選區。阿德恩於2017年8月1日就任了[[新西蘭工黨|新西蘭工黨]]黨魁。

在[[意識形態|意識形態]]方面，阿德恩自認同時是[[社會民主主義|社會民主主義者]]<ref name="Murphy">{{cite news|last1=Murphy|first1=Tim|title=What Jacinda Ardern wants|url=https://www.newsroom.co.nz/2017/07/31/40717/what-jacinda-wants|accessdate=2017-08-15|work=Newsroom|date=2017-08-01}}</ref>與[[進步主義|進步主義者]]<ref>{{cite news|title=Live: Jacinda Ardern answers NZ's questions |url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/national/politics/95407204/live-ask-jacinda-ardern-anything|work=Stuff|date=2017-08-03}}</ref>。作爲[[勞工運動|勞工運動]]的支持者，她反對[[新西蘭國家黨|新西蘭國家黨]]所提倡的高收入者減稅政策<ref>{{cite news|last1=Satherley|first1=Dan|title=High earners don't want tax cuts – Ardern|url=http://www.newshub.co.nz/home/politics/2017/07/high-earners-don-t-want-tax-cuts-ardern.html|accessdate=2017-08-15|work=Newshub|date=2017-07-14}}</ref>，并認同一個[[福利國家|福利國家]]應當爲「無法自食其力的人群」提供安全保障<ref name="Murphy"/>。在文化立場上，阿德恩支持[[同性婚姻|同性婚姻]]、曾於2013年爲《[[婚姻（婚姻定義）補充法案|婚姻（婚姻定義）補充法案]]》投下支持票<ref>{{cite news|title=Marriage equality bill: How MPs voted |url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/waikato-times/news/8566347/Marriage-equality-bill-How-MPs-voted|work=Waikato Times|date=2013-04-18}}</ref>、并爲[[墮胎|墮胎]]法律的自由化努力<ref>{{cite news|title=English, Little, Ardern on abortion laws |url=https://yournz.org/2017/03/13/english-little-ardern-on-abortion-laws/|work=Your NZ|date=2017-03-13}}</ref>。

==投身政治==
2001年，阿德恩在[[懷卡托大學|懷卡托大學]]畢業後，作爲研究人員供職於時任總理[[海倫·克拉克|海倫·克拉克]]的辦公室。她隨後前去英國、擔任了當時[[英國首相|英國首相]][[托尼·布萊爾|托尼·布萊爾]]的政策顧問<ref name="People - New Zealand Labour Party">{{cite web|url=http://labourparty.org.nz/people/|title=People – New Zealand Labour Party|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081223024509/http://labourparty.org.nz/people/|archivedate=2008-12-23}}</ref>，并在2008年被選爲{{le|國際社會主義青年聯盟|International Union of Socialist Youth}}的主席<ref>{{cite web|last1=Kirk|first1=Stacey|title=Jacinda Ardern says she can handle it and her path to the top would suggest she's right|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/dominion-post/news/politics/95327574/Jacinda-Ardern-says-shes-can-handle-it-and-her-path-to-the-top-would-suggest-shes-right|work=The Dominion Post|publisher=Stuff|accessdate=2017-08-15|language=en|date=2017-08-01}}</ref>。2008年至2017年，阿德恩開始了長達十年的國會[[名單比例代表制|名單比例代表制]]議員生涯。2017年下半年，{{le|安妮特·金|Annette King}}辭任新西蘭工黨副主席，該職位經由黨内一致通過、由阿德恩接替。

2017年8月1日，原工黨黨魁[[安德魯·利特爾_(政治家)|安德魯·利特爾]]因該黨[[民意調查|民意調查]]支持率創下歷史新低之後宣佈辭職，阿德恩則成爲了新的工黨領袖<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/nz/news/article.cfm?c_id=1&objectid=11897447|accessdate=2017-08-01|title=Andrew Little quits: Jacinda Ardern is new Labour leader, Kelvin Davis is deputy|date=2017-08-01|work=The New Zealand Herald}}</ref>。在阿德恩的領導下，工黨在過去12年來首次在民調中擊敗了曠日持久的對手國家黨。在同年9月23日的普選當中，工黨贏得46議席、相較原本席位淨增長14個名額，緊隨在[[比爾·英格利希|比爾·英格利希]]治下贏得56議席的國家黨之後<ref>{{cite web|title=2017 General Election – Official Results|url=http://www.electionresults.govt.nz/electionresults_2017/|publisher={{le|新西蘭選舉委員會|Electoral Commission (New Zealand)|Electoral Commission}}|accessdate=2017-10-07}}</ref>。隨著[[新西蘭第一黨|新西蘭第一黨]]領袖[[溫斯頓·彼特斯|溫斯頓·彼特斯]]宣佈將與工黨組成聯合政府，阿德恩在下一届政府出任[[新西蘭總理|新西蘭總理]]。

==總理生涯==
2017年10月26日，阿德恩正式接替英格利希出任新西蘭第40任、歷來第三位女性、及自1856年以來最年輕的總理。

2018年9月，她成為首位帶同自己的嬰兒出席[[聯合國大會|聯合國大會]]的女性領導人，並就氣候變化、性別平等等議題發表談話。<ref>{{cite news |last1=Roy |first1=Eleanor Ainge |title=Jacinda Ardern makes history with baby Neve at UN general assembly |url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2018/sep/25/jacinda-ardern-makes-history-with-baby-neve-at-un-general-assembly |accessdate=27 September 2018 |work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] |date=24 September 2018 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Cole |first=Brendan |url=https://www.newsweek.com/new-zealand-prime-minister-makes-history-becoming-first-woman-bring-baby-un-1137401 |title=Jacinda Ardern: New Zealand Prime Minister Makes History By Becoming First Woman to Bring Baby into U.N.Assembly |publisher=Newsweek.com |date= |accessdate=28 September 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Full speech: 'Me too must become we too' – Jacinda Ardern calls for gender equality, kindness at UN |url=https://www.tvnz.co.nz/one-news/new-zealand/full-speech-me-too-must-become-we-jacinda-ardern-calls-gender-equality-kindness-un |accessdate=29 September 2018 |work=[[TVNZ|TVNZ]] |language=en}}</ref>

在2019年3月15日發生[[基督城清真寺槍擊案|基督城清真寺槍擊案]]發生後，阿爾登透過電視講話發表聲明，稱那是國家最黑暗的一日之一，並向死傷者致哀，同時將事件定性為[[恐怖襲擊|恐怖襲擊]]，<ref>{{cite web |last1=Britton |first1=Bianca |title=New Zealand PM full speech: 'This can only be described as a terrorist attack' |url=https://edition.cnn.com/2019/03/15/asia/new-zealand-jacinda-ardern-full-statement-intl/index.html |website=CNN |accessdate=16 March 2019 |date=15 March 2019}}</ref>翌日她更表示會硏究收緊槍械管制。

==個人生活==

阿德恩從小在一個[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|摩門教]]家庭長大，2005年因支持[[LGBT權利運動|LGBT同志平權]]運動而離教，<ref name="Knight2017">{{cite news|url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/lifestyle/news/article.cfm?c_id=6&objectid=11789352|title=The politics of life: The truth about Jacinda Ardern|last=Knight|first=Kim|newspaper=The New Zealand Herald|date=2017-01-29|accessdate=2017-08-15}}</ref>2017年1月，她稱自己是個[[不可知論|持不可知論者]]<ref name="Knight2017"/>。2018年2月17日，阿德恩成為第一個參加[[驕傲遊行|同志遊行]]的紐西蘭總理。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2018/feb/19/jacinda-ardern-becomes-first-new-zealand-pm-to-take-part-in-gay-pride-parade|title=Jacinda Ardern becomes first New Zealand PM to march in gay pride parade|last=|first=|date=2018-02-19|work=The Guardian|accessdate=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=|archivedate=|}}</ref>

她目前的伴侶是電視及廣播節目主持人{{tsl|en|Clarke Gayford|克拉克·蓋福德}}。阿德恩成為總理後，他們養的貓{{Tsl|en|Paddles (cat)||槳槳}}成為「第一家貓」，它甚至也有個[[Twitter|推持]]帳號。但2017年11月，槳槳成為第一家貓後沒幾天，發生車禍意外而不幸離世。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/entertainment/news/article.cfm?c_id=1501119&objectid=11674545|title=Clarke Gayford: Jacinda Ardern is the best thing that's ever happened to me|last=Knight|first=Kim|date=2016-07-16|newspaper=The New Zealand Herald |issn=1170-0777 |access-date=2016-07-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/nz/news/article.cfm?c_id=1&objectid=11941627|title=Jacinda Ardern's cat Paddles has died after being hit by a car|date= 2017-11-07|publisher=|accessdate=2017-11-07}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/nov/08/paddles-first-cat-of-new-zealand-dies-hit-by-car-jacinda-ardern|title=Paddles, First Cat of New Zealand and social media star, dies after being hit by car|first=Charles|last=Anderson|date=2017-11-07|work=The Guardian|accessdate=2017-11-08|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171107232111/https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/nov/08/paddles-first-cat-of-new-zealand-dies-hit-by-car-jacinda-ardern|archivedate=2017-11-07}}</ref>

2018年1月19日，阿德恩宣布她懷孕，預產期是2018年6月。阿德恩總理透露，自己和男友蓋福德一直想要有小孩，但曾被醫生告知需要醫療協助才可受孕。2017年10月，就職前兩週，發現自己受孕，非常驚喜。<ref>{{cite news | url= http://www.nzherald.co.nz/nz/news/article.cfm?c_id=1&objectid=11978029 | title= Prime Minister Jacinda Ardern announces pregnancy | date=2018-01-19 | work=New Zealand Herald | accessdate=2018-01-19}}</ref>她懷孕的消息掀起了全球媒體熱議，超過800家的新聞及雜誌報導這項消息，電視節目及社群網絡也對她的產假、適任與否熱烈討論，甚至引發「皇家寶寶」（{{lang-en|royal baby}}）熱潮。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2018/jan/26/jacinda-ardern-pregnant-new-zealand-baby-mania|title='I’m pregnant, not incapacitated': PM Jacinda Ardern on baby mania|last=Ainge Roy|first=Eleanor|date=2018-01-26|work=The Guardian|accessdate=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=|archivedate=|}}</ref>阿德恩是世界史上，第二位在任內懷孕的[[民選|民選]][[國家領導人|國家領導人]]，第一位是已故的[[巴基斯坦總理|巴基斯坦總理]][[班娜姬·布托|班娜姬·布托]]。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/morning-mix/wp/2018/01/19/new-zealands-prime-minister-is-pregnant-i-am-not-the-first-woman-to-multitask-she-says/|title=New Zealand's prime minister is pregnant. 'I am not the first woman to multitask,' she says.|last=Schmidt|first=Samantha|date=2018-01-19|work=Washington Post|accessdate=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=|archivedate=|}}</ref>

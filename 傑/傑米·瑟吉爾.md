'''傑米·瑟吉爾'''（{{lang-en|'''Jimmy Sheirgill'''}}，{{bd|1972年|12月3日|catIdx=Sheirgill, Jimmy}}）是[[印度|印度]]男演員和制片人，主要作品為[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]電影。<ref>[https://tribune.com.pk/story/710419/producing-films-was-a-bad-experience-jimmy-shergill/ “Producing films was a bad experience: Jimmy Shergill”]. The Express Tribune. 19-05-2014.</ref>

瑟吉爾較知名的電影有《[[真愛永存|真愛永存]]》（2000）、《[[傻瓜大哥再出擊|傻瓜大哥再出擊]]》（2006）、《Tanu Weds Manu》（2011）和《[[我的名字叫可汗|我的名字叫可汗]]》（2010）等等。<ref>[http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/jimmy-shergill-happy-birthday-actor-not-in-a-hurry-to-be-a-star-delivered-great-performances-4964782/lite/ “Happy Birthday Jimmy Shergill: The actor who chooses his craft over stardom”]. Indian Express. 03-12-2017.</ref>

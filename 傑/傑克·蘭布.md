'''雅各·萊恩·蘭布'''（{{lang-en|Jacob Ryan Lamb}}，{{bd|1990年|10月9日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟亞利桑那響尾蛇隊的捕手。他於2014年登上大聯盟，2017年入選明星賽。

==早期==
蘭布生於西雅圖。高中時就已經是隊上的明星球員。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/high-school/baseball/story/_/id/4159489/opposites-attract-slugging-pair/ |title=Bishop Blanchet (Seattle) sluggers Jake Lamb and Josh Sale may not have a ton in common off the field, but when it comes to baseball they're on the same page. – ESPN |publisher=Espn.go.com |date=June 16, 2009   |accessdate=August 8, 2014}}</ref>

在他高中畢業後，隨即投入[[2009年美國職棒大聯盟選秀|2009年美國職棒大聯盟選秀]]，他在第38輪被匹茲堡海盜隊選走。不過他並未與球隊簽約，而是繼續就讀華盛頓大學。<ref>{{cite web|last=Wilmoth |first=Charlie |url=https://www.bucsdugout.com/2016/1/7/10727896/looking-back-at-the-pirates-2009-draft |title=Looking back at the Pirates' 2009 draft |publisher=Bucs Dugout |date=January 7, 2016 |accessdate=March 2, 2018}}</ref>
==職業生涯==
大學畢業的蘭姆投入[[2012年美國職棒大聯盟選秀|2012年美國職棒大聯盟選秀]]，他在選秀會上第6輪被亞利桑那響尾蛇選走。2013年，他在亞利桑那秋季聯盟出賽。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article/ari/d-backs-prospect-jake-lamb-enjoying-arizona-fall-league-opportunity?ymd=20131112&content_id=63867446&vkey=news_ari |title=D-backs prospect Jake Lamb enjoying Arizona Fall League opportunity | dbacks.com: News |publisher=Mlb.mlb.com |date=June 30, 2014 |accessdate=August 8, 2014}}</ref>2014年受邀參與春訓。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article/mlb/arizona-diamondbacks-spring-training-set-to-start-early-this-year?ymd=20140109&content_id=66453962&vkey=news_mlb |title=Arizona Diamondbacks' Spring Training set to start early this year | MLB.com: News |publisher=Mlb.mlb.com |date=January 9, 2014 |accessdate=August 8, 2014}}</ref>該年他也漸漸從2A升上3A。<ref>{{cite web|author=Nick Piecoro, azcentral sports 2:15 p.m. MST August 7, 2014 |url=http://www.azcentral.com/story/sports/mlb/diamondbacks/2014/08/07/arizona-diamondbacks-promoting-jake-lamb/13738591/ |title=Diamondbacks promoting prospect Jake Lamb |publisher=Azcentral.com |date= |accessdate=August 8, 2014}}</ref>

2014年7月31日，響尾蛇隊交易Martin Prado至洋基隊。蘭布在8月7日登上大聯盟。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.azcentral.com/story/sports/mlb/diamondbacks/2014/08/08/jake-lambs-call-majors-came-week-later-expected/13762007/ |title=Youth movement continues with Jake Lamb call up |publisher=Azcentral.com |date= |accessdate=March 2, 2018}}</ref>他出賽37場，打擊率2成30，4轟。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.seattletimes.com/sports/mariners/jake-lamb-emerging-major-leaguer-from-seattle-comes-home/ |title=Jake Lamb, emerging major-leaguer from Seattle, comes home |publisher=The Seattle Times |date=July 26, 2015 |accessdate=March 2, 2018}}</ref>2015年，他因傷只出賽105場。打擊率2成63，6轟34分打點。2016年上半季，他的打擊率為2成91，20轟61分打點。他也順利入選生涯首次明星賽。<ref>{{cite web|last=Gilbert |first=Steve |url=https://www.mlb.com/news/d-backs-jake-lamb-4th-in-all-star-final-vote/c-188781982 |title=D-backs' Jake Lamb 4th in All-Star Final Vote |publisher=MLB.com |date=January 20, 2016 |accessdate=March 2, 2018}}</ref>該季他打擊率為2成49，29轟91分打點。

2017年，他再度入選明星賽。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/mlb/2017/07/11/diamondbacks-all-star-game-paul-goldschmidt-zack-greinke/466760001/ |title=Resurgent Diamondbacks well-represented at All-Star Game |publisher=Usatoday.com |date= |accessdate=March 2, 2018}}</ref>該年他打擊率為2成48，30轟105分打點。2017年7月21日，響尾蛇隊前2棒David Peralta和[[A·J·波拉克|A·J·波拉克]]第一局面對[[麥斯·薛澤|麥斯·薛澤]]都擊出全壘打。隨後第3棒的蘭布再補上一支全壘打，達成難得一見的背靠背靠背全壘打的演出。2018年8月2日，他動了肩膀手術球季報銷。<ref>http://www.sportingnews.com/us/mlb/news/mlb-diamondbacks-jake-lamb-injury-update-out-for-season-with-shoulder-injury/e8h36nmjyr1014nflsnl13blg</ref>該季他打擊率為2成22，6轟31分打點。

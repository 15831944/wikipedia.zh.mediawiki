'''口服给药（{{lang-en|Oral administration}}）''' ('''''per os''''') 是一种[[给药途径|给药途径]]，通过[[嘴|嘴]]吞服[[药物|药物]]使其在体内发挥作用。许多药物都可以口服给药，因为其往往在全身产生作用，通过[[血流|血流]]输送到身体的各个部位。<ref name=IQWiG-oral>{{cite web|last=Institute for Quality and Efficiency in Health Care|title=Oral medications|url=http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmedhealth/PMH0005195/|work=Informed Health Online|publisher=Institute for Quality and Efficiency in Health Care|accessdate=22 June 2013}}</ref>

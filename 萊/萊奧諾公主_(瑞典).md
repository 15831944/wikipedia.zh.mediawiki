'''萊奥諾公主，哥特蘭女公爵殿下'''（'''''HRH'' Prinsessan Leonore, hertiginna av Gotland''', {{bd|2014年|2月20日|catIdx=L}}）是[[瑞典|瑞典]]公主及王室的成員之一。萊奥諾於2014年2月20日生於[[美國|美國]][[紐約|紐約]]威爾康奈爾醫療中心<ref>{{cite web|author=Reuters in Stockholm |url=http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/feb/21/sweden-princess-madeleine-gives-birth-daughter |title=Sweden's Princess Madeleine gives birth to daughter | World news |publisher=theguardian.com |date= |accessdate=2014-02-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.swedenabroad.com/en-GB/Embassies/Riga/Current-affairs/News/HRH-Princess-Madeleine-gave-birth-to-a-daughter-on-February-20-2014-sys/ |title=H.R.H. Princess Madeleine gave birth to a |publisher=SwedenAbroad |date= |accessdate=2014-02-21 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140225024644/http://www.swedenabroad.com/en-GB/Embassies/Riga/Current-affairs/News/HRH-Princess-Madeleine-gave-birth-to-a-daughter-on-February-20-2014-sys/ |archivedate=2014年2月25日 |df= }}</ref>，其父母先後在紐約及倫敦定居<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.upi.com/Entertainment_News/2014/02/21/Swedens-Princess-Madeleine-gives-birth-to-a-daughter/UPI-49731393000448/ |title=Princess Madeleine of Sweden gives birth to a daughter in NYC |publisher=UPI.com |date= |accessdate=2014-02-21}}</ref>。

2月26日，[[卡爾十六世·古斯塔夫|卡爾十六世·古斯塔夫]]國王會見內閣，公布公主的名字和封爵<ref>{{cite web|title=Princess Madeleine gives birth to baby girl|url=http://ca.hellomagazine.com/royalty/2014022117118/princess-madeleine-of-sweden-gives-birth/}}</ref><ref name="nametitle">{{cite press release|title=H.R.H. Princess Leonore Lilian Maria, Duchess of Gotland  |url=http://www.kungahuset.se/royalcourt/royalfamily/latestnews/latestnews/hrhprincessleonorelilianmariaduchessofgotland.5.19aaa1d3143e3582c02f90.html | publisher=[[Royal_Court_of_Sweden|Royal Court of Sweden]] | date=2014-02-26 |accessdate=2014-02-16}}</ref>，並宣布公主位列[[瑞典王位繼承|王位繼承順序]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Här är hon - Prinsessan Leonore|url=http://www.svt.se/nyheter/sverige/namnet-blir-offentligt}}</ref>。
要保持瑞典王位繼承權，她必須成為[[瑞典信義會|瑞典信義會]]成員，並在瑞典成長<ref>[https://lagen.nu/1810:0926 Swedish Constitution's Order of Succession], §4:"As § 2 of the 1809 Constitution Act expressly states that the King shall always be of the pure evangelical faith, such as in the unaltered Augsburg Confession, and the Uppsala Meeting decision of the year 1593 adopted and explained, thus shall also Princes and Princesses of the Royal House be brought up in the same faith and within the kingdom. Member of the Royal Family not professing this faith, would be from all rights of succession excluded.".</ref>。在美國紐約出生的萊奥諾公主透過美國籍的父親而擁有美國及瑞典[[雙重國籍|雙重國籍]]。<ref>[http://royalcentral.co.uk/europe/sweden/exclusive-swedish-royal-court-confirms-princess-leonores-citizenship-status-79362 "Exclusive: Swedish Royal Court confirms Princess Leonore’s citizenship status"]. Royal Central. 29 March, 2017.</ref>

2014年6月8日，公主在瑞典進行洗禮，她其中教父母是其阿姨[[維多利亞王儲|維多利亞王儲]]，塔基亚娜·达波，恩斯特·阿本斯佩格-特劳恩伯爵，帕特里克·索莫尔拉西，爱丽丝·巴姆弗德，路易丝·戈特利布。

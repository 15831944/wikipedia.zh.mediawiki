'''萊特肯尼'''（{{lang-en|Letterkenny}}，{{lang-ga|Leitir Ceanainn}}）是[[愛爾蘭共和國|愛爾蘭共和國]]的一座[[城市|城市]]，位於[[多尼戈爾郡|多尼戈爾郡]]，是多尼戈爾郡人口最多的城市。萊特肯尼有人口19,588人<ref name = "cso2011">{{cite web | title = Census 2011 Results – Profile 1 Town and Country – Population Distribution and Movements and Population by Area | work = Central Statistics Office Census 2011 Reports | publisher = [[Central_Statistics_Office_(Ireland)|Central Statistics Office Ireland]] |date=April 2012 | url = http://www.cso.ie/en/media/csoie/census/documents/census2011vol1andprofile1/Press%20release%20Census%202011%20Profile%201%20for%20website.pdf |format=PDF| accessdate = 24 June 2012}}</ref>，是愛爾蘭西北部的中心城市之一。

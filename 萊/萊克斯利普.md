'''萊克斯利普'''（{{lang-en|Leixlip}}，{{lang-ga|Léim an Bhradáin}}）是[[愛爾蘭共和國|愛爾蘭共和國]]的一座[[城市|城市]]，位於[[基爾代爾郡|基爾代爾郡]]東北部。萊克斯利普現在有人口15,452人<ref>{{cite web |url=http://census.cso.ie/sapmap2011/Results.aspx?Geog_Type=LT&Geog_Code=06010 |title=Leixlip Legal Town Results |publisher=[[Central_Statistics_Office_(Ireland)|Central Statistics Office]] |year=2011 }}</ref>，是基爾代爾郡的第四大城市，也是愛爾蘭的第23大城市<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cso.ie/en/media/csoie/census/documents/census2011vol1andprofile1/Tables,7,and,12.pdf |title=Census 2011, Table 7, Population of each town of 1,500 population and over |publisher=[[Central_Statistics_Office_(Ireland)|Central Statistics Office]] |year=2011}}</ref>。

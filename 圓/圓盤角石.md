'''圓盤角石'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Discitoceras'''''}}）是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[鸚鵡螺亞綱|鸚鵡螺類]]。牠們生活在[[深海|深海]][[藻|藻]][[礁|礁]]的周圍，游水能力很強，以觸手捕捉小動物為食。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author={{link-en|西里爾·沃克 (古生物學家)|Cyril Walker (palaeontologist)|Walker, Cyril}} | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=136 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
圓盤角石擁有一反捲的殼及寬口的核心和[[圓|圓]][[腹|腹]]，殼表有著緊密的環紋狀肋條，紋路和殼盤捲的邊線平行。<ref name="Fossils" />

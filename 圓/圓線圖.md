'''圓線圖'''（circle diagram）是由圖形方式表示[[电机|电机]]性能的方式，最早是由A. Heyland及B.A. Behrend在1894年、1895提出<ref name="Behrend (1921)">{{cite book|last=Behrend|first=B.A.|title=The Induction Motor and Other Alternating Current Motors, their Theory and Principles of Design|year=1921|publisher=McGraw-Hill|page=ix|url=http://openlibrary.org/books/OL7033483M/The_induction_motor_and_other_alternating_current_motors_their_theory_and_principles_of_design|accessdate=4 January 2013}}</ref><ref name="Heyland (1894)">{{cite web|last=Heyland|first=A.|title=A Graphical Method for the Prediction of Power Transformers and Polyphase Motors)|journal=ETZ|year=1894|volume=15|pages=561–564|url=http://translate.googleusercontent.com/translate_c?depth=1&hl=en&rurl=translate.google.ca&sl=de&tl=en&u=http://www.regione.taa.it/GIUNTA/enel/Heilbronnertr_td.pdf&usg=ALkJrhjSZbhRQLilTGDiDl0ISV9eO9k-tQ|accessdate=4 January 2013}}</ref><ref name="circle">{{cite journal|title=The General Circle Diagram of Electrical Machinery|publisher=American Institute of Electrical Engineers, Transactions of the|author1=Terman, Frederick Emmons |author2=Freedman, Cecil Louis |author3=Lenzen, Theodore Louis |author4=Rogers, Kenneth Alfred |date=Jan 1930|volume=49}}</ref>。是由電機輸入電壓及電流的軌跡來繪製<ref>{{cite book|title=Electrical Machines|publisher=Tata McGraw-Hill Education|page=359|edition=2008|author=S.K.Bhattacharya|ISBN=0-07-066921-X}}</ref>。圓線圖可以用來表示[[交流發電機|交流發電機]]、[[同步馬達|同步馬達]]、[[變壓器|變壓器]]及[[感應馬達|感應馬達]]的性能。'''Heyland圖'''是圓線圖應用在感應馬達上的近似表示法，其中假設定子輸入電壓、轉子電阻及轉子電抗為定值，定子電阻及鐵損為零<ref name="circle"/><ref name="Heyland (1906)">{{cite book|last=Heyland|first=Alexander|title=A Graphical Treatment of the Induction Motor|year=1906|publisher=McGraw Publishing Company|url=https://archive.org/stream/graphicaltreatme00heylrich#page/n3/mode/2up |others=G.H. Rowe, R.E. Hellmund (trans.) |accessdate=10 January 2013}}</ref><ref name="Ph-to-Ph (2006)">{{cite web|last=Phase to Phase BV|title=The Asynchronous Motor Model|url=http://www.phasetophase.nl/pdf/Asynchronous_machine_model.pdf|accessdate=10 January 2013|year=2006|pages=5–6|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140810132604/http://www.phasetophase.nl/pdf/Asynchronous_machine_model.pdf|archivedate=2014年8月10日|df=}}</ref>。另一種常見的圓線圖是針對固定氣隙的感應馬達，描述如下<!--Another common circle diagram form is as described in the two constant air-gap induction motor images shown here,--><ref name="Knowlton (1949)">{{cite conference|booktitle=Standard Handbook for Electrical Engineers|edition=8th|editor-last=A.E. 其中
* R<sub>s</sub>, X<sub>s</sub>：定子電阻及定子漏電抗
* R<sub>r</sub><sup>'</sup>, X<sub>r</sub><sup>'</sup>, s：定子電阻及定子漏電抗、轉子滑差
* R<sub>c</sub>, X<sub>m</sub>,：鐵損及機械損，磁化電抗
* V<sub>s</sub>：定子電壓
* I<sub>0</sub> = OO<sup>'</sup>, I<sub>BL</sub> = OA, I<sub>1</sub> =OV：無載電流、堵轉電流，工作作電流
* Φ<sub>0</sub>, Φ<sub>BL</sub>：無載功因角，堵轉功因角
* P<sub>max</sub>,  s<sub>Pmax</sub>, PF<sub>max</sub>, T<sub>max</sub>, s<sub>Tmax</sub>：最大輸出功率及對應氣隙，最大功因、最大轉矩及對應氣隙
* η<sub>1</sub>, s<sub>1</sub>, PF<sub>1</sub>, Φ<sub>1</sub>,：工作電流下的效率、滑差、功因及功因角
* AB：表示轉子功率輸入，除以同步轉速後為啟動轉矩

圓線圖是根據[[開路測試|開路測試]]以及變壓器的[[短路測試|短路測試]]或是[[馬達堵轉測試|馬達堵轉測試]]所得，會得到一個符合O<sup>'</sup>和A點的半圓。

圓線圖因為假設氣隙為定值，因此會引入誤差，其他的誤差有因為磁飽和對轉子磁阻及電抗的影響，以及從無載到工作速度範圍之間的轉子速度變化，對轉子磁阻及電抗的影響

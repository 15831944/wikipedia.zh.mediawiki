'''促甲状腺激素受体'''（{{lang-en|thyrotropin receptor}}，'''TSH receptor'''，'''TSHR'''）是一种接受[[促甲状腺激素|促甲状腺激素]]（thyrotropin）信号，刺激细胞生成[[甲状腺激素|甲状腺激素]]（T4）和[[三碘甲状腺激素|三碘甲状腺激素]]（T3）的跨膜受体。TSHR是{{link-en|膜内在蛋白|Integral membrane protein}}中[[G蛋白偶联受体|G蛋白偶联受体]]超家族的一员<ref name="pmid15231707">{{cite journal | author = Farid NR, Szkudlinski MW | title = Minireview: structural and functional evolution of the thyrotropin receptor | journal = Endocrinology | volume = 145 | issue = 9 | pages = 4048–57 | year = 2004 | pmid = 15231707 | doi = 10.1210/en.2004-0437 }}</ref>，与{{le|Gs蛋白|Gs alpha subunit}}相连接<ref name="pmid20378719">{{cite journal | author = Calebiro D, Nikolaev VO, Lohse MJ | title = Imaging of persistent cAMP signaling by internalized G protein-coupled receptors | journal = J. Mol. Endocrinol. | volume = 45 | issue = 1 | pages = 1–8 |date=July 2010 | pmid = 20378719 | doi = 10.1677/JME-10-0014 | url =  }}</ref>。

该受体主要出现在{{le|甲状腺上皮细胞|thyroid epithelial cell}}的表面。

==功能==
Upon binding circulating [[thyrotropin|thyrotropin]], a G-protein signal cascade activates adenylyl cyclase and intercellular levels of cAMP rise. cAMP activates all functional aspects of the thyroid cell, including iodine pumping; [[thyroglobulin|thyroglobulin]] synthesis, iodination, endocytosis and proteolysis; thyroid peroxidase activity; and hormone release.

'''次等人類'''，也稱'''低等人'''<ref>{{cite book||publisher=秀威出版|title=反恐戰略與文明衝突|pp=44|year=2008|accessdate=2017-07-20|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=VS9yBPl6KkgC|author=俞力工|isbn=9789866732751}}</ref>或'''下等人'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dw.com/zh/%E6%BB%91%E7%A8%BD%E5%B8%8C%E7%89%B9%E5%8B%92%E4%BB%96%E5%9B%9E%E4%BE%86%E4%BA%86/a-16629949?&zhongwen=simp|title=滑稽希特勒：《他回来了》|author=乐然|publisher=德新社|website=德国之声|date=2013-02-26|accessdate=2017-07-20}}</ref>（{{lang-de|Untermensch}}，{{IPA-de|ˈʔʊntɐˌmɛnʃ}}；{{lang-en|Subhuman}}）是德語中的一個詞，因[[納粹德國|納粹德國]]使用其形容非雅利安之低下種族而成爲貶義。這一詞匯通常被用於概括納粹口中的「東方民族」（{{lang|en|the masses from the East}}），包含[[猶太人|猶太人]]、[[羅姆人|羅姆人]]、[[斯拉夫人|斯拉夫人]]（主要涉及[[波蘭人|波蘭人]]、[[塞爾維亞人|塞爾維亞人]]、與戰爭後期的[[俄羅斯人|俄羅斯人]]）<ref>Revisiting the National Socialist Legacy: Coming to Terms With Forced Labor, Expropriation, Compensation, and Restitution page 84 Oliver Rathkolb</ref><ref name="Gumkowski">{{cite book |url=http://www.dac.neu.edu/holocaust/Hitlers_Plans.htm |title=Hitler's Plans for Eastern Europe |first1=Janusz |last1=Gumkowski |first2=Kazimierz |last2=Leszczynski |first3=Edward (translator) |last3=Robert |work=Poland Under Nazi Occupation |format=Paperback |pages=219 |publisher=Polonia Pub. House |edition=First |year=1961 |asin=B0006BXJZ6 |accessdate=12 March 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110409034704/http://www.dac.neu.edu/holocaust/Hitlers_Plans.htm |archivedate=2011年4月9日 }} at [[Wayback_machine|Wayback machine]].</ref>。除極少數例外情況，該術語也被用作形容許多[[黑人|黑人]]與[[有色人種|有色人種]]成員<ref name=pamphlet1942>{{cite book |url=http://www.holocaustresearchproject.org/holoprelude/deruntermensch.html |title=Der Untermensch "The subhuman" |author=Reichsführer-SS |publisher=SS Office |location=Berlin |year=1942 |accessdate=12 March 2014}}</ref>。與此同時，納粹認爲猶太人亟待通過[[最終解決方案|最終解決方案]]在[[猶太人大屠殺|大屠殺]]中被完全滅絕<ref>Snyder, T (2011) Bloodlands, Europe between Hitler and Stalin, Vintage, P144-5, 188</ref>， 而羅姆人、{{le|身體殘疾|Physical disability|身體殘疾者}}及{{le|發育性殘疾|Developmental disability|發育性殘疾者}}也均需要被[[種族滅絕|徹底清除]]<ref>Mineau, André (2004).

'''順性別'''（{{lang-en|Cisgender}}），是一個用於[[性別認同|性別認同]]符合他們出生時的[[性別指定|性別指定]]的人。順性別也可以被定義為那些具有“[[性別認同|性別認同]]或執行的[[性別角色|性別角色]]是社會認為與他們[[生物性別|生物性別]]相符”的人。<ref>Crethar, H. C. & Vargas, L. A. (2007). ''Multicultural intricacies in professional counseling.'' In J. Gregoire & C. Jungers (Eds.), The counselor's companion: What every beginning counselor needs to know. Mahwah, NJ: Lawrence Erlbaum. {{ISBN|0-8058-5684-6}}. p. 59.</ref> 這與[[跨性別|跨性別]]這個詞相反。<ref name="Schilt">{{Cite journal|last1=Schilt |last2=Westbrook|first1=Kristen|first2=Laurel |title= Doing Gender, Doing Heteronormativity: 'Gender Normals,' Transgender People, and the Social Maintenance of Heterosexuality|journal=[[Gender_&_Society|Gender & Society]]|volume=23|issue=4|pages=440–464 [461]|doi =10.1177/0891243209340034|date = August 2009}}</ref>有批評指順性別的用詞太過[[政治正確|政治正確]]，因為絕大部分的人們的[[性别指定|性别指定]]及社會性別均是天生及相同，無需特別強調有關用辭。

== 詞源和術語 ==
顺性别一词与[[跨性別|跨性別]]相对。由顺性别的概念还衍生出其他的术语，包括：顺性女（Cis Female或Cis Woman），即出生时的[[性别指定|性别指定]]是女性，自己也认为自己是女性；顺性男（Cis Male或Cis Man），即出生时的[[性别指定|性别指定]]是男性，自己也认为自己是男性。以顺性别为标准看待社会的世界观被称作“[[顺性别主义|顺性别主义]]”（cissexism）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://geneq.berkeley.edu/lgbt_resources_definiton_of_terms#cisgender|title=Definition of Terms|work=Gender Equality Source Center of UC Berkeley|accessdate=2015-08-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thedailybeast.com/articles/2014/02/15/the-complete-glossary-of-facebook-s-51-gender-options.html|title=What Each of Facebook’s 51 New Gender Options Means|work=The Daily Beast|date=2014-02-15|accessdate=2015-08-04}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ncgs.org/Pdfs/Transgender/Educational/TransNeedsAssessmentReport.pdf|title=Trans Needs Assessment Report|work=Canadian AIDS Society|accessdate=2015-08-04|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923163119/http://www.ncgs.org/Pdfs/Transgender/Educational/TransNeedsAssessmentReport.pdf|archivedate=2015年9月23日|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.guokr.com/article/438003/|title=Facebook的56个非传统性别，都是啥意思？|work=果壳网|date=2014-02-15|accessdate=2015-08-04}}</ref>順性別也认为自身的性別認同絕不属于[[多元性别|多元性别]]。<ref>Crethar, H. == 批評 ==

=== 從女性主義和性別研究 ===
Krista Scott-Dixon在2009年寫道：“我更喜歡non-trans這個詞，以及其他選擇，如cissexual / cisgendered。”<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Scott-Dixon|first=Krista|authorlink=Krista Scott-Dixon|title=Public health, private parts: A feminist public-health approach to trans issues|journal=Hypatia|doi=10.1111/j.1527-2001.2009.01044.x|volume=24|issue=3|pages=33–55|year=2009}}</ref>她認同這個觀點是因為她認為術語“non-trans”更加清晰，幫助使跨性別個體正常化。

女性和性別研究學者Mimi Marinucci寫道，有些人認為“跨-順性別”與“男性 - 女性”性別二元組一樣危險或自欺欺人，因為它與異性戀人群相比，將任意識別為女同性戀，男同性戀或雙性戀（LGB）的人混為一談，與跨性別人群相比則過於簡單化。將LGB個體與異性戀表徵可能存在問題，非跨性別人士可能認為LGB個體與跨性別個體不同，“在他們自己的性別認同和性別表達與文化對他們的看法之間沒有任何的不相容”。<ref>{{cite book|last=Marinucci|first=Mimi|publisher=Zed Books|year=2010|title=Feminism is Queer: The Intimate Connection between Queer and Feminist Theory|pages=125–126}}</ref>

Glosswitch在英國雜誌新政治家中寫道，如果一個基本的性別二元性不存在，那麼一個人的性別認同與他們的出生性別相符的想法就是保持一種刻板印象。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.newstatesman.com/lifestyle/2014/02/i-dont-feel-i-match-my-gender-so-what-does-it-mean-be-called-cis|title=I don't feel I "match" my gender, so what does it mean to be called cis?|website=www.newstatesman.com|access-date=2016-09-09}}</ref>

=== 來自雙性人組織 ===
{{See also|雙性人}}
雙性人的出生時具有非典型的生物性別特徵，這可能會使出生性別指定複雜化並導致不自主或強制的醫療。<ref name="Dreger">{{cite book |last=Domurat Dreger |first=Alice |title=Hermaphrodites and the Medical Invention of Sex |year=2001 |publisher=Harvard University Press |location=USA |isbn=0-674-00189-3 }}</ref><ref name="jointun">[http://www.who.int/reproductivehealth/publications/gender_rights/eliminating-forced-sterilization/en/ Eliminating forced, coercive and otherwise involuntary sterilization, An interagency statement], [[World_Health_Organization|World Health Organization]], May 2014.</ref>根據Interex Youth Inter / Youth計劃的Interact倡導者的說法，cisgender這個術語可能會讓雙性人特徵的人感到困惑。<ref>[http://interactyouth.org/post/97343969730/inter-act-has-been-working-with-mtvs-faking-it-on Inter/Act Youth • Inter/Act has been working with MTV’s Faking It on...] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140916025255/http://interactyouth.org/post/97343969730/inter-act-has-been-working-with-mtvs-faking-it-on |date=September 16, 2014 }} [[Interact_Advocates_for_Intersex_Youth|Inter/Act Youth]]. Retrieved October 17, 2014.</ref> “雙性人平等運動”的Hida Viloria指出，“出生時的性別指定”一詞在順性別者的定義中被使用，卻沒有註意到嬰兒在世界大多數地區都被指定為男性或女性，而不考慮世界上的雙性人地位，並且指出這樣做無視了雙性人的誕生並在二元男性/女性性別基礎中構建了性別認同。這種定義未能說明某些天生雙性人的性別認同非二元的存在，以及基於二元性別對雙性人的歧視。說明其為產後性別特徵而不是性別認同或表達，例如“正常化”嬰兒生殖器手術。<ref>[http://hidaviloria.com/caught-in-the-gender-binary-blind-spot-intersex-erasure-in-cisgender-rhetoric/ Caught in the Gender Binary Blind Spot: Intersex Erasure in Cisgender Rhetoric], [[Hida_Viloria|Hida Viloria]], August 18, 2014. Retrieved October 17, 2014.</ref><ref>[http://oii.org.au/21336/intersex-for-allies/ Intersex for allies], [[Organisation_Intersex_International_Australia|Organisation Intersex International Australia]], October 8, 2014. Retrieved October 17, 2014.</ref>組織Intersex International Australia認為，儘管大多數雙性人不是跨性別人士，但由於雙性人的經驗或經歷“非自願醫療治療以強加刻板性別特徵”的風險，因此該術語存在問題。<ref>[http://oii.org.au/21336/intersex-for-allies/ Intersex for allies], [[Organisation_Intersex_International_Australia|Organisation Intersex International Australia]], October 8, 2014. Retrieved October 17, 2014.</ref>雙性人教授已經提出使用術語“ipso性別”而不是順性別來表示同意其醫學性別指定的雙性人。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Sandeen|first1=Autumn|title=Ipso gender: A third term for intersex people|url=http://lgbtweekly.com/2014/09/18/ipso-gender-a-third-term-for-intersex-people/|website=LGBT Weekly|accessdate=April 28, 2016|date=September 18, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171219115824/http://lgbtweekly.com/2014/09/18/ipso-gender-a-third-term-for-intersex-people/|archive-date=2017-12-19|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

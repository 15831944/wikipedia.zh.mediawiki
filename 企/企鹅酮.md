'''企鹅酮'''（{{lang-en|Penguinone}}），[[分子式|分子式]]为C<sub>10</sub>H<sub>14</sub>O，是一种[[结构式|结构式]]的形状类似于[[企鹅|企鹅]]的[[有机物|有机物]]<ref>{{cite book|author=May, Paul W.|year=2008|title=Molecules with Silly or Unusual Names|publisher=Imperial College Press|location=London|page=35|isbn=1848162073}}; {{cite web|author=May, Paul W.|date=1997–2010|url=http://www.chm.bris.ac.uk/sillymolecules/sillymols.htm|title=Molecules with Silly or Unusual Names|publisher=University of Bristol|accessdate=2011-12-10}}</ref>。

Penguinone这个[[混成词|混成词]]由penguin（企鹅）和后缀-one（[[酮|酮]]）所组成<ref>{{cite journal|author=Laszlo, Pierre|year=2004|title=Science as Play|journal=American Scientist|volume=92|issue=5|pages=398|doi=10.1511/2004.5.398|url=http://www.americanscientist.org/issues/pub/science-as-play/3}}</ref>。

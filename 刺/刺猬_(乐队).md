'''刺猬'''（{{lang-en|Hedgehog}}）是2005年在中国北京成立的乐队<ref name="about the band">{{Cite web|url=http://hedgehogrock.com/article/6/|title=About the band|accessdate=2012-01-10}}</ref>。他们的音乐风格受到了[[:en:The_Jesus_and_Mary_Chain|耶稣和玛丽链]]、[[雷蒙斯|雷蒙斯]]和[[涅槃乐队|涅槃]]的影响<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rockinchina.com/w/Interview_with_Hedgehog|title=Interview with Hedgehog|accessdate=4 January 2012|author=Azchael|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924091715/http://www.rockinchina.com/w/Interview_with_Hedgehog|archive-date=2015-09-24|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。乐队鼓手阿童木（石璐）以其娇小的外貌和狂躁的打鼓风格且其偶尔同时作为鼓手和主唱，成为乐队的焦点之一<ref name="Newby">{{Cite web|url=http://www.timeoutbeijing.com/features/Music/11484/Preview-Hedgehog-%28indie-rock%29.html|title=Preview: Hedgehog (indie rock)|accessdate=16 February 2012|author=Newby|first=Jake|publisher=Time Out Group Ltd}}</ref><ref name="drownedinsound.com">{{Cite web|url=http://drownedinsound.com/in_depth/4147269-a-musical-revolution--the-china-wave|title=A Musical Revolution: The China Wave / In Depth // Drowned In Sound|accessdate=2014-08-22|author=Gourlay|date=2013-12-09|first=Dom|publisher=Drownedinsound.com}}</ref>，其艺名来源于[[铁臂阿童木|铁壁阿童木]]。

2011年，乐队与[[:en:Xiu_Xiu|Xiu-Xiu]]一起在美国演出，同年在纽约录制了第一张在国外录制的录音室专辑《阳光、欢乐、枪》（Sun Fun Gun）<ref name="about the band">{{Cite web|url=http://hedgehogrock.com/article/6/|title=About the band|accessdate=2012-01-10}}</ref>，并于2012年在中国发行专辑。

'''水迷宫'''（water maze），是一种用来测验动物记忆的实验设备，其中的路径充满了水，使动物具有逃脱的[[动机|动机]] 。<ref name="Schenk">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.pn/books?lr=&id=gGACD7imeroC&pg=PA145|title=Handbook of Spatial Research Paradigms and Methodologies|last=Schenk|first=Françoise|publisher=[[Psychology_Press|Psychology Press]]|year=1998|isbn=978-0-86377-807-0|editor-last=Foreman|editor-first=Nigel|editor2-last=Gillett|editor2-first=Raphael|volume=Volume 2: Clinical and Comparative Studies|location=East Sussex, United Kingdom|pages=145–188|chapter=5: The Morris Water Maze (is not a maze)}}</ref>

存在许多不同的水迷宫，如{{link-en|T-迷宮|T-maze|T-和Y-迷宫}}、[[辛辛那提水迷宫|辛辛那提水迷宫]]和[[放射臂迷宫|放射臂迷宫]]。<ref name="Abeelen">{{Cite journal|title=Inheritance of discrimination learning ability and retention in BA and DBA mice|date=March 1981|journal=[[Behavior_Genetics_(journal)|Behavior Genetics]]|issue=2|doi=10.1007/BF01065628|volume=11|pages=173–177|pmid=7271683}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|title=Cincinnati water maze: A review of the development, methods, and evidence as a test of egocentric learning and memory|last=Vorhees|first=Charles V.|last2=Williams|first2=Michael T.|journal=[[Neurotoxicology_and_Teratology|Neurotoxicology and Teratology]]|doi=10.1016/j.ntt.2016.08.002|volume=57|pages=1–19|pmc=5056837|pmid=27545092}}</ref><ref name="Hyde">{{Cite journal|title=Water version of the radial-arm maze: learning in three inbred strains of mice|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0006-8993(97)01417-0|date=March 1998|journal=[[Brain_Research|Brain Research]]|accessdate=2014-05-23|issue=2|doi=10.1016/S0006-8993(97)01417-0|volume=785|pages=236–244|pmid=9518631}}</ref>水迷宫被用来测试辨别学习和空间学习能力。[[莫里斯水航行任务|莫里斯水航行任务]]通常也被称为一个“水迷宫任务”，但这是错误的，因为它严格地说，并不是一个迷宫。这些迷宫辅助研究的发展，例如海马突触可塑性、[[NMDA受体|NMDA受体]]功能和研究神经退化性疾病，如[[阿尔茨海默氏症|阿尔茨海默氏症]]。

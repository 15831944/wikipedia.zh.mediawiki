'''豪萨托尼河'''（{{lang-en|Housatonic River}}，{{IPAc-en|ˌ|h|uː|s|ə|ˈ|t|ɒ|n|ɪ|k}}）是美国东北部的一条河流，长约{{Convert|149|mi}}<ref name="Plan">{{cite report |title=Housatonic River Basin Final Natural Resources Restoration Plan, Environmental Assessment, and Environmental Impact Evaluation for Connecticut |date=July 2009|publisher=State of Connecticut Department of Environmental Protection, USFWS, NOAA |url=http://www.ct.gov/deep/lib/deep/natural_resources/housatonic/2013_august_housatonic_ct_final_amendment_to_the_rpea.pdf |accessdate=2014-11-09 }}</ref><ref name="NHD">U.S. Geological Survey. National Hydrography Dataset high-resolution flowline data. [http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/ The National Map]   {{Webarchive|url=https://www.webcitation.org/66gupqQDM?url=http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/|date=2012-04-05}}, accessed April 1, 2011</ref>，位于[[麻薩諸塞州|麻薩諸塞州]]的西部和[[康乃狄克州|康乃狄克州]]的西部。其流域面积约{{Convert|1950|sqmi|km2}}，覆盖了[[康乃狄克州|康乃狄克州]]西南部，最终流向东南偏南进入[[長島海灣|長島海灣]]。其东侧即为[[康乃狄克河|康乃狄克河]][[流域|流域]]。生活在这一地区的鸟类和鱼类会富集大量[[多氯联苯|多氯联苯]]而带有健康风险。<ref name="www2.epa.gov">{{cite web|url=http://www2.epa.gov/ge-housatonic/rest-river-ge-pittsfieldhousatonic-river-site|title=Rest of River of the GE-Pittsfield/Housatonic River Site|accessdate=25 September 2015}}</ref><ref name="EPA2">{{Cite web|url=http://www2.epa.gov/ge-housatonic/understanding-pcb-risks-ge-pittsfieldhousatonic-river-site|title=Understanding PCB Risks at the GE-Pittsfield/Housatonic River Site|accessdate=27 October 2015|publisher=EPA}}</ref><ref name="MDPH">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mass.gov/eohhs/docs/dph/environmental/investigations/housatonic-river-report-2011.pdf|title=General State-wide Advice for Eating Recreationally Caught Fish and Waterfowl In Massachusetts|accessdate=28 October 2015|publisher=MDPH}}</ref>

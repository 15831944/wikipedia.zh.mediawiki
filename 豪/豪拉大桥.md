'''豪拉大桥'''（Howrah Bridge）是[[印度|印度]]一座跨越[[胡格利河|胡格利河]]的悬臂桥，长705米<ref name = "roadtraf">{{cite web|url=http://www.roadtraffic-technology.com/projects/howrahbridge/|title=Howrah Bridge|accessdate=2011-11-21}}</ref>
，位于[[西孟加拉邦|西孟加拉邦]]的[[豪拉|豪拉]]与[[加尔各答|加尔各答]]两市之间，建于1936年到1942年，1943年2月3日开放<ref name="history">{{cite web|url=http://www.howrahbridgekolkata.gov.in/History_Middle.htm|title=History of the Howrah Bridge|date=|accessdate=2011-11-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130413124649/http://www.howrahbridgekolkata.gov.in/History_Middle.htm|archive-date=2013-04-13|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name="holiday"/>。1965年6月14日该桥更名为'''Rabindra Setu'''，得名于印度首位[[诺贝尔文学奖|诺贝尔文学奖得主]]、孟加拉大诗人[[泰戈尔|泰戈尔]]<ref name="holiday">{{cite web|url=http://www.holidayiq.com/howrah-bridge.php |title=Howrah Bridge – The Bridge without Nuts & Bolts!|date= |accessdate=2011-11-21}}</ref>。但仍普遍称为'''豪拉大桥'''。

豪拉大桥是[[胡格利河|胡格利河]]上的四座桥梁之一，[[加尔各答|加尔各答]]的标志之一。每天的交通流量达到约10万辆，<ref name='traffic'>{{cite web|url=http://www.howrahbridgekolkata.gov.in/Traffic%20Flow.xls|title=Flow of Traffic on an average week day (8AM to 8 PM)|accessdate=2011-11-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110818021858/http://howrahbridgekolkata.gov.in/Traffic%20Flow.xls|archive-date=2011-08-18|dead-url=yes}}</ref> 以及可能超过15万行人<ref name='drop'>{{cite news|url= http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2003-05-29/kolkata/27281185_1_howrah-bridge-kopt-bird-droppings|title=Bird droppings gnaw at Howrah bridge frame|accessdate=2011-11-21 | work=The Times Of India|date=2003-05-29}}</ref>，使之成为世界上最繁忙的悬臂桥，<ref name="hosanna">{{cite web|url=http://www.howrahbridgekolkata.gov.in/Hosanna.htm|title=Hosanna to Howrah Bridge!|date=|accessdate=2011-11-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111117054744/http://howrahbridgekolkata.gov.in/Hosanna.htm|archive-date=2011-11-17|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name="wikimapia">{{cite web|url=http://wikimapia.org/7033110/Howrah-Bridge-Rabindra-setu|title=Howrah Bridge|date= |accessdate=2011-11-21}}</ref>在其建造时为世界第三长的悬臂桥<ref name='viktor'>{{cite book | author = Victor D. Johnson | title = Essentials Of Bridge Engineering  | year = 2007| publisher = Oxford & Ibh Publishing Co. Pvt Ltd | page = 259}}</ref>，目前是世界第六长的同类型的桥梁<ref name="NSBA-longbridge">{{citation |url=http://www.aisc.org/Content/ContentGroups/Documents/NSBA5/20_NSBA_LongestSpans.PDF |title=National Steel Bridge Alliance: World's Longest Bridge Spans |last=Durkee |first=Jackson |publisher=American Institute of Steel Construction, Inc |date=1999-05-24 |accessdate=2009-01-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081230152332/http://www.aisc.org/Content/ContentGroups/Documents/NSBA5/20_NSBA_LongestSpans.PDF |archivedate=2008-12-30 }}</ref>。

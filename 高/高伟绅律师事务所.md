'''高伟绅律师事务所'''（{{lang-en|Clifford Chance}}）是一家总部设在英国伦敦的[[律师事务所|律师事务所]]，也是[[魔术圈|魔术圈]]成员之一。<ref name=busweek/> 该公司的律师数目和收入都位列全球前五。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.law.com/jsp/tal/PubArticleTAL.jsp?id=1202433980888|title=The Global 100: Most Revenue 2009|accessdate=13 August 2010|publisher=American Lawyer}}</ref> 2016/17年该所总收入15.4亿[[英镑|英镑]]，每个合伙人利润137.5万英镑。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thelawyer.com/clifford-chance-back-in-%C2%A31m-pep-club-as-revenues-nudge-up/1008491.article|title=Clifford Chance back in £1m PEP club as revenues nudge up|accessdate=17 July 2011|publisher=The Lawyer| date=5 July 2011}}</ref>
David Childs从2006年开始担任管理合伙人。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://business.timesonline.co.uk/tol/business/law/article3681363.ece|title=David Childs|accessdate=28 August 2010|publisher=Times Online| date=21 April 2008}}</ref>

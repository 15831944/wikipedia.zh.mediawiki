'''高里·蘭凱什'''<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ftchinese.com/story/001074156|title=批评印度教民族主义的女记者遭枪杀|author=艾米·卡兹明|translator=何黎|publisher=《[[金融时报|金融时报]]》|date=2017-09-06|accessdate=2017-09-06}}</ref>（{{lang-kn|ಗೌರಿ ಲಂಕೇಶ್}}，{{lang-en|Gauri Lankesh}}；{{bd|1962年|1月29日|2017年|9月5日}}），生於[[印度|印度]][[卡納塔克邦|卡納塔克邦]][[班加羅爾|班加羅爾]]，是一名[[新聞從業員|新聞從業者]]出身的[[行動主義|活動家]]。作爲社會與政治領域的評論者，蘭凱什的作品和她對言論自由與{{le|新聞道德與標準|Journalism ethics and standards|新聞道德}}的畢生支持使其聞名於世。

==生平==
高里·蘭凱什曾在由其父親{{le|P·蘭凱什|P. Lankesh}}創立的[[卡納達語|卡納達語]]周刊《{{le|Lankesh Patrike}}》擔任編輯，也經營有自己的刊物《{{lang|en|Gauri Lankesh Patrika}}》。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/india-news/gauri-lankesh-a-journalist-known-for-anti-establishment-pro-dalit-stand/story-gOa7zJ7Ces2Fv0ZlFJNOTN.html |title=Gauri Lankesh, a journalist known for anti-establishment, pro-Dalit stand |date=5 September 2017 |newspaper=[[Hindustan_Times|Hindustan Times]] |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906000950/http://www.hindustantimes.com/india-news/gauri-lankesh-a-journalist-known-for-anti-establishment-pro-dalit-stand/story-gOa7zJ7Ces2Fv0ZlFJNOTN.html |archivedate=6 September 2017 |df= }}</ref>

2017年9月5日，蘭凱什在其位於班加羅爾城「{{lang|en|Rajarajeshwari Nagar}}」地區的居所遭到致命槍擊，凶手身份未知、動機不明。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/bengaluru/noted-journalist-gauri-lankesh-shot-dead-at-her-house/articleshow/60380886.cms |title=Senior journalist Gauri Lankesh shot dead at her house |author=Rajiv Kalkod |newspaper=[[The_Times_of_India|The Times of India]] |date=5 September 2017 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906000949/http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/bengaluru/noted-journalist-gauri-lankesh-shot-dead-at-her-house/articleshow/60380886.cms |archivedate=6 September 2017 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Senior journalist Gauri Lankesh shot dead at her Bengaluru home, kin demands CBI probe|url=http://indiatoday.intoday.in/story/journalist-gauri-lankesh-shot-dead-bengaluru/1/1041572.html|accessdate=5 September 2017|work=India Today|date=5 September 2017|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906000951/http://indiatoday.intoday.in/story/journalist-gauri-lankesh-shot-dead-bengaluru/1/1041572.html|archivedate=6 September 2017|df=}}</ref>

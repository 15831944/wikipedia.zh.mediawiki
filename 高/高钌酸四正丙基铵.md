'''高钌酸四正丙基铵'''（缩写'''TPAP'''或'''TPAPR'''）是一种[[化合物|化合物]]，化学式为N(C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>7</sub>)<sub>4</sub>RuO<sub>4</sub>，有时被称作Ley–Griffith试剂，用于有机合成。它是离子化合物，氧化性很强，但是其单电子还原的衍生物是用于将[[醇|醇]]转化为[[醛|醛]]的温和氧化剂。<ref>{{cite journal |first1= Steven V. |last1= Ley |authorlink1= Steven V. Ley|first2= Joanne |last2= Norman |first3= William P. |last3= Griffith |first4= Stephen P. |last4= Marsden |title= Tetrapropylammonium perruthenate, Pr<sub>4</sub>N<sup>+</sup>RuO<sub>4</sub><sup>−</sup>, TPAP: A catalytic oxidant for organic synthesis |journal= [[Synthesis_(journal)|Synthesis]] |year= 1994 |pages= 639–666 |doi= 10.1055/s-1994-25538}} (review article)</ref>该氧化剂也可用于将[[伯醇|伯醇]]直接氧化至[[羧酸|羧酸]]。使用较高的催化剂负载量，较大量的助氧化剂，以及两当量的水。在这种情况下，[[醛|醛]]与水反应形成[[偕二醇|偕二醇]]水合物，然后再次被氧化。<ref>{{cite journal |title= Applications of Zr-catalyzed carbomagnesation and Mo-catalyzed macrocyclic ring closing metathesis in asymmetric synthesis. Enantioselective total synthesis of Sch 38516 (Fluvirucin B<sub>1</sub>) |first1= Z. |last1= Xu |first2= C. W. |last2= Johannes |first3= A. F. |last3= Houri |first4= D. S. |last4= La |first5=D. A. |last5= Cogan |first6= G. E. |last6= Hofilena |first7= A. H. |last7= Hoveyda |journal= [[J._Am._Chem._Soc.|J. Am. Chem. Soc.]] | year= 1997 |volume= 119 |pages= 10302–10316 |doi= 10.1021/ja972191k }}</ref>

氧化反应产生水，可以通过分子筛去除。TPAP价格昂贵，但它可以以催化量使用。通过加入化学计量比的助氧化剂（如[[N-甲基吗啉-N-氧化物|N-甲基吗啉-N-氧化物]]）<ref>{{cite journal |title= Preparation and use of tetra-''n''-butylammonium per-ruthenate (TBAP reagent) and tetra-''n''-propylammonium per-ruthenate (TPAP reagent) as new catalytic oxidants for alcohols |first1=  William P. |last1 = Griffith |first2= Steven V. |last2= Ley |authorlink2= Steven V. Ley |first3= Gwynne P. |last3= Whitcombe |first4= Andrew D. |last4= White |journal = [[J._Chem._Soc.,_Chem._Commun.|J. Chem. Soc., Chem. Commun.]] |year= 1987 |pages= 1625-1627 |doi= 10.1039/C39870001625}}</ref>或[[氧气|氧气]]<ref>{{cite journal |title= Tetra-''n''-propylammonium perruthenate (TPAP)-catalysed oxidations of alcohols using molecular oxygen as a co-oxidant |first1= Roman |last1= Lenz |first2= Steven V. |last2= Ley |authorlink2= Steven V. Ley |journal= [[J._Chem._Soc.,_Perkin_Trans._1|J. Chem. Soc., Perkin Trans. 1]]|year= 1997 |pages= 3291–3292 |doi= 10.1039/A707339I}}</ref>来维持催化循环。

'''高突足襞蛞蝓'''（学名：{{lang|la|'''''Laevicaulis alte'''''}}）为[[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[有肺類|有肺類]][[足襞蛞蝓科|足襞蛞蝓科]][[襞蛞蝓属|襞蛞蝓属]]的动物。分布于[[日本|日本]]、[[印度|印度]]、马来群岛以及东南亚各国、[[台湾|台湾]]以及[[中国|中国]]的[[广东|广东]]、[[海南|海南]]、[[广西|广西]]、[[香港|香港]]、[[澳门|澳门]]等地，生活环境为陆地，多生活于山区、丘陵地带灌木丛、草丛中、公园、住宅附近乱石堆裡、石块下以及喜潮湿、多腐殖质的环境。<ref name='id11144'/>

==簡述==
本物種是一種有着圓圓、深色的身體，沒有外殼的蛞蝓。長約7到8厘米。它的皮膚有輕微結節狀。中央龍骨是彩色米色。
這蛞蝓有一個獨特的，非常窄腳;少年標本有腳1毫米寬和成人標本有腳的寬度僅為4或5毫米。
觸角是小的，2或3毫米長，並且它們很少延伸超過罩的邊緣。

''Laevicaulis alte'' is a round, dark-coloured slug with no shell, 7 or 8 cm long. Its skin is slightly tuberculated. The central [[keel_(slug)|keel]] is beige in colour.

This slug has a unique, very narrow foot; juvenile specimens have a foot 1 mm wide and adult specimens have a foot that is only 4 or 5 mm wide.

The [[tentacle|tentacle]]s are small, 2 or 3 mm long, and they are only rarely extended beyond the edge of the [[mantle_(mollusc)|mantle]].

==寄生物==
本蛞蝓是[[廣東住血線蟲|廣東住血線蟲]]（''[[Angiostrongylus_cantonensis|Angiostrongylus cantonensis]]''）的[[中間宿主|中間宿主]]，可引起[[广州管圆线虫病|广州管圆线虫病]]<ref name=sunrise /><ref name=Env&Eco />。

==捕食者==
本物種會被[[蛙類|蛙類]]''[[Rana_tigrina|Rana tigrina]]''獵食，從而亦成為了[[廣東住血線蟲|廣東住血線蟲]]（''[[Angiostrongylus_cantonensis|Angiostrongylus cantonensis]]''）的[[中間宿主|中間宿主]]<ref name=sunrise /><ref name=Env&Eco />。

==Habitat==
This species lives in dry areas, mostly at lower altitudes.{{Citation needed|date=May 2009}}
[[Image:Laevecaulis-small.JPG|thumb]]

==Feeding habits==
What this slug eats in the wild is not known.
In captivity this slug will eat:
* [[Dandelion|Dandelion]] leaves
* [[Tomato|Tomato]]es - it nibbles the skin first and then the pulp
* [[cucumber|cucumber]]
* [[apple|apple]]s
* [[spinach|spinach]] 
* When other items are not available, it will eat [[dill|dill]]

In captivity it will not eat:
* [[grape|grape]]s

==Life cycle==
This slug hatches from eggs. This species has several adaptations for living in dry conditions: a rounded shape with as small as possible surface area, and a narrow foot to reduce evaporation.

Juvenile specimens search for food nearly always at night, and stay buried in the soil during the day. Larger specimens are active during the day sometimes. This slug can grow up from 0.5 cm to approximately 4 cm in length in 7 months.

'''高氯酸六(二甲基亚砜)合钴(II)'''是一种[[配位化合物|配位化合物]]，化学式为[Co(C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>6</sub>SO)<sub>6</sub>](ClO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>，它是具有爆炸性的粉色晶体。<ref>Jane E. Macintyre. Dictionary of Inorganic Compounds, Supplement 1. CRC Press, 1993. ISBN 0412301202, 9780412301209. pp 105.</ref>

==制备==
将六水合高氯酸钴溶解在热的DMSO中，减压蒸馏后析出晶体，将晶体过滤并用丙酮洗涤，用[[五氧化二磷|五氧化二磷]]干燥数小时，得到纯的[Co(C<sub>2</sub>H<sub>6</sub>SO)<sub>6</sub>](ClO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。<ref name=Migdal-Mikuli>{{cite journal|title=Phase polymorphism of [Co(DMSO)6](ClO4)2 studied by differential scanning calorimetry|volume=426|issue=1|pages=191–198|date=2005-02-01|issn=0040-6031|doi=10.1016/j.tca.2004.07.021|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0040603104003466|journal=Thermochimica Acta|accessdate=2019-01-22|author=A Migdał-Mikuli, E. Szostak}}</ref>高氯酸钴水合物的丙酮溶液和过量的DMSO溶液反应，也能得到产物。<ref name=Selbin>{{cite journal|title=Metallic complexes of dimethylsulphoxide|volume=16|issue=3|pages=219–224|date=1961-02-01|issn=0022-1902|doi=10.1016/0022-1902(61)80493-4|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0022190261804934|journal=Journal of Inorganic and Nuclear Chemistry|accessdate=2019-01-22|author=J. Selbin, W. E. Bull, L. H. Holmes}}</ref>

==结构==
高氯酸六(二甲基亚砜)合钴(II)在298 K时，属[[三方晶系|三方晶系]]，[[空间群|空间群]]''P''3<sub>1</sub>/c，[[晶胞参数|晶胞参数]]a=12.572 A，V=1564 A<sup>3</sup>，分子中，配体二甲基亚砜以其[[氧|氧]]原子与钴配位。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Cation solvation in the solid state – temperature-dependent crystal structures in some metal perchlorates solvated by dimethylsulfoxide|volume=357|issue=8|pages=2365–2373|date=2004-06-10|issn=0020-1693|doi=10.1016/j.ica.2003.12.035|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0020169304000076|journal=Inorganica Chimica Acta|accessdate=2019-01-22|author=Eric J. Chan, Brian G. Cox, Jack M. Harrowfield, Mark I. Ogden, Brian W. Skelton, Allan H. White}}</ref>

'''高雅''' ('''Sophistication''') 是一種精細的品質，顯示出好的[[品味|品味]]、[[智慧|智慧]]和巧妙，而非粗糙、[[愚笨|愚笨]]和[[傭俗|傭俗]]。<ref>{{citation |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=OjwEQgAACAAJ&dq=intitle:Sophistication |title=Sophistication: A Literary and Cultural History |author=Faye Hammill |year=2010 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110922020125/http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=OjwEQgAACAAJ&dq=intitle:Sophistication |archivedate=2011-09-22 }}</ref> 以社會階級來理解，高雅可以跟[[社會地位|社會地位]]、[[特權階層|特權階層]]以及[[社會優越性|社會優越性]]等概念有關。<ref>

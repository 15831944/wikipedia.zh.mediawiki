'''高氏兄弟'''（{{Lang-en|Gao Brothers}}），中国当代艺术家组合，哥哥高兟生于1956年，弟弟高强生于1962年<ref>{{cite journal|language=zh-cn|author=汪峥|title=高氏兄弟:生来不是为反抗|journal=长城月报|year=2011|issue=5}}</ref>。二人均为山东济南人，现居北京。1985年起合作，从事绘画、装置、行为等艺术活动。1989年的装置作品《子夜的弥撒》使二人一举成名<ref>{{cite book|author=吴虹飞|language=zh-cn|title=听我讲话要小心：文化名人访谈录|year=2011年|publisher=武汉出版社|isbn=9787543063402|pages=98-102|url=http://lz.book.sohu.com/chapter-1286656.html|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150528031922/http://lz.book.sohu.com/chapter-1286656.html|archivedate=2015-05-28}}</ref>，并在90年代中期逐渐获得国际声誉<ref>{{lang|en|{{cite web|author=Gina Basso|title=The Gao Brothers: Old Haunts and New Visions|url=http://www.sfstation.com/the-gao-brothers-a5791|accessdate=2014-05-30|language=en}}}}</ref>。2003年，二人在[[798艺术区|798艺术区]]开办工作室<ref name="wu">{{cite journal|language=zh-cn|author=[[吴虹飞|吴虹飞]]|title=高氏兄弟 被迫反抗的艺术|journal=南方人物周刊|year=2010|issue=41|url=http://collection.sina.com.cn/cjrw/20101123/16342441.shtml|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150528001022/http://collection.sina.com.cn/cjrw/20101123/16342441.shtml|archivedate=2015-05-28|accessdate=2014-05-30|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。

高氏兄弟不少作品触及中国政治，并表达他们对中国政治和社会的态度<ref>{{lang|en|{{cite web|author=Daisy Wyatt|title=Body politic: Gao Brothers photographs are naked criticism of modern China|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/art/features/body-politic-gao-brothers-photographs-are-naked-criticism-of-modern-china-8658015.html|accessdate=2014-05-30|language=en}}}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=高氏兄弟：用艺术为自由呐喊|url=http://helanonline.cn/article/4901|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150528001206/http://helanonline.cn/article/4901|archivedate=2015-05-28|publisher=荷兰在线|accessdate=2014-05-30|language=zh-cn|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。这类作品一直引发巨大争议<ref>{{cite web|title=专访：高氏兄弟谈中国荒诞现实与艺术|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/zhongwen/simp/china/2013/06/130605_gao_brothers.shtml|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150528001422/http://www.bbc.co.uk/zhongwen/simp/china/2013/06/130605_gao_brothers.shtml|archivedate=2015-05-28|language=zh-cn|accessdate=2014-05-30|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。有观者指责其表达过于直接，缺乏艺术性<ref name="NYT">{{lang|en|{{cite news|author=Jimmy Wang|title=In China, a Headless Mao Is a Game of Cat and Mouse|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2009/10/06/arts/design/06gao.html?pagewanted=2&_r=0|accessdate=2014-05-30|newspaper=The New York Times|date=2009-10-05|language=en}}}}</ref>，但亦有论者认为高氏兄弟的作品怀有「对正义，民主，诗意的热切情怀」<ref>{{cite web|author=俞心樵|title=当代性的充盈和嘹亮|url=http://www.artlinkart.com/cn/artist/txt_ge/d72auws/141cswpj|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150528001528/http://www.artlinkart.com/cn/artist/txt_ge/d72auws/141cswpj|archivedate=2015-05-28|language=zh-cn|accessdate=2014-05-30|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。

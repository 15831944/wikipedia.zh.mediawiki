'''高氯酸锆'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为Zr(ClO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>4</sub>，它是挥发性晶体，由[[四氯化锆|四氯化锆]]和无水[[高氯酸|高氯酸]]在液氮温度下反应得到。它在真空中于70°C缓慢升华，这表面该分子是共价型的。<ref name="Babaeva1977">{{cite journal|last1=Babaeva|first1=V. N.|last2=Rosolovskii|first2=V. Ya.|title=Anhydrous zirconium perchlorate|journal=Bulletin of the Academy of Sciences of the USSR Division of Chemical Science|date=March 1977|volume=26|issue=3|pages=445–449|doi=10.1007/BF01179439}}</ref>

高氯酸锆可以形成[[复盐|复盐]]，如Cs<sub>2</sub>Zr(ClO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>6</sub>和(NO<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>Zr(ClO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>6</sub>。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Krivtsov|first1=N. V.|last2=Babaeva|first2=V. P.|last3=Rosolovskii|first3=V. Ya.|title=A thermochemical study of cesium and nitrile perchloratozirconates|journal=Bulletin of the Academy of Sciences of the USSR Division of Chemical Science|date=April 1990|volume=39|issue=4|pages=817–820|doi=10.1007/BF00960355}}</ref>

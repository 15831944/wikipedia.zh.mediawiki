'''高解析度音频'''（{{lang-en|High-resolution audio}}）也被称为 '''High-definition audio'''或'''Hi-Res audio'''，是一些录制音乐零售商和[[高保真|高保真]]还音设备供应商所使用的营销术语。<ref name=cnet140320>{{cite web|last=Shankland|first=Stephen|title=Sound bite: Despite Pono's promise, experts pan HD audioSound bite: Despite Pono's promise, experts pan HD audio|url=http://news.cnet.com/8301-1023_3-57620489-93/sound-bite-despite-ponos-promise-experts-pan-hd-audio/|work=c{{!}}net|publisher=CBS Interactive Inc.|accessdate=22 March 2014|quote=the "HD audio" movement also called high-resolution or high-definition audio}}</ref><ref name=giz0613>{{cite web|last=Aguilar|first=Mario|title=What Is High-Resolution Audio?|url=https://gizmodo.com/what-is-high-resolution-audio-1252637824|work=Gizmodo|publisher=Gawker Media|accessdate=17 March 2014|date=June 2013|quote=High-resolution audio is a new industry marketing term}}</ref>它指的是[[采样率|采样率]]高于44.1 kHz和（或）[[音频位深|位深]]高于16-bit的音乐。因为它通常代表了96 kHz（或甚至更高的采样率），所以有时也会写作“96k”，意味着 48 kHz的[[奈奎斯特频率|奈奎斯特频率]]。但也存在被标为HD Audio的44.1 kHz，24-bit的音乐。

==定义==
[[File:High-resolution_audio.svg|thumb]]与带宽]]

高解析度音频通常表示拥有比[[CDDA|音乐CD]]（44.1 kHz/16bit）拥有更高采样率和（或）位深的音乐文件。<ref name=giz0613 />

在2014年，[[美國唱片業協會|美國唱片業協會]](RIAA），与{{tsl|en|Consumer Electronics Association|美国消费电子产品协会}}，数字娱乐集团（DEG）和The Recording Academy Producers & Engineers Wing合作，制订了一个高分辨率音频标准。此标准表示：“无损音频能够再现音乐录制时全频段的声音，这些音频有着比CD（48kHz/20-bit或更高位深）更好的音质，并能表现艺术家，制作人或工程师最初的意图。<ref>{{cite web |title=High Resolution Audio Initiative Gets Major Boost with New "Hi-Res MUSIC" Logo and Branding Materials for Digital Retailers |url=https://www.riaa.com/high-resolution-audio-initiative-gets-major-boost-with-new-hi-res-music-logo-and-branding-materials-for-digital-retailers/ |website=The Recording Industry Association of America(RIAA) |accessdate=21 August 2018}}</ref>

这包括了采样率高于44,100 Hz，位深高于16-bit的由[[脉冲编码调制|PCM]]编码的音频<ref name=giz0613 />和使用如[[脉冲密度调制|PDM]]编码技术的音频。<ref name=what0313>{{cite web|title=High-resolution audio: everything you need to know|url=http://www.whathifi.com/news/high-resolution-audio-everything-you-need-to-know|work=What Hi-Fi?|publisher=Haymarket Publishing|accessdate=11 October 2016|date=10 June 2015 <!-- was 10 March 2014-->|quote=there's no universal standard for high-res audio}}</ref>

能够储存高解析度音频的文件格式包括[[FLAC|FLAC]]，[[Apple_Lossless|ALAC]]，[[WAV|WAV]]，[[音频交换文件格式|AIFF]]和用于[[Super_Audio_CD|Super Audio CD]]（SACD）的[[Direct_Stream_Digital|DSD]] 。<ref name=what0313 />

==历史==
首个尝试推出高解析度音频的是的于1995年发布的{{tsl|en|High Definition Compatible Digital}}。<ref name="russo">{{cite web|url=http://hometoys.com/emagazine.php?url=/ezine/08.12/russo/index.htm |title=Home Technology eMagazine - Classic Home Toys Installment #19 The Final CD Format: HDCD |publisher=HomeToys |date= |accessdate=2012-08-05 |quote=HDCD is capable of higher quality sound reproduction because HDCD encodes the equivalent of 20 bits worth of data |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140318122826/http://hometoys.com/emagazine.php?url=%2Fezine%2F08.12%2Frusso%2Findex.htm |archivedate=2014-03-18 |df= }}</ref>接下来出现了三种声称其音质优于[[CDDA|音乐CD]]的[[光碟|光碟]]格式，包括1998年发布的DAD，1999年发布的SACD与2000年发布的[[DVD-Audio|DVD-Audio]]，但它们都没有被广泛使用<ref name=pcmag />

随着21世纪初在线音乐零售的兴起，2008年{{tsl|en|HDtracks}}推出了高解析度音频下载。<ref name=pcmag>{{cite web|title=Definition of:high-resolution audio|url=https://www.pcmag.com/encyclopedia/term/57530/high-resolution-audio|work=PCMag|publisher=Ziff Davis|accessdate=18 March 2014|quote=HDtracks (http://www.hdtracks.com) pioneered high-resolution audio via download}}</ref>

进一步在光碟上推广高解析度音频的尝试包括，[[Blu-ray|Blu-ray]]2009年发布的的Pure Audio与2013年发布的{{tsl|en|High Fidelity Pure Audio}}<ref name="dvdab">{{cite web|title=Universal Music bets on consumer longing for quality with hi-fi Pure Audio|url=http://www.dvd-and-beyond.com/display-article.php?article=1935|work=DVD & Beyond|publisher=Globalcom Limited|accessdate=4 January 2014}}</ref>而[[尼爾·楊|尼爾·楊]]的{{tsl|en|Pono (digital music service)|Pono}}服务的发布，则加剧了2014年在线高解析度音频服务的竞争。<ref name=forbes0314>{{cite web|last=O'Malley Greenburg|first=Zack|title=How Neil Young's Pono Music Raised $2 Million in Two Days|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/zackomalleygreenburg/2014/03/13/how-neil-youngs-pono-music-raised-2-million-in-two-days/|work=Forbes|publisher=Forbes.com LLC|accessdate=15 March 2014|quote=He’ll have some competition. Already, services like HDtracks.com have seen triple-digit growth in downloads of top-notch digital files}}</ref>

而带有“Hi-Res AUDIO”标志的消费类音频产品则代表此设备符合日本电子情报技术产业协会（JEITA）所定义的高解析度音频的产品所需的规格。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Japan Audio Society {{!}} Hi-Res Audio Logo|url=https://www.jas-audio.or.jp/english/hi-res-logo-en|work=www.jas-audio.or.jp|accessdate=2019-02-08}}</ref>

最近，为重建自身在消费类音频产品领域的领导地位，[[索尼|索尼]]提供了一系列高分辨率音频产品，以重申其对高分辨率音频领域发展的承诺。<ref>https://discoverhiresaudio.com/#productsNav</ref>

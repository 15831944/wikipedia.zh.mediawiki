'''劍橋大學出版社訴巴頓案'''（Cambridge University Press et al. v. Patton et al.；亦稱 ''劍橋大學出版社訴貝克爾案''(Cambridge University Press et al. v. Becker)），[[判例引註|1：2008cv01425]]，為[[美國喬治亞北區聯邦地區法院|美國喬治亞北區聯邦地區法院]]正在審理中的案件。其中三家出版商，[[劍橋大學出版社|劍橋大學出版社]]，[[賽吉出版公司|賽吉出版公司]]及[[牛津大學出版社|牛津大學出版社]]，最初於2008年提起針對[[喬治亞州立大學|喬治亞州立大學]][[盗版|侵犯版權]]的訴訟。<ref name=LastRound>{{Citation | title = Last Round of Filings Made in Georgia State U. Fair-Use Lawsuit | journal = The Chronicle of Higher Education | date = 4 August 2011 | ref = harv | url = http://chronicle.com/blogs/ticker/last-round-of-filings-made-in-georgia-state-u-fair-use-lawsuit/35088}}</ref>
 
原告聲稱喬治亞州立大學通過其{{le|課程儲備|Course reserve|電子儲備}}系統"系統地、廣泛地和未經授權地複制及分發大量受版權保護的作品"。<ref name=PublishersSue>{{Citation | last = Hafner | first = Katie | title = Publishers Sue Georgia State on Digital Reading Matter | journal = New York Times | date = April 16, 2008 | ref = harv | url = https://www.nytimes.com/2008/04/16/technology/16school.html}}</ref>喬治亞州立大學聲稱其係統沒有侵犯版權，因為其使用的是[[合理使用|合理使用]]的版權方法。<ref name=BothSides>{{Citation | last = Albanese | first = Andrew | title = Both Sides Angle for Victory in E-reserve Case | journal = Publishers Weekly | date = 15 March 2010 | ref = harv | url = http://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/print/20100315/42430-both-sides-angle-for-victory-in-e-reserve-case-.html}}</ref>

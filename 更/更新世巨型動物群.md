'''更新世巨型動物群'''（{{lang-en|Pleistocene megafauna}}）是指成年體重超過{{Convert|44|kg|lb}}，生活於[[更新世|更新世]]而在[[第四紀滅絕事件|第四紀滅絕事件]]中滅絕的[[巨型動物群|巨型動物群]]。

==古生態學==
[[末次冰期|末次冰期]]為[[更新世|更新世]]晚期[[第四紀冰河時期|第四紀冰河時期]]中的最近一次[[冰期|冰期]]，始於 125,000<ref name=ipcc2007>{{cite web|author=Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (UN)|title=IPCC Fourth Assessment Report: Climate Change 2007 - Palaeoclimatic Perspective|publisher=The Nobel Foundation|url=https://www.ipcc.ch/publications_and_data/ar4/wg1/en/spmsspm-a-palaeoclimatic.html|access-date=2019-02-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151030211417/http://www.ipcc.ch/publications_and_data/ar4/wg1/en/spmsspm-a-palaeoclimatic.html|archive-date=2015-10-30|dead-url=yes}}</ref> 年前，結束於 14,500<ref name=clark2009>{{cite journal|doi=10.1126/science.1172873|pmid=19661421|title=The Last Glacial Maximum|journal=Science|volume=325|issue=5941|pages=710|year=2009|last1=Clark|first1=P. {{link-en|猛瑪平原|mammoth steppe}}為一極廣闊的平原，範圍自[[伊比利半島|伊比利半島]]開始橫跨[[歐亞大陸|歐亞大陸]]，跨越[[白令陸橋|白令陸橋]]並包括[[阿拉斯加|阿拉斯加]]及[[育空|育空]]地區。白令陸橋的出現肇因於陸地冰蓋封存水所造成的海平面下降，陸橋在 11,000 年前因海平面上升而消失<ref>{{cite journal|doi=10.1038/382060a0|title=Life and times of the Bering land bridge|journal=Nature|volume=382|issue=6586|pages=60|year=1996|last1=Elias|first1=Scott A.|last2=Short|first2=Susan K.|last3=Nelson|first3=C. ==理論==
四個理論被認為是更新世巨型動物群滅絕的主要原因：被人類[[狩獵|狩獵]]殆盡<ref>Marc A. Carrasco, Anthony D. Barnosky, Russell W. Graham [http://www.plosone.org/article/info%3Adoi%2F10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0008331 Quantifying the Extent of North American Mammal Extinction Relative to the Pre-Anthropogenic Baseline] ''plosone.org'' December 16, 2009</ref>、[[氣候變遷|氣候變遷]]、[[疾病|疾病]]散播、或是[[彗星|彗星]]與[[小行星|小行星]]撞擊<ref name="Globe9-25-07pA2">{{Citation|last=Nickerson|first=Colin| title= Cosmic blast may have killed off megafauna Scientists say early humans doomed, too| url= http://www.boston.com/news/nation/articles/2007/09/25/cosmic_blast_may_have_killed_off_megafauna/| page =A2. | publisher = The Boston Globe | location = Boston, MA | date =September 25, 2007}}</ref>。以上理論並非完全互斥，可能是綜合了兩項以上的原因才導致了滅絕。目前找到的證據多半指向人類活動是更新世巨型動物群滅絕的主因<ref>{{Cite journal|date = 25 July 2014 | title = Vanishing fauna (Special issue)|journal=[[Science_(journal)|Science]]|volume=345|issue=6195| pages = 392–412 | url =http://www.sciencemag.org/site/special/vanishing/index.xhtml?_ga=1.172413478.429831163.1471029002 |doi=10.1126/science.345.6195.392| bibcode=| last1 = Vignieri| first1 = S.}}</ref>。

[[澳洲|澳洲]]巨型動物群由[[有袋類|有袋類]]、[[單孔目|單孔目]]、[[鱷目|鱷目]]、[[龜鱉目|龜鱉目]]、[[巨蜥|巨蜥]]及數種[[不會飛的鳥|不會飛的鳥]]組成，包括[[巨型短面袋鼠|巨型短面袋鼠]]、[[雙門齒獸屬|雙門齒獸屬]]、[[袋獅|袋獅]]、[[牛頓巨鳥|牛頓巨鳥]]、{{link-en|馳鳥科|Dromornithidae}}、[[沃那比蛇|沃那比蛇]]、[[古巨蜥|古巨蜥]]<ref>[http://science.uniserve.edu.au/school/quests/mgfauna.html Australia's Megafauna]</ref><ref>[http://www.abc.net.au/science/features/megafauna/ Death of the Megafauna]</ref>。

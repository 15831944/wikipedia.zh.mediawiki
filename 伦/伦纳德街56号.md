'''伦纳德街56号'''（{{lang|en|56 Leonard Street}}）是位于[[美国|美国]][[纽约市|纽约市]][[翠貝卡|翠貝卡]]的一栋在建摩天大楼，高{{convert|821|ft|m}}，共57层。<ref name=ctbuh/>荣获[[普利兹克建筑奖|普利兹克建筑奖]]的瑞士设计事务所[[赫尔佐格和德梅隆|赫尔佐格和德梅隆]]将其描述为“在天空中堆建起来的房屋”。<ref>{{Cite web|title = Stacked houses in the sky|url = http://www.wsp-pb.com/en/High-Rise/News/Stacked-houses-in-the-sky/|website = WSP Group|access-date = 2016-02-01}}</ref>在完工后，它将是[[翠貝卡|翠貝卡]]最高楼。<ref>{{Cite news|title = Condo's Price Is Straight Up|date = June 26, 2013|url = http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424127887324183204578567650666240708.html|last = Barbanel|first = Josh|newspaper = The Wall Street Journal}}</ref>

该大楼于2017年完工。<ref name="Sheftell">{{cite news |title=Jenga-like 60-story skyscraper would be Tribeca's tallest |work=New York Daily News |author=Jason Sheftell |date=May 9, 2013 |url=http://www.nydailynews.com/life-style/real-estate/tribeca-new-tallest-60-story-tower-rising-jenga-game-article-1.1339903}}</ref>

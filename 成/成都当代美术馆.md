'''成都当代美术馆'''（{{lang-en|Museum of Contemporary Art Chengdu}}）是一座位于四川[[成都|成都]]的当代美术馆。<ref>[http://hyperallergic.com/264969/a-chinese-performance-artist-who-balances-politics-and-poetics/ "A Chinese Performance Artist Who Balances Politics and Poetics"]. ''Hyperallergenic'', by R. Orion Martin on December 30, 2015</ref> 专门从事中国和全球各地展览，翻译和收藏[[中国当代艺术|中国当代艺术]]。<ref>[http://italy.exibart.com/news/articolo.php?id_cat=42&id_news=1013 "China: In the spotlight-Chengdu Museum of Contemporary Art"]..''Exibart'', Italy, July 27th, 2011</ref><ref name=schrade>[https://internchina.com/modern-art-in-china-the-moca-chengdu/ "Modern Art in China? The MOCA Chengdu"]. By Sebastian Schrade on April 21, 2014</ref><ref>https://www.ft.com/content/3fe88606-37c7-11e6-a780-b48ed7b6126f?mhq5j=e2</ref>

==历史==
由中国建筑师[[刘家琨|刘家琨]]设计建造，2011年面向公众开放。<ref>http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/regional/2011-07/03/content_12824868.htmChengdu "Museum of Contemporary Art opens"]. By Huang Zhiling ''China Daily'' Sichuan Bureau, 2011-07-03</ref>由国有企业[[成都高新投资集团|成都高新投资集团]]有限公司全资兴建并投资运营.<ref>[http://hyperallergic.com/306057/despite-scandals-and-slashed-funding-an-art-museum-perseveres-in-china/ "Despite Scandals and Slashed Funding, an Art Museum Perseveres in China"]. ''Hyperallergenic'', by R. Orion Martin on June 20, 2016</ref><ref>http://www.archdaily.com/199671/moma-chengdu-studio-ramoprimo</ref>艺术史学家[[吕澎|吕澎]]担任首任馆长。

2014年，博物馆尚未展出永久收藏品，但已经开设了现代艺术展览馆和摄影展。<ref name=schrade /><ref>[http://www.loeildelaphotographie.com/en/2016/09/30/article/159920995/xiao-quan-our-generation/ "Xiao Quan, Our Generation"]. ''The Eye of Photography'', September 30, 2016 - China , written by Jean Loh</ref>

==展览==
除了主办亚洲艺术家展览之外，博物馆还举办了西方艺术家的回顾展，<ref name="Berghuis2006">{{cite book|author=Thomas J. Berghuis|title=Performance Art in China|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=mRmppyhh9W0C&pg=PA266|year=2006|publisher=Timezone 8 Limited|isbn=978-988-99265-9-5|pages=266–}}</ref> 包括：[[托尼·克拉格|托尼·克拉格]]和[[毕加索|毕加索]]以及和艺术馆合作，诸如[[巴黎现代艺术博物馆|巴黎现代艺术博物馆]]展示当代艺术家的作品。

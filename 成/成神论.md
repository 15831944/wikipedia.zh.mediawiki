{{东方基督教}}

'''成神论'''（{{lang-el|θέωσις}} / ''theosis'')是一个[[希腊语|希腊语]][[单词|单词]]，意为'''神化'''或'''使神圣'''。信徒的变化，是将[[基督|基督]][[耶稣|耶稣]]的教导和他的[[福音|福音]]付诸实践（称为[[Praxis_(Eastern_Orthodoxy)|实践]]），是[[基督教神学|基督教神学]]，特别是[[东正教神学|东正教神学]]和[[天主教神学|天主教神学]]的特色<ref>"The ''Catechism (of the Catholic Church)'' readily invokes the Eastern Fathers to emphasize the mystery of divinization (see §1589, 1988), which Catholic and Orthodox have affirmed together" ([http://books.google.com/books?id=nzTkg_A6CWkC&pg=PA715&dq=%22catholic+church%22+divinization&hl=en&ei=OkgeTOiqDMmVsQbWk7iDDg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=7&ved=0CEcQ6AEwBg#v=onepage&q=%22catholic%20church%22%20divinization&f=false Encyclopedia of Christian Theology, vol. 1, article ''Holiness'']).</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=umB7LPLCP6QC&pg=PA122&dq=%22catholic+church%22+divinization&hl=en&ei=yVoeTI-2Ed3bsAa80MmfDg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=6&ved=0CEAQ6AEwBTge#v=onepage&q&f=false Jennifer Ferrara, Patricia Sodano Ireland, ''The Catholic Mystique'' (Our Sunday Visitor 2000 ISBN 1-931709-91-2), p. 122]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=DgtUoMqm594C&pg=PA259&dq=%22catholic+church%22+divinization&hl=en&ei=OkgeTOiqDMmVsQbWk7iDDg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CC8Q6AEwAQ#v=onepage&q=%22catholic%20church%22%20divinization&f=false Francis J. Caponi, ''Divinization in Roman Catholicism'']</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=u2a87RuY2icC&pg=PA409&dq=%22catholic+church%22+divinization&hl=en&ei=OkgeTOiqDMmVsQbWk7iDDg&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=5&ved=0CD0Q6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=%22catholic%20church%22%20divinization&f=false Thomas Langan, ''The Catholic Tradition'' (University of Missouri Press 1998 ISBN 0-8262-1183-6), p. 409]</ref>.

== 东正教神学 ==
[[File:StJohnClimacus.jpg|thumb]]《神圣的天梯》，[[西乃山|西乃山]][[圣凯瑟琳修道院|圣凯瑟琳修道院]]]]

[[亚他那修|亚他那修]]写道“基督成為人，為教我們得成聖”<ref>''On the Incarnation'' 54:3, PG 25:192B</ref>。他的话是此教义的最好说明。原本似乎荒唐的事—堕落的，有罪的人可以成为圣洁，如同圣洁的神—通过耶稣基督，神的化身，已变为可能。

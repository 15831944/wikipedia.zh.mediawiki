'''麗莎·喬伊'''（{{lang-en|Lisa Joy}}）是一名美國女[[編劇|編劇]]、[[電影導演|导演]]和电视制片人。她是[[HBO|HBO]]影集《[[西部世界|西部世界]]》的共同創作者和执行制作人。其他作品包括《[[火线警告|火线警告]]》（擔任联合製片人）以及《[[點到即止|點到即止]]》。喬伊創作的《西部世界》在2017年7月獲得了[[艾美獎|艾美獎]]提名。<ref name="Esquire - 29 September 2016 - Westworld Is Not the Next Game of Thrones—It's Much More Than That">{{cite news|url=http://www.esquire.com/entertainment/tv/news/a49073/westworld-interview-jonathan-nolan-lisa-joy/|title=Westworld Is Not the Next Game of Thrones—It's Much More Than That|last=Miller|first=Matt|date=2016-09-29|work=[[Esquire_(magazine)|Esquire]]|publisher=[[Hearst_Magazines|Hearst Magazines]]|accessdate=2016-10-19}}</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
麗莎·喬伊在[[紐澤西州|紐澤西州]]長大。她的父亲是英國人，母亲是台湾人。<ref name="Elle">{{Cite web|url=https://www.elle.com/culture/movies-tv/a19181638/lisa-joy-westworld-tv-interview-season-two/|title=With 'Westworld', Lisa Joy Is Rewriting Women's Power Story Line in Hollywood and Beyond|date=2018-04-17|last=Fortini|first=Amanda|quote=Joy, who is 40, was raised in a hardworking immigrant family (her dad is from England; her mom is from Taiwan) in suburban New Jersey.}}</ref>

在進入娛樂圈前，喬伊是加州的一名職業律師。她毕业于[[哈佛法学院|哈佛法学院]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://members.calbar.ca.gov/fal/Member/Detail/262132|title=State Bar of CA :: Lisa Joy|accessdate=2016-10-20|last=California|first=The State Bar of}}</ref>喬伊後來嫁給劇作家[[乔纳森·诺兰|乔纳森·诺兰]]（[[克里斯托弗·诺兰|克里斯托弗·诺兰]]的弟弟）。两人是在克里斯托弗·诺兰的電影《[[凶心人|凶心人]]》的首映會上認識<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/2014/08/on-the-rise-2014-13-screenwriters-to-watch-273392/|title=On The Rise 2014: 13 Screenwriters To Watch {{!}} IndieWire|accessdate=2016-10-20|last=Staff|first=The Playlist}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.dailyherald.com/article/20161018/entlife/161018833/|title=A-minus at Loyola Academy fueled 'Westworld' creator|last=Gire|first=Jamie Sotonoff and Dann|date=2016-10-18|newspaper=Daily Herald|accessdate=2016-10-20}}</ref>

喬伊和丈夫诺兰育有一子一女。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QD1Bj1egE3U|title=What Does A Showrunner Bring To A Deserted Island?|accessdate=2016-09-12|date=2014-04-15|last=King|first=Larry|publisher=[[YouTube|YouTube]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/10/02/arts/television/is-the-ambitious-westworld-hbos-next-big-hit.html?_r=0|title=Is 'Westworld' HBO's Next Big Hit?|date=2016-09-29|publisher=New York Times}}</ref>

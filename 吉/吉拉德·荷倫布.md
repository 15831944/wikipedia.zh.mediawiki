'''吉拉德·荷倫布'''（Gerard Horenbout'''，'''約{{bd|1465年||1541年}}） 是一個[[法兰德斯|法兰德斯]]的微圖畫家，屬於晚期[[早期尼德蘭繪畫|佛蘭芒原始繪畫]]家。有許多證據認為吉拉德有可能就是{{tsl|en|Master of James IV of Scotland|蘇格蘭詹姆士四世畫師|}}<ref name="James p. 242" /><ref>Scot McKendrick. "Reviving the Past," in ''Illuminating the Renaissance: The Flemish Triumph of Manuscript Painting in Europe.'' Getty Publications; 1 July 2003. ISBN 978-0-89236-704-7. p. 428.</ref>。

==傳記==
荷倫布在[[根特|根特]]居住和工作，並且以[[泥金裝飾手抄本|手抄本的插圖畫家]]聞名。他也製作彩色玻璃、壁毯、刺繡設計、鐵製作品和鑲板畫。最早紀錄到有關荷倫布事蹟的資料可追溯到1487年，因參與{{tsl|en|Guild of Saint Luke|聖湖公會|}}<ref name="James p. 242">Susan E. James. ''The Feminine Dynamic in English Art, 1485-1603: Women As Consumers, Patrons and Painters''. Ashgate Publishing Company; 2009. ISBN 978-0-7546-6381-2. p. 242.</ref><ref name="Richardson" />。參與公會後不久，荷倫布與瑪格麗特·薩凡德斯（Margaret Svanders）結婚，並育有六子，其中{{tsl|en|Lucas Horenbout|盧卡斯·荷倫布|盧卡斯}}與{{tsl|en|Susanna Hornebolt|蘇珊娜·荷倫布|蘇珊娜}}後來也成為畫家。其他孩子還有還有兒子埃洛伊（Eloy）和尤里斯（Joris）<ref name="James p. 242" />。

他至少有兩個學徒，一個在1498年，一個在1502年。在1515年，他擔任{{tsl|en|Archduchess Margaret of Austria|薩沃伊公爵夫人瑪格麗特|}}的畫家，也曾短暫的在英王[[亨利八世|亨利八世]]宮廷裡工作。1521年，[[阿尔布雷希特·丢勒|丢勒]]帶了和吉拉德之女{{tsl|en|Susanna Hornebolt|蘇珊娜·荷倫布|蘇珊娜}}有手抄本插圖拜訪他。他的兒子{{tsl|en|Lucas Horenbout|盧卡斯·荷倫布|盧卡斯}}也是一名知名畫家<ref name="Richardson">{{cite book |last1=Richardson |first1=Carol M. |last2=Woods |first2=Kim |others=Michael W. Franklin |title=Renaissance art reconsidered: an anthology of primary sources |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SqNxBd8LS20C&pg=PA10&dq=%22gerard+horenbout%22&as_brr=3&ei=cJhUSt6rJYfEMvPI-JQH |accessdate=8 July 2009 |year=2007 |publisher=Wiley-Blackwell  |isbn=978-1-4051-4641-8 |pages=10–11}}</ref>。

他的太太薩凡德斯於1529年去世<ref name="Emerson p. 113">Kathy Lynn Emerson, [http://www.questia.com/read/59429201 ''Wives and Daughters: The Women of Sixteenth Century England'']. Troy, NY: Whitston, 1984. p. 113.</ref><ref name="Thorne p. 220">James Thorne. ''[https://books.google.com/books?id=qjwQAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA220 Handbook to the Environs of London: Alphabetically Arranged, Containing an Account of Every Town and Village, and of All Places of Interest, Within a Circle of Twenty Miles Round London]''. John Murray; 1876. p. 220.</ref>。他為此做了一個黃銅匾牌，後來發現於{{tsl|en|All Saints Church, Fulham|富勒姆諸聖堂|倫敦富勒姆的全聖教堂}}<ref name="British History" /><ref>Susan E. James. ''The Feminine Dynamic in English Art, 1485-1603: Women As Consumers, Patrons and Painters''. Ashgate Publishing Company; 2009. ISBN 978-0-7546-6381-2. pp. 242-243.</ref>{{#tag:ref|It is described as a memorial to "Margaret (Saunders), wife of Gerard Hornebolt, c. 1529, lozenge-shaped plate with head and shoulders of woman in shroud, and angels on each side above inscription, below a shield-of-arms, a cheveron between three martlets with an escutcheon on the cheveron charged with a mill-rind cross between four crescents for Hornebolt impaling a winnowing-fan with a molet of six points in chief for des Vanders quartering a cheveron between three moors' heads for Deman, between initials G.M. Flemish work."<ref name="British History">[http://www.british-history.ac.uk/report.aspx?compid=119698 Fulham: Parish Church of All Saints.] An Inventory of the Historical Monuments in London, Volume 2: West London (1925), pp. 31-37. Retrieved 29 January 2014.</ref>|group="nb"}}。

吉拉德逝世於1540年或1541<ref name="James p. 242" />。

'''吉爾波特症候群'''（{{lang|en|Gilbert's syndrome}}，簡稱'''GS'''）為一種常見的遺傳性[[肝臟疾病|肝臟疾病]]，盛行率約3-12%<ref name=mayo>{{cite web|work=Mayo Clinic|title=Gilbert's syndrome|date=July 2, 2015|url=http://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/gilberts-syndrome/basics/definition/con-20024904}}</ref><ref name=MedLine>{{cite web|last=Dugdale|first=David C.|title=Gilbert's disease|url=http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/000301.htm|work=MedlinePlus|publisher=U.S. National Library of Medicine|accessdate=10 February 2014|date=2013-05-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Gilbert Syndrome|url=http://www.liverfoundation.org/abouttheliver/info/gilbertsyndrome/|publisher=American Liver Foundation}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Gilbert's syndrome|url=http://www.nhs.uk/conditions/gilbertssyndrome/Pages/Introduction.aspx|work=NHS Choices}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=What is Gilbert syndrome?|date=February 2012|publisher=Genetics Home Reference|url=http://ghr.nlm.nih.gov/condition/gilbert-syndrome}}</ref>。

患者體内的非共軛性膽紅素會顯著提升，但沒有明顯併發症，患者可能會在運動後產生輕微黃疸。

造成此疾病的原因為患者的{{tsl|en|glucuronyltransferase|葡萄糖醛酸苷轉移酶}}功能不佳<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Bosma|first1=PJ|last2=Chowdhury|first2=JR|last3=Bakker|first3=C|last4=Gantla|first4=S|last5=de Boer|first5=A|last6=Oostra|first6=BA|last7=Lindhout|first7=D|last8=Tytgat|first8=GN|last9=Jansen|first9=PL|last10=Oude Elferink|first10=RP|title=The genetic basis of the reduced expression of bilirubin UDP-glucuronosyltransferase 1 in Gilbert's syndrome.|journal=The New England Journal of Medicine|date=2 November 1995|volume=333|issue=18|pages=1171–5|pmid=7565971|doi=10.1056/nejm199511023331802}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Koiwai|first1=O|last2=Nishizawa|first2=M|last3=Hasada|first3=K|last4=Aono|first4=S|last5=Adachi|first5=Y|last6=Mamiya|first6=N|last7=Sato|first7=H|title=Gilbert's syndrome is caused by a heterozygous missense mutation in the gene for bilirubin UDP-glucuronosyltransferase.|journal=Human Molecular Genetics|date=July 1995|volume=4|issue=7|pages=1183–6|pmid=8528206|doi=10.1093/hmg/4.7.1183}}</ref>，使非共軛膽紅素無法轉化為較為水溶的共軛膽紅素。

'''吉大港動物園'''（{{lang-bn|চট্টগ্রাম চিড়িয়াখানা}}、{{lang-en|Chittagong Zoo}}）是[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]][[吉大港市|吉大港市]]的一座[[動物園|動物園]]，位於{{link-en|佛伊湖|Foy's Lake}}旁的坡地，於1988年由吉大港市官員MA Mannon等人開始規劃興建<ref name=Ara>{{cite web|title=Chittagong zoo attracts more visitors|url=http://www.newagebd.net/article/34140/chittagong-zoo-attracts-more-visitors|publisher=New Age Bangladesh|author=Ferdous Ara|date=2018-02-05|access-date=2018-11-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180314070705/http://www.newagebd.net/article/34140/chittagong-zoo-attracts-more-visitors#|archive-date=2018-03-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>，並於1989年2月建成開放。截至2015年，園內共有320隻動物，包括28種[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]、4種[[爬蟲類|爬蟲類]]與35種[[鳥類|鳥類]]，每天約有800-1000人造訪。園區因人少缺乏、管理不善，而有衛生欠佳、設施破舊與流浪狗等問題<ref name="Sharp decline">{{cite news |last=Uddin |first=Minhaj |date=8 March  2015 |title=Sharp decline of visitors at Chittagong Zoo |url=http://www.thedailystar.net/sharp-decline-of-visitors-at-chittagong-zoo-31857 |newspaper={{link-en|The Daily Star (Bangladesh)|The Daily Star (Bangladesh)}} |location=[[Chittagong|Chittagong]] |access-date=2018-11-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180616102746/https://www.thedailystar.net/sharp-decline-of-visitors-at-chittagong-zoo-31857# |archive-date=2018-06-16 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。2017年12月，共有約91000人次參觀吉大港動物園<Ref name=Ara/>。2018年，園內共有約360隻動物<Ref name=albino/>。

2018年7月，園內有一隻[[孟加拉白虎|孟加拉白虎]]誕生，是孟加拉國的首隻白虎<ref name=albino>{{cite web|url=https://www.dhakatribune.com/bangladesh/2018/09/08/bangladesh-s-first-ever-albino-tiger-cub-doing-well-at-chittagong-zoo|title=Bangladesh's first-ever albino tiger cub doing well at Chittagong Zoo|publisher=Dhaka Tribune|date=2018-09-08|access-date=2018-11-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180908175756/https://www.dhakatribune.com/bangladesh/2018/09/08/bangladesh-s-first-ever-albino-tiger-cub-doing-well-at-chittagong-zoo#|archive-date=2018-09-08|dead-url=no}}</ref>。8月，園方規劃要建造一個新的鳥舍，以容納[[情侶鸚鵡|情侶鸚鵡]]、[[棕斑鳩|棕斑鳩]]、[[雉|雉]]、[[紅領綠鸚鵡|紅領綠鸚鵡]]、[[雞尾鸚鵡|雞尾鸚鵡]]與[[金剛鸚鵡|金剛鸚鵡]]等六種鳥類<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dhakatribune.com/bangladesh/nation/2018/08/29/chittagong-zoo-to-build-new-aviary|title=Chittagong Zoo to build new aviary|date=2018-08-29|publisher=Dhaka Tribune|access-date=2018-11-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180829101547/https://www.dhakatribune.com/bangladesh/nation/2018/08/29/chittagong-zoo-to-build-new-aviary#|archive-date=2018-08-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

'''赛睿''' (英文名'''steelseries''')是[[丹麦|丹麦]]的游戏外设和配件制造商，包括[[耳機|耳机]]，[[电脑键盘|键盘]]，[[鼠标|鼠標]]和[[鼠标垫|鼠標垫]]。

==历史==
赛睿是由Jacob Wolff-Petersen创立于2001年。<ref>{{cite interview|url=http://www.whiteboardmag.com/how-to-bootstrap-50-million-steelseries-founder-wolff-petersen/ |title=How to bootstrap from zero to 50 million $: SteelSeries founder Jacob Wolff-Petersen |publisher=Whiteboardmag.com |accessdate=2014-10-03}}</ref>公司原名Soft Trading后于2007年改名为SteelSeries（赛睿）<ref>{{cite press release |url=http://steelseries.com/blog/press/soft-trading-aps-changes-name-to-steelseries-aps |title=Soft Trading ApS Changes Name To SteelSeries ApS |publisher=SteelSeries.com |accessdate=2014-10-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141006122351/http://steelseries.com/blog/press/soft-trading-aps-changes-name-to-steelseries-aps |archivedate=2014-10-06 }}</ref>

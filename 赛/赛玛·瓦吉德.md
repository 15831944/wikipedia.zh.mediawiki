'''赛玛·瓦吉德'''（{{lang-en|Saima Wazed}}，{{bd|1972年|12月9日|}}<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.theindependentbd.com/home/printnews/61747|title=Sheikh Hasina’s 70th birthday today Independent|website=www.theindependentbd.com|access-date=2018-04-20}}</ref>），是孟加拉国自闭症活动家、她也是孟加拉国总理[[谢赫·哈西娜|谢赫·哈西娜]]的女儿。赛玛·瓦吉德是[[世界卫生组织|世界卫生组织]]25名成员的心理健康问题专家咨询小组成员<ref>{{web cite|title=Autism campaigner Putul listens to the troubles of people with disabilities, stresses on inclusive development|url=http://bdnews24.com/health/2015/08/09/autism-campaigner-putul-listens-to-the-troubles-of-people-with-disabilities-stresses-on-inclusive-development|website=bdnews24.com|accessdate=11 February 2016}}</ref>。

'''约卢·阿比尔'''（'''Iolu Johnson Abil'''，{{bd|1942年||catIdx=A}}），是[[瓦努阿图|瓦努阿图]]的政治家。2009年9月2日宣誓就任[[瓦努阿图總統|瓦努阿图總統]]。<ref name=rnzi>{{cite news |first=|last=|title=Iolu Abil elected as Vanuatu’s President |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=48866|work=[[Radio_New_Zealand_International|Radio New Zealand International]]|publisher=|date=2009-09-02 |accessdate=2009-09-02}}</ref><ref name=pd>{{cite news |first=|last=|title=Iolu Abil elected as Vanuatu's new president |url=http://english.people.com.cn/90001/90777/90851/6746660.html|work=[[People's_Daily|People's Daily]]|publisher=|date=2009-09-02 |accessdate=2009-09-02}}</ref>

==简介==
约卢·阿比尔出生于瓦努阿图南端的[[塔納島|塔納島]]上，<ref name=rnzi/><ref name=bio>{{cite news |first=|last=|title=Some People of Vanuatu: Iolu Abbil |url=http://books.google.pl/books?id=yBRMSRbOSLUC&pg=PA62&lpg=PA62&dq=Iolu+Abbil&source=bl&ots=15xkVlZmi1&sig=NMeFD6kPNYtBfxX_lWUt-cs0azg&hl=pl&ei=uSqeSuPnKcaJsAafrr20AQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=6#v=onepage&q=Iolu%20Abbil&f=false |work=Yumi Stanap, "Some People of Vanuatu," pages 62–64|publisher=[[Google_Books|Google Books]], Institute of Pacific Studies. The University of South Pacific and Lotu Pasifika Productions|year=1981 |accessdate=2009-09-03}}</ref>是瓦努阿图长老宗的信徒。<ref name=rnzi2>{{cite news |first=|last=|title=Vanuatu’s newly elected President continues long public role|url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=48884|work=[[Radio_New_Zealand_International|Radio New Zealand International]]|publisher=|date=2009-09-03 |accessdate=2009-09-03}}</ref>
他曾经担任瓦努阿图内政和卫生部长，是瓦努阿库党党员。在2009年9月2日的大选中击败前总统[[卡尔科特·马塔斯凯莱凯莱|卡尔科特·马塔斯凯莱凯莱]]在内的11名候选人，<ref name=rnzi/><ref name=pd/>当选为瓦努阿图第七任总统。<ref name=rnzi/>当天晚上，正式宣誓就职。

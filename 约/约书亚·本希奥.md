'''约书亚·本希奥'''（{{lang-fr|'''Yoshua Bengio'''}}，{{bd|1964年|3月5日|||CatIdx=Bengio, Yoshua}}），[[加拿大人|加拿大]][[计算机科学|计算机科学家]]，因[[人工神经网络|人工神经网络]]和[[深度学习|深度学习]]领域的研究而闻名。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.technologyreview.com/s/539226/ibm-pushes-deep-learning-with-a-watson-upgrade/|title=IBM Pushes Deep Learning with a Watson Upgrade|accessdate=July 31, 2016|date=July 9, 2015|last1=Knight|first1=Will|website=MIT Technology Review}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=Deep learning|first1=Yann|last2=Bengio|first2=Yoshua|date=2015|journal=Nature|issue=7553|doi=10.1038/nature14539|volume=521|pages=436–444|pmid=26017442|last3=Hinton|first3=Geoffrey|last1=LeCun}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.recode.net/2015/7/15/11614684/ai-conspiracy-the-scientists-behind-deep-learning|title=Welcome to the AI Conspiracy: The 'Canadian Mafia' Behind Tech's Latest Craze|accessdate=July 31, 2016|date=July 15, 2015|last1=Bergen|first1=Mark|last2=Wagner|first2=Kurt|website=Recode}}</ref>

與Ian Goodfellow、Aaron Courville兩人合著 [http://www.deeplearningbook.org/ Deep Learning ]。

與Geoffrey Hinton,Yann Lecun同為2018年圖靈獎得主。

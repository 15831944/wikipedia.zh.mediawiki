'''约翰·布拉德利·赫斯特''',（John Bradley Hirst, 1942年7月9日-2016年2月3日）<ref name=BirthAnnouncement>{{cite news |url=http://nla.gov.au/nla.news-article48873966 |title=Family Notices |newspaper=[[The_Advertiser_(Adelaide)|The Advertiser (Adelaide)]] |volume=LXXXV, |issue=26135 |location=South Australia |date=10 July 1942 |accessdate=14 April 2016 |page=10 |via=National Library of Australia}}</ref><ref Name=DeathNotice>{{cite web|title=Death notice|url=http://tributes.heraldsun.com.au/notice/251422005|website=Herald Sun|publisher=The Herald and Weekly Times Pty Ltd (News Corp)|accessdate=11 February 2016}}</ref>澳大利亚历史学家和社会评论员。从1968年到2006年退休，他在[[拉筹伯大学|拉筹伯大学]]任教，并在澳大利亚电影协会和澳大利亚国家博物馆任职。他被称为“历史学家、公共知识分子和积极的公民”。<ref name=Markwell>{{Citation | author1=Markwell, Don | author2=Gerson, Elliot F., (writer of preface.) | title='Instincts to lead' : on leadership, peace, and education | publication-date=2013 | publisher=Australia Connor Court Publishing | isbn=978-1-922168-70-2 }}</ref>

==经历==
赫斯特出生于阿德莱德，曾就读于[[安莱高中|安莱高中]]，并在[[阿德莱德大学|阿德莱德大学]]进行本科和研究生学习。1968年，他放弃了之前成为卫理公会牧师的愿望，被任命为墨尔本[[拉筹伯大学|拉筹伯大学]]的讲师。他的妻子和同学克里斯汀陪他去了墨尔本。他们有两个孩子，凯瑟琳和大卫。<ref name=StuartMacintyre>{{cite news|last1=Stuart Macintyre, Graeme Davison|title=Shunning awards, historian set his own true course|url=http://www.smh.com.au/comment/obituaries/shunning-awards-historian-set-his-own-true-course-20160310-gnfd9b.html|accessdate=11 Apr 2016|work=SMH|date=16 Mar 2016}}</ref> 赫斯特后来成为拉筹伯大学历史系的系主任和读者。他于2006年退休，是拉筹伯大学的荣誉退休学者，直到去世。<ref name=Steger1>{{cite news|last=Steger|first=Jason|date=6 February 2016|title=Leading Australian historian and public intellectual John Hirst has died|url=http://www.theage.com.au/entertainment/books/leading-australian-historian-and-public-intellectual-john-hirst-has-died-20160205-gmmk85.html|newspaper=The Age|location=Melbourne|access-date=6 February 2016}}</ref><ref name=buckingham>{{cite news|last1=Buckingham-Jones|first1=Sam|title=Public intellectual has last argument: John Hirst dies at 73|url=http://www.theaustralian.com.au/news/nation/public-intellectual-has-last-argument-john-hirst-dies-at-73/news-story/33381247fe016a170c96f5957ecf1f15|accessdate=10 February 2016|work=Australian|date=8 February 2016}}</ref>1977年到1980年，赫斯特被借调到墨尔本大学编辑澳大利亚领先的历史杂志《历史研究》。<ref name="Frost">{{cite journal|last1=Frost|first1=Alan|title=Challenging the Orthodoxies: a distinctive figure in our intellectual life|journal=Australian Book Review|date=September 2010|issue= 324|pages=15–16}}</ref> 退休后，他定期去悉尼指导研究生撰写论文，但没有报酬。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Russell|first1=Terry|title=Remembering JB Hirst, A Very Different Historian|url=https://newmatilda.com/2016/02/16/remembering-jb-hirst-a-very-different-historian/|accessdate=1 March 2016|work=New Matilda|date=16 February 2016}}</ref> 
赫斯特于2016年2月3日去世，享年73岁。<ref name=DeathNotice />

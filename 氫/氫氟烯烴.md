'''氫氟烯烴'''（Hydrofluoroolefins，簡稱HFO）是由[[氫|氫]]、[[氟|氟]]、[[碳|碳]]組成的不飽和[[有機化合物|有機化合物]]。這些[[有机氟化学|有机氟化学]]常會作為[[冷媒|冷媒]]使用，氫氟烯烴和傳統的[[氯氟烃|氯氟烃]]（CFC）及{{le|氫氟烴|hydrofluorocarbon}}（HFC）不同，這兩種都是飽和有機化合物，而氫氟烯烴是[[烯烃|烯烃]]。

HFO冷媒屬於零[[臭氧破壞潛勢|臭氧破壞潛勢]]（OZP）以及低[[全球暖化潛勢|全球暖化潛勢]]（GWP）冷媒，比氯氟烃、氫氟烴、氫氯氟烃更對地球友善，是良好的替代品。

HFO冷媒屬於「第四代」[[冷媒|冷媒]]，全球暖化潛勢只有氫氟烴的0.1%<ref name=C&I2012>{{Cite journal |language=it |title= HETEROGENEOUS CATALYSIS UNDER MICROWAVE HEATING |journal=La Chimica & L'Industria |publisher=Società Chimica Italiana |url= https://www.soc.chim.it/sites/default/files/chimind/pdf/2012_4_78_ca.pdf |issue= 4 |date= May 2012 |pages=22 }}</ref><ref>[https://www.soc.chim.it/sites/default/files/chimind/pdf/2012_4_17_ca.pdf HFO, i nuovi gas refirgerant]</ref><ref>[http://www.fluorocarbons.org/library/179/79/Hydrofluoroolefins-HFOs/ Hydrofluoroolefins (HFOs)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120204021421/http://www.fluorocarbons.org/library/179/79/Hydrofluoroolefins-HFOs/ |date=2012-02-04 }}, European Fluorocarbons Technical Committee</ref>。目前有使用的HFO有[[2,3,3,3-四氟丙烯|2,3,3,3-四氟丙烯]]（HFO-1234yf）及{{le|1,3,3,3-四氟丙烯|1,3,3,3-tetrafluoropropene}}（HFO-1234ze）<ref name=C&I2012/><ref>[http://www51.honeywell.com/honeywell/news-events/press-releases-details/10.07.08GlobalWarming.html?c=31 Honeywell Sells Novel Low-Global-Warming Blowing Agent To European Customers], Honeywell press release, Oct. 7, 2008</ref>。而{{le|1-氯-3,3,3-三氟丙烯|1-chloro-3,3,3-trifluoropropene}}（HFO-1233zd）也正在開發中<ref>{{cite journal | journal = Chemical & Engineering News | year = 2011 | issue = 49 | volume = 89 | pages = 31–32 | title = Replacing the Replacements | author = Cheryl Hogue|doi = 10.1021/cen-v089n049.p031}}</ref>。

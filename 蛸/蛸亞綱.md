'''蛸亞綱'''（學名：{{lang|la|Coleoidea}}<ref name=Cephalopods>{{cite book en|id=ISBN 0-19-852761-6|author=Marion Nixon and J.Z. Young|year=2003|publisher=Oxford University Press|location=New York|title=The brains and lives of cephalopods}}</ref>）是[[頭足綱|頭足綱]]下的一類[[軟體動物|軟體動物]]。它們不像[[鸚鵡螺亞綱|鸚鵡螺亞綱]]般有硬殼，最多只有用來控制浮沉的內骨。一些[[物種|物種]]甚至完全沒有[[骨頭|骨頭]]，一些則以[[軟骨|軟骨]]來支撐。

蛸亞綱的分類是以觸手的數量及結構來定界的。已[[滅絕|滅絕]]及最原始的[[箭石下綱|箭石下綱]]相信是有10隻觸手。較現代的物種其中一對觸手可能已經進化或退化了。[[十腕總目|十腕總目]]的第四對手臂進化成為有吸盤的長觸手。[[八腕總目|八腕總目]]的第二對手臂有不同的改變：[[幽靈蛸目|幽靈蛸目]]演化成感應絲狀物；[[八腕目|八腕目]]則完全退化。

==演化歷史==
最古老的蛸亞綱物種是源自約3.3億年前[[石炭紀|石炭紀]]的[[密西西比世|密西西比世]]。也有發現一些屬於[[泥盆紀|泥盆紀]]的[[化石|化石]]<ref name="Bandel et al. 1983">{{cite journal en |author1=Bandel, Klaus |author2=Reitner, J. |author3= Sturmer, W. |last-author-amp=yes |year=1983|title=Coleoidea from the Lower Devonian Black Slate ("Hunsruck-Schiefer")|journal=Neues Jahrbuch für Geologie und Paläontologie, Abhandlungen|volume=165|issue=3|pages=397-417|location=[[斯圖加特|Stuttgart]] ([[德國|德國]])}}</ref>，但有古生物學家不認同<ref name="Nishighuchi & Mapes 2008">{{Cite book en|author=Nishiguchi, Michelle, & Mapes, Royal K.|year=2008|chapter=Cephalopoda|title=Phylogeny and evolution of the Mollusca|editor=Ponder, Winston F., & Lindberg, David R.|pages=163-199|location=Dordrecht, The Netherlands|publisher=Springer|id=ISBN 978-0520250925}}</ref>。
其實其他的頭足綱物種亦[[內殼|內殼]]（例如：''[[Sphooceras|Sphooceras]]''的化石顯示這個物種亦有內殼），所以內殼的存在這個特徵有可能是蛸亞綱物種在演化過程中把殼「內化」了，而事發時間推斷在[[志留紀|志留紀]]<ref>{{Cite journal|title=An endocochleate experiment" in the Silurian straight-shelled cephalopod ''Sphooceras''|author=Turek V|author2= Manda Š|journal = Bulletin of Geosciences|url=http://www.geology.cz/bulletin/contents/art1335|volume=87|issue=4|pages=767–813|doi=10.3140/bull.geosci.1335}}</ref>。依照這個理論推論，一種在早期到中期[[寒武紀|寒武紀]]出現的化石種類''[[Nectocaris|Nectocaris]]''可能是一種經過二代外殼消失的頭足綱物種<ref name="Smith2010">{{Cite journal | doi = 10.1038/nature09068 | title = Primitive soft-bodied cephalopods from the Cambrian | url = http://people.ds.cam.ac.uk/ms609/nectocaris.html | year = 2010 | last1 = Smith | first1 = M. R. | last2 = Caron | first2 = J. B. | journal = Nature | volume = 465 | pages = 469–472 | pmid = 20505727 | issue = 7297 | bibcode = 2010Natur.465..469S | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160127211756/http://people.ds.cam.ac.uk/ms609/nectocaris.html | archivedate = 2016-01-27 }}</ref><ref name="Smith2013">{{cite journal | doi = 10.1666/12029 | last1 = Smith | first1 = M. R. | year = 2013 | title = Nectocaridid ecology, diversity and affinity: early origin of a cephalopod-like body plan | journal = Paleobiology | volume = 39 | issue = 2 | url = http://www.psjournals.org/doi/abs/10.1666/12029 | pages = 291–321 | jstor = }}</ref>。

到了石炭紀，蛸亞綱已經有多樣的形態。雖然這些類別傳統上都分類為[[箭石下綱|箭石下綱]]，但它們的分別明顯顯示它們並非近親。<ref name="Dougushaeva et al. 2007">{{Cite book en|author=Doguzhaeva, Larisa A., Mapes, Royal H., & Mutvei, Harry|year=2007|chapter=A Late Carboniferous Coleoid Cephalopod from the Mazon Creek Lagerstätte (USA) with a radula, arm hooks, mantle tissues, and ink|title=Cephalopods Present & Past: New Insights and Fresh Perspectives|editor=Landman, Neil H., Davis, Richard Arnold, & Mapes, Royal H.|pages=121-43|location=Berkeley & Los Angeles, California, USA|publisher=University of California Press}}</ref>

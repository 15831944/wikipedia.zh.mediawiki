'''咖啡湾'''（英语：Coffee Bay；{{lang-af|Koffiebaai}}）是位于[[南非|南非]][[东开普省|东开普省]][[狂野海岸|狂野海岸]]一个村镇，在[[德班|德班]]以南250公里<ref>{{cite news|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=pZ8aAAAAIBAJ&sjid=QywEAAAAIBAJ&pg=6584,1947447&dq=coffee-bay&hl=en|title=Ship sinking in rough seas|date=4 August 1991|work=The Milwaukee Journal|accessdate=2011-02-07}}</ref>，人口258人<ref>{{cite book|last1=Fitzpatrick|first1=Mary|last2=Armstrong|first2=Kate|title=South Africa, Lesotho & Swaziland|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=y4c1a6GSQ8cC&pg=PA288&dq=%22coffee+bay%22+%22eastern+cape%22&hl=en&ei=eblPTZzZBcWHcYHwnPsF&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCwQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=%22coffee%20bay%22%20%22eastern%20cape%22&f=false|year=2006|publisher=[[Lonely_Planet|Lonely Planet]]|isbn=1-74059-970-5|page=287}}</ref>。

'''微软Lumia 540'''是一款以[[Windows_Phone_8.1|Windows Phone 8.1]]开发的[[微软Lumia|微软Lumia]][[智能手机|智能手机]]，RAM为1GB，在印度、中东、非洲和亚太市场推出。<ref>[http://www.windowscentral.com/microsoft-lumia-540-dual-sim-official-5-inch-720p-display-and-1gb-ram-under-150 Microsoft Lumia 540 Dual SIM is official with 5-inch 720p display and 1GB RAM for $150]</ref> 其他内部规格与早期Lumia 5xx系列设备大致相同。<ref name="WindowsCentralMicrosoftLumia540">{{Template:Cite news|last = Baxi|first = Abhishek|title = Hands-on and first impressions: Microsoft Lumia 540.|url = http://www.windowscentral.com/first-impressions-lumia-540|newspaper = Windows (Phone) Central|date = 17 May 2015}}</ref>

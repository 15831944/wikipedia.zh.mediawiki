'''微陽症'''（{{lang-en|Micropenis}}）是指尺寸极不寻常地小的[[人類的陰莖|阴茎]]，可由于[[雄激素|雄激素]]分泌过少、胎儿时期母体摄入[[雌激素|雌激素]]过多、基因缺陷等原因导致，可通过激素疗法和手术的方式治疗。



== 诊断 ==
通常的标准是[[勃起|勃起]]后从上方量得[[人类阴茎尺寸|阴茎尺寸]]小于平均长度2.5个方差，<ref name="pmid7366061">{{cite journal |author=Lee PA |title=Micropenis. I. Criteria, etiologies and classification |journal=The Johns Hopkins medical journal |volume=146 |issue=4 |pages=156–63 |year=1980 |pmid=7366061 |doi= |name-list-format=vanc|author2=Mazur T |author3=Danish R |display-authors=3 |last4=Amrhein |first4=J |last5=Blizzard |first5=RM |last6=Money |first6=J |last7=Migeon |first7=CJ}}</ref>或小于7厘米（对成年男子来说，平均勃起长度为12.5厘米）。<ref name="ScienceDaily04">ScienceDaily.com (2004). "[http://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2004/12/041206205001.htm Surgeons Pinch More Than An Inch From The Arm To Rebuild A Micropenis] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071001004732/http://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2004/12/041206205001.htm |date=2007-10-01 }}," 6 Dec. 2004, URL accessed 2 April 2012.</ref>该症在出生之后通常就能很快确诊。大约0.6%的男性有小阴茎的症状。<ref name="ScienceDaily04"/>

== 成因 ==
[[File:Micropenis_erect_with_ruler.jpg|thumb]]
胎儿期间[[雄激素|雄激素]]分泌不足或作用减少是造成小阴茎的最主要的原因。造成这种雄激素异常的病症有睾丸发育异常、[[克氏综合征|克氏综合征]]、{{tsl|en|Leydig cell hypoplasia|睾丸间质细胞发育不全}}等，特别是[[睾酮|睾酮]]或双氢睾酮合成异常（{{tsl|en|17,20-lyase deficiency|17、20-裂解酶缺陷症}}、{{tsl|en|5-alpha-reductase deficiency|5α-还原酶缺乏症}}）、{{tsl|en|androgen insensitivity syndrome|雄激素不敏感综合征}}、[[腦下垂體|腦下垂體]]刺激异常（{{tsl|en|gonadotropin|促性腺}}缺乏症）等其他先天性[[性腺功能低下症|性腺功能低下症]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=Micropenis 是什麼？|url=https://www.news-medical.net/health/What-is-Micropenis.aspx|website=News-Medical.net|accessdate=2018-01-27|language=en|date=2012-06-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170905185858/https://www.news-medical.net/health/What-is-Micropenis.aspx|archivedate=2017-09-05}}</ref>此外，小阴茎也可能因为一些不涉及到[[性染色体|性染色体]]的基因缺陷综合征而引起。

胎儿时期若母亲服用了一些调节雌激素的{{tsl|en|fertility medication|受孕药品}}（如[[己烯雌酚|己烯雌酚]]），也会造成阴茎异常或长度小于正常尺寸的情况。<ref>{{cite news|author=Center for Disease Control|title=DES Update: Consumers|url=https://www.cdc.gov/des/consumers/about/effects_sons.html|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140317014942/http://www.cdc.gov/DES/CONSUMERS/about/effects_sons.html|archivedate=2014-03-17}}</ref>

== 治疗 ==

=== 荷尔蒙治疗 ===

在胎儿时期和童年时期期间，阴茎的生长速度会受到[[睾酮|睾酮]]的强烈影响，[[生长激素|生长激素]]对此也有一定程度的干预。

小阴茎症状的病因无论是什么，如果它在婴儿时期就能确诊的话，通常医生会开出与睾酮有关的药<ref name="pmid15201804">{{cite journal |vauthors=Ishii T, Sasaki G, Hasegawa T, Sato S, Matsuo N, Ogata T |title=Testosterone enanthate therapy is effective and independent of SRD5A2 and AR gene polymorphisms in boys with micropenis |journal=J. Urol. |volume=172 |issue=1 |pages=319–24 |year=2004 |pmid=15201804 |doi=10.1097/01.ju.0000129005.84831.1e}}</ref>（一般疗程不超过三个月）。这一治疗方法通常会让阴茎稍作增长，从而增加青春期时阴茎进一步成长的几率，但是一般很难达到正常的大小。在童年时期一般不会开睾酮方面的药，以免过早出现男性的[[第二性征|第二性征]]、增加[[骨龄|骨龄]]等。（此外，有迹象表明，过早开出睾酮的药物可能会导致成年后阴茎变短<ref name="pmid7609189">{{cite journal |vauthors=McMahon DR, Kramer SA, Husmann DA |title=Micropenis: does early treatment with testosterone do more harm than good? |journal=J. Urol. |volume=154 |issue=2 Pt 2 |pages=825–9 |year=1995 |pmid=7609189|doi=10.1016/S0022-5347(01)67175-1}}</ref>，但也有后续研究认为使用荷尔蒙并不会导致此类现象。<ref name=Bin-Abbas>{{cite journal|last1=Bin-Abbas|first1=B.|last2=Conte|first2=F. A.|last3=Grumbach|first3=M. M.|last4=Kaplan|first4=S. L.|title=Congenital hypogonadotropic hypogonadism and micropenis: effect of testosterone treatment on adult penile size why sex reversal is not indicated|journal=The Journal of Pediatrics|date=1999|volume=134|issue=5|pages=579–583|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10228293|issn=0022-3476|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170801083041/https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10228293|archivedate=2017-08-01}}</ref>）

荷尔蒙疗法在青春期后可继续，但仅限于患有性腺机能减退的男性。和[[人类身高|身高]]类似，阴茎在青春期结束后会停止发育，此时再摄入睾酮并不会使得阴茎进一步增长（或者增长幅度很小）。

=== 手术 ===
有鉴于荷尔蒙很难让阴茎增长至平均大小，有些患者会采取和{{tsl|en|phalloplasty|阴茎成形术}}类似的手术——[[阴茎增长术|阴茎增长术]]。但此类手术通常仅限于荷尔蒙疗法失败的患者<ref name=Wiygul>{{cite journal|last1=Wiygul|first1=Jeremy|last2=Palmer|first2=Lane S.|title=Micropenis|journal=The Scientific World JOURNAL|date=2011|volume=11|pages=1462–1469|doi=10.1100/tsw.2011.135|url=https://www.hindawi.com/journals/tswj/2011/348457/abs/|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180129004745/https://www.hindawi.com/journals/tswj/2011/348457/abs/|archivedate=2018-01-29}}</ref>，且成功率不高，因而并没有推广开来，也很少有在童年时期使用的案例。此类手术的方法很多，比如在阴茎皮下植入皮下软硅胶<ref>{{cite web|title=Penis Implants Exist Now, and They Start at a Size Large|url=http://www.cosmopolitan.com/sex-love/news/a52737/penis-implants-are-here-and-they-start-at-a-size-large/|website=[[柯夢波丹|柯夢波丹]]|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160225172053/http://www.cosmopolitan.com/sex-love/news/a52737/penis-implants-are-here-and-they-start-at-a-size-large/|archivedate=2016-02-25}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=The Bionic Dick Is Here|url=https://www.gq.com/story/penis-enlargement-surgery?|publisher=[[GQ|GQ]]|author=Amy Wallace|date=2016-01-26|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170927055832/https://www.gq.com/story/penis-enlargement-surgery|archivedate=2017-09-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=New implant extends a penis by TWO INCHES and lasts for life|url=http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/features/7014522/Surgeon-develops-silicone-implant-that-extends-a-penis-by-TWO-INCHES-and-lasts-for-life.html|publisher=The Sun|accessdate=2016-03-24|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160321105543/http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/features/7014522/Surgeon-develops-silicone-implant-that-extends-a-penis-by-TWO-INCHES-and-lasts-for-life.html|archivedate=2016-03-21}}</ref>。术后，有部分患者报告称对阴茎的外观不太满意，但大部分患者都有着足够的性功能。<ref name=Wiygul />

此外，亦有患者会进行性别转换手术，将阴茎切除并以女性身份继续生活下去，<ref name=Bin-Abbas />但由于此跨性别手术常常在婴儿时期进行，因而没有征得患者本人同意，从而为患者在生活上造成了困扰。<ref>{{cite journal|author1=Kenneth Kipnis|author2=Milton Diamond|title=Pacific Center for Sex and Society - Pediatric Ethics and the Surgical Assignment of Sex|journal=The Journal of Clinical Ethics|date=Winter 1998|volume=9|issue=4|page=398-410|url=http://www.hawaii.edu/PCSS/biblio/articles/1961to1999/1998-pediatric-ethics.html|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170806192617/http://www.hawaii.edu/PCSS/biblio/articles/1961to1999/1998-pediatric-ethics.html|archivedate=2017-08-06}}</ref>

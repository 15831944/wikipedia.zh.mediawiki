'''溼實驗室'''（Wet Lab）是一個科學概念，與「[[乾實驗室|乾實驗室]]」（Dry Lab）相對。溼實驗室相比乾實驗室，在進行實驗時，需要用到較多的[[化學試劑|化學試劑]]，因而得名。相比之下，乾實驗室則注重通過各種儀器進行計算，以歸納出實驗材料的物理模型<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.arenaconsultants.net/downloads/ARTICALS/Basic%20Architectural%20Design%20Considerations%20for%20a%20Laboratory.pdf|title=Basic Architectural Design Considerations for a Laboratory|last=|first=|date=|website=|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2018-01-30}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://agsci.psu.edu/students/careers/career-resources/laboratory-research-skills|title=Laboratory/Research Skills (Current Students)|website=Current Students (Penn State College of Ag Sciences)|language=en-us|access-date=2018-01-30}}</ref>。與乾實驗室相比，溼實驗室所在的房間需要經過專門設計，對安全防護的要求相對較高，實驗室成員入室前，一般要經過專門培訓<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Quinn|first=T. Alexander|last2=Kohl|first2=Peter|date=2013-03-15|title=Combining wet and dry research: experience with model development for cardiac mechano-electric structure-function studies|url=https://academic.oup.com/cardiovascres/article/97/4/601/282874|journal=Cardiovascular Research|language=en|volume=97|issue=4|pages=601–611|doi=10.1093/cvr/cvt003|issn=0008-6363}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ehs.research.uiowa.edu/52-general-laboratory-safety-procedures|title=5.2   General Laboratory Safety Procedures {{!}} Environmental Health and Safety|website=ehs.research.uiowa.edu|language=en|access-date=2018-01-30}}</ref>。

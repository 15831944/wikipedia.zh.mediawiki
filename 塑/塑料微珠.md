'''塑料微珠'''（{{lang-en|Microbeads}}）是一类-{zh-cn:通过;zh-tw:透過;}-工业生产制成的、直径小于1毫米的固体塑料微粒。<ref>Arthur et al. (eds.). 2009. [http://marinedebris.noaa.gov/file/2192/download?token=5dvqb-YY Proceedings of the International Research Workshop on the Occurrence, Effects, and Fate of Microplastic Marine Debris.] {{Wayback|url=http://marinedebris.noaa.gov/file/2192/download?token=5dvqb-YY |date=20170902133847 }} National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration Technical Memorandum. NOS-OR&R-30</ref>这些微粒通常由[[聚乙烯|聚乙烯]]制成，但也有部分由[[聚丙烯|聚丙烯]]、[[聚苯乙烯|聚苯乙烯]]等材料制成。因其有{{tsl|en|exfoliation (cosmetology)|去角质|去除角质与死皮}}之效果，故在牙膏等{{tsl|en|personal care|个人护理}}产品中有广泛应用。[[生物医学|生物医学]]以及医疗卫生科学领域也会使用塑料微珠。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b07lfkq2|title=Microbeads, Meal kits, You and Yours - BBC Radio 4|website=BBC|access-date=2018-03-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160802072708/http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b07lfkq2|archive-date=2016-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>市售的一支磨砂洗面奶中可能就含有超过30万颗的塑料微珠。<ref>{{cite news|title=新西兰对塑料微珠发出禁令，“塑料微珠禁令风”何时刮到中国？|url=http://www.sohu.com/a/165612573_99919252|work=搜狐网|date=2017-08-18|access-date=2018-03-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180319003932/http://www.sohu.com/a/165612573_99919252|archive-date=2018-03-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>

塑料微珠易造成[[塑料颗粒水污染|塑料颗粒水污染]]，影响水生生物从而造成{{tsl|en|environmental hazard|环境灾害|}}。塑料微粒在水中可以残留数千年而不消失，即便是可以生物降解的类型，也很难在短时间内降解。<ref>{{cite journal|author1=United Nations Environment Programme|title=Marine plastic debris and microplastics – Global lessons and research to inspire action and guide policy change|url=https://wedocs.unep.org/rest/bitstreams/11700/retrieve|journal=|author=|access-date=2018-03-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180126125408/https://wedocs.unep.org/rest/bitstreams/11700/retrieve|archive-date=2018-01-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>

在美国，{{tsl|en|Microbead-Free Waters Act 2015|2015年无微珠水域法案|}}分阶段禁止了塑料微珠在水洗化妆品中的使用。<ref name="H.R.1321 - Microbead-Free Waters Act of 2015">{{cite web|title=H.R.1321 - Microbead-Free Waters Act of 2015|url=https://www.congress.gov/bill/114th-congress/house-bill/1321|website=Congress.gov|publisher=Congress.gov|accessdate=2017-07-14|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170701095905/https://www.congress.gov/bill/114th-congress/house-bill/1321|archive-date=2017-07-01|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=输美化妆品企业关注《2015年无微珠水域法案》的实施|url=http://www.cixi.gov.cn/art/2016/3/30/art_14726_1290239.html|work=[[慈溪市|慈溪市]]政府信息公开|access-date=2018-03-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180318184616/http://www.cixi.gov.cn/art/2016/3/30/art_14726_1290239.html|archive-date=2018-03-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>台湾已经立法禁止塑料微珠的生产与进口。<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://chemicalwatch.com/asiahub/58391/ |title=存档副本 |access-date=2018-03-18 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180316214432/https://chemicalwatch.com/asiahub/58391/ |archive-date=2018-03-16 |dead-url= }}</ref>[[中国内地|中国内地]]的业内人士称内地的塑料微珠使用量不大，且已经大幅减少，但2018年底前[[中华人民共和国环保部|环保部]]应会有相关法规出台。而在香港，[[无塑海洋|无塑海洋]]等环保组织也在努力使香港业界各企业开始停止使用塑料微珠。<ref>{{cite news|title=英国将禁售含塑料微珠个护产品，中国相关禁令或于今年出台-新华网|url=http://www.xinhuanet.com/fashion/2018-01/22/c_1122293019.htm|work=新华网|date=2018-01-22|access-date=2018-03-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180318183725/http://www.xinhuanet.com/fashion/2018-01/22/c_1122293019.htm|archive-date=2018-03-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>

==用途与归类==

塑料微珠是指工业生产得到的直径小于1[[毫米|毫米]]的固体塑料颗粒。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.cnn.com/2015/09/19/us/8-trillion-microbeads-pollute-water-daily-irpt/index.html | title=8 trillion microbeads pollute U.S. aquatic habitats daily | publisher=CNN | date=2015-09-19 | access-date=2015-09-20 | author=Imam, Jareen | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150920034541/http://www.cnn.com/2015/09/19/us/8-trillion-microbeads-pollute-water-daily-irpt/index.html | archive-date=2015-09-20 | dead-url=no }}</ref>这类塑料通常由[[聚乙烯|聚乙烯]]、[[聚丙烯|聚丙烯]]和[[聚苯乙烯|聚苯乙烯]]等常见塑料的微粒组成。<ref>{{cite web|title=Microbeads|url=http://www.5gyres.org/microbeads/|publisher=5 Gyres|accessdate=2015-10-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151004002240/http://www.5gyres.org/microbeads/|archive-date=2015-10-04|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="envca">{{cite web|url=http://www.ec.gc.ca/ese-ees/default.asp?lang=En&n=ADDA4C5F-1|title=Microbeads – A Science Summary|date=July 2015|publisher=Environment Canada|accessdate=2015-10-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151003211240/http://www.ec.gc.ca/ese-ees/default.asp?lang=En&n=ADDA4C5F-1|archive-date=2015-10-03|dead-url=no}}</ref>

牙膏、洗面奶等{{tsl|en|personal care|个人护理|}}产品中会加入塑料微珠，将其作为去角质剂使用。<ref name="smith">{{cite web|url=http://www.smithsonianmag.com/smart-news/five-things-know-about-congress-vote-ban-microbeads-180957501/|title=Five Things to Know About Congress' Vote to Ban Microbeads|accessdate=2015-12-10|author=Danny Lewis|date=2015-12-10|work=Smithsonian magazine|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151212180548/http://www.smithsonianmag.com/smart-news/five-things-know-about-congress-vote-ban-microbeads-180957501/|archive-date=2015-12-12|dead-url=no}}</ref>因其颗粒直径相对统一，日用品（如沐浴乳、洗面奶等）中加入的塑料微珠产生了[[球向效应|球向效应]]，增加了润滑性，使得此类产品使用起来会有丝滑的感觉。化妆品中常加入彩色的塑料微珠以增加其视觉吸引力。<ref>[http://www.cosmeticsandtoiletries.com/formulating/ingredient/pigment/89521652.html Solid Polyethylene Microspheres for effects in color cosmetics] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120304184713/http://www.cosmeticsandtoiletries.com/formulating/ingredient/pigment/89521652.html |date=2012-03-04 }} Cosmetics and Toiletries.com, April 2010</ref>此外，[[非处方药|非处方药]]（即OTC药物{{notetag|美国等国家非处方药的定义较广，部分个人护理产品也可能被视为非处方药。
<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cga.ct.gov/2015/ENVdata/Tmy/2015HB-05286-R000311-Sean%20Moore,%20Consumer%20Healthcare%20Products%20Association-TMY.PDF|title=HEALTHCARE PRODUCTS ASSOCIATION concerning HOUSE BILL 5286 presented to the JOINT ENVIRONMENT COMMITTEE STATE OF CONNECTICUT|access-date=2018-03-21|author=CHPA|date=2015-03-11|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170222223806/https://www.cga.ct.gov/2015/envdata/tmy/2015HB-05286-R000311-Sean%20Moore,%20Consumer%20Healthcare%20Products%20Association-TMY.PDF|archive-date=2017-02-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>
}}）中也可能加入塑料微珠。

{{tsl|en|microbead (research)|塑料微珠 (科研)|部分塑料微珠}}在显微镜检查、流体成像、流体分析、流程故障检查中有所应用。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pcimag.com/Articles/Feature_Article/BNP_GUID_9-5-2006_A_10000000000000723862|title=Opaque Polyethylene Microspheres for Coatings Applications|publisher=|access-date=2018-03-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111130022450/http://www.pcimag.com/Articles/Feature_Article/BNP_GUID_9-5-2006_A_10000000000000723862|archive-date=2011-11-30|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://consumerist.com/2015/12/29/say-goodbye-to-microbeads-president-signs-act-to-ban-microscopic-plastic-particles/|title=Say Goodbye To Microbeads: President Signs Act To Ban Microscopic Plastic Particles|last=Kieler|first=Ashlee|date=2015-12-29|website=Consumerist|access-date=2016-06-13|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160811073043/https://consumerist.com/2015/12/29/say-goodbye-to-microbeads-president-signs-act-to-ban-microscopic-plastic-particles/|archive-date=2016-08-11|dead-url=no}}</ref>

==环境影响==

=== 来源与分布 ===
一般情况下，[[污水處理|污水處理]]设施的滤网无法将被冲刷进下水道的塑料微珠滤出，从而使其进入河流水道，最终导致[[塑料颗粒水污染|塑料颗粒水污染]]。<ref>{{cite journal| last2=Sewell|first2=M.A.|date=2009|title=Contributing to marine pollution by washing your face: microplastics in facial cleansers|journal=Marine Pollution Bulletin| volume=58| issue=8| pages=1225–1228| doi=10.1016/j.marpolbul.2009.04.025|last1=Fendall|first1=L.S.}}</ref>在美国，塑料微珠已污染[[五大湖|五大湖]]，湖面的微塑料颗粒密度非常高，尤其是[[伊利湖|伊利湖]]。据[[纽约州立大学|纽约州立大学]]研究，五大湖表层平均每平方英里就悬浮着1500至110万个微塑料颗粒。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.pbs.org/newshour/bb/tiny-plastic-microbeads-pile-problems-great-lakes/|title=Plastic microbeads pile up into problems for the Great Lakes|website=PBS NewsHour|language=en-US|access-date=2016-06-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160620003659/http://www.pbs.org/newshour/bb/tiny-plastic-microbeads-pile-problems-great-lakes/|archive-date=2016-06-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>

=== 潜在危害性 ===
塑料微珠可以吸收并富集[[农药|农药]]和[[多環芳香烴|多環芳香烴]]等[[污染物|污染物]]。<ref name="smith" /><ref>{{Cite news|last=Johnston|first=Christopher|title=Personal Grooming Products May Be Harming Great Lakes Marine Life.|url=http://www.scientificamerican.com/article/microplastic-pollution-in-the-great-lakes/|date=2013-06-25|access-date=2016-02-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160129074728/http://www.scientificamerican.com/article/microplastic-pollution-in-the-great-lakes/|archive-date=2016-01-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>一项研究显示，微塑料的摄入对动物幼体伤害有限。<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Kaposi|first=Katrina L.|last2=Mos|first2=Benjamin|last3=Kelaher|first3=Brendan P.|last4=Dworjanyn|first4=Symon A.|date=2014-01-01|title=Ingestion of microplastic has limited impact on a marine larva|journal=Environmental Science & Technology|volume=48|issue=3|pages=1638–1645|doi=10.1021/es404295e|issn=1520-5851 |pmid=24341789|bibcode=2014EnST...48.1638K}}</ref>但也有研究显示，吞食过塑料的鲈鱼会做出异常行为，导致其无法注意到捕食者而更难存活。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/fish-eat-microplastic-microbeads_us_5751ed11e4b0ed593f1472c1|title=Fish Freaking LOVE To Eat Plastic, And That's A Problem|date=2016-06-03|website=The Huffington Post|access-date=2016-06-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160614233126/http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/fish-eat-microplastic-microbeads_us_5751ed11e4b0ed593f1472c1|archive-date=2016-06-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>小型鱼类、两栖类、鸟类、大型哺乳类动物等几类动物都可能误食塑料。误食之可能造成其中毒，这些毒物亦可通过[[食物链|食物链]]不断富集，从而危害其他物种。通过该途径在食物链中转移的有害物质包括[[多氯聯苯|多氯聯苯]]（PCBs）、[[DDT|DDT]]、[[多環芳香烴|多環芳香烴]](PAHs)等[[疏水性|疏水性]]污染物。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ag.ny.gov/pdfs/Microbeads_Report_5_14_14.pdf|title=Microbeads Report|access-date=2016-12-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161221012622/http://ag.ny.gov/pdfs/Microbeads_Report_5_14_14.pdf|archive-date=2016-12-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>

其危害性相关的研究有待完善，动物实验显示了这些污染物对动物行为的影响，<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Microplastic Moves Pollutants and Additives to Worms, Reducing Functions Linked to Health and Biodiversity|url=http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0960982213012530|last=Browne|first=Mark Anthony|last2=Niven|first2=Stewart J.|journal=Current Biology|issue=23|doi=10.1016/j.cub.2013.10.012|volume=23|pages=2388–2392|last3=Galloway|first3=Tamara S.|last4=Rowland|first4=Steve J.|last5=Thompson|first5=Richard C.|author=|access-date=2018-04-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180421162835/https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0960982213012530|archive-date=2018-04-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>但未有直接证据显示这些污染物可以转移到人类身上并造成影响。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Plastic and Human Health: A Micro Issue?|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.est.7b00423|last=Wright|first=Stephanie L.|last2=Kelly|first2=Frank J.|date=2017-06-07|journal=Environmental Science & Technology|issue=12|doi=10.1021/acs.est.7b00423|volume=51|pages=6634–6647|language=EN}}</ref>对塑料微粒的检测技术水平限制了相关研究。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Potential Health Impact of Environmentally Released Micro- and Nanoplastics in the Human Food Production Chain: Experiences from Nanotoxicology|url=http://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.est.5b01090|last=Bouwmeester|first=Hans|last2=Hollman|first2=Peter C. H.|date=2015-07-15|journal=Environmental Science & Technology|issue=15|doi=10.1021/acs.est.5b01090|volume=49|pages=8932–8947|language=EN|last3=Peters|first3=Ruud J. B.}}</ref>当前的诸多对塑料微珠的限制措施是一种预防性措施。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/environment/2017/sep/06/plastic-fibres-found-tap-water-around-world-study-reveals|title=Plastic fibres found in tap water around the world, study reveals|accessdate=2018-04-20|date=2017-09-05|last=Carrington|first=Damian|work=the Guardian|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180418232101/https://www.theguardian.com/environment/2017/sep/06/plastic-fibres-found-tap-water-around-world-study-reveals|archive-date=2018-04-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>

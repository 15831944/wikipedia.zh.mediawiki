'''珍妮特·温特森'''，[[英帝國司令勳章|CBE]]（{{Lang-en|'''Jeanette Winterson'''}}，{{bd|1959年|8月27日|||catIdx=Winterson, Jeanette}}）是一位[[英国|英国]][[作家|作家]]。

==早年生活==
温特森出生于[[英格兰|英格兰]][[曼彻斯特|曼彻斯特]]。<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2011/oct/28/jeanette-winterson-all-about-my-mother |title=Jeanette Winterson: all about my mother |newspaper=The Guardian |location=London |accessdate=2011-10-29 |date=2011-10-29}}</ref>在[[兰开夏郡|兰开夏郡]]的[[阿克寧頓|阿克寧頓]]长大，她打算成为一名[[五旬节运动|五旬节运动]]的[[基督教传教活动|基督教传教士]]，她6岁就开始传福音和布道。<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2000/sep/02/fiction.jeanettewinterson | location=London | work=The Guardian | title=Power surge | first=Libby | last=Brooks | date=2000-09-02}}</ref><ref>[http://www.springerlink.com/content/grn602ql627005wx/ International Journal of Sexuality and Gender Studies, Volume 6, Number 4]. SpringerLink. Retrieved on 26 August 2011.</ref>

到了16岁的温特森已经确定自己是[[女同性恋|女同性恋]]并离开家。<ref name=glbtq>{{cite web|author=Patricia Juliana Smith |title=Winterson, Jeanette (b. 1959) |url=http://www.glbtq.com/literature/winterson_j.html |work=glbtq Encyclopedia |date=2006-07-24 |accessdate=2008-12-04 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081227012032/http://www.glbtq.com/literature/winterson_j.html |archivedate=27 十二月 2008 }}</ref>不久后她来到[[阿克林顿与罗戴尔学院|阿克林顿与罗戴尔学院]]学习，靠打零工维持生计，并且考入了[[牛津大学|牛津大学]]圣凯瑟琳学院学习英语。<ref>[http://www.jeanettewinterson.com/pages/content/index.asp?PageID=207 Winterson profile] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120325155501/http://www.jeanettewinterson.com/pages/content/index.asp?PageID=207 |date=2012-03-25 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.lancashiretelegraph.co.uk/news/4288955.Amazon_sorry_for_book_sales_error_which_hit_Accrington_author/ ''Lancashire Telegraph'' "Amazon sorry for book sales error which hit Accrington author" 14 April 2009]</ref>

==个人生活==
2002年，温特森结束了她与[[BBC|BBC]]广播电台的Peggy Reynolds12年的伴侣关系。<ref>{{cite news | author=Maya Jaggi | title=Saturday Review: Profile: Jeanette Winterson  | url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2004/may/29/fiction.jeanettewinterson | work=The Guardian | accessdate=2008-12-04 | location=London | date=2004-05-29}}</ref>后来她曾与戏剧导演[[Deborah_Warner|Deborah Warner]]和治疗师[[Susie_Orbach|Susie Orbach]]有过恋情。<ref>{{cite news | author=Stuart Jeffries | title=Jeanette Winterson: 'I thought of suicide'  | url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/books/2010/feb/22/jeanette-winterson-thought-of-suicide | work=The Guardian | accessdate=2011-08-15 | location=London | date=2010-02-22}}</ref>她的小说''The Passion''灵感来自于她与她的文稿代理人[[Pat_Kavanagh_(agent)|Pat Kavanagh]]的绯闻。<ref>{{cite news | first=Dipesh  | last=Gadher | title=Lesbian novelist Jeanette Winterson planned last visit to dying ex-lover | url=http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/books/article5014564.ecel | work=The Sunday Times | date=2008-10-26 | accessdate=2011-03-17}}</ref>2015年，她与心理治疗师Susie Orbach结婚。<ref>{{cite news | author=Stuart Jeffries | title=Jeanette Winterson: 'I thought of suicide'  | url=https://www.theguardian.com/books/2010/feb/22/jeanette-winterson-thought-of-suicide | work=The Guardian | accessdate=2011-08-15 | location=London | date=2010-02-22}}</ref>

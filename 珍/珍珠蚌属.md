'''珍珠蚌屬'''（學名：{{lang|la|''Margaritifera''}}）是一個[[珠蚌目|珠蚌目]][[珍珠蚌科|珍珠蚌科]][[淡水動物|淡水]][[雙殼綱|雙殼綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]]的[[屬_(生物)|屬]]{{r|WoRMS_571966}}。

==物種==
珍珠蚌屬包括20多個現生種和化石種，分別如下{{r|WoRMS_571966|MUSSELp}}：
* ''[[Margaritifera_antiqua|Margaritifera antiqua]]'' <small>Gu & Yu, 1976</small> †：見於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]。
* ''[[Margaritifera_auricularia|Margaritifera auricularia]]'' <small>(Spengler, 1793)</small>：見於[[西南歐|西南歐]]。
* [[珠母珍珠蚌|珠母珍珠蚌]] ''[[Margaritifera_dahurica|Margaritifera dahurica]]'' <small>(Middendorff, 1850)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_falcata|Margaritifera falcata]]'' <small>([[Augustus_Addison_Gould|Gould]], 1850)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_flabellata|Margaritifera flabellata]]'' <small>(Goldfuss, 1837)</small> †
* [[Margaritifera_hembeli|Margaritifera hembeli]]'' <small>(Conrad, 1838)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_homsensis|Margaritifera homsensis]]'' <small>(I. Lea, 1865)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_laevis|Margaritifera laevis]]'' <small>(Haas, 1910)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_laosensis|Margaritifera laosensis]]'' <small>(Lea, 1863)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_margaritifera|Margaritifera margaritifera]]'' <small>(Linnaeus, 1758)</small>
** ''[[Margaritifera_margaritifera_durrovensis|Margaritifera margaritifera durrovensis]]'' <small>(Phillips, 1928)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_marocana|Margaritifera marocana]]'' <small>(Pallary, 1918)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_marrianae|Margaritifera marrianae]]'' <small>R. I. Johnson, 1983</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_monodonta|Margaritifera monodonta]]'' <small> (Say, 1829)</small>
* ''[[Margaritifera_togakushiensis|Margaritifera togakushiensis]]'' <small> Kondo & Kobayashi, 2005</small>

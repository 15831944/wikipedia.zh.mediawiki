'''珍妮麗亞·狄索薩'''（{{lang-en|'''Genelia D'Souza'''}}，{{bd|1987年|8月5日|catIdx=D’Souza, Genelia}}）是[[印度|印度]]电影女演员，主要出現在[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]和南印度電影。在2003年正式出道以前，她因和巨星[[阿米塔布·巴沙坎|阿米塔布·巴沙坎]]拍摄的广告而走紅。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/entertainment/tamil/movies/entertainment/tamil/movies/photo-features/genelia-dsouza-lesser-known-facts/photostory/48329430.cms “Genelia D’Souza: Lesser Known Facts”]. Times of India. 03-08-2015.</ref>狄索薩之后較成功的電影包括：《Bommarillu》、《Tujhe Meri Kasam》和《Jaane Tu... Ya Jaane Na》等。<ref>[https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/im-ready-for-a-comeback-says-genelia-dsouza/ “I’m ready for a comeback, says Genelia D’Souza”]. The Indian Express. 25-02-2015.</ref>

她的丈夫是同为演员的[[列特·狄舒穆|列特·狄舒穆]]（Ritesh Deshmukh），两人共育有两个儿子。<ref>[https://www.ndtv.com/entertainment/genelia-dsouza-and-riteish-deshmukhs-pic-makes-us-believe-in-love-actually-1866274 “Genelia D'Souza And Riteish Deshmukh's Pic Makes Us Believe In Love, Actually”]. NDTV. 12-06-2018.</ref>

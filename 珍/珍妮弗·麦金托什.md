'''珍妮弗·麦金托什'''（{{lang-en|Jennifer McIntosh}}，{{bd|1991年|6月17日||}}）生于苏格兰爱丁堡，是一名英国女子射击运动员<ref name="walesonline">{{cite web |url=http://www.walesonline.co.uk/sports/london-olympics-2012/olympic-news/2012/07/27/can-great-britain-s-jennifer-mcintosh-win-the-first-gold-medal-of-olympics-2012-91466-31486225/ |title=Can Great Britain's Jennifer McIntosh win the first gold medal of Olympics 2012 |date=2012-07-27 |accessdate=2012-07-28 |work=Wales Online}}</ref>，曾就读于道勒学院（{{lang|en|Dollar Academy}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dollaracademy.org.uk/assets/000/007/811/Rector's_Newsletter_May_2018_Print_original.pdf|title=Rector's Newsletter May 2018|date=2018-05|accessdate=2018-11-16}}</ref>。她的母亲雪莉·麦金托什（{{lang|en|Shirley McIntosh}}）曾获得四枚英联邦运动会奖牌<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.falkirkherald.co.uk/sport/seonaid-mcintosh-wins-gold-at-world-championships-1-4795854|title=Seonaid McIntosh wins gold at World Championships|publisher=Falkirk Herald|date=2018-09-06|accessdate=2018-11-16}}</ref>。父亲唐纳德·麦金托什（{{lang|en|Donald McIntosh}}）也曾参加[[2002年英联邦运动会|2002年英联邦运动会]]，退役后担任英国射击队的教练<ref>{{cite web |author1=Neil Drysdale |title=Firing a salute to the shooting star who excelled with Scotland's cartridge family |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180921082749/https://www.pressandjournal.co.uk/fp/sport/other/1564563/firing-a-salute-to-the-shooting-star-who-excelled-with-scotlands-cartridge-family/ |archivedate=2018-09-21 |date=2018-09-18}}</ref>。妹妹[[斯奥妮达·麦金托什|斯奥妮达·麦金托什]]同样也是射击运动员<ref>{{cite news|title=Seonaid McIntosh emulates sister with European Shooting Championships

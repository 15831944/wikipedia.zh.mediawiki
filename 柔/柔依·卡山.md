'''柔依·史威考德·卡山'''（{{lang-en|Zoe Swicord Kazan}}，{{bd|1983年|9月9日}}）<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.playbill.com/celebritybuzz/article/137395-PLAYBILLCOMS-CUE-A-Zoe-Kazan- |title = CUE & A: Zoe Kazan | work = Playbill | date = 2010-03-09}}</ref><ref name=FamilySearch-CABirthIndex-1983>{{cite web|title=Zoe Swicord Kazan - California, Birth Index|url=https://familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:VLTK-P2D|website=FamilySearch|accessdate=2014-10-21}}</ref><ref name="BroadwaySpace.com">{{cite web | url = http://www.broadwayspace.com/page/30-under-30-zoe-kazan | title = 30 Under 30 – Zoe Kazan | publisher = BroadwaySpace.com | year = 2011 | quote = Age: 27 (09/09/83) | access-date = 2018-11-16 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180828055815/http://www.broadwayspace.com/page/30-under-30-zoe-kazan | archive-date = 2018-08-28 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>是一名[[美國|美國]]女演員、劇作家及編劇。她曾出演過多部[[百老匯|百老匯]]作品，同時也會撰寫戲劇劇本。2018年，卡山和合作夥伴[[保羅·迪諾|保羅·迪諾]]共同撰寫了迪諾的執導作《{{le|狂野生活|Wildlife (film)}}》。

== 個人生活 ==
卡山從2007年以來就持續與合作夥伴[[保羅·迪諾|保羅·迪諾]]維持著情侶關係<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2012/07/22/movies/ruby-sparks-with-zoe-kazan-and-paul-dano.html |title=Actual Couples Explore a Fantasy, the Aesthetic Sort|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|date=2012-07-20|accessdate=2015-07-03}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.smh.com.au/entertainment/movies/sydney-film-festival/love-and-mercys-paul-dano-relates-to-brian-wilsons-tortured-genius-20150601-ghdtjz.html|title=Love and Mercy's Paul Dano relates to Brian Wilson's tortured genius |work=[[The_Sydney_Morning_Herald|The Sydney Morning Herald]]|date=2015-06-04|accessdate=2015-07-03}}</ref>。兩人有一個女兒 Alma Day ，出生於2018年8月<ref>{{cite web |url=https://people.com/parents/paul-dano-zoe-kazan-welcome-daughter/ |title=Paul Dano and Zoe Kazan Welcome a Daughter |publisher=''[[People_(magazine)|People]]'' |date=2018-10-25}}</ref>。

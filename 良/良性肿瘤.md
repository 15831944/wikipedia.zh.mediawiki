'''良性肿瘤'''（英語：'''benign tumor'''）是[[肿瘤|肿瘤]]的一种，通常区别于[[恶性肿瘤|恶性肿瘤]]。良性肿瘤的生长速度缓慢，呈膨胀性生长，表面较光滑。<ref name="isbn0-443-10101-9">{{cite book |author=Wilson, Kathleen Atkins; Waugh, Anne; Chambers, Graeme; Grant, Allison; Ross, Janet |title=Ross and Wilson anatomy and physiology in health and illness |publisher=Churchill Livingstone|location=Edinburgh |year=2006 |pages=53–54 |isbn=0-443-10101-9 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref><ref name="isbn0-7613-2833-5">{{cite book |author=Nunn, Laura Silverstein; Silverstein, Alvin; Silverstein, Virginia B.|title=Cancer|publisher=Twenty-First Century Books |location=Brookfield, Conn |year=2006|pages=11–12|isbn=0-7613-2833-5 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref><ref name="isbn0-7817-9516-8">{{cite book|author=David Lowell Strayer; Raphael Rubin; Rubin, Emanuel |title=Rubin's pathology: clinicopathologic foundations of medicine |publisher=Wolters Kluwer/Lippincott Williams & Wilkins |location=Philadelphia |year=2008 |pages=138–139|isbn=0-7817-9516-8 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref>

==病理==
由于[[良性|良性]]瘤体在局部会不断增大（一般无全身症状），压迫周围的正常组织，但并不侵入邻近的正常组织内，瘤体多呈球形、结节状。瘤体周围常形成包膜，因此与正常组织分界明显，<ref name="isbn0-321-31198-1">{{cite book|author=Ober, William B.; Martini, Frederic |title=Fundamentals of anatomy & physiology |publisher=Pearson Benjamin Cummings |location=San Francisco |year=2006 |pages= |isbn=0-321-31198-1 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref>用手触摸，推之可移动。除非长在要害部位，良性肿瘤一般不会致命，大多数可被完全切除，很少有复发。虽说良性肿瘤不会致命，但是他们可能会[[基因突变|基因突变]]成为[[恶性肿瘤|恶性肿瘤]]，<ref name="pmid1911211">{{cite journal |author=Clark WH |title=Tumour progression and the nature of cancer |journal=Br. J. Cancer |volume=64|issue=4|pages=631–44 |date=October 1991 |pmid=1911211|pmc=1977704 |doi= 10.1038/bjc.1991.375|url=}}</ref>这就是有时需要用[[手术|手术]]移除[[肿瘤|肿瘤]]的原因。<ref name="isbn0-321-27045-2">{{cite book|author=Reece, Jane; Campbell, Neil; Urry, Lisa |title=Biology |publisher=Pearson Benjamin Cummings |location=San Francisco |year=2005 |page= 232|isbn=0-321-27045-2 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref>

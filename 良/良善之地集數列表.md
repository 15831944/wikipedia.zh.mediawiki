《[[良善之地|良善之地]]》（{{lang-en|''The Good Place''}}）是一部由{{le|邁克爾·舒爾|Michael Schur}}開創，於2016年9月19日在[[全國廣播公司|NBC]]開播的美國奇幻[[情景喜劇|情境喜劇]]。<ref name="Premiere">{{cite web |url= http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/nbc-sets-fall-premiere-dates-903206|title=NBC Sets Fall Premiere Dates, Gives 'Good Place' a Choice Preview Slot |work=[[荷里活報道|好萊塢報導]]|first=Michael|last=O'Connell|date=2016-05-15|accessdate=2016-05-18}}</ref>2015年8月13日，[[全國廣播公司|NBC]]於[[電視評論家協會|電視評論家協會]]上，直接宣布系列預訂13集。<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/nbc-orders-comedy-series-mike-815187|title=NBC Orders Comedy Series From Mike Schur, Tina Fey and Robert Carlock Pilot|author1=Lesley Goldberg|publisher=好萊塢報導|date=2015-08-13|accessdate=2016-09-20}}</ref>2017年1月30日，NBC宣布續訂第2季。<ref name="Season 2 Renewal">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/01/the-good-place-renewed-season-2-by-nbc-1201897567/|title=‘The Good Place’ Renewed For Season 2 By NBC|last=Andreeva|first=Nellie|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=2017-01-30|accessdate=2017-01-30}}</ref>11月21日，NBC宣布續訂第3季，集數同樣維持13集。<ref name="Season 3 Renewal">{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/11/the-good-place-renewed-season-3-nbc-1202213247/|title=‘The Good Place’ Renewed For Season 3 By NBC|author=Denise Petski|work=Deadline.com|date=2017-11-21|accessdate=2017-11-21}}</ref>

<!-- 定義及症狀 -->
'''良性陣發性姿勢性眩暈'''（Benign paroxysmal positional vertigo，簡稱'''BPPV'''）為一種[[內耳|內耳]]誘發的疾病<ref name=NIH2015/>，患者會有重複性短暫[[眩暈|眩暈]]的症狀，頭部移動時會感到天旋地轉<ref name=Bh2008>{{cite journal|last1=Bhattacharyya|first1=N|last2=Baugh|first2=RF|last3=Orvidas|first3=L|last4=Barrs|first4=D|last5=Bronston|first5=LJ|last6=Cass|first6=S|last7=Chalian|first7=AA|last8=Desmond|first8=AL|last9=Earll|first9=JM|last10=Fife|first10=TD|last11=Fuller|first11=DC|last12=Judge|first12=JO|last13=Mann|first13=NR|last14=Rosenfeld|first14=RM|last15=Schuring|first15=LT|last16=Steiner|first16=RW|last17=Whitney|first17=SL|last18=Haidari|first18=J|last19=American Academy of Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery|first19=Foundation|title=Clinical practice guideline: benign paroxysmal positional vertigo.|journal=Otolaryngology--head and neck surgery : official journal of American Academy of Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery|date=November 2008|volume=139|issue=5 Suppl 4|pages=S47-81|pmid=18973840|doi=10.1016/j.otohns.2008.08.022|laysummary=http://eurekalert.org/pub_releases/2008-11/aaoo-edr102808.php |laysource=AAO-HNS |laydate=2008-11-01}}</ref>，甚至連就寢翻身時都會有暈動感<ref name=NIH2015/>。每次眩暈通常不會超過一分鐘<ref name=NIH2015/>，患者常會伴隨[[惡心|惡心]]的症狀<ref name=PMH2014/>。本疾病為導致眩暈的疾病中最常見的一種<ref name=Bh2008/><ref name=Dick2014>{{cite book|last1=Dickson|first1=Gretchen|title=Primary Care ENT, An Issue of Primary Care: Clinics in Office Practice, Volume 41, Issue 1 of The Clinics: Internal Medicine|date=2014|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9780323287173|page=115|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=JczaAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA115|accessdate=25 July 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160815220701/https://books.google.ca/books?id=JczaAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA115|archivedate=15 August 2016|df=}}</ref>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
良性陣發性姿勢性眩暈可能肇因於[[頭部外傷|頭部創傷]]，或單純發生於年長者<ref name=NIH2015/>，確切原因通常無法找到<ref name=NIH2015/>，可能的致病機轉是內耳的[[耳石|耳石]]鬆動<ref name=NIH2015/>，本疾病與{{link-en|迷路炎|labyrinthitis}}及[[美尼爾氏症|美尼爾氏症]]同屬於{{le|平衡障礙|balance disorder}}<ref name=NIH2015>{{cite web|title=Balance Disorders|url=https://www.nidcd.nih.gov/health/balance-disorders|date=August 10, 2015|website=NIDCD|accessdate=25 July 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160727230246/https://www.nidcd.nih.gov/health/balance-disorders|archivedate=27 July 2016|df=}}</ref>。診斷本疾病時通常會進行{{le|頭位變換眼振檢查|Dix–Hallpike test}}，若為此疾病患者應會出現[[眼球震顫|眼球震顫]]的情形，同時必須排除其他可能的原因<ref name=Bh2008/>。通常患者不需進行[[醫學影像|醫學照影]]<ref name=Bh2008/>。

<!-- 預防、治療及預後 -->
本疾病的常見治療方式包含{{le|Epley氏耳石復位術|Epley maneuver}}或{{le|Brandt–Daroff二氏耳石復位術|Brandt–Daroff exercises}}<ref name=NIH2015/><ref name="Cochrane2014">{{cite journal|last1=Hilton|first1=MP|last2=Pinder|first2=DK|title=The Epley (canalith repositioning) manoeuvre for benign paroxysmal positional vertigo.|journal=The Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews|date=8 December 2014|volume=12|pages=CD003162|pmid=25485940|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD003162.pub3}}</ref>，藥物有時會被用於改善[[惡心|惡心]]的問題<ref name=PMH2014/>，有證據顯示{{le|倍他司汀|betahistine}}可能有幫助，但一般不須用藥<ref name=Bh2008/><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Murdin|first1=L|last2=Hussain|first2=K|last3=Schilder|first3=AG|title=Betahistine for symptoms of vertigo.|journal=The Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews|date=21 June 2016|issue=6|pages=CD010696|pmid=27327415|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD010696.pub2}}</ref>。良性陣發性姿勢性眩暈並非嚴重疾病<ref name=PMH2014/>，通常在一至二周後就會消解<ref name=PMH2014/>。但在某些人身上可能會持續再發<ref name=PMH2014>{{cite web|title=Positional vertigo: Overview|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmedhealth/PMH0072680/|website=PubMed Health|accessdate=25 July 2016|language=en|date=30 January 2014}}</ref>。

<!-- 歷史、流行病學及文化 -->
[[羅伯特·巴拉尼|羅伯特·巴拉尼]]在1921年第一次描述了此病的發生<ref>{{cite book|last1=Daroff|first1=Robert B.|title=Bradley's neurology in clinical practice.|date=2012|publisher=Elsevier Saunders|location=Philadelphia, PA|isbn=9781455728077|edition=6th|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=mpnaPQd_fZsC&pg=PT3022|chapter=Chapter 37|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161221002531/https://books.google.ca/books?id=mpnaPQd_fZsC&pg=PT3022|archivedate=2016-12-21|df=}}</ref>。約有2.4％的人在某個時間點受到影響<ref name=Bh2008/>。活到80歲以上的人，有10%曾受到影響<ref name=Dick2014/>，發病一般在50到70歲之間.<ref name=Dick2014/>，。而良性陣發性姿勢性眩暈對女性的影響是男性的兩倍<ref name=PMH2014/>。

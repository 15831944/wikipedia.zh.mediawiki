'''昆明鸡血藤属'''（学名：{{lang|la|'''''Callerya'''''}}）为[[豆科|豆科]][[蝶型花亞科|蝶型花亞科]]的植物<ref name=sp2000>{{cite web |url=http://base.sp2000.cn/colchina_c13/browse_taxa.php?path=,{FCF1372B-BE6F-4AED-8A29-28F85D35DECB},{E28162C7-1394-4777-AFED-2AC929D0146E},{678195D9-5325-4963-939A-348E0D4F0728},{8CFDD410-D9AF-47C7-94E1-F72D09291148},614e7d4e-9551-4dce-957a-f83518083577,{78976E8F-CDE9-4013-AB79-F68BE2EF347A},420791b8-dce1-4f37-9c4b-92a84a21bb6c&selected_taxon=420791b8-dce1-4f37-9c4b-92a84a21bb6c |title=Callerya nitida |publisher=[[中国生物物种名录|中国生物物种名录]] |edition=2011版 |editor=[[朱相云|朱相云]] |date=2009-05-25 |accessdate=2016-04-11 |language=zh-hans }}{{dead link|date=2018年3月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。

部分物種舊屬同[[族_(生物)|族]]的[[崖豆藤属|崖豆藤属]]。
遺傳分析顯示：本屬與[[紫藤属|紫藤属]]（{{lang|la|''Wisteria''}}）有着密切的關連，其染色體均有八條，跟其他同屬[[崖豆藤族|崖豆藤族]]的其他物種不同<ref>{{cite journal|journal= American Journal of Botany|year=2000| volume=87|issue=3|pages=418–30 |url=http://www.amjbot.org/content/87/3/418.full.pdf |last1=Hu |first1=Jer-Ming |last2=Lavin |first2=Matt|last3=Wojciechowski |first3=Martin F |last4=Sanderson |first4=Michael J. | doi = 10.2307/2656638| title=Phylogenetic Systematics of the Tribe Millettieae (Leguminosae) Based on Chloroplast trnK/matK Sequences and Its Implications for Evolutionary Patterns in Papilionoideae| pmid=10719003}}</ref>。

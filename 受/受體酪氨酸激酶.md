'''受體酪氨酸激酶'''（Receptor tyrosine kinases，RTKs）為對多種多肽類[[生長因子|生長因子]]、[[細胞因子|細胞因子]]，及[[激素|激素]]具有高[[解离常数|親和性]][[細胞表面受體|細胞表面受體]]。[[人類基因組|人類]]目前已知約有90種[[酪氨酸激酶|酪氨酸激酶]][[基因|基因]]，其中有59個基因會轉譯出受體酪氨酸激酶<ref name=robinson1>{{cite journal | vauthors = Robinson DR, Wu YM, Lin SF | title = The protein tyrosine kinase family of the human genome | journal = Oncogene | volume = 19 | issue = 49 | pages = 5548–57 | date = November 2000 | pmid = 11114734 | doi = 10.1038/sj.onc.1203957 }}</ref>。

除了正常的生理功能之外，此類蛋白也在許多類[[癌症|癌細胞]]的發育和調控上扮演重要角色<ref name="Receptor tyrosine kinase signalling as a target for cancer intervention strategies">{{cite journal | vauthors = Zwick E, Bange J, Ullrich A | title = Receptor tyrosine kinase signalling as a target for cancer intervention strategies | journal = Endocrine-Related Cancer | volume = 8 | issue = 3 | pages = 161–73 | date = September 2001 | pmid = 11566607 | doi = 10.1677/erc.0.0080161 }}</ref>。此類基因的變異會活化許多一系列級聯反映，影響蛋白質的表達<ref>{{cite journal | vauthors = Lemmon MA, Schlessinger J | title = Cell signaling by receptor tyrosine kinases | journal = Cell | volume = 141 | issue = 7 | pages = 1117–34 | date = June 2010 | pmid = 20602996 | pmc = 2914105 | doi = 10.1016/j.cell.2010.06.011 }}</ref>。受體酪氨酸激酶為[[酪氨酸激酶|酪氨酸激酶]]中較大的一個蛋白質家族，此類蛋白的特點是具有疏水性的穿膜區，反之{{tsl|en|non-receptor tyrosine kinase|非受體類酪氨酸激酶|其他類的酪氨酸激酶}}則沒有穿膜區<ref name="pmid10966463">{{cite journal | vauthors = Hubbard SR, Till JH | title = Protein tyrosine kinase structure and function | journal = Annual Review of Biochemistry | volume = 69 | issue =  | pages = 373–98 | year = 2000 | pmid = 10966463 | doi = 10.1146/annurev.biochem.69.1.373 }}</ref>。

==命名==
受體酪氨酸激酶（Receptor tyrosine kinase，RTK）有時也會被稱為'''酪氨酸受體激酶'''（Tyrosine receptor kinase，TRK）或'''酪氨酸激酶受體'''（Tyrosine kinase receptor，TKR）。

'''本胆烷二酮'''（{{lang-en|Etiocholanedione}}，也称为'''5β-雄烷二酮 '''5β-androstanedione 或'''本胆烷-3,17-二酮 '''etiocholane-3,17-dione）是一种[[天然产物|天然的]][[本胆烷|本胆烷]]（5β-[[雄烷|雄烷]]）类[[甾体|甾体]]物质，和一些[[雄激素|雄激素]]如[[睾酮|睾酮]]、[[双氢睾酮|双氢睾酮]]（DHT）、[[脱氢表雄酮|脱氢表雄酮]]（DHEA）、[[雄烯二酮|雄烯二酮]]的[[内源|内源]]性[[代谢产物|代谢产物]]<ref name="HMDB">{{cite web|url=http://www.hmdb.ca/metabolites/HMDB0003769|website=hmdb.ca|title=Human Metabolome Database: Showing metabocard for Etiocholanedione (HMDB0003769)|accessdate=2018-07-13}}</ref>。它是[[雄烷二酮|5α-雄烷二酮]]（5α-Androstanedione）在5号C原子上的[[差向异构体|差向异构体]]<ref name="HMDB" />，在经过[[5β还原酶|5β还原酶]]催化后，一系列5β-甾体物质包括本胆烷二酮都不具有[[雄激素|雄激素]]活性<ref name="pmid10415598">{{cite journal | vauthors = Bradlow HL, Murphy J, Byrne JJ | title = Immunological properties of dehydroepiandrosterone, its conjugates, and metabolites | journal = Ann. N. Y. Acad. Sci. | volume = 876 | issue = | pages = 91–101 | date = June 1999 | pmid = 10415598 | doi = 10.1111/j.1749-6632.1999.tb07627.x | url = }}</ref><ref name="McKeagMoeller2007">{{cite book|author1=Douglas McKeag|author2=James L. Moeller|title=ACSM's Primary Care Sports Medicine|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=KoeRrA_-td0C&pg=PA616|year=2007|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=978-0-7817-7028-6|pages=616–}}</ref>。本胆烷二酮有一定的[[造血作用|造血作用]]<ref name="pmid10415598" />和[[體重下降|减肥作用]]<ref name="McKeagMoeller2007" /><ref name="Rippe2013">{{cite book|author=James M.

'''本·列文'''(Ben Lewin，1946年8月6日-) 是一位[[澳洲|澳洲]]-[[美国|美国]]电影导演和编剧。

== 生平 ==
本·列文生于[[波兰|波兰]]，小时候随家人移居澳洲[[墨尔本|墨尔本]]<ref name="biographies">{{cite web|url=http://www.suchmuchfilms.com/biographies.html|title=Biographies - Ben Lewin|publisher=suchmuchfilms.com|accessdate=2012-09-21|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121110064926/http://www.suchmuchfilms.com/biographies.html|archivedate=2012-11-10}}</ref>。六岁时他患上了[[小儿麻痹症|小儿麻痹症]]，导致他一直使用[[拐杖|拐杖]]生活至今<ref>{{cite news|last=Horn|first=John|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/movies/2012/01/the-sundance-film-festival-is-famous-for-discovering-fresh-talent-steven-soderbergh-quentin-tarantino-and-carey-mulligan-am.html|title=Sundance 2012: A 65-year-old takes on disability and sex in 'The Surrogate'|date=2012-01-22|publisher=''[[Los_Angeles_Times|Los Angeles Times]]''|accessdate=2012-09-21}}</ref>。他获得了[[墨尔本大学|墨尔本大学]]的[[法学|法学]]学位。1971年，他在获得可去英国的影视学院（National Film and Television School）学习电影的奖学金后放弃了[[律师|律师]]职业<ref>{{cite news|last=Hooks|first=Barbara|url=http://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1300&dat=19870414&id=2u5UAAAAIBAJ&sjid=yZIDAAAAIBAJ&pg=2027,2829773|title=A world of comedy in Acland Street|date=1987-04-14|publisher=''[[The_Age|The Age]]''|accessdate=2012-09-21}}</ref><ref name="biographies" />。毕业后，他留在英国制作电视。

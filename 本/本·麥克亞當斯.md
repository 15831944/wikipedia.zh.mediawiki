2018年，麦克亚当斯在犹他州第四选区的国会众议员选举中击败了[[共和黨_(美國)|共和党]]现任议员米娅·洛夫（Mia Love）。 在超过25万张选票中，他比对手多694票获胜。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2018/11/20/its-over-democrat-ben/|title=It’s over. == 法律事业 ==
从法学院毕业后，他在[[纽约|纽约市]]的戴維斯·波尔克&沃德韋爾（Davis Polk & Wardwell）律师事务所做过一段时间的助理律师。<ref name="UtahLawProfile"><div>本杰明·迈克尔·亚当斯:犹他大学法学院志愿教师</div></ref><ref name="McAdamsProfile Deseret">{{Cite web|author=Roche|first=Lisa Riley|title=New Utah state senator Ben McAdams is youthful but not naive|url=http://www.deseretnews.com/article/705360440/New-Utah-state-senator-Ben-McAdams-is-youthful-but-not-naive.html?pg=all|publisher=Deseret News|accessdate=February 9, 2015}}</ref> 麦克亚当斯和他的家人随后回到犹他州，在那里他进入了盐湖城的多尔西 & 惠特尼（Dorsey & Whitney）律师事务所，从事证券法工作。<ref name="McAdamsProfile Deseret" /><ref name="UtahLawProfile" /> 麦克亚当斯后来成为盐湖城市长拉尔夫·贝克尔（Ralph Becker）的高级顾问。<ref name="UtahLawProfile" />

== 犹他州参议院任期 ==
在2009年12月19日的一次特别选举中，麦克亚当斯接替斯科特·麦考伊（Scott McCoy）成为犹他州参议院第二选区的参议员。<ref>“民主党推选麦克亚当斯为立法机关议员”</ref>随后在2010年11月2日正式当选参议员，任期四年。

=== 立法记录 ===
作为一名立法者，麦克亚当斯联合发起了S.B.0150法案，这是一项旨在促进学校和阅读能力低于年级水平的小学生的家长之间交流的措施。州长加里·赫伯特（Gary Herbert）2010年3月29日签署该法案，使之成为法律。<ref>{{Cite web|title=SB0150|url=https://le.utah.gov/~2010/bills/static/SB0150.html|publisher=Utah State Legislature}}</ref>

2011年3月，麦克亚当斯提出一项法案，禁止对犹他州的[[同性戀|同性恋]]和[[跨性別|跨性别者]]在就业和住房方面歧视。他就此事举行[[聽證會|听证会]]的动议在党派投票中失败。<ref><div>《倡导者》，2011年3月2日</div></ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://archive.sltrib.com/article.php?id=51331829&itype=CMSID|author=Robert Gehrke|title=Senate: No hearing for anti-discrimination bill|work=[[The_Salt_Lake_Tribune|The Salt Lake Tribune]]|date=February 28, 2011}}</ref>盐湖城在2009年通过了一项类似的措施。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.heraldextra.com/news/local/govt-and-politics/legislature/senator-introducing-statewide-nondiscrimination-bill/article_eda9226d-a06e-581a-abfd-1e03fd61c6cd.html|publisher=Daily Herald|title=Senator introducing statewide nondiscrimination bill}}</ref>

麦克亚当斯投票反对H.B.353法案，该法案允许医务人员基于道德或宗教理由拒绝提供[[堕胎|堕胎]]服务。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://le.utah.gov/~2011/bills/static/HB0353.html|title=HB0353|work=le.utah.gov}}</ref>他还投票反对H.B. 461法案，此法案将堕胎的强制等待时间从24小时延长到72个工作小时，后来发现这项法律并没有阻止妇女堕胎。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://le.utah.gov/~2012/bills/static/HB0461.html|title=HB0461|work=le.utah.gov}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2018/07/26/despite-rep-mia-loves/|title="Utah Democrat insists he’s just as anti-abortion as his conservative opponent"|author=Richards|first=Connor|date=July 27, 2018|work=|publisher=Salt Lake Tribune|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref>麦克亚当斯说，他投票反对这些法案，是因为他认为这些法案起草得很糟糕，而不是因为他支持堕胎。<ref name="BeyondTheAttackAds">{{Cite news|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2018/10/28/beyond-attack-ads-here/|title=Beyond the attack ads: Here are the candidates' stands on issues in the Mia Love vs. Ben McAdams race|date=October 28, 2018|publisher=Salt Lake Tribune}}</ref>（参看下面的政治立场）

他被选为2016年犹他州的美国参议员及2016年犹他州州长竞选的潜在候选人，但是他没有竞选这两个职位。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.deseretnews.com/article/865626030/Poll-Josh-Romney-would-be-a-tough-opponent-for-Sen-Mike-Lee.html?pg=all|title=Poll: Josh Romney would be a tough opponent for Sen. === 无家可归者的政策 ===
他受犹他州议会委托，在盐湖县外盐湖城为一个新的无家可归者收容所选址。报纸《''Deseret News''》报道，这项任务被认为具有政治破坏性，因为无论最终选择的地点在哪，都会遇到当地预料之中的强烈反对。<ref name="DeseretHomeless">{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900010260/salt-lake-mayor-ben-mcadams-renews-pledge-to-nix-homeless-site-if-revenue-bill-fails.html|title=Salt Lake County Mayor Ben McAdams renews pledge to pull support of homeless site if revenue bill fails|publisher=Deseret News|date=February 13, 2018}}</ref>麦克亚当斯推荐了[[南盐湖城_(犹他州)|南盐湖城]]，但遭到了该市市长切丽·伍德(Cherie Wood)的反对。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/865676819/County-chooses-South-Salt-Lake-for-new-homeless-resource-center.html|title=Mayor calls plan for homeless shelter a 'lethal blow' for South Salt Lake|publisher=Deseret News|date=March 31, 2017}}</ref>此前，[[德雷珀_(犹他州)|德雷珀]]市市长特洛伊·沃克(Troy Walker)曾自愿提供德雷珀市的两个场地建造收容所，后来迫于公众压力取消了这一计划。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/865676709/I-dont-think-we-can-do-it-Draper-mayor-rescinds-homeless-sites-offer-during-raucous-public.html|publisher=Deseret News|title='I get it now': Draper mayor rescinds homeless sites offer during raucous public gathering}}</ref>

在提出推荐之前，麦克亚当斯花了两个晚上在盐湖城的街道上扮成一个无家可归者来收集信息。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2017/08/06/sl-co-mayor-ben-mcadams-posed-as-a-homeless-person-for-3-days-and-2-nights-heres-what-he-saw/|title=Mayor Ben McAdams posed as a homeless person for 3 days and 2 nights. Here’s what he saw.}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/865686230/I-didnt-feel-safe-Mayor-Ben-McAdams-describes-secret-nights-on-street-in-shelter.html|title='I didn't feel safe': Mayor Ben McAdams describes secret nights on street, in shelter|publisher=Deseret News|date=August 6, 2017}}</ref> 虽然他的任务是推荐庇护所地点，但他承诺不会支持庇护所破土动工，除非犹他州立法机构通过一项法案，<ref name="DeseretHomeless">{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900010260/salt-lake-mayor-ben-mcadams-renews-pledge-to-nix-homeless-site-if-revenue-bill-fails.html|title=Salt Lake County Mayor Ben McAdams renews pledge to pull support of homeless site if revenue bill fails|publisher=Deseret News|date=February 13, 2018}}</ref>靠其他城市筹集资金来设立基金库。

麦克亚当斯呼吁采取一种“完全不同的方法... 来解决无家可归问题”，并称无家可归“是一种顽固而复杂的社会挑战”<ref name="DeseretHomeless">{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900010260/salt-lake-mayor-ben-mcadams-renews-pledge-to-nix-homeless-site-if-revenue-bill-fails.html|title=Salt Lake County Mayor Ben McAdams renews pledge to pull support of homeless site if revenue bill fails|publisher=Deseret News|date=February 13, 2018}}</ref>

=== 税收和预算 ===
2014年他支持更新及复兴 [[动物园站_(广州)|动物园]]、艺术和公园的Zap税<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ksl.com/article/32145598/parks-and-recreation--zap-tax-on-the-2014-ballot|title=ZAP tax on the 2014 ballot|publisher=ksl.com|author=Sara Jarman|date=October 29, 2014}}</ref> 。每消費10美元，ZAP稅額為1美分。 它部分资助了190多个县级文化艺术组织，以及30个公园与游憩设施，包括Hogle动物园、Tracy Aviary、Loveland Living Planet Aquarium及其他。该税在2014年获得近77%的县选民支持。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900006468/sl-county-oks-zap-tax-funding-for-25-organizations.html|title=S.L. County OKs ZAP tax funding for 25 organizations|date=December 28, 2017|publisher=Deseret News}}</ref> 

他提出的2018年县预算以5比4的投票结果获得县议会通过。反对派的预算比他先前提出的初步13亿美元预算多出36万7千美元。预算中包括部分使用Oxbow监狱（Oxbow Jail）资金、为县政府雇员加薪2.5%以及新建图书馆。<ref>{{Cite news|title=After bickering, Salt Lake County Council passes 2018 budget of $1.3B — and change|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/2017/12/20/after-bickering-salt-lake-county-council-passes-16-billion-2018-budget/|publisher=Salt Lake Tribune}}</ref>

麦克亚当斯提出了2019年的县预算，其中包括在前几年的[[公共安全|公共安全]]预算基础上增加资金，全面开放使用牛轭湾监狱（Oxbow Jail）的资金，以及帮助当地[[治安官|治安官]]招聘和留住公共安全官员的资金。该预算不包括增税，麦克亚当斯表示，新预算方案总金额减少了约1800万美元。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ksl.com/article/46412629/salt-lake-county-mayor-again-prioritizes-oxbow-jail-public-safety-in-2019-budget-proposal|title=Salt Lake County mayor again prioritizes Oxbow Jail, public safety in 2019 budget proposal|date=October 23, 2018|author=Katie McKellar|publisher=ksl.com}}</ref>经过一些修改，盐湖城议会一致通过了预算。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2018/12/05/salt-lake-county-council/|title=Salt Lake County Council passes $1.5 billion 2019 budget they say prioritizes public safety without a tax increase|publisher=Salt Lake Tribune|date=December 4, 2018}}</ref>

=== 经济及社区发展 ===
他支持S.B.199法案（又称社区保护法案），该法案赋予盐湖县各乡镇有决定其未来治理结构的权力。这导致了米爾克里克（Millcreek）的居民投票赞成合并选举。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/865624201/Community-preservation-bill-creates-governance-options-for-Salt-Lake-County.html?pg=all|publisher=Deseret News|title=Community preservation bill creates governance options for Salt Lake County}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://archive.sltrib.com/article.php?id=3133985&itype=CMSID|title=Millcreek to become Salt Lake County's 17th city|publisher=Salt Lake Tribune}}</ref>

作为盐湖县长官，麦克亚当斯是私人非营利慈善机构United Way Worldwide盐湖县分会的董事。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://uw.org/board/|title=Board - United Way Salt Lake|accessdate=August 11, 2018}}</ref>他实施了一种pay-for-success（为成功投资）模式，邀请第三方投资者为学前教育投资，并在达到特定基准阶段时获得投资回报。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.attorneyatlawmagazine.com/salt-lake-city/mayor-ben-mcadams/|title=Mayor Ben McAdams: Working Hard In Public Service|publisher=Attorney At Law Magazine|accessdate=August 11, 2018}}</ref>2016年，United Way因该模式及其“靠资料和数据来实行的合作方式”而认可了他。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://uw.org/2016-changemakers-mayor-ben-mcadams/|title=2016 Changemakers:Mayor Ben McAdams|publisher=United Way|date=May 12, 2016}}</ref>

2016年，他反对了在[[西喬丹_(猶他州)|西喬丹]]市建立[[Facebook|Facebook]][[数据中心|数据中心]]的提议。西乔丹市的长官指责来自盐湖县的反对，尤其是麦克亚当斯的反对，因为Facebook最终将数据中心设在了[[新墨西哥州|新墨西哥州]]而不是西乔丹。这个价值25亿美元的数据中心本可以从市政府和县得到1.95亿美元的税收减免。麦克亚当斯认为这个数据中心太昂贵，因为它最多只能直接创造130个工作岗位。支持该数据中心的人辩称，它将为该地区带来更多的开发和投资。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://archive.sltrib.com/article.php?id=4352467&itype=CMSID|title=West Jordan blames Ben McAdams for losing out on Facebook data center|date=September 25, 2016|publisher=Salt Lake Tribune}}</ref>

麦克亚当斯参与了与州领导层就建设[[盐湖城|盐湖城]]内河港口计划的谈判。2018年1月，他强烈认为土地使用和分区权应留在地方。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900009019/land-deal-for-inland-port-in-salt-lake-in-process.html|title=Land deal for inland port in Salt Lake in process|date=January 30, 2018}}</ref>犹他州议会通过了SB023法案，设立了内河港务局。他相信“该法案在很大程度上是公平的”。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900012490/governor-to-sign-controversial-utah-inland-port-bill-despite-city-outcry.html|title=Governor to sign controversial Utah inland port bill despite city outcry|publisher=Deseret News|date=March 8, 2018}}</ref>

在担任长官期间，盐湖城一直保持着AAA级债券评级。<ref name="BittersweetGoodbye">{{Cite web|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900048349/ben-mcadams-says-bittersweet-goodbye-to-salt-lake-county-mayors-office.html|title=Ben McAdams says 'bittersweet' goodbye to Salt Lake County mayor's office|publisher=Deseret News|date=December 28, 2018}}</ref>

=== 竞选 ===
'''2018年'''

2017年10月18日他宣布将争取民主党提名，与现任犹他州第四国会选区共和党众议员米娅·洛夫(Mia Love)竞争。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ksl.com/?sid=46182704&nid=148&title=salt-lake-county-mayor-ben-mcadams-running-for-congress|title=Salt Lake County Mayor Ben McAdams running for Congress {{!}} KSL.com|accessdate=October 19, 2017}}</ref>2018年4月28日，麦克亚当斯在[[民主党全国代表大会|民主党全国代表大会]]上获得提名。在全国代表大会72%的代表支持下，他避免了初选。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.usnews.com/news/best-states/utah/articles/2018-04-28/utah-democrats-gather-to-select-congressional-nominees|title=Utah Democrats Nominate McAdams for House Race Against Love|publisher=U.S. News|date=April 28, 2018}}</ref>

在竞选期间，他与美国众议院民主党领袖保持着距离，表示如果当选，他不会支持[[南希·佩洛西|南希 · 佩洛西]]竞选众议院[[議長|议长]]。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2018/05/03/mcadams-would-vote-no-on-a-speaker-pelosi-love-campaign-says-if-dems-win-so-does-pelosi/|title=Love campaign says if Dems win, so does Pelosi, but McAdams says he wouldn’t support the minority leader}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/elections/democrats-opposing-pelosi-n899536|title=Democrats opposing Pelosi|date=August 10, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.usnews.com/news/best-states/utah/articles/2018-05-03/democrat-mcadams-wont-support-pelosi-for-speaker|title=Democrat McAdams Won't Support Pelosi for Speaker|date=May 3, 2018}}</ref>

2018年6月，[[有线电视新闻网|美国有线电视新闻网]]报道称，这场竞选被认为“对两党都很重要”，因为共和党的米娅·洛夫“在移民问题上顶住了总统[[唐纳德·特朗普言论的真实性|唐纳德•特朗普]]的压力”，还有因为“全国民主党人认为麦克亚当斯是党在红草地（指共和党占优势的地方）上站稳脚跟的最佳机会之一”。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cnn.com/2018/06/29/politics/mia-love-ben-mcadams-utah/index.html|title=The real race to watch in Utah this November is not Mitt Romney's -- it's Mia Love's}}</ref>

他得到了[[蓝狗联盟|蓝狗联盟]]的支持。蓝狗联盟是一个由保守派和温和派民主党人组成的众议院核心小组，强调财政责任（Fiscal responsibility）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180808043210/https://bluedogdems.com/|title=Archive of bluedogdems.com|date=August 8, 2018}}</ref>他也得到了环保选民联盟（League of Conservation Voters）的支持<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://origin.lcv.org/article/lcv-action-fund-endorses-ben-mcadams-congress/|title=LCV Action Fund Endorses Ben McAdams for Congress|date=June 15, 2018}}</ref>

11月20日最终结果显示，麦克亚当斯以比对手多694票，即比对手多0.257%的得票率获胜。 他的得票率大于自动重新计票所需的0.25% 。<ref name="nov20">{{Cite web|url=https://fox13now.com/2018/11/20/final-votes-in-salt-lake-utah-counties-tallied-mcadams-in-the-lead-over-mia-love/|title=Final votes counted in Salt Lake, Utah counties; McAdams victory holds|date=November 20, 2018|publisher=Fox13|author=Taylor Hartman}}</ref> 米娅·洛夫和麦克亚当斯的竞选团队以及外界团体总共花费了大约1140万美元，使其成为犹他州历史上最昂贵的竞选之一。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://kutv.com/news/local/mcadams-vs-love-race-was-one-of-the-most-expensive-in-utah-history}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.apnews.com/0ff703c6668a48a0a41069d11a412ac3}}</ref> 预计外界团体在2020年的竞选时会花费巨资再次反对他。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900048573/utahs-new-democratic-party-congressman-ben-mcadams-already-has-a-target-on-his-back-washington-dc-trump.html|title=Utah's new Democratic congressman already has a target on his back, and he's not the only one|publisher=Deseret News|author=Matthew Brown|date=January 2, 2019}}</ref>

随着赢得选举，他成为自2012年[[吉姆·馬特森|吉姆·馬特森]]（Jim Matheson）于第四选区获得连任以来第一位从犹他州选出的民主党国会议员。

Utah’s only Democrat in Congress casts his first vote against Pelosi as speaker}}</ref> 此前，他曾与众议院其他民主党议员一起联署承诺反对南希•佩洛西出任众议院议长。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2018/11/19/16-democrats-vow-to-oppose-nancy-pelosi-as-next-house-speaker.html|title=16 Democrats say they will oppose Nancy Pelosi as the next House speaker|date=19 Nov 2018|accessdate=21 Nov 2018|publisher=CNBC}}</ref>

== 政治立场 ==
麦克亚当斯自称是一个温和的民主党人。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://wtop.com/national/2018/04/utah-democrats-gather-to-select-congressional-nominees/|title=Utah Democrats nominate McAdams for House race against Love {{!}} WTOP|date=April 28, 2018|work=WTOP|accessdate=August 10, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.ksl.com/article/46310286/mcadams-wilson-easily-win-nominations-at-democratic-state-convention|title=McAdams, Wilson, easily win nominations at Democratic state convention|accessdate=August 10, 2018}}</ref> 他强调他和共和党人努力合作，并认为共同努力解决问题是很重要的。<ref name="WrongToTalk">{{Cite news|url=https://utahpolicy.com/index.php/features/today-at-utah-policy/18947-mcadams-says-democrats-are-wrong-to-talk-about-impeaching-president-trump|title=McAdams says Democrats are wrong to talk about impeaching President Trump|publisher=Utahpolicy.com}}</ref>

=== 经济政策 ===
虽然该法案通过时他并不是国会议员，但他表示反对[[2017年的减税和就业法案|2017年的减税和就业法案]]，因为预计未来10年[[美國國債|美国国债]]将增加1.5万亿美元，还认为這有利于“富人而不是中产阶级”。<ref name="DeseretNewsToneOfRace">{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900036945/ben-mcadams-democratic-challenger-disappointed-with-tone-of-race.html|date=October 14, 2018|title=Ben McAdams: Democratic challenger disappointed with tone of race|publisher=Deseret News|author=Lisa Riley Roche}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://benmcadamsforcongress.com/blog/issue/budgettaxes/|title=Budget & Taxes|publisher=Ben McAdams for Congress|accessdate=October 15, 2018}}</ref>他曾表示，他支持税收法现代化，但税收改革法案是“在财政上不负责任的”，而且该法案“是从子孙后代那里借来的”。<ref name="DeseretNewsToneOfRace" />

=== 能源政策 ===
麦克亚当斯支持增加[[可再生能源|可再生能源]]比例並开发新技术。 他支持在可再生能源和传统[[化石燃料|化石燃料]]能源之间取得平衡的国家能源模式，以及减少车辆排放。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://benmcadamsforcongress.com/blog/issue/energy-environment/|title=Energy & Environment|publisher=Ben McAdams for Congress|accessdate=October 15, 2018}}</ref><ref name="DeseretNewsToneOfRace">{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900036945/ben-mcadams-democratic-challenger-disappointed-with-tone-of-race.html|date=October 14, 2018|title=Ben McAdams: Democratic challenger disappointed with tone of race|publisher=Deseret News|author=Lisa Riley Roche}}</ref>

=== 医疗政策 ===
在盐湖縣長官任期時他呼吁国会修改[[患者保护与平价医疗法案|患者保护与平价医疗法案]]（也被称为奥巴马医改）而不是废除它。<ref name="DeseretNewsToneOfRace">{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900036945/ben-mcadams-democratic-challenger-disappointed-with-tone-of-race.html|date=October 14, 2018|title=Ben McAdams: Democratic challenger disappointed with tone of race|publisher=Deseret News|author=Lisa Riley Roche}}</ref>此前，他曾呼吁国会在儿童健康保险计划（Children's Health Insurance Program）的资金于2017年9月到期后续期。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sltrib.com/opinion/commentary/2017/12/02/ben-mcadams-congress-to-tiny-tim-no-health-insurance-for-you/|title=Ben McAdams: Congress to Tiny Tim: ‘No health insurance for you.’|author=Ben McAdams|publisher=Salt Lake Tribune}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://benmcadamsforcongress.com/blog/issue/healthcare/|title=Health Care|publisher=Ben McAdams for Congress|accessdate=October 15, 2018}}</ref>

=== 移民政策 ===
麦克亚当斯呼吁进行全面的移民改革，其中包括加强边境安全，增加合法移民，以及為參與[[童年入境者暫緩遣返手續|童年入境者暫緩遣返手續]]（DACA）計劃的人提供永久性解决方案。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://benmcadamsforcongress.com/blog/issue/immigration/|title=Immigration|publisher=Ben McAdams for Congress|accessdate=October 15, 2018}}</ref><ref name="DeseretNewsToneOfRace">{{Cite news|url=https://www.deseretnews.com/article/900036945/ben-mcadams-democratic-challenger-disappointed-with-tone-of-race.html|date=October 14, 2018|title=Ben McAdams: Democratic challenger disappointed with tone of race|publisher=Deseret News|author=Lisa Riley Roche}}</ref>

在2018年議員竞选期间，麦克亚当斯反对川普总统在美国南部与墨西哥边境修建隔离墙的计划。<ref name="BeyondTheAttackAds">{{Cite news|url=https://www.sltrib.com/news/politics/2018/10/28/beyond-attack-ads-here/|title=Beyond the attack ads: Here are the candidates' stands on issues in the Mia Love vs. Ben McAdams race|date=October 28, 2018|publisher=Salt Lake Tribune}}</ref> 在[[2018年至2019年美国政府停摆|2018年至2019年美国政府停摆]]期间，他说他可能愿意投票支持边境保护資金還有邊境牆，如果这是包括在妥协法案之中，如移民改革和夢想家計劃的修正<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://kutv.com/news/local/mcadams-says-border-wall-is-not-immoral-could-vote-for-it-under-certain-circumstances|title=McAdams says border wall is not immoral, could vote for it under certain circumstances|publisher=KUTV|date=January 20th 2019}}</ref>

=== 同性婚姻 ===
麦克亚当斯支持[[同性婚姻|同性婚姻]]。在最高法院对[[奥贝格费尔诉霍奇斯案|奥贝格费尔诉霍奇斯案]]做出裁决后，同性伴侣有權在美國結婚。他说:“[[安東尼·肯尼迪|安東尼·肯尼迪]]大法官在他的意见中说同性伴侣有结婚的权利源自第十四修正案中对權力平等的保障”。这一决定印证了我一直以来的信念——所有家庭都应该在法律面前得到平等的对待。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.good4utah.com/news/local-utah-state-news-/mayor-ben-mcadams-pleased-with-supreme-court-same-sex-marriage-ruling/164411753|title=Mayor Ben McAdams 'pleased' with Supreme Court same-sex marriage ruling|date=June 26, 2015|work=GOOD4UTAH|accessdate=August 10, 2018}}</ref>

=== 堕胎 ===

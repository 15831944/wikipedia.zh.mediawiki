'''潔克·薛佛'''（{{lang-en|Jac Schaeffer}}，{{bd|1978年}}）是一位電影導演、製作人和編劇，知名作品有《{{le|愛情時鐘|TiMER}}》<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/tribeca_09_interview_timer_director_jac_schaeffer|title=Tribeca '09 Interview: "TiMER" Director Jac Schaeffer|date=2009-04-20|work=Indiewire|accessdate=2014-03-28}}</ref>。

== 經歷 ==
潔克·薛佛獲得[[普林斯頓大學|普林斯頓大學]]學士學位<ref>{{cite web|title=Jac Schaeffer Biography|url=http://staceystern.com/jac-schaeffer-biography/|publisher=Stacey Stern|accessdate=2014-04-06}}</ref>，[[南加州大學電影藝術學院|南加州大學電影藝術學院]]碩士學位<ref>{{cite web|last=Tomlinson|first=Brett|title=Tribeca connection : Q&A with writer, director, and producer Jac Schaeffer ’00|url=http://blogs.princeton.edu/paw/2009/04/tribeca-connect/#more-6680|work=Princeton Alumni Weekly|accessdate=2014-04-06|date=2009-04-22}}</ref>。

薛佛為2019年5月上映的的電影《[[詐騙女神|詐騙女神]]》編劇<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://variety.com/2017/film/news/anna-hathaway-rebel-wilson-comedy-release-date-1202615221/|title=Anne Hathaway-Rebel Wilson Comedy to Hit Theaters in August|last=McNary|first=Dave|date=2017-11-15|work=Variety|access-date=2018-01-11|language=en-US}}</ref>，影片由[[安妮·海瑟薇|安妮·海瑟薇]]主演<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://deadline.com/2017/01/anne-hathaway-rebel-wilson-nasty-women-dirty-rotten-scoundrels-remake-mgm-1201889950/|title=Anne Hathaway, Rebel Wilson Are The ‘Nasty Women’ In MGM’s ‘Dirty Rotten Scoundrels’ Remake|last=Jr|first=Mike Fleming|date=2017-01-19|work=Deadline|access-date=2018-01-10|language=en-US}}</ref>。

薛佛為[[漫威影業|漫威影業]]的電影《[[驚奇隊長_(電影)|驚奇隊長]]》的聯合編劇之一，並且為另一部漫威電影由[[史嘉蕾·喬韓森|史嘉蕾·喬韓森]]主演的《[[黑寡婦_(電影)|黑寡婦]]》執筆劇本<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/marvel-black-widow-jac-schaeffer-screenwriter-1202643908/|title=Marvel’s Standalone ‘Black Widow’ Movie Gains Momentum With Jac Schaeffer Writing|last=Kroll|first=Justin|date=2018-01-10|work=Variety|access-date=2018-01-10|language=en-US}}</ref>，隨後{{le|內德·本森|Ned Benson}}重新劇本<ref>http://collider.com/black-widow-script-ned-benson/</ref>。

'''收腹器'''（{{lang-en|'''Abdomenizer'''}}，或：{{lang|en|'''Abdomeniser'''}}）是由[[加拿大|加拿大]]脊椎按摩師丹尼斯（Dennis Colonello）發明的用於[[腹部運動|腹部鍛煉]]的體育器材。<ref>{{Cite patent | inventor-last = Colonello | inventor-first = Dennis | inventorlink = | inventor2-last = | inventor2-first = | inventorlink2 = | publication-date = | issue-date = 21 June 1988 | title = Apparatus for use in exercising the abdominal muscles | country-code = US | postscript = <!-- None --> | description = | patent-number = 4752067 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=N-0DAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA18&dq=|title=Abdomenizer advert|date=17 October 1989|work=Weekly World News|accessdate=2009-06-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://nl.newsbank.com/nl-search/we/Archives?p_product=GF&s_site=grandforks&p_multi=GF&p_theme=realcities&p_action=search&p_maxdocs=200&p_topdoc=1&p_text_direct-0=0ED00A169DEE221B&p_field_direct-0=document_id&p_perpage=10&p_sort=YMD_date:D&s_trackval=GooglePM|title=Green garbage bags, zippers and ginger ale, Canada's contributions many|date=1 July 2001|work=Grand Forks Herald|accessdate=2009-06-23}}</ref> 通過[[美國|美國]][[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[坎頓_(俄亥俄州)|坎頓]]的一家公司以[[諮詢廣告|諮詢廣告]]的形式向外界推廣。<ref name=guardian2005>[http://www.guardian.co.uk/lifeandstyle/2005/nov/12/shopping3 What's what: Design expert Oliver Bennett selects objects that he finds inspirational - and those that are simply a waste of space], [[The_Guardian|The Guardian]], 2005</ref> 該設備是一個扁平的鞍狀熱成型塑料，可以對[[尾骨|尾骨]]施壓。<ref name="discount">{{cite news|url=http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_m3092/is_n1_v31/ai_11768357/|title=From 'as seen on TV' to as seen at the store - exercise equipment marketing  , |date=6 January 1992|work=Discount Store News|accessdate=2009-06-23}}</ref>

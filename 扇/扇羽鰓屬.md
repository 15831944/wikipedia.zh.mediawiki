'''扇羽鰓屬'''（學名：''{{lang|la |Flabellina}}''），又名'''扇鰓海蛞蝓屬'''{{r|Davies}}，是[[裸鰓目|裸鰓目]][[枝鰓亞目|枝鰓亞目]]之下的一個[[海蛞蝓|海蛞蝓]]的[[屬_(生物)|屬]]、[[海洋生物|海洋]][[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]][[扇羽鰓科|扇羽鰓科]]的成員{{r|WoRMS_138019}}。

==分類歷史==
扇羽鰓屬由Gray於1850年建立，模式種為''[[Flabellina_affinis|Flabellina affinis]]'' and characterised by the {{tsl|en|cerata|}} being arranged on peduncles and the {{tsl|en|rhinophore|}}s being annulate. Many species were added by subsequent authors. In 1981 the genus ''{{tsl|en|Coryphella|}}'' was merged under the older name ''Flabellina'' as despite a large range of morphological characters in the 64 species of Flabellinidae and {{tsl|en|Coryphellidae|}} known at that time, no clear distinction could be found to separate the species into the two genera.<ref>Gosliner T.M., Griffiths R.J. (1981) Description and revision of some South African aeolidacean Nudibranchia (Mollusca, Gastropoda). Annals of the South African Museum 84: 105–150.</ref> In 2017 an integrative study of the 71 species then considered to be included in ''Flabellina'' redistributed the species into seven families and 26 genera.<ref>Korshunova, T.; Martynov, A.; Bakken, T.; Evertsen, J.; Fletcher, K.; Mudianta, W.; Saito, H.; Lundin, K.; Schrödl, M.; Picton, B. (2017). [https://doi.org/10.3897/zookeys.717.21885 Polyphyly of the traditional family Flabellinidae affects a major group of Nudibranchia: aeolidacean taxonomic reassessment with descriptions of several new families, genera, and species (Mollusca, Gastropoda).] ZooKeys. 717: 1-139.</ref>

==生物學==
如同其他蓑海牛裸鰓動物物種那樣，本屬物種在吃掉[[刺胞動物|刺胞動物]]後，能將牠們的[[刺絲胞|刺絲胞]]儲存在自身背部的[[角突|角突]]裡{{r|Frick2003|doris}}。

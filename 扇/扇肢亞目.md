'''扇肢亞目'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''Flabellifera'''}}）是一個[[甲殼亞門|甲殼亞門]][[軟甲綱|軟甲綱]][[等足目|等足目]]之下的一個[[棄用生物分類|棄用生物分類]]。這個亞目有超過3000物種，但後來證實若不是[[多系群|多系群]]的話，就是一個[[並系群|並系群]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crustacea.net/crustace/www/flabelli.htm |title=Flabellifera |publisher=[[Australian_Museum|Australian Museum]] |author=J. K. Lowry |work=Crustacea: The Higher Taxa |language=en |date=1999-11-02 |accessdate=2011-07-06 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://tolweb.org/notes/?note_id=4175 |title=Suborder Flabellifera |work=Guide to the Coastal Marine Isopods of California |author=Richard Brusca, Vania R. Coelho & Stefano Taiti |accessdate=January 28, 2009 |publisher=[[Tree_of_Life_Web_Project|Tree of Life Web Project]]}}</ref>。所以現時其部分物種已分開了成為[[Sphaeromatidea|Sphaeromatidea]]，而餘下的物種跟原來的[[寄生亞目|寄生亞目]]合併，組成了新的[[縮頭水蝨亞目|縮頭水蝨亞目]]（Cymothoida）<ref>{{cite journal |title=Higher classification of the flabelliferan and related Isopoda based on a reappraisal of relationships |author=Angelika Brandt & Gary C.

'''叔丁硫醇'''是一种[[有机硫化合物|有机硫化合物]]，化学式为(CH<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>CSH。它可以用作天然气的增味剂，或用作调味剂。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.thegoodscentscompany.com/data/rw1056611.html | title = tert-butyl mercaptan | publisher = thegoodscentscompany.com}}</ref>

==制备==
有多篇文章将叔丁硫醇列为烘培马铃薯的微量气味成分之一，<ref>{{cite journal | title =Volatile Sulfur Compounds in Potatoes | year =  1964 |author1=Gumbmann, M. R. |author2=Burr, H. K. | journal =  Journal of Food and Agricultural Chemistry | volume =12 | pages =404–408 | doi =10.1021/jf60135a004 | issue =5}}</ref>但由于[[叔丁基|叔丁基]]化合物在自然界中比较少见，它也被人怀疑。该化合物最早于1890年由Leonard Dobbin通过[[叔丁基氯|叔丁基氯]]和[[硫化锌|硫化锌]]的反应制得。<ref>{{cite journal |author=Dobbin, Leonard |year=1890 |title= On tertiary Butyl Mercaptan|journal= Journal of the Chemical Society, Transactions|volume=57 |issue= |pages=639–643 |doi=10.1039/ct8905700639}}</ref>

在1932年，由格氏试剂''t''-BuMgCl和硫反应，得到相应的硫醇盐，经过[[水解|水解]]制备。<ref>{{cite journal | doi =10.1002/prac.19321340901 | title =Tertiäres Butylmercaptan | year =  1932 |author1=Rheinboldt, Heinrich |author2=Mott, Friedrich |author3=Motzkus, Erwin |author4=A. D. McMaster |author5=B. M. Mattson |author6=S. T. Michel | journal =  Journal fuer Praktische Chemie (Leipzig) | volume =134 | pages =257–281 | issue =9–12}}</ref>其反应为：
:''t''-BuMgCl  +  S   →   ''t''-BuSMgCl
:''t''-BuSMgCl  +  H<sub>2</sub>O   →   ''t''-BuSH  +  Mg(OH)Cl

当今在工业上，叔丁硫醇由[[异丁烯|异丁烯]]和[[硫化氢|硫化氢]]在粘土（氧化硅铝）催化剂上反应生产。<ref>{{cite journal |author1=Schulze, W.A. |author2=Lyon, J.P. |author3=Short, G.H.  |lastauthoramp=yes |year=1948 |title=Synthesis of Tertiary Alkyl Mercaptans |journal= Industrial and Engineering Chemistry|volume=40 |pages= 2308–2313|publisher= American Chemical Society |doi= 10.1021/ie50468a019 |issue= 12 }}</ref>

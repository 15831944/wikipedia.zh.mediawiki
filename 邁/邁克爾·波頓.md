'''邁克爾·波頓'''（{{lang-en|Michael Bolton}}，本姓：Bolotin<ref name="JWSociety of NH">[http://pages.cthome.net/hirsch/bolton.htm Michael Bolton: Biography] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101018114141/http://pages.cthome.net/hirsch/bolton.htm |date=2010-10-18 }}, The Jewish Historical Society of New Haven, 1998.</ref>，{{bd|1953年|2月26日}}），美國著名唱作歌手，1975年出道，曾經醉心搖滾曲風<ref name="iommiautobiography">{{cite book |last=Iommi |first=Tony |title=Iron Man: My Journey Through Heaven and Hell with Black Sabbath |year=2011 |publisher=Da Capo Press |isbn=978-0306819551}}</ref>，專輯總累計銷售量達7.5千萬張，多次贏得[[全美音樂獎|全美音樂獎]]及[[格林美獎|格林美獎]]等殊榮<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.twcc.com/articles/2014/06/06/t/time-warp-turntable-jams-music-n-mullets.6|title=Time Warp Turntable Jams: Music ‘N’ Mullets|last=Reporter|first=TWC|publisher=[[Time_Warner_Cable|Time Warner Cable]]|date=2014-06-06|accessdate=2014-06-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://lancasteronline.com/entertainment/michael-bolton-may-not-be-hip-but-he-s-happening/article_5d16f32d-8230-58e9-941c-8c8d684fd9a8.html|title=Michael Bolton may not be hip, but he's happening|last=Ferguson|first=Jon|publisher=[[Lancaster_Newspapers,_Inc.|Lancaster Newspapers, Inc.]]|date=2013-02-21|accessdate=2014-05-23}}</ref>。

== 早年生活 ==
邁克爾本姓Bolotin，生於一個猶太家庭，雙親在他年幼時已經分開<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.davidovit.com/articles/Bolton.pdf |title= Michael Bolton: The Voice Within |author=Aliza Davidovit |publisher=Davidovit.com |accessdate= 2013-02-18}}</ref>，他的祖父母及外祖父母都是來自[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]的移民<ref>{{cite web|title=George "Bullet" Bolotin|url=http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gr&GRid=102290618|website=findagrave.com|accessdate=15 March 2015}}</ref>。

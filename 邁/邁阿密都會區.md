'''邁阿密都會區'''又稱'''大邁阿密'''或'''南佛羅里達'''，為世界[[大都會區列表_(依人口排列)|第67大都會區]]及[[美國|美國]]第8大[[城市群|城市群]]。邁阿密都會區位於[[佛罗里达州|佛罗里达州]]南部 。<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Williams |first1=Darlene F. |year=2005 |title=Proposed Metropolitan Area Definitions for FY2006 Income Limits and Estimates of Median Family Income |journal=[[聯邦公報|聯邦公報]] |pages=74988–74993 |url=http://federalregister.gov/a/05-24115 |accessdate=January 12, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/book/browse?type=lcsubc&key=Miami%20Metropolitan%20Area%20%28Fla.%29&c=x |title=Miami Metropolitan Area (Fla.) |author=John Mark Ockerbloom |date=January 1, 2011 |work=The Online Books Page |publisher=[[宾夕法尼亚大学|宾夕法尼亚大学]] |accessdate=January 12, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bls.gov/ro4/cexmiam.pdf |title=CONSUMER EXPENDITURES FOR THE MIAMI AREA: 2008–09 |date=October 28, 2010 |work=News Release |publisher=[[美國勞工部|美國勞工部]] ({{tsl|en|Bureau of Labor Statistics||Bureau of Labor Statistics}}) |accessdate=January 12, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://ntl.bts.gov/DOCS/473.html |title= Journey-To-Work Trends in the United States and its Major Metropolitan Areas 1960–1990 |date=June 2003 |work=Research and Innovative Technology Administration National Transportation Library |publisher={{tsl|en|Federal Highway Administration||Federal Highway Administration}} |accessdate=January 12, 2011}}</ref>2016年時都會區人口為6,066,387，邁阿密都會區為佛羅里達州最大都會區及[[美國東南部|美國東南部第2大都會區]]。

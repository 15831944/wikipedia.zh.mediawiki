'''叢權蛺蝶'''（學名：''Dynamine coenus''）是屬於[[蛺蝶科|蛺蝶科]][[苾蛺蝶亞科|苾蛺蝶亞科]]的一種[[蝴蝶|蝴蝶]]，是[[權蛺蝶屬|權蛺蝶屬]]的一種。分佈於南美洲中至南部。

== 亞種 ==
* '''''D. c. coenus''''' － 指名亞種，分佈於[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]
* '''''D. c. leucothea''''' <small>(Bates, 1865)</small> － 分佈於[[玻利維亞|玻利維亞]]和巴西[[亞馬遜州|亞馬遜州]]<ref>Butler, 1877. On the Lepidoptera of the Amazons, collected by James W. H. Trail, Esq. during the years 1873 to 1875 Trans. Ent. Soc. Lond. 1877 (2) : [http://archive.org/stream/transactionsofen1877roya#page/117/mode/1up 117], pl. [http://archive.org/stream/transactionsofen1877roya#page/n566/mode/1up 3], f. 2</ref>
* '''''D. c. albidula''''' <small>Weeks, 1901</small> － 分佈於玻利維亞和阿根廷<ref>Weeks, 1901. New diurnal Lepidoptera from Bolivia Can. Ent. 33 (10): [http://archive.org/stream/canadianentomolo33ento#page/266/mode/1up 266]</ref>
* '''''D. c. ambicoenus''''' <small>Hayward, 1935</small>
* '''''D. c. effusiva''''' <small>Hayward, 1931</small>

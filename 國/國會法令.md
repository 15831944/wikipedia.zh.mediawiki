'''國會法令'''（{{lang-en|'''Act of Parliament'''}}；議會立法），又稱[[主體法例|主體法例]]，是由[[國會|國會]]（[[立法機構|立法機構]]）通過的[[法規|法規]]（statute）。<ref name=Gillespie>{{cite book|first=Alisdair|last=Gillespie|title=The English Legal System|date=18 April 2013|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-965709-4|pages=23–25|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=sZ2cAQAAQBAJ&pg=PA23&dq=%22Primary+legislation+is+that+which+is+known+as+an+Act+of+Parliament%22&hl=en&sa=X&ei=bZPnUs2ECY2O7QbwiYGACA&ved=0CDEQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=%22Primary%20legislation%20is%20that%20which%20is%20known%20as%20an%20Act%20of%20Parliament%22&f=false}}</ref>在[[愛爾蘭共和國|愛爾蘭共和國]]，國會法令正式稱為[[愛爾蘭共和國法令|愛爾蘭國會法令]]（Act of the Oireachtas）；而在[[美國|美國]]則稱為[[美國國會法案|國會法案]]（Act of Congress）。<ref name=Encyclopedia>{{cite|title=Legislation|work=International Encyclopedia of the Social Sciences|publisher=Thomson Gale|year=2008|url=http://www.encyclopedia.com/social-sciences-and-law/law/law/legislation|accessdate=19 November 2016}}</ref>

'''國民自由黨'''（{{lang-af|National Freedom Party}}，{{lang-en|National Freedom Party}}）是[[南非|南非]]的一個[[政黨|政黨]]。其於2011年1月25日由[[因卡塔自由黨|因卡塔自由黨]]前主席{{le|扎内勒·馬古瓦扎-姆西比|Zanele kaMagwaza-Msibi}}聯同其他前黨員成立<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.thenewage.co.za/Detail.aspx?news_id=8738&cat_id=1010|title = Magwaza-Msibi launches National Freedom Party|publisher = The New Age|date = Jan 26, 2011<!-- 9:58AM-->|access-date = 2019-03-27|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150204093045/http://www.thenewage.co.za/Detail.aspx?news_id=8738&cat_id=1010|archive-date = 2015-02-04|dead-url = yes}}</ref>。

== 選舉結果 ==
在{{le|2011年南非市政選舉|South African municipal election, 2011|2011年市政選舉}}中，國民自由黨於整個南非獲得2.4%的選票<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.elections.org.za/content/LGEPublicReports/197/Detailed%20Results/National.pdf |title=Results Summary - All Ballots: All Provinces |publisher=Independent Electoral Commission |date=25 May 2011 |accessdate=26 May 2011}}</ref>，而在夸祖魯-納塔爾省則獲得10.4%選票<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.elections.org.za/content/LGEPublicReports/197/Detailed%20Results/KN.pdf |title=Results Summary - All Ballots: KwaZulu-Natal |publisher=Independent Electoral Commission |date=25 May 2011 |accessdate=26 May 2011}}</ref>。它贏得了{{le|埃迪姆貝地方自治市|eDumbe Local Municipality}}的多數席位以及{{le|農戈馬地方自治市|Nongoma Local Municipality}}的多個席位。在[[2014年南非大選|2014年南非大選]]後，國民自由黨的維羅尼卡·扎内勒·姆西比被任命為科技部副部長<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.parliament.gov.za/live/content.php?Item_ID=184&MemberID=299|title=Parliament of the Republic of South Africa - Parliament of South Africa}}</ref>。由於未向獨立選舉委員會支付選舉費，該黨被取消參加{{le|2016年南非市政選舉|South African municipal election, 2016|2016年市政選舉}}的資格<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.bdlive.co.za/national/politics/2016/07/21/nfp-in-last-ditch-court-bid-to-contest-local-government-elections | title=NFP in last-ditch court bid to contest local government elections | publisher=Business Day Live | date=21 July 2016 | accessdate=9 August 2016 | author=Mthetwa, Bongani}}</ref>。

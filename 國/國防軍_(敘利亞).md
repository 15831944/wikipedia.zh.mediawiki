'''國防軍'''（{{lang-ar|قوات الدفاع الوطني}}），或譯'''國家防衛軍'''，是[[叙利亚|叙利亚]]一支親政府世俗[[民兵|民兵]]部隊，在2012年夏天之後成立。<ref name="iranian-strategy">Will Fulton, Joseph Holliday, and Sam Wyer, [http://www.understandingwar.org/report/iranian-strategy-syria ''Iranian Strategy in Syria''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160201135117/http://www.understandingwar.org/report/iranian-strategy-syria |date=2016-02-01 }}, Institute for the Study of War, May 2013</ref>敘利亞政府在[[叙利亚内战|叙利亚内战]]期間組建了該支民兵，<ref name="Institute for the Study of War">{{cite news|title=SYRIA UPDATE: THE FALL OF AL-QUSAYR|url=http://understandingwar.org/backgrounder/syria-update-fall-al-qusayr|accessdate=2013-06-07 |publisher=Institute for the Study of War}}</ref>由自願加入的民眾組成。

敘利亞政府向國防軍成員發放薪金和提供武器裝備。<ref name="reuters">{{cite news|title=Insight: Battered by war, Syrian army creates its own replacement|url=http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/04/21/us-syria-crisis-paramilitary-insight-idUSBRE93K02R20130421|accessdate=May 29, 2013|newspaper=Reuters|date=April 21, 2013}}</ref><ref name="Rise of the militias">{{cite news |title=Rise of the militias |author=Michael Weiss |url=https://now.mmedia.me/lb/en/commentaryanalysis/rise-of-the-militias |newspaper=NOW. |date=17 May 2013 |accessdate= |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131105035326/https://now.mmedia.me/lb/en/commentaryanalysis/rise-of-the-militias |archivedate=2013年11月5日 }}</ref>

2015年的報道指國防軍的人數有90,000<ref name="MEEAssadmilitias">[http://www.middleeasteye.net/news/who-are-pro-assad-militias-syria-2030619965 Who are the pro-Assad militias in Syria?] Middle East Eye, 25 September 2015</ref>至100,000人<ref name="The Shia crescendo">{{cite news|title=The Shia crescendo|url=http://www.economist.com/news/middle-east-and-africa/21647367-shia-militias-are-proliferating-middle-east-shia-crescendo|work=The Economist|date=28 March 2015|accessdate=24 June 2015}}</ref>。

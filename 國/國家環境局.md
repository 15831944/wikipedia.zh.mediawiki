'''国家环境局'''（{{lang-en|National Environment Agency}}，[[缩写|缩写]]：'''NEA'''）是[[新加坡|新加坡]][[环境及水源部|环境及水源部]]辖下的部门，专责维持及改善环境卫生。该机构对抗污染，维护公众健康，提供气象信息。<ref name="NEA About Us">{{cite news | url = https://www.nea.gov.sg/corporate-functions/who-we-are/about-us | author =  | title = About Us | publisher = National Environment Agency | date =  | access-date = 2019-04-22 | 7 =  | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20190410044132/https://www.nea.gov.sg/corporate-functions/who-we-are/about-us | archive-date = 2019-04-10 | dead-url = no }}</ref> 

从2019年4月1日起，国家环境局的食品检测工作由新成立的新加坡食品局负责。 <ref name="SFA">{{cite news | url = https://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/singapore/story20190401-944886 | author = 杨漾 | title = 新加坡食品局今天正式成立 | publisher = 联合早报 | date = 2019-04-01 | access-date = 2019-04-22 | 7 =  | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20190401041227/https://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/singapore/story20190401-944886 | archive-date = 2019-04-01 | dead-url = no }}</ref>

==组织==
国家环境局管理的部门包括：
* 公共卫生部
* 环境保护部
* [[新加坡气象局|新加坡气象局]]

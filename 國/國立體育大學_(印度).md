'''國立體育大學'''（{{Lang-en|National Sports University}}）是[[印度|印度]][[曼尼普爾|曼尼普爾]]的一所體育大學<ref>http://yas.nic.in/en/yas2/draft-bill-setting-national-sports-university-manipur</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.dnaindia.com/sport/report-manipur-government-starts-process-of-land-acquisition-for-national-sports-varsity-2038318 |title= Manipur government starts process of land acquisition for national sports varsity |date= 25 November 2014 |website= [[Daily_News_and_Analysis|Daily News and Analysis]] |access-date= 9 July 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://kanglaonline.com/2015/07/bjp-conducts-second-consultative-meeting-on-sports-university-issue/ |title= BJP Conducts Second Consultative Meeting On Sports University Issue |date=6 July 2015 |website= Kangla Online |access-date= 9 July 2017}}</ref>。[[印度總理|總理]][[納倫德拉·莫迪|納倫德拉·莫迪]]於2018年3月16日為國立體育大學奠基<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://ddinews.gov.in/videos/national/pm-lays-foundation-stone-national-sports-university-various-development-projects|title=PM lays foundation stone of National Sports University & various development projects at Imphal {{!}} DD News|website=ddinews.gov.in|language=en|access-date=2018-03-17}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/national/manipur-inspires-india-narendra-modi/article23274879.ece|title=Manipur inspires India: Narendra Modi|last=Karmakar|first=Rahul|date=2018-03-16|work=The Hindu|access-date=2018-03-17|language=en-IN|issn=0971-751X}}</ref>。

印度已有一些{{Link-en|州立大學 (印度)|State university (India)|州立大學}}專注於體育教育，例如：{{Link-en|泰米爾納德邦體育大學|Tamil Nadu Physical Education and Sports University}}、{{Link-en|古吉拉特邦體育大學|Swarnim Gujarat Sports University}}等，以及一所名為{{Link-en|拉克什米拜國立體育學院|Lakshmibai National Institute of Physical Education}}的{{Link-en|准许大学|Deemed University}}，但这是印度第一所專注於體育教育的{{Link-en|中央大学 (印度)|Central University (India)|中央大學}}。

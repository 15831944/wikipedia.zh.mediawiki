'''國際航空集團'''（'''International Consolidated Airlines Group'''，[[縮寫|簡稱]]'''IAG'''）是一家[[英國|英國]]及[[西班牙|西班牙]]合資的{{link-en|航空控股公司列表|List of airline holding companies|航空控股公司}}，總部在英國[[倫敦|倫敦]]，公司則在西班牙[[馬德里|馬德里]]註冊成立。2011年1月由[[英國航空|英國航空]]和[[西班牙國家航空|西班牙國家航空]]合資而成<ref name="IBTimes merger">{{cite web|url=http://www.ibtimes.co.uk/articles/20100408/international-airlines-group-formed-ba-signs-merger-iberia.htm|title=International Airlines Group formed as BA signs merger with Iberia|date=8 April 2010|publisher=IBTimes|accessdate=11 April 2010}}</ref><ref name="newstatesman">{{cite web|url=http://www.newstatesman.com/business/2010/04/merger-airlines-groups-iberia|title=British Airways and Iberia agree on merger|last=Vamburkar|first=Meenal|date=8 April 2010|work=New Statesman |location=UK|accessdate=11 April 2010}}</ref>。根據2010年收益，它是歐洲第三大、世界第七大航空公司<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.flightglobal.com/articles/2011/07/22/359695/world-airline-rankings-regional-picture.html|title=World Airline Rankings: Regional picture|date=22 July 2011|work=Flight Global|accessdate=31 July 2011}}</ref>。

自2011年1月24日以來，國際航空公司主要在[[倫敦證券交易所|倫敦證券交易所]]和[[FTSE_100|FTSE 100]]交易<ref name=guard482010>[http://www.guardian.co.uk/business/2010/apr/08/british-airways-name-to-disappear British Airways name will disappear from FTSE if Iberia merger goes ahead | Business]. The Guardian (2010-04-08). Retrieved on 2012-07-07.</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/business-11862956|title=BA Iberia merger gets approval from shareholders|publisher=BBC News | date=29 November 2010}}</ref>。此外也在[[馬德里證券交易所|馬德里]]、巴塞羅那、畢爾巴鄂和瓦倫西亞證券交易所上市<ref name=bbc2412011>{{Cite news |title=IAG shares begin trading, replacing BA and Iberia |author=|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/business-12265332 |publisher=BBC News |date=24 January 2011 |accessdate=24 January 2011}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news| url=http://uk.reuters.com/article/idUKTRE66C22I20100713 |title=British Airways stands firm over crew dispute |accessdate=18 August 2010|agency=Reuters | date=13 July 2010}}</ref>。同年4月1日在[[IBEX_35|IBEX 35]]掛牌<ref>{{cite web |date=8 March 2011 |accessdate=7 May 2011 |title=Results of the quarterly follow up meeting of the Technical Advisory Committee of the IBEX® Indices |url=http://www.sbolsas.com/ing/ficheros/documentos/comiteasesor/AC04-11eng.pdf |publisher=[[Bolsas_y_Mercados_Españoles|Sociedad de Bolsas]] |format=PDF |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120323161316/http://www.sbolsas.com/ing/ficheros/documentos/comiteasesor/AC04-11eng.pdf |archivedate=2012年3月23日 }}</ref>。

2012年12月，IAG將英國航空、英國中部航空和西班牙國家航空的貨運拆分併入單獨的[[國際航空集團貨運|國際航空集團貨運]]中。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.logisticsmanager.com/Articles/Article.aspx?liArticleID=19610|title=IAG Cargo launched as cargo brand for BA and Iberia|accessdate=12 October 2013|publisher=Logistics Manager|date=3 December 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20131012182728/http://www.logisticsmanager.com/Articles/Article.aspx?liArticleID=19610|archivedate=2013年10月12日}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.aircargonews.net/news/single-view/news/iag-cargo-launches-brand.html|title=IAG Cargo launches brand|accessdate=12 October 2013|publisher=Air Cargo News|date=5 December 2012}}</ref>

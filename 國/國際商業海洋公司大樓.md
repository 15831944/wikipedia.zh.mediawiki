'''國際商業海洋公司大樓'''（International Mercantile Marine Company Building）是位於[[美國|美國]][[紐約市|紐約市]][[百老匯|百老匯]]的一座歷史建築。國際商業海洋公司大樓興建於1882年，是一座安妮皇后式風格建築，曾是[[國際商業海洋公司|國際商業海洋公司]]的總部<ref name="NysNrhpNom">{{cite web| url = https://cris.parks.ny.gov/| title = Cultural Resource Information System (CRIS)| publisher = [[New_York_State_Office_of_Parks,_Recreation_and_Historic_Preservation|New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation]]| format = Searchable database| accessdate = 2015-12-01}}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} ''Note:'' This includes {{cite web | url = https://cris.parks.ny.gov/Uploads/ViewDoc.aspx?mode=A&id=34464&q=false | title = National Register of Historic Places Registration Form: International Mercantile Marine Company Building | accessdate = 2015-12-01 | author = Kathleen LaFrank | format = PDF | date = May 1989 }} ''See also:'' {{cite web |url=https://cris.parks.ny.gov/Uploads/ViewDoc.aspx?mode=A&id=34463&q=false |title=Accompanying photos }}</ref>。1991年，該建築被列入[[國家史蹟名錄|國家史蹟名錄]]。

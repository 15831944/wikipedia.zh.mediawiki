}}</ref>2012年海地的管理部门拆除了该建筑的废墟<ref>{{cite news| url =https://www.nytimes.com/2012/09/14/world/americas/haitis-national-palace-being-demolished.html| title =Palace in Haiti, Damaged by Quake, Is Being Razed| publisher =纽约时报| date =2012-09-14| accessdate =2018-08-24| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160717203102/https://www.nytimes.com/2012/09/14/world/americas/haitis-national-palace-being-demolished.html| archivedate =2016-07-17}}</ref>，2017年海地总统[[喬夫內爾·莫伊斯|莫伊斯]]宣布将对宫殿进行重建。<ref>{{cite news |title=Haiti to rebuild quake-damaged palace |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-latin-america-39649680 |accessdate=2018-08-23 |work=BBC News |date=2017-04-19}}</ref>

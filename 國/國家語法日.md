'''國家語法日'''（{{lang-en|National Grammar Day}}）是美國一個由私人提倡的節日，日期定於每年的3月4日。<ref name="baltimoresun">McIntyre, John.  [http://www.baltimoresun.com/news/language-blog/bal-prepare-yourself-for-national-grammar-day-20150227-story.html3 "Prepare yourself for National Grammar Day"],'' [[The_Baltimore_Sun|The Baltimore Sun]]'', February 27, 2015; retrieved October 29, 2015.</ref><ref name="cnn">Zdanowicz, Christina.  [http://www.cnn.com/2014/03/03/living/grammar-day-irpt "Every day is grammar day when you've got this job"]'', [[CNN|CNN]], July 17, 2014; retrieved October 29, 2015.</ref>  

國家語法日最早於2008年由{{tsl|en|Martha Brockenbrough|瑪莎·布羅肯布魯羅}}設立，她也是''Things That Make Us [Sic]''一書的作者及「良好語法促進學會」（Society for the Promotion of Good Grammar）的創辦人。<ref name="npr">Martin, Rachel. [https://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=185247355 "How Possessive: The Apostrophe's Place In Space"],'' [[NPR|NPR]]'', May 19, 2013; retrieved October 29, 2015.</ref>節日的目的在於提倡，並且推廣正確的[[美式英語|美式英語]]語法。

'''蒙古人种'''（Mongoloid），是[[西方人|西方人]]定義一个居住范围包括[[东亚|东亚]]、[[中亚|中亚]]、[[东南亚|东南亚]]、[[北亚|北亚]]、[[南亚|南亚]]、[[北极地区|北极地区]]、[[美洲|美洲]]以及[[太平洋|太平洋]]岛屿上的种族，为高加索人种、尼格罗人种、澳大利亚人种的混血。<ref>''[[Meyers_Konversations-Lexikon|Meyers Konversations-Lexikon]]'', 4th edition, 1885-90.</ref><ref name="Beckman2001">{{cite journal |author1=L.E. Beckman |author2=K. Sjoberg |author3=S. Eriksson |author4=L. Beckman |year=2001 |title=Haemochromatosis gene mutations in Finns, Swedes and Swedish Saamis |journal=Human Heredity |volume=52 |pages= 110–112 |pmid=11474212 |issue=2 |doi=10.1159/000053362}}</ref>。根据18-19世纪[[法国|法国]][[人类学家|人类学家]][[喬治·居維葉|喬治·居維葉]]的理论，蒙古人种与[[高加索人种|高加索人种]]、[[尼格罗人种|尼格罗人种]]并列为人类的三大种族。蒙古人种有一些常见的生物学特征（表型），比如[[內眥贅皮|內眥贅皮]]、 {{link-en|中国型牙齿和巽他型牙齿|sinodonty|中国型牙齿|Sinodonty and Sundadonty}}、[[铲形门齿|铲形门齿]]以及{{link-en|幼态维持|neoteny}}<ref name="Mongolian Spot">[http://www.drgreene.com/21_1143.html Mongolian Spot] DrGreen.com</ref><ref>[http://www.tokyo-med.ac.jp/genet/msp/index.htm Mongolian Spot] - English information of Mongolian spot, written by Hironao NUMABE, M.D., Tokyo Medical University.</ref>。根据今天的观点，蒙古人种在分类学上已不再有意义，仅是一个历史沿用名称，因为将人类分为三大种族的学说并不准确<ref name="Frisby2013">{{cite book|author=Craig L. Frisby|title=Meeting the Psychoeducational Needs of Minority Students: Evidence-Based Guidelines for School Psychologists and Other School Personnel|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=wX8ehZ4Vgq0C&pg=PA571|date=11 March 2013|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=978-0-470-94075-4|pages=571}}</ref>。

在[[西方國家|西方國家]]，类似于“Albinist”过去曾是用于形容患[[白化病|白化病]]的白人的隐语，蒙古人种（Mongoloid）也曾用于指[[唐氏综合症|唐氏综合症]]的患者。但这种用法因为具有冒犯性，目前已不常用<ref name="Smay and Armelagos">{{cite web |last1=Smay |first1=Diana |last2= Armelagos |first2=George |publisher= Emory University |title=Galileo Wept: A Critical Assessment of the Use of Race in Forensic Anthropology |url=http://www.anthropology.emory.edu/FACULTY/ANTGA/Web%20Site/PDFs/Galileo%20Wept-%20A%20Critical%20Assessment%20of%20the%20Use%20of%20Race%20in%20Forensic%20Anthropology.pdf |format=PDF }}</ref><ref name=Lieberman>{{cite journal |last=Lieberman |first=Leonard |title= Out of Our Skulls: Caucasoid, Mongoloid, Negroid?|url= http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/an.1997.38.9.56/abstract |doi=10.1111/an.1997.38.9.56 |volume=38 |journal=Anthropology News |pages=56}}</ref><ref name=Templeton>{{cite web |last=Templeton |first=Alan R. |work=Washington University |title=Human Races: A Genetic and Evolutionary Perspective |url=http://www.realfuture.org/GIST/Readings/Templeton(1998).pdf |format=PDF |publisher=Realfuture.org }}</ref><ref name=Keevak>Keevak, Michael. "Becoming Yellow: A Short History of Racial Thinking". Princeton: Princeton University Press, 2011. {{ISBN|978-0-691-14031-5}}.</ref>。

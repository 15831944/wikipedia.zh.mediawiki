'''托马斯·爱迪生纪念桥'''（{{lang-en|Thomas A. Edison Memorial Bridge}}）<ref>Ohio Revised Code [http://codes.ohio.gov/orc/5533.18 5533.18]</ref>，常称为'''爱迪生桥'''（{{lang-en|Edison Bridge}}）<ref>{{Gnis|1040081|Edison Bridge}}</ref>，是[[2号俄亥俄州州道|2号俄亥俄州州道]]和 [[269号俄亥俄州州道|269号俄亥俄州州道]]共用的通过桑达斯基湾的桥梁，长约{{Convert|2049|ft|0}}<ref name="odnr">{{Cite web|url=http://coastal.ohiodnr.gov/portals/coastal/pdfs/atlas/CH13_transportation.pdf|title=Transportation: Bridges in the Coastal Region|accessdate=2015-04-11|year=2007|work=Ohio Coastal Atlas |publisher=[[Ohio_Department_of_Natural_Resources|Ohio Department of Natural Resources]]}}</ref>，于1965年完成<ref name="sandlib">{{Cite web|url=http://sanduskyhistory.blogspot.com/2007/04/sandusky-bay-bridge-linked-two-counties.html|title=Sandusky Bay Bridge Linked Two Counties|accessdate=2008-04-07|date=2007-04-30|work=[[Sandusky,_Ohio|Sandusky]] Library Archives Research Center}}</ref>，以附近的[[米兰_(俄亥俄州)|米兰]]出生的发明家[[爱迪生|托马斯·爱迪生]]的名字命名<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.newspapers.com/newspage/4807664/|title=Edison Bridge Bill Wins OK in House|date=1965-02-23|work=[[Sandusky_Register|Sandusky Register]]|accessdate=2015-04-11}}</ref>。

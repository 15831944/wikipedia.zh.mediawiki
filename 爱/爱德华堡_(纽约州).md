'''爱德华堡'''（Fort Edward）是[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]][[华盛顿县_(纽约州)|华盛顿县]]的一个城镇。2011年人口普查的人口为10,205人。 它包含华盛顿县的县城，<ref name="About">{{cite web|url=http://www.fortedwardnewyork.net/about.htm|title=About Fort Edward|publisher=|quote=The Town is also home to the Washington County Office Building housing nearly all the county offices and support groups.|accessdate=July 7, 2009|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091115093807/http://www.fortedwardnewyork.net/about.htm|archivedate=2009年11月15日}}</ref><ref name="Address">{{cite web |url=http://www.co.washington.ny.us/Departments/bos/bos1.htm |title=Board of Supervisors |quote=383 Broadway |publisher=Washington County |accessdate=July 13, 2009 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090630130315/http://www.co.washington.ny.us/Departments/bos/bos1.htm |archivedate=2009年6月30日 }}</ref>位于哈得孙福尔斯和爱德华堡村之间[[4号美国国道|4号美国国道]]沿线的市政中心综合大楼。<ref name="GOOGLEMAPS">[https://www.google.com/maps/place/383+Broadway,+Fort+Edward,+NY+12828/@43.2883897,-73.5876703,15z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x89dfd1615729474b:0x77fa9be54967835e Google Maps (383 Broadway, Fort Edward, New York)], Retrieved Jan. 14, 2015.</ref><ref name="NYCG">[http://www.nycourts.gov/courts/4jd/washington/index.shtml New York State Unified Court System (Washington County)], Retrieved Jan. 14, 2015.</ref> 1994年建成后，大部分行政办公室都从原县城哈得孙福尔斯迁到了这个地方。<ref name="TOK">[http://www.kingsburyny.gov/goverment.html Town of Kingsbury, New York], Retrieved Jan. 14, 2015.</ref> 爱德华堡镇是[[格伦斯福尔斯|格伦斯福尔斯]]大都会统计区的一部分。<ref>{{cite web |title= Metropolitan Areas and Components, 1999, with FIPS Codes |url= http://www.census.gov/population/estimates/metro-city/99mfips.txt |accessdate= July 7, 2009 |publisher= US Census Bureau}}</ref>

该镇还有一个名叫爱德华堡的村庄。 两者均位于县西部边界。

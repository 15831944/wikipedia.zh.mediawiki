'''[[愛沙尼亞|愛沙尼亞]]'''歷史上和傳統上是一個[[基督新教|基督新教]][[路德宗|路德宗]]國家<ref name="IvkovićHaberfeld2015">{{cite book|last1= Ivković|first1= Sanja Kutnjak|last2= Haberfeld|first2= M.R.|title= Measuring Police Integrity Across the World: Studies from Established Democracies and Countries in Transition|date= 2015-06-10|publisher= Springer |language= en |isbn= 9781493922796|page= 131|quote= Estonia is considered Protestant when classified by its historically predominant major religion (Norris and Inglehart 2011) and thus some authors (e.g., Davie 2003) claim Estonia belongs to Western (Lutheran) Europe, while others (e.g., Norris and Inglehart 2011) see Estonia as a Protestant ex-Communist society.}}</ref><ref name=MarshallCavendish2010/><ref name="Rausing2004">{{cite book|last= Rausing|first= Sigrid|title= History, Memory, and Identity in Post-Soviet Estonia: The End of a Collective Farm|year= 2004|publisher= Oxford University Press|language= en |isbn= 9780199263189|page= 96|quote= Protestantism has done much to inform the moral world view of the Estonians, particularly the process of distinguishing themselves from the Russians.}}</ref>，然而，愛沙尼亞是世界上“宗教信仰比例低”的國家之一，只有14％的人宣稱宗教是其他們的日常生活重要組成部分。 <ref>{{cite web|url= http://euobserver.com/social/27587|title= Estonians least religious in the world|date= 2009-02-11 |accessdate= 2014-01-09|publisher= [[EU_Observer|EU Observer]]}}</ref>

愛沙尼亞宗教人口主要是[[基督徒|基督徒]]，宗派大都屬於[[路德宗|路德宗]]和[[正教會|正教會]]。<ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.postimees.ee/1218764/eestis-on-90-usuvoolu-lilla-leegi-hoidjad-kopimistid-tulekummardajad|title= Eestis on 90 usuvoolu: lilla leegi hoidjad, kopimistid, tulekummardajad...|trans-title= Estonia has 90 religious affiliations: Keepers of the violet flame, Kopimists, Fire worshipers|date= 2013-04-29 |accessdate= 2014-01-09|publisher= [[Postimees|Postimees]]}}</ref>在[[蘇聯|蘇聯]]的[[無神論|無神論]]政策下，“大多數家庭的宗教傳統鏈條被打破”。<ref name=MarshallCavendish2010/><ref>{{cite web |title=Is Estonia really the least religious country in the world? |last=Ringvee |first=Ringo |date=2011-09-16 |publisher=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] |accessdate=2014-10-14 |quote=For this situation there are several reasons, starting from the distant past (the close connection of the churches with the Swedish or German ruling classes) up to the Soviet-period atheist policy when the chain of religious traditions was broken in most families. In Estonia, religion has never played an important role on the political or ideological battlefield. The institutional religious life was dominated by foreigners until the early 20th century. The tendencies that prevailed in the late 1930s for closer relations between the state and Lutheran church [...] ended with the Soviet occupation in 1940.}}</ref>第二次世界大戰前，愛沙尼亞約有80％的新教基督徒 ；屬於[[路德宗|路德宗]]。

在2001年至2011年的人口普查中，正教會信仰人數超越了路德宗，成為該國最大的[[基督教派系|基督宗教教派]]。然而，[[路德宗|路德宗]]仍然是[[愛沙尼亞人|愛沙尼亞人]]中最受歡迎的宗教教派（11％是路德教派），[[正教會|正教會]]主要由的斯拉夫族裔（約佔45％）所信奉。根據[[塔爾圖大學|塔爾圖大學]]指出，不信教的愛沙尼亞人不一定是[[無神論者|無神論者]]，[[新異教主義|新異教主義]]、佛教徒和印度教徒宣稱自己是“不信教”的人。<ref>Martin Noorkõiv (6 November 2012). [http://blog.ut.ee/the-estonian-atheist-experiment/ "The Estonian Atheist Experiment"]. University of Tartu Blog.</ref>

==歷史==
[[File:Religious_map_of_Estonia.png|thumb]]

[[File:Religious_differences_in_Estonia.png|thumb]]
公元十三世紀，[[條頓騎士團|條頓騎士團]]把基督宗教傳進了愛沙尼亞，在[[宗教改革|宗教改革]]前，愛沙尼亞地區主要教會為[[天主教|天主教]]。在宗教改革期間，{{link-en|愛沙尼亞福音路德教會| Estonian Evangelical Lutheran Church}}變成主要教會。<ref name="FrancoeurNoonan2004">{{cite book|last1=Francoeur|first1=Robert T.|last2=Noonan|first2=Raymond J.|title=The Continuum Complete International Encyclopedia of Sexuality|year=2004|publisher=[[A&C_Black|A&C Black]]|isbn=9780826414885|page=361|quote=The dominant religion in Estonia is Evangelical Lutheranism. Estonians were Christianized by the Teutonic Knights in the 13th century. During the Reformation, Lutheranism spread, and the church was officially established in Estonia in 1686.}}</ref> 在[[第二次世界大戰|第二次世界大戰]]之前，愛沙尼亞約有80％是新教基督徒，絕大多數屬於路德教派，也有[[歸正宗|歸正宗]]及其他新教教派。Robert T. Francoeur和Raymond J. Noonan寫道：“1925年，教會與國家分離，但宗教教育仍在學校教授，牧師在[[塔爾圖大學|塔爾圖大學]]神學院受訓。隨著蘇聯的佔領和{{link-en|蘇聯反宗教立法的執行|implementation of anti-Christian legislation}}，基督新教教會失去了三分之二以上的神職人員，教會財產被國有化，神學院被關閉。愛沙尼亞許多教堂1941年至1944年在德國佔領愛沙尼亞時被摧毀。蘇聯解體後，反宗教的法律被廢除。<ref name="Francoeur2004">{{cite book|last1=Francoeur|first1=Robert T.|last2=Noonan|first2=Raymond J.|title=The Continuum Complete International Encyclopedia of Sexuality|year=2004|publisher=[[A&C_Black|A&C Black]]|isbn=9780826414885|page=361|quote=It was not until 1998 that the state's religious policies became tolerant, and by 1990, repressive legislation was annulled. }}</ref>

==人口統計==
不到三分之一的愛沙尼亞人有宗教信仰； 其中大部分是正教會（主要是斯拉夫族裔）或[[路德宗|路德宗]]教派。還有一些少數其他宗教教派：[[穆斯林|穆斯林]]，[[猶太教|猶太教]]和[[佛教|佛教]]。

===調查===
*[[蓋洛普|蓋洛普]] 2006-2008年的一項調查顯示，14％的愛沙尼亞人表示“宗教是你日常生活的重要組成部分”這個問題是積極的，這在所有調查的143個國家中是最低的。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gallup.com/poll/114211/Alabamians-Iranians-Common.aspx|title=What Alabamians and Iranians Have in Common|date=2009-02-09|publisher=[[The_Gallup_Organization|Gallup]]|first1=Steve|last1=Crabtree|first2=Brett|last2=Pelham|accessdate=2010-02-21}}</ref>
*2015年[[皮尤研究中心|皮尤研究中心]]的一項調查發現，愛沙尼亞人口中有51％宣稱是基督徒，45％是無宗教的（這個類別包括無神論者，不可知論者和表態“沒有一定信仰”的人），而2％屬於其他信仰。<ref name="Religious Belief in Central and Eastern Europe">{{cite web |author=ANALYSIS |url=http://assets.pewresearch.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/11/2017/05/10104119/CEUP-FULL-REPORT.pdf |title=Religious Belief and National Belonging in Central and Eastern Europe |date=2017-05-10 |accessdate=2017-05-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170513130508/http://assets.pewresearch.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/11/2017/05/10104119/CEUP-FULL-REPORT.pdf |archivedate=2017年5月13日 }}</ref>基督徒中東正教25％，路德宗20％，其他基督徒的5％和天主教1％。<ref>[http://www.pewforum.org/2017/05/10/religious-belief-and-national-belonging-in-central-and-eastern-europe/ Religious Belief and National Belonging in Central and Eastern Europe: National and religious identities converge in a region once dominated by atheist regimes]</ref>非宗教人士有9％無神論者，1％[[不可知論者|不可知論者]]和35％“沒有什麼特定信仰”。<ref>[http://www.pewforum.org/2017/05/10/religious-affiliation/ Religious Belief and National Belonging in Central and Eastern Europe: 1. Religious affiliation]; [[Pew_Research_Center|Pew Research Center]], 10 May 2017</ref>
*[[國際社會調查計畫|國際社會調查計畫]]2015年調查發現，愛沙尼亞人口有57.0％宣布屬於一個基督宗教教派（東正教27.6％，路德宗26.0％，其他基督宗派3.3％），只有38.9％的人宣稱沒有宗教信仰。<ref>{{Cite journal|last=|first=|date=2015|title=Country specific religious affiliation or denomination: Estonia - weighted|url=http://zacat.gesis.org/webview/velocity?v=2&previousmode=table&stubs=http%3A%2F%2F193.175.238.79%3A80%2Fobj%2FfVariable%2FZA6770_V174&weights=http%3A%2F%2F193.175.238.79%3A80%2Fobj%2FfVariable%2FZA6770_V441&regMod=corr&analysismode=table&study=http%3A%2F%2F193.175.238.79%3A80%2Fobj%2FfStudy%2FZA6770&gs=5&V174slice=0&mode=table&top=yes&executepdf=true|journal=International Social Survey Programme: Work Orientations IV - ISSP 2015|volume=|pages=|via=[[GESIS_–_Leibniz_Institute_for_the_Social_Sciences|GESIS]]}}</ref>

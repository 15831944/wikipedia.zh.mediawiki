'''爱丁堡皇家学会会员'''(FRSE)是授予[[爱丁堡皇家学会|爱丁堡皇家学会]]所认定的「在所处学科中非常杰出的」个人<ref name=frse>{{cite web|url=https://www.se.org.uk/fellows/|location=Edinburgh|title=Fellows of the Royal Society of Edinburgh (FRSE)|publisher=royalsoced.org.uk|author=Anon|year=2016}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。

==选举==
每年三月份将会推选出约50名新[[院士|院士]]<ref name=frse/> {{As of|2016}}。截至2016年度，共选举出了1650名会员，其中包括71名荣誉院士(HFRSE)和76名正式院士<ref name=frse/><ref name=frse2>{{cite web|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160330014912/https://www.royalsoced.org.uk/cms/files/fellows/lists/fellows.pdf |author=Anon|year=2016||archivedate=2016-03-30|url=https://www.royalsoced.org.uk/cms/files/fellows/lists/fellows.pdf |publisher=[[爱丁堡皇家学会|爱丁堡皇家学会]]|location=Edinburgh|title=Royal Society of Edinburgh Fellows as of 2016-05-13}}</ref>。

[[院士|院士]]们拥有使用“FRSE”[[勋衔|勋衔]]的资格。

==院士==

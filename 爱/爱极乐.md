'''爱极乐'''（{{lang-ms|Ayer Keroh}}）為位於[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[马六甲|马六甲]]的城鎮，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.malacca.ws/ayer-keroh/|title=Ayer Keroh in Malacca|work=malacca.ws}}</ref>區域內設有爱极乐工業區及Taman Tasik Utama工業區。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mphtj.gov.my/en/kawasan-perindustrian-ayer-keroh |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2015-05-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150526132403/http://www.mphtj.gov.my/en/kawasan-perindustrian-ayer-keroh |archivedate=2015-05-26 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mphtj.gov.my/en/kawasan-perindustrian-taman-tasik-utama |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2015-05-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150526132050/http://www.mphtj.gov.my/en/kawasan-perindustrian-taman-tasik-utama |archivedate=2015-05-26 |df= }}</ref>為马六甲马尼帕尔医学院的所在地。

'''指示病例'''（{{lang-en|index case}}），又称为'''原发病例'''（{{lang-en|primary case}}），在[[流行病学|流行病学]]调查中是指在一定人群中的最初的病例。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://science.education.nih.gov/supplements/nih1/diseases/other/glossary/act1-gloss3.htm |title=Diseases - Activity 1 - Glossary, page 3 of 5 |publisher=Science.education.nih.gov |date= |accessdate=2010-11-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100607201109/https://science.education.nih.gov/supplements/nih1/diseases/other/glossary/act1-gloss3.htm |archivedate=2010-06-07 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://wordnetweb.princeton.edu/perl/webwn?s=index%20case |title=WordNet Search - 3.0 |publisher=Wordnetweb.princeton.edu |date= |accessdate=2010-11-03}}</ref>指示病例可能表示[[疾病|疾病]]的来源、可能的传播以及多次爆发之间的传染源。指示病例是表明一次爆发的首个病例。如果发现多个最初的病例的话，会依次以原发（primary）、二代（secondary）、三代（tertiary）等进行命名。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cdc.gov/ncidod/EID/vol4no4/parry.htm |title=Sporadic STEC O157 Infection: Secondary Household Transmission in Wales |publisher=Cdc.gov |date=1994-01-01 |accessdate=2010-11-03}}</ref>

"Patient Zero" 特別指[[基坦·杜加|基坦·杜加]]，他是[[艾滋病|艾滋病]]在北美传播的指示病例。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://medical-dictionary.thefreedictionary.com/Patient+Zero |title=Patient Zero - definition of Patient Zero in the Medical dictionary - by the Free Online Medical Dictionary, Thesaurus and Encyclopedia |publisher=Medical-dictionary.thefreedictionary.com |date= |accessdate=2010-11-03}}</ref>

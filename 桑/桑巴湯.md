'''桑巴'''是南部印度的一种菜汤，用秋葵、番茄、土豆、洋葱、扁豆等各种蔬菜制成，一般加入辣椒粉、葫芦巴种子等调味。

根據食物歷史學家{{tsl|en|K. T. Achaya||K. T. Achaya}}的說法，文獻中現存對該食物的最早記載可追溯到17世紀<ref>{{cite book |author=G. J. V. Prasad |chapter=Idli, Dosai, Sambar, Coffee: Consuming Tamil Identity |editor1=Shweta Rao Garg |editor2=Deepti Gupta |title=The English Paradigm in India: Essays in Language, Literature and Culture |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kHk2DwAAQBAJ&pg=PA99 |year=2017 |publisher=Springer Singapore |isbn=978-981-10-5332-0 |pages=98–99}}</ref>。在一個故事中，[[馬拉塔帝國|馬拉塔帝國]]統治者[[桑巴吉|桑巴吉]]因其主廚不在，向湯中添入[[酸豆|酸豆]]，無意間發明了該湯<ref>[https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/chennai/Sambar-the-great-Tamil-dish-of-Maharashtrians/articleshow/43514999.cms Sambar: the great Tamil dish of Maharashtrians]</ref>。

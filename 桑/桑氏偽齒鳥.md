'''桑氏偽齒鳥'''（''Pelagornis sandersi）''是一種已經[[絕種|絕種]]的[[鳥類|鳥類]]，化石發現於2,500萬年前的[[漸新世|漸新世]][[恰特期|恰特期]]。<ref name=ibtimes>{{cite web|date=2014-07-07|accessdate=2014-07-07|url=http://www.ibtimes.co.uk/pelagornis-sandersi-worlds-biggest-bird-was-twice-big-albatross-24ft-wingspan-1455666|title=Pelagornis Sandersi: World's Biggest Bird Was Twice as Big as Albatross with {{convert|24|ft|m}} Wingspan|work=[[International_Business_Times|International Business Times]]|author=Osborne, Hannah|archivedate=2014-07-07|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140707213208/http://www.ibtimes.co.uk/pelagornis-sandersi-worlds-biggest-bird-was-twice-big-albatross-24ft-wingspan-1455666|deadurl=no}}</ref>桑氏偽齒鳥的[[翼展|翼展]]估計約在{{convert|6.1|and|7.4|m|ft|abbr=on}}之間<ref name="PNAS-20140707">{{Cite journal | last1 = Ksepka | first1 = D. T. | date = 2014-07-07 | title = Flight performance of the largest volant bird | journal = [[Proceedings_of_the_National_Academy_of_Sciences|Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences]] | volume =  111| issue =  | pages =  10624–10629| publisher =  | jstor =  | doi = 10.1073/pnas.1320297111 | url = http://www.pnas.org/content/early/2014/07/02/1320297111.abstract | format =  | accessdate = 2014-07-08 | pmid=25002475 | pmc=4115518}}</ref><ref name=bbc/>，為目前發現到翼展最大的鳥類，為現存[[漂泊信天翁|漂泊信天翁]]{{convert|3.5|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}翼展的兩倍大。<ref name=bbc>{{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-28164063|title=Fossil of 'largest flying bird' identified|date=2014-07-07|accessdate=2014-07-08|first=Rebecca|last=Morelle|publisher=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|archivedate=2014-07-07|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140707223539/http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-28164063|deadurl=no}}</ref>目前翼展第二大的鳥類為[[阿根廷巨鷹|阿根廷巨鷹]]，估計約在{{convert|5.09|and|6.07|m|ft|abbr=on}}之間。

桑氏偽齒鳥的生活模式可能和現存的信天翁一樣，在海上[[滑翔|滑翔]]與尋找食物。<ref name=feltman>{{cite news|date=2014-07-07|accessdate=2014-07-11 |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/health-science/a-newly-declared-seabird-species-may-be-the-largest-to-ever-live/2014/07/07/0940783c-05e4-11e4-8a6a-19355c7e870a_story.html|title=A newly declared species may be the largest flying bird to ever live|work=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]]|author=Feltman, Rachel}}</ref>

==發現歷史==
目前唯一所知桑氏偽齒鳥的化石樣本來自於1983年的[[南卡羅萊納州|南卡羅萊納州]]查爾斯頓國際機場，由欲在此建造新航廈的建築工人發現，這塊區域的地質年代為2,500萬年前，在當時是一片海洋。<ref name=feltman/>物種命名緣由來自考古團隊的領導Albert Sanders ，為查爾斯頓博物館的前館長。<ref name=fox>{{cite web|url=http://www.foxnews.com/science/2014/07/07/world-largest-flying-bird-was-like-nothing-alive-today/|title=World's largest flying bird was like nothing alive today|agency=[[Fox_News|Fox News]]|date=2014-07-07|accessdate=2014-07-07|author=Choi, Charles Q.|deadurl=no|archivedate=2014-07-08|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140708085728/http://www.foxnews.com/science/2014/07/07/world-largest-flying-bird-was-like-nothing-alive-today/}}</ref>化石目前展示在查爾斯頓博物館內，在2014年由 Dan Ksepka 定為新種。<ref name=ibtimes/>

==外型特徵==
桑氏偽齒鳥的腳十分粗短，可能只能透過跳下懸崖的形式起飛，飛行速度估計可達{{convert|60|km/h|mph|abbr=on}}。<ref name=guardian>{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/science/2014/jul/07/fossils-airport-largest-flying-bird-pelagornis-sandersi|title=Fossils dug up at airport may be largest flying bird ever found|work=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|date=2014-07-07|accessdate=2014-07-07|author=Karim, Nishad|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140708084812/http://www.theguardian.com/science/2014/jul/07/fossils-airport-largest-flying-bird-pelagornis-sandersi|archivedate=2014-07-08|deadurl=no}}</ref>和所有偽齒鳥科的成員一樣，桑氏偽齒鳥在喙的邊緣上也有類似牙齒的突起，這些突起可以協助生物更能緊緊咬住滑溜的獵物。

==與翼龍比較==
桑氏偽齒鳥的翼展{{convert|6.1|-|7.4|m|ft|abbr=on}}為翼展最大的鳥類，但並不是翼展最大的飛行生物。一些[[翼龍|翼龍]]如[[哈特茲哥翼龍|哈特茲哥翼龍]]、[[風神翼龍|風神翼龍]]，其翼展估計可達{{convert|10|-|11|m|ft|abbr=on}}。<ref name="BBC-2014">{{cite web|title=Hatzegopteryx|date=2014|work=[[BBC_Nature|BBC Nature]]|accessdate=2014-07-08|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/nature/life/Hatzegopteryx|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140327225235/http://www.bbc.co.uk/nature/life/Hatzegopteryx|archivedate=2014-03-27|deadurl=yes}}</ref>

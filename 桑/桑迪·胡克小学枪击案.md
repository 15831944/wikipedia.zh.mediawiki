'''桑迪·胡克小学校園枪击案'''（{{lang-en|'''Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting'''}}）是一宗2012年12月14日在[[美国|美国]][[康涅狄格州|康涅狄格州]][[費爾菲爾德縣_(康乃狄克州)|費爾菲爾德縣]][[纽敦_(康涅狄格州)|纽敦鎮]][[桑迪·胡克小學|桑迪·胡克小學]]发生的枪击案，当中28人（包括20名儿童）死亡。在枪手亚当·兰扎（{{lang-en|Adam Lanza}}）自杀之前，18名儿童和6名成人在学校被杀。另有3名儿童受重伤被送至医院，其中2名在医院中死亡。<ref name="cbc" />枪手的哥哥瑞安·兰扎（{{lang-en|Ryan Lanza}}）被康涅狄格州警察和[[FBI|FBI]]讯问，但没有被称为嫌犯。<ref name="CNN2"/>这是美国历史上死亡人数第二多的校园枪击案。

==枪击==
2012年12月14日，在上午9:30之前，亚当·兰扎被相信在他们位于纽敦的家中枪杀了他的母亲，52岁的南希·兰扎。侦查员后来在她的床上发现了她的尸体，穿着睡衣，有头部中4枪的证据。<ref name="NPRAP12-16-12"/>亚当驾驶他母亲的车来到桑迪·胡克小学<ref name="Gunman dead after killing 20 children, 6 adults at Connecticut elementary school"/><ref name="esposito" />，强行进入了大楼<ref name="nytimesForcedEntry"/><ref name="Newtown school shooting: Sandy Hook School"/>亚当·兰扎<ref name="bigstory">{{cite web|title=AP SOURCE: SUSPECT DROVE TO MOTHER'S SCHOOL|url=http://bigstory.ap.org/article/ap-source-20-year-old-suspect-had-ties-school|publisher=AP|accessdate=2012-12-14}}</ref>身穿黑色服装及橄榄绿色马甲，佩戴耳塞，携带来福枪弹匣<ref name="clothing">{{cite web|url=http://www.tampabay.com/news/nation/frustrating-search-for-newtown-conn-shooter-adam-lanzas-motive/1267513|work=The Washington Post|title=Frustrating search for Newtown, Conn., shooter Adam Lanza's motive|date=December 24, 2012|accessdate=December 28, 2012}}</ref><ref name="clothing_2">{{cite web|url=http://www.courant.com/news/connecticut/newtown-sandy-hook-school-shooting/hc-sandyhook-lanza-earplugs-20130106,0,2370630.story|title=Sandy Hook Shooter Adam Lanza Wore Earplugs|work=Hartford Courant|first1=Dave|last1=Altimari|first2=Jon|last2=Lender|date=January 6, 2013|accessdate=January 7, 2013}}</ref>。媒体早先报道他身穿[[護甲|護甲]]<ref name="Tragedy at an Elementary School"/><ref name="Authorities Search for Motives Behind Elementary School Shooting Massacre"/>是错误的<ref name="clothing_3">{{cite web|url=http://www.nhregister.com/articles/2012/12/27/news/doc50dce5187860b303917697.txt|work=New Haven Register|location=New Haven, Connecticut|title=Newtown shooter Adam Lanza was not wearing body armor during massacre|date=December 27, 2012|accessdate=December 28, 2012}}</ref>。

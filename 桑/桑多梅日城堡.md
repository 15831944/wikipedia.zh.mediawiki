'''桑多梅日皇家城堡'''（Sandomierz Royal Castle）是位於[[波蘭|波蘭]]城市[[桑多梅日|桑多梅日]]的一座中世紀時期的城堡建築。這座城堡始建於[[卡齊米日三世|卡齊米日三世]]時期，並在16世紀擴建<ref>{{cite book | author = Maria Sołtysiak, Katarzyna Wierzbicka| coauthors = | title = Museums in Poland: guidebook| year = 2001| editor = | page =214 | pages = | chapter = | chapterurl = | publisher =Wydawnictwo DiG| location = | isbn =83-7181-198-5| url = | format = | accessdate = }}</ref>。原本的城堡在1656年被毀<ref name="bedford">{{cite book | author = Neal Bedford| coauthors = | title = Poland| year = 2008 | editor = | page =226 | pages = | chapter = | chapterurl = | publisher =Lonely Planet | location = | isbn = 1-74104-479-0| url = | format = | accessdate = }}</ref>。後來該城堡被改建為文藝復興風格建築，西翼則是博物館。

'''桑索斯'''（{{lang-grc-gre|Ξάνθος}}）是一名[[底比斯_(希臘)|底比斯]]王，是第16位<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=8pIaAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA192&lpg=PA192&dq=xanthus+a+theban+kings&source=bl&ots=vCG_Pf_9Qx&sig=e6BjXZ13vl6U6MCfp4XeIRGYcPc&hl=en&ei=w4-RTs6NL8O38gO044Au&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=6&ved=0CD4Q6AEwBTgK#v=onepage&q=xanthus%20a%20theban%20kings&f=false An universal history: The ancient part, Volume 6 (Google Books) T. Osborne, 1747] retrieved 13:16 9.10.11</ref> 同时也是底比斯的末代君主。<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=-JugHJqR3ZcC&pg=PA68&lpg=PA68&dq=Xanthus,+a+Theban+king&source=bl&ots=24oAfG0Iou&sig=eICU12plX8gNdIddTpygCUmcF6I&hl=en&ei=oYuRTtumDcK68gPAuNko&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=2&ved=0CCEQ6AEwATgK#v=onepage&q=Xanthus%2C%20a%20Theban%20king&f=false  from page numbered sixty eight,The Rights of War and Peace, in Three Books: Wherein Are Explained, the Law of Nature and Nations, and the Principal Points Relating to Government written Hugo Grotius, Jean Barbeyrac] – retrieved 9.10.11</ref> 他统治时间可能很短暂，有时被称作[[玻俄提亚|玻俄提亚]]人的国王，底比斯人的国王。他被[[阿提卡|阿提卡]]的{{le|美兰托斯|Melanthus}}杀死。<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=CSatY6CEYuYC&pg=PA432&lpg=PA432&dq=xanthus+a+theban+kings&source=bl&ots=u_MSPJccac&sig=mzjReGgE_e73QMm2aS1X04De0_I&hl=en&ei=jo2RTuCaGYWz8QPlqsgp&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=3&sqi=2&ved=0CC8Q6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=xanthus%20a%20theban%20kings&f=false  page 432 A  history of Greece, Volume 1 (Google Books) Connop Thirlwall]</ref>

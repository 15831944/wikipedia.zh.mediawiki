'''桑德撞擊坑'''（'''Sander'''）是一個位於[[水星|水星]][[卡洛里盆地|卡洛里盆地]]內的撞擊坑。它的撞擊坑壁是暗色區域，而在它的底部有數個明亮區域。和[[芭蕉撞擊坑|芭蕉撞擊坑]]不同的是，它的射紋系統並未完全發展，但在本質上是明亮的<ref>[http://messenger.jhuapl.edu/gallery/sciencePhotos/image.php?gallery_id=2&image_id=195 "A Closer Look at Albedo and Color Variations on Mercury"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140714130344/http://messenger.jhuapl.edu/gallery/sciencePhotos/image.php?gallery_id=2&image_id=195 |date=2014-07-14 }}, Johns Hopkins University, [[約翰·霍普金斯大學應用物理實驗室|Applied Physics Laboratory]]</ref>。該撞擊坑以德國攝影師{{link-en|奧古斯特·桑德|August Sander}}命名<ref>[http://messenger.jhuapl.edu/gallery/sciencePhotos/image.php?page=1&gallery_id=2&image_id=292 "A Trio of Craters: Munch, Sander, and Poe"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140714164139/http://messenger.jhuapl.edu/gallery/sciencePhotos/image.php?page=1&gallery_id=2&image_id=292 |date=2014-07-14 }}, Johns Hopkins University, Applied Physics Laboratory</ref>。

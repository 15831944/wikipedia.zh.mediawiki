'''德里克图片书馆'''（{{lang-en|'''Drik Picture Library Ltd.'''}}），是一个孟加拉国达卡的图片书馆。创始人是[[沙希杜尔·阿拉姆|沙希杜尔·阿拉姆]]。

2015年，沙希杜爾·阿拉姆举办由秘鲁学者{{le|豪尔赫·维拉科尔塔|Jorge Villacorta}}策划的展览“Crossfire”，广受好评。然而，展覽其後被警方关闭，导致全国范围内的抗议爆发<ref>{{cite news| url=http://lens.blogs.nytimes.com/2010/03/16/showcase-137/ | work=The New York Times | first=David | last=Gonzalez |title=Where Death Squads Struck in Bangladesh | date=2010-03-16|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.demotix.com/news/283513/crossfire-exhibition-sparks-angry-police-reaction |title='Crossfire' exhibition sparks angry police reaction |work=Demotix |date=2010-03-22 |author=Bayazid Akter |accessdate=2015-03-31 |language=en |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150102040943/http://www.demotix.com/news/283513/crossfire-exhibition-sparks-angry-police-reaction |archivedate=2015年1月2日 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{citation|url=http://www.shahidulnews.com/drik-photo-power |author=Satish Sharma |date=n.d. |title=Drik: Photo power |accessdate=2015-03-31|language=en}}</ref><ref name=Representing>{{citation |title=Representing 'Crossfire': politics, art and photography |url=http://www.shahidulnews.com/representing-'crossfire'-politics-art-and-photography |author=Rahnuma Ahmed |date=n.d. |accessdate=2015-03-31 |language=en }}{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。德里克的律师向政府发出法律通知后，警方的路障方被移走。法院的回应和随后的事件，使得展览馆能够在3月31日开放展览，供公众参观<ref name=Representing/>。

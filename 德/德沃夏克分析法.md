{{engname|德沃夏克分析法|'''Dvorak Technique'''}}是由[[維農·德沃夏克|維農·德沃夏克]]（{{lang|en|Vernon Dvorak}}）根據多年經驗及統計概括後所創下的一項利用[[地球同步衛星|地球同步衛星]]的[[可見光|可見光]]及[[紅外線|紅外線]]衛星雲圖來評估[[熱帶氣旋|熱帶氣旋]]強度的方法。

德沃夏克分析法是現時國際通行的分析法。這套分析法在1984年發表，1987年正式由[[世界氣象組織|世界氣象組織]]通過使用。

==数值测定==
大部分地區都會把德沃夏克分析法作為確定熱帶氣旋最大持續風速的主要方法，這種方法是以[[氣象衛星|氣象衛星]]的觀測數值來進行判斷<ref name="ODT">{{cite web | author1 = Christopher S. Velden |author2 = Timothy L. Olander | author3 = Raymond M. Zehr | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140223085207/http://tropic.ssec.wisc.edu/misc/adt/odt/odt.html | title= Objective Dvorak Technique | url = http://tropic.ssec.wisc.edu/misc/adt/odt/odt.html | publisher = University of Wisconsin–Madison | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | archivedate = 2014-02-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>，通過對螺旋[[雨帶|帶]]的規模和風眼與風眼牆之間的溫度差來確定熱帶氣旋的最大持續風速和[[氣壓|氣壓]]<ref>{{cite web | author = Chris Landsea | date = 2010-06-08 | url = http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/H1.html | title = Subject: H1) What is the Dvorak technique and how is it used? | publisher = Atlantic Oceanographic and Meteorological Laboratory | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | archivedate = 2014-01-25 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140125190925/http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/H1.html | deadurl = no}}</ref>。[[低氣壓|低氣壓區]]中心的中心[[氣壓|中心氣壓]]值為估計值。颶風的強度則是根據[[登陸_(氣象學)|登陸]]時間和最大強度一起得出<ref name="NHC SSHS">{{cite web | author = National Hurricane Center | title = Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Scale Information | publisher = National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration | date = 2006-06-22 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20140111191521/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutsshws.php | archivedate = 2014-01-11 |url = http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/aboutsshws.php | accessdate = 2014-02-23 | deadurl = no}}</ref>。對精細衛星圖像上單獨雲層的追蹤還可以用來進一步估計熱帶氣旋的表面風速<ref>{{cite web | author1 = A. F. Hasler | author2 = K. Palaniappan | author3 = C. Kambhammetu | author4 = P. Black | author5 = E. Uhlhorn | author6 = D. Chesters | url = http://journals.ametsoc.org/doi/abs/10.1175/1520-0477(1998)079%3C2483%3AHRWFWT%3E2.0.CO%3B2 | title = High-Resolution Wind Fields within the Inner Core and Eye of a Mature Tropical Cyclone from GOES 1-min Images | accessdate = 2014-02-23}}</ref>。

如果可以的話，科研人員也會使用船舶和陸地的觀測數據。[[大西洋|大西洋]]以及[[太平洋|太平洋]]的中部和東部仍然會動用偵察機飛入熱帶氣旋來確定飛行高度層的風力而[[太平洋|太平洋]]西北部则由香港天文台委托飞行服務队下投探空仪确定风力，這個數據加以調整後可以對最大持續風速做出相當可靠的估算。經過之前十年使用[[全球定位系統|全球定位系統]][[投落送|投落送]]後得出的大量數值已經證實，將飛行高度層取樣的風速減少10%以後，就可以用來對接近地表的最大持續風速作出估算<ref name="Franklin">{{cite journal | author1 = James L. Franklin | author2 = Michael L. Black | author3 = Krystal Valde | url = http://cat.inist.fr/?aModele=afficheN&cpsidt=15034652 | title = GPS dropwindsonde wind profiles in hurricanes and their operational implications | issn = 0882-8156 | accessdate = 2014-02-23 |page = 32-44 |year = 2003 |volume=18 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120921172526/http://cat.inist.fr/?aModele=afficheN&cpsidt=15034652 | deadurl = no | archivedate = 2012-09-21 | journal = Weather and forecasting}}</ref>。[[氣象雷達|多普勒氣象雷達]]也可以用來以相同的方法確定接近陸地熱帶氣旋的表面風速<ref>{{cite journal | author1 = J. TUTTLE | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120921172543/http://cat.inist.fr/?aModele=afficheN&cpsidt=1801644 | author2 = R.

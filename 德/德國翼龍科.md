'''德國翼龍科'''（Germanodactylidae）是[[翼龍目|翼龍目]][[翼手龍亞目|翼手龍亞目]]的一科，目前包含：[[德國翼龍|德國翼龍]]、[[諾曼底翼龍|諾曼底翼龍]]、[[敦達古魯翼龍|敦達古魯翼龍]]。

德國翼龍科的分類位置仍有爭議，大部份研究將牠們歸類於[[準噶爾翼龍超科|準噶爾翼龍超科]][[演化支|演化支]]<ref name=fastnacht2005>Fastnacht, M. (2005). "The first dsungaripterid pterosaur from the Kimmeridgian of Germany and the biomechanics of pterosaur long bones." ''Acta Palaeontologica Polonica'', '''50''' (2): 273–288. {{cite web |url=http://www.dinopark.de/ice/incoming/www.dinopark.de/extarticle/image_2/The%20first%20dsungaripterid%20pterosaur.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2011-10-04 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120425045530/http://www.dinopark.de/ice/incoming/www.dinopark.de/extarticle/image_2/The%20first%20dsungaripterid%20pterosaur.pdf |archivedate=2012-04-25 }}</ref>，也有研究將牠們歸類於[[梳頜翼龍超科|梳頜翼龍超科]]<ref name=luji2006>Lu J. and Ji Q. (2006). "Preliminary results of a phylogenetic analysis of the pterosaurs from western Liaoning and surrounding areas." ''Journal of the Paleontological Society of Korea'', '''22'''(1): 239-261.

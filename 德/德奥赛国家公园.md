'''德奥赛国家公园'''（{{lang-ur|{{nastaliq|دیوسائی نیشنل پارک}}}}，[[巴尔蒂语|巴尔蒂语]]：{{nastaliq|غبیارسہ}}）位于[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]北部，绝大部分位于[[吉尔吉特-巴尔蒂斯坦|吉尔吉特-巴尔蒂斯坦]][[阿斯多县|阿斯多县]]内的一片[[高山气候|高山气候]]平原上<ref>{{Cite web|title=Deosai National Park The 2nd Highest Plateau in The World|url=http://www.skardu.pk/deosai-national-park/|work=Skardu.pk|date=2016-06-11|language=en|last=Editor|author=|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190309061643/http://www.skardu.pk/deosai-national-park/|archive-date=2019-03-09|dead-url=no}}</ref>，横跨阿斯多县、[[斯卡杜县|斯卡杜县]]和{{Link-en|卡尔芒谷|Kharmang Valley}}，平均海拔{{convert|4114|m|ft}}<ref>{{Cite book|title=Climate change impacts on high-altitude ecosystems|last=Öztürk|first=Münir|publisher=Springer|year=2015|isbn=9783319128597|location=|pages=|last2=Hakeem|first2=Khalid Rehman|last3=Faridah-Hanum|first3=I.|last4=Efe|first4=Recep}}</ref>。“德奥赛”在乌尔都语中意为“巨人之地”，而此地的巴尔蒂语名称意为“夏之地”。该地知名于丰富的动植物资源，春季可见大量野花和[[蝴蝶|蝴蝶]]。

== 游览路线 ==
游客可由北面的斯卡杜县，或从西面的阿斯多县进入此国家公园。从斯卡杜县出发的路线是最短的。[[巴尔蒂斯坦|巴尔蒂斯坦]]{{Link-en|古尔塔利谷|Gultari Valley}}的居民常来此旅游，但很少有人在此定居。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Deosai: Anything but plain|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/208818/deosai-anything-but-plain/|work=The Express Tribune|date=2011-07-17|language=en|author=|publisher=|accessdate=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181107043931/https://tribune.com.pk/story/208818/deosai-anything-but-plain/|archive-date=2018-11-07|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Deosai Plains: Welcome to surreal Pakistan|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/1178524|work=DAWN.COM|date=2015-04-27|language=en|first=Syed Mehdi|last=Bukhari|author=|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181223102715/https://www.dawn.com/news/1178524|archive-date=2018-12-23|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=My search for the elusive ‘giant’ of Deosai|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/1250740|work=DAWN.COM|date=2016-04-08|language=en|first=Osman Ehtisham|last=Anwar|author=|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190116145740/https://www.dawn.com/news/1250740|archive-date=2019-01-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 文化提及 ==
法国学者{{Internal link helper/en|米歇尔·佩思尔|Michel Peissel}}声称，生活于德奥赛的[[喜马拉雅旱獭|喜马拉雅旱獭]]就是[[公元前5世纪|公元前5世纪]]的[[希腊|希腊]]历史学家[[希罗多德|希罗多德]]所述的“{{Link-en|掘金蚁|Gold-digging ant}}”，人类可利用这种动物发掘黄金。当地的部落有从喜马拉雅旱獭的洞穴中发掘[[砂金|砂金]]的习惯<ref>{{Cite book|title=The Ants' Gold: The Discovery of the Greek El Dorado in the Himalayas|last=Peissel|first=Michel|publisher=Collins|year=1984|isbn=978-0-00-272514-9|location=|pages=}}</ref>。

此外，巴基斯坦歌手{{Link-en|阿里·扎法尔|Ali Zafar}}也正从事与一部以德奥赛高原为主题的电影的工作<ref>{{Cite web|title=Just in: Ali Zafar dishes on Deosai, his first film production!|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/1182711|work=DAWN.COM|date=2015-05-18|language=en|first=Entertainment|last=Desk|author=|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181107010214/https://www.dawn.com/news/1182711|archive-date=2018-11-07|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Ali Zafar’s dream to culminate on the heights of Deosai|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/888322/ali-zafars-dream-to-culminate-on-the-heights-of-deosai/|work=The Express Tribune|date=2015-05-18|language=en|author=|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181107040906/https://tribune.com.pk/story/888322/ali-zafars-dream-to-culminate-on-the-heights-of-deosai/|archive-date=2018-11-07|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Ali Zafar is fighting to get fit for role in upcoming film 'Deosai'|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/901087/ali-zafar-is-fighting-to-get-fit-for-role-in-upcoming-film-deosai/|work=The Express Tribune|date=2015-06-10|language=en|author=|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181107042418/https://tribune.com.pk/story/901087/ali-zafar-is-fighting-to-get-fit-for-role-in-upcoming-film-deosai/|archive-date=2018-11-07|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

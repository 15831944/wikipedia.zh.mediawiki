'''德莱木'''（'''Daylam'''、'''Daylaman'''、'''Dailam'''、'''Deylam'''、'''Deilam'''），是[[伊朗|伊朗]][[吉蘭省|吉蘭省]]内陆山区的名字。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=hvx9jq_2L3EC&lpg=PP1&pg=PA198&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=daylam&f=false|title=The Cambridge History of Iran|last=Frye|first=Richard Nelson|last2=Fisher|first2=William Bayne|last3=Madelung|first3=W.|date=1975-06-26|publisher=Cambridge University Press|year=|isbn=9780521200936|location=|pages=|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=hvx9jq_2L3EC&dq=the+cambridge+history+of+iran+4&hl=da&source=gbs_navlinks_s&redir_esc=y|title=The Cambridge History of Iran|last=Frye|first=Richard Nelson|last2=Fisher|first2=William Bayne|last3=Bosworth|first3=C. E.|date=1975-06-26|publisher=Cambridge University Press|year=|isbn=9780521200936|location=|pages=|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.iranicaonline.org/articles/buyids|title=BUYIDS - Encyclopaedia Iranica, Vol. IV, Fasc. 6|last=Tilman|first=Nagel|date=1990|website=www.iranicaonline.org|publisher=Routledge & Kegan Paul|location=London u.a.|pages=578–586|language=en|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-03-04}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=arELc_R1orcC&dq=The+Buwayhid+Dynasty+in+Iraq+334H/945+to+403H/1012:+Shaping+Institutions+for+the+future&hl=da&source=gbs_navlinks_s&redir_esc=y|title=The Buwayhid Dynasty in Iraq 334h., 945 to 403h., 1012: Shaping Institutions for the Future|last=Donohue|first=John J.|date=2003-01-01|publisher=BRILL|year=|isbn=9004128603|location=|pages=|language=en|access-date=3 February 2014}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=I_9tAAAAMAAJ&q=The+Buwayhid+dynasty+of+Baghdad,+334/946-447/1055&dq=The+Buwayhid+dynasty+of+Baghdad,+334/946-447/1055&hl=da&sa=X&ei=L6nvUsiEGcfl4QT3noHQAQ&redir_esc=y|title=The Buwayhid Dynasty of Baghdad, 334/946-447/1055|last=Kabir|first=Mafizullah|date=1964-01-01|publisher=Iran Society|year=|isbn=|location=|pages=|language=en|access-date=3 February 2014}}</ref>以地名命名其居民，称为[[德莱木人|德莱木人]]（Daylamites）。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.iranicaonline.org/articles/deylamites#pt2|title=DEYLAMITES – Encyclopaedia Iranica, Vol. BII, Fasc. 4|last=Wilferd Madelung|first=Wolfgang Felix,|date=1995|website=www.iranicaonline.org|pages=342–347|language=en|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-03-04}}</ref>

《[[明史|明史]]》作'''答儿密'''，服属[[撒马儿罕|撒马儿罕]]（[[帖木儿帝国|帖木儿帝国]]）。居海中，地不满百里，人不满千家。没有城郭，上下都住板屋。懂得耕种，产物有毛褐、布缕、[[马|马]]、[[骆驼|骆驼]]、[[牛|牛]]、[[羊|羊]]。刑罚只有捶打。交易兼用银钱。[[永乐|永乐]]年间遣使到[[中国|中国]]朝贡，赐给他《[[大统历|大统历]]》和文绮、[[药|药]]、[[茶|茶]]等物<ref>《明史》西域传四</ref>。

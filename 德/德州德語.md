'''德州德語'''（{{lang-en|Texas German}}；{{lang-de|Texasdeutsch}}）是一種[[德語|德語]]的方言、從19世紀中葉開始由定居於[[美國|美國]][[德克萨斯州|德克萨斯州]]的[[德國|德國]]移民後裔所使用。這些[[德州德國人|德州德國人]]（German Texans）在{{tsl|en|Texas Hill Country|德州山鄉}}建立一些新城鎮如[[新布朗費爾斯_(德克薩斯州)|新布朗費爾斯]]、[[弗雷德里克斯堡_(德克薩斯州)|弗雷德里克斯堡]]、{{tsl|en|Boerne|伯尼 (德克薩斯州)|伯尼}}，{{tsl|en|Walburg|瓦爾伯}}，及{{tsl|en|Comfort|康佛特}}。及在德州山鄉以東的{{tsl|en|Schulenburg|舒倫堡}}和[[魏瑪|魏瑪]]等城鎮。

==歷史和文檔==
大多數幾代的美國[[德州德國人|德州德國人]]亦持續在自己定居後的家園和社區講德語。在[[第一次世界大戰|第一次世界大戰]]期間德州教育法規建立強制性僅以英語教學，無論在校外面講什麼語言、要求孩子在學校裡必須學習英語。在[[第一次世界大戰|第一次世界大戰]]和[[第二次世界大戰|第二次世界大戰]]期間，由於這些社區[[文化偏見|文化偏見]]（Cultural bias）的增加，德州德語的使用者傾向說英語，而僅有一些德州德國人將德語傳給後代。<ref>{{Cite news| last = O'Connor| first = Kyrie| title = Texas German dying out: language of settlers aging with its users| work = San Antonio Express-News| accessdate = 2013-06-02| date = 2013-03-10| url = http://www.mysanantonio.com/news/local_news/article/Texas-German-dying-out-4343915.php}}</ref>

Some 1,035 people report speaking German at home in [[Fredericksburg,_Texas|Fredericksburg]],<ref>[http://www.mla.org/map_data_results&state_id=48&place_id=27348&cty_id=%20 MLA Language Map Data Center results, Fredricksburg, Texas, all languages]</ref> the town with the largest community of Texas German speakers, representing 12.48% of the total population, 840 in [[New_Braunfels,_Texas|New Braunfels]],<ref name="mla1">[http://www.mla.org/cgi-shl/docstudio/docs.pl?map_data_results MLA Language Map Data Center results, Fredericksburg, Texas]</ref> 150 in [[Schulenburg|Schulenburg]],<ref name="mla1"/> 85 in [[Stonewall,_Texas|Stonewall]],<ref>[http://www.mla.org/map_data_results&state_id=48&place_id=70460&cty_id=%20 MLA Language Map Data Center results, Stonewall, Texas]</ref> 70 in [[Boerne|Boerne]],<ref name="mla1"/> 65 in [[Harper,_Texas|Harper]],<ref>[http://www.mla.org/map_data_results&state_id=48&place_id=32456&cty_id=%20 MLA Language Map Data Center results, Harper, Texas]</ref> 45 in [[Comfort,_Texas|Comfort]]<ref>[http://www.mla.org/map_data_results&state_id=48&place_id=16228&cty_id=%20 MLA Language Map Data Center results, Comfort, Texas]</ref> and 19 in [[Weimar,_Texas|Weimar]],<ref name="mla1"/> all of which except for Schulenburg and Weimar, lie in the traditional Texas German heartland of the Hill Country. In all 82,100 German-speakers reside in the state of Texas,<ref name="mla1"/> including European German speakers.

==德語和英語比較==
Texas German is intelligible to anyone with an understanding of continental German, though it adapted to U.S. measurement and legal terminologies. German words were invented or English was "Germanicized" for words not present in 19th century German. In some cases, these new words also exist in modern Standard German, but with a different meaning. For instance, the word ''Luftschiff'' (used for "airplane") means [[airship|airship]] in Standard German.

'''德克薩斯休斯敦聖殿'''（Houston Texas Temple）是[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的第97座聖殿。1997年，耶穌基督後期聖徒教會宣布將在休斯敦建設聖殿<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/31692/Land-owner-remembers-promise-made-to-the-Lord.html |title= Land owner remembers promise made to the Lord |first= Wendy |last= Nielsen |first2= Harriet |last2= Rasmussen |date= June 20, 1998 |newspaper= [[Church_News|Church News]] }}</ref>。2000年，休斯敦聖殿舉行了奉獻儀式<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/38406/HOUSTON-TEXAS-How-glorious-and-complete-is-Thy-plan.html |title= HOUSTON TEXAS: 'How glorious and complete is Thy plan' |date= Sep 2, 2000  |newspaper= Church News }}</ref>。休斯敦聖殿面積{{convert|33970|sqft|m2}}<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/38394/Facts-and-figures-Houston-Texas-Temple.html |title= Facts and figures: Houston Texas Temple |date= Sep 2, 2000 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref>。

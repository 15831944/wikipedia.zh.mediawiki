'''德特勒夫·布赫霍尔茨'''（{{lang-de|'''Detlev Buchholz'''}}，{{bd|1944年|5月31日}}），[[德国|德国]][[理论物理学|理论物理学家]]。他的研究领域为[[量子场论|量子场论]]，尤其是代数量子场论。 

== 部分出版物 ==
* ''Quantenfeldtheorie ohne Felder'', Physik Journal Bd.7, 2008, Nr.8/9 (Article in German on Buchholz's receipt of the Planck medal)
* [[arxiv:math-ph/0011044|Buchholz „Algebraic QFT – a status report“, Plenary Talk 12. International Congress Mathematical Physics, 2000]]
* [[arxiv:hep-th/9910243|Haag, Buchholz „The Quest for understanding relativistic quantum physics“, J.Math.Phys. 2000]]
* [[arxiv:hep-th/9811233|Buchholz „Current Trends in Axiomatic QFT'', Ringberg Symposium 1998, Lecture Notes in Physics, Bd. 558, 2000, S.43'']]

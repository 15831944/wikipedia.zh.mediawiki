'''德里巨擘足球會'''（'''Delhi Dynamos Football Club'''）是一支位於[[印度|印度]][[德里|德里]]的職業足球會，目前於[[印度超級聯賽|印度超級聯賽]]角逐。<ref name=danish>{{cite web|title=Junker og Skoubo drager til Indien|url=http://www.bold.dk/nyt/junker-og-skoubo-drager-til-indien|publisher=bold.dk|accessdate=15 July 2014|date=15 July 2014}}</ref><ref name="Cities Announced">{{cite web|last=Basu|first=Saumyajit|title=Stars embrace soccer through Indian Super League|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/sports/football/indian-super-league/top-stories/Stars-embrace-soccer-through-Indian-Super-League/articleshow/33712666.cms#write|work=Times of India|accessdate=22 April 2014}}</ref> The team is owned and operated by [[Delhi|Delhi]]-based [[DEN_Networks|DEN Networks]].<ref name="Cities Announced"/>

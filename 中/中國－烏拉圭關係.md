'''中國－烏拉圭關係'''是中國與烏拉圭之間的外交關係。中國於[[蒙得維的亞|蒙得維的亞]]設有大使館，<ref>[http://embassy-finder.com/china_in_montevideo_uruguay Embassy of the PRC in Montevideo]</ref>烏拉圭於[[北京|北京]]設有大使館<ref>[http://embassy-finder.com/uruguay_in_beijing_china Embassy of Uruguay in Beijing]</ref>及在[[香港|香港]]設有一領事館。<ref>[http://embassy-finder.com/uruguay_in_hong-kong_hong-kong Consulate of Uruguay in Hong Kong]</ref>兩國均為[[77國集團|77國集團]]成員。

== 歷史 ==
烏拉圭曾經與[[中華民國|中華民國]]擁有外交關係，直至1988年與[[中華人民共和國|中華人民共和國]]建交後才斷交。<ref>[http://spanish.china.org.cn/xi-lamei/guanxi/3.htm Uruguayan President visits China] {{es icon}}</ref>烏拉圭的華人移民相對較少。

兩國有以下幾項協定。
*[https://web.archive.org/web/20150403000642/http://www0.parlamento.gub.uy/htmlstat/pl/convenios/conv16881.htm Mutual investment promotion and protection agreement (1993)] {{es icon}}
*[https://web.archive.org/web/20160304135052/http://www.parlamento.gub.uy/htmlstat/pl/convenios/conv16514.htm Scientific and technological cooperation agreement (1993)] {{es icon}}
*[https://web.archive.org/web/20080618051614/http://www.parlamento.gub.uy/htmlstat/pl/convenios/conv18226.htm Preferential loan (2006)] {{es icon}}

中國是烏拉圭最重要的貿易伙伴。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.elobservador.com.uy/noticia/242279/china-se-convirtio-en-el-mayor-socio-comercial-de-uruguay/ |title=China, Uruguay's most important trading partner |date=28 January 2013 |publisher=[[El_Observador_(Uruguay)|El Observador]] |access-date=2015-03-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130131173317/http://www.elobservador.com.uy/noticia/242279/china-se-convirtio-en-el-mayor-socio-comercial-de-uruguay/ |archive-date=2013-01-31 |dead-url=yes }} {{es icon}}</ref>

2013年5月，烏拉圭總統[[何塞·穆希卡|何塞·穆希卡]]到達北京進行正式訪問。<ref>{{cite web|title=Official visit of José Mujica to China|accessdate=15 September 2013|publisher=Presidencia de la República|url=http://medios.presidencia.gub.uy/jm_portal/2013/noticias/NO_J214/Agenda.pdf}} {{es icon}}</ref>

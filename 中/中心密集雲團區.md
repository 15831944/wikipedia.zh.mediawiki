'''中心密集雲團區'''（{{lang-en|'''Central dense overcast'''}}，簡稱：'''CDO'''）是圍繞熱帶氣旋之中心的大型雲團區域，由其眼壁的發展而形成。它可以是圓形、有角、橢圓形或不規則的形狀<ref name="Vern">{{cite journal|author=Dvorak, Vernon F.|title=A Technique For the Analysis and Forecasting of Tropical Cyclone Intensities From Satellite Pictures|pages=5–8|date=1973-02|publisher=National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration}}</ref>，這個特徵出現在熱帶風暴或颶風強度的熱帶氣旋中。在[[風暴|風暴]]達到颶風強度後，[[風眼|風眼]]就會在中心密集雲團區的中心形成。中心密集雲團區與風眼的距離以及中心密集雲團區內的雲與風眼之間的溫差有助於確定熱帶氣旋的強度。強度不斷變化的熱帶氣旋在其中心密集云團區內，有比穩定的風暴更多的閃電。使用[[衛星圖像|衛星圖像]]中熱帶氣旋的中央密集雲團區也可用於確定其強度。熱帶氣旋中的[[最高持續風速|最高持續風速]]以及最高的降雨量通常位於中央密集雲團區的最冷雲層之下。

==特徵==
[[File:Winston_2016-02-12_1200Z.png|thumb]]
它是圍繞在衛星云圖上熱帶氣旋中心的大面積[[雷暴|雷暴]]<ref name="CDO AMS">{{cite web|date=2000-06|author=American Meteorological Society|url=http://amsglossary.allenpress.com/glossary/browse?s=c&p=19|title=AMS Glossary: C|work=Glossary of Meteorology|accessdate=2006-12-14|publisher=Allen Press|deadurl=no|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20061216012623/http://amsglossary.allenpress.com/glossary/browse?s=c&p=19|archivedate=2006-12-16}}</ref><ref name="FAQ CDO">{{cite web| author=Landsea, Chris| title=What is a "CDO"?| date=2005-10-19| publisher=Atlantic Oceanographic and Meteorological Laboratory| accessdate=2006-06-14| url=http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/tcfaqHED.html| deadurl=no| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060615212545/http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/tcfaqHED.html| archivedate=2006-06-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=A Satellite Classification Technique For Subtropical Cyclones|page=9|author1=Hebert, Paul H.  |author2=Kenneth O. Poteat|date=July 1975|publisher=National Weather Service Southern Region Headquarters}}</ref>。當熱帶氣旋的眼壁開始發展，其中央密集雲團區便會形成<ref>{{cite book|title=Hurricanes of the North Atlantic: Climate and Society|page=3|author1=Elsner, James B.  |author2=A. Birol Kara|ISBN=978-0195125085|publisher=Oxford University Press|date=1999-06-10}}</ref>；其發展之前可以是窄而密集的C形對流帶。在發展初期，雲團區的形狀通常是有角或橢圓形的，隨著熱帶氣旋增強，雲團區的尺寸逐漸增大，並且顯得更為圓滑<ref>{{cite journal|journal=Monthly Weather Review|page=422|volume=103|title=Tropical Cyclone Intensity Analysis and Forecasting From Satellite Imagery|author=Dvorak, Vernon F.|date=1975-05|doi=10.1175/1520-0493(1975)103<0420:tciaaf>2.0.co;2|bibcode = 1975MWRv..103..420D }}</ref>，並形成成熟的螺旋雲帶。在低[[垂直風切變|垂直風切變]]的環境中，中心密集雲團區會較圓<ref name="FAQ CDO"/>
。熱帶氣旋中惡劣的天氣位於中央密集雲團區內最深的[[對流|對流]]；但對於處於穩定狀態的熱帶氣旋，中央密集雲團區內幾乎完全沒有[[閃電|閃電]]活動<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vaisala.com/Vaisala%20Documents/Scientific%20papers/Long%20range%20lightning%20nowcasting%20applications%20for%20meteorology.pdf|title=Long Range Lightning Nowcasting Applications For Meteorology|author=Demetriades, Nicholas W.S.|author2=Martin J. Murphy|author3=Ronald L. Holle|last-author-amp=yes|publisher=Vaisala|date=2005-06-22|accessdate=2012-08-12|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151002112512/http://www.vaisala.com/Vaisala%20Documents/Scientific%20papers/Long%20range%20lightning%20nowcasting%20applications%20for%20meteorology.pdf|archivedate=2015-10-02}}</ref>。

[[熱帶氣旋|熱帶氣旋]]內最強的風一般是位於中心密集雲團區內最深的[[對流|對流]]下，在[[衛星圖像|衛星圖像]]上會被顯示為最冷的{{tsl|ja|雲頂|雲頂_(氣象學)|雲頂}}。<ref name="HSU"/>[[最大风速半径|最大风速半径]]通常在中心密集雲團區最冷的雲層範圍內<ref name="HSU">{{cite web|author1=Hsu, S. A.|author2=Adele Babin|url=http://www.nwas.org/ej/hsu/hsu_babin_2005.pdf|title=Estimating the Radius of Maximum Winds Via Satellite During Hurricane Lili (2002) Over the Gulf of Mexico|accessdate=2007-03-18|date=February 2005|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120206133851/http://www.nwas.org/ej/hsu/hsu_babin_2005.pdf|archivedate=2012-02-06|df=}}</ref>，亦為熱帶氣旋降雨最強的區域<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://www.mri-jma.go.jp/Publish/Technical/DATA/VOL_14/14_abs.pdf|title=The Study on the Changes of the Three-dimensional Structure and the Movement Speed of the Typhoon through its Life Time|author=Muramatsu, Teruo|journal=Tech. ==風眼==
中央密集雲團區中心的風眼是天氣平靜的地區。[[風暴|風暴]]的風眼是一個大致圓形的區域，[[直徑|直徑]]通常是30-65公里。氣旋最低的[[氣壓|氣壓]]會在風眼測得，可能比風暴以外的[[大氣壓力|大氣壓力]]低15％<ref name="FAQ eye">{{cite web | author=Landsea, Chris | author2=Sim Aberson | last-author-amp=yes | title=What is the "eye"? | date=2004-08-13 | publisher=Atlantic Oceanographic and Meteorological Laboratory | accessdate=2006-06-14 | url=http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/A11.html | deadurl=no | archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20110704015040/http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/tcfaq/A11.html | archivedate=2011-07-04 }}</ref>。但在較弱的熱帶氣旋中，風眼不會太清晰<ref name="FAQ eye"></ref>。

==強度預測==
[[File:Dvorak1984DevelopmentalPatterns.png|thumb]]
在熱帶氣旋的強度估計中，有幾種模式可以用來確定其強度的上限和下限。中央密集雲團區模式是其中之一，利用中央密集雲團區的大小來預計，同時也會考慮中央密集雲團區的形狀。風眼越靠近雲團區，風暴越強勁。中央密集雲團區模式強度從熱帶風暴強度開始，相當於每小時64公里<ref name="evolution">{{cite journal|url=http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/pdf/06velden.pdf|title=The Dvorak Tropical Cyclone Intensity Estimation Technique: A Satellite-Based Method That Has Endured For Over 30 Years|author=Velden, Chstopher|author2=Bruce Harper|author3=Frank Wells|author4=John L. Beven II|author5=Ray Zehr|author6=Timothy Olander|author7=Max Mayfield|author8=Charles “Chip” Guard|author9=Mark Lander|author10=Roger Edson|author11=Lixion Avila|author12=Andrew Burton|author13=Mike Turk|author14=Akihiro Kikuchi|author15=Adam Christian|author16=Philippe Caroff|author17=Paul McCrone|last-author-amp=yes|date=2006-09|pages=1195-1214|doi=10.1175/bams-87-9-1195|journal=Bulletin of the American Meteorological Society|accessdate=2012-09-26|bibcode=2006BAMS...87.1195V|deadurl=no|archiveurl=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20081107145950/http://www.nhc.noaa.gov/pdf/06velden.pdf|archivedate=2008-11-07}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=Objectively Determining the Rotational Center of Tropical Cyclones in Passive Microwave Satellite Imagery|author1=Wimmers, Anthony J.  |author2=Christopher S. Velden|date=September 2012|volume=49|number=9|publisher=American Meteorological Society|journal=Journal of Applied Meteorology and Climatology|pages=2013–2034|doi=10.1175/2010jamc2490.1|bibcode = 2010JApMC..49.2013W }}</ref>。

同時，中心密集雲團區的範圍增大，是熱帶氣旋逐漸增強的其中一個特徵。

但在每小時105公里和每小時160公里之間最高持續風速的熱帶氣旋可能會令可見光和紅外衛星圖像上的中心變得模糊，致使判斷其強度成為一個更大的挑戰<ref>{{cite web|url=https://docs.google.com/viewer?a=v&q=cache:TNjsYx3-4tYJ:https://ams.confex.com/ams/30Hurricane/webprogram/Manuscript/Paper205619/hurrconf_2012_archer_extabs.pdf+Objectively+Determining+the+Rotational+Center+of+Tropical+Cyclones+in+Passive+Microwave+Satellite+Imagery+Anthony+J.+Wimmers+and+Christopher+S.+Velden&hl=en&gl=us&pid=bl&srcid=ADGEEShshgH1vysQyzdH8CmUQ2cUchwb6JJdg8q2-vIiIJ8uf5nFrmjAtTXCMD2PFrFah5R6Hl5q9A3x1-gV5iiLUzeVQbXO0D35UokP9lPv3wG6KVCjTOQp8zDmToJZzaKoiIXCEdP4&sig=AHIEtbTlbbUiMfUOQ_0o0lCPuM4jkc47bw|title=Advances in Objective Tropical Cyclone Center Fixing Using Multispectral Satellite Imagery|author1=Wimmers, Anthony  |author2=Chistopher Velden|year=2012|accessdate=2012-08-12|publisher=American Meteorological Society}}</ref>。

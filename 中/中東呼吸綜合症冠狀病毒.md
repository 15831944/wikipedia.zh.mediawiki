'''中東呼吸系統綜合症冠狀病毒'''是導致[[中東呼吸系統綜合症|中東呼吸系統綜合症]]的[[病原體|病原體]]（[[英文|英文]][[縮寫|縮寫]]：{{lang|en|MERS-CoV}} 或代號：{{lang|en|EMC/2012}}『HCoV-EMC/2012』）引起<ref name=deGroot>{{cite journal|author=De Groot RJ |title=Middle East Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus (MERS-CoV): Announcement of the Coronavirus Study Group |journal=Journal of Virology |date=2013-05-15 | doi=10.1128/JVI.01244-13 |pmid=23678167 |pmc=3700179 |volume=87 |issue=14 |pages=7790–2|author-separator= |author2=and others |displayauthors=1 }}</ref>，有別於[[嚴重急性呼吸道綜合爭冠狀病毒|嚴重急性呼吸道綜合爭冠狀病毒]]和普通[[傷風|傷風]][[冠狀病毒|冠狀病毒]]。<ref name="rapid">{{cite web|url=http://www.ecdc.europa.eu/en/publications/Publications/novel-coronavirus-rapid-risk-assessment-update.pdf|title=ECDC Rapid Risk Assessment - Severe respiratory disease associated with a novel coronavirus|date=2013-02-19|accessdate=08-06-2015}}</ref>中東冠狀病毒具[[核糖核酸病毒|單鏈核糖核酸]]，為 {{lang|la|β}}冠狀病毒屬的新成員，按[[基因組|基因組]]區別被分為了甲、乙兩大[[系統發生學|系統發生]][[支序分類學|分支]]；最早期的感染病例是由甲類病毒造成，與後來的不同。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://wwwnc.cdc.gov/eid/article/20/6/14-0299_article.htm|title=MERS Coronaviruses in Dromedary Camels, Egypt|date=06-2014|accessdate=08-06-2015}}</ref>

中東冠狀病毒主要襲擊[[支氣管|支氣管]]上的無[[纖毛|纖毛]][[上皮細胞|上皮細胞]]。它能避開宿主體內的起始[[免疫反應|免疫反應]]，並阻止免疫細胞分泌[[干擾素|干擾素]]。此[[向性|向性]]在眾多呼吸系統病毒裡實屬獨特，因為其他病毒大多都襲擊纖毛上皮細胞。<ref name="Kindler-2013">{{Cite journal|last1=Kindler|first1=E.|last2=Jónsdóttir|first2=H. ==傳播途徑==
2010年至2013年间进行的一项研究，在310头单峰[[骆驼|骆驼]]的身上评估MERS的发病率，揭示出MERS冠状病毒中和抗体在这些动物的血清中的滴度高<ref name=Hemida12dec2013>{{cite journal | last=Hemida | first=MG | display-authors=etal | title=Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) coronavirus seroprevalence in domestic livestock in Saudi Arabia | journal=Eurosurveillance | date=2013-12-12 | volume=18 | issue=50 |url=http://www.eurosurveillance.org/ViewArticle.aspx?ArticleId=20659}}{{open access}}</ref>。进一步对沙特单峰骆驼鼻拭子上的MERS冠状病毒进行的研究测序，发现它们的序列以前与以前的人类测序隔离。从个别骆驼的身上还发现鼻咽处有一个以上的基因组变体<ref name=Briese29apr2014>{{cite journal | url = http://mbio.asm.org/content/5/3/e01146-14 | title = Middle East Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus Quasispecies That Include Homologues of Human Isolates Revealed through Whole-Genome Analysis and Virus Cultured from Dromedary Camels in Saudi Arabia | last = Briese | first = T. | display-authors=etal | date = 2014-04-29 | journal = mBio | accessdate = 2014-05-20 | doi = 10.1128/mBio.01146-14}} {{open access}}</ref>。据报一名沙特男子给几头生病骆驼的鼻子敷药后七天发病，后来他和一只骆驼都被发现带有相同的MERS冠状病毒毒株。现在尚未明确病毒从骆驼传播给人类的方法<ref name=BBC5jun2014>{{cite news | url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/health-27695140 | title=Camel infection 'led to Mers death'| first=James | last=Gallagher | work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] | date=2014-06-05 | accessdate=2014-06-05}}</ref><ref name=Azhar4jun2014>{{cite journal | url = http://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa1401505 | title = Evidence for Camel-to-Human Transmission of MERS Coronavirus | last = Azhar | first = E. I. | display-authors=etal | date = 2014-06-04 | journal = The New England Journal of Medicine | accessdate = 2014-06-06 | doi = 10.1056/NEJMoa1401505}}</ref>。世界卫生组织建议避免接触骆驼，只吃全熟的骆驼肉，用巴氏法杀毒的[[駱駝奶|駱駝奶]]，避免饮用骆驼尿。骆驼尿被认为是各种中东疾病的治疗药物<ref name=CTV10may2014>{{cite news | url=http://www.ctvnews.ca/health/who-warns-about-camel-products-contact-over-rising-mers-concerns-1.1815947 | title=WHO warns about camel products, contact over rising MERS concerns | CTV News | work=CTV News | date=2014-05-10 | accessdate=2014-05-22}}</ref>。沙特农业部建议人们避免接触骆驼，或是在接触时佩戴呼吸面罩<ref name=BBC11may2014>{{cite news | url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-27362513 | title=Mers virus: Saudis warned to wear masks near camels | work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] | date=2014-05-11 | accessdate=2014-05-22}}</ref>。有些人不听政府的意见，亲吻了骆驼以表蔑视<ref name=BBC13may2014>{{cite news | url=http://www.bbc.com/news/blogs-news-from-elsewhere-27393045 | title=Saudi Arabia: Farmers flout Mers warning by kissing camels | work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]] | date=2014-05-13 | accessdate=2014-05-22}}</ref>。

有限的证据表明MERS冠状病毒不会在人与人间持续传播，无论是在家里或是在医院等卫生保健机构<ref name=HPA14mar2013>{{cite journal | url = http://www.eurosurveillance.org/ViewArticle.aspx?ArticleId=20427 | title = State of Knowledge and Data Gaps of Middle East Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus (MERS-CoV) in Humans | author = The Health Protection Agency (HPA) UK Novel Coronavirus Investigation team | date = 2013-03-14 | journal = Eurosurveillance | accessdate = 2014-05-21 | doi = 10.1371/currents.outbreaks.0bf719e352e7478f8ad85fa30127ddb8}} {{open access}}</ref><ref name=Assiri8jan2013>{{cite journal | url = http://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa1306742 | title = Hospital Outbreak of Middle East Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus | last = Assiri | first = A. | display-authors=etal | date = 2013-01-08 | journal = The New England Journal of Medicine | accessdate = 2014-05-20 | doi = 10.1056/NEJMoa1306742}}</ref>。大多数的传播发生在“密切接触医疗机构或居家危重病人的情况下”，并没有症状表明会传播给无症状感染者<ref name=WHO8aug2013>{{cite web | url = http://www.who.int/csr/disease/coronavirus_infections/MERS_home_care.pdf?ua=1 | title = Rapid advice note on home care for patients with Mi ddle East respiratory syndrome coronavirus (MERS-CoV) infecti on presenting with mild symptoms and management of contacts | date = 2013-08-08 | accessdate = 2014-05-21| publisher = [[WHO|WHO]]}}</ref>。群集范围从1人到26人，平均数为2.7<ref name=Cauchemez13nov2013>{{cite journal | url = http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1473309913703049 | title = Middle East respiratory syndrome coronavirus: quantification of the extent of the epidemic, surveillance biases, and transmissibility | last = Cauchemez | first = S. | display-authors=etal | date = January 2014 | journal = The Lancet Infectious Diseases | accessdate = 2014-05-21 | doi = 10.1016/S1473-3099(13)70304-9}}</ref>。

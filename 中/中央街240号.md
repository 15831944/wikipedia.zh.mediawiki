'''中央街240号'''（240 Centre Street）原为“纽约市警察总部”大楼（New York City Police Headquarters），位于美国[[纽约市|纽约市]][[曼哈顿|曼哈顿]]，介于布隆街（Broome Street）与格兰街（Grand Street）之间，建于1905-1909年，由Hoppin＆Koen公司设计。1909年到1973年[[纽约市警察局|纽约市警察局]]的总部设此。1988年改建为豪华[[公寓|公寓]]。现在称为'''警察大厦公寓'''。<ref name=nycland>{{cite nycland}}, p.41</ref><ref name=aia>{{cite AIA4}}, pp. 84-85</ref><ref>Cusack, Andrew. [http://www.andrewcusack.com/2006/09/27/the-old-police-headquarters/ "The Old Police Building"]</ref><ref name="NysNrhpNom">{{cite web| url = https://cris.parks.ny.gov/| title = Cultural Resource Information System (CRIS)| publisher = [[New_York_State_Office_of_Parks,_Recreation_and_Historic_Preservation|New York State Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation]]| format = Searchable database| accessdate = 2016-04-01}}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} ''Note:'' This includes {{cite web | url = https://cris.parks.ny.gov/Uploads/ViewDoc.aspx?mode=A&id=28207&q=false | title = National Register of Historic Places Registration Form: Former Police Headquarters Building | accessdate = 2016-04-01 | author1 = Virginia Kurshan | author2 = Joan R. Olshansky | author3 = Elizabeth Spencer-Ralph | format = PDF | date = August 1979 }} and [https://cris.parks.ny.gov/Uploads/ViewDoc.aspx?mode=A&id=28209&q=false ''Accompanying photographs'']</ref>

[[西奥多·罗斯福|西奥多·罗斯福]]曾在此担任纽约市警察局长<ref>Riis, Jacob A. ''The Making of an American''. 1901.</ref> 。

警察大楼于1978年被列为纽约市地标，<ref name=nycland /> 并于1980年被列入[[国家史迹名录|国家史迹名录]]。

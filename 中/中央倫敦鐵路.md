'''中央倫敦鐵路'''（{{Lang-en|Central London Railway}}，'''CLR'''），又被称为“两[[便士|便士]]地铁”（{{lang-en|Twopenny Tube}}{{notetag|name=tun|tube特指[[鑽挖式隧道|盾构法]]建造的圆形截面隧道}}），是[[倫敦|倫敦]]歷史上一条深层地铁线路，其路線相當於今日[[倫敦地鐵|倫敦地鐵]]的[[中央線_(倫敦地鐵)|倫敦地鐵]]中部部分。

倫敦中央鐵路公司設立於1889年。在于1895年成功筹资后，中央線於1896年至1900年间完成施工。1913年，倫敦中央鐵路被[[倫敦地鐵公司|倫敦地鐵公司]]收購。开通初期的中央铁路长达{{convert|9.14|km}}全部区间都位于地下，而且是[[复线铁路|复线铁路]]；全程共有13个车站，西起[[牧羊叢|牧羊丛]]，东至[[英格兰银行|英格兰银行]]，而车辆段和供电站则位于西端终点站的北边<ref name="dist">{{cite web
| title = Clive's Underground Line Guides, Central line, Layout
| publisher = Clive D. W. Feathers
| url = http://www.davros.org/rail/culg/central.html#layout
| accessdate=2010年3月30日}}</ref>。在改建为环线的计划被否决后，本线在1908年向西延长到了{{Translink|en|Wood Lane tube station (Central line)|伍德巷站 (中央铁路)|伍德巷站}}，又在1912年延长到了[[利物浦街车站|利物浦街车站]]。到1920年，本线又沿着[[大西部铁路|大西部铁路]]延长到了[[伊灵|伊灵]]，里程也增加到了{{convert|17.57|km}}<ref name="dist"/>。

在产生了数年的净利润后，由于其他地铁和公交线路的竞争，中央铁路的客流量开始减少。1913年，本线的客流量被伦敦主要的地铁运营商——[[伦敦地下电气铁路公司|伦敦地下电气铁路公司]](UERL)超越。1933年，中央铁路和UERL成为了[[国有企业|公有制企业]]。

==建造过程==
===筹划(1889–1892)===
[[File:Central_London_Railway_1889.png|thumb]]
1889年11月，中央伦敦铁路发布了一份关于{{tsl|en|Local and Personal Acts of Parliament in the United Kingdom||私人法案}}的告示。相关法案随后被展示于1890年的[[英国议会|议会]]{{tsl|en|parliamentary session|立法期间}}<ref>{{London Gazette
|date=26 November 1889
|issue=25996
|pages=6640–6642

}}</ref>。法案建议修建一条地下[[電氣化鐵路|电气化铁路]]；线路两端起点分别位于[[貝斯沃特|贝斯沃特]]里的{{tsl|en|Queensway (London)|皇后路}}和{{tsl|en|Bayswater Road|贝斯沃特路}}口和[[倫敦市|伦敦城]]里的[[威廉王街|威廉王街]]，经过贝斯沃特路、[[牛津街|牛津街]]、{{tsl|en|High Holborn|霍本高街}}、[[霍本|霍本]]、{{tsl|en|Holborn Viaduct|霍本高架}}、{{tsl|en|Newgate|纽盖特}}、[[齐普赛街|齐普赛街]]和[[家禽街_(伦敦)|家禽街]]，并连接当时还在施工的[[城市及南伦敦铁路|城市及南伦敦铁路]](C&SLR)。车站则分别位于皇后路、[[大理石拱门|大理石拱门]]、牛津圆环、[[托特纳姆宫路|托特纳姆宫路]]、{{tsl|en|Southampton Row|南安普顿道}}、{{tsl|en|Holborn Circus|霍本圆环}}、{{tsl|en|St. Martin's Le Grand|圣马丁大道}}和威廉王街地下{{sfn|Badsey-Ellis|2005|p=43}}。

隧道的设计直径为{{convert|11|ft}}，由[[隧道掘進機|盾构机]]开凿，再由[[铸铁|铸铁]]板砌成；在车站区域，隧道的直径会根据设计，在{{convert|22|ft}}和{{convert|29|ft}}之间变化。变电站和车辆段则选址在皇后路西侧的一个{{convert|1.5|acre}}的空地处。每个车站都将有从街面到地下站台的液压电梯{{sfn|Badsey-Ellis|2005|p=44}}。

然而，此计划遭到了多方的反对。
*[[大都会铁路|大都会铁路]]和{{tsl|en|District Railway|区域铁路 (伦敦)|区域铁路}}的反对最为强烈。由于中央铁路的走向与内环线{{notetag|name=IC|1=内环线由两家公司共同运营，路线大致为今[[环状线_(倫敦地鐵)|环状线]]}}相近，两家公司担心客流量会被分流。
*[[伦敦市法团|伦敦市法团]]担心施工会像C&SLR的建造一样，再次引起[[地层下陷|沉降]]，并影响路线周围的建筑。
*[[圣保罗座堂|圣保罗座堂]]的[[座堂主任牧师|主任牧师]]和[[教士|教士]]们担心施工会损毁教堂的地基。
*{{tsl|en|Joseph Bazalgette|约瑟夫·巴扎尔杰特爵士}}担心隧道会损坏城市的排水系统。
最后法案虽然在下议院通过，却被上议院否决。上议院建议推迟表决，直到C&SLR开通，其运营情况得到评估之后。{{sfn|Badsey-Ellis|2005|pp=44–45}}[[File:Central_London_Railway_1891.png|thumb]]1890年11月，C&SLR开通后，CLR向次年的立法期间提交了一份新的法案<ref>{{London Gazette
|date=25 November 1890
|issue=26109
|pages=6570–6572

}}</ref>。同时，线路西端也延长至[[牧羊丛|牧羊丛]]的东侧并经过{{tsl|en|Notting Hill Gate|诺丁山门}}和{{tsl|en|Holland Park Avenue|荷兰公园大道}}，而车辆段和供电站也改为{{tsl|en|Wood Lane|伍德路}}的东侧、终点站的北端。

{{transH}} 

The route was extended at the western end to run under Notting Hill High Street (now [[Notting_Hill_Gate|Notting Hill Gate]]) and [[Holland_Park_Avenue|Holland Park Avenue]] to end at the eastern corner of [[Shepherd's_Bush|Shepherd's Bush Green]], with the depot and power station site relocated to be north of the terminus on the east side of [[Wood_Lane|Wood Lane]]. The westward extension of the route was inspired by the route of abandoned plans for the London Central Subway, a sub-surface railway that was briefly proposed in early 1890 to run directly below the roadway on a similar route to the CLR.{{sfn|Badsey-Ellis|2005|p=47}} The eastern terminus was changed to [[Cornhill,_London|Cornhill]] and the proposed Southampton Row station was replaced by one in [[Bloomsbury|Bloomsbury]]. Intermediate stations were added at Lansdowne Road, [[Notting_Hill_Gate|Notting Hill Gate]], Davies Street (which the CLR planned to extend northwards to meet Oxford Street) and at [[Chancery_Lane|Chancery Lane]].{{sfn|Day|Reed|2008|p=52}} The earlier plan to connect to the C&SLR was dropped and the diameter of the CLR's tunnels was increased to {{convert|11|ft|6|in|m|2}}.{{sfn|Badsey-Ellis|2005|p=47}} This time the bill was approved by both Houses of Parliament and received [[Royal_Assent|Royal Assent]] on {{Nowrap|5 August}} 1891 as the ''Central London Railway Act, 1891''.<ref>{{London Gazette
|date=7 August 1891
|issue=26190
|page=4245
}}</ref> In November 1891, the CLR publicised another bill. The eastern end of the line was re-routed north-east and extended to end under the [[Great_Eastern_Railway|Great Eastern Railway's]] (GER's) terminus at [[Liverpool_Street_station|Liverpool Street station]] with the Cornhill terminus dropped and a new station proposed at the [[Royal_Exchange,_London|Royal Exchange]].<ref>{{London Gazette
|date=27 November 1891
|issue=26227
|pages=6506–6507

}}</ref> The proposals received assent as the ''Central London Railway Act, 1892'' on {{Nowrap|28 June}} 1892.<ref>{{London Gazette
|date=1 July 1892
|issue=26303
|pages=3810–3811

}}</ref>

[[File:Central_London_Railway_1892.png|thumb]]
The money to build the CLR was obtained through a [[Syndicate#Finance|syndicate]] of financiers including [[Ernest_Cassel|Ernest Cassel]], Henry Oppenheim, [[Darius_Ogden_Mills|Darius Ogden Mills]], and members of the [[Rothschild_banking_family_of_England|Rothschild family]].{{sfn|Wolmar|2005|pp=147–148}} On {{Nowrap|22 March}} 1894, the syndicate incorporated a contractor to construct the railway, the Electric Traction Company Limited (ETCL), which agreed a construction cost of £2,544,000 (approximately £{{Formatprice|{{Inflation|UK|2544000|1894|r=-4}}|0}} today){{Inflation-fn|UK}} plus £700,000 in 4 per cent [[debenture_stock|debenture stock]].{{sfn|Bruce|Croome|2006|p=5}} When the syndicate offered 285,000 CLR company [[share_(finance)|shares]] for sale at £10 each in June 1895,{{sfn|Bruce|Croome|2006|p=5}} only 14 per cent was bought by the British public, which was cautious of such investments following failures of similar railway schemes.{{sfn|Wolmar|2005|p=147}} Some shares were sold in Europe and the United States, but the unsold remainder was bought by members of the syndicate or by the ETCL.{{sfn|Bruce|Croome|2006|p=5}}

'''中國－密克羅尼西亞聯邦關係'''（{{lang-en|China–Federated States of Micronesia relations}}），是指[[中國|中國]]和[[密克羅尼西亞聯邦|密克羅尼西亞聯邦]]之間的外交關係，兩國於1989年9月11日建交。<ref name="FMPRC">{{cite web |title=Micronesia |accessdate=27 January 2010 |date=9 December 2003 |publisher=Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China |url=http://www.fmprc.gov.cn/eng/wjb/zzjg/bmdyzs/gjlb/3402/}}</ref>中國於1990年在密克羅尼西亞聯邦首都[[帕利基爾|帕利基爾]]設立大使館，並於1991年派出首位大使。<ref name=FMPRC/>起初，密克羅尼西亞聯邦駐日大使兼任駐華大使，直至其於2007年在北京設立大使館。 <ref name="FSM">{{cite web |title=FSM Government opens embassy in China |date=10 May 2007 |accessdate=27 January 2010 |publisher=Government of the Federated States of Micronesia |url=http://www.fsmgov.org/press/pr051007.htm |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100125035819/http://www.fsmgov.org/press/pr051007.htm |archive-date=2010-01-25 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>[[密克羅尼西亞聯邦總統|密克羅尼西亞聯邦總統]][[約翰·哈格萊爾加姆|約翰·哈格萊爾加姆]]在1990年成為首位訪問中國的國家元首。<ref name=FMPRC/><ref>{{cite web |title=China and FSM signed the Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement |publisher=Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China |date=3 January 2010 |url=http://www.fmprc.gov.cn/eng/wjb/zwjg/zwbd/t649524.htm}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Department of Foreign Affairs, Overseas Embassies, Consulates, and Missions |publisher=Government of the Federated States of Micronesia |accessdate=22 January 2014 |url=http://www.fsmgov.org/ovmis.html}}</ref>

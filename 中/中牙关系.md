'''中牙關係'''是指[[中國|中國]]和牙買加之間的外交關係，兩國於1972年11月21日建交。<ref name="FMPRC">{{cite web |title=Jamaica |accessdate=27 January 2010 |date=26 November 2003 |publisher=Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China |url=http://www.fmprc.gov.cn/eng/wjb/zzjg/ldmzs/gjlb/3503/}}</ref>中國自1973年就在牙買加首都[[京斯敦|-{zh-cn:金斯敦;zh-tw:京斯敦;}-]]設有大使館。<ref name=FMPRC/> 牙買加並沒有駐華外交機構，直至1992年牙買加駐日本大使館兼駐華事務為止。<ref name=FMPRC/>牙買加後來在[[北京|北京]]設立正式大使館。<ref>{{cite web |title=Jamaican Embassies and High Commissions Abroad |publisher=United Nations |date=January 2010 |accessdate=27 January 2010 |url=http://www.un.int/jamaica/missions.htm |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100522200833/http://www.un.int/jamaica/missions.htm |archivedate=2010年5月22日 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Chinese Ambassador offers educational support |date=3 October 2007 |accessdate=27 January 2010 |publisher=[[Gleaner_Company|Gleaner Company]] |url=http://www.jamaica-gleaner.com/gleaner/20071003/news/news7.html |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110811235339/http://jamaica-gleaner.com/gleaner/20071003/news/news7.html |archivedate=2011年8月11日 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Chinese Red Cross Donates US$30,000 for Hurricane Relief |date=25 August 2007 |accessdate=27 January 2010 |publisher=Government of Jamaica |url=http://www.jis.gov.jm/land_environment/html/20070824T200000-0500_12841_JIS_CHINESE_RED_CROSS_DONATES_US_30_000_FOR_HURRICANE_RELIEF.asp }}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>2007年，中國宣佈其會於2008年為超過20000名牙買加人提供技術訓練。<ref>{{cite news |title=China Commits US$500 Million for Caribbean Investments |date=21 September 2007 |accessdate=27 January 2010 |publisher=Government of Jamaica |url=http://www.jis.gov.jm/water_housing/html/20070920T100000-0500_13038_JIS_CHINA_COMMITS_US_500_MILLION_FOR_CARIBBEAN_INVESTMENTS.asp }}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

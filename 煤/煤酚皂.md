'''煤酚皂'''是一种含有[[苯酚|苯酚]]或[[甲酚|甲酚]]的，稍有[[消毒藥水|消毒作用]]的肥皂（'''药皂'''）。这两种酚类物质一般从煤炭或石油中提取。<ref>W. H. Simmons and H. A. Appleton, ''The Handbook of Soap Manufacture'', Echo Library, 2007, [https://books.google.com/books?id=Cugdqp4_KL4C&pg=PA104 p. 104].
*{{cite web|url=http://www.allwords.com/word-carbolic+soap.html |title=Definition of carbolic soap |publisher=Allwords.com |date=2007-03-29 |accessdate=2010-08-19}}</ref><ref>http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/142724/cresylic-acid</ref>

1834年，[[弗里德利布·费迪南德·龙格|弗里德利布·费迪南德·龙格]]在[[煤焦油|煤焦油]]发现了石炭酸，即[[苯酚|苯酚]]。<ref>F. F. Runge (1834) [http://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=wu.89048351654;view=1up;seq=81 "Ueber einige Produkte der Steinkohlendestillation"] (On some products of coal distillation), ''Annalen der Physik und Chemie'', '''31''' :  65-78.  On page 69 of volume 31, Runge names phenol "Karbolsäure" (coal-oil-acid, carbolic acid).  Runge characterizes phenol in:  F. F. Runge (1834) [http://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=wu.89048351654#page/308/mode/1up "Ueber einige Produkte der Steinkohlendestillation,"] ''Annalen der Physik und Chemie'', '''31''' : 308-328.</ref>1865年8月，[[约瑟夫·李斯特，第一代李斯特男爵|约瑟夫·李斯特]]博士将一块粘过苯酚溶液的棉布裹在了一位十一岁男孩的伤口上。这位病人的腿受车轮碾压造成复合骨折，在{{tsl|en|Glasgow Royal Infirmary|格拉斯哥皇家医院}}收治。四天后换药时，李斯特发现伤口未受感染；六周后，李斯特惊喜地发现男孩的骨折已经合上，且无化脓的危险。<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Lister|first1=Joseph|title=ON THE ANTISEPTIC PRINCIPLE IN THE PRACTICE OF SURGERY|journal=The Lancet|date=1867-09-21|volume=90|issue=2299|pages=353–356|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0140673602518274|doi=10.1016/s0140-6736(02)51827-4}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Lister|first1=Joseph|title=ON THE EFFECTS OF THE ANTISEPTIC SYSTEM OF TREATMENT UPON THE SALUBRITY OF A SURGICAL HOSPITAL|journal=The Lancet|date=1870-01-01|volume=95|issue=2418|pages=2–4|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S014067360231273X|doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(02)31273-X}}</ref>1894年，{{tsl|en|William Lever, 1st Viscount Leverhulme|威廉·利弗，第一代利华休姆子爵|威廉·利弗}}在市场上推出了第一款大量生产的煤酚皂，{{tsl|en|Lifebuoy}}。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.lifebuoy.com/about-us/history-of-health/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2014-10-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141006155150/http://www.lifebuoy.com/about-us/history-of-health/ |archivedate=2014-10-06 }} A History of Health, lifebuoy.com</ref> 

<!-- 特点 -->
煤酚皂最显著的特点就是其深粉色到红色的颜色，由其中的苯酚造成。苯酚可见于各种工业和日用化工品，对皮肤有时有刺激性。<ref>{{cite web |title=ATSDR - Medical Management Guidelines (MMGs): Phenol |url=https://www.atsdr.cdc.gov/mmg/mmg.asp?id=144&tid=27 |website=www.atsdr.cdc.gov |accessdate=2018-09-02 |language=en}}</ref>经常使用煤酚皂的人群可能会发现皮肤因此红肿。是为煤酚皂现已在医院内被更加温和的皮肤消毒清洁剂替代的原因之一。

<!-- 用途 -->
通过杀灭细菌，煤酚皂还可以稍微提供一些除去体臭的作用。[[国际红十字与红新月运动|国际红十字与红新月运动]]等救灾机构至今仍会给灾民提供煤酚皂。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://reliefweb.int/rw/rwb.nsf/db900SID/OCHA-64CGLW?OpenDocument |title=ReliefWeb ť Document ť West Africa Appeal No. 01.02/2001 Programme Update No. 2 |publisher=Reliefweb.int |date= |accessdate=2010-08-19}}
*{{cite web|url=http://www.verc.org/image1.jsp |title=Village Education Resource Center |publisher=VERC |date= |accessdate=2010-08-19}}</ref>

煤酚皂对于1920到1970年代的欧美人士来说，记忆深刻。直到1980年代末，[[大不列顛島|大不列顛島]]的不少国立学校还使用煤酚皂。直到1960年代英国学校禁止[[体罚|体罚]]前，说脏话的学生会被要求用{{tsl|en|washing out mouth with soap|用肥皂洗嘴巴|用药皂洗嘴巴}}。<ref>{{cite web |title=CARBOLIC SOAP |url=http://balsallheathhistory.co.uk/system/resources/W1siZiIsIjIwMTMvMDgvMDgvMTFfNDNfNTJfMTk2X0NhcmJvbGljX1NvYXAucGRmIl1d/Carbolic%20Soap.pdf |website=Diamond Balsall Health |accessdate=2018-09-02 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170223064928/http://balsallheathhistory.co.uk/system/resources/W1siZiIsIjIwMTMvMDgvMDgvMTFfNDNfNTJfMTk2X0NhcmJvbGljX1NvYXAucGRmIl1d/Carbolic%20Soap.pdf |archivedate=2017-02-23}}</ref>

煤酚皂仍然广泛用于加勒比地区。在牙买加和巴拿马的大部分药店和超市里都能找到煤酚皂。<ref>{{cite web |last1=Limited |first1=Jamaica Observer |title=Blue Power to expand production of specialty soap line |url=http://www.jamaicaobserver.com/entertainment/Blue-Power-to-expand-production-of-specialty-soap-line_64839 |website=Jamaica Observer |quote=Tanna says his two main products, laundry soap Blue Power and bathing soap Carbolic, have been selling well in the United States, Guyana, Barbados, St Vincent, Suriname, as well as here in Jamaica.}}</ref>“甲酚皂溶液”是[[中国药典|中国药典]]规定的[[通用名药物|通用名药物]]，<ref>{{cite web |title=甲酚皂溶液 (Saponated Cresol Solution) |url=http://www.yaobiaozhun.com/yd2015/view.php?id=2505 |work=中国药典二部（2015）|language=zh |date=2015}}</ref>其对应的固态煤酚“药皂”在[[SARS|SARS]]爆发期间广泛使用。<ref>{{cite news |title=同舟共济　全力以赴――上海支援全国抗击非典纪实 |url=http://www.people.com.cn/GB/shizheng/19/20030520/996188.html |accessdate=2018-09-02 |work=人民网 |date=2003-05-20 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20180902134153/www.people.com.cn/GB/shizheng/19/20030520/996188.html |archivedate=2018-09-02}}</ref>

'''文化干擾'''，又稱'''文化反堵'''，是一種[[反消費主義|反消費主義]]的[[社會運動|社會運動]]<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=w0TSfW4MU4QC&pg=PA274 ''Images of the street: planning, identity, and control in public space''] By Nicholas R. Fyfe, p. 274</ref><ref>Gavin Grindon [https://books.google.com/books?id=U4fpAAAAMAAJ ''Aesthetics and Radical Politics'']</ref><ref name="Binay">"Investigating the Anti-consumerism Movement in North America: The Case of Adbusters';" Binay, Ayse; (2005); dissertation, University of Texas</ref>，目的在於反抗[[主流媒體|主流媒體]]及其塑造的文化<ref>[https://books.google.com/books?id=Y4auF2ysN0wC&pg=PA5&lpg=PA5 p. 5 ''Culture Jamming: Ideological Struggle and the Possibilities for Social Change'' ]; 2008; Nomai, Afsheen Joseph</ref><ref name="Cortese2008">{{cite book | author=Anthony Joseph Paul Cortese| title=Provocateur: Images of Women and Minorities in Advertising|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=K6pO6kUa9w0C&pg=PA22| accessdate=3 December 2012|year=2008| publisher=Rowman & Littlefield| isbn=978-0-7425-5539-6| page=22}}</ref>，並暴露商業文化可能存在的問題、寵重新塑造文化定義。常用手段為[[異軌|異軌]]<ref name="Boden">Boden, Sharon and Williams, Simon J. (2002) "Consumption and Emotion: The Romantic Ethic Revisited", Sociology 36(3):493–512</ref>。

“Culture jamming”一詞最早由美國“[[Negativland|Negativland]]”樂隊的唐·喬伊斯（Don Joyce）在1984年提出<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.negativland.com/news/?p=696 |title=Don Joyce (2/9/44 – 7/22/15) |publisher=Negativland}}</ref><ref name="Lloyd03">Lloyd, Jan (2003) [http://www.hums.canterbury.ac.nz/cult/research/lloyd.htm ''Culture Jamming: Semiotic Banditry in the Streets''], in ''Cultural Studies Department'': University of Canterbury,
Christchurc</ref><ref name="Dery90Nyt"/><ref name="Dery2010Revisited">Dery, Mark (2010) New Introduction and revisited edition of [http://markdery.com/?page_id=154 ''Culture Jamming: Hacking, Slashing, and Sniping in the Empire of the Signs]'', October 8, 2010</ref>，詞源是“[[無線電幹擾|radio jamming]]”（公共頻道被個人入侵）<ref name="Dery90Nyt">[[Mark_Dery|Dery, Mark]] (1990)[https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9C0CE7DF123EF930A15751C1A966958260&pagewanted=all ''The Merry Pranksters And the Art of the Hoax''], NYtimes article, December 23, 1990.</ref>。但實際上在1950年代這個詞就已經出現了<ref name="Carducci">[[Vince_Carducci|Carducci, Vince]] (2006) "Culture Jamming: A Sociological Perspective", Journal of Consumer Culture 6(1): 116–138</ref>。

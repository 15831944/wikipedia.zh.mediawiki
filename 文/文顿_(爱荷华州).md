'''文顿'''（Vinton）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[本顿县_(爱阿华州)|本顿县]]的城市和县城，[[2010年美国人口普查|2010年美国人口普查]]中的人口为5257人，比2000年人口普查的5102人多。<ref name="USCB">{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |title=Population & Housing Occupancy Status 2010 |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] American FactFinder |accessdate=2011-06-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721034521/http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |archivedate=2011-07-21 }}</ref><ref name="IADC">{{cite web|url=http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |title=Data from the 2010 Census |publisher=State Data Center of Iowa |accessdate=2011-06-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718201045/http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |archivedate=2011-07-18 }}</ref> 它也是[[锡达拉皮兹|锡达拉皮兹]]大都会统计区的一部分。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx|accessdate=2011-06-07|title=Find a County|publisher=National Association of Counties}}</ref> 

==历史==
温顿成立于1849年<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=aYQUAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA327#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=The History of Benton County, Iowa, Containing a History of the County, Its Cities, Towns, &c | publisher=Western Historical | year=1878 | page=327}}</ref>，以国务委员彭宁·文顿命名。<ref>{{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=q_lKAQAAIAAJ&pg=PA11#v=onepage&q&f=false | title=History of the Origin of the Place Names in Nine Northwestern States | year=1908 | pages=11}}</ref>第一条铁路线在1869年延伸到了温顿，同年建市。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.bentoncountyiowa.com/towns/vinton.html | title=Vinton | publisher=County of Benton, IA | accessdate=7 August 2014 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080421091415/http://www.bentoncountyiowa.com/towns/vinton.html | archivedate=2008年4月21日 }}</ref>

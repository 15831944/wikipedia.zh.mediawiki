'''文件币'''（{{lang-en|'''Filecoin'''}}）是一个[[开放源代码|开源]]、面向大众开放的[[密碼貨幣|加密货币]]和数字支付系统。文件币系统旨在打造一个基于[[区块链|区块链]]系统的数据存储和检索方法。<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=http://observer.com/2017/07/dropbox-ipo-filecoin-ico/|title=Showdown in the Cloud: Dropbox IPO, Meet the Filecoin ICO|date=2017-07-11|work=Observer|language=en-US|access-date=2017-08-11}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2017/08/10/filecoins-ico-opens-today-for-accredited-investors-after-raising-52m-from-advisers/|title=Filecoin’s ICO opens today for accredited investors after raising $52M from advisers {{!}} TechCrunch|accessdate=2017-08-11|last=Tepper|first=Fitz}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/latest-hot-digital-coin-offering-187-millionin-one-hourfor-filecoin-1502481514|title=Latest Hot Digital Coin Offering: $187 Million in One Hour for Filecoin|last=Vigna|first=Paul|date=2017-08-11|work=Wall Street Journal|accessdate=2017-08-11|language=en-US|issn=0099-9660}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://arstechnica.com/information-technology/2017/08/investors-poured-millions-into-a-storage-network-that-doesnt-exist/|title=Investors poured millions into a storage network that doesn’t exist yet|work=Ars Technica|accessdate=2017-08-11|language=en-us}}</ref>它是[[星际文件系统|协议实验室]]以[[星际文件系统|星际文件系统]]为基础而创造的。

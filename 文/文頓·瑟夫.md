'''文顿·格雷·瑟夫'''（{{lang-en|'''Vinton Gray Cerf'''}}，{{bd|1943年|6月23日|||catIdx=Cerf, Vint}}），[[美國|美國]][[網際網路|網際網路]]先驅，被公認為「網際網路之父」之一<ref>(see [http://www.gcn.com/print/25_2/38005-1.html?topic=interview Interview with Vinton Cerf] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070609092123/http://www.gcn.com/print/25_2/38005-1.html?topic=interview |date=2007-06-09 }}, from a January 2006 article in ''Government Computer News''), Cerf is willing to call himself one of the internet fathers, citing Bob Kahn and Leonard Kleinrock in particular as being others with whom he should share that title.</ref>，與[[TCP/IP|TCP/IP]]共同發明者[[羅伯特·卡恩|羅伯特·卡恩]]共享了這一頭銜<ref>{{cite web |url=http://awards.acm.org/homepage.cfm?srt=all&awd=140 |title=ACM Turing Award, list of recipients |publisher=Awards.acm.org |accessdate=2011-12-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091212132624/http://awards.acm.org/homepage.cfm?awd=140&srt=all |archivedate=2009年12月12日 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ieee.org/about/awards/medals/bell.html#sect3 |title=IEEE Alexander Graham Bell Medal |publisher=Ieee.org |date=2009-07-07 |accessdate=2011-12-02}}</ref>。他的貢獻一再得到承認和讚揚，榮譽學位和獎項包括國家技術勳章<ref name="vita"/>、[[圖靈獎|圖靈獎]]<ref name="turing">[https://www.nytimes.com/2005/02/16/technology/16internet.html?oref=login Cerf wins Turing Award] February 16, 2005</ref>、[[總統自由勳章|總統自由勳章]]<ref name="whitehouse">[http://georgewbush-whitehouse.archives.gov/news/releases/2005/11/20051109-10.html 2005 Presidential Medal of Freedom recipients] from the White House website</ref>、[[馬可尼獎|馬可尼獎]]和[[美國國家工程院|美國國家工程院]]成員。

《'''宇宙有道理'''》（{{lang-en|''How the Universe Works''}}），是[[美國|美國]][[探索頻道|探索頻道]]從2010年開始製播的[[天文|天文]][[科普|科普]]節目<ref>{{cite web|url=http://dsc.discovery.com/videos/how-the-universe-works|title=How the Universe Works Videos|publisher=[[Discovery_Channel|Discovery Channel]]|accessdate=2010-09-03|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100822051239/http://dsc.discovery.com/videos/how-the-universe-works/|archivedate=2010-08-22}}</ref>。自第2季起改於[[科學頻道|科學頻道]]播放<ref>{{cite web |url=http://science.discovery.com/tv/how-the-universe-works/episodes.html |title=How the Universe Works Season 2 |publisher= [[科学频道|Science]] |accessdate=2012-07-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/showatch/how-the-universe-works/listings|accessdate= 2014-07-10|title=How the Universe Works listings on TheFutonCritic}}</ref>，迄今已播出6季，每季8至10集不等。

== 概要 ==
該節目探究宇宙如何從無到有，由當初針孔般小點演變成現今所見的浩瀚宇宙。並納入最新天文發現或相關物理進展進行探討，佐以科學家及3DCG模擬動畫解析。為[[科学频道|科学频道]]太空節目的「[[蓝筹股|-{zh-cn:蓝筹股;zh-tw:績優股;}-]]」（blue-chip）系列<ref>{{cite press release |url=https://corporate.discovery.com/discovery-newsroom/science-channel-unveils-its-2016-17-upfront-slate-led-by-search-for-the-next-mythbusters-and-the-return-of-punkin-chunkin/ |script-title=Science Channel Unveils Its 2016-17 Upfront Slate Led By 'Search for the Next MythBusters' and the Return of 'Punkin Chunkin' |work=[[探索傳播|Discovery Communications]] |publisher=|via= |author= |language= |date= 2016-03-31 |accessdate=|archiveurl= |archivedate=}}</ref>。

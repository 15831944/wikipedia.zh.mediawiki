'''硅铝层'''是[[地球化学|地球化学]]中所指[[地球|地球]]的[[大陆地壳|大陆地壳]]岩石圈，得名于其主要构成化学元素[[硅|硅]]与[[铝|铝]]。<ref>Continental crust has been defined as ''That type of the Earth’s crust which underlies the continents and the continental shelves: it is equivalent to the sial''. {{Cite book|editor1=Neuendorf, Klaus K. E. |editor2=Mehl, James P. |editor3=Jackson, Julia A. |year=2005 |title=Glossary of Geology |edition=5th |location=Alexandria, Virginia |publisher=American Geological Institute |page=139|isbn=978-3-540-27951-8}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book|author=Smith, Frederick Gordon |year=1963 |title=Physical Geochemistry |edition= |location=Reading, Massachusetts |publisher=[[Addison-Wesley|Addison-Wesley]] |page=379|oclc=253612701 }}</ref>

也称硅铝层岩石为[[长英质|长英质]]，因为其包含很高比例的硅铝[[长石|长石]]矿物。<ref name="Ritter">{{Cite book|author= Ritter, Michael E. |year=2006|chapter=Chapter EM:  Earth Materials and Structure: The Earth's Interior: The Crust |title=The Physical Environment: An Introduction to Physical Geography |url=http://www.uwsp.edu/geo/faculty/ritter/geog101/textbook/earth_materials_structure/earth_interior.html |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071111064056/http://www.uwsp.edu/geo/faculty/ritter/geog101/textbook/earth_materials_structure/earth_interior.html|archivedate=11 November 2007 |deadurl=yes}}</ref>

硅铝层最初由[[爱德华·修斯|爱德华·修斯]]在19世纪提出。已经被岩相、重力测量、地震证据所证实。<ref>{{Cite book|author=Kuenen, Philip Henry |year=1950 |title=Marine Geology |publisher=Wiley |location=New York |page=[https://books.google.com/books?id=gqA_rpZG9X4C&pg=PA117 117] |oclc=489742 }}</ref>

==性质==
硅铝层的密度低(2700–2800 kg/m<sup>3</sup><ref>{{Cite book|editor=Fairbridge, Rhodes W. |year=1967|title=The Encyclopedia of Atmospheric Sciences and Astrogeology|location=New York |publisher=Reinhold Publishing |page=323|oclc=430153 }}</ref>)。与硅镁层分界面并不对应于[[康拉德不连续面|康拉德不连续面]]。<ref>{{Cite journal|author=Monastersky, Richard |year=1989 |title=Inner Space |journal=Science News |volume=136 |issue=17|pages=266–268, page 266 |jstor=3973827 |doi=10.2307/3973827}}</ref> 而是相当随意地设定为平均密度2800 kg/m<sup>3</sup>的地方。<ref name="Ritter" />

根据[[地殼均衡|地殼均衡]]理论<ref name="Bridges">{{Cite book|author=Bridges, Edwin Michael |year=1990 |title=World Geomorphology | publisher=[[Cambridge_University_Press|Cambridge University Press]] |location=Cambridge, England |page=[https://books.google.com/books?id=-ZCyyxIZPaIC&pg=PA13 13] |isbn=978-0-521-38343-1 }}</ref>  山脉既向下也向上扩展其岩石圈<ref name="Bridges" />，因此大陆地壳的硅铝层厚度从5 km到70 km。<ref>{{Cite book|author=Lliboutry, Luis |year=2000 |title=Quantitative Geophysics and Geology |publisher=Springer-Praxis |location=London |page=[https://books.google.com/books?id=C7rny3qA6RMC&pg=PA152 152] |isbn=978-1-85233-115-3}}</ref>

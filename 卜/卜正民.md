'''卜正民'''（{{lang-en|Timothy James Brook}}，{{bd|1951年|1月6日|||catIdx=B卜}}）出生于加拿大安大略省[[多伦多市|多伦多市]]，毕业于[[多伦多大学|多伦多大学]]、[[哈佛大学|哈佛大学]]，是一位加拿大的[[汉学家|汉学家]]。<ref name="BrookBiography">{{cite web| last = Staff| first = | authorlink =| title = Biography - Brook, Timothy (James) (1951-): An article from: Contemporary Authors| publisher = [[Gale_(publisher)|Thomson Gale]]| date = 16 December 2007| url = http://www.amazon.com/Biography-Brook-Timothy-Contemporary-Authors/dp/B0007SGKWC| accessdate = 2010-01-26}}</ref><ref name="CanadianWhosWho2003">{{cite book| last = Lumley| first = Elizabeth| authorlink = | coauthors = | title = Canadian Who's Who 2003, Volume 38| publisher = [[University_of_Toronto_Press|University of Toronto Press]]| year = May 2003| location = Toronto, Canada | page = 172| url = http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=R2m2UmK2mS4C&pg=PA172&lpg=PA172&dq=%22Geographical+Sources+of+Ming-Qing+History%22+brook&source=bl&ots=4Wvd1fMft0&sig=clov4zGcG6gyD3fUR5M_oqm0DNo&hl=en&ei=uCdgS4OpNoyi0gT08ODiDA&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=6&ved=0CBMQ6AEwBTge#v=onepage&q=%22Geographical%20Sources%20of%20Ming-Qing%20History%22%20brook&f=false| isbn = 0-8020-8867-8| accessdate = 2010-01-27}} 28th edition (May 2003).</ref><ref name="WashingtonPostDirda">{{cite web| last = Dirda| first = Michael| title = Painting the World: How a hunger for tea and tobacco created global trade.| work = [[Washington_Post|Washington Post]]| date = 27 January 2008| url = http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2008/01/24/AR2008012402715.html| accessdate = 2010-01-22}}</ref><ref name="ObserverConrad">{{cite web| authorlink = Peter Conrad (academic)| last = Conrad| first = Peter| title = A time when every picture told a story| work = [[The_Observer|The Observer]]| date = 29 June 2008| url = http://www.guardian.co.uk/culture/2008/jun/29/review.features1| accessdate = 2010-01-22}}</ref>

== 生平 ==
1951年，卜正民出生在加拿大安大略省[[多伦多市|多伦多市]]並成長於該地。<ref name="BrookBiography"/><ref name="CanadianWhosWho2003"/><ref name="CBCNews"/>

卜正民在[[多伦多大学|多伦多大学]]取得[[英语文学|英语文学]]学士学位，1977年又在[[哈佛大学|哈佛大学]]取得东亚历史学硕士学位，1984年在哈佛大学取得东亚历史学和东亚语言博士学位。<ref name="SJCProfile"/>

1984年开始，卜正民在[[阿尔伯塔大学|阿尔伯塔大学]]擔任研究员；1986年，卜正民担任多伦多大学教授助理；1997年，卜正民担任[[斯坦福大学|斯坦福大学]]教授；1999年以後，他一直担任多伦多大学教授。<ref name="SJCProfile"/>

2007年，卜正民担任[[不列颠哥伦比亚大学|不列颠哥伦比亚大学]][[圣约翰学院|圣约翰学院]]教授；他是不列颠哥伦比亚大学亚洲研究所的[[西藏|西藏]]问题专家。<ref name="OxfordCentreLaunched">{{cite web| last = Staff| first = | authorlink = | title = New Oxford China Centre launched| publisher = [[University_of_Oxford|University of Oxford]]| date = 14 May 2008| url = http://www.ox.ac.uk/media/news_stories/2008/080514.html| accessdate = 2010-01-28| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20110609200401/http://www.ox.ac.uk/media/news_stories/2008/080514.html| archivedate = 2011年6月9日}}</ref>

卜正民的学术研究領域主要是中国历史明史、中国封建社会法律、[[日本帝国|日本帝国]]侵华战争史、1937年到1945年日本帝国亚洲侵略史。<ref name="WashingtonPostDirda"/><ref name="SJCProfile">{{cite web| last = Staff| first = | title = Timothy James Brook (profile)| publisher = St. John's College, University of British Columbia| date = October 2004| url = http://www.iar.ubc.ca/introduction/brook.html| accessdate = 2010-01-22| deadurl = yes| archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20100204214812/http://www.iar.ubc.ca/introduction/brook.html| archivedate = 2010-02-04}}</ref><ref name="IndependentBarratt">{{cite web| last = Barrett| first = TH| title = Vermeer's Hat, by Timothy Brook: The fine art of global trade| work = [[The_Independent|The Independent]]| date = 1 August 2008 | url = http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/books/reviews/vermeers-hat-by-timothy-brook-882263.html | accessdate = 2010-01-22}}</ref><ref name="HarvardUniversityPress">{{cite web| authorlink = | last = Staff| first = | title = Death by a Thousand Cuts| publisher = [[Harvard_University_Press|Harvard University Press]]| year = 2008| url = http://www.hup.harvard.edu/catalog/BRODEA.html| accessdate = 2010-01-23}}</ref>

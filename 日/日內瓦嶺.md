'''日內瓦嶺'''（{{lang-en|Geneva Spur}}，{{lang-fr|Eperon des Genevois}}<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=84ttGahHDEgC&pg=PA132&lpg=PA132|author=Baron John Hunt Hunt & John Hunt|title=The Ascent of Everest|year=1993|page=132|isbn=9780898863611}}</ref>），也稱做'''馬鞍山脊'''（{{lang-en|Saddle Rib}}）<ref name="DyhrenfurthChapterTwo">{{cite book|author1=[[Günter_Dyhrenfurth|G. O. Dyhrenfurth]]|title=To the Third Pole|publisher=Werner Laurie|edition=1955|url=}} "Chapter Two [...] Saddle Rib"</ref>，是[[珠穆朗瑪峰|珠穆朗瑪峰]]的一個地理景觀，位於珠穆朗瑪峰和[[洛子峰|洛子峰]]峰頂附近，為一個巨大的岩石支柱<ref name=grylls/><ref name=george>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=fAFF7DBhJOsC&pg=PA155|first=George|last=Plimpton|authorlink=George Plimpton|title=As Told at the Explorers Club: More Than Fifty Gripping Tales of Adventure|year=2005|pages=155–156|isbn=9781592286584}}</ref>。日內瓦嶺高於第三營與黃色地帶，低於第四營與{{le|南坳|South Col}}<ref name=grylls>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=wKTJkDj8vrYC&pg=PA226|first=Bear|last=Grylls|authorlink=Bear Grylls|title=The Kid Who Climbed Everest|year=2004|page=226|isbn=9781592284931}}</ref>。此支柱為一處{{le|山咀|Spur (topography)}}<ref>https://www.pbs.org/wgbh/nova/everest/climb/waytosummitsou.html</ref>，位於南坳附近。與該景觀相連的是一處[[鞍部|鞍部]]<ref>http://www.popularhistoria.se/artiklar/erovringen-av-mount-everest/</ref>，位於珠穆朗瑪峰和洛子峰之間。

日內瓦嶺海拔約在25,000至26,000英里之間<ref name=george/>。

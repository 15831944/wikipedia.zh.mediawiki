《'''日出'''》（{{lang-en|'''Sunrise: A Song of Two Humans'''}}）是由[[德国|德国]]导演[[F·W·穆瑙|F·W·穆瑙]]执导的一部[[美國|美國]][[默片|默片]]，於1927年上映。這部電影是首部在劇中插入[[音樂|音樂]]的電影，並且贏得了[[第1屆奧斯卡金像獎|第1屆奧斯卡金像獎]]的藝術作品獎<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/1st-winners.html |title=1st Academy Awards Winners - Oscar Legacy |date= |accessdate=2013-03-23 |archiveurl=https://archive.today/http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/legacy/ceremony/1st-winners.html |archivedate=2017-07-04 |deadurl=bot: unknown }}，</ref>。電影被收入[[國家電影登記部|國家電影登記部]]<ref>{{cite book |last= Eagen|first= Daniel|title= America's Film Legacy: The Authoritative Guide to the Landmark Movies in the National Film Registry|origmonth= |url= |format= |accessdate= |edition= |series= |date= |year= 2010|month= |publisher= Continuum Pub Group|location= New York|language= |isbn= 9780826429773|pages= 131-133}}</ref>，並且被選入[[AFI百年百大電影|AFI百年百大電影]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://connect.afi.com/site/DocServer/100Movies.pdf?docID=301 |title=100 Years...100 Movies |accessdate=2009-09-18 |work=American Film Institute }}</ref>。

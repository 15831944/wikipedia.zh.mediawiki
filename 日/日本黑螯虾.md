'''日本黑螯蝦'''（學名：'''''Cambaroides japonicus'''''；日語：ニホンザリガニ）是[[擬螯蝦屬|擬螯蝦屬]]下的一種螯蝦，原產於日本北部<ref>{{cite journal |title=Behavior of juveniles of the Japanese endemic species ''Cambaroides japonicus'' (Decapoda: Astacidea: Cambaridae), with observations on the position of the spermatophore attachment on adult females |author=Tadashi Kawai |author2=Gerhard Scholtz |last-author-amp=yes |journal=[[Journal_of_Crustacean_Biology|Journal of Crustacean Biology]] |volume=22 |issue=3 |year=2002 |pages=532–537 |jstor=1549735 |doi= 10.1651/0278-0372(2002)022[0532:BOJOTJ]2.0.CO;2}}</ref>。它是一種小型螯蝦，體長一般在50–60mm，甲殼呈灰色。由於受到來自美國的入侵物種[[信號小龍蝦|信號小龍蝦]]的威脅，日本[[環境省|環境省]]將其列為易危物種<ref>{{cite journal |journal=[[Journal_of_Crustacean_Biology|Journal of Crustacean Biology]] |volume=24 |issue=3 |pages=496–501 |year=2004 |doi=10.1651/C-2484 |title=Reproduction of the alien crayfish species ''Pacifastacus leniusculus'' in Lake Shikaribetsu, Hokkaido, Japan |author=Kazuyoshi Nakata |author2=Akira Tanaka |author3=Seiji Goshima |last-author-amp=yes}}</ref>。

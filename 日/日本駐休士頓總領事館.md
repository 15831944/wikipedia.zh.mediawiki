'''日本驻休士顿总领事馆'''（{{lang-ja|在ヒューストン日本国総領事館}}）是[[日本政府|日本政府]]在[[美国|美国]][[德克萨斯州|德克萨斯州]][[休士顿|休士顿]]设立的外交代表机构。馆址设在{{link-en|休士顿中心|Houston Center|休士顿中心二号楼}}（2 Houston Center）3000号套房，其领事范围为[[德克萨斯州|德克萨斯州]]、[[俄克拉荷马州|俄克拉荷马州]]。<ref>"[http://www.us.emb-japan.go.jp/jicc/HoustonLink.htm Consulate-General of Japan in Houston] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080629075939/http://www.us.emb-japan.go.jp/jicc/HoustonLink.htm |date=2008-06-29 }}." [[Embassy_of_Japan_in_Washington,_D.C.|Embassy of Japan in Washington, D.C.]] Retrieved on December 24, 2008.</ref>
 
==历史==
领事馆起初位于[[侯斯頓富國銀行廣場|富國銀行廣場]]（以前称为第一州际银行广场）5300号套房，后来遷至富国银行广场2300号套房。<ref>"[http://www.library.ohiou.edu/subjects/eastasia/diplomat.htm East Asia Diplomatic Representatives in the United States] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080516185636/http://www.library.ohiou.edu/subjects/eastasia/diplomat.htm |date=2008-05-16 }}." ''[[Ohio_University|Ohio University]]''. Retrieved on December 24, 2008.</ref><ref>https://web.archive.org/web/20020804090635/http://www.houston.us.emb-japan.go.jp/</ref>

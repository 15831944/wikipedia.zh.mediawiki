'''日本统计学会''' ({{lang-en|Japan Statistical Society}}<ref name="UNEAJ">"[https://www.jstage.jst.go.jp/article/rengo/2/0/2_KJ00001248399/_article Japan Statistical Society (Nihon Tokei Gakkai)]", ''Information Bulletin of the Union of National Economic Associations in Japan'', Vol. 2 (1982) p. 90-91</ref> '''JSS''')是一家日本统计学方向的学会。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.jss.gr.jp/en/society/ |title=About JSS|publisher= The Japan Statistical Society|accessdate=7 November 2017}}</ref>是[[国际统计学会|国际统计学会]]的会员。<ref>"[https://isi-web.org/index.php/about-isi/who-is-isi/members/organizational Organizational Members]", [[国际统计学会|国际统计学会]]</ref>

==历史==
成立于1931年，<ref name="UNEAJ" /><ref name="Springer" />其会员包括研究员，教师和专业各领域的统计学家。<ref name="Springer" />发行期刊包括《日本统计学会期刊》 (英语双年版)，《日本统计学会期刊》 (日语双年版)。<ref name="Springer">"[https://www.springer.com/series/13497 JSS Research Series in Statistics], [[Springer_Science+Business_Media|Springer]]</ref><ref>"[http://www.jss.gr.jp/en/publications/ Publications]", The Japan Statistical Society</ref>

《'''无爱可诉'''》（{{lang-ru|Нелюбовь}}）是一部2017年[[安德烈·萨金塞夫|安德烈·萨金塞夫]]执导的俄罗斯剧情片，入选[[第70届戛纳影展|第70届戛纳影展]]主竞赛片并获得评审团奖。<ref name="Main">{{cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/actualites/articles/the-2017-official-selection |title=The 2017 Official Selection |work=Cannes |date=2017-04-13 |accessdate=2017-04-13 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20170414095343/http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/actualites/articles/the-2017-official-selection |archivedate=2017-04-14 }}</ref><ref name="IW">{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/2017/04/cannes-2017-lineup-list-film-festival-schedule-1201804813/|title=2017 Cannes Film Festival Announces Lineup: Todd Haynes, Sofia Coppola, ‘Twin Peaks’ and More|work=IndieWire|date=2017-04-13|accessdate=2017-04-13}}</ref>[[索尼经典影业|索尼经典影业]]已经取得影片的北美发行权<ref name="SPC"/>，[[北京微影时代|北京微影时代]]获得中国发行权<ref>{{cite news|last1=Ford|first1=Rebecca|title=Cannes: China's Weying Takes Five Competition Films|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/cannes-chinas-weying-takes-five-competition-films-1005950|accessdate=2017-05-25|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|publisher=The Hollywood Reporter|date=2017-05-21}}</ref>。

==剧情==
讲述了一对想要离婚的夫妻，在一次争吵之后，他们的儿子失踪了，于是大家展开了搜寻工作。

==评价==
在戛纳影展上本片得到3.2分，是场刊评分的最高一部，有多位影评人都称赞该片并预测本片会得到[[金棕榈奖|金棕榈奖]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com/entertainment/movies/2017/05/26/and-the-palme-dor-at-cannes-goes-to-who-knows-howell.html |title=And the Palme d’Or at Cannes goes to — who knows?: Howell |last=Howell |first=Peter |work=[[The_Toronto_Star|The Toronto Star]] |date=2017-05-26 |accessdate=2017-05-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rogerebert.com/festivals-and-awards/cannes-2017-palme-dor-predictions |title=CANNES 2017: PALME D'OR PREDICTIONS |last=Kenigsberg |first=Ben |work=Rogerebert.com |date=2017-05-27 |accessdate=2017-05-27}}</ref>。

在[[烂番茄|烂番茄]]上有89%的新鲜度<ref>https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/loveless_2017/</ref>，[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]上获得88分<ref>http://www.metacritic.com/movie/loveless-2017</ref>。

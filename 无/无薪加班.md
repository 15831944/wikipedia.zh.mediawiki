'''无薪加班'''，或'''工资盗窃'''（{{lang-en|Wage theft}}）指的是[[雇主|雇主]]拒绝支付[[雇员|雇员]]正当收入，形式多种多样，其中主要有不支付加班费，违反最低[[工资|工资]]标准，{{le|将雇员归为临时工|Misclassification of employees as independent contractors}}，非法克扣工资，强迫员工下班后[[加班|加班]]，完全不支付工资。

在美国，无薪加班的情况很普遍，[[非法移民|非法移民]]群体和低收入群体更常遭遇无薪加班<ref name=NYTStudyreport>{{cite news|title=Low-Wage Workers Are Often Cheated, Study Says|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2009/09/02/us/02wage.html|accessdate=May 29, 2012|newspaper=The New York Times|date=September 1, 2009|author=Steven Greenhouse}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=More Workers Are Claiming  'Wage Theft' |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2014/09/01/business/more-workers-are-claiming-wage-theft.html|accessdate=August 31, 2014|newspaper=The New York Times|date=August 31, 2014|author=Steven Greenhouse}}</ref>。一项2014年发布的调查报告表明，美国国内的雇主通过让员工无薪加班，每年少发放的工资达数十亿美元<ref>Brady Meixell and Ross Eisenbrey (September 11, 2014). [http://www.epi.org/publication/epidemic-wage-theft-costing-workers-hundreds/ An Epidemic of Wage Theft Is Costing Workers Hundreds of Millions of Dollars a Year]. Economic Policy Institute. Retrieved June 8, 2015.</ref>。

'''无线零配置'''（Wireless Zero Configuration，缩写WZC）也称无线自动配置（Wireless Auto Configuration）或WLAN自动配置（WLAN AutoConfig），是[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]] XP及更高版本中包含的一项以[[Windows服务|服务]]运行的{{le|无线连接管理实用工具|Wireless connection management utility}}。它会基于用户喜好和各种默认设置来动态选择一个[[无线网络|无线网络]]进行连接。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.microsoft.com/technet/community/columns/cableguy/cg1102.mspx|title=Windows XP Wireless Auto Configuration: The Cable Guy, dx|date=November 2002|work=[[Microsoft_TechNet|TechNet]]|publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]]}}
</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.smallbusinesscomputing.com/webmaster/article.php/3577111|title=Windows Wireless Zero Configuration: Five Steps to Sanity|date=January 12, 2006|last=Weiss|first=Aaron}} Article on problems with WZC connecting to unwanted APs, etc., and workarounds
</ref>

== 概述 ==
无线零配置于[[Windows_XP|Windows XP]]首次引入。[[Windows_Vista|Windows Vista]]和[[Windows_7|Windows 7]]中提供等效功能的服务称为“WLAN AutoConfig”，基于Windows Vista中引入的Native Wi-Fi架构。

早期的Windows XP中没有供开发者创建无线客户端程序和管理配置文件与连接的无线LAN API。在Windows Vista发布后，微软发布了KB918997<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://support.microsoft.com/kb/918997/|title=Developers cannot create wireless client programs that manage wireless profiles and connections over the Wireless Zero Configuration service in Microsoft Windows XP Service Pack 2 (SP2)|date=December 5, 2006|work=Support|publisher=[[Microsoft|Microsoft]]|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061213115530/http://support.microsoft.com/kb/918997/|archivedate=December 13, 2006}}</ref>来为Windows XP SP2增添无线LAN API。它后来也被集成在Windows XP Service Pack 3中。

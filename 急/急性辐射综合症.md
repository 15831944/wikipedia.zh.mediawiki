'''急性辐射综合症'''，也被称为'''辐射中毒'''或'''辐射病'''（英文缩写ARS），是一种患者在24小时内暴露于大剂量的[[游离辐射|游离辐射]]下导致的[[症候群|症候群]]，症状可持续多达数个月。<ref name=Review10>{{cite journal |author=Donnelly EH, Nemhauser JB, Smith JM, ''et al.'' |title=Acute radiation syndrome: assessment and management |journal=South. Med. J. |volume=103 |issue=6 |pages=541–6 |pmid=20710137 |doi=10.1097/SMJ.0b013e3181ddd571 |url=|date=June 2010}}</ref><ref name=Review09>{{cite journal |author=Xiao M, Whitnall MH |title=Pharmacological countermeasures for the acute radiation syndrome |journal=Curr Mol Pharmacol |volume=2 |issue=1 |pages=122–33 |pmid=20021452 |doi= 10.2174/1874467210902010122|url=|date=January 2009}}</ref> 本术语意指[[急性|急性]]医疗问题，而不是产生于长期辐射暴露的[[慢性辐射综合症|慢性辐射综合症]]。<ref>{{cite web|title=Acute Radiation Syndrome |publisher=Centers for Disease Control and Prevention |date=2005-05-20 |url=http://www.bt.cdc.gov/radiation/ars.asp |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151204211423/http://www.bt.cdc.gov/radiation/ars.asp |archivedate=2015-12-04 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite document |publisher=National Center for Environmental Health/Radiation Studies Branch |title=Acute Radiation Syndrome |format=PDF |date=2002-04-09 |url=http://www.umt.edu/research/Eh/pdf/AcuteRadiationSyndrome.pdf |accessdate=2009-06-22 |postscript=<!--None-->}}</ref><ref name="arsphysicianfactsheet">{{cite web|title=Acute Radiation Syndrome: A Fact Sheet for Physicians |publisher=Centers for Disease Control and Prevention |date=2005-03-18 |url=http://www.bt.cdc.gov/radiation/arsphysicianfactsheet.asp |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060716035111/http://www.bt.cdc.gov/radiation/arsphysicianfactsheet.asp |archivedate=2006-07-16 }}</ref>

发病和症状类型取决于患者的辐射暴露情况。剂量较小的辐射对[[消化系统|消化系统]]产生影响，比如恶心，呕吐，血指数下降的相关症状如感染和出血。大剂量的照射会导致神经系统损伤症状和快速死亡。治疗急性辐射综合症的一般方法为输血和抗生素。<ref name=Review10/>

引发慢性辐射综合症的低剂量辐射，虽不能导致急性综合症，但经年累月的照射下也可能出现类似的症状。<ref name=Chronic95>{{cite journal |author=Reeves GI, Ainsworth EJ |title=Description of the chronic radiation syndrome in humans irradiated in the former Soviet Union |journal=Radiat. Res. |volume=142 |issue=2 |pages=242–3 |pmid=7724741 |doi= 10.2307/3579035|url=|date=May 1995}}</ref> 辐射暴露同时也会提升某些其他疾病的罹患機率，比如[[癌症|癌症]]。<ref>{{cite journal zh |quotes = |author =伍浩松 |date = |year =2006 |month = |title =任何辐射都可能导致癌症 |journal =国外核新闻 |volume =7 |issue = |pages =22 |publisher = |location = |issn = |pmid = |doi = |bibcode = |oclc = |id = |url = |language = |format = |ccessdate = |laysummary = |laysource = |laydate = |quote = }}</ref> 这些疾病有时也被作为辐射病来提及，但它们从不被包含在急性辐射综合症中。

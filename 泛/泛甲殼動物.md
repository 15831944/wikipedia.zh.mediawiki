'''泛甲殼動物'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|Pancrustacea}}）是[[甲殼類|甲殼類]]及[[六足亞門|六足亞門]]的總稱。<ref>{{cite journal|author=J. Zrzavý & P. Štys|title=The basic body plan of arthropods: insights from evolutionary morphology and developmental biology|journal=Journal of Evolutionary Biology|year=1997|volume=10|pages=353–67|doi=10.1007/s000360050029}}</ref>這個分類與[[缺角類|缺角類]]假就有矛盾，因缺角類只包含[[多足綱|多足綱]]及[[六足亞門|六足亞門]]，並認為甲殼類是較為疏遠的。截至2010年，泛甲殼動物已被廣泛接受。<ref>{{cite journal|author=Omar Rota-Stabelli, Ehsan Kayal, Dianne Gleeson, Jennifer Daub, Jeffrey L. Boore, Maximilian J. Telford, Davide Pisani, Mark Blaxter & Dennis V. Lavrov|year=2010|title=Ecdysozoan mitogenomics: evidence for a common origin of the legged invertebrates, the Panarthropoda|journal=Genome Biology and Evolution|volume=2|pages=425–40|doi=10.1093/gbe/evq030|pmid=20624745|url=http://gbe.oxfordjournals.org/content/2/425|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120710135634/http://gbe.oxfordjournals.org/content/2/425|archivedate=2012-07-10}}</ref>

多項[[分子|分子]]研究都支持泛甲殼動物是[[單系群|單系群]]<ref>{{cite journal|author=Jeffrey W. Shultz & Jerome C. Regier|title=Phylogenetic analysis of arthropods using two nuclear protein-encoding genes supports a crustacean + hexapod clade|journal=Proceedings of the Royal Society B|volume=267|pages=1011–9|url=http://www.life.umd.edu/entm/shultzlab/opiliomd/papers/ProcRSocLondB.pdf|doi=10.1098/rspb.2000.1104|year=2000|pmid=10874751|issue=1447}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=A review of arthropod phylogeny: new data based on ribosomal DNA sequences and direct character optimization|author=Gonzalo Giribet & Carles Ribera|journal=Cladistics|volume=16|pages=204-31|year=2000|doi=10.1111/j.1096-0031.2000.tb00353.x}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Francesco Nardi, Giacomo Spinsanti, Jeffrey L. Boore, Antonio Carapelli, Romano Dallai & Francesco Frati|title=Hexapod origins: monophyletic or paraphyletic?|journal=Science|volume=299|pages=1887-9|year=2003|url=http://heth.bio.ed.ac.uk/teaching/zoo4/arthropods/Nardi_2003_Science_Hexapoda.pdf|doi=10.1126/science.1078607|pmid=12649480|issue=5614}}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author=Jerome C. Regier ''et al.''|year=2010|title=Arthropod relationships revealed by phylogenomic analysis of nuclear protein-coding sequences|journal=Nature|volume=463|pages=1079–83|doi=10.1038/nature08742|pmid=20147900|issue=7284}}</ref>，但大部份甲殼類都是[[昆蟲|昆蟲]]的[[並系群|並系群]]，即昆蟲是[[演化|演化]]自甲殼類祖先。

==历史==
1927年，美国人阿洛伊斯·班傑明·薩里格（Alois Benjamin Saliger）发明了一种叫做「精神電話」 （Psycho-Phone）的睡眠学习法，宣称人的大脑在睡眠时最易接受指令<ref>{{cite news |author= |title=Psycho-Phone |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=DlUQAAAAIAAJ&q=Saliger+psycho-phone&dq=Saliger+psycho-phone&hl=en&ei=iorlTL2SDMmr8Aa5s-i6DQ&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CC4Q6AEwAA |quote=Well, sir, since 1927, Mr. Saliger has sold more than 2500 Psycho-phones ... |work=[[The_New_Yorker|The New Yorker]] |year=1933 |accessdate=2010-11-18 }}</ref>。

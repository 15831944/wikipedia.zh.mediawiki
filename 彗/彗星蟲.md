'''彗星蟲'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Encrinurus'''''}}）是生存於中[[奧陶紀|奧陶紀]]至早[[泥盆紀|泥盆紀]]的一[[屬|屬]][[三葉蟲|三葉蟲]]，生活在淺[[海|海]]中。這類三葉蟲以其獨特的[[草莓|草莓]]狀[[頭|頭]]甲而為人所知，頭甲綴有巨大結節。[[頰|頰]]部棘刺很短。長尾甲在中軸處有更多的體節。<ref>{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=62 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

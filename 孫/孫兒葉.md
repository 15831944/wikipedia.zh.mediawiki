Korea blanks Cuba 6-0 in first baseball game at new dome |url=http://english.yonhapnews.co.kr/culturesports/2015/11/04/91/0702000000AEN20151104010751315F.html |newspaper=[[韓國聯合通訊社|韓國聯合通訊社]] |location=Seoul |date=2015-11-04 |accessdate=2015-11-10 }}</ref>。

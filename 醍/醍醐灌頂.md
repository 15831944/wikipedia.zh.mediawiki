'''醍醐灌頂'''（'''''Enlightened'''''）是美國的一部電視劇，由[[蘿拉·鄧恩|蘿拉·鄧恩]]主演。電視劇于2011年10月10日在HBO首播，共播出10集<ref>{{cite web |url=http://tvcomedies.about.com/b/2010/04/09/hbo-picks-up-enlightened-with-laura-dern.htm |title=HBO Picks Up 'Enlightened' With Laura Dern |publisher=Tvcomedies.about.com |date=2010-04-09 |accessdate=2011-10-25 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111002194154/http://tvcomedies.about.com/b/2010/04/09/hbo-picks-up-enlightened-with-laura-dern.htm |archive-date=2011-10-02 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。HBO已宣佈續訂這部電視劇的第二季，播出8集<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/news/2013/01/04/hbo-cinemax-programming-overview-339510/20130104hbo08/|title=HBO/Cinemax Programming Overview|work=The Futon Critic|date=January 4, 2013|accessdate=January 10, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite news| url=http://artsbeat.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/12/20/hbo-renews-enlightened-but-cancels-three-other-shows/ | work=The New York Times | first=Dave | last=Itzkoff | title=HBO Renews ‘Enlightened' but Cancels Three Other Shows | date=2011-12-20}}</ref>。將在2013年1月播出<ref>{{Cite web |title= HBO Series Schedule – (2012) |url= http://hbowatch.com/hbo-series-premiere-schedule/ |publisher= HBO |accessdate= August 16, 2012 |archiveurl= https://www.webcitation.org/69xFDn1B2?url=http://hbowatch.com/hbo-series-premiere-schedule/ |archivedate= 2012-08-16 |deadurl= no }}</ref>。台灣譯名為《頓悟人生》。

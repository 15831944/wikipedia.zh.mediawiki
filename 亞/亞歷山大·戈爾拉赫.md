'''亞歷山大·戈尔拉赫'''（{{lang-de|'''Alexander Görlach'''}}，{{bd|1976年|12月28日||}}）是[[德國|德國]]作家、學者、記者及企業家。他以創始和出版辯論雜誌{{link-en|The European}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theeuropean.de/alexander-goerlach|title=Alexander  Görlach|date=2016-12-25|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theeuropean-magazine.com/alexander-goerlach--2|title=Alexander Görlach|date=2016-10-06|publisher=}}</ref>而出名，也於2009年到2016年擔任該刊的主編<ref>{{cite web|url=https://factoryberlin.com/magazine/newconomy/|title=NEWCONOMY - Factory Berlin|date=2016-04-28|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://meedia.de/2015/08/21/european-macher-goerlach-die-zukunft-gehoert-der-debatte/|title=European-Macher Görlach: „Die Zukunft gehört der Debatte“ › Meedia|website=meedia.de}}</ref>。亞歷克斯現任[[哈佛大學|哈佛大學]]哈佛學院【衛民主】的附屬教授<ref>{{cite web|url=http://fdrfoundation.org/in-defense-of-democracy-preserving-the-post-enlightenment-narrative-in-the-21st-century/|title=In Defense of Democracy: Preserving the Post Enlightenment Narrative in the 21st Century – FDR Foundation|website=fdrfoundation.org}}</ref>。 他現任英國[[劍橋大學|劍橋大學]]藝術、社會科學與人文科學研究中心的研究員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.crassh.cam.ac.uk/people/profile/alexander-goerlach|title=Alexander Görlach – CRASSH|website=www.crassh.cam.ac.uk}}</ref>，也拿到了語言學博士學位和比較宗教博士學位<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.alexandergoerlach.com/about/|title=Academic|website=Alexander Görlach}}</ref>。亞歷克斯是卡內基國際關係倫理委員會的資深研究員<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.carnegiecouncil.org/people/alexander-grlach|title=Alexander Görlach - Carnegie Council for Ethics in International Affairs|website=www.carnegiecouncil.org}}</ref>，也是伯格魯恩研究院([https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berggruen_Institute Berggruen Institute])的資深顧問<ref>{{cite web|url=http://berggruen.org/members/148|title=Berggruen|website=berggruen.org}}</ref>。他出版網路雜誌[http://www.saveliberaldemocracy.com www.saveliberaldemocracy.com]，還擔任[[紐約時報|紐約時報]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/12/15/opinion/why-islam-gets-second-class-status-in-germany.html|title=Opinion - Why Islam Gets Second-Class Status in Germany|first=Alexander|last=Görlach|date=2016-12-15|publisher=|via=NYTimes.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/09/28/opinion/germanys-retrograde-record-on-gay-rights.html|title=Opinion - Germany’s Retrograde Record on Gay Rights|first=Alexander|last=Görlach|date=2016-09-27|publisher=|via=NYTimes.com}}</ref>和紐埃蘇黎世報<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nzz.ch/meinung/dialog-der-religionen-als-ob-es-gott-nicht-gaebe-ld.1313536|title=Dem Islam hilft am Ende nur der Säkularismus - NZZ|first=Alexander|last=Görlach|date=2017-08-31|publisher=|via=NZZ}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nzz.ch/meinung/dialog-der-religionen-zeitalter-der-identitaet-ld.1298652|title=Zeitalter der Identität - NZZ|first=Alexander|last=Görlach|date=2017-06-01|publisher=|via=NZZ}}</ref>的作家，也為德國商業雜誌Wirtschaftswoche撰寫專欄<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wiwo.de/themen/G%C3%B6rlachs-Gedanken|title=Görlachs-Gedanken » Aktuelle News und Exklusiv-Meldungen|website=www.wiwo.de}}</ref>。

'''亞利桑那梅薩聖殿'''（Mesa Arizona Temple）<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.azcentral.com/community/mesa/articles/2009/09/22/20090922mr-gntemple0927asf.html |title= Mesa temple is a big draw |first= David M. |last= Brown |date= September 27, 2009 |newspaper= [[The_Arizona_Republic|The Arizona Republic]] }}</ref>是[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的第七座聖殿，位於[[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]][[梅薩_(亞利桑那州)|梅薩]]。亞利桑那梅薩聖殿始建於1922年，竣工於1927年。1974年，聖殿一度關閉並進行重建<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.ldschurchtemples.com/mesa |title=Mesa Arizona Temple |work=LDSChurchTemples.com |accessdate=2012-10-09 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121025184805/http://www.ldschurchtemples.com/mesa/ |archivedate=2012-10-25 }}</ref>，在1975年重新開放<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.lds.org/ensign/1975/06/news-of-the-church/president-kimball-rededicates-arizona-temple |contribution= President Kimball Rededicates Arizona Temple |title= News of the Church |journal= [[Ensign_(LDS_magazine)|Ensign]] |date=June 1975 }}</ref><ref>{{Citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/61726/Mesa-Arizona-Temple-We-praise-Thy-Holy-Name-our-Beloved-Father.html |title= We praise Thy Holy Name, our Beloved Father |date= April 19, 1975 |newspaper= [[Church_News|Church News]] }}</ref>。

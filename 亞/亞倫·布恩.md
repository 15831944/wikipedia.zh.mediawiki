'''亞倫·約翰·布恩'''（{{lang-en|Aaron John Boone}}，{{bd|1973年|3月9日}}），為美國職棒大聯盟紐約洋基的教練。祖父雷·布恩、父親鮑伯·布恩及哥哥布雷特·布恩皆為前大聯盟選手。布恩最廣為人知的就是2003年美聯冠軍賽，面對[[提姆·韋克菲爾德|提姆·韋克菲爾德]]第一球就擊出再見全壘打，幫助洋基殺進世界大賽。
==職業生涯==
===辛辛那提紅人===
紅人隊於1994年選秀以第三順位將布恩選進來。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/draft/?year_ID=1994&draft_round=3&draft_type=junreg&query_type=year_round |title=3rd Round of the 1994 MLB June Amateur Draft |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date=1970-01-01 |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref>布恩於1997年6月初登板。<ref>http://m.mlb.com/cutfour/2015/06/20/131833324/aaron-boone-takes-brothers-place-in-mlb-debut</ref>1998年球季末，紅人以兩對兄弟組成了內野陣容，為大聯盟唯一一次創舉：一壘手Stephen Larkin、二壘手Bret Boone、游擊手Barry Larkin以及三壘手布恩。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theplayerstribune.com/barry-larkin-brother-stephen-reds-1998/ |title=Games That Matter | By Barry Larkin |publisher=Theplayerstribune.com |date=2015-10-01 |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.insidesocal.com/tomhoffarth/2010/02/23/aaron-boone-put/ |title=Aaron Boone puts his USC degree, and his heart, to new work for ESPN | Farther Off the Wall |publisher=Insidesocal.com |date=2010-02-23 |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref>

2002年9月22日，布恩在八局上半擊出他在河濱球場的最後一支全壘打。該年他162場全數出賽，擊出生涯新高單季26轟。2003年，他入選明星賽。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cincinnati.com/story/sports/mlb/reds/countdown/2015/05/12/100-reds-all-stars-countdown-aaron-boone/27120867/ |title=Day 63: Aaron Boone, 2003 Reds' All-Star |publisher=Cincinnati.com |date=2015-05-12 |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref>
===紐約洋基===
2003年7月31日，洋基用Brandon Claussen和Charlie Manning向紅人交易布恩。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story?id=1587958|title=Reds' breakup continues: Boone dealt to Yankees|date=2003-07-31|website=ESPN.com}}</ref>他在洋基出賽54場，打擊率2成54，擊出6支全壘打，31分打點。<ref name=release/>

在[[2003年美國聯盟冠軍賽|2003年美國聯盟冠軍賽]]，布恩在11局面對紅襪投手[[提姆·韋克菲爾德|提姆·韋克菲爾德]]擊出再見全壘打，以6比5氣走紅襪，使得貝比魯斯魔咒持續發威。<ref>{{cite news|title=It's the Curse of the Boonebino Shot in 11th Makes History of Sox|date=2003-10-17|first=Anthony|last= McCarron|newspaper=New York Daily News|page=78}}</ref>美國節目Baseball Tonight將這支全壘打評為棒球史上最棒的九支全壘打之一。<ref>{{cite book|last=Vaccaro|first=Mike|authorlink=Mike Vaccaro|year=2006|title=Emperors and Idiots: The Hundred Year Rivalry Between the Yankees and Red Sox, From the Very Beginning to the End of the Curse}}</ref>

2004年1月，布恩左膝前十字韌帶撕裂傷。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/news/story?id=1719255 |title=Hurt playing hoops, Boone might miss season |publisher=Espn.com |date=2004-01-28 |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref><ref>https://www.si.com/vault/2004/04/05/366859/a-boone-to-baseball</ref>洋基在該年2月27日將布恩釋出。<ref name=release>{{cite web|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/news/story?id=1744985 |title=With release, Boone is a free agent |publisher=Espn.com |date=2004-02-26 |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref>
===克里夫蘭印地安人===
2004年6月，布恩向印地安人簽了2年大聯盟合約。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.the-daily-record.com/article/20040627/SPORTS/306279979 |title=Indians, Boone agree on two-year contract - Sports - The Daily Record - Wooster, OH |publisher=The Daily Record |date= |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref>2005年，布恩出賽154場，打擊率2成43，擊出16支全壘打，60分打點。<ref>{{cite web |author=By Kevin Yanik / Special to MLB.com |url=http://cleveland.indians.mlb.com/news/print.jsp?ymd=20051214&content_id=1281814&vkey=news_cle&fext=.jsp&c_id=cle |title=Batting Around with Aaron Boone | Cleveland Indians |publisher=Cleveland.indians.mlb.com |date=2017-01-02 |accessdate=2017-12-02 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171202152933/http://cleveland.indians.mlb.com/news/print.jsp?ymd=20051214&content_id=1281814&vkey=news_cle&fext=.jsp&c_id=cle |archivedate=2017年12月2日 |df= }}</ref>2006年，印地安人再和布恩簽一年約。<ref>{{cite web|author=MLB.com |url=http://cleveland.indians.mlb.com/content/printer_friendly/cle/y2005/m08/d03/c1156034.jsp |title=Indians and Boone re-structure 2006 contract; Option for next year exercised |publisher=Cleveland.indians.mlb.com |date=2004-06-26 |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref>該季他打擊率2成51，敲出7支全壘打。<ref name=marlins>{{cite web|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/news/story?id=2714191 |title=Marlins sign veteran third baseman Aaron Boone |publisher=Espn.com |date=2006-12-29 |accessdate=2017-12-02}}</ref>
===生涯後期===
2006年12月29日，布恩以一年92萬5000美元跟佛羅里達馬林魚簽約。<ref name=marlins/><ref>{{cite press release|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/press_releases/press_release.jsp?ymd=20061229&content_id=1768338&vkey=pr_fla&fext=.jsp&c_id=fla?iframe=true&width=100%&height=100% |title=Marlins sign infielder Aaron Boone |accessdate=2008-09-07 |website=[[MLB.com|MLB.com]] |date=2006-12-29  }}</ref>2007年他出賽69場，打擊率2成86。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sun-sentinel.com/sports/other/sfl-flspmlbnote25nboct25,0,2742568.story |title=Marlins park top priority - South Florida Sun-Sentinel.com |publisher=Web.archive.org |date=2007-10-27 |accessdate=2017-12-02 |deadurl=bot: unknown |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071027104341/http://www.sun-sentinel.com/sports/other/sfl-flspmlbnote25nboct25,0,2742568.story |archivedate=2007-10-27  }}</ref>

2007年12月6日，布恩以一年100萬美元跟華盛頓國民簽約。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/news/story?id=3144218&campaign=rss&source=MLBHeadlines |agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]] |website=[[ESPN.com|ESPN.com]] |title=Nats add Boone, avoid arbitration with Pena, Langerhans |accessdate=2008-09-07 |date=2007-12-06 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121023005606/http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/news/story?id=3144218&campaign=rss&source=MLBHeadlines |archivedate=2012-10-23 |deadurl=no  }}</ref>

2008年12月18日，布恩以一年75萬美元跟休士頓太空人簽約。<ref>{{cite press release |website=MLB.com |date=2008-12-18 |accessdate=2008-12-18 |url=http://houston.astros.mlb.com/news/press_releases/press_release.jsp?ymd=20081218&content_id=3721952&vkey=pr_hou&fext=.jsp&c_id=hou |title=Astros sign Boone to one-year deal |archivedate=2012年2月25日 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120225064425/http://houston.astros.mlb.com/news/press_releases/press_release.jsp?ymd=20081218&content_id=3721952&vkey=pr_hou&fext=.jsp&c_id=hou |df= }}</ref>

Sports]] |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140530211018/http://sports.yahoo.com/mlb/news?slug=txastrosboone |archivedate=2014-05-30 |deadurl=no  }}</ref>9月2日，為布恩該季初登板。他守一壘，3個打數無安打。9月16日，布恩宣布退休。<ref>{{cite news|website=ESPN.com |date=2009-09-02 |url=http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/recap?gameId=290902116 |title=Lee's two-run homer powers Lilly, Cubs past Astros |agency=Associated Press |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121103043136/http://sports.espn.go.com/mlb/recap?gameId=290902116 |deadurl=no |archivedate=2012-11-03  }}</ref>並於10月4日進行生涯最終戰。

===重返洋基===
2017年12月，洋基宣布布恩為洋基隊史第33任總教練。

==個人生活==
布恩的祖先是美國開拓者丹尼爾·布恩，祖父雷·布恩為1948年印地安人世界大賽奪冠班底。父親是7屆金手套獎得主鮑伯·布恩。哥哥布雷特·布恩入選3次明星賽，拿下4次金手套。

布恩的太太Laura Cover為1998年10月的[[玩伴女郎|玩伴女郎]]。他們現居在[[斯科茨代爾_(亞利桑那州)|斯科茨代爾]]。<ref name=pbnov08>{{cite journal|periodical=Playboy|publisher=[[Playboy|Playboy]]|title=Playmate News|volume=55|date=November 2008|pages=143–144}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://sports.espn.go.com/espn/page3/story?page=mcintyre/playmates |title=Players and their favorite Playmates |author=Jason McIntyre |website=ESPN.com |archivedate=2012-11-04 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121104091501/http://sports.espn.go.com/espn/page3/story?page=mcintyre%2Fplaymates |deadurl=no  }}</ref>他們有4個小孩，Brandon, Bella, Jeanel和Sergot。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://heavy.com/sports/2017/12/aaron-boone-wife-laura-cover-family-playboy/ |title=Laura Cover, Aaron Boone’s Wife: 5 Fast Facts You Need to Know |website=[[Heavy.com|Heavy.com]] |first=Chris |last=Bucher |date=2017-12-02 |accessdate=2017-12-05}}</ref>

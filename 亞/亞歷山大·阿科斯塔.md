'''雷內·亞歷山大·「亞歷克斯」·阿科斯塔'''（{{lang-en|Rene Alexander "Alex" Acosta}}；{{bd|1969年|1月16日|}}）<ref>{{cite news |url=http://archive.tcpalm.com/news/us-attorney-alexander-acosta-to-lead-fius-law-school-ep-398936799-347426931.html |title=U.S. Attorney Alexander Acosta to lead FIU's law school |last1=Weaver |first1=Jay |last2=Yanez |first2=Luisa |date=May 28, 2009 |work=[[The_Miami_Herald|The Miami Herald]]}}</ref> ，是[[美国|美国]]的檢察官，現任{{le|佛羅里達國際大學法學院|Florida International University College of Law}}院長。他是一名[[共和黨_(美國)|共和黨]]人，於前總統[[乔治·沃克·布什|乔治·沃克·布什]]時期被任命為{{le|國家勞工關係委員會|National Labor Relations Board}}委員，其後在{{le|美國司法部民權司|United States Department of Justice Civil Rights Division|司法部民權司}}和{{le|佛羅里達州南區聯邦檢察官|United States Attorney for the Southern District of Florida|佛羅里達州南區}}{{le|美國檢察官|United States Attorney|聯邦檢察部門}}任職。2017年2月16日，時任美國總統[[唐納·川普|唐納·川普]]提名阿科斯塔出任[[美国劳工部长|美国劳工部长]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.politico.com/story/2017/02/trump-to-announce-alexander-acosta-as-labor-secretary-nominee-235089|title=Trump to name Alexander Acosta as labor secretary nominee|publisher=|accessdate=February 16, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/02/16/politics/donald-trump-white-house-announcement/index.html|title=Trump to announce Alexander Acosta as labor secretary pick|first=John King, Manu Raju and Dan Merica|last=CNN|publisher=|accessdate=February 16, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/politics/2017/02/16/president-trump-hold-press-conference/97992390/|title=Trump to name Alexander Acosta as new Labor secretary nominee|publisher=|accessdate=February 16, 2017}}</ref>。

'''亞足聯球會賽事排名'''是[[亚洲足球联合会|亚洲足球联合会]]（AFC）2014年成立的排名系統。<ref>{{cite web|title=Al Hilal lead the way as AFC announces Club Ranking system|url=http://www.the-afc.com/afc-champions-league-2015/al-hilal-lead-the-way-as-afc-announces-club-rankingㄋ-system|accessdate=20 April 2015}}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> 排名基於參賽隊伍在最近4年[[亚足联冠军联赛|亚足联冠军联赛]]和[[亞洲足協盃|亞洲足協盃]]的成績，以及成員協會[[國際足總世界排名|國際足總世界排名]]。這個排名用於哪個球隊可以參加亚足联冠军联赛的初賽資格。

AFC也公布了球會排名，目前只有球隊實力訊息參考價值。

== 排名 ==
=== 成員國排名 ===
<!--==== 計算原理 ====
The Club Competitions Ranking (former Member Association Ranking, or MA ranking) is used to rank associations and also used for seeding teams in the competitions. It is also used to allocate the number of entries and entry points to the competitions.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://res.cloudinary.com/deltatreafcprod/image/upload/gvwuaoq7r4jwg7bobcdp.pdf|title=Entry Manual: AFC Club Competitions 2017-2020|last=|first=|date=|website=Asian Football Confederation|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=}}</ref> There are however many shifts each year with associations not meeting off field requirements.

The ranking consists of two parts. First the average points the member association's clubs over the past four year (weighing 90%), and the member association's [[FIFA_World_Rankings|FIFA World Rankings]] (weighing 10%). The leading team in each category is given 90 and 10 points respectively. All other associations are adjusted proportionally to the leading team. So the theoretical maximum of the ranking is 100 points, when the association's team have won the most points in the four year ranking and also are the number one AFC team in the FIFA rankings. Before 2017, the weighting for the FIFA ranking part was 30%.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.the-afc.com/uploads/afc/files/afc_ma_ranking_mechanics.pdf|title=AFC MA Ranking Mechanics|last=|first=|date=|website=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170831114521/http://www.the-afc.com/uploads/afc/files/afc_ma_ranking_mechanics.pdf|archive-date=2017-08-31|dead-url=|access-date=2017-12-08}}</ref>

Associations earn points in the ACL and ACC starting at the group stage by:<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://res.cloudinary.com/deltatreafcprod/image/upload/ot916hkge9ey89ck5v5h.pdf|title=AFC Club Competitions Ranking Mechanics|last=|first=|date=|website=Asian Football Confederation|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=}}</ref>
*3 points for a win, 1 for a draw in the Champions League
*3 points for each club that advances to the knock-out stage
*1 point for a win, 1/3 for a draw in the AFC Cup
*1 point for each club that advances to the knock-out stage
If an association has more than one team (at group stage), the average points per team are used for the Club Competitions ranking.

Starting from 2017 there is a small number of points to be earned in the preliminary rounds.
*ACL preliminary/playoff stage bonus: entry (per team) 0.3, each win (except by penalty kick) 0.3 and each draw 0.15
*ACC preliminary/playoff stage bonus: entry (per team) 0.1, each win (except by penalty kick) 0.1 and each draw 0.05

ACC knockout stage points:
*West/Central/South/East zone: 1.6 points for a win, 1.6/3 for a draw.
*ASEAN zone: 0.89 point for a win, 0.89/3 points for a draw.-->

====計算原理====
The club ranking is calculated as points earned in the past four years plus 20% of the Club Competitions ranking points (proportionally adjusted so that the top nation here gets 20% of 90 = 18 points). Before 2017, the top nation gets 20% of 70 = 14 points.

Clubs earn points in the AFC Champions League and AFC Cup by:<ref name=":0" />
*Qualifying play-off stage :
**1.5 points for participation in the Champions League
**1.5 points for a win, 0.5 for a draw in the Champions League
**0.5 points for participation in the AFC Cup
**0.5 points for a win, 0.5/3 for a draw in the AFC Cup
*Group and knockout stage :
**3 points for participation in group stage and round of 16 of the Champions League
**1 point for participation in quarter-final, semi-final, and final of the Champions League
**3 points for a win, 1 for a draw in the Champions League
**1 point for participation in group stage and round of 16 of the AFC Cup
**1/3 point for participation in quarter-final, semi-final, and final of the AFC Cup
**1 point for a win, 1/3 for a draw in the Champions League
AFC hasn't announced ranking formula for 2017-2020 season of AFC Cup, since the competition format is different.-->

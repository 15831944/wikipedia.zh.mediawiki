'''亞洲游泳錦標賽'''為亞洲主要的[[游泳|游泳]]賽事，每四年舉行一次。賽事由[[亞洲游泳總會|亞洲游泳總會]]所主辦。<ref name=def>''[http://english.peopledaily.com.cn/200601/18/eng20060118_236265.html Singapore to host 7th Asian Swimming Championships in March]''. Published by [[Xinhua|Xinhua]]; posted online by ''[[People's_Daily_Online|People's Daily Online]]'' on 2006-01-18. Retrieved 2012-06-04.</ref> 第十屆賽事將於2016年在[[日本|日本]]舉行。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.asiaswimmingfederation.org/newsview.php?newsid=53|title=10th Asia Swimming Championships|publisher=Asia Swimming Federation|accessdate=2014-05-08|language=en}}</ref>

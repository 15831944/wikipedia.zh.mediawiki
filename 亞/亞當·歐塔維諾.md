'''亞當·羅伯特·歐塔維諾'''（{{lang-en|Adam Robert Ottavino}}, {{bd|1985年|11月22日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟紐約洋基的投手。他先前曾待過紅雀與洛磯兩隊。

==職業生涯==
===聖路易紅雀===
2006年大聯盟選秀，紅雀隊以第一輪30順位選進了歐塔維諾。他從1A起步。<ref>{{cite web|author=Steve Batterson |url=https://qctimes.com/sports/ottavino-hot-bats-send-swing-to-sixth-straight-win/article_c5f788bd-353e-5e5b-9f4d-cd31ca0b0bc8.html |title=Ottavino, hot bats send Swing to sixth straight win | Sports |publisher=qctimes.com |date=August 31, 2006 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>2008年升上2A，<ref name=roster>{{cite web|url=https://www.milb.com/milb/news/8-springfield-cardinals-added-to-40-man-roster/c-7695606 |title=8 Springfield Cardinals added to 40-Man Roster |publisher=MiLB.com |date=November 20, 2009 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>2009年升上3A，上半季成績是慘淡的0勝9敗，不過下半季投出7勝3敗。<ref>{{cite web|author=DERRICK GOOLD • dgoold@post-dispatch.com > 314-340-8285 |url=https://www.stltoday.com/sports/baseball/professional/cardinals-consider-putting-adam-ottavino-in-the-bullpen/article_3e1044ac-fe02-512a-8af0-1a363c0518c6.html |title=Cardinals consider putting Adam Ottavino in the bullpen | St. Louis Cardinals |publisher=stltoday.com |date=March 22, 2010 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>

2009年球季結束後，紅雀將歐塔維諾加入了40人名單。<ref>{{cite web|last=Goold |first=Derrick |url=https://www.stltoday.com/sports/baseball/professional/ottavino-sparkles-at-memphis/article_21210672-6301-11df-9faf-0017a4a78c22.html |title=Ottavino sparkles at Memphis | St. Louis Cardinals |publisher=stltoday.com |date=May 18, 2010 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>2010年5月29日，歐塔維諾以先發投手的身分登板。該年他出賽5場，有3場先發。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/teams/STL/2010.shtml |title=2010 St. Louis Cardinals Statistics |publisher=Baseball-Reference.com |date=January 1, 1970 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>2010年球季結束後，歐塔維諾被移出40人名單。<ref>{{cite web|author=Joe Strauss |url=https://www.stltoday.com/sports/baseball/professional/ottavino-outrighted-as-cards-clean-up--man-roster/article_97724b04-f435-11df-b2ab-0017a4a78c22.amp.html |title=Ottavino outrighted as Cards clean up 40-man roster | St. Louis Cardinals |publisher=stltoday.com |date=November 19, 2010 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>2011年整季都待在3A。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlb.com/cardinals/news/cardinals-add-three-pitchers-to-40-man-roster/c-26002130 |title=Cardinals add three pitchers to 40-man roster | St. Louis Cardinals |publisher=Mlb.com |date=May 24, 2018 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>
===科羅拉多洛磯===
2012年4月3日，科羅拉多洛磯簽下歐塔維諾。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.purplerow.com/2012/4/3/2923454/colorado-rockies-claim-adam-ottavino-st-louis-cardinals-waivers|title=Colorado Rockies claim Adam Ottavino From Cardinals|first=Andrew|last=Martin|work=Purple Row|publisher=SB Nation|date=April 3, 2012|accessdate=January 24, 2019}}</ref>並將他轉型為後援投手。該季他投出5勝1敗，防禦率卻高達4.56。

2013年，他將球衣背號改為0號。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://blogs.denverpost.com/rockies/2013/02/19/adam-ottavino-will-switch-to-no-0-for-the-regular-season/13374/|title=Colorado Rockies' Adam Ottavino to switch to No. 0 for MLB season|work=Denver Post|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref>該季他的防禦率下降到2.64。2014年，歐塔維諾1勝4敗，防禦率3.60。

2015年，球隊原終結者LaTroy Hawkins表現不盡理想，歐塔維諾成為新任終結者。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/12694053/adam-ottavino-replaces-latroy-hawkins-colorado-rockies-closer |title=Adam Ottavino replaces LaTroy Hawkins as Colorado Rockies closer |publisher=Espn.com |date=April 15, 2015 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>然而，2015年5月4日，歐塔維諾因為右手肘受傷，<ref>{{cite web|last1=Todd|first1=Jeff|title=Ottavino had partially torn UCL in right elbow.|url=http://www.mlbtraderumors.com/2015/05/adam-ottavino-has-partially-torn-ucl.html|website=mlbtraderumors.com|publisher=MLB Trade Rumors|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>動了Tommy John手術，球季提前報銷。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Gleeman|first1=Aaron|title=Ottavino needs Tommy John.|url=http://hardballtalk.nbcsports.com/2015/05/04/rockies-closer-adam-ottavino-needs-tommy-john-surgery/|website=hardballtalk.nbcsports.com|publisher=NBC Sports|accessdate=May 4, 2015}}</ref>

2015年球季結束後，洛磯隊和他簽下了3年1040萬美元的合約。<ref>{{cite web|last=Wiley |first=Matt |url=https://gazette.com/sports/ottavino-and-rockies-agree-to-m--year-contract/article_ae54f709-46bd-5649-9eac-29a69fafe05b.html |title=Ottavino and Rockies agree to $10.4M, 3-year contract | Sports Coverage |publisher=gazette.com |date=December 21, 2015 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>2016年，他締造了個人出賽連續37場，連投了31局無失分的隊史紀錄。<ref>{{cite web|last=Harding |first=Thomas |url=https://www.mlb.com/rockies/news/adam-ottavinos-scoreless-games-streak-ends/c-198010366 |title=Adam Ottavino's scoreless games streak ends | Colorado Rockies |publisher=Mlb.com |date=May 24, 2018 |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>2018年，他投出6勝4敗，防禦率2.34，拿下大聯盟最多次的34次中繼成功。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fangraphs.com/leaders.aspx?pos=all&stats=pit&lg=all&qual=10&type=0&season=2018&month=0&season1=2018&ind=0&team=0&rost=0&age=0&filter=&players=0&sort=11,d |title=Major League Leaderboards » 2018 » Pitchers » Standard Statistics | FanGraphs Baseball |publisher=Fangraphs.com |date= |accessdate=January 17, 2019}}</ref>
===紐約洋基===
2019年1月24日，洋基隊以三年2700萬美元的合約簽下歐塔維諾。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/25786311/free-agent-relief-pitcher-adam-ottavino-sign-new-york-yankees |title=Free-agent relief pitcher Adam Ottavino to sign with New York Yankees |publisher=Espn.com |date=January 18, 2019 |accessdate=January 25, 2019}}</ref>加入洋基後，歐塔維諾成為洋基隊史上第一位使用0號做為背號的球員，也是洋基隊唯一一位使用單數背號的球員。<ref>{{cite web|author=NorthJersey.com Digital Desk |url=https://www.northjersey.com/story/sports/mlb/yankees/2019/01/24/adam-ottavino-first-new-york-yankees-player-wear-no-0-number-zero/2671314002/ |title=Adam Ottavino will be first New York Yankees player to wear No. 0 |publisher=Northjersey.com |date= |accessdate=January 25, 2019}}</ref>

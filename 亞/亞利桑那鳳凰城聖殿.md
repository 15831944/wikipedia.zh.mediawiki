'''亞利桑那鳳凰城聖殿'''（Phoenix Arizona Temple）是[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的一座聖殿，位於[[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]][[鳳凰城|鳳凰城]]，是耶穌基督後期聖徒教會的第144座聖殿。鳳凰城聖殿於2008年宣布建設<ref name=LDS_Press_Release>{{citation |contribution= New Temple for Phoenix Arizona Announced |title= News Story |work= Newsroom |publisher= LDS Church |date= May 24, 2008 |url= http://www.mormonnewsroom.org/ldsnewsroom/eng/news-releases-stories/new-temple-for-phoenix-arizona-announced |accessdate= 2012-11-02 }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/52018/President-Monson-announces-new-temple-in-Phoenix-Ariz.html | title= President Monson announces new temple in Phoenix, Ariz. |date= 2008-05-24 |newspaper= [[Church_News|Church News]] |accessdate= 2012-11-02 }}</ref>，在2011年動工<ref>{{citation |first= Jill B. |last= Adair |date= June 5, 2011 |title= Ground broken for Phoenix Arizona Temple |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/61009/Ground-broken-for-Phoenix-Arizona-Temple.html |newspaper= [[Church_News|Church News]] |accessdate= 2012-11-02}}</ref><ref>{{citation |first= Scott |last= Taylor |date= June 5, 2011 |title= Mormon temple in Phoenix now started |url= http://www.deseretnews.com/article/700141689/Mormon-temple-in-Phoenix-now-started.html |newspaper= [[Deseret_News|Deseret News]] |accessdate= 2012-11-02}}</ref>，2014年10月10日至11月1日期間對外開放<ref name=Newsroom2014-08-07>{{citation |url= http://www.mormonnewsroom.org/article/public-invited-to-tour-phoenix-arizona-temple |contribution= Public Invited to Tour the Phoenix Arizona Temple |title= News Release |work= Newsroom [MormonNewsroom.org] |publisher= [[LDS_Church|LDS Church]] |date= 7 August 2014 }}</ref>，並在2014年11月16日奉獻<ref name=Newsroom2014-11-16>{{citation |url= http://www.mormonnewsroom.org/article/phoenix-arizona-temple-dedicated-president-thomas-monson |contribution= Phoenix Arizona Temple Dedicated by President Thomas S. Monson |date= 16 November 2014 |title= News Release |work= Newsroom [MormonNewsroom.org] |publisher= LDS Church }}</ref>。

'''亞當·崔佛·恩格爾'''（{{lang-en|Adam Trevor Engel}}, {{bd|1991年|12月9日|}}），為美國職棒大聯盟芝加哥白襪的外野手。

==職業生涯==
2013年大聯盟選秀，白襪隊於第19輪選進恩格爾。他從高階1A起步。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kyforward.com/adam-engel-agrees-to-terms-with-white-sox-will-open-pro-career-in-pioneer-league/|title=Adam Engel agrees to terms with White Sox, will open pro career in Pioneer League|publisher=|accessdate=January 13, 2016}}</ref>2014年則是待在秋季聯盟，還一度打過澳職。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cincinnati.com/story/sports/high-school/ohio-high-school/2014/12/18/loveland-adam-engel-australia/20617595/|title=Loveland's Engel getting swings down under|date=December 18, 2014|work=Cincinnati.com|accessdate=January 13, 2016}}</ref>

2016年，恩格爾在亞利桑納秋季聯盟的打擊率為4成03，拿下了聯盟MVP。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.mlb.com/news/article/157855490/adam-engel-named-arizona-fall-league-mvp|title=Adam Engel named Arizona Fall League MVP|work=Major League Baseball|accessdate=January 13, 2016}}</ref>球季結束後，白襪隊將恩格爾加入40人名單。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/sports/baseball/whitesox/ct-white-sox-rule-5-draft-20161118-story.html|title=White Sox protect three players from Rule 5 draft|first=Colleen|last=Kane|publisher=|accessdate=November 19, 2016}}</ref>

2017年5月27日，在3A的恩格爾登上大聯盟，在生涯第3個打席擊出大聯盟首安。

2018年，在全大聯盟的中外野手中，恩格爾的守備率是最低的，僅.981。<ref>[https://www.fangraphs.com/leaders.aspx?pos=cf&stats=fld&lg=all&qual=y&type=0&season=2018&month=0&season1=2018&ind=0&team=0&rost=0&age=0&filter=%20&players=0&sort=21,d Major League Leaderboards » 2018 » Center Fielders » Fielding Statistics | FanGraphs Baseball<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>

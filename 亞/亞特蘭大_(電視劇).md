《'''亞特蘭大'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Atlanta'''''}}）是一部於[[FX_(電視頻道)|FX]]播出的[[美國|美國]]電視劇，由[[唐納·葛洛佛|唐納·葛洛佛]]創作和主演。FX於2015年10月訂下該劇首季的10集<ref name=seriesorder/>。FX於2016年9月6日首播後兩週，宣布《亞特蘭大》已續訂了第二季<ref>{{cite web |url=http://deadline.com/2016/07/american-horror-story-season-6-premiere-date-atlanta-better-things-get-fx-debuts-1201782791/ |title='American Horror Story', 'Atlanta' & 'Better Things' Get FX Premiere Dates |publisher=Deadline |first=Erik |last=Pedersen |date=2016-07-05 |accessdate=2016-07-06}}</ref>。第二季《亞特蘭大：趁火打劫》定於2018年3月1日首播<ref>{{cite web |url=http://tvline.com/2018/01/05/the-americans-final-season-6-premiere-date-fx/ |title=FX Sets Premieres for The Americans' Final Season, Atlanta, Getty Saga Trust |publisher=TVLine |first=Kimberly |last=Roots |date=2018-01-05 |accessdate=2018-01-05}}</ref>

該劇贏得了普遍讚譽和許多獎項，其中包括了兩座[[金球獎|金球獎]]和兩座[[艾美獎|艾美獎]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2017/tv/news/donald-glover-emmys-lead-actor-trump-1202557335/|title=Donald Glover Wins Emmy For Lead Actor In A Comedy Series|first=Danielle|last=Turchiano|date=2017-09-17|work=Variety|accessdate=2017-09-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/donald-glover-emmy_us_59bf1fb7e4b0edff971d1b1d|title=Donald Glover Is First Black Director To Win An Emmy In Comedy|first=Todd|last=Van Luling|date=2017-09-17|work=HuffPost|accessdate=2017-09-19}}</ref>。

== 劇情 ==
該劇描述一對表兄弟試圖利用{{le|亞特蘭大嘻哈|Atlanta hip hop}}的方式，以改善他們自己和他們家人的生活。

== 製作 ==
[[FX_(電視頻道)|FX]]於2013年8月開始決定製作《亞特蘭大》<ref>{{cite web |url=http://deadline.com/2013/08/donald-glover-to-create-and-star-in-music-themed-fx-comedy-series-project-557750/ |title=Donald Glover To Create And Star In Music-Themed FX Comedy Series Project |publisher=Deadline |first=Nellie |last=Andreeva |date=2013-08-06 |accessdate=2016-09-07}}</ref>，並於2014年12月由FX Networks公司訂下了試播集<ref>{{cite web|author1=FX press release|title=FX Networks Orders Comedy Series Pilot "Atlanta" from Award-Winning Actor and Writer Donald Glover|url=http://thefutoncritic.com/news/2014/12/12/fx-networks-orders-comedy-series-pilot-atlanta-from-award-winning-actor-and-writer-donald-glover-33512/20141212fx01/|publisher=The Futon Critic|accessdate=2016-01-22|date=2014-12-12}}</ref>。該集由{{le|村井浩|Hiro Murai}}執導，並於[[喬治亞州|喬治亞州]][[亞特蘭大|亞特蘭大]]開拍<ref name=futoncast>{{cite web|author1=FX press release|title=Donald Glover Pilot "Atlanta" Casts Its Leads as Production Gets Underway|url=http://thefutoncritic.com/news/2015/07/02/donald-glover-pilot-atlanta-casts-its-leads-as-production-gets-underway-39510/20150702fx01/|publisher=The Futon Critic|accessdate=2016-01-22|date=2015-07-02}}</ref>。2015年10月，該劇已被訂下10集的製作<ref name=seriesorder>{{cite web|author1=FX press release|title="Atlanta" Ordered to Series on FX - Comedy Created by and Starring Donald Glover Picked Up for 10-Episode First Season|url=http://thefutoncritic.com/news/2015/10/15/atlanta-ordered-to-series-on-fx-comedy-created-by-and-starring-donald-glover-picked-up-for-10-episode-first-season-129012/20151015fx01/|publisher=The Futon Critic|accessdate=2016-01-22|date=2015-10-15}}</ref>。統籌[[唐納·葛洛佛|唐納·葛洛佛]]說，「這座城市影響了表演的基調。」<ref>{{cite web|last1=N'Duka|first1=Amanda|title=Donald Glover On ‘Atlanta’: "I Just Think That It’s The Most American Place" — TCA|url=http://deadline.com/2016/01/fx-atlanta-cast-and-producers-tca-1201684453/|publisher=Deadline|accessdate=2016-01-22|date=2016-01-16}}</ref>

2017年1月，《亞特蘭大》續訂了第二季，但因葛洛佛工作檔期上的繁忙，使FX宣布第二季直到2018年才會回歸<ref name="s2-2018">{{cite web |url=http://tvline.com/2017/01/12/atlanta-season-2-premiere-delayed-2018-fx-han-solo-movie/ |title=Atlanta Season 2 Delayed Until 2018 |publisher=TVLine |first=Andy |last=Swift |date=2017-01-12 |accessdate=2017-01-12}}</ref>。

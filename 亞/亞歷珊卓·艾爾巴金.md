'''亞歷珊卓·艾爾巴金'''（{{lang-ru|Алекса́ндра Аса́новна Элбакя́н}}<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://runet-id.com/103103/|title=Элбакян Александра Асановна / RUNET-ID|website=runet-id.com|access-date=2017-01-07}}</ref>，{{bd|1988年|11月6日}}）是一位[[哈萨克斯坦|哈萨克斯坦]]籍研究生、<ref>{{cite news |last1=Rosenwald|first1=Michael S.|title=This student put 50 million stolen research articles online. And they’re free.|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/local/this-student-put-50-million-stolen-research-articles-online-and-theyre-free/2016/03/30/7714ffb4-eaf7-11e5-b0fd-073d5930a7b7_story.html?tid=sm_fb|work=[[华盛顿邮报|Washington Post]]|date=30 March 2016}}</ref> 計算機程序員以及[[Sci-Hub|Sci-Hub]]的創建者。<ref>{{cite news |url=https://openaccess.unt.edu/symposium/2016/info/transcript-and-translation-sci-hub-presentation |publisher=[[北德克薩斯大學|University of North Texas]] |title=Transcript and translation of Sci-Hub presentation |access-date=January 1, 2017 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal|last=Dylla|first=H. Frederick|date=2016-03-21|title=No need for researchers to break the law to access scientific publications|url=http://scitation.aip.org/content/aip/magazine/physicstoday/news/10.1063/PT.5.2031|journal=Physics Today|doi=10.1063/PT.5.2031|issn=0031-9228}}</ref>[[自然_(期刊)|自然期刊]]将她列为2016年度十大科学人物之一。<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.nature.com/news/nature-s-10-1.21157#/elbakyan|title=Nature’s 10 Ten people who mattered this year.|date=2016-03-12|journal=SpringerNature|doi=10.1038/540507a|access-date=2016-03-28}}</ref>其事迹被用来与[[亚伦·斯沃茨|亚伦·斯沃茨]]相比较。<ref>{{Cite web|first=David |last=Kravets |url=https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2016/04/a-spiritual-successor-to-aaron-swartz-is-angering-publishers-all-over-again/ |title=A spiritual successor to Aaron Swartz is angering publishers all over again |website=Ars Technica |publisher=Condé Nast |date=3 April 2016 |access-date=18 April 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170111180537/http://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2016/04/a-spiritual-successor-to-aaron-swartz-is-angering-publishers-all-over-again/ |archive-date=January 11, 2017 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>

==简历==
艾爾巴金于1988年11月6日生于苏联时代的哈萨克斯坦[[阿拉木圖|阿拉木圖]]，<ref name="VK">{{cite web|url=https://vk.com/alexandra.elbakyan|title= Alexandra Elbakyan|website=Vk.com|language=ru|accessdate=6 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.ladn.eu/reflexion/articles-parus-dans-ladn-revue/alexandra-elbakyan-la-pirate-scientifique/ |title=Alexandra Elbakyan: la pirate scientifique |year=2016 |language=fr |author={{tsl|en|Coralie Trinh Thi||Coralie Trinh Thi}} |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170111182330/http://www.ladn.eu/reflexion/articles-parus-dans-ladn-revue/alexandra-elbakyan-la-pirate-scientifique/ |archive-date=January 11, 2017 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>有亚美尼亚、斯拉夫和亚洲血统。<ref>{{cite web|url= https://twitter.com/ringo_ring/status/700626318124134400|title= Alexandra Elbakyan|website=Twitter.com|date=19 February 2016|accessdate=6 October 2016}}</ref>艾爾巴金在[[阿斯塔納|阿斯塔納]]完成了大学学业，2009年获得{{le|哈萨克国立科技大学|Kazakh National Technical University}}的[[计算机科学|计算机科学]][[理學士|理學士]]学位，专业为[[信息安全|信息安全]]。<ref>{{cite news|page=22 |url=http://doc.utwente.nl/69553/1/bacteriahunt_e09project5.pdf |publisher=[[特文特大学|University of Twente]] |title=Bacteria Hunt:A multimodal, multiparadigm BCI game}}</ref>并在此之间成为一名[[黑客_(计算机安全)|黑客]]。其后她在莫斯科找到了一份[[计算机安全|计算机安全]]方向的工作。之后于2010年到德国[[弗莱堡大学|弗莱堡大学]]参与一项关于[[脑机接口|脑机接口]]的项目，对[[超人类主义|超人类主义]]思想产生兴趣。于当年参加[[佐治亚理工学院|佐治亚理工学院]]的夏季交流项目，她的研究主题为“神经科学与意识”。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://pwp.gatech.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/516/2016/10/PotterGroup.html |title=People |publisher=[[佐治亚理工学院|Georgia Institute of Technology]] |quote=Alexandra Elbakyan [...] Summer 2010 [...] Programming and data analysis |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170111210530/http://pwp.gatech.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/516/2016/10/PotterGroup.html |archive-date=January 11, 2017 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>

她于2011年返回哈萨克斯坦后创建了Sci-Hub，《[[科学_(期刊)|科学]]》称该网站为“可敬的利他主义抑或大型犯罪组织，取决于你是哪方”。<ref name="Bohannon-28-April-2016">{{cite journal|first=John |last=Bohannon|title=The frustrated science student behind Sci-Hub|journal=[[科学_(期刊)|Science]]|doi=10.1126/science.aaf5675|url=http://www.sciencemag.org/news/2016/04/alexandra-elbakyan-founded-sci-hub-thwart-journal-paywalls|date=29 April 2016|volume=352|number=6285}}</ref>Sci-Hub对本应付费的学术论文提供提供大量自动且免费的下载。她被出版商[[愛思唯爾|愛思唯爾]]在美国法院起诉，被判赔偿1500万美元罚金。<ref>{{cite news |first=Stephen |last=Buranyi |url=https://www.theguardian.com/science/2017/jun/27/profitable-business-scientific-publishing-bad-for-science |title=Is the staggeringly profitable business of scientific publishing bad for science? |newspaper=[[衛報|The Guardian]] |date=27 June 2017 }}</ref>艾爾巴金为了逃避引渡的风险，因此四处躲藏。<ref name="JB">{{cite journal ||first=John |last=Bohannon|title=Who's downloading pirated papers? Everyone|journal=[[科学_(期刊)|Science]]|doi=10.1126/science.aaf5664|url=http://www.sciencemag.org/news/2016/04/whos-downloading-pirated-papers-everyone|date=29 April 2016|volume=352|number=6285|pages=508–512}}</ref>

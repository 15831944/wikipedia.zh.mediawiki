'''亞達伯拉象龜屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：'''''Aldabrachelys'''''<ref name=landw57 />）是[[龜鱉目|龜鱉目]][[陸龜科|陸龜科]]的一個[[屬|屬]]，主要生活在[[塞舌耳|塞舌耳群島]]和[[馬達加斯加|馬達加斯加]]附近的[[阿爾達布拉環礁|阿爾達布拉環礁]]和週邊島嶼，故以環礁名命名。此屬物種因其原始標本實際上屬於[[黃腿象龜|黃腿象龜]]而備受爭，<ref name=bour06>Bour, R. 2006. Identity of Testudo gigantea Schweigger, 1812 and rediscovery of the type specimen. Emys, 13(4): 12–23.</ref> 但是仍然通過請願而保存下來。<ref name=fraz>Frazer, J., 2009. ''Testudo gigantea'' Schweigger,  1812 (currently ''Geochelone'' (''Aldabrachelys'') ''gigantea'';  Reptilia, Testudines): proposed conservation of usage of the specific name by  maintenance of a designated neotype, and suppression of ''Testudo  dussumieri'' Gray, 1831 (currently ''Dipsochelys  dussumieri'') BZN 66(1):[http://www.nhm.ac.uk/hosted_sites/iczn/BZNMar2009cases.html#case3463 Case:3463]{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

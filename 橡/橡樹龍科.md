'''橡樹龍科'''（Dryosauridae）是[[鳥臀目|鳥臀目]][[鳥腳下目|鳥腳下目]][[禽龍類|禽龍類]]的一科，生存於[[侏儸紀|侏儸紀]]晚期到[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期，分布於[[北美洲|北美洲]]、[[非洲|非洲]]與[[歐洲|歐洲]]<ref name=NW90>{{cite book |last=Norman |first= David B. |coauthors=Weishampel, David B. |editor=Weishampel, David B.; Dodson, Peter; and Osmólska, Halszka (eds.) |title=The Dinosauria |year=1990 |publisher=University of California Press |location=Berkeley |isbn=0-520-06727-4 |pages=510–533 |chapter=Iguanodontidae and related ornithopods}}</ref><ref name=DBN04>{{cite book |last= Norman|first=David B. |editor= Weishampel, D.B., Dodson, P., and Osmólska, H. (eds.)|title=The Dinosauria |edition=2nd |year= 2004|publisher=University of California Press |location=Berkeley |isbn=0-520-24209-2 |pages=413–437 |chapter=Basal Iguanodontia}}</ref><ref name=JROetal07>{{cite book |last=Ruiz-Omeñaca |first=José Ignacio |coauthors=Pereda Suberbiola, Xabier; and Galton, Peter M. |year=2007 |chapter=''Callovosaurus leedsi'', the earliest dryosaurid dinosaur (Ornithischia: Euornithopoda) from the Middle Jurassic of England |editor=Carpenter, Kenneth (ed.) |title=Horns and Beaks: Ceratopsian and Ornithopod Dinosaurs |publisher=Indiana University Press |location=Bloomington and Indianapolis |pages=3–16 |isbn=0-253-34817-X}}</ref><ref name=PMG09>{{cite journal |last=Galton |first=Peter M. |year=2009 |title=Notes on Neocomian (Late Cretaceous) ornithopod dinosaurs from England – ''Hypsilophodon'', ''Valdosaurus'', "Camptosaurus", "Iguanodon" – and referred specimens from Romania and elsewhere |journal=Revue de Paléobiologie |volume=28 |issue=1 |pages=211–273 |url=http://www.ville-ge.ch/mhng/paleo/paleo-pdf/28-1/pal-28-1-11.pdf |format=pdf }}</ref>。

==敘述==
橡樹龍科是群行動快速的小型[[草食性|草食性]]恐龍，外表介於[[稜齒龍類|稜齒龍類]]與[[禽龍類|禽龍類]]之間，與[[彎龍科|彎龍科]]、[[禽龍科|禽龍科]]的差異在於牙齒與骨盆。牠們的前肢長，頭骨呈三角形，嘴部缺乏牙齒，其頜部結構可增加兩側的咀嚼力道。身體結構很類似稜齒龍類，修長後肢顯示奔跑速度快。

目前已發現成年體與幼年體化石。已在[[美國|美國]]西部、[[非洲|非洲]]、[[歐洲|歐洲]]等地發現化石。

==分類==
橡樹龍科被定義為：包含[[橡樹龍|高橡樹龍]]，但不包含[[副櫛龍|沃克氏副櫛龍]]在內的最大演化支。在過去研究歷史，橡樹龍科的[[卡洛夫龍|卡洛夫龍]]、[[康納龍|康納龍]]常被認為是[[疑名|疑名]]；[[荒漠龍|荒漠龍]]、''Dysalotosaurus'', [[艾爾雷茲龍|艾爾雷茲龍]]（''Elrhazosaurus''）常被認為是[[模式屬|模式屬]][[橡樹龍|橡樹龍]]的種。近年研究發現，這些屬是有效的獨立屬<ref name=JROetal07/><ref name=PMG09/><ref name=iguanacolossus>{{cite journal | last1 = McDonald | first1 = A.T. | last2 = Kirkland | first2 = J.I. | last3 = DeBlieux | first3 = D.D. | last4 = Madsen | first4 = S.K. | last5 = Cavin | first5 = J. | last6 = Milner | first6 = A.R.C. | last7 = Panzarin | first7 = L. | year = 2010 | title = New Basal Iguanodonts from the Cedar Mountain Formation of Utah and the Evolution of Thumb-Spiked Dinosaurs | url = | journal = PLoS ONE | volume =  5| issue = 11| page = e14075 | doi = 10.1371/journal.pone.0014075 | pmid = 21124919 | pmc=2989904}}</ref><ref name="McDonald">{{Cite journal|author=Andrew T.

'''花紋鴨'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Salvadorina waigiuensis''）又名'''薩爾瓦多水鴨'''，是[[新幾內亞|新幾內亞]]的一種[[特有種|特有種]]鳥類，屬於[[單型|單型]]屬'''花紋鴨屬'''（''Salvadorina''），即該屬只有花紋鴨一種物種。花紋鴨偏好棲息於流動快速的河流中<ref name = "Wow"/>，但也可能在靜水中被發現<ref name = "Strange">{{cite book | first = Morten | last = Strange | title = A Photographic Guide to the Birds of Indonesia | year = 2013 | publisher = Tuttle Publishing | location = North Clarendon, VT, USA| isbn = 1462910327 | page = | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=0hnRAgAAQBAJ&pg=PT66}}</ref>，[[IUCN|IUCN]]將其列為[[易危物種|易危物種]]<ref name=iucn/>，其數量可能因棲地破壞、人類與狗的獵捕而下降<ref name="Kear2">{{cite book | title = Man and Wildfowl | first = Janet | last = Kear | year = 2010 | publisher = A&C Black | location = London, UK | isbn = 1408137615 | page = 188 | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=3QfDYeWiUyAC&pg=PA188 | access-date = 2018-08-15 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20170725180228/https://books.google.com/books?id=3QfDYeWiUyAC&pg=PA188 | archive-date = 2017-07-25 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

==外形==
花紋鴨體長38-43公分，是體型較小的鴨<ref name = "Wow">{{cite book | title = Wildfowl of the World | first1 = Malcolm | last1 = Ogilvie | first2 = Steve| last2 = Young| year = 2002 | publisher = New Holland Publishers| location = London, UK | isbn = 1-84330-328-0| page = 84 | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=RT8qww1cyUMC&pg=PA84}}</ref>。其頭部、頸部與身體均為深棕色，其羽毛有白色與深棕色相間，如虎般的條紋，[[喙|喙]]為黃色，腿為橘色。雌雄鴨外形只有眼睛顏色不同，雌鴨眼睛呈棕色，雄鴨的則為紅色，且雄鴨體形較雌鴨稍大<ref name="Kear">{{cite journal | last = Kear | first = J. | journal = Wildfowl | year = 1975 | title = Salvadori's Duck of New Guinea | volume = 26 | pages = 104–111 | url = http://wildfowl.wwt.org.uk/index.php/wildfowl/article/viewFile/500/500 | author =  | access-date = 2018-08-15 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180416215143/https://wildfowl.wwt.org.uk/index.php/wildfowl/article/viewFile/500/500 | archive-date = 2018-04-16 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。花紋鴨的[[領域_(動物)|領域性]]很強，其翅上的突出物可能是與其他種鴨打鬥而演化出的構造<ref name=arkive>{{cite web|url=https://www.arkive.org/salvadoris-teal/salvadorina-waigiuensis|title=Salvadori’s teal (Salvadorina waigiuensis)|publisher=Wildscreen Arkive|access-date=2018-08-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160422074910/http://www.arkive.org/salvadoris-teal/salvadorina-waigiuensis/|archive-date=2016-04-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==分類==
花紋鴨最早於1894年由[[第二代罗斯柴尔德男爵沃尔特·罗斯柴尔德|第二代罗斯柴尔德男爵沃尔特·罗斯柴尔德]]與德國鳥類學家{{link-en|恩斯特·哈特爾特|Ernst Hartert}}描述，當時即被歸為新屬花紋鴨屬<ref name="ITIS">{{cite web | url = https://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt?search_topic=TSN&search_value=553504 | title = ITIS Report: ''Salvadorina'' | publisher = [[Integrated_Taxonomic_Information_System|Integrated Taxonomic Information System]] | accessdate = 2014-10-22 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160315155205/http://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt?search_topic=TSN&search_value=553504 | archive-date = 2016-03-15 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，其屬名為紀念義大利鳥類學家{{link-en|托馬索·薩爾瓦多里|Tommaso Salvadori}}<ref name = "Kear"/>。據說標本是在新幾內亞西北方的[[衛吉島|衛吉島]]採集到，該地也是這種鴨[[種小名|種小名]]的來源<ref>{{cite book | title = The Helm Dictionary of Scientific Names | first = James A. | last = Jobling | year = 2010 | location = London, UK | publisher = Christopher Helm | isbn = 978-1-4081-2501-4|page=406}}</ref>，但該島並非現在花紋鴨的分布範圍，有學者認為原始的標籤不精確，而懷疑真實採集地點應是新幾內亞島上的[[多貝拉伊半島|多貝拉伊半島]]山區<ref name = "Kear"/>。

原本花紋鴨與南美洲的{{link-en|湍鴨|Torrent Duck}}和紐西蘭的{{link-en|山藍鴨|Hymenolaimus malacorhynchos}}一起被歸入湍鴨族（Merganettini），它們被認為與[[秋沙鴨亞科|秋沙鴨]]和{{link-en|潛水鴨|diving duck}}較為接近<ref name = "Kear"/>，1940年代[[恩斯特·瓦尔特·迈尔|恩斯特·瓦尔特·迈尔]]根據解剖特徵將花紋鴨歸入屬於[[鑽水鴨|鑽水鴨]]的[[鴨屬|鴨屬]]中<ref name = "Kear"/><ref name="Delacour">{{ cite journal | journal = The Wilson Bulletin | title = The Family Anatidae | url = https://sora.unm.edu/sites/default/files/journals/wilson/v057n01/p0042-p0055.pdf | first = Jean | last = Delacour | first2 = Ernst | last2 = Mayr | volume = 57 | issue = 1 | pages = 3–55 | author =  | access-date = 2018-08-15 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20141023050331/https://sora.unm.edu/sites/default/files/journals/wilson/v057n01/p0042-p0055.pdf | archive-date = 2014-10-23 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，不過後續又有研究將花紋鴨移回獨立的花紋鴨屬中<ref name="Bruce">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=wRR1CwAAQBAJ&pg=PA56&lpg=PA56&dq=Salvadorina+taxonomy&source=bl&ots=maGlk4QCIz&sig=FOPV_kdstgNtP0JUyb09x3mSu3w&hl=zh-TW&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwi90Mvm2O_cAhWpllQKHZTLBwQ4ChDoATABegQIBxAB#v=onepage&q=Salvadorina%20taxonomy&f=false|title=Birds of New Guinea: Distribution, Taxonomy, and Systematics|author=Bruce M. Beehler, Thane K. Pratt|publisher=Princeton University Press|year=2016|isbn=1400880718}}</ref>。花紋鴨在演化上自成一個孤立的[[單系群|支序]]，尚不知哪些物種與其關係較近<ref name="Bruce"/>。

==生態==
現時花紋鴨只分布在新幾內亞北部的山區，而不在低地出現<ref name="Bruce"/>。這種鴨可以潛水覓食，也可以像[[鑽水鴨|鑽水鴨]]在水面附近尋找食物<ref name="Birdlife">{{cite web | url = http://www.birdlife.org/datazone/speciesfactsheet.php?id=423 | title = Salvadori's Teal ''Salvadorina waigiuensi'' | publisher = [[BirdLife_International|BirdLife International]] | accessdate = 2014-10-22 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20141023043153/http://www.birdlife.org/datazone/speciesfactsheet.php?id=423 | archive-date = 2014-10-23 | dead-url = no }}</ref>，其食物包括植物、昆蟲<ref name = "Chambers">{{cite journal | jstor = 2559420 | journal = Journal of Tropical Ecology | last = Chambers | first = M. R. | title = The Freshwater Lakes of Papua New Guinea: An Inventory and Limnological Review | year = 1987 | volume = 3 | issue = 1 | pages = 1–23  | registration = yes | doi=10.1017/s0266467400001073}}</ref>，可能還有小型的魚類<ref name = "Strange"/>。

花紋鴨的繁殖季從五月開始，持續到十月中旬，其巢築於近水邊被雜草、灌木遮蓋的隱蔽處，由雜草搭成，有當地人曾表示在河邊的蕃薯田中看過花紋鴨的巢<ref name="Kear"/>。一巢可產3-4顆蛋，孵化時間不明（但應超過28天）。曾有人觀察到雌鴨會將小鴨背在背上，這在鴨類中是相當不尋常的行為（其他種鴨中只有[[麝鴨|麝鴨]]常有此行為）<ref name="Kear"/>。

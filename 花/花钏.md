'''花钏'''（{{lang-sa|चित्रांगदा}}，[[IAST|IAST]]：{{IAST|Citrāngadā}}），印度古代史诗《[[摩诃婆罗多|摩诃婆罗多]]》人物。她是[[曼尼普尔邦|摩尼城]]国王花乘之女，[[般度族|般度族]]第三子[[阿周那|阿周那]]的第三位妻子，与阿周那育有一子{{tsl|en|Babruvahana|褐乘}}<ref>{{cite book|title=The Mahabharata of Krishna-Dwaipayana Vyasa|ISBN=9781465526373|url=https://books.google.com.ph/books?id=ygA2240G74kC}}</ref>。阿周那在十二年林居<ref>{{cite book|last=Ganguli|first=Kisari Mohan|title=The Mahabharata: Book 1: Adi Parva|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160329184536/http://www.sacred-texts.com:80/hin/m01/m01216.htm|publisher={{tsl|en|Internet Sacred Text Archive||Internet Sacred Text Archive}}|chapter=SECTION CCXV|date=1883–1896|accessdate=2018-08-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160329184536/http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m01/m01216.htm|archive-date=2016-03-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>期间与花钏相遇并结为夫妻，但据载在那之前，花钏便已听闻了他的故事，并深深爱上了他<ref>{{cite book|title=Jaya: An Illustrated Retelling of the Mahabharata|ISBN=9780143104254|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=gzN2_fu0mIYC}}</ref>。

阿周那离开后，褐乘和花钏仍留在摩尼城，前者最终成为了国王<ref name=LXXXI>{{cite book|last=Ganguli|first=Kisari Mohan|title=The Mahabharata: Book 14: Anugita Parva|url=http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m14/m14081.htm|publisher={{tsl|en|Internet Sacred Text Archive||Internet Sacred Text Archive}}|chapter=SECTION LXXXI|date=1883–1896|accessdate=2016-04-03|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160420195656/http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m14/m14081.htm|archive-date=2016-04-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

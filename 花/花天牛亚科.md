'''花天牛亚科'''（學名：{{lang|la|Lepturinae}} ）{{r|zgdwz}}是[[天牛科|天牛科]][[昆蟲|昆蟲]]的一個[[亚科|亚科]]，在全世界包括9個族、約150個屬{{r|Bouchard2011}}，當中有在中國分佈的有4族63属173种{{r|zgdwz}}。
這一血統在[[北半球|北半球]]最為多樣化。過往{{tsl|en|Necydalinae|}}亞科的物種被併入本亞科，但近年再被認為是一個單獨的亞科。 2016年，舊屬[[#木花天牛族|木花天牛族]]的[[无瘤花天牛属|无瘤花天牛属]]（''[[Caraphia|Caraphia]]''）獨立出來成為[[无瘤花天牛族|无瘤花天牛族]]（[[Caraphiini|Caraphiini]]），是本亞科的第十個族{{r|Ohbayashi}}。此外，仍有部分屬仍處於地位未定。

{{TransH}}
Usually among the smaller members of their family, these beetles are of a slender shape – particularly the <!-- [[Thorax_(insect_anatomy)|thorax]] -->[[胸節_(昆蟲)|胸節 (昆蟲)]] is markedly less wide than the wings, while the <!-- [[elytra|elytra]] -->[[翅鞘|翅鞘]] tips are often pointed. They differ from most other longhorn beetles in that the <!-- [[antenna_(biology)|antenna]] -->[[觸角|觸角]]e are not directly adjacent to the <!-- [[compound_eye|compound eye]] -->[[複眼|複眼]]s. Hence, the latter are generally oval in outline, rather than having an indentation where the antennae originate, or even being divided by them. In addition, <!-- [[sexual_dichromatism|sexual dichromatism]] -->[[两性异形|两性异形]] is not infrequently seen in lepturines; usually, longhorn beetles are not <!-- 页面不存在 -->[[sexual_dimorphism|dimorphic]] or only have longer antennae in males.

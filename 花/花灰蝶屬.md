Flos Doherty 1889. the Plushblues. Version 28 March 2008 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Flos/112309/2008.03.28 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。幼蟲會與[[臭蟻屬|臭蟻屬]]螞蟻（''Dolichoderus''）[[共生|共生]]。

'''花生過敏'''（Peanut allergy）是對[[花生|花生]]的[[食物過敏|食物過敏]]，和{{le|樹堅果過敏|tree nut allergy}}不同。身體的過敏症狀包括[[癢|癢]]、[[荨麻疹|荨麻疹]]、[[血管性水肿|血管性水肿]]、[[皮膚炎|皮膚炎]]、流鼻涕、[[哮喘|哮喘]]、腹痛、[[低血壓|低血壓]]、腹瀉，也可能會[[心搏停止|心搏停止]]<ref name=Cli2015/>。可能會出現[[過敏性休克|過敏性休克]]的症狀<ref name=Cli2015>{{cite journal |vauthors=Loza C, Brostoff J |title=Peanut allergy |journal=Clin. Exp. Allergy |volume=25 |issue=6 |pages=493–502 |year=1995 |pmid=7648456 |doi= 10.1111/j.1365-2222.1995.tb01086.x|url=}}</ref>。

<!-- 病因 -->
花生過敏是因為[[免疫系统|免疫系统]]產生的[[即發性過敏反應|即發性過敏反應]]所造成<ref name="Canadian Medical Association">{{cite journal |vauthors=Al-Muhsen S, Clarke AE, Kagan RS |title=Peanut allergy: an overview |journal=CMAJ |volume=168 |issue=10 |pages=1279–1285 |year=2003 |pmid=12743075 |pmc=154188 |doi= |url=}}</ref>。花生過敏「因為其發生率、持續性以及過敏反應的潛在嚴重性，屬於嚴重的食物過敏。」<ref name=Cli2015/>。

<!-- 預防及治療 -->
預防可以在孕婦及嬰兒的飲食中，提早加入少量的花生來進行<ref name="Ierod">{{cite journal|last1=Ierodiakonou|first1=D|last2=Garcia-Larsen|first2=V|last3=Logan|first3=A|last4=Groome|first4=A|last5=Cunha|first5=S|last6=Chivinge|first6=J|last7=Robinson|first7=Z|last8=Geoghegan|first8=N|last9=Jarrold|first9=K|last10=Reeves|first10=T|last11=Tagiyeva-Milne|first11=N|last12=Nurmatov|first12=U|last13=Trivella|first13=M|last14=Leonardi-Bee|first14=J|last15=Boyle|first15=RJ|title=Timing of Allergenic Food Introduction to the Infant Diet and Risk of Allergic or Autoimmune Disease: A Systematic Review and Meta-analysis.|journal=JAMA|date=20 September 2016|volume=316|issue=11|pages=1181–1192|pmid=27654604|doi=10.1001/jama.2016.12623}}</ref><ref name=Tog2017>{{cite journal|last1=Togias|first1=A|last2=Cooper|first2=SF|display-authors=etal|title=Addendum guidelines for the prevention of peanut allergy in the United States: Report of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases–sponsored expert panel|journal=Journal of Allergy and Clinical Immunology|date=2017|volume=139|issue=1|pages=29–44 |doi=10.1016/j.jaci.2016.10.010 |pmid=28065278|pmc=5226648}}</ref>，建議花生過敏風險高的嬰兒最早可以在4個月時，在可以接受醫療照顧的地方接觸花生食品<ref name=Gre2019>{{cite journal | last=Greer | first=FR | last2=Sicherer | first2=SH | last3=Burks | first3=AW | title=The Effects of Early Nutritional Interventions on the Development of Atopic Disease in Infants and Children: The Role of Maternal Dietary Restriction, Breastfeeding, Hydrolyzed Formulas, and Timing of Introduction of Allergenic Complementary Foods. | journal=Pediatrics | volume=143 | issue=4 | year=2019 | issn=0031-4005 | pmid=30886111 | doi=10.1542/peds.2019-0281 | page=}}</ref>。針對過敏型反應的典型治療方式是注射[[肾上腺素|肾上腺素]]<ref name="Canadian Medical Association"/>。

<!-- 流行病學 -->
在對花生過敏的人之中，部分人只是对花生有轻度过敏反应，但是，花生也会令一些人出现过敏性休克。美國有千分之六的人對花生过敏<ref name="niaid">{{cite web |id= [http://www.niaid.nih.gov/topics/foodAllergy/understanding/Pages/quickFacts.aspx NIH.gov QuickFacts] |title=Addendum Guidelines for the Prevention of Peanut Allergy in the United States: Summary for Parents and Caregivers |publisher=National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases |date=17 January 2017 |url=https://www.niaid.nih.gov/sites/default/files/peanut-allergy-prevention-guidelines-parent-summary.pdf|accessdate=16 November 2017}}</ref>，這也是常見和食物有關的致命過敏反應及接近致命過敏反應<ref name="aafa">{{Cite web |title=Allergy Facts and Figures |publisher=Asthma and Allergy Foundation of America|date=2017|accessdate=16 November 2017|url=http://www.kidswithfoodallergies.org/page/peanut-allergy.aspx}}</ref>。{{Fact|在英国，每200个人当中有大约一人对[[花生|花生]]敏感。在英国，每年大约有10个人因为对花生的过敏反应死亡|time=2019-04-13T02:19:22+00:00}}。除[[巴西|巴西]]外，尚未有國家強制要求食品標示其中含有的微量過敏原<ref name="ANVISA2016">{{cite web|title=Agência Nacional de Vigilância Sanitária Guia sobre Programa de Controle de Alergênicos|url=https://www.saude.rj.gov.br/comum/code/MostrarArquivo.php?C=MzIxOQ%2C%2C|publisher=Agência Nacional de Vigilância Sanitária (ANVISA)|date=2016|accessdate=7 April 2018}}</ref>。

<!-- 定義及症狀 -->
'''膿痂疹'''（Impetigo）是涉及表層[[皮膚|皮膚]]的[[細菌感染|細菌感染]]<ref name=Ib2015/>，常見臉、手臂或是腳上出現淡黃色痂皮<ref name=Ib2015/>，發生在腹股溝和腋窩的大型[[水疱|水疱]]較少見<ref name=Ib2015>{{cite journal |last1=Ibrahim|first1=F|last2=Khan|first2=T|last3=Pujalte|first3=GG|title=Bacterial Skin Infections.|journal=Primary care|date=December 2015|volume=42|issue=4|pages=485–99|pmid=26612370|doi=10.1016/j.pop.2015.08.001}}</ref>。病灶可能有疼痛或是搔癢感<ref name=AFP2014/>，不常引起[[發燒|發燒]]<ref name=AFP2014/>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
膿痂疹通常由[[金黃色葡萄球菌|金黃色葡萄球菌]]或{{le|化膿性鏈球菌|Streptococcus pyogenes}}感染所引起<ref name=AFP2014/>。其風險因子包含參與{{le|日間照護|day care}}、擁擠環境、[[營養不良|營養不良]]、[[糖尿病|糖尿病]]、{{le|接觸式運動|contact sport}}、皮膚破損，如蚊蟲叮咬、[[皮膚炎|皮膚炎]]、[[疥瘡|疥瘡]]和[[皰疹|皰疹]]<ref name=AFP2014/><ref name=Adam2002>{{cite journal |last1=Adams|first1=BB|title=Dermatologic disorders of the athlete.|journal=Sports Medicine|date=2002|volume=32|issue=5|pages=309–21|pmid=11929358|doi=10.2165/00007256-200232050-00003}}</ref>。膿痂疹可以經接觸傳染<ref name=AFP2014/>。通常由其症狀進行診斷<ref name=AFP2014/>。

<!-- 預防及治療 -->
膿痂疹的預防方式包含[[洗手|洗手]]、避免接觸感染者、清潔傷口<ref name=AFP2014/>。通常使用[[莫匹羅星|莫匹羅星]]、{{le|夫西地酸|fusidic acid}}等[[抗生素|抗生素]]治療<ref name=AFP2014/><ref name=Kon2012>{{cite journal |last1=Koning|first1=S|last2=van der Sande|first2=R|last3=Verhagen|first3=AP|last4=van Suijlekom-Smit|first4=LW|last5=Morris|first5=AD|last6=Butler|first6=CC|last7=Berger|first7=M|last8=van der Wouden|first8=JC|title=Interventions for impetigo.|journal=The Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews|date=18 January 2012|volume=1|pages=CD003261|pmid=22258953|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD003261.pub3}}</ref> 。大範圍感染時，可能會給予口服的抗生素如[[頭孢氨苄|頭孢氨苄]]<ref name=AFP2014>{{cite journal |last1=Hartman-Adams|first1=H|last2=Banvard|first2=C|last3=Juckett|first3=G|title=Impetigo: diagnosis and treatment.|journal=American Family Physician|date=15 August 2014|volume=90|issue=4|pages=229–35|pmid=25250996}}</ref>，然而，曾有[[抗生素抗藥性|抗生素抗藥性]]的紀錄<ref name=AFP2014/>。

<!-- 流行病學及預後 -->
在2010年，膿痂疹影響約一億四千萬的人口（約是世界人口總數的2%）<ref name=LancetEpi2012>{{cite journal |last=Vos|first=T|title=Years lived with disability (YLDs) for 1160 sequelae of 289 diseases and injuries 1990-2010: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2010.|journal=Lancet|date=Dec 15, 2012|volume=380|issue=9859|pages=2163–96|pmid=23245607|doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(12)61729-2|url=http://www.documentation.ird.fr/hor/fdi:010059240}}</ref>， 膿痂疹可以發生於各個年齡層，但好發於幼童<ref name=AFP2014/>，在某些地方又被稱為學校瘡（school sores）<ref name=Vic2017>{{cite web|title=Impetigo - school sores|url=https://www.betterhealth.vic.gov.au/health/conditionsandtreatments/impetigo-school-sores|website=Bettel Health Channel|accessdate=10 May 2017|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170705235520/https://www.betterhealth.vic.gov.au/health/conditionsandtreatments/impetigo-school-sores|archivedate=5 July 2017|df=}}</ref>。若不經治療，大部分的人可以在三週內好轉<ref name=AFP2014/>。可能發生的併發症包含[[蜂窩性組織炎|蜂窩性組織炎]]和[[急性增生性腎小球腎炎|鏈球菌感染後腎小球腎炎]]<ref name=AFP2014/> 。膿痂疹的英文為Impetigo，源自於[[拉丁文|拉丁文]]的''impetere''，意為「攻擊」<ref>{{cite book|last1=Limited|first1=Wordsworth Editions|title=Concise English Dictionary|date=1993|publisher=Wordsworth Editions|isbn=9781840224979|page=452|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=qYHPuEwaBswC&pg=PA452|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161003055957/https://books.google.ca/books?id=qYHPuEwaBswC&pg=PA452|archivedate=2016-10-03|df=}}</ref>。

'''顯微鏡座AU''' (AU Mic)是一顆[[紅矮星|紅矮星]]<ref>{{cite journal|author = Maran, S. P., Woodgate, B. E., Carpenter, K. G., Robinson, R. D., Shore, S. N., and Linsky, J. L.|title = An Investigation of the Flare Star AU Mic with the Goddard High Resolution Spectrograph on the Hubble Space Telescope|journal = Bulletin of the American Astronomical Society|volume = 23|pages = 1382|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1991BAAS...23.1382M|accessdate = 2007-05-30|date=September 1991}}</ref>，距離[[地球|地球]]10[[秒差距|秒差距]](32[[光年|光年]])遠 – 大約是與[[太陽|太陽]]最近恆星距離的8倍<ref name = "KALASETAL04">{{cite journal|url = http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/full/303/5666/1990|title = Discovery of a Large Dust Disk Around the Nearby Star AU Microscopii|author = Paul Kalas, Michael C. Liu and Brenda C. Matthews|date = 2004-03-26 |journal = [[Science_(journal)|Science]]|volume = 303|number = 5666|pages = 1990–1992|doi = 10.1126/science.1093420|pmid = 14988511}}</ref>顯微鏡座疔瀟。苽擭AU是一顆年輕的恆星，只有1,200萬歲，不到[[太陽|太陽]]年齡的1%<ref name= PLAVCHANETAL05">{{cite journal|author = Peter Plavchan, M. Jura, and S. J. Lipsc|title = Where Are the M Dwarf Disks Older Than 10 Million Years?|journal = The Astrophysical Journal|date = 2005-10-01 |volume = 631|issue = 2|pages = 1161–1169|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2005ApJ...631.1161P|doi = 10.1086/432568}}</ref>，質量祇有太陽的一半，光度則只有十分之一它是位於疔瀟。苽擭[[顯微鏡座|顯微鏡座]]的一顆[[變星|變星]]，所以這顆恆星是依據[[變星命名|變星命名]]規則命名的顯微鏡疔瀟。苽擭 AU是[[繪架座β移動星群|繪架座β移動星群]]的成員之一<ref>{{cite journal|title = Young Stars Near the Sun|author = B Zuckerman and Inseok Song|journal = Annual Review of Astronomy &Astrophysics|volume = 42|issue = 1|pages = 685–721|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2004ARA%26A..42..685Z|doi = 10.1146/annurev.astro.42.053102.134111|date=September 2004}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author = Barrado y Navascués, David; Stauffer, John R.; Song, Inseok; Caillault, J.-P.|title = The Age of beta Pictoris|journal = The Astrophysical Journal|date = 1999-08-01|volume = 520|issue = 2|pages = L123–L126|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1999ApJ...520L.123B|accessdate = 2007-05-14|doi = 10.1086/312162}}</ref>，它也可能受到[[顯微鏡座AT|顯微鏡座AT]]的約束，而是一對[[聯星|聯星]] <ref>{{cite journal|author = Monsignori Fossi, B. C.; Landini, M.; Drake, J. J.; Cully, S. L.|title = The EUV spectrum of AT Microscopii|journal = Astronomy & Astrophysics|volume = 302|pages = 193|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1995A&A...302..193M|accessdate = 2007-06-08|date=October 1995}}</ref>如同疔瀟。苽擭[[繪架座β|繪架座β]]一樣，顯微鏡座AU有一個已知是[[岩屑盤|岩屑盤]]的[[星周盤|星周盤]]。

== 恆星的性質 ==
顯微鏡座AU是一顆小的M型恆星，實際半徑只有[[太陽|太陽]]的60%，質量大約是太陽的50%<ref name="DELZANNA2002">{{cite journal|author = Del Zanna, G.; Landini, M. and Mason, H. E.|title = Spectroscopic diagnostics of stellar transition regions and coronae in the XUV: AU Mic in quiescence|journal = Astronomy and Astrophysics|volume = 385|pages = 968–985|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2002A%26A...385..968D|accessdate = 2007-05-30|doi = 10.1051/0004-6361:20020164|date=April 2002}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|url = http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/full/sci;303/5666/1982|title = Nearby Planetary Disks|author = David Mouillet|journal = Science|volume = 303|number = 5666|pages = 1982–1983|date = 2004-03-26 |doi = 10.1126/science.1095851|pmid = 15044792}}</ref>

顯微鏡座AU表面的[[有效溫度|有效溫度]]是3730[[熱力學溫標|K]]<ref name = "LINSKYETAL82">{{cite journal|author = Linsky, J. L., Bornmann, P. L., Carpenter, K. G., Hege, E. K., Wing, R. F., Giampapa, M. S.|title = Outer atmospheres of cool stars. XII - A survey of IUE ultraviolet emission line spectra of cool dwarf stars|journal = The Astrophysical Journal|date = 1982-09-15|issue = 1|volume = 260|pages = 670–694|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1982ApJ...260..670L|accessdate = 2007-06-14|doi = 10.1086/160288}}</ref>。

== 變異性 ==
顯微鏡座AU的光度以近似[[正弦|正弦]]的形式以4.865天的週期變化著，光度變化的振幅相對於時間非常緩慢在疔瀟。苽擭[[UBV光電系統|V頻道]]的光度變化在1971年只有0.3[[絕對星等|等]]，在1980年竟然祇有0.1等<ref>{{cite journal|author = Butler, C. J., Doyle, J. G., Andrews, A. D., Byrne, P. B., Linsky, J. L., Bornmann, P. L.|title = Rotational modulation and flares on RS CVn and BY DRA systems. II - IUE observations of BY Draconis and AU Microscopii|journal = Astronomy and Astrophysics|volume = 174|number = 1-2|pages = 139–157|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1987A%26A...174..139B|accessdate = 2007-05-28|date=March 1987}}</ref>。

顯微鏡座AU已經被[[電磁波頻譜|電磁波頻譜]]中從[[X射線|X射線]]至[[無線電|無線電]]所有的波段觀測過，並被確認是一顆在所有波段都有活動的[[耀星|[閃]焰星]] <ref>{{cite journal|author = Maran, S. P.; Robinson, R. D.; Shore, S. N.; Brosius, J. W.; Carpenter, K. W.; Vedder, Peter W.; Vallerga, John V.|title = Extreme Ultraviolet Explorer deep survey observations of a large flare on AU Microscopii|journal = The Astrophysical Journal|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1993ApJ...414L..49C|volume = 414|number = 2|pages = L49–L52|date = 1993-09-10 |accessdate = 2007-05-28|doi = 10.1086/156986}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author = Kundu, M. Kunkel|title = Activity in Flare Stars in the Solar Neighborhood|journal = The Astrophysical Journal Supplement|year = 1973|volume = 25|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1973ApJS...25....1K|accessdate = 2007-05-28|pages = 1|doi = 10.1086/190263}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|author = Butler, C. == 岩屑盤 ==
[[File:Debris_disk_AU_Mic_HST.jpg|left]]的顯微鏡座AU[[岩屑盤|岩屑盤]]影像。]]
在2003年，[[保羅·卡拉斯|保羅·卡拉斯]]和他的研究夥伴首度使用[[夏威夷|夏威夷]][[毛納基山天文台|毛納基山天文台]]2.2米口徑的望遠鏡，在可見光的波段解析出顯微鏡座AU有塵埃的[[星周盤|星周盤]]<ref name = "KALASETAL04" />這些大的岩屑盤以側面朝向著地球疔瀟。苽擭<ref>{{cite journal|author = Paul Kalas, James R. Graham and Mark Clampin|title = A planetary system as the origin of structure in Fomalhaut's dust belt|journal = Nature|volume = 435|date = 2005-06-23 |pages = 1067–1070|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2005Natur.435.1067K|doi = 10.1038/nature03601|issue = 7045}}</ref>，測量到其半徑至少有200[[天文單位|天文單位]]距離恆星有如此大的距離疔瀟。苽擭，盤中塵埃的[[壽命|壽命]]足以超過顯微鏡 AU現在的年齡<ref name = "KALASETAL04" />盤中氣體和塵埃粒子的質量比率大約是疔瀟。苽擭6：1，遠低於通常假設的100：1的初始值<ref name="ROBERGEETAL05">{{cite journal|author = Aki Roberge, Alycia J. Weinberger, Seth Redfield, and Paul D. Feldman|title = Rapid Dissipation of Primordial Gas from the AU Microscopii Debris Disk|journal = The Astrophysical Journal|date = 2005-06-20 |volume = 626|issue = 2|pages = L105–L108|url =http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2005ApJ...626L.105R|doi = 10.1086/431899}}</ref>，因此被歸類為"缺乏氣體"的岩屑盤估計在可見的盤中氣體總量大約相當於月球的質量疔瀟。苽擭，而當塵埃產生成更大的[[星子|星子]]時，估計質量至少是月球的6倍<ref>{{cite journal|author = C. H. Chen, B. M. Patten, M. W. Werner, C. D. Dowell, K. R. Stapelfeldt, I. Song, J. R. Stauffer, M. Blaylock, K. D. Gordon, and V. Krause|title = A Spitzer Study of Dusty Disks around Nearby, Young Stars|journal = The Astrophysical Journal|date = 2005-12-01 |volume = 634|issue = 2|pages = 1372–1384|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2005ApJ...634.1372C|doi = 10.1086/497124}}</ref>。

顯微鏡座AU的[[天文光譜學|光譜能量分佈]]在[[次微米天文學|次微米]]波長上顯示岩屑盤內有一個擴展到17天文單位的洞孔<ref>{{cite journal|title = A Submillimeter Search of Nearby Young Stars for Cold Dust: Discovery of Debris Disks around Two Low-Mass Stars|author = Michael C. Liu, Brenda C. Matthews, Jonathan P. Williams, and Paul G. Kalas|journal = [[The_Astrophysical_Journal|The Astrophysical Journal]]|date = 2004-06-10 |volume = 608|issue = 1|pages = 526–532|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2004ApJ...608..526L|doi = 10.1086/392531}}</ref>，而散射光的影像估計洞孔的半徑是12天文單位<ref name = "KIRSTETAL05">{{cite journal|author = John E. Kirst, D. R. Ardila, D. A. Golimowski, M. Clampin, H. C. Ford, G. D. Illingworth, G. F. Hartig, F. Bartko, N. Benítez, J. P. Blakeslee, R. J. Bouwens, L. D. Bradley, T. J. Broadhurst, R. A. Brown, C. J. Burrows, E. S. Cheng, N. J. G. Cross, R. Demarco, P. D. Feldman, M. Franx, T. Goto, C. Gronwall, B. Holden, N. Homeier, L. Infante, R. A. Kimble, M. P. Lesser, A. R. Martel, S. Mei, F. Mennanteau, G. R. Meurer, G. K. Miley, V. Motta, M. Postman, P. Rosati, M. Sirianni, W. B. Sparks, H. D. Tran, Z. I. Tsvetanov, R. L. White, AND W. Zheng|title = Hubble Space Telescope Advanced Camera for Surveys Coronagraphic Imaging of the AU Microscopii Debris Disk|journal = The Astronomical Journal|volume = 129|issue = 2|pages = 1008–1017|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2005AJ....129.1008K|doi = 10.1086/426755|date=February 2005}}</ref>結合光譜能量分佈和表面的亮度疔瀟。苽擭，估計洞孔的半徑更小，只有1-10天文單位<ref name = "METCHEVETAL05">{{cite journal|author = Stanimir A. Metchev, Joshua A. Eisner, and Lynne A. Hillenbrand|title = Adaptive Optics Imaging of the AU Microscopii Circumstellar Disk: Evidence for Dynamical Evolution|journal = The Astrophysical Journal|date = 2005-03-20 |volume = 622|issue = 1|pages = 451–462|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2005ApJ...622..451M|doi = 10.1086/427869}}</ref>。
在40天文單位之內，盤的內部結構顯示出是[[非對稱|非對稱]]的<ref name = "LIU04">{{cite journal|title = Substructure in the Circumstellar Disk Around the Young Star AU Microscopii|author = Michael C. Liu|journal = Science|date = 2004-09-03 |volume = 305|number = 5689|pages = 1442–1444|url = http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/full/305/5689/1442|doi = 10.1126/science.1102929|pmid = 15308766}}</ref>，內部的結構已經與受到較大天體影響或最近有行星形成的比較，期望能看見更大的身影<ref name = "LIU04"/>。

出現在內部的洞孔和不對稱引導了很多的天文學家在此搜尋行星循著顯微鏡疔瀟。苽擭 AU的軌道，迄2007年尚無任何可以導致行星的發現<ref name = "METCHEVETAL05" /><ref name = "MASCIADRIETAL05">{{cite journal|author = E. Masciadri, R. Mundt, Th. Henning, and C. Alvarez|title = A Search for Hot Massive Extrasolar Planets around Nearby Young Stars with the Adaptive Optics System NACO|journal = The Astrophysical Journal|date = 2005-06-01 |volume = 625|issue = 2|pages = 1004–1018|url = http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2005ApJ...625.1004M|doi = 10.1086/429687}}</ref>。

盤面上的表面亮度(單位面積的亮度)是從恆星的投影距離<math>b</math>和形狀特性的函數在盤面內側疔瀟。苽擭15天文單位距離內的密度似乎是一個定值<ref name = "KIRSTETAL05" />，大約<math>b \approx 15 AU</math>密度開始降低；開始減緩的較慢，大約是<math>b^{-\alpha}</math> 此處<math>\alpha \approx 1.8</math>；再往外<math>b \approx 43 AU</math>，光度下降得更陡峭，大約是 <math>b^{-\alpha}</math>，此處<math>\alpha \approx 4.8</math><ref name = "KIRSTETAL05" />這種疔瀟。苽擭"破裂幂律"形狀與[[繪架座β|繪架座β]]的盤面輪廓非常相似。

== 觀測的方法 ==
顯微鏡座AU的盤面已經被各種不同波段的[[波長|波長]]觀測過，給了我們這個系統不同型態的資訊在疔瀟。苽擭[[可見光|光學波長]]下觀測到的光線是恆星的光，它們被塵埃粒子反射(散射)進入我們的視線內觀察這些波長時疔瀟。苽擭，利用[[日冕儀|日冕儀遮版]]阻擋直接來自恆星的光，這樣可以提供高分辨率的盤面影像因為波長比塵埃粒子直徑大的光波不易被散射疔瀟。苽擭，在不同的波長上比較(例如，可見光和紅外線)就可以得知盤面上塵埃粒子尺寸大小的訊息<ref name="ucbn0108">{{cite news | first=Robert | last=Sanders | title=Dust around nearby star like powder snow | publisher=UC Berkeley News | date=2007-01-08 | url=http://www.berkeley.edu/news/media/releases/2007/01/08_dust.shtml | accessdate=2007-01-11 }}</ref>。

[[File:Hypothetical_terrestrial_planet_and_moon_orbiting_the_red_dwarf_star_AU_Microscopii.jpg|right]]
[[哈伯太空望遠鏡|哈伯太空望遠鏡]]和[[凱克天文台|凱克望遠鏡]]都曾用來進行光學的觀測，也曾經使用[[紅外線|紅外線]]和次微米波觀測過這個系統由塵埃粒子直接發射出的光線呈現出內熱的結果疔瀟。苽擭(修正[[黑體|黑體]]輻射)，但在這種波長下無法解析出盤面，所以這樣的觀測只能測量來自系統整體的光觀測的波長越長疔瀟。苽擭，就能提供越大塵埃粒子的訊息，也觀察到距離恆星越遠的範圍，這樣的觀測已經由[[詹姆士·克拉克·瑪克斯威爾望遠鏡|詹姆士·克拉克·瑪克斯威爾望遠鏡]]和[[史匹哲太空望遠鏡|史匹哲太空望遠鏡]]完成。

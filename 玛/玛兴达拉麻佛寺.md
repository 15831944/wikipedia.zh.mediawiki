'''玛兴达拉麻佛寺'''，或译'''摩哂陀罗摩寺 '''（{{lang-en|Mahindarama Buddhist Temple}}，也称「牛奶佛寺」）是[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[槟城州|槟城州]]首府[[乔治市|乔治市]]的一座[[上座部佛教|上座部]][[佛教|佛教]][[神庙|寺庙]]。该庙于1918年创立，是马来西亚早期的佛教寺庙之一，<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|title=Mahindarama Buddhist Temple : 85 Years of History (1918 - 2003)|last=|first=|publisher=Mahindarama Dhamma Publication|year=2004|isbn=|location=[[乔治市|乔治市]], [[槟城|槟城]]}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://www.mahindaramatemple.com/msps/history-mahindarama.htm|title=Mahindarama Buddhist Temple|website=www.mahindaramatemple.com|access-date=2017-07-06}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/news/community/2008/05/19/devotees-all-set-for-wesak-day/|title=Devotees all set for Wesak Day - Community {{!}} The Star Online|website=www.thestar.com.my|access-date=2017-07-06}}</ref>其位于金宝路旁，是每年乔治市[[卫塞节|卫塞节]]庆典的主要地点。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2017/05/11/grand-float-parade-caps-off-celebrations/|title=Grand float parade caps off celebrations - Nation {{!}} The Star Online|last=Trisha|first=N.|access-date=2017-07-06}}</ref>该庙也吸引数以万计[[北马|北马]]区域的学生前来祭拜以祈求在考试中顺利。也因如此，学生们将牛奶祭拜给白身佛像，也因此玛兴达拉麻佛寺被称为牛奶神庙。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sinchew.com.my/node/730036|title=初中评估考试明开始．准考生牛奶献佛求佳绩|date=2008-10-13|publisher=[[星洲网|星洲网]]}}</ref>

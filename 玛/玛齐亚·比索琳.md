'''瑪齊亞·比索琳'''（{{lang-it|Marzia Bisognin}}，{{IPA-it|ˈmartsja bizoˈɲin|lang}}；{{bd|1992年|10月21日}}），較廣為人知的是化名'''Marzia '''（前称'''CutiePieMarzia'''），意大利YouTuber。截至2018年12月，Marzia已經有超過750萬名訂閱者與超過1300万觀看次數<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/user/CutiePieMarzia/about|title=CutiePieMarzia about|publisher=YouTube|work=CutiePieMarzia|accessdate=2014-09-22}}</ref>。

== YouTube ==
「Marzia」是YouTube上最有名的美容化妝頻道之一，頻道每個月平均有16萬的新訂閱者<ref name=Shields2014a>{{cite web|last=Shields|first=Mike|url=http://blogs.wsj.com/cmo/2014/05/08/beauty-is-a-monster-category-on-youtube-and-its-still-growing/|title=Beauty is a Monster Category on YouTube, and it's Still Growing|publisher=''The Wall Street Journal''|work=CMO Today|date=2014-05-08|accessdate=2014-09-22}}</ref>，除此之外，「Marzia」也是擁有最多訂閱者的意大利人頻道<ref name=Particelli2014>{{cite web|last=Particelli|first=Daniele|url=http://www.downloadblog.it/post/115774/i-5-canali-italiani-piu-seguiti-su-youtube|title=I 5 canali italiani più seguiti su YouTube|publisher=''Blogo''|work=Download Blog|date=2014-09-18|accessdate=2014-09-29|language=it}}</ref><ref name=Sanna2014>{{cite web|last=Sanna|first=Umberto|url=http://www.tecnocino.it/2014/09/articolo/i-5-canali-youtube-italiani-piu-seguiti/54461/|title=I 5 canali Youtube italiani più seguiti|publisher=''Tecnocino''|date=2014-09-22|accessdate=2014-09-29|language=it}}</ref>。「Marzia」在2014年多了超過220萬的訂閱者，是意大利人頻道之冠<ref name=Cohen2015>{{cite web|last=Cohen|first=Joshua|url=http://www.tubefilter.com/2015/01/23/top-250-most-subscribed-youtube-channels-worldwide-2014/|title=Top 250 Most Subscribed YouTube Channels Worldwide in 2014|publisher=''Tubefilter''|date=2015-01-23|accessdate=2015-06-22}}</ref>。比索琳稱她的粉絲為「Marzipans」<ref name="Demirgian2015">{{cite web|last = Demirgian|first = Francesca|url = http://velvetstyle.it/2015/06/09/cutiepiemarzia-marzia-bisognin-youtube-tutorial-moda-bellezza/|title = CutiePieMarzia, chi è la fashion youtuber che fa impazzire le giovanissime [VIDEO]|publisher = ''Velvet Style''|date = 2015-06-09|accessdate = 2015-06-22|language = it}}</ref>。

2018年10月22日，比索琳宣佈退出YouTube，在她发布的最后一个视频<ref>{{cite AV media|last=Bisognin|first=Marzia|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KvVOf7Mx5iM|title=Goodbye Youtube.|publisher=YouTube|work=CutiePieMarzia|date=2018-10-22|accessdate=2018-12-29}}</ref>中她提到退出Youtube是因为她想尝试一些新事物。

=== 頻道形式 ===
「Marzia」的影片題材多為時尚、美容、[[DIY|DIY]]、[[VLOG|VLOG]]和[[电子游戏|遊戲]]<ref name=X2013>{{cite web|last=X |first=Ashleigh |url=http://www.magazinesandmodels.com/2013/06/marzia-bisognin-interview.html |title=Marzia Bisognin Interview |publisher=''Magazines and Models'' |date=2013-06-22 |accessdate=2014-09-22 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141006104521/http://www.magazinesandmodels.com/2013/06/marzia-bisognin-interview.html |archivedate=6 十月 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Knorr|first=Caroline|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/common-sense-media/what-kids-are-really-watc_b_9197366.html|title=What Kids Are Really Watching on YouTube|publisher=''Huffington Post''|date=2016-02-11|accessdate=2016-04-11}}</ref>，且觀看人群多為13-24歲的女性<ref name=Mutterle2014/>。雖然她是意大利人，但她在影片中大多說英語，以吸引來自世界各地的觀眾觀看<ref>{{cite web|url=http://cinema.sky.it/cinema/news/2015/08/11/top-5-youtubers-italia.html|title=Frank Matano e le altre Youtube star Made in Italy|date=2015-08-13|publisher=Sky Italia|language=it|accessdate=2016-07-23|location=Milano}}</ref>。

== 其他 ==
「Marzia」的成功使比索琳踏上時裝設計師之路<ref name=Trowbridge2015>{{cite web|last=Trowbridge|first=Cecily|url=http://www.bustle.com/articles/59431-5-inspiring-youtubers-who-prove-coupledom-doesnt-define-you-because-you-may-need-a-reminder-on|title=5 Inspiring YouTubers Who Prove Coupledom Doesn't Define You (Because You May Need A Reminder on Valentine's Day)|publisher=''Bustle''|date=2015-01-20|accessdate=2015-06-22}}</ref>。2014年，她設計了「Project Shoe」的其中一款鞋「雛菊」（Daisy）<ref>{{cite web|last=Olivia|first=Sage|url=https://sageolivia.com/2014/07/27/daisy-by-marzia/|title="Daisy" by Marzia|work=Sage Olivia|date=2014-07-27|accessdate=2017-02-21}}</ref>。

除了在自己的頻道創作影片外，比索琳也曾為一些網路動畫配音。例如《Oscar's Hotel for Fantastical Creatures》的卡麗（Carrie）<ref>{{cite web|last=Pearce|first=Tilly|url=http://teneightymagazine.com/2015/08/16/new-oscars-hotel-characters-announced-at-summer-in-the-city-2015/|title=New Oscar's Hotel Characters Announced at Summer in the City 2015|publisher=''TenEighty''|date=2015-08-16|accessdate=2016-04-11}}</ref>和《Pugatory》中基於她的狗瑪雅（Maya）設計的角色<ref name=Rody2015>{{cite web|last=Rody-Mantha|first=Bree|url=http://streamdaily.tv/2015/10/28/new-series-pugatory-features-the-voice-talent-of-pewdiepie/|title=New series Pugatory features the voice talent of PewDiePie|publisher=''StreamDaily''|date=2015-10-28|accessdate=2016-04-11}}</ref>。

2015年7月，比索琳所作的恐怖小說《夢之家》（{{lang-it|''La Casa Dei Sogni''}}）出版<ref>{{Cite book|title = Dream House|url = https://books.google.com/books?id=iphvBgAAQBAJ|publisher = Newton Compton Editori|date = 2015-01-29|isbn = 9788854179660|language = it|first = Marzia "CutiePie"|last = Bisognin}}</ref>，同時她也計劃於2016年發行一本科幻小說<ref name=Rody2015/>。

== 商業合作 ==
比索琳和她自2011年開始交往的[[PewDiePie|菲利克斯·謝爾貝格]]（PewDiePie）在YouTube上都有相當規模的訂閱數，導致他們有許多的廣告<ref name=Bauckhage2014>{{cite web|last=Bauckhage|first=Tobias|url=http://variety.com/2014/digital/news/social-media-buzz-november-man-labors-to-overcome-as-above-so-below-1201294050/|title=Social Media Buzz: 'November Man' Labors to Overcome 'As Above, So Below'|publisher=''Variety''|date=2014-08-29|accessdate=2014-09-22}}</ref>。2014年，比索琳和謝爾貝格2人獲電影《[[忐忑_(電影)|忐忑]]》的製作團隊邀請到[[巴黎地下墓穴|巴黎地下墓穴]]{{註|實際上只有謝爾貝格前往巴黎地下墓穴。}}為他們製作「地下尋寶」形式的宣傳影片<ref name=Coyle2014>{{cite web|last=Coyle|first=Meghan|url=http://www.etcentric.org/youtube-power-couple-produces-miniseries-for-horror-movie/|title=YouTube Power Couple Produces Miniseries for Horror Movie|publisher=''ET Centric''|date=2014-09-02|accessdate=2014-09-29}}</ref>。

2014年10月，[[美國廣播公司|ABC]]邀請比索琳為其新劇集《[[自拍_(電視劇)|自拍]]》宣傳<ref name=Poggi2014>{{cite web|last=Poggi|first=Jeanine|url=http://adage.com/article/media/tv-networks-promote-shows-youtube/295313/|title=ABC Revamps YouTube Marketing to Better Reach Digital Viewers|publisher=''Advertising Age''|date=2014-10-08|accessdate=2014-10-10}}</ref>。

'''玛洛莉·科默福德'''（{{lang-en|Mallory Comerford}}，{{bd|1997年|9月6日||catIdx=Comerford, Mallory}}）生于密歇根州卡拉马祖，是一名美国女子游泳运动员，主攻短距离自由泳。科默福德2015年毕业于普莱恩韦尔高中<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlive.com/news/kalamazoo/index.ssf/2015/05/plainwell_high_school_names_to.html|title=Plainwell High School names top 18 in Class of 2015|website=MLive.com|date=2015-05-14|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>，同年就读于路易斯维尔大学<ref>{{cite web|url=https://gocards.com/news/2016/9/13/swimming-diving-mallory-comerford-named-to-the-usa-junior-national-team.aspx?mobile=skip|title=Mallory Comerford Named to the USA Junior National Team|publisher=Louisville Cardinals|date=2016-09-13|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>，同时加入美国国家青年队<ref>{{cite web|url=https://swimmingworld.azureedge.net/news/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/National-Junior-Team-2015-2016.pdf|title=2015-2016 USA Swimming National Junior Team |work=USA Swimming|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>。2017年游泳世锦赛，科默福德获得了五枚金牌，全部来自于接力项目。她也因该届世锦赛上的表现而获得美国游泳协会颁发的年度最佳突破奖<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.swimmingworldmagazine.com/news/swimming-world-presents-mallory-comerford-coming-into-her-own/|title=Swimming World Presents: Mallory Comerford – Coming Into Her Own|publisher=Swimming World Magazine|date=2018-03-02|accessdate=2018-12-16}}</ref>。

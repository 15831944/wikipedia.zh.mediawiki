'''鴛鴦屬'''（'''''Aix'''''）是[[鴨亞科|鴨亞科]]的一個屬，有時劃歸[[麻鸭亚科|麻鸭亚科]]<ref>{{Cite journal |author1=Johnson, Kevin P.  |author2=Sorenson, Michael D. |year=1999 |title=Phylogeny and biogeography of dabbling ducks (genus ''Anas''): a comparison of molecular and morphological evidence |journal=[[Auk_(journal)|Auk]] |volume=116 |issue=3 |pages=792–805 |url=http://sora.unm.edu/sites/default/files/journals/auk/v116n03/p0792-p0805.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]] |accessdate=September 2, 2008 |doi=10.2307/4089339}}</ref><ref>{{Cite book |author1=Madge, Steve  |author2=Burn, Hilary |year=1987 |title=Wildfowl : an identification guide to the ducks, geese and swans of the world |publisher=[[Helm_Identification_Guides|Christopher Helm]] |location=London |isbn=0-7470-2201-1}}</ref>，其下只有[[美国木鸭|美国木鸭]]和[[鴛鴦|鴛鴦]]這兩個種。Aix源自古希臘語，[[亞里士多德|亞里士多德]]用它來指一種未知潜水鳥。<ref name= job90>{{cite book | last= Jobling | first= James A | year= 2010| title= The Helm Dictionary of Scientific Bird Names | publisher = Christopher Helm | location = London | isbn = 978-1-4081-2501-4 |page = 37}}</ref>

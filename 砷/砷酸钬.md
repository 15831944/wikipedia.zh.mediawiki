'''砷酸钬'''是[[钬|钬]]的[[砷酸盐|砷酸盐]]，化学式为HoAsO<sub>4</sub>。它有着很好的热稳定性，其p''K''<sub>sp,c</sub>为23.87±0.08。<ref>Firsching, F. Henry. Solubility products of the trivalent rare-​earth arsenates. ''Journal of Chemical and Engineering Data'', 1992. 37 (4): 497-499. DOI:10.1021/je00008a028</ref>
==制备==
砷酸钬可由[[砷酸钠|砷酸钠]]（Na<sub>3</sub>AsO<sub>4</sub>）和[[氯化钬|氯化钬]]（HoCl<sub>3</sub>）在[[溶液|溶液]]中反应制得：<ref>Gabisoniya, Ts. D.; Nanobashvili, E. M.. Synthesis of rare earth metal arsenates{{ru}}. ''Soobshcheniya Akademii Nauk Gruzinskoi SSR'' (1980), 97(2), 345-8. ISSN:0002-3167</ref>
: Na<sub>3</sub>AsO<sub>4</sub> + HoCl<sub>3</sub> → 3 NaCl + HoAsO<sub>4</sub>↓

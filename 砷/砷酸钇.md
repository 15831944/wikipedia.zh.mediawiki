'''砷酸钇'''是一种[[无机化合物|无机化合物]]，化学式为YAsO<sub>4</sub>，具有[[锆石|锆石]]或[[白钨矿|白钨矿]]结构。<ref>{{cite journal|doi=10.1103/physrevb.83.134109|title=Experimental and theoretical study of structural properties and phase transitions in YAsO|date=2011|journal=Physical Review B|volume=83|issue=13|url=https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/PhysRevB.83.134109|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=D. Errandonea}}</ref>

==制备==
将[[三氧化二砷|三氧化二砷]]用[[硝酸|硝酸]]氧化，[[氧化钇|氧化钇]]溶于[[硝酸|硝酸]]，两者混合，于60°C反应得到。<ref>Strzep, Adam; Watras, Adam; Zawisza, Katarzyna; Boutinaud, Philippe; Wiglusz, Rafal J.. Forgotten and Resurrected Chernovite-​(Y)​: YAsO4 Doped with Eu3+ Ions as a Potential Nanosized Luminophore. ''Inorganic Chemistry'', 2017. 56 (18): 10914-19025. DOI:10.1021/acs.inorgchem.7b01089</ref>

==制备==
砷酸镝可由[[砷酸钠|砷酸钠]]（Na<sub>3</sub>AsO<sub>4</sub>）和[[氯化镝|氯化镝]]（DyCl<sub>3</sub>）在[[溶液|溶液]]中反应制得：<ref>Gabisoniya, Ts. D.; Nanobashvili, E. M.. Synthesis of rare earth metal arsenates{{ru}}. ''Soobshcheniya Akademii Nauk Gruzinskoi SSR'' (1980), 97(2), 345-8. ISSN:0002-3167</ref>
: Na<sub>3</sub>AsO<sub>4</sub> + DyCl<sub>3</sub> → 3 NaCl + DyAsO<sub>4</sub>↓

'''砷酸钷'''是[[钷|钷]]的[[砷酸盐|砷酸盐]]，化学式为PmAsO<sub>4</sub>，具有[[放射性|放射性]]。

砷酸钷具有硅酸锆（ZrSiO<sub>4</sub>）结构。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Structure maps of ABX4 inorganic compounds|journal=Russian Journal of Inorganic Chemistry|url=https://link.springer.com/article/10.1134/S0036023612120133|date=2012-12-01|volume=57|issue=12|doi=10.1134/s0036023612120133|language=en|pages=1584–1587|issn=0036-0236|accessdate=2018-04-02|author=V. E. Lashin, N. A. Khritokhin, O. V. Andreev}}</ref>
==制备==
砷酸钷可由[[砷酸二氢钠|砷酸二氢钠]]（NaH<sub>2</sub>AsO<sub>4</sub>）和[[硝酸钷|硝酸钷]]（Pm(NO<sub>3</sub>)<sub>3</sub>）在[[溶液|溶液]]中反应制得。<ref>Escobar, Marta E.; Baran, Enrique J. Precipitation of rare earth arsenates from aqueous solutions{{de}}. ''Zeitschrift fuer Chemie'', 1978. 18 (11): 418-419. ISSN:0044-2402.</ref>

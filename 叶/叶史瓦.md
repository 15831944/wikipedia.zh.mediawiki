'''叶史瓦'''（{{IPAc-en|j|ə|ˈ|ʃ|iː|v|ə}}; {{lang-he-n|ישיבה}}）或'''授业座'''是一种[[犹太人|犹太人]]的宗教教育机构，主要负责对犹太教传统宗教典籍，尤其是《[[塔木德|塔木德]]》和《[[妥拉|妥拉]]》进行研究和教授。叶史瓦的学习分为日间的统一授课（称为“shiurim”） ，以及课下的学习讨论（称为“Chavrusa”）<ref name="agora">{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=8uZvs6_fGeMC |title=World Wide Agora |last=Liebersohn |first=Aharon |page=155 |year=2009 |isbn=9781409284772}}</ref><ref name="Forta">{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NhdoWlrzcg4C&pg=PA89&dq=chavruta#v=onepage&q=chavruta&f=false |title=Judaism |last=Forta |first=Arye |page=89 |publisher=Heinemann Educational |year=1989 |isbn=0-435-30321-X}}</ref>。

在过去，叶史瓦的学习只能由男性参加。当代，[[猶太教正統派|猶太教正統派]]以外宗派的叶史瓦以及部分现代正統派的叶史瓦也对女性开放<ref name="Forta">{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NhdoWlrzcg4C&pg=PA89&dq=chavruta#v=onepage&q=chavruta&f=false |title=Judaism |last=Forta |first=Arye |page=89 |publisher=Heinemann Educational |year=1989 |isbn=0-435-30321-X}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://matzav.com/sarah-schenirer-ah-the-mother-of-the-bais-yaakov-movement-on-her-yahrtzeit-today-26-adar |title=Sarah Schenirer a”h, the Mother of the Bais Yaakov Movement, On Her Yahrtzeit, Today, 26 Adar |website=matzav.com |date=2013-03-08 |accessdate=2018-10-06}}</ref>。

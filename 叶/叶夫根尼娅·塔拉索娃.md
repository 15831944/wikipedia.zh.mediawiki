'''叶夫根尼娅·马克西莫夫娜·塔拉索娃'''（{{lang-ru|Евгения Максимовна Тарасова}}，{{bd|1994年|12月17日||}}）生于鞑靼斯坦共和国喀山，是一名俄罗斯女子花样滑冰运动员，主攻双人滑<ref name="FSR-ET">{{cite web |url= http://fsrussia.ru/biografiya/60-tarasova-evgenia.html |script-title=ru:Тарасова Евгения Максимовна |work={{lang|ru|Федерация фигурного катания на коньках России}} |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140926143001/http://fsrussia.ru/biografiya/60-tarasova-evgenia.html |archivedate=2014-09-26 |deadurl= no |accessdate=2019-02-04}}</ref>。塔拉索娃1998年开始接触花样滑冰，她在早年时期主攻单人滑<ref name=fskate>{{cite web |url= http://www.fskate.ru/skaters/103.html |script-title=ru:Тарасова Евгения Максимовна |website= fskate.ru |accessdate=2019-02-04 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180704020931/http://www.fskate.ru/skaters/103.html |archivedate=2018-07-04}}</ref>。青年时期，她移居至莫斯科，决定转攻双人滑<ref name=GS141024>{{cite news |url= http://www.goldenskate.com/2014/10/evgenia-tarasova-vladimir-morozov/ |title= Tarasova and Morozov looking to make impact |first= Tatjana |last= Flade |work= Golden Skate |date= 2014-10-24 |accessdate=2014-10-24}}</ref>。刚开始时，她的搭档是Egor Chudin<ref name="ISU-1112">{{cite web |url= http://www.isuresults.com/bios/isufs00013407.htm |title= Evgenia TARASOVA / Egor CHUDIN: 2011/2012 |publisher= International Skating Union |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20130910181327/http://www.isuresults.com/bios/isufs00013407.htm |archivedate= 2013-09-10 |deadurl= no }}</ref>。2012年春，她开始与[[弗拉基米尔·叶夫根耶维奇·莫洛佐夫|弗拉基米尔·莫洛佐夫]]搭档<ref name=GS141024/>。

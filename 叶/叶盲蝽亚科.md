'''叶盲蝽亚科'''（學名：{{lang|la|Phylinae}}）是[[盲蝽科|盲蝽科]]之下的一個[[植食性|植食性]][[半翅目|半翅目]][[昆蟲|昆蟲]]的[[亞科|亞科]]{{r|Zhang 2010}}。

==分類==
過往基於Carvalho (1952)的分類<ref>Carvalho, J. C. M. (1952). On the major classification of the Miridae (Hemiptera): With keys to subfamilies and tribes and a catalogue of the world genera. ''Anais da Academia brasileira de Ciencias 24(1) 31-110.</ref>，本亞科物種分為五個族{{r|Zhang 2010}}；但現時這些族都已經重組和再細分。下列現時主要分類法的內容，分成七個族：
* [[蚁叶盲蝽族|蚁叶盲蝽族]]{{r|Zhang 2010}} [[Hallodapini|Hallodapini]] {{small|Van Duzee, 1916}}（=[[敖盲蝽族|敖盲蝽族]]{{r|Zhang 2010}} [[Auricillocorini|Auricillocorini]] {{small|Schuh, 1984}}）
*[[Nasocorini|Nasocorini]] <small>Reuter, 1883</small>
*[[Exaeretini|Exaeretini]] <small>Puton, 1875</small>
*[[Cremnorrhinini|Cremnorrhinini]] <small>Reuter, 1883</small>
**[[Cremnorrhina|Cremnorrhina]] <small>Reuter, 1883</small>（=[[Pronotocrepini|Pronotocrepini]] <small>Knight, 1929</small>, now a junior synonym of Cremnorrhina）
**[[Coatonocapsina|Coatonocapsina]] <small>Menard, 2013</small>
* [[奇盲蝽族|奇盲蝽族]]{{r|Zhang 2010}} [[Leucophoropterini|Leucophoropterini]] <small>Schuh, 1974</small>
**[[Tuxedoina|Tuxedoina]] <small>Menard, 2013</small>
** [[奇盲蝽亚族|奇盲蝽亚族]]{{r|Zhang 2010}} [[Leucophoropterina|Leucophoropterina]] <small>Schuh, 1974</small>
* [[叶盲蝽族|叶盲蝽族]]{{r|Zhang 2010}} [[Phylini|Phylini]] <small>Douglas and Scott, 1865</small>：包括75個屬、超過550個物種<ref name="bugref_348155">{{cite web|url=https://bugguide.net/node/view/348155|title=Phylini Tribe Information|website=BugGuide.net|publisher=Iowa State University|acces-sdate=2018-01-25}}</ref><ref name="ITIS_717649">{{ITIS |id=717649 |taxon=Phylini |access-date=2018-01-25}}</ref>。在2013年修訂前，本族物種原為[[多系群|多系群]]<ref>{{Cite news|author1=Menard, K. L. |author2=Schuh, R. T. |author3=Woolley, J. B.  |lastauthoramp=yes |year=2013|title=Total-evidence phylogenetic analysis and reclassification of the Phylinae (Insecta: Heteroptera: Miridae), with the recognition of new tribes and subtribes and a redefinition of Phylini|journal=Cladistics|doi=10.1111/cla.12052}}</ref>；現時已改善。
**[[Keltoniina|Keltoniina]] <small>Menard, 2013</small>
** [[叶盲蝽亚族|叶盲蝽亚族]]{{r|Zhang 2010}} [[Phylina|Phylina]] <small>Douglas and Scott, 1865</small>
**[[Oncotylina|Oncotylina]] <small>Douglas and Scott, 1865</small>
*[[Semiini|Semiini]] <small>Knight, 1923</small>

**[[Semiina|Semiina]] <small>Knight, 1923</small>

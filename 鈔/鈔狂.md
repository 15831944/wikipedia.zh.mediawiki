《'''鈔狂'''》（{{lang-en|"Psycho"}}）是美国说唱歌手[[波斯特·马龙|波斯特·马龙]]的歌曲，{{tsl|en|Ty Dolla Sign||Ty Dolla Sign}}助阵献唱。歌曲作为波斯特·马龙的第二支录音室专辑《{{tsl|en|Beerbongs & Bentleys|趴踢人生}}》的第三支单曲，于2018年2月23日由[[聯眾唱片|聯眾唱片]]发行。<ref>{{cite news|last=Wicks|first=Amanda|title=Post Malone and Ty Dolla Sign Team for "Psycho": Listen|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/post-malone-and-ty-dolla-sign-team-for-psycho-listen/|accessdate=2018-02-23|work=Pitchfork|date=2018-02-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Thiessen|first=Brock|title=Post Malone and Ty Dolla $ign Connect for "Psycho"|url=https://exclaim.ca/music/article/post_malone_and_ty_dolla_ign_connect_for_psycho|accessdate=2018-02-23|work={{tsl|en|Exclaim!||Exclaim!}}|date=2018-02-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=New Music: Post Malone feat. Ty Dolla $ign – 'Psycho'|url=http://www.rap-up.com/2018/02/22/new-music-post-malone-ty-dolla-sign-psycho/|accessdate=2018-02-23|work={{tsl|en|Rap-Up||Rap-Up}}|date=2018-02-22}}</ref>歌曲的创作和制作由{{tsl|en|Louis Bell|路易·贝尔}}和马龙完成，Ty Dolla Sign也参与了歌曲的创作。歌曲的歌词聊了马龙的生活方式，以及探讨了名声过大和诚信问题的主题。<ref>{{cite news|last=Mastrogiannis|first=Nicole|title=Post Malone Teams Up With Ty Dolla $ign on New Song "Psycho"|url=https://channel955.iheart.com/content/2018-02-22-post-malone-teams-up-with-ty-dolla-ign-on-new-song-psycho/|accessdate=2018-02-23|work={{tsl|en|iHeartRadio||iHeartRadio}}|date=2018-02-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Wright|first=Hayden|title=Post Malone Drops 'Psycho' Featuring Ty Dolla $Ign: Listen|url=http://news.radio.com/2018/02/23/post-malone-psycho-ty-dolla-sign/|accessdate=2018-02-23|work=[[CBS电台|CBS Radio]]|date=2018-02-23}}</ref>歌曲空降美国《公告牌》[[告示牌|百强单曲榜]]第二位，次于[[德雷克_(歌手)|德雷克]]的《[[神的旨意|神的旨意]]》，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8231969/drake-gods-plan-post-malone-psycho-hot-100|title=Drake's 'God's Plan' No. 1 on Billboard Hot 100 for Sixth Week, Post Malone's 'Psycho' Debuts at No. 2|work=Billboard|accessdate=2018-03-17}}</ref>并于2018年6月升至第一位。<ref name="Hot 100">{{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8460299/post-malone-psycho-no-1-hot-100|title=Post Malone's 'Psycho' Is No. 1 On The Hot 100|work=Billboard|last=Trust|first=Gary|date=2018-06-11|accessdate=2018-06-12}}</ref>

==发行和宣传==
马龙在2017年1月4日发出了歌曲的第一则预告，一段人在录音室放歌曲的视频。<ref>{{cite news|last=DeVille|first=Chris|title=Post Malone – "Psycho" (Feat. Ty Dolla $ign)|url=https://www.stereogum.com/1984347/post-malone-psycho-feat-ty-dolla-ign/music/|accessdate=2018-02-23|work={{tsl|en|Stereogum||Stereogum}}|date=2018-02-23}}</ref>一年后在2018年2月21日，他在推特上放出三十秒的歌曲片段，<ref>{{cite news|last=Schmitt|first=Tyler|title=Post Malone Teams Up with Ty Dolla $ign for New Song 'Psycho'|url=http://variancemagazine.com/sounds/8968-post-malone-psycho-featuring-ty-dolla-sign|accessdate=2018-02-27|work={{tsl|en|Variance (magazine)||Variance}}|date=2018-02-23}}</ref>展现主歌的旋律。<ref>{{cite news|last=Lamarre|first=Carl|title=Post Malone Teases Ty Dolla $ign-Assisted Track 'Psycho' on Twitter|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8214675/post-malone-teases-new-song-psycho-ty-dolla-sign|accessdate=2018-02-23|work=Billboard|date=2018-02-22}}</ref>单曲封面中主体是一只狼和一架[[推土機|推土機]]，机身上印有“Posty Co.”的字样。<ref>{{cite news|last=Berry|first=Peter|title=Post Malone Previews New Song "Psycho" Featuring Ty Dolla Sign|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/video/2018/02/post-malone-psycho-ty-dolla-sign/|accessdate=2018-02-23|work={{tsl|en|XXL (magazine)||XXL}}|date=2018-02-21}}</ref>此外还为歌曲推出了产品，三件不同款的长袖[[T恤|T恤]]。<ref>{{cite news|last=Krastz|first=Roger|title=Post Malone Releases "Psycho" Merchandise|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/lifestyle/2018/02/post-malone-releases-psycho-merchandise/|accessdate=2018-02-27|work=XXL|date=2018-02-26}}</ref>

==作曲==
依据杂志《[[告示牌_(雜誌)|公告牌]]》和《[[Spin_(雜誌)|Spin]]》的报道，歌曲有着慢摇[[陷阱音樂|陷阱乐]]节奏并且旋律上与马龙2015年的歌曲《{{tsl|en|White Iverson|}}》相似。<ref>{{cite news|last=Kenney|first=Kevan|title=Post Malone & Ty Dolla $ign Drop New Song 'Psycho'|url=https://www.billboard.com/video/bbnews022318postmalone-8215379|accessdate=2018-02-27|work=Billboard|date=2018-02-23}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Arcand|first=Rob|title=Post Malone – "Psycho" ft. Ty Dolla $ign|url=https://www.spin.com/2018/02/post-malone-psycho-ft-ty-dolla-ign/|accessdate=2018-02-23|work=Spin|date=2018-02-23}}</ref>

==音乐视频==
《鈔狂》的官方音乐视频在2018年3月22日公开。视频由詹姆斯·迪纳执导。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=au2n7VVGv_c|title=Post Malone – Psycho ft. Ty Dolla $ign|work=YouTube|date=2018-03-22}}</ref>拍摄了马龙驾驶一辆{{tsl|en|FV103 Spartan||FM103 Spartan}}[[装甲输送车|装甲输送车]]，用[[火焰喷射器|火焰喷射器]]与狼搏斗，以及马龙和Sign处在飞机残骸中。

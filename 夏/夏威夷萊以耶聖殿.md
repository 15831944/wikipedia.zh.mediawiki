'''夏威夷萊以耶聖殿'''（Laie Hawaii Temple）是一座[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的聖殿，位於夏威夷群島中的瓦胡島，距檀香山有{{convert|35|mi|km}}<ref name="Aikau-2008">{{cite journal |last= Aikau |first= Hokulani K. |title= Resisting Exile in the Homeland: He Mo'olemo No Lā'ie |journal= American Indian Quarterly |volume= 32 |issue= 1 |pages= 70–95 |publisher= [[University_of_Nebraska_Press|University of Nebraska Press]] |location= Lincoln, NE |date=Winter 2008 |issn= 0095-182X |doi= 10.1353/aiq.2008.0003}}</ref>。聖殿的遊客中心年均有超過100,000人參觀<ref name="Kayal-2004">{{cite news |last= Kayal |first= Michele |title= Mormons Spruce Up Their Aging Hawaiian Outpost |newspaper= [[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date= 2004-11-27 |url= http://www.nytimes.com/2004/11/27/national/27religion.html |accessdate= 2012-10-10 }}</ref>。

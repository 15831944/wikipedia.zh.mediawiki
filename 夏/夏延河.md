'''夏延河'''（{{lang-en|Cheyenne River}}<ref>{{Cite book|title=New Lakota Dictionary|publisher=Lakota Language Consortium|year=2011|isbn=978-0-9761082-9-0|editor-last=Ullrich|editor-first=Jan|edition=2nd|location=Bloomington, IN|pages=607, 626, 883|lccn=2008922508}}</ref>，或写作Chyone River<ref>{{Cite book|title=The life and papers of Frederick Bates|publisher=Missouri Historical Society|year=1923|editor-last=Marshall|editor-first=Thomas Maitland|location=St Louis MO}} vol 1, p 203 - 1807 ''Hunting Licenses Granted July 6  Primo and Bessonett  On the Chyone''</ref>）名字来自曾经生活在那里的[[夏延族|夏延族]]原住民<ref>{{Cite book|url=http://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015027015455;view=1up;seq=351|title=South Dakota place-names, v.3|last=[[Federal_Writers'_Project|Federal Writers' Project]]|publisher=University of South Dakota|year=1940|page=4}}</ref>，是[[密苏里河|密苏里河]]的一条支流，位于[[美国州份|美国]]的[[怀俄明州|怀俄明州]]和[[南达科他州|南达科他州]]，长约为475公里，流域面积约62 800平方公里<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://lowercheyenne.respec.com/Portals/6/Project%20Summary.pdf|title=Archived copy|accessdate=2010-11-14|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715174435/http://lowercheyenne.respec.com/Portals/6/Project%20Summary.pdf|archivedate=2011-07-15}} Cheyenne River Phase I TMDL Assessment</ref>，其中约60%的流域面积位于南达科他州。<ref name="hogan">{{Cite book|title=The Geography of South Dakota|last=Hogan|first=Edward Patrick|last2=Fouberg|first2=Erin Hogan|publisher=The Center for Western Studies – [[Augustana_College_(South_Dakota)|Augustana College]]|year=2001|isbn=0-931170-79-6|edition=Third|location=Sioux Falls, SD}}</ref>

'''夏威夷科納聖殿'''（Kona Hawaii Temple）是第70座[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的聖殿，位於夏威夷島科納，也是夏威夷的第二座耶穌基督後期聖徒教會聖殿和太平洋群島的第六座耶穌基督後期聖徒教會聖殿。夏威夷科納聖殿在1998年5月7日宣布建立<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.churchnewsarchive.com/articles/31405/Locations-for-six-of-30-small-temples-given.html |title= Locations for six of 30 small temples given |date= May 23, 1998 |newspaper= [[Church_News|Church News]] }}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url= http://www.churchnewsarchive.com/articles/35271/The-announcements-of-new-holy-edifices-bring-joy-and-tears.html |title= The announcements of new holy edifices bring joy and tears |first= Janet |last= Kruckenberg |date= Feb 20, 1999 |newspaper= [[Church_News|Church News]] }}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>，在1999年開工<ref name=dedication>{{citation |url= http://www.lds.org/ensign/2000/04/news-of-the-church/two-new-temples-dedicated-president-hinckley-visits-saints-in-pacific |title= Two New Temples Dedicated, President Hinckley Visits Saints in Pacific |date=April 2000 |journal= [[Ensign_(LDS_magazine)|Ensign]] |accessdate= 2012-09-21 }}</ref>。

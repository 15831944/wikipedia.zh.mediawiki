'''夏洛特·麥金妮'''（{{lang-en|'''Charlotte McKinney'''}}，{{bd|1993年|8月6日}}）是一名美國女[[模特兒|模特兒]]和[[演員|演員]]。

==早期生活==
麥金妮出生於[[奧蘭多_(佛羅里達州)|奧蘭多]]<ref name=nypost>{{cite web|url=http://nypost.com/2015/01/31/charlotte-mckinney-dishes-on-her-racy-super-bowl-ad/|title=Charlotte McKinney is the real star of the Super Bowl|first=Gregory E.|last=Miller|work=[[New_York_Post|New York Post]]|date=2015-01-31|accessdate=2015-03-04}}</ref>。她有一個名為Garland的姊姊<ref name=dailymail/>。麥金妮有著[[失讀症|失讀症]]的困擾，而使自己在學校時常受欺負<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2936571/Super-Bowl-star-Charlotte-McKinney-reveals-mean-girls-battle-dyslexia-drove-quit-high-school.html|title=Super Bowl 2015 Carls Jr star Charlotte McKinney reveals how mean girls drove her to quit school - Daily Mail Online|work=Mail Online|date=2015-02-02|accessdate=2015-03-04}}</ref>。她就讀了[[威廉·布恩高中|威廉·布恩高中]]<ref name=dailymail>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2936571/Super-Bowl-star-Charlotte-McKinney-reveals-mean-girls-battle-dyslexia-drove-quit-high-school.html|title=Sexy Super Bowl star Charlotte McKinney reveals how mean girls and a battle with dyslexia drove her to quit high school|work=Mail Online|date=2015-02-02|accessdate=2015-03-04}}</ref>。

==職業生涯==
於17歲高中輟學後<ref name=dailymail/><ref name=Hyman>{{cite web|last1=Hyman|first1=Dan|title=The Women We Love of Instagram: Charlotte McKinney|url=http://www.esquire.com/lifestyle/sex/a28502/charlotte-mckinney-instagram/|website=Esquire|accessdate=2015-03-04}}</ref>，她形容自己是「有著大胸部為特徵的體態」<ref name=Hyman/>，並努力追求自己的模特兒生涯。她靠加入[[Instagram|Instagram]]而使自己獲得廣大的知名度<ref name=Falzone>{{cite web|last1=Falzone|first1=Diana|title=Charlotte McKinney: Guess model respects Kate Upton's career, feels bad about nude photo hack|url=http://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/2014/09/09/charlotte-mckinney-respects-kate-upton-business-acumen-feels-awful-about-nude/|website=Fox News|accessdate=2015-03-04}}</ref>。之後便開始接廣告和代言，其中包括[[Guess|Guess]]<ref name=Falzone/>。麥金妮與[[Wilhelmina_Models|Wilhelmina Models]]公司簽約成為職業模特兒<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.orlandosentinel.com/entertainment/tv/tv-guy/os-dancing-with-the-stars-orlando-charlotte-mckinney-20150224-post.html|title='Dancing': Orlando's Charlotte McKinney to compete|first=Hal |last=Boedeker|work=[[Orlando_Sentinel|Orlando Sentinel]] |date=2015-02-24|accessdate=2015-03-04}}</ref>。

麥金妮較知名的是於2015年1月在[[第四十九屆超級盃|第四十九屆超級盃]]上釋出的[[卡樂星|卡樂星]]全天然漢堡廣告，並在網上以[[病毒式行銷|病毒式]]地流傳<ref>[http://www.people.com/article/charlotte-mckinney-dad-loves-carls-jr-commercial Charlotte McKinney's Dad 'Friggin' Loved' Her Carl's Jr. Super Bowl Commercial] ''[[People_(magazine)|People]]'' (January 29, 2015)</ref>。使她被稱為「下一個[[凱特·阿普頓|凱特·阿普頓]]」，引起了全國各地的關注<ref name=Falzone/><ref name="apoloformen">{{cite web|url= http://www.apoloformen.com/charlotte-mckinney-photos/|title= 50 hottest photos of Charlotte Mckinney |publisher=apoloformen.com|accessdate=2016-03-15}}</ref>。同年，麥金妮也於電影《[[傻喬歷險記2|傻喬歷險記2]]》中參演。

'''胞質融合'''（{{lang|en|Plasmogamy}}）是[[真菌|真菌]][[有性生殖|有性生殖]]的一個過程，兩株真菌[[菌絲體|菌絲體]]菌絲的[[細胞質|細胞質]]發生融合，但[[細胞核|細胞核]]尚未融合，使融合後的細胞中同時具有兩個[[染色體倍性|單套]]的細胞核。胞質融合的下一步驟為{{tsl|en|karyogamy|核融合 (真菌)|核融合}}，即兩細胞核融合為一個兩倍體的細胞核，並進行[[減數分裂|減數分裂]]產生[[孢子|孢子]]<ref name="Britannica">{{Cite encyclopedia | title = fungus (biology) :: Sexual reproduction | encyclopedia = Britannica | url = http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/222357/fungus/57957/Sexual-reproduction | accessdate = 20 April 2015 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150429134201/http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/222357/fungus/57957/Sexual-reproduction# | archive-date = 2015-04-29 | dead-url = no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Abedon|first=Stephen|title=Plasmogamy|url=http://www.biologyaspoetry.com/terms/plasmogamy.html|work=Biology as Poetry|accessdate=25 October 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304001449/http://www.biologyaspoetry.com/terms/plasmogamy.html#|archive-date=2016-03-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>。在[[子囊菌門|子囊菌門]]與[[擔子菌門|擔子菌門]]等高等真菌中，胞質融合與核融合中間間隔的時間很長，期間真菌細胞維持異質雙核的狀態，稱為{{link-en|雙核體|dikaryon}}，在低等真菌中，胞質融合後核融合則通常會立刻發生<ref name="Britannica" />。

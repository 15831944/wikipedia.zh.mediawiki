'''窩咸森林足球會'''（'''Waltham Forest Football Club'''），是一間來自[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[沃爾瑟姆森林區|沃爾瑟姆森林區]]的球會，在2013年7月宣布經過[[伊爾福德|伊爾福德]]的五年後返回自治市鎮，並在窩咸旅館運動場的前地獲得租約，他們在{{link-en|艾塞克斯郡高級聯賽|Essex Senior Football League}}的第一個賽季拿到賽季的第11名，現時在{{link-en|艾塞克斯郡高級聯賽|Essex Senior Football League}}角逐。<ref>[https://www.thenonleaguefootballpaper.com/latest-news/isthmian-league-step-three/7258/the-stags-return-home-after-five-year-exile/ The Stags Return Home After Five-Year Exile] Steven Coney, Bostik Leagues 30th July 2013</ref><ref>[http://walthamforestecho.co.uk/waltham-forests-local-football-club/ Waltham Forest’s Local Football Club] 4TH NOVEMBER 2014 walthamforestecho</ref><ref>[http://www.yellowad.co.uk/article.cfm?id=128723&headline=Waltham%20Forest%20turn%20to%20crowdfunding%20to%20save%20cash%20strapped%20club&sectionIs=sport&searchyear=2017 Waltham Forest turn to crowdfunding to save cash strapped club] yellowad 13 November 2017</ref>

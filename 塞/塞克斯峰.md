'''塞克斯峰'''（{{lang-en|Sex Peak}}）為座在[[美国|美国]][[蒙大拿州|蒙大拿州]][[桑德斯縣_(蒙大拿州)|桑德斯縣]]的山峰<ref>{{GNIS|790254}}</ref>，坐落在{{le|庫特奈國家森林|Kootenai National Forest}}內<ref>{{cite web | url=http://visitmt.com/listing/categories_NET/MoreInfo.aspx?IDRRecordId=6320&SiteId=1 | title=Sex Peak Lookout | publisher=Montana Office of Tourism | accessdate=2014-11-18}}</ref>。此座山峰海拔高度{{convert|5751|ft}}，為蒙大拿州第2,052高峰<ref>{{cite web | url=http://peakery.com/sex-peak-montana/ | title=Sex Peak | publisher=Peakery.com | accessdate=2014-11-18}}</ref>。

據稱公園官員和林務員談論有關[[性交|性交]]的話題，因而將此山峰命名為「Sex Peak」<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.recreation.gov/camping/sex-peak-lookout-rental/r/campgroundDetails.do?contractCode=NRSO&parkId=75300 | title=Sex Peak Lookout Rental, MT | publisher=Recreation.gov | accessdate=2014-11-18}}</ref><ref name="USFS" />。

位在塞克斯峰最高處的塞克斯峰瞭望站（Sex Peak Lookout Station）為座已退役的{{le|火警瞭望塔|fire lookout tower}}<ref name="USFS">{{cite web | url=http://www.fs.usda.gov/recarea/kootenai/recreation/hiking/recarea/?recid=49901&actid=101 | title=Sex Peak Lookout | publisher=[[United_States_Forest_Service|United States Forest Service]] | accessdate=2014-11-18}}</ref><ref>{{GNIS|794354|name=Sex Peak Lookout Station}}</ref>。

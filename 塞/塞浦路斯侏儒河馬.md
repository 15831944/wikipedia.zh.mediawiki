'''塞浦路斯侏儒河馬'''（''Hippopotamus minor''）是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[河馬|河馬]]，在[[全新世|全新世]]早期前生存在[[塞浦路斯|塞浦路斯]]。

塞浦路斯侏儒河馬重約200公斤，與現存的[[倭河馬|倭河馬]]差不多大小。但是牠們縮小的原因相信是[[島嶼侏儒化|島嶼侏儒化]]，因細小的環境而限制了[[基因|基因]]的發展。估計牠們高約76厘米及長121厘米。<ref>{{cite news en | first=Menelaos | last=Hadjicostis | title=Dwarf Hippo Fossils Found on Cyprus | date=Thursday, 06 Dec 2007 | publisher=The Associated Press | url =http://www.ctv.ca/servlet/ArticleNews/story/CTVNews/20071206/hippo_fossil_071206/20071206 | work =Fox Television | accessdate = 2007-12-06}}</ref>

塞浦路斯侏儒河馬於11000-9000年前期間滅絕，於當時是塞浦路斯上最大型的動物。牠們是[[草食性|草食性]]的，並沒有天敵存在。<ref name="PNAS on Dwards">{{Cite book en|author=Burness, Gary P. and Jared Diamond, and Timothy Flannery|title=Dinosaurs, dragons, and dwarfs: The evolution of maximal body size|publisher=Proc Natl Acad Sci U S A|date=2001 Nov 27|url=http://www.pnas.org/cgi/reprint/251548698v1.pdf}}</ref>

在塞浦路斯的發掘位點提供了證據，顯示塞浦路斯侏儒河馬可能是因在塞浦路斯的早期[[人類|人類]]而滅絕的。<ref name="ASOR on Cyprus">{{Cite book en|title=The earliest prehistory of Cyprus from colonization to exploitation |author=Swiny, Stuart |publisher=American Schools of Oriental Research |year=2001 |url=http://www.asor.org/pubs/swiny.pdf |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070202102124/http://www.asor.org/pubs/swiny.pdf |archivedate=2007-02-02 }}</ref>

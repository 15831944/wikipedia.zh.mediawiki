'''塞勒姆的血統'''（{{lang-en|Bloodlines of Salem}}）是一個位於[[美國|美國]][[鹽湖城|鹽湖城]]的{{Link-en|家族史協會|Family history society|家族史團體}}，縮寫為'''BLoS'''。它所宣揚的宗旨是提供一個「參訪者分享對於1692年[[塞勒姆審巫案|塞勒姆審巫案]]，其[[塞勒姆審巫案涉案人員列表|參與者]]及他們的家人的想法和資訊的地方。許多參訪者已經研究並證明出他們的家系與一位或更多的塞勒姆審巫案關係人有關。這些審判在超過三個世紀前展開，且一直在[[歷史|歷史]]、[[法律|法律]]和[[宗教|宗教]]的研究領域中顯著突出。然而，無論他們是一位業餘研究者、職業研究者或『塞勒姆學家』，他們對審巫案的研究都不會受限於他們自己的血統。」<ref name="Richard">{{Cite news |title= Bloodlines of Salem |url= http://www.internet-genealogy.com/ |work= Internet Genealogy; Net Notes Section, p. 6 |publisher= Moorshead Magazines Ltd. |location= Niagara Falls, N.Y. |date= August–September 2008 |accessdate= 2008-08-03}}</ref><ref name="Kerstens">{{Cite news |title= Digital Genealogist |url= http://www.digitalgenealogist.com/pubs/08dgmayjun.pdf |work= Digital Genealogist; vol. 2, no. 3, News Briefs Section, p. 4 |publisher= Digital Genealogist LLC |location= Plymouth, Mich. |date= May–June 2008 |accessdate= 2008-08-02 |deadurl= yes |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20081119232130/http://www.digitalgenealogist.com/pubs/08dgmayjun.pdf |archivedate= 19 November 2008}}</ref><ref name="Eastman">{{Cite news |title= Bloodlines of Salem |url= http://blog.eogn.com/eastmans_online_genealogy/2008/04/bloodlines-of-s.html |work= Eastman's Online Genealogy Newsletter |publisher= Richard W. == 歷史 ==
該團體於2007年在[[鹽湖城|鹽湖城]]成立。鹽湖城有著世界級的[[家譜圖書館|家譜圖書館]]，鼓勵並提供資料給塞勒姆審巫案關係人的後代與親屬，或懷疑自己是後代親屬的人，又或者是單純著迷於這場審巫案的人們。<ref name="Richard"/><ref name="Mysterious">{{Cite news |title= Bloodlines of Salem |url= http://www.mysterioussocieties.com/2009/03/bloodlines-of-salem.html |work= Mysterious Societies |publisher= Mysterious Societies |location=  |date= 10 March 2009 |accessdate= 2009-03-25 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20160124175808/http://www.mysterioussocieties.com/2009/03/bloodlines-of-salem.html |archive-date= 2016-01-24 |dead-url= yes }}</ref><ref name="Wagner">{{Cite news |title= Salem Witch Trial history |url= http://www.reunionsmag.com/familyreunions/family_history_genealogy2.html |work= Reunions Magazine |publisher= Reunions Magazine Inc. |location= Milwaukee, Wisc. |date= 1 September 2008 |accessdate= 2009-03-25}}</ref><ref name="Kuebler">{{Cite news |title= Roadkill from the info highway |url= http://www.rue-morgue.com/horror_101_roadkill.php |work= Rue Morgue |publisher= Marrs Media Inc. |location= Toronto |date= June 2008 |accessdate= 2008-09-06 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20160303181402/http://www.rue-morgue.com/horror_101_roadkill.php |archive-date= 2016-03-03 |dead-url= yes }}</ref><ref name="Cleveland">{{Cite news|title=Family-history group welcomes Salem Witch Trial descendants: Descendents of Framingham's Salem's End first settlers invited |url=http://www.framingham.com/news/history/family_history_group_welcomes_salem_witch_trial_descendants.htm |work=Framingham (Mass.) == 活動 ==
該團體的研究人員調查並發表了部分名人身為塞勒姆審巫案關係人的後代與親屬的證據。在2007年的調查中揭露了曾參與《[[哈利波特|哈利波特]]》電影系列演出的演員[[湯姆·費爾頓|湯姆·費爾頓]]和當時的犧牲者之一{{Link-en|約翰·普羅克特|John Proctor (Salem witch trials)}}有著親屬關係<ref name="Whitaker">{{Cite news |title= Exclusive: 'Draco Malfoy' is a witch: Harry Potter villain Draco Malfoy is related to real-life witches |url=http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/news/238447/Draco-Malfoy-is-a-witch.html |work= The (London) Sun |publisher= News Group Newspapers Ltd. |location= London |date= 22 June 2007 |accessdate= 2007-06-22 |first= Thomas |last= Whitaker}}</ref>。2008年的調查則發現，[[美國總統|美國總統]][[喬治·沃克·布希|喬治·沃克·布希]]是多達217名關係人的親屬，其中包括了他的10世祖[[瑪麗·帕克|瑪麗·帕克]]。<ref name="Dowbenko">{{Cite news |title= Bush's Satanic & Witchcraft Bloodlines |url= http://www.conspiracyplanet.com/channel.cfm?channelid=44&contentid=5449 |work= ConspiracyPlanet.com |publisher= Conspiracy Planet |location= Pray, Mont. |date= 3 October 2008 |accessdate= 2008-10-03}}</ref>

'''塞納公司'''（Cerner Corporation） 是一家美国[[卫生信息技术|卫生信息技术]]公司，提供卫生信息技术解决方案、服务以及设备。<ref name="Q4 2017 Results">{{cite web|last1=Smith|first1=Dan|title=Cerner Reports Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2017 Results|url=https://globenewswire.com/news-release/2018/02/06/1333947/0/en/Cerner-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2017-Results.html|website=globenewswire.com/|publisher=GlobeNewswire|accessdate=12 February 2018}}</ref>

该公司成立1979年，称“PGI & Associates”，1984年改现名<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cerner.com/public/Cerner_2.asp?id=27167 |title=Cerner Corporation: Cerner Timeline |publisher=Cerner.com |date= |accessdate=2010-03-16 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100103222117/http://cerner.com/public/Cerner_2.asp?id=27167 |archivedate=2010-01-03 |df= }}</ref>，1986年上市<ref>[http://www.fundinguniverse.com/company-histories/Cerner-Corporation-Company-History.html "International Directory of Company Histories"], Vol. 16. St. James Press, 1997</ref>，随后规模逐渐扩大<ref name="fundinguniverse.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.fundinguniverse.com/company-histories/cerner-corporation-history/|title=Cerner Corporation History}}</ref>。

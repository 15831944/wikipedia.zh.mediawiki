'''塞尼卡湖怪兽'''（英文：Seneca Lake Monster）是指出没于[[美国|美国]][[纽约州|纽约州]]{{link-en|塞尼卡湖|Seneca Lake (New York)}}的[[神秘生物列表|神秘动物]]，其历史可追溯至19世纪。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://senecalakemonster.wordpress.com/the-seneca-lake-serpent/|title=What Is The Seneca Lake Monster?|date=2015-05-16|newspaper=Seneca Lake Monster|accessdate=2018-07-24|language=en-US}}</ref>1900年7月14日，[[蒸汽船|蒸汽船]]Otetiani号的乘客目击一只神秘动物，据船长的观察，这只动物25英尺。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://mysteriousuniverse.org/2016/04/exploring-american-monsters-new-york/|title=Exploring American Monsters: New York {{!}} Mysterious Universe|accessdate=2018-07-24|work=Mysterious Universe|language=en-US}}</ref>据《{{link-en|星期日电讯报|The Sunday Telegraph}}》的报道，这只动物之后受到船只撞击而死亡。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.fltimes.com/news/geneva-night-out-founder-to-city-council-protect-lake-monster/article_d9a6f198-0b85-11e5-b511-1be208771e3d.html|title=Geneva Night Out founder to City Council: Protect ‘lake monster’|last=dshaw@fltimes.com|first=DAVID L. SHAW|newspaper=Finger Lakes Times|accessdate=2018-07-24|language=en}}</ref>据报道，[[地质学|地质学家]]George R. Elwood教授也是目击者之一，他详细记录了怪物的信息：尸体类似[[鱘科|鲟鱼]]、两排三角形白色牙齿、总长25英尺、头部长4英尺且呈三角形、重约1000磅。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://phantomsandmonsters.wikifoundry.com/page/Monsters+and+Legends+-+Seneca+Lake,+NY|title=Monsters and Legends - Seneca Lake, NY - The Social Paranormal Network|accessdate=2018-07-24|work=phantomsandmonsters.wikifoundry.com}}</ref>在此之后，塞尼卡湖附近的居民相继看见了类似巨型{{link-en|鲤鱼|Carp}}或[[海豚|海豚]]的神秘动物在湖中游动。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://hws.edu/alumni/pssurvey/summer13/sea_monster.aspx|title=The Pulteney St. Survey|accessdate=2018-07-24|work=hws.edu|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180728002915/http://hws.edu/alumni/pssurvey/summer13/sea_monster.aspx|archive-date=2018-07-28|dead-url=yes}}</ref>2015年8月5日，[[傑尼瓦市_(紐約州)|杰尼瓦市]]以6比1的投票结果修改城市法第206章-狩猎与[[狩獵陷阱|诱捕]]，禁止任何人伤害塞尼卡湖怪兽及其后代。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://senecalakemonster.wordpress.com/|title=Seneca Lake Monster|accessdate=2018-07-24|work=Seneca Lake Monster|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.fltimes.com/news/city-council-oks-linden-street-closure-plan/article_4ee60174-3c46-11e5-ae2b-3726beb520c2.html|title=City Council OKs Linden Street closure plan|last=dshaw@fltimes.com|first=DAVID L. SHAW|newspaper=Finger Lakes Times|accessdate=2018-07-24|language=en}}</ref>

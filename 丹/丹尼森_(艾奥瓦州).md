'''丹尼森'''（Denison）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[克劳福德县_(爱阿华州)|克劳福德县]]的城市和县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web|url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=2011-06-07 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531210815/http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=May 31, 2011 }}</ref>

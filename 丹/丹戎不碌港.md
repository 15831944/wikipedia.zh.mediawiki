'''丹戎不碌港'''為[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]]吞吐量最大及最先進的[[港口|港口]]，<ref>{{cite web|title=The 13,466-island problem|url=http://www.economist.com/news/special-report/21693404-after-decades-underinvestment-infrastructure-spending-picking-up-last|website=The Economist|accessdate=16 June 2017}}</ref>處理印度尼西亚船舶運輸總量50%以上。位於{{tsl|en|North Jakarta|北雅加達}}{{tsl|en|Tanjung Priok|丹戎不碌}}，由印度尼西亞國有企業{{tsl|en|Indonesia Port Corporations||PT Pelindo II}}營運。貨櫃碼頭被稱為'''雅加達國際貨櫃碼頭'''('''JICT''')，由{{tsl|en|Hutchison Port Holdings|和記港口集團}}及{{tsl|en|Indonesia Port Corporations||Pelindo II}}共同營運，為[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]]最大的[[貨櫃碼頭|貨櫃碼頭]]。<ref>[http://www.worldportsource.com/ports/IDN_Port_of_Jakarta_266.php Port Commerce]</ref><ref>[http://www.hph.hk/business/ports/asia/jakarta.htm Hutchinson Port Holdings] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20070708023136/http://www.hph.hk/business/ports/asia/jakarta.htm |date=2007-07-08 }}</ref>

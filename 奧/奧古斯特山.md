'''奧古斯特山'''（Mount Augustus）是位於[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]][[西澳大利亞州|西澳大利亞州]]的一座[[山峰|山峰]]。奧古斯特山的海拔高度是高於海平面{{convert|1106|m|ft}}，高於附近地面{{convert|860|m|ft}}。奧古斯塔山的底面積達4795公頃，有時被宣傳為世界最大的單體岩石<ref>http://www.mtaugustustouristpark.com/general-information.html</ref><ref>{{Citation | author1=Tassell, Dave | title=Augustus: ancient giant of the west | journal=Australian Outdoors. Yearbook | publication-date=1978 | issue=1978 | pages=114–117 | url=http://trove.nla.gov.au/work/39258631 | accessdate=11 January 2014 }}</ref>，但並沒有學術依據<ref>{{Citation | author1=Bourman, Robert P | author2=Ollier, Cliff | author3=Buckman, Solomon, Dr | title=Mount Augustus geology and geomorphology | publication-date=2010-01-01 | publisher=Research Online | url=http://trove.nla.gov.au/work/73698414 | accessdate=11 January 2014 }} - notably in the abstract: ''Mount Augustus has often been compared with Ayers Rock, and unwarranted claims have been made that it is the biggest rock in the world and the world's biggest monolith.'' and ''Other rock types present on Mount Augustus include metamorphic and igneous rocks, which preclude it from being termed a monolith.'' see abstract - [http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1745-5871.2009.00608.x/abstract;jsessionid=16E8EC24BE6383BF75C5D6793A04711B.f02t02]</ref>。

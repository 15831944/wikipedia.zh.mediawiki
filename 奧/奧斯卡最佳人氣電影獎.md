'''奥斯卡最佳人氣電影獎'''（{{lang-en|'''Academy Award for Outstanding Achievement in Popular Film'''}}，另稱為{{lang-en|''' Academy Award for Best Popular Film'''}}），是於2018年8月8日設立的[[奧斯卡金像獎|奧斯卡金像獎]]獎項<ref>{{cite web|last=Feinberg|first=Scott|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/race/academy-plans-three-hour-oscars-telecast-adds-popular-film-category-1133138|title=Oscars Won't Televise All Awards, Adds Popular Film Category|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|date=2018-08-08|accessdate=2018-10-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Lang|first1=Brent|last2=Rubin|first2=Rebecca|url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/academy-sets-three-hour-broadcast-adds-popular-film-category-1202899382/|title=Academy Adds Popular Film Category, Sets Three-Hour Oscar Broadcast|work=Variety|date=2018-08-08|accessdate=2018-10-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Travis|first=Ben|url=https://www.empireonline.com/movies/news/oscars-creating-popular-film-category-academy-awards-reform/|title=Oscars Creating 'Popular Film' Category In Academy Awards Reform|work=Empire|date=2018-08-08|accessdate=2018-10-22}}</ref>。

== 歷史 ==
繼[[第74届奥斯卡金像奖|2001年]]設立[[奧斯卡最佳動畫片獎|最佳動畫片獎]]後，所新增加的獎項類別<ref>{{cite web|last=Lee|first=Benjamin|url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2018/aug/08/oscars-film-academy-adds-first-new-award-category-since-2001|title=Oscars: Academy to add achievement in popular film category|work=The Guardian|date=2018-08-08|accessdate=2018-10-22}}</ref>。獲得該獎項提名的電影，也具資格獲得[[奥斯卡最佳影片奖|最佳影片奖]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.indiewire.com/2018/08/oscars-best-popular-film-can-compete-best-picture-1201992460/|title=Oscars: The Academy Confirms Movies Eligible for Best Popular Film Can Also Compete for Best Picture|last=Sharf|first=Zack|date=2018-08-08|work=IndieWire|access-date=2018-08-14|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://star.ettoday.net/news/1231136|title=奧斯卡向收視率低頭！ 新增「人氣流行電影獎」|accessdate=2018-08-09|author=許瑞麟|publisher=東森新聞|date=2018-12-12|language=zh-hant}}</ref>。

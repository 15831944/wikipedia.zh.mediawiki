'''奧莉伊·卡拉瓦爾侯'''（{{lang-en|'''Auli'i Cravalho'''}}，{{bd|2000年|11月22日|||catIdx=C}}）是一位[[美國|美國]]女演員和歌手。她於2016年首度演出電影《[[海洋奇緣|海洋奇緣]]》。

== 早期生活 ==
卡拉瓦爾侯生於[[夏威夷州|夏威夷]]{{link-en|科哈拉 (夏威夷)|Kohala, Hawaii|科哈拉}}<ref name="Daniel" />，她擁有中國<ref>{{cite web|last1=Wallace|first1=Don|script-title=en:"Moana" Star Auli‘i Cravalho is Not Your Average Disney Princess|url=http://www.honolulumagazine.com/Honolulu-Magazine/November-2016/Moanas-Aulii-Cravalho-is-Not-Your-Average-Disney-Princess/|publisher=Honolulu Magazine|accessdate=2017-12-17}}</ref>、愛爾蘭<ref>{{cite tweet |user=auliicravalho|number=822964481407881216|date=2017-01-21|title=Yes indeed! I've got the luck of the Irish}}</ref>、夏威夷族、葡萄牙和波多黎各的血統<ref name="Wang">{{cite news|last1=Wang|first1=Frances Kai-Hwa|title=The Next Disney Princess is Native Hawaiian AuliCravalho|url=http://www.nbcnews.com/news/asian-america/next-disney-princess-native-hawaiian-aulii-cravalho-n440131|accessdate=2017-02-27|work=NBC News|publisher=NBCUniversal|date=2015-10-07|location=New York}}</ref>。

卡拉瓦爾侯曾與媽媽普娜妮·卡拉瓦爾侯居住在{{link-en|米利拉尼 (夏威夷)|Mililani, Hawaii|米利拉尼}}。

== 職業生涯 ==
卡拉瓦爾侯曾表示，她最初不打算去《[[海洋奇緣|海洋奇緣]]》的試鏡，因為她覺得「在[[YouTube|YouTube]]上已經有了很多很棒的提案」<ref name="Rice">Rice, Lynette (7 October 2015). [http://www.people.com/article/disney-princess-moana-aulii-cravalho "Meet the Next Disney Princess – and Get a First Look at Her Movie, Moana!"] ''[[People_(magazine)|People]]''. New York: Time Inc. Retrieved on 2016-06-14.</ref>。然而，一位[[歐胡島|歐胡島]]中介在一個慈善比賽中發現了卡拉瓦爾侯，他鼓勵卡拉瓦爾侯去面試角色<ref name="Wang" /><ref name="Rice" />。[[華特迪士尼公司|華特迪士尼公司]]曾表示，卡拉瓦爾侯是數百名女演員中最後一個參與面試的人<ref name="Wang" /><ref name="ledbetter">Ledbetter, Carly (2015-10-08). [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/disney-princess-moana-aulii-cravalho_us_56166c96e4b0dbb8000d3a3d "Meet Your New Disney Princess 'Moana', Played By 14-Year-Old Auli'i Cravalho"]. ''[[The_Huffington_Post|The Huffington Post]]''. Retrieved on 2016-06-14.</ref>。

2017年2月，宣布她將加入[[國家廣播公司|NBC]]電視劇《{{link-en|非凡戲劇團 (電視劇)|Rise (TV series)|非凡戲劇團}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/02/moana-star-aulii-cravalho-cast-drama-high-nbc-pilot-1202018615/|script-title=en:‘Moana’ Star Auli’i Cravalho Cast In NBC Pilot ‘Drama High’ From Jason Katims & Jeffrey Seller|first=Nellie|last=Andreeva|date=2017-02-27|publisher=|accessdate=2017-03-21}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2017/05/rise-for-god-and-country-picked-upseries-nbc-1202084736/|script-title=en:‘Rise’ & ‘For God And Country’ Picked Up To Series By NBC|last=Andreeva|first=Andreeva|work=Deadline Hollywood|date=2017-05-04|accessdate=2017-05-13}}</ref>。

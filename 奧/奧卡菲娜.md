'''{{PAGENAME}}'''（[[藝名|藝名]]：'''Awkwafina'''，本名：'''林家珍'''，{{lang-en|'''Nora Lum'''}}，{{bd|1988年|6月2日}}），美籍中韓混血兒，是知名的[[美國|美國]][[饒舌|饒舌歌手]]及[[女演員|女演員]]，較為人熟悉的作品包括電影《[[盜海豪情：8美千嬌|盜海豪情：8美千嬌]]》和《[[瘋狂亞洲富豪|瘋狂亞洲富豪]]》<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.instyle.tw/celebrity/article.php?i=2765|title=從餐廳服務生到演出好萊塢鉅片 奧卡菲娜Awkwafina實現星夢|publisher=InStyle|date=2018年8月17日|author=Yi Chang}}</ref>。

==早年生活==
奧卡菲娜在[[紐約市|紐約市]]出生<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pri.org/stories/2014-09-17/awkwafina-changing-hip-hop-one-vulgar-hilarious-lyric-time|title=Awkwafina is changing hip-hop one vulgar, hilarious lyric at a time|publisher=|accessdate=18 July 2018}}</ref>，在[[皇后區|皇后區]][[森林小丘社區|森林小丘]]長大，父親沃利（{{lang|en|Wally}}）是[[华裔美国人|华裔美国人]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://mobile.twitter.com/awkwafina/status/389151854724407296|title=Twitter|website=mobile.twitter.com|accessdate=18 July 2018}}</ref>，母親移民自[[韓裔美國人|韓國]]，是位畫家<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.albany.edu/journalism/assets/nlum.pdf|title=Nora Lum – Immigration Paper, University at Albany, SUNY|last=|first=|date=|website=www.albany.edu|publisher=|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|accessdate=25 October 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.nbcnews.com/news/asian-america/how-nora-lum-became-rapper-awkwafina-instead-meat-inspector-n188271|title=How Nora Lum Became Rapper Awkwafina Instead of A Meat Inspector|last1=Lee|first1=Traci|date=26 August 2014|work=NBC News|accessdate=26 August 2014|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|publisher=NBC UNIVERSAL}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.usmagazine.com/entertainment/news/5-things-to-know-about-oceans-eight-star-awkwafina-w465908/|title=5 Things to Know About 'Ocean's Eight' Star Awkwafina|date=25 February 2017|publisher=|accessdate=18 July 2018}}</ref>。她的[[曾祖父|曾祖父]]是1940年代來自中國的移民，在[[法拉盛|法拉盛]]經營林氏（{{lang|en|Lum's}}）粵菜餐廳<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.usmagazine.com/entertainment/news/5-things-to-know-about-oceans-eight-star-awkwafina-w465908/|title=5 Things to Know About 'Ocean's Eight' Star Awkwafina|date=2017-02-25|work=Us Weekly|access-date=2018-04-03|language=en-US}}</ref>。她的母親在她4歲時離世，她自小由祖母照顧，並受其深遠影響<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=http://iamkoream.com/september-cover-story-awkwafina-establishing-her-presence/|title=September Cover Story: Awkwafina Establishing Her Presence {{!}} KoreAm Journal|website=iamkoream.com|access-date=2016-05-01}}</ref>。

'''奧氏恐螯蝦'''（學名：''{{lang|la|Dinochelus ausubeli}}''）是'''恐螯蝦屬'''（''Dinochelus''）之下的唯一一個物種{{r|Davies|page1=90|Ahyong}}。本物種是一種細小的深海[[龍蝦|龍蝦]]，在2007年的一次{{link-en|國際海洋生物探測|Census of Marine Life}}時被發現{{r|Davies|page1=90|Ahyong}}，2010年被描述{{r|Ahyong}}。
elongated, and bear many long teeth on the inner surface.

==型態描述==
本物種的[[頭胸甲|頭胸甲]]長約{{convert|31|mm}}，終其一生其身體皆呈通透的白色，另外在其頭胸甲的中間、尾部、觸角及on the first [[pereiopod|pereiopod]]s (including the claws)均帶桃紅色<ref name="Ahyong"/>。左右兩邊的螫的體積差異頗大。

==分佈與發現==
本物種目前只知道存在於其[[模式產地|模式產地]]，即{{Coord|15|56|N|121|45|E}}，位於[[菲律賓|菲律賓]][[呂宋島|呂宋島]][[巴勒爾灣|巴勒爾灣]]海域{{r|Ahyong}}。
這個物種是在2007年的一次{{link-en|國際海洋生物探測|Census of Marine Life}}時，被[[拖網|拖網船]]於{{convert|250|m}}深的海底撈獲。這次深測被譽為「21世紀首個十年描述海洋生物的最重要發現」<ref>{{cite web |url=http://newswire.rockefeller.edu/2011/02/01/newly-discovered-deep-sea-lobster-named-for-rockefeller%E2%80%99s-jesse-ausubel/ |title=Newly discovered deep sea lobster named for Rockefeller's Jesse Ausubel |date=February 1, 2011 |work=Newswire |publisher=[[Rockefeller_University|Rockefeller University]] |access-date=2011-04-27 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120708014756/http://newswire.rockefeller.edu/2011/02/01/newly-discovered-deep-sea-lobster-named-for-rockefeller%E2%80%99s-jesse-ausubel/ |archivedate=2012年7月8日 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://eolspecies.lifedesks.org/pages/23791 |title=''Dinochelus ausubeli'' |author=Leo Shapiro |work=EOL Species Rapid Response (Lifedesk) |publisher=[[Encyclopedia_of_Life|Encyclopedia of Life]] & Lifedesk community |accessdate=27 April 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110815183045/http://eolspecies.lifedesks.org/pages/23791 |archivedate=15 August 2011 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2011/04/26/science/26ausubel.html?_r=1&nl=todaysheadlines&emc=tha210|title=A passion for nature, and really long lists |author=Nicholas Wade |date=25 April 2011 |work=Science - Scientist at Work |publisher=[[New_York_Times|New York Times]] |accessdate=4 April 2011}}</ref>。這個物種其後於2010年正式為海洋生物學家所描述{{r|Ahyong}}。

==分類學==
The new species was placed in a separate genus, ''Dinochelus'', whose name derives from the <!-- [[Ancient_Greek_language|Greek]] -->[[古希臘語|古希臘語]] roots {{lang|el|[[:wikt:δεινός|δεινός]]}} (''dinos''), meaning "terrible" or "fearful", and {{lang|el|[[:wikt:χηλή|χηλή]]}} (''chela''), meaning "<!-- [[claw|claw]] -->[[爪|爪]]".<ref name="Ahyong"/> The <!-- [[specific_name_(zoology)|specific epithet]] -->[[种加词|种加词]] "ausubeli" honours {{link-en|Jesse H. Ausubel}}, the sponsor of the Census of Marine Life, "in recognition of his vision and support for marine biodiversity exploration".<ref name="Ahyong"/>

恐螯蝦屬過往並不屬於海螯蝦科，而是被分類到同屬<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[海螯蝦總科|海螯蝦總科]]之下的<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[Thaumastochelidae|Thaumastochelidae]]科，而這個科之下還有 ''[[Thaumastocheles|Thaumastocheles]]'' 和 ''{{link-en|Thaumastochelopsis|Thaumastochelopsis}}''這兩個屬{{r|Ahyong}}。These genera all show very long, toothed claws with a bulbous base, with one claw much longer than the other.<ref name="Ahyong"/>。本物種在型態上與上述兩個屬相同，但當使用[[细胞色素c氧化酶|细胞色素c氧化酶]]對[[DNA測序|DNA測序]]時，本物種的結果顯示與這兩個屬只是[[旁系群|旁系群]]關係，未至於同屬一個科{{r|Ahyong}}。It is distinguished from either genus by the unusual, T-shaped form of the {{link-en|epistome|epistome}}.<ref name="Ahyong"/>

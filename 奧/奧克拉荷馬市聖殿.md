'''奧克拉荷馬市聖殿'''（Oklahoma City Oklahoma Temple）是第95座[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的聖殿，管理奧克拉荷馬州、阿肯色州、堪薩斯州和密蘇里州的耶穌基督後期聖徒教會。奧克拉荷馬市聖殿於1999年在[[育空_(奧克拉荷馬州)|奧克拉荷馬州]]開工<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/33465/Ground-broken-for-first-temple-in-Oklahoma.html |title= Ground broken for first temple in Oklahoma |date= July 10, 1999 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref>，2000年竣工<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/38254/Oklahoma-City-Oklahoma-A-sacred-and-beautiful-structure.html |title= Oklahoma City Oklahoma: 'A sacred and beautiful structure' |date= Aug 5, 2000 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref>，面積{{convert|10769|sqft|m2}}<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.ldschurchnewsarchive.com/articles/38238/Oklahoma-City-Oklahoma-Temple-facts-and-figures.html |title= Oklahoma City Oklahoma Temple facts and figures |date= Aug 5, 2000 |newspaper= Church News }}</ref>。

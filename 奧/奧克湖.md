'''奧克湖'''（Loch Oich）是[[蘇格蘭高地|蘇格蘭高地]]的一個淡水湖，也是[[卡利多尼安運河|卡利多尼安運河]]的一部分和最高點<ref name=Gazetteer>{{cite web |title=The Gazetteer for Scotland |url=http://www.scottish-places.info/features/featurefirst2404.html |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100917165355/http://www.scottish-places.info/features/featurefirst2404.html |archivedate=2010-09-17 }}</ref>，地處[[大峽谷_(蘇格蘭)|大峽谷]]，位於[[尼斯湖|尼斯湖]]和[[洛其湖|洛其湖]]之間<ref>{{cite web |title=Bathymetrical Survey of the Fresh-Water Lochs of Scotland, 1897-1909 |url=http://www.nls.uk/maps/early/bathymetric/main_info.html  |publisher=[[National_Library_of_Scotland|National Library of Scotland]] |archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20070208122134/http://www.nls.uk/maps/early/bathymetric/main_info.html |archivedate = 2007-02-08}}</ref>。奧克湖是一個淡水湖，擁有豐富的生態系統。

'''奧利弗·丁利'''（{{lang-en|'''Oliver Dingley'''}}，{{bd|1992年|11月24日}}）為[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]]跳水運動員。

==生涯==
2016年2月，丁利獲得[[2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會|2016年夏季奧林匹克運動會]]參賽資格，為1948年來第一位取得奧運參賽資格的愛爾蘭跳水運動員<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.irishtimes.com/sport/other-sports/oliver-dingley-makes-irish-diving-history-and-qualifies-for-rio-1.2543306|title=Oliver Dingley makes Irish diving history and qualifies for Rio: Ireland has not been represented in diving at the Olympic games since 1948 in London|work=The Irish Times|date=21 February 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.rte.ie/sport/swimming/2016/0221/769769-oliver-dingley/|title=Oliver Dingley 'humbled' after booking place at Rio|work=RTÉ Sport|date=21 February 2016}}</ref><ref name=independent_21022016>{{cite news|url=http://www.independent.ie/sport/other-sports/oliver-dingley-becomes-first-irish-diver-to-reach-olympics-in-68-years-34472056.html|title=Oliver Dingley becomes first Irish diver to reach Olympics in 68 years|newspaper=Irish Independent|date=21 February 2016}}</ref>。丁利也成為第一位闖進奧運決賽的愛爾蘭跳水運動員，他最終在男子個人3公尺跳板項目排名第8<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.rte.ie/sport/olympics/2016/0816/809712-oliver-dingley-diving-3m-springboard/|title=Oliver Dingley dives into Rio 2016 3m springboard final|work=RTÉ Sport|date=16 August 2016}}</ref>。

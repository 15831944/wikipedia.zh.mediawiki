'''兰博基尼Aventador LP 700-4'''是[[兰博基尼|兰博基尼]]生產的一款雙門雙座[[超級跑車|超級跑車]]，內部開發代號為LB834，首次公開亮相於2011年2月28日的[[日內瓦車展|日內瓦車展]]上，並在車展中受到熱烈的追捧。<ref>{{cite web|author=di Andrea Zuanni 17/11/2010|url=http://www.motori.it/tecnica/6998/lamborghini-tutti-i-dettagli-e-i-video-del-nuovo-v12.html |title=Lamborghini: tutti i dettagli e i video del nuovo V12 - Tecnica |publisher=Motori.it |date=17 November 2010 |accessdate=21 July 2011}}</ref>兰博基尼表示，自2011年起，Aventador將取代[[兰博基尼Murciélago|兰博基尼Murciélago]]原本的位置成為其新的旗艦跑車。Aventador的售價相當昂貴，兰博基尼為其設定的建議零售價為379,700美元。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://europeanmotornews.com/2011/03/01/the-lamborghini-aventador-lp-700-4/ |title=THE LAMBORGHINI AVENTADOR LP 700-4 | European Motor News |publisher=Europeanmotornews.com |date=16 April 2009 |accessdate=23 September 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111005190223/http://europeanmotornews.com/2011/03/01/the-lamborghini-aventador-lp-700-4/# |archive-date=2011-10-05 |dead-url=yes }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.drivingsupercars.com/home/?p=729 |title=Lamborghini Aventador LP 700-4. A new raging Bull | DrivingSupercars.com Driving supercars |publisher=Drivingsupercars.com |date=13 July 2011 |accessdate=16 September 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.botbnews.com/2011/03/lamborghini-aventador-lp700-4-finally-revealed-at-geneva/ |title=Lamborghini Aventador LP700-4 finally revealed at Geneva – update with video and live images « BOTB News |publisher=Botbnews.com |date= |accessdate=16 September 2011}}</ref>

和兰博基尼跑車的傳統命名法則一樣，Aventador這個名字也是來源於一頭獲得西班牙鬥牛賽獎杯的，擁有傳奇色彩的公牛。<ref name=autoblogindepth>{{cite web|url=http://www.autoblog.com/2011/02/28/2012-lamborghini-aventador-lp700-4-in-depth/ |title=2012 Lamborghini Aventador LP700-4: In Depth | publisher=Autoblog | date=28 February 2011 | accessdate=1 March 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://carscoop.blogspot.com/2011/03/lamborghini-aventador-lp700-4-video-and.html |title=Lamborghini Aventador LP700-4: Video and Mega Galleryx矿 with 115 Photos | publisher=Carscoop | date=2 March 2011 | accessdate=3 March 2011}}</ref>Aventador的產量將被限制在4000輛，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.autocar.co.uk/News/NewsArticle.aspx?AR=255814 |title=Lambo set for 4000 Aventadors |author= |date=10 March 2011 |work= |publisher=autocar.co.uk |accessdate=31 March 2011}}</ref>而曾經的兰博基尼Murciélago則總共生產了4099輛。兰博基尼官方聲稱Aventador比現在市面在售的兰博基尼跑車都要領先至少兩代，其使用了一級方程式賽車式的懸掛底盤，以及碳纖維材質的頂棚，<ref>Ingear Magazine 6 March 2011</ref> 全車外觀的線條設計更是大量借鑒了限量版的[[兰博基尼雷文頓|兰博基尼雷文頓]]以及[[兰博基尼Sesto_Elemento|兰博基尼Sesto Elemento]]概念車。

'''他'''（英语：'''Him'''），是为同性恋观众制作的色情电影。
==简介==
电影集中在一个年轻的同性恋者与耶稣基督的生活中发展色情关系。这部电影几乎无人所知，直到1979年，在书籍《金色土耳其奖》中出现，在书中被称为“最色情的色情学说。”<ref>{{cite book |title=The Golden Turkey Awards: Nominees and Winners, The Worst Achievements in Hollywood History |last=Medved |first=Harry |author2=Michael Medved |year=1980 |publisher=Perigree Book |location=NY |isbn= |page=122 }}</ref>
==遗失==
在书籍中，并没有找到该电影。它被在线杂志《Film Threat》称为最抢手的[[散失电影|散失电影]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.filmthreat.com/features/1899 |title=Film Threat's Top 10 Lost Films, Part 4 |author=Hall, Phil |date=March 1, 2007 |work= |publisher= |accessdate=16 November 2014 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150217005822/http://www.filmthreat.com/features/1899 |archivedate=February 17, 2015 }}</ref>  

部分网站认为该电影是个骗局，但是《Screw magazine》《Variety》和《The Village Voice》对该电影的评论被找到，<ref name=obscurity>{{cite web |url=http://obscurityandbeyond.blogspot.com/2010/02/lost-movie-detective-him-1974.html |title=Lost Movie Detective: Him |author=|date=February 11, 2010 |work=To Obscurity and Beyond... |publisher= |accessdate=3 March 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://mesmerize.proboards.com/index.cgi?board=entertainment&action=display&thread=2399 |title=HIM, Reviewed by Al Goldstein |author=Anderson, Billy A. |date=December 16, 2005 |work=Mesmerize |publisher= |accessdate=3 March 2010 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715114748/http://mesmerize.proboards.com/index.cgi?board=entertainment&action=display&thread=2399 |archivedate=July 15, 2011 }} ''quoting'' {{cite news |title=Dirty Diversions: Queen of the Jews: Christ's Second Coming |first=Al |last=Goldstein |newspaper=[[Screw_magazine|Screw]] |date=April 29, 1974 |at=21 }}</ref>几份报纸广告提及该电影在纽约戏剧播放。<ref name=obscurity/><ref>Essay on the film's alleged urban legend status [http://filmchatblog.blogspot.com/2005/05/gay-jesus-movie-hoax-or-fact.html]</ref>

这部电影可能是最近的遗失电影。

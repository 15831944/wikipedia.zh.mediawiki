[[File:Diariong_Tagalog.jpg|thumb]]《'''他加禄新闻报'''》（{{lang-tl|Diariong Tagalog}}）是第一份以[[他加禄语|他加禄语]]出版的报纸。[[马塞洛·德尔皮拉尔|马塞洛·德尔皮拉尔]]1882年创办于[[马尼拉|马尼拉]]。该报率先宣传反殖民思想，报道了许多西班牙公职人员滥用职权的丑闻。由于其高度的政治敏感性，自7月1日创刊起只发行了六个月就被西班牙殖民当局查禁，德尔皮拉尔也被迫流亡国外。<ref>{{cite book| editor1-last = Kintanar | editor1-first = Thelma B. | title = Cultural Dictionary for Filipinos | url = https://books.google.com.ph/books/about/Cultural_dictionary_for_Filipinos.html?id=H1AOAQAAMAAJ&redir_esc=y|  edition = Second | location = Lungsod Quezon | publisher = University of the Philippines Press | year = 2009 | language = Ingles}}</ref>[[黎剎|黎剎]]曾撰写一篇题为《爱国的热忱》（Amor Patrio）的文章，由德尔皮拉尔译成他加禄文发表于8月20日的该报。<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.philstar.com/freeman-opinion/309434/today-past | title = Today in the past | language = Ingles | date = Nobyembre 29, 2005 | accessdate = Setyembre 15, 2015 | publisher = [[The_Philippine_Star|The Philippine Star]] | last = Samson | first = Lucero }}</ref>该报关闭后，《向导》、《自由报》等一批他加禄文社会抗争报纸相继创立，最终诱发了1896年的菲律宾革命。<ref>Artemio R. Guillermo编. Historical Dictionary of the Philippines. 第三版. 2012. 页138.</ref>

'''詹姆斯·赫顿''' {{Post-nominals|post-noms=[[愛丁堡皇家學會|FRSE]]}}（{{lang-en|James Hutton}} {{IPAc-en|ˈ|h|ʌ|t|ən}}，1726年6月3日 [[西洋舊曆|OS]] <small>（1726年6月14日 [[西洋新曆|NS]]）</small> – 1797年3月26日)，[[苏格兰|苏格兰]][[地质学|地质学]]家、[[医生|医生]]、[[博物学|博物学]]家、[[化学|化学]]家、实验农场主。<ref name="Waterston">{{cite book |last1=Waterston |first1=Charles D |last2=Macmillan Shearer |first2=A
|title=Former Fellows of the Royal Society of Edinburgh 1783–2002: Biographical Index |url=http://www.royalsoced.org.uk/cms/files/fellows/biographical_index/fells_indexp1.pdf |volume=I |year=2006  |publisher=[[The_Royal_Society_of_Edinburgh|The Royal Society of Edinburgh]] |location=Edinburgh |isbn=978-0-902198-84-5}}</ref> 他的理论主要是关于地质学和地质时期，包括了[[火成论|火成说]]和[[均变论|均变论]]。赫頓的理論為現代地質學的發展奠定了基礎，因而常被稱為“現代地質學之父”。<ref name="MillennialPlaque">{{cite web |url=http://www.ed.ac.uk/explore/people/plaques/jameshutton.html |title=Millennial Plaques: James Hutton |author=[[University_of_Edinburgh|University of Edinburgh]] |publisher=(Hutton's [http://www.ed.ac.uk/explore/people/plaques/ Millennial Plaque] |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071101080843/http://www.ed.ac.uk/explore/people/plaques/jameshutton.html |archivedate=2007-11-01 }}</ref><ref name="Denby">{{cite web |url=http://www.newyorker.com/archive/2004/10/11/041011crat_atlarge |title=Northern Lights: How modern life emerged from eighteenth-century Edinburgh |author=David Denby |work=[[The_New_Yorker|The New Yorker]] |publisher=Review of [[James_Buchan|James Buchan]]'s ''Crowded With Genius (Capital of the Mind'' in the UK) |date=11 October 2004}}</ref>

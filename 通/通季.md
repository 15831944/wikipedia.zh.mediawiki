'''通季'''是[[南蘇丹|南蘇丹]]西北部的小鎮，由[[通治州|通治州]]負責管轄，是該州的首府。通季位於首都[[朱巴|朱巴]]西北約525公里<ref>[http://distancecalculator.globefeed.com/Sudan_Distance_Result.asp?fromplace=Tonj%20(Bahr%20al%20Ghazal%20Wilayat)%20&toplace=Juba%20()%20(Airport)%20&fromlat=7.2733333&tolat=4.872006&fromlng=28.6838889&tolng=31.601117 Nautical Distance Between Juba And Tonj With Map & Distance Marker]</ref>，平均海拔高度427米<ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/SU/32/Tonj.html Average Elevation of Tonj, South Sudan]</ref>，鎮上有機場設施，2010年人口約17,340<ref>[http://www.hereorthere.com/places/tonj/people Estimated Population In 2010]</ref>。

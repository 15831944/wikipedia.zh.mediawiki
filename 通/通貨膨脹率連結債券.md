'''通貨膨脹率連結債券'''，俗稱'''抗通膨債券'''，為[[本金|本金]]隨[[通貨膨脹率|通貨膨脹率]]調整之[[債券|債券]]。創設用意是避免債券[[報酬率|報酬率]]受[[通貨膨脹|通貨膨脹]]侵蝕之風險。史上第一個發行此類債券的例子為[[美國|美國]][[麻塞諸塞州|麻州]]海灣公司（Massachusetts Bay Company）於1780年發行。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nber.org/papers/w10183.pdf|title=The Invention of Inflation-Indexed Bonds in Early America|author=Shiller}}, "Both Principal and Interest to be paid in the then current Money of said STATE, in a greater or less SUM, according as Five Bushels of CORN, Sixty-eight Pounds and four-seventh Parts of a Pound of BEEF, Ten Pounds of SHEEPS WOOL, and Sixteen Pounds of SOLE LEATHER shall then cost, more or less than One Hundred and Thirty Pounds current money, at the then current Prices of said ARTICLES.</ref>目前多個政府皆有發行此類債券。

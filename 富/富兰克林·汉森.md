'''富兰克林·汉森'''（{{lang-en|Franklin Hansen}}，{{bd|1897年|5月2日|1982年|1月13日}}）为一位美国[[音讯工程师|音讯工程师]]。他因电影《{{tsl|en|A Farewell to Arms (1932 film)||告别武器}}》而赢得了[[奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖|奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖]]，<ref name="Oscars1934">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1934 |title=The 6th Academy Awards (1934) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=August 5, 2011|work=oscars.org}}</ref>并获得了4次提名。

==精选影视作品==
汉森曾赢得1次[[奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖|奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖]]，并获得4次提名。
获奖：
* ''{{tsl|en|A Farewell to Arms (1932 film)||告别武器}}'' (1932)

提名：
* ''{{tsl|en|The Love Parade||璇宫艳史}}'' (1930)<ref name="Oscars1929">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1931 |title=The 3rd Academy Awards (1929/30) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-08-05|work=oscars.org}}</ref>
* ''{{tsl|en|Cleopatra (1934 film)||埃及艳后}}'' (1934)<ref name="Oscars1935">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1935 |title=The 7th Academy Awards (1935) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-08-07|work=oscars.org}}</ref>
* ''{{tsl|en|The Lives of a Bengal Lancer (film)||傲世军魂}}'' (1935)<ref name="Oscars1936">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1936 |title=The 8th Academy Awards (1935) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-08-07|work=oscars.org}}</ref>
* ''{{tsl|en|The Texas Rangers (1936 film)||德州游侠}}'' (1936)<ref name="Oscars1937">{{Cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/oscars/ceremonies/1937 |title=The 9th Academy Awards (1936) Nominees and Winners |accessdate=2011-08-09|work=oscars.org}}</ref>

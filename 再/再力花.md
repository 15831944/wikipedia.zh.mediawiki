'''再力花'''（[[學名|學名]]：'''''Thalia dealbata'''''；{{lang-en|'''Powdery thalia'''}}，'''Hardy canna'''，'''Powdery alligator-flag'''）是原產於[[美國南部|美國南部]]和中部及[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]的一種[[水生植物|水生植物]]<ref>Fraser, John. 1794. Icon. Thalia dealbata t. 1, ''Thalia dealbata'' (the publication apparently lost but the illustrations still surviving at the British Museum in London fide Tropicos)</ref><ref>[http://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/page/766823#page/368/mode/1up Roscoe, William. 1807.  Transactions of the Linnean Society of London 8: 340, ''Thalia dealbata'' ]</ref><ref>[http://www.biodiversitylibrary.org/page/7430545#page/405/mode/1up Rafinesque, Constantine Samuel. 1838.  Flora Telluriana 4: 51. ''Spirostylis biflora'']</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.yuanlin.com/flora/HTML/Info/2006-5/Yuanlin_Flora_1771.HTML|title=再力花||publisher=中國園林網|accessdate=2014-06-10}}</ref><ref>[http://bonap.net/MapGallery/County/Thalia%20dealbata.png Biota of North America Program, map, ''Thalia dealbata'']</ref>。在[[溫哥華|溫哥華]]這樣的北方地區也可以生存<ref>[http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=1&taxon_id=222000385 Flora of North America v 22, ''Thalia dealbata'']</ref><ref>[http://plants.usda.gov/java/profile?symbol=THDE USDA Profile]</ref><ref>[http://www.wildflower.org/plants/result.php?id_plant=thde University of Texas Native Plant Database]</ref>。

再力花高約{{convert|6|ft|m}}，開紫羅蘭色小花，圓錐花絮，長{{convert|8|in|cm}}。葉子為披針形、呈藍綠色、邊緣為紫色<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ces.ncsu.edu/depts/hort/consumer/factsheets/water-garden/marginalaq/thalia_dealbata.html|title=Marginal Aquatics: Thalia dealbata|last=Evans|first=Erv|publisher=North Carolina State University|accessdate=15 May 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100719130045/http://www.ces.ncsu.edu/depts/hort/consumer/factsheets/water-garden/marginalaq/thalia_dealbata.html|archivedate=2010年7月19日|df=}}</ref><ref>[http://www.missouribotanicalgarden.org/gardens-gardening/your-garden/plant-finder/plant-details/kc/a646/thalia-dealbata.aspx Missouri Botanical Garden]</ref>。

'''千島短尾貓'''是一种源自[[千岛群岛|千岛群岛]]、[[库页岛|库页岛]]、[[勘察加半島|勘察加半島]]的[[猫品种|猫品种]]<ref name="TICA PDF">{{cite web |url= http://www.tica.org/members/publications/standards/kb.pdf |title=Kurilian Bobtail Breed Group (KB/KBL) |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |work=TICA.org |year=2008 |publisher=[[The_International_Cat_Association|The International Cat Association]] (TICA) |location=Harlingen, Texas |accessdate=2011-12-12}}</ref><ref name="TICA KB">{{cite web |url= http://www.tica.org/public/breeds/kb/intro.php |title=TICA Kurilian Bobtail Breed Introduction |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |work=TICA.org |year=2011 |publisher=''op. cit.'' |accessdate=2011-11-24}}</ref>，有短毛和長毛的變種，尾巴均極短，體型與[[曼島貓|曼島貓]]<ref name="TICA KB" /><ref name="FIFe PDF">{{cite web |url=http://www.fifeweb.org/dnld/std/KBL-KBS.pdf |title=Kurilean Bobtail Longhair/Shorthair: KBL/KBS |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |publisher=[[Fédération_Internationale_Féline|Fédération Internationale Féline]] (FIFe) |location= |work=FIFeWeb.org |date=1 January 2010 |accessdate=2011-12-12 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年10月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>、[[日本短尾貓|日本短尾貓]]有相似之處<ref name="TICA PDF" /><ref name="TICA JB">{{cite web |url= http://www.tica.org/members/publications/standards/jb.pdf |title=Japanese Bobtail Breed Group (JB/JBL) |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |work=TICA.org |year=2009 |publisher=''op. cit.'' |accessdate=2011-12-12}}</ref>。千島短尾貓的短毛品種是自然繁殖產生的<ref name="TICA KB" /><ref name="FIFe PDF" />，19世紀時已經出現在千島群島了<ref name="TICA KB" />。俄國人將其作為寵物培育，再加上擁有捕鼠能力，20世紀中葉之後傳播到了歐洲其他國家<ref name="TICA KB" />。

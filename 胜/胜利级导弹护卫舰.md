'''胜利级导弹护卫舰'''（{{lang-en|Victory-Class Missile Corvette}}）是[[新加坡共和国海军|新加坡共和国海军]]的多用途船只，由新加坡引进德国{{Le|吕森造船厂|Lürssen}}的MGB 62型设计方案建造而成<ref>{{Cite book|title=Jane's Fighting Ships 2009-2010|last=Saunders|first=Stephen|publisher=Jane's Information Group|year=2009|isbn=978-0710628886|location=Coulsdon, Surrey, UK|pages=726}}</ref>。胜利级共建造六艘，分别于1990年至1991年投入服役并编入新加坡海軍的188[[中队|中队]]。

== 历史 ==
于60至70年代，新加坡海军主要职责是充当海岸警卫队。70年代后期，海军的高级[[军官|军官]]计划扩大海军的责任和能力。<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|title=Defending the Lion City|last=Huxley, Tim|publisher=Allen & Unwin|year=2001|isbn=1-86508-118-3}}</ref>维护并保持新加坡日益增长的区域[[贸易|贸易]]变成了海军所需扮演的角色。海军也必须捍卫新加坡的海上通讯线路。作为方案的一部分，于1983年海军拟议订购一个中队的巡邏艦，并交由吕森造船厂建造。<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://mil.sohu.com/20120602/n344656434.shtml|title=新加坡“胜利”级巡邏艦装备“扫描鹰”无人机-搜狐军事频道|accessdate=2018-06-14|work=mil.sohu.com}}</ref>第一艘胜利号于德国建造，其余五艘由新加坡船舶和工程建造（即现在的[[新加坡科技工程有限公司|新加坡科技工程有限公司]]）。这型巡邏艦装备与声纳和鱼雷，是新加坡海军首类有反[[潛艇|潛艇]]能力的军舰。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mindef.gov.sg/oms/navy/careers/our-assets/victory-class-missile-corvette.html|title=Navy Careers - Our Assets Victory Class Missile Corvette|accessdate=2018-06-14|author=|date=|work=www.mindef.gov.sg|publisher=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180615093514/https://www.mindef.gov.sg/oms/navy/careers/our-assets/victory-class-missile-corvette.html|archivedate=2018-06-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title=1988 - RSN's Missile Corvettes | work=MINDEF | url=http://www.mindef.gov.sg/imindef/about_us/history/maturing_saf/v04n06_history.html | accessdate=2004-09-26 | deadurl=no | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071007060625/http://www.mindef.gov.sg/imindef/about_us/history/maturing_saf/v04n06_history.html | archivedate=2007-10-07 | df= }}</ref>

1996年时，每一艘巡邏艦都配有两座八联装[[闪电-1导弹|闪电-1舰对空导弹发射器]]。主桅后部的平台和[[舰桥|舰桥]]顶部分别安装有第二个火控雷达和光电指示器。船体也增设了减摇鳍，以提高舰艇航海时的稳定性。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Jane's Fighting Ships, 2007-2008|last=Saunders, Stephen|publisher=Jane's Information Group|year=2007|isbn=978-0-7106-2799-5}}</ref>

2009年，海军宣布对该级巡邏艦实施延寿改造。2011年8月23日，升级后的英武号在新加坡与[[美国海军|美国海军]]一同举行的{{Le|联合海上战备训练|Cooperation Afloat Readiness and Training}}中成功进行了巴拉克导弹的实弹发射。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mindef.gov.sg/oms/imindef/resourcelibrary/cyberpioneer/ed/chinese/weapons/2013/may13_weapon.html#.WyK7m6czbNM|title=升级导弹驱潜舰|accessdate=2018-06-14|work=www.mindef.gov.sg|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180615004527/https://www.mindef.gov.sg/oms/imindef/resourcelibrary/cyberpioneer/ed/chinese/weapons/2013/may13_weapon.html#.WyK7m6czbNM|archivedate=2018-06-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.navy.mil/submit/display.asp?story_id=23901|title=CARAT 2006 Kicks Off in Singapore|last=Affairs|first=This story was written by Senior Chief Mass Communication Specialist Melinda Larson, Destroyer Squadron 1 Public|accessdate=2018-06-14|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180615093514/http://www.navy.mil/submit/display.asp?story_id=23901|archivedate=2018-06-15}}</ref>

2012年，每艘胜利级增配一架[[扫描鹰无人机|扫描鹰无人机]]，英武号是首艘进行相应升级的舰艇。不过，该升级也使巡邏艦丧失了反潜艇能力。<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{cite web|url=http://navaltoday.com/2013/12/30/singapore-revamps-its-victory-class-corvettes/|title=Singapore Revamps Its Victory-Class Corvettes|publisher=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140809010721/http://navaltoday.com/2013/12/30/singapore-revamps-its-victory-class-corvettes/|archivedate=2014-08-09}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.janes.com/article/79656/singapore-trials-s-100-camcopter-on-littoral-mission-vessel|title=Singapore trials S-100 Camcopter on Littoral Mission Vessel  Jane's 360|accessdate=2018-06-14|author=|date=|work=www.janes.com|publisher=|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180506062128/http://www.janes.com/article/79656/singapore-trials-s-100-camcopter-on-littoral-mission-vessel|archivedate=2018-05-06}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/singapore-navys-unmanned-aerial-vehicles-make-their-debut-in-an-overseas-exercise|title=Singapore navy's unmanned aerial vehicles make their debut in an overseas exercise|last=hermesauto|date=2016-09-09|newspaper=The Straits Times|accessdate=2018-06-14|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180615093514/https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/singapore-navys-unmanned-aerial-vehicles-make-their-debut-in-an-overseas-exercise|archivedate=2018-06-15}}</ref>

2014年3月，[[马来西亚航空370号班机空难|马航370号班机空难]]发生时，气势号（连同其他两艘新加坡军舰）参与了多国搜寻失联客机的行动。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.asiaone.com/singapore/singapore-sends-2-warships-naval-helicopter-search-flight-mh370|title=Singapore sends 2 warships, naval helicopter to search for flight MH370|newspaper=AsiaOne|accessdate=2018-06-16|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180616153949/http://www.asiaone.com/singapore/singapore-sends-2-warships-naval-helicopter-search-flight-mh370|archivedate=2018-06-16|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.straitstimes.com/asia/se-asia/missing-malaysia-airlines-plane-singapore-sends-2-warships-naval-helicopter-to-search|title=Missing Malaysia Airlines plane: Singapore sends 2 warships, naval helicopter to search for flight MH370|last=migration|date=2014-03-09|newspaper=The Straits Times|accessdate=2018-06-16|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180712044516/https://www.straitstimes.com/asia/se-asia/missing-malaysia-airlines-plane-singapore-sends-2-warships-naval-helicopter-to-search|archivedate=2018-07-12|df=}}</ref>

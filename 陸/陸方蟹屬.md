'''陸方蟹屬'''（{{lang|la|''Geograpsus''}}）是[[十足目|十足目]][[短尾下目|短尾下目]]（[[螃蟹|螃蟹]]）[[方蟹科|方蟹科]]的其中一個屬，包括有五個品種<ref name="Ng">{{cite journal |journal=[[Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |year=2008 |volume=17 |pages=1–286 |title=Systema Brachyurorum: Part I. An annotated checklist of extant Brachyuran crabs of the world |author=Peter K. L. Ng, Danièle Guinot & Peter J. F. Davie |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s17/s17rbz.pdf |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]]}}</ref><ref name="Paulay">{{cite journal |author=Gustav Paulay & John Starmer |year=2011 |title=Evolution, insular restriction, and extinction of oceanic land crabs, exemplified by the loss of an endemic ''Geograpsus'' in the Hawaiian Islands |journal=[[PLoS_ONE|PLoS ONE]] |volume=6 |issue=5 |page=e19916 |doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0019916 |url=http://www.plosone.org/article/info%3Adoi%2F10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0019916}}</ref>。

目前在[[台灣|台灣]]分布的陸方蟹屬生物有以下三種<ref name=twcatalog>{{cite web |url=http://catalog.digitalarchives.tw/Catalog/List.jsp?CID=4897 |title=生物:動物界:節肢動物門:甲殼綱:十足目:方蟹科 |publisher=[[數位典藏與學習聯合目錄|數位典藏與學習聯合目錄]] |language=zh-hant |accessdate=2012-10-18 }}</ref>：
* [[毛足陸方蟹|毛足陸方蟹]] ''Geograpsus crinipes'' <small>(Dana, 1851)</small>
* [[格雷陸方蟹|格雷陸方蟹]] ''Geograpsus grayi'' <small>(H. Milne-Edwards, 1853)</small>
* [[斯氏陸方蟹|斯氏陸方蟹]] ''Geograpsus stormi'' <small>De Man, 1895</small>

其他地區尚有：
*''[[Geograpsus_lividus|Geograpsus lividus]]'' <small>(H. Milne-Edwards, 1837)</small>，模式種。

以下品種只有化石：
*† [[塞氏陸方蟹|塞氏陸方蟹]] ''Geograpsus severnsi'' <small>Paulay & Starmer, 2011</small>

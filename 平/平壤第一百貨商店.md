'''平壤第一百貨商店'''（{{lang-ko|평양제1백화점}}）是[[北韓|北韓]]最大的百貨商店，位於[[平壤直轄市|平壤直轄市]][[中區域|中區域]]勝利街<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://cybernk.net/infoText/InfoHumanCultureDetail.aspx?mc=CC0305&sc=A32025001&tid=CC020500035384&direct=1|title=평양제1백화점 - 북한지역정보넷|accessdate=2018-07-24|author=|date=|work=cybernk.net|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180724064134/http://cybernk.net/infoText/InfoHumanCultureDetail.aspx?mc=CC0305&sc=A32025001&tid=CC020500035384&direct=1#|archive-date=2018-07-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>，[[金日成廣場|金日成廣場]]附近<ref name="Hokkanen2013">{{cite book|last=Hokkanen|first=Jouni|chapter=Pohjois-Korea: Matkailijan opas|trans-chapter=North Korea: Traveler's Guide|title=Pohjois-Korea: Siperiasta itään|year=2013|publisher=Johnny Kniga|location=Helsinki|language=fi|isbn=978-951-0-39946-0|trans-title=North Korea: East of Siberia|page=[10]}}</ref>。以前只有在國慶節、建黨紀念日或領導人生日等重大節日才憑票限量供應民眾購物，2009年貨幣改革後平日也開放購物<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.afinance.cn/new/tpxw/201108/368325.html|title=朝鲜平壤百货商店内景照曝光|access-date=2012-09-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130407135509/http://www.afinance.cn/new/tpxw/201108/368325.html#|archive-date=2013-04-07|dead-url=no}}</ref>。這所商店是北韓少數開放外國觀光客購物的商店之一<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.koryogroup.com/tours/kumgangsan.html|title=Kumgangsan Tour|publisher=Koryo Tour Group|accessdate=July 6, 2013|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130411055556/http://www.koryogroup.com/tours/kumgangsan.html|archive-date=2013-04-11|dead-url=yes}}</ref> ，朝鮮人可直接以[[朝鮮圓|朝鮮圓]]購物，外國人則只有在購買進口商品時可使用朝幣，購買朝鮮本國商品時需支付外幣<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/herald/2004-09/13/content_1990507.htm|title=独家探访平壤第一百货|publisher=新華網|access-date=2012-09-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101103011310/http://news.xinhuanet.com/herald/2004-09/13/content_1990507.htm#|archive-date=2010-11-03|dead-url=no}}</ref>。平壤第一百貨商店是北韓全國最大的零售商店，販售的商品包括電子用品、服飾、家具、玩具、廚具與食品等<ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.businessinsider.com/2012-02-25/news/31098345_1_north-korea-koryo-hotel-kim-jong|title=North Koreans Experience The Marvels Of A Supermarket Firsthand|author=Pak Won Il|publisher=Business Insider|date=Feb 25, 2012|accessdate=July 6, 2013|archive-url=https://archive.is/20130410162923/http://articles.businessinsider.com/2012-02-25/news/31098345_1_north-korea-koryo-hotel-kim-jong|archive-date=2013-04-10|dead-url=yes}}</ref> ，也常是大型商品展的舉行地點<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kcna.co.jp/item/2012/201212/news06/20121206-13ee.html|title=Commodity Exhibition Held at Pyongyang Department Store No. 1|publisher=[[朝鮮中央通訊社|朝鮮中央通訊社]]|date=Dec 6, 2012|accessdate=July 6, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141012081210/http://www.kcna.co.jp/item/2012/201212/news06/20121206-13ee.html|archive-date=2014-10-12|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，據傳這家商店是與[[中國|中國]]商人合夥經營的<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nkeconwatch.com/2006/11/01/the-political-economy-of-chinese-investment-in-north-korea|title=The Political Economy of Chinese Investment in North Korea|author=Jae Cheol Kim|publisher=North Korean Economy Watch|date=Nov 1, 2006|accessdate=July 6, web|url=http://www.nkeconwatch.com/2013/02/22/north-korean-products-in-department-stores-on-the-rise|title=North Korean products in department stores on the rise|author=Curtis Melvin|publisher=North Korean Economy Watch|date=Feb 22, 2013|accessdate=July 6, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180722155408/http://www.nkeconwatch.com/2013/02/22/north-korean-products-in-department-stores-on-the-rise/#|archive-date=2018-07-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

瑞典籍的秘密採訪記者{{link-sv|卡羅琳·薩贊格|Caroline Salzinger}}在書中描述她造訪平壤第一百貨商店的歷程：剛抵達時商店還關著{{sfn|Salzinger|2008|p=47}}，她身邊導遊分散其注意力的同時，其他人才匆忙跑去開門，並倉促尋找路人以充當顧客，當她與導遊踏進店內時手扶梯才剛啟動{{sfn|Salzinger|2008|p=48}}。扮演顧客的路人顯得不太熟悉在百貨公司購物該有的舉動，當卡羅琳好不容易選好商品要結帳時，收銀員也表現相當生疏，不知道要將商品放在塑膠袋中遞給她{{sfn|Salzinger|2008|p=49}}。另外她在書中也透露有另一位來自西方國家的外交官曾觀察平壤第一百貨長達一小時，都沒有任何顧客進出{{sfn|Salzinger|2008|p=49}}。但有名在平壤住了15個月的[[聯合國|聯合國]]專員表示他在2010年左右經常在這家商店購買食品與便宜的香菸，也有遇到許多當地顧客<ref>Black market cash, The real value of N. Korean Won, in: NKNews, 1 September 2014; http://www.nknews.org/2014/09/black-market-cash-the-real-value-of-n-korean-won/ {{Wayback|url=http://www.nknews.org/2014/09/black-market-cash-the-real-value-of-n-korean-won/# |date=20180722070138 }}</ref> 。

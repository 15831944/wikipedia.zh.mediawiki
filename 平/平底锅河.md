'''平底锅河'''（{{lang-en|Fryingpan River}}）是[[咆哮叉河|咆哮叉河]]的一条支流，[[科罗拉多河|科罗拉多河]]的二级支流，位于[[美国|美国]][[科罗拉多州|科罗拉多州]]中西部，长约{{convert|42|mi|km}}<ref name=NHD>U.S. Geological Survey. National Hydrography Dataset high-resolution flowline data. [http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/ The National Map] {{webarchive |url=https://www.webcitation.org/66gupqQDM?url=http://viewer.nationalmap.gov/viewer/ |date=2012-04-05 }}, accessed March 18, 2011</ref>，大部分位于[[怀特河国家森林|怀特河国家森林]]境内，建有蓄水量{{convert|102000|acre.ft}}的Ruedi水库<ref name=DoI>U.S Department of the Interior: "Water and Power resources Service, Project Data." U.S. Government Printing Office, 1981</ref> 。

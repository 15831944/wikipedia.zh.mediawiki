'''平壤民俗公園'''（{{lang-ko|평양민속공원}}）是[[北韓|北韓]][[平壤|平壤]]的一座[[主題樂園|主題樂園]]。位於[[大城山|大城山]]山腳下，樂園主題以朝鮮歷史為主，2008年12月開始建造，2012年9月完工開放<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&v=yfmgcG7Zn-Q# |title=North Korea builds Theme Park about Korean History 마감단계에 이른 평양민속공원건설" |access-date=2012-09-09 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150313073656/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yfmgcG7Zn-Q&feature=player_embedded# |archive-date=2015-03-13 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。[[肅川郡|肅川郡]]與[[沙里院市|沙里院市]]亦有民俗公園。公園中的韓國民俗村是受歡迎的景點<ref name=NKFolklore>{{cite news|title=Why is North Korea Building a Mammoth Size “Folklore Park”?|url=http://www.nknews.org/2012/05/why-is-north-korea-building-a-mammoth-size-folklore-park/|accessdate=May 23, 2012|newspaper=NK News|date=May 23, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121211092246/http://www.nknews.org/2012/05/why-is-north-korea-building-a-mammoth-size-folklore-park/#|archive-date=2012-12-11|dead-url=no}}</ref>。平壤民俗公園鮮少有觀光客造訪，每有觀光客也都是隨官方旅行團前來的。2016年六月起平壤民俗公園以整修為由關閉，新聞報導指出這可能與[[金正恩|金正恩]]與他的姑父[[張成澤|張成澤]]的恩怨有關，後者於2013年被處決前曾主導平壤民俗公園的建造<ref>{{cite news|title=North Korea shuts down Pyongyang model village, apparently on Kim’s order|url=http://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2016/06/14/asia-pacific/north-korea-shuts-pyongyang-model-village-apparently-kims-order/|accessdate=June 15, 2016|newspaper=Japan Times|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161023061124/http://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2016/06/14/asia-pacific/north-korea-shuts-pyongyang-model-village-apparently-kims-order#|archive-date=2016-10-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

'''旋鰓蟲屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Spirobranchus''}}）是[[龍介蟲科|龍介蟲科]]下一[[屬|屬]]細小的[[環節動物|環節動物]]<ref>{{cite book |first1=Edward E. |last1=Ruppert |first2=Richard S. |last2=Fox |first3=Robert D. |last3=Barnes |title=Invertebrate Zoology: A Functional Evolutionary Approach, |edition=7th | publisher = Brooks/Cole - Thomson Learning Inc.|location=Belmont, [[加州|CA]] ([[USA|USA]]) | date = 2004 | pages = 963| isbn = 0-03-025982-7 }}</ref>。

'''吳琪雁'''（{{lang-en|Cheyenne Goh}}，{{bd|1999年|3月2日||}}）是一名[[新加坡|新加坡]]女子[[短道速滑|短道速滑]]運動員。<ref name=ST>{{Cite web|url=http://www.isu.html.infostradasports.com/cache/TheASP.asp@PageID=302037&SportID=302&Personid=1551194&TaalCode=2&StyleID=0&Cache=2.html?701473|title=ST - Person Bio|accessdate=2018-07-31|work=www.isu.html.infostradasports.com|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180715123157/http://www.isu.html.infostradasports.com/cache/TheASP.asp%40PageID%3D302037%26SportID%3D302%26Personid%3D1551194%26TaalCode%3D2%26StyleID%3D0%26Cache%3D2.html?701473|archive-date=2018-07-15|dead-url=no}}</ref>她是历史上首位代表新加坡参加冬季奥林匹克运动会的选手。

==生平==
吳琪雁出生于新加坡，她4歲時隨家人移居[[加拿大|加拿大]][[亞伯達省|亞伯達省]][[艾德蒙頓|艾德蒙頓]]，並在那裡開始學習滑冰。吳琪雁首次接触溜冰的场地是艾德蒙頓的一个户外溜冰场。当时吳琪雁拒绝使用任何辅助配备在溜冰场上帮助取得平衡，并且成功溜冰。这证明了吳琪雁对溜冰有天赋。因而吳琪雁决定继续练习。<ref name="Her World">{{cite web | title=6 things you didn’t know about Winter Olympian Cheyenne Goh and her sport | website=Her World | url=https://www.herworld.com/features/women-now/singapore-winter-olympic-cheyenne-goh-speed-skating-pyeongchang | access-date=2018-08-05 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180805091930/https://www.herworld.com/features/women-now/singapore-winter-olympic-cheyenne-goh-speed-skating-pyeongchang | archive-date=2018-08-05 | dead-url=no }}</ref>她先选择主攻冰球項目，但是训练8年后放弃冰球，於2012年開始改練短道速滑。<ref name="Her World"/><ref name="Munirah 2017">{{cite web | last=Munirah | first=Sharifah | title=Dream-chaser Cheyenne puts studies on ice | website=The New Paper | date=2017-12-15 | url=https://www.tnp.sg/sports/team-singapore/dream-chaser-cheyenne-puts-studies-ice | access-date=2018-07-31 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180715152028/https://www.tnp.sg/sports/team-singapore/dream-chaser-cheyenne-puts-studies-ice | archive-date=2018-07-15 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

吳琪雁有一名弟弟，年龄相差4岁，是一名青年职业脚车运动员。<ref name="Her World"/>

==职业生涯==
===2016-2017赛季===
2016年起，吳琪雁师从[[韩国|韩国]]著名的前女子[[短道速滑|短道速滑]]运动员[[全利卿|全利卿]]。<ref name=ST/><ref name="Munirah 2018">{{cite web | last=Munirah | first=Sharifah | title=Singapore's first Winter Olympian Cheyenne Goh inspires youngsters | website=The New Paper | date=2018-02-26 | url=https://www.tnp.sg/sports/team-singapore/singapores-first-winter-olympian-cheyenne-goh-inspires-youngsters | access-date=2018-08-03 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180803023107/https://www.tnp.sg/sports/team-singapore/singapores-first-winter-olympian-cheyenne-goh-inspires-youngsters | archive-date=2018-08-03 | dead-url=no }}</ref>她在2016-17赛季短道速滑世界杯卡尔加里站赛事的500米项目中获得第18名，这是吳琪雁职业生涯中的世界杯最佳排名。<ref name=ST/>2017年2月，她参加了在[[日本|日本]][[札幌|札幌]]举办的[[2017年亚洲冬季运动会|亚洲冬季运动会]]，成为首位参加这一赛事的新加坡女子短道速滑选手<ref name="Chia 2017"/>，最终她在500米、1000米、1500米三个项目上分别获得第11名、第12名和第13名<ref name="Pyeongchang 2018">{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/short-track-speed-skating/athlete-profile-n3020947-cheyenne-goh.htm|title=Short Track Speed Skating Athlete Profile - Cheyenne GOH|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2018-08-05|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180228041353/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/short-track-speed-skating/athlete-profile-n3020947-cheyenne-goh.htm|archivedate=2018-02-28}}</ref>。一个月后，她又参加了在荷兰鹿特丹举办的[[2017年世界短道速滑锦标赛|世界短道速滑锦标赛]]，又成为首次参与这一赛事的新加坡女选手<ref name="Munirah 2017"/>，最终她在500米、1000米、1500米三个项目上分别获得第41名、第45名和第43名<ref name="Pyeongchang 2018"/>。

===2017-2018赛季===
2017年8月，吳琪雁参与了[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[吉隆坡|吉隆坡]]主办的[[2017年东南亚运动会|东南亚运动会]]的短道速滑赛事，最终她获得两枚银牌和一枚铜牌，其中两枚银牌分别来自于1000米和3000米接力，而铜牌则来自于500米项目<ref name="Channel NewsAsia 2017">{{cite web | title=Singapore's first winter Olympian Cheyenne Goh looks forward to 2018 games in Korea | website=Channel NewsAsia | date=2017-12-08 | url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/sport/singapore-s-first-winter-olympian-cheyenne-goh-looks-forward-to-9478810 | access-date=2018-07-31 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171226130613/https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/sport/singapore-s-first-winter-olympian-cheyenne-goh-looks-forward-to-9478810 | archive-date=2017-12-26 | dead-url=no }}</ref>。

同年11月，吴琪雁在2017-18赛季短道速滑世界杯上海站女子1500米项目中获得第20名，她也凭借着这一站的成绩获得足够积分。短道速滑世界杯4站比赛结束后，她凭借着1500米项目世界第37位的排名<ref name=ST/>成功获得奥运资格，不仅成为史上首位获得冬季奥林匹克运动会参赛资格的新加坡运动员<ref name="Munirah 2017"/><ref name="Chia 2017">{{cite web | last=Chia | first=Nicole | title=Winter sports: Cheyenne Goh, 18, becomes first Singapore athlete to qualify for Olympics, will race in Pyeongchang | website=The Straits Times | date=2017-11-24 | url=https://www.straitstimes.com/sport/cheyenne-goh-18-becomes-first-singaporean-athlete-to-qualify-for-winter-olympics-will-race-at | access-date=2018-07-31 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171202165248/http://www.straitstimes.com/sport/cheyenne-goh-18-becomes-first-singaporean-athlete-to-qualify-for-winter-olympics-will-race-at | archive-date=2017-12-02 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Channel NewsAsia 20172">{{cite web | title=Skater Cheyenne Goh qualifies for Winter Olympics, a first for Singapore | website=Channel NewsAsia | date=2017-11-24 | url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/skater-cheyenne-goh-qualifies-for-winter-olympics-a-first-for-9437560 | access-date=2018-07-31 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180227231812/https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/skater-cheyenne-goh-qualifies-for-winter-olympics-a-first-for-9437560 | archive-date=2018-02-27 | dead-url=no }}</ref>，还成为史上首位获得冬奥短道速滑项目参赛资格的东南亚运动员<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.zaobao.com/news/sports/story20171125-813727|title=新加坡运动员首次入选冬奥会|publisher=[[联合早报|联合早报]]|date=2017-11-25|accessdate=2017-11-25}}</ref>。在上海站1500米比赛的预赛中，吴琪雁被分在第7组，比赛一开始她处于落后阶段，后来原本领先的其他选手意外相撞，这使得吴琪雁得以第三个冲过终点，比赛结束后裁判判定先于吴琪雁冲过终点的其中一人犯规<ref>{{cite web|url=http://shorttrack.sportresult.com/Results.aspx?evt=11213100000049&gen=w&ref=18602&view=rou&sub=spl&rac=7|title=Audi ISU World Cup 2017/18 - 2018年2月17日，吳琪雁参与了[[2018年冬季奥林匹克运动会短道速滑女子1500米比赛|平昌冬奥女子1500米比赛]]，最终她以2分36.971秒的成绩止步于资格赛，未能突围至半决赛。<ref name="Channel NewsAsia 2018">{{cite web | title=Speed Skating: Cheyenne Goh misses out on semis in Singapore's Winter Olympics debut | website=Channel NewsAsia | date=2018-02-27 | url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/cheyenne-goh-speed-skating-singapore-winter-olympics-debut-semis-9968314 | access-date=2018-07-31 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180310104642/https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/cheyenne-goh-speed-skating-singapore-winter-olympics-debut-semis-9968314 | archive-date=2018-03-10 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://web.archive.org/web/20180218024132/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/resOWG2018/pdf/OWG2018/STK/OWG2018_STK_C73A_STKW1500M-------------HEAT--------.pdf|title=Short Track Speed Skating|accessdate=2018-07-31|date=2018-02-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802170828/http://web.archive.org/web/20180218024132/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/resOWG2018/pdf/OWG2018/STK/OWG2018_STK_C73A_STKW1500M-------------HEAT--------.pdf|archive-date=2018-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>

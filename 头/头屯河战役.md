'''头屯河战役'''是苏联红军入侵新疆后与回族军阀[[马仲英|马仲英]]在新疆省昌吉头屯河的一场战役。

1934年[[盛世才|盛世才]]引[[苏联入侵新疆|苏联入侵新疆]]围剿马仲英。马仲英的[[回族|回族]]部队[[中央陆军新编第36师|中央陆军新编第36师]]在冻结的头屯河边被[[苏联红军|苏联红军]]攻击。战役持续20多日，苏军使用了[[芥子气|芥子气]]。<ref name="Pearson">{{cite web|url=http://www.fas.org/bwc/papers/review/cwtable.htm|title=Uses of CW since the First World War|last=|first=|publisher=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref>中央陆军新编第36师士兵身穿羊皮衣伪装于雪中，使用快马弯刀短距离攻击苏联红军机关枪点，并挫败苏军钳制，双方均伤亡惨重。因伤亡过大和武器落后，马仲英下令撤退。<ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=bEzNwgtiVQ0C&pg=PA112&dq=ma+zhongying+russian+cars&cd=2#v=onepage&q=storming%20the%20red%20army&f=false|title=Wild West China: The Taming of Xinjiang|author=Christian Tyler|year=2004|publisher=Rutgers University Press|location=New Brunswick, NJ|isbn=0-8135-3533-6|page=112|pages=|accessdate=2010-06-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=IAs9AAAAIAAJ&pg=PA120&dq=soviet+GPU+troops&hl=en&ei=QuwWTLPEMoX7lwfu_pTjCw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1&ved=0CCsQ6AEwAA#v=snippet&q=machine%20gun%20posts%20whilst%20wielding&f=false|title=Warlords and Muslims in Chinese Central Asia: A Political History of Republican Sinkiang 1911-1949|author=Andrew D.

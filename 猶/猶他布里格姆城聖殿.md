'''猶他布里格姆城聖殿'''（Brigham City Utah Temple）是[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的一座聖殿，位於[[猶他州|猶他州]][[布里格姆城_(猶他州)|布里格姆城]]。2009年，耶穌基督後期聖徒教會宣布將建設這座聖殿<ref>{{cite news |url= http://www.deseretnews.com/article/705334251/Five-new-LDS-temples-planned.html |title= Brigham City among five new locales for LDS temples |date= October 3, 2009 |accessdate= 2012-10-15 |first= Scott |last= Taylor |newspaper= [[Deseret_News|Deseret News]] }}</ref>，並在2010年7月31日動工<ref name="ldsnews">{{citation |contribution= President Packer Presides at Groundbreaking of Brigham City Utah Temple |url= http://www.mormonnewsroom.org/ldsnewsroom/eng/news-releases-stories/president-packer-presides-at-groundbreaking-of-brigham-city-utah-temple |title= News Release |work= Newsroom |publisher= [[LDS_Church|LDS Church]] |date= July 31, 2010 |accessdate= 2012-10-15 }}</ref>。2012年8月18日至9月15日期間， 猶他布里格姆城聖殿開放參觀。9月22日，聖殿正式奉獻<ref>{{citation |contribution= Mormon Brigham City Utah Temple Open House and Dedication Dates Announced |url= http://www.mormonnewsroom.org/article/mormon-brigham-city-utah-temple-open-house-dedication-dates |title= News Release |work= Newsroom |publisher= LDS Church |date= April 19, 2012 |accessdate= 2012-10-15 }}</ref>。

'''猶他查懋聲聖殿'''（Payson Utah Temple）是一座位於[[猶他州|猶他州]][[查懋聲_(猶他州)|查懋聲]]的[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]聖殿。查懋聲聖殿在2010年宣布修建，是猶他州的第15座耶穌基督後期聖徒教會聖殿<ref>{{citation |url= http://www.mormonnewsroom.org/ldsnewsroom/eng/news-releases-stories/president-monson-announces-new-temple-in-payson-utah |title= President Monson Announces New Temple in Payson, Utah |work= Newsroom |publisher= LDS Church |date= January 25, 2010 |accessdate= 2012-11-06 }}</ref>。2015年，猶他查懋聲聖殿竣工<ref>{{citation |first= Donald W. |last= Meyers |date= October 18, 2011 |title= Mormons break ground for Payson Temple |url= http://www.sltrib.com/sltrib/news/52694330-183/temple-church-construction-oaks.html.csp |newspaper= [[The_Salt_Lake_Tribune|The Salt Lake Tribune]] |accessdate= 2012-11-06 }}</ref><ref>Compare with the 1996 [[Mount_Timpanogos_Utah_Temple|Mount Timpanogos Utah Temple]] at 107,240 sq ft on a 16.7 acre lot</ref>。

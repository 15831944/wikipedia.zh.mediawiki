'''猶太教重建派'''（{{Lang-he-n|יהדות רקונסטרוקציוניסטית}}）是一個現代猶太人運動，將猶太教視為一個逐漸發展的文明。這一教派基於莫德采科普蘭的概念發展，原本是[[猶太教保守派|猶太教保守派]]內部的一個流派，自1920年代後期開始發展，並在1955年自保守派分離<ref name="Kaplan2010">{{cite book|last=Kaplan|first=Mordecai M.|authorlink=Mordecai Kaplan|title=Judaism as a Civilization: Toward a Reconstruction of American-Jewish Life|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=mtGFZbJut0gC|accessdate=27 February 2017|year=2010|publisher=Jewish Publication Society|location=Philadelphia, PA|isbn=978-0-8276-1050-7|pages=i–viii}}</ref>，在1967年創建了拉比學院<ref name="KareshHurvitz2005">{{cite book|last1=Karesh|first1=Sara E|last2=Hurvitz|first2=Mitchell M.|title=Encyclopedia of Judaism|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Z2cCZBDm8F8C&pg=PA417|accessdate=27 February 2017|year=2005|publisher=Infobase Publishing|location=New York City, NY|isbn=978-0-8160-6982-8|page=417}}</ref>。在美國和加拿大有超過100個猶太會堂屬於猶太教重建派<ref>http://www.rrc.edu/node/1193, "Movement Restructuring FAQs", Reconstructionist Rabbinical College, June 4, 2012</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.jewishexponent.com/article/25852/Do_the_Jewish_Streams_Have_a_Future/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2012-06-05 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120514172921/http://www.jewishexponent.com/article/25852/Do_the_Jewish_Streams_Have_a_Future/ |archivedate=2012-05-14 |df= }}, "Do the Jewish Streams Have a Future?", The Jewish Exponent, May 09, 2012</ref>。

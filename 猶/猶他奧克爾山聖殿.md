'''猶他奧克爾山聖殿'''（Oquirrh Mountain Utah Temple）是[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]的一座聖殿，位於[[猶他州|猶他州]][[鹽湖城|鹽湖城]]郊外的[[南喬丹_(猶他州)|南喬丹]]。猶他奧克爾山聖殿是[[鹽湖城谷地|鹽湖城谷地]]的第四座聖殿，也是猶他州的第13座聖殿<ref>{{citation |contribution= Open House and Dedication Dates Announced for Oquirrh Mountain Utah Temple |url= http://www.mormonnewsroom.org/ldsnewsroom/eng/news-releases-stories/open-house-and-dedication-dates-announced-for-oquirrh-mountain-utah-temple |title= News Story |work= Newsroom |publisher= [[LDS_Church|LDS Church]] |date= January 31, 2009 |accessdate= 2012-10-15 }}</ref>。

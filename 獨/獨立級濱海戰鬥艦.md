'''獨立級濱海戰鬥艦'''（{{lang-en|'''Independence-class littoral combat ship'''}}）是一種由[[通用动力|通用动力]]公司研發的[[濒海战斗舰|濒海战斗舰]]<ref>"[http://www.generaldynamics.com/news/press_releases/2003/July17,%202003%20News%20Release-3.htm General Dynamics Bath Iron Works Team Wins Preliminary Design Award for U.S. Navy’s Littoral Combat Ship] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928004658/http://www.generaldynamics.com/news/press_releases/2003/July17%2C%202003%20News%20Release-3.htm |date=2007-09-28 }}". General Dynamics press release, 17 July 2003.</ref>，採用罕見的{{tsl|en|Trimaran|三體船}}設計。[[美國海軍|美國海軍]]的另外一種濒海战斗舰是由[[洛克希德·马丁|洛克希德·马丁]]公司研發[[自由级濒海战斗舰|自由级濒海战斗舰]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.navy.mil/navydata/fact_display.asp?cid=4200&tid=1650&ct=4|title=US Navy Fact File: LITTORAL COMBAT SHIP CLASS - LCS|accessdate=8 June 2012}}</ref>。

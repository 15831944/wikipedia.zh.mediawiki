'''摩根·赫德'''（{{lang-en|Morgan Hurd}}，{{bd|2001年|7月18日}}），中文昵称'''夏松'''<ref name=xinhua>{{cite web|url=http://news.xinhuanet.com/overseas/2017-10/09/c_129717477.htm|title=美国华裔体操少女世锦赛平衡木再夺银牌|date=2017-10-09|website=新华网}}</ref>，美国女子体操运动员。

== 生平 ==
摩根·赫德出生于[[中国|中国]][[广西|广西]][[梧州|梧州]]，出生后即被父母遗弃。她11个月大的时候时，被来自美国[[特拉华州|特拉华州]]的一个家庭从中国孤儿院领养。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://olympics.nbcsports.com/2018/08/14/morgan-hurd-gymnastics/amp/|title=Morgan Hurd’s path to gymnastics gold began with 6-hour bus ride|work=[[NBC_Sports|NBC Sports]]|date=August 14, 2018}}</ref> 三岁起，她开始练体操。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.flogymnastics.com/article/55897-morgan-hurd-on-the-rise-with-2020-olympics-in-sight#.WW4dXHTyvyJ |title=Morgan Hurd On The Rise With 2020 Olympics In Sight |work=Flo Gymnastics |date=March 10, 2017}}</ref>

2017年，赫德开始参加成年组比赛。8月，她在全美锦标赛中获全能第六名，并得以加入美国国家队。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.flogymnastics.com/article/60565-2017-u-s-women-s-national-team-named-at-p-g-championships#.WZ2DYHQrLyI|title=2017 U.S. Women's National Team Named At P&G Championships|work=FloGymnastics|date=August 20, 2017}}</ref>9月，她取得了代表美国队参加[[2017年世界竞技体操锦标赛|2017年世界竞技体操锦标赛]]的资格。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.flogymnastics.com/article/61939-meet-the-2017-u-s-women-s-world-championships-team#.WcO75nTyvyI|title=Meet The 2017 U.S. Women's World Championships Team|work=FloGymnastics|date=September 20, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://usagym.org/pages/post.html?PostID=20823&prog=h|title=USA Gymnastics names U.S. Women’s Team for 2017 World Championships|work=[[USA_Gymnastics|USA Gymnastics]]|date=September 20, 2017}}</ref>在世锦赛上，赫德发挥出色，最终以55.232的比分夺得女子全能冠军。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://au.news.yahoo.com/a/37382406/hurd-captures-surprise-all-around-world-gymnastics-gold/|title=Hurd captures surprise all-around World gymnastics gold|last=AFP|first=|date=October 6, 2017|work=yahoo.com|access-date=October 7, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171007120732/https://au.news.yahoo.com/a/37382406/hurd-captures-surprise-all-around-world-gymnastics-gold/|archive-date=2017-10-07|dead-url=yes}}</ref>也成为史上第一位在亚洲出生的世锦赛女子全能冠军。同时，她还在单项[[平衡木|平衡木]]比赛中获得了一枚银牌。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://usagym.org/pages/post.html?PostID=20910&prog=|title=Comaneci, J.K. Rowling applaud Hurd as U.S. women end Worlds with pair of silvers|date=October 8, 2017|work=USA Gymnastics}}</ref>

'''摩臣公共運輸交匯處'''（{{lang-en|Mawson Interchange}}）是南澳洲[[阿德萊德|阿德萊德]]交通的大型公共運輸交匯處，位於[[摩臣湖|摩臣湖]]。為[[高勒線|高勒線]]以及巴士中轉站，距離[[阿得雷德車站|阿德萊德站]]約14.3公里（8.9英里）。<ref>To the west of the station lies the [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australian_Rail_Track_Corporation Australian Rail Track Corporation] [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Standard_gauge standard gauge] line to [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crystal_Brook,_South_Australia Crystal Brook].[http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/var/metro/storage/original/application/8a768a6fdea5fb9418c2b174b850e308.pdf Gawler Central timetable] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140210204945/http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/var/metro/storage/original/application/8a768a6fdea5fb9418c2b174b850e308.pdf|date=10 February 2014}} Adelaide Metro 4 February 2013</ref>

== 歷史 ==
摩臣公共運輸交匯處屬於'''摩臣連接計劃'''（Mawson Connector）工程一部份，整個工程造價為1130萬澳元。摩臣連接計劃工程於2005年1月開始動工，包括興建一條1.3公里的歐德司馬富道（Elder Smith Road），連接[[梳士巴利公路|梳士巴利公路]]及[[大北道|大北道]]；以行車天橋方式跨越[[高勒線|高勒線]]，並興建公共運輸交匯處。摩臣公共運輸交匯處於2006年2月26日啓用，相隔二十年首個新火車站。<ref>[http://www.infrastructure.sa.gov.au/completed_projects/mawson_connector Mawson Interchange] Department of Planning, Transport & Infrastructure</ref>

== 交通接駁 ==
除了火車以外，阿德萊德交通營運五條巴士路線途經公共運輸交匯處：
* 222： [[阿德雷得中央商業區|市中心]]<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/routes/222 Route 222] Adelaide Metro</ref>
* 224： 伊利沙伯站↔市中心<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/routes/224 Route 224] Adelaide Metro</ref>
* 411： [[梳士巴利公共運輸交匯處|梳士巴利公共運輸交匯處]]<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/routes/411 Route 411] Adelaide Metro</ref>
* 501：市中心（經：[[O-Bahn公交车道|O-Bahn]]）<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/routes/501 Route 501] Adelaide Metro</ref>
* 565：賢告農場<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/routes/224 Route 565] Adelaide Metro</ref>

在'''杜雲士鐵路交匯計劃'''（{{lang-en|Torrens Rail Junction Project}}）工程進行期間，本站以南之[[高勒線|高勒線]]車站均會停駛，並設免費接駁巴士連接市中心：<ref>[http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/Announcements/Planned-Disruptions/Outer-Harbor-Grange-and-Gawler-rail-line-closures Outer Harbor, Grange and Gawler rail line closures]</ref><ref>[http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/Announcements/Planned-Disruptions/Outer-Harbor-Grange-and-Gawler-train-lines-temporary-closures-June-long-weekend Outer Harbor, Grange and Gawler train lines temporary closures June long weekend]</ref><ref>[http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/Announcements/Planned-Disruptions/Outer-Harbor-Grange-and-Gawler-line-July-closure Outer Harbor, Grange and Gawler line July closure]</ref><ref>[http://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/Announcements/Planned-Disruptions/Outer-Harbor-Grange-and-Gawler-rail-line-closures-August-2017 Outer Harbor, Grange and Gawler rail line closures August 2017]</ref>
* ML1： [[阿德雷得中央商業區|市中心]]（各停）<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/routes/222 Route 222] Adelaide Metro</ref>
* ML2： [[阿德雷得中央商業區|市中心]]（特急）<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/routes/222 Route 222] Adelaide Metro</ref>

'''摩尼字母'''是一个位于第一輔助平面的[[Unicode區段|Unicode区段]]，收录了用于拼写[[粟特语|粟特语]], [[安息语|安息语]]，[[法尔斯语|法尔斯语]]的摩尼字母。<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.unicode.org/L2/L2011/11123r-n4029r-manichaean.pdf | title=N4029R: Second revised proposal for encoding the Manichaean script in the SMP of the UCS | first1=Michael | last1=Everson | first2=Desmond | last2=Durkin-Meisterernst | first3=Roozbeh | last3=Pournader | first4=Shervin | last4=Afshar | publisher=Working Group Document, ISO/IEC JTC1/SC2/WG2 | date=2011-05-10 }}</ref> 

本区块定义了5个标准化变体。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.unicode.org/Public/UNIDATA/StandardizedVariants.txt|title=Unicode Character Database: Standardized Variation Sequences | publisher=The Unicode Consortium}}</ref>

'''摩德羅'''（[[IAST|IAST]]：{{IAST|Mādra}}，發音：{{IPA-sa|maːdrə|}}）是一個古地區及其居民的名稱，位於[[印度次大陸|印度次大陸]]西北部。摩德羅國首都據信位於[[锡亚尔科特|锡亚尔科特]]（今巴基斯坦境內）<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=e8o5HyC0-FUC&pg=PA144&lpg=PA144&dq=chandrahas+sialkot&source=bl&ots=PDYObWfXq9&sig=JtqY5bk099FNjp6MMcrGdGVIyqQ&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiwgNnM4YHZAhVP22MKHQlNDa4Q6AEIODAG#v=onepage&q=chandrahas%20sialkot&f=false|title=Encyclopaedia of Untouchables Ancient, Medieval and Modern|last=Kumar|first=Raj|date=2008|publisher=Gyan Publishing House|isbn=9788178356648|language=en}}</ref>。邊境據信自興都庫什部分地區（可能遠達伊朗東北部）延伸至今日的印度[[旁遮普地区|旁遮普]]與[[哈里亚纳邦|哈里亞納]]。在印度史詩《[[摩訶婆羅多|摩訶婆羅多]]》描述道{{tsl|en|Madra Kingdom|摩德羅國}}軍隊由國王{{tsl|en|Shalya|沙利耶}}率领，疆域由今旁遮普西北推进到了[[哈里亚纳邦|哈里亚纳]]<ref>{{cite book|last1=Menon|first1=Ramesh|title=The Mahabharata, A modern rendering|date=2006|publisher=iUniverse|isbn=9780595401888}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Ganguly|first1=Kisari|title=The Mahabharata|url=http://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/m08/index.htm|website=www.sacred-texts.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Przyluski|first1=Jean|title=Ancient Peoples of the Punjab|date=1960|publisher=K. L. Mukhopadhyaya|location=University of Minnesota|pages=vi, 7-8}}</ref>。摩德羅在梵語及巴利語文學中被大量引用，一些學術作品稱其為[[吠陀文化|吠陀時期]][[刹帝利|刹帝利]]的一部分<ref>{{cite book|last1=Law|first1=Dr. Bimla Charan|title=Some Ksatriya Tribes of Ancient India|date=1924|publisher=University of Calcutta|pages=214–229|url=https://archive.org/stream/someksatriyatrib035136mbp#page/n259/mode/2up}}</ref>。近代学术研究以此为参考，认为摩德罗王国至少存續了兩千年，並認為其位於[[拉维河|拉维河]]與[[奇納布河|奇納布河]]之間<ref>{{cite book|last1=Hamadani|first1=Agha Hussain|title=The frontier policy of the Delhi Sultans|date=1986|publisher=National Institute of Historical and Cultural Research|page=112}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Bajpai|first1=Shiva Chandra (S.C.)|title=Lahaul-Spiti: A Forbidden Kingdom in the Himalayas|date=1987|publisher=Indus Publishing Company|page=11|edition=Fourth|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=W4mCH1oCKTkC&pg=PA11&lpg=PA11&dq=madra+tribe&source=bl&ots=TRttXqRPC8&sig=8wmTZgW4IqpHxcUbGR8XlkwUjCk&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwib-p3u3KXNAhXBFj4KHbDHB9EQ6AEINjAE#v=onepage&q=madra%20tribe&f=false}}</ref>。摩德羅國則由沙利耶建立。

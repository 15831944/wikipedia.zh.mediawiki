'''格罗索山油田'''是一处位于南亚平宁地区的[[油田|油田]]。该油田发现于2006年，由[[地中海油气公司|地中海油气公司]]开发。它从2011年起开始产[[石油|油]]。格罗索油田的总探明储量大约为1亿桶（17.7×10<sup>6</sup>吨），平均日产量为{{Convert|15000|oilbbl/d|m3/d}}。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.medoilgas.com/resources/Serra%20San%20Bernardo%20Drilling%20Operations%205th%20October%202006.pdf|title=SERRA SAN BERNARDO LICENCE – DRILLING OPERATIONS APPROVED|accessdate=2010-09-06|date=2007|publisher=Mediterranean Oil & Gas|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110714065231/http://www.medoilgas.com/resources/Serra%20San%20Bernardo%20Drilling%20Operations%205th%20October%202006.pdf|archivedate=2011-07-14}}</ref>

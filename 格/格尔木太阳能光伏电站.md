'''格尔木太阳能光伏电站'''是一个位于中国[[青海省|青海省]][[格尔木|格尔木]]的[[光伏电站|光伏电站]]。<ref name=cluster>{{cite web|title=Golmud Desert Solar Cluster - satellite view|url=http://www.wiki-solar.org/map/sites/index.html?Golmud_Desert_Cluster?Pm61538?12?36.37,95.18|publisher=Wiki-Solar|accessdate=13 August 2013}}</ref> 此电站始建于2009年八月<ref>[http://www.thebioenergysite.com/news/4403/largest-solar-power-plant-begins-construction Largest Solar Power Plant Begins Construction]</ref>，并在2011年10月29日建成<ref>[http://newscontent.cctv.com/news.jsp?fileId=120151 China-PV Power Station] "Restrictions: Not access Chinese mainland"</ref><ref>[http://www.accendo.ro/news/res/74-golmud-solar-park China PV plant largest in the world ] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120405034226/http://www.accendo.ro/news/res/74-golmud-solar-park |date=2012-04-05 }}</ref>。其中有80兆瓦由[[英利|英利]]投资<ref>[http://www.solarnovus.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=3103:yingli-green-energy-to-supply-110-mw-of-pv-modules-to-huanghe-hydropower&catid=37:business-news&Itemid=241 Yingli Green Energy to Supply 110 MW of PV Modules to Huanghe Hydropower]</ref>。这个项目在2012年获得了[[中国电力优质工程奖|中国电力优质工程奖]]。其一年预计输出电能317.2[[吉瓦时|吉瓦时]]。
<ref>[http://eng.cpicorp.com.cn/NewsCenter/CorporateNews/201206/t20120607_200313.htm Golmud 200MW PV Station Won China Quality Power Project Award] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120725025143/http://eng.cpicorp.com.cn/NewsCenter/CorporateNews/201206/t20120607_200313.htm |date=2012-07-25 }}</ref>

此外，[[龙源电力|龙源电力]]在2011年在青海省格尔木建设了一个20兆瓦的太阳能产业园。<ref>[http://www.pvsolarchina.com/chint-solar-successfully-completed-golmud-20mw-photovoltaic-power-station.html Chint Solar successfully completed Golmud 20MW photovoltaic power station]</ref><ref>[http://www.suntrix.cn/home-en/news-events/company-news/golmud.aspx Suntrix Golmud Project Shows Extraordinary Effect in the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120405011653/http://www.suntrix.cn/home-en/news-events/company-news/golmud.aspx |date=2012-04-05 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.renewbl.com/2011/11/11/astronergy-completes-20-mw-golmud-solar-project-in-china.html Astronergy completes 20 MW Golmud solar project in China] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120405022737/http://www.renewbl.com/2011/11/11/astronergy-completes-20-mw-golmud-solar-project-in-china.html |date=2012-04-05 }}</ref> <ref name=cluster />  在格尔木，现已有570兆瓦的太阳能电站。<ref>[http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/usa/business/2012-03/02/content_14744509.htm Qinghai leads in photovoltaic power] Updated: 2012-03-02</ref>

'''格雷德币'''（Gridcoin）实现了一种“研究证明”（Proof-of-Research, POR）方案，该方案向在分布式计算平台 [[BOINC|Berkeley Open Infrastructure for Network Computing]]（BOINC）上执行有用的科学计算的用户奖励 Gridcoin。<ref name=":0">{{Cite book|title=Crowdware: A Framework for GPU-Based Public-Resource Computing with Energy-Aware Incentive Mechanism|last=Dong|first=Zhongli|last2=Lee|first2=Young Choon|last3=Zomaya|first3=Albert Y|year=2015|isbn=978-1-4673-9560-1|pages=266|doi=10.1109/CloudCom.2015.73}}</ref>Gridcoin 使用了一个更节能的[[权益证明|权益证明]]（Proof-of-stake，PoS）系统 — 尽管它没有明确地解决计算能力的能源成本。<ref name=":0" />

Gridcoin 试图通过其研究证明和权益证明协议来缓解加密货币挖掘对环境能源的影响。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Environmentalism in Cryptoanarchism: Gridcoin Case Study|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/323767603_Environmentalism_in_Cryptoanarchism_Gridcoin_Case_Study|last=Chohan|first=Usman|journal=SSRN Electronic Journal|accessdate=8 October 2018|doi=10.2139/ssrn.3131232|volume=Jan 2018}}</ref><ref name="Bloomberg">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2018-01-11/bitcoin-seen-addressing-power-hog-problem-with-prime-number-find|title=A Prime Number Could Be the Answer to Bitcoin's Power Problem|accessdate=7 Oct 2018|author=Tirone|date=10 Jan 2018|first=Jonathan|work=Bloomberg}}</ref>

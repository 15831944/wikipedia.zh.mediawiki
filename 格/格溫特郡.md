'''格溫特郡'''（{{lang-en|Gwent}}）是[[英國|英國]][[威爾斯|威爾斯]]的一個郡，設立於1974年，據1972年地方制度法而設立。郡名取自於古代的格溫特王國。格溫特郡位於威爾斯東南部。2003年，格溫特郡擴大了轄區<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.opsi.gov.uk/legislation/wales/wsi2003/20030974e.htm|title=The Preserved Counties (Amendment to Boundaries) (Wales) Order 2003|publisher=[[Office_of_Public_Sector_Information|Office of Public Sector Information]]|date=2003-04-01|accessdate=2010-09-11}}</ref>。2007年，格溫特郡有人口560,500人<ref>2007 population estimate, calculated using 2003 borders for Blaenau Gwent, Caerphilly, Monmouthshire, Newport, Torfaen.  Source: {{cite web|url=http://www.statistics.gov.uk/downloads/theme_population/Mid_2007_UK_England_&_Wales_Scotland_and_Northern_Ireland%20_21_08_08.zip |title=Table 10: Mid-2006 to Mid-2007 Population Estimates, Components of population change for local authorities in the United Kingdom |publisher=[[Office_for_National_Statistics|Office for National Statistics]] |accessdate=2010-09-11 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110728154121/http://www.statistics.gov.uk/downloads/theme_population/Mid_2007_UK_England_%26_Wales_Scotland_and_Northern_Ireland%20_21_08_08.zip |archivedate=2011-07-28 |df= }}</ref>，是威爾斯人口最多的區域。

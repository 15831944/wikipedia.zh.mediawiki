'''格里·哈奇'''（{{lang|ga|Gerry Hutch}}，{{bd|1963年||}}）是一个爱尔兰人，前罪犯。他是爱尔兰两起最大的持械抢劫的主要嫌疑犯<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.independent.ie/irish-news/hitting-them-where-it-hurts-26112893.html |title=Hitting them where it hurts |work=Independent |author=Martin Breheny |date=01/07/2000 |accessdate=August 6, 2014}}</ref><ref name=badfella>{{cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=mebuBdkoWTAC&pg=PT243 |title=Badfellas |publisher=Penguin |authorlink=Paul Williams (Irish journalist) |author=Paul Williams |year=2011 |page=243-45 |accessdate=August 6, 2014}}</ref>。1985年出狱之后，由于追求简单的生活方式，开始信仰宗教，而且主导“有纪律，[[禁慾主義|禁慾主義]]的生活方式”，被称为“修道士”<ref name="Examiner1">{{cite web|url=http://archives.tcm.ie/irishexaminer/2000/04/01/current/ipage_7.htm |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070227155155/http://archives.tcm.ie/irishexaminer/2000/04/01/current/ipage_7.htm |title=Hutch to Clear Outstanding Liabilities in a £1.2m Deal with the State |first=Conor |last=Keane |publisher=[[Irish_Examiner|Irish Examiner]] |archivedate=2007-02-27 |date=1 April 2000 |accessdate=2010-09-29 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>。他在都柏林市中心区的帮助生活条件差的儿童，并鼓励他们不去吸毒<ref name="Examiner1"/>。

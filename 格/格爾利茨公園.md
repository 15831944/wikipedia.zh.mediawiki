'''格爾利茨公園'''（Görlitzer Park）是[[德國|德國]]首都[[柏林|柏林]]的一座公園，位於[[十字山|十字山]]<ref>{{cite web|title=Görlitzer Park|url=http://www.berlin.de/orte/sehenswuerdigkeiten/goumlrlitzer-park/|publisher=Berlin.de|accessdate=24 May 2015|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150524220614/http://www.berlin.de/orte/sehenswuerdigkeiten/goumlrlitzer-park/|archivedate=2015年5月24日}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Drugs in Berlin: Greens Push Weed Legalization in Park|url=http://www.spiegel.de/international/germany/goerlitzer-park-drug-problem-new-push-for-legal-cannabis-sales-a-921738.html|accessdate=24 May 2015|publisher=Spiegel Online|date=September 12, 2013|first1=Hendrik|last1=Ternieden|first2=Anna-Lena|last2=Roth}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Paterson|first1=Tony|title=Kreuzberg: Berlin's hip district becomes a battleground between dealers and gentrification|url=http://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/europe/kreuzberg-berlins-hip-district-becomes-a-battleground-between-dealers-and-gentrification-9909118.html|accessdate=24 May 2015|work=The Independent|date=7 December 2014}}</ref>。公園的西北角是[[格爾利茨站_(柏林地鐵)|格爾利茨站]]。公園設立於1980年代，在此之前這裡曾是一座火車站。{{Fact|這裡也是一個購買[[大麻|大麻]]的熱點。}}

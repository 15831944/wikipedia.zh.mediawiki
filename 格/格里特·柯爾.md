'''格里特·艾倫·柯爾'''（{{lang-en|Gerrit Alan Cole}}, {{bd|1990年|9月8日|}}），為2011年選秀狀元，是一名右投手，現效力於休士頓太空人隊。

==棒球生涯==
===匹茲堡海盜===
在海盜隊的主力選手James McDonald及Wandy Rodríguez受傷之後，柯爾就在2011年6月13日被叫上大聯盟，<ref name=debut>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20130608&content_id=50023430&vkey=news_mlb&c_id=mlb |title=Pirates top prospect Gerrit Cole set to make Major League debut vs. Giants | MLB.com: News |publisher=Mlb.mlb.com |first=Tom|last=Singer|date=June 8, 2013 |accessdate=October 11, 2013}}</ref> <ref name=game5>{{cite web|author=John Perrotto, Special for USA TODAY Sports |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/mlb/2013/10/08/gerrit-cole-adam-wainwright-game-5-starters/2948873/ |title=Gerrit Cole on Game 5: 'Situation you dream about' |publisher=Usatoday.com |date=October 9, 2013 |accessdate=October 14, 2013}}</ref>並在面對首位打者就用3顆球送出三振，並在首打席就擊出2分打點二壘安打。他成為1907年的Nick Maddox之後，第一位在先發前四場皆奪勝的海盜投手。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sports.yahoo.com/news/pirates-cole-wins-4th-straight-022427468--mlb.html|title=Pirates' Cole wins 4th straight to start career – Yahoo! Sports|date=June 29, 2013|work=[[Yahoo!|Yahoo!]]|accessdate=June 29, 2013}}</ref>也是大聯盟40年來第5人。<ref>{{cite press release|title=ESPN SportsCenter per Elias Sports Bureau|date=June 28, 2013}}</ref>

2013年9月，柯爾獲選為國聯單月最佳新秀。那個月他4勝0敗，防禦率僅1.69。<ref>{{cite press release|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20130930&content_id=62245710&vkey=pr_mlb&c_id=mlb |title=Gerrit Cole of the Pittsburgh Pirates voted National League Rookie of the Month for September | MLB.com: News |publisher=Mlb.mlb.com |date=September 30, 2013 |accessdate=October 14, 2013}}</ref>後來他2013年整季10勝7敗，防禦率3.22。2013年國聯分區賽，柯爾於第二場先發，主投6局5K，被擊出2安失一分拿下勝投。不過在第五戰不敵完投9局僅失一分的[[亞當·溫賴特|亞當·溫賴特]]，最終海盜無緣進軍國聯冠軍賽。<ref>{{cite web|last=Baxter |first=Kevin |url=http://www.latimes.com/sports/baseball/mlb/dodgers/la-sp-cardinals-pirates-20131010,0,5883300.story |title=Cardinals eliminate Pirates, look to take on Dodgers in NLCS opener Friday in St. Louis |publisher=latimes.com |date=October 9, 2013 |accessdate=October 14, 2013}}</ref>

2014年9月7日，柯爾面對芝加哥小熊隊投手Blake Parker擊出生涯首轟。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hardballtalk.nbcsports.com/2014/09/07/video-gerrit-cole-hit-a-no-doubt-home-run-the-first-of-his-career|title=Video: Gerrit Cole hit a no-doubt home run, the first of his career – HardballTalk|work=nbcsports.com|accessdate=February 18, 2015}}</ref> 9月23日，柯爾擊敗Alex Wood，助海盜擠下勇士進軍季後賽。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.post-gazette.com/sports/pirates/2014/09/23/Pirates-clinch-postseason-berth-with-3-2-win/stories/201409230232 |title=Time to celebrate: Pirates clinch second consecutive postseason berth |first=Bill|last=Brink|work=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|date=September 23, 2014|accessdate=September 24, 2014}}</ref>不過海盜在外卡驟死賽就輸給巨人。

2015年4月，柯爾拿下國聯單月最佳投手，該月他4勝0敗，防禦率1.76。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wpxi.com/news/sports/pirates-ace-gerrit-cole-named-nl-pitcher-month/nk8nm/|title=Pirates ace Gerrit Cole named NL Pitcher of the Month|work=wpxi.com|accessdate=May 7, 2015}}</ref>海盜連續第三年進軍季後賽，他們決定在外卡戰派出柯爾對決小熊隊的[[傑克·艾瑞亞特|傑克·艾瑞亞特]]，不過柯爾只投5局就被敲出2轟，掉了4分。反觀艾瑞亞特完投完封9局送出11K。海盜再度在外卡戰中箭落馬。<ref>[http://www.cbssports.com/mlb/gametracker/boxscore/MLB_20151007_CHC@PIT CBS Sports]</ref>

2016年7月27日，柯爾面對西雅圖水手送出個人生涯首次完投9局。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.upi.com/Sports_News/2016/07/28/Gerrit-Cole-pitches-shutout-Pittsburgh-Pirates-rock-Seattle-Mariners/3141469680740/ |title=Gerrit Cole pitches shutout; Pittsburgh Pirates rock Seattle Mariners|first=Shelly|last=Anderson|publisher=''The Sports Xchange''|date=July 27, 2016|accessdate=July 27, 2016}}</ref>
===休士頓太空人===
2018年1月13日，海盜隊用柯爾向太空人交易Joe Musgrove, Michael Feliz, Colin Moran和Jason Martin。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/astros-land-gerrit-cole-in-trade-with-pirates/c-264677578|title=Astros land Cole in 5-player deal with Pirates|work=mlb.com|date=January 13, 2018|accessdate=January 13, 2018|first=Brian|last=McTaggart}}</ref>

在他穿上太空人球衣的初登板，他就主投7局送出11K僅失1分。<ref>{{cite web|title=Gerrit Cole twirls gem, fans 11 Rangers to win debut|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/astros-gerrit-cole-strikes-out-11-in-debut/c-270395828|publisher=MLB|accessdate=April 1, 2018}}</ref>

4月29日，柯爾在面對運動家時送出單場12K，以總計61K打破了太空人投手在四月的三振紀錄。在太空人的第一個月，柯爾2勝1敗，防禦率僅1.73。<ref>{{cite web|title=Astros' Gerrit Cole: Strikes Out 12|url=https://www.cbssports.com/fantasy/baseball/news/astros-gerrit-cole-strikes-out-12/|publisher=cbssports.com|accessdate=May 2, 2018}}</ref>

5月4日，柯爾面對響尾蛇投出單場16K，並投出生涯首次完封勝。<ref>{{cite web|title=Gerrit Cole strikes out 16 in one-hitter against Diamondbacks|url=https://www.mlb.com/news/gerrit-cole-has-16-strikeouts-in-1st-shutout/c-275335554|publisher=MLB|accessdate=May 4, 2018}}</ref>
==個人生活==
柯爾從小就是洋基迷，他最愛洋基傳奇球星[[盧·賈里格|盧·賈里格]]及已退役後援投手[[馬里安諾·李維拉|馬里安諾·李維拉]]。

柯爾的老婆艾咪·克勞佛，為舊金山巨人游擊手Brandon Crawford的妹妹。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.post-gazette.com/sports/pirates/2015/07/16/Pirates-Gerrit-Cole-engaged-to-former-UCLA-softball-player-pittsburgh/stories/201507160184|title=Pirates' Gerrit Cole engaged to former UCLA softball player|first=Amy|last=Brooks|work=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|date=July 16, 2015|accessdate=October 5, 2015}}</ref>

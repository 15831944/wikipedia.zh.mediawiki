'''格拉斯哥卡利多尼安大學'''（Glasgow Caledonian University）位于[[苏格兰|苏格兰]]的[[格拉斯哥|格拉斯哥]]。此大学的前身是一所设于1875年的学院，当时有110位学生。大學歷史可追溯到1875年，根據1993年的一份[[國會法案|國會法案]]創立。按學生人數是蘇格蘭最大的大學之一。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.gcu.ac.uk/theuniversity/aboutglasgowcaledonian/abouttheuniversity/ | title = About the University | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = GCU}}</ref>

常列在英國[[新大學|新大學]]的前十位，<ref name="worldranking">{{cite web | url = http://worldranking.blogspot.com/2009/12/top-10-new-universities-in-uk.html | title = Top 10 New Universities in the UK | accessdate = 2013-02-11 | author = World Ranking Guide}}</ref><ref name="glasgowstudentsportcity">{{cite web | url = http://www.glasgowstudentsportcity.com/education-network/glasgow-caledonian-university | title = Glasgow Caledonian University | accessdate = 2013-02-11 | author = Glasgow Student Sport City}}</ref> 全球排名在400位以內。<ref name='University Alliance'>{{cite web | url = http://www.unialliance.ac.uk/site/member/glasgow-caledonian/ | title = Glasgow Caledonian University | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = University Alliance}}</ref><ref name='Salford'>{{cite web | url = http://staff.salford.ac.uk/news/details/2072%3E | title = John Wilson joins Glasgow Caledonian | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = Salford University}}</ref><ref name='Scottish Government'>{{cite web | url = http://www.scotland.gov.uk/Resource/Doc/345511/0114977.pdf | title = Response to Scottish Government Green Paper on Higher Education | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = Scottish Government}}</ref> 是一座有創新活力的大學。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://archive.scottish.parliament.uk/business/businessBulletin/bb-09/bb-07-27f.htm | title = Scottish Parliament Business Bulletin No. 115/2009 | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = Scottish Parliament}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.guardian.co.uk/education/2009/may/10/universityguide-glasgow-caledonian-uni | title = University Guide 2013: Glasgow Caledonian University | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = Guardian}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.scotland.gov.uk/News/Speeches/Speeches/First-Minister/standrewsday07 | title = First Minister St Andrew's Day Speech 2007 | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = Scottish Government}}</ref>

2012年[[諾貝爾和平獎|諾貝爾和平獎]]得主[[穆罕默德·尤納斯|穆罕默德·尤納斯]]被選為大學[[校監|校監]]，是第一位被選為蘇格蘭大學校監的非英國人。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.gcu.ac.uk/yunus/principalsspeech/ | title = Chancellor's Appointment - Principal's Speech | accessdate = 2012-12-30 | author = GCU | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://archive.is/20130620001939/http://www.gcu.ac.uk/yunus/principalsspeech/ | archivedate = 2013-06-20 }}</ref>

2013年英國[[高等教育統計局|高等教育統計局]]將其畢業生的就業能力列為全蘇格蘭第二，全英第十一。<ref name="HESA Employability">{{cite web | url = http://www.hesa.ac.uk/content/view/2912/393/ | title = 2011/12 Performance Indicators - Employment of Graduates | accessdate = 2013-07-11 | author = HESA}}</ref> 同年國際學生滿意度為全英第二。<ref name="internationalstudents">{{cite web | url = http://www.gcu.ac.uk/newsevents/news/article.php?id=65910 | title = GCU second top in UK for international student satisfaction | accessdate = 2013-11-02 | author = GCU}}</ref>

==歷史==

該大學的歷史可以追溯到1875年成立的格拉斯哥女王學院（'''The Queen's College, Glasgow'''）和1971年成立的格拉斯哥技校（'''Glasgow Polytechnic'''）。

女王學院主要提供[[家政學|家政學]]教育，1975年在百年慶典上接受來自[[伊莉莎白二世|伊莉莎白二世]]的皇家榮譽，伊莉莎白二世本人在1944年開始成為該大學的贊助人。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.gashe.ac.uk:443/isaar/C0774.html | title = Queen's College, Glasgow | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = GASHE}}</ref>

格拉斯哥技术院校曾是蘇格蘭最大的工程學文憑及學位提供者之一。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.gashe.ac.uk:443/isaar/C0868.html | title = Glasgow College of Technology | accessdate = 2012-09-05 | author = GASHE}}</ref>

1994年4月1日兩機構合併形成格拉斯哥卡利多尼安大學，卡利多尼安一詞即[[喀里多尼亞|Caledonia]]，今蘇格蘭的詩體拉丁名。

==校區==
[[File:GCU_Campus_View.png|thumb]]

格拉斯哥卡利多尼安大學的校區風格較為現代化，坐落於格拉斯哥市中心。2010年在[[倫敦|倫敦]]增設研究生院，也是第一個這樣做的蘇格蘭大學。大學校園採用節能設計。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.clarke-energy.com/2013/glasgow-caledonian-university-chp-district-heating-scheme/ | title = Glasgow Caledonian University CHP & District Heating Scheme | accessdate = 2013-09-22 | author = Clarke Energy}}</ref>

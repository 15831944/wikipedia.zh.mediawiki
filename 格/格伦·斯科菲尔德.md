'''格伦·A·斯科菲尔德'''（{{lang-en|Glen A. Schofield}}）是[[Sledgehammer_Games|Sledgehammer Games]]的联合创始人兼总经理<ref name="Sledgehammer People">{{cite web|url=http://www.sledgehammergames.com/studio/people|work=Inside the Studio|title=Executive Team|publisher=Sledgehammer Games|accessdate=2012-11-28|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121127131215/http://www.sledgehammergames.com/studio/people|archivedate=2012-11-27}}</ref>。他曾任[[维瑟罗游戏|EA维瑟罗游戏]]的副总裁兼总经理，还是[[第三人称射击游戏|第三人称射击游戏]]《[[死亡空间_(游戏)|死亡空间]]》的创作者和执行制作人。斯科菲尔德受过美术训练，开发了50多部游戏，题材从儿童到动作不等，这些游戏总收入超过30亿美元<ref name=GamerFitNation>{{cite news|title=True Gamer of the Week: Glen Schofield|last=Figaro|first=Brittany|url=http://gamerfitnation.com/2011/08/true-gamer-of-the-week-glen-schofield/|accessdate=2012-11-28|newspaper=GamerFitNation|date=2011-08-04|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121018173708/http://gamerfitnation.com/2011/08/true-gamer-of-the-week-glen-schofield/|archivedate=2012-10-18}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Rising Star Award: Glen Schofield|url=http://www.ggu.edu/press-room/profiles/alumni/glen-schofield|work=Profiles|publisher=Golden Gate University|accessdate=2012-11-29}}</ref><ref name="Digital Trends profile">{{cite news|last=Flemming|first=Ryan|title=Inside Sledgehammer Games and the Biggest Gamble You Never Knew About|url=http://www.digitaltrends.com/gaming/inside-sledgehammer-games-and-the-biggest-gamble-you-never-heard-of/|accessdate=2013-03-02|newspaper=Digital Trends|date=2013-02-16}}</ref>。

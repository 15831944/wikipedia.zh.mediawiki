'''遲發性肌肉痛'''（{{Lang-en|'''delayed onset muscle soreness''', '''DOMS'''}}）是一種運動之後24—72小時出現的肌肉痠痛或不舒適。通常會持續兩至三日。
==原因==
舊式理論普遍認為該痛症為[[乳酸|乳酸]]積聚所引起，但此说法已经基本被否定。因为也能产生乳酸堆积的肌纤维的收缩性运动不会造成遲發性肌肉痛；<ref name="Armstrong 529–38"/>只有肌纤维的伸展性运动才会造成此症。此外，多项对乳酸的研究表明，运动后一小时内乳酸就会降低到正常水平，因此不能在更晚的时候引发疼痛。<ref name="Cheung 2003 145–64"/>

近期醫學研究證實真正起因為肌肉伸展時，因為不習慣運動量，[[肌肉纖維|肌肉纖維]]有微細損傷。<ref>Roth, S. (2006, January 23).　[http://www.sciam.com/print_version.cfm?articleID=000E100E-25C3-13D1-A5C383414B7F0000 Why does lactic acid build up in muscles? And why does it cause soreness?] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071021022131/http://sciam.com/print_version.cfm?articleID=000E100E-25C3-13D1-A5C383414B7F0000 |date=2007-10-21 }} ScientificAmerica.com. Retrieved on July 24, 2006.</ref>

另有一種說法是痛症並不是因為[[肌肉細胞|肌肉細胞]]受損，而是肌內強化過程之中細胞膨脹，壓住[[神經|神經]]、[[血管|血管]]，引致延遲性肌肉痠痛。<ref>Connolly, D. A., Sayers, S. P. & McHugh, M. P. (2003) [http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/query.fcgi?cmd=Retrieve&db=PubMed&list_uids=12580677&dopt=Abstract Treatment and prevention of delayed onset muscle soreness (abstract.)] Journal of Strength Conditioning Research, 17(1):197-208. Retrieved from PubMed.gov on July 24, 2006.</ref> <ref>Szymanski, D. (2003). [http://apt.allenpress.com/aptonline/?request=get-abstract&issn=1533-4295&volume=023&issue=04&page=0007 Recommendations for the avoidance of delayed-onset muscle soreness.]{{dead link|date=2018年4月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} Strength and Conditioning Journal 23(4): 7–13.</ref><ref>Ji-Guo, Y. (2003). [http://www.diva-portal.org/umu/reports/abstract.xsql?dbid=105 Re-evaluation of exercise-induced muscle soreness: an immunohistochemical and ultrastructural study.] Abstract of unpublished doctoral dessertation, Umeyå Universittet, Sweden. ISBN 91-7305-503-4</ref> 

DOMS在1902年被{{tsl|en|Theodore Hough}}指出是肌肉断裂的结果。<ref>{{cite journal|last=Hough|first=Theodore|year=1902|title=Ergographic studies in muscular soreness|journal=[[American_Journal_of_Physiology|American Journal of Physiology]]|volume=1902|issue=7|pages=76–92|doi=10.1080/23267224.1902.10649879}}; {{cite journal|pmc=2048417|pmid=19971340 | volume=5 | title=ERGOGRAPHIC STUDIES IN MUSCULAR FATIGUE AND SORENESS|year=1900|journal=J Boston Soc Med Sci|pages=81–92|author=Hough T}}</ref>这些断裂发生在[[肌原纤维节|肌原纤维节]]在Z轴的显微[[病变|病变]]。<ref name="Armstrong 529–38">{{cite journal|last=Armstrong|first=RB|title=Mechanisms of exercise-induced delayed onset muscular soreness: a brief review|journal=Medicine and science in sports and exercise|date=December 1984|volume=16|issue=6|pages=529–38|pmid=6392811|doi=10.1249/00005768-198412000-00002}}</ref> 被归因于肌肉拉伸运动（eccentric exercise）时肌肉伸展而增加的张力。<ref name="Gulick 145–52">{{cite journal|last=Gulick|first=DT|author2=Kimura, IF |author3=Sitler, M |author4=Paolone, A |author5= Kelly, JD |title=Various treatment techniques on signs and symptoms of delayed onset muscle soreness|journal=Journal of athletic training|date=April 1996|volume=31|issue=2|pages=145–52|pmid=16558388 |pmc=1318445}}</ref>这会引起[[肌动蛋白|肌动蛋白]]与[[肌凝蛋白|肌凝蛋白]]的桥式交叉（cross-bridge）在松弛之前的分离，最终导致剩余活动的{{tsl|en|motor units|运动单位}}承受更大的张力。<ref name="Gulick 145–52"/>这增加了加重恶化的风险，损害了[[肌原纤维节|肌原纤维节]]。当在这些结构上发生{{tsl|en|microtrauma|微创伤}}时，肌肉组织中的{{tsl|en|nociceptor|痛觉感受器}}受到刺激引发了痛觉。<ref name="Cheung 2003 145–64">{{cite journal|last=Cheung|first=K|author2=Hume, P |author3=Maxwell, L |title=Delayed onset muscle soreness: treatment strategies and performance factors|journal=Sports medicine (Auckland, N.Z.)|year=2003|volume=33|issue=2|pages=145–64|pmid=12617692|doi=10.2165/00007256-200333020-00005}}</ref>

另一项对DOMS引发疼痛的解释是"[[酶|酶]]{{tsl|en|Efflux (microbiology)|外排 (微生物学)|外排}}"理论。微创伤后，正常存储于[[肌浆网|肌浆网]]的[[钙|钙]]在受损肌肉上蓄积。[[细胞呼吸作用|细胞呼吸作用]]被抑制，所需的主动运输钙回到[[肌浆网|肌浆网]]的[[三磷酸腺苷|三磷酸腺苷]]减慢。累积的钙 激活了[[蛋白酶|蛋白酶]]与[[磷脂酶|磷脂酶]]导致肌蛋白分解与变性。<ref>{{cite journal|last=Stauber|first=WT|title=Eccentric action of muscles: physiology, injury, and adaptation|journal=Exercise and sport sciences reviews|year=1989|volume=17|pages=157–85|pmid=2676546|doi=10.1249/00003677-198900170-00008}}</ref> 这引发了[[炎症|炎症]], 痛觉是由于积累的[[组胺|组胺]]、[[前列腺素|前列腺素]]、[[钾|钾]]所致。<ref name="Cheung 2003 145–64"/><ref>{{cite journal|last=Armstrong|first=RB|title=Initial events in exercise-induced muscular injury|journal=Medicine and science in sports and exercise|date=August 1990|volume=22|issue=4|pages=429–35|pmid=2205778|doi=10.1249/00005768-199008000-00002}}</ref>

{{vgname|俠盜獵車手V|en=Grand Theft Auto V}}是一款由[[Rockstar_North|Rockstar North]]开发，[[Rockstar_Games|Rockstar Games]]发行的[[開放世界|開放世界]][[动作冒险游戏|动作冒险游戏]]。玩家可以自由漫游虚构的圣安地列斯州（以[[南加利福尼亚州|南加利福尼亚州]]为蓝本），及其开放的乡村和虚构的城市[[洛圣都|洛圣都]]（以洛杉矶为蓝本）。游戏于2011年10月25日公布<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.gamespot.com/articles/rockstar-announces-gta-v/1100-6342136/ |title=Rockstar announces GTA V |date=2011-10-25 |publisher=[[GameSpot|GameSpot]]. [[CBS互動|CBS Interactive]] |accessdate=2012-03-15 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131022031407/http://www.gamespot.com/articles/rockstar-announces-gta-v/1100-6342136/ |archivedate=2013-10-22 }}</ref>，受到广泛关注<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2012/mar/09/grand-theft-auto-bang-bang-youre-dead |title=Bang, bang, you're dead: how Grand Theft Auto stole Hollywood's thunder |last=Poole |first=Steven |date=2012-03-09 |newspaper=[[衛報|The Guardian]] |publisher=Guardian Media Group |accessdate=2012-04-03 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130926211936/http://www.theguardian.com/technology/2012/mar/09/grand-theft-auto-bang-bang-youre-dead |archivedate=2013-09-26 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://news.cnet.com/8301-10797_3-57414909-235/how-grand-theft-auto-changed-video-games-and-the-world/ |title=How Grand Theft Auto changed video games (and the world) |date=2012-04-17 |last=Terdiman |first=Daniel |publisher=[[CNET|cnet]]. UGO Networks |accessdate=2012-05-05 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130517203747/http://news.cnet.com/8301-10797_3-57414909-235/how-grand-theft-auto-changed-video-games-and-the-world/ |archivedate=2013-05-17 }}</ref>。在2012年[[斯派克电子游戏大奖|斯派克电子游戏大奖]]上，本作获得最受期待游戏奖<ref name=spike2012>{{cite web |last=Video Game Awards |first= |title=Every VGA Winner From Years Past |url=http://www.spike.com/articles/6aoa62/vgx-every-vga-winner-from-years-past |publisher=[[Spike_(电视频道)|Spike]] |accessdate=2013-11-10 |date=2011-12-13 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131118192232/http://www.spike.com/articles/6aoa62/vgx-every-vga-winner-from-years-past |archivedate=2013-11-18 }}</ref>。游戏于2013年9月17日在[[PlayStation_3|PlayStation 3]]和[[Xbox_360|Xbox 360]]平台发行，2014年11月18日在[[PlayStation_4|PlayStation 4]]和[[Xbox_One|Xbox One]]平台发行，2015年4月14日发行[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]]版。评论聚合网站[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]基于PlayStation 3版本的50条评论<ref name="MCPS3">{{cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/playstation-3/grand-theft-auto-v |title=Grand Theft Auto V for PlayStation 3 Reviews |publisher=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]. [[CBS互動|CBS Interactive]] |accessdate=2013-11-15 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131201023319/http://www.metacritic.com/game/playstation-3/grand-theft-auto-v |archivedate=2013-12-01 }}</ref>，Xbox 360版本的58条评论<ref name="MCX360">{{cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/xbox-360/grand-theft-auto-v |title=Grand Theft Auto V for Xbox 360 Reviews |publisher=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]]. [[CBS互動|CBS Interactive]] |accessdate=2013-11-15 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131217183158/http://www.metacritic.com/game/xbox-360/grand-theft-auto-v |archivedate=2013-12-17 }}</ref>，PlayStation 4版本的66条评论<ref name="MCPS4">{{cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/playstation-4/grand-theft-auto-v |title=Grand Theft Auto V for PlayStation 4 Reviews |work=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]] |publisher=[[CBS互動|CBS Interactive]] |accessdate=2014-11-18 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170319100538/http://www.metacritic.com/game/playstation-4/grand-theft-auto-v |archivedate=2017-03-19 }}</ref> 和Xbox One版本的14条评论<ref name="MCXONE">{{cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/game/xbox-one/grand-theft-auto-v |title=Grand Theft Auto V for Xbox One Reviews |work=[[Metacritic|Metacritic]] |publisher=[[CBS互動|CBS Interactive]] |accessdate=2014-11-21 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170320045443/http://www.metacritic.com/game/xbox-one/grand-theft-auto-v |archivedate=2017-03-20 }}</ref>，为游戏打出97分，为“普遍好评”。《侠盗猎车手V》发行后二十四小时内的全球收入超过8.157亿美元，相当于1121万的销量<ref name="guinness">{{cite web |url=http://www.ign.com/articles/2013/10/09/gta-5-currently-holds-seven-guinness-world-records |title=GTA 5 Currently Holds Seven Guinness World Records |last=Karmali |first=Luke |publisher=[[IGN|IGN]]. Ziff Davis |date=2013-10-09 |accessdate=2013-10-10 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131009113036/http://www.ign.com/articles/2013/10/09/gta-5-currently-holds-seven-guinness-world-records |archivedate=2013-10-09 }}</ref>。发行三天后，游戏成为有史以来销量最快的娱乐产品，其销售额超过10亿美元<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ign.com/articles/2013/09/20/gta-5-sales-hit-1-billion-in-three-days |title=GTA 5 Sales Hit $1 Billion in Three Days |last=Goldfarb |first=Andrew |publisher=[[IGN|IGN]]. Ziff Davis |date=2013-09-20 |accessdate=2013-09-21 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130923083915/http://www.ign.com/articles/2013/09/20/gta-5-sales-hit-1-billion-in-three-days |archivedate=2013-09-23 }}</ref>。

游戏出现在多个2013年最佳游戏的年终榜单上，获得了[[CNET|CNET]]<ref name="CNET BVG"/>、[[Edge_(杂志)|Edge]]<ref name="EdgeBG"/>、[[金摇杆奖|金摇杆奖]]<ref name=goldenjoy/>、[[Machinima.com|Machinima]]的Inside Gaming<ref name="InsideGaming2013"/、Spike VGX<ref name="VGX-win"/>、[[偏锋杂志|偏锋杂志]]<ref name="Slant BVG"/>和[[時代雜誌|時代雜誌]]<ref name="Time VG"/>评出的年度游戏。还被Canada.com<ref name="Canada"/>、[[GameSpot|GameSpot]]<ref name="GSXBOX"/>和[[IGN|IGN]]<ref name="IGNXBOX"/>评为最佳Xbox游戏，并获得[[Destructoid|Destructoid]]评出的最佳多平台游戏<ref name="Dest-MULT"/>。[[Inside游戏奖|Inside游戏奖]]将本作评为“最具沉浸感游戏”<ref name="InsideGaming2013"/>，BAFTA为其颁发最佳英国游戏奖<ref name="bafta"/>。Rockstar Games和Rockstar North赢得了Edge的最佳工作室和最佳开发者奖<ref name="EdgeSOTY"/>，以及第十届英国学院电子游戏大奖的BAFTA学院奖学金奖<ref name="bafta"/>。

游戏内的多种元素获奖。游戏角色[[崔佛·菲利普|崔佛·菲利普]]和[[俠盜獵車手V角色列表|拉玛·戴维斯]]多次获提名最佳角色，而[[Giant_Bomb|Giant Bomb]]则认为拉玛是游戏中最好的角色<ref name="GiantBomb"/>。游戏的音乐还获得了Spike VGX<ref name="VGX-win"/>、[[Hardcore_Gamer|Hardcore Gamer]]<ref name="HG-Sound"/>和[[每日电讯报|每日电讯报]]<ref name="Telegraph"/>的奖项。在线多人模式《[[俠盜獵車手Online|俠盜獵車手Online]]》赢得了[[GameTrailers|GameTrailers]]<ref name="GameTrailers"/>和[[英国电影和电视艺术学院|BAFTA]]<ref name="bafta"/>的最佳多人游戏奖，以及IGN的最佳Xbox 360多人游戏奖<ref name="IGN-XBOXMultiplayer"/>。《Online》也被[[Game_Revolution|Game Revolution]]<ref name="GR-Disappoint"/>和Hardcore Gamer<ref name="HG-Disappoint"/>提名最受失望奖。《侠盗猎车手V》获得了每日电讯报电子游戏奖中的最佳技术成就奖<ref name="Telegraph"/>，在第十四届[[游戏开发者选择奖|游戏开发者选择奖]]中获最佳技术奖<ref name="GameDevelopersAwards"/>。游戏的图像和艺术设计还获得了IGN<ref name="IGN-XBOXGraphics"/>、《每日电讯报》<ref name="Telegraph"/>和BAFTA的奖项，以及游戏开发者选择奖的提名<ref name="GameDevelopersAwards"/>。在IGN的2013年度最佳奖中，游戏获得了多个奖项，包括最佳Xbox 360图像<ref name="IGN-XBOXGraphics"/>、最佳Xbox 360音效<ref name="IGN-XBOXSound"/>以及Xbox 360和PlayStation 3最佳动作游戏等<ref name="IGN-Action"/>。

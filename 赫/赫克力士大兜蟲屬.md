'''赫克力士大兜蟲屬'''（''Dynastes''）是[[金龜子|金龜科]][[兜蟲亞科|兜蟲亞科]]的一屬，又稱為'''大兜蟲屬'''<ref>Hope, F.W. 1837. On the ''Golofa'' Beetle of Venezuela and its allied species. Transactions of the Entomological Society of London 2(1): 42–45.</ref>。

== 物種 ==
根據2018年黃仁磐博士的論文，本屬可分為2亞屬<ref>{{Cite journal|title=The Hercules beetles (subgenus ''Dynastes'', genus ''Dynastes'', Dynastidae): a revisionary study based on the integration of molecular, morphological, ecological, and geographic analyses|author=|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/320432668_The_Hercules_beetles_subgenus_Dynastes_genus_Dynastes_Dynastidae_a_revisionary_study_based_on_the_integration_of_molecular_morphological_ecological_and_geographic_analyses|last=Pan|first=Huang, Jen|date=2017-09-15|journal=|issue=|doi=|others=|year=|volume=|page=|language=|issn=0076-8405|pmid=}}</ref>：

* ''Theogenes'' 亞屬
** [[海神大兜蟲|海神大兜蟲]] ''Dynastes neptunus neptunus'' (Quensel, 1805)
** 海神大兜蟲 rouchei亞種 ''Dynastes neptunus rouchei'' (Nagai, 2005)
**[[撒旦大兜蟲|撒旦大兜蟲]] ''Dynastes satanas'' (Moser, 1909)
* ''Dynastes'' 亞屬
** 白兜蟲種群（White Hercules Group）

*** [[墨龍白兜蟲|墨龍白兜蟲]]''Dynastes moroni'' (Nagai, 2005)

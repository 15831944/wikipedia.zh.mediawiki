'''欧洲政党'''（{{lang-en|European political party}}）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.parties-and-elections.eu/content.html|title=Parties & Elections in Europe|author=Wolfram Nordsieck|accessdate=11 July 2015}}</ref> 是运行于[[欧洲|欧洲]]各国和[[欧洲联盟|欧洲联盟]]机构内部的一种政党组织。它们受欧盟法律监督并得到欧盟的拨款作为活动经费。它们往往由各国政党共同创建，而非个人。除了受欧盟正式认可的欧洲政党外，还有一些性质类似于欧洲政党的欧洲范围内的国际政党组织存在。<ref>{{cite web|last1=Lelieveldt|first1=Herman|last2=Princen|first2=Sebatiaan|title=The politics of the European Union|url=https://books.google.com/books?hl=en&lr=&id=U0rpNQWakqkC&oi=fnd&pg=PR3&dq=The+politics+of+the+European+Union&ots=jNyVA_ADef&sig=ZqhqssrMibR4M5K_G-pzLTiiWYc#v=onepage&q=The%20politics%20of%20the%20European%20Union&f=false|website=Google Books|publisher=Cambridge University Press|accessdate=25 April 2015}}</ref>

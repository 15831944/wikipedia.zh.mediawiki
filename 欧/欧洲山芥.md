'''欧洲山芥'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Barbarea vulgaris'''''}}）为[[十字花科|十字花科]][[山芥属|山芥属]]下的一个种。

== 特徵 ==

歐洲山芥中包含的化學物質有[[皂素|皂素]]、[[類黃酮|類黃酮]]<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1021/jf200412c | title = Polymorphism for Novel Tetraglycosylated Flavonols in an Eco-model Crucifer, Barbarea vulgaris | year = 2011 | last1 = Dalby-Brown | first1 = Lea | last2 = Olsen | first2 = Carl Erik | last3 = Nielsen | first3 = Jens Kvist | last4 = Agerbirk | first4 = Niels | journal = Journal of Agricultural and Food Chemistry | volume = 59 | issue = 13 | pages = 6947–56 | pmid = 21615154}}</ref> 和[[芥子油苷|芥子油苷]]。<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/j.phytochem.2011.01.034 | title = Isoferuloyl derivatives of five seed glucosinolates in the crucifer genus Barbarea | year = 2011 | last1 = Agerbirk | first1 = Niels | last2 = Olsen | first2 = Carl Erik | journal = Phytochemistry | volume = 72 | issue = 7 | pages = 610–23 | pmid = 21354584}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/S0031-9422(02)00750-1 | title = Glucosinolates, flea beetle resistance, and leaf pubescence as taxonomic characters in the genus Barbarea (Brassicaceae) | year = 2003 | last1 = Agerbirk | first1 = Niels | last2 = Ørgaard | first2 = Marian | last3 = Nielsen | first3 = Jens Kvist | journal = Phytochemistry | volume = 63 | pages = 69–80 | pmid = 12657300 | issue = 1}}</ref>

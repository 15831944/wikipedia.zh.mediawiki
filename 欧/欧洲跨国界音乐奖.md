'''欧洲跨国界音乐奖'''(European Border Breakers Awards，EBBA)旨在表彰过去一年在除了自己国家外，在欧洲其他国家也取得成功的十名音乐人或乐队。该奖自2004年起每年举办一次。<ref>[http://ec.europa.eu/culture/our-programmes-and-actions/prizes/european-border-breakers-awards_en.htm European Border Breakers Awards (EBBA)]</ref>其获奖者包括[[愛黛兒|愛黛兒]], [[Aura_Dione|Aura Dione]], [[Anna_Calvi|Anna Calvi]], [[東京飯店酷兒|東京飯店酷兒]], [[The_Baseballs|The Baseballs]], [[Zaz|Zaz]], [[Lykke_Li|Lykke Li]], [[Milow|Milow]], [[凱特·瑪露|凱特·瑪露]], [[戴米恩·莱斯|戴米恩·莱斯]], [[Caro_Emerald|Caro Emerald]], [[司徒迈|司徒迈]]和[[蒙福之子樂團|蒙福之子樂團]]等。<ref>[http://www.subba-cultcha.com/news/article.php?contentID=22143 "Winners of the 2011 European Border Breakers Awards"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101207231600/http://www.subba-cultcha.com/news/article.php?contentID=22143 |date=2010-12-07 }}, Subba-cultcha.com.  2011年9月19日</ref>

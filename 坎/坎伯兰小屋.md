'''坎伯兰小屋'''（Cumberland Lodge）是一座17世纪皇家乡间别墅，位于[[温莎城堡|温莎城堡]]以南3.5英里，[[温莎大公园|温莎大公园]]内。1947年，乔治六世将其出租给教育慈善基金会，举行会议、讲座和讨论，关心社会面临的紧迫问题。其主要受益者是大学生，每年有4,000学生来访。自1986年以来已举行过100多次会议。议题包括：
* 人民是警察?<ref>[http://www.cumberlandlodge.ac.uk/Resources/Cumberland%20Lodge/Past%20Conferences%20Reports/People%20are%20the%20Police%20Conference%20Report.pdf The People Are The police? Cumberland Lodge Report]{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>
* 毒品及危害<ref>[http://www.cumberlandlodge.ac.uk/Resources/Cumberland%20Lodge/Past%20Conferences%20Reports/Drugs%20and%20Harm%20Summary%20Report.pdf Drugs and Harm, Cumberland Lodge Report] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110409231723/http://www.cumberlandlodge.ac.uk/Resources/Cumberland%20Lodge/Past%20Conferences%20Reports/Drugs%20and%20Harm%20Summary%20Report.pdf |date=2011-04-09 }}</ref>
* 第三部门的未来<ref>[http://www.cumberlandlodge.ac.uk/Resources/Cumberland%20Lodge/Report%20Images/Rosie%20Chapman%20on%20the%20Third%20Sector.pdf The Future of the Third Sector, Cumberland Lodge Report]{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>
* 宗教和新闻<ref>[http://www.cumberlandlodge.ac.uk/Resources/Cumberland%20Lodge/Past%20Conferences%20Reports/Religion%20and%20the%20News%20Summary%20Report.pdf Religion and the News, Cumberland Lodge Report]{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>
* 监督和执法的限度<ref>[http://www.cumberlandlodge.ac.uk/Resources/Cumberland%20Lodge/Past%20Conferences%20Reports/Cumberland%20Lodge%20Police%202010%20Report.pdf Surveillance and the Limits of Law Enforcement, Cumberland Lodge Report]{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>
* 改变生活的期望<ref>[http://www.cumberlandlodge.ac.uk/Resources/Cumberland%20Lodge/Past%20Conferences%20Reports/Changing%20Expectations%20of%20Life%20Report.pdf Changing Expectations of Life, Cumberland Lodge Report]{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

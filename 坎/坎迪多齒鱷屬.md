'''坎迪多齒鱷屬'''（屬名：''Candidodon''）是[[鱷形超目|鱷形超目]][[諾托鱷類|諾托鱷類]]的ㄧ屬，生存於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]早期的[[巴西|巴西]]。屬名意為「Cândido的牙齒」，是以巴西科學家Cândido Simões Ferreira為名。

坎迪多齒鱷的最明顯特徵，是[[顎骨|顎骨]]的內鼻孔非常長<ref>de Andrade, M. B., Bertini, R. J. and Pinheiro, A. E. P. (2006) Observations on the palate and choanae structures in Mesoeucrocodylia (Archosauria, Crocodylomorpha): phylogenetic implications. ''Revista Brasileira de Paleontologia'' '''9'''(3):323-332.</ref>。在2004年，坎迪多齒鱷被歸類於新建立的[[坎迪多齒鱷科|坎迪多齒鱷科]]（Candidodontidae）<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/S1342-937X(05)71079-0 | last1 = Carvalho | first1 = I. | last2 = Ribeiro | first2 = L. C. B. | last3 = Avilla | first3 = L. | year = 2004 | title = ''Uberabasuchus terrificus'' sp. nov., a new Crocodylomorpha from the Bauru Basin (Upper Cretaceous), Brazil | url = | journal = Gondwana Research | volume = 7 | issue = 4| pages = 975–1002 }}</ref>。坎迪多齒鱷最初被認為是[[馬里利亞鱷|馬里利亞鱷]]的近親。2006年的[[種系發生學|種系發生學]]研究，提出馬里利亞鱷其實是[[科瑪繪鱷|科瑪繪鱷]]的近親，屬於[[科瑪繪鱷科|科瑪繪鱷科]]；而馬里利亞鱷、坎迪多齒鱷其實是遠親<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1206/0003-0082(2006)3512[1:ROTCMO]2.0.CO;2 | last1 = Zaher | first1 = H. | last2 = Pol | first2 = D. | last3 = Carvalho | first3 = A. B. | last4 = Riccomini | first4 = C. | last5 = Campos | first5 = D. | last6 = Navas | first6 = W. | year = 2006 | title = Re-description of the cranial morphology of ''Mariliasuchus amarali'', and its phylogenetic affinities (Crocodyliformes, Notosuchia) | url = | journal = American Museum Novitates | volume = 3512 | issue = | pages = 1–40 }}</ref>。如果這個研究屬實，坎迪多齒鱷科將只有坎迪多齒鱷屬。

'''坎布里亞大學'''（{{lang-en|University of Cumbria}}）是一所位於[[英國|英國]][[坎布里亞郡|坎布里亞郡]]的[[公立大學|公立大學]]，主校區在[[卡萊爾市|卡萊爾市]]。<ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.theguardian.com/education/2005/feb/01/highereducation.newuniversities19922012 | title=Green light for University of Cumbria plans | newspaper=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]] | date=1 February 2005 | last=MacLeod | first=Donald}}</ref><ref name="BBCopens">{{cite web | url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/england/cumbria/6925895.stm | title=County university opens its doors | website=[[BBC_News_Online|BBC News]] | date=1 August 2007}}</ref>在[[蘭卡斯特_(蘭開夏郡)|蘭卡斯特]]、[[安布爾賽德|安布爾賽德]]、[[彭里斯_(坎布里亞郡)|彭里斯]]、[[巴罗弗内斯|巴罗弗内斯]]和[[塔村區|塔村區]]有分校區。成立於2007年，是由[[兰开斯特圣马丁学院|兰开斯特圣马丁学院]]和[[坎布里亞藝術學院|坎布里亞藝術學院]]合併，再加上[[中央蘭開夏大學|中央蘭開夏大學]]的坎布里亞校區組成的。<ref name="BBCopens" /><ref name="Harris report">{{cite web|url=http://www.hefce.ac.uk/news/hefce/2005/cumbrep/ |title=Proposal for a new University of Cumbria welcomed by HEFCE |website=[[The_National_Archives_(United_Kingdom)|The National Archives]] |deadurl=bot: unknown |archiveurl=http://webarchive.nationalarchives.gov.uk/20120118164822/http://www.hefce.ac.uk/news/hefce/2005/cumbrep/ |archivedate=2012-01-18 }}</ref>

== 逝世 ==
2013年10月20日，贾马鲁尔·基拉姆三世因[[多器官衰竭|多器官衰竭]]去世，享年75岁。 在临终前，他请求把自己埋葬在[[苏禄苏丹国|苏禄苏丹国]]的首都棉邦镇（Maimbung）。 他留下了八个孩子及两位遗孀。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rappler.com/nation/41779-sulu-sultan-jamalul-kiram-iii-dies/|title=Sulu Sultan Jamalul Kiram III dies|accessdate=21 October 2013|author=Angela Casauay|date=20 October 2013|work=Agence France-Presse|publisher=Rappler}}</ref> 众多的政治人物向这最后的苏丹表达了敬意，包括[[棉兰老穆斯林自治区|棉兰老穆斯林自治区]]的前总督Mujiv Sabbihi Hataman 和[[蘇祿省|苏禄省]]副省长 Abdusakur Mahail Tan、<ref>{{cite web|url=http://newsinfo.inquirer.net/510807/armm-governor-sulu-vice-governor-extend-condolences-to-kirams-family|title=ARMM governor, Sulu vice governor extend condolences to Kiram’s family|accessdate=21 October 2013|author=Julie S. Alipala|date=20 October 2013|publisher=Philippine Daily Inquirer}}</ref> 菲律宾前统治者夫人[[伊梅尔达·马科斯|伊梅尔达·马科斯]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gmanetwork.com/news/story/331705/news/regions/lack-of-clearance-hampers-burial-before-sundown-for-kiram|title=Lack of clearance hampers burial before sundown for Kiram|accessdate=21 October 2013|date=20 October 2013|publisher=[[GMA_News|GMA News]]}}</ref> 以及 [[馬拉坎南宮|马拉坎南宫]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gmanetwork.com/news/story/331677/news/nation/palace-sympathizes-with-kirams-over-sultan-s-death|title=Palace sympathizes with Kirams over sultan’s death|accessdate=21 October 2013|date=20 October 2013|publisher=GMA News}}</ref>

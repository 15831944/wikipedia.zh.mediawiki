'''贾森·比格斯'''（{{lang-en|Jason Biggs}}，{{bd|1978年|5月12日|||catIdx= B比}}）出生于[[美国|美国]][[新泽西州|新泽西州]][[摩里斯縣_(新澤西州)|摩里斯县]]，为美国演员，以出演《[[美国派|美国派]]》系列电影而知名全美。

== 生平 ==
1978年，比格斯出生于[[美国|美国]][[新泽西州|新泽西州]][[摩里斯縣_(新澤西州)|摩里斯县]]，母亲安吉拉是一个[[护士|护士]]，父亲加里·比格斯，是一家航运公司的经理。<ref> [http://www.teenhollywood.com/d/4484/1038/jason-biggs-is-hot.html TeenHollywood.com – Jason Biggs is Hot]</ref><ref>[http://www.filmreference.com/film/20/Jason-Biggs.html Jason Biggs profile at FilmReference.com]</ref> 
比格斯成长于[[博根县|博根县]]，并且在博根县高中读书。<ref>McKinley, Jesse. [http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=940DE1DA133BF932A05750C0A9649C8B63 "THEATER; Bye, Bye 'American Pie'; Mrs. Robinson Is Calling"], ''[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]'', March 31, 2002. Accessed March 3, 2008. "Unlike that character, Mr. Biggs was proving to be a pretty normal teenager, playing high school tennis and holding a series of menial jobs (flower delivery boy, sandwich maker, kitchen staff at a hospital). In 1996, he graduated from Hasbrouck Heights High School and started at [[New_York_University|New York University]], where he enrolled in the College of Arts and Sciences."</ref> 
读高中时，比格斯擅长[[体育|体育]]，尤其是[[网球|网球]]。<ref>{{cite newspaper|author=Della Cava, Marco R.|title=Acting dreams no longer 'Pie' in the sky Huggable Jason Biggs is on a roll after losing his cinematic virginity|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/USAToday/access/56888405.html?dids=56888405:56888405&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&type=current&date=Jul+25,+2000&author=Marco+R.+della+Cava&pub=USA+TODAY&desc=Acting+dreams+no+longer+'Pie'+in+the+sky+Huggable+Jason+Biggs+is+on+a+roll+after+losing+his+cinematic+virginity&pqatl=google|newspaper=[[USA_Today|USA Today]]|date=July 25, 2000}}</ref> 
比格斯是一个[[意大利裔美国人|意大利裔美国人]]，同时信奉[[天主教|天主教]]。<ref name="italian">[http://www.jewishjournal.com/home/preview.php?id=7272 "A Nice Not-Jewish Boy"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120716221231/http://www.jewishjournal.com/home/preview.php?id=7272 |date=2012-07-16 }}</ref><ref>[http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/film/article462198.ece Jason Biggs profile at ''Times Online'']</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
2008年4月23日，比格斯和珍妮·莫伦结婚，目前，两人居住在[[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]][[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]。<ref>[http://www.starmagazine.com/jason_biggs_married_wedding/news/14136 Jason Biggs gets hitched!]</ref>

'''贾森·布朗'''（{{lang-en|Jason Brown}}，{{bd|1994年|12月15日||}}）生于加利福尼亚州洛杉矶，是一名美国男子花样滑冰运动员，主攻单人滑。他的父亲在一家照明公司工作，母亲则是一名电视制作人。<ref name=IN110721>{{cite web |url= http://www.icenetwork.com/skaters/detail.jsp?id=100826&mode=I |title= Jason Brown |publisher= IceNetwork.com |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110721113012/http://www.icenetwork.com/skaters/detail.jsp?id=100826&mode=I |archivedate= 2011-07-21 |deadurl= yes |accessdate=2019-03-22}}</ref><ref name=DH140211>{{cite news |url= http://www.dailyherald.com/article/20140211/news/140219796/ |title= Highland Park parents celebrate son's Olympic medal |first= Russell |last= Lissau |work= Daily Herald |date=2014-02-11|accessdate=2019-03-22 }}</ref>贾森还有一个姐姐和一个弟弟，贾森在三岁半时，姐姐受邀参加滑冰派对，母亲为了确保姐姐的安全，将三个孩子都带到滑冰课上练习滑冰<ref name=GS110710>{{cite news |url= http://www.goldenskate.com/2011/07/brown-is-ready-for-anything-except-a-haircut/ |title= Brown is ready for anything – except a haircut |first= Elvin |last= Walker |date=2011-07-10 |work= Golden Skate |accessdate=2011-07-11 }}</ref>。

贾森高中毕业于高地公园高中<ref name=IN110721/>。2013年，他被科罗拉多大学斯普林司分校录取<ref name=UCCS131111>{{cite news |url= https://gazette.com/sports/uccs-freshman-jason-brown-in-medal-contention-after-olympics-men/article_3f4a7541-42ad-5f4e-badc-5763530da1f4.html |title=UCCS freshman Jason Brown in medal contention after Olympics men's short program |publisher= The Gazette |date=2014-02-13 |accessdate=2019-03-22}}</ref>。

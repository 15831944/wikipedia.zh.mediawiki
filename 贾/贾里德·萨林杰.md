'''贾里德·萨林杰'''（{{lang-en|'''Jared Sullinger'''}}，{{bd|1992年|3月4日|||Sullinger, Jared}}），美国职业篮球运动员，曾效力于NBA联盟。他在[[2012年NBA选秀|2012年NBA选秀]]中于首轮第21位被选中。

==高中生涯==
他曾入选2010届 [[ESPNU|ESPNU]]100佳，并获评第二名，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://insider.espn.go.com/ncb/recruiting/tracker/espnu100?&season=2010&action=upsell&appRedirect=http%3a%2f%2finsider.espn.go.com%2fncb%2frecruiting%2ftracker%2fespnu100%3f%26season%3d2010 |title=College Basketball Recruiting - ESPNU 100 - ESPN |publisher=Insider.espn.go.com |date= |accessdate=2010-08-26}}</ref>[[Scout.com|Scout.com]]将其评为第四名，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://scouthoops.scout.com/a.z?s=75&p=9&c=4&pid=88&yr=2010 |title=Scout.com: Football Recruiting |publisher=Scouthoops.scout.com |date= |accessdate=2010-08-26}}</ref>而在[[Rivals.com|Rivals.com]]则排名第五。<ref name=rivals>{{cite web|url=http://rivalshoops.rivals.com/viewrank.asp?ra_key=1909 |title=Yahoo Sports: Rivals.com Ranking - Rivals150 for class of 2010 |publisher=Rivalshoops.rivals.com |date= |accessdate=2010-08-26}}</ref>在他高中的最后一年，萨林杰带领Northland High School赢得了全部21场比赛，并在全国高中排名中获得第一。<ref name="dispatch1">{{cite web |url=http://www.dispatch.com/live/content/sports/stories/2010/03/10/naismith-awards.html |title=Northland High School basketball player, father win national Naismith awards |publisher=The Columbus Dispatch |date=2010-03-10 |accessdate=2010-08-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100314065158/http://www.dispatch.com/live/content/sports/stories/2010/03/10/naismith-awards.html |archivedate=2010-03-14 }}</ref> Sullinger played in the 4th annual [[Boost_Mobile|Boost Mobile]] Elite 24 Hoops Classic.<ref>{{cite web |last= |first= |url=http://www.slamonline.com/online/college-hs/college/2009/08/boost-mobile-elite-24-roster-announced/ |title=Boost Mobile Elite 24 Roster Announced |publisher=Slam Online |date=2009-08-05 |accessdate=2010-08-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100704010316/http://www.slamonline.com/online/college-hs/college/2009/08/boost-mobile-elite-24-roster-announced/ |archivedate=2010-07-04 }}</ref>

萨林杰曾入选参加在[[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]][[哥伦布_(俄亥俄州)|哥伦布]]举办的2010麦当劳全美篮球赛，<ref>{{cite web|last= |first= |url=http://www.slamonline.com/online/college-hs/high-school/2010/02/mcdonalds-all-american-game-team-announced/ |title=McDonald’s All-American Game Team Announced |publisher=Slam Online |date=2010-02-11 |accessdate=2010-08-26}}</ref>并与 [[Harrison_Barnes|Harrison Barnes]]获得co-MVP。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.maxpreps.com/news/m2xSkj0QEd-lugAcxJTdpg/mcdonalds-all-american-night--knights-3-pointer-wins-it-for-the-west.htm |title=McDonald's All-American night: Knight's 3-pointer wins it for the West - MaxPreps News |publisher=Maxpreps.com |date=2010-03-31 |accessdate=2010-08-26}}</ref>他也被选入参加2010年在[[波特兰_(俄勒冈州)|波特兰]][[玫瑰园球场|玫瑰园球场]]举办的[[耐克尖峰对抗赛|耐克尖峰对抗赛]]<ref>{{cite web|last= |first= |url=http://www.slamonline.com/online/college-hs/high-school/2010/01/its-the-usa-against-everyone-else/ |title=It’s the USA Against Everyone Else |publisher=Slam Online |date=2010-01-20 |accessdate=2010-08-26}}</ref>以及在[[纽约|纽约]][[麦迪逊广场花园|麦迪逊广场花园]]的2010年乔丹经典篮球赛。<ref>{{cite web|last= |first= |url=http://www.slamonline.com/online/college-hs/high-school/2010/02/jordan-brand-all-american-game-team-announced/ |title=Jordan Brand All-American Game Team Announced |publisher=Slam Online |date=2010-02-18 |accessdate=2010-08-26}}</ref>

==职业生涯==
===波士顿凯尔特人===
2012年6月29，萨林杰在第一轮第21顺位被波士顿凯尔特人选中。7月3日，凯尔特人宣布与萨林杰和其他两名新秀（[[Fab_Melo|Fab Melo]]和[[Kris_Joseph|Kris Joseph]]）签署为期两年的合同。<ref name="SullingerSigns">{{cite web|last=Forsberg |first=Chris |url=http://espn.go.com/blog/boston/celtics/post/_/id/4695851/cs-sign-sullinger-melo-joseph |title=C's sign Sullinger, Melo, Joseph |publisher=ESPN.com |date=July 3, 2012 |accessdate=}}</ref>7月5日，凯尔特人宣布萨林杰入选该队参加奥兰多夏季联赛和2012年NBA夏季联赛的名单。

2012年7月9日，萨林杰在奥兰多举办的夏季联赛中完成首秀，全场12投6中，罚球7中7，在24分钟内共得到20分6个篮板球。

2013年2月1日，萨林杰因背傷整季報銷。

===多倫多暴龍===
塞爾提克替補大前鋒薩林傑透過推特證實自己將加盟暴龍隊。ESPN記者史坦恩（Marc Stein）隨後補充報導，薩林傑簽下1年合約，年薪約600萬美元

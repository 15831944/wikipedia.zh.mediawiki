'''經濟虐待'''（{{lang-en|Economic abuse}}）是一種[[家庭暴力|家庭暴力]]、[[虐待|虐待]]形式，當一個家庭成員或親密伴侶控制另一個家庭成員或伴侶獲得經濟資源時。<ref name ="Adams (2008)">{{cite journal |last=Adams |first=Adrienne E. |last2=Sullivan |first2=Cris M. |last3=Bybee |first3=Deborah |last4=Greeson |first4=Megan R. |title=Development of the Scale of Economic Abuse |journal=Violence Against Women |date=May 2008 |volume=14 |issue=5 |pages=563–588 |doi=10.1177/1077801208315529 |pmid=18408173}}<!--|accessdate=19 Sep 2011--></ref>經濟虐待削弱了受影響者人支出的能力，迫使受影響人在經濟上依賴行為人。<ref name="Adams (2008)" /><ref name="Brewster (2003)2">{{cite journal|title=Power and Control Dynamics in Pre-stalking and Stalking Situations|last=Brewster|first=M. P.|journal=Journal of Family Violence|issue=4|doi=10.1023/A:1024064214054|year=2003|volume=18|pages=207–217}}</ref><ref name="Sanders (2004)">{{cite journal |last=Sanders |first=Cynthia K. |first2=Meg |last2=Schnabel |title=Organizing for Economic Empowerment of Battered Women: Women’s Savings Accounts |work=Center for Social Development, George Warren Brown School of Social Work, Washington University |series=Working Paper No. 04-15 |date=2004 |url=https://csd.wustl.edu/publications/documents/wp04-15.pdf}}</ref>

經濟虐待可以是[[父母|父母]]虐待[[未成年|未成年]]或未有自立能力的[[子女|子女]]、[[婚姻|已婚者]]或[[同居|同居]]伴侶虐待無獨立經濟能力的[[配偶|配偶]]或伴侶，子女虐待[[年老|年老]]父母，或身為家庭[[經濟支柱|經濟支柱]]之成員虐待其他成員等。

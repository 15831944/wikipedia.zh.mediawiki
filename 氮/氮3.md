在[[化學|化學]]中，'''氮3'''（Trinitrogen）又稱為'''疊氮自由基'''，是[[氮气|氮气]]的[[同素異形體|同素異形體]]，於1956年在實驗室中製得<ref name="jin">{{cite journal |last1=Jin |first1=Lin |last2=Yu |first2=Xue-fang |last3=Pang |first3=Jing-lin |last4=Zhang |first4=Shao-wen |last5=Ding |first5=Yi-hong |title=Theoretical Study on the Reactions of the Cyclic Trinitrogen Radical toward Oxygen and Water |journal=The Journal of Physical Chemistry A |date=30 July 2009 |volume=113 |issue=30 |pages=8500–8505 |doi=10.1021/jp810741v|bibcode=2009JPCA..113.8500J }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |last1=Thrush |first1=B. A. |title=The Detection of Free Radicals in the High Intensity Photolysis of Hydrogen Azide |journal=Proceedings of the Royal Society A: Mathematical, Physical and Engineering Sciences |date=10 April 1956 |volume=235 |issue=1200 |pages=143–147 |doi=10.1098/rspa.1956.0071|bibcode=1956RSPSA.235..143T }}</ref>，每個[[分子|分子]]具有三個氮原子，直鏈和環狀的結構皆是可能的<ref name=han>{{cite journal |last1=Hansen |first1=N. |last2=Wodtke |first2=A. M. |title=Velocity Map Ion Imaging of Chlorine Azide Photolysis:  Evidence for Photolytic Production of Cyclic-N3 |journal=The Journal of Physical Chemistry A |date=December 2003 |volume=107 |issue=49 |pages=10608–10614 |doi=10.1021/jp0303319|bibcode=2003JPCA..10710608H }}</ref>。在[[熱力學|熱力學]]方面它具有高能量，或可以作過渡性的能量存儲。
==性質==
直鏈狀的氮3其分子結構具有D<sub>∞h</sub>的二面體群對稱性、其平均鍵長為1.8115 Å；第一個激发态A<sup>2</sup>Σ<sub>u</sub>的能量比基態高約4.56[[電子伏特|電子伏特]]。<ref name=han/>

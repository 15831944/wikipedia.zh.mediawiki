'''氮紅'''或'''偶氮玉紅'''（{{lang|en|Azorubine}}），是一種[[偶氮染料|偶氮染料]]<ref name=Ullmann>{{cite encyclopedia|author1=Klaus Hunger|author2=Peter Mischke|author3=Wolfgang Rieper|author4=Roderich Raue|author5=Klaus Kunde|author6=Aloys Engel|display-authors=3|title=Azo Dyes|encyclopedia=Ullmann’s Encyclopedia of Industrial Chemistry|year=2005|publisher=Wiley-VCH|place=Weinheim|doi=10.1002/14356007.a03_245}}</ref>，通過[[化學合成|化學合成]]作為二鈉鹽產生。在其乾燥狀態下，呈現紅色至栗色。其主要用於主要用於發酵後進行熱處理的食品中。[[E編碼|E編碼]]爲'''E129'''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/compound/6321394?from=summary|title=Pubchem entry|date=2005-11-20|accessdate=2019-04-16|work=Pubchem|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190404183600/https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/compound/6321394?from=summary|archive-date=2019-04-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==應用==
氮紅在[[美國|美國]]於1939年被給予編碼'''[[联邦食品、药品和化妆品法案|FD&C]] Red 40'''，用於外用藥物和化妝品。由於沒有任何一方有興趣就其進行安全研究，它在1963年從名單上除名。其在美國從未用於食品中<ref name=FDAdyecomm>FDA. {{cite web|url=http://www.fda.gov/downloads/AdvisoryCommittees/CommitteesMeetingMaterials/FoodAdvisoryCommittee/UCM248549.pdf|title=Background Document for the Food Advisory Committee: Certified Color Additives in Food and Possible Association with Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder in Children|date=2011-03-30|accessdate=2019-04-16|work=FDA|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151106080629/http://www.fda.gov/downloads/AdvisoryCommittees/CommitteesMeetingMaterials/FoodAdvisoryCommittee/UCM248549.pdf|archive-date=2015-11-06|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="FDA">{{cite web|work=FDA|date=2008-11-09|accessdate=2019-04-16|url=http://www.fda.gov/downloads/Food/GuidanceComplianceRegulatoryInformation/ComplianceEnforcement/ucm072848.pdf|title=Food and Drug Administration, Compliance Program Guidance Manual, Chapter 03 - Foodborne Biological Hazards|page=37|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170215171030/http://www.fda.gov/downloads/Food/GuidanceComplianceRegulatoryInformation/ComplianceEnforcement/ucm072848.pdf|archive-date=2017-02-15|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

在[[歐洲聯盟|歐盟]]，氮紅被稱為[[E編碼|E編碼]]中的'''E122'''，並批准用於特定食品和飲品，如[[乾酪|-{zh-cn:奶酪; zh-hk:芝士; zh-tw:起司}-]]、[[水果乾|水果乾]]和一些酒精飲料中<ref>{{cite web|url=https://webgate.ec.europa.eu/sanco_foods/main/index.cfm?event=substance.view&identifier=11|title=Azorubine entry in EU Food Additive Database|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141211094754/https://webgate.ec.europa.eu/sanco_foods/main/index.cfm?event=substance.view&identifier=11|archivedate=2014-12-11|accessdate=2014-12-06|deadurl=yes}}</ref>，並且允許在藥物中用作[[賦形劑|賦形劑]]<ref>{{cite web|work=EU|date=2007-06-19|url=http://www.ema.europa.eu/docs/en_GB/document_library/Scientific_guideline/2009/09/WC500003382.pdf|title=Guideline on Excipients in the Dossier for Application for Marketing Authorisation of a Medicinal Product|access-date=2019-04-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180219014954/http://www.ema.europa.eu/docs/en_GB/document_library/Scientific_guideline/2009/09/WC500003382.pdf|archive-date=2018-02-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>{{rp|4}}<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ec.europa.eu/food/fs/sfp/addit_flavor/flav08_en.pdf|title=Directive 94/36/EC - European Commission|work=EU|access-date=2019-04-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160509062829/http://ec.europa.eu/food/fs/sfp/addit_flavor/flav08_en.pdf|archive-date=2016-05-09|dead-url=no}}</ref>{{rp|16}}。

[[國際食品法典委員會|國際食品法典委員會]]沒有作出關於氮紅的規定<ref>{{cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060610005124/http://www.codexalimentarius.net/gsfaonline/additives/details.html?id=91|title=Azorubine (Carmoisine) (122) in the GSFA Online Database|accessdate=2014-12-06|work=GSFA}}</ref>。

==安全性==
沒有證據表示氮紅具[[突變原|致突變性]]或[[致癌物質|致癌性]]。1983年[[世界衛生組織|世衛]]訂立了其0-4 mg/kg的[[一日可接受攝取量|一日可接受攝取量]]（ADI）<ref>{{cite web|work=WHO|year=1983|url=http://whqlibdoc.who.int/trs/WHO_TRS_696.pdf|title=Evaluation of certain food additives and contaminants|isbn=92 4 120696 9|access-date=2019-04-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120921024124/http://whqlibdoc.who.int/trs/WHO_TRS_696.pdf|archive-date=2012-09-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>{{rp|19}}。

沒有證據支持食物色素導致[[食物不耐症|食物不耐症]]和兒童的[[注意力不足過動症|-{zh-cn:多动障碍; zh-tw:過動症; zh-hk:過度活躍症;}-]]行為<ref>{{cite book|last1=Tomaska|first1=LD|last2=Brooke-Taylor|first2=S.|chapter=Food Additives - General|pages=449-454|title=Encyclopedia of Food Safety|volume=2|publisher=Academic Press|year=2013|isbn=9780123786135}}</ref>{{rp|452}}。某些食用色素可能會成為遺傳預先傾向性者的觸發因素，但有關理據薄弱<ref name=FDAdyecomm/><ref name=Peds2012>{{cite journal |author1=Millichap JG |author2=Yee MM |title=The diet factor in attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder|journal=Pediatrics |volume=129 |issue=2 | pages=330–337 |date=February 2012 |pmid=22232312|doi=10.1542/peds.2011-2199 }}</ref>。

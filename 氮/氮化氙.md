'''Xe(N<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>'''是一种稳定的范德华力化合物，在高压下由[[氙|氙]]和[[氮|氮]]反应得到，存在两种结构。<ref>{{cite journal|doi=10.1103/PhysRevB.94.174109|title=<math>\mathrm{Xe}({\mathrm{N}}_{2}{)}_{2}</math> compound to 150 GPa: Reluctance to the formation of a xenon nitride|url=https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/PhysRevB.94.174109|journal=Physical Review B|volume=94|issue=17|pages=174109|date=2016-11-16|accessdate=2019-02-05|author=D. Laniel, G. Weck, P. Loubeyre}}</ref>

==制备及结构==
氙和氮在高压下（5 GPa）化合，形成I型的Xe(N<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>，将压力升高至14 GPa，发生相变，生成II型的Xe(N<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>。<ref name=Nature>{{cite journal|title=Formation of xenon-nitrogen compounds at high pressure|journal=Scientific Reports|volume=6|pages=34896|date=2016-10-17|doi=10.1038/srep34896|issn=2045-2322|url=https://www.nature.com/articles/srep34896|language=en|accessdate=2019-01-28|author=Eugene Gregoryanz, Philip Dalladay-Simpson,  Mungo Frost, Jack Binns, Robin Turnbull, Ross T. Howie}}</ref>

: Xe + 2 N<sub>2</sub> → Xe(N<sub>2</sub>)<sub>2</sub>

I型的化合物属[[立方晶系|立方晶系]],[[空间群|空间群]]''Fd''{{overline|3}}''m'',II型属[[四方晶系|四方晶系]]，空间群''I''4<sub>1</sub>/''amd''。<ref name=Nature />I型具有C15拉维斯结构，II型具有高压SiS<sub>2</sub>结构。在45 GPa以上可以发出荧光。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Synthesis, crystal structure and phase transition of a Xe–N2 compound at high pressure: experimental indication of orbital interaction between xenon and nitrogen|doi=10.1039/C6CP06552J|language=en|date=2016-12-21|journal=Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics|volume=19|issue=1|pages=59–63|issn=1463-9084|url=https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2017/cp/c6cp06552j|accessdate=2019-01-28|author=M. Hasegawa, F. Matsuzaki, K. Niwa}}</ref>

==其它氮化物==
在146 GPa以上，氙和氮反应可以生成XeN<sub>6</sub><ref>注释：XeN<sub>6</sub>的CAS号为2071193-57-0，由SciFinder查得。</ref>，它是一种高能量密度的氮化物，分子中存在N-N和Xe-N共价键。<ref>{{cite journal|doi=10.1103/PhysRevB.92.094104|title=Stable xenon nitride at high pressures|url=https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/PhysRevB.92.094104|journal=Physical Review B|volume=92|issue=9|pages=094104|date=2015-09-11|accessdate=2019-01-28|author=Feng Peng, Yanchao Wang, Hui Wang, Yunwei Zhang, Yanming Ma}}</ref>

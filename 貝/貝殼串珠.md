'''貝殼串珠'''（'''Wampum'''）是[[北美洲|北美洲]][[东部林地印第安人|东部林地印第安人]]所使用的一種類似貨幣的物件。“Wampum”一詞本意就是“白色貝殼珠子”，由[[沟螺|沟螺]]的[[殼軸|殼軸]]製成<ref name=lsd>Dubin, Lois Sherr. ''North American Indian Jewelry and Adornment: From Prehistory to the Present''. New York: Harry N. Abrams, 1999: 170-171. ISBN 0-8109-3689-5.</ref>。而類似的“Sewant”、“Zeewant”或“suckauhock”則是由[[蚌蠣|蚌蠣]]或其他[[牡蠣|牡蠣]]的殼製成的<ref>Jaap Jacobs. ''The Colony of New Netherland: A Dutch Settlement in Seventeenth-century America''. Cornell University Press, 2009. pg. 14</ref>。

==歷史==
“Wampum”一詞為“wampumpeag”的縮寫，源自[[馬薩諸塞語|馬薩諸塞語]]或[[纳拉干西特語|纳拉干西特語]]，意思是“白色貝殼珠子”<ref>{{OEtymD|Wampum|accessdate=2011-10-15}}</ref>。16世紀初，殖民者在今紐約州附近的地區發現了這種東西<ref name="lsd"/>。傳説中，[[易洛魁联盟|易洛魁联盟]]的创建者[[海華沙|海華沙]]是貝殼串珠的創造者，起初是用它來記錄信息<ref>[http://www.degiyagoh.net/great_law.htm Gawyehnehshehgowa: Great Law of Peace.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090209225312/http://www.degiyagoh.net/great_law.htm |date=2009-02-09 }} ''Degiya'göh Resources.'' (retrieved 14 March 2009)</ref>。

歐洲人抵達北美後開始大規模製造貝殼串珠，結果導致通貨膨脹。<ref name=Otto>[[Paul_Otto_(historian)|Otto, Paul]] [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4Mnmyi96lEE] "Henry Hudson, the Munsees, and the Wampum Revolution" (retrieved 5 September 2011)</ref>

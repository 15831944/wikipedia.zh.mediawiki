'''貝克·里根·梅菲爾德'''({{lang-en|'''Baker Reagan Mayfield'''}}){{bd|1995年|4月14日}}）是[[美國|美國]]職業美式足球運動員，現效力於[[國家美式足球聯盟|國家美式足球聯盟]]的[[克里夫蘭布朗|克里夫蘭布朗]]，場上位置為[[四分衛|四分衛]]。他曾為[[奧克拉荷馬大學|奧克拉荷馬大學]]打過大學足球，並參加了[[2018年NFL選秀|2018年NFL選秀]]，以首輪第一順位被選中。

==高中生涯==
梅菲爾德是[[特拉維斯湖高中|特拉維斯湖高中]]騎士[[高中美式足球|美式足球]]隊的先發四分衛。他帶領特拉維斯湖高中在兩個賽季中拿到了25勝2敗的戰績，且贏得了2011年4A州冠軍。他在整個高中生涯中，總共傳出6,255碼，67個達陣與8次被攔截。<ref name=TTU />

==大學生涯==

===德克薩斯理工大學===
在[[2013年德克薩斯理工大學紅突擊者美式足球隊|2013賽季]]開季前不久，梅菲爾德接替受傷的[[邁克爾·布魯爾|邁克爾·布魯爾]]成為先發四分衛。<ref name=ESPN>{{cite web |authorlink=Jake Trotter |last=Trotter |first=Jake |title=Source: Tech to start Baker Mayfield |url=http://espn.go.com/college-football/story/_/id/9614528/texas-tech-red-raiders-start-baker-mayfield-qb-source-says |publisher=ESPN |accessdate=September 2, 2013}}</ref>梅菲爾德被認為是第一個在開幕戰實際擔任四分衛位置的一年級生。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.espn.com/espn/feature/story/_/id/17284078/oklahoma-sooners-qb-baker-mayfield-used-defying-critics |title=All Baker Mayfield needs is hate |last=Keown |first=Tim |website=ESPN.com |accessdate=November 4, 2017}}</ref>

在首次對陣[[南方衛理會大學|南方衛理會大學]]時，梅菲爾德傳出了413碼與四次達陣，並以60傳43中的成績打破由[[比利·喬·托利亞|比利·喬·托利亞]]保持的學校紀錄，且是[[NCAA第一級別美式足球碗賽分區|NCAA第一級別FBS]]僅有完成此紀錄的四名大一新生之一。<ref name=Dodd>{{cite web |last=Dodd |first=Dennis |title=Kingsbury the kingmaker finds another one in head coaching debut |url=http://www.cbssports.com/collegefootball/writer/dennis-dodd/23404211/kingsbury-the-kingmaker-finds-another-one-in-head-coaching-debut |publisher=CBS Sports |accessdate=September 11, 2013}}</ref><ref name=FBS_p2>{{cite web |title=2011 Football Bowl Subdivision Records |url=http://fs.ncaa.org/Docs/stats/football_records/2011/FBS.pdf |publisher=NCAA |accessdate=September 5, 2011 |page=2}}</ref> For his performance, Mayfield was named [[Big_12_Conference|Big 12 Conference]] Offensive Player of the Week – the first freshman Texas Tech quarterback to be named so since current Red Raider head coach [[Kliff_Kingsbury|Kliff Kingsbury]] in [[1999_Texas_Tech_Red_Raiders_football_team|1999]].<ref name=FWST>{{cite web |last=Stevenson |first=Stefan |title=Mayfield earns Big 12 honor, a first for Texas Tech since Kingsbury in '99 |url=http://www.star-telegram.com/2013/09/02/5127169/walsh-will-start-for-oklahoma.html |publisher=Fort Worth Star-Telegram |accessdate=September 2, 2013}}</ref> The game featured the last four former Lake Travis quarterbacks combined on both teams: [[Garrett_Gilbert|Garrett Gilbert]], [[Michael_Brewer|Michael Brewer]], Collin Lagasse, and Mayfield.<ref name=Yahoo>{{cite web |last=Bromberg |first=nick |authorlink=Nick Bromberg |title=Texas Tech-SMU matchup features the last four Lake Travis High School quarterbacks |url=https://sports.yahoo.com/blogs/ncaaf-dr-saturday/texas-tech-smu-matchup-features-last-four-lake-172617499--ncaaf.html |publisher=Yahoo Sports |accessdate=September 2, 2013}}</ref>

Following the Red Raiders' second victory over [[2013_Stephen_F._Austin_Lumberjacks_football_team|Stephen F. Austin]], Mayfield's 780 season yards and seven touchdowns already exceeded the 755 yards and six touchdowns accrued by Texas Tech's last true freshman quarterback, Aaron Keesee, in 10 games.<ref name="Sports Reference 84">{{cite web |title=1984 Year Summary |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/cfb/years/1984.html |publisher=Sports-Reference.com |accessdate=September 11, 2013}}</ref> After being affected by injury and losing the starting job to fellow true freshman [[Davis_Webb|Davis Webb]], Mayfield finished the season with 2,315 yards on 218-of-340 completions with 12 touchdowns and 9 interceptions.

Mayfield was named one of 10 semifinalists for the [[Burlsworth_Trophy|Burlsworth Trophy]] in November; the award is given to the best player in Division I football who began his college career as a walk-on.<ref name=Burlsworth>{{cite news |title=Mayfield named semifinalist for the Burlsworth Trophy |url=http://www.kcbd.com/story/24014356/mayfield-named-semifinalist-for-the-burlsworth-trophy |accessdate=November 20, 2013 |newspaper=KCBD |date=November 19, 2013}}</ref>

Mayfield earned Big 12 Conference Freshman Offensive Player of the Year for the 2013 season. Mayfield announced that he would be leaving the program due to a 'miscommunication' with the coaching staff.<ref name="transfer">{{cite news |last=Graham |first=Mike |title=Is QB Baker Mayfield seeking a transfer from Texas Tech? ===奧克拉荷馬大學===
梅菲爾德在2014年1月轉學至[[奧克拉荷馬大學|奧克拉荷馬大學]]，but had not contacted the Sooner coaching staff. Mayfield further elaborated in an interview with [[ESPN|ESPN]] that he sought to transfer due to scholarship issues and a perception that he had earned the starting position and that further competition was not "really fair".<ref name="transfer"/><ref>{{cite web |last=Trotter |first=Jake |title=Q&A: Former Texas Tech QB Baker Mayfield |url=http://espn.go.com/blog/big12/post/_/id/78697/qa-former-texas-tech-qb-baker-mayfield |publisher=ESPN |accessdate=January 9, 2014}}</ref> The alleged scholarship issues were denied by Texas Tech coach Kliff Kingsbury.<ref name=NewsOK>{{cite news |last1=Kersey |first1=Jason |title=Oklahoma football: How people in Lubbock view Baker Mayfield |url=http://newsok.com/article/5454584 |accessdate=December 15, 2015 |agency=NewsOK.com |date=October 19, 2015}}</ref>

In February 2014, Oklahoma head coach [[Bob_Stoops|Bob Stoops]] confirmed that Mayfield would be walking on for the [[Oklahoma_Sooners_football|Oklahoma Sooners]]. Mayfield was not eligible to play until the 2015 season, and lost a season of eligibility due to Big 12 Conference transfer rules following an unsuccessful appeal of his transfer restrictions.<ref name=OU>{{cite news |last=Shadid |first=Trent |title=Bob Stoops confirms Baker Mayfield's transfer from Texas Tech |url=http://newsok.com/bob-stoops-confirms-baker-mayfields-transfer-from-texas-tech/article/3930877 |accessdate=February 7, 2014 |newspaper=The Oklahoman |date=February 5, 2014}}</ref> 

====2015年賽季====
On August 24, 2015, Mayfield was named the starting quarterback for the Sooners after winning an open quarterback competition against [[Trevor_Knight|Trevor Knight]]. On September 6, 2015, Mayfield started against [[2015_Akron_Zips_football_team|Akron]]. Mayfield totaled 388 yards through the air with 3 touchdowns on 23 completions in the 41–3 win.<ref>{{cite web |author= |url=http://espn.go.com/college-football/game?gameId=400763404 |title=Akron vs. Oklahoma – Game Summary – September 5, 2015 – ESPN |publisher=Espn.go.com |date= |accessdate=September 20, 2015}}</ref> In the second game of the 2015 season, Mayfield started at [[2015_Tennessee_Volunteers_football_team|Tennessee]] at [[Neyland_Stadium|Neyland Stadium]]. The Sooners were ranked 19th at the time and the Volunteers were ranked 23rd. Mayfield started off very slow in the game, not even reaching midfield until the 13-minute mark of the 4th quarter. Oklahoma came back from a 17-point deficit to win the game in double overtime. Mayfield threw for 187 yards and three touchdowns on 19 completions while throwing two interceptions early in the game in the 31–24 2OT win.<ref>{{cite web |author= |url=http://espn.go.com/college-football/game?gameId=400603868 |title=Oklahoma vs. Tennessee – Game Summary – September 12, 2015 – ESPN |publisher=Espn.go.com |date=September 13, 2015 |accessdate=September 20, 2015}}</ref> In the third game of the season, Mayfield got the start against [[2015_Tulsa_Golden_Hurricane_football_team|Tulsa]]. He had a career day throwing for 487 yards and four touchdowns, including 316 yards at half. Mayfield also ran for 85 yards and two touchdowns in the 52–38 win.<ref>{{cite web |author= |url=http://espn.go.com/college-football/game?gameId=400763418 |title=Tulsa vs. Oklahoma – Game Summary – September 19, 2015 – ESPN |publisher=Espn.go.com |date= |accessdate=September 20, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.tulsaworld.com/sportsextra/ousportsextra/no-oklahoma-vs-tulsa-baker-mayfield-s-big-half-has/article_a819258c-fd31-5c3d-9647-47d83a0c4e4b.html |title=Air Apparent: Baker Mayfield needs record-setting day for OU to beat Tulsa |last=Bailey |first=Eric |publisher=Tulsa World |access-date=September 20, 2015}}</ref>

Mayfield finished the year with 3,700 passing yards, 36 touchdowns, and seven interceptions. He finished fourth in the [[Heisman_Trophy|Heisman Trophy]] voting and helped lead Oklahoma to the [[College_Football_Playoff|College Football Playoff]] semifinal ([[2015_Orange_Bowl|2015 Orange Bowl]]). However, they lost to [[2015_Clemson_Tigers_football_team|Clemson]] by a score of 37–17.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/cfb/boxscores/2015-12-31-clemson.html|title=Orange Bowl - Oklahoma vs Clemson Box Score, December 31, 2015|publisher=Sports Reference|accessdate=10 September 2017}}</ref>

====2016年賽季====
Mayfield started the 2016 season off with 323 passing yards and two touchdowns in a 33–23 loss to #15 Houston.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/ncaaf/2016/09/05/oklahoma-qb-mayfield-takes-blame-for-loss-to-houston/89889632/ |title=Oklahoma QB Mayfield takes blame for loss to Houston |work=USA TODAY |access-date=2018-10-29 |language=en}}</ref> In the rivalry game against Texas on October 8, he had 390 passing yards, three touchdowns, and two interceptions in the 45–40 victory.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.star-telegram.com/sports/college/big-12/university-of-texas/article106998882.html |title=Charlie Strong’s case no better after Texas falls to No. 20 Oklahoma |work=Star-Telegram |access-date=2018-10-29 |language=en}}</ref> On October 22, in a 66–59 victory over Texas Tech, Mayfield had 545 passing yards and seven touchdowns in the historical matchup against [[Patrick_Mahomes|Patrick Mahomes]]. Mahomes tallied 734 passing yards and five touchdowns to go along with Mayfield's numbers in a game that broke various single-game passing records.<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.espn.com/blog/big12/post/_/id/115714/mayfield-outduels-record-setting-mahomes-in-return-to-lubbock |title=Baker Mayfield outduels record-setting Patrick Mahomes II in return to Lubbock |work=ESPN.com |access-date=2018-10-29 |language=en}}</ref> Over the final five games of the regular season, Mayfield totaled 1,321 passing yards, 15 passing touchdowns, and three interceptions to go along with three rushing touchdowns. All five games were victories for the Sooners.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.sports-reference.com/cfb/players/baker-mayfield-1/gamelog/2016/#29-33-sum:gamelog |title=Baker Mayfield 2016 Game Log (Games 8-12) |website=College Football at Sports-Reference.com |language=en |access-date=2018-10-29}}</ref>

It was announced in December 2016 that Mayfield and his top receiving target, [[Dede_Westbrook|Dede Westbrook]], would be finalists for the 2016 [[Heisman_Trophy|Heisman Trophy]]. It was also announced that they would play in the [[2017_Sugar_Bowl|2017 Sugar Bowl]]. Mayfield ended up finishing third in the Heisman voting.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/cfb/awards/heisman-2016.html|title=2016 Heisman Trophy Voting|publisher=Sports Reference|accessdate=10 September 2017}}</ref>

In the 2017 Sugar Bowl, Mayfield helped lead the Sooners to a 35–19 victory over [[2016_Auburn_Tigers_football_team|Auburn]]. He finished the bowl game 19-for-28 with 296 passing yards and two touchdowns earning him the MVP award.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sports-reference.com/cfb/boxscores/2017-01-02-oklahoma.html|title=Sugar Bowl - Auburn vs Oklahoma Box Score, January 2, 2017|publisher=Sports Reference|accessdate=10 September 2017}}</ref>

====2017年賽季====
2017年9月9日，梅菲爾德在球隊獲勝後，將奧克拉荷馬大學的旗幟插在[[俄亥俄體育場|俄亥俄體育場]]草地的O上面，這樣的舉動讓球迷們十分不滿，而本人也在不久後道歉。<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.si.com/college-football/2017/09/11/baker-mayfield-apologizes-planting-oklahoma-flag-video |title=Watch: Baker Mayfield Apologizes for Planting Oklahoma Flag on Ohio State's Field |last=Rollins |first=Khadrice |date=September 11, 2017 |website=Sports Illustrated |access-date=June 11, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.latimes.com/sports/sportsnow/la-sp-oklahoma-ohio-state-mayfield-20170911-story.html |title=Baker Mayfield apologizes for planting Oklahoma flag in middle of Ohio State field following Sooners' win - Los Angeles Times |last=Schilken |first=Chuck |work=Los Angeles Times |access-date=2018-10-06}}</ref>

2017年11月4日，梅爾爾德在對上同州世仇[[奧克拉荷馬州立大學|奧克拉荷馬州立大學]]時，繳出了生涯新高的598碼傳球，並以36傳24中5次傳球達陣的成績，帶領球隊以62-52擊潰對手。梅菲爾德同時作為奧克拉荷馬大學的首發四分衛，在瘋人大戰(奧克拉荷馬大學與奧克拉荷馬州立大學的世仇對決)中，達成生涯3-0的橫掃成績。

===「梅菲爾德條款」===
當梅菲爾德打完他的大一球季之後，便從德克薩斯理工大學轉至奧克拉荷馬大學。梅菲爾德向NCAA上訴，希望能立即取得為奧克拉荷馬大學上場比賽的資格，因為他是藉由試訓管道進入德克薩斯理工大學，且沒有接受任何獎學金。梅菲爾德主張他並不適用跟領取獎學金的球員一樣的轉學制度。但上訴被駁回了，NCAA認為他並不符合立即上場的資格。[[12大聯盟|12大聯盟]]的转校规则，他只能到2015-16赛季才能出战，而且将会损失一个赛季的大学球员资格。<ref name="Rule4">{{cite web |title=Conference Handbook |url=http://www.big12sports.com/fls/10410/pdfs/handbook/ConferenceHandbook.pdf |website=Big12Sports.com |publisher=Big 12 Conference |accessdate=September 3, 2016}}</ref><ref name="rule3">{{cite web |last1=Aber |first1=Ryan |title=OU football journal: Baker Mayfield named The Sporting News' Player of the Year |url=http://newsok.com/article/5467486 |publisher=NewsOK |accessdate=September 3, 2016}}</ref>

奧克拉荷馬大學官方要求德州理工大學同意梅菲爾德上場，但德州理工大學拒絕並反對了這項要求。<ref name="Rule2">{{cite web |last1=Fowler |first1=Jeremy |title=Texas Tech lifts scholarship ban for OU's Baker Mayfield |url=http://www.cbssports.com/college-football/news/texas-tech-lifts-scholarship-ban-for-ous-baker-mayfield/ |website=CBSSports.com |publisher=CBS Sports |accessdate=September 3, 2016}}</ref>梅菲爾德被迫在2014年停賽，也損失了12大聯盟規則中要求的一年大學球員資格。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://espn.go.com/college-football/story/_/id/11612827/oklahoma-baker-mayfield-appeal-denied-ncaa |title=Baker Mayfield has appeal denied |last=Trotter |first=Jake |date=September 29, 2014 |website=ESPN.com |accessdate=June 3, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://newsok.com/article/5475949 |title=Could Baker Mayfield transfer and get his fourth season elsewhere? |last=Tramel |first=Berry |date=January 31, 2016 |website=NewsOK.com |accessdate=June 3, 2016}}</ref>

2016年6月1日，12大聯盟的代表們提出，讓像是梅菲爾德這樣藉由試訓且沒有領取獎學金的球員能夠擺脫轉學的限制，這項提案在第二天的投票中以7-3的比數通過，梅菲爾德也得以繼續參加2017年的比賽。而梅菲爾德的前學校德州理工大學也投下同意票。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://espn.go.com/college-football/story/_/id/15912568/big-12-reversal-makes-baker-mayfield-eligible-oklahoma-sooners-2017 |title=Baker Mayfield eligible for OU in '17 after Big 12 OK's tweaked rule |last=Trotter |first=Jake |date=June 2, 2016 |website=ESPN.com |accessdate=June 3, 2016}}</ref><ref name="Rule">{{cite web |last1=Hoover |first1=John |title=Kingsbury: Texas Tech voted for ‘Baker Mayfield Rule’ |url=http://thefranchiseok.com/kingsbury-texas-tech-voted-for-baker-mayfield-rule/ |accessdate=September 3, 2016}}</ref>

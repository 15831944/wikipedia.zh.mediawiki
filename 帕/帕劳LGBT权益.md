'''女同性恋，男同性恋，双性恋和跨性别（[[LGBT|LGBT]]）'''人群在'''[[帕劳|帕劳]]'''面临着许多法律和社会方面的挑战。自2014年7月23日新的《刑法》生效后，同性性行为在帕劳合法化。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gaynz.com/articles/publish/3/article_15872.php|title=GayNZ.com Palau decriminalises sex between men|publisher=|accessdate=2015-07-27|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170214223543/http://www.gaynz.com/articles/publish/3/article_15872.php|archivedate=2017年2月14日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.islandtimes.us/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=16941:new-penal-code-takes-effect|title=New Penal Code takes effect|publisher=|accessdate=2017-07-17}}</ref>在过去，男性之间的同性性行为是违法的，可判处十年徒刑；然而，女性之间的同性性行为是合法的。<ref name="ILGA 2013">{{cite web|url=http://old.ilga.org/Statehomophobia/ILGA_State_Sponsored_Homophobia_2013.pdf|title=State-sponsored Homophobia A world survey of laws prohibiting same sex activity between consenting adults|accessdate=2017-07-17|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6IEmVxpKn?url=http://old.ilga.org/Statehomophobia/ILGA_State_Sponsored_Homophobia_2013.pdf|archivedate=2013-07-19}}</ref>2011年10月1日，该岛政府承诺同性性行为全面非刑罪化。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.scoop.co.nz/stories/WO1110/S00303/decriminalizing-homosexuality-step-to-genuine-legal-equality.htm|title=Decriminalizing homosexuality step to genuine legal equality|publisher=|accessdate=2015-07-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.freedomhouse.org/report/freedom-world/2013/palau|title=Palau|publisher=|accessdate=2015-07-27}}</ref>

帕劳的宪法将婚姻定义为男女之间。帕劳在2008年制定了同性婚姻禁令。这项禁令是2008年11月4日公民投票通过的22项修正案之一。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://c2d.ch/detailed_display.php?lname=votes&table=votes&page=1&parent_id=&sublinkname=results&id=57796&profile=1&continent=Australia/Oceania&insti=&res=&year=&page=1|title=C2D - Centre for research on direct democracy|publisher=|accessdate=2015-07-27|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150415061530/http://c2d.ch/detailed_display.php?lname=votes&table=votes&page=1&parent_id=&sublinkname=results&id=57796&profile=1&continent=Australia%2FOceania&insti=&res=&year=&page=1|archivedate=2015-04-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hawaii.edu/aplpj/2011/11/23/283/|title=Asian-Pacific Law & Policy Journal  » Blog Archive   » The Rights and Liberties of the Palau Constitution by Kevin Bennardo|publisher=|accessdate=2015-07-27|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150417201745/http://www.hawaii.edu/aplpj/2011/11/23/283/|archivedate=2015年4月17日}}</ref>

同性伴侣公开感情可能会被冒犯。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gov.uk/foreign-travel-advice/palau/local-laws-and-customs|title=Palau travel advice - GOV.UK|publisher=|accessdate=2015-07-27}}</ref>

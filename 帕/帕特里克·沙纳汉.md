'''帕特里克·迈克尔·沙纳汉'''（{{lang-en|'''Patrick Michael Shanahan'''}}，{{bd|1954年|6月5日}}）是美國商人和[[唐纳德·特朗普|唐纳德·特朗普]]政府官員，以及現任-{zh-tw:代理;zh-hans:代理;zh-hk:署理}-[[美國國防部長|美國國防部長]]。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.seattletimes.com/business/boeing-aerospace/trump-taps-boeing-executive-and-patrick-shanahan-for-deputy-secretary-of-defense/|title=Trump taps Boeing executive Pat Shanahan for deputy secretary of defense|author=Gates|first=Dominic|date=March 16, 2017|work=The Seattle Times|author2=Brunner|first2=Jim}}</ref><ref name="cnn">{{Cite news|url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/07/18/politics/senate-confirms-pentagon-patrick-shanahan/index.html|title=Senate confirms the Pentagon's new No. 2|author=Herb|first=Jeremy|date=July 18, 2017|publisher=CNN|accessdate=18 July 2017}}</ref>

2019年1月，接替[[詹姆斯·马蒂斯|詹姆斯·马蒂斯]]職務，成為-{zh-tw:代理;zh-hans:代理;zh-hk:署理}-國防部長。<ref name="nytimes">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/12/23/us/politics/trump-mattis.html|work=The New York Times|title=Trump, Angry Over Mattis’s Rebuke, Removes Him 2 Months Early President Trump, with Defense Secretary Jim Mattis, left, during a briefing in October.|language=en}}</ref>

==生平==
1954年生於[[华盛顿州|华盛顿州]]，入讀[[華盛頓大學|華盛頓大學]]攻讀機械工程以[[理學學士|理學學士]]畢業，其後到[[麻省理工学院|麻省理工学院]]進修其專業並獲取[[理學碩士|理學碩士]]，並於[[麻省理工斯隆管理學院|麻省理工斯隆管理學院]]研讀[[工商管理碩士|工商管理碩士]]課程。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.seattletimes.com/business/boeing-aerospace/trump-taps-boeing-executive-and-patrick-shanahan-for-deputy-secretary-of-defense/|title=Trump taps Boeing executive Pat Shanahan for deputy secretary of defense|date=2017-03-16|work=The Seattle Times|accessdate=2017-05-22|language=en-US}}</ref><ref name="DoD-Biography">{{Cite web|url=https://www.defense.gov/About/Biographies/Biography-View/Article/1252116/patrick-shanahan/|title=Patrick Shanahan > U.S. Department of Defense > Biography|accessdate=6 October 2017|work=United States Department of Defense}}</ref>

== 职业生涯 ==
[[File:John_Kerry_and_Pat_Shanahan.jpg|right]]]]
沙納漢一直不停涉獵[[波音|波音]]不同的軍工業務，建立了對國防不淺的了解。起初1986年入職，負責電腦服務以及參與[[波音777|波音777]]計劃。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://www.boeing.com/company/bios/patrick-shanahan.page|title=Boeing: Patrick (Pat) Shanahan|accessdate=22 May 2017|work=Boeing|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170616095047/http://www.boeing.com/company/bios/patrick-shanahan.page|archivedate=2017-06-16|language=en|dead-url=no}}</ref>其職業生涯中，曾出任波音導彈防禦系統以及眾多客機計劃的管理層。<ref name=":3">{{Cite news|url=https://www.geekwire.com/2017/boeing-exec-pat-shanahan-deputy-defense-secretary/|title=Boeing exec Pat Shanahan chosen to become deputy defense secretary|date=2017-03-16|work=GeekWire|accessdate=2017-05-22|language=en-US}}</ref>同時，亦在重新開發[[波音787|波音787]]計劃中扮演著重要的先鋒角色<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.bizjournals.com/seattle/news/2016/03/11/two-puget-sound-boeing-veterans-who-helped-get-787.html|title=Two Puget Sound Boeing veterans who helped get 787 back on track promoted|accessdate=2017-05-22|author=|date=|first=|work=www.bizjournals.com|archiveurl=|archivedate=}}</ref>，更於2008年時有「搞定先生」的外號。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://articles.latimes.com/2008/feb/24/business/fi-sunprofile24|title=Boeing uses him as its heavy hitter|author=|first=|date=2008-02-24|work=Los Angeles Times|accessdate=2017-05-22|deadurl=|language=en-US|issn=0458-3035|archiveurl=|archivedate=}}</ref>

他擔任了[[波音民用飞机集团|波音民用飞机集团]]副主席以及[[波音757|波音757]]項目總經理的職務，全面負責757系列的設計、製造及盈利控制。<ref name=":0"></ref>此外，於[[波音767|波音767]]計劃和工業製造擁有領導地位。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.seattletimes.com/business/boeing-aerospace/787-visionary-out-new-chief-must-make-it-fly/|title=787 visionary out; new chief must make it fly|date=2007-10-17|work=The Seattle Times|accessdate=2017-05-22|language=en-US}}</ref>其後到[[費城|費城]]出任{{le|波音旋翼機系統|Boeing Rotorcraft Systems}}的副主席及總經理。<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=http://thehill.com/policy/defense/324301-trump-nominates-boeing-vp-to-to-deputy-defense-secretary|title=Trump nominates Boeing VP for deputy Defense secretary|date=2017-03-16|work=TheHill|accessdate=2017-05-22}}</ref>，負責所有美軍位於費城與[[亞利桑那州|亞利桑那州]][[梅薩_(亞利桑那州)|梅薩]]的航空計畫以及飛行活動<ref name=":0"></ref>，包括[[V-22魚鷹式傾轉旋翼機|V-22魚鷹式傾轉旋翼機]]、[[CH-47_契努克|CH-47 契努克]]和[[AH-64阿帕契直升機|AH-64阿帕契直升機]]。<ref name=":1" />然後成為波音導彈防禦系統的副主席及總經理，從2004年開始監管[[陸基中段防禦系統|陸基中段防禦系統]]、空中雷射系統以及進階戰略性雷射計劃。<ref name="DoD-Biography">{{Cite web|url=https://www.defense.gov/About/Biographies/Biography-View/Article/1252116/patrick-shanahan/|title=Patrick Shanahan > U.S. Department of Defense > Biography|accessdate=6 October 2017|work=United States Department of Defense}}</ref><ref name=":0"></ref>接著出任[[波音787|波音787]]計劃的副主席及總經理，於對計劃十分關鍵的2007至08年間的帶領整個項目，<ref name=":0" />之後自從2008年12月起擔任[[波音民用飞机集团|波音民用飞机集团]]飛機計劃的高級副主席。<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/markets/stocks|title=Executive Profile {{!}} Patrick M. Shanahan|accessdate=2017-05-22|work=Bloomberg.com|archiveurl=|archivedate=}}</ref>

2016年4月，獲擢升至波音主公司，成為波音高級副主席（供應鏈及運營），<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/markets/stocks|title=Executive Profile {{!}} Patrick M. Shanahan|accessdate=2017-05-22|work=Bloomberg.com|archiveurl=|archivedate=}}</ref>職責涵蓋生產運營及供應商管理，<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=http://thehill.com/policy/defense/324301-trump-nominates-boeing-vp-to-to-deputy-defense-secretary|title=Trump nominates Boeing VP for deputy Defense secretary|date=2017-03-16|work=TheHill|accessdate=2017-05-22}}</ref>主要實施有關進階生產科技及全球供應鏈的策略性佈置。<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.washington.edu/regents/officers/patrick-m-pat-shanahan/|title=Patrick (Pat) Shanahan  {{!}}  Board of Regents|accessdate=2017-05-22|work=www.washington.edu|language=en}}</ref>

沙納漢曾為波音公司執行委員會成員。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.seattletimes.com/business/white-house-picks-boeing-executive-as-pentagons-no-2/|title=White House picks Boeing executive as Pentagon’s No. 2|date=2017-03-16|work=The Seattle Times|accessdate=2017-05-22|language=en-US}}</ref>

== 国防部副部长 ==
2017年3月16日，[[美國總統|美國總統]][[特朗普|特朗普]]表示希望提名沙纳汉為[[美国国防部副部长|美国国防部副部长]]──[[美國國防部|國防部]]文官第二最高級的職位。<ref name=":3"></ref>沙其後正式提名纳汉為國防部副部長，以特朗普所建議的近年最大軍事預算升幅的國防部預算為基礎，去領導擴充美軍的規模。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/04/01/business/patrick-shanahan-pentagon-nominee.html|title=A Pentagon Test for Boeing’s Mr. Fix-It|author=Drew|first=Christopher|date=2017-04-01|work=The New York Times|accessdate=2017-05-22|issn=0362-4331}}</ref>其[[美國參議院|參議院]]確認聽證會於同年6月20日舉行，會中支持提供極具殺傷力武器予[[烏克蘭|烏克蘭]]的參議員[[約翰·麥凱恩|約翰·麥凱恩]]因不滿沙纳汉對此議題的書面回覆而威脅妨礙其提名，沙纳汉回應指自己仍未能獲取有關的機密軍事資訊，故未能落實有關決定。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/06/20/politics/mccain-threatens-block-trump-pentagon-nominee/index.html|title=McCain threatens to block Trump's Pentagon nominee|author=Herb|first=Jeremy|date=June 20, 2017|publisher=CNN|accessdate=26 June 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://thehill.com/policy/defense/338576-mccain-threatens-to-block-deputy-defense-secretary-nominee|title=McCain threatens to block Trump's deputy Defense nominee|author=Kheel|first=Rebecca|date=June 20, 2017|publisher=The Hill|accessdate=26 June 2017}}</ref>

沙纳汉於2017年7月18日以票數92比7獲参议院確認，正式就職第33任國防部副部長。<ref name="cnn">{{Cite news|url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/07/18/politics/senate-confirms-pentagon-patrick-shanahan/index.html|title=Senate confirms the Pentagon's new No. 2|author=Herb|first=Jeremy|date=July 18, 2017|publisher=CNN|accessdate=18 July 2017}}</ref><ref name="DoD-Biography"></ref>

2018年12月23日，[[美國總統|美國總統]]特朗普決定提早結束马蒂斯的任期，任命沙納漢於2019年1月1日接任代理[[美國國防部長|國防部長]]的職務<ref name="nytimes"></ref>。

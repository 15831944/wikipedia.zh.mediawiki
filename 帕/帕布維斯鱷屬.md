'''帕布維斯鱷屬'''（屬名：''Pabwehshi''）是[[鱷形超目|鱷形超目]][[波羅鱷科|波羅鱷科]]的一屬。[[正模標本|正模標本]]（編號GSP-UM 2000）是一個部分上頜與下頜。化石發現於[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]][[俾路支省|俾路支省]]的Pab組地層，地質年代相當於[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]晚期的[[馬斯垂克階|馬斯垂克階]]。帕布維斯鱷是第一個發現於[[南亞|南亞]]的白堊紀[[鱷形類|鱷形類]]。牠們的嘴部牙齒互相交錯，邊緣成鋸齒狀。

在2001年，[[傑佛瑞·威爾森|傑佛瑞·威爾森]]（Jeffrey A. Wilson）等人將這些化石敘述、命名。[[模式種|模式種]]是'''巴基斯坦帕布維斯鱷'''（''P. pakistanensis''） ，屬名意為「Pab的野獸」，''wehshi''在[[烏爾都語|烏爾都語]]意為「野獸」<ref name=JAWetal01>{{cite journal |last=Wilson |first=Jeffrey A. |coauthors=Malkani, M. Sadiq; and Gingerich, Philip D. |year=2001 |title=New crocodyliform (Reptilia, Mesoeucrocodylia) from the Upper Cretaceous Pab Formation of Vitakri, Balochistan (Pakistan) |journal=Contributions from the Museum of Paleontology, University of Michigan |volume=30 |issue=12 |pages=321–336}}</ref>。

在早期分類法裡，帕布維斯鱷被歸類於[[波羅鱷科|波羅鱷科]]，是[[犬齒鱷|犬齒鱷]]、[[波羅鱷|波羅鱷]]的近親<ref name=JAWetal01/><ref name=TC05>{{cite journal |last=Turner |first=A.H. |coauthors=and Calvo, J.O. |year=2005 |title=A new sebecosuchian crocodyliform from the Late Cretaceous of Patagonia |journal=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology |volume=25 |issue=1 |pages=87–98 |doi=10.1671/0272-4634(2005)025[0087:ANSCFT]2.0.CO;2}}</ref><ref name=Nascimento10>{{cite journal |year=2010 |title=A new species of ''Baurusuchus'' (Crocodyliformes, Mesoeucrocodylia) from the Upper Cretaceous of Brazil, with the first complete postcranial skeleton described from the family Baurusuchidae |url=http://www.scielo.br/pdf/paz/v50n21/a01v5021.pdf |journal=Papeis Avulsos de Zoologia |volume=50 |issue=21 |pages=323‑361 |author=Paulo Miranda Nascimento and Hussam Zaher}}</ref><ref name=TS10>{{cite journal |last=Turner |first=A.H. |coauthors=and Sertich, J.W. |year=2010 |title=Phylogenetic history of ''Simosuchus clarki'' (Crocodyliformes: Notosuchia) from the Late Cretaceous of Madagascar  |journal=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology |volume=30 |issue=6, Memoir 10 |pages=177–236 |doi=10.1080/02724634.2010.532348}}</ref><ref name=Campinasuchus>{{cite journal |year=2011 |title=''Campinasuchus dinizi'' gen. et sp. nov., a new Late Cretaceous baurusuchid (Crocodyliformes) from the Bauru Basin, Brazil |url=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2011/f/zt02871p042.pdf |journal=Zootaxa |volume=2871 |issue= |pages=19–42 |author=Ismar De Souza Carvalho, Vicente De Paula Antunes Teixeira, Mara Lúcia Da Fonseca Ferraz, Luiz Carlos Borges Ribeiro, Agustín Guillermo Martinelli, Francisco Macedo Neto, Joseph J. W. Sertich, Gabriel Cardoso Cunha, Isabella Cardoso Cunha and Partícia Fonseca Ferraz }}</ref><ref name=Lorosuchus>{{cite journal |author=Diego Pol and Jaime E. Powell |year=2011 |title=A new sebecid mesoeucrocodylian from the Rio Loro Formation (Palaeocene) of north-western Argentina |journal=Zoological Journal of the Linnean Society |volume=163 |pages=S7–S36 |doi=10.1111/j.1096-3642.2011.00714.x |url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1096-3642.2011.00714.x/pdf}}</ref>。在2007年，其他研究人員發現帕布維斯鱷與[[西貝鱷類|西貝鱷類]]的[[佩羅鱷科|佩羅鱷科]]有相似處<ref name=LAS07>{{cite journal |last=Larsson |first=H.

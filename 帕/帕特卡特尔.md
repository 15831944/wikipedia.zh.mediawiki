'''帕特卡特尔'''（{{lang-nah|Patecatl}}）是[[阿兹特克神话|阿兹特克神话]]中的龙舌兰之神，亦象征治愈与丰饶。他也是[[乌羽玉|乌羽玉]]的发现者。<ref>{{cite book|last1=Holmer|first1=Rick|title=The Aztec Book of Destiny|date=2005|publisher=BookSurge LLC|location=North Charleston, South Carolina|isbn=9781419611636|page=83|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=cRuupQa8J-AC&pg=PA89&dq=Patecatl&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwimmoG_kIXXAhXEs1QKHW0qDsgQ6AEIJzAA#v=onepage&q=Patecatl&f=false|accessdate=2017-10-22|language=en|quote=The provider of the Shadow Coul Tonalli for those born on Grass Malinalli is the Medicine God Patecatl who symbolizes the health and healing effects of medicinal herbs that grow from the "jaws of earth".}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Lurker|first1=Manfred|title=The Routledge Dictionary of Gods and Goddesses, Devils and Demons|date=2004|publisher=Taylor & Francis e-Library|location=London|isbn=0-203-64351-8|page=150|edition=2nd|url=https://archive.org/stream/TheRoutledgeDictionaryOfGodsAnd/The%20Routledge%20Dictionary%20of%20Gods%20and_djvu.txt|accessdate=2017-10-22|quote=Patecatl Aztec god of medicine and  'lord of the pulque root'. His wife is the  pulque-goddess — > Mayahuel.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Daysign Poll|url=http://www.mexicolore.co.uk/aztecs/calendar/daysign-poll|website=www.mexicolore.co.uk|accessdate=2017-10-22|language=en|date=5  October  2015}}</ref>他与妻子[[马亚韦尔|马亚韦尔]]结合，孕育出{{tsl|en|Centzon Totochtin|四百兔众神}}<ref>{{cite web|title=Patecatl, Lord of the Land of Medicines|url=https://www.azteccalendar.com/god/Patecatl.html|website=www.azteccalendar.com|accessdate=2017-10-22|language=en}}</ref>。

[[阿兹特克历法|阿兹特克历法]]中，有十三天（即一个周期）受帕特卡特尔所支配。上一个周期的主宰者为[[米克特兰特库特利|米克特兰特库特利]]，后一位则为{{tsl|en|Itztlacoliuhqui|伊兹特拉科利赫基}}（{{lang|nah|Itztlacoliuhqui}}）。

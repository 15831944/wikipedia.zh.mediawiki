'''帕哈苗文'''是一個[[Unicode區段|Unicode區段]]，主要包含用於書寫[[苗語川黔滇次方言|苗語]]的帕哈苗文字符。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.unicode.org/L2/L2012/12013-n4175-pahawh-hmong.pdf | title=N4175: Final proposal to encode the Pahawh Hmong script in the UCS | first1=Michael | last1=Everson | publisher=Working Group Document, ISO/IEC JTC1/SC2/WG2 | date=2012-01-20}}</ref>

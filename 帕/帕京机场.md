== 历史 ==
在帕京机场建成之前，锡金是印度唯一一个不通民航的邦，最近的机场是位于[[西孟加拉邦|西孟加拉邦]]的{{link-en|巴格多格拉机场|Bagdogra Airport}}和不丹的[[帕羅機場|帕罗机场]]，帕京机场的建成为旅客们节省了5个小时。<ref name=":2" />

2008年10月，印度经济事务内阁委员会批准了建设帕京机场的规划。建筑承包商{{Link-en|旁劳埃德|Punj Lloyd}}公司于次年1月签订了价值26.4亿[[卢比|卢比]]的合同，来建设跑道、滑行道、机坪排水系统和绿地工程的电力设施。2009年2月，时任印度民航局长{{Link-en|普拉夫·帕铁|Praful Patel}}参与了机场的奠基仪式。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Projects Monitor :: Pakyong, first-ever airport in Sikkim, makes steady progress|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120719003847/http://www.projectsmonitor.com/AIRPT/pakyong-firstever-airport-in-sikkim-makes-steady-progress|work=web.archive.org|date=2012-07-19|accessdate=2019-03-23}}</ref>

机场建设本应在2012年完工，但由于遭到当地村民抗议，工程不得不在2014年1月暂时停工。印度机场管理局介入调解，向村民支付了部分补偿金，使工程从当年10月得以继续。但次年1月，村民再次发动抗议。<ref>{{Cite news|title=Work on N-E's first greenfield airport comes to a halt|url=https://www.business-standard.com/article/current-affairs/work-on-n-e-s-first-greenfield-airport-comes-to-a-halt-115013001616_1.html|newspaper=Business Standard India|date=2015-01-30|first=Supratim|last=Dey|author=|work=|access-date=2019-03-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180612163036/https://www.business-standard.com/article/current-affairs/work-on-n-e-s-first-greenfield-airport-comes-to-a-halt-115013001616_1.html|archive-date=2018-06-12|dead-url=no}}</ref>2015年7月，机场管理局和锡金邦政府签署[[諒解備忘錄|谅解备忘录]]，承诺在8月15日前动迁受影响的居民，以便机场工程能在10月继续。<ref name=":3">{{Cite news|title=AAI, Sikkim govt sign MoU for Pakyong Airport|url=https://www.business-standard.com/article/pti-stories/aai-sikkim-govt-sign-mou-for-pakyong-airport-115070301322_1.html|newspaper=Business Standard India|date=2015-07-03|accessdate=2019-03-23|first=Press Trust of|last=India|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180612163333/https://www.business-standard.com/article/pti-stories/aai-sikkim-govt-sign-mou-for-pakyong-airport-115070301322_1.html|archive-date=2018-06-12|dead-url=no}}</ref>抗议导致的延期以及跑道边的山坡发生[[泥石流|泥石流]]导致工程停顿两次，将工程成本从30.9亿卢比提高到60.5亿卢比。<ref name=":3" />出于整治泥石流的考虑，建造过程中尽可能采用了生态化的山体固化技术。<ref>{{Cite web|title=The Pakyong Airport landslide issue in India|url=https://blogs.agu.org/landslideblog/2015/01/29/the-pakyong-airport-landslide-issue-in-india/|work=The Landslide Blog|date=2015-01-29|language=en|author=|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180612184456/https://blogs.agu.org/landslideblog/2015/01/29/the-pakyong-airport-landslide-issue-in-india/|archive-date=2018-06-12|dead-url=no}}</ref>

2018年3月5日，[[印度空軍|印度空军]]一架[[多尼爾228|多尼尔228]]飞机在帕京机场着陆，是第一架在该机场着陆的飞机<ref>{{Cite news|title=IAF's Dornier aircraft lands at Pakyong airport|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/industry/transportation/airlines-/-aviation/iafs-dornier-aircraft-lands-at-pakyong-airport/articleshow/63174344.cms|newspaper=The Economic Times|date=2018-03-05|author=|work=|access-date=2019-03-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180612142902/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/industry/transportation/airlines-/-aviation/iafs-dornier-aircraft-lands-at-pakyong-airport/articleshow/63174344.cms|archive-date=2018-06-12|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。民航方面，香料航空在同年1月获准开通前往[[加尔各答|加尔各答]]的航线，并在3月10日用[[DASH-8|庞巴迪Dash 8]] 400系列78座飞机试行在该机场着陆。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Spicejet Bombardier Q400 78 seater aircraft makes historic landing at Pakyong Airport – The Voice of Sikkim|url=https://voiceofsikkim.com/2018/03/10/spicejet-bombardier-q400-78-seater-aircraft-makes-historic-landing-at-pakyong-airport/|language=en|author=|date=|publisher=|access-date=2019-03-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180828023404/https://voiceofsikkim.com/2018/03/10/spicejet-bombardier-q400-78-seater-aircraft-makes-historic-landing-at-pakyong-airport/|archive-date=2018-08-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>

2018年5月5日，帕京机场获得了{{Link-en|印度民用航空总局|Directorate General of Civil Aviation (India)}}的商用航空许可。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Sikkim's Pakyong Airport gets license|url=http://www.millenniumpost.in/kolkata/sikkims-pakyong-airport-gets-license-298074|author=|date=|publisher=Millennium Post|access-date=2019-03-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180924224904/http://www.millenniumpost.in/kolkata/sikkims-pakyong-airport-gets-license-298074|archive-date=2018-09-24|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 结构 ==
帕京机场原本估计造价350[[克若|克若]]（35亿卢比）<ref name=":2" />，由[[印度机场管理局|印度机场管理局]]建造。[[跑道|跑道]]长宽为{{Convert|1700 by 30|m|ft}}，[[滑行道|滑行道]]长{{Convert|116|m|ft}}，[[機坪|机坪]]面积为{{Convert|106 by 76|m|ft}}，可同时容纳两架[[ATR_72|ATR 72]]民航机。<ref name=":3" /><ref name=":4">{{Cite news|title=Sikkim gets its first airport and the country its 100th at Pakyong|url=https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/other-states/what-you-need-to-know-about-pakyong-airport-in-sikkim-indias-100th-airport/article25026303.ece|newspaper=The Hindu|date=2018-09-24|issn=0971-751X|language=en|author=|work=|access-date=2019-03-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180925103526/https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/other-states/what-you-need-to-know-about-pakyong-airport-in-sikkim-indias-100th-airport/article25026303.ece|archive-date=2018-09-25|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 军事用途 ==
帕京机场距离中国和印度边境的[[实际控制线|实际控制线]]只有60公里<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":4" />，所以具有重要的军事意义。[[2017年中印军队洞朗对峙事件|2017年中印军队洞朗对峙事件]]就发生在该机场以西54公里，[[中华人民共和国|中国]]、[[印度|印度]]、[[不丹|不丹]]三国交界的[[洞朗|洞朗]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=China is telling India to accept changing realities|url=https://www.scmp.com/week-asia/geopolitics/article/2102547/standoff-china-telling-india-accept-changing-realities|work=South China Morning Post|date=2017-07-16|accessdate=2019-03-22|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180821160359/https://www.scmp.com/week-asia/geopolitics/article/2102547/standoff-china-telling-india-accept-changing-realities|archive-date=2018-08-21|dead-url=no}}</ref>。据报道，如有必要，印度空军的飞机有能力在该机场降落<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|title=Good news: Sikkim's first airport ready to start operations, 5 facts you need to know|url=https://www.dnaindia.com/business/report-good-news-sikkim-s-first-airport-ready-to-start-operations-5-facts-you-need-to-know-2651714|work=DNA India|date=2018-08-19|accessdate=2019-03-22|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190207231644/https://www.dnaindia.com/business/report-good-news-sikkim-s-first-airport-ready-to-start-operations-5-facts-you-need-to-know-2651714|archive-date=2019-02-07|dead-url=no}}</ref>。这引发了[[印度内政部|印度内政部]]和{{Link-en|民用航空部 (印度)|Ministry of Civil Aviation (India)|民用航空部}}有关机场管辖权的争议。内政部支持当地警察管理机场，民航局支持负责印度境内其他59个机场的[[中央工业安全部队|中央工业安全部队]]管理机场<ref>{{Cite web|title=2 mantris spar over Sikkim airport security - Times of India|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/2-mantris-spar-over-sikkim-airport-security/articleshow/65126190.cms|work=The Times of India|accessdate=2019-03-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181206070919/https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/2-mantris-spar-over-sikkim-airport-security/articleshow/65126190.cms|archive-date=2018-12-06|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

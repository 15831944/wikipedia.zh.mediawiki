'''嘉芙蓮·韋斯特'''（{{lang-en|Catherine West}}，{{bd|1966年|9月14日}}），[[英國|英國]][[工黨_(英國)|工黨]]籍政治人物，自2015年5月起擔任[[霍恩西和伍德格林_(英國國會選區)|霍恩西和伍德格林選區]][[英國下議院|國會議員]]。
==生平==
===早年生涯===
韋斯特在1966年9月14日<ref name=newhamrecorder />出生於[[澳洲|澳洲]][[雪梨_(城市)|雪梨]]<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.abc.net.au/radionational/programs/religionandethicsreport/australian-born-quaker-elected-to-the-uk-parliament/6467314|title=Australian-born Quaker elected to the UK Parliament|publisher=Australian Broadcasting Corporation|date=2015-05-13|accessdate=2019-01-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150520021535/http://www.abc.net.au/radionational/programs/religionandethicsreport/australian-born-quaker-elected-to-the-uk-parliament/6467314|archive-date=2015-05-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>，並在當地長大<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.thejc.com/news/uk-news/new-labour-mp-catherine-west-seeks-strong-ties-with-jewish-community-1.66827|title=New Labour MP Catherine West seeks strong ties with Jewish community|publisher=The Jewish Chronicle|date=2015-05-27|accessdate=2019-01-01}}</ref>。她曾入讀{{tsl|en|https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ravenswood_School_for_Girls|雷文斯伍德女子學校}}，其後入讀[[雪梨大學|雪梨大學]]<ref name=newhamrecorder>{{cite news|url=https://www.newhamrecorder.co.uk/seasonal/election/labour_s_catherine_west_i_know_how_to_beat_liberal_democrats_1_3989028|title=Labour’s Catherine West: ‘I know how to beat Liberal Democrats’|publisher=Newham Recorder|date=2015-03-13|accessdate=2019-01-01|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170305020546/https://www.newhamrecorder.co.uk/seasonal/election/labour_s_catherine_west_i_know_how_to_beat_liberal_democrats_1_3989028|archivedate=2017-03-05}}</ref>，之後在[[倫敦大學亞非學院|倫敦大學亞非學院]]取得中國政治學碩士學位<ref name=about>{{cite web|url=https://www.catherinewest.org.uk/about/|title=About|work=Catherine West MP|publisher=Labour|accessdate=2019-01-01}}</ref>。

===政治生涯===
2002年5月2日至2014年5月22日間，韋斯特曾為{{tsl|en|Tollington (ward)|多靈頓選區}}{{tsl|en|Islington London Borough Council|伊斯林頓倫敦自治市議會}}議員<ref name=newhamrecorder />。她曾在2004年至2013年10月10日擔任該議會的工黨黨團領袖<ref name=islingtontribune>{{cite news|url=http://archive.islingtontribune.com/news/2012/nov/islington-council-leader-faces-contest-race-be-mp|title=Islington Council leader faces contest in race to be MP|publisher=Islington Tribune|date=2012-11-23|accessdate=2019-01-02}}</ref>，並在2010年10月6日至2013年10月10日間擔任該議會領袖<ref name=islingtontribune />。

在[[2015年英國大選|2015年英國大選]]中，韋斯特當選[[霍恩西和伍德格林_(英國國會選區)|霍恩西和伍德格林選區]]國會議員<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.bbc.com/news/politics/constituencies/E14000752 | title=Hornsey & Wood Green Parliamentary constituency | publisher=BBC | accessdate=2015-05-07 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150506022710/http://www.bbc.com/news/politics/constituencies/E14000752 | archive-date=2015-05-06 | dead-url=no }}</ref>，不久後獲任命為負責[[美洲|美洲]]、[[亞太地區|亞太地區]]、[[大洋洲|大洋洲]]和[[英國海外領土|海外領土]]的[[外交及聯邦事務部|影子外交及聯邦事務國務大臣]]<ref name=about />。2015年9月，韋斯特曾聯同{{tsl|en|Tulip Siddiq|圖利普·西德迪克}}和[[凱爾·斯塔摩|凱爾·斯塔摩]]一同去信時任[[英國首相|首相]][[大衛·金馬倫|大衛·金馬倫]]要求採取緊急行動解決因[[敘利亞內戰|敘利亞內戰]]而導致的[[敘利亞內戰難民|難民危機]]<ref name="bdnews247">{{cite news|last=Pasha|first=Syed Nahas|url=http://www.bdnews24.com/world/2015/09/05/tulip-siddiq-urges-pm-cameron-to-take-urgent-action-to-address-refugee-crisis-in-europe|title=Tulip Siddiq urges PM Cameron to take urgent action to address refugee crisis in Europe|work=|location=London|publisher=Bdnews24.com|date=2015-09-05|accessdate=2015-09-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150906094948/http://bdnews24.com/world/2015/09/05/tulip-siddiq-urges-pm-cameron-to-take-urgent-action-to-address-refugee-crisis-in-europe|archive-date=2015-09-06|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="prothom-alo">{{cite news|last=|first=|url=https://en.prothomalo.com/international/news/78227/Tulip-seeks-action-to-end-refugee-crisis|title=Tulip seeks action to end refugee crisis|work=|location=Dhaka|publisher=Prothom Alo|date=2015-09-05|accessdate=2015-09-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180730140356/http://en.prothomalo.com/international/news/78227/Tulip-seeks-action-to-end-refugee-crisis|archive-date=2018-07-30|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

2016年[[英國去留歐盟公投|英國去留歐盟公投]]期間，韋斯特支持英國留在[[歐洲聯盟|歐洲聯盟]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-politics-eu-referendum-35616946|title=EU vote: Where the cabinet and other MPs stand|date=2016-06-22|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=2019-01-03|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181217104600/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-politics-eu-referendum-35616946|archive-date=2018-12-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>。在公投後，她成立了英歐關係國會小組<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.publications.parliament.uk/pa/cm/cmallparty/161012/uk-eu-relations.htm|title=House of Commons - Register Of All-Party Parliamentary Groups as at 12 October 2016: UK-EU Relations|first=The Committee Office, House of|last=Commons|website=www.publications.parliament.uk|accessdate=2017-06-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170214102944/https://www.publications.parliament.uk/pa/cm/cmallparty/161012/uk-eu-relations.htm|archive-date=2017-02-14|dead-url=no}}</ref>。2017年6月，韋斯特因為無視工黨黨鞭指令，支持一項使英國留在[[歐洲單一市場|歐洲單一市場]]的{{tsl|en|Speech from the throne|御座致辭}}修訂案，因而被撤去影子外交與聯邦事務國務大臣的職務<ref>{{cite news|last=Asthana|first=Anushka|url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2017/jun/29/jeremy-corbyn-sacks-three-frontbenchers-after-single-market-vote|title=Jeremy Corbyn sacks three frontbenchers after single market vote|work=The Guardian|date=2017-06-29|access-date=2017-06-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170629210309/https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2017/jun/29/jeremy-corbyn-sacks-three-frontbenchers-after-single-market-vote|archive-date=2017-06-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

2017年，韋斯特聯同[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]人權委員會副主席[[羅哲斯|羅哲斯]]、前[[自由民主黨_(英國)|自由民主黨]]黨魁[[艾思定|艾思定]]、上議院議員[[大衛·阿爾頓|大衛·阿爾頓]]、前外交及聯邦事務大臣[[聶偉敬|聶偉敬]]和[[御用大律師|御用大律師]]{{tsl|en|Geoffrey Nice|傑弗里·尼斯}}一同成立非政府組織[[香港監察|香港監察]]，以監察及匯報[[香港|香港]]的發展<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.post852.com/237541/%E7%BE%85%E5%93%B2%E6%96%AF%E7%89%BD%E9%A0%AD%E7%B5%84%E7%B9%94hong-kong-watch%E6%88%90%E7%AB%8B%E3%80%80%E5%BD%AD%E5%AE%9A%E5%BA%B7%E6%8C%87%E9%97%9C%E5%BF%83%E9%A6%99%E6%B8%AF%E4%B8%A6%E9%9D%9E/|title=羅哲斯牽頭組織Hong Kong Watch成立　彭定康指關心香港並非外國勢力插手|publisher=852郵報|date=2017-12-13|accessdate=2018-12-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180616051618/https://www.post852.com/237541/%e7%be%85%e5%93%b2%e6%96%af%e7%89%bd%e9%a0%ad%e7%b5%84%e7%b9%94hong-kong-watch%e6%88%90%e7%ab%8b%e3%80%80%e5%bd%ad%e5%ae%9a%e5%ba%b7%e6%8c%87%e9%97%9c%e5%bf%83%e9%a6%99%e6%b8%af%e4%b8%a6%e9%9d%9e/|archive-date=2018-06-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==個人生活==
除了英語，韋斯特亦懂包括[[現代標準漢語|現代標準漢語]]在內的五種語言。她亦曾在[[中國|中國]][[南京|南京]]教授英語<ref name=about />。

韋斯特為[[貴格會|貴格會]]信徒，她是2015年大選中當選的2名貴格會信徒之一（另一人為{{tsl|en|Ruth Cadbury|露絲·卡伯里}}）<ref>{{cite news | title = First Quaker MPs elected in a decade | url = http://www.quaker.org.uk/news/first-quaker-mps-elected-decade/ | work = Quakers in Britain | publisher = Religious Society of Friends (Quakers) | date = 2015-05-08| accessdate=2019-01-03|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150524100057/http://www.quaker.org.uk/news/first-quaker-mps-elected-decade/|archivedate=2015-05-24}}</ref>。

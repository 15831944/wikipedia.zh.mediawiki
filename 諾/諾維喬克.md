'''諾維喬克'''（{{lang-ru|новичок|Novichok|新手、新人}}<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.newscientist.com/article/2163502-what-are-novichok-nerve-agents-and-did-russia-do-it/|title=What are Novichok nerve agents and did Russia do it?|publisher=|access-date=2019-02-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181220181646/https://www.newscientist.com/article/2163502-what-are-novichok-nerve-agents-and-did-russia-do-it/|archive-date=2018-12-20|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=Oxford Russian Dictionary|publisher=Oxford University Press|edition=3|year=2000|page=265}}</ref>），是[[蘇聯|蘇聯]]與[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]於1971年至1993年間開發的一系列神經毒劑{{NoteTag|Jonathon B. Tucker writes that approval to commence research into "fourth generation" chemical weapons was given by the [[Central_Committee_of_the_Communist_Party|Central Committee of the Communist Party]] and the [[Soviet_Council_of_Ministers|Soviet Council of Ministers]] in May 1971. Vil Mirzayanov, the Russian scientist who first alerted the West to the existence of the Novichok agents, states that testing of Novichok-7 was successfully completed in 1993—after the signing of the [[Chemical_Weapons_Convention|Chemical Weapons Convention]] but before Russia ratified the treaty and when it came into force.<ref name="auto">{{Harvnb|Tucker|2006|p=231}}</ref><ref name="Cong">{{Citation|work=Global Proliferation of Weapons of Mass Destruction: Hearings Before the Permanent Subcommittee on Investigations of the Committee on Governmental Affairs, 104th Cong.|pages=393–405|year=1995|title=Dismantling the Soviet/Russian Chemical Weapons Complex: An Insider's View|first=Vil|last=Mirzayanov}}</ref>}}<ref name=Cong /><ref>{{Harvnb|Tucker|2006|pp=231–233}}</ref>。研發諾為喬克之科學家聲稱這是他們所研發出最毒的神經毒劑，有些衍生物的毒性可能為VX的五至八倍<ref name="Birstein"/><ref name="Albats">{{Harvnb|Albats|1994|pp=325–328}}</ref>，有些衍伸物質毒性可能為[[梭曼|梭曼]]（甲氟膦酸頻那酯）的十倍<ref>{{Harvnb|Croddy|Wirtz|Larsen|2001|p=201}}</ref>

諾維喬克為蘇聯FOLIANT計畫中的一環<ref name="auto"/><ref>{{Harvnb|Pitschmann|2014|p=1765}}</ref>，其有五種衍生物據信被用於軍事用途上<ref>{{Harvnb|Tucker|2006|p=233}}</ref>。最通用的為諾維喬克5號A-232<ref>{{Harvnb|Tucker|2006|p=253}}</ref>。諾維喬克試劑從未被用在戰場上<ref name=":0" />。英國政府證實諾維喬克藥劑在2018年3月被用於[[英國前俄羅斯間諜毒殺案|英國前俄羅斯間諜毒殺案]]上，四間來自世界各地[[禁止化學武器組織|禁止化學武器組織]]的實驗室一致確認，發生於英格蘭威爾特郡索爾茲伯里的英國前俄羅斯間諜毒殺案中有使用諾維喬克<ref name=":0">{{cite web |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/politics/sergei-and-yulia-skripal-in-salisbury-attack-was-a-novichok-nerve-agent-confirms-chemical-weapons-a8301121.html |title=Poison used on Sergei and Yulia Skripal in Salisbury attack was novichok nerve agent, confirms chemical weapons watchdog |newspaper=The Independent |date=12 April 2018 |author=Benjamin Kentish |access-date=2019-02-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180906103528/https://www.independent.co.uk/news/uk/politics/sergei-and-yulia-skripal-in-salisbury-attack-was-a-novichok-nerve-agent-confirms-chemical-weapons-a8301121.html |archive-date=2018-09-06 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。在此案發生四個月後，於威爾特郡埃姆斯伯里發生的2018埃姆斯伯里毒殺案同樣有檢驗出諾維喬克<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-44719639|title=Wiltshire pair poisoned by Novichok nerve agent|date=2018-07-04|work=BBC News|access-date=2018-07-04|language=en-GB|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180704205634/https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-44719639|archive-date=2018-07-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

米爾則雅諾夫（{{Lang|ru|Мирзаянов}}）是首次提供了這些毒劑相關描述的人<ref name="Vil" />。這些毒劑會散佈在氣溶膠的[[气溶胶|超細粉末]]中，而非氣體或蒸氣中；且具有十分獨特的性質。要么使用不屬於[[禁止化学武器公约|禁止化學武器公約]]管控物質的材料合成，或者是通過條約制度檢查無法檢測到的辦法，來合成一組性質相似的{{Tsl|en|Binary chemical weapon|二元混合型化學武器|二元試劑}}<ref name="Hidalgo">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/415742.stm|title=US dismantles chemical weapons|first=Louise|last=Hidalgo|date=9 August 1999|work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|accessdate=20 July 2007|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181220124328/http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/415742.stm|archive-date=2018-12-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>。該系列中最有效的化合物——諾維喬克-5和諾維喬克-7——據稱比VX強大約五到八倍<ref name="peplow">{{Citation|url=https://cen.acs.org/articles/96/i12/Nerve-agent-attack-on-spy-used-Novichok-poison.html|title=Nerve agent attack on spy used 'Novichok' poison|date=19 March 2018|volume=96|issue=12|work=[[Chemical_&_Engineering_News|Chemical & Engineering News]]|first=Mark|last=Peplow|accessdate=16 March 2018|page=3|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180315165924/https://cen.acs.org/articles/96/i12/Nerve-agent-attack-on-spy-used-Novichok-poison.html|archive-date=2018-03-15|dead-url=no}}</ref>。而「諾維喬克」名稱是指毒劑的二元形式，最終化合物由其代碼號（例如A-232）表示。第一組諾維喬克系列化合物實際上是已知的V系列神經毒劑{{Tsl|en|VR (nerve agent)|VR神經毒劑|VR}}的二元試劑<ref name="peplow" />，而後續的諾維喬克試劑則是其他化合物的二元形式，例如A-232和A-234。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Z. POTENCIÁLNÍ BOJOVÉ CHEMICKÉ LÁTKY|author=|url=https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Zbynek_Kobliha/publication/288702130_Potential_Chemical_Warfare_Agents/links/569cdd9a08ae8f8ddc700c3b/Potential-Chemical-Warfare-Agents.pdf|last=Kobliha|first=Halámek E|journal=Chemicke Listy|issue=5|doi=|others=|year=2011|volume=105|page=323–333|pmid=|access-date=2019-02-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180317102147/https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Zbynek_Kobliha/publication/288702130_Potential_Chemical_Warfare_Agents/links/569cdd9a08ae8f8ddc700c3b/Potential-Chemical-Warfare-Agents.pdf|archive-date=2018-03-17|dead-url=no}}</ref>

根據1997年1月24日軍事情報摘要中{{Tsl|en|National Ground Intelligence Center|美國陸軍國家地面情報中心}}的機密報告顯示<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.globalsecurity.org/wmd/library/news/russia/1997/msg00043c.htm|title=Russia dodges chemical arms ban By Bill Gertz THE WASHINGTON TIMES Tuesday|first=John|last=Pike|website=www.globalsecurity.org|access-date=2019-02-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180423060909/https://www.globalsecurity.org/wmd/library/news/russia/1997/msg00043c.htm|archive-date=2018-04-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>，在「富利安特計劃」（{{Lang|en|Foliant programme}}）下開發的A-232毒劑、及其[[乙基|乙基]]類似物A-234，「與VX一樣具有劇毒，對梭曼的抑制處理具有抗性，比VX更難檢測和更容易合成」。據報吿顯示，這些藥劑的二元版本使用[[乙腈|乙腈]]和一種「偽裝成農藥[[前体|前體]]」的有機磷酸鹽。且[[A-234_(神經毒劑)|A-234]]的毒性可能為[[VX神經毒劑|VX]]的五至八倍<ref>{{Citation|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-43429152|title=Spy poisoning: Putin most likely behind attack – Johnson|date=16 March 2018|work=[[BBC_News|BBC News]]|accessdate=16 March 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181129222143/https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-43429152|archive-date=2018-11-29|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="peplow" />

=== 化學 ===
[[File:Novichok_synthesis.png|缩略图]]修飾過的[[乙二醇|乙二醇]]與[[三氯化磷|三氯化磷]]反應，來生成稱之為{{Tsl|en|Phospholane|磷雜環戊烷}}的環結構。最後的氯原子被氟取代（親核取代）。最後該化合物會與[[光氣肟|光氣肟類]]氯化物反應，以打開環鏈並產生產物氟[[膦|膦]]酸鹽。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/288702130|title=Potential Chemical Warfare Agents|publisher=Chemicke Listy|author=Emil Halámek and Zbynek Kobliha|date=May 2011}}</ref>]]
按照化學武器專家[[喬納森·塔克|喬納森·塔克]]（{{Lang|en|Jonathan Tucker}}）的說法，在「富利安特計劃」中開發的第一組二元配方是用於製造「第33號物質」（即{{Tsl|en|VR (nerve agent)|VR神經毒劑|VR}}毒劑），該毒劑與廣為人知的[[VX神經毒劑|VX]]非常相似，但不同之處僅在於其分子鏈上的氮與氧原子上的烷基[[取代基|取代基]]。而「這類化學武器的代號是諾維喬克。」<ref>{{cite journal|title=Nerve agent attack on spy used 'Novichok' poison|url=https://cen.acs.org/articles/96/i12/Nerve-agent-attack-on-spy-used-Novichok-poison.html|last=Peplow|first=Mark|date=19 March 2018|journal=Chemical & Engineering News|issue=12|volume=96|page=3|access-date=28 March 2018|author=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180315165924/https://cen.acs.org/articles/96/i12/Nerve-agent-attack-on-spy-used-Novichok-poison.html|archive-date=2018-03-15|dead-url=no}}</ref>

社會上已經報導了諾維喬克的各種各樣的潛在結構。其衍生物都具有機磷核心（有時<chem>P=O</chem>被<chem>P=S</chem>或<chem>P=Se</chem>取代），而諾維喬克常被用於描述的是{{Tsl|en|Phosphoramidate|氨基磷酸酯}}類或{{Tsl|en|Phosphonate|膦酸酯}}類的神經毒素，而最為通常是氟化的酯（參見{{Tsl|en|Monofluorophosphate|單氟磷酸酯}}）。諾維喬克的有機[[基團|基團]]種類更多，然而其共同的取代基則是[[光氣肟|光氣肟]]或其類似基團。後者本身就是一種強效化學武器（如{{Tsl|en|Nettle agent|蕁麻毒素}}），這預計會增加諾維喬克毒劑所可能造成的毒害作用。許多聲稱諾維喬克類的結構還含有{{Tsl|en|Cross-linking agent|交聯劑}}[[基序|基序]]，後者可以在幾個位置與[[乙酰胆碱酯酶|乙酰膽鹼酯酶]]的活性位點發生共價結合，這可能也解釋了為什麼是毒劑的特徵就是酶的快速變性。

一位來自[[加州大學聖地亞哥分校|加州大學聖地亞哥分校]]的化學家——佐蘭·拉迪奇（{{Lang|en|Zoran Radić}}）將「米爾則雅諾夫版本的A-232結構」和「[[乙酰胆碱酯酶|乙酰膽鹼酯酶]]的[[活性位點|活性位點]]」之間的抗性進行了計算機對接研究。該模型預測兩者具有很高的[[結合親和力|結合親和力]]，且A-232與[[乙酰胆碱酯酶|乙酰膽鹼酯酶]]活性位點中的[[絲氨酸|絲氨酸]][[殘基|殘基]]緊密配合形成[[共價鍵|共價鍵]]；其與[[沙林|沙林]]和[[梭曼|梭曼]]這類已建立數據庫的神經毒劑一樣，具有相類似結合模式。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=U.K. attack shines spotlight on deadly nerve agent developed by Soviet scientists|url=http://www.sciencemag.org/news/2018/03/uk-attack-shines-spotlight-deadly-nerve-agent-developed-soviet-scientists|last=Stone|first=R.|date=19 March 2018|journal=[[Science_(journal)|Science]]|doi=10.1126/science.aat6324|access-date=28 March 2018|author=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180325074620/http://www.sciencemag.org/news/2018/03/uk-attack-shines-spotlight-deadly-nerve-agent-developed-soviet-scientists|archive-date=2018-03-25|dead-url=no}}</ref>

'''諾布爾汽車'''（Noble Automotive Ltd.）是[[英國|英國]]的一家跑車企業，總部位於[[萊斯特|萊斯特]]。諾布爾汽車創建於1999年，由Lee Noble創立於西約克郡[[利茲|利茲]]<ref name="Fenix Automotive">{{cite web | url = http://www.carmagazine.co.uk/News/Search-Results/Industry-News/Lee-Noble-announces-his-new-supercar-project/ | title = Lee Noble announces his new supercar project | accessdate = 19 November 2009 | date = 19 November 2009 | publisher = Bauer Media Limited }}</ref>。2009年Lee Noble出售該公司之後，諾布爾汽車搬遷到萊斯特<ref>{{cite web |last=Leicester |first=John F, |url=http://www.thisisleicestershire.co.uk/news/163-200-000-car-built-county/article-1275965-detail/article.html |title=Noble car to be built in Leicestershire |publisher=Thisisleicestershire.co.uk |date=22 August 2009 |accessdate=9 August 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090826134319/http://www.thisisleicestershire.co.uk/news/163-200-000-car-built-county/article-1275965-detail/article.html |archivedate=26 August 2009 |df=dmy-all }}</ref>。

'''諾姆·麥克唐納'''（Norman Gene）<ref>The capitalization of Norm Macdonald's surname has been inconsistently reported in publications such as ''TVGuide'', but books discussing Norm such as Shales (2003) and [http://books.google.com/books?id=hAdZIuU0wFgC&pg=PA60&dq=norm+macdonald&hl=en&ei=KtvcTdDWFomosQPcvI2SBw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=6&ved=0CEoQ6AEwBQ#v=onepage&q=norm%20macdonald&f=false Crawford] (2000), the Game Show Network, and Comedy Central ''[[Sports_Show_with_Norm_Macdonald|Sports Show with Norm Macdonald]]'' and comedy CD all consistently report "Macdonald" (lowercase "d") as his surname.</ref><ref name="TVGuide">{{cite web |url=http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/norm-macdonald168320 |title=Norm Macdonald biography |publisher=[[TV_Guide|TVGuide.com]] |accessdate=11 December 2015 |archivedate=2015-12-11 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6dhR8i4tQ?url=http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/norm-macdonald/168320 |deadurl=no }}</ref>是[[加拿大|加拿大]]的一位演員、作家、製作人和喜劇人。他曾是[[週六夜現場|週六夜現場]]五季的固定來賓，並且曾主持了三年的Weekend Update。在1999年至2001年，他曾創建了諾姆秀（The Norm Show）。他被喜劇中心評為最偉大的100名喜劇演員第83位。

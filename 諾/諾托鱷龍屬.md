'''諾托鱷龍屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Notochampsa''）是種已滅絕[[鱷形超目|鱷形超目]]，是[[原鱷亞目|原鱷亞目]]的一屬<ref name=WAD90>{{cite journal |doi=10.1098/rstb.1990.0185 |last=Walker |first=A. D. |year=1990 |title=A revision of ''Sphenosuchus acutus'' Haughton, a crocodylomorph reptile from the Elliot Formation (Late Triassic or Early Jurassic) of South Africa |journal=Philosophical Transactions: Biological Sciences |volume=330 |issue=1256 |pages=1–120}}</ref>，化石被發現於[[南非|南非]]的[[卡魯超群|卡魯超群]]的[[艾略特組|艾略特組]]上層、以及[[克萊倫斯組|克萊倫斯組]]（Clarens Formation）下層，地質年代相當於[[侏羅紀|侏羅紀]]早期<ref name=NDS75>{{cite journal |last=Nash |first=D. S. |year=1975 |title=The morphology and relationships of a crocodilian, ''Orthosuchus stormbergi'', from the Upper Triassic of Lesotho |journal=Annals of the South African Museum |volume=67 |issue=1975)(7 |pages= 227–329}}</ref><ref name=DJF05>{{cite journal |doi=10.1016/j.jafrearsci.2005.07.014 |last=Durand |first=J. F. |year=2005 |title=Major African contributions to Palaeozoic and Mesozoic vertebrate palaeontology |journal=Journal of African Earth Sciences |volume=43 |issue=1–3 |series=Phanerozoic Evolution of Africa |pages=53–82}}</ref><ref name=KAR84>{{cite journal |last=Kitching |first=J. W. |coauthors= and Raath, M. A. |year=1984 |title=Fossils from the Elliot and Clarens formations (Karoo sequence) of the northeastern Cape, Orange Free State and Lesotho, and a suggested biozonation based on tetrapods |journal=Paleontologica Africana |volume=25 |pages=111–125}}</ref>。

在1904年，[[羅伯特·布魯姆|羅伯特·布魯姆]]（Robert Broom）敘述、命名這些化石，[[模式種|模式種]]是'''伊氏諾托鱷龍'''（''N. istedana''）<ref name=BR04>{{cite journal |last=Broom |first=R. |year=1904 |title= On a new crocodilian genus (''Notochampsa'') from the upper Stormberg beds of South Africa |journal=Geological Magazine, new series, decade 5 |volume=1 |pages=582–584}}</ref>。同時，布魯姆還命名第二個種，長足諾托鱷龍（''N. longipes''）。在1924年，長足諾托鱷龍被建立為新屬，[[紅鱷龍屬|紅鱷龍屬]]（''Erythrochampsa''）<ref name=HSH24>{{cite journal |last=Haughton |first=S. H. |year=1924 |title=The fauna and stratigraphy of the Stormberg Series |journal=Annals of the South African Museum |volume=12 |pages=323–497}}</ref>。

[[諾托鱷龍科|諾托鱷龍科]]（Notochampsidae）名稱來自於諾托鱷龍屬，近年學界較少使用諾托鱷龍科，本科普遍被認為可能是原鱷科的[[異名|異名]]<ref name=TS>{{Cite web |url=http://www.taxonsearch.org/dev/taxon_edit.php?tax_id=593&Action=View |title=Taxon Search - Notochampsidae |access-date=2009-09-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080405193833/http://www.taxonsearch.org/dev/taxon_edit.php?Action=View&tax_id=593 |archive-date=2008-04-05 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。在過去歷史中，諾托鱷龍科曾經包含：[[佩德弋鱷|佩德弋鱷]]、[[原鱷|原鱷]]、''Dyoplax''、''Platyognathus''<ref name=RAS56>{{cite book |last=Romer |first=A. S. |year=1956 |title=Osteology of the Reptiles |publisher=University of Chicago Press |location=Chicago |isbn=0-89464-985-X}}</ref>，以及[[直鱷|直鱷]]、''Microchampsa''<ref name=SR73>{{cite book |last=Steel |first=R. |year=1973 |chapter=Crocodylia |editor=Kuhn, O. (ed.) |title=Handbuch der Palaoherpetologie |edition=16 |publisher=G. Fischer Verlag |location=Stuttgart |pages=1–116}}</ref>。 諾托鱷龍曾經被歸類於[[喙頭鱷亞目|喙頭鱷亞目]]<ref name=HFV25>{{cite journal |doi=10.1007/BF02118235 |last=Huene |first=F. von |year=1925 |title=Die Bedeutung der ''Sphenosuchus''-Gruppe fur den Ursprung der Krokodile |journal=Z. Indukt. Abstamm.-u. Vererblehre |volume=38 |pages=307–322}}</ref>。

'''諾基亞Lumia 735'''（由'''微軟Lumia 735''' 替代）是一款由[[微軟移動|微軟移動]]開發的[[Windows_Phone_8.1|Windows Phone 8.1]][[智能手機|智能手機]]。 亮相於2014年9月4日的[[柏林IFA|柏林IFA]]，該設備是一個中檔智能手機，并以“自拍”爲賣點——一個500萬像素，廣角前置攝像頭。這款手機將會在某些市場推出3G雙卡雙待版本，以Lumia730品牌銷售。<ref name=pcworld-835>{{cite web|title=Microsoft announces two Lumia phones, always-on Cortana, and clever new mobile accessories|url=http://www.pcworld.com/article/2601743/microsoft-announces-two-lumia-phones-always-on-cortana-and-clever-new-mobile-accessories.html|website=PC World|accessdate=12 September 2014}}</ref><ref name=cnet-735>{{cite web|title=Nokia Lumia 735 Review: The Lumia 735 is cheery, comfy, cheap and has 4G|url=http://www.cnet.com/products/nokia-lumia-735/|website=CNET|accessdate=12 September 2014}}</ref>

2015年5月，微軟發布了微軟品牌的的改進版本的硬件并内置更高版本的軟體。<ref name=WindowsMobilePowerUserMicrosoftLumia735>{{cite news|last=Hassan|first=Mehedi|title=Microsoft Lumia 735 might be coming to Verizon soon.|url=http://wmpoweruser.com/microsoft-lumia-735-might-be-coming-to-verizon-soon/|newspaper=Windows Mobile PowerUser|date=6 May 2015}}</ref>

==特點==
===硬件===
諾基亞Lumia 735擁有1.2 GHz [[Multi-core_processor|quad-core]] [[ARM_Holdings|ARM]] [[ARM_Cortex-A7|Cortex-A7]]，和[[Qualcomm|Qualcomm]] [[Adreno|Adreno]] 305 [[GPU|GPU]]，和擁有1GB的系統[[内存|内存]]. 

諾基亞Lumia 735配備了8GB的內部存儲容量，同時還支持[[microSD|microSD]]擴展。

===作業系統和軟體===
諾基亞Lumia 735 运行[[Windows_Phone_8.1|Windows Phone 8.1]][[作業系統|作業系統]]并帶有Lumia Denim。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pcworld.com/article/2601743/microsoft-announces-two-lumia-phones-always-on-cortana-and-clever-new-mobile-accessories.html |title=Microsoft releases two new Lumia Phones, reveal new features |publisher=PC World |accessdate=8 November 2014}}</ref>

'''諾森伯蘭開發計劃'''（'''Northumberland Development Project'''）是[[英超|英超]]球隊[[熱刺|熱刺]]修建一座新的俱樂部主場球場，以取代[[白鹿巷球場|白鹿巷球場]]的計劃。<ref name="Reveal">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/t/tottenham_hotspur/7699135.stm|title=Tottenham reveal new ground plan |date=2008-10-30|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=2008-12-24}}</ref>。目前該計劃還沒有確定開始日期。新球場預計可以容納 58,000名觀眾<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tottenhamhotspur.com/futureplans/scheme/masterplan.html|title=Masterplan|publisher=Tottenham Hotspur|accessdate=2009-08-21}}</ref>。新的球場將建設在現在的白鹿巷球場的附近<ref name="Images">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport1/hi/football/teams/t/tottenham_hotspur/7784290.stm|title=Spurs reveal images of new ground|date=2008-12-15|publisher=BBC Sport|accessdate=2008-12-24}}</ref>。計劃預計將耗資4億英鎊<ref name="Telegraph">{{cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/leagues/premierleague/tottenham/3454004/Tottenham-Hotspur-release-plans-for-new-stadium-as-Harry-Redknapp-praised-Football.html|title=Tottenham Hotspur release plans for new stadium as Harry Redknapp praised|last=Isaacs|first=Marc|date=2008-11-14|work=Daily Telegraph|accessdate=2008-12-24}}</ref>。新球場將會公開以企業贊助的方式征名，而非繼續使用白鹿巷的名稱<ref name="Reveal"/>。

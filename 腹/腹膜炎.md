'''腹膜炎'''，是指一種發生於[[腹膜|腹膜]]的[[炎症反應|炎症反應]]。該反應主要由[[細菌|細菌]][[感染|感染]]、[[化學物質|化學物質]]、物理性傷害等因素引起，且很可能因沒有及時[[治療|治療]]而危及[[生命|生命]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmedhealth/PMHT0022963/|title=Peritonitis - National Library of Medicine|accessdate=2018-01-05|last=pmhdev|work=PubMed Health}}</ref>其徵狀可能包含劇烈[[疼痛|疼痛]]、腹部腫脹、[[發燒|發燒]]或[[體重|體重]]減少等。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/peritonitis/|title=Peritonitis|accessdate=2018-01-05|work=nhs.uk|language=en-GB}}</ref>部分或整個腹部可能因此對痛覺敏感。<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=wGclDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA979#v=onepage&q&f=false|title=Ferri's Clinical Advisor 2018 E-Book: 5 Books in 1|last=Ferri|first=Fred F.|date=2017-05-25|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|year=|isbn=9780323529570|location=|pages=|language=en|chapter=}}</ref>[[併發症|併發症]]可能有[[休克|休克]]及[[急性呼吸窘迫症候群|急性呼吸窘迫症候群]]等。<ref name=":1">{{Cite news|url=http://www.merckmanuals.com/professional/gastrointestinal-disorders/acute-abdomen-and-surgical-gastroenterology/acute-abdominal-pain|title=Acute Abdominal Pain - Gastrointestinal Disorders - Merck Manuals Professional Edition|newspaper=Merck Manuals Professional Edition|accessdate=2018-01-05|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.merckmanuals.com/home/digestive-disorders/symptoms-of-digestive-disorders/acute-abdominal-pain#v14496269|title=Acute Abdominal Pain - Digestive Disorders - Merck Manuals Consumer Version|newspaper=Merck Manuals Consumer Version|accessdate=2018-01-05|language=en-US}}</ref>

腹膜炎的成因有[[腸胃穿孔|腸胃穿孔]]、[[胰腺炎|胰腺炎]]、[[骨盆腔發炎|骨盆腔發炎]]、[[胃及十二指腸潰瘍|胃及十二指腸潰瘍]]、[[肝硬化|肝硬化]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://webcache.googleusercontent.com/search?q=cache:zFrn8vu51yQJ:https://www.cgh.org.tw/tw/content/depart/AB00/DL/B0000000039.pdf+&cd=18&hl=en&ct=clnk&gl=tw|title=肝硬化病人 小心腹膜炎|accessdate=2018-01-05|last=dress|work=webcache.googleusercontent.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://vector.pixnet.net/blog/post/37306045-%E8%82%9D%E7%A1%AC%E5%8C%96%E4%BD%B5%E7%99%BC%E4%B9%8B%E8%87%AA%E7%99%BC%E6%80%A7%E8%85%B9%E8%86%9C%E7%82%8E|title=肝硬化併發之自發性腹膜炎 @ 秤子維的秘密花園 :: 痞客邦 PIXNET ::|last=(vector)|first=vector|newspaper=秤子維的秘密花園|accessdate=2018-01-05|language=zh-TW}}</ref>或[[闌尾炎|闌尾炎]]等。<ref name=":0" />風險因素有[[腹水|腹水]]及[[腹膜透析|腹膜透析]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://webcache.googleusercontent.com/search?q=cache:98eDslBLaVMJ:https://www.tmuh.org.tw/UploadFile/files/200%25E8%2585%25B9%25E8%2586%259C%25E9%2580%258F%25E6%259E%2590%25E6%2584%259F%25E6%259F%2593%25E8%2585%25B9%25E8%2586%259C%25E7%2582%258E.pdf+&cd=10&hl=en&ct=clnk&gl=tw|title=腹膜透析感染腹膜炎|accessdate=2018-01-05|last=operator|work=webcache.googleusercontent.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.kmuh.org.tw/www/kmcj/data/9012/4853.htm|title=連續可活動性腹膜透析(CAPD)病人的腹膜炎|accessdate=2018-01-05|work=www.kmuh.org.tw}}</ref>等。<ref name=":1" />其診察方式主要以[[理學檢查|理學檢查]]、[[血液檢查|血液檢查]]、[[醫學影像|醫學影像]]等為基礎。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nhsdirect.wales.nhs.uk/encyclopaedia/p/article/peritonitis/|title=NHS Direct Wales - Encyclopaedia : Peritonitis|accessdate=2018-01-05|work=www.nhsdirect.wales.nhs.uk}}</ref>

其治療通常包含[[抗細菌藥|抗細菌藥]]、[[靜脈注射|靜脈注射]]、[[鎮痛藥|鎮痛藥]]及手術等。<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":0" />其他療法可能包含{{Link-en|鼻胃管灌食|Nasogastric intubation}}或[[輸血|輸血]]等。<ref name=":1" />若無接受治療，患者將可能在數日內[[死亡|死亡]]。<ref name=":1" />

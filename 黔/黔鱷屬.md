'''黔鱷屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Qianosuchus''}}）是種水生[[主龍類|主龍類]]，生存於[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]中期（[[安尼西階|安尼西階]]）的[[中國|中國]][[貴州省|貴州省]][[盤縣|盤縣]]，屬於[[關嶺組|關嶺組]]，約2億4000萬年前。化石是兩個幾乎完整的身體骨骼、一個壓碎的頭顱骨<ref name=NSJ11>{{cite journal|last=Nesbitt|first=S.J.|year=2011 |title=The early evolution of archosaurs: relationships and the origin of major clades|journal=Bulletin of the American Museum of Natural History|volume=352|pages=1–292|url=http://digitallibrary.amnh.org/dspace/bitstream/2246/6112/1/B352.pdf|doi=10.1206/352.1}}</ref>。

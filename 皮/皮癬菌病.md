<!-- 定義和症狀 -->
'''皮癬菌病'''（Dermatophytosis），也稱為'''金钱癣'''，是一種[[真菌感染|真菌感染]]引起的[[皮膚病|皮膚病]]<ref name=CDC2015Def>{{cite web|title=Definition of Ringworm|url=https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/definition.html|website=CDC|accessdate=5 September 2016|date=December 6, 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160905114011/http://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/definition.html|archivedate=5 September 2016|df=}}</ref>，通常它會導致皮膚發紅、發癢、脫屑圓形皮疹<ref name=CDC2015Sym/>，受影響的區域可能會脫髮<ref name=CDC2015Sym/>，感染後四到十四天開始出現症狀<ref name=CDC2015Sym>{{cite web|title=Symptoms of Ringworm Infections|url=https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/symptoms.html|website=CDC|accessdate=5 September 2016|date=December 6, 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160120042431/http://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/symptoms.html|archivedate=20 January 2016|df=}}</ref>，在感染時間內可能會影響多個區域<ref name=Dom2013/>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
大約40種真菌會引起皮癬<ref name=CDC2015Def/>，主要病原菌是[[毛癬菌|毛癬菌]]、{{le|小孢子菌|Microsporum}}或[[表皮癬菌|表皮癬菌]]類<ref name=CDC2015Def/>。危險因子包括使用公共淋浴，有肢體接觸的運動（如[[摔跤|摔跤]]）、過度出汗、與動物接觸、[[肥胖|肥胖]]以及[[免疫抑制|免疫功能低下]]<ref name=CDC2015Risk/><ref name=Dom2013/>，癬可以在人與動物或人與人之間傳播<ref name=CDC2015Risk>{{cite web|title=Ringworm Risk & Prevention|url=https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/risk-prevention.html|website=CDC|accessdate=5 September 2016|date=December 6, 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160907031747/http://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/risk-prevention.html|archivedate=7 September 2016|df=}}</ref>。診斷通常基於外觀和症狀<ref name=CDC2015Diag/>，也可以通過{{le|微生物培養|microbial culture}}和刮取病灶樣本進行顯微鏡檢確認<ref name=CDC2015Diag>{{cite web|title=Diagnosis of Ringworm|url=https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/diagnosis.html|website=CDC|accessdate=5 September 2016|date=December 6, 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160808220703/http://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/diagnosis.html|archivedate=8 August 2016|df=}}</ref>。

<!-- 預防及治療 -->
預防方法是保持皮膚乾燥，不要赤腳走路，也不要共用個人物品<ref name=CDC2015Risk/>。治療通常使用{{le|抗真菌藥膏|Antifungal cream}}，如[[克催瑪汝|克催瑪汝]]或[[咪康唑|咪康唑]]<ref name=CDC2015Tx/>，如果頭皮也受到感染，可能需要口服抗真菌藥如[[氟康唑|氟康唑]]<ref name=CDC2015Tx>{{cite web|title=Treatment for Ringworm|url=https://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/treatment.html|website=CDC|accessdate=5 September 2016|date=December 6, 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160903003922/http://www.cdc.gov/fungal/diseases/ringworm/treatment.html|archivedate=3 September 2016|df=}}</ref>。

<!-- 流行病學及歷史 -->
在任何時間點，高達20％的人口都可能被癬感染<ref name=Gh2009>{{cite book|author1=Mahmoud A. Ghannoum|author2=John R. Perfect|title=Antifungal Therapy|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kk_vBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA258|date=24 November 2009|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=978-0-8493-8786-9|page=258|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908204835/https://books.google.com/books?id=kk_vBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA258|archivedate=8 September 2017|df=}}</ref>，腹股溝感染在男性中更為常見，而頭皮和身體的感染在兩性中均相同<ref name=Dom2013/>。頭皮感染在兒童中最常見，而腹股溝感染最常見於老年人<ref name=Dom2013>{{cite book|last1=Domino|first1=Frank J.|last2=Baldor|first2=Robert A.|last3=Golding|first3=Jeremy|title=The 5-Minute Clinical Consult 2014|date=2013|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9781451188509|page=1226|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2C2MAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA1226|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160915020200/https://books.google.com/books?id=2C2MAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA1226|archivedate=2016-09-15|df=}}</ref>。[[古代史|古代史]]中即有癬的描述<ref>{{cite book|last1=Bolognia|first1=Jean L.|last2=Jorizzo|first2=Joseph L.|last3=Schaffer|first3=Julie V.|title=Dermatology|date=2012|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=978-0702051821|page=1255|edition=3|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=A78BaiEKnzIC&pg=RA1-PA1255|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160915014956/https://books.google.com/books?id=A78BaiEKnzIC&pg=RA1-PA1255|archivedate=2016-09-15|df=}}</ref>。

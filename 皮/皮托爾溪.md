'''皮托爾溪'''（{{lang-en|'''Pithole Creek'''}}）是位處[[美國|美國]][[賓夕凡尼亞州|賓夕凡尼亞州]]西北部[[阿勒格尼河|阿勒格尼河]]的支流，流域達{{convert|41.8|sqmi}}。皮托爾溪在距離奧爾波里斯{{convert|0.5|mi}}的一處與阿勒格尼河接壤。<ref name = "gertler">{{cite book|author=Gertler, Edward|title=Keystone Canoeing|publisher=Seneca Press|year=2004|ISBN=0-9749692-0-6|language=en}}</ref>皮托爾本身的意思為“坑洞”<ref>{{Cite news|title=A peak {{sic}} at Pithole's past|last=Hirschl|first=Beatrice Paul|date=1996-08-12|newspaper=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|page=D1|language=en}}</ref>，附近的鬼鎮[[皮托爾城|皮托爾城]]亦以此河流命名。<ref>{{Cite book |last=Darrah |first=William Culp |title=Pithole, the vanished city |year=1972 |location=Gettysburg |publisher=William Culp Darrah |isbn=0-913116-03-3 |lccn=72078194 |language = en|ref=harv|page=1}}</ref>

'''彼得·保羅·蒙哥馬利·布塔朱吉'''（{{lang-en|Peter Paul Montgomery Buttigieg}}，{{IPAc-en|ˈ|b|uː|t|ə|dʒ|ə|dʒ}}；{{bd|1982年|1月19日}}），美国[[民主党_(美国)|民主党]]籍政治人物，[[印第安纳州|印第安纳州]][[南本德_(印地安納州)|南本德]]市长。

== 生平 ==
布塔朱吉出生于印第安纳州南本德，其父母约瑟夫·布塔朱吉（Joseph Buttigieg）与珍妮弗·安妮·蒙哥马利（Jennifer Anne Montgomery）皆为[[圣母大学|圣母大学]]教授。<ref>{{cite web |last1=Trebay |first1=Guy |title=Mayor Pete Gets Married, Then Takes His Husband to a Pride Party |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/18/fashion/weddings/mayor-peter-buttigieg-wedding-democratic-party.html |website=The New York Times |accessdate=January 24, 2019 |date=June 18, 2018}}</ref>2004年，他以极优等（''magna cum laude''）成绩毕业于[[哈佛大学|哈佛大学]]，获历史与文学学士学位。<ref name=Alfaro-190123>{{cite news | url=https://www.businessinsider.com/pete-buttigieg-mayor-of-south-bend-for-president-2020-election-2019-1 | title=Pete Buttigieg, mayor of South Bend, Indiana, launches 2020 presidential bid | work=Business Insider | date=January 23, 2019 | accessdate=March 25, 2019 | first=Mariana | last=Alfaro}}</ref>此后他以[[罗德学者|罗德学者]]身份赴英国留学，2007年获[[牛津大学彭布罗克学院|牛津大学彭布罗克学院]][[哲学、政治学及经济学|哲学、政治学及经济学]]硕士学位。<ref name=Alfaro-190123 /><ref>{{cite web | url=http://business.nd.edu/news_and_events/speaker_series/ten_years_hence/ten_years_hence_bio.aspx?bio=10480&year=2012 | title =TEN YEARS HENCE: Pete Buttigieg, Mayor, City of South Bend | author =University of Notre Dame Mendoza College of Business| publisher =nd.edu | date =March 30, 2012}}</ref>

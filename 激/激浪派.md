'''激浪派'''（Fluxus）是1960年代至1970年代間活躍的一個激進的國際文藝流派<ref name="Friedman, Ken 2011. p.35">Friedman, Ken. 2011. "Fluxus: A Laboratory of Ideas." ''Fluxus and the Essential Qualities of Life''. Jacquelynne Baas, editor. Chicago: University of Chicago Press, p.35.</ref><ref>''Nationalencyclopedin'' (''Swedish National Encyclopedia''). 2016. "Fluxus." Accessible at: http://www.ne.se/uppslagsverk/encyclopedi/lång/fluxus Accessed September 11, 2016.</ref><ref>Wainwright, Lisa S. 2016. "Fluxus." ''Britannica Academic'' (Encyclopedia Britannica Online).</ref><ref name="Ruhé, Harry 1979">Ruhé, Harry. 1979. ''Fluxus, the Most Radical and Experimental Art Movement of the Sixties'' Amsterdam: Editions Galerie A.</ref><ref>Ruhé, Harry. 1999. "Introduction." ''25 Fluxus Stories'' Amsterdam: Tuja Books, p. 4.</ref>。他們提出了一些新的藝術主張，如[[迪克·希金斯|迪克·希金斯]]的“中介藝術”<ref>Higgins, Dick. 1966. "Intermedia." ''Something Else Newsletter''. Vol. 1, No. 1, February, pp. 1-3.</ref><ref>Higgins, Dick. 2001. "Intermedia" ''Multimedia: From Wagner to Virtual Reality''. Randall Packer and Ken Jordan, eds. New York: W.W. Norton and Co., pp. 27–32.</ref><ref>Higgins, Dick. 1984. ''Horizons: The Poetics and Theory of the Intermedia''. Carbondale and Edwardsville: Southern Illinois University Press</ref><ref>Hannah B Higgins,"The Computational Word Works of Eric Andersen and Dick Higgins", ''Mainframe Experimentalism: Early Digital Computing in the Experimental Arts, Hannah Higgins, & Douglas Kahn, eds., pp. 271-281</ref>、[[亨利·弗林特|亨利·弗林特]]的“[[觀念藝術|觀念藝術]]”<ref>Flynt, Henry. 1961. "Concept Art: Innperseqs." Reprinted in 1963: ''An Anthology''. La Monte Young, ed. New York: Jackson Mac Low and La Monte Young, np.</ref><ref>Flynt, Henry. 1963. "Essay: Concept Art: Provisional Version." ''An Anthology''. La Monte Young, ed. New York: Jackson Mac Low and La Monte Young, np.</ref>和[[白南准|白南准]]和[[沃尔夫·福斯特尔|沃尔夫·福斯特尔]]的“錄像藝術”<ref>Paik, Nam June. 1993. ''Nam June Paik: eine Data Base. La Biennale di Venezia. XLV Esposizione lnternazionale D’Arte, June 13 – October 10, 1993''. Klaus Bussmann and Florian Matzner, eds. Venice and Berlin: Biennale di Venezia and Edition Cantz.</ref><ref>Hanhardt, John and Ken Hakuta. 2012. ''Nam June Paik: Global Visionary''. London and Washington, D.C.: D. Giles, Ltd., in association with the Smithsonian American Art Museum.</ref><ref>Fundacio Joan Miro. 1979. ''Vostell. Environments Pintura Happenings Dibuixos Video de 1958 a 1978''. Barcelona: Fundacio Joan Miro.</ref>。受到[[杜尚|杜尚]]的影響，大多數激浪派藝術家以反商業和[[反藝術|反藝術]]為己任，強調概念而非成品的重要性<ref>{{Cite web|title = Fluxus Movement, Artists and Major Works|url = http://www.theartstory.org/movement-fluxus.htm|accessdate = 2015-10-06}}</ref>。而Fluxus一詞則由[[喬治·麥素納斯|喬治·麥素納斯]]創造于1961年<ref>{{Cite book|title = In the Spirit of Fluxus|last = Armstrong|first = Elizabeth|publisher = The Occasion of the Exhibition|year = 1993|isbn =9780935640403|location = Minneapolis|pages = 24}}</ref>。

1960年代的許多實驗藝術家都屬於這一流派，[[威斯巴登|威斯巴登]]、哥本哈根、斯德哥爾摩、阿姆斯特丹、倫敦和紐約都有大量激浪派藝術家存在。他們的興趣極為廣泛，而互相之間亦有衝突<ref>Zurbrugg, Nicholas. 1990. “A Spirit of Large Goals.” – Dada and Fluxus at Two Speeds. ''Fluxus!'' Nicholas Zurbrugg, Francesco Conz, and Nicholas Tsoutas, eds. Brisbane, Australia: Institute of Modern Art, p. 29.</ref><ref>Smith, Owen F. 1998. ''Fluxus: The History of an Attitude''. San Diego: San Diego State University Press.</ref>。比如儘管激浪派創始人之一的喬治·麥素納斯發表過所謂的《激浪派宣言》，但很少有其他激浪派藝術家對他的說法表示讚同，甚至很多藝術家都不認為他屬於激浪派<ref>Higgins, Dick. 1992. "Fluxus: Theory and Reception." ''Lund Art Press'', Vol II, No 2, pp. 25-46.</ref><ref>Higgins, Dick. 1998. "Fluxus: Theory and Reception." ''The Fluxus Reader'', Ken Friedman, ed. Chichester, West Sussex: Wiley Academy Editions, pp. 218-236.</ref>。雖然如此，激浪派也在一定程度上對開放“藝術”之定義做出了貢獻<ref>Tate Gallery. "Nam June Paik. Fluxus, Performance, Participation." Tate Online. Accessible at: http://www.tate.org.uk/whats-on/exhibition/nam-june-paik/nam-june-paik-room-guide/nam-june-paik-section-2 Accessed September 15, 2016.</ref>。

{{Chemical engineering}}

'''化學程序建模'''（{{lang-en|chemical process modeling}}）是[[化學工程|化學工程]][[程序設計_(化工)|程序設計]]所使用的[[電腦模擬|電腦模擬]]技術。建模過程通常使用專用軟體來定義一組相連接的系統<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=http://en.processium.com/process-simulation/chemical-process-simulation.htm|title=Chemical process simulation : Processium, process simulation (chemical process simulation and conception)|last=|first=|date=|website=|publisher=|access-date=2017-06-24|quote=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150710045707/http://en.processium.com/process-simulation/chemical-process-simulation.htm|archive-date=2015-07-10|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，求解系統[[穩態|穩態]]或動態行為以進行預測。系統組成與連結以[[過程流程圖|程序流程圖]]表示<ref name=":0" />。系統建模可能如兩物質於反應槽裡混合般簡易，也可能如整個[[氧化鋁|氧化鋁]]精煉製程般複雜<ref>{{cite web |url=http://icsoba.org/sites/default/files/2015paper/aluminapapers/AA10%20-%20Process%20simulation%20in%20VM-CBA%20alumina%20refinery.pdf |title=Process simulation in VM-CBA alumina refinery |last= |first= |date= |website= |publisher=The International Committee for Study of Bauxite, Alumina & Aluminium |access-date=2017-06-24 |quote=}}</ref>。化學程序建模需要了解囊括於模擬中的化學品與單元組件（如儲槽、泵、管道、加壓管線等）等之特性與規格。

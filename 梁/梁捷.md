'''梁捷''' ({{bd|1975年|2月11日||}})是一位广东企业家，[[UC优视|UC优视]]首席技术官。<ref>[http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=46476173  company profile], BusinessWeek</ref> 

==生平==
1975年出生于[[广东|广东]][[广州|广州]]，毕业于[[华南理工大学|华南理工大学]]，2004年与[[何小鹏|何小鹏]]共同创建[[UC优视|UC优视]]，<ref>[https://www.reuters.com/article/2014/06/11/us-alibaba-group-ucweb-idUSKBN0EM04P20140611 Alibaba to buy out UCWeb in China's biggest internet merger], ''Reuters'', June 11, 2014</ref><ref>[https://www.forbes.com/sites/jlim/2014/06/11/alibaba-fully-buys-out-ucweb-in-chinas-biggest-internet-deal-ever/ Alibaba Fully Buys Out UCWeb in China's Biggest Internet Deal Ever], ''Forbes'' magazine, June 11, 2014</ref>2013年[[UC优视|UC优视]]和[[阿里巴巴集团|阿里巴巴集团]]成立合资公司推出的移动搜索引擎[[神马搜索|神马搜索]]，其担任董事长。<ref>[http://www.marketwatch.com/story/alibaba-ucweb-plan-mobile-search-joint-venture-2014-04-28 Alibaba, UCWeb plan mobile-search joint venture], ''MarketWatch'', June 28, 2014</ref>2014年6月[[UC优视|UC优视]]被[[阿里巴巴集团|阿里巴巴集团]]收购。

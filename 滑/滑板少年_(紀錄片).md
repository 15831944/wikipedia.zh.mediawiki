《'''滑板少年'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Minding The Gap'''''}}）是美籍華裔青年導演劉冰的[[紀錄片|紀錄片]]。影片講述美國[[伊利諾伊州|伊利諾伊州]][[羅克福德|羅克福德]]三位熱愛滑板的年輕人走到一起的故事。[[爛番茄|爛番茄]]給予本片100%的新鮮度，影片獲得[[日舞影展|日舞影展]]美國紀錄片評審團特別獎最佳突破電影製作獎，並被提名為[[第91屆奧斯卡金像獎|第91屆奧斯卡金像獎]][[奧斯卡最佳紀錄片|最佳紀錄片獎]]<ref name="deadline" /><ref>{{cite web |last1=France |first1=Lisa Respers |title=Oscars 2019: See who's nominated |url=https://www.cnn.com/2019/01/22/entertainment/oscar-nominations-2019/index.html |website=CNN |accessdate=2019-01-22 |date=2019-01-22 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190122135911/https://www.cnn.com/2019/01/22/entertainment/oscar-nominations-2019/index.html |archive-date=2019-01-22 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

== 發行 ==
影片於{{le|2018年日舞影展|2018 Sundance Film Festival}}首映，獲得美國紀錄片評審團特別獎最佳突破電影製作獎<ref name="deadline">{{cite news |last1=Evans |first1=Greg |title=Hulu Acquires Bing Liu' 'Minding The Gap'; Doc Nabbed Sundance Jury Award |url=https://deadline.com/2018/06/hulu-bing-liu-minding-the-gap-documentary-acquisition-1202403812/ |accessdate=2018-11-21 |work=Deadline Hollywood |date=2018-06-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181128210728/https://deadline.com/2018/06/hulu-bing-liu-minding-the-gap-documentary-acquisition-1202403812/ |archive-date=2018-11-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。2018年6月，[[Hulu|Hulu]]買下影片發行權，于2018年8月17日在影院和Hulu發行該片<ref name="deadline"/><ref>{{cite news |last1=Metz |first1=Nina |title=A conversation with Bing Liu about his documentary 'Minding the Gap' |url=https://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/movies/ct-mov-bing-liu-interview-minding-the-gap-0817-story.html |accessdate=2018-11-21 |work=Chicago Tribune |date=2018-08-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181128164751/https://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/movies/ct-mov-bing-liu-interview-minding-the-gap-0817-story.html |archive-date=2018-11-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。[[美國公共電視台|美國公共電視台]]於在《POV》欄目中2019年2月18日播出本片<ref>{{cite news |last1=Phillips |first1=Michael |title=Chicagoan of the Year: Bing Liu, Rockford filmmaker could make a splash at the Oscars |url=https://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/movies/ct-ae-movies-coty-1230-story.html |accessdate=2019-01-07 |publisher=Chicago Tribune |date=2018-12-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190102152513/https://www.chicagotribune.com/entertainment/movies/ct-ae-movies-coty-1230-story.html |archive-date=2019-01-02 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

'''因丁村'''（{{lang-my|အင်းဒင်}}）是一处位于[[緬甸|緬甸]][[若開邦|若開邦]]北部的村落<ref name="Taylor">{{cite news |last1=Taylor |first1=Adam |title=In a first, Burmese military admits that soldiers killed Rohingya found in mass grave |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/worldviews/wp/2018/01/10/in-a-first-burmese-military-admits-soldiers-killed-rohingya-found-in-mass-grave/ |accessdate=2018-01-10 |website=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]] |date=2018-01-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180110215124/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/worldviews/wp/2018/01/10/in-a-first-burmese-military-admits-soldiers-killed-rohingya-found-in-mass-grave/ |archive-date=2018-01-10 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |quote=The military announced on Dec. 18 that a mass grave containing 10 bodies had been found at the coastal village of Inn Din, about 50 km (30 miles) north of the state capital Sittwe. The army appointed a senior officer to investigate. |agency=[[Reuters|Reuters]] |website=[[The_Japan_Times|The Japan Times]] |title=Myanmar military: Soldiers murdered 10 captured Rohingya 'terrorists' after Buddhists forced them into grave |url=https://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2018/01/11/asia-pacific/crime-legal-asia-pacific/myanmar-military-soldiers-murdered-10-captured-rohingya-terrorists-buddhists-forced-grave/ |date=2018-01-11 |access-date=2018-01-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180111011845/https://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2018/01/11/asia-pacific/crime-legal-asia-pacific/myanmar-military-soldiers-murdered-10-captured-rohingya-terrorists-buddhists-forced-grave/ |archive-date=2018-01-11 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，人口約6000人，村民主要由[[若開族|若開族]]與[[羅興亞族|羅興亞族]]构成<ref name="Kipgen">{{cite news |last1=Kipgen |first1=Nehginpao |title=A difficult year for Myanmar |url=http://www.thehindu.com/opinion/op-ed/a-difficult-year/article22413581.ece |accessdate=2018-01-11 |website=[[The_Hindu|The Hindu]] |date=2018-01-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180425054109/http://www.thehindu.com/opinion/op-ed/a-difficult-year/article22413581.ece |archive-date=2018-04-25 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

2017年12月，村內發現了一处亂葬崗，其中埋有十名羅興亞族村民屍體。2018年1月，[[緬甸國防軍|緬甸國防軍]]对外承認，他们在清理叛亂分子時，若干士兵與若開族村民曾杀死十名罗兴亚人。<ref name="st-afp">{{cite news |title=Myanmar security forces took part in killing 10 Rohingya: Army |url=http://www.straitstimes.com/asia/se-asia/myanmar-security-forces-took-part-in-killing-10-rohingya-army |accessdate=2018-01-10 |agency=[[Agence_France-Presse|AFP]] |website=Straights Times |date=2018-01-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180910164545/https://www.straitstimes.com/asia/se-asia/myanmar-security-forces-took-part-in-killing-10-rohingya-army |archive-date=2018-09-10 |dead-url=no }}</ref>

==基本状况==
[[File:Battle_of_Arakan,_1943-_a_Sampan_Convoy_on_the_Mayu_River_Art.IWMARTLD3340.jpg|thumb]]上的[[舢板|舢板]]护卫队]]

因丁村位於[[孟加拉灣|孟加拉灣]]沿岸的墨玉半島，地处[[墨玉河|墨玉河]]和[[加叻丹河|加叻丹河]]三角洲一带<ref name="webmap">{{cite web |title=Arakan Research Center |url=http://www.trekthailand.net/myanmar/map/mrauku.jpg |website=Trek Thailand |accessdate=2018-01-10}}</ref>，位于[[拉岱當|拉岱當]]以西、首府[[實兌|實兌]]以北，鄰近[[貌奪|貌奪]]<ref name="Taylor" /><ref name="bbc180110" />。当地居民主要以種植稻米、檳榔和捕魚为收入來源<ref name="khai" />。東洋工程開發公司泰國子公司計劃在附近建設燃煤發電廠，但遭到當地居民反對<ref name="khai">{{cite journal |last1=Khai |first1=Khen Suan |title=Japan's Official Development Assistance Diplomacy towards Burma in Post 2012 |journal=International Conference on Burma/Myanmar Studies |date=2015 |page=10 |url=http://www.burmalibrary.org/docs21/Transition/Khen-Suan-Khai-2015-Japan's_Official_Development_Assistance_Deplomacy_to_towards_Burma_in_Post_2012-en.pdf |accessdate=2018-01-10 |publisher=University Academic Service Centre (UNISERV), Chiang Mai University}}</ref>。

==因丁大屠殺==
{{main|因丁大屠殺}}
2017年12月，緬甸軍方发表声明称，他们將着手調查一处埋有不明身份屍體的墳墓<ref name="bbc180110">{{cite news |title=Rohingya crisis: Myanmar army admits killings |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-42639418 |accessdate=2018-01-10 |website=[[BBC|BBC]] |date=2018-01-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181023211402/https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-42639418 |archive-date=2018-10-23 |dead-url=no }}</ref>，军方通过總司令[[敏昂來|敏昂來]]的[[Facebook|Facebook]]帖文宣布了这一消息<ref name="time">{{cite news |title=A Mass Grave Has Been Found in Western Myanmar, the Military Says |url=http://time.com/5070606/myanmar-mass-grave-rakhine-rohingya/ |accessdate=2018-01-10 |agency=[[Reuters|Reuters]] |website=[[Time_(magazine)|Time]] |date=2017-12-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171223043133/http://time.com/5070606/myanmar-mass-grave-rakhine-rohingya/ |archive-date=2017-12-23 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

2018年1月10日，据媒体報導，緬甸軍方承認，2017年9月2日时，曾有人殺害該村附近的羅興亞穆斯林<ref name="bbc180110" />。軍方在第二篇Facebook帖子中公佈了調查結果，並表示他們曾做出决定，杀死他們在因丁村墓地中拘留的羅興亞穆斯林<ref name="st-afp" />。被杀死的羅興亞人隨後被埋葬在村內的一個亂葬坑里。軍方表示，其士兵幫助村民對他們稱為“孟加拉恐怖分子”的人進行報復<ref name="bbc180110" />。

調查村中亂葬崗證據的兩名路透社記者在[[仰光|仰光]]被捕，並被判處七年徒刑。[[大赦國際|大赦國際]]的一名顧問表示，衛星照片表明，因丁村周圍的羅興亞人住宅已經被有组织地燒毀。<ref name="Taylor" />

'''錦林息公共運輸交匯處'''（{{lang-en|Klemzig Interchange}}），前稱'''錦林息站'''（{{lang-en|Klemzig Station}}），是[[南澳州|南澳州]][[阿德萊德|阿德萊德]][[O-Bahn巴士專線|O-Bahn巴士專線]]的中途站。

== 歷史 ==
錦林息公共運輸交匯處屬於O-Bahn第一期工程，於1986年3月2日正式啓用，<ref>Busway Information, Paper One: Background History of the Northeast Corridor Transportation Proposals, South Australian Department of Transport 1983</ref><ref>O-Bahn Busway Information (Brochure), South Australian Department of Transport 1987</ref>由時任州長[[潘龍忠|潘龍忠]]（John Bannon）主持開幕儀式；直至3月9日才正式通車使用。<ref>"First stage of O-Bahn is up and running" ''[[Adelaide_Advertiser|Adelaide Advertiser]]'' 3 March 1986 page 11</ref><ref>"Bus-route protests block O-Bahn" ''Adelaide Advertiser'' 10 March 1986 page 17</ref><ref>Annual Report for year ended 30 June 1986 [[State_Transport_Authority_(South_Australia)|State Tranport Authority]] pages 17-19</ref><ref>Northeast Busway (Brochure), State Transport Authority 1990</ref><ref>[http://infrastructure.sa.gov.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0005/157487/OBAHN_FACT_SHEET_-_BACKGROUND_FINAL.pdf O-Bahn City Access Project Background] [[Department_of_Planning,_Transport_&_Infrastructure|Department of Planning, Transport & Infrastructure]] February 2015</ref>此站是O-Bahn由[[阿德雷得中央商業區|阿德萊德市中心]]出發的第一個車站，位於柯士文機利士道（OG Road）的東邊。

此站興建目的是，與外環線巴士路線交匯。但由於此站巴士停靠位置比其餘交匯處短，不少O-Bahn路線在繁忙時間以'''特急'''（Express）名義「飛站」。本站共有29條O-Bahn路線停靠，包括前往[[屋登|屋登]]（Oakden）的路線。<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/var/metro/storage/images/timetables-maps/interchanges/klemzig-interchange/60687-12-eng-AU/Klemzig-Interchange.png Klemzig Interchange] Adelaide Metro</ref><ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/stops/view/16667 Zone A Klemzig Interchange - East side] Adelaide Metro</ref><ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/stops/view/17189 Zone B Klemzig Interchange - West side] Adelaide Metro</ref>此站是唯一一個O-Bahn交匯處不設巴士專線離開本站。

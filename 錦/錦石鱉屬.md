'''錦石鱉屬'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Onithochiton'''''}}）是[[多板綱|多板綱]][[新石鳖亞綱|新石鳖亞綱]][[石鳖目|石鳖目]][[石鳖科|石鳖科]]的一個屬，分佈於[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]、[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]]及[[南非|南非]]。

==物種==
根據[[WoRMS|WoRMS]]，本屬包括下列各個物種<ref name=WoRMS_205084 />：
*''[[Onithochiton_ashbyi|Onithochiton ashbyi]]'' <small>Bednall & Matthews, 1906</small>
*''[[Onithochiton_discrepans|Onithochiton discrepans]]'' <small>Hedley & Hull, 1912</small>
*''[[Onithochiton_erythraeus|Onithochiton erythraeus]]'' <small>Thiele, 1909</small>:有其他文獻不包括本物種。
*''[[Onithochiton_gotoi|Onithochiton gotoi]]'' <small>van Belle, 1993</small>
*''[[Onithochiton_helenae|Onithochiton helenae]]'' <small>(Mackay, 1933)</small>
* [[平濑锦石鳖|平濑锦石鳖]] ''[[Onithochiton_hirasei|Onithochiton hirasei]]'' <small>Pilsbry, 1901</small>
*''[[Onithochiton_literatus|Onithochiton literatus]]'' <small>(Krauss, 1848)</small>
*''[[Onithochiton_lyellii|Onithochiton lyellii]]'' <small>(Sowerby in Broderip & Sowerby, 1832)</small>
* [[美拉蒂锦石鳖|美拉蒂锦石鳖]] ''[[Onithochiton_maillardi|Onithochiton maillardi]]'' <small>(Deshayes, 1863)</small>
*''[[Onithochiton_margueritae|Onithochiton margueritae]]'' <small>Kaas, Van Belle & Strack, 2006</small>
*''[[Onithochiton_neglectus|Onithochiton neglectus]]'' <small>Rochebrune, 1881</small>
*''[[Onithochiton_noemiae|Onithochiton noemiae]]'' <small>(Rochebrune, 1884)</small>

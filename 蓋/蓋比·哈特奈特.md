'''查爾斯·里歐·"蓋比"·哈特奈特'''（{{lang-en|Charles Leo "Gabby" Hartnett}}，{{bd|1900年|12月20日|1972年|12月20日|}}），綽號「老番茄臉」，為前美國職棒大聯盟的捕手。19年棒球生涯有18年效力於小熊隊，1941年效力於巨人隊。

哈特奈特是大聯盟史上第一位單季達成20轟的捕手。他還參與了許多大聯盟著名的事件，比如1932年世界大賽，[[貝比·魯斯|貝比·魯斯]]的預告全壘打、[[卡爾·哈伯爾|卡爾·哈伯爾]]在1934年明星賽連續投出5次三振、1937年明星賽，幾乎讓Dizzy Dean提前結束棒球生涯的重傷。

1938年球季末，哈特奈特在第9局擊出再見全壘打，幫助小熊闖進世界大賽。

在[[強尼·班奇|強尼·班奇]]出現之前，哈特奈特被認為是國聯最強的捕手。他生涯四度進入世界大賽，卻從未奪冠。他生涯256轟一度是捕手的全壘打紀錄。1955年，他入選名人堂。

==職業生涯==
===早年===
哈特奈特出生於[[羅德島州|羅德島州]]的[[文索基特_(羅得島州)|文索基特]]，他有13個弟弟妹妹。1921年，他開始打職業棒球。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/minors/player.cgi?id=hartne001cha |title=Gabby Hartnett minor league statistics |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>當時巨人隊總教練[[約翰·麥格勞_(棒球選手)|約翰·麥格勞]]派出球探來看看哈特奈特的實力。<ref name="Scout's Report on Hartnett Proved Wrong">{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/?id=KDMDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA46&dq=gabby+hartnett+baseball+digest#v=onepage&q&f=false |title=Scout's Report on Hartnett Proved Wrong |author=Birtwell, Roger |publisher=Books.Google.com |date=February 1970 |work=Baseball Digest |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>不過球探卻認為哈特奈特的手太小，作為捕手無法在大聯盟生存，因此沒有簽下他。<ref name="Scout's Report on Hartnett Proved Wrong"/>最終由小熊隊簽下哈特奈特。
===生涯前期===
1922年，哈特奈特開啟了他的職業生涯，他做為Bob O'Farrell的替補。由於他沉默害羞的性格，讓其他人給了他「Gabby」這個綽號。<ref name="Gabby Hartnett at Baseball Statistics">{{cite web |url=http://www.baseball-statistics.com/HOF/Hartnett.html |title=Gabby Hartnett at Baseball Statistics |publisher=baseball-statistics.com |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>1922年7月22日，O'Farrell在比賽中受傷，哈特奈特成為主戰捕手。該年他打擊率2成99，16轟67分打點。<ref>{{cite book |author=James, Bill |title=[[The_Bill_James_Historical_Baseball_Abstract|The Bill James Historical Baseball Abstract]] |publisher=Free Press |location=New York |year=2001 |page=401 |isbn=0-684-80697-5}}</ref>1924年9月20日，哈特奈特接捕了[[格羅佛·克里夫蘭·亞歷山大|格羅佛·克里夫蘭·亞歷山大]]的300勝比賽。<ref>{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/?id=vTIDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA90&dq=gabby+hartnett+baseball+digest#v=onepage&q&f=false |title=When Old Alex Won his 300th Game |author=Ahrens, Art |publisher=Books.Google.com |date=March 1976 |work=Baseball Digest |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>由於哈特奈特在接替O'Farrell的位置後表現不差，因此O'Farrell於1925年被交易至紅雀隊。<ref>{{cite book |title=The Glory of Their Times|author=[[Lawrence_Ritter|Lawrence Ritter]] |publisher=Collier Books|isbn=0-688-11273-0|page=235}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/players/o/o'farbo01.shtml |title=Bob O'Farrell |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>
===新星崛起===
1925年，哈特奈特單季擊出24轟，打破1893年由Jack Clements締造的單季最多轟捕手紀錄。<ref name="Record for Most Home Runs by a Catcher">{{cite web|url=http://rockymtn.sabr.org/sabr.cfm?a=cms,c,734,3,158 |title=Record for Most Home Runs by a Catcher |publisher=sabr.org |accessdate=February 12, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100714044545/http://rockymtn.sabr.org/sabr.cfm?a=cms%2Cc%2C734%2C3%2C158 |archivedate=July 14, 2010 |df=mdy }}</ref>他在全壘打排行僅次於[[羅傑·霍斯比|羅傑·霍斯比]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1925-batting-leaders.shtml |title=1925 National League Batting Leaders |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>儘管該年他的失誤次數是大聯盟捕手最多，不過他的阻殺率相當高。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1925-fielding-leaders.shtml |title=1925 National League Fielding Leaders |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>1925年12月，有傳言說哈特奈特將被交易至巨人隊，換取捕手Frank Snyder和外野手Irish Meusel。不過小熊隊的總經理Bill Veeck駁斥此一謠言，並宣布不會交易哈特奈特到任何球隊。<ref>{{cite news |title=Many Big Trades Expected At Meet |agency=I.N.S. |work=The Telegraph-Herald |page=9 |date=December 9, 1925 |accessdate=February 13, 2011 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=vytFAAAAIBAJ&sjid=VbsMAAAAIBAJ&pg=4608,69252&dq=gabby+hartnett&hl=en}}</ref>

1926年對哈特奈特是個失望的一年，打擊率掉到2成75，僅41分打點。不過到了1927年，他的打擊率上升至2成94，10轟80分打點。該年他在MVP票選排名第10。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/awards/awards_1927.shtml#NLmvp |title=1927 National League Most Valuable Player Award ballot |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=June 28, 2012 }}</ref>
===生涯巔峰===
1928年，哈特奈特的打擊率首度超過3成。該年他也打破了Jack Clements生涯72轟的捕手全壘打紀錄。<ref name="Record for Most Home Runs by a Catcher"/>隨著經驗增長，他的失誤次數也愈來愈少。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1928-fielding-leaders.shtml |title=1928 National League Fielding Leaders |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=June 28, 2012 }}</ref>

1929年，一場原因不明的手臂疾病讓他出賽次數大幅減少。不過小熊奪下國聯龍頭。<ref name="Gabby Hartnett at The Baseball Page">{{cite web|url=http://www.thebaseballpage.com/players/hartnga01.php |archive-url=https://archive.is/20130203184935/http://www.thebaseballpage.com/players/hartnga01.php |dead-url=yes |archive-date=February 3, 2013 |title=Gabby Hartnett at The Baseball Page |publisher=thebaseballpage.com |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>1929年世界大賽，哈特奈特在系列賽中只有3個打席，而且全數吞下三振。<ref name="Gabby Hartnett at Baseball Statistics"/><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/players/h/hartnga01.shtml#batting_postseason::none |title=Gabby Hartnett post-season statistics |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>小熊最終1勝4敗輸給運動家隊無緣拿下冠軍。1930年，哈特奈特觸底反彈，打擊率3成39，37轟和生涯新高的122分打點。單季37轟也豎下了捕手單季全壘打紀錄，直到1953年才被Roy Campanella打破。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://members.tripod.com/bb_catchers/catchers/hr_record_sea.htm |title=Progression of Season Catcher Homerun Record |publisher=The Encyclopedia of Baseball Catchers |accessdate=June 27, 2012 }}</ref>

1932年，小熊隊投手群擁有全聯盟最低的防禦率，小熊以4場勝差力壓海盜隊進入世界大賽。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1932.shtml#teams_standard_pitching::7 |title=1932 National League Team Statistics and Standings |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>1932年世界大賽第3場，哈特奈特擔任主戰捕手，那場也出現了[[貝比·魯斯|貝比·魯斯]]的預告全壘打。儘管他在系列賽打擊率3成13，擊出1支全壘打，小熊隊最終還是遭到洋基隊橫掃。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1932_WS.shtml |title=1932 World Series |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>

1933年，哈特奈特以替補捕手的身分入選第一屆的明星賽，<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/boxes/ALS/ALS193307060.shtml |title=1933 All-Star Game |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>這也開啟了哈特奈特連續6次入選明星賽的紀錄。1934年上半季，他的打擊率為3成36，13轟，他也入選該年明星賽的先發捕手。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/players/gl.cgi?id=hartnga01&t=b&year=1934 |title=1934 Gabby Hartnett batting log |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/boxes/NLS/NLS193407100.shtml |title=1934 All-Star Game |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>而他也在明星賽上與投手[[卡爾·哈伯爾|卡爾·哈伯爾]]成功配合，讓哈伯爾締造明星賽連續5次三振的紀錄。該年他打擊率2成99，22轟90分打點。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1934-fielding-leaders.shtml |title=1934 National League Fielding Leaders |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=June 30, 2012 }}</ref>

1935年，哈特奈特的打擊率高達3成44。該年小熊隊投手群的防禦率又是全聯盟最低。小熊隊再度闖進世界大賽，<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/leagues/NL/1935.shtml#teams_standard_pitching::7 |title=1935 National League Team Statistics and Standings |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=January 23, 2011 }}</ref>卻以2比4輸給老虎隊。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1935_WS.shtml |title=1935 World Series |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>

1936年，哈特奈特表現普普，打擊率依舊來到3成07，不過卻只有7轟64分打點。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/boxes/NLS/NLS193607070.shtml |title=1936 All-Star Game |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=June 28, 2012 }}</ref>小熊隊以第三名坐收。1937年，哈特奈特的打擊率來到個人生涯新高的3成54，他在MVP票選排名第二。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/awards/awards_1937.shtml#NLmvp |title=1937 National League Most Valuable Player Award ballot |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>3成54的打擊率也一度是捕手單季打擊率紀錄，直到1997年才被[[麥克·皮耶薩|麥克·皮耶薩]]打破。<ref>{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/?id=wy4DAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA38&dq=hartnett+piazza+baseball+digest#v=onepage&q&f=false |title=Here's How Division Races Shape Up for the '96 Season |author=Vass, George |publisher=Books.Google.com |date=April 1996 |work=Baseball Digest |accessdate=February 16, 2011 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/players/p/piazzmi01.shtml |title=Mike Piazza career statistics |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 16, 2011 }}</ref>
===「黃昏時刻的全壘打」===
1938年7月20日，小熊隊老闆宣布37歲的哈特奈特接任Charlie Grimm的總教練一職。<ref>{{cite news |title=Gabby Hartnett Succeeds Grimm As Cub Manager |agency= |work=The Daily Times |page=9 |date=July 21, 1938 |accessdate=February 12, 2011 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=B6siAAAAIBAJ&sjid=sK8FAAAAIBAJ&pg=1671,5851154&dq=gabby+hartnett&hl=en}}</ref>當時小熊隊還落後海盜隊6場勝差。<ref name="1938 Chicago Cubs Schedule">{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/teams/CHC/1938-schedule-scores.shtml |title=1938 Chicago Cubs Schedule |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>9月27日，小熊與海盜進行3連戰，小熊先贏下第一場。<ref name="Homer In The Gloamin'">{{cite web |url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20070720&content_id=2099223&vkey=news_chc&fext=.jsp&c_id=ch |title=Homer In The Gloamin' |publisher=mlb.com |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>

9月28日，小熊與海盜打成5比5平手。由於當時天色昏暗，裁判宣布若9局結束還是平手則隔日再戰。在該場比賽9局下2出局，哈特奈特面對海盜隊投手Mace Brown，球數絕對落後的情況下，擊出了再見全壘打。整座球場的球迷陷入瘋狂，<ref>{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/?id=FTQDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA32&dq=gabby+hartnett+baseball+digest#v=onepage&q=gabby%20hartnett%20baseball%20digest&f=false |title=When Gabby Hartnett Hit His Homer In The Gloamin' |author=Carmichael, John |publisher=Books.Google.com |date=October 1978 |work=Baseball Digest |accessdate=February 16, 2011 }}</ref>這支全壘打也被譽為是「黃昏時刻的全壘打」。

該年9月，小熊隊拿下19勝3敗1和的成績，最後擠下海盜進軍世界大賽。然而1938年世界大賽，小熊隊又遭洋基隊橫掃。小熊隊在這10年間打了4次世界大賽，卻一次冠軍都沒得到。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/postseason/1938_WS.shtml |title=1938 World Series |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>
===生涯後期===
1939年9月28日，哈特奈特打破了由Ray Schalk保持的1727場捕手出賽場次紀錄。<ref>{{cite news |title=Chicago Catcher-Manager Has Equalled or Cracked Long Time Backstop Mark |agency=Associated Press |work=The Calgary Herald |page=7 |date=August 29, 1939 |accessdate=February 12, 2011 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=DCFkAAAAIBAJ&sjid=S3sNAAAAIBAJ&pg=1708,5809655&dq=gabby+hartnett&hl=en}}</ref>最後他的出賽場次在1945年被Al Lopez打破。<ref>{{cite news |title=Al Lopez Set Major Loop Catching Mark |agency= |work=The Daily Times |page=5 |date=August 3, 1945 |accessdate=June 27, 2012 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=rpQiAAAAIBAJ&sjid=XK8FAAAAIBAJ&pg=2346,4816287&dq=al+lopez+gabby+hartnett&hl=en}}</ref>

經過了2個失望的球季，哈特奈特在1940年11月13日被小熊隊釋出。<ref>{{cite news |title=Gabby Hartnett Dismissed as Chicago Cubs Manager in National League |agency=Associated Press |work=Montreal Gazette |page=15 |date=November 14, 1940 |accessdate=February 12, 2011 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=ar8tAAAAIBAJ&sjid=JZkFAAAAIBAJ&pg=4362,2366595&dq=gabby+hartnett&hl=en}}</ref>該年12月3日，他與巨人隊簽下1年合約。<ref>{{cite news |title=Gabby Hartnett To Coach Giants |agency=Associated Press |work=The Calgary Herald |page=7 |date=December 3, 1940 |accessdate=February 12, 2011 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=Hx1kAAAAIBAJ&sjid=MXsNAAAAIBAJ&pg=2234,143008&dq=gabby+hartnett&hl=en}}</ref>1941年，他出賽64場，打擊率3成。1941年9月24日，40歲的哈特奈特宣布退休。

==退休之後==
退休後，哈特奈特在小聯盟執教了5個球季，1946年正式退休。<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.baseball-reference.com/minors/player.cgi?id=hartne001cha#standard_managing::none |title=Gabby Hartnett minor league manager record |publisher=Baseball Reference |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>1955年1月26日，他與[[喬·迪馬喬|喬·迪馬喬]]、Ted Lyons與Dazzy Vance一同入選名人堂。<ref>{{cite news |title=Di Mag, Lyons, Hartnett, Vance Voted To Hall |agency=United Press International |work=The Victoria Advocate |page=10 |date=January 27, 1955 |accessdate=February 12, 2011 |url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=PGRTAAAAIBAJ&sjid=f4UDAAAAIBAJ&pg=1178,2554751&dq=gabby+hartnett&hl=en}}</ref>1981年，Lawrence Ritter和Donald Honig在撰寫「The 100 Greatest Baseball Players of All Time」時，將哈特奈特列入其中。1999年，他還入選大聯盟世紀明星隊的最終決選名單內。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://mlb.mlb.com/mlb/history/mlb_history_moreinfo.jsp |title=Major League Baseball All-Century Team |publisher=mlb.com |accessdate=February 12, 2011 }}</ref>

1972年12月20日，72歲的哈特奈特因肝硬化死於伊利諾州的帕克里奇，這一天剛好也是哈特奈特72歲的生日。

'''蓋·歐柏曼'''（Guy Opperman<ref name="ukwhoswho.com">{{citation |chapter-url= http://www.ukwhoswho.com/view/article/oupww/whoswho/U251456/ |chapter=Guy Opperman |title=[[Who's_Who_(UK)|Who's Who]] |publisher=[[A_&_C_Black|A & C Black]] |accessdate= |subscription=yes}}</ref>，{{bd|1965年|5月18日}}）是一位[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]政治人物，他的黨籍是[[保守黨_(英國)|保守黨]]。自2010年開始，他擔任[[赫克瑟姆_(英國國會選區)|赫克瑟姆]]選區選出的英國下議院議員<ref name="Election 2010">{{cite news |url=http://www.hexhamcourant.co.uk/news/opperman-wins-for-tories-1.705670?referrerPath=/1.257779 |title=Election 2010: Hexham Results |newspaper=[[Hexham_Courant|Hexham Courant]] |publisher=CN Group |date= |accessdate=7 May 2010 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120229231345/http://www.hexhamcourant.co.uk/news/opperman-wins-for-tories-1.705670?referrerPath=%2F1.257779 |archivedate=29 二月 2012 }}</ref>。他是一個著名的[[維生工資|維生工資]]支持者<ref>{{cite web |url=http://money.aol.co.uk/2013/08/08/tory-mp-backs-living-wage-campaign/ |title=Tory MP backs living wage campaign |publisher=[[AOL|AOL]] |date=8 August 2013 |accessdate= |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160131020926/http://money.aol.co.uk/2013/08/08/tory-mp-backs-living-wage-campaign/ |archive-date=2016-01-31 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。

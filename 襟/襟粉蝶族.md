'''襟粉蝶族'''（學名：'''''Anthocharidini'''''）是[[鱗翅目|鱗翅目]][[粉蝶科|粉蝶科]][[粉蝶亞科|粉蝶亞科]]中的一個[[族_(生物)|族]]。共有10個屬，廣泛分佈於世界各地<ref>Brower, Andrew V. Z. 2009. Anthocharidini Tutt 1894. Version 03 June 2009 (under construction). http://tolweb.org/Anthocharidini/76415/2009.06.03 in The Tree of Life Web Project, http://tolweb.org/ {{en}}</ref>。

== 種系發生 ==
襟粉蝶族是''[[Teracolini|Teracolini]]''的[[旁系群|旁系群]]<ref name=w2014>Wahlberg, N., Rota, J., Braby, M. F., Pierce, N. P. & Wheat, C. W. 2014: Revised systematics and higher classification of pierid butterflies (Lepidoptera: Pieridae) based on molecular data. Zoologica Scripta 43: 641-650. [http://dx.doi.org/10.1111/zsc.12075 doi:10.1111/zsc.12075] {{en}}</ref>。而襟粉蝶族中有兩個[[演化支|演化支]]<ref>Braby, M. F., Vila, R. & Pierce, N. E. (2006). Molecular phylogeny and systematics of the Pieridae (Lepidoptera: Papilionoidea): higher classification and biogeography. Zoological Journal of the Linnean Society, 147, 239–275.</ref>，分別為分佈於[[新熱帶界|新熱帶界]]的秀粉蝶演化支<ref>Braby, M. F. & Nishida, K. (2007). The immature stages, larval food plants and biology of Neotropical mistletoe butterflies (Lepidoptera: Pieridae). I. The Hesperocharis group (Anthocharidini). Journal of the Lepidopterists’ Society, 61, 181–195.</ref>和全北界的襟粉蝶演化支。

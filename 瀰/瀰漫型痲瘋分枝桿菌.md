'''瀰漫型痲瘋分枝桿菌'''（[[學名|學名]]''Mycobacterium lepromatosis''）是一種[[細菌|細菌]]，與[[麻風桿菌|麻風桿菌]]一同引起[[麻風病|麻風病]]。它到了最近的2008年才被發現。<ref>{{Cite journal|author=Han, X. Y., Seo, Y. H., Sizer, K. C., Schoberle, T., May, G. S., Spencer, J. S., Li, W., Nair, R. G.|title=A new Mycobacterium species causing diffuse lepromatous leprosy|journal=Am. J. Clin. Pathol.|year=2008|volume=130|issue=6|pages=856-64}}</ref>從其[[16S_核糖體RNA|16S 核糖體RNA]]中可見它們是與麻風桿菌有所分別。<ref>{{Cite journal|author=Han, X. Y., Sizer, K. C., Thompson, E. J., Kabanja, J., Li, J., Hu, P., Gómez-Valero, L., Silva, F. J.|title=Comparative sequence analysis of ''Mycobacterium leprae'' and the new leprosy-causing ''Mycobacterium lepromatosis''|journal=J. Bacteriol|year=2009|volume=191|issue=19|pages=6067-74}}</ref>

《'''割喉島'''》（{{lang-en|''Cutthroat Island''}}）是一部1995年上映的浪漫喜劇冒險電影。由[[雷尼·哈林|雷尼·哈林]]導演、[[吉娜·戴維斯|吉娜·戴維斯]]、[[馬修·莫汀|馬修·莫汀]]、[[法蘭克·蘭吉拉|法蘭克·蘭吉拉]]等人主演。<ref>{{cite news|title=Troubled Route to Pirate Epic 'Cutthroat'; Movies: As the swashbuckling adventure starring Geena Davis, directed by her husband, Renny Harlin, opens this weekend, financial woes surround its release.|work= [[The_Los_Angeles_Times|The Los Angeles Times]]|date=2005-12-21 |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1995-12-21/entertainment/ca-16222_1_renny-harlin|accessdate=2011-05-24 |last=Brennan |first=Judy}}</ref>該片由美國、法國、德國及義大利聯合製作。

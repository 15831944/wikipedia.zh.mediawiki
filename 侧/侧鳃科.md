'''侧鳃科'''（學名：{{lang|la|Pleurobranchidae}}），亦作'''側鰓海牛科'''，是[[分類學|分類學]]上[[海蛞蝓|海蛞蝓]]的一個[[科_(生物)|科]]、[[海洋生物|海洋]][[腹足綱|腹足綱]][[軟體動物|軟體動物]][[側鰓類|側鰓類]][[支序|支序]]的成員。過往是[[背楯目|背楯目]]之下的一個科{{r|TaiBIF}}，現時是[[裸側類|裸側類]]支序的成員。
Species in the family Pleurobranchidae have a prominent [[mantle_(mollusc)|mantle]] and an internal shell that becomes reduced or is lost completely in adults.<ref name="Notaspidea">{{cite journal | author=R. Willan| title=Phylogenetic systematics of the Notaspidea (Opisthobranchia) with a reappraisal of families and genera| journal=Am. Malacol. Bull.| year=1987| volume=5| pages=215–241}}</ref> Some adult species have been seen feeding on [[ascidian|ascidian]]s. [[Larva|Larva]]l pleurobranchids can be planktotrophic (feeding on [[plankton|plankton]]), lecithotrophic (deriving nutrition from [[yolk|yolk]]), or direct developing.

Many species produce secretions from their rich glandular mantle as a chemical defense against predators.<ref name="mucus">{{cite journal |author1=Aldo Spinella |author2=Ernesto Mollo |author3=Enrico Trivellone |author4=Guido Cimino | title=Testudinariol A and B, two unusual triterpenoids from the skin and the mucus of the marine mollusc ''Pleurobranchus testudinarius''| journal=Tetrahedron| date=December 1997| volume=53| issue=49| url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science?_ob=ArticleURL&_udi=B6THR-3SDCT91-V&_user=10&_rdoc=1&_fmt=&_orig=search&_sort=d&view=c&_acct=C000050221&_version=1&_urlVersion=0&_userid=10&md5=b54fd176224b1354702ef12999bd20d2| pages=16891–16896| doi=10.1016/S0040-4020(97)10124-7}}</ref>
Even the production of [[sulfuric_acid|sulfuric acid]] has been reported.<ref name="secretions">{{cite journal | author=T. Thompson| title=Acidic allomones in marine organisms| journal=J. Mar. Biol. Assoc. UK| year=1988| volume=68| pages=499–517 | doi=10.1017/S0025315400043368 | issue=3}}</ref>

==分類==
Until 2005, this family was placed in the suborder [[Notaspidea|Notaspidea]]. However, in the [[Taxonomy_of_the_Gastropoda_(Bouchet_&_Rocroi,_2005)|taxonomy of Bouchet & Rocroi]] (2005), the family Pleurobranchidae was placed in the superfamily '''Pleurobranchoidea''', the only family belonging to the subclade '''Pleurobranchomorpha''' (sister to the subclade Nudibranchia), part of the clade [[Nudipleura|Nudipleura]].

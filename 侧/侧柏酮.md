'''侧柏酮'''（{{lang-en|Thujone}}）又称“'''崖柏酮'''”或“'''守酮'''”，是一种[[酮|酮]]以及一种[[萜烯|单萜]]，天然存在于两个[[非对映异构|非对映体]]形式：(−)-α-侧柏酮和(+)β-侧柏酮<ref>{{ cite journal | journal = [[Journal_of_Agricultural_and_Food_Chemistry|J. Agric. Food Chem.]] | year = 1999 | volume = 47 | issue = 5 | pages = 2048–2054 | title = Essential Oils from Dalmatian Sage (''Salvia officinalis'' L.): Variations among Individuals, Plant Parts, Seasons, and Sites | first1 = N. B. | last1 = Perry | first2 = R. E. | last2 = Anderson | first3 = N. J. | last3 = Brennan | first4 = M. H. | last4 = Douglas | first5 = A. J. | last5 = Heaney | first6 = J. A. | last6 = McGimpsey | first7 = B. M. | last7 = Smallfield | doi = 10.1021/jf981170m | pmid=10552494}}</ref><ref>{{ cite journal | journal = [[Helvetica_Chimica_Acta|Helv. Chim. Acta]] | year = 1997 | volume = 80 | issue = 3 | pages = 623–639 | title = Palladium-Catalysed Intramolecular Cyclisations of Olefinic Propargylic Carbonates and application to the diastereoselective synthesis of enantiomerically pure (−)-α-thujone | first1 = W. | last1 = Oppolzer | first2 = A. | last2 = Pimm | first3 = B. | last3 = Stammen | first4 = W. E. | last4 = Hume | doi = 10.1002/hlca.19970800302 }}</ref>。侧柏酮类似有[[薄荷醇|薄荷醇]]的气味。尽管有关侧柏酮最广为人知的是，它是[[苦艾酒|苦艾酒]]中所含的一种[[化学物质|化学物质]]，但最近的研究表明苦艾酒只含有少量侧柏酮，所以侧柏酮可能不是报道中提及的[[苦艾酒|苦艾酒]]能致幻的原因。侧柏酮能作用于[[大脑|大脑]]中的[[γ-氨基丁酸受体|GABA受体]]和[[5-羟色胺3受体|5-HT<sub>3</sub>受体]]<ref name="5-HT3">{{cite journal | doi = 10.1016/j.neuropharm.2003.09.022 | author = Deiml T, Haseneder R, Zieglgänsberger W, Rammes G, Eisensamer B, Rupprecht R, Hapfelmeier G | title = Alpha-thujone reduces 5-HT3 receptor activity by an effect on the agonist-reduced desensitization | journal = Neuropharmacology | volume = 46 | issue = 2 | pages = 192–201 | pmid = 15002407|date=Feb 2004}}</ref>。许多国家都对[[食物|食物]]或[[饮料|饮料]]中侧柏酮的含量的都做了限制。

除了天然的(−)-α-侧柏酮和(+)β-侧柏酮的，理论上侧柏酮还存在另外两个[[对映异构|对映体]]形式：(+)-α-侧柏酮和(−)β-侧柏酮：

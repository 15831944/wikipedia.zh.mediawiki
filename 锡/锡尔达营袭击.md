'''锡尔达营袭击'''（{{lang-en|Silda camp attack}}）发生于2010年2月15日，许多纳萨尔毛派叛乱分子在印度西孟加拉邦的锡尔达（距<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[梅迪尼普尔|梅迪尼普尔]]60公里）袭击了印度安全部队。由此造成{{link-en|东部边境步枪队|Eastern Frontier Rifles}}24名准军事人员死亡以及数人（据信）被绑架，这次袭击严重打击了政府对反叛分子的进攻。<ref name="htimes1">{{cite news|url=http://www.hindustantimes.com/Maoist-attack-Chidambaram-accepts-failure/H1-Article1-509356.aspx|title=Maoist attack: Home Minister accepts failure in some aspects|date=February 16, 2010|newspaper=[[印度斯坦时报|Hindustan Times]]|accessdate=February 17, 2010|location=New Delhi|agency=[[印度报业托拉斯|Press Trust Of India]]|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100218053511/http://www.hindustantimes.com/Maoist-attack-Chidambaram-accepts-failure/H1-Article1-509356.aspx|archivedate=2010年2月18日|df=}}</ref><ref name="timesofindia1">{{cite news|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2010-02-16/india/28149839_1_maoist-attack-people-s-liberation-guerrilla-army-efr|title=Maoists kill 21 jawans in West Bengal|date=February 16, 2010|author2=Mandal, Caesar|newspaper=[[Times_of_India|Times of India]]|accessdate=February 17, 2010|location=New Delhi|agency=[[印度时报|TNN]]|author1=Mahato, Sukumar}}</ref><ref name="ndtv1">{{cite news|url=http://www.ndtv.com/news/india/maoists_attack_police_camp_in_west_midnapore.php|title=Naxals massacre cops, furious Centre wants answers|author=Banerjee, Monideepa|date=February 16, 2010|newspaper=[[新德里电视台|NDTV]]|accessdate=February 17, 2010|location=New Delhi}}</ref>

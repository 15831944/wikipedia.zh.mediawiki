'''锡耶纳牧山银行'''（Banca Monte dei Paschi di Siena S.p.A. {{IPA-it|ˈbanka ˈmonte dei ˈpaski di ˈsjɛːna}}；縮寫：'''BMPS'''、'''MPS'''）是[[意大利|意大利]]的一家銀行，總部位於[[錫耶納|錫耶納]]。其歷史可以追溯到1472年（{{Time ago|4 March 1472|spellout=auto}}）成立的一家當鋪，現在的锡耶纳牧山银行則創始於1624年（{{Time ago|1624|spellout=auto}}），是世界上[[按年份排列的銀行|最古老或至少第二古老的銀行]]，也是意大利第三或第四大商業和零售銀行<ref name="veconomist" >{{cite news|author=|title=Italy’s fourth-biggest bank returns to the stockmarket|url=https://www.economist.com/news/finance-and-economics/21730672-shares-bailed-out-bank-start-trading-again-italys-fourth-biggest-bank|work=[[The_Economist|The Economist]]|date=26 October 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bancobpm.it/media/2017-02-10-Banco-BPM-FY-2016-results.pdf|title=Results as at 31 December 2016|date=10 February 2017|access-date=13 February 2017|publisher=Banco BPM}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mbres.it/sites/default/files/resources/download_it/ps_6_8.pdf|date=24 October 2017|access-date=13 February 2017|title=LE PRINCIPALI BANCHE ITALIANE [2017]|publisher=Mediobanca|language=it}}</ref>。2016年至2017年遭遇經融危機，在意大利政府注資纾困後得以緩解<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.business-standard.com/article/international/world-s-oldest-bank-scrambles-to-raise-5-bn-euros-to-avert-collapse-116121100794_1.html|title=World's oldest bank scrambles to raise 5 bn euros to avert collapse|first=|last=Reuters|date=12 December 2016|publisher=|via=Business Standard}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2016/12/22/cramer-why-wall-street-doesnt-care-that-italys-monte-dei-paschi-is-on-the-brink-of-collapse.html|title=Cramer: Why Wall Street doesn't care that the world's oldest bank is on the brink of collapse|first=Abigail|last=Stevenson|date=22 December 2016|publisher=}}</ref>。

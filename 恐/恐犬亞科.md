'''恐犬亞科'''（学名：''Borophaginae''）是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]一類[[犬科|犬科]]，是[[北美洲|北美洲]]的[[特有種|特有種]]，生存於4000-250萬年前的[[漸新世|漸新世]]至[[上新世|上新世]]。<ref name=lioncrush>{{cite web|last=Postanowicz |first=Rebecca |title=Lioncrusher's Domain:  Canidae |url=http://www.lioncrusher.com/family.asp?family=Canidae |accessdate=2006-04-12 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060319035906/http://www.lioncrusher.com/family.asp?family=Canidae |archivedate=2006-03-19 }}</ref>

恐犬亞科是從[[古犬亞科|古犬亞科]][[演化|演化]]而來，體型上更為大型，一些恐犬亞科像現今的[[獅|獅]]般大，有些則像[[浣熊|浣熊]]或[[郊狼|郊狼]]。牠們佔有[[新生代|新生代]]北美洲多個[[生態位|生態位]]，由細小的[[雜食性|雜食性]]至強壯的[[肉食性|肉食性]]都有。<ref name=lioncrush/><ref name=Wangetal>{{cite journal en| last = Wang| first = Xiaoming| coauthors = Richard Tedford, Beryl Taylor| title = Phylogenetic systematics of the borophaginae| journal = Bulletin of the American Museum of Natural History| volume = 243| date = 1999-11-17| url = http://hdl.handle.net/2246/1588| accessdate = 2007-07-08 }}</ref>  

恐犬亞科一般都有強壯及像[[鬣狗|鬣狗]]的[[牙齒|牙齒]]及[[顎骨|顎骨]]，而且化石很豐富及廣泛分佈。牠們有可能是[[生態系統|生態系統]]中的[[頂級掠食者|頂級掠食者]]。<ref name=Wangetal/><ref name=turner>{{cite book en|author=Alan Turner|title=National Geographic: Prehistoric Mammals|location=Washington, D.C.|publisher=Firecrest Books Ltd.|year=2004|pages=112-114|id=ISBN 0-7922-7134-3}}</ref>[[種系發生學|種系發生學]]顯示恐犬亞科是高度多樣化的。<ref name=Wangetal/>

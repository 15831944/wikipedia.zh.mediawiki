《'''金融大王'''》（{{lang-en|'''''The Wolf of Wall Street'''''}}）是一部1929年的美國{{link-en|前編碼荷里活|Pre-Code Hollywood|前編碼}}電影，由{{link-en|羅蘭德·V·李|Rowland V. Lee}}執導，主演是{{link-en|喬治·班克羅夫特|George Bancroft (actor)}}、[[保羅·盧卡斯|保羅·盧卡斯]]、{{link-en|奧嘉·巴古拉諾娃|Olga Baclanova}}和{{link-en|南絲·嘉勞|Nancy Carroll}}。故事和劇本由Doris Anderson編寫。

本片最初是製作成為一部無聲電影，後來完全重新拍攝為有聲<ref>Films in Review – Volume 37 1986, p. 12; ''"THE WOLF OF WALL STREET. 1929. Paramount. Ben Schulberg, producer. With George Bancroft, Paul Lukas, Olga Baclanova and Nancy Carroll. Originally made as a silent, ''The Wolf of Wall Street'' was completely re-filmed as a talking ..."''</ref>，成為班克羅夫特的第一部有聲電影。<ref>Roy Liebman. ''From silents to sound: a biographical encyclopedia'' 1998, p. 16;<br>'"Sound: George Bancroft retained his star status through his first few talkies, his sound debut coming in ''The Wolf of Wall Street'' (1929)"'.</ref>

劇情講述一個無情的交易員（班克羅夫特 飾演）壟斷銅幣市場，然後賣空，他發了財，但最終搞毀了自己和他朋友的資金。<ref>{{Cite web |url = http://www.indiewire.com/2013/12/the-other-wolf-of-wall-street-180006/ |title = The 'Other' Wolf of Wall Street |author = Leonard Maltin |date = 2013-12-30 |publisher = IndieWire |language = en}}</ref><ref>Kenneth White Munden. ''The American Film Institute Catalog of Motion Pictures'' (1997); Cast: George Bancroft (The Wolf), Baclanova (Olga, his wife), Paul Lukas (David Tyler), Nancy Carroll (Gert, the maid), ... "The Wolf of Wall Street corners the market in copper and then sells short, making a fortune ... To revenge himself, The Wolf deliberately ruins himself and Tyler in the market and then walks out on his wife."</ref><ref>Paul L. Nemcek. ''The films of Nancy Carroll'' (1969), p. 75;<br>''"With George Bancroft What the critics said about THE WOLF OF WALL STREET Film Daily: George Bancroft as the roughneck engineering a pool in Wall Street to get the sucker is immense, as usual, but the lack of action and weak story cramp.."''</ref>

'''金新朝'''（{{lang-ko|김신조}}，{{bd|1942年|6月2日}}）是1968年[[青瓦台事件|青瓦台事件]]中，[[朝鮮民主主義人民共和國|朝鮮民主主義人民共和國]][[民族保衛省|民族保衛省]]警察局所屬124軍部隊武裝游擊隊31名特種人員中的兩名存者之一。<ref>{{cite news |newspaper=New York Times | author=Flora Lewis | title=Seoul Feels a Cold Wind From the North | date=February 18, 1968 | url=http://select.nytimes.com/gst/abstract.html?res=F10B12FC3F5E1A7B93CAA81789D85F4C8685F9}}</ref>行動失敗後，拘押在[[韓國|韓國]]審問一年，1970年獲韓國公民身份。<ref>{{cite news | title=What would Jesus do to North Korea? | newspaper=Asia Times | author=Sunny Lee | date=February 27, 2008 | url=http://www.atimes.com/atimes/Korea/JB27Dg01.html}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=The face of South Korea's boogeyman | newspaper=Los Angeles Times | date=July 18, 2010 | author=John M. Glionna | url=http://www.latimes.com/news/nationworld/world/la-fg-korea-spy-20100718,0,5046450,full.story}}</ref>定居韓國後，金新朝在[[首爾聖樂教會|首爾聖樂教會]]任牧師，結婚並育有兩個孩子。<ref>{{cite news |title=Failed North Korean Assassin Assimilates in the South|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2010/12/18/world/asia/18seoul.html?pagewanted=all&_r=0 |first=Mark |last=McDonald |date=December 17, 2010 |work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |accessdate=April 8, 2014 }}</ref>

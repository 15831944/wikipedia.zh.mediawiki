'''金文泰公共圖書館''' （{{lang-en|Clementi Public Library}}）是一所位于[[金文泰_(新加坡)|金文泰]]规划区{{link-en|新加坡圖書館管理局|National Library Board}}旗下的[[公共圖書館|公共圖書館]]。此图书馆位于{{link-en|金文泰广场|Clementi_Mall}}里的五楼。2001年图书馆正式开幕。

==历史==
2001年4月金文泰公共圖書館正式于{{link-en|金文泰广场|Clementi_Mall}}的五楼开幕，时任新加坡贸易与工业部长[[林勋强|林勋强]]主持了开幕典礼。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.asiaone.com/News/AsiaOne+News/Singapore/Story/A1Story20110512-278338.html|title=Clementi Mall set for grand official opening|accessdate=2018-06-14|author=|date=|work=www.asiaone.com|publisher=[[海峽時報|海峽時報]]|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180203235729/http://www.asiaone.com/News/AsiaOne%2BNews/Singapore/Story/A1Story20110512-278338.html|archivedate=2018-02-03|df=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.asiaone.com/News/AsiaOne+News/Singapore/Story/A1Story20110521-279928.html|title=The Clementi Mall officially opens today|accessdate=2018-06-14|author=|date=|work=www.asiaone.com|publisher=[[海峽時報|海峽時報]]|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171030022042/http://www.asiaone.com/News/AsiaOne%2BNews/Singapore/Story/A1Story20110521-279928.html|archivedate=2017-10-30|df=}}</ref>图书馆藏品共计218,435件，座位则有约200个。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://m.sohu.com/n/443994048/?pvid=000115_3w|title=读书全指南——新加坡即将推行全国读书运动-新闻频道-手机搜狐|accessdate=2018-06-09|work=m.sohu.com|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180712043503/http://m.sohu.com/n/443994048/?pvid=000115_3w|archivedate=2018-07-12|df=}}</ref>

'''金兰教会''' (KMC) 是一座韩国[[监理会|监理会]]教堂，位于[[韩国|韩国]][[首尔|首尔]][[中浪区|中浪区]]忘忧洞。第19届世界循道宗大会在此举行<ref>{{cite news | first = Lillian | last = Kwon | title = 19th World Methodist Conference Closes with New Vision for Evangelism | date = 2006-07-25 | url = http://www.christiantoday.com/article/19th.world.methodist.conference.closes.with.new.vision.for.evangelism/7038.htm | work = Christian Today | accessdate = 2011-09-09}}</ref>。

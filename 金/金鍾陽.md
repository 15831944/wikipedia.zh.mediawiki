'''金鍾陽''' （{{lang-ko|김종양}}，{{bd|1961年|10月30日||}}）為[[大韓民國|大韓民國]]前警官和現任[[國際刑警組織|國際刑警組織]]主席。

==生平==
金鍾陽曾经在2014至2015年任職韓國[[京畿道|京畿道]]地方警察廳長，亦曾經在2015至2018年擔任國際刑警执行委员会亞洲區代表<ref>[https://www.interpol.int/About-INTERPOL/Structure-and-governance/Executive-Committee INTERPOL Executive-Committee]</ref>和國際刑警組織高級副主席<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://news.dwnews.com/global/big5/news/2018-10-07/60089225.html|title=國際刑警組織孟宏偉失蹤案明了 代替者確定|accessdate=2018-10-08|website=多维新闻网}}</ref>。

2018年10月7日，由于前主席[[孟宏伟|孟宏伟]]被[[中国共产党中央纪律检查委员会|中央纪委]][[中华人民共和国国家监察委员会|国家监委]]调查而提出辞呈，金鍾陽开始代理[[國際刑警組織|國際刑警組織]]主席一职<ref name="IndianExpress2018-10-08">{{Cite news|url=https://indianexpress.com/article/world/missing-interpol-president-meng-hongwei-resigns-new-chief-to-be-elected-in-november-5390995/|title=Missing Interpol President Meng Hongwei resigns, new chief to be elected in November|author=Express Web Desk|last=|date=8 October 2018|work=|publisher=Indian Express|accessdate=8 October 2018|quote=Senior Vice-President Kim Jong Yang of South Korea will be the acting president of the international criminal police organization until the new president is elected at a meeting in Dubai on 18-21 November, Interpol said.}}</ref><ref name="CNN2018-10-08">{{Cite news|url=https://edition.cnn.com/2018/10/07/europe/missing-interpol-president-china-meng-hongwei/index.html/|title=Chinese government admits head of Interpol 'under investigation' after disappearance|author=Eliott C. McLaughlin|last=|date=8 October 2018|work=|publisher=CNN|author2=Saskya Vandoorne|last2=|accessdate=8 October 2018|quote=Interpol said Sunday night that South Korea's Kim Jong Yang, a vice president representing Asia on Interpol's executive committee, would serve as acting president until the organization's general assembly picks a permanent president next month.|last3=Ben Westcott}}</ref>；2018年11月21日，金鍾陽被正式选为国际刑警组织主席<ref>{{cite news |author1=陈慧璋 |title=获英美支持 韩国代表出任国际刑警新主席 |url=https://www.zaobao.com.sg/realtime/china/story20181121-909523 |accessdate=2018-11-21 |publisher=联合早报 |date=2018-11-21}}</ref>。

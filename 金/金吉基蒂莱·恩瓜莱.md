'''金吉基蒂莱·博凯罗·恩瓜莱'''（Kinjikitile "Bokero" Ngwale，？－1905年8月4日），[[坦桑尼亚|坦桑尼亚]][[灵媒|灵媒]]，1905年领导[[马及马及叛乱|马及马及叛乱]]反抗[[德意志帝国|德国]]殖民统治。

恩瓜莱是来自南坦桑尼亚的[[马通比人|马通比人]]，1904年自家乡失踪，数日后归来，称自己被蛇神洪戈（Hongo）附体<ref name="CH">{{cite book | last = Hoehler-Fatton | first = Cynthia | title = Women of Fire and Spirit | publisher = Oxford University Press US | year = 1996 | page = 73 | url = https://books.google.com/?id=UMqvQxXhL48C | isbn =0-19-509790-4 }}</ref><ref name="PJ">{{cite book | last = Jestice | first = Phyllis G. | title = Holy People of the World: A Cross-Cultural Encyclopedia | publisher = ABC-CLIO | year = 2004 | page = 480 | url = https://books.google.com/?id=H5cQH17-HnMC | isbn =1-57607-355-6 }}</ref><ref name="BBC">{{Cite news | title = The Story of Africa - Religious Resistance | newspaper = BBC World Service | url =http://www.bbc.co.uk/worldservice/africa/features/storyofafrica/11chapter7.shtml | accessdate=2008-03-24 | postscript = <!--None--> }}</ref>。恩瓜莱声称可以通过蛇神与博凯拉神（Bokera）对话<ref name="PJ" />，并呼吁追随者摒弃部落分歧共同反抗德国统治<ref name="GM">{{cite book | last = Mwakikagile | first = Godfrey | title = Africa and the West | publisher = Nova Publishers | year = 2000 | page = 70 | url = https://books.google.com/?id=iKVCGuSzNhkC | isbn = 1-56072-840-X}}</ref>，称此为祖先的指引<ref name="PJ" />。他借此掀起马及马及叛乱<ref name="PJ" />，并将“圣水”马及给予追随者，声称信徒可借此刀枪不入<ref name="PJ" /><ref name="BBC" />。1905年7月，马通比部族武装进攻当地官邸，恩瓜莱被俘<ref name="HR">{{cite book | last = Roupp | first = Heidi | title = Teaching World History: A Resource Book | publisher = M.E. Sharpe| page = 229 | url = https://books.google.com/?id=9hOEhKbqwwgC | isbn =1-56324-420-9 | year = 1997 }}</ref>，8月4日因叛国罪被绞死<ref name="GM" /><ref name="HR" />。其部族继续率兵反抗德国，1907年才被彻底镇压<ref>{{cite book | last = Boahen | first = A. Adu| title = Africa Under Colonial Domination, 1880-1935 | publisher = James Currey Publishers | year = 1990 | page = 80 | url = https://books.google.com/?id=YKy_YzYeIjoC | isbn =0-85255-097-9 }}</ref>。今日坦桑尼亚人将恩瓜莱视作民族独立运动的先驱<ref name="GM" />。

'''金翠'''（Kim Thúy，{{bd|1968年||}}）越南裔[[加拿大|加拿大]][[作家|作家]]<ref name=alum>[http://www.nouvelles.umontreal.ca/udem-news/news/20100209-from-lawyer-to-novelist-an-alumnas-amazing-journey.html "From lawyer to novelist: an alumna's amazing journey"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141012090534/http://www.nouvelles.umontreal.ca/udem-news/news/20100209-from-lawyer-to-novelist-an-alumnas-amazing-journey.html |date=2014-10-12 }}. Université de Montréal, February 9, 2010.</ref>，出生於[[越南共和國|越南共和國]][[胡志明市|西貢]]。

金翠首部小說《Ru》贏得2010年[[加拿大總督獎|加拿大總督獎]]<ref name="montrealgazette.com">[https://montrealgazette.com/news/Eight+Quebec+writers+Governor+General+prizes/3839880/story.html "Eight Quebec writers win Governor General's prizes"]. ''The Gazette'', November 17, 2010.</ref>。

==生平==
在十歲時，金翠與父母和兩個兄弟離開[[越南|越南]]，加入了超過一百萬的[[越南船民|越南船民]]，他們在1975年[[西貢淪陷|西貢淪陷]]後逃離了越南[[共產黨|共產黨]]政權<ref name=canenc>[http://www.thecanadianencyclopedia.ca/en/article/kim-thuy/ “Kim Thúy”], by Myriam Fontaine, Maude-Emmanuelle Lambert, at the Canadian Encyclopedia; published February 27, 2012; last edited January 18, 2018; retrieved May 28, 2018</ref>。金翠抵達[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]]<ref>[https://www.macleans.ca/culture/books/kim-thuy-on-how-refugee-literature-differs-from-immigrant-literature/ “Kim Thúy on how ‘refugee literature’ differs from immigrant literature“], by Brian Bethune, at Macleans; published April 11, 2018, retrieved May 28, 2018</ref>由[[联合国难民事务高级专员办事处|联合国难民事务高级专员办事处]]管理的難民營，並度過了四個月<ref>[https://www.npr.org/2012/11/24/165563101/a-refugees-multilayered-experience-in-ru “A Refugee's Multilayered Experience In 'Ru'”], interview by Scott Simon, at [[National_Public_Radio|National Public Radio]], on Weekend Edition Saturday; aired on November 24, 2012; retrieved May 28, 2018</ref> ，然後她的父母因法語熟練而獲得[[加拿大|加拿大]]難民身份<ref name=bloom>[https://bloom-site.com/2013/09/18/qa-with-kim-thuy/ “Q&A with Kim Thúy”], interview by Terry Hong, at BLOOM; published September 18, 2013, retrieved May 28, 2018</ref>。1979年末，金翠和家人來到[[魁北克|魁北克]]東部鄉鎮的[[格兰比_(魁北克省)|格蘭比]]，後來定居在[[蒙特利爾|蒙特利爾]]<ref>[http://canadianimmigrant.ca/canadas-top-25-immigrants/canadas-top-25-immigrants-2015/kim-thuy “RBC Top 25 Canadian Immigrants: Canada’s Top 25 Immigrants: Kim Thúy”], by Lisa Evans, at Canadian Immigrant; retrieved May 28, 2018 </ref>。

金翠在[[蒙特利爾大學|蒙特利爾大學]]獲得[[語言學|語言學]]和[[翻譯學|翻譯學]]學位（1990年），後來獲得了同一所學校[[法律|法律]]學位（1993年）<ref name=ryerson>[https://library.ryerson.ca/asianheritage/authors/thuy/ “Asian Heritage in Canada: Kim Thúy”] at Ryerson Library, Library and Archives; retrieved May 28, 2018</ref>。 在早期的職業生涯中，金翠擔任翻譯和口譯員，後來加入總部位於蒙特利爾的Strikeman Elliott律師事務所<ref name=riveroflife>[https://www.theglobeandmail.com/arts/books-and-media/kim-thuys-river-of-life/article545170/ “Kim Thúy’s river of life”], by John Barber, at ''[[The_Globe_and_Mail|The Globe and Mail]]''; published February 5, 2012; updated April 30, 2018; retrieved May 28, 2018</ref>。她因此回到越南，以加拿大人專家身分，為該國共產黨領導層提供[[資本主義|資本主義]]試探性建議。她在同一家公司工作時遇到另一半，這對夫婦在越南任職時育有第一個孩子。他們的第二個孩子是在搬到[[泰國|泰國]][[曼谷|曼谷]]之後出生的。

金翠回到蒙特利爾之後，開了一家餐館Ru de Nam<ref name=ryerson/>，主打現代越南菜<ref name=bloom/>。她以餐館老闆工作了五年，之後利用一整年的時間寫作，並因Ru de Nam的老客戶而獲得第一本書的出版合約<ref name=bloom/>。

==作家生涯==
金翠創作的首部小說《Ru》在2010年[[加拿大總督獎|加拿大總督獎]]法語小說獎。雪拉·費舒曼（Sheila Fischman）翻譯的《Ru》英文版於2012年出版。《Ru》入圍2012年豐業銀行[[吉勒獎|吉勒獎]]和2013年Amazon.ca首部作小說獎。2015年，這部小說贏得了[[卡麥隆·貝利|卡麥隆·貝利]]（Cameron Bailey）主持的《[[加拿大閱讀競賽|加拿大閱讀競賽]]》（Canada Reads）。

2016年，金翠出版第三部小說《Vi》。雪拉·費舒曼翻譯的英文版於2018年出版<ref>[http://www.cbc.ca/books/6-new-canadian-books-to-watch-for-in-april-1.4604657 "6 new Canadian books to watch for in April"]. CBC Books, April 4, 2018.</ref>。這本書入圍2018年豐業銀行[[吉勒獎|吉勒獎]]<ref>[https://www.cbc.ca/books/esi-edugyan-patrick-dewitt-tanya-tagaq-among-12-authors-longlisted-for-2018-scotiabank-giller-prize-1.4824355 "Esi Edugyan, Patrick deWitt, Tanya Tagaq among 12 authors longlisted for 2018 Scotiabank Giller Prize"]. CBC Books, September 17, 2018.</ref>。

2018年，金翠入圍[[新學院文學獎|新學院文學獎]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://calgaryherald.com/pmn/entertainment-pmn/books-entertainment-pmn/canadian-writer-kim-thuy-among-finalists-for-new-academy-prize-in-literature/wcm/75845cdc-8660-4277-93d7-1a1e63d24cb9|title=Canadian writer Kim Thuy among finalists for New Academy Prize in Literature|website=Calgary Herald|date=2018-08-29|access-date=2018-09-11}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thelocal.se/20180829/four-writers-nominated-for-swedens-new-nobel-literature-prize-neil-gaiman-haruki-murakami-kim-thuy-maryse-conde|title=Four writers shortlisted for 'the new Nobel Literature Prize'|first=Emma|last=Löfgren|website=The Local|date=2018-08-29|access-date=2018-09-11}}</ref>。

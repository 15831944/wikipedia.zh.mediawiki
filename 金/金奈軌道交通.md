'''金奈軌道交通'''（{{lang-en|Chennai Mass Rapid Transit System}}）是[[印度鐵路|印度鐵路]]持有的子公司，是{{link-en|印度南方鐵路|Southern Railway (India)|南方鐵路}}營運的{{link-en|高架鐵路|elevated railway}}。此線也是印度第一條高架鐵路線<ref name="yearbook09">{{cite book|title=Year Book 2009|publisher=Bright Publications|page=569|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NHLtQ7yOrAYC&printsec=frontcover}}</ref>。雖然與{{link-en|金奈市郊鐵路|Chennai Suburban Railway}}分離，但是兩者都是由{{link-en|印度南方鐵路|Southern Railway (India)|南方鐵路}}營運，以及使用較寬的[[地鐵|地鐵]]網絡。以11.71億印度盧比興建<ref name="MassRejectedTransitSystem">{{cite news

 | accessdate = 15 August 2012}}</ref>，路線由{{link-en|金奈海灘|Chennai Beach}}至{{link-en|維拉徹里|Velachery}}，全長19.34公里，共18個車站，每日平均有100,000人次使用<ref name="MRTS Goes full Nine-car"/>。此線將舊馬德拉斯的中心商業區與資訊科技走廊連結起來，容量預計可高達每日42.5萬人<ref>{{cite journal

MRTS預計將由{{link-en|金奈軌道鐵路有限公司|Chennai Metro Rail Limited}}管理，將所有高架和地下鐵路軌道劃歸同一機構下<ref>{{cite news|first=Ajai|last=Sreevatsan |url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/Chennai/article553984.ece?homepage=true|title=Metro Rail may take over MRTS|newspaper=[[The_Hindu|The Hindu]]|date=6 August 2010 |location=Chennai, India}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|first=Arun|last=Janardhanan|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/chennai/Chennai-Metro-rail-likely-to-take-over-MRTS/articleshow/39454072.cms|title=Chennai Metro rail likely to take over MRTS |newspaper=[[The_Times_of_India|The Times of India]]|date=2 August 2014 |location=Chennai}}</ref>。完成後，所有現時的MRTS列車將空調化，車費亦會提高至金奈地鐵水平。該轉換預計在2021年完成<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/chennai/metromrts-merger-hunt-on-for-consultant/article9126039.ece |title=Metro-MRTS merger: Hunt on for consultant |date=20 September 2016 |author=Sunitha Sekar |work= The Hindu}}</ref>。

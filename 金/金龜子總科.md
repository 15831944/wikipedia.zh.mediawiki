'''金龜子總科'''（{{lang|la|'''Scarabaeoidea'''}}）是[[多食亞目|多食亞目]][[昆蟲|昆蟲]]之下的一個總科，由獨自一個總科構成'''金龜子下目'''（{{lang|la|'''Scarabaeiformia'''}}）。本總科與[[隱翅蟲下目|隱翅蟲下目]]之下三個總科的關係密切<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1365-3113.2005.00293.x/abstract |last1=Beutel |first1=R. G. |last2=Leschen |first2=R.A.B. |year=2005 |title=Phylogenetic analysis of Staphyliniformia (Coleoptera) based on characters of larvae and adults |journal={{tsl|en|Systematic Entomology||Systematic Entomology}} |volume=30 |issue=4 |doi=10.1111/j.1365-3113.2005.00293.x |language=en }}</ref>。
在這個總科之下有約35,000個物種，而且每年都有約200個新物種被發現<ref>{{cite journal | issue = 32 | journal = Zootaxa | pages = 1–48 | year = 2002 | title = A checklist of the Scarabaeoidea (Coleoptera) of Panama | author = Brett C. Ratcliffe | url = http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2002f/z00032f.pdf}}</ref>。現時本總科之下的各科亦在重組中，所以以下分類列表仍有可能變動。

==分類==
根據''{{tsl|en|American Beetles||American Beetles}}''，本總科包括有以下各個科<ref>{{cite book | author = {{tsl|en|Ross H. Arnett, Jr.||Ross H. Arnett, Jr.}} and {{tsl|en|Michael C. Thomas||Michael C. Thomas}} | title = {{tsl|en|American Beetles||American Beetles}} | publisher = {{tsl|en|CRC Press||CRC Press}} | year = 2001–2002}}</ref>：
* {{tsl|en|Belohinidae||Belohinidae}} <small>Paulian 1959</small>
* {{tsl|en|Bolboceratidae||Bolboceratidae}} <small>{{tsl|en|François Louis de la Porte, comte de Castelnau||Laporte de Castelnau}}, 1840</small>
* [[Ceratocanthidae|Ceratocanthidae]] <small>White 1842</small> (= Acanthoceridae)
* {{tsl|en|Diphyllostomatidae||Diphyllostomatidae}} <small>Holloway 1972</small> ({{tsl|en|false stag beetle||false stag beetle}}s)
* {{tsl|en|Geotrupidae||Geotrupidae}} <small>{{tsl|en|Pierre André Latreille||Latreille}}, 1802</small> ({{tsl|en|earth-boring dung beetle||earth-boring dung beetle}}s)
* {{tsl|en|Glaphyridae||Glaphyridae}} <small>{{tsl|en|William Sharp MacLeay||MacLeay}}, 1819</small> ({{tsl|en|bumble bee scarab beetle||bumble bee scarab beetle}}s)
* {{tsl|en|Glaresidae||Glaresidae}} <small>Semenov-Tian-Shanskii and Medvedev 1932</small> ({{tsl|en|enigmatic scarab beetle||enigmatic scarab beetle}}s)
* {{tsl|en|Hybosoridae||Hybosoridae}} <small>{{tsl|en|Wilhelm Ferdinand Erichson||Erichson}}, 1847</small> ({{tsl|en|scavenging scarab beetle||scavenging scarab beetle}}s)
*[[鍬形蟲科|鍬形蟲科]]（Lucanidae <small>Latreille 1804</small>）
* {{tsl|en|Ochodaeidae||Ochodaeidae}} <small>{{tsl|en|Étienne Mulsant||Mulsant}} and Rey 1871</small> ({{tsl|en|sand-loving scarab beetle||sand-loving scarab beetle}}s)
* {{tsl|en|Passalidae||Passalidae}} <small>{{tsl|en|William Elford Leach||Leach}}, 1815</small> ({{tsl|en|bess beetle||bess beetle}}s)
* {{tsl|en|Pleocomidae||Pleocomidae}} <small>LeConte 1861</small> ({{tsl|en|rain beetle||rain beetle}}s)
*[[金龜子科|金龜子科]]（Scarabaeidae <small>Latreille 1802</small>）
* {{tsl|en|Trogidae||Trogidae}} <small>MacLeay 1819</small> ({{tsl|en|hide beetle||hide beetle}}s)

'''迴盲瓣'''（Ileocecal valve）是分隔[[大腸|大腸]]和[[小腸|小腸]]的[[括约肌|括约肌]]<ref name="IC">{{cite journal | last1 = Pollard | first1 = MF | last2 = Thompson-Fawcett | first2 = MW | last3 = Stringer | first3 = MD | title = The human ileocaecal junction: anatomical evidence of a sphincter | journal = Surgical and Radiologic Anatomy | volume = 34 | issue = 1 | pages = 21–9 | year = 2012 | pmid = 21863224 | doi = 10.1007/s00276-011-0865-z }}</ref>。主要功能是避免大腸內的物質迴流進[[回肠|回肠]]<ref>Barret KE. "Lange Gastrointestinal Physiology". The McGraw-Hill Companies, 2006.</ref>。每天約有二公升的流體會經過迴盲瓣進入大腸。

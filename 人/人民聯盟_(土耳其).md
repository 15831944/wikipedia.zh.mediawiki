是一个[[土耳其|土耳其]]的[[选举联盟|选举联盟]]，于二月2018年二月由 正义与发展党(AKP)和 [[民族主义行动党|民族主义运动党]] (MHP)成立。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sozcu.com.tr/2018/gundem/akp-grup-toplantisi-43-2234166/|title=Cumhurbaşkanı Erdoğan AKP-MHP ittifakının ismini açıkladı: Cumhur İttifakı|accessdate=20 February 2018}}</ref> 联盟的成立是为了贏得[[2018年土耳其大選|2018年大选]]，並支持總統[[雷傑普·塔伊普·埃爾多安|雷傑普·塔伊普·埃爾多安]]連任。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-turkey-politics-election/turkeys-nationalist-opposition-to-back-erdogan-in-2019-election-idUSKBN1EX170?il=0|title=Turkey's nationalist opposition to back Erdogan in 2019 election|accessdate=20 February 2018}}</ref>

'''人天生就是政治動物'''（{{lang-en|Man is by nature a political animal}}<ref name="MacKenzie2009">{{cite book|author=Iain MacKenzie|title=Politics: Key Concepts in Philosophy|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=K8h_JXBdMe0C&pg=PA141|date=24 July 2009|publisher=A&C Black|isbn=978-0-8264-8794-0|pages=141–}}</ref>）出自[[古希腊|古希腊]]哲学家[[亚里士多德|亚里士多德]]的《政洽学》第一篇第二章，<ref name="沈志求1980">{{cite book|author=沈志求|title="资本论"典故注釋|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=JZpwAAAAIAAJ|year=1980|publisher=中国人民大学出版社}}</ref>原话是"anthropos phusei politikon zoon".<ref name="MunchowShamdasani2002">{{cite book|author1=Michael Munchow|author2=Sonu Shamdasani|title=Speculations After Freud: Psychoanalysis, Philosophy and Culture|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UTuIAgAAQBAJ&pg=PA34|date=31 January 2002|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-134-90227-9|pages=34–}}</ref>

==基本释义==
“人天生就是政治動物”主要原因在于人有语言、七情六欲、能够区分黑白善恶是非曲直，是对人与生俱来的社会性的判断，[[政治性|政治性]]是人区别于动物的重要特征，但这并不意味着人天生就是[[政治人|政治人]]，而仅仅只能说是[[社会人|社会人]]。<ref>{{cite book|title=福建论坛|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=BE83AQAAIAAJ|year=2005|publisher=福建论坛杂志社}}</ref>

英国知名史学家[[汉弗莱·戴维·芬德利·基托|汉弗莱·戴维·芬德利·基托]]认为这句话更确切的英文翻译应该是"Man is an animal whose nature it is to live in a polis (city)"意即人是一种天生生活在城邦中的动物。<ref name="Kitto1991">{{cite book|author=H. Kitto|title=The Greeks|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=3VaPakM6oPoC|date=28 February 1991|publisher=Penguin Books Limited|isbn=978-0-14-193721-2}}</ref>

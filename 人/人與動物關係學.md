'''人與動物關係學'''（{{lang-en|'''Anthrozoology'''}}，又稱{{lang-en|''human–animal studies''}}，稱為'''HAS'''），又譯'''人類動物互動科學'''<ref name=appledaily>{{cite news |url=http://hk.apple.nextmedia.com/international/art/20120809/16587126 |title=全球聲討南韓吃狗肉 炎夏進補　年宰吃250萬隻 |publisher=[[蘋果日報_(香港)|蘋果日報]] |language=zh-hant |date=2012-08-09 |accessdate=2012-08-10 }}</ref>，是一項專門研究[[人類|人類]]跟其他[[動物|動物]]的互動關係的動物學分支。這一門學科是一項跨學科的研究，包括有：[[人類學|人類學]]、[[民族學|民族學]]、[[醫學|醫學]]、[[心理學|心理學]]、[[獸醫學|獸醫學]]和[[動物學|動物學]]<ref>DeMello, Margo. ''Teaching the Animal: Human–Animal Studies Across the Disciplines''. Lantern Books, 2010, p. xi.
*Also see [http://www.animalsandsociety.org/content/index.php?pid=41 Animals & Society Institute] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130625044717/http://www.animalsandsociety.org/content/index.php?pid=41 |date=2013-06-25 }}, accessed February 23, 2011.
*Note: the term should not be confused with "animal studies," which often refers to [[animal_testing|animal testing]].</ref>。人類動物互動科學研究的其中一個焦點，是把人類和動物對雙方面各自關係的正面效應量化，以及雙方互動的現實研究<ref>{{cite book |last1=Mills |first1=Daniel S. |chapterurl=http://books.google.com/books?id=vrueZDfPUzoC&pg=PA28 |chapter=Anthrozoology |title=The Encyclopedia of Applied Animal Behaviour and Welfare |publisher=CABI |year=2010 |pages=28–30 |language=en}}</ref>。

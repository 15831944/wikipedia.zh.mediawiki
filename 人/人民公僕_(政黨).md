'''人民公僕'''（{{Lang-uk|Слуга народу|Sluha Narodu}}）是一個[[乌克兰|乌克兰]]政黨，於2018年3月31日經{{le|司法部 (烏克蘭)|Ministry of Justice (Ukraine)|司法部}}註冊登記而成立<ref name="SPppno31217b">{{Ru icon}} [https://ukranews.com/news/560269-mynyust-zaregystryroval-partyyu-sluga-naroda The Ministry of Justice registered the party "Servant of the people"], [[Ukrainian_News_Agency|Ukrainian News Agency]] (26 April 2018)</ref>。該黨的名稱取自烏克蘭電視台「第95季」（Квартал-95）的熱門電視節目《{{le|人民公僕|Servant of the People}}》<ref name="SPpp31217b" /><ref name="SPppno31217b" />。黨的領袖為第95季的律師{{link-uk|伊万巴·卡諾夫|Баканов Іван Геннадійович}}<ref name="SPpp31217b">{{Uk icon}} [https://www.unian.ua/m/politics/2276034-yurist-zelenskogo-zareestruvav-novu-politichnu-partiyu-sluga-narodu.html Lawyer Zelensky has registered a new political party "Servant of the people"], [[烏克蘭獨立新聞社|UNIAN]] (3 December 2017)</ref>。當第95季決定成立政黨時，他們表示為避免其他人以「憤世嫉俗政治」為目的，而決定率先取用《人民公敵》為政黨名稱<ref name="Zelensky7196270">{{Uk icon}} [https://www.pravda.com.ua/articles/2018/10/25/7196270/ The boundary of a joke. As Zelensky prepares for the election], [[Ukrayinska_Pravda|Ukrayinska Pravda]] (25 October 2018)</ref>。

法律上，人民公僕黨的前身為「決定性變革黨」（{{Lang-uk|Партія рішучих змін}}）<ref name="Zelensky7196270">{{Uk icon}} [https://www.pravda.com.ua/articles/2018/10/25/7196270/ The boundary of a joke. As Zelensky prepares for the election], [[Ukrayinska_Pravda|Ukrayinska Pravda]] (25 October 2018)</ref><ref>{{Uk icon}} [https://ukr.lb.ua/news/2017/12/02/383656_partiya_rishuchih_zmin_zminila_nazvu.html "Party of Decisive Change" changed its name to "Servant of the people"], LB.ua (2 December 2017)</ref>。

== 歷史 ==
在2017年12月的「{{le|拉祖姆科夫中心|Razumkov Centre}}」民調中，人民公僕的支持率為4%<ref>{{Uk icon}} [https://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2018/01/23/7169284/ The Council has a chance to go through 7 parties - polls], [[Ukrayinska_Pravda|Ukrayinska Pravda]] (23 January 2018)</ref>，接近議會選舉的5%[[選舉門檻|選舉門檻]]<ref>{{Cite news|title=Parliament passes law on parliamentary elections|url=https://en.interfax.com.ua/news/general/85998.html|accessdate=28 April 2018|agency=[[Interfax-Ukraine|Interfax-Ukraine]]|publisher=[[Interfax-Ukraine|Interfax-Ukraine]]|date=17 November 2011}}</ref><ref name="politics/10095878-vib" />。2018年4月，「{{le|評分組織|評分}}」的{{le|2019年烏克蘭國會選舉|2019 Ukrainian parliamentary election|2019年國會選舉}}{{le|2019年烏克蘭國會選舉民調|Opinion polling for the 2019 Ukrainian parliamentary election|民調}}中，人民公僕的支持率上升至9%<ref name="politics/10095878-vib">{{Uk icon}} [https://www.unian.ua/m/politics/10095878-vibori-do-radi-v-liderah-batkivshchina-partiya-gricenka-i-sluga-narodu-zelenskogo-bpp-pishov-na-dno-opituvannya.html Elections to the Council: in the leaders of "Motherland", the party Gritsenko and "Servant of the people" Zelensky, BPP went to the bottom - poll], [[烏克蘭獨立新聞社|UNIAN]] (26 April 2018)</ref>。同年6月的「評分」民調中，人民公僕更加獲得10.4%受訪者的支持<ref>{{Uk icon}} [http://ratinggroup.ua/research/ukraine/v_ozhidanii_prezidentskih_vyborov_nastroeniya_ukraincev.html In anticipation of presidential elections: the mood of Ukrainians], [[Sociological_group_"RATING"|"RATING"]] (26 June 2018)</ref>。

2018年12月尾，人民公僕宣布派出[[弗拉基米尔·泽连斯基|弗拉基米尔·泽连斯基]]參加[[2019年烏克蘭總統選舉|2019年總統選舉]]<ref name="lection2019555634b">{{Uk icon}} [https://ua.interfax.com.ua/news/election2019/555634.html Zelensky: Party "Servant of the people" goes into politics], [[Interfax-Ukraine|Interfax-Ukraine]] (26.12.2018)</ref><ref name="ZelenskyRT63697b">[https://www.rt.com/news/447930-ukraine-comedian-president-zelenskiy/ Clown show: Stand-up comic enters Ukrainian presidential race… and could win], [[RT_(TV_network)|RT News]] (2 January 2018)</ref>。2019年1月的民調顯示泽连斯基仍在落後，但兩個月後便開始在民意調查中領先其他對手<ref>[https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/europe/parallel-universe-the-front-runner-seeking-to-be-ukraines-president-plays-one-on-tv/2019/03/09/2a4cc22e-3a8c-11e9-b10b-f05a22e75865_story.html?utm_term=.a63b958a9f41 ‘Parallel universe’: The front-runner seeking to be Ukraine’s president plays one on TV]</ref>。最終，泽连斯基在首輪投票中獲得最多票數，與爭取連任的[[彼得·波罗申科|彼得·波罗申科]]晉身第二輪投票<ref>Source: [https://www.cvk.gov.ua/pls/vp2019/wp300pt001f01=719.html Central Election Commission]</ref>。

2019年4月，泽连斯基表明人民公僕將不會加入[[彼得·波罗申科集团“团结”|波罗申科的「團結」集團]]或{{le|在野平台－為了生命|Opposition Platform — For life|在野平台}}<ref name="3daysbUpeZib">{{Ru icon}} [https://www.rbc.ua/rus/news/vladimir-zelenskiy-nam-vygodno-raspustit-1555546435.html Vladimir Zelensky: It is beneficial for us to dissolve the Rada, but we will think and act according to the law], [[RBC_Ukraine|RBC Ukraine]] (18 April 2019)</ref>。

同月的總統選舉第二輪投票中，泽连斯基得票超過70%，成功當選新任總統。泽连斯基同時成為烏克蘭在1991年引入總統直選以來，得票率最高的民選總統。

== 意識形態 ==
2019年4月的訪談中，泽连斯基表示其支持免費發放[[医用大麻|医用大麻]]、支持[[墮胎|墮胎]]、並希望立法將[[性交易|賣淫]]和[[賭博|賭博]]合法化<ref name="3daysbUpeZib">{{Ru icon}} [https://www.rbc.ua/rus/news/vladimir-zelenskiy-nam-vygodno-raspustit-1555546435.html Vladimir Zelensky: It is beneficial for us to dissolve the Rada, but we will think and act according to the law], [[RBC_Ukraine|RBC Ukraine]] (18 April 2019)</ref>。另一方面，泽连斯基反對{{le|管有及攜帶武器合法化|Right to keep and bear arms}}<ref name="3daysbUpeZib" />。

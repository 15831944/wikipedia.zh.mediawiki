'''人民党'''（[[英语|英语]]：The People's Party，TPP）是[[萨摩亚|萨摩亚]]的一个政党。 该党成立于2008年11月。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=41170 |title=Another new political party established in Samoa |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2008-07-29 |accessdate=2008-11-25 }}</ref> ，最初成立的目的是反对[[道路通行方向|道路通行方向]]從右行轉為左行的提案。该党的主席是[[Tole’afoa_Solomona_To’ailoa|Tole’afoa Solomona To’ailoa]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=43322 |title=New political party in Samoa |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2008-11-25 |accessdate=2008-11-25 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.samoaobserver.ws/local/LNPages/0708/2908ln004.htm "Backers of The People’s Party under wraps"], ''Samoa Observer'', July 29, 2008</ref> 按照To'ailoa的说法,该党 "不会仅关注道路走向问题...也会关注其他影响我们民众生活方式的议题。"<ref>[http://www.samoaobserver.ws/local/LNPages/0708/2908ln004.htm "Backers of The People’s Party under wraps"], ''Samoa Observer'', July 29, 2008</ref> <ref>[http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=41185 "New party in Samoa aims wider than road switch repeal"], Radio New Zealand International, July 30, 2008</ref>

'''人类腿部'''，或称'''人腿'''，一般指的是[[人体|人体]]的整个[[下肢|下肢]]部分<ref>{{Cite web| title= Lower Extremity|url=http://www.nlm.nih.gov/cgi/mesh/2007/MB_cgi?mode=&term=Lower+Extremity&field=entry|work=Medical Subject Headings (MeSH)| publisher=National Library of Medicine|accessdate=2009-04-18}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mercksource.com/pp/us/cns/cns_hl_dorlands_split.jsp?pg=/ppdocs/us/common/dorlands/dorland/five/000060202.htm#000060202|title=Lower limb|work=Dorland's Medical Dictionary for Healthcare Consumers|publisher=Elsevier|accessdate=2009-04-18}}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>，包括[[脚|足部]]、[[大腿|大腿]]甚至[[髋关节|髋关节]]等。然而，{{tsl|en|human anatomy|人体解剖}}一般谈及“人腿”时，指的只是从[[膝|膝盖]]到[[腳踝|腳踝]]的这一段下肢，也称“小腿”。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nlm.nih.gov/cgi/mesh/2007/MB_cgi?mode=&term=Leg&field=entry |title=Leg |work=Medical Subject Headings (MeSH) |publisher=National Library of Medicine |accessdate=2009-04-18 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151222151236/http://www.nlm.nih.gov/cgi/mesh/2007/MB_cgi?mode=&term=Leg&field=entry |archivedate=2015-12-22 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=leg|url=http://www.mercksource.com/pp/us/cns/cns_hl_dorlands_split.jsp?pg=/ppdocs/us/common/dorlands/dorland/five/000058188.htm|work=Dorland's Medical Dictionary for Healthcare Consumers|publisher=Elsevier|accessdate=2009-04-18}}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref><ref>{{MerriamWebsterDictionary|leg}}</ref>腿部在{{tsl|en|standing|站立}}以及进行[[舞蹈|舞蹈]]等各种活动中都会用到，也占据了人体的很大一部分重量。女性的腿通常来说具有较大的[[人体解剖学方位|人体解剖学方位]]与[[膝|胫股]]角度，但是[[股骨|股骨]]和[[胫骨|胫骨]]的长度通常短于男性。<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Shultz SJ, Nguyen AD, Schmitz RJ |title=Differences in lower extremity anatomical and postural characteristics in males and females between maturation groups |journal=J Orthop Sports Phys Ther |volume=38 |issue=3 |pages=137–49 |year=2008 |pmid=18383647 |doi=10.2519/jospt.2008.2645 |url=}}</ref>

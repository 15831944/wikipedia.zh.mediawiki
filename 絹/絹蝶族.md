'''絹蝶族'''（學名：Parnassiini）是[[鳳蝶科|鳳蝶科]][[絹蝶亞科|絹蝶亞科]]中的一個[[族_(生物)|族]]，由2個[[屬|屬]]組成，包含了68個物種。廣泛分佈於[[全北界|全北界]]，從歐洲西南部、亞洲至北美洲西部一帶。幼蟲的[[寄主|寄主]]為[[蒺藜科|蒺藜科]]、[[景天科|景天科]]、[[虎耳草科|虎耳草科]]和[[罌粟科|罌粟科]]植物<ref>Michel F., Rebourg C., Cosson E., Descimon H. 2008. Molecular phylogeny of Parnassiinae butterflies (Lepidoptera: Papilionidae) based on the sequences of four mitochondrial DNA segments. Ann. Soc. Entomol. Fr.  44:1–36.</ref><ref>Condamine F.L., Sperling F.A., Wahlberg N., Rasplus J.Y., Kergoat G.J. 2012. What causes latitudinal gradients in species diversity? Evolutionary processes and ecological constraints on swallowtail biodiversity. Ecol. Lett.  15:267–277.</ref>。

== 種系發生 ==

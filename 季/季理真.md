'''季理真'''（{{bd|1964年|}}），[[美国|美国]][[数学家|数学家]]，[[密歇根大学|密歇根大学]][[安娜堡|安娜堡分校]]数学教授。

== 教育经历 ==
季理真出生于[[浙江|浙江]][[温州市|温州]]。<ref>[http://www.hep.com.cn/booksintro/shuxueyushuxueren/zhubianjieshao.htm 《数学与数学人》丛书主编介绍] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20051124091329/http://www.hep.com.cn/booksintro/shuxueyushuxueren/zhubianjieshao.htm|date=2005-11-24}}</ref>1984年，他获得了[[杭州大学|杭州大学]]（现[[浙江大学|浙江大学]]）学士学位。1984年至1985年，他是杭州大学数学系的研究生。1985年，赴美留学，1987年获得[[聖地牙哥加利福尼亞大學|加州大学圣地亚哥分校]]硕士学位。1991年，他获得了[[东北大学_(美国)|东北大学]][[哲學博士|博士学位]]，导师是R. Mark Goresky和[[丘成桐|丘成桐]]。<ref>[http://genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/id.php?id=18739 The Mathematics Genealogy Project – Lizhen Ji]</ref>

== 出版物 ==
除了[[学术出版|学术论文]]，季理真出版或共同撰写了许多有影响力的著作，包括：
* ''Compactifications of Symmetric and Locally Symmetric Spaces''; by Armand Borel, and Lizhen Ji.<ref>[https://www.springer.com/birkhauser/mathematics/book/978-0-8176-3247-2 Compactifications of Symmetric and Locally Symmetric Spaces]</ref>
* ''Arithmetic Groups and Their Generalizations: What, Why, and How''; by Lizhen Ji.
* ''Geometry, Analysis and Topology of Discrete Groups''; co-edited by Lizhen Ji, [[刘克峰|Kefeng Liu]], Yang Lo, and [[丘成桐|Shing-Tung Yau]].
* ''Compactifications of Symmetric Spaces (Progress in Mathematics, Vol 156)''; by Y. Guivarc'H, Lizhen Ji, and J. C. Taylor.
* ''Geometry Analysis and Topology of Discrete Groups''; by Lizhen Ji, Kefeng Liu, and Yang Lo.
* ''Handbook of Geometric Analysis''; by Lizhen Ji.<ref>[http://new.hep.com.cn/portal/product/index?bk=25288-00 Higher Education Press: Handbook of Geometric Analysis] {{Webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20120710185734/http://new.hep.com.cn/portal/product/index?bk=25288-00|date=2012-07-10}}</ref>
* ''Mathematics and Mathematical People''; Chief-editor Lizhen Ji.<ref>[http://www.hep.com.cn/booksintro/shuxueyushuxueren/index.htm 《数学与数学人》（Mathematics and Mathematical People）丛书] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20050830160804/http://www.hep.com.cn/booksintro/shuxueyushuxueren/index.htm|date=2005-08-30}}</ref>
* ''Advanced Lectures in Mathematics''; Chief-editor Lizhen Ji.<ref>[http://intlpress.com/books/ALM_Series.htm Advanced Lectures in Mathematics (''ALM'')] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090504054049/http://intlpress.com/books/ALM_Series.htm|date=2009-05-04}}</ref>

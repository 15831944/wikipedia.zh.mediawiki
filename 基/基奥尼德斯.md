''Handbook to Life in Ancient Greece''.Oxford University Press,USA.ISBN 9780195124910.</ref>[[古希腊|古希腊]][[阿提卡|阿提卡]]最早的[[喜剧|喜剧]][[诗人|诗人]]之一，他与侪辈诗人[[马格奈斯|马格奈斯]]齐名，他于公元前487年在[[城邦|城邦]][[酒神节|酒神节]]上获得胜利。<ref>Langslow 1988:''languages and dialects''</ref><ref>Joint Association of Classical Teachers 1978a and b: ''Learning Greek''</ref>

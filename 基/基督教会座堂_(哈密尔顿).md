'''基督教会座堂'''（Christ's Church Cathedral）是[[加拿大圣公会|加拿大圣公会]]尼亚加拉教区的主教座堂，位于加拿大[[安大略省|安大略省]]哈密尔顿北詹姆士街252号<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.niagara.anglican.ca/parishes/index.cfm?PID=104 |title=Christ's Church Cathedral home page |publisher=Niagara.anglican.ca |accessdate=December 4, 2011 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年10月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>，建于1835年，早于多伦多<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.stjamescathedral.on.ca/ |title=Welcome |publisher=Stjamescathedral.on.ca |accessdate=December 4, 2011}}</ref>、金斯顿<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.stgeorgescathedral.on.ca/index.htm |title=Welcome |publisher=Stgeorgescathedral.on.ca |accessdate=December 4, 2011}}</ref>、伦敦<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.stpaulscathedral.on.ca/ |title=St. Paul's Cathedral HomePage |publisher=Stpaulscathedral.on.ca |date=April 21, 2011 |accessdate=December 4, 2011}}</ref>、[[哈利法克斯|哈利法克斯]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cathedralchurchofallsaints.com/ |title=The Cathedral Church of All Saints |publisher=The Cathedral Church of All Saints |accessdate=December 4, 2011}}</ref>、Fredericton<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.christchurchcathedral.com/ |title=Christ Church Cathedral, Fredericton |publisher=Christchurchcathedral.com |accessdate=December 4, 2011}}</ref>和圣约翰<ref>[http://www.sji.ca/cathedral/ The Anglican Cathedral of St. John the Baptist<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070929204420/http://www.sji.ca/cathedral/ |date=2007-09-29 }}</ref>的座堂，是加拿大第二古老的圣公会座堂，仅次于魁北克的圣三一座堂<ref>{{cite web |url=http://quebec.cathedral.ca/ |title=Cathedral of the Holy Trinity |publisher=Quebec.cathedral.ca |accessdate=December 4, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120322074305/http://quebec.cathedral.ca/ |archivedate=2012年3月22日 }}</ref>。

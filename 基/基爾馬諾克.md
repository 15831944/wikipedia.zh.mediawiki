'''基爾馬諾克'''（{{lang-en|Kilmarnock}}，{{lang-gd|Cille Mheàrnaig}}）是位於[[英國|英國]][[蘇格蘭|蘇格蘭]][[東艾爾郡|東艾爾郡]]的一座城市，有人口46,179人<ref>Brinkhoff (2007)</ref>，是蘇格蘭人口第15多的城市<ref>[http://www.gro-scotland.gov.uk/statistics/theme/population/estimates/special-area/settlements-localities/mid-2008/index.html General Register Office for Scotland - Settlements and Localities - Mid-2008] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110101075852/http://gro-scotland.gov.uk/statistics/theme/population/estimates/special-area/settlements-localities/mid-2008/index.html |date=2011-01-01 }}. Gro-scotland.gov.uk. Retrieved on 2013-07-16.</ref><ref>[http://webarchive.nationalarchives.gov.uk/+/http://www.dca.gov.uk/constitution/city/citygj.htm#part6 [ARCHIVED CONTENT] Department for Constitutional Affairs]. Webarchive.nationalarchives.gov.uk. Retrieved on 2013-07-16.</ref>，也是艾爾郡的第二大都市<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.ayrshirescotland.com/towns/kilmarnock.html | title = Kilmarnock Largest town in Ayrshire | accessdate=8 May 2008}}</ref>。現在基爾馬諾克的音樂劇和電影產業快速發展。

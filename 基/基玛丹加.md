'''基玛丹加'''（英语: Gimadanga），是[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]][[戈巴尔甘尼县|戈巴尔甘尼县]][[汤基帕拉乌帕齐拉|汤基帕拉乌帕齐拉]][[帕特加蒂地区|帕特加蒂地区]]的最大村。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://geonames.nga.mil/namesgaz/ |title=NGA GeoName Database |publisher=[[National_Geospatial_Intelligence_Agency|National Geospatial Intelligence Agency]] |accessdate=January 4, 2016}}</ref>

该村以农业经济为主，另有部分人替政府工作。<ref name="community">{{cite web |url=http://203.112.218.65:8008/WebTestApplication/userfiles/Image/PopCen2011/COMMUNITY_Gopalganj.pdf |title=Community Report: Gopalganj |author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> |website=Population & Housing Census 2011 |publisher=Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics |format=PDF |accessdate=October 23, 2017}}</ref>

该村学校有四所：一所中学，两所小学，一所[[伊斯兰学校|伊斯兰学校]]。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://patgatiup.gopalganj.gov.bd/union_schools |script-title=শিক্ষা প্রতিষ্ঠানসমূহ |trans-title=Educational Institutions |website=Patagati Union |language=bn |access-date=4 January 2016 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>

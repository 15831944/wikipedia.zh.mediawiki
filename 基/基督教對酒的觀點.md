[[巴勒斯坦|巴勒斯坦]]的氣候和土地都很適宜種植葡萄，<ref name="Ewing">Ewing, p. 824.</ref> 當時葡萄酒是一種重要的商品。<ref>See Broshi, ''passim'' (for instance, p. 29: Palestine was "a country known for its good wines").</ref><ref>Compare [http://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=2Ch2:3,10 2Ch 2:3,10]</ref> 葡萄園有圍牆、籬笆以及[[瞭望塔|瞭望塔]]守衛，以防強盜及動物的威脅。<ref>[http://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Ps80:8-15;Is5:1-2;Mk12:1;SS2:15 Ps 80:8-15; Is 5:1f; Mk 12:1; compare SS 2:15]</ref>

[[File:MigdalHaemek5.jpg|thumb]]的地點，右邊是壓榨葡萄的池子，左邊的坑用來收集葡萄汁]]

葡萄的[[收穫_(葡萄酒)|豐收]]帶來歡樂，<ref>[http://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Is16:10;Jr48:33 Compare Is 16:10; Jr 48:33]</ref><ref name="Wine Making">{{cite encyclopedia|title=Wine Making|encyclopedia=Illustrated Dictionary of Bible Life & Times|pages=374f}}</ref><ref>Broshi, p. 24.</ref> 一些收穫的葡萄會被馬上吃掉，而其他的則被製成葡萄乾以及釀成葡萄酒。<ref name="Wine Making"/>

釀造葡萄酒時，在壓榨之後就開始進行[[發酵_(葡萄酒)|發酵]]，并被倒入巨大的陶罐中密封保存。如果需要運輸，則倒入葡萄酒囊（一般由[[製革業|鞣製]]的[[山羊|山羊]]皮製成）中。<ref name="Ewing"/> 數星期后後發酵完畢，被倒入更大容器里貯藏後者直接外售。<ref name="Wine Making"/><ref name="Broshi26">Broshi, p. 26.</ref> 通常當時的葡萄酒中會加入香料等物質以掩蓋其缺陷。<ref>Broshi, p. 27.</ref>

[[住棚節|住棚節]]被描述為葡萄豐收和壓榨後緊接而來的節日。<ref>[http://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Dt16:13-15 Dt 16:13-15]</ref>

== 基督教歷史和傳統中的酒精 ==

=== 基督之前 ===

基督之前[[希伯來人|希伯來人]]認為葡萄酒是神創造的這個世界的一部份，因此雖然過度飲酒會帶來危害，但它“必然本來就是好的”。<ref>Raymond, p. 48.</ref> 猶太人也更強調快樂而不是[[節欲|節欲]]。<ref>Raymond, p. 49.</ref>

直到巴比倫之囚（大約公元前537年左右）後和舊約結束時，葡萄酒一直都是“各年齡段和各階層中的常見飲品、營養的重要來源、節日中最重要的成份、一種被廣泛認可的藥物、任何要塞的供應品以及重要的商品”，被當做“希伯來人生活中不可少的一個元素”。<ref>{{cite book| url=http://books.google.com/?id=AWk8FGoJwWAC&printsec=frontcover | author=[[David_J._Hanson|David J. [[中世紀|中世紀]]的僧侶是成功的啤酒和葡萄酒釀造人之一，<ref name="West">{{cite book|author=Jim West | title=Drinking with Calvin and Luther! | publisher=Oakdown Books |year=2003 |isbn=0-9700326-0-9 | page=22ff}}</ref> 同時他們每天被允許可以喝5升啤酒，並且[[齋戒|齋戒]]期間也可以飲用啤酒。<ref>{{cite journal | url=http://www.thewavemag.com/pagegen.php?pagename=article&articleid=26032 | title=Sin & Tonic: Making beer, wine, and spirits is not the Devil’s work | author=Kevin Lynch | journal=The Wave Magazine | volume=6 | issue=19 | date=September 20 — October 3, 2006 | accessdate=2007-01-22 | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061112204103/http://www.thewavemag.com/pagegen.php?pagename=article&articleid=26032 | archivedate=2006年11月12日 | deadurl=yes }}</ref><ref>[[Will_Durant|Will Durant]] describes the customs of [[England|England]] in the late Middle Ages: "a gallon of beer per day was the usual allowance per person, even for nuns" ({{cite book| author=Will Durant | title=The Reformation |year=1957 | location = New York | publisher = Simon and Schuster| page=113}})</ref> [[聖本篤|聖本篤]]（死於547年）創立的[[圣本篤會規|圣本篤會規]]中偏向認為僧侶每日應無葡萄酒，同時也相信戒酒是抑制物慾的通途。<ref>Holy Rule of St. Benedict, Chapter XL.</ref> 但是他本人也曾指出這是令人不愉快的。故而圣本篤做了讓步，認為每日可以飲用四分之一升（或半升）的葡萄酒，<ref>That is, either about half a [[pint|pint]] or a full pint. [[托馬斯·阿奎那|托馬斯·阿奎那]]（死於1274年）認為適度飲酒不影響被救贖，但是特定的某些人需要完全戒酒。<ref>{{cite book |author=[[Thomas_Aquinas|Thomas Aquinas]] |title=Summa Theologica |chapter=Second Part of the Second Part, Question 149, Article 3 - Whether the use of wine is altogether unlawful? |chapterurl=http://www.ccel.org/ccel/aquinas/summa.SS_Q149_A3.html |accessdate=2008-04-17 |quote=A man may have wisdom in two ways. 1319年，[[貝爾納多·托洛梅伊|貝爾納多·托洛梅伊]]最初堅持比本篤會更嚴格的禁欲規則。其追隨者是“狂熱的完全禁欲者”，甚至摧毀了他們自己的葡萄園，但這相關規章不久便被修改了。<ref>{{cite encyclopedia |author=J. C. Almond |encyclopedia=Catholic Encyclopedia |title=Olivetans |url=http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/11244c.htm |year=1913 |quote=St. Bernard Ptolomei's idea of monastic reform was that which had inspired every founder of an order or congregation since the days of St. Benedict—a return to the primitive life of solitude and austerity. Severe corporal mortifications were ordained by rule and inflicted in public. The usual ecclesiastical and conventual fasts were largely increased and the daily food was bread and water ... They were also fanatical total abstainers; not only was St. Benedict's kindly concession of a hemina of wine rejected, but the vineyards were rooted up and the wine-presses and vessels destroyed ... Truly, relaxation was inevitable. It was never reasonable that the heroic austerities of St. Bernard and his companions should be made the rule, then and always, for every monk of the order ... It was always the custom for each one to dilute the wine given him.}}</ref>

因為天主教聖餐飲用的需要，<ref name="AltarWine">{{ws|"[[s:Catholic_Encyclopedia_(1913)/Altar_Wine|Altar Wine]]" in the 1913 ''Catholic Encyclopedia''}}</ref>除了一些酒精過敏的僧侶，<ref name="EucharistWine">{{cite web|url=http://www.americancatholic.org/Messenger/May1996/Wiseman.asp#F3|title=Ask the Wise Man: Eucharistic Wine and an Alcoholic Priest; Hosts for the Gluten-allergic | work=St. Anthony Messenger |date=May 1996 | accessdate=2007-01-22 }}</ref>[[傳教士|傳教士]]們將葡萄種植技術帶到了幾乎所有他們能抵達的地方，以生產葡萄酒並用以進行[[彌撒|彌撒]]。<ref name="West"/>  天主教有許多早期和中世紀[[聖人|聖人]]都與酒有關，例如啤酒的[[主保聖人|主保聖人]][[聖艾德里安|聖艾德里安]]、[[釀酒者|釀酒者]]和[[酒保|酒保]]以及葡萄酒[[商人|商人]]的主保聖人[[聖阿曼德|聖阿曼德]]、[[都爾的瑪爾定|都爾的瑪爾定]]以及[[葡萄酒商|葡萄酒商]]的主保聖人[[聖文森特|聖文森特]]。<ref name="West"/>

在[[東正教|東正教]]，除去聖餐之外，其他一些儀式也需要用到葡萄酒。在聖餐之後，信徒們會飲用一杯溫葡萄酒。不過在大多是齋日東正教圖都是不可以喝葡萄酒的，[[聖特立馮|聖特立馮]]是葡萄園工人的主保聖人。在[[塞爾維亞東正教會|塞爾維亞東正教會]]中，葡萄就會被用在慶祝一個叫做Slava的節日。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.macedonian-heritage.gr/Wine/WineReligionCulture.html|title=Wine, Religion and Culture|publisher=Macedonian Heritage|year=2003|accessdate=2007-02-22}}</ref>

=== 宗教改革===


在[[宗教改革|宗教改革]]中，從[[馬丁·路德|馬丁·路德]]、[[約翰·加爾文|約翰·加爾文]]到[[慈運理|慈運理]]、[[約翰·諾克斯|約翰·諾克斯]]都支持在儀式中使用葡萄酒，<ref>See West, ''Drinking'' and Mathison, "Protestant Transubstantiation" parts 2 and 3 for many examples.</ref> 加爾文本人在[[日內瓦|日內瓦]]的年薪中就包括幾桶葡萄酒。<ref>{{cite journal|author=Jim West | title=A Sober Assessment of Reformational Drinking | journal=Modern Reformation |date=March /April 2000 | volume=9| issue=2 | accessdate=2007-01-22}}</ref> [[路德會|路德會]][[協和信條|協和信條]]（1576年）、<ref>[http://www.ccel.org/ccel/schaff/creeds3.iii.iv.html#iii.iv-p25.766 Article 7]</ref> 衛理公會[[教綱_(衛理公會)|教綱]]（1784年）<ref>[http://www.ccel.org/ccel/schaff/creeds3.v.vi.html#v.vi-p42 Article 18]</ref> 和[[再洗禮派|再洗禮派]]<ref>Belgic Confession (1561), [http://www.ccel.org/ccel/schaff/creeds3.iv.viii.html#iv.viii-p7.1780 article 35]</ref><ref>Heidelberg Catechism (1563), [http://www.ccel.org/ccel/schaff/creeds3.iv.vi.html#iv.vi-p0.1627 questions 78-80]</ref><ref>Thirty-Nine Articles (1571), [http://www.ccel.org/ccel/schaff/creeds3.iv.xi.html#iv.xi-p40.50 article 28]</ref><ref>Westminster Confession of Faith (1647), [http://www.ccel.org/ccel/schaff/creeds3.iv.xvii.ii.html#iv.xvii.ii-p9.760 chapter 29, paragraph 3]</ref> 也都支持使用葡萄酒。在[[英國|英國]]，[[清教徒|清教徒]]也支持飲用葡萄酒和[[麥芽酒|麥芽酒]]。<ref>West, ''Drinking'', pp. 68ff.</ref>

=== 美洲殖民地 ===

最初前往[[美洲|美洲]]的移民幾乎每人都帶著一定數量的酒，<ref>West, ''Drinking'', pp. 79ff.</ref> 並將之用於幾乎任何領域，包括神職授任、葬禮等等。<ref>West, ''Drinking'', p. 86.</ref> [[美洲殖民地|美洲殖民地]]牧師、[[哈佛大學|哈佛大學]]校長[[英克里斯·马瑟|英克里斯·马瑟]]在一次布道時說：<ref>Increase Mather (1673)."Wo to Drunkards."</ref>{{cquote|飲酒本身是上帝的一個好的造物，我們應懷著感激的心去接受它，但是酗酒卻是來自撒旦}}

=== 衛理公會===

[[約翰·衛斯理|約翰·衛斯理]]認為[[烈酒|烈酒]]，例如[[白蘭地|白蘭地]]和[[威士忌|威士忌]]不應用於除醫療外的其他地方，並且說不加選擇地將[[蒸餾|蒸餾器]]賣給他人甚於神譴的毒藥和謀殺。<ref>{{cite book |title=The Sermons of John Wesley |editor=Thomas Jackson (ed.)|year=1999 |author=John Wesley |chapter=On the Use of Money |chapterurl=http://wesley.nnu.edu/john_wesley/sermons/050.htm |accessdate=2008-05-20 |publisher=Wesley Center for Applied Theology at Northwest Nazarene University |origyear=1872}}</ref> 1744年，衛斯理給衛理公會下的一些組織的指示中要求他們“不品嘗任何含酒精的液體...除非有醫師許可”。<ref>{{cite book |author=Methodist Episcopal Church |others=with explanatory notes by Thomas Coke and Francis Asbury |title=Doctrines and Discipline of the Methodist Episcopal Church |year=1798 |chapter=Directions given to the Band-Societies. December 25th, 1744. |edition=10th |chapterurl=http://books.google.com/books?id=zOoCAAAAQAAJ&pg=RA1-PA150 |page=150}}</ref> 1780年，在[[巴爾的摩|巴爾的摩]]的一場衛斯理工會會議上，教徒們公開反對烈酒生產并決定與不願放棄生產烈酒的人脫離關係。<ref name="Bangs">{{cite book |author=Nathan Bangs |title=A History of the Methodist Episcopal Church |volume=1 |year=1838 |publisher=T. Mason and G. Lane for the Methodist Episcopal Church |location=New York |url=http://books.google.com/?id=vSURAAAAIAAJ&pg=PA134 |page=134f}}</ref> 在[[禁酒運動#美國#早期禁酒運動：1784年-1861年|第一波美國禁酒運動]]之後，他們將戒酒的範圍擴展到了除烈酒之外的其他酒精飲料。<ref name="Bangs"/><ref>{{cite book |author=Henry J. Fox and William B. Hoyt |chapter=Rule Respecting Intoxicating Liquors |title=Quadrennial Register of the Methodist Episcopal Church |year=1852 |publisher=Case, Tiffany & Co. |location=Connecticut |url=http://books.google.com/?id=YNeBt07_IaUC&pg=PA200 |page=200f}}</ref>

衛斯理的宗教會規在1784年被美國的[[美以美會|美以美會]]接受了，承認葡萄酒可用於聖餐並且任何人都可以享用。<ref>Coke and Asbury, [http://books.google.com/books?id=zOoCAAAAQAAJ&pg=RA1-PA150#PPA24,M1 notes on Article XIX], p. 24.</ref>

=== 禁酒運動 ===
{{Main|禁酒運動}}

很多大規模的[[禁酒運動|禁酒運動]]都是在19世紀之後發起的，而它們又多數只是反烈酒，<ref>{{cite encyclopedia |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=EchvNjm6alIC&pg=PP14#PPA246,M1 |title=Temperance Reform |author=[[John_McClintock_(theologian)|John McClintock]] and [[James_Strong_(theologian)|James Strong]] (eds.) |publisher=Harper and Brothers | location=New York |year=1891 |encyclopedia=Cyclopaedia of Biblical, Theological, and Ecclesiastical Literature |volume=X |page=245f |quote=In January, 1826, Rev. Calvin Chapin published in the ''Connecticut Observer'' a series of articles in which he took the ground that the only real antidote for the evils deprecated is total abstinence, not only from distilled spirits, but from all intoxicating beverages. His position, however, was generally regarded as extreme, and he had few immediate converts to his opinions.}}</ref> 在反對者看來烈酒更便宜也更容易使人醉酒，同時也並不都反對適當飲用其他的酒精飲料。然而隨著美洲大陸[[第二次大覺醒|第二次大覺醒]]的發生，禁酒運動也逐漸開始排斥一切酒精飲料了。<ref name="ProtTrans4">{{cite journal|author=Keith Mathison | title=Protestant Transubstantiation - Part 4: Origins of and Reasons for the Rejection of Wine | url=http://thirdmill.org/newfiles/kei_mathison/TH.Mathison.Prot.Transub.4.html | journal=IIIM Magazine Online | volume=3| issue=4 |date=January 22–28, 2001 | accessdate=2007-01-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | author=Ra McLaughlin | url=http://thirdmill.org/answers/answer.asp/file/99906.qna/category/ch/page/questions/site/iiim | title=Protestant Transubstantiation (History of) | publisher=[[Third_Millennium_Ministries|Third Millennium Ministries]] | accessdate=2007-01-22}}</ref><ref>Pierard, p. 28.</ref>

最終的結果是酒開始被厭棄，以至於開始從聖餐等宗教儀式中逐漸減少了。<ref name="ProtTrans4"/><ref>{{cite encyclopedia| title=Wine |encyclopedia=The Oxford Companion to the Bible| author=M. D. Coogan| editor=[[Bruce_Metzger|Bruce Metzger]] and M. D. Coogan |publisher=Oxford University Press, USA |year=1993 |isbn=978-0-19-504645-8 |pages= 799f}}</ref> 然而在許多教會中葡萄酒這樣的葡萄釀造的酒還是很受歡迎的，而一些教會則宣稱需要在聖餐中使用“未發酵的酒”，<ref name="TuckerKaren">{{cite book |last=Tucker |first=Karen B. Westerfield |chapter=The Lord's Supper |title=American Methodist Worship |url=http://books.google.com/?id=I1TDD5-CLlEC&pg=PA151&lpg=PA151&vq=unfermented |year=2001 |publisher=Oxford University Press |location=New York |isbn=0-19-512698-X|page=151}}</ref> 因此一些禁酒者開始用濃縮葡萄汁取代葡萄酒。<ref name="BacchiocchiSamuele">{{cite book |last=Bacchiocchi |first=Samuele |chapter=The Preservation of Grape Juice |title=Wine in the Bible |url=http://www.biblicalperspectives.com/books/wine_in_the_bible/3.html |year=1989 |publisher=Signal Press & Biblical Perspectives |isbn=1-930987-07-2 |access-date=2013-03-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121228173516/http://www.biblicalperspectives.com/books/wine_in_the_bible/3.html |archive-date=2012-12-28 |dead-url=yes }}</ref> 1869年，[[托马斯·布拉姆韦尔·韦尔奇|托马斯·布拉姆韦尔·韦尔奇]]<ref name="HallettAnthony">{{cite encyclopedia |last=Hallett |first=Anthony |coauthors=Diane Hallett |encyclopedia=Entrepreneur Magazine Encyclopedia of Entrepreneurs |title=Thomas B. Welch, Charles E. Welch |url=http://books.google.com.au/books?id=2PJICBnrpDgC&pg=PA481&lpg=PA481&ots=8VlKeO8Ww8&output=html&sig=so5s1ltOJsPrzKXXiHfYQLDO4Kw |year=1997 |publisher=John Wiley and Sons |isbn=0-471-17536-6 |pages=481–483}}</ref> 找到了利用[[巴士德消毒法|巴士德消毒法]]保存葡萄汁的方法，這樣教會就可以方便地在聖餐中使用葡萄汁而不必擔憂其腐敗問題了。

1838年至1845年，[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]]禁酒人士、神父[[西奧博爾德·馬修|西奧博爾德·馬修]]一共對大約三到四百萬人做了禁酒宣傳，並和一些美國人形成了許多禁酒團體，但是其影響力卻十分有限。1872年，[[美國基督教完全禁酒聯盟|美國基督教完全禁酒聯盟]]聯合這些團體成立，1913年其會員達到90,000人，其中不乏青少年和女性，其宗旨是通過說服普通人而不是利用政治手段來達成目的，其行為受到了兩任[[天主教教宗|天主教教宗]][[利奧十三世|利奧十三世]]（1878年）和[[庇護十世|庇護十世]]（1906年）的贊許。<ref>{{cite encyclopedia |author=W. Liese, J. Keating, and W. Shanley |year=1912 |title=Temperance Movements |encyclopedia=The Catholic Encyclopedia |location=New York |publisher=Robert Appleton Company |accessdate=2008-05-19 |url=http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14482a.htm}}</ref> 不過也有相當的反對聲音，例如[[密爾沃基|密爾沃基]]的大主教[[塞巴斯蒂安·傑拉爾德·梅斯梅爾|塞巴斯蒂安·傑拉爾德·梅斯梅爾]]公開譴責禁酒運動遵循了“絕對錯誤的準則”并蓄意破壞教會“最神聖的秘密”——即聖餐，並且禁止他[[主教教區|主教教區]]內的神父支持禁酒運動，而建議他們採取溫和的態度。<ref>{{cite news |title=Prelate Assails Dry Law. Archbishop Messmer Forbids Catholic Help to Amendment |work=The New York Times |date=June 25, 1918 |page=13 |url=http://query.nytimes.com/mem/archive-free/pdf?res=9A07E6DB173EE433A25756C2A9609C946996D6CF |accessdate=2008-05-20 | format=PDF}}</ref> 最終，天主教教義並沒有受到禁酒運動太大的影響。<ref>McClintock and Strong, "Temperance Reform", p. 248: "[T]he [temperance] cause received a new impulse from the presence and labors of father Mathew, the Irish apostle of temperance, who came to America in June and spent sixteen months of hard work chiefly among the Irish Catholics. 路德教和[[英國國教|英國國教]]也沒有轉變它們的中立地位，甚至英國的宗教禁酒團體事實上也不完全主張禁酒。<ref name="Crusade">{{cite book | author=Lilian Lewis Shiman | title=Crusade Against Drink in Victorian England | publisher=St. Martin's Press |year=1988 | isbn=0-312-17777-1 | page=5}}</ref> 而其他的教會則頻繁出現在禁酒的舞臺上，<ref>{{cite book |title=Ardent Spirits: The Rise and Fall of Prohibition |author=John Kobler |year=1993 |publisher=Da Capo Press |isbn=0-306-80512-X |page=53}}</ref><ref name="Engs">Engs: "Levine has noted that 'in Western societies, only Nordic and English-speaking cultures developed large, ongoing, extremely popular temperance movements in the nineteenth century and the first third or so of the twentieth century.' ==現今==

現在，基督教關於酒的觀點大致可分為溫和主義、迴避主義和禁酒主義。后二者觀點有相似之處，其區別在於前者支持利用理智來達成禁酒的目的，而後者則偏向利用法律。<ref name="Gentry"/>

=== 禁酒主義===

自禁酒運動衰退之後，持禁酒主義的人數便逐漸減少。依舊持該論點的教會和組織有[[南美浸信會|南美浸信會]]<ref name="sbc">{{cite web |title=Resolution On The Liquor Situation |year=1938 |publisher=Southern Baptist Convention|url=http://www.sbc.net/resolutions/amResolution.asp?ID=64 |quote=We declare afresh our unalterable opposition to the whole liquor traffic, whisky, beer, and wine, and to the license system by which this most blighting and corrupting traffic fastened upon our body social and body politic....

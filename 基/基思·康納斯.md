'''基思·康納斯'''（Carmen Keith Conners，{{bd|1933年|3月20日|2017年|7月5日}}）是美國[[心理學家|心理學家]]，是早期創建[[注意力不足過動症|注意力不足過動症]]（ADHD）診斷標準的人之一<ref name="nytimes1"/>，他曾創建針對ADHD病患的家長及老師的评定量表（Conners' Rating Scale）<ref name="ReynoldsFletcher-Janzen2007">{{cite book|author1=Cecil R. Reynolds|author2=Elaine Fletcher-Janzen|title=Encyclopedia of Special Education A Reference for the Education of Children, Adolescents, and Adults with Disabilities and Other Exceptional Individuals|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=wdNpBchvdvQC&pg=PA535|year=2007|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=978-0-471-67798-7|pages=535–}}</ref>，以及Conners'成人ADHD自评量表（Conners Adult ADHD Rating Scales，簡稱CAARS）<ref>[https://www.pearsonclinical.com.au/products/view/382 Multiple perspectives help provide a well-rounded view of ADHD]</ref>，以及，不過他在2013年也曾表示，他認為ADHD有過度診斷的情形<ref name=NYT2013>{{cite news|last1=Schwarz|first1=Alan|title=The Selling of Attention Deficit Disorder|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2013/12/15/health/the-selling-of-attention-deficit-disorder.html|accessdate=26 February 2015|issue=14 December 2013|newspaper=The New York Times|date=14 December 2013|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150301054334/http://www.nytimes.com/2013/12/15/health/the-selling-of-attention-deficit-disorder.html|archivedate=1 March 2015|df=dmy-all}}</ref>。

基思·康納斯在1933年3月20日出生於[[犹他州|犹他州]]的{{le|宾厄姆坎宁|Bingham Canyon, Utah}}，家中有三個小孩，父親是麥克·康納斯（Michael Conners）<ref name="nytimes1">{{cite web|author=BENEDICT CAREY|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/07/13/health/keith-conners-dead-psychologist-adhd-diagnosing.html?mcubz=0 |title=Keith Conners, Psychologist Who Set Standard for Diagnosing A.D.H.D., Dies at 84 - The New York Times |publisher=Nytimes.com |date=July 13, 2017 |accessdate=July 17, 2017}}</ref>。

基思·康納斯在2017年7月5日在[[北卡羅來納州|北卡羅來納州]]的[[德罕_(北卡羅來納州)|德罕]]過世，享年84歲<ref name="nytimes1"/>。

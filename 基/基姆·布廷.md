'''基姆·布廷'''（{{lang-fr|Kim Boutin}}，{{bd|1994年|12月16日||}}）生于魁北克省舍布魯克，是一名加拿大短道速滑运动员。她曾在2018年平昌冬奥获得女子1000米银牌、女子500米<ref name="telegraph20180213">{{cite news|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/winter-olympics/2018/02/13/elise-christie-bids-team-gbs-first-medal-winter-olympics-live/|title=History repeats itself as speed skater Elise Christie crashes out in final at Winter Olympics|last=Hodges|first=Vicky|date=2018-02-13|publisher=[[每日电讯报|The Telegraph]]|accessdate=2018-02-13}}</ref>和女子1500米铜牌。她是史上第一位在同一届奥运短道速滑三个个人项目上均获得奖牌的加拿大运动员以及第一位获得女子1500米奖牌的加拿大运动员<ref>{{cite news |title=Samuel Girard becomes Canada's 1st Olympic champion in 1,000m short track |author=Pete Evans |url=https://olympics.cbc.ca/news/article/canada-boutin-girard-skate-for-medals-short-track.html |date=2018-02-17 |accessdate=2018-02-17 }}{{dead link|date=2018年5月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。由于布廷在平昌冬奥期间表现出色，她获选为该届冬奥加拿大队[[2018年冬季奧林匹克運動會閉幕式入場旗手|闭幕式旗手]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://stillmed.olympic.org/media/Document%20Library/OlympicOrg/Games/Winter-Games/Games-PyeongChang-2018-Winter-Olympic-Games/Ceremonies/PyeongChang-2018-Closing-Ceremony-Flag-Bearers.pdf|title=Closing Ceremony Flagbearers - Olympic Winter Games, PyeongChang 2018|date=2018-02-25|publisher=[[國際奧林匹克委員會|IOC]]|accessdate=2018-02-25|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180225122818/https://stillmed.olympic.org/media/Document%20Library/OlympicOrg/Games/Winter-Games/Games-PyeongChang-2018-Winter-Olympic-Games/Ceremonies/PyeongChang-2018-Closing-Ceremony-Flag-Bearers.pdf|archivedate=2018-02-25}}</ref><ref name="flagbearer">{{cite news|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/sports/olympics/speed-skater-kim-boutin-named-canadas-flag-bearer-for-closing-ceremony/article38104316/|title=Speed skater Kim Boutin named Canada's flag bearer for closing ceremony|date=2018-02-24|work=The Canadian Press|publisher=[[环球邮报|The Globe and Mail]]|access-date=2018-02-24}}</ref>。

==运动生涯==
2017年8月底，布廷入选加拿大奥运短道速滑队<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sportsnet.ca/olympics/three-time-olympic-gold-medallist-hamelin-lead-canadas-2018-short-track-team/|title=Gold medallist Hamelin to lead Canada's 2018 short-track team|date=2017-08-30|publisher=Sportsnet |access-date=2017-11-16}}</ref>。在女子500米决赛中，布廷原本以43.881秒的成绩第四个到达终点，后来由于第二个到达终点的韩国选手[[崔珉禎|崔珉禎]]因犯规而被取消成绩，布廷递补获得铜牌<ref name="telegraph20180213"/>。比赛回放显示，崔珉禎和布廷间有大约三次身体接触。赛后有不少韩国网民在她的社交媒体评论区中进行人身攻击，部分人甚至发出死亡威胁。<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/sports/2018/02/702_244249.html |title= What happened during Choi's disqualification? |first=Nam |last=Hyun-woo |newspaper=The Korea Times |date=2018-02-14|accessdate=2018-02-14}}</ref>[[加拿大奥林匹克委员会|加拿大奥林匹克委员会]]对此事非常重视，[[皇家加拿大骑警|皇家加拿大骑警]]也就此展开调查<ref>{{cite news |title=Samuel Girard, Kim Boutin prove Canadian short track's next generation has arrived |url=https://olympics.cbc.ca/news/article/samuel-girard-kim-boutin-prove-canadian-short-track-next-generation-has-arrived.html |publisher=CBC Sports |date=2018-02-17 |accessdate=2018-02-17 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180217201625/https://olympics.cbc.ca/news/article/samuel-girard-kim-boutin-prove-canadian-short-track-next-generation-has-arrived.html |archivedate=2018年2月17日 |df= }}</ref>。

在后面进行的1500米比赛中，布廷没有受到网络上人身威胁的影响，在决赛中第三个到达终点，再次收获铜牌<ref>{{cite news|title=Samuel Girard, Kim Boutin prove Canadian short track's next generation has arrived|url=https://olympics.cbc.ca/news/article/samuel-girard-kim-boutin-prove-canadian-short-track-next-generation-has-arrived.html|publisher=CBC Sports|date=2018-02-17|accessdate=2018-02-17}}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>。在短道速滑最后一个比赛日中，她在1000米决赛中以1分29秒956的成绩收获银牌<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/olympics/2018/02/22/canadas-kim-boutin-takes-silver-in-womens-1000m-short-track-speedskating.html|title=Kim Boutin flies her way to 1,000-metre silver|date=2018-02-22|accessdate=2018-05-05|publisher=Toronto Star}}</ref>。她也因为在2018年冬奥期间发挥出色而获选为该届冬奥[[2018年冬季奥林匹克运动会加拿大代表团|加拿大代表团]]闭幕式旗手<ref name="flagbearer"/>。

一个月后，布廷参加了在蒙特利尔举办的短道速滑世锦赛，最终收获两枚铜牌，分别来自女子1500米和女子3000米接力<ref>{{cite web|title=Hamelin wins 1500m world title in Montreal, Boutin brings home bronze |url=https://olympic.ca/2018/03/17/hamelin-wins-1500m-world-title-in-montreal-boutin-brings-home-bronze/ |publisher=Canadian Olympic Committee|date=2018-03-17|accessdate=2018-05-05}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Charles Hamelin becomes speed skating world champion on home soil |url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/2018/03/18/charles-hamelin-becomes-speed-skating-world-champion-on-home-soil.html |publisher=Toronto Star|date=2018-03-18|accessdate=2018-05-05}}</ref>。

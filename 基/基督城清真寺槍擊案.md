== 襲擊事件 ==

=== 域卡頓光明清真寺 ===
[[努爾清真寺_(基督城)|努爾清真寺]]（{{lang-ar|مسجد النور}} ''Masjid Al Noor''）位於{{le|域卡頓 (新西蘭)|Riccarton, New Zealand|域卡頓}}的迪恩斯大街（Deans Ave），是此次襲擊中遇襲地之一<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/australasia/new-zealand-mosque-shooting-latest-updates-christchurch-hagley-park-a8823961.html |title=Armed police deployed after shots fired at New Zealand mosque |last=Sharman |first=Jon |date=2019-03-15 |publisher=''[[獨立報|The Independent]]'' |language=en|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。當局相信遇襲時有超過500人在清真寺內參加[[主麻日|主麻日]]<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.tvnz.co.nz/one-news/new-zealand/police-responding-critical-incident-in-christchurch |title=LIVE: Mass shooting at Christchurch mosque as police respond to 'active shooter' situation |date=2019-03-15 |publisher={{tsl|en|1 News||1 News NOW}} |accessdate=2019-03-15}}</ref>。一名住在清真寺附近的居民向記者表示，其親眼目睹槍手在行車路上放下[[自動火器|自動火器]]後逃離現場<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.abc.net.au/news/2019-03-15/armed-police-respond-to-suspected-shooting-christchurch-mosque/10904306 |title=Reports of multiple casualties in Christchurch mosque shooting |date=2019-03-15 |publisher={{tsl|en|ABC News (Australia)||ABC News}} |language=en-AU|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。

=== 林伍德伊斯蘭中心 ===
林伍德伊斯蘭中心是此次襲擊中第二個遇襲的地方<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.indianweekender.co.nz//Pages/ArticleDetails/7/9258/New-Zealand/Christchurch-gets-its-second-mosque|title=Christchurch gets its second mosque|website=Indian Weekender|language=en-NZ|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.newshub.co.nz/home/new-zealand/2019/03/christchurch-mosque-shooting-alleged-footage-emerges-of-apparent-gunman.html |title=Christchurch mosque shooting: Footage emerges of alleged gunman |last=Barraclough |first=Breanna |date=2019-03-15 |publisher={{tsl|en|Newshub||Newshub}}|access-date=2019-03-15 |language=en}}</ref>，当地警方證實兩宗襲擊存在關連<ref>{{cite news |last1=Molyneux |first1=Vita |title=Live updates: Six people have reportedly been killed in Christchurch shootings near mosque |url=https://www.newshub.co.nz/home/new-zealand/2019/03/gunshots-heard-near-hagley-park-in-christchurch.html |accessdate=2019-03-15 |publisher=Newshub|date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。

=== 土製炸彈 ===
当地警方在一台車輛上發現了數個[[簡易爆炸裝置|簡易爆炸裝置]]，其後[[紐西蘭國防軍|國防軍]]引爆了所有爆炸裝置，無人受傷<ref name="radionz-384811" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.foxnews.com/world/multiple-fatalities-at-new-zealand-mosque-shooting-police|title=4 in custody after New Zealand mass shootings at mosques; IEDs found in cars|last=Casiano|first=Louis|date=2019-03-14|website=Fox News|language=en|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。被發現的土製炸彈都被連接至一個不明裝置。

=== 社群直播 ===
其中一名槍手行兇時在[[Facebook|Facebook]]上進行了長達16分鐘的[[網路直播|網路直播]]，其自稱為28歲的「布伦顿·塔兰特」，在[[澳洲|澳洲]]出生<ref name="nzherald_2019-03-15"/>。在行兇前，他在[[8chan|8chan]]發佈了一標題為「大颠覆（The Great Replacement）」的宣言，相信是參考自「{{le|白人種族滅絕陰謀論|white genocide conspiracy theory}}」和「{{le|大颠覆陰謀論|The Great Replacement conspiracy theory}}」。塔兰特在宣言中概述了他的攻击，声称从[[不列顛法西斯聯盟|不列顛法西斯聯盟]]领导人[[奥斯瓦尔德·莫斯利|奥斯瓦尔德·莫斯利]]那里获得灵感，并将其称为“民族 - 民族主义法西斯主义者”<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.news.com.au/world/pacific/gunman-who-opened-fire-on-christchurch-mosque-addresses-attack-in-manifesto/news-story/70372a39f720697813607a9ec426a734|title=Christchurch mosque shooting: Gunman posts manifesto detailing reasons for attack|website=news.com.au|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。槍手的[[Twitter|Twitter]]帐户曾經發佈[[新納粹主義|新納粹主義]]的[[十四字真言|十四字真言]]和{{le|黑太陽|Black Sun (symbol)}}，並寫上[[仇外|仇外]]言論<ref name="nzherald_2019-03-15">{{cite news |url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/nz/news/article.cfm?c_id=1&objectid=12213076 |title=Mosque shooting: Christchurch gunman livestreamed shooting |date=2019-03-15 |newspaper=''The New Zealand Herald''|access-date=2019-03-15 |language=en-NZ |issn=1170-0777}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.kotaku.com.au/2019/03/new-zealand-gunman-says-subscribe-to-pewdiepie-moments-before-livestreaming-shooting/ |title=New Zealand Gunman Says 'Subscribe To PewDiePie' Moments Before Livestreaming Shooting |date=2019-03-15 |website=Kotaku Australia |language=en|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。此直播被轉發至多個直播平台，包括{{le|LiveLeak|LiveLeak}}和[[YouTube|YouTube]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/national/crime/111320003/christchurch-mosque-shootings-man-claims-responsibility-for-attack-records-assault-on-video|title=Christchurch mosque shootings: Man claims responsibility for shootings, records assault on video|website=Stuff}}</ref>。包括[[新西兰电信|新西兰电信]]、{{le|新西蘭沃達丰|Vodafone New Zealand}}在內的多個新西蘭[[互聯網服務供應商|互聯網服務供應商]]已經限制網民瀏覽LiveLeak等發布肇事直播片段的服務平台<ref>{{Cite web|title=Christchurch Mosque Shooting|url=https://www.geekzone.co.nz/forums.asp?forumid=184&topicid=248227|work=www.geekzone.co.nz|accessdate=2019-03-15}}</ref>。

恐襲的生還者被送到附近的醫院，其中{{le|基督城醫院|Christchurch Hospital}}戒備森嚴<ref name="auto">{{Cite news |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-47578798 |title=Deadly mass shooting at New Zealand mosques |date=2019-03-15|access-date=2019-03-15 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.9news.com.au/2019/03/15/12/38/shooting-christchurch-news-new-zealand |title=Four arrested after mass shooting at mosque, Islamic centre |website=www.9news.com.au|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。[[坎特伯雷区|坎特伯雷区]]{{le|坎特伯雷区衛生局|Canterbury District Health Board|衛生局}}事後啟動[[大量傷患事件|大量傷患應變計劃]]<ref name="auto"/>。

遇襲清真寺附近的學校都被封鎖<ref name=":1" />，執法部門呼籲全國所有清真寺即時關閉，直至另行通知，警方亦已經派出警員到所有清真寺加強保安<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/national/111323598/armed-police-guard-mosques-around-new-zealand |title=Armed police guard mosques around New Zealand |website=Stuff |language=en|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。[[紐西蘭支線航空|紐西蘭支線航空]]宣布以安全為由，所有由[[基督城國際機場|基督城國際機場]]起飛的航班已被取消<ref>{{cite news |title= Air New Zealand cancels flights, offers 'flexibility' |url= https://www.stuff.co.nz/business/111323903/air-new-zealand-offers-flexibility-for-christchurch-flights |first= Susan |last= Edmunds |date= 2019-03-15 |publisher= Stuff.co.nz}}</ref>。{{le|國內外安全協調委員會|Officials Committee for Domestic and External Security Co-ordination}}召開緊急會議，統籌政府各部門回應恐襲<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/national/politics/111318893/mosque-shooting-sparks-top-level-crisis-meeting-in-wellington |title=Top level crisis meeting in Wellington after Christchurch mosque shooting |website=Stuff |accessdate=2019-03-15}}</ref>。

{{le|2018－2019年孟加拉及新西蘭板球隊比賽|Bangladeshi cricket team in New Zealand in 2018–19#3rd Test}}的第三場[[板球對抗賽|板球對抗賽]]亦因安全問題而暫停<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/sport/cricket/111314794/bangladesh-cricket-team-escape-christchurch-mosque-shooting |title=Black Caps v Bangladesh test cancelled after gunmen attack Christchurch mosques |website=Stuff.co.nz}}</ref>。{{le|孟加拉國家板球隊|Bangladesh National Cricket Team}}亦計劃到光明清真寺參觀，但進入寺內前不久即發生恐怖襲擊<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.citynews1130.com/2019/03/14/bangladesh-cricket-team-flees-mosque-shooting/ |title=Bangladesh cricket team flees mosque shooting |website=City News 1130|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.cricket.com.au/news/bangladesh-cricket-team-escape-scare-in-christchurch-new-zealand-test-series/2019-03-15 |title=Cricketers escape NZ mosque shooting |website=Cricket Australia |language=en|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。隊員其後逃離現場，並返回{{le|赫利體育場|Hagley Oval}}的更衣室<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/story/_/id/26269215/bangladesh-tour-new-zealand-called-christchurch-terror-attack |title=Bangladesh tour of New Zealand called off after Christchurch terror attack |date=2019-03-15 |website=ESPNcricinfo |language=en|access-date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。

恐襲兇手在襲擊前於社交媒體上表示計劃攻擊[[达尼丁|達尼丁]]的正道清真寺（Al Huda Mosque），因此{{le|新西蘭警方|New Zealand Police}}的{{le|武裝分子特務隊|Armed Offenders Squad}}搜查達尼丁{{le|安達臣灣|Andersons Bay}}的一間住所。警方亦封鎖清真寺附近薩默維爾街的部分路段，以及疏散住戶。[[奥塔哥大学|奥塔哥大学]]以安全隱憂為由，宣布延後原定在襲擊翌日舉行的150週年街頭匯演<ref>{{cite news |last1=Kidd |first1=Rod |last2=Miller |first2=Tim |title=Part of Dunedin street evacuated after report city was original target |url=https://www.odt.co.nz/news/dunedin/part-dunedin-street-evacuated-after-report-city-was-original-target |accessdate=2019-03-15 |publisher=''{{tsl|en|Otago Daily Times||Otago Daily Times}}'' |date=2019-03-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Mosque shootings: AOS on Dunedin street after report city was original target |url=https://www.nzherald.co.nz/nz/news/article.cfm?c_id=1&objectid=12213148 |accessdate=2019-03-15 |publisher=''[[新西兰先驱报|New Zealand Herald]]'' |date=2019-03-15}}</ref>。

据[[俄罗斯|俄罗斯]][[卫星通讯社|卫星网]]援引新西兰媒体报导，新西兰[[奥克兰市|奥克兰市]]火车站和商场附近至少发生两起爆炸，暂时没有伤亡报告<ref>[http://www.chinanews.com/wap/detail/zw/gj/2019/03-15/8781135.shtml 新西兰奥克兰市发生至少两起爆炸 伤亡不明]</ref>。后经证实，两个包裹中装有的物品为工程施工设备，与爆炸案无关<ref>{{Cite web |url = https://www.sohu.com/a/301508344_260616? |title = 奥克兰警方答澎湃：爆炸案与清真寺枪击案无关|website=搜狐网 |date = 2019-03-15 |language = zh |accessdate = 2019-03-15 }}</ref>。

事后新西兰一名18岁男子传播基督城清真寺枪击事件网络直播视频，并且发布一张照片，以[[努爾清真寺_(基督城)|努爾清真寺]]为背景，写道“目标锁定”，且网络聊天信息多有煽动极端暴力，该男子于3月15日事发当日被逮捕，法院裁定其不得[[保释|保释]]<ref>[http://www.xinhuanet.com/world/2019-03/19/c_1210085439.htm 涉嫌传播恐袭“直播”视频　新西兰男子被捕]</ref>。

槍擊案發生一周內，新西蘭迅速修改槍支法，军用[[半自动步枪|半自动步枪]]和[[突击步枪|突击步枪]]被禁止<ref>[https://m.guancha.cn/internation/2019_03_21_494471.shtml 新西兰总理：禁售所有攻击性武器，包括突击步枪和半自动步枪
2019-03-21 12:08:11]</ref>。

2019年4月4日，凶手的[[母国|母国]][[澳大利亚议会|澳大利亚议会]]通过一项政府提交的法案，认定社交媒体平台放任用户直播暴力画面构成犯罪，企业主管可判处3年监禁再加1050万[[澳元|澳元]]或相当于企业年营业额10%的罚款；社交平台明知用户直播发生在澳大利亚境内的暴力行径而没有通报[[澳大利亚联邦警察|澳大利亚联邦警察]]，将处以最高84万澳元罚款<ref>[http://www.xinhuanet.com/world/2019-04/05/c_1210100395.htm 澳大利亚立法严惩社交媒体“网络直播暴行”]</ref>。

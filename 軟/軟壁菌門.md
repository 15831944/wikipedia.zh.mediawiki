'''軟壁菌門'''（Tenericutes），又譯作'''無壁菌門'''，是[[細菌界|細菌界]]下的一個[[門_(生物學)|門]]。該門下有一個綱，即{{link-en|柔膜細菌綱|Mollicutes}}（Mollicutes）。該門命名於1984年<ref>[http://ijs.sgmjournals.org/cgi/reprint/34/3/355 ''Validation of the Publication of New Names and New Combinations ...''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090418020056/http://ijs.sgmjournals.org/cgi/reprint/34/3/355 |date=2009-04-18 }} Int. J. of Sust. Bact.; July 1984, p. 355-357</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Taxonomy/Browser/wwwtax.cgi?mode=Tree&id=544448 |title=Tenericutes |accessdate=2008-09-11 |work=Taxonomy Browser |publisher=NCBI |date= }}</ref><ref>Ludwig, W.; Schleifer, K.-H., and Whitman, W.B. (In press, release in 2009). "Revised road map to the phylum Firmicutes". ''In'' P. De Vos ''et al.'' (eds.) ''Bergey's Manual of Systematic Bacteriology'', 2nd ed., vol. 3 (The Firmicutes). Springer-Verlag, New York. {{ISBN|0-387-95041-9}} [http://www.bergeys.org/outlines/Bergeys_Vol_3_Outline.pdf ''Revised Road Map to the Phylum Firmicutes''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100921022410/http://bergeys.org/outlines/Bergeys_Vol_3_Outline.pdf |date=2010-09-21 }}</ref>。該門下的典型物種有[[支原體|支原體]]、[[螺原體|螺原體]]（Spiroplasma）、{{Link-en|脲原体|Ureaplasma}}（Ureaplasma），以及[[植原體|植原體]]（Phytoplasma）。軟壁菌門屬於[[革蘭氏陰性菌|革蘭氏陰性菌]]；其中，一部分軟壁菌門下的物種是生理狀況下{{link-en|胎盤菌群|placental microbiome}}的一部分<ref name="MorKwon2015">{{cite journal|last1=Mor|first1=Gil|last2=Kwon|first2=Ja-Young|title=Trophoblast-microbiome interaction: a new paradigm on immune regulation|journal=American Journal of Obstetrics and Gynecology|volume=213|issue=4|year=2015|pages=S131–S137|issn=0002-9378|doi=10.1016/j.ajog.2015.06.039| PMID=26428492}}</ref><ref name=Todar>{{cite web |url=http://www.textbookofbacteriology.net/e.coli.html |title=Pathogenic ''E. coli'' |accessdate=2007-11-30 |last=Todar |first=K. |work=Online Textbook of Bacteriology |publisher=University of Wisconsin–Madison Department of Bacteriology}}</ref>。軟壁菌門的細菌通常對人體無害。

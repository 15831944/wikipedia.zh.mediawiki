'''科荷馬鱷屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Coahomasuchus''）是[[堅蜥目|堅蜥目]][[鍬鱗龍科|鍬鱗龍科]]的一屬，化石發現於[[德州|德州]]的Dockum群，地質年代為[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]晚期的[[卡尼階|卡尼階]]晚期<ref>Heckert, A. B., and Lucas, S. G. (2000). Taphonomy, phylogeny, biostratigraphy, biochronology, paleobiogeography, and evolution of the Late Triassic Aetosauria (Archosauria: Crurotarsi). ''Zentralbl. Geol. Paläontol'' 11–12:1539–1587.</ref>。與其他堅蜥類相比，科荷馬鱷的體型較小，身長小於1公尺。科荷馬鱷的背部鱗甲平坦，缺乏其他堅蜥類的鱗甲長刺<ref>{{cite journal | doi = 10.1080/02724634.1999.10011122 | last1 = Heckert | first1 = A. B. | last2 = Lucas | first2 = S. G. | year = 1999 | title = A new aetosaur (Reptilia: Archosauria) from the Upper Triassic of Texas and the phylogeny of aetosaurs | url = | journal = [[Journal_of_Vertebrate_Paleontology|Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology]] | volume = 19 | issue = 1| pages = 50–68 }}</ref>。科荷馬鱷的外表類似其近親[[堅蜥|堅蜥]]。

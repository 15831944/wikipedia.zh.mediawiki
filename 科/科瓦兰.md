'''科瓦兰'''（Kovalam）是[[印度|印度]][[喀拉拉邦|喀拉拉邦]]南端的一个[[海滩|海滩]]市镇，濒临[[阿拉伯海|阿拉伯海]]，位于[[特里凡得琅|特里凡得琅]]市以南16公里<ref>.{{citeweb|url=http://www.keralatourism.org/destination/destination.php?id=29|title=Kovalam|author=|date=|work=|publisher=|accessdate=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120921161352/https://www.keralatourism.org/destination/destination.php?id=29|archive-date=2012-09-21|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

科瓦兰意为椰树林。它也被称为南方的天堂<ref name="Kovalam1">{{cite web| url =http://www.kovalam.com/html/about.htm| title =Paradise of South India - Kovalam| accessdate =2008-01-07| last =| first =| work =| publisher =kovalam.com| deadurl =yes| archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20071011041353/http://www.kovalam.com/html/about.htm| archivedate =2007-10-11}}</ref>

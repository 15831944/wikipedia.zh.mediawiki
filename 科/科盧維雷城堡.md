'''科盧維雷城堡'''（{{lang-et|Koluvere linnus}}；{{lang-de|Schloss Lohde}}）是[[愛沙尼亞|愛沙尼亞]]西部城鎮科盧維雷的一座[[城堡|城堡]]建築。這座城堡的歷史可以追溯至13世紀<ref>{{cite book | last = Hein| first = Ants| authorlink = | title = Eesti Mõisad - Herrenhäuser in Estland - Estonian Manor Houses | publisher = Tänapäev | series = | volume = | edition = | date = 2009| location = Tallinn | page = 103| language = | url = | doi = | id = | isbn = 978-9985-62-765-5 | mr = | zbl = | jfm = }}</ref>。在利窩尼亞戰爭期間，瑞典和俄羅斯軍隊曾在這座城堡的附近發生過戰鬥。1924年至2001年期間，該城堡曾被福利機構使用<ref>{{cite book | last = Hein| first = Ants| authorlink = | title = Eesti Mõisad - Herrenhäuser in Estland - Estonian Manor Houses | publisher = Tänapäev | series = | volume = | edition = | date = 2009| location = Tallinn | page = 103| language = | url = | doi = | id = | isbn = 978-9985-62-765-5 | mr = | zbl = | jfm = }}</ref>。

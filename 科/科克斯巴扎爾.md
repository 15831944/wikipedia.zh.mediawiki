'''科克斯巴扎爾'''（{{lang-bn|কক্সবাজার}}、{{lang-en|Cox's Bazar}}）是[[孟加拉國|孟加拉國]]東南部[[吉大港專區|吉大港專區]]濱海的一座城市，位於[[吉大港市|吉大港市]]南部150公里，為一座漁港，以世界上最長的天然沙灘（120公里）聞名<ref>{{Cite news|url = https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-19340259|title = Bangladesh's Cox's Bazar: A paradise being lost?|last = Ethirajan|first = Anbarasan|date = 26 December 2012|work = [[英國廣播公司世界新聞頻道|BBC World]]|access-date = 29 January 2015|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180614004354/https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-19340259#|archive-date = 2018-06-14|dead-url = no}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |date=2004 |editor-last=Panday |editor-first=V.C. |title=Environment, Security and Tourism Development in South Asia: Tourism development in South Asia |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ByQi2kN7FNgC&pg=PA100 |series=Environment, Security and Tourism Development in South Asia |volume=3 |location=New Delhi |publisher=Gyan Publishing House |pages=100–101 |isbn=978-81-8205-139-3}}</ref><ref>{{cite book |date=2003 |title={{tsl|en|The Europa World Year Book||The Europa World Year Book}} |chapter=Bangladesh: Tourism |chapterurl=https://books.google.com/books?id=XLvU9lroRuUC&pg=PA679 |publisher=Taylor & Francis  |pages=679 |isbn=978-1-85743-227-5}}</ref>。科克斯巴扎爾的別稱為''Panowa''，意為黃色的花朵。

科克斯巴扎爾得名自曾在此地擔任專員的[[英國東印度公司|英國東印度公司]]官員{{link-en|海勒姆·考克斯|Hiram Cox}}，他任內曾處理此地的[[若開人|若開人]]難民問題<ref name="Haksar2009">{{cite book|author=Nandita Haksar|title=Rogue Agent: How India's Military Intelligence Betrayed the Burmese Resistance|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=vkXJveQavMEC&pg=PT55|year=2009|publisher=Penguin Books India|isbn=978-0-14-306489-3|pages=55–56}}</ref>。1799年考克斯逝世，為紀念他對難民安置工作的貢獻，此地一座市場以他為名，稱為科克斯市場（Cox's Bazar）。印度次大陸許多英國殖民時代的地名都隨著殖民時代的結束而被更改，科克斯巴扎爾是少數仍留下的地名。

科克斯巴扎爾是孟加拉熱門的旅遊城市之一。2013年，孟加拉國在此地設立了旅遊警察，以保障觀光客的權益，並保護觀光景點的自然生態<ref>{{Cite news|url = http://www.dhakatribune.com/safety/2013/aug/31/seashores-get-tourist-police|title = Seashores to get tourist police|last = Mahmud|first = Tarek|date = 31 August 2013|work = {{tsl|en|Dhaka Tribune||Dhaka Tribune}}|access-date = 18 June 2015|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150618021612/http://www.dhakatribune.com/safety/2013/aug/31/seashores-get-tourist-police#|archive-date = 2015-06-18|dead-url = no}}</ref>。

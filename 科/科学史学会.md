'''科学史学会''' ({{lang-en|History of Science Society}})是一个[[科学史|科学史]]相关的学会。

==历史==
1924年由比利时科学家[[乔治·萨顿|乔治·萨顿]]和美国科学家[[劳伦斯·约瑟夫·亨德森|劳伦斯·约瑟夫·亨德森]]成立，总部位于[[圣母大学|圣母大学]]。<ref name="ACLS">{{cite web|title=History of Science Society|url=https://www.acls.org/societies/societies.aspx?sid=6ba64f5c-c9a2-db11-a735-000c2903e717|website=American Council of Learned Societies|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref><ref name="HSS">{{cite web|title=History of the Society|url=http://hssonline.org/about/history-of-the-society/|website=History of Science Society|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref><ref name="Smith">{{cite web|last1=Smith|first1=Charles H.|title=Henderson, Lawrence Joseph (United States 1878-1942)|url=http://people.wku.edu/charles.smith/chronob/HEND1878.htm|website=Some Biogeographers, Evolutionists and Ecologists: Chrono-Biographical Sketches|accessdate=13 February 2016}}</ref>目前学会在全世界有超过3000名会员。<ref name="HSS"/>

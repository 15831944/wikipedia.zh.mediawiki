[[科索沃|科索沃]]首次遞交[[奧斯卡金像獎|奧斯卡金像獎]]的[[奧斯卡金像獎最佳外語片獎|最佳外語片獎]]，是在2014年的第一次。<ref name="87th">{{cite web |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/oscars-kosovo-selects-three-windows-734975 |title=Oscars: Kosovo Selects 'Three Windows and a Hanging' for Foreign-Language Category |accessdate=23 September 2014 |work=Hollywood Reporter}}</ref>奧斯卡最佳外語片獎由[[美國電影藝術與科學學院|美國電影藝術與科學學院]]頒發，為[[奧斯卡獎|奧斯卡獎]]中頒發給年度美國以外最佳非英語電影的獎項<ref name="Rules">{{cite web | title = Rule Thirteen: Special Rules for the Foreign Language Film Award | work = Academy of Motion Picture Arts、Sciences | publisher = [[Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts、Sciences|Academy of Motion Picture Arts、Sciences]] | url = http://www.oscars.org/awards/academyawards/rules/86/rule13.html | accessdate = 2013-08-26}}</ref>。

'''科迪·威尔逊'''（'''Cody Rutledge Wilson'''，{{bd|1988年|1月31日|}}）是一位美国的[[密码学无政府主义|密码学无政府主义者]]<ref name="verge">{{cite web|url=https://www.theverge.com/2013/4/12/4209364/guns-want-to-be-free-what-happens-when-3d-printing-and-crypto-anarchy|first=Janus|last=Kopfstein|title=What happens when 3D printing and crypto-anarchy collide?|publisher=[[The_Verge|The Verge]]|date=April 12, 2013|accessdate=May 8, 2013}}</ref><ref name="Mboard1">{{cite web|url=http://motherboard.vice.com/blog/whisteblowers-and-the-crypto-anarchist-underground-an-interview-with-andy-greenberg|first=DJ|last=Pangburn|title=Whistleblowers and the Crypto-Anarchist Underground: An Interview with Andy Greenberg|publisher=Motherboard.tv|date=September 13, 2013|accessdate=October 30, 2013}}</ref>，{{tsl|en|Free-market anarchism|自由市场无政府主义者}}和{{tsl|en|Right to keep and bear arms in the United States|枪支权利}}活动家<ref name="blink">{{cite web|url=http://blink.li/3271/interview/all-markets-become-black|first=Daniel|last=Fallenstein|title=All markets become black|publisher=Blink|date=December 27, 2012|accessdate=January 14, 2013}}{{dead link}}</ref>。在他因性侵犯罪被捕辞职之前，他是{{tsl|en|Defense Distributed}}的创始人，这是一个开发和发布[[开源|开源]]枪支设计的[[非营利组织|非营利组织]]，該組織致力於提供他們稱作「维基武器」（wiki weapons）的槍隻設計[[電腦輔助設計|電腦輔助設計]]檔案，可用於[[3D打印|3D打印]]和数字制造上。<ref name=Reason1>{{cite news|last=Doherty|first=Brian|title=What 3-D Printing Means for Gun Rights|url=http://reason.com/archives/2012/12/12/what-3-d-printing-means-for-gun-rights|newspaper=[[理性_(雜誌)|理性]]|date=December 12, 2012|accessdate=December 15, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Brown|first=Rich|url=https://news.yahoo.com/you-don-t-bring-a-3d-printer-to-a-gun-fight----yet.html|title=You don't bring a 3D printer to a gun fight - yet|publisher=News.yahoo.com|date=September 7, 2012|accessdate=October 6, 2012}}</ref>

'''科學報告'''（{{lang-en|''Scientific Reports''，缩写为''Sci. Rep.''}}）是一本英國[[自然出版集團|自然出版集團]]（NPG）旗下的線上[[开放获取|开放获取]]綜合[[自然科學|自然]][[科學期刊|科學期刊]]。該期刊稱只要具有科學價值的[[論文|論文]]都能在該刊物上發表，而不會過於關注文章的重要性、影响力<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nature.com/srep/journal-policies/referees#criteria |title=''Criteria for publication''|work=[[Guide_to_referees|Guide to referees]]}}</ref>。

2016年，一篇發表在{{link-en|學術廚房|Scholarly Kitchen}}網站上的博文稱科學報告可能會取代同樣是开放获取期刊的[[PLOS_ONE|PLOS ONE]]，成爲世界上規模最大的學術期刊<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://scholarlykitchen.sspnet.org/2016/08/23/scientific-reports-on-track-to-become-largest-journal-in-the-world/|title=Scientific Reports On Track To Become Largest Journal In The World|last=Davis|first=Phil|date=2016-08-23|website=The Scholarly Kitchen|access-date=2016-08-24}}</ref>。2017年第一季度的報告表明，科學報告已在2016年9月正式成爲世界上最大的学术期刊<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.timeshighereducation.com/blog/mega-journals-future-stepping-stone-it-or-leap-abyss| title = Mega-journals: the future, a stepping stone to it or a leap into the abyss? | date=2016-10-13|newspaper=Times Higher Education (THE)|access-date=2016-10-16}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://scholarlykitchen.sspnet.org/2017/04/06/scientific-reports-overtakes-plos-one-as-largest-megajournal/|title=Scientific Reports Overtakes PLOS ONE As Largest Megajournal|last=By|date=2017-04-06|website=The Scholarly Kitchen|access-date=2017-04-06}}</ref>。

==檢索==
《科學報告》期刊被[[科学引文索引|SCI]]、[[化學文摘|化學文摘]]（CAS）、[[PubMed|PubMed]]/[[MEDLINE|MEDLINE]]<ref>{{cite web | last = | first = | authorlink = | coauthors = | title = Displaying Record for Publication: Scientific Reports | work = CAS Source Index (CASSI) Search Tool| publisher = American Chemical Society| date = | url = http://cassi.cas.org/publication.jsp?P=eCQtRPJo9AQyz133K_ll3zLPXfcr-WXf8KWwkyV6gFjVpHk_ltqbPfSm92QSN2f8Ms9d9yv5Zd9zeE3j9KU9vCYRbB8DMIXMMs9d9yv5Zd-xQD7_O9vMRvSeF7gkTFF3| format = | doi = | accessdate = 2016-12-25 }}</ref>等數據庫收錄。研究者可通過這些數據庫查到科學報告期刊上論文的摘要。2015年，科學報告的SCI[[影響因子|影響因子]]（IF）達到5.228。唯2016年該期刊的影響因子回落到了4.259<ref name=WoS>{{cite book |year=2015 |chapter=Scientific Reports |title=2015 [[Journal_Citation_Reports|Journal Citation Reports]] |publisher=[[Thomson_Reuters|Thomson Reuters]] |edition=Science |accessdate=|series=[[Web_of_Science|Web of Science]] |postscript=.}}</ref>。

==內容==
科學報告是一本基於[[同行評審|同行評審]]和[[版面費|版面費]]運行機制的大型線上开放获取期刊，定位與PLOS ONE期刊相似。該刊重視文章內容的質量，而不過度關注文章的影响力<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.timeshighereducation.co.uk/story.asp?sectioncode=26&storycode=414822&c=1 |title=''Nature's'' open-access offering may sound death knell for subs model |work=[[The_Times_Higher_Education|The Times Higher Education]] |date=2011-01-13 |accessdate=2011-01-23}}</ref>。該期刊的編輯團隊人數眾多，規模超過一千人<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nature.com/srep/about/editorial-board|title=Editorial Advisory Panel and Editorial Board}}</ref>。2015年，一名《科學報告》的編輯馬克·馬斯林在該期刊開始試行生命科學类論文加價快速評審服務後辭職<ref>{{cite web |url=http://news.sciencemag.org/scientific-community/2015/03/editor-quits-journal-over-pay-expedited-peer-review-offer-updated |title=Editor quits journal over pay-for-expedited peer-review offer |last1=Bohannon |first1=John |last2= |first2= |date=2015-03-27 |website= Science Insider |publisher= Science/AAAS|access-date=2015-07-23 |quote=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://links.information.nature.com/servlet/MailView?ms=NDgyOTU3OTAS1&r=MTMzNDk2Mzk5MDM5S0&j=NjQzMDQ3NjY2S0&mt=1&rt=0 |title=NPG announces fast-track service|last1=Quaderi  |first1=Nandita |last2= |first2= |date=2015-03-24 |website= Nature |publisher= NPG|access-date=2015-07-23 |quote=}}</ref>。

'''科里登'''（Corydon）是[[美国|美国]][[艾奥瓦州|艾奥瓦州]][[韦恩县_(爱阿华州)|韦恩县]]的城市，是韦恩县的县城。<ref name="GR6">{{cite web |url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |accessdate=2011-06-07 |title=Find a County |publisher=National Association of Counties |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6YQozzgAf?url=http://www.naco.org/Counties/Pages/FindACounty.aspx |archivedate=2015-05-10 }}</ref>2010年人口普查中的人口为1,585人，比2000年人口普查的1,591人少。<ref name="USCB">{{cite web|url=http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |title=Population & Housing Occupancy Status 2010 |publisher=[[United_States_Census_Bureau|United States Census Bureau]] American FactFinder |accessdate=May 8, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721034521/http://factfinder2.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?pid=DEC_10_PL_GCTPL2.ST13&prodType=table |archivedate=July 21, 2011 |df= }}</ref><ref name="IADC">{{cite web|url=http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |title=Data from the 2010 Census |publisher=State Data Center of Iowa |accessdate=May 8, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718201045/http://www.iowadatacenter.org/archive/2011/02/feb10 |archivedate=July 18, 2011 |df= }}</ref>

科里登在1851年被制定和规划，当年晚些时候被指定为县城。 该城镇由县法官塞特·安德森以他的老家乡印第安纳州科里登命名。<ref>City of Corydon Iowa {{cite web|url=http://www.cityofcorydoniowa.com/visiting/history/| title=City of Corydon Iowa/Visiting/History}}</ref>

科里登是奥运金牌得主George Saling的故乡。 Saling在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]的[[1932年夏季奥林匹克运动会|1932年夏季奥林匹克运动会]]中赢得了110米障碍，时间为14.6秒。<ref name="SROly">{{cite web|url=http://www.sports-reference.com/olympics/athletes/sa/george-saling-1.html|title= George Saling|publisher= SPORTS-REFERENCE/Olympic sports|accessdate=March 10, 2011}}</ref>

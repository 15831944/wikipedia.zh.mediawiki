「'''百年燈泡'''」（'''Centennial Light'''）是全世界最長壽的[[電燈泡|電燈泡]]，且至今仍在持續運作中。它位在[[美國|美國]][[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[利佛摩_(加利福尼亞州)|利弗莫爾市]]東大道4550號，由[[利弗莫爾-普萊森消防站|利弗莫爾-普萊森消防站]]負責維護<ref name="npr">{{cite news | title=Century Light Bulb | publisher=[[National_Public_Radio|National Public Radio]] | date=2001-06-10 | url=http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=1124201 | accessdate=2007-01-15 |language=en}}</ref>。這顆燈泡自從1901年裝設完成後就一直發亮，迄今已亮了超過110年，而且很少被關掉過。百年燈泡以其超長壽命創下了[[金氏世界紀錄|金氏世界紀錄]]<ref>{{citation | publisher = Guiness World Records | url = http://www.guinnessworldrecords.com/Search/Details/Longest-burning-light-bulb/63240.htm | title = Longest burning light bulb |language=en}}</ref>，並為《[[李普利的信不信由你|李普利的信不信由你]]》和[[奇異|通用電氣]]等媒體所報導<ref name= VIA>{{citation | url = http://www.viamagazine.com/top_stories/articles/lightbulb02.asp | title = The Little Bulb That Could… and Does | type = article | journal = VIA | accessdate = 2007-01-27 |language=en}}.</ref>，也被視為現代電燈泡工業採行「[[計劃報廢|計劃報廢]]」的證據。<ref name=tests>{{citation | url = http://www.mercurynews.com/ci_17287091?source=most_emailed&nclick_check=1 | title = Tests shine light on the secret of the Livermore light bulb|newspaper=[[Contra_Costa_Times|Contra Costa Times]]|last=Benca|first=Jeanine|date=February 6, 2011}}.</ref>

== 歷史 ==
百年燈泡是手工吹製而成的30或60[[瓦特|瓦特]]燈泡（目前亮度相当于4瓦特夜灯）<ref name=tests/><ref name="centennialfacts"/><ref name ="USAToday">{{citation | date = 2003-04-02 | url = http://www.usatoday.com/tech/webguide/hotsites/2003/2003-04-02-hotsites.htm | title = Centennial bulb | newspaper = [[USA_Today|USA Today]] | accessdate = 2007-01-27 |language=en}}</ref>，採用[[碳|碳]]質[[電燈泡#構造|燈絲]]，1890年代後期由[[謝爾比電氣公司|謝爾比電氣公司]]（Shelby Electric Company）於[[美國|美國]][[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]]的[[謝爾比_(俄亥俄州)|謝爾比]]生產製造<ref name="centennialfacts">{{cite web |url= http://www.centennialbulb.org/facts.htm | accessdate = 2007-01-20 | location = Livermore | publisher = Centennial Light | title = Facts |language=en}}</ref>；許多有相似生產背景的電燈泡，至今亦仍保存良好、可正常使用<ref>{{citation | publisher = Centennial Bulb | url = http://www.centennialbulb.org/shelby-steve.htm | title = Steve's Shelby Collection | accessdate =2007-01-20 |language=en}}</ref>。據席爾法·柏納爾·貝克表示，「百年燈泡」的歷史可追溯至1901年，由她的父親丹尼斯·柏納爾贈予利弗莫爾消防站<ref name = "centennialfacts" />。柏納爾原是利弗莫爾電力與水力公司的負責人；他在賣掉公司時把燈泡捐獻給消防站。這段故事也由當代的消防志工傳為佳話<ref name = "Trivalley Herald">{{citation | url = http://www.centennialbulb.org/newsppr3.htm | title = Light Bulb May Be World's Oldest | publisher = Centennial Bulb | accessdate = 2009-10-14 |language=en}}</ref>。

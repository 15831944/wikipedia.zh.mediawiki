'''全国停火协议'''（{{lang-my|တစ်နိုင်ငံလုံး ပစ်ခတ်တိုက်ခိုက်မှု ရပ်စဲရေးသဘောတူစာချုပ်}}，{{lang-en|Nationwide Ceasefire Agreement}}，[[缩写|缩写]]'''NCA'''）是缅甸政府与少数民族地方武装组织之间的具有里程碑意义的停火协议。草案于2015年3月31日由大多数受邀方达成协议，<ref>{{cite web|title=Myanmar's Nationwide Ceasefire Agreement - Institute for Security and Development Policy|url=http://isdp.eu/publication/myanmars-nationwide-ceasefire-agreement/|website=Institute for Security and Development Policy|accessdate=2017-10-15}}</ref>该协议于2015年10月15日由[[登盛|登盛]]总统签署。<ref>{{cite news|title=Myanmar signs ceasefire with eight armed groups|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-myanmar-politics/myanmar-signs-ceasefire-with-eight-armed-groups-idUSKCN0S82MR20151015|accessdate=2017-10-15|work=Reuters|date=2015-10-14}}</ref>来自联合国、英国、挪威、日本和美国的观察员和代表见证了签署。<ref name="blogs.cfr.org">{{cite web|url=http://blogs.cfr.org/asia/2015/10/07/myanmars-cease-fire-deal-comes-up-short/ |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151009042537/http://blogs.cfr.org/asia/2015/10/07/myanmars-cease-fire-deal-comes-up-short/ |archive-date=2015-10-09 |dead-url=yes |title=Asia Unbound » Myanmar’s Cease-Fire Deal Comes up Short |accessdate=2015-10-21  }}</ref><ref name="Ray Pagnucco and Jennifer Peters">{{cite news|title=Myanmar's National Ceasefire Agreement isn't all that national|url=https://news.vice.com/article/myanmars-national-ceasefire-agreement-to-end-68-year-civil-war-isnt-all-that-national|accessdate=2015-10-18|newspaper=Vice News|date=2015-10-15|author=Ray Pagnucco and Jennifer Peters}}</ref>政府每年在签署协议周年纪念日举行仪式。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Thiha|first1=Amara|title=On the second anniversary of the NCA, is Myanmar keeping peace on track?|url=https://frontiermyanmar.net/en/on-the-second-anniversary-of-the-nca-is-myanmar-keeping-peace-on-track|accessdate=2017-10-15|work=Frontier Myanmar|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Nationwide Ceasefire Agreement is a Forlorn Hope|url=https://www.irrawaddy.com/opinion/guest-column/nationwide-ceasefire-agreement-forlorn-hope.html|accessdate=2017-10-15|work=The Irrawaddy|date=2017-10-12}}</ref>

缅甸政府最初向15个不同民族的地方武装组织发出邀请，邀请他们参加长期和平谈判。然而，7个受邀组织在谈判中由于察觉到不公平而拒绝签署或退出。<ref>{{cite news|title=Myanmar Signs Historic Cease-Fire Deal With Eight Ethnic Armies|url=http://www.rfa.org/english/news/myanmar/deal-10152015175051.html|accessdate=2017-10-15|work=Radio Free Asia|language=en}}</ref>

[[拉祜民主联盟|拉祜民主联盟]]和[[新孟邦党|新孟邦党]]后来实现了停火<ref>{{cite news|last1=Zaw Lin|first1=Nyein|last2=Min Mang|first2=Lun|title=New Mon State Party, Lahu Democratic Union to sign ceasefire|url=https://www.mmtimes.com/news/new-mon-state-party-lahu-democratic-union-sign-ceasefire.html|accessdate=2018-03-28|work=The Myanmar Times|date=2018-01-24|language=en}}</ref>并于2018年2月13日签署了该协议。<ref>{{cite news|title=2 groups join Myanmar government's peace process|url=https://apnews.com/e668736f3c4b44cfa310addefd4da1e3|accessdate=2018-03-28|work=AP News|date=2018-02-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=New Mon State Party and Lahu Democratic Union sign NCA|url=http://www.president-office.gov.mm/en/?q=issues/peace/id-8468|publisher=Office of the President of Myanmar|accessdate=2018-03-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Analysis: A Win for Peace Commission as Mon, Lahu Groups Sign NCA|url=https://www.irrawaddy.com/news/burma/analysis-win-peace-commission-mon-lahu-groups-sign-nca.html|accessdate=2018-03-28|work=The Irrawaddy|date=2018-02-13}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=NCA signing ceremony for NMSP, LDU to take place on 13 Feb|url=http://www.mizzima.com/news-domestic/nca-signing-ceremony-nmsp-ldu-take-place-13-feb|accessdate=2018-03-28|work=Mizzima|date=2018-02-06|language=en}}</ref>

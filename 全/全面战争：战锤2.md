{{Vgname|全面战争：战锤2|en=Total War: Warhammer II|cn=全面战争：战锤II|tw=全軍破敵：戰鎚II}}是一款由[[Creative_Assembly|Creative Assembly]]开发，[[世嘉公司|世嘉公司]]于2017年年9月28日发行的电子游戏。本作自发售日起同步支持中文。<ref>{{Cite web |url = http://steamcommunity.com/games/594570/announcements/detail/1449450072445113540 |title = Total War: WARHAMMER I & II available in Chinese! |author = CA Whelan |date = 2017-08-11 |publisher = STEAM |language = en,zh |accessdate = 2017-09-30 }}</ref>本作依旧结合了[[全面战争系列|全面战争系列]]的玩法和[[Games_Workshop|Games Workshop]]公司战锤系列的[[戰鎚_(設定)|背景设定]]。是《[[全面战争系列|全面战争系列]]》第十一部作品及“全面战争：战锤”三部曲的第二作。<ref name="ign_announce" />

本作中，战场设为四块新大陆：Ulthuan、Naggaroth、The Southlands、Lustria。<ref name="pcgamer_announce">{{cite web | url=http://www.pcgamer.com/total-war-warhammer-2-announced-with-epic-three-way-battle-trailer/ | title=Total War: Warhammer 2 announced with epic three-way battle trailer | work=[[PC_Gamer|PC Gamer]] | first=Joe | last=Donnelly | date=2017-03-31 | accessdate=2017-04-01}}</ref>

<!-- 定義及醫療用途 -->
'''全血'''為標準[[捐血|捐血]]流程後所取得的人類[[血液|血液]]<ref name=Hill2007/>，通常用於治療大量[[出血|出血]]、{{le|換血療法|exchange transfusion}}與{{le|自體輸血|Autotransplantation}}上<ref name=Con2016/><ref name=Hill2007>{{cite book|last1=Hillyer|first1=Christopher D.|title=Blood Banking and Transfusion Medicine: Basic Principles & Practice|date=2007|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=0443069816|page=190|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=3QwXx_enKbcC&pg=PT211|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170112182648/https://books.google.ca/books?id=3QwXx_enKbcC&pg=PT211|archivedate=2017-01-12}}</ref>。一單位的全血可提升受血者之[[血红蛋白|血红蛋白]]濃度約10 g/L<ref name=Plu2007/>。在輸全血前會進行{{le|交叉試驗|Cross matching}}<ref name=Con2016/><ref name=Ba2016/>，並以[[靜脈注射|靜脈注射]]的方式注入受血者體內<ref>{{cite book|last1=Linton|first1=Adrianne Dill|title=Introduction to Medical-Surgical Nursing|date=2015|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9781455776412|page=287|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=mi3uBgAAQBAJ&pg=PA287|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170914035128/https://books.google.ca/books?id=mi3uBgAAQBAJ&pg=PA287|archivedate=2017-09-14}}</ref>。

<!-- 副作用及機制 -->
接受全血可能會有的副作用包括[[过敏|过敏]]（例如[[過敏性休克|過敏性休克]]）、[[溶血反應|溶血反應]]、[[高鉀血症|高鉀血症]]、[[感染|感染]]、{{le|體液過載|volume overload}}與[[輸血相關急性肺損傷|輸血相關急性肺損傷]]<ref name=Con2016/><ref name=Plu2007/>。全血的成份包含[[紅血球|紅血球]]、[[白血球|白血球]]、[[血小板|血小板]]與[[血漿|血漿]]<ref name=Plu2007/>。全血最好在採集後一天內使用，而其最長保存時間約三週<ref name=Ba2016/><ref name=Plu2007>{{cite book|last1=Plumer|first1=Ada Lawrence|title=Plumer's Principles and Practice of Intravenous Therapy|date=2007|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9780781759441|page=422|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=VtybuvktyWkC&pg=PA422|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170112180236/https://books.google.ca/books?id=VtybuvktyWkC&pg=PA422|archivedate=2017-01-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Marini|first1=John J.|last2=Wheeler|first2=Arthur P.|title=Critical Care Medicine: The Essentials|date=2012|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9781451152845|page=267|edition=4|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=pD24mJE9Rh4C&pg=PA267|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170111000331/https://books.google.ca/books?id=pD24mJE9Rh4C&pg=PA267|archivedate=2017-01-11}}</ref>。全血採集時會混入[[抗凝劑|抗凝劑]]與[[防腐劑|防腐劑]]來延長保存時間<ref>{{cite book|last1=Rudmann|first1=Sally V.|title=Textbook of Blood Banking and Transfusion Medicine|date=2005|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=072160384X|page=205|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=dXdISwJQJFIC&pg=PA205|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170112184139/https://books.google.ca/books?id=dXdISwJQJFIC&pg=PA205|archivedate=2017-01-12}}</ref>。

'''母港'''（{{lang|en|Home port}}）指的是[[船隻|船隻]]做為基地的[[港口|港口]]，就像是人類的[[家|家]]一樣。母港通常也是船隻的船籍港（{{lang|en|Port of registy}}，又稱註冊港）<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.wcoomd.org/files/1.%20Public%20files/PDFandDocuments/Conventions/naireng[1].pdf |title = INTERNATIONAL CONVENTION ON MUTUAL ADMINISTRATIVE ASSISTANCE FOR THE PREVENTION, INVESTIGATION AND REPRESSION OF CUSTOMS OFFENCES }}{{Dead link|date=2018年7月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>。但不少[[商船|商用船隻]]的母港與船籍港並不同，例如許多船隻的船籍會登記在[[巴拿馬|巴拿馬]]、[[賴比瑞亞|賴比瑞亞]]甚至是不靠海的[[蒙古|蒙古]]等國，這類船隻通常被稱為[[權宜船|權宜船]]。除了[[軍用船艦|軍用船艦]]，大多數的船隻在船尾會標示船名及船籍港<ref>{{cite web|publisher=United States Government |title=Title 46, Code of Federal Regulations, Section 67.123 |url=http://ecfr.gpoaccess.gov/cgi/t/text/text-idx?c=ecfr&sid=08b11a85ee90d38de960b99922a3e8ab&rgn=div8&view=text&node=46:2.0.1.3.23.9.1.3&idno=46 |accessdate=2007-04-17 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070930171151/http://ecfr.gpoaccess.gov/cgi/t/text/text-idx?c=ecfr&sid=08b11a85ee90d38de960b99922a3e8ab&rgn=div8&view=text&node=46%3A2.0.1.3.23.9.1.3&idno=46 |archivedate=2007-09-30 }}</ref>。

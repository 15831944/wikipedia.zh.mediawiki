'''菊苣族'''（学名：{{lang|la|Cichorieae}}）为[[菊科|菊科]][[舌状花亚科|舌状花亚科]]的一[[族_(生物)|族]]，全族约93属、1600多個可交配的物种、超過7000 {{tsl|en|apomixis|雜交}}種。广布[[東半球|東]][[北半球|北半球]][[温带|温带]]地区{{r|eflora}}<ref name=flora>{{Cite book|last1= Brouillet|first1= Luc|last2= Barkley|first2= Theodore M.|last3= Strother|first3= John L.|contribution= Cichorieae|year= 2006|title= Flora of North America|editor-last= Flora of North America Editorial Committee (from 1993)|volume= 19|pages= 214|url= http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=1&taxon_id=20701|location= New York/Oxford|publisher= Oxford University Press}}</ref> 。常见的如[[生菜|生菜]]，[[蒲公英|蒲公英]]等植物就属于该族。
Cichorieae all have milky latex and flowerheads that only contain one type of {{tsl|en|Glossary of botanical terms|}}. The genera ''<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[Gundelia|Gundelia]]'' and ''{{tsl|en|Warionia|}}'' only have <!-- [[Asteraceae|disk florets]] -->[[菊科|菊科]], while all other genera only have <!-- [[Asteraceae|ligulate florets]] -->[[菊科|菊科]]. The genera that contain most species are ''<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[Taraxacum|Taraxacum]]'' (Crepidinae subtribe) with about 1600 apomictic species, ''<!-- 页面不存在 -->[[Hieracium|Hieracium]]'' with about 770 sexually reproducing and 5200 apomictic species, and ''<!-- [[Pilosella|Pilosella]] -->[[山柳菊属|山柳菊属]]'' with 110 sexually reproducing and 700 apomictic species (both Hieraciinae).<ref name=KGL>{{cite book|last1= Kilian|first1= Norbert|last2= Gemeinhlzer|first2= Birgit|last3= Lack|first3= hans Walter|chapter= 24. Cichorieae|chapter-url= https://www.bgbm.org/sites/default/files/documents/Cichorieae_Chapter24.pdf|title= Systematics, evolution and biogeography of Compositae|editor1-last= Funk|editor1-first= V. A.|editor2-last= Susanna|editor2-first= A.|editor3-last= Stuessy|editor3-first= T. E.|editor4-last= Bayer|editor4-first= R.J.|publisher= International Association for Plant Taxonomy|location= Vienna|accessdate= 2016-11-18}}</ref> Well known members include <!-- [[lettuce|lettuce]] -->[[莴苣|莴苣]], <!-- 页面不存在 -->[[chicory|chicory]], <!-- [[dandelion|dandelion]] -->[[蒲公英|蒲公英]], and <!-- [[Tragopogon|salsify]] -->[[婆罗门参属|婆罗门参属]].

== Description ==
Most species are <!-- [[Herbaceous_plant|herbaceous]] -->[[草本植物|草本植物]], <!-- [[Perennial_plant|perennial]] -->[[多年生植物|多年生植物]], short-lived or <!-- [[annual_plant|annual plant]] -->[[一年生植物|一年生植物]]s, rarely <!-- [[subshrub|subshrub]] -->[[亚灌木|亚灌木]]s, <!-- [[shrub|shrub]] -->[[灌木|灌木]]s or <!-- [[vine|vine]] -->[[攀緣植物|攀緣植物]]s. All Cichorieae-species have {{tsl|en|laticifer|}} in both the roots, stems and leaves, and this occurs to be a unique character among the Asteraceae, although latex as such occurs rather widespread in this family. The leaves are in a {{tsl|en|Rosette (botany)|}} or <!-- [[Leaf|alternately set]] -->[[葉|葉]] along the stem, but this is the dominant situation in the Asteraceae. The only exception in the Cichorieae are the opposite lower leaves of ''{{tsl|en|Shinnersoseris|}}''. Traditionally, the Cichorieae consisted of taxa with flowerheads only containing <!-- [[Plant_reproductive_morphology|bisexual]] -->[[植物生殖型態學|植物生殖型態學]] ligulate florets (having a strap-shaped <!-- [[petal|corolla]] -->[[花瓣|花瓣]] with five teeth at its tip), a rare character that is further present only in the genera ''{{tsl|en|Catamixis|}}'', ''{{tsl|en|Glossarion|}}'', ''{{tsl|en|Hyaloseris|}}'' ({{tsl|en|Mutisieae|}}), and ''{{tsl|en|Fitchia|}}'' (<!-- [[Heliantheae|Heliantheae]] -->[[向日葵族|向日葵族]]). However, recently the genera ''Gundelia'' and ''Warionia'' have been included in the Cichorieae, and those two genera have heads containing only disk flowers.<ref name=KGL />

==分類==
In his ''Elemens de botanique ou methode pour connoître les plantes'' of 1694, {{tsl|en|Joseph Pitton de Tournefort|}} first described this group as a <!-- [[taxon|taxonomic unit]] -->[[分類單元|分類單元]], calling it the "13th class of the plant kingdom". He only assigned <!-- [[taxon|taxa]] -->[[分類單元|分類單元]] to it that are still regarded part of the Cichorieae today. {{tsl|en|Sébastien Vaillant|}} gave this group the name "Cichoracées" in 1723. Since the name predates the start of the <!-- [[Linnean_nomenclature|Linnean nomenclature]] -->[[二名法|二名法]] in 1753, it is not {{tsl|en|Validly published name|}}, but <!-- [[Jean-Baptiste_Lamarck|Jean-Baptiste Lamarck]] -->[[让-巴蒂斯特·拉马克|让-巴蒂斯特·拉马克]] and <!-- 页面不存在 -->[[Augustin_Pyramus_de_Candolle|Augustin Pyramus de Candolle]] used the name Cichorieae in the ''Synopsis Plantarium in Flora Gallica Descriptarum'', published in 1806. The name Lactuceae that was coined by <!-- [[Henri_Cassini|Henri Cassini]] -->[[亨利·卡西尼|亨利·卡西尼]] in 1819, comprises the same group of taxa and is thus a <!-- [[Synonym_(taxonomy)|synonym]] -->[[異名|異名]]. Obviously, over the centuries since the group was first identified, numerous new taxa have been described that are now included in the Cichorieae, and the group has been divided in different subgroups using various morphological character states by authors 例如：卡西尼、[[大卫·顿|大卫·顿]], {{tsl|en|Christian Friedrich Lessing|}}, A.P. De Candolle, <!-- [[George_Bentham|George Bentham]] -->[[乔治·边沁|乔治·边沁]] 及{{tsl|en|Karl August Otto Hoffmann|}}.<ref name=KGL />，

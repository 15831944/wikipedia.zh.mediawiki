'''俾路支解放军'''（{{lang-ur|بلوچستان لبریشن آرمی}}、簡寫'''BLA'''）是[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]與[[阿富汗|阿富汗]]一個[[俾路支人|俾路支人]]的武裝組織<ref>{{cite web|last=Raza|first=Irfan|title=BLA declared terrorist organisation, banned|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/187183/bla-declared-terrorist-organisation-banned|publisher=Dawn|date=10 April 2006|accessdate=10 April 2006|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180904192128/https://www.dawn.com/news/187183/bla-declared-terrorist-organisation-banned#|archive-date=2018-09-04|dead-url=no}}</ref>。2004年起，俾路支解放军武力對抗巴基斯坦政府，旨在為長年受壓迫的俾路支人爭取平權與[[民族自決|民族自決]]<ref>{{cite news|last1=Iaccino|first1=Ludovica|title=Balochistan: Baloch leader calls citizens 'most oppressed in world', urges halt to aid to Pakistan|url=http://www.ibtimes.co.uk/balochistan-baloch-leader-calls-citizens-most-oppressed-world-urges-halt-aid-pakistan-1508447|accessdate=19 December 2016|work=International Business Times UK|date=29 June 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180702093347/https://www.ibtimes.co.uk/balochistan-baloch-leader-calls-citizens-most-oppressed-world-urges-halt-aid-pakistan-1508447#|archive-date=2018-07-02|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Pakistan stands exposed. Even UN recorded its brutalities in Balochistan|url=http://www.dailyo.in/politics/pakistan-united-nations-balochistan-wgeid-human-rights-pok-modi/story/1/12653.html|accessdate=19 December 2016|work=dailyo.in|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180702122249/https://www.dailyo.in/politics/pakistan-united-nations-balochistan-wgeid-human-rights-pok-modi/story/1/12653.html#|archive-date=2018-07-02|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Writer|first1=Malik Siraj Akbar Contributing|title=The End of Pakistan's Baloch Insurgency?|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/malik-siraj-akbar/the-end-of-pakistans-balo_b_6090920.html|accessdate=19 December 2016|work=The Huffington Post|date=3 November 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171012040326/https://www.huffingtonpost.com/malik-siraj-akbar/the-end-of-pakistans-balo_b_6090920.html#|archive-date=2017-10-12|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Cassman|first1=Daniel|title=Balochistan Liberation Army {{!}} Mapping Militant Organizations|url=http://web.stanford.edu/group/mappingmilitants/cgi-bin/groups/view/297|accessdate=19 December 2016|work=web.stanford.edu|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180319065647/http://web.stanford.edu/group/mappingmilitants/cgi-bin/groups/view/297#|archive-date=2018-03-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>。俾路支解放军大多在巴基斯坦的[[俾路支省|俾路支省]]活動，常對{{tsl|en|Pakistan Armed Forces|巴基斯坦武裝部隊}}發動攻擊<ref>{{cite web|last=Butt|first=Qaiser|url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/564257/heritage-under-attack-pkmap-says-it-views-ziarat-residency-as-a-symbol-of-slavery/|title=Heritage under attack: PkMAP says it views Ziarat Residency as a ‘symbol of slavery’ – The Express Tribune|publisher=Tribune.com.pk|date=2013-06-17|accessdate=2013-06-17}}</ref>。

俾路支解放军被巴基斯坦與[[英國|英國]]視為[[恐怖組織|恐怖組織]]<ref name="Home Office">{{cite book|last1=Office|first1=Home|title=PROSCRIBED TERRORIST ORGANISATIONS|date=15 July 2016|publisher=Home Office|page=9|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/538297/20160715-Proscription-website-update.pdf|accessdate=16 July 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161213023331/https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/538297/20160715-Proscription-website-update.pdf#|archive-date=2016-12-13|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/456294/list-of-banned-organisations-in-pakistan/|title=List of banned organisations in Pakistan|publisher=Tribune.com.pk|accessdate=14 October 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.start.umd.edu/start/data_collections/tops/terrorist_organization_profile.asp?id=3961|title=Terrorist Organization Profile - START - National Consortium for the Study of Terrorism and Responses to Terrorism|publisher=Start.umd.edu |date= |accessdate=2013-06-17|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20120623023249/http://www.start.umd.edu/start/data_collections/tops/terrorist_organization_profile.asp?id=3961|archivedate=2012-06-23}}</ref>，但[[美國|美國]]、[[歐盟|歐盟]]與[[印度|印度]]尚未將其列為恐怖組織<ref>{{cite news|title=Balochistan United Army (BLA)C|url=http://www.trackingterrorism.org/group/balochistan-united-army-bla|accessdate=19 December 2016|work=trackingterrorism.org|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161220193427/http://www.trackingterrorism.org/group/balochistan-united-army-bla#|archive-date=2016-12-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

==外國支援==
[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]指控俾路支解放军為[[印度|印度]]所操控，且印度於[[阿富汗|阿富汗]][[坎達哈|坎達哈]]與[[賈拉拉巴德_(阿富汗)|賈拉拉巴德]]等地的領事館為其提供軍事、經濟資助和武裝訓練，目的為造成巴基斯坦動盪<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/84902/wikileaks-india-russia-supporting-baloch-insurgency/|title=MPs told Russia, India and UAE involved in Baloch insurgency - The Express Tribune|date=3 December 2010|publisher=|access-date=2018-11-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141222162916/http://tribune.com.pk/story/84902/wikileaks-india-russia-supporting-baloch-insurgency/#|archive-date=2014-12-22|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.outlookindia.com/magazine/story/raw-is-training-600-balochis-in-afghanistan/231032|title='RAW Is Training 600 Balochis In Afghanistan'|website=outlookindia.com|access-date=2018-11-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180913062358/https://www.outlookindia.com/magazine/story/raw-is-training-600-balochis-in-afghanistan/231032#|archive-date=2018-09-13|dead-url=no}}</ref>。有一些西方觀察家也認為印度暗中支持俾路支解放军<ref>{{cite book|title=Dictionary of Terrorism|publisher=Polity|isbn=978-0745643021|url=https://books.google.com.pk/books?id=ZgmXxoLHv8MC&pg=PA48#v=onepage&q&f=false|author=David Wright-Neville|edition=1st|accessdate=3 June 2012|pages=48–49|date=11 May 2010}}</ref>，但俾路支解放军的領導人否認其組織與印度有任何關聯<ref name="Marri">{{cite news|last1=Marri|first1=Hyrbyair|title=Will never seek help from India: Hyrbyair Marri|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/1212164|accessdate=16 July 2016|publisher=Dawn|date=2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161023201801/http://www.dawn.com/news/1212164#|archive-date=2016-10-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

一則[[維基解密|維基解密]]洩漏的電報顯示[[巴基斯坦三軍情報局|巴基斯坦三軍情報局]]認為印度、[[阿拉伯聯合大公國|阿拉伯聯合大公國]]與[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]政府均暗中援助、支持俾路支解放军<ref>{{cite news|title=MPs told Russia, India and UAE involved in Baloch insurgency|url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/84902/wikileaks-india-russia-supporting-baloch-insurgency/|date=December 3, 2010|work=The Express Tribune|accessdate=December 23, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141222162916/http://tribune.com.pk/story/84902/wikileaks-india-russia-supporting-baloch-insurgency/#|archive-date=2014-12-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

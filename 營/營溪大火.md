California fire rises to 23|accessdate=November 11, 2018|work=Newser|agency=Associated Press}}</ref><ref name="SFChronicleCampFire">{{Cite web|url=https://www.sfchronicle.com/california-wildfires/article/At-least-five-dead-as-Camp-Fire-lays-waste-to-13378522.php|title=Camp Fire is most destructive wildfire in California history: 9 dead, 6,713 structures incinerated|accessdate=November 10, 2018|author=Alexander|date=November 10, 2018|first=Kurtis|work=San Francisco Chronicle}}</ref>。这场火灾以火源「營溪路」 (Camp Creek Road) 命名，火灾发生于2018年11月8日，位于[[北加州|北加州]]的[[布特郡_(加利福尼亞州)|布特郡]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sfgate.com/california-wildfires/article/Why-is-it-called-Camp-Fire-name-Butte-County-destr-13380824.php|title=Why is it called the Camp Fire? 多個郡縣的市民被逼疏散，其中包括[[天堂_(加利福尼亚州)|天堂]]、{{Le|茅屋區|Magalia, California}}、{{Le|森特维尔區|Centerville, California}}、{{Le|康考|Concow, California}}、{{Le|跳蚤區|Pulga, California}}、{{Le|巴特溪峽谷|Butte Creek Canyon, California}}和{{Le|扬基山|Yankee Hill, California}}，并威胁到{{Le|巴特溪谷|Butte Valley, California}}、[[奇科_(加利福尼亚州)|奇科]]、{{Le|森林牧场|Forest Ranch, California}}、[[地獄_(加利福尼亞州)|地獄]]、{{Le|因斯基普|Inskip}}、{{Le|奥罗维尔|Oroville}}和{{Le|斯特灵城|Stirling City}}。<ref name="KEZI 9 News">{{Cite web|url=https://www.kezi.com/content/news/CAL-FIRE-Butte-County--500045591.html|title=Camp Fire: Latest Numbers|accessdate=November 12, 2018|author=McVicker|date=November 9, 2018|first=David|work=KEZI 9 News|publisher=ABC|language=en}}</ref><ref name="Afzal_11/9/2018">{{Cite web|url=https://www.stl.news/paradise-calif-bodies-found-in-burnt-cars-as-calif-fire-incinerates-town/207267/|title=PARADISE, Calif. - 加州消防局於11月25日宣布，營溪大火已被完全控制。失蹤人數為249人<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.eastbaytimes.com/2018/11/22/5714558/|title=Camp Fire is deadliest U.S. wildfire in 100 years; eerily similar to 1918 inferno that killed 453|date=2018-11-22|newspaper=East Bay Times|accessdate=2018-11-26|language=en-US}}</ref>，最後修訂為3人。

== 時序 ==
以下時間均為太平洋標準時間（UTC-8）

2018年11月8日日出，營溪大火發生，消防局於早上6時33分首次接到相關報告，火災位於加州跳蚤區巴特溪縣营溪路附近。營溪大火後后不久，先鋒消防隊員被派遣到[[羽毛河|羽毛河]]上{{Le|波壩|Poe Dam}}附近的[[太平洋瓦電公司|太平洋瓦電公司]]（PG&E）电力线附近，報告指該處的灌木起火。抵達10分钟后，先鋒隊观察到该地区湿度低和风大，火势迅速蔓延，且非常猛烈。

11月9日上午，美国国家气象局对北加州内陆大部分地区以及南加州发出红旗警告。PG&E表示电力线路已经中断<ref name="MercCampOrigin">{{Cite news|url=https://www.mercurynews.com/2018/11/09/pge-power-lines-may-have-sparked-deadly-butte-county-wildfire-according-to-radio-transmissions/|title=PG&E power lines may have sparked deadly Camp Fire, according to radio transmissions|date=November 9, 2018|work=[[The_Mercury_News|The Mercury News]]}}</ref>。火灾发生后不久，巴特溪县警长办公室下令天堂镇疏散<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://twitter.com/CAL_FIRE/status/1060322869652013056|title=CAL FIRE on Twitter|author=Cal Fire|date=November 7, 2018|work=Red Flag Warning - Twitter|accessdate=November 9, 2018}}</ref>，其他地点也发布了疏散令或疏散警告，并設立紧急避难所。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://sacramento.cbslocal.com/2018/11/08/camp-fire-butte-county-paradise/|title=PARADISE LOST: Cal Fire Says Camp Fire Has Wiped Out California Town|date=November 8, 2018|work=CBS Sacramento|accessdate=November 9, 2018}}</ref>

由于火灾蔓延速度过快，大部分康考镇和天堂镇的居民在大火到来之前无法撤离。火势蔓延得如此之快，以至于消防队不再试图控制火势，而是協助其他人逃離火場<ref name="Afzal_11/9/2018">{{Cite web|url=https://www.stl.news/paradise-calif-bodies-found-in-burnt-cars-as-calif-fire-incinerates-town/207267/|title=PARADISE, Calif. - Bodies found in burnt cars as Calif. fire incinerates town|accessdate=November 11, 2018|author=Afzal|date=November 9, 2018|first=Rizwan|work=STL.News}}</ref>。截至11月9日晚上8点，大火已经烧毁了8100公頃土地，威胁大约15000座建筑物。該區风速接近時速80公里，使火势得以迅速增长<ref name="IU12-08pm">{{Cite web|url=http://cdfdata.fire.ca.gov/pub/cdf/images/incidentfile2277_4157.pdf|title=Camp Fire|accessdate=November 8, 2018|date=November 8, 2018|publisher=[[California_Department_of_Forestry_and_Fire_Protection|Cal Fire]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-46146354|title=California wildfire leaves town in ruins|date=November 9, 2018|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=November 9, 2018}}</ref>。加利福尼亚林业和防火部首領麥景葛（Scott McLean）表示「天堂的社区基本上已经被燒毀了，強风把整個城鎮殲滅掉了」<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.kcra.com/article/butte-county-wildfire-grows-to-70k-acres/24838602|title=Butte County wildfire grows to 70,000 acres|date=November 9, 2018|publisher=KCRA|accessdate=November 9, 2018}}</ref>。

据消防部門报道，截至11月10日上午，大火火場面积已经扩大到4萬公顷 ，其中五分一面積已被控制。估计有6713座建筑物被大火摧毁，超过了{{Le|塔布斯火灾|Tubbs Fire}}，成为加利福尼亚州历史上最具破坏性的野火<ref name="Ravani">Ravani，Sarah,"California wildfire: 破坏性的营火扩大到7万英亩"，加拿大亚太邮报(2018年11月9日)</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://cdfdata.fire.ca.gov/pub/cdf/images/incidentfile2277_4162.pdf|title=Campfire Incident Update|accessdate=November 10, 2018|publisher=Cal Fire}}</ref>。同日又发现了十四具尸体，獲確認的死亡人數為23人。翌日又有6具尸体被发现，死亡人数上升到29人<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/storyline/western-wildfires/firefighters-battle-deadly-california-blazes-powerful-winds-return-n934996|title=Death toll climbs to 29 in California's Camp Fire, state's deadliest in 85 years|author=|first=|date=November 11, 2018|publisher=NBC News|accessdate=}}</ref>。

截至11月13日早晨，消防部門报告火場面积达5.1万公顷，百分之30的火場被控制<ref name="IU11-13am">{{Cite web|url=http://cdfdata.fire.ca.gov/pub/cdf/images/incidentfile2277_4226.pdf|title=Incident Update, November 13, 2018 7:00 am|publisher=}}</ref>。除了商业建筑和其他建筑物外，大火还摧毁了8700多处住宅，大部分发生在火灾头两天<ref name="WP_11122018">{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/nation/2018/11/12/camp-fire-death-toll-climbs-investigators-face-grim-task-finding-remains/?noredirect=on&utm_term=.8b247f33a45a|title=As Camp Fire death toll climbs, investigators face grim task of finding remains|author=Bever|first=Lindsey|date=November 12, 2018|publisher=[[The_Washington_Post|The Washington Post]]|author2=Achenbach|first2=Joel|last3=Wootson Jr.|first3=Cleve R.}}</ref>。死亡人数增至42人，超越1933年格里菲斯公园大火（1933 Griffith Park Fire）成为加利福尼亚州历史上最致命的野火，當年大火僅导致29人死亡<ref name="IU11-13am" />。到了13号晚上，死亡人数已经增加到48人<ref name="KCRA_staff_11/13/2018">{{Cite web|url=https://www.kcra.com/article/town-of-pulga-evacuated-due-to-fire-butte-county-sheriffs-office-says/24838602|title=Death toll rises to 48 in Butte County wildfire|accessdate=2018-11-14|author=KCRA Staff|date=2018-11-14|work=[[KCRA-TV|KCRA]]}}</ref>。

截至11月15日，消防部門調配来自美国各地和美国西部的資源，出動了5596名消防员、622架消防車、75架供水車、101組救火隊、103台推土机、24架直升机<ref name="inciweb">{{Cite web|url=https://inciweb.nwcg.gov/incident/6250/|title=Camp Fire|accessdate=November 13, 2018|publisher=InciWeb Incident Information System, United States Forest Service}}</ref>。

截至11月15日早上7:00，百分之40的火場已被控制，近5.7萬公頃土地被烧毁，仍然威胁着15,500座建筑物。

11月16日下午6时的報告指火場面積達146,000英亩，死亡人数从63人增加到71人<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.kcra.com/article/71-killed-more-than-1000-listed-missing-in-butte-county-wildfire/24838602|title=71 killed, more than 1,000 listed missing in Butte County wildfire|author=Staff|first=KCRA|date=2018-11-14|work=KCRA|accessdate=2018-11-17|language=en}}</ref>。

== 影响 ==
[[File:Hot_spots_and_a_large_plume_of_smoke_from_the_so-called_Camp_Fire_in_Northern....webm|缩略图]]
少数疏散路线出現交通堵塞，导致駕駛車決定放棄车辆，直接步行離開。大火吞噬了被困在车内的人以及车外的一人，造成至少四人死亡<ref name="CBS">{{Cite web|url=https://sanfrancisco.cbslocal.com/2018/11/09/camp-fire-chico-paradise-butte-evacuations-ordered/|title=At Least 9 Dead In Butte County Fire; 6,500 Homes Lost, 90,000 Acres Burned|accessdate=November 9, 2018|work=CBS San Francisco}}</ref>。许多老年人被路人和邻居疏散，至少有一名被疏散者跳进水库以躲避大火<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-46202902|title=Wildfire survivors: 'We swam to safety'|date=2018-11-15|work=BBC News|accessdate=2018-11-15|language=en-GB}}</ref>。

大火发生的第一天，康考社区和天堂镇內百分之80至90的建築物被燒毀，至少有五所公立学校、一个圣诞树农场和超过7000个其他建筑被摧毀<ref name="destroyed">{{Cite web|url=https://weather.com/news/news/2018-11-09-northern-california-wildfire-camp-fire-paradise|title=At Least 23 Dead in Wildfire That Destroyed Northern California Town and Is Now the Most Destructive Fire in California History|accessdate=November 11, 2018|author=Wright|date=November 11, 2018|first=Pam|publisher=The Weather Channel}}</ref>。11月10日，美国最后一座三跨普拉特式桁架桥—位于巴特溪的[[蜂蜜跑廊橋|蜂蜜跑廊橋]]被烧成灰烬<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sfgate.com/california-wildfires/article/honey-run-covered-bridge-destroyed-chico-fire-13379409.php|title=132-year-old Honey Run Covered Bridge, the last of its kind, destroyed by wildfire|author=Robertson|first=Michelle|date=November 10, 2018|work=[[San_Francisco_Chronicle|San Francisco Chronicle]]|accessdate=November 10, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sfchronicle.com/california-wildfires/article/Camp-Fire-destroyed-132-year-old-wooden-covered-13381315.php|title=Camp Fire destroyed 132-year-old wooden covered bridge that was monument to Gold Rush era|author=Hernández, Lauren|date=November 10, 2018|work=San Francisco Chronicle|accessdate=November 11, 2018}}</ref>。

据估计截至11月11日，已经有5.2万人撤离<ref name="McBride_Gutierrez_Asimov_11/11/2018">{{Cite web|url=https://www.sfgate.com/california-wildfires/article/Butte-County-fire-only-grows-slightly-as-13381900.php|title=More than 4,000 now fighting Camp Fire, with a wary eye on the wind|accessdate=November 12, 2018|author=McBride|date=November 11, 2018|first=Ashley|work=[[San_Francisco_Chronicle|San Francisco Chronicle]]}}</ref>。

截至11月16日黃昏，火災造成71人死亡，另有逾1000人下落不明<ref name="Sacramento Bee">{{Cite web|url=https://www.sacbee.com/latest-news/article221806595.html|title=Camp Fire death toll hits 71 on eve of Trump’s visit to California|date=November 16, 2018|publisher=}}</ref>。

大火产生的烟雾导致整个[[舊金山灣區|舊金山湾区]]和[[中央谷地|中央谷地]]出現广泛的空气污染，迫使湾区5个县和[[沙加緬度_(加利福尼亞州)|薩克拉門托]]几十个区的公立学校在11月16日关闭<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sfgate.com/california-wildfires/article/camp-fire-wildfire-smoke-sf-california-wind-13396555.php|title=When will air quality improve in the Bay Area?|author=Dowd|first=Katie|date=15 November 2018|work=SFGate|author2=Graff|first2=Amy|accessdate=16 November 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://abc30.com/health/smoke-from-several-wildfires-affecting-air-quality-in-the-valley/4697050/|title=Smoke from several wildfires affecting air quality in the Valley|date=15 November 2018|work=KFSN-TV Fresno|accessdate=16 November 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sfchronicle.com/california-wildfires/article/Smoke-hazard-forces-dozens-of-Bay-Area-schools-to-13396652.php|title=Public schools across the Bay Area will be closed Friday due to smoke hazard|author=McBride|first=Ashley|date=15 November 2018|work=San Francisco Chronicle|author2=Wu|first2=Gwendolyn|accessdate=16 November 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sacbee.com/news/local/education/article221700665.html|title=See which Sacramento-area schools, colleges are closed Friday due to air quality|author=McGough|first=Michael|date=15 November 2018|work=The Sacramento Bee|author2=Moleski|first2=Vincent|accessdate=16 November 2018}}</ref>。

指责加州的森林管理不善是發生多宗森林大火的原因。他又在推特威胁要终止联邦援助，除非加州改善對森林的管理<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.mercurynews.com/2018/11/10/trump-blames-poor-forest-management-for-california-fires-threatens-to-revoke-funding/|title=Trump blames poor forest management for California fires, threatens to revoke funding|author=Deruy|first=Emily|date=November 10, 2018|work=The Mercury News|accessdate=November 10, 2018}}</ref>。

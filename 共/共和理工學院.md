'''共和理工學院'''（{{lang-en|'''Republic Polytechnic'''}}，縮寫：RP）是[[新加坡|新加坡]]的一所高等學校，是新加坡最早在所有科目中採用[[問題導向學習|問題導向學習]]的學校。<ref>[http://sundaytimes.lk/100516/Education/ed18.html The Sunday Times: Education, Republic Polytechnic Singapore]</ref><ref>[http://www.channelnewsasia.com/stories/singaporelocalnews/view/1055852/1/.html Channel News Asia: Republic Polytechnic Sees its Fifth and Largest Batch of Graduates]</ref>

2007年7月31日，共和理工學院的[[兀蘭|兀蘭]]校區由新加坡總理[[李顯龍|李顯龍]]揭幕，<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.rp.sg/pmopening/ |title=Official Opening Ceremony of Republic Polytechnic |access-date=2013-10-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080628095740/http://www.rp.sg/pmopening/ |archive-date=2008-06-28 |dead-url=yes }}</ref> 校區建築是由日本建築師[[槙文彦|槙文彦]]設計的，<ref>[http://www.maki-and-associates.co.jp/e/project/43.html Maki and Associates: Architecture and Planning] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110503231249/http://www.maki-and-associates.co.jp/e/project/43.html |date=2011-05-03 }}</ref> 2006年完工，容量約為一萬三千名學生。<ref>[http://www.beca.com/projects/buildings/education/republic_polytechnic_singapore_2.aspx BECA: Republic Polyechnic, Teaching Environment with Added Energy Efficiency] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110707221000/http://www.beca.com/projects/buildings/education/republic_polytechnic_singapore_2.aspx |date=2011-07-07 }}</ref>

2008年，該學院獲得[[ASEAN|ASEAN]]最佳實踐節能建築獎。<ref>[http://www.news.gov.sg/public/sgpc/en/media_releases/agencies/ema/press_release/P-20100722-1/AttachmentPar/0/file/ASEAN%20Energy%20Awards.pdf Singapore Bags 5 ASEAN Energy Awards BCA.gov.sg: Singapore Bags 5 ASEAN Energy Awards] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718072345/http://www.news.gov.sg/public/sgpc/en/media_releases/agencies/ema/press_release/P-20100722-1/AttachmentPar/0/file/ASEAN%20Energy%20Awards.pdf |date=2011-07-18 }}</ref> 2009年11月，被新加坡環境理事會授予[[綠色建築|綠色建築]]稱號。<ref>[http://www.sec.org.sg/awards/ecooffice Singapore Environment Council: Project Eco-office] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110903230908/http://www.sec.org.sg/awards/ecooffice |date=2011-09-03 }}</ref><ref>[http://www.ecoaction.sg/PastEcoAction2009.aspx Past Eco Action Day] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100704174516/http://www.ecoaction.sg/PastEcoAction2009.aspx |date=2010-07-04 }}</ref> 2010年又獲得總統設計獎。<ref>[http://www.designsingapore.org/pda_public/gallery.aspx?sid=50 DesignSingapore.org: President*s Design Award 2009]</ref>

== 科系 ==

2013年，共和學院有六個學校（school）和兩個學術中心，提供37種全日制專業。<ref>[http://www.moe.gov.sg/education/post-secondary/ Ministry of Education, Singapore: Post-secondary Education]</ref><ref>[http://www.thegovmonitor.com/world_news/asia/singapore-outlines-importance-of-polytechnic-education-40222.html GovMonitor: Singapore Outlines Importance Of Polytechnic Education] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101014115241/http://www.thegovmonitor.com/world_news/asia/singapore-outlines-importance-of-polytechnic-education-40222.html |date=2010-10-14 }}</ref>

* School of Applied Science
* School of Engineering
* School of Hospitality
* School of Infocomm
* School of Sports, Health and Leisure
* School of Technology for the Arts
* Centre for Enterprise and Communication

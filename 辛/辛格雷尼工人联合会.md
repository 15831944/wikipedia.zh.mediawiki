'''辛格雷尼工人联合会'''（{{lang-te|సింగరేణి కార్మిక సమాఖ్య}}），简称'''西卡萨'''（Sikasa；{{lang-te|సికాస}}），是一个激进的煤矿工人工会。<ref name="epw"/>据称，与[[印度共产党（毛主义）|印度共产党（毛主义）]]有联系。<ref>[http://www.hindu.com/2004/11/24/stories/2004112409790400.htm Andhra Pradesh News : Five Singareni unions call strike today]. The Hindu (2004-11-24). Retrieved on 2011-08-14.</ref> 维斯瓦纳特（Vishwanath）是西卡萨的书记。<ref>[http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2003-02-09/hyderabad/27267410_1_sccl-management-singareni-workers-singareni-collieries-company-limited Rs 100 cr loss to SCCL from strike – Times Of India]. Articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com (2003-02-09). Retrieved on 2011-08-14.</ref>

== 历史 ==
西卡萨成立于1980年。1981年，它领导了56天的罢工。<ref name="pw">[http://www.bannedthought.net/India/PeoplesMarch/PM1999-2006/archives/2000/may2k/world%20bank.htm World Bank’s Dictate on Singareni]. Bannedthought.net (1997-08-04). Retrieved on 2011-08-14.</ref>侯赛因（hussain）是西卡萨的创始主席。<ref name="pv">[http://www.bannedthought.net/India/Vanguard/1985/VG-1985-06-Jun.doc Red salutes to martyrs]. bannedthought.net. DOC file. 6 June 1985</ref> 工会于1982年4月19日登记。<ref>Ram Reddy, R. ''[https://books.google.com/books?id=Rh4TOzv2toMC&pg=PA134 Industrial Relations in India: A Study of the Singareni Collieries]''. New Delhi, India: Mittal Publications, 1990. p. 134</ref>

西卡萨于1982年6月10日至11日在Godavarikhani举行了第一次会议。900名代表和330名兄弟代表出席。会议召开之前，警方进行了镇压，会议组织者被禁止举行任何公开游行。<ref name="pv"/>

据称，西卡萨与[[印度共产党（马列）人民战争|印度共产党（马列）人民战争]]有关，作为该党的合法和地下组织发挥作用，与该党的武装部队(dalams)有联系。<ref name="epw">''[https://books.google.com/books?id=WOqwAAAAIAAJ Economic and Political Weekly]'', Volume 31. Bombay: Sameeksha Trust, 1996. p. 515</ref> 西卡萨提出了煤田劳动者关切的几个问题，如煤矿安全、改善工作条件和工资、广泛的酗酒、暴力行为和承包商对矿工的剥削。<ref name="id">[http://www.idsa.in/event/MaoistMassOrganisationsandMassMovement_pvramana Maoist Mass Organisations and Mass Movement | Institute for Defence Studies and Analyses]. Idsa.in (2010-04-23). Retrieved on 2011-08-14.</ref>

从1988年到1992年，西卡萨在煤田领导了大量的罢工，使辛格雷尼煤矿有限公司（Singareni Collieries Company Ltd，SCCL）的运作陷入瘫痪。<ref>Siddiqui, Moid, and R. H. Khwaja. ''[https://books.google.com/books?id=-y8sYCVsvWgC&pg=PA230 The Acrobatics of Change: Concepts, Techniques, Strategies and Execution]''. New Delhi: Response Books, 2008. p. 200</ref> 随着SCCL接近破产，西卡萨的影响力逐渐减弱，因为在该公司被推迟重组期间，警方的镇压行动得到加强。西卡萨在1992年被禁。<ref name="pw"/>

2000年，两名西卡萨领导人在[[阿迪拉巴德县|阿迪拉巴德县]]与警察的枪战中丧生。<ref>[http://www.hinduonnet.com/thehindu/2000/04/18/stories/04182012.htm Two Sikasa leaders killed] hinduonnet.com (2000-04-18). Retrieved on 2011-08-02.</ref>

西卡萨于2004年复活。<ref>[http://www.hindu.com/2004/10/21/stories/2004102108310400.htm Andhra Pradesh News : Naxalites on mass recruitment drive]. ''The Hindu'' (2004-10-21). Retrieved on 2011-08-14.</ref>安得拉邦政府于2006年4月决定延长对西卡萨的禁令。<ref>[http://www.unhcr.org/refworld/pdfid/4b6fe22d0.pdf Title: India  Tamil Nadu  RYL  Naxalites  PWG  CPI-ML  Tamizhga Rahul Gandhi Peravai]. (PDF). Retrieved on 2011-08-14.</ref>

'''天主教哈爾科夫-扎波羅熱教區'''（{{lang-la|Dioecesis Kharkiviensis-Zaporizhiensis}}、{{lang-uk|Харківсько-Запорізька дієцезія}}、{{lang-ru|Диоцез Харькова-Запорожья}}）是[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]在[[烏克蘭|烏克蘭]]的一個[[教區|教區]]，屬[[天主教利沃夫總教區|利沃夫總教區]]。成立於2002年5月4日。<ref>[http://www.vatican.va/holy_father/john_paul_ii/apost_constitutions/documents/hf_jp-ii_apc_20020504_kharkiviensis-zaporizhiensis_lt.html 成立教區的宗座憲令]</ref>

教區範圍包括烏克蘭東部、以俄語居民為主的廣大地區，教座位於[[哈爾科夫|哈爾科夫]]。2006年有教友61,200人，佔轄區總人口0.3%。教區下轄七十個堂區，有十二名司鐸。教區現時[[教座出缺|教座出缺]]，由{{link-en|達尼老·希羅科拉迪烏克|Stanislav Shyrokoradiuk}}擔任宗座署理；<ref>{{cite web |title=Rinunce e nomine, 02.02.2019 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2019/02/02/0089/00188.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2019-03-15 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190315163713/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2019/02/02/0089/00188.html |archivedate=2019-03-15 |language=it |date=2019-02-02}}</ref>輔理和榮休主教分別為{{link-en|若望·索比沃|Jan Sobilo}}和{{link-en|瑪利亞諾·布切克|Marian Buczek}}<ref>{{cite web |title=RINUNCE E NOMINE, 30.10.2010 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2010/10/30/0661/01501.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2019-03-15 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190315174459/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2010/10/30/0661/01501.html |archivedate=2019-03-15 |language=it |date=2010-10-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Rinunce e nomine, 12.04.2014 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2014/04/12/0266/00584.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2019-03-15 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190315175544/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2014/04/12/0266/00584.html |archivedate=2019-03-15 |language=it |date=2014-04-12}}</ref>。

'''天蛇'''（[[學名|學名]]''Yurlunggur camfieldensis''）是[[澳洲|澳洲]]一種已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[巨蛇科|巨蛇科]]。<ref name="Scanlon, 1992"/><ref name=paleodb>[http://paleodb.org/cgi-bin/bridge.pl?action=checkTaxonInfo&taxon_no=64892 Paleobiology Database]
</ref>

天蛇是一種大型的[[頂級掠食者|頂級掠食者]]，自[[中新世|中新世]]已開始出沒。它們被分類到巨蛇科之中。<ref name="Fauna of Australia 26">{{cite book|last=Hutchinson|first=Mark N.|coauthor=Stephen C. Donnellan|title=|editor=C.G.Glasby G.J.B.Ross P.L.Beesley|publisher=Australian Government Publishing Service|date=1993|edition=Online|series=Fauna of Australia|volume=2A|pages=15|chapter=26. Biogeography and Phylogeny of the Squamata|isbn=978 0 644 32429 8|url=http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/abrs/publications/fauna-of-australia/pubs/volume2a/26-fauna-2a-squamata-biogeography.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110320222511/http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/abrs/publications/fauna-of-australia/pubs/volume2a/26-fauna-2a-squamata-biogeography.pdf|archivedate=2011-03-20}}</ref>

天蛇的[[屬|屬]]名是來自[[阿納姆地|阿納姆地]]（Arnhem Land）的[[彩虹蛇|彩虹蛇]]；[[種小名|種小名]]則是以發現它們化石的地層來命名。<ref name=paleodb/>天蛇可以長達6米及厚30厘米，比較像[[巨蜥|巨蜥]]。[[約翰·斯坎倫|約翰·斯坎倫]]（John Scanlon）認為天蛇就是巨蜥[[演化|演化]]為[[蛇|蛇]]的證據。天蛇的[[化石|化石]]包括了很少有的完整[[頭顱骨|頭顱骨]]及顎骨，且都是在[[昆士蘭|昆士蘭]]西北部[[里弗斯利|里弗斯利]]（Riversleigh）發現。<ref name="ABC">{{cite news|url=http://www.abc.net.au/science/news/stories/s1567127.htm|title=Huge skulls clues to snake evolution|last=Salleh|first=Anna|date=2006-02-16|work=ABC Science Online|publisher=Australian Broadcasting Corporation|accessdate=2009-01-31}}</ref>

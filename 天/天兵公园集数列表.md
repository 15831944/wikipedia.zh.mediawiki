《'''[[天兵公園|天兵公園]]'''》（英文：'''-{Regular Show}-'''）是一部由{{le|詹姆斯·加兰·昆塔|J. G. Quintel}}创造并在[[卡通频道|卡通频道]]播映的美国动画喜剧。自从这部动画在2010年9月6日首次亮相后，已播送超过230集的系列。故事围绕着一个名字叫鸟哥{{#tag:ref|人物名字为台湾卡通频道配音时所叫的昵称，与正常译名不同。|group="注"}}的[[冠蓝鸦|冠蓝鸦]]和一个叫阿天的[[浣熊|浣熊]]两个朋友之间的日常生活，他们年龄设定大约23岁，并受雇于一座民营公园的管理员。《天兵公园》主要描述他们日常工作的例行公事并经常企图在工作期间偷懒，而通常结果都是发生了一些超现实的遭遇。其他的主要角色包括在公园的同事洛畸，尚青，看到鬼，公园经理棒棒伯，还有他们的老板班森。

在昆塔制作一部短剧用来提供给频道的展示项目「{{le|卡通学院|The Cartoonstitute}}」之前，他曾经担任卡通频道动画《[[瘋狂夏令營|瘋狂夏令營]]》和《{{le|阿嗨大冒险|The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack}}》的作者和幕僚长<ref>{{cite news |url=http://blogs.mcall.com/tvwatchers/2013/02/cartoon-network-announces-new-season-for-show-with-local-ties.html |title=Cartoon Network announces new season for show with local ties |work=The Morning Call |date=2013-02-06 |accessdate=2013-05-21 |author=Lauer-Williams, Kathy |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6GmbNZ1QU?url=http://blogs.mcall.com/tvwatchers/2013/02/cartoon-network-announces-new-season-for-show-with-local-ties.html |archivedate=2013-05-21 |deadurl=no }}</ref><ref name="Comics Beat">{{cite web |url=http://www.comicsbeat.com/2012/05/16/interview-regular-show-creator-jg-quintel-on-indie-comics-and-cartoons/ |title=Interview: Regular Show creator JG Quintel on indie comics and cartoons |publisher=Comics Beat |date=2012-05-16 |accessdate=2013-05-21 |author=O'Leary, Shannon |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120519000938/http://www.comicsbeat.com/2012/05/16/interview-regular-show-creator-jg-quintel-on-indie-comics-and-cartoons/ |archivedate=2012-05-19 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。昆塔为项目制作了《天兵公园》的试播节目，其中的角色使用了2005年的《来自棒棒糖岛的天真男人》（The Naïve Man from Lolliland）和2006年的《上午两点与下午两点》（2 in the AM PM），两者均是他在[[加州藝術學院|加州藝術學院]]时的学生作品<ref name="Animation 1">{{cite journal |url=http://www.animationmagazine.net/people/the-sublime-madness-of-j-g-quintel/ |title=The Sublime Madness of J. G. Quintel |journal=Animation |date=April 17, 2012 |accessdate=2013-05-21 |author=Zahed, Ramin |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6Gmc376rW?url=http://www.animationmagazine.net/people/the-sublime-madness-of-j-g-quintel/ |archivedate=2013-05-21 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。虽然「卡通学院」项目最终取消，但卡通频道的执行委员会批准《天兵公园》系列的继续制作<ref>{{cite web |url=http://news.turner.com/article_display.cfm?article_id=4597 |title=Cartoon Network Announces Comedy Animation Greenlights |publisher=[[Turner_Broadcasting_System|Turner]] |date=2009-08-13 |accessdate=2013-05-21 |author=Lach, Dawn |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090818231649/http://news.turner.com/article_display.cfm?article_id=4597 |archivedate=2009-08-18}}</ref>。这部动画的头两季造就了高的收视率，{{le|尼尔森媒体研究|Nielsen Media Research}}以首要的人口统计来计算，这部动画在同时段的收视率位列第一<ref name="Animation 2">{{cite journal |url=http://www.animationmagazine.net/tv/new-season-of-regular-show-arrives-monday/ |title=New Season of 'Regular Show' Arrives Monday |journal=Animation |date=2011-09-16 |accessdate=2013-05-21 |author=Zahed, Ramin |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6GmdFh5gX?url=http://www.animationmagazine.net/tv/new-season-of-regular-show-arrives-monday/ |archivedate=2013-05-21 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。而在2013年5月，这档节目平均每个星期大约有2至2.5百万的观众收看。

《天兵公园》在评论家中获得正面的评价，他们提到这部动画的特别之处是其影响力渗透于各个年龄层<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/by-topic/industry-news/comics/article/51954-how-cartoon-network-became-a-haven-for-some-of-the-best-independent-comic-book-creators-working-today.html |title=How Cartoon Network Became a Haven for Some of the Best Independent Comic Book Creators Working Today |journal=[[Publishers_Weekly|Publishers Weekly]] |date=2012-05-15 |accessdate=2013-05-21 |author=O'Leary, Shannon |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6Gmh3MlDw?url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/by-topic/industry-news/comics/article/51954-how-cartoon-network-became-a-haven-for-some-of-the-best-independent-comic-book-creators-working-today.html |archivedate=2013-05-21 |deadurl=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.awn.com/blogs/10till6/new-regular-show-party-pack-dvd-anything-regular |title=New Regular Show: Party Pack DVD is Anything But Regular |publisher=Animation World Network |date=2013-03-15 |accessdate=2013-05-22 |author=Sarto, Dan |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6GnkdEOO2?url=http://www.awn.com/blogs/10till6/new-regular-show-party-pack-dvd-anything-regular |archivedate=2013-05-22 |deadurl=no }}</ref>，简化的动画风格<ref name="Comics Beat"/>，还有经常见到有借鉴于1980年代流行文化的片段<ref name="MTV">{{cite web |url=http://geek-news.mtv.com/2012/04/03/interview-regular-show-jg-quintel/ |title=Interview: 'The Regular Show' Creator J.G. Quintel |publisher=[[MTV|MTV]] |date=2012-04-03 |accessdate=2013-05-22 |author=Webb, Charles |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/6GnbnnZ7l?url=http://geek-news.mtv.com/2012/04/03/interview-regular-show-jg-quintel/ |archivedate=2013-05-22 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。这部动画获得四项[[黄金时段艾美奖|黄金时段艾美奖]]提名，其中第三季的其中一集《蛋卷王》获得了{{le|黃金時段艾美獎最佳動畫短片|Primetime Emmy Award for Short-Format Animation}}殊荣<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.emmys.com/shows/regular-show |title=Regular Show |publisher=Academy of Television Arts & Sciences |accessdate=2013-05-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.emmys.com/shows/regular-show-christmas-special |title=Regular Show: The Christmas Special |publisher=Academy of Television Arts & Sciences |accessdate=2013-07-19}}</ref>。这部系列还获得了两项[[安妮奖|安妮奖]]的提名<ref>{{cite web |url=http://annieawards.org/38th-annie-awards |title=38th Annual Annie Nominations |publisher=ASIFA-Hollywood |accessdate=2013-05-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://annieawards.org/39th-annie-awards |title=39th Annual Annie Nominations |publisher=ASIFA-Hollywood |accessdate=2013-05-22}}</ref>，还有三项{{le|英国学院孩童奖|BAFTA Children's Awards}}提名<ref>{{cite web |url=http://awards.bafta.org/award/2011/childrens/international |title=2011 Children's International |publisher=[[British_Academy_of_Film_and_Television_Arts|British Academy of Film and Television Arts]] |accessdate=2013-05-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://awards.bafta.org/award/2011/childrens/bafta-kids-vote-powered-by-yahoo-top-10s-television |title=2011 Children's BAFTA Kids Vote Powered By Yahoo! |publisher=[[British_Academy_of_Film_and_Television_Arts|British Academy of Film and Television Arts]] |accessdate=2013-05-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://awards.bafta.org/award/2013/childrens/international |title=2013 Children's International |publisher=[[British_Academy_of_Film_and_Television_Arts|British Academy of Film and Television Arts]] |accessdate=2013-11-17}}</ref>。《天兵公园》的第八季在2015年7月由卡通频道宣布这将会是整部动画的最后一季<ref name="The Hollywood Reporter">{{cite news |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/adventure-time-regular-show-3-807037 |title='Adventure Time,' 'Regular Show,' 3 More Renewed at Cartoon Network |work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |date=2015-07-07 |accessdate=2015-07-07}}</ref><ref name="Barsanti">{{Citation|mode=cs1|last=Barsanti|first=Sam|date=2016-09-13|url=http://www.avclub.com/article/regular-show-blast-itself-orbit-final-season-242500|title=''Regular Show'' to blast itself into orbit for final season|work=The A.V.

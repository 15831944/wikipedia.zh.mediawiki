'''天谷大辅'''（{{lang-ja|天谷 大輔}}，{{bd|1977年|4月29日||}}），日本[[独立游戏|独立游戏]]开发者，亦以发布作品时所用名义'''像素工作室'''（{{lang-en|Pixel Studio}}）而闻名。

== 游戏 ==
《[[洞窟物语|洞窟物语]]》是天谷大辅最著名的作品。在历时5年的独力开发后，他在2004年发布了这款基于[[PC|PC]]的[[免费软件|免费]][[平台游戏|平台游戏]]。《[[洞窟物语|洞窟物语]]》自发布伊始便广受评论家好评<ref>{{cite web | author = Sharkey, Scott | date = 2005-02-02 | url = http://www.1up.com/features/freeloader-doukutsu-monogatari | title = Freeloader: Doukutsu Monogatari | publisher = [[1UP.com|1UP.com]] | accessdate = 2009-05-21 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20121104194433/http://www.1up.com/features/freeloader-doukutsu-monogatari | archivedate = 2012-11-04 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.insidemacgames.com/news/story.php?ArticleID=12206 | title = Doukutsu Monogatari Freeware Available | publisher = [[Inside_Mac_Games|Inside Mac Games]] | accessdate = 2009-05-21 | date = 2005-10-12 | first = Eddie | last = Park}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal | title = The Rise and Fall (and Rise?) of Japan | magazine = [[Electronic_Gaming_Monthly|Electronic Gaming Monthly]] | pages = 63 | accessdate = 2009-05-21 | date = September 2008}}</ref>，并在2006年7月跻身瑞典电玩杂志《{{Tsl|en|Super PLAY}}》的“史上最佳50款免费游戏”排行榜榜首<ref>{{Cite journal | title = GRATIS TOPP 50 | magazine = [[Super_PLAY|Super PLAY]] | pages = 118–121 | accessdate = 2009-05-21 |date = July 2006}}</ref>。后来他还参与了[[Nicalis|Nicalis]]制作的《洞窟物语》Wii移植版《洞窟物语+》的开发，并负责重新进行人物设计<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.nicalis.com/games.php?game=cavestory | title = CAVE STORY, Games - NICALIS | accessdate = 2013-01-19 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20130114065256/http://www.nicalis.com/games.php?game=cavestory | archivedate = 2013-01-14 }}</ref>。

除《[[洞窟物语|洞窟物语]]》外，天谷大辅还制作了许多作品，如发布于2000年的《{{Tsl|en|Ikachan|乌贼君}}》。近来他还针对iOS平台开发了Rockfish<ref>{{cite web| url = http://indiegames.com/2011/12/interview_cave_story_dev_ayama.html | title = Cave Story Dev Daisuke Amaya on Rockfish and iOS Development | work = IndieGames | author = John Polson | date = 2011-12-02 | accessdate = 2013-01-19}}</ref>。

2011年间，游戏开发者大会邀请他到会演讲，而在演讲中他以《The Story of Cave Story》为题展开，从分析《洞窟物语》入手，阐述自己制作独立游戏的心得，并总结了做好游戏的要素<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.gamasutra.com/view/news/123781/GDC_2011_Daisuke_Amaya_Draws_Lessons_From_Cave_Storys_Development.php | author = Kyle Orland | date = 2011-03-04 | title = GDC 2011: Daisuke Amaya Draws Lessons From Cave Story's Development | work = Gamasutra | accessdate = 2013-01-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url = http://igdshare.org/content/story_of_cavestory | title =  《洞窟物語》的開發物語 | author = KWL | date = 2012-04-06 | publisher = IGDSHARE | accessdate = 2013-01-19}}</ref>。

== 音频软件 ==
天谷大辅也制作了许多音频处理与音乐创作软件，并以{{Tsl|en|piano roll|钢琴纸卷}}式界面作为作曲软件的编辑界面，如在制作洞窟物语时他就使用自制的Org Maker来制作轻巧的.org格式的音乐<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.cavestory.org/downloads_musictools.php | title = Cave Story (Doukutsu Monogatari), A Tribute Site | accessdate = 2013-01-15 }}</ref>。他亦开发了Org Maker的后继版本{{Tsl|en|PxTone}}，并将之设计为一款免费的音频编辑套装。借助这一软件，用户可以自制音频采样，亦可创作音乐<ref>{{cite web | url = http://pistonsource.iiichan.net/misc/pxhistory.html | title = SHORT HISTORY OF PISTON COLLAGE/PXTONE | accessdate = 2013-01-15 | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20120323125849/http://pistonsource.iiichan.net/misc/pxhistory.html | archivedate = 2012-03-23 }}</ref>。

在[[天主教會|天主教]]中，'''主教'''是一个[[聖秩聖事|聖秩聖事]]{{tsl|en|Minister (Catholic Church)|天主教牧师|牧师}}，可全权掌管{{tsl|en|holy orders (Catholic Church)|圣秩}}[[聖禮|聖禮]]，也负责传授教律<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vatican.va/archive/ccc_css/archive/catechism/p123a9p4.htm#888 |title=Catechism of the Catholic Church – Christ's Faithful – Hierarchy, Laity, Consecrated Life |publisher=Vatican.va |date=1946-02-20 |accessdate=2014-04-01}}</ref>、依照自己的准则来管理天主教信众<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vatican.va/archive/ccc_css/archive/catechism/p123a9p4.htm#894 |title=Catechism of the Catholic Church – Christ's Faithful – Hierarchy, Laity, Consecrated Life |publisher=Vatican.va |date=1946-02-20 |accessdate=2014-04-01}}</ref>、圣化世界<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vatican.va/archive/ccc_css/archive/catechism/p123a9p4.htm#893 |title=Catechism of the Catholic Church – Christ's Faithful – Hierarchy, Laity, Consecrated Life |publisher=Vatican.va |date=1946-02-20 |accessdate=2014-04-01}}</ref>并代表整个教会。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.vatican.va/archive/ccc_css/archive/catechism/p2s2c3a6.htm#1553 |title=Catechism of the Catholic Church – The sacrament of Holy Orders |publisher=Vatican.va |date= |accessdate=2014-04-01 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140424192537/http://www.vatican.va/archive/ccc_css/archive/catechism/p2s2c3a6.htm#1553 |archivedate=2014-04-24 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vatican.va/archive/ccc_css/archive/catechism/p123a9p3.htm#832 |title=CCC – PART 1 SECTION 2 CHAPTER 3 ARTICLE 9 PARAGRAPH 3 |publisher=Vatican.va |date=1975-12-14 |accessdate=2014-04-01}}</ref>天主教认为[[使徒_(基督教)|使徒]]在[[五旬節|五旬節]]间被[[聖靈|聖靈]]赋予了特别的[[神恩|神恩]]，是天主教的[[主教|主教]]之由来。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.scborromeo.org/ccc/p2s2c3a6.htm#III|title=Catechism 1556|publisher=|accessdate=2014-10-06}}</ref>天主教相信，这份特殊的神恩可在圣秩圣礼期间，由[[使徒統緒|不间断的主教传承]]传递下去。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.scborromeo.org/ccc/p2s2c3a6.htm#III|title=Catechism 1555–1556|publisher=|accessdate=2014-10-06}}</ref>

主教一律由男性担任。<ref>{{cite web|title=APOSTOLIC LETTER  ORDINATIO SACERDOTALIS OF JOHN PAUL II TO THE BISHOPS  OF THE CATHOLIC CHURCH  ON RESERVING PRIESTLY ORDINATION  TO MEN ALONE|url=http://www.vatican.va/holy_father/john_paul_ii/apost_letters/1994/documents/hf_jp-ii_apl_19940522_ordinatio-sacerdotalis_en.html|publisher=Libreria Editrice Vaticana|accessdate=2014-05-30}}</ref>此外，[[教会法|教会法]]378 § 1要求[[拉丁禮教會|拉丁禮教會]]主教应当：
#拥有坚定不渝的信仰、良好的平行、虔诚、对灵魂的热忱、智慧、谨慎、人类的勇气等其他的品格；
#声望良好；
#三十五岁及以上；
# 已担当[[司铎|司铎]]五年及以上；
# 至少是[[宗教經典|宗教經典]]、[[神學|神學]]、[[教会法|教会法]]的[[博士|博士]]或者至少是[[执业学位|执业学位]]（由Apostolic See批准的学府颁发相应学术证明），亦或是同等领域的专家。

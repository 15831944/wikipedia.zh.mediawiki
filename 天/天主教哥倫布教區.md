'''天主教哥倫布教區'''（{{lang-la|Dioecesis Columbensis}}、{{lang-en|Roman Catholic Diocese of Columbus}}）是[[美國|美國]]一個[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]][[教區|教區]]，屬[[天主教辛辛那提總教區|辛辛那提總教區]]。成立於1868年3月3日。<ref>[http://www.colsdioc.org/AboutUs/DioceseHistory.aspx Diocese History]</ref>

範圍包括[[俄亥俄州|俄亥俄州]]中南部，教座位於州府[[哥倫布_(俄亥俄州)|哥倫布]]。2006年有教友252,103人（佔轄區總人口10.3%）、106個堂區、216名司鐸。現任主教為{{link-en|羅勃·J·布倫南|Robert J. Brennan}}<ref name="Vatican20190131">{{cite web |title=Resignations and Appointments, 31.01.2019 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2019/01/31/190131b0.html |publisher=Holy See Press Office |accessdate=2019-03-09 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190309134908/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2019/01/31/190131b0.html |archivedate=2019-03-09 |language=en |date=2019-01-31}}</ref>，榮休主教為{{link-en|雅各伯·安多尼·格里芬|James Anthony Griffin}}和{{link-en|費德廉·F·坎貝爾|Frederick F. Campbell}}<ref>{{cite web |title=RINUNCE E NOMINE, 14.10.2004 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2004/10/14/0501/01303.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2019-03-09 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190309135500/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2004/10/14/0501/01303.html |archivedate=2019-03-09 |language=it |date=2004-10-14}}</ref><ref name="Vatican20190131" />。

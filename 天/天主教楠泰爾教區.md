'''天主教楠泰爾教區'''（{{lang-la|Dioecesis Nemptodurensis}}、{{lang-fr|Diocèse de Nanterre}}）是[[法國|法國]]一個[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]][[教區|教區]]，屬[[天主教巴黎總教區|巴黎總教區]]。

教區成立於1966年10月9日，<ref>[http://www.vatican.va/holy_father/paul_vi/apost_constitutions/documents/hf_p-vi_apc_19661009_neptodurensis_lt.html 成長立教區的宗座憲令]</ref>位於[[上塞納省|上塞納省]]，2004年有教友857,000人（佔轄區總人口60.0%）、八十個堂區、263名司鐸。現任教區主教為{{link-de|瑪竇·魯熱|Matthieu Rougé}}<ref>{{cite press release |title=Resignations and Appointments, 05.06.2018 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2018/06/05/180605a.html |publisher=Holy See Press Office |accessdate=2018-12-13 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181213163503/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2018/06/05/180605a.html |archivedate=2018-12-13 |language=en |date=2018-06-05}}</ref>，榮休主教為{{link-fr|吉拉·多庫爾特|Gérard Daucourt}}和{{link-fr|方濟各·法夫雷奧|François Favreau}}<ref>{{cite press release |title=RINUNCE E NOMINE, 14.11.2013 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2013/11/14/0746/01684.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2018-12-13 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181213164733/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2013/11/14/0746/01684.html |archivedate=2018-12-13 |language=it |date=2013-11-14}}</ref><ref>{{cite press release |title=RINUNCE E NOMINE, 18.06.2002 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2002/06/18/0318/01005.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2018-12-13 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181213164928/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2002/06/18/0318/01005.html |archivedate=2018-12-13 |language=it |date=2002-06-18}}</ref>。

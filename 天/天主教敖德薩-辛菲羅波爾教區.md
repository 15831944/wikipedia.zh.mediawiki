'''天主教敖德薩-辛菲羅波爾教區'''（{{lang-la|Dioecesis Odesensis-Sympheropolitana}}、{{lang-uk|Одесько-Cімферопольська дієцезія}}、{{lang-ru|Диоцез Одессы-Симферополя}}）是[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]在[[烏克蘭|烏克蘭]]的一個[[教區|教區]]，屬[[天主教利沃夫總教區|利沃夫總教區]]。成立於2002年5月4日。<ref>[http://www.vatican.va/holy_father/john_paul_ii/apost_constitutions/documents/hf_jp-ii_apc_20020504_odesensis-sympheropolitana_lt.html 成立教區的宗座憲令]</ref>

教區範圍包括[[克里米亞|克里米亞]]、[[敖德薩州|敖德薩州]]、[[赫爾松州|赫爾松州]]、[[尼古拉耶夫州|尼古拉耶夫州]]、[[基洛夫格勒州|基洛夫格勒州]]和[[第聶伯羅彼得羅夫斯克州|第聶伯羅彼得羅夫斯克州]]西部，教座位於[[敖德薩|敖德薩]]。2009年有教友16,360人，佔轄區總人口0.2%。教區下轄七十個堂區，有十二名司鐸。現任主教為{{link-en|博斯勞·貝爾納基|Bronislaw Bernacki}}<ref>{{cite web |title=RINUNCE E NOMINE, 04.05.2002 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2002/05/04/0236/00728.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2019-03-15 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190315172226/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2002/05/04/0236/00728.html |archivedate=2019-03-15 |language=it |date=2002-05-04}}</ref>、助理主教和輔理主教分別為{{link-en|達尼老·希羅科拉迪烏克|Stanislav Shyrokoradiuk}}和{{link-en|雅欽多·佩爾|Jacek Pyl}}<ref>{{cite web |title=Rinunce e nomine, 02.02.2019 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2019/02/02/0089/00188.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2019-03-15 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190315163713/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2019/02/02/0089/00188.html |archivedate=2019-03-15 |language=it |date=2019-02-02}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=RINUNCE E NOMINE, 23.11.2012 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2012/11/23/0680/01559.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2019-03-15 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190315171646/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2012/11/23/0680/01559.html |archivedate=2019-03-15 |language=it |date=2012-11-23}}</ref>。

'''天主教哈科特港教區''' （{{lang-la|Diocesis Portus Harcurtensis}}）是[[尼日利亞|尼日利亞]]一個[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]][[教區|教區]]，屬[[天主教卡拉巴爾總教區|卡拉巴爾總教區]]。

1961年5月16日升為教區。<ref>{{cite journal|journal=[[宗座公報|宗座公報]]|publisher=梵蒂岡出版社|date=1962年|volume=54|title= ''Quod Apostolis''|page= 260|url=http://www.vatican.va/archive/aas/documents/AAS%2054%20%5B1962%5D%20-%20ocr.pdf}}</ref>2004年有教友218,412人（佔轄區人口4.8%）、六十個堂區、124名司鐸。現任教區主教為{{link-en|加彌祿·阿爾奇邦·埃托庫多|Camillus Archibong Etokudoh}}<ref>{{cite web |title=RINUNCE E NOMINE, 04.05.2009 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2009/05/04/0297/00690.html#RINUNCIA%20DELL%E2%80%99ARCIVESCOVO%20DI%20KASAMA%20(ZAMBIA) |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2019-04-05 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190405150021/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2009/05/04/0297/00690.html |archivedate=2019-04-05 |language=it |date=2009-05-04}}</ref>，輔理主教為{{link-de|巴特利爵·厄盧克|Patrick Eluke}}<ref>{{cite web |title=Resignations and Appointments, 12.02.2019 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2019/02/12/190212b.html |publisher=Holy See Press Office |accessdate=2019-04-05 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190405132247/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2019/02/12/190212b.html |archivedate=2019-04-05 |language=en |date=2019-02-12}}</ref>。

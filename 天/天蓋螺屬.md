'''天蓋螺屬'''（學名：''{{lang|la|Diodora}}''）是[[裂螺科|裂螺科]][[天蓋螺亞科|天蓋螺亞科]]之下的一個由小型到中型[[海螺|海螺]]組成的[[屬|屬]]{{r|WoRMS_138011}}。

== Life habits==
Like all other fissurellids, ''Diodora'' species are herbivores, and use the <!-- [[radula|radula]] -->[[齒舌|齒舌]] to scrape algae from rocks. An exception is ''D. apertura'', which grazes on sponges<ref>{{Cite journal | url  = http://mollus.oxfordjournals.org/content/33/1/2 | journal=Journal of Molluscan Studies|title=Torsion and the adult snail: a re-evaluation|date=1958|volume=33|issue=1|pages=2–10|doi=10.1093/oxfordjournals.mollus.a064794 |last1=Morton|first1=J. E.}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | title =  Molluscan diets | last1=Graham|first1= A. | journal = Journal of Molluscan Studies | year= 1955 | url =  http://mollus.oxfordjournals.org/content/31/3-4/144.short | volume= 31 | issue= 3–4 | pages= 144–158|doi=10.1093/oxfordjournals.mollus.a064734 }}</ref> such as ''{{tsl|en|Hymeniacidon|}}''.<ref name="Fretter2009">{{Cite journal | last1 = Fretter | first1 = V. | title = Observation on the life history and functional morphology of Cerithiopsis tubercularis (Montagu) and Triphora perversa (L.) | journal = Journal of the Marine Biological Association of the United Kingdom | volume = 29 | issue = 03 | pages = 567 | year = 2009 | doi = 10.1017/S0025315400052784 }}</ref>

Water for respiration and excretion is drawn in under the edge of the shell and exits through the "keyhole" at or near the apex.

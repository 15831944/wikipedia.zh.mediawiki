'''天主教克洛赫教區'''（{{lang-la|Dioecesis Clogheriensis}}、{{lang-ga|Deoise Chlochair}}）是[[羅馬天主教|羅馬天主教]]在[[愛爾蘭|愛爾蘭]]的一個[[教區|教區]]，屬[[天主教阿馬總教區|阿馬總教區]]。

[[File:DioceseClogher.png|thumb]]
教區成立於454年。範圍包括[[莫納亨郡|莫納亨郡]]和[[弗馬納郡|弗馬納郡]]等。座堂位於[[莫納亨|莫納亨]]，現任主教為[[利亞姆·麥克代德|利亞姆·麥克代德]]。2007年，在當地113,401人口中，有教友86,483人，佔轄區總人口76.3%、37個堂區、82名司鐸、6名修士、139名修女。現任主教為{{link-de|老楞佐·達菲|Lawrence Duffy}}<ref>{{cite press release |title=Rinunce e nomine, 08.12.2018 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2018/12/08/0914/01990.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2018-12-14 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181214063309/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2018/12/08/0914/01990.html |archivedate=2018-12-14 |language=it |date=2018-12-08}}</ref>，榮休主教為{{link-en|若瑟·達菲 (主教)|Joseph Duffy (bishop)|若瑟·達菲}}及{{link-en|威廉·麥克代德|Liam MacDaid}}<ref>{{cite press release |title=RINUNCE E NOMINE, 06.05.2010 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2010/05/06/0280/00650.html |publisher=Sala Stampa della Santa Sede |accessdate=2018-12-14 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181214064317/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/it/bollettino/pubblico/2010/05/06/0280/00650.html |archivedate=2018-12-14 |language=it |date=2010-05-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite press release |title=Other Pontifical Acts, 01.10.2016 |url=http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2016/10/01/161001d.html |publisher=Holy See Press Office |accessdate=2018-12-14 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181214064142/http://press.vatican.va/content/salastampa/en/bollettino/pubblico/2016/10/01/161001d.html |archivedate=2018-12-14 |language=en |date=2016-10-01}}</ref>。

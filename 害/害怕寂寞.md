== 背景 ==
盖瑞克斯认为歌曲是和美国歌手{{le|碧碧·雷斯哈|Bebe Rexha}}2016年合作的单曲《{{le|以爱之名 (歌曲)|In the Name of Love (Martin Garrix and Bebe Rexha song)|以爱之名}}》的续作，尽管七首歌的迷你专辑已经有潜在的续作<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.festivalsherpa.com/martin-garrix-debuts-new-song-ft-dua-lipa-ava-nye-2017-myanmar/|title=Martin Garrix Debuts New Song Ft. Dua Lipa At AVA NYE 2017 Myanmar! - Festival Sherpa {{!}} Online Guide to Festivals|last=Kaur|first=Harnoor|date=2017-01-04|work=|newspaper=Festival Sherpa|language=en-US|access-date=2017-01-26|via=}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite news|url=http://www.idolator.com/7655771/martin-garrix-dua-lipa-scared-to-be-lonely-cover|title=Martin Garrix & Dua Lipa Break Cover {{!}} Idolator|last=Wass|first=Mike|date=2017-01-19|work=|newspaper=Idolator|access-date=2017-01-26|via=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefader.com/2017/01/27/martin-garrix-and-dua-lipa-scared-to-be-lonely|title=Martin Garrix And Dua Lipa Connect On "Scared To Be Lonely"|last=Renshaw|first=David|date=2017-01-27|website=The FADER|publisher=|access-date=2017-02-01}}</ref>。他认为歌曲（和《以爱之名》一样）有着欢快的感觉和“更深刻的歌词”是很重要的<ref name=":2" />。

2017年1月19日，单曲封面发布<ref name=":2" />，其中两位艺人并排站在高山之上，歌曲名称在他们头顶上<ref name=":1" />。站右边的盖瑞克斯望着左侧的女歌手，和《以爱之名》类似<ref name=":1" />。

== 作曲 ==
歌曲由盖瑞克斯携手{{le|乔治奥·图伊福特|Giorgio Tuinfort}}、纳撒尼尔·坎帕尼（{{lang|en|Nathaniel Campany}}）、凯尔·席勒（{{lang|en|Kyle Shearer}}）、格鲁吉亚·古（{{lang|en|Georgia Ku}}）和阿齐扎·古泽耶诺瓦（{{lang|en|Aziza Guseynova}}）创作，英国唱作歌手[[杜娃·黎波|杜娃·黎波]]献唱<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.josepvinaixa.com/blog/martin-garrix-dua-lipa-scared-to-be-lonely/|title=Martin Garrix & Dua Lipa "Scared to Be Lonely" (Single Premiere)|date=2017-01-26|website=Ultimate Music|publisher=|access-date=2017-01-26}}{{dead link|date=2017年12月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。歌曲被认为是一首“流行乐气息浓厚”的曲目，创作精致，合成器钩符“耐人寻味”<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://popcrush.com/scared-to-be-lonely-martin-garrix-dua-lipa/|title='Scared to Be Lonely': Dua Lipa Crashes Onto the Dance Floor With Martin Garrix|last=Stern|first=Bradley|date=2017-01-26|website=PopCrush|publisher=|language=en-US|access-date=2017-02-01}}</ref>。和盖瑞克斯与{{le|碧碧·雷斯哈|Bebe Rexha}}合作的另一首单曲《{{le|以爱之名 (歌曲)|In the Name of Love (Martin Garrix and Bebe Rexha song)|以爱之名}}》类似，该曲也采用了[[未来低音|未来低音]]节拍和“性感”的女声<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://wegotthiscovered.com/music/martin-garrix-debuted-new-song-new-years-eve-festival/|title=Martin Garrix Debuted A New Song At New Year's Eve Festival|last=Jones|first=Connor|date=2017-01-03|work=|newspaper=We Got This Covered|language=en-US|access-date=2017-01-19|via=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://edmchicago.com/2017/01/04/martin-garrix-teases-new-track-dua-lipa-video/|title=Martin Garrix Teases New Track with Dua Lipa (Video)|last=Suarez|first=Pedro|date=2017-01-04|website=EDM Chicago|publisher=|access-date=2017-01-19}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://stoneyroads.com/2017/01/martin-garrix-has-previewed-a-new-future-bass-release|title=Martin Garrix Has Previewed A New Future Bass Release|last=Smith|first=Joseph|date=2017-01-04|work=Stoney Roads|access-date=2017-04-29|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|language=en}}</ref>。

== 音乐录影带 ==
官方音乐录影带于歌曲发行当天在马汀的[[YouTube|YouTube]]频道上线。视频由布莱克·克拉里奇（{{lang|en|Blake Claridge}}）执导编剧，卡拉夫·伦敦（{{lang|en|Craft London}}）和路易斯·纽坎比（{{lang|en|Lois Newcombe}}）制作。截至2017年6月，视频点击量突破1.8亿次<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e2vBLd5Egnk&list=PLcJ8DfGqCsmxqh_B5CXOgNgmdakasEkop|title=Martin Garrix & Dua Lipa - Scared To Be Lonely (Official Video)|work=Youtube|date=2017-01-26|accessdate=2017-01-27}}</ref>。

'''派克賓館麵包卷'''（{{lang-en|Parker House roll}}）在1870年代由波士顿歐尼帕克豪斯酒店發明的圓麵包，其作法是以[[擀麵棍|擀麵棍]]將麵團變成橢圓形並從中間折成兩半<ref>{{cite news|title=Recipe: Holley's Habit-Forming Parker House Benne Seed Rolls|publisher=Houstonia|date=2017-10-30|author=Sarah Rufca Nielsen|url=https://www.houstoniamag.com/articles/2017/10/30/holleys-benne-seed-rolls-recipe|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.houstoniamag.com/articles/2017/10/30/holleys-benne-seed-rolls-recipe|archivedate=2017-11-09|deadurl=no}}</ref>，它們是用牛奶製成，有黄油的、柔軟的、甜的和脆的<ref name=Oxford>Smith, Andrew F, ed. Oxford Encyclopedia of Food and Drink in America. New York:Oxford University Press, 2004, Volume 1</ref>。最早的食譜出現在1880年代，他們創作的故事有好幾種變化<ref>Mariani, John F. Encyclopedia of American Food and Drink. New York:Lebhar-Friedman, 1999</ref><ref>Early, Eleanor. New England Cookbook. New York:Random House, 1954</ref>，但全都牽涉憤怒的糕點廚師將未完成的麵包卷放入烤箱，使其外觀凹陷<ref name=Oxford/>；[[芬妮·法默|芬妮·法默]]在1896年{{link-en|波士頓廚藝學校食譜|Boston Cooking-School Cook Book}}上發表了其食譜。

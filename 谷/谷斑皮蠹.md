'''谷斑皮蠹'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Trogoderma granarium''）是[[斑皮蠹屬|斑皮蠹屬]]下的一種昆蟲，原産於[[南亞|南亞]]，是世界上最具毀滅性的農業害蟲之一。<ref name="Stibick-1">[http://www.aphis.usda.gov/import_export/plants/manuals/emergency/downloads/nprg-khapra.pdf Stibick, J. (2007) ''New Pest Response Guidelines: Khapra Beetle''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080920192924/http://www.aphis.usda.gov/import_export/plants/manuals/emergency/downloads/nprg-khapra.pdf |date=2008-09-20 }} APHIS–PPQ–Emergency and Domestic Programs, United States Department of Agriculture, Riverdale, Maryland, p. 1-1</ref> 也是[[世界百大外來入侵種|世界百大外來入侵種]]之一。<ref name=ifas>
[http://entomology.ifas.ufl.edu/creatures/urban/beetles/khapra_beetle.htm University of Florida Food & Agricultural Services]</ref> 因為即使沒有食物也可以存活很長時間，所以這種昆蟲很難根除。此外對許多殺蟲劑都有免疫力。<ref name="ifas"/> 在美國甚至有專門為檢測進口的大米中是否有谷斑皮蠹的聯邦檢疫措施。<ref>
[http://www.chicagotribune.com/news/local/breaking/chi-khapra-beetle-found-in-rice-shipment-from-india-at-ohare-20110823,0,5481867.story Chicago Tribune]</ref>

== 描述 ==

成年的谷斑皮蠹呈褐色，長約{{convert|1.6|-|3|mm}}，寬{{convert|0.9|-|1.7|mm}}。幼蟲體長可達{{convert|5|mm}}體表有紅褐色毛髮。卵呈圓柱形，一端圓滑，一端尖突，長度約{{convert|0.7|mm}}，寬{{convert|0.25|mm}}，重約{{convert|0.02|mg}}。<ref name="Hadaway">Hadaway, A.B. (1956) "The bology of the dermestid beetles ''Trogoderma granarium Everts'' and ''Trogoderma versicolor (Creutz)''" ''Bulletin of Entomological Research'' 46(4): 781-796</ref>

== 控制 ==

[[甲基溴|甲基溴]]煙燻法是最有效的驅除這種昆蟲的方法，<ref>[http://www.issg.org/database/species/ecology.asp?si=142&fr=1&sts Global Invasive Species Database]</ref> 在印度也用使用[[印度楝|印度楝]]粉末的驅除法，<ref>[http://www.eppo.org/QUARANTINE/insects/Trogoderma_granarium/TROGGA_ds.pdf EPPO Quarantine Pest Data Sheet] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123022136/http://www.eppo.org/QUARANTINE/insects/Trogoderma_granarium/TROGGA_ds.pdf |date=2008-11-23 }}</ref> 不過這種方法產生的強烈氣味只能燻走昆蟲，而不能將他們殺死。

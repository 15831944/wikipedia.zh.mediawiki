在[[生物學|生物學]]中，'''林冠'''是指植物[[羣落|羣落]]或[[農作物|農作物]]裡的離地部分，特別是[[樹林|樹林]]中各棵樹的[[樹冠|樹冠]]<ref>Campbell, G.S., and J.M. Norman. 1990. The description and measurement of plant canopy structure. pp. 1-19 In: Russell, G., B. Marshall, and P.G. Jarvis (editors). ''Plant Canopies: Their Growth, Form and Function''. Cambridge University Press.</ref><ref>Moffett, M.W. 2000. What's ''up''? A critical look at the basic terms of canopy biology. ''Biotropica'' 32:569-596.</ref><ref>Hay, R., and R. Porter. 2006 ''Physiology of Crop Yield'' (Second edition). Wiley-Blackwell. ISBN 1405108592, ISBN 978-1405108591</ref>。包括樹冠所有的叶片、枝条、小枝及[[附生植物|附生植物]]<ref>{{cite journal|author1=Nadkarni|coauthors=Nalini M. Parker, Geoffrey G. Ford, E. David Cushing, Judy B. Stallman, Cara|title=The International Canopy Network: A pathway for interdisciplinary exchange of scientific information on forest canopies|journal=Northwest Science|date=1996|issue=70|pages=104-108|url=https://research.libraries.wsu.edu/xmlui/bitstream/handle/2376/1277/v70%20p104%20Nadkarni%20et%20al.PDF?sequence=1|accessdate=2016-05-12}}</ref>。

有時該術語林冠是用來指個別樹或一組的樹木的葉子的外層的範圍。通常一棵樹有茂密的樹冠，会阻止阳光照到在低处生長的植物。

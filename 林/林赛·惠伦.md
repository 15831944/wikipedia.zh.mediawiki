'''林赛·玛丽·惠伦·格雷弗'''（{{lang-en|'''Lindsay Marie Whalen Greve'''}}，{{bd|1982年|5月9日|}}），美国前职业篮球运动员，现任明尼苏达大学女子篮球队主教练。<ref name="WhalenHiredGophers">{{Cite web|title=Lindsay Whalen Takes Over Women's Basketball|url=http://www.gophersports.com/sports/w-baskbl/spec-rel/041218aaa.html|work=Minnesota Golden Gophers|accessdate=12 April 2018}}</ref><ref name="WhalenHiredGophersStrib">{{Cite web|author=Fuller|first=Marcus|title=Lindsay Whalen hired by Gophers as women's basketball coach|url=http://www.startribune.com/lindsay-whalen-hired-by-gophers-as-women-s-basketball-coach/479556393/|work=Star Tribune|publisher=Star Tribune|accessdate=12 April 2018}}</ref>她曾随美国国家队参加2012年和2016年两届奥运。

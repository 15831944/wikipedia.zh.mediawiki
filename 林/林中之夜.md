{{Vgname|林中之夜|en=Night in the Woods|hk=tw|aka=夜猫森林}}是一款由三間遊戲公司共同開發的[[冒险游戏|冒险游戏]]，其發行在[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]]、[[macOS|macOS]]、[[Linux|Linux]]和[[PlayStation_4|PlayStation 4]]版本的時間為2017年2月21日，[[Xbox_One|Xbox One]]平台為同年的12月13日，[[Nintendo_Switch|Nintendo Switch]]平台上則為隔年的2月1日發售。<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://twinfinite.net/2018/01/night-in-the-woods-is-coming-to-switch-this-week/ |title=Night in the Woods Is Coming to Switch This Week |website=twinfinite.net |language=en-US |access-date=2018-01-16}}</ref>，而[[iOS|iOS]]和[[Android|Android]]則會在2018年內公開。開發遊戲的三間開發商分別為Infinite Fall、22nd Century Toys和負責開發引擎和手機版的{{link-en|Secret Lab|Secret Lab}}<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.secretlab.com.au/blog/2017/11/14/how-night-in-the-woods-uses-yarn-spinner|title=How Night in the Woods Uses Yarn Spinner|work=Secret Lab|access-date=2018-03-23|language=en-AU|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180319085001/https://www.secretlab.com.au/blog/2017/11/14/how-night-in-the-woods-uses-yarn-spinner|archive-date=2018-03-19|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.secretlab.com.au/blog/2017/10/24/small-towns-small-screens-night-in-the-woods-is-coming-to-mobile|title=Small Towns, Small Screens: Night in the Woods is coming to mobile|work=Secret Lab|access-date=2018-03-23|language=en-AU|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180323154719/https://www.secretlab.com.au/blog/2017/10/24/small-towns-small-screens-night-in-the-woods-is-coming-to-mobile|archive-date=2018-03-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

玩家所操控的角色是一位輟學回到家鄉後，發現整個地方變得與兒時的記憶完全不同的的年輕女性梅伊（Mae）。遊戲在發售後獲得較多良好的評價，其正面評價多為遊戲中的故事、對話和角色塑造方面。

== 故事與遊戲玩法 ==
瑪格麗特·「梅伊」·博羅夫斯基（Margaret "Mae" Borowski）是一位來自充滿{{link-en|獸形神現|Zoomorphism|擬人動物}}的城鎮——[[負鼠|負鼠]]泉（Possum Springs）的獨生貓女孩<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://curiouscat.me/bombsfall/post/127359166|title=Developer Scott Benson on Characters' Identity|last=Benson|first=Scott|date=2017-03-30|website=curiouscat.me|access-date=2018-02-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180218090342/https://curiouscat.me/bombsfall/post/127359166|archive-date=2018-02-18|dead-url=no}}</ref>。梅伊中途輟學後回到故鄉，住在父母家中的閣樓，但她發現城鎮自煤礦關閉後發生多次變化，同時亦發現了一個可怕的事實，致使她要進入附近的樹林以尋找事情的真相。梅伊不得不面對這小鎮幾十年來一直隱藏著的可怕秘密，其不僅涉及該鎮的礦井，還牽扯到她的老朋友凱西·哈特利（Casey Hartley）最近失蹤的案件。其他遊戲角色包括她的三位好友，他們分別是喜好吸煙的總角之交碧翠絲·「貝婭」·桑塔爾諾（Beatrice "Bea" Santello，鱷魚）、好動的狐狸格雷戈里·「格雷格」·李（Greggory "Gregg" Lee），以及格雷格的男朋友安格斯·迪蘭尼（Angus Delaney，熊）。《{{link-en|Paste (雜誌)|Paste (magazine)|Paste}}》稱《林中之夜》的主題包含「精神疾病、[[抑鬱症|抑鬱症]]、中下階層的困境和美國小鎮的緩慢衰落」<ref name="PasteT10">{{cite web|url=http://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2015/06/the-10-best-videogames-we-played-at-e3-2015.html|title=The 10 Best Videogames We Played at E3 2015|last2=Irwin|first2=Jon|date=2015-06-22|last1=Martin|first1=Garrett|work=Paste|accessdate=2017-03-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180910094609/https://www.pastemagazine.com/articles/2015/06/the-10-best-videogames-we-played-at-e3-2015.html|archive-date=2018-09-10|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

操縱梅伊的時候，玩家可以進行跑跳動作和學習《林中之夜》中的其他{{link-en|遊戲機制|Game mechanics|機制}}以探索負鼠泉。本森將玩家的行動描述為「探索、交談、觀察再聯繫」，而霍洛卡則稱遊戲「以敘事為核心」而非「{{link-en|遊戲玩法|gameplay}}優先」<ref name="KillScreen">{{cite web|url=http://killscreendaily.com/articles/charming-melancholy-night-woods-picks-where-gone-home-left/|title=The charming, melancholy Night in the Woods picks up where Gone Home left off|last=Cox|first=Davis|date=2014-05-01|work=Kill Screen|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150908221108/http://killscreendaily.com/articles/charming-melancholy-night-woods-picks-where-gone-home-left/|archivedate=2015-09-08|deadurl=yes|accessdate=2015-09-05}}</ref>。儘管本森表示：「它更像是『你要和這個人一起行動嗎？』；『好的，酷哦』。或許那個角色在遊戲結束前都不認識你，不過只要跟他互動，你就一定看得到他的故事情節」，但玩家的{{link-en|互動式敘事|Interactive storytelling|決定}}確實會左右《林中之夜》的故事進程<ref>{{cite web| last = Matulef| first = Jeffrey| url = http://www.eurogamer.net/articles/2014-06-22-games-they-are-achangin-a-look-at-nostalgic-adventure-night-in-the-woods| title = Night in the Woods is like Gone Home, in third-person, with talking animals| work = [[Eurogamer|Eurogamer]]| date = 2014-06-25| accessdate = 2015-02-25| archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180715181249/https://www.eurogamer.net/articles/2014-06-22-games-they-are-achangin-a-look-at-nostalgic-adventure-night-in-the-woods| archive-date = 2018-07-15| dead-url = no}}</ref>。

== 開發 ==
開發商在2013年10月22日在[[Kickstarter|Kickstarter]]上進行接受募捐活動，其籌款目標為{{US$|50,000}}。不過，令人震驚的是，籌款目標僅在26小時內完成<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1307515311/night-in-the-woods/|title=Kickstarter, Night in the Woods project page|publisher=Kickstarter|date=2013-11-22|accessdate=2015-02-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180828170456/https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1307515311/night-in-the-woods/|archive-date=2018-08-28|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1307515311/night-in-the-woods/posts/639908|title=FULLY FUNDED! Also, stretch goals? Consoles? Linux?|publisher=Kickstarter|date=2013-10-24|accessdate=2015-02-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180828170438/https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1307515311/night-in-the-woods/posts/639908|archive-date=2018-08-28|dead-url=no}}</ref>。遊戲最終籌得金額超過{{US$|200,000}}。開放團隊利用剩餘的15萬來製作動畫和遊戲中一款名為[[Roguelike|Roguelike]]的小遊戲。

本森認為延伸出更多內容會有更多的支持者進行募捐，但Infinite Fall反對增加內容，並認為增加後可能出現[[範圍蔓延|範圍蔓延]]問題<ref name=PAR>{{cite web|last=Kuchera|first=Ben| url=http://www.penny-arcade.com/report/article/the-goals-that-refused-to-stretch-why-night-in-the-woods-said-no-to-kicksta| title=The goals that refused to stretch: Why Night in the Woods said no to Kickstarter tradition| work=The Penny Arcade Report| date=2013-11-12| accessdate=2015-09-05| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131211184102/http://penny-arcade.com/report/article/the-goals-that-refused-to-stretch-why-night-in-the-woods-said-no-to-kicksta| archivedate=2013-12-11| deadurl=yes}}</ref>。除外，本森亦說出其編寫的《林中之夜》劇本受到{{link-en|克里斯·韋爾|Chris Ware}}、{{link-en|邁克·米尼奧拉|Mike Mignola}}、[[瑪麗·布萊爾|瑪麗·布萊爾]]、[[弗蘭納里·奧康納|弗蘭納里·奧康納]]和{{link-en|理查·斯卡里|Richard Scarry}}的影響<ref name=Gamasutra>{{cite web| last=Ligman| first=Kris| url=http://gamasutra.com/view/news/205993/QA_How_Night_in_the_Woods_taps_into_a_real_sense_of_community.php| title=Q&A: How Night in the Woods taps into a real sense of community| work=[[Gamasutra|Gamasutra]]| date=2013-12-02| accessdate=2015-09-05| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180828170316/http://www.gamasutra.com/view/news/205993/QA_How_Night_in_the_Woods_taps_into_a_real_sense_of_community.php| archive-date=2018-08-28| dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name=Zam>{{cite web| last=Ewert-Krocker| first=Nate| url=http://www.zam.com/article/1370/rust-belt-gothic-lead-writer-scott-benson-unpacks-the-art-that-inspired-night-in-the-woods| title='Rust Belt Gothic': lead writer Scott Benson unpacks the art that inspired Night in the Woods| work=ZAM Network| date=2017-03-09| accessdate=2017-03-11| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180307144045/http://www.zam.com/article/1370/rust-belt-gothic-lead-writer-scott-benson-unpacks-the-art-that-inspired-night-in-the-woods| archive-date=2018-03-07| dead-url=no}}</ref>。

2013年12月，霍洛卡、本森和共同作家霍肯貝里發布了一款名叫《最長之夜》（''Longest Night''）的擴充內容<ref>{{cite web|url=https://finji.itch.io/longest-night|title=Longest Night|work=[[itch.io|itch.io]]|date=2013-12-21|accessdate=2017-02-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180819082032/https://finji.itch.io/longest-night|archive-date=2018-08-19|dead-url=no}}</ref>。隔年同月，再次推出新的擴充內容——《失落星座》（''Lost Constellation''）。在後續進展中，兩者均成為遊戲內容中的一部分<ref>{{cite web | url = https://finji.itch.io/lost-constellation | title = Lost Constellation | date = 2014-12-31 | accessdate = 2015-02-25 | work = [[itch.io|itch.io]] | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20180925021059/https://finji.itch.io/lost-constellation | archive-date = 2018-09-25 | dead-url = no }}</ref>。2017年12月13日，Finji在PC、macOS、Linux、PlayStation 4和Xbox One平台上發行《林中之夜》的擴展版遊戲《怪誕之秋》（''Weird Autumn''）。

'''孟婆'''<ref>English pronunciation approximately {{IPAc-en|,|m|V|ng|'|p|ou}}</ref>(Meng-p'o)是冥王星暗黑色的[[冥王星地理#指節套環|指節套環]]區域中的一節。孟婆斑橫跨本初子午線(經度0度)，在被潮汐鎖定的[[冥衛一|夏戎]]的正下方，東邊就是鯨魚（[[克蘇魯區|克蘇魯區]]）的尾巴。這個名字出自中國地府讓人遺忘前世的[[孟婆|孟婆]]{{IPAc-cmn|m|eng|4|p|o|2}}<ref>{{cite web|last1=Chang|first1=Kenneth|title=NASA’s New Horizons Spacecraft Completes Flyby of Pluto|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2015/07/15/science/space/nasa-new-horizons-spacecraft-reaches-pluto.html|publisher=The New York Times|accessdate=14 July 2015|date=14 July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Rogers|first1=Adam|title=The New, Nerdy Mythology of Pluto's Place Names|url=http://www.wired.com/2015/07/new-nerdy-mythology-plutos-place-names/|publisher=Wired|accessdate=14 July 2015|date=14 July 2015}}</ref>。

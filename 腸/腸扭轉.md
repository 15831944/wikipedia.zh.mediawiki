'''腸扭轉'''（volvulus）是指人體內環形[[消化道|消化道]]沿著本身及固定大小腸的[[腸繫膜|腸繫膜]]打結，最後會導致{{link-en|腸阻塞|Bowel obstruction}}<ref name=NIH2013>{{cite web|title=Anatomic Problems of the Lower GI Tract|url=https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/health-topics/digestive-diseases/anatomic-colon/Pages/facts.aspx#Volvulus|website=NIDDK|accessdate=3 August 2016|date=July 2013|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160728003747/https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/health-topics/digestive-diseases/anatomic-colon/Pages/facts.aspx#Volvulus|archivedate=28 July 2016|df=}}</ref>。症狀包括[[腹痛|腹痛]]、{{link-en|腹脹|Bloating}}、嘔吐、[[便秘|便秘]]以及{{link-en|血便|Blood in stool}}<ref name=NIH2013/><ref name=Ros2013/>，病徵可能迅速或緩慢出現<ref name=Ros2013/>。[[腸繫膜|腸繫膜]]可能會因扭轉旋緊，導致流向該部位腸道的[[血液|血液]]供應被切斷，產生缺血性腸疾<ref name=NIH2013/> 。在此情形下，患者可能會有[[發燒|發燒]]或觸診腹部時感到劇痛等症狀<ref name=Ros2013/>。 

此症的危險因素包括胚胎時期發生中腸扭轉（Intestinal malrotation）先天性缺陷、結腸過大、先天性巨結腸症,（Hirschsprung's disease）、[[妊娠|妊娠]]以及腸[[沾黏|沾黏]]<ref name=NIH2013/>。長期[[便秘|便秘]]及高纖維飲食可能增加罹患風險<ref name=Gin2012/>。
此症最常影響成人腸道的部位為[[乙狀結腸|乙狀結腸]]，[[盲腸|盲腸]]次之<ref name=NIH2013/>；在兒童中則是以[[小腸|小腸]]較為常見<ref>{{cite book|last1=Wilkins|first1=Lippincott Williams &|title=Professional Guide to Diseases|date=2009|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|isbn=9780781778992|page=283|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=1h6vu60L6FcC&pg=PA283|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160816225038/https://books.google.ca/books?id=1h6vu60L6FcC&pg=PA283|archivedate=2016-08-16|df=}}</ref>。胃部也可能罹患此症<ref>{{cite book|last1=Feldman|first1=Mark|last2=Friedman|first2=Lawrence S.|last3=Brandt|first3=Lawrence J.|title=Sleisenger and Fordtran's Gastrointestinal and Liver Disease: Pathophysiology, Diagnosis, Management, Expert Consult Premium Edition - Enhanced Online Features|date=2010|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=1437727670|page=384|edition=9|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=zEZOqB6r9hwC&pg=PA384|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160816210234/https://books.google.ca/books?id=zEZOqB6r9hwC&pg=PA384|archivedate=2016-08-16|df=}}</ref> 。常見的診斷方式為[[醫學影像|醫學影像]]，如{{link-en|普通X光片|Projectional radiography}}、 [[大腸鋇劑灌腸造影|大腸鋇劑灌腸造影]]或電腦斷層掃描<ref name=NIH2013/>。 

[[乙狀結腸|乙狀結腸]]扭轉的初步治療有時會藉由{{le|結腸鏡檢查|Sigmoidoscopy}}或加上[[大腸鋇劑灌腸造影|大腸鋇劑灌腸造影]]檢查以確定病灶<ref name=Gin2012/>。因此症有復發的高風險，故通常建議在檢查後的2天內施行{{le|腸切除術|Bowel resection}}<ref name=Gin2012/>。但如果腸本身已嚴重扭轉，或是供應腸的血流已被切斷時，則應立刻動手術<ref name=NIH2013/>。在盲腸扭轉的情形，通常有部分的腸必需予以手術割除<ref name=Gin2012/>。而如果腸本身仍然健康，則有可能將其移回正常位置並加以縫合固定<ref name=NIH2013/><ref name=Gin2012/>。 

腸扭轉早在西元前1550年的[[古埃及|古埃及]]地區就有相關的病歷記載，而最常發生在非洲、[[中東|中東]]地區以及印度人身上<ref name=Gin2012/><ref name=Gin2012>{{cite journal|last1=Gingold|first1=D|last2=Murrell|first2=Z|title=Management of colonic volvulus.|journal=Clinics in colon and rectal surgery|date=December 2012|volume=25|issue=4|pages=236–44|pmid=24294126|doi=10.1055/s-0032-1329535|pmc=3577612}}</ref> 。在美國，此症每年的發生率為約10萬人中有2至3人.<ref name=Ros2013>{{cite book|last1=Marx|first1=John|last2=Walls|first2=Ron|last3=Hockberger|first3=Robert|title=Rosen's Emergency Medicine - Concepts and Clinical Practice|date=2013|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=1455749877|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=uggC0i_jXAsC&pg=RA1-PA74|language=en|chapter=95|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160816195750/https://books.google.ca/books?id=uggC0i_jXAsC&pg=RA1-PA74|archivedate=2016-08-16|df=}}</ref><ref name=Gor2007>{{cite book|last1=Gordon|first1=Philip H.|last2=Nivatvongs|first2=Santhat|title=Principles and Practice of Surgery for the Colon, Rectum, and Anus, Third Edition|date=2007|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=9781420017991|page=971|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=GEXMBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA971|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160817004842/https://books.google.ca/books?id=GEXMBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA971|archivedate=2016-08-17|df=}}</ref>；乙狀結腸及盲腸扭轉發生的年齡則通常介於30至70歲之間<ref name=NIH2013/><ref>{{cite book|last1=Beck|first1=David|last2=Beck|first2=David E.|title=Handbook of Colorectal Surgery: Third Edition|date=2012|publisher=JP Medical Ltd|isbn=9781907816208|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=yJbXNf0XKysC&pg=PT454|language=en|chapter=23|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160816222554/https://books.google.ca/books?id=yJbXNf0XKysC&pg=PT454|archivedate=2016-08-16|df=}}</ref>。此症的癒後與腸組織[[受損死亡|壞疽]]相關<ref name=Ros2013/>。英文的「volvulus」此字是從拉丁文的「volvere」而來，意思是「滾動」<ref name=Gin2012/>。

'''腸-腦軸線'''（gut–brain axis）是消化道和[[中樞神經系統|中樞神經系統]]的生物化學信號<ref name=2014Wangrev />。這個詞已延伸來描述{{le|腸道菌種|gut flora}}在交互作用中的角色，有時也會用'''菌-腸-腦軸線'''（microbiome-gut-brain axis）具體指出菌種在其中的角色<ref name=2014Wangrev>{{cite journal | last1 = Wang | first1 = Y | last2 = Kasper | first2 = LH | date = May 2014 | title = The role of microbiome in central nervous system disorders | url = | journal = Brain Behav Immun | volume = 38 | issue = | pages = 1–12 | doi = 10.1016/j.bbi.2013.12.015 | pmid = 2437046 }}</ref><ref name=Mayer2014rev>{{cite journal | last1 = Mayer | first1 = EA | last2 = Knight | first2 = R | last3 = Mazmanian | first3 = SK | display-authors = 3 | last4 = et al | year = 2014 | title = Gut microbes and the brain: paradigm shift in neuroscience | url = http://www.jneurosci.org/content/34/46/15490.full.pdf | format = PDF | journal = J Neurosci | volume = 34 | issue = | pages = 15490–15496 | doi = 10.1523/JNEUROSCI.3299-14.2014 | pmid = 25392516 | pmc=4228144}}</ref><ref name="DinanandCryan2015">{{cite journal|last1=Dinan|first1=T.G|last2=Cryan|first2=2015|title=The impact of gut microbiota on brain and behavior: implications for psychiatry|journal=Curr Opin Clin Nutr Metab Care|date=2015|volume=18|pages=552–558|pmid= 26372511|doi=10.1097/MCO.0000000000000221}}</ref>。

以較廣的定義來看，腸-腦軸線包括[[中樞神經系統|中樞神經系統]]、中樞內分泌系統及中樞免疫系統，其中包括[[下丘脑-垂体-肾上腺轴|下丘脑-垂体-肾上腺轴]]（HPA轴）、[[自主神经系统|自主神经系统]]中的交感神經系統、副交感神經系統（[[迷走神经|迷走神经]]）及[[肠神经系统|肠神经系统]]，以及腸道中的{{le|微生物群|microbiota}}<ref name=2014Wangrev /><ref name="DinanandCryan2015" />。

研究者對此領域開始關注的原因是因為2004年的一篇研究，發現腸道中沒有菌種的小鼠，其HPA軸對壓力的反應比一般腸道中有菌種的小鼠要誇張許多<ref name=2014Wangrev />。到2016年1月為止，有研究針對腸道菌種在腸-腦軸線中的角色進行分析，或分析腸-腦軸線產生的神经活性化合物，或是分析人在不同精神狀態下神經的差異，研討腸道菌種在壓力下的變化，或是研究不同[[益生菌|益生菌]]（稱為[[Psychobiotics|Psychobiotics]]）的影響，不過這些研究的規模不大，而且還不能一概而论，目前還不清楚腸道菌種的變化到底是疾病的結果、是造成疾病的原因，或者只是在腸-腦軸線中回授迴路的一部份<ref name=JFP2016rev>{{cite journal | url = http://www.jfponline.com/specialty-focus/nutrition/article/targeting-gut-flora-to-treat-and-prevent-disease/fae361971eb9601f1c2a720016f976c1.html | pmid = 26845162 | volume = 65 | title = Targeting gut flora to treat and prevent disease | year = 2016 | journal = J Fam Pract | pages = 34–8 | last1 = Schneiderhan | first1 = J | last2 = Master-Hunter | first2 = T | last3 = Locke | first3 = A | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20160815012710/http://www.jfponline.com/specialty-focus/nutrition/article/targeting-gut-flora-to-treat-and-prevent-disease/fae361971eb9601f1c2a720016f976c1.html | archivedate = 2016-08-15 }}</ref><ref name=2014Wangrev />。有一篇2016年的[[系統綜述|系統綜述]]針對臨床前實驗及小規模人體實驗進行探討，結論是有些市售益生菌種有潛力用在{{le|中樞神經系統疾病|central nervous system disorder}}上<ref name="CNS SystRev 2016">{{cite journal | vauthors = Wang H, Lee IS, Braun C, Enck P | title = Effect of probiotics on central nervous system functions in animals and humans - a systematic review | journal = J. Neurogastroenterol Motil. | volume = | issue = | pages = | date = July 2016 | pmid = 27413138 | doi = 10.5056/jnm16018|quote=We reviewed the effect of probiotics on the central nervous system in randomized controlled trials in animals and humans, and analyzed the possibility of translating animal models to human studies because few human studies have been conducted to date. According to the qualitative analyses of current studies, we can provisionally draw the conclusion that B. longum, B. breve, B. infantis, L. helveticus, L. rhamnosus, L. plantarum, and L. casei were most effective  in improving CNS function, including psychiatric disease-associated functions (anxiety, depression, mood, stress response) and memory abilities.}}</ref>。

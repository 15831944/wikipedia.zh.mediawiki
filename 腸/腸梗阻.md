'''腸梗阻'''（Bowel obstruction或Intestinal obstruction），係為[[腸臟|腸部]]的{{le|機能性阻塞|Ileus}}，造成無法正常進行[[消化作用|消化]]運動<ref name=SBO2010>{{cite book|last=Fitzgerald|first=J. Edward F.|title=Small Bowel Obstruction|year=2010|publisher=Wiley-Blackwell|location=Oxford|doi=10.1002/9781444315172.ch14|isbn=9781405170253|pages=74–79|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=iduO1gYydz0C&pg=PA74|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908221043/https://books.google.com/books?id=iduO1gYydz0C&pg=PA74|archivedate=September 8, 2017|df=mdy-all}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last1=Adams|first1=James G.|title=Emergency Medicine: Clinical Essentials (Expert Consult -- Online)|date=2012|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=1455733946|page=331|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=rpoH-KYE93IC&pg=PA331|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908221043/https://books.google.com/books?id=rpoH-KYE93IC&pg=PA331|archivedate=September 8, 2017|df=mdy-all}}</ref>，發生部位可能是[[小腸|小腸]]或是[[大腸|大腸]]<ref name=Gor2015>{{cite journal|last1=Gore|first1=RM|last2=Silvers|first2=RI|last3=Thakrar|first3=KH|last4=Wenzke|first4=DR|last5=Mehta|first5=UK|last6=Newmark|first6=GM|last7=Berlin|first7=JW|title=Bowel Obstruction.|journal=Radiologic clinics of North America|date=November 2015|volume=53|issue=6|pages=1225–40|pmid=26526435|doi=10.1016/j.rcl.2015.06.008}}</ref>，症狀及體徵有[[腹痛|肚子痛]]、[[嘔吐|嘔吐]]、[[腹部脹氣|腹部脹氣]]和無法排[[屁|氣]]<ref name=Gor2015/>。嚴重到要送醫的突發性{{link-en|急腹症|Acute abdomen}}成因中，機械性阻塞約佔了 5-15%<ref name=Gor2015/><ref name=SBO2010/>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
腸梗阻的成因如下，患有[[沾黏|沾黏]]、[[疝|疝氣]]、[[腸扭轉|腸扭轉]]、[[子宮內膜異位症|子宮內膜異位症]]、[[炎症性腸病|發炎性腸道疾病]]，[[闌尾炎|闌尾炎]]、[[憩室炎|憩室炎]]、{{link-en|缺血性腸道疾病|Ischemic colitis}}、[[結核病|結核病]]和[[腸套疊|腸套疊]]，長有[[瘜肉|贅生物]]<ref name=Gor2015/><ref name=SBO2010/>。小腸梗阻常見成因為沾黏和疝氣，大腸梗阻常見成因則為腫瘤和腸扭轉<ref name=Gor2015/><ref name=SBO2010/>，可能的確診方式為照[[X射線|X光]]，不過照[[X射線電腦斷層掃描|電腦斷層掃描]]更為精確<ref name=Gor2015/>，為孩童或[[妊娠|孕婦]]確診時，照[[超音波|超音波]]或[[核磁共振成像|核磁共振成像]]較有幫助<ref name=Gor2015/>。

<!-- 預防及治療 -->
保守的治療方式為動手術<ref name=SBO2010/>，典型的治療方式為[[靜脈注射|打針]]，{{link-en|鼻胃管插管|Nasogastric intubation}}以緩解腸道症狀、給予[[鎮痛藥|止痛藥]]<ref name=SBO2010/>，也常使用[[抗生素|抗生素]]<ref name=SBO2010/>。小腸梗阻的病例中，約有 25% 需要動手術<ref name=Fer2014/>。可能的併發症有[[敗血症|敗血症]]、腸[[缺血|缺血]]和[[腸胃穿孔|腸穿孔]]<ref name=Gor2015/>。

<!-- 流行病學 -->
2015 年約有 320 萬起腸梗阻病例，其中有 26.4 萬起死亡<ref name=GBD2015Pre>{{cite journal|last1=GBD 2015 Disease and Injury Incidence and Prevalence|first1=Collaborators.|title=Global, regional, and national incidence, prevalence, and years lived with disability for 310 diseases and injuries, 1990-2015: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2015.|journal=Lancet|date=8 October 2016|volume=388|issue=10053|pages=1545–1602|pmid=27733282|doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(16)31678-6|pmc=5055577}}</ref><ref name=GBD2015De>{{cite journal|last1=GBD 2015 Mortality and Causes of Death|first1=Collaborators.|title=Global, regional, and national life expectancy, all-cause mortality, and cause-specific mortality for 249 causes of death, 1980-2015: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2015.|journal=Lancet|date=8 October 2016|volume=388|issue=10053|pages=1459–1544|pmid=27733281|doi=10.1016/s0140-6736(16)31012-1|pmc=5388903}}</ref>，腸梗阻發生在男女的比例均等，在任何年齡層都可能發生<ref name=Fer2014>{{cite book|last1=Ferri|first1=Fred F.|title=Ferri's Clinical Advisor 2015: 5 Books in 1|date=2014|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=9780323084307|page=1093|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=icTsAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA1093|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908221043/https://books.google.com/books?id=icTsAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA1093|archivedate=September 8, 2017|df=mdy-all}}</ref>，早在西元前 1550 年，古埃及的{{link-en|埃伯斯草紙醫典|Ebers Papyrus}}就記有詳細的病例<ref>{{cite book|last1=Yeo|first1=Charles J.|last2=McFadden|first2=David W.|last3=Pemberton|first3=John H.|last4=Peters|first4=Jeffrey H.|last5=Matthews|first5=Jeffrey B.|title=Shackelford's Surgery of the Alimentary Tract|date=2012|publisher=Elsevier Health Sciences|isbn=1455738077|page=1851|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=VTE-h2D1SNEC&pg=PA1851|language=en|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170908221043/https://books.google.com/books?id=VTE-h2D1SNEC&pg=PA1851|archivedate=September 8, 2017|df=mdy-all}}</ref>。

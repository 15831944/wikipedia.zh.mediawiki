電影《'''[[怪獸與牠們的產地_(電影)|怪獸與牠們的產地]]'''》的'''原聲帶'''於2016年11月18日由{{le|水塔音樂|WaterTower Music}}發行，作曲者為[[詹姆斯·紐頓·霍華|詹姆斯·紐頓·霍華]]<ref name="Soundtrack">{{cite news|url=http://www.ew.com/article/2016/10/07/fantastic-beasts-soundtrack-title-theme|title=Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them theme debuts on Pottermore|first1=Jessica|date=2016-10-07|work=Entertainment Weekly|accessdate=2016-10-27|last1=Derschowitz}}</ref>。

== 製作 ==

2016年4月，宣布將由詹姆斯·紐頓·霍華譜寫《怪獸與牠們的產地》的配樂<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.nme.com/news/film/fantastic-beasts-james-newton-howard-to-score-jk-875743|title='Fantastic Beasts': James Newton Howard to score JK Rowling's 'Harry Potter' spin-off|author=|date=2016-04-09|newspaper=NME|accessdate=2018-10-04|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://filmmusicreporter.com/2016/04/07/james-newton-howard-to-score-fantastic-beasts-and-where-to-find-them/|title=James Newton Howard to Score ‘Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them’|accessdate=2018-10-04|date=2016-04-07|publisher=Film Music Reporter|language=en-US}}</ref>。霍華共花了七個月的時間來譜寫配樂<ref name="Pottermore">{{cite web|url=https://www.pottermore.com/news/pottermore-officially-debuts-main-theme-from-fantastic-beasts-soundtrack|title=Exclusive: Pottermore debuts the main theme from the soundtrack for Fantastic Beasts|accessdate=2018-10-04|website=[[Pottermore|Pottermore]]}}</ref>。[[Pottermore|Pottermore]]網站於同年10月發布首支官方主題曲<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/pottermore-debuts-fantastic-beasts-theme-936077|title=Pottermore Debuts 'Fantastic Beasts' Theme Tune|date=2016-10-07|newspaper=The Hollywood Reporter|accessdate=2018-10-04|language=en}}</ref>。有部分沿用[[約翰·威廉斯|約翰·威廉斯]]為[[哈利波特系列電影|哈利波特電影]]譜寫的「[[嘿美主題|嘿美主題]]」<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.hk01.com/%E9%9B%BB%E5%BD%B1/116783/%E5%93%88%E5%88%A9%E6%B3%A2%E7%89%B9-%E6%8F%9B%E9%81%8E4%E4%BD%8D%E4%BD%9C%E6%9B%B2%E5%AE%B6-%E6%9C%80%E7%B6%93%E5%85%B8%E4%BB%8D%E6%98%AFjohn-williams%E8%AD%9C%E7%9A%84%E4%B8%BB%E9%A1%8C%E6%9B%B2|title=【哈利波特】換過4位作曲家　最經典仍是John Williams譜的主題曲|last=羅偉強|date=2017-09-04|newspaper=香港01|accessdate=2018-10-04|language=zh-HK}}</ref>，但也加入了新寫的主題<ref name="Pottermore" />。

配樂中的歌曲「Blind Pig」由[[J·K·羅琳|J·K·羅琳]]親自撰寫歌詞，[[澳洲|澳洲]]歌手{{Le|Emmi (澳洲歌手)|Emmi (Australian singer)|Emmi}}演唱<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pottermore.com/news/fantastic-beasts-jazz-singer-emmi-interview|title=Fantastic Beasts jazz singer Emmi talks to Pottermore|date=2016-11-25|newspaper=Pottermore|accessdate=2018-10-04|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/av/entertainment-arts-37979350/how-this-singer-got-a-magic-nod-from-jk-rowling|title=How singer got magic nod from JK Rowling|accessdate=2018-10-04|date=2016-11-15|publisher=BBC News|language=en}}</ref>。

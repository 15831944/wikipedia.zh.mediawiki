'''杰玛·阿特登'''（{{lang-en|''' Gemma Arterton '''}}，{{bd|1986年|2月12日<ref name=mix/>|||catIdx= J杰玛}}）出生于[[英国|英国]][[肯特郡|肯特郡]]的格莱夫森德，英国著名女[[演员|演员]]。

== 简介 ==
[[File:GemmaArtertonSept10TIFF.jpg|right]].]]

艾特頓出生於[[英国|英国]][[肯特郡|肯特郡]]的格莱夫森德，母亲是清洁工，父亲是焊工<ref name="DailyM1">[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/pages/live/articles/showbiz/bazbamigboye.html?in_article_id=460653&in_page_id=1794 How St Trinian's Gemma makes easy work of a Shakespearean comedy] Daily Mail - 8 June 2007</ref>。
她有一个妹妹叫做“汉娜·简”<ref name="DailyM1"/><ref name="armstrongref">{{cite news|last=Armstrong|first=Stephen|coauthors=|title=Gemma Arterton stars in Lost in Austen and Tess of the d’Urbervilles|pages=|publisher=Times Online|date=31 August 2008|url=http://entertainment.timesonline.co.uk/tol/arts_and_entertainment/tv_and_radio/article4625692.ece|accessdate=25 March 2010 | location=London}}</ref><ref name="ref465">{{cite news|last=Fletcher|first=Damien|coauthors=|title=Exclusive: Bond Girl Gemma Arterton shops at Primark and mum's a cleaner|pages=|publisher=Mirror|date=9 May 2008|url=http://www.mirror.co.uk/celebs/news/2008/09/05/exclusive-bond-girl-gemma-arterton-shops-at-primark-and-mum-s-a-cleaner-115875-20724218/|accessdate=25 March 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=James Mottram |url=http://www.heraldscotland.com/arts-ents/film-tv-features/gemma-arterton-from-a-council-estate-to-clash-of-the-titans-1.1016929 |title=Gemma Arterton – from a council estate to Clash of the Titans |publisher=Heraldscotland.com |date=29 March 2010 |accessdate=13 October 2010}}</ref>。

早年艾特頓在[[菲尔德文法学校|菲尔德文法学校]]读书<ref name="DailyM2">[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/pages/live/femail/article.html?in_article_id=463675&in_page_id=1879 St Trinian's girl Gemma Arterton on the new skool rules] Daily Mail - 21 June 2007</ref>，
后来她毕业于[[英国|英国]][[皇家戏剧艺术学院|皇家戏剧艺术学院]]<ref>[http://www.rada.org/alumni/directory.aspx?id=108&frm=y&sur=Arterton&nme=Gemma&sub=%20&ttl=%20&yr=%20 2007 Graduate: Gemma Arterton]{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} RADA</ref>。

== 个人生活 ==
[[File:Gemma_Arterton_in_Moscow.jpg|thumb]]

艾特頓曾和[[西班牙|西班牙]][[马术|马术师]]爱德华多·穆尼奥斯有过6个月的恋爱关系，一直到2008年12月<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nationalledger.com/ledgerpop/article_272632264.shtml|title=Gemma Arterton Wedding (Photos) - Secret Ceremony in Spain|work=National Ledger|date=8 June 2010|accessdate=10 June 2010}}</ref>。
2010年6月5日，她秘密地嫁给了一个销售经理名叫“斯特凡诺·安提尔”，两人在[[西班牙|西班牙]][[安达卢西亚|安达卢西亚]]举行了婚礼<ref name="showbizlondon">{{cite web|url=http://sify.com/news/arterton-weds-catelli-news-international-kgimadadjdj.html |title=Arterton weds Catelli |publisher=Sify.com |date=8 June 2010 |accessdate=13 October 2010}}</ref>。
目前阿特登生活在[[伦敦|伦敦]]<ref>[http://www.guardian.co.uk/film/2010/sep/09/tamara-drewe-gemma-arterton-interview Tamara Drewe and the rise of Gemma Arterton]</ref>。

'''杰克·科比'''（{{lang-en|Jack Kirby}}，原名'''雅各布·库兹伯格'''（{{lang-en|Jacob Kurtzberg}}），{{bd|1917年|8月28日|1994年|2月6日|catIdx=Jack Kirby}}），[[美国|美国]]著名的[[漫画家|漫画家]]、编辑和编剧。被称为“美国漫画艺术大师”，有“国王”（The King）的绰号，創作的角色大多都在[[漫威漫畫|漫威漫畫]]與[[DC漫畫|DC漫畫]]裡。<ref name="a">{{cite web | language =  | publisher = Lambiek | title = Jack Kirby | url = http://lambiek.net/artists/k/kirby.htm | author =  | date =  | accessdate =  }}</ref>

==获奖和荣誉==
===艾利奖（Alley Award）===
*1963年：喜爱的短故事（Favorite Short Story）；<ref>[http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/alley63.php 1963 Alley Awards]</ref>
*1964年：
**最佳小说（Best Novel）；
**最佳新连环画或书籍（Best New Strip or Book）；<ref>[http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/alley64.php 1964 Alley Awards]</ref>
*1965年：最佳短故事（Best Short Story）；<ref>[http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/alley65.php 1965 Alley Awards]</ref>
*1966: 最佳专业作品，固定小专栏（Best Professional Work, Regular Short Feature）；<ref>[http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/alley66.php 1966 Alley Awards]</ref>
*1967年：
**最佳铅笔画家（Best Pencil Artist）；
**最佳专业作品，固定小专栏（Best Professional Work, Regular Short Feature）；<ref name=alley67>[http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/alley67.php 1967 Alley Awards]</ref>
*1968:
** 最佳专业作品，最佳固定小专栏（Best Professional Work, Best Regular Short Feature）<ref>[http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/alley68.php 1968 Alley Awards] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131024235420/http://www.hahnlibrary.net/comics/awards/alley68.php |date=2013-10-24 }}</ref>
** 最佳专业作品，名人堂（Best Professional Work, Hall of Fame）<ref>Mark Hanerfeld, who counted the votes, first listed ''Nick Fury, Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D.'' as the winner.  Later, he noticed that he had counted votes for a) "Fantastic Four by Jack Kirby", b) "Fantastic Four by Stan Lee", and c) "Fantastic Four by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby", separately. Had they been counted as one feature, these votes combined would have given the ''Fantastic Four'' the victory.</ref>

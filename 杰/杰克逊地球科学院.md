杰克逊地球科学院的历史可以追溯到1888年地质系的成立，最终于2005年该学院终于成为大学的一个独立学院。<ref>Historical Milestones of the Jackson School http://www.jsg.utexas.edu/about/history/</ref>已故的约翰·A和凯瑟琳·G·杰克逊捐赠了2.72亿美金而成立了该学院.<ref>Gifts contributed by oilman John A. Jackson to The University of Texas at Austin total $272 million {{cite web |url=http://www.jsg.utexas.edu/news/rels/092503.html |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2009-01-13 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080828024914/http://www.jsg.utexas.edu/news/rels/092503.html |archivedate=2008-08-28 }}</ref> 该学院截止2015年12月31日共收到捐款442.3万美金<ref>http://utimco.org/scripts/PrivateEndowInfo/collList.asp?comp=10</ref>

莎朗·莫舍博士是杰克逊地球科学院院长。<ref>Sharon Mosher, National Geoscience Leader, Becomes Dean of Jackson School of Geosciences http://www.jsg.utexas.edu/news/2009/06/sharon-mosher-national-geoscience-leader-becomes-dean-of-jackson-school-of-geosciences/</ref>

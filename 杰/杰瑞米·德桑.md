'''杰瑞米·德桑'''（{{bd|1966年|6月14日}}，{{lang-en|Jeremy Dyson}}）是一位英格兰[[编剧|编剧]]。他与演员[[史蒂夫·佩姆伯顿|史蒂夫·佩姆伯顿]] , [[马克·加蒂斯|马克·加蒂斯]]和编剧[[里斯·谢尔史密斯|里斯·谢尔史密斯]]<ref>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/comedy/previews/chat_league1.shtml Interview with Reece Shearsmith on bbc.co.uk]</ref>共同创作了情景喜剧《[[绅士联盟|绅士联盟]]》，后来又编创了伦敦西区剧目《鬼故事》。

==早年生活==
德桑出生于英格兰[[約克郡|約克郡]][[利兹|利兹]]，母Elaine Saville，父Melvin Dyson。<ref name="YEP">"[http://www.yorkshireeveningpost.co.uk/news/latest-news/jeremy_dyson_leeds_writer_in_literary_spotlight_1_2247907 Jeremy Dyson: Leeds writer in literary spotlight]", ''[[Yorkshire_Evening_Post|Yorkshire Evening Post]]'', 14 July 2010. Retrieved 2 July 2011</ref> 他有一个哥哥（Andrew Dyson）和一个妹妹（Jane Dyson），后者在[[利兹大学|利兹大学]]攻读了[[心理学|心理学]]学位，之后在[[Northern_Film_School|北方影视学校]]获得了编剧的文学硕士学位。<ref name="Ghost">"[http://www.ghoststoriestheshow.co.uk/cast-creative/ Jeremy Dyson - Writer] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100705144019/http://www.ghoststoriestheshow.co.uk/cast-creative/ |date=2010-07-05 }}", ghostsroriestheshow.co.uk. Retrieved 2 July 2011</ref>他曾住在[[伦敦|伦敦]][[海布里|海布里]]，后来搬到了[[西约克郡|西约克郡]][[伊尔克利|伊尔克利]]。<ref name="Salter">Salter, Jessica (2011) "[http://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/theatre/comedy/8274932/World-of-Jeremy-Dyson-writer.html World of Jeremy Dyson, writer]", ''[[Daily_Telegraph|Daily Telegraph]]'', 21 January 2011.

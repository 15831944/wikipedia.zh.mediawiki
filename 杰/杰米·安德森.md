'''杰米·路易丝·安德森'''（{{lang-en|Jamie Louise Anderson}}，{{bd|1990年|9月13日||}}）生于加利福尼亚州南太浩湖，是一名美国女子单板滑雪运动员，主攻障碍技巧和大跳台项目<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cbssports.com/olympics/news/winter-olympics-jamie-anderson-wins-gold-again-in-snowboard-slopestyle/|title=Winter Olympics: Jamie Anderson wins gold again in snowboard slopestyle|publisher=CBS Sports|date=2018-02-12|accessdate=2019-01-26}}</ref>。她在家中八个孩子当中排行老五<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.xgames.com/article/10409698/reigning-snowboard-slopestyle-queen-jamie-anderson-talks-sochi-tricks|title=Reigning snowboard slopestyle queen Jamie Anderson talks Sochi and tricks|publisher=XGames|date=2014-02-05|accessdate=2019-01-26|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190126090917/http://www.xgames.com/article/10409698/reigning-snowboard-slopestyle-queen-jamie-anderson-talks-sochi-tricks|archivedate=2019-01-26}}</ref>，她的其中一个姐姐Joanie同样也是单板滑雪选手<ref>{{cite web|url=http://time.com/5143976/olympics-2018-jamie-anderson-snowboarding-gold/|title=Olympics 2018: Jamie Anderson Wins Snowboarding Gold|publisher=Time|date=2018-02-12|accessdate=2019-01-26}}</ref>。她也正是受其两个姐姐的影响在九岁时开始练习单板滑雪<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/snowboard/athlete-profile-n3018726-jamie-anderson.htm|title=Snowboarding {{!}} Athlete Profile: Jamie ANDERSON|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-01-26|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180421140927/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/snowboard/athlete-profile-n3018726-jamie-anderson.htm|archivedate=2018-04-21}}</ref>。由于她和其他几个兄弟姐妹都在家中接受教育，她每天都有机会前往家附近的山去练习单板滑雪<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2014/02/10/sports/olympics/anderson-slopestyles-star-on-top-again.html|title=Jamie Anderson, Slopestyle's Star, Is on Top Again|first=John|last=Branch|date=2014-02-09|publisher=The New York Times|accessdate=2018-02-12|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180110222834/https://www.nytimes.com/2014/02/10/sports/olympics/anderson-slopestyles-star-on-top-again.html|archivedate=2018-01-10}}</ref>。

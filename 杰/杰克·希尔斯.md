'''杰克·希尔斯'''（{{Lang|en|Jack G. Hills}}）是一名美国天文学家。他曾提出[[内奥尔特云|内奥尔特云]]的概念，该区域因此也冠上他的姓，称为“希尔斯云”<ref>{{Cite web|publisher=Department of Physics and Astronomy|title=Stellar Dynamics: Jack Hills|url=http://www.physics.ku.edu/astronomy/history/horak_files/stellar_dynamics_jack_h.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141215073806/http://www.physics.ku.edu/astronomy/history/horak_files/stellar_dynamics_jack_h.html|archive-date=15 December 2014}}</ref><ref name="Moltenbrey2015">{{cite book|author=Michael Moltenbrey|title=Dawn of Small Worlds: Dwarf Planets, Asteroids, Comets|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Bf_NCgAAQBAJ&pg=PA97|date=24 October 2015|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-3-319-23003-0|page=97}}</ref>。他大部分职业生涯都是在美国[[洛斯阿拉莫斯国家实验室|洛斯阿拉莫斯国家实验室]]渡过的。从1998年开始，他成为该实验室的研究员<ref>[http://www.lanl.gov/collaboration/fellows/fellows-biographies.php Laboratory Fellows from 1981 to the present] Los Alamos National Security ''J. G. Hills has made large contributions to Laboratory and international programs in astrophysics, interplanet science, and asteroid interdiction. He also is recognized worldwide as one of the major authorities in the field of stellar dynamics and has made seminal contributions to the quantitative understanding of interactions between binary and single stars in clusters.''</ref>。

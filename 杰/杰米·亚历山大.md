'''杰米·亚历山大'''（{{lang-en|'''Jaimie Alexander'''}}，{{bd|1984年|3月12日|||catIdx= Y亚历山大}}）出生于[[美国|美国]][[南卡罗莱纳州|南卡罗莱纳州]][[格林维尔_(南卡罗来纳州)|格林维尔]]，美国女演员。

== 生平 ==
1984年，亚历山大出生在[[美国|美国]][[南卡罗莱纳州|南卡罗莱纳州]][[格林维尔_(南卡罗来纳州)|格林维尔]]，4岁时，全家搬迁到[[德克萨斯州|德克萨斯州]]。亚历山大有4个兄弟，是家中唯一的女兒。<ref name=gel>{{Cite news|last=|first=|coauthors=|title=Heritage board will honor a great patron of Grapevine|pages=|publisher=Fort Worth Star-Telegram|date=2007-06-12|url=http://nl.newsbank.com/nl-search/we/Archives?p_product=ST&s_site=dfw&p_multi=ST&p_theme=realcities&p_action=search&p_maxdocs=200&p_topdoc=1&p_text_direct-0=11A24FBD684AD1C0&p_field_direct-0=document_id&p_perpage=10&p_sort=YMD_date:D&s_trackval=GooglePM|accessdate=2007-06-12|quote=Jaimie Tarbush a former Grapevine resident now known professionally as Jaimie Alexander}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.mattsmoviereviews.net/spotlight-interview-thor.html|title=Interview with Thor actors Jaimie Alexander and Tom Hiddelston|work=Trespass Magazine|first=Matthew|last=Pejkovic|accessdate=2011-08-16}}</ref>

亚历山大读小学时就沉迷于戏剧表演，读高中時因为不能唱歌而被戏剧高中踢出。<ref name="movieline">{{cite web |last=VanAirsdale |first=S.T. |date=2011-05-23 |title=Jaimie Alexander on Her Thor Breakthrough and Visions of Lara Croft |url=http://www.movieline.com/2011/05/jaimie-alexander-on-her-thor-breakthrough-and-visions-of-lara-croft.php |work=[[Movieline|Movieline]] |accessdate=2011-05-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110527011043/http://www.movieline.com/2011/05/jaimie-alexander-on-her-thor-breakthrough-and-visions-of-lara-croft.php |archive-date=2011-05-27 |dead-url=yes }}</ref> 

当她17岁的时候，她就被星探发掘，经理兰迪·詹姆斯赠送给他一些剧本，毕业后，她搬到洛杉矶开始演艺生涯。<ref name="movieline" />

'''杰克逊-格威尔特奖章'''（{{lang-en|'''Jackson-Gwilt Medal'''}}）是由[[英國皇家天文學會|皇家天文学会]]（RAS）颁发的奖项，设立于1897年。最初的标准是奖励对天文仪器或技术的发明、改进或发展，在[[观测天文学|观测天文学]]或[[天文学史|天文学史]]领域的成就。从2017年起，天文学史从该奖项的评奖范围中移除，转移到了一个新奖项——[[英國皇家天文學會|艾格尼丝·玛丽·克拉克奖章]]。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ras.org.uk/awards-and-grants/awards/2821-history-medal|title=Agnes Mary Clerke Medal for Historical Research in Astronomy or Geophysics|accessdate=7 September 2017|language=en-gb|last1=Webb|first1=Annette|website=www.ras.org.uk}}</ref>

该奖项以汉娜·杰克逊的名字命名（ ''[[名字|姓]]''Gwilt）。她是约瑟夫·格威尔特（建筑师，[[皇家天文学会院士|皇家天文学会院士]]）的侄女和乔治·格威尔特（皇家天文学会院士）的女儿，也是奖章的最初捐助者。<ref name="jgm-award">{{cite web|last1=Down|first1=Emily|title=Jackson-Gwilt Medal|url=http://www.ras.org.uk/awards-and-grants/awards/2275-jackson-gwilt-medal|website=www.ras.org.uk|publisher=[[Royal_Astronomical_Society|Royal Astronomical Society]]|accessdate=7 September 2017|language=en-gb|quote=The Jackson-Gwilt Medal, after the Gold Medal the oldest offered by the Society, derives from a gift by Mrs Hannah Jackson-Gwilt, niece of the well-known architect and former fellow, Joseph Gwilt. She gave the Society a capital sum which was to be used after her death for the award of a medal and a cash prize.}}</ref>

'''杰克逊·阿利·马丁内斯·巴伦西亚'''（{{lang-es|'''Jackson Arley Martínez Valencia'''}}，{{bd|1986年|10月3日|||Martinez, Jackson}}），是一名[[哥伦比亚|哥伦比亚]]职业足球运动员，司职[[前锋_(足球)|前锋]]，現時被[[中国足球超级联赛|中国足球超级联赛]]球会[[广州恒大淘宝足球俱乐部|广州恒大淘宝]]外借至[[葡超|葡超]][[樸迪莫倫斯體育會|樸迪莫倫斯]]，他曾是[[哥倫比亞國家足球隊|哥倫比亞國家隊]]成員。

他因為在場上跳舞慶祝入球的動作而被戲稱為"Cha Cha Cha"。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sofoot.com/jackson-martinez-lautre-buteur-colombien-167659.html |title=Jackson Martínez, l’autre buteur colombien - Coupes d'Europes |publisher=SO FOOT.com |date=2013-03-13 |accessdate=2014-04-15}}</ref> 著名球評家[[Tim_Vickery|Tim Vickery]]形容他是一個的out-and-out射手，「一個可以用左右兩腳取得入球的[[前鋒_(足球)#中鋒|中鋒]]。」<ref>{{cite web|last=Vickery|first=Tim|title=Neymar a match for Messi on Day of the Goal|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/blogs/timvickery/2012/03/neymar_a_match_for_messi_on_da.html#more|publisher=BBC|accessdate=17 March 2012}}</ref> 他在[[波爾圖足球俱樂部|波爾圖]]效力三個賽季都成為[[葡超|葡超]]最佳射手。

马丁内斯在2009年開始為哥倫比亞國家隊上場，分別參加[[2011年美洲國家盃|2011]]和[[2015年美洲盃足球賽|2015年美洲盃]]決賽周，也參加了[[2014年世界盃|2014年世界盃]]。

==俱樂部生涯==

===麦德林独立===
马丁内斯在[[哥伦比亚足球甲级联赛|哥伦比亚足球甲级联赛]]球队[[麦德林独立|麦德林独立]]开始职业足球生涯。他在2009赛季第二阶段联赛中射进18球，成为[[哥倫比亞足球甲級聯賽|哥倫比亞足球甲級聯賽]]最佳射手，幫助麦德林独立奪取第五個聯賽冠軍之餘也打破[[Léider_Preciado|莱德·普莱西亚多]]保持的17个进球的最高纪录。<ref>[http://www.futbolred.com/mustang/noticias/finalvuelta09ii/con-18-tantos-jackson-martinez-fue-el-goleador-del-torneo-finalizacion/6809267 Con 18 tantos, Jackson Martínez fue el goleador del torneo Finalización]</ref>
===恰帕斯美洲虎===
在哥倫比亞經過數訶賽季的鍛練以後，馬丁內斯受到阿根廷和墨西哥俱樂部的注視。2009年下半年，麦德林独立和[[K联赛|K联赛]]球队[[蔚山现代足球俱乐部|蔚山现代]]就马丁内斯的转会事宜达成协议，<ref>[http://www.caracol.com.co/noticias/deportes/jackson-martinez-jugara-en-el-ulsan-hyunday-de-corea/20091119/nota/912447.aspx Jackson Martinez jugará en el Ulsan Hyunday de Corea]</ref> 但由于合同条款并没有达成一致，最终马丁内斯没有加盟蔚山现代。<ref>[http://www.terra.com.co/deportes/articulo/html/dep729506-jackson-martinez-ya-no-iria-al-ulsan-corea.htm Jackson Martínez ya no iría al Ulsan Corea]</ref> 2010年，他最終转会加盟[[墨西哥足球甲级联赛|墨西哥足球甲级联赛]]球队[[恰帕斯美洲虎|恰帕斯美洲虎]]，他在第一個賽季最終打進9球，幫助俱樂部以第四名完成賽事。在[[2011年南美自由盃|2011年南美自由盃]]，作為恰帕斯核心球員的馬丁尼斯協助俱樂部打進四分之一決賽。他在隨後的[[2011年至2012年墨西哥足球甲級聯賽#夏季聯賽|2012年春季联赛]]成为球队队长，最終在該賽季打進30個進球。他在墨西哥效力期間的出色表現令他獲得[[哥倫比亞國家足球隊|哥倫比亞]]的徵召，也同時獲得葡萄牙班霸[[波爾圖足球俱樂部|波爾圖]]的關注。

In the final round of the year's Champions League group stage, Martínez scored a goal against [[Paris_Saint-Germain_F.C.|Paris Saint-Germain]] in a 2–1 defeat. Despite the loss, Porto advanced to the next round as the second-placed club in their group. Against [[Moreirense_F.C.|Moreirense]], he scored a goal following an assist from James Rodríguez.

In the beginning of the second half of the league, Martínez scored a header in a 1–0 victory over [[C.D._Nacional|Nacional]]. He then played in ''[[O_Clássico|O Clássico]]'' against rivals [[S.L._Benfica|Benfica]], where he scored in a 2–2 draw. In the delayed match against [[Vitória_F.C.|Vitória de Setúbal]], Martínez scored a double, one via the penalty spot. In his next match, he sealed a 5–0 victory with a 90th minute goal against [[Gil_Vicente_F.C.|Gil Vicente]]. He scored a hat-trick against Vitória de Guimarães, which included two headers, making it his first hat-trick for Porto. At the time, he had scored 18 goals in 17 matches in the [[Primeira_Liga|Primeira Liga]]. Jackson continued his goal-scoring streak after netting the equaliser in a 1–1 draw with Olhanense, followed up with another against Beira-Mar one week later. Martínez added two more goals against Rio Ave, including a penalty and a volley following a cross by James Rodríguez. For his prolific scoring pace, Martínez was named the February [[SJPF_Player_of_the_Month|SJPF Player of the Month]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dn.pt/desporto/porto/interior.aspx?content_id=3081367 |title=Jackson Martínez eleito o melhor jogador de fevereiro - Desporto - DN |publisher=Dn.pt |date= |accessdate=2014-04-15 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150610221929/http://www.dn.pt/desporto/porto/interior.aspx?content_id=3081367 |archivedate=2015-06-10 }}</ref>

Martínez made his [[Taça_da_Liga|Taça da Liga]] debut in the [[2012–13_Taça_da_Liga|2012–13 Taça da Liga]] semi-finals against Rio Ave, where he failed to score a goal. Regardless, Porto won the match 4–0 and advanced to the finals. He ended his goal drought in a 0–3 away victory over Moreirense, where he scored a brace.

In the final match of the league season against [[F.C._Paços_de_Ferreira|Paços de Ferreira]], Martínez sealed the 2–0 victory after scoring the second goal and assuring Porto's third consecutive league title, finishing just one point ahead of rivals Benfica, who had also won their final match. The goal marked his 26th goal in the league, securing him top goal-scoring honours. With winning the [[Supertaça_Cândido_de_Oliveira|Portuguese SuperCup]] earlier in the season, finishing as top goal scorer and winning the league title (in just his debut season in Europe), Martínez had a successful season debut.<ref>http://pt.fifa.com/worldfootball/clubfootball/news/newsid=2081974.html?intcmp=newsreader_news_box_1 {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140416191702/http://pt.fifa.com/worldfootball/clubfootball/news/newsid%3D2081974.html?intcmp=newsreader_news_box_1 |date=2014-04-16 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/memberassociations/association=por/news/newsid=1954050.html?rss=1954050+Unbeaten+Porto+wrap+up+27th+league+title |title=Unbeaten Porto wrap up 27th league title |publisher=UEFA.com |date=2013-05-19 |accessdate=2014-04-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Porto claim Portuguese title, Benfica win |url=http://www.fifa.com/world-match-centre/nationalleagues/nationalleague=portugal-liga-2000000033/news/newsid/208/202/6/index.html?intcmp=newsreader_news_box_1 |publisher=FIFA |accessdate=30 June 2015 |date=19 May 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160121110415/http://www.fifa.com/world-match-centre/nationalleagues/nationalleague%3Dportugal-liga-2000000033/news/newsid/208/202/6/index.html?intcmp=newsreader_news_box_1 |archivedate=21 一月 2016 }}</ref>

====2014–15賽季====

In the final minute and added time, he scored a penalty and then equalised for the final score of 2–2, which was dubbed "stunning" and "remarkable" by [[UEFA|UEFA]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Martínez earns Porto stunning draw at Shakhtar|url=http://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/season=2015/matches/round=2000548/match=2014337/postmatch/report/|accessdate=1 October 2014|publisher=[[UEFA|UEFA]]|date=30 September 2014}}</ref> In the quarter-final first leg on 15 April 2015, he scored the last goal in a 3–1 victory over [[FC_Bayern_Munich|Bayern Munich]], also being fouled by goalkeeper [[Manuel_Neuer|Manuel Neuer]] for the penalty that [[Ricardo_Quaresma|Ricardo Quaresma]] ultimately converted for the opening goal.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Hafez|first1=Shamoon|title=FC Porto 3-1 Bayern Mun|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/32295979|accessdate=15 April 2015|work=BBC Sport|date=15 April 2015}}</ref> Martínez netted again in the second leg six days later, heading a consolation from [[Héctor_Herrera_(footballer)|Héctor Herrera]]'s cross as Porto lost 1–6 in [[Munich|Munich]], thus being eliminated from the competition.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Begley|first1=Emlyn|title=Bayern Mun 6-1 FC Porto|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/32380841|accessdate=22 April 2015|work=BBC Sport|date=21 April 2015}}</ref>

===馬德里競技===
Martínez had been reportedly linked with a move to Spanish side [[Atlético_Madrid|Atlético Madrid]] and on 27 June 2015, he told the press the Madrid side deal was "done," also stating he was "excited about this move."<ref>http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/33300271</ref> It was later announced by Porto's official website that Atlético would pay the full release fee for Martínez, which would be worth €35 million.<ref>http://www.fcporto.pt/en/noticias/Pages/Atletico-de-Madrid-vai-exercer-clausula-de-rescisao-de-Jackson.aspx</ref> On 15 July, Atlético officially announced the transfer.<ref>http://www.clubatleticodemadrid.com/noticias/acuerdo-con-el-porto-para-el-traspaso-de-jackson-martinez</ref> He made his [[La_Liga|La Liga]] debut on 22 August, being substituted after an hour for [[Fernando_Torres|Fernando Torres]] in a 1–0 home win over [[UD_Las_Palmas|UD Las Palmas]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Atl Madrid 1–0 Las Palmas|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/34011857|accessdate=23 August 2015|date=22 August 2015}}</ref> A week later, Martínez came off the bench and scored his first goal for Atlético in a 3–0 victory over [[Sevilla_FC|Sevilla FC]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Sevilla 0-3 Atletico Madrid: Jackson off the mark in La Liga as Llorente's return ends in defeat|url=http://www.goal.com/en-gb/match/sevilla-vs-atl%C3%A9tico-madrid/2086276/report|accessdate=22 October 2015|publisher=Goal.com|date=30 August 2015}}</ref>

===广州恒大===
马丁内斯在哥伦比亚足球甲级联赛球队麦德林独立开始职业足球生涯。他在2009赛季第二阶段联赛中射进18球，成为联赛最佳射手，并打破莱德·普莱西亚多保持的17个进球的最高纪录。2009年下半年，麦德林独立和K联赛球队蔚山现代就马丁内斯的转会事宜达成协议，但由于合同条款并没有达成一致，最终马丁内斯没有加盟蔚山现代。2010年，他转会加盟墨西哥足球甲级联赛球队恰帕斯美洲虎，2012年春季联赛成为球队队长。
2012年7月7日，马丁内斯以880万欧元的身价加盟葡萄牙足球超级联赛球队波尔图，违约金为3500万欧元。
2015年7月1日，葡超球队波尔图在官网上宣布，西甲球队马德里竞技激活了球员马丁内斯的3500万欧违约金条款，这名哥伦比亚国脚将成为床单军团一员，他将签约4年。在过去三个赛季，他代表波尔图出场133次，打入92球，这名前锋的到来也将接替曼朱基齐离队的位置。
2015年7月16日，西甲球队马德里竞技官方宣布，杰克逊·马丁内斯正式加盟。直到年底，代表马德里竞技各项比赛出场22次仅仅打入3球并有2次助攻，没有表现前葡超金靴的实力。
2016年2月3日，中超冠军广州恒大以4200万欧元的价格引进马丁内斯，成为了中超历史上的新标王。之后其中超转会身价记录陆续被江苏苏宁引入的[[阿莱士·特谢拉|阿莱士·特谢拉]]和上海上港引入的[[浩克_(足球运动员)|浩克]]打破。但马丁内斯来到[[广州恒大|广州恒大]]后并没有发挥出他应有的水平，更应为伤病被伤愈复出的另一个前锋[[阿兰|阿兰]]取代他的首发位置。2018年夏天，他被租借到[[樸迪莫倫斯體育會|樸迪莫倫斯]]。

==国家队生涯==
年僅22歲的马丁内斯在2009年8月26日第一次收到[[哥伦比亚国家足球队|哥伦比亚国家足球队]]的征召，参加同年9月5日对阵[[厄瓜多尔国家足球队|厄瓜多尔]]和[[乌拉圭国家足球队|乌拉圭]]的[[2010年世界盃外圍賽_(南美洲區)|2010年世界盃外圍賽]]比赛<ref>[http://colombia.golgolgol.net/Futbol_Sudamericano/Eliminatorias_al_Mundial/Noticias/Giovanni_Moreno__principal_novedad_de_la_nueva_convocatoria_de_Lara__125-68262.html Giovanni Moreno, principal novedad de la nueva convocatoria de Lara] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20130703055122/http://colombia.golgolgol.net/Futbol_Sudamericano/Eliminatorias_al_Mundial/Noticias/Giovanni_Moreno__principal_novedad_de_la_nueva_convocatoria_de_Lara__125-68262.html |date=2013-07-03 }}</ref> 9月5日，马丁内斯在哥伦比亚与厄瓜多尔的比赛中替补出场，并在81分钟首开纪录，射进职业生涯的首个国家队比赛进球，帮助哥伦比亚最终2比0击败对手。<ref>[http://colombia.golgolgol.net/Futbol_Sudamericano/Eliminatorias_al_Mundial/En_Vivo/home_175.html Colombia vs Ecuador] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20130502074310/http://colombia.golgolgol.net/Futbol_Sudamericano/Eliminatorias_al_Mundial/En_Vivo/home_175.html |date=2013-05-02 }}</ref> 9月9日，他在和乌拉圭的比赛中再进一球，但最终哥伦比亚2比4不敌对手。<ref>[http://www.ar.terra.com/eliminatorias2010/interna/0,,OI3966547-EI10327,00.html Colombia cae ante Uruguay 3-1 y se aleja del Mundial]</ref> 2011年6月7日，他被時任主教練[[埃爾南·達里奧·高梅茲|埃爾南·達里奧·高梅茲]]選入哥倫比亞在[[2011年美洲國家盃|2011年美洲國家盃]]決賽周的23人大軍。<ref>[http://www.record.xl.pt/Futebol/Nacional/1a_liga/Porto//interior.aspx?content_id=701188 Falcão e Guarín na Copa América (Falcão and Guarín at the Copa América)] {{Wayback|url=http://www.record.xl.pt/Futebol/Nacional/1a_liga/Porto//interior.aspx?content_id=701188 |date=20140221205128 }}; [[Record_(newspaper)|Record]], 7 June 2011 (Portuguese)</ref>

2014年6月2日，马丁内斯被選入哥倫比亞在[[2014年世界杯足球賽|2014年世界杯足球賽]]的23人大軍。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://msn.foxsports.com/soccer/story/recovering-falcao-left-off-colombia-s-final-23-player-world-cup-roster-060214|title=Recovering Falcao left off Colombia's final 23-player World Cup roster|date=2 June 2014|accessdate=26 June 2014|work=Fox Sports}}</ref> 在小組賽第一場以後替補身份上場協助國家隊擊敗[[希臘國家足球隊|希臘]]以後，马丁内斯在小組賽最後一場面對[[日本國家足球隊|日本隊]]時，他分別在下半場第55、82分鐘梅開二度，協助國家隊4以2比大勝對手。<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/football/world-cup/10923335/Japan-vs-Colombia-Jackson-Martinez-scores-twice-as-Carlos-Valderramas-side-top-group-to-set-up-Uruguay-tie.html|title=Jackson Martínez scores twice as Carlos Valderrama's side top group to set up Uruguay tie|date=24 June 2014|work=Daily Telegraph|accessdate=25 June 2014}}</ref>

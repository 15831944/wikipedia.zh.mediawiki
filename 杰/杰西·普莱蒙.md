'''杰西·普莱蒙'''（{{lang-en|'''Jesse Plemons'''}}，{{bd|1988年|4月2日|||catIdx= P普莱蒙}}）是一名美国男演员。

== 生平 ==
1988年，普莱蒙出生于[[美国|美国]][[德克萨斯州|德克萨斯州]][[达拉斯|达拉斯]]，后来居住到奥斯丁，一直至今。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://featuresblogs.chicagotribune.com/entertainment_tv/2007/03/jesse_plemons.html|title=Jesse Plemons of 'Friday Night Lights'|accessdate=2008-11-15|date=March 20, 2007|work=[[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]]|first = Maureen|last = Ryan}}</ref>

2007年，普莱蒙在[[德州理工大学|德州理工大学]]独立学区毕业，<ref>{{cite press release |title= ‘Friday Night Lights’ Star Earns Diploma at a Distance Through Texas Tech Independent School District|publisher= [[Texas_Tech_University|Texas Tech University]]|date= May 1, 2007|url= http://www.newswise.com/articles/view/529567|accessdate= 2008-11-15}}</ref> 他多才多艺，会弹[[吉他|吉他]]、唱牛仔歌和[[印第安人|印第安人]]的歌曲。<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.variety.com/article/VR1117978526.html?categoryid=2031&cs=1| title = Jesse Plemons| accessdate = 2008-11-15| date = January 6, 2008| publisher = ''[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]''}}</ref>

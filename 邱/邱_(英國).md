'''邱'''（Kew {{IPAc-en|k|ju:}}），或譯'''基尤'''，是英國[[倫敦|倫敦]][[泰晤士河畔列治文區|泰晤士河畔列治文區]]下屬一區<ref name="GLA 2002">{{cite book | url=http://www.london.gov.uk/sites/default/files/archives/uploads-tr11_villages.pdf | title=A City of Villages: Promoting a sustainable future for London's suburbs | publisher=[[Greater_London_Authority|Greater London Authority]] | work=SDS Technical Report 11 | ISBN= 1-85261-393-9 |date=August 2002 | accessdate=16 January 2014}}</ref>，是倫敦的郊區之一<ref name="Vision">{{cite web | url=http://www.visionofbritain.org.uk/place/1963 | title=History of Kew, in Richmond upon Thames and Surrey | publisher=GB Historical GIS / [[University_of_Portsmouth|University of Portsmouth]] | work=A Vision of Britain through Time. | accessdate=3 June 2014}}</ref>，位于[[查令十字|查令十字]]西南{{convert|7.1|mi|km|1}}處。[[邱園|邱園]]位于此地。[[法國大革命|法國大革命]]發生時有很多難民搬來，但現在已經是倫敦的富人區之一，此地沒有摩天大樓，綠化良好。

“Kew”（最初寫作“Cayho”）一詞源自[[古法語|古法語]]“kai”（渡口）和[[古英語|古英語]]“hoh”（突出的河岸）。<ref>[[Adrian_Room|Room, Adrian]]: ''Dictionary of Place-Names in the British Isles'', [[Bloomsbury_Publishing|Bloomsbury]], 1988, {{ISBN|978-0747501701}}</ref>

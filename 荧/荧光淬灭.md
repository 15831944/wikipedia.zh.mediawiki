'''荧光淬灭'''（{{Lang-en|'''Quenching'''}}）猝滅是當常溫物質經射線照射（通常為紫外線或X射線）所發出會[[螢光|螢光]]強度降低的任何過程。

淬滅受壓力和溫度的影響很大，許多過程都可導致淬滅，例如[[激發態|激發態]]反應，能量轉移，[[複合物|複合物]]形成和碰撞淬滅。氧分子，[[碘離子|碘離子]]和[[丙烯酰胺|丙烯酰胺]]是常見的[[化學猝滅劑|化學猝滅劑]]<ref>''Acrylamide and iodide fluorescence quenching as a structural probe of tryptophan microenvironment in bovine lens crystallins.'' Phillips SR, Wilson LJ, Borkman RF. Curr Eye Res. 1986 Aug;5(8):611-9.</ref> 。眾所周知的[[奎寧|奎寧]]熒光猝滅劑則是[[氯離子|氯離子]]。<ref>''Fluorescence experiments with quinine'' James E. O'Reilly J. Chem. Educ., 1975, 52 (9), p 610 {{DOI|10.1021/ed052p610}}</ref><ref>''Photophysics in a disco: Luminescence quenching of quinine'' LouAnn Sacksteder , R. M. Ballew , Elizabeth A. Brown , J. N. Demas , D. Nesselrodt and B. A. DeGraff J. Chem. Educ., 1990, 67 (12), p 1065 {{DOI|10.1021/ed067p1065}}</ref><ref>''Halide (Cl-) Quenching of Quinine Sulfate Fluorescence: A Time-Resolved Fluorescence Experiment for Physical Chemistry'' Jonathan H. Gutow J. Chem. Educ., 2005, 82 (2), p 302 {{DOI|10.1021/ed082p302}}</ref> 

有一系列效应会导致荧光淬灭，例如：
* 复杂结构
* 内部转换
* 能量转移到其他分子，所谓的[[灭剂|灭剂]]

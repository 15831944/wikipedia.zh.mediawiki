'''查理·湯瑪士·考克斯'''（{{lang-en|Charlie Thomas Cox}}，{{bd|1982年|12月15日|}}）是一名[[英國|英國]]男演員。最著名代表作為[[2007年電影|2007年電影]]《[[星塵傳奇|星塵傳奇]]》，飾演主角崔斯坦·索恩。考克斯表示自從拍攝本片，與女主角[[克萊兒·丹妮絲|克萊兒·丹妮絲]]成為親密友人。<ref>"[http://www.happenmagazine.com/magazine/article2.aspx?articleid=8829 Happen Magazine]", Accessed 22 August 2007.</ref>

2014年5月27日，宣布考克斯將於漫威漫畫改編的影集《[[夜魔俠_(電視劇)|夜魔俠]]》中主演[[夜魔俠|夜魔俠]]，該劇於2015年4月10日首播<ref>{{cite web|url= http://variety.com/2014/digital/news/charlie-cox-to-star-in-daredevil-tv-series-for-marvel-and-netflix-1201194139/|title=Charlie Cox to Star in ''Daredevil'' TV Series for Marvel and Netflix|publisher=''[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]]''|date=May 27, 2014|accessdate=May 27, 2014|archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20140911182408/http://variety.com/2014/digital/news/charlie-cox-to-star-in-daredevil-tv-series-for-marvel-and-netflix-1201194139/|archivedate= September 11, 2014|deadurl=no}}</ref>。他也在[[HBO|HBO]]電視劇《[[酒私風雲|酒私風雲]]》第二、三季中飾演Owen Sleater<ref>{{Cite web |url = https://www.yahoo.com/entertainment/news/boardwalk-empire-alum-charlie-cox-cast-john-cusack-212919169.html |title = ‘Boardwalk Empire’ Alum Charlie Cox Cast in John Cusack's CBS Pilot |author = Tim Kenneally |date = 2014-02-22 |publisher = yahoo |language = en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url = https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/boardwalk-empires-charlie-cox-star-682362 |title = 'Boardwalk Empire's' Charlie Cox to Star in CBS' Wall Street Drama |author = Lesley Goldberg |date = 2014-02-21 |publisher = The Hollywood Reporter |language = en}}</ref>。

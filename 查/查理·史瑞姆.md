'''查理·史瑞姆'''（{{lang-en|'''Charlie Shrem'''}}，{{bd|1989年|11月25日}}<ref name="mraskin">Max Raskin, [http://www.businessweek.com/articles/2013-04-10/meet-the-bitcoin-millionaires “Meet the Bitcoin Millionaires,”] ''[[Bloomberg_Businessweek|Bloomberg Businessweek]]'', 2013-04-10.</ref>），[[美國|美國]][[企業家|企業家]]和[[比特幣|比特幣]]倡議者，他是{{link-en|BitInstant}}的聯合創始人，亦是{{link-en|比特幣基金會|Bitcoin Foundation}}的創始成員和前副主席。2014年，美國政府致力打擊[[絲路_(購物網站)|絲路]]網站上的匿名[[毒品|毒品]]交易，將史瑞姆以「協助和教唆營運未經許可的轉帳業務」的罪名判監兩年<ref>{{cite news|author1=Nate Raymond|title=Bitcoin backer gets two years prison for illicit transfers|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/2014/12/19/us-usa-crime-bitcoin-idUSKBN0JX2CW20141219|accessdate=2014-12-20|work=Reuters|publisher=Thompson Reuters|date=2014-12-19}}</ref>。

==早年生活==
史瑞姆在[[紐約|紐約]][[布魯克林|布魯克林]]出生及長大<ref name="lifeboat.com">{{cite web|url=http://lifeboat.com/ex/bios.charlie.shrem |title=Lifeboat Foundation Bios: Charlie Shrem |publisher=Lifeboat.com |date= |accessdate=2014-01-29}}</ref>，擁有敘利亞猶太裔血統<ref name="mkaminer">Michael Kaminer, [http://forward.com/articles/193160/bitcoins-jewish-whiz-kid/?p=all “Bitcoin’s Jewish Whiz Kid,”] ''[[The_Forward|The Forward]]'', 2014-02-23.</ref><ref name="rsidel"/>，2012年[[布鲁克林学院|布鲁克林学院]]畢業，擁有經濟和金融學士學位<ref name="lifeboat.com"/><ref name="rsidel">Robin Sidel, [https://www.wsj.com/news/articles/SB10001424052702304450904579365201803918312 “Hard Times for a Bitcoin Evangelist,”] {{Wayback|url=https://www.wsj.com/news/articles/SB10001424052702304450904579365201803918312 |date=20170111220333 }} ''[[Wall_Street_Journal|Wall Street Journal]]'', 2014-02-05.</ref>。

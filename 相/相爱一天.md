'''《相爱一天》''' ({{lang-th|แฟนเดย์ แฟนกันแค่วันเดียว}}, [[皇家泰語轉寫通用系統|RTGS]]: Fanday…Fan Kun Khae Wan Diaw) 是由{{le|班庄·比辛达拿刚|Banjong Pisanthanakun}}执导的2016年泰国浪漫电影，由[[辰塔维·塔纳西维|辰塔维·塔纳西维]]和[[Mew_Nittha|尼塔莎·吉拉尤吉恩]]主演。它讲述的是一个男人在他梦中情人暂时失忆的一天时间里，不顾一切假装是她男朋友，和她相处一天的故事。<ref>{{cite web|first1=Silvia|last1=Wong|title=Banjong's ''One Day'' on GDH's debut slate|url=http://www.screendaily.com/news/banjongs-one-day-on-gdhs-debut-slate/5107175.article|website=[[Screendaily.com|Screendaily.com]]|accessdate=2016-10-26|date=2 August 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first1=Pajee|last1=Parinyaporn|title=Love on the slopes|url=http://www.nationmultimedia.com/news/life/box_office/30292450|website=[[The_Nation_(Thailand)|The Nation]]|accessdate=2016-10-27|date=9 August 2016}}</ref>

2016年10月，班庄与两位主演辰塔维（Chantavit）和尼塔莎（Nittha）一起在新加坡宣传这部爱情片。这部电影也是Nittha首次在大银幕上亮相。<ref>{{cite web|first1=Jing Yi|last1=Hon|title=Shutter director Banjong Pisanthanakun gets romantic with new movie|url=http://www.todayonline.com/entertainment/movies/shutter-director-banjong-pisanthanakun-gets-romantic-new-movie-0|website=[[Today_(Singapore_newspaper)|Today]]|accessdate=2016-10-26|date=24 October 2016}}</ref>

== 剧情 ==
Denchai（[[辰塔维·塔纳西维|辰塔维·塔纳西维]]饰）爱上了他的同事Nui（[[Mew_Nittha|Nittha Jirayungyurn]]饰），但没有勇气表达对她的感情。他在公司旅行中终于有了机会。他只想和Nui在一起一天，他的愿望成真了。<ref>{{cite web|title=New Thai Movie from GDH ''One Day''|url=http://www.wowthaipop.com/gdh-fanday-new-thai-movie/|website=WOW Thai pop|accessdate=2016-10-26|date=21 August 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Lead Cast and Director of ''One Day'' to Promote Movie in Singapore!|url=https://www.incinemas.sg/article-details.aspx?id=2439|website=InCinemas.sg|accessdate=2016-10-26|date=15 October 2016}}</ref>

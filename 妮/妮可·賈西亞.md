'''妮可·賈西亞'''（{{lang-fr|Nicole Garcia}})是法國女演員與導演。

== 生平 ==
1998年，妮可·賈西亞執導的第三部劇情長片《{{link-fr|五克拉愛情|Place Vendôme (film)}}》獲選為[[威尼斯影展|第55屆威尼斯影展]]正式競賽片，女主角[[凱撒琳·丹尼芙|凱撒琳·丹尼芙]]獲得最佳女演員獎<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.americancinemapapers.com/files/VENICE_1998.htm|title=VENICE – 1998 – SPEARS OF LIGHT|accessdate=6 October 2013}}</ref>；她曾以《{{link-fr|被告 (2002年電影)|L'Adversaire (film, 2002)|被告}}》(2002)、《{{link-fr|查理的意見|Selon Charlie}}》(2006)與《{{link-fr|熾戀|Mal de pierres}}》(2016)三度進入[[坎城影展|坎城影展]]競賽單元<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.infoplease.com/ipa/A0906761.html|title=2002 Cannes Film Festival  |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref><ref name=IMDB>{{cite web |url=http://www.imdb.com/Sections/Awards/Cannes_Film_Festival/2002 |title=Cannes Film Festival: 2002 |publisher=Internet Movie Database |accessdate=25 May 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/4359864/year/2006.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Charlie Says |accessdate=2009-12-13|work=festival-cannes.com}}</ref><ref name="IW">{{cite web |url=http://www.indiewire.com/article/cannes-live-stream-2016-film-festival-lineup-list |title=2016 Cannes Film Festival Announces Lineup |accessdate=14 April 2016 |work=IndieWire}}</ref><ref name="VarCannes">{{cite web |url=http://variety.com/2016/film/festivals/cannes-film-festival-official-selection-lineup-2016-1201753269/ |title=Cannes 2016: Film Festival Unveils Official Selection Lineup |accessdate=14 April 2016 |work=Variety}}</ref>。

'''妮塔·穆克什·安巴尼'''（{{Lang-en|'''Nita Mukesh Ambani'''}}，婚前名為妮塔·達勒爾，{{bd|1963年|11月1日|catIdx=Ambani, Nita}}）是[[印度|印度]]女商人、活动家、慈善家。<ref>[https://yourstory.com/2016/11/story-of-nita-ambani/amp “Nita Ambani – the caterpillar that morphed into a bu…siness woman”]. Your Story. 01-11-2016.</ref>她亦是[[信实工业|信实工业]]旗下信实基金會（Reliance Foundation）的主席。<ref>[https://www.reliancefoundation.org/who-we-are “Message from Chairperson – Nita Ambani”]. Reliance Foundation.</ref>

安巴尼出生於印度中產家庭，父母均為[[古吉拉特邦|古吉拉特人]]。她的丈夫是印度首富[[穆克什·安巴尼|穆克什·安巴尼]]，<ref>[https://www.forbes.com/profile/mukesh-ambani/?list=india-billionaires “Profile - Mukesh Ambani”]. Forbes.</ref>婚前她是一名小學教師。<ref>[https://www.timesofindia.com/life-style/spotlight/The-private-life-of-Nita-Ambani/amp_articleshow/10288259.cms “The private life of Nita Ambani”]{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}. Times of India. 24-04-2017.</ref>安巴尼和丈夫育有兩子一女。

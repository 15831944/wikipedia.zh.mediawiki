'''妮迪·阿加瓦爾'''（英語：Nidhhi Agerwal，{{bd|1993年|8月17日|catIdx=Agerwal, Nidhhi}}）是[[印度|印度]]模特、舞者和演员，曾出演[[宝莱坞|宝莱坞]]和[[泰卢固语|泰卢固語]]电影。 2017年她在电影《''{{tsl|en|Munna Michael||Munna Michael}}''》中首次亮相。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.deccanchronicle.com/entertainment/bollywood/150816/tiger-shroffs-heroine-for-munna-michael-has-been-confirmed.html|title=Confirmed! Tiger Shroff to romance Nidhhi Agerwal in are Munna Michael|author=|first=|date=15 Aug 2016|work={{tsl|en|Deccan Chronicle||Deccan Chronicle}}|accessdate=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.firstpost.com/entertainment/munna-michael-starring-tiger-shroff-nawazuddin-siddiqui-to-release-on-21-july-3396758.html|title=Munna Michael starring Tiger Shroff, Nawazuddin Siddiqui to release on 21 July|author=|first=|date=21 April 2017|work=[[Firstpost|Firstpost]]|accessdate=}}</ref> 她是2014年雅马哈魅力女神（Yamaha Fascino Miss Diva）决赛选手。<ref name="budding star">{{Cite news|date=8 November 2017|title=Nidhhi Agerwal: Steamy pictures of the budding star|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/photo-feature/nidhhi-agerwal-steamy-pictures-of-the-budding-star/beauty-in-blue/photostory/61560747.cms|work=The Times of India|accessdate=25 August 2018}}</ref>

== 早年生活 ==
她出生在印度[[海得拉巴_(印度)|海德拉巴]]，於[[班加羅爾|班加罗尔]]长大。曾在Vidyashilp學院（Vidyashilp Academy）和Vidya Niketan學校上学（Vidya Niketan School）。她毕业于{{tsl|en|Christ University||班加羅爾基督大学}}工商管理专业。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://beautypageants.indiatimes.com/miss-diva/miss-diva-contestants/2014/Nidhhi-Agerwal/profile/42481675.cms|title=Nidhhi Agerwal|author=|first=|date=|work={{tsl|en|Times Internet||Indiatimes}}|archiveurl=|archivedate=|accessdate=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://beautypageants.indiatimes.com/Tiger-Shroff-to-romance-Nidhhi-Agerwal-in-Munna-Michael/eventshow/53797004.cms|title=Tiger Shroff to romance Nidhhi Agerwal in Munna Michael|author=|first=|date=21 August 2016|work={{tsl|en|Times Internet||Indiatimes}}|accessdate=|archiveurl=|archivedate=|deadurl=}}</ref>她在[[芭蕾舞|芭蕾舞]]、{{tsl|en|Kathak||Kathak舞}}和[[肚皮舞|肚皮舞]]方面训练有素。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://beautypageants.indiatimes.com/I-always-wanted-to-be-an-actor-Nidhhi-Agerwal/I-always-wanted-to-be-an-actor-Nidhhi-Agerwal/eventshow/54998665.cms|title=I always wanted to be an actor - Nidhhi Agerwal|author=|first=|date=22 October 2016|work={{tsl|en|Times Internet||Indiatimes}}|accessdate=|archiveurl=|archivedate=|deadurl=}}</ref>

== 事业 ==
2016年导演{{tsl|en|Sabbir Khan||薩比爾·可汗}}證实，阿加瓦尔将和[[泰格·史洛夫|泰格·史洛夫]]一起出演他的电影《''{{tsl|en|Munna Michael||Munna Michael}}''》。她是从300名候选人中選拔出来的。关于她对表演的热情，她说：“我一直想成为一名演员。每次我看到[[艾西瓦娅·雷|艾西瓦娅·雷]]出現廣告板裡，我就告诉自己，总有一天我的脸也会出现在那里”。她也被要求在电影拍攝结束前签署不约会条款。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://movies.ndtv.com/bollywood/tiger-shroffs-munna-michael-co-star-niddhi-agerwal-made-to-sign-no-dating-clause-1470855|title=Tiger Shroff's Munna Michael Co-Star Niddhi Agerwal Made To Sign No Dating Clause!|author=|first=|date=6 October 2016|work=[[新德里电视台|NDTV]]|accessdate=|archiveurl=|archivedate=|deadurl=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Actress-Nidhhi-Agerwal-okay-with-no-dating-clause/articleshow/54733603.cms|title=Actress Nidhhi Agerwal okay with no mating clause|author=|first=|date=29 Jan 2017|work={{tsl|en|Times Internet||Indiatimes}}|accessdate=|archiveurl=|archivedate=|deadurl=}}</ref>她还签下了由{{tsl|en|Shree Narayan Singh||斯里·納拉揚·辛格}}执导的一部{{tsl|en|KriArj Entertainment||KriArj娛樂}}的电影。这将是她的第二部宝莱坞电影和繼電影《{{tsl|en|Toilet: Ek Prem Katha||''Toilet: Ek Prem Katha''}}》和《{{tsl|en|Batti Gul Meter Chalu||''Batti Gul Meter Chalu''}}》後斯里·納拉揚·辛格的第三次執導電影。

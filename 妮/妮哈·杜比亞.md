'''妮哈·杜比亞'''（{{lang-en|'''Neha Dhupia'''}}，{{bd|1980年|8月27日|catIdx=Dhupia, Neha}}）<ref>[https://www.hindustantimes.com/bollywood/neha-dhupia-turns-37-i-once-organised-a-surprised-party-for-my-birthday/story-0MQm527L6JatUpJ3fE3JlJ.html “Neha Dhupia turns 37: I once organised a surprised party for my birthday”]. Hindustan Times. 27-08-2017.</ref>是[[印度|印度]]女演員和選美皇后。她的主要作品是[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]、[[泰卢固语|泰卢固语]]和[[马拉雅拉姆语|马拉雅拉姆语]]电影。

她較受歡迎的電影有《Tumhari Sulu》（2017）和《Singh Is Kinng》（2008）等等。<ref>[http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/tumhari-sulu-actor-neha-dhupia-4939974/ “Tumhari Sulu actor Neha Dhupia: When there is no work, I create my own”]. The Indian Express. 16-11-2017.</ref>

杜比亞的丈夫[[安格·貝迪|安格·貝迪]]（Angad Bedi）亦是一位演員和模特兒。兩人在2018年5月10日举行了私人婚礼。<ref>[https://www.ndtv.com/entertainment/neha-dhupia-gets-married-to-angad-bedi-see-first-pic-1850186 “Neha Dhupia Marries Angad Bedi. See Wedding Pic”]. NDTV. 10-05-2018.</ref>

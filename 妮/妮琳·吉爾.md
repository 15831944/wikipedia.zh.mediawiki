'''妮琳·卡爾·吉爾'''（{{lang-en|'''Neelam Kaur Gill'''}}，{{bd|1995年|4月27日|catIdx=Gill, Neelam}}）是一名[[英国|英国]]女性時尚模特兒。<ref>[https://www.standard.co.uk/lifestyle/esmagazine/neelam-gill-the-first-british-indian-to-break-into-the-modelling-alist-a2959081.html “Neelam Gill: the first British Indian to break into the modelling A-list”]. Evening Standars. 01-10-2015.</ref>她以在知名品牌[[巴寶莉|巴寶莉]]、[[Abercrombie_&_Fitch|Abercrombie & Fitch]]和雜誌[[时尚_(杂志)|时尚]]上出現而為人所知。<ref>[https://www.independent.co.uk/life-style/fashion/features/burberry-enlists-first-indian-model-as-its-new-beauty-muse-9685846.html “BURBERRY ENLISTS FIRST INDIAN MODEL NEELAM GILL AS ITS NEW BEAUTY MUSE”]. The Independent. 22-08-2014.</ref>

吉爾來自英國一名印度裔[[錫克教|錫克教]]家庭，祖父母從印度旁遮普移民到英國。<ref>[https://www.vogue.in/content/neelam-johals-crazy-modelling-story/ “Neelam Johal’s crazy modelling story”]. Vogue India. 15-01-2014.</ref>

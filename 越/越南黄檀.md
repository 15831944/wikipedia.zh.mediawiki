'''越南黄檀'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Dalbergia tonkinensis'''''}}），一說即'''東京黃檀'''、'''越南黃花梨'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chinatimber.org/news/51311.html |title=越南黄花梨就是东京黄檀吗？ |publisher=中國木材網轉自中华古典家具网 |date=2013-08-22|accessdate=2018-03-31}}</ref>，为[[豆科|豆科]][[黄檀属|黄檀属]]下的一种小喬木，高{{convert|5|-|13|m}}，生長於[[海南島|海南島]]和[[越南|越南]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=2&taxon_id=242316810 |title=''Dalbergia tonkinensis'' |author=Dezhao Chen |author2=Dianxiang Zhang |author3=Kai Larsen |last-author-amp=yes |work=Flora of China |publisher=Missouri Botanical Garden, St. Louis, MO & Harvard University Herbaria, Cambridge, MA |accessdate=18 May 2012}}</ref>，其數量由於棲息地喪失和過度砍伐而處於下降當中<ref name=IUCN/>。

中國是越南黃檀的主要消費地，在這裡它主要被用來製作家具<ref name="DPA-1">{{Cite news |agency=Deutsche Presse Agentur |title=Endangered Vietnam tree felled for timber |date=27 March 2017 |newspaper=[[The_Australian|The Australian]]  }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |author=Van Bien (Dan Viet) |title=Why do Chinese people have a mania for with Dan Huynh wood (Sua wood) |publisher=Truc Huy Villa |year=2015 |url=http://truchuyvilla.com.vn/view-news.php?lang=en&ma=news&item=122 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170612152047/http://truchuyvilla.com.vn/view-news.php?lang=en&ma=news&item=122# |archive-date=2017-06-12 |deadurl=yes |access-date=2018-03-30 }}</ref>。越南政府禁止商業化經銷越南黃檀，但私下交易和拍賣並未遭禁<ref name="DPA-1" />。作為一種名貴植物，一些越南紫檀樹的價格超過了100萬美元<ref name="DPA-1" /><ref>{{Cite news|title=200-year-old sua tree sold for over $1 million |date=11 April 2017 |newspaper=VietNamNet Online Newspaper |url=http://english.vietnamnet.vn/fms/environment/176083/200-year-old-sua-tree-sold-for-over--1-million.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170612151105/http://english.vietnamnet.vn/fms/environment/176083/200-year-old-sua-tree-sold-for-over--1-million.html |archive-date=12 June 2017 |deadurl=no }}</ref>。

'''越南更新革命党'''（{{lang-vi|Việt Nam Canh Tân Cách Mạng Đảng}}），简称'''越新'''（{{lang-vi|Việt Tân}}），是一个在[[越南|越南]]境内秘密活动以及在越南国外活动的政党，其目的是以和平和政治手段在对越南进行改革，建立[[民主|民主]]制度。<ref name="senate.gov">{{cite web|url=http://www.senate.gov/~foreign/testimony/2008/DiemTestimony080312p.pdf|format=PDF|title=Testimony before the Senate Foreign Relations Committee Subcommittee on East Asian and Pacific Affairs|accessdate=2008-05-07|date=2008-03-12|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080502193956/http://www.senate.gov/~foreign/testimony/2008/DiemTestimony080312p.pdf|archivedate=五月 2, 2008|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref name="AlistairCommendsVietTan">{{cite web|url=http://www.alistaircoe.com.au/2009/viet-tan/|title=Alistair commends Vietnam pro-democracy movement|author=Alistair Coe|accessdate=2009-12-06|date=2009-11-12}}</ref>

越新的前身是1982年9月10日由前[[南越|南越]]海军中将[[黄基明|黄基明]]建立的[[越南国家统一解放阵线|越南国家统一解放阵线]]（Mặt trận Quốc gia Thống nhất Giải phóng Việt Nam），该组织曾在地下活跃二十年之久。2004年9月19日，继任主席[[阮金|阮金]]解散了这个组织，改组为越南更新革命党并延续至今。<ref name="ReasonsPublicVietTan">{{cite web|url=http://www.viettan.org/article.php3?id_article=830|title=Reasons for the Public Introduction of Viet Tan|author=Kim Nguyen|accessdate=2008-05-13|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20061014064035/http://www.viettan.org/article.php3?id_article=830|archivedate=十月 14, 2006|deadurl=yes}}</ref>

越新目前被越南当局列为[[非法政党|非法政党]]和[[恐怖组织|恐怖组织]]，<ref name = thayer>{{cite web|url=http://johnib.wordpress.com/tag/viet-tan/|title=Background Briefing: Viet Tan|author=Carlyle A. Thayer|accessdate=2007-08-03|date=2007-05-05}}</ref><ref name = TehranTimes>{{cite web|url=http://johnib.wordpress.com/tag/viet-tan/|title=Pro-reform party coddled in U.S|accessdate=2008-04-15|date=2007-12-17}}</ref>然而事实上越新的成员们一直致力以非暴力手段来改变越南。<ref name="senate.gov"/><ref name="HumanRightsWatchReleaseConvictedActivists">{{cite web |url=http://www.hrw.org/news/2013/01/09/vietnam-release-convicted-activists|title=Vietnam: Release Convicted Activists - Drop Charges Against Blogger Le Quoc Quan|date=2013-01-09|publisher=Human Rights Watch|accessdate=19 July 2012}}</ref>美国政府也承认没有任何证据显示越新有任何与恐怖组织相似的罪恶行径，<ref name="FoxNewsVietnamArrestsApril2012">{{cite web|url=http://www.foxnews.com/world/2012/04/29/vietnam-arrests-us-pro-democracy-activist/|accessdate=2012-05-02|work=Associated Press|date=2012-04-29|title=Vietnam arrests US pro-democracy activist}}</ref>并认为越新是一个“提倡民主改革的和平组织”。<ref name="UnitedNationsConcerned">{{cite web|url=http://www.un.org/apps/news/story.asp?NewsID=43906&Cr=vietnam&Cr1=|title=UN human rights office concerned over convictions of 14 activists in Vietnam|accessdate=2013-01-11|date=2013-01-11|publisher=United Nations}}</ref>

'''越南网络审查'''阻止[[越南|越南]]网民接触到批评越南政府言论的网站，海外政党、国际人权组织等网站，以及其他被政府认为有不恰当内容的网站<ref name="rsf" />。据报道，越南的[[网络警察|网络警察]]监控着国内的众多网吧，网络[[持不同政见者|持不同政见者]]会被关入监狱。越南当局同时使用法律和技术手段控制着网民上网。当局试图使用控制、监视以及对网络使用进行监管的手段，{{what|因而}}被称作“竹子防火墙”（{{lang-vi|bức tường lửa tre}}）<ref name="Bamboo Firewall">{{cite journal |url = http://repository.jmls.edu/jitpl/vol20/iss4/4/|author=Robert N. Wilkey| title=Vietnam’s Antitrust Legislation and Subscription to E-ASEAN: An End to the Bamboo Firewall Over Internet Regulation? |journal=The John Marshall Journal of Computer and Information Law |volume=XX |date=Summer 2002 |issue=4 |publisher=The John Marshall Law School |accessdate=2012-01-11}}</ref>。

2011年，{{link-en|开放网络促进会|OpenNet Initiative}}将越南的网络过滤等级归入最严重的级别，该国对于政治的网络监控无处不在<ref name="ONISS-Nov2011">OpenNet Initiative [http://opennet.net/research/data  "Summarized global Internet filtering data spreadsheet"], 8 November 2011 and [http://opennet.net/research/profiles "Country Profiles"], the OpenNet Initiative is a collaborative partnership of the Citizen Lab at the Munk School of Global Affairs, University of Toronto; the Berkman Center for Internet & Society at Harvard University; and the SecDev Group, Ottawa</ref>。2012年，越南被[[无国界记者|无国界记者]]列为12个“互联网敌人”国家之一<ref name="rsf">{{cite web|url=http://www.rsf.org/article.php3?id_article=26095&Valider=OK|author=Reporters Without Borders|title=Internet Enemies: Vietnam|accessdate=2008-07-15|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://arquivo.pt/wayback/20090702141343/http://www.rsf.org/article.php3?id_article=26095&Valider=OK|archivedate=2009-07-02}}</ref><ref name="RWBEnemies">[http://march12.rsf.org/i/Report_EnemiesoftheInternet_2012.pdf ''Internet Enemies''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120323215225/http://march12.rsf.org/i/Report_EnemiesoftheInternet_2012.pdf |date=2012-03-23 }}, Reporters Without Borders (Paris), 12 March 2012</ref>。

虽然越南政府宣称为了防止色情淫秽网站对该国的侵扰而采取封锁措施，但不少被封锁的网站包含了可能会动摇[[越南共产党|越南共产党]]执政根基的政治和宗教迷信内容<ref>{{cite news |url = http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/newsroom/opennet_vietnam |title = OpenNet Initiative Vietnam Report: University Research Team Finds an Increase in Internet Censorship in Vietnam |publisher=Berkman Center for Internet & Society at Harvard University |date=2006-08-05 |accessdate=2008-07-15}}</ref>。[[国际特赦组织|国际特赦组织]]报道，越南拘捕了许多网络活动家<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.amnesty.org/en/library/asset/ASA41/010/2006/en/dom-ASA410102006en.html |title = Viet Nam: Internet repression creates climate of fear |date=2006-10-22 |accessdate=2008-07-15|author=Amnesty International}} {{Dead link |date = October 2010 |bot = H3llBot}}</ref>。

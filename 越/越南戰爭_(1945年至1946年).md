'''越南戰爭'''，英國稱為'''Operation Masterdom'''（支配行動）<ref>George Rosie and Bradley Borum, Operation Masterdom: Britain's Secret War in Vietnam</ref>，越南稱為'''Nam Bộ kháng chiến'''（南方抗戰）<ref>[http://www.saigon-gpdaily.com.vn/Culture_Art/2011/9/96752/ Concert to mark 66th anniversary of the Southern Resistance War] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111219121130/http://www.saigon-gpdaily.com.vn/Culture_Art/2011/9/96752/ |date=2011-12-19 }}</ref>，是在[[第二次世界大戰|第二次世界大戰]][[日本投降|日本投降]]後在[[越南|越南]]發生的[[戰爭|戰爭]]，由[[英属印度|英属印度]]及[[法國|法國]]的[[特遣隊|特遣隊]]、以及[[日本|日本]][[南方軍_(日本陸軍)|南方軍]]的部隊對抗[[越南|越南]][[共产主义|共产主义]]的[[越南獨立同盟會|越南獨立同盟會]]，其目的是要爭奪越南的控制權。

==傷亡人數==

根據官方統計，英國有介入的第一次越南戰爭之傷亡人數為：40名大英及印第安士兵，法國及日本傷亡人數略多於此，[[越盟|越盟]]死傷人數估計為2700，其中約有600人死於英國士兵之手，其餘則是被法國及日本士兵殺死。非官方的統計數字或許會較多，但考慮到越盟的統計方法，正確的數字應該永遠無法得知了。
==重要性==


Four more bloody decades of fighting lay ahead which would end in defeat for two major world players. From March to July, 1946, armed and backed up by the French, the Viet Minh systematically set about executing leaders and members of nationalist Vietnamese groups, as Ho's lieutenant Lê Duẩn said, "(to) wipe out the reactionaries." Known as the "Great Purge", the goal was to eliminate everyone thought dangerous to the Communist Party of Vietnam, and tens of thousands of nationalists, Catholics and others were massacred from 1946–1948.[13]

Between May and December, Ho Chi Minh spent four months in France attempting to negotiate full independence and unity for Vietnam, but failed to obtain any guarantee from the French. After a series of violent clashes with Viet Minh, French forces bombarded Hai Phong harbor and occupied Hanoi, forcing Viet Minh forces to retreat into the jungle. On December 19, 1946, 30,000 Viet Minh under Giap initiated the National Resistance War with an attack on French troops at Hanoi.[13] The War in Vietnam of 1946 – 1954, had begun.

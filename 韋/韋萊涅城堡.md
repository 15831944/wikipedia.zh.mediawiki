'''韋萊涅城堡'''（{{lang-sl|Velenjski grad}}）是位於[[斯洛維尼亞|斯洛維尼亞]]城市[[韋萊涅|韋萊涅]]的一座城堡，保存狀況良好。這座城堡現在作為藝術和文化博物館使用<ref name=Municipality>{{cite web|url=http://www.velenje.si/en/960|title= Natural and Cultural Heritage Buildings in the Municipality of Velenje|accessdate=December 8, 2010|publisher=Municipality Valenje}}</ref><ref name= McKelvie>{{Cite book|last= McKelvie|first= Robin|author2=Jenny McKelvie |title=  Slovenia, 2nd|page=199|accessdate=December 8, 2010|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=h_d3xtr7rDEC&pg=PA199|publisher= Bradt Travel Guides|year= 2008|isbn=1-84162-211-7}}</ref><ref name= Stewart>{{Cite book|last= Stewart|first= James|title= Slovenia|page=195|accessdate=December 8, 2010|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=qwBUkHaz76QC&pg=PA1alen95&dq=Velenje+Castle&hl=en&ei=cZcATYeONMXzcd3-0M0E&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=5&ved=0CD4Q6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=Velenje%20Castle&f=false|publisher=New Holland Publishers|year=2006|isbn=1-86011-336-2}}</ref><ref name=Muzej>{{cite web|url=http://www.muzej-velenje.si/english/castle.php |title=Velenje castle |accessdate=December 8, 2010 |publisher=Muzej Valenje |deadurl=bot: unknown |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120430090701/http://www.muzej-velenje.si/english/castle.php |archivedate=April 30, 2012 |df= }}</ref><ref name=Info>{{cite web|url=http://www.slovenia.info/?grad=3621&lng=2|title= Castle Velenje:The Savinjska region|accessdate=December 8, 2010|publisher= slovenia.info}}</ref>。韋萊涅城堡在1270年就已經被提及。

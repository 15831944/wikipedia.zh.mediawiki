'''韋智林／福雷斯特·惠勒'''（{{lang-en|'''Forrest Wheeler'''}}，{{bd|2004年|3月21日|catIdx=Wheeler}}）是一位華裔[[美國|美國]]兒童[[演員|演員]]。2014年，加入[[美國廣播公司|ABC]][[情景喜劇|情境喜劇]]《[[菜鳥新移民|菜鳥新移民]]》主演陣容，該劇於2015年開播。<ref name="Kelley 2017">{{cite web|url=http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/tv/la-et-st-fresh-off-the-boat-day-20170502-story.html|title=ABC sitcom 'Fresh Off the Boat' gets its own day in L.A.|date=2017-05-02|last=Kelley|first=Sonaiya|website=latimes.com|access-date=2017-08-06}}</ref><ref name="Otterson Otterson 2017">{{cite web|url=http://variety.com/2017/tv/news/speechless-renewed-1202421120/|title=‘Speechless,’ ‘Fresh Off the Boat’ Renewed at ABC|date=2017-05-12|last=Otterson|first=Joe|last2=Otterson|first2=Joe|website=[[綜藝_(雜誌)|綜藝雜誌]]|access-date=2017-08-06}}</ref><ref name="Keene 2015">{{cite web|url=http://collider.com/ian-chen-forrest-wheeler-fresh-off-the-boat-tv-performer-of-the-week/|title=TV Performers of the Week: Ian Chen and Forrest Wheeler, ‘Fresh Off the Boat’|date=2015-11-20|last=Keene|first=Allison|website=Collider|access-date=2017-08-06}}</ref><ref name="Commentary 2015">{{cite web|url=https://qz.com/329384/is-fresh-off-the-boat-a-network-sitcom-or-the-rawest-realest-tv-show-about-asian-americans-ever-yes/|title=Is "Fresh off the Boat" a network sitcom or the rawest, realest TV show about Asian Americans ever? Yes|author=Commentary|date=2015-01-20|website=Quartz|access-date=2017-08-06}}</ref><ref name="BSCkids 2015">{{cite web|url=http://www.bsckids.com/2015/11/forrest-wheeler-talks-fresh-off-the-boat-interview/|title=Forrest Wheeler Talks "Fresh Off The Boat" – Interview|date=2015-11-04|website=BSCkids|access-date=2017-08-06}}</ref>

韋智林是中美混血，父親為美國人，母親則為[[香港|香港]]人，故會些許的[[粵語|粵語]]，<ref>{{cite news|url=http://udn.com/NEWS/ENTERTAINMENT/ENT7/9109852.shtml |title=「菜鳥移民」明年笑翻ABC |author=馬雲 |date=2014-12-06 |work=[[北美世界日報|世界日報]] |accessdate=2017-03-20 |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20150212061216/http://udn.com/NEWS/ENTERTAINMENT/ENT7/9109852.shtml |archivedate=2015-02-12 |deadurl=yes }}</ref> 而喜歡[[武術|武術]]的他，也修習了極限武術多年。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://star.ettoday.net/news/749501|title=《菜鳥》黃氏兄弟超愛台灣！　發音標準唱「台語老歌」|publisher=[[ETtoday新聞雲|ETtoday星光雲]]|author=裴璐|date=2016-08-05|access-date=2018-01-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.appledaily.com.tw/appledaily/article/headline/20160806/37337667/ |title=楊昇德演《菜鳥》刈包名廚 想開實驗性餐廳 |author=李子凡 |date=2016-08-06 |work=蘋果日報 |accessdate=2017-03-19 |location=台北 |language=zh-tw |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160806135548/http://www.appledaily.com.tw/appledaily/article/headline/20160806/37337667/ |archivedate=2016-08-06 |deadurl=no }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.porticomedia.com/entNewsC/showCP/555047|title=美劇《菜鳥新移民》快閃訪台 黃氏兄弟大秀武術與台語歌|publisher=-{杰}-德影音|date=2016-08-05|access-date=2018-01-06}}</ref>

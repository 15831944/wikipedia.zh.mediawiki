'''韋恩·洛特爾'''（{{lang-en|'''Wayne Lotter'''}}，{{bd|1965年|12月4日|2017年|8月16日|catIdx=Lotter}}），[[南非|南非]]野生動物[[保育運動|保育員]]。他是[[坦尚尼亞|坦尚尼亞]]的保護區管理解決方案（{{lang|en|Protected Area Management Solutions}}，{{lang|en|PAMS}}）基金會共同創辦人<ref>{{cite news | title = 知名非洲大象守護者 遭槍殺身亡 | publisher = [[自由時報|自由時報]] | url = http://news.ltn.com.tw/news/world/breakingnews/2168297 | date = 2017-08-19 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = zh-tw}}</ref>，並曾擔任{{link-en|國際公園保育員協會|International Ranger Federation}}副主席<ref>{{cite news | title = New thinking needed in the war on ivory poachers | publisher = [[ITV新聞|ITV新聞]] | url = http://www.itv.com/news/2014-02-11/new-thinking-needed-in-the-war-on-ivory-poachers/ | date = 2014-02-11 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref>。

== 生平 ==
韋恩·洛特爾於1990年取得[[保育生物學|生態保育]]碩士學位，之後在[[非營利組織|非營利組織]]和政府單位從事野生動物保育機構工作，最先在南非擔任{{link-en|公園保育員|Park ranger}}。他是坦尚尼亞的保護區管理解決方案（{{lang|en|PMAS}}）基金會共同創辦人，該基金會教導數千名非洲人生態保育相關觀念<ref>{{cite web | title = Wayne Lotter: Rights body calls for justice | website=www.ippmedia.com | url = http://www.ippmedia.com/en/news/wayne-lotter-rights-body-calls-justice | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref>。洛特爾曾參與創立坦尚尼亞全國及跨國重刑案調查機構（{{lang-en|National and Transnational Serious Crimes Investigation Unit}}），該組織曾成功追捕數名惡名昭彰的[[象牙|象牙]][[走私|走私者]]<ref>{{cite news | title = Renowned anti-poaching leader slain by gunmen in Tanzania | first = Kevin | last = Sieff | publisher = [[華盛頓郵報|華盛頓郵報]] | url = https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/worldviews/wp/2017/08/18/renowned-anti-poaching-leader-shot-dead-by-gunmen-in-tanzania/ | date = 2017-08-18 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref>。

在2015年[[新聞週刊|新聞週刊]]的專訪中，洛特爾被描述為「熱心且有魅力」<ref>{{cite news | title = The Global Fight for Tanzania’s Elephants | first = Oscar | last = Lopez | publisher = [[新聞週刊|新聞週刊]] | url = http://www.newsweek.com/global-fight-tanzanias-elephants-386324 | date = 2015-10-22 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref>。他亦被邀請參與2016年[[Netflix|Netflix]]的紀錄片《{{link-en|象牙博弈|The Ivory Game}}》，但他要求改以{{lang|en|PMAS}}基金會僱用的保育員參與演出<ref>{{cite news | title = Ex-KNP game ranger tackling poaching the unconventional way | work = Lowvelder | url = http://lowvelder.co.za/378445/ex-knp-game-ranger-tackling-poaching-unconventional-way/ | date = 2017-03-10 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref>。

== 遇害 ==
洛特爾常收到{{link-en|死亡威脅|death threats}}。他在2017年8月16日於坦尚尼亞[[三蘭港|三蘭港]]乘坐計程車時被射殺<ref>{{cite news | title = SA conservationist killed in Tanzania after almost a decade of death threats | first = Elise | last = Tempelhoff | work = News24 | url = http://www.news24.com/SouthAfrica/News/sa-conservationist-killed-in-tanzania-after-almost-a-decade-of-death-threats-20170818 | date = 2017-08-18 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | title = Anti-poaching activist Wayne Lotter shot dead in Tanzania | publisher = ITV新聞 | url = http://www.itv.com/news/2017-08-17/anti-poaching-activist-wayne-lotter-shot-dead-in-tanzania/ | date = 2017-08-17 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref>。他的死引起國際關注，[[泰晤士報|泰晤士報]]上亦有訃告<ref>{{cite news | title = Wayne Lotter | publisher = [[泰晤士報|泰晤士報]] | url = https://www.thetimes.co.uk/article/wayne-lotter-obituary-f0790vk5h | date = 2017-08-19 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref>。

英國[[靈長類學|靈長類學]]、[[動物行為學|動物行為學]]、[[人類學|人類學家]]暨[[聯合國和平使者|聯合國和平使者]][[珍·古德|珍·古德]]表示洛特爾是她的英雄且富有勇氣<ref>{{cite web | title = Wayne Lotter: Remembering a Wildlife Hero & Friend | first = Jane | last = Goodall | authorlink = 珍·古德 | website = Jane Goodall's Good for All News | url = http://news.janegoodall.org/2017/08/18/wayne-lotter-remembrance/ | date = 2017-08-18 | accessdate = 2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref>，其他野生動物保育組織亦對洛特爾的生前作為表示讚揚<ref>{{cite news | title = Crusader Who Saved Elephants From Poachers Is Shot Dead in Tanzania | first = Christine | last = Hauser | publisher = [[紐約時報|紐約時報]] | issn = 0362-4331 | url = https://www.nytimes.com/2017/08/18/world/africa/elephant-poachers-wayne-lotter.html | date = 2017-08-18 | accessdate=2017-08-23 | language = en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | title = Conservation loses one of its best | first = Azzedine | last = Downes | website = [[國際愛護動物基金會|國際愛護動物基金會]] | url = http://www.ifaw.org/united-states/news/conservation-loses-one-its-best | date = 2017-08-17 | accessdate = 2017-08-22 | language = en}}</ref>。

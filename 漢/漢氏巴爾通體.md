'''漢氏巴爾通體'''（''Bartonella henselae''）是一種常見的、可導致[[貓抓病|貓抓病]]的[[真細菌|真細菌]]<ref name="pmid8905096">{{cite journal |author=Jerris RC, Regnery RL |title=Will the real agent of cat-scratch disease please stand up? |journal=Annu. Rev. Microbiol. |volume=50 |issue= |pages=707–25 |year=1996 |pmid=8905096 |doi=10.1146/annurev.micro.50.1.707}}</ref>，屬於巴爾通體屬。該細菌通過{{link-en|三聚体自身转运蛋白粘附素|trimeric autotransporter adhesin}}與宿主細胞貼合，進而入侵。

==特徵==
漢氏巴爾通體是一種[[革蘭氏陰性菌|革蘭氏陰性]][[桿菌|桿菌]]<ref name="Diddi">{{cite journal |pmc=3657863 |title=Strategy for identification & characterization of ''Bartonella henselae'' with conventional & molecular methods |author1=Kavita Diddi |author2=Rama Chaudhry |author3=Nidhi Sharma |author4=Benu Dhawan |date=2011-03-28 |journal=Indian Journal of Medical Research |volume=137 |pages=380–387 }}</ref>，該菌可以通過裂解—離心血培養法培養<ref name="la scola">{{cite journal |author1=Bernard La Scola |author2=Didier Raoult |date=1999-03-17 |title=Culture of ''Bartonella quintana'' and ''Bartonella henselae'' from Human Samples: a 5-Year Experience (1993 to 1998) |url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC84980/pdf/jm001899.pdf |journal=Journal of Clinical Microbiology |volume=37 |issue=6 |pages=1899–1905 |accessdate=2016-12-01}}</ref>。通過{{link-en|Warthin-Starry染色法|Warthin-Starry stain}}或類似的{{link-en|銀染法|Silver stain}}可以檢測到侵入組織中的漢氏巴爾通體。

漢氏巴爾通體的種名「''henselae''」係紀念1953年出生的美國微生物學家黛安·瑪麗·亨塞爾（ Diane Marie Hensel ）。她在1985年一場爆發於美國俄克拉何馬州的漢氏巴爾通體疫情中收集了大量的漢氏巴爾通體菌株、樣品<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bacterio.net/bartonella.html|title=Bartonella|first=A.C.|last=Parte|website=www.bacterio.net}}</ref>。

==臨床醫學==
漢氏巴爾通體通常寄生於貓的血液中，當人被貓抓傷後，該菌就可能傳染給人。此外，[[蜱|蜱]]也可能做為傳染媒介將貓攜帶的漢氏巴爾通體傳染給人<ref name="Klotz2011">{{cite journal|year=2011|title=Cat-scratch Disease|url=http://www.aafp.org/link_out?pmid=21243990|journal=American Family Physician|volume=83|issue=2|pages=152–5|pmid=21243990|vauthors=Klotz SA, Ianas V, Elliott SP}}</ref>。人被傳染漢氏巴爾通體後可能會患上貓抓病，表現出淋巴結腫大、疲倦、[[發熱|發熱]]、[[頭疼|頭疼]]等症狀<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Perkocha LA, Geaghan SM, Yen TS, Nishimura SL, Chan SP, Garcia-Kennedy R, Honda G, Stoloff AC, Klein HZ, Goldman RL |title=Clinical and pathological features of bacillary peliosis hepatis in association with human immunodeficiency virus infection |journal=The New England Journal of Medicine |volume=323 |issue=23 |pages=1581–6 |year=1990 |pmid=2233946 |doi=10.1056/NEJM199012063232302 }}</ref>。

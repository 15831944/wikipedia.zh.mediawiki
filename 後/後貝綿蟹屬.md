'''後貝綿蟹屬'''（學名：{{lang|la|'''''Metadynomene'''''}}）为[[貝綿蟹科|貝綿蟹科]]下的一个屬，也是'''後貝綿蟹亞科'''（{{lang|la|'''''Metadynomeninae'''''}}）之下的唯一一個屬<ref name=zootaxa>{{cite journal |last1=Guinot |first1=Danièle |year=2008 |journal=''Zootaxa'' |issue='''1850''' |pages=1-26 |date=2008-07-02 |title=A re-evaluation of the Dynomenidae Ortmann, 1892 (Crustacea, Decapoda, Brachyura, Podotremata), with the recognition of four subfamilies |url=http://www.mapress.com/zootaxa/2008/f/z01850p026f.pdf |language=en }}</ref>。本屬的模式種是''[[Dynomene_devaneyi|Dynomene devaneyi]]'' <small>[[Takeda|Takeda]], 1977</small><ref name="WoRMS">{{cite WoRMS |id=439281 |title=''Metadynomene'' McLay, 1999 |accessdate=2015-09-28 |date=2011-01-19 |last=Davie |first=Peter }}</ref>。

==物種==
本屬有四個物種<ref name=zootaxa /><ref name="WoRMS" />：
* ''[[Metadynomene_crosnieri|Metadynomene crosnieri]]'' <small>McLay, 1999</small>
* ''[[Metadynomene_devaneyi|Metadynomene devaneyi]]'' <small>(Takeda, 1977)</small>
* [[種島後貝綿蟹|種島後貝綿蟹]] ''Metadynomene tanensis'' <small>(Yokoya, 1933)</small><ref>{{TaiBNET| zh=種島後貝綿蟹 | id=416688 | binomial=Metadynomene tanensis | authority=Yokoya, 1933 | var= | accessdate=2013-01-24}}</ref>
* ''[[Metadynomene_tuamotu|Metadynomene tuamotu]]'' <small>Ng & McLay, 2010</small>

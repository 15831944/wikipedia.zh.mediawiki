'''虛擬加工'''（Virtual machining）系統是整合[[虚拟现实|虚拟现实]]及[[工具機|工具機]]的制造系統，在製造和生產上配合不同的電腦以及軟體，目的是為了可以在[[虚拟现实|虚拟现实]]系統的真實環境下[[模擬|模擬]]其特性、[[誤差|誤差]]，並進行[[三維模型|建模]]<ref>{{Cite journal |url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0010448513000997 |first=Mohsen |last=Soori |first2=Behrooz |last2=Arezoo |first3=Mohsen |last3=Habibi |title=Dimensional and geometrical errors of three-axis CNC milling machines in a virtual machining system |work=Computer Aided Design 45 |date=November 2013 |volume=45 |issue=11 |pages=1306–1313 |issn=0010-4485 |via=ScienceDirect |url-access=subscription |doi=10.1016/j.cad.2013.06.002}}</ref>。虛擬加工可以在[[生產線|生產線]]沒有實際測試的情形下，讓產品可以正常生產<ref name=journal>{{Cite journal |url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0278612514000442 |first=Mohsen |last=Soori |first2=Behrooz |last2=Arezoo |first3=Mohsen |last3=Habibi |title=Virtual machining considering dimensional, geometrical and tool deflection errors in three-axis CNC milling machines |work=Journal of Manufacturing Systems |volume=33 |issue=4 |date=October 2014 |pages=498–507 |issn=0278-6125 |via=ScienceDirect |url-access=subscription |doi=10.1016/j.jmsy.2014.04.007}}</ref>。

'''伊斯兰教'''是[[厄立特里亚|厄立特里亚]]第一大宗教信仰，2017年时占总人口的51.6%<ref>{{cite web|url=https://acs-italia.org/wp-content/uploads/Eritrea.pdf|title=ERITREA Total population: 5.35 million Religions|access-date=2019-01-19}}</ref>。其中99%都是[[逊尼派穆斯林|逊尼派穆斯林]]<ref name="Pew Research Center">{{Citation | editor-last = Miller | editor-first = Tracy | date = October 2009 | publisher = [[Pew_Research_Center|Pew Research Center]] | title = Mapping the Global Muslim Population: A Report on the Size and Distribution of the World’s Muslim Population | format = PDF | url = http://pewforum.org/newassets/images/reports/Muslimpopulation/Muslimpopulation.pdf | page = 39 | accessdate = 2018-04-30 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20091010050756/http://pewforum.org/newassets/images/reports/Muslimpopulation/Muslimpopulation.pdf | archive-date = 2009-10-10 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>。
[[File:Asmara,_moschea_01.JPG|thumb]]
伊斯兰教徒最早于[[古莱什|古莱什]]迫害穆斯林时来到此地，并被[[阿克苏姆帝国|阿克苏姆帝国]]安置在{{tsl|en|Negash|内加什}}。穆斯林主要聚居于该国东部、沿海平原以及邻近[[苏丹|苏丹]]边境的西部低地。其中大部分属于[[亚非语系|亚非语系]]社区，包括{{tsl|en|Tigre people|提格雷人}}、[[紹約人|紹約人]]、[[阿法爾人|阿法爾人]]、{{tsl|en|Rashaida people|拉謝達人}}、[[貝扎人|貝扎人]]以及{{tsl|en|Bilen people|比伦人}}<ref name=report>[https://www.state.gov/g/drl/rls/irf/2007/90096.htm International Religious Freedom Report 2007: Eritrea]. United States [[Bureau_of_Democracy,_Human_Rights_and_Labor|Bureau of Democracy, Human Rights and Labor]] (2007-09-14). ''This article incorporates text from this source, which is in the [[public_domain|public domain]].''</ref>。[[提格利尼亞人|提格利尼亞人]]中亦有5%的穆斯林。部分使用[[尼罗-撒哈拉语系|尼罗-撒哈拉语系]]的民族亦信仰伊斯兰教，包括{{tsl|en|Nara people|那拉人}}及{{tsl|en|Kunama people|库纳马人}}。

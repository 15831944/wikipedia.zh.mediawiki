'''南加州大学4.11枪击案'''（也称“瞿铭和吴颖谋杀案”）发生于2012年4月11日。两个[[中国|中国]][[研究生|研究生]]在[[南加州大学|南加州大学]]洛杉矶校区一英里外於他们的[[宝马|宝马]]车内被枪击身亡。

这起事件在中文圈引起了大量对媒体不负责任地塑造两个“[[炫富|炫富]]”学生的不满，特别是在许多[[中文媒体|中文媒体]]批评两位受害者炫耀自己的财富之后，然而另一方面，國外媒體認為平民駕駛寶馬者相對普及，反之對於社會治安的的反思報導較多，本案就發生在南加州洛杉矶校区坐落于一个臭名昭著的有着大量罪案历史的社区，这起事件也引起了大量对于该校区的安全的担忧。<ref name='huffingtonpost419'>{{cite news | first = Noel Irwin | last = Hentschel | title = Loss of Two USC Students From China and Their American Dream | date = 2012-04-19 | url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/noel-irwin-hentschel/usc-shooting-ying-wu-ming-qu_b_1438248.html | work = The Huffington Post | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref>

Javier Bolden（案发时19岁）和Byran Barnes（案发时20岁）作为谋杀的嫌疑人被逮捕。Barnes在2014年被认定谋杀罪，并被判处终身监禁不得保释。Bolden亦被认定有罪，并在11月被判处终身监禁不得保释。

==细节==
2012年4月11日凌晨1点整，在离南加州校区一英里的雷蒙德大道2700区，学生瞿铭和吴颖在他们停着的宝马2003内被枪杀身亡。在注意到两个男人带着枪接近他们的车时，瞿成功地跑到车外附近的房子并试图砸门，但是被槍数次击中头部。吴坐在前面乘客座位上，胸部中一枪而死。枪击发生时，正下着大雨，导致案发地条件艰难。瞿和吴都被送到洛杉矶市中心的加利福尼亚医院医学中心，并在抵院時被宣告死亡。这两位23岁的学生都来自中国，攻读电气工程专业<ref name='cbsnews411'>{{cite news | title = Ying Wu and Ming Qu Murder Case: $125,000 reward offered for info about USC students slain in their car | date = 2012-04-11 | url = http://www.cbsnews.com/8301-504083_162-57414663-504083/ying-wu-and-ming-qu-murder-case-$125000-reward-offered-for-info-about-usc-students-slain-in-their-car/ | work = CBS News | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref>。调查人员相信作案动机可能是劫车或者抢劫<ref name='showingofftheirbmw'/>。

===逮捕===
警察追踪了一部被凶手从谋杀现场拿走的手机。在2012年5月18日，通过该线索逮捕了20岁的住在南加州大学校区附近的Byran Barnes。第二个嫌疑人，住在[[棕櫚谷_(加利福尼亞州)|棕櫚谷]]的19岁的Javier Bolden也在那天被逮捕。他们都被指控两项谋杀罪<ref name='cbsnews521'>{{cite news | title = Ming Qu and Ying Wu Murder Case: Two arrested in the killings of USC graduate students, LA police say | date = 2012-05-21 | url = http://www.cbsnews.com/8301-504083_162-57438318-504083/ming-qu-and-ying-wu-murder-case-two-arrested-in-the-killings-of-usc-graduate-students-la-police-say/ | work = CBS News | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref><ref name="cbslocal-20120518">{{cite news | title = Arrests Made In Connection With Murder Of 2 USC Students | date = 2012-05-18 | url = http://losangeles.cbslocal.com/2012/05/18/arrests-made-in-connection-with-murder-of-2-usc-students/ | work = CBS Los Angeles | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref>。

Bolden和Barnes被指控另一个与本案不相关的谋杀未遂，该案发生在2011年，在一个洛杉矶南部的聚会上，导致一个女性严重受伤，一个男性瘫痪<ref name='hawaiinews'>{{cite news| first = Linda| last = Deutsch| title = 2 charged in killing of 2 USC students from China| date = 2012-05-22| url = http://www.hawaiinewsnow.com/story/18578984/2-men-due-for-arraignment-in-usc-shootings?clienttype=printable| work = Associated Press| accessdate = 2012-05-30}}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。警官还怀疑Barnes在2月12日在一个聚会上开了很多枪，打伤了一个20岁的男性<ref name='hawaiinews'/>。根据检察官的说法，这二人符合可判處死刑的條件<ref name='dailytrojan522'>{{cite news | title = District attorney releases charges for two suspects | date = 2012-05-22 | url = http://dailytrojan.com/2012/05/22/district-attorney-releases-charges-for-two-suspects/ | work = Daily Trojan | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref>。

===反应===
枪击案后的一周，大约有一千人聚集在洛杉矶{{link-en|圣殿礼堂|Shrine Auditorium}}哀悼两名受害人。中国驻洛杉矶总领事馆总领事[[邱绍芳|邱绍芳]]做了如下声明：“中华人民共和国外交部、教育部和洛杉矶总领事馆在悲剧发生后立即行动，致力于和各方一道努力来处理该事件。”<ref name='huffingtonpost419'/><ref name="uscmemorial">{{cite news | first = Asher | last = Feldman | title = USC gathers to remember Ying Wu, Ming Qu | date = 2012-04-18 | url = http://dailytrojan.com/2012/04/18/usc-gathers-to-remember-ying-wu-ming-qu/ | work = Daily Trojan | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref>洛杉矶市长{{link-en|Antonio Villaraigosa}}对事件表达了哀悼。

===批评===
美国和英国媒体注意到许多中国网民的关注点在于受害者不必要地炫耀网民们所认为的奢侈品<ref name='laweekly412'>{{cite news | first = Simone | last = Wilson | title = Ying Wu, Ming Qu Murdered in BMW: Some in China Blame USC Grad Students for 'Showing off Their Wealth' | date = 2012-04-12 | url = http://blogs.laweekly.com/informer/2012/04/ying_wu_ming_qu_murdered_bmw_china_blame_usc_students_showing_off_wealth.php | work = LA Weekly | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref>。案件发生之后，[[每日邮报|每日邮报]]发表了一篇关注这一现象的匿名文章。文章中指出部分中文新闻媒体对受害者缺少同情，反而关注于受害者的财富。文章中引述两位受害者朋友的声明称“瞿铭和吴颖在他们将近两年的南加州大学求学过程中过着简朴的生活。为了减少生活开支，他们都和自己的室友合租”。<ref name='showingofftheirbmw'>{{cite news | title = 'They were showing off their wealth': Unsympathetic Chinese media says two USC students murdered in luxury BMW were killed because of their car | date = 2012-04-12 | url = http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2129081/They-showing-wealth-Chinese-media-says-USC-students-murdered-BMW-killed-make-car.html | work = Dailymail.co.uk | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref>

南加州校区周边区域是一个低收入的城镇社区，有着很高的犯罪率记录<ref name='huffingtonpost419'/><ref name='nytimes518'>{{cite news | first = | last =  | title = 2 arrested in USC Chinese student killings| date = 2012-05-18 | url = http://www.nytimes.com/2012/05/20/us/2-arrested-in-fatal-shooting-of-students-from-china.html | work = New York Times | accessdate = 2012-05-30}}</ref>。 两位学生的父母为谎报校区安全问题起诉该校。 该诉讼被洛杉矶高等法院法官Michael Johnson于2013年驳回。在一份“Causation is an insurmountable issue for the plaintiffs”报告中，Johnson认定杀人和学校自己报告的保护学生的举措之间没有关联<ref>CBS Los Angeles "Wrongful Death Lawsuit Against USC Dismissed," February 15, 2013 http://losangeles.cbslocal.com/2013/02/15/wrongful-death-lawsuit-against-usc-dismissed/</ref><ref>abc 7 Eyewitness News, Los Angeles "Judge Sides with USC in Wrongful Death Lawsuit Over Fatally Shot Students," November 13, 2012 http://abc7.com/archive/8884335/</ref>。

===纪念短片===
事件发生后，南加州大学校友为该事件先后制作了两部短片，分别为《我们的声音》与《[[天使之城_(2012年電影)|City of Dreams]]》。前者为记录短片，为澄清媒体错误报道而制作。后者为事件改编的纪念电影短片<ref>{{cite news | last = Hernandez | first = Kristen | date = 2012-11-13 | title= Short film honors slain Chinese grad students |url= https://dailytrojan.com/2012/11/13/short-film-honors-slain-chinese-grad-students/ |newspaper= Daily Trojan | language= en | archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20160502004138/http://dailytrojan.com/2012/11/13/short-film-honors-slain-chinese-grad-students/ |archive-date= 2016-05-02 }}</ref>。该纪念短片于2012年5月筹拍，同年11月在洛杉矶[[蒙特利公园|蒙特利公园市]][[AMC电影院|AMC电影院]]上映。

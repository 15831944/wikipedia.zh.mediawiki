'''南蘇丹護照'''是南蘇丹政府發行給該國公民的國際旅行證件。南蘇丹共和國於2012年1月開始發行被國際承認的電子護照。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sudanradio.org/south-sudan-passports-and-ids-be-launched-3-january-2012|title=South Sudan Passports And IDs To Be Launched On 3 January 2012 {{!}} Sudan Radio Service|last=|first=|date=27 December 2011|website=|publisher=Sudan Radio Service|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121231212858/http://www.sudanradio.org/south-sudan-passports-and-ids-be-launched-3-january-2012|archive-date=31 December 2012|dead-url=yes|access-date=10 December 2017|df=}}</ref>這些護照於2012年1月3日由總統[[薩爾瓦·基爾·馬亞爾迪特|薩爾瓦·基爾·馬亞爾迪特]]於首都[[朱巴|朱巴]]的一場儀式上發出。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sudantribune.com/South-Sudan-launches-passports-and,41183|title=South Sudan launches passports and national ID cards - Sudan Tribune: Plural news and views on Sudan|last=|first=|date=5 January 2012|website=|publisher=[[Sudan_Tribune|Sudan Tribune]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120108205503/http://www.sudantribune.com/South-Sudan-launches-passports-and,41183|archive-date=8 January 2012|access-date=10 December 2017|df=}}</ref>新護照的有效期限為五年。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gossmission.org/goss/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=1281&Itemid=192|title=H.E Kiir launches center for issuing of national documents of RSS|last=|first=|date=4 January 2012|website=Embassy of the Republic of South Sudan in Washington, D.C.|publisher=Gossmission.org|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120429044251/http://www.gossmission.org/goss/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=1281&Itemid=192|archive-date=29 April 2012|dead-url=yes|accessdate=2016-08-05}}</ref>

'''南极服役奖章'''（{{lang-en|Antarctica Service Medal，缩写：ASM}}）是由[[美国国会|美国国会]]在1960年7月7日立法设立的奖章。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.usap.gov/travelAndDeployment/documents/PublicLawRibbonColors.pdf|title=An Act to provide for the presentation of a medal to persons who have served as members of a United States expedition to Antarctica|accessdate=January 18, 2013|date=July 7, 1960|format=PDF|publisher=[[美國南極洲計劃|美國南極洲計劃]]}}</ref><ref name="ASM">{{Cite web|url=http://www.tioh.hqda.pentagon.mil/Catalog/Heraldry.aspx?HeraldryId=15305&CategoryId=4&grp=4&menu=Decorations%20and%20Medals&ps=24&p=0|title=Antarctica Service Medal|accessdate=December 24, 2013|publisher=The Institute of Heraldry: Office of the Administrative Assistant to the Secretary of the ARMY|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131225110857/http://www.tioh.hqda.pentagon.mil/Catalog/Heraldry.aspx?HeraldryId=15305&CategoryId=4&grp=4&menu=Decorations%20and%20Medals&ps=24&p=0|archivedate=2013年12月25日|df=}}</ref>设立该奖章的目的是作为一个[[美國軍事勳獎|美国军事勋奖]]授予在[[南极洲|南极洲]]服役或训练的美军人员。

== 奖章形状 ==
南极服役奖章是铜制的，直径1.25英寸。 奖章正面刻有身穿防寒服的极地工作人员图像及“ANTARCTICA SERVICE”（南极服役）字样。奖章背面刻有"COURAGE"（勇气）, "SACRIFICE"（牺牲）,  "DEVOTION"（奉献）字样。

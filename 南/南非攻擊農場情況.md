'''{{link-en|南非農業|Agriculture in South Africa|南非農業社區}}'''自多年來經常遭受到'''攻擊'''<ref name="Committee">{{cite web|title=Report of the Committee of Inquiry into Farm Attacks – 31 July 2003|url=http://www.saps.gov.za/statistics/reports/farmattacks/farmattacks_2003.htm|accessdate=7 December 2013}}</ref>。根據[[南非|南非]]報章的報導，大多數農場攻擊者都是年輕[[黑人|黑人]]，而大多數受害者是南非的[[白人|白人]]農場主<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/news/world/africa/article7078730.ece|title=White farmers 'being wiped out'|work=Sunday Times|date=28 March 2010|author=Dan McDougall|accessdate=24 May 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news | author=Adriana Stuijt | url=http://www.digitaljournal.com/article/267463 | title=Two more S.African farmers killed: death toll now at 3,037| publisher=Digital Journal| date=17 February 2009 | accessdate=24 May 2011}}</ref>，但這說法經常受到異議<ref>{{cite web|last=Nechama|first=Brodie|title=Are SA whites really being killed "like flies"?|url=http://www.africacheck.org/reports/are-white-afrikaners-really-being-killed-like-flies/|publisher=Africa Check|accessdate=7 December 2013}}</ref>。

雖然政府認為沒有證據顯示攻擊農場是有組織的，但白人農場主相信攻擊為了驅趕他們離開自己的農地<ref name="nyet">{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/1998/07/16/world/rural-white-south-africa-afraid-and-armed.html|title=Rural White South Africa: Afraid, and Armed|author=Suzanne Daley|work=New York Times|date=16 July 1998|accessdate=24 May 2011}}</ref>。2010年，極右翼政治人物[[尤金·特雷布蘭奇|尤金·特雷布蘭奇]]在他的農場被殺，引起國際社會關注南非農場受到攻擊的問題<ref>[http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2010/apr/04/eugene-terreblanche-south-african-white-supremacist Eugene Terre'Blanche, opponent of White farm murders is hacked to death] ''The Guardian''. 4 April 2010</ref>。

2015年7月，南非團體「非洲論壇」宣稱該年上半年在南非有116宗襲擊農場主事件，造成27名農場主喪生。[[新华网|新华网]]的報導宣稱南非農場主「被杀害的概率远远高于其他南非人」。從1994年至2015年，有超過3,000名白人農場主被殺害。「非洲論壇」當時宣稱有許多案件沒有被偵破，有時即使疑犯被捕也未必得到審判<ref>{{Cite news |url=http://www.xinhuanet.com/world/2015-07/06/c_1115826796.htm |title=记者手记：在刀尖上舔血——南非农场主安全状况堪忧 |publisher=新華網 |date=2015-07-06}}</ref>。

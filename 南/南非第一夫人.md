'''南非第一夫人'''（{{lang-af|Eerste Lady van Suid-Afrika}}，{{lang-en|First Lady of South Africa}}）是[[南非總統|南非總統]]的妻子或最年長妻子所擁有的[[頭銜|頭銜]]。現任南非第一夫人為第5任南非總統[[西里爾·拉馬福薩|西里爾·拉馬福薩]]的妻子{{le|謝玻·莫采佩|Tshepo Motsepe}}<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.brandsouthafrica.com/investments-immigration/business/economy/first-lady-of-the-republic-of-south-africa-dr-motsepe-headed-to-the-u-s-for-an-official-visit |title=First Lady of the Republic of South Africa, Dr Motsepe headed to the U.S for an official visit |publisher=Brand South Africa |date=2018年9月18日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.goodthingsguy.com/people/tshepo-motsepe/ |title=South Africa’s First Lady… 6 things you need to know |publisher=Good Things Guy |date=2018年2月18日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.yourfamily.co.za/features/meet-south-africas-new-first-lady-tshepo-motsepe |title=Meet South Africa’s New First Lady – Tshepo Motsepe |publisher=Your Family}}</ref>。

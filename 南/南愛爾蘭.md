'''南愛爾蘭'''（{{lang-ga|Deisceart Éireann}}）是[[聯合王國|聯合王國]]一個短暫存在的自治區，1921年5月3日宣布成立，但從來沒有實質的建立起來，於1922年12月6日正式解散。<ref>Statutory Rules & Orders published by authority, 1921 (No. 533). Additional source for 3 May 1921 date: Alvin Jackson, ''Home Rule - An Irish History'', Oxford University Press, 2004, p198; Southern Ireland did not become a state (or pejoratively, a ''statelet''). Its constitutional roots remained the [[Act_of_Union_1800|Act of Union]], two complementary Acts, one passed by the [[Parliament_of_Great_Britain|Parliament of Great Britain]], the other by the [[Parliament_of_Ireland|Parliament of Ireland]].</ref>

'''南非刺桐'''({{lang|en|Erythrina caffra}}、'''{{lang|en|Coast coral tree}}'''、'''{{lang|en|African coral tree}}'''、'''{{lang|en|Kaffir coral tree}}'''、象牙紅、金剛刺桐、火焰刺桐、火炬刺桐), 屬於[[喬木|喬木]]，原產於[[非洲|非洲]]東南部。往往被培育種植，也被大量引進到[[印度|印度]]種植。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.ildis.org/LegumeWeb?version~10.01&LegumeWeb&tno~1720&genus~Erythrina&species~caffra | work = ILDIS World Database of Legumes, version 10.01 | title = Erythrina caffra | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://www.webcitation.org/6GF0F9UFb?url=http://www.ildis.org/LegumeWeb?version~10.01 | archivedate = 2013-04-29 }}</ref> 南非刺桐亦為[[美國|美國]][[加利福尼亞州|加利福尼亞州]][[洛杉磯|洛杉磯]]的市樹。<ref>{{cite book |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MkArAAAAYAAJ |title=Tropical Legumes: Resources for the Future |author=Advisory Committee on Technology Innovation, Board on Science and Technology for International Development, Commission on International Relations, National Research Council |publisher=National Academy of Sciences |year=1979 |page=258}}</ref>

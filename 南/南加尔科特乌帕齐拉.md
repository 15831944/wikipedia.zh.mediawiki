'''南加尔科特'''（{{lang-bn|নাঙ্গলকোট}}，{{lang-en|Nangalkot}}）是[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]]的一个[[乌帕齐拉|乌帕齐拉]]<ref name=Banglapedia>{{citation 
|author=Md. Mobashwer Hussain
|chapter=Nangalkot Upazila
|chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=Nangalkot_Upazila
|title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |editor=Sirajul Islam and Ahmed A. Jamal |publisher=[[Asiatic_Society_of_Bangladesh|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh]] |year=2012 |edition=Second}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://en.prothom-alo.com/bangladesh/news/125601/Rashedun-staying-at-Comilla-home-when-Nazim-killed|title=Rashedun staying at Comilla home when Nazim killed in Dhaka: Family|newspaper=Prothom Alo|access-date=2016-11-12}}</ref>，位于[[吉大港专区|吉大港专区]]的[[库米拉县|库米拉县]]。该地总面积236.44平方千米，东西部分别与[[拉克沙姆乌帕齐拉|拉克沙姆乌帕齐拉]]、[[恰乌达格拉姆乌帕齐拉|恰乌达格拉姆乌帕齐拉]]接壤，南部毗邻[[森巴格乌帕齐拉|森巴格乌帕齐拉]]与[[达甘布伊扬乌帕齐拉|达甘布伊扬乌帕齐拉]]<ref name="Banglapedia" />。

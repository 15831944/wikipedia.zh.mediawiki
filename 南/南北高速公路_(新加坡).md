'''南北高速公路'''（{{lang-en|'''North–South Expressway'''}}；簡稱：'''NSE'''；{{lang-ms|Lebuhraya Utara-Selatan}}），是新加坡一條正在興建的[[高速公路|高速公路]]，預計2020年完成。建造新高速公路的計劃於2008年1月30日公佈，為[[陆路交通管理局|陆路交通管理局]]對轄下道路網絡交通流量作出評估後的建議，公路開通後全長{{convert|21|km}}，將新加坡北部地區與{{link-en|东海岸公园大道|East Coast Parkway}}連接起來<ref name="raymondspeech3">{{cite web | url=https://app-pac.mica.gov.sg/data/vddp/embargo/27390895.htm | title=Speech by Mr. Raymond Lim, Minister for transport, at visit to , 30 January 2008, 10.15 AM | publisher=Singapore Government Media Release | date=30 January 2008 }}{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref><ref name="renamed_from_1_on_20130420195324">{{cite press release | publisher = Land Transport Authority | date = 2011-11-15 | url = http://app.lta.gov.sg/apps/news/page.aspx?c=2&id=mjtut44y69ma2c6354z6ob21shwo5gewr7tj44yhiqvkx240r0 | title = Full alignment of the North-South Expressway unveiled | deadurl = yes | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20131230233246/http://app.lta.gov.sg/apps/news/page.aspx?c=2&id=mjtut44y69ma2c6354z6ob21shwo5gewr7tj44yhiqvkx240r0 | archivedate = 2013-12-30 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news | publisher = Channel NewsAsia | url = http://www.channelnewsasia.com/stories/singaporelocalnews/view/1165553/1/.html | title = New expressway sparks big land acquisition | author = Hetty Musfirah | date = 20121-11-15}}</ref>。

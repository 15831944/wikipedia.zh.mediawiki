'''南中帝汶县'''（[[印尼语|印尼语]]：Kabupaten Timor Tengah Selatan）是[[印度尼西亚|印度尼西亚]][[东努沙登加拉省|东努沙登加拉省]]的一个县，位于[[帝汶岛|帝汶岛]]上，县治在[[索埃|索埃]]。南中帝汶县成立于1958年<ref>{{Citation
| author = [[Government_of_Indonesia|Government of Indonesia]]
| url = 
| title = Establishment of the Second-level Administrative Regions under the First-level Administrative Region of Bali, West Nusa Tenggara and East Nusa Tenggara
| language = id
| id = UU No. 69/1958
| date = 9 August 1958
| accessdate = 2009-11-25
| publisher = Indonesia Ministry of Law and Justice
}}</ref>。2010年普查，全县有人口440,470人。

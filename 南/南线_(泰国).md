'''南线'''是由[[泰国国铁|泰国国铁]]（SRT）建造并拥有的[[铁路线|铁路线]]，位于[[泰国|泰国]]的[[曼谷|曼谷]]以南地区，总长度为1,144.29[[公里|公里]]是该国最长的铁路线。

* [[Su-ngai_Kolok_Main_Line|Su-ngai Kolok Main Line]]: from [[Bangkok|Bangkok]] to [[Su-ngai_Kolok_District|Su-ngai Kolok District]], [[Narathiwat_Province|Narathiwat Province]]
* [[Thon_Buri_Line|Thon Buri Line]]: short branch line in [[Bangkok|Bangkok]]

* [[Khiri_Rat_Nikhom_Line|Khiri Rat Nikhom Line]]: from [[Surat_Thani|Surat Thani]] to [[Khiri_Rat_Nikhom_District|Khiri Rat Nikhom District]]
* [[Kantang_Line|Kantang Line]]: from [[Thung_Song_District|Thung Song District]], [[Nakhon_Si_Thammarat_Province|Nakhon Si Thammarat Province]] to [[Kantang_District|Kantang District]], [[Trang_Province|Trang Province]]

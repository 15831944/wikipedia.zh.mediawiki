'''南十字座ζ'''是在[[南十字座|南十字座]]南部的一顆[[聯星|聯星]]。它可以用肉眼直接看見，[[視星等|視星等]]為4.06<sup>m</sup>，距離[[太陽|太陽]]大約360[[光年|光年]]。它是[[天蠍-半人馬星協|天蠍-半人馬星協]]的子集團，下天蠍-半人馬座星協的成員<ref name=Chen2012>{{citation | title=A Spitzer MIPS Study of 2.5-2.0 M<sub>⊙</sub> Stars in Scorpius-Centaurus | display-authors=1 | last1=Chen | first1=Christine H. | last2=Pecaut | first2=Mark | last3=Mamajek | first3=Eric E. | last4=Su | first4=Kate Y. L. | last5=Bitner | first5=Martin | journal=The Astrophysical Journal | volume=756 | issue=2 | id=133 | pages=24 | date=September 2012 | doi=10.1088/0004-637X/756/2/133 | bibcode=2012ApJ...756..133C | arxiv=1207.3415 | postscript=. }}</ref>。

這是一顆雙譜的[[聯星#光譜聯星|光譜聯星]]<ref name=chini2012>{{citation | title=A spectroscopic survey on the multiplicity of high-mass stars | journal=Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society | volume=424 | issue=3 | page=1925 | year=2012 | display-authors=1 | last1=Chini | first1=R. | last2=Hoffmeister | first2=V. H. | last3=Nasseri | first3=A. | last4=Stahl | first4=O. | last5=Zinnecker | first5=H. | bibcode=2012MNRAS.424.1925C | doi=10.1111/j.1365-2966.2012.21317.x | arxiv=1205.5238 | postscript=. }}</ref>。光譜與[[B型主序星|B型主序星]]匹配，[[恆星分類|恆星分類]]屬於B2.5V。它有一顆微弱的視覺伴星，視星等12.49<ref>{{cite journal | last1=Gahm | first1=G. F. | last2=Ahlin | first2=P. | last3=Lindroos | first3=K. P. | title=A study of visual double stars with early type primaries.

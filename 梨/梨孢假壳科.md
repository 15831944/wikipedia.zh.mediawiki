'''梨孢假殼科'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''Apiosporaceae'''}}）是[[真菌界|真菌界]][[子囊菌門|子囊菌門]] 的一個科<ref>{{cite journal|last=Lumbsch TH, Huhndorf SM. |title=Outline of Ascomycota – 2007 |journal=Myconet |volume=13 |issue= |pages=1–58 |id= |url=http://www.fieldmuseum.org/myconet/outline.asp |accessdate= |publisher=The Field Museum, Department of Botany, Chicago, USA |date=December 2007 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090318003134/http://www.fieldmuseum.org/myconet/outline.asp |archivedate=2009-03-18 }}</ref>，於1998年首次被描述<ref>{{cite journal |author=Hyde KD, Fröhlich J, Taylor JE. |year=1998 |title=Fungi from palms. XXXVI. Reflections on unitunicate ascomycetes with apiospores |journal=Sydowia |volume= 50|issue=1 |pages=21–80}}</ref>。本科內的所有物種均帶有[[孢子|孢子]]，透過分解及消化植物，特別是[[棕榈科|棕榈科]]及[[禾本科|禾本科]]的植物而取得營養。可進行無性繁殖的屬有[[色孢子節菱孢菌屬|色孢子節菱孢菌屬]]和''[[Cordella|Cordella]]''<ref>{{cite book |author=Cannon PF, Kirk PM. |title=Fungal Families of the World |publisher=CABI|location=Wallingford |year=2007 |page=14 |isbn=0-85199-827-5}}</ref>。

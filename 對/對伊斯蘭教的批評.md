'''對伊斯蘭教的批评'''自[[伊斯蘭教|伊斯蘭教]]创立以來就已存在。早期對伊斯蘭的批評來自[[基督徒|基督徒]]，例如9世紀前多數激進基督徒稱[[伊斯兰教|伊斯兰教]]为[[異端|異端]]<ref name="John of Damascus2">De Haeresibus by [[大馬士革的聖約翰|John of Damascus]]. See Migne 《{{le|希腊人教父|Patrologia Graeca}}》, vol. 94, 1864, cols 763–73. An English translation by the Reverend John W Voorhis appeared in ''The Moslem World'' for October 1954, pp. 392–98.</ref>。到后来[[穆斯林世界|穆斯林世界]]本身也遭到批评。<ref name="WarraqPoetry">{{cite book|last=Warraq|first=Ibn |title=Leaving Islam: Apostates Speak Out |publisher=Prometheus Books |year=2003 |isbn=1-59102-068-9 |page=67}}</ref><ref name="Ibn Kammuna">Ibn Kammuna, ''Examination of the Three Faiths'', trans. Moshe Perlmann (Berkeley and Los Angeles, 1971), pp. 148–49</ref>在[[九一一袭击事件|九一一袭击事件]]和其他[[伊斯蘭恐怖主義|恐怖攻擊]]之後，西方國家对伊斯蘭教的批评得到延续。<ref>{{cite news|title=Islam’s Problem With Blasphemy|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2015/01/14/opinion/islams-problem-with-blasphemy.html|date=13 January 2015|first=Mustafa|last=Akyol|newspaper=[[纽约时报|The New York Times]]|accessdate=16 January 2015}}</ref>其後也由這些批判，激起了[[反伊斯蘭|反伊斯蘭]]和大部分歐洲國家反[[移民|移民]]的浪潮的理由。

在对伊斯兰教的批判中，批评对象主要包括伊斯兰教“最后的先知”[[穆罕默德|穆罕默德]]的伦理道德<ref name="Oussani">[http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10424a.htm Mohammed and Mohammedanism], by Gabriel Oussani, ''Catholic Encyclopedia''. Retrieved April 16, 2006.</ref><ref name="WarraqQuest">Ibn Warraq, The Quest for Historical Muhammad (Amherst, Mass.:Prometheus, 2000), 103.</ref>、《[[古兰经|古兰经]]》的真实性及其合乎道德的程度等。<ref name="BibleInQuran">[http://jewishencyclopedia.com/view.jsp?artid=1032&letter=B#3068 Bible in Mohammedian Literature.], by Kaufmann Kohler Duncan B. McDonald, ''Jewish Encyclopedia''. Retrieved April 22, 2006.</ref>非洲和印度的圖畫描述了他們認為伊斯蘭教對土著文化的破壞。 其他的批評集中在伊斯蘭世界歷史上和現代伊斯蘭國家的人權問題，包括在伊斯蘭法律的統治下[[婦女與伊斯蘭教|對女性待遇]]、[[LGBT|LGBT]]人士和宗教和少數民族。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.freedomhouse.org/template.cfm?page=22&year=2005&country=6825|title=Saudi Arabia|publisher=}}</ref><ref name="IslamInEurope">{{cite news| publisher=[[纽约书评|The New York Review of Books]] | date=2006-10-05 | title=Islam in Europe | author=Timothy Garton Ash | url=http://www.nybooks.com/articles/19371}}</ref><ref name="IslamInEurope"/>

== 對穆斯林移民的批評 ==
[[歐洲移民危機|歐洲移民危機]]開始以後，在西方國家各種極右政黨以那些穆斯林移民文化與[[德國|德國]]、[[法國|法國]]等國文化不相容為由，作為被批評的對象，例如[[德國另類選擇|德國另類選擇]]有禁止[[叫拜樓|宣禮塔]]的言論。<ref name="telegraph">{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2016/05/01/far-right-afd-party-says-muslims-not-welcome-in-germany/|title=Far right AfD party says Muslims not welcome in Germany|publisher=}}</ref>

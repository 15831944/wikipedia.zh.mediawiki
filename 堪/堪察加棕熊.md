'''堪察加棕熊'''（''Ursus arctos beringianus''；{{lang-ru|Камчатский бурый медведь}}），又叫'''西伯利亞棕熊'''，是[[棕熊|棕熊]]的一個亞種，分佈于[[堪察加半島|堪察加半島]]、[[卡拉金斯克島|卡拉金斯克島]] 、[[千島群島|千島群島]]等地<ref name="soviet">''Mammals of the Soviet Union'' Vol.II Part 1a, SIRENIA AND CARNIVORA (Sea cows; Wolves and Bears), V.G Heptner and N.P Naumov editors, Science Publishers, Inc. USA. 1998. {{ISBN|1-886106-81-9}}</ref>，它與阿拉斯加地區的棕熊有很近的親緣關係，可能是[[科迪亞克棕熊|科迪亞克棕熊]]的祖先<ref name="evolution">McLellan, B.N. and D. Reiner. 1994. [http://www.bearbiology.com/fileadmin/tpl/Downloads/URSUS/Vol_9/McLellan_Reiner_Vol_9.pdf A review of bear evolution]. Int. Conf. Bear Res. and Manage. 9: pp. 85–96. (PDF) . Retrieved on 2011-09-26.</ref>。堪察加棕熊很少襲擊人類<ref>[http://www.bearbiology.com/fileadmin/tpl/Downloads/URSUS/Vol_9/Revenko_Vol_9.pdf BROWN BEAR (URSUS ARCTOS PISCATOR) REACTION TO HUMANS ON KAMCHATKA], I. A. Revenko, Kamchatka Ecology and Environmental Institute, pr. Rybakov, 19 a, Petropavlovsk-Kamchatskiy, 683024, Russia. (PDF) . Retrieved on 2011-09-26.</ref>。

==形態==
[[File:Oursonne_et_son_petit.jpg|thumbnail]]

堪察加棕熊是一種大型棕熊，可能是歐亞大陸最大的棕熊<ref name="tourism">''Prospects for polar tourism'' by John Snyder and Bernard Stonehouse, published by CABI, 2007, {{ISBN|1-84593-247-1}}</ref>，體長可達3米，重可達650公斤。它與[[東北棕熊|東北棕熊]]的區別在於後者的毛色更黑，頭骨更加狹長。

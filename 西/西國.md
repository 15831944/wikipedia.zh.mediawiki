'''西國'''（West Country）是指[[英格蘭|英格蘭]]西南部的一個地區<ref>{{cite web |url=http://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/west-country|title=the West Country|accessdate=2 May 2017}}</ref>。除了指[[布里斯托爾灣|布里斯托爾灣]]和[[英吉利海峽|英吉利海峽]]之間之外，西國的界線並沒有明確定義。西國通常包括[[康沃爾郡|康沃爾郡]]、[[德文郡|德文郡]]、[[多塞特郡|多塞特郡]]、[[薩默塞特郡|薩默塞特郡]]，並且有時也包括[[格洛斯特郡|格洛斯特郡]]和[[威尔特郡|威尔特郡]]的部分地區。有時西國和西南英格蘭等義<ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/england/devon/7644254.stm |title=BBC - "ITV West Country staff to be axed", referring to Bristol and Plymouth |publisher=BBC News |date=2008-09-30 |accessdate=2010-05-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.blue-badge-guides.com/WestCountry.html | archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110708073356/http://www.blue-badge-guides.com/WestCountry.html |title=Blue Badge Tourist Guides - The West Country |publisher=Blue-badge-guides.com |date= |accessdate=2010-05-22|archivedate=8 July 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.picturesofengland.com/mapofengland/south-west-map.html |title=The West Country |publisher=Pictures of England |date=2007-08-02 |accessdate=2010-05-22}}</ref>。西國地區有著自己獨特的方言<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/insideout/southwest/series7/devon-dialect.shtml|title=THE DEVON DIALECT CHALLENGE|accessdate=2 May 2017}}</ref>。

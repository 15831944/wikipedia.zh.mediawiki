'''西蒙·海耶斯'''（{{lang-en|Simon Hayes}}）英国[[音频工程师|音频工程师]]<ref name="dpamicrophones">{{cite web |url=http://www.dpamicrophones.com/en/users-artists/Hayes.aspx |title=Simon Hayes, Production Sound Mixer, Les Misérables |accessdate=2013-03-08|work=dpamicrophones}}</ref>和[[巴西柔术|巴西柔术]]教练。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.carlsongracieteam.org.uk/simon-hayes-awarded-blackbelt-by-wilson-junior/|title=Simon Hayes awarded Blackbelt by Wilson Junior {{!}} Carlson Gracie UK|website=www.carlsongracieteam.org.uk|language=en-US|access-date=2018-05-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://onthemat.com/simon-hayes-bjj-black-belt/|title=Simon Hayes – BJJ Black Belt!|access-date=2018-05-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.ukbjjbeltregister.org/blackbelts.php|title=Verified UK BJJ Black Belt List|last=Browne,seanpbrowne@gmail.com|first=Sean|website=www.ukbjjbeltregister.org|language=en|access-date=2018-05-20}}</ref>他因电影[[悲惨世界_(2012年电影)|悲惨世界]]，在[[第85届奥斯卡金像奖|第85届奥斯卡金像奖]]赢得了[[奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖|奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖]]。<ref name="Oscars2013">{{cite web |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-20973004 |title=Oscars 2013: Full list of winners |accessdate=2013-03-08 |work=BBC News}}</ref>自1995年起，海耶斯参与制作了50多部电影。海耶斯是Wilson Junior的学生，巴西柔术黑带三段选手。

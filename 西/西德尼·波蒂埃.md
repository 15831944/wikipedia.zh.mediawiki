'''西德尼·波蒂埃[[爵士|爵士]]'''（'''Sir Sidney Poitier'''，[[KBE|KBE]]<ref>Note: Because Poitier is a citizen of The Bahamas, a Commonwealth realm that subscribes to the British Honours System, this is a substantive (as opposed to honorary) knighthood, which entitles him to the style "Sir". Poitier rarely employs the title, although only in connection with his official ambassadorial duties.</ref>，{{IPAc-en|icon|ˈ|p|w|ɑː|t|j|eɪ}} 或 {{IPAc-en|ˈ|p|w|ɑː|t|i|.|eɪ}}; {{bd|1927年|2月20日|catIdx=Poitier}}），生于[[美国|美国]][[迈阿密|迈阿密]]，[[美国|美国]]演员，导演，作家和外交官，全世界第一位黑人[[奥斯卡最佳男主角奖|奥斯卡影帝]]。

1963年，波蒂埃凭借在电影《原野百合花》（Lilies of the Field）<ref>{{IMDb name|1627|section=awards}}</ref>中的表演成为第一位赢得[[奥斯卡最佳男主角奖|奥斯卡最佳男主角奖]]的黑人演员。<ref>[[James_Baskett|James Baskett]] won an [[Academy_Honorary_Award|Honorary Academy Award]] for his performance in [[Walt_Disney|Walt Disney]]'s ''[[Song_of_the_South|Song of the South]]'' (1946). The award was not competitive. see [http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0059934/awards Awards for James Baskett, Internet Movie Database]</ref>1999年，[[美国电影学会|美国电影学会]]评选波蒂埃入围[[AFI百年百大明星|AFI百年百大明星]]，位居25名男演员的第22位。

波蒂埃曾执导了一些流行电影，2002年，在他获得奥斯卡最佳男主角奖38年后，波蒂埃因为他的非凡表现力获得由[[美国电影艺术与科学学院|美国电影艺术与科学学院]]颁发的[[奥斯卡终身成就奖|奥斯卡终身成就奖]]。<ref>[http://awardsdatabase.oscars.org/ampas_awards/DisplayMain.jsp?curTime=1200268027690 ''Sidney Poitier awards: Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences'' awards database] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120114044424/http://awardsdatabase.oscars.org/ampas_awards/DisplayMain.jsp?curTime=1200268027690 |date=2012-01-14 }}</ref>自1997年以来他一直是巴哈马驻[[日本|日本]]大使。他也是巴哈马驻[[联合国教科文组织|联合国教科文组织]]大使。2009年8月12日，西德尼·波蒂埃荣获[[美国总统|美国总统]][[奥巴马|奥巴马]]颁发的[[总统自由勋章|总统自由勋章]]，这是美国平民的最高荣誉。<ref>[http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2009/08/12/AR2009081203383.html article 12 August 2009 ] ''Washington Post''</ref>
[[File:Poitier_Belafonte_Heston_Civil_Rights_March_1963.jpg|thumb]]期間於[[林肯紀念堂|林肯紀念堂]]，伴其身旁的是{{link-en|哈利·貝拉方提|Harry Belafonte}}（中）和[[卻爾登·希斯頓|卻爾登·希斯頓]]]]

==个人生活==
1950年4月29日波蒂埃与Juanita Hardy结婚，1965年离婚。1976年1月23日他与Joanna Shimkus结婚，Joanna是一个加拿大出生的立陶宛和爱尔兰后裔女星。他与他的第一任妻子有四个女儿，与他的第二任妻子有两个女儿：Beverly<ref>[http://www.wlbt.com/story/1099232/poitier-henderson-holds-book-signing?redirected=true Poitier-Henderson Holds Book Signing - WLBT 3 - Jackson, MS:]</ref>，Pamela<ref>[http://www.nytimes.com/2005/02/12/nyregion/12ossie.html The New York Times > New York Region > Hundreds Mourn Ossie Davis in Harlem]</ref>，Sherri<ref>[http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/latimes/access/59787000.html?dids=59787000:59787000&FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&type=current&date=Apr+09%2C+1988&author=BOB+POOL&pub=Los+Angeles+Times+(pre-1997+Fulltext)&desc=Daughters+of+King%2C+Malcolm+X+Also+Have+a+Message&pqatl=google Daughters of King, Malcolm X Also Have a Message]</ref>，Gina<ref>[http://nl.newsbank.com/nl-search/we/Archives?p_product=AT&p_theme=at&p_action=search&p_maxdocs=200&p_topdoc=1&p_text_direct-0=0EB7C1C062445229&p_field_direct-0=document_id&p_perpage=10&p_sort=YMD_date:D&s_trackval=GooglePM Atlanta News, Sports, Atlanta Weather, Business News | ajc.com]</ref>，Anika<ref>[http://www.nytimes.com/1994/05/21/style/chronicle-166936.html Chronicle - New York Times]</ref>，Sydney Tamiia<ref>[http://articles.nydailynews.com/2003-01-06/entertainment/18227142_1_sydney-tamiia-poitier-upn-cbs-productions Advice For Upn: Get Rid Of 'Abby' - New York Daily News] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120730082314/http://articles.nydailynews.com/2003-01-06/entertainment/18227142_1_sydney-tamiia-poitier-upn-cbs-productions |date=2012-07-30 }}</ref>。

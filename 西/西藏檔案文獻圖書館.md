'''西藏檔案文獻圖書館'''是由[[達賴喇嘛|達賴喇嘛]]於1970年在[[達蘭薩拉|達蘭薩拉]]創辦的圖書館，於1971年開始運作。它現在是世界上[[佛學|佛學]]與[[藏學|藏學]]的研究重鎮之一。<ref>{{Cite news| url=http://www.thetibetpost.com/zh/features/arts-and-culture/1961-qtibetan-culture-still-so-relevant-todayq-library-director-geshe-lhakdor| title=館長拉多格西：『西藏文化與今日世界息息相關』| date=2011-08-26| newspaper=國際西藏郵報| access-date=2017-10-04| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171004141708/http://www.thetibetpost.com/zh/features/arts-and-culture/1961-qtibetan-culture-still-so-relevant-todayq-library-director-geshe-lhakdor| archive-date=2017-10-04| dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | title=History | url=http://tibetanlibrary.org/#/history | website=西藏檔案文獻圖書館 | access-date=2017-10-04 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171002130233/http://www.tibetanlibrary.org/#/history#/history | archive-date=2017-10-02 | dead-url=no }}{{en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite journal | journal=Bulletin of Tibetology | title=Inventory of Tibetan historical literature | author=Nirmal Chandra Sinha | authorlink=尼爾瑪爾·錢德拉·辛哈 | url=http://himalaya.socanth.cam.ac.uk/collections/journals/bot/pdf/bot_1986_02_04.pdf | format=PDF | access-date=2017-10-04 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150527144201/http://himalaya.socanth.cam.ac.uk/collections/journals/bot/pdf/bot_1986_02_04.pdf | archive-date=2015-05-27 | dead-url=no }}{{en}}</ref>

拉卓格西館長表示，該圖書館的主要工作是保存西藏文化。<ref>{{Cite news | url=http://www.thetibetpost.com/zh/outlook/interviews-and-recap/5113-the-main-work-is-to-save-the-tibetan-culture-director-of-tibetan-library | title=西藏檔案文獻圖書館館長：主要工作是保存西藏文化 | date=2016-07-25 | newspaper=國際西藏郵報 | access-date=2017-10-04 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171004191654/http://www.thetibetpost.com/zh/outlook/interviews-and-recap/5113-the-main-work-is-to-save-the-tibetan-culture-director-of-tibetan-library | archive-date=2017-10-04 | dead-url=no }}{{en}}</ref>其西藏口述歷史計畫紀錄二十世紀中期西藏社會和生活的第一手經驗，目前已完成了209位藏人的口述歷史，並在網路上發表了120支訪談記錄。<ref>{{Cite news | url=http://www.thetibetpost.com/zh/features/education-and-society/3873-new-oral-history-of-tibetan-elders-from-south-india | title=南印度藏人長者新口述歷史 | date=2014-02-11 | newspaper=國際西藏郵報 | access-date=2017-10-04 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171004193247/http://www.thetibetpost.com/zh/features/education-and-society/3873-new-oral-history-of-tibetan-elders-from-south-india | archive-date=2017-10-04 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url=http://library.columbia.edu/locations/eastasian/tibetan/guide/history/oralhistory.html | title=Oral Histories | website=Columbia University Libraries | access-date=2017-10-04 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171224221440/http://library.columbia.edu/locations/eastasian/tibetan/guide/history/oralhistory.html | archive-date=2017-12-24 | dead-url=no }}{{en}}</ref>

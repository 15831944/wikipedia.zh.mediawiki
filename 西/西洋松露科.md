'''西洋松露科'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''Tuberaceae'''}}；{{IPAc-en|ty|oo|b|@|'|r|ay|s|ee}}），又名'''块菌科'''，是一種[[子囊菌門|子囊菌門]][[mycorrhizal|mycorrhizal]]類的[[真菌|真菌]]。美食中的極品[[松露|松露]]就是這一科的生物。

It includes the genus ''[[Tuber_(fungus)|Tuber]]'', which includes the so-called "true" [[truffle_(fungi)|truffles]]. It was characterized by the Belgian botanist [[Barthélemy_Charles_Joseph_du_Mortier|Barthélemy Charles Joseph du Mortier]] in 1822. A [[molecular_phylogenetics|molecular]] study of [[ribosomal_DNA|ribosomal DNA]] by mycologist Kerry O'Donnell in 1997 found that a small [[clade|clade]] now redefined as [[Helvellaceae|Helvellaceae]] is most closely related to the Tuberaceae<ref name=ODonnell1997>{{cite journal |author=O'Donnell K, Cigelnik E, Weber NS, Trappe JM. |year=1997 |title=Phylogenetic relationships among ascomycetous truffles and the true and false morels inferred from 18S and 28S ribosomal DNA sequence analysis |journal=Mycologia |volume=89 |issue=1 |pages=48–65 |doi=10.2307/3761172 |jstor=3761172}}</ref>。

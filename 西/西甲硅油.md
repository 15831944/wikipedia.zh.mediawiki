'''西甲硅油'''（[[INN|INN]]：'''simeticone'''，USAN：'''simethicone'''）是一種消泡劑，臨床上主要用於舒緩由過量氣體引起的[[腹部脹氣|腹部脹氣]]、不適或疼痛。

== 醫療用途 ==
[[File:Simeticone_40mg_pack.jpg|缩略图]]
西甲硅油主要用於舒緩[[消化道|消化道]]內由過量氣體所引起的病症，當中包括[[腹脹|腹脹]]、[[打嗝|打嗝]]和[[胃腸氣|胃腸氣]]。<ref name="USOTClabel">{{Cite web|url=https://dailymed.nlm.nih.gov/dailymed/drugInfo.cfm?setid=d93ff75d-9cdb-476c-bb65-eabeefb91a34|title=Simethicone tablet|accessdate=27 August 2017|date=April 2016|publisher=NIH DailyMed|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180626164148/https://dailymed.nlm.nih.gov/dailymed/drugInfo.cfm?setid=d93ff75d-9cdb-476c-bb65-eabeefb91a34|archive-date=2018-06-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>
但現時缺乏證據表明西甲硅油對以上用途有效。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://patients.gi.org/topics/belching-bloating-and-flatulence/|title=Belching, Bloating, and Flatulence|date=July 2013|last=Modi|first=Ronak|publisher=American College of Gastroenterology|language=en|access-date=2018-09-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180728001044/http://patients.gi.org/topics/belching-bloating-and-flatulence/|archive-date=2018-07-28|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="drugs.com">{{Cite web|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/simethicone.html|title=Simethicone|publisher=[[drugs.com|drugs.com]]|quote=Efficacy not established for the symptomatic relief of immediate postprandial upper abdominal distress|access-date=2018-09-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180626164005/https://www.drugs.com/monograph/simethicone.html|archive-date=2018-06-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>

現時沒有證據表明西甲硅油可用於治療[[嬰兒哭鬧|嬰兒哭鬧]]，<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Pain-relieving agents for infantile colic.|last=Biagioli|first=E|last2=Tarasco|first2=V|date=16 September 2016|journal=The Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews|doi=10.1002/14651858.CD009999.pub2|volume=9|pages=CD009999|pmid=27631535|last3=Lingua|first3=C|last4=Moja|first4=L|last5=Savino|first5=F}}</ref>而且不建議用於此目的。<ref name="drugs.com"/>

西甲硅油並沒有已知的副作用。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.drugs.com/sfx/simethicone-side-effects.html|title=Simethicone Side Effects in Detail - Drugs.com|accessdate=27 August 2017|publisher=Drugs.com|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180626164426/https://www.drugs.com/sfx/simethicone-side-effects.html|archive-date=2018-06-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 藥理學 ==
西甲硅油是一種消泡劑，可降低氣泡的[[表面張力|表面張力]] ，使其在消化道中結合成較大的氣泡，從而促進氣泡的排出。西甲硅油不能減少或阻止氣體產生。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://iffgd.org/symptoms-causes/intestinal-gas/treatment.html|title=Treatment of Gas|date=4 September 2015|publisher=International Foundation for Functional Gastrointestinal Disorders|language=en-gb|access-date=2018-09-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180908023137/https://www.iffgd.org/symptoms-causes/intestinal-gas/treatment.html|archive-date=2018-09-08|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 化學成分 ==
西甲硅油的化學成分是[[聚二甲基矽氧烷|聚二甲基矽氧烷]]（α-(trimethylsilyl)-ω-methylpoly[oxy(dimethylsilylene)]）與[[二氧化硅|二氧化硅]]組成的混合物。<ref name=INN/>

== 歷史 ==
西甲硅油的[[国际非专利药品名称|国际非专利药品名称]]是simeticone。它在1999年被添加到INN推薦名單中。<ref name="INN">{{Cite journal|title=Recommended INN List 42|url=http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/druginformation/innlists/RL42.pdf?ua=1|date=1999|journal=WHO Drug Information|issue=3|volume=13|page=202}}</ref>

西甲硅油以許多不同的[[商品名|商品名]]或作為[[复方制剂|复方制剂]]銷售。同時它也可作獸用藥銷售。<ref name="brands">{{Cite web|url=https://www.drugs.com/international/simeticone.html|title=Simeticone international brands|accessdate=27 August 2017|publisher=Drugs.com|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170711130129/https://www.drugs.com/international/simeticone.html|archive-date=2017-07-11|dead-url=no}}</ref>

西班牙裔人的祖先分布广泛，族群多样性非常强。因此西班牙裔并不是一个[[种族|种族]]，而更像一类[[族群|族群]]，以語言和文化维繫。

==西班牙裔和拉丁裔的区别==
西班牙裔和拉丁裔经常会被人们混用。事实上，西班牙裔的定义相比而言较为狭义，指的是说[[西班牙语|西班牙语]]的人或者祖籍为[[西班牙|西班牙]]的人；而拉丁裔的则较为广义，指的是拥有[[拉丁美洲|拉丁美洲]]祖先的人们（因此还包括[[巴西人|巴西人]]，尽管大部分巴西人说[[葡萄牙语|葡萄牙语]]）<ref name="Dejud2007">{{cite book|author=Carlos Dejud|title=The Relationship Among Ethnic Identity, Psychological Well-being, Academic Achievement, and Intergroup Competence of School-age Hispanic/Latino Youth|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=G6h6I0trgKUC&pg=PA21|year=2007|publisher=ProQuest|isbn=978-0-549-29853-3|page=21}}</ref><ref name="Ready1991">{{cite book|author=Timothy Ready|title=Latino Immigrant Youth: Passages from Adolescence to Adulthood|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=Qz3gHaea22MC&pg=PA14|year=1991|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-0-8153-0057-1|page=14}}</ref><ref>[http://csuchico-dspace.calstate.edu/handle/10211.4/222 Latino: People with roots in the Spanish- and Portuguese-speaking Americas. ==族群分布==
西班牙裔或者拉丁裔占据了[[美国|美国]]16.9%的人口，即5300万人。<ref name="census.gov">{{cite web|url=http://www.census.gov/newsroom/releases/archives/population/cb12-90.html|date=MAY 17, 2012|title=Most Children Younger Than Age 1 are Minorities, Census Bureau Reports|accessdate=2014-05-04|language=en}}</ref>由此而成为继[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]，超过[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]，[[哥伦比亚|哥伦比亚]]和[[西班牙|西班牙]]的第二大西班牙社群。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rawstory.com/rs/2013/06/15/u-s-now-has-most-spanish-speakers-outside-mexico/|title=U.S. now has most Spanish speakers outside Mexico|author=Agence France-Presse|date=June 15, 2013|accessdate=2014-05-04|language=en}}</ref>

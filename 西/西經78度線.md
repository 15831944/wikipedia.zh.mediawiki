'''西經78度線'''是[[本初子午線|本初子午線]]向西78度的[[經線|經線]]，是為西經78°。此線經線由[[北極點|北極點]]起穿過[[北冰洋|北冰洋]]、[[北美洲|北美洲]]、[[大西洋|大西洋]]、[[加勒比海|加勒比海]]、[[中美洲|中美洲]]、[[南美洲|南美洲]]、[[太平洋|太平洋]]、[[南冰洋|南冰洋]]以及[[南極洲|南極洲]]直到[[南極點|南極點]]。此段半圓路徑長{{convert|20003932|km}}，於[[赤道|赤道]]與本初子午線距{{convert|8683|km}}遠。<ref name="noaa">{{cite web | language =en| url  = http://www.ngs.noaa.gov/PUBS_LIB/inverse.pdf
 | title   = Direct and Inverse Solutions of Geodesics on the Ellipsoid with application of nested equation
 | author  = T. Vincenty
 | date    = 1975-04
}}</ref><ref name="latlon">{{cite web | language =en| url = http://www.movable-type.co.uk/scripts/latlong-vincenty.html
 | title   = Vincenty formula for distance between two Latitude/Longitude points
 | website = Movable type scripts
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>

西經78度線與[[東經102度線|東經102度線]]形成一個[[大圓|大圓]]。

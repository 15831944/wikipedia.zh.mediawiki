'''西蒙·艾斯休，'''[[美国陆军|美国军队]][[少尉|少尉]]<ref name="times20180605">{{Cite news|url=https://www.armytimes.com/news/your-army/2018/06/05/groundbreaking-west-point-grad-recognized-as-a-top-10-college-woman-of-the-year/|title=Groundbreaking West Point grad recognized as a top 10 College Woman of the Year|last=Nash|first=Noah|date=5 June 2018|publisher=Army Times|accessdate=2 July 2018}}</ref> 。於2018年畢業自[[西點軍校|西點軍校]]，並獲選為[[罗德奖学金|罗德学者]]。在2017年，她成為首位獲得西點軍校學生軍團長的[[非裔美国人|非裔美国]]女性。<ref name="atimes">{{Cite web|url=https://www.armytimes.com/news/your-army/2017/08/15/simone-askew-is-first-black-woman-to-lead-west-point-cadets/|title=Simone Askew is First Black Woman to Lead West Point Cadets}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |author1=孫洐源 |title=華府女生成為西點軍校首位非裔女性頂尖學員 |url=http://www.epochtimes.com/b5/17/8/20/n9548017.htm |accessdate=2018-12-12 |work=大紀元 |date=2017-08-20 |language=zh-TW}}</ref> 被視為美軍[[種族|種族]]和性别平等的重要一步。

'''西帕克托纳尔'''（{{lang-nci|Cipactonal}}）是[[阿茲特克神話|阿兹特克神话]]中主管占卜与历法的神袛。传说中西帕克托纳尔和{{Internal link helper/en|奥索莫科|Oxomoco}}是第一对人类夫妇，在创造与进化方面类似于基督文明中的亚当与夏娃。<ref name="Austin1988">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=y-0UAAAAYAAJ|title=The human body and ideology: concepts of the ancient Nahuas|last=Austin|first=Alfredo López|publisher=University of Utah Press|accessdate=26 October 2011|year=1988|isbn=978-0-87480-260-3|page=238}}</ref><ref name="Austin1996">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=EgAVAAAAYAAJ|title=The rabbit on the face of the moon: mythology in the mesoamerican tradition|last=Austin|first=Alfredo López|publisher=University of Utah Press|accessdate=26 October 2011|year=1996|isbn=978-0-87480-527-7|pages=102–6}}</ref> 他们育有一子，名为[[皮尔特辛特库特利|皮尔特辛特库特利]]，后来取了索奇克特萨尔的女儿为妻<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.utexas.edu/courses/stross/ant322m_files/cosmology.htm|title=Aztec cosmology|accessdate=26 October 2011|publisher=University of Texas|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120111093701/http://www.utexas.edu/courses/stross/ant322m_files/cosmology.htm|archive-date=2012-01-11|dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name="Austin1996" />。 

== 描述 ==
[[File:Codex_Borbonicus.jpg|缩略图]]
根据[[夸奥蒂特兰|夸奥蒂特兰]]的记载，西帕克托纳尔和奥索莫科掌管着历法<ref name="Foundation1995">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0gF6AAAAMAAJ|title=Papers of the New World Archaeological Foundation|author=New World Archaeological Foundation|publisher=New World Archaeological Foundation|accessdate=26 October 2011|year=1995|page=227}}</ref>，他们亦出现在以[[基切语|基切语]]书写的圣书[[波波爾·烏|波波爾·烏]]中<ref name="SquierComparato1990">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=iGUSAQAAIAAJ|title=Observations on the archaeology and ethnology of Nicaragua|first1=Ephraim George|last2=Comparato|first2=Frank E.|publisher=Labyrinthos|accessdate=26 October 2011|year=1990|isbn=978-0-911437-08-9|page=29|last1=Squier}}</ref>。[[尼加拉瓜|尼加拉瓜]]的一些学者则认为他们的身份正好相反，例如费尔南德斯·德奥维埃多·巴尔德斯曾宣称西帕克托纳尔实为女性，而奥索莫科才是男性，并将其中一人称为塔马加斯塔德（Tamagastad，尼加拉瓜传说中与西帕克托纳尔共为创造神）<ref name="Austin1996" />，{{tsl|en|E. G. Squier|埃弗赖姆·乔治·斯基埃尔}}与弗兰克·E·孔帕拉托则认为他们是巫师<ref name="SquierComparato1990"/>。而在[[納瓦特爾語|納瓦特爾語]]所描述的塔莫安錢的四位祭祀中，除奥索莫科为女性外，其余三位（包括西帕克托纳尔）皆性别不明<ref name="Boone2007">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=WnEvi8fu0kwC&pg=PA24|title=Cycles of time and meaning in the Mexican books of fate|last=Boone|first=Elizabeth Hill|publisher=University of Texas Press|accessdate=26 October 2011|year=2007|isbn=978-0-292-71263-8|pages=24, 25–}}</ref>。根据[[波旁尼克手抄本|波旁尼克手抄本]]的描绘，奥索莫科和西帕克托纳尔通常背着烟草或葫芦<ref name="SmithBoone2005" />。在另外一些描述中，双神中的女性常带着蝴蝶面具，用一根导管播撒玉米和豆子<ref name="NowotnyEverett2005">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Y5xFXuYfWaYC&pg=PA61|title=Tlacuilolli: style and contents of the Mexican pictorial manuscripts with a catalog of the Borgia Group|first1=Karl Anton|last2=Everett|first2=George A.|last3=Sisson|first3=Edward B.|publisher=University of Oklahoma Press|accessdate=26 October 2011|year=2005|isbn=978-0-8061-3653-0|page=61|last1=Nowotny}}</ref>。[[佛罗伦萨手抄本|佛罗伦萨手抄本]]中则描绘了奥索莫科给绳子打结时的画面<ref name="Boone2007" />。在雅乌特佩克附近仍矗立着奥索莫科和西帕克托纳尔的石像<ref name="SmithBoone2005">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eiccAQAAIAAJ|title=Painted books and indigenous knowledge in Mesoamerica: manuscript studies in honor of Mary Elizabeth Smith|first1=Mary Elizabeth|last2=Boone|first2=Elizabeth Hill|publisher=Middle American Research Institute|accessdate=26 October 2011|year=2005|isbn=978-0-939238-99-6|page=14|last1=Smith}}</ref>。

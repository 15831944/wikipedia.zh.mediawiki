'''西山飯店'''（{{朝|서산호텔|西山호텔}}）是[[朝鮮民主主義人民共和國|朝鮮民主主義人民共和國]][[平壤直轄市|平壤直轄市]]的一座一級酒店，因坐落於[[大同江|大同江]]、[[普通江|普通江]]交匯的西山山頭之上而得名。<ref name="Korea1998">{{cite book|author=European Union Chamber of Commerce in Korea|title=A Practical business guide on the Democratic People's Republic of Korea|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=gpTwAAAAMAAJ|accessdate=22 April 2013|year=1998|publisher=The Chamber|page=122}}</ref><ref>[http://www.korea-dpr.com/service_facilities.html Democratic People's Republic of Korea - Service facilities] (  {{webarchive|url=https://www.webcitation.org/6Fbtbrezc?url=http://www.korea-dpr.com/service_facilities.html |date=2013-04-03 }})</ref>飯店於1989年建成，其直接目的是為[[第13屆世界青年與學生聯歡節|第13屆]][[世界青年與學生聯歡節|世界青年與學生聯歡節]]的參與者提供食宿。樓體高約95米，有30層，510個間客房。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.emporis.com/building/sosanhotel-pyongyang-northkorea|title=Sosan Hotel|publisher=Emporis|accessdate=22 April 2013}}</ref>距[[平壤站|平壤站]]約4公里。<ref name="StoreyTaylor1995">{{cite book|last1=Storey|first1=Robert|last2=Taylor|first2=Chris|last3=Lindenmayer|first3=Clem|title=North-east Asia: a Lonely Planet shoestring guide|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=tG2yAAAAIAAJ|accessdate=22 April 2013|year=1995|publisher=Lonely Planet Publications|isbn=978-0-86442-250-7|page=427}}</ref>

'''西沙折额蟹'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|''Micippa xishaensis''}} <small>Chen, 1979</small>）为[[蜘蛛蟹總科|蜘蛛蟹總科]][[折额蟹属|折额蟹属]]的动物。折额蟹属原屬[[蜘蛛蟹科|蜘蛛蟹科]][[宝石蟹亚科|宝石蟹亚科]]（Mithracinae）<ref name="sms" />，今屬[[宝石蟹科|宝石蟹科]]（Mithracidae <small>MacLeay, 1838</small>）<ref name = "COL">{{cite web |url= http://www.catalogueoflife.org/annual-checklist/2011/search/all/key/micippa/match/1|title= Species 2000 & ITIS Catalogue of Life: 2011 Annual Checklist.|accessdate=2012-09-24 |authors= Bisby F.A., Roskov Y.R., Orrell T.M., Nicolson D., Paglinawan L.E., Bailly N., Kirk P.M., Bourgoin T., Baillargeon G., Ouvrard D. (red.)|year= 2011|publisher=Species 2000 |location=Reading, UK.}}</ref><ref name="Grave">{{cite journal |journal = [[Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |year=2009 |volume = Suppl. 21 |pages=1–109 |title = A classification of living and fossil genera of decapod crustaceans |first1=Sammy |last1=De Grave |first2=N. Dean |last2=Pentcheff |first3=Shane T. |last3=Ahyong |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s21/s21rbz1-109.pdf |language=en }}</ref>。其物種體較厚實而圓，头胸甲长26.0亳米，19.1亳米。足細長，行動遲緩。多數食腐肉。分布于[[南中国海|南中国海]]的[[西沙群岛|西沙群岛]]<ref name="sms" />。

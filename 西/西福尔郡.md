'''西福尔'''（'''Vestfold'''）[[挪威|挪威]]东南部一郡，毗邻[[布斯克吕|布斯克吕]]和[[泰勒马克|泰勒马克]]。面积2224平方公里,人口239,907。位于奥斯陆峡湾西岸。郡首府[[滕斯贝格|滕斯贝格]]（Tønsberg）。

== 历史 ==

挪威第一位國王[[哈拉尔一世|金发哈拉尔]](Harald Fairhair)的家乡，听说来自这个地区。那时西福尔包括[[Lier,_Norway|Lier]]和[[Eiker|Eiker]]，是一个狭窄王国。西福尔的国王包括:

* Erik Agnarsson
* [[Halfdan_Hvitbeinn|Halfdan Hvitbeinn]] (西福尔的一部分)
* [[Eystein_Halfdansson|Eystein Halfdansson]]
* [[Halfdan_the_Mild|Halfdan the Mild]]
* [[Gudrød_the_Hunter|Gudrød the Hunter]]
* [[Halfdan_the_Black|Halfdan the Black]]，与他的兄弟[[Olaf_Gudrødsson|Olaf Gudrødsson]]一起
* [[Ragnvald_the_Mountain-High|Ragnvald the Mountain-High]]
* [[Harald_Fairhair|Harald Fairhair]]
* [[Bjørn_Farmann|Bjørn Farmann]]
* [[Olaf_Haraldsson_Geirstadalf|Olaf Haraldsson Geirstadalf]]，Bjørn的兄弟
* [[Harald_Gudrødsson_Grenske|Harald Gudrødsson Grenske]], [[976|976]]-[[987|987]]

[[Kaupang|Kaupang]], a town from the [[Viking_Age|Viking Age]] is believed to be the first town in Norway, although [[Tønsberg|Tønsberg]] is the oldest town in Norway still existing. By the [[10th_century|10th century]] the local kings had established themselves as the first dynasty to begin the unification of Norway.

在1918年之前西福尔郡叫''[[Jarlsberg_og_Larvik_Amt|Jarlsberg og Larvik Amt]]''。

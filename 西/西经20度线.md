'''西经20度线'''是本初子午線向西20度的經線，是為西經20°。此經線由[[北極點|北極點]]起穿過[[北冰洋|北冰洋]]、[[格陵蘭|格陵蘭]]、[[冰島|冰島]]、[[大西洋|大西洋]]、[[南冰洋|南冰洋]]以及[[南極洲|南極洲]]直到[[南極點|南極點]]。此段半圓路徑長{{convert|20003932|km}}，於[[赤道|赤道]]與本初子午線距{{convert|2226|km}}遠。<ref name="noaa">{{cite web | language =en | url     = http://www.ngs.noaa.gov/PUBS_LIB/inverse.pdf
 | title   = Direct and Inverse Solutions of Geodesics on the Ellipsoid with application of nested equation
 | author  = T. Vincenty
 | date    = 1975-04
}}</ref><ref name="latlon">{{cite web | language =en | url = http://www.movable-type.co.uk/scripts/latlong-vincenty.html
 | title   = Vincenty formula for distance between two Latitude/Longitude points
 | website = Movable type scripts
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>在某些層面上，此線被用作分開由[[歐亞大陸|歐亞大陸]]及[[非洲|非洲]]組成的[[旧大陆|旧大陆]] 以及由[[美洲|美洲]]組成的[[新大陆|新大陆]]。<ref>{{cite web | language =en | url = https://www.nationalgeographic.org/encyclopedia/hemisphere/
 | title   = hemisphere 
 | website = National Geographic Society
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>

西經20度線與[[東經160度線|東經160度線]]形成一個[[大圓|大圓]]。

於[[南極洲|南極洲]]，此線是[[英屬南極領地|英屬南極領地]]和[[挪威|挪威]]的[[毛德皇后地|毛德皇后地]]的疆界<ref>{{cite web | language =en | url = https://www.arcgis.com/apps/MapJournal/index.html?appid=2b1fd17f462047c087e9ce27152b2379
 | title   = Territorial Claims of the Antarctic
 | website = Esri
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>，而由[[北緯5度線|北緯5度線]]至 [[南纬60度线|南纬60度线]] 這一段经线是[[無核武地帶|拉丁美洲無核武地帶]]的東方疆界<ref>{{cite web | language =en | url = https://www.state.gov/p/wha/rls/70658.htm
 | title   = Treaty for the Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons in Latin America and the Caribbean (Treaty of Tlatelolco)
 | website = [[美國國務院|United States Department of State]]
 | accessdate = 2017-05-17
}}</ref>。

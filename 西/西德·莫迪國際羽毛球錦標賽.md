'''西德·莫迪國際羽毛球錦標賽'''是從2009年開始每年在[[印度|印度]]舉行的[[羽毛球|羽毛球]]比賽，賽事成立初期屬於[[世界羽聯大獎賽|世界羽聯大獎賽]]系列（{{lang|en|The BWF Grand Prix}}）的其中一站，故又名[[印度羽毛球大獎賽|印度羽毛球大獎賽]]。該賽事與[[印度羽毛球公開賽|印度羽毛球公開賽]]為兩項不同的賽事。

== 賽事歷史 ==
為紀念已故羽毛球運動員[[西德·莫迪|西德·莫迪]]，印度北方邦羽球協會在1991年至2003年間在[[勒克瑙|勒克瑙]]舉辦名為西德·莫迪紀念羽毛球錦標賽（{{lang|en|Syed Modi Memorial Badminton Tournament}}）的國內賽事<ref>{{en}}{{Cite news |url=http://cities.expressindia.com/fullstory.php?newsid=110341 |title=Aparna Popat regains his crown at Syed Modi Badminton tourney |date=2004-12-14 |publisher=Lucknow Newsline |accessdate=2019-04-13 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20050404045537/http://cities.expressindia.com/fullstory.php?newsid=110341 |archivedate=2005-04-04}}</ref><ref>{{en}}{{Cite news |url=http://archive.indianexpress.com/news/lucknow-loses-syed-modi-badminton/698550/ |title=Lucknow loses Syed Modi badminton |newspaper=Indian Express |accessdate=2019-04-13 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170607151442/http://archive.indianexpress.com/news/lucknow-loses-syed-modi-badminton/698550/ |archivedate=2010-10-17}}</ref>。2004年時改制為國際賽<ref>{{en}}{{Cite news |url=https://www.thehindu.com/2004/12/14/stories/2004121402581900.htm |title=Contrasting wins for Gopichand, Aparna |date=2004-12-14 |newspaper=[[印度報|印度報]] |accessdate=2019-04-13 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160217084141/http://www.thehindu.com:80/2004/12/14/stories/2004121402581900.htm |archivedate=2016-02-17}}</ref><ref>{{en}}{{Cite news |url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/My-win-is-not-good-for-Indian-badminton-Gopi/articleshow/959022.cms |title=My win is not good for Indian badminton: Gopi |date=2004-12-15 |publisher=印度時報 |accessdate=2019-04-13 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181215080930/https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/My-win-is-not-good-for-Indian-badminton-Gopi/articleshow/959022.cms? |archivedate=2018-12-15}}</ref>，但在2005年至2008年間曾因為北方邦羽球協會與[[北方邦|北方邦]]政府之間的僵局而停辦<ref>{{en}}{{Cite news |url=https://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P3-979279821.html |title=Syed Modi badminton put off |date=2005-11-17 |publisher=HighBeam Research |accessdate=2019-04-13 |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160209180231/https://www.highbeam.com/doc/1P3-979279821.html |archivedate=2016-02-09}}</ref>。2009年，該項賽事以[[西德·莫迪國際羽毛球錦標賽|西德·莫迪國際羽毛球錦標賽]]為名重新開辦，並成為[[世界羽聯大獎賽|世界羽聯大獎賽]]的其中一站。2018年，世界羽聯實施全新的[[世界羽聯世界巡迴賽|世界巡迴賽]]體系，本賽事現屬於第二等別第五級賽事（超級300賽），總獎金提升至15萬美元<ref>{{en}}{{Cite web |url=https://extranet.bwfbadminton.com/docs/events/3171/docs/Syed%20Modi%20International%202018%20_%20Prospectus%20_%20FINAL%20_%20REVISED%2029%20October%202018.pdf |title=Prospectus - SYED MODI INTERNATIONAL BADMINTON CHAMPIONSHIPS – 2018 |accessdate=2019-04-13 |publisher=印度羽球協會 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181103052237/https://extranet.bwfbadminton.com/docs/events/3171/docs/Syed%20Modi%20International%202018%20_%20Prospectus%20_%20FINAL%20_%20REVISED%2029%20October%202018.pdf |archivedate=2018-11-03 |deadurl=no}}</ref>。{{-}}

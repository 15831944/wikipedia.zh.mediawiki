'''西班牙青檸'''（學名：{{lang|la|''Melicoccus bijugatus''}}，又名： Spanish lime、genip、guinep、genipe、ginepa、 kenèp、quenepa、quenepe、chenet、canepa、mamón、limoncillo、skinip、kinnip、huaya、ackee或mamoncillo<ref>Bailey, L.H.; Bailey, E.Z.; the staff of the Liberty Hyde Bailey Hortorium. 1976. ''Hortus third: A concise dictionary of plants cultivated in the United States and Canada''. Macmillan, New York.</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=cjHCoMQNkcgC&pg=PA808 |title=The Encyclopedia of Fruit and Nuts |editor1=Janick, Jules |editor2=Paull, Robert E. |page=808|year=2008 |accessdate=13 July 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=XuluBgAAQBAJ&pg=PA167 |title=Exotic Fruits and Nuts of the New World|author1=Duarte, Odilo |author2=Paull, Robert E. |year=2015 |isbn=9781780645056 |accessdate=13 July 2015}}</ref>），是一種[[無患子科|無患子科]][[蜜果屬|蜜果屬]]植物，吃起來有如真正的[[青檸|青檸]]一樣，甜蜜蜜，又叫蜜果。主要产自于北美洲、南美洲和部分[[加勒比海国家|加勒比海国家]]。

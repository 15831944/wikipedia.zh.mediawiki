'''西捷安可航空'''（{{lang|en|'''WestJet Encore'''}}）是一家總部位於[[加拿大|加拿大]][[艾伯塔省|艾伯塔省]][[卡尔加里|卡尔加里]]的航空公司，於2013年6月成立，它為[[西捷航空|西捷航空]]的[[子公司|子公司]]。同時還執行一些包機任務，例如從麥克納斯特大學到曼尼托巴大學的定期航班。

西捷安可航空在加拿大执飞区域航线，以卡尔加里、多伦多、哈利法克斯出发到达的航线居多。航点包括较小城市，例如[[布兰登|布兰登]]到卡尔加里。<ref name="Brandon and oil industry">{{cite news|last1=Forlanski|first1=Tamara|title=WestJet lands in Brandon with service to Calgary|url=https://globalnews.ca/news/557244/brandon-gets-westjet-service-starting-sept-3/|accessdate=2018-02-09|publisher=Global News|date=2013-05-13|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209122844/https://globalnews.ca/news/557244/brandon-gets-westjet-service-starting-sept-3/|archivedate=2018-02-09}}</ref>其他航线为已有西捷航空增加频率，例如往返卡尔加里与萨斯卡通的航线。<ref name="Saskatoon schedule">{{cite news|last1=Streck|first1=Aaron|title=WestJet Encore touches down in Saskatoon|url=https://globalnews.ca/news/671271/westjet-encore-touches-down-in-saskatoon/|accessdate=2018-02-09|publisher=Global News|date=2013-06-25|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209122839/https://globalnews.ca/news/671271/westjet-encore-touches-down-in-saskatoon/|archivedate=2018-02-09}}</ref>西捷安可航空服务3个加拿大以外的航点：波士顿、纳什维尔、默特尔比奇（季节性）。<ref name="MYR SC">{{cite news|last1=McGee|first1=Kendell|title=Airlines announce more direct flights, lower fares in and out of MYR|url=http://www.wbtw.com/news/airlines-announce-more-direct-flights-lower-fares-in-and-out-of-myr/868551671|accessdate=2018-04-10|publisher=WBTV|date=2017-01-19}}</ref><ref name="BOS service and BNA">{{cite web|title=WestJet Encore inaugurates service to Boston|url=https://www.meetingscanada.com/boston-toronto-westjet-encore-service/|publisher=Meetings Canada|accessdate=2018-02-05|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180209182156/https://www.meetingscanada.com/boston-toronto-westjet-encore-service/|archivedate=2018-02-09}}</ref>

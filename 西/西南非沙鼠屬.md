'''西南非沙鼠屬'''（'''''Gerbillurus'''''）又稱'''南非小沙鼠屬'''，是[[鼠科|鼠科]]中的一個[[屬|屬]]，包含下列[[物種|物種]]：
* [[Hairy-footed_Gerbil|Hairy-footed Gerbil]] (''Gerbillurus paeba'')
* [[Namib_Brush-tailed_Gerbil|Namib Brush-tailed Gerbil]] (''Gerbillurus setzeri'')
* [[Dune_Hairy-footed_Gerbil|Dune Hairy-footed Gerbil]] (''Gerbillurus tytonis'')
* [[Bushy-tailed_Hairy-footed_Gerbil|Bushy-tailed Hairy-footed Gerbil]] (''Gerbillurus vallinus'')

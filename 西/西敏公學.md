'''西敏聖彼得皇家書院'''（{{Lang-en|'''The Royal College of St. Peter in Westminster'''}}），簡稱'''西敏公學'''（'''Westminster School'''），座落於[[倫敦|倫敦]]市中心[[西敏寺|西敏寺]]周邊範圍，<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/641068/Westminster-Abbey |title=Westminster Abbey |publisher=Britannica.com |date= |accessdate=2012-04-26}}</ref>是[[英國|英國]]一所有名的[[獨立學校_(英國)|獨立學校]]。<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.ft.com/cms/s/0/3e8fa0d4-7f9a-11dd-a3da-000077b07658.html | title = How to read the great rebellion | newspaper = Financial Times | location = London | date = 12 September 2008 | first1= Simon | last1 = Briscoe | first2 = David | last2 = Turner }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=This web site is designed and hosted by Biznetix - Your Business Internet Experts |url=http://www.boydell.co.uk/43831546.htm |title=Bibliography of Westminster Abbey, 9781843831549, £55.00/$105.00, 4 2005 |publisher=Boydell.co.uk |date=2005-11-10 |accessdate=2011-08-30}}</ref>

西敏公學歷史可上溯至公元11世紀，歷來知名校友包括[[本·瓊森|本·瓊森]]、[[羅伯特·虎克|羅伯特·虎克]]、<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.roberthooke.org.uk/memorial.htm |title=A Memorial to Robert Hooke 1635 |publisher=Roberthooke.org.uk |date= |accessdate=2011-08-30}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |author=Rod Beavon |url=http://www.rod.beavon.clara.net/robert_hooke.htm |title=Robert Hooke |publisher=Rod.beavon.clara.net |date=1999-03-26 |accessdate=2011-08-30 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110823034809/http://www.rod.beavon.clara.net/robert_hooke.htm |archivedate=2011-08-23 }}</ref>[[克里斯多佛·雷恩|克里斯多佛·雷恩爵士]]、[[約翰·洛克|約翰·洛克]]、[[傑里米·邊沁|傑里米·邊沁]]、<ref>{{cite web|url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=DESdN6WwN_gC&pg=PA1&lpg=PA1&dq=westminster+school&source=bl&ots=c2vQlWCULw&sig=PDMYQMccix5Q49JIcx74ZstwWN8&hl=en&ei=W6D8SZ_fHt7LjAeq1NiRAw&sa=X&oi=book_result&ct=result&resnum=1 |title=Philosophy in the modern world - Google Books |publisher=Books.google.co.uk |date=2007-06-15 |accessdate=2011-08-30}}</ref>[[愛德華·吉朋|愛德華·吉朋]]、[[艾倫·亞歷山大·米恩|A·A·米恩]]、以及其中七任[[英國首相|英國首相]]等。西敏公學傳統上鼓勵學生作獨立批判思考，男生7歲起可入讀該校附屬[[西敏小學|小學]]，然後到13歲升讀中學部；而女生年滿16歲才可入讀。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.boardingschools.hobsons.com/school/Westminster-School-475.html |title=Westminster School - UK Boarding Schools Guide |publisher=Boardingschools.hobsons.com |date= |accessdate=2011-08-30 }}{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>學校約有學生750名，其中約四分一為[[寄宿學校|寄宿生]]，寄宿生通常逢星期六上午校下課後，才會回家渡過周末。<ref>[http://guides.tatler.co.uk/WCS/Schools/2009/Details.aspx?Type=Public&Area=London&ID=2246&List=%20 Schools Guide]{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}, ''The Tatler'', 2009.</ref>

西敏公學是根據《[[1868年公學法令|1868年公學法令]]》下界定9所英國[[公學|公學]]（又稱「克拉倫登學校」）的其中一所，<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.publicschools.co.uk/guide.htm |title=A Guide to Public Schools in the United Kingdom |publisher=Publicschools.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2011-08-30 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090123104454/http://www.publicschools.co.uk/guide.htm |archivedate=2009-01-23 }}</ref>其他「克拉倫登學校」成員還包括[[伊頓公學|伊頓公學]]、[[哈羅公學|哈羅公學]]、[[溫徹斯特公學|溫徹斯特公學]]和[[查特豪斯公學|查特豪斯公學]]等。

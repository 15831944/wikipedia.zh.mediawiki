'''西直门外大街'''是[[北京市|北京市]][[西城區|西城區]]的一条重要道路，因位在[[西直门|西直门]]外故称西直门外大街。位于[[西城区|西城区]]西北部。东起[[西直门|西直门]]立桥，西至[[白石桥路|白石桥路]]。有[[北京展览馆|北京展览馆]]<ref>{{Cite web|url =http://www.expo-china.com/pages/hall/200311/58/index.shtml|title =北京展览馆|accessdate =2015-12-18|archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20160308001323/http://www.expo-china.com/pages/hall/200311/58/index.shtml|archivedate =2016-03-08|deadurl =yes}}</ref>、[[北京动物园|北京动物园]]<ref>{{Cite web|url =http://m.city8.com/bj/chuxingfuwu/M555167_WIIY|title= 北京动物园馆}}</ref>、[[北京天文館|北京天文館]]<ref>{{Cite web|url =https://www.google.com.tw/search?q=%E8%A5%BF%E7%9B%B4%E9%96%80%E5%A4%96%E5%A4%A7%E8%A1%97%E7%9A%84%E6%99%AF%E9%BB%9E&npsic=0&rflfq=1&tbm=lcl&ved=0ahUKEwiwgp778uXJAhVnGaYKHcq_B38Q0CcILQ&rlfi=hd:;si:&tbs=lf_msr:-1,lf:1,lf_ui:1&oll=39.93809050361607,116.33919100000003&ospn=0.003948581824403163,0.00996708869934082&oz=17&fll=39.93814808707693,116.34099344445804&fspn=0.003948578501841382,0.010149478912353516&fz=17&ei=fz10VvDGMeeymAXK_574Bw&qop=1|title= 西直門外大街的景點}}</ref>等景點。

《西藏白话报》（{{lang-bo|ནུབ་བོད་ཀྱི་ཕལ་སྐད་ཚགས་པར}}； 威利轉寫：nub bod kyi phal skad tshags par<ref name="Chayet2010">{{cite book|author=Anne Chayet|title=Edition, Éditions: L'écrit Au Tibet, Évolution Et Devenir|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9FBIAQAAIAAJ|year=2010年|publisher=Indus|isbn=978-3-940659-02-6|page=169頁}}</ref>）是1909年（也有說1907年）清朝[[駐藏大臣|駐藏大臣]][[聯豫|聯豫]]與[[張蔭棠|張蔭棠]]在[[拉萨|拉萨]]创办的{{link-en|石印|Lithography}}报纸。它是西藏最早的报纸，也是中国最早的[[藏文|藏文]]报纸。<ref>{{Cite news| url=http://www.cssn.cn/yyx/yyx_gsjj/201510/t20151003_2482813.shtml| title=浅谈西藏藏文报纸的发展及作用| date=2015年10月3日| newspaper=西藏日报| access-date=2017-09-25| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170926042453/http://www.cssn.cn/yyx/yyx_gsjj/201510/t20151003_2482813.shtml| archive-date=2017-09-26| dead-url=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=中国西藏| url=https://books.google.com/books?id=W5t4fMW_YugC&pg=PA140| year=2004年| publisher=五洲传播出版社| isbn=978-7-5085-0607-4| pages=140頁}}</ref><ref name="BernstorffWelck2003">{{cite book|author1=Dagmar Bernstorff| author2=Hubertus von Welck|title=''Exile as Challenge: The Tibetan Diaspora''| url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_lLiIvbf9S4C&pg=PA171| year=2003| publisher=Orient Blackswan| isbn=978-81-250-2555-9|page=171頁}}</ref>

聯豫與張蔭棠為了推行改革，實行新政，決定創辦《西藏白话报》。《西藏白话报》每十日發行不到一百份（也有說三、四百份）。當時多數漢人與藏人不懂彼此語言，因此《西藏白话报》同時使用漢文與藏文。<ref>{{Cite web| title=''The earliest Tibetan newspaper in Tibet''| date=2005-07-01| author=白润生| url=http://en.tibet.cn/culture/tibetology/200801/t20080117_355641.htm| archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151117030936/http://en.tibet.cn/culture/tibetology/200801/t20080117_355641.htm| archivedate=2015-11-17| website=China Tibet Information Center| deadurl=yes}}{{en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news | url=http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/china/2008-09/25/content_7059864_7.htm | title=Protection and Development of Tibetan Culture | newspaper=China Daily | date=2008-09-25 | access-date=2017-10-02 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304052111/http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/china/2008-09/25/content_7059864_7.htm | archive-date=2016-03-04 | dead-url=no }}{{en}}</ref>

目前沒有任何史料顯示《西藏白话报》對藏人有影響。<ref>{{Cite thesis | title=''The Melong: An Example of the Formation of a Tibetan Language Press'' | author=Anna Sawerthal | degree=MA | year=2011年 | publisher=University of Vienna, Austria | url=http://library.columbia.edu/content/dam/libraryweb/locations/eastasian/tibet_guide/Melong_Anna_Sawerthal.pdf | format=PDF | pages=55頁 | access-date=2017-09-28 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304104049/http://library.columbia.edu/content/dam/libraryweb/locations/eastasian/tibet_guide/Melong_Anna_Sawerthal.pdf | archive-date=2016-03-04 | dead-url=no }}{{en}}</ref>

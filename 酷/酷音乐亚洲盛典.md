'''酷音乐亚洲盛典''' ({{Lang-en|Ku Music Asian Music Awards}})是是[[腾讯|腾讯]]子公司[[酷狗音乐|酷狗音乐]]和[[酷我音乐|酷我音乐]]自2015年开始举办的年度音乐奖项。<ref name="Kim">{{cite web |url= https://sg.style.yahoo.com/kang-ta-shinee-garner-awards-at-ku-music-045041789.html|title= Kang Ta & SHINee garner awards at 'KU MUSIC ASIAN MUSIC AWARDS'|author= Kim Dong-Joo|date= March 31, 2016|accessdate= August 20, 2016|work= sg.style.yahoo.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.soompi.com/2015/04/23/ftisland-wins-asias-popular-band-award-at-cool-music-asia-festival-award/|title= FTISLAND Wins "Asia’s Popular Band" Award at Cool Music Asia Festival Award|date= April 23, 2015|accessdate= August 20, 2016|work= [[Soompi|Soompi]]}}</ref><ref name="Woorim">{{cite web |url= http://www.bntnews.co.uk/app/news.php?nid=29831|title= Hwang Chi Yeol Won ‘Most Able Vocalist of the Year’ in China|author= Woorim Ahn|date= March 30, 2016|accessdate= August 20, 2016|work= BNTNews}}</ref>

'''蓝梦'''（{{lang-en|''Blue Dream''}}），又称'''蓝莓阴霾'''<ref name=backes>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=RQ9oBAAAQBAJ&lpg=PA124&vq=Blue%20dream&pg=PA124#v=snippet&q=Blue%20dream&f=false|title=Cannabis Pharmacy: The Practical Guide to Medical Marijuana|last=Backes|first=Michael|date=September 9, 2014|publisher=Black Dog & Leventhal|page=124-125|accessdate=2017-03-12}}</ref>，属主导[[Sativa|Sativa]]类型的[[杂交大麻|杂交大麻]]<ref name=hecht>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UUaYAgAAQBAJ&lpg=PA240&vq=blue%20dream&pg=PA92#v=snippet&q=blue%20dream&f=false|title=Weed Land: Inside America's Marijuana Epicenter and How Pot Went Legit|last=Hecht|first=Peter|date=May 2, 2014|publisher=[[University_of_California_Press|University of California Press]]|page=92|accessdate=2017-03-12}}</ref>，于2003年左右在[[美国|美国]][[加利福尼亚州|加利福尼亚州]]的[[圣克鲁斯县|圣克鲁斯县]]使用[[蓝莓_(大麻)|蓝莓]]与[[阴霾_(大麻)|阴霾]]杂交制成<ref name=backes />。

蓝梦是通过扦插繁殖的克隆株，以生产大型收成而闻名<ref name=king>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ioVP9F17-s0C&lpg=PT120&vq=blue%20dream&pg=PT120#v=snippet&q=blue%20dream&f=false|title=The Cannabible 3|last=King|first=Jason|date=April 20, 2011|publisher=Potter/TenSpeed/Harmony|accessdate=2017-03-12}}</ref>，每株高达七盎司<ref name=jameson>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0KTJAwAAQBAJ&lpg=PA18&vq=blue%20dream&pg=PA18#v=snippet&q=blue%20dream&f=false|title=Nick's Notes: My 1st Half Dozen Indoor Medical Marijuana Growing Lessons|last=Jameson|first=Nick|date=2012|page=18|accessdate=2017-03-12}}</ref>。在一些开放医疗用大麻的国家，其被广泛用于医疗用途，截止2014年，测试显示其含有高达25％的[[四氢大麻酚|四氢大麻酚]]（THC）和2％的[[大麻二酚|大麻二酚]]（CBD）<ref name=backes />。

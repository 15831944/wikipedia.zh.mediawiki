'''黎巴嫩－马来西亚关系'''（{{lang-ms|Hubungan Lebanon-Malaysia}}）是指[[黎巴嫩|黎巴嫩]]和[[马来西亚|马来西亚]]之间的双边关系，黎巴嫩在[[吉隆坡|吉隆坡]]设有一间大使馆<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.foreign.gov.lb/files/Lebanese%20diplomatic%20missions%20abroad.pdf|title=Lebanese Embassies, Missions, Consulates, Honorary Consulates abroad|publisher=Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Emigrants of the Republic of Lebanon|accessdate=4 May 2014|page=13|format=PDF}}</ref>，马来西亚也在[[贝鲁特|贝鲁特]]设有一间大使馆。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kln.gov.my/web/lbn_beirut/home|title=Official Website of Embassy of Malaysia, Beirut|publisher=Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Malaysia|accessdate=4 May 2014}}</ref>

==安全关系==
马来西亚曾经是{{link-en|联合国驻黎巴嫩临时部队|United Nations Interim Force in Lebanon}}的成员国，当时马来西亚共派遣了877名维和人员。

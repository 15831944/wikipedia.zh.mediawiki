'''蠟蘑屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Laccaria''）是[[軸腹菌科|軸腹菌科]]的一個[[屬_(生物)|屬]]，共有約75種物種，分布於世界各地溫帶與熱帶的區域<ref name=Kirk2008>{{cite book |vauthors=Kirk PM, Cannon PF, Minter DW, Stalpers JA |title=Dictionary of the Fungi |edition=10th |publisher=CABI |location=Wallingford |year=2008|page=357|isbn=0-85199-826-7}}</ref><ref>Mueller GM. (1985). Numerical taxonomic analyses on ''Laccaria'' (Agaricales). ''Mycologia'' 77: 121-129.</ref>，常與植物形成{{link-en|外菌根|Ectomycorrhiza}}。本屬的[[模式種|模式種]]為[[紅蠟蘑|紅蠟蘑]]，另外[[紫蠟蘑|紫蠟蘑]]與{{link-en|雙色蠟蘑|Laccaria_bicolor}}亦是本屬的常見物種。有些本屬物種可在實驗室的培養基上生長，還有些物種的[[擔孢子|擔孢子]]可在培養基上萌發，可作為研究外菌根的[[模式生物|模式生物]]<ref>[[G._K._Podila|Podila GK]], Zheng J, Balasubramanian S, Sundaram S, Hiremath S, Brand JH, Hymes MJ. (2002). Fungal gene expression in early symbiotic interactions between ''Laccaria bicolor'' and red pine. ''Plant and Soil'' 244: 117–128.</ref>，其中雙色蠟蘑是第一種[[基因組|基因組]]被[[已測序的生物|定序]]的外菌根真菌<ref>{{Cite journal| last1 = Martin | first1 = F.| last2 = Selosse | first2 = M.| title = The ''Laccaria'' genome: a symbiont blueprint decoded| journal = New Phytologist| volume = 180| issue = 2| pages = 296–310| year = 2008| pmid = 19138220| doi = 10.1111/j.1469-8137.2008.02613.x}}</ref>。本屬物種有四種交配型，任兩種不同交配型間均可交配<ref>{{cite journal|author=Martin F, Selosse MA.|year=2008)|title=The ''Laccaria'' genome: a symbiont blueprint decoded|publisher=''New Phytologist''|volume=180|issue=2|page=296-310}}</ref>。

==描述==
蠟蘑屬物種的[[蕈褶|蕈褶]]通常較粗且間距較大，顏色為紫色至肉色，與[[蕈柄|蕈柄]]相連（adnate）或稍微沿蕈柄向下生長（decurrent）。多數物種的[[擔孢子|擔孢子]]為白色，且表面有飾物<ref name="isbn0-520-03660-3">{{cite book |author=Orr, Dorothy B. |author2=Orr, Robert Thomas |title=Mushrooms of Western North America (California Natural History Guides) |publisher=University of California Press |location=Berkeley |year=1980 |pages= |isbn=0-520-03660-3 |oclc= |doi= |accessdate=}}</ref>。

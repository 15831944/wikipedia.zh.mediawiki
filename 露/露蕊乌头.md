'''露蕊乌头'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{sname|'''''Gymnaconitum gymnandrum'''''}}）为[[毛茛科|毛茛科]][[露蕊乌头属|露蕊乌头属]]的唯一一个[[种|种]]，曾经属于[[乌头属|乌头属]]。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=''Gymnaconitum'', a new genus of Ranunculaceae endemic to the Qinghai-Tibetan Plateau|author=Wang, Wei; Liu, Yang; Yu, Sheng-Xiang; Gao, Tian-Gang; Chen, Zhi-Duan|url=http://www.jstor.org/stable/taxon.62.4.713|journal=Taxon|issue=4|doi=|others=|year=2013|volume=62|page=713-722|pmid=}}</ref>为总状花序，约3-30朵蓝紫色花，顶向式开花，花期6–8月,果期7–9月<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Li|first1=Lin|last2=Lu|first2=Ningna|last3=Fan|first3=Baoli|last4=Zhao|first4=Zhigang|title=Effect of flowering time on floral sexual durations and phenotypic gender in dichogamous Aconitum gymnandrum|journal=Biodiversity Science|date=2016|volume=24|issue=6|pages=665–671|doi=10.17520/biods.2015254}}</ref>。

露蕊乌头依靠虫媒传粉，传粉昆虫主要是[[熊蜂|熊蜂]]类<ref>{{cite journal|last1=Lu|first1=N.-N.|last2=Li|first2=X.-H.|last3=Li|first3=L.|last4=Zhao|first4=Z.-G.|title=Variation of nectar production in relation to plant characteristics in protandrous Aconitum gymnandrum|journal=Journal of Plant Ecology|date=2015-03-26|volume=8|issue=2|pages=122–129|doi=10.1093/jpe/rtv020}}</ref>。

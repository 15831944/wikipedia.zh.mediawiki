'''烯丙基环戊二烯基钯'''是一种[[有机钯化学|有机钯化合物]]，化学式为(C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)Pd(C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)。它是红色具有挥发性的化合物，有不愉快的气味。它可溶于大部分有机溶剂，它是[[夹心型配合物|夹心型配合物]]，[[环戊二烯|环戊二烯]]环与[[烯丙基|烯丙基]]配体将Pd夹在中间。<ref name=Otsuka/>

==制备==
该配合物可由[[氯化烯丙基鈀二聚物|氯化烯丙基鈀二聚物]]和[[环戊二烯基钠|环戊二烯基钠]]反应得到：<ref name=Otsuka>{{cite journal | author = Y. Tatsuno, T. Yoshida, S. Otsuka | title = (3-Allyl)Palladium(II) Complexes | journal = [[Inorg._Synth.|Inorg. Synth.]] | year = 1979 | volume = 19 | pages = 220 | doi = 10.1002/9780470132500 | series = Inorganic Syntheses | editor1-last = Shriver | editor1-first = Duward F. | isbn = 978-0-470-13250-0}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | author = R. B. King | title = Organometallic Chemistry of the Transition Metals. Organometallic Chemistry of the Transition Metals. III. Reactions between Sodium Cyclopentadienide and Certain Complex Transition Metal Halides | journal = [[Inorg._Chem.|Inorg. :2 C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>5</sub>Na  + (C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)<sub>2</sub>Pd<sub>2</sub>Cl<sub>2</sub>   →   2 (C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>4</sub>)Pd(C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>5</sub>)  +  2 NaCl

==结构与反应==
这个[[18电子规则|18电子配合物]]采取具有[[分子对称性|C<sub>s</sub>对称性]]的半夹心结构，即分子具有对称平面。该配合物容易通过[[还原消除反应|还原消除反应]]分解。
:C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>5</sub>PdC<sub>3</sub>H<sub>5</sub> → Pd(0) + C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>5</sub>C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>5</sub>

该化合物易和[[异腈|异腈]]反应产生具有近似式[Pd(CNR)<sub>2</sub>]<sub>''n''</sub>的簇。 它与大体积的烷基膦反应生成二配位的钯(0)[[配合物|配合物]]：<ref>{{cite journal |author1=Otsuka, S. |author2=Yoshida, T. |author3=Matsumoto, M. |author4=Nakatsu, K. J. | title = Cyclohexyneplatinum(0) Complexes Containing Di-t-butylphenylphosphine, t-butyldiphenylphosphine or Trimethylphosphine | year = 1976 | journal = [[Australian_Journal_of_Chemistry|Australian Journal of Chemistry]] | volume = 45 | issue = 1 | pages = 135–142 | doi = 10.1071/CH9920135}}</ref>
 
:CpPd(allyl)  +  2 PR<sub>3</sub>   →    Pd(PR<sub>3</sub>)<sub>2</sub>  +  C<sub>5</sub>H<sub>5</sub>C<sub>3</sub>H<sub>5</sub>

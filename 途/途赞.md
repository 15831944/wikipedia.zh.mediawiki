'''途赞''' (英语: chozun)，原名"AFTR", 是一个用于帮助旅行者在途旅中匹配自己生活方式的一个旅行移动平台。<ref>{{cite news|title=Business App of the Month - chozun|url=http://www.intellectuapp.com/2016/06/travel-app-of-month-chozun.html#.WHeTptIrKG4|accessdate=12 January 2017|publisher=''intellectuapp.com''|date=19 June 2016}}</ref><ref>KATY CARIC, {{cite news|title=Expats: Find The Best Services On The Go with Chozun!|url=https://hongkong.theexpat.com/blog/expats-find-the-best-services-on-the-go-with-chozun/|accessdate=12 January 2017|publisher=''hongkong.theexpat.com''|date=15 August 2016}}</ref><ref>DENHAM SADLER, {{cite news|title=App aims to let travellers find a bit of home wherever they are|url=http://www.startupsmart.com.au/advice/business-planning/app-aims-to-let-travellers-find-a-bit-of-home-wherever-they-are/|accessdate=12 January 2017|publisher=''hongkong.theexpat.com''|date=13 October 2015}}</ref>

==历史==
途赞由两位女创始人Zia Word和Teresa Truda于2015年在[[墨尔本|墨尔本]]创立。<ref>{{cite news|title=Problem solving with Teresa Truda, CEO and Founder of chozun|url=https://www.meetup.com/en-AU/The-Melbourne-Entrepreneurs-Networking-Group/events/234743677/|accessdate=12 January 2017|publisher=''meetup.com''|date=3 November 2016}}</ref><ref>Gerald Ainomugisha, {{cite news|title=Reppin’ Australia right: Meet the Melbourne tech start-up that won the US$50,000 grand prize at an Asian competition|url=http://anthillonline.com/flying-the-aussie-flag-high-meet-the-melbourne-tech-start-up-that-won-us50000-grand-prize-at-an-asian-competition/|accessdate=12 January 2017|publisher=''anthillonline.com''|date=3 November 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=AFTR from Australia wins Asia Super Team 2015 in Taiwan|url=http://bizeventsasia.com/Article/Details/1679|accessdate=12 January 2017|publisher=''bizeventsasia.com''|date=}}{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> 几个月后，她们决定扩张亚洲市场，并以中国出境游旅客为重心。途赞目前将其总部设立在中国[[上海市|上海]]，并在墨尔本及东南亚设立办公室。<ref>{{cite news|title=Teresa Truda IN CONVERSATION WITH RACHEL SHEPHEARD|url=http://fridaybestmag.com/interview-teresa-truda/|publisher=''fridaybestmag.com''|date=}}</ref> 途赞有一支专业的技术团队，通过人工智能，大数据等帮助用户匹配相应的服务商户<ref>{{cite news|title=Meet Taiwan Announced 2015 Asia Super Team Winner|url=http://www.iccaworld.org/newsarchives/archivedetails.cfm?id=5227|publisher=''iccaworld.org''|date=}}</ref>

==服务==
途赞根据旅客的喜好<ref>{{cite news|title=Chozun - Personalized On-Demand Services|url=http://www.intellectuapp.com/2016/06/chozun-personalized-on-demand-services.html#.WHeTq9IrKG4|publisher=''intellectuapp.com''|date=15 June 2016}}</ref> 提供值得信赖的个性化服务和当地体验。旅客可在移动平台通过信用卡等其它方式来预定并支付所需服务。目前服务范围覆盖亚洲，东南亚及澳洲，IOS & 安卓用户均可使用。<ref>{{cite news|title=Chozun - Your New Best App Travel Companion|url=http://www.appstov.com/2016/06/chozun-your-new-best-app-travel.html|publisher=''appstov.com''|date=}}</ref>

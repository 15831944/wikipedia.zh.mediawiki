'''莫普拉人'''是印度南部[[喀拉拉邦|喀拉拉邦]]同名穆斯林社区的成员<ref name="Miller1">Miller, E. Roland. "Mappila Muslim Culture" State University of New York Press, Albany (2015); p. xi.</ref><ref name=Panikkar>Panikkar, K. N., ''Against Lord and State: Religion and Peasant Uprisings in Malabar 1836–1921''. Oxford University Press, 1989</ref>，是喀拉拉邦穆斯林的重要主体。2011年时占全邦人口的的26.56%，为第二大族群，仅次于印度教徒（54.73%）<ref name="census_2016_religion">{{cite web |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/C-01/DDW00C-01%20MDDS.XLS |title=Population by religious community - 2011 |publisher=Office of the Registrar General & Census Commissioner |work={{tsl|en|2011 Census of India||2011 Census of India}} |accessdate=2015-08-25 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150825155850/http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/C-01/DDW00C-01%20MDDS.XLS |archivedate=2015-08-25}}</ref>。莫普拉人与喀拉拉邦其他宗教社区一样，皆使用[[馬拉亞利人|馬拉亞利人]]的[[马拉雅拉姆语|马拉雅拉姆语]]<ref name="brill">Miller, Roland. E., "Mappila" in "The Encyclopedia of Islam". Volume VI. E. J. Brill, Leiden. 1987 [https://books.google.co.in/books?id=ChEVAAAAIAAJ&redir_esc=y]. pp. 458-56.</ref><ref>" "Oh! Calicut!" ''Outlook Traveller'' {{cite web |url=http://traveller.outlookindia.com/issuecontent.aspx?pagenum=0&id=1077 |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2012-02-17 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20120715200049/http://traveller.outlookindia.com/issuecontent.aspx?pagenum=0&id=1077 |archivedate=2012-07-15  }} December, 2009</ref>。

一些学者认为，莫普拉为南亚定居于此最古老的穆斯林社区<ref name="Miller1" /><ref name="TheEncyclopediaofIslam2">Miller, R. E. "Mappila" in ''The Encyclopedia of Islam'' Volume VI. Leiden E. J. Brill 1988 p. 458-66 [https://books.google.co.in/books?id=SiBkMSIZ2LYC&dq=editions:lTASeHyksMsC&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwik6pvDw-vbAhWLKo8KHdNqBeUQ6AEIKzAB]</ref>。总言之，莫普拉人包括所有本土穆斯林的后裔以及中东人（既有阿拉伯人，又有非阿拉伯人）的混血后裔<ref name="HafizMohamadNP">Hafiz Mohamad, N. P. "Socioeconomic determinants of the continuity of matrilocal family system among Mappila Muslims of Malabar" Unpublished PhD thesis (2013) Department of History, University of Calicut [http://hdl.handle.net/10603/11310]</ref><ref name="PPRazakAbdul">P. P., Razak Abdul "Colonialism and community formation in Malabar: a study of muslims of Malabar" Unpublished PhD thesis (2013) Department of History, University of Calicut [http://hdl.handle.net/10603/13105]</ref>。

莫普拉社区起源于西亚与喀拉拉之间的商业往来<ref name=Panikkar />。早在公元7世纪，伊斯兰教徒便来到了喀拉拉邦所在的[[马拉巴尔海岸|马拉巴尔海岸]]<ref name="brill" />。在欧洲人于香料贸易中抢先之前，莫普拉是一个繁荣的贸易社区，主要位于喀拉拉海岸城市的中心区域。莫普拉人与中东源源不断地交流对本身的生活、习俗与文化产生了巨大影响，形成了独特的印度——伊斯兰结合文化，涵盖了文学、艺术、食物、语言及音乐等多个方面<ref name="brill" />。

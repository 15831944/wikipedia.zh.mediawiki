'''莫林宫'''（Palazzo Molin del Cuoridoro）是[[意大利|意大利]][[威尼斯|威尼斯]]的一座建于十五世纪的历史保护建筑，位于[[圣马可区|圣马可区]]<ref>The Times, 18 January 2013, 'Palazzo is the Perfect Party Pad' http://www.thetimes.co.uk/tto/life/property/overseas/article3660344.ece</ref>，靠近历史悠久的[[凤凰剧院|凤凰剧院]]<ref>Palazzomolin.com http://www.palazzomolin.com/palazzo/index.php</ref>。

该建筑是第九十九任威尼斯总督弗朗切斯科·莫林的公馆，为威尼斯哥特式风格，其主立面是哥特式花卉风格的一个例子<ref>Il Sole 24 Ore, 'Palazzo Molin del Cuoridoro a Venezia' (13 November 2012), http://www.living24.it/palazzo-molin-del-cuoridoro-a-venezia/0,1254,58_ART_7960,00.html</ref>，在十四世纪和十五世纪颇为流行，入口上方有莫林家族的纹章<ref>Palazzomolin.com http://www.palazzomolin.com/palazzo/history.php</ref>。被列为历史保护建筑。该建筑于2013年修复，并改建为18套公寓<ref>Evening Standard, 'National debt isn't always bad for the overseas homes market' (17 December 2012), http://www.homesandproperty.co.uk/property_news/holiday_homes/homesmarketnationaldebt.html</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://property.luxos.com/index.php/latest-properties/For-Sale/29-18-Luxury-Apartments-on-Sale-in-Palazzo-Molin |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2013-02-13 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130411072226/http://property.luxos.com/index.php/latest-properties/For-Sale/29-18-Luxury-Apartments-on-Sale-in-Palazzo-Molin |archivedate=2013-04-11 }}</ref>，同时保持了宫殿的历史特征，包括两个水闸和窗户<ref>London Evening Standard, 'Buy in Venice and enjoy the love affair' (26 October 2012), http://www.homesandproperty.co.uk/property_news/holiday_homes/buyinveniceandenjoytheloveaffair.html</ref>。

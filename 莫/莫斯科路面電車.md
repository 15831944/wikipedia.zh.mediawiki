'''莫斯科路面電車'''分為兩個網路系統，於1872年開始運行<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.railjournal.com/index.php/light-rail/fokstrot-lrvs-enter-service-in-moscow.html?channel=542|title=Fokstrot LRVs enter service in Moscow|last=Barrow|first=Keith|date=3 June 2014|work=International Railway Journal|accessdate=4 June 2014}}</ref>。莫斯科路面電車的兩個網路全長{{convert|181|km|mi|abbr=on}}，是世界第四大路面電車網，僅次於[[墨爾本輕鐵|墨爾本輕鐵]]、[[聖彼得堡路面電車|聖彼得堡路面電車]]和[[柏林街車|柏林街車]]<ref name="wiener linien">{{cite web |url=http://www.wienerlinien.at/wl/ep/contentView.do/contentTypeId/1001/channelId/-26075/programId/9419/pageTypeId/9081/contentId/25061 |title=Wien hat das fünftgrößte Straßenbahnnetz der Welt |language=de |trans_title=Vienna has the fifth largest tramway network in the world |publisher=[[Wiener_Linien|Wiener Linien]] |website=www.wienerlinien.at |year=2011 |accessdate=31 August 2013 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131031054522/http://www.wienerlinien.at/eportal/ep/contentView.do/contentTypeId/1001/channelId/-26075/programId/9419/pageTypeId/9081/contentId/25061 |archivedate=31 October 2013}}</ref>，是莫斯科第二個公共交通系統。

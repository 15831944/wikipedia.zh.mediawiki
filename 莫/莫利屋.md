'''莫利屋'''（{{Lang-en|Molly house}}）是18世紀時期英格蘭的一種面向同性戀及異裝癖人士的酒吧和社交場所，是今日同性戀酒吧的原型之一。在18世紀的英格蘭，「莫利」一詞雖然有女性之意，但通常是指同性戀男性<ref name=Grose1796>{{Cite document |last=Grose |first=Francis |year=1796 |title=A classical dictionary of the vulgar tongue |edition=3 |publisher=Printed for Hooper and Wigstead |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=TbJKAAAAMAAJ |quote=MOLLY, a miss Molly, an effeminate fellow, a sodomite.}}</ref><ref>[http://rictornorton.co.uk/eighteen/molly2.htm The Gay subculture in eighteenth century England] Rictor Norton Quote: However, I think we have to exercise some caution and avoid jumping to the conclusion that just because we do not hear of the molly subculture or effeminate queens before 1700, therefore they did not exist until 1700.</ref>。最著名的莫利屋是位於倫敦的Margaret Clap。

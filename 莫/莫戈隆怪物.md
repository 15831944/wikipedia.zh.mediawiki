'''莫戈隆怪物'''（英语：Mogollon Monster）是一种出没于[[美国|美国]][[亞利桑那州|亚利桑那州]][[莫戈隆边缘|莫戈隆边缘]]的[[神秘生物列表|神秘生物]]，它通常被描述为一种类似[[大腳怪|大脚怪]]的生物。

== 描述 ==
据报道，莫戈隆怪物身高约7英尺（2.1米），身体被红色或黑色毛发覆盖，散发着令人作呕的气味，如同[[臭鼬猿|臭鼬猿]]一般。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.weirdus.com/states/arizona/bizarre_beasts/mongollon_monster/index.php|title=Weird Arizona|accessdate=2018-05-12|work=www.weirdus.com}}</ref>在[[美國原住民|美国原住民]]的传说中，莫戈隆怪物是一名被转化的[[人|人类]]。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://visitcryptoville.com/2015/06/25/mogollon-monster-of-arizona/|title=Mogollon Monster of Arizona|date=2015-06-25|newspaper=CryptoVille|accessdate=2018-05-12|language=en-US}}</ref>

== 目击 ==
莫戈隆怪物的首次目击可追溯至1903年，{{link-en|《亚利桑那共和报》|The Arizona Republic}}{{NoteTag|另有资料提及目击者曾接受《威廉斯新闻》（Williams News）采访。|name="not only"}}）报道目击者I.W. Stevens在[[科羅拉多大峽谷|科罗拉多大峡谷]]遭遇一只白色毛发的[[野人|野人]]的新闻。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.dcourier.com/news/2017/oct/28/searching-mogollon-monster/|title=Mogollon Monster has been seen in Grand Canyon area and as far south as Prescott Valley?|newspaper=The Daily Courier|accessdate=2018-05-12|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=http://visitcryptoville.com/2015/06/25/mogollon-monster-of-arizona/|title=Mogollon Monster of Arizona|date=2015-06-25|newspaper=CryptoVille|accessdate=2018-05-12|language=en-US}}</ref>已逝世的神秘动物学家唐·戴维斯（Don Davis）表示在1940年的{{link-en|佩森|Payson, Arizona}}他曾目击过莫戈隆怪物。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.azcentral.com/story/opinion/op-ed/claythompson/2017/03/29/ask-clay-gather-round-tales-mogollon-monster/99749318/?from=new-cookie|title=Ask Clay: Gather round for tales of the Mogollon Monster|newspaper=azcentral|accessdate=2018-05-12|language=en}}</ref>最近的目击来自2014年，有目击者向《Cryptozoology News》报告在佩森看见莫戈隆怪物。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.dcourier.com/news/2017/oct/28/searching-mogollon-monster/|title=Mogollon Monster has been seen in Grand Canyon area and as far south as Prescott Valley?|newspaper=The Daily Courier|accessdate=2018-05-12|language=en}}</ref>

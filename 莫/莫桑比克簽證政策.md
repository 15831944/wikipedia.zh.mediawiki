==落地签==

持有证明出于休闲目的旅行的文件（回程机票，住宿确认或邀请函），所有国家的公民都可以在抵达所有边境口岸时获得落地签。该签证有效期为30天。尽管签证表明只单次入境有效，但前往斯威士兰和南非的游客发现，如果仍然在30天的有效期内，则依然可二次入境。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mozambicanhotels.com/tourist-information/mozambique-tourism-news/mozambique-tourist-visa-now-valid-for-two-entries|title=Mozambique Tourist Visa Now Valid for Two Entries - Mozambican Hotels|website=www.mozambicanhotels.com|accessdate=20 March 2018}}</ref>马普托机场最近开始接受信用卡支付签证费。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mozambicanhotels.com/tourist-information/mozambique-tourism-news/tourism-visas-can-now-be-paid-for-with-credit-cards-at-maputo-international-airport|title=Tourism Visas can now be paid for with Credit Cards at Maputo International Airport - Mozambican Hotels|website=www.mozambicanhotels.com|accessdate=6 December 2017}}</ref>

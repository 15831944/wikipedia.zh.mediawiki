'''Wm莫里遜超市公司'''（Wm Morrison Supermarkets plc），是[[英國|英國]]的第四大連鎖[[超市|超市]]，僅次於[[樂購|樂購]]、[[森寶利|森寶利]]、[[阿斯達_(超市)|阿斯達]]<ref name="co-op">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/business/7508982.stm |title=Co-op buys Somerfield for £1.57bn |publisher=BBC News |date=16 July 2008 |accessdate=12 March 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.theweek.co.uk/64830/morrisons-cuts-petrol-price-to-99p-a-litre |title=Morrisons cuts petrol price to 99p a litre |publisher=The Week |date=22 November 2016 |accessdate=22 November 2016}}</ref>，總部位於[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[西約克郡|西約克郡]][[布拉德福德|布拉德福德]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://your.morrisons.com/Help-and-information/Contact-Us/ |title=Contact Us |publisher=Wm Morrison Supermarkets PLC|accessdate=26 July 2014}}</ref>。莫里遜超市由威廉·莫里遜創建於1899年。在2004年之前，莫里遜主要在英格蘭北部地區開設店鋪。但在經過併購之後，該公司在南英格蘭、威爾斯和蘇格蘭的店鋪明顯增加。2016年，莫里遜在英格蘭、威爾斯、蘇格蘭共有498家店鋪，並且在直布羅陀也有一家店鋪<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/finance/newsbysector/retailandconsumer/11054892/Morrisons-in-Gibraltar-the-unlikely-success-story.html|title=Morrisons in Gibraltar – the unlikely success story|publisher=|accessdate=10 July 2016}}</ref>。2015年，莫里遜佔英國超市業界11%的市場份額<ref name="business-reporter1">[http://business-reporter.co.uk/2015/06/30/morrisons-tops-supermarket-sales-growth-figures/  ''Morrisons tops supermarket sales growth figures'', Business Reporter, Daily Telegraph, London, 30 June 2015] {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304185834/http://business-reporter.co.uk/2015/06/30/morrisons-tops-supermarket-sales-growth-figures/ |date=4 March 2016 }}.Retrieved: 14 September 2015.</ref>。該公司也是[[倫敦證券交易所|倫敦證券交易所]]的上市公司之一。

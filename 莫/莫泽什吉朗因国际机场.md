== 蒂米卡机场枪击事件 ==

当地时间1996年4月15日，萨努利少尉（Sanurip）在蒂米卡机场使用Pindad SS1突击步枪对士兵开枪。事件共造成16人死亡（其中五人为平民，当中包括一名新西兰人），11人非致命伤，被定性为屠杀<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.library.ohiou.edu/indopubs/1996/04/17/0001.html|title=IN: Timika Airport Killings|accessdate=2017-01-08|last=(apakabar@clark.net)|first=apakabar@clark.net|work=www.library.ohiou.edu|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160306011912/https://www.library.ohiou.edu/indopubs/1996/04/17/0001.html|archive-date=2016-03-06|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。萨努利少尉被击中腿部后被其他士兵逮捕<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/news/article/WORLD-DATELINES-3147225.php|title=WORLD DATELINES|newspaper=SFGate|accessdate=2017-01-08}}</ref>。事件动机不明，推测Sanurip少尉患有抑郁症，也可能患有疟疾<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.library.ohiou.edu/indopubs/1996/04/18/0009.html|title=IN/IRJA: KMP - ABRI Officer Kills 1|accessdate=2017-01-08|last=(apakabar@clark.net)|first=apakabar@clark.net|work=www.library.ohiou.edu|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160306133816/https://www.library.ohiou.edu/indopubs/1996/04/18/0009.html|archive-date=2016-03-06|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，还有人认为萨努利少尉刚与上司发生争执<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.library.ohiou.edu/indopubs/1996/04/18/0022.html|title=IN/IRJA: More Reports on Timika Kil|accessdate=2017-01-08|last=(apakabar@clark.net)|first=apakabar@clark.net|work=www.library.ohiou.edu|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120213141403/http://www.library.ohiou.edu/indopubs/1996/04/18/0022.html|archive-date=2012-02-13|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

1997年4月23日，萨努利少尉在查亚普拉军事法庭被判处死刑。1997年6月18日高等军事法庭在[[泗水|泗水]]驳回萨努利少尉的上诉，于是他向印尼最高法院上诉<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.library.ohiou.edu/indopubs/1997/09/19/0017.html|title=[INDONESIA-L] AI - Death Penalty Ap|accessdate=2017-01-08|last=(apakabar@clark.net)|first=apakabar@clark.net|work=www.library.ohiou.edu|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170109022328/https://www.library.ohiou.edu/indopubs/1997/09/19/0017.html|archive-date=2017-01-09|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。期间在医院死亡<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.ie/books?id=BdETAQAAMAAJ&redir_esc=y|date=2003-01-01|publisher=Arsa Raya Perdana|language=en|chapter=Tempo: Indonesia's Weekly News Magazine}}</ref>。

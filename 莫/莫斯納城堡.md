'''莫斯納城堡'''（{{lang-pl|Pałac w Mosznej}}，{{lang-de|Schloss Moschen}}）是[[波蘭|波蘭]]的一座歷史悠久的城堡和住宅，位於波蘭西南部村莊莫斯納。這座城堡是上西里西亞地區最著名的古蹟之一，經常被列入世界最美麗城堡之列<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.finelivingadvice.com/moszna-one-of-the-most-beautiful-palaces-in-the-world/|title=Moszna, One Of The Most Beautiful Palaces In The World - Fine Living Advice|date=3 June 2015|publisher=|accessdate=11 November 2017|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20171111151811/http://www.finelivingadvice.com/moszna-one-of-the-most-beautiful-palaces-in-the-world/|archivedate=2017-11-11|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thebookoftravel.com/moszna-castle-one-of-the-most-beautiful-castles-in-the-world/|title=Moszna Castle: One of The Most Beautiful Castles in The World - The Book of Travel|website=www.thebookoftravel.com|accessdate=11 November 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://m.thevintagenews.com/2017/05/07/the-moszna-castle-in-poland-is-one-of-the-most-magnificent-castles-in-the-world/|title=The Moszna Castle in Poland is one of the most magnificent castles in the world|date=7 May 2017|publisher=|accessdate=11 November 2017}}</ref>。城堡始建於17世紀。

'''莫爾頓·馬吉爾·亨特'''（{{lang|en|Morton Magill Hunt}}，{{bd|1920年|2月20日|2016年|3月12日}}）是一位美國心理學家，也是位科普作家，作品可見於《[[紐約客|紐約客]]》、《{{Link-en|紐約時報雜誌|The New York Times Magazine}}》與《[[哈潑雜誌|哈潑雜誌]]》。<ref name="google">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=VDmnWg8hrBkC|title=''The Quotable Lover''|author=Butler, N.|date=2003|publisher=Globe Pequot Press|accessdate=2015-05-24|isbn=9781592280094|page=219|language=en|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150524095757/https://books.google.ca/books?id=VDmnWg8hrBkC|archivedate=2015-05-24}}</ref> 亨特畢業於[[天普大學|天普大學]]與[[賓夕法尼亞大學|賓夕法尼亞大學]]，自1949年起從事自由寫作工作，主要書寫社會與行為科學領域的文章。他一生至少撰寫18本書與超過450篇文章。<ref>{{cite book|title=''How science takes stock: the story of meta-analysis''|last=Hunt|first=Morton|publisher=The Russell Sage Foundation|year=1997|isbn=0-87154-398-2|location=New York|language=en}}</ref><ref name="google2">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=kHREAAAAMAAJ|title=Contemporary Authors: First revision|author=Gale Research Company|date=1969|publisher=Gale Research Company|accessdate=May 24, 2015|issue=v. 5-8|language=en}}</ref><ref name="google3">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.ca/books?id=9XwOAQAAMAAJ|title=''Who's who in Writers, Editors & Poets, United States & Canada''|author=Johnson, C.|date=1995|publisher=December Press|accessdate=2015-05-24|issn=1049-8621|language=en}}</ref>

'''莫諾哈爾迪乌帕齐拉'''（{{lang-bn|মনোহরদী}}，{{lang-en|Monohardi Upazila}}）是[[孟加拉国|孟加拉国]][[諾爾辛迪縣|諾爾辛迪縣]]的一个[[乌帕齐拉|乌帕齐拉]]，位於[[達卡专区|達卡专区]]的[[諾爾辛迪縣|諾爾辛迪縣]]。<ref name=Banglapedia>{{citation |author=Sayed Abdullah Al Mamun Chowdhury |chapter={{{3}}} Upazila |chapter-url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title={{{3}}}_Upazila |title=Banglapedia: National Encyclopedia of Bangladesh |editor=Sirajul Islam and Ahmed A. Jamal |publisher={{tsl|en|Asiatic Society of Bangladesh||Asiatic Society of Bangladesh}} |year=2012 |edition=Second }}{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref>。

==人口==
據{{tsl|en|1991 Bangladesh census|1991年孟加拉國人口普查}}，莫諾哈爾迪共有戶數47,397戶，人口275112人。其中男性佔比50.39%，女性佔比49.61%；成年人口122,359人。該地7歲以上人口之平均識字率為27.3%，低於全國平均值（32.4%）。<ref name="census">{{cite web |  accessdate = November 10, 2006 |  url = http://www.bangladeshgov.org/mop/ndb/arpc91_v1/tables04.htm | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20050327072826/http://www.bangladeshgov.org/mop/ndb/arpc91_v1/tables04.htm | archivedate = 2005-03-27 | title = Population Census Wing, BBS.}}</ref>

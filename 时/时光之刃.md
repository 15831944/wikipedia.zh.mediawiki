{{vgname|时光之劍|en=Blades of Time}}是一款由[[Gaijin_Entertainment|Gaijin Entertainment]]开发，[[科乐美|科乐美]]发行于[[Xbox360|Xbox360]]、[[PlayStation_3|PlayStation 3]]、[[Microsoft_Windows|Microsoft Windows]]、[[Mac_OS_X|Mac OS X]]平台的[[砍殺遊戲|砍杀类型]][[动作冒险游戏|动作冒险游戏]]。<ref name="siliconera06092011">{{cite web |url=http://www.siliconera.com/2011/06/09/konamis-blades-of-time-has-neat-time-rewind-feature-to-clone-yourself/ |title=Konami’s Blades of Time Has Neat “Time Rewind” Feature To Clone Yourself |author=Ishaan |date=2011-06-09 |work= |publisher=siliconera |accessdate=2011-08-05|language=en}}</ref>本作是《[[鬼刃|鬼刃]]》的[[精神续作|精神续作]]。<ref>{{cite web| title=Blades of Time Seeks to Keep the Good of X-Blades (Thong) and Do Away With the Bad (Everything Else)| publisher=games abyss.com | url=http://www.gamesabyss.com/blades-of-chaos-seeks-to-keep-the-good-of-x-blades-thong-and-do-away-with-the-bad-everything-else/
| accessdate=2011-12-02|language=en}}</ref>游戏在北美于2012年3月6日发售，在日本于2012年3月8日发售<ref name="Official Website">{{cite web |url=http://gaijinent.com/en/games/blades_of_time/ |title=Gaijin Entertainment — Blades of Time |author=Gaijin Entertainment |date=2011-07-21 |work= |publisher=Gaijin Entertainment |accessdate=2011-08-05|language=en}}</ref>。之后游戏发布了[[Windows|Windows]]和[[Mac_OS_X|Mac OS X]]版本<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cinemablend.com/games/Blades-Time-Coming-PC-Mac-April-18th-40711.html |title=Blades Of Time Coming To PC, Mac On April 20h |author=Usher, William |date=2012-03-22 |work= |publisher=cinemablend |accessdate=2012-03-23|language=en}}</ref>。Windows版于2012年4月21日公布，Mac OS X版本数日后跟进。

'''右翼政黨聯盟''' （{{Lang-he-n|איחוד מפלגות הימין}})是以色列[[右派|右翼]]至[[極右派|极右翼]] [[錫安主義|宗教錫安主义]]政党的联盟，其中包括[[猶太人家園|犹太人家园]] 、{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}和{{Link-en|民族聯盟-重生|Tkuma}}。该聯盟是在[[2019年以色列議會選舉|2019年以色列議會选举]]之前创建的<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/259430|title='Union of the Right-Wing Parties' submits Knesset list|work=[[Israel_National_News|Israel National News]]|language=en|accessdate=22 February 2019|author=Hezki Baruch}}</ref> ，此前[[以色列总理|以色列总理]][[本雅明·内塔尼亚胡|班傑明·納坦尼雅胡]]敦促[[猶太人家園|犹太人家园]]接受{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}作为其选举名单的一部分，以避免分散右翼政黨的选票。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.timesofisrael.com/why-netanyahu-brokered-a-deal-with-kahanes-political-heirs-and-why-it-matters/|title=Why Netanyahu brokered a deal with Kahane’s political heirs, and why it matters|author=Oster|first=Marcy|work=[[The_Times_of_Israel|The Times of Israel]]|language=en-US|accessdate=22 February 2019}}</ref>  双方的象征是“טב”。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ynet.co.il/articles/0,7340,L-5467949,00.html|title=אלי ישי נשאר בחוץ: זו רשימת "איחוד מפלגות הימין"|date=22 February 2019|work=ynet|language=he|accessdate=22 February 2019}}</ref>

== 背景 ==
2019年1月，{{Link-en|民族聯盟-重生|Tkuma}}和{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}组建了联合名单。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/258370|title=Negotiations between National Union, Otzma Yehudit underway|work=[[Israel_National_News|Israel National News]]|language=en|accessdate=22 February 2019|author=Hezki Baruch}}</ref>然而，谈判最终于2019年2月11日失败。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/258901|title=National Union, Otzma Yehudit negotiations end|work=[[Israel_National_News|Israel National News]]|language=en|accessdate=22 February 2019|author=Staff}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/258901|title=National Union, Otzma Yehudit negotiations end|work=[[Israel_National_News|Israel National News]]|language=en|accessdate=22 February 2019|author=Staff}}</ref> 

2019年2月12日，{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}与[[猶太人家園|犹太人家园]]进行了会谈，為了达成有關於联合名单的可能性。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/258935|title=Jewish Home alliance with Otzma Yehudit?|work=[[Israel_National_News|Israel National News]]|language=en|accessdate=22 February 2019|author=Hezki Baruch}}</ref> 

2019年2月15日，{{Link-en|民族聯盟-重生|Tkuma}}和[[猶太人家園|犹太人家园]]再次同意了一份名单，{{Link-en|民族聯盟-重生|Tkuma}}获得了名单上的其他席位。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.timesofisrael.com/jewish-home-and-national-union-agree-to-reunite-for-knesset-run/|title=Jewish Home and National Union agree to reunite for Knesset run|author=Magid|first=Jacob|work=[[The_Times_of_Israel|The Times of Israel]]|language=en-US|accessdate=22 February 2019}}</ref> 

据报道，2019年2月19日，{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}和[[猶太人家園|犹太人家园]]之间的谈判陷入僵局。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/259305|title=Otzmah negotiations deadlocked|work=[[Israel_National_News|Israel National News]]|language=en|accessdate=22 February 2019|author=Mordechai Sones}}</ref> 

2019年2月20日，在[[本雅明·内塔尼亚胡|納坦尼雅胡]]承诺[[猶太人家園|犹太人家园]]2個內閣部长职位以换取与{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}合作後，{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}和[[猶太人家園|犹太人家园]]同意接受聯合名单。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.timesofisrael.com/jewish-home-central-committee-overwhelmingly-backs-merger-with-extremist-party/|title=Jewish Home votes overwhelmingly to back merger with extremist party|author=Magid|first=Jacob|work=[[The_Times_of_Israel|The Times of Israel]]|language=en-US|accessdate=22 February 2019}}</ref> 

该党试图让{{Link-en|一起|Yachad_(political_party)}}加入聯合名单，但由于{{Link-en|一起|Yachad_(political_party)}}的[[拉比|拉比]]{{Link-en|梅厄馬祖茲|Meir_Mazuz}}的拒绝，導致谈判失败。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/259429|title=Eli Yishai to run alone|work=Israel National News|language=en|accessdate=23 February 2019|date=21 February 2019|author=Hezki Baruch}}</ref>虽然{{Link-en|一起|Yachad_(political_party)}}的領導[[埃利·伊沙伊|埃利·伊沙伊]]宣称是因為阿耶·德里否决了[[埃利·伊沙伊|伊沙伊]]的潜在部长職位。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/259540|title=Yishai: Deri vetoed deal for me to run with Jewish Home|work=Israel National News|language=en|accessdate=25 February 2019|date=25 February 2019|author=Staff}}</ref> 

== 争议 ==
由于{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}的極端意识形态，使得[[本雅明·内塔尼亚胡|納坦尼雅胡]]试图让{{Link-en|猶太力量|Otzma_Yehudit}}与[[猶太人家園|犹太人家园]]一起被列入選舉名单此舉引發争议，这导致它被[[美国以色列公共事务委员会|美國以色列公共事務委員會]] <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.haaretz.com/us-news/.premium-ajc-changes-course-denounces-far-right-party-courted-by-netanyahu-1.6959216|title=Prominent Jewish Group Changes Course, Denounces Far-right Party Courted by Netanyahu|author=Tibon|first=Amir|date=22 February 2019|work=Haaretz|accessdate=22 February 2019|language=en}}</ref>、 [[反誹謗聯盟|反诽谤联盟]] <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.jpost.com/Israel-Elections/Jewish-groups-speak-out-against-union-of-Bayit-Yehudi-with-Otzmah-party-581291|title=Jewish groups speak out against union of Bayit Yehudi with Otzma Party|work=[[The_Jerusalem_Post|The Jerusalem Post]]|accessdate=22 February 2019|author=Jeremy Sharon}}</ref>和{{Link-en|美國猶太人委員會|American_Jewish_Committee}} 譴責。 <ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.timesofisrael.com/major-us-jewish-groups-slam-racist-and-reprehensible-extremist-israeli-party/|title=AIPAC slams ‘racist and reprehensible’ extremist party wooed by Netanyahu|work=[[The_Times_of_Israel|The Times of Israel]]|accessdate=22 February 2019|author=Staff|date=22 February 2019}}</ref>

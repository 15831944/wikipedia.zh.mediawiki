'''馬克斯·利奇菲爾德'''（Max Litchfield，{{bd|1995年|3月4日}}）是[[英國|英國]]的一位[[游泳|游泳]]運動員<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.les-sports.info/max-litchfield-natation-spf233001.html |title=Max Litchfield |website=les-sports.info |accessdate=6 August 2016}}</ref>。他代表英國參加了2016年奧運會的比賽<ref name=":0">{{cite web|title=Max Litchfield - Swimming - News, Olympic Results and History|url=https://www.teamgb.com/athletes/max-litchfield|website=www.teamgb.com|accessdate=7 August 2016|language=en}}</ref>，以及2014年和2016年LEN歐洲游泳錦標賽<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.britishswimming.org/news/latest-swimming-news/litchfield-leads-british-attack-final-day/|title=Litchfield leads British attack on final day|last=|first=|date=|website=British Swimming|publisher=British Swimming|access-date=2016-08-07}}</ref>，並且代表英格蘭參加了2014年英聯邦運動會<ref name=":1">{{cite web|title=British Swimming Athlete Profile|url=https://www.britishswimming.org/athlete-swimming-profiles/british-swimming-profiles/max-litchfield/|website=British Swimming|publisher=British Swimming|accessdate=7 August 2016}}</ref>。他主要擅長自由泳和接力項目。

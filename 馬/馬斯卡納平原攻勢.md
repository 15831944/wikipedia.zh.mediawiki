'''馬斯卡納平原攻勢'''是敘利亞政府軍在2017年[[東阿勒頗攻勢_(2017年)|東阿勒頗攻勢]]取得進展後，繼續進攻[[阿勒颇省|阿勒颇省]]東部[[伊斯兰国|伊斯兰国]]餘下控制區的軍事行動。

==攻勢==
2017年5月9日，敘政府軍在阿勒頗省東部再度進攻伊斯蘭國，<ref name="restarts">{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-restarts-east-aleppo-offensive-russian-airstrikes-wipe-isis-militants/|title=Syrian Army restarts east Aleppo offensive as Russian airstrikes wipe out ISIS militants|first=Chris|last=Tomson|date=9 May 2017|publisher=|accessdate=11 May 2017}}</ref>俄軍則猛烈空襲敵方據點支援敘軍作戰。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/russian-jets-hammer-isil-ahead-syrian-militarys-offensive-east-aleppo/|title=Russian jets hammer ISIL ahead of Syrian military's offensive in east Aleppo|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=10 May 2017|publisher=|accessdate=11 May 2017}}</ref>

5月12日，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-syrian-army-liberates-strategic-airbase-east-aleppo/|title=Breaking: Syrian Army liberates strategic airbase in east Aleppo|first=Leith|last=Fadel|date=12 May 2017|publisher=}}</ref>敘軍收復吉拉赫（Jirah）空軍基地。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.news24.com/World/News/syria-army-seizes-airbase-from-is-military-source-monitor-20170513|title=Syria army seizes airbase from ISIS: Monitor|publisher=}}</ref>

5月17日，以[[老虎部隊|老虎部隊]]為首的敘軍在吉拉赫空軍基地以南和西南取得進展，收復11條村。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/battle-east-aleppo-intensifies-syrian-army-troops-advance-towards-isil-stronghold/|title=Battle for east Aleppo intensifies as Syrian Army troops advance towards ISIL stronghold|last=Fadel|first=Leith|date=17 May 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |access-date=18 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>

5月21日至24日，老虎部隊收復另外11條村，距離伊斯蘭國控制的[[馬斯卡納|馬斯卡納]]（Maskanah）不足6公里。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-tiger-forces-liberate-two-sites-isis-eastern-aleppo-cs/|title=BREAKING: Tiger Forces liberate two sites from ISIS in eastern Aleppo CS|last=Illingworth|first=Andrew|date=21 May 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |access-date=21 May 2017|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/map-syrian-army-juggernaut-annihilates-isis-defenses-eastern-aleppo-countryside/|title=Map: Syrian Army juggernaut annihilates ISIS defenses in eastern Aleppo countryside|last=Illingworth|first=Andrew|date=23 May 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News access-date=23 May 2017|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://southfront.org/isis-run-eastern-aleppo-tiger-forces-liberate-5-villages/|title=ISIS Is On Run In Eastern Aleppo. Tiger Forces Liberate 5 More Villages|date=24 May 2017|access-date=24 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>

5月26日，老虎部隊收復距離馬斯卡納-{只}-有3公里的另一條村，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://southfront.org/tiger-forces-increase-pressure-on-isis-stronghold-of-maskanah-liberate-al-sukariya/|title=Tiger Forces Increase Pressure On ISIS Stronghold Of Maskana, Liberate Al-Sukariya|date=26 May 2017|access-date=26 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>次晨又收復11條村，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-liberates-11-villages-along-lake-jabbul-rural-aleppo-huge-isis-collapse/|title=Syrian Army liberates 11 villages along Lake Jabbul in rural Aleppo after huge ISIS collapse|last=Tomson|first=Chris|date=27 May 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |access-date=27 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>推進至距離馬斯卡納-{只}-有1.5公里的地方。<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-seizes-maskanah-train-station-isis-clings-last-aleppo-stronghold/|title=Syrian Army seizes Maskanah train station as ISIS clings on to its last Aleppo stronghold|date=27 May 2017|publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/isis-devastated-syrian-army-liberates-sites-blitz-offensive-rural-aleppo/|title=ISIS devastated as Syrian Army liberates more sites in blitz offensive through rural Aleppo|last=Tomson|first=Chris|date=27 May 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |access-date=27 May 2017|language=en}}</ref>

6月3日，敘軍收復伊斯蘭國在阿勒頗省最後一個重要據點馬斯卡納。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/breaking-syrian-army-liberates-maskanah-isil-expelled-aleppo/|title=Breaking: Syrian Army liberates Maskanah, ISIL expelled from Aleppo|last=Fadel|first=Leith|date=3 June 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |access-date=3 June 2017|language=en}}</ref>6月4日，敘軍推進至[[拉卡省|拉卡省]]界。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-advances-raqqa-border-maskanahs-capture/|title=Syrian Army advances on Raqqa border after Maskanah's capture|last=Fadel|first=Leith|date=4 June 2017|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |access-date=4 June 2017|language=en}}</ref>

6月8日，伊斯蘭國主動放棄位於[[阿薩德湖|阿薩德湖]]附近，在敘政府軍與[[敘利亞民主力量|敘利亞民主力量]]前線之間的約20條村，<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.almasdarnews.com/article/syrian-army-roll-isis-retreats-20-villages-western-raqqa/|title=Syrian Army on the roll as ISIS retreats from 20 villages in western Raqqa|date=2017-06-08|work=AMN - Al-Masdar News |access-date=2017-06-08|language=en}}</ref>敘政府軍在當日收復其中15條村。<ref>[http://www.syriahr.com/2017/06/08/قوات-النظام-تتوغل-داخل-الريف-الغربي-لل/ قوات النظام تتوغل داخل الريف الغربي للرقة وتسيطر على 15 قرية على حساب تنظيم”الدولة الإسلامية”]</ref>

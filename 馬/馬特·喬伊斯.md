'''馬特·喬伊斯'''（{{lang-en| Matthew R. Joyce }}，{{bd|1984年|8月3日}}）出生於[[佛羅里達州|佛羅里達州]][[坦帕_(佛羅里達州)|坦帕]]，現為美國職棒大聯盟[[奧克蘭運動家|奧克蘭運動家]]的[[外野手|外野手]]。

== 早期生活 ==
喬伊斯曾於2005年時效力佛羅里達南方書院。當他在球隊裡打熱身賽時，他在十局後以二分平手跟底特律老虎打平<ref>{{cite news | title=TIGERS, MOCS MANAGE 2-2 TIE; SPRING TRAINING | work=[[The_Ledger|The Ledger]] | date=March 3, 2005 | first=Mike | last=Cobb | accessdate=2008-05-06 | page=C1}}</ref>。在這場比賽的幾天後，喬伊斯是其中一個在草稿中的球員，其餘三個則是[[Geoff_Strickland|Geoff Strickland]]、[[Jeff_Howell_(baseball)|Jeff Howell]]及[[Matt_Mercurio|Matt Mercurio]]<ref>{{cite news | title=FSC MAKES A MAJOR IMPACT; COLLEGE KNOW-HOW: FOUR MOCS JOINED BY PEARCE, ALONSO | work=[[The_Ledger|The Ledger]] | date=June 8, 2005 | accessdate=2008-05-06 | first=Rick | last=Brown | page=C1}}</ref><ref name="bbref-minors">{{cite web | title=Matthew Joyce Statistics (Minor Leagues) | url=http://minors.baseball-reference.com/players.cgi?pid=7507 | accessdate=2008-05-06 | work=Baseball-Reference.com | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090219013106/http://minors.baseball-reference.com/players.cgi?pid=7507 | archivedate=2009-02-19 }}</ref>。

== 大聯盟生涯 ==
喬伊斯是[[底特律老虎|底特律老虎]]於大聯盟選秀會中第12輪所選進的選手。在1A有高達0.332的打擊率<ref name="bbref-minors"/><ref>{{cite news | title=FSC MAKES A MAJOR IMPACT; COLLEGE KNOW-HOW: FOUR MOCS JOINED BY PEARCE, ALONSO | work=[[The_Ledger|The Ledger]] | date=June 8, 2005 | accessdate=May 6, 2008 | first=Rick | last=Brown | page=C1}}</ref>。

2008年12月10日，老虎以喬伊斯和[[坦帕灣光芒|坦帕灣光芒]]交易[[艾德溫·傑克森|艾德溫·傑克森]]<ref>{{cite news |author=Bill Chastain |url=http://tampabay.rays.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20081210&content_id=3712176&vkey=news_tb&fext=.jsp&c_id=tb |title=Rays ship Jackson to Tigers for Joyce |publisher=mlb.com |date=January 15, 2006 |accessdate=April 5, 2011 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120225065833/http://tampabay.rays.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20081210&content_id=3712176&vkey=news_tb&fext=.jsp&c_id=tb |archivedate=2012年2月25日 |df= }}</ref>。

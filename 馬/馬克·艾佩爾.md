'''馬克·史都華·艾佩爾'''（{{lang-en|Mark Stewart Appel}}, {{bd|1991年|7月15日|}}），為2013年美國職棒大聯盟選秀的選秀狀元。

==高中及大學生涯==
高中時期，艾佩爾是一名後援投手，並曾投出過一場無安打比賽<ref>{{cite news|url=http://grfx.cstv.com/photos/schools/stan/sports/m-basebl/auto_pdf/022311-appel-perfectgm.pdf|title=Notebook: Appel, Reckling shining|first=Kendall |last=Rogers|format=PDF|publisher=Perfect Game USA|accessdate=June 5, 2012}}</ref>。

2009年，底特律老虎以第15輪第450順位挑選艾佩爾，但是他並未簽約，而是去就讀史丹佛大學<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/c/a/2009/06/08/SPMU183353.DTL |title=Nothing can rival the bond between a couple of Kettleers |publisher=Sfgate.com |date=June 9, 2009 |accessdate=June 5, 2012 |first=Steve |last=Kroner}}</ref>。大二時成為先發投手<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.paloaltoonline.com/news/show_story.php?id=16259 |title=Stanford baseball loses its Pac-10 opener to USC, 13–8 |first=Rick |last=Eymer|work=[[Palo_Alto_Weekly|Palo Alto Weekly]] |date=March 26, 2010 |accessdate=June 5, 2012}}</ref>。2010年夏天，艾佩爾在新英格蘭大學棒球聯盟投出6勝1敗，防禦率1.87<ref>{{cite web|url=http://necbl.bbstats.pointstreak.com/team_stats.html?teamid=6458&seasonid=330&view=pitching|title=New England Collegiate Baseball League - Pointstreak Stats|website=necbl.bbstats.pointstreak.com}}</ref>。2011年，他投出6勝7敗，防禦率3.02。

2012年，匹茲堡海盜以第一輪第8順位挑選艾佩爾。但是他還是沒有簽約，而是繼續在大學打球<ref name=mlbpirates>{{cite web|url=http://pittsburgh.pirates.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20120713&content_id=34877524&vkey=news_pit&c_id=pit |title=Pirates unable to reach deal with Appel | pirates.com: News |publisher=Pittsburgh.pirates.mlb.com |date= |accessdate=June 7, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/college/2013/05/16/stanford-ace-mark-appel-has-moved-past-last-years-draft/2179943/ |title=Stanford ace Mark Appel has moved past last year's draft |publisher=Usatoday.com |date=May 16, 2013 |accessdate=June 7, 2013}}</ref>。

畢業前，他投出10勝4敗，防禦率2.12。在106又1⁄3局投球中三振130名打者。
==職業生涯==
2013年大聯盟選秀，太空人以第一順位選進艾佩爾<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20130606&content_id=49804508&notebook_id=49805272&vkey=notebook_hou&c_id=hou |title=Astros take Houston native Appel with top pick |work=MLB.com |date=May 24, 2013 |accessdate=June 7, 2013}}</ref>。6月19日，他以簽約金635萬美元加盟太空人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20130618&content_id=51028658&vkey=news_mlb&c_id=mlb |title=No. 1 overall MLB Draft pick Mark Appel signs with Astros |work=MLB.com |date=June 19, 2013 |accessdate=July 20, 2013}}</ref>。在短期1A出賽2場就被拉上1A，還和前一年的選秀狀元Carlos Correa當隊友<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.milb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20130719&content_id=54107224&fext=.jsp&vkey=recap&sid=t565|title=Bullpen, offense boost Bandits over TinCaps, 6–3 |first=Marco|last=LaNave|work=MiLB.com|date=July 19, 2013|accessdate=July 20, 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.qctimes.com/sports/baseball/professional/minor/midwest-league/top-pick-appel-will-make-history-with-bandits/article_6f5f8618-7b7b-57cb-8cdd-08dbfde03c45.html?mobile_touch=true|title=Top pick Appel to make history with Bandits|first=Steve|last=Batterson|work=[[Quad-City_Times|Quad-City Times]]|date=July 10, 2013|accessdate=July 20, 2013}}</ref>。太空人在8月提前結束艾佩爾的投球工作<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hardballtalk.nbcsports.com/2013/08/14/no-1-pick-mark-appel-will-be-shut-down-soon-by-astros/ |title=No. 1 pick Mark Appel will be shut down soon by Astros|work=HardballTalk |publisher=[[NBC_Sports|NBC Sports]] |date=August 14, 2013 |accessdate=March 16, 2014}}</ref>。合計他先發10場，3勝1敗，3.79防禦率，送出33次三振<ref>{{cite web|last=Heneghan |first=Kelsie |url=http://www.milb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20140601&content_id=77835522&fext=.jsp&vkey=news_milb&sid=milb |title=Astros' Appel 'humbled' by tough loss |work=MiLB.com |date=June 1, 2014 |accessdate=June 5, 2014}}</ref>。

2014年，艾佩爾受邀參加春訓，他卻在1月時進行闌尾切除手術而延後出賽時間<ref>{{cite web|url=http://houston.astros.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20140315&content_id=69375752&notebook_id=69378372&vkey=notebook_hou&c_id=hou |title=Appel throws for second time this week|work=MLB.com|date=March 15, 2014 |accessdate=March 16, 2014}}</ref>。他在春訓最後一場比賽登板，面對墨西哥聯盟球隊投了3局無失分<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.foxnews.com/sports/2014/03/30/mark-appel-makes-1st-spring-training-start-pitches-astros-to-6-1-win-over/|title=Mark Appel makes 1st spring training start, pitches Astros to 6–1 win over Veracruz|agency=Associated Press|work=Fox News|date=March 30, 2014|accessdate=April 3, 2014}}</ref>。球隊開季將他升至高階1A<ref>{{cite web|url=http://blog.chron.com/ultimateastros/2014/04/02/mark-appel-carlos-correa-to-start-at-class-a-lancaster/ |title=Ultimate Astros » Mark Appel, Carlos Correa to start at Class A Lancaster |work=Houston Chronicle|date=April 2, 2014|accessdate=April 3, 2014}}</ref>。然而，他在高階1A卻投出了2勝5敗，防禦率9.74的糟糕成績。而太空人依舊將艾佩爾升上2A。他在2A投出1勝2敗，防禦率3.69。太空人把他送到亞利桑那秋季聯盟。他在那邊投了31局，防禦率2.61，送出24次三振。

2015年，艾佩爾在2A投出5勝1敗，防禦率4.26的成績<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hardballtalk.nbcsports.com/2015/06/24/astros-promote-former-no-1-pick-mark-appel-to-triple-a/|title=Astros promote former No. 1 pick Mark Appel to Triple-A|first=Aaron|last=Gleeman|work=NBC Sports|date=June 24, 2015|accessdate=July 3, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.milb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20150817&content_id=143633840&fext=.jsp&vkey=news_milb&sid=milb|title=PCL notes: Appel adjusting in Fresno: Astros No. 2 prospect enjoying success since promotion from Double-A|first=Chris|last=Jackson|work=MiLB.com|date=August 17, 2015|accessdate=August 17, 2015}}</ref>。六月他被升上3A，但是他在3A的防禦率卻高達4.48<ref>{{cite web|url=http://blog.chron.com/ultimateastros/2015/09/12/astros-succeeding-without-aid-of-no-1-pick-mark-appel/|title=Astros succeeding without aid of No. 1 pick Mark Appel|first=Evan|last=Drellich|work=Houston Chronicle|date=September 12, 2015|accessdate=September 14, 2015}}</ref>。

2015年12月12日，太空人將艾佩爾、Vince Velasquez、Brett Oberholtzer、Tom Eshelman和Harold Arauz交易至費城人換取Ken Giles和Jonathan Arauz<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mlb.nbcsports.com/2015/12/12/ken-giles-trade-announced-with-2013-no-1-pick-mark-appel-headed-to-the-phillies/|title=Ken Giles trade announced, with 2013 No. 1 pick Mark Appel headed to the Phillies|first=D. J.|last=Short|publisher=NBC Sports|work=HardballTalk|accessdate=December 12, 2015}}</ref>。然而艾佩爾在3A依舊投得相當掙扎，5勝4敗，防禦率5.14<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.milb.com/milb/news/philadelphia-phillies-designate-mark-appel-for-assignment/c-262239566 |title=Phillies DFA 2013 No. 1 Draft pick Appel |publisher=MiLB.com |date= |accessdate=February 1, 2018}}</ref>。2017年11月20日，費城人將艾佩爾指定轉讓<ref>{{cite news|title=Phils DFA Mark Appel as they set 40-man roster for Rule 5 draft|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/21494231/philadelphia-phillies-dfa-former-no-1-overall-pick-mark-appel|accessdate=November 22, 2017|publisher=ESPN.com|date=November 20, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last1=Zolecki|first1=Todd|title=Phillies add 4 prospects to 40-man roster|url=http://m.phillies.mlb.com/news/article/262243274/phillies-add-4-pitchers-to-40-man-roster/|accessdate=November 22, 2017|publisher=MLB.com|date=November 20, 2017}}</ref>。

2018年2月1日，遲遲無法升上大聯盟的艾佩爾拋下震撼彈，宣布暫時離開棒球界<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.espn.com/mlb/story/_/id/22285378/ex-no-1-pick-mark-appel-steps-away-baseball|title=Ex-No. 1 pick Appel steps away from baseball|work=ESPN.com|access-date=2018-02-02}}</ref>。若他無法回歸，將會是史上第三位未登上大聯盟的選秀狀元。

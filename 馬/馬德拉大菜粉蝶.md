'''馬德拉大菜粉蝶'''（''Pieris brassicae wollastoni''）是一種[[極危|極危]]或可能[[滅絕|滅絕]]的大菜粉蝶亞種，僅分布於[[馬德拉|馬德拉]]<ref>John Feltwell: ''Large white butterfly: The Biology, Biochemistry and Physiology of Pieris Brassicae (Linnaeus)''. Springer, 1982 ISBN 90-6193-128-2</ref>。它们体长约55到65毫米。它們的栖息地位于[[月桂林|月桂林]]中<ref>[http://www.madeiranature.com/index/cms/page/-/page/nature_fauna_insects/articleId/307/articleTitle/large-white/lang/en ''Pieris brassicae wollastoni''] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110714014701/http://www.madeiranature.com/index/cms/page/-/page/nature_fauna_insects/articleId/307/articleTitle/large-white/lang/en |date=2011-07-14 }}</ref> 。最後一个[[标本|标本]]采集]於1977年，现时相信它們的數量已經極度稀少甚至可能已經[[滅絕|滅絕]]<ref>[http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/news/uk/science/article2796656.ece Man drives butterfly into extinction and it could be bad news for us too]</ref>，它們的衰落的原因可能是外來[[蝴蝶|蝴蝶]]所攜帶的[[病毒|病毒]]所致<ref>Gardiner, B. (2003) ''The possible cause of extinction of Pieris brassicae wollastoni Butler (Lepidoptera: Pieridae)''. Entomologist's Gazette 54:267–268</ref> 。

其[[拉丁學名|拉丁學名]]是為了紀念昆蟲學家[[:w:Thomas_Vernon_Wollaston|Thomas Vernon Wollaston]]。

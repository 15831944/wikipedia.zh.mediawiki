'''馬穆卡·巴赫塔澤'''（{{lang-ka|მამუკა ბახტაძე}}；{{bd|1982年|6月9日|}}），是[[格鲁吉亚|格鲁吉亚]]政治人物及現任[[格鲁吉亚总理|总理]]。他的總理及內閣成員提名於[[格鲁吉亚议会|议会]]內得99票比6票通過<ref>[https://www.rferl.org/a/georgia-parliament-approves-bakhtadze-as-prime-minister/29307191.html Georgian Parliament Approves Bakhtadze As Prime Minister]</ref><ref>[https://www.dailysabah.com/europe/2018/06/20/georgia-approves-former-finance-minister-bakhtadze-as-new-premier Georgia approves former Finance Minister Bakhtadze as new premier]</ref> ；他亦曾擔任過財政部部長。

'''馬德琳·麦卡恩失蹤事件'''是發生在2007年5月3日晚[[葡萄牙|葡萄牙]][[阿爾加維|阿爾加維]]{{link-en|普拉亞·達·盧什|Praia da Luz}}的一件女童失踪案。失蹤的是[[英國|英國]]女童'''馬德琳·貝絲·麥卡恩'''（'''Madeleine Beth McCann'''，{{bd|2003年|5月12日}}），當時她只是一個三歲的女童。此案是現代歷史上最知名的人口失蹤案之一，麥卡恩的行蹤至今不明。<ref>[http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/1902515/Master-of-media-circus-for-Madeleine-McCann.html "Master of media circus for Madeleine McCann"], ''The Daily Telegraph'', 24 April 2008.</ref>

瑪德琳當時正在葡萄牙和雙親凱特（Kate）和格里·麥卡恩（Gerry McCann）及其他家庭成員一起度假。其父母在晚上八時半離開在公寓一樓睡覺的她和其孿生胞弟前往{{convert|50|m}}外的餐廳就餐。<ref>以下連結有顯示公寓及餐廳之間的直接距離：[http://uk.reuters.com/article/2007/05/04/uk-portugal-girl-idUKL0439120620070504 "Kidnapping concern for missing girl in Portugal"], Reuters, 4 May 2007; Kate McCann, ''Madeleine'', Bantam Press, 2011 (hardback edition), p. 54.</ref> 在晚上10點時其母發現瑪德琳失蹤，葡萄牙警方最初認為這是一起誘拐綁架案，但後來誤讀英國發出的DNA分析報告而誤判瑪德琳已經死亡。<ref>Esther Addley, [http://www.guardian.co.uk/uk/2012/apr/27/madeleine-mccann-hope "Madeleine McCann: hope and persistence rewarded"], ''The Guardian'', 27 April 2012: "It was, the [Portuguese] attorney general found, largely due to a catastrophic misinterpretation of the evidence collected by ... [Leicestershire police] that the Portuguese team came to suspect the McCanns in the disappearance."</ref> 2007年9月其父母被懷疑謀殺了女孩，但次年7月得以澄清，當月葡萄牙司法部長宣佈結案。<ref>James Sturcke and agencies, [http://www.guardian.co.uk/uk/2008/jul/21/madeleinemccann.internationalcrime "McCanns and Murat formally cleared in case of missing Madeleine"], ''The Guardian'', 21 July 2008.</ref>

直到2011年5月[[蘇格蘭場|蘇格蘭場]]在英國內政大臣的要求下宣佈展開[[格蘭赫行動|格蘭赫行動]]（Operation Grange）介入調查前，麥卡恩夫婦一直也僱用私人偵探進行調查。2013年10月葡萄牙警方也開始重新調查此案，當月蘇格蘭場發佈了嫌犯的電子合成照片，其中一張顯示了一名男子攜帶著瑪德琳在事發當晚走向沙灘。此外包括[[J·K·羅琳|J·K·羅琳]]在內的社會各界人士都給此案提供了懸賞金。<ref name=Laville14Oct2013>Sandra Laville, [http://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2013/oct/14/british-detectives-efits-madeleine-mccann-suspect "British detectives release efits of Madeleine McCann suspect"], ''The Guardian'', 14 October 2013.</ref>

這樁失蹤案引發了廣泛的國際關注。麥卡恩夫婦在瑪德琳失蹤後被錯誤地認為涉嫌謀殺而遭到來自[[推特|推特]]和各種小報的人身攻擊，<ref>For Diana comparison, Nicola Rehling, "'Touching Everyone': Media Identifications, Imagined Communities and New Media Technologies in the Case of Madeleine McCann," in Ruth Parkin-Gounelas (ed.), ''The Psychology and Politics of the Collective: Groups, Crowds and Mass Identifications'', Routledge 2012, p. [http://books.google.com/books?hl=en&lr=%20&id=jBzHwqMfdegC&oi=fnd&pg=PA152 152ff].For Twitter, Eilis O'Hanlon, [http://www.independent.ie/opinion/analysis/eilis-ohanlon-the-sad-rise-of-cyber-courts-full-of-twittering-bullies-26848113.html "Eilis O'Hanlon: The sad rise of cyber courts full of Twittering bullies"], ''Sunday Independent'' (Ireland), 29 April 2012.Also see Brian Cathcart, [http://www.newstatesman.com/law-and-reform/2008/10/madeleine-mccann-daily-british "The Real McCann Scandal"], ''New Statesman'', 23 October 2008.</ref> 2008年他們收到了賠償金及來自英國出版商[[北殼集團|北殼集團]]的道歉，這也導致要求限制新聞輿論的聲音增強。<ref>[http://www.levesoninquiry.org.uk/hearing/2011-11-23pm/ "Wednesday 23 November 2011; afternoon session"], Kate and Gerry McCann's testimony, Leveson Inquiry, from 08:40 mins; [http://www.levesoninquiry.org.uk/evidence/?witness=gerry-mccann Transcript and witness statement of Gerry McCann] and [http://www.levesoninquiry.org.uk/evidence/?witness=kate-mccann Kate McCann], Leveson Inquiry.</ref><ref>James Robinson, [http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/2011/nov/23/leveson-inquiry-mccann-testimony-tabloids "Leveson inquiry: McCanns deliver damning two-hour testimony"], ''The Guardian'', 23 November 2011.</ref>

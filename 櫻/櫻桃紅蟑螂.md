'''樱桃红蟑螂'''（学名：''Blatta lateralis''或''Shelfordella lateralis''）<ref name=eol-classification>{{ cite web | title = Taxonomic Information for Turkestan Cockroach (Shelfordella lateralis) | work = Encyclopedia of Life | url = http://eol.org/pages/1076305/names?all=1 | accessdate = 2014-01-05 }}</ref>，又称'''东突厥蟑螂'''、'''樱桃蟑螂'''、'''樱桃红蟑'''，是[[蜚蠊科|蜚蠊科]]的一种蟑螂，原产于北非和中亚。成虫个体在{{convert|3|cm}}左右<ref name=eol2/>。雄虫甲壳呈褐红色，雌虫呈黑褐色，且身体较粗。樱桃红蟑螂可以飞行<ref name=eol2/><ref name="WernerOlson1994">{{cite book|author1=Floyd G. Werner|author2=Carl E. Olson|title=Insects of the Southwest|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=VoMIKiBKS28C&pg=PA5|accessdate=2013-12-13|year=1994|publisher=Fisher Books|isbn=978-1-55561-060-9|pages=5–6}}</ref>。它是一种常见的宠物饲料，而且因为寿命较长、不会鸣叫（产生噪音）、价格低廉而有取代[[蟋蟀|蟋蟀]]成为主要昆虫类饲料的趋势<ref name=eol2>{{cite web|title=Turkestan Cockroach – Blatta lateralis – Integrated Taxonomic Information System (ITIS) – Overview – Encyclopedia of Life|work=Encyclopedia of Life|url=http://eol.org/pages/1076305/hierarchy_entries/46355653/overview|accessdate=2013-12-13}}</ref><ref name=Jacobi>{{cite news|title=The Perfect Roach—Blatta lateralis (The Turkestan roach or red-runner)|last=Jacobi|first=Michael|work=Arachnoculture E-Zine|publisher=Michael Jacobi's Exotic Fauna|issue=5|url=http://exoticfauna.com/arachnoculture/5/AC-i5a6-p1.html|accessdate=2013-12-13}}</ref>。

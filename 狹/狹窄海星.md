'''狹窄海星'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Stenaster'''''}}）是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[海星|海星]]，其[[化石|化石]]主要分布在[[歐洲|歐洲]]。本屬小而怪異的結構使其被劃入不同的分類群如[[海星綱|海星綱]]和[[蛇尾綱|蛇尾綱]]等。牠們的五條[[腕|腕]]短小，圓盤很寬闊。寬闊的步帶小骨延伸穿過腕。<ref>{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=187 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

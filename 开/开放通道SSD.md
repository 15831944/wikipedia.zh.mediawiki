'''开放通道固态驱动器'''（{{lang-en|'''open-channel solid state drive'''}}）是一种特殊的[[固态硬盘|固态硬盘]]，它不在驱动器的[[固件|固件]]中实现[[快閃記憶體檔案系統|闪存翻译层]]（FTL），而是将物理固态存储的管理任务转交到计算机的[[操作系统|操作系统]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://events.linuxfoundation.org/sites/events/files/slides/LightNVM-Vault2015.pdf|title=Open-Channel Solid State Drives|author=Matias Bjørling|date=March 12, 2015}}</ref>[[Linux内核|Linux内核]]4.4版本通过名为[[LightNVM|LightNVM]]的抽象层提供对遵循[[NVM_Express|NVM Express]]规范的开放通道SSD的支持。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=linux-44-features&num=1|title=A Look At The New Features Of The Linux 4.4 Kernel|author=Michael Larabel|date=15 November 2015|publisher=Phoronix}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=Linux-4.4-LightNVM|title=LightNVM Support Is Going Into Linux 4.4|author=Michael Larabel|date=3 November 2015publisher=Phoronix}}</ref>

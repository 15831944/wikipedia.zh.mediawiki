'''开放图书馆'''是一个致力于“每本发表的书都有一张网页”的在线项目。由[[亚伦·斯沃茨|亚伦·斯沃茨]]<ref name="BBC2007">{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/magazine/6924022.stm|title=A library bigger than any building|date=2007-07-31|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=2010-07-06|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20091127185623/http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/magazine/6924022.stm|archivedate=2009-11-27|df=}}</ref><ref name="Guardian2009">{{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2009/jan/22/library-search-engines-books|title=Why you can't find a library book in your search engine|last=Grossman|first=Wendy M|date=2009-01-22|newspaper=The Guardian|accessdate=2010-07-06|deadurl=no|location=London|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140114010642/http://www.theguardian.com/technology/2009/jan/22/library-search-engines-books|archivedate=2014-01-14|df=}}</ref>，[[布魯斯特·卡利|布魯斯特·卡利]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://aaronsw.jottit.com/howtoget|title=Aaron Swartz: howtoget|accessdate=2015-06-05|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150523080104/http://aaronsw.jottit.com/howtoget|archivedate=2015-05-23|website=Aaronsw.jottit.com|deadurl=no|df=}}</ref>，Alexis Rossi<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://openlibrary.org/about/people.en|title=The Open Library Team {{!}} Open Library|last=OpenLibrary.org|language=en|website=openlibrary.org|access-date=2018-07-16}}</ref>，Anand Chitipothu<ref name=":0" /> 和 Rebecca Malamud<ref name=":0" /> 创建。开放图书馆是非盈利组织[[互联网档案馆|互联网档案馆]]的项目，并接受了{{Internal link helper/en|加州州立图书馆|California State Library}}和 {{Internal link helper/en|Kahle 和 Austin 基金会|Kahle/Austin Foundation}}的资助。

可以在其中看到许多公众领域和不再版图书的在线版本。

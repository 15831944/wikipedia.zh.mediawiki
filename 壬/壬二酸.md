'''壬二酸'''是一種飽和[[二羧酸|二羧酸]]，化學式為HOOC(CH<sub>2</sub>)<sub>7</sub>[[羧酸|COOH]]。在標準狀態下，純壬二酸呈白色粉末狀。壬二酸自然存在於[[小麥|小麥]]、[[黑麥|黑麥]]和[[大麥|大麥]]等榖物中。壬二酸可作為[[聚合物|聚合物]]和[[增塑劑|增塑劑]]等化工產品的前體，它也是外用抗痤瘡藥物和某些頭髮和皮膚養護劑的成分。<ref name=Boy/>

== 製備 ==
在工業上，壬二酸可透過[[油酸|油酸]]的臭氧分解生產，而過程的副產品為[[壬酸|壬酸]]。生活在正常皮膚上的[[真菌|真菌]][[秕糠馬拉癬菌|秕糠馬拉癬菌]]會自然分泌壬二酸，而壬二酸也是壬酸的細菌分解產物。

9-十八碳烯酸经过强氧化剂（如[[高锰酸钾|高锰酸钾]]氧化）可以制得壬二酸。<ref>Shi, Chunwei; Zhou, Wenfeng; Zhao, Shanlin; ''et al''. A method for synthesis of azelaic acid by combined oxidation with hydrogen peroxide and potassium permanganate[P]. CN 104447279. 2014.</ref>

== 生物作用 ==
在植物中，壬二酸是感染後防禦反應中所涉及的一種[[植物激素|植物激素]]。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Vegetative Warfare|author=Sarah Everts|url=http://cen.acs.org/articles/89/i5/Vegetative-Warfare.html|date=2011-01-31|journal=[[Chemical_&_Engineering_News|Chemical & Engineering News]]|issue=5|volume=89|pages=53–55|subscription=yes|access-date=2018-10-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150225015312/http://cen.acs.org/articles/89/i5/Vegetative-Warfare.html|archive-date=2015-02-25|dead-url=no}}</ref>它能誘導[[水楊酸|水楊酸]]的積累，而水楊酸則是植物防禦反應的重要組成部分。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Priming in Systemic Plant Immunity|last=Jung|first=H. W.|last2=Tschaplinski|first2=T. J.|journal=Science|issue=5923|doi=10.1126/science.1170025|year=2009|volume=324|pages=89–91|bibcode=2009Sci...324...89W|pmid=19342588|last3=Wang|first3=L.|last4=Glazebrook|first4=J.|last5=Greenberg|first5=J. == 應用 ==

=== 聚合物及相關材料 ===
壬二酸酯可用於潤滑劑和增塑劑。在潤滑劑工業中，它可用作[[鋰|鋰]][[潤滑脂|潤滑脂]]的增稠劑。壬二酸與六亞甲基二胺的[[共聚物|共聚物]]是特種塑料[[尼龍|尼龍]]-6,9。<ref name=Boy>{{cite encyclopedia|first1=Boy |last1=Cornils |first2=Peter |last2=Lappe |title=Dicarboxylic Acids, Aliphatic |encyclopedia=Ullmann's Encyclopedia of Industrial Chemistry |date=2006 |publisher=Wiley-VCH |location=Weinheim |DOI=10.1002/14356007.a08_523}}</ref>

=== 醫學用途 ===
{{medical}}
[[File:Azelaic_acid_cream.jpg|缩略图]]
壬二酸可用於治療輕度至中度的粉刺性和炎性[[痤瘡|痤瘡]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.webmd.com/skin-problems-and-treatments/acne/azelaic-acid-for-acne-vulgaris|title=Azelaic Acid for Acne|access-date=2018-10-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150710151550/http://www.webmd.com/skin-problems-and-treatments/acne/azelaic-acid-for-acne-vulgaris|archive-date=2015-07-10|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="drugs.com">{{cite web|url=https://www.drugs.com/mtm/azelaic-acid-topical.html|title=Azelaic acid topical|website=Drugs.com|access-date=2018-10-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171015151147/https://www.drugs.com/mtm/azelaic-acid-topical.html|archive-date=2017-10-15|dead-url=no}}</ref>壬二酸能殺死感染毛孔的痤瘡細菌，還會降低[[角蛋白|角蛋白]]的產生，而角蛋白是一種促進痤瘡細菌生長的天然物質。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Azelaic acid in the treatment of papulopustular rosacea – A systematic review of randomized controlled trials|last=Liu|first=R. H.|last2=Smith|first2=M. K.|journal=Arch. Dermatol.|issue=8|doi=10.1001/archderm.142.8.1047|year=2006|volume=142|pages=1047–1052|pmid=16924055|last3=Basta|first3=S. A.|last4=Farmer|first4=E. R.}}</ref>由於它具有[[消炎|消炎]]作用，壬二酸還可以用作治療[[玫瑰痤瘡|玫瑰痤瘡]]的外用藥物。它可以治療玫瑰痤瘡引起的腫塊和腫脹。壬二酸的作用機制是通過抑制過度活躍的蛋白酶活性，該蛋白酶能將導管素轉化為抗微生物肽LL-37。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Cathelicidin LL-37: An Antimicrobial Peptide with a Role in Inflammatory Skin Disease|last=Reinholz|first=M.|last2=Ruzicka|first2=T.|journal=Annals of Dermatology|issue=2|doi=10.5021/ad.2012.24.2.126|year=2012|volume=24|pages=126–135|pmc=3346901|pmid=22577261|last3=Schauber|first3=J.}}</ref>壬二酸也可用於治療皮膚色素沉著，包括黃褐斑和[[炎症|炎症]]後色素過度沉著，尤其是對於那些膚色較深的人。它也被推薦作為[[對苯二酚|對苯二酚]]的代替品。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Skin lightening preparations and the hydroquinone controversy|last=Draelos|first=Z.|date=Sep–Oct 2007|journal=Dermatol. Ther.|issue=5|doi=10.1111/j.1529-8019.2007.00144.x|volume=20|pages=308–313|pmid=18045355}}</ref>作為[[酪氨酸酶|酪氨酸酶]]抑製劑，壬二酸可抑制[[黑色素|黑色素]]的合成。<ref name="Grimes2007">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ALt93yGZccQC&pg=PA74|title=Aesthetics and Cosmetic Surgery for Darker Skin Types|last=Grimes|first=Pearl E.|date=2007-07-01|publisher=Lippincott Williams & Wilkins|accessdate=2011-08-09|isbn=978-0-7817-8403-0|pages=74 ff.}}</ref>

與治療[[脫髮|脫髮]]藥物[[非那甾胺|非那甾胺]]和{{link-en|度他雄胺|dutasteride}}相似的是，壬二酸也是一種有效的[[5α還原酶|5α還原酶]]抑制劑。<ref name=pmid3207614>{{cite journal |doi=10.1111/j.1365-2133.1988.tb03474.x |pmid=3207614 |title=Inhibition of 5α-reductase activity in human skin by zinc and azelaic acid |journal=British Journal of Dermatology |volume=119 |issue=5 |pages=627–32 |year=1988 |last1=Stamatiadis |first1=D |last2=Bulteau-Portois |first2=Marie-Claire |last3=Mowszowicz |first3=Irene }}</ref>

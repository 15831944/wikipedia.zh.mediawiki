'''薑母茶'''（{{lang-en|Ginger tea}}）是一種由薑製成的亞洲[[药用植物|草藥]][[饮料|饮料]]，在[[东亚|东亚]]、[[南亚|南亚]]、[[东南亚|东南亚]]和[[中东|中东]]有著悠久的歷史<ref>{{cite web|url=http://umm.edu/health/medical/altmed/herb/ginger|title=Ginger (Overview)|publisher=University of Maryland Medical Center|date=2015-06-22|accessdate=2016-03-21}}</ref>，通常可用來預防感冒、幫助消化、解除腹瀉和噁心、作為咳嗽和喉嚨痛的補救和改善[[循环系统|血液循環]]等<ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1346&dat=19910519&id=4O4vAAAAIBAJ&sjid=DPwDAAAAIBAJ&pg=3308,2593313&hl=en|title=Soothing Ginger Tea|publisher=Lakeland Ledger|date=1991-05-19|accessdate=2016-03-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1129&dat=20000217&id=cOpRAAAAIBAJ&sjid=LXADAAAAIBAJ&pg=5068,4910438&hl=en|title=Flu-fighting foods can relieve symptoms|publisher=Pittsburgh Post-Gazette|date=2000-02-17|accessdate=2016-03-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|author=Henry Phillips|title=History of cultivated vegetables: comprising their botanical, medicinal, edible, and chemical qualities; natural history; &c., &c|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=pDkaAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA216|year=1827|publisher=Henry Colburn|pages=216–}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1309&dat=19920103&id=yYJUAAAAIBAJ&sjid=MJADAAAAIBAJ&pg=2000,806399&hl=en|title=Wonder herb called ginger|publisher=New Straits Times|date=1992-01-03|accessdate=2016-03-22}}</ref><ref name="ginger">{{cite web|url=http://ww1.utusan.com.my/utusan/info.asp?y=2010&dt=0502&pub=Utusan_Malaysia&sec=Kesihatan&pg=kn_05.htm|title=Buat sendiri di rumah|language=ms|publisher=Utusan Malaysia|date=2010-05-02|accessdate=2016-03-22}}</ref>。

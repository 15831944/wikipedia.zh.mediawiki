'''游戏物管理委员会'''（{{kor|谚=게임물관리위원회|汉=게임物管理委員會|名=南|附=助|罗=Geimmul Gwalli Wiwonhoe}}）是[[大韩民国|大韩民国]]根据《游戏业促进法》<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.grac.or.kr/download/FileDown.aspx?fileName=GAME+INDUSTRY+PROMOTION+ACT.pdf|title=GAME INDUSTRY PROMOTION ACT|accessdate=|author=국가법령정보센터|date=|publisher=}}</ref>于2013年12月成立的[[电子游戏分级系统|电子游戏分级]]组织。<ref>{{Cite web|title=GAME RATING and ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE{{!}}Overview|url=https://www.grac.or.kr/english/about/overview.aspx|work=www.grac.or.kr|accessdate=2019-04-07|author=|date=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180708132334/http://www.grac.or.kr/english/about/overview.aspx|archive-date=2018-07-08|dead-url=no}}</ref>该机构的前身{{Le|映像物管理委员会|Korea Media Rating Board|映-{}-像物管理委员会}}曾把一款有赌博性质的游戏《Sea Story》划为全年龄段，因而遭到媒体批评<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/biz/2011/09/123_95415.html|title=Blizzard's rush for cash?|author=|date=2011-9-25|work=Korea Times|accessdate=|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140826161522/http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/biz/2011/09/123_95415.html|archivedate=2014-8-26}}</ref>，时任韩国总理[[韩明淑|韩明淑]]为此向国民道歉。<ref>{{Cite web|title=South Korean PM apologizes for video game controversy - Taipei Times|url=http://www.taipeitimes.com/News/world/archives/2006/08/30/2003325478|work=www.taipeitimes.com|accessdate=2019-04-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140826113708/http://www.taipeitimes.com/News/world/archives/2006/08/30/2003325478|archive-date=2014-08-26|dead-url=no}}</ref>随后由游戏物管理委员会接管电子游戏分级事务。<ref>{{Cite book|title=Digital Development in Korea: Building an Information Society|last=Oh|first=Myung|publisher=Taylor & Francis|year=2011|isbn=978-1-136-81313-9|location=|pages=147-148|last2=Larson|first2=James}}</ref>

== 争议 ==
有人质疑游戏物管理委员会是[[韩国网络审查|韩国网络审查]]的一部分，并可能使用行政手段阻止游戏在该国发行。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Censorship in South Korea: Game over|author=|url=https://www.economist.com/asia/2011/04/14/game-over|date=2011-04-14|journal=The Economist|issue=|doi=|others=|year=|volume=|page=|issn=0013-0613|pmid=|access-date=2019-04-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190327135223/https://www.economist.com/asia/2011/04/14/game-over|archive-date=2019-03-27|dead-url=no}}</ref>该委员会也禁止了[[Facebook遊戲列表|Facebook游戏]]在韩国上架。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://koreanoodles.com/korea-blocks-facebook-game-apps/|title=Korea Blocks Facebook Game Apps|accessdate=|author=Lee|date=2014-9-2|publisher=Koreanoodles|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140905171717/http://koreanoodles.com/korea-blocks-facebook-game-apps/|archivedate=2014-9-5}}</ref>

'''匈牙利觀光業'''有著悠久的歷史<ref name=Hudman>{{cite book |title=Geography of Travel & Tourism |last=Hudman |first=Lloyd E. |author2=Eva H. Essa |author3=Richard H. Jackson  |year=2002 |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=MyGjpyNAur0C |publisher=Thomson Delmar Learning |isbn=0-7668-3256-2 |pages=284–285 }}</ref>。2002年，匈牙利是世界第13大外國遊客參觀國 <ref name=Hall>{{cite book |title=Tourism and Transition: Governance, Transformation and Development
  |last=Hall |first=Derek R. |url=http://books.google.com/books?id=VWsb148C1zAC |year=2004 |publisher=CABI Publishing |isbn=0-85199-748-1 |pages=74 }}</ref>。2004年至2005年，匈牙利的遊客數增長了解決7%<ref name=Lyman>{{cite news |first=Rick |last=Lyman |title=Budapest Is Stealing Some of Prague’s Spotlight |url=http://travel.nytimes.com/2006/09/03/travel/03journey.html?pagewanted=1 |work=The New York Times |date=2006-09-03 |accessdate=2008-05-24 }}</ref>，其中大部分來自歐洲<ref name=Hudman/>。許多觀光客都通過汽車到達匈牙利並只停留較短時間<ref name=Boniface>{{cite book |title=Worldwide Destinations: The Geography of Travel and Tourism |last=Boniface |first=Brian G. |author2=Christopher P. Cooper  |year=2005 |publisher=Butterworth-Heinemann |isbn=0-7506-5997-1 |pages=210 }}</ref>。匈牙利的觀光旺季是四月至十月，其中又以七月和八月最佳<ref name=Hudman/>。[[布達佩斯|布達佩斯]]是匈牙利最受歡迎的城市<ref name=Hall/>。

'''廣東米蝦'''（學名：'''''Caridina cantonensis'''''）是[[米蝦屬|米蝦屬]]的一種蝦，為[[中國|中國]]特有物種<ref name=fc>[http://www.fishchannel.com/aquarium-fish/keeping-dwarf-shrimp.aspx "Keeping And Breeding Dwarf Freshwater Shrimp"]. ''Fish Channel''.</ref><ref name=tfh>[http://www.tfhmagazine.com/details/articles/the-latest-buzz-freshwater-bee-shrimp-full-article.htm "The Latest Buzz: Freshwater Bee Shrimp"]. ''Tropical Fish Magazine''.</ref>。它是一種濾食動物<ref>{{cite book|title=Freshwater and Marine Aquarium|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=w9Q4AQAAIAAJ|year=2009|publisher=R/C Modeler Corporation}}</ref>，也以海藻及其他植物碎屑爲食<ref name=fy>[http://www.fishyou.com/shrimp-crs.php "Crystal Red Shrimp"]. ''FishYou''.</ref>。生命週期為18個月<ref name=fc />。

廣東米蝦是一種觀賞蝦<ref name=want>[http://www.wantchinatimes.com/news-subclass-cnt.aspx?id=20140512000049&cid=1102 Ornamental varieties net big money for Taiwan Fu Shrimp"] {{Webarchive|url=https://archive.is/20150119021047/http://www.wantchinatimes.com/news-subclass-cnt.aspx?id=20140512000049&cid=1102 |date=2015年1月19日 }}. Staff Reporter, ''Want China Times'', 2014-05-12</ref>，但它對水質和pH值要求都比較高，因而飼養困難<ref name=al>[http://www.aquariumslife.com/freshwater-shrimp/bee-shrimp-caridina-cantonensissp-bee/ "Bee Shrimp – Caridina cantonensissp. 'Bee']. ''Aquariums Life''.</ref><ref name=fy />。它有紅、藍、黑、金等多種體色。<ref name=fc /><ref name=want />

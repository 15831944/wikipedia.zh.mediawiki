'''廣義長壘'''<ref name="Bray2">{{cite news|url=http://www.bbc.com/travel/feature/20110420-scale-the-great-wall-of-vietnam |title=Scale the Great Wall of Vietnam |last=Bray |first=Adam |date=2011-04-22 |work=[[BBC_Online|BBC Online]] |accessdate=2011-04-25 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5yCDtS791?url=http://www.bbc.com/travel/feature/20110420-scale-the-great-wall-of-vietnam |archivedate=2011-04-25 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>（{{lang-vi|Trường lũy Quảng Ngãi}}<ref name="Trung">{{cite news|url=http://thethaovanhoa.vn/133N20100423115352634T0/giai-ma-truong-luy-dai-nhat-dong-nam-a.htm |title=Giải mã Trường Lũy dài nhất Đông Nam Á |last=Trung |first=Đoàn Hữu |date=2011-04-23 |work=Thể thao & Văn hóa Online |language=vi |accessdate=2011-04-25 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5yCY0HU5l?url=http://thethaovanhoa.vn/133N20100423115352634T0/giai-ma-truong-luy-dai-nhat-dong-nam-a.htm |archivedate=2011-04-25 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>），簡稱'''長壘'''，是[[越南|越南]]中部的一處歷史遺跡。該遺址是一處長為127.4公里的城牆，從北面[[廣義省|廣義省]]境內延伸到南面的[[平定省|平定省]]境內。該城牆是[[東南亞|東南亞]]境內最長的城牆<ref name="Bray1">{{cite news|url=http://articles.cnn.com/2011-01-25/world/vietnam.wall_1_vietnam-wall-hadrian-s-wall-quang-ngai-province|title=Vietnam's own 'great wall' uncovered|last=Bray|first=Adam|date=2011-01-25|publisher=[[CNN|CNN]]|accessdate=2011-04-25}}{{Dead link|date=2018年9月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>
。西方人也把它稱做'''越南長城'''（Great Wall of Vietnam）<ref name="Minh">{{cite news|url=http://www.saigon-gpdaily.com.vn/Culture_Art/2010/4/81235/|title=Int'l team studying Southeast Asia's longest rampart in VN|last=Minh|first=Ha|date=2011-04-17|work=[[Sài_Gòn_Giải_Phóng|SGGP]]|accessdate=2011-04-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721143025/http://www.saigon-gpdaily.com.vn/Culture_Art/2010/4/81235/|archive-date=2011-07-21|dead-url=yes}}</ref>
。

根據[[阮朝|阮朝]]的《[[大南實錄|大南實錄]]》記載，1819年，在[[嘉隆帝|嘉隆帝]]的命令下，[[黎文悅|黎文悅]]修建了廣義長壘。阮朝年間，正式名稱為'''鎮蠻長壘'''（Tĩnh Man trường lũy）。

'''低速区'''（LVZ）是[[上地幔|上地幔]]接近[[岩石圈|岩石圈]]与[[软流圈|软流圈]]边界处，[[地震波|地震波]]横波速度显著偏低、电导率显著偏高的区域。深度从80km至300km。其中部分区域的地震波纵波缺失。<ref name=Stixrude>{{cite journal|title=Mineralogy and elasticity of the oceanic upper mantle: Origin of the low-velocity zone |journal=Journal of Geophysical Research |volume=110 |page=B03204 |url=http://supercronopio.es.ucl.ac.uk/~crlb/RESEARCH/PAPERS/StixrudeCLB05a.pdf |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110830060832/http://supercronopio.es.ucl.ac.uk/~crlb/RESEARCH/PAPERS/StixrudeCLB05a.pdf |dead-url=yes |archive-date=2011-08-30 |doi=10.1029/2004JB002965 |date=2005 |author1=L Stixrude |author2=C Lithgow-Bertolloni |lastauthoramp=yes |bibcode=2005JGRB..11003204S }}</ref><ref name=Stixrude/><ref name=Rost/><ref name=Vine/>

另外一个地震波速显著降低的区域在[[核幔邊界|核幔邊界]]的不超过50km的薄层，称为{{tsl|en|Ultra low velocity zone|超低速区}}（ULVZ）。<ref name=Rost>{{cite book |title=Superplumes: beyond plate tectonics |author=EJ Garnero, MS Thorne, A McNamara & S Rost |editor1=David A Yuen |editor2=Shigenori Maruyama |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-BnTZbh6FJMC&pg=PA139 |page=139 |chapter=Chapter 6: Fine-scale ultra-low-velocity zone layering at the core-mantle boundary and superplumes |isbn=1-4020-5749-0 |date=2007 |publisher=Springer}}</ref>

1959年，[[賓諾·古登堡|賓諾·古登堡]]在研究地震波的波速时指出在距离震中1-15°的区域内，纵波振幅会呈现突然增加然后呈指数下降的现象。<ref name="Bruno">{{cite book|last=Gutenberg|first=B.|title=Physics of the Earth's Interior|publisher=[[Academic_Press|Academic Press]]|location=New York|date=1959|pages=240|isbn=0-12-310650-8}}</ref><ref name="Don">{{cite book|last=Anderson|first=D.L.|title=Theory of the Earth|publisher=[[Blackwell_Scientific_Publications|Blackwell Scientific Publications]]|location=Boston|date=1989|chapter=3. 地震波的横波在低速区下降了3–6%，比纵波波速变化更显著。<ref name="Brown">{{cite book|last=Brown|first=G.C.|author2=Mussett A.E. |title=The inaccessible earth|publisher=[[Taylor_&_Francis|Taylor & Francis]]|pages=235|isbn=978-0-04-550028-4|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Y5QVAAAAIAAJ&pg=PA142&dq=asthenosphere+%22low-velocity+zone%22&cd=4#v=onepage&q=asthenosphere%20%22low-velocity%20zone%22&f=false|accessdate=2010-02-20|date=1981}}</ref>[[雷氏不連續面|雷氏不連續面]]是低速区的下界。
 
只需要0.05–0.1 %的水就可导致1%的熔融，产生低速区。[[地盾|地盾]]下缺乏低速区可解释为地热梯度太小，阻止了部分熔融。<ref name="Condie">{{cite book|last=Condie|first=K.C.|title=Plate tectonics and crustal evolution|publisher=[[Butterworth-Heinemann|Butterworth-Heinemann]]|date=1997|pages=282|isbn=978-0-7506-3386-4|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=HZrA6OQzsvgC&pg=PA123&dq=mantle+%22low-velocity+zone%22++anisotropy&cd=8#v=onepage&q=mantle%20%22low-velocity%20zone%22%20%20anisotropy&f=false|accessdate=2010-02-20}}</ref>

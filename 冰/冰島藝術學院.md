'''冰島藝術學院'''（{{lang-is|Listaháskóli Íslands}}）是一所位於[[冰島|冰島]][[雷克雅未克|雷克雅未克]]的高等教育機構，只為冰島學生提供[[藝術|藝術]]類大學教育。<ref name=Lisa>Lisa Z. Valdimarsdottir, [http://www.arlisnorden.org/arlis/arkitekturgruppen/files/arkitekturmote2009/09Iceland.pdf Iceland Academy of the Arts, Department of Design and Architecture - Library]{{dead link|date=2017年11月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} (pdf)</ref> 1998年9月21日 <ref>[http://english.lhi.is/about-the-academy/administration/organizational-charter/ Institutional Charter] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110722022413/http://english.lhi.is/about-the-academy/administration/organizational-charter/ |date=2011-07-22 }}, Iceland Academy of the Arts.</ref> 由冰島戲劇學院和雷克雅未克藝術學院合併而成。<ref name=Lisa/><ref>[http://bella.mrn.stjr.is/utgafur/iaa-accreditation-arts.pdf Accreditation Report - Arts - Iceland Academy of the Arts] {{webarchive|url=http://wayback.vefsafn.is/wayback/20100409202612/http%3A//bella.mrn.stjr.is/utgafur/iaa%2Daccreditation%2Darts.pdf |date=2010-04-09 }} (pdf), 3 July 2007, pp. 7, 28.</ref> 1999年秋開始正式授課。<ref>Jóhannes Þórðarson, [http://cirrusnetwork.net/wp-content/uploads/MA_Proposal_Presentation_Johannes_Thordarson.pdf MA proposal for Applied Arts at the Iceland Academy of the Arts - dept. of design and architecture] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110723042533/http://cirrusnetwork.net/wp-content/uploads/MA_Proposal_Presentation_Johannes_Thordarson.pdf |date=2011-07-23 }}(pdf), 22 January 2010: "IAA started in 1999".</ref> 其學科有[[藝術教育|藝術教育]]、設計和建築、美術、音樂和戲劇舞蹈。

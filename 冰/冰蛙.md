'''冰蛙'''（英文：'''IceFrog'''）是一名[[游戏设计师|游戏设计师]]，他以《[[魔兽争霸3|魔兽争霸3]]》地图[[DotA|DotA]]开发者中任期最长且工作至今而知名。目前受雇于Valve公司，并负责领导开发原[[DotA|DotA]]的独立续作《[[Dota_2|Dota 2]]》。<ref>{{cite web | last = Biessener | first = Adam | url = http://gameinformer.com/b/features/archive/2010/10/13/dota-2-announced-details.aspx | title = Valve's New Game Announced, Detailed: Dota 2 | date = 2010-10-13 | publisher = Game Informer | access-date = 2012-07-07 | archive-url = https://www.webcitation.org/68qKNrWxl?url=http://www.gameinformer.com/b/features/archive/2010/10/13/dota-2-announced-details.aspx | archive-date = 2012-07-01 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>2005年，冰蛙从[[史蒂夫·费克|史蒂夫·费克]]处接手了《[[DotA|DotA]]》的主流版本《[[DotA_Allstars|DotA Allstars]]》，并自此从事维护和发展工作<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.eurogamer.net/articles/2011-08-16-the-story-of-dota-article?page=2 | title=The Story of DOTA| last= Dean | first = Paul | date = 16 August 2011 | publisher = Eurogamer}}</ref>。自此开始，冰蛙为游戏加入了大量的新特性，其中包括各种英雄以及游戏设定。每发布一个修改版时，官网上都会发布对应的更新列表。<ref>{{cite web|author=IceFrog |url=http://www.getdota.com/changelog.txt |title=Latest DotA Changelog |publisher=GetDota.com |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070830231424/http://www.getdota.com/changelog.txt |archivedate=2007-08-30 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>冰蛙一向低调行事，从未公开以真实姓名抛头露面。<ref>{{cite web|author=Waldbridge, Michael |date=2008-05-30 |url=http://www.gamesetwatch.com/2008/05/column_the_game_anthropologist_defense_of.php |title=The Game Anthropologist: Defense of the Ancients: An Underground Revolution |publisher=GameSetWatch |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090417003613/http://www.gamesetwatch.com/2008/05/column_the_game_anthropologist_defense_of.php |archivedate=2009-04-17 |deadurl=yes }}</ref>

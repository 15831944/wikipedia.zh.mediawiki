'''冰人奧茨'''（{{lang-de|Ötzi}}），也称'''錫米拉溫人'''（Similaun man）或'''厄茨人'''，以其發現地所在山谷而命名，是1991年於[[奧茨塔爾阿爾卑斯山脈|奧茨塔爾阿爾卑斯山脈]][[冰川|冰川]]發現的一具因冰封而保存完好的天然[[木乃伊|木乃伊]]，地點位於[[奧地利|奧地利]]及[[意大利|意大利]]邊境附近<ref>{{citation|author=James Neill|title=Otzi, the 5,300 Year Old Iceman from the Alps: Pictures & Information|date=27 October 2004|url=http://www.wilderdom.com/evolution/OtziIcemanAlpsPictures.htm|accessdate=8 March 2007}}</ref>。該具木乃伊约生存于公元前3300年的[[史前時期|史前時期]]，並有66%的可能是死於前3239年至前3105年之间<ref name=radiocarbondating>{{cite journal |last=Bonani |first=Georges |last2=Ivy |first2=Susan D. |display-authors=etal |date=1994 |title=AMS {{SimpleNuclide2|Carbon|14}} Age Determination of Tissue, Bone and Grass Samples from the Ötzal Ice Man  |url=http://digitalcommons.library.arizona.edu/objectviewer?o=http%3A%2F%2Fradiocarbon.library.arizona.edu%2FVolume36%2FNumber2%2Fazu_radiocarbon_v36_n2_247_250_v.pdf |journal=Radiocarbon |publisher=The Department of Geosciences, The University of Arizona  |volume=36 |issue=2 |pages= 247-250 |access-date=February 4, 2016}}</ref>。他是[[歐洲|歐洲]]最古老的、保存最完好的人類木乃伊。现在意大利[[波爾查諾|波爾查諾]]的[[南蒂罗尔考古学博物馆|南蒂罗尔考古博物館]]公開展覽。

== 發現 ==
[[File:Tisenjoch_&_Hauslabjoch.jpg|thumb]]
[[File:Oetzi_Memorial.jpg|thumb]]

「冰人奧茨」最先由兩名[[德國|德國]]登山遊客西蒙夫婦（Helmut和Erika Simon）於1991年9月19日共同發現的，最初被認為是一具現代人的屍體，及後奧地利當局把冰人送往[[因斯布鲁克|因斯布鲁克]]化驗，發現冰人竟來自5300年前，是迄今最古老的木乃伊。同年10月的调查表明，发现地属于意大利境内<ref>[http://www.iceman.it/en/node/233 Description of the Discovery] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111213024011/http://www.iceman.it/en/node/233 |date=2011-12-13 }} at the South Tyrol Museum of Archaeology web site</ref><ref>{{cite book |title=Iceman: Uncovering the Life and Times of a Prehistoric Man Found in an Alpine Glacier |author=Brenda Fowler |page=37 ''ff'' |publisher=University of Chicago Press |year=2001 |isbn=978-0-226-25823-2}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=The Incredible Age of the Find |url=http://www.iceman.it/en/oetzi-age |publisher=[[South_Tyrol_Museum_of_Archaeology|South Tyrol Museum of Archaeology]] |date=2013 |accessdate=August 3, 2015 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150624085502/http://www.iceman.it/en/oetzi-age |archivedate=2015年6月24日 }}</ref>。

冰人的最初发现权归属曾经引发诉讼，有一名[[斯洛文尼亚|斯洛文尼亚]]女演员和一名[[瑞士|瑞士]]女性也声称自己首先发现了冰人。2006年法庭判定发现者是西蒙夫妇<ref>[http://www.iceman.it/en/node/241 The Border Question] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111008075754/http://www.iceman.it/en/node/241 |date=2011-10-08 }}, at the museum site</ref>。

發現者赫尔穆特·西蒙（Helmut Simon）在2004年10月前往阿爾卑斯山登山時失蹤，一星期後被發現死亡，該處距離「冰人奧茨」發現處大約200公里。

== 科學鑑證 ==
鑑定小組把「冰人奧茨」作出詳細的化驗，包括檢查、照[[X光|X光]]、量度及年代測定，對內臟及身體組織作微細檢查，在他的隨身用具上找到[[花粉|花粉]]<ref>{{citation|title=WWI bodies are found on glacier|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/europe/3592268.stm|publisher=BBC News  |date=23 August 2004}}</ref>。

根据它被发现的地点（属于[[奥茨塔尔阿尔卑斯山脉|奥茨塔尔阿尔卑斯山脉]]地区），这个冰人被称为“奥兹”。「冰人奧茨」死時大约45岁，身高1.65m，体重50公斤，穿38码的鞋子，而花粉、塵粒及牙齒琺瑯質的分析結果中得知冰人的童年時代於現今[[意大利|意大利]]波爾查諾北部的[[韦尔图尔诺|韦尔图尔诺]]附近居住，及後遷往原居地以北大約50公里的地方。檢查肺部發現肺中相當黑，推測應是常常吸入營火的煙<ref name="Guardian: Defrosted">{{citation|author=Rory Carroll|title=Iceman is defrosted for gene tests: New techniques may link Copper Age shepherd to present-day relatives|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/international/story/0,,373487,00.html|newspaper=[[The_Guardian|The Guardian]]|date=26 September 2000}}</ref>。

經過分析冰人[[消化系統|消化系統]]的殘留物質，其中包含兩餐，其中一餐在死前八小時曾吃過[[羱羊|羱羊]]和[[紅鹿|紅鹿]]的肉。以及[[穀類|穀類]]，根莖類和水果。兩餐都包含了精製過的[[小麥|小麥]]麩皮，推測極可能是食用了麵包<ref>{{citation|author=T.G. Holden|chapter=The Food Remains from the Colon of the Tyrolean Ice Man|editor1=Keith Dobney|editor2=Terry O'Connor|title=Bones and the Man: Studies in Honour of Don Brothwell|location=Oxford|publisher=Oxbow Books|year=2002|pages=35–40|isbn=978-1-84217-060-1}}</ref>。

花粉研究顯示較早食用的一餐是在中海拔地帶的針葉林中吃的。而最後一餐中包含了小麥以及豆類的花粉，應該是在人工種植的田間食用的。另外發現完好無缺的歐洲穗子榆花粉，保存良好，顯示奧茲死時應是春季。有趣的是小麥是在夏末收割，另外發現的黑刺莓花粉是秋季才會出現，推測這些食物是前一年保存下來的<ref>{{citation|author=Wolfgang Müller |title=Origin and Migration of the Alpine Iceman|url=http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/short/302/5646/862|journal=[[Science_(journal)|Science]]|date=31 October 2003|volume=302|issue=5646|pages=862–866|publisher=[[American_Association_for_the_Advancement_of_Science|AAAS]]|doi=10.1126/science.1089837|accessdate=18 October 2007|laysummary=http://www.mummytombs.com/otzi/identity.htm|laysource=Mummy Tombs|laydate=16 December 2007|pmid=14593178|name-list-format=vanc|display-authors=1|last2=Fricke|first2=H|last3=Halliday|first3=AN|last4=McCulloch|first4=MT|last5=Wartho|first5=JA}}</ref><ref>{{citation|author=A.G. Heiss & K. Oeggl |title= The plant macro-remains from the Iceman site (Tisenjoch, Italian-Austrian border, eastern Alps): new results on the glacier mummy's environment |journal=Veget Hist Archaeobot |date= 19 February 2008 |doi=10.1007/s00334-007-0140-8|volume=18|pages=23–35}}</ref>。

頭髮的分析結果，發現含高量的[[銅|銅]]及[[砷|砷]]粒子，由於奧茲攜帶著一把高純度（99.7%）銅斧，科學家推斷奧茲曾參與銅的精鍊作業。經過研究其[[脛骨|脛骨]]、[[股骨|股骨]]以及[[骨盆|骨盆]]，科學家斷定奧茲的工作需要在山岳地帶長距離行走，然而這並不是[[紅銅時代|紅銅時代]]大多數歐洲人的生活方式，因此推定奧茲是一位高海拔的牧羊人<ref>{{citation|title=Iceman's final meal|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/science/nature/2262615.stm|publisher=BBC News  |date=16 September 2002|author1=<Please add first missing authors to populate metadata.>}}</ref>。

奧茲消化系統中有鞭蟲以及其他腸道寄生蟲。其右[[肋骨|肋骨]]有三或四支斷裂，應是死後遭冰雪掩埋壓碎的。經過研究其指甲，顯示奧茲在死前半年曾生病過三次，最後的一次是在死前兩星期<ref>{{citation|author=Christopher Ruff|title=Body size, body proportions, and mobility in the Tyrolean "Iceman"|journal=Journal of Human Evolution|date=July 2006|volume=51|issue=1 |pages=91–101|doi=10.1016/j.jhevol.2006.02.001|pmid=16549104|last2=Holt|first2=BM|last3=Sládek|first3=V|last4=Berner|first4=M|last5=Murphy Jr|first5=WA|last6=Zur Nedden|first6=D|last7=Seidler|first7=H|last8=Recheis|first8=W}}</ref>。

研究发现其肩部有箭伤，箭杆被拔出，箭头留在体内，拔出时加重了箭伤。死因为失血过多，死前8小时正前往山谷。他身旁还放置了一把铜制的斧头和一个装有14只箭的箭袋。可推断死时奥茨正把随身物品放在一边休息。

除武器外冰人还携带了一些毒品或者带有兴奋剂成分的药品。而且身上有多处[[紋身|紋身]]，左腕刺有2颗穗子，膝盖上刺有一个十字，并在背部刺有十条直线。部分紋身位置與[[中國|中國]]的[[針灸|針灸]]學說的穴位相應，這些穴位可以作治療消化系統寄生蟲及骨骼退化等疾病、正是冰人所患的疾病。

在衣著方面，他穿著草織袍子及皮背心，而防水鞋子則以[[熊|熊]]和鹿皮製造。

冰人奥茨是距今为止发现的最早并保存最完好的冰封欧洲人类木乃伊，具有相当大研究价值<ref name="disco">{{cite news|first=Rossella |last=Lorenzi |url=http://news.discovery.com/history/otzi-face-reconstruction-110225.html |title=The Iceman Mummy: Finally Face to Face |date=25 February 2011 |accessdate=24 June 2011 |publisher=[[Discovery_News|Discovery News]] |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110619001445/http://news.discovery.com/history/otzi-face-reconstruction-110225.html |archivedate=19 六月 2011 }}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=Spindler|first=Konrad|title=The man in the ice|year=1995|publisher=Phoenix|isbn=0-7538-1260-6|pages=178–184}}</ref>。冰人身上的紋身被認為是具有和[[指壓|指壓]]或[[針灸|針灸]]相像的疼痛舒緩效用<ref name="redorbit.com"/>。如果屬實，這將比中國最早記載的針灸使用（公元前一千年）早上二千年。

=== 遗传分析 ===
根据2012年2月28日的一份报告，奥茨的全部基因信息已被测序<ref name="keller2012">{{cite journal |last1=Keller |first1=Andreas |last2=Graefen |first2=Angela |last3=Ball |first3=Markus |last4=Matzas |first4=Mark |last5=Boisguerin |first5=Valesca |last6=Maixner |first6=Frank |last7=Leidinger |first7=Petra |last8=Backes |first8=Christina |last9=Khairat |first9=Rabab |last10=Forster |first10=Michael |last11=Stade |first11=Björn |last12=Franke |first12=Andre |last13=Mayer |first13=Jens |last14=Spangler |first14=Jessica |last15=McLaughlin |first15=Stephen |last16=Shah |first16=Minita |last17=Lee |first17=Clarence |last18=Harkins |first18=Timothy T. |last19=Sartori |first19=Alexander |last20=Moreno-Estrada |first20=Andres |last21=Henn |first21=Brenna |last22=Sikora |first22=Martin |last23=Semino |first23=Ornella |last24=Chiaroni |first24=Jacques |last25=Rootsi |first25=Siiri |last26=Myres |first26=Natalie M. |last27=Cabrera |first27=Vicente M. |last28=Underhill |first28=Peter A. |last29=Bustamante |first29=Carlos D. |last30=Vigl |first30=Eduard Egarter |display-authors=2 |date=28 February 2012 |title=New insights into the Tyrolean Iceman's origin and phenotype as inferred by whole-genome sequencing |journal=Nature Communications |volume=3 |page=698 |doi=10.1038/ncomms1701 |pmid=22426219}}</ref>。奥茨的[[人类Y染色体DNA单倍型类群|人类Y染色体DNA单倍型类群]]属于由M201, P287, P15, L223和L91（G-L91, ISOGG分类G2a2b, 之前称为 "G2a4"）等SNP点位定义的[[人类Y染色体DNA单倍型类群#单倍型类群_F_(G,_H_&_IJK)|单倍群G]]。

2013年10月有报道指出19名男性现代[[蒂罗尔州|蒂罗尔人]]与奥茨有亲缘关系。Institute of Legal Medicine的科学家们分析了3,700名蒂罗尔人献血者的DNA后，发现有19人与这位5,300年前的人有相同的基因突变<ref>{{cite web|date=10 October 2013|title=Link to Oetzi the Iceman found in living Austrians|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-24477038|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=12 October 2013|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131012043826/http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-24477038|archivedate=12 October 2013}}</ref>。

2008年意大利[[卡梅里诺大学|卡梅里诺大学]]的研究组对冰人的[[线粒体DNA|线粒体DNA]]分析表明，他不属于任何已知的现代人种<ref>{{citation|author= Luca Ermini [''et al.'']|title= Complete Mitochondrial Genome Sequence of the Tyrolean Iceman |journal= Current Biology |date= 30 October 2008 |pmid=}}.</ref><ref name="natgeo">{{cite news | first = Ker  | last = Than | url = http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2011/06/110623-iceman-mummy-otzi-meal-goat-stomach-science/ | title = Iceman's Stomach Sampled—Filled With Goat Meat  |date = 23 June 2011 | accessdate =24 June 2011 | publisher=[[National_Geographic_Society|National Geographic]] }}</ref>。

根据[[常染色体|常染色体]]DNA推断，奥茨与南欧人有最接近的关系，特别是[[异域物种形成#地理隔离|地理隔离]]的[[科西嘉人|科西嘉人]]和[[撒丁岛|撒丁岛]]人<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hudsonalpha.org/ancient-dna-reveals-genetic-relationship-between-todays-sardinians-and-neolithic-europeans|title=Ancient DNA reveals genetic relationship between today’s Sardinians and Neolithic Europeans – HudsonAlpha Institute for Biotechnology|date=19 November 2015|publisher=|accessdate=30 August 2016|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161008061113/http://hudsonalpha.org/ancient-dna-reveals-genetic-relationship-between-todays-sardinians-and-neolithic-europeans|archivedate=8 October 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nature.com/articles/ncomms1701|title=New insights into the Tyrolean Iceman's origin and phenotype as inferred by whole-genome sequencing|first=|last=|publisher=Nature|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170717171733/http://www.nature.com/articles/ncomms1701|archivedate=17 July 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|url=http://www.nature.com/news/iceman-s-dna-reveals-health-risks-and-relations-1.10130|title=Iceman's DNA reveals health risks and relations|first=Ewen|last=Callaway|publisher=|accessdate=30 August 2016|doi=10.1038/nature.2012.10130|journal=Nature|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160911110500/http://www.nature.com/news/iceman-s-dna-reveals-health-risks-and-relations-1.10130|archivedate=11 September 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://notizie.tiscali.it/articoli/scienza/12/02/tratti-genetici-oetzi-abitanti-sardegna-corsica.html |title=Tratti genetici comuni tra la mummia Oetzi e gli attuali abitanti di Sardegna e Corsica |date=28 February 2012 |publisher=Tiscali |deadurl=no |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120302190033/http://notizie.tiscali.it/articoli/scienza/12/02/tratti-genetici-oetzi-abitanti-sardegna-corsica.html |archivedate=2 March 2012 }}</ref>。

通过对冰人奧茨DNA测序显示，冰人可能属于已经在欧洲消失種族。冰人生有褐色的眼睛、[[O型血|O型血]]，且患有[[乳糖不耐症|乳糖不耐症]]<ref>{{cite journal|coauthors=Graefen, Angela, Ball, Markus, Matzas, Mark, Boisguerin, Valesca, Maixner, Frank, Leidinger, Petra, Backes, Christina, Khairat, Rabab, Forster, Michael, Stade, Björn, Franke, Andre, Mayer, Jens, Spangler, Jessica, McLaughlin, Stephen, Shah, Minita, Lee, Clarence, Harkins, Timothy T., Sartori, Alexander, Moreno-Estrada, Andres, Henn, Brenna, Sikora, Martin, Semino, Ornella, Chiaroni, Jacques, Rootsi, Siiri, Myres, Natalie M., Cabrera, Vicente M., Underhill, Peter A., Bustamante, Carlos D., Vigl, Eduard Egarter, Samadelli, Marco, Cipollini, Giovanna, Haas, Jan, Katus, Hugo, O'Connor, Brian D., Carlson, Marc R.J., Meder, Benjamin, Blin, Nikolaus, Meese, Eckart, Pusch, Carsten M., Zink, Albert|title=New insights into the Tyrolean Iceman's origin and phenotype as inferred by whole-genome sequencing|journal=Nature Communications|date=NaN-NaN-NaN|volume=3|pages=698|doi=10.1038/ncomms1701|last=Keller|first=Andreas}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.discovery.com/history/oetzi-iceman-mummy-blood-120502.html|title=Iceman Lived a While After Arrow Wound|author=|work=DNews}}</ref>。

== 古代罪案說 ==
在CAT掃瞄結果當中，發現冰人其中一邊肩膊被一枚箭頭深深刺入，而雙手、雙腕及胸膛也有不少傷痕，顯示冰人曾與敵人打鬥，並負傷逃走，因失血過多而死亡。血液樣本色素顯示冰人的傷口曾流血達18小時，在死前把隨身物品放在一旁並躺下休息，隨後死去<ref>{{Citation|last=Samadelli |first=M |author2=M Melis |author3=M Miccoli |author4=E Egarter-Vigl |author5=AR Zink |title=Complete Mapping of the Tattoos of the 5300-year-old Tyrolean Iceman |journal=Journal of Cultural Heritage |date=2015 |volume=16 |number=5 |pages=753–758 |doi=10.1016/j.culher.2014.12.005}}</ref><ref name="redorbit.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.redorbit.com/news/science/1113319184/scan-finds-new-tattoos-on-5300-year-old-iceman-012215/ |title=Scan finds new tattoos on 5300-year-old Iceman |last1=Deter-Wolf |first1=Aaron |date= January 22, 2015 |access-date= November 16, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=Pabst |first=M.A. |author2=I Letofsky-Papst |author3=E Bock |author4=M Moser |author5=L Dorfer |author6=E Egarter-Vigl |author7=F Hoffer |title=The Tattoos of the Tyrolean Iceman: A Light Microscopical, Ultrastructural and Element Analytical Study |journal=Journal of Archaeological Science |date=2009 |volume=36 |pages=2335–2341 |doi=10.1016/j.jas.2009.06.016}}</ref>。

== 冰人詛咒 ==

'''折背龜屬'''（'''''Kinixys'''''）是[[陸龜科|陸龜科]]下的一個[[屬|屬]]。其下生物只生活在[[非洲|非洲]]，<ref>[http://www.unc.edu/~dtkirkpa/stuff/kinixys.html  Kirkpatrick, D. 1998. African Hingeback Tortoises of the Genus ''Kinixys'', Reptile & Amphibian Magazine 54: 32-37.] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121021051047/http://www.unc.edu/~dtkirkpa/stuff/kinixys.html |date=2012-10-21 }}</ref>  既有[[食肉類|食肉類]]也有[[食草類|食草類]]。

== 種 ==
該屬下現有6[[種|種]]：
* [[鐘紋折背龜|鐘紋折背龜]] ''Kinixys belliana''
* [[鋸齒折背龜|鋸齒折背龜]] ''Kinixys erosa''
* [[荷葉折背龜|荷葉折背龜]] ''Kinixys homeana''
* [[洛巴策折背龜|洛巴策折背龜]] ''kinixys lobatsiana''
* [[壘包折背龜|壘包折背龜]] ''Kinixys natalensis''
* [[斑紋折背龜|斑紋折背龜]] ''Kinixys spekii''

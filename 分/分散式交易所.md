'''分散式交易所'''是一種不存在中央權力的[[去中心化|分散式]]運作[[加密貨幣交易所|加密货币交易所]]。分散式交易所允許[[對等網路|點對點]]進行[[密碼貨幣|加密貨幣]]交易。<ref name="wsj">{{Cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/why-cryptocurrency-exchange-hacks-keep-happening-1531656000|title=Cryptocurrency Exchanges Are Getting Hacked Because It’s Easy|author=Russolillo|first=Steven|date=2018-07-16|work=Wall Street Journal|accessdate=2018-09-11|author2=Jeong|first2=Eun-Young|language=en-US|issn=0099-9660}}</ref>
[[File:Switcheo_ledger.png|替代=]]
分散式交易所用戶並不需要將資產轉移至交易所，因此降低了因交易所遭黑客攻擊令資產被盜的風險。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.digitaljournal.com/business/big-investment-in-cryptocurrency-startup-altcoin-io/article/531556|title=Big investment in cryptocurrency startup Altcoin.io|author=Sandle|first=Tim|date=2018-09-09|work=Digital Journal|accessdate=2018-09-11}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/alexis-ohanians-vc-firm-invests-2-1-million-in-crypto-trading-platform-1520365270|title=Alexis Ohanian’s VC Firm Invests in Crypto Trading Platform|author=Castellanos|first=Sara|date=2018-03-06|work=Wall Street Journal|accessdate=2018-09-11|language=en-US|issn=0099-9660}}</ref>分散式交易所亦可以防止由虛假交易（[[wash_trade|wash trade]]）造成的虛假交易量或價格操縱。相對於實施[[認識你的客戶|認識解你的客户]]程序的一般交易所，分散式交易所的匿名性較高。

鑑於分散式交易所面對的交易量低與[[市场流通性|市场流通性]]不足，<ref name="wsj" />一個名為0x的項目正在建設分散式交易所之間的可互換協議，希望可以解決流通性問題。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://techcrunch.com/2018/07/16/0x/|title=0x lets any app be the Craigslist of cryptocurrency|work=TechCrunch|accessdate=2018-11-24|language=en-US}}</ref>

== 缺点 ==
由於不需進行[[認識你的客戶|認識解你的客户]]過程以及交易並不可逆，若用戶的帳戶密碼或私钥被盜，用戶將面臨資產損失而沒有途徑追討。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2018-01-30/record-crypto-heist-raises-the-appeal-of-a-new-type-of-exchange|title=Bloomberg - Record Crypto Heist Raises the Appeal of a New Type of Exchange|work=www.bloomberg.com|accessdate=2018-11-17}}</ref>

== 分散程度 ==
分散式交易所中仍然可以存在中心化的要素，例如一些分散式交易所的控制權可能集中於一些中央權威。一個明顯的例子是IDEX透過對交易平台的控制而令紐約市民無法使用該平台提交買賣訂單。<ref name=":1">{{Cite tweet|user=Aurora_dao|number=1054917418621984768|date=23 Oct 2018|title=#IDEX will begin blocking new orders from users with New York State IP addresses on Thursday, October 25th (6pm UTC). Cancels and withdrawals will remain active.}}</ref>

2018年7月，有報導指出分散式交易所Bancor遭受黑客攻擊，在資產被凍結前損失為1350萬美元。<ref name="bancor-freeze-loss">{{Cite web|url=https://www.zdnet.com/article/another-hack-rocks-cryptocurrency-trading-bancor-loses-23-5-million/|title=Another hack rocks cryptocurrency trading: Bancor loses $13.5 million|author=Osborne|first=Charlie|work=ZDNet|language=en|accessdate=2018-12-13}}</ref>同年9月，Newdex亦發生基於[[EOS.IO|EOS.IO]]區塊鏈的代幣遭黑客入侵的事件。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Hackers create 1B fake EOS to rob ‘decentralized’ cryptocurrency exchange|url=https://thenextweb.com/hardfork/2018/09/18/eos-hackers-exchange-fake/|work=Hard Fork {{!}} The Next Web|date=2018-09-18|accessdate=2019-02-08|language=en-us|first=David|last=Canellis}}</ref>[[莱特币|莱特币]]的創造者[[Charlie_Lee_(computer_scientist)|查理.李]]在推特表示，如果一家交易所可以令用戶損失資產或有權凍結用戶資產，那麼該交易所便不可能是分散式的。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2018/07/10/bancor-loses-23-5m/|title=The crypto world’s latest hack sees Bancor lose $23.5M|work=TechCrunch|language=en-US|accessdate=2018-12-13}}</ref>

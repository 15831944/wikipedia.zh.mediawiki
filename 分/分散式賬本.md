'''分散式賬本'''（又稱'''共享賬本'''﹑'''分散式賬本技術'''，{{lang-en|distributed ledger technology}}，簡稱：DLT）是一個於多站點﹑多國家或多家機構所組成的網絡上進行電子數據複製﹑共享及同步的同識，<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/492972/gs-16-1-distributed-ledger-technology.pdf|title=Distributed Ledger Technology: beyond block chain|accessdate=|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref>當中並不存在中史管理員或集中的[[電腦數據存貯器|数据存储]]。<ref name="raiib">{{Cite book|url=https://play.google.com/store/books/details?id=uNM0DQAAQBAJ|title=Restructuring and Innovation in Banking|last=Scardovi|first=Claudio|publisher=Springer|accessdate=21 November 2016|year=2016|isbn=9783319402048|page=36}}</ref>

[[對等網路|對等網絡]]與[[共識機制|共識機制]]確保了跨[[節點|節點]]間的數據複製。<ref name="raiib" />區塊鏈系統是分散式賬本的一種，該系統可以是公共或私人的。

== 原理 ==
分散式賬本的[[数据库|數據庫]]分佈於[[對等網路|對等網絡]]的節點(設備)上，當中每個節點都複製及存儲與賬本完全相同的副本並獨立更新。

分散式賬本的主要優點是不存在中央權威。當一個賬本出現更新，每個節點都將執行一筆新交易，然後所有節點以共識機制投票決定哪一個副本是正確的。一旦達成共識，所有其他節點都會按照正確副本的數據進行更新。<ref>{{Cite journal|title=Distributed ledger technology: Applications and implications|last=Maull|first=Roger|last2=Godsiff|first2=Phil|date=21 Sep 2017|journal=FINRA|issue=5|doi=10.1002/jsc.2148|volume=26|pages=481–489|last3=Mulligan|first3=Catherine|last4=Brown|first4=Alan|last5=Kewell|first5=Beth}}</ref><ref name="Shaan">{{Cite web|author=Ray|first=Shaan|title=The Difference Between Blockchains & Distributed Ledger Technology|url=https://towardsdatascience.com/the-difference-between-blockchains-distributed-ledger-technology-42715a0fa92|work=Towards Data Science|accessdate=25 September 2018|date=2018-02-20}}</ref> 

賬本透過加密密鑰與簽名而控制其安全性。 <ref>{{Cite web|author=UK Government Chief Scientific Advisor|title=Distributed Ledger Technology: beyond block chain|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/news/distributed-ledger-technology-beyond-block-chain|work=Gov.uk|publisher=UK Government|accessdate=25 September 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|author=Brakeville|first=Sloane|title=Blockchain basics: Introduction to distributed ledgers|url=https://www.ibm.com/developerworks/cloud/library/cl-blockchain-basics-intro-bluemix-trs/index.html|work=IBM Developer|publisher=IBM|accessdate=25 Sep 2018|date=18 Mar 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|author=Rutland|first=Emily|title=Blockchain Byte|url=https://www.finra.org/sites/default/files/2017_BC_Byte.pdf|work=FINRA|publisher=R3 Research|accessdate=25 September 2018}}</ref>

== 应用 ==
2016年，一些银行對分散式賬本支付進行測試<ref name="cbl">{{Cite news|url=http://www.afr.com/technology/central-banks-look-to-the-future-of-money-with-blockchain-technology-trial-20161117-gss4nd|title=Central banks look to the future of money with blockchain technology trial|author=|accessdate=7 December 2016|date=21 November 2016|work=Australian Financial Review|publisher=Fairfax Media Publications}}</ref> ，探討投資分散式賬本是否有助於其業務。<ref name="raiib">{{Cite book|url=https://play.google.com/store/books/details?id=uNM0DQAAQBAJ|title=Restructuring and Innovation in Banking|last=Scardovi|first=Claudio|publisher=Springer|accessdate=21 November 2016|year=2016|isbn=9783319402048|page=36}}</ref>建立於[[区块链|區塊鏈]]上的[[加密貨幣|加密貨幣]]是分散式賬本的實際應用，比較知名的有[[比特幣歷史|比特幣]]﹑[[以太坊|以太坊]]﹑[https://eos.io/ EOS]等，一些商戶亦開始考慮是否接受[[加密貨幣|加密貨幣支付]]。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Should Your Company Accept Bitcoin And Other Cryptocurrency Payments?|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/kateharrison/2018/09/10/should-your-company-accept-bitcoin-and-other-cryptocurrency-payments/|work=Forbes|accessdate=2019-01-31|language=en|first=Kate|last=Harrison}}</ref>

== 类型 ==
分散式賬本可以是有權限或不設權限的，分別在於任何人或只有被許可的人可以去運行節點並驗證交易。<ref name="types">{{Cite web|title=Blockchains & Distributed Ledger Technologies|work=Blockchain Hub}}</ref>賬本的共識算法亦有不同，例如[[工作量證明|工作量証明]]﹑[[持有量證明|持有量証明]]﹑[[持有量証明|代理持有量証明]]等。此外，賬本亦可能是可[[挖礦|挖礦]]（節點的工作貢獻者可以取得新貨幣）或不可[[挖礦|挖礦]]（[[加密貨幣|加密貨幣]]的創造者從一開始持有全部貨幣）的。

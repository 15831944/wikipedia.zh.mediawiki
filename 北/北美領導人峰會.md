'''北美领导人峰会'''（{{lang-en|North American Leader's Summit}}，簡稱NALS，又或被[[大眾媒體|媒體]]稱為「'''三個老友的峰會'''」，{{lang-en|Three Amigos Summit}}）是[[加拿大总理|加拿大总理]]、[[墨西哥总统|墨西哥总统]]和[[美国总统|美国总统]]的三方年度峰会<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.winnipegfreepress.com/canada/three-amigos-summit-not-so-chummy-145886435.html|title=Three Amigos summit not so chummy|last=Cheadle|first=Bruce|date=April 3, 2012|work=Winnipeg Free Press|accessdate=January 27, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thestar.com/news/world/article/1155077--analysis-stephen-harper-faces-tricky-terrain-during-three-amigos-summit|title=Analysis: Stephen Harper faces tricky terrain during ‘Three Amigos' summit|last=Potter|first=Mitch|date=April 2, 2012|work=Toronto Star}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.npr.org/2016/06/29/483976561/obama-to-meet-mexican-canadian-counterparts-in-ottawa|title=Obama To Meet Mexican-Canadian Counterparts In Ottawa|last=Horsley|first=Scott|date=June 29, 2016|work=Morning Edition|publisher=NPR|accessdate=January 27, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://trade.gov/nacp/nals.asp|title=North American Leaders' Summit (NALS)|accessdate=January 27, 2017|publisher=International Trade Administration, US Department of Commerce}}</ref>。 此峰會最初是[[北美安全与繁荣伙伴关系|北美安全与繁荣伙伴关系]]的一部分，北美安全与繁荣伙伴关系由美墨加三國在2005年建立，2009年後不再活躍<ref name="coha 2016 nals">{{Cite web|url=http://whitehouse.gov/news/releases/2007/06/20070615-10.html|title=President Bush to Attend North American Leaders' Summit in Canada|accessdate=January 27, 2017|date=June 15, 2007|publisher=Office of the White House Press Secretary|archiveurl=https://georgewbush-whitehouse.archives.gov/news/releases/2007/06/20070615-10.html|archivedate=January 20, 2009}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.coha.org/three-amigos-convene-again-the-2016-north-american-leaders-summit/|title=Three Amigos Convene Again: The 2016 North American Leaders’ Summit|accessdate=January 27, 2017|date=July 1, 2016|last=Baril|first=Sophie-Anne}}</ref>。

最近一次的北美领导人峰会于2016年6月29日舉行，由[[加拿大总理|加拿大总理]][[賈斯汀·杜魯多|賈斯汀·杜魯多]]主辦，會場在[[安大略省|安大略省]][[渥太華|渥太華]]的[[加拿大國立美術館|加拿大國立美術館]]。當日杜魯多接待了美国总统[[奥巴马|奥巴马]]和墨西哥总统[[恩里克·培尼亚·涅托|恩里克·培尼亚·涅托]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2016/06/29/canada-hosts-north-american-leaders-summit|title=Canada hosts North American Leaders’ Summit|accessdate=January 27, 2017|date=June 29, 2016|publisher=Office of the Prime Minister of Canada|language=en}}</ref>。三位领导人讨论[[各地LGBT權利|LGBT權利]]的共同承诺（尤其[[2016年奥兰多夜店枪击案|同性戀夜店枪击案]]事件發生過後），[[可再生能源|可再生能源]]發展和[[自由貿易|自由貿易]]<ref name="time 2016 nals remarks">{{Cite news|url=http://time.com/4388789/three-amigos-summit-transcript-obama-nieto-trudeau/|title=Read the Remarks From the ‘Three Amigos’ Summit Press Conference|last=Reilly|first=Katie|date=June 29, 2016|work=Time|location=New York, NY}}</ref>。各国领导人还宣布建立北美气候、清洁能源和环境伙伴关系以及公佈相关计划<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://pm.gc.ca/eng/news/2016/06/29/leaders-statement-north-american-climate-clean-energy-and-environment-partnership|title=Leaders’ Statement on a North American Climate, Clean Energy, and Environment Partnership|accessdate=January 27, 2017|date=June 29, 2016|publisher=Office of the Prime Minister of Canada|language=en}}</ref> 。2016年的峰会是三位领导人第一次也是最后一次会面，杜魯多在[[2015年加拿大联邦大选|2015年11月大选]]中當選、奧巴馬任期在2017年1月結束、涅托任期亦在2018年2月結束。杜魯多形容是次会面為「友好的，正如你期待朋友般，但也有点辛酸。」

There was no summit in 2013.}}</ref>。

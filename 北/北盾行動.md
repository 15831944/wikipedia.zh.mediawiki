'''北盾行動'''是[[以色列|以色列]]在2018年12月4日展開的[[军事行动|军事行动]]，目标為尋找并摧毁[[真主党|真主党]]所建造由[[黎巴嫩|黎巴嫩]]进入以色列北部的[[地道戰|隧道]]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-46437629|title=Israel targets Hezbollah 'terror tunnels'|date=4 December 2018|publisher=BBC News|accessdate=5 December 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.cnn.com/2018/12/04/middleeast/israel-lebanon-hezbollah-tunnels-intl/index.html|title=Israel begins operation to 'expose and thwart' Hezbollah tunnels|last=Carey|first=Andrew|date=4 December 2018|publisher=CNN|last2=Liebermann|first2=Oren|accessdate=5 December 2018|last3=Wedeman|first3=Ben|last4=Schwartz|first4=Michael}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.jta.org/2018/12/04/news-opinion/israel-launches-operation-destroy-hezbollah-tunnels-northern-border|title=Israel launches operation to destroy Hezbollah tunnels on northern border|date=4 December 2018|accessdate=5 December 2018|agency=Jewish Telegraphic Agency}}</ref>。这次行动是伊朗－以色列衝突的其中一部分。国际社会普遍认为真主党是獲伊朗資助和撐腰的恐怖组织<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://trad.cn.rfi.fr/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20181204-%E4%BB%A5%E8%BB%8D%E5%AE%A3%E5%B8%83%E7%99%BC%E5%8B%95%E6%91%A7%E6%AF%80%E7%9C%9F%E4%B8%BB%E9%BB%A8%E6%89%80%E6%8C%96%E6%8E%98%E6%94%BB%E6%93%8A%E6%80%A7%E9%9A%A7%E9%81%93%E8%A1%8C%E5%8B%95|title=以軍宣布發動“摧毀真主黨所挖掘攻擊性隧道”行動|accessdate=2018-12-06|date=2018-12-04|work=法廣|language=zh-Hant}}</ref>，因此是次軍事行動獲得[[歐盟|歐盟]]等西方國家支持<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.israelnationalnews.com/News/News.aspx/255788|title=EU supports Israel in 'Northern Shield' operation|accessdate=2018-12-06|work=Israel National News|language=en}}</ref>。

北盾行动中，[[以色列國防軍|以色列國防軍]]部署了作战工兵部队，包括雅哈隆特种部队（Yahalom）、[[重型動力機械|重型機械]]和推土机。行动是在[[以色列军事情报局|以色列军事情报局]]和以色列北方司令部的合作下进行<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.upi.com/amp/Israel-launches-operation-to-destroy-Hezbollah-tunnels/8901543904030/|title=Israel launches operation to destroy Hezbollah tunnels|last=Cone|first=Allen|date=4 December 2018|accessdate=5 December 2018|agency=United Press International}}</ref>。

北盾行动第一天，以色列国防军表示在Metula附近搗破了一条進入以色列的隧道，長度約為220米，其中的37米位處以色列境內。《[[华盛顿邮报|华盛顿邮报]]》指該隧道可能被以色列监视了几年<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/world/middle_east/israel-announces-operation-to-destroy-hezbollah-tunnels-in-its-territory/2018/12/04/f9e74058-f7cb-11e8-8642-c9718a256cbd_story.html|title=Israel announces operation to destroy Hezbollah tunnels under its northern border|last=Morris|first=Loveday|date=4 December 2018|work=The Washington Post|last2=Eglash|first2=Ruth|accessdate=5 December 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bostonglobe.com/news/world/2018/12/04/israel-announces-operation-destroy-hezbollah-tunnels-its-territory/LuqOK7qbamO7UwSJmWaGlL/story.html|title=Israel announces operation to destroy Hezbollah tunnels in its territory|accessdate=5 December 2018|date=4 December 2018|last=Loveday Morris}}</ref>。

2018年12月6日，以軍表示發現第二條地道，起端於黎巴嫩鄰近以黎邊界的Ramyah村。<ref>{{cite news|last=Kubovich|first=Yaniv |url=https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/idf-finds-another-hezbollah-tunnel-crossing-into-israel-asks-un-to-help-destroy-it-1.6723416|title=IDF Reveals It Found Another Hezbollah Tunnel Crossing Into Israel, Asks UN to Help Destroy It|work=Haaretz|date=6 December 2018|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref>

2018年12月11日，以軍表示找到第三條跨境地道的位置。<ref>{{cite news|last1=Kubovich |first1=Yaniv |last2=Landau |first2=Noa |url=https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/israel-says-it-discovered-third-cross-border-hezbollah-tunnel-1.6740348|title=Israel Says It Discovered Third Cross-border Hezbollah Tunnel|work=Haaretz|date=11 December 2018|access-date=2018-12-12}}</ref>

2018年12月16日，以軍表示找到第四條跨境地道的位置。<ref>[https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/israeli-army-says-fourth-hezbollah-attack-tunnel-found-crossing-from-lebanon-1.6746693 Israeli Army Says Fourth Hezbollah Attack Tunnel Found Crossing From Lebanon], Haaretz, 16 December 2018</ref>

2018年12月25日，以軍表示在數日前找到第五條地道，並已炸毀。<ref>https://www.timesofisrael.com/army-finds-destroys-fifth-attack-tunnel-from-lebanon/</ref>

2019年1月13日，以軍找到第六條地道。<ref name=sixth/>

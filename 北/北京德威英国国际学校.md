'''北京德威英国国际学校''' ('''Dulwich College Beijing''') [[北京|北京]]英国国际学校。 北京德威英国国际学校是[[德威学校|德威学校]]([[:en:Dulwich_College|Dulwich College]])所属学校。<ref name=SpencerBoat>Spencer, Richard. "[http://www.telegraph.co.uk/culture/3656887/Slow-boater-to-China.html Slow boater to China]." 《[[每日电讯报|每日电讯报]]》. 2 December 2006. Retrieved on 21 June 2013.</ref> 2005年8月北京德威英国国际学校公开招生。北京德威英国国际学校有1,450多个学生(1岁学生-18岁学生)。<ref>"[http://www.dulwich-beijing.cn/page.cfm?p=355 An Introduction]." 北京德威英国国际学校. Retrieved on October 12, 2013.</ref>

北京德威英国国际学校营运两个校园:Legend Garden Campus(1岁学生-18岁学生)在[[顺义区|顺义区]]和Riviera Main Campus(1岁学生-6岁学生)在[[朝阳区_(北京市)|朝阳区]]。<ref>"[http://www.dulwich-beijing.cn/page.cfm?p=362 Contact & Visitor Info]." 北京德威英国国际学校. Retrieved on October 12, 2013. "Legend Garden Campus[...]89 Capital Airport Road Shunyi District Beijing 101300 PRC" and "Riviera Main Campus[...]1 Xiang Jiang Bei Lu Jing Shun Lu Chao Yang District Beijing 100103 PRC" - Chinese addresses: Legend Garden: "北京德威英国国际学校 北京市顺义区首都机场路89号丽京花园7区 邮编：101300" Riviera Main Campus: "北京德威英国国际学校 北京市朝阳区京顺路香江北路1号香江花园 邮编：100103"</ref>

北京德威英国国际学校的英文课和数学课采用小班教学。<ref>Liu, Anqi. "[http://english.cctv.com/program/chinatoday/20091120/101587.shtml Student life at international schools]." ([http://archive.is/Zpxax Archive]) [[中国中央电视台|中国中央电视台]]. November 20, 2009. Retrieved on October 12, 2013. "Chen Xialin, Chinese Dpt. Director of Dulwich College International School of Beijing, said, "Our school's biggest goal is to allow children study in a confident environment. So English and mathematics are taught in small classes, which are divided by different levels. We hope students could learn with others who are at the same level as he is. So he can study in a relatively relaxed environment. ""</ref>

'''北歐金'''是一種金色[[銅合金列表|銅合金]]，用在[[歐元硬幣|歐元硬幣]]其中三個面額：50分、20分、10分。它也在其他國家使用了好幾年，[[瑞典|瑞典]]10[[瑞典克朗|克朗]]是最早使用北歐金的硬幣<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.scda.com/copper/development-history.html |title=Nordic Gold in our pockets |date= |website=scda.com |publisher=Scandinavian Copper Development Association |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120723022617/http://www.scda.com/copper/development-history.html |archive-date=July 23, 2012 |access-date=May 12, 2015}}</ref>（因此稱為北歐金）。<ref>{{cite web | author= Predecimal.com | url= http://www.predecimal.com/euroarticle.htm | title=The selection of the alloy for the New Euro Coins | accessdate=2007-05-29}}</ref>它的組成是89%[[铜|铜]]、5%[[铝|铝]]、5%[[锌|锌]]和1%[[锡|锡]]。<ref>{{cite web | author= Gizmology.net | url= http://gizmology.net/metals.htm | title= Notes on metals | accessdate=2015-01-16}}</ref>

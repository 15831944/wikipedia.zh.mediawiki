'''北歐器具'''（{{lang-en|Nordic Ware}}）或稱'''北歐鋁製品公司'''，是一家總部設於美國[[明尼阿波利斯|明尼阿波利斯]]聖路易斯公園郊區的公司。在1950年代初，美國引進了圓環平底鍋<ref name=businessweek>{{cite web |url=http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=6922001 |title= Company Overview of Northland Aluminum Products, Inc. |publisher=Bloomberg Businessweek |accessdate=2013-09-02}}</ref>。

1946年由戴維·達奎斯特（創辦人亨利·戴維和多蘿西·達奎斯特的兒子）創立<ref name=NYT>{{cite web|work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2005/01/06/obituaries/h-david-dalquist-86-bundt-pans-inventor-dies.html?_r=0|title=H. David Dalquist, 86, Bundt Pan's Inventor, Dies|author=The Associated Press|date=2005-01-06}}</ref>，並於1950年與他的妻子多蘿西、他的兄弟麥克·S·達奎斯特以及他們的朋友唐納德·尼格恩，將該商標命名為“圓環”（Bundt）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nordicware.com/about/founders|title=Nordic Ware's Founders|accessdate=2013-09-02|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130807033509/http://www.nordicware.com/about/founders|archivedate=2013-08-07}}</ref><ref name=slphistory>{{cite web|url=http://www.slphistory.org/history/nordicware.asp |title=St. Louis Park Historical Society — Nordic Ware |accessdate=2008-08-19 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090105222913/http://www.slphistory.org/history/nordicware.asp |archivedate=2009-01-05 }}</ref><ref name=abc>{{cite AV media | people=Muir, David (Journalist)| date=2011-04-21 | title=ABC World News: Made in America--Nordic Ware | medium=News cast - Web version | location=Lebanon | publisher=ABC News}}</ref>。北歐器具仍然是由家族擁有經營權，戴維·達奎斯特是最早的公司總裁<ref name=businessweek/>。

除了圓環平底鍋外，北歐器具在炊具和烤盤方面也是個開拓者。他們推出了專利的“微笑環行”（Micro-Go-Round）產品，或稱“自動食物旋轉器”<ref>{{cite news|title=Opportunity LOL Reviews: Nordic Ware's Potato Baker |url=http://www.opplol.com/nordicwarepotatobaker.html |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110220161113/http://www.opplol.com/nordicwarepotatobaker.html |archivedate=2011-02-20}}</ref>。

北歐器具在美國北部銷售了7,000多萬套器具<ref>{{cite news|title=Minnesota Made, Family-Owned|url=https://www.nordicware.com/heritage/|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170605041820/https://www.nordicware.com/heritage/|date=2016-10-22|archivedate=2017-06-05|deadurl=yes}}</ref>。為慶祝公司成立六十週年，公司11月15日指定為“圓環國民日”<ref name=anniversary>{{cite press |url = http://www.nordicware.com/pressroom/view/2011-07-29-6 |title = Light the Candles on the Bundt Cake—Announcing the 60th Anniversary of Nordic Ware |publisher = Nordic Ware |date = 2006-01-22 |place = Minneapolis |accessdate = 2012-12-19 |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20120622091335/http://www.nordicware.com/pressroom/view/2011-07-29-6 |archive-date = 2012-06-22 |dead-url = yes }}</ref>。該公司每年也舉辦一場“橫跨包美國”比賽來慶祝最好的[[圓環蛋糕|圓環蛋糕]]作品，以及每月舉辦兩次烹飪課程；每場比賽只有24人，星期二晚上6點30分至8點30分舉行<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nordicware.com/bundts-across-america/winners-japan|title=2010 Japan's Contest Winners|accessdate=2011-02-20|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071118182755/http://www.nordicware.com/bundts-across-america/winners-japan|archivedate=2007-11-18}}</ref>。

北歐器具是少數幾間在美國生產大部份的炊具和烤盤公司之一，並向50個國家提供產品。他們只有三件物品不符合國際運輸資格：麵包師傅的喜悅烹飪噴霧、圓環蛋糕[[攪拌機|攪拌機]]和吸菸水壺<ref name=abc/><ref>{{cite news|title=Ineligible Items (FAQ section)|url=https://www.nordicware.com/international-orders/|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170605050243/https://www.nordicware.com/international-orders/|archivedate=2016-11-23|deadurl=no}}</ref>。

== 所在地 ==
在他們的明尼阿波利斯總部和製造工廠，北歐器具品牌被畫在靠近明尼蘇達州立公路100號和明尼蘇達州立高速公路7號交匯處的鉤棍─哈格琳實驗混凝土穀物電梯上面<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.slphistory.org/history/nordicware.asp|title=St. Louis Park Historical Society - Nordic Ware|accessdate=2008-08-19|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090105222913/http://www.slphistory.org/history/nordicware.asp|archivedate=2009-01-05}}</ref>。{{link-en|糧食電梯|Grain elevator}}是美國第一個在世界上成為[[钢筋混凝土|钢筋混凝土]]圓形電梯的公司<ref>{{cite web |last= |first= |url=http://www.mnhs.org/library/tips/history_topics/70peavey.html |title=Peavey–Haglin Experimental Concrete Grain Elevator |work=Minnesota Historical Society Library: History Topics |publisher=Minnesota Historical Society | accessdate = 2006-04-08}}</ref>。在北歐器具之前，糧食電梯為木材公司簽署了標誌，直至北歐器具購買土地並投資40,000美元擴大恢復項目<ref name=slphistory/>。

'''北京国安精英队'''（{{lang-en|Beijing Guoan Talent Singapore FC}}）是一支中国的职业[[足球|足球]]队。该球队曾经参加{{tsl|en|2010 S.League||2010年新加坡联赛|2010年的新加坡足球联赛}}。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091226185925/http://sports.zaobao.com/pages12/soccer091222b.shtml|title=中国超级联赛冠军北京国安将角逐新联赛|accessdate=2018-08-02|date=2009-12-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180802055839/https://web.archive.org/web/20091226185925/http://sports.zaobao.com/pages12/soccer091222b.shtml|archive-date=2018-08-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>

==球队介绍==
北京国安精英队是[[中国足球协会超级联赛|中国足球协会超级联赛]][[北京中赫国安足球俱乐部|北京中赫国安足球俱乐部]]的一支子球队。北京中赫国安足球俱乐部的青年队球员组成了北京国安精英队。北京国安精英队是第9支参与新加坡职业足球联赛的海外球队。<ref name="Editorial 2010">{{cite web | last=Editorial | first=Reuters | title=French and Chinese sides enter Singapore league for 2010 | website=IN | date=2010-01-18 | url=https://in.reuters.com/article/idINIndia-45478320100118 | access-date=2018-08-02 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160630100512/http://in.reuters.com/article/idINIndia-45478320100118 | archive-date=2016-06-30 | dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name="Channel NewsAsia 2017">{{cite web | title=Five on Friday: 5 ways to save the S.League | website=Channel NewsAsia | date=2017-04-23 | url=https://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/sport/five-on-friday-5-ways-to-save-the-s-league-8780238 | access-date=2018-08-02 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170616115303/http://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/sport/five-on-friday-5-ways-to-save-the-s-league-8780238 | archive-date=2017-06-16 | dead-url=no }}</ref>2011年间，新加坡足球总会决定终止北京国安精英队参与新加坡职业足球联赛，取而代之的是[[丹戎巴葛联足球俱乐部|丹戎巴葛联足球俱乐部]]<ref name="Chia 2017">{{cite web | last=Chia | first=Alvin | title=Football: Tanjong Pagar's Liu will run as an independent | website=The Straits Times | date=2017-03-24 | url=https://www.straitstimes.com/sport/football/tanjong-pagars-liu-will-run-as-an-independent | access-date=2018-08-02 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170327024730/http://www.straitstimes.com/sport/football/tanjong-pagars-liu-will-run-as-an-independent | archive-date=2017-03-27 | dead-url=no }}</ref>

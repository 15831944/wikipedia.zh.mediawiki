'''北达科他大学'''（University of North Dakota；缩写：UND）是位于美国[[北达科他州|北达科他州]][[大福克斯|大福克斯]]的一所公立研究型大学。它创立于1883年，比北达科他州的建立还早六年，为该州最古老的高等教育机构。

==历史==
[[File:University_of_North_Dakota_School_of_Medicine.jpg|thumb]]
该大学创立于1883年，比北达科他州的建立还早六年<ref name="founding">{{cite web |url=http://www.und.edu/history/ |title=UND History: The Founding |accessdate=September 17, 2007 |publisher=University of North Dakota |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927012500/http://www.und.edu/history/ |archivedate=September 27, 2007 }}</ref>，次年9月8日开课，早年只是一所小型学校，校区位于市郊。<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.und.edu/history/1890.html |title=UND History: 1890 |accessdate=September 17, 2007 |publisher=University of North Dakota |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070528020936/http://www.und.edu/history/1890.html |archivedate=May 28, 2007 }}</ref>

一战时一度停课<ref name="history1910">{{cite web|url=http://www.und.edu/history/1910.html |title=UND History: 1910 |accessdate=September 17, 2007 |publisher=University of North Dakota |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060914173723/http://www.und.edu/history/1910.html |archivedate=September 14, 2006 }}</ref>，二战过后学校才开始逐步扩大<ref name="history1940">{{cite web|url=http://www.und.edu/history/1940.html |title=UND History: 1940 |accessdate=September 17, 2007 |publisher=University of North Dakota |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060914173859/http://www.und.edu/history/1940.html |archivedate=September 14, 2006 }}</ref>。[[1997年红河水灾|1997年红河水灾]]导致学校再度停课<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.und.edu/history/1990.html |title=UND History: 1990 |accessdate=September 17, 2007 |publisher=University of North Dakota |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20060914174133/http://www.und.edu/history/1990.html |archivedate=September 14, 2006 }}</ref>。

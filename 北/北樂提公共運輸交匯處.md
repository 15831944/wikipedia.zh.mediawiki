'''北樂提公共運輸交匯處'''（{{lang-en|Paradise Interchange}}）是[[南澳州|南澳州]][[阿德萊德|阿德萊德]][[O-Bahn巴士專線|O-Bahn巴士專線]]的公共運輸交匯處。

== 歷史 ==
北樂提公共運輸交匯處是[[O-Bahn巴士專線|O-Bahn巴士專線]]第一期的終點站，於1986年3月2日正式啓用，<ref>Busway Information, Paper One: Background History of the Northeast Corridor Transportation Proposals, South Australian Department of Transport 1983</ref><ref>O-Bahn Busway Information (Brochure), South Australian Department of Transport 1987</ref> 由時任州長[[潘龍忠|潘龍忠]]（John Bannon）主持開幕儀式；直至3月9日才正式通車使用。<ref>"First stage of O-Bahn is up and running" ''[//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adelaide_Advertiser Adelaide Advertiser]'' 3 March 1986 page 11</ref><ref>"Bus-route protests block O-Bahn" ''Adelaide Advertiser'' 10 March 1986 page 17</ref><ref>Annual Report for year ended 30 June 1986 [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_Transport_Authority_(South_Australia) State Tranport Authority] pages 17-19</ref>1989年8月20日，O-Bahn第二期工程通車，終點站改至[[茶樹廣場公共運輸交匯處|茶樹廣場公共運輸交匯處]]<ref>"Thousands at opening of $104m busway" ''Adelaide Advertiser'' 21 August 1989 page 2</ref><ref>Northeast Busway (Brochure) State Transport Authority 1990</ref><ref>[http://infrastructure.sa.gov.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0005/157487/OBAHN_FACT_SHEET_-_BACKGROUND_FINAL.pdf O-Bahn City Access Project Background] [//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Department_of_Planning,_Transport_%26_Infrastructure Department of Planning, Transport & Infrastructure] February 2015</ref>

本站是O-Bahn的第二個車站，位於[[錦林息公共運輸交匯處|錦林息]]和茶樹廣場的中間，距離[[阿德雷得中央商業區|阿德萊德市中心]]約6公里（3.7英里）。

本站設有625個停車位、上落客區。本站設有出入口予巴士進入O-Bahn。

共有34條巴士路線停靠本站。<ref>[https://www.adelaidemetro.com.au/content/download/5585/97314/file/15005_Paradise_Interchange_map_Jan2015.pdf Paradise Interchange] Adelaide Metro</ref>

'''[[朝鲜民主主义人民共和国|朝鲜民主主义人民共和国]]'''的[[植物相|植物相]]與北半球其他溫帶地區有許多相似之處，其生態系多屬[[北方針葉林|北方針葉林]]<ref name="kolbek">{{cite book|author1=Jirí Kolbek |author2=Ivan Jarolímek |author3=Milan Valachovic |title=Forest Vegetation of Northeast Asia|editor1=Jirí Kolbek |editor2=Miroslav Šrůtek |publisher=Springer|year=2003|pages=264, 294|chapter=8: Forest Vegetation of the Northern Korean Peninsula|isbn=1402013701|url=https://books.google.com/?id=VmtPnUbH-uIC&pg=PA263&dq=Pentactina+rupicola#v=onepage&q=Pentactina%20rupicola&f=false}}</ref>。目前共記錄有2898種植物，其中14%為[[特有種|特有種]]<ref name="Haggett"/>。朝鲜平地的原生植物泰半因都市擴張與農業等人類活動而消失，高地則尚存有原生的[[松柏門|松柏]]，其中又以[[赤松|赤松]]為大宗<ref name="kolbek"/>。

-{zh-hant:北韓;zh-hans:朝鲜}-的植物中，有4種被認為[[瀕臨絕種|瀕臨絕種]]<ref name="Haggett">{{cite book|last=Haggett|first= Peter |title=Encyclopedia of World Geography|publisher=Marshall Cavendish|year=2001|pages=3088|isbn=0761472894|url=https://books.google.com/?id=om_31QGM6psC&pg=PA3088&dq=Flora+of+North+Korea+overview#v=onepage&q=Flora%20of%20North%20Korea%20overview&f=false}}</ref>，其中又有3種因形態特殊，在其他現生植物中均無特別相似的類群，而被歸入各自的[[單型|單型屬]]中<ref name=bcgi/>。其中[[薔薇科|薔薇科]]植物''Pentactina rupicola''只分布於[[江原道_(北韓)|江原道]]的[[金剛山_(朝鮮)|金剛山區]]，分子證據顯示薔薇科中，它與分布於北美的{{link-en|岩绣菊属|Petrophytum}}的親緣關係最為接近<ref>{{cite journal|author1=Chunghee Lee |author2=Suk-Pyo Hong |year=2011|title=Phylogenetic relationships of the rare Korean monotypic endemic genus Pentactina Nakai in the tribe Spiraeeae (Rosaceae) based on molecular data|journal=Plant Systematics and Evolution|volume=294|issue=3–4|pages=159–66|doi=10.1007/s00606-011-0457-8}}</ref>。另一種瀕臨絕種的植物是{{link-en|朝鲜白连翘|Abeliophyllum distichum}}，分布於[[朝鮮半島|朝鮮半島]]中部，除了在-{zh-hant:北韓;zh-hans:朝鲜}-被栽種外，也作為[[園藝|園藝植物]]被引進英國與北美<ref name="Kim 1998">{{cite journal | author1 = Kim, Y. S. | author2 = Maunder, M. | year = 1998 | title = Plants in Peril, 24: ''Abeliophyllum distichum'' | journal = [[Curtis's_Botanical_Magazine|Curtis's Botanical Magazine]] | volume = 15 | issue = 2 | pages = 141–146 | doi = 10.1111/1467-8748.00155 | url = http://www.aseanbiodiversity.info/Abstract/51008814.pdf | author =  | access-date = 2018-08-22 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20140513215150/http://www.aseanbiodiversity.info/Abstract/51008814.pdf | archive-date = 2014-05-13 | dead-url = yes }}</ref>，-{zh-hant:北韓;zh-hans:朝鲜}-曾發行以其為主題的郵票。''Sophora koreensis''也是-{zh-hant:北韓;zh-hans:朝鲜}-瀕臨絕種的植物，原本屬於單型屬''Echinosophora''，但有研究以基因序列分析[[槐屬|槐屬]]物種與它的演化關係，主張此植物應該被歸入槐屬中.<ref>{{cite journal|vauthors=Lee WK, Tokuoka T, Heo K |year=2004 |title=Molecular evidence for the inclusion of the Korean endemic genus " Echinosophora" in ''Sophora'' (Fabaceae), and embryological features of the genus|journal=Journal of Plant Research|volume=117|issue=3|pages=209–19|pmid=15221584|doi=10.1007/s10265-004-0150-x}}</ref>。

北韓最大的[[植物園|植物園]]是1959年完工、位於首都[[平壤|平壤]][[大城山|大城山]]山腳的[[朝鮮中央植物園|朝鮮中央植物園]]<ref name=bcgi>{{cite web|url=http://www.bgci.org/worldwide/article/512/|title=Botanic Gardens of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK)|last=Wan Ik Ri|year=2006|work=BGCI|accessdate=23 March 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180822113705/http://www.bgci.org/worldwide/article/512/#|archive-date=2018-08-22|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

'''北悉尼'''（Scottish Gaelic: Am Bàr）是一个曾经的镇，位于[[新斯科舍省|新斯科舍省]]。现为Cape Breton的一个社区。<ref>[http://www66.statcan.gc.ca/eng/acyb_c1942-eng.aspx?opt=/eng/1942/194201570095_p.%2095.pdf], Censuses 1871-1941</ref><ref>[http://www66.statcan.gc.ca/eng/acyb_c1932-eng.aspx?opt=/eng/1932/193201410103_p.%20103.pdf], Censuses 1871-1931</ref><ref>[http://www66.statcan.gc.ca/eng/acyb_c1955-eng.aspx?opt=/eng/1955/195501710145_p.%20145.pdf], Census 1941-1951</ref><ref>[http://www66.statcan.gc.ca/eng/acyb_c1966-eng.aspx?opt=/eng/1966/196602190191_p.%20191.pdf Census 1956-1961]</ref><ref>[http://www66.statcan.gc.ca/eng/acyb_c1967-eng.aspx?opt=/eng/1967/196702210189_p.%20189.pdf], Census 1961</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.gov.ns.ca/finance/publish/CENSUS/Census%201.pdf |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2018年4月20日 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131005011332/http://www.gov.ns.ca/finance/publish/CENSUS/Census%201.pdf |archivedate=2013年10月5日 |df= }}, Censuses 1981-2001</ref><ref>[http://www12.statcan.gc.ca/census-recensement/2006/dp-pd/prof/92-591/index.cfm?Lang=E], Census 2006</ref>


北悉尼位于悉尼海湾的北部。[[布雷顿角岛|布雷顿角岛]]的东海岸。北悉尼是加拿大大西洋沿岸的一个重要据点。

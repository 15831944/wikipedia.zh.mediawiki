'''圣若翰洗者堂'''<ref>[http://cmc-terrasanta.com/en/video/the-parish-of-saint-john-the-baptiste-in-acre-7763.html «The parish of Saint John the Baptiste in Acre»]</ref> ({{lang-he|כנסיית סן חואן באוטיסטה}}; {{lang-la|Ecclesia Sancti Ioannis Baptistae}})是[[以色列|以色列]]北部城市[[阿卡|阿卡]]的一座[[罗马天主教|罗马天主教]]教堂<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ils8BAAAQBAJ|title=DK Eyewitness Travel Guide Jerusalem, Israel, Petra & Sinai|date=2014-09-01|publisher=Penguin|isbn=9781465432698|language=en}}</ref>。它非常靠近海岸和灯塔。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.custodia.org/default.asp?id=1882|title=Acre|website=www.custodia.org|access-date=2016-05-18}}</ref>

目前的教堂是由方济各会建于1737年。

'''圣弥额尔圣福里安圣殿主教座堂'''（{{lang-pl|Bazylika katedralna Świętych Michała i Floriana w Warszawie}}）是[[天主教华沙-布拉格教区|天主教华沙-布拉格教区]]的[[主教座堂|主教座堂]]，位於[[波蘭|波蘭]]首都[[華沙|華沙]]東部的{{link-en|布拉格 (華沙)|Praga|布拉格區}}。教堂的尖塔高達75米。聖弗洛里安主教座堂也是波蘭反抗[[俄羅斯|俄羅斯]]統治的象徵之一<ref name=church>{{pl icon}} [http://katedra-floriana.home.pl/cms/historia/budowa-i-konsekracja Cathedral of St. Michael the Archangel and St. Florian the Martyr website] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110314002637/http://katedra-floriana.home.pl/cms/historia/budowa-i-konsekracja |date=2011-03-14 }} Retrieved March 29, 2011</ref><ref>[http://www.warsawtour.pl/en/tourist-attractions/cathedral-of-st-michael-the-archangel-and-florian-the-martyr-katedra-sw-michala- Official Tourist Website of Warsaw] {{Wayback|url=http://www.warsawtour.pl/en/tourist-attractions/cathedral-of-st-michael-the-archangel-and-florian-the-martyr-katedra-sw-michala- |date=20150118173742 }}, Warsaw Tourist Office. Retrieved March 30, 2011.</ref>。[[華沙起義|華沙起義]]時教堂被德國人摧毀<ref>[http://www.warsawuprising.com/faq.htm Warsaw Uprising 1944], WarsawUprising.com, developed and maintained by Project InPosterum. Retrieved March 30, 2011.</ref>，但在戰後教堂得到重建。1972年教堂重新開放。

'''圣公会圣安德烈堂'''（St. Andrew's Episcopal Church）是[[查尔斯顿_(南卡罗来纳州)|查尔斯顿 (南卡罗来纳州)]]的一座古老教堂<ref name = nrhpinv>{{Cite web | last = Bull | first = Elias B. | coauthors = | title = Saint Andrews Episcopal Church | work = National Register of Historic Places - Nomination and Inventory | date = 1973-07-24 | url = http://www.nationalregister.sc.gov/charleston/S10817710061/S10817710061.pdf | format = pdf | accessdate = 2012-06-23}}</ref><ref name = scdah>{{Cite web
| title = Saint Andrews Episcopal Church, Charleston County (S.C. Hwy. 61, Charleston vicinity) | work = National Register Properties in South Carolina | publisher = South Carolina Department of Archives and History | url = http://www.nationalregister.sc.gov/charleston/S10817710061/index.htm | accessdate = 2012-06-23}}</ref>。

它建于1706年，是南卡罗来纳州现存最古老的教堂<ref>http://www.oldstandrews.org/ (9/7/10)</ref>。1973年列入[[国家史迹名录|国家史迹名录]]<ref name = nris/>。

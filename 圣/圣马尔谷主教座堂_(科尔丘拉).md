'''科尔丘拉圣马尔谷主教座堂''' <ref>[http://www.gcatholic.org/churches/europe/4036.htm Cathedral of St. Mark in Korčula]</ref><ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.ve/books?id=5PSBrPoGf-8C&pg=PA240&dq=Kor%25C4%258Dula+cathedral&hl=es&sa=X&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=Kor%2525C4%25258Dula%252520cathedral&f=false|title=Western Balkans|last=McAdam|first=Marika|date=2009-01-01|publisher=Lonely Planet|isbn=9781741047295|language=en}}</ref> ({{lang-hr|Katedrala sv. Marka}})是[[克罗地亚|克罗地亚]][[科尔丘拉|科尔丘拉]]的一座[[罗马天主教|罗马天主教]]教堂<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=MAxGSGHvN1AC|title=Croatia|last=McKelvie|first=Robin|last2=McKelvie|first2=Jenny|date=2006-01-01|publisher=New Holland Publishers|isbn=9781845370626|language=en}}</ref>，位于半岛顶端。

该堂建于15世纪到16世纪中叶。祭台画为丁托列托的作品。洗礼堂内装饰有耶稣青铜雕像，为克罗地亚雕塑家 Frane Krsinica的作品<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mediterano.hr/c/korcula_island/korcula/general_information/?ln=hr|title=Grad Korcula - otok Korčula - opće informacije|website=www.mediterano.hr|access-date=2016-08-25}}</ref>。

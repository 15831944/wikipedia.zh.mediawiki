'''圣约翰街'''（St John's Street）是[[英格兰|英格兰]][[剑桥|剑桥]]市中心的一条历史街道<ref>[http://www.cambridge.gov.uk/public/pdfs/St%20Johns%20Street.pdf St John's Street] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110927072948/http://www.cambridge.gov.uk/public/pdfs/St%20Johns%20Street.pdf |date=2011-09-27 }}, [[Cambridge_City_Council|Cambridge City Council]].</ref><ref>[http://www.cambridgeonline.co.uk/streets/St_Johns_Street/ St John's Street] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100118123556/http://www.cambridgeonline.co.uk/streets/St_Johns_Street |date=2010-01-18 }}, [http://www.cambridgeonline.co.uk/ Cambridge Online].</ref>北连桥街、圆形教堂街和西德尼街，南接[[三一街|三一街]]、[[国王街|国王街]]以及[[特兰平顿街|特兰平顿街]]。

[[剑桥大学圣约翰学院|圣约翰学院]]位于街道西侧，因而得名。其大门和礼拜堂钟楼颇雄伟。

'''圣母圣殿''' <ref>[http://www.gcatholic.org/churches/europe/0504.htm Basilica of Our Lady (Former Cathedral)]</ref> ({{lang-fr|Basilique de Notre-Dame de Tongre}} {{lang-nl|Onze-Lieve-Vrouwe Basiliek}})又名'''通赫伦旧主教座堂''' 是[[比利时|比利时]]<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.ve/books?id=FtlMAgAAQBAJ&pg=RA4-PA355&dq=Onze-Lieve-Vrouwebasiliek+(Tongeren)&hl=es&sa=X&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=Onze-Lieve-Vrouwebasiliek%252520(Tongeren)&f=false|title=The Grove Encyclopedia of Medieval Art and Architecture|last=Hourihane|first=Colum|date=2012-12-06|publisher=OUP USA|isbn=9780195395365|language=en}}</ref>[[通赫伦|通赫伦]]<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.co.ve/books?id=nS5AllNl3P8C&pg=PA81&dq=Onze-Lieve-Vrouwebasiliek+(Tongeren)&hl=es&sa=X&redir_esc=y|title=Vlaanderen : Limburg - Antwerpen - Oost-Vlaanderen - West-Vlaanderen - Brussel en Vlaams-Brabant|date=2005-01-01|publisher=ANWB Media - Boeken & Gidsen|isbn=9789018019464|language=nl}}</ref>主要的天主教堂，以及原通赫伦教区的主教座堂。

该堂为[[哥特式|哥特式]]，建于13世纪。

目前的钟楼高64米，建于1442-1541年<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.basiliektongeren.be/|title=Archeologische Opgravingen - Basiliek van Tongeren|website=www.basiliektongeren.be|access-date=2016-09-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160819020039/http://www.basiliektongeren.be/|archive-date=2016-08-19|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。

堂内的圣母像可追溯到1475年。

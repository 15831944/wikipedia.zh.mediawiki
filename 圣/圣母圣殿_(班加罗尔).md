'''圣母圣殿'''（St. Mary's Basilica）是一座[[罗马天主教|罗马天主教]][[宗座圣殿|宗座圣殿]]，位于[[印度|印度]][[卡纳塔克邦|卡纳塔克邦]][[班加罗尔|班加罗尔]]。该堂创建于17世纪，是班加罗尔最古老的教堂之一。该堂在1973年被教宗[[保禄六世|保禄六世]]升格为[[宗座圣殿|宗座圣殿]]，这是卡纳塔克邦的第一座宗座圣殿<ref name="Chandramouli">{{cite news|url=http://www.thehindu.com/thehindu/mp/2002/08/29/stories/2002082900270200.htm|title=Home to all faiths|date=2002-08-29|publisher=The Hindu|issue=Bangalore|last1=Chandramouli|first1=K|accessdate=2015-01-07}}</ref><ref name="book">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=zzWFvPc2xP0C&pg=PA15&lpg=PA15&dq=%22st+mary's+basilica%22+%22is+the+only+church%22&source=web&ots=-KffEuXi6N&sig=8XVOVxkNmbgdWomBYdnBm2Mtma4#PPA15,M1|title=Bangalore:An Expat Survival Guide|publisher=Chillibreeze publication|author=Preethi Sharma and Nishi Vishwanathan|accessdate=2007-08-15}}</ref><ref name=Smriti>{{cite book|last1=Srinivas|first1=Smriti|title=Landscapes of Urban Memory: The Sacred and the Civic in India's High-Tech City|date=2001|publisher=University of Minnesota Press|location=Minneapolis|isbn=1452904898|page=18|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=RmOvKsagbAgC&pg=PA18&dq=cantonment+market,+bangalore&hl=en&sa=X&ei=Fqu8VIPdCoTh8AXK8YHYCA&ved=0CCoQ6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=cantonment%20market%2C%20bangalore&f=false|accessdate=2015-01-19}}</ref>，也是印度第六座宗座圣殿。

该堂以每年8月30日至9月7日举行的九日敬禮（Novena）活动而闻名，以英语、卡纳达克语和泰米尔语提供弥撒，吸引了整个班加罗尔地区的教友。

==建筑==
[[File:Mass,_St_Mary's_Basilica_Bangalore.jpg|right]]
该堂为哥特式风格，拥有高高的尖顶，和美丽的[[花窗玻璃|花窗玻璃]]<ref name="hist4">{{cite news|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2005-10-16/bangalore/27838029_1_references-church-blessing|author=Anita Rao Kashi|title=Blessing faithful, soothing the eye|publisher=Times Internet Limited|work=Online Edition of The Times of India, dated 2005-10-16|accessdate=2007-08-16|date=2005-10-16}}</ref>。这些花窗玻璃在二战期间曾被拆卸，1947年恢复。

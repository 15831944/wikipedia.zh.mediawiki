'''圣基尔达岛鹪鹩'''([[学名|学名]]:''Troglodytes troglodytes hirtensis'')是[[苏格兰|苏格兰]][[圣基尔达岛|圣基尔达岛]]特有的一种[[鹪鹩|鹪鹩]][[亚种|亚种]]。它们在[[苏格兰盖尔语|苏格兰盖尔语]]中被称为“Dreathan-donn”("Dhra-in-doun"<ref>{{cite journal|title=The Ornithology of St. Kilda|author=Dixon, Charles| doi=10.1111/j.1474-919X.1885.tb06236.x| journal=Ibis| volume=27| issue=1|year=1885| pages=69–97}}</ref>)。<ref name=wi>[http://www.thewesternisles.co.uk/birds/st-kilda-wren.htm The Western Isles: St Kilda Wren] {{Wayback|url=http://www.thewesternisles.co.uk/birds/st-kilda-wren.htm |date=20080720111201 }}</ref>

==描述==
圣基尔达岛鹪鹩与内陆的鹪鹩最明显的差异在于，圣基尔达岛鹪鹩体型比他们更大。除此之外，圣基尔达岛鹪鹩的羽毛颜色更灰，红褐色较少。而跟分布在其它苏格兰岛屿上的亚种相比，它们的鸟喙更长更强壮，它们的羽毛颜色更灰和灰白。<ref>McGowan, R.Y.; Clugston, D.L.; & Forrester, R.W. (2003). Scotland's endemic subspecies. ''Scottish Birds'' '''24''': 18-35.</ref>

==现状==
圣基尔达岛鹪鹩现时在圣基尔达岛非常常见，2002年的统计显示岛上共有230对育种对。<ref name=sknr>[http://www.eoearth.org/article/St_Kilda_(Hirta)_National_Nature_Reserve,_United_Kingdom#Fauna St Kilda National Nature Reserve: Fauna]</ref>

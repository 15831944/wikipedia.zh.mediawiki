'''交尾刺'''（{{lang|en|copulatory spicule}}）是雄性[[线虫动物门|線蟲]]的交配器官之一。<ref name="Cheng86">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=d4GQlYzode8C&pg=PA479&dq|title=General Parasitology|last=Cheng|first=Thomas C.|date=1986|publisher=Elsevier Science|isbn=0323140106|edition=2nd|location=Oxford|page=479}}</ref>

雄性線蟲擁有一至二個交尾刺，能夠打開雌性線蟲的陰戶，協助雄蟲的授精，而非直接利用交尾刺授精。<ref name="Nematode Behaviour">{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=gr10r8veZ9gC&pg=PA136&dq=|title=Nematode Behaviour|last=Gaugler|first=edited by Randy|last2=Bilgrami|first2=Anwar L.|date=2004|publisher=CABI|isbn=0851999557|location=Wallingford|pages=136–138}}</ref><ref>Chitwood, B. G. & Chitwood, M. B. (1950). Introduction to Nematology (Vol. 1). Baltimore: Monumental Printing Co.{{Doi|10.5962/bhl.title.7355}} (Function of spicule: pages 123-124)</ref> 在授精過程中，雄性線蟲會依賴另一個稱作{{tsl|en|Gubernaculum (nematode anatomy)|引帶}}的性器官來引導交尾刺。

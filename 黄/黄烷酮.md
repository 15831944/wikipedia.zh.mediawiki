'''黄烷酮'''类（{{lang-en|flavanones}}）是[[黄酮类化合物|黄酮类化合物]]中的一类，属于[[芳香性|芳香]][[酮|酮]]，通常为无色，以[[糖苷|糖苷]]形式存在于植物中<ref>{{cite web|title=flavanone|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/flavanone|website=Merriam-Webster|accessdate=17 July 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite|title=Webster's New World College Dictionary|date=2014|publisher=Houghton Mifflin Harcourt Publishing Company|edition=Fifth|chapter-url=http://www.yourdictionary.com/flavanone|accessdate=17 July 2017|chapter=flavanones|via=YourDictionary.com}}</ref>。
常见的黄烷酮苷类有：
*[[柚皮苷|柚皮苷]]
*[[新橙皮苷|新橙皮苷]]

==代谢==
黄烷酮可由[[查耳酮异构酶|查耳酮异构酶]]催化[[查耳酮|查耳酮]]样化合物合成。[[黄烷酮-4-还原酶|黄烷酮-4-还原酶]]可将 (2''S'')-[[黄烷-4-醇|黄烷-4-醇]] 和 [[NADP|NADP]]<sup>+</sup>转化为 (2''S'')-黄烷酮、[[NADPH|NADPH]]和 H<sup>+</sup>。此外，还有很多途径可以对映选择性地生成黄烷酮及相关化合物。<ref>{{cite journal | pmid = 22876166 | year = 2012 | last1 = Nibbs | first1 = AE | last2 = Scheidt | first2 = KA | title = Asymmetric Methods for the Synthesis of Flavanones, Chromanones, and Azaflavanones | volume = 2012 | issue = 3 | pages = 449–462 | doi = 10.1002/ejoc.201101228 | pmc = 3412359 | journal = European Journal of Organic Chemistry}}</ref>

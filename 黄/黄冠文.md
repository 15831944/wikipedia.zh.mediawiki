'''黄冠文'''，[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[雪兰莪|雪兰莪]][[雪兰莪州议会|州议会]][[新古毛|新古毛]]议员，[[国阵|国阵]][[马华公会|马华公会]]籍<ref>''[http://www.selangor.gov.my/en/main.php?Content=vertsections&SubVertSectionID=139&VertSectionID=3&CurLocation=3&IID=%20&Page=1 Executive Committee Member of Selangor State Government] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110629140231/http://www.selangor.gov.my/en/main.php?Content=vertsections&SubVertSectionID=139&VertSectionID=3&CurLocation=3&IID=&Page=1 |date=2011-06-29 }}''.

'''黄烷'''（{{lang-en|flavans}}）是一种[[苯并吡喃|苯并吡喃]]衍生物，是植物中许多[[黄酮类化合物|黄酮类化合物]]的骨架结构，包括[[黄烷-3-醇|黄烷-3-醇]]、[[黄烷-4-醇|黄烷-4-醇]]和黄烷-3,4-二醇（[[无色花色素|无色花色素]]）。<ref>Proanthocyanidin glycosides and related polyphenols from cacao liquor and their antioxidant effects. Tsutomu Hatano, Haruka Miyatake, Midori Natsume, Naomi Osakabe, Toshio Takizawa, Hideyuki Ito and Takashi Yoshida, Phytochemistry, April 2002, Volume 59, Issue 7, Pages 749–758, {{doi|10.1016/S0031-9422(02)00051-1}}</ref>

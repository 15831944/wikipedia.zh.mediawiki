'''楔状軟骨'''（cuneiform cartilages；cunei- 源於拉丁語：cunei(楔形)；亦稱為{{le|海因里希·奧古斯特·里斯伯格|Heinrich August Wrisberg|里斯伯格}}軟骨、楔形軟骨；楔 (cuneiform)/xiē（ㄒㄧㄝˉ））在人類[[喉|喉]]部中是兩個小而細長的黃色彈性[[軟骨|軟骨]]片，位於[[杓狀會厭襞|杓狀會厭襞]]的兩側。

楔状軟骨是配對的軟骨，坐於[[杓狀軟骨|杓狀軟骨]]之上並與之一起移動。<ref>{{Cite book| last1 = Rosen | first1 = Clarke A.| last2 = Simpson | first2 = Blake| title = Operative Techniques in Laryngology| year  = 2008 | publisher = Springer| url   = http://www.springerlink.com/content/978-3-540-68107-6 | accessdate = November 2013| ref   = harv}}</ref>且位於[[小角軟骨|小角軟骨]]的前上方，並且這兩對軟骨（楔状軟骨及小角軟骨）的存在導致[[黏膜|黏膜]]表面上的小凸起。<ref name="Seikel-etal-2010">{{Cite book| last1 = Seikel | first1 = J. Anthony| last2 = King | first2 = Douglas W.| last3 = Drumright | first3 = David G.| title = Anatomy & Physiology for Speech, Language, and Hearing | edition = 4th| year  = 2010 | location = Delmar, NY | publisher = Cengage Learning| isbn  = 1-4283-1223-4 | ref = harv}}</ref><!-- p=183 -->楔状軟骨由[[杓狀會厭襞|杓狀會厭襞]]所覆蓋，楔状軟骨形成[[喉入口|喉入口]]的側面，而小角軟骨形成後側面，[[會厭軟骨|會厭軟骨]]位於前面。<ref>{{Cite book| title     = The Essentials of Respiratory Care | page = 77| publisher = Elsevier Health Sciences| url       = https://books.google.com/books?id=RZJ5AAAAQBAJ&pg=PA77 | accessdate = November 2013| isbn      = 9780323027007 | ref = harv}}</ref>

楔状軟骨的功能是支持聲帶和會厭軟骨的側面。他們還為嵌入的褶皺襞帶提供了一定程度的堅實性。<ref name="Seikel-etal-2010" />

《'''去爬山的綜藝-山頂會談'''》（簡稱《'''山頂會談'''》，{{kor|k='''정상회담'''}}，{{lang-en|'''Going to the Mountains Show: Summit Conference'''}}）為[[韓國|韓國]][[E_Channel|E Channel]]綜藝節目<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mbn.mk.co.kr/pages/news/newsView.php?news_seq_no=3479704|title=[MK현장]`진짜` 산으로 간 예능 `정상회담`, 재미·감동 다 잡을까|publisher=MBN News|accessdate=2018-03-16|date=2018-03-15|language=ko}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://sports.khan.co.kr/sports/sk_index.html?art_id=201803151453003&sec_id=540201|title=[스경X현장] 산으로 가는 예능 ‘정상회담’, 등산예능 새 장르 지평 열까|publisher=sports khan|accessdate=2018-03-16|date=2018-03-15|language=ko}}</ref>，由[[盧弘喆|盧弘喆]]、[[金旻鐘|金旻鐘]]、{{link-ko|金甫城|김보성 (배우)}}、[[趙優鐘|趙優鐘]]、[[智珉|智珉]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.allkpop.com/article/2018/03/jimin-says-climbing-up-a-mountain-feels-similar-to-her-aoa-career|title=Jimin says climbing up a mountain feels similar to her AOA career?|publisher=allkpop|accessdate=2018-04-30|date=2018-03-24|language=en-US}}</ref>等人共同主持<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sports.chosun.com/news/news.htm?id=201803160100120760008697&ServiceDate=20180315|title=[SC현장]"연장+해외촬영 노린다"…등산 예능 '정상회담' 대박 가즈아(종합)|publisher=sports chosun|accessdate=2018-03-16|date=2018-03-15|language=ko}}</ref>，節目主軸為向大眾公開「山」的面貌，並展示「山」的魅力，同時也紀錄爬山過程中成員的互動、點滴與故事。

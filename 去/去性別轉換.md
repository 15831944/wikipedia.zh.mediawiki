在[[跨性別|跨性別]][[性別轉換|性別轉換]]之后，'''去性別轉換'''（{{lang-en|Detransition}}）是将一个人的[[性别表達|性别表達]]或[[性别特征|性别特征]]改变为符合一个人出生時的[[性别指定|性别指定]]的过程。<ref>{{cite web|title=Detransitioning: Going From Male To Female To Male Again|url=http://www.vocativ.com/culture/lgbt/detransitioning-male-female-male-again/|website=Vocativ|accessdate=1 September 2017|date=15 June 2015}}</ref>去[[性別轉換|性別轉換]]也称为性別重新转换。<ref>{{cite web|title=Transitioning Back To One’s Assigned Sex At Birth|url=http://transadvocate.com/transitioning-back-to-ones-assigned-sex-at-birth_n_9946.htm|website=The TransAdvocate|accessdate=1 September 2017|language=en|date=7 August 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170920093038/http://transadvocate.com/transitioning-back-to-ones-assigned-sex-at-birth_n_9946.htm|archive-date=2017-09-20|dead-url=yes}}</ref>

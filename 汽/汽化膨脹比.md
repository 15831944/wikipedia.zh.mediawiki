[[液化|液化]]或{{le|低溫|cryogenic}}物質的'''膨脹比'''是指在一定量物質在其液體時[[体积|体积]]，和其在[[室溫|室溫]]、一大氣壓力下，氣體時体积的比<ref>{{cite book | title = Emergency Characterization of Unknown Materials | author = Rick Houghton | publisher = CRC Press | year = 2007 | isbn = 0-8493-7968-7 | url = http://books.google.com/books?id=GA_0smWGxwwC&pg=PA22&dq=liquid+gas+%22Expansion+ratio%22&lr=&as_brr=0&ei=u5AHSYX-ApmctAPK_-X_AQ }}</ref>。

若封閉容器中裝有液化物質，而有相當多的液體[[蒸發|蒸發]]，所產生的壓力可能會破壞[[压力容器|压力容器]]。因此需使用{{le|洩壓閥|pressure relief valve}}及{{le|排氣閥|vent valves}}<ref>[http://www.airproducts.com/nr/rdonlyres/38000ecf-e07b-4288-8ff9-73699c70c882/0/safetygram27.pdf Safetygram-27 Cryogenic Liquid Containers] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081207051502/http://www.airproducts.com/NR/rdonlyres/38000ECF-E07B-4288-8FF9-73699C70C882/0/safetygram27.pdf |date=2008-12-07 }}</ref>。

液化或低溫物質從其沸點到室溫下的膨脹比為：
*[[液氮|液氮]] 1:696
*[[液氦|液氦]] 1:757
*[[氩|氩]] 1:847
*[[液氢|液氢]] 1:851
*[[液氧|液氧]] 1:860
*[[氖|氖]]的膨脹比最高，有1:1445<ref name="Association2012">{{cite book|others=Compressed Gas Association|title=Handbook of Compressed Gases|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=5EfhBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA82|accessdate=8 March 2016|date=2012-12-06|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=9781461306733|page=82}}</ref><ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.lindecanada.com/en/aboutboc/safety/cryogenic_liquids/characteristics.php| archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20120218125124/http://www.lindecanada.com/en/aboutboc/safety/cryogenic_liquids/characteristics.php |archive-date = 2012-02-18| title = Characteristics| publisher = The Linde Group}}</ref>。

'''什科茨扬''' ({{IPA-sl|ˈʃkɔːtsjan|pron}}; {{lang-it|San Canziano}})是一座位于[[斯洛維尼亞沿海地區|斯洛維尼亞沿海地區]]的小型村落。村落附近的[[什科茨揚洞群|什科茨揚洞群]]由其命名，是一座世界遗产<ref>[http://www.divaca.si/index.php Divača municipal site] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090302002649/http://www.divaca.si/index.php |date=2009-03-02 }}</ref>

The local church is dedicated to {{tsl|en|Cantius, Cantianius, and Cantianella||Saint Cantianius}} and belongs to the [[堂区|堂区]] of {{tsl|en|Rodik||Rodik}}.<ref>[http://kp.rkc.si/dokumenti/zupnije/seznam.pdf Roman Catholic Diocese of Koper List of Churches May 2008] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090306055057/http://kp.rkc.si/dokumenti/zupnije/seznam.pdf |date=2009-03-06 }}</ref>

'''什切青港'''是[[波蘭|波蘭]]城市[[什切青|什切青]]的深水[[港口|港口]]，地處[[波羅的海|波羅的海]]沿岸，靠近[[什切青潟湖|什切青潟湖]]。2006年，什切青港的貨物處理量達9,965,000噸，佔波蘭港口總貨物處理量的16.5%<ref name="gus07">{{cite book|title=Statistical yearbook of maritime economy|publisher=Central Statistical Office - Statistical Office in Szczecin|location=Warsaw, Szczecin|year=2007|pages=65, 86|issn=0867-082X|url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/cps/rde/xbcr/gus/PUBL_rocznik_gospodarki_morskiej_2007.pdf|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090408000937/http://www.stat.gov.pl/cps/rde/xbcr/gus/PUBL_rocznik_gospodarki_morskiej_2007.pdf|archivedate=2009-04-08|df=}}</ref>。

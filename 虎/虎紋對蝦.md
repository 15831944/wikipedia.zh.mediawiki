'''虎纹对虾'''（学名：''Penaeus esculentus''），又名'''褐虎对虾'''，是[[对虾属|对虾属]]下的一种虾，原产于并大规模养殖于[[澳大利亚|澳大利亚]]附近海域。<ref>{{cite book |url=ftp://ftp.fao.org/docrep/fao/009/ac477e/AC477E09.pdf |chapter=''Penaeus (Penaeus) esculentus'' Haswell, 1879 |title=FAO species catalogue. Vol. 1. Shrimps and prawns of the world |publisher=[[Food_and_Agriculture_Organization|Food and Agriculture Organization]] |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]] |author=[[Lipke_Holthuis|Lipke B. Holthuis]] |year=1980 |isbn=92-5-100896-5}}</ref>

==形态==
幼年个体生活在海草从中<ref>{{cite book |title=The biology of the Penaeidae |series=Advances in marine biology |author=W. Dall |publisher=[[Academic_Press|Academic Press]] |year=1990 |isbn=978-0-12-026127-7 |pages=489}}</ref>，在头胸甲长度达到{{convert|32|mm}}左右时性成熟<ref>{{cite journal |title=Reproductive dynamics of the tiger prawn ''Penaeus esculentus'', and a comparison with ''P. semisulcatus'', in the north-western Gulf of Carpentaria, Australia |author=P. J. Crocos |journal=[[Australian_Journal_of_Marine_and_Freshwater_Research|Australian Journal of Marine and Freshwater Research]] |volume=38 |issue=1 |pages=91–102 |doi=10.1071/MF9870091 |url=http://www.publish.csiro.au/paper/MF9870091.htm}}</ref>。成年个体长度可达{{convert|155|mm}}<ref name="SLB">{{cite web |url=http://www.sealifebase.org/summary/SpeciesSummary.php?id=25352 |publisher=SeaLifeBase |title=''Penaeus esculentus'', brown tiger prawn |date=25 February 2009}}</ref>，与[[草虾|草虾]]十分相似，但个头要小一些，颜色也要更加发褐一些<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.shrimpnews.com/FarmedSpecies.html |title=Farmed species |publisher=Shrimp News International |accessdate=9 January 2010 |archivedate=25 June 2008 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080625023917/http://www.shrimpnews.com/FarmedSpecies.html}}</ref>。它们一般生活在水下{{convert|200|m}}以上。<ref>{{cite web |title=Species ''Penaeus (Penaeus) esculentus'' Haswell, 1879 |work=[[Australian_Faunal_Directory|Australian Faunal Directory]] |url=http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/abrs/online-resources/fauna/afd/taxa/Penaeus_%28Penaeus%29_esculentus |publisher=[[Department_of_the_Environment,_Water,_Heritage_and_the_Arts|Department of the Environment, Water, Heritage and the Arts]] |date=21 October 2008 |access-date=2018-10-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111230044922/http://www.environment.gov.au/biodiversity/abrs/online-resources/fauna/afd/taxa/Penaeus_%28Penaeus%29_esculentus# |archive-date=2011-12-30 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>

==分布==
虎纹对虾原产于澳大利亚东部和西部附近的温暖海域，东西两岸的虎纹对虾在形态上没有太大的差异。<ref>{{cite journal |author1=Robert D. Ward |author2=Jennifer R. Ovenden |author3=Jennifer R. S. Meadows |author4=Peter M. Grewe |author5=Sigrid A. Lehnert |title=Population genetic structure of the brown tiger prawn, ''Penaeus esculentus'', in tropical northern Australia |journal=Marine Biology|volume=148 |issue=3 |year=2006 |doi=10.1007/s00227-005-0099-x |pages=599–607}}</ref>

==养殖业==

虎纹对虾的年产量可达{{convert|500|t}}，可与草虾杂交<ref>{{cite journal |journal=Aquaculture |volume=133 |issue=2 |year=1995 |pages=103–111 |doi=10.1016/0044-8486(95)00013-R |title=Interspecific hybridization of the tiger prawns ''Penaeus monodon'' and ''Penaeus esculentus'' |author1=J. A. H. Benzie |author2=M. Kenway |author3=E. Ballment |author4=S. Frusher |author5=L. Trott }}</ref>，而本身的价格比生长更为迅速的草虾要高。<ref>{{cite journal |title=Commercial grow-out performance and cost-benefit analysis for farm production of the brown tiger shrimp ''Penaeus esculentus'' |author=Sandy J. Keys, Peter J. Crocos & Oscar J. Cacho |doi=10.1080/13657300409380371 |journal=Aquaculture Economics & Management |volume=8 |issue=5/6 |year=2004 |pages=295–308}}</ref>

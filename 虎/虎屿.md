'''虎屿'''（{{lang-ms|Pulau Rimau}}，{{lang-en|Rimau Island}}）为[[马来西亚|马来西亚]][[槟城州|槟城州]][[槟岛|槟岛]]南部的一个[[无人岛|无人岛]]，北临[[西南县|西南县]]县城[[峇六拜|峇六拜]]，毗邻[[马六甲海峡|马六甲海峡]]，距槟岛约{{Convert|832|meter|mile}}<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thestar.com.my/metro/views/2016/12/01/living-the-lighthouse-life-it-can-get-lonely-but-it-is-also-a-retreat-to-revive-the-body-and-soul/|title=Living the lighthouse life - Views|publisher=The Star Online|author=A.R. Amiruddin|language=en|access-date=2017-08-07}}</ref><ref name="nst">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nst.com.my/lifestyle/sunday-vibes/2017/08/264675/guardian-angels-ships|title=Guardian angels of ships|date=2017-08-06|work=NST Online|access-date=2017-08-07|language=en}}</ref><ref name="unc">{{Cite web|url=http://www.unc.edu/~rowlett/lighthouse/myw2.htm|title=Lighthouses of Malaysia: West Malaysia West Coast|website=www.unc.edu|access-date=2017-08-07}}</ref>。该岛有一座高{{Convert|17|metre|feet}}的白色[[铸铁|铸铁]]灯塔，于1885年由[[大英帝国|大英帝国]]建造，至今灯器仍在营运，每10秒闪两次白光，为由南驶入[[槟威海峡|槟威海峡]]的船只指引方向。<ref name="nst"/><ref name="unc"/>

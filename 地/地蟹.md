'''地蟹'''指的是生活在陸地上[[螃蟹|螃蟹]]，包含[[地蟹科|地蟹科]]、[[澤蟹科|澤蟹科]]和一些其他科下的屬（例如[[相手蟹屬|相手蟹屬]]）下一系列不同的蟹，<ref name="Hartnoll">{{cite book |editor=Warren W. Burggren & Brian Robert McMahon |year=1988 |title=Evolution, systematics, and geographical distribution |publisher=[[Cambridge_University_Press|Cambridge University Press]] |isbn=978-0-521-30690-4 |author=Richard G. Hartnoll |chapter=Biology of the land crabs: an introduction |pages=6–54}}</ref> 不過這個詞通常指的都是地蟹科的生物。<ref name="Burggren"/>

==習性==
在水生、半陸生、陸生蟹之間其實沒有明確的區分，<ref name="Burggren">{{cite book |editor=Warren W. Burggren & Brian Robert McMahon |year=1988 |title=Biology of the Land Crabs |publisher=[[Cambridge_University_Press|Cambridge University Press]] |isbn=978-0-521-30690-4 |author=Warren W. Burggren & Brian Robert McMahon |chapter=Biology of the land crabs: an introduction |pages=1–5}}</ref> 大多數陸生蟹仍然會回到水中產卵。<ref name="Little">{{cite book |author=Colin Little |year=1990 |title=The Terrestrial Invasion: an Ecophysiological Approach to the Origins of Land Animals |series=Cambridge studies in ecology |publisher=[[Cambridge_University_Press|Cambridge University Press]] |isbn=978-0-521-33669-7 |chapter=Life on land |pages=201–275}}</ref> 此外地蟹中既包含一些[[淡水蟹|淡水蟹]]也包含一些海生蟹。<ref name="Dobson">{{cite journal |author=Michael Dobson |year=2004 |title=Freshwater crabs in Africa |journal=[[Freshwater_Forum|Freshwater Forum]] |volume=21 |pages=3–26 |url=https://www.fba.org.uk/journals/index.php/FF/article/view/175/75 |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]]}}</ref> 一些地蟹儘管生活在離海洋數千米遠之地，但仍然每年會遷徙到海邊產卵。<ref name="Little"/> 例如[[聖誕島紅蟹|聖誕島紅蟹]]每年都會隨著印度洋季風大規模集體遷徙，每日行進{{convert|1.46|km}}。<ref>{{cite book |author=Ben Hoare |year=2009 |title=Animal Migration: Remarkable Journeys in the Wild |publisher=[[University_of_California_Press|University of California Press]] |isbn=978-0-520-25823-5 |chapter=Red crab |pages=74–75}}</ref>

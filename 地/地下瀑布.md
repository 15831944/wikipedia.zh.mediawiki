'''地下瀑布'''（{{lang|en|subterranean waterfall}}）是一種地下生成的[[瀑布|瀑布]]，常見於[[洞穴|洞穴]]或[[礦坑|礦坑]]。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.worldreviewer.com/info/10-most-incredible-cave-waterfalls-on-earth.html|title=10 Most Incredible Cave Waterfalls on Earth|accessdate=2016-04-04|language=en|work=World Reviewer}}</ref> 地下瀑布是洞穴系統中的常見地貌，水流沿著有相當高度的洞穴結構垂直或近乎垂直地流瀉而下。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=KOnHiX7xIKIC&pg=PA32|title=Waterfalls of Virginia and West Virginia: A Hiking and Photography Guide|author=Kevin Adams|date=2002-06-01|publisher=Menasha Ridge Press|isbn=978-0-89732-414-4|page=32|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=Caves and Karst of the Yorkshire Dales|first1=Tony|last2=Murphy|first2=Phil|date=2013|publisher=British Cave Research Association|isbn=978-0900265-46-4|editor1-first=Tony|editor2-last=Lowe|editor2-first=Dave|location=Buxton|page=136|last1=Waltham|editor1-last=Waltham|language=en}}</ref> 全世界最高的地下瀑布位於[[斯洛維尼亞|斯洛維尼亞]]，瀑布高達{{Convert|400|m}}。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wondermondo.com/Countries/E/Slovenia/SlovenianLittoral/Vrtiglavica.htm|title=Vrtiglavica Cave and Waterfall|accessdate=2017-04-04|last1=Pavils|first1=Gatis|website=Wondermondo|language=en}}</ref>

'''地蝦目'''（[[學名|學名]]Bathynellacea）是一[[目|目]]生活在[[地下水|地下水]]間隙的[[甲殼類|甲殼類]]。<ref name="Lowry">{{cite web|url=http://crustacea.net/crustace/www/bathynel.htm|author=J. K. Lowry|accessdate=2009-11-26|work=Crustacea, the Higher Taxa: Description, Identification, and Information Retrieval|title=Bathynellacea (Malacostraca)}}</ref>一些[[物種|物種]]可以忍受低[[鹽|鹽]][[濃度|濃度]]，且最少有一[[非洲|非洲]]物種是嗜熱的，能夠抵受達55℃。<ref name="jrank"/>地蝦目是微小及瞎眼的，腳很短及脆弱<ref name="EB">{{cite web|url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/359445/malacostracan/33792/Annotated-classification#|title=Crustacean|work=Encyclopædia Britannica|accessdate=2009-11-26}}</ref>，最長只有3.4毫米。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mdfrc.org.au/bugguide/display.asp?class=18&subclass=33&order=29&Couplet=0&Type=3|title=Bathynellacea (bathynellaceans)|work=Identification and Ecology of Australian Freshwater Invertebrates|author=|accessdate=2009-11-26|publisher=Murray Darling Freshwater Research Centre}}</ref>除了[[南極洲|南極洲]]及一些[[島嶼|島嶼]]（包括[[斐濟|斐濟]]、[[新喀里多尼亞|新喀里多尼亞]]及[[加勒比地區|加勒比地區]]）外，在每個[[大洲|大洲]]都可以見到它們。<ref name="jrank">{{cite web|url=http://animals.jrank.org/pages/1766/Bathynellaceans-Bathynellacea.html|title=Bathynellacea|work=Animal Life Resource|publisher=Jrank|accessdate=2009-11-26}}</ref>

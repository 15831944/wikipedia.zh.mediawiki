'''地狱峡谷'''（{{lang-en|Hells Canyon}}）是条{{convert|10|mi|km}}宽的[[峡谷|峡谷]]，位于美国东[[俄勒冈州|俄勒冈州]]、东[[华盛顿州|华盛顿州]]和西[[爱达荷州|爱达荷州]]。它是{{tsl|en|Hells Canyon National Recreation Area|地狱峡谷休闲区}}的一部分，也是[[北美洲|北美洲]]最深的[[峡谷|河谷]]<ref name = "NFS Overview">{{cite web | title = Hells Canyon National Recreation Area: Hells Canyon Overview | publisher = U.S. Forest Service | url = http://www.fs.fed.us/hellscanyon/overview/index.shtml | accessdate = 2010-12-26 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101206120003/http://www.fs.fed.us/hellscanyon/overview/index.shtml |archivedate=2010-12-06 |deadurl=yes}}</ref>，在[[斯内克河|斯内克河]]的冲刷下其峡谷深达{{convert|7993|ft|m}}。<ref>{{cite web|title=Hells Canyon National Recreation Area: Establishment of HCNRA |publisher=U.S. Forest Service |url=http://www.fs.fed.us/hellscanyon/about_us/establishment.shtml |accessdate=2010-12-26 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101203161920/http://www.fs.fed.us/hellscanyon/about_us/establishment.shtml |archivedate=2010-12-03 }}</ref>

'''地理極點'''是[[行星|行星]]、[[天然衛星|衛星]]或其它較大天體的[[自轉軸|自轉軸]]與其表面交會的點。如在[[地球|地球]]上的[[北極|北極]]和[[南極|南極]]，其它天體的極也稱為南極和北極，分別會在該天體赤道兩側各90[[度|度]]之處。<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=6DhWw_cYLicC&pg=PA557&dq=geographical+pole&hl=en&sa=X&ei=_1GHVfLmAeea7gbu0IOQBg&ved=0CCsQ6AEwAg#v=onepage&q=geographical%20pole&f=false |title= Elsevier's Dictionary of Geography: in English, Russian, French, Spanish and German|last1= Kotlyakov| first1= Vladimir| last2= Komarova| first2= Anna| date=2006|page=557|accessdate=22 June 2015}}</ref>每一顆行星都有地理極點 。<ref>{{cite book|url= https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=KbEKqJc3u6MC&pg=PA224&lpg=PA224&dq=geographical+poles+on+other+planets&source=bl&ots=pDnyYT8Xx1&sig=ngTcmpyUswgDDcakf_6Xxo2Ql0s&hl=en&sa=X&ei=3GGIVYbSO8yxsAGFqaaICg&ved=0CEcQ6AEwBg#v=onepage&q=geographical%20poles%20on%20other%20planets&f=false|title= Aether and Gravitation|last1= Hooper| first1=William| page=224|date=2008|accessdate=22 June 2015}}</ref>

天體自轉的攝動意味著地理極點會在它的表面上稍稍的徘迴。例如，地球的北極和南極在數年的時間內會有幾米的移動。<ref>{{cite book|url= https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=_JOp5eDFtrsC&pg=PA106&dq=geographical+poles+move+slightly&hl=en&sa=X&ei=eGKIVarQC8u2swGO0amQAg&ved=0CEoQ6AEwBw#v=onepage&q=geographical%20poles%20move%20slightly&f=false| title= Wonderfully Weird World|last1=Schar |first1= Ray|page=106|date= 2010|accessdate=22 June 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Lovett |first=Richard A. |url=http://www.scientificamerican.com/article/climate-change-has-shifted-location-north-south-poles/ |title=Climate Change Has Shifted the Locations of Earth's North and South Poles |publisher=Scientificamerican.com |date=2013-05-14 |accessdate=2015-06-26}}</ref>當[[地圖學|製圖]]需要精確和不變的座標，{{cn|date = 2014 年 5 月}}地理極點的平均位置作為固定的製圖桿，成為物體大圓[[經度|經度]]的交會點。

如果，像[[地球磁場|地球]]，一個天體產生[[磁場|磁場]]，就會有[[天體#磁極|磁極]] 。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://discovermagazine.com/2014/dec/23-20-things-you-didnt-know-about-the-north-pole |title=20 Things You Didn't Know About... the North Pole |publisher=DiscoverMagazine.com |date=2014-11-18 |accessdate=2015-06-26}}</ref>

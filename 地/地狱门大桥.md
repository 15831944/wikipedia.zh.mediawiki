'''地狱门大桥'''（{{lang-en|Hell Gate Bridge}}）最初被称为'''纽约联络线铁路桥'''（New York Connecting Railroad Bridge）<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2000/03/19/nyregion/fyi-304220.html|title=F.Y.I.|last=Schneider|first=Daniel B.|date=2000-03-19|newspaper=The New York Times|accessdate=2011-01-23}}</ref> 或 '''东河拱桥'''（The East River Arch Bridge）<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1991/11/30/nyregion/long-unlucky-rail-bridge-hits-55-million-repair-jackpot.html|title=Long Unlucky, Rail Bridge Hits $55 Million Repair Jackpot|last=Gruson|first=Lindsey|date=1991-11-30|newspaper=The New York Times|accessdate=2011-01-23}}</ref>是[[纽约|纽约市]]的一座长{{Convert|1017|ft|m|0|adj=on}}<ref>The arch is {{Convert|1087.5|ft|m}} measured center to center of the concrete towers.</ref>的钢拱铁路桥。大桥跨越了[[東河_(紐約市)|东河]]的[[海峡|海峡]]“地狱之门”，连接了[[皇后區|皇后區]][[阿斯托里亞_(皇后區)|阿斯托里亞]]和[[曼哈頓|曼哈顿]]。

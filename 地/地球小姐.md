'''地球小姐'''（{{lang-en|'''Miss Earth'''}}）是一项年度的国际性[[选美|选美]]赛事，旨在提升人们的[[环保|环保]]意识<ref>{{cite news|title=Afghanistan: Anti-Pageant Judges|url=http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9B07E3DC1430F933A05753C1A9659C8B63&scp=1&sq=miss%20earth%20vida%20samadzai&st=cse|publisher=The New York Times Company|first=World News|last=New York Times|date=2003-10-30|accessdate=2009-01-03}}</ref><ref name=chinadaily.com.cn/english/doc/2004-10/25/content_385531.htm>{{cite news|title=Miss Earth 2004 beauty pageant|url=http://www.chinadaily.com.cn/english/doc/2004-10/25/content_385531.htm|publisher=China Daily|first=Reuters|last=News|date=2004-10-25|accessdate=2007-10-23}}</ref>，是世界{{Link-en|四大選美活動|Big Four international beauty pageants}}之一，其他比賽為[[世界小姐|世界小姐]]、[[环球小姐|环球小姐]]和[[国际小姐|国际小姐]]。其总部设在菲律宾。<br />
2006年，地球小姐与[[联合国环境规划署|联合国环境规划署]]的地球卫士奖项合作，每年为在环保方面做出杰出贡献的个人和政策官员奖励<ref>{{cite news|title=Mr. Eric Falt, UNEP Director of Communications and Alexandra Braun, Miss Earth 2005|url=http://www.unep.org/champions/Photo_Gallery/championssingapore/i_default.asp?Frame=Zoom&PicIndex=7|publisher=United Nations Environment Programme|first=Eric|last=Falt|date=2006-04-21|accessdate=2009-09-20|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080519233755/http://www.unep.org/champions/Photo_Gallery/championssingapore/i_default.asp?Frame=Zoom&PicIndex=7|archivedate=2008-05-19}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Champions of the Earth 2006 gala dinner|url=http://www.unep.org/champions/Photo_Gallery/championssingapore/i_default.asp?Frame=Zoom&PicIndex=13|publisher=United Nations Environment Programme|first=Eric|last=Falt|date=2006-04-21|accessdate=2009-09-20|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://webarchive.loc.gov/all/20071215123430/http://www.unep.org/champions/Photo_Gallery/championssingapore/i_default.asp?Frame=Zoom&PicIndex=13|archivedate=2007-12-15}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=2007 UNEP Champions of the Earth Awards Make Big Splash at Gala Ceremony in Singapore|url=http://www.unep.org/Documents.Multilingual/Default.asp?ArticleID=5567&DocumentID=504&l=en|publisher=United Nations Environment Programme|first=Nick|last=Nuttall|date=2007-04-19|accessdate=2009-09-20|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=http://webarchive.loc.gov/all/20160929042345/http%3A//www.unep.org/Documents.Multilingual/Default.asp?ArticleID%3D5567%26DocumentID%3D504%26l%3Den|archivedate=2016-09-29}}</ref>。
目前地球小姐賽事都在菲律賓當地舉行，只有2010年和2015年賽事在[[越南|越南]]和[[奧地利|奧地利]]舉辦。

==奖项设置==
===新头冠===
在第9届选美比赛中，地球小姐公布了其新的头冠，将由2009年世界小姐得主首次佩戴。这顶新头冠由100%的回收贵金属制造，包括14k黄金和白银。

===旧头冠===
2001年地球小姐头冠和2002年－2008年使用的[[施华洛世奇|施华洛世奇]]头冠均由菲律宾设计师Arnel Papa制作<ref>{{cite news|title=Miss Earth 2005 News Updates|url=http://www.missosology.org/missearth2005/missearth05news.html|publisher=The Inquirer|first=Giovanni Paulo J.|last=Yazon|date=2005-10-26|accessdate=2008-01-21|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080112072952/http://www.missosology.org/missearth2005/missearth05news.html|archivedate=2008-01-12}}</ref>。

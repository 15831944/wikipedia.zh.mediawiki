《'''地球末日'''》（[[英文|英文]]：Last Days on Earth）是由[[20/20_(节目)|20/20]]在[[美國廣播公司|美國廣播公司]]播出的科學特別[[節目|節目]]<ref>[http://abcnews.go.com/2020/story?id=2319986 Last Days on Earth] 20/20, ABC News, 2006-08-31</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://leadercall.com/columns/x1593269326/Last-days-on-earth-part-II?keyword=secondarystory |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2011-11-03 |0=2013-06-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130610085725/http://leadercall.com/columns/x1593269326/Last-days-on-earth-part-II?keyword=secondarystory |archivedate=2013-06-10 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://leadercall.com/columns/x1593269016/Last-days-on-earth/print |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2011-11-03 |0=2013-06-10 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130610085726/http://leadercall.com/columns/x1593269016/Last-days-on-earth/print |archivedate=2013-06-10 }}</ref>，並在2006年8月由[[歷史頻道|歷史頻道]]播出。<ref>{{cite news|last=Moon|first=Troy|title=End of the world still a big seller|url=http://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/pnj/access/1690812551.html?FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&type=current&date=Jan+05%2C+2009&author=Troy+Moon&pub=Pensacola+News+Journal&desc=End+of+the+world+still+a+big+seller&pqatl=google|accessdate=3 November 2011|newspaper=Pensacola News Journal|date=2009-01-05}}</ref>

該節目敘述的[[世界末日|世界末日]]有[[火山爆發|火山爆發]]、[[撞擊事件|小行星撞擊]]、[[流感|流感]]、[[氣候變遷|氣候變遷]]、[[伽瑪射線暴|伽瑪射線暴]]、[[核戰爭|核戰爭]]、Machine rule。主持的[[科學家|科學家]]有[[加来道雄|加来道雄]]、[[奈爾·德葛拉司·泰森|奈爾·德葛拉司·泰森]]、[[史蒂芬·霍金|史蒂芬·霍金]]、Kevin Warwick。在2007年，它獲得了[[艾美獎|艾美獎]]的提名。<ref>[http://www.emmyonline.org/mediacenter/_txt/news_28th_nominees_all.txt  Nominees for the News & Documentary Emmy Awards in 32 Categories Announced by NATAS] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071202205026/http://www.emmyonline.org/mediacenter/_txt/news_28th_nominees_all.txt |date=2007-12-02 }} Media Center, National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences</ref>

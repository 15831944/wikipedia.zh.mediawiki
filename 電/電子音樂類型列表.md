* [[氛围音乐|Ambien]]（氛圍音樂）

** [[Ambient#Organic_ambient|Organic ambient]]
** [[Ambient#Isolationist_ambient|Isolationist]]

** [[2Step|2Step]]，又稱[[Speed_garage|Speed garage]]
** [[Breakbeat_hardcore|Breakbeat hardcore]]
** [[Breakcore|Breakcore]]
** [[Brokenbeat|Brokenbeat]]
** [[Drill_and_bass|Drill and bass]]
** [[Drum_and_bass|Drum and bass]] (鼓打貝斯)
** [[早期叢林舞曲|Jungle]]（叢林音樂）
* [[Darkwave|Darkwave]]
* [[Downtempo|Downtempo]]
** [[新爵士|Nu jazz]]
** [[Trip_hop|Trip hop]]（又稱"Bristol Sound"）

** [[Ghetto_house|Ghetto house]]
** [[Hard_house|Hard house]] 
** [[Hip_house|Hip house]]

** [[New_Beat|New Beat]]
** [[Progressive_house|Progressive house]]
** [[Tech_house|Tech house]]
** [[Vocal_house|Vocal house]]

** [[Bitpop|Bitpop]]

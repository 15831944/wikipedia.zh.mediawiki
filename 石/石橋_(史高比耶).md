'''石橋'''（{{lang-mk|Камен мост}}，{{lang-tr|Taşköprü}}）是位於[[馬其頓共和國|馬其頓共和國]]首都[[史高比耶|史高比耶]][[發達河|發達河]]上的一座橋樑。這座橋被認為是史高比耶的象徵，出現在史高比耶的市徽上<ref>Official portal of the city of Skopje: [http://www.skopje.gov.mk/EN/DesktopDefault.aspx?tabindex=0&tabid=34 City symbols]. – Retrieved on 13 May 2009.</ref>。石橋連接了[[馬其頓廣場|馬其頓廣場]]和[[舊巴扎|舊巴扎]]。現在的石橋修建於1451年至1469年之間<ref>[http://books.google.bg/books?id=YVPshBLnW_cC&pg=PA139&dq=Stone+Bridge+skopje+Kamen+Most&hl=bg&redir_esc=y#v=onepage&q=Kamen%20Most&f=false Macedonia, Bradt Travel Guid, Thammy Evans, Bradt Travel Guides, 2010, ISBN 1841622974, p. 139.]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.bg/books?id=4JFPWuWCBzgC&pg=PA24-IA3&dq=skopje+Kamen+Most&hl=bg#v=onepage&q&f=true Skopje In Your Pocket 2006-2007, Sco, Jeroen van Marle, In Your Pocket, p. 27.]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.bg/books?hl=bg&id=elk9AAAAMAAJ&dq=%D0%BA%D0%B0%D0%BC%D0%B5%D0%BD%D0%B8%D0%BE%D1%82+%D0%BC%D0%BE%D1%81%D1%82+%D0%A1%D0%9A%D0%9E%D0%9F%D0%88%D0%95&q=+Roman+foundations+Sultan+Mehmed+II+ Macedonia, Jovan Popovski, Turistička štampa, 1969,  p. 66.]</ref>。

'''石蓮海百合'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Encrinus'''''}}）是已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[海百合|海百合]]最著名的一[[屬|屬]]<ref name="arm">{{cite journal|url=http://palaeontology.palass-pubs.org/pdf/Vol%2032/Pages%20483-497.pdf |title=The Arm Structure and Mode of Feeding of the Triassic Crinoid ''Encrinus liliiformis'' |author=Jefferies, R. P. S. |journal=[[Palaeontology_(journal)|Palaeontology]] |year=1989 |volume=32 |issue=3 |pages=483–97 |publisher=[[Palaeontological_Association|The Palaeontological Association]] |format=[[Portable_Document_Format|PDF]] |accessdate=March 25, 2012 }}{{dead link|date=December 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>，其[[化石|化石]]主要分布在[[歐洲|歐洲]]。<ref name="dk"></ref>牠們的莖較長並呈[[圓柱|圓柱]]形，還包括有間距規則的膨脹小柱。冠部蜷伏並很緊密，骨板大而膨脹。牠們長有十支短小堅實的肢，每肢僅在基座分叉一次。石蓮海百合生活在淺[[海|海]]中，能將其肢展開成扇狀以捕捉食物，落在扇狀肢臂中的[[浮游生物|浮游生物]]會沿著埋藏於肢內的凹槽被送入[[口|口]]中。當牠們遭遇危險時，肢會作出防衛動作。<ref>{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author=Walker, Cyril | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink=Cyril Walker (palaeontologist) | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=171 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

'''石綿沉滯症'''也稱為'''石綿肺'''，是一種[[石綿|石綿]]纖維造成[[肺|肺]]長期[[發炎|發炎]]及{{link-en|肺部纖維化|Pulmonary fibrosis|纖維化留疤}}的病症<ref name=Mer2013/>，症狀有[[呼吸困難|呼吸困難]]、咳嗽、[[喘鳴|喘鳴]]和[[胸痛|胸痛]]<ref name=NHS2017Int>{{cite web|title=Asbestosis symptoms and treatments|url=https://www.nhsinform.scot/illnesses-and-conditions/lungs-and-airways/asbestosis|website=NHS Inform|accessdate=19 December 2017|date=6 April 2017}}</ref>，可能帶來的併發症有[[肺癌|肺癌]]、[[間皮瘤|間皮瘤]]和[[肺性心|肺性心臟疾病]]<ref name=NHS2017Int/><ref name=EU2009>{{cite web|url=http://www.euro.who.int/__data/assets/pdf_file/0015/123072/AQG2ndEd_6_2_asbestos.PDF |title=World Health Organization. Air Quality Guidelines, 2nd Edition—Asbestos |format=PDF |accessdate=2009-12-20 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110524203454/http://www.euro.who.int/__data/assets/pdf_file/0015/123072/AQG2ndEd_6_2_asbestos.PDF |archivedate=May 24, 2011 }}</ref>。

<!-- 病因及診斷 -->
石綿沉滯症的肇因為[[吸入|呼吸]]到有石綿纖維的空氣<ref name=NHS2017Int/>,，通常是基於長期暴露於相當大量的石綿纖維之中的結果<ref name=NHS2017Int/>，典型患者僅有石綿纖維的相關工作者<ref name=EU2009/>。所有類型的石綿纖維都有很高的風險<ref name=NHS2017Ca>{{cite web|title=Asbestosis symptoms and treatments|url=https://www.nhsinform.scot/illnesses-and-conditions/lungs-and-airways/asbestosis#causes|website=NHS Inform|accessdate=19 December 2017|date=6 April 2017}}</ref>，一般認為現存的石綿應該靜置不要使用<ref name=NHS2017Pre>{{cite web|title=Asbestosis symptoms and treatments|url=https://www.nhsinform.scot/illnesses-and-conditions/lungs-and-airways/asbestosis#prevention|website=NHS Inform|accessdate=19 December 2017|date=6 April 2017}}</ref>。確診方式是判斷患者是否長期暴露於該環境，輔以[[醫學影像|醫學影像]]<ref name=Mer2013/>。石綿沉滯症屬於一種{{link-en|間質性肺纖維化症|Pulmonary fibrosis}}<ref name=Mer2013>{{cite web|title=Asbestosis - Pulmonary Disorders|url=http://www.merckmanuals.com/en-ca/professional/pulmonary-disorders/environmental-pulmonary-diseases/asbestosis|website=Merck Manuals Professional Edition|accessdate=19 December 2017|language=en-CA|date=May 2014}}</ref>。

<!-- 治療及流行病學 -->
石綿沉滯症並無特定的治療方式<ref name=NHS2017Int/>，可能的建議療法使用{{link-en|氧療|Oxygen therapy|氧氣治療}}，以及[[戒菸|戒菸]]，因為肺部有石綿沉滯症後，比較容易受到感染，也建議施打[[季節性流感疫苗|季節性流感疫苗]]、[[肺炎鏈球菌疫苗|肺炎鏈球菌疫苗]]<ref name=NIH2017Tx>{{cite web|title=Asbestosis symptoms and treatments|url=https://www.nhsinform.scot/illnesses-and-conditions/lungs-and-airways/asbestosis#treatment|website=NHS Inform|accessdate=19 December 2017|date=6 April 2017}}</ref>。2015 年石綿沉滯症患者約有 15.7 萬人，其中約有 3600 人死亡<ref name=GBD2015De>{{cite journal|last1=GBD 2015 Mortality and Causes of Death|first1=Collaborators.|title=Global, regional, and national life expectancy, all-cause mortality, and cause-specific mortality for 249 causes of death, 1980-2015: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2015.|journal=Lancet|date=8 October 2016|volume=388|issue=10053|pages=1459–1544|pmid=27733281|doi=10.1016/s0140-6736(16)31012-1|pmc=5388903}}</ref><ref name=GBD2015Pre>{{cite journal|last1=GBD 2015 Disease and Injury Incidence and Prevalence|first1=Collaborators.|title=Global, regional, and national incidence, prevalence, and years lived with disability for 310 diseases and injuries, 1990-2015: a systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2015.|journal=Lancet|date=8 October 2016|volume=388|issue=10053|pages=1545–1602|pmid=27733282|doi=10.1016/S0140-6736(16)31678-6|pmc=5055577}}</ref>，為了避免罹患此症，許多國家禁止使用石綿<ref name=NHS2017Int/>。

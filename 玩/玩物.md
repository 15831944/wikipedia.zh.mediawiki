《'''玩物'''》（{{lang-ko|노리개}}）是2013年4月18日上映的一部[[韓國|韓國]]電影，以发生在韓國女星[[張紫妍|張紫妍]]身上的真实事件為藍本拍摄，讲述了韩国娱乐圈的黑暗面，包括[[陪睡|陪睡]]和[[性虐待|性虐待]]<ref>{{cite web|last1=Conran|first1=Pierce|title=Trailer for NORIGAE Seeks Justice|url=http://twitchfilm.com/2013/03/trailer-for-norigae-seeks-justice.html|website=[[Twitchfilm|Twitch]]|accessdate=2014-08-07|date=26 March 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131231000308/http://twitchfilm.com/2013/03/trailer-for-norigae-seeks-justice.html|archivedate=31 December 2013|df=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Sunwoo|first1=Carla|title=''Norigae'' spotlights darker side of show business|url=http://koreajoongangdaily.joins.com/news/article/article.aspx?aid=2970012|website=[[Korea_JoongAng_Daily|Korea JoongAng Daily]]|accessdate=2014-08-07|date=12 April 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Now showing|url=http://koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/culture/2013/04/135_134615.html|website=[[The_Korea_Times|The Korea Times]]|accessdate=2014-07-09|date=25 April 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Lee|first1=Claire|title=Film inspired by true story lacks sensitivity for real-life victim|url=http://m.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20130423000934&ntn=0|website=[[The_Korean_Herald|The Korean Herald]]|accessdate=2014-08-07|date=30 April 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last1=Quinn|first1=Paul|title=Choi Seung-ho Interview|url=http://www.hangulcelluloid.com/choiseunghointerview.html|website=Hangul Celluloid|accessdate=2014-08-07|date=February 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Quinn|first=Paul|title=Norigae (2013 South Korea) Review|url=http://www.hangulcelluloid.com/norigae.html|work=Hangul Celluloid|accessdate=2014-08-07}}</ref>。

==剧情==
主角女星鄭智熙因遭遇职场潜规则，被強迫陪酒及性交易，最后自殺。記者李長浩和檢察官金美萱对此展开调查，希望能查明真相。

'''慈母鯨'''（[[學名|學名]]''Maiacetus''）是一[[屬|屬]]生存於[[始新世|始新世]]中期[[巴基斯坦|巴基斯坦]]的[[鯨魚|鯨魚]]。其下只有一個[[物種|物種]]，名為''M. inuus''。當中的一個標本是懷孕的雌性連同其胎兒<ref>{{cite journal en|author=Gingerich PD, Ul-Haq M, von Koenigswald W, Sanders WJ, Smith BH, Zalmout IS|title=New protocetid whale from the middle eocene of pakistan: birth on land, precocial development, and sexual dimorphism|journal=PLoS ONE|volume=4|issue=2|pages=e4366|year=2009|pmid=19194487|pmc=2629576|doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0004366|url=http://dx.plos.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0004366}}</ref>，是第一個描述的[[古鯨亞目|古鯨亞目]]胎兒標本。從胎兒的位置估計，牠們是在陸上[[繁殖|繁殖]]的。<ref name=SN>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sciencenews.org/view/generic/id/40547/title/Earliest_whales_gave_birth_on_land|title=Earliest whales gave birth on land|author=Science News|date=2009-02-03}}</ref>慈母鯨代表了陸上[[哺乳動物|哺乳動物]]重回[[海洋|海洋]]的過渡。牠們棲息在陸地與海洋的交界，來回於海洋及陸地之間。<ref name=SF>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sciencefriday.com/program/archives/200902067 |title=Ancient Whale Relative Gave Birth on Land |author=Science Friday |date=2009-02-06 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090928051126/http://www.sciencefriday.com/program/archives/200902067 |archivedate=2009-09-28 }}</ref>

慈母鯨是中等的體形，[[骨骼|骨骼]]長約2.6米，估計重達280-390公斤。

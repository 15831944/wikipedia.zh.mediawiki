《'''慈善紀事報'''》（{{lang-en|'''''The Chronicle of Philanthropy'''''}}）是一家總部位於[[美國|美國]][[華盛頓特區|華盛頓特區]]的雙週刊新聞報紙，一年共出版18期。主要報導與[[慈善|慈善]]相關的新聞。1988年由Stacy Palmer創立。<ref>Cate Dody, [http://www.nytimes.com/2007/09/10/business/media/10philanthropy.html Who’s the Most Charitable of Us All? Celebrities Don’t Always Make the Cut], NY Times, September 10, 2007, </ref> 現由出版《[[高等教育紀事報|高等教育紀事報]]》的高等教育紀事報有限公司負責出版。

== 其他 ==

慈善紀事報負責每年出版400大慈善組織排名和<ref>[http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2011/10/18/charities-struggle-in-har_n_1015810.html America's Top Fundraising Charities], Huffington Post, October 18, 2011</ref> 50大慈善家排名。<ref>Mike Boehm, [http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/culturemonster/2012/02/chronicle-philanthropy-top-arts-donors.html America's 50 top philanthropists include 12 arts donors], LA Times, February 6, 2012</ref><ref>Maria Di Mento and Caroline Preston, [http://chronicle.com/article/College-Benefactors-Lead/130680/ College Benefactors Lead 'Philanthropy 50' Ranking of 2011's Top Donors], The Chronicle of Higher Education, February 6, 2012</ref>

'''岩穴蕨'''（[[学名|学名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Monachosorum maximowiczii'''''}}）又名'''穴子蕨'''<ref>{{cite encyclopedia |encyclopedia=Flora of China |volume=2-3 |pages=148 |url=http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=2&taxon_id=242332921 |accessdate=2018-08-30}}</ref>，原为[[稀子蕨科|稀子蕨科]][[岩穴蕨属|岩穴蕨属]]（''Ptilopteris''）下的一个种<ref>{{cite journal |author1=秦仁昌 |title=中国蕨类植物科属的系统排列和历史来源(续) |journal=植物分类学报 |date=1978 |volume=16 |issue=4 |pages=16-37 |url=http://www.plantsystematics.com/qikan/Cpaper/zhaiyao.asp?bsid=13326 |accessdate=2018-08-30}}</ref>，后归入[[碗蕨科|碗蕨科]][[稀子蕨属|稀子蕨属]]<ref name="biods.2016231">{{cite journal |last1=Liu |first1=Li |last2=Shu |first2=Jiangping |last3=Wei |first3=Hongjin |last4=Zhang |first4=Rui |last5=Shen |first5=Hui |last6=Yan |first6=Yuehong |title=De novo transcriptome analysis of the rare fern Monachosorum maximowiczii (Dennstaedtiaceae) endemic to East Asia |journal=Biodiversity Science |date=2016 |volume=24 |issue=12 |pages=1325–1334 |doi=10.17520/biods.2016231}}</ref>。分布于中国东部至日本一带，为东亚特有种<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Li |first1=Huie |last2=Yao |first2=Weijie |last3=Fu |first3=Yaru |last4=Li |first4=Shaoke |last5=Guo |first5=Qiqiang |last6=Fu |first6=Binying |title=De Novo Assembly and Discovery of Genes That Are Involved in Drought Tolerance in Tibetan Sophora moorcroftiana |journal=PLoS ONE |date=2015-01-05 |volume=10 |issue=1 |pages=e111054 |doi=10.1371/journal.pone.0111054}}</ref>,生长在气候寒冷的海拔800–2500米地区<ref name="biods.2016231" />。

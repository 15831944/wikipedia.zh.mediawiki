'''岩鱷屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Sillosuchus''）又名'''席爾鱷'''，是種史前[[爬行動物|爬行動物]]，屬於[[鑲嵌踝類主龍|鑲嵌踝類主龍]]的[[波波龍科|波波龍科]]，生存於[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]晚期的[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]<ref name="Alcober & Parrish, 1997">Alcober O, Parrish JM. 1997. A new poposaurid from the upper Triassic of Argentina. ''Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology'' '''17''':548–556.</ref>，身長估計約3公尺<ref>http://www.docstoc.com/docs/84490716/GenusSpecies-Skull-Height-Length-Weight-Stage-Ablepharus--L</ref>。

岩鱷的化石發現於阿根廷[[圣胡安省_(阿根廷)|聖胡安省]]，保存於[[聖胡安國立大學|聖胡安國立大學]]。屬名在[[古希臘文|古希臘文]]意為「Sill的[[鱷魚|鱷魚]]」，是以阿根廷古生物學William Dudley Sill為名<ref name="William Dudley Sill">{{cite web |url=http://www.vertpaleo.org/source/blog/post.cfm/william-bill-sill-svp-member-passed-away-at-70 |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2011-10-13 |0=2012-04-03 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120403072022/http://www.vertpaleo.org/source/blog/post.cfm/william-bill-sill-svp-member-passed-away-at-70 |archivedate=2012-04-03 }}</ref>。 

在波波龍科中，岩鱷是已知惟一生存於[[北美洲|北美洲]]以外的物種。[[蘇牟龍|蘇牟龍]]、[[波波龍|波波龍]]、[[靈鱷|靈鱷]]都生存於北美洲<ref name="Nesbitt & Norell, 2006">Nesbitt SJ, Norell MA. 2006. Extreme convergence in the body plans of an early suchian (Archosauria) and ornithomimid dinosaurs (Theropoda). ''Proceedings of the Royal Society of London B'' '''273''': 1045–1048.</ref><ref name="Nesbitt, 2007">Nesbitt S. 2007. The anatomy of ''Effigia okeeffeae'' (Archosauria, Suchia), theropod-like convergance, and the distribution of related taxa. ''Bulletin of the American Museum of Natural History'' '''302''': 84 pp.</ref>。

'''木层孔菌属'''（[[學名|學名]]：''Phellinus''，意為「軟木」<ref name="Halpern 2007">{{cite book |author=Halpern GM |year=2007 |chapter=''Phellinus linteus'' |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=FlrpouUh740C&pg=PA95 |page=95 |title=Healing Mushrooms |isbn=978-0-7570-0196-3}}</ref>）是[[鏽革孔菌科|鏽革孔菌科]]的一個[[屬|屬]]，許多物種為導致樹木{{tsl|en|Wood-decay fungus|木材腐朽菌|白腐病}}的[[植物病理學|植物病原真菌]]，也有些物種可產生天然[[酚類|酚類]]{{link-en|苯乙烯基吡喃酮|Hispidin}}<ref name="Lee 2007">{{cite journal |author=Lee I-K, Yun B-S. |year=2007 |title=Highly oxygenated and unsaturated metabolites providing a diversity of hispidin class antioxidants in the medicinal mushrooms ''Inonotus'' and ''Phellinus'' |journal=Bioorganic & Medicinal Chemistry |volume=15 |issue=10 |pages=3309–14 |pmid=17387019 |doi=10.1016/j.bmc.2007.03.039}}</ref>。本屬物種的[[子實體_(真菌)|子實體]]不具有[[扣子體|扣子體]]，顏色為棕色，[[菌絲|骨骼菌絲]]呈黃棕色，[[菌髓|菌髓]]質地堅韌，似木頭或軟木<ref name="Martin 1990">{{cite book |author=Ellis MB, Ellis JB. |chapter=''Phellinus'' |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=vowdIZ7GqD4C&pg=PA145 |pages=145–50 |title=Fungi Without Gills (Hymenomycetes and Gasteromycetes): An Identification Handbook |publisher=Chapman and Hall |location=London, UK |year=1990 |isbn=978-0-412-36970-4}}</ref>，其中[[橢圓嗜藍孢孔菌|橢圓嗜藍孢孔菌]]（過去屬於[[嗜藍孢孔菌屬|嗜藍孢孔菌屬]]）可產生世界上最大的真菌子實體<ref name="Dai 2011">{{cite journal |author=Dai Y-C, Cui B-K. |year=2011 |title=''Fomitiporia ellipsoid'' has the largest fruiting body among the fungi |journal=Fungal Biology |volume=115 |issue=9 |pages=813–4 |pmid=21872178 |doi=10.1016/j.funbio.2011.06.008}}</ref><ref name="Dai 2012">{{cite journal |author=Cui B-K, Decock C. |year=2012 |title=''Phellinus castanopsidis'' sp. nov. (Hymenochaetaceae) from southern China, with preliminary phylogeny based on rDNA sequences |journal=Mycological Progress |volume=12 |issue=2 |pages=341–51 |doi=10.1007/s11557-012-0839-5}}</ref>。

==利用==
[[澳洲原住民|澳洲原住民]]將木层孔菌属物種用於[[傳統醫學|傳統醫學]]中，認為燃燒木层孔菌產生的煙霧吸入後可治療喉嚨痛，稍微燒黑的子實體碎屑與開水一起服用則可治療咳嗽、喉嚨痛、發燒與腹瀉等，不過他們使用的具體物種仍未被確認<ref name="Kalotas 1996">{{cite book |author=Kalotas A. |year=1996 |title=Introduction—Fungi in the Environment |series=Fungi of Australia  |volume=1B |chapter=Aboriginal knowledge and use of fungi |location=Melbourne |publisher=CSIRO |url=http://www.anbg.gov.au/fungi/aboriginal.html |isbn=978-0-643-05936-8 |pages=269–95}}</ref>。

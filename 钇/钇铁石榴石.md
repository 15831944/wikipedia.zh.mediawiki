'''钇铁石榴石''' （英语：'''Yttrium iron garnet'''或'''YIG）''' 是一种合成[[石榴石|石榴石]], 化学成分为 Y<sub>3</sub>Fe<sub>2</sub>(FeO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>3</sub>或Y<sub>3</sub>Fe<sub>5</sub>O<sub>12。</sub>它是一种[[亚铁磁性|亚铁磁性]]材料<ref name=AmElements>{{cite web | url = http://www.americanelements.com/yttrium-iron-garnet.html | title = Yttrium Iron Garnet - YIG | author =<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> | website = American Elements | access-date = April 1, 2015}}</ref> ，[[居里点|居里点]]为560 [[kelvin|K]]，<ref name=Cherepanov>{{cite journal | url = https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/037015739390107O | title = The Saga of YIG: Spectra, Thermodynamics, Interaction and Relaxation of Magnons in a Complex Magnet |author1=Vladimir Cherepanov |author2=Igor Kolokolov |author3=Victor L'Vov  |last-author-amp=yes | journal = Physics Reports | volume = 229 | number = 3 | date = 1993 | pages = 84–144 | access-date = December 12, 2018}}</ref>YIG也可称为钇铁氧体石榴石，或称为铁氧化钇或氧化钇铁，后两种名称通常与粉末形式有关。 <ref name=AZONano>{{cite web | url = http://www.azonano.com/article.aspx?ArticleID=3344 | title = Yttrium Iron Oxide / Yttrium Ferrite (Y3Fe5O12) Nanoparticles – Properties, Applications | date = September 10, 2013 | author =<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.--> | website = AZoNano.com | access-date = April 1, 2015}}</ref>在YIG中，五个三价铁离子占据两个[[八面体|八面体]]和三个[[四面体|四面体]]位置，其中三价钇离子由不规则立方体中的八个氧离子配位。 两个配位点中的铁离子表现出不同的[[自旋|自旋]]，形成[[磁性|磁性]]<ref name="Cherepanov" />。 例如，通过用[[稀土元素|稀土元素]]取代特定位点，可以获得有趣的磁性。<ref name="Goulon">{{cite journal | url = http://m.iopscience.iop.org/1367-2630/14/6/063001/pdf/1367-2630_14_6_063001.pdf | title = Site-selective couplings in x-ray-detected magnetic resonance spectra of rare-earth-substituted yttrium iron garnets |author1=J Goulon |author2=A Rogalev |author3=F Wilhelm |author4=G Goujon |author5=A Yaresko |author6=Ch Brouder |author7=J Ben Youssef  |last-author-amp=yes | journal = New Journal of Physics | volume = 14 | date = 2012| pages = 1–30 | access-date = April 2, 2015}}</ref>

它具有很高的[[韦尔代常数|韦尔代常数]]，这导致了[[法拉第效应|法拉第效应]]；<ref name="Grattan2">{{cite book|editor1=K.T.V. Grattan|editor2=B.T. Meggitt|date=1999|title=Optical Fiber Sensor Technology: Volume 3: Applications and Systems|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=1HmAz8L2qnYC&pg=PA214|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|pages=214–215|isbn=9780412825705|access-date=April 2, 2015}}</ref>它也在微波频率中具有高[[品質因子|Q因子]]值<ref name="Belov2">{{cite book|author1=Leonid Alekseevich Belov|author2=Sergey M. Smolskiy|author3=Viktor Neofidovich Kochemasov|last-author-amp=yes|date=2012|title=Handbook of RF, Microwave, and Millimeter-wave Components|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bHhYjINB6KMC&pg=PA150|publisher=Artech House|page=150|isbn=9780412825705|access-date=April 2, 2015}}</ref>，对[[红外线|红外]]波长的吸收低至1200nm ，<ref name="Sirohi2">{{cite book|author=Rajpal S. Sirohi|date=1990|title=Optical Components, Systems and Measurement Techniques|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=pXwG5Z9pfk0C&pg=PA80|publisher=CRC Press|page=80|isbn=9780824783952|access-date=April 2, 2015}}</ref>并且在[[电子自旋共振|电子自旋共振]]中的[[线宽|线宽]]非常小。这些特性使其可用于[[超導體|超导体]]中的[[磁光成像|磁光成像]]（MOI）。<ref name="Oslo2">{{cite web|url=http://www.mn.uio.no/fysikk/english/research/groups/amks/superconductivity/mo/|title=Magneto-Optical Imaging of Superconductors|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|publisher=Department of Physics, University of Oslo|date=November 30, 2010|access-date=April 2, 2015}}</ref>

YIG被用于[[微波|微波]]、[[声学|声学]]、[[光学|光学]]和[[磁光效應|磁光]]中，例如[[微波滤波器|微波滤波器]]，声学发射器和[[换能器|换能器]]。<ref name="Alamos2">{{cite web|url=http://periodic.lanl.gov/39.shtml|title=Periodic Table of Elements: Yttrium|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|publisher=Los Alamos National Laboratory|access-date=April 1, 2015}}</ref> 在光的波长超过600nm时，它是透明的。 它也被用于[[固体激光器|固态激光器]]的[[法拉第旋转器|法拉第旋转器]]，数据存储和各种[[非线性光学|非线性光学]]应用。<ref>Holm, U., Sohlstrom, H., & Brogardh, T. (1984). "YIG-Sensor Design for Fiber Optical Magnetic-Field Measurements". Proceedings of the Society of Photo-Optical Instrumentation Engineers, 514, 333–336.</ref>

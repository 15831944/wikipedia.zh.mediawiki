'''异柠檬酸脱氢酶 (NAD<sup>+</sup>)'''（{{lang-en|isocitrate dehydrogenase (NAD+)}}，[[EC编号|EC]] [http://enzyme.expasy.org/EC/1.1.1.41 1.1.1.41]）是一种以[[烟酰胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸|NAD<sup>+</sup>]]或[[烟酰胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸磷酸|NADP<sup>+</sup>]]为[[受体_(生物化学)|受体]]、作用于[[供体|供体]][[次甲基|CH]]-[[羟基|OH]][[基团|基团]]上的[[氧化还原酶|氧化还原酶]]。这种[[酶|酶]]能[[催化|催化]]以下[[酶促反应|酶促反应]]：

:[[异柠檬酸|异柠檬酸]] + [[烟酰胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸|NAD<sup>+</sup>]] <math>\rightleftharpoons</math> [[2-酮戊二酸|2-酮戊二酸]] + [[二氧化碳|CO<sub>2</sub>]] + NADH

异柠檬酸脱氢酶 (NAD<sup>+</sup>)需要[[二价锰离子|Mn<sup>2+</sup>]]或[[二价镁离子|Mg<sup>2+</sup>]]以维持活性。[[真核细胞|真核细胞]]中的异柠檬酸脱氢酶 (NAD<sup>+</sup>)以两种形式存在：其中一种与NAD<sup>+</sup>相关联且仅在[[线粒体|线粒体]]中被发现并可以[[变构调节|变构调节]]；另一种与NADP<sup>+</sup>还存在于[[细胞质基质|细胞质基质]]中但不能[[变构调节|变构调节]]。<ref>{{cite book | author = Camacho, M.L., Brown, R.A., Bonete, M.J., Danson, M.J. and Hough, D.W. | title = Isocitrate dehydrogenases from ''Haloferax volcanii'' and ''Sulfolobus solfataricus'': enzyme purification, characterisation and N-terminal sequence. | journal = FEMS Microbiol. Lett. | volume = 134 | year = 1995 | pages = 85-90 | PMID = 8593959  | url = http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8593959?dopt=Abstract | language = en}}</ref> 这两种酶——异柠檬酸脱氢酶 (NAD<sup>+</sup>)与[[异柠檬酸脱氢酶_(NADP+)|异柠檬酸脱氢酶 (NADP<sup>+</sup>)]]并不相同，前者不能以[[草酰琥珀酸|草酰琥珀酸]]作为[[底物|底物]]。某些[[物种|物种]]的异柠檬酸脱氢酶 (NAD<sup>+</sup>)也能利用NADP<sup>+</sup>，但催化的[[反应速率|反应速率]]较慢。<ref>{{cite journal | author=Inoue, H., Tamura, T., Ehara, N., Nishito, A., Nakayama, Y., Maekawa, M., Imada, K., Tanaka, H. and Inagaki, K. | title = Biochemical and molecular characterization of the NAD+-dependent isocitrate dehydrogenase from the chemolithotroph ''Acidithiobacillus thiooxidans''. | journal = FEMS Microbiol. Lett. | volume = 214 | year = 2002 | pages = 127-132 | PMID = 12204383 | url = http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/12204383?dopt=Abstract | language = en}}</ref>

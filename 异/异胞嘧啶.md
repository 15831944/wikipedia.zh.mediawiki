'''异胞嘧啶'''（简写：'''rS'''）是一种[[嘧啶|嘧啶]]碱基，是[[胞嘧啶|胞嘧啶]]（'''C'''）的同分异构体。它和[[异鸟嘌呤|异鸟嘌呤]]（'''B'''）可以在DNA中配对，<ref>{{cite web | url = http://portal.acs.org/portal/acs/corg/content?_nfpb=true&_pageLabel=PP_ARTICLEMAIN&node_id=841&content_id=CNBP_025023&use_sec=true&sec_url_var=region1&__uuid=46c9dd8e-b812-4a8d-9f18-18cb11f7a887 | title = Isocytosine | work = Molecule of the Week | accessdate = November 1, 2012 | publisher = [[American_Chemical_Society|American Chemical Society]]}}</ref>但通常在[[八文字DNA|八文字RNA]]中使用。<ref name="SCI-20190222">{{cite journal|last=Hoshika|first=Shuichi|display-authors=etal|date=22 February 2019|title=Hachimoji DNA and RNA: A genetic system with eight building blocks |journal=[[Science_(journal)|Science]]|volume=363|issue=6429|pages=884-887|doi=10.1126/science.aat0971|subscription=yes}}</ref>

[[File:IG-iC_DNA_base_pair.svg|thumb]]

它可由[[苹果酸|苹果酸]]和[[胍|胍]]反应得到：<ref>{{cite journal | title = A New Synthesis of Isocytosine | author = William T. Caldwell , Harry B. Kime | journal = J. Am. Chem. Soc. | year = 1940 | volume = 62 | issue = 9 | pages = 2365–2365 | doi = 10.1021/ja01866a028}}</ref>

[[File:Synthesis_Isocytosine.svg|thumb]]

它还被用于金属配合物成键、氢键、互变异构及质子转移效应的物理化学研究中。<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.sigmaaldrich.com/catalog/product/sigma/i2127?lang=en | title = Isocytosine | accessdate = November 1, 2012 | publisher = [[Sigma-Aldrich|Sigma-Aldrich]]}}</ref>

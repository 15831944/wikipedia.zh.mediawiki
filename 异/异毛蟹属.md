'''异毛蟹属'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|''Heteropilumnus''}}）是[[毛刺蟹總科|毛刺蟹總科]][[毛刺蟹科|毛刺蟹科]][[毛刺蟹亞科|毛刺蟹亞科]]的一個屬，原屬[[扇蟹總科|扇蟹總科]]的[[扇蟹科|扇蟹科]]<ref name="Ng">{{cite journal|journal=[[Raffles_Bulletin_of_Zoology|Raffles Bulletin of Zoology]] |year=2008 |volume=17 |pages=1–286 |title=Systema Brachyurorum: Part I. An annotated checklist of extant Brachyuran crabs of the world |author=Peter K. L. Ng, Danièle Guinot & Peter J. F. Davie |url=http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s17/s17rbz.pdf |format=[[PDF|PDF]] |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110606061453/http://rmbr.nus.edu.sg/rbz/biblio/s17/s17rbz.pdf |archivedate=2011-06-06 }}</ref>，生活於[[潮間帶|潮間帶]]。

==物種==
本屬包括有以下各物種<ref name="Ng" />：
*''Heteropilumnus amoyensis Gordon, 1931
*''Heteropilumnus angustifrons (Alcock, 1900)
*[[披发异毛蟹|披发异毛蟹]] ''Heteropilumnus ciliatus (Stimpson, 1858)
*''Heteropilumnus cristatus (Rathbun, 1909)
*''Heteropilumnus fimbriatus (H. Milne Edwards, 1834)
*''Heteropilumnus granulimanus Ward, 1933
*''Heteropilumnus hirsutior (Lanchester, 1900)
*''Heteropilumnus holthuisi Ng & L. W. H. Tan, 1988
*''Heteropilumnus lanuginosus (Klunzinger, 1913)
*''Heteropilumnus longipes (Stimpson, 1858)
*''Heteropilumnus longisetum Davie & Humpherys, 1997
*''Heteropilumnus mikawaensis Sakai, 1969
*''Heteropilumnus sasekumari Serène, 1971
*''Heteropilumnus satriai Yeo, Rahayu & Ng, 2004
*''Heteropilumnus setosus (A. Milne-Edwards, 1873)
*''Heteropilumnus splendidus (de Man, 1929)
*''Heteropilumnus stormi de Man, 1895
*''Heteropilumnus trichophoroides de Man, 1895
*''Heteropilumnus trichophorus de Man, 1895

WoRMS還包括以下兩個在其他分類歸入本屬的物種：
*''Heteropilumnus fibriatus'' accepted as ''Heteropilumnus fimbriatus'' (H. Milne Edwards, 1834)
*''Heteropilumnus quadrispinosus'' accepted as ''Viaderiana quadrispinosa'' (Zehntner, 1894)

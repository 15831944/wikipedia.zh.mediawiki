'''妲露拉·莱莉'''（{{lang-en|Talulah Riley}}，{{bd|1985年|9月26日}}），英国女演员。

== 生平 ==
莱莉出生于英格兰[[赫特福德郡|赫特福德郡]][[赫默尔亨普斯特德|赫默尔亨普斯特德]]。<ref>[http://www.telegraph.co.uk/books/authors/talulah-riley-fifty-shades-inspired-me-to-create-a-sexually-conn/ Talulah Riley: 'Fifty Shades inspired me to create a sexually conniving female character'] Retrieved 2016-10-29.</ref>她曾就读于{{le|切尔滕纳姆女子学院|Cheltenham Ladies' College}}、{{le|伯克姆斯特德学校|Berkhamsted School}}与{{le|哈博戴斯阿斯克女子学校|Haberdashers' Aske's School for Girls}}，并曾在[[英国公开大学|英国公开大学]]攻读学位。<ref>{{cite news|author1=Lesley O'Toole|title=Actress Talulah Riley shares her obsession with mortality and life with a billionaire|url=http://metro.co.uk/2013/05/17/actress-talulah-riley-shares-her-obsession-with-mortality-and-life-with-a-billionaire-3763292|work=[[Metro_(British_newspaper)|Metro]]|date=2013-05-17}}</ref>

她出演过的主要演片包括《[[傲慢与偏见_(2005年电影)|傲慢与偏见]]》、《{{le|新乌龙女校|St Trinian's (film)}}》、《{{le|新乌龙女校2|St Trinian's 2: The Legend of Fritton's Gold}}》、《西部世界》等。此外，她还主演过《[[费城故事|费城故事]]》、《{{le|夏日烟云|Summer and Smoke}}》等戏剧。<ref>{{cite web|last=Read|first=Rachel|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/nottingham/content/articles/2006/09/27/summer_and_smoke_review_0906_event_feature.shtml|title=Summer and Smoke @ Nottingham Playhouse|publisher=BBC Nottingham|date=2006-09-27}}</ref>

2010年，莱莉与著名企业家[[伊隆·马斯克|伊隆·马斯克]]成婚。<ref>{{cite web|author=Alan Boyle|url=http://www.nbcnews.com/science/spacexs-millionaire-founder-tweets-about-marital-split-6C10402497|title=SpaceX's millionaire founder tweets about marital split|publisher=NBCNews.com|date=2012-01-18|accessdate=2013-09-28}}</ref>两人于2012年离婚。<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/hannahelliott/2012/03/26/at-home-with-elon-musk-the-soon-to-be-bachelor-billionaire|title=At Home With Elon Musk: The (Soon-to-Be) Bachelor Billionaire|work=Forbes|date=2012-03-26|accessdate=2013-09-28}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Troup|first=Rose|url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/worldnews/northamerica/usa/9493191/SpaceX-founder-Elon-Musk-pays-wife-4-million-in-divorce-settlement.html|title=SpaceX founder Elon Musk pays wife $4 million in divorce settlement|work=The Telegraph|date=2012-08-22|accessdate=2013-09-28}}</ref>然而，他们于次年再度结婚<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.cbsnews.com/news/tesla-and-spacex-elon-musks-industrial-empire|title=Tesla and SpaceX: Elon Musk's industrial empire|date=2014-03-30|accessdate=2014-03-30}}</ref>，并在2016年又一次离婚。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tv3.ie/xpose/article/entertainment-news/220688/Elon-Musk-and-Talulah-Riley-reach-divorce-settlement|title=Elon Musk and Talulah Riley reach divorce settlement|work=[[TV3_(Ireland)|TV3 (Ireland)]]|date=2016-10-21|accessdate=2016-10-22}}</ref>

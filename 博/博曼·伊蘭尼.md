'''博曼·伊蘭尼'''（{{lang-en|'''Boman Irani'''}}，{{bd|1959年|12月2日|catIdx=Irani}}）是一名有[[波斯人|波斯人]]血统的[[印度|印度]]男演員。他大多在[[寶萊塢|寶萊塢]]電影中扮演喜剧角色和反派。

伊蘭尼最為人所知的角色是2009年电影《[[三傻大闹宝莱坞|三傻大闹宝莱坞]]》中的維爾·沙哈史達布德罕「病毒」博士并得到[[印度電影觀眾獎|印度電影觀眾獎]]的最佳男配角殊榮。<ref>[http://www.thehindu.com/features/cinema/3-Idiots-still-awaits-10-year-test-Boman-Irani/article16578321.ece “ CINEMA '3 Idiots' still awaits 10 year test: Boman Irani”]. The Hindu. 20-03-2010.</ref><ref>[https://m.economictimes.com/3-idiots-shines-at-filmfare-awards/articleshow/5627130.cms “3 Idiots shines at Filmfare Awards”]. The Economic Times. 28-02-2010.</ref>他其他知名的電影有《Munna Bhai M.B.B.S.》和《[[來自星星的傻瓜PK|來自星星的傻瓜PK]]》等等。<ref>[http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/pk-is-special-for-boman-irani-despite-small-role/ “‘PK’ is special for Boman Irani despite small role”]. The Indian Express. 20-10-2014.</ref>

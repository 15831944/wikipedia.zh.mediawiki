'''尤利乌斯·博兰斯勒鋼琴有限公司'''（Julius Blüthner Pianofortefabrik [[GmbH|GmbH]]）是一家總部位於德國[[萊比錫|萊比錫]]的鋼琴生產商<ref name="Grove">[http://www.oxfordmusiconline.com/subscriber/article/grove/music/03320?q=bluthner&search=quick&pos=1&_start=1#firsthit "Blüthner"], ''Grove Music Online'', 2009. Accessed 14 April 2009.</ref>，常與[[貝希斯坦|貝希斯坦]]、[[施坦威|施坦威]]、[[貝森朵夫|貝森朵夫]]並稱四大鋼琴產商<ref name="Courtney">{{cite web| url=http://www.courtneypianos.co.uk/restored.html| publisher=Courtney Pianos Ltd| title=Restoration| accessdate=6 February 2017| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150118042532/http://www.courtneypianos.co.uk/restored.html| archive-date=2015-01-18| dead-url=yes}}</ref><ref name="Bath">{{cite web| title=Bluthner Pianos at The Piano Shop Bath| publisher=The Piano Shop Bath| url=http://www.thepianoshopbath.co.uk/2013/05/bluthner-pianos/| date=10 May 2013| accessdate=6 February 2017}}</ref><ref name="henderson">{{cite web| title=About Us| url=http://www.hendersonpianos.com.au/company.html| publisher=Henderson Pianos Sydney Australia| accessdate=6 February 2017}}</ref>。它由[[尤利乌斯·博兰斯勒|尤利乌斯·博兰斯勒]]在1853年創立于萊比錫<ref name="AgeTable">{{cite web| title=Table of Blüthner Serial Numbers| url=http://www.bluthner.co.uk/our-pianos/bluthner-piano-serial-numbers/| publisher=Blüthner UK| accessdate=6 February 2017}}</ref>。

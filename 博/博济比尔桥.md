'''博济比尔桥'''是印度东北部[[阿萨姆邦|阿萨姆邦]]横跨[[布拉马普特拉河|布拉马普特拉河]]的公铁两用桥。建成后，将使得河两岸的铁路成网，并沟通NH15号公路。<ref name="articles.timesofindia.indiatimes">{{cite news|title=Bogibeel Bridge project marks 10 years with slow work progress|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2012-04-21/guwahati/31378487_1_mega-project-dibrugarh-senior-railway-official|accessdate=26 May 2013|newspaper=Times of India|date=April 21, 2012}}</ref>也是{{le|印度最長的水上橋樑列表|List of longest bridges above water in India|印度最長的鐵路兼公路橋樑}}。

== 位置==
该桥位于[[迪布鲁格尔|迪布鲁格尔]]与[[德马吉|德马吉]]的下游。<ref name="railway-technology">{{cite web|title=Bogibeel Rail Bridge, India|url=http://www.railway-technology.com/projects/bogibeel-rail-bridge/|accessdate=26 May 2013}}</ref>据中国主张的国境线仅{{Convert|20|km}}，是[[提斯浦尔|提斯浦尔]]上游的{{tsl|en|Upper Assam|上阿萨姆}}500万居民唯一的跨河桥。<ref name="indianexpress">{{cite news|title=A long wait for longest bridge in country|url=http://www.indianexpress.com/news/a-long-wait-for-longest-bridge-in-country/945859/0|accessdate=26 May 2013|newspaper=The Indian Express|date=May 6, 2012}}</ref><ref name="zeenews.india">{{cite news|title=Strategic Brahmaputra bridge to be ready by 2015?|url=http://zeenews.india.com/news/north-east/strategic-brahmaputra-bridge-to-be-ready-by-2015_771755.html|accessdate=26 May 2013|newspaper=Zee News|date=April 26, 2012}}</ref>

该桥将使得河北岸的[[伦吉亚-穆尔贡塞莱格铁路|伦吉亚-穆尔贡塞莱格铁路]]与河南岸的{{tsl|en||卢姆丁-迪布鲁格尔铁路}}连通形成路网。为此，在南岸修建了{{tsl|en|Chaulkhowa}}至[[莫兰哈特|莫兰哈特]]的{{Convert|44|km|mi|adj=mid|-long}}线路，在北岸修建了Dhamalgaon至{{tsl|en|Sisiborgaon}}线路。 
== 结构==
42个[[钢筋混凝土|钢筋混凝土]]桥墩，每跨长{{Convert|125|m}}，钢桁架焊接结构。下部为铁路桥。上部为公路桥。<ref name="articles.timesofindia.indiatimes" /><ref name="railway-technology" />
== 历史==
1985年，为解决阿萨姆种族仇杀而出台的{{tsl|en|Assam Accord|阿萨姆协议}}把此桥列为必修的基础设施之一。<ref>{{cite journal|title='Assam Accord still vital'|journal=Frontline|date=14–27 August 2010|volume=27|issue=17|url=http://www.frontline.in/navigation/?type=static&page=flonnet&rdurl=fl2717/stories/20100827271704400.htm|accessdate=26 May 2013}}</ref> 原定在第九个五年计划期间修建。<ref>{{cite journal|title=Note of conciliation|journal=Frontline|date=4–17 December 2004|volume=21|issue=25|url=http://www.frontline.in/navigation/?type=static&page=flonnet&rdurl=fl2125/stories/20041217008000600.htm|accessdate=26 May 2013}}</ref> 2002年4月21日在瓦杰帕伊内阁任内开工。<ref>{{cite news|title=Even after a decade, Bogibeel bridge waits to see light of the day|url=http://www.sentinelassam.com/state1/story.php?sec=2&subsec=2&id=114642&dtP=2012-12-01&ppr=1|accessdate=26 May 2013|newspaper=The Sentinel|date=April 22, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303235710/http://www.sentinelassam.com/state1/story.php?sec=2&subsec=2&id=114642&dtP=2012-12-01&ppr=1|archive-date=2016-03-03|dead-url=yes}}</ref>在极为缓慢进度下，2007年获得印度国家项目状态。因此，{{tsl|en|Ministry of Finance (India)|印度财政部}}出资75%，其余由{{tsl|en|Ministry of Railways (India)|印度铁道部}}出资。<ref name="indianexpress" />

该桥原定七年竣工。但进度与经费都严重超过预期。最初预算176.7亿{{INR}}，后修订为323亿{{INR}}，现在估计竣工预算为499.6亿{{INR}}。<ref>{{cite news|title=Bogibeel Rail-Cum-Road Bridge Project Targeted for Completion by March 2017|url=http://pib.nic.in/newsite/PrintRelease.aspx?relid=107472|accessdate=25 July 2014|agency=Press Information Bureau|publisher=Government of India|date=25 July 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Railways battle to complete strategic Brahmaputra bridge by 2015|url=http://india.nydailynews.com/business/8cdebdfcb7edd11dfd3e2dbd42ea957d/railways-battle-to-complete-strategic-brahmaputra-bridge-by-2015|accessdate=26 May 2013|newspaper=Daily News|date=April 25, 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130630010810/http://india.nydailynews.com/business/8cdebdfcb7edd11dfd3e2dbd42ea957d/railways-battle-to-complete-strategic-brahmaputra-bridge-by-2015|archivedate=2013年6月30日}}</ref> Bogibeel bridge is slated to be completed by the March 2017.<ref>{{cite news|title=Bogibeel Rail-Cum-Road Bridge Project Targeted for Completion by March 2017|url=http://pib.nic.in/newsite/PrintRelease.aspx?relid=107472|accessdate=25 July 2014|agency=Press Information Bureau|publisher=Government of India|date=25 July 2014}}</ref>
2008年4月，{{tsl|en|Northeast Frontier Railway|东北边境铁路局}}发包给{{tsl|en|Gammon India}}，国内最大的私营建筑公司，建设子项目；[[政府和社会资本合作|公私合营]]的{{tsl|en|Hindustan Construction Company|印度斯坦建筑公司}}与德国DSD Brouckenbau GmbH，VNR Infrastructures联营体获得了大桥主体的建造合同。<ref>{{cite news|title=HCC, associates bag Rs 987-cr railway order|url=http://www.thehindubusinessline.com/companies/hcc-associates-bag-rs-987cr-railway-order/article2656574.ece|accessdate=26 May 2013|newspaper=The Hindu Businessline|date=24 November 2011}}</ref>
 
由于当地的强降雨，施工只能在每年的11月至3月旱季。<ref>{{cite news|title=Wet season major constraint for India's longest rail-cum-road bridge|url=http://www.infrawindow.com/news/wet-season-major-constraint-for-india-s-longest-rail-cum-road-bridge_2765/|accessdate=26 May 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150123181106/http://www.infrawindow.com/news/wet-season-major-constraint-for-india-s-longest-rail-cum-road-bridge_2765/|archivedate=2015年1月23日|df=}}</ref>

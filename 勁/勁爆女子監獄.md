《'''女子监狱'''》（{{lang-en|'''''Orange Is the New Black'''''}}）是一部[[美国|美国]]电视剧。<ref name="duocast">{{cite web |url = http://www.deadline.com/2012/09/duo-cast-in-netflixs-orange-is-the-new-black-don-stark-upped-on-vhs-bounce/ |title = Duo Cast In Netflix's 'Orange Is The New Black', Don Stark Upped On VH's 'Bounce'|last = Andreeva|first = Nellie|date = 2012-09-17|publisher = Deadline.com|accessdate = 2012-09-17}}</ref>制作人为[[Jenji_Kohan|Jenji Kohan]]，2013年7月11日在[[Netflix|Netflix]]首播。<ref name="premiere">{{cite web |url = http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/netflix-orange-new-black-premiere-date-jenji-kohan-448610 |title = Netflix Sets Premiere Date for Jenji Kohan's 'Orange Is the New Black'|work = [[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]] |last = Goldberg|first = Lesley|date = 2013-04-30|accessdate = 2013-04-30}}</ref>制作公司为{{link-en|狮门电视|Lionsgate Television}}，根据[[Piper_Kerman|Piper Kerman]]的监狱经历回忆录《Orange Is the New Black》改编。<ref name="based">{{cite web | url = http://www.deadline.com/2011/07/netflix-eyeing-second-original-series-comedy-from-weeds-creator-jenji-kohan/#more-149314 | title = Netflix Eyeing Second Original Series – Comedy From ''Weeds'' Creator Jenji Kohan }}</ref>主要角色有因为参与运输毒品而入狱的Piper Chapman（{{link-en|泰勒·席林|Taylor Schilling}}饰演），Piper的毒贩前女友Alex Vause（[[劳拉·普莱潘|劳拉·普莱潘]]饰演）。

2013年6月27日，Netflix宣布拍摄第二季并于2014年播出。<ref name="season2">{{cite web|url = http://www.deadline.com/2013/06/netfilx-renews-orange-is-the-new-black-for-second-season/ |title = Netflix Renews ‘Orange Is The New Black’ For Second Season|work = Deadline.com|last = Andreeva|first = Nellie|date = 2013-06-27|accessdate = 2013-06-27}}</ref>2018年10月，確定第七季會是最後一季，於2019年播放<ref>{{Cite web |url = http://www.vulture.com/2018/10/orange-is-the-new-black-end-season-7.html |title = Orange Is the New Black's Prison Sentence Will End With Season 7 |author = Dee Lockett |publisher = Vulture |language = en}}</ref>。

* [[劳拉·普莱潘|劳拉·普莱潘]] 饰演 Alex Vause 愛莉克斯·沃斯，派柏的前女友，與派柏有著複雜的感情關係，派柏對其是又愛又恨。<ref>{{cite web | url=http://o.canada.com/2013/08/01/orange-is-the-new-blacks-laura-prepon-on-rebuking-lesbian-advances-nudity-and-lena-dunhams-karla-hamolka-jokes/ | title=Orange is the New Black’s Laura Prepon on rebuking lesbian advances and Lena Dunham’s Karla Homolka joke | publisher=[[Postmedia_Network|Postmedia Network]] | work=[[Canada.com|Canada.com]] | date=2013-08-01 | author=Dekel, Jon}}</ref>
* [[Michael_Harney|Michael J. Harney]] 饰演 Sam Healy 監獄的資深輔導員。
* [[Michelle_Hurst|Michelle Hurst]] 饰演 Miss Claudette 海地裔的移民，早年孤身來到美國，收容無家可歸的孤兒，成立清潔公司，因殺掉一名虐待孤兒的客戶而入獄，在第一季時為派柏的室友，後因與獄警發生肢體衝突被轉往高級戒備監獄，之後便未再登場。
* [[Kate_Mulgrew|Kate Mulgrew]] 饰演 Galina 
* [[Jason_Biggs|Jason Biggs]] 饰演 Larry Bloom Piper的未婚夫，早先是派柏閨密波莉的鄰居，後與派柏認識，論及婚嫁時，才得知派柏早年是同性戀，派柏因參與其同性戀人愛莉克斯·沃斯的運毒計畫，被判刑1年半，賴瑞與派柏婚事只得暫擱，但時間與距離最終沖淡兩人感情，在得知派柏在獄中與前女友愛莉克斯過從甚密時，提出分手。

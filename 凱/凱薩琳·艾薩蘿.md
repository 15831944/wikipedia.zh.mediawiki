'''凱薩琳·安·艾薩蘿'''（Catherine Ann Asaro），[[美國|美國]][[作家|作家]]。

==生平==
凱薩琳·艾薩蘿出生於美國奧克蘭，畢業於[[哈佛大學|哈佛大學]]。

==家庭==
凱薩琳·艾薩蘿的伴侶是天文學家John Kendall Cannizzo<ref>[http://astrophysics.gsfc.nasa.gov/staff/CVs/John.Cannizzo/ Goddard Spaceflight Center]</ref>，目前育有一女<ref>[http://cresst.umd.edu/Opportunities/Cannizzo%20Report%202010.pdf X-Ray variability: Quasi-periodic Oscillations] {{webarchive|url=http://archive.wikiwix.com/cache/20110814000000/http://cresst.umd.edu/Opportunities/Cannizzo%20Report%202010.pdf |date=2011-08-14 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.yagp.org/eng/SC_regwin05.php |title=Cathy Cannizzo, Youth America Grand Prix |access-date=2015-02-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120410191023/http://www.yagp.org/eng/SC_regwin05.php |archive-date=2012-04-10 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>。

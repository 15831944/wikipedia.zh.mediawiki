
'''凱利·麥克高尼戈爾'''（Kelly Mcgonigal）是一名出身于1977年的美国心理學家。凱利·麥克高尼戈爾曾在[[波士顿大学|波士顿大学]]获学士和硕士学位，在[[斯坦福大学|斯坦福大学]]获博士学位。凱利·麥克高尼戈爾目前供职于史丹佛大學商學院<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/experts/kelly-mcgonigal-phd|title=Kelly McGonigal Ph.D|publisher=Psychology Today}}</ref>。凱利有一个双胞胎姐妹{{link-en|珍妮·麥克高尼戈爾|Jane  Mcgonigal}}，职业是游戏设计师<ref>[http://kellymcgonigal.com/about-kelly-mcgonigal/  Kelly McGonigal's website About] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140416191747/http://kellymcgonigal.com/about-kelly-mcgonigal/ |date=2014-04-16 }}</ref><ref>''ELLE'' Magazine, June 22, 2011 [http://www.elle.com/pop-culture/reviews/super-girl-2 Super girl] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140618192352/http://www.elle.com/pop-culture/reviews/super-girl-2 |date=2014-06-18 }}</ref>。

2005年到2012年，凱利曾担任同行评审学术期刊“the International Journal of Yoga Therapy”（大意：瑜伽治疗国际期刊）主编。凱利的主要研究领域为自我控制，她认为“自我控制就像[[肌肉|肌肉]]一样，使用过后就会进入疲劳状态”<ref>Watson, D.L., ''Self-Directed Behavior: Self-Modification for Personal Adjustment'', 10th ed. (2013) Cengage Learning, page 4</ref><ref>''New York Times'', January 6, 2012, [https://www.nytimes.com/2012/01/08/nyregion/at-candle-79-an-organic-vegan-book-party.html?_r=0 At the Table | Candle 79, You Say You Made a Resolution]</ref><ref>washingtonpost.com live Q and A, July 24, 2007, [https://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/discussion/2007/07/23/DI2007072300775.html Debt and Stress, Kelly McGonigal]</ref><ref>[http://msbusiness.com/businessblog/2013/09/04/biz-video-kelly-mcgonigal-make-stress-friend/ Mississippi Business Journal blog] September 4, 2013</ref><ref>Molbray. Scot, ''Huffington Post'', 10/12/13 [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/cooking-light/cooking-light-weight-loss-challenge_b_4070741.html Who is thin and why]</ref><ref>Mundahl, Steven, The ''Alchemy of Authentic Leadership'' (2013) Balboa Press,  page 42</ref><ref>NBC ''Today'' show [http://www.nbc.com/news-sports/today-show/2012/01/willpower-is-key-to-keep-new-year-resolutions/ 'Willpower' is key to keep New Year resolutions]{{Dead link|date=2018年8月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。2013年前，凱利的学说认为冥想练习可以提升人面对挑战时的抗压能力。但在2013年后，她改变了观点，认为人面对压力时的态度才是关键<ref>Huff post  Lifestyle, Jan Bruce, 09/30/2013 [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/mequilibrium/stress-is-good_b_3998259.html  Stress is good]</ref>。

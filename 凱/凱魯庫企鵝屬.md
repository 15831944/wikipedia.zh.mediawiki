'''凱魯庫企鵝屬'''（學名：''Kairuku''，意思為「帶回食物的潛水者」）為一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[企鵝|企鵝]]<ref name=Kairuku>{{Cite journal|author=Ksepka, D.T.|author2=Fordyce, R.E.|author3=Ando, T.|author4=Jones, C.M.|last-author-amp=yes |year=2012 |title=New fossil penguins (Aves, Sphenisciformes) from the Oligocene of New Zealand reveal the skeletal plan of stem penguins |journal=Journal of Vertebrate Paleontology |volume=32 |issue=2 |pages=235–254 |doi=10.1080/02724634.2012.652051 }}</ref>，包括兩個物種：''{{link-en|K. grebneffi|Kairuku grebneffi}}'' 及 ''K. waitaki''<ref>[http://blogs.scientificamerican.com/observations/2012/02/27/giant-prehistoric-penguin-was-bigger-than-an-emperor/ Giant Prehistoric Penguin was Bigger Than an Emperor | Observations, Scientific American Blog Network<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>。化石發現於地層年代[[漸新世|漸新世]]晚期（約2,500 - 2,700萬年前）.<ref name=AP>{{cite news|title=Scientists reconstruct ‘elegant’ giant penguin that lived in New Zealand 26 million years ago|agency=Associated Press|date=March 1, 2012|publisher=Dawn.com|url=http://www.dawn.com/2012/03/01/scientists-reconstruct-extinct-giant-penguin.html|accessdate=March 1, 2012}}</ref>的[[紐西蘭|紐西蘭]] {{link-en|Kokoamu Greensand}} 地層組<ref name=Kairuku/>。

== 描述 ==
凱魯庫企鵝屬的物種 ''Kairuku grebneffi'' 體長約 {{convert|1.5|m|ft}} ，站起來時高度達 {{convert|1.3|m|ft}}<ref name=Kairuku/>，體重接近 {{convert|60|kg|lb}}，比[[皇帝企鵝|皇帝企鵝]]還重上 50%<ref name=AP />。牠們擁有目前已知包括所有現存與已滅絕的企鵝中最長的[[肱骨|肱骨]]<ref name=Kairuku />，更長的嘴喙、更瘦長的身體<ref name=discovery>{{cite news|title=Ancient Penguin Weighed 130 Pounds|author=Jennifer Viegas|date=February 27, 2012|work=Discovery News|url=http://news.discovery.com/animals/penguin-tallest-fattest-new-zealand-120227.html|accessdate=March 1, 2012}}</ref>、更長的鰭狀肢，行動可能較現存的企鵝更為靈活<ref name=discovery /><ref name=AFP>{{cite news|title=Fossils show huge penguin once roamed New Zealand|agency=AFP|publisher=Google|date=February 28, 2012|url=https://www.google.com/hostednews/afp/article/ALeqM5hDOe538Gwyarqsobxnxf_eR926Jw?docId=CNG.cfcd37cf53571fe3837c4aed03e3a309.3d1|accessdate=March 1, 2012}}</ref>。

凱魯庫企鵝利用牠們尖銳的嘴喙在海中捕捉魚和烏賊<ref name=discovery />，可能比現存的企鵝更善於游泳，並會游至更深遠的海洋中<ref name=AP />，主要天敵為鯊魚和[[鮫齒鯨|鮫齒鯨]]<ref name=discovery />

== 大眾文化 ==
在遊戲《[[方舟：生存進化|方舟：生存進化]]》中，玩家可以馴服並飼養凱魯庫企鵝。

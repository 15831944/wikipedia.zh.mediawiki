'''凱旋青年公園'''（{{lang-ko|개선청년공원}}）是[[北韓|北韓]][[平壤|平壤]]的一座[[遊樂園|遊樂園]]，於1984年開幕，位置靠近[[金日成競技場|金日成競技場]]，座落於[[牡丹峰|牡丹峰]]西山腳下。遊樂園佔地約40公頃<ref name="vnc"/>，設有[[旋轉木馬|旋轉木馬]]、遊戲屋與[[摩天輪|摩天輪]]等設施，其建立是[[凱旋門_(平壤)|凱旋門]]揭幕儀式的一部份<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.wikimapia.org/609033/Kaeson-Youth-Park# |title=存档副本 |access-date=2012-09-09 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110728004208/http://wikimapia.org/609033/Kaeson-Youth-Park# |archive-date=2011-07-28 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。
{{commonscat|Kaeson Youth Park}}
2010年四月[[金正日|金正日]]造訪前，凱旋青年公園進行了一次整修<ref name=AJW>{{cite news|title=Kim Jong Il inspects Pyongyang amusement park|url=http://ajw.asahi.com/article/asia/korean_peninsula/AJ201112060001|accessdate=May 23, 2012|newspaper=The Asahi Shimbun|date=December 6, 2011|author=Yoshihiro Makino|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20111207194329/http://ajw.asahi.com/article/asia/korean_peninsula/AJ201112060001|archivedate=December 7, 2011|df=}}</ref><ref>[http://www.divinecaroline.com/22245/59401-eerie-amusement-parks-north-korea "The Eerie Amusement Parks of North Korea"] by Dahlia Rideout DivineCaroline.Com {{webarchive |url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120102050145/http://www.divinecaroline.com/22245/59401-eerie-amusement-parks-north-korea |date=January 2, 2012 }}</ref>，據說遊玩當時園內的全部十種設施共需1600[[朝元|朝元]]<ref name=AJW/>，同年園內還增設了一架從[[義大利|義大利]]進口的雲霄飛車<ref name=CNN>{{cite news|title=The happiest place in North Korea|url=http://travel.cnn.com/explorations/life/inside-north-koreas-amusement-parks-possibly-happiest-places-country-113961|accessdate=May 23, 2015|newspaper=cnn.com|date=August 20, 2011|author=Barry Neild|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180329151011/http://travel.cnn.com/explorations/life/inside-north-koreas-amusement-parks-possibly-happiest-places-country-113961/#|archive-date=2018-03-29|dead-url=no}}</ref>。截至2013年，園內的設施包括[[碰碰車|碰碰車]]、{{link-en|旋轉咖啡杯|Teacups}}、{{link-en|旋轉鞦韆|Swing ride}}與自由落體。

'''凱斯特聖艾德蒙'''（Caistor St Edmund），或譯'''凯斯托圣埃德蒙'''，是位於英國[[諾福克郡|諾福克郡]][[塔斯河|塔斯河]]河畔的一個村莊，2011年人口普查時有289位居民<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7&b=11121562&c=NR14+8QL&d=16&e=62&g=6450618&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=0&s=1441806698033&enc=1|title=Parish population 2011|accessdate=2015-09-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304130016/http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7&b=11121562&c=NR14+8QL&d=16&e=62&g=6450618&i=1001x1003x1032x1004&m=0&r=0&s=1441806698033&enc=1#|archive-date=2016-03-04|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。此地保留有[[羅馬不列顛|羅馬不列顛]]時代的集鎮及[[爱西尼人|爱西尼人]]首都“Venta Icenorum”遺址，地名本身在[[安敦尼行记|安敦尼行记]]上有載<ref>{{cite web|url=http://penelope.uchicago.edu/Thayer/E/Gazetteer/Periods/Roman/Topics/Engineering/roads/Britain/_Texts/CODROM/1*.html|title=Roman Roads in Britain|author=Thomas Coddington|year=1903|publisher=Society for Promoting Christian Knowledge|accessdate=2008-02-14}}</ref>。

'''凱特琳·瑪麗·「凱蒂」·洛茨'''（{{lang-en|Caitlin Marie "Caity" Lotz}}，{{bd|1986年|12月30日|catIdx=L}}），是美國演員、舞蹈員、歌手、模特。著名作品有《''{{link-en|Death Valley (TV series)|Death Valley (TV series)|Death Valley}}''》、《[[死亡約定_(2012電影)|死亡約定]]》、《[[绿箭侠_(电视剧)|綠箭俠]]》、《[[明日傳奇|明日傳奇]]》。

== 早年生活 ==
凱蒂出生於美國[[加州|加州]]的聖地亞哥。

== 職業生涯 ==
凱蒂以舞蹈員身份出道。曾隨[[艾薇兒·拉維尼|艾薇兒·拉維尼]]及[[Lady_Gaga|Lady Gaga]]表演，<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.metro.co.uk/film/900935-caity-lotz-there-were-a-few-spooky-moments-on-the-set-of-the-pact "Caity Lotz: There were a few spooky moments on the set of The Pact"]. </cite></ref>演出作品包括[[Lady_Gaga|Lady Gaga]]的〈[[愛情狗仔|愛情狗仔]]〉及〈[[爱情游戏|爱情游戏]]〉<ref name="MTV"><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.mtv.com/shows/death_valley/cast_member.jhtml?personalityId=15015 "Caity Lotz"]. </cite></ref> [[大衛·庫塔|大衛·庫塔]]及[[艾絲黛兒|艾絲黛兒]]的〈One Love〉、[[賽琳娜·戈梅茲|賽琳娜·戈梅茲]]的〈[[灰姑娘的歌舞情緣|灰姑娘的歌舞情緣]]〉、Faith Evans的〈Mesmerized〉、[[T-Pain|T-Pain]]的〈Freeze〉、[[JoJo|JoJo]]的〈Baby It's You〉及[[卡絲卡達樂團|卡絲卡達樂團]]的〈[[Evacuate_the_Dancefloor_(歌曲)|Evacuate the Dancefloor]]〉。她亦出現在[[銳跑|銳跑]]（Reebok）、[[Jack_in_the_Box|Jack in the Box]]、[[T-Mobile|T-Mobile]]的廣告中，又在《The Legion of Extraordinary Dancers》中跳舞。曾參與Hip-hop theater制作的《Groovaloo》及在電影《[[舞力全開3D|舞力全開3D]]》<ref name="GAB"><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.genderacrossborders.com/2010/08/24/caity-lotz-talks-dance-traveling-back-in-time-for-mad-men/ "Caity Lotz talks Dance, Traveling Back in Time for Mad Men"] {{Wayback|url=http://www.genderacrossborders.com/2010/08/24/caity-lotz-talks-dance-traveling-back-in-time-for-mad-men/ |date=20150531083520 }}. </cite></ref>中作為特技替身。凱蒂也是一個武術家，學過[[跆拳道|跆拳道]]、[[武術|武術]]、[[泰拳|泰拳]]。她是[[跑酷|跑酷]]和Tricking的發燒友。<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.daemonstv.com/2011/07/21/caity-lotz-interview/ "Exclusive Interview: Kick Ass Caity Lotz From MTV’s DEATH VALLEY Talks About Zombies, Parcour and Twilight’s Jackson Rathbone"]. </cite></ref>

2005年，凱蒂加入女子組合Soccx。2006年，發佈她們首張單曲〈From Dusk Till Dawn (Get the Party Started)〉。2007年發佈單曲〈Scream Out Loud〉。两首單曲均在德國達到前十名。首張專輯《Hold On》亦在同年發佈。而第三首首單曲〈Can't Take My Eyes Off You〉則在2008年推出。<ref name="MTV"><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.mtv.com/shows/death_valley/cast_member.jhtml?personalityId=15015 "Caity Lotz"]. </cite></ref>

凱蒂為雜誌《[[男士健康|男士健康]]》<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.menshealth.com/sex-women/caity-lotz "That's What She Said: Caity Lotz"]. </cite></ref> 及《[[君子雜誌_(刊物)|君子雜誌]]》擔任模特。後來上過《Me In My Place》的封面<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.esquire.com/women/me-in-my-place/caity-lotz-pictures-2011#fbIndex1 "Esquire Presents Me in My Place with Caity Lotz"]. </cite></ref>

在2006年，凱蒂開始了她的演藝生涯。在電影《''{{link-en|Bring It On: All or Nothing|Bring It On: All or Nothing}}''》擔任了一個小角色。隨後，在2010年，她在《''{{link-en|Law & Order: LA|Law & Order: LA}}''》第三集中客串並在[[AMC|AMC]]電視劇《[[廣告狂人|廣告狂人]]》第四季中飾演Stephanie，Anna Draper的侄女。

2011年，凱蒂在[[音樂電視網|MTV]]恐怖劇《''{{link-en|Death Valley (TV series)|Death Valley (TV series)|Death Valley}}''》擔任其中一名主角Officer Kirsten Landry。凱蒂自己完成了所有特技場面。 <ref name="GAB"><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.genderacrossborders.com/2010/08/24/caity-lotz-talks-dance-traveling-back-in-time-for-mad-men/ "Caity Lotz talks Dance, Traveling Back in Time for Mad Men"] {{Wayback|url=http://www.genderacrossborders.com/2010/08/24/caity-lotz-talks-dance-traveling-back-in-time-for-mad-men/ |date=20150531083520 }}. </cite></ref>

2012年，凱蒂演出了《''{{link-en|Live at the Foxes Den|Live at the Foxes Den}}''》。並在《''{{link-en|Battle of the Year (film)|Battle of the Year (film)|Battle of the Year}}''》和[[喬許·霍洛威|喬許·霍洛威]]<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/josh-holloway-chris-brown-planet-b-boy-cast-253734 "Josh Holloway, Laz Alonso and Chris Brown Lead 'Planet B Boy' Cast"]. </cite></ref>合作。又和[[卡斯柏·范狄恩|卡斯柏·范狄恩]]及{{link-en|安妮絲·布魯克納|Agnes Bruckner}}共同演出《[[死亡約定_(2012電影)|死亡約定]]》<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://www.fearnet.com/news/interviews/b24865_exclusive_death_valley_star_caity_lotz.html "Exclusive: 'Death Valley' Star Caity Lotz on Her Sundance Film 'The Pact'"]. </cite></ref>。  <ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://filmguide.sundance.org/film/120112/the_pact "THE PACT"]. </cite></ref> <ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">[http://insidemovies.ew.com/2012/01/26/caity-lotz-saves-the-pact/ "Sundance 2012: Caity Lotz -- extremely hot, and incredibly badass -- saves 'The Pact'"]. </cite></ref>在大熱劇集《[[绿箭侠_(电视剧)|綠箭俠]]》第二季及第三季中接替Jacqueline MacInnes Wood飾演[[黑金絲雀|莎拉·蘭斯]]。<ref><cite class="citation news" contenteditable="false">Abrams, Natalie (July 19, 2013). </cite></ref><ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">Prudom, Laura (July 30, 2013). </cite></ref> 她將繼續在《[[明日傳奇|明日傳奇]]》中擔任該角色。在2014年發佈的科幻電影《''{{link-en|The Machine (film)|The Machine (film)|The Machine}}''》中領銜演出。<ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">William Bibbiani (16 April 2014). </cite></ref><ref><cite class="citation web" contenteditable="false">Ohad Amram (15 November 2013). </cite></ref> 在2014年5月，她在《广告狂人》第七季重新以Stephanie身份亮相。''

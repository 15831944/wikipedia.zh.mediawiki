'''凱特·蕭蘭'''（{{lang-en|Cate Shortland}}，{{bd|1968年|8月10日}}）是一位[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]]電影電視劇導演、編劇，知名作品有《{{le|墮落飄零燕|Somersault (film)}}》、《{{le|少女洛荷|Lore (film)}}》以及《[[柏林綜合症|柏林綜合症]]》。

== 早期經歷 ==
凱特·蕭蘭生於[[澳大利亞|澳大利亞]][[新南威爾斯州|新南威爾斯州]]{{le|特莫拉|Temora, New South Wales}}，畢業於澳洲廣播電視電影學校。

== 職業生涯 ==
在製作數部獲獎短片之後，蕭蘭於2004年執導了電影處女作《{{le|墮落飄零燕|Somersault (film)}}》，影片成功參展[[第57屆戛納電影節|第57屆戛納電影節]][[一種注目|注目單元]]<ref name="festival-cannes.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/4199808/year/2004.html |title=Festival de Cannes: Somersault |accessdate=2009-12-03|work=festival-cannes.com|language=en}}</ref>。2005年，蕭蘭執導澳洲電視電影《{{le|寂靜 (電影)|The Silence (2006 film)|寂靜}}》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.screenaustralia.gov.au/the-screen-guide/t/the-silence-2005/20975?stxt=the%20silence&yend=2018|title=THE SILENCE|language=en}}</ref>。第二部劇情長片《{{le|少女洛荷|Lore (film)}}》於2012年在[[悉尼|悉尼]]電影節首映<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sff.org.au/public/films/program/films-a-z/lore/|title="Lore", ''Sydney Film Festival 2012''|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120815184926/http://sff.org.au/public/films/program/films-a-z/lore/ |date=2012-08-15|archivedate=2012-06-09|language=en}}</ref>。影片代表澳大利亞參選[[第85屆奧斯卡金像獎|第85屆奧斯卡金像獎]]的[[奧斯卡最佳外語片獎|奧斯卡最佳外語片獎]]，但未入圍<ref name="Oscars">{{cite web|url=http://www.vogue.com.au/culture/art/australian+film+lore+up+for+an+oscar,19719|title=Australian film Lore up for an Oscar|accessdate=2012-09-07|work=Vogue|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120909013815/http://www.vogue.com.au/culture/art/australian+film+lore+up+for+an+oscar,19719|archivedate=2012-09-09|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|last=Rohter|first=Larry|title=Viewing the Nazis Through Their Children’s Eyes|url=http://carpetbagger.blogs.nytimes.com/2013/02/04/viewing-the-nazis-through-their-childrens-eyes/|newspaper=The New York Times|date=2013-02-04|language=en}}</ref>。2015年，蕭蘭公佈正在製作第三部劇情長片《[[柏林綜合症|柏林綜合症]]》，影片根據同名小說改編，[[泰瑞莎·帕瑪|泰瑞莎·帕瑪]]主演<ref>{{cite web|last1=Jagernauth|first1=Kevin|title=Teresa Palmer To Lead Cate Shortland's 'Berlin Syndrome'|url=http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/teresa-palmer-to-lead-cate-shortlands-berlin-syndrome-20150515|accessdate=2015-09-23|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151019234038/http://blogs.indiewire.com/theplaylist/teresa-palmer-to-lead-cate-shortlands-berlin-syndrome-20150515|archive-date=2015-10-19|dead-url=yes}}</ref>，於2017年在[[柏林|柏林]][[日舞影展|日舞影展]]首映<ref>{{cite web|last1=Debruge|first1=Peter|title=Sundance Film Festival Unveils 2017 Competition, Next 2018年7月，蕭蘭確認執導[[漫威影業|漫威影業]]的電影《[[黑寡婦_(電影)|黑寡婦]]》<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/black-widow-movie-be-directed-by-cate-shortland-1126708|title='Black Widow' Movie Finds Director in Cate Shortland (Exclusive)|last=Kit|first=Borys|work=[[The_Hollywood_Reporter|The Hollywood Reporter]]|date=2018-07-12|accessdate=2018-07-12|archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/70riB79jH?url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/black-widow-movie-be-directed-by-cate-shortland-1126708|archivedate=2018-07-12|deadurl=no|language=en}}</ref>。

== 個人經歷 ==
蕭蘭[[皈依猶太教|改宗]][[猶太教|猶太教]]<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.smh.com.au/entertainment/movies/staring-at-the-scars-20120907-25izf.html | work=The Sydney Morning Herald | title=Staring at the scars|language=en}}</ref>。2009年與電影製作人托尼·克勞茲結婚<ref>{{cite web|last1=George|first1=Sandy|title=Take Two: Cate Shortland and Tony Krawitz|url=http://if.com.au/2012/09/21/article/Take-Two-Cate-Shortland-and-Tony-Krawitz/WHHEDPDDMI.html|language=en}}</ref>，並領養兩個孩子<ref>{{cite web|last1=Tinkham|first1=Chris|title=Cate Shortland Building From the Details|url=http://www.undertheradarmag.com/interviews/cate_shortland/|language=en}}</ref>。

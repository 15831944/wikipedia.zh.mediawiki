'''磅同'''（{{lang-km|ក្រុងកំពង់ធំ}}，意为“大港口”），另译'''磅通'''，是[[柬埔寨|柬埔寨]][[磅同省|磅同省]]的省会城市，坐落于{{le|森河|Steung Saen River}}沿岸。<ref>Communicating Climate-Change and Natural Hazard Risk and Cultivating Jeanette L. Drake, Yekaterina Y. Kontar, John C. Eichelberger 3319201611  2015 "Kampong Thom is one of the major regional centers in central Cambodia and is located on the lower Stung Sen River basin."</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=http://bookingyourtravel.com/city/kampong-thom-city-69.html |title=Kampong Thom, a picturesque town on the banks of the Stung Saen River |access-date=2017-07-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170206232500/http://bookingyourtravel.com/city/kampong-thom-city-69.html |archive-date=2017-02-06 |dead-url=yes }}</ref>该市是[[金边|金边]]至[[暹粒市|暹粒]][[6號國道_(柬埔寨)|6号国道]]的中间站。<ref>Matt Jacobson ''Adventure Cambodia: An Explorer's Travel Guide''   9749575539 2004 p131 "Highway 6 continues on to the left, going to Kampong Thom and Siem Reap."</ref>2016年12月，在磅同市和磅特墨县（Kampong Thmor）举行了“中柬优秀电影巡映”活动。<ref>{{cite news|title=“中柬优秀电影巡映”活动在柬埔寨磅通省举行|url=http://www.hinews.cn/news/system/2016/12/20/030890609.shtml|publisher=[[国际在线|国际在线]]|date=2016-12-20|}}</ref>

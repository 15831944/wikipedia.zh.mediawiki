'''普舒帕瓦麗'''（{{Lang-en|'''Pushpavalli'''}}，{{bd|1925年|1月3日|1991年|catIdx=Pushpavalli}}）是[[印度|印度]]女演員，主要演出[[泰米尔语|泰米尔语]]和[[泰卢固语|泰卢固语]]电影。她最為所知的是她和已婚南印度男影星[[占美尼·加尼山|占美尼·加尼山]]的關係。<ref>[https://blogs.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/tracking-indian-communities/gemini-ganesan-emperor-of-romance-on-and-off-screen/ “Gemini Ganesan — emperor of romance on and off screen”]. The Times of India Blog. 23-03-2016.</ref>普舒帕瓦麗其後更為加尼山誕下兩名女兒，共中長女[[瑞卡|瑞卡]]是印度最知名的女演員之一。<ref>[https://www.hindustantimes.com/regional-movies/mahanati-s-deleted-scene-throws-light-on-rekha-and-father-gemini-ganesan-s-relationship-watch-video/story-BB9hvKmgf6RxJRwYOG0o0O.html "Mahanati’s deleted scene throws light on Rekha and father Gemini Ganesan’s relationship.

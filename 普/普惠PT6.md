'''普拉特 & 惠特尼 加拿大 PT6'''是最常见的一类[[涡桨|涡桨]][[航空发动机|航空发动机]]。由[[普拉特·惠特尼加拿大公司|普拉特·惠特尼加拿大公司]]研制。从1963年至2016年，累计交付51 000台，4亿飞行小时。2016年的飞行停机率为间隔651 126小时一次。 

'''PT6A'''覆盖{{cvt|580|and|1940|shp}}；'''PT6B/C'''是用于直升机的涡轴发动机。

== Development==
1956年， 普惠加拿大总裁Ronald Riley指示发动机经理Dick Guthrie建立一支涡桨发动机设计团队，准备活塞式[[普惠黄蜂系列发动机|普惠黄蜂系列发动机]]的升级换代产品。从{{tsl|en|National Research Council (Canada)|加拿大国家研究委员会}}与{{tsl|en|Orenda Engines|奥伦达发动机}}公司招募工程师。1958年，由普拉特·惠特尼加拿大的12名引擎工程师组成的团队在加拿大开始研制第一款小型涡轮引擎 PT6，预期功率450轴马力。1960年2月首次运行。<ref name=pt6nation_1/><ref>{{cite journal|title=Pratt's 'dirty dozen'|journal=Aviation Week and Space Technology|pages=42–43}}</ref>1961年5月30日装在{{tsl|en|Beech 18}}飞机首飞。首个全性能成品在1963年下线，1963年获得适航证。1964年交付给了客户，首个装机机型{{tsl|en|Beech Queen Air|比奇空中女王}}。并吸引美国军方订货后，开启了{{tsl|en|King Air|比奇空中国王}}机型，累计生产超过7,000架。<ref>{{cite news |url= http://aviationweek.com/blog/little-engine-could-and-did |title= The Little Engine That Could ... and Did |date= Oct 30, 2012 |author= Business Aviation Now |work= Aviation Week Network}}</ref>

从1963年到2016年，功重比改进了50%, {{tsl|en|brake specific fuel consumption|有效燃油消耗率}}（比油耗）改进了20%，{{tsl|en|overall pressure ratio|总压比}}达到了14:1。<ref name=AirInsightJune2016>{{cite news |url= http://airinsight.com/2016/06/09/discussion-pratt-whitney-canada-president-john-saabas/ |title= A Discussion with Pratt & Whitney Canada President John Saabas |publisher= AirInsight |date= June 9, 2016}}</ref> 

==设计==
[[File:Reduction_Gear.jpg|thumb]]

由容易分离维护的两部分组成：涡轮机与{{tsl|en|Free-turbine turboshaft|自由涡轮}}。<ref name=pt6nation_2>{{cite web|url=http://www.pt6nation.com/en/articles/article/history-an-engine-ahead-of-its-time/|title=An Engine Ahead of Its Time|author=<!--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-->|website=PT6 Nation|publisher=Pratt & Whitney Canada}}</ref> 低压[[轴流压缩机|轴流压缩机]]在中小功率版本有3级，在大功率版本有4级。单级{{tsl|en|centrifugal compressor|离心式压缩机}}后，通过折叠型环形{{tsl|en|combustion chamber|燃烧室}}，高压燃气再通过单级高压涡轮（转速45,000 rpm）。然后流经动力涡轮（约30,000 rpm），对于小型版本为单级，对于大中型版本为两级。涡桨发动机使用两级的行星输出减速齿轮箱，驱动螺旋桨转速1,900 到 2,200 rpm。发动机尾气通过两个侧向的排气口。

==型号==
'''PT6A'''涡桨发动机，在初期覆盖了580 至 920 轴马力，现在覆盖到1940轴马力的大功率型号。'''PT6B''' 与 '''PT6C''' 是涡轴发动机。

Several other versions of the PT6 have appeared over time :
* the '''Large PT6''' added another power turbine stage and a deeper output reduction, producing almost twice the power output, between 1,090 and {{convert|1920|shp|abbr=on}} ;
* the '''PT6B''' is a [[helicopter|helicopter]] turboshaft model, featuring an offset reduction gearbox with a freewheeling clutch and power turbine governor, producing {{convert|1000|hp|abbr=on}} at 4,500 rpm ;
* the '''PT6C''' is a helicopter model, with a single side-mounted exhaust, producing {{convert|2000|hp|abbr=on}} at 30,000 rpm, which is stepped down in a user-supplied gearbox ;
* the '''[[Pratt_&_Whitney_Canada_PT6T|PT6T Twin-Pac]]''' consists of two PT6 engines driving a common-output reduction gearbox, producing almost {{convert|2000|hp|abbr=on}} at 6,000 rpm ;
* the '''ST6''' is a version intended for stationary applications, originally developed for the [[UAC_TurboTrain|UAC TurboTrain]], and now widely used as [[auxiliary_power_unit|auxiliary power unit]]s on large aircraft, as well as many other roles.<ref>

===PT6C===
;PT6C
:The '''PT6C''' is a 1600 to 2300 horsepower (1190 to 1720 kW) engine for helicopters and tiltrotors.

===PT6D===
;PT6D-114A
:The PT6D-114A is based on the PT6A-114A. The main difference is the deletion of the second-stage reduction gearing and output shaft, because the engine is intended for integration with a combining gearbox incorporating power turbine governors and a propeller output shaft.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rgl.faa.gov/Regulatory_and_Guidance_Library/rgMakeModel.nsf/0/90C641493420043B8625752F006482E8?OpenDocument|title=PT6 Type Certificate Data Sheet Information|publisher=[[Federal_Aviation_Administration|Federal Aviation Administration]]}}</ref>

;Soloy Dual Pac:2x PT6D-114A engines driving a single propeller through a combining gearbox, capable of independent operation.

===PT6T===
;[[Pratt_&_Whitney_Canada_PT6T|PT6T]]
:Twin PT6 power units combining outputs through a gearbox for use in helicopters.

===ST6===
;ST6
:The '''ST6''' is a variant of the PT6 that was originally developed as a powerplant for the [[UAC_TurboTrain|UAC TurboTrain]] power cars, but later developed as a stationary power generator and [[auxiliary_power_unit|auxiliary power unit]].
;ST6B
:The '''ST6B-62''' was a 550 bhp (410 kW) version of the PT6 developed for use in the [[STP-Paxton_Turbocar|STP-Paxton Turbocar]], raced in the 1967 [[Indianapolis_500|Indianapolis 500]].<ref name="Magazines1967">{{cite journal|author=Bill Kilpatrick|title=The Big Engine That Almost Did|journal=Popular Mechanics|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=rtMDAAAAMBAJ&pg=PA69|date=August 1967|publisher=Hearst Magazines|pages=69–71|accessdate=2011-06-26|issn=0032-4558}}</ref>

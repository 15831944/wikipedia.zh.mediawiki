'''普通人與獨立人格'''<ref>{{Citation |title=Ordinary People and Independent Personalities party releases its election slate |newspaper=The Slovak Spectator |date=15 December 2011 |url=http://spectator.sme.sk/articles/view/44844/10/ordinary_people_and_independent_personalities_party_releases_its_election_slate.html |accessdate=31 January 2012}}</ref> （{{lang-sk|Obyčajní Ľudia a nezávislé osobnosti，缩写为OĽaNO}}），通常簡稱為'''普通人'''（Obyčajní Ľudia），是[[斯洛伐克|斯洛伐克]][[保守主義|保守主義]]政黨。該黨在[[2010年斯洛伐克議會選舉|2010年斯洛伐克議會選舉]]中推薦四位參選人進入[[自由與團結黨_(斯洛伐克)|自由與團結黨]]選舉名單並全數當選。黨魁為議員[[伊果·馬托維奇|伊果·馬托維奇]]（Igor Matovič）。

普通人黨與自由與團結黨在議會中達成合作協議。2010年6月與7月<ref name="SaS chairman speaks about">{{cite news |title=SaS chairman speaks about its new MPs from the Ordinary People civic association |first=Zuzana |last=Vilikovská |newspaper=[[The_Slovak_Spectator|The Slovak Spectator]] |date=15 June 2010 |url=http://spectator.sme.sk/articles/view/39247/10/sas_chairman_speaks_about_its_new_mps_from_the_ordinary_people_civic_association.html |accessdate=}}</ref>，傳聞普通人黨將拒絕支持包括自由與團結黨在內的中間偏右執政聯盟。<ref>{{cite news |title='Ordinary Man' MP Matovič accuses Fico of lying |first=Zuzana |last=Vilikovská |newspaper=[[The_Slovak_Spectator|The Slovak Spectator]] |date=5 August 2010 |url=http://spectator.sme.sk/articles/view/39711/10/ordinary_man_mp_matovic_accuse_fico_of_lying.html |accessdate=}}</ref><ref name="Ordinary People faction will give">{{cite news |title=SaS: Ordinary People faction will give up their parliamentary seats if they leave SaS caucus |first=Zuzana |last=Vilikovská |newspaper=[[The_Slovak_Spectator|The Slovak Spectator]] |date=3 August 2010 |url=http://spectator.sme.sk/articles/view/39698/10/sas_ordinary_people_faction_will_give_up_their_parliamentary_seats_if_they_leave_sas_caucus.html |accessdate=}}</ref>

2010年8月，馬托維奇表示現在不是成為獨立政黨的時機。<ref name="Ordinary People faction will give" /> 然而在2011年10月28日，普通人黨正式登記，馬托維奇宣布該黨將參加[[2012年斯洛伐克議會選舉|下屆選舉]]，與獨立人士和[[公民保守黨|公民保守黨]]、[[斯洛伐克保守民主黨|斯洛伐克保守民主黨]]（Conservative Democrats of Slovakia）推出選舉名單。<ref name="Visegrad"/>

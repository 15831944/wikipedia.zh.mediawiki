'''普雷西'''（英文：Pressie）是指出没于[[美国|美国]][[苏必利尔湖|苏必利尔湖]]的{{link-en|湖怪（神秘动物学）|Lake monster|湖怪}}，这是一种长75英尺，头部类似[[马|马]]、颈部很长、尾部类似[[鯨|鲸鱼]]的[[神秘生物列表|神秘动物]]，其历史可追溯至19世纪。<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://wrkr.com/michigan-cryptozoology/|title=The 5 Most Cryptic Creatures in Michigan|accessdate=2018-09-01|work=1077 WRKR|language=en}}</ref>据记载，[[美國原住民|美国原住民]]称苏必利尔湖怪为“Mishipishu”。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://cryptomundo.com/bigfoot-report/lake-monsters-sea-serpents-and-lake-superior/|title=Lake Monsters, Sea Serpents and Lake Superior|last=Cryptids|first=Cryptomundo,|date=2005-12-03|newspaper=Cryptomundo|accessdate=2018-09-01|language=en-US}}</ref>

== 历史 ==
*1894年9月，在{{link-en|怀特菲什角|Whitefish Point}}-{{link-en|科珀港|Copper Harbor, Michigan}}，两艘轮船的船员观察到一只背部突出水面6-8英尺的动物在湖中游动。<ref name=":0">{{Cite news|url=https://cryptomundo.com/bigfoot-report/pressie-the-lake-superior-monster/|title=Pressie, The Lake Superior Monster|last=Cryptids|first=Cryptomundo,|date=2013-07-10|newspaper=Cryptomundo|accessdate=2018-09-01|language=en-US}}</ref>

*1897年，[[明尼蘇達州|明尼苏达州]][[杜魯斯|杜鲁斯]]一名男性称遭遇一只疑似大海蛇的动物袭击，另有3名目击者看见这只巨蛇。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://cryptidz.wikia.com/wiki/Pressie|title=Pressie|newspaper=Cryptid Wiki|accessdate=2018-09-01|language=en}}</ref>

*1977年，兰迪·布朗（Randy Braun）拍摄到一张疑似是湖中游动巨蛇的照片，据说这张照片可能是在{{link-en|波丘派恩山|Porcupine Mountains}}一带拍摄。<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://eu.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/2015/10/21/michigan-monsters-mysteries-dogman-bigfoot/74340526/|title=The Dogman and other Michigan mysteries|newspaper=Detroit Free Press|accessdate=2018-09-01|language=en}}</ref>

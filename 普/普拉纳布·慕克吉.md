'''普拉纳布·慕克吉'''（{{lang-en|Pranab Kumar Mukherjee}}，1935年12月11日－）是第13任[[印度总统|印度总统]]，于2012年7月25日就任<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.outlookindia.com/items.aspx?artid=769603|title=Pranab Mukherjee wins Presidential elections|date=22 July 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120723063458/http://news.outlookindia.com/items.aspx?artid=769603|archivedate=2012年7月23日}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.indianexpress.com/news/its-official-pranab-mukherjee-set-to-become-president-of-india/977854/|title=It's official, Pranab Mukherjee set to become President of India|date=22 July 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ndtv.com/blog/show/presidential-poll-pranab-mukherjee-crosses-halfway-mark-set-to-become-president-231924|title=Live blog NDTV|date=22 July 2012}}</ref>，成为首位[[孟加拉人|孟加拉人]]印度总统<ref>http://zeenews.india.com/news/exclusive/pranab-mukherjee-the-13th-president-of-india_789045.html{{dead link|date=2018年1月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>。他在政坛已经活跃了超過40年，曾任国防部长、外交部长和财政部长等职<ref>[http://dhruvplanet.com/7888/pranab-mukherjee-is-indias-13th-president-interesting-facts-details-profile/ Pranab Mukherjee is India's 13th President: Interesting Facts - Profile] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120725131803/http://dhruvplanet.com/7888/pranab-mukherjee-is-indias-13th-president-interesting-facts-details-profile/ |date=2012-07-25 }}</ref>。

==生平==
他生于[[英属印度|英属印度]][[孟加拉|孟加拉]]省的一个[[孟加拉人|孟加拉人]]家庭，信仰[[印度教|印度教]]。他父亲 Kamada Kumar Mukherjee 在印度[[印度独立运动|独立时期]]是名[[印度国大党|印度国大党]]成员，并在[[英国|英国]][[监狱|监狱]]里被关押了10多年<ref name="NDTV">{{cite web|url=http://www.ndtv.com/article/people/who-is-pranab-mukherjee-231318 |title=Who is Pranab Mukherjee? |publisher=NDTV|date= 15 June 2012|accessdate= 11 July 2012}}</ref>，父亲后来从1952年至1964年是西孟加拉议会 (WBLC)的议员<ref name="NDTV"/><ref name="PMI">{{cite web|url=http://www.pranabmukherjee.in/|title=Biography|publisher=Pranab Mukherjee|accessdate=11 July 2012|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100904170154/http://www.pranabmukherjee.in/|archivedate=2010年9月4日}}</ref>。

慕克吉首先进入比尔布姆的 Suri Vidyasagar College 学习，后来获得[[加尔各答大学|加尔各答大学]]<ref name="GOVT">{{cite web|url=http://india.gov.in/govt/loksabhampbiodata.php?mpcode=4195|title=Shri Pranab Mukherjee|publisher=Government of India|accessdate= 11 July 2012}}</ref>的[[历史|历史]]与[[政治学|政治学]]硕士学位，他也拥有加尔各答大学的[[法学|法学]]学位<ref name="NDTV"/><ref name="PMI"/>。2011年他获得英国[[伍爾弗漢普頓大学|伍爾弗漢普頓大学]]（University of Wolverhampton）的荣誉[[文学博士|文学博士]]<ref name="Honorary degree Doctorate">{{cite web|url=http://www.thehindubusinessline.com/industry-and-economy/government-and-policy/article2054913.ece|title=Honorary doctorate for Pranab from UK university|work=Our Bureau|publisher=The Hindu, June, 2011|accessdate=2011-06-13|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121008233627/http://www.thehindubusinessline.com/industry-and-economy/government-and-policy/article2054913.ece|archive-date=2012-10-08|dead-url=yes}}</ref>。2012年3月又获得印度{{Link-en|阿萨姆大学|Assam University}}（Assam University）荣誉文学博士<ref name=small_prize>[http://www.telegraphindia.com/1120716/jsp/frontpage/story_15733694.jsp#.UAvioWEe4sc Small price for big prize]</ref>。

===当选总统===
2012年6月15日，他被[[团结进步联盟|团结进步联盟]]提名为总统候选人<ref>{{cite web|last=Prabhu|first=Chawla|title=Pranab nominated after Mulayam-Sonia secret meet|url=http://ibnlive.in.com/news/pranab-nominated-after-mulayamsonia-secret-meet/266362-37-64.html|accessdate=4 July 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://in.news.yahoo.com/hunt-begins-for-head-of-state.html |title=存档副本 |accessdate=2015-06-29 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141025031731/https://in.news.yahoo.com/hunt-begins-for-head-of-state.html |archivedate=2014-10-25 }}</ref>，他的竞争对手是来自[[国民民主联盟|国民民主联盟]]（NDA）的[[P.A._Sangma|P.A. Sangma]]（独立人士，1996年至1998年担任国会下院议长）<ref>{{cite news|title=Pranab Mukherjee, Sangma final candidates for Prez polls|url=http://www.dnaindia.com/india/report_pranab-mukherjee-sangma-final-candidates-for-prez-polls_1710719|accessdate=4 July 2012|newspaper=Daily News and Analysis|date=4 July 2012}}</ref>。

总统选举由国会两院议员及各邦议会议员组成的选举团选出。今年的选举团包括4120名省议员和776名国会议员，议员手中握有的选票依各自选区人口多寡而异，今年有总数为109万8000张选票<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.zaobao.com/gj/gj120723_003.shtml | language= | title=印度前财长当选总统 |publisher=联合早报 |date=2012-07-23 |accessdate= }}</ref>。总统选举于7月19日举行，结果于7月22日公布。他获得了713,763张选举票赢得选举，对手Sangma获得了315,987张选票<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ibnlive.in.com/news/live-counting-of-votes-begins-for-president-poll/272800-37-64.html|title=CNNIBN Blog|accessdate=22 July 2012|date=22 July 2012}}</ref>。

'''破骨細胞'''（Osteoclast，其中Osteo-來自[[古希臘語|古希臘語]]''ὀστέον''(osteon) ，意爲骨，-clast來自古希臘語''κλαστός'' (clastos)，意爲破壞）是一種存在於[[骨|骨]]組織中的細胞，由多個[[單核細胞|單核細胞]]融合而成，直徑最大可達到100微米，[[細胞核|細胞核]]的數目一般是6-50個<ref name=Zhang2005>{{cite book|title=組織學與胚胎學|author=章爲等|publisher=四川大學出版社|year=2005|ISBN=7-5614-2971-1|pages=35-36|section=第四章 軟骨和骨}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Basle MF, Mazaud P, Malkani K, Chretien MF, Moreau MF, Rebel A | year = 1988 | title = Isolation of osteoclasts from Pagetic bone tissue: morphometry and cytochemistry on isolated cells | url = | journal = Bone | volume = 9 | issue = 1| pages = 1–6 | pmid = 2837260 | doi=10.1016/8756-3282(88)90020-8}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Jain N, Weinstein RS | year = 2009 | title = Giant osteoclasts after long-term bisphosphonate therapy: diagnostic challenges | url = | journal = Nat Rev Rheumatol | volume = 5 | issue = 6| pages = 341–6 | doi = 10.1038/nrrheum.2009.87 | pmid = 19491914 }}</ref>。破骨細胞的主要功能是通過釋放[[乳酸|乳酸]]、[[檸檬酸|檸檬酸]]、[[碳酸|碳酸]]，以及碳酸酐酶、溶酶體酶等，對骨組織進行分解破壞<ref name=Zhang2005/>。該細胞於1873年由德國的[[阿尔伯特·冯·科立克|阿尔伯特·冯·科立克]]最早發現<ref name="cells of bone">{{citation |author1=Nijweidi Peter J. |author2=Feyen, Jean H. M. | year = 1986 | title = Cells of Bone: Proliferation, Differentiation, and Hormonal Regulation | journal = Physiological Reviews | volume = 66 | issue = 4| pages = 855–886 | pmid = 3532144 }}</ref>。

在牙發育過程中，能吸收脫落[[乳牙|乳牙]]牙根的破牙本質細胞與破骨細胞相似，兩者存在一定的聯繫<ref name="The Free Dictionary">{{cite web|url=http://medical-dictionary.thefreedictionary.com/odontoclast |title=Odontoclast |publisher=Farlex, The Free Dictionary |year=2007 |accessdate=2013-11-06 }}</ref><ref name=":0">{{cite journal |vauthors=Wang Z, McCauley LK |title=Osteoclasts and odontoclasts: signaling pathways to development and disease |journal=Oral Diseases |volume=17 |issue=2 |pages=129–42 |date=March 2011  |pmid=20659257 |doi=10.1111/j.1601-0825.2010.01718.x}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{cite book|author=K. Chatterjee|title=Essentials of Oral Histology|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=1_ge-OtrElQC&pg=PA155&lpg=PA155&dq=Odontoclast|date=1 December 2006|publisher=Jaypee Brothers Publishers|isbn=978-81-8061-865-9|page=155}}</ref>。

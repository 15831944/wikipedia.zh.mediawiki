'''總鐵結合能'''（Total iron-binding capacity，'''TIBC'''），有時又稱'''運鐵蛋白結合能'''（transferrin iron-binding capacity）為醫學上用於表示[[转铁蛋白|转铁蛋白]]攜帶血中游離[[铁|铁]]的能力<ref name="urlMedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia: Total iron binding capacity">{{cite web |url=https://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/003489.htm |title=MedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia: Total iron binding capacity |work= |accessdate=2008-12-31}}</ref>。TIBC的數值由抽血檢測獲得，評估血液中有能力和鐵結合的蛋白質有多少，並以此間接量測[[转铁蛋白|转铁蛋白]]的含量<ref name="pmid12507977">{{cite journal |vauthors=Yamanishi H, Iyama S, Yamaguchi Y, Kanakura Y, Iwatani Y |title=Total iron-binding capacity calculated from serum transferrin concentration or serum iron concentration and unsaturated iron-binding capacity |journal=Clin. Chem. |volume=49 |issue=1 |pages=175–8 |date=January 2003 |pmid=12507977 |doi= 10.1373/49.1.175|url=http://www.clinchem.org/cgi/pmidlookup?view=long&pmid=12507977}}</ref>。過去由於直接測量转铁蛋白相當昂貴，因此藉由量測TIBC來較為簡單便宜<ref name="pmid12476940">{{cite journal |vauthors=Kasvosve I, Delanghe J |title=Total iron binding capacity and transferrin concentration in the assessment of iron status |journal=Clin. Chem. Lab. Med. |volume=40 |issue=10 |pages=1014–8 |date=October 2002 |pmid=12476940 |doi=10.1515/CCLM.2002.176 |url=http://www.reference-global.com/doi/abs/10.1515/CCLM.2002.176 }}{{Dead link|date=2018年6月 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=no }}</ref><ref name="pmid9439462">{{cite journal  |vauthors=Gambino R, Desvarieux E, Orth M, etal |title=The relation between chemically measured total iron-binding capacity concentrations and immunologically measured transferrin concentrations in human serum |journal=Clin. Chem. |volume=43 |issue=12 |pages=2408–12 |date=December 1997 |pmid=9439462 |doi= |url=http://www.clinchem.org/cgi/pmidlookup?view=long&pmid=9439462}}</ref>。即使今日直接測定转铁蛋白已非難事，但TIBC依然廣被使用<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.accuspeedy.com.tw/A16_search_items/006_sp_biochem/230_TIBC.htm|title=TIBC/UIBC|accessdate=2018-03-21|work=www.accuspeedy.com.tw}}</ref>。

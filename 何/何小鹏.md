'''何小鹏''' ({{bd|1977年|11月3日||}})是一位湖北企业家，[[UC优视|UC优视]]和[[小鹏汽车|小鹏汽车]]联合创始人。<ref>[http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=46476173  company profile], BusinessWeek</ref>

==生平==
1977年出生在[[湖北|湖北]][[黄石|黄石]]，毕业于[[华南理工大学|华南理工大学]]计算机科学专业，2004年与[[梁捷|梁捷]]共同创建[[UC优视|UC优视]]，2014年6月被[[阿里巴巴集团|阿里巴巴集团]]收购。同年共同创建[[小鹏汽车|小鹏汽车]]并担任董事长。<ref>[https://www.reuters.com/article/2014/06/11/us-alibaba-group-ucweb-idUSKBN0EM04P20140611 Alibaba to buy out UCWeb in China's biggest internet merger], ''Reuters'', June 11, 2014</ref><ref>[https://www.forbes.com/sites/jlim/2014/06/11/alibaba-fully-buys-out-ucweb-in-chinas-biggest-internet-deal-ever/ Alibaba Fully Buys Out UCWeb in China's Biggest Internet Deal Ever], ''Forbes'' magazine, June 11, 2014</ref>2017年8月22日宣布辞去[[阿里巴巴集团|阿里巴巴集团]]所有职务。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.top-news.top/news-13953107.html|title=Ali entered the new car BAT Xiaopeng investment field repairer |last=|first=|work=Forbes|access-date=2018-03-06|language=en}}</ref>

'''何清园批发城'''（{{lang-en|Kenanga Wholesale City}}，简称为KWC），是[[马来西亚|马来西亚]]首个服装批发商场。<ref name="Malaysia first wholesale">{{cite web | url=http://www.tonguechic.com/beauty/the-rookies-guide-to-kenanga-wholesale-city/ | title=About KWC | publisher=[http://www.tonguechic.com] (Official website) | accessdate=13 January 2012}}</ref>该商场位于[[吉隆坡|吉隆坡]]市中心{{tsl|ms|Pudu, Kuala Lumpur|半山芭}}的肯納格路，从[[武吉免登|吉隆坡金三角]]至此只需几分钟。<ref name="KWCLocation">{{Cite web|url=http://www.timeoutkl.com/Shopping/venues/Kenanga-Wholesale-City|title=KWC Location|accessdate=8 April 2014|publisher=Time Out KL}}</ref>

'''旅行卡'''（Travelcard）是一種可以通用於[[倫敦地鐵|倫敦地鐵]]、[[倫敦地上鐵|倫敦地上鐵]]、[[倫敦交通局鐵路|倫敦交通局鐵路]]、[[碼頭區輕便鐵路|碼頭區輕便鐵路]]、[[倫敦電車連線|倫敦電車連線]]、[[倫敦交通局巴士|倫敦交通局巴士]]及[[大倫敦|大倫敦]]地區[[英國全國鐵路|英國全國鐵路]]的可在規定時間內無限次使用的鐵路通票<ref name=fares_09>{{cite web|url=http://www.tfl.gov.uk/assets/downloads/fares-and-tickets-zones1-6.pdf |publisher=[[Greater_London_Authority|Greater London Authority]] |date=January 2009 |author=[[Transport_for_London|Transport for London]] |title=Your guide to fares and tickets within Zones 1-6 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090117151557/http://www.tfl.gov.uk/assets/downloads/fares-and-tickets-zones1-6.pdf |archivedate=17 January 2009 }}</ref>。旅行卡可以是帶有磁條的紙質票券，也可以印在[[牡蠣卡|牡蠣卡]]上。旅行卡於1983年開始發行<ref name=contract>{{cite book | title=A new fares contract for London | author=Tony Grayling & Stephen Glaister | year=2000}}</ref><ref name=poster_may_83>{{cite web| url=http://www.ltmcollection.org/posters/poster/poster.html?_IXMAXHITS_=1&IXinv=2000/4694&IXsummary=results/results&IXsearch=tickets&_IXFIRST_=351 | title=Smile please, by unknown artist, 1983| year=1983 | publisher=London Transport}}</ref>。

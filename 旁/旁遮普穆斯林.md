'''旁遮普穆斯林'''（{{lang-pa|{{Nastaliq|پنجابی مسلمان}}}}）是居住于[[旁遮普地区|旁遮普地区]]的一个语言、地理及宗教族群。主要由[[旁遮普人|旁遮普人]]构成<ref name="auto">{{cite book|last=Gandhi|first=Rajmohan|title=Punjab: A History from Aurangzeb to Mountbatten|year=2013|page=1|publisher=Aleph Book Company|location=New Delhi, India, Urbana, [[伊利诺伊州|Illinois]]|isbn=978-93-83064-41-0}}</ref>。旁遮普穆斯林为信仰[[伊斯兰教|伊斯兰教]]且讲[[旁遮普語|旁遮普語]]的族群。总人口9000多万<ref name="auto"/>，是巴基斯坦最大的民族，也是继阿拉伯<ref>Margaret Kleffner Nydell [https://books.google.com/books?id=ZNoiieefqAcC&printsec Understanding Arabs: A Guide For Modern Times], Intercultural Press, 2005, {{ISBN|1931930252}}, page xxiii, 14</ref>及孟加拉穆斯林<ref>roughly 170 million in [[孟加拉国|Bangladesh]] and 130 million in the [[印度|Republic of India]] ([[世界概况|CIA Factbook]] 2014 estimates, numbers subject to rapid population growth); about 10 million {{tsl|en|Bangladeshis in the Middle East||Bangladeshis in the Middle East}}, 1 million {{tsl|en|Bengalis in Pakistan||Bengalis in Pakistan}}, 5 million {{tsl|en|British Bangladeshi||British Bangladeshi}}.</ref>后第三大穆斯林族群<ref>{{cite book|last=Gandhi|first=Rajmohan|title=Punjab: A History from Aurangzeb to Mountbatten|year=2013|page=2|publisher=Aleph Book Company|location=New Delhi, India, Urbana, [[伊利诺伊州|Illinois]]|isbn=978-93-83064-41-0}}</ref>。族群主体为[[伊斯兰教|伊斯兰教]][[遜尼派|遜尼派]]，但也有小部分属[[什叶派|什叶派]]及其他派别，包括起源于旁遮普的[[阿赫迈底亚|阿赫迈底亚]]社群。

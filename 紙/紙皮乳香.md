'''紙皮乳香'''（學名：'''''Boswellia papyrifera'''''），又稱'''蘇丹乳香'''<ref>[http://www.dict.cc/english-german/Sudanese+frankincense.html Äthiopischer Weihrauch (Ethiopian incense)]</ref>，埃塞爾比亞人稱其為“itan zaf”<ref>[http://www.isd.org.et/publications/Annual%20reports/ISD%202011%20Annual%20Report.pdf Agroforestry in Ethiopia], bottom of p. 3.</ref>，是[[乳香屬|乳香屬]]下的一種植物，生產[[乳香|乳香]]，分佈于[[埃塞俄比亞|埃塞俄比亞]]、[[厄立特里亞|厄立特里亞]]和[[蘇丹|蘇丹]]。這種乳香具有清新的檸檬香或松香<ref>[http://www.floracopeia.com/about/ethiopia-oils-and-resins/ Ethiopian Oils and Resins]</ref>，是主要的乳香來源之一<ref>{{cite journal |first= Arthur O. |last=Tucker |title=Frankincense and myrrh |url=https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/BF02859654 |journal=Economic Botany |date=October 1986 |volume=40 |issue=4 |pages=425–433 |doi=10.1007/BF02859654}}</ref>，富含[[乙酸辛酯|乙酸辛酯]]（57.1–65.7%）和[[正辛醇|正辛醇]]（3.4–8.8%）<ref>{{cite journal |first=D. |last=Bekana |first2=T. |last2=Kebede |first3=M. |last3=Assefa |first4=H. |last4=Kassa |title=Comparative Phytochemical Analyses of Resins of ''Boswellia'' Species (''B. papyrifera'' (Del.) Hochst., ''B. neglecta'' S. Moore, and ''B. rivae'' Engl.) from Northwestern, Southern, and Southeastern Ethiopia |journal=ISRN Analytical Chemistry |pages=374678 |year=2014 |doi=10.1155/2014/374678 |url=https://www.hindawi.com/archive/2014/374678/cta/}}</ref>。

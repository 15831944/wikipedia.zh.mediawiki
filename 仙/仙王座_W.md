'''仙王座 W'''是位於[[仙王座|仙王座]]這個[[星座|星座]]的一顆[[聯星|光譜聯星]]和[[變星|變星]]。他被認為是[[仙王座_OB1|仙王座 OB1]][[星協|星協]]的成員，距離我們大約8,000光年<ref name=famaey/><ref name=polyakova/>。

== 發現 ==
仙王座W是在1865年被報告為變星，它的變光範圍在7.3至8.3等<ref name=chandler>{{cite journal|bibcode=1896AJ.....16..145C|title=Third catalogue of variable stars|journal=Astronomical Journal|volume=16|pages=145|author1=Chandler|first1=S. C.|year=1896|doi=10.1086/102484}}</ref>。它在1903年出版的[[波恩星表|波恩星表]]篇目為BD+57°2568，而在[[HD星表|HD星表]]的序號是HD 214369。

在1925年，仙王座 W被列入[[Be星|Be星]]的表單中。它的光譜類型被認定為低溫的Mep <ref name=merrill>{{cite journal|bibcode=1925ApJ....61..389M|title=Discovery and Observations of Stars of Class Be|journal=Astrophysical Journal|volume=61|pages=389|author1=Merrill|first1=P. W.|last2=Humason|first2=M. L.|last3=Burwell|first3=C. G.|year=1925|doi=10.1086/142899}}</ref>。依據1949年的頻譜，它被歸類為K0ep Ia，但也被認為有一顆小的高溫伴星，並加上不尋常的紅外過量<ref name=bidelman>{{cite journal|bibcode=1954ApJS....1..175B|title=Catalogue and Bibliography of Emission-Line Stars of Types Later than B|journal=Astrophysical Journal Supplement|volume=1|pages=175|author1=Bidelman|first1=William P.|year=1954|doi=10.1086/190007}}</ref><ref name=woolf>{{cite journal|bibcode=1973ApJ...185..229W|title=Infrared emission from unusual binary stars|journal=Astrophysical Journal|volume=185|pages=229|author1=Woolf|first1=N. J.|year=1973|doi=10.1086/152411}}</ref>。研究來自伴星的紫外線光譜吸收線，它的光譜被歸類為B0-1<ref name=wing>{{cite journal|bibcode=1981NASCP2171..341W|title=Notes on the early-type components of W Cep, O Cet, CH Cyg, AR Mon, and BL Tel|journal=In NASA.  Goddard Space Flight Center  the Universe at Ultraviolet Wavelengths:  the First Two Yrs. of Intern. Ultraviolet Explorer  p 341-347 (SEE N81-25893 16-90)|volume=2171|author1=Wing|first1=R. F.|last2=Carpenter|first2=K. G.|year=1981}}</ref>。

== 系統 ==
仙王座 W的系統包括一顆K型[[超巨星|超巨星]]與一顆光譜類型為早期B型的非超巨星伴星。這顆恆星有不尋常的[[譜線|發射線]]，包括由含有塵埃和電離氣體的星周包層產生的許可和禁制的Fe<sub>II</sub>線<ref name=wallerstein>{{cite journal|bibcode=1971ApJ...166..725W|title=On the Infrared Excess of W Cephei and Similar Stars|journal=Astrophysical Journal|volume=166|pages=725|author1=Wallerstein|first1=George|year=1971|doi=10.1086/150996}}</ref>。使用[[散斑成像|斑點干涉]]解析出兩顆成員相距0.262"<ref name=prieur>{{cite journal|bibcode=2002ApJS..139..249P|title=High Angular Resolution Observations of Late-Type Stars|journal=The Astrophysical Journal Supplement Series|volume=139|pages=249|author1=Prieur|first1=J. L.|last2=Aristidi|first2=E.|last3=Lopez|first3=B.|last4=Scardia|first4=M.|last5=Mignard|first5=F.|last6=Carbillet|first6=M.|year=2002|doi=10.1086/338029}}</ref>。已提出可能的軌道週期是2,090日<ref name=polyakova>{{cite journal|bibcode=2006Ap.....49..164P|title=Variations in the brightness and polarization of W Cep|journal=Astrophysics|volume=49|issue=2|pages=164|author1=Polyakova|first1=T. A.|year=2006|doi=10.1007/s10511-006-0017-z}}</ref>。

== 變異性 ==
仙王座 W的亮度變化從7等至9等。[[變星總表|變星總表]]將它列為[[半規則變星|半規則變星]]，試圖找出週期但是只顯示隨機的光度變化<ref name=kiss>{{cite journal|bibcode=2006MNRAS.372.1721K|arxiv=astro-ph/0608438|title=Variability in red supergiant stars: Pulsations, long secondary periods and convection noise|journal=Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society|volume=372|issue=4|pages=1721|author1=Kiss|first1=L. L.|last2=Szabó|first2=Gy. M.|last3=Bedding|first3=T. R.|year=2006|doi=10.1111/j.1365-2966.2006.10973.x}}</ref><ref name=percy>{{cite journal|bibcode=2009JRASC.103...11P|title=Long Secondary Periods in Pulsating Red Supergiant Stars|journal=Journal of the Royal Astronomical Society of Canada|volume=103|pages=11|author1=Percy|first1=John R.|last2=Sato|first2=Hiromitsu|year=2009}}</ref>。

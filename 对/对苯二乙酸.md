'''对苯二乙酸'''是一种[[有机化合物|有机化合物]]，[[化学式|化学式]]为C<sub>10</sub>H<sub>10</sub>O<sub>4</sub>。

==制备==
对苯二乙酸可由[[对苯二乙腈|对苯二乙腈]]在[[盐酸|盐酸]]溶液中于100°C水解反应制得。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://pubs.rsc.org/en/Content/ArticleLanding/2011/GC/c1gc15694b|issue=10|doi=10.1039/c1gc15694b|pages=2888|title=Application of biocatalysis towards asymmetric reduction and hydrolytic desymmetrisation in the synthesis of a β-3 receptor agonist|volume=13|journal=Green Chemistry|date=2011|issn=1463-9262|language=en|accessdate=2018-11-30|author=Matthew Badland, Michael P. Burns, Robert J. Carroll, Roger M. Howard, Daniel Laity, Nathan J. Wymer}}</ref>
:NCCH<sub>2</sub>C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>4</sub>CH<sub>2</sub>CN + H<sub>2</sub>O → HOOCCH<sub>2</sub>C<sub>6</sub>H<sub>4</sub>CH<sub>2</sub>COOH

[[对二苄氯|对二苄氯]]和[[一氧化碳|一氧化碳]]的反应也能得到对苯二乙酸。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S0040402013010429|issue=35|doi=10.1016/j.tet.2013.06.083|pages=7264–7268|title=An efficiently cobalt-catalyzed carbonylative approach to phenylacetic acid derivatives|volume=69|journal=Tetrahedron|date=2013-09|issn=0040-4020|accessdate=2018-11-30|author=Meng-yao She, Da-wei Xiao, Bing Yin, Zheng Yang, Ping Liu, Jian-li Li, Zhen Shi}}</ref><ref>Li, Jianli; Pang, Peng; Xiao, Dawei. 2-​(2-​Carboxylphenyl)​benzimidazole cobalt complex and application in synthesis of phenylacetic acid or its derivatives. ''Faming Zhuanli Shenqing'', 2013. 7. 10, CN 103193829 A</ref>

==化学性质==

对苯二乙酸可以和一些过渡金属盐反应，如混合配体的镉配合物的制备<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/cg200961a|issue=10|doi=10.1021/cg200961a|pages=4667–4675|title=Three-Dimensional Cd(II) Coordination Polymers Based on Semirigid Bis(Methylbenzimidazole) and Aromatic Polycarboxylates: Syntheses, Topological Structures and Photoluminescent Properties|volume=11|journal=Crystal Growth & Design|date=2011-10-05|issn=1528-7483|language=EN|accessdate=2018-11-30|author=Chunying Xu, Linke Li, Yinping Wang, Qianqian Guo, Xianjuan Wang, Hongwei Hou, Yaoting Fan}}</ref>；和镧系金属的盐类的反应也有报道，如在溶剂热（水：乙醇＝10：1）的条件下和[[硝酸钕|硝酸钕]]反应，生成[Nd<sub>2</sub>(PDA)<sub>3</sub>(H<sub>2</sub>O)]·2H<sub>2</sub>O。<ref>{{cite journal|url=https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/zaac.200900452|issue=7|doi=10.1002/zaac.200900452|pages=1392–1396|title=Structure and Magnetic Properties of Two Lanthanide 1,4-Phenylenediacetate Compounds|volume=636|journal=Zeitschrift für anorganische und allgemeine Chemie|date=2009-11-27|issn=0044-2313|language=en|accessdate=2018-11-30|author=Zilu Chen, Wei Xiong, Zhong Zhang, Fupei Liang}}</ref>

'''罗伯特·艾伦·维特霍夫'''（{{lang-en|Robert Allen "Rob" Wiethoff}}，{{bd|1976年|9月15日}}），美国男性[[演员|演员]]、[[配音员|配音员]]，因在[[Rockstar_Games|Rockstar Games]]出品的电子游戏《[[荒野大镖客：救赎|荒野大镖客：救赎]]》及续作《[[荒野大镖客：救赎2|荒野大镖客：救赎2]]》中饰演{{le|约翰·马斯顿|John Marston (character)}}而知名。

== 个人生活 ==
维特霍夫于1976年生于美国[[印第安纳州|印第安纳州]][[西摩_(印第安纳州)|西摩]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=Rob Wiethoff|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2147789/|work=IMDb|accessdate=2019-04-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171002033906/http://www.imdb.com/name/nm2147789/|archive-date=2017-10-02|dead-url=no}}</ref>，父亲是医生，而他自己后来在印第安纳大学获得了{{Le|通识学士|Bachelor of General Studies}}学位。<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=The voice of Red Dead: What happened to John Marston|url=https://www.polygon.com/red-dead-redemption/2018/10/22/18010610/red-dead-redemption-john-marston-rob-wiethoff-rdr|work=Polygon|date=2018-10-22|accessdate=2019-04-06|first=Patrick|last=Stafford|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190401021958/https://www.polygon.com/red-dead-redemption/2018/10/22/18010610/red-dead-redemption-john-marston-rob-wiethoff-rdr|archive-date=2019-04-01|dead-url=no}}</ref>在[[洛杉矶|洛杉矶]]参与《荒野大镖客》的制作时，维特霍夫结识了他后来的妻子。他们后来回到西摩，育有两子。<ref name=":0" />

== 演艺事业 ==
维特霍夫在印第安纳大学时曾到洛杉矶旅行一星期，在洛杉矶期间有人把他引荐给一名制片人。在这位制片人的引导下，他前往[[波多黎各|波多黎各]]担任剧组工作，但因为资金短缺等原因最终返回美国。<ref name=":0" />此后直到荒野大镖客系列的机会来临，维特霍夫都只饰演一些小角色。<ref>{{Cite web|title=The Men And Women Behind Gaming's Most Iconic Characters|url=https://kotaku.com/the-men-and-women-behind-gamings-most-iconic-characters-5597081|work=Kotaku|accessdate=2019-04-06|language=en|first=Luke|last=Plunkett|author=|date=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190220141613/https://kotaku.com/the-men-and-women-behind-gamings-most-iconic-characters-5597081|archive-date=2019-02-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>

2008年12月，维特霍夫在回家的路上接到经纪人的电话，经纪人声称有一个在“一部未公开标题的游戏”中试演角色的机会。试演后，维特霍担心不会被接受；但几天后他就接到被录用的通知，这部游戏最后定名为《荒野大镖客：救赎》。<ref>{{Cite web|title=A Crazy, Paranoid Story From Red Dead Redemption's Development|url=https://kotaku.com/a-crazy-paranoid-story-from-red-dead-redemptions-devel-5883252|work=Kotaku|accessdate=2019-04-06|language=en|first=Luke|last=Plunkett|author=|date=|publisher=}}</ref>

维特霍夫在《荒野大镖客：救赎》中的表现好评如潮。2013年[[Game_Informer|Game Informer]]把他评为“十佳游戏配音演员”的第四名<ref>{{Cite web|title=GameInformer.com is currently undergoing maintenance.|url=http://www.gameinformer.com/themes/gi5/construction.html|work=www.gameinformer.com|accessdate=2019-04-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190205174121/http://www.gameinformer.com/themes/gi5/construction.html|archive-date=2019-02-05|dead-url=no}}</ref>，《{{Le|Complex (杂志)|Complex (magazine)|Complex}}》杂志把他列为25名最佳游戏配音的第2名，并称其“以无可置疑的勇敢结束了他的演出”（暗指《荒野大镖客：救赎》的结局）<ref>{{Cite web|title=The 25 Greatest Voice Acting Performances In Video GamesRob Wiethoff|url=https://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2013/04/the-25-greatest-voice-acting-performances-in-video-games/rob-wiethoff|work=Complex|accessdate=2019-04-06|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180923113132/https://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2013/04/the-25-greatest-voice-acting-performances-in-video-games/rob-wiethoff|archive-date=2018-09-23|dead-url=no}}</ref>。

尽管取得了前所未有的成功，罗伯特·维特霍夫之后再无太多主演，更专注于养家糊口。<ref name=":0" />2013年他参与了广播剧《代号天鹅座》的录制。<ref>{{Cite web|title=Red Dead Redemption voice actor joins iOS game|url=https://www.gamespot.com/articles/red-dead-redemption-voice-actor-joins-ios-game/1100-6412653/|work=GameSpot|date=2013-08-07|accessdate=2019-04-06|language=en|first=Eddie|last=Makuch|author=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180613235055/https://www.gamespot.com/articles/red-dead-redemption-voice-actor-joins-ios-game/1100-6412653/|archive-date=2018-06-13|dead-url=no}}</ref>

2016年起，维特霍夫参与《荒野大镖客：救赎2》的制作，继续饰演约翰·马斯顿。<ref>{{Cite web|title=We Played 2 Hours of Red Dead Redemption 2 - Here's What Happened|url=https://www.ign.com/articles/2018/09/20/we-played-2-hours-of-red-dead-redemption-2-heres-what-happened|work=IGN|date=2018-09-20|accessdate=2019-04-06|language=en|first=Joe|last=Skrebels|author=|publisher=|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190220154415/https://www.ign.com/articles/2018/09/20/we-played-2-hours-of-red-dead-redemption-2-heres-what-happened|archive-date=2019-02-20|dead-url=no}}</ref>

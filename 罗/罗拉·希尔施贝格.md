'''罗拉·希尔施贝格'''（{{lang-en|Lora Hirschberg}}，{{bd|1963年}}）美国女[[音频工程师|音频工程师]]。<ref name="dolby">{{Cite web|url=http://www.dolby.com/gb/en/about-us/who-we-are/interviews/movie-interviews/lora-hirschberg-dark-knight.html |title=Peter Cowie talks to Lora Hirschberg About Her Views on Sound for Film |accessdate=2012-12-23 |work=dolby.com}}</ref>她赢得了1次[[奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖|奥斯卡最佳音响效果奖]]，并获得了1次提名。自1990年起，希尔施贝格参与制作了110多部电影。希尔施贝格的同性妻子是劳拉。<ref name="afterellen">{{Cite web|url=http://www.afterellen.com/movies/2011/02/the-oscars-were-a-great-year-for-lesbians-but-better-for-a-king?page=1,1 |title=The Oscars were "a great year for lesbians," but better for a king |accessdate=2012-12-23 |work=afterellen |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130116064102/http://www.afterellen.com/movies/2011/02/the-oscars-were-a-great-year-for-lesbians-but-better-for-a-king?page=1,1 |archivedate=2013-01-16 |deadurl=yes |df= }}</ref>

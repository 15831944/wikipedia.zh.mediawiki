'''罗伯特·约翰森'''（{{lang-no|Robert Johansson}}，{{bd|1990年|3月23日||}}）生于奥普兰郡利勒哈默尔，是一名挪威男子跳台滑雪运动员。罗伯特·约翰森在五岁时跟随父亲开始练习跳台滑雪<ref name="aftenposten">{{cite web|url=https://www.aftenposten.no/100Sport/vintersport/Johansson-var-lei-seg-og-ville-legge-opp-Ett-ar-senere-satte-han-ny-verdensrekord-232502b.html|script-title=no:Johansson var lei seg og ville legge opp. Ett år senere satte han ny verdensrekord.|publisher={{lang|no|Aftenposten}}|date=2017-03-19|accessdate=2019-02-03}}</ref>。他的姐姐琳达同样也是跳台滑雪选手<ref name="aftenposten"/>。他于2013年在[[芬兰|芬兰]][[库萨莫|库萨莫]]完成了个人的世界杯分站赛首秀<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/ski-jumping/athlete-profile-n3026353-robert-johansson.htm|title=Ski Jumping {{!}} Athlete Profile: Robert JOHANSSON|work=Pyeongchang 2018|accessdate=2019-02-03|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180421132228/https://www.pyeongchang2018.com/en/game-time/results/OWG2018/en/ski-jumping/athlete-profile-n3026353-robert-johansson.htm|archivedate=2018-04-21}}</ref>。

罗伯特·约翰森最大的特点为其独特的翘八字胡，他的胡子在2018年平昌冬奥期间成为媒体焦点<ref>{{cite news |author=Josh Peter |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/winter-olympics-2018/2018/02/10/winter-olympics-2018-ryan-johansson-mustache-ski-jumper/326199002/ |title=Winter Olympics 2018 breakout star: Ski jumper Robert Johansson's mustache? |newspaper=USA Today |date=2018-02-10 |accessdate=2018-02-10}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.thelocal.no/20180211/norwegian-wins-moustache-games-for-magnificent-facial-hair |title=Norwegian wins 'Moustache Games' for magnificent facial hair |newspaper=The Local |date=2018-02-11 |agency=AFP |accessdate=2018-02-11 }}</ref>。

== 早年生活 ==
法罗1987年12月19日出生在纽约市，父母分别是女演员[[米亚·法罗|米亚·法罗]]和[[伍迪·艾伦|伍迪·艾伦]]<ref name="nytimes-birth">{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1987/12/22/arts/son-born-to-mia-farrow-and-woody-allen.html|title=Son Born to Mia Farrow And Woody Allen|date=1987-12-22|newspaper=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]]|accessdate=2013-10-02|agency=[[Associated_Press|Associated Press]]}}</ref>。他现在被称为罗南，本名来自名人堂棒球投手[[薩奇·佩吉|薩奇·佩吉]]<ref>{{Cite news | last1 = Corliss | first1 = Richard | first2 = Georgia | last2 = Harbison | title = Woody Allen and Mia Farrow: Scenes From A Breakup | url = http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,976378-2,00.html | work = [[Time_(magazine)|Time]] | date = 1992-08-31 | access-date = 2010-10-01 | archivedate=2014-12-17 | archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20141217071038/http://content.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,976378-2,00.html | deadurl=no}}</ref>和他的外祖母，女演员{{le|莫琳·奥沙利文|Maureen O'Sullivan}}。2013年，米亚·法罗提出猜测歌手兼演员[[法蘭·仙納杜拉|法蘭·仙納杜拉]]是法罗的亲生父亲<ref name="vanityfair.com">{{cite web| url = http://www.vanityfair.com/online/daily/2013/10/mia-farrow-children-family-scandal | title = Mia Farrow and Eight of Her Children Speak Out on Their Lives, Frank Sinatra, and the Scandals They've Endured| work = [[Vanity_Fair_(magazine)|Vanity Fair]] | date=2013-10-02|access-date=2013-10-02|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131002065230/http://www.vanityfair.com/online/daily/2013/10/mia-farrow-children-family-scandal|archivedate=2013-10-02|deadurl=yes}}</ref><ref name="Woody-doubt">{{cite news |url = https://www.nytimes.com/2014/02/09/opinion/sunday/woody-allen-speaks-out.html |title = Woody Allen Speaks Out |last = Allen |first = Woody |date = 2014-02-07 |newspaper = The New York Times}}</ref>。

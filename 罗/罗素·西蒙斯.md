'''罗素·西蒙斯'''（{{lang-en|Russel Simmons}}）是一位美国企业家，[[Yelp|Yelp]]联合创始人兼首席技术官<ref>{{cite news|url=http://tech.fortune.cnn.com/2010/04/09/yelp-grows-a-pair/#more-23147|title=Yelp grows up|last=Smalera|first=Paul|date=April 9, 2010|work=Fortune|publisher=CNN|accessdate=4 August 2010|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100804135310/http://tech.fortune.cnn.com/2010/04/09/yelp-grows-a-pair/#more-23147|archivedate=2010年8月4日}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url = https://techcrunch.com/2010/06/14/russel-simmons-yelp/|title = Yelp Co-Founder And CTO Russel Simmons Is Out|date = 2010-06-14|accessdate = 2013-12-23|website = [[TechCrunch|TechCrunch]]|first = Robin|last = Wauters}}</ref>

==生平==
1998年毕业于[[伊利诺伊大学厄巴纳-香槟分校|伊利诺伊大学厄巴纳-香槟分校]][[计算机科学|计算机科学]]专业，<ref>{{cite news| author =| title =The U. of I. breeding ground| quote = The online review site was founded in 2004 by two U. of I. graduates and former PayPal employees, Jeremy Stoppelman (computer engineering, 1999) and Russel Simmons (computer science, 1998).| newspaper =[[Chicago_Tribune|Chicago Tribune]]| date =April 28, 2012| url =http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2012-04-28/business/ct-biz-0429-bf-startup-u-box-20120428_1_max-levchin-jawed-karim-social-bookmarking-service| accessdate =March 16, 2015}}</ref>后在[[PayPal|PayPal]]担任软件架构师一段时间，2004年10月和[[杰里米·斯托普尔曼|杰里米·斯托普尔曼]]成立[[Yelp|Yelp]]直到2010年6月离开。<ref name=":0" />他是[[PayPal黑手党|PayPal黑手党]]的其中成员之一。

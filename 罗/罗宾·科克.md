'''罗宾·科克'''（Robin Cook，{{bd|1940年|5月4日}}），[[美国|美国]][[医生|医生]]兼[[小说家|小说家]]，生于[[美国|美国]][[纽约|纽约]]<ref name="NYT">{{cite web |last=Fabrikant |first=Geraldine |url=https://www.nytimes.com/1996/01/21/business/talking-money-with-dr-robin-cook-prescription-real-estate-and-lots-of-it.html |title=TALKING MONEY WITH: DR. ROBIN COOK |work=[[The_New_York_Times|The New York Times]] |date=January 21, 1996 |accessdate=February 22, 2017}}</ref>。罗宾·科克写作主题是[[医学|医学]]以及[[公共卫生|公共卫生]]，多部作品曾登上《[[纽约时报|纽约时报]]》[[紐約時報暢銷書榜|畅销书榜]]，作品总销量超过4亿份<ref>[http://www.aeispeakers.com/print.php?SpeakerID=261 AEI Speakers], American Entertainment International Speakers Bureau. "Robin Cook Biography". Second and fifth paragraphs. Retrieved April 8, 2012.</ref>。

罗宾·科克本科毕业于[[维思大学|维思大学]]，后赴[[哥倫比亞大學醫學院|哥伦比亚大学医学院]]攻读[[临床医学|临床医学]]，获[[医学博士|医学博士]]（M.D.）学位。罗宾·科克也曾在[[哈佛大学|哈佛大学]]接受过训练<ref>[http://www.naplesnews.com/news/2012/mar/27/cooking-another-medical-thriller/ Cooking Another Medical Thriller], Naple News. By Sandy Reed. "Q&A about [Robin Cook's] 31st book and much more." Sixth paragraph. March 27, 2012. Retrieved April 7, 2012.</ref>。

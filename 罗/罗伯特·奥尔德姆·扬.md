'''罗伯特·奥尔德姆·扬'''（{{lang-en|Robert Oldham Young}}，{{bd|1952年|3月6日}}）是一名美国理疗师，他和他的[[碱性食物|碱性食物减肥法]]、“酸碱体质”理论<ref name="sickTired9">{{cite book | last=Young | first=Robert | year=2001 | title=Sick and Tired | url=https://books.google.com/?id=I8sL16sztrAC&pg=PA10&lpg=PA10&dq=Young+Robert+Sick+and+Tired+text | pages=9–11 | isbn=1-58054-030-9 | accessdate=2012-09-07}}</ref><ref name="pHMiracle">{{cite book | last=Young | first=Robert | year=2002 | title=The pH Miracle | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=rC7PV5oJNXkC | pages=1–11 | isbn=0-446-52809-9}}</ref><ref name="AboutUs">{{cite web|url=http://www.phmiracleliving.com/t-about.aspx |title=pH Miracle Living, About Us |publisher=Phmiracleliving.com |date=2005-03-31 |accessdate=2012-09-07}}</ref>因一度登上[[奥普拉秀|奥普拉秀]]而出名，节目上他宣称自己治愈了一位罹患[[乳腺癌|乳腺癌]]的、名叫做金·廷卡姆（{{lang|en|Kim Tinkham}}）的女性，但此人实际在节目播出不久之后就因乳腺癌去世<ref>Warner, Anthony. (2017). ''The Angry Chef: Bad Science and the Truth About Healthy Eating'', Oneworld Publications. {{ISBN|9781786072177}}</ref>。

他的理论因缺乏科学证据和实际效果而备受质疑<ref name=Gorski>{{cite web|last1=Gorski|first1=David|title=pH Miracle Living "Dr." Robert O. Young is finally arrested, but will it stop him? « Science-Based Medicine|url=https://www.sciencebasedmedicine.org/ph-miracle-living-dr-robert-o-young-finally-arrested-but-will-it-stop-him/|website=www.sciencebasedmedicine.org|accessdate=27 January 2016|date=27 January 2014}}</ref>，而他本人则于2014年1月因盗窃罪和无证行医罪被捕，2017年6月被判三年零八个月有期徒刑<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nbcsandiego.com/news/local/pH-Miracle-Author-Robert-O-Young-Sentenced-431659933.html|title='pH Miracle' Author Robert O. Young Sentenced|publisher=}}</ref>。2018年11月，圣地亚哥法庭判其赔偿一名癌症患者1.05亿美元<ref >{{cite web| date=2018-11-08 | title=酸碱体质论创始人被罚，1.05亿美元罚款填不上伪科学的坑|url=http://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2018-11-08/doc-ihmutuea8060140.shtml | publisher=新浪财经 | accessdate=2018-11-08}}</ref>。

== 生平==
其個人網站聲稱他在1970年代早期曾獲獎學金在[[猶他大學|猶他大學]]讀生物學和商學，但沒有畢業<ref name="sdu">{{cite web |url=http://www.sandiegouniontribune.com/sdut-criminal-trial-robert-young-ph-miracle-2016feb03-story.html |title=Split verdict in 'pH Miracle' case|newspaper=The San Diego Union-Tribune|date=3 February 2016  |author=Teri Figueroa|accessdate= 19 January 2017}}</ref>。之後前往[[倫敦|倫敦]]為[[耶穌基督後期聖徒教會|耶穌基督後期聖徒教會]]傳教兩年。美國的克萊頓自然健康學院（Clayton College of Natural Health）曾頒給他數個學位（包括一個1995年授予的Ph.D及一個1999年授予的「自然療法博士」（ND）學位），但這實際是一所[[野雞大學|野雞大學]]<ref>{{cite news | url = http://www.tuscaloosanews.com/article/20070211/NEWS/702110399/1007/DATELINE09?p=5&tc=pg | title = State’s diploma mills draw academic ire | first = Adam | last = Jones | work = Tuscaloosa News | date =February 11, 2007 | accessdate = February 14, 2007}} | page=5</ref><ref>In 2008 Clayton's website stated that it was "A non-traditionally accredited school of naturopathy". {{cite web|url=http://www.ccnh.edu/ |title=Educating the Leaders of Natural Health  |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080726152327/http://www.ccnh.edu/|website=Clayton College of Natural Health |date=2008<!--copyright date on Web page-->|archivedate=July 26, 2008}}</ref>。

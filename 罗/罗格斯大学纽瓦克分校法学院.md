'''罗格斯大学纽瓦克分校法学院'''（The School of Law at Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey--Newark，<ref name="Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey--Newark " />简称 Rutgers School of Law–Newark）是一所自1908年起在[[美国|美国]][[新泽西州|新泽西州]][[纽瓦克_(新泽西州)|纽瓦克]]创设的法学院，附属于{{link-en|罗格斯大学纽瓦克分校|Rutgers–Newark}}。<ref name="Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey--Newark ">{{cite web | url=http://grad-schools.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-graduate-schools/top-law-schools/rutgers%2C-the-state-university-of-new-jersey-newark-03098 | title=Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey--Newark | accessdate=2012-10-02 | deadurl=yes | archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120515222159/http://grad-schools.usnews.rankingsandreviews.com/best-graduate-schools/top-law-schools/rutgers,-the-state-university-of-new-jersey-newark-03098 | archivedate=2012-05-15 }}</ref>
该学院在《[[美国新闻与世界报道|美国新闻与世界报道]]》的[[美国法学院排名|法学院排名]]中排名84名。<ref name="Top 2012 Law School Rankings">{{cite web | url=http://www.top-law-schools.com/rankings.html | title=Top 2012 Law School Rankings | accessdate=2012-10-02}}</ref>

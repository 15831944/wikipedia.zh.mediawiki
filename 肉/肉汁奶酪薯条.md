'''肉汁奶酪薯条'''（Poutine，{{IPAc-en|p|uː|ˈ|t|iː|n}}; <small>魁北克法语发音：</small>{{IPA-fr|put͡sɪn||Qc-Poutine.ogg}}）是[[加拿大|加拿大]][[魁北克|魁北克]]<!--魁北克人现在算作一个“nation”，那就提高一下级别吧-->的特色[[快餐|快餐]]料理，做法是在炸[[薯条|薯条]]上加[[乾酪|乾酪]]块，然后浇上肉汁或肉卤。<ref>https://www.thestar.com/news/canada-150/2017/06/23/is-poutine-canadas-national-treasure-or-culinary-appropriation-canadian-myths.html</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://members.shaw.ca/kcic1/poutine.html|title=Poutine 101|last=Knight|first=A.|date=11 June 2007|publisher=Knight's Canadian Info Collection|archive-url=http://arquivo.pt/wayback/20091227205226/http://members.shaw.ca/kcic1/poutine.html|archive-date=27 December 2009|dead-url=yes|accessdate=1 July 2011}}</ref> 有时候还会加入[[火腿|火腿]]，[[香肠|香肠]]或者[[鮭鱼|鮭鱼]]，通常用作配餐。现在在加拿大全境和美国北部都卖。<ref>{{cite web |last=Thorn |first=Bret |date=8 October 2015 |title=Consumers load up on topped fries |website=Nation's Restaurant News |url=http://nrn.com/food-trends/consumers-load-topped-fries}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=McGrath |first=Charles |title=In Cape Breton, a Rugged Golf Getaway |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2013/06/09/travel/in-cape-breton-a-rugged-golf-getaway.html?pagewanted=all |accessdate=1 July 2013 |newspaper=The New York Times |date=21 June 2013}}</ref><ref name="jamieoliver.com">{{cite web |last=Garrett |first1=Jonny |date=14 October 2014 |title=Tips for the perfect poutine |website=JamieOliver.com |url=http://www.jamieoliver.com/news-and-features/features/tips-perfect-poutine/}}</ref>

現時芝士淋肉汁薯條的發源地仍然有爭議。魁北克省內的[[德拉蒙市|德拉蒙市]]（Drummondville）、維多利亞市（Victoriaville）及黎瑞雷聖約翰市（Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu）各有一間食肆自稱為魁北克美食芝士淋肉汁薯條的發源地。

此外，俄罗斯总统[[弗拉基米尔·弗拉基米罗维奇·普京|弗拉基米尔·弗拉基米罗维奇·普京]]的姓名在法语中也写作Poutine。<ref>{{cite press release |title=Putin/Poutine |date=3 April 2005 |url=http://www.nytimes.com/2005/04/03/magazine/03ONLANGUAGE.html?_r=0 |accessdate=14 January 2013}}</ref>{{notetag|因為發音問題，要寫成 Poutine，否則就會跟脏话[[:en:wiktionary:putain|Putain]]同音。}}

这道菜最开始起源于50年代左右。但对于起源的地区目前还存有争议。

'''肉碱3-脱氢酶'''（{{lang-en|carnitine 3-dehydrogenase}}，[[EC编号|EC]] [http://enzyme.expasy.org/EC/1.1.1.108 1.1.1.108]），是一种以[[烟酰胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸|NAD<sup>+</sup>]]或[[烟酰胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸磷酸|NADP<sup>+</sup>]]为[[受体_(生物化学)|受体]]、作用于[[供体|供体]][[次甲基|CH]]-[[羟基|OH]][[基团|基团]]上的[[氧化还原酶|氧化还原酶]]。这种[[酶|酶]]能[[催化|催化]]以下[[酶促反应|酶促反应]]：

:[[肉碱|肉碱]] + [[烟酰胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸|NAD<sup>+</sup>]] <math>\rightleftharpoons</math> 3-[[脱氢肉碱|脱氢肉碱]] + NADH + [[氢离子|H<sup>+</sup>]]

上述反应是[[肉碱|L-肉碱]][[生物降解|生物降解]]过程的第一步，<ref>{{cite journal | author = Hanschmann H, Ehricht R, Kleber HP. | title = Purification and properties of L(-)-carnitine dehydrogenase from ''Agrobacterium sp.'' | journal = Biochim Biophys Acta. | volume = 1290 | issue = 2 | pages = 177-183 | pmid = 8645721 | url = http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/8645721 |date=Jun 4, 1996}}</ref>该反应被用于L-肉碱生产以及量化地鉴定[[生物|生物]][[体液|体液]]中L-肉碱的水平。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Clemens Uanschou, Roswitha Frieht, and Fritz Pittner | title = What to Learn from a Comparative Genomic Sequence Analysis of L-Carnitine Dehydrogenase | journal = Monatsh. Chem. | volume = 136 | issue = 8 | pages = 1365-1381 | year = 2005 | doi = 10.1007/s00706-005-0331-x | url = http://www.springerlink.com/content/kn5531797l238128/ }}</ref>从[[根瘤菌|根瘤菌]]（''Rhizobium sp.''）和[[半透明黄单胞菌|半透明黄单胞菌]]（''Xanthomonas translucens''）[[细胞|细胞]]中分别提取出的肉碱3-脱氢酶Rs-CDH和Xt-CDH的[[相对分子质量|相对分子质量]]分别为50[[原子质量单位|kDa]]和37kDa，它们的[[蛋白质一级结构|初级结构]]与[[3-羟酰辅酶A脱氢酶|3-羟酰辅酶A脱氢酶]]有高度的相似性。<ref>{{cite journal | author = Arima J, Uesumi A, Mitsuzumi H, Mori N. | title = Biochemical characterization of L-carnitine dehydrogenases from ''izobium sp.'' and ''Xanthomonas translucens''. | journal = Biosci Biotechnol Biochem. | year = 2010 | volume = 74 | issue = 6 | pages = 1237-1242 | pmid = 20530902 | url = http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20530902 }}</ref>

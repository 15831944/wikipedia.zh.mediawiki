'''肉汁清湯'''(Bouillon)，在[[法國飲食文化|法國飲食文化]]裡面，是指[[高湯|高湯]]。<ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=edPzm5KSMmYC&pg=PA300&lpg=PA300&dq=Bouillon+broth&source=bl&ots=2dWs1UlmsU&sig=mfGPNDnUUdU8O1H2d3-2_qk44PA&hl=en&sa=X&ei=PIowUK2bOe7hiwLP74GwBQ&ved=0CDMQ6AEwAA#v=onepage&q=Bouillon%20broth&f=false Understanding Food: Principles and Preparation - Amy Christine Brown - Google Books<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref><ref>[http://books.google.com/books?id=AkUC8txOpJIC&pg=PA186&lpg=PA186&dq=Bouillon+broth&source=bl&ots=Xvb9heEctc&sig=VGcUenlt2S8zgsYNR0cogWa_Alk&hl=en&sa=X&ei=PIowUK2bOe7hiwLP74GwBQ&ved=0CEsQ6AEwBA#v=onepage&q=Bouillon%20broth&f=false Cookery for the Hospitality Industry - Graham Dodgshun, Michel Peters, David O'Dea - Google Books<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>這名字的來源是動詞''bouillir''，代表''將…煮沸''。<!--It is usually made by the simmering of [[Mirepoix_(烹飪)|''mirepoix'']] and aromatic herbs (一般使用[[香草束|香草束]]，''bouquet garni'') with either [[牛肉|牛肉]], [[小牛肉|小牛肉]], or [[家禽|家禽]]的骨頭 bones and/or with [[蝦|蝦]], or [[蔬菜|蔬菜]] in boiling water.
-->
注意這裡所說不是[[海地|海地]]的''bouillon soup''或者[[高湯|高湯]]（stock）。高湯與肉汁清湯的作法相似，但是在法式料理的目的不同。

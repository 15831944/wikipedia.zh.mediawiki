'''士嘉堡－愛靜閣選區'''（{{lang-en|Scarborough—Agincourt}}）是一個位於[[加拿大|加拿大]][[安大略省|安大略省]][[多倫多|多倫多]]的聯邦選區，西起維多利亞公園大道（{{lang|en|Victoria Park Avenue}}），北及[[士刁士大道|士刁士大道]]（即多倫多市和[[約克區|約克區]]的界線），東至密蘭大道（{{lang|en|Midland Avenue}}），南臨[[安大略401號省道|安大略401號省道]]<ref name="parlinfo"/><ref>{{cite web|title=Scarborough—Agincourt Map -Elections Canada|url=http://www.elections.ca/res/cir/maps2/mapprov.asp?map=35093&lang=e|website=www.elections.ca|publisher=Elections Canada}}</ref>，包含士刁士、[[愛靜閣|愛靜閣]]和[[美麗徑|美麗徑]]等[[士嘉堡|士嘉堡]]社區。

此選區於1987年從約克－士嘉堡選區分割而成<ref name="parlinfo">{{cite web|title=History of Federal Ridings since 1867: Scarborough—Agincourt |url=https://lop.parl.ca/About/Parliament/FederalRidingsHistory/hfer.asp?Language=E&Search=Det&Include=Y&rid=961 |publisher=[[加拿大國會|加拿大國會]]圖書館 |accessdate=2017-09-15 }}</ref>，1988年至今在[[加拿大國會|加拿大國會]][[加拿大眾議院|眾議院]]駐有議席。加拿大聯邦選區從2012年起重新分配，此選區的界線出現變動，密蘭大道以東範圍改撥新成立的[[士嘉堡北選區|士嘉堡北選區]]<ref name=report>{{Citation|url=http://www.redecoupage-federal-redistribution.ca/content.asp?section=on&dir=now/reports&document=index&lang=e|title=Final Report – Ontario}}</ref><ref name=timeline>{{Citation|url=http://www.redecoupage-federal-redistribution.ca/content.asp?section=info&document=index&dir=overview&lang=e|title=Timeline for the Redistribution of Federal Electoral Districts}}</ref>，[[2015年加拿大聯邦大選|2015年聯邦大選]]起生效。

==人口資料==
士嘉堡－愛靜閣選區有多項全國之冠，包括移民佔選區人口最多（67.8%）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www12.statcan.gc.ca/census-recensement/2006/dp-pd/tbt/Rp-eng.cfm?LANG=E&APATH=3&DETAIL=0&DIM=0&FL=A&FREE=0&GC=0&GID=0&GK=0&GRP=1&PID=89444&PRID=0&PTYPE=88971,97154&S=0&SHOWALL=0&SUB=0&Temporal=2006&THEME=72&VID=0&VNAMEE=&VNAMEF= |title=Immigrant Status and Place of Birth (38), Sex (3) and Age Groups (10) for the Population of Canada, Provinces, Territories and Federal Electoral Districts (2003 Representation Order), 2006 Census - 20% Sample Data  |publisher=2.statcan.gc.ca |accessdate=2012-11-23}}</ref>、來自亞洲和中東的移民佔選區人口最多（53.0%）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www12.statcan.ca/census-recensement/2006/ref/dict/app-ann010-eng.cfm#appj9 |title=Appendix J Comparison of places of birth disseminated in 2006, 2001 and 1996 |publisher=[[加拿大統計局|加拿大統計局]] |date=2009-11-20 |accessdate=2012-11-30}}</ref>、來自中國大陸的移民佔選區人口最多（24.7%）、以及在家中使用華語（沒有指明何種方言）的居民佔選區人口最多（12.0%）<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www12.statcan.gc.ca/census-recensement/2011/dp-pd/tbt-tt/Rp-eng.cfm?TABID=2&LANG=E&APATH=3&DETAIL=1&DIM=0&FL=A&FREE=0&GC=10001&GK=4&GRP=1&PID=103281&PRID=0&PTYPE=101955&S=0&SHOWALL=0&SUB=0&Temporal=2011&THEME=90&VID=0&VNAMEE=&VNAMEF= |title=First Official Language Spoken (7), Detailed Language Spoken Most Often at Home (232), Age Groups (17A) and Sex (3) for the Population Excluding Institutional Residents of Canada, Provinces, Territories and Federal Electoral Districts (2003 Representation Order), 2011 Census |publisher=[[加拿大統計局|加拿大統計局]] |accessdate=2012-11-19}}</ref>。

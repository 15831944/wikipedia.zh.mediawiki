'''計算神學''' (Computational theology) 或'''計算宗教學'''(Computational religion)或'''計算創造論'''(Computational creationism)是一個[[Prototype|原生]]的[[學術|學術]][[研究|研究]]，旨在透過[[電腦科學|電腦科學]]及[[計算機科技|計算機科技]]來了解及解釋[[神學|神學]]及[[宗教|宗教]]現象。<ref>[http://tuvalu.santafe.edu/~phooper/Hooper2012ReligionCommentary.pdf Modeling the evolution of religious institutions] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140727004510/http://tuvalu.santafe.edu/~phooper/Hooper2012ReligionCommentary.pdf |date=2014-07-27 }}, Religion, Brain & Behavior</ref><ref>[http://www.ingentaconnect.com/content/brill/mtsr/2012/00000024/00000003/art00004 Computing Religion: A New Tool in the Multilevel Analysis of Religion], Method & Theory in the Study of Religion, Volume 24, Number 3, 2012 , pp. 267-290(24)</ref>

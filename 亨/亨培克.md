'''亨培克'''，又譯斯坦利·霍恩貝克（{{lang-en|Stanley Kuhl Hornbeck}}，{{bd|1883年|5月4日|1966年|12月10日}}<ref>{{findagrave|49207251}}</ref>），二戰時美國外交官、[[遠東問題|遠東問題]]（對中、對日關係）教授。[[美國國務院|美國國務院]]遠東事務處處長（1928年–1937）、[[美國國務卿|美國國務卿]][[科德尔·赫尔|科德尔·赫尔]]特別顧問（1937–1944）、美國駐荷蘭大使（1944–1947）。[[牛津大學|牛津大學]][[羅德獎學金|羅德獎學金]]學生。曾在美國與中國大學任教，著書八本。

==珍珠港==

儘管一生在美國政府服務出色，他在日本[[偷襲珍珠港|偷襲珍珠港]]前10天執筆的備忘錄錯誤分析日本不可能向美開戰，甚為聞名。事緣年輕的美國外交部官員[[Charles_W._Yost|Charles W. Yost]]問及美國對日本石油禁運，亨培克寫道：

{{cquote|In the opinion of the undersigned, the Japanese Government does not desire or intend or expect to have forthwith armed conflict with the United States.... Were it a matter of placing bets, the undersigned would give odds of five to one that the Japan and the United States will not be at "war" on or before March 1 (a date more than 90 days from now, and after the period during which it has been estimated by our strategists that it would be to our advantage for us to have "time" for further preparation and disposals).<ref>{{cite book|title=Pearl Harber: Warning and Decision pp.264-265|author=Wohlstetter, Roberta|publisher=''[[Stanford_University_Press|Stanford University Press]]''|year=1962}}</ref>}}

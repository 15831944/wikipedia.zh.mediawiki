'''亨利·塔庫·普納'''（{{lang-en|Henry Tuakeu Puna}}<ref>[http://www.stats.gov.ck/NewsEvents/Rolls/22Manihiki.pdf Parliamentary General Election – Main Electoral Roll – Manihiki] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101001070142/http://www.stats.gov.ck/NewsEvents/Rolls/22Manihiki.pdf |date=2010-10-01 }}, Cook Islands Registrar of Electors, 10 May 2010.</ref>；{{bd|1949年|8月6日|}}<ref name=cinsworn/>），是一名[[庫克群島|庫克群島]]政治人物。

他在{{le|2004年庫克群島大選|Cook Islands general election, 2004}}{{le|曼尼希基 (庫克群島議會選區)|Manihiki (Cook Islands electorate)|曼尼希基選區}}首次參選，競逐前總理{{le|罗伯特·温顿|Robert Woonton}}的議席<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=11968 |title=Cooks opposition warns of prime minister losing seat over pearl industry decline |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2004-09-06 |accessdate=2010-01-05}}</ref>，最初他以微弱劣勢敗選<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=12106 |title=Cooks PM Woonton retains seat |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2004-09-14 |accessdate=2010-01-05}}</ref>，但他提出選舉呈請挑戰結果<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=12307 |title=Cook Islands prime minister named in election petition |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2004-09-23 |accessdate=2010-01-05}}</ref>。後來呈請決定維持原判，但因為有部分選民喪失投票資格，令重新點票結果出現平局<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=13735 |title=Cooks to vote for new Prime Minister after election draw leads to by-election |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2004-12-12 |accessdate=2010-01-05}}</ref>，需要進行{{le|2005年曼尼希基補選|Manihiki by-election 2005|補選}}；補選中普納終於勝出<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=14781 |title=Preliminary results show Puna wins Cook Islands by-election |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2005-02-10 |accessdate=2010-01-05}}</ref>，唯{{le|2006年庫克群島大選|Cook Islands general election, 2006|2006年大選}}裡他的議席敗給{{le|阿皮伊·皮奧|Apii Piho}}<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=27171 |title=Leader of Cook Islands Party at a loss over election result |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2006-10-01 |accessdate=2010-01-05}}</ref>

他在{{le|2010年庫克群島大選|Cook Islands general election, 2010|2010年大選}}再次當選為曼尼希基選區議員，並在11月30日宣誓就任[[庫克群島總理列表|庫克群島總理]]<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rnzi.com/pages/news.php?op=read&id=57367 |title=New Cook Islands PM sworn in |publisher=Radio New Zealand International |date=2010-11-30 |accessdate=2010-12-01}}</ref>。

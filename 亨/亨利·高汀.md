'''亨利·高汀'''（{{lang-en|Henry Golding}}；{{bd|1987年|2月5日||}}）是一名[[馬來西亞|馬來西亞]][[演員|演員]]、[[模特兒|模特兒]]及[[主持|主持]]<ref>{{cite web|author=Dave McNary |url=https://variety.com/2017/film/news/henry-golding-simple-favor-blake-lively-anna-kendrick-1202507301/ |title=Henry Golding Signs for 'Simple Favor' Opposite Blake Lively, Anna Kendrick |publisher=[[Variety_(magazine)|Variety]] |date=26 July 2017 |accessdate=27 July 2017}}</ref><ref name="star1">{{cite web|url=http://www.star2.com/entertainment/movies/movie-news/2017/07/27/henry-golding-to-star-alongside-blake-lively/ |title=Henry Golding to star alongside Blake Lively |publisher=Star2.com |date= |accessdate=27 July 2017}}</ref>。2014年，亨利主持[[英國廣播公司世界新聞頻道|BBC]]的旅遊節目。<ref name="The Travel Show on BBC">{{Cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/profiles/1h0sVft438P3G3c6xVnXJnJ/henry-golding|title=The Travel Show - Henry Golding - BBC World News|website=BBC|language=en-GB|access-date=2018-06-07}}</ref>他以在《[[瘋狂亞洲富豪|瘋狂亞洲富豪]]》中飾演楊尼克而為人熟知。

== 個人生活 ==
=== 婚姻 ===

=== 家庭 ===

'''喬安·佛洛格'''（{{lang-en|'''Joanne Froggatt'''}}，{{bd|1980年|8月23日|catIdx=Froggatt, Joanne}}）是一位[[英国|英国]]女[[演員|演員]]。她因演出[[英國|英國]]熱門電視劇《[[唐顿庄园|唐顿庄园]]》中的女僕長施安娜（Anna Smith）而為人所熟悉。<ref>[https://variety.com/2017/tv/news/joanne-froggatt-downton-abbey-movie-1202510391/ “Joanne Froggatt on ‘Downton Abbey’ Movie: ‘Nothing Has Happened‘“]. Variety. 29-07-2017.</ref>她亦因此角色以贏得[[第72屆金球獎|第72屆金球獎]]最佳電視劇女配角獎項。<ref>[https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/tv/news/golden-globes-2015-downton-abbey-star-joanne-froggatt-wins-for-role-in-rape-storyline-9971414.html “Joanne Froggatt: Downton Abbey star wins Golden Globe for role in rape storyline”].

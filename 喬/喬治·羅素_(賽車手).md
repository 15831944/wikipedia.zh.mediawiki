'''喬治·羅素'''（{{lang-en|George Russell}}，{{bd|1998年|2月15日}}）是一名[[英國|英國]][[賽車手|賽車手]]，现为[[一级方程式|一级方程式]][[威廉姆斯车队|威廉姆斯车队]]的车手。

==職業生涯==

===卡丁車===
羅素生於[[英格蘭|英格蘭]][[諾福克郡|諾福克郡]][[金斯林|金斯林]]，<ref name="Russell">{{cite news|title=About George Russell|publisher=George Russell|work=georgerussellracing.com|url=http://www.georgerussellracing.com/about.html|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141215003947/http://www.georgerussellracing.com/about.html|archivedate=2014-12-15|deadurl=yes}}</ref>自2006年開始駕駛[[卡丁車|卡丁車]]，並在2009年晉升至兒童組級別，贏得{{le|英國賽車協會|Motor Sports Association}}錦標賽冠軍及英國公開賽冠軍。<ref name="MSA Cadet Report 2009">{{cite web|url=http://www.formulakartstars.com/2009-formula-kart-stars-season-review-msa-british-cadet-champion-george-russell/|title=2009 Formula Kart Stars Season Review: MSA British Cadet Champion George Russell|website=FormulaKartStars.com|publisher=Formula Kart Stars|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20130123193220/http://www.formulakartstars.com/2009-formula-kart-stars-season-review-msa-british-cadet-champion-george-russell/|archivedate=2013-01-23|deadurl=yes}}</ref>2010年，他轉戰羅泰克斯（{{lang|en|Rotax}}）卡丁車挑戰賽Mini Max級別，贏得英國超級一（{{lang|en|Super One British}}）、英國卡丁車明星方程式（{{lang|en|Formula Kart Stars British}}）及卡丁車大師英國大獎賽（{{lang|en|Kartmasters British Grand Prix}}）冠軍。羅素在2011年晉升至{{le|KF3|KF3}}級別，贏得美國超級卡丁車國際大賽（{{lang|en|Superkarts! USA SuperNationals}}）冠軍，並連續兩年蟬聯國際卡丁車協會（{{lang|en|CIK-FIA}}）歐洲冠軍。<ref>{{cite news|title=CIK-FIA European KF3 Championship 2012 standings|work=driverdb.com|publisher=Driver Database|url=https://www.driverdb.com/championships/standings/european-championship---kf3/2012/|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140723063547/https://www.driverdb.com/championships/standings/european-championship---kf3/2012/|archivedate=2014-07-23|deadurl=no}}</ref>2013年，羅素在{{le|KF1|KF1}} CIK-FIA世界錦標賽名列第19名，並結束了卡丁車職業生涯。<ref name="Russell"/><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.formula4.com/drivers/2014-drivers/george-russell.aspx|title=Driver: George Russell|work=BRDC Formula 4 Championship|publisher=British Racing Drivers' Club|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20141208225452/http://www.formula4.com/drivers/2014-drivers/george-russell.aspx|archivedate=2014-12-08|deadurl=yes}}</ref>

===雷諾方程式2.0===
2014年，羅素首次駕駛[[開輪式賽車|單人座賽車]]，參賽[[雷諾方程式2.0北歐盃|雷諾方程式2.0北歐盃]]錦標賽。最初，他先與{{le|普瑞馬車隊|Prema Powerteam}}簽下一紙合約，<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.paddockscout.com/prema-sign-russell-bosak-for-formula-renault-alps-campaign/|title=Prema sign Russell, Bosak for Formula Renault Alps campaign|last=Khorounzhiy|first=Valentin|date=2014-03-10|publisher=Paddock Scout|work=paddockscout.com|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140317225747/http://www.paddockscout.com/prema-sign-russell-bosak-for-formula-renault-alps-campaign/|archivedate=2014-03-17|deadurl=no}}</ref>但是，卻在賽季開始前幾日轉為效力{{le|科伊蘭寧車隊|Koiranen GP}}。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.paddockscout.com/russell-and-tang-set-to-move-teams-ahead-of-alps-opener/|title=Russell and Tang set to move teams ahead of Alps opener|last=Allen|first=Peter|date=2014-04-01|publisher=Paddock Scout|work=paddockscout.com|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160306094838/http://www.paddockscout.com/russell-and-tang-set-to-move-teams-ahead-of-alps-opener|archivedate=2016-03-06|deadurl=no}}</ref>雖然因病缺席蒙札分站，但他仍在年度積分榜取得第4名，並有一次在[[紅牛賽道|紅牛賽道]]頒獎台完賽的成績。<ref>{{cite news|title=Formula Renault 2.0 Alps 2014 standings|work=driverdb.com|publisher=Driver Database|url=https://www.driverdb.com/championships/standings/formula-renault-20-alps/2014/|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303200753/https://www.driverdb.com/championships/standings/formula-renault-20-alps/2014/|archivedate=2016-03-03|deadurl=no}}</ref>

羅素也以[[外卡|外卡]]車手的身分參與了[[雷諾方程式2.0歐洲盃|雷諾方程式2.0歐洲盃]]的兩場分站賽事，分別為科伊蘭寧及{{le|Tech 1車隊|Tech 1 Racing}}出賽{{le|莫斯科賽道|Moscow Raceway|莫斯科}}與賽季收官站[[赫雷斯賽道|赫雷斯]]分站。其中，他在赫雷斯更以[[桿位|桿位]]起跑奪冠。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.paddockscout.com/guest-driver-george-russell-delivers-tech-1s-first-eurocup-win-of-2014/|title=Guest driver George Russell delivers Tech 1's first Eurocup win of 2014|last=Khorounzhiy|first=Valentin|date=2014-10-19|publisher=Paddock Scout|work=paddockscout.com|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170202145627/http://www.paddockscout.com/guest-driver-george-russell-delivers-tech-1s-first-eurocup-win-of-2014|archivedate=2017-02-02|deadurl=no}}</ref>

===四級方程式賽車===
2014年，羅素也參戰[[英國車手俱樂部四級方程式錦標賽|英國車手俱樂部四級方程式錦標賽]]，效力於衛冕冠軍蘭南車隊（{{lang|en|Lanan Racing}}）。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.formula4.com/news/multiple-karting-champion-george-russell-joins-f4-with-lanan-racing.aspx|title=Multiple Karting champion George Russell joins F4 with Lanan|work=BRDC Formula 4 Championship|publisher=British Racing Drivers' Club|date=2013-12-06|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20140114180318/http://www.formula4.com/news/multiple-karting-champion-george-russell-joins-f4-with-lanan-racing.aspx|archivedate=2014-01-14|deadurl=yes}}</ref>他與隊友{{le|阿瓊·馬伊尼|Arjun Maini}}、HHC車隊（{{lang|en|HHC Motorsport}}）雙雄{{le|塞南·菲爾丁|Sennan Fielding}}及{{link-de|拉烏爾·海曼|Raoul Hyman}}，在賽季收官站{{le|斯內特頓賽道|Snetterton Circuit|斯內特頓}}爭奪年度冠軍。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.formula4.com/news/race-three-title-permutations.aspx|title=Race Three Title Permutations|work=BRDC Formula 4 Championship|publisher=British Racing Drivers' Club|date=2014-10-26|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://archive.is/20141208225441/http://www.formula4.com/news/race-three-title-permutations.aspx|archivedate=2014-12-08|deadurl=yes}}</ref>自桿位起跑後，羅素贏得贏得他本賽季的第四座分站冠軍，同時以3分之差擊敗馬伊尼，取得該年度的車手冠軍。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.paddockscout.com/george-russell-wins-brdc-f4-title-decider-to-steal-crown/|title=George Russell wins BRDC F4 title decider to steal crown|last=Allen|first=Peter|date=2014-10-26|publisher=Paddock Scout|work=paddockscout.com|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170212091209/http://www.paddockscout.com/george-russell-wins-brdc-f4-title-decider-to-steal-crown|archivedate=2017-02-12|deadurl=no}}</ref>

拿下英國車手俱樂部四級方程式錦標賽冠軍後，羅素獲得為{{le|雅頓車隊|Arden International}}至[[阿布達比|阿布達比]][[亞斯碼頭賽道|亞斯碼頭賽道]]測試[[GP3系列賽|GP3賽車]]的機會。<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.paddockscout.com/ceccon-leads-jones-and-russell-as-gp3-test-begins/|title=Ceccon leads Jones and Russell as GP3 test begins|last=Allen|first=Peter|date=2014-11-27|publisher=Paddock Scout|work=paddockscout.com|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160306163923/http://www.paddockscout.com/ceccon-leads-jones-and-russell-as-gp3-test-begins|archivedate=2016-03-06|deadurl=no}}</ref>2014年12月，羅素擊敗{{le|亞歷山大·阿爾邦|Alexander Albon}}、{{le|本·巴尼科特|Ben Barnicoat}}、塞南·菲爾丁、{{le|塞布·莫里斯|Seb Morris}}及{{le|哈里森·斯科特|Harrison Scott}}等入圍車手，成為有史以來最年輕的{{le|麥拉倫汽車運動BRDC獎|McLaren Autosport BRDC Award}}得主，將獲得10萬[[英鎊|英鎊]]的獎金、{{le|英國車手俱樂部|British Racing Drivers' Club}}會員資格，以及為[[麥拉倫車隊|麥拉倫車隊]]參與[[一級方程式賽車|一級方程式賽車]]測試的機會。<ref>{{cite news|title=George Russell wins 2014 McLaren AUTOSPORT BRDC Award|url=http://www.autosport.com/news/report.php/id/117079|first=Scott|last=Mitchell|work=Autosport|publisher=Haymarket Publications|date=2014-12-07|accessdate=2014-12-08|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303212734/http://www.autosport.com/news/report.php/id/117079|archivedate=2016-03-03|deadurl=no}}</ref>

2015年2月，羅素入選為英國車手俱樂部超級之星（{{lang|en|SuperStars}}）計畫的12名車手之一，是該計畫有史以來最年輕的入選車手。<ref>{{cite news|title=BRDC announces 2015 SuperStars|url=http://www.brdc.co.uk/BRDC-Announces-2015-SuperStars|work=brdc.co.uk|publisher=British Racing Drivers' Club|date=2015-02-23|accessdate=2015-04-08|quote=I am particularly delighted to announce five new SuperStars this year and welcome our youngest ever recruit George Russell to the programme...|language=en|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160324110704/http://www.brdc.co.uk/BRDC-Announces-2015-SuperStars|archivedate=2016-03-24|deadurl=no}}</ref>

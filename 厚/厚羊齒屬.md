'''厚羊齒屬'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Pachypteris'''''}}），又名'''厚葉蕨屬'''、'''厚葉種子蕨屬'''，是一[[屬|屬]]已[[滅絕|滅絕]]的[[種子蕨|種子蕨]]<ref>{{Cite book | last1 = Taylor| first1 = Thomas N. | last2 = Taylor | first2 = Edith L. | last3 = Krings | first3 = Michael | title = Paleobotany: The Biology and Evolution of Fossil Plants| publisher = Elsevier Science | year = 2008 | location = | pages = 695–696| url = | doi = | id = | isbn = 978-0-12-373972-8}}</ref>，生存於早[[三疊紀|三疊紀]]至晚[[白堊紀|白堊紀]]。它們生長在熱帶森林中。其[[化石|化石]]分布於全世界。<ref name="Fossils">{{cite book | title=Fossils | publisher=[[Dorling_Kindersley|Dorling Kindersley]] | author={{link-en|西里爾·沃克 (古生物學家)|Cyril Walker (palaeontologist)|Walker, Cyril}} | last2=Ward | first2=David | authorlink2=David Ward (palaeontologist) | series=Smithsonian Handbooks | year=2002 | page=299 | isbn=0789489848 | edition=revised, illustrated}}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
厚羊齒的典型植株高度約為2[[公尺|公尺]]。小[[複葉|複葉]]有著規則和不規則的裂片外觀及厚實的[[角質層|角質層]]。它們是最後一批種子蕨，在白堊紀晚期宣告絕滅。<ref name="Fossils" />

'''盔菊石'''（[[學名|學名]]：{{lang|la|'''''Hoplites'''''}}）是生存在早[[白堊紀|白堊紀]][[海洋|海洋]]中的一[[屬|屬]][[菊石|菊石]]。<ref name=Amedro2014>Amédro, F., Matrion, B., Magniez-Jannin, F., & Touch, R. (2014). La limite Albien inférieur-Albien moyen dans l’Albien type de l’Aube (France): ammonites, foraminifères, séquences. Revue de Paléobiologie, 33(1), 159-279.</ref>牠們是[[盔菊石超科|盔菊石超科]]的代表屬。<ref name="Hoplites">{{Cite web |url = http://liusir.pixnet.net/blog/post/248636420-hoplites-%E7%9B%94%E8%8F%8A%E7%9F%B3-%28%E6%B3%95%E5%9C%8B%29 |title = Hoplites 盔菊石 (法國) @ 菊海神遊 Swimming with Ammonites :: 痞客邦 :: |language = zh-hant |accessdate = 2018-06-06 }}</ref>其[[化石|化石]]被發現於[[歐洲|歐洲]]、[[格陵蘭|格陵蘭]]、[[墨西哥|墨西哥]]和[[阿根廷|阿根廷]]等地。<ref name="Fossilworks">{{Cite web |url = http://fossilworks.org/bridge.pl?a=taxonInfo&taxon_no=15010 |title = Fossilworks: Hoplites |language = en |accessdate = 2018-06-06 }}</ref>

== 特徵 ==
盔菊石的殼表有著粗壯的[[V|V]]形肋條，其末端明顯隆起，基部則有膨大的結節。殼體兩側的肋條末端互相交錯排列，於腹側中央則不相連，由一淺溝隔開。殼口為特殊的類[[六邊形|六邊形]]。<ref name="Hoplites" /><ref name=Wright1996>Wright, C. W. (1996). Treatise on Invertebrate Paleontology, Part L, Mollusca 4: Cretaceous Ammonoidea (with contributions by JH Calloman (sic) and MK Howarth). Geological Survey of America and University of Kansas, Boulder, Colorado, and Lawrence, Kansas, 362.</ref>

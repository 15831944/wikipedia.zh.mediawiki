'''表本胆烷醇酮'''（{{lang-en|Epietiocholanolone}}，也叫做'''3β-羟-5β-雄烷-17-酮''' 3β-hydroxy-5β-androstan-17-one，或者'''本胆烷-3β-醇-17-酮''' '''etiocholan-3β-ol-17-one'''）是一种[[本胆烷|本胆烷]]（5β-雄烷）衍生的[[甾体|甾体]]物质，也是[[睾酮|睾酮]]在[[肝脏|肝脏]]中的主要[[代谢产物|代谢产物]]<ref name="NagrathMalhotra2012">{{cite book|author1=Arun Nagrath|author2=Narendra Malhotra|author3=Seth Shikha|title=Progress in Obstetrics & Gynecology|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eVZ58V9C3U4C&pg=PA265|date=31 July 2012|publisher=JP Medical Ltd|isbn=978-93-5025-779-1|pages=265–}}</ref><ref name="HorskyPresl2012">{{cite book|author1=J. Horsky|author2=J. Presl|title=Ovarian Function and its Disorders: Diagnosis and Therapy|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=7IrpCAAAQBAJ&pg=PA107|date=6 December 2012|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=978-94-009-8195-9|pages=107–}}</ref><ref name="Robert2010">{{cite book|author=MORFIN Robert|title=Les stéroïdes naturels de A à Z|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=SZ31AQAAQBAJ&pg=PA428|date=20 December 2010|publisher=Lavoisier|isbn=978-2-7430-1918-1|pages=428–}}</ref>。其[[代謝途徑|代謝途徑]]为睾酮通过[[5β还原酶|5β还原酶]]转化为[[5β-双氢睾酮|5β-双氢睾酮]]<ref name="NagrathMalhotra2012" />，5β-双氢睾酮再通过[[3β-羟基类固醇脱氢酶|3β-羟基类固醇脱氢酶]]转化为[[3β-本胆烷二醇|3β,5β-雄烷二醇]]（又叫3β-本胆烷二醇），3β-本胆烷二醇再通过[[17β-羟基类固醇脱氢酶|17β-羟基类固醇脱氢酶]]转化为表本胆烷醇酮<ref name="Robert2010" />。另外，[[5β-雄烷二酮|5β-雄烷二酮]]也可通过[[3β-羟基类固醇脱氢酶|3β-羟基类固醇脱氢酶]]转化为表本胆烷醇酮<ref name="Robert2010" /><ref name="MakinGower2010">{{cite book|author1=Hugh L. J. Makin|author2=D.B. Gower|title=Steroid Analysis|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=lzdsEhKWb1gC&pg=PA462|date=4 June 2010|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=978-1-4020-9775-1|pages=462–}}</ref>。在肝脏中，表本胆烷醇酮经过{{le|葡萄苷酸化|glucuronidation}}和{{le|硫酸化|sulfation}}增强了水溶性，再经由[[尿液|尿液]][[排泄作用|排出体外]]<ref name="Robert2010" /><ref name="pmid18054529">{{cite journal | vauthors = Hauser B, Deschner T, Boesch C | title = Development of a liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry method for the determination of 23 endogenous steroids in small quantities of primate urine | journal = J. Chromatogr. B | volume = 862 | issue = 1-2 | pages = 100–12 | year = 2008 | pmid = 18054529 | doi = 10.1016/j.jchromb.2007.11.009 | url = }}</ref>。

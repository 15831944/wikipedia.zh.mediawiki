'''表達詞彙'''、'''表達性詞彙'''（{{lang-en|Expressive vocabulary}}）指的是那些可以被人們以符合文法與邏輯的創造或表達且能寫下或說出口的詞彙。<ref name="Georgia Pathway">{{cite web | title=Moving Words from the Receptive to the Expressive Lexicon Assists in Vocabulary Development Among Students | website=Georgia Pathway | url=http://www.georgialiteracy.org/detail/92/all--true/vobid--279/ | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref><ref name="Baister">{{cite web | last=Baister | first=Julie | title=What is the difference between receptive vocabulary and expressive vocabulary? | website=Dyslexia Positive | url=http://www.dyslexiapositive.org.uk/discussion/what-is-the-difference-between-receptive-vocabulary-and-expressive-vocabulary/ | access-date=2018-08-25}}</ref><ref name="Armonia Mazzega Pinto Souza 2015 pp. 759–765">{{cite journal | last=Armonia | first=Aline Citino | last2=Mazzega | first2=Laís Carvalho | last3=Pinto | first3=Fernanda Chequer de Alcântara | last4=Souza | first4=Aline Cristina Rocha Fiori de | last5=Perissinoto | first5=Jacy | last6=Tamanaha | first6=Ana Carina | title=Relação entre vocabulário receptivo e expressivo em crianças com transtorno específico do desenvolvimento da fala e da linguagem | journal=Revista CEFAC | publisher=FapUNIFESP (SciELO) | volume=17 | issue=3 | year=2015 | issn=1516-1846 | doi=10.1590/1982-021620156214 | pages=759–765 | url=http://www.scielo.br/scielo.php?pid=S1516-18462015000300759&script=sci_arttext&tlng=en}}</ref>

研究顯示，表達詞彙在學習[[第二語言|第二語言]]的年輕人的第二語言學習過程中扮演一定的腳色。<ref name="Hoff Rumiche Burridge Ribot 2014 pp. 433–444">{{cite journal | last=Hoff | first=Erika | last2=Rumiche | first2=Rosario | last3=Burridge | first3=Andrea | last4=Ribot | first4=Krystal M. | last5=Welsh | first5=Stephanie N. | title=Expressive vocabulary development in children from bilingual and monolingual homes: A longitudinal study from two to four years | journal=Early childhood research quarterly | publisher=Elsevier BV | volume=29 | issue=4 | date=2014-05-13 | issn=0885-2006 | pmid=25089074 | pmc=4114737 | doi=10.1016/j.ecresq.2014.04.012 | pages=433–444}}</ref><ref name="GIBSON OLLER JARMULOWICZ ETHINGTON pp. 102–116">{{cite journal | last=GIBSON | first=TODD A. | last2=OLLER | first2=D.

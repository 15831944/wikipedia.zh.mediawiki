'''极紫外光刻'''（{{lang-en|Extreme ultraviolet lithography}}，也称'''EUV'''或'''EUVL'''）是一种使用[[极紫外辐射|极紫外]]（EUV）波长的{{tsl|en|next-generation lithography|下一代光刻}}技术，目前预期使用13.5[[纳米|纳米]]，预计将于2020年得到广泛应用<ref>[http://semiengineering.com/the-week-in-review-manufacturing-116/ Intel 7nm by 2019]</ref><ref>[http://www.electronicsweekly.com/blogs/mannerisms/manufacturing-mannerisms/glofo-looks-for-7nm-leadership-2016-05/ Globalfoundries EUV by 2020]</ref><ref>[https://www.semiwiki.com/forum/content/5721-samsung-10nm-7nm-strategy-explained.html Samsung 7nm by 2020]</ref><ref>[http://www.extremetech.com/computing/221532-tsmc-will-begin-10nm-production-this-year-claims-5nm-by-2020 TSMC 5nm by 2020]</ref> 。

极紫外光刻的实际应用比原先估计的将近晚了10多年。<ref name="lithoguru.com">[http://www.lithoguru.com/scientist/essays/100WbytheEndoftheYear.ppsx ]</ref><ref>[http://electroiq.com/blog/2016/05/euvl-taking-it-down-to-5nm/ ] {{Wayback|url=http://electroiq.com/blog/2016/05/euvl-taking-it-down-to-5nm/ |date=20160724194957 }}{{不可靠来源}}</ref>

'''郭献川''' （英文：'''Henry Kwek Hian Chuan''') 出生于1976年4月20日，<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://mothership.sg/2015/08/5-things-we-noticed-from-k-shanmugams-grc-team-in-nee-soon/|title=5 things we noticed from K Shanmugam’s GRC team in Nee Soon|author=Jeanette Tan and Tan Xing Qi|accessdate=August 26, 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150828013106/http://mothership.sg/2015/08/5-things-we-noticed-from-k-shanmugams-grc-team-in-nee-soon/|archivedate=2015年8月28日|df=}}</ref> 是[[新加坡|新加坡]] 的[[商人|商人]]与[[政治家|政治家]]。他是执政的[[人民行动党|人民行动党]] (PAP) 的一名[[国会议员|国会议员]] （义顺集选区）。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.businesstimes.com.sg/government-economy/ge2015-pap-retains-nee-soon-grc|title=GE2015: PAP retains Nee Soon GRC|author=Choudhury, Amit Roy|date=12 September 2015|newspaper=[[Business_Times_(Singapore)|The Business Times]]|accessdate=14 November 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151117025404/http://www.businesstimes.com.sg/government-economy/ge2015-pap-retains-nee-soon-grc|archivedate=2015年11月17日|df=}}</ref>

他是间[[管理咨询|管理咨询]]公司的执行董事，职责是负责管理该公司的投资业务。<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.todayonline.com/singapore/new-faces-louis-ng-henry-kwek-join-pap-team-nee-soon-grc|title=New faces Louis Ng, Henry Kwek join PAP team for Nee Soon GRC|date=26 August 2015|newspaper=[[Today_(Singapore_newspaper)|TODAY]]|accessdate=14 November 2015|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20151102185103/http://www.todayonline.com/singapore/new-faces-louis-ng-henry-kwek-join-pap-team-nee-soon-grc|archivedate=2015年11月2日|df=}}</ref> 

== 教育 ==
他所就读的学府包括[[新加坡南洋华侨中学|新加坡南洋华侨中学]]与[[维多利亚初级学院|维多利亚初级学院]]，并且荣获[[史丹佛大學|斯坦福大学]][[经济学|经济学]]与[[管理学|管理学]]双学位。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.parliament.gov.sg/sites/default/files/cv/Henry%20Kwek%20bio.pdf|title=Henry Kwek bio|accessdate=13 October 2016|website=Parliament of Singapore|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170327153028/http://www.parliament.gov.sg/sites/default/files/cv/Henry%20Kwek%20bio.pdf|archivedate=2017年3月27日|df=}}</ref>

== 政治生涯 ==
自从获选后，他已为选区内的低收入家庭开办了补习计划。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/volunteers-help-needy-break-out-of-poverty-cycle|title=Volunteers help needy break out of poverty cycle|accessdate=3 October 2016|last1=Oh|first1=Rachel|website=Straits Times|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161003200433/http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/volunteers-help-needy-break-out-of-poverty-cycle|archivedate=2016年10月3日|df=}}</ref> 他还启动了一个“共用厨房”为选区内低收入的居民提供免费餐食。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/volunteers-help-needy-break-out-of-poverty-cycle|title=Volunteers help needy break out of poverty cycle|accessdate=3 October 2016|last1=Oh|first1=Rachel|website=Straits Times|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161003200433/http://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/volunteers-help-needy-break-out-of-poverty-cycle|archivedate=2016年10月3日|df=}}</ref>

他曾在国会提及关于给予新加坡本土企业与起步公司更多增长机会的课题。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/mps-debate-help-for-smes/2459674.html|title=MPs debate help for SMEs and low-wage workers|accessdate=3 October 2016|last1=Saad|first1=Imelda|website=TODAY|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161003192355/http://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/mps-debate-help-for-smes/2459674.html|archivedate=2016年10月3日|df=}}</ref>

== 个人生活 ==
他已婚，家中也有饲养一条宠物狗（日本狐狸狗）。<ref>{{cite web|url=http://mothership.sg/2016/03/first-time-pap-mp-henry-kwek-wants-to-be-your-kebun-baru-tour-guide-social-worker-historian/|title=First-time PAP MP Henry Kwek wants to be your Kebun Baru tour guide/ social worker/ historian|accessdate=13 October 2016|last1=Tan|first1=Martino|website=Mothership.sg|deadurl=no|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161013151119/http://mothership.sg/2016/03/first-time-pap-mp-henry-kwek-wants-to-be-your-kebun-baru-tour-guide-social-worker-historian/|archivedate=2016年10月13日|df=}}</ref>
